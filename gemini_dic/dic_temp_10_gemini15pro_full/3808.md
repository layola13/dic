agnostic:/æɡˈnɒstɪk/ | adj. n.| 不可知论的，不可知论者 | Relating to or characteristic of an agnostic; holding the belief that it is impossible to know whether God exists or not. |  例句：He described himself as an agnostic. 他自称是一个不可知论者。| 近义词：skeptical, doubting, unbelieving | 反义词：religious, devout, believing | 词性：adjective, noun
agnostic:/æɡˈnɒstɪk/ |  n.| 不可知论者 | A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God. | 例句：She's an agnostic and doesn't attend any church. 她是一个不可知论者，不去任何教堂。| 近义词：skeptic, doubter | 反义词：believer, theist | 词性：noun
