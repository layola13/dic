fraudulency:/ˈfrɔːdjʊlənsɪ/
n.
欺诈；欺骗行为
The act of being fraudulent; deceitfulness.
He was accused of fraudulency in the sale of the property.  他被指控在出售房产时存在欺诈行为。
The company's fraudulency was eventually exposed by an investigative journalist. 这家公司的欺诈行为最终被一名调查记者曝光。
近义词：fraud, deceit, dishonesty, deception, chicanery
反义词：honesty, integrity, probity
词性: 名词

fraudulent:/ˈfrɔːdʒələnt/
adj.
欺诈的；欺骗的
Characterized by, involving, or proceeding from fraud.
The fraudulent scheme cost investors millions of dollars.  这个欺诈计划使投资者损失了数百万美元。
He presented fraudulent documents to support his claim. 他提交了伪造的文件来支持他的说法。
近义词：dishonest, deceptive, crooked, shady
反义词：honest, genuine, legitimate
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *fraudulentia, from Latin fraudulentus (“fraudulent”) + -ia.  Ultimately from Latin fraus (“fraud, deceit”).
