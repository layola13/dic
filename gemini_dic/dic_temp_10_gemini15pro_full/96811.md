inertitude:/ˌɪnərˈtɪtjuːd/ /ˌɪnərˈtuːd/
n.
惰性；迟钝；不活动
Lack of energy or activity; sluggishness.
His inertitude prevented him from finishing the project on time. 他的惰性使他没能按时完成项目。

The inertitude of the government in the face of the crisis was appalling. 政府面对危机时的不作为令人震惊。


近义词:inactivity, inertia, passivity, sluggishness, torpor
反义词:activity, energy, vigor, dynamism
词性:名词
