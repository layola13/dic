musicianer: /mjuːˈzɪʃənər/ 
| n. | 音乐家 (古旧用法，现已弃用) | An archaic or dialectal term for "musician."  This word is no longer in common use. |  He was a skilled musicianer in the king's court. (古旧用法) 他是国王宫廷里一位技艺娴熟的音乐家。| 近义词: musician, instrumentalist, performer | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider non-musician, amateur) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Middle English *musicioner, derived from Old French musicien.  The -er suffix is a common agent suffix in English, but in this case, the form with just -ian became the standard.
