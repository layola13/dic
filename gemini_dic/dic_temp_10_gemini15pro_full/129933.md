non-litigious:/ˌnɒn lɪˈtɪdʒəs/| adj.|非诉讼的|Not inclined to engage in lawsuits; avoiding or settling disputes outside of court.|例句：The company prefers a non-litigious approach to resolving disagreements.  这家公司更喜欢用非诉讼的方式解决分歧。

例句：They sought a non-litigious solution to the contract dispute. 他们寻求了一种非诉讼的合同纠纷解决方案。|近义词：amicable, conciliatory, non-contentious|反义词：litigious, contentious, adversarial|词性:形容词
