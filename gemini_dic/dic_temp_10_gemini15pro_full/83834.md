hakim:/ˈhɑːkiːm/ (US), /hæˈkiːm/ (UK)| n.|法官，统治者，医生|A person who holds judicial or executive power, especially in some Islamic countries.  Also, a physician or healer in traditional Arabic medicine.|例句：The hakim listened carefully to both sides of the case. 法官仔细地听取了双方的陈述。

例句：The hakim prescribed a herbal remedy for the illness.  这位医生开了一种草药来治疗这种疾病。|近义词：judge, magistrate, ruler, physician, doctor|反义词:defendant, subject, patient|词性:noun
词源: From Arabic حَكِيم (ḥakīm), meaning "wise," "sage," "philosopher," or "physician."  Ultimately from the root حَكَمَ (ḥakama), "to judge" or "to rule."
