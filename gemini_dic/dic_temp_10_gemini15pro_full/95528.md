inclusions:/ɪnˈkluːʒənz/
n.
内含物，包含物
something that is included within something else.
The price includes flights and hotel accommodation.  价格包含机票和酒店住宿。
synonyms:  component, element, constituent, ingredient, part, portion
antonyms: exclusions, omissions
noun

inclusion:/ɪnˈkluːʒən/
n.
包含，纳入；包容性
the act of including someone or something as part of a group, set, or total.
The book’s value stems from its inclusion of multiple perspectives. 这本书的价值在于它包含了多种观点。
synonyms: incorporation, insertion, addition, admittance, involvement
antonyms: exclusion, omission, rejection
noun
