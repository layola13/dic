gruffish:/ˈɡrʌfɪʃ/ | adj. |粗暴的，生硬的，不友好的 |  Having a gruff or surly manner; unfriendly or grumpy.  | He gave a gruffish reply and turned away. 他粗声粗气地回答了一句，然后就走开了。 |  例句：The old man gave a gruffish response to my question. 老人对我的问题作了粗鲁的回答。

例句：Her gruffish demeanor hid a kind heart. 她粗暴的态度掩盖了一颗善良的心。


近义词：curt, brusque, surly, grumpy, rude | 反义词：polite, courteous, friendly, amiable, genial | 词性: adjective
