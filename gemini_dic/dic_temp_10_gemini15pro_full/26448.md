byss:/bɪs/| n.| 细麻，深渊 |A fine, flax-like fiber, typically made from cotton or linen, used for embroidery and weaving; also, poetically, an abyss or bottomless gulf.|The ancient Egyptians used byss to wrap their mummies.| 近义词: abyss, chasm, gulf, linen, flax | 反义词: summit, peak | 词性: noun

byssal:/ˈbɪsəl/| adj.| 深渊的，细麻的 |Relating to or consisting of byssus; inhabiting the depths of the sea.|The byssal threads anchored the mussel to the rock.|近义词: abyssal, deep-sea | 反义词: surface, shallow | 词性: adjective
