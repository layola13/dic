habituation:/həˌbɪtʃuˈeɪʃən/ | n. |习惯化; 适应 |The process of becoming accustomed to something; a decrease in response to a stimulus after repeated presentations. |After a few weeks, the noise from the construction site no longer bothered me due to habituation.  几周后，由于习惯了，建筑工地的噪音不再困扰我了。| habituation | acclimation, adaptation, desensitization| sensitization, awareness | noun

habituate:/həˈbɪtʃueɪt/ | vt. |使习惯于 |To make or become accustomed or used to something. |The trainer habituated the horses to the sounds of traffic.  训练员让马匹习惯了交通噪音。| accustom, adapt, inure | unaccustom, wean | verb
