autotrophic:/ˌɔːtəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 自养的，自营的 |  (of an organism) capable of synthesizing its own food from inorganic substances, using light or chemical energy.  | Green plants are autotrophic. 绿色植物是自养的。 | Autotrophic organisms use photosynthesis or chemosynthesis to create their own food. 自养生物利用光合作用或化合作用来制造自己的食物。 | 近义词:self-nourishing, self-feeding | 反义词:heterotrophic | 词性:形容词
