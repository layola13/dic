deoxidize:/diːˈɒksaɪdaɪz/ | v. | 除氧，脱氧 | To remove oxygen from a substance. | The process deoxidizes the molten steel. 这个过程可以去除钢水中的氧。|
例句：
The chemist deoxidized the compound by removing the oxygen atoms. 化学家通过去除氧原子使化合物脱氧。

Aluminum is often used to deoxidize steel. 铝经常被用来给钢脱氧。

近义词：reduce, deoxydize | 反义词：oxidize | 词性：动词

deoxidization:/diːˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 脱氧，除氧 | The process of removing oxygen from a substance. | Deoxidization is a crucial step in steelmaking. 脱氧是炼钢过程中的一个关键步骤。|
例句：
The deoxidization of the metal prevented corrosion. 金属的脱氧防止了腐蚀。

The winemaker controlled the deoxidization process carefully. 酿酒师仔细地控制脱氧过程。

近义词：reduction | 反义词：oxidation | 词性：名词

deoxidizer:/diːˈɒksaɪdaɪzər/ | n. | 脱氧剂，除氧剂 | A substance that removes oxygen from another substance. | Aluminum is a common deoxidizer in metallurgy. 铝是冶金学中常用的脱氧剂。|
例句：
The deoxidizer was added to the solution to remove dissolved oxygen.  将脱氧剂添加到溶液中以去除溶解氧。

A deoxidizer is essential in certain chemical reactions.  脱氧剂在某些化学反应中是必不可少的。

近义词：reducing agent, reductant | 反义词：oxidizing agent, oxidant | 词性：名词
