lorgnette:/lɔːrˈnjɛt/ | n. |  长柄眼镜，观剧镜 | A pair of eyeglasses with a handle, used for looking at things more closely, especially at the theater or opera. | She raised her lorgnette to get a better look at the painting. 她举起长柄眼镜，想更仔细地看看这幅画。 |  Near synonyms: opera glasses, eyeglasses | Antonyms: None | Noun
