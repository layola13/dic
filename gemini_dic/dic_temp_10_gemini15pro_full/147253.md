overmerit:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪt/
adjective
过分褒奖的，评价过高的
having more merit ascribed than is warranted; unduly praised or rewarded.
His performance was overmerited, considering the simplicity of the task. 他的表现被过誉了，考虑到任务的简单性。
The book was overmerited by the critics; it wasn't that groundbreaking.  这本书被评论家过分赞扬了；它并没有那么开创性。
synonyms: overpraised, overvalued, overrated
antonyms: underrated, undervalued, underappreciated
词性: adjective
