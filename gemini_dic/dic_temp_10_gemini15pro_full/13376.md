atmospherically:/ˌætməsˈfɪrɪkli/| adv.|大气地；气氛上；在空气中|Relating to the atmosphere of a place; creating a particular mood or feeling.|The restaurant was atmospherically lit with candles.  这家餐厅用蜡烛照明，营造了很好的氛围。| The music was atmospherically perfect for the scene. 这段音乐与场景的气氛完美融合。|近义词：ambiently, moodily|反义词: blandly, plainly|词性:副词

词源: From atmosphere + -ic + -ally.  "Atmosphere" comes from the Greek "atmos" (vapor) and "sphaira" (sphere).
