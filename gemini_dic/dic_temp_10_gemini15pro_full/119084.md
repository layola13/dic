metrically:/ˈmɛtrɪkli/ | adv. | 度量地；韵律地；以米为单位地 | In a way that relates to the system of measurement based on the metre, or to poetic metre. | The poem is metrically complex. 这首诗在韵律上很复杂。 | synonymously, quantitatively | qualitatively | adverb

Etymology:  From "metric" + "-ally". "Metric" comes from French "métrique", ultimately from Greek "metron" (measure).
