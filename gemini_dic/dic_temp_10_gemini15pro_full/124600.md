mousetail:/ˈmaʊsteɪl/| n. |鼠尾；鼠尾草|A mousetail is the tail of a mouse. It can also refer to certain types of plants that resemble a mouse's tail.|The mousetail disappeared into a hole in the wall.  老鼠的尾巴消失在墙上的一个洞里。|The mousetail plant is also known as Myosurus.  鼠尾草植物也被称为鼠尾草属。|Synonyms: (for plant) Myosurus|Antonyms: (no direct antonym)|noun
