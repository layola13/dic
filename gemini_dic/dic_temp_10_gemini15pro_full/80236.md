goer:/ˈɡoʊər/| n. |常客，爱好者；参加者|A person who regularly attends or participates in a particular activity or event.  A person who goes to a specified place or type of place.|例句：He's a regular goer to the opera. 他是歌剧院的常客。

例句：She's a real party goer. 她真是个派对爱好者。|近义词：attendant, frequenter, participant, visitor|反义词:stay-at-home, homebody|Noun
