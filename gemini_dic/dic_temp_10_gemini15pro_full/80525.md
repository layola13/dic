goodbyes:/ɡʊdˈbaɪz/
n.
告别，再见
an expression of farewell
例句：We said our goodbyes and left the party. 我们告别后离开了派对。

例句：She waved goodbye to her friends. 她向她的朋友们挥手告别。

近义词：farewells, adieus
反义词:greetings, hellos
词性:名词(plural noun)
