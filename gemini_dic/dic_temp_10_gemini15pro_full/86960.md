henchmen: /ˈhɛntʃmən/ | n. | 帮凶；爪牙；走狗 | A faithful follower, especially one prepared to engage in crime or violence on behalf of their leader. | The villain's henchmen carried out his evil plan.  恶棍的爪牙执行了他的邪恶计划。 |  The dictator surrounded himself with ruthless henchmen. 独裁者身边围绕着冷酷无情的爪牙。 | Synonyms: minion, accomplice, thug, goon | Antonyms: leader, boss, opponent, adversary | noun
