dowdily:/ˈdaʊdɪli/| adv.|邋遢地，不整洁地，寒酸地|In a dowdy or shabby manner; lacking smartness and freshness.|She dressed dowdily in an old, shapeless coat. 她穿着过时、不成形的旧外套，显得邋遢。|近义词：shabbily, frumpily, drably|反义词：smartly, stylishly, elegantly|词性:副词
词源: From dowdy + -ly.  Dowdy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from doll, referring to a shabbily dressed doll.
