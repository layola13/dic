automated:/ˈɔːtəmeɪtɪd/
adjective
自动化的
operated by machines or computers, without needing human control
例句：The factory is now fully automated. 这家工厂现在完全自动化了。

例句：Automated systems can greatly improve efficiency. 自动化系统可以大大提高效率。

近义词：automatic, mechanized, robotic
反义词：manual, hand-operated
词性：adjective
