headstrongness:/ˈhedstrɒŋnɪs/ /ˈhɛdˌstrɔŋnɪs/| n.|固执，倔强，刚愎自用|The quality of being determined to do what you want, even when other people advise you not to.|His headstrongness led him to make several poor decisions. 他的固执导致他做了几个糟糕的决定。|近义词：stubbornness, obstinacy, willfulness|反义词：compliance, docility, obedience|词性:名词

Etymology:
From headstrong + -ness.  Headstrong itself comes from head + strong, literally meaning "strong in the head" or "having a strong will."
