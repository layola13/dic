doctrinally:/dɒkˈtraɪnəli/
adverb
教义上地；学说上地
In terms of doctrine; from a doctrinal point of view.
The statement was doctrinally sound, adhering strictly to established principles. 这份声明在教义上是合理的，严格遵守既定原则。
His views are doctrinally pure, reflecting the original teachings without deviation. 他的观点在教义上很纯粹，反映了最初的教义，没有偏离。
Synonym: dogmatically, theoretically
Antonym: practically, empirically
词性: 副词
