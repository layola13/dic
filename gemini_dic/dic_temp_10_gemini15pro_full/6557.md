amyous: /ˈeɪmɪəs/ | adj. | 无肌肉的，肌肉萎缩的 | Lacking muscle; atrophied. |  Example: The disease left him amyous and weak.  这种疾病使他肌肉萎缩，身体虚弱。

Example:  The amyous condition of his legs made walking difficult.  他腿部肌肉萎缩，行走困难。 | 近义词: atrophied, muscleless | 反义词: muscular, strong | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Greek *a- "without" + mys "muscle"
