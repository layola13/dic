antiasthma:/ˌæntiˈæzmə/ | adj. | 抗哮喘的；治疗哮喘的 |  Used to describe medications or treatments that prevent or alleviate asthma symptoms.  Relating to the prevention or treatment of asthma. |  She was prescribed an antiasthma inhaler. 她被开了抗哮喘吸入器。 | synonyms: anti-asthmatic | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "asthma-inducing" as a contrasting concept) | adjective
