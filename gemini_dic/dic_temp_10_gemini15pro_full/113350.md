luxuriousnesses:/ləɡˈʒʊəriəsnəsɪz/
noun
奢华，豪华（的狀態或性質）
The state or quality of being luxurious; an instance of this.  It refers to an abundance of luxury or multiple instances of luxurious things or experiences.
She reveled in the luxuriousnesses of the five-star hotel, from the silk sheets to the gourmet meals. 她沉醉于这家五星级酒店的奢华之中，从丝绸床单到美味佳肴，无一不让她感到满意。
The sheer luxuriousnesses of her lifestyle were breathtaking. 她奢华的生活方式令人叹为观止。
近义词: luxuries, extravagances, opulence
反义词: austerity, simplicity, poverty
词性: noun
