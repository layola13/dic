crop-up:/krɒp ʌp/ (British) /krɑːp ʌp/ (American)| v. |突然出现，意外发生|To appear or occur unexpectedly.|例句：A problem cropped up at work.  工作中出现了一个问题。

例句：New challenges are cropping up all the time. 新的挑战层出不穷。|近义词：arise, emerge, occur, surface, pop up|反义词: be planned, be expected|词性:动词
