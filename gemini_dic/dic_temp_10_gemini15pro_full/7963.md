antherogenous:/ænθərˈɒdʒɪnəs/| adj.|花药生的，由花药产生的|Produced or borne on the anther.  Relating to the development of pollen within the anther.|Antherogenous structures were observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到了花药生的结构。|The antherogenous tissue showed signs of mutation. 花药生组织显示出突变的迹象。|Synonyms: antheral|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly consider terms relating to other parts of the flower, e.g., gynoecial)|adjective
