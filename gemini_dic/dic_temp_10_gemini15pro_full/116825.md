maxillary: /ˈmæksɪləri/ | adj. | 上颌的，上颌骨的 | Relating to the jaw or jawbone, especially the upper jaw. | The maxillary artery supplies blood to the upper jaw. 上颌动脉为上颌供血。| Synonym: jaw | Antonym: mandibular | adjective
