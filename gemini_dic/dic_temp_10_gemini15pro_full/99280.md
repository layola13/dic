intercontinental:/ˌɪntərˌkɒntɪˈnɛntəl/ /ˌɪntərˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntəl/
adjective
洲际的
spanning or occurring between continents; involving two or more continents.
例句：
An intercontinental ballistic missile can travel thousands of miles.
一枚洲际弹道导弹可以飞行数千英里。

例句：
Intercontinental flights are often long and tiring.
洲际航班通常漫长而疲惫。

近义词：international, global, worldwide
反义词：intracontinental, domestic
词性：adjective
