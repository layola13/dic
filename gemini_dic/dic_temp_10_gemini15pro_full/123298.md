monogenous:/məˈnɒdʒɪnəs/ | adj. |单基因的，单源的| Having a single origin; produced by a single gene or organism. |例句：The disease is monogenous, meaning it's caused by a mutation in a single gene.  这种疾病是单基因的，意味着它是由单个基因突变引起的。

例句：The monogenous culture of bacteria allowed for easier study of its properties.  单源培养的细菌更容易研究其特性。|近义词：unifactorial, single-gene|反义词: polygenic, multifactorial | 词性: adjective
