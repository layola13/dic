ditziest:/ˈdɪt͡siɪst/| adj.|最愚蠢的，最轻浮的|Superlative adjective of dizzy.  Meaning extremely silly, frivolous, or scatterbrained.|She made the ditziest remark in the meeting, leaving everyone speechless.|Synonyms: silliest, flightiest, giddiest, most scatterbrained|Antonyms: smartest, wisest, most sensible|adjective
