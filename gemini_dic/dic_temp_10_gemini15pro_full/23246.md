bowman:/ˈboʊmən/| n. |弓箭手，射手|A person who uses a bow and arrow, especially in hunting or warfare. |例句：The bowman took careful aim at the target. 射手仔细瞄准目标。

例句：The skilled bowman could hit a moving target from a hundred yards.  熟练的弓箭手可以从一百码外射中移动的目标。

|近义词：archer, marksman |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
