anacrusis:/ˌænəˈkruːsɪs/ | n. |  弱起，起拍，不完全小节 | An anacrusis is a note or sequence of notes which precede the first downbeat of a bar. | The piece begins with an anacrusis of two eighth notes. 这首曲子以两个八分音符的弱起开始。| synonym: upbeat, pickup | antonym: downbeat | noun

anacrusis:/ˌænəˈkruːsɪs/ | n. |  弱起，起拍，不完全小节 |  It refers to the unstressed notes at the beginning of a musical phrase that precede the first full bar.  | The song has a distinctive anacrusis that sets the rhythm for the entire piece. 这首歌有一个独特的弱起，为整首曲子设定了节奏。| synonym: upbeat, pickup | antonym: downbeat | noun
