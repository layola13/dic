oarswoman:/ˈɔːrzˌwʊmən/ | n. | 女划手；女性桨手 | A woman who rows a boat, especially as a member of a crew. |  The oarswoman pulled strongly, propelling the boat through the water. 女划手用力划桨，推动着小船在水中前进。

The experienced oarswoman guided the boat skillfully through the rough waters.  经验丰富的女子桨手熟练地引导船只穿过汹涌的水面。 | 近义词：rower, crew member | 反义词：coxswain (舵手 -  more of a contrasting role than an opposite) | 词性：noun
