ekarin:/ˈekərɪn/ | n. | 角蛋白| A fibrous structural protein that is the key structural material making up the outer layer of human skin, hair and nails.  It is also the structural protein of wool, horns, claws, hooves, feathers, scales, beaks, and turtle shells.|  Example: Ekarin is a crucial component of the epidermis. 角蛋白是表皮的重要组成部分。

Example:  The strength of hair and nails comes from ekarin. 头发和指甲的强度来自角蛋白。| 近义词: keratin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a structural protein) | 词性: noun
