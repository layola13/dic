chloral: /ˈklɔːrəl/ | n. |三氯乙醛; 水合氯醛| A colorless oily liquid with a pungent irritating odor, used in the manufacture of DDT and as a hypnotic drug.  It forms a hydrate that is used as a sedative. | Chloral hydrate was once a popular sedative. 水合氯醛曾是一种流行的镇静剂。 | synonyms:  trichloroacetaldehyde, trichloroethanal | antonyms:  N/A | noun
