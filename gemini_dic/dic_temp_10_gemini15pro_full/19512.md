biennially: /baɪˈɛniəli/ | adv. | 两年一次地；每两年一次地 | Happening every two years. | The festival is held biennially. 这个节日每两年举办一次。| Synonyms: every two years, every other year | Antonyms: annually, yearly | adverb

biennial: /baɪˈɛniəl/ | adj. | 两年一次的；持续两年的 | Happening every two years or lasting for two years. |  The biennial conference attracts scholars from around the world.  这个两年一度的会议吸引了来自世界各地的学者。| Synonyms: two-yearly | Antonyms: annual, yearly | adjective

biennial: /baɪˈɛniəl/ | n. | 两年生植物 | A plant that lives for two years, flowering and producing seeds in the second year. |  Parsley is a biennial. 欧芹是一种两年生植物。| Synonyms: two-year plant | Antonyms: annual, perennial | noun
