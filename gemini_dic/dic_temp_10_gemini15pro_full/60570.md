elasticness:/ɪˌlæˈstɪsnəs/| n. |弹性，弹力|The elasticness of the rubber band allows it to stretch and return to its original shape.  橡皮筋的弹性使它能够伸展并恢复到原来的形状。|例句：The elasticness of demand is an important concept in economics. 需求弹性是经济学中的一个重要概念。|近义词：flexibility, resilience, springiness|反义词: rigidity, stiffness|noun

elasticness:/ɪˌlæˈstɪsnəs/| n. |灵活性，适应性|The elasticness of the company's policy allows them to adapt to changing market conditions. 公司政策的灵活性使他们能够适应不断变化的市场环境。|例句：The project requires a team with a high degree of elasticness. 这个项目需要一个高度灵活的团队。|近义词：adaptability, plasticity|反义词: inflexibility, rigidity|noun
