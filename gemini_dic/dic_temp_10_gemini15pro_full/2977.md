aegilops: /ˈiːdʒɪlɒps/ (US) /ˈiːɡɪlɒps/ (UK)| n. |山羊草属; 鹅观草属; 麦病|A genus of Eurasian and North African grasses (tribe Triticeae) that includes the goat grasses and is closely related to the genus Triticum (wheat). Some species are important weeds of grain fields, and some are thought to be ancestors of cultivated wheat.|Aegilops tauschii is believed to be the donor of the D genome to bread wheat.|近义词: goat grass, Triticum (for some species)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
