complimented:/ˈkɒmplɪmentɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɪmentɪd/ (US)| v. |称赞；赞扬；恭维|past tense and past participle of compliment -  express praise and admiration to (someone) for something.|He complimented her on her new dress. 他称赞了她的新裙子。|近义词：praised, lauded, commended|反义词：criticized, condemned|词性:动词

compliment:/ˈkɒmplɪmənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɪment/ (US)| n. vt.|称赞；赞扬；恭维|a polite expression of praise or admiration.|She received many compliments on her performance. 她因表演而受到很多赞扬。|近义词：praise, accolade, tribute|反义词：criticism, insult|词性:名词/动词

词源:
From Middle French compliment, from Italian complimento (“expression of civility, reverence”), from Latin complēmentum (“that which fills up or completes”), from complēre (“to fill up, complete”).
