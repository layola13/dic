oxheart:/ˈɒksˌhɑːt/ (UK), /ˈɑːksˌhɑːrt/ (US)| n. adj.|牛心；牛心形的；（西红柿的）一种品种|A large, fleshy variety of tomato shaped like a heart.  Also, the heart of an ox.|The chef used oxheart tomatoes in the salad. 厨师在沙拉中使用了牛心番茄。|Synonyms: beefsteak tomato, large tomato|Antonyms: cherry tomato, grape tomato|noun

oxheart:/ˈɒksˌhɑːt/ (UK), /ˈɑːksˌhɑːrt/ (US)| adj.|牛心形的|Shaped like the heart of an ox; heart-shaped, especially of tomatoes.|These oxheart tomatoes are perfect for stuffing. 这些牛心番茄非常适合做馅料。|Synonyms: heart-shaped, cordate|Antonyms: round, oval|adjective
