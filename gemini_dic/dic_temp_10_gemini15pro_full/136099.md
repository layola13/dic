nonrecuperative:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈkjuː.pər.ə.tɪv/
adjective
非回收的，非蓄热的
not designed to recover or reuse heat or energy.
The process is nonrecuperative, meaning the heat generated is lost to the environment. 这个过程是非回收的，意味着产生的热量会散失到环境中。
The furnace operates on a nonrecuperative principle. 这座熔炉的运作原理是非蓄热的。
non-regenerative, non-recycling
recuperative, regenerative, recycling
