bigo:/ˈbiːɡoʊ/| n. | 盛大的活动；热闹的场面；喧闹；混乱 | A large, elaborate, and often ostentatious event or gathering. |  例句：The wedding was a real bigo.  婚礼真是个盛大的场面。| 近义词：bash, shindig, extravaganza, spectacle | 反义词：small gathering, intimate affair | 词性: 名词

词源：Bigo is likely a shortened and altered form of "big do," which itself is informal British English for a large or important social event.  "Do" in this sense comes from the verb "to do," meaning to perform or carry out an action or activity.
