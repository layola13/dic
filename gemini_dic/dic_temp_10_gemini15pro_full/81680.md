greencloth:/ˈɡriːnklɒθ/ (UK), /ˈɡriːnklɔːθ/ (US)| n. |赌桌；赌博；赌场|A baize-covered table, especially one used for gambling. It can also refer to gambling itself or a gambling establishment.|He lost a fortune at the greencloth. 他在赌桌上输了一大笔钱。|近义词：gambling table, casino, gaming table|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From 'green' + 'cloth'.  Originally referred to the green baize used to cover gambling tables.
