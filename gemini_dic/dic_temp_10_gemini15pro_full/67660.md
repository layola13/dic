extrusion:/ɪkˈstruːʒən/| n.| 挤压, 突出, 挤出物|The process of shaping material by forcing it through a die.  This can create various shapes, like pipes or rods.|The extrusion of the molten plastic created a long, thin tube. 熔融塑料的挤压形成了一个细长的管子。|近义词：forcing, pushing, expulsion|反义词:intromission, insertion, intrusion|词性:noun
