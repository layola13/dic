mortgagees:/ˌmɔːrɡəˈdʒiːz/
n.
抵押权人，受押人
The mortgagees are entitled to the payments from the mortgagor. 抵押权人有权从抵押人那里获得付款。
The mortgagees held a meeting to discuss the foreclosure process. 抵押权人召开会议讨论取消抵押品赎回权的程序。
Synonyms: lender, creditor
Antonyms: mortgagor, debtor
