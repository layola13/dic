ninepins:/ˈnaɪnˌpɪnz/| n.|九柱戏|A game similar to bowling, played with nine wooden pins set up in a diamond formation and a ball rolled to knock them down.|We played ninepins in the park yesterday. 我们昨天在公园玩了九柱戏。|Near synonyms: bowling, skittles|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "no game")|noun


ninepins:/ˈnaɪnˌpɪnz/| n.|混乱，一团糟 (figurative, less common)|A state of disorder or confusion.|The room was in complete ninepins after the party. 派对结束后，房间里乱七八糟。|Near synonyms:  chaos, disarray, mess|Antonym: order, tidiness|noun
