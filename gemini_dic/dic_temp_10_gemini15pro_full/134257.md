noninflammability:/ˌnɒnɪnˌflæməˈbɪləti/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪnˌflæməˈbɪlɪti/ (UK)| n. |不可燃性，不燃性|The quality or state of being noninflammable; not easily set on fire. |This fabric's noninflammability makes it ideal for use in children's clothing. 这种织物的不可燃性使其非常适合用于儿童服装。|Synonym: fire resistance, incombustibility|Antonym: flammability, combustibility|noun
