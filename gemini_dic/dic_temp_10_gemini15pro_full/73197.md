force-feeds:/fɔːrs ˈfiːdz/ | v. | 强行喂食 | To make someone eat or drink something, especially when they are unwilling or unable to do so themselves. | The farmer force-feeds the geese to fatten them up for foie gras. 农民强行喂鹅，是为了让它们长肥以制作鹅肝。

The nurses had to force-feed the patient through a tube. 护士不得不通过管子强行给病人喂食。 | 近义词：stuff, cram, overfeed | 反义词: starve, deprive of food | verb


force-feed:/fɔːrs ˈfiːd/ | n. | 强行喂食 | The act of force-feeding. | The force-feed of geese is a controversial practice. 强行喂鹅的做法备受争议。

The patient was put on a force-feed regimen. 病人被安排了强制喂食疗程。| 近义词：gavage | 反义词: fasting | noun


Etymology: "force" (from Old French "force") + "feed" (from Old English "fēdan")
