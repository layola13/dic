gauffre:/ˈɡɔːfrə/ (US), /ˈɡɒflə/ (UK)| n.|华夫饼，格子饼|A thin cake or waffle, especially one cooked in a waffle iron.|例句：I love eating a warm gauffre with maple syrup. 我喜欢吃淋枫糖浆的热华夫饼。

例句：The gauffres in Brussels are famous for their crispy texture. 布鲁塞尔的华夫饼以其酥脆的口感而闻名。|近义词：waffle, wafer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps pancake as a different type of breakfast food)|词性:noun
