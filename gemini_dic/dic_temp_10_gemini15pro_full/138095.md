normativeness:/ˈnɔːrmətɪvnəs/ | n. | 规范性，标准性 | The quality or state of being normative; conforming to or based on norms. | The normativeness of standardized tests is often debated. 标准化考试的规范性经常受到争议。|  The sociologist studied the normativeness of gender roles in different cultures.  这位社会学家研究了不同文化中性别角色的规范性。 |近义词: prescriptiveness, regularity, standard | 反义词: abnormality, deviance, nonconformity | 词性: noun
