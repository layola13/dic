hypersensitivenesses:/ˌhaɪpərsɛnsɪˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.| 过度敏感；过敏；神经过敏
|The state of being excessively or abnormally sensitive.  A plural form of hypersensitiveness.|
|Her hypersensitivenesses to certain foods made dining out difficult. 她对某些食物的过度敏感使得外出就餐很困难。|
|His hypersensitivenesses to criticism made it hard to work with him.  他对批评的过度敏感使得很难与他共事。

|近义词: sensitivities, allergies, susceptibilities|
|反义词: insensitivity, indifference, tolerance|
|词性: noun (plural)|
