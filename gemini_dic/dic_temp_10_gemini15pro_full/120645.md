ETYMOLOGY->

mis- :  Old English *mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly, amiss," from PIE *mei- (1) "to change, go, move."  Indicates "wrong, bad, wrongly."

register: From Middle English registren, from Old French registre, from Medieval Latin registrum “list, record book,” from Latin regerere “to carry back, bring back, report,” from re- “back” + gerere “to carry, bear.”
