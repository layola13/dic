agglutinate:/əˈɡluːtɪneɪt/| v.  adj.| 凝集，粘合|To stick together to form a mass; to cause to adhere.|The red blood cells agglutinated. 红细胞凝集了。|近义词：adhere, clump, coalesce, congeal|反义词:separate, disperse|verb
agglutinate:/əˈɡluːtɪneɪt/| adj.| 凝集的，粘合的|Stuck together; adhering.|The agglutinated mass of cells was difficult to analyze.  粘在一起的细胞团很难分析。|近义词：adhesive, cohesive|反义词:separate, dispersed|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin agglutinatus, past participle of agglutinare "to glue to," from ad- "to" + glutinare "to glue," from gluten "glue."
