noce:/ˈnoʊseɪ/ (US), /ˈnəʊseɪ/ (UK)| n. |婚礼；婚宴|A wedding celebration; nuptials.|例句：Their noce was a grand affair, held in a beautiful Tuscan villa. 他们的婚礼是一场盛大的活动，在托斯卡纳的一座美丽的别墅举行。

例句：The noce lasted for three days and included a traditional feast. 婚礼持续了三天，包括传统的盛宴。|近义词：wedding, marriage, nuptials|反义词:divorce, separation|词性:noun
词源:From Italian *nozze* "wedding," plural of *nozza*, from Latin *nuptiae* "wedding."
