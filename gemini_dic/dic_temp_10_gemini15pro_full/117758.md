melasma:/məˈlæzmə/ | n. | 褐黄斑，黄褐斑 |A common skin problem that causes brown or grayish-brown patches, usually on the face.|  She developed melasma during pregnancy. 她在怀孕期间出现了黄褐斑。 | 近义词:chloasma, hyperpigmentation | 反义词: hypopigmentation | 词性: noun
