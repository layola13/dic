natals: /ˈneɪtəlz/ | n. | 出生，诞生; 出生地;  (astronomy) Natal chart; Natal position of planets. | Natal refers to the time or place of someone's birth.  It often appears in an astrological context, referring to a natal chart (a map of the positions of the planets at the time of birth) or natal position (the position of a planet at the time of birth).  It can also refer to a person's birthplace. |  She was interested in learning about her natal chart. 她对了解自己的星盘很感兴趣。 |  birth, nativity |  death | noun
