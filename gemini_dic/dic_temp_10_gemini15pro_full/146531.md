overglad:/ˌōvərˈɡlæd/ | adj. | 过高兴的，欣喜若狂的 | Excessively glad; overjoyed.  | She was overglad to hear the good news. 她听到这个好消息欣喜若狂。| 近义词：overjoyed, delighted, ecstatic, thrilled | 反义词：sad, unhappy, depressed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "glad" (adjective meaning "pleased; happy").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "glad."
