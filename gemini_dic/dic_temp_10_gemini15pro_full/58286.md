drupe:/druːp/
n.
核果
A fleshy fruit with thin skin and a central stone containing the seed, e.g., a plum, cherry, almond, or olive.
Peaches, plums, and cherries are all drupes.  桃子、李子和樱桃都是核果。
Near Synonyms: stone fruit
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a biological classification)
noun
