misadvises:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪzɪz/
verb
错误地建议；给予不良的劝告
To give someone bad or incorrect advice.
He misadvised his client, leading to a significant financial loss. 他错误地建议了他的客户，导致了重大的经济损失。
The lawyer misadvised her on her legal rights. 律师就她的合法权利给了她错误的建议。
Synonym: counsels wrongly, gives bad advice, misguides
Antonym: advises correctly, guides well
词性: 动词
