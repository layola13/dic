micromanages:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒɪz/
|v.|微观管理，事无巨细地管理|to control every part of a situation, including small details, often in a way that is annoying or causes problems|例句：He micromanages his employees, which stifles their creativity. 他对员工进行微观管理，扼杀了他们的创造力。

例句：The new CEO has a tendency to micromanage, creating a tense atmosphere in the office. 新任首席执行官倾向于事无巨细地管理，在办公室里营造了一种紧张的气氛。
|近义词：oversupervise, overmanage|反义词:delegate, empower|词性:动词
