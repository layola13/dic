companionship:/kəmˈpæn.jən.ʃɪp/ | n. |友谊；陪伴；伴侣关系|The state of being with someone and enjoying their company.  |例句：She enjoyed the companionship of her pet cat. 她享受着宠物猫的陪伴。

例句：He missed the companionship of his colleagues after retirement.  退休后，他怀念与同事们在一起的时光。|近义词：fellowship, camaraderie, friendship, company |反义词：isolation, loneliness, solitude|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French compaignie, from compaing ‘companion’, from late Latin companio.
