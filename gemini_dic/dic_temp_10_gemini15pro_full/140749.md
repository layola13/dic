omnishambles:/ˈɒmnɪˌʃæmbəlz/
noun
彻底混乱；一团糟
A situation of total chaos and confusion.
The government's handling of the crisis has been an utter omnishambles. 政府对这场危机的处理完全是一团糟。
The project became an omnishambles due to poor planning.  由于计划不周，这个项目变得一团糟。
Synonym: disaster, mess, fiasco, shambles, debacle
Antonym: order, organization, success, triumph
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
The word "omnishambles" is a portmanteau of "omni-" (meaning "all" or "every") and "shambles" (meaning "a state of total disorder").  It was first used in the British political satire television programme The Thick of It in 2009 and quickly gained popularity.
