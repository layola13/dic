hatchery:/ˈhætʃəri/| n. |孵化场；孵化器|A place where eggs are hatched, especially fish or poultry eggs.  A building or enclosure for hatching eggs, especially those of fish or poultry. |例句：The salmon hatchery releases millions of young fish into the river each year. 每年，鲑鱼孵化场都会向河里放生数百万条幼鱼。

例句：The chicken hatchery provides chicks to farms across the country.  这家鸡孵化场向全国各地的农场提供小鸡。|近义词：breeding ground, nursery|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
