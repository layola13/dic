lithagogue: /ˈlɪθəɡɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈlɪθəɡɑːɡ/ (US)| n.| 排石剂；利尿剂 |A medicine or other substance that promotes the discharge of calculi (stones), especially from the kidneys or bladder. | Example 1: The doctor prescribed a lithagogue to help pass the kidney stone. 医生开了排石剂来帮助排出肾结石。

Example 2:  This herbal tea acts as a gentle lithagogue.  这种草药茶是一种温和的排石剂。|Synonym:  diuretic, antilithic |Antonym:  N/A |noun
