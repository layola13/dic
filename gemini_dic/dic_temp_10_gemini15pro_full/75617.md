fuger:/ˈfjuːdʒər/| n.|逃犯，躲避者|A person who flees or escapes, especially from justice or pursuit. |The fuger was apprehended after three months on the run.  这个逃犯在逃亡三个月后被捕。|近义词:escapee, runaway, fugitive|反义词:captive, prisoner|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fugere, from Anglo-Norman fugere, from Old French fugir (“to flee”), from Latin fugere (“to flee, run away, escape”).
