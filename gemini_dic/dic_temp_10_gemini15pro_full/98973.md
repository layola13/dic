inter-polishing:/ˌɪntərˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. |相互抛光，交互抛光|The process of polishing two or more surfaces against each other to achieve a smooth finish.| The gears require inter-polishing to ensure smooth operation. 齿轮需要相互抛光以确保平稳运转。|  inter-lapping, mutual polishing | N/A | adjective
Etymology: "inter-" (Latin, meaning "between, among, mutually") + "polishing" (from "polish," ultimately from Latin "polire," meaning "to smooth, refine").
