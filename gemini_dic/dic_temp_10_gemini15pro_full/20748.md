blanching:/ˈblɑːntʃɪŋ/ /ˈblæn(t)ʃɪŋ/| n. v.| 变白，焯水，|The process of briefly immersing food in boiling water, then in ice water, to stop cooking and preserve color and nutrients.|  She blanched the almonds before adding them to the cake. 她将杏仁焯水后再加入蛋糕中。|近义词：scalding, parboiling|反义词: browning, roasting|词性:名词/动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *blanchir "to whiten, wash," from blanc "white" (from Frankish *blank "white, shining," from Proto-Germanic *blankaz "white, bright").
