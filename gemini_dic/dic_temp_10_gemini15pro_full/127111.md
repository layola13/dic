nationalizing: /ˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈnæʃnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
国有化
The act of transferring privately owned businesses or industries to government control.
The government is nationalizing the railways. 政府正在将铁路国有化。
The government announced its plan for nationalizing key industries. 政府宣布了将关键产业国有化的计划。

Synonyms:  socializing, collectivizing, appropriating, expropriating, taking over
Antonyms: privatizing, denationalizing
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From nationalize + -ing. Nationalize comes from nation + -al + -ize. Nation traces back to Latin "natio" (race, breed, stock, kind, nation), from "nat-" (born) + "-io" (suffix forming nouns of action or condition).
