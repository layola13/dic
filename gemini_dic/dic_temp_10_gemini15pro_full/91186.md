hydrating:/ˈhaɪdreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
保湿的，补水的
Causing or promoting hydration; increasing the water content.
This face cream is very hydrating.  这款面霜非常保湿。
The athlete drank a hydrating sports drink after the marathon.  马拉松比赛后，运动员喝了补充水分的运动饮料。
近义词:moisturizing, replenishing
反义词:dehydrating, drying
词性:adjective

词源: From hydrate + -ing.  Hydrate comes from the Greek "hydōr" meaning water.
