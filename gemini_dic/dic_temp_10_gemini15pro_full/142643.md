ostracion:/ˌɒstrəˈseɪʃən/ /ˌɑːstrəˈseɪʃən/| n. |排斥，放逐；孤立|Exclusion, banishment; a state of being isolated.|例句：He suffered ostracion for his unconventional beliefs.  他因为自己非传统的信仰而遭受排斥。

例句：The ostracion of the whistleblower created a climate of fear in the company.  对告密者的排斥在公司内造成了一种恐惧的气氛。|近义词：exclusion, banishment, isolation, shunning|反义词:inclusion, acceptance, welcome|词性:noun

词源:From Latin *ōstrāciōn- (stem of ōstrāciō ) "voting by ostracism, banishment by ostracism," from Greek ostrakismos, from ostrakizein "banish by voting with potsherds," from ostrakon "tile, potsherd," literally "shell, earthenware."  来自拉丁语 *ōstrāciōn-（ōstrāciō 的词干）“通过陶片放逐投票，通过陶片放逐”，来自希腊语 ostrakismos，来自 ostrakizein “用陶片投票放逐”，来自 ostrakon “瓦片，陶片”，字面意思是“贝壳，陶器”。
