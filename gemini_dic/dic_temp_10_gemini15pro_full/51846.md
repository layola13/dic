dexterity:/dɛkˈstɛrɪti/ /dɛkˈstɛrəti/
n. | 灵巧，敏捷，熟练 | Skill and grace in physical movement, especially in using the hands; adroitness. | The surgeon showed surprising dexterity during the complex operation.  外科医生在复杂的手术中展现了惊人的灵巧。|  skill, adroitness, agility, deftness, proficiency | clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness
