bistre:/ˈbɪstər/ | n. adj.|褐色， bistre颜料|A brownish pigment made from the soot of wood, especially beechwood, and used in pen and wash drawings. Also: a yellowish-brown colour.|The artist used bistre to create a moody atmosphere in his landscape drawing. 艺术家用褐色颜料在他的风景画中营造了一种忧郁的氛围。|近义词：brown, sepia, umber|反义词: white, black |词性: 名词，形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bistre*, from Old French *bistre*, perhaps from a place name.
