competer:/kəmˈpiːtər/ (US), /kəmˈpɛtɪtər/ (UK)| n.|竞争者，参赛者|A person or organization that competes against others, especially in business or sports.|例句：Several competent competitors participated in the race.  几位有实力的参赛者参加了比赛。

例句：Our main competitor is launching a new product next month. 我们的主要竞争对手下个月将推出一款新产品。

近义词：rival, contender, contestant, opponent|反义词：ally, partner|词性：名词
