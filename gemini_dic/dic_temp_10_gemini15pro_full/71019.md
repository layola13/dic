firmer:/ˈfɜːrmər/ (US) /ˈfɜːmə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更坚固的，更坚定的，更稳定的|More firm; comparatively or relatively firm.|The mattress felt much firmer than her old one.  这床垫比她旧的要硬得多。|近义词：stronger, steadier, more stable, more solid|反义词：softer, weaker, less stable, less solid|词性:形容词
firmer:|n.|使牢固的人（或物）|Something that makes something else firmer.|Adding a firmer to the paint will help it last longer. 在油漆中加入固化剂可以使其更持久。|近义词：reinforcement, stabilizer|反义词：weakener|词性:名词
