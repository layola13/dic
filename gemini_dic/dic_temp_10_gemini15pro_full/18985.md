bestir:/bɪˈstɜːr/
verb
奋起；激励；行动起来
To rouse oneself to action; exert oneself.
He finally bestirred himself to go to work. 他终于鼓起劲去上班了。
近义词：rouse, awaken, stir, activate, energize
反义词：rest, relax, be idle, be still
词性: verb

bestir:/bɪˈstɜːr/
Etymology: From be- + stir.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely." "Stir" comes from Old English *styrian, meaning "to move, agitate."
