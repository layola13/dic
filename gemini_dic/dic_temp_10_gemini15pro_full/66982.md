expert:/ˈɛkspɜːrt/ (US), /ˈekspɜːt/ (UK)| n. adj.|专家，熟练的|A person who has a comprehensive and authoritative knowledge of or skill in a particular area.|She's an expert in 19th-century French literature. 她是一位19世纪法国文学专家。|近义词：specialist, professional, proficient, skilled, adept|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner|词性: 名词, 形容词
expert:/ˈɛkspɜːrt/ (US), /ˈekspɜːt/ (UK)| adj.|熟练的，专业的|Having or showing special skill or knowledge derived from training or experience.|He's an expert skier. 他是一位滑雪高手。|近义词：skilled, proficient, adept, masterful|反义词：unskilled, inept, clumsy|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *expert, past participle of experir “to test, try,” from Latin experiri “to try, test, experience,” from ex- “out of” + periri “to try.”
