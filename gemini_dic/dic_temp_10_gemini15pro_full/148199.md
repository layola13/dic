overshipments:/ˈoʊvərˈʃɪpmənts/
noun
超额装运；过量装运
the act of shipping more goods than ordered or required.
The overshipments resulted in a surplus of inventory. 超额装运导致库存过剩。
The company apologized for the overshipments and offered a refund. 公司为超额装运道歉并提供退款。
synonyms: excess shipments, surplus shipments
antonyms: undershipments, shortages
n.
