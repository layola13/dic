beatifical:/biːætˈɪfɪkəl/
| adj. |使人快乐的；幸福的；令人愉悦的|Making blessed or blissful; blissful.|The beatifical sunshine warmed her face. 阳光让她感到幸福。|近义词：blissful, happy, joyful, delightful|反义词：miserable, unhappy, sorrowful|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin beatificus, from Latin beatus ‘blessed’ + -ficus (from facere ‘to make’).
