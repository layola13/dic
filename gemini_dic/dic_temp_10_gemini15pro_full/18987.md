猛烈攻击；强烈抨击；以风暴袭击

The critics bestormed the actor for his poor performance. 批评家们猛烈抨击这位演员糟糕的表演。
The ship was bestormed by huge waves. 这艘船遭到巨浪的袭击。
近义词:attack, assail, bombard, batter
反义词:protect, defend, shield
词性:v.
