mercuric:/mərˈkjʊrɪk/
adjective
汞的；含汞的
of or containing mercury, especially in the divalent state.
Mercuric chloride is a highly toxic compound.  氯化汞是一种剧毒化合物。
近义词:  mercurous (sometimes), hydrargyric (obsolete)
反义词:  N/A
词性:  adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin mercuricus, from Latin mercurius ‘mercury’.
