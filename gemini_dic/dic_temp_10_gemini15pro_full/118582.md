mesotheca:/ˈmɛzəˌθikə/| n. |间皮，中皮|The thin membrane that lines the coelom (body cavity) of invertebrates such as mollusks and brachiopods.  It also forms the mesenteries that support the internal organs. |The mesotheca of the brachiopod was carefully examined under the microscope.| 近义词:mesentery (in some contexts)| 反义词: None| 词性: noun
