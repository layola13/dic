homelinesses:/ˈhoʊmlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
the quality or qualities of being homely; unattractiveness; plainness
The homelinesses of the furniture made the room feel cozy.  房间里家具的朴素使它感觉很舒适。
n.
the quality or state of being simple and unpretentious; homelike character; coziness
She appreciated the homelinesses of the small town. 她欣赏小镇的质朴。
近义词:simplicity, plainness, unpretentiousness, coziness
反义词:elegance, sophistication, beauty
