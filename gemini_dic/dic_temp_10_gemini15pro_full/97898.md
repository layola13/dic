innernesses:/ˈɪnərnɪsɪz/
n.
内心深处；本质；深层个性
The qualities that make up one's inner self; the essence of a person's character.
He explored the innernesses of his soul through meditation. 他通过冥想探索自己灵魂的深处。
inner being, core, essence
exteriority, superficiality
