absorbedly:/əbˈzɔːrbidli/ /əbˈsɔːrbidli/
adverb
全神贯注地，专心地
In a manner showing that one's attention is completely engaged.
She listened absorbedly to the music. 她全神贯注地听着音乐。

He worked absorbedly on the project, oblivious to the passing of time. 他专心致志地做着这个项目，忘记了时间的流逝。

近义词: intently, fixedly, raptly, attentively
反义词: distractedly, inattentively
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From absorb (吸收) + -ed (过去分词后缀) + -ly (副词后缀).  Absorb comes from Latin absorbere "to suck up, swallow down," from ab- "away" + sorbere "to suck in."

USAGE->
She listened absorbedly as the speaker described her journey. = 演讲者描述她的旅程时，她听得全神贯注。
The children watched the magician absorbedly, captivated by his tricks. = 孩子们全神贯注地看着魔术师，被他的戏法迷住了。


PHRASE->
