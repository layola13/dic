outweary: /ˌaʊtˈwɪri/
verb
使极度疲乏，使筋疲力尽
to make someone extremely tired
The long journey outwearied the travellers. 漫长的旅程使旅行者筋疲力尽。

近义词: exhaust, fatigue, tire, wear out
反义词: refresh, energize, invigorate
词性: verb
