deregulatory:/diːˌreɡjʊləˈtɔːri/
adjective
放松管制的；解除管制的
Relating to or promoting deregulation; the removal or reduction of government regulations in a particular industry.
The deregulatory policies of the 1980s led to increased competition in the airline industry. 20世纪80年代的放松管制政策导致航空业竞争加剧。
The government implemented deregulatory measures to stimulate economic growth. 政府实施放松管制措施以刺激经济增长。
近义词:liberalizing, freeing
反义词:regulatory, controlling
词性:adjective
