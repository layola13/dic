cryohydrate:/ˈkraɪoʊˈhaɪdreɪt/| n. |水合物；冰状包合物|A compound formed by the combination of water with another substance, in which the water molecules are incorporated into the crystal structure.|Methane cryohydrates are found on the ocean floor. 甲烷水合物存在于海底。|Synonyms: hydrate, clathrate|Antonyms: anhydrous compound|noun
