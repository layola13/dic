cooper's:/ˈkuːpərz/| n.|库珀的；制桶匠的；桶匠的|A possessive form of cooper, indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with a cooper (a person who makes or repairs barrels, casks, and other wooden containers) or a person named Cooper.|例句：This is Cooper's workshop. 这是库珀的工作坊。

例句：We went to Cooper's house for dinner. 我们去库珀家吃晚饭了。|近义词: barrel maker's, cask maker's |反义词: None|词性:Possessive noun
