lobbying: /ˈlɑːbiɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɒbiɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|游说，疏通|The act of attempting to influence decisions made by officials in the government, most often legislators or members of regulatory agencies.|The tobacco industry's lobbying efforts have been successful in delaying anti-smoking legislation. 烟草行业的游说工作成功地推迟了反吸烟立法的实施。|synonyms: advocacy, persuasion, influence peddling|antonyms: opposition, protest, resistance|noun

lobbying: /ˈlɑːbiɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlɒbiɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|游说|Present participle of lobby|They are lobbying for stricter gun control laws. 他们在游说更严格的枪支管制法。|synonyms: advocate, persuade, influence|antonyms: oppose, protest, resist|verb
