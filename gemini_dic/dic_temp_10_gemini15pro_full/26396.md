by-the-way:/baɪ ðə weɪ/ | adv. | 顺便提一下；附带说一句 | Used to introduce a new topic or piece of information, often something that is not completely relevant to the main topic of conversation. | Example: By the way, did you hear about John's new job?  顺便一提，你听说约翰的新工作了吗？ | Example: I'm going to the store. By the way, do you need anything? 我要去商店。顺便问一下，你需要什么吗？ | Synonym: incidentally, parenthetically | Antonym: purposefully, intentionally | adverb

incidentally:/ɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/ | adv. | 顺便提一下；附带说一句 |  Used to add a remark unconnected to the main topic of discussion. | Example: Incidentally, I saw your brother yesterday. 顺便说一下，我昨天看到你弟弟了。 | Example: Incidentally, have you heard from John lately? 顺便问一下，你最近有约翰的消息吗？| Synonym: by the way, parenthetically | Antonym:  purposefully, pointedly | adverb
