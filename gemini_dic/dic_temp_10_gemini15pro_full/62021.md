enarched:/ɪˈnɑːrtʃt/
adjective
拱形的，弓形的
formed into an arch or arches.
The cathedral has beautiful enarched doorways.  大教堂有美丽的拱形门口。
Synonyms: arched, curved, bowed
Antonyms: straight, flat
词性: 形容词
