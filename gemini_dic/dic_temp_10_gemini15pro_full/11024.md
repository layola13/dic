arcade:/ɑːrˈkeɪd/ | n. |拱廊，游乐中心，商场|A covered passageway with arches along one or both sides, often with shops. A place with many coin-operated video games or other amusement machines.|We strolled through the arcade, browsing the various shops. 我们在拱廊里漫步，浏览着各种各样的商店。|The children spent hours playing games at the arcade. 孩子们在游戏厅玩了好几个小时。|Synonyms: passageway, galleria, mall, game room |Antonyms: open area, street|noun


arcade:/ɑːrˈkeɪd/ | adj. |拱廊式的|Of or relating to an arcade.|The arcade building housed a variety of businesses. 这座拱廊式的建筑容纳了各种各样的企业。|The hotel featured an arcade walkway leading to the pool. 这家酒店有一条通往游泳池的拱廊式走道。|Synonyms: arched, covered|Antonyms: open, uncovered|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the French word "arcade," which itself comes from the Italian "arcata," meaning "arch," ultimately derived from the Latin "arcus," also meaning "arch."
