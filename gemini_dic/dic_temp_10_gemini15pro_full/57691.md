drawloom:/ˈdrɔːluːm/
noun
提花织机
A loom for weaving figured fabrics, in which the warp threads are lifted in various combinations by a mechanical device controlled by a pattern.
The intricate tapestry was created on a drawloom.  这块复杂的挂毯是在提花织机上创作的。
Synonyms: Jacquard loom, figured loom
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps simple loom or handloom as less complex alternatives)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'draw' (Old English dragan, "to pull, drag") + 'loom' (Old English geloma, "tool, implement").  The name refers to the mechanism that "draws" or lifts the warp threads.
