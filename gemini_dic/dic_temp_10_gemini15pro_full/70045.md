fetterless:/ˈfɛtərlɪs/
adjective
无拘无束的，自由自在的
Free from fetters (chains or restraints); unrestrained.
The fetterless spirit of the wild horses roamed the plains. 野马无拘无束的精神在平原上驰骋。
近义词:free, unrestrained, unbound, unconstrained, liberated
反义词:constrained, restricted, bound, shackled, fettered
词性:adjective

fetter:/ˈfɛtər/
noun
脚镣；束缚；羁绊
A chain or shackle placed on the feet of a prisoner or animal; something that confines or restrains.
The prisoner's fetters prevented him from escaping.  囚犯的脚镣使他无法逃跑。
近义词:chains, shackles, restraints, bonds, constraints
反义词:freedom, liberty, liberation
词性:noun

fetter:/ˈfɛtər/
verb
束缚；羁绊
To restrain with fetters; to confine or restrict.
His creativity was fettered by the strict rules.  严格的规则束缚了他的创造力。
近义词:restrain, confine, restrict, bind, shackle
反义词:free, liberate, release, unbind
词性:verb
