nonnarcissism:/ˌnɒnˈnɑːsɪzɪzəm/ | n. | 非自恋 | The state or quality of not being narcissistic; lack of self-obsession.  |  Her nonnarcissism was refreshing in an industry full of ego. 在一个充满自我膨胀的行业里，她的非自恋令人耳目一新。 | 近义词:  humility, modesty, selflessness | 反义词: narcissism, egotism, self-centeredness | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + narcissism.  "Narcissism" comes from the Greek myth of Narcissus, who fell in love with his own reflection.  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
