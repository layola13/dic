curveball:/ˈkɜːrvˌbɔːl/ (US) /ˈkɜːvˌbɔːl/ (UK)| n.|曲线球；难预测的问题；意外的难题|A baseball pitch thrown with a spin so that its path curves as it approaches the batter.  Figuratively, something unexpected and difficult to deal with.|The batter wasn't expecting the curveball and struck out.  击球手没料到曲线球，结果三振出局了。|近义词：surprise, twist, challenge|反义词: predictability, expectation|noun
