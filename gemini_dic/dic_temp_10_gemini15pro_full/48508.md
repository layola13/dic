deerhound:/ˈdɪərhaʊnd/| n.|猎鹿犬|A tall, rough-coated sighthound bred for hunting deer.|例句：The deerhound gracefully pursued the stag across the open moor.  猎鹿犬优雅地在开阔的荒野上追逐雄鹿。

例句：Queen Victoria was a known admirer of deerhounds. 维多利亚女王是猎鹿犬的著名仰慕者。|近义词：Scottish deerhound, wolfhound|反义词: (No direct antonym for a breed of dog.  Could consider "lapdog" as a contrasting breed.)|词性:noun
