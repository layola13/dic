fiscally:/ˈfɪskəli/
adjective
财政上地
Relating to government revenue, especially taxes.  Concerning public finances.
The government is fiscally responsible. 政府的财政状况良好。
The project is fiscally sound. 这个项目在财政上是可靠的。
近义词：financially, economically
反义词：N/A
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *fiscalis, from fiscus (“public treasury, purse”), from Latin fiscus (“money basket, purse”).
