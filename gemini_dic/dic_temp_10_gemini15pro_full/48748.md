definiteness:/ˈdɛfɪnɪtnəs/| n.|明确性，确定性|The state or quality of being definite; precision.|The definiteness of his answer surprised me. 他的回答的明确性让我感到惊讶。|近义词：certainty, precision, clarity, distinctness|反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, indefiniteness, uncertainty|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From definite + -ness.  "definite" comes from Late Latin *defīnītus, past participle of Lat. dēfīnīre "to limit, define," from de- "completely" + fīnīre "to bound, limit," from fīnis "end, boundary."
