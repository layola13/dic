antiexpansionism:/ˌæntiɪkˈspænʃənɪzəm/
n.
反扩张主义
Opposition to expansion, especially territorial expansion of a country.
The senator's antiexpansionism made him unpopular with those who favored aggressive foreign policy.  参议员的反扩张主义立场使他不受那些支持侵略性外交政策的人的欢迎。
近义词：noninterventionism, isolationism
反义词：expansionism, imperialism
词性：名词
