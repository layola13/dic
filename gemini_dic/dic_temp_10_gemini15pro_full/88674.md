hoaxed:/hoʊkst/ | v. | 欺骗，捉弄 | To trick or deceive someone with a made-up story or elaborate prank. |  He hoaxed the media with a fake story about a UFO sighting. 他用一个关于UFO目击的假故事欺骗了媒体。

The artist hoaxed the art world with a series of forged paintings.  这位艺术家用一系列伪造的画作欺骗了艺术界。

近义词: tricked, deceived, fooled, duped, conned | 反义词: exposed, debunked | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "hoax" is uncertain, but it possibly comes from "hocus," a shortened form of "hocus pocus," a term used by magicians.
