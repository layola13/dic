outedged:/ˌaʊtˈɛdʒd/
|v.|胜过，优于，略胜一筹|To surpass or defeat by a narrow margin, especially in a competitive situation.|Our team outedged their rivals in the final seconds of the game. 我们的团队在比赛的最后几秒钟险胜对手。|
|v.|具有…优势;略胜一筹|To have a slight advantage over someone or something.|The company outedged its competitors by offering better customer service.  该公司通过提供更好的客户服务略胜于竞争对手。|
近义词:surpass, outdo, outperform, eclipse, better
反义词:lose to, be defeated by
词性:动词
