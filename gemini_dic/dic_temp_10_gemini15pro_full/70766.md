finesse:/fɪˈnes/ | v. n. | 精巧地处理；策略；技巧 | To handle a situation or problem in a skillful and delicate way; subtle skill in handling difficult or delicate situations. |  She finessed the difficult negotiation with grace and skill. 她以优雅和技巧巧妙地完成了艰难的谈判。

The diplomat finessed the border dispute between the two countries.  这位外交官巧妙地解决了这两个国家之间的边界争端。 | 近义词: manipulate, maneuver, contrive, strategize | 反义词: bungle, mishandle, blunder | 词性: verb, noun

finesse:/fɪˈnes/ | n. | 精巧；技巧；策略 | Subtle skill in handling difficult or delicate situations; refinement and delicacy of performance, execution, or artisanship. |  The pianist played the piece with great finesse.  这位钢琴家以极高的技巧演奏了这首曲子。

He admired the finesse with which she handled the crisis. 他钦佩她处理危机时的技巧。 | 近义词: skill, dexterity, artistry, subtlety, tact, diplomacy | 反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucherie | 词性: noun
