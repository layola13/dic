bandage:/ˈbændɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈbændɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|包扎|To cover or bind with a bandage.|The nurse bandaged his arm. 护士包扎了他的手臂。|近义词：dress, wrap, bind|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 uncover  揭开)|verb


词源: From Old French *bandage (unattested), from bande "band, strip", from Frankish *binda, from Proto-Germanic *bindaną "to bind."
