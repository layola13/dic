overrigorousness:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪɡərəsnəs/
| n. | 过于严谨; 过分严格 | The state of being excessively rigorous or strict. | His overrigorousness in applying the rules alienated his staff. 他在应用规则方面过于严谨，疏远了他的员工。|  Example 2: The overrigorousness of the experiment's design led to inconclusive results. 实验设计过于严谨导致结果不确定。 |  Synonyms:  excessiveness, strictness, rigidity, inflexibility | Antonyms:  leniency, laxity, flexibility, tolerance | noun
