nonsubjected:/nɒn.səbˈdʒektɪd/
adjective
未受…支配的；未屈服于…的
not subjected to something; not having been made to experience or suffer something unpleasant or difficult
The report detailed the experiences of nonsubjected populations.  这份报告详细描述了未受压迫人群的经历。
The nonsubjected tribes maintained their traditional way of life. 未受征服的部落保持着他们的传统生活方式。
近义词:independent, autonomous, free, liberated
反义词:subjected, subordinate, oppressed, controlled
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + subjected, the past participle of the verb subject.  "Subject" comes from the Latin word "subjectus," meaning "placed under."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning of the root word.
