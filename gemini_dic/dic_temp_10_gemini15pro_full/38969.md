confine:/ˈkɒnfaɪn/ | v. n. |限制，局限；禁闭；分娩|To keep someone or something within limits; to restrict something to a particular place or situation.  A boundary or limit.|The illness confined him to his house.  这种病使他只能待在家里。|近义词：restrict, limit, imprison, restrain|反义词：liberate, free, release|词性:动词,名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *confiner*, from Latin confinis (“bordering upon, having a common boundary”), from con- (“together”) + finis (“end, boundary, limit”).
