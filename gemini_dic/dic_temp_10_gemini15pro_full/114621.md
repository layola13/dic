malformation:/ˌmælfɔːrˈmeɪʃn/
n.
畸形，缺陷
A malformation is a defect or deformity in the structure of a body part.
The baby was born with a heart malformation.  这个婴儿出生时患有心脏畸形。
The radiation caused malformations in the developing fetus. 辐射导致发育中的胎儿畸形。
近义词：deformity, defect, abnormality
反义词：normality
