literalize: /ˈlɪtərəlaɪz/ | v. | 使字面化；逐字解释 | To interpret something literally or word for word, often without regard to the intended meaning or context. |  He tends to literalize everything, so metaphors and similes are lost on him. 他倾向于逐字解释一切，所以隐喻和明喻对他来说毫无意义。| Synonyms: explicate, interpret literally | Antonyms: interpret figuratively, symbolize | verb

literalize: /ˈlɪtərəlaɪz/ | v. | 逐字翻译；照字面意义理解 | To translate something word for word without considering the overall meaning or context. | The translator literalized the poem, losing its beauty and nuance. 译者逐字翻译了这首诗，失去了它的美感和细微之处。 | Synonyms: translate literally, render verbatim | Antonyms: paraphrase, interpret freely | verb
