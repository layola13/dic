ballister:/ˈbælɪstər/ (US), /ˈbælɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|栏杆，扶手，(弩或投石器的) 横梁|A short post or pillar, typically one of a series supporting a rail or coping.|The grand staircase featured intricately carved balusters.  这座宏伟的楼梯以精雕细琢的栏杆为特色。|Synonyms: railing, banister, balustrade|Antonyms: none|noun

ballister:/ˈbælɪstər/ (US), /ˈbælɪstə(r)/ (UK)| v.|用栏杆围护|To provide with balusters.|They ballistered the deck for safety. 他们为了安全给甲板装上了栏杆。|Synonyms: rail, fence|Antonyms: none|verb
