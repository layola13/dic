bewailer:/bɪˈweɪlər/| n. | 哀悼者；恸哭者 |A person who expresses great sadness or disappointment, often in a very vocal way. | She was a constant bewailer of her misfortunes. 她总是不断地哀叹自己的不幸。| The villagers were bewailers of the lost harvest. 村民们都在为失去的收成而恸哭。|  mourner, lamenter, griever | comforter, celebrant | noun
