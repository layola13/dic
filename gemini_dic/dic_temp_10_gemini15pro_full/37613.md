communicatory:/kəˈmjuːnɪkətɔːri/
adjective
交际的；表达的；传达的
Relating to or used for communication.
Her communicatory skills are excellent, making her a successful negotiator. 她的沟通技巧非常出色，使她成为一名成功的谈判者。

The teacher employed various communicatory activities in the classroom. 老师在课堂上运用了各种交际活动。
近义词: communicative, expressive
反义词: uncommunicative, inarticulate
词性: adjective
