codexes:/ˈkoʊˌdɛksiːz/
n.
法典；手抄本；药典
An ancient manuscript text in book form.  A collection of rules or regulations.
This library houses several ancient codexes.  这个图书馆藏有几部古代法典。
The pharmaceutical codex lists all approved medications. 药典列出了所有批准的药物。
synonyms: manuscripts, volumes, compilations, books, collections
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Could consider "individual pages," "fragments," or for content, "disorganized information")
Etymology: From Latin "codex," meaning "tree trunk, block of wood, book."  Originally referred to wooden tablets covered in wax used for writing.
