brachiolaria:/brækiəˈlɑːriə/
n.|腕幼虫|A free-swimming larval stage in many starfishes, characterized by three adhesive arms and a ciliated band.|The brachiolaria larva eventually settles on the seabed and metamorphoses into a juvenile starfish.|近义词: larva, bipinnaria|反义词: adult (starfish)|词性: Noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *brachiolaria*, from Latin *brachium* "arm" + *-aria* (suffix forming nouns).
