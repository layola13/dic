inter-layering:/ˌɪntərˈleɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 交错层叠，夹层，层间 | The process of placing something between layers of another material. | The cake was made with inter-layering of sponge and cream.  蛋糕是用海绵蛋糕和奶油交错层叠制成的。|  The geologist studied the inter-layering of sedimentary rocks. 地质学家研究了沉积岩的层间结构。 | layering, stratification | delamination | noun

inter-layering:/ˌɪntərˈleɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 交错层叠的 | Describing something that is arranged in inter-layers. |  The inter-layering design of the fabric provided extra warmth. 这种交错层叠设计的布料提供了额外的保暖性。 | layered, stratified | homogeneous | adjective
