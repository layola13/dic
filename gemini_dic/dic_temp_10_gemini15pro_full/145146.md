overassertively:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪvli/
adverb|过于自信地，过分武断地|In a manner that is too confident and forceful, often to the point of being aggressive or disrespectful.|She overassertively interrupted the speaker, causing a disruption in the flow of the presentation. 她过于自信地打断了发言者，扰乱了演示的流程。|Synonym: aggressively, pushily, domineeringly|Antonym: submissively, timidly, passively|词性: 副词

overassertive:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪv/
adjective|过于自信的，过分武断的|Too confident and forceful, often to the point of being aggressive or disrespectful.|His overassertive behavior alienated his colleagues. 他过分自信的行为疏远了他的同事。|Synonym: aggressive, pushy, domineering|Antonym: submissive, timid, passive|词性: 形容词


词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "too much") + "assert" (verb meaning "to state confidently") + "-ive" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs)
