nonpersuasive:/ˌnɒnpəˈsweɪsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈsweɪsɪv/| adj.|没有说服力的，不具劝导性的|Not able to persuade someone to do or believe something.|His nonpersuasive arguments failed to sway the jury. 他没有说服力的论证没能左右陪审团。|The nonpersuasive presentation left the investors unconvinced.  没有说服力的演示让投资者们不信服。|近义词：unconvincing, ineffective, weak|反义词：persuasive, convincing, compelling|词性:形容词

词源:

From non- + persuasive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "persuasive" comes from the Latin word "persuadere," meaning "to persuade."
