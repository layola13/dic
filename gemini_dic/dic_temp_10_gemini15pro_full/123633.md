montoir:/mɔ̃.twaʁ/ (French pronunciation)| n.|上马台，骑马凳|A raised platform or block used to assist in mounting a horse.|The groom brought the montoir so the lady could mount her horse easily.  马夫拿来了上马台，以便女士可以轻松地上马。|例句：The antique montoir was ornately carved.  这古老的上马台雕刻精美。|近义词：mounting block, horse block|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *montoir*, from *monter* "to mount," from Vulgar Latin *montare*, from Latin *mons*, *montis* "mountain".
