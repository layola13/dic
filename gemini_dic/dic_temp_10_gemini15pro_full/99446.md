interestednesses:/ˈɪntrəstɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
利害关系；利益攸关
The state of having interests or concerns, especially in a particular matter.
Their interestednesses in the project were clear. 他们在该项目中的利害关系显而易见。
近义词:interests, stakes, concerns
反义词:apathy, indifference
词性:名词
