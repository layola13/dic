interceptor:/ˈɪntərˌseptər/| n.|拦截器；截击机；拦截导弹|A thing or person that intercepts someone or something.|The fighter jet acted as an interceptor, preventing the enemy bomber from reaching its target. 这架战斗机充当拦截器，阻止敌方轰炸机到达目标。|synonyms: blocker, defender, interrupter|antonyms: facilitator, enabler|noun

intercept:/ˌɪntərˈsept/| vt.|拦截；截取；截断|Obstruct (someone or something) so as to prevent them from continuing to a destination.|The police intercepted a shipment of drugs. 警方截获了一批毒品。|synonyms: catch, seize, stop|antonyms: release, allow, permit|verb
