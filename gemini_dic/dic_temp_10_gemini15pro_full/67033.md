explanate: /ˈɛkspləˌneɪt/ | v. | 解释；说明；阐明 | To make plain or clear; explain in detail. | He explanated the complex theory in simple terms. 他用简单的术语解释了这个复杂的理论。| The professor explanated the historical background of the war. 教授解释了这场战争的历史背景。 | 近义词: explain, clarify, elucidate, interpret | 反义词: confuse, obscure, complicate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin explānātus, past participle of explānāre (“to make plain or clear, explain”), from ex- (“out”) + plānus (“plain, level, clear”).
