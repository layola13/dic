hard-rocker:/ˈhɑːrd ˈrɒkər/ (US) /ˈhɑːrd ˈrɒkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |硬摇滚乐手；硬摇滚乐迷|A musician or fan of hard rock music, a subgenre of rock music characterized by aggressive vocals and distorted electric guitars.|例句：The hard-rocker wore a leather jacket and ripped jeans.  这位硬摇滚乐迷穿着皮夹克和破洞牛仔裤。|近义词：metalhead, headbanger|反义词:pop fan, folk music enthusiast|词性:noun
