outdoors:/ˌaʊtˈdɔːrz/ /ˌaʊtˈdɔːrs/| adv.  n. |户外，野外|being or relating to the open air; outside a building|  They sell outdoor clothing and equipment. 他们出售户外服装和设备。|近义词：exterior, external|反义词:interior, internal|词性:形容词 (rarely used)


词源: From out + doors, originally a noun phrase meaning "out of doors."  The adverbial use developed from this phrase.
