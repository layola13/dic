nonvirulently:/ˌnɒnˈvɪrʊləntli/
adverb
无毒地；非毒性地
in a nonvirulent manner; without virulence.
The bacteria was cultured nonvirulently in the lab.  这种细菌在实验室里以非毒性的方式培养。
The modified virus acted nonvirulently, causing no harm to the host.  改良后的病毒表现为非毒性，对宿主没有造成伤害。

近义词: harmlessly, innocuously, safely
反义词: virulently, toxically, harmfully
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  非，相反的
virulent (adjective): extremely severe or harmful in its effects.  剧毒的；恶性的
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives.  副词后缀
