continent:/ˈkɒntɪnənt/ (US) /ˈkɑːntɪnənt/ (UK)| n. |大陆，洲|A large, continuous area of land, especially one considered as a collective region geographically or politically.|例句：Asia is the largest continent. 亚洲是最大的洲。

例句：There are seven continents in the world. 世界上有七大洲。


近义词：landmass, mainland|反义词:island, archipelago|词性:noun
