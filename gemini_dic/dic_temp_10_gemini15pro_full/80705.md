goriest:/ˈɡɔːriɪst/ /ˈɡoʊriɪst/| adj.|最血腥的，最残忍的|The goriest scene in the movie was the final battle.  这部电影中最血腥的场景是最后的战斗。|例句：This is the goriest horror movie I've ever seen. 这是我看过的最血腥的恐怖电影。|近义词：bloodiest, gruesome, violent|反义词:peaceful, pleasant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *gory* + *-est*.  *Gory* comes from Old English *gōrig*, meaning "covered in gore," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gauraz*, "filth, dirt."
