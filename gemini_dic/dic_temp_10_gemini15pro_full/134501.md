nonlegumes:/nɒnˈlɛɡjuːmz/ | n. | 非豆科植物 | Plants that are not members of the legume family (Fabaceae).  These plants do not have nitrogen-fixing bacteria in their root nodules and therefore do not contribute to soil nitrogen enrichment in the same way legumes do. |  Many gardeners rotate nonlegumes with legumes to maintain soil health. 许多园丁将非豆科植物与豆科植物轮作以保持土壤健康。| synonyms: non-leguminous plants | antonyms: legumes, leguminous plants | noun
