caregiving:/ˈkerˌɡɪvɪŋ/ | n. |照护；护理；照顾 | The act of providing care and support to someone who needs it, such as an elderly person, a child, or someone with a disability. |  She found caregiving for her elderly mother to be both rewarding and challenging. 她发现照顾年迈的母亲既有回报又有挑战性。|  My grandmother needed constant caregiving in her final years.  我祖母在最后几年需要持续的照顾。| 近义词: care, support, assistance, nursing | 反义词: neglect | 词性: 名词
