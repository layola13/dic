catalepsy:/ˈkætəˌlepsi/| n.|僵住症，木僵|A medical condition characterized by a trance-like state, with loss of voluntary motion and failure to react to stimuli.|The patient experienced a catalepsy during the hypnosis session.  这个病人在催眠过程中出现了僵住症。|Synonyms: trance, rigidity, immobility|Antonyms: mobility, flexibility|noun

catalepses:/ˈkætəˌlepˌsiz/| n.|僵住症（复数）|The plural form of catalepsy, referring to multiple instances of the condition.|The doctor documented several catalepses in the patient's history.  医生记录了病人历史上几次僵住症的发作。|Synonyms: trances, rigidities, immobilities|Antonyms: mobilities, flexibilities|noun
