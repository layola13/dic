overextrapolation: /ˌəʊvərɪkˈstræpəleɪʃn/ | n. | 过度外推，过度推断 |  The act of inferring or concluding something beyond the range of what is known or justified by the available data.  Projecting trends too far beyond the data set used to create them. |  His prediction of a million-dollar profit by next year seems like an overextrapolation based on current sales figures. 他预测明年利润将达到一百万美元，根据目前的销售数据来看，这似乎是过度推断。 | overgeneralization, overestimation |  underestimation, interpolation | noun
