evensong:/ˈiːvənsɒŋ/ (US) /ˈiːvnsɒŋ/ (UK)| n.|晚祷，晚课|A service of evening prayer, especially in the Anglican Church.  It corresponds to Vespers in the Roman Catholic and Eastern Orthodox churches.|The choir sang a beautiful evensong.|Synonyms: vespers, evening prayer|Antonyms: matins, morning prayer|noun
