oxygenic:/ˌɒksɪˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
产氧的; 氧气的
Producing oxygen; relating to oxygen.
Photosynthesis is the main source of oxygenic respiration. 光合作用是产氧呼吸的主要来源。

Cyanobacteria are oxygenic photosynthetic bacteria. 蓝细菌是产氧光合细菌。
synonym: aerobic, air-breathing
antonym: anaerobic, anoxic
词性: adjective
