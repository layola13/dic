consonantally:/ˌkɑːnsəˈnæn(t)əli/
adv.
辅音地; 就辅音而言
Relating to or consisting of consonants; in a manner characterized by consonants.
The two words are consonantally similar but differ in their vowels. 这两个词辅音相似，但元音不同。
近义词: phonetically
反义词: vocally
词性: 副词
