goslings:/ˈɡɒzlɪŋz/ (UK), /ˈɡɑːzlɪŋz/ (US)| n. |小鹅，幼鹅|A young goose.  A gosling is a young goose, typically from hatching until it grows its adult plumage.|例句：The goslings followed their mother closely. 小鹅们紧紧地跟着它们的妈妈。

例句：The farmer protected the goslings from predators.  农夫保护小鹅免受捕食者的伤害。


|近义词：young geese, chicks (more general)|反义词:adult geese|词性:noun
