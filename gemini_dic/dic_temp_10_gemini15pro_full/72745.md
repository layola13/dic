foge:/foʊdʒ/ | n. | 浓雾，烟雾，瘴气 | A thick cloud of smoke or vapor, especially one that obscures visibility. | The battlefield was shrouded in a dense foge. 战场被浓雾笼罩。| 近义词：fog, mist, haze | 反义词：clearness, clarity | 词性：名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *fogge*, perhaps from Old Norse *fok* (“drifting snow, spray”).  Cognate with Icelandic *fok* (“drifting snow”), Norwegian *fok* (“drifting snow”).  The verb sense probably arose from the noun.  Compare also fog (verb).
