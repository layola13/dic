on-the-ground:/ˌɒn ðə ˈɡraʊnd/ /ˌɑːn ðə ˈɡraʊnd/| n.|实地情况，现场情况|The situation as it really is in a particular place.|例句：The on-the-ground reality is quite different.  现场的实际情况截然不同。|近义词：reality, situation, state of affairs|反义词：theory, speculation|词性:名词
