ferociously:/fəˈroʊʃəsli/
adverb
凶猛地，残暴地
in a ferocious manner; savagely
The lion attacked ferociously. 狮子凶猛地发起了攻击。
近义词:savagely, fiercely, violently, brutally
反义词:gently, mildly, calmly, peacefully
词性:副词

ferociously:/fəˈroʊʃəsli/
adverb
激烈地，猛烈地
intensely or extremely
The fire burned ferociously. 火势非常凶猛。
近义词:intensely, extremely, vehemently, powerfully
反义词:mildly, moderately, gently, softly
词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ferōciōsus, from Latin ferōx (“fierce, wild, savage”) + -iōsus.
