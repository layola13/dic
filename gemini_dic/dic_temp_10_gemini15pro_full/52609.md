digestible:/daɪˈdʒɛstəbl/ /dɪˈdʒɛstɪbl/
adjective
易消化的; 可理解的
Easily digested or understood.
The food was light and digestible.  食物清淡易消化。
The information was presented in a clear and digestible format.  信息以清晰易懂的格式呈现。
近义词: palatable, easy to understand, comprehensible
反义词: indigestible, incomprehensible
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin *digestibilis, from Latin digest- ‘digested’, from the verb digerere (see digest).
