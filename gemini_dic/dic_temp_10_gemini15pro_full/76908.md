ganoidei:/ˌɡæˈnɔɪdiˌaɪ/| n.| 硬鳞鱼纲|A group of primitive ray-finned fishes characterized by ganoid scales.  A largely extinct group of bony fishes with thick, bony scales, including the garpikes, sturgeons, and paddlefishes. |Example 1: Ganoidei were once a dominant group of fishes.  硬鳞鱼纲曾经是鱼类中的一个优势群体。
