offendedness:/əˈfɛndɪdnɪs/| n.|冒犯；被冒犯的状态；恼怒|The offendedness in her voice was clear. 她声音里的恼怒很明显。|例句：His offendedness at her remarks was palpable. 他对她的言论感到非常生气。

例句：Her offendedness subsided as she realized he hadn't meant to be rude. 当她意识到他不是故意粗鲁的时候，她的怒气消了。|近义词：resentment, indignation, umbrage|反义词:pleasure, delight|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From offended + -ness.  Offended comes from the Old French *offendre, ultimately from Latin offendere "to strike against, stumble, displease."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
