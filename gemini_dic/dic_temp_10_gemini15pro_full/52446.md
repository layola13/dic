diecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/
adjective
雌雄异株的
Having male and female reproductive organs in separate plants or animals.
Holly trees are diecious, meaning that some trees have only male flowers and others have only female flowers. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着有些树只有雄花，而另一些树只有雌花。

The ginkgo tree is a diecious species, with separate male and female trees. 银杏树是雌雄异株的物种，雄树和雌树是分开的。

synonym: dioecious
antonym: monoecious
词性: adjective
