echopathy:/ˌekəˈpɑːθi/ /ˌeɪkəˈpæθi/ | n. |回声病，模仿言语|The involuntary repetition of words or phrases spoken by another person.  This can be a symptom of certain neurological conditions.| Example:  The doctor observed echopathy in the patient during the neurological examination. 医生在神经检查期间观察到病人的回声病。

Example:  Her echopathy was a clear sign of her advancing dementia. 她的回声病是她痴呆症加重的明显迹象。 |Synonym: echolalia|Antonym: (No direct antonym exists, but one might consider "independent speech" or "original thought")|noun
