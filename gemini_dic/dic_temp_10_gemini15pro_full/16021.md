n.
巴纳特地区
A historical region in Central Europe, now divided between western Romania, eastern Serbia, and a small part of southeastern Hungary.
The Banat is known for its diverse cultural heritage. 巴纳特地区以其多元的文化遗产而闻名。
The fertile plains of the Banat are ideal for agriculture.  巴纳特的肥沃平原是农业的理想之地。
