drollingly:/ˈdrəʊlɪŋli/ | adv. | 痴迷地，傻笑着地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive or foolish fondness or admiration; often with a silly grin. | He looked at her drollingly, clearly smitten. 他痴迷地看着她，显然是被迷住了。| 近义词: adoringly, fondly, lovingly, foolishly | 反义词: disapprovingly, critically, scornfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "drool" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Drool" originates from the Middle Dutch "drōlen," meaning "to drivel."
