gigaton:/ˈɡɪɡətʌn/ | n. |十亿吨|A unit of mass equal to one billion (10^9) tons, often used to measure greenhouse gas emissions.|The annual global CO2 emissions are estimated to be over 36 gigatons. 全球每年的二氧化碳排放量估计超过 360 亿吨。|近义词:billion tons|反义词: (no direct antonym, consider units like kiloton, megaton)|词性:noun
