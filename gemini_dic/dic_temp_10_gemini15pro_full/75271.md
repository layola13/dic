frons:/frɒnz/ (BrE) /frɑːnz/ (AmE)| n. |额头；前面；表面|The area of the face above the eyes and below the hairline; the forehead; also, a similar surface on an insect or other arthropod.|The frons of the beetle was a shiny metallic green. 甲虫的额部是闪亮的金属绿色。|Synonyms: forehead, brow|Antonyms: occiput, nape|noun

frons:/frɒnz/ (BrE) /frɑːnz/ (AmE)| n. |叶面|The surface of a leaf.|The frons of the fern was covered in spores. 蕨类植物的叶面覆盖着孢子。|Synonyms: leaf surface, blade|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps underside or abaxial surface)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frōns*, forehead, front.
