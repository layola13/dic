parry:/ˈpæri/ (US), /ˈpɑːri/ (UK)| v. n. | 挡开，避开（打击、问题等）；回避；抵挡 | To ward off (a blow, thrust, or the like) usually by deflecting or turning it aside; to evade or turn aside something. |  He parried the blow with his sword. 他用剑挡开了那一击。 | synonyms: deflect, block, avert, evade, fend off | antonyms: accept, receive, yield, submit | verb

parry:/ˈpæri/ (US), /ˈpɑːri/ (UK)| n. | 挡开，避开 | The act of parrying. | His parry was swift and precise. 他的抵挡迅速而精准。 | synonyms: deflection, block, evasion | antonyms: acceptance, submission | noun
