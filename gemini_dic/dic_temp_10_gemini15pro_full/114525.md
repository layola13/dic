It can also be used affectionately between close friends, but this usage is highly context-dependent and can be easily misconstrued.|Example 1:  "Malaka! You almost hit me with your car!" （混蛋！你差点用车撞到我！）

Example 2: "Hey malaka, what are you doing tonight?" (嘿，小子，你今晚干什么？ - *仅限于非常亲密的朋友之间*)|Near synonyms: asshole, jerk, wanker, idiot, moron|Antonyms: (Generally, terms of endearment or respect, though direct opposites don't exist for strong insults) friend, buddy, mate|noun
