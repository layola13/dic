hedonics:/hɪˈdɒnɪks/ /hiːˈdɑːnɪks/
n.
享乐主义；快乐论；快乐学
The study of pleasure and happiness; the branch of psychology that deals with pleasant and unpleasant feelings.
Hedonics plays a significant role in consumer behavior. 享乐主义在消费者行为中起着重要作用。
Researchers are exploring the relationship between income and hedonics. 研究人员正在探索收入与享乐之间的关系。
近义词: pleasure, happiness, enjoyment, gratification, satisfaction, delight
反义词: pain, suffering, displeasure, dissatisfaction, unhappiness
词性: noun

hedonic:/hɪˈdɒnɪk/ /hiːˈdɑːnɪk/
adj.
享乐的；快乐的
Of or relating to pleasure.
Many people make hedonic purchases without considering the long-term consequences. 许多人进行享乐性购买而不考虑长期后果。
The spa offered a range of hedonic treatments.  该水疗中心提供一系列享乐疗法。
近义词: pleasurable, enjoyable, delightful, gratifying
反义词: unpleasant, unenjoyable, displeasing
词性: adjective
