concertante:/kɒntʃɛˈtanteɪ/ /ˌkɑːntʃərˈtɑːnteɪ/
n.|协奏风格的；协奏曲；|A style of composition, usually for orchestra, in which two or more solo instruments are prominent.| Mozart wrote several works in the concertante style.|近义词:concerted, soloistic|反义词:orchestral, tutti|词性:名词, 形容词

concertante:/kɒntʃɛˈtanteɪ/ /ˌkɑːntʃərˈtɑːnteɪ/
adj.|协奏风格的；|Characterized by the prominent display of two or more solo instruments.|The concertante style was popular in the Classical period.|近义词:concerted, soloistic|反义词:orchestral, tutti|词性:形容词
