nummulitic: /ˌnʌmjuˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |货币虫的，钱币石的 | Containing or resembling nummulites (large lens-shaped fossil shells of a type of foraminifer). | The cliffs are composed of nummulitic limestone. 这些悬崖是由货币虫灰岩构成的。 | The quarry yielded a large quantity of nummulitic rock.  这个采石场产出了大量的货币虫岩石。 | 近义词: foraminiferal | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider non-fossiliferous) | 词性: adjective

nummulite: /ˈnʌmjʊlaɪt/ | n. |货币虫，钱币石 | A large lens-shaped fossil shell of a foraminifer. | Nummulites are common in Eocene rocks. 货币虫在始新世岩石中很常见。 |  The limestone contained numerous nummulites.  石灰岩中包含大量的货币虫。 | 近义词: foraminifer | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
