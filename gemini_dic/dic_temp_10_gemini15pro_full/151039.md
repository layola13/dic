parapetless:/ˈpærəpɪtlɪs/
adj.
无栏杆的，无矮墙的
lacking a parapet; having no parapet.
The parapetless roof offered a breathtaking, albeit slightly terrifying, view of the city.  无栏杆的屋顶提供了令人叹为观止的城市景观，尽管有点吓人。
The bridge was parapetless, making the crossing feel precarious. 这座桥没有栏杆，让人觉得过桥很危险。
近义词:unguarded, unprotected, open
反义词:protected, guarded, railed
词性:adjective


词源:
From parapet + -less.  Parapet comes from the Italian "parapetto" (from "parare" - to protect/defend + "petto" - chest/breast).  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and denotes "without" or "lacking".
