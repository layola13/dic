hotly:/ˈhɒtli/ (British), /ˈhɑːtli/ (American)| adv.|激烈地，热烈地，愤怒地|In a heated or passionate way, often involving anger or controversy.|The proposal was hotly debated.  这项提议引起了激烈的辩论。|
The two candidates hotly contested the election. 两位候选人激烈地竞争选举。|
intensely, fiercely, passionately, vehemently|mildly, calmly, coolly|adverb
