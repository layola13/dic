diluvialist:/ˌdaɪluːˈviːəlɪst/
noun
灾变论者，洪积世学者
A person who believes in or studies the diluvial theory, which attributes geological formations to the effects of a great flood, especially the Noachian deluge described in the Bible.
The diluvialist argued that the canyon was carved by a massive flood. 灾变论者认为峡谷是由一场大洪水雕刻而成的。

diluvialist:/ˌdaɪluːˈviːəlɪst/
n.
灾变论者，洪积世学者
A person who believes that the current geological features of the Earth were formed by a catastrophic flood, particularly the biblical flood.
The diluvialist pointed to the layered sediments as evidence of a rapid, massive flood. 这位灾变论者指出层状沉积物是大规模快速洪水的证据。

近义词:catastrophist
反义词:uniformitarian
