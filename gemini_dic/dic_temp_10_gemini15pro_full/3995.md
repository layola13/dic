aha:/əˈhɑː/ /ɑˈhɑː/
interjection
啊哈;表示领悟、惊喜、胜利等
An exclamation of satisfaction, triumph, or surprise.
Aha! I've found it! 啊哈！我找到它了！
Aha, I knew you were hiding something! 啊哈，我就知道你在隐瞒什么！
synonyms:eureka, bingo, gotcha
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for an interjection of this type)
词性:感叹词
