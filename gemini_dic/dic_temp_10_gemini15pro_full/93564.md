imagining:/ɪˈmædʒɪnɪŋ/
n.|想象，设想|The act of forming a mental image or concept of something. |She spent hours imagining her life as a famous actress. 她花了几个小时想象自己成为著名女演员的生活。|近义词：visualizing, conceiving, fantasizing|反义词：perceiving, experiencing
v.|想象，设想|Forming a mental image or concept of something not present to the senses. |I can't imagine living without music. 我无法想象没有音乐的生活。|近义词：envisioning, picturing, supposing|反义词：knowing, experiencing
词性: present participle of imagine
