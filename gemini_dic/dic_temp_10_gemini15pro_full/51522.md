detritus:/dɪˈtraɪtəs/| n.|碎屑，残渣；废墟|Waste or debris of any kind, organic or inorganic; fragments resulting from disintegration or wearing away.|The forest floor was covered with detritus.|Synonyms: debris, waste, rubble, remains, fragments|Antonyms: wholeness, entirety|noun

detritus:/dɪˈtraɪtəs/| n.|（比喻）无价值的东西|Something that is considered worthless or unimportant.|The museum was filled with the detritus of past civilizations.|Synonyms: junk, rubbish, trash, dross|Antonyms: treasure, valuable|noun
