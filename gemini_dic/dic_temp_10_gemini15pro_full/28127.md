capitation:/ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃn/
n.
人头费；按人头计算的报酬；按人头分配
a fixed amount of money paid to a doctor, hospital, or other health care provider for each patient they care for, regardless of the amount of care the patient needs.
例句：The doctor receives a capitation payment for each patient registered with the practice.  医生对每个注册的病人收取人头费。

例句：Capitation payments encourage healthcare providers to focus on preventative care. 人头费支付鼓励医疗保健提供者关注预防性护理。

近义词：per capita payment, fixed payment, flat rate
反义词：fee-for-service
词性: 名词
