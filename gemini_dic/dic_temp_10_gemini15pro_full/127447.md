nebular:/ˈnɛbjʊlə(r)/| adj.|星云的；云状的；朦胧的|Relating to a nebula or nebulae; cloudlike, hazy.|例句：The nebular hypothesis explains the formation of the solar system. 星云假说解释了太阳系的形成。|例句：His explanation was rather nebular. 他的解释相当模糊。|近义词：cloudy, hazy, misty, vague|反义词：clear, distinct|词性:形容词
