lyingly:/ˈlaɪɪŋli/
adv.
虚伪地，不诚实地
in a deceptive or untruthful manner.
She smiled lyingly, hiding her true intentions. 她虚伪地笑着，掩盖了她的真实意图。
近义词:dishonestly, falsely, deceptively
反义词:truthfully, honestly, sincerely
词性:副词
