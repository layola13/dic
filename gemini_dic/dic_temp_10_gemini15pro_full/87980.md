high-maintenance:/ˌhaɪ ˈmeɪntənəns/ | adj. |高维护的，难伺候的|  Needing a lot of attention, care, or money.  | Example 1:  She's a high-maintenance girlfriend, always expecting expensive gifts. 她是一个高维护的女朋友，总是期待昂贵的礼物。

Example 2: That sports car is high-maintenance, requiring frequent and costly repairs. 那辆跑车维护成本很高，需要经常进行昂贵的维修。 | 近义词：demanding, needy, fussy | 反义词：low-maintenance, easygoing, undemanding | 词性: 形容词
