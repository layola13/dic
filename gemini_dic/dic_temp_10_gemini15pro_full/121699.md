mispractising: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪsɪŋ/
verb
渎职；失职；滥用职权
engaging in malpractice, especially in a profession like medicine or law.
The doctor was accused of mispractising medicine.  这个医生被指控滥用医疗职权。
He lost his license for mispractising law. 他因渎职失去了律师执照。
Synonyms: malpractice, misconduct, wrongdoing, negligence
Antonyms:  due diligence, ethical practice, proper conduct
