fenestrule:/ˈfɛnɪstruːl/| n. |小窗，透光孔|A small window-like opening, especially in a bryozoan (苔藓虫) or other invertebrate.|The fenestrule in the bryozoan's skeleton allowed for water flow.  苔藓虫骨骼上的小窗允许水流通过。|Synonym: aperture, opening, pore|Antonym: closure, blockage|noun
