diagnose: /ˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊs/ | v. | 诊断 | To identify the nature and cause of an illness or other problem through examination. | The doctor diagnosed the illness as pneumonia. 医生诊断这种疾病为肺炎。 | identify, determine, recognize | miss, overlook | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diagnosticare, from Greek diagignōskein "to distinguish, discern," from dia- "thoroughly" + gignōskein "to know."
