haubergeon:/ˈhɔːbərdʒən/| n. |锁子甲，锁子甲衣|A medieval coat of mail, typically sleeveless and reaching to mid-thigh, made of interlinked metal rings.|The knight donned his haubergeon before riding into battle. 骑士在骑马出战前穿上了他的锁子甲。|Synonyms: chain mail, coat of mail, byrnie|Antonyms: None (lack of specific antonym for a historical piece of armor)|Noun
