advert-to:/ədˈvɜːt tuː/
verb
提到；谈及；注意
to refer or turn attention to something
He adverted to the problem in his speech. 他在演讲中提到了这个问题。

The chairman briefly adverted to the sales figures. 主席简要地提到了销售数字。

Synonyms: refer to, mention, allude to, touch upon, speak of, comment on
Antonyms: ignore, disregard, overlook
词性: 动词
