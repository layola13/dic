panophthalmitis:/pænˌɒfθælˈmaɪtɪs/
n.
全眼炎
Inflammation of all the tissues of the eye.
Panophthalmitis is a serious condition that can lead to blindness. 全眼炎是一种严重的疾病，会导致失明。
The patient presented with severe pain and redness, suggestive of panophthalmitis. 病人表现出剧烈的疼痛和发红，提示可能是全眼炎。
