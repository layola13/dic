displeasurable:/dɪsˈplɛʒərəbəl/
adjective
令人不愉快的，讨厌的
causing displeasure; not enjoyable.
例句：The movie was a displeasurable experience. 这部电影是一次令人不快的经历。

例句：The smell was quite displeasurable.  这气味相当难闻。

近义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, uncomfortable, annoying, irritating
反义词：pleasant, enjoyable, agreeable, delightful, pleasurable
词性: adjective
