darnest:/ˈdɑːrnɪst/| adj.|最…的（用于加强语气，通常表示恼怒或惊讶）|Used to emphasize how annoying, surprising, etc. something is.  It's a milder form of "damned" and is considered a euphemism.| That's the darnest thing I've ever heard!  那是我听过的最奇怪的事情！|The darnest cat keeps coming into my garden. 那只讨厌的猫一直跑到我的花园里来。|Synonyms: blessed, confounded, blamed|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's an intensifier.  One might consider words that diminish rather than intensify, such as "slightly," "somewhat")|adjective
