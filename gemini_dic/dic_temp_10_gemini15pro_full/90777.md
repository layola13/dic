humorous:/ˈhjuːmərəs/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərəs/ (UK)| adj.|幽默的，滑稽的|Causing laughter and amusement; funny.|例句：He told a humorous story that made everyone laugh. 他讲了一个幽默的故事，让大家都笑了。|近义词：funny, comical, witty, amusing|反义词：serious, solemn, grave|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin humorosus, from Latin humor ‘moisture’. The original sense was ‘moist, damp’, later ‘capricious, whimsical’, finally (in the 17th cent.) ‘amusing’.
