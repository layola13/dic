ameliorants:/əˈmiːliərənts/
|n.|改良剂；改进剂|Substances added to soil to improve its physical properties, such as its texture, water retention, or drainage.|The farmer used soil ameliorants to increase crop yields. 农民使用土壤改良剂来提高作物产量。|synonyms: improvers, enhancers, conditioners|antonyms: degradants, pollutants|noun
