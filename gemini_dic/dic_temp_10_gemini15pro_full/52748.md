dilapidated:/dɪˈlæpɪdeɪtɪd/| adj.|破旧的，失修的|Falling into ruin or decay, usually from neglect or age.  Characterized by a state of disrepair or ruin due to neglect or age.|The dilapidated house stood on a deserted street.  这栋破旧的房子坐落在一条荒凉的街道上。|The old barn was dilapidated and in need of serious repairs.  旧谷仓破旧不堪，需要大修。|近义词：ramshackle, rundown, derelict, tumbledown|反义词：well-maintained, pristine, renovated|词性:形容词
