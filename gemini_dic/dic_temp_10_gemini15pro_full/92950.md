ideographical:/ˌaɪdiəˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
表意的
representing ideas or concepts directly, rather than sounds
Chinese characters are ideographical, representing whole words or concepts.  汉字是表意的，代表整个单词或概念。
The ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs were an ideographical writing system. 古埃及象形文字是一种表意文字系统。
近义词:symbolic, emblematic, pictographic
反义词:phonetic, alphabetic
词性:adjective
词源: From idea + -o- + graphical.
