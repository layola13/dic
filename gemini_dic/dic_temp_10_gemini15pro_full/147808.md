overprovidentness:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːvɪdəntnəs/
noun
过度谨慎；过分节俭
The state of being excessively provident; undue care for the future.
His overprovidentness led him to save excessively, even to the point of depriving himself of basic comforts. 他过分的节俭让他过度储蓄，甚至到了剥夺自己基本舒适的地步。
foresight, prudence, thrift
improvidence, extravagance, recklessness
