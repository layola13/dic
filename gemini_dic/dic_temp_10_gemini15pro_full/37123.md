combustious:/kəmˈbʌstʃəs/
adjective
易燃的；易激动的
Easily catching fire; quick to ignite. Also, easily roused to excitement or anger.
The warehouse stored combustious materials, making it a fire hazard.  仓库储存了易燃材料，使其成为火灾隐患。
He has a combustious personality and is easily angered. 他性格暴躁，很容易生气。
synonyms: flammable, inflammable, incendiary, volatile, fiery
antonyms: nonflammable, fire-resistant, calm, placid
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin combustibilis, from comburere ‘burn up’ (see combust).
