nonseismic:/ˌnɒnˈsaɪzmɪk/
adjective
非地震的；非由地震引起的
Not caused by or related to an earthquake.
The area experienced a nonseismic tremor. 这个地区经历了一次非地震性震颤。
Landslides can be triggered by nonseismic events such as heavy rainfall.  山体滑坡可以由非地震事件引发，例如暴雨。
synonyms: aseismic
antonyms: seismic
词性: 形容词
