marring:/ˈmɑːrɪŋ/
mar:/mɑːr/ | v. |损害，破坏，玷污|To damage or spoil something, making it less perfect or attractive. | The beautiful scenery was marred by the ugly billboards.  美丽的景色被丑陋的广告牌破坏了。| Synonyms: spoiling, damaging, impairing, disfiguring | Antonyms: improving, enhancing, perfecting | Verb

marring:/ˈmɑːrɪŋ/
mar:/mɑːr/ | n. |损害，瑕疵，缺陷|A mark or flaw that spoils the appearance of something. |  A single marring on the surface of the antique vase greatly reduced its value.  古董花瓶表面的一点瑕疵大大降低了它的价值。| Synonyms: blemish, flaw, defect, imperfection | Antonyms: perfection, flawlessness | Noun
