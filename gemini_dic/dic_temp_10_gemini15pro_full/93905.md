immortelle:/ˌɪmɔːrˈtɛl/| n. adj.|永久花，不凋花；不朽的，永恒的|A plant whose flowers can be dried without losing their colour, or the dried flower itself.  Figuratively, it can also refer to something that lasts forever. |例句：She wore a crown of immortelles. 她戴着永久花编织的花冠。

例句：The poet wrote of immortelle fame. 诗人写了关于不朽的荣誉。|近义词：everlasting, unfading, eternal, perpetual|反义词：mortal, temporary, transient|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: From French *immortelle*, feminine of *immortel*, from Latin *immortalis*, from *in-* (not) + *mortalis* (mortal).
