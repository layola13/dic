hemispherical:/ˌhemɪˈsfɛrɪkəl/
adjective

Relating to, or shaped like, a hemisphere (half of a sphere).
The dome of the building was hemispherical.  这栋建筑的穹顶是半球形的。
The planetarium projector displayed images on the hemispherical ceiling. 天文馆的投影仪将图像投射到半球形的天花板上。

近义词:dome-shaped, half-round, semiglobular
反义词:flat, planar
