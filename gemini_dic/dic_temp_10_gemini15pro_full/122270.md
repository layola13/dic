mitigating:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 减轻的，缓和的 | lessening the gravity or seriousness of something |  The judge took his mitigating circumstances into account when deciding his sentence. 法官在判决时考虑了他的减轻罪责的情况。 |  alleviating, extenuating, lessening, reducing | aggravating, exacerbating | adjective

mitigate:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/| v.| 减轻，缓和 |  to make something less severe, serious, or painful | The government is trying to mitigate the effects of the recession. 政府正在努力减轻经济衰退的影响。 | alleviate, reduce, lessen, diminish | aggravate, worsen, exacerbate | verb

词源：From Late Latin *mitigatus, past participle of mitigare (“to soften, make mild”), from Latin mitis (“mild, soft”) + -igō.
