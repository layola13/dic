foxly:/ˈfɒksli/ | adj. | 狡猾的，像狐狸的 | Resembling or characteristic of a fox, especially in cunning or slyness. | He gave her a foxly grin. 他狡黠地对她一笑。|  She had a foxly way of getting what she wanted. 她有一种狡猾的方式得到她想要的东西。| 近义词: cunning, sly, wily, artful, crafty | 反义词: naive, ingenuous, artless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From fox + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" transforms nouns into adjectives.  The word "fox" itself comes from Old English "fox," which in turn is derived from Proto-Germanic "*fuhsaz."
