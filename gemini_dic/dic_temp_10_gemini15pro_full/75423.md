frugivorous:/fruːˈdʒɪvərəs/
adjective
食果的；果食性的
Feeding on fruit; fruit-eating.
Many bats are frugivorous, playing a vital role in seed dispersal. 许多蝙蝠以水果为食，在种子传播中起着至关重要的作用。
Tropical rainforests are home to a wide variety of frugivorous animals. 热带雨林是各种食果动物的家园。
herbivorous, vegetarian
carnivorous, omnivorous
adjective
