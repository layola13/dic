cotswold:/ˈkɑːtswɔːldz/ (US) /ˈkɒtswəʊldz/ (UK)| n.|科茨沃尔德|A range of hills in south central and south west England, an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.|We spent a week walking in the Cotswolds. 我们在科茨沃尔德徒步旅行了一周。|Synonyms:  hills, uplands, wolds|Antonyms: lowlands, valleys|noun

cotswold:/ˈkɑːtswɔːld/ (US) /ˈkɒtswəʊld/ (UK)| adj.|科茨沃尔德式的，科茨沃尔德地区的|Relating to the Cotswolds region of England, typically characterized by rolling hills, picturesque villages, and traditional stone buildings.|The Cotswold stone walls added charm to the cottage. 科茨沃尔德的石墙给小屋增添了魅力。|Synonyms: rustic, charming, picturesque|Antonyms: modern, urban|adjective
