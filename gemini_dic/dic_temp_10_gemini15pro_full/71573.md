flashiness:/ˈflæʃɪnɪs/
n.|华丽；炫耀；俗丽|The quality of being showy, ostentatious, or gaudy.|The flashiness of his clothes was a bit much for a Tuesday afternoon. 他衣服的华丽程度对于一个周二下午来说有点过了。|近义词: gaudiness, showiness, ostentation|反义词:subduedness,  understatement, modesty|词性: 名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From flashy + -ness. Flashy itself comes from flash (v.) + -y.  Ultimately from Old Norse *flasa "to rush."
