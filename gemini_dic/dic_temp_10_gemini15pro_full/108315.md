adjective
戴桂冠的；荣誉加身的；桂树似的
crowned with laurel; producing or yielding laurel.
The lauriferous poet received accolades for his moving verses.  这位戴桂冠的诗人因其感人的诗句而受到赞誉。

synonyms: triumphant, victorious, honored, decorated
antonyms: disgraced, dishonored, defeated
