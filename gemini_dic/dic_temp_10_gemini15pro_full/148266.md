overslung:/ˌoʊvərˈslʌŋ/| adj.|上悬式的，上置式的|Having the supporting members or suspension located above, rather than below, the object they are carrying.  This design typically offers increased ground clearance.|The jeep had an overslung spare tire. 这辆吉普车有一个上置式的备胎。|近义词：overhead, mounted above|反义词：underslung|词性:形容词
