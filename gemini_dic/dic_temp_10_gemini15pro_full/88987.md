holistic-medicine:/hōˈlɪstɪk ˈmɛdəsɪn/| n.|整体医学|A system of medicine that considers the whole person -- body, mind, and spirit -- in the prevention and treatment of disease.|Example 1: She turned to holistic medicine after conventional treatments failed to alleviate her chronic pain. 她转向了整体医学，在传统的治疗方法未能缓解她的慢性疼痛之后。

Example 2: Holistic medicine emphasizes preventative care and lifestyle changes. 整体医学强调预防性护理和生活方式的改变。|Synonyms: alternative medicine, complementary medicine, integrative medicine|Antonyms: conventional medicine, allopathic medicine|noun


holistic:/hōˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|整体的，全面的|Characterized by the belief that the parts of something are intimately interconnected and explicable only by reference to the whole.|Example 1: He took a holistic approach to his health, considering diet, exercise, and stress management. 他对自己的健康采取了整体的方法，考虑了饮食、锻炼和压力管理。

Example 2: The holistic design of the building considered its impact on the surrounding environment. 这栋建筑的整体设计考虑了其对周围环境的影响。|Synonyms: comprehensive, integrated, whole|Antonyms: fragmented, partial, segmented|adjective


medicine:/ˈmɛdəsɪn/| n.|药，医学|The science or practice of the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease.|Example 1: She is studying medicine at university. 她在大学学习医学。

Example 2: The doctor prescribed him some medicine for his cough. 医生给他开了一些治咳嗽的药。|Synonyms: medication, drug, remedy|Antonyms: poison, toxin|noun
