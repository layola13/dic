hobgoblin:/ˈhɒbˌɡɒblɪn/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbˌɡɑːblɪn/ (US)| n. |妖怪; 鬼怪; 令人烦恼或恐惧的事物|A mischievous and often malicious sprite or goblin.  A source of worry or fear. |例句：The old house was said to be haunted by hobgoblins.  据说那栋老房子里闹鬼怪。

例句：Fear of failure became his personal hobgoblin.  对失败的恐惧成了他个人的心魔。|近义词：goblin, bogeyman, bugbear, gremlin|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "comfort", "reassurance")|词性:noun
