bookless:/ˈbʊklɪs/
adjective
无书的；没有书的
Without books; lacking books.
The bookless classroom relied heavily on technology. 这间没有书的教室严重依赖科技。
The traveler, bookless and bored, stared out the train window.  这位旅客没有带书，感到无聊，凝视着火车窗外。


近义词:without books
反义词:bookish, well-stocked with books
词性:adjective
