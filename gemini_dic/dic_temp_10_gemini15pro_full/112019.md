long-lost:/ˈlɒŋ lɒst/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋ lɔːst/ (US)| adj.|失散已久的，久违的|Having been lost or out of contact for a long time.|I met my long-lost cousin at the reunion. 我在聚会上遇到了我失散多年的表弟。|近义词：missing, absent, forgotten|反义词:found, present, remembered|词性:形容词
词源:Compound adjective formed from "long" (meaning a long duration) and "lost" (past participle of "lose," meaning no longer possessed or known).
