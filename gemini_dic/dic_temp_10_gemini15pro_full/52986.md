dinkiest:/ˈdɪŋkiɪst/| adj.|最小的，最矮小的|Smallest or shortest (often used affectionately or humorously).|例句：She drives the dinkiest little car I've ever seen. 她开着我见过的最小的车。

例句：He lives in the dinkiest apartment in the building. 他住在这栋楼里最小的公寓。|近义词：smallest, tiniest, miniature, minute|反义词：largest, biggest, huge, enormous|词性:形容词

词源:  A diminutive form of *dinky,* which itself is of unknown origin.  Theories suggest it may be related to Scottish *dink* (neat, trim), or perhaps imitative.
