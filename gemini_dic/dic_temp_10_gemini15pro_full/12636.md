assaulters:/əˈsɔːltərz/
n.
攻击者；袭击者
People who commit assault; those who attack others.
The assaulters were apprehended by the police shortly after the incident. 攻击者在事件发生后不久就被警方逮捕了。
Several assaulters were caught on camera during the riot.  几名袭击者在骚乱中被摄像机拍到。
Synonyms: attackers, aggressors, assailants
Antonyms: victims, defenders, protectors
