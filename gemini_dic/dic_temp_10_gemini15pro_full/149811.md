paleogaean:/ˌpeɪliːoʊˈdʒiːən/
adjective
古陆的；泛大陆的
Relating to or denoting the hypothetical supercontinent Pangaea.
The paleogaean flora was remarkably uniform. 古陆植物群非常一致。
The paleogaean distribution of certain fossils supports the theory of continental drift. 某些化石的古陆分布支持大陆漂移学说。
近义词: Pangean
反义词: None
