malices:/ˈmælɪsɪz/| n.|恶意，怨恨|The statement was made with malice. 这番话是出于恶意。|近义词: spite, animosity, malevolence, ill will, rancor|反义词: benevolence, goodwill, kindness|noun

Etymology->
From Old French *malice* (Modern French *malice*), from Latin *malitia* "badness, bad quality, vice, wickedness, ill-will, spite," from *malus* "bad, evil."
