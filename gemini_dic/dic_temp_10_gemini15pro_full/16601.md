baromacrometer:/ˌbærəʊməˈkrɒmɪtər/
n.
气压高度计，气压测高计
An instrument that determines altitude by measuring atmospheric pressure.
The pilot used the baromacrometer to determine the plane's altitude. 飞行员使用气压高度计确定飞机的高度。
The hiker checked his baromacrometer to see how much further he had to climb.  徒步旅行者查看了他的气压高度计，看看他还需要爬多远。
Synonyms: altimeter, altitude meter, pressure altimeter
Antonyms: depth gauge


ETYMOLOGY->
From baro- (Greek: weight, pressure) + -meter (Greek: measure).
