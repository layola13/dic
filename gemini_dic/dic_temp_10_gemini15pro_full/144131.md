outsearched:/ˌaʊtˈsɜːrtʃt/
verb
胜过搜索；在搜索方面超过
to surpass in searching; to be more effective at searching than someone or something else.
Google outsearched its competitors and became the dominant search engine.  谷歌在搜索方面胜过其竞争对手，成为主要的搜索引擎。
He outsearched everyone in the library to find the rare manuscript. 他在图书馆里比任何人都搜索得更有效率，找到了那份珍稀的手稿。
synonyms: outmaneuver, outperform, surpass, eclipse
antonyms:  underperform, lose to
