locketed:/ˈlɒkɪtɪd/ /ˈlɑːkɪtɪd/| adj.| 上锁的；被锁住的；装在小盒里的；珍藏在心中的 | Enclosed in a locket; cherished as precious. | She wore a locketed photograph of her grandmother. 她戴着一个装有她祖母照片的小盒。| 近义词：treasured, cherished, locked away | 反义词：forgotten, discarded | 词性：形容词

词源:  From the noun "locket" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old French "loquet" (latch, lock).
