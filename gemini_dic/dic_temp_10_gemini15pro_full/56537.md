dominated: /ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|被支配的，被控制的，占主导地位的|influenced or controlled by something or someone; featuring something or someone as the most important or noticeable aspect|例句：The landscape is dominated by towering mountains.  这片景观以高耸的山脉为主。|近义词：controlled, governed, ruled, influenced, overpowered|反义词：submissive, subordinate, controlled (by another), independent|词性:形容词

dominated: /ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪtɪd/ (US)| v.|（dominate的过去式和过去分词）支配，控制，占主导地位|past tense and past participle of "dominate"|例句：He dominated the conversation. 他控制了谈话。|近义词：controlled, governed, ruled, influenced, overpowered|反义词：submitted, yielded, conceded|词性:动词

词源: From the Latin word "dominari," meaning "to rule" or "to master."
