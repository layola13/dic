hanse: /ˈhɑːnzə/ | n. | 汉萨同盟 | A medieval league of north German towns and cities, formed in the 13th century, to protect their mutual trading interests. | The Hanseatic League, or Hanse, was a powerful force in medieval trade. 汉萨同盟，或称汉萨，是中世纪贸易中的一支强大力量。| The Hanse dominated trade in the Baltic and North Seas for centuries.  汉萨同盟几个世纪以来一直主导着波罗的海和北海的贸易。 | synonyms: league, alliance, federation | antonyms: division, separation | noun
