nonreciprocally:/ˌnɑːnˌresɪˈprɑːkli/ | adv. | 非互惠地，单向地 |  In a way that is not reciprocal; unidirectionally.  Not mutually exchanged or felt. | Information flows nonreciprocally from DNA to RNA. 信息从 DNA 到 RNA 的流动是非互惠的。| The two surfaces interact nonreciprocally. 这两个表面相互作用是非互惠的。 | synonym: unidirectionally, asymmetrically | antonym: reciprocally, mutually | adverb
