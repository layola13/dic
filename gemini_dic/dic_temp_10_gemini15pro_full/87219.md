hermit:/ˈhɜːmɪt/ | n. |隐士，遁世者|A person who lives in solitude, especially for religious reasons.  A recluse.|The old hermit lived in a cave in the mountains. 这位老隐士住在山里的一个洞穴里。|Synonyms: recluse, anchorite, solitary, ascetic|Antonyms: socialite, extrovert|noun
