destinying:/ˈdɛstɪnɪŋ/
verb
注定，预定
The act of assigning a fate or purpose to someone or something.
He felt he was destinying himself to a life of loneliness. 他感觉他注定要过孤独的一生。
preordaining, foreordaining, predestining
None (as "destinying" implies an active role, a lack of action wouldn't be a direct opposite)
v.
