bend over backward:/bɛnd ˈoʊvər ˈbækwərd/| idiom | 竭尽全力，费尽心思 | To make a great effort to please or accommodate someone. | Example: The hotel staff bent over backward to make our stay enjoyable.  酒店员工竭尽全力让我们住得愉快。 | Example: She bent over backward to help him get the job. 她费尽心思帮他得到那份工作。| Synonyms: go the extra mile, pull out all the stops, go out of one's way | Antonyms: do the bare minimum, lift a finger (sarcastically) | idiom

bend over backward:/bɛnd ˈoʊvər ˈbækwərd/| verb phrase | 竭尽全力，费尽心思 | To exert oneself to the utmost; to make extreme efforts. | Example: I bent over backward to get that project finished on time. 我竭尽全力按时完成了那个项目。 | Example: He's always bending over backward for his clients. 他总是为他的客户费尽心思。 | Synonyms:  fall all over oneself, do everything in one's power | Antonyms: do nothing, neglect | verb phrase
