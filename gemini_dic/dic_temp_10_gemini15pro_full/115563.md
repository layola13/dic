Maracanã:/ˌmærəkəˈnɐ̃/ | n. | 马拉卡纳球场 |  A large football stadium in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, officially named Estádio Jornalista Mário Filho.  It is one of the largest stadiums in the world and has hosted numerous important football matches, including the finals of the 1950 and 2014 FIFA World Cups. |  Brazil won the gold medal in football at the Maracanã during the 2016 Summer Olympics. 巴西在2016年夏季奥运会期间的马拉卡纳球场赢得了足球金牌。| synonym: stadium, arena | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific stadium.
