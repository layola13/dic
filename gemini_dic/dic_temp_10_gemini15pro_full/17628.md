beboppers:/ˈbiːbɑːpərz/
noun
比波普爵士乐演奏者/爱好者
Musicians or fans of bebop, a style of jazz characterized by fast tempos, complex harmonies, and virtuosic improvisation.
Charlie Parker and Dizzy Gillespie were prominent beboppers. 查理·帕克和迪兹·吉莱斯皮是著名的比波普爵士乐演奏家。
synonyms: jazz musicians, bebop enthusiasts
antonyms: traditional jazz musicians


ETYMOLOGY->
From bebop (a type of jazz) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting persons associated with a particular activity or thing).  The word "bebop" itself is onomatopoeic, likely imitating the rhythmic phrasing of the music.
