gianthood:/ˈdʒaɪənthʊd/
n.
巨大；伟人身份；巨人世界
The state of being a giant; exceptional size or power.
He achieved gianthood in the field of physics. 他在物理学领域取得了巨大的成就。
Near synonyms:  enormity, immensity, vastness, hugeness,  greatness, eminence, stature
Antonyms: smallness, insignificance, littleness
noun
