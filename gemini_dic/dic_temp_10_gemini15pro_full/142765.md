ottomanize:/ˈɒtəmənʌɪz/
verb
奥斯曼化，土耳其化
To make something or someone conform to Ottoman Turkish customs, culture, or political systems.
The empire sought to ottomanize its diverse populations.  帝国试图使其多样化的人口奥斯曼化。
The region was gradually ottomanized over several centuries.  这个地区在几个世纪中逐渐被奥斯曼化。
近义词: Turkify, Islamize
反义词: Westernize, Europeanize
词性: v.
