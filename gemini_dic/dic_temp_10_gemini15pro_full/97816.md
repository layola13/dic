inkfish:/ˈɪŋkˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 墨鱼，乌贼 | Any of various cephalopod mollusks, especially of the genera *Sepia* and *Sepiola*, related to the octopus and squid, having eight arms, two tentacles, and an internal shell.
