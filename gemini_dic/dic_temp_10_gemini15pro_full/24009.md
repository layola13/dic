breathy:/ˈbreθi/ (US), /ˈbriːði/ (UK)| adj.|呼吸声的，气喘吁吁的|Characterized by audible breathing; husky.|Her voice was breathy and low. 她的声音低沉而带喘息。|近义词：husky, whispered, panting|反义词：clear, strong|词性:形容词
Etymology: From breath + -y.  "Breath" comes from Old English "bræþ." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
