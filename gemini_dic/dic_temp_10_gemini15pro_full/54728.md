disparageable:/dɪˈspærɪdʒəbl/
adjective
可贬低的，可轻视的
able to be disparaged or belittled.
Her conduct was so egregious that it was easily disparageable. 她的行为如此恶劣，很容易遭到贬低。
近义词:criticizable, condemnable, censurable
反义词:praiseworthy, commendable, laudable
词性:adjective

词源： From disparage + -able.  Disparage comes from Old French *desparagier “to marry unequally,” from des- “away, down” + parage “rank, lineage.”  The meaning shifted to "degrade" or "belittle" in English.
