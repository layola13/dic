incommensurable:/ɪnkəmˈɛnʃərəbəl/ /ɪnkəmˈɛnʃərəbl/
adjective
不可公度的，不能相比的
Not able to be judged by the same standard; having no common standard of measurement.
例句：The two theories are incommensurable and cannot be compared directly. 这两个理论不可公度，无法直接比较。

例句：His contribution to the project is incommensurable with the small amount he was paid. 他对这个项目的贡献与他得到的少量报酬是不成比例的。
近义词：incomparable, disparate, incompatible, disproportionate
反义词：commensurable, comparable, similar, proportionate
词性：adjective
