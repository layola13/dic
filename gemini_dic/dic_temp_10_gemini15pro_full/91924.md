hypermasculinity:/ˌhaɪpərˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪti/ | n. |  超级男子气概，极端男性化 | Exaggerated adherence to stereotypical masculine gender roles, often emphasizing physical strength, aggression, and sexuality. |  He felt pressured to conform to the expectations of hypermasculinity in his fraternity. 他感到有压力要符合他兄弟会中极端男子气概的期望。 | synonyms: machismo, exaggerated masculinity | antonyms: femininity, androgyny | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek "hyper," meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
masculinity: from Late Latin "masculinus," meaning "male, manly"
