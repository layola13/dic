encrinal:/ɛnˈkraɪnəl/
adjective
海百合的；与海百合有关的
Relating to or resembling a crinoid (sea lily).
The museum boasts an impressive collection of encrinal fossils.  博物馆拥有一批令人印象深刻的海百合化石。
近义词: crinoid, crinoidean
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could use "non-encrinal" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective
词源: From French *encrinal*, from en- + crin ("lily") + -al. Ultimately from Latin crīnum ("lily").
