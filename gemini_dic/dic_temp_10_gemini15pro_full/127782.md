neighboring:/ˈneɪbərɪŋ/| adj.|邻近的，附近的|Located next to or very near something else. |例句：We visited the neighboring towns.  我们参观了邻近的城镇。|例句：The neighboring country declared war. 邻国宣战了。|近义词：adjacent, adjoining, nearby, close|反义词：distant, far, remote|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *neigheborynge, equivalent to neighbor + -ing.  Ultimately from Old English *nēahgebūr, from nēah (“near”) + gebūr (“dweller”).
