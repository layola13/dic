getter:/ˈɡɛtər/| n.|获取器; 获得者|A getter is a method that reads the value of a property.  It's often used in object-oriented programming to encapsulate data access. |The `getName()` method is a getter for the `name` property.  `getName()` 方法是 `name` 属性的获取器。|近义词:accessor, retriever|反义词:setter|词性:noun
