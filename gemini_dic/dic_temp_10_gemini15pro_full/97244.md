inflexed:/ɪnˈflɛkst/ | adj. | 弯曲的，屈曲的；词形变化的 | Bent or turned; having undergone inflection (grammatical change in form). | The dancer's body was gracefully inflexed. 舞者的身体优雅地弯曲着。 The verb "to be" is highly inflexed.  动词“to be”的词形变化很多。 | 近义词：bent, curved, declined, conjugated | 反义词：straight, unbent, undeclined | 词性：形容词

词源: From Latin *inflexus, past participle of inflectere "to bend, curve," from in- "in, into" + flectere "to bend."
