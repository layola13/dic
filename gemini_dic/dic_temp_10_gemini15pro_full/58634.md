dumbed:/dʌmd/
|v.|使变傻，简化|Simplified or made less intelligent.  The textbook dumbed down the complex theory so that everyone could understand it.  教科书简化了复杂的理论，以便每个人都能理解。|simplify, oversimplify|complicate, elaborate|verb
dumbed:/dʌmd/
|adj.|愚蠢的，简化的|(Usually used in combination) Made less intelligent or complex.  The dumbed-down version of the software was easier to use, but lacked many features.  简化版的软件更容易使用，但缺少许多功能。|simplified, watered-down|complex, sophisticated|adjective
