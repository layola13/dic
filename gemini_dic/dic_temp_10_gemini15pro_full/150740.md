paraclete: /ˈpærəkliːt/ (US), /ˈpærəklaɪt/ (UK)| n.| 劝慰者，辩护者，圣灵 | One that pleads another's cause, especially an intercessor for humanity with God.  Specifically, the Holy Spirit.  It derives from the Greek word παράκλητος (paráklētos), meaning "one called alongside to help." |  Example 1:  The Paraclete, the Holy Spirit, will guide you. 圣灵，也就是劝慰者，会指引你。

Example 2: He prayed for the Paraclete to comfort him. 他祈求保惠师来安慰他。 | Synonym: advocate, intercessor, comforter, helper, counselor | Antonym: accuser, prosecutor | noun
