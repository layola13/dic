geotropism:/dʒiːˈɒtrəpɪzəm/
|n.|向地性|The growth or movement of a plant in response to gravity.|The roots exhibit positive geotropism, growing downwards, while the shoots show negative geotropism, growing upwards. 植物的根表现出正向地性，向下生长，而芽则表现出负向地性，向上生长。|gravitropism| |noun|
