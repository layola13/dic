outcoached: /ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃt/
verb
以更好的教练方式击败
To defeat by having a better coach or coaching strategy.
The Warriors outcoached the Cavaliers in the finals. 勇士队在决赛中以更好的教练策略击败了骑士队。
Our team was clearly outcoached in the second half.  我们的球队在下半场明显在教练方面处于劣势。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass
Antonyms: underperform, lose to
词性: 动词
