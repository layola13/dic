deporter:/dɪˈpɔːrtər/
noun|驱逐出境者|A person who is deported or exiled from a country.|The deportee was sent back to his country of origin.|Synonym: exile, refugee|Antonym: citizen, resident|n.

deport:/dɪˈpɔːrt/
verb|驱逐出境|To expel (a person, especially a foreigner) from a country.|The government deported the illegal immigrants.|Synonym: expel, banish, exile|Antonym: admit, welcome, naturalize|v.
