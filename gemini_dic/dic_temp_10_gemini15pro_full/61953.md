emulsifies:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪz/
verb
乳化
causes (a liquid) to become an emulsion.
The detergent emulsifies the grease and oil. | 洗涤剂乳化油脂。
Synonyms: blend, mix, combine, merge
Antonyms: separate, divide
词性: 动词
