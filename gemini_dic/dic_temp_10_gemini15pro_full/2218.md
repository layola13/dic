addressability:/əˌdrɛsəˈbɪləti/| n.|可寻址性，可编址性|The ability of a device or system to be individually identified and accessed.  The ability of individual locations in memory or storage to be accessed directly. |The increased addressability of memory allows for more efficient data retrieval. 内存可寻址性的提高使得数据检索更加高效。

The network offers high addressability, enabling precise targeting of individual customers. 该网络提供了高可寻址性，可以精确地针对个体客户。|近义词：reachability, accessibility|反义词：inaccessibility|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From address + -ability.  "Address" ultimately comes from the Latin "ad" (to) + "directus" (straight).


USAGE->
The addressability of each component is crucial for effective system management.  每个组件的可寻址性对于有效的系统管理至关重要。

Improved addressability of data enhances the performance of the database.  改进的数据可寻址性增强了数据库的性能。
