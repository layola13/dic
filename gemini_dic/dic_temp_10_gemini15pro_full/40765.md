convinced:/kənˈvɪnst/| adj.|确信的，信服的|Feeling certain that something is true.|例句：I am convinced of his innocence. 我确信他是无辜的。|近义词：certain, sure, persuaded|反义词：uncertain, doubtful, unconvinced|词性:形容词
词源:From Late Latin *convinctus, past participle of convincere (“to overcome, convict”), from com- (“together, with”) + vincere (“to conquer”).
