complexness:/kəmˈpleksnɪs/
n.
复杂性，复杂度
the state or quality of being complex.
The complexness of the situation made it difficult to find a solution.  这种情况的复杂性使得很难找到解决方案。
complexity, intricacy, complication
simplicity, easiness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From complex + -ness.
