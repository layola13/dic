interlinear:/ˌɪntərˈlɪniər/ | adj.|行间的，逐行对照的|Placed or printed between the lines of a text, typically a different language or a literal translation.|The book presented the poem in its original language with an interlinear translation. 这本书以原文和逐行对照的翻译呈现了这首诗。|Synonyms: line-by-line, parallel, literal|Antonyms: summarized, paraphrased|Adjective
