obnoxiously:/əbˈnɑːkʃəsli/ /ɒbˈnɒkʃəsli/| adv.|令人讨厌地，讨厌地，令人反感地|In a very unpleasant or offensive way.  It describes behavior that is loud, rude, or attention-seeking in a way that annoys other people.|He laughed obnoxiously loud at his own jokes. 他对着自己的笑话发出令人讨厌的大笑。|The music from the party next door was obnoxiously loud. 隔壁派对的音乐吵得令人讨厌。|近义词: offensively, rudely, boisterously,  unpleasantly|反义词: politely, respectfully, discreetly|词性:副词

obnoxious:/əbˈnɑːkʃəs/ /ɒbˈnɒkʃəs/| adj.|讨厌的，令人反感的，可憎的|Extremely unpleasant. It describes someone or something that is very annoying or offensive.|He was being obnoxious and wouldn't leave us alone. 他很讨厌，不肯放过我们。|The smell from the factory was obnoxious. 工厂散发出的气味令人作呕。|近义词: offensive, unpleasant, disagreeable,  repulsive|反义词: pleasant, agreeable, delightful|词性:形容词
