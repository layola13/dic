anticaking:/ˌæntiˈkeɪkɪŋ/
adjective
防结块的
Preventing something, especially a powdered substance, from forming lumps or sticking together.
Anticaking agents are added to salt to keep it free-flowing.  食盐中添加了抗结剂，以保持其自由流动。
The flour contains anticaking agents.  面粉中含有抗结剂。
近义词: free-flowing, non-caking
反义词: caking, clumping
词性: adjective

anticaking:/ˌæntiˈkeɪkɪŋ/
noun
防结块剂
A substance that prevents something, especially a powdered substance, from forming lumps or sticking together.
Silica gel is a common anticaking agent.  硅胶是一种常见的防结块剂。
The product label lists the anticaking agent used.  产品标签列出了所使用的防结块剂。
近义词: desiccant, free-flow agent
反义词: coagulant
词性: noun
