dalits:/ˈdɑːlɪts/| n.|贱民，不可接触者|Members of the lowest caste in India, formerly called "untouchables," who have historically been subjected to severe social and economic discrimination.|例句：The dalits continue to face discrimination in many areas of life. 贱民在生活的许多方面仍然面临歧视。

例句：Government policies aim to uplift the dalits and improve their socioeconomic status. 政府政策旨在提升贱民并改善他们的社会经济地位。|近义词：untouchables, outcastes|反义词: Brahmins (highest caste) |词性: Noun
