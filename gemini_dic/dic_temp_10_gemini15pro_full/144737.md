over-annotates:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌteɪts/ | v. | 过度注释，过度注解 |  Adds excessive annotations or notes to something, often to the point of obscuring the original meaning. | The professor over-annotates his students' papers, making it difficult to see their original work. 教授在他的学生的论文上做了过多的注释，以至于很难看到他们最初的作品。 | over-explains, over-documents | under-annotates | verb
