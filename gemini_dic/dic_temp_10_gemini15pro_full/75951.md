funiculars:/fəˈnɪkjələrz/
|n.|缆车；缆索铁路|A cable railway, especially one on a mountainside.|We took the funicular up the steep hillside. 我们乘坐缆车上了陡峭的山坡。|
|近义词：cable car, incline railway, mountain railway|反义词：-|词性：名词|

funicular:/fəˈnɪkjələr/
|adj.|缆索的；用缆索拉动的|Of or relating to a funicular railway.|The funicular system was designed by a Swiss engineer. 这个缆索系统是由一位瑞士工程师设计的。|
|近义词：cable-operated|反义词：-|词性：形容词|
