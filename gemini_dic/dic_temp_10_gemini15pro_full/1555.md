achieved: /əˈtʃiːvd/
verb
达到，取得，实现
Successfully bring about or reach (a desired objective or result) by effort, skill, or courage.
She achieved her ambition of becoming a doctor. = 她实现了成为一名医生的抱负。

He finally achieved his goal of running a marathon. = 他最终实现了跑马拉松的目标。

近义词: accomplished, attained, realized, gained, earned
反义词: failed, missed, lost
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English acheven, achieven (“to finish, complete, carry out, accomplish, perform”), from Old French achever (“to finish, come to an end, complete, perfect, execute”), from a- (“to”) + chief (“head, end”).  Ultimately from Latin caput (“head”).
