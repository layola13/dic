dialectical:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪkəl/
adjective
辩证的
Relating to or employing dialectic; concerned with or acting through opposing forces.
Example:  The dialectical method involves examining and resolving contradictions. 辩证法包含对矛盾的检验和解决。

Example:  He offered a dialectical interpretation of the historical events. 他对这些历史事件提出了辩证的解释。

synonym: argumentative, logical, reasoned
antonym: dogmatic, unreasoning, intuitive
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dialecticus, from Ancient Greek διαλεκτικός (dialektikós, “relating to conversation”), from διαλέγομαι (dialégomai, “I converse”).
