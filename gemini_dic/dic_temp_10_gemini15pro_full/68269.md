faker:/ˈfeɪkər/| n.|骗子，伪造者，冒牌货|A person who pretends to be someone or something they are not, typically in order to deceive others.|He was exposed as a faker, pretending to be a qualified doctor. 他被揭露是一个骗子，假装是一个合格的医生。|Synonyms: impostor, fraud, charlatan, pretender|Antonyms: genuine, authentic|noun

faker:/ˈfeɪkər/| adj.|假的，伪造的|(informal) Not genuine; fake.|He tried to pay with faker money. 他试图用假币付款。|Synonyms: counterfeit, forged, sham, bogus|Antonyms: real, genuine, authentic|adjective
