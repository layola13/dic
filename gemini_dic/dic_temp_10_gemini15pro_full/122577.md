moderators:/ˈmɒdəreɪtəz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəreɪtərz/ (US)| n. |主持人，仲裁者，调解人|A person who presides over a discussion or meeting, ensuring that it stays on topic and that participants follow the rules of conduct.  Also, a person who monitors content on a website or online forum, deleting or editing inappropriate material.|The moderators kept the debate civil and focused on the key issues.  主持人使辩论保持文明，并集中讨论关键问题。|The forum moderators deleted several offensive posts. 论坛版主删除了几篇攻击性帖子。|synonyms:  chairperson, facilitator, arbiter, mediator, censor|antonyms: participant, instigator|noun
