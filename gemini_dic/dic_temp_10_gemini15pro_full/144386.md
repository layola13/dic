outstretch:/ˌaʊtˈstretʃ/ | v. |伸展，延伸|To extend or stretch out (something, especially a hand or arm).|She outstretched her arms to embrace her son. 她伸开双臂拥抱她的儿子。|The eagle outstretched its wings and soared into the sky.  老鹰展开翅膀，翱翔天空。|近义词：extend, spread, reach out|反义词:contract, withdraw|动词

词源:  From Middle English *outstrechen, from Old English *ūtstreċċan, from *ūt (“out”) + *streċċan (“to stretch”). Cognate with Dutch uitstrekken (“to stretch out”), German ausstrecken (“to stretch out”).
