ethylenes:/ˈeθəlˌinz/
n.
乙烯类
A group of unsaturated hydrocarbons containing a carbon-carbon double bond.
Ethylene is used in the production of polyethylene plastics. 乙烯用于生产聚乙烯塑料。
近义词:alkenes, olefins
反义词:alkanes
