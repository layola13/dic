detention:/dɪˈtɛnʃən/| n.|拘留，扣押，留堂|The act of detaining or the state of being detained; enforced delay.|He was sentenced to five days' detention. 他被判处五天拘留。|Synonyms: confinement, imprisonment, custody, arrest, holding|Antonyms: release, liberation, freedom|noun

detention:/dɪˈtɛnʃən/| n.|留堂|A punishment in which a student is made to stay at school after others have gone home.|She got detention for talking in class. 她因为上课讲话被留堂。|Synonyms: punishment, penalty, discipline|Antonyms: reward|noun
