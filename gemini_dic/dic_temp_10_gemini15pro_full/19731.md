bill:/bɪl/| n. vt.|账单，钞票，法案，议案，鸟嘴，给…开账单，宣布，把…列入节目单|A piece of paper money, a statement of charges for goods or services, a draft of a proposed law presented to a legislature, the beak of a bird.|例句：Could you bring me the bill, please? 请把账单给我好吗？|近义词：invoice, account, check, note, beak|反义词:payment, receipt|noun
bill:/bɪl/| vt.|给…开账单，宣布，把…列入节目单|To send a bill to someone, to advertise or announce something, to schedule something.|例句：The theater billed her as a rising star. 剧院宣传她是冉冉升起的新星。|近义词：invoice, charge, advertise, announce, schedule|反义词:pay|verb
