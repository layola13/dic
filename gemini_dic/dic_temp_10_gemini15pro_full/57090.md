doughtier:/ˈdɔːtɪər/ | adj. | 更勇敢的，更坚定的 | Braver and more resolute; more valiant.  |  Example 1: He was a doughtier warrior than his brother. 他是一位比他兄弟更勇敢的战士。

Example 2:  She proved to be a doughtier opponent than anyone expected. 她被证明是一个比任何人预期的都更坚定的对手。 | 近义词: braver, more courageous, more valiant, stouter, bolder | 反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful | 词性: adjective

doughty:/ˈdaʊti/ | adj. | 勇敢的，坚定的 | Brave and resolute; valiant. | Example 1: The doughty knight faced the dragon. 勇敢的骑士面对巨龙。

Example 2:  She was a doughty defender of human rights. 她是一位勇敢的人权捍卫者。 | 近义词: brave, courageous, valiant, resolute, bold | 反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful | 词性: adjective


词源:
From Middle English *doughti, *duhti, from Old English *dōhtig (“valiant”), from Proto-Germanic *dugtīgaz (“suitable, useful”).  Ultimately from the same root as *dugō (“advantage, use”).  The comparative form *doughtier is formed by adding the suffix *-er, and the superlative *doughtiest adds *-est.
