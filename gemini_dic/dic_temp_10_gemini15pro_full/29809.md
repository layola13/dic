cauf:/kɔːf/ | n. |  渔夫的篮子; 鱼篓 | A basket used by fishermen, typically made of wicker or similar material. | The fisherman carried his cauf full of freshly caught trout. 渔夫提着装满新鲜捕获的鳟鱼的鱼篓。 | 近义词: creel, basket | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps container if considering its function as something to hold things) | 词性: noun

词源: Likely of obscure origin. Possibly related to Old French *chaufe, meaning a stove or furnace, perhaps in the sense of a container holding something. This etymology is speculative.
