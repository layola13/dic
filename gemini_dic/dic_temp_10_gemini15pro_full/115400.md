manstealing:/ˈmænˌstiːlɪŋ/
n.
男人偷窃;拐卖男子;拐骗男人;诱拐男人
The act of stealing or kidnapping a man.  This term is often used in a historical context, particularly regarding the forced recruitment or enslavement of men.
Historically, manstealing was a serious crime, often punishable by death. 从历史上看，偷窃男人是一项严重的罪行，通常会被判处死刑。
The practice of manstealing was prevalent during certain periods of history. 在某些历史时期，偷窃男人的做法很普遍。
近义词: kidnapping, abduction, trafficking
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "liberation," "rescue")
词性: noun
