friars:/ˈfraɪərz/| n.|修士；托钵僧；<古>（男）修道士|Members of certain religious orders, especially the four mendicant orders (Augustinians, Carmelites, Dominicans, and Franciscans).|The friars lived a simple life of poverty and devotion. 这些修士过着简朴的贫困和虔诚的生活。|The friars gathered for evening prayer. 修士们聚集在一起做晚祷。|synonyms: monk, brother, religious|antonyms: layman, secular|noun
