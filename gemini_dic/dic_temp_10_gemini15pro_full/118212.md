mephitis: /məˈfaɪtɪs/ /mɪˈfaɪtɪs/
noun
臭气，恶臭
A noxious exhalation from the earth; a strong, offensive smell; stench.
例句：The mephitis from the rotting carcass was overwhelming.  腐烂尸体的臭气令人难以忍受。

例句：A mephitis of stale cigarette smoke hung in the air.  空气中弥漫着一股陈腐的香烟味。
近义词：stench, reek, odor, fetor
反义词：fragrance, aroma, perfume
词性: noun
