dropdown:/ˈdrɒpdaʊn/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpdaʊn/ (US)| n. adj.|下拉菜单，下拉式，下拉列表|A list of items that appears on a computer screen when you click on a word or icon, allowing you to choose one of the items.|Example: Select your country from the dropdown menu. 从下拉菜单中选择你的国家。|Synonyms: list box, pull-down menu, option menu, combo box|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider "text field," "input box" if contrasting with interactive selection)|Noun, Adjective
