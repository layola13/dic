beloochee:/bəˈluːtʃi/ /bəˈloʊtʃi/
noun|俾路支人；俾路支语|A member of a predominantly Muslim people inhabiting chiefly Baluchistan. Also: the Iranian language of the Baluchi.|例句：The Beloochee are known for their rich cultural traditions. 俾路支人以其丰富的文化传统而闻名。|例句：He is fluent in Beloochee. 他能说流利的俾路支语。|近义词：Baluchi, Baloch|反义词:|n.

beloochee:/bəˈluːtʃi/ /bəˈloʊtʃi/
adjective|俾路支的；俾路支人的；俾路支语的|Relating to Baluchistan, its inhabitants, or their language.|例句：Beloochee embroidery is highly prized. 俾路支刺绣备受珍视。|例句：She is studying Beloochee history. 她正在学习俾路支历史。|近义词：Baluchi, Baloch|反义词:|adj.
