beading:/ˈbiːdɪŋ/ | n. | 串珠, 珠饰, 珠子细工 | Small, decorative balls or cylinders, typically pierced for threading onto a string or wire; the action of threading beads onto a string or wire to create jewelry or other decorative items. |  She was wearing a necklace with intricate beading. 她戴着一条做工复杂的串珠项链。 | synonyms: beadwork, stringing, ornamentation | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

beading:/ˈbiːdɪŋ/ | n. | (液体表面)成珠状, (汗水)凝成珠 | The formation of small droplets on a surface, as of sweat on skin. |  Beading of condensation formed on the cold glass.  冷凝水在冰冷的玻璃上凝结成珠。 | synonyms: droplets, condensation | antonyms: evaporation, absorption | noun
