nondefamatory:/ˌnɒndɪˈfæmətri/ | adj. | 非诽谤性的 | Not containing or constituting defamation; not slanderous or libelous. | The article was found to be nondefamatory and therefore legally permissible. 这篇文章被认定为非诽谤性，因此在法律上是允许的。|  The lawyer argued that his client's statements were nondefamatory and protected by free speech.  律师辩称，其当事人的言论不具诽谤性，受言论自由的保护。 | Synonyms: harmless, innocent, inoffensive | Antonyms: defamatory, libelous, slanderous | adjective
