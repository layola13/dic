overattentive: /ˌoʊvərəˈtɛntɪv/ | adj. | 过于关心的，过分殷勤的 | Paying excessive attention; too attentive.  Showing more care and attention than is usual or necessary. |  He was so overattentive to her needs that she felt smothered. 他对她需求的过度关心让她感到窒息。

She found his overattentive behavior rather irritating. 她觉得他过分殷勤的行为相当烦人。 | 近义词:  fussy, solicitous, officious | 反义词: inattentive, negligent, indifferent | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "attentive" (from Latin "attendere," meaning "to stretch toward, give heed to").
