hassler:/ˈhæslər/| n.| 难缠的人，麻烦制造者，强买强卖的人|A person who aggressively sells things, especially on the street; a person who causes trouble or difficulty.|例句：The street hasslers were trying to sell fake watches to tourists. 街上的强买强卖者试图向游客兜售假表。|近义词：hustler, peddler, nuisance, bother, pest|反义词:helper, assistant|词性:noun

hassle:/ˈhæsəl/| n. vt.| 麻烦，困难；烦扰，纠缠|A situation causing difficulty or trouble; to annoy or bother persistently.|例句：It was a real hassle getting the visa.  获得签证真是麻烦。|近义词：trouble, difficulty, bother, annoyance, inconvenience|反义词:ease, convenience, help|词性:noun, verb

词源:
Likely from the earlier verb *hassen* "to annoy, harass," of uncertain origin.  May be related to the Middle Dutch *hatsen*, "to set dogs on, incite," or to the Low German *hasseln*, "to quarrel, wrangle."
