chippered:/ˈtʃɪpərd/
adjective
发出啁啾声的；快活的
making cheerful sounds; feeling cheerful and lively
The chippered birds greeted the dawn.  鸟儿欢快地迎接黎明。
He woke up feeling chippered and ready to start the day. 他醒来时感到兴高采烈，准备开始新的一天。
近义词: cheerful, lively, merry, chirpy
反义词: gloomy, sad, depressed
词性: adjective

chippered:/ˈtʃɪpərd/
verb
(鸟)发出啁啾声；使快活；使振奋
(of a bird) make short, sharp, high-pitched sounds; make someone feel cheerful and lively
The birds chippered in the trees. 鸟儿在树上唧唧喳喳地叫。
The good news chippered him up. 好消息让他振作起来。
近义词: chirp, twitter, cheer, enliven
反义词: sadden, depress
词性: verb


词源:  From the verb "chirp," imitative of a bird's sound, + "-er" (forming agent nouns) + "-ed" (forming past participles or adjectives).
