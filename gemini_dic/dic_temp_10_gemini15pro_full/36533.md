collaboration:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/| n.|合作，协作|The project was a collaboration between several departments. 这个项目是几个部门合作的成果。|例句：The collaboration between the two companies produced a successful product.  两家公司的合作生产出了一款成功的产品。

例句：The scientists announced a new research collaboration. 科学家们宣布了一项新的研究合作。

近义词：cooperation, partnership, teamwork, alliance|反义词:conflict, competition, rivalry|词性:noun

词源: Late 19th century: from French, or from late Latin collaboratio(n-), from the verb collaborare ‘work with’, from col- ‘together’ + laborare ‘to work’.
