all-rounder:/ˌɔːlˈraʊndər/ | n. | 全才；全能选手；多面手 | A person who is competent in many different fields or activities. |  He's a real all-rounder – he excels at sports, music, and academics. 他是一个真正的全才——他在体育、音乐和学术上都很优秀。| versatile person, jack-of-all-trades | specialist | noun


all-rounder:/ˌɔːlˈraʊndər/ | adj. | 全面的；多方面的 | Having or exhibiting many skills or qualities. |  This is an all-rounder product suitable for various applications. 这是一款适用于各种用途的多功能产品。 | versatile, comprehensive | specialized, limited | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From all + rounder (comparative of round, meaning "complete, full").
