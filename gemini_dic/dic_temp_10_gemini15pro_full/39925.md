constrict:/kənˈstrɪkt/| v.|限制，约束|To limit or restrict something, often freedom or ability.|The government's policies constricted economic growth. 政府的政策限制了经济增长。|synonyms: restrict, limit, restrain, curb|antonyms: free, liberate, release, expand|verb
