one-on-one:/ˌwʌn ɑːn ˈwʌn/ /ˌwʌn ɒn ˈwʌn/| adj. n.|一对一的，一对一谈话|A meeting or other interaction involving only two people.|例句：I had a one-on-one with my boss about my performance review. 我和我的老板进行了一对一的绩效评估面谈。|近义词：individual, private, personal|反义词: group, collective|词性:形容词,名词

one-on-ones:/ˌwʌn ɑːn ˈwʌnz/ /ˌwʌn ɒn ˈwʌnz/| n.|一对一谈话（复数）|Multiple instances of one-on-one meetings or interactions.|例句：The coach scheduled regular one-on-ones with each player. 教练安排了与每个球员的定期一对一谈话。|近义词：individual meetings, private discussions|反义词: group meetings|词性:名词
