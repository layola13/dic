lothario:/ləˈθɑːrioʊ/ /ləˈθɛərioʊ/
noun
放荡公子；玩弄女性的男子
a man who has many sexual relationships with women
例句：He was known as a lothario, breaking hearts wherever he went. 他被称为一个花花公子，走到哪里都会伤透女人的心。

例句：The aging lothario still tried to charm the young women at the bar.  这个老色鬼仍然试图在酒吧里吸引年轻女性。

近义词：womanizer, playboy, philanderer, rake, seducer
反义词：faithful husband/partner, monogamist
词性：noun
