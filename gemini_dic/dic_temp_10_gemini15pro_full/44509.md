crosslet:/ˈkrɒslɪt/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːslɪt/ (US)| n. |十字形饰物，四臂等长的小十字架 |A small cross with arms of equal length, each terminating in a short transverse bar.  Often used in heraldry. |The knight's shield bore a silver crosslet on a field of blue. 骑士的盾牌上有一个蓝色背景的银色十字形饰物。| synonym: cross, crucifix | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider symbols of other religions or lack of symbol) | noun
