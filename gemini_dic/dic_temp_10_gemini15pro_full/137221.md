nonsuppressivenesses:/ˌnɒnsəˈprɛsɪvnəsnɪsɪz/
n.
非抑制性，不压制性
The state or quality of being nonsuppressive; instances of not suppressing something.  The plural form emphasizes multiple occurrences or aspects of this quality.
The nonsuppressivenesses of the new government led to a flourishing of artistic expression. 新政府的不压制性导致了艺术表达的繁荣。
Near Synonyms: tolerances, permissivenesses, forbearances
Antonyms: repressions, suppressions
词性: noun
