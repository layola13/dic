overself:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛlf/ | n. | 超我; 超越自我 |  The overself represents the highest level of self-awareness and spiritual development, a transcendent state beyond the ego. |  He strives to connect with his overself through meditation. 他努力通过冥想与他的超我连接。| Synonyms: higher self, transpersonal self, true self | Antonyms: ego, lower self | noun

Etymology:  "Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "self" (Old English self, from Proto-Germanic *selbaz). The concept of "overself" is relatively modern, primarily used in spiritual and psychological contexts.
