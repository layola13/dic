immetrical:/ɪˈmetrɪkəl/
| adj. | 不对称的，不匀称的 | Lacking symmetry; not symmetrical. | The artist intentionally made the sculpture immetrical to create a sense of imbalance.  艺术家故意使雕塑不对称，以营造一种不平衡感。| The building's immetrical design made it stand out from the surrounding architecture.  这座建筑不对称的设计使其在周围的建筑中脱颖而出。 | asymmetrical, uneven, unbalanced, disproportionate, irregular | symmetrical, balanced, even, regular | adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + metrical (relating to measurement or meter).  Ultimately from Greek *metron* (measure).
