default's:/dɪˈfɔːlts/| n.|违约，默认，系统设定|The loan went into default after he missed three payments.  他连续三个月未付款，贷款违约了。|近义词：failure, omission, lapse|反义词:compliance, fulfillment|noun

Etymology: From Old French *defaulte*, past participle of *defaillir* "to fail, be lacking," from Latin *defallere* "to deceive, fail," from *de-* "down, away" + *fallere* "to deceive."
