一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? = 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for so long? = 他们一直在讨论什么那么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 你到今天结束前会完成什么？
What will she have achieved by next year? = 到明年她会取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
