morganatic:/ˌmɔːrɡəˈnætɪk/
adjective
贵贱通婚的，婚后妻子及子女无权继承丈夫爵位财产的
Of, relating to, or being a marriage between a person of royal or noble birth and a person of lower social rank, in which the rank of the lower-ranking spouse and any children of the marriage are not raised to that of the higher-ranking spouse and neither the spouse nor any children can inherit the higher-ranking spouse's titles or possessions.
The king entered into a morganatic marriage with his mistress. 国王与他的情妇缔结了贵贱通婚。

morganatic:/ˌmɔːrɡəˈnætɪk/
adjective
贵贱通婚的
Relating to a marriage between a member of a royal family and someone of a lower social rank, in which the lower-ranking partner and any children do not receive the royal status, titles, or property of the royal partner.
Their marriage was morganatic, so she never became queen. 他们的婚姻是贵贱通婚，所以她从未成为王后。

Synonyms: left-handed marriage
Antonyms: regular marriage
词性: adjective
