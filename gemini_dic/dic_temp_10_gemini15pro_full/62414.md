endognathal:/ˌɛndoʊˈnæθəl/ | adj. | 内颚的；内口式的 | Having the mouthparts enclosed within the head, as in insects and some other arthropods. | Example 1: Endognathal insects have their mouthparts retracted within the head capsule. 内颚昆虫的口器缩回头部壳内。

Example 2:  The endognathal condition is considered an evolutionary advancement in insects. 内颚状态被认为是昆虫的进化进步。 | Near synonyms: entognathous | Antonyms: ectognathal | Word type: Adjective
