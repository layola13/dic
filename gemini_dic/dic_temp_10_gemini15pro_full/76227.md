futurable:/ˈfjuːtʃərəbl/
adjective
可预见的，未来可能的
able to be foreseen or predicted; relating to the future.
The futurable impact of climate change is a major concern. 气候变化对未来的潜在影响是一个主要问题。
synonyms: foreseeable, predictable, anticipated, prospective
antonyms: unforeseeable, unpredictable, unexpected
