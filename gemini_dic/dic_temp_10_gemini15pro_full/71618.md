flatten:/ˈflætən/| v.|使变平；使变单调；彻底打败；（尤指经济）使萧条；|to make something flat or flatter|The steamroller flattened the road surface.  蒸汽压路机把路面压平了。|level, smooth, compress|raise, roughen|verb
flatten:/ˈflætən/| adj.|平坦的；单调的；萧条的|flat|The countryside around here is very flatten.  这附近的乡村很平坦。|level, smooth|uneven, rough|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flettian, from *flett "floor, dwelling," from Proto-Germanic *flatja- "flat, level," from PIE *plat- "spread, flat."
