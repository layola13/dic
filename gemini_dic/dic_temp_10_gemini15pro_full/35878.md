cockyolly:/ˌkɒkiˈɒli/ | adj. | 狂妄自大的，骄傲自满的 |  Arrogant and conceited; overly self-assured, often in a boastful or showy way.  Derived from "cocky" (arrogant) and likely influenced by words like "jolly" (cheerful, lively), though the connection is more in sound than meaning. | He strutted around with a cockyolly air, bragging about his latest conquest. 他趾高气扬地走来走去，吹嘘着他最新的战利品。|  Synonyms: arrogant, conceited, pompous, swaggering, haughty | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | Adjective
