feeblest:/ˈfiːblɪst/| adj.|最微弱的，最虚弱的|Superlative form of feeble: most feeble.  Meaning the weakest, faintest, or least effective.|Her feeblest attempt at a smile was still heartwarming. 她即使是最微弱的笑意也仍然温暖人心。|Synonyms: weakest, frailest, faintest, slightest|Antonyms: strongest, healthiest, most powerful|adjective
