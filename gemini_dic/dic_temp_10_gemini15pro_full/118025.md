memphis:/ˈmɛmfɪs/| n.|孟菲斯（美国田纳西州西南部城市）|A city in southwestern Tennessee, on the Mississippi River.  It is a major center for the music industry, especially blues and rock and roll.|Elvis Presley's home, Graceland, is located in Memphis.  猫王故居雅园位于孟菲斯。|近义词: (As a place name, it has no direct synonyms.)|反义词: (As a place name, it has no direct synonyms.)|词性:noun

Etymology: Named after Memphis, Egypt, an ancient capital on the Nile River.
