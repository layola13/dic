digestif:/diːˈʒɛstɪf/ /daɪˈdʒɛstɪf/ | n. | 餐后酒，消化酒 | An alcoholic drink, such as brandy or liqueur, taken after a meal to aid digestion. |  After dinner, we enjoyed a digestif of fine cognac.  晚餐后，我们享用了上好的干邑白兰地作为餐后酒。

| Synonyms: after-dinner drink, liqueur | Antonyms:  aperitif | noun
