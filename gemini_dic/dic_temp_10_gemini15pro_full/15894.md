ballotage:/ˌbæləˈtɑːʒ/ /ˈbælətɑːʒ/ | n.|决选投票；复选|A second ballot held to decide an election when no candidate has received an absolute majority in the first round.  A system of voting in which if no candidate receives an absolute majority, a second ballot is held between the two highest-scoring candidates.|The presidential election went to a second ballotage.  总统选举进入了第二轮投票。|runoff, second ballot|first ballot|noun

ballotage:/ˌbæləˈtɑːʒ/ /ˈbælətɑːʒ/ | vt.|将…交付复选|To submit (a candidate or issue) to a second ballot.|The candidate was ballotaged after the first round of voting.  第一轮投票后，候选人进入了复选。|put to a second ballot, submit to a runoff| - |verb
