pastorlike:/ˈpæstərlaɪk/
adjective
像牧师的；牧师般的
Resembling or characteristic of a pastor, especially in being kind, gentle, or devoted to the spiritual well-being of others.
His pastorlike demeanor made him approachable and trustworthy. 他像牧师一样的举止使他显得平易近人且值得信赖。
Synonyms: priestly, ministerial, clerical, devout, pious, saintly
Antonyms:  irreligious, impious, ungodly
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From pastor + -like.  "Pastor" comes from Late Latin *pastor, from Latin pastor "shepherd," from pascere "to feed, pasture."  "-like" is an Old English suffix meaning "having the form or characteristics of."
