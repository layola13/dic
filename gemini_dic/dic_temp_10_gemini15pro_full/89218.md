homework: /ˈhoʊmwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈhəʊmwɜːk/ (UK)
noun
家庭作业
schoolwork that a student is required to do at home
My son has a lot of homework tonight.  我的儿子今晚有很多家庭作业。
I finished my homework early so I could watch TV. 我很早就完成了作业，所以可以看电视了。
synonyms: assignment, schoolwork, studies
antonyms: leisure, playtime, free time
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "home" + "work".  "Home" meaning one's dwelling place, and "work" meaning tasks or labor.
