basilicae:/bəˈsɪlɪkiː/
n.
长方形教堂；巴西利卡（尤指古罗马或基督教的）
The plural form of basilica, referring to large oblong halls or churches, especially in ancient Roman or Christian architecture.
Many ancient Roman basilicae were converted into churches. 许多古罗马长方形教堂被改建成了教堂。
The basilicae of Rome are magnificent. 罗马的长方形教堂很壮观。
近义词:cathedrals, churches
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps small chapels or shrines)
词性:Noun
