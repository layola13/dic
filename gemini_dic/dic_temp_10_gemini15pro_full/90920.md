hurdle:/ˈhɜːrdl/ (US) /ˈhɜːdl/ (UK)| n. v.|障碍；栏架；跨栏赛跑；克服(障碍)；跳过(栏架)|A hurdle is a frame or fence for jumping over in a race, or any problem or difficulty that must be overcome.|He fell at the last hurdle. 他在最后一个栏架处摔倒了。|近义词：obstacle, barrier, difficulty, challenge, impediment|反义词:advantage, aid, assistance|noun
hurdle:/ˈhɜːrdl/ (US) /ˈhɜːdl/ (UK)| v. |克服(障碍)；跳过(栏架)|To hurdle means to overcome an obstacle or jump over a hurdle.|The company hurdled the financial crisis. 公司克服了金融危机。|近义词：overcome, clear, jump, negotiate, surmount|反义词:fail, surrender, succumb|verb
