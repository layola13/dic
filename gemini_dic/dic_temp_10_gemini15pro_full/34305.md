clarion:/ˈklæriən/| adj. n.|嘹亮的，清晰的；号角|Loud and clear; a medieval trumpet.|The clarion call to action roused the troops. 嘹亮的行动号召激起了部队的士气.  |The king’s clarion sounded, signaling the start of the battle. 国王的号角响起，标志着战斗开始。|近义词：resonant, ringing, trumpet|反义词：muffled, faint, hushed|词性:形容词,名词
词源: From Old French *clarien (clarion, trumpet), from Vulgar Latin *clario, from Latin clarus (clear, bright, loud).
