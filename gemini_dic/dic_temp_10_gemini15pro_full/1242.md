accomplished: /əˈkʌmplɪʃt/ /əˈkɑːmplɪʃt/
adjective
熟练的，有才华的；完成的
Highly skilled and proficient.  Successfully completed.
She is an accomplished pianist. 她是一位技艺精湛的钢琴家。
The merger was finally accomplished after months of negotiations. 经过几个月的谈判，合并终于完成了。
近义词: skilled, proficient, talented, gifted, completed, finished, achieved
反义词: unskilled, incompetent, inept, unfinished, incomplete
词性: adjective

accomplish: /əˈkʌmplɪʃ/ /əˈkɑːmplɪʃ/
verb
完成，实现；达到
To successfully complete or achieve something.  To reach a certain state or condition.
He accomplished his goal of running a marathon. 他完成了跑马拉松的目标。
We can accomplish more if we work together. 如果我们一起工作，我们可以完成更多的事情。
近义词: achieve, complete, fulfill, attain, realize
反义词: fail, abandon, forfeit
词性: verb

词源: From Middle English *accomplisshen*, from Anglo-Norman *acumplir*, from Old French *accomplir*, from Latin *ad complēre* (“to fill up, complete”), from *ad* (“to, towards”) + *complēre* (“to fill”).
