nonfictitiously:/ˌnɒnfɪkˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
非虚构地；真实地
in a way that is based on facts and not made up; truthfully
He wrote nonfictitiously about his experiences in war. 他如实地写下了他的战争经历。
Synonyms: truthfully, factually, accurately, genuinely, really
Antonyms: fictitiously, falsely, untruthfully
