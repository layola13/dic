abnegation's:/ˌæbnɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n.|放弃，克制，舍己|The act of renouncing or rejecting something.|例句：The monk's life was one of abnegation's, devoid of worldly pleasures.  这位僧侣的生活是克己的，没有任何世俗的乐趣。

例句：Her abnegation's of personal comfort for the sake of her family was admirable. 她为了家庭而放弃个人舒适的行为令人钦佩。|近义词：self-denial, renunciation, sacrifice|反义词:indulgence, self-gratification|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abnegationem, accusative singular of abnegatio (“a denial”), from abnegare (“to deny, refuse, renounce”), from ab- (“away from”) + negare (“to deny”).
