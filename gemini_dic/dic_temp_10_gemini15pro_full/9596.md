antisophists:/ˈæntiˈsɑːfɪsts/
n.
反对智者的人；反诡辩论者
Those who opposed the Sophists, a group of ancient Greek teachers of rhetoric and philosophy, often criticized for their relativistic views on truth and morality.
Plato was considered one of the prominent antisophists.  柏拉图被认为是杰出的反智者之一。
Synonyms: opponent of sophistry, critic of sophism
Antonyms: sophist, rhetorician
