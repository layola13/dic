farawayness:/ˌfɑːrəˈweɪnəs/| n.|遥远，远方|The farawayness of the stars makes them seem mysterious. 星星的遥远使它们显得神秘。|例句：There's a certain farawayness in his eyes. 他的眼神里有一种遥远的感觉。|例句：The photograph captured the farawayness of the mountains. 这张照片捕捉到了群山的遥远。|近义词：remoteness, distance, farness|反义词：nearness, closeness|词性:名词
Etymology:  From "far away" + "-ness". "Far" comes from Old English "feorr," from Proto-Germanic "*ferra-." "Away" comes from Old English "onweg," from "on" (on) and "weg" (way). "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
