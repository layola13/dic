belletristical:/ˌbɛlɪˈtrɪstɪkəl/
adjective
文学的；文艺的
Of or relating to belles-lettres; literary or artistic.  Characterized by literary style, typically elegant or refined.
Her writing style is quite belletristical, full of imagery and symbolism. 她的写作风格非常文艺，充满了意象和象征。
The professor gave a belletristical lecture on the development of the novel.  教授做了一个关于小说发展的文学讲座。
近义词:literary, artistic, refined, elegant
反义词:functional, practical, mundane
词性:adjective

词源:
From French *belles-lettres* ("fine letters"), from *belle* ("beautiful") + *lettres* ("letters").
