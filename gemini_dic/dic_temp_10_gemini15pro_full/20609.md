blackhead:/ˈblækˌhed/| n. |黑头|A small, dark plug of dried sebum and dead skin cells that blocks a pore, especially on the face.|例句：She tried squeezing the blackhead on her nose. 她试着挤掉鼻子上的黑头。

例句：He has a lot of blackheads on his forehead. 他的额头上有很多黑头。|近义词：comedo, zit, blemish|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider clear skin)|词性:noun
