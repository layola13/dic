brainwashing:/ˈbreɪnwɒʃɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbreɪnwɑːʃɪŋ/ (US)| n.|洗脑|The systematic and often forcible indoctrination to induce someone to give up basic political, social, or religious beliefs and attitudes and to accept contrasting regimented ideas.|The cult uses a variety of techniques, including sleep deprivation and chanting, for brainwashing its members.  邪教组织使用各种各样的手段，包括睡眠剥夺和反复吟唱，来给其成员洗脑。|synonyms: indoctrination, propaganda, mind control, coercion, manipulation|antonyms: deprogramming, education, enlightenment|noun
