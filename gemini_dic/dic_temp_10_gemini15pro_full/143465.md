outgabbled:/ˌaʊtˈɡæbəld/
verb
(outgabble的过去式和过去分词)
中文翻译：比...更健谈；胜过；击败
英文解析：Past tense and past participle of outgabble, meaning to talk more loudly and rapidly than someone else, so as to overwhelm or defeat them in argument or conversation.
例句：He outgabbled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中比他的对手更健谈。
例句：She was easily outgabbled by the more experienced salesperson. 她很容易就被更有经验的销售员说得哑口无言。
近义词：outtalk, outargue, outweigh
反义词：be silenced, be outdone
