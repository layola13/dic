nonincriminating:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
无罪的；不构成犯罪的；不牵连的
Not tending to incriminate; not suggesting that someone is guilty of a crime.

He gave a nonincriminating answer to the police officer's question.
他对警察的提问给出了一个无罪的回答。


The lawyer advised her client to stick to nonincriminating statements.
律师建议她的客户坚持无罪的陈述。


Synonyms:
exonerating, absolving, vindicating, clearing, acquitting

Antonyms:
incriminating, implicating, condemning, accusing


Etymology:
From non- + incriminating.  Incriminating comes from Late Latin incriminatus, past participle of incriminare "to accuse," from in- "in" + criminari "to accuse," from crimen "accusation, crime."
