fixity:/ˈfɪksɪti/| n.|固定性，不变性，稳定性|The fixity of the exchange rate provided stability for businesses.  汇率的固定性为企业提供了稳定性。|The fixity of his stare was unsettling. 他凝视的固定性令人不安。|近义词：stability, permanence, firmness|反义词：instability, fluidity, variability|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Middle English *fixite, from Old French fixité, from Latin fixitās (“fixedness”), from fixus (“fixed”), perfect passive participle of figō (“to fix, fasten”).
