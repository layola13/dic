collusive:/kəˈluːsɪv/
adjective
共谋的，串通的
Involving or characterized by collusion. Secretly cooperating for a dishonest or deceitful purpose.
例句：The collusive activities of the two companies resulted in higher prices for consumers.  两家公司的串通行为导致消费者价格上涨。

例句：The investigation uncovered a collusive scheme to fix prices in the industry. 调查揭露了该行业操纵价格的共谋计划。

近义词：conspiratorial, conniving, clandestine, fraudulent
反义词：honest, aboveboard, legitimate
词性: adjective
