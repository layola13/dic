murein:/ˈmjʊəraɪn/ | n. | 肽聚糖;胞壁质 | A polymer that is the main constituent of bacterial cell walls. It is a peptidoglycan consisting of polysaccharide chains cross-linked by short peptides. | The murein layer protects bacteria from osmotic lysis. 肽聚糖层保护细菌免受渗透裂解。 | The integrity of the murein sacculus is essential for bacterial survival. 肽聚糖囊的完整性对细菌的生存至关重要。 | synonyms: peptidoglycan | antonyms: N/A | noun
