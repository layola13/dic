lumbrical:/ˈlʌmbrɪk(ə)l/| adj. n.|蚓状的，蚓状肌|Relating to or resembling a worm; any of the small muscles in the hand and foot, associated with the tendons of the flexor digitorum profundus.|The lumbrical muscles help with fine motor control of the fingers. 蚓状肌有助于手指的精细运动控制。|近义词：vermiform|反义词:|adjective, noun
