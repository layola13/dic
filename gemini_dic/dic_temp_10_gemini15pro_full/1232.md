accompany /əˈkʌmpəni/ | vt. | 陪伴，伴随；伴奏 | To go somewhere with someone.  To happen or exist at the same time as something else. To play a musical instrument while someone else sings or plays the main tune. | She accompanied me to the doctor. 她陪我去看了医生。

The disease is often accompanied by fever. 这种疾病常伴有发烧。

The pianist accompanied the singer. 钢琴家为歌手伴奏。 | 近义词: escort, attend, go with, come with,  supplement | 反义词: leave, abandon | 动词

with /wɪð/ | prep. | 和…在一起；具有；用；关于 | In the company or presence of. Possessing, having, or carrying. Using (a tool or implement). | I went to the park with my friends. 我和朋友们去了公园。

A house with a garden.  带花园的房子。

He cut the bread with a knife. 他用刀切面包。

He's been struggling with the problem for weeks. 他几个星期以来一直在努力解决这个问题。 | 近义词: alongside, beside, by means of | 反义词: without, against | 介词

accompany with |  | 伴随；和…一起 |  Although "accompany with" is sometimes used, it is generally considered redundant and less idiomatic than simply using "accompany."  "Accompany" already implies "with," so adding "with" is unnecessary and can sound awkward.  | Incorrect:  He accompanied with me to the store.  Correct: He accompanied me to the store. 他陪我去商店。

Incorrect: The storm was accompanied with heavy rain.  Correct: The storm was accompanied by heavy rain.  暴风雨伴着大雨。 | 近义词: go with, attend | 反义词: leave, abandon |  词组


USAGE->

"Accompany with" is generally avoided.  The following examples demonstrate correct usage of "accompany" and "with":

一般现在时:

He accompanies his children to school every day. = 他每天陪孩子上学。
She accompanies her singing with guitar playing. = 她边弹吉他边唱歌。


一般过去时:

She accompanied him to the party last night. = 她昨晚陪他去参加了聚会。
Thunder accompanied the lightning. = 雷声伴随着闪电。


现在完成时:

He has accompanied many famous singers on stage. = 他在舞台上为许多著名歌手伴奏过。
The letter has been accompanied by a gift. = 这封信附带了一份礼物。


过去完成时:

She had accompanied her grandmother to the doctor before she went to work. = 她上班前陪祖母去看医生了。
The meal had been accompanied by fine wine. = 这顿饭配有上好的葡萄酒。


将来时:

I will accompany you to the airport tomorrow. = 我明天陪你去机场。
The performance will be accompanied by a live orchestra. = 演出将由现场管弦乐队伴奏。



PHRASE->

(Phrases using "accompany" - avoid "accompany with")

accompany someone to somewhere = 陪伴某人去某地
accompany something by something =  用…来补充…
accompany on an instrument = 用乐器伴奏


注意事项:

"Accompany with" is considered redundant. Use "accompany" followed by a direct object or "accompany" followed by "by" when indicating something that happens concurrently.
