/faɪl/ | n. v. |文件，档案；锉刀；纵列；归档；提出（申请等）|A collection of data or information stored under a specific name, usually on a computer or other electronic device. Also, a tool with a roughened surface used for smoothing or shaping metal, wood, etc. As a verb, it means to store (data) electronically or to submit (a legal document or application).|

例句：
I saved the file on my computer.
我将文件保存在我的电脑上。

Please file these documents alphabetically.
请按字母顺序归档这些文件。


近义词：document, record, data, dossier (n.); submit, lodge (v.)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun, verb


/faɪl/ | v. | 排成纵队行进|To walk in a line, one behind the other.|

例句：
The students filed into the classroom.
学生们排成一列走进教室。

The soldiers filed past the reviewing stand.
士兵们排成一列走过检阅台。


近义词：march, parade, procession
反义词: disperse, scatter
词性: verb
