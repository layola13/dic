mocha:/ˈmoʊkə/ (US) /ˈmɒkə/ (UK)| n. |摩卡咖啡；摩卡色|A type of coffee made with espresso, chocolate syrup, steamed milk, and often whipped cream. Also, a reddish-brown color.|例句：I ordered a mocha latte with extra whipped cream. 我点了一杯摩卡拿铁，加了很多鲜奶油。

例句：The walls were painted a warm mocha color. 墙壁被漆成了温暖的摩卡色。|近义词：latte, cappuccino, caffè mocha|反义词: (color) white, black |词性: noun

mocha:/ˈmoʊkə/ (US) /ˈmɒkə/ (UK)| adj. |摩卡色的|Having the color of mocha; reddish-brown.|例句：She wore a mocha-colored sweater. 她穿着一件摩卡色的毛衣。

例句：He chose a mocha-colored leather sofa. 他选择了一个摩卡色的皮沙发。|近义词：reddish-brown, chocolate-colored|反义词: (color) white, black |词性: adjective
