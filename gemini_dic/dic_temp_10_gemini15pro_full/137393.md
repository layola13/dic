nontenantable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnəntəbəl/
adjective
不可出租的，不宜居住的
Not fit to be rented or occupied, especially because of disrepair or unsafe conditions.
The apartment was declared nontenantable after the fire.  这套公寓在火灾后被宣布为不可出租。
The building was deemed nontenantable due to severe structural damage.  由于严重的结构损坏，该建筑被认为不宜居住。
synonym:uninhabitable, unlivable, unoccupiable
antonym: habitable, livable, rentable
词性: adjective
