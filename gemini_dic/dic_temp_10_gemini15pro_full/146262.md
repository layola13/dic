overfacile: /ˌoʊvərˈfæsɪl/ | adj. | 过于轻易的，过于肤浅的 | Too easily done or achieved; superficial.  Characterized by superficiality or a lack of depth, especially in thought or expression. | His overfacile solution to the complex problem was met with skepticism. 他对这个复杂问题过于简单的解决办法遭到了质疑。|  The book offered an overfacile analysis of the political situation. 这本书对政治形势的分析过于肤浅。 |  Synonyms: simplistic, shallow, superficial, glib, facile | Antonyms: profound, deep, thorough, complex, nuanced | 词性: 形容词
