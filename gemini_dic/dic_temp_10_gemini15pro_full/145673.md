overcooking:/ˌoʊvərˈkʊkɪŋ/ | n. | 过度烹饪；煮得过久 | The act of cooking food for too long, resulting in dry, tough, or burnt food. |  The steak was tough due to overcooking. 牛排因为煮太久而变硬了。| synonyms: burning, scorching | antonyms: undercooking | noun

overcook:/ˌoʊvərˈkʊk/ | vt. | 把…煮得太久；过度烹饪 | To cook food for too long, resulting in dry, tough, or burnt food. | Be careful not to overcook the vegetables. 小心别把蔬菜煮太久了。| synonyms: burn, scorch | antonyms: undercook | verb
