disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡjər/
verb
损毁，毁容
to spoil the appearance of something or someone, especially their face, by causing damage to it
The acid disfigured her face. 酸液毁了她的容貌。
synonyms: deform, mar, mutilate, spoil
antonyms: beautify, enhance, improve
词性: 动词

disfigurement:/dɪsˈfɪɡjərmənt/
noun
毁容，缺陷
the state of being disfigured, or something that disfigures
He suffered permanent disfigurement to his face and hands. 他的脸和手永久性毁容。
synonyms: blemish, defect, deformity, imperfection
antonyms: perfection, beauty
词性: 名词
