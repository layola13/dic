changs:/tʃæŋz/ | n. | 长氏中餐馆 (通常指P.F. Chang's) |  An American restaurant chain serving Asian-inspired cuisine. | We went to Chang's for dinner last night. 我们昨晚去长氏吃晚饭了。| synonyms: P.F. Chang's, Chinese restaurant (loosely), Asian restaurant (loosely) | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:  "Chang's" is derived from the name of Paul Fleming Chang, the co-founder of P.F. Chang's China Bistro.  While "Chang" is a common Chinese surname, in this context, it refers specifically to the restaurant brand.
