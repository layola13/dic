mansam:/ˈmænsəm/| n.| 人参 | Ginseng, a short, slow-growing plant with fleshy roots, prized in East Asia for its medicinal properties. | He bought some mansam to boost his energy. 他买了一些人参来增强体力。| 近义词: ginseng | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially other herbs with opposite effects, but this is context-dependent) | 词性: noun


词源: From Korean 인삼 (insam), ultimately from Chinese 人蔘 (rénshēn), literally "man root," due to the root's resemblance to a human figure.
