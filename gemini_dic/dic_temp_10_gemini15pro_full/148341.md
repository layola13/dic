overspeak: /ˌoʊvərˈspik/ | v. | 过言，说得过多，打断别人说话 | To speak too much or too long, often interrupting others; to speak excessively and thus obscure the meaning. | He tends to overspeak when he's nervous, making it hard to follow his point. 他紧张的时候往往说得太多，让人很难理解他的意思。| 近义词: ramble, prate, chatter | 反义词: be concise, listen | 词性：动词

overspeak: /ˌoʊvərˈspik/ | v. | 盖过…的声音 | To speak louder than someone else so that they cannot be heard. | The loud music overspoke the quiet conversation. 喧闹的音乐盖过了轻声的谈话。 | 近义词: drown out | 反义词: be inaudible | 词性：动词

词源: From "over-" (excessively) + "speak" (to utter words).
