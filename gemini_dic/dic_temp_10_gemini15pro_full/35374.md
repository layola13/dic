co-occurred:/ˌkoʊ əˈkɜːrd/
|v.|共同发生，同时出现|
|The two events co-occurred, leading scientists to believe there might be a connection.|这两个事件同时发生，使科学家们相信它们之间可能存在联系。|
|Symptoms of anxiety and depression often co-occurred in patients.|焦虑和抑郁的症状经常在病人身上同时出现。|
|近义词：coincided, happened together|
|反义词：occurred separately, occurred independently|
|词性：动词|
|词源：co- (together) + occur (happen)|
