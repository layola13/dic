moralizations:/ˌmɔːrəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|道德化; 说教|The act or process of moralizing, or the result of it; a comment or lesson that is intended to teach someone about right and wrong behavior. |His constant moralizations about drinking were beginning to annoy everyone. 他不断地对喝酒说教，开始惹恼所有人。|
|近义词:sermons, lectures, preachments, homilies, admonitions|
|反义词: amorality, immorality|
|词性: Noun (Plural)|
