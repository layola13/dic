nuptial:/ˈnʌpʃəl/ | adj. n. |  婚姻的；婚礼的；婚礼，结婚典礼 | Relating to marriage or a wedding. |  例句：They exchanged nuptial vows. 他们交换了结婚誓言。

例句：The nuptial celebrations lasted for three days. 婚礼庆典持续了三天。

近义词：marital, matrimonial, wedding | 反义词：divorce, separation | 词性：adjective, noun

nuptials:/ˈnʌpʃəlz/ | n. | 婚礼，结婚典礼 | A wedding or marriage ceremony. | 例句：Their nuptials were held in a small chapel. 他们的婚礼在一个小教堂举行。

例句：We were invited to their nuptials next month. 我们被邀请参加他们下个月的婚礼。


近义词：wedding, marriage ceremony | 反义词：divorce, separation | 词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "nuptialis,"  from "nuptus," past participle of "nubere" ("to marry," literally "to veil," referring to the Roman custom of the bride wearing a veil).
