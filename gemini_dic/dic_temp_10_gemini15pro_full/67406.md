externalities: /ˌɛkstərˈnælɪtiz/ | n. | 外部性，外部效应 |  A side effect or consequence of an industrial or commercial activity that affects other parties without this being reflected in the cost of the goods or services involved. | Pollution from a factory is a negative externality. 工厂的污染是一个负外部性。 |  side effects, spillover effects, repercussions | benefits, internalization | noun
