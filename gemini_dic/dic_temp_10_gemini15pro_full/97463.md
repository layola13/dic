ingate:/ˈɪnɡeɪt/
noun
入口，进口，浇口
An entrance or gate, especially one providing access to a mold for molten metal.  The point at which something enters a system or process.
Molten metal is poured into the mold through the ingate.  熔融金属通过浇口倒入模具。
The data is processed at the ingate of the system. 数据在系统的入口处进行处理。
Synonyms: entrance, entry, inlet, access point
Antonyms: exit, outlet, outgate
