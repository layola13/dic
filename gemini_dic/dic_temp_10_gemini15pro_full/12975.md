asthmatically:/æsˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 喘息地，气喘吁吁地 | In a way that suggests someone is experiencing asthma; with difficulty breathing. |  He coughed asthmatically after running up the stairs.  他跑上楼梯后气喘吁吁地咳嗽。| Synonyms: breathlessly, wheezingly, gaspingly | Antonyms: easily, smoothly |词性: 副词
