awed:/ɔːd/| adj.|敬畏的，充满敬畏的|Filled with awe; inspired by a feeling of reverence, fear, and wonder.|例句：She was awed by the vastness of the Grand Canyon. 她被大峡谷的广阔所震撼。|近义词： amazed, astonished, wonderstruck, impressed|反义词： unimpressed, indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *aguðr "filled with fear, respect," from agi "fear, terror" + -uðr, adjectival suffix.  Related to Old English ege "fear, terror." The meaning shifted from "filled with fear" to "filled with reverential fear and wonder" in Middle English.
