outreigning:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnɪŋ/
verb
超越统治；比…统治时间更长
ruling longer than; exceeding in the duration of a reign
King Henry VIII outreigned all the other Tudor monarchs.  亨利八世的统治时间超过了所有其他都铎王朝的君主。
The current president has outreigned his predecessor by two years. 现任总统的统治时间比他的前任长了两年。
Synonyms: outlast, survive, exceed
Antonyms:  precede, predate
