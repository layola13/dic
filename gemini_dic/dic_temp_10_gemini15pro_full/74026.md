forwardnesses:/ˈfɔːrwərdnəsɪz/
n.
大胆;鲁莽;冒失;唐突
The quality or state of being forward; boldness or presumptuousness.
Her forwardness in addressing the CEO directly surprised everyone. 她直接和CEO说话的唐突让每个人都感到惊讶。
He apologized for his forwardness in assuming she would want to dance. 他为自己冒昧地认为她想跳舞而道歉。

近义词: boldness, audacity, presumptuousness, impertinence, impudence
反义词: shyness, timidity, reserve, diffidence, modesty
词性: 名词(plural)
