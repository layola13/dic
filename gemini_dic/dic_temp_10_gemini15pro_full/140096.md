off-key:/ˌɒfˈkiː/ (UK) /ˌɔːfˈkiː/ (US)| adj.|走调的，不和谐的，不合适的|Not in tune; not in harmony, either literally (of music) or figuratively (of a situation or feeling).|Her singing was slightly off-key, making the performance less enjoyable. 她唱歌有点走调，使得演出效果打了折扣。

The comedian's jokes were off-key and failed to connect with the audience.  这个喜剧演员的笑话不合时宜，没能引起观众的共鸣。|近义词：out of tune, discordant, inappropriate, unsuitable|反义词：in tune, harmonious, appropriate, suitable|词性:形容词
