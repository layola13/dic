butterball:/ˈbʌtərˌbɔːl/| n.|黄油球，胖墩，小肥仔|A plump, rounded person or animal, often referring to a young bird, especially a turkey, fattened for eating.  It can also refer to something shaped like a ball of butter.|例句：The farmer raised a flock of butterballs for Thanksgiving. 农民养了一群小肥仔过感恩节。

例句：The baby's chubby cheeks made him look like a little butterball. 宝宝胖乎乎的脸颊让他看起来像个小胖墩。|近义词：chubby, plump, rotund, portly (for people), fattened, well-fed (for animals)|反义词：thin, skinny, lean, scrawny|词性:名词
