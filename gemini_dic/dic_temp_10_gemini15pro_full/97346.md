infotainment:/ˌɪnfoʊˈteɪnmənt/ | n. | 资讯娱乐；信息娱乐 |  A blend of information and entertainment.  It refers to content designed to be both informative and entertaining, often found in media like television, radio, and the internet. |  The car's infotainment system includes GPS, a touch screen, and a DVD player. 汽车的信息娱乐系统包括GPS、触摸屏和DVD播放器。 | Synonyms: edutainment | Antonyms: N/A | noun
