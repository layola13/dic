gunships:/ˈɡʌnʃɪps/ | n. | 武装直升机；炮艇 | A heavily armed aircraft, helicopter or ship, equipped with guns and other weapons, designed to attack ground targets. | The gunships circled overhead, providing cover for the ground troops. 武装直升机在头顶盘旋，为地面部队提供掩护。| The navy deployed gunships to patrol the coastal waters. 海军部署了炮艇来巡逻沿海水域。 | Synonyms: attack helicopter, warship, combat aircraft | Antonyms: unarmed aircraft, passenger ship, transport helicopter | noun
