gospelize:/ɡɒspəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːspəlaɪz/ (US)| v. |传福音；使基督教化|To preach the gospel to; to convert to Christianity. |Missionaries worked to gospelize the indigenous population.  传教士致力于向土著居民传福音。

The king hoped to gospelize the entire kingdom. 国王希望将整个王国基督教化。|Synonyms: evangelize, Christianize, proselytize|Antonyms: secularize, dechristianize|Verb
