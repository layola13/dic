allophonically:/ˌæləˈfɒnɪkli/| adv.| 音位变体地，同位异音地 |In terms of pronunciation variants, where sounds are considered the same phoneme but pronounced slightly differently depending on the surrounding sounds. For example, the 't' in 'top' and 'stop' are allophones.|The word "butter" is often pronounced allophonically as "budder" in casual speech.  “butter”这个词在日常口语中经常被音位变体地发成“budder”。|Near synonyms: phonetically, pronunciationally|Antonyms: N/A|Word type: Adverb
