overillustrate:/ˌoʊvərˈɪləstreɪt/
verb
过度图解；插图过多
To illustrate excessively; to use too many illustrations.
The textbook was overillustrated, making it distracting for the students. 这本教科书插图过多，让学生分心。
The author overillustrated his point with far too many examples. 作者用了太多的例子，过度阐述了他的观点。
Synonyms: overdecorate, embellish excessively
Antonyms: underillustrate
词性: 动词
