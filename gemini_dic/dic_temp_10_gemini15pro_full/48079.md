declarer:/dɪˈkleərər/| n.|声明者；宣告者；（尤指桥牌游戏中）定约人|A declarer is a person who makes a statement, especially the player in a card game (especially bridge) who wins the bidding and plays both their own hand and their partner's.|The declarer played a risky hand.|近义词：announcer, proclaimer, bidder (in bridge)|反义词:None|Noun
