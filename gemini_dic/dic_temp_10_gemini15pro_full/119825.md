miladis:/mɪˈleɪdiz/| n.| 女士们；小姐们 (古用法或戏谑用法)|A term of address for women, equivalent to "ladies."  It's archaic or humorous in modern usage. |例句：Miladis, your carriage awaits. 女士们，你们的马车在等着。

例句：He bowed low and addressed the group as "miladis." 他深深地鞠了一躬，称呼这群人为“女士们”。|近义词：ladies, women, mesdames|反义词: gentlemen, men|词性:名词
