choppiest:/ˈtʃɒpiɪst/| adj.|最波涛汹涌的，最不稳定的|Characterized by short, irregular waves, typically caused by strong winds or conflicting currents.|The choppiest waters I've ever sailed in were off the coast of Cape Horn. 我航行过的最波涛汹涌的水域是在合恩角海岸附近。|近义词:rough, turbulent, agitated|反义词:calm, smooth, tranquil|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From chop (v.) + -y + -est.  "Chop" referring to short, irregular waves dates back to the 16th century, likely of imitative origin.
