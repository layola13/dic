bryology:/braɪˈɒlədʒi/
n.|苔藓学|The study of mosses, liverworts, and hornworts.|Bryology is a fascinating field of botany. 苔藓学是植物学中一个引人入胜的领域。|
近义词:  muscology (specifically the study of mosses)|
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps something like "phanerogamic botany" which is the study of seed plants)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βρύον (brúon, “moss”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”)
