olidous: /oʊˈlɪdɪəs/
adjective
有气味的；散发气味的
emitting a smell, especially a strong or unpleasant one.
例句：The olidous fumes from the factory choked the nearby residents. 工厂散发出的刺鼻烟雾呛到了附近的居民。

例句：The olidous swamp was a breeding ground for mosquitoes.  散发着臭味的沼泽是蚊子的滋生地。

近义词：smelly, stinky, foul, noxious, fetid
反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling, odorless, inodorous
词性：形容词

词源：From Latin *olidus, from olēre (“to smell”).
