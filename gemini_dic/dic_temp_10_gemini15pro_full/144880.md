over-restrictions:/ˌoʊvər-rɪˈstrɪkʃənz/| n.|过度限制，过分约束|Excessive limitations or constraints imposed on someone or something. |The over-restrictions on free speech stifled creativity. 过度限制言论自由扼杀了创造力。|The over-restrictions on trade hindered economic growth.  贸易的过度限制阻碍了经济增长。|近义词：limitations, constraints, regulations|反义词:freedom, liberty, deregulation|词性:名词

Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "restriction" (from Latin restrictus, past participle of restringere "to bind fast," from re- "back" + stringere "to draw tight").
