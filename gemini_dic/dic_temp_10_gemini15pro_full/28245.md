captain:/ˈkæptɪn/ (US), /ˈkæptɪn/ (UK)| n. vt.|队长，船长，机长，上尉|A person who is in charge of a team, ship, aircraft, or company of soldiers. |The captain steered the ship through the storm. 船长驾驶着船只穿过风暴。|近义词：leader, chief, commander, skipper (for ships)|反义词：crew, subordinate|noun
captain:/ˈkæptɪn/ (US), /ˈkæptɪn/ (UK)| vt.|担任…的队长，率领|To act as the captain of a team or group. |She captained the team to victory. 她率领团队取得了胜利。|近义词：lead, command|反义词：follow|verb
