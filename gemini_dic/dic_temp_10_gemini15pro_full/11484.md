arhythmous:/əˈrɪðməs/
adjective
无节奏的，不规律的
Lacking rhythm or regularity.
His arhythmous heartbeat concerned the doctor. 他的无规律心跳让医生很担心。
His breathing was shallow and arhythmous. 他的呼吸很浅而且不规律。
近义词: irregular, erratic, unsteady
反义词: rhythmic, regular, steady
词性: adjective
