cooperative:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪv/ /kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| adj. n.|合作的；合作社|Involving mutual assistance in working toward a common goal.  A farm, business, or other organization that is owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.|例句：The project requires cooperative effort from all team members.  这个项目需要所有团队成员的共同努力。|近义词：collaborative, joint, shared|反义词：uncooperative, competitive|词性:形容词

cooperative:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪv/ /kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| n.|合作社|A farm, business, or other organization that is owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits.|例句：She joined a housing cooperative. 她加入了一个住宅合作社。|近义词：collective, commune|反义词：corporation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin cooperativus, from cooperari "to work together," from co- "together" + operari "to work."
