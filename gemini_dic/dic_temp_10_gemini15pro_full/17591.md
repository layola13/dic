beautified:/ˈbjuːtɪfaɪd/
Verb|美化了的，变美的|Made beautiful; enhanced in appearance.|The artist beautified the old building with a mural.  艺术家用壁画美化了那栋老建筑。|近义词：enhanced, adorned, embellished, decorated, improved|反义词：uglified, spoiled, marred, defaced|v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From beautify + -ed.  Beautify comes from the French "beautifier," from "beau" (beautiful) + "-fier" (to make).  Ultimately derived from Latin "bellus" (beautiful).
