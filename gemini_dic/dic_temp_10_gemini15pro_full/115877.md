marlins:/ˈmɑːrlɪnz/| n.|马林鱼；旗鱼|A large, fast-swimming fish found in warm seas, some species of which are caught for sport or food.  Specifically often refers to members of the family Istiophoridae (billfishes).|例句：The marlins are known for their speed and agility. 马林鱼以速度和敏捷著称。

例句：The fisherman landed a huge marlin after a long fight.  渔夫经过长时间的搏斗，钓到了一条巨大的马林鱼。|近义词：billfish, sailfish, swordfish|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish)|词性:noun
