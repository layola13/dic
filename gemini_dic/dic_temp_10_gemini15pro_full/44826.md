crushingly:/ˈkrʌʃɪŋli/
adverb|压倒性地；毁灭性地；沉重地|Overwhelmingly; destructively; in a way that crushes or overwhelms.|The news hit her crushingly.  这个消息沉重地打击了她。|The defeat was crushingly disappointing. 这场失败令人极度失望。|近义词:overwhelmingly, devastatingly, oppressively|反义词:mildly, gently, slightly|词性:副词
词源: From the verb "crush" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Crush" originates from Old French "croisir," meaning "to break, crack."
