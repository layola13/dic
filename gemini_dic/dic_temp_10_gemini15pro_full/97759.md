initiative:/ɪˈnɪʃətɪv/ /ɪˈnɪʃiətɪv/| n. |主动性，积极性；倡议；主动权|The ability to assess and initiate things independently.  The power or opportunity to act or take charge before others do.|She took the initiative in organizing the project. 她主动组织了这个项目。|synonyms: enterprise, drive, ambition, resourcefulness, leadership|antonyms: apathy, passivity, indifference|noun

initiative:/ɪˈnɪʃətɪv/ /ɪˈnɪʃiətɪv/| adj. |主动的，创始的|Introducing or starting something.|The initiative phase of the project is complete. 项目的初始阶段已经完成。|synonyms: introductory, beginning, original, pioneering|antonyms: concluding, final, last|adjective
