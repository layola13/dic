deadlihood:/ˈdedliˌhʊd/ (US) /ˈdɛdlihʊd/ (UK)| n.|生计，生活来源|A person's livelihood is the way they earn money in order to live.  It refers to the means of securing the necessities of life.|Many people lost their livelihoods after the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，许多人失去了生计。|近义词：living, subsistence, income, means of support|反义词: unemployment, poverty|noun
