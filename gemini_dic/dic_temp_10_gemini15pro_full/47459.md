dealable:/ˈdiːləbəl/| adj.|可商量的，可交易的，可处理的|Something that is dealable is open to negotiation or compromise, or can be handled or managed effectively.|The price is dealable, so let's talk. 价格可以商量，我们谈谈吧。|Synonyms: negotiable, manageable, tractable|Antonyms: intractable, non-negotiable, unmanageable|adjective
