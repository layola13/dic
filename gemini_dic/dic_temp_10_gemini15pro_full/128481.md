newspapered:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərd/
verb
用报纸覆盖；用报纸包
covered or wrapped with newspapers
The floor was newspapered to protect it during the painting.  地板上铺了报纸，以便在油漆时保护它。
近义词: covered, wrapped
反义词: uncovered, unwrapped
词性:v.


Etymology:  Derived from "newspaper" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).  "Newspaper" itself comes from "news" + "paper."
