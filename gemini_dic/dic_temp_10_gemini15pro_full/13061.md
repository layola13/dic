astrohatch:/ˈæstroʊhætʃ/| n.|太空舱口；航天舱口|An opening, like a hatch, in a spacecraft, used for entering or exiting.|The astronaut opened the astrohatch and stepped out onto the lunar surface. 宇航员打开了太空舱口，踏上了月球表面。|近义词：hatch, airlock, portal|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源: "astro-" (from Greek "astron," meaning "star") + "hatch" (from Old English "hæcc," meaning "gate").  The word is a combination of "astro," relating to space or stars, and "hatch," referring to a door or opening, specifically on a ship or aircraft.
