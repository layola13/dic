osteological:/ˌɒstiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌɑːstiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
骨骼学的；骨学上的
Relating to the study of bones.
Osteological research has shed light on the evolutionary history of hominids.  骨骼学研究阐明了人类的进化史。
The museum's osteological collection is extensive, containing specimens from various time periods and geographical locations.  博物馆的骨骼收藏非常丰富，包含来自不同时期和地理位置的标本。
Near synonyms: skeletal, bony
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms related to soft tissue like "muscular" or "visceral")
词性: adjective
