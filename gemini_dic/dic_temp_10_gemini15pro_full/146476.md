overfunded: /ˌoʊvərˈfʌndɪd/
adjective
资金过剩的，过度拨款的
Having received more funding than is needed or can be effectively used.
The overfunded program was criticized for wasting taxpayer money.  这个资金过剩的项目因浪费纳税人的钱而受到批评。
The charity was overfunded and had to decide how to allocate the surplus.  这个慈善机构资金过剩，不得不决定如何分配剩余资金。
近义词：overcapitalized, excessively funded
反义词：underfunded, poorly funded
词性：adjective
词源：over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + funded (past participle of "fund")
