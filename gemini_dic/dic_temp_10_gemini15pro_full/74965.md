fricace 不是一个标准的英语单词。你可能是想问 **efficace** (法语) 或 **efficacious** (英语).  我将分别解释这两个词。

**1. Efficace (法语)**

/efiˈkas/ | adj. | 有效的，灵验的 | Effective, producing the desired result. |  Ce médicament est très efficace contre la douleur.  这药止痛很有效。 |  Synonymes: efficient, performant | Antonymes: inefficace, inutile | adjective

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Ce remède est efficace. = This remedy is effective.
Cette méthode est efficace pour apprendre le français. = This method is effective for learning French.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
N/A (French doesn't have a present continuous tense equivalent to English.)

复合过去时 (Passé Composé - functionally similar to Present Perfect):
Ce traitement a été efficace. = This treatment has been effective.
Le médicament s'est avéré efficace. = The medicine has proven effective.

愈过去时 (Plus-que-Parfait - functionally similar to Past Perfect):
Le médicament avait été efficace auparavant. = The medicine had been effective before.
La stratégie avait été efficace jusqu'à maintenant. = The strategy had been effective until now.

简单将来时 (Futur Simple):
Ce médicament sera efficace. = This medicine will be effective.
La nouvelle loi sera efficace dès le mois prochain. = The new law will be effective as of next month.


(Other French tenses are less directly comparable to English and less relevant for a single adjective like "efficace".)


PHRASE-> (French)
Très efficace = Very effective
Remède efficace = Effective remedy
Traitement efficace = Effective treatment
Méthode efficace = Effective method
Stratégie efficace = Effective strategy



**2. Efficacious (英语)**

/ˌefɪˈkeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 有效的，灵验的 |  Producing the desired result; effective. | The vaccine has proven efficacious in preventing the disease. 疫苗已被证明可有效预防这种疾病。| Synonyms: effective, potent, powerful | Antonyms: ineffective, useless, futile | adjective


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
This medicine is efficacious.  这种药很有效。
The new policy is efficacious in reducing crime. 新政策在减少犯罪方面很有效。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The treatment is proving efficacious. 这种疗法正在被证明是有效的。
The new strategy is being efficacious in attracting customers. 新策略在吸引顾客方面很有效。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The drug has been efficacious in treating the illness. 这种药物在治疗这种疾病方面一直很有效。
Her efforts have proven efficacious. 她的努力已被证明是有效的。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Researchers have been studying how efficacious the drug is. 研究人员一直在研究这种药物的疗效如何。
The team has been working on an efficacious solution. 团队一直在致力于寻找有效的解决方案。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The medicine was efficacious. 这种药很有效。
The strategy was efficacious in achieving our goals. 该战略在实现我们的目标方面很有效。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The treatment was proving efficacious when the patient suddenly recovered. 当病人突然康复时，治疗正被证明是有效的。
We were developing an efficacious solution when the problem resolved itself. 我们正在开发一个有效的解决方案，问题就自行解决了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The medicine had been efficacious in previous trials. 这种药物在之前的试验中一直很有效。
The strategy had proven efficacious before the market crashed. 在市场崩溃之前，该战略已被证明是有效的。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Scientists had been researching efficacious treatments for years before the breakthrough. 在取得突破之前，科学家们多年来一直在研究有效的治疗方法。
The company had been developing an efficacious product for months before it launched. 在发布之前，该公司已经开发了几个月的有效产品。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The new drug will be efficacious in treating the disease. 这种新药将能有效治疗这种疾病。
The plan will be efficacious in achieving our objectives. 该计划将能有效地实现我们的目标。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Researchers will be testing how efficacious the new vaccine is next year. 研究人员明年将测试新疫苗的有效性。
The team will be implementing an efficacious strategy next quarter. 该团队将在下一季度实施有效的策略。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

By the end of the decade, scientists will have developed an efficacious cure. 到本世纪末，科学家将开发出一种有效的治疗方法。
