cryonically:/kraɪˈɒnɪkli/
adverb|低温地；冷冻地|in a way that involves or relates to cryonics, the practice of preserving a person's body at very low temperatures after death, in the hope that they can be revived in the future|例句：His body was cryonically frozen. 他的遗体被低温冷冻了。|近义词: cryogenically|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "naturally," "conventionally," or at "room temperature")
词性: adverb
