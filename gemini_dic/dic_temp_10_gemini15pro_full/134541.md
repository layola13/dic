nonlinkage:/ˌnɒnˈlɪŋkɪdʒ/ |n.| 非连锁 |The absence of linkage between genes; the situation where two or more genes are located on different chromosomes or are far apart on the same chromosome and thus assort independently during meiosis.|  Scientists discovered nonlinkage between the two traits. 科学家发现这两个性状不连锁。|Synonyms: independent assortment| Antonyms: linkage| noun
