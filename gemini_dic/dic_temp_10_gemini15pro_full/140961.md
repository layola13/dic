one-upmanship: /ˌwʌnˈʌpmənʃɪp/
n.
 one-upmanship |力压别人一头的行为；胜人一筹 |The constant one-upmanship between the two brothers was tiresome.  两兄弟之间持续的争强好胜让人厌烦。|
Synonyms: rivalry, competition, one-upmanship, outdoing, outmaneuvering
Antonyms: cooperation, collaboration, concession
noun
