brightener:/ˈbraɪtnər/| n.|增白剂，光亮剂，漂白剂|Something that brightens; specifically : a chemical agent used to whiten fabrics, paper, or plastics. |例句：This laundry detergent contains a brightener to make your whites whiter.  这种洗衣液含有一种增白剂，可以让你的白色衣物更白。

例句：She used a hair brightener to give her dull locks some shine. 她使用了一种头发光亮剂，让暗淡的头发焕发光彩。|近义词：whitener, bleach, enhancer|反义词：darkener, duller|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bright + -en + -er.  Bright (meaning shining) comes from Old English *beorht, from Proto-Germanic *berhtaz.  The suffix -en forms verbs meaning "to make or become," while -er forms nouns denoting an agent or instrument.
