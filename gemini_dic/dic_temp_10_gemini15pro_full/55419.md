distillatory:/dɪˈstɪlətɔːri/ | adj. n.| 蒸馏的；蒸馏室；酿酒厂 | relating to, or used for distillation; a place where distilling is done. | The distillatory process separates the alcohol from the fermented mixture.  蒸馏过程将酒精从发酵混合物中分离出来。 | The old distillatory was converted into a museum.  旧酿酒厂被改建成了博物馆。 | 近义词: distillery, still | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun

distillery:/dɪˈstɪləri/ | n.| 蒸馏室；酿酒厂 | a place where alcoholic drinks are made. |  We visited a whisky distillery in Scotland. 我们参观了苏格兰的一家威士忌酒厂。 | The distillery produces a range of fine spirits. 这家酿酒厂生产各种优质烈酒。 | 近义词: still, distillatory | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


词源:
From Late Latin *distillatorius, from Latin distillare "to drip down," from dis- "down" + stillare "to drip," from stilla "a drop."
