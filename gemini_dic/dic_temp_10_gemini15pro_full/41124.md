coprophagous:/ˌkɒprəˈfeɪɡəs/| adj.|食粪的，嗜粪的|Feeding on dung or excrement.  This adaptation allows some animals to extract nutrients that were not fully digested during the initial passage through the digestive system.|Rabbits are coprophagous, reingesting their soft, nutrient-rich feces.|近义词:scatophagic|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like *herbivorous* or *carnivorous* depending on context)
詞性:adjective
