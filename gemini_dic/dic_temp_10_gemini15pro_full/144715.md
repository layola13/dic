ovated:/ˈoʊveɪtɪd/| adj.|卵形的，椭圆形的|Having an oval shape, like an egg.  Resembling an ovum. |The architect designed an ovated window for the church.  建筑师为教堂设计了一个椭圆形的窗户。|Near synonyms: oval, elliptical, egg-shaped|Antonyms:  round, square, rectangular|adjective

ovation:/oʊˈveɪʃən/| n.|热烈欢迎，欢呼，鼓掌|An enthusiastic public reception, especially by means of applause. |The opera singer received a standing ovation. 歌剧演唱家受到了观众的起立鼓掌。|Synonyms: applause, cheers, acclamation, plaudits|Antonyms:  booing, hissing, disapproval|noun
