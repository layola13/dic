documentary:/ˌdɑːkjəˈmentəri/ /ˌdɒkjəˈmentəri/| n. adj.|纪录片，纪实性的|A film or television or radio programme that provides a factual record or report.|例句：I watched a fascinating documentary about the Amazon rainforest. 我看了一部关于亚马逊雨林的引人入胜的纪录片。|近义词：nonfiction, factual, report|反义词：fictional, fabricated|词性：名词, 形容词

documentary:/ˌdɑːkjəˈmentəri/ /ˌdɒkjəˈmentəri/| adj.|纪实的，文献的|Consisting of documents.|例句：The museum has a large collection of documentary evidence about the town's history.  博物馆收藏了大量关于该镇历史的文献资料。|近义词：evidentiary, recorded, archival|反义词：fictitious, unsubstantiated|词性：形容词

词源：From the French word "documentaire,"  ultimately from Latin "documentum" meaning "lesson" or "proof."
