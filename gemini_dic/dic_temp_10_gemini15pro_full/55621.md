diswont:/dɪsˈwʌnt/ | v. |  戒除(习惯); 使不习惯 | To cause to lose a habit or custom; to break of a habit. |  The soldier was diswonted from rising early after leaving the army.  这名士兵退伍后改掉了早起的习惯。 | 近义词: break, wean, discontinue | 反义词: accustom, habituate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From dis- + wont (n.) "habitual practice, custom," from Old English gewuna "custom, habit, use, wont," from Proto-Germanic *gewunjo (source also of Old Frisian wuna, Old Saxon giwono "custom," Old High German giwona "custom, manner," German Gewohnheit "habit, custom"), from PIE root *gwē- (1) "to live, be alive, vital force"
