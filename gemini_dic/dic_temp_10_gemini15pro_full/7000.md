androgynous:/ænˈdrɒdʒɪnəs/ | adj. |雌雄同体的，兼具两性的 | Having both male and female characteristics or qualities.  |  例句：The androgynous model was chosen for the unisex clothing line.  这个雌雄同体的模特被选中为中性服装系列代言。

例句：Their fashion sense is quite androgynous, blending both masculine and feminine elements. 他们的时尚感非常中性，融合了男性化和女性化的元素。

近义词：unisex, gender-neutral, epicene | 反义词：masculine, feminine | 词性: adjective


androgynal: /ænˈdrɒdʒɪnl/ | adj. | 雌雄同体的，兼具两性的 | Relating to or exhibiting androgyny. | 例句：The singer cultivated an androgynal image. 这位歌手打造了一种雌雄同体的形象。

例句：The androgynal character in the play challenged traditional gender roles.  戏剧中雌雄同体的角色挑战了传统的性别角色。


近义词：androgynous, unisex, gender-neutral | 反义词：masculine, feminine |词性: adjective


词源: From Greek *ἀνδρόγυνος (andrógunos)  'man-woman', from ἀνήρ (anḗr) 'man' + γυνή (gunḗ) 'woman'.
