aleberry:/ˈeɪlbɛri/ | n. |麦芽酒浆|A beverage made from ale boiled with spices, sweetened with sugar, and thickened with bread or oatmeal.|In olden times, aleberry was a common breakfast drink. 在过去，麦芽酒浆是一种常见的早餐饮料。|Synonyms: posset, caudle|Antonyms: None|noun

aleberry:/ˈeɪlbɛri/ | n. |麦芽啤酒浆|A sweetened and spiced ale, often thickened with breadcrumbs or oatmeal. |The tavern offered warm aleberry on cold nights. 这家酒馆在寒冷的夜晚提供热麦芽啤酒浆。|Synonyms:  wassail, hot toddy (loosely)|Antonyms: None|noun
