mythopoeic:/ˌmɪθəˈpɔɪɪk/ | adj. |神话创作的；神话般的；造神的| Relating to the creation of myths; having the power to create myths; mythical. |  The Lord of the Rings is a mythopoeic work, creating a whole new world of mythology.  《指环王》是一部神话般的作品，创造了一个全新的神话世界。

|  The writer's mythopoeic imagination allowed him to craft compelling narratives.  这位作家的神话创作想象力使他能够创作出引人入胜的叙事。| 近义词:mythmaking, legendary, mythical | 反义词:realistic, factual | 词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *muthos* (myth) + *poiein* (to make)
