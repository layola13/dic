forewarn:/fɔːrˈwɔːrn/| vt.|预先警告，事先告诫|to warn someone of a danger or problem that is likely to happen in the future|The weather forecast forewarned us of the approaching hurricane. 天气预报预先警告我们飓风即将来临。

The guide forewarned them about the dangers of the jungle. 导游事先告诫他们丛林的危险。|近义词：caution, alert, prewarn|反义词: surprise, ambush|verb
