disparate:/ˈdɪspərət/| adj.|不同的，异类的，迥然不同的|Fundamentally different; entirely unlike.|例句：The two groups held disparate views on the issue.  这两个团体对这个问题持有截然不同的观点。|近义词：different, distinct, diverse, various, heterogeneous|反义词：similar, alike, homogeneous, uniform|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disparātus*, past participle of *disparāre* (“to separate”), from *dis-* (“apart”) + *parāre* (“to prepare”).
