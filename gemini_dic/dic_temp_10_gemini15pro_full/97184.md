infixed:/ɪnˈfɪkst/| adj.|插入的，嵌入的|An infixed word or affix is one that is inserted within another word or morpheme.|The word "bloody" is sometimes used as an infix in British English, as in "abso-bloody-lutely".  这个单词“bloody”有时在英式英语中用作插入语，例如在“abso-bloody-lutely”中。|近义词：inserted, embedded, intercalated|反义词：removed, extracted|词性:形容词
