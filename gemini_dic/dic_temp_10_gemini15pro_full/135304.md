nonpatentability:/ˌnɒnˌpeɪtəntəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnˌpeɪtəntəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可专利性
The state or quality of not being patentable; inability to be patented.
The nonpatentability of the discovery posed a problem for the researchers.  这项发现的不可专利性给研究人员带来了问题。
The lawyer argued the nonpatentability of the software algorithm.  律师辩称该软件算法不可申请专利。
近义词:unpatentability
反义词:patentability
词性:noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
patent (noun/verb): from Latin *patent- "lying open," past participle of patēre "lie open, be open, be evident, be exposed"
-able (suffix):  from Latin *-abilis, suffix forming adjectives meaning "able to be," "capable of being"
-ity (suffix): from Latin *-itās, suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives
