eyrie:/ˈɪəri/ /ˈɛəri/ | n. | 鹰巢；高处住所；高处|A large nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle, typically built high in a tree or on a cliff.  A high or inaccessible place. |The eagle soared above its eyrie on the cliff face.  这只鹰在悬崖峭壁上的巢穴上方翱翔。|Synonyms: nest, aerie, perch, roost, retreat, sanctuary|Antonyms: ground, base, bottom|noun
