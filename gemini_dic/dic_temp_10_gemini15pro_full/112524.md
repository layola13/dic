lovablenesses:/ˈlʌvəblənəsɪz/
n.
可爱之处；令人喜爱之处
The quality of being lovable; the state or condition of being lovable.
Her lovablenesses are numerous, including her kindness and sense of humor. 她的可爱之处有很多，包括她的善良和幽默感。
synonyms: charm, attractiveness, appeal, sweetness
antonyms: unpleasantness, unattractiveness, repulsiveness
词性: 名词
