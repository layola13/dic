interlaying:/ˌɪntərˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
交错放置，插入，隔层放置
The process of placing something between layers of something else.
Workers were interlaying sheets of paper between the glass plates to prevent scratching. 工人们在玻璃板之间交错放置纸张以防止刮伤。

interlaying:/ˌɪntərˈleɪɪŋ/
noun
交错放置，插入，隔层放置（的行为）
The act of placing something between layers of something else.
The interlaying of absorbent material helped protect the fragile artifacts.  插入吸收性材料有助于保护易碎的文物。


近义词:inserting, interposing, layering, sandwiching
反义词:removing, extracting

词性: verb, noun
