atropism:/ˈætrəˌpɪzəm/| n. |向性，趋向性|The tendency of an organism or part of an organism to turn away from or toward a stimulus, such as light, gravity, or chemicals.|Example 1: The plant exhibited negative phototropism, bending away from the light source. 植物表现出负向光性，远离光源弯曲。

Example 2:  The roots of the plant displayed positive gravitropism, growing downwards towards the earth. 植物的根表现出正向重力性，向下朝着地球生长。|Synonym: tropism|Antonym: N/A|noun
