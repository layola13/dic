hurrahs:/hʊˈrɑːz/ /həˈrɑːz/
|n.|欢呼声，喝彩声
|A shout of joy or approval.
|The crowd erupted in hurrahs as the team scored the winning goal. |人群爆发出欢呼声，因为球队打进了制胜一球。
|近义词：cheers, applause, acclamations
|反义词：boos, jeers
|词性：名词

hurrah:/hʊˈrɑː/ /həˈrɑː/
|int.|好哇！万岁！
|Used to express excitement, joy, or approval.
|Hurrah! We won!|好哇！我们赢了！
|近义词：hooray, yay
|反义词：alas, boo
|词性：感叹词

hurrah:/hʊˈrɑː/ /həˈrɑː/
|vi.|欢呼，喝彩
|To shout in excitement, joy, or approval.
|The fans hurrahed as the band took the stage.|当乐队上台时，歌迷们欢呼雀跃。
|近义词：cheer, applaud
|反义词：boo, jeer
|词性：动词


词源:
From the exclamation "huzzah," of uncertain origin, perhaps from a sailor's cry. First recorded in the mid-18th century.
