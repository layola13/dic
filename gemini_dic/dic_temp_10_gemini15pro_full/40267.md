continual:/kənˈtɪnjuəl/| adj.|持续的，频繁的|Recurring regularly and frequently, but with interruptions.|The continual noise from the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地的持续噪音使人难以集中注意力。|近义词：frequent, repeated, recurrent, incessant|反义词:occasional, sporadic, intermittent|词性:形容词
continual:/kənˈtɪnjuəl/| adj.|不停的，不间断的 (Technically incorrect, but often used this way)|Continuing without interruption (though this meaning is often confused with continuous).|She faced continual interruptions throughout the meeting.  在整个会议期间，她不断地被打断。（尽管严格来说这里应该用continuous）|近义词：constant, continuous, persistent|反义词:intermittent, occasional|词性:形容词
