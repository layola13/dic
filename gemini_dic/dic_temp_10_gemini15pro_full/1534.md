achatina:/ˌækəˈtiːnə/
n.
非洲大蜗牛
A large air-breathing land snail, especially of the genus Achatina, native to Africa and now a serious pest in many tropical areas.
Achatina fulica is considered one of the most invasive species. 非洲大蜗牛被认为是最具入侵性的物种之一。

The achatina can grow up to 8 inches long. 非洲大蜗牛可以长到8英寸长。

近义词:giant African land snail,
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially small snail or native snail depending on context)
词性:noun
