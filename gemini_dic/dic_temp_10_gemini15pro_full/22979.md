botulin:/ˈbɒtʃʊlɪn/ | n. | 肉毒杆菌毒素 | A neurotoxic protein produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum. | Botulin is used in small doses to treat muscle spasms. 肉毒杆菌毒素少量用于治疗肌肉痉挛。 | Synonyms: botulinum toxin, Botox | Antonyms: None | noun

botulins:/ˈbɒtʃʊlɪnz/ | n. | 肉毒杆菌毒素（复数） |  Plural form of botulin, referring to different types or doses of the neurotoxin.  | Different botulins are used for various cosmetic and medical procedures. 不同的肉毒杆菌毒素用于各种美容和医疗程序。 | Synonyms: botulinum toxins (plural), Botox (often used generically) | Antonyms: None | noun
