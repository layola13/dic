element:/ˈelɪmənt/ | n. | 元素；要素；成分；自然环境 |A part or component of a larger whole; a basic principle; a natural environment. |例句：Hydrogen is an element. 氢是一种元素。|近义词：component, factor, ingredient, constituent, part |反义词: whole, entirety| noun


element:/ˈelɪmənt/ | n. |  (plural elements)  [usually plural] the basic principles of a subject of study |The rudimentary principles of a subject. |例句：He has mastered the elements of grammar. 他已经掌握了语法基础。|近义词：basics, fundamentals, rudiments |反义词: advanced concepts| noun


element:/ˈelɪmənt/ | n. | group of people |A group of people, especially one perceived as distinctive within a larger group. |例句：The criminal element in the city is growing. 城市中的犯罪分子越来越多。|近义词：group, faction, sector, segment |反义词: none| noun
