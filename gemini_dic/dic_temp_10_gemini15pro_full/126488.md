mytilotoxine:/ˌmɪtɪloʊˈtɒksɪn/ | n. | 贝类毒素 | A type of neurotoxin found in shellfish, particularly mussels, that can cause paralytic shellfish poisoning (PSP). |  Exposure to high levels of mytilotoxine can be fatal.  接触高剂量的贝类毒素可能是致命的。|Synonyms:  shellfish toxin, neurotoxin, saxitoxin | Antonyms:  antidote, remedy | noun
