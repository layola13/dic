entailments:/ɪnˈteɪlmənts/| n.|必然结果；必要的推论；牵连事项；|Something that is entailed is a necessary part or consequence of it.  Entailments are the logical consequences or necessary conditions that follow from a statement or action.|Building a new airport entails demolishing a lot of houses and businesses. 建设一个新机场需要拆除许多房屋和企业。|synonyms: consequences, implications, ramifications, results|antonyms: causes, origins, sources|noun
