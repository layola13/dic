encourage:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/ | v. |鼓励，激励|To inspire with courage, spirit, or hope.|例句：The teacher encouraged the students to ask questions. 老师鼓励学生提问。|近义词：inspire, motivate, support, stimulate|反义词:discourage, dissuade|verb

encourager:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒər/ | n. |鼓励者，激励者|A person who inspires with courage, spirit, or hope.|例句：She is a great encourager of young artists. 她是一位极力鼓励年轻艺术家的优秀人士。|近义词：motivator, supporter, inspirer|反义词:deterrent, discourager|noun

encouraging:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. |鼓舞人心的，令人鼓舞的|Giving courage, spirit, or hope.|例句：The news was very encouraging. 这消息非常鼓舞人心。|近義詞：inspiring, hopeful, promising|反義詞:discouraging, disheartening|adjective
