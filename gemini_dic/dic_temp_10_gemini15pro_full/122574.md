moderatism: /ˈmɒdərətɪzəm/ (US) /ˈmɒdərətɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |温和主义；适度；中庸|The quality of being moderate; avoidance of extremes.|He preached moderation in all things. 他提倡凡事都要适度。|近义词: temperance, restraint, middle-of-the-roadism|反义词: extremism, radicalism, fanaticism|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *moderatismus, from Latin moderatus (past participle of moderari "to moderate"), from modus "measure".
