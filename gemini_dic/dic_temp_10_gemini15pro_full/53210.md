direct:/dəˈrɛkt/ /daɪˈrɛkt/| adj. v.|直接的，直系的；指导，导演|The flight is direct to New York. 这趟航班直飞纽约。|近义词：straight, immediate, guide, conduct|反义词:indirect, circuitous|词性:形容词

direct:/dəˈrɛkt/ /daɪˈrɛkt/| v.|指导，导演|He directed the movie. 他导演了这部电影。|近义词：guide, conduct, manage|反义词:misdirect, mislead|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *directus*, past participle of *dirigere* "to set straight, guide," from *dis-* "apart" + *regere* "to guide, rule."
