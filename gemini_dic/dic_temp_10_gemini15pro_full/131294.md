noncereals:/nɒnˈsɪəriəlz/ /nɑːnˈsɪriəlz/| n.|非谷类食物|Foods that are not cereals, such as pulses, roots, and tubers.|Pulses, such as lentils and chickpeas, are important noncereals in many diets. 豆类，如扁豆和鹰嘴豆，是许多饮食中重要的非谷类食物。

Noncereals like potatoes and yams provide essential carbohydrates. 像土豆和山药这样的非谷类食物提供必要的碳水化合物。|近义词:non-grain foods|反义词:cereals, grains|词性:名词
