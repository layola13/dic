grots:/ɡrɒts/| n.|丑陋的生物；讨厌的家伙；（尤指）地精|Unpleasant or ugly creatures, especially goblins or similar mythical beings.|The grots scurried away into the darkness. 地精们 scurried 逃进了黑暗中。|例句：The cave was infested with grots.  这个洞穴里到处都是地精。

例句：He called the unruly children a bunch of grots. 他把那些不守规矩的孩子叫做一群讨厌鬼。|近义词：goblins, monsters, creatures, brutes|反义词:beauties, angels|词性:名词

词源:
Likely a shortened form of "grotesque," influenced by words like "grubby" and "snot."  可能源自“grotesque”（怪诞的）的缩写形式，受到“grubby”（肮脏的）和“snot”（鼻涕）等词的影响。
