现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This tense is not commonly used with "lets" due to its contracted nature and focus on immediate action.

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
This construction is not used with "lets."  "Let" in this sense cannot take a passive form.

疑问句:
Lets go to the movies, shall we? 我们去看电影，好吗？ (This is a suggestion phrased as a question).

否定句:
