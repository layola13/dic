brayed:/breɪd/| v.| 驴叫；大声而刺耳地喊叫；| to make the loud, harsh cry of a donkey; to speak or shout loudly and harshly | The donkey brayed loudly. 驴子大声地叫。| The politician brayed his empty promises to the crowd.  政客向人群大声喊着空洞的承诺。 |近义词：shrieked, roared, yelled, screamed|反义词: whispered, murmured|词性:动词
bray:/breɪ/| n.| 驴叫声；刺耳的声音| the loud, harsh cry of a donkey; a loud, harsh sound | The bray of the donkey echoed through the valley. 驴叫声在山谷中回荡。|  The bray of the trumpets announced the arrival of the king. 刺耳的喇叭声宣告了国王的到来。|近义词：shriek, roar, yell, scream|反义词: whisper, murmur|词性:名词

词源：
From Middle English *brayen, from Old French braire (“to cry, shout, neigh”), from Vulgar Latin *bracare (“to bray”), from Late Latin bragire, probably of imitative origin. Cognate with Old High German brehan (“to neigh”), Old Norse brœgða (“to move quickly, to weave”).
