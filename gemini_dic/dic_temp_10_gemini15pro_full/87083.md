heptavalent:/ˌheptəˈveɪlənt/
| adj. |七价的；七价原子 |Having a valence of seven.  An atom or element that can form seven bonds with other atoms or elements.|Chromium exists in several oxidation states, including heptavalent chromium. 铬以几种氧化态存在，包括七价铬。|Synonyms: septivalent |Antonyms: monovalent, divalent, trivalent|adjective
