liard:/liˈɑːrd/ | n. | 法国旧辅币；小钱；无价值的东西 | A former French coin of small value; a trifling sum; something worthless. | He wouldn't give a liard for it. 他对此不屑一顾。| 近义词：penny, cent, trifle | 反义词：fortune, wealth | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *lierre, from Latin *lira, from Greek λίτρα (lítra, “pound”). Ultimately from Assyrian *li’tru (“pound”).
