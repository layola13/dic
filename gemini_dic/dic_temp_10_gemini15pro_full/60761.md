electrolier:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈlɪr/| n.|枝形吊灯|A decorative light fixture, usually suspended from a ceiling, with several branches or arms holding electric lamps.  Often ornate and used in grand or formal settings.|The grand ballroom was illuminated by a dazzling crystal electrolier.| 近义词:chandelier, pendant light|反义词:lamp, sconce|noun
