intellectually:/ˌɪntəˈlektʃuəli/ | adv. | 智力上地，理智地 | In a way that relates to the intellect.  |  She stimulated him intellectually. 她激发了他的求知欲。 | 近义词: mentally, cerebrally | 反义词: emotionally, physically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intellectualiter, from intellectualis (“intellectual”) + -iter (adverbial suffix).  The root word is Latin intellectus ("understanding, comprehension"), from intellegō ("I understand").
