mumbler:/ˈmʌmblər/ (US), /ˈmʌmblə(r)/ (UK)| n. |含糊不清说话的人；咕哝者|A person who speaks indistinctly, often quietly and with the mouth partly closed.|He was such a mumbler that I could barely understand a word he said. 他说话含糊不清，我几乎听不懂他在说什么。|近义词：mutterer, murmurer|反义词: orator, speaker|noun
