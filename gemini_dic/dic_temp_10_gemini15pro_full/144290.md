outspend:/ˌaʊtˈspend/| v. |超过…的开支；比…花费更多|To spend more money than (someone or something).|例句：The advertising campaign outspent its competitors by a large margin. 这个广告活动的花费远远超过了它的竞争对手。

例句：He outspent her two to one. 他的花费是她的两倍。|近义词：outlay, surpass, exceed|反义词:underspend|词性:动词
