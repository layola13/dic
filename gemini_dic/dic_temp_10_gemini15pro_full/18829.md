berylline:/ˈbɛrəˌlaɪn, bəˈrɪlˌaɪn/ |adj.|绿玉色的，绿宝石色的|Of a pale green color, resembling that of beryl.|Her eyes were a striking berylline green. 她的眼睛是醒目的绿玉色。|近义词:emerald, jade, aquamarine|反义词:ruby, scarlet|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From beryl + -ine.  Beryl itself comes from Latin beryllus, from Greek βήρυλλος (bḗryllos), ultimately from Prakrit verulia, from Sanskrit वैडूर्य (vaiḍūrya).
