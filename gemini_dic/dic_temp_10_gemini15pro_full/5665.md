altimeter:/ˈæltɪˌmiːtər/ | n. |高度计，测高仪 |An instrument that measures altitude, especially of an aircraft.  | The pilot checked the altimeter to confirm their cruising altitude. 飞行员查看了高度计以确认他们的巡航高度。| The altimeter showed they were rapidly losing altitude. 高度计显示他们正在迅速下降。| synonym: altitude gauge, elevation meter, hypsometer | antonym: depth gauge | noun
