lopsidednesses:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪdnəsɪz/ /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪdnəsɪz/
noun
不平衡；不对称
the state of being lopsided; lack of balance or symmetry.
The lopsidednesses of the electoral map were striking. 选举地图的不对称性十分显著。
近义词: imbalance, asymmetry, disproportion, unevenness
反义词: balance, symmetry, evenness, equilibrium
词性: noun

lopsidedness:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪdnəs/ /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪdnəs/
noun
不平衡；不对称
the state of being lopsided; lack of balance or symmetry.
The lopsidedness of the score reflected the difference in skill between the two teams.  比分上的差距反映了两队之间技术水平的差异。
近义词: imbalance, asymmetry, disproportion, unevenness
反义词: balance, symmetry, evenness, equilibrium
词性: noun

lopsided:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪd/ /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪdnəs/
adjective
不平衡的，倾斜的
Heavier or larger on one side than the other; unevenly balanced; askew.
The painting hung lopsided on the wall.  这幅画挂在墙上，歪歪斜斜的。
近义词: unbalanced, uneven, asymmetrical, askew
反义词: balanced, even, symmetrical, straight
词性: adjective


词源:  From lop + sided. Lop originally meant "hang loosely or limply," and a lopsided object appears to be drooping or sagging on one side.
