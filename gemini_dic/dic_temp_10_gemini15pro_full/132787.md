nonduplicative:/ˌnɒnˌdjuːplɪˈkeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非重复的，无重复的 | Not duplicative; not containing duplicates; having no duplicates. |  The report provides nonduplicative counts of beneficiaries.  这份报告提供了受益人的非重复计数。 |  The database ensures nonduplicative entries of customer information.  数据库确保客户信息的非重复条目。 | 近义词: unique, distinct, non-redundant | 反义词: duplicate, redundant, repetitive | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + duplicative (from duplicate, meaning "to copy or repeat")
