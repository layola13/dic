orgulous: /ˈɔːrɡjʊləs/ | adj. | 傲慢的，自大的 | Excessively proud; haughty. |  例句：He was so orgulous that he refused to acknowledge anyone's help. 他如此傲慢，以至于拒绝承认任何人的帮助。

例句：Her orgulous demeanor alienated many of her colleagues. 她傲慢的举止疏远了许多同事。

近义词：haughty, arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, proud, pompous | 反义词：humble, modest, meek, unassuming | 词性：形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *orgelus, from Old French orguillus (“proud”), from orgueil (“pride”), from Latin orgōlium.
