mildew:/ˈmɪlduː/| n. vi. vt.|霉菌，发霉，使发霉|A superficial growth of fungi that occurs in damp conditions, especially on plants or on organic materials such as cloth or paper. It can cause damage or discoloration.|例句：The damp air encouraged mildew to grow on the walls. 潮湿的空气使墙上发了霉。|近义词：mold, fungus, mustiness|反义词:freshness, cleanness|词性:noun, verb

mildew:/ˈmɪlduː/| vi. vt.|发霉，使发霉|To become covered with mildew; to cause something to become covered with mildew.|例句：The books mildewed in the damp basement. 书籍在地下室的潮湿环境中发霉了。|近义词：mold, rot, decay|反义词:preserve, protect|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mildiew, from milde "mild" + dew. The sense is of a harmful growth fostered by mild, damp conditions.
