outwrench:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃ/
verb
用力扭出;  猛扭
to wrench out; to pull or twist out with force.
He outwrenched the rusty bolt from the beam. 他用力把生锈的螺栓从横梁上拧了出来。
近义词:wrest, wrench, tear, rip, extract
反义词:insert, implant, embed

词性
verb
