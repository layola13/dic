abscond: /əbˈskɒnd/ (UK) /æbˈskɑːnd/ (US)
verb
潜逃；逃遁；躲藏
To leave hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection of or arrest for an illegal action such as theft.
The cashier absconded with the company's funds. = 出纳携款潜逃了。

The suspect absconded before the police arrived. = 嫌疑犯在警察到达之前逃跑了。

近义词: flee, escape, run away, decamp, bolt
反义词: remain, stay, surrender, face, appear
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscondere "to hide, conceal, put away," from ab- "away" + condere "to put together, store, hide."
