misadapted:/ˌmɪsəˈdæptɪd/ | adj. | 适应不良的，不适应的 | Not properly adapted; poorly suited to a particular purpose or environment. | The polar bear, used to icy climates, was misadapted to the sudden heatwave.  北极熊，习惯了寒冷的气候，对突如其来的热浪很不适应。 |  maladapted, ill-suited, unfit, unsuited, inappropriate | adapted, suited, fit, well-suited, appropriate | adjective
