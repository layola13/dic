foreseeable:/fɔːrˈsiːəbl/ /fɔːrˈsiː.ə.bəl/
adjective
可预见的，可以预知的
able to be foreseen or predicted.
The accident was caused by a foreseeable mechanical failure. 这起事故是由可预见的机械故障引起的。
It is foreseeable that the price of oil will rise again.  可以预见的是，油价将会再次上涨。

近义词: predictable, anticipated, expected
反义词: unforeseeable, unpredictable, unexpected
词性: adjective


词源:
From fore- + see + -able.  "Fore-" comes from Old English "fore," meaning "before, in front."  "See" comes from Old English "sēon," meaning "to perceive with the eyes."  "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or worthiness.
