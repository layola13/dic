magazyn: /ˈmaɡazɪn/ | n. | 杂志，仓库 | A publication containing articles and illustrations, typically covering a particular subject or area of interest; a store for goods. | I subscribed to a fashion magazine. 我订阅了一份时尚杂志。|  The goods are stored in the warehouse magazine. 货物存放在仓库里。| 近义词：journal, periodical, publication, storehouse, depot | 反义词: (No direct opposite for a physical object like a magazine or warehouse.) | Noun
