didactic:/daɪˈdæktɪk/
adjective
教诲的，说教的
Intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive.
The poem is not just entertaining, but also didactic. 这首诗不仅有趣，而且具有教诲意义。

didactic:/daɪˈdæktɪk/
adjective
教导的
designed or intended to teach people something
He adopted a didactic tone in his lecture. 他在讲座中采用了一种教导的语气。


synonym:instructive, educational, pedagogic, moralistic, preachy
antonym:uninformative, unenlightening

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *didacticus*, from Ancient Greek διδακτικός (didaktikós, “apt at teaching”), from διδάσκειν (didáskein, “to teach”).
