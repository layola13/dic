nonaccidentally:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛntəli/
adverb
非偶然地，故意地
Not by accident; deliberately.
She nonaccidentally deleted the file, knowing it contained incriminating evidence. 她故意删除了该文件，因为她知道其中包含对她不利的证据。
He nonaccidentally bumped into her, hoping to start a conversation. 他故意撞了她一下，希望能和她搭讪。
近义词: deliberately, intentionally, purposely, on purpose
反义词: accidentally, unintentionally, by chance, incidentally
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + accidentally (adverb)
