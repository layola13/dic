broad-brush:/ˌbrɔːd ˈbrʌʃ/| n.|概括的描述；粗略的分析|a general description or analysis, lacking detail|The initial broad-brush assessment suggested the project was feasible. 最初的粗略评估表明该项目是可行的。|例句：The government's broad-brush approach to education reform has been criticized. 政府对教育改革的粗略做法受到了批评。|近义词：overview, summary, synopsis|反义词：in-depth analysis, detailed study|词性:名词
