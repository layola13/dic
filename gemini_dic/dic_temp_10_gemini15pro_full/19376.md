bibulosity:/ˌbaɪbjuˈlɒsɪti/
n.
饮酒过度;嗜酒
Excessive fondness for or indulgence in alcoholic beverages.
He was known for his bibulosity and often frequented the local pubs. 他以嗜酒闻名，经常光顾当地的酒吧。
近义词: alcoholism, drunkenness, inebriety
反义词: sobriety, abstinence, temperance
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin *bibulōsus* ("given to drinking"), from *bibō* ("I drink").
