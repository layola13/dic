drumbeaters:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːtərz/
noun
鼓手; 击鼓者; 宣传员
A person who plays a drum, especially as part of a band or orchestra, or someone who promotes something enthusiastically.
The drumbeaters led the parade. 鼓手们领着游行队伍。
The company hired drumbeaters to publicize the new product. 公司雇佣了宣传员来宣传新产品。
synonyms: drummer, percussionist, advocate, promoter, propagandist
antonyms: critic, opponent, detractor
n.

PHRASE->
beat the drum for:  为...摇旗呐喊;大力宣传
The politician beat the drum for his new education policy.  这位政治家大力宣传他的新教育政策。

a drumbeat of: 一连串的;接连不断的
There has been a drumbeat of bad news recently. 最近坏消息不断。

march to the beat of a different drum:  特立独行；标新立异
He marches to the beat of a different drum. 他特立独行。

drum something into someone's head:  反复灌输；谆谆教诲
The teacher drummed the grammar rules into our heads. 老师反复向我们灌输语法规则。

drum up support/business/interest:  争取支持/招揽生意/激起兴趣
They are trying to drum up support for their candidate. 他们正在努力争取对他们候选人的支持。
