paraquet:/ˈpærəkɛt/ /ˈpɑːrəkɛt/ | n. |长尾小鹦鹉|A small, slender parrot with a long, graduated tail, typically brightly coloured. |例句：The paraquet perched on the branch, preening its vibrant green feathers. 长尾小鹦鹉栖息在树枝上，梳理着它鲜艳的绿色羽毛。
