metropolitan:/ˌmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən/
| adj.|大都市的；大城市的|Relating to or characteristic of a metropolis; belonging to a large city.|She enjoys the metropolitan lifestyle. 她喜欢大都市的生活方式。|
近义词:cosmopolitans, city dwellers
反义词:rural residents, provincials
词性:noun, adjective (depending on usage)


词源:
From Late Latin *metropolitanus, from Ancient Greek μητροπολίτης (mētropolítēs, “citizen of a metropolis”), from μήτηρ (mḗtēr, “mother”) + πόλις (pólis, “city”).  The original meaning referred to a bishop of a metropolis, later expanding to encompass inhabitants of large cities.
