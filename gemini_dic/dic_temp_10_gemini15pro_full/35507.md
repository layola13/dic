coadjuvant:/koʊˈædʒʊvənt/| adj. n.| 辅助的，佐剂；辅助物，佐剂 |Something that assists another thing, especially a drug or therapy, in its action or effect. |The doctor prescribed a coadjuvant therapy to boost the effectiveness of the main treatment. 医生开了辅助治疗来提高主要治疗的效果。|Synonyms: synergistic, ancillary, adjuvant, assisting, supplementary |Antonyms: primary, main, principal|adjective, noun

coadjuvant:/koʊˈædʒʊvənt/| n.| 辅助物，佐剂 |A substance that enhances the body's immune response to an antigen. |The vaccine contains a coadjuvant to stimulate a stronger immune response.  这种疫苗含有一种佐剂，可以刺激更强的免疫反应。|Synonyms: adjuvant, booster, immunostimulant|Antonyms: immunosuppressant|noun
