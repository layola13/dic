duskiness:/ˈdʌskɪnɪs/
n.
昏暗；暗淡；朦胧
the quality of being rather dark in colour.
The duskiness of the room made it difficult to see.
房间的昏暗使人难以看清。

近义词:dimness, darkness, gloom, shadowiness
反义词:brightness, lightness, radiance

duskiness:/ˈdʌskɪnɪs/
n.
(肤色)黝黑
darkness of complexion.
The duskiness of her skin was accentuated by the white dress.
白色的连衣裙更加突显了她黝黑的肤色。

近义词:swarthiness, darkness
反义词:fairness, paleness


词源: From dusk + -iness.
