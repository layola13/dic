over-theatrical:/ˌəʊvə(r)θiˈætrɪkəl/
adjective
过分戏剧化的; 过于夸张的
Excessively theatrical; exaggerated or melodramatic in style or behavior.
Her reaction to the news was over-theatrical, complete with gasping and fainting. 她对这个消息的反应过于戏剧化，伴随着喘息和昏厥。
dramatic, exaggerated, histrionic, melodramatic, affected
understated, subtle, low-key, restrained
adjective
