nonrandomness:/ˌnɒnˈrændəmnəs/| n.| 非随机性，非偶然性|The nonrandomness of the sample selection caused bias in the results.  样本选择的非随机性导致结果偏差。|The study demonstrated a clear pattern of nonrandomness in the data. 这项研究证明了数据中明显的非随机性模式。|近义词：intentionality, purposefulness, design|反义词：randomness, chance, haphazardness|词性: noun
Etymology: non- + randomness.  "Randomness" comes from the Old French word "randon" meaning "impetuosity, speed, violence."
