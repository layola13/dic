mail's:/meɪlz/
noun
邮件的；邮政的
Possessive form of mail, indicating ownership or association with mail.
The mail's late arrival delayed the important document. 邮件的迟到延误了重要文件。
近义词: postal, post's
反义词: N/A
词性: Possessive noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *male, from Frankish *malha "bag, sack," from Proto-Germanic *malho- "bag, sack."
