nomadic: /noʊˈmædɪk/ | adj. | 游牧的；流浪的 |  Roaming about from place to place; having no fixed abode.  Relating to or characteristic of nomads. | The nomadic tribes moved their herds across the vast plains.  游牧部落把他们的牲畜群赶过广阔的平原。 |  The family lived a nomadic lifestyle, traveling from town to town. 这个家庭过着游牧式的生活，从一个城镇到另一个城镇旅行。 | itinerant, wandering, migratory, roving | settled, sedentary | adjective
