booby-trap:/ˈbuːbiː træp/ | n. | 诡雷；陷阱；圈套 | A device or situation designed to harm or trick someone unexpectedly. |  The soldiers were careful to avoid any booby-traps. 士兵们小心翼翼地避开所有诡雷。|
Synonyms: trap, snare, pitfall, ambush | Antonyms: safeguard, protection, security | noun

ETYMOLOGY->

From "booby," a term for a foolish or naive person (from Spanish *bobo*), + "trap."  The image is of a simple trap that even a booby would fall for.
