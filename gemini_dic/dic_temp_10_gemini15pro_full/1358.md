accumulating: /əˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
积累，积聚
Gradually gathering or increasing in number or quantity.
The snow is accumulating on the roof. = 屋顶上的雪越积越多。

Evidence is accumulating that he committed the crime. = 证明他犯罪的证据越来越多。

近义词: gathering, collecting, amassing, increasing, accruing
反义词: decreasing, diminishing, dwindling, dispersing
词性: Verb (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin accumulare "to heap up," from ad- "to" + cumulus "heap."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Dust is accumulating on the shelves. = 灰尘在架子上积聚。
He is accumulating wealth through investments. = 他通过投资积累财富。

一般过去时:
The evidence accumulating against him led to his arrest. = 针对他的证据越来越多，最终导致他被捕。
Debts were accumulating rapidly during that period. = 那段时间债务迅速累积。

现在完成时:
She has been accumulating experience in the field for years. = 多年来，她一直在该领域积累经验。
They have been accumulating evidence against the suspect. = 他们一直在收集针对嫌疑人的证据。

过去完成时:
By the time the police arrived, a crowd had been accumulating outside the building. = 警察到达时，大楼外已经聚集了一群人。
He had been accumulating debt for several years before declaring bankruptcy. = 在宣布破产之前，他已经积累了多年的债务。

将来时:
The interest on your savings will be accumulating over time. = 你的储蓄利息会随着时间的推移而累积。
Scientists predict that greenhouse gases will continue accumulating in the atmosphere. = 科学家预测温室气体将继续在大气中累积。

被动语态:
A large amount of data is being accumulated by the researchers. = 研究人员正在积累大量数据。
Sufficient evidence was accumulated to convict the defendant. = 积累了足够的证据来给被告定罪。
