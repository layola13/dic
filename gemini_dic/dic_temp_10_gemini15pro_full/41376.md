corky:/ˈkɔːrki/| adj.|软木似的；活泼的；（非正式）恼怒的；|Resembling cork in texture or buoyancy; lively, brisk; (informal) slightly annoyed or irritated.|The wine had a corky smell. 这酒有股软木塞味。|近义词： buoyant, lively, sprightly, irritable, annoyed |反义词：dull, lifeless, calm, pleased|adjective

corky:/ˈkɔːrki/| n.|（澳大利亚俚语）左撇子|A left-handed person (Australian slang).|He's a corky, so he sits on the left side of the table. 他是个左撇子，所以他坐在桌子的左边。|近义词：lefty, southpaw |反义词：righty |noun
