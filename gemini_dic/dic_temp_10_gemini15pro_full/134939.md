ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non < *ne oinom (not one), meaning "not".  Indicates negation or absence.
mysticism (n.): From Late Latin mysticismus, from Greek mystikos "initiated," from mystēs "one initiated into mysteries."  Refers to beliefs or practices involving mystical experiences or insights.
-ism (suffix):  Forms nouns denoting a doctrine, system, practice, or characteristic.
