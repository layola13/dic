brain-washed:/breɪnˌwɒʃt/ /breɪnˌwɑːʃt/| adj.|洗脑的，被灌输的|The process of systematically and forcibly indoctrinating someone into accepting radically different beliefs by using psychological manipulation and pressure.|He acted like a brainwashed robot, repeating the same phrases over and over. 他表现得像个被洗脑的机器人，一遍又一遍地重复着同样的短语。|近义词：indoctrinated, programmed|反义词：independent-minded, freethinking|词性:形容词
brain-washing:/breɪnˌwɒʃɪŋ/ /breɪnˌwɑːʃɪŋ/| n.|洗脑|The act or process of brainwashing someone.|The cult used sleep deprivation and repetitive chanting as part of their brainwashing techniques.  这个邪教组织利用睡眠剥夺和反复吟唱作为他们洗脑技术的一部分。|近义词：indoctrination, propaganda|反义词：education, enlightenment|词性:名词

词源:  "brainwashing" is a calque (loan translation) of the Chinese term 洗脑 (xǐnǎo), which literally means "wash brain". It gained prominence during the Korean War, referring to the methods used by Chinese communists to indoctrinate American prisoners of war.
