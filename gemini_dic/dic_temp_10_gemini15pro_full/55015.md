disputeless:/dɪˈspjuːtlɪs/
adj.
无可争议的；无异议的
Undisputed; unquestionable; beyond doubt or debate.
The evidence presented was disputeless, leading to a swift verdict.  无可争议的证据导致了快速的判决。
His claim to the throne was disputeless. 他对王位的继承权无可争议。
近义词：undisputed, incontestable, undeniable, irrefutable, unquestionable
反义词：disputed, debatable, controversial, questionable
词性: adjective

词源:
From dispute + -less.  Dispute comes from Old French disputer, from Latin disputare "discuss, examine, argue," frequentative of dis- "apart" + putare "to reckon, think."
