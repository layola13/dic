brainstormed:/ˈbreɪnstɔːrmd/ | v. | 集思广益；集体讨论；头脑风暴 |  Past participle of "brainstorm," meaning to generate ideas creatively and collaboratively.  | We brainstormed solutions to the problem. 我们集思广益地讨论了问题的解决方案。 | The team brainstormed marketing strategies for the new product. 团队集体讨论了新产品的营销策略。 | 近义词：discussed, deliberated, collaborated | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) worked individually, kept silent | 词性: 动词 (past participle)

brain-storm:/ˈbreɪnstɔːrm/ | n. | 头脑风暴；集体讨论 | A sudden, severe mental disturbance. An intense, creative group discussion. |  The team held a brainstorm session to generate new ideas. 团队举行了一次头脑风暴会议以产生新的想法。 | The marketing team had a brainstorm about the new campaign.  市场团队就新活动进行了一次头脑风暴。 | 近义词: idea generation, discussion, collaboration | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) individual work, lack of discussion | 词性: 名词
