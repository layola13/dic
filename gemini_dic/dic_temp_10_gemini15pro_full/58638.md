dumbfound:/dʌmˈfaʊnd/| vt.|使惊呆，使目瞪口呆|To make speechless with amazement; astonish.|The news completely dumbfounded me.  这个消息让我完全惊呆了。|Synonyms: astound, amaze, stun, bewilder, shock|Antonyms: bore, tire, expect|verb

dumbfound:/dʌmˈfaʊnd/| adj.(obsolete, rare) | 哑口无言的，惊呆的 | Unable to speak, especially because of surprise. |He was dumbfounded by the sudden accusation. 他被突如其来的指控惊得哑口无言。|Synonyms: speechless, stunned, astonished |Antonyms: articulate, eloquent |adjective
