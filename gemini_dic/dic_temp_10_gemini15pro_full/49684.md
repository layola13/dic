demitint:/ˈdɛmiˌtɪnt/ | n. | 半色调 | A printing technique that simulates continuous tone imagery through the use of dots, varying either in size or in spacing. | The artist used demitint to create a subtle shading effect. 艺术家使用半色调来创造一种微妙的阴影效果。| synonym: halftone | antonym: full tone | noun

demitint:/ˈdɛmiˌtɪnt/ | adj. | 半色调的 | Relating to or made using the demitint technique. | The demitint print gave the image a vintage look. 这张半色调印刷品使图像具有复古感。| synonym: halftone | antonym: full tone | adjective
