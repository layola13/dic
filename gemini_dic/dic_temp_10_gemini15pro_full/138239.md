nostalgists: /nɒˈstældʒɪsts/ | n. | 怀旧的人；念旧的人 | People who feel or express nostalgia; those who long for things, people, or situations of the past. |  Many nostalgists yearn for the simpler days of their childhood. 许多怀旧的人渴望童年时代更简单的日子。

The cafe, decorated with vintage posters, was a haven for nostalgists. 这家装饰着复古海报的咖啡馆是怀旧者的天堂。| 近义词: sentimentalists, romanticists | 反义词: modernists, futurists | 词性: noun
