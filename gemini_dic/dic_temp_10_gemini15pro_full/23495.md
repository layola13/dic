brahmin:/ˈbrɑːmɪn/ /ˈbrɑːmən/| n.|婆罗门；（有时用作贬义）自以为文化程度或社会地位很高的人|A member of the highest Hindu caste, that of the priesthood.|例句：The brahmin chanted ancient verses. 婆罗门吟诵着古老的经文。

例句：He acted like a brahmin, looking down on everyone else. 他表现得像个婆罗门，瞧不起其他人。|近义词：priest, clergyman|反义词：outcast, pariah|词性:noun

词源: From Sanskrit *brāhmaṇa*, from *brahman* (“sacred knowledge, absolute reality”).
