accrue: /əˈkruː/
verb
积累，增加
(of sums of money or benefits) be received by someone in regular or increasing amounts over time.  (指款项或利益) 随时间推移而定期或越来越多地收到。
Interest will accrue on the account at a rate of 5% per annum.  该账户将以每年 5% 的利率累积利息。

My savings have accrued a significant amount of interest over the years. 多年来，我的储蓄累积了大量的利息。

近义词: accumulate, gather, collect, amass
反义词: decrease, diminish, dwindle, deplete
词性: verb

Interest accrues on this account monthly. =  这个账户每月都会累积利息。
Benefits accrue to members of the association. =  协会成员可获得利益。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:
