courageous:/kəˈreɪdʒəs/| adj.|勇敢的，有胆量的|Possessing or showing courage; brave.|例句：It was a courageous decision to speak out against the injustice.  公开反对不公正是一个勇敢的决定。|近义词：brave, valiant, bold, daring, fearless, intrepid, heroic|反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corageus (Modern French courageux), from corage (“courage”) + -eux (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin cor (“heart”).
