overresisting:/ˌoʊvərɪˈzɪstɪŋ/
verb
过度抵抗，抵抗过度
Resisting something too much or with excessive force.
The lawyer argued that the defendant was overresisting arrest, leading to the escalation of force. 律师辩称被告过度抵抗逮捕，导致了武力的升级。
over-resisting arrest:过度抵抗逮捕
overzealous, excessive
under complying, yielding
