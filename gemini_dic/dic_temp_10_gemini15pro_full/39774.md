consomme:/kɑːnˈsoʊmeɪ/ /ˈkɒnsɒmeɪ/| n.|清汤，浓缩肉汤|A clear soup made from richly flavored stock that has been clarified, often served hot.|She served a delicate consommé as a starter. 她以清淡的浓缩肉汤作为开胃菜。|近义词：broth, stock, bouillon|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thick soup")|noun
