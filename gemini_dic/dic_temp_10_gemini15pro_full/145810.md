overdedicating: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度奉献；过分投入
dedicating excessively, devoting too much time or effort to something
She was overdedicating herself to her work, neglecting her family and friends. 她过度投入工作，忽略了家人和朋友。
He was so overdedicating to the project that he forgot to eat or sleep. 他对这个项目过于投入，以至于忘记了吃饭和睡觉。
近义词: overworking, overexerting, overcommitting
反义词: neglecting, underworking
词性: Verb (present participle)

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "dedicate" (from Latin "dedicare" meaning "to declare, devote").
