faunae:/ˈfɔːniː/
n.
动物群
The animals of a particular region, habitat, or geological period.
The faunae of the Galapagos Islands are unique and diverse. 加拉帕戈斯群岛的动物群独特而多样。
The researchers were studying the faunae of the Amazon rainforest. 研究人员正在研究亚马逊雨林的动物群。
synonyms: animals, wildlife, creatures
antonyms: flora (植物群)
noun
