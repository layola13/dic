overspeeds:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːdz/ | v. (overspeeds, overspeeding, oversped) | 超速行驶；超速运转 | To operate or cause to operate at a speed in excess of the prescribed limit.  Driving over the speed limit is dangerous and illegal. | 例句：The car oversped on the highway and was pulled over by the police. 汽车在高速公路上超速行驶，被警察拦了下来。

例句：The engine oversped and caused damage to the machinery. 引擎超速运转，导致机器损坏。| 近义词：exceed the speed limit, drive too fast | 反义词：drive slowly, decelerate | 词性：动词

overspeed:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːd/ | n. | 超速 | The act or instance of exceeding a prescribed speed. | 例句：Overspeed is a common cause of traffic accidents. 超速是交通事故的常见原因。

例句：The overspeed of the turbine caused significant vibrations. 涡轮的超速引起了剧烈的振动。| 近义词：excessive speed | 反义词：low speed | 词性：名词


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "beyond") + "speed" (noun or verb relating to velocity).  From Old English "sped" (success, prosperity, speed).
