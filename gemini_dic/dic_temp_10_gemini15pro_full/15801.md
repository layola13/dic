balkanize: /ˈbɔːlkənaɪz/ /ˈbæl-/ | vt. | 巴尔干化；使分裂 | To divide (a region or body) into smaller mutually hostile states or groups. |  The Austro-Hungarian empire was balkanized after World War I.  奥匈帝国在第一次世界大战后巴尔干化了。

balkanized: /ˈbɔːlkənaɪzd/ /ˈbæl-/ | adj. | 巴尔干化的；分裂的 | Divided into small, mutually hostile units. | The formerly unified country became balkanized after the civil war.  这个曾经统一的国家在内战后变得支离破碎。

balkanization: /ˌbɔːlkənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌbæl-/ | n. | 巴尔干化；分裂 | The division of a region or body into smaller mutually hostile states or groups. | The balkanization of the region has led to constant conflict. 该地区的巴尔干化导致了持续不断的冲突。


近义词: fragment, divide, partition, splinter
反义词: unite, unify, integrate
词性:  balkanize (动词), balkanized (形容词), balkanization (名词)
