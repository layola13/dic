huskinesses:/ˈhʌskɪnɪsɪz/
n.
沙哑声，嘶哑声
a rough or hoarse quality of voice
Her voice was filled with huskinesses from crying all night. 她的声音因为哭了一整夜而充满了沙哑。
Synonyms: hoarseness, roughness, raspiness
Antonyms: clarity, smoothness, mellowness
词性: 名词(plural)
