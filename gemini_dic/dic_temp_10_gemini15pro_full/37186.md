comediennes:/kəˌmiːdiˈɛnz/
|n.|女喜剧演员|Female comedians; women who perform comedy, especially making people laugh by telling jokes or funny stories.|The comediennes had the audience in stitches. 这些女喜剧演员让观众笑个不停。|Synonyms:  female comics, humorists, funny women|Antonyms: tragediennes, serious actresses|Noun
Etymology->
From French comédienne, feminine of comédien (“actor”), from Italian commedia (“comedy”), from Latin comoedia (“comedy”), from Ancient Greek κωμῳδία (kōmōidía, “comedy, revel”), from κῶμος (kômos, “revel, merrymaking”) + ᾠδή (ōidḗ, “song”).
