contourne:/kɒnˈtʊəneɪ/ /kɑːnˈtʊrn/ | v. | 绕过，避开 | To go around or bypass something, especially an obstacle or difficult situation. |  The army contoured the enemy's defenses by taking a longer route through the mountains. 军队绕过敌人的防线，选择了一条更长的山路。

The hikers contoured the swamp by following a narrow trail.  徒步旅行者沿着一条狭窄的小路绕过了沼泽。| 近义词：bypass, circumvent, skirt | 反义词：confront, face | 词性：动词
