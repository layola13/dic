antiaristocratic:/ˌæntiˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk/
adj.
反贵族政治的；反对贵族统治的
Opposed to or hostile towards aristocracy or government by an aristocracy.
The French Revolution was fueled by strong antiaristocratic sentiments. 法国大革命是由强烈的反贵族情绪推动的。
The writer's antiaristocratic views are evident in his novels.  这位作家的反贵族观点在他的小说中很明显。
近义词:anti-elitist, egalitarian, democratic
反义词:aristocratic, elitist, oligarchic
词性:adjective
