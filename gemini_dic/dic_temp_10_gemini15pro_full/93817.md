immigrant:/ˈɪmɪɡrənt/| n. adj.|移民，移居者；移民的|A person who comes to live permanently in a foreign country.  A person who has immigrated.|Many immigrants come to this country seeking better opportunities. 许多移民来到这个国家寻求更好的机会。|The immigrant population has contributed significantly to the country's economy. 移民人口对国家经济做出了重大贡献。|近义词：newcomer, settler, migrant|反义词：emigrant, native|词性: noun, adjective

词源: From Latin *immigrantem*, present participle of *immigrare* "to move or pass into," from *in-* "into, in, on, upon" + *migrare* "to depart, change, move."
