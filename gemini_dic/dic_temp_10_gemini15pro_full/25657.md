bunds:/bʊndz/
n.
德国政府债券
German government bonds.
German Bunds are considered a safe investment. 德国债券被认为是一种安全的投资。
近义词:bonds, government securities
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of bond)
词性: Noun


bund:/bʊnd/
n.
堤坝，堤岸；捆，束；联盟，同盟
1. An embankment or an artificial raised bank.
The river bund prevented the town from flooding.  河堤阻止了小镇被洪水淹没。
2. Something that binds or restrains. (Less common usage)
3. A league or alliance, particularly a political one. (Often used in historical contexts)
The political bund was formed to oppose the government. 这个政治联盟的成立是为了反对政府。
近义词: embankment, levee, dyke (for definition 1);  alliance, league, federation (for definition 3)
反义词: (No direct antonym for all meanings)
词性: Noun
