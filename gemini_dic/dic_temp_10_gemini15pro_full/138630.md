nucleolus:/njuːˈkliːələs/, /nuːˈkliːələs/ | n. | 核仁 | A small, round structure found inside the nucleus of a cell. It is not membrane-bound and is involved in the production of ribosomes. | The nucleolus is clearly visible under a microscope.  核仁在显微镜下清晰可见。

The nucleolus plays a crucial role in protein synthesis. 核仁在蛋白质合成中起着至关重要的作用。 |  近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include: nuclear body, ribosome factory) | 反义词: (No antonym) | 词性: Noun
