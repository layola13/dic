coelacanths:/ˈsiːləkænθs/ | n. | 腔棘鱼 | A rare order of fish, Coelacanthiformes, that includes two extant species and was thought to have been extinct since the Cretaceous period.  They are known as "living fossils." |  Coelacanths were thought to be extinct until one was caught in 1938.  腔棘鱼曾被认为已经灭绝，直到1938年有人捕获了一条。 |  Synonyms: living fossil | Antonyms: None | Noun
