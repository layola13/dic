innovations:/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃənz/ | n. |创新，革新| The introduction of new things, ideas or ways of doing something. |  Many technological innovations have changed the way we live. 许多技术创新改变了我们的生活方式。


The company is known for its constant stream of innovations. 这家公司以其源源不断的创新而闻名。


近义词: advancements, developments, improvements, breakthroughs
反义词: stagnation, tradition


词性: 名词(复数)


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin innovatio(n-), from the verb innovare, from in- ‘into’ + novus ‘new’.
