coppler:/ˈkɒplər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːplər/ (US)| n.|扣环，联结器，接合器|A device used to connect two things, especially parts of machinery. |The coppler on the train carriage failed, causing a delay. 火车车厢的联结器故障了，导致延误。| The coppler joined the two pipes together securely. 联结器将两根管子牢固地连接在一起。| synonyms: coupler, connector, link, joint, fastener| antonyms: separator, divider | noun
