cabalistic:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|神秘的，隐秘的，玄奥的|Relating to or characteristic of the Kabbalah (Jewish mysticism) or other esoteric doctrines; occult; mysterious. Having a secret or hidden meaning.|The old book was filled with cabalistic symbols, making it difficult to understand.|Synonyms: occult, esoteric, mystical, arcane, cryptic, enigmatic|Antonyms: clear, obvious, straightforward, exoteric|adjective
