inclusion:/ɪnˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 包容，包含；内含物 |The act of including someone or something as part of a group, set, or thing.  |例句：The inclusion of diverse perspectives is crucial for effective teamwork.  包容不同的观点对于高效的团队合作至关重要。

例句：The price includes flights and accommodation.  价格包含机票和住宿。|近义词：incorporation, integration, addition |反义词：exclusion, omission |词性:noun
