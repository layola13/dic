forspent:/fɔːrˈspent/
adjective
筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的
utterly worn out or exhausted
The forspent marathon runners collapsed at the finish line.  筋疲力尽的马拉松运动员在终点线倒下了。
After a long day's work, I felt utterly forspent.  一天辛苦工作后，我感到筋疲力尽。
Synonym: exhausted, worn out, spent, drained, fatigued
Antonym: energetic, invigorated, refreshed
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *forspenten*, from Old English *forspendan* (“to spend, use up, exhaust”), from *for-* (“completely, utterly”) + *spendan* (“to spend”).
