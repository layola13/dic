floreted:/ˈflɔːrɪtɪd/ /ˈflɑːrɪtɪd/
adjective
华丽的；花哨的；辞藻华丽的
Decorated or adorned with flowers or flourishes; also, excessively ornate in language or style.
The writer's floreted prose, though beautiful, sometimes obscured the meaning.  这位作家的华丽散文虽然优美，但有时却模糊了意义。
近义词：ornate, embellished, flowery, flamboyant
反义词：plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective
