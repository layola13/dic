cascader's:/kæˈskeɪdərz/| n.|瀑布群的；倾泻者的；级联器的|belonging to or associated with a cascader or a cascade.  Something related to a series of waterfalls or something arranged in a series of stages, each of which feeds the next.|The cascader's roar filled the valley. 瀑布群的轰鸣声响彻山谷。|近义词：waterfall's, series', tiered|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly 'single', 'isolated')|noun, possessive
