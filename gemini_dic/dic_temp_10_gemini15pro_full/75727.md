full-timers:/ˌfʊlˈtaɪmərz/
noun
全职人员
People who work a full-time job, typically 35-40 hours per week.
Many companies offer benefits to their full-timers, such as health insurance and paid time off. 许多公司为其全职员工提供福利，例如健康保险和带薪休假。
Full-timers are essential for maintaining consistent workflow and productivity. 全职员工对于保持一致的工作流程和生产力至关重要。
Synonyms: permanent staff, full-time employees
Antonyms: part-timers, temporary staff
