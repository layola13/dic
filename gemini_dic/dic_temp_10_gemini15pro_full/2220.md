addressed:/əˈdrɛst/ | v. | 演说；处理；称呼；写姓名地址 | to speak to; to deal with; to direct one's words to; to write the name and address of someone on something | He addressed the audience with passion. 他热情地对观众发表了演讲。

The committee addressed the issue of funding. 委员会讨论了资金问题。| 近义词：dealt with, tackled, spoke to, directed, contacted | 反义词：ignored, neglected | 词性：动词

addressed:/əˈdrɛst/ | adj. | 已处理的；已写地址的 | having been dealt with; having a name and address written on it | The addressed envelope was returned to sender. 写了地址的信封被退回给寄件人。

The addressed concerns were documented in the meeting minutes. 已处理的问题记录在会议记录中。 | 近义词：dealt with, handled, labeled | 反义词：unaddressed, pending | 词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *adressen*, from Old French *adresser*, from Late Latin *addirectiāre* (“to make straight, to direct”), from Latin *ad* (“to”) + *directus* (“straight”).


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She addresses the crowd with confidence. = 她自信地向人群发表讲话。
He addresses the letter to his mother. = 他把信寄给他母亲。

一般过去时:
The president addressed the nation last night. = 总统昨晚向全国发表了讲话。
They addressed the problem immediately. = 他们立即解决了这个问题。


现在完成时:
I have addressed all the concerns raised by the customers. = 我已经解决了所有客户提出的问题。
The government has addressed the issue of unemployment. = 政府已经处理了失业问题。


过去完成时:
He had addressed the letter before he realized the mistake. = 他在意识到错误之前已经写好了信的地址。
They had addressed the issue several times before a solution was found. = 在找到解决方案之前，他们已经多次讨论过这个问题。


将来时:
The CEO will address the staff tomorrow. = 首席执行官明天将对员工讲话。
We will address this matter at the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上讨论这个问题。


被动语态:
The issue was addressed by the management team. = 管理团队处理了这个问题。
The letter was addressed to the wrong person. = 信寄错了人。

疑问句:
How should I address the Queen? = 我应该如何称呼女王？
Has this issue been addressed yet? = 这个问题已经处理了吗？

否定句:
He did not address the elephant in the room. = 他没有提及房间里显而易见的问题。
The letter was not addressed correctly. = 信的地址写错了。


PHRASE->
address oneself to =  致力于；着手处理
address the needs of = 满足...的需求
address the problem/issue/challenge = 解决问题/问题/挑战
address a gathering/meeting/conference = 在聚会/会议/大会上讲话
address a letter/package/parcel = 给信件/包裹/包裹写地址
