cudgeling:/ˈkʌdʒəlɪŋ/
n.| 棍棒打击; 痛打|The act of hitting someone with a cudgel.|He received a cudgeling for stealing the apples. 他因为偷苹果而遭到一顿痛打。|
近义词:beating, thrashing, bludgeoning
反义词:caressing, stroking
词性:noun (gerund or present participle)

cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/
n.| 棍棒; 短棍|A short, thick stick used as a weapon.|The farmer used a cudgel to defend himself against the wolves. 农夫用一根短棍保护自己免受狼的袭击。|
vt.| 用棍棒打|To beat with a cudgel.|The guard cudgelled the prisoner into submission. 守卫用棍棒打囚犯，使他屈服。|
近义词:club, stick, bludgeon
反义词:feather, pillow
词性:noun, verb

词源:
From Middle English *kuggil*, from Old French *coignoille* (“cudgel, club”), from Vulgar Latin *cuneolus*, diminutive of Latin *cuneus* (“wedge”).
