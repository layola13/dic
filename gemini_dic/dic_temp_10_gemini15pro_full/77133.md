gasconade:/ˌɡæskəˈneɪd/| n. vi.|夸夸其谈，吹牛，自吹自擂|Boastful or extravagant talk or behavior.  A vaunting and pompous manner of speaking or acting.|He engaged in a lengthy gasconade about his supposed accomplishments. 他对他所谓的成就大肆吹嘘。|近义词：boasting, bragging, vaunt, bluster|反义词：modesty, humility|词性:noun, verb

词源: From French *gasconnade*, from Gascon, inhabitant of Gascony, France, known for boastfulness.
