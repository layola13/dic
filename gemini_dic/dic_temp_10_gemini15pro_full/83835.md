hakims:/ˈhɑːkiːmz/ | n. | judges; Islamic judges | Plural form of hakim, referring to multiple judges, particularly in an Islamic context. | The hakims deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict.  法官们商议了几个小时才做出裁决。 |  judges, magistrates, justices | N/A | noun


Etymology:
From Arabic حَاكِم (ḥākim, “ruler, governor, judge”), active participle of حَكَمَ (ḥakama, “to rule, govern, judge”).
