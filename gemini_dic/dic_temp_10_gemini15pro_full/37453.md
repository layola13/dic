commissariat:/ˌkɒmɪˈsɛːrɪət/ /ˌkɑːmɪˈsɛːriət/| n.|军需处，粮秣，伙食|A department responsible for supplying food and equipment, especially to an army. |The army's commissariat was responsible for feeding the troops. 军队后勤部门负责士兵的伙食。|Synonyms: supply, logistics, provisioning|Antonyms: deprivation, shortage|noun

commissariat:/ˌkɒmɪˈsɛːrɪət/ /ˌkɑːmɪˈsɛːriət/| n.|（尤指苏联的）人民委员部|A government department in the Soviet Union. |The People's Commissariat for Internal Affairs was responsible for state security. 内务人民委员部负责国家安全。|Synonyms: ministry, department, agency|Antonyms: N/A|noun
