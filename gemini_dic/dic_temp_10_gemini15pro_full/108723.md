leany:/ˈliːni/| adj.|瘦的，憔悴的|Thin, especially because of illness or hunger; emaciated.|The refugees arrived looking leany and exhausted. 难民们到达时看起来又瘦又疲惫。|近义词：thin, gaunt, emaciated, skinny, slender|反义词：fat, plump, obese, chubby|词性:形容词

词源：From Middle English *lene, from Old English *hlæne (“lean, meager, thin”), from Proto-Germanic *hlainjaz (“lean”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lēynos (“thin, lean”). Cognate with Dutch leen (“fief”), German Lehen (“fief”), Old Norse lán (“loan”).
