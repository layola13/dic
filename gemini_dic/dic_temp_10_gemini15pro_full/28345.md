carapace:/ˈkærəpeɪs/| n.|甲壳，外壳|A hard outer covering or shell, such as that of a turtle or crustacean.|The turtle retreated into its carapace when it sensed danger.  乌龟感觉到危险时就缩进了壳里。|近义词：shell, covering, exoskeleton|反义词:(none -  lack of a carapace would be described, not a direct opposite)
