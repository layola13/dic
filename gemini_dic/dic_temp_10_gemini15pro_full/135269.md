nonpareil:/ˌnɒn.pəˈrel/ /ˌnɑːn.pəˈrel/ | n. adj.| 糖珠，无与伦比的人或物 |A small, round candy sprinkle used for decorating cakes and other desserts. Also, a person or thing that is unequaled or unmatched.| The cake was decorated with colorful nonpareils. 蛋糕上装饰着五颜六色的糖珠。 | 近义词:unequalled, peerless, matchless, incomparable| 反义词: ordinary, common, mediocre| 词性:名词, 形容词

nonpareil:/ˌnɒn.pəˈrel/ /ˌnɑːn.pəˈrel/ | adj.| 无与伦比的，最佳的 |Having no equal; peerless. |  He is a nonpareil athlete. 他是一位无与伦比的运动员。 | 近义词:unequalled, peerless, matchless, incomparable| 反义词: ordinary, common, mediocre| 词性:形容词

Etymology: From French *nonpareil* "unequaled, peerless," from Old French *non pareil*, literally "not equal."
