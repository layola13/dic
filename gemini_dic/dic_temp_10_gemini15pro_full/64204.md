equidifferent:/ˌiːkwɪˈdɪfərənt/
adjective
等差的
Having equal differences; increasing or decreasing by the same amount.
The numbers 4, 7, 10, 13 are equidifferent, as the difference between successive terms is always 3.
这些数字4、7、10、13是等差的，因为连续项之间的差始终是3。
equidistant, uniform
varying, unequal
adjective
