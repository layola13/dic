cloddier:/ˈklɑːdiər/ /ˈklɒdiə(r)/ | adj. | 更结块的，更土块的 | More cloddy; having more or larger clods (of earth).  A comparative form of "cloddy." | The soil in this field is much cloddier than in the other one. 这块田里的土壤比另一块田里的土块更多。 |The rain made the ground even cloddier. 雨水使得地面更加结块。 |  近义词：lumpier, chunkier | 反义词：smoother, finer | 词性:形容词

cloddy:/ˈklɑːdi/ /ˈklɒdi/ | adj. | 结块的，土块的 | Consisting of or full of clods of earth or clay.  | The field was cloddy and difficult to plow.  这块田地满是土块，难以耕作。 | After the rain, the path was cloddy and slippery. 雨后，小路泥泞不堪，很滑。 | 近义词：lumpy, bumpy, chunky | 反义词：smooth, fine | 词性:形容词


词源:
From clod + -y.  "Clod" comes from Old English *cluda, related to clot.
