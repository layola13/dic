compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪt/ (US)| v. |补偿，赔偿，弥补|to give something to someone in return for something that they have lost or damaged; to provide something good to balance or reduce the bad effects of something|例句：The company compensated her for the injury. 公司赔偿了她受伤的损失。|近义词：reimburse, recompense, offset, make up for|反义词： penalize, deprive, fail to reward|verb

compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪt/ (US)| n. |补偿，赔偿（罕用名词形式，通常用compensation）|(Rarely used as a noun, usually "compensation" is used) The act of compensating.|例句：He received some compensate for his troubles. 他因遭受的麻烦得到了一些补偿。(更常见的表达是：He received some compensation for his troubles.)|近义词：recompense, restitution, redress|反义词： penalty, forfeiture|noun (rare)
