nucule:/ˈnjuːkjuːl/| n.|小坚果，闭果|A small, hard, one-seeded fruit that does not split open at maturity, typical of many plants in the mint family (Lamiaceae) and the borage family (Boraginaceae).|The nucule of the plant was surprisingly large for its species.|近义词：nutlet, achene|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nucule*, from Latin *nux* ("nut") + *-cula* (diminutive suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱneu̯-*.
