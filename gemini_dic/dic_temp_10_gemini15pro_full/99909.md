internationalization:/ˌɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | /ˌɪntərˌnæʃənəˌləˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |国际化|The process of adapting a product or service to different languages and cultures.|例句：The internationalization of the company is a key part of its growth strategy.  公司的国际化是其增长战略的关键部分。

例句：Internationalization is crucial for businesses seeking to expand their global reach.  对于寻求扩大全球影响力的企业来说，国际化至关重要。

|近义词：globalization, localization|反义词: regionalization, localization|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From international + -ization.  "International" comes from  "inter-" (between) + "national" (relating to a nation).  "-ization" is a suffix forming nouns meaning the action or process of making or becoming.
