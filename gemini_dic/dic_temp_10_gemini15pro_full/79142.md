gladness's:/ˈɡlædnəsɪz/
n.
快乐，高兴
The possessive form of gladness, indicating belonging to or associated with gladness.
例句：The gladness's impact on her health was evident. 快乐对她的健康的影响是显而易见的。

例句：He felt the gladness's warm embrace. 他感受到了快乐的温暖拥抱。

近义词：joy, happiness, delight, glee
反义词：sadness, sorrow, grief, unhappiness
词性：名词 (Noun)


词源:
From Old English *glædnes, from glæd (“glad”).
