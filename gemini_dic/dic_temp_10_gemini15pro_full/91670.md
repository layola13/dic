hyperchlorinations:/ˌhaɪpərˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|过度氯化（作用）|The act of adding excessive amounts of chlorine to something, typically water, often for disinfection purposes.  It can lead to the formation of harmful byproducts.|
|Excessive chlorination can lead to the formation of harmful byproducts. 过度氯化会导致有害副产物的形成。|
|The city implemented hyperchlorination of the water supply after the bacterial contamination.  该市在细菌污染后对供水进行了过度氯化。|
