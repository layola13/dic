nonbending:/ˌnɒnˈbɛndɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈbɛndɪŋ/| adj.|不易弯曲的，坚硬的，不屈服的|Not bending; rigid; inflexible.|The nonbending steel beam supported the entire structure. 这根不易弯曲的钢梁支撑着整个结构。|近义词：rigid, inflexible, stiff, unyielding|反义词：flexible, pliable, bending, yielding|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Old French non-, from Latin non "not."
bending (present participle of bend): From Old English bendan "to bend, curve," of Germanic origin.
