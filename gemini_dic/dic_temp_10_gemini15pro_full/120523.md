minxishly:/ˈmɪŋksɪʃli/| adv.|妖娆地，轻佻地，风骚地|In a manner suggestive of a minx; flirtatiously or playfully mischievous.|She smiled minxishly and batted her eyelashes. 她妖娆地笑着，眨了眨睫毛。|近义词：flirtatiously, playfully, teasingly, mischievously|反义词：demurely, modestly, seriously|词性：副词

词源:
From minx + -ish + -ly.  Minx itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Low German *minske  meaning "nun" (ironically), or perhaps a variant of mink, referring to the animal's sleekness and quick movements.
