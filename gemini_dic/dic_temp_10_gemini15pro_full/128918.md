nincompoop:/ˈnɪŋkəmˌpuːp/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or stupid person.|例句：He's such a nincompoop, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个傻瓜，他又把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be a nincompoop, think before you act! 别犯傻了，三思而后行！|近义词：fool, idiot, moron, simpleton|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:noun
