geospatially:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈspeɪʃəli/
adjective
地理空间地
Relating to or involving the geographical location of something.  Existing in or having to do with space on or near the Earth's surface.
例句：Geospatially referenced data is crucial for urban planning. 地理空间参考数据对于城市规划至关重要。

例句：The software analyzes geospatially distributed environmental factors. 该软件分析地理空间分布的环境因素。

近义词：geographically, spatially
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "nonspatially" or "aspatially" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective
