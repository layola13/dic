cosplayer:/ˈkɒspleɪər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːspleɪər/ (US)| n. |角色扮演者|A person who dresses up as a character from a book, movie, video game, or other work of fiction, often at conventions or other events.| She's a talented cosplayer who creates her own costumes. 她是一位才华横溢的角色扮演者，自己制作服装。|  He met a lot of fellow cosplayers at the anime convention. 他在动漫大会上遇到了很多志同道合的角色扮演者。| 近义词:costume player, role-player| 反义词: None| 词性: noun
