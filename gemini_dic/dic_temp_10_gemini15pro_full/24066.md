例句：The sun beat brenningly down on the desert. 烈日灼烤着沙漠。|近义词：fiercely, intensely, passionately, ardently|反义词：mildly, gently, calmly|词性:副词
词源: From "burning" + "-ly"
"Burning" comes from Old English "byrnan" (to burn), of Germanic origin, related to Dutch "branden" and German "brennen".
"-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
