hypophyllous:/ˌhaɪpəˈfɪləs/| adj.|叶下的，生于叶下的|Growing on the underside of a leaf.|Example: The fern has hypophyllous sori. 这种蕨类植物的孢子囊群生于叶下。|Example:  Hypophyllous fungi are often found in humid environments.  叶下生长的真菌常见于潮湿的环境。|Synonym: abaxial|Antonym: adaxial|adjective
