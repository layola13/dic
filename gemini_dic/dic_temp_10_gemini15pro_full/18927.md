PHRASE->
bespeak of something:  表明某事  His actions bespeak of his generosity. 他的行为表明了他的慷慨。
bespeak someone something: 为某人预订某物  I bespoke him a table at the restaurant. 我为他预订了餐厅的座位。
bespeak something for someone: 为某人预订某物  She bespoke a new suit for her husband. 她为丈夫订做了一套新西装。
bespeak attention/consideration etc:  值得注意/考虑等  The issue bespeaks careful consideration. 这个问题值得仔细考虑。
bespeak the fact: 表明事实  The results bespeak the fact that the strategy is working. 结果表明该策略正在奏效。
