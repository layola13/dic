muricate:/ˈmjʊərɪkeɪt/ | adj. | 有刺的；粗糙的；表面不平的 | Covered with short, sharp points or prickles. | The muricate surface of the seashell was rough to the touch. 海贝的粗糙表面摸起来很粗糙。 |  The muricate seed pods clung to my clothes. 带刺的种子荚粘在我的衣服上。 | 近义词: prickly, spiny, thorny, barbed | 反义词: smooth, soft | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *muricatus*, past participle of *muricāre* "to make like a murex," from *murex* "murex," a kind of shellfish with a spiny shell.
