on-the-heels-of:/ɒn ðə hiːlz ɒv/ |prepositional phrase|紧随…之后，紧接着…|closely following something or someone|例句：The second disaster followed on the heels of the first. 第二场灾难紧随第一场灾难而来。|例句：A new crisis arose on the heels of the previous one. 新的危机紧接着之前的危机出现。|近义词：immediately after, following closely, close behind, in quick succession|反义词：long after, preceding, before|词性：介词短语
