goosefish:/ˈɡuːsfɪʃ/| n. |鮟鱇鱼，琵琶鱼|A bottom-dwelling marine fish with a large head and mouth and an illicium (a modified dorsal fin spine) that lures prey. It's also known as anglerfish or monkfish.|例句：The goosefish uses its bioluminescent lure to attract prey in the dark depths of the ocean. 鮟鱇鱼利用其生物发光的诱饵在黑暗的海洋深处吸引猎物。

例句：Goosefish is considered a delicacy in some cultures. 在某些文化中，鮟鱇鱼被认为是一种美味佳肴。|近义词：anglerfish, monkfish|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of fish)|词性:noun
