misinterpreters:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprətərz/
n.
误解者；曲解者
People who misunderstand or misinterpret something.
The misinterpreters twisted her words to suit their own agenda. 误解者歪曲了她的原话以迎合他们自己的意图。
The author felt frustrated by the misinterpreters of his work. 作者对曲解他作品的人感到沮丧。
Synonyms: misconstruers, distorters
Antonyms: interpreters, explainers
noun
