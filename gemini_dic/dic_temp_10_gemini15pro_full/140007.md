oeconomical:/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkl/ /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪkl/| adj.|经济的，节俭的|Avoiding waste and being efficient with resources.  Managing money or resources in a careful and sensible way.|She is an economical housekeeper. 她是一位节俭的家庭主妇。|It's more economical to buy in bulk. 批量购买更划算。|近义词：thrifty, frugal, sparing|反义词：extravagant, wasteful|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin oeconomicus, from Greek oikonomikos, from oikonomia ("household management").
