overinflating:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度充气；过分膨胀
The act of inflating something beyond its safe or recommended limit.
They were warned against overinflating the tires.  他们被警告不要给轮胎充气过量。
The media was accused of overinflating the importance of the event. 媒体被指责过分夸大了事件的重要性。
overinflate
inflate, overblow, pump up
deflate, underinflate
