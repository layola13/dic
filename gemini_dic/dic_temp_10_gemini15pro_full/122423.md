moaners:/ˈməʊnəz/ (UK) /ˈmoʊnərz/ (US)| n. |抱怨者，牢骚满腹的人|People who habitually complain or express dissatisfaction.|The moaners are never happy, no matter what you do.  抱怨者们从来都不开心，无论你做什么。|The restaurant was full of moaners complaining about the service.  这家餐馆里满是抱怨服务的人。|近义词：complainers, whiners, grumblers|反义词：optimists, enthusiasts|词性: 名词
