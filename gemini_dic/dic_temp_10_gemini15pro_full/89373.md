homoorgan:/ˌhoʊmoʊˈɔːrɡən/| n. |同种器官|An organ transplanted between genetically identical individuals (identical twins).  Formed from "homo-" (same) and "organ".|Identical twins can receive homoorgan transplants without the risk of rejection.|近义词: isograft, syngraft|反义词: allograft, xenograft|noun
