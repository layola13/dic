nonrestorative:/ˌnɒn rɪˈstɒrətɪv/ | adj. |非恢复性的| Not tending to restore health, strength, or consciousness; not restorative.|  The surgery was nonrestorative, and he remained paralyzed.  这手术是非恢复性的，他仍然瘫痪。|  The patient received nonrestorative care focused on comfort.  病人接受了以舒适为主的非恢复性护理。 |Near synonyms:  irreversible, incurable, non-remedial | Antonyms: restorative, curative, remedial | Adjective
