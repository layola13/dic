non-renewable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈnjuːəbl/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈnuːəbl/| adj.|不可再生的|Not able to be renewed; not replaceable after use.|Fossil fuels are non-renewable resources. 化石燃料是不可再生资源。|The world is increasingly reliant on non-renewable energy sources. 世界越来越依赖不可再生能源。|近义词：finite, exhaustible|反义词: renewable, sustainable|词性:形容词
Etymology->
non-:  prefix meaning "not" from Latin *non*
renewable: from re- "again" + new + -able.
