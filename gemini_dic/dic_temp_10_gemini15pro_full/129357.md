nolition:/nəˈlɪʃən/
n.
不愿; 不情愿; 厌恶
Unwillingness to do something; reluctance.
He expressed his nolition to participate in the project. 他表达了不愿参与这个项目。
Near Synonyms: reluctance, unwillingness, aversion, disinclination, resistance
Antonyms: willingness, eagerness, enthusiasm, desire
