miscodes:/ˌmɪsˈkoʊdz/
| v. |错误编码，写错代码|To write or enter code incorrectly, resulting in errors or unexpected behavior in a computer program. | He frequently miscodes variables, leading to debugging nightmares. 他经常把变量写错，导致调试噩梦连连。 | The intern miscoded the database query, causing the website to crash.  实习生写错了数据库查询代码，导致网站崩溃。| misspell, mistype, blunder | correctly code, accurately code | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + code (from Latin 'codex' meaning 'book' or 'tablet').  The modern sense related to computer programming emerged in the mid-20th century.
