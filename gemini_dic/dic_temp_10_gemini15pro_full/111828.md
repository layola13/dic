loggerheaded:/ˈlɒɡəhedɪd/ /ˈlɔːɡərˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|顽固的，愚笨的，迟钝的|Stubborn, stupid, and unwilling to change your opinion, like the hard head of a loggerhead turtle.|He's so loggerheaded, he won't listen to anyone's advice. 他太固执了，谁的建议也不听。|The loggerheaded politician refused to compromise.  这位固执的政治家拒绝妥协。|近义词: obstinate, stubborn, pigheaded, bullheaded, inflexible|反义词: amenable, compliant, flexible, open-minded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From loggerhead (a type of sea turtle known for its large head) + -ed. The large head of the turtle was associated with stupidity and stubbornness.
