abducting:/əbˈdʌktɪŋ/
verb
绑架，劫持
The act of taking someone away illegally, typically by force or deception.
The police are investigating the abducting of a young child. 警方正在调查一名幼儿被绑架的案件。

The millionaire's son was abducting from his boarding school. 百万富翁的儿子从他的寄宿学校被绑架了。

kidnapping, seizing, snatching
rescuing, releasing
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abductus, past participle of abducere (“to lead away”), from ab- (“away”) + ducere (“to lead”).
