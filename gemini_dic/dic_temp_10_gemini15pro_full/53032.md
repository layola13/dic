diodon:/ˈdaɪədɒn/ (US) /ˈdaɪədən/ (UK)| n. |刺豚，二齿鲀|A type of fish characterized by its ability to inflate its body into a ball shape when threatened, covered in spines.|例句：The diodon puffed itself up into a spiky ball when the diver approached. 刺豚在潜水员靠近时膨胀成一个带刺的球。

例句：Diodon are found in tropical and subtropical waters around the world.  刺豚分布在世界各地的热带和亚热带水域。|近义词：pufferfish, porcupinefish, blowfish|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From Greek *di* (two) + *odous* (tooth), referring to the fused teeth that form a beak-like structure.  由希腊语 *di*（两个）+ *odous*（牙齿）组成，指的是融合形成喙状结构的牙齿。
