mccarthyism: /məˈkɑːrθiɪzəm/ | n. |麦卡锡主义|A campaign against alleged communists in the US government and other institutions carried out under Senator Joseph McCarthy in the period 1950–54. Many of the accusations were unsupported. |例句：The McCarthyism era was a dark period in American history. 麦卡锡主义时代是美国历史上的黑暗时期。

例句：His accusations were pure McCarthyism. 他的指控纯粹是麦卡锡主义。|近义词：witch-hunt, red-baiting, demagoguery |反义词: tolerance, liberalism |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of US Senator Joseph McCarthy (1908–57) + -ism.
