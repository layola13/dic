attitudinally:/ˌætɪˈtjuːdɪnəli/
adverb
态度上地
In a way that relates to attitude.
She reacted attitudinally, showing her displeasure through her body language. 她从态度上做出了反应，通过肢体语言表达了她的不满。
The twins, though genetically identical, responded attitudinally different to the news.  这对双胞胎虽然基因相同，但对这个消息的态度反应却不同。
近义词:mentally, emotionally, psychologically
反义词:behaviorally, physically
词性:adverb
