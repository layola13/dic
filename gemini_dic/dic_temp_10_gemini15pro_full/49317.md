delightful:/dɪˈlaɪtfəl/| adj.|令人愉快的；可爱的|Causing delight; highly pleasing.|"We had a delightful evening." 我们度过了一个愉快的夜晚。|近义词：pleasant, enjoyable, charming, lovely|反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, awful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *delitful*, from *deliten* (“to delight”) + *-ful* (“full of”), equivalent to *delight* + *-ful*.
