feedings:/ˈfiːdɪŋz/
noun
喂食；饲养；一次喂食的量
the act of giving food, especially to a baby or animal, or the food given.
The baby needs four feedings a day.  这个婴儿每天需要喂四次。
The farmer is responsible for the feedings of all his livestock.  农民负责喂养他所有的牲畜。
Synonyms: meals, nourishment, provisions
Antonyms: starvation, deprivation
