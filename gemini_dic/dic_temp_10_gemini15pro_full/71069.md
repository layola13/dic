firstly:/ˈfɜːstli/ (UK) /ˈfɜːrstli/ (US)| adv.|首先，第一|Used to introduce the first point in a list or sequence.  It indicates the initial item in a series.|Firstly, I want to thank you all for coming. 首先，我要感谢大家的到来。|Firstly, we need to gather all the necessary materials. 首先，我们需要收集所有必要的材料。|近义词：first, in the first place, to begin with, initially|反义词: lastly, finally|词性:副词
