organzine:/ˈɔːɡənziːn/ | n. |强捻丝，经丝|A type of silk yarn that is tightly twisted and strong, often used for warp threads in weaving.|The dress was made of a beautiful organzine silk. 这件衣服是用漂亮的强捻丝绸制成的。|近义词：silk thread, warp yarn|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loosely: weft yarn) |noun
