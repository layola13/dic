ecumenist:/ɪˈkjuːmənɪst/| n. |大公主义者；普世教会合一运动的支持者|A person who advocates or promotes ecumenism, the principle or aim of promoting unity among the world's Christian churches.|例句：The ecumenist worked tirelessly to bridge the divides between different denominations.  这位大公主义者不知疲倦地工作，以弥合不同教派之间的分歧。

例句：She is a renowned ecumenist, dedicated to fostering dialogue between various faiths. 她是一位著名的大公主义者，致力于促进不同信仰之间的对话。|近义词：unifier, conciliator |反义词:sectarian, schismatic |词性:noun

ecumenic:/ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪk/| adj. |大公的；普世的；世界范围的|Representing a number of different Christian churches.|例句：The ecumenical council brought together leaders from across the globe. 世界大公会议汇集了来自世界各地的领袖。

例句：The Pope’s ecumenical efforts have been praised by many. 教皇为促进普世教会合一所做的努力受到了许多人的赞扬。|近义词：universal, global, worldwide |反义词:local, parochial, sectarian |词性:adjective
