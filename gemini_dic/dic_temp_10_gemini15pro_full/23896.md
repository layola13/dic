breakage:/ˈbreɪkɪdʒ/| n. |破损；损坏；破损量；损耗|The act or process of breaking, or the state of being broken.  The breakage of the vase was accidental. 花瓶的破损是意外造成的。|例句：

The insurance policy covers accidental breakage.
该保险单涵盖意外损坏。

There was a lot of breakage during the move.
搬家过程中有很多东西破损了。

|近义词：damage, loss, fracture, rupture|反义词:repair, mending|词性:名词
