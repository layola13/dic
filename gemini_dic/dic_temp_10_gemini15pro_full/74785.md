freighted:/ˈfreɪtɪd/
adjective
充满的；装满的；承载…的
Loaded or burdened with something, typically something heavy or troublesome.
The ship was freighted with goods. 这艘船装满了货物。
The air was freighted with tension.  空气中充满了紧张气氛。
synonyms: laden, burdened, loaded, charged
antonyms: unburdened, empty, light
adjective

freighted:/ˈfreɪtɪd/
verb (past tense: freighted, past participle: freighted)
运送（货物）；装载
Transport (goods) by freight.  (This usage is less common than the adjective form)
The company freighted the goods by rail.  这家公司通过铁路运输货物。
They freighted the ship with timber.  他们用木材装载了这艘船。
synonyms: transport, ship, convey, carry
antonyms: unload, discharge

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *freighten*, from Old French *freter* (to hire, load a ship), from Frankish *fraht*  (freight, load, cargo), from Proto-Germanic *fraχtaz* (load, cargo). Ultimately related to "freight" (n.).
