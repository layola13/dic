incriminator:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪtər/ | n. |控告者，告发者| Someone or something that incriminates another person or thing.  This word is less common than "accuser". | The leaked email proved to be a powerful incriminator against the CEO. 这封泄露的电子邮件成为指控首席执行官的有力证据。| 近义词: accuser, informant, witness | 反义词: defender, exonerator | noun
