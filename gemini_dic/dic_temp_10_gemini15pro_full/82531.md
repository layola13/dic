guaco:/ˈɡwɑːkoʊ/ | n. |瓜果, 一种南美攀缘植物; 由其叶子制成的药物 |A tropical American climbing plant (Mikania guaco), or a medicine made from its leaves, believed to be an antidote to snakebites. |The indigenous people used guaco to treat snakebites.  土著人用瓜果治疗蛇咬。|Synonym:  snakebite antidote, bejuco |Antonym:  poison, venom |noun
