drossless:/ˈdrɒsləs/| adj.|无渣的，纯净的|Free from dross; pure.|The refiner produced drossless gold. 提炼者生产出了纯金。|近义词：pure, refined, unalloyed|反义词：impure, contaminated, adulterated|词性:形容词
词源:  From dross + -less.  Dross (meaning waste matter, refuse) comes from Old English *dros, of uncertain origin.
