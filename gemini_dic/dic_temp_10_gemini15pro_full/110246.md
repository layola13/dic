lifers:/ˈlaɪfərz/
noun
终身囚犯; 无期徒刑犯
People serving life sentences in prison.  A person sentenced to life imprisonment.
例句：The lifers spend their days in the prison yard. 终身囚犯们在监狱的院子里度过他们的日子。

例句：He became a lifer after being convicted of murder. 他因谋杀罪被判处无期徒刑，成了终身囚犯。
近义词：inmate, convict, prisoner
反义词：(None - opposite would be someone not serving a life sentence)
词性:n. (noun)
