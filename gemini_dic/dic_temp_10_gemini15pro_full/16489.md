barged:/bɑːrdʒd/
verb
闯入， barge的过去式和过去分词
moved forcefully or roughly
He barged into the meeting without knocking. 他没敲门就闯进了会议室。
近义词：crashed, burst, stormed, plowed
反义词：tiptoed, sneaked

barged:/bɑːrdʒd/
verb
(用驳船)运输，barge的过去式和过去分词
transport by barge
The coal was barged down the river. 煤炭是用驳船沿着河运下来的。
近义词：transported, shipped, conveyed
反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用其他交通工具运输，例如：trucked, flown)
