azimuths: /ˈæzɪməθs/ | n. | 方位，方位角 |  The horizontal angle or direction of a compass bearing from north measured clockwise.  It is a measurement of angular distance from north. | The surveyor measured the azimuths of several landmarks. 测量员测量了几个地标的方位角。| The azimuth of the rising sun is always east.  冉冉升起的太阳的方位角始终是东方。 |  bearings, directions | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *azimut, from Arabic السمت (as-samt, “the direction, the way, the path”), from سَمَتَ (samata, “he went, traveled, followed a road”).
