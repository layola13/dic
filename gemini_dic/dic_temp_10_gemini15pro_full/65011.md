estrogens: /ˈɛstrədʒənz/ | n. | 雌激素 | Any of a group of steroid hormones that are secreted by the ovaries in females and that stimulate the development of female secondary sexual characteristics.  They are also produced in lesser amounts in males by the testes and adrenal cortex. |  High levels of estrogens are present during pregnancy. 怀孕期间雌激素水平很高。|  Estrogen replacement therapy can help alleviate menopausal symptoms. 雌激素替代疗法可以帮助缓解更年期症状。 | synonyms: female sex hormones | antonyms: androgens | noun
