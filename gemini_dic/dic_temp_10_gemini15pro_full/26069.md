bushmen's:/ˈbʊʃmənz/
noun
布须曼人的（东西）
belonging to or associated with Bushmen.
The bushmen's traditional way of life is increasingly threatened. 布须曼人的传统生活方式正日益受到威胁。
The museum displayed examples of bushmen's art.  博物馆展出了布须曼人的艺术作品。
Synonyms: San people's, hunter-gatherer's
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "settler's" in some contexts)
词性:  noun (possessive)
