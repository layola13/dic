insufferableness:/ɪnˈsʌfərəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
难以忍受；令人厌恶
The quality or state of being insufferable; intolerableness.
Her insufferableness made it difficult to work with her. 她令人难以忍受的特质使人难以与她共事。
insupportability, intolerableness, unendurableness
tolerability, pleasantness
noun
