anathematical:/ˌænəθəˈmætɪkəl/
adjective
被数学诅咒的；厌恶数学的；与数学无关的
Accursed by mathematics; averse to mathematics; not related to mathematics.
He was anathematical when it came to complex equations. 他面对复杂的方程式时就显得被数学诅咒了一样。
The poet's mind was anathematical, preferring metaphors to measurements.  这位诗人的思维与数学无关，更喜欢隐喻而不是测量。
近义词:innumerate, non-mathematical, number-blind
反义词:mathematical, numerate
词性:adjective
