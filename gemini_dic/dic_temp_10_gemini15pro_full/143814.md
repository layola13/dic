outpart:/ˈaʊtpɑːrt/| v. (archaic)|分配，分发|To distribute or assign (something) as a share or portion.|He outparted the land among his sons. 他把土地分给了他的儿子们。|Synonyms: allot, apportion, distribute, divide|Antonyms: collect, gather, amass, consolidate|verb
