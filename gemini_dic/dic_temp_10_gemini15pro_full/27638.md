candelabra: /ˌkændəˈlɑːbrə/ (singular), /ˌkændəˈlɑːbrə/ or /ˌkændəˈlɑːbriː/ (plural)| n.|烛台；枝状大烛台|A large branched candlestick or lamp with several lights.|The dining room table was adorned with a silver candelabra. 餐厅的餐桌上装饰着一个银质烛台。|Synonyms: candlestick, chandelier|Antonyms: lamp, light|noun

candelabras: /ˌkændəˈlɑːbrəz/| n.|烛台（复数）|Plural form of candelabra, referring to multiple branched candlesticks or lamps.|Two large candelabras flanked the fireplace. 壁炉两侧有两个大烛台。|Synonyms: candlesticks, chandeliers|Antonyms: lamps, lights|noun
