obeisantly:/oʊˈbiːsəntli/ /əˈbiːsəntli/
| adv. |顺从地，服从地|In a manner that shows obedience; submissively.  Showing compliance or submissiveness to authority.|She obeisantly followed her mother's instructions. 她顺从地听从了母亲的指示。|Synonym: submissively, compliantly, dutifully, respectfully, meekly|Antonym: defiantly, rebelliously, disobediently|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *obeisauntly*, from Old French *obeissant* (obedient) + *-ly* (suffix forming adverbs).  Ultimately from Latin *oboedientem* (obedient).
