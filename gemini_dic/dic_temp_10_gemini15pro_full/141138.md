onychophora:/ˌɒnɪˈkɒfərə/ /ˌɑːnɪˈkɑːfərə/
noun
缓步动物门；有爪动物门
A phylum of small, worm-like animals with segmented bodies and paired, claw-bearing legs, also known as velvet worms.
Onychophora are found in tropical and subtropical regions.  缓步动物门动物分布于热带和亚热带地区。
Velvet worms are fascinating members of the Onychophora phylum.  天鹅绒虫是缓步动物门中引人入胜的成员。
synonyms: velvet worms, peripatus (common name for some species)
antonyms: N/A (Onychophora is a specific taxonomic group, not a concept with an opposite.)
