inflictable:/ɪnˈflɪktəbl/| adj.|可施加的，可招致的|Capable of being inflicted; that can be imposed (as a punishment or something unpleasant).|例句：The judge deemed the punishment inflictable. 法官认为可以施加这种惩罚。

例句：The disease is inflictable by contaminated water. 这种疾病可以通过受污染的水传播。|近义词：imposable, applicable, visitable|反义词：unimposable, preventable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inflictabilis, from Latin inflictus (past participle of infligere "to strike against, dash upon; inflict") + -abilis "-able". Infligere itself is from in- "in, on, upon" + fligere "to strike".
