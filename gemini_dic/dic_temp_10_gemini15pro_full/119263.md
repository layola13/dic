michery:/ˈmɪtʃəri/| n.|恶作剧，捣蛋，鬼把戏|Secret acts of mischief; playful trickery or roguishness.|The children were full of michery, hiding their father's glasses and giggling.|近义词：mischief, pranks, shenanigans, trickery|反义词:obedience, good behavior|词性:noun
Etymology:  From Middle English *micherie, from Old French *micherie, from micher "to hide, conceal," of uncertain origin.
