navel-gazing:/ˈneɪvəlˌɡeɪzɪŋ/
n.|自省；沉思；孤芳自赏|Excessive contemplation of oneself or one's own thoughts and feelings.|例句：The author criticizes the navel-gazing of some modern philosophers.  作者批评了一些现代哲学家的孤芳自赏。

例句：He was tired of her constant navel-gazing and wanted her to engage more with the world. 他厌倦了她不断的自省，希望她能更多地融入这个世界。
|近义词：introspection, self-absorption, rumination|反义词：extroversion, outward focus|Noun
