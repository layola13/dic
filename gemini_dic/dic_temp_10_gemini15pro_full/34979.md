clog:/klɑːɡ/ /klɒɡ/| n. vt. vi.|障碍物，堵塞；堵塞，阻塞|1.(n.) something that blocks a pipe or opening; 2.(v.) to block something.|例句：The hair is a clog in the drain. 头发是排水管里的堵塞物。|例句：The drain clogged with hair. 排水管被头发堵塞了。|近义词：(n.) obstruction, blockage; (v.) block, obstruct|反义词：(v.) clear, unblock|词性:名词,动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cloggen, from Old North French *cloguer (“to impede with a clog”),  probably from a Germanic source akin to clog (“a block of wood”).  Related to Dutch kloggen (“to clog”).
