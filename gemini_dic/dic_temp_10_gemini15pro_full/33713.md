circadianly:/sɜːrˈkeɪdiənli/
adverb
昼夜节律地，以昼夜节律的方式
In a way that follows a daily cycle, especially one relating to the 24-hour cycle of physiological processes in living beings.
Melatonin is released circadianly, peaking at night and falling during the day.  褪黑激素是按照昼夜节律释放的，在夜间达到峰值，白天则下降。
The hospital lights were dimmed circadianly to mimic natural light patterns. 医院的灯光会按照昼夜节律变暗，以模拟自然光模式。
近义词: rhythmically, cyclically
反义词: arrhythmically, irregularly
词性: adverb

Etymology: From circadian + -ly. Circadian from circa (about) + dies (day).
