gibbier:/ˈɡɪbiər/| adj.|更驼背的，更弯腰驼背的|Comparative form of gibbous: more gibbous.  More humpbacked or hunched.|The old man became gibbier with each passing year.  这位老人一年比一年驼背。|近义词：more hunched, more stooped, more crooked|反义词:straighter, more erect|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *gibbous, from Latin gibbosus (“humpbacked”), from gibbus (“hump, hunch”).
