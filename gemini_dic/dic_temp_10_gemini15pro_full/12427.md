askaris:/ɑːsˈkɑːriːz/
n.
士兵，(尤指)非洲士兵
Native African soldiers, especially in the former colonies of East Africa.
The British employed askaris in their East African colonies.  英国在东非殖民地雇佣了阿斯卡利士兵。
mercenaries, troops, soldiers
civilians
