misprovoked: /ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊkt/
adjective
被无故激怒的；无故被挑衅的
Unjustly or without due cause provoked; provoked without sufficient reason.
His reaction seemed completely misprovoked. 他的反应看起来完全是无故的。
Near Synonyms: unprovoked, unwarranted, needless, gratuitous
Antonyms: justified, provoked, warranted
词性: adjective
