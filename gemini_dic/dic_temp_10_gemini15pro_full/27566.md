canalises:/ˈkænəlaɪzɪz/
verb
引导；使固定化；使…集中
to cause something to follow a particular route or to go in a particular direction
例句：The government is trying to canalise all investment into rural areas. 政府正试图将所有投资引导到农村地区。

例句：His energies were canalised into writing. 他的精力都集中在写作上。
Synonyms: channel, direct, guide, funnel
Antonyms: disperse, scatter
词性:v.
