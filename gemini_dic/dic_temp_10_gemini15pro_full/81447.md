gratifying:/ˈɡrætɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj.|令人满足的；喜人的；使人高兴的|Giving pleasure or satisfaction. |例句：It's gratifying to know that our work is appreciated. 知道我们的工作受到赏识是令人欣慰的。

例句：It was a gratifying victory after so much hard work.  经过如此艰辛的工作，这是一场令人欣慰的胜利。|近义词：satisfying, rewarding, fulfilling, pleasing|反义词：disappointing, frustrating, disheartening|词性:形容词

词源: From gratify + -ing.  "Gratify" comes from the Latin gratificari "to oblige, to please," from gratus "pleasing, agreeable, thankful" + -ficari, combining form of facere "to make, to do."
