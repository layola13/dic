carsick:/ˈkɑːrˌsɪk/ /ˈkɑːrˌsɪk/| adj.|晕车的，乘车时感到恶心的|Experiencing nausea and/or vomiting induced by the motion of a vehicle, especially a car.|Example: I get carsick on long journeys. 我长途旅行会晕车。
Example:  He felt carsick after the winding mountain road.  在蜿蜒的山路上行驶后，他感到晕车。|近义词：travel-sick, motion-sick|反义词: None|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From car + sick.  "Sick" in this context retains its older meaning of "ill" or "nauseated."
