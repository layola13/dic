geodesist:/ˌdʒiːˈɒdɪsɪst/ /ˌdʒiːˈoʊdɪsɪst/
noun
大地测量学家
A specialist in geodesy, the science that deals with the shape and area of the Earth or large portions of it.
The geodesist used GPS technology to accurately measure the movement of tectonic plates.  这位大地测量学家使用GPS技术精确测量了构造板块的运动。
The geodesist calculated the precise coordinates of the summit.  这位大地测量学家计算了峰顶的精确坐标。
synonyms: surveyor, cartographer
