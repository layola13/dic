hyopastron:/haɪˈɒpəstrɒn/
noun
下腹部; 腹下部
The lower part of the abdomen.
The surgeon made an incision in the hyopastron.  外科医生在下腹部做了一个切口。

The pain was localized in the hyopastron. 疼痛集中在下腹部。

synonym: hypogastrium, lower abdomen
antonym: epigastrium


Etymology: From Ancient Greek  *ὑπό* (hupó, “under”) + *ἄστρον* (ástron, “star-like, star-shaped”).  Relates to the arrangement of abdominal muscles.
