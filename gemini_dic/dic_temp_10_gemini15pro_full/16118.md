bangs:/bæŋz/
noun
刘海
A fringe of hair cut straight across the forehead.
Her bangs covered her eyebrows. 她的刘海遮住了眉毛。
synonyms: fringe, bangs
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a hairstyle)
