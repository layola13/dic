capitalina:/kapiˌtaliːnə/
n.|首都的（女性）居民；首都的（女性）事物|A female resident of a capital city, or something related to a capital city and feminine in gender.
|María, a capitalina, loved the hustle and bustle of Mexico City.|玛丽亚，一位首都居民，热爱墨西哥城的喧嚣。
|The capitalina architecture of the building was impressive.|这座建筑的首都风格建筑令人印象深刻。
|近义词：citizen (市民), resident (居民)|反义词:(No direct antonym, could be 'rural' (乡村的) if referring to location)
