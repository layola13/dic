flibbertigibbet:/ˈflɪbərtiˌdʒɪbɪt/| n.|轻浮的人，喋喋不休的人|A frivolous, flighty, or excessively talkative person.|She's such a flibbertigibbet, always flitting from one thing to another.|近义词：scatterbrain, chatterbox, busybody|反义词：serious person, quiet person|词性: noun

Etymology:
Origin uncertain, perhaps imitative of chattering.  First recorded in the 16th century.
