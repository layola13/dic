heists:/heɪsts/
n.
抢劫(尤指银行)；劫持；盗窃
An act of stealing something valuable, typically by a planned operation.
The thieves made off with a haul of jewels in a daring daylight heist.  这群盗贼在光天化日之下大胆抢劫，盗走了一批珠宝。
synonyms: robberies, burglaries, thefts, holdups
antonyms: returns, donations, restitutions
noun
