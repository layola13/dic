condensing:/kənˈdɛnsɪŋ/
verb
冷凝；凝结
The process of a gas or vapor changing to a liquid.
Steam condensed on the cold window.  水蒸气在冰冷的窗户上凝结。
The author condensed his long novel into a short story. 作者把他的长篇小说缩写成一个短篇故事。
synonyms:  liquefying, solidifying, abridging, shortening
antonyms:  evaporating, expanding, lengthening
verb

condensing:/kənˈdɛnsɪŋ/
adjective
冷凝的；凝结的
Relating to or involving condensation.
a condensing lens  冷凝透镜
The condensing steam created a lot of water. 凝结的蒸汽产生了大量的水。
synonyms:  liquefying, compacting
antonyms:  evaporating, expanding
adjective
