biome: /ˈbaɪoʊm/ | n. | 生物群系 | A large naturally occurring community of flora and fauna occupying a major habitat, e.g., forest, tundra. |  The Amazon rainforest is a biome known for its biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林是一个以生物多样性而闻名的生物群系。| synonyms: ecosystem, habitat, ecoregion | antonyms: N/A | noun

biome: /ˈbaɪoʊm/ | n. | 生物群系 |  A major ecological community, characterized by the dominant forms of plant and animal life and the prevailing climate. | The tundra biome is characterized by permafrost and low-growing vegetation. 冻原生物群系的特征是永久冻土和低矮的植被。| synonyms: community, flora, fauna | antonyms: N/A | noun
