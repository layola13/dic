lengthier:/ˈlɛŋθiər/| adj.|更长的，更冗长的|Comparative form of "lengthy," meaning taking more time or space than necessary or desirable.|The director insisted on a lengthier explanation of the scene. 导演坚持要对场景进行更冗长的解释。|The debate was lengthier than expected. 这场辩论比预期的要长。|近义词：longer, more extensive, protracted|反义词：shorter, briefer, concise|词性: adjective
词源: From "lengthy" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Lengthy" comes from "length" + "-y" (adjective suffix). "Length" ultimately derives from Old English "lengthu."
