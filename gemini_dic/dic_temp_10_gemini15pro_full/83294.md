gynandromorph:/ˌɡaɪˈnændrəˌmɔːrf/ | n. |雌雄嵌合体|An organism, typically an insect, that displays both male and female characteristics in its body.  This is caused by an error in cell division early in development.|Example: The researchers discovered a gynandromorph butterfly with one male wing and one female wing. 研究人员发现了一只雌雄嵌合的蝴蝶，它的一只翅膀是雄性，另一只翅膀是雌性。

Example: Gynandromorphs are valuable for studying sex determination in insects. 雌雄嵌合体对于研究昆虫的性别决定很有价值。|近义词:chimera|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


gynandromorphic:/ˌɡaɪˌnændrəˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. |雌雄嵌合的|Having both male and female characteristics.|Example: The gynandromorphic fly had a male eye on one side and a female eye on the other.  这只雌雄嵌合的苍蝇一侧有一只雄性眼睛，另一侧有一只雌性眼睛。

Example:  Gynandromorphic traits can be seen in various species, not just insects. 雌雄同体的特征不仅在昆虫中，在其他物种中也能看到。|近义词:hermaphroditic (in some contexts), intersex (in some contexts)|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective


Etymology:  From Greek *gynē* ("woman") + *andr-* ("man") + *-morph* ("form").
