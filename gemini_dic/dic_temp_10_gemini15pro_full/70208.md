fibrosis:/faɪˈbroʊsɪs/
n.
纤维化，纤维变性
The formation of excess fibrous connective tissue in an organ or tissue in a reparative or reactive process.  This can be a result of injury, inflammation, or other factors, leading to thickening and scarring.
Lung fibrosis can make it difficult to breathe. 肺纤维化会导致呼吸困难。

近义词: scarring, induration
反义词: resolution, healing
词性: noun


fibrosis:/faɪˈbroʊsɪs/
noun
纤维化
The thickening and scarring of connective tissue, usually as a result of injury.
His liver fibrosis was caused by years of heavy drinking. 他的肝纤维化是由多年的酗酒引起的。

近义词:  scarring, sclerosis
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms related to tissue regeneration or healing)
词性: noun
