chipmunk:/ˈtʃɪpmʌŋk/ | n. |花栗鼠|A small, striped North American squirrel with large cheek pouches.| We saw a chipmunk scampering up a tree. 我们看到一只花栗鼠窜上了一棵树。|  squirrel, ground squirrel | None. | noun
Etymology: From the Ojibwe word *aǧiimwa*  meaning "one who descends headfirst".
