dissectors:/dɪˈsɛktərz/
n.|解剖者；仔细分析者|A person who dissects, especially an animal or plant for scientific examination.  A person who analyzes something in minute detail. |The dissectors carefully examined the frog's nervous system.|近义词：anatomists, analysts, examiners|反义词：synthesizers|词性：名词

dissector:/dɪˈsɛktər/
n.|解剖器；解剖工具|A tool or instrument used for dissecting.|The dissector used a scalpel to make the first incision.|近义词：scalpel, forceps, probe|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
