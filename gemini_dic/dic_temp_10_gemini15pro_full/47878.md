decelerators:/diːˈsɛləˌreɪtərz/
|n.|减速器；减速装置；减速剂|A device or substance used to reduce speed or slow down a process. |The car's decelerators helped it to slow down safely on the steep hill. 汽车的减速器帮助它在陡峭的山坡上安全减速。|synonyms: brakes, retarders, moderators|antonyms: accelerators, boosters|noun
