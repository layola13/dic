curse:/kɜːrs/ (UK), /kɝːs/ (US)| n. vt.|诅咒，咒骂；祸根|A solemn utterance intended to invoke a supernatural power to inflict harm or punishment on someone or something.  The witch put a curse on the prince.  女巫诅咒了王子。|近义词：hex, jinx, malediction|反义词：blessing|词性:名词,动词
curses:/ˈkɜːsɪz/ (UK), /ˈkɝːsɪz/ (US)| n.|诅咒，咒骂 (curse的复数形式)|Multiple solemn utterances intended to invoke a supernatural power to inflict harm or punishment.  He muttered curses under his breath. 他低声咒骂。|近义词：hexes, jinxes, maledictions|反义词：blessings|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *curs, from Proto-Germanic *kursaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (to cry out). Related to the verb "curse."
