constructively:/kənˈstrʌktɪvli/
adv.
建设性地
in a way that produces something useful or helpful.
We need to discuss this issue constructively. 我们需要建设性地讨论这个问题。
She offered some constructive criticism. 她提出了一些建设性的批评。
Synonyms: helpfully, usefully, productively, positively
Antonyms: destructively, negatively, unhelpfully
词性: 副词
