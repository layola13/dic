circumlocutionary: /ˌsɜːrkəmˌloʊkjuˈtɔːri/ /ˌsɜːrkəmləˈkjuːʃəneri/
| adj. | 迂回的，绕圈子的 | Expressing something in a roundabout way; using many words where fewer would do. | He gave a circumlocutionary answer, avoiding the direct question. 他给了一个迂回的答案，避开了直接的问题。 | The politician's circumlocutionary statement left everyone confused about his actual stance.  这位政客的迂回声明让每个人都对他的实际立场感到困惑。 | verbose, prolix, periphrastic | direct, concise, succinct | adjective
