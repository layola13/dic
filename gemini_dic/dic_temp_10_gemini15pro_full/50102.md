dentition:/dɛnˈtɪʃən/
n.
齿列；牙列
The arrangement, type, and number of teeth in a given species at a given age.
The horse's dentition indicated it was a young adult. 这匹马的齿列表明它是一匹年轻的成年马。
synonyms: teeth, set of teeth
antonyms: edentulousness
