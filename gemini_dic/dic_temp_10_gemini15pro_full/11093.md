archaizing:/ˈɑːrkeɪˌaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
模仿古风的，古色古香的
Imitating or suggesting the style of an earlier period, especially in language or art.
Her archaizing diction made the poem sound like it was written centuries ago. 她那古色古香的措辞使这首诗听起来像是几个世纪以前写成的。
archaic, antiquated, obsolete
modern, contemporary
adjective
