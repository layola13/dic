neotectonic:/ˌniːoʊtɛkˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 新构造的 | Relating to or designating tectonic activity or structural deformation of the earth's crust that is relatively recent geologically, typically occurring during the last 2.6 million years (the Quaternary Period). |  The neotectonic activity in the region has shaped the landscape.  该地区的新构造活动塑造了地貌。| Synonyms: recent tectonic, Quaternary tectonic | Antonyms: ancient tectonic, paleotectonic | adjective
