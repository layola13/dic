fricatives:/ˈfrɪkətɪvz/
noun
摩擦音
Consonant sounds produced by forcing air through a narrow channel in the vocal tract, creating friction.
The sounds /f/, /v/, /θ/, /ð/, /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /h/ are fricatives in English.
英语中的/f/、/v/、/θ/、/ð/、/s/、/z/、/ʃ/、/ʒ/、/h/都是摩擦音。
近义词:spirants
反义词:plosives, stops
