adversion:/ədˈvɜːrʒən/
n.
厌恶，反感
A strong feeling of dislike or aversion.
He had a deep adversion to violence. 他对暴力深恶痛绝。

近义词: dislike, aversion, antipathy, repugnance, abhorrence
反义词: liking, fondness, affection, attraction

词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin adversio(n-), from the verb advertere (see advert).
