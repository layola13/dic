gulpingly:/ˈɡʌlpɪŋli/
adverb
adv.
大口地，吞咽地
in a gulping manner; by taking large swallows.
He drank the water gulpingly after his long run.  他长跑后大口地喝水。
She gulpingly ate the last piece of cake. 她狼吞虎咽地吃掉了最后一块蛋糕。
近义词: greedily, ravenously, voraciously
反义词: slowly, delicately, daintily
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "gulp" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Gulp" originates from the Middle Dutch "gulpen," meaning "to guzzle."
