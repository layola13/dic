gradability:/ˌɡreɪdəˈbɪləti/| n.|可分级性，等级性|The quality of an adjective or adverb that allows it to be expressed in different degrees (e.g., comparative and superlative forms).|The gradability of "tall" allows us to say "taller" and "tallest."  高的可分级性让我们可以说“更高”和“最高”。|scalability, variability|ungradability|noun
