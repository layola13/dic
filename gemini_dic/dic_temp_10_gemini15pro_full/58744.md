dunged:/dʌndʒd/
|adj.|
|肮脏的，粪便覆盖的；（尤指）关在地牢里的|
|Covered with dung; (especially) confined in a dungeon.|
|The prisoners were kept in a dark, dunged cell.  囚犯们被关在一个黑暗肮脏的牢房里。|
|The stable was dunged and smelled strongly. 马厩里满是粪便，气味难闻。|
|近义词: filthy, dirty, soiled, manured|
|反义词: clean, pristine, immaculate|
|词性:形容词|


Etymology: From *dung* (noun) + *-ed* (suffix forming adjectives).  *Dung* comes from Old English *dung*, of Germanic origin, related to Dutch *dong* and German *Dung*. The *-ed* suffix is a common way to form adjectives from nouns, indicating a state or condition.
