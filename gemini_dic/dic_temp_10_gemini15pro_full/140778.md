omphalo:/ˈɒmfələʊ/ (UK) /ˈɑːmfəloʊ/ (US)| n.|脐，肚脐，中心点|The omphalo is the point on a surface representing the center of a sphere from which the surface is mapped.|The cartographer marked the omphalo on the map projection.  制图师在地图投影上标出了中心点。|Synonym: navel, center, hub|Antonym: periphery, edge|noun

omphalos:/ˈɒmfələs/ (UK) /ˈɑːmfələs/ (US)| n.|脐，肚脐，中心点|(Variant spelling of omphalo)  omphalos是omphalo的另一种拼写|The ancient Greeks considered Delphi the omphalos of the world. 古希腊人认为德尔菲是世界的中心。|Synonym: navel, center, hub|Antonym: periphery, edge|noun
