illuminates:/ɪˈluːməˌneɪts/
verb
照亮；阐明；启发
makes something clear and easier to understand; shines light on something
The streetlights illuminate the road at night.  路灯在夜间照亮道路。
Her smile illuminated her whole face. 她的笑容照亮了她的整张脸。
light up, brighten, clarify, elucidate
obscure, darken, confuse, obfuscate
词性:动词
