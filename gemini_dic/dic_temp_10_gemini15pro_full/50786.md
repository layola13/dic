descend:/dɪˈsend/ | v. | 下降，下来；遗传；屈尊 | To move from a higher to a lower place, position, or value.  To be inherited through generations. To lower oneself in status or dignity. | The plane began to descend. 飞机开始下降。| synonyms: drop, fall, sink, decline, stoop | antonyms: ascend, rise, climb, scale | verb

descend:/dɪˈsend/ | n. | 下降；斜坡；血统 |  An act of descending; a downward slope; lineage. (Noun form is less common) | The hikers began their descend into the valley.  徒步旅行者开始下降到山谷。| synonyms: descent, decline, slope | antonyms: ascent, rise, climb | noun

PHRASE->
descend on/upon  突然访问；突袭  Relatives descended on us for the holidays.  亲戚们突然来访过节。
descend into  陷入；堕落  The country descended into chaos. 这个国家陷入了混乱。
descend from  起源于；是…的后裔  He is descended from a long line of warriors.  他是武士世家的后裔。
descend to  降低身份去做…；堕落到…  He would never descend to cheating. 他永远不会堕落到作弊的地步。
descend through  通过…流传下来  The story has descended through generations. 这个故事世代相传。
