antirealism:/ˌæntiˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. |反现实主义| The doctrine that there is no objective reality independent of the mind, or that we have no way of knowing such a reality.  It encompasses a variety of philosophical positions, including skepticism, idealism, and some forms of constructivism. |  Many postmodern philosophers subscribe to antirealism, arguing that truth is relative and socially constructed. 许多后现代哲学家赞同反现实主义，认为真理是相对的，是社会建构的。

Antirealism in art often involves dreamlike or fantastical imagery. 艺术中的反现实主义通常包含梦幻般的或奇幻的图像。 | 近义词：irrealism, idealism, skepticism | 反义词：realism |词性: 名词
