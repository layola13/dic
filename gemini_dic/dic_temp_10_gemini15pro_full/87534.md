heterogenetic:/ˌhɛtərəʊdʒɪˈnɛtɪk/
heterogenetic:| adj.|异种发生的，异源的|Originating outside an organism; not originating within the organism; differing in kind or nature.|The scientist studied the heterogenetic origins of the disease.|近义词：exogenous, external, foreign|反义词：endogenous, internal, native|词性:形容词

词源:
From hetero- (different) + genetic (originating).  Ultimately from Greek *héteros* (different) and *génesis* (origin).
