dolly:/ˈdɒli/ (UK) /ˈdɑːli/ (US)| n. |小推车，手推车；玩偶，玩具娃娃；多莉（人名）|A small wheeled platform for moving heavy objects; a child's toy doll, often representing a baby or small child; a female given name, short for Dorothy.|She used a dolly to move the heavy boxes. 她用小推车搬运沉重的箱子。|近义词：handcart, trolley, pushcart (for the wheeled platform); doll, plaything (for the toy); |反义词: (No direct antonym) |名词

dolly:/ˈdɒli/ (UK) /ˈdɑːli/ (US)| v. |移动摄影机，（尤指）用移动式摄影车拍摄|To move a camera, especially on a wheeled platform, during filming.|The camera dollied smoothly along the track. 摄影机沿着轨道平稳地移动。|近义词：track, pan|反义词: (No direct antonym) |动词

词源：Dolly 源于人名 Dorothy 的昵称，后来引申为玩具娃娃的意思。用于指移动摄影车的 dolly 则可能源于早期电影拍摄中使用的一种小型平台车，也可能与 doll 的关联在于这种平台车像玩具一样可以被操控移动。
