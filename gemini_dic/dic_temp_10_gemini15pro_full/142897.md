out-on-a-limb:/ˌaʊt ɒn ə ˈlɪm/ | adj. | 处于危险境地；孤立无援；冒险 | In a risky or precarious position; isolated and unsupported; taking a chance. |  He went out on a limb to defend his friend, even though it could have cost him his job. 他为了维护他的朋友而冒险，即使这可能会让他丢掉工作。

| Example:  She's out on a limb with her radical proposal.  她以其激进的提议让自己处于孤立无援的境地。

| Synonyms: vulnerable, exposed, precarious, risky, in jeopardy | Antonyms: safe, secure, protected, supported | Adjective
