paradelike:/pəˈreɪdˌlaɪk/
adj.
像游行一样的，仪式般的
Resembling a parade; ceremonial and ostentatious.
The wedding procession had a paradelike quality, with the bride and groom waving from a horse-drawn carriage.  婚礼队伍有一种游行般的感觉，新郎新娘坐在马车上挥手致意。
The opening ceremony was very paradelike, with lots of flags and marching bands. 开幕式非常像游行，有很多旗帜和游行乐队。
近义词:ceremonial, formal, ostentatious, pompous
反义词:informal, casual, understated
词性:adjective

词源:
From parade + -like.  由parade(游行) + -like(像...一样的)组成.
