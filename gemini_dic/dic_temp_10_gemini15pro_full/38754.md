condom:/ˈkɒndɒm/ (UK), /ˈkɑːndəm/ (US)| n. |避孕套，安全套|A thin rubber sheath worn on a man's penis during sexual intercourse to prevent pregnancy or the transmission of sexually transmitted diseases.|Example 1:  Always use a condom to protect yourself from STIs.  总是使用安全套来保护自己免受性传播感染。

Example 2: Condoms are available at most pharmacies.  大多数药店都有避孕套出售。|Synonym: prophylactic, rubber, sheath|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but the absence of a condom increases risk.)|noun
