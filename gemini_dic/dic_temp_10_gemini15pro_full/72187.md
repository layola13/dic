floppies:/ˈflɒpiz/ (UK) /ˈflɑːpiz/ (US)| n. |软盘|Flexible disks, typically 3.5 inches in size, used for storing computer data.  These are now largely obsolete.|I found a box of old floppies in the attic. 我在阁楼里发现了一盒旧软盘。|Synonyms: diskettes|Antonyms: hard drives, flash drives|noun

floppy:/ˈflɒpi/ (UK) /ˈflɑːpi/ (US)| adj. |松软的|Soft and flexible; not stiff.|The puppy's ears were floppy. 小狗的耳朵耷拉着。|Synonyms: limp, droopy|Antonyms: stiff, rigid|adjective
