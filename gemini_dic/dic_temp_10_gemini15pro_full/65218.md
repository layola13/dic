etiologically:/ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
病因学地，病原学地
Relating to or concerned with etiology; from a causal point of view.
The disease is etiologically linked to a specific virus. 这种疾病在病因学上与一种特定病毒有关联。
Synonym: causally
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider words like "symptomatically" or "descriptively" to contrast focus)
Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From etiology + -ally.  Etiology itself comes from French étiologie, from Greek aitiologia, from aitia "cause" + -logia "-logy".
