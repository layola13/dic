ineptitude:/ɪˈneptɪtjuːd/
n.
无能；笨拙；不称职
lack of skill or ability.
Her ineptitude as a manager led to the project's failure. 她作为经理的无能导致了项目的失败。

The team's ineptitude was evident in their disastrous performance. 该团队的无能体现在他们灾难性的表现中。
近义词：incompetence, inability, clumsiness, awkwardness
反义词：competence, skill, ability, aptitude
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ineptitudo, from Latin ineptus (“unfit, unsuitable”) + -itudo (“-itude”). Ineptus is from in- (“not”) + aptus (“fit, suitable”).
