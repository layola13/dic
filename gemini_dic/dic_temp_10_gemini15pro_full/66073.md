excambie:/ɛksˈkæmbi/ | v. | exchange, barter | To give something in return for something else; to trade.  Derived from Old French "escambier," from Late Latin "excambiare," from Latin "ex-" (out) + "cambiare" (to change).|  He excambied his old bicycle for a new one. 他用旧自行车换了一辆新的。| Synonyms: trade, barter, swap, exchange | Antonyms: keep, retain, hoard | verb
