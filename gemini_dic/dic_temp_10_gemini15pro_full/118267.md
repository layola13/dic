merchantable:/ˈmɜːrtʃəntəbl/ /ˈmɜːrʃəntəbl/| adj.|适于销售的；商品化的；可销售的|Fit for sale; of sufficient quality to be bought and sold.  Suitable for market; of commercially acceptable quality.|The fruit was small but merchantable. 这种水果个头小，但可以销售。|Synonyms: marketable, saleable, tradable|Antonyms: unmarketable, unsaleable|adjective
