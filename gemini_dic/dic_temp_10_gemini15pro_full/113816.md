madame:/ˈmædəm/ (US), /məˈdɑːm/ (UK)| n.|女士，夫人|A polite term of address for a woman, especially one in a position of authority or respect.  Originally a French title equivalent to "my lady."|例句：Madame Curie was a brilliant scientist. 居里夫人是一位杰出的科学家。

例句：The letter was addressed to "Madame President." 这封信是寄给“总统夫人”的。|近义词：Mrs., lady, ma'am|反义词:sir, mister|词性:noun
