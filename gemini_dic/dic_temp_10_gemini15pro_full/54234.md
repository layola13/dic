disgallant:/dɪsˈɡælənt/
adjective
缺乏绅士风度的，失礼的，无礼的
Lacking in courtesy and consideration, especially to women; ungentlemanly.
His disgallant behavior towards his date earned him a sharp rebuke. 他对约会对象的失礼行为招致了严厉的斥责。
近义词:rude, impolite, ungentlemanly, discourteous, boorish
反义词:gallant, courteous, polite, respectful, chivalrous
词性:adjective
