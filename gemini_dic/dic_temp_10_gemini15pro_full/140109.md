off-piste:/ˌɒf ˈpiːst/ /ˌɔːf ˈpiːst/ | adj. adv. | 野外滑雪的，非雪道的；偏离常规的 | Skiing or snowboarding on areas of a mountain outside of marked or designated trails.  Figuratively, it means unconventional or outside the established norms. |  He enjoys the challenge of off-piste skiing. 他喜欢野外滑雪的挑战。 | Synonyms: backcountry, ungroomed, unorthodox, unconventional | Antonyms: on-piste, groomed, conventional, orthodox | adjective, adverb

off-piste:/ˌɒf ˈpiːst/ /ˌɔːf ˈpiːst/ | n. | 野外滑雪区域 | The area of a mountain outside of marked or designated trails used for skiing or snowboarding. | The off-piste offered fresh powder and breathtaking views. 野外滑雪区提供了新鲜的粉雪和令人叹为观止的景色。| Synonyms: backcountry | Antonyms: piste, groomed slopes | noun
