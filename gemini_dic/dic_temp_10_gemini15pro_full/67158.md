expositionary:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃəneri/
adjective
说明性的，解释性的，阐述的
Serving to expound or set forth; explanatory.
The professor delivered an expositionary lecture on the history of philosophy. 教授做了一场关于哲学史的解释性讲座。
explanatory, expository, illustrative, elucidative
argumentative, persuasive, narrative
adjective
