culvertail: /ˈkʌlvərˌteɪl/ (US) /ˈkʌlvəteɪl/ (UK) | n. v. | 鸠尾榫，鸠尾接合；用鸠尾榫接合 | A type of joint used in woodworking and other construction, where two pieces are interlocked by a series of trapezoidal projections (tails) and recesses (pins).  To join using this method. |  The drawers of the antique dresser were assembled with dovetail joints.  这个古董梳妆台的抽屉是用鸠尾榫组装的。  The carpenter expertly culvertailed the two boards together. 木匠熟练地用鸠尾榫将两块板连接在一起。 | 近义词: dovetail | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "butt joint" as a contrasting joint type) | 词性: noun, verb


culvertail: /ˈkʌlvərˌteɪl/ (US) /ˈkʌlvəteɪl/ (UK)| adj. | 鸠尾形的 | Shaped like a dovetail or using a dovetail joint. |  The culvertail shape of the joint made it incredibly strong.  这个接头的鸠尾形状使它非常坚固。 | 近义词: dovetailed | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective
