nogging:/ˈnɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n.|水平支撑；填充木条；填充材料；(俚语)唠叨|Horizontal timbers fixed between studs or joists to give support or to prevent draughts.  Informally, nagging or persistent complaining.|The builder used nogging between the joists to strengthen the floor.  建造者在托梁之间使用了填充木条来加固地板。  Stop your nogging – I've heard enough!  别再唠叨了 – 我已经听够了！|Synonyms: filling, packing, stuffing, (informal) nagging, carping|Antonyms:  (For the building sense:)  emptiness, void  (For the informal sense:) praise, encouragement|noun

nogging:/ˈnɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| v.|用水平支撑加固；唠叨|To fix horizontal timbers between studs or joists; to nag persistently.|He nogged the wall to make it more stable. 他用水平支撑加固了墙壁，使其更加稳固。  She's been nogging him all day about cleaning the garage. 她整天都在唠叨他清理车库的事。|Synonyms:  reinforce, brace, support, (informal) pester, badger|Antonyms: weaken, undermine, (informal) encourage, praise |verb
