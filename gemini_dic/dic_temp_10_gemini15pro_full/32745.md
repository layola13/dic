chloroplastic:/ˌklɔːrəˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 叶绿体的；含叶绿素的 | Relating to chloroplasts, organelles found in plant cells and other organisms that conduct photosynthesis. | Chloroplastic DNA is inherited maternally in most plants.  叶绿体DNA在大多数植物中是母系遗传的。

The chloroplastic structure is crucial for photosynthesis. 叶绿体结构对光合作用至关重要。| 近义词: chlorophyllous | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective
