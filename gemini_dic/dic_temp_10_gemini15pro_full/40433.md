contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntreri/ (US)| adj. n. adv.|相反的，对立的；相反的事物（意见等）；反对地|Contrary to popular belief, cats can be very affectionate. 与普遍的看法相反，猫实际上很 affectionate. |The contrary view is that pollution is not a serious problem. 相反的观点是污染不是一个严重的问题.|近义词:opposite, conflicting, adverse, opposed|反义词:similar, alike, conforming, agreeable|词性:形容词, 名词, 副词

contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntreri/ (US)| n.|相反的事物（意见等）|He always does the contrary of what I tell him. 他总是做与我告诉他的相反的事情.|Her actions were contrary to her words. 她的行为与她的言语相悖.|近义词:opposite, reverse|反义词:same, identical|词性:名词


contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntreri/ (US)| adv.|反对地|He acted contrary to his own best interests. 他的行为违背了他自己的最大利益.|The wind was blowing contrary to the direction of the ship. 风向与船的航向相反.|近义词:against, opposite to|反义词:with, along with|词性:副词


USAGE->
词源：来自拉丁语 contrarius，意为“相反的”，源自 contra “反对”。
