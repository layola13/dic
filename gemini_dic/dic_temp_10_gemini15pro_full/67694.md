exulcerate: /ɛkˈsʌlsəreɪt/
verb
使溃烂；使恶化；激怒
To cause or make worse an ulcer.  To make (a bad situation or negative feeling) worse. To irritate or annoy greatly.
The constant criticism exulcerated his already fragile self-esteem.  不断的批评使他原本脆弱的自尊心更加恶化。
His unkind words exulcerated the already tense situation. 他不友善的话使原本紧张的局势更加恶化。
synonym: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate, inflame, irritate
antonym: soothe, heal, alleviate, ameliorate
词性: 动词
