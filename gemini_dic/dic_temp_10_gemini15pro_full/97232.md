inflectedness:/ɪnˈflɛktɪdnəs/ | n. | 曲折性，词形变化性 | The degree to which a word is inflected; the number and variety of forms a word can take. |  The inflectedness of Latin verbs is much greater than that of English verbs. 拉丁语动词的词形变化性比英语动词大得多。| inflection, variability | invariability | noun
