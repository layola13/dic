cutworm:/ˈkʌtwɜːrm/| n.|切根虫|The larvae of certain moths that live in the soil and feed on the stems of young plants at or near ground level, often severing them.|例句：Cutworms destroyed her tomato seedlings. 切根虫毁掉了她的番茄幼苗。

例句：The farmer used pesticide to control the cutworms in his field. 农民使用杀虫剂来控制田里的切根虫。|近义词：pest, grub|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps beneficial insect)|词性:noun
