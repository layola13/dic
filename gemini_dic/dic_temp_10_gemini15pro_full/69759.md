feriae:/ˈfɛri.iː/ /ˈfɪəri.iː/
noun
假日，节日 (尤指古罗马的)
holidays, especially in ancient Rome.
The Roman calendar included numerous feriae, days set aside for religious observances and public festivals.  罗马历法包含许多节日，这些日子专门用于宗教仪式和公共节日。
During feriae, businesses were closed and public games were often held. 在节日期间，商店关门，经常举行公共比赛。
近义词: holidays, festivals, celebrations
反义词: workdays
词性: noun
