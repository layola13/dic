arachnida:/əˈræk.nɪ.də/ | n. |蛛形纲|A class of chelicerate arthropods that includes spiders, scorpions, mites, and ticks. They are characterized by having eight legs, two body sections (cephalothorax and abdomen), and no antennae.|例句：Spiders, scorpions, and mites belong to the class Arachnida. 蜘蛛、蝎子和螨都属于蛛形纲。

例句：Arachnida are predominantly terrestrial arthropods. 蛛形纲动物主要是陆生节肢动物。|近义词：arachnid|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
