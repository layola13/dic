cultishness:/ˈkʌltɪʃnəs/
n.
狂热；膜拜；邪教性质
Excessive or exaggerated devotion to a person, group, or idea, resembling the devotion characteristic of a cult.
The cultishness surrounding the new tech CEO was evident in the way his employees hung on his every word.  这位新科技公司首席执行官周围的狂热氛围显现在他的员工对他所说的一切都奉若圭臬。

The band's cultishness has grown over the years, with fans traveling from all over the world to see them perform.  多年来，这支乐队的狂热追随者越来越多，粉丝们从世界各地赶来观看他们的演出。
near synonym: fanaticism, zealotry, obsession
near antonym: apathy, indifference, detachment
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
cult + -ish + -ness

cult (n.):  From Latin cultus ("worship, reverence"), past participle of colere ("to cultivate, till, inhabit, protect, worship").

-ish (suffix):  Forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like," "having the quality of," or "belonging to."

-ness (suffix):  Forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
