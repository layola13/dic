blinged:/blɪŋd/ | v. | 镶满珠宝，闪亮装饰 | To adorn with bling; to be covered in sparkly, showy jewelry. | She blinged out her phone case with rhinestones. 她用莱茵石装饰了她的手机壳。| The rapper blinged his teeth with diamonds.  这位说唱歌手用钻石装饰了他的牙齿。| 近义词： adorned, decorated, embellished, bejeweled | 反义词： plain, simple, undecorated | 词性: 动词

blinged:/blɪŋd/ | adj. | 镶满珠宝的，闪亮的 | Covered in bling; adorned with sparkly, showy jewelry. | He wore a blinged-out watch. 他戴着一块镶满珠宝的手表。 | She had a blinged-up car. 她有一辆闪亮的汽车。 | 近义词： sparkly, flashy, ostentatious | 反义词： plain, simple, understated | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Bling" itself likely originated as an onomatopoeia, mimicking the sound of light reflecting off shiny objects like jewelry. "Blinged" is simply the past participle form of the verb "to bling," which emerged after "bling" became popularized in hip-hop culture in the 1990s.
