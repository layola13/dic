miocene:/ˈmaɪəˌsiːn/ | adj. n.| 中新世的，中新世 |  Relating to or denoting the epoch of geological time following the Oligocene and preceding the Pliocene, or the system of rocks deposited during it.  | The miocene epoch lasted from about 23 to 5.3 million years ago. 中新世距今约2300万年到530万年。| Synonyms:  Tertiary period (more broad) | Antonyms:  modern, recent | adjective, noun
