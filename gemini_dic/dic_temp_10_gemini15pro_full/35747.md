cochineal:/ˈkɒtʃɪniːl/ /ˈkoʊtʃɪniːl/| n.|胭脂虫；胭脂红|A scale insect (Dactylopius coccus) native to Mexico and Central America that feeds on prickly pear cacti.  The dried bodies of the females are used to produce a crimson dye.|例句：Cochineal insects are used to produce a natural red dye. 胭脂虫被用来生产一种天然的红色染料。

例句：The vibrant red color of the textile was achieved using cochineal. 这块纺织品鲜艳的红色是用胭脂虫染成的。|近义词：carmine, crimson|反义词:|noun
