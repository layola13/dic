nutritiousness:/njuˈtrɪʃəsnəs/
n.
营养价值；滋养度
The nutritiousness of a food depends on its vitamin and mineral content.
一种食物的营养价值取决于其维生素和矿物质含量。
nutritional value, nourishment
malnutrition, unhealthiness
noun
