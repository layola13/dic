epicycloidal:/ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɔɪdəl/
adjective
外摆线的; 圆外旋轮线的
Relating to or resembling an epicycloid, a curve traced by a point on a circle that rolls around the outside of a fixed circle.
The gears in this clock use an epicycloidal profile for smoother operation.  这个钟的齿轮使用外摆线轮廓来实现更平滑的运转。
The design incorporated intricate epicycloidal patterns.  这个设计包含了复杂的外摆线图案。
近义词: trochoidal
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps hypocycloidal if discussing gear profiles)
词性: adjective
