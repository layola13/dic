acidify:/əˈsɪdɪfaɪ/| vt.|酸化，使变酸|To make something more acidic by increasing the concentration of hydrogen ions.|Pollution can acidify lakes and streams. 污染会使湖泊和溪流酸化。

The burning of fossil fuels acidifies the oceans. 燃烧化石燃料会使海洋酸化。|近义词：sour, acidulate|反义词:alkalize, neutralize|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acidifier*, from acide (“acid”) + -fier (suffix forming verbs meaning “to make” or “to become”). Ultimately from Latin acidus (“sour, sharp, tart”)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Acid rain acidifies the soil. = 酸雨使土壤酸化。
Industrial emissions acidify the air. = 工业排放使空气酸化。

一般过去时:
The spill acidified the river. = 泄漏物使河流酸化。
The chemical reaction acidified the solution. = 化学反应使溶液酸化。

现在完成时:
The increasing levels of carbon dioxide have acidified the ocean. =  二氧化碳含量的增加已经使海洋酸化。
Human activities have acidified many lakes. = 人类活动已经使许多湖泊酸化。

过去完成时:
The rain had acidified the lake before the fish died. = 在鱼死亡之前，雨水已经使湖泊酸化。
The factory's emissions had acidified the surrounding area for years. = 工厂的排放物多年来已经使周围地区酸化。

将来时:
Continued pollution will acidify the groundwater. = 持续的污染会使地下水酸化。
Climate change will further acidify the oceans. = 气候变化将进一步使海洋酸化。


被动语态:
The lake was acidified by the rain. = 湖水被雨水酸化了。
The soil is being acidified by industrial pollutants. = 土壤正被工业污染物酸化。
