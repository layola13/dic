hypoxaemia: /ˌhaɪpɒkˈsiːmiə/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpɑːkˈsiːmiə/ (US)| n. |低氧血症|A condition in which there is an abnormally low concentration of oxygen in the blood.|The climber suffered from hypoxaemia at high altitude.|近义词: hypoxemia (US spelling), anoxemia|反义词: hyperoxemia|词性: noun

hypoxia: /haɪˈpɒksiə/ (UK) /haɪˈpɑːksiə/ (US)| n. |组织缺氧|Deficiency in the amount of oxygen reaching the tissues.|The stroke caused hypoxia in parts of the brain.|近义词: anoxia|反义词: hyperoxia|词性: noun
