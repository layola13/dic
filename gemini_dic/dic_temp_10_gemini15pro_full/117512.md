meeknesses:/ˈmiːknəsɪz/
n.
温顺，谦逊，柔顺
The quality of being meek; submissiveness or humility.
Her meeknesses made her an easy target for bullies. 她的温顺使她很容易成为被欺负的对象。
The pastor praised the meeknesses of the congregation. 牧师称赞会众的谦逊。
近义词: humility, gentleness, submissiveness, docility
反义词: arrogance, aggressiveness, boldness, assertiveness
词性: 名词(plural)

meekness:/ˈmiːknəs/
n.
温顺，谦逊，柔顺
The quality of being meek; submissiveness or humility.
He accepted the criticism with meekness. 他谦逊地接受了批评。
Her meekness is often mistaken for weakness. 她的温顺常被误认为是软弱。
近义词: humility, gentleness, submissiveness, docility
反义词: arrogance, aggressiveness, boldness, assertiveness
词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *meknesse, from Old English *mēcness, from mēc (“meek”) + -ness. Cognate with Scots meiknes (“meekness”), West Frisian makens (“meekness”), Dutch meekheid (“meekness”), German Meekheit (“meekness”).
