diabolify: /diːˈæbəlaɪfaɪ/ /daɪˈæbəlaɪfaɪ/
verb
中文翻译：妖魔化；将…描绘成魔鬼；诽谤；中伤
英文解析：To represent as diabolically evil; to portray as wicked or depraved.  To vilify; slander.
例句：The media diabolified the candidate, portraying him as a dangerous extremist. 媒体将这位候选人妖魔化，把他描绘成一个危险的极端分子。

例句：Opponents of the new law diabolified it as an attack on personal freedom.  新法律的反对者将其妖魔化，称其是对个人自由的攻击。

近义词：vilify, demonize, slander, denigrate, calumniate, defame
反义词：deify, praise, extol, laud
词性：v.
