geothermal:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈθɜːrməl/| adj.|地热的|Of, relating to, or produced by the internal heat of the earth.|Iceland utilizes geothermal energy for heating and electricity. 冰岛利用地热能源供暖和发电。|近义词：volcanic, earthly|反义词:none|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- (earth) + -thermal (heat).  Ultimately from Greek gē (earth) and thermē (heat).
