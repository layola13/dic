etosha:/ɪˈtoʊʃə/ | n. |埃托沙|A large national park in northwestern Namibia, known for its abundant wildlife and salt pan. |We saw many elephants and lions in Etosha National Park. 我们在埃托沙国家公园看到了许多大象和狮子。|Synonyms: game reserve, wildlife sanctuary|Antonyms: urban area, city|noun

etosha:/ɪˈtoʊʃə/ | n. |埃托沙盐沼/埃托沙盆地|A large, endorheic salt pan, forming part of the Etosha National Park in Namibia. |The Etosha pan stretches for miles, a shimmering white expanse. 埃托沙盐沼绵延数英里，一片闪闪发光的白色广阔区域。|Synonyms: salt flat, pan|Antonyms: lake, ocean|noun
