extrapunitively:/ˌekstrəˈpʌnɪtɪvli/| adv.|外罚性地；向外攻击地|Directing blame or aggression outward, towards others or the environment, rather than inwards towards oneself.|Example: He reacted extrapunitively, blaming his colleagues for the project's failure. 他以向外攻击的方式做出反应，将项目的失败归咎于他的同事。

Example:  Extrapunitively, she lashed out at the customer service representative, even though the issue wasn't their fault. 她以向外攻击的方式斥责了客服代表，即使问题不是他们的错。|近义词：aggressively, outwardly, blaming others|反义词：intropunitively, self-blaming|词性: 副词
