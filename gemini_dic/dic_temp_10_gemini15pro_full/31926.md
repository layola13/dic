cheilopoda:/kaɪˈlɒpədə/ | n. | 唇足纲 | A class of arthropods, including centipedes and millipedes, characterized by numerous pairs of legs.  | Centipedes belong to the class Chilopoda. 蜈蚣属于唇足纲。 | synonyms: Myriapoda (though this includes millipedes which are now in a separate class Diplopoda), centipedes | antonyms: None (as it is a taxonomic classification) | noun
