drainages:/ˈdreɪnɪdʒɪz/
n.
排水系统，排水，排水量
the process of removing water or other liquids from an area.  The amount of water or other liquid that drains from an area. Systems of drains.
The drainages in the city were overwhelmed by the heavy rain. 城市的排水系统被暴雨淹没了。
The farmer improved the drainages of his fields.  这位农民改善了田地的排水系统。
sewerage, effluent, runoff
intake, absorption
noun
