displacement's:/dɪsˈpleɪsmənts/ | n. | 位移；取代；排水量 | The displacement of the native population caused widespread unrest.  土著居民的被迫迁徙造成了 widespread 的动荡。| 例句：The ship's displacement is 10,000 tons. 这艘船的排水量是10000吨。 | 近义词：shift, removal, substitution | 反义词：placement, insertion | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *displaciamentum, from displaciare "displease," from dis- "not" + placere "to please."  The sense shifted from "displeasure" to "removal from a place."  The meaning related to volume comes from the volume of water displaced by a floating object.
