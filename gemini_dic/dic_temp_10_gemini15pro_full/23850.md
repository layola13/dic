breadlessnesses:/ˈbrɛdləsnɪsɪz/
noun
无面包的状态；缺面包的状况
The state of being without bread; a lack of bread.
The breadlessnesses of the Great Depression were devastating for many families.  大萧条时期的缺粮对许多家庭来说是毁灭性的。
The prolonged drought led to widespread breadlessnesses throughout the region. 长期干旱导致整个地区普遍缺粮。
近义词: scarcity of bread, lack of bread, food shortage, famine
反义词: abundance of bread, plenty of bread
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
bread (from Old English *brēad) + -less (suffix indicating absence) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives) + -es (plural suffix)
