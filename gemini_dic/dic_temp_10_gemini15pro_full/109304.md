lendable:/ˈlendəbl/
adjective|可借出的，可贷的|Suitable or available for lending.|例句：Are your notes lendable? 你的笔记可以借吗？|近义词：loanable, available for loan|反义词：unlendable, non-lendable|词性：形容词

Etymology: From "lend" + "-able" (suffix denoting capability or suitability).  "Lend" comes from Old English "lænan,"  meaning "to grant the temporary use of."
