favoredly:/ˈfeɪvərəbli/
adverb
有利地，赞许地
in a way that shows approval or support; favorably.
The proposal was favoredly received by the committee. 委员会对这个提议表示赞同。
His work was reviewed favoredly by critics.  评论家们对他的作品给予了好评。
近义词:favorably, approvingly, positively
反义词:unfavorably, negatively, disapprovingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From favor (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives from nouns) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin "favere" (to favor).
