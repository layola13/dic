nimbly:/ˈnɪmbli/| adv.|敏捷地，灵活地|Moving quickly and lightly.|The dancer moved nimbly across the stage.  舞者在舞台上轻盈地移动。|The squirrel nimbly scampered up the tree. 松鼠敏捷地爬上了树。|近义词：quickly, swiftly, deftly, adroitly, agilely|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, slowly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nimbely*, from Old English *numol* (“capable of catching, quick to grasp, readily moving, swift”) + *-līce* (suffix forming adverbs).  The Old English word is related to Dutch *nemen* (“to take”), German *nehmen* (“to take”), and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nemaną* (“to take”).
