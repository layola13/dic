cystoid:/ˈsɪstɔɪd/| adj.|囊样的，囊状的|Resembling a cyst; cystic.  Having the form of a cyst.|例句：Cystoid macular edema is a common complication after cataract surgery.  白内障手术后囊样黄斑水肿是一种常见并发症。

cystoid:/ˈsɪstɔɪd/| n.|囊状体|A cyst-like structure.|例句：The biopsy revealed a small cystoid in the tissue sample. 活检显示组织样本中有一个小囊状体。
