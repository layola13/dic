eyghen:/ˈaɪən/| archaic pronoun, adjective|自己的，拥有|Archaic form of "own".  Originally a possessive pronoun meaning "one's own," it could also function adjectivally.  It evolved from Old English *āgen.|He hath made me eyghen lord of all.| 近义词:own, proper, personal|反义词:another's, alien|pronoun, adjective
