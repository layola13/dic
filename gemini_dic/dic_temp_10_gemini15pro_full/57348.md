dowryless: /ˈdaʊri.ləs/ | adj. | 没有嫁妆的 | Without a dowry (money or property brought by a bride to her husband on their marriage). |  She came from a poor family and was dowryless. 她来自一个贫穷的家庭，没有嫁妆。 |  Example: In some cultures, being dowryless can make it difficult for a woman to find a husband. 在某些文化中，没有嫁妆的女性很难找到丈夫。 | 近义词: portionless, dowerless | 反义词: dowered | 词性: 形容词
