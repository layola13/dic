aam:/ɑːm/ | n. | 印度芒果 | Indian mango; a mango, especially one grown in India. | We enjoyed fresh aam during our trip to India. 我们在印度旅行期间享用了新鲜的芒果。 | The vendor sold a variety of aams at the market.  小贩在市场上出售各种各样的芒果。 | synonyms: mango | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi आम (ām), from Sanskrit आम्र (āmra). Ultimately from Proto-Dravidian *māṅkay.

USAGE->
The aam is a delicious fruit. = 芒果是一种美味的水果。

Aam lassi is a popular drink in India. = 芒果酸奶是印度的流行饮品。
