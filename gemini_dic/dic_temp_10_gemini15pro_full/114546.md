malaria:/məˈlɛəriə/ | n. |疟疾|An infectious disease characterized by cycles of chills, fever, and sweating, caused by a parasite transmitted by the bite of an infected mosquito.|She contracted malaria while traveling in Africa. 她去非洲旅行时感染了疟疾。|Synonyms: ague, paludism|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun


malaria:/məˈlɛəriə/ | n. |瘴气|Noxious air or exhalations formerly believed to cause disease, especially malaria.|The swamp gave off a malaria that made them sick. 沼泽散发出瘴气，使他们生病。|Synonyms: miasma, bad air|Antonyms: fresh air, pure air|noun
