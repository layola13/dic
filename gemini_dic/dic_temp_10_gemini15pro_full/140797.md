on-and-on:/ˌɒn ən ˈɒn/ (UK) /ˌɑːn ən ˈɑːn/ (US)| adv.|持续不断地，不停地|Continuing without interruption or pause.  It describes something that happens repeatedly or for a long time without stopping.|He talked on and on about his trip. 他滔滔不绝地讲他的旅行。|continuously, incessantly, endlessly|briefly, shortly|adverb
