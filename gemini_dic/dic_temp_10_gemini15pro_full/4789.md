algid:/ˈældʒɪd/ | adj. | 寒冷的；冰冷的；像死人一样的 | Characterized by coldness, especially of the skin; chilly.  Relating to or resembling the chill of death. |  His skin felt algid, a sign of the worsening infection. 他的皮肤感觉冰冷，这是感染恶化的迹象。

His face was algid and lifeless. 他的脸冰冷毫无生气。 | 近义词: cold, chilly, frigid, icy, gelid | 反义词: warm, hot | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *algidus*, from algēre "to be cold."
