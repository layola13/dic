biasedly: /ˈbaɪəsɪdli/
| adv. | 有偏见地，偏袒地 | In a way that shows bias or prejudice; unfairly.  Favoring one side or viewpoint over others. | The judge was accused of acting biasedly in the case.  法官被指责在此案中带有偏见地行事。| The media reported biasedly on the election, favoring one candidate over the other. 媒体对选举进行了偏颇的报道，偏袒一位候选人。 |  partially, unfairly, prejudicially | impartially, fairly, objectively | adverb
