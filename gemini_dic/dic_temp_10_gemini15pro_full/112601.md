lovingnesses:/ˈlʌvɪŋnəsɪz/
noun
爱的表达;慈爱;深情
The state or quality of being loving, or an instance of this.  Expressing affection, kindness, or great fondness.
She showered her children with lovingnesses. 她对孩子们充满了慈爱。
Synonyms: affection, tenderness, fondness, warmth, kindness, devotion, care, attachment
Antonyms: coldness, indifference, hostility, hatred, animosity
词性: 名词
