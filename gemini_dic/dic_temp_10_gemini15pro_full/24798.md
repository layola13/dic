brotherliness:/ˈbrʌðərlɪnɪs/
n.
兄弟情谊，友爱
The quality or state of being brotherly; brotherly affection.
He showed great brotherliness towards his younger siblings, always looking out for them. 他对弟弟妹妹表现出极大的兄弟情谊，总是照顾他们。
brotherhood, fraternity, camaraderie, friendship
enmity, hostility, animosity
