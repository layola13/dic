liguliflorous:/ˌlɪɡjʊlɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
舌状花冠的
Having ligulate florets; composed of ligulate florets.  A liguliflorous plant has flowers made up of strap-shaped petals, like those of a dandelion.
例句：Dandelions are liguliflorous plants. 蒲公英是舌状花冠植物。

例句：The daisy, a liguliflorous flower, brightened the meadow.  雏菊，一种舌状花冠的花，使草地更加明亮。

近义词：composite, asteraceous
反义词：(No common direct antonym; one could describe flowers with other structures, e.g., tubular, cruciform)
词性: adjective
