noninformational:/ˌnɒnɪnfərˈmeɪʃənl/ | adj. | 非信息的，不提供信息的 | Not conveying information; not informative.  |  This noninformational message was a waste of time. 这条不提供任何信息的短信浪费了时间。 | The noninformational lecture left the students confused.  这堂没有信息的讲座让学生们感到困惑。 | 近义词: uninformative, meaningless, pointless | 反义词: informative, meaningful, useful | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + informational (adjective derived from "information")
