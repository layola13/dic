furnus:/ˈfɜːrnəs/| n.|熔炉，火炉|A structure or enclosure in which a very high temperature can be generated, for example for smelting metals or firing pottery.|The blacksmith worked diligently at his furnus, shaping the glowing metal. 铁匠在他的熔炉旁辛勤工作，塑造着灼热的金属。|Synonyms: oven, kiln, forge, furnace|Antonyms: refrigerator, freezer|noun
