commemorating: /kəˈmɛməreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
纪念的，庆祝的
serving to recall and honor the memory of someone or something
例句：The statue was erected commemorating the soldiers who died in the war. 这座雕像是为了纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵而建造的。

例句：The ceremony commemorating the 50th anniversary of the company was a grand affair.  庆祝公司成立50周年的仪式非常隆重。

近义词：celebrating, honoring, memorializing
反义词：forgetting, ignoring
词性：adjective

commemorate: /kəˈmɛməreɪt/
verb
纪念，庆祝
recall and show respect for (someone or something)
例句：A ceremony to commemorate the victims of the disaster. 一个纪念灾难受害者的仪式。

例句：They commemorated their victory with a parade. 他们举行游行庆祝胜利。

近义词：celebrate, honor, memorialize
反义词：forget, ignore
词性：verb

commemoration: /kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən/
noun
纪念，庆祝
the act of remembering or marking a significant event
例句：The commemoration of the centenary of the composer's birth.  纪念这位作曲家诞辰一百周年。

例句：The annual commemoration of the town's founding.  小镇每年一度的建镇纪念活动。

近义词：celebration, observance, remembrance
反义词：oblivion
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemoratio (“remembrance”), from Latin commemorare (“call to mind, recount, relate”), from com- (“together”) + memorare (“to bring to remembrance”), from memor (“mindful”).
