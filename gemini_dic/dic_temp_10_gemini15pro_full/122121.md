mistranscribe:/ˌmɪstrænˈskraɪb/ | v. | 抄写错误，誊写错误 | To write or type something incorrectly, usually by changing some of the letters or words. | She mistranscribed the meeting notes and the date was wrong. 她抄写会议记录时抄错了，日期写错了。| He mistranscribed the doctor's instructions, leading to a dosage error. 他抄错了医生的指示，导致用药剂量错误。 | 近义词: miscopy, misspell, err | 反义词: transcribe accurately, copy correctly | 词性: 动词
