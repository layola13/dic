accolading: /ˈækəˌleɪdɪŋ/
noun
赞扬，表彰；荣誉；奖励
an expression of praise or admiration; an award
The film received accolades from critics and audiences alike.  这部电影获得了评论家和观众的一致好评。

He received many accolades for his contributions to the field of medicine. 他因对医学领域的贡献而获得了很多赞誉。

近义词: praise, commendation, tribute, honor, award, recognition
反义词: criticism, condemnation, censure
词性: noun
