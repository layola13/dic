multitracking:/ˌmʌltiˈtrækɪŋ/ | n. | 多轨录音 | The technique of recording different instruments or vocals on separate tracks of a recording medium, which can then be mixed together. | The band used multitracking to create a rich, layered sound. 乐队使用多轨录音来创造丰富多层次的声音。 |  Multitracking allows for greater flexibility in the mixing process. 多轨录音在混音过程中提供了更大的灵活性。 | 近义词: overdubbing | 反义词: live recording | 词性: noun
