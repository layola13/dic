hooping:/ˈhuːpɪŋ/ | n. | 呼啦圈运动；旋转；箍状物 | The act of playing with a hoop, especially a Hula Hoop. Also refers to the sound made by something spinning or rotating rapidly. | She's really good at hooping. 她玩呼啦圈很厉害。| The airplane made a loud hooping sound as it went into a dive. 飞机俯冲时发出巨大的呼呼声。 | synonyms:  gyrating, whirling, spinning, rotating | antonyms: stillness, immobility | noun

hooping:/ˈhuːpɪŋ/ | adj. | 巨大的；非常的（非正式） |  (Informal) Extremely large or great; whopping. |  We had a hooping good time at the party. 我们在派对上玩得非常开心。 | synonyms: enormous, huge, massive, gigantic, whopping | antonyms: tiny, small, minuscule | adjective
