overmystify: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪstɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 神秘化；过度神秘化；使过于神秘 | To make something unnecessarily mysterious or complicated; to obscure or confuse by making something seem more complex than it is. | The author overmystified the plot, making it difficult to follow.  作者把情节弄得过于神秘，让人难以理解。 |  The government overmystified the new policy, leading to widespread confusion. 政府把新政策弄得神乎其神，导致了广泛的困惑。 | 近义词: obfuscate, confuse, complicate | 反义词: clarify, simplify, explain | 词性: 动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "mystify" (from "mystery" + "-fy," meaning "to make mysterious")
