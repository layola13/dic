acutilobate: /əˌkjuːtiˈloʊbeɪt/
adjective
尖裂的
Having lobes with pointed ends or tips.  Specifically used in botany to describe leaf shapes.
The acutilobate leaves of the fig tree are distinctive.  无花果树尖裂的叶子很特别。
The botanist identified the plant by its acutilobate leaves.  植物学家通过其尖裂的叶子识别了这种植物。
近义词: sharp-lobed, pointed-lobed
反义词: obtuselobate (钝裂的)
词性: adjective
词源: Latin *acutus* (sharp, pointed) + *lobatus* (lobed)
