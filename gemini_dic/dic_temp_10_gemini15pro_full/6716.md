analogize:/əˈnælədʒaɪz/
verb
作类比，比拟
To explain or clarify something by comparing it to something else.
He analogized the human brain to a computer. 他把人脑比作电脑。

analogize:/əˈnælədʒaɪz/
verb
作类比，比拟
To draw an analogy; to make a comparison between two things to show similarity.
She analogized her experience to climbing a mountain. 她把她的经历比作爬山。


近义词:compare, liken, equate, correlate
反义词:differentiate, distinguish
词性:verb
