amyelous: /eɪˈmaɪələs/
| adj. | 无髓的；无脊髓的 | Lacking a spinal cord or bone marrow; lacking myelin. |  The amyelous fetus was not viable.  这个无脊髓的胎儿不能存活。 | Example:  Amyelous nerve fibers conduct impulses more slowly. 无髓神经纤维传导冲动更慢。| Synonyms: nonmedullated, unmyelinated | Antonyms: myelinated, medullated | adjective
