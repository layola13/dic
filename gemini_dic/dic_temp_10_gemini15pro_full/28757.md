carnification:/ˌkɑːrnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
肉化，肉样变
The process by which tissue becomes firm and fleshy, resembling muscle.
The lung showed carnification due to pneumonia. 肺炎导致肺部肉样变。
