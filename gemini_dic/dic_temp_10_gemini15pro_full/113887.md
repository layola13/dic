madrasah:/məˈdræsə/ /ˈmædrəsə/| n. |伊斯兰学校，经学院|A Muslim school or college, especially one attached to a mosque.|例句：He studied at a madrasah in Cairo. 他在开罗的一所经学院学习。

例句：The madrasah offered courses in theology and law.  这所经学院提供神学和法律课程。|近义词：Islamic school, religious school, seminary|反义词:secular school|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic مدرسة (madrasa), meaning "school," from the root درس (darasa), meaning "to study."
