chitin:/ˈkaɪtɪn/ (US) /ˈkaɪtɪn/ (UK)|n.|几丁质；甲壳素|A fibrous substance consisting of polysaccharides and forming the major constituent in the exoskeleton of arthropods and the cell walls of fungi.|Chitin is the main component of the exoskeleton of insects. 几丁质是昆虫外骨骼的主要成分。

Chitin is also found in the cell walls of fungi. 几丁质也存在于真菌的细胞壁中。|近义词: None (Chitin is a unique substance)|反义词: None|词性: noun

chitinous:/ˈkaɪtɪnəs/ (US) /ˈkaɪtɪnəs/ (UK)|adj.|几丁质的；含几丁质的|Resembling or containing chitin.|The insect's chitinous exoskeleton protected it from predators.  昆虫的几丁质外骨骼保护它免受捕食者的侵害。

The fungal cell wall is chitinous. 真菌的细胞壁是几丁质的。|近义词: None|反义词: None|词性: adjective
