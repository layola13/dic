bobstay:/ˈbɒbˌsteɪ/ (US) /ˈbɒbˌsteɪ/ (UK)| n. |首斜桁支索，艏撑杆|A rope or chain that runs from the stem of a sailing vessel to the end of the bowsprit, preventing the latter from lifting.|The bobstay was crucial in keeping the bowsprit stable in heavy seas. 首斜桁支索对于在波涛汹涌的大海中保持艏撑杆的稳定至关重要。|近义词:bowsprit stay|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源:  From "bob" (meaning a dangling object) + "stay" (a rope or chain used for support).
