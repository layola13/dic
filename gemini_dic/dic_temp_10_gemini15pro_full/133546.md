nonfinancially:/ˌnɒnfaɪˈnænʃəli/ | adv. | 非财务地；非金钱地 |  Not related to or involving money or finance. |  She was rewarded nonfinancially, with extra vacation time. 她得到了非财务奖励，即额外的假期。|  The company values its employees nonfinancially by offering flexible work arrangements. 公司通过提供灵活的工作安排来非财务地重视其员工。| 近义词: nonmonetarily, intangibly | 反义词: financially, monetarily | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "financial" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
