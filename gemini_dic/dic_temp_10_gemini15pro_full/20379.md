bisulphuret:/baɪˈsʌlfjʊrɛt/ |n.| 二硫化物 |A compound containing a disulphide (S₂⁻²) anion.|Iron bisulphuret is a common mineral.  二硫化铁是一种常见的矿物。

The miners discovered a vein of iron bisulphuret. 矿工们发现了一条二硫化铁矿脉。|Synonym: disulphide, disulfide |Antonym: None (lack of a direct chemical antonym)|noun
