gaol:/dʒeɪl/ | n. | 监狱 |A place of confinement for persons held in lawful custody, especially while awaiting trial or serving a prison sentence.  A prison.  Primarily used in British English. |The thief was sent to gaol for his crimes.  小偷因犯罪被送进了监狱。| synonyms: prison, jail, penitentiary | antonyms: freedom, liberty | noun

gaol:/dʒeɪl/ | v. | 监禁 |To confine someone in a gaol.  Primarily used in British English. | He was gaoled for five years. 他被监禁了五年。| synonyms: imprison, incarcerate, jail | antonyms: release, liberate, free | verb
