antiapoplectic:/ˌæntiæpəˈplɛktɪk/
adjective
预防中风的；抗中风的
Preventing or relieving apoplexy (stroke).
The doctor prescribed antiapoplectic medication for the patient with high blood pressure. 医生给这位高血压病人开了预防中风的药。
近义词:antistroke
反义词:(None -  the opposite would imply causing a stroke, which isn't a typical word)
词性:adjective
词源: From anti- + apoplectic, from Greek *ἀποπληκτικός (apoplēktikos, “struck down by apoplexy”), from ἀποπλήσσω (apoplēssō, “I strike down”), from ἀπό (apo, “off, away from”) + πλήσσω (plēssō, “I strike”).
