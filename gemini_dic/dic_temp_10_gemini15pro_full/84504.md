halysites:/ˈhælɪˌsaɪtiːz/
n.
链珊瑚
An extinct genus of tabulate coral, characterized by interconnected corallites forming chain-like structures.
Halysites fossils are common in Silurian and Devonian rocks.  链珊瑚化石在志留纪和泥盆纪岩石中很常见。
Halysites colonies could grow to a considerable size.  链珊瑚群体可以长到相当大的尺寸。
