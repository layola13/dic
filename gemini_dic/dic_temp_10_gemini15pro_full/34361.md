classicality:/ˌklæsɪˈkælɪti/| n. |古典性；古典主义；古典作品|The quality or state of being classical, particularly in art, literature, or music. It embodies adherence to traditional standards, elegance, and timelessness.|The building's design reflects a sense of classicality. 这座建筑的设计体现了一种古典气质。|The pianist's performance was marked by an extraordinary classicality. 这位钢琴家的演奏以非凡的古典性为标志。|近义词：classicism, traditionalism, elegance|反义词：modernity, avant-garde, unconventionality|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From classical + -ity.  Classical derives from Late Latin classicus, meaning "belonging to the highest class of citizens," later used to refer to authors of the first rank. The suffix -ity denotes a state or quality.
