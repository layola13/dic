flyest:/ˈflaɪɪst/ | adj. | 最酷的，最时尚的，最好的 | Superlative form of "fly," meaning fashionable, stylish, excellent, or cool, especially in a contemporary or urban context.  | Example: She has the flyest sneakers I've ever seen. 她拥有我见过最酷的运动鞋。

Example: That car is the flyest ride on the block. 那辆车是这个街区最拉风的座驾。


近义词: coolest, freshest, dopest, illest, sickest | 反义词: uncool, lame, corny, dated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Derived from "fly," which originally referred to insects in flight. Its slang meaning of "stylish" or "fashionable" emerged in African American Vernacular English (AAVE) in the mid-20th century.  The "-est" suffix indicates the superlative form.
