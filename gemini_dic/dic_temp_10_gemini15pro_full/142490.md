osmidrosis:/ˌɒzmɪˈdrəʊsɪs/ /ˌɑːzmɪˈdroʊsɪs/
noun
狐臭；体臭
The medical term for body odor, especially an unusually strong or unpleasant one, resulting from bacterial decomposition of sweat.
Example: He suffered from osmidrosis, which made him self-conscious in social situations.  他患有狐臭，这让他在社交场合感到很不自在。

Example:  Osmidrosis can be embarrassing, but there are treatments available. 狐臭可能令人尴尬，但有一些治疗方法可用。
synonym:bromhidrosis, body odor, B.O.
antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider) pleasant scent, fragrance
