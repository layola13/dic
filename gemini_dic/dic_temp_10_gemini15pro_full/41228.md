coralligerous:/ˌkɒrəˈlɪdʒərəs/
adj.
珊瑚的；产珊瑚的；多珊瑚的
Containing, bearing, or composed of coral.
The coralligerous limestone formations are evidence of ancient reefs. 这些产珊瑚的石灰岩地层是古代珊瑚礁的证据。
coralline, coralliferous
