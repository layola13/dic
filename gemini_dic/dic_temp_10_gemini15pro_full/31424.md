charioteers:/ˈtʃæriəˈtɪərz/| n.|战车驾驶员，御者|A person who drives a chariot, especially in ancient times.  Those skilled charioteers dominated the races.|The charioteers of ancient Rome were highly skilled. 古罗马的战车驾驶员技艺高超。|synonyms: driver, rider, pilot|antonyms: passenger, pedestrian|noun
