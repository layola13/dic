bighted:/ˈbaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被咬的；腐烂的；受损的 | Damaged or corroded, especially of a rope or cable.  It can also mean affected by blight, a plant disease. | The bighted rope snapped under the strain. 被咬坏的绳子在拉力下断了。| Synonyms: corroded, decayed, damaged, blighted | Antonyms: sound, healthy, intact | adjective

blighted:/ˈblaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 枯萎的；衰败的；遭灾的 | Damaged or spoiled; affected by blight (a plant disease) or something that impairs growth, progress, or prosperity. | The bighted crops failed to yield a harvest.  枯萎的庄稼颗粒无收。| Synonyms: withered, ruined, spoiled, blighted | Antonyms: flourishing, thriving, healthy | adjective
