dolled:/dɑːld/ /dɒld/ | adj. |打扮好的，盛装的|dressed up smartly or elaborately, especially in a way considered conventionally attractive.|例句：She looked absolutely dolled up for the party. 她为了派对打扮得非常漂亮。|近义词：dressed up, decked out, glamorous, stylish|反义词：casual, unkempt, scruffy|词性：形容词
词源：doll (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives)  From the notion of dressing up like a doll.
