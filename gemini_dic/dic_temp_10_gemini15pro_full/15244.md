backgammon:/ˈbækɡæmən/| n.|西洋双陆棋|A board game for two players, each with 15 pieces, that are moved according to the throw of dice and in which the object is to be the first to remove all one's pieces from the board.|例句：We played backgammon all afternoon. 我们一下午都在玩西洋双陆棋。|例句：He's a skilled backgammon player. 他是一位西洋双陆棋高手。|近义词：table game, board game|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *backgammon*, from back + gamen (“game”).  The "back" probably refers to the pieces being returned to the bar when captured.
