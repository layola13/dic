pamper:/ˈpæmpər/ | v. | 娇惯；纵容；溺爱 | to treat with excessive indulgence, kindness, or care |  He pampers his dog with gourmet treats. 他用美味的食物娇惯他的狗。 | synonyms: indulge, spoil, coddle, baby | antonyms: neglect, ignore, discipline | verb
