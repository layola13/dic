dendrochronologically: /ˌdendroʊˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 树木年代学地 |  In a way that relates to dendrochronology, the science of dating events and variations in environment by studying the annual growth rings of trees. | The age of the wooden beams was determined dendrochronologically.  这些木梁的年代是用树木年代学方法测定的。 | 近义词:  By tree-ring dating | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
* dendro- (from Greek *dendron* meaning "tree")
* chrono- (from Greek *chronos* meaning "time")
* -logy (from Greek *-logia* meaning "study of")
* -ically (adverbial suffix)
