antiglobalization:/ˌæntiˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|反全球化|Opposition to the increase of global trade and economic interdependence among countries.|The antiglobalization movement gained momentum in the late 1990s. 反全球化运动在20世纪90年代末获得了发展势头。|
|近义词：anti-globalism|反义词：globalization, internationalism|
|词性：noun|


antiglobalization:/ˌæntiˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃən/
|adj.|反全球化的|Opposed to globalization.|He holds strong antiglobalization views. 他持有强烈的反全球化观点。|
|近义词：anti-globalist|反义词：pro-globalization|
|词性：adjective|
