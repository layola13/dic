hybridizable:/ˌhaɪbrɪˈdaɪzəbl/
adjective
可杂交的
Capable of undergoing hybridization; able to be hybridized.
This new strain of rice is hybridizable with several other varieties.  这种新品种的水稻可以与其他几个品种杂交。
hybridizing, crossable, interbreedable
non-hybridizable, sterile
adjective
