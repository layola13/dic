gismondine:/ˈdʒɪzməndaɪn/| n. |吉斯蒙丁石|A zeolite mineral, a hydrated calcium aluminium silicate.  It occurs as white or colorless crystals.|Gismondine is often found in volcanic rocks. 吉斯蒙丁石常发现于火山岩中。|The gismondine crystals were tiny but perfectly formed. 吉斯蒙丁石晶体很小，但形状完美。|近义词:zeolite, Phillipsite |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  Carlo Giuseppe Gismondi (1762–1826), Italian mineralogist, + -ine.
