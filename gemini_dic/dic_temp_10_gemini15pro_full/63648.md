eozoic:/ˌiːəˈzoʊɪk/ |adj.| 始生代的 |Of or relating to the Eozoic era, the earliest era of geologic time, preceding the Archean. |The eozoic rocks contain few fossils. 始生代的岩石中几乎没有化石。| The eozoic era witnessed the formation of Earth's crust. 始生代见证了地壳的形成。 |近义词: Precambrian |反义词: Phanerozoic |词性: 形容词
