contorting:/kənˈtɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
扭曲，扭动
twisting or bending out of its normal shape
The dancer was contorting her body into incredible positions.  舞者将她的身体扭曲成不可思议的姿势。
Synonyms: twisting, writhing, warping, deforming
Antonyms: straightening, smoothing, unbending
verb

contort:/kənˈtɔːrt/
verb
扭曲，扭动
to twist or bend out of its normal shape
His face was contorted in agony. 他的脸因痛苦而扭曲。
Synonyms: twist, writhe, warp, deform
Antonyms: straighten, smooth, unbend
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin contortus, past participle of contorquēre (“to twist violently”), from con- (“together, with”) + torquēre (“to twist”).
