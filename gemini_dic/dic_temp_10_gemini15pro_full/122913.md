molting:/ˈmoʊltɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊltɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |蜕皮; 换毛|The process of shedding periodically the outer covering of the body (feathers, skin, hair, etc.), which is then replaced by a new growth.|The snake is molting its skin. 这条蛇正在蜕皮。|Synonyms: shedding, sloughing|Antonyms: growing|noun

molt:/moʊlt/ (US) /məʊlt/ (UK)| v. |蜕皮; 换毛|To shed periodically the outer covering of the body (feathers, skin, hair, etc.), which is then replaced by a new growth.|The bird molted its feathers in the spring. 这只鸟在春天换毛。|Synonyms: shed, slough|Antonyms: grow|verb
