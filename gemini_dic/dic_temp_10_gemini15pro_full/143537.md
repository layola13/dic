outgunning:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌnɪŋ/
verb
胜过，以火力压制
defeating someone by having more or better weapons
The rebels were clearly outgunned by the government troops. 叛军显然在火力上逊于政府军。
近义词: overpowering, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: being outgunned, being overpowered
词性: v.

outgunned:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌnd/
adjective
火力不足的
having fewer or worse weapons than someone else
The outgunned police force had little chance against the heavily armed criminals.  火力不足的警察部队几乎没有机会对抗全副武装的罪犯。
近义词: underarmed, overpowered
反义词: outgunning, overpowering
词性: adj.


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "gun" (noun referring to firearms) + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
