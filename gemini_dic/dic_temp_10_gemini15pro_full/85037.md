harborer:/ˈhɑːrbərər/ (US) /ˈhɑːbərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|窝藏者，庇护者|A person who shelters or hides someone, especially someone who is wanted by the authorities.|The police were searching for the harborer of the escaped convict. 警方正在搜捕窝藏逃犯的人。|近义词：protector, shelterer, concealer|反义词：exposer, betrayer|noun

harbor:/ˈhɑːrbər/ (US) /ˈhɑːbə(r)/ (UK)| vt. n.|窝藏，庇护；港口|To give shelter or refuge to; a place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter.|She harbored a fugitive from justice. 她窝藏了一个逃犯。/ The ship sailed into the harbor. 船驶进了港口。|近义词：(v.) shelter, protect, conceal; (n.) port, haven|反义词：(v.) expose, betray; (n.)  (no direct opposite)|verb, noun
