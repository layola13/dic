enameler:/ɪˈnæmələr/ | n. | 搪瓷工；珐琅匠 | A person who applies enamel to metal, glass, or pottery. | The enameler carefully painted the intricate design onto the copper plate.  搪瓷工小心翼翼地把复杂的图案画在铜板上。| 近义词:  enamellist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

enameler:/ɪˈnæmələr/ | v. | 给…涂瓷釉；用珐琅装饰 | To coat or decorate with enamel. |  She enameled the jewelry with vibrant colors. 她用鲜艳的颜色给珠宝上了珐琅。| 近义词: glaze, varnish | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *enamelour, *enamlour, from Anglo-Norman enamaillour, enamellur, enemailur, from Old French esmailleur, from esmail (“enamel”) + -eur.  Equivalent to enamel + -er.
