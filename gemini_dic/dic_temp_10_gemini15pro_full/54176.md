disequilibrium:/ˌdɪsɪːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ /ˌdɪsˌiːkwəˈlɪbriəm/
noun
不平衡，失衡
a lack of equilibrium or stability; imbalance.
The sudden drop in oil prices caused a disequilibrium in the global economy.  原油价格的暴跌导致了全球经济的失衡。
The economy is in a state of disequilibrium. 经济处于失衡状态。
近义词: imbalance, instability, asymmetry
反义词: equilibrium, balance, stability
词性: noun

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + equilibrium (from Latin aequus "equal" + libra "balance").
