obsecration:/ˌɒbsɪˈkreɪʃn/ | n. |恳求，乞求，哀求 | An earnest or insistent request, especially one made in the name of something sacred.  It implies a desperate plea or begging. |  He uttered an obsecration to the gods for mercy. 他向上帝恳求怜悯。

|Synonyms:  entreaty, supplication, plea, appeal, petition, prayer, imploration|Antonyms: demand, command, order|noun


obsecrate:/ˈɒbsɪkreɪt/ | v. |恳求，乞求，哀求 | To beseech or implore earnestly; to beg or pray for something. | She obsecrated him to change his mind. 她恳求他改变主意。

|Synonyms:  entreat, supplicate, plead, appeal, petition, pray, implore|Antonyms: demand, command, order|verb
