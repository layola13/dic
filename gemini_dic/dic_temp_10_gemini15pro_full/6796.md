anaplasty:/ˈænəˌplæsti/ | n. | 整形术，修复术 | Surgical restoration or repair of lost, injured, or malformed body parts, especially by plastic surgery. |  Example: He underwent anaoplasty to reconstruct his ear after the accident. 他在事故后接受了整形手术来重建耳朵。

Example: Anaoplasty can significantly improve the appearance and function of damaged tissues. 整形术可以显著改善受损组织的外观和功能。 | Synonym: reconstructive surgery, plastic surgery | Antonym: deformation, disfigurement | noun
