impallid:/ɪmˈpælɪd/ | adj. |  暗淡的，无生气的| Lacking vigor or intensity. | The impallid light of the moon cast long shadows.  暗淡的月光投下长长的影子。 | synonyms: dull, lifeless, lackluster | antonyms: vibrant, bright, vivid | adjective
