in-action:/ɪn ˈækʃ(ə)n/| adj.|在行动中，在运行中，在实际操作中|Happening, working, or being used.  It describes something that is currently operating or being demonstrated.|例句：We saw the new software in action. 我们看到了新软件的实际操作。|近义词：operating, functioning, working, active, live|反义词:inactive, idle, static|词性:形容词

in-action:/ɪn ˈækʃ(ə)n/| n.|行动，活动，运转|The process of doing something, especially when it is happening and can be seen.|例句：The machine is now in action. 机器现在正在运转。|近义词：operation, activity, function, performance|反义词:inaction, idleness|词性:名词
