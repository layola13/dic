hogsty:/ˈhɒɡstaɪ/ (UK), /ˈhɑːɡstaɪ/ (US)| n. |猪圈；肮脏的地方|A pen or enclosure for pigs; a filthy or messy place.|The farm's hogsty was in dire need of cleaning.  农场里的猪圈急需清理。|

The student's dorm room was a complete hogsty. 学生的宿舍简直就是一个猪圈。|近义词：pigsty, pigpen, sty|反义词: palace, mansion|名词
