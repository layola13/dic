dispositional:/ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃənl/| adj.|性情的，气质的；素质上的；倾向的|Relating to or determined by disposition; inherent.  Characterized by a particular mindset or tendency.|例句：Dispositional factors, such as personality traits, can influence a person's behavior.  像性格特征这样的性格因素会影响一个人的行为。|例句：Researchers are studying the relationship between dispositional optimism and health outcomes.  研究人员正在研究性格乐观与健康结果之间的关系。|近义词：inherent, innate, inborn, constitutional, temperamental|反义词：acquired, learned, situational|词性:adjective

词源:
From disposition + -al.  Disposition from Late Latin *dispositio(n-), from Latin disponere “put in different places, arrange,” from dis- “apart” + ponere “to put, place.”
