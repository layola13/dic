croft:/krɒft/ | n. | 小农场，小块耕地；小屋，棚屋 | A small enclosed field used for pasture or crops, often near a house.  A small farm, especially a tenant farm.  | The farmer kept a few sheep in his croft.  农民在他的小农场里养了几只羊。 |  The family lived in a small croft near the loch. 这家人住在湖边的一个小棚屋里。 | synonyms: smallholding, paddock, field, plot, homestead | antonyms: estate, ranch, plantation | noun
