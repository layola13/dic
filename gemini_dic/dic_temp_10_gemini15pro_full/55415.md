distillable:/dɪˈstɪləbl/ | adj. | 可蒸馏的 | Capable of being distilled; able to be purified or separated by distillation. |  The essential oils are distillable from the plant.  从植物中可以蒸馏出精油。 | synonyms:  purifiable, refinable | antonyms: non-distillable, undistillable | adjective
