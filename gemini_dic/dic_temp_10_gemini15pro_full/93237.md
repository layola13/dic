iine:/iːneɪ/ |interj.| 好啊；赞；不错 |Expressing approval or agreement, similar to "good", "nice", or "okay".  It's often used in online communication, especially on social media. |例句：Iine, let's meet at 7 pm. 好啊，我们晚上七点见。

例句：Iine! I'm looking forward to it. 好啊！我很期待。|近义词：good, nice, okay, cool, great|反义词：bad, terrible, awful|词性: interjection

词源:  Borrowed from Japanese いいね (iine), which itself is a contraction of いい (ii, “good”) + ね (ne, a sentence-ending particle).
