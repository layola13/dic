paretic: /pəˈrɛtɪk/ /pəˈretɪk/
adjective
轻瘫的，部分麻痹的
Affected with or characterized by paresis.  A condition of muscular weakness caused by nerve damage or disease; partial paralysis.
The paretic limb was difficult to move.  瘫痪的肢体难以移动。

The stroke left him with a paretic right arm. 中风使他的右臂瘫痪。
partially paralyzed, weak, weakened
healthy, strong, normal
