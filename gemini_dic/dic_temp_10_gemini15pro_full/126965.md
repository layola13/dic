narrowcasting: /ˈnæroʊˌkæstɪŋ/ | n. | 窄播；定向广播；小范围广播 | The transmission of television programs to a comparatively small audience defined by special interest or geographical location.  It is the opposite of broadcasting, which is the transmission of programs to a large, undifferentiated audience. |  Cable television offers opportunities for narrowcasting to niche markets. 有线电视为针对利基市场的窄播提供了机会。

Narrowcasting allows advertisers to target specific demographics. 窄播允许广告商针对特定的人口统计数据。 | 近义词: niche marketing, targeted advertising | 反义词: broadcasting | 词性: noun
