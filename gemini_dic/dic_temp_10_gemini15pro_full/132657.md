nondisingenuous:/ˌnɒndɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/ | adj. |  真诚的，坦率的 |  Not disingenuous; genuine, sincere, and honest.  Characterized by sincerity and honesty; not attempting to deceive or mislead. |  Her nondisingenuous apology felt truly heartfelt. 她真诚的道歉让人感觉发自内心。

His nondisingenuous approach to the problem earned him the respect of his colleagues.  他坦诚处理问题的方式赢得了同事们的尊重。 | 近义词：sincere, honest, genuine, candid, frank, straightforward, artless | 反义词：disingenuous, insincere, dishonest, deceitful, duplicitous, artful | 词性：形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
disingenuous (adjective): from dis- (prefix) meaning "opposite of" + ingenuous (adjective) meaning "innocent, naive, artless," ultimately from Latin *ingenuinus, "native, freeborn," from in- "in" + gignere "to beget."
