pasturers:/ˈpæstʃərərz/
n.
牧场主；牧民
People who own or manage pastures; graziers.
The pasturers moved their cattle to higher ground during the floods.  牧场主在洪水期间把牛转移到了地势较高的地方。
The pasturers were concerned about the drought affecting their land. 牧场主担心干旱会影响他们的土地。
synonyms: herders, ranchers, graziers, stockmen, cattlemen
antonyms: farmers (while some farmers may also be pasturers, the terms are not interchangeable, and farmers can cultivate crops rather than just raise livestock)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *pasturer*, from Old French *pastureor*,  from Late Latin *pasturator*, from Latin *pasturare* "to pasture,"  from *pastura* "pasture."
