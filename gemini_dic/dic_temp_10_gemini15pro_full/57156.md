dowdy:/ˈdaʊdi/ | adjective | 邋遢的，过时的，不时尚的 | lacking smartness or style; shabby.  |  She looked dowdy in her old-fashioned clothes. 她穿着过时的衣服，显得邋遢。 | 近义词: frumpy, drab, shabby, unfashionable | 反义词: stylish, fashionable, chic, elegant | 词性: adjective

dowdies:/ˈdaʊdiz/ | noun | 邋遢的女人，过时的人 |  plural of dowdy; women who are dowdy or unfashionable. | The dowdies at the party stood out amongst the stylishly dressed guests.  派对上那些衣着邋遢的女人在衣着时髦的宾客中显得格格不外。 | 近义词: frumps | 反义词: fashionistas, trendsetters | 词性: noun
