encaenia:/ɪnˈsiːniə/
noun
毕业典礼；校庆日；周年纪念庆典
A ceremony at Oxford University commemorating its founders and benefactors.  It includes a sermon, speeches, and the conferring of honorary degrees.
The University's encaenia is a grand occasion.  大学的毕业典礼是一个盛大的场合。
近义词：commencement, graduation, celebration
反义词：N/A
词性: n.


etymology:
From Late Latin encænia, from Ancient Greek ἐνκαίνια (enkaínia, “renewal, dedication”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + καινός (kainós, “new”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱey- (“new”).
