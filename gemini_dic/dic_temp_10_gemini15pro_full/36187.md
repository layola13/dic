cofound:/koʊˈfaʊnd/ | vt. | 共同创立，共同创建 | To establish something (such as a business) jointly with another or others. |  Example 1: Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak cofounded Apple Computer. 史蒂夫·乔布斯和史蒂夫·沃兹尼亚克共同创立了苹果电脑公司。

Example 2:  She cofounded a non-profit organization dedicated to helping underprivileged children. 她与他人共同创立了一个致力于帮助贫困儿童的非营利组织。 | 近义词: establish, create, launch, start | 反义词: dissolve, dismantle | 词性: 动词
