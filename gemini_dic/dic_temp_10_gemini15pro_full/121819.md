misregulated:/ˌmɪsˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
失调的，管理不善的
poorly or improperly regulated
The financial markets were misregulated, leading to the crash. 金融市场监管不力，导致了崩盘。
Misregulated industries can pose a threat to public safety. 监管不力的行业可能对公共安全构成威胁。
synonyms: mismanaged, uncontrolled, disorderly, chaotic
antonyms: regulated, controlled, orderly, organized
词性: adjective
