detoxified:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 解毒的，排毒的 |  freed from toxic substances or qualities; made harmless. | The patient was detoxified after accidentally ingesting poison.  病人意外摄入毒药后接受了戒毒治疗。| cleansed, purified, sanitized | contaminated, polluted | adjective

detoxify:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪ/ | v. | 解毒，排毒 | to remove toxic substances or qualities; to make harmless. | The clinic detoxifies patients addicted to drugs. 这家诊所为吸毒成瘾的病人解毒。| cleanse, purify, sanitize | contaminate, pollute | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove") + toxic + -ify (suffix meaning "to make"). The word "toxic" comes from the Latin "toxicum" meaning "poison."
