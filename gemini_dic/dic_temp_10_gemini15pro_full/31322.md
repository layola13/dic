chaptering:/ˈtʃæptərɪŋ/
n.
分章节；章节划分
The novel's chaptering is well-structured, making it easy to follow. 这部小说的章节结构很好，很容易理解。
The chaptering of the textbook is designed to facilitate learning. 这本教科书的章节划分旨在方便学习。
近义词:division, segmentation
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "continuous flow")


Etymology:
From the verb "chapter" + "-ing" suffix, ultimately from Latin "capitulum" (small head, chapter).
