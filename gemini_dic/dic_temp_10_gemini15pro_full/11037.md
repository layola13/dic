arcana:/ɑːrˈkeɪnə/ /ɑːrˈkɑːnə/ | n. | 奥秘，秘密，神秘事物 |  Secrets or mysteries.  Things that are not known to most people; esoteric knowledge | The magician revealed some of his arcana to his apprentice.  魔术师向他的学徒透露了他的一些秘密。 | synonyms: mysteries, secrets, enigmas, esoterica | antonyms: common knowledge, open secrets | noun

arcane:/ɑːrˈkeɪn/ /ɑːrˈkɑːn/ | adj. | 神秘的，秘密的，晦涩难懂的 |Understood by few; mysterious or secret. |  He spoke in arcane language that only a few scholars could understand. 他用只有少数学者才能理解的晦涩语言讲话。 | synonyms: mysterious, secret, esoteric, obscure, cryptic | antonyms: common, known, public, obvious, clear | adjective
