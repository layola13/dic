palimpsestic:/ˌpælɪmpˈsɛstɪk/ | adj. | 重写的，多层书写的，(像羊皮纸一样)反复擦写后的 | resembling or being a palimpsest; having diverse layers or aspects apparent beneath the surface. | The city's architecture is palimpsestic, with layers of history visible in its buildings. 这座城市的建筑是重写的，其建筑中可见历史的层次。| The palimpsestic nature of memory allows new experiences to overwrite old ones.  记忆的重写性质允许新的经历覆盖旧的经历。 | layered, multilayered, stratified | homogenous, uniform | adjective
