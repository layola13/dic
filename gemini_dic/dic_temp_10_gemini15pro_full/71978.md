flimsiness:/ˈflɪmzɪnəs/
noun
脆弱; 薄弱; 易损坏; 不结实
the quality of being flimsy and easily damaged.
The flimsiness of the material made it unsuitable for the project. 这种材料的脆弱性使其不适合该项目。
frailty, weakness, delicacy
strength, sturdiness, robustness
n.
