funambulist:/fəˈnæmbjʊlɪst/| n. |走钢丝者；走绳索的人|A person who walks on a tightrope or slack rope.|The funambulist walked across the high wire with incredible grace.  走钢丝者以惊人的优雅走过高空钢丝。|Synonyms: tightrope walker, rope walker, acrobat|Antonyms: none|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "funambulus" (rope-walker), from "funis" (rope) + "ambulare" (to walk).
