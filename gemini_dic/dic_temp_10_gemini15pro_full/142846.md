out-flashed:/ˌaʊtˈflæʃt/| v.|胜过，以更强的光芒盖过|To surpass in flashing; to shine more brightly than.|The fireworks out-flashed the city lights. 焰火的光芒胜过了城市的灯光。|Synonyms: outshine, eclipse, overshadow|Antonyms: be outshone by, be eclipsed by|verb

out-flashed:/ˌaʊtˈflæʃt/| adj.|被胜过的，被更强的光芒盖过的|Surpassed in flashing; shone less brightly than.|The city lights were out-flashed by the fireworks. 城市的灯光被焰火的光芒盖过了。|Synonyms: outshone, eclipsed, overshadowed|Antonyms: outshining, eclipsing, overshadowing|adjective
