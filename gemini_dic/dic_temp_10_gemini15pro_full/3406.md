afoam:/əˈfoʊm/| adj.| 满是泡沫的；起泡沫的 | Covered with foam; foaming.| The beer was afoam in the mug. 杯子里的啤酒满是泡沫。| The waves crashed afoam against the rocks. 海浪翻腾着泡沫拍打在岩石上。|近义词：foamy, frothy, bubbly|反义词：flat, still|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + foam.  The prefix "a-" in this context means "in a state of" or "on."  The word "foam" comes from the Old English "fām," which in turn derives from the Proto-Germanic "*faimaz."
