aerosphere:/ˈɛroʊsfɪr/| n. |大气层，空气层|The aerosphere is the gaseous envelope surrounding a celestial body, such as the Earth.|例句：The aerosphere protects us from harmful radiation from the sun. 大气层保护我们免受来自太阳的有害辐射。

例句：Pollution in the aerosphere is a growing concern. 大气层的污染日益令人担忧。|近义词：atmosphere, air, sky|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:  From aero- + sphere.  "Aero-" comes from Greek ἀήρ (aēr, "air"), and "-sphere" comes from Greek σφαῖρα (sphaîra, "sphere, globe, ball").
