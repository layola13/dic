chicanery:/ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/
|n.|欺骗；诡计；狡辩|Deception or trickery, especially by use of clever but misleading language; sophistry.|The lawyer used chicanery to get his client off the hook. 律师用诡计使他的客户脱身。|近义词：trickery, deception, subterfuge, guile, fraud|反义词：honesty, integrity, straightforwardness|词性:名词


chicaner:/ʃɪˈkeɪnər/
|n.|耍花招的人；诡辩者|A person who uses chicanery.|He was known as a chicaner, always looking for a loophole in the rules. 他被称为诡辩者，总是在规则中寻找漏洞。|近义词：trickster, deceiver, cheat, fraudster|反义词：honest person|词性:名词
