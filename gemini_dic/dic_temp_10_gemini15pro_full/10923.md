aranga:/əˈræŋɡə/ | n. | 阿兰加（一种新西兰的木制长笛）| A traditional Māori end-blown flute made of wood, often carved and decorated. | Example: The haunting sound of the aranga filled the valley.  阿兰加笛子令人难忘的声音充满了山谷。| Example: He learned to play the aranga from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里学会了吹奏阿兰加笛。| Synonym: flute, kōauau (another Māori flute) | Antonym: N/A | Noun
