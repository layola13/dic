antiempiricist:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪrɪsɪst/| n. adj.| 反经验主义者，反经验主义的 |  Someone who opposes or rejects empiricism, the philosophical view that knowledge comes primarily from sensory experience. |  The antiempiricist argued that innate ideas, not experience, formed the basis of knowledge.  反经验主义者认为，先天观念而非经验构成了知识的基础。|  近义词: rationalist,  apriorist | 反义词: empiricist | 词性: noun, adjective

antiempiricist:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪrɪsɪst/| adj.| 反经验主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of antiempiricism. | Kant's transcendental idealism is sometimes considered a form of antiempiricism.  康德的先验唯心主义有时被认为是一种反经验主义。|  近义词: rationalistic,  aprioristic | 反义词: empirical | 词性: adjective
