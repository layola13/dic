halisauria:/ˌhælɪˈsɔːriə/
n.
halisaurians (复数)
海蜥类
A group of extinct marine reptiles of the order Sauropterygia.  They were characterized by long necks and paddle-like limbs, adapted for swimming in the oceans of the Mesozoic Era.
The halisauria, like the plesiosaurs, were well-adapted to marine life.  海蜥类与蛇颈龙一样，非常适应海洋生活。
The fossil record shows a wide diversity of halisauria. 化石记录显示了海蜥类的多样性。
synonyms:  no exact synonyms, but related groups include plesiosaurs, nothosaurs
antonyms:  N/A (extinct group, no direct opposite)
