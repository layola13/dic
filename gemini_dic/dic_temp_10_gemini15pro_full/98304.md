insight:/ˈɪnsaɪt/| n.|洞察力，领悟，见解|the capacity to gain an accurate and deep intuitive understanding of a person or thing.|She had a sudden insight into the true nature of the problem. 她突然洞察到了问题的本质。|近义词：understanding, perception, comprehension, awareness|反义词：ignorance, obtuseness|词性:名词

词源: From late Middle English *insiʒt*, from *in- ‘into’ + siʒt ‘sight’.*  Essentially, "seeing into" something.
