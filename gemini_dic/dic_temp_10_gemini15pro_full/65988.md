exaltednesses:/ɪɡˈzɔːltɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
崇高，尊贵（复数）
The state of being exalted; elevated character or rank.  A plural form of exaltedness, referring to multiple instances of this quality.
Their Exaltednesses, the ambassadors, entered the hall. 各位阁下，大使们进入了大厅。
Exaltednesses rarely mingle with the common people.  要人们很少与平民百姓混在一起。
近义词：majesties, highnesses, eminences
反义词：lowliness, humbleness, insignificance
词性: noun (plural)
