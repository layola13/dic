nonleguminous:/ˌnɒnˈleɡjuːmɪnəs/
adjective
非豆科的
Not belonging to the Leguminosae family (also called Fabaceae or pea family); not a legume.
Soybeans are leguminous, while rice is nonleguminous.
大豆是豆科植物，而水稻则是非豆科植物。
Many nonleguminous trees fix nitrogen.
许多非豆科树木能固氮。

synonym: non-legume
antonym: leguminous
词性: adjective
