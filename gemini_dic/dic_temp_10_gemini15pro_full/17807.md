bedrooms: /ˈbedruːmz/ (US) /ˈbedrʊmz/ (UK)
n.
卧室（复数）
Rooms furnished and used for sleeping.
The house has three bedrooms and two bathrooms. 这房子有三间卧室和两间浴室。

This hotel offers a variety of bedrooms, from single to suites. 这家酒店提供各种类型的卧室，从单人间到套房。

近义词: chambers, sleeping quarters
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 living rooms, common areas)
词性: 名词
