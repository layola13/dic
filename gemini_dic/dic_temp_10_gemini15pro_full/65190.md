ethos: /ˈiːθɒs/ (UK) /ˈiːθɑːs/ (US) | n. |  气质，精神特质； 道德准则；风气，社会思潮 |  The characteristic spirit of a culture, era, or community as manifested in its beliefs and aspirations. | The ethos of the company is one of innovation and collaboration.  这家公司的精神是创新与合作。| synonym: spirit, character, atmosphere, climate, mood, feeling, tenor, essence, nature | antonym:  None (Ethos describes a characteristic, not a polar opposite.) | noun
