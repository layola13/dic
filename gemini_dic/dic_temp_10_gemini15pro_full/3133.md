aesthete: /ˈesˌθiːt/ /ˈɛsˌθiːt/ | n. | 审美家；唯美主义者 | A person who has or affects to have a special appreciation of art and beauty.  | He’s a true aesthete, surrounding himself with beautiful objects. 他是一位真正的审美家，周围都是美丽的物品。

| Oscar Wilde was considered an aesthete. 奥斯卡·王尔德被认为是一位唯美主义者。| 近义词:connoisseur, art lover, epicure | 反义词:philistine | 词性:noun
