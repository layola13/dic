courteously: /ˈkɜːrtiəsli/ (US) /ˈkɜːtɪəsli/ (UK) | adv. | 礼貌地，客气地 | In a polite and respectful manner. Showing good manners and consideration for others. | She courteously offered her seat to the elderly woman. 她礼貌地把座位让给了那位老妇人。|  He courteously held the door open for her. 他礼貌地为她把门打开。 | politely, respectfully, graciously | rudely, impolitely, disrespectfully | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corteis, from court + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin cohortem "enclosed place, cohort."
