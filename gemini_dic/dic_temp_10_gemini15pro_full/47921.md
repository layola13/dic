deceptive:/dɪˈsɛptɪv/| adj.|欺骗性的，误导性的|Giving an appearance or impression different from the true one; misleading.|Her sweetly innocent look was deceptive. 她那甜美无辜的表情是骗人的。|近义词：misleading, deceitful, fallacious, spurious|反义词：honest, genuine, truthful|词性:形容词
