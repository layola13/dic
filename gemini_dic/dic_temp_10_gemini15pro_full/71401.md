flagship:/ˈflæɡʃɪp/ | n. | 旗舰；最重要产品；佼佼者 |  The most important product, idea, building, etc. in a group or organization. Originally, the ship carrying the admiral or commander of a fleet. | The company's flagship store is in New York City. 该公司的旗舰店在纽约市。 | synonyms: centerpiece, showpiece, cornerstone | antonyms:  minor product, insignificant item | noun

flagship:/ˈflæɡʃɪp/ | adj. |  旗舰的；首要的；至关重要的 | Serving as the flagship; most important. | This is their flagship product, representing the best of their technology. 这是他们的旗舰产品，代表了他们最好的技术。| synonyms: premier, leading, primary | antonyms: secondary, subsidiary, minor | adjective
