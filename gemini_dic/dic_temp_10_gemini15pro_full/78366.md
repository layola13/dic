gesticulate:/dʒɛsˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. | 做手势；用手势表达 | To make gestures, especially while speaking, to emphasize one's words or convey meaning. | He gesticulated wildly, trying to explain his point. 他疯狂地做着手势，试图解释他的观点。|  She gesticulated to the waiter to bring the bill. 她用手势示意服务员拿账单过来。| synonyms: gesture, motion, signal, sign | antonyms: be still, freeze | verb
