deciding:/dɪˈsaɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 决定；裁定；解决；断定 |Present participle of "decide".  The act of making a choice or reaching a conclusion. |  They are deciding where to go for vacation. 他们正在决定去哪里度假。| Synonyms: determining, resolving, settling | Antonyms: hesitating, wavering | verb (present participle)

decide:/dɪˈsaɪd/ | v. | 决定；裁定；解决；断定 | Come to a resolution in the mind as a result of consideration. | I decided to go to the park. 我决定去公园。| Synonyms: determine, resolve, settle, conclude | Antonyms: hesitate, waver | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French decider, from Latin decidere (“to cut off, decide”), from de- (“off”) + caedere (“to cut”).
