noncreeping:/nɒnˈkriːpɪŋ/ | adj. | 非蔓延的，不爬行的 | Not creeping; not spreading gradually or stealthily. | This type of ivy is a noncreeping variety, so it won't take over your garden. 这种常春藤是不蔓延的品种，所以它不会霸占你的花园。| 近义词: noninvasive, contained | 反义词: creeping, invasive | 词性: 形容词

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "creeping" (present participle of "creep," from Old English "creopan")
