dearies:/ˈdɪəriːz/
|n.|亲爱的们；宝贝们|A term of endearment used to address a group of people affectionately.|例句： "Goodbye, dearies," she said to her grandchildren. “再见，宝贝们，”她对她的孙子孙女们说。|近义词：sweeties, darlings, honeys|反义词: (No direct antonym for a term of endearment)|Noun
