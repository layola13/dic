nongratification: /ˌnɒnˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 不满足；未能满足；不称心 | The state of not being gratified; lack of gratification. |  His nongratification stemmed from the project's failure.  他的不满足源于项目的失败。| 近义词: dissatisfaction, disappointment, frustration | 反义词: gratification, satisfaction, fulfillment | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "gratification" (from Late Latin "gratificatio," meaning "pleasing, thanks").
