basanite:/ˈbæsəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 玄武岩, 试金石 | A dark, fine-grained volcanic rock, similar to basalt, but containing a higher percentage of alkali oxides and silica. It is often used as a touchstone to test the purity of gold. |  This basanite statue is incredibly detailed. 这座玄武岩石雕像非常精致。 |  Basanite was used in ancient times to test the purity of gold alloys.  玄武岩在古代被用来测试金合金的纯度。| synonyms: touchstone, basalt, aphanite | antonyms: N/A | noun
