frameo: /ˈfreɪmiːoʊ/
noun
相框，数码相框
A digital picture frame that allows users to share photos and videos wirelessly.
I sent a picture to Grandma's Frameo. 我给奶奶的Frameo数码相框发送了一张照片。
近义词: digital photo frame, smart photo frame
反义词: traditional photo frame, physical photo frame
词性: n.


Etymology:
"Frameo" is a relatively new word, likely coined in the 21st century. It combines "frame" (referring to a picture frame) and "o" (possibly suggesting connection or online functionality). It lacks a deep etymological history like older words.
