overconsiderateness:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˌsɪdərɪtnɪs/
n.
过度体贴; 过分周到
Excessive consideration for the needs and feelings of others.
Her overconsiderateness sometimes made her seem hesitant and indecisive. 她过分的体贴有时让她显得犹豫不决。
近义词:solicitude, thoughtfulness
反义词:inconsiderateness, thoughtlessness
词性:名词


overconsiderate:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈsɪdərɪt/
adj.
过度体贴的; 过分周到的
Showing excessive consideration for the needs and feelings of others.
He was overconsiderate to the point of being a doormat. 他过分体贴，到了任人摆布的地步。
近义词:solicitous, thoughtful
反义词:inconsiderate, thoughtless
词性:形容词


词源:
From over- + considerate + -ness.  "Over-" prefix meaning "excessive" combined with "considerate" (showing careful thought for the feelings of others) and the suffix "-ness" which forms nouns expressing a state or quality.
