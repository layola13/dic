interclashes:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃɪz/
noun
冲突，交锋
Clashes or conflicts between different groups or factions within a larger entity.
例句：The interclashes between different departments hindered the company's progress.  不同部门之间的冲突阻碍了公司的发展。

例句：Political interclashes within the ruling party led to instability. 执政党内部的政治冲突导致了不稳定。
近义词：conflicts, clashes, struggles, frictions
反义词：harmony, agreement, cooperation
词性:n.


interclash:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃ/
verb
（使）冲突，交锋
To clash or conflict with each other, especially within a larger group.
例句：The two rival gangs interclashed on the streets.  两个敌对的帮派在街上发生冲突。

例句：Different cultural values can interclash and create misunderstandings. 不同的文化价值观可能会发生冲突并造成误解。
近义词：clash, conflict, collide, contend
反义词：harmonize, cooperate, agree
词性:v.
