gilts:/ɡɪlts/
n.
英国政府债券；母猪
British government bonds; a female pig that has produced young.
The gilts market has been volatile recently. 最近英国政府债券市场波动较大。
The farmer sold his prize-winning gilt. 农民卖掉了他的获奖母猪。
近义词：(for British government bonds) government securities, government bonds, treasuries; (for sow) sow
反义词：(for sow) boar
词性:noun
