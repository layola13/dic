anticipatable:/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪtəbl/
adjective
可预见的，可预期的
able to be anticipated.

The company's success was anticipatable given its innovative products. 鉴于其创新的产品，该公司的成功是可以预见的。
predictable, foreseeable, expected
unpredictable, unforeseen, unexpected
