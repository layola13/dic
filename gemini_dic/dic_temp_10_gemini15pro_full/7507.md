annapolis: /əˈnæpəlɪs/
proper noun
安纳波利斯
Annapolis is the capital of the U.S. state of Maryland. It is located on the Severn River near its mouth on the Chesapeake Bay.  It is also home to the United States Naval Academy.
Annapolis is a historic city with a rich maritime heritage.
近义词: (As a capital city) capital city, state capital.  (As a location) port city.
