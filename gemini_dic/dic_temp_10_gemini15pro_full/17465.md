bearded:/ˈbɪrdɪd/| adj.|有胡须的，蓄着胡须的|Having a beard; covered with a beard. |例句：He had a long, white, bearded face. 他有一张长长的、白色的、有胡子的脸。

例句：A bearded man entered the room. 一个留着胡子的男人走进了房间。|近义词：hirsute, unshaven|反义词：clean-shaven, beardless|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English *beardad, from beard + -ed.  The Old English "beard" comes from Proto-Germanic *bardaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhardhā-.
