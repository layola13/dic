noncomplacency:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪsənsi/ | n. | 不自满，警觉 | The state of not being complacent; a lack of complacency.  Being aware of potential dangers and challenges and actively working to address them. |  The company's noncomplacency about its market share has kept it innovative.  这家公司对其市场份额的不自满使其保持创新。

The coach stressed the importance of noncomplacency, even after their winning streak. 教练强调了即使在连胜之后也不要自满的重要性。| 近义词：vigilance, alertness, mindfulness | 反义词：complacency, apathy, indifference | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From non- + complacency.  "Complacency" comes from the Latin "complacēre" (to please greatly), from "com-" (with, together) + "placēre" (to please).
