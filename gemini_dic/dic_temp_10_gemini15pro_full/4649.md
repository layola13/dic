alecithal:/ˌeɪˈlɛsɪθəl/
adjective
无黄的；缺乏卵黄的
Lacking yolk; having very little yolk.  Specifically referring to eggs or ova.
Alecithal eggs are characteristic of placental mammals.  无黄卵是胎盘哺乳动物的特征。
The human ovum is alecithal. 人类的卵子是无黄的。
Synonyms: yolkless
Antonyms: polylecithal, mesolecithal, macrolecithal, microlecithal
词性: adjective
