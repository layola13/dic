counterstyle:/ˈkaʊntərstaɪl/| n.|对照风格，相反的风格|A style that is deliberately different from or opposed to another style. |例句：The architect chose a modern counterstyle to the traditional architecture of the surrounding buildings.  这位建筑师选择了一种与周围建筑的传统风格截然不同的现代风格。

例句：Her minimalist counterstyle to the ornate Victorian decor was striking. 她极简的风格与华丽的维多利亚式装饰形成了鲜明的对比。|近义词：contrast, antithesis, opposite|反义词：similarity, conformity|词性：名词

词源：counter- (prefix meaning "against, opposite") + style
