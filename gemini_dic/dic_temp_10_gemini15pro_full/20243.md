birlaw:/ˈbɪrlɔː/| n.|乡规民约，地方习惯法|A system of local customary law, especially in Scotland, regulating land use and common resources.|The birlaw court dealt with minor offences in the village.  这个乡规民约法庭处理村里的轻微犯罪。|Near synonyms: custom, tradition, local law|Antonyms: statute, national law|noun
