learned: /lɜːrnd/ (British) /ˈlɜːrnɪd/ (American)| adj.| 有学问的；博学的；学到的| Having acquired knowledge or skill through study or experience. |例句：He is a learned man in the field of history. 他是一位在历史领域很有学问的人。

例句：This behavior is learned, not innate. 这种行为是习得的，而不是天生的。


近义词：educated, knowledgeable, scholarly, erudite|反义词：ignorant, uneducated, unlearned|词性:形容词

learned: /lɜːnd/ (British) /ˈlɜːrnd/ (American)| v. (past tense and past participle of learn) |学习；学会；得知；认识到| Acquired knowledge or skill. |例句：I learned to play the piano when I was young. 我小时候学会了弹钢琴。

例句：We learned about the accident from the news. 我们从新闻里得知了这起事故。


近义词：studied, discovered, found out|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可能用 forgot (忘记) 或 unlearned (后来忘记，不再相信)) |词性:动词
