impartable:/ɪmˈpɑːrtəbl/ | adj. |可传授的，可 imparted 的|Capable of being imparted; communicable. |例句：She possessed an impartable enthusiasm for learning. 她对学习有着可传授的热情。

例句：The wisdom of the elders is impartable to the younger generation. 长者的智慧是可以传授给年轻一代的。|近义词：communicable, transmissible, teachable |反义词：incommunicable, unteachable |词性: adjective
