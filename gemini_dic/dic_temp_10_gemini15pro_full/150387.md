panone: /pəˈnoʊn/ | n. |彩通；彩通色卡；彩通配色系统|A standardized color matching system used in printing and graphic design, specifying colors using a numbered system. |例句：The logo should be printed in Pantone 186 C.  标志应该用彩通186 C印刷。

例句：She specified the exact shade of blue using a Pantone swatch. 她用彩通色卡指定了确切的蓝色阴影。|近义词：color, shade, hue|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
