despair:/dɪˈspeər/ /dɪˈspɛr/| n. vi.|绝望，丧失信心|The feeling of having lost all hope.  |He sank into despair after losing his job. 他失业后陷入了绝望。|
近义词：hopelessness, despondency|反义词：hope, optimism|词性:名词, 动词


despair:/dɪˈspeər/ /dɪˈspɛr/| vi. |绝望，丧失信心|To lose all hope. |She despaired of ever finding a cure. 她对找到治愈方法感到绝望。|
近义词：give up, lose hope|反义词：hope, persevere|词性:动词


词源: From Old French *despeirer, from des- "away, without" + esperer "to hope," from Latin sperare "to hope."
