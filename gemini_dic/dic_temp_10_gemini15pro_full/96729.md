inefficiently:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃəntli/| adv.|效率低地，无能地|Not achieving maximum productivity; wasting or failing to make the best use of time or resources.|He worked inefficiently, wasting time on trivial tasks. 他工作效率低下，在琐碎的任务上浪费时间。|The project was managed inefficiently, leading to cost overruns.  这个项目管理效率低下，导致成本超支。| inefficiently, clumsily, wastefully | efficiently, effectively, productively | adverb
