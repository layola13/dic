echometer:/ɛˈkɒmɪtər/ (UK), /ɛˈkɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |回声仪，回声探测器|An instrument that measures the time taken for a sound wave to travel to a distant object and return, thus determining the distance to the object. It is often used for measuring the depth of water or detecting objects underwater.|The echometer showed a depth of 30 meters. 回声仪显示深度为30米。|
The fishing boat used an echometer to locate schools of fish.  渔船使用回声探测器来定位鱼群。|Synonyms: sonar, depth finder, fathometer|Antonyms: None|Noun
