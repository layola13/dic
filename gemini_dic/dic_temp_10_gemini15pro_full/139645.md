occulting:/əˈkʌltɪŋ/
adjective
掩星的；遮蔽的
Cutting off light from a celestial body by intervention.  The process of one celestial body passing in front of another, temporarily blocking its light from reaching an observer.
The occulting disk in the coronagraph blocks the light from the Sun's photosphere. 日冕仪中的遮光盘挡住了来自太阳光球的光。
The Moon is occulting Mars tonight. 今晚月亮将遮掩火星。
Synonyms: eclipsing, obscuring, hiding, blocking
Antonyms: revealing, exposing, uncovering
