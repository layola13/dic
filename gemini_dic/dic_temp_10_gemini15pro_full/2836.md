She admired his adventuresomeness and his willingness to try new things. 她钦佩他的冒险精神和勇于尝试新事物的意愿。 |近义词: daring, boldness, audacity, intrepidity |反义词: timidity, cowardice, caution, fearfulness |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adventurousness, from adventurous + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin adventurus, future participle of advenire "to arrive," from ad- "to" + venire "to come."
