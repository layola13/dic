infantes:/ɪnˈfæntiːz/
noun
婴儿，幼儿（尤指七岁以下的儿童）；步兵；西班牙和葡萄牙的王子或公主
Children under seven years of age, especially infants; foot soldiers; princes or princesses of Spain or Portugal.
The king's infantes were known for their privileged upbringing. 国王的幼儿以其优越的教养而闻名。
Synonyms: babies, toddlers, children, infants, foot soldiers
Antonyms: adults, elders, veterans, officers
