heteropoda:/ˌhɛtərˈɒpədə/ | n. | 异足蛛科 | A family of spiders commonly known as huntsman spiders or giant crab spiders.  These spiders are known for their large size and crab-like leg arrangement. | Example: The heteropoda venatoria is a common species of huntsman spider found in tropical regions.  异足蛛（狩猎蛛）是一种常见的狩猎蛛，发现于热带地区。

Example: Heteropoda are known for their speed and agility, making them effective hunters. 异足蛛以其速度和敏捷性而闻名，这使它们成为有效的猎手。 | 近义词: huntsman spider, giant crab spider | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology: From the Greek "heteros" (different) + "pous" (foot), referring to the varying lengths of their legs.
