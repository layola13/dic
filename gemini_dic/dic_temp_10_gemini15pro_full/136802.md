nonshareholders:/ˌnɒnˈʃɛəˌhəʊldəz/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈʃerˌhoʊldərz/| n.|非股东|People who do not own shares in a company.|Example: Nonshareholders are not entitled to vote at the annual meeting. 非股东无资格在年会上投票.|Example: The company decided to offer discounts to both shareholders and nonshareholders. 公司决定向股东和非股东都提供折扣。|Synonym: non-owners|Antonym: shareholders|Noun
