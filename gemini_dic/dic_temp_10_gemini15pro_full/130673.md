nonaerobic:/ˌnɒn.eəˈrəʊ.bɪk/ (US) /ˌnɑːn.erˈoʊ.bɪk/| adj.|非需氧的，厌氧的|Not requiring oxygen for growth or survival.  Relating to or denoting an organism that does not require free oxygen for respiration.|Example: Nonaerobic bacteria thrive in environments without oxygen. 非需氧细菌在无氧环境中 thrive。|Example:  Certain nonaerobic microorganisms are responsible for fermentation processes. 某些非需氧微生物负责发酵过程。|Synonym: anaerobic|Antonym: aerobic|adjective
