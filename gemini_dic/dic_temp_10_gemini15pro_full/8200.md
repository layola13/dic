anti-economic:/ˌæntiˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk/, /ˌæntiˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪk/ | adj. | 反经济的；不经济的 |  Contrary to the principles of economics; wasteful or unprofitable.  Not conducive to economic growth or efficiency. |  The government's anti-economic policies led to a recession. 政府的反经济政策导致了经济衰退。 |  The project was deemed anti-economic due to its high costs and low projected returns.  这个项目由于成本高、预期回报低而被认为是不经济的。 | 近义词: uneconomical, wasteful, unprofitable | 反义词: economical, profitable, efficient | 词性: adjective
