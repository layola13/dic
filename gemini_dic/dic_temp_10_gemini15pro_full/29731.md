catling:/ˈkæt.lɪŋ/ | n. | 小猫；幼猫；阉割公猫 |A young cat; a kitten. Also, a male cat that has been castrated. | The farmer's cat had a litter of five catlings. 农民家的猫生了一窝五只小猫。| The vet recommended catling the tomcat to prevent spraying. 兽医建议阉割这只公猫以防止它乱喷尿。| 近义词: kitten, kitty | 反义词: tomcat (specifically for an uncastrated male cat) | 词性: noun
