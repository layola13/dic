overinsist:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪst/| v. | 过分坚持；过分强调 | To insist too much or too strongly; to persist in stating or demanding something beyond what is reasonable or necessary. | He overinsisted on his point, making everyone uncomfortable. 他过分强调他的观点，让每个人都不舒服。| She overinsisted that she was right, even after being presented with evidence to the contrary. 即使在出示了相反的证据之后，她仍然过分坚持自己是对的。|近义词: belabor, harp on, dwell on |反义词: concede, yield, downplay |词性: 动词
