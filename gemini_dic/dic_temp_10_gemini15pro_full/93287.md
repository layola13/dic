ilixanthin:/ɪlɪˈzænθɪn/| n. |伊利黄素|A carotenoid pigment found in certain berries, giving them a reddish-orange color. It is a type of xanthophyll.|Berries like goji and wolfberry contain high levels of ilixanthin, contributing to their vibrant color. 枸杞和沙棘等浆果含有大量的伊利黄素，这有助于它们鲜艳的颜色。|近义词：zeaxanthin, lutein|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
