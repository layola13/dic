carotene:/ˈkærəˌtiːn/ /ˈkærətiːn/| n.|胡萝卜素|An orange or red plant pigment found in carrots and many other fruits and vegetables. It is converted to vitamin A in the body.|Carrots are a good source of carotene. 胡萝卜是胡萝卜素的良好来源。|Synonyms: carotenoid|Antonyms: None|Noun

carotene:/ˈkærəˌtiːn/ /ˈkærətiːn/| n.|胡萝卜素|A group of red and orange pigments, found in carrots and other plants, that are converted in the body to vitamin A.|Dark leafy greens also contain carotene. 深色绿叶蔬菜也含有胡萝卜素。|Synonyms: carotenoid|Antonyms: None|Noun
