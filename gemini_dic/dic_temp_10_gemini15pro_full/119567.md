microtomist: /maɪˈkrɒtəmɪst/ (UK) /maɪˈkroʊtəmɪst/ (US)
|n.|显微切片员，显微切片技术员|A person skilled in using a microtome to prepare thin sections of tissues for microscopic examination.|The experienced microtomist prepared flawless sections of the brain tissue.  经验丰富的显微切片员准备了完美的大脑组织切片。|Synonyms: histotechnician, histology technician|Antonyms: N/A
|noun|
