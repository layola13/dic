mischannels:/ˌmɪsˈtʃænəlz/ | v. | 误导，错误地引导 | To direct (something, such as funds or information) to a wrong recipient or purpose. |  The organization mischanneled funds intended for disaster relief.  该组织误用了用于救灾的资金。

The government was accused of mischanneling public resources. 政府被指控滥用公共资源。

| 近义词：misdirect, misapply, misuse | 反义词: direct correctly, allocate properly | 词性: 动词
