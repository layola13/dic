beanlike:/ˈbiːnlaɪk/
adjective
像豆的;豆状的
Resembling a bean in shape or size.
The small, beanlike seeds are dispersed by the wind. 这些小的、像豆一样的种子被风吹散。
The plant has small, beanlike pods. 这种植物有小的、豆状的豆荚。
近义词:bean-shaped, fabaceous
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., round, elongated)
词性: adjective
