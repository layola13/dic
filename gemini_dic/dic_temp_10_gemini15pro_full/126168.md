mutt:/mʌt/| n. |杂种狗，笨蛋|A dog of mixed breed. Informally, it can also refer to a foolish or inept person.|例句：That mutt of mine ate my homework!  我的那只笨狗吃了我的作业！

例句：He's such a mutt; he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，他又把钥匙锁在车里了。|近义词：mongrel, cur, dog (informal), fool, idiot|反义词:purebred (for dog)|词性:noun
