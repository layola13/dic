fabricating:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
制造; 捏造; 伪造
Inventing or producing something false.
例句：He was accused of fabricating evidence. 他被指控伪造证据。

例句：The company is fabricating parts for airplanes.  这家公司正在制造飞机零件。

近义词：manufacturing, constructing, inventing, forging, falsifying, concocting
反义词：dismantling, destroying, demolishing, telling the truth


词性:v.  动词(fabricate的现在分词)
