arpeggi:/ɑːrˈpɛdʒiːz/ (plural)|n.|琶音（复数）|The plural form of "arpeggio," referring to multiple instances of this musical technique.|The pianist’s arpeggis were breathtaking.|近义词：broken chords|反义词:chords|词性:noun
