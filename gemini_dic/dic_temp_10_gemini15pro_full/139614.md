occasioned:/əˈkeɪʒənd/
verb
引起的，造成的
caused or brought about
The heavy rain occasioned several traffic accidents. 暴雨造成了几起交通事故。
The president's visit occasioned a lot of excitement. 总统的访问引起了很大的兴奋。
近义词：caused, produced, prompted, brought about
反义词：prevented, averted
词性：v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *occasionare, from Latin occasio (“occasion, opportunity, convenient time, cause, ground, reason”), from occidere (“to fall, set, perish, die, be slain; happen, befall”), from ob- (“towards, against”) + cadere (“to fall”).
