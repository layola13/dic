electrostrictive:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈstrɪktɪv/
adjective
电致伸缩的
Relating to or exhibiting electrostriction, the change in shape or dimensions of a dielectric material under the influence of an electric field.
Electrostrictive materials can be used in actuators and sensors. 电致伸缩材料可用于致动器和传感器。
Electrostrictive polymers are being investigated for use in artificial muscles.  电致伸缩聚合物正在被研究用于人造肌肉。
近义词: piezoelectric (in some contexts)
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-electrostrictive")
词性: adjective
