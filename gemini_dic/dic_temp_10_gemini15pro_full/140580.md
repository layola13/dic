oligopetalous: /ˌɒlɪɡəˈpɛtələs/ /ˌɑːlɪɡoʊˈpɛtələs/
adjective
少瓣的；寡瓣的
Having few petals (typically fewer than the typical number for the plant family).
The oligopetalous flower had only four petals, unlike its close relatives which usually have five.  这种少瓣的花只有四片花瓣，不像它的近亲通常有五片。
The botanist identified the rare oligopetalous variant of the normally five-petaled flower.  植物学家识别出了这种通常为五瓣花的稀有少瓣变种。
近义词: micropetalous
反义词: polypetalous
词性: adjective

词源:
From Ancient Greek *ὀλίγος (olígos, “few”) + πέταλον (pétalon, “leaf, petal”)
