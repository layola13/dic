deceptory:/dɪˈsɛptəri/ | adj. | 欺骗性的，迷惑性的 | Intended to deceive or mislead; deceptive. |  The deceptory advertisement led many customers to believe the product was much better than it actually was.  欺骗性的广告让许多顾客相信产品比实际情况好得多。 | Example 2: The magician's deceptory tactics kept the audience captivated. 魔术师的欺骗性策略让观众着迷。 | 近义词: misleading, deceptive, fallacious, fraudulent, deceitful | 反义词: honest, truthful, genuine, authentic, sincere | 词性: 形容词


Etymology: From Late Latin *dēceptōrius, from Latin dēcipere (“to deceive”). Ultimately from de- + capere (“to take”).
