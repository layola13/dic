dragonish:/ˈdræɡənɪʃ/| adj.|像龙的；凶猛的|Resembling a dragon, either in appearance or temperament; fierce, ferocious.|The gargoyle on the building had a dragonish appearance.  这栋建筑上的滴水嘴兽有着像龙一样外表。|The CEO's dragonish demeanor intimidated the new employees.  这位首席执行官凶猛的举止吓坏了新员工。|近义词：draconian, fierce, ferocious, intimidating|反义词：gentle, meek, docile|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *dragon* + *-ish*.  The suffix *-ish* forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like," "of the nature of," or "having the quality of."
