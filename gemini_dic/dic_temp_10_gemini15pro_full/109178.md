legitimization:/ləˌdʒɪtɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |合法化，正当化|The legitimization of gambling has led to a surge in casinos.  赌博的合法化导致赌场激增。|The government's actions lacked legitimization. 政府的行为缺乏合法性。 | 近义词: justification, validation, sanction | 反义词: invalidation, condemnation | noun

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/ | adj. |合法的，正当的| Is his business legitimate?  他的生意合法吗？| It's a perfectly legitimate question.  这是一个完全合理的问题。 | 近义词: legal, valid, lawful | 反义词: illegal, unlawful, illegitimate | adjective

legitimately:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmətli/ | adv. |合法地，正当地|The company operates legitimately. 这家公司合法经营。|He legitimately won the election. 他合法地赢得了选举。| 近义词: legally, validly, lawfully | 反义词: illegally, unlawfully | adverb

legitimize:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/ | v. |使合法化，使正当化|The government has legitimized the use of marijuana for medicinal purposes. 政府已经将医用大麻合法化。|  They tried to legitimize their actions by claiming they were following orders.  他们试图通过声称自己是在服从命令来使自己的行为合法化。 | 近义词: justify, validate, legalize | 反义词: invalidate, condemn | verb


词源:  From French légitimer, from Latin legitimus  "lawful," from lex (genitive legis) "law."
