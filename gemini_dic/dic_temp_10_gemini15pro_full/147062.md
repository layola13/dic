overlegislates:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪts/
verb
过度立法
to create too many laws, especially about minor matters, making it difficult or confusing to obey them.
The city council has overlegislated on parking regulations, creating a complex and confusing system.  市议会在停车规定上过度立法，创造了一个复杂且令人困惑的系统。

The government has been accused of overlegislating on environmental issues, stifling innovation and economic growth. 政府被指责在环境问题上过度立法，扼杀了创新和经济增长。

近义词: overregulate, overgovern
反义词: deregulate
