otocyst:/ˈoʊtəsɪst/ | n. | 耳囊，听囊 | A fluid-filled sac in the vertebrate embryo that develops into the inner ear. | The otocyst plays a crucial role in the development of hearing and balance. 耳囊在听觉和平衡的发展中起着至关重要的作用。|  The researchers studied the development of the otocyst in zebrafish. 研究人员研究了斑马鱼耳囊的发育。 | synonyms: auditory vesicle, otic vesicle | antonyms: None | noun
