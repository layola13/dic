libations:/laɪˈbeɪʃənz/
noun
奠酒，祭酒；(尤指酒的)饮料
a drink poured out as an offering to a god, or a formal drinking of a toast
The priest offered libations to the gods.  祭司向神明献上奠酒。
We made libations to the earth goddess. 我们向大地女神献上了奠酒。
Synonyms: drinks, beverages, refreshments
Antonyms: abstinence, sobriety
n.
