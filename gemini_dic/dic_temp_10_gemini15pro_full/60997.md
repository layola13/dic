eligibly:/ˈɛlɪdʒəbli/
adverb|合格地，符合条件地|in a way that makes someone or something suitable to be chosen for something|He is eligibly employed. 他有资格就业。|
近义词:suitably, appropriately, properly
反义词:ineligibly, improperly, unsuitably
词性:adverb

eligibility:/ˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪləti/
noun|合格，符合条件|the state of having the right to do or obtain something through satisfaction of the appropriate conditions.|His eligibility for the position is not in question.  他对这个职位的资格没有问题。|
近义词:qualification, suitability, fitness
反义词:ineligibility, unsuitability, unfitness
词性:noun

eligible:/ˈɛlɪdʒəbl/
adjective|有资格的，合格的|having the right to do or obtain something through satisfaction of the appropriate conditions.|Only people over 18 are eligible to vote. 只有18岁以上的人才有资格投票。|
近义词:qualified, entitled, suitable
反义词:ineligible, unqualified, unsuitable
词性:adjective
