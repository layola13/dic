amylases: /ˈæmɪleɪzɪz/ | n. | 淀粉酶 |  Enzymes that catalyze the hydrolysis of starch into sugars.  Amylases are present in saliva and pancreatic juices. | Saliva contains amylases that begin the digestion of starch. 唾液中含有开始消化淀粉的淀粉酶。

Amylases are used in the production of various foods and beverages. 淀粉酶用于生产各种食品和饮料。 | 近义词: enzyme 酶 | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑抑制剂 inhibitor) | 词性: noun 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *amylase*, from amyle “starch” (from Ancient Greek *ámylon* "starch") + -ase (suffix forming names of enzymes).
