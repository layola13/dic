gustable:/ˈɡʌstəbl/ | adj. | 可口的，美味的 | Pleasant to the taste; savory. | The chef prepared a gustable feast for the king.  厨师为国王准备了一顿美味的盛宴。| 近义词: palatable, tasty, delicious, savory | 反义词: unpalatable, distasteful, unsavory | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *gustabilis*, from Latin gustare "to taste," from gustus "taste."
