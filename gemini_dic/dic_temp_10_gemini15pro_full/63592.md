envisions:/ɪnˈvɪʒənz/
verb
展望，想象
To form a mental picture of something, especially something that you want to happen or create in the future.
The architect envisions a building with a glass roof.  这位建筑师设想了一座带有玻璃屋顶的建筑。
She envisions a future where everyone has access to clean water. 她展望了一个人人都能获得清洁用水的未来。
