mousebutton:/ˈmaʊs ˌbʌtn/| n.|鼠标按键|A button on a computer mouse that is pressed to perform a specific action.|例句：I clicked the left mouse button to select the file. 我点击鼠标左键选择了文件。

例句：Double-click the right mouse button to open the context menu. 双击鼠标右键打开上下文菜单。|近义词：mouse key|反义词:None|词性:noun
