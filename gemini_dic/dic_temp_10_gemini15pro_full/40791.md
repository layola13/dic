convocation:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈkeɪʃn/| n.|会议；集会；召集；正式集会|a large formal assembly of people|The university convocation was held in the grand hall. 大学毕业典礼在大会堂举行。|近义词：assembly, gathering, meeting, congress|反义词：dispersal, separation|noun

convoke:/kənˈvoʊk/| vt.|召集；召开|to call together or summon, especially for a formal meeting|The president convoked Congress to address the nation. 总统召集国会向全国发表讲话。|近义词：summon, convene, assemble|反义词：dismiss, adjourn|verb
