pasigraphy:/pæsˈɪɡrəfi/ /pəˈsɪɡrəfi/
n.
世界语;  通行文字;  普遍文字;  国际语;
A system of universal writing, using symbols or other characters that are not specific to any particular language.
Pasigraphy seeks to convey meaning through symbols that are universally understood, regardless of language.  世界语试图通过普遍理解的符号来传达意义，而不管使用何种语言。
Pasigraphy, a universal written language, was a popular intellectual pursuit in the 17th and 18th centuries.  世界语是一种通用的书面语言，在17和18世纪是一种流行的知识追求。

近义词: ideography, semasiography
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) vernacular, dialect
