beepers:/ˈbiːpərz/
n.
寻呼机；传呼机；BP机
A small electronic device that you carry so that people can leave messages for you.
例句：He clipped the beeper to his belt. 他把寻呼机别在腰带上。

例句：In the 1990s, beepers were a popular way to stay in touch. 在20世纪90年代，寻呼机是一种很流行的保持联系的方式。

近义词：pager, paging device
反义词：（无常用反义词）
词性：名词
