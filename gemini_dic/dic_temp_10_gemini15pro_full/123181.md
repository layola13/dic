mono-substitution:/ˌmɒnəʊˌsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən/ /ˌmɑːnoʊˌsʌbstɪˈtuːʃən/ | n. |单取代，单取代反应| The process of replacing one atom or group in a molecule with another single atom or group.  This often refers to reactions in organic chemistry where one hydrogen atom is replaced by another functional group.|  Mono-substitution of benzene with a nitro group yields nitrobenzene.  苯的硝基单取代生成硝基苯。| substitution, displacement | multi-substitution, poly-substitution | noun
