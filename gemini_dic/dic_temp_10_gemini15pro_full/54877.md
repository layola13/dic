displeasureably:/dɪsˈplɛʒərəbli/
adverb
不愉快地，令人不快地
in a way that expresses or causes displeasure.
He coughed displeasureably when I interrupted him. 当我打断他时，他很不高兴地咳嗽了一声。
She stared displeasureably at the mess. 她不悦地盯着那堆乱七八糟的东西。
unpleasantly, disagreeably
pleasantly, agreeably
