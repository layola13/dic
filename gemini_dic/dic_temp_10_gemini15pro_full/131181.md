nonbrowsers:/nɒnˈbraʊzəz/
n.
非浏览器用户
People who do not use web browsers.
Many nonbrowsers access internet content through dedicated apps. 许多非浏览器用户通过专用应用程序访问互联网内容。
Smart TVs and game consoles are often used by nonbrowsers. 智能电视和游戏机经常被非浏览器用户使用。
non-users, app users
browser users
