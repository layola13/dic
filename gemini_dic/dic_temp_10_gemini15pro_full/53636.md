discernments:/dɪˈsɜːnmənts/
|n.|识别力，洞察力，辨别力|The act or faculty of discerning; insightful judgment.  The ability to judge well.|Her critical discernments of the artist's work were highly valued. 她对这位艺术家作品的批判性见解非常受重视。|近义词: insights, perceptions, judgments, acumen, perspicacity|反义词: ignorance, blindness, obtuseness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: discernment, from Old French discernement, from discerner ‘distinguish,’ from Latin discernere ‘separate, distinguish.’
