doublethink:/ˈdʌbəlθɪŋk/ | n. | 双重思想，双重思考 | The act of simultaneously accepting two mutually contradictory beliefs as correct, often induced by propaganda or social pressure. |  He engaged in doublethink, believing both that the Party was infallible and that his own memories were faulty. 他陷入了双重思想，既相信党是绝对正确的，又相信自己的记忆是错误的。

The Party's control over information enabled them to enforce doublethink on the population. 党对信息的控制使他们能够对民众实施双重思想。| 近义词: duplicity, hypocrisy, cognitive dissonance | 反义词: clear thinking, logical reasoning | 词性: noun
