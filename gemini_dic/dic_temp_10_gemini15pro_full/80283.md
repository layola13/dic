going on:/ˈɡoʊɪŋ ɑːn/ (US) /ˈɡəʊɪŋ ɒn/ (UK)| idiom |发生，进行，继续，持续|Something that is happening; an event or series of events.| Example: I don't know what's going on. 我不知道发生了什么。| Example: There's a party going on at the neighbor's house. 邻居家正在举行派对。|近义词：happening, occurring, taking place|反义词: stopping, ending |idiom
