nocuous:/ˈnɒkjuəs/ (UK) /ˈnɑːkjuəs/ (US)| adj.|无害的，无毒的|Not harmful or poisonous; harmless.|例句：The substance is believed to be nocuous to humans.  这种物质被认为对人体无害。

例句：Many snakes are nocuous and pose no threat to people. 许多蛇是无害的，对人没有威胁。


近义词：harmless, innocent, innocuous, inoffensive, safe|反义词：harmful, noxious, poisonous, toxic, dangerous|词性：adjective
