half-translated:/ˌhæf trænsˈleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半翻译的，部分翻译的 | Partially translated; not fully translated from one language to another.  A text where only some parts have been rendered into the target language. | The document was half-translated, with some sections still in the original German. 这份文件只翻译了一半，有些部分仍然是德语。

The novel was half-translated when the translator fell ill. 小说翻译到一半时，译员病倒了。 | partially translated, incompletely translated | fully translated, completely translated | adjective
