agroindustrial:/ˌæɡroʊɪnˈdʌstriəl/ | adj. | 农业工业的 | Relating to or involved in both agriculture and industry, typically referring to the processing of agricultural products. | The country's agroindustrial sector is booming. 这个国家的农业工业部门正在蓬勃发展。|  The government is investing heavily in agroindustrial development. 政府正在大力投资农业工业发展。 | 近义词: agribusiness, agricultural-industrial | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, could consider pre-industrial or traditional farming) | 词性: adjective
