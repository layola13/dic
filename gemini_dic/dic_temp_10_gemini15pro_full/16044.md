banded:/ˈbændɪd/| adj.|有带的，有条纹的，联合的|Marked with bands; striped; united in a group or for a common purpose.|The banded snake slithered through the grass.  那条带状的蛇在草丛中滑行。|The researchers studied the banded coral shrimp. 研究人员研究了带状珊瑚虾。|Synonyms: striped, ringed, belted, allied, united|Antonyms: plain, unstriped, unbanded|adjective

banded:/ˈbændɪd/| v. (band的过去式和过去分词)|绑扎，联合|Past tense and past participle of "band," meaning to tie or fasten with a band, or to unite in a group.|The pigeons were banded for tracking purposes. 这些鸽子被绑上了带子以便追踪。|The workers banded together to demand better wages. 工人们联合起来要求更高的工资。|Synonyms: tied, fastened, joined, united|Antonyms: separated, divided|verb
