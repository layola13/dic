diglyph:/ˈdaɪɡlɪf/| n.|双字母，二合字母|A diglyph is a sequence of two letters or graphemes that represent one speech sound (phoneme).|The “ph” in “phone” is a diglyph representing the /f/ sound.|近义词：digraph|反义词:|名词
