divest:/daɪˈvɛst/ /dɪˈvɛst/| vt.|剥夺，脱去，卸下，使摆脱|To deprive or dispossess, especially of property, authority, or title; also, to rid oneself of something.|The company is divesting itself of its less profitable subsidiaries. 这家公司正在剥离其盈利较低的子公司。|近义词：strip, deprive, rid, dispose of|反义词：invest, acquire, endow|verb

divest:/daɪˈvɛst/ /dɪˈvɛst/| vt.|出售，转让（股份等）|To sell off or dispose of (assets, investments, etc.).|The government decided to divest its holdings in the telecommunications company. 政府决定出售其持有的电信公司股份。|近义词：sell, liquidate, unload|反义词：buy, purchase, acquire|verb
