dolabra:/dɒˈleɪbrə/ /doʊˈleɪbrə/| n. |锄斧; 镐锄; |A pickaxe-like tool used in ancient Rome for digging, chopping, and other tasks. It had a blade on one side and an axe or adze head on the other.|The legionary used his dolabra to dig a trench. 军团士兵用他的锄斧挖了一条沟渠。|近义词：pickaxe, mattock, axe, adze|反义词: None (Tools don't typically have antonyms)|Noun
