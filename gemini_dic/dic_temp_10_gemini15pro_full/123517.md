monotonic:/məˈnɒtənɪk/ (US) /ˌmɑːnəˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|单调的，单一的|Characterized by a constant increase or decrease; lacking variation.|The monotonic sound of the dripping faucet drove me crazy. 单调的水龙头滴水声快把我逼疯了。|近义词:unchanging, unvaried, tedious|反义词:varied, diverse, fluctuating|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *mónos* "single, alone" + *tonos* "tone, pitch, accent".
