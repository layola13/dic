nonbeneficed: /ˌnɒnbɛˈnɪfɪst/ | adj. |  无俸禄的；无圣俸的 | Not having a benefice (a church appointment that provides a fixed income). |  The nonbeneficed clergy often struggle financially. 无俸圣职人员经常在经济上苦苦挣扎。| 近义词: unbeneficed | 反义词: beneficed | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + beneficed, from  Late Latin beneficium "favor, kindness, benefit," from bene "well" + facere "to do."
