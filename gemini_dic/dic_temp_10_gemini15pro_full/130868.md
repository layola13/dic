nonarchitecture:/ˌnɑːnˈɑːrkɪˌtɛktʃər/
n.|非建筑; 非建筑学|The state of not being architecture; something that does not conform to the principles or aesthetics of architecture.| The sprawling, unplanned development on the outskirts of the city was a testament to nonarchitecture. 城市郊区杂乱无章、无规划的开发证明了非建筑的存在。|  The hastily erected temporary shelters could only be described as nonarchitecture. 仓促搭建的临时住所只能说是“非建筑”。 |Near synonyms: non-building, non-structure |Antonyms: architecture, building, structure
