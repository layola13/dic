gold:/ɡoʊld/ (US) /ɡəʊld/ (UK)| n. adj.|金，黄金；金色；金色的|A precious yellow metal, highly valued for its beauty and rarity. Also, having the color of gold.|

例句：
The ring is made of pure gold.  这枚戒指是由纯金制成的。

She won a gold medal at the Olympics. 她在奥运会上赢得了一枚金牌。


近义词：aureate, golden|反义词:silver|词性:名词, 形容词
