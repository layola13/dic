boondoggle:/ˈbuːndɒɡl/ (US) /ˈbuːndɒɡl/ (UK)| n. | 浪费时间或金钱的琐碎而无用的工作或活动；无用的装饰物 | a project or activity that is wasteful or pointless but gives the appearance of having value; a braided cord or thong, often brightly colored, originally made by Boy Scouts and used for various purposes, such as a lanyard or keychain |  The new airport is considered by many to be a white elephant, a costly boondoggle.  许多人认为新机场是一个华而不实的东西，一个昂贵的、无用的工程。 | synonyms: waste, extravagance, folly, triviality | antonyms: necessity, essential, practicality | noun

boondoggle:/ˈbuːndɒɡl/ (US) /ˈbuːndɒɡl/ (UK)| v. |  浪费时间或金钱做琐碎而无用的工作 | to waste time or money on trivial or useless activities | The committee boondoggled for months before producing a useless report.  委员会浪费了几个月的时间，最终却只拿出了一份毫无用处的报告。 | synonyms: waste, squander, fritter away | antonyms: utilize, exploit, make good use of | verb
