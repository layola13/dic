confraternity:/ˌkɒnfrəˈtɜːnɪti/ /ˌkɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnəti/
n.
宗教兄弟会，联谊会，同行
a brotherhood, especially one of a religious character; any association of men for a common purpose
例句：He joined a confraternity dedicated to helping the poor. 他加入了一个致力于帮助穷人的宗教兄弟会。

例句：The local artists formed a confraternity to promote their work.  当地艺术家们组成了一个联谊会来推广他们的作品。

近义词：brotherhood, fellowship, association, society, guild, league
反义词：  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示敌对或孤立的词)  rivalry, isolation
词性：noun
