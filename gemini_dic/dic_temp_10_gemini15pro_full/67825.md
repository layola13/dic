n.|目击，见证|The act of seeing something happen, especially a crime or accident.|She described her eyewitnessing of the robbery to the police. 她向警方描述了她目击抢劫案的过程。|近义词: observing, seeing, witnessing|反义词: ignoring, overlooking|词性: Noun (Gerund)

-ing:/ɪŋ/
suffix.|进行时态后缀；动名词后缀|Used to form the present participle of verbs and gerunds.|He is running. 他正在跑步.  Running is good exercise. 跑步是很好的运动。|近义词: N/A|反义词: N/A|词性: Suffix

词源:
From "eye" (Old English ēage) + "witness" (Middle English witnes, from Old English witnes, meaning "knowledge, testimony").  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb "witness" into a gerund, creating the noun "eyewitnessing."
