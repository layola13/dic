melampyrite:/ˌmɛlæmˈpaɪraɪt/ |n.|红锌矿，红锌矿石|A rare mineral consisting of zinc and iron sulfide, typically reddish-brown in color.  It is a source of zinc. |Melampyrite is sometimes found in association with sphalerite. 红锌矿有时与闪锌矿伴生。|Synonym:  roeblingite (partially)|Antonym: N/A|noun
