diiambo:/ˌdaɪˈæmboʊ/| n.| 双音步,抑扬格| A metrical foot consisting of two syllables, a short or unstressed syllable followed by a long or stressed syllable. | The poem is written in diiambs. 这首诗是用抑扬格写的。 | Example:  The SUN | is UP.  太阳升起来了。| synonym: iamb, iambus | antonym: trochee | noun
