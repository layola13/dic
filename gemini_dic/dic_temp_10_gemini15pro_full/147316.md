overmoralistic: /ˌoʊvərˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
过分道德化的；过分说教的
Excessively concerned with morals; tending to judge harshly based on one's own moral standards.  Characterized by imposing or advocating a moral standard considered too strict.
She gave him an overmoralistic lecture about the dangers of drinking. 她对他进行了一场关于饮酒危害的说教，显得过分道德化。
His overmoralistic attitude alienated many of his friends. 他过分说教的态度疏远了他的许多朋友。

近义词: moralistic, puritanical, self-righteous, sanctimonious
反义词: amoral, immoral, lenient, permissive
词性: adjective
