haemodynamics:/ˌhiːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ /ˌheməʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/| n. |血流动力学|The study of the physical principles governing blood flow and the forces involved.|例句：The haemodynamics of the circulatory system are complex. 循环系统的血流动力学很复杂。

例句：Monitoring haemodynamics is crucial during surgery. 手术过程中监测血流动力学至关重要。|近义词：hemodynamics, circulatory dynamics|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek αἷμα (haîma, “blood”) + dynamics.
