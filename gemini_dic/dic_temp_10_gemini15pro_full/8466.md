antiarthritic:/ˌæntiɑːrˈθrɪtɪk/

antiarthritic:/ˌæntiɑːrˈθrɪtɪk/
noun|抗关节炎药|A medication or treatment that relieves or prevents arthritis.|She was prescribed a new antiarthritic to manage her pain. 她被开了一种新的抗关节炎药来控制疼痛。|近义词:anti-inflammatory, antirheumatic|反义词:None|词性:noun
