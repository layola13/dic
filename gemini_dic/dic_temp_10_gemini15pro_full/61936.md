emulgent:/ɪˈmʌldʒənt/
adjective|乳化的；使软化的|Having the power of softening or relaxing; soothing, especially to the bowels; aperient.|The doctor prescribed an emulgent medicine to relieve the patient's constipation.|近义词：laxative, purgative, aperient, softening, demulcent|反义词：astringent, binding|词性: adjective
noun|润肠剂，软化剂|A substance that softens or soothes; an aperient.|The emulgent helped to ease his discomfort.|近义词：laxative, purgative, aperient|反义词：astringent|词性: noun
