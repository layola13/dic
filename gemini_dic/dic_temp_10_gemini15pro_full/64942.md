esteemable:/ɪˈstiːməbl/
adjective
值得尊敬的；可估计的
worthy of esteem; capable of being estimated
He is an esteemed and estimable gentleman. 他是一位受人尊敬且值得敬重的绅士。
近义词：respectable, admirable, worthy, honorable
反义词：contemptible, despicable, disreputable
词性：adjective
词源：From Late Latin aestimabilis, from aestimare "to estimate, value," from aes "bronze, money" (from PIE root *ois- "gold") + -timare frequentative of PIE root *da- "to divide."  The sense evolution is from "valuable" to "worthy of esteem."
