bailing: /ˈbeɪlɪŋ/ | n. v. | 保释，舀水，放弃 | The act of releasing someone from custody by paying bail.  The act of removing water from a boat. The act of abandoning a difficult situation. | He was released on bail after his arrest. 他被捕后获得保释。  They were bailing water out of the sinking boat. 他们正在将水从下沉的船中舀出。  He was bailing on the project at the last minute. 他在最后一刻放弃了这个项目。 | 近义词：(for releasing from custody) releasing, freeing; (for removing water) scooping, ladling; (for abandoning) abandoning, deserting, quitting | 反义词：(for releasing from custody) imprisoning, detaining; (for removing water) flooding; (for abandoning) supporting, staying, persisting | 词性: noun, verb
词源：From Middle English *bailen, from Old French baillier (“to control, have charge of, deliver, enclose”), from Late Latin bajulare (“to bear a burden”), from Latin bajulus (“porter, carrier”).
