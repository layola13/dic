passee:/ˈpæseɪ/ (US), /pɑːˈseɪ/ (UK)| adj.|过时的，落伍的，不再流行的|No longer fashionable or popular; out of date.|Her style is a little passé. 她的风格有点过时了。|例句：That kind of music is passé now. 那种音乐现在已经过时了。|例句：Wearing a hat to a baseball game is passé. 戴帽子去看棒球比赛已经过时了。|近义词：outdated, old-fashioned, outmoded, unfashionable|反义词：modern, fashionable, contemporary, trendy|词性:形容词

词源: From French passé, past participle of passer (“to pass”).
