half-Greek:/ˌhɑːf ˈɡriːk/ | adj. | 半希腊的；混血的（一半希腊血统） |  Of mixed Greek and other ancestry.  A person who has one parent of Greek descent and one parent of another descent. |  Her mother was English and her father Greek, making her half-Greek.  她母亲是英国人，父亲是希腊人，所以她是半个希腊人。| Near synonyms: half-blood, mixed-race | Opposite: pure-blooded, full-blooded | adjective
