lepers:/ˈlɛpərz/| n.|麻风病人；受蔑视的人|A person who has leprosy, a chronic infectious disease that affects the skin, nerves, and mucous membranes.  Figuratively, it can also refer to someone who is ostracized or marginalized.|In biblical times, lepers were often ostracized from society. 在圣经时代，麻风病人经常被社会排斥。|The homeless are often treated like modern-day lepers.  无家可归者经常被像现代麻风病人一样对待。|Synonyms: outcast, pariah, untouchable|Antonyms: insider, accepted, mainstream|noun
