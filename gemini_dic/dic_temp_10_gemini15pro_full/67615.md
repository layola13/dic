extraverting:/ˈɛkstrəvɜːtɪŋ/
verb
外向；使外向
Turning something outwards or making something more extroverted.
She is extraverting her personality by joining a drama club. 她通过加入戏剧俱乐部来展现她的外向性格。
He's been extraverting his previously shy son by encouraging him to participate in group activities. 他一直鼓励他以前害羞的儿子参加集体活动，使他变得更加外向。
近义词: externalizing, expressing
反义词: introverting, internalizing
词性: verb

extravert:/ˈɛkstrəvɜːt/

Being an extravert, he thrives in social situations. 作为一个外向的人，他在社交场合很活跃。
