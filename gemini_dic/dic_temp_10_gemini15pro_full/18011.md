beggestere:/ˈbɛɡəstərə/ (Middle English pronunciation approximation - no modern equivalent)| n.| female beggar; beggar woman|A woman who begs; a female beggar.  This word is archaic and no longer in common use. It comes from Middle English.  The modern equivalent would be "beggar woman" or simply "beggar".|Example:  The beggestere approached the knight, pleading for alms. (The beggar woman approached the knight, pleading for alms.)|近义词: beggar, mendicant, pauper|反义词: philanthropist, benefactor|词性: noun
