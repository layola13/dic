horrify:/ˈhɒrɪfaɪ/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrɪfaɪ/ (US)| v. |使恐惧，使震惊|to cause someone to feel extremely shocked or disgusted.|The news of the massacre horrified the world.  大屠杀的消息震惊了世界。

The violent images in the film horrified many viewers.  电影中的暴力画面吓坏了许多观众。|近义词：terrify, appall, shock, disgust|反义词：delight, please, soothe|词性:动词
词源: From Late Latin *horrificāre, from Latin horrificus (“causing horror”), from horror (“horror, dread”) + -ificus (“-making”).
