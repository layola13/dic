eked-out:/iktˈaʊt/| adj.|补充的，勉强维持的| supplemented; made to last by adding something extra or by stretching resources; meagerly and laboriously maintained |  They eked out a living by selling vegetables from their garden. 他们靠卖自家花园里的蔬菜勉强维持生计。|近义词:stretched, supplemented, augmented, bolstered, sustained, spun out, scraped by|反义词:squandered, wasted, depleted|词性:形容词

eked-out:/iktˈaʊt/| v.|勉强维持，补充|to make a small supply of something such as money or food last longer by carefully using very small amounts of it| They eked out their meager income by growing vegetables. 他们通过种植蔬菜来补充微薄的收入。|近义词:stretch, supplement, augment, bolster, sustain, spin out, scrape by|反义词:squander, waste, deplete|词性:动词
