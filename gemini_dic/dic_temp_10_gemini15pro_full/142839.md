out-dodged:/ˌaʊtˈdɒdʒd/ | v. | 比...更善于躲避; 以躲避胜过 | Successfully avoided someone or something by dodging. | The fox out-dodged the hounds. 狐狸比猎犬更善于躲避，成功逃脱了。 | The spy out-dodged the security cameras.  间谍成功躲避了监控摄像头。 | Synonyms: outmaneuver, outwit, evade, elude | Antonyms: surrender, submit | verb
