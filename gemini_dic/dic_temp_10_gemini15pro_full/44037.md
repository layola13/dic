词源: From Middle English *crimsin, from Old Spanish cremesín (“crimson”), from Medieval Latin cremesinus (“kermes-colored”), from Arabic قرمز‎ (qirmiz, “kermes”), from Sanskrit कृमिज (kṛmija, “produced by a worm”). Kermes refers to a red dye derived from insects.
