arblast:/ˈɑːrblæst/ | n. |  弩炮；石弩 | A medieval siege engine for hurling large stones or other missiles. | The arblast was a powerful weapon in siege warfare. 弩炮是攻城战中的强大武器。| The arblast hurled a massive stone towards the castle walls. 弩炮向城堡城墙投掷了一块巨石。 | synonyms: ballista, catapult, trebuchet | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *arbaleste, from Late Latin arcubalista, from Latin arcus "bow" + ballista "catapult".  Ultimately from Greek βάλλειν (ballein, “to throw”).
