nontenablenesses: /ˌnɒnˈtɛnəbəlnɪsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛnəbəlnɪsɪz/ | n. | 不可维持性；站不住脚 | The nontenableness of his position became increasingly clear. 他的立场越来越站不住脚。|  The report exposed the nontenablenesses of the government's economic policy.  这份报告揭露了政府经济政策的不可维持性。 | 近义词: untenability, instability, precariousness | 反义词: stability, soundness, viability | 词性: 名词

词源:  From nontenable + -ness.  Nontenable comes from Old French *non tenable, from non- "not" + tenable "holdable", from tenir "to hold".
