former:/ˈfɔːrmər/ (US) /ˈfɔːmə(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.|以前的，前任的；前者|Something that is former existed earlier in time but no longer exists now.  It can also refer to the first of two things mentioned.|例句：My former boss is now a successful entrepreneur. 我以前的老板现在是一位成功的企业家。|近义词：previous, prior, past, erstwhile, preceding|反义词：latter, current, present, subsequent|词性:形容词

former:/ˈfɔːrmər/ (US) /ˈfɔːmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|前者|Refers to the first of two things or people mentioned.|例句：Of the two options, the former is more appealing. 在这两个选项中，前者更有吸引力。|近义词：first|反义词：latter|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *former*, from Old English *forma* ("first"), from Proto-Germanic *furmō* ("first"), from Proto-Indo-European *pr̥h₂mós* ("foremost, first").  Cognate with Old Frisian *forma*, Old Saxon *formo*, Old High German *formo* (German *vor*), Old Norse *fyrmr*, Gothic *𐍆𐍂𐌿𐌼𐌰* (*fruma*).
