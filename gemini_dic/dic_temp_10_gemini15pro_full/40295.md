continuous:/kənˈtɪnjuəs/ (US), /kənˈtɪnjuːəs/ (UK)| adj.|连续的，持续的|forming an unbroken whole; without interruption.|The continuous noise of the traffic kept me awake.  交通的持续噪音让我无法入睡。|Synonyms: constant, incessant, uninterrupted, unbroken|Antonyms: intermittent, discontinuous, sporadic|adjective

continuous:/kənˈtɪnjuəs/ (US), /kənˈtɪnjuːəs/ (UK)| adj.|延伸的，连绵不断的|extending or prolonged for a long time.|The mountains formed a continuous chain along the horizon.  群山沿着地平线形成连绵不断的山脉。|Synonyms: extended, prolonged, ongoing|Antonyms: short, brief, limited|adjective
