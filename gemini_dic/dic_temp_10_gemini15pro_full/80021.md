go-the-vole:/ˌɡoʊ ðə ˈvoʊl/ | n. | 走田鼠; 赌博狂; 亡命之徒 | A reckless or desperate person, especially one who gambles heavily.  Origin uncertain, possibly from "vole," a small rodent known for its prolific breeding and thus suggesting a person who quickly squanders their resources. | He was a real go-the-vole, betting his entire savings on a single horse race. 他真是个亡命之徒，把全部积蓄都押在一场赛马上了。 | 近义词:gambler, risk-taker, daredevil | 反义词:conservative, cautious | 词性:名词
