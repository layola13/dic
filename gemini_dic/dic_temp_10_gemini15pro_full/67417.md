exterraneous:/ˌekstəˈreɪniəs/
adjective
外来的；无关的；非必要的
Coming from outside; not belonging to or part of something; not essential
例句：The exterraneous noise from the street made it hard to concentrate. 街上传来的外来噪音使人难以集中注意力。

例句：Exterraneous information is not relevant to the main topic of the essay.  无关信息与论文的主题无关。
synonym: external, foreign, irrelevant, extraneous, immaterial
antonym: internal, inherent, relevant, essential, intrinsic
词性: adjective
