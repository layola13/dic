hyperemotionally:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəli/
adverb
过度情绪化地; 过于感情用事地
in a hyperemotional way; excessively emotional
She reacted hyperemotionally to the news. 她对这个消息反应过度情绪化。
近义词:overly emotional, excessively sentimental, dramatically, hysterically
反义词:calmly, rationally, stoically
词性:adverb

hyperemotionally:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊʃənəli/
副词
过度情绪化地; 过于感情用事地
in a hyperemotional way; excessively emotional
He spoke hyperemotionally about the incident. 他非常情绪化地谈论了这件事。
近义词:over-the-top, histrionic, melodramatic
反义词:apathetically, impassively, indifferently
词性:adverb
