homophone:/ˈhɒməfəʊn/ (UK) /ˈhɑːməfoʊn/ (US)| n. |同音词|A word that is pronounced the same as another word but has a different meaning, and often a different spelling.|例句：
"To," "too," and "two" are homophones.
“To”、“too”和“two”是同音词。

The words "flower" and "flour" are homophones.
“flower”（花）和“flour”（面粉）是同音词。

|近义词：homonym|反义词: heteronym|词性:noun
