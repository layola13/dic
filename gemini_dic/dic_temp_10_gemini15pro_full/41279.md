cordialize:/ˈkɔːrdʒəlaɪz/ /ˈkɔːrdiəlaɪz/
verb
使友好；使热诚；使兴奋
To make cordial; to render warm, affectionate, or hearty.
The two countries cordialized their relationship after years of tension.  两国在多年紧张关系后，关系变得友好。
The host cordialized the atmosphere with his warm welcome.  主人用热情的欢迎使气氛变得友好。
近义词:befriend, fraternize
反义词:alienate, estrange
词性:v.

Etymology:
From cordial + -ize.  From Late Latin cordiālis "of or for the heart," from Latin cord- "heart".
