fratery: /ˈfreɪtəri/ | n. | 兄弟情谊; 友爱 | A feeling of friendship and brotherhood. | There was a strong sense of fratery among the soldiers. 士兵之间有着强烈的兄弟情谊。| synonyms: brotherhood, camaraderie, fellowship | antonyms: animosity, hostility | noun
