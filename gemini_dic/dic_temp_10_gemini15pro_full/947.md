academian:/ˌækəˈdiːmiən/ | n. |学者; 大学教师; 研究员| A person who is a member of an academy or society of scholars or artists, especially one who is a specialist in a particular branch of learning; a scholar or university teacher. |  He is a respected academician in the field of linguistics. 他是语言学领域受尊敬的学者。

The academician gave a lecture on the history of art.  这位学者做了一个关于艺术史的讲座。 | 近义词: scholar, intellectual, academic, professor | 反义词: ignoramus, philistine | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *académicien*, from *académie* (academy) + *-ien* (suffix forming nouns denoting persons). Ultimately from Greek *Ἀκαδήμεια* (Akadḗmeia), the name of a grove near Athens where Plato taught.
