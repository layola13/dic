debunking:/diːˈbʌŋkɪŋ/
n.|揭穿；驳斥；拆穿|the act of exposing the falseness or hollowness of a myth, idea, or claim.|The scientist dedicated her career to debunking popular myths about nutrition.  这位科学家毕生致力于揭穿关于营养的流行谬论。|exposing, discrediting, refuting|confirming, validating|noun
debunk:/diːˈbʌŋk/
v.|揭穿；驳斥；拆穿|expose the falseness or hollowness of (a myth, idea, or claim).|The article debunks the myth that eating fat makes you fat. 这篇文章揭穿了吃脂肪会使人发胖的谬论。|expose, discredit, refute|confirm, validate|verb
