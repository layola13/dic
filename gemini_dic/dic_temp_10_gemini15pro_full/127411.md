nearsides:/ˈnɪəˌsaɪdz/| adj.|近侧的；左侧的|Situated on or relating to the side of a vehicle or other object that is nearest the kerb or edge of the road, especially the left side in countries where traffic drives on the right.|例句：The nearsides front tyre is flat. 近侧前胎瘪了。|例句：Check the nearsides mirror before turning left. 左转前检查左侧后视镜。|近义词：left-hand (in right-hand traffic countries), driver's side (in right-hand traffic countries)|反义词：offside, far side|词性: 形容词
