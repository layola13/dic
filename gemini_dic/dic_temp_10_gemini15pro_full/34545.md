clear-cut:/ˌklɪrˈkʌt/| adj.|清晰的，明确的，明显的|sharply defined; easy to perceive or understand|The evidence against him was clear-cut.  证据确凿地表明他有罪。

The company has clear-cut goals for the next five years.  这家公司对未来五年有明确的目标。|近义词：definite, obvious, unambiguous, evident, precise|反义词：ambiguous, vague, uncertain, indefinite, obscure|词性:形容词

clear-cut:/ˌklɪrˈkʌt/| n.|皆伐|The act or process of cutting down all the trees in an area.|The clear-cut left the hillside bare and vulnerable to erosion.  皆伐使得山坡光秃秃的，容易受到侵蚀。

The environmental impact of clear-cut logging is devastating. 皆伐对环境的影响是毁灭性的。|近义词：clearfelling, deforestation|反义词：afforestation, reforestation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From clear + cut.  Meaning "sharply defined" is from 1878; of timber, from 1903.
