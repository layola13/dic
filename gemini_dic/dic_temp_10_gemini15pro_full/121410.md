misinfer:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɜːr/ /ˌmɪsɪnˈfɚ/
verb
错误地推断；误解
To infer incorrectly; to draw a wrong conclusion.
He misinferred her silence as agreement. 他把她的沉默误解为同意。
misinterpret, misconstrue, misunderstand
correctly infer, understand correctly
verb
