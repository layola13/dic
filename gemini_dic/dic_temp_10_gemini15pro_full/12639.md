assay:/ˈæseɪ/ /əˈseɪ/
n.|化验，分析；试验；尝试|a laboratory test to determine the content or quality of a metal or ore|The geologist performed an assay on the rock sample. 地质学家对岩石样本进行了化验。
vt.|化验，分析；试验；尝试|to test, analyze, or evaluate|They assayed the gold to determine its purity. 他们化验了黄金以确定其纯度。
近义词:test, analyze, evaluate, examine
反义词:synthesize
词性:名词,动词
