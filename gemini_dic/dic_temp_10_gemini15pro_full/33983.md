citable:/ˈsaɪtəbl/| adj.|可引用的，可被记载的|Something citable is worthy of being cited or quoted, especially in a scholarly work.  It refers to something that is credible and authoritative enough to be used as a source or reference. |例句：Her research paper was filled with citable sources. 她的研究论文充满了可引用的资料。

例句：The judge's ruling established a citable precedent. 法官的裁决确立了一个可引用的先例。|近义词：quotable, referable, mentionable|反义词: unreliable, unmentionable|词性:adjective
cite:/saɪt/| vt.|引用，引证；表彰，嘉奖|To quote (a passage, book, author, etc.) as evidence for or justification of an argument or statement, especially in a scholarly work.|词源: From Latin citāre ‘to summon, urge, rouse’, frequentative of ciēre ‘to move’.|词性:verb
