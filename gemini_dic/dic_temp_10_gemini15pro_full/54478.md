disinterested:/dɪsˈɪntrəstɪd/ | adj. | 公正的，无私的；不感兴趣的 |  Free from bias or self-interest; not influenced by personal considerations. Also, having no interest in something; indifferent. |  The judge was disinterested in the outcome of the case, ensuring a fair trial. 法官对案件的结果不感兴趣，确保了审判的公正。

She seemed disinterested in the conversation and kept looking at her phone. 她似乎对谈话不感兴趣，一直看着她的手机。 | 近义词：impartial, unbiased, neutral, indifferent, apathetic | 反义词：biased, prejudiced, partial, interested, keen | 词性: adjective
