packetized:/ˈpækɪtaɪzd/| adj.|打包的，分组化的|Converted into or transmitted as packets.|The data is packetized before being sent over the network. 数据在通过网络发送之前被打包。|The packetized voice was clear and easy to understand. 打包后的语音清晰易懂。|Synonym: segmented, fragmented|Antonym: unsegmented, integrated|adjective
