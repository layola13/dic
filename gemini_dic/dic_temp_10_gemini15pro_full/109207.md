lehman:/ˈliːmən/| n. |雷曼; 雷曼兄弟(公司名)| Lehman Brothers Holdings Inc. was a global financial services firm.  Before filing for bankruptcy in 2008, Lehman was the fourth-largest investment bank in the United States (behind Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, and Merrill Lynch).| The collapse of Lehman Brothers triggered a global financial crisis. 雷曼兄弟的倒闭引发了全球金融危机。|Synonym:  Lehman Brothers (more specific), investment bank (more general) | Antonym: N/A | noun
