curatic: /kjʊˈrætɪk/ | adj. | 策展的，策展式的 | Relating to or characteristic of a curator or the process of curation. |  The museum's curatic approach was innovative and engaging.  博物馆的策展方式新颖且引人入胜。 |  The gallery's curatic vision focused on emerging artists.  画廊的策展愿景集中在新兴艺术家身上。 | Synonyms: curatorial | Antonyms: uncurated, disorganized | adjective

Etymology:  From "curate" (v.) + "-ic" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Curate" comes from Latin "cura" (care, concern) and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*kōr-" (to heed, take care of).
