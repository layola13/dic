inspiringly:/ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋli/
adverb
鼓舞人心地，激励地
in a way that makes someone feel enthusiastic and eager to do something.
Her speech was inspiringly delivered, motivating the entire audience. 她的演讲非常鼓舞人心，激励了全体观众。
The teacher inspiringly presented the complex scientific theory. 老师以鼓舞人心的方式讲解了复杂的科学理论。
Synonyms: encouragingly, upliftingly, motivatingly, stimulatingly
Antonyms: discouragingly, depressingly, dishearteningly
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From inspire + -ing + -ly.  Inspire comes from the Latin inspirare "to breathe or blow into," figuratively "to infuse (a feeling, etc.), influence, prompt."
