flatlets:/ˈflæt.ləts/ (UK) /ˈflæt.lɪts/ (US)| n. |小公寓，套房|A small, self-contained apartment, usually part of a larger building.  It typically includes a living room, bedroom, kitchen, and bathroom.|例句：She found a cheap flatlet near the university. 她找到了大学附近一间便宜的小公寓。

例句：The building was converted into several flatlets for students.  这栋楼被改建成了几套学生公寓。

|近义词：apartment, studio, flat, bedsit|反义词:house, mansion|词性:noun
