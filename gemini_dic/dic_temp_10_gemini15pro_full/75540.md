fubsy:/ˈfʌbzi/
adjective
矮胖的，圆胖的；肥胖的
Short and stout; plump.  Having a short, stout, and somewhat rounded figure. Suggests a comfortable, cozy plumpness, often applied to animals or elderly people.
The fubsy cat curled up on the rug.  那只胖乎乎的猫蜷缩在地毯上。
近义词:plump, chubby, portly, stout, rotund
反义词:thin, slender, lean, skinny
词性:adjective

fubsy:/ˈfʌbzi/
adjective
（尤指衣服）过厚的，笨重的
(especially of clothes) thick and clumsy
She wore a fubsy coat that made her look even wider. 她穿着一件厚重的外套，使她看起来更胖了。
近义词: bulky, cumbersome, thick, heavy
反义词:light, thin, slim-fitting
词性:adjective
