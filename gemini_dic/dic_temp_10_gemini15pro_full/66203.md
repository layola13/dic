excitability:/ɪkˌsaɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
兴奋性，激动性
The excitability of nerve cells allows for rapid communication throughout the body.
神经细胞的兴奋性使得全身能够快速交流。
The excitability of the fans increased as the game went on.
随着比赛的进行，球迷们的兴奋性越来越高。
Synonym: irritability, responsiveness, sensitivity
Antonym:  inertness, apathy, impassivity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin excitabilitās, from Latin excitabilis (“excitable”), from excitāre (“to excite”).  Equivalent to excitable +‎ -ity.
