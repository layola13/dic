giftednesses:/ˈɡɪftɪdnɪsɪz/| n. |天赋，才能（复数）|The state of being gifted; exceptional talent or natural ability. It refers to the plural form of "giftedness," indicating multiple areas of talent or exceptional abilities in different individuals or within a single person.|She displayed remarkable giftednesses in both music and mathematics. 她展现了在音乐和数学方面非凡的天赋。|近义词：talents, abilities, aptitudes, capabilities|反义词：inabilities, inadequacies|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "gifted" + "-ness."  "Gifted" comes from the Old Norse word "gipt" meaning "gift" or "dowry." The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
