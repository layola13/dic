aphrasia:/əˈfreɪʒə/ /əˈfreɪziə/
n.
失语症,表达不能
Loss of the ability to express oneself by speech, writing, or signs, due to an injury or disease to the brain centers controlling language.
After the stroke, the patient experienced aphrasia and could no longer speak. 中风后，病人患上了失语症，不能再说话了。
近义词:dysphasia, loss of speech
反义词:fluency
词性:名词
