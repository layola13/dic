lore's:/lɔːrz/ |n.| 传说，知识，学问 |lore's refers to the traditional knowledge and stories, especially those of a particular group or culture, that are typically passed down orally. It is the possessive form of "lore". |The lore's of ancient Greece are fascinating. 古希腊的传说令人着迷。 | synonym: traditions, myths, legends, folklore | antonym: facts, reality | noun
