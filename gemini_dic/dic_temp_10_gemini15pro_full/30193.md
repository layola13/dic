celestine:/ˈsɛləstaɪn/ /ˈsɛləstiːn/ | n. adj.|天青石，蔚蓝色|A mineral, strontium sulfate (SrSO4), occurring in orthorhombic crystalline masses or in fibrous or granular form, typically pale blue in color. Also, a pale blue color.|例句：The celestine geode shimmered with an ethereal light. 天青石晶洞发出空灵的光芒。

|例句：She wore a dress of celestine blue. 她穿着一件天蓝色连衣裙。|近义词：azure, sky-blue, cerulean|反义词:dark blue, navy|词性: 名词, 形容词
Etymology: From Latin caelestis "heavenly," from caelum "sky, heaven."
