midpoints:/ˈmɪdpɔɪnts/ |n.|中点，中间点|The midpoint between A and B is C.  A和B的中点是C。|例句：The researchers calculated the midpoints of the lines. 研究人员计算了这些线的中间点。

例句：We'll meet at the midpoint between our two houses. 我们将在两家之间的中间点见面。|近义词：center, middle, halfway point|反义词:endpoint, extremity|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mid + point.  Mid is from Old English midd "middle," from Proto-Germanic *midja- "middle." Point is from Old French point "dot, punctuation mark, stitch, sharp end," from Latin punctum "prick, sting, puncture; point, small hole," from pungere "to prick, pierce, sting."
