overimitativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌɪmɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/
n.
过度模仿
The tendency to copy observed actions or behaviors, even those that are irrelevant or unnecessary to achieving a goal.
Children often display overimitativeness, copying even the smallest details of an adult's actions.  孩子们经常表现出过度模仿，即使是成人行为中最小的细节也会模仿。
Overimitativeness can be a valuable learning strategy, but it can also lead to inefficient behavior. 过度模仿可能是一种有价值的学习策略，但也可能导致低效的行为。
近义词: mimicry, emulation,  aping
反义词: independence, originality
词性: noun

Etymology:
From 'over-' (excessive) + 'imitative' (inclined to imitate) + '-ness' (forming nouns expressing a state or quality). 源于“over-”（过度）+“imitative”（倾向于模仿）+“-ness”（构成表示状态或性质的名词）。
