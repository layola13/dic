one-way:/ˌwʌnˈweɪ/ | adj.|单程的，单向的|Moving or operating in one direction only. |  Example: a one-way street 单行道 | Synonyms: unidirectional, single-direction | Antonyms: two-way, round-trip | adjective

one-way:/ˌwʌnˈweɪ/ | n.|单程票，单行道|A ticket for a journey in one direction only; a road allowing travel in only one direction. | Example:  I bought a one-way ticket to London. 我买了一张去伦敦的单程票。| Synonyms: single, unidirectional | Antonyms: return, round-trip | noun

one-way:/ˌwʌnˈweɪ/ | adv.|单程地，单向地|In one direction only. | Example: The street is one-way now. 这条街现在是单行道了。| Synonyms: unidirectionally, singly | Antonyms: two-way, round-trip | adverb
