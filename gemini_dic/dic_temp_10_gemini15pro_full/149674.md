paisleyest:/ˈpeɪzliɪst/
adjective
最佩斯利的; 佩斯利花纹最多的
most characteristic of or resembling paisley; having the most paisley
Her paisleyest scarf was her favorite. 她最佩斯利风的围巾是她的最爱。
近义词: most ornate, most decorative
反义词: plainest, simplest
词性: adjective


词源:
From paisley + -est (superlative suffix).  Paisley itself comes from the town of Paisley in Scotland, where paisley-patterned shawls were originally manufactured.
