bazoom:/bəˈzuːm/
|n.|隆隆声，快速移动（尤指车辆）的声音|A sudden loud noise, often used to describe the sound of rapid movement, especially of a vehicle.|The car went bazoom past us.|近义词:zoom, whoosh, whiz|反义词:crawl, creep|名词


Etymology: Imitative origin, echoing the sound it represents.  Likely a variation of "zoom."
