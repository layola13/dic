mowable:/ˈmoʊəbl/| adj.|可修剪的，可刈割的|Suitable for mowing; able to be mowed.  Something that can be cut with a lawnmower or similar tool.|The grass in the park is mowable. 公园里的草可以修剪。|近义词：clippable, trimmable|反义词:unmowable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mow + -able.  "Mow" comes from Old English *māwan, from Proto-Germanic *mēaną, from Proto-Indo-European *meh₁- (“to mow, reap”). The suffix "-able" comes from Old French -able and Latin -abilis, meaning "capable of being".
