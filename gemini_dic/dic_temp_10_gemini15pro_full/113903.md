madrigalianism:/ˌmædrɪˈɡeɪliənɪzəm/
|n.| 牧歌风格；牧歌体裁；牧歌精神 |The quality or style characteristic of a madrigal. A tendency towards elaborate or sentimental expression in art, literature, or music, reminiscent of the style of madrigals. | The composer's later works show a marked madrigalianism.  这位作曲家后期的作品展现出明显的牧歌风格。|Synonyms: lyricism, melodiousness, musicality|Antonyms: dissonance, cacophony|noun
