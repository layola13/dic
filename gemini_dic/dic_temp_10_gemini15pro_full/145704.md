overcourteous: /ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtiəs/ | adj. | 过分礼貌的，过分殷勤的 | Excessively polite or courteous, often to the point of being insincere or obsequious.  | He was so overcourteous that it felt fake. 他太殷勤了，感觉很假。 |  She gave an overcourteous bow. 她行了一个过分谦恭的鞠躬。 | 近义词: obsequious, unctuous, fawning | 反义词: rude, impolite, discourteous | 词性: adjective

词源:
From over- + courteous.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Courteous" comes from Old French "corteis," meaning "having courtly manners."
