exagitate: /ɪɡˈzædʒɪteɪt/
|v.|激怒；使…烦躁；使…加剧|To make (someone) annoyed or agitated.|The constant noise exagitated the already stressed students.  持续不断的噪音让已经压力很大的学生们更加烦躁。|The traffic jam further exagitated his frustration. 交通堵塞使他更加沮丧。| 近义词:irritate, annoy, provoke, exacerbate, worsen|反义词:calm, soothe, pacify, alleviate|词性:动词
词源: From Latin exagitat-, past participle stem of exagitare "to drive out, harass," from ex- "out" + agitare "to drive, agitate"
