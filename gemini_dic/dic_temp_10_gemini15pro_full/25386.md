bulbous:/ˈbʌlbəs/ | adj. | 球茎状的；膨胀的；圆胖的 | Fat, round, or bulging. Resembling a bulb, especially in roundness. |  The plant has a bulbous root.  这种植物有球茎状的根。

The man had a rather bulbous nose.  这个男人有一个相当圆胖的鼻子。| 近义词: rounded, swollen, bulging, protuberant | 反义词: slender, thin, narrow | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin bulbosus, from Latin bulbus (“bulb, onion”).
