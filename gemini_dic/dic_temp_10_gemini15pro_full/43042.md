cowhided:/ˈkaʊhaɪdɪd/
adjective
用牛皮鞭打的; 鞭打的
Whipped or flogged with a cowhide.
例句：The cowhided prisoner bore the scars of his punishment.  用牛皮鞭打的囚犯身上带着惩罚的伤疤。

例句：He felt cowhided and humiliated after the public reprimand. 在公开谴责之后，他感到受到了鞭打和羞辱。
近义词：whipped, flogged, lashed, beaten
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "pampered," "coddled")
词性: adjective
