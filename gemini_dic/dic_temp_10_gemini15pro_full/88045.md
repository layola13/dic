higher-up:/ˈhaɪər ˌʌp/ | n. | 高层；上级；上司 | A person of higher rank or authority in an organization. |  Example: The higher-ups decided to implement a new policy. 高层决定实施一项新政策。

Example: I'll have to check with the higher-ups before I can approve your request. 我必须先和上级确认一下才能批准你的请求。 | Synonyms: superior, boss, executive, senior, manager | Antonyms: subordinate, junior, underling | noun
