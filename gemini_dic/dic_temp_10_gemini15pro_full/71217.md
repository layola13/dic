fitful:/ˈfɪtfəl/| adj.|断断续续的，一阵阵的，间歇的|Occurring in fits and starts; not continuous or regular.|Her sleep was fitful and disturbed by nightmares. 她睡得很不安稳，被噩梦惊扰。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, spasmodic|反义词：continuous, steady, constant|词性:形容词

词源: From fit (n.) + -ful. Originally meaning "liable to fits or paroxysms," later generalized to any kind of intermittent activity.
