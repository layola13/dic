heli-skiing:/ˈheliːˌskiːɪŋ/
n.
直升机滑雪
The sport of being transported by helicopter to the top of a remote hill or mountain in order to ski down through deep, untouched snow.
We went heli-skiing in the Canadian Rockies. 我们去加拿大落基山脉进行了直升机滑雪。
近义词:backcountry skiing, off-piste skiing
反义词:resort skiing, on-piste skiing
词性:noun
