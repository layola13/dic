overcreep:/ˈoʊvərkriːp/
verb
蔓延；逐渐蔓延；侵蚀；侵占
To spread slowly and steadily over an area or surface, often in a way that is undesirable or unnoticed.
The ivy overcrept the wall of the old house. 常春藤蔓延了老房子的墙壁。
The sea is slowly overcreeping the coastal areas.  海水正慢慢侵蚀沿海地区。
近义词: encroach, overrun, infiltrate, invade
反义词: recede, withdraw, retreat
词性: v.

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix indicating excess or completeness) + "creep" (from Old English *creopan, meaning "to move slowly and stealthily"). The combination suggests a creeping motion that extends beyond normal bounds.
