loutish:/ˈlaʊtɪʃ/| adj.|粗鲁的；笨拙的；没教养的|Resembling or characteristic of a lout; awkward, clumsy, and unmannered.|He behaved in a loutish manner, pushing people out of his way.|近义词：boorish, churlish, oafish, clumsy, awkward|反义词：refined, graceful, polite, courteous|词性:形容词
词源:From lout + -ish.
