hoick:/hɔɪk/ | v. |猛地拉起; 提升; 扔; 逐出|To lift or pull something with a sudden movement; to throw something carelessly; to expel someone from a place. |例句：He hoicked the heavy suitcase onto the rack. 他猛地把沉重的行李箱举到架子上。

例句：She hoicked the ball over the fence. 她把球扔过了篱笆。

近义词：jerk, yank, heave, chuck, boot out |反义词: lower, drop |词性: 动词


hoicked:/hɔɪkt/ | v. |猛地拉起（hoick的过去式和过去分词）|Past tense and past participle of "hoick."|例句：He hoicked himself out of the chair. 他猛地从椅子上站起来。

例句：They hoicked him out of the club for bad behaviour. 他们因为他行为不端而把他赶出了俱乐部。


近义词：jerked, yanked, heaved, chucked |反义词: lowered, dropped |词性: 动词
