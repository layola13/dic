bystanders:/ˈbaɪˌstændərz/
n.
旁观者，局外人
people who witness an event but are not involved in it
The bystanders watched in horror as the accident unfolded. 旁观者惊恐地看着事故发生。

Several bystanders rushed to help the victim. 几名旁观者冲过去帮助受害者。

onlookers, witnesses, observers
participants, actors

noun
