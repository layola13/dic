nonresponsibly:/ˌnɒnrɪˈspɒnsɪbli/ | adv. | 不负责任地 | In a manner that avoids or neglects responsibility; carelessly. | He behaved nonresponsibly by ignoring his duties. 他不负责任地忽视了自己的职责。|  She spent her money nonresponsibly and ended up in debt. 她不负责任地花钱，最后负债累累。| 近义词: irresponsibly, carelessly, recklessly | 反义词: responsibly, carefully, conscientiously | 词性: 副词
