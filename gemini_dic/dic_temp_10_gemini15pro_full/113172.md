lurchers:/ˈlɜːrtʃərz/
n.
猎犬（尤指杂交猎犬）, 偷袭者
Dogs, especially cross-bred ones, often of a type formerly used for hunting hares by coursing, or a person who moves in a furtive or hesitant way.
例句：The lurchers strained at their leashes, eager to chase the rabbit. 猎犬拼命拉扯着皮带，渴望追逐兔子。

例句：He lurked in the shadows like a lurcher, waiting for his opportunity. 他像个偷袭者一样潜伏在阴影中，等待时机。
近义词：hounds, poachers
反义词: protectors, guardians
词性: 名词 (plural)

lurcher:/ˈlɜːrtʃər/
n.

例句：The lurcher was a mix of greyhound and collie. 这只猎犬是灰狗和柯利牧羊犬的杂交品种。

例句：He felt like a lurcher, sneaking around the back of the building. 他感觉自己像个偷袭者，在建筑物后面鬼鬼祟祟地走动。
近义词：hound, poacher
反义词: protector, guardian
词性: 名词 (singular)
