anticrack:/ˌæntiˈkræk/| adj.|防裂的，抗裂的|Designed to prevent cracking.  Used especially for materials or coatings.|例句：They used an anticrack additive in the concrete. 他们在混凝土中使用了防裂添加剂。|例句：The anticrack paint protected the walls from developing hairline fractures.  这种抗裂涂料保护墙壁不产生细微裂缝。|近义词：crack-resistant, shatterproof, fracture-resistant|反义词:fragile, brittle|词性:形容词
