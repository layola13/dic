galt:/ɡɔːlt/ | adj. n.| 鲁莽的，轻率的；鲁莽的人，轻率的人 | A Scottish word meaning reckless, rash, or playful, sometimes also used as a noun to refer to a person who is reckless or rash.  Derived from the Scots word "galt," meaning "frolicsome" or "playful." It's primarily used in Scottish English and can have slightly different connotations depending on context, sometimes implying a playful recklessness rather than outright foolishness. | He made a galt decision to invest all his money in a single stock. 他轻率地决定把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|  He's such a galt, always getting into trouble. 他真是个鲁莽的人，总是惹麻烦。| 近义词: reckless, rash, impetuous, impulsive | 反义词: cautious, careful, prudent | 词性: adjective, noun
