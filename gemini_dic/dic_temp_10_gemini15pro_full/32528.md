chinwag:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡ/| n. |闲聊，聊天|A friendly, informal conversation. |例句：We had a good chinwag over a cup of tea. 我们边喝茶边愉快地聊了会儿天。|近义词：chat, gab, gossip|反义词:silence, quiet|noun

chinwag:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡ/| v. |闲聊，聊天|To have a friendly, informal conversation. |例句：They were chinwagging away in the corner. 他们在角落里聊得热火朝天。|近义词：chat, gab, gossip|反义词:be silent, keep quiet|verb

词源：chin + wag.  "Chin" refers to the jaw, and "wag" refers to movement.  So, a "chinwag" is literally a wagging of the chin, as one does while talking.  It originated in British slang.
