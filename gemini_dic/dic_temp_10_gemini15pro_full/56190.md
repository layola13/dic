dodge-out:/dɒdʒ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 躲避，逃避，溜走 | To leave quickly and discreetly, often to avoid something unpleasant or an obligation. | Example 1: He dodged out of the meeting as soon as he could. 他一有机会就溜出了会议。

Example 2: She dodged out the back door to avoid the reporters. 她从后门溜出去躲避记者。| 近义词: sneak out, slip away, duck out | 反义词: face, confront | 词性: 动词短语
