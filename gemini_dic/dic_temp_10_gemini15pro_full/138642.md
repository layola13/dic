nuddle:/ˈnʌd(ə)l/| v.| 依偎；蜷缩；拥抱；轻推；|To lie close and snug, as for warmth or from affection. Also, to gently push or prod.|The kittens nuddled together for warmth. 小猫们依偎在一起取暖。|近义词：cuddle, snuggle, nestle, huddle|反义词:separate, disperse|词性:动词

nuddle:/ˈnʌd(ə)l/| n.| 依偎；拥抱；|An act of cuddling or snuggling.|She gave her son a quick nuddle before he left for school.  她在儿子上学前快速地拥抱了他一下。|近义词：cuddle, snuggle, embrace|反义词:separation|词性:名词
