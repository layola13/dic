nonavoidance:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdəns/| n. |不回避，不逃避|The act or practice of not avoiding something, especially something difficult or unpleasant.  It implies a conscious decision to confront or engage with a challenging situation.|Her nonavoidance of the issue impressed everyone. 她对这个问题的不回避给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|Near synonyms: acceptance, confrontation, engagement|Near antonyms: avoidance, evasion, denial|noun
