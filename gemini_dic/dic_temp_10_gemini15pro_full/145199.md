overbaking:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪkɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|过度烘烤|Baking something for too long, resulting in a dry or burnt product.|The cake was dry and crumbly because of overbaking. 蛋糕因为烤过头了所以又干又碎。|例句：I think I overbaked the cookies; they're a bit hard. 我想我把饼干烤过头了；它们有点硬。|近义词：burning, scorching|反义词：underbaking
