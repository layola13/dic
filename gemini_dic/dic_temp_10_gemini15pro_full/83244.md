gymnastically:/dʒɪmˈnæstɪkli/| adv.|体操般地，以体操的方式|In a manner resembling or characteristic of gymnastics; with agility and skill.  She moved gymnastically across the stage. |近义词：agilely, nimbly, acrobatically, deftly|反义词:clumsily, awkwardly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From gymnastic + -ally.  Ultimately from Greek *gymnos* (naked) + *askein* (to exercise).
