optimism's: /ˈɑːptɪmɪzəmz/ | n. |乐观主义，乐观精神| The possessive form of "optimism," referring to a quality or characteristic belonging to or associated with optimism.  It indicates ownership or possession related to the concept of optimism. | Example: Optimism's power can heal. 乐观的力量可以治愈。 | Example:  We need optimism's bright light in these dark times. 在这些黑暗的时期，我们需要乐观主义的光明。|Synonym: hopefulness, positiveness | Antonym: pessimism, negativity | noun
