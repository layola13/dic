It can also refer to the qualities or characteristics associated with such officials, often implying an aloof and bureaucratic approach. |  He rose through the ranks to achieve mandarinship. 他一路晋升，最终获得了官僚地位。| The report criticized the mandarinship of the government. 该报告批评了政府的官僚作风。 | synonyms: bureaucracy, officialdom, hierarchy | antonyms: democracy, egalitarianism | noun
