nefariously:/nɪˈfeəriəsli/ | adv. | 恶毒地，邪恶地 | in a wicked or criminal manner. |  He nefariously plotted to sabotage his rival's campaign. 他阴险地密谋破坏对手的竞选活动。

The dictator nefariously suppressed dissent and imprisoned his opponents.  独裁者邪恶地压制异议并监禁他的反对者。

| 近义词: wickedly, evilly, villainously, diabolically | 反义词: righteously, virtuously, nobly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nefarius "wicked, impious," from Latin nefas "sin, wrong, impiety," from ne- "not" (from PIE root *ne- "not") + fas "divine law, right," related to fari "to speak" (from PIE root *bha- (2) "to speak, tell").
