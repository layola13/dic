chinwagged:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡd/
verb
闲聊，聊天
engaged in casual conversation.
We chinwagged for hours about old times. 我们聊了几个小时的旧时光。
近义词: chatted, gossiped, talked
反义词:  was/were silent, didn't speak
