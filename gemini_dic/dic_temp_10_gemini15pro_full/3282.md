affixable:/əˈfɪksəbl/
adjective
可附加的
able to be affixed; attachable.
The sticker is affixable to any smooth surface.  这个贴纸可以贴在任何光滑的表面上。
The prefix "un-" is affixable to many words. “un-”这个前缀可以加在很多单词前面。
Synonyms: attachable, appendable, fixable
Antonyms: unattachable, irremovable
词性: adjective
