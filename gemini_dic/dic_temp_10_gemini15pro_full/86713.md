helmetlike:/ˈhɛlmɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像头盔的；头盔状的
Resembling a helmet in shape or appearance.
The beetle had a helmetlike carapace.  这只甲虫有一个像头盔一样的甲壳。
The rock formation was helmetlike in its appearance.  这块岩石的形状像头盔。
helmet-shaped, helmet-formed
