brah:/brɑː/
noun
兄弟，哥们儿
A slang term used to address a male friend or acquaintance, similar to "dude" or "bro".
Hey brah, what's up? 嘿，兄弟，怎么了？
dude, bro, man
(No real antonym for a casual term of address)
n.

brah:/brɑː/
interjection
哎，哟
Used as an exclamation of surprise, excitement, or emphasis.
Brah, that wave was huge! 哎，那浪好大！
wow, dude, man
(No real antonym for an interjection)
