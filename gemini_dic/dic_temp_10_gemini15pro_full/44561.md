crotchety: /ˈkrɒtʃɪti/ (US) /ˈkrɒtʃəti/ (UK)| adj.|脾气古怪的，易怒的|Bad-tempered and irritable, especially because of old age or illness.  Often used to describe someone who complains a lot and is easily annoyed.|例句：My grandfather's getting a bit crotchety in his old age.  我的祖父年纪大了，脾气有点古怪。

例句：The crotchety old man yelled at the children for playing in his yard.  那个脾气暴躁的老人对在院子里玩耍的孩子们大喊大叫。|近义词：grumpy, irritable, cantankerous, bad-tempered, irascible|反义词：good-natured, amiable, pleasant, affable|词性: adjective
