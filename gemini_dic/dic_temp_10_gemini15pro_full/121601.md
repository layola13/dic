misordered:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrdərd/
adjective
混乱的，次序颠倒的
not arranged in the correct order
The files were misordered, making it difficult to find the information.  这些文件顺序混乱，很难找到信息。
The books on the shelf were misordered, with fiction mixed in with non-fiction.  书架上的书摆放混乱，小说和非小说混在一起。
近义词:disordered, jumbled, chaotic, disorganized
反义词:ordered, organized, arranged, sorted
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- + ordered.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Ordered" comes from the verb "order," which means to arrange something in a particular way.
