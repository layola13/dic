nonpresciently:/ˌnɒnˈprɛʃəntli/ | adv. | 非预见地，非先见地 | Without prior knowledge of the future; in a way that does not involve prescience or foresight. |  The algorithm nonpresciently allocates resources based on current demand. 该算法根据当前需求非预见地分配资源。|  The robot acted nonpresciently, reacting only to immediate stimuli.  机器人非预见地行动，只对即时刺激做出反应。| 近义词: reactively, instinctively, without foresight | 反义词: presciently, with foresight | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "prescient" (adjective meaning "having knowledge of events before they take place") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
