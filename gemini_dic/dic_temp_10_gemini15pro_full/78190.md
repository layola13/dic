geosynchronous:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsɪŋkrənəs/
adjective
地球同步的
Relating to a satellite that orbits the Earth at the same speed as the Earth rotates, so that it seems to remain in a fixed position in the sky.
A geosynchronous satellite orbits Earth once every 24 hours.  地球同步卫星每 24 小时绕地球运行一次。

The company launched a geosynchronous communications satellite.  该公司发射了一颗地球同步通信卫星。

近义词: geostationary
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
