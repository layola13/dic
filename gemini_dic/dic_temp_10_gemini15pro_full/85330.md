harpooner:/hɑːrˈpuːnər/ | n. | 捕鲸人；用鱼叉捕鱼的人 | A person who hunts whales or other large fish with a harpoon. | The harpooner stood ready to strike. 捕鲸人准备好要投掷鱼叉了。| The experienced harpooner aimed carefully at the whale. 经验丰富的捕鲸人小心地瞄准鲸鱼。 | 近义词:whaler | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

词源:  From harpoon + -er.  "Harpoon" itself comes from Middle French "harpon," probably of Germanic origin.
