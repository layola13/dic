expansivities:/ɪkˌspænˈsɪvɪtiz/| n. |膨胀性，膨胀系数|The expansivities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的膨胀系数差异很大。|例句：The engineer considered the expansivities of the metals when designing the bridge. 工程师在设计桥梁时考虑了金属的膨胀系数。

例句：Knowing the expansivities of the materials is crucial for predicting their behavior under temperature changes. 了解材料的膨胀系数对于预测它们在温度变化下的行为至关重要。

近义词：expansion coefficients, thermal expansion coefficients|反义词: contractivities|词性:noun
