antibody:/ˈæntiˌbɑːdi/ (US) /ˈæntiˌbɒdi/ (UK)| n. |抗体|A blood protein produced in response to and counteracting a specific antigen. Antibodies combine chemically with substances which the body recognizes as alien, such as bacteria, viruses, and foreign substances in the blood.|例句：Antibodies help the body fight infection. 抗体帮助身体抵抗感染。

|例句：The vaccine stimulates the production of antibodies. 疫苗刺激抗体的产生。

|近义词：immunoglobulin|反义词: antigen|词性: noun
