nonpedagogical:/ˌnɒnpɛdəˈɡɒdʒɪk(ə)l/| adj.|非教学的，非教育学的|Not related to teaching or education; not suitable for teaching or education.  Lacking educational value or purpose.|The movie, while entertaining, was rather nonpedagogical.  这部电影虽然有趣，但没有什么教育意义。|His nonpedagogical approach to the subject made it difficult for students to learn.  他对这门学科的非教学方法使学生难以学习。|近义词：uneducational, uninstructive, non-didactic|反义词：pedagogical, educational, didactic|词性:adjective
