nebraskans:/nəˈbræskənz/
noun
内布拉斯加州人
People who live in or are from Nebraska, a state in the Midwestern region of the United States.
Many Nebraskans are involved in agriculture. 许多内布拉斯加州人从事农业。
近义词: Nebraskan people, residents of Nebraska
反义词: (No direct antonym, could list residents of other states, e.g., Iowans, Kansans)
词性: noun (plural)
