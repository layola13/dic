monotreme:/ˈmɒnətriːm/ (US) /ˈmɑːnətriːm/| n. |单孔目动物|An egg-laying mammal, such as the echidna or platypus, of the order Monotremata, characterized by a single opening (cloaca) for the passage of eggs, sperm, urine, and feces.|The platypus is a fascinating monotreme found in Australia.  鸭嘴兽是一种迷人的单孔目动物，发现于澳大利亚。

The echidna, another monotreme, is covered in spines.  针鼹，另一种单孔目动物，全身覆盖着刺。|近义词：prototherian (更专业的术语)|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑marsupial (有袋动物) 或 placental mammal (胎盘哺乳动物) 表示不同类型的哺乳动物) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *μόνος (mónos, “single, alone”) + τρῆμα (trêma, “hole, opening”).
