elbows:/ˈelbəʊz (UK), ˈelboʊz (US)/
n.|肘部；（衣服的）肘部|The part of the arm where the upper and lower arm bones meet.  The part of a sleeve that covers the elbow.|
She rested her elbows on the table. 她把肘部支在桌子上。
The sleeves of his jacket were worn at the elbows.  他的夹克袖子的肘部磨破了。
|近义词:  cubitus (anatomical term)|反义词: 無|词性: 名词

elbows:/ˈelbəʊz (UK), ˈelboʊz (US)/
v.|用肘推；用肘挤|To push someone or something with one's elbow.|
He elbowed his way through the crowd. 他用肘部挤过人群。
She elbowed me in the ribs to get my attention. 她用胳膊肘捅了捅我的肋骨以引起我的注意。
|近义词: jostle, shove|反义词: 無|词性: 动词
