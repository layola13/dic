dialogue: /ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəlɔːɡ/ (US)
| n. |对话，交谈；(戏剧、小说等中的)对白| a conversation between two or more people, especially a formal discussion; the words spoken by characters in a play, film, or novel |例句：The dialogue in the movie was witty and engaging. 电影中的对白诙谐而引人入胜。|近义词：conversation, discussion, exchange, talk |反义词: monologue, silence | noun


dialogue: /ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəlɔːɡ/ (US)
| v. | 对话，交谈| to engage in a conversation or discussion |例句：The two sides have agreed to dialogue to resolve the conflict. 双方已同意通过对话解决冲突。|近义词：converse, discuss, talk, negotiate |反义词: argue, dispute, fight | verb

Etymology:
From the Late Latin dialogus, from Greek dialogos "conversation," from dialegesthai "to converse," from dia "through" + legein "to speak."
