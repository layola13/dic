frowstily:/ˈfrɑʊstɪli/ | adv. | 霉臭地，闷闷不乐地 |  In a musty, stale, or stuffy manner; also, in a dull, old-fashioned, or unsociable way.  Characterized by a stale, stuffy smell and a dull, uninviting atmosphere, often suggesting a lack of fresh air and a sense of neglect. |  The old books lay frowstily in the dusty attic.  这些旧书霉味十足地躺在满是灰尘的阁楼里。|  musty, stale, stuffy, dull, old-fashioned, unsociable | fresh, airy, vibrant, modern, sociable | adverb
