fusillading:/ˌfjuːsɪˈleɪdɪŋ/ |n. |枪击，扫射；(比喻)大量的批评、问题等|A continuous discharge of firearms or outburst of criticism or questions. |The troops were subjected to a heavy fusillading from enemy positions.| 近义词: barrage, bombardment, volley | 反义词: trickle, dribble | 词性: noun (gerund/present participle)

fusillade:/ˌfjuːsɪˈleɪd/ | n.  v. | 枪击，扫射；(比喻)大量的批评、问题等 | A continuous discharge of firearms or outburst of criticism or questions. | The speaker was met with a fusillade of questions from the audience.  | 近义词: barrage, bombardment, volley | 反义词: silence, lull | 词性: noun, verb

Etymology: From French *fusillade*, from *fusiller* "to shoot," from *fusil* "musket," from Middle French *fusile* "steel for striking fire," from Old French *focile* "steel for striking fire," from Latin *focīle* "steel for striking fire," from *focus* "hearth, fireplace." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*dʰegʷʰ-* "to burn."
