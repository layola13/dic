nonrealists:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪsts/ | n. | 非现实主义者；不切实际的人 | People who do not base their beliefs or actions on what is actually happening or possible but on what they wish to be true.  |  The nonrealists in the group continued to insist on their plan, despite all evidence pointing to its inevitable failure.  这些非现实主义者不顾所有证据都指向其计划必然失败，仍坚持他们的计划。 |  idealists, dreamers, utopians, fantasists  |  realists, pragmatists | noun
