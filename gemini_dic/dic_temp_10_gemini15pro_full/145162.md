overassuming:/ˌoʊvərəˈsuːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于自信的，自以为是的，傲慢的 | Having or showing too much confidence; arrogant. |  He was so overassuming that he alienated all his colleagues. 他太自以为是，疏远了所有同事。| She made an overassuming remark about her role in the project. 她对自己在项目中的作用发表了自以为是的评论。| 近义词: arrogant, presumptuous, conceited, haughty | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix) + assuming (present participle of assume)

"over-" 前缀表示 "过度" 或 "超出"

"assume"  动词，意为 "假设"、"承担"、"呈现"

因此，"overassuming"  表示 "过度假设" 或 "承担过多"，引申为 "过于自信" 或 "自以为是"。
