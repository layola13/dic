fusibility:/ˌfjuːzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可熔性，易熔性 | The quality of being fusible; capable of being melted or liquefied by heat. | The fusibility of the metal makes it suitable for casting.  这种金属的可熔性使它适合铸造。 |  meltability, liquefiability | infusibility, refractoriness | noun
