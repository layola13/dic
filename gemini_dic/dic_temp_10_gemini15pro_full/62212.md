encouraging:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
令人鼓舞的；鼓励的
making you feel more hopeful or confident
例句：The news was encouraging.  这个消息令人鼓舞。

例句：She gave me an encouraging smile. 她给了我一个鼓励的微笑。

近义词：positive, supportive, inspiring, optimistic, hopeful
反义词：discouraging, disheartening, depressing, negative
词性：adjective
