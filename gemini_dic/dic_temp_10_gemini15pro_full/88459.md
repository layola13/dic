historiography:/hɪˌstɔːriˈɒɡrəfi/ (US) /hɪˌstɔːriˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |史学；历史编纂学|The study of how history is written, including the methods used by historians and the development of historical thought.  It analyzes historical narratives and interpretations rather than past events themselves. | Example 1:  "Her dissertation explores the historiography of the French Revolution." 她博士论文探讨了法国大革命的史学。

Example 2: "Recent historiography has challenged traditional views of the Civil War." 近期的史学研究挑战了对内战的传统观点。|Synonyms: historical writing, historical scholarship|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym; perhaps "historical negationism" in a limited sense)|noun
