ikons:/ˈaɪkɒnz/ (UK) /ˈaɪkɑːnz/ (US)| n.|圣像，图标，偶像|A representation or picture of a sacred personage, often considered sacred itself; a symbol or graphic representation on a screen in a graphical user interface.|The church was filled with ikons of various saints. 教堂里摆满了各种圣人的圣像。|synonyms: image, symbol, icon, emblem, representation|antonyms: none|noun

ikons:/ˈaɪkɒnz/ (UK) /ˈaɪkɑːnz/ (US)| n.|偶像，崇拜对象|A person or thing regarded as a representative symbol or as worthy of veneration.|Bob Marley is an ikon to many reggae fans. 鲍勃·马利是许多雷鬼乐迷的偶像。|synonyms: idol, hero, star, legend|antonyms: nobody, unknown|noun
