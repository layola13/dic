firehouses:/ˈfaɪərˌhaʊzɪz/
noun
消防站，消防队
A building housing fire engines and the firemen who operate them.
The firehouses across the city were on high alert during the heatwave.  全城各地的消防站都在高温天气期间高度戒备。
