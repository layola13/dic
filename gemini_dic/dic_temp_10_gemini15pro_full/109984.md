liberty:/ˈlɪbərti/ (US) /ˈlɪbəti/ (UK)| n.|自由，自由权；许可，特权；放肆，失礼|The state of being free within society from oppressive restrictions imposed by authority on one's way of life, behavior, or political views.|例句：The Statue of Liberty stands as a symbol of freedom and democracy. 自由女神像象征着自由和民主。|近义词：freedom, independence, autonomy|反义词：oppression, captivity, restriction|noun
