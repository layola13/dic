nonet:/noʊˈnɛt/| n.|九重奏；九人小组；九行诗|A composition for nine voices or instruments.  A group of nine people or things. A poem with nine lines.|The composer wrote a beautiful nonet for strings.  作曲家写了一首优美的弦乐九重奏。 The nonet of judges reached a unanimous verdict. 九名法官组成的审判小组达成了一致判决。  She penned a poignant nonet about lost love. 她写了一首关于失恋的凄美九行诗。|近义词：ennead, group |反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider duo, trio for smaller groups) |词性:noun
词源: From Italian *nonetto*, diminutive of *nove* ("nine"), from Latin *novem* ("nine").
