compressed:/kəmˈprɛst/ | adj. |压缩的；浓缩的；简要的|Reduced in volume by pressure; condensed; abridged.|The file is compressed to save space.  该文件被压缩以节省空间。| Compressed air is used in many industrial applications. 压缩空气在许多工业应用中使用。 |Synonyms: compact, condensed, squeezed, compacted, constricted | Antonyms: expanded, decompressed, enlarged, inflated | adjective


Etymology->
From Latin *compressus*, past participle of comprimere (“to press together”), from com- (“together”) + premere (“to press”).
