flag-wavings:/ˈflæɡˌweɪvɪŋz/
|n.|挥旗，炫耀爱国主义（通常指夸张或不真诚的）| ostentatious displays of patriotism, often implying excessive or insincere enthusiasm. | The politician's speech was full of flag-wavings and empty promises.  这位政客的演讲充满了挥舞旗帜和空洞的承诺。|  The ceremony was marked by much flag-waving and patriotic singing.  仪式上有很多挥旗和爱国歌曲的演唱。| 近义词:  jingoism, patriotism (sometimes), nationalism (sometimes) | 反义词: dissent, pacifism | 词性: 名词

flag-waving:/ˈflæɡˌweɪvɪŋ/
|adj.|挥舞旗帜的，爱国主义的（常用于贬义，指夸张或不真诚的）| characterized by ostentatious displays of patriotism, often implying excessive or insincere enthusiasm. |  His flag-waving rhetoric didn't impress the skeptical audience. 他那挥舞旗帜般的华丽辞藻并没有打动那些持怀疑态度的听众。 |  The crowd grew tired of the speaker's flag-waving pronouncements.  人群对演讲者挥舞旗帜般的声明感到厌烦。| 近义词: jingoistic, patriotic (sometimes, pejorative), nationalistic (sometimes, pejorative) | 反义词:  critical, skeptical | 词性: 形容词
