outmarching:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃɪŋ/
verb
超越行军，在行军中胜过，发展得比...快
Surpassing in marching, outstripping in development or progress.
The smaller company outmarched its larger rivals in technological innovation.  这家小公司在技术创新方面超过了规模更大的竞争对手。
The army outmarched the enemy and captured the city.  这支军队行军速度超过了敌人，并占领了这座城市。
outstrip, surpass, exceed
lag behind, fall behind
v.
