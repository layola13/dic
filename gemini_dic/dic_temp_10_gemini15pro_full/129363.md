nolo:/ˈnoʊloʊ/
adjective
不愿的；不情愿的
unwilling or reluctant.
The defendant entered a plea of *nolo contendere*.  被告不抗辩。
近义词: unwilling, reluctant, disinclined
反义词: willing, eager, keen
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *nolo contendere*, literally "I do not wish to contend."  源自拉丁语 *nolo contendere*，字面意思是“我不愿争辩”。
