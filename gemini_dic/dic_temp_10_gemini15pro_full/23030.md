boulevard:/ˈbuːləvɑːrd/ (US) /ˈbuːləvɑːd/ (UK)| n.|林荫大道，大街|A wide, often tree-lined street in a town or city.|例句：We strolled along the boulevard, enjoying the evening air. 我们沿着林荫大道漫步，享受着夜晚的空气。

例句：The shops on this boulevard are very expensive. 这条大道上的商店都很贵。

近义词：avenue, street, thoroughfare|反义词: alley, lane|词性:noun
