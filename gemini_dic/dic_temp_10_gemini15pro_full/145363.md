overburnt:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrnt/| adj.|烧过头的，烤焦的|Burned or cooked for too long, resulting in a dark color and often a bitter taste.|The toast was overburnt and tasted like charcoal. 吐司烤焦了，尝起来像木炭。|The cake was slightly overburnt around the edges. 蛋糕边缘有点烤焦了。|synonyms: burnt, scorched, charred|antonyms: undercooked, raw|adjective
