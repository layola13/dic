hispanola:/ˌhɪspænˈjoʊlə/ | n. | 海地岛 (Hispaniola) | An island in the West Indies, comprising the republics of Haiti and the Dominican Republic. | Christopher Columbus landed on Hispaniola in 1492.  克里斯托弗·哥伦布于 1492 年登陆海地岛。 | synonym: Haiti, Dominican Republic, Santo Domingo | antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology->
From Spanish *La Española* ("the Spanish (island)"), feminine of *español* ("Spanish").
