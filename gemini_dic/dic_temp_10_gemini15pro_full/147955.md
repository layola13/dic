overreservedness:/ˌoʊvərˌrɪˈzɜːrvdnəs/
|n.|过度拘谨，过分矜持|The state of being excessively reserved or restrained in one's behavior and interactions with others.  Characterized by a reluctance to express emotions or opinions openly, and a tendency to be shy or withdrawn in social situations.|Her overreservedness made it difficult for her to make new friends. 她过分的矜持让她很难结交新朋友。|reserve, reticence, shyness, modesty, inhibition|extroversion, boldness, outspokenness, confidence, assertiveness|noun
