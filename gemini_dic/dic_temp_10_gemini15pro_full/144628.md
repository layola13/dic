outwishing: /ˌaʊtˈwɪʃɪŋ/
|v.| 比…更希望；在愿望上胜过|To wish more intensely or effectively than someone else.|She outwished him for the last piece of cake. 她比他更想要最后一块蛋糕。|例句：He outwished everyone for the leading role in the play. 他比所有人都更渴望在剧中扮演主角。|近义词：outdo, surpass, excel|反义词: be outdone by, lose to|词性: 动词
