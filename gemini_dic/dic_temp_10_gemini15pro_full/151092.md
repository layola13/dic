parasang:/ˈpærəsæŋ/| n.|帕桑，波斯里格|A unit of distance in ancient Persia, varying from about 30 to 36 stadia (roughly 3.5 to 4 miles).|例句：The army marched for several parasangs before making camp.  军队行进了几个帕桑才扎营。

例句：It was said that the journey was many parasangs long. 据说这段旅程有许多帕桑长。|近义词：league, mile|反义词:|noun
