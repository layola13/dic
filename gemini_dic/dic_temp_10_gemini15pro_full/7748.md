anorexics:/ˌænəˈrɛksɪks/
|n.|
|厌食症患者|
|People with anorexia nervosa, a serious eating disorder characterized by a distorted body image and an obsessive fear of gaining weight.|
|Support groups can be helpful for anorexics and their families. 厌食症患者及其家人可以从互助小组中受益。|
|Anorexics often engage in excessive exercise. 厌食症患者经常进行过度运动。|
|近义词: people with anorexia, individuals with anorexia|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "healthy eaters," "people with healthy eating habits")|
|词性: noun (plural)|


anorexic:/ˌænəˈrɛksɪk/
|adj.|
|厌食的，患厌食症的|
|Relating to or suffering from anorexia nervosa.|
|She was dangerously anorexic. 她患有严重的厌食症。|
|He had an anorexic appearance. 他看起来像得了厌食症。|
|近义词: emaciated, skeletal|
|反义词: healthy, well-nourished|
|词性: adjective|


词源: From Greek *ánorexía* "lack of appetite,"  from *an-* "without" + *órexis* "appetite."
