deliveress:/dɪˈlɪvərɪs/ | n. | 女递送员，女投递员 | A woman who delivers something, such as mail, packages, or food. |  The deliveress dropped off the package at my doorstep. 女递送员把包裹送到我家门口了。|  The deliveress handed me a bouquet of flowers. 女递送员递给我一束花。 | 近义词: delivery woman, delivery driver, courier | 反义词: recipient, receiver | 词性: 名词

词源：From "deliverer" + "-ess" (feminine suffix). "Deliverer" comes from Old French "delivrer" meaning "to free, liberate, deliver".
