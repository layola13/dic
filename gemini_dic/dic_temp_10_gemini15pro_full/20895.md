blea:/bliː/
n.
inner bark; bast
The inner bark of a tree, especially of the oak.
The craftsman carefully stripped the blea from the oak tree to use in his basket weaving.  工匠小心翼翼地从橡树上剥下韧皮，用于编织篮子。
近义词：bast, inner bark
反义词:outer bark
词性: noun

blea:/bliː/
n.
sapwood
The soft, outer layers of wood between the bark and the heartwood, often lighter in color.
The blea of the young tree was still soft and easily carved.  这棵小树的边材还很软，很容易雕刻。
近义词：sapwood, alburnum
反义词:heartwood
词性: noun
