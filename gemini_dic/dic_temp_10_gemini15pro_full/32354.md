childlike:/ˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪk/| adj.|孩子般的，天真的|Having qualities associated with a child, such as innocence, trust, and enthusiasm.|She had a childlike wonder about the world. 她对世界充满孩子般的好奇。|近义词：innocent, naive, artless|反义词：sophisticated, worldly, mature|词性:形容词

词源: From child + -like.  The suffix "-like" forms adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of".
