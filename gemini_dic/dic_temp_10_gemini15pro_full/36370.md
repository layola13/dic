coindication:/ˌkoʊɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 并存病症；共同适应症 |The presence of two or more conditions or diseases in the same patient.  A drug or treatment may be a coindication for multiple conditions.| The patient presented with depression and anxiety, a common coindication.  This drug has coindications for both pain and inflammation. |Synonyms: comorbidity, concomitant disorder | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly health, wellness)|noun
