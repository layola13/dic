dabbled:/ˈdæbəld/| v.|涉猎，浅尝，嬉水|To casually or superficially engage in an activity, often without serious intent or commitment.|He dabbled in painting but never became a serious artist. 他涉猎过绘画，但从未成为一位真正的艺术家。|近义词：experimented, tinkered, toyed|反义词：mastered, specialized|词性:动词

dabble:/ˈdæbəl/| v.|涉猎，浅尝，嬉水|To dip something lightly and repeatedly into a liquid.|She dabbled her fingers in the cool water. 她用手指在凉水中轻轻地拍打。|近义词：dip, splash|反义词：immerse, submerge|词性:动词
