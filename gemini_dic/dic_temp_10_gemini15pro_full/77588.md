gee:/dʒiː/
| int.|哎呀；哇；（表示惊讶、赞叹等）|An exclamation of surprise, admiration, etc.|Gee, that's a big cake! 哇，那蛋糕真大！|近义词：wow, gosh, my goodness|反义词:|感叹词

gee:/dʒiː/
| n.|马；（尤指役使的）|A horse, especially one used for draught work.|The old gee was tired after a long day plowing. 老马耕了一整天，累了。|近义词：horse, nag (informal)|反义词:|名词

gee:/dʒiː/
| v. (used with an object)|驾驭；赶（牲口）|To urge (an animal, especially a horse) on.|He geed the horses into a trot. 他吆喝着马儿小跑起来。|近义词：drive, urge, goad|反义词:|动词
