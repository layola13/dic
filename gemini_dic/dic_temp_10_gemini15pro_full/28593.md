caregiver:/ˈkerˌɡɪvər/| n.|照顾者，护理员|A person who provides care for a child, an elderly person, or someone who is sick or disabled.|My grandmother's caregiver is very attentive and compassionate. 我祖母的护理员非常细心和富有同情心.|
例句：
The caregiver assists the patient with daily tasks. 护理员帮助病人完成日常工作。

The family hired a caregiver to help with their aging parents. 这家人雇了一个护理员来照顾他们年迈的父母。


近义词：attendant, nurse, helper, guardian|反义词: ward, dependent|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From care + giver.  "Giver" comes from the Old English "giefan" meaning "to give". "Care" comes from Old English "caru, cearu" meaning "anxiety, sorrow, grief." Over time, the meaning of "care" shifted to encompass not just worry, but also the act of looking after someone.
