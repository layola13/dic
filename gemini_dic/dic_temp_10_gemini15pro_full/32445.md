chiming-in:/ˈtʃaɪmɪŋ ɪn/
| v.|插话；附和；加入(谈话)|Adding one's voice or opinion to a conversation, often briefly or in agreement.|例句：He kept chiming in with helpful suggestions. 他不断地插话提供有用的建议。|例句：She chimed in with a witty remark. 她插了一句妙语。|近义词：interjecting, interrupting, joining in, contributing|反义词:remaining silent, abstaining|词性:动词

chiming-in:/ˈtʃaɪmɪŋ ɪn/
| n.|插话；附和|The act of adding one's voice or opinion to a conversation.|例句：Her constant chiming-in was starting to annoy everyone. 她不断的插话开始惹恼大家了。|例句：The meeting was productive despite the occasional chiming-in from the back of the room. 尽管房间后面偶尔有人插话，会议还是富有成效的。|近义词：interjection, interruption|反义词:silence|词性:名词
