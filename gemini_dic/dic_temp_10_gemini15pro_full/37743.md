compartmenting:/kəmˈpɑːtməntɪŋ/
|n.|划分；分隔；隔离（尤指感情或思想）|The act of separating something into sections or categories, especially emotions or thoughts.|She's very good at compartmenting her life, keeping work and personal issues separate. 她很擅长把自己的生活划分开来，把工作和个人问题分开处理。|
|近义词：categorizing, classifying, separating, dividing, isolating|
|反义词：integrating, combining, unifying|
|词性：动名词 (gerund), 名词(noun)|
|词源：From compartment + -ing.  Compartment itself comes from French compartiment, from comparter "to divide," from com- "together" + partir "to divide."|



compartmentalize:/ˌkəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪz/
|v.|划分；分隔；隔离（尤指感情或思想）|To separate something into sections or categories, especially emotions or thoughts.|He compartmentalized his life, never letting his work interfere with his family. 他把自己的生活划分开来，从不让工作干扰家庭。|
|近义词：categorize, classify, separate, divide, isolate|
|反义词：integrate, combine, unify|
|词性：动词 (verb)|
|词源：From compartment + -ize. Compartment itself comes from French compartiment, from comparter "to divide," from com- "together" + partir "to divide."|
