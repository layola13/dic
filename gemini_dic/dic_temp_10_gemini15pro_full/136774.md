adjective
非天使的；不像天使的；不神圣的
Not angelic or resembling an angel; not heavenly or divine.
The politician's nonseraphical behavior shocked his supporters.  这位政客不神圣的行为震惊了他的支持者。
His nonseraphical language was a surprise coming from a priest.  从一个牧师口中说出如此不神圣的语言真是令人惊讶。

Antonyms:  seraphic, angelic, divine, heavenly, sacred, holy
