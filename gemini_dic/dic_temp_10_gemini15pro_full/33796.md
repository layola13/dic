circumduction:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈdʌkʃən/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈdʌkʃən/ | n. |环转，绕环运动|The circular movement of a limb or eye.  The act of moving a limb or extremity so that the distal end describes a circle while the proximal end remains relatively fixed.|She demonstrated circumduction of the shoulder joint. 她演示了肩关节的环转运动。|近义词：rotation, gyration|反义词: N/A|名词
