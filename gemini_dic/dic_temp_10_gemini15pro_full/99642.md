interlard:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːrd/| vt.|插入；点缀；（尤指以不相干的事）穿插；|To insert or introduce something between other things, especially between parts of a discourse or narrative.|He interlarded his conversation with anecdotes. 他在谈话中穿插着一些轶事。|Synonyms: intersperse, interweave, intermix|Antonyms: separate, divide, extract|Verb

interlard:/ˌɪntərˈlɑːrd/| n.|点缀物；穿插物|Something inserted or introduced between other things.|His speech was full of interlards of humor. 他的演讲充满了幽默的点缀。|Synonyms: insertion, addition, interpolation|Antonyms: omission, deletion, subtraction|Noun
