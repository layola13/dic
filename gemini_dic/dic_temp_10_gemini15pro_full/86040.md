healthiest:/ˈhɛlθiɪst/| adj.|最健康的|Superlative form of healthy.  Having the best health or being most conducive to good health.|She chose the healthiest option on the menu. 她选择了菜单上最健康的选择。|近义词：fittest, well, sound, robust|反义词:sickest, unhealthiest|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *helthiest, from Old English hǣlþigest (“healthiest”), superlative form of hǣlþig (“healthy”).
