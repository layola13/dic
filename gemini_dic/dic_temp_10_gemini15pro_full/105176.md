key:/kiː/ /ki/| n. adj.|钥匙；关键；关键人物；（键盘的）键；调；声调；解释；图例|A key is a small piece of shaped metal with incisions cut to fit the wards of a particular lock, which is inserted into a lock and turned to open or close it.  A key can also refer to something crucial or essential, a solution, or a system of tones in music.|例句：I lost my car keys. 我丢了我的车钥匙。

例句：This is the key to success. 这是成功的关键。|近义词：clue, solution, answer, secret, code, password, tone, pitch|反义词:lock, insignificance, triviality|词性:名词，形容词
