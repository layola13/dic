bloodwort:/ˈblʌdwɜːrt/ | n. | 血根草，血水草 | A North American plant (Sanguinaria canadensis) with a red root and sap, white flowers, and palmately lobed leaves.  It is also known as bloodroot. |  例句：The bloodwort bloomed early this spring.  今年春天血根草开得很早。

例句：Native Americans used bloodwort as a dye and medicine.  美洲原住民用血根草作染料和药物。| 近义词：bloodroot | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
