corvage: /ˈkɔːrvɪdʒ/ | n. |徭役；劳役；义务劳动 |  A feudal obligation to perform unpaid labor for a lord or landowner, especially harvesting crops. |  The peasants were burdened by corvage and other feudal dues.  农民们承受着徭役和其他封建税的负担。 | synonyms: forced labor, statute labor, compulsory service | antonyms: paid labor, voluntary work | noun
