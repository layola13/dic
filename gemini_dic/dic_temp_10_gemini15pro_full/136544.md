It describes something, usually food or drink, that is not sweetened with artificial sweeteners, particularly saccharin.|This brand offers a nonsaccharin sweetener.  这个品牌提供无糖甜味剂。|synonyms: sugar-free, unsweetened, natural|antonyms: saccharin, artificially sweetened|adjective
