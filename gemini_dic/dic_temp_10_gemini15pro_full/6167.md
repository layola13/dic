aming: /ˈeɪmɪŋ/
|adj.| 瞄准的; 旨在...的; 致力于...的 | aiming at/for something: directing something towards a particular goal or target.  |  He's aiming for a gold medal at the Olympics. 他力争在奥运会上获得金牌。| synonyms: targeting, striving, intending | antonyms: ignoring, neglecting | adjective

aming: /ˈeɪmɪŋ/
|n.| 瞄准; 致力于... | the act of pointing or directing something at a target. | The aiming of the rifle was precise.  步枪的瞄准很精确。| synonyms: targeting, directing | antonyms: missing, ignoring | noun
