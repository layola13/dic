omnipatient:/ˌɒmniˈpeɪʃənt/ | adj. | 全方位耐心的; 对所有病人都有耐心的 |  Showing patience towards all patients; universally patient.  This word is a neologism, combining "omni" (all) with "patient" (showing tolerance). | The omnipatient nurse calmed the anxious family members and thoroughly explained the procedure.  这位极其有耐心的护士安抚了焦虑的家属，并详细解释了手术过程。|  tolerant, understanding, forbearing, compassionate | impatient, intolerant, irritable | adjective
