aggrandisement: /əˈɡrændɪzmənt/ /əˈɡrændɪzmənt/ | n. |  增大；扩张；提高；夸大；强化 | the act of increasing the wealth or power of a person or country |  He sought his own aggrandizement. 他在追求个人权势的扩张。 | synonyms: enlargement, expansion, elevation, exaggeration, enhancement | antonyms: diminution, reduction, decrease, understatement | noun

aggrandize: /əˈɡrændaɪz/ /əˈɡrændaɪz/ | vt. |  增大；扩张；提高；夸大；强化 | to increase the power, status, or wealth of; to make appear greater | He aggrandized himself by claiming victories that were not his. 他把不是他的胜利说成是他的，以此来抬高自己。 | synonyms: enlarge, expand, elevate, exaggerate, enhance | antonyms: diminish, reduce, decrease, understate | verb
