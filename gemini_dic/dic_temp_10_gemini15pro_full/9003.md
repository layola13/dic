antiliturgy:/ˌæntɪˈlɪtərdʒi/ | n. | 反礼拜仪式；非传统仪式 |  A rite or ceremony performed in opposition to or as a parody of a traditional liturgy. | The play presented a darkly comedic antiliturgy that satirized traditional religious practices.  这出戏呈现了一种黑色喜剧的反礼拜仪式，讽刺了传统的宗教习俗。| Synonyms: counter-ritual, anti-ceremony | Antonyms: liturgy, ritual | noun
