aphids:/ˈeɪfɪdz/ (US), /ˈeɪfɪdz/ (UK)| n. |蚜虫|Small, soft-bodied insects that suck sap from plants. They reproduce rapidly and can cause significant damage to crops.|Example 1: The roses were covered in aphids. 这些玫瑰上爬满了蚜虫。

Example 2: Aphids can transmit plant viruses. 蚜虫会传播植物病毒。|Near synonyms: greenfly, blackfly, plant lice|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific insect)|Noun
