eugen:/ˈjuːdʒən/ |n.|优生|The science of improving a human population by controlled breeding to increase the occurrence of desirable heritable characteristics. Developed largely by Francis Galton as a method of improving the human race, it fell into disfavor only after the perversion of its doctrines by the Nazis.|Eugenics was a popular social movement in the early 20th century. 优生学在20世纪早期是一个受欢迎的社会运动。|  Eugenics is now considered a dangerous pseudoscience. 优生学现在被认为是一种危险的伪科学。| synonyms:  controlled breeding, selective breeding | antonyms:  random breeding, natural selection | noun
