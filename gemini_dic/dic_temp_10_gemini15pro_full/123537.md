monotrophic:/ˌmɒnəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 单食性的，专性寄生的 | Obtaining nourishment from only one kind of food or host.  | This fungus is monotrophic, meaning it can only grow on a specific type of tree. 这种真菌是单食性的，意味着它只能在特定类型的树上生长。 | Example: The monotrophic fungus depends entirely on its host tree for survival.  这种单食性真菌完全依赖其寄主树生存。| Synonyms: specialized, obligate | Antonyms: polyphagous, generalist | Word type: Adjective
