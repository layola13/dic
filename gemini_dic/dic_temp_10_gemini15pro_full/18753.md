berings:/ˈbɛərɪŋz/
n.
轴承; 方位; 关系; 意义
Bearings are parts of machines that allow one part to turn smoothly against another.  方位是指方向或位置。 关系是指事物之间的联系或关联。 意义是指某事的重要性或目的。
The bearings in the engine need to be replaced.  引擎中的轴承需要更换。
The ship lost its bearings in the fog. 船在雾中迷失了方向。
His remarks had no bearing on the subject. 他的话与主题无关。
This discovery has a great bearing on our understanding of the universe. 这一发现对我们理解宇宙具有重要意义。
近义词: relevance, significance, direction, position, orientation
反义词: irrelevance, insignificance

词性: 名词
