biodiesels:/ˈbaɪoʊˌdiːzəlz/| n. |生物柴油|A fuel made from vegetable oils, animal fats, or recycled greases. It can be used in diesel engines.| Biodiesels are becoming a popular alternative to traditional diesel. 生物柴油正成为传统柴油的流行替代品。| Biodiesel is made from renewable resources. 生物柴油由可再生资源制成。|synonyms: biofuel, biodiesel fuel|antonyms: petroleum diesel, fossil fuel|noun
