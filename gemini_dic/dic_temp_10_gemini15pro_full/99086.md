interbranchial: /ˌɪntərˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective
鳃间的
Situated between gill arches or filaments.  Relating to the space between the gills.
The interbranchial septum separates the gill chambers.  鳃间隔将鳃腔分开。
The interbranchial arteries supply blood to the gills.  鳃间动脉向鳃供血。
Synonyms: interlamellar
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like 'extrabranchial' - outside the gills)
词性: adjective
