faller:/ˈfɔːlər/| n.|伐木工；跌倒者；落物|A person who fells trees or who has fallen.|The faller skillfully brought down the giant redwood.  伐木工熟练地砍倒了巨大的红杉树。|The injured faller was carried off the mountain. 受伤的跌倒者被抬下了山。|近义词:logger, lumberjack (for one who fells trees), tumblera|反义词: climber, riser|词性:noun
