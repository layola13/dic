overorganize:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪz/
verb|过度组织，过分安排|To organize something to an excessive degree, often resulting in unnecessary complexity or rigidity.|She overorganized the trip, creating a minute-by-minute itinerary that left no room for spontaneity. 她把旅行安排得过于细致，制定了一个精确到分钟的行程，完全没有留下任何自由发挥的空间。|例句：He overorganized his closet, categorizing his clothes by color, season, and fabric. 他把衣橱整理得过于井井有条，按颜色、季节和面料对衣服进行了分类。|近义词：micromanage, overmanage|反义词: underorganize, neglect|词性:v.

overorganize:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪz/
adjective|过度组织的，过分安排的|Excessively organized, often to the point of being inflexible or counterproductive.|Her overorganized approach to parenting stifled her children's creativity. 她过度组织的育儿方式扼杀了孩子们的创造力。|例句：The overorganized filing system made it difficult to find anything quickly. 过于复杂的归档系统使得很难快速找到任何东西。|近义词：overly structured, rigid|反义词: disorganized, chaotic|词性:adj.
