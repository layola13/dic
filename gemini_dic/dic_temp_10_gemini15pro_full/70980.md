firers:/ˈfaɪərərz/
noun
点火者，纵火者，射击者
People who set fires, or those who shoot guns.
The firers escaped before the police arrived. 纵火者在警察到达之前逃跑了。
The firers took aim at the targets. 射击手瞄准了目标。
synonyms: arsonists, shooters, gunmen, incendiaries
antonyms: firefighters, extinguishers
