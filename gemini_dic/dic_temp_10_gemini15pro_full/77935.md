genocides: /ˈdʒɛnəˌsaɪdz/
| n. |种族灭绝|The systematic and deliberate killing of a large number of people from a particular nation or ethnic group with the aim of destroying that nation or group.|The Rwandan genocide was a horrific event in human history. 卢旺达种族灭绝是人类历史上一个可怕的事件。|synonyms: massacre, mass murder, extermination, ethnic cleansing|antonyms: preservation, protection, salvation|noun
