oxytocin: /ˌɒksɪˈtəʊsɪn/ (UK) /ˌɑːksɪˈtoʊsɪn/ (US)| n.|催产素; 爱情激素|A hormone released by the pituitary gland that causes increased contraction of the uterus during labor and stimulates the ejection of milk into the ducts of the breasts.|例句：Oxytocin is often called the "love hormone".  催产素常被称为“爱情激素”。

例句：Synthetic oxytocin is used to induce labor. 合成催产素用于引产。

近义词:  None (for the hormone itself, although there are drugs that mimic its effects) |反义词:  None (for the hormone itself)|词性: noun
