derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋ ˈduː/
n.
勇敢的行为；大胆的举动
A daring action or feat; adventurous courage.
He was known for his derring-do on the battlefield. 他因在战场上的英勇行为而闻名。
Synonym:bravery, courage, audacity, valor, heroism
Antonym:cowardice, timidity, fearfulness


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *derryng do*, literally "daring to do".  A combination of the present participle *derryng* (from *dar*, "to dare") and the infinitive *do*.  The phrase became fossilized as a single word.
