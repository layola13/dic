fatality:/fəˈtælɪti/ | n. |死亡；致命事故；宿命；灾祸|An event resulting in death; a death caused by accident or disaster. |The earthquake caused thousands of fatalities. 地震造成了数千人死亡。|Synonyms: death, casualty, mortality, demise |Antonyms: survival, life |Noun

fatality:/fəˈtælɪti/ | n. | 宿命; (命中注定的) 厄运 |The state of being destined to die. |He seemed resigned to his fatality. 他似乎听天由命了。|Synonyms: destiny, fate, doom |Antonyms: chance, fortune |Noun
