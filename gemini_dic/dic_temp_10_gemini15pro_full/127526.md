necrology: /nəˈkrɒlədʒi/ | n. |讣告；死亡名单；死亡学| A list of people who have died, especially recently, or an obituary notice of a particular person.  A branch of history dealing with the deaths of persons. |例句：The newspaper published a necrology of prominent figures who had died during the year. 这家报纸刊登了一份年度杰出人物讣告。

例句：The church keeps a necrology of its members. 教堂保存着其成员的死亡记录。|近义词：obituary, death notice, mortality list |反义词: birth record |词性: noun
