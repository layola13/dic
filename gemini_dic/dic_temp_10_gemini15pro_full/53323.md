disabuse:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːz/
verb
纠正，使醒悟，打消…的错误想法
to persuade someone that what they believe is not true
例句：He quickly disabused me of my fanciful notions. 他很快打消了我那些不切实际的想法。

例句：I had to disabuse her of the notion that she was somehow indispensable to the organization. 我不得不让她明白，她对这个组织并非不可或缺。

近义词：undeceive, enlighten, correct, set straight
反义词：deceive, mislead, misinform
词性：v.
