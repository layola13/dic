internalized:/ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 内化的，融入的 |  Absorbed and integrated into one's own beliefs, values, and personality. | Example 1: She had internalized her parents' values of hard work and discipline. 她已经将父母的勤奋和自律的价值观内化了。

Example 2:  The company's culture of innovation was deeply internalized by its employees. 公司的创新文化被员工们深深地内化了。 | Synonyms: integrated, assimilated, incorporated, absorbed | Antonyms: externalized, rejected, repudiated | word property: adjective
