beadwork:/ˈbiːdwɜːrk/
n.
珠饰；串珠工艺品
Decorative or practical objects made by stringing beads together.
She sells her intricate beadwork at the local craft market. 她把她复杂的珠饰作品在当地的手工艺品市场出售。
The museum displayed Native American beadwork of remarkable artistry. 博物馆展出了技艺精湛的美国原住民珠饰。
近义词：beading
反义词: 无
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bead + work.  "bead" comes from Old English *bede meaning "prayer" (as beads were used for counting prayers).  Later, the meaning shifted to the small, perforated objects themselves. "work" comes from Old English weorc meaning "work, labor, action, deed."
