no-nos:/ˈnoʊˌnoʊz/
noun
禁忌，禁区
things that are forbidden; taboos
例句：There are certain no-nos when it comes to discussing religion. 在讨论宗教问题时，有一些禁忌。
例句：Office no-nos include gossiping and taking personal calls during meetings. 办公室的禁忌包括在会议期间八卦和接听私人电话。
近义词：taboos, prohibitions
反义词:permissions, allowances
词性:noun


no-no:/ˈnoʊˌnoʊ/
noun
禁忌，禁区
something that is forbidden; a taboo
例句：Smoking in the hospital is a definite no-no. 在医院吸烟绝对是禁忌。
例句：Wearing jeans to a formal event is a major no-no. 穿牛仔裤参加正式活动是绝对不行的。
近义词：taboo, prohibition
反义词:permission, allowance
词性:noun


词源:  "no-no" is a reduplication of "no," emphasizing the prohibition.  It likely originated in the mid-20th century, possibly as baby talk or slang.
