curvet:/kərˈvɛt/ | n. vi. vt.|腾跃，跃马式跳跃；(使)腾跃，(使)跃马式跳跃 |A rising or leaping of a horse, in which it raises both its forelegs at once, then raises its hind legs before the forelegs touch the ground; to perform such a leap.| The horse curvetted gracefully around the arena.  马在竞技场优雅地腾跃。 | 近义词：prance, caper, leap, bound | 反义词:  trudge, plod |词性：名词, 动词
