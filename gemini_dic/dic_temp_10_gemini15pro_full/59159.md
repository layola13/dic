dynes:/daɪnz/| n. |达因|A unit of force equal to the force that imparts an acceleration of one centimeter per second per second to a mass of one gram.|A force of 1000 dynes will accelerate a 1 kg mass at 0.1 cm/s^2.  一千达因的力会使一个1千克的质量以0.1厘米/秒^2的加速度加速。|近义词:unit of force|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *dyne*, from Ancient Greek *dýnamis* (δύναμις, “power, force”).
