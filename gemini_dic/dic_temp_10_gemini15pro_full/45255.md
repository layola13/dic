cultisms: /ˈkʌltɪzəmz/
noun
异教；狂热崇拜；膜拜
Excessive devotion to a person, idea, or thing, resembling the veneration given to a religious cult.
The cultisms surrounding the new health guru worried his family.  对这位新的健康大师的狂热崇拜让他的家人很担心。
synonyms:  cults, sects, denominations, factions, fanaticism, obsession
antonyms:  disinterest, apathy, indifference
