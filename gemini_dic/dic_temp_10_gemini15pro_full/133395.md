nonfactitiously:/ˌnɒnfækˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
非人为地；非虚构地；真实地
In a genuine or natural manner; not artificial or contrived.
She nonfactitiously expressed her joy at the news. 她由衷地表达了听到这个消息的喜悦。
The data was collected nonfactitiously, ensuring the integrity of the research.  这些数据是非人为收集的，确保了研究的完整性。
近义词:genuinely, authentically, naturally, sincerely
反义词:artificially, falsely, contrivedly
词性:adverb

词源:
From non- + factitious (artificial, contrived) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Factitious" ultimately derives from the Latin "facere" meaning "to make" or "to do."
