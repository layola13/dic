nonstimulants:/ˌnɒnˈstɪmjʊlənts/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪmjələnts/| n. |非兴奋剂|Medications that do not stimulate the central nervous system.|Example: Nonstimulants are often prescribed for ADHD.  非兴奋剂通常用于治疗多动症。|Synonyms:  non-stimulating medications, alternative ADHD medications|Antonyms: stimulants, amphetamines|Noun


nonstimulant:/ˌnɒnˈstɪmjʊlənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈstɪmjələnt/| adj. |非刺激性的|Not stimulating the central nervous system.|Example:  A nonstimulant approach to treatment was recommended.  建议采用非刺激性疗法。|Synonyms: non-stimulating, calming, soothing|Antonyms: stimulating, exciting|Adjective
