forebrains:/ˈfɔːrbreɪnz/ /ˈfɔːrˌbreɪnz/
noun
前脑
The anterior part of the brain, including the cerebrum, thalamus, and hypothalamus.  It is responsible for higher-level cognitive functions.
The forebrain plays a crucial role in decision-making. 前脑在决策中起着至关重要的作用。

Damage to the forebrain can result in severe cognitive impairments.  前脑损伤会导致严重的认知障碍。

近义词: prosencephalon
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but conceptually contrasting) hindbrain, rhombencephalon
词性: n.
