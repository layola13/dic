convince:/kənˈvɪns/| vt.|说服，使确信|To persuade someone to believe something or to do something.|例句：I managed to convince him to come with us. 我设法说服了他跟我们一起来。|近义词：persuade, assure, satisfy|反义词：dissuade, deter|verb

convinced:/kənˈvɪnst/| adj.|确信的，信服的|Completely certain about something.|例句：I'm convinced that he's telling the truth. 我确信他说的是实话。|近义词：certain, sure, positive|反义词：uncertain, doubtful|adjective
