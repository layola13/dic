ankerite:/ˈæŋkəraɪt/| n.|铁白云石|A calcium, iron, and magnesium carbonate mineral, Ca(Fe,Mg,Mn)(CO3)2.  It is a member of the dolomite group. Ankerite forms a series with dolomite and kutnohorite.|Ankerite is commonly found in sedimentary rocks. 铁白云石常见于沉积岩中。|Synonyms: ferroan dolomite, brown spar|Antonyms: None|noun
