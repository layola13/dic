ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *claspen*, from Old Norse *klaspa* (“to clasp, grasp, fasten”), from Proto-Germanic *klapsōną* (“to clap, flap, slap”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kel- (“to strike, hit”).
