mellifluous:/məˈlɪfluəs/ | adj. |流畅的；甜美的；悦耳的 | (of a voice or words) sweet or musical; pleasant to hear |  Her mellifluous voice soothed the crying baby. 她甜美的声音安抚了哭泣的婴儿。 | The speaker's mellifluous tones captivated the audience. 演讲者流畅的语调吸引了观众。 |  euphonious, harmonious, sweet, dulcet, smooth | harsh, grating, strident, raucous | adjective
