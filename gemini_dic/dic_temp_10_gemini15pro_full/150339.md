panickier:/ˈpænɪkiər/| adj.|更容易恐慌的，更神经质的|Comparative adjective of "panicky," meaning exhibiting more signs of panic or anxiety.  It describes someone who is more easily prone to panic than another person or than they were previously.|She seemed panickier than usual about the upcoming exam. 她似乎比平时更担心即将到来的考试。|The horse became panickier when it heard the thunder.  马听到雷声后更加惊慌失措。|近义词：more anxious, more nervous, more apprehensive, more fearful|反义词：calmer, more relaxed, more composed|词性: adjective
词源: From "panic" + "-y" (forming adjectives) + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Panic" ultimately derives from the Greek god Pan, whose sudden appearances were said to cause irrational fear.
