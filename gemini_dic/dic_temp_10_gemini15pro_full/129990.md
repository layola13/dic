non-nationalistic:/ˌnɒnˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非民族主义的
Not adhering to nationalistic principles; not promoting or marked by excessive patriotism or national pride.
The organization promotes a non-nationalistic approach to global issues.  这个组织提倡用非民族主义的方式处理全球问题。
Her views are surprisingly non-nationalistic for a politician. 对于一个政治家来说，她的观点竟然是非民族主义的，这很令人惊讶。

近义词:internationalist, cosmopolitan, globalist
反义词:nationalistic, patriotic, chauvinistic
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix) + nationalistic (adjective)
"non-" comes from Latin meaning "not".  "nationalistic" derives from "nation" (ultimately from Latin "natio" meaning "birth, race, stock, kind, species") with the suffix "-al" forming an adjective and "-istic" indicating adherence to a doctrine or principle.
