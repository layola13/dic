condor:/ˈkɑːndɔːr (US), ˈkɒndɔː(r) (UK)| n.|秃鹰|A very large American vulture with a bare head and neck and a ruff of white feathers on the lower neck, typically having black plumage with some white markings, especially on the wings.|We saw a condor soaring high above the Andes. 我们看到一只秃鹰在安第斯山脉上空翱翔。|近义词：vulture, eagle|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal)|词性:noun
