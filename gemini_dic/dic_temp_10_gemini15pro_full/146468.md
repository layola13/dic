overfrustration: /ˌoʊvərfrʌˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 过度沮丧，过度挫折 | The state of being excessively frustrated.  Excessive frustration can lead to burnout. |  He suffered from overfrustration after failing the exam multiple times. 他多次考试不及格后感到过度沮丧。 | 近义词: extreme frustration, excessive frustration | 反义词: satisfaction, contentment | 词性: 名词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "frustration" (from Latin "frustratus," past participle of "frustrare" meaning "to deceive, disappoint, make vain")
