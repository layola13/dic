neogaean:/ˌniːəʊˈdʒiːən/
| adj. | 新生代的 | Relating to or denoting the Neogene period or system of rocks. | The neogaean period saw significant changes in climate and biodiversity. 新生代时期见证了气候和生物多样性的重大变化。 | The fossil record provides insights into the neogaean flora and fauna. 化石记录提供了对新生代动植物群的深入了解。| 近义词: Cenozoic | 反义词: Paleozoic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Neo- (new) + Greek gaia (earth)
