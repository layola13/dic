liegiancy:/ˈliːdʒənsi/| n.|忠诚，臣服；效忠义务|(The state or quality of being a liegeman; allegiance.)  A vassal's or subject's duty of allegiance and service to a feudal lord. |例句：The king demanded complete liegiancy from his subjects. 国王要求臣民绝对的忠诚。

例句：The knight pledged his liegiancy to the king. 骑士宣誓效忠国王。|近义词：allegiance, loyalty, fidelity, devotion, fealty|反义词：treachery, disloyalty, infidelity, rebellion|词性:noun
