orographic:/ˌɔːrəˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌɔːrəˈɡrɑːfɪk/| adj.|地形引起的，地形学的|Relating to mountains, or caused by the effect of mountains on the movement of air.|The orographic lift of the air created heavy rainfall on the windward side of the mountains. 地形抬升作用使得山脉的迎风坡降雨量很大。|近义词：mountainous, upland|反义词:lowland, flat|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek *óros* "mountain" + *-graphikos* "related to writing or describing," from *graphein* "to write."
