glitterato:/ˌɡlɪtəˈrɑːtoʊ/
noun
闪光漆; 闪光效果
A decorative effect produced by applying glitter, especially to shoes or other fashion items.
Her shoes had a glitterato finish. 她的鞋子有闪光效果。
近义词:glitter, sparkle, shimmer
反义词:dull, matte
词性:n.

glitterato:/ˌɡlɪtəˈrɑːtoʊ/
adjective
闪光的
Covered with or having the appearance of glitter.
She wore a glitterato dress to the party. 她穿了一件闪闪发光的连衣裙去参加派对。
近义词:glittery, sparkling, shimmering
反义词:dull, matte
词性:adj.
