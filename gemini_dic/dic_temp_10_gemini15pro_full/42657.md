counterstating:/ˌkaʊntərˈsteɪtɪŋ/
|v.|反驳，提出相反的意见|Presenting an opposing argument or viewpoint; contradicting a previous statement.|He counterstated the claim by presenting evidence to the contrary. 他通过提供相反的证据反驳了这一说法。|The defense attorney counterstated the prosecution's narrative. 辩护律师反驳了控方的说法。|近义词:contradicting, refuting, rebutting, opposing, gainsaying|反义词:confirming, supporting, corroborating, endorsing|verb

counterstatement:/ˈkaʊntərˌsteɪtmənt/
|n.|反驳，相反的陈述|A statement made in opposition to another; a contradiction.|Her counterstatement cast doubt on the official version of events. 她的反驳使人对官方的事件说法产生了怀疑。|The witness's counterstatement threw the whole case into question. 证人的反驳使整个案件都成了问题。|近义词:contradiction, rebuttal, refutation, opposition|反义词:confirmation, support, corroboration, endorsement|noun
