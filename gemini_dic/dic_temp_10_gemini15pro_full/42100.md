cotrustee:/ˌkoʊˈtrʌˈstiː/
n.|共同受托人|A person who jointly administers a trust with one or more other people.|My sister and I are cotrustees of our father's estate. 我和我的姐姐是我父亲遗产的共同受托人。|trustee, co-administrator| - |noun
