nonparallelism:/ˌnɑnˈpærəˌlɛlɪzəm/ | n. | 非平行，不平行性；(语法)平行结构失误 | Lack of parallelism; failure to maintain a consistent grammatical structure in a sentence or series of sentences.  |  The sentence "She enjoys swimming, hiking, and to ride a bike" demonstrates nonparallelism; it should be "She enjoys swimming, hiking, and riding a bike." 她喜欢游泳、徒步旅行和骑自行车（此句显示了非平行结构；它应该改为“她喜欢游泳、徒步旅行和骑自行车”。) |  inconsistency, disparity | parallelism, consistency | noun
