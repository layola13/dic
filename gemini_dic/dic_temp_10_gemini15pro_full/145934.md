overdiscounted:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪskaʊntɪd/ | adj. | 过度折扣的，过低估价的 | Discounted to an excessive degree, often resulting in a price below fair market value or intrinsic worth. | Example: The overdiscounted merchandise was quickly snatched up by bargain hunters.  过度打折的商品很快被精明的购物者抢购一空。

Example:  Investors considered the stock overdiscounted due to the recent market downturn. 由于最近的市场低迷，投资者认为该股票被过度低估了。 | 近义词: undervalued, underpriced | 反义词: overvalued, overpriced | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "discounted" (past participle of "discount," from Old French "desconter," meaning "to count off, deduct").
