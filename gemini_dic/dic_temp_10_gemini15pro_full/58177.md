drowning:/ˈdraʊnɪŋ/ /ˈdraʊnɪŋ/| n. |溺水；沉没；淹没|The act of dying by submersion in water, or the state of being drowned. | The lifeguard saved the child from drowning. 救生员把孩子从溺水中救了出来。| 近义词: submersion, asphyxiation | 反义词: survival, rescue | 词性: noun

drowning:/ˈdraʊnɪŋ/ /ˈdraʊnɪŋ/| adj. |溺水的；快要淹死的|Experiencing drowning or being close to drowning. | He heard the drowning man's cries for help. 他听到了溺水者呼救的声音。| 近义词: sinking, submerging | 反义词: floating, surfacing | 词性: adjective


词源: From Old English *druncnian, from Proto-Germanic *drunknōną (“to drown, sink, be submerged”), from Proto-Indo-European *dhreug- (“to deceive, to make muddy, to disturb”).
