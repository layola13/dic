contumelious: /ˌkɒntjʊˈmiːliəs/ /ˌkɑːntʃuˈmiːliəs/
adjective
傲慢的，辱骂的，轻蔑的
(adj.) insolently abusive and humiliating; scornful and arrogant
例句：His contumelious remarks about his opponent were met with boos from the crowd. 他对对手的傲慢无礼的言论遭到了人群的嘘声。

例句：The manager's contumelious treatment of his staff led to high turnover. 经理对员工傲慢无礼的待遇导致了高离职率。

近义词：insolent, abusive, scornful, disdainful, contemptuous
反义词：respectful, polite, courteous, humble

词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin contumeliosus, from contumelia ‘abuse, insult’ (see contumely).
