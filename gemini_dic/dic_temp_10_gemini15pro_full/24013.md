brede:/breɪd/| n.|宽度；编织物；辫子；烤肉架|A woven band or braid, especially of hair; something plaited or intertwined; width; a piece of roasted meat.|She wore her hair in a long brede. 她把头发扎成一条长辫子。|Synonyms: braid, plait, tress, weave, width|Antonyms: narrowness|noun

brede:/briːd/| n.|烤肉；肉片|A piece of roasted meat.|The king feasted on brede and wine. 国王享用了烤肉和葡萄酒。|Synonyms: roast, joint|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "raw meat")|noun

brede:/briːd/| v.|繁殖；生育；饲养|To breed; to raise.|They brede cattle on the ranch. 他们在牧场上饲养牛。|Synonyms: breed, raise, rear|Antonyms: cull, slaughter|verb
