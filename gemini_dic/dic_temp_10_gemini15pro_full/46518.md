daker:/ˈdeɪkər/ | adj. | 更暗的; 更阴沉的 | Darker means more dark than something else. | The room became darker as the sun set.  房间随着太阳落山变得更暗了。| The sky grew darker and it started to rain. 天空变得更暗，开始下雨了。 | synonyms: dimmer, gloomier, murkier | antonyms: lighter, brighter | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *daker*, comparative of *dak*, from Old English *doce*, meaning "dark". Related to Dutch *duk*, German *dunkel*.
