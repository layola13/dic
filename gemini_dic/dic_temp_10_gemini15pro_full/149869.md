paliform:/ˈpælɪfɔːrm/
adjective
桩状的，栅栏状的
Resembling a stake or palisade.  Characterized by a series of closely spaced, upright structures.
The bacteria formed a paliform arrangement on the agar plate.  细菌在琼脂平板上形成了栅栏状的排列。
The paliform crystals were easily visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下很容易看到这些桩状晶体。
近义词:  palisaded, stake-like, fence-like
反义词:  amorphous, shapeless
词性: adjective
