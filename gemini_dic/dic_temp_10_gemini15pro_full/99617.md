interjoin:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɔɪn/
verb
互相连接，相互结合
to join together or with one another; to be joined together
The two pieces of wood were interjoined with glue.  这两块木头用胶水粘在一起。
The fates of the two families were interjoined. 这两个家族的命运紧紧相连。
近义词: join, connect, unite, link, combine
反义词: separate, divide, disconnect
