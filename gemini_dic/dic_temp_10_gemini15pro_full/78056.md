geocachers:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃərz/
n.
寻宝者; 地理藏宝游戏玩家
People who participate in the recreational activity of geocaching, which involves using GPS coordinates to find hidden containers (caches) at specific locations.
Geocachers from around the world gathered for the annual Mega-Event.  世界各地的寻宝者聚集在一起参加年度大型活动。
The geocachers searched diligently for the hidden cache. 寻宝者们努力寻找隐藏的宝藏。
近义词:treasure hunters, cache finders
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite)
词性:noun (plural)
