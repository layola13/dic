evict:/ɪˈvɪkt/| vt.|驱逐，逐出|To force someone to leave a place, especially a building, where they live or work, usually because they have not paid rent or have broken a law or agreement.|The landlord threatened to evict the tenants for not paying the rent. 房东威胁要驱逐不付房租的房客。|The family was evicted from their apartment after failing to pay rent for three months.  这个家庭因三个月未付房租而被赶出公寓。|synonyms: expel, oust, remove, dispossess|antonyms: admit, welcome|verb
