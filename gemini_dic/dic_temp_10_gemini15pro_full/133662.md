nonfortuitous:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈtuːɪtəs/
adjective
非偶然的，非意外的
Not occurring by chance; deliberate or planned.
例句：The nonfortuitous meeting led to a fruitful collaboration. 这场非偶然的会面促成了一次富有成果的合作。

例句：Her choice of words was clearly nonfortuitous and designed to provoke a reaction. 她措辞的选择显然并非偶然，而是故意要激起反应。

近义词：intentional, deliberate, planned, purposeful
反义词：fortuitous, accidental, chance, random
词性: adjective
