nestorianism:/ˌnɛstɔːriənɪzəm/ | n. |聂斯托里主义|A Christological doctrine that emphasizes the distinction between the human and divine natures of Jesus Christ, asserting that he was two distinct persons united in one body. |例句：Nestorianism was condemned as heresy by the Council of Ephesus in 431.  聂斯托里主义在431年的以弗所会议上被定为异端。

例句：The spread of Nestorianism reached as far as China. 聂斯托里主义的传播远至中国。|近义词：heresy, heterodoxy|反义词：orthodoxy|词性:noun
