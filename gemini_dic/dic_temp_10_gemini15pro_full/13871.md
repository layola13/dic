augment:/ɔːɡˈment/ | v. | 增加，增大，加强 | To make something greater in size, amount, or degree. |  The company augmented its staff by hiring five new employees. 公司通过雇佣五名新员工增加了员工人数。

| 近义词: increase, expand, enhance, boost, supplement | 反义词: decrease, diminish, reduce, lessen, weaken | 词性: 动词

augments:/ɔːɡˈments/ | v. | 增加，增大，加强（第三人称单数） |  He augments his income by working on weekends. 他通过周末工作来增加收入。 | 近义词: increases, expands, enhances, boosts, supplements | 反义词: decreases, diminishes, reduces, lessens, weakens | 词性: 动词


augment:/ˈɔːɡment/ | n. | 增加，增大，加强 | An addition or increase. | The recent augment to their team has improved productivity.  最近团队的扩充提高了生产力。 | 近义词: increase, addition, supplement, enhancement | 反义词: decrease, reduction, diminution | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Old French augmenter, from Latin augmentare "to increase," from augmentum "an increase," from augere "to increase."
