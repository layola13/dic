disorderedly:/dɪsˈɔːdədli/
adverb
混乱地；无秩序地
in a disordered or confused way
The books were piled disorderedly on the desk. 书混乱地堆在桌子上。
The troops retreated disorderedly. 部队溃不成军地撤退了。
近义词:chaotically, messily, haphazardly
反义词:orderly, neatly, methodically
词性:adverb
词源:From disorder + -ly.
