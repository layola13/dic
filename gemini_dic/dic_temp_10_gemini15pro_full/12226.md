asbestoses:/æsˈbɛstəsiz/
|n.|石棉（复数）|A fibrous mineral formerly used for fireproofing and insulation, now known to cause serious lung diseases when inhaled.  The plural form is less common than the singular "asbestos".|The old building was filled with asbestoses, posing a health hazard to the demolition crew. 这栋旧楼里到处都是石棉，对拆迁人员构成健康危害。|
|近义词:chrysotile, amphibole|反义词: (No direct antonym, but safer alternatives exist like fiberglass insulation)|词性:noun
