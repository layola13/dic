dowager:/ˈdaʊədʒər/| n.|寡妇，遗孀（尤指拥有亡夫财产或头衔的贵妇）|A woman who holds some title or property from her deceased husband, especially one of high rank. |例句：The dowager queen still held considerable influence. 太后仍然拥有相当大的影响力。

例句：The dowager countess lived in a grand mansion.  伯爵遗孀住在一座宏伟的宅邸里。

近义词：widow, matron|反义词: bachelor, spinster|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *douagiere, from douage (“dower”), from douer (“to endow”), ultimately from Latin dotare (“to endow”).
