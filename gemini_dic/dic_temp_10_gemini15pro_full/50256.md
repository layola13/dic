depeople:/diːˈpiːpəl/
verb
减少人口
To reduce the population of a place, especially by mass expulsion or extermination.
The dictator's regime systematically depeopled the countryside, forcing farmers into urban slums.  独裁者的政权有计划地减少农村人口，强迫农民进入城市贫民窟。
The plague depeopled entire villages. 瘟疫使整个村庄人口减少。
synonyms: evacuate, displace, exile
antonyms: populate, inhabit
词性: 动词
