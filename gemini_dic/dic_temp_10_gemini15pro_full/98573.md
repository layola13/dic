institutionalises:/ɪnˌstɪtjuːʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使制度化；使机构化 | To establish something as a convention or norm within an organization or culture; to place someone in an institution. | The government institutionalises new regulations to protect consumers. 政府将新的消费者保护条例制度化。 |  synonyms: formalize, systematize, normalize, establish, enshrine | antonyms: informalize, personalize, abolish, dismantle | verb

institutionalizes:/ɪnˌstɪtjuːʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使制度化；使机构化 | To place someone in an institution such as a hospital or prison. |  He was institutionalized for mental health issues. 他因为精神健康问题而被送进了机构。|  synonyms: commit, intern, confine | antonyms: release, discharge, liberate | verb
