intaglio:/ɪnˈtælioʊ/ /ɪnˈtɑːliəʊ/
n.|阴雕；凹雕；雕刻；凹版印刷品|A design or inscription carved into a material so that the image lies below the surrounding surface.|The artist specialized in intaglio printmaking.  这位艺术家专攻凹版版画。|
近义词:engraving, carving, etching
反义词:cameo, relief
词性:noun

intaglio:/ɪnˈtælioʊ/ /ɪnˈtɑːliəʊ/
adj.|阴雕的；凹雕的|Cut or engraved into a surface.|The ring featured an intaglio design of a lion.  这枚戒指的特点是阴雕的狮子图案。|
近义词:engraved, incised
反义词:raised, embossed
词性:adjective
