grok:/ɡrɑːk/| v. |理解；深刻理解；彻底了解|To understand deeply and intuitively.|例句：I didn't really grok what he was saying about quantum physics. 我并没有真正理解他说的关于量子物理的内容。

例句：She grokked the complexities of the situation immediately. 她立刻就明白了情况的复杂性。|近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, perceive|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Robert A. Heinlein's science fiction novel *Stranger in a Strange Land* (1961), from the Martian word *gook*, meaning "to drink" or "to share the same blood." The meaning evolved to encompass understanding something so thoroughly that it becomes part of oneself.
