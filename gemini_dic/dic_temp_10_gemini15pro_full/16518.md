bark:/bɑːrk/ (US) /bɑːk/ (UK)| n. v.|树皮，吠叫|The rough outer covering of a tree trunk, branch, or twig.  To make a short, loud, explosive cry, typically of a dog.|The dog started to bark at the stranger. 狗开始对着陌生人吠叫。|近义词：(v.) yelp, howl, woof |反义词:(v.) whimper, meow|noun, verb
