intermittingly:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtəntli/ | adv. | 断断续续地，间歇地 | Happening or appearing repeatedly but not continuously; starting, stopping, and starting again. | The lights flickered intermittently. 灯断断续续地闪烁。|  The pain returned intermittently. 疼痛间歇性地发作。 | 近义词: sporadically, occasionally, periodically, from time to time | 反义词: continuously, constantly, incessantly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intermittent- (stem of intermittens), present participle of intermittere “to discontinue, interrupt,” from inter- "between" + mittere "to send, let go."
