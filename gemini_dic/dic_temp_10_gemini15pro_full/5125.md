alleging:/əˈledʒɪŋ/
verb
声称，断称，指控
present participle of allege.  Means claiming or asserting something without proof.
The lawsuit is alleging that the company engaged in fraudulent practices.  这起诉讼声称该公司从事欺诈行为。
The article alleged that the mayor had accepted bribes. 这篇文章声称市长收受贿赂。
synonyms: claiming, asserting, maintaining, declaring, stating
antonyms: denying, refuting, disproving
v.
