PHRASE->
fold in:  拌入，混合  Fold in the egg whites carefully. 小心地拌入蛋白。
fold up:  折叠，倒闭  The business folded up after a few months. 这家公司几个月后就倒闭了。
fold out:  展开，打开  Fold out the map and let's see where we are.  展开地图，让我们看看我们在哪里。
fold away:  折叠收藏  The table folds away easily for storage. 这张桌子很容易折叠存放。
two-fold:  双重的，两倍的  The problem has a two-fold solution. 这个问题有一个双重解决方案。
