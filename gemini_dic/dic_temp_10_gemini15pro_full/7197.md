angiostomous:/ˌændʒiˈɒstəməs/
adj.
狭口纲的; (线虫)有狭窄口腔的
Having a narrow mouth opening; (of nematodes) belonging to the subclass Angiosomia, characterized by a narrow stoma or mouth.
Angiostomous nematodes are found in various environments. 狭口纲线虫在各种环境中都能找到。
The scientist studied the angiostomous features of the worm. 科学家研究了这种蠕虫的狭口特征。
近义词: narrow-mouthed (for the general sense)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, might depend on the specific context. For example, if referring to stoma size in nematodes, an antonym might relate to a wider stoma)
词性: adjective
