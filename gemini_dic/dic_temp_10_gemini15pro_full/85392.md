hartlepools:/ˈhɑːrtlpuːlz/ | n. |哈特尔浦|A town in County Durham, England, on the North Sea coast, formed by the union of Old Hartlepool and West Hartlepool. |Hartlepools is a popular seaside resort. 哈特尔浦是一个受欢迎的海滨度假胜地。| synonym: town, city, municipality | antonym: countryside, rural area | noun
Etymology: From Old English *heortlēah meaning "hart clearing" + pōl meaning "pool".
