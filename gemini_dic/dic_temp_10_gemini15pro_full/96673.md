industrywide:/ˈɪndəstriwaɪd/| adj.|全行业的，整个行业的|Affecting all or most of the companies in a particular industry. |The recession caused an industrywide downturn. 经济衰退导致全行业低迷。|近义词：industry-spanning, across the industry, sector-wide|反义词: company-specific, firm-specific|词性:形容词

Etymology:  "industry" + "wide". "Industry" comes from Old French "industrie" meaning "diligence, activity". "Wide" comes from Old English "wīd".
