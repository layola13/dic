communicatively:/kəˈmjuːnɪkətɪvli/
adv.
交际地，沟通地
in a way that relates to communication or expressing yourself
She listened communicatively, nodding and asking questions. 她带着交流的意图倾听，点头并提出问题。
近义词: expressively, eloquently, articulately
反义词: inarticulately, uncommunicatively
词性: 副词
