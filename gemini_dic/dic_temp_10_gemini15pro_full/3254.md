affirmative:/əˈfɜːrmətɪv/ | adj. n. | 肯定的；赞成的；肯定词；肯定语 | Expressing agreement or consent; a word or statement that expresses agreement or consent. | She gave an affirmative answer to the question. 她对这个问题给出了肯定的回答。|  The board voted in the affirmative. 董事会投了赞成票。 | synonyms: positive, approving, consenting | antonyms: negative, dissenting | adjective

affirmative:/əˈfɜːrmətɪv/ | n. | 肯定；赞成 |  Something that is affirmative, such as a word, reply, or vote. |  He replied in the affirmative. 他给予了肯定的答复。 | synonyms: yes, agreement, consent | antonyms: negative, no, dissent | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *affirmatif*, from Old French *affirmatif*, from Latin *affirmātīvus*, from *affirmō* (“I affirm”).
