babbling:/ˈbæblɪŋ/| n. adj.|胡言乱语，喋喋不休|The continuous meaningless sounds of a baby or someone speaking foolishly.|The baby was babbling happily in its crib.|近义词：chattering, prattling, gibberish|反义词: silence, quiet|n.

babbling:/ˈbæblɪŋ/| adj.|胡言乱语的，喋喋不休的|Characterized by meaningless or foolish speech.|He dismissed her comments as babbling nonsense.|近义词：chattering, prattling, incoherent|反义词: articulate, coherent|adj.
