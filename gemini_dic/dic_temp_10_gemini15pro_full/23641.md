branded:/ˈbrændɪd/| adj.|烙印的，打上烙印的；（商品）名牌的，品牌的|Marked with a brand; (of goods) having a particular brand name.|The branded cattle grazed on the open range. 打上烙印的牛在开阔的牧场上吃草。|The store sells a variety of branded clothing. 这家商店出售各种品牌的服装。|Synonyms: labeled, marked, trademarked|Antonyms: generic, unbranded, unmarked|adjective
