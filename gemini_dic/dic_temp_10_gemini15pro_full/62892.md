enlightenednesses:/ɪnˈlaɪtndnəsɪz/
n.
觉悟，启迪，开明
the state of being enlightened, having or showing a rational, modern, and well-informed outlook.
He reached a state of spiritual enlightenedness through meditation. 他通过冥想达到了一种精神上的觉悟。
Synonyms: awareness, understanding, wisdom, insight
Antonyms: ignorance, blindness, delusion
词性: 名词
