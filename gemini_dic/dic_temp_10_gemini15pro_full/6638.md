anaesthetically: /ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk(ə)li/ (US) /ˌænɪsˈθetɪkli/ (UK) | adv. |麻醉地，麻木地 | In a way that produces or relates to anaesthesia; numbly. | The patient was anaesthetically numb during the surgery. 病人在手术期间被麻醉得失去了知觉。| The area was treated anaesthetically before the procedure.  在手术前对该区域进行了麻醉处理。 | 近义词: numbly, insensibly | 反义词: sensitively, consciously | 词性: 副词

词源: From anesthetic + -ally.  "Anesthetic" comes from the Greek roots "an-" (without) and "aisthesis" (sensation).
