limitableness:/ˈlɪmɪtəblənəs/
n.
可限制性, 被限制的可能性
The quality or state of being limitable; capability of being limited.
The limitableness of human lifespan has always been a subject of philosophical and scientific inquiry.  人类寿命的可限制性一直是哲学和科学探究的主题。
The report discussed the limitableness of the new technology's application. 报告讨论了这项新技术应用的局限性。
近义词: finitude, boundedness, restrictability
反义词: infinitude, unboundedness, limitlessness
词性: noun
