paneer:/pəˈniːr/| n.| 印度奶酪，芝士 |A fresh cheese common in South Asian cuisine. It is an unaged, non-melting soft cheese made by curdling milk with a vegetable-based acid, such as lemon juice, vinegar, citric acid or yogurt. | Paneer is a key ingredient in many Indian dishes. 印度奶酪是许多印度菜肴中的关键成分。|  Paneer tikka masala is a popular dish. 印度奶酪咖喱鸡是一道很受欢迎的菜。| synonyms: Indian cheese, chhena (in some contexts) | antonyms: N/A (It's a type of food, not a concept with an opposite.) | noun
