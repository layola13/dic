disorder:/dɪsˈɔːrdər/ (US), /dɪsˈɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|混乱，失调，疾病|A state of confusion or lack of organization; an illness that disrupts normal physical or mental functions.|The room was in a state of disorder. 房间里一片混乱。|synonyms: chaos, disarray, mess, confusion, ailment, sickness, disease|antonyms: order, organization, health, harmony|noun

disorder:/dɪsˈɔːrdər/ (US), /dɪsˈɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| v.|使混乱，扰乱|To disrupt the systematic functioning or arrangement of.|The loud noise disordered my thoughts.  巨大的噪音扰乱了我的思绪。|synonyms: disarrange, disrupt, disturb, confuse|antonyms: order, organize, arrange, systematize|verb
