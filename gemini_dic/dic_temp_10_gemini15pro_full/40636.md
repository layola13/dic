conventionalisms: /kənˈvɛnʃənəlɪzəmz/
| n. | 习俗；惯例；陈规 |  Behaviors or practices that are widely accepted and followed within a particular society, group, or field, often without any inherent reason or logic.  |  The artist challenged the conventionalisms of portrait painting by using unconventional colors and perspectives. 这位艺术家通过使用非传统的颜色和视角挑战了肖像画的传统。 |  customs, traditions, norms, practices | unconventionalities, originalities | noun
