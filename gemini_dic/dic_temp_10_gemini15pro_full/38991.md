confirmative:/kənˈfɜːrmətɪv/| adj.|肯定的，确认的|Serving to confirm; corroborative.  Affirming or giving assent; ratifying.|The blood test was confirmative of the initial diagnosis. 血液测试证实了最初的诊断。|近义词：corroborative, affirming, validating, positive, supportive|反义词：negative, disconfirming, contradictory|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *confīrmātīvus, from Latin confirmātus (past participle of confirmāre ‘to strengthen, establish, confirm’), from con- ‘together’ + firmāre ‘to strengthen’, from firmus ‘strong’.
