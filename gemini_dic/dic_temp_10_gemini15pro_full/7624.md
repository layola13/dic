annuity:/əˈnjuːɪti/
n.|年金；养老金；年金保险|A fixed sum of money paid to someone each year, typically for the rest of their life.|例句：She receives an annuity of $10,000 per year. 她每年收到一万美元的年金。

例句：He invested in an annuity to provide income for his retirement. 他投资年金以保障退休收入。

近义词：pension, allowance, payment
反义词:lump sum
词性:noun
