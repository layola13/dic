misconnected:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛktɪd/
adjective
连接错误的，接错的
incorrectly connected; wrongly joined
The phone lines were misconnected, and I ended up talking to a stranger. 电话线路接错了，我最后和一个陌生人通话。
The plumber misconnected the pipes, causing a flood in the bathroom. 水管工接错了水管，导致浴室被淹。
miswired, wrongly connected, improperly connected
correctly connected, properly wired
adjective
