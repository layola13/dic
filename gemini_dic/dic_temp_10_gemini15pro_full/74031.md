forwent:/fɔːrˈwɛnt/| v.|放弃，抛弃|To abstain from; do without.  It implies a conscious choice to not do or have something, often something pleasurable or advantageous.|She forwent dessert to save room for dinner.|近义词：abstained from, refrained from, sacrificed, relinquished|反义词：indulged in, partook of|词性:动词
