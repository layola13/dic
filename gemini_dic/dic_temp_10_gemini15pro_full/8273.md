anti-itch:/ˌæntiˈɪtʃ/
adjective
止痒的
Preventing or relieving itching.
This cream is anti-itch and will soothe your mosquito bites.  这种药膏可以止痒，能舒缓你的蚊虫叮咬。
Synonyms:  antipruritic,  soothing
Antonyms: irritant
词性: adjective
