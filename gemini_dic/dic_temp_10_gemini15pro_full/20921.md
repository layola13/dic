blearier:/ˈblɪəriər/ (US), /ˈblɪəriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更模糊的，更朦胧的，更昏花的|More blurry or dimmed, especially of vision due to tiredness, illness, or crying.  It can also describe something that is less distinct or clear in a more general sense.|My eyes are blearier than usual this morning. 我的眼睛今天早上比平时更模糊。|Synonyms: blurrier, hazier, mistier, dimmer|Antonyms: clearer, sharper, brighter|adjective


bleary:/ˈblɪəri/ (US), /ˈblɪəri/ (UK)| adj.|模糊的，朦胧的，昏花的|Blurry or dimmed, especially of vision due to tiredness, illness, or crying. It can also describe something that is less distinct or clear in a more general sense.|His bleary eyes showed he hadn't slept well. 他朦胧的双眼表明他没睡好。|Synonyms: blurry, hazy, misty, dim|Antonyms: clear, sharp, bright|adjective


Etymology: From Middle English *blerie, from Old English *blear "dim, misty".
