parolee:/pəˌroʊˈliː/ /pəˌrɒˈliː/
n.
假释犯，获假释者
A person who has been released from prison on parole.
The parolee is required to report to his parole officer regularly.  这个假释犯必须定期向他的假释官报告。
ex-convict, ex-prisoner
prisoner, convict
noun
