biting:/ˈbaɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 咬人的；辛辣的；刺骨的；尖刻的 | inclined to bite; sharply critical and mordant |  The biting wind chilled me to the bone. 刺骨的寒风冻透了我的骨头。 | synonyms: sharp, stinging, cutting, acrid, pungent, mordant | antonyms: mild, gentle, bland | adjective

bitting:/ˈbɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 咬；叮 | the act of biting something | The bitting of the horse was strong. 这匹马咬得很用力。| synonyms: bite, nip, gnaw | antonyms: release | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bitinge*, from Old English *bīting*, verbal noun of *bītan* (“to bite”).  Cognate with West Frisian *biting* (“biting”), Dutch *bijting* (“corrosion”), German *Beizung* (“corrosion, etching”). More at bite.
