depths:/depθs/
noun
深度，深处；（感情的）深处；（事物的）底蕴；深渊
The greatest depths of the ocean.
海洋的最深处。

The book explores the depths of human emotion.
这本书探究了人类情感的深处。

He stared into the depths of her eyes.
他凝视着她的眼睛深处。

Etymology: From Old English *dēopþu, from dēop ("deep").  Cognate with Dutch diepte, German Tiefe.

Synonym: abyss, profundity, deep
Antonym: surface, shallows
