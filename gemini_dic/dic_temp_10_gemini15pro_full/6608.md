anacoluthon:/ˌænəkəˈluːθɒn/ (US) /ˌænəkəˈluːθɑːn/| n. | 语法中断，句法不一致|A sentence or construction that lacks grammatical sequence; a change in the grammatical construction within the same sentence, leaving the initial construction unfinished.  A sudden break in a sentence's grammatical structure.|Example: "And then the music began, the dancers swirling - I couldn't help but feel a sense of joy."  然后音乐开始了，舞者旋转着——我不禁感到一阵喜悦。|Example: "She started to say something, then stopped, a look of confusion on her face - the phone rang." 她开始说些什么，然后停了下来，脸上露出困惑的表情——电话响了。|Synonym:  syntactic discontinuity, grammatical inconsistency,  broken sentence,  sentence fragment, aposiopesis|Antonym:  grammatical coherence, syntactic continuity|noun
