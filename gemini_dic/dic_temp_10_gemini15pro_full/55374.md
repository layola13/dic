distastes:/dɪsˈteɪsts/
| n. |厌恶，反感|a feeling of dislike or disgust; aversion.|He couldn't hide his distaste for the vulgar jokes. 他无法掩饰自己对粗俗笑话的厌恶。|近义词：aversion, dislike, repugnance, disgust|反义词： liking, fondness, relish|noun
distaste:/dɪsˈteɪst/
| v. |使厌恶，使反感|to cause someone to feel dislike or disgust.|The bitter medicine distasted her. 苦药让她感到恶心。|近义词：repel, disgust, offend|反义词：please, attract, delight|verb
