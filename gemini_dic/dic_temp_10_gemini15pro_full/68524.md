fancies:/ˈfænsiz/ | v. | 想像；认为；想要 |  To imagine, believe, or desire something. | He fancies himself a great singer. 他自认为是一个很棒的歌手。 | synonyms: imagine, believe, think, suppose, desire, want | antonyms: dislike, reject | verb
