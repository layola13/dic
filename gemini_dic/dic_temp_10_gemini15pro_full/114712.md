malleation:/ˌmæliˈeɪʃən/
n.
捶打；锻造；延展性
the act of hammering or beating something into shape, or the condition of being hammered or beaten into shape.
The blacksmith's malleation of the iron created a beautiful horseshoe. 铁匠捶打铁块，打造出一个漂亮的马蹄铁。
近义词: hammering, forging, shaping, beating
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用brittle)
词性: 名词
