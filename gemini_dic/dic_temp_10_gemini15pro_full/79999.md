go-kart:/ˈɡoʊˌkɑːrt/| n.|卡丁车|A small, open-framed vehicle with four wheels and an engine, used for racing or recreation, especially by children.|例句：He loves racing go-karts on the weekends. 他喜欢在周末开卡丁车比赛。

例句：The go-kart track was busy with families enjoying the sunshine. 卡丁车赛道上挤满了享受阳光的家庭。|近义词：kart, buggy|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑常规车辆 car, truck)|词性:noun
