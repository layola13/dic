denier:/dɪˈnaɪər/| n.|否认者，拒绝者|a person who denies something, especially something that is generally considered to be true|例句：He is a climate change denier. 他是一个气候变化否认者。|例句：The Holocaust deniers spread their hateful propaganda online.  大屠杀否认者在网上散播他们可恶的宣传。|近义词：skeptic, doubter, cynic|反义词:believer, supporter|noun

denier:/dɪˈnaɪər/| n.|旧时一种极细的纱线单位|a unit of weight used to measure the fineness of yarn|例句：The stockings were made of 15 denier yarn.  这些长袜是用15旦尼尔的纱线制成的。|例句：She prefers 40 denier tights for winter. 她冬天更喜欢穿40旦尼尔的紧身裤。|近义词: (In this sense, no direct synonym, the term is specific to yarn measurement)|反义词: (Not applicable to this sense)|noun
