mzee:/mˈzeɪ/ (UK), /mˈziː/ (US)| n.|老者，长者，受尊敬的人|A term of respect for an older man, especially an elder or leader, commonly used in East Africa.|例句：The mzee shared his wisdom with the younger generation.  这位长者与年轻一代分享了他的智慧。

例句：The villagers sought the mzee's advice on important matters. 村民们就重要的事情征求这位长者的意见。

近义词：elder, patriarch, senior|反义词:youth, youngster|词性:noun
