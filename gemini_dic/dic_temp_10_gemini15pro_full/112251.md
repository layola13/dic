lootus: /ˈloʊtəs/ | n. |莲花; 荷花; 忘忧果|A large water lily with showy flowers, floating leaves, and fleshy rhizomes. In Buddhism and Hinduism, the lotus flower is regarded as a symbol of purity and enlightenment.|例句：The lotus flower is a symbol of purity. 莲花是纯洁的象征。

例句：The lotus bloomed beautifully in the pond. 荷花在池塘里盛开得很美。|近义词：water lily|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly decay, corruption in a symbolic sense) |词性: noun
