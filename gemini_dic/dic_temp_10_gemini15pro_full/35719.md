cocatalysts:/koʊˈkætəlɪsts/| n.|助催化剂；共催化剂|A substance that increases the rate of a chemical reaction without itself undergoing any permanent chemical change, working in conjunction with a primary catalyst.|The cocatalysts enhanced the activity of the main catalyst, significantly improving the reaction yield.  助催化剂增强了主催化剂的活性，显著提高了反应产率。|Synonyms: promoter, activator|Antonyms: inhibitor, poisoner|Noun
