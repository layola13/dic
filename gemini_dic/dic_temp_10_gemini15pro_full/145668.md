overcontrolling:/ˌōvər kənˈtrōlɪŋ/
adjective
过度控制的
Excessively controlling; dominating another person's behavior.
例句：He was criticized for his overcontrolling management style.  他因过度控制的管理风格而受到批评。

例句：Her overcontrolling nature made it difficult for her children to develop independence. 她过度控制的天性使她的孩子们难以培养独立性。

近义词：domineering, authoritarian, possessive, restrictive
反义词：lenient, permissive, relaxed, easygoing
词性：adjective


overcontrol:/ˌōvər kənˈtrōl/
verb
过度控制
To control excessively; to dominate another person's behavior.
例句：She tended to overcontrol her children's lives. 她倾向于过度控制孩子的生活。

例句：He overcontrolled every aspect of the project, leading to resentment from the team. 他过度控制项目的每个方面，导致团队的怨恨。


近义词：dominate, micromanage, regulate, restrict
反义词：empower, free, liberate, release
词性：verb
