nonvocal:/ˌnɑːnˈvoʊkəl/ | /ˌnɒnˈvəʊkəl/ | adj. | 非发声的，不用语言表达的 | Not produced by or involving the voice; not spoken. |  Her nonvocal communication, through body language and facial expressions, was incredibly effective. 她不用语言表达的交流方式，通过肢体语言和面部表情，非常有效。 | The dancer conveyed a range of emotions through nonvocal expression. 舞者通过非发声的表达传达了一系列的情感。 | 近义词: silent, unspoken, mute, unvoiced | 反义词: vocal, spoken, voiced | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + vocal (from Latin vocalis, relating to the voice)
