opinionate: /əˈpɪnjəneɪt/
adjective
固执己见的，武断的
Holding stubbornly to one's own opinions, especially without considering the views of others.
He's very opinionated and dogmatic. 他非常固执己见，而且武断。

opinionate: /əˈpɪnjəneɪt/
verb
发表武断的意见
To express one's opinions forcefully and often without considering the views of others.
He's always opinionating about politics. 他总是武断地发表政治观点。

近义词:dogmatic, opinionated, assertive, insistent, adamant
反义词:open-minded, receptive, flexible
词性: adjective, verb
词源: From the noun "opinion" + "-ate" (suffix forming verbs and adjectives).
