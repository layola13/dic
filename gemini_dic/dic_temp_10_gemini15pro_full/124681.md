movers:/ˈmuːvərz/| n. |搬运工；搬家公司；推动者；动议者|People or a company that help people move their possessions from one property to another.  A person or thing that initiates or facilitates change or progress. |The movers will arrive at 8 am tomorrow.  搬运工明天早上8点会到。


The movers carefully packed all the furniture. 搬运工小心翼翼地打包了所有家具。|近义词：removal company, van line,  porters, haulers,  instigators, catalysts |反义词:stayers, inhibitors |词性:noun
