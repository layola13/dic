bankroll:/ˈbæŋkrəʊl/ (US) /ˈbæŋkrəʊl/ (UK)| n. vt.|资金；赌本；为…提供资金|A bankroll is the amount of money someone has available for a particular purpose, especially for gambling or for starting a business.  It can also be used as a verb meaning to finance something.|

例句：
He lost his entire bankroll at the casino. 他在赌场输光了所有的赌本。

He bankrolled the new startup company. 他为这家新的初创公司提供资金。


近义词：stake, funds, financing, capital|
反义词:debt, liability|
词性:noun, verb|

词源: bank + roll.  "Bank" referring to money, and "roll" referring to a roll of bills or coins.|
