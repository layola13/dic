cytologies: /saɪˈtɑːlədʒiz/ | n. | 细胞学 | The study of cells, especially their structure, function, and chemistry. | The cytologies of the tumor cells revealed their cancerous nature. 肿瘤细胞的细胞学检查揭示了它们的癌性。 | 近义词: cell biology | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From cyto- + -logy.  "cyto-" comes from Greek "kytos" meaning "hollow vessel, receptacle, basket," later "cell." "-logy" comes from Greek "-logia" meaning "speaking, discourse," from "legein" "to speak."
