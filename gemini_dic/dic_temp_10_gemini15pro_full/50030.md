denormalization:/ˌdiːnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |反规范化|The process of increasing redundancy in a database by adding redundant data or grouping data to improve read performance, often at the expense of write performance.  It is the reverse process of normalization.|The denormalization of the database improved query speed significantly.  反规范化数据库显著提高了查询速度。|synonyms: data redundancy, redundancy, data duplication|antonyms: normalization|noun

denormalize:/ˌdiːnɔːrmələˈaɪz/ | v. |反规范化|To convert (data) from a normalized form to a denormalized form.|They decided to denormalize the table to improve query performance. 他们决定反规范化表格以提高查询性能。|synonyms: introduce redundancy, duplicate data|antonyms: normalize|verb
