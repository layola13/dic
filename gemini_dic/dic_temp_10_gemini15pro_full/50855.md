|n.|解除种族隔离|The desegregation of schools was a landmark achievement. 学校的种族隔离解除是一项里程碑式的成就。|

|近义词:integration, mixing|
|反义词:segregation, discrimination|

desegregate:/diːˈseɡrɪɡeɪt/
|v.|解除种族隔离|The government desegregated the schools in 1954. 政府在1954年解除了学校的种族隔离。|
|英文解析:To eliminate segregation in; integrate racially.|
|例句:The company was forced to desegregate its workforce. 公司被迫取消其员工队伍的种族隔离。|
|例句:The Supreme Court decision desegregated public schools. 最高法院的判决解除了公立学校的种族隔离。|
|近义词:integrate, mix|
|反义词:segregate, discriminate|
|词性:verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + segregation.  Segregation comes from Late Latin segregātiōn- (stem of segregātiō), from segregāre "to set apart," from se- "apart" + greg- (stem of grex) "flock, herd."
