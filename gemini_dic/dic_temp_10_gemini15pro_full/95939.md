incubational:/ˌɪŋkjuˈbeɪʃənəl/| adj.|孵化的，潜伏的|Relating to the incubation period of a disease; the period between exposure to an infection and the appearance of the first symptoms.  |例句：The incubational period for chickenpox is about two weeks. 水痘的潜伏期大约是两周。

例句：The patient is in the incubational stage of the disease. 病人处于疾病的潜伏期。|近义词：latent, dormant|反义词：active, manifest|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *incubationālis, from Latin incubātiōn- (stem of incubātiō ), from incubāre ‘to hatch’.
