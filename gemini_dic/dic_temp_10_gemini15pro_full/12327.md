aseptic:/əˈsɛptɪk/ /eɪˈsɛptɪk/
adjective
无菌的；防腐的；冷漠的
free from contamination caused by harmful bacteria, viruses, or other microorganisms; surgically sterile; lacking feeling or emotion.
The operating room must be kept aseptic.  手术室必须保持无菌状态。
The nurse used an aseptic technique to clean the wound. 护士使用无菌技术清理伤口。
sterile, antiseptic, hygienic
septic, contaminated, infected
adjective

aseptic:/əˈsɛptɪk/ /eɪˈsɛptɪk/
noun
防腐剂；无菌物
a substance that prevents infection.
The doctor applied an aseptic to the burn. 医生在烧伤处涂抹了防腐剂。
Various aseptics were used to sterilize the equipment.  各种防腐剂被用来消毒设备。
antiseptic, disinfectant
