angelling:/ˈeɪndʒəlɪŋ/
n.
垂钓,钓鱼；装作天使般纯洁的人
The act of fishing with a rod and line, especially for trout or salmon.  A person who affects innocent sweetness or virtue.
He enjoys angelling in the clear mountain streams. 他喜欢在清澈的山涧里钓鱼。
She acted like such an angelling, but I knew she was up to something. 她表现得像个天使，但我知道她肯定在搞鬼。
近义词:fishing, angling, innocent, virtuous
反义词:wicked, devilish
词性:名词
