cairned:/kɛrnd/
adjective
以石冢堆砌的；有石冢的
Marked by a cairn or cairns.
The cairned summit of the mountain was visible from miles away.  山頂上堆砌的石冢在几英里外都能看到。
近义词:rocky, stony
反义词:clear, open
词性:adjective


cairned:/kɛrnd/
verb
用石冢堆砌
To mark with a cairn.
They cairned the grave of their beloved pet. 他们用石冢堆砌了他们心爱宠物的坟墓。
近义词:bury, inter
反义词:exhume, unearth
词性:verb
