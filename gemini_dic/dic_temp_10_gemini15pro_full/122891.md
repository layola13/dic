mollycoddler:/ˈmɒliˌkɒdlə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdlər/ (US)| n. |娇惯的人；溺爱孩子的人；宝贝蛋|A person who pampers or overprotects someone, especially a child.|He's a real mollycoddler with his children, always picking up after them. 他对他的孩子真是溺爱，总是跟在他们后面收拾残局。
