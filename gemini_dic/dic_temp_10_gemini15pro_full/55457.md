distinguishes:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪz/ | v. | 区分，辨别 | This characteristic distinguishes the species from all others.  这个特征将该物种与所有其他物种区分开来。|例句：He distinguishes between right and wrong. 他能辨别是非。 | 近义词：differentiates, separates, discerns | 反义词：confuses, blends, mixes | 词性: 动词


distinguish:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/ | v. | 区分，辨别，使杰出，使显眼 | To recognize or treat (someone or something) as different. | 例句：She distinguished herself as a talented artist. 她使自己成为一名杰出的艺术家。| 近义词：differentiate, separate, discern, characterize | 反义词：confuse, blend, mix | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French distinguer, from Latin distinguere (to separate by pricking, mark by points, distinguish, adorn), from dis- (apart) + stinguere (to prick, extinguish).
