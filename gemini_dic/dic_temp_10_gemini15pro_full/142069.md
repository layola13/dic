orgiastic:/ɔːrˈdʒiːæstɪk/
adj.
狂欢的；放荡的
Characterized by or resembling an orgy; wild and unrestrained revelry.
The festival culminated in an orgiastic celebration involving music, dancing, and feasting.  这个节日以一场包含音乐、舞蹈和盛宴的狂欢庆祝活动达到高潮。
The music created an orgiastic atmosphere.  音乐营造了一种狂欢的氛围。
近义词：wild, frenzied, unrestrained, bacchanalian, debauched
反义词： restrained, subdued, controlled, calm, peaceful
词性：adjective
词源：From French *orgiaste* or directly from Greek *orgiastikos*, from *orgia* ("secret rites, orgy").
