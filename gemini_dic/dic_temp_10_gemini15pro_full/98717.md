insured:/ɪnˈʃʊərd/ | adj. n.| 已投保的，被保险人 |  Covered by insurance; a person or entity covered by an insurance policy. |  例句：The insured received compensation after the accident. 被保险人在事故后得到了赔偿。| 近义词：covered, protected | 反义词: uninsured, unprotected | 词性:形容词

insured:/ɪnˈʃʊərd/ | n.| 被保险人 | A person or entity covered by an insurance policy. | 例句：The insurance company contacted the insured to discuss the claim. 保险公司联系了被保险人讨论索赔事宜。| 近义词：policyholder, beneficiary | 反义词: insurer | 词性:名词

词源: From the past participle of *insure*, from *in-* (expressing inclusion) + *sure*.
