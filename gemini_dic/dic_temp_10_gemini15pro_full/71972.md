flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/| n. v.|欺骗；花言巧语；|A trick or deception, often involving elaborate or misleading language.  Also, to deceive someone in this way.|He made his money through flimflam and fast talk. 他靠花言巧语和欺骗赚钱。|近义词：trickery, deception, fraud, swindle|反义词：honesty, truthfulness|词性：名词，动词
flimflamming:/ˈflɪmˌflæmɪŋ/| n. adj.|欺骗；花言巧语的；|The act of deceiving someone with elaborate or misleading language. Also used as an adjective to describe something deceptive.|He was accused of flimflamming investors out of their savings. 他被指控骗取投资者的积蓄。|近义词：deceiving, fraudulent, misleading|反义词：honest, truthful|词性：名词，形容词
