connumeration:/kəˌn(j)uːməˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 列举，枚举；详尽的列表 | A detailed list or recounting of things, typically one by one. |  She gave a connumeration of all the reasons why she couldn't attend the meeting. 她列举了所有她不能参加会议的理由。

| Example: The witness provided a connumeration of the events leading up to the accident. 目击者详细列举了事故发生前的一系列事件。

| Synonyms: enumeration, listing, recital, recounting | Antonyms: summary, abridgement | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *connumeratio, from Latin con- 'together' + numerare 'to count'.
