cleverly:/ˈklevərli/| adv.|聪明地，巧妙地，灵巧地|In a clever or skillful way.  Showing intelligence and resourcefulness.|She cleverly solved the puzzle. 她巧妙地解决了这个难题。|近义词：skillfully, ingeniously, adroitly, smartly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, foolishly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Old English "glēawlīce," meaning "wisely, prudently."  "Glēaw" meant "wise, prudent, skillful."
