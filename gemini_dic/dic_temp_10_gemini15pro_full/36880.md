coloratura:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊrə/ (US), /ˌkʌləˈtʊərə/ (UK)| n.| 花腔；炫技的装饰音；花腔女高音|A rapid melodic passage, usually ornamented, requiring great vocal agility and virtuosity, or a soprano specializing in such passages.|The soprano delivered a breathtaking coloratura in the final act. 女高音在最后一幕表演了令人叹为观止的花腔。|近义词：ornamentation, embellishment, flourish, trill|反义词:simplicity|词性:noun
