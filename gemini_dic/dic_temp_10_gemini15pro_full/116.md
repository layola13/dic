abalienate: /əˈbeɪliəneɪt/
verb
疏远；转让；让渡
To cause someone to feel that they are no longer part of a group; to transfer ownership or rights to another.
The new policy abalienated many voters. 新政策疏远了许多选民。
He abalienated his property to his son. 他把财产转让给了他的儿子。
近义词: estrange, alienate, transfer, convey
反义词: unite, reconcile, retain
词性: verb

the act of abalienating: 疏远的行为  The act of abalienating his friends made him lonely. 疏远朋友的行为使他感到孤独。
become abalienated: 变得疏远  He became abalienated from his community. 他变得与他的社区格格不入。
