nonveracity:/ˌnɒnvəˈræsɪti/ | n. | 不真实，虚假 |Lack of truth or accuracy; falsehood. |  His nonveracity was evident in his constantly changing stories. 他的故事不断变化，表明他并不诚实。|  The journalist was fired for nonveracity in his reporting. 由于报道失实，这名记者被解雇了。 | synonyms: falsehood, mendacity, untruthfulness, dishonesty, fabrication, deception, lie | antonyms: truth, veracity, honesty, accuracy, integrity | noun
