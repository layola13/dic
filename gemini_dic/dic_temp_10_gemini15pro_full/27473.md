campanes:/kæmˈpeɪnz/ | n. | bells (usually plural) | a set of bells, often in a church tower, or the sound they make | The campaniles of Venice are famous for their beauty. 威尼斯的钟楼以其美丽而闻名。|近义词：bells, chimes |反义词: silence |词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *campanes (plural), from Late Latin campana "bell," originally "bell of Campania" (a region in Italy where bells were made), from Campānia, name of the region.
