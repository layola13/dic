hust:/hʌst/ | v. n. | 努力, 奔忙, 兜售; 喧嚣, 忙碌 | to work hard and energetically; to push or force (someone) roughly; energetic activity |  He hustled to finish the project on time. 他为了按时完成项目而努力工作。 | 近义词: hustle, bustle, strive, push, shove | 反义词: dawdle, linger, procrastinate | 词性: 动词, 名词
