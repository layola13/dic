depopularizes:/diːˈpɒpjələraɪzɪz/
verb
使不受欢迎，使失去流行
reduces the popularity of something or someone
Social media algorithms can depopularize certain viewpoints. 社交媒体算法可能会使某些观点失去流行。
The rise of streaming services has depopularized traditional cable television. 流媒体服务的兴起使得传统的有限电视不再受欢迎。
Synonyms: marginalize, diminish, undermine
Antonyms: popularize, promote, boost
词性: 动词
