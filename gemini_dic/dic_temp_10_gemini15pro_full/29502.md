catarrhal:/kəˈtɑːrəl/| adj.|卡他性的，粘膜炎的|Relating to or characterized by catarrh, an inflammation of a mucous membrane, especially of the respiratory passages, marked by excessive secretion of mucus and often by cough, congestion, and other symptoms.|He had a catarrhal cough and a runny nose. 他咳嗽带痰，还流鼻涕。|Synonyms: mucous, phlegmy, rheumy|Antonyms: dry, non-mucous|adjective

catarrhal:/kəˈtɑːrəl/| n.|黏膜炎|Inflammation of a mucous membrane, especially of the respiratory passages.|The doctor diagnosed him with nasal catarrh. 医生诊断他患有鼻黏膜炎。|Synonyms: catarrh, inflammation|Antonyms: health|noun
