mangudai:/ˌmæŋɡuˈdaɪ/
n.
蒙古骑射手，一种蒙古骑兵
A type of Mongol horse archer, known for their skill with composite bows and maneuverability.
The mangudai rained arrows down upon the fleeing enemy.  蒙古骑射手向逃跑的敌人射出一阵箭雨。

The mangudai were a crucial part of the Mongol armies.  蒙古骑射手是蒙古军队的重要组成部分。

近义词: horse archer, mounted archer
反义词: foot soldier, infantryman
词性: noun
