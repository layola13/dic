misaimed:/ˌmɪsˈeɪmd/ | adj. | 瞄准错误的；目标错误的 | Directed towards a wrong target or purpose; badly aimed. | The missile was misaimed and hit a civilian building.  导弹瞄准错误，击中了一栋民用建筑。|  The campaign was misaimed and failed to reach its target audience.  这场运动的目标错误，未能触及目标受众。| synonyms: misdirected, errant, astray, inaccurate, inappropriate | antonyms: well-aimed, accurate, appropriate, targeted, on target | adjective
