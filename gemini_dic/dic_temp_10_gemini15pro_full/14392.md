autostability:/ˌɔːtoʊstəˈbɪləti/ | n. |自动稳定性|The ability of a system, particularly a vehicle or aircraft, to maintain stability or return to a stable state without external input or control. | The car's autostability made it easy to handle even at high speeds. 这辆车的自动稳定性使它即使在高速行驶时也很容易操控。|  The aircraft demonstrated excellent autostability in turbulent conditions. 这架飞机在湍流条件下表现出了优异的自动稳定性。| synonyms: self-stability, inherent stability | antonyms: instability, lability | noun
