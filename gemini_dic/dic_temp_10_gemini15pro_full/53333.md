disacquaint:/ˌdɪsəˈkweɪnt/
verb
使不熟悉；使疏远
To cause to lose familiarity with someone or something; to make unfamiliar.
He had become disacquainted with his former friends after years of living abroad.  在国外生活多年后，他已经和以前的朋友疏远了。
The long illness disacquainted him with his work.  长期的疾病使他对工作生疏了。
近义词:estrange, alienate
反义词:acquaint, familiarize
词性:v.
