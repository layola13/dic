|n.|无限；无边无际；浩瀚无垠|The boundlessness of the universe inspire awe and wonder. 宇宙的浩瀚无垠激发了敬畏和惊奇。|The vastness of the ocean impressed him with a sense of boundlessness.  广阔的海洋让他感受到一种无限感。|近义词：infinity, immensity, vastness|反义词：limitation, finiteness, restriction|词性:名词

词源:
From boundless + -ness.  Boundless itself comes from bound (meaning "limit") + -less (meaning "without").  Therefore, boundlessness literally means "the state of being without limits".
