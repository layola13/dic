anemometry:/ˌænɪˈmɒmɪtri/ | n. |风速测定法；风速测量学|The study of wind speed and direction, typically using instruments like anemometers.  |例句：The anemometry data showed a significant increase in wind speed during the storm.  风速测定数据显示，风暴期间风速显著增加。

|例句：Accurate anemometry is crucial for weather forecasting and wind energy assessment. 精确的风速测量对于天气预报和风能评估至关重要。|近义词：wind measurement|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology: From Greek *ánemos* ("wind") + *-metria* ("measurement").
