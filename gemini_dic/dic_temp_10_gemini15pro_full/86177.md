heartwise:/ˈhɑːrtwaɪz/
adjective
明智的; 凭直觉的; 就内心而言的;
Wise or prudent in emotional matters; guided by the heart or intuition.
She made a heartwise decision to follow her dreams. 她凭直觉做出了追随梦想的决定。
近义词: intuitive, insightful, discerning, wise, sagacious
反义词: foolish, unwise, imprudent
词性: adjective

heartwise:/ˈhɑːrtwaɪz/
adverb
凭直觉地
In a way that is wise or prudent in emotional matters; guided by the heart or intuition.
Heartwise, she knew it was the right decision. 她凭直觉知道这是正确的决定。
近义词: intuitively, instinctively
反义词: foolishly, unwisely
词性: adverb
