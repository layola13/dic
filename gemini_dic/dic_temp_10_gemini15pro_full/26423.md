byliners:/ˈbaɪˌlaɪnərz/
n.
署名作者；专栏作家
A byliner is a journalist who contributes articles to a publication, typically a newspaper or magazine, and whose name is prominently displayed with their work.
The newspaper featured byliners from several well-known journalists. 这家报纸刊登了几位著名记者的署名文章。

byliner:/ˈbaɪˌlaɪnər/
n.
署名文章；专栏文章
A byliner is an article or column in a publication that is attributed to a specific author.
Her latest byliner in the magazine discussed the impact of social media on politics.  她在杂志上的最新专栏文章讨论了社交媒体对政治的影响。


近义词:contributor, columnist, writer, journalist, author
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ghostwriter" if considering anonymity)
词性: noun
