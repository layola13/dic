nonviviparous: /ˌnɒnˌvɪvɪˈpærəs/ /ˌnɑːnˌvɪvɪˈpærəs/
adjective
非胎生的
Not producing living young, but producing eggs that hatch outside the body.
The platypus, while a mammal, is nonviviparous and lays eggs.  鸭嘴兽虽然是哺乳动物，但不是胎生，而是产卵。
Snakes are generally nonviviparous, although some species give birth to live young. 蛇通常是非胎生的，尽管有些物种会产下幼崽。
近义词: oviparous, egg-laying
反义词: viviparous, live-bearing
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + viviparous.  "Viviparous" comes from Latin "vivus" (alive) + "parere" (to bring forth).
