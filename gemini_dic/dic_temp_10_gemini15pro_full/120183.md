mindlessly:/ˈmaɪndləsli/| adv.|无意识地，心不在焉地|Without thought or attention; mechanically.|He mindlessly scrolled through social media. 他无意识地翻阅着社交媒体。|She mindlessly chewed her gum during the meeting.  她在会议期间心不在焉地嚼着口香糖。|近义词：automatically, mechanically, thoughtlessly, absentmindedly|反义词：thoughtfully, deliberately, consciously|词性:副词
Etymology: From "mindless" + "-ly." "Mindless" comes from "mind" (from Old English "gemynd") + "-less" (a suffix indicating lack of something).
