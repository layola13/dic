disutilize:/dɪsˈjuːtəlaɪz/ | v. |  使失效，使闲置，不再使用 | To render something unusable or idle; to cease using something. | The company disutilized the old factory after building a new, more modern one.  这家公司在建造了一座新的、更现代化的工厂后，就不再使用旧工厂了。 |  The government disutilized several power plants after investing in renewable energy sources. 政府在投资可再生能源后，使几家发电厂闲置了。 | 近义词: decommission, retire, mothball | 反义词: utilize, employ, commission | 词性: 动词

词源:  dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + utilize (from Latin utilis "useful")
