bovinities:/boʊˈvɪnɪtiz/
|n.|愚蠢；牛一样的迟钝；|stupid or foolish remarks or ideas, often related to a bovine-like slowness of mind.|His speech was full of bovinities, leaving the audience bewildered. 他的演讲充满了愚蠢的言论，让观众感到困惑。|近义词：inanities, absurdities, silliness, foolishness|反义词：wisdom, intelligence, brilliance|词性:名词

词源:
From Latin *bovīnitās*, from bovīnus (“of or pertaining to cattle, ox-like”) + -itās (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
