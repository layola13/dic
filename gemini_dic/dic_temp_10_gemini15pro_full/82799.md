guinea:/ˈɡɪni/| n.|几内亚（西非国家）; 基尼（英国旧金币）; 几尼（用于某些商品和服务的计价单位）|A country in West Africa. Formerly a British gold coin. A guinea is also a unit of value used for pricing some goods and services.|例句：Guinea gained independence in 1958. 几内亚于1958年获得独立。

例句：The painting sold for 200 guineas. 这幅画以200几尼的价格售出。|近义词： (for the monetary unit) pound|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a country or currency)|词性:noun


guinea pig:/ˈɡɪni pɪɡ/| n.|豚鼠;实验对象|A rodent often kept as a pet. Also used to refer to a person used in an experiment or trial of something new.|例句：My daughter wants a guinea pig for her birthday. 我女儿生日想要一只豚鼠。


例句：The new drug was tested on guinea pigs. 这种新药在豚鼠身上进行了测试。|近义词：(for the animal) cavy (for experimental subject) test subject|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
