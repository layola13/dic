oily:/ˈɔɪli/ | adj. | 油腻的；含油的；油滑的 | Covered or saturated with oil; greasy. Also, resembling oil in appearance, texture, or behavior; unctuous. | The mechanic's hands were oily after working on the engine. 技工修完发动机后，双手油腻腻的。

The duck was covered in oily sauce. 鸭子淋满了油腻的酱汁。

| 近义词: greasy, fatty, unctuous, slick | 反义词: dry, clean, non-greasy | 词性: adjective


oily:/ˈɔɪli/ | n. | 油状物 | A substance that is oily. (Rare usage) |  There was a strange oily on the surface of the water. 水面上有一层奇怪的油状物。

The spill left a thick oily all over the road. 泄漏的油在路上留下了一层厚厚的油状物。


| 近义词: grease, oil | 反义词:  N/A (noun form is rarely used) | 词性: noun (rare)
