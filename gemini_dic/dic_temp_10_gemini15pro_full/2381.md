adipoceriform: /ˌædɪpəˈsɪərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
尸蜡的，类似尸蜡的
Resembling adipocere; waxy.  The substance is formed by the decomposition of body fat in moist or wet conditions, and has a waxy or soapy appearance.
The forensic scientist noted the adipoceriform state of the remains.  法医科学家注意到遗体呈尸蜡状。
The body, submerged for months, had undergone adipoceriform changes.  这具浸泡了几个月的尸体已经发生了尸蜡样的变化。

近义词: waxy, soapy, ceruminous
反义词: (No direct antonyms, but consider)  decomposed, putrid, skeletal
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From adipocere + -iform.  Adipocere comes from Latin *adipōcēra, from adipis ("fat") + cēra ("wax").  -iform is a suffix meaning "having the form of," from Latin -formis.
