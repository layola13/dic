camarilla:/ˌkæməˈrɪlə/ | n. | 小集团，派系；私党 | A group of unofficial advisors, often close friends or associates, who influence a powerful person, especially a ruler. | The president’s camarilla was known for its secrecy and influence. 总统的私人顾问团因其神秘性和影响力而闻名。| The king was heavily influenced by his camarilla of nobles. 国王深受贵族小集团的影响。 |  clique, cabal, faction | opposition, opponents | noun
