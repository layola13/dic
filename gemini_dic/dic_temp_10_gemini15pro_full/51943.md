diabolically:/ˌdaɪəˈbɒlɪkli/ /ˌdaɪəˈbɑːlɪkli/
adjective
恶魔般地；极其邪恶地；残忍地
in an extremely wicked or cruel way; characteristic of the Devil
例句：The prison was diabolically overcrowded.  这座监狱拥挤得令人发指。

例句：He laughed diabolically. 他恶魔般地大笑。
近义词：wickedly, evilly, fiendishly, cruelly, satanically
反义词：angelically, divinely, virtuously
词性:adjective
