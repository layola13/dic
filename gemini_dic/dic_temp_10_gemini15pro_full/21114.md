blissfully:/ˈblɪsfəli/| adv.|幸福地；快乐地；狂喜地|In a blissful manner; extremely happily; full of joy.|She sighed blissfully as she sank into the hot bath. 她舒服地泡进热水澡里，幸福地叹了口气。|happily, joyfully, ecstatically|miserably, unhappily|adverb

Etymology->
From bliss + -ful + -ly.  "Bliss" comes from Old English "bliþs," meaning "joy, happiness, kindness, grace, mercy."
