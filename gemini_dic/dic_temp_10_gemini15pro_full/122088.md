mistier:/ˈmɪstiər/ | adj. | 更雾的，雾气更重的 | Covered with or obscured by mist, to a greater degree than something else that is also misty. | The valley was mistier this morning than yesterday. 今天早上山谷里的雾比昨天更浓。|近义词：foggier, hazier, murkier |反义词：clearer, brighter |词性:形容词

mistier:/ˈmɪstiər/ | adj. | 更神秘的，更模糊的 | More vague, obscure, or unclear.  |  Her explanation was even mistier than before. 她的解释比以前更含糊不清了。 | 近义词：vaguer, more obscure, less clear |反义词：clearer, more definite | 词性:形容词
