comedians:/kəˈmiːdiənz/| n. |喜剧演员|A comedian is a person who makes people laugh by telling jokes or funny stories, or by acting in a funny way.  It's the plural form of "comedian".|The comedians performed a hilarious skit. 喜剧演员们表演了一个滑稽的小品。|
The comedians entertained the crowd with their witty jokes.  喜剧演员们用他们机智的笑话娱乐了观众。|
synonyms: humorists, comics, jokers|antonyms: tragedians, dramatists|noun
