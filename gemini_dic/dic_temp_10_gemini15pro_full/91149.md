hydantoic:/ˌhaɪdænˈtɔɪɪk/ | adj. | 乙内酰脲的；乙内酰脲酸的 | Relating to or derived from hydantoin.  A hydantoic acid is a derivative of hydantoin having the structure of a urea with one of its nitrogens substituted by a carboxymethyl group. | Hydantoic acid is an intermediate in the synthesis of hydantoin. 乙内酰脲酸是合成乙内酰脲的中间体。 | The chemist studied the properties of various hydantoic acid derivatives. 化学家研究了各种乙内酰脲酸衍生物的性质。 | synonyms: ureidoacetic, carbamoylacetic | antonyms: N/A | adjective
