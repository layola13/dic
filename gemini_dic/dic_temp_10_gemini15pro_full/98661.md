insular:/ˈɪnsjələr/ | adj. | 岛屿的；偏狭的；孤立的 | Relating to or characteristic of an island; narrow-minded or illiberal; isolated. | The islanders maintained their insular traditions. 岛民保持着他们孤立的传统。|  He has an insular view of the world. 他对世界有一种狭隘的看法。 | 近义词：narrow-minded, provincial, parochial, isolated, detached | 反义词：cosmopolitan, broad-minded, open-minded, worldly | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *insulāris*, from *īnsula* (“island”).
