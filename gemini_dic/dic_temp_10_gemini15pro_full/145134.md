overarticulated: /ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过分清晰发音的；过度 articulation 的 | Pronouncing each syllable very distinctly and precisely, sometimes to an excessive degree. |  The actor overarticulated his lines, making him sound unnatural.  这位演员的台词发音过于清晰，听起来很不自然。

She overarticulated the word "nuclear" as if speaking to a child. 她把“nuclear”这个词的发音过度清晰，好像在跟孩子说话。 | 近义词: precise, distinct, exaggerated  | 反义词: slurred, mumbled, indistinct | 词性: adjective

词源: over- + articulate + -ed
"over-" 表示“过度”，“过分”
"articulate" 来自拉丁语 "articulatus," 过去分词 "articulare," 意思是“清晰地连接或表达”，源自 "artus," 意思是“关节”。
"-ed" 过去分词后缀，用于构成形容词。
