fells:/felz/
n.|荒野，丘陵地带；（尤指）高沼地|A high barren or stony moorland, especially in northern England.|例句：The windswept fells of the Lake District. 被风吹拂的湖区荒原。|近义词：moors, uplands, highlands|反义词:valleys, lowlands|词性:名词

fells:/felz/
v.|砍伐（树木）；击倒；杀死|Third-person singular simple present indicative form of fell.|例句：He fells a tree with a single blow. 他一击就砍倒了一棵树。|近义词：cut down, chop down, knock down|反义词:plant, grow|词性:动词
