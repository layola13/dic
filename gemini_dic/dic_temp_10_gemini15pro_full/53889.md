discorded:/dɪˈskɔːrdɪd/
adjective
不和谐的，不一致的
Characterized by conflict, disagreement, or lack of harmony.
The meeting ended in discorded voices and angry shouts. 会议在不和谐的声音和愤怒的叫喊声中结束。
The discorded notes of the beginner pianist grated on my ears. 初学钢琴家不和谐的音符刺激了我的耳朵。
Synonyms:  discordant, clashing, conflicting, inharmonious, jarring, cacophonous
Antonyms: harmonious, agreeable, concordant, consonant, tuneful
词性: 形容词
