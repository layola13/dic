exchangeably:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒəbli/| adv.|可互换地，交换地|in a way that can be exchanged with each other; with the possibility of being exchanged.|The terms "soda" and "pop" are used exchangeably in some regions. 在某些地区，“苏打水”和“汽水”这两个词可以互换使用。|Synonyms: interchangeably, reciprocally, alternatively|Antonyms: non-interchangeably, irreplaceably|Part of Speech: Adverb

exchange:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/| n. v.|交换，交流，兑换|n. an act of giving one thing and receiving another (especially of the same type or value) in return. v. give something and receive something of the same kind in return.|We exchanged gifts at Christmas. 我们在圣诞节交换了礼物.  They exchanged phone numbers. 他们交换了电话号码。|Synonyms: trade, swap, barter|Antonyms: keep, retain, hoard|Part of Speech: Noun, Verb
