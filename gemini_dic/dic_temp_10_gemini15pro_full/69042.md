faubourg:/ˈfoʊbʊərɡ/ (US), /ˈfəʊbʊəɡ/ (UK)| n. |郊区，市郊；城郊住宅区|A suburb, especially one in or near Paris. Originally, it referred to an area outside the city walls.|She lives in a charming faubourg of Paris.|近义词: suburb, outskirts, periphery|反义词: city center, downtown|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *forsbourg, from fors "outside" (from Latin foris "outside") + bourg "town" (from Frankish *burg).
