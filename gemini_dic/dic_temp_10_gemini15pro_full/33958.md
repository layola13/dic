cis:/sɪs/| adj.|顺式，同侧的|On the same side as; denoting or relating to a molecular structure in which two particular atoms or groups lie on the same side of a given plane in the molecule, in particular denoting an isomer in which substituents at opposite ends of a carbon–carbon double bond are on the same side of the bond.|Cis and trans isomers have different physical and chemical properties. 顺式和反式异构体具有不同的物理和化学性质。|近义词：syn|反义词:trans|词性:形容词
