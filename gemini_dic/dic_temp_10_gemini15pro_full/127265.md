navarch:/ˈnævɑːrk/ | n. |海军上将；舰队司令|A commander of a fleet or squadron; an admiral.|The navarch gave the order to attack. 海军上将下令进攻。|近义词: admiral, fleet commander |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps subordinate officer) |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *navarchus, from Greek ναύαρχος (naúarkhos), from ναῦς (naûs, “ship”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule, command”).
