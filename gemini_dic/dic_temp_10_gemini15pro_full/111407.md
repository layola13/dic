livor:/ˈlaɪvɔːr/| n. |瘀斑，尸斑|A discoloration of the skin caused by the pooling of blood after death.|Example 1: The livor mortis indicated the body had been lying on its back for several hours. 尸斑表明尸体仰卧了好几个小时。

Example 2: The pathologist noted the livor in his report. 病理学家在他的报告中记录了瘀斑。|Synonym: lividity, hypostasis|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific physiological process) |noun
