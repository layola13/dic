noiseless:/ˈnɔɪzləs/| adj.|无声的，安静的|Without any noise; silent.|例句：The owl's flight was noiseless. 猫头鹰的飞行悄无声息。|近义词：silent, quiet, hushed|反义词：noisy, loud, clamorous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "noise" + "-less". "Noise" comes from Old French "noise" (quarrel, uproar), possibly influenced by Latin "nausea" (seasickness). "-less" is a suffix meaning "without".
