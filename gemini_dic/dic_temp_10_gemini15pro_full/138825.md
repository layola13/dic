nuncupatory:/nʌŋˈkjuːpətɔːri/ |/nʌŋˈkʌpətɔːri/
adj.|口头的；口述的；未立正式遗嘱的|Declared or done orally; made by word of mouth, not in writing, especially of a will or testament.|He made a nuncupatory will on his deathbed. 他在临终前口述了遗嘱。|Synonyms: oral, verbal, spoken, unwritten|Antonyms: written, documented, formal|adjective
