bilinguist: /baɪˈlɪŋɡwɪst/ | n. | 双语者；通晓两种语言的人 | A person who can speak two languages fluently. |  She is a bilinguist in English and French. 她是一位精通英语和法语的双语者。

| bilingual |  adj. | 双语的 | Able to speak two languages fluently. | The school offers a bilingual program in English and Spanish. 这所学校提供英语和西班牙语的双语课程。

| bilingualism | n. | 双语现象 | The ability to speak two languages fluently. | Bilingualism is becoming increasingly common in today's globalized world. 在当今全球化的世界中，双语现象变得越来越普遍。


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + linguist (language expert). Ultimately from Latin lingua (tongue, language).

SYNONYMS->
polyglot, multilingual (person)

ANTONYMS->
