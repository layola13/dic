nonsupposed:/ˌnɒnsəˈpəʊzd/ /ˌnɑːnsəˈpoʊzd/
adjective
非预期的，不应该的，未被假定的
Not supposed to happen or exist; unexpected or inappropriate.
She found herself in a nonsupposed meeting. 她发现自己在一个非预期的会议上。
His nonsupposed arrival caused quite a stir. 他未被假定的到来引起了一阵骚动。
近义词:unexpected, unintended, unforeseen, unanticipated
反义词:expected, intended, foreseen, anticipated
词性:adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "supposed" (past participle of "suppose")
