hoboes:/ˈhoʊboʊz/| n. |流浪汉，游民|A homeless person, typically one who travels from place to place.|例句：The hoboes gathered around the fire, sharing stories and food. 流浪汉们聚集在篝火旁，分享故事和食物。

例句：During the Great Depression, many people became hoboes, riding the rails in search of work. 在大萧条时期，许多人成了流浪汉，乘坐火车四处寻找工作。


|近义词：vagrant, tramp, drifter, bum|反义词:resident, settler|词性:名词
