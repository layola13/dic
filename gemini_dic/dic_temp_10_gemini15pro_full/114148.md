mahayanas:/ˌmɑːhəˈjɑːnəz/ | n. | 大乘佛教 | One of the two main existing branches of Buddhism and a term for classification of Buddhist philosophies and practice.  It developed out of early Buddhist schools in India. |  Many Mahayana sutras emphasize the Bodhisattva ideal. 许多大乘佛经强调菩萨理想。|  The Dalai Lama follows the Mahayana tradition. 达赖喇嘛遵循大乘佛教的传统。| synonyms: Greater Vehicle,  | antonyms: Hinayana (now considered pejorative and replaced by Theravada) | noun
