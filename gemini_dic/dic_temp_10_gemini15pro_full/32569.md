chirality: /kaɪˈrælɪti/ | n. | 手性 | The property of a molecule that makes it impossible to superimpose it on its mirror image.  A chiral molecule cannot be superimposed on its mirror image. |  Many biological molecules exhibit chirality, such as amino acids and sugars. 许多生物分子都表现出手性，例如氨基酸和糖类。|  The chirality of a drug can affect its pharmacological activity. 药物的手性会影响其药理活性。 |  handedness, asymmetry | symmetry | noun
