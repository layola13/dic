gregarious:/ɡrɪˈɡɛəriəs/| adj.|爱社交的，群居的|Fond of company; sociable.  Tending to associate with others of one's kind: living in flocks or loosely organized communities.|例句：Gregarious animals, such as sheep, tend to form groups.  像绵羊这样的群居动物倾向于组成群体。

例句：She's such a gregarious person, always surrounded by friends. 她是一个非常合群的人，总是被朋友包围着。|近义词：sociable, outgoing, convivial, affable|反义词：solitary, antisocial, introverted|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gregarius*, from grex "flock, herd".  Related to congregate and aggregate.
