nonascendancy:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsi/
n.
缺乏优势，非支配地位
lack of dominance or controlling influence; the state of not being in a position of power or authority
The party's nonascendancy in the polls is a cause for concern.  该党在民意调查中缺乏优势令人担忧。
Her nonascendancy in the company hierarchy was frustrating. 她在这个公司等级制度中没有优势，这让她很沮丧。
lack of power, subordination, inferiority
dominance, ascendancy, superiority
noun
