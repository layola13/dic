calfskin: /ˈkɑːfskɪn/ (US) /ˈkɑːfskɪn/ (UK)
n.|小牛皮; 小牛皮革|The leather made from the hide of a calf.  A soft, smooth leather.|She bought a pair of expensive calfskin gloves.|近义词：leather, hide, kip, kidskin|反义词: -(none direct antonym, could consider synthetic leather as a functional opposite)
