chun:/tʃʌn/| n.|大块，厚片，相当大的量|a thick piece or lump of something; a substantial amount of something|例句：He cut the cheese into large chuns. 他把奶酪切成大块。|例句：She spends a good chun of her time reading. 她花了很多时间读书。|近义词：lump, hunk, piece, chunk, portion|反义词: sliver, bit, scrap|词性:名词


词源:
Possibly related to "chunk," which has a similar meaning and may share a common origin, perhaps imitative.  The exact etymology is uncertain.
