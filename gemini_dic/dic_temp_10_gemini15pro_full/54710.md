disorienting:/dɪsˈɔːriˌɛntɪŋ/ /dɪsˈɔːriˌentɪŋ/
adjective
使人迷惑的，使人失去方向感的
causing a person to lose their sense of direction, or to feel confused
The flashing lights were disorienting.  闪烁的灯光令人迷惑。
近义词: confusing, bewildering, perplexing
反义词: orienting, guiding, clarifying
词性: adjective
