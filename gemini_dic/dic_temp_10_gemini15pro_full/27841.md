canonically:/kəˈnɒnɪkli/ /kəˈnɑːnɪkli/| adv.|经典地；权威地；正统地；按照教会法的规定|According to or ordered by canon law; following or in agreement with accepted rules or standards.|The book is canonically considered the greatest of its genre. 这本书被公认为是该类型中最伟大的作品。|近义词：officially, authoritatively, traditionally, doctrinally|反义词：unofficially, heterodoxly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *canonicus* “according to church law”, from Greek *kanōnikos* "according to rule," from *kanōn* "rule, standard."
