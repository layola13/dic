funner:/ˈfʌnər/| adj.|更有趣的，更好玩的|A nonstandard comparative form of fun, considered incorrect by many.  It is used colloquially, especially in informal speech, to mean "more fun."|This game is funner than the last one. 这个游戏比上一个更好玩。|The party was way funner than I expected.  这个派对比我想象的要有趣得多。|近义词：more fun, more enjoyable, more entertaining|反义词：less fun, more boring, less enjoyable|词性:adjective
