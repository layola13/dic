hypogynous:/ˌhaɪpəˈdʒaɪnəs/
adj.
下位花的，子房上位花的
(of a flower) having the other floral parts inserted below the gynoecium.
The rose family is characterized by hypogynous flowers.  玫瑰科植物的特点是下位花。
近义词: superior (referring to the ovary)
反义词: epigynous, perigynous
词性: adjective
