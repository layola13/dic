disfigurers:/dɪsˈfɪɡjərərz/
n.
损毁者，毁容者
People who disfigure something, especially someone's face or body.
The vandals were disfigurers of public art. 这些故意破坏者损毁了公共艺术品。
The acid attack left her with permanent scars, the attacker a cruel disfigurer. 酸性袭击使她留下了永久的伤疤，袭击者是一个残忍的毁容者。
synonyms: vandals, defacers, mutilators, destroyers
antonyms: restorers, beautifiers, healers
noun
