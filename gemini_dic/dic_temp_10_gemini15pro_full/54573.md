disloyally:/dɪsˈlɔɪəli/ | adv. | 不忠诚地，背信弃义地 | In a manner that lacks loyalty; faithlessly. | He acted disloyally towards his friends. 他对他的朋友们不忠。 | 近义词: faithlessly, treacherously, perfidiously | 反义词: loyally, faithfully | 词性: 副词

词源: dis- (not) + loyal + -ly (adverb suffix)  "dis-" 表示否定，"loyal" 表示忠诚的，"-ly" 是副词后缀。
