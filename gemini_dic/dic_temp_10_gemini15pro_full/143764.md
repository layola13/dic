outmastered:/ˌaʊtˈmæstərd/
verb
胜过，技艺高超过，智胜
surpassed in skill or mastery; defeated by greater skill
He outmastered his opponent in the chess tournament. 他在国际象棋比赛中战胜了对手。
近义词: outwit, outmaneuver, surpass, defeat, conquer, vanquish
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb (past tense and past participle of outmaster)
