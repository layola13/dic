nomadian:/ˈnoʊmædən/ /nəʊˈmeɪdiən/| n. adj.|游牧民，游牧的|A member of a people or tribe that has no permanent abode but moves from place to place, usually seasonally and within a well-defined territory.  A person who lives a nomadic lifestyle.|Example: The nomads traveled across the desert seeking pasture for their animals. 这些游牧民穿越沙漠寻找牧场放牧他们的牲畜。

Example:  She lived a nomadic life, moving from city to city for her work. 她过着游牧般的生活，为了工作从一个城市搬到另一个城市。|近义词：rover, wanderer, migrant, traveler|反义词:settler, resident|词性:名词, 形容词
