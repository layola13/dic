fused:/fjuːzd/| adj.|熔化的，融合的，保险丝烧断的|Joined or blended together by or as if by melting; combined; mixed. Also, having blown a fuse (electrical).|The two metals were fused together at high temperature.  这两种金属在高温下熔合在一起。|The lights went out because the fuse had fused. 灯灭了，因为保险丝烧断了。|近义词：melted, blended, merged, combined, integrated|反义词：separate, divided, disconnected|词性:形容词

fuse:/fjuːz/| n. v.|保险丝，熔断器；导火线，引信；熔合，融合|A safety device that melts and interrupts an electric current if it exceeds a safe level. A cord of readily combustible material for igniting an explosive. To melt or join by melting.|The fuse blew and the lights went out. 保险丝烧断了，灯灭了。|The company decided to fuse the two departments. 公司决定合并这两个部门。|近义词：(n.) detonator, wick, (v.) melt, blend, merge|反义词：(v.) separate, divide|词性:名词,动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fusus*, past participle of *fundere* "to pour, melt," from PIE root *gheu-* "to pour."
