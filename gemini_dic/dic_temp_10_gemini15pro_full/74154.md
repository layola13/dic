foundlings:/ˈfaʊnd.lɪŋz/
n.
弃婴，被遗弃的婴儿
A young child found abandoned and whose parents are unknown.
The orphanage was full of foundlings, each with a heartbreaking story.  这所孤儿院里全是弃婴，每个孩子都有着令人心碎的故事。
Synonyms: orphan, waif, stray
Antonyms: adopted child
