backplate:/ˈbækpleɪt/| n.|背板，底板|A plate forming a back or support for something.|The backplate of the watch was engraved with a special message.  手表背板上刻着特别的讯息。|Synonyms: backing, support, mount|Antonyms: front, face|noun

backplate:/ˈbækpleɪt/| n.|护背板，防护板|A protective plate worn on the back.|The hockey player's backplate protected him from serious injury. 曲棍球运动员的护背板保护他免受重伤。|Synonyms: shield, guard, protector|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly "vulnerable area")|noun
