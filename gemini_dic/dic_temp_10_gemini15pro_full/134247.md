noninfective:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛktɪv/
adjective
非感染性的
Not capable of causing infection.
This strain of the virus is noninfective and poses no threat to public health.  这种病毒株是非感染性的，不会对公众健康构成威胁。
The wound was cleaned thoroughly to ensure it remained noninfective.  伤口被彻底清洁以确保它保持非感染性。
近义词:non-contagious, non-communicable
反义词:infective, contagious, communicable
词性:adjective
词源:non- (prefix meaning "not") + infective (adjective meaning "capable of causing infection")
