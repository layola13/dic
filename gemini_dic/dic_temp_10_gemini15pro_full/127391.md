near-easterly:/ˌnɪrˈiːstərli/
near-easterly| adj.|近东的；近东风的|From "near" (close to) + "easterly" (from the east).  Describes a wind direction slightly north or south of due east.|The near-easterly wind carried the scent of the sea. 近东风带来了海洋的气息。|Synonyms: east-northeasterly, east-southeasterly|Antonyms: westerly, near-westerly|adjective

near-easterly:/ˌnɪrˈiːstərli/
near-easterly| n.|近东风|A wind blowing from a direction near east.|The near-easterly filled the sails and propelled the ship forward. 近东风鼓满了帆，推动着船前进。|Synonyms:  east-northeasterly wind, east-southeasterly wind|Antonyms: westerly wind, near-westerly wind|noun
