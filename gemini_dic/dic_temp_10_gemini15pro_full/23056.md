bouncier:/ˈbaʊnsiər/
adjective
更有弹性的；更活泼的
More bouncy; having more bounce.
The new basketball is bouncier than the old one.  这个新篮球比旧的更有弹性。
The children were bouncier after their nap. 孩子们午睡后更加活泼。
近义词:springier, livelier, more resilient
反义词:less bouncy, stiffer, less lively
词性:adjective

bounciest:/ˈbaʊnsiəst/
adjective
最有弹性的；最活泼的
Most bouncy; having the most bounce.
This is the bounciest ball I've ever played with. 这是我玩过的最有弹性的球。
Of all the children, she was the bounciest. 在所有孩子中，她是最活泼的。
近义词:springiest, liveliest, most resilient
反义词:least bouncy, stiffest, least lively
词性:adjective
