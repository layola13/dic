almadia:/ælˈmeɪdiə/ | n. | 独木舟，木筏 | A raft of logs fastened together, typically used for transporting goods down a river. |  Example 1: The almadia drifted slowly down the river, laden with timber.  独木舟载着木材，缓缓地顺流而下。

 Example 2:  Almadias were a common sight on the river in those days, carrying everything from grain to people.  在那些日子里，独木舟是河上常见的景象，从粮食到人，什么都运。 | 近义词: raft, canoe, boat | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps ship for a larger, more complex vessel) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Spanish *almadiya*, from Arabic *al-ma‘adiya* meaning "the ferry".
