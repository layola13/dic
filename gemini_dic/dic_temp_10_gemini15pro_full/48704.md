defibrillator:/diːˈfɪbrɪleɪtər/ | n. | 除颤器；去纤颤器 | A device that delivers an electric shock to the heart to restore its normal rhythm. | The paramedics used a defibrillator to revive the man who had suffered a cardiac arrest.  医护人员使用除颤器使心脏骤停的男子苏醒过来。 | Synonyms: AED (automated external defibrillator), heart starter | Antonyms:  N/A | noun

defibrillate:/diːˈfɪbrɪleɪt/ | v. |  除颤；去纤颤 | To deliver an electric shock to the heart to restore its normal rhythm. | The doctors defibrillated the patient's heart. 医生对病人的心脏进行除颤。 | Synonyms: shock, cardiovert | Antonyms: N/A | verb
