cosmoramic:/ˌkɒzməˈræmɪk/ /ˌkɑːzməˈræmɪk/| adj.|宇宙全景的；包罗万象的|Relating to or resembling a cosmorama; a comprehensive or panoramic view of the universe or of a particular subject.|例句：The museum offered a cosmoramic view of the history of flight.  博物馆提供了关于飞行历史的宇宙全景图。

例句：Her novel provides a cosmoramic sweep of Russian history. 她的小说展现了俄罗斯历史的全景。|近义词：panoramic, comprehensive, sweeping, all-encompassing, broad, wide-ranging|反义词：limited, narrow, restricted|词性：形容词
词源:  From cosmo- + -orama, after cosmorama, an exhibition of views, from Greek kosmos "world, universe" + horama "sight, spectacle".
