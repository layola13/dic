hemialbumin:/ˌhɛmiˈælbjʊmɪn/
n.
半白蛋白
A protein derivative formed by the hydrolysis of albumin. It represents a smaller molecular weight fragment of the original albumin molecule.
Hemialbumin was detected in the patient's urine sample. 在病人的尿液样本中检测到了半白蛋白。
近义词: albumin fragment, derived protein
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "whole albumin")
词性: noun
