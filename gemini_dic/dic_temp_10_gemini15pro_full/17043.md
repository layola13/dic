bathhouse:/ˈbæθhaʊs/ (US), /ˈbɑːθhaʊs/ (UK)| n. |浴室，澡堂；公共浴池|A building equipped for bathing, often offering various types of baths, steam rooms, saunas, etc.  It can be a private or public facility.| The old Roman bathhouse was a center of social life. 古罗马的澡堂是社交生活的中心。

The bathhouse offers a variety of spa treatments.  这家澡堂提供各种各样的水疗护理。|近义词：spa, sauna, steam room, public bath|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'home bathroom')|noun
