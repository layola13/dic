inspectability:/ɪnˌspektəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可检查性，可检验性 | The quality of being easily inspected or examined.  The ease with which something can be inspected or examined to ensure it meets certain criteria or standards. | The inspectability of the software is crucial for finding bugs.  该软件的可检查性对于查找错误至关重要。 | inspectability, examinability, verifiability | obscurity, opacity | noun
