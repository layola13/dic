aide-memoire:/ˌeɪd memˈwɑːr/ | n. | 备忘录; 提词单 | An aide-memoire is something, such as a note, that helps you remember something. |  The president referred to his aide-memoire before answering the reporter's question. 总统在回答记者提问前参考了他的备忘录。

|Example: She kept an aide-memoire of all the things she needed to buy. 她把所有要买的东西都记在一张备忘录上。|Synonym: memorandum, crib sheet, cheat sheet, prompt | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "obstacle to memory" conceptually opposite) | noun
