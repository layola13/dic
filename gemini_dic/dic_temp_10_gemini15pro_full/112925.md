lug's:/lʌɡz/
noun
行李，耳朵，笨蛋 (informal)
belongings, especially when heavy or bulky; an ear; a clumsy or stupid person (informal)
He hauled his lugs up the stairs. 他把行李搬上楼。
Synonyms: baggage, luggage, suitcase,  (for ear) auricle, pinna, (for person) klutz, oaf
Antonyms: (for person) genius, expert
词性: 名词 (Noun)

PHRASE->

lug something up/down stairs 提着某物上/下楼
lug someone/something off to somewhere 把某人/某物拖到某地
lug it  (informal)  快点走；跑开


注意事项:
lug's 本身就是一个所有格形式，表示“某人的行李/耳朵”。单独使用lug更常见。
lug作为名词指“耳朵”时，通常用复数形式lugs。
lug作为名词指“笨蛋”时，是非正式用法。
