autumnally: /ɔːˈtʌmnəli/ | adv. | 秋季地，像秋季一样地 | In a manner characteristic of autumn; in an autumnal way. | The leaves fell autumnally. 叶子像秋天一样飘落。|  The wind blew autumnally through the trees. 秋风吹过树林。| 近义词: fall-like | 反义词: spring-like | 词性: 副词

词源: From autumn + -ally.  "Autumn" comes from Old French "autompne," ultimately from Latin "autumnus." "-ally" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a ... manner."
