embasement:/ɪmˈbɛɪsmənt/ /ɛmˈbeɪsmənt/ | n. | 窘迫，尴尬 | The state of being embarrassed; shame or awkwardness. |  She blushed with embarrassment. 她窘迫得脸红了。

embasement:/ɪmˈbɛɪsmənt/ /ɛmˈbeɪsmənt/ | n. | 困窘;阻碍 | Something that embarrasses or hinders. | Lack of funds was an embarrassment to their project.  缺乏资金对他们的项目是一个阻碍。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *embasement, from Old French embaser, from en- + bas "low."

SYNONYM->
shame, humiliation, mortification, awkwardness, discomfort, chagrin, discomfiture, sheepishness, abashment

ANTONYM->
confidence, composure, poise, pride, self-assurance
