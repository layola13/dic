mellite:/ˈmɛlaɪt/| n.|蜜蜡石，蜜石|A mineral consisting of hydrated aluminum mellitate, occurring in yellow crystals in brown coal.|Mellite is a rare mineral found in association with fossilized wood. 蜜蜡石是一种罕见的矿物，与木化石共生。|Synonyms: honeystone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
