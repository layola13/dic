dowager:/ˈdaʊədʒər/| n.|寡妇；贵妇人|A woman who holds some title or property from her deceased husband, especially the widow of a king, duke, etc. | The dowager queen still held considerable influence. 太后仍然拥有相当大的影响力。|Synonyms: widow, matron, lady|Antonyms: bachelor, spinster|Noun

dowagers:/ˈdaʊədʒərz/| n.|寡妇们；贵妇人们|Plural form of "dowager," referring to multiple women who hold titles or property from their deceased husbands.|The dowagers of the noble families gathered for the annual ball.  贵族家庭的寡妇们聚集在一起参加年度舞会。|Synonyms: widows, matrons, ladies|Antonyms: bachelors, spinsters|Noun
