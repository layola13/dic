falsifier:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪər/ | n. | 伪造者；弄虚作假者 |  A person who falsifies something, such as a document, signature, or piece of information. | The police arrested the falsifier who had been creating counterfeit money. 警方逮捕了那个一直在制造假币的伪造者。 |  The art expert identified the painting as a forgery and began searching for the falsifier.  艺术专家认定这幅画是赝品，并开始寻找伪造者。 | synonyms: forger, counterfeiter, liar, cheat, fraud, impostor | antonyms: authenticator, verifier | noun


falsify: /ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 伪造；篡改；歪曲 |  To make (something) false or untrue; to alter (something) so as to mislead. |  He falsified the documents to make it look like he owned the land. 他伪造了文件，使看起来像他拥有这块土地。 | She falsified her age on the application form. 她在申请表上谎报了年龄。 | synonyms: forge, counterfeit, fake, distort, misrepresent, fabricate | antonyms: verify, authenticate, confirm | verb
