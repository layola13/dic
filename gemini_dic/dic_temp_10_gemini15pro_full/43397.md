crankiness's:/ˈkræŋkinəsɪz/
n.
暴躁，易怒
The state or quality of being cranky; crankiness.
Her crankiness's this morning was due to a lack of sleep. 她今天早上的暴躁是由于睡眠不足。
近义词：irritability, grumpiness, petulance, cantankerousness
反义词：good humor, cheerfulness, pleasantness
词性: 名词 (noun)

词源：From cranky + -ness.  "Cranky" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Middle Dutch *kranck (“sick, weak”).
