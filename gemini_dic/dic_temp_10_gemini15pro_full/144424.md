outsulked:/ˌaʊtˈsʌlkt/
verb
比…更愠怒；生闷气胜过
To sulk longer or more intensely than someone else.
She outsulked her brother, refusing to speak for a full week. 她比她哥哥更会生闷气，整整一个星期都不说话。
近义词：outlast, surpass in sulking
反义词：yield, give in
