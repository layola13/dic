meltingness's:/ˈmɛltɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
融化性，熔化度（罕见）
The quality or state of being melting or meltable.  This word is extremely uncommon and often replaced by more common terms like "meltability," "fusibility," or simply "melting point."
The meltingness's of the alloy surprised the scientists.  这种合金的熔化度使科学家们感到惊讶。
Near synonyms: meltability, fusibility, liquidity (when melted)
Antonyms: solidity, infusibility, refractoriness
