hummocking:/ˈhʌməkɪŋ/ | n. | 隆起，形成小丘 | The process of forming hummocks, small mounds or hills.  | The hummocking of the terrain made it difficult to traverse. 地形起伏不平，难以通行。 | 近义词：mounding, ridging | 反义词：flattening | 词性: noun
词源: From hummock + -ing.  Hummock ultimately comes from a Low German word meaning "small hill."
