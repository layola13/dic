arc:/ɑːrk/ | n. | 弧，弧形，电弧 | A curved line or shape. A luminous discharge of electricity between two electrodes. | The rainbow formed a beautiful arc across the sky. 彩虹在天空中形成了美丽的弧形。 The welder created an arc to join the two pieces of metal. 焊工制造了一个电弧来连接两块金属。 |  近义词: curve, arch, bend | 反义词: line, straight | noun

arc:/ɑːrk/ | v. | 形成弧形，呈弧形运动 | To move in a curved path. | The ball arced through the air. 球在空中划出一道弧线。 The car arced around the corner. 汽车绕着拐角转弯。 | 近义词: curve, bend, arch | 反义词: straighten | verb
