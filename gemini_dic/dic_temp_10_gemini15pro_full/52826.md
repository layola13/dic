diluting:/daɪˈluːtɪŋ/
v.
稀释;冲淡
making (a liquid) thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent.
The chemist is diluting the acid with water. 化学家正在用水稀释酸。
近义词:thinning, weakening, watering down
反义词:concentrating, strengthening
词性:Verb, present participle of dilute.
