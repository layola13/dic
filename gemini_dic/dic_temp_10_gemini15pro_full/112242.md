loot:/luːt/
n.|战利品，赃物，掠夺物|Goods, especially private property, taken from an enemy in war or by robbers. |The soldiers looted the city after it fell. 士兵们在城市沦陷后洗劫了这座城市。|synonyms: spoils, plunder, booty, prize |antonyms:  restitution, return|noun
loot:/luːt/
v.|掠夺，洗劫|To steal goods from (a place), typically during a war or riot.|Rioters looted shops and set fire to cars. 暴徒洗劫了商店并放火烧汽车。|synonyms: ransack, pillage, plunder|antonyms: protect, guard|verb
