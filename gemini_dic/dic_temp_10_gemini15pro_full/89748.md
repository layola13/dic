hopping-mad:/ˈhɒpɪŋ mæd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːpɪŋ mæd/ (US)| adj.|暴跳如雷，气急败坏|Extremely angry; furious.|例句：He was hopping-mad when he found out his car had been vandalized. 他发现他的车被破坏了，气得暴跳如雷。

例句：She was hopping-mad that they had forgotten her birthday.  他们忘记了她的生日，她气得要命。|近义词：furious, enraged, livid, infuriated|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From "hopping" (jumping up and down, as in agitation or excitement) + "mad" (angry).  The image is of someone so angry they are practically jumping up and down with rage.
