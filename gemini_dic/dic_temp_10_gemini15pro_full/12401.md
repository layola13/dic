asic: /ˈeɪsɪk/ (US) /ˈeɪsɪk/ (UK)| n. |专用集成电路|An integrated circuit customized for a particular use, rather than intended for general-purpose use.|Example: Designing an ASIC for a specific task can be more efficient than using a general-purpose processor. 为特定任务设计专用集成电路比使用通用处理器更有效。|Synonym: custom chip, integrated circuit, microchip|Antonym: general-purpose processor|Noun
