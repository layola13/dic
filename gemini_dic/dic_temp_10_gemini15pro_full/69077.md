fauna:/ˈfɔːnə/| n. |动物群|The animals of a particular region, habitat, or geological period.|例句：The fauna of the Amazon rainforest is incredibly diverse. 亚马逊雨林的动物群极其多样化。

例句：Studying the local fauna can reveal much about the ecosystem. 研究当地的动物群可以揭示很多关于生态系统的信息。|近义词：wildlife, animals|反义词:flora|noun
