discrimination:/dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |歧视，区别，辨别力|The act of treating someone differently based on their race, religion, gender, etc., or the ability to recognize and understand the difference between things.|例句：Discrimination based on race is illegal. 基于种族的歧视是非法的。

discrimination:/dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |鉴别力；识别力|The ability to recognize and understand the difference between things.|例句：He showed great discrimination in his choice of wines. 他在选择葡萄酒方面展现了极佳的鉴赏力。


近义词：bias, prejudice, bigotry (for negative discrimination), distinction, differentiation|反义词: fairness, impartiality, equality|词性:noun
