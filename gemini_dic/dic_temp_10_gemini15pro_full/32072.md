cherubs:/ˈtʃɛrəbz/| n. |小天使，胖娃娃|A winged angelic being described in biblical tradition as attending on God. Often depicted as a plump child with wings.|The painting depicted a host of cherubs fluttering around the Virgin Mary. 这幅画描绘了一群小天使围绕着圣母玛利亚飞舞。|Synonyms: angel, seraph, putto|Antonyms: devil, demon|Noun
