neurophysiology:/ˌnʊəroʊˌfɪzɪˈɑːlədʒi/ /ˌnjʊərəʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒi/| n. |神经生理学|The branch of physiology that deals with the functions of the nervous system.|Neurophysiology helps us understand how the brain works. 神经生理学帮助我们理解大脑是如何工作的.|Neuroscience, neurobiology| Psychology |noun
Etymology->
From neuro- + physiology.  Neuro- comes from Greek *neuron* "nerve." Physiology comes from Greek *phusis* "nature" + *-logia* "study."
