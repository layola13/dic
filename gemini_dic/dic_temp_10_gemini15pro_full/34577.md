clearing-house:/ˈklɪərɪŋ haʊs/| n.|清算所；交换中心；信息交流中心|A central agency or institution that balances accounts between parties, especially in financial transactions, or that coordinates the distribution of information. |例句：The clearing-house processes millions of transactions every day. 清算所每天处理数百万笔交易。

例句：The organization acts as a clearing-house for information on endangered species. 该组织充当濒危物种信息的交换中心。|近义词：exchange, center, hub|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

clearing-house:/ˈklɪərɪŋ haʊs/| n.|票据交换所|A place where banks exchange checks and settle balances. |例句：The checks were sent to the clearing-house for processing. 支票被送到票据交换所进行处理。

例句：The modern banking system relies heavily on efficient clearing-houses. 现代银行系统严重依赖高效的票据交换所。|近义词：check exchange, clearing center|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
