blackener:/ˈblækənər/ | n. |涂黑剂，使变黑之物|Something that blackens something else. |例句：The shoe blackener made my shoes look new again. 鞋油让我的鞋子看起来焕然一新。

例句：The silver blackener reacted quickly with the metal.  这种使银变黑的药剂与金属快速反应。|近义词：darkener, dye |反义词: brightener, lightener |词性:noun


blacken:/ˈblækən/ | v. |使变黑，涂黑；诽谤，诋毁|To make or become black; to defame or slander someone. |例句：The smoke blackened the walls of the house. 烟熏黑了房子的墙壁。

例句：He tried to blacken her reputation with lies. 他试图用谎言败坏她的名声。|近义词：darken, tarnish, defame |反义词: whiten, brighten, clear |词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blacnian (to blacken), from *blac (black).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *blakaz (burnt, black).
