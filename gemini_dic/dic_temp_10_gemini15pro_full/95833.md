incorporealities:/ɪnkɔːrpɔːriˈælɪtiz/
n.
无形体；非物质性；抽象概念
The state or quality of being incorporeal; immateriality.  Lacking material form or substance.
He pondered the incorporealities of the soul. 他思考灵魂的非物质性。
Synonyms: immaterialities, intangibles, abstractions
Antonyms: corporealities, physicalities, tangibles
noun
