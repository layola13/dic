beccabunga:/ˌbekəˈbʌŋɡə/ | n. | 布卡本加；溪畔水芥 | A creeping perennial herb (Veronica beccabunga) of the snapdragon family, growing in shallow running water and having small blue flowers.  | Example: The beccabunga thrived along the edges of the clear stream.  溪畔水芥在清澈的小溪边茂盛地生长着。 | Example: We identified the beccabunga by its characteristic small blue flowers and its aquatic habitat. 我们通过其特有的蓝色小花及其水生栖息地识别出溪畔水芥。 | Synonym: brooklime | Antonym: N/A | noun
