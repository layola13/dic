nippily:/ˈnɪpɪli/
adjective
寒冷地，刺骨地
bitingly cold; chilling.
The wind blew nippily across the frozen lake. 寒风刺骨地吹过结冰的湖面。
近义词:bitingly, sharply, coldly
反义词:warmly, mildly
词性:adjective

nippily:/ˈnɪpɪli/
adverb
少量地; 适度地
in a nippy manner; quickly and smartly.  (This meaning is less common.)
He nippily finished his chores and went out to play.  他快速地完成了家务，然后出去玩耍了。(此用法较少见)
近义词:quickly, smartly, briskly
反义词:slowly, sluggishly
词性:adverb


词源:From "nippy" (meaning biting or sharp) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Nippy" likely originated from "nip" (meaning to pinch or bite), which has roots in Proto-Germanic and Old Norse.
