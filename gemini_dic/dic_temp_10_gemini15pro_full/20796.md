blarneyed:/ˈblɑːr.nid/ | adj. |  奉承的；花言巧语的；油嘴滑舌的 | Persuaded by flattery or artful talk; sweet-talked. | He blarneyed her into lending him the money. 他花言巧语地哄她借钱给他。 | Synonyms: flattered, cajolled, sweet-talked, wheedled | Antonyms: straightforward, honest, frank | adjective

Etymology: From Blarney Stone, a stone in Blarney Castle, Ireland, said to impart eloquence to those who kiss it.  Ultimately from Irish *blárna "field, plain" (compare Irish blárnaim "I flatter").
