hindutva:/ˈhɪndʊtvə/| n. |印度教特性；印度教至上主义|An ideology seeking to establish the hegemony of Hindus and Hindu values within India.  It promotes Hindu cultural, national, and religious identity.|

例句：Hindutva has become a significant political force in India.  印度教特性已经成为印度一股重要的政治力量。

例句：Critics argue that Hindutva can be exclusionary towards minorities. 批评人士认为，印度教至上主义可能会排斥少数族裔。


近义词：Hindu nationalism, Hindu supremacy|反义词: Secularism, pluralism |词性:noun
