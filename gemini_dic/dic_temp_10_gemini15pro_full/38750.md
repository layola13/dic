condominium:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (US), /ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (UK)| n. |公寓；一套公寓；共同统治；共管|A building or complex of buildings containing a number of individually owned apartments or houses.|She lives in a condominium overlooking the ocean. 她住在一栋可以俯瞰大海的公寓里。|Synonym: apartment, flat, unit|Antonym: house, detached house|noun

condominiums:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (US), /ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (UK)| n. |公寓（复数）；多套公寓|Plural form of condominium.|They are building new condominiums downtown. 他们在市中心建造新的公寓楼。|Synonym: apartments, flats, units|Antonym: houses, detached houses|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *condominium*  (joint sovereignty), from *con-* (together) + *dominium* (ownership, right of ownership).
