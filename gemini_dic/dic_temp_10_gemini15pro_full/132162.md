noncurrently:/ˌnɒnˈkʌrəntli/ | adv. | 非即期地，非流动地 | Not currently; not at the present time; in a way that does not pertain to the current period. |  The company listed its noncurrently assets on the balance sheet.  该公司在其资产负债表上列出了非流动资产。|  The loan is classified as noncurrently due to its extended repayment terms. 这笔贷款由于其延长的还款期限而被归类为非流动负债。| 近义词: long-term, fixed | 反义词: currently, short-term | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "currently" (adverb meaning "at the present time").  "Current" ultimately derives from the Latin "currere," meaning "to run."
