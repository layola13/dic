overindustrializing: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度工业化
The act of developing too much industry in a region or country, often leading to negative consequences such as pollution, resource depletion, and social disruption.
Many critics argue that the city is overindustrializing, leading to severe air pollution. 许多评论家认为，这座城市过度工业化，导致严重的空气污染。
The rapid overindustrializing of the region has put a strain on its natural resources. 该地区快速的过度工业化给其自然资源带来了压力。
近义词: overdeveloping
反义词: underdeveloping
词性: verb (present participle)
