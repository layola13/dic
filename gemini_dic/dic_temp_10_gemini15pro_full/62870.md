enlard:/ɪnˈlɑːrd/ /ɛnˈlɑrd/| vt.|使肥沃，使富足；润色（文章）；在…中加入（脂肪或猪油）|To enrich or enhance something, often by adding fat or lard. enlard:/ɪnˈlɑːrd/ /ɛnˈlɑrd/| n.|（烹调用的）猪油|Lard used in cooking.|The recipe called for a tablespoon of enlard.  食谱需要一汤匙猪油。|近义词：lard, fat|反义词: N/A|noun
