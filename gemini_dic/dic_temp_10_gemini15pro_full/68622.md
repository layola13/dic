fantasying:/ˈfæntəsaɪzɪŋ/
v.
幻想，想象
The act of indulging in fantasies; imagining or daydreaming.
She was fantasying about winning the lottery. 她正幻想赢得彩票。
He spends most of his time fantasying about being a rock star. 他大部分时间都在幻想成为摇滚明星。
近义词:daydreaming, imagining, visualizing
反义词:facing reality

词性:v. (动名词)
