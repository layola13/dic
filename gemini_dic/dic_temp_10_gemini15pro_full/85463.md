hask:/hɑːsk/| n.|干草；干草堆；一刈干草|A pile of cut and dried grass, used as animal fodder.|The farmer stacked the hask in the barn. 农民把干草堆放在谷仓里。|近义词：hay, fodder, straw|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *hask*, from Old English *hæsc* (“bundle of brushwood”), from Proto-Germanic *haskiz (“bundle of twigs”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kes- (“to cut”).  Cognate with Dutch *hes*, German *Haspel* (“reel, spool”).
