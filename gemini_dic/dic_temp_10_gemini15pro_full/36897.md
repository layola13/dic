colorings:/ˈkʌlərɪŋz/
noun
着色，染色，配色方案
the act of adding color to something; a substance used to add color; a particular combination of colors
The artist experimented with different colorings for the mural.  这位艺术家尝试了不同的配色方案来绘制壁画。
The fabric's colorings faded after several washes.  这种织物的颜色在几次洗涤后褪色了。
synonyms: colorations, hues, tints, shades, pigmentation
antonyms: bleachings, fadings, dullness
