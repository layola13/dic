microcyte:/ˈmaɪkrəˌsaɪt/| n. |小红细胞|A small red blood cell, usually less than 5 microns in diameter, characteristic of certain anemias.  It contains less than the normal amount of hemoglobin.|Blood tests revealed a high number of microcytes, indicating iron deficiency anemia. 血液检查显示小红细胞数量很高，表明缺铁性贫血。|Synonyms: microerythrocyte|Antonyms: macrocyte|noun
