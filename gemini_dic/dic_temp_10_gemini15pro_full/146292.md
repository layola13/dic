overfatten:/ˌōvərˈfætən/ | v. | 过度喂肥；使过于肥胖 | To make excessively fat; fatten to an excessive degree. | The farmer overfattened the geese for the holiday feast.  农民为了节日盛宴过度喂肥了鹅。

The dog was overfattened on table scraps. 这条狗吃餐桌上的残羹剩饭吃得过胖了。| 近义词：overfeed | 反义词: starve, underfeed | 词性:动词
