commercialising:/kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzɪŋ/
| v.|商业化|The process of turning something into a product or service that can be sold for profit.|The company is commercialising a new technology.  这家公司正在将一项新技术商业化。|近义词：marketing, monetizing, merchandising|反义词：decommercializing|词性:动词-ing形式

commercialise:/kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/
| v.|商业化|To make something available for sale and profit.|They plan to commercialise their research findings.  他们计划将他们的研究成果商业化。|近义词：market, monetize, merchandise|反义词：decommercialize|词性:动词

commercialization:/kəˌmɜːʃələˈzeɪʃən/
| n.|商业化|The act or process of commercialising something.|The commercialization of the internet has changed the world. 互联网的商业化改变了世界。|近义词：marketing, monetization, merchandising|反义词：decommercialization|词性:名词
