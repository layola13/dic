overabundances:/ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndənsɪz/
noun
过度丰富，过剩
An excessive amount of something.  The state of being overabundant. Plural form of overabundance.
The overabundances of food at the feast was astonishing.  宴会上食物的过剩令人惊讶。
synonyms:  excesses, superfluity, plethora, surfeit, glut
antonyms:  scarcity, dearth, lack, deficiency, insufficiency
n.
