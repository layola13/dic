cattleless:/ˈkætəllɪs/
adjective
无牛的；没有牲口的
Without cattle.
The farmer, once prosperous, now found himself cattleless after the devastating drought.  这个曾经繁荣的农民，在毁灭性的干旱之后发现自己没有了牲口。
The once-thriving ranch now stood desolate and cattleless.  曾经繁荣的牧场现在荒凉而没有牲口。
destitute, livestockless
stocked (with cattle)
adjective
