beblubber:/bɪˈblʌbər/
verb
哭诉，哭哭啼啼
To weep loudly and excessively; to cry noisily and unrestrainedly.
The child beblubbered when his ice cream fell on the ground. 孩子冰淇淋掉到地上时哭了起来。
近义词:cry, weep, sob, wail
反义词:laugh, chuckle, giggle
词性:v.

beblubber:/bɪˈblʌbər/
noun
哭诉，哭闹
Loud, excessive weeping; noisy and unrestrained crying.
Her beblubbering made it difficult to understand what had happened. 她的哭闹让人很难理解发生了什么。
近义词:crying, weeping, sobbing, wailing
反义词:laughter, giggling
词性:n.


词源:  "be-" (prefix indicating intensive action) + "blubber" (to weep noisily).  The word "blubber" itself likely imitates the sound of crying.
