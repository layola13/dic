Etymology->
From Spanish *gentío ("crowd, mob"), from Latin gentēs ("nations, peoples, clans"). Originally used by Portuguese sailors to refer to the inhabitants of India, the term was later applied to the penguin due to its supposed resemblance to a hooded figure.  The Gentoo Linux distribution takes its name from this penguin, known for its speed and intelligence.
