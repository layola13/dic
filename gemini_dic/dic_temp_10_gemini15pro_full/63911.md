epiphora:/ɪˈpɪfərə/ | n. | 首语重复；尾句重复 | The repetition of a word or phrase at the end of successive clauses, sentences, or verses. |  "I have a dream" speech by Martin Luther King Jr. is a famous example of epiphora. 马丁·路德·金的“我有一个梦想”演讲是首语重复的著名例子。

| Synonyms: epistrophe, anaphora (similar but at the beginning) | Antonyms: N/A | noun


epiphora:/ɪˈpɪfərə/ | n. | (医学) 流泪；溢泪 | Excessive tearing or watering of the eyes. |  The doctor diagnosed her epiphora as a blocked tear duct. 医生诊断她的流泪是由于泪管堵塞造成的。

| Synonyms: lacrimation, watering eyes | Antonyms: N/A | noun
