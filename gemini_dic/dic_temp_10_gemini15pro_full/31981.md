chemoreceptors:/ˈkɛməʊrɪˌsɛptəz/ (UK) /ˈkɛmoʊrɪˌsɛptərz/ (US)| n. |化学感受器|Sensory receptors that transduce a chemical signal into an action potential. These can be found in the taste buds, olfactory epithelium, carotid bodies, and aortic bodies.|The chemoreceptors in the carotid body detect changes in blood oxygen levels. 颈动脉体中的化学感受器检测血液中氧含量的变化。|近义词:sensory receptors, chemical sensors|反义词: None|词性:Noun

chemoreceptor:/ˈkɛməʊrɪˌsɛptə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɛmoʊrɪˌsɛptər/ (US)| n. |化学感受器|A sensory cell or organ responsive to chemical stimuli.|The insect's antennae are covered in chemoreceptors. 昆虫的触角上覆盖着化学感受器。|近义词:sensory receptor, chemical sensor|反义词: None|词性:Noun
