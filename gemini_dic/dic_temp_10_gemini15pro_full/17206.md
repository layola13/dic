bauxitic:/bɔːkˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
铝土矿的；似铝土矿的
Relating to, resembling, or containing bauxite.
The region is known for its bauxitic deposits.  该地区以其铝土矿矿床而闻名。
The sample was identified as bauxitic clay.  该样本被鉴定为铝土质粘土。
Synonyms:  aluminous, lateritic
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider using terms related to other rock types, e.g., siliceous, granitic)
词性: adjective
