dicynodont:/daɪˈsaɪnəˌdɒnt/ |/daɪˈsaɪnoʊˌdɑːnt/| n. |二齿兽|An extinct mammal-like reptile of the late Permian and Triassic periods, characterized by a beak-like mouth with only two tusks (in the upper jaw) and a turtle-like body.|The dicynodont fossils were found in South Africa.  这些二齿兽化石是在南非发现的。|
|近义词:therapsid (泛指兽孔目动物)|反义词: N/A (extinct animals don't have direct opposites)|词性:noun
