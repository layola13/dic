de-powers:/diːˈpaʊəz/
|v.|使失去权力，剥夺权力|To deprive of power or authority; to reduce the power or effectiveness of something or someone.|The new law de-powers local governments.  新法律剥夺了地方政府的权力。|
|v.|使失效，使无效|To make something ineffective or inoperative.|The latest software update de-powers some of the older features.  最新的软件更新使一些旧功能失效了。|
|Synonym:|weaken, disable, disarm, disempower|
|Antonym:|empower, strengthen, enable|
|verb|
