deific:/diˈɪfɪk/
adjective
神圣的；神性的
Relating to or proceeding directly from a god or deity; divine.
The ancient temple was filled with deific symbols. 古老的寺庙里充满了神圣的象征。
deific power 神力
近义词:divine, godly, sacred, holy
反义词:profane, secular, unholy, earthly
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *deificus, from Latin deus (“god”) + -ficus (from facere (“to make, do”)).
