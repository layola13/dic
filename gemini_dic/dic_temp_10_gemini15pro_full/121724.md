misprofess:/ˌmɪsprəˈfɛs/
verb
假装, 虚伪地表示, 谎称
to profess falsely; to make a false profession of
He misprofessed love for her, only wanting her money. 他假装爱她，只是为了她的钱。
Synonyms: feign, pretend, sham
Antonyms:  genuinely profess, be sincere
词性: 动词
