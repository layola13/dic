flatbread:/ˈflætbrɛd/ | n. | 薄饼，扁面包 | A thin, flat, often unleavened bread. | We had some delicious flatbread with hummus. 我们吃了一些美味的鹰嘴豆泥配薄饼。|  Near synonyms: pita, naan, tortilla | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "loaf" as a contrasting bread type) | noun


Etymology: From "flat" + "bread".  "Flat" meaning having a level surface, and "bread" referring to a food made of flour, water, and yeast or another leavening agent.
