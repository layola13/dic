decompose:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊz/ (US), /ˌdiːkəmˈpəʊz/ (UK)| v. |分解，腐烂，腐败|To break down into simpler components, to rot or decay.|例句：The dead leaves decompose and enrich the soil. 枯叶分解并使土壤肥沃。

例句：The body will decompose rapidly in warm weather.  尸体在温暖的天气下会迅速腐烂。|近义词：decay, rot, break down, disintegrate|反义词: synthesize, compose, combine|词性:动词
