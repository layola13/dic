cosponsorship:/koʊˈspɒnsərʃɪp/
n.
共同赞助，联合赞助
The act of sponsoring something together with another or other individuals or organizations.
The conference benefited greatly from the cosponsorship of several local businesses.  这次会议得益于几家当地企业的共同赞助。
The bill received bipartisan cosponsorship in the Senate.  该法案在参议院获得了 bipartisan 的共同赞助。
joint sponsorship, shared sponsorship
sole sponsorship
noun
