leptiform:/ˈlɛptɪfɔːrm/| adj.| λεπτός (leptos, “thin, delicate”) + forma (form)|瘦的，细长的，纤细的；鳞片状的|The leptiform larva is characterized by its slender body.  这种细长的幼虫的特点是身体纤细。|The butterfly's wings had a delicate, leptiform pattern. 蝴蝶的翅膀上有着精致的、鳞片状的图案。|近义词：slender, thin, delicate, scale-like|反义词:thick, robust|词性:形容词
