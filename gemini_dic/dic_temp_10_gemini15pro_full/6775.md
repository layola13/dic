ananthous:/əˈnænθəs/
adjective
无花的
without flowers; flowerless
The ananthous plant reproduced through spores.  这种无花植物通过孢子繁殖。
The fern is an ananthous plant. 蕨类植物是一种无花植物。
Near synonyms: flowerless, nonflowering
Antonyms: flowering, floriferous
word type: adjective
