interleaf:/ˌɪntərˈliːf/
n.|插入页，衬页|A leaf inserted between the leaves of a book, especially between printed leaves.|The printer included several interleaves for notes. 印刷商在书页之间加了几张衬页以便做笔记。|insert, leaf|blank page|noun
interleaf:/ˌɪntərˈliːf/
vt.|加衬页于，插入|To insert interleaves into (a book).|She interleaved the manuscript with blank pages for comments.  她在原稿中插入了空白页以便添加评论。|insert, intercalate|remove|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + leaf.
