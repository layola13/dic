denying:/dɪˈnaɪɪŋ/
v. |否认，拒绝|The company is denying all responsibility for the accident.  该公司否认对这起事故负有任何责任。|例句：He denied stealing the money. 他否认偷了钱。|近义词：refusing, rejecting, contradicting|反义词：admitting, accepting, confirming|词性:动词
denying:/dɪˈnaɪɪŋ/
adj. |否认的，拒绝的|She had a denying attitude. 她持否认的态度。|例句：His denying look made me doubt his sincerity. 他否认的表情让我怀疑他的真诚。|近义词：negative, dismissive|反义词：affirmative, accepting|词性:形容词
