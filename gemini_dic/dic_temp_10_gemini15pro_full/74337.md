fragmental:/fræɡˈmɛntəl/ | adj. |碎片的，片段的；不完整的| Consisting of fragments; broken or incomplete. | The archaeologist discovered fragmental pottery at the dig site. 考古学家在挖掘现场发现了碎片陶器。| The witness could only provide a fragmental account of the accident. 目击者只能提供对事故的片段描述。| 近义词: fragmentary, broken, incomplete, piecemeal | 反义词: whole, complete, entire | 词性: 形容词

fragmental:/fræɡˈmɛntəl/ | n. |碎片，片段 | A small part broken off or detached. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.) | The museum displayed several fragmentals of ancient sculptures.  博物馆展出了几件古代雕塑的碎片。（这种用法不如形容词形式常见）| The researchers analyzed the fragmentals of the meteorite. 研究人员分析了陨石的碎片。（这种用法不如形容词形式常见）| 近义词: fragment, piece, shard | 反义词: whole, entirety | 词性: 名词
