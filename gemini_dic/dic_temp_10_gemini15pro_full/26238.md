butternut:/ˈbʌtərˌnʌt/ (US), /ˈbʌtənʌt/ (UK)| n. adj.|白胡桃；白胡桃木；黄油坚果色|A type of squash with a sweet, nutty flavor and a tan-colored, pear-shaped shell; also refers to the tree that produces this squash and its wood.  The color is a light beige-y orangish brown.|例句：I made butternut squash soup for dinner. 我晚饭做了白胡桃南瓜汤。|近义词：squash, pumpkin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, adjective

butternut:/ˈbʌtərˌnʌt/ (US), /ˈbʌtənʌt/ (UK)| adj.|黄油坚果色的|Having the pale beige-y orange-brown color of a butternut squash.|例句：She wore a butternut sweater. 她穿了一件黄油坚果色的毛衣。|近义词：tan, beige, buff|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
