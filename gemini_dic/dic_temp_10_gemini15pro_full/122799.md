moldier:/ˈmoʊldiər/ | adj. | 更发霉的，更陈腐的 | Comparative form of moldy: more moldy.  Covered with or smelling of mold to a greater degree. | The bread in the back of the cupboard was moldier than the bread in the front.  橱柜后面的面包比前面的面包更发霉。| The basement smelled moldier after the flood. 洪水过后，地下室闻起来更发霉了。 | 近义词: mustier, fustiest, danker | 反义词: fresher, cleaner | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moldi, from Old English *moldig, from mold (“mold”) +‎ -ig. Cognate with Dutch mollig (“moldy”).
