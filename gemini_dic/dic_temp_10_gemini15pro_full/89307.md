homoerotic:/ˌhəʊməʊˈɪrɒtɪk/ /ˌhoʊmoʊɪˈrɑːtɪk/| adj.|同性情色的；同性恋爱的|Of or relating to homoeroticism; pertaining to or characterized by sexual desire directed toward a member of the same sex.|His paintings were full of homoerotic imagery. 他的绘画充满了同性情色的意象。|The novel explores homoerotic themes. 这部小说探讨了同性恋爱的主题。|近义词：homosexual, gay, lesbian|反义词：heterosexual|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From homo- + erotic.  "Homo-" comes from Greek "homos" meaning "same". "Erotic" comes from Greek "erōtikos" meaning "of love, relating to love."
