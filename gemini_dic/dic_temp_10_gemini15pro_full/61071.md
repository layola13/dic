ellipses:/ɪˈlɪpsɪːz/| n.|省略号，省略，|A series of three dots (...) indicating an omission of words in a thought or quotation.|The ellipses suggest a pause or unfinished thought in the sentence.|近义词：omission, deletion|反义词:inclusion, addition|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ellipsis, from Ancient Greek ἔλλειψις (élleipsis, “a falling short, defect, ellipse”), from ἐλλείπω (elleípō, “I leave out, omit”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + λείπω (leípō, “I leave”).
