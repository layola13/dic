milepost:/ˈmaɪlˌpoʊst/| n. |里程碑，里程标志，重要事件|A marker by the side of a road that shows distances, or an important stage in a process or development.|  The signing of the treaty was a milepost in international relations.  签署该条约是国际关系中的一个里程碑。| synonym: milestone, landmark, waymark | antonym: setback, regression | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "mile" + "post".  "Mile" comes from Old English "mil," from Latin "milia passuum" (thousand paces). "Post" comes from Old French "poste," from Latin "positus" (placed).
