enjoy:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/ (US) /ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/ (UK)| v.|享受；喜爱；享有|To take pleasure or satisfaction in something.  To experience something good or pleasurable.|I enjoy reading books. 我喜欢读书。|Synonyms: like, appreciate, relish, savor|Antonyms: dislike, hate, detest|verb

enjoy:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/ (US) /ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/ (UK)| v.|享受；喜爱；享有|To receive pleasure or satisfaction from.|She enjoys good health. 她享有健康的身体。|Synonyms: possess, benefit from|Antonyms: suffer from, lack|verb
