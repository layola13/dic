ordered:/ˈɔːrdərd/ (US), /ˈɔːdəd/ (UK)| adj.|有序的，整齐的，安排好的| Arranged or organized in a systematic or methodical way.  Derived from the verb "order," meaning to arrange something in a particular way.|The books were ordered alphabetically by author. 这些书按作者姓名字母顺序排列。|The troops lined up in ordered ranks. 部队排成整齐的队列。|近义词：arranged, organized, systematized, methodical, tidy, neat|反义词：disordered, chaotic, messy, random, haphazard|adjective
