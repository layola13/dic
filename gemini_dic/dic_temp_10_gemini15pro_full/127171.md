naturalistically:/ˌnætʃərəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 自然主义地，逼真地 | In a way that is true to life, based on careful observation of nature and natural processes. | The artist painted the scene naturalistically, capturing the subtle changes in light and shadow.  艺术家自然地描绘了这个场景，捕捉到了光线和阴影的微妙变化。 | Examples: The documentary portrayed the animals naturalistically. 这部纪录片逼真地描绘了这些动物。  He described the event naturalistically, avoiding any exaggeration or embellishment. 他自然地描述了这件事，避免任何夸大或修饰。 | Synonyms: realistically, authentically, faithfully, truly, lifelike | Antonyms: artificially, unrealistically, synthetically, unnaturally | 词性: 副词
