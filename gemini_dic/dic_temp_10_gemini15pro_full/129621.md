non-consequence:/nɒnˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ | n. | 无关紧要；不重要；没有逻辑后果 | Lack of importance or logical connection; something that doesn't matter or doesn't follow logically. | The misspelling was a non-consequence in the grand scheme of things. 拼写错误在大局中无关紧要。| synonyms: insignificance, irrelevance, triviality | antonyms: consequence, importance, significance | noun

non-consequence:/nɒnˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ | adj. | 无关紧要的；不重要的 | Not important or significant; having no noticeable effect. |  His opinion on the matter was non-consequent. 他对此事的意见无关紧要。 | synonyms: insignificant, irrelevant, trivial | antonyms: consequential, important, significant | adjective
