indehiscence:/ˌɪndiˈhɪsəns/ | n. | 不开裂，不裂开 | The indehiscence of certain fruits makes seed dispersal more challenging. | Certain fruits, like berries, exhibit indehiscence, meaning they don't split open to release seeds. | 近义词: nondehiscence | 反义词: dehiscence | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin *indehiscens*, from *in-* ("not") + *dehiscens*, present participle of *dehiscere* ("to gape, split open").  Ultimately from *de-* ("from, down, away") + *hiscere* ("to yawn, gape").
