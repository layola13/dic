ambulant:/ˈæmbjʊlənt/ (US), /ˈæmbjʊlɑːnt/ (UK)| adj.| 走动的，流动的，非卧床的 | Able to walk; not confined to bed. | The hospital has a special unit for ambulant patients. 这家医院有一个专门收治可以走动的病人的单元。|  He is now ambulant after the surgery. 手术后他现在可以走动了。 |近义词: mobile, walking, peripatetic |反义词: bedridden, immobile, sedentary | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ambulantem, present participle of ambulare ("to walk"), from ambire ("to go around").
