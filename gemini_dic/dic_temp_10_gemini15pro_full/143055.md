outblustering: /ˌaʊtˈblʌstərɪŋ/
verb
以更大的声势压倒；咆哮胜过
overpowering or drowning out (someone or something) with a louder, more forceful, or blustering sound or manner.
The politician outblustered his opponent in the debate.  这位政治家在辩论中以更大的声势压倒了他的对手。
outbluster, outshout, drown out, overpower
-, -
词性:v.
