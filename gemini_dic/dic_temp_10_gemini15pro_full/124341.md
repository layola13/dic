motif's:/moʊˈtiːfs/
noun
主题的，图案的 (plural form of motif)
A recurring theme, subject or idea.  The possessive form of "motif," indicating ownership or association with a motif.
The artist skillfully incorporated natural motif's into his paintings.  这位艺术家巧妙地将自然主题融入到他的绘画中。
synonyms: themes, patterns, designs, figures, elements
antonyms: randomness, chaos, disorder
