likeliest:/ˈlaɪkliɪst/| adj.|最可能的|Most likely; having the greatest probability. |She is the likeliest candidate to win the election. 她是最有可能赢得选举的候选人。|The likeliest outcome is a draw. 最有可能的结果是平局。|近义词：most probable, most likely|反义词：least likely|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *likliest, from like + -liest (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *līkaz ("like, similar").
