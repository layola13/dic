basinal:/ˈbeɪsɪnəl/| adj.|盆地的，流域的|Relating to or characteristic of a basin, especially a geological basin.|The basinal sediments are rich in fossils. 这些盆地沉积物富含化石。|近义词：basin-shaped, basinlike|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "upland" or "mountainous")|词性: adjective

词源: From "basin" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives). "Basin" comes from Old French "bacin" and ultimately from Late Latin "baccinus", meaning "basin, bowl".
