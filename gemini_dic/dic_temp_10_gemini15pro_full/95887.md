The correct spelling is **increase**.  There is no word "increate".  Here's the analysis for **increase**:

increase: /ɪnˈkriːs/ (UK) /ɪnˈkriːs/ (US)| n. v.|增加,增长,增多; 增加,增大,增多|The company saw a significant increase in sales this year.  这家公司今年的销售额显著增长。|  He increased the volume on the radio. 他把收音机的音量调大了。 |近义词：rise, growth, augment, expand|反义词:decrease, decline, reduce, diminish|词性:名词,动词

Etymology: From Old French *encreiss-, encres-,* present participle stem of *encreistre* "to grow," from Latin *increscere* "to grow in, grow upon, increase," from *in-* "in, on" + *crescere* "to grow."
