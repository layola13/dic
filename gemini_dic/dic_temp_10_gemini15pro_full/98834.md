intelligential: /ɪnˌtɛlɪˈdʒɛnʃəl/
adjective
智力的；理智的；需用智力的
Relating to intellect. Showing intellect. Requiring the use of the intellect.
She enjoys intelligential pursuits like reading and chess. 她喜欢阅读和下棋之类的智力活动。
Synonyms: intellectual, cerebral, mental
Antonyms: physical, instinctual, emotional
词性: 形容词
