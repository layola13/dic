myotomy:/maɪˈɒtəmi/ | n. | 肌切开术 |  The surgical incision or division of a muscle.  It's a procedure where a surgeon cuts into a muscle to release tension or spasm. |  The patient underwent a myotomy to relieve swallowing difficulties.  病人接受了肌切开术以缓解吞咽困难。 |  Surgical incision of a muscle. 手术切开肌肉。 | synonyms:  muscle incision, sfinterotomy | antonyms: myoplasty (muscle repair) | noun
