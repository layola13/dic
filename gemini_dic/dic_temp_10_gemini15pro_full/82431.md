gruffing:/ˈɡrʌfɪŋ/| adj.|粗声粗气的，生硬的|Speaking in a rough, low voice, often in an unfriendly way.  A gruff manner of speaking, typically indicating impatience or hostility.|He answered me in a gruffing tone. 他用粗声粗气的语气回答我。|Synonyms: hoarse, rough, gravelly, curt, brusque, surly|Antonyms: gentle, soft, smooth, polite, courteous|adjective
