passivize:/ˈpæsɪvaɪz/| v. |使…被动化，使…变成被动语态|To make something passive or to be made passive.  To change a sentence from the active voice to the passive voice.|The sentence "The dog bit the man" can be passivized to "The man was bitten by the dog."  狗咬了那个人 这句话可以被改为被动语态 人被狗咬了。|synonyms: deactivate, demobilize|antonyms: activate, mobilize|verb

passivize:/ˈpæsɪvaɪz/| v. |使…消极，使…不活跃|To make someone or something less active or effective.|The long illness passivized him, leaving him weak and unable to work.  长期的疾病使他变得消极，让他虚弱无力工作。|synonyms: subdue, suppress|antonyms: energize, stimulate|verb
