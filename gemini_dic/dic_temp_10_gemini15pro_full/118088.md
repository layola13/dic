menial:/ˈmiːniəl/| adj. n.|卑微的，仆人的；佣人|Not requiring much skill and lacking prestige.  Relating to unskilled, typically poorly paid work.|例句：She found herself stuck in a menial job with no prospects for advancement. 她发现自己被困在一个没有晋升前景的卑微工作中。

例句：He wasn't afraid of menial labor and was willing to do any task required. 他不害怕干粗活，愿意做任何需要做的工作。|近义词：unskilled, humble, lowly, servile, degrading|反义词：skilled, prestigious, exalted, noble|词性:形容词, 名词

menial:/ˈmiːniəl/| n.|佣人|A domestic servant.|例句：The menials were expected to work long hours for little pay.  这些佣人被期望长时间工作，报酬却很少。

例句： In the past, menials performed the most tedious household chores. 在过去，佣人做最繁琐的家务。|近义词：servant, domestic, maid, lackey|反义词：master, employer, boss|词性:名词
