categorematic:/ˌkætɪɡɔːrɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. |  范畴的，明确的 |  Of or relating to a categorematic word; a word that can stand by itself as the subject or predicate of a proposition, such as a noun or an adjective, as opposed to a syncategorematic word, such as a preposition, conjunction, or article. |  "Man" is a categorematic word, while "of" is syncategorematic. | 近义词: categorical, explicit | 反义词: syncategorematic | 词性: adjective
