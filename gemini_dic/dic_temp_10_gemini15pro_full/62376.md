endnote:/ˈɛndˌnoʊt/ | n. | 尾注，注释 | A short piece of text placed at the end of a document or section, adding a comment, citation, or reference. | The endnotes provided additional context for the historical events described in the book. 这本书的尾注为所描述的历史事件提供了额外的背景。| synonym: footnote, annotation, comment | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps main text) | noun

endnote:/ˈɛndˌnoʊt/ | v. | 加尾注 | To add an endnote to a document or section. |  The author endnoted the source of the quotation. 作者在引文后加了尾注。 | synonym: annotate, reference, cite | antonym: (No direct antonym) | verb
