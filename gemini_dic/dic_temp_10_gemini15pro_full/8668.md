anticonsumerism:/ˌænti kənˈsjuːmərɪzəm/
|n.|反消费主义|Opposition to consumerism, the equation of personal happiness with the purchase and consumption of material possessions.|Many young people are embracing anticonsumerism as a way of life. 许多年轻人将反消费主义作为一种生活方式。|Synonyms: anti-materialism, simple living|Antonyms: consumerism, materialism|noun


anticonsumerisms:/ˌænti kənˈsjuːmərɪzəmz/
|n.|反消费主义（复数，不常用）|Multiple instances or forms of opposition to consumerism.  This plural form is less common than the singular.|The various anticonsumerisms emerging today reflect diverse motivations. 如今出现的各种反消费主义反映了不同的动机。|Synonyms: anti-materialisms, simple livings (plural forms are less common)|Antonyms: consumerisms, materialisms (plural forms are less common)|noun
