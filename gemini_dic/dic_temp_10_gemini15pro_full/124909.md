mudflap:/ˈmʌdflæp/ (US) /ˈmʌdflæp/ (UK)| n.|挡泥板|A flap, usually made of rubber or plastic, attached behind a wheel of a vehicle to protect the bodywork from mud and spray.|The mudflaps on my truck are cracked and need replacing. 我的卡车的挡泥板裂了，需要更换。|近义词：splash guard, fender skirt|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
