Etymology:  From Middle English *meine, *meyne, from Old French meigne, mesnie, mesnée (“household, retinue, following”), from Vulgar Latin *mansionata, from Late Latin mansio (“dwelling, house; stay, sojourn”), from Latin maneō (“I stay, remain”).
