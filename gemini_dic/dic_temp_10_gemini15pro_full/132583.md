nondiffusible:/ˌnɒndɪˈfjuːzɪbl/ | adj. | 不可扩散的；不能渗透的 | Not able to diffuse or spread out, especially through a membrane. |  Large protein molecules are nondiffusible through cell membranes.  大的蛋白质分子不能扩散穿过细胞膜。| Synonyms: impermeable, non-permeable | Antonyms: diffusible, permeable | adjective
