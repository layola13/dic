mustered-out:/ˈmʌstərd aʊt/| v.|正式退伍，退役|(of soldiers) To be discharged from military service.|The regiment was mustered-out after the armistice. 该军团在停战后正式解散。|例句：He was mustered-out with full honors. 他光荣退伍。|近义词：discharge, demobilize|反义词：enlist, draft|词性:动词

词源:  From "muster out," meaning to assemble troops for inspection and discharge.  源于"muster out"，意为集合军队进行检查和遣散。
