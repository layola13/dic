monembryony:/ˌmɒnəˈɛmbrɪəni/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɛmbrɪəni/ | n. | 单胚生殖 | Development of a single embryo from a fertilized egg, as opposed to polyembryony.  The production of only one embryo from a zygote. |  Many angiosperms exhibit monembryony. 许多被子植物表现出单胚生殖。|  Apomixis is a form of asexual reproduction that mimics monembryony.  无融合生殖是一种模仿单胚生殖的无性繁殖形式。 | synonyms:  single embryony | antonyms: polyembryony | noun
