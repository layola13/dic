flavaniline:/ˈflævənɪlɪn/| n. |黄烷胺, 黄烷氨|A synthetic yellow dye derived from quinoline. It is a quinoline derivative and a fluorescent dye.|Flavaniline was used to dye silk in the 19th century. 黄烷胺在19世纪被用来染丝。|Synonyms: quinoline yellow,  |Antonyms: N/A|noun
Etymology-> From flavanone + aniline.  "flavanone" refers to a class of flavonoids, and "aniline" is a primary aromatic amine.
