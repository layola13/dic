一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I am a student. = 我是一名学生。
He is a teacher. = 他是一名老师。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is reading a book. = 她正在读一本书。
They are playing basketball. = 他们正在打篮球。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have been to China. = 我去过中国。
She has finished her homework. = 她已经完成了她的作业。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been studying English for three years. = 我学习英语已经三年了。
They have been working on this project for a month. = 他们已经在这个项目上工作了一个月了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was a student. = 我曾经是一名学生。
He was a teacher. = 他曾经是一名老师。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
She was reading a book when I called. = 我打电话时她正在读书。
They were playing basketball yesterday. = 他们昨天在打篮球。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had been to China before I went to Japan. = 在我去日本之前我去过中国。
She had finished her homework before dinner. = 她在晚饭前完成了作业。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been studying English for three years when I moved to the US. = 当我搬到美国时，我已经学习英语三年了。
They had been working on this project for a month when they finally finished it. = 当他们最终完成这个项目时，他们已经在这个项目上工作了一个月了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be a doctor. = 我将成为一名医生。
He will be a lawyer. = 他将成为一名律师。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
She will be reading a book tomorrow. = 她明天将正在读书。
They will be playing basketball next week. = 他们下周将正在打篮球。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have been to China by next year. = 到明年我将去过中国。
She will have finished her homework by tomorrow. = 到明天她将完成她的作业。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been studying English for five years by next year. = 到明年我将学习英语五年了。
They will have been working on this project for two months by next week. = 到下周他们将在这个项目上工作两个月了。

The house is being built. = 房子正在被建造。

疑问句:
Are you a student? = 你是学生吗？
Is he a teacher? = 他是老师吗？

否定句:
I am not a student. = 我不是学生。
He is not a teacher. = 他不是老师。
