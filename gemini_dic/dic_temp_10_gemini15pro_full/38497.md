conciator:/kənˈsaɪeɪtər/ | n. | 调解人，和事佬 | A person who acts as a mediator between two disputing people or groups. |  例句：The conciliator helped the two parties reach a compromise. 调解人帮助双方达成了妥协。

例句：The experienced conciliator skillfully navigated the complex negotiations. 经验丰富的调解员巧妙地引导了复杂的谈判。


近义词：mediator, arbitrator, peacemaker | 反义词：agitator, instigator | 词性: noun
