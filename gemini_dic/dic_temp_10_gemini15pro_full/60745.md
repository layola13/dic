electrodeless:/ɪˈlɛktrəʊdlɪs/
adjective
无电极的
Lacking electrodes; operating or functioning without electrodes.
Electrodeless lamps are becoming increasingly popular. 无电极灯越来越受欢迎。
Electrodeless discharge is a key component of this new technology. 无电极放电是这项新技术的关键组成部分。
non-electrode, electrode-free
using electrodes

Etymology:  "electrode" + "-less"
"electrode" comes from "electro-" (from Greek "elektron" meaning "amber," referencing the static electrical properties of amber) + "-ode" (from Greek "hodos" meaning "way" or "path").  "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
