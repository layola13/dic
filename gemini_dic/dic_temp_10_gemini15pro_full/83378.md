gyrostat:/ˈdʒaɪərəstæt/ | n. | 陀螺仪，回转仪 | A device consisting of a rapidly rotating flywheel so mounted that its axis of rotation can turn freely in certain or all directions, and capable of maintaining its orientation regardless of the motion of the body to which it is attached. | The satellite uses a gyrostat to maintain its stability.  卫星使用陀螺仪来保持稳定性。| Synonyms: gyroscope, flywheel | Antonyms: N/A | noun


gyrostat:/ˈdʒaɪərəstæt/ | n. | 陀螺稳定器，陀螺调节器 | A mechanism or device using a gyrostat for stabilization or control. | The ship's gyrostat helps it navigate through rough seas.  这艘船的陀螺稳定器帮助它在波涛汹涌的海面上航行。| Synonyms: stabilizer, regulator | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *gyros* (round, circle) + *statos* (standing, stationary).
