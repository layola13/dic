misconjecture:/ˌmɪskənˈdʒɛktʃər/
n.|错误的推测，猜错|An incorrect or faulty conjecture. | He made a serious misconjucture about the strength of the opposition. 他严重错误地估计了反对派的势力。|近义词:miscalculation, misjudgment, mistake, error|反义词:correct conjecture, accurate guess|词性:noun

misconjecture:/ˌmɪskənˈdʒɛktʃər/
v.|错误地推测，猜错|To conjecture incorrectly; make a wrong guess.|She misconjuctured his motives for leaving. 她错误地揣测了他离开的动机。|近义词:miscalculate, misjudge, err|反义词:conjecture correctly, guess accurately|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + conjecture (to guess). The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, astray."  "Conjecture" comes from the Latin "conjectūra,"  from "conicere" meaning "to throw together," from "com-" (together) + "iacere" (to throw).
