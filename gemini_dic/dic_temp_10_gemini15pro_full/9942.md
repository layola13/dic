aphaeresis:/əˈfɛrɪsɪs/| n.|省略首音，元音省略|The word 'lone' is derived from 'alone' by aphaeresis. 单词 'lone' 是由 'alone' 通过省略首音派生出来的。|Aphaeresis is the loss of one or more sounds from the beginning of a word. 省略首音是指单词开头的一个或多个音的丢失。|Example 1: The word 'neath is an aphaeresis of 'beneath'.  单词 'neath' 是 'beneath' 的省略首音形式。

Example 2:  'Round' for 'around' is an example of aphaeresis. 'Round' 代替 'around' 是省略首音的一个例子。|近义词：apocope, elision|反义词: prosthesis, prothesis|词性:noun
