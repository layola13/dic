footmanship:/ˈfʊtmənʃɪp/| n.|步法；仆役的技能；（尤指）步兵的步法|The butler's footmanship was impeccable, moving silently and efficiently through the grand house.  那位管家的步法无可挑剔，在豪宅中安静而高效地移动着。|例句：His footmanship was so light, he barely made a sound. 他的脚步很轻，几乎没有发出任何声音。|例句：The soldiers' footmanship was essential to their success in the difficult terrain. 士兵们的步法对于他们在复杂地形中的成功至关重要。|近义词：skill, dexterity, proficiency|反义词:clumsiness, awkwardness|词性:名词

词源:
From footman +‎ -ship.  Footman (步兵，男仆) 源于 foot (脚) + man (人). -ship是名词后缀，表示状态，技能或品质。
