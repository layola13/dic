mythologies:/ˌmɪˈθɑːlədʒiz/
n.
神话；神话学；神话集
A collection of myths, especially one belonging to a particular religious or cultural tradition.
Greek mythologies are full of gods and goddesses.  希腊神话充满了神和女神。
The book explores the mythologies of ancient Egypt. 这本书探讨了古埃及的神话。
synonyms:myths, legends, lore, fables, tales, stories
antonyms:facts, realities, truths
noun
