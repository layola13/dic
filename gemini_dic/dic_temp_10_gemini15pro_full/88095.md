hightail:/ˈhaɪteɪl/| v.|迅速离开，逃之夭夭|To leave somewhere quickly, often because you are frightened or embarrassed.|例句：When he saw the police coming, he hightailed it out of there. 当他看到警察来了，他就迅速逃离了那里。|例句：The robbers hightailed it after grabbing the cash. 抢劫犯抢了现金后迅速逃跑了。|近义词：flee, escape, run away, bolt, decamp|反义词：arrive, approach, stay|词性:动词
