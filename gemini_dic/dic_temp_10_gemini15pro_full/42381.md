counterblast:/ˈkaʊntərblæst/ | n. |反击；驳斥；对抗|A forceful reply or attack against criticism or an opposing view.|例句：The politician issued a counterblast to the allegations of corruption.  这位政治家对腐败指控进行了驳斥。

例句：Her book was a counterblast to the prevailing wisdom on child-rearing. 她的书是对流行育儿观念的驳斥。|近义词：rebuttal, refutation, retort, riposte |反义词:confirmation, corroboration, support|词性:名词

Etymology:
From counter- + blast.  "Counter-" indicates opposition or retaliation, while "blast" originally referred to a strong gust of wind or a loud trumpet sound, later evolving to mean any forceful utterance or attack.
