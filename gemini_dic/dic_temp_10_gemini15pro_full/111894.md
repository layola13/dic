logout:/ˈlɔːɡaʊt/ | n. vi.|注销，退出|The act of logging out of a computer system or online service.  The user ends their session and is no longer actively connected.|Example: Please ensure you logout of your bank account after each session. 请确保每次使用后都从您的银行账户注销。|Example: After finishing his work, he performed a logout and shut down his computer. 完成工作后，他注销并关闭了电脑。|近义词：sign out, sign off|反义词：login, log in|词性:名词/动词
