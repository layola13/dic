endosmometer:/ɛnˌdɑːzˈmɑːmɪtər/ /ɛnˌdɒzˈmɒmɪtər/
noun
内渗计
An instrument for measuring osmotic pressure, especially of solutions of colloids.
The endosmometer showed a significant increase in pressure as the concentration of the solution increased.  内渗计显示，随着溶液浓度的增加，压力也显著增加。
The researcher carefully filled the endosmometer with the experimental solution. 研究人员小心地将实验溶液注入内渗计中。
synonyms: osmometer
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider instruments measuring opposing forces like outward pressure)
n.
