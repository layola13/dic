initiating:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 开始的，创始的；开始，发起 |  starting something, setting something in motion.  | The company is initiating a new marketing campaign. 公司正在启动新的营销活动. | Synonyms: commencing, beginning, launching, instigating | Antonyms: concluding, finishing, terminating, ending | Verb/Adjective

initiating:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪŋ/ |  | 开始，发起 | To admit someone into a society, often with a special ceremony. | He was initiated into the fraternity last semester. 他上学期加入了兄弟会。 | Synonyms: induct, introduce, inaugurate | Antonyms: exclude, expel | Verb


Etymology:  From Late Latin *initiatus, past participle of initiare "to begin, introduce," from Latin initium "beginning."
