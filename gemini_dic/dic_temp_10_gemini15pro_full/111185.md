lithographical:/ˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪkəl/| adj.|平版印刷的，石印的|Relating to lithography, a printing method based on the immiscibility of oil and water.|The museum has a collection of lithographical prints.  博物馆收藏了一些石版画。|Synonyms: planographic, offset|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-lithographic")|adjective

lithography:/lɪˈθɒɡrəfi/| n.|平版印刷术，石印术|A printing process that uses a flat stone or metal plate on which the image areas are worked using a greasy substance so that the ink will adhere to them by, while the non-image areas are made ink-repellent.|Lithography revolutionized the printing industry. 平版印刷术彻底改变了印刷业。|Synonyms: planography, offset printing|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
