coordinative:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnətɪv/
adjective
协调的；同等的
Relating to coordination, the harmonious functioning of different parts or systems.  In chemistry, it refers to a type of bonding where both electrons in the shared pair come from the same atom.
The athlete demonstrated coordinative ability in the complex gymnastics routine.  这位运动员在复杂的体操动作中展现了协调能力。
The nurse explained the coordinative care plan for the patient's recovery. 护士解释了病人康复的协调护理计划。
近义词:cooperative, collaborative, harmonious, integrated
反义词:uncoordinated, disjointed, conflicting
词性:adjective

coordination:/koʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/
noun
协调；配合
The act or process of coordinating or of being coordinated. The ability to move different parts of your body together smoothly and efficiently.
The project requires close coordination between the various departments. 这个项目需要各个部门之间的密切协调。
Her hand-eye coordination is exceptional. 她的手眼协调能力非常出色。
近义词:cooperation, collaboration, harmony, integration
反义词:incoordination, disorganization, conflict
词性:noun

coordinator:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtər/
noun
协调员；协调人
A person who coordinates.
The event coordinator ensured everything ran smoothly. 活动协调员确保一切顺利进行。
She works as a volunteer coordinator for the local charity. 她在当地慈善机构担任志愿者协调员。
近义词:organizer, manager, director, facilitator
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
