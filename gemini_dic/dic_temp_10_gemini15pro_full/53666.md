disinclined: /ˌdɪsɪnˈklaɪnd/
词性: adjective
中文翻译: 不情愿的；不愿意的
英文解析: Unwilling or reluctant to do something.
例句: I am disinclined to agree with his proposal. 我不愿意同意他的提议。

例句: She was disinclined to go out in the cold. 她不愿意在寒冷的天气里出门。

近义词: reluctant, unwilling, averse
反义词: inclined, willing, eager
