aspy:/ˈæspi/ | adj. n. | 间谍的；像间谍的；间谍 | Like a spy; relating to or characteristic of a spy; a spy. | He had an aspy manner. 他举止鬼鬼祟祟的。 |  The aspy gentleman in the corner was clearly eavesdropping. 角落里那个鬼鬼祟祟的绅士显然在偷听。 | 近义词:furtive, secretive, clandestine, undercover, spy | 反义词:open, honest, forthright | 词性:adjective, noun

词源: From asp (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  Ultimately from Latin aspis, from Greek aspis "asp, shield". The connection to "spy" likely comes from the asp's reputation as a secretive and venomous snake.
