aphasia: /əˈfeɪʒə/ (US), /əˈfeɪzɪə/ (UK)| n. |失语症|The inability to use language effectively, usually due to damage to the brain.|She developed aphasia after a head injury. 她头部受伤后患上了失语症。|Synonym: dysphasia|Antonym:  Normal speech, fluency|noun
