maisonettes:/ˌmeɪzəˈnɛts/| n. |小复式公寓；小型公寓楼|A maisonette is a small apartment on two levels, typically one of several in a converted house or as part of a planned development.|例句：They live in a charming maisonette in the city center. 他们住在市中心一套迷人的小复式公寓里。

例句：The maisonettes were built in the 1970s. 这些小型公寓楼建于20世纪70年代。|近义词：apartment, flat, duplex|反义词:|n.
