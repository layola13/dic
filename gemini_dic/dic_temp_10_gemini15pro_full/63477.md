entrust:/ɪnˈtrʌst/ | vt. | 委托；托付 | To give someone the responsibility of taking care of something or someone. | I entrusted my lawyer with the sale of my house. 我委托我的律师出售我的房子。 |  She entrusted her children to the care of their grandparents. 她把孩子托付给祖父母照顾。 | 近义词：consign, commit, delegate | 反义词：keep, retain | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old French *en- "in" + truster "to trust," from Latin *tortiare "to twist, torture," from tortus "twisted, crooked." The sense development is from "to oppress" to "to entrust."
