/bɪˈneɪm/ | vt. | 给…命名；指定 | To give a name to; to nominate. |  He benamed his son after his grandfather. 他以祖父的名字为他儿子命名。

She was benamed as the CEO. 她被任命为首席执行官。 | 近义词: name, call, designate, appoint | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From be- + name.  The prefix "be-" is an Old English prefix meaning "thoroughly," "all around," or "to make."  So, "bename" literally means "to thoroughly name" or "to make a name for."
