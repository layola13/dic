imbastardize:/ɪmˈbæstərdaɪz/
verb
使…堕落，败坏；掺杂，使…不纯
to corrupt or debase; to contaminate or make impure
The mass media have imbastardized the language. 大众媒体败坏了语言。
imbastardize the morals  败坏道德
Near synonyms:  corrupt, debase, degrade, pervert, contaminate, adulterate, alloy
Antonyms: purify, refine, elevate, ennoble
