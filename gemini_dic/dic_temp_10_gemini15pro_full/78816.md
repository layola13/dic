gimleted:/ˈɡɪmlɪtɪd/| adj.|钻孔的；锐利的；敏锐的；|Having been pierced with or as if with a gimlet; (of a stare or gaze) piercingly sharp and observant.|He fixed me with a gimleted stare. 他用锐利的目光盯着我。|近义词：piercing, sharp, penetrating, keen, acute|反义词：dull, unobservant, blunt|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From gimlet + -ed.  Gimlet itself comes from Old French *guimplet, diminutive of guimbe, "gimlet," probably of Germanic origin.
