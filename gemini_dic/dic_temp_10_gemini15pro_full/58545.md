dugongs:/ˈduːɡɒŋz/ (UK) /ˈduːɡɑːŋz/ (US)| n. |儒艮|A large, plant-eating marine mammal related to the manatee, found in warm coastal waters of the Indian and western Pacific Oceans.|例句：Dugongs are gentle giants of the sea. 儒艮是海洋中的温柔巨人。

|例句：The dugong population is declining due to habitat loss. 由于栖息地丧失，儒艮的数量正在下降。|近义词：sea cow, manatee|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an animal)|Noun
