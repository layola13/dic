desmobacteria:/ˌdɛzmoʊbækˈtɪəriə/ | n. | 铁细菌; 铁氧化菌 | Filamentous bacteria that derive energy by oxidizing iron compounds, typically found in iron-rich waters. |  Desmobacteria are crucial in the iron cycle of aquatic environments. 铁细菌在水生环境的铁循环中至关重要。| Desmobacteria can contribute to the formation of ochre deposits in streams.  铁细菌会导致溪流中赭石沉积物的形成。 | synonyms: iron bacteria, iron-oxidizing bacteria | antonyms: iron-reducing bacteria | noun
