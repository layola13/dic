borewell:/ˈbɔːrwɛl/ | n. | 井眼；钻井 | A deep hole drilled into the ground to obtain water, oil, or gas. |  We had a borewell dug in our backyard to access groundwater. 我们在后院挖了一口井，以便取用地下水。


The borewell provides water for irrigation. 这口井为灌溉提供水。


近义词：well, borehole | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "surface water source") | 词性: noun
