guns:/ɡʌnz/| n.|枪支，炮，枪|A weapon incorporating a metal tube from which bullets, shells, or other projectiles are propelled by explosive force, typically making a characteristic loud noise.|The police found two guns in the car. 警方在车里发现了两把枪。|近义词：firearms, weaponry, arms|反义词:peace, disarmament|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gunne, probably from the female personal name Gunhilda (from Old Norse Gunnhildr, composed of gunnr "war" and hildr "battle"), used as a nickname for a siege engine.
