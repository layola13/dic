Longmynd: /ˈlɒŋmaɪnd/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋmaɪnd/ (US) | n. |朗迈恩德|A heath and moorland plateau in Shropshire, England, forming the southwestern part of the Shropshire Hills Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.| We hiked across the Long Mynd and enjoyed the stunning views. 我们徒步穿越了朗迈恩德，欣赏了迷人的景色。| 近义词: moor, heath, plateau | 反义词: valley | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Old English *langmyned, meaning "long mountain" or "long ridge."
