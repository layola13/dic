mythology:/mɪˈθɑːlədʒi/ | n. |神话；神话学| A body of myths, especially relating to a particular person, people, religion, or culture. | Greek mythology is full of fascinating stories. 希腊神话充满了引人入胜的故事。|  The mythology surrounding the founding of Rome is complex and layered.  围绕罗马建城的各种神话错综复杂，层层叠叠。 | synonyms: lore, myths, legends, folklore, fables, traditions | antonyms:  reality, history, truth, fact | noun
