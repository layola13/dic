indefinable:/ˌɪndɪˈfaɪnəbl/
adjective
难以定义的；无法解释的；模糊不清的
Not able to be defined or described exactly.
Her beauty is indefinable. 她的美难以言喻。
The painting evokes an indefinable feeling of sadness. 这幅画唤起一种难以名状的悲伤。
synonyms: inexpressible, indescribable, undefinable, nameless
antonyms: definable, describable, explicable, specific
词性: 形容词
