ATP:/ˌeɪ tiː ˈpiː/ /ˌeɪ tiː ˈpiː/ | n. |三磷酸腺苷; Adenosine Triphosphate|  A nucleotide, C10H16N5O13P3, composed of adenine, ribose, and three phosphate groups, that supplies energy for many biochemical cellular processes by undergoing enzymatic hydrolysis especially to ADP.  |ATP provides the energy for muscle contraction. 三磷酸腺苷为肌肉收缩提供能量。| synonym:  energy currency | antonym:  ADP | noun
