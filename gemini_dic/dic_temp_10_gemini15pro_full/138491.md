novice:/ˈnɒvɪs/ (British), /ˈnɑːvɪs/ (American)| n.|新手，初学者|A person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation.|例句：He's a novice at skiing. 他是个滑雪新手。

例句：She's a complete novice in the kitchen. 她在厨房里完全是个新手。

近义词：beginner, learner, trainee, apprentice, neophyte|反义词：expert, professional, veteran, master|词性：noun
