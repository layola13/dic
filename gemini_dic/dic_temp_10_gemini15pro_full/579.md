abounded:/əˈbaʊndɪd/
verb
大量存在；充满；富于
existed in large numbers or amounts
The lake abounded with fish. = 湖里有很多鱼。
Wild animals abound in the forest. = 森林里野生动物很多。
synonyms: teem, overflow, swarm, be plentiful, be rife
antonyms: lack, be scarce
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from, away" + undare "to surge, rise in waves," from unda "wave."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The garden abounds with flowers. = 花园里开满了鲜花。
Life abounds with opportunities. = 生活中充满了机遇。

一般过去时:
The river abounded with salmon in the past. = 过去这条河里有很多鲑鱼。
Rumors abounded after the incident. = 事件发生后谣言四起。

现在完成时:
Complaints have abounded since the new policy was introduced. = 新政策实施以来，投诉不断。
Opportunities have abounded for graduates in recent years. = 近年来，毕业生的机会很多。

过去完成时:
The area had abounded with wildlife before the construction began. =  在施工开始之前，该地区有很多野生动物。
The market had abounded with cheap goods before the inflation. = 通货膨胀之前，市场上有很多便宜的商品。

将来时:
The new park will abound with recreational facilities. = 新公园将拥有丰富的娱乐设施。
The future will abound with challenges and opportunities. = 未来将充满挑战和机遇。


虚拟语气:
If the forest abounded with deer, we would see them more often. = 如果森林里有很多鹿，我们会更经常地看到它们。
I wish the ocean still abounded with whales. = 我希望海洋里仍然有很多鲸鱼。

被动语态: (abounded 不常用于被动语态，但理论上可以)
The country is abounded with natural resources. = 这个国家自然资源丰富。（用法略有不自然）
The area was abounded with ancient ruins. = 该地区有很多古代遗迹。（用法略有不自然）

疑问句:
Does the area abound with wildlife? = 这个地区有很多野生动物吗？
Did the market abound with cheap goods? = 市场上有许多便宜的商品吗？

否定句:
The desert does not abound with vegetation. = 沙漠里没有多少植被。
The report did not abound with details. = 该报告没有提供很多细节。


PHRASE->
(abounded本身不常用于词组，以下列出一些包含abound的常见用法)
abound in/with something =  富于/充满某物
abound with life = 充满生机
stories abound about/of... =  关于…的故事很多
rumors abound that... =  谣言盛传…
let something abound = 让某事物大量存在
