argentamine:/ɑːrˈdʒɛntəmiːn/ | n. | 银胺，一种用于检测醛类的有机化合物 | An organic compound used as a reagent for aldehydes. | Argentamine reacts with aldehydes to form a silver mirror. 银胺与醛类反应形成银镜。|  Argentamine is crucial in certain chemical tests.  银胺在某些化学测试中至关重要。 | 近义词: Tollens' reagent, ammoniacal silver nitrate | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun

argentamine:/ɑːrˈdʒɛntəmiːn/ | n. | 银氨溶液 | A solution of silver nitrate in ammonia, used in histology and as a test for aldehydes. | The argentamine stained the tissue sample a dark brown. 银氨溶液将组织样本染成深棕色。 | The pathologist used argentamine to identify the presence of aldehydes. 病理学家使用银氨溶液来鉴定醛的存在。| 近义词:  ammoniacal silver, diamminesilver(I) | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
