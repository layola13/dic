noncompetitor:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtər/ | n. |非竞争者，非竞争对手|A person or entity that is not involved in competition with another in a particular market or area.|例句：The company partnered with a noncompetitor to expand its reach.  这家公司与一个非竞争对手合作以扩大其影响范围。

例句：They shared information freely with noncompetitors in the industry. 他们在行业内与非竞争者自由地共享信息。|近义词：non-rival, collaborator|反义词：competitor, rival|词性：名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + competitor (from Latin competere, "to strive together")
