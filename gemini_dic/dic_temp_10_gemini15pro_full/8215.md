anti-freudian:/ˌænti-ˈfrɔɪdiən/
adjective
反弗洛伊德的；反对弗洛伊德学说的
Opposed to or rejecting the theories of Sigmund Freud, especially his emphasis on the importance of sexuality in human behavior.
His anti-Freudian stance made him unpopular among his colleagues. 他的反弗洛伊德立场使他在同事中不受欢迎。
anti-psychoanalytic, non-Freudian
Freudian
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + Freudian, referring to Sigmund Freud (1856-1939), Austrian neurologist and founder of psychoanalysis.
