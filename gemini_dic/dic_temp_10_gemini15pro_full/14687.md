awakeable:/əˈweɪkəb(ə)l/
adjective
可唤醒的；可被唤醒的
able to be woken
The baby is easily awakeable.  这个婴儿很容易被唤醒。
The coma patient was miraculously awakeable after a month.  昏迷的病人一个月后奇迹般地苏醒了。
近义词:rousable, wakenable
反义词:unwakeable, unrousable
词性:adjective
