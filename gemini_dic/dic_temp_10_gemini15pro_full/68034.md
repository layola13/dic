factorization:/ˌfæk.tər.əˈzeɪ.ʃən/ | n. |因式分解，因子分解|The process of breaking down a mathematical expression into smaller components (factors) that multiply together to give the original expression. |The factorization of 12 is 2 x 2 x 3. 12的因式分解是2 x 2 x 3。|近义词：decomposition, factoring |反义词: expansion, multiplication |词性:noun
