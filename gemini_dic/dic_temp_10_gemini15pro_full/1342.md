accruing:/əˈkruːɪŋ/| v. | 累积；增加；（利息等）自然增值 |  (of sums of money or benefits) increasing gradually as time passes. | Interest is accruing on my savings account. 我的储蓄账户上的利息在不断累积。| synonyms: accumulating, collecting, gathering, growing, increasing, mounting | antonyms: decreasing, diminishing, dwindling, lessening, reducing | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acreue (“increase”), from acreistre (“to increase”), from Latin accrescere (“to grow larger”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + crescere (“to grow”).

USAGE->
Present Participle:
He is accruing wealth through wise investments. = 他通过明智的投资积累财富。
The company is accruing debt at an alarming rate. = 该公司的债务正以惊人的速度增长。

Present Continuous:
My vacation days are accruing while I work. = 我工作期间假期天数在累积。
The benefits are accruing to the members of the program. = 该计划的成员正在享受这些好处。

Past Continuous:
While he was away, interest was accruing on his loan. = 他不在的时候，他的贷款利息在不断累积。
They were accruing points for every purchase they made. = 他们每次购物都在累积积分。

Present Perfect Continuous:
The snow has been accruing all day. = 雪下了一整天了。
He has been accruing vacation time for months. = 他已经累积了几个月的假期时间。

Past Perfect Continuous:
They had been accruing debt for years before they declared bankruptcy. = 在他们宣布破产之前，他们已经负债多年了。
She had been accruing miles on her credit card for all her business trips. = 她一直在用她的信用卡累积所有商务旅行的里程。

Future Continuous:
The interest will be accruing on your account until you withdraw the money. = 在您提取款项之前，您的帐户将持续累积利息。
The company will be accruing more customers with their new marketing campaign. = 该公司将通过新的营销活动获得更多客户。

PHRASE->
accruing interest =  累积利息
accruing benefits = 累积收益
costs accruing from = 由...产生的成本
accruing liabilities =  累积负债
accruing wealth = 累积财富
