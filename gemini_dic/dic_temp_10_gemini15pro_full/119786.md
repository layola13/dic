migrainoid:/ˈmaɪɡreɪnɔɪd/| adj.|类似偏头痛的|Resembling or characteristic of a migraine; similar to migraine but lacking some of the classic symptoms.|She experienced migrainoid headaches, characterized by intense throbbing pain but without the visual disturbances typical of a true migraine.|近义词：migraine-like, migraineous|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "non-migrainous" or describing the absence of headache symptoms)|词性:形容词
