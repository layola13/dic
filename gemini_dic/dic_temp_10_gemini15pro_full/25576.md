bumblepuppy:/ˈbʌmbəlˌpʌpi/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜|A foolish or naive person, especially a young one.  Often used affectionately.|例句：He's such a bumblepuppy, he lost his keys three times this week. 他真是个傻瓜，这周已经丢了三次钥匙了。

例句：Don't worry, you'll learn the ropes eventually, you little bumblepuppy. 别担心，你最终会学会的，你这个小笨蛋。|近义词：nincompoop, simpleton, goofball|反义词:genius, mastermind|词性:名词
