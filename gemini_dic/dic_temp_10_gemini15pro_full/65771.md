everydays:/ˈevriˌdeɪz/ | adj. (informal, nonstandard) | 每天的；日常的 | Refers to something happening or used every day. This word is considered nonstandard and informal.  It is better to use "everyday" as the adjective. | Example:  She wore her everydays clothes to the market.  她穿着日常的衣服去市场.
