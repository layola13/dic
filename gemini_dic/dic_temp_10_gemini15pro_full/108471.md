lay-line:/ˈleɪ laɪn/
n.
航线，方位线
An imaginary line between a boat and a mark, along which the wind allows the boat to sail directly to the mark.
The yacht crossed the lay-line too early and had to tack. 这艘游艇过早地越过了方位线，不得不转向。

lay-line:/ˈleɪ laɪn/
n.
(比喻)最佳路线，最有效途径
The company is trying to find the lay-line for future growth. 这家公司正试图找到未来增长的最佳途径。

lay-line:/ˈleɪ laɪn/
n.
(神秘学)能量线，地脉
Some people believe that lay-lines are paths of mystical energy. 一些人相信方位线是神秘能量的路径。


Etymology:  "lay" (from Old Norse *leggja "to lay, put, place") + "line" (from Old French ligne, ultimately from Latin linea "linen thread, line")

Synonyms: rhumb line, direct course, optimal path

Antonyms: indirect route, detour
