antivaccinationist:/ˌæntivaksɪˈneɪʃənɪst/
n.
反疫苗者，疫苗接种反对者
A person who opposes vaccination.
The antivaccinationist spread misinformation about the safety of vaccines.  反疫苗者散布关于疫苗安全性的错误信息。
The politician, known for his antivaccinationist views, faced criticism from the medical community.  这位以反疫苗观点闻名的政治家面临着医学界的批评。
近义词:anti-vaxxer
反义词:pro-vaccinationist, vaccine advocate
词性:noun
