friezelike:/ˈfriːzlaɪk/| adj.| 像饰带的，像雕饰的|Resembling a frieze, especially in its continuous, horizontal, and often decorative nature.|The wallpaper had a friezelike pattern of stylized leaves and flowers.  壁纸上有像饰带一样的叶子和花朵图案。|The top edge of the wall was painted with a friezelike decoration. 墙壁上缘绘有类似饰带的装饰。|近义词：decorative, ornamental, embellished|反义词：plain, simple|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From *frieze* + *-like*.
*Frieze* comes from Old French *frise*, a coarse woolen cloth, ultimately of Germanic origin.  *-like* is a native English suffix meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," or "similar to."
