mib:/mɪb/ | n. |  (俚语)百万字节 (megabyte) | A mib is a unit of information equal to one million bytes.  It's short for mebibyte, which is technically 2^20 bytes (1,048,576 bytes). However, "mib" is often used informally to mean a million bytes. | My hard drive has 500 mibs of space. 我的硬盘有500兆字节的空间。 | 近义词: MB, mebibyte | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 Kib - kilobyte) | 词性: 名词
