ETYMOLOGY->
From 'headmistress' + '-ship'.  'Headmistress' is derived from 'head' + 'mistress'.  'Head' meaning the leader or chief, and 'mistress' historically a female teacher or person in charge.  '-ship' is a suffix denoting a state, office, or skill.
