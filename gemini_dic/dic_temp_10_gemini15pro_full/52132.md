diapause:/ˈdaɪəpɔːz/| n. |滞育，休眠|A period of suspended development in an insect, other invertebrate, or mammal embryo, especially during unfavorable environmental conditions.|The insect entered diapause during the winter months.  这种昆虫在冬季进入了滞育期。|Near synonyms: dormancy, quiescence, hibernation, aestivation|Antonyms: activity, growth, development|Noun
