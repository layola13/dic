ozonation:/ˌoʊzəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 臭氧化，臭氧处理 | The process of treating something with ozone, typically for the purposes of disinfection or purification. |  Ozone is a powerful oxidant and ozonation is effective in removing bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms. 臭氧是一种强氧化剂，臭氧化可有效去除细菌、病毒和其他微生物。| Synonyms: ozone treatment, ozone purification | Antonyms: N/A | noun

ozonate: /ˈoʊzəneɪt/ | v. | 用臭氧处理 | To treat or impregnate with ozone. |  The water supply is ozonated to kill bacteria.  供水经过臭氧处理以杀死细菌。 | Synonyms: treat with ozone, purify with ozone | Antonyms: N/A | verb
