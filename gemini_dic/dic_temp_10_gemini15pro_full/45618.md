cursed:/ˈkɜːrsɪd/ (US), /ˈkɜːsɪd/ (UK)| adj.|被诅咒的；可恨的；讨厌的|Affected by a curse; damnable; extremely annoying.|The cursed ring brought bad luck to its owner. 这枚被诅咒的戒指给它的主人带来了厄运。|Synonyms: damned, doomed, accursed, blighted, hexed|Antonyms: blessed, fortunate, lucky|adjective


cursed:/ˈkɜːst/ (US), /ˈkɜːst/ (UK)| v. (past tense and past participle)|诅咒；咒骂|To invoke evil or misfortune on; to swear at.|He cursed his bad luck. 他咒骂自己的坏运气。|Synonyms: damn, execrate, anathematize|Antonyms: bless, praise|verb
