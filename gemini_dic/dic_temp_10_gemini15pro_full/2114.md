adaptable:/əˈdæptəbl/ | adj. | 适应性强的，能适应的 | Able to adjust to new conditions. | She's a very adaptable person and will quickly learn the new procedures. 她是一个适应能力很强的人，很快就能学会新的流程。

He is adaptable to different learning styles. 他能适应不同的学习方式。 | 近义词：flexible, versatile, adjustable | 反义词：inflexible, rigid, unadaptable | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt + -able. Adapt comes from the Latin word "adaptare," meaning "to fit."

USAGE->
She is adaptable to any situation. = 她能适应任何情况。
This software is adaptable to various operating systems. = 这个软件可以适应各种操作系统。


PHRASE->
highly adaptable = 适应性很强
adaptable to change = 适应变化
adaptable to different environments = 适应不同的环境
adaptable skills = 适应性技能
adaptable nature = 适应性强的性格
