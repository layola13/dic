animatronic:/ˌænɪməˈtrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 电子动画的 | Operated by electronic devices; robotic. | The animatronic dinosaurs in the museum fascinated the children.  博物馆里的电子恐龙让孩子们着迷。| robotic, automated | - | adjective
