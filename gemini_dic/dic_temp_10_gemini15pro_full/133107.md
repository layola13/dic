nonerroneously:/ˌnɑːnɪˈroʊniəsli/ | adv. | 无误地，正确地 |  In a manner that is free from error; correctly.  | The problem was solved nonerroneously.  这个问题被正确地解决了。| The data was entered nonerroneously into the system. 数据被无误地输入到系统中。 | accurately, correctly, flawlessly, perfectly | erroneously, incorrectly, faultily | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + erroneously.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "erroneously" is the adverbial form of "erroneous," meaning "containing error; mistaken."  "Erroneous" ultimately comes from the Latin word "errare," meaning "to wander" or "to err."
