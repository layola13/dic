anthropogeographical:/ˌænθrəpəʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
人类地理学的
Relating to the study of the relationship between human beings and their geographical environment.
The anthropogeographical factors influenced the settlement patterns of the early civilizations.  早期文明的定居模式受到人类地理因素的影响。

anthropogeographical:/ˌænθrəpəʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
人文地理学的
Concerned with the geographical distribution of humankind and the relationship between human activities and the environment.
Her dissertation explored the anthropogeographical implications of climate change on coastal communities. 她的论文探讨了气候变化对沿海社区的人文地理影响。

近义词: human geographical, geo-human
反义词:  (无明确的反义词)
词性: adjective


词源:
anthropo- (from Greek 'anthrōpos' meaning 'human being') + geographical (from Greek 'geographia' meaning 'description of the earth')
