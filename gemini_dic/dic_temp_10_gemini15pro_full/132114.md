noncrop:/ˈnɒnkrɒp/ (US) /ˈnɒnkrɒp/ (UK)| n.|非耕地；非作物|Land that is not used for growing crops.  It can include forests, pastures, urban areas, etc.|例句：The noncrop area was used for grazing. 这片非耕地被用来放牧。|例句：The study compared biodiversity in crop and noncrop habitats.  这项研究比较了作物和非作物栖息地的生物多样性。|近义词：fallow land, wasteland, uncultivated land|反义词: cropland, farmland|词性:名词
