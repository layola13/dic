outwrestling:/ˌaʊtˈrɛslɪŋ/
verb
以摔跤击败，在摔跤中胜过
defeating someone in wrestling; surpassing in wrestling skill.
He outwrestled his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮摔跤比赛中击败了对手。
近义词: outmaneuver, overpower, defeat, conquer
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
