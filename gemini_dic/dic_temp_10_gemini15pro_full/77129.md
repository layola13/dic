gasbag:/ˈɡæsˌbæɡ/| n.|夸夸其谈的人；空话连篇的人；<非正式> 健谈的人|A person who talks at length but says little of value; a loquacious person.  A gasbag filled with hot air.  |He's such a gasbag, always boasting about his achievements.|近义词：windbag, blowhard, braggart|反义词：reticent person, quiet person|词性:名词

Etymology:
gas + bag.  "Gas" referring to empty talk, and "bag" referring to a container.  The image is of someone filled with empty air/talk.
