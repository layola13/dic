nonindulgent:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌldʒənt/
adjective
不放纵的，不纵容的，克制的
Not indulging oneself or others; strict, austere, not allowing oneself or others to have or do too many pleasant things that may not be good for them.
She adopted a nonindulgent approach to parenting, believing that children thrived on structure and discipline. 她采取了一种不放纵的育儿方式，认为孩子们在有条理和纪律的环境中茁壮成长。
The monks lived a nonindulgent life, devoid of luxury and excess.  僧侣们过着不放纵的生活，没有奢侈和过度。
近义词: austere, strict, spartan, abstemious, disciplined
反义词: indulgent, permissive, lenient, extravagant, luxurious
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non "not".
indulgent (adjective): from Latin indulgentem (nominative indulgens) "kind, tender, yielding," present participle of indulgere "be kind or tender to, yield to, give oneself up to," from in- "in" + dulcis "sweet."
