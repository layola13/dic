chrysotype:/ˈkrɪsəˌtaɪp/ | n. |金印照片，金盐印相|A photographic printing process using a gold salt, resulting in a purplish-brown image. |Chrysotype is a beautiful and archival printing process. 金盐印相是一种漂亮且可永久保存的印相工艺。| The museum displayed several chrysotypes from the 19th century.  博物馆展出了几幅19世纪的金盐印相。 |近义词: gold print, kallitype |反义词:  None (no direct opposite for a specific printing process)|词性:noun
