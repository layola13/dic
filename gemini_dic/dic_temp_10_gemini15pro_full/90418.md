hoyy: /hɔɪ/ | interjection | 嗨，你好 | An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey".  Used to get someone's attention or to express a friendly greeting. | Hoyy, what's up? 嗨，怎么了？ | 近义词：hi, hey, hello | 反义词: goodbye, farewell | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Likely a variant of "hoy," which is itself a shortening of "ahoy," a nautical term used to call out to other ships.  Over time, it became a more general greeting.
