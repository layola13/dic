nondeformed:/ˌnɒndiˈfɔːmd/ /ˌnɑːndiˈfɔːrmd/| adj.|未变形的，未畸形的|Not having been deformed; retaining its original shape.|The nondeformed metal sheet was ready for processing. 未变形的金属板已准备好进行加工。|The bone structure remained nondeformed after the impact. 撞击后骨骼结构保持未变形。|近义词：undistorted, intact, undamaged, perfect|反义词：deformed, distorted, misshapen|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + deformed (from Latin deformare, "to disfigure")
