beatifically:/ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪkli/
adverb
幸福地；快乐地；狂喜地
in a manner suggesting bliss
She smiled beatifically as she held her newborn baby. 她抱着新生儿，幸福地笑了。
The sun shone beatifically on the peaceful landscape.  阳光幸福地照耀着宁静的景色。
近义词:blissfully, rapturously, serenely, ecstatically
反义词:miserably, wretchedly, unhappily
词性:adverb

词源:
From beatific + -ally.  Beatific comes from Late Latin *beatificus, from Latin beatus (“blessed, happy”) + -ficus (“-making, -causing”).  Ultimately from Latin beare ("to make happy").
