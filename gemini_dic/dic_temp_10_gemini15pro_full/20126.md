bipartisanisms:/ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzənɪzəmz/
n.
两党合作；跨党派合作
instances of bipartisan cooperation or agreement between two major political parties.
The recent bipartisanisms in Congress have led to the passing of several important bills.  最近国会中的两党合作促成了几项重要法案的通过。
The bipartisanisms on this issue are encouraging. 在这个问题上的两党合作令人鼓舞。
近义词:cooperation, collaboration, agreement, unity
反义词:partisanship, division, discord, gridlock
词性:名词

bipartisan:/ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzən/
adj.
两党的；跨党派的
involving or supported by two political parties, especially the two major parties.
The bill received bipartisan support in the Senate.  该法案在参议院获得了 bipartisan 支持。
A bipartisan committee was formed to investigate the matter.  成立了一个两党委员会来调查此事。
近义词:nonpartisan, cross-party
反义词:partisan
词性:形容词

词源:  bi- (two) + partisan (a strong supporter of a party, cause, or person).  "Bipartisan" emerged in the mid-19th century, and "bipartisanisms" is a later derivation, referring to specific instances of such cooperation.
