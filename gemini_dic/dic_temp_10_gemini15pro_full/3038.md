aerobiological:/ˌɛəroʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
空气生物学的
Relating to the study of airborne organisms, such as pollen, spores, and bacteria, and their effects on living things.
例句：
Aerobiological studies are important for understanding the spread of plant diseases.  空气生物学研究对于理解植物疾病的传播非常重要。

The aerobiological sampling revealed high concentrations of fungal spores. 空气生物学采样显示真菌孢子浓度很高。

近义词:
airborne
atmospheric

反义词:
None

词性:
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + biological (relating to biology).  "Aero-" comes from the Greek "aer" (air). "Biological" comes from "bio" (life) + "-logy" (study of).
