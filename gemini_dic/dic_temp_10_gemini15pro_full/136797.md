nonseverances:/ˌnɒnˈsɛvərənsɪz/
n.
不分离；非分割；不解雇；(尤指)留任
The act or state of not being severed or separated, especially the continued employment of a person.
The contract guaranteed nonseverance for a period of five years.  该合同保证五年内不解雇。
Near synonyms: continuation, retention,  intactness, preservation
Antonyms: severance, separation, dismissal, termination
noun
