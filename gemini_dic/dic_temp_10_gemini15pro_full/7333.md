angwantibo: /ˌæŋɡwənˈtiːboʊ/ | n. | 婴猴, 金熊猴 | A small, nocturnal primate with large eyes, found in the rainforests of West Africa. It is also known as a golden potto or a potto. |  The angwantibo is a shy, arboreal creature. 婴猴是一种害羞的树栖生物。|  The angwantibo's diet consists mainly of insects and fruit. 婴猴主要以昆虫和水果为食。| 近义词: potto, golden potto | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From the Twi language of Ghana.
