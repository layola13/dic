myohaematin:/ˌmaɪəˈhiːmətɪn/ | n. | 肌红蛋白 | A red protein containing haem, which carries and stores oxygen in muscle cells. | Myohaematin plays a vital role in supplying oxygen to muscles during strenuous exercise. 肌红蛋白在剧烈运动时为肌肉供氧起着至关重要的作用。 | Synonyms: myoglobin | Antonyms: None | Noun
