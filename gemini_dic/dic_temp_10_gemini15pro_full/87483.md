heterarchy:/ˈhɛtərɑːrki/ | n. | 异质统治，异层级，非等级结构 | A system of organization where elements are unranked or where they possess the potential to be ranked in a number of different ways.  | Example 1: The company embraced heterarchy, allowing teams to self-organize and adapt to changing project needs.  公司采用了异质统治结构，允许团队自主组织并适应不断变化的项目需求。

Example 2:  The anthropologist studied the heterarchy within the tribe, noting how leadership shifted depending on the situation.  这位人类学家研究了部落内部的异层级结构，注意到领导地位是如何根据情况而变化的。| Synonym: non-hierarchy, network, polyarchy | Antonym: hierarchy, pecking order | noun
