flecker:/ˈflekər/ | v. | 斑点，使…有斑点，点缀 | To cover with flecks; to spot. | The artist fleckered the canvas with paint. 艺术家在画布上点缀颜料。 |  The baker fleckered the cake with chocolate shavings. 面包师用巧克力屑点缀蛋糕。 | 近义词：speckle, mottle, dapple | 反义词：clear, plain | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Low German *vlecken, from Proto-Germanic *flekkaz (“spot, stain”). Cognate with German Fleck (“spot, stain”), Dutch vlek (“spot, stain”), Swedish fläck (“spot, stain”).
