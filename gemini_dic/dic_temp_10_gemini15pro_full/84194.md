half-rebelliousness:/ˌhæf rɪˈbɛljəsnəs/
|n.|半叛逆，略带叛逆|A state of being partially or mildly rebellious; a tendency to resist authority or convention in a limited or moderate way.|She displayed a half-rebelliousness by wearing a slightly unconventional dress to the formal event. 她穿着略微非传统的礼服参加正式活动，表现出一种半叛逆的态度。|Example: His half-rebelliousness manifested in his choice of music and clothing. 他的半叛逆性体现在他对音乐和服装的选择上。|Synonyms: mild rebelliousness, partial disobedience, slight insubordination|Antonyms: obedience, conformity, submission|Noun

half:/hæf/
|adj. n. adv.|一半的，半个；一半地|One of two equal parts of a whole.|I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半的披萨。|Synonyms: one-half, moiety|Antonyms: whole, double|Adjective/Noun/Adverb

rebelliousness:/rɪˈbɛljəsnəs/
|n.|叛逆，反叛|The quality or state of being rebellious; defiance of authority.|Teenage rebelliousness is often expressed through clothing and music choices. 青少年的叛逆通常通过服装和音乐的选择来表达。|Synonyms: defiance, disobedience, insubordination|Antonyms: obedience, conformity, submission|Noun
