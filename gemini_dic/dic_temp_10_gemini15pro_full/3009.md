aeration:/eəˈreɪʃən/
noun
通风；透气；充气
The process of introducing air into a substance, such as soil or water.
Good soil aeration is essential for healthy plant growth.  良好的土壤通气对植物健康生长至关重要。

The aeration of the water improved its quality.  水的充气改善了水质。

近义词:ventilation, oxygenation
反义词:suffocation, asphyxiation
词性:n.
