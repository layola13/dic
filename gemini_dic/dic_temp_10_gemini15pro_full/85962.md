headquarters:/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtərz/ (US) /ˈhedˈkwɔːtəz/ (UK)| n. |总部；司令部|The company's headquarters is located in New York City. 该公司的总部位于纽约市。|The army moved its headquarters closer to the front.  军队将总部移至更靠近前线的地方。|近义词：head office, main office, center of operations|反义词:branch, satellite office|noun


headquarter:/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtər/ (US) /ˈhedˈkwɔːtə/ (UK)| v. |设立总部；以…为总部|The company is headquartered in London. 该公司总部设在伦敦。|The international organization is headquartered in Geneva. 这个国际组织的总部设在日内瓦。|近义词：base, locate, station|反义词:N/A|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the French phrase "quartier général," meaning "general quarters."  "Quartier" referred to a section or quarter of a space, particularly in a military context.  "Général" refers to the general officer or overall command.  Over time, the term evolved into the single word "headquarters" in English.
