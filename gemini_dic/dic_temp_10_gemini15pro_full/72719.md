foddered:/ˈfɒdəd/ /ˈfɑːdərd/| v. |喂养，供给饲料|To feed (livestock) with fodder.|The farmer foddered the cows with hay. 农民用干草喂牛。|近义词：feed, nourish, provision|反义词:starve, deprive|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fodren*, from Old English *foðrian* (“to feed, nourish, support”), from Proto-Germanic *fōdrijaną* (“to feed”), from *fōdrą* (“fodder”). Cognate with Dutch *voederen* (“to feed”), German *füttern* (“to feed”), Icelandic *fóðra* (“to feed, fodder”).
