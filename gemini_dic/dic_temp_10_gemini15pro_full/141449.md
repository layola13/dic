opifice: /ˈɒpɪfɪs/ (UK) /ˈɑːpɪfɪs/ (US)| n. |工作室，工场；营业场所|A place of work, especially one devoted to a particular occupation or industry.  It often implies a small-scale or specialized workplace.|例句：The artist worked tirelessly in his opifice, surrounded by canvases and paints. 艺术家在他工作室里不知疲倦地工作，周围都是画布和颜料。

例句：The watchmaker's opifice was filled with tiny tools and intricate mechanisms. 钟表匠的工作室里摆满了微小的工具和复杂的机械装置。

近义词：workshop, studio, office, workplace|反义词: home, residence |词性: noun
