cutesiest:/ˈkjuːtiɪst/| adj.|最可爱的|Superlative form of cute: most cute.  It means extremely adorable, charming, or endearing.|That is the cutesiest puppy I've ever seen! 那是我见过的最可爱的小狗！|The baby's cutesiest outfit was the one with the bunny ears. 宝宝最可爱的衣服是那件带兔子耳朵的。|近义词：adorable, charming, dearest, sweetest|反义词：ugliest, homeliest|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From cute + -est (superlative suffix).  "Cute" itself is likely a shortened form of "acute," originally meaning sharp or clever, but evolving to mean attractive in a delicate way.
