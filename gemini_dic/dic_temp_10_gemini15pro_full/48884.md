deframe:/dɪˈfreɪm/ | vt. | 使蒙羞；使丢脸 | To bring shame or disgrace upon. |  His reputation was deframed by false accusations.  他的名誉因不实指控而受损。 | synonyms: disgrace, dishonor, discredit | antonyms: honor, respect, esteem | verb
