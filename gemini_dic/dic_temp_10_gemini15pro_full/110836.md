linse:/ˈlɪnzə/ (US), /ˈlɪnzə/ (UK)| n. |透镜，晶状体，扁豆|A piece of glass or other transparent substance with curved sides for concentrating or dispersing light rays, or something resembling such a lens, such as the crystalline lens of the eye, or a lentil (a type of legume). |例句：The camera's linse was scratched. 相机的镜头被刮花了。
