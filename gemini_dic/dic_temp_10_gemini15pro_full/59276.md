eagernesses:/ˈiːɡərnəsɪz/
n.
热切，渴望
The state of being eager; keenness.
She displayed great eagerness to learn. 她表现出强烈的学习渴望。
近义词：enthusiasm, keenness, avidity
反义词：apathy, indifference, reluctance
词性：名词
