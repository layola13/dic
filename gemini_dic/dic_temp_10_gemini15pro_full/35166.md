clowning:/ˈklaʊnɪŋ/| n. |小丑表演；扮小丑；胡闹|The act of behaving like a clown, often to amuse others.  It can also refer to foolish or frivolous behavior.|He entertained the children with his clowning. 他扮小丑逗孩子们开心。|Synonyms: buffoonery, antics, tomfoolery|Antonyms: seriousness, solemnity|noun

clowning:/ˈklaʊnɪŋ/| v. |扮小丑；胡闹|Behaving in a foolish or playful way, like a clown.|Stop clowning around and get to work! 别再胡闹了，快去工作！|Synonyms: fooling around, messing about, playing the fool|Antonyms: behaving, working seriously|verb
