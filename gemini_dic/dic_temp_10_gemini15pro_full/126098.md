mutant:/ˈmjuːtənt/ (US), /ˈmjuːtnt/ (UK)| n. adj.|突变体，变异体；变异的|An organism, gene, or chromosome that has undergone mutation.|例句：The scientists studied the effects of radiation on mutants. 科学家们研究了辐射对突变体的影响。

例句：The mutant strain of the virus was highly contagious. 这种病毒的突变株具有高度传染性。|近义词：variation, variant, sport|反义词：wild type, normal|词性：名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mūtāns*, present participle of *mūtāre* "to change," from PIE root *mei- (1) "to change, alter."
