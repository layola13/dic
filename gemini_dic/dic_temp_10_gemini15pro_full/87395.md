herrerasaurus:/ˌhɛrərəˈsɔːrəs/
n.
埃雷拉龙
A genus of early theropod dinosaur from the Late Triassic period.  It is one of the oldest known dinosaurs.
Herrerasaurus ischiualastes was one of the top predators of its time. 埃雷拉龙是当时顶级掠食者之一。
The discovery of Herrerasaurus helped paleontologists understand the early evolution of dinosaurs.  埃雷拉龙的发现帮助古生物学家了解恐龙的早期进化。
