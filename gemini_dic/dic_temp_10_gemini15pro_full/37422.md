commers:/ˈkʌmərz/
noun
新来者， 商人
people who come to a place, especially to buy or sell goods; traders.
In the marketplace, the commers bartered for goods. 在市场上，商人互相交换货物。
newcomers, traders, merchants, visitors
natives, residents
n.
