nullable: /ˈnʌləbl/ | adj. | 可空的，可为null的 |  Able to have a value of null.  In computer programming, a nullable type can hold a normal value of its underlying type or a special value representing "null" (meaning no value). |  This field is nullable, so you don't have to enter a value.  这个字段是可空的，所以你不需要输入值。|  optional, non-essential | required, mandatory | adjective
