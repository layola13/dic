elate: /ɪˈleɪt/ | v. | 使高兴，使兴高采烈 | To make someone feel very happy and excited. | The news of their victory elated the entire team.  他们的胜利的消息让整个团队欣喜若狂。 |  近义词: exhilarate, thrill, delight | 反义词: depress, sadden, deject | 词性: 动词

elate: /ɪˈleɪt/ | adj. (obsolete, rare) |  兴高采烈的，得意洋洋的 | Filled with happiness and excitement; elated. |  He felt elated after winning the race. 他在赢得比赛后感到兴高采烈。 | 近义词:  exhilarated, thrilled, delighted | 反义词: depressed, saddened, dejected | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin ēlātus (“lifted up, raised”), past participle of efferō (“I carry out, bring forth”).
