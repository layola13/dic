indical:/ɪnˈdɪk(ə)l/ | adj.|指示的，索引的|Relating to or having the nature of an index; serving to point out. | This word is indical of his personality.  这个词指示了他的个性。| synonymous:  indexical, demonstrative | antonymous:  non-indexical | adjective

indical:/ɪnˈdɪk(ə)l/ | n.|指示词|A word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context of its utterance, such as 'I', 'here', 'now'. |"Here" is an indical expression. “这里”是一个指示词。| synonymous:  deictic, indexical | antonymous:  non-indexical, non-deictic | noun
