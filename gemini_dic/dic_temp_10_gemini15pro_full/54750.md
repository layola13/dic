dispassion:/dɪsˈpæʃən/
n.|冷静；客观；不动感情|Absence of passion; coolness; composure.|He spoke with dispassion about the accident, even though he had been injured. 他冷静地谈论了那场事故，尽管他自己也受了伤。|
近义词:calmness, composure, objectivity, detachment, coolness, serenity, impassivity, indifference
反义词:passion, fervor, zeal, ardor, enthusiasm, excitement
词性:名词

dispassionate:/dɪsˈpæʃənət/
adj.|冷静的；客观的；不动感情的|Not influenced by strong emotion, and therefore able to be rational and impartial.|A dispassionate judge is essential for a fair trial.  公正的审判需要一位冷静的法官。|
近义词:calm, composed, objective, detached, cool, serene, impassive, indifferent, unbiased, impartial
反义词:passionate, fervent, zealous, ardent, enthusiastic, excited, biased, partial
词性:形容词
