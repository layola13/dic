hawthorny:/ˈhɔːθɔːrni/ | adj. | 山楂的；多刺的；棘手的；艰难的 |  Resembling or characteristic of a hawthorn; full of thorns; difficult; problematic. |  The path was hawthorny and difficult to traverse.  这条小路长满了荆棘，很难通行。| 近义词: thorny, prickly, spiny, difficult, tricky | 反义词: smooth, easy, simple | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Old English *hagþornig, from hagþorn "hawthorn."  The "haw" element likely refers to the fruit of the hawthorn, while "thorn" refers to the thorny branches.
