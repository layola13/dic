displacing:/dɪsˈpleɪsɪŋ/
verb
取代，移置，置换
the act of moving something from its place or position.
The construction project is displacing many families from their homes.  这个建设项目使许多家庭流离失所。
dislodge, remove, replace, supplant
retain, keep, hold
