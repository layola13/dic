outfrothed:/ˌaʊtˈfrɒθd/ |adj.|  充满泡沫的; 激动地表达的 | His outfrothed pronouncements betrayed his agitation. 他激动地表达暴露了他的不安。 | 近义词：frothy, effervescent, agitated | 反义词: calm, composed |adjective
