depressurize:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪz/ /diːˈprɛʃərˌaɪz/
verb
减压；卸压
To release pressure from something that has been pressurized, such as an aircraft cabin or a sealed container.  To reduce the pressure or stress in a difficult or tense situation.
The pilot depressurized the cabin in preparation for landing. 飞行员为着陆做准备而降低了机舱压力。

The government is trying to depressurize the tense political situation. 政府正在努力缓和紧张的政治局势。

近义词: decompress, vent, release
反义词: pressurize, compress
词性: verb
