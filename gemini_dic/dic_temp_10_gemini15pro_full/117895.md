melons:/ˈmelənz/| n.|甜瓜（复数）|The large, round fruit of a plant of the gourd family, with sweet, juicy flesh and typically a green rind.|
She bought two ripe melons at the market. 她从市场上买了两个熟透的甜瓜。

The melons were sweet and juicy. 这些甜瓜甜美多汁。

近义词:cantaloupes, watermelons, honeydews|反义词:None|词性:Noun

词源：From Old French *melon (unattested), from Latin mēlō, melōnis (“melon”), from Ancient Greek μῆλον (mēlon, “apple, any tree-fruit; quince”).  The sense of “melon” is probably derived from the quince apple, which has a melon-like aroma.
