disciplinable:/ˈdɪsəplɪnəbl/
adjective
可训练的，可管教的
Capable of being disciplined; amenable to discipline.
That child is very disciplinable; he always follows instructions.  那个孩子很听话，他总是遵守指示。
The team was highly disciplinable and responded well to the coach's training.  这个团队纪律性很强，对教练的训练反应良好。
近义词:trainable, teachable, manageable, controllable
反义词:unruly, disobedient, incorrigible, intractable
词性:adjective
