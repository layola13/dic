blastocarpous: /blæstəˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
胚芽生殖的，种子在果实未成熟时就萌发的
Germinating while still attached to the parent plant; (of seeds) germinating prematurely while still enclosed in the fruit.
The mangrove exhibits a blastocarpous mode of reproduction, with its seeds germinating while still on the tree.  红树林表现出一种胚芽生殖的繁殖方式，其种子还在树上的时候就开始萌发。
The blastocarpous seeds of the plant began to sprout while still inside the fruit.  这种植物的胚芽生殖种子在果实内就开始发芽了。
近义词: viviparous
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) dormant, non-viviparous

词性: adjective
