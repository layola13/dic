choralism:/ˈkɔːrəlɪzəm/ | n. |合唱主义；合唱风格；合唱精神|The spirit of working together towards a common goal, often likened to the harmony in a choir.  A style or principle of choral singing or composition. | The choralism in this piece is reminiscent of Bach.  这首曲子的合唱风格让人想起巴赫。|  The project’s success was due to the choralism of the team.  这个项目的成功归功于团队的合作精神。 |  collaboration, teamwork, unison, harmony |  individualism, discord, disharmony | noun
