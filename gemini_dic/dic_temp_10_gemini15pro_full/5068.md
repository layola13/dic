all-the-while:/ˌɔːl ðə ˈwaɪl/| adv.|一直；始终；与此同时|Continuously during the entire time that something else is happening.|He was complaining about the food all-the-while he continued to eat.  他一边抱怨食物不好吃，一边还在不停地吃。|Synonyms: meanwhile, at the same time, throughout, all along|Antonyms: never, intermittently|adverb

Etymology: From "all the while," a phrase combining "all" (meaning the entirety of) and "while" (a period of time).
