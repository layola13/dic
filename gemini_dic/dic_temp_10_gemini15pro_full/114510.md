maladministration:/ˌmælədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃn/
|n.|管理不善，滥用职权|The report revealed widespread maladministration within the government. 报告揭露了政府内部普遍存在的管理不善。|English Explanation:  Maladministration refers to dishonest or incompetent management of affairs, especially public affairs. It implies mismanagement, inefficiency, or corruption in the handling of responsibilities.|例句：The maladministration of the fund led to significant financial losses.  基金管理不善导致了巨大的经济损失。

|例句：Several officials were accused of maladministration and corruption. 几名官员被指控管理不善和腐败。|近义词：mismanagement, misgovernment, incompetence, corruption|反义词：good governance, efficient administration|词性：名词

maladministrations:/ˌmælədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃnz/
|n.|管理不善（复数）|The committee investigated several maladministrations within the organization. 委员会调查了该组织内部的几起管理不善事件。|English Explanation: The plural form of maladministration, referring to multiple instances of dishonest or incompetent management.|例句：Numerous maladministrations plagued the company, leading to its eventual downfall.  无数的管理不善困扰着这家公司，最终导致了它的倒闭。

|例句：The report detailed a series of maladministrations that had occurred over several years.  该报告详细列举了多年来发生的一系列管理不善事件。|近义词：mismanagements, misgovernments|反义词：good governance practices|词性：名词
