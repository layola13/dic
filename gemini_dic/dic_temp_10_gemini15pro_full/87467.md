hessian:/ˈhɛsiən/| n. adj.|粗麻布，粗麻布制的|A coarse, strong fabric made from jute or hemp, used for sacks, etc.  A person from Hesse in Germany.|The shopkeeper wrapped the vase in hessian.|近义词：burlap, sackcloth|反义词:silk, satin|词性:noun, adjective

hessian:/ˈhɛsiən/| adj.|黑森佣兵|Relating to a German mercenary soldier hired by the British to fight in the American Revolution.|Hessian troops were notorious for their brutality.|近义词：mercenary|反义词:patriot|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hesse, a region in Germany.  Originally referred to soldiers from Hesse, later to the coarse cloth that they often wore.
