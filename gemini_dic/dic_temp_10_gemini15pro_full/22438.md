bookest:/ˈbʊkɪst/ | adj. (non-standard, humorous) | 最爱读书的，最书呆子气的 | This is a humorous, non-standard superlative form of "bookish," meaning very studious or devoted to reading. It's unlikely to be found in formal writing. |  Example: He's the bookest guy I know, always with his nose in a book. 他是我认识的最爱读书的人，总是埋头苦读。 Example: She's so bookest, she even reads dictionaries for fun. 她太书呆子气了，甚至读字典都觉得有趣。 | 近义词: bookish, studious, well-read | 反义词: unintellectual, unscholarly | 词性: 形容词
