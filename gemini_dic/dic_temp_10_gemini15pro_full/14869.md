ayahuasca:/ˌaɪəˈwɑːskə/ | n. | 南美卡皮木茶；死藤水 |Ayahuasca is a psychoactive brew made from the Banisteriopsis caapi vine and other plants, traditionally used for religious and spiritual purposes in indigenous communities of the Amazon basin. | He participated in an ayahuasca ceremony in Peru. 他在秘鲁参加了一个死藤水仪式。| 近义词: yagé (another name for ayahuasca),  entheogen (general term for psychoactive substances used in religious contexts) | 反义词: None (no direct antonym for a specific plant-based brew) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Quechua *aya- "spirit, ancestor" + *waska "rope, vine, liana".
