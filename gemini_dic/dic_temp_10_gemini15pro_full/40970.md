cooping:/ˈkuːpɪŋ/ | n. | 合作; 监禁; 鸡舍 |The act of cooperating or working together; confinement or imprisonment, especially of chickens in a coop. |  The chickens are cooping up in the barn during the storm.  鸡群在暴风雨期间都被关在谷仓里。 | 近义词: collaborating, confining, imprisoning | 反义词: liberating, freeing | 词性: noun (derived from the verb "coop")

cooping:/ˈkuːpɪŋ/ | v. | 将...关进鸡笼; 限制; 监禁 |  To confine in a small space, like a coop; to restrict or imprison. | He's been cooped up in his apartment all week because of the flu. 他因为流感整个星期都被关在他的公寓里。 | 近义词: confining, imprisoning, restricting | 反义词: liberating, freeing | 词性: verb (present participle of "coop")

词源: From Middle English *coupen, from Old English *cūpan (“to buy, barter, exchange”), from Proto-Germanic *kōpjaną (“to buy”).  More recently, associated with "coop" (n.), referring to a cage or enclosure for poultry, leading to the sense of confinement.
