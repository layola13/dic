derisory:/dɪˈraɪsəri/ /dɪˈrɪsɔːri/
adjective
嘲笑的，嘲弄的，微不足道的
Expressing contempt or ridicule; ridiculously small or inadequate.
He made a derisory offer of $10 for the antique vase. 他对那只古董花瓶提出了10美元的嘲弄性报价。
synonyms:contemptuous, mocking, scornful, disdainful, ridiculous, laughable, paltry, insignificant, pitiful, measly
antonyms:respectful, admiring, substantial, significant, generous
词性:形容词

derisory:/dɪˈraɪsəri/ /dɪˈrɪsɔːri/
adjective
