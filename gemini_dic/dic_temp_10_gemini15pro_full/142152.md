Oriskany: /ˌɔːrɪˈskeɪni/ | n. | 奥里斯卡尼 |  A place name, specifically referring to a town in New York and also famously associated with the USS Oriskany, an aircraft carrier. The name derives from an Iroquois word meaning "nettles growing in the water." |  例句：He grew up near Oriskany, New York. 他在纽约奥里斯卡尼附近长大。
