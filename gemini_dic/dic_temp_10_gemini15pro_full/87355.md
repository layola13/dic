heroicomical:/ˌhɛrəʊɪˈkɒmɪkəl/ |adj.|滑稽英勇的；亦庄亦谐的| Combining elements of heroic and comical styles or subject matter.  A story or character that is both brave and funny. | The play was a heroicomical portrayal of a bumbling knight attempting a daring rescue. 这部戏剧滑稽地刻画了一位笨手笨脚的骑士试图进行一次大胆的营救。 |synonyms: mock-heroic, tragi-comic, burlesque | antonyms: serious, tragic, dramatic | adjective
