billowy:/ˈbɪloʊi/ (US) /ˈbɪləʊi/ (UK)| adj.|波涛汹涌的，滚滚的，如波浪般翻滚的|Resembling waves or billows; swelling or rolling outward.  Characterized by great swelling waves or surges.|The billowy clouds drifted across the sky.  波涛汹涌的云朵飘过天空。|Synonyms: undulating, rolling, surging, waving, swelling|Antonyms: flat, smooth, still, calm|adjective


billowy:/ˈbɪloʊi/ (US) /ˈbɪləʊi/ (UK)| adj.|宽松的，蓬松的|Loose and full, like a billow.|She wore a billowy white dress. 她穿着一件宽松的白色连衣裙。|Synonyms: loose, flowing, voluminous, puffy|Antonyms: tight, fitted, close-fitting|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From billow + -y.  "Billow" comes from Old Norse *bylgja, meaning "wave."
