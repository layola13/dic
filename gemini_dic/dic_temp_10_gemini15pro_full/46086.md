cyclorama:/ˌsaɪkləˈræmə/ | n. | 全景画，环形画| A large curved backdrop, often used in film or theatre, that surrounds the stage on three sides and is typically painted to depict a scene or landscape. | Example 1: The cyclorama depicted a vast, star-filled sky, creating a sense of infinite space. 全景画描绘了广阔的星空，营造出无限空间的感觉。

Example 2: The director used a cyclorama to create a realistic backdrop for the battle scene. 导演用环形画为战斗场景创造了逼真的背景。 | 近义词: backdrop, panorama | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
