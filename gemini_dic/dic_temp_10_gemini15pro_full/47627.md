debilitate:/dɪˈbɪlɪteɪt/
verb
使衰弱，使虚弱
to make someone or something physically weak
The illness debilitated him for months.  这种疾病使他虚弱了好几个月。

debilitate:/dɪˈbɪlɪteɪt/
verb
削弱；使无力
to weaken something, or make it less effective
Prolonged strike action debilitated the industry. 长期罢工削弱了该产业。

Etymology:
From Latin debilitāre, from debilis "weak".

Synonyms:
weaken, enfeeble, incapacitate, cripple, devitalize, sap, exhaust

Antonyms:
strengthen, invigorate, fortify, empower
