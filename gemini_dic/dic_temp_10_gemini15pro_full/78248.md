gerland:/ˈɡɜːrlənd/ | n. | 花环，花冠， garlands (plural) | A circular band of flowers and leaves worn on the head or neck, typically used as a decoration or symbol of victory. |  She wore a gerland of white roses in her hair. 她头发上戴着白色的玫瑰花环。| synonym: wreath, chaplet, crown | antonym: n/a | noun
