bmc:/biː ɛm siː/ | n. | 商业模式画布 (Business Model Canvas) | A strategic management and lean startup template for developing new or documenting existing business models. It is a visual chart with elements describing a firm's or product's value proposition, infrastructure, customers, and finances. | Example: The startup used a BMC to outline their business plan for investors. 这家初创公司使用商业模式画布向投资者概述了他们的商业计划。| Example:  We revised our BMC after pivoting to a new target market. 在转向新的目标市场后，我们修改了我们的商业模式画布。| Synonym: business plan, business model | Antonym: N/A | noun
