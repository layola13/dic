coilability:/ˌkɔɪləˈbɪləti/
n.
可绕性；卷绕性
The ability of a material, especially metal, to be wound or coiled into a desired shape without cracking or breaking.
The coilability of the copper wire is excellent, allowing it to be easily wound into tight coils for use in transformers.  这种铜线的可绕性非常好，可以很容易地绕成紧密的线圈用于变压器。
This new alloy boasts improved coilability compared to traditional materials.  这种新合金与传统材料相比，具有更好的可绕性。
flexibility, malleability, ductility
stiffness, rigidity
noun
