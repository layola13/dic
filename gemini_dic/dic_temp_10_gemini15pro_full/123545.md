monoxylon:/ˈmɒnəksaɪlən/ /ˈmɑːnəksaɪlɑːn/
n.|独木舟|A boat made from a single piece of wood, typically a hollowed-out log.|The fishermen used a monoxylon to navigate the shallow river. 渔民们用独木舟在浅水河中航行。|canoe, dugout|ship, yacht|noun
Etymology->
From Greek mónos ("single, alone") + xýlon ("wood").
