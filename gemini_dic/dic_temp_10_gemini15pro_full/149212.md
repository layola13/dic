oxyhaemoglobin:/ˌɒksiˈhiːməˌɡləʊbɪn/ /ˌɑːksiˈhiːməˌɡloʊbɪn/ | n. |氧合血红蛋白|A bright red substance formed by the combination of haemoglobin with oxygen, present in oxygenated blood.|The pulse oximeter measures the percentage of oxyhaemoglobin in the blood.  脉搏血氧仪测量血液中氧合血红蛋白的百分比。|Synonym: HbO2|Antonym: deoxyhaemoglobin|noun
