outclassing:/ˌaʊtˈklæsɪŋ/
verb
超越，胜过
Surpassing or exceeding in quality, skill, or performance, especially by a wide margin.
The new model outclasses its predecessors in terms of fuel efficiency.  这款新车型在燃油效率方面胜过其前身。
The experienced chess player easily outclassed his younger opponent.  这位经验丰富的棋手轻松击败了他的年轻对手。
Synonyms: surpassing, excelling, outshining, outstripping, trumping
Antonyms: underperforming, lagging behind
词性:动词
