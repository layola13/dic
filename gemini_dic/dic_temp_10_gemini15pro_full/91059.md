hustler:/ˈhʌslər/| n.| 骗子；精力旺盛的人；妓女；野心家 |Someone who is aggressive, especially in business or other financial dealings, and often uses questionable methods.| He's a real hustler, always looking for the next big deal. 他是个十足的野心家，总是在寻找下一个大买卖。 | synonyms: go-getter, entrepreneur, self-starter, striver, ambitious person | antonyms: slacker, loafer, idler | noun

hustle:/ˈhʌslər/| v.| 催促；赶紧；硬挤；强拉；兜售 | To make someone move quickly by pushing or pulling them roughly. To sell something aggressively, especially by approaching strangers.|  He hustled us out of the room. 他把我们从房间里赶了出去。 | synonyms: push, shove, jostle, rush, hurry | antonyms: dawdle, linger | verb
