dilation:/daɪˈleɪʃən/ /dɪˈleɪʃən/
|n.|扩张；膨胀；扩大|Synonym: expansion, enlargement, widening|Antonym: contraction, constriction, narrowing|Etymology: From Late Latin *dīlātiōn- (stem of dīlātiō), from Latin dīlātāre (“to spread out”), frequentative of differre (“to carry different ways, spread, scatter”).|词性: 名词
