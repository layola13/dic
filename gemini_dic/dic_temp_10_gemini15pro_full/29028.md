cartooned:/kɑːrˈtuːnd/
adjective
卡通化的；漫画化的
depicted or represented as in a cartoon.
例句：The politician was cartooned with an exaggeratedly large nose.  这位政治家被画成漫画，鼻子被夸张地放大了。

例句：The children's book cartooned the animals to make them more appealing. 这本儿童读物把动物卡通化，使它们更具吸引力。

近义词：caricatured, illustrated, stylized
反义词：realistic, naturalistic
词性：adjective
