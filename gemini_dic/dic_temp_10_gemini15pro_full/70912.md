firearmed:/ˈfaɪərɑːrmd/
adjective
携带武器的；装备枪械的
equipped with firearms; carrying a gun or guns
The police confronted the firearmed suspect. 警察与携带武器的嫌疑犯对峙。
近义词: armed, weaponed, gun-toting
反义词: unarmed, defenseless
词性: adjective
词源: fire + armed.  "Armed" comes from Old French "armer," from Latin "armare," meaning "to arm, equip with weapons."
