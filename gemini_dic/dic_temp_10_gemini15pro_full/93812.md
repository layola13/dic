immethodize:/ɪˈmɛθədaɪz/ | v. | 使…成为方法，方法化 | To convert a function into a method that can be called on an object. |

例句：
The library allows you to immethodize any function, making it easier to work with objects.
这个库允许你将任何函数方法化，使它更容易与对象一起工作。
