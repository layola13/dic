agreeableness:/əˈɡriːəblnəs/
n.
随和，亲切，宜人
the quality of being pleasant and easy to get along with
She is known for her agreeableness and willingness to help. 她以随和乐于助人而闻名。
近义词：amiability, affability, pleasantness, congeniality
反义词：disagreeableness, hostility, unpleasantness


ETYMOLOGY->
From agreeable + -ness.  Agreeable traces back to the Old French *agreable (modern French agréable), from Late Latin *agreabilis, from Latin gratus "pleasing, agreeable, thankful" + ad- "to".
