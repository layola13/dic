baroni:/bəˈroʊni/| n.|男爵夫人；女男爵|A baroness; a female baron. |Example: The Baroni hosted a grand ball at her castle. 女男爵在她的城堡里举办了一个盛大的舞会。|Example:  Maria Baroni, a renowned philanthropist, donated a large sum to the local hospital.  著名的慈善家玛丽亚·巴罗尼向当地医院捐赠了一大笔钱。|Synonyms: baroness, noblewoman|Antonyms: commoner|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *baroni*, plural of *barone* ("baron"), from Old Frankish *baro* ("freeman, warrior"). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *barō* (“man”).
