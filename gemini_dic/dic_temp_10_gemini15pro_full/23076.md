bounden:/ˈbaʊndən/
adjective
义不容辞的；有义务的
Obligatory; required.  Bound by oath, duty, or law.
例句：It is our bounden duty to help those in need.  帮助需要帮助的人是我们的义不容辞的责任。

例句：She felt it was her bounden duty to tell the truth. 她觉得说出真相是她义不容辞的责任。

近义词：obligatory, mandatory, required, compulsory, incumbent
反义词：optional, voluntary

词性：adjective

词源：From Middle English *bounden*, past participle of *binden* (“to bind”), from Old English *bindan*, from Proto-Germanic *bindaną*, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰendʰ-*. Cognate with Dutch *binden* (“to bind”), German *binden* (“to bind”),  Swedish *binda* (“to bind”).  Originally the past participle of "bind," meaning "bound,"  it evolved to mean "obligated" or "required."
