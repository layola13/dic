circularity:/ˌsɜːrkjəˈlærəti/ | n. | 循环性，圆形，迂回 | The argument suffered from circularity, using the conclusion as a premise.  这个论证犯了循环论证的错误，用结论作为前提。 | The circularity of the reasoning made it unconvincing. 推理的循环性使其无法令人信服。 | 近义词：roundness, cyclicity, circuitousness | 反义词：linearity, straightness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *circularitas, from Latin circularis "circular" + -itas. Ultimately from Latin circulus "circle".
