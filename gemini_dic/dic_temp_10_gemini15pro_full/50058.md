dense:/dɛns/ | adj. | 稠密的，密集的，浓厚的，愚钝的 | Closely packed together; thick.  |  The forest was dense with trees.  这片森林树木茂密。 | synonyms: thick, compact, crowded, concentrated | antonyms: sparse, thin, light, scattered | adjective


dense:/dɛns/ | adj. | 愚钝的，迟钝的 | Slow to understand; stupid. | He's a bit dense sometimes and doesn't get the joke. 他有时有点迟钝，听不懂笑话。 | synonyms: slow, stupid, obtuse, thick | antonyms: intelligent, sharp, bright, quick-witted | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *densus* (whence also French *dense*), past participle of *densāre* "to thicken," from *dēnsus* "thick, dense, crowded," perhaps from PIE root *dhen-* "to run, flow" (in the sense of "pressed together").
