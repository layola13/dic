coprolite: /ˈkɒprəlaɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːprəlaɪt/ (US)| n. |粪化石；化石粪便|Fossilized feces.  It provides valuable information about the diet and lifestyle of extinct animals. |Example: The paleontologist discovered a coprolite that contained fish bones, suggesting the animal was piscivorous.  古生物学家发现了一块含有鱼骨的粪化石，这表明这种动物是食鱼的。

Example:  Coprolites can be used to study the parasites that infected ancient creatures. 粪化石可以用来研究感染古代生物的寄生虫。|近义词: fossilized feces|反义词: (None -  There's no direct opposite for a fossilized object)|词性: noun
