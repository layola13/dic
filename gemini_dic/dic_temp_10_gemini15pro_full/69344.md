federalism: /ˈfɛdərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 联邦制 | A system of government in which power is divided between a central authority and constituent political units (such as states or provinces). | The United States operates under a system of federalism. 美国实行联邦制。|  Federalism allows states to have some autonomy while still being unified as a nation. 联邦制允许各州拥有一定的自治权，同时仍然作为一个统一的国家。| synonym: confederation, decentralization | antonym: centralism, unitary system | noun
