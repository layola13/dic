词源:  From Old French *mirour (modern French miroir), from Late Latin *miratorium, from Latin mirari "to wonder at," from mirus "wonderful, astonishing." The change in the ending was influenced by Old North French *mireor.


需要注意的是，"mirr"本身是一个名词，没有动词或形容词形式。上述例句中出现的动词形式，如"looking," "reflects," "broke"等，都是与"mirr"搭配使用的其他动词。
