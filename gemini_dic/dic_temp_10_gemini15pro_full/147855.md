overrash:/ˈoʊvərˌræʃ/ | adj. | 过于轻率的，鲁莽的 | Too hasty and reckless; acting without sufficient caution. | He made an overrash decision and regretted it later. 他做了一个过于轻率的决定，后来后悔了。|  She was overrash in her judgment of the situation. 她对形势的判断过于草率。 | 近义词: reckless, impulsive, hasty, impetuous, precipitate | 反义词: cautious, prudent, careful, deliberate, circumspect | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "over-" (excessive) + "rash" (acting without due consideration).  "Rash" ultimately comes from Old High German "rasc" (quick).
