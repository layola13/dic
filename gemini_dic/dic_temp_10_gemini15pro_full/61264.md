emarginate:/ɪˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/ | adj. |  边缘缺刻的，边缘有凹陷的 | Having a margin that is notched or indented. | The emarginate leaves of the plant were a key identifying feature.  这种植物边缘缺刻的叶子是其关键识别特征。| Synonym: notched, indented, retuse | Antonym: entire, smooth | adjective

emarginate:/ɪˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/ | vt. |  使边缘缺刻；降低…的边缘 | To make a margin notched or indented; to lower or reduce the margin of something. |  The craftsman carefully emarginated the edge of the wooden plaque. 工匠小心地将木牌的边缘刻出缺口。| Synonym: notch, indent, reduce | Antonym: extend, enlarge | verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēmarginātus*, past participle of *ēmargināre* (“to take away the margin”), from *ē* (“out of, from”) + *margināre* (“to furnish with a border”), from *margo* (“edge, border”).
