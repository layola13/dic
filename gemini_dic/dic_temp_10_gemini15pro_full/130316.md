non-technically:/ˌnɒnˈtɛknɪkəli/ | adv. | 非技术性地，通俗地 |  In simple, easy-to-understand terms, avoiding technical jargon.  |  He explained the complex process non-technically so everyone could understand. 他用通俗易懂的语言解释了这个复杂的过程，以便每个人都能理解。 | Example: She described the issue non-technically, using everyday language. 她用日常语言非技术性地描述了这个问题。 | Synonyms: simply, plainly, easily, understandably | Antonyms: technically, complexly, intricately | word type:adverb
