assert:/əˈsɜːrt/ (UK) /əˈsɜːrt/ (US)| v.|断言；维护；坚持|To state or declare something positively, often without proof. To defend or maintain (a right, claim, etc.).|He asserted his innocence. 他坚称自己无罪。|Synonyms: declare, affirm, maintain, avow|Antonyms: deny, refute, contradict|verb

assert:/əˈsɜːrt/ (UK) /əˈsɜːrt/ (US)| n.|断言；主张|A positive statement or declaration, often without proof.|His assertion of innocence was not believed. 他关于无罪的声明没有人相信。|Synonyms: declaration, affirmation, contention|Antonyms: denial, refutation, contradiction|noun
