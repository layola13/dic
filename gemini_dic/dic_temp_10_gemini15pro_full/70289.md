fidelities:/fɪˈdelɪtiz/
|n.|忠诚，忠实；保真度；精确度|The plural form of fidelity, meaning the quality of being faithful or loyal. It can also refer to the degree of exactness with which something is copied or reproduced.|例句：He swore his fidelities to the king. 他宣誓效忠国王。

|例句：The recording has remarkable fidelities to the original performance. 这份录音对原始表演的还原度非常高。
|近义词：loyalties, allegiances, accuracies, precisions|反义词：treacheries, betrayals, infidelities, inaccuracies|词性:名词
