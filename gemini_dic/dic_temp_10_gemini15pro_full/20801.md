blase:/blɑːˈzeɪ/ /bleɪˈzeɪ/| adj.|厌倦的，漠不关心的，玩腻了的|Unimpressed or indifferent to something because one has experienced or seen it so often before; world-weary.|He was blase about the dangers of the trip, having done it many times before. 他对这次旅行的危险漠不关心，因为他已经做过很多次了。|近义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested, world-weary, jaded|反义词：enthusiastic, excited, impressed, passionate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French blasé, past participle of blaser "to satiate," perhaps from Old Occitan blasar "to fill up with air," from Vulgar Latin *blasare, from Latin flare "to blow."
