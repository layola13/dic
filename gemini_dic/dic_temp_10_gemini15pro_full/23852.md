breadline:/ˈbredlaɪn/ (US) /ˈbredlaɪn/ (UK)| n. |贫困线；靠救济度日；排队领救济食物的人群|A line of people waiting to receive free food or other necessities, typically because they are very poor.  It can also refer to the level of poverty at which people require such assistance.|Many families are struggling to stay above the breadline. 许多家庭都在为维持生计而苦苦挣扎。|synonyms: poverty line, dole queue, welfare line|antonyms: affluence, wealth|noun
