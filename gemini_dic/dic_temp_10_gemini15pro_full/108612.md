leadenness:/ˈlɛdənɪs/| n. |沉重；迟钝；呆滞；阴郁|The state of being leaden, characterized by heaviness, dullness, or sluggishness.  It can refer to a physical or mental state.|例句：The leadenness of her limbs made it difficult to climb the stairs. 她的四肢沉重，爬楼梯很困难。

例句：A leadenness of spirit settled over the group after they heard the bad news.  听到坏消息后，一种沉闷的情绪笼罩着整个团队。|近义词：heaviness, dullness, sluggishness, inertia, torpor, apathy, listlessness|反义词：lightness, liveliness, energy, enthusiasm, vigor|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From leaden + -ness.  Leaden itself comes from Old English *lēaden, derived from lead (the metal), referring to its heavy weight.
