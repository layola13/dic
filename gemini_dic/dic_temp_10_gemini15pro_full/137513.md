nontraders:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪdərz/ | n. | 非交易者，非贸易商 | People who do not engage in trading or commercial activities. | Example:  The workshop was attended by both traders and nontraders. 研讨会由交易者和非交易者共同参加。

Example:  Nontraders often misunderstand the complexities of the stock market. 非交易者常常误解股票市场的复杂性。

|Synonyms: nonprofessionals, amateurs, laypeople | Antonyms: traders, professionals, merchants | noun
