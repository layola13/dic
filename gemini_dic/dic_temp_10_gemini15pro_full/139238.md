obligable:/ˈɒblɪɡəbl/ | adj. | 有义务的，应尽义务的 | Bound by duty or obligation; obliged.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |  He felt obligable to help his family. 他觉得有义务帮助他的家人。| 近义词：obliged, bound, compelled | 反义词：unobligated, free | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *obligabilis, from Latin obligare (“to oblige”).
