complimenting:/ˈkɒmplɪmentɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɪmentɪŋ/ (US)| v. (present participle)|赞美，恭维|The act of expressing praise or admiration for someone or something.|She was complimenting him on his new suit. 她正在赞美他的新西装。|Synonyms: praising, applauding, commending|Antonyms: criticizing, insulting, condemning|Verb (present participle)

complimenting:/ˈkɒmplɪmentɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmplɪmentɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|赞美的，恭维的|Expressing praise or admiration.|She made some complimenting remarks about his work. 她对他的工作做出了一些赞美的评价。|Synonyms: flattering, approving, laudatory|Antonyms: critical, disapproving, derogatory|Adjective
