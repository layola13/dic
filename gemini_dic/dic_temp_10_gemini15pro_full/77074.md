garrote:/ɡəˈrɒt/ /ɡəˈrɑːt/| n. vt.|绞索，勒死，抢劫|A method of execution or assassination by strangulation with a cord or wire. Also, the cord or wire used.  The act of strangling or throttling someone.|The victim was found garroted with a piece of wire. 受害者被发现被人用一段铁丝勒死了。|近义词：strangle, choke, throttle|反义词: release, free|noun, verb


garrotter:/ɡəˈrɒtər/ /ɡəˈrɑːtər/| n.|行凶抢劫者，勒索强盗|A person who garrotes someone, especially as a means of robbery.|The garrotter was apprehended by the police shortly after the attack. 抢劫者在袭击后不久就被警方逮捕了。|近义词：mugger, robber, thief|反义词: victim|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *garrote* "cudgel, throttling stick," related to Old Occitan *garrot*, from Gaulish *garros* "pole, staff".
