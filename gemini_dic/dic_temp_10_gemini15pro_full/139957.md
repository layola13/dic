odontolite:/ˈoʊdənˌtoʊˌlaɪt/ | n. |齿松石，羟基磷灰石|A mineral form of turquoise-blue to green hydroxylapatite, often used as a gemstone. It is formed from fossil bone or tooth material. |Example: The museum displayed a beautiful odontolite necklace.  博物馆展出了一条漂亮的齿松石项链。|Example:  Odontolite is sometimes confused with turquoise. 齿松石有时会被误认为是绿松石。|Synonym: bone turquoise, fossil turquoise, apatite|Antonym: N/A|Noun
