auxetophone:/ɔːkˈsɛtəfoʊn/| n.|助音器，扩音器|A device, typically a horn-shaped attachment for a gramophone, designed to amplify sound without the use of electricity.|The museum's collection includes a rare auxetophone, capable of producing remarkably loud sound for its time.|近义词:amplifier, loudspeaker, megaphone|反义词:mute, silencer|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek "auxein" (to increase) + "phone" (sound).
