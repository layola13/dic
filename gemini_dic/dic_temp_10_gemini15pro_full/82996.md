gunny:/ˈɡʌni/| n.|(US Marines slang) Gunnery Sergeant|A non-commissioned officer rank in the US Marine Corps, above Staff Sergeant and below Master Sergeant/First Sergeant.|The gunny barked orders at the recruits.|近义词：Sergeant (general term)|反义词: None|noun
