feud:/fjuːd/
noun
世仇；长期不和；争执
a prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute between two families, clans, or other groups.
The two families have been locked in a bitter feud for generations. 这两个家族几代人以来一直陷于激烈的世仇之中。

verb
长期争吵；长期不和
be engaged in a prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute.
The two families have been feuding for years. 这两个家族多年来一直不和。

近义词: quarrel, dispute, conflict, rivalry
反义词: harmony, agreement, peace, reconciliation
词性: noun, verb

词源:
From Old French *feude, from Frankish *faida "feud," from Proto-Germanic *faihizō "hostility, feud," from *faih "hostile, hateful," from PIE *peyk- "to attack, comb."
