pasteboards:/ˈpeɪstˌbɔːrdz/| n.|硬纸板（通常指多张）|Stiff, thick sheets of paper or cardboard, typically used for mounting artwork or making models.  Historically, they were also used for theatrical backdrops.|The artist arranged her drawings on several pasteboards.|近义词：cardboard, card stock, mounting board|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thin paper")|noun
