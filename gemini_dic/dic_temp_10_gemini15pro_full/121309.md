misfolded:/ˌmɪsˈfoʊldɪd/ | adj. |  错误折叠的，折叠不正确的 | Incorrectly folded, especially referring to proteins. |  The misfolded proteins can cause diseases. 错误折叠的蛋白质会导致疾病。 |  malformed, deformed | correctly folded | adjective

misfolded:/ˌmɪsˈfoʊldɪd/ | v. |  错误地折叠 | To fold incorrectly. | He misfolded the napkin. 他把餐巾纸叠错了。 |  mishandle, fumble | fold correctly | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + folded, past participle of fold.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "badly," or "incorrectly." "Fold" comes from the Old English "fealdan," meaning "to double over."
