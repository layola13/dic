moralistic: /ˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 说教的，道学气的 | Characterized by or expressive of a concern with morality, especially a concern with the morals of others.  Exhibiting strong moral principles, sometimes in a self-righteous or excessive way. |  He had a very moralistic attitude towards life. 他对人生抱有一种非常说教的态度。 |  She gave me a moralistic lecture on the dangers of drinking. 她对我进行了一番关于饮酒危害的说教。 |  sanctimonious, preachy, self-righteous, judgmental | permissive, tolerant, broad-minded | adjective
