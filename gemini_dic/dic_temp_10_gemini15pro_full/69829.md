ferret-out:/ˈferɪt aʊt/
verb
搜寻出，找出（尤指隐藏或难以发现的事物）
to search out, discover, or bring to light, especially something hidden or difficult to find
The reporter ferreted out the corruption scandal. 记者揭露了腐败丑闻。
近义词: uncover, unearth, discover, expose, detect
反义词: conceal, hide, bury
词性: verb

ferret:/ˈferɪt/
noun
雪貂
a small, domesticated, weasel-like mammal used for hunting rabbits and rats
My pet ferret loves to play hide-and-seek. 我的宠物雪貂喜欢玩捉迷藏。
近义词: polecat (related species)
反义词: (No direct antonym for the animal itself)
词性: noun


词源:
From the Old French word *fureter*, meaning "to hunt with a ferret," derived from *furet*, meaning "ferret" (the animal).  The animal itself gets its name from the Latin *furittus*, meaning "little thief," due to its habit of stealing small prey.
