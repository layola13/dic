infill:/ˈɪnfɪl/| n. v.|填充物，填充，|The builder used sand as infill for the foundations. 建筑工人用沙子作为地基的填充物。|例句：They used concrete to infill the hole. 他们用混凝土填充了洞。|近义词：fill, packing, stuffing|反义词:excavate, remove|词性:名词/动词
词源: From in- + fill.  "In-" is a prefix meaning "in, into, within, towards, or on." "Fill" comes from the Old English "fyllan," meaning "to make full, fill up, satisfy."
