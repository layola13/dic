liberalizers:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzərz/
noun
自由化者，自由主义者
Those who advocate for or implement policies that promote liberalization, often in the context of economics, politics, or social issues.  Those who make something less strict.
The liberalizers argued for deregulation of the financial markets. 自由化者主张放松对金融市场的管制。
Liberalizers pushed for greater freedom of speech and assembly. 自由化人士推动更大的言论和集会自由。
synonyms: reformers, progressives, advocates
antonyms: conservatives, traditionalists, reactionaries
n.
