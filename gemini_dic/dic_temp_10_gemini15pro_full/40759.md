convictism: /kənˈvɪktɪzəm/
n.
罪犯主义，囚犯制度
A system of penal transportation and forced labour, historically practiced in Australia.  A social system based on the labour of convicts.
Australia's history is deeply intertwined with convictism, as thousands were transported there from Britain. 澳大利亚的历史与罪犯制度紧密相连，成千上万的人从英国被运送到那里。
The legacy of convictism continues to shape Australian identity. 罪犯制度的遗产继续塑造着澳大利亚人的身份。
近义词: penal colony, transportation system
反义词:  rehabilitation system
