lukewarm:/ˈluːkˌwɔːrm/ (US), /ˈljuːkˌwɔːm/ (UK)| adj.|微温的，不冷不热的；不热情的，冷淡的|Moderately warm; neither hot nor cold; lacking enthusiasm or conviction.|The coffee had gone lukewarm. 咖啡已经变温了。|synonyms: tepid, mild, indifferent|antonyms: hot, passionate, enthusiastic|adjective

lukewarm:/ˈluːkˌwɔːrm/ (US), /ˈljuːkˌwɔːm/ (UK)| n.|微温的状态|The state of being moderately warm; tepidity.|The lukewarmness of the bath was disappointing.  这洗澡水的微温令人失望。|synonyms: tepidity, mildness|antonyms: heat, fervor|noun
