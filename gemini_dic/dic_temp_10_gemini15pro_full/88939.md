holdout:/ˈhoʊldaʊt/ | n. | 拒不妥协的人（或物）；抵抗者；坚持者；（尤指）拒不卖地的人 | a person, country, or organization that refuses to agree or act with others, especially one that refuses to sell land | The last holdouts finally agreed to the terms of the contract.  最后的坚持者最终同意了合同条款。 |  resistor, dissenter, objector | conformist, cooperator | noun

holdout:/ˈhoʊldaʊt/ | n. | 抵制；坚持；拒绝 | resistance; persistence in refusing to do something |  Their holdout against the new regulations is costing the company money.  他们对新规的抵制使公司损失了资金。| resistance, defiance, opposition | compliance, submission | noun
