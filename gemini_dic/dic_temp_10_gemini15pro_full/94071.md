impassionedness:/ɪmˈpæʃəndnɪs/
noun
激情; 热情; 激昂
The state of being impassioned; fervor; ardor.
Her impassionedness for the cause was evident in her fiery speech. 她对这项事业的热情在她激昂的演讲中显而易见。
Synonyms: fervor, ardor, passion, zeal, intensity
Antonyms: apathy, indifference, coldness, detachment
