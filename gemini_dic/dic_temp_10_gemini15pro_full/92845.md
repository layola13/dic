icteric:/ɪkˈtɛrɪk/ /aɪˈktɛrɪk/
adjective
黄疸的；患黄疸病的
Affected by jaundice, having yellowing of the skin, eyes, and mucous membranes.
The patient's skin appeared icteric, suggesting liver dysfunction.  病人皮肤呈现黄疸，提示肝功能障碍。
