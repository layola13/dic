begs:/beɡz/| v.|乞求，恳求；（事实、情况等）表明，显示|The situation begs the question of whether we need more security.  这种情况引出了我们是否需要加强安保的问题。|近义词：pleads, implores, entreats, indicates, suggests|反义词:demands, insists, commands|词性:动词(third-person singular present tense)

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He begs for money on the street. = 他在街上乞讨钱。
She begs her mother to let her go to the party. = 她恳求她妈妈让她去参加派对。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The dog is begging for a treat. = 这只狗正在乞求得到款待。
He is begging her to reconsider. = 他正在恳求她重新考虑。
