lying-in:/ˈlaɪɪŋ ɪn/ | n. |分娩期，产褥期 |The period of time after a woman has given birth to a child, during which she rests and recovers. |  She had a difficult lying-in after the birth of her twins. 她生完双胞胎后，产褥期很艰难。

|近义词：confinement, postpartum period |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑怀孕期 pregnancy) | 词性: 名词

lying-in:/ˈlaɪɪŋ ɪn/ | adj. |产褥的 |Relating to or used during the period of confinement after childbirth. | She needed lying-in clothes and supplies. 她需要产后服和用品。

|近义词：postpartum, postnatal |反义词:  prenatal (产前的) | 词性: 形容词
