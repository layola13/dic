deliberators:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtərz/
n.
审议者，商讨者，讨论者
People who engage in deliberation, carefully considering and discussing something before making a decision.
The deliberators spent hours weighing the pros and cons of the proposal.  审议者花了几个小时权衡该提案的利弊。
The deliberators finally reached a consensus after a lengthy discussion.  经过长时间的讨论，审议者最终达成了一致。
近义词: discussants, panelists, jurors, judges
反义词:  impulsive people, rash people
