graminaceous:/ˌɡræmɪˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|禾本科的，似禾草的|Relating to or resembling grasses; belonging to the grass family (Gramineae or Poaceae).|例句：The field was covered with graminaceous plants. 这片田野覆盖着禾本科植物。

例句：Rice, wheat, and corn are all graminaceous crops. 水稻、小麦和玉米都是禾本科作物。|近义词：grassy, gramineal|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *graminaceus*, from *gramen* ("grass") + *-aceus* (suffix forming adjectives).
