divedapper:/ˈdaɪvˌdæpər/| n. |翠鸟，潜鸟|A small bird that dives into water to catch fish.  A skilled diver.|例句：The kingfisher, a type of divedapper, darted into the stream.  翠鸟，一种潜鸟，冲进了小溪。

例句：He was a divedapper in the pool, effortlessly swimming underwater. 他在泳池里像个潜鸟，毫不费力地在水下游动。|近义词：kingfisher, dabchick, dipper|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑land bird 陆地鸟)|词性:noun
