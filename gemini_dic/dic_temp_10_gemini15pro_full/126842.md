naphthol:/ˈnæfθɑːl, ˈnæfθɒl/| n. |萘酚|An aromatic organic compound derived from naphthalene by replacing one hydrogen atom with a hydroxyl group. There are two isomers: alpha-naphthol and beta-naphthol.|Naphthol is used in the production of dyes and pigments.|近义词：hydroxynaphthalene|反义词: None|noun
