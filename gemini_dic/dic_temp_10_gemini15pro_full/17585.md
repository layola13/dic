beauticians:/bjuːˈtɪʃənz/
n.|美容师|A person, especially a woman, whose job is to give beauty treatments.|Many beauticians offer a range of services, including manicures and facials. 许多美容师提供一系列服务，包括美甲和面部护理。|cosmetologists, estheticians| - |noun

Etymology:
From beauty + -ician (a suffix forming agent nouns).  Ultimately from Latin bellus (“beautiful”)
