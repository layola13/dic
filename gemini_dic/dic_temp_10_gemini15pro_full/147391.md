overnice:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪs/| adj.|过分殷勤的，过分客气的|Excessively nice; too polite or agreeable, often to the point of seeming insincere or affected.  It suggests an unnatural or excessive display of niceness that can be perceived as  ingratiating or even suspicious.|例句：He was so overnice to the boss that it seemed insincere. 他对老板过分殷勤，显得不真诚。

例句：Her overnice manner made me uncomfortable. 她过分客气的态度让我感到不舒服。|近义词：ingratiating, obsequious, unctuous, fawning|反义词：rude, blunt, abrupt|词性:adjective
