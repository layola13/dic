antimonopolists:/ˌæntiˈmənɒpəlɪsts/ | n. | 反垄断者；反垄断主义者 | People who oppose monopolies or advocate for policies to prevent or dismantle them.  Those who actively work against the concentration of economic power in the hands of a few. |  The antimonopolists argued that the merger would stifle competition. 反垄断者认为这次合并会扼杀竞争。|  The antimonopolists lobbied Congress for stricter regulations. 反垄断者游说国会制定更严格的法规。 | Synonyms: trust-busters,  competitors, regulators | Antonyms: monopolists, oligopolists | Noun
