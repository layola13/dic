attorneyism: /əˈtɜːrniɪzəm/
n.
律师主义; 过度依赖律师; 律师的职业作风; 律师的习气
The excessive or undue influence of lawyers or the legal profession, especially in political or business affairs; the practices or characteristics of lawyers.
The report criticized the growing attorneyism in government.  这份报告批评了政府中日益增长的律师主义。
The company's culture of attorneyism stifled innovation. 该公司的律师主义文化扼杀了创新。

近义词: legalism, litigiousness
反义词:  (No clear antonym, perhaps informality, deregulation)
词性: noun
