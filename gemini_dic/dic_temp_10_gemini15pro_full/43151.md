coziness:/ˈkoʊzinəs/| n.|舒适，温馨，惬意|The coziness of the cabin made us feel at home. 小屋的舒适让我们感觉像在家一样。|例句：The room exuded an air of coziness with its soft lighting and plush furniture. 房间里柔和的灯光和豪华的家具营造出一种舒适的氛围。

例句：She loved the coziness of the fireplace on a cold winter's night.  她喜欢在寒冷的冬夜里壁炉边的温暖舒适。|近义词：comfort, warmth, snugness, intimacy|反义词:discomfort, coldness, bleakness|词性:名词

词源: From cozy + -ness.  Cozy (1820-30) probably comes from Scots cozie, of uncertain origin, perhaps from Middle Low German kose "blanket, coverlet."
