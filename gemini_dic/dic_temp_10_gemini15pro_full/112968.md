lukewarmly:/ˈluːkwɔːrmli/ | adv. | 不冷不热地；不热心地；冷淡地 |  In a manner that lacks enthusiasm or conviction; half-heartedly.  | She greeted the proposal lukewarmly, showing little interest. 她对这个提议反应冷淡，几乎没有表现出兴趣。| He lukewarmly applauded the performance, clearly unimpressed.  他冷淡地鼓掌，显然没有被打动。 | 近义词: apathetically, indifferently, half-heartedly | 反义词: enthusiastically, eagerly, passionately | 词性: 副词

词源: From lukewarm + -ly.  Lukewarm itself comes from Old English *hlēow-wearm, meaning "tepid," literally "lukewarm."  "Luke" in this context is related to Old English hlēow, meaning "shelter," suggesting a protected warmth, not a full heat.
