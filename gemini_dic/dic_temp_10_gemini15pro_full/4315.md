aiyo:/ˈaɪjoʊ/ | int. | 哎呀; 天哪 | An exclamation expressing mild annoyance, frustration, surprise, or exasperation.  Derived from various Chinese dialects (e.g., Cantonese 哎吔 aai1 ya1, Hokkien  哎喲 ai1 yō). | Aiyo, I forgot my keys! 哎呀，我忘记带钥匙了！| Near Synonyms: oh dear, oh no, oops, gosh, good heavens | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's an interjection.  Words expressing satisfaction might be considered loosely antonymous, e.g., great, wonderful) | Interjection
