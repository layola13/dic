highnesses:/ˈhaɪnəsəz/| n.|殿下们；各位殿下|A plural form of address for multiple people of royal or noble rank.  Used to address more than one person holding the title of Highness. |Your Highnesses, we are honored by your presence. 殿下们，我们很荣幸能有您的光临。|Their Highnesses have arrived. 殿下们已经到了。|近义词：majesties, excellencies|反义词:commoners|词性:名词
