crenation:/kriːˈneɪʃən/| n.|  (细胞)锯齿状收缩，圆齿状，[医]齿状缘|The crenation of red blood cells is often a sign of dehydration. 红细胞的锯齿状收缩通常是脱水的迹象。|shrinking, contracting| swelling, expansion|noun
Etymology->
From Latin *crenatio*, from crenatus (“notched, indented”), from crena (“notch”).
