gaffers:/ˈɡæfəz/| n. |灯光师；（电影、电视的）照明工；|A gaffer is the head electrician on a movie or television set, responsible for the design and execution of the lighting plan.|The gaffer adjusted the lights to create a dramatic mood. 灯光师调整灯光以营造戏剧性的气氛。|The gaffer yelled for someone to bring him a spare bulb. 照明工大喊让别人给他拿个备用灯泡。|Synonyms: chief electrician, lighting technician, head of lighting|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "grip" which refers to other crew members)|Noun
