half-jelled:/ˌhɑːfˈdʒɛld/
adj.
半凝固的，部分胶化的
Partially gelled; not completely set.  The mixture was half-jelled, still slightly liquid in the center.
The pudding was half-jelled, so I put it back in the fridge. 布丁半凝固了，所以我把它放回冰箱里。
Near Antonyms: runny, liquid, fluid
Antonyms: set, firm, solid
词性: 形容词
