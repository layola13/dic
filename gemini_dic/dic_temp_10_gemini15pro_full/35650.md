coaxers:/ˈkoʊksərz/ (US) /ˈkəʊksəz/ (UK)| n. |劝诱者；哄劝者| People who persuade someone gently and patiently to do something.| The coaxers finally got the shy child to join the game. 劝诱者最终让害羞的孩子加入了游戏。|Synonyms: persuaders, wheedlers, cajolers|Antonyms: discouragers, dissuaders|noun
