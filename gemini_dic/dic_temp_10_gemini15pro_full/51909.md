dhoti:/ˈdoʊti/| n. |缠腰布，兜提|A garment worn by men in India, consisting of a long piece of cloth wrapped around the waist and legs.|He wore a traditional white dhoti. 他穿着一件传统的白色缠腰布。|Synonym: lunghi, mundu|Antonym: trousers, pants|noun

dhootis:/ˈdoʊtiz/| n. |缠腰布（复数）|Plural form of dhoti, referring to multiple garments.|He bought several dhootis as souvenirs. 他买了几条缠腰布作为纪念品。|Synonym: lungis, mundus|Antonym: trousers, pants|noun
