bandana: /bænˈdænə/ (US) /bænˈdɑːnə/ (UK)| n. |头巾，大手帕|A large, brightly colored handkerchief, often worn on the head or around the neck.|例句：He wore a red bandana around his head. 他头上戴着一条红色的头巾。

例句：She used a bandana to tie up her hair. 她用头巾扎起了头发。

近义词：handkerchief, scarf, kerchief |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
