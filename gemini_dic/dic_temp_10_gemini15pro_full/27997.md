canyoning: /ˈkænjənɪŋ/ /kænjəniŋ/ | n. |峡谷漂流；溪降|The descent of canyons using various techniques such as rappelling, swimming, and jumping. | We went canyoning in Utah last summer.  我们去年夏天在犹他州进行了峡谷漂流。| 近义词: canyoneering, rappelling, gorge walking | 反义词: hiking, climbing | 词性: 名词

词源: From canyon + -ing,  influenced by French *canyonisme*.  The sport itself originated in Europe.
