palette: /ˈpælɪt/ (US), /ˈpælət/ (UK) | n. | 调色板；颜料；（计算机程序的）颜色选择器；（事物的）范围，种类 | A thin board or slab on which an artist lays and mixes colors.  A range of colors or qualities. | The artist squeezed paint onto his palette. 艺术家把颜料挤到调色板上。|  The film offers a rich palette of visual and emotional textures. 这部电影提供了丰富的视觉和情感质感。 | synonyms: range, spectrum, array, gamut | antonyms: monochrome, limitation | noun

palette: /ˈpælɪt/ (US), /ˈpælət/ (UK) | n. | （食品的）风味，味道 | The complex of sensations in the mouth produced by a substance.  | The wine has a fruity palette. 这款酒果味浓郁。 | synonyms: flavor, taste, savor | antonyms: blandness | noun
