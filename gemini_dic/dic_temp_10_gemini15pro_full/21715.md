boardrooms:/ˈbɔːrdruːmz/ /ˈbɔːrdrʊmz/
|n.|会议室；董事会会议室|A room where a company's board of directors meets.|The important decision was made in the boardrooms.  这个重要的决定是在董事会会议室做出的。|meeting rooms, conference rooms|  (No direct antonym, could consider 'offices' or 'workspaces' in a broader sense)|noun

boardroom:/ˈbɔːrdruːm/ /ˈbɔːrdrʊm/
|n.|会议室；董事会会议室；（公司）高层|A room where a company's board of directors meets. / The highest level of management in a company.|The boardroom approved the merger. 董事会批准了合并。|meeting room, conference room| (No direct antonym, could consider 'offices' or 'workspaces' in a broader sense)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "board" (a table, especially one used for meetings) + "room".  "Board" in this sense ultimately comes from the Old English "bord," meaning a plank or table.
