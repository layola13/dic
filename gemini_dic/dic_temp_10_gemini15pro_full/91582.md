hyperacuities:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkjuːɪtiz/ | n. | 超常的敏锐度；超常的精确度 |  The ability to discriminate sensory stimuli with far greater precision than is typical.  This refers to sensory performance that exceeds normal limits. | Bats exhibit hyperacuities in hearing, allowing them to navigate and hunt in complete darkness.  蝙蝠在听力方面表现出超常的敏锐度，使它们能够在完全黑暗的环境中导航和狩猎。| heightened sensitivities, exceptional acuities | reduced sensitivities, impaired acuities | noun
