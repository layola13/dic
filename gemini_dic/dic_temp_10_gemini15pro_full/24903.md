bruited:/ˈbruːtɪd/
verb
散布，传播（消息、谣言等）
to make (something, such as a rumor or report) widely known
The rumor was bruited about that the company was in financial trouble.  这家公司陷入财务困境的谣言四处传播。
synonyms: spread, circulate, disseminate, broadcast, rumor, noise abroad
antonyms: conceal, suppress, withhold
