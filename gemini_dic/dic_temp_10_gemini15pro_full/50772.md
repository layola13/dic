desaturations:/diːˌsætʃəˈreɪʃənz/
noun
去饱和，脱饱和；血氧饱和度下降
A decrease in the saturation of a substance, especially of oxygen in the blood.
The patient experienced several desaturations overnight.  病人整夜经历了几次血氧饱和度下降。
近义词:hypoxemia, oxygen desaturation
反义词:saturation, oxygenation
词性:n.


desaturation:/diːˌsætʃəˈreɪʃən/
noun
去色；降低色彩饱和度
The process of reducing the intensity of color in an image.
The photographer used desaturation to create a vintage look. 摄影师使用去色来营造复古外观。
近义词:bleaching, fading
反义词:saturation
词性:n.
