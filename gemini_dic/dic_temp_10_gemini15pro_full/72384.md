fluctiferous:/ˌflʌktɪˈfɛrəs/
adjective
波动的，起伏的
bearing or producing waves; fluctuating
The fluctiferous nature of the stock market makes it a risky investment. 股票市场的波动性使其成为一项高风险投资。
近义词: fluctuating, waving, undulating, oscillatiing
反义词: stable, steady, constant, unchanging
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *fluctifer, from fluctus (“wave”) + -fer (“bearing”).
