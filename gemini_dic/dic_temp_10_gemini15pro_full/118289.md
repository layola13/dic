mercur:/ˈmɜːrkjʊər/ (US), /ˈmɜːkjʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|水银; 水星|A heavy, silvery-white metallic element, liquid at room temperature, also known as quicksilver. Also refers to the planet closest to the sun. |Mercury is used in thermometers. 水银用于温度计。|The planet Mercury has no moons. 水星没有卫星。|Synonyms: quicksilver (for the element), hydrargyrum (technical)|Antonyms: None (for the element), None (for the planet)|noun
