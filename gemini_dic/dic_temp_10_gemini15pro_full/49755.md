demolishable:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃəbl/ /dɪˈmɑːlɪʃəbl/| adj.|可拆除的，可摧毁的|Capable of being demolished; able to be destroyed.|The old building was declared demolishable. 这栋老建筑被宣布可以拆除。|The dilapidated shed is easily demolishable.  这破旧的小棚很容易拆除。|近义词：destructible, raze, tear down|反义词:indestructible, durable|词性:adjective
词源: From demolish + -able.  Demolish comes from the Old French demolir, from Latin demoliri, from de- "down" + moliri "to build, construct".
