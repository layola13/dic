group:/ɡruːp/ | n. | 组，群 | A number of people or things that are located close together or are considered or treated as a unit. |  The group of tourists gathered around the guide.  这群游客聚集在导游周围。| synonyms:  cluster, bunch, collection | antonyms: individual, single | 词性: 名词 (Noun)
group:/ɡruːp/ | vt. | 分组，聚集 | Arrange (people or things) into a group or groups. | The teacher grouped the students by ability. 老师按能力将学生分组。 | synonyms:  categorize, classify, arrange | antonyms: separate, divide, disperse | 词性: 动词 (Verb)
