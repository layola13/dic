darlings: /ˈdɑːrlɪŋz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːrlɪŋz/ (US)
|n.|亲爱的，宝贝，心肝|A term of endearment, often used to address loved ones or close friends, sometimes also used sarcastically.|例句：He called all his friends "darlings". 他称呼所有朋友都叫“亲爱的”。|例句：She looked at her children and said, "Come on, darlings, let's go." 她看着她的孩子们说：“来吧，亲爱的们，我们走吧。”|近义词：sweethearts, dears, beloveds|反义词：enemies, foes|词性：名词
