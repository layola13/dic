bearablenesses:/ˈbɛərəbəlnəsɪz/
noun
可忍受性；可容忍性
The quality or state of being bearable.  The plural form of bearableness.
The bearablenesses of everyday life can be found in small moments of joy.  日常生活中可忍受之处在于点点滴滴的快乐时光。
The stoic philosophy emphasizes finding bearablenesses in suffering.  斯多葛学派哲学强调在苦难中寻找可忍受之处。
synonyms: tolerances, endurances, manageability
antonyms: intolerableness, unendurableness, insufferableness
