differenced:/ˈdɪfrənst/ | adj. | 有区别的，不同的 |  Distinguished; showing a difference.  Resulting from subtraction. | The differenced data showed a clear trend. 差异数据显示出一个清晰的趋势。 | The two versions of the software were differenced to identify the changes. 对这两个版本的软件进行了比较，以找出变化。 | 近义词: distinct, separate, varied, contrasted, dissimilar | 反义词: similar, alike, identical, uniform, homogeneous | 词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "difference," which comes from the Old French "diferer" and ultimately from the Latin "differre," meaning "to carry apart," "to differ," "to disagree." The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle and adjective.
