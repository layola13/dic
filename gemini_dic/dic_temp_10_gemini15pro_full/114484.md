malacoderm:/ˈmæləkoʊˌdɜːrm/| n. |软甲虫|An insect of the Malacodermata, a group of beetles characterized by soft wing covers.  These beetles are often bioluminescent, like fireflies.|Fireflies are malacoderms known for their bioluminescence. 萤火虫是软甲虫，以其生物发光而闻名。|The museum's collection includes several rare species of malacoderms.  博物馆的收藏包括几种稀有的软甲虫。|近义词：soft-bodied beetle, firefly (for some species)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider beetles with hardened elytra)|词性:noun
