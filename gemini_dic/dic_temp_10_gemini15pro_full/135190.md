nonorganics:/ˌnɒnɔːrˈɡænɪks/ | n. |无机物|Substances that are not derived from living organisms and do not contain carbon-hydrogen bonds, except for simple carbon compounds like carbonates and oxides.|Farmers are increasingly relying on nonorganics to boost crop yields.|Synonyms: inorganic matter, inorganic substances|Antonyms: organics, organic matter|noun
