insecureness:/ɪnsɪˈkjuːrətnəs/| n. |不安全感，不自信|The state of being insecure; lack of confidence or assurance.|She overcame her insecurity and spoke confidently in front of the crowd. 她克服了不安全感，在人群面前自信地发言。|近义词：anxiety, self-doubt, vulnerability|反义词: confidence, security, self-assurance|词性: 名词
Etymology: From insecure + -ness. Insecure from in- 'not' + secure.
