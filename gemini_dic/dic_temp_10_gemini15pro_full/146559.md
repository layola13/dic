overgracious: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪʃəs/
adj.
过分殷勤的；过分客气的
excessively gracious; too polite or kind in a way that seems insincere
例句：His overgracious manner made her suspicious. 他过分殷勤的态度让她起了疑心。

例句：She found his overgracious welcome a little unsettling. 她觉得他过分热情的欢迎有点令人不安。


近义词：unctuous, obsequious, fawning, sycophantic
反义词：rude, impolite, discourteous, ungracious


词性: adjective
