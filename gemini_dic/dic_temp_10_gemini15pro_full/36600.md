colleagues:/ˈkɒliːɡz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːliːɡz/ (US)| n. |同事，同僚|A person with whom one works, especially in a profession or business.|例句：I get along well with my colleagues. 我和我的同事相处得很好。

例句：My colleagues and I worked late last night to finish the project.  我和我的同事昨晚工作到很晚才完成项目。

近义词：co-workers, associates, peers, partners|反义词：competitors, rivals, opponents|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Old French *collegue* "fellow student," from Latin *collega* "partner in office," from *com-* "with" + *lega-*, "one authorized to act as deputy," related to *legare* "to depute, bequeath."
