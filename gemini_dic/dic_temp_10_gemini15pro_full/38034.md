components:/kəmˈpoʊnənts/ /kəmˈpəʊnənts/| n.|组成部分，成分，部件|A component is one of several parts that together make up a whole system, machine, etc.|The electronic components of the computer are very sophisticated. 这台电脑的电子元件非常精密。|synonyms: parts, elements, constituents, ingredients|antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

components:/kəmˈpoʊnənts/ /kəmˈpəʊnənts/| n.|要素，因素|A component is one of several important parts that combine to form something.|Trust is a vital component of a successful marriage.  信任是成功婚姻的重要组成部分。|synonyms: factor, element, ingredient, aspect|antonyms: none|noun
