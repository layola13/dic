odiousnesses:/ˈoʊdiəsnəsɪz/
noun
可憎的事物；令人厌恶的事物
The state or quality of being odious; hatefulness; extreme unpleasantness.
The odiousnesses of slavery were well documented.  奴隶制度的种种可憎之处都有详细的记录。
近义词: abominations, atrocities, horrors, vilenesses
反义词: pleasantries, delights, virtues
词性: noun (plural)

odious:/ˈoʊdiəs/
adjective
可憎的；令人厌恶的
Extremely unpleasant; repulsive.
The dictator's odious regime was finally overthrown.  令人憎恶的独裁政权最终被推翻了。
近义词: repulsive, repugnant, abhorrent, detestable
反义词: pleasant, delightful, agreeable, admirable
词性: adjective
