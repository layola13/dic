coscripting:/ˈkoʊˌskrɪptɪŋ/ | n. | 共同创作，合作编写 | The process of writing something together, often a screenplay or script.  It involves two or more writers working collaboratively to develop and complete a written work. | Example 1: The coscripting process allowed both writers to contribute their unique strengths to the project. 共同创作的过程让两位作家都能为项目贡献他们独特的优势。

Example 2: Coscripting can be a challenging but rewarding experience. 合作编写剧本可能是一个充满挑战但回报丰厚的经历。 | Synonyms: collaboration, joint authorship, co-writing | Antonyms: solo writing, independent authorship | noun
