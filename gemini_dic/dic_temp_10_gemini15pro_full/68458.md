familiarisingly:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzɪŋli/
adv.
使人熟悉地，亲切地
in a way that makes something or someone become familiar
He spoke familiarisingly about the town, as if he'd lived there all his life. 他说起这个小镇就好像在那里住了一辈子似的，让人感觉很熟悉。
近义词: intimately, personally, warmly
反义词: distantly, coldly, formally
词性: 副词
