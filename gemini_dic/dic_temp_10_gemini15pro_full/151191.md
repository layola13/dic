parchingly:/ˈpɑːrtʃɪŋli/| adv.|干燥地，炎热地|In a way that causes dryness, especially of the throat; extremely thirst-inducing.|The sun beat down parchingly on the desert. 太阳炙烤着沙漠，使人感到口渴。|Synonyms: aridly, dryly, thirstily|Antonyms: damply, humidly|Adverb
