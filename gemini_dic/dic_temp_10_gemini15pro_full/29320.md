castigate:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪt/| v. |严厉批评；责骂；惩罚 |To criticize or reprimand severely. |例句：The teacher castigated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师严厉批评了考试作弊的学生。

例句：The politician was castigated in the media for his controversial remarks.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论而受到媒体的严厉批评。

近义词：reprimand, rebuke, censure, chastise, scold |反义词: praise, commend, laud |词性: verb

词源: From Latin castigare "to correct, chastise," from castus "pure" + agere "to drive, lead, do."
