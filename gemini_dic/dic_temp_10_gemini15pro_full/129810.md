non-gradable:/nɒnˈɡreɪdəbl/ /nɑːnˈɡreɪdəbl/| adj.|不可分级的，绝对的|An adjective that cannot be used with grading adverbs like "very" or "extremely" because its meaning is already absolute. It represents a complete or total quality that cannot be intensified.|例句：The room was freezing, not just cold. 房间冷得像冰窖，而不仅仅是冷。|例句：The answer is either right or wrong. It cannot be very right. 答案要么是对的，要么是错的。它不可能非常对。|近义词：absolute, categorical|反义词：gradable|词性:形容词
