normativity:/nɔːrməˈtɪvəti/ | n. | 规范性，常态 |  The state or quality of being normative, relating to or deriving from a standard or norm, especially of behavior.  It refers to the ways in which norms, standards, and values shape and influence behavior, thought, and social interactions. | The professor questioned the normativity of traditional gender roles. 教授质疑传统性别角色的规范性。| The study examined the normativity surrounding marriage and family. 这项研究考察了围绕婚姻和家庭的规范性。 |  standard, normalcy, conventionality | nonconformity, deviance | noun
