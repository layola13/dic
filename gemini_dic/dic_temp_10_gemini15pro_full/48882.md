defragmenting:/diːfræɡˈmɛntɪŋ/
|v.| 碎片整理；整理碎片 |The process of reorganizing the files on a hard drive so that the pieces of each file are stored contiguously, improving access speed.| I am defragmenting my hard drive to improve its performance. 我正在整理我的硬盘碎片以提高其性能。|Synonyms: optimizing, reorganizing, consolidating|Antonyms: fragmenting, scattering, disordering|Verb

defragmentation:/diːfræɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/
|n.| 碎片整理；去碎片 |The process of defragmenting a hard drive.| Regular defragmentation can improve your computer's speed. 定期整理碎片可以提高你的电脑速度。|Synonyms: optimization, reorganization, consolidation|Antonyms: fragmentation, scattering, disordering|Noun
