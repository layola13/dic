flaccidity:/flækˈsɪdəti/
n.
松弛；软弱；无力
lack of firmness or stiffness; the state of being flaccid
The flaccidity of the skin is a sign of aging.  皮肤松弛是衰老的迹象。

近义词:limpness, flabbiness, looseness
反义词:firmness, tightness, tautness
词性:名词(单数)

flaccid:/ˈflæsɪd/
adj.
松弛的；软弱的；无力的
(of part of the body) soft and hanging loosely or limply, especially through lack of tone
The flowers were flaccid from lack of water. 这些花因为缺水而变得软弱无力。

近义词:limp, flabby, loose
反义词:firm, tight, taut
词性:形容词
