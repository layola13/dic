callot:/ˈkælət/ | n. | 召集令；征兵令 | A call to arms; a summons to military service. |  The king issued a callot for all able-bodied men to join the army. 国王向所有体格健全的男子发出入伍的征兵令。 | synonyms: summons, call-up, levy, conscription | antonyms: discharge, release, demobilization | noun

Etymology: From French *callot*, diminutive of *cale*, "goose feather" (used for writing the summons).
