megalomaniac:/ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniæk/ | n. adj.| 狂妄自大的人；夸大狂|A person who has an obsessive desire for power and believes themselves to be more important and powerful than they truly are. | He was a megalomaniac who believed he could rule the world. 他是一个夸大狂，认为自己可以统治世界。| The CEO's megalomaniac plans nearly bankrupted the company.  这位CEO狂妄自大的计划几乎使公司破产。| 近义词: egomaniac, narcissist, tyrant | 反义词: humble person, modest person | 词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Greek *megalo- "great" + *mania "madness".
