overbearingness:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋnɪs/
n.
傲慢；专横；盛气凌人
The quality of being overbearing; arrogance or domineering behavior.
His overbearingness alienated his colleagues. 他的专横疏远了他的同事。
近义词：arrogance, haughtiness, domineeringness, imperiousness, high-handedness
反义词：humility, modesty, meekness
词性: 名词

overbearing:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋ/
adj.
专横的；盛气凌人的；傲慢的
Too confident and inclined to tell other people what to do.
He had an overbearing manner. 他态度专横。
近义词：domineering, arrogant, haughty, imperious, high-handed
反义词：humble, modest, meek
词性: 形容词


词源:
From overbearing + -ness.  Overbearing itself comes from the verb "overbear," meaning to overwhelm or dominate.  "Over-" is a prefix indicating excess, and "bear" in this context means to carry or exert influence.
