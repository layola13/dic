half-quarter:/ˈhæf ˈkwɔːrtər/ |adj.| 半刻，八分之一 |A half-quarter is one-eighth of a whole.  It refers to a period of time, specifically 7.5 minutes, often used in the context of sports or other timed events. |The game ended in a half-quarter draw.  比赛以八分之一平局结束。| Near synonyms: one-eighth, 7.5 minutes | Antonym: double quarter |noun
