manatees:/ˈmænəˌtiz/ | n. | 海牛 | Large, aquatic mammals found in warm, shallow waters, known for their gentle nature and slow movements.  They are herbivores and often referred to as "sea cows." |  Manatees are often seen grazing on seagrass in Florida's coastal waters. 海牛经常在佛罗里达州的沿海水域吃海草。 | synonyms: sea cows | antonyms: N/A | noun
