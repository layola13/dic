conforms:/kənˈfɔːrmz/
verb
符合；遵照；顺应
(third-person singular simple present conforms, present participle conforming, simple past conformed, past participle conformed)  Behaves or is in accordance with.

The data conforms to the predicted model.  这些数据与预测模型相符。

New buildings must conform to safety regulations. 新建筑物必须符合安全规定。

近义词: complies, adheres, obeys, follows, agrees
反义词: deviates, differs, disagrees, violates

词性: verb
