leave someone in the lurch: /liːv ˈsʌmwʌn ɪn ðə lɜːrtʃ/ | idiom | 置某人于困境，抛弃某人 | To abandon someone in a difficult situation; to fail to help someone when they need you. |  He left me in the lurch when he didn't show up to help me move. 他没有出现帮我搬家，把我给坑了。| 近义词: abandon, desert, forsake | 反义词: support, assist | idiom

leave someone in the lurch: /liːv ˈsʌmwʌn ɪn ðə lɜːrtʃ/ | idiom | 置某人于困境，抛弃某人 | My so-called best friend left me in the lurch when I needed her most. 我所谓的最好的朋友在我最需要她的时候抛弃了我。| 近义词: strand, leave high and dry | 反义词: help, rescue | idiom
