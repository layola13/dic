geophysicists:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪsɪsts/
n.
地球物理学家
A scientist who studies the physics of the Earth, including its structure, composition, and physical properties.
Geophysicists use a variety of methods to study the Earth, including seismic surveys, gravity measurements, and magnetic field measurements.  地球物理学家运用各种方法研究地球，包括地震勘测、重力测量和磁场测量。
The geophysicists analyzed the seismic data to determine the location of the earthquake. 地球物理学家分析了地震数据以确定地震的位置。
Near Synonyms: earth scientists, geoscientists
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
