authenticating:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtɪŋ/
Verb
验证；鉴定
the present participle of authenticate
例句：The system is authenticating your username and password.  系统正在验证你的用户名和密码。

例句：He is authenticating the signature on the document. 他正在鉴定文件上的签名。

近义词：verifying, validating, confirming
反义词：falsifying, forging

词性：v.

词源：From authenticate + -ing.  Authenticate comes from Late Latin authenticāre "to give authority to," from Latin authenticus "authoritative" + -āre (forming verbs).
