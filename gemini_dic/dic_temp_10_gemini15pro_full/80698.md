gorgonian:/ɡɔːrˈɡəʊniən/| n. |柳珊瑚；海扇|A type of soft coral with a branching, fan-like, or tree-like structure, often brightly colored.|Divers marveled at the vibrant gorgonians swaying in the current. 潜水员惊叹于在水流中摇曳的鲜艳柳珊瑚。|近义词：sea fan, soft coral|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 hard coral 硬珊瑚作为对比)|词性:noun


gorgonian:/ɡɔːrˈɡoʊniən/| adj. |柳珊瑚的；海扇的|Relating to or characteristic of gorgonians.|The gorgonian gardens were teeming with life. 柳珊瑚花园里充满了生机。|近义词：sea fan, soft coral (adj.)|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词)|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Gorgoneus*, relating to the Gorgons, three monstrous sisters in Greek mythology, whose hair was made of snakes.  The perceived resemblance of the coral's branching structure to the snakes' hair led to the name.
