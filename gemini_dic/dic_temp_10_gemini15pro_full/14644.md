avouchable:/əˈvaʊtʃəbl/
n.|可证实的，可担保的|Able to be vouched for; verifiable.  Guaranteed. |The information he provided was avouchable by several witnesses.|近义词: verifiable, confirmable, attestable, provable |反义词: unverifiable, unprovable, questionable|词性:adjective

词源: From the verb "avouch" + "-able" suffix.  "Avouch" comes from Old French "avouer" meaning "to admit, confess, acknowledge," ultimately from Latin "advocare" meaning "to summon, call to one's aid."
