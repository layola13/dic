disorient:/dɪsˈɔːrient/ /dɪsˈɔːrɪɛnt/| vt. |使迷失方向，使不知所措|to cause (someone) to lose their sense of direction, place, or time.  To confuse.|The flashing lights disoriented me. 闪光灯使我迷失了方向。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, confound|Antonyms: orient, guide, direct|Verb

disorientation:/dɪsˌɔːriənˈteɪʃn/ /dɪsˌɔːrɪɛnˈteɪʃn/| n. |迷失方向，不知所措|the condition of having lost one's sense of direction.|She experienced disorientation after the long flight. 长途飞行后她感到迷失方向。|Synonyms: confusion, bewilderment|Antonyms: orientation, clarity|Noun
