nonadministratively:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪnɪstrətɪvli/
| adv. | 非行政地；不通过行政途径地 |  In a manner that is not related to or involved in the administration or management of an organization or government. | The issue was resolved nonadministratively through direct negotiation between the parties.  这个问题是通过双方直接谈判非行政地解决的。| Synonyms: informally, unofficially | Antonyms: administratively, officially | word type: adverb
