clustering:/ˈklʌstərɪŋ/ | n. | 聚类，群集 | The process of grouping similar objects or data points together.  | Example: Clustering analysis is used in marketing to segment customers.  聚类分析在市场营销中用于细分客户。

Example: The clustering of galaxies provides insights into the structure of the universe. 星系的聚集为了解宇宙的结构提供了见解。 | 近义词: grouping, aggregation, assembling | 反义词: separation, dispersal, division | noun
