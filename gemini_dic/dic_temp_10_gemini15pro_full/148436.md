overstimulating: /ˌoʊvərˈstɪmjuleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度刺激的；刺激过度的 | Providing too much stimulation; causing excessive excitement or irritation. | The flashing lights and loud music were overstimulating for the baby. 闪烁的灯光和嘈杂的音乐对婴儿来说刺激过度。|  The constant notifications from social media can be overstimulating. 社交媒体不断发来的通知可能会让人过度兴奋。 | 近义词: overwhelming, excessive, hyperstimulating | 反义词: calming, soothing, relaxing | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "stimulating" (present participle of "stimulate," from Latin "stimulare" meaning "to prick, goad, urge").
