imperially:/ɪmˈpɪəriəli/ | adv. | 以帝王的姿态，专横地，帝国地 | In a manner befitting an emperor; in a grand or ostentatious way; relating to an empire. | He surveyed his domain imperially, as if all he saw belonged to him. 他以帝王的姿态审视着他的领地，仿佛看到的一切都属于他。|  The building was imperially decorated with gold and marble. 这座建筑用黄金和大理石装饰得富丽堂皇。 | Synonyms: majestically, regally, grandly, pompously, domineeringly | Antonyms: humbly, modestly, democratically, submissively | adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imperialis, from Latin imperium (“command, power, empire”), from imperare (“to command”), from in- + parere (“to obey”).
