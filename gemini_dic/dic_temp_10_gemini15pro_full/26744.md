cadre:/ˈkɑːdrə/ (US), /ˈkɑːdə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 骨干，干部；核心小组|A small group of trained people who form the basic unit of a military, political, or business organization. |The party's cadre of young activists were instrumental in the campaign. 该党的年轻积极分子骨干在竞选活动中发挥了重要作用。

The company has a cadre of skilled engineers.  这家公司拥有一支熟练的工程师队伍。|近义词：core, nucleus, group, team|反义词:masses, rank and file|noun
