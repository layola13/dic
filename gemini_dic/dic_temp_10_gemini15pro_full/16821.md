basenet:/ˈbeɪsˌnɛt/| n. |基础网络，基准网络|A neural network architecture that is pre-trained on a large dataset and then fine-tuned for a specific task.  It serves as a starting point for transfer learning.|Example:  The researchers used a ResNet-50 basenet for their image classification task. 研究人员使用ResNet-50基础网络进行图像分类任务。

Example:  This basenet is trained on ImageNet and can be adapted for object detection.  这个基础网络在ImageNet上训练，可以用于目标检测。|近义词：base model, pre-trained model, foundation model|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) custom model, scratch model|词性:noun
