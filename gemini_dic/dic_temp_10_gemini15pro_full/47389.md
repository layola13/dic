deadened:/ˈdednd/ (US), /ˈdedənd/ (UK)| adj.| 减弱的，抑制的，沉闷的 |Reduced in intensity, force, or acuteness; muted or suppressed.  Also, made less sensitive or responsive.|The thick carpet deadened the sound of their footsteps. 厚厚的地毯减弱了他们脚步的声音。| synonyms: muffled, subdued, dulled, muted, softened | antonyms: amplified, heightened, intensified, sharpened | adjective

deadened:/ˈdednd/ (US), /ˈdedənd/ (UK)| v. |(使)减弱，(使)抑制，(使)沉闷|(past participle of *deaden*) To make less intense, strong, or sharp; to reduce the force or acuteness of something.|The drug deadened the pain in his leg.  这种药物减轻了他腿部的疼痛。| synonyms: muted, suppressed, dulled | antonyms: amplified, heightened, intensified | verb
