auditions:/ɔːˈdɪʃənz/
n.|试镜，试演，试唱|A short performance given by an actor, musician, dancer, etc., so that a director or conductor can decide if they are good enough to be in a play, concert, etc.|She went to several auditions before getting the part. 她参加了几次试镜才得到这个角色。|tryouts, hearings, tests|rejections, refusals|noun
