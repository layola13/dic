misdrew:/mɪsˈdruː/
verb
错误地画；画错；写错；理解错
drew incorrectly; represented incorrectly.
The artist misdrew the proportions of the human figure.  这位艺术家把人物的比例画错了。
misrepresented, distorted, misstated
