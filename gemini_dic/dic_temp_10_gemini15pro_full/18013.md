beggingly:/ˈbeɡɪŋli/| adv.|恳求地，乞求地|In a way that shows you are asking for something very seriously and hoping that the other person will agree.  It suggests humility and a strong desire to receive what is being asked for.|The child looked at her beggingly for another cookie.  孩子恳求地望着她，想要再一块饼干。|近义词：pleadingly, imploringly, supplicatingly|反义词：demandingly, arrogantly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From beg + -ing + -ly.  "Beg" comes from Middle English beggen, from Old French begard, meaning "mendicant friar."
