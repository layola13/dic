fondness:/ˈfɑːndnəs/ (US) /ˈfɒndnəs/ (UK)| n.|喜爱，钟爱；溺爱|Affection or liking for someone or something.|例句：She had a great fondness for her grandchildren. 她非常疼爱她的孙子孙女。

例句：He spoke of his fondness for the outdoors. 他谈到他对户外活动的喜爱。

近义词：affection, liking, love, attachment, tenderness, warmth, devotion, passion, predilection, preference

反义词：aversion, dislike, hatred, antipathy, indifference

词性: noun
