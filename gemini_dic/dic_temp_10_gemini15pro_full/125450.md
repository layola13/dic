multiplexing:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksɪŋ/| n.|多路复用，多路传输|The process of combining multiple signals into one signal over a shared medium, such as a single cable or radio frequency.  This allows for efficient use of bandwidth and resources.|Modern telecommunications rely heavily on multiplexing to transmit large amounts of data. 现代通信严重依赖多路复用技术来传输大量数据。|synonyms: multiple transmission, signal combining|antonyms: demultiplexing|noun
