nonconciliating: /ˌnɒnkənˈsɪlieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不妥协的，不让步的 | Unwilling to compromise or make concessions; inflexible. |  The nonconciliating attitude of the CEO made negotiations difficult.  首席执行官不让步的态度使得谈判变得困难。

The nonconciliating stance of the government prolonged the strike. 政府不妥协的立场延长了罢工。| 近义词：inflexible, unyielding, intransigent, uncompromising | 反义词：conciliatory, accommodating, flexible, yielding | 词性: adjective

词源: From non- (not) + conciliating (from conciliate, from Latin conciliare "to bring together, unite in friendship," from concilium "council").
