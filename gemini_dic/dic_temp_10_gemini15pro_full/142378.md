orthorhombic:/ˌɔːrθəˈrɒmbɪk/ | adj. |正交的，斜方的| Having three unequal axes intersecting at right angles.  A crystal system characterized by three mutually perpendicular axes of unequal lengths.|Barite crystallizes in the orthorhombic system. 重晶石在正交晶系中结晶。|近义词: rectangular, orthogonal |反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a crystal system)|词性: adjective
