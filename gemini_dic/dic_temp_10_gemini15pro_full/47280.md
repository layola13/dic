de-democratize:/diː.dɪˈmɒkrəˌtaɪz/ | v. | 去民主化，削弱民主 | To undermine or weaken democratic institutions, practices, or principles.  | The new laws are seen as an attempt to de-democratize the country. 这些新法律被认为是试图削弱国家的民主。 | The government's actions have de-democratized the electoral process, making it less fair and transparent. 政府的行为已经削弱了选举进程的民主性，使其不那么公平和透明。 | 近义词: undermine democracy, subvert democracy, weaken democracy | 反义词: democratize, strengthen democracy | 词性: 动词
