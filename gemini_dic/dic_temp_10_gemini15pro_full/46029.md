cybersquatting: /ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtɪŋ/ | n. |域名抢注，网络域名抢注|The act of registering a domain name that is the same as, or confusingly similar to, a well-known trademark, with the intent to profit from it. | Example: Cybersquatting is illegal in many countries.  域名抢注在许多国家都是非法的。

Example: He was accused of cybersquatting on the company's trademark. 他被指控抢注了该公司的商标域名。| 近义词: domain hijacking, domain name grabbing | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) legitimate domain registration | 词性: noun
