gavotte:/ɡəˈvɒt/ /ɡəˈvɑːt/| n.|加沃特舞；加沃特舞曲|A moderate tempo dance of French peasant origin, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries, in quadruple time with an upbeat of two crotchets and beginning on the third beat of the bar. Also: music for this dance.|The orchestra played a lively gavotte.|近义词：dance, minuet, bourrée|反义词:|noun
