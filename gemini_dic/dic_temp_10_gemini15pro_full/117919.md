melting-pot:/ˈmɛltɪŋ pɑːt/ | n. |大熔炉；多元文化融合的场所|A place where different peoples, styles, theories, etc., are mixed together.|The United States is often called a melting pot of cultures.|近义词:  crucible, blend, mix|反义词: segregation, division|词性: 名词

Etymology: From the process of melting metals together in a pot to create an alloy.  The metaphorical use for cultural mixing became prominent in the early 20th century.
