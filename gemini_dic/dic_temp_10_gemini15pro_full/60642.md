electee:/ɪˌlekˈtiː/
noun
当选人；被选中的人
a person who has been elected but has not yet taken office
The electee will begin his term in January.  当选人将于一月份开始任期。
Synonyms:  successful candidate, winner, victor
Antonyms:  loser, defeated candidate
n.
