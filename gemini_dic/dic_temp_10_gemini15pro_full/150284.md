panelation:/ˌpænəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |镶板; 嵌板; 分格;|The act or process of decorating with panels, or the panels themselves.  The division of something into panels. |The panelation of the wall added a touch of elegance to the room.  房间墙壁的镶板增添了一丝优雅。 |The intricate panelation of the ceiling was breathtaking. 天花板复杂的嵌板令人叹为观止。|synonyms: paneling, wainscoting, partitioning | antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider: smooth surface, plain wall) | noun
