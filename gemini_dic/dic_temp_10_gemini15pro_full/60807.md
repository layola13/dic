ETYMOLOGY->
From electro- + optical.  "Electro-" comes from "electricity", ultimately from Greek "elektron" (amber, as static electricity was first observed by rubbing amber). "Optical" comes from Late Latin "opticus", from Greek "optikos" (of or for sight), from "opsis" (sight).
