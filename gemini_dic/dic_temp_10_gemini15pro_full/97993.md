inoffensively:/ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪvli/| adv.|无冒犯地；不伤人地|Not causing any offence or harm.|She spoke inoffensively about the controversial topic. 她谈论这个有争议的话题时措辞谨慎，没有冒犯任何人。|He dressed inoffensively for the occasion. 他穿着得体，不失庄重。|近义词：harmlessly, politely, discreetly, tactfully|反义词：offensively, rudely, aggressively|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + offensive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Offensive" ultimately derives from the Latin "offendere" (to strike against, offend).
