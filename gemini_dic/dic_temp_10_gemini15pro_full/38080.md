composure:/kəmˈpəʊʒə(r)/ /kəmˈpoʊʒər/ | n. | 镇静；沉着；泰然自若 |The state of being calm and in control of oneself. |She maintained her composure despite the chaos around her. 她尽管周围一片混乱，却依然保持镇静。|  calmness, poise, self-control | agitation, anxiety, nervousness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *composĭtūra ("a putting together, arrangement"), from Latin compōnō ("put together, arrange"). Equivalent to compose +‎ -ure.
