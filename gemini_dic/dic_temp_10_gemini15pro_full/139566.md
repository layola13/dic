obumbrant:/əˈbʌmbrənt/ | adj. | 阴暗的；遮蔽的；使黯然的 | Overshadowing; darkening; making gloomy or dim. | The obumbrant clouds threatened rain. 阴沉的云预示着即将下雨。|  The setting sun cast an obumbrant glow over the mountains. 落日给群山蒙上了一层昏暗的光芒。 | 近义词: shadowy, dark, gloomy, dim, overcast | 反义词: bright, sunny, radiant, luminous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *obumbrans*, present participle of *obumbrare* (“to overshadow”), from *ob* (“over, against, toward”) + *umbra* (“shade, shadow”).
