myro:/ˈmaɪroʊ/
noun|Myro, 迈乐|A brand of robotics kits designed for educational purposes, often used in schools and universities.  The kits allow users to build and program robots using a variety of sensors and actuators.|Example: Students are using Myro robots to learn about programming. 学生们正在使用迈乐机器人学习编程。|Synonym: robotics kit, educational robot|Antonym: N/A|n.
