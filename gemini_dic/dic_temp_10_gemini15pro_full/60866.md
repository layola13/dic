The poem was elegiacal in tone, mourning the death of a close friend. 这首诗的基调是哀伤的，悼念一位挚友的去世。 | Synonyms: mournful, plaintive, melancholic, lamenting | Antonyms: joyful, celebratory, cheerful | word type: adjective
