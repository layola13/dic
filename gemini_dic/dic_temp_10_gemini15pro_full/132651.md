nondiscursively:/ˌnɑːndɪˈskɜːrsɪvli/
adverb
非论证地；非推论地；直觉地
Not proceeding by reasoning or argumentation; intuitively.
Example:  She understood the meaning nondiscursively, through a feeling of empathy. 她直觉地理解了意思，通过一种感同身受的感觉。

Example:  The artist expressed the emotion nondiscursively through color and form. 艺术家通过色彩和形式非论证地表达了这种情感。


Synonym: intuitively, instinctively, directly, immediately
Antonym: discursively, logically, rationally, analytically
词性: 副词
