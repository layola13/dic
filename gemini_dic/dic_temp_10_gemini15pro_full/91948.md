hypermiraculousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɪˈrækjələsnəsɪz/ | n. | 超神迹，极度神奇 |  The state or quality of being extremely miraculous; exceeding the ordinary or natural; supernatural to an extreme degree.  This word emphasizes an almost unbelievable level of miraculousness. |  The hypermiraculousnesses of her survival after such a devastating accident left everyone speechless.  她在那场毁灭性的意外后幸存下来，其极度神奇的程度让每个人都哑口无言。 | 近义词: extraordinariness, incredibility, wondrousness | 反义词: ordinariness, commonness | 词性: 名词

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek):  over, above, beyond. 表示“超越，过度”
"miraculous" (Latin miraculosus):  wonderful, marvelous.  来自 "miraculum" (wonder, marvel).
"-ness" (Old English -nes): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
