liane:/liˈɑːn/ | n. | 藤本植物; 藤蔓 | A woody climbing plant that roots in the ground and climbs on other plants or structures for support. |  The monkeys swung from liane to liane through the jungle canopy. 猴子在丛林树冠中从一根藤蔓荡到另一根藤蔓。|  climber, vine | tree, shrub | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *liane*, from Provençal *liano*, from Latin *ligare* "to bind."
