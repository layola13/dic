diagraphics:/ˌdaɪəˈɡræfɪks/ | n. |双字母; 二合字母;  |The study of digraphics involves understanding how two letters combine to represent a single sound. | Digraphics are important in English spelling, such as 'sh' in 'ship' or 'ch' in 'chin'. 双字母在英语拼写中很重要，例如“ship”中的“sh”或“chin”中的“ch”。| synonyms: digraphs | antonyms: N/A | noun
