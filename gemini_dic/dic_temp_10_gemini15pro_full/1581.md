achromatism: /əˈkroʊməˌtɪzəm/
n.
消色差，无色，全色盲
The absence of color or chromatic aberration.  The inability to perceive colors.
The telescope lens was corrected for achromatism. 这架望远镜的镜头经过了消色差校正。
The artist's achromatism was reflected in his stark black and white paintings. 这位艺术家的全色盲反映在他鲜明的黑白画作中。
近义词: colorlessness, monochromatism
反义词: chromaticism, polychromatism
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + chroma (color) + -ism (state or condition).  Ultimately from Greek *a- (not) + khroma (color).
