disfavorably:/dɪsˈfeɪvərəbli/
adverb
不利地；不适宜地；不友好地
in a way that shows disapproval or creates a bad impression
例句：The new policy was viewed disfavorably by the public. 这项新政策受到了公众的批评。

例句：The economic downturn affected the company disfavorably. 经济下滑对公司造成了不利影响。

近义词：unfavorably, adversely, negatively
反义词：favorably, positively, advantageously
词性：adverb
