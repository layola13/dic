gawn:/ɡɔːn/| n.| 凝视，注视；痴呆的凝视 |A foolish stare.  He stood there gawking at her.|例句：He stood there with a foolish gawn on his face. 他站在那儿，脸上带着愚蠢的凝视。

例句：She caught him gawning at her across the room. 她发现他在房间的另一头呆呆地盯着她。|近义词：stare, gaze, gape|反义词：glance|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ganen, *gaunen, from Old Norse gauna (“to gape, stare”).
