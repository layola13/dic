gi:/dʒiː/ | n. |  GI, (government issue) 军需品; 美国兵 |  Abbreviation for "government issue," referring to supplies or equipment provided to US soldiers.  Also commonly used to refer to a US soldier. | Example:  The GIs received their new uniforms.  这些美国士兵收到了他们的新制服。

Example: He was a GI during the Vietnam War. 他是越南战争期间的一名美国士兵。| Synonym: soldier, serviceman, trooper | Antonym: civilian | noun
