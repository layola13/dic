crouches:/ˈkraʊtʃɪz/| v.|蹲伏，蜷缩|Third-person singular present tense form of crouch.  是动词crouch的第三人称单数现在式形式，表示“蹲下，蜷缩”。|The cat crouches in the grass, watching the birds. 猫蹲在草丛里，注视着鸟儿。|近义词：stoops, bends, squats|反义词:stands, rises|verb

crouch:/kraʊtʃ/| v. n.|蹲伏，蜷缩；蹲伏的姿势|To stoop or bend low, especially with the knees bent.|He crouched down to tie his shoelaces. 他蹲下来系鞋带。|The lion adopted a crouch before springing on its prey. 狮子在扑向猎物之前摆出了蹲伏的姿势。|近义词：stoop, bend, squat|反义词:stand, rise|verb, noun
