electoral:/ɪˈlɛktərəl/| adj.|选举的，与选举有关的|Relating to elections or electors.|The electoral college is a system used in the United States to elect the president. 选举团是美国用来选举总统的制度。|The new electoral law requires voters to show identification. 新的选举法要求选民出示身份证。|近义词：voting, election, polling|反义词: none|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *ēlectorālis, from Latin ēlector ("voter"). Ultimately from Latin ēligere ("to choose out").
