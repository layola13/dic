paraph:/ˈpærəfreɪz/ | v. n. |  释义; 改述; 解释;  | To express something using different words, especially in order to make it easier to understand. | She paraphrased the poem for the class. 她为全班同学解释了这首诗。| synonyms: reword, rephrase, restate, put in other words, summarize, epitomize, encapsulate, abridge, condense | antonyms: quote, copy, plagiarize | verb

paraph:/ˈpærəfreɪz/ | n. | 释义; 改述; 解释; | A rewording of something written or spoken by someone else. |  The teacher asked for a paraphrase of the paragraph. 老师要求对这段文字进行释义。| synonyms: rewording, rephrasing, restating, version, rendition, interpretation | antonyms: quotation, citation | noun
