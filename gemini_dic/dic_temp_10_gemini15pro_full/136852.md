nonsimultaneous:/ˌnɒnsaɪməlˈteɪniəs/ /ˌnɑːnsaɪməlˈteɪniəs/
adjective
不同时的；非同时发生的
not happening or existing at the same time.
Example: The two events were nonsimultaneous; one occurred before the other.  这两个事件不是同时发生的；一个发生在另一个之前。

Example: The nonsimultaneous release of the movies in different countries led to confusion about the plot.  这些电影在不同国家不同时上映导致了剧情的混乱。

synonym: asynchronous, nonconcurrent, staggered
antonym: simultaneous, concurrent, synchronous
adjective
