monitorship:/ˈmɒnɪtəʃɪp/ (US) /ˈmɒnɪtəʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|监督；监视；指导|The act or process of monitoring something or someone, or the position of a monitor.  The monitorship of the peace process is crucial.|The UN established a monitorship to oversee the elections. 联合国设立了一个监督机构来监督选举。|synonyms: supervision, oversight, observation, surveillance|antonyms: neglect, disregard|noun
