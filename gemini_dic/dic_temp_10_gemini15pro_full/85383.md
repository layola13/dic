harshnesses:/ˈhɑːrʃnɪsɪz/
noun
严酷；粗糙；刺耳；残酷
The harshnesses of winter can be difficult to bear. 冬天的严酷难以忍受。
The harshnesses of the terrain made travel difficult.  地势的崎岖使得旅行困难。
The harshnesses of his words hurt her deeply. 他言语的刻薄深深地伤害了她。
The harshnesses of life in the refugee camp were shocking.  难民营生活的艰辛令人震惊。
The harshnesses of the economic climate led to many business closures.  严峻的经济形势导致许多企业倒闭。

Synonym: severity, rigour, austerity, cruelty, roughness
Antonym: gentleness, kindness, mildness, softness, leniency
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
