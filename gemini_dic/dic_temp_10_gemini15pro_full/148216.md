overshrinks:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋks/
verb
过度缩水
causes something to shrink excessively.
The dry cleaner overshrunk my favorite sweater.  干洗店把我最喜欢的毛衣洗缩水了。
My wool socks overshrank in the hot wash. 我的羊毛袜在热水里洗缩水了。
近义词: shrinks excessively, shrinks too much
反义词: stretches, expands
词性: verb
