orpheline:/ˈɔːrfəlɪn/ | n. |孤儿（尤指女孩）; 孤行(印刷); |A young girl without parents.  A single, short line at the beginning or end of a paragraph, separated from the rest of the paragraph. |例句：The war left many children as orphans. 战争使许多孩子成了孤儿。

例句：The typesetter tried to avoid orphans in the final layout.  排字员试图避免在最终排版中出现孤行。|近义词：orphan (更通用), waif, stray |反义词: N/A (孤儿没有确切的反义词) |词性: 名词
