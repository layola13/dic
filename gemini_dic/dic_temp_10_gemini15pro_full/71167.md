fissiparity:/fɪsɪˈpærɪti/ | n. |裂殖生殖；分裂生殖| Reproduction by spontaneous division of the body into two or more new organisms. |例句：Amoebas reproduce by fissiparity. 变形虫通过裂殖生殖繁殖。

例句：Fissiparity is a common form of asexual reproduction in single-celled organisms. 裂殖生殖是单细胞生物常见的无性繁殖形式。|近义词：binary fission, schizogony|反义词: sexual reproduction|词性:noun
