albicore: /ˈælbɪkɔːr/ (US), /ˈælbɪkɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |长鳍金枪鱼|A large, fast-swimming tuna (Thunnus alalunga) found in warm and temperate seas, commercially valued as a food fish.|We caught a huge albicore on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕获了一条巨大的长鳍金枪鱼。|近义词：tuna, longfin tuna, albacore|反义词: None|词性: noun

albicore: /ˈælbɪkɔːr/ (US), /ˈælbɪkɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |白色|White or whitish; pale. (Archaic/Rare usage)|The ancient text described a mythical beast with albicore fur. 古老的文献描述了一种长着白色皮毛的神秘野兽。|近义词：white, pale, light|反义词: dark, black|词性: adjective (rare)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *albacora* (now *albacora*), from Arabic *al-bakūra* meaning "the young camel", then "tunny-fish".  The Arabic term likely refers to the swiftness of the fish, like a young camel.
