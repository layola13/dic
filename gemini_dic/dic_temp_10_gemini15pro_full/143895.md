outpower:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊər/
verb
胜过；在力量上超过
to defeat someone by having more power than they do
例句：The bigger army easily outpowered the smaller one.  更大的军队轻易地击败了较小的军队。

例句：She outpowered her opponent in the final round of the competition.  她在比赛的最后一轮战胜了她的对手。

近义词：overpower, defeat, conquer, subdue, vanquish
反义词：lose to, surrender to, be defeated by
词性：v.
