backfill:/ˈbækˌfɪl/| n. v.|回填，回填土，补充人员|The process of refilling an excavated area, such as a trench or foundation, with excavated material or other suitable material.  Also refers to the material used for this purpose or the act of replacing a vacant position.|They had to backfill the hole after laying the pipe.  他们在铺设管道后必须回填洞穴。|Synonyms: refill, fill in, replace, replenish|Antonyms: excavate, dig, empty|noun
