lector:/ˈlɛktər/ | n. | 讲师，阅读者 | A person who gives lectures, especially in a university or college.  A person who reads, especially one who reads aloud or for an audience. | The lector delivered a fascinating lecture on the history of art.  这位讲师做了一个关于艺术史的精彩讲座。| synonyms: lecturer, reader, instructor, teacher | antonyms: student, pupil | noun

lector:/ˈlɛktər/ | n. | (尤指教堂中诵读经文或祈祷文的) 朗读者 | A person who reads aloud during a religious service, especially one who reads lessons or prayers. | The lector read a passage from the Gospel of John. 朗读者读了一段约翰福音。| synonyms: reader, minister, clergyman | antonyms: listener, congregation | noun
