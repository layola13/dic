overfavourable:/ˌoʊvərˈfeɪvərəbl/| adj.|过分有利的，过于优惠的|Too helpful or advantageous; more favourable than is fair or reasonable.|The terms of the loan were overfavourable to the borrower.  这笔贷款的条款对借款人过于优惠。|The report painted an overfavourable picture of the company's finances. 这份报告把公司的财务状况描绘得过于乐观。|近义词: advantageous, beneficial, preferential|反义词: unfavourable, disadvantageous, detrimental|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "favourable" (from Latin "favorabilis," meaning "pleasing, propitious")
