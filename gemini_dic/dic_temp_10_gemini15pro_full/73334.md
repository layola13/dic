foree:/ˈfɔːrsi/ | n. | 力量，暴力，强制力 | Power or strength; violence or constraint; compulsion. | The foree of the storm uprooted several trees.  暴风雨的力量把几棵树连根拔起。|  近义词：force, power, strength, compulsion, coercion | 反义词: weakness, impotence | 词性: 名词

foree:/ˈfɔːrsi/ | n. | 被迫或受约束的人（常指军队、船员） | A body of persons subject to some force or constraint, especially a body of troops or a ship's company. | The naval foree was dispatched to the troubled waters. 海军部队被派往了这片动荡的水域。| 近义词：troops, forces, company, crew | 反义词: civilians | 词性: 名词

词源：
