famous: /ˈfeɪməs/ | adj. | 著名的，有名的 | Known by many people; celebrated. |  She is a famous actress. 她是一位著名的女演员。|  He is famous for his paintings. 他以他的画作而闻名。 | 近义词: renowned, celebrated, well-known, prominent, distinguished | 反义词: unknown, obscure, infamous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *famosus* "talked of, notorious," from *fama* "fame, report."

famous as: 作为...而闻名  She is famous as a novelist. 她作为小说家而闻名。
become famous: 成名 He became famous overnight. 他一夜成名。
rise to fame: 声名鹊起 She rose to fame in her early twenties. 她二十出头就声名鹊起。
world-famous: 世界闻名的 The Eiffel Tower is a world-famous landmark. 埃菲尔铁塔是世界闻名的地标。
