niggling:/ˈnɪɡlɪŋ/| adj.|琐碎恼人的；烦扰的；使人不得安宁的|Causing slight but persistent annoyance, discomfort, or anxiety.|That niggling doubt just wouldn't go away. 那挥之不去的疑虑就是不肯消失。|近义词：annoying, irritating, bothersome, nagging|反义词：soothing, calming, comforting|词性:形容词

niggle:/ˈnɪɡl/| v. n.|1.(v.)为小事烦恼；吹毛求疵；不断地烦扰；2.(n.)小烦恼；小问题|1.(v.)To worry or annoy somebody slightly and persistently; 2.(n.)A slight but persistent worry, pain, or feeling of discomfort.|1.(v.)He keeps niggling me about the cost. 他一直为费用和我斤斤计较。 2.(n.)I had a niggle in my back. 我背部有点不舒服。|近义词：(v.)fuss, carp, quibble; (n.)worry, concern|反义词：(v.)ignore, overlook; (n.)relief, comfort|词性:动词，名词
