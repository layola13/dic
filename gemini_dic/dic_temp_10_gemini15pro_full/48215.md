decongregating: /diːkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
分散，疏散人群
The act of dispersing or separating a group of people, especially to reduce crowding or enforce social distancing.
The governor ordered the decongregating of large gatherings to slow the spread of the virus. 州长下令驱散大型集会以减缓病毒传播。
Police are decongregating the protesters in the park. 警察正在驱散公园里的抗议者。
dispersing, scattering, separating
assembling, gathering, congregating
