oscitant: /ˈɒsɪtənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːsɪtənt/ (US) | adj. | 打哈欠的，困倦的，懒散的 | Yawning, sleepy, sluggish, showing lack of interest; inattentive. |  He gave an oscitant wave and slumped back in his chair. 他打了个哈欠，懒洋洋地瘫回椅子上。|  Synonyms: drowsy, lethargic, listless, apathetic, indolent | Antonyms: alert, energetic, vigorous, attentive, enthusiastic | adjective

Etymology: From Latin *oscitans*, present participle of *oscitare* "to yawn," frequentative of *os* "mouth."
