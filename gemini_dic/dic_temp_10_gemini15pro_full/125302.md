multigenerous:/ˌmʌltiˈdʒɛnərəs/
adjective
多代同堂的；跨代的
Spanning or involving multiple generations, especially within a family or household.
例句：Multigenerous households are becoming increasingly common as housing costs rise.  多代同堂的家庭随着住房成本的上升变得越来越普遍。

例句：The family reunion was a multigenerous affair, with attendees ranging from toddlers to great-grandparents. 家庭聚会是一个跨代的盛事，从蹒跚学步的孩子到曾祖父母都有参加。
近义词：intergenerational, cross-generational
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective


Etymology:
"multi-" (prefix): from Latin "multus" meaning "many"
"-generous": related to "generation" from Latin "generatio" meaning "birth, production, race, descent"
