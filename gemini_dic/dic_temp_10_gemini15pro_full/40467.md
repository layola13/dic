contributable:/kənˈtrɪbjutəbəl/ | adj. | 可归因的，可归功于的，促成的 |  Capable of being attributed to a specific cause or factor; tending to contribute to a result. | The rise in crime is attributable to several factors, including poverty and unemployment.  犯罪率的上升可归因于多种因素，包括贫困和失业。|  ascribable, imputable, attributable | negligible,无关紧要的 | adjective

contribute:/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/ | v. | 贡献，捐献，投稿 | To give something, especially money, in order to help achieve or provide something. |  He contributed a large sum of money to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。 | donate, give, supply | withhold, withdraw | verb

contribution:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃn/ | n. | 贡献，捐款，稿件 | Something that you contribute or do to help produce or achieve something together with other people, or to help make something successful. | Her contribution to the project was invaluable. 她对这个项目的贡献是无价的。 | donation, gift, offering | deduction, subtraction | noun
