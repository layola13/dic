heterodox:/ˈhɛtərəˌdɑːks/| adj.|异端的，非正统的|Holding opinions that differ from established beliefs, especially in religion or philosophy.|例句：His heterodox views on economics often sparked controversy. 他非正统的经济学观点经常引发争议。

例句：The artist's heterodox approach to painting challenged traditional aesthetics.  这位艺术家非正统的绘画方法挑战了传统的审美观。

近义词：unorthodox, unconventional, dissenting, heretical, iconoclastic|反义词：orthodox, conventional, traditional, conformist|词性：形容词
