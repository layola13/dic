owl-like:/ˈaʊlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像猫头鹰的; 似猫头鹰的
Resembling an owl in appearance or behavior.  Having characteristics typical of an owl, such as large eyes, a nocturnal habit, or a silent flight.
The child, with her large, round eyes, had an owl-like appearance.  这个孩子有着大而圆的眼睛，看起来像猫头鹰。
The creature swooped down with an owl-like silence.  这个生物像猫头鹰一样悄无声息地俯冲下来。
近义词:owlish, nocturnal, predatory
反义词:diurnal, noisy
