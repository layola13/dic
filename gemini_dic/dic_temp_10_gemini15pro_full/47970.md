decil:/ˈdesɪl/| n. |十分位数|One of ten equal groups into which a population can be divided according to the distribution of values of a particular variable.|The income of the third decil increased by 5%. 第三十分位数的收入增加了5%。|近义词：quantile|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
