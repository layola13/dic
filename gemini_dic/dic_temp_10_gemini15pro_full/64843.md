espian:/ɛˈspiən/ | n. | 间谍，密探 | A person who secretly collects and reports information about the activities of another country or organization. |  例句：The espian was caught transmitting secrets to the enemy.  这个间谍在向敌人传递机密时被抓获。

例句：She worked as an espian for the government during the war.  战争期间她为政府做间谍。| 近义词：spy, secret agent, undercover agent | 反义词: loyalist, patriot | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French espie, from espier "to spy," from Frankish *spehon "to look out, examine," from Proto-Germanic *spehon, from PIE root *spek- "to observe."
