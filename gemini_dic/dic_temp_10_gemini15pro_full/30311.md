censers:/ˈsensərz/
n.
香炉
A container in which incense is burned, typically during religious ceremonies.
The priest swung the censers, filling the air with the scent of frankincense.  牧师摇晃着香炉，空气中弥漫着乳香的气味。
近义词: incensories, thuribles
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Could consider "air freshener" as an antonym for its function in a modern context, though not a true opposite.)
词性: Noun
