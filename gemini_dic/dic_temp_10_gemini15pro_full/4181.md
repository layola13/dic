airdroppable:/ˈɛərdɹɒpəbəl/ (US) /ˈeədrɒpəbl/ (UK)
adjective
可空投的
Suitable for being delivered by airdrop.
例句：The supplies were packaged in airdroppable containers.  这些物资被打包成可空投的集装箱。

例句：The humanitarian aid is airdroppable to remote areas. 人道主义援助可以空投到偏远地区。

近义词：parachutable
反义词: non-airdroppable (非正式)

词性: adjective
