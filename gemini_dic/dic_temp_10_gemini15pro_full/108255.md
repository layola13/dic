launcher:/ˈlɔːntʃər (UK), /ˈlɑːntʃər (US)| n.|发射器，启动器|A device used to launch something, such as a rocket, missile, or grenade.  A program that starts other programs.|例句：The rocket launcher was aimed at the target. 火箭发射器瞄准了目标。

例句：I clicked on the application launcher to open the program. 我点击了应用程序启动器来打开程序。|近义词：starter, trigger, catapult, activator|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

launcher:/ˈlɔːntʃər (UK), /ˈlɑːntʃər (US)| n.|发射装置，发射台|A structure or platform from which something is launched, such as a rocket or spacecraft.|例句：The satellite was launched from the Kennedy Space Center launcher. 卫星是从肯尼迪航天中心发射台发射的。
