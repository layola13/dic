makings:/ˈmeɪkɪŋz/
noun
素质，材料；成功的潜质
the qualities needed for something; the ingredients
He has the makings of a great leader. 他具备成为伟大领袖的素质。
potential, qualities, attributes
shortcomings, flaws
n.
