neurotoxins:/ˈnjʊərəʊˌtɒksɪnz/ (UK) /ˈnʊroʊˌtɑːksɪnz/ (US)| n.|神经毒素|Poisons that act on the nervous system, disrupting or destroying nerve function.|Example 1:  Some snakes produce potent neurotoxins that can paralyze their prey. 一些蛇会产生强效的神经毒素，可以麻痹它们的猎物。

Example 2:  Botulinum toxin, a neurotoxin produced by bacteria, is used in small doses in cosmetic procedures. 肉毒素，一种由细菌产生的神经毒素，在美容手术中少量使用。|近义词：nerve poisons, neuropoisons|反义词: neurotrophic factors (promote nerve growth)|词性:noun
