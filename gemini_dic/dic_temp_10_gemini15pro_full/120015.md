millinet:/ˌmɪlɪˈnɛt/ | n. | 米利奈特（一种细密的棉织物） | A fine, plain-weave cotton fabric, typically bleached or unbleached, used for clothing, bed linen, and bookbinding. |  例句：She wore a dress made of soft millinet. 她穿着一件柔软的米利奈特连衣裙。

例句：Millinet was a popular fabric for summer dresses in the 19th century.  米利奈特是19世纪夏季连衣裙的流行面料。

近义词：muslin, batiste, voile | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑粗糙的布料) canvas, denim | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mille*, thousand + *-inet*, diminutive suffix.  Referencing the fine threads used in the fabric.
