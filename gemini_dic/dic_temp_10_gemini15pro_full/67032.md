She has explained why she was late. 她已经解释了为什么迟到。

PHRASE->
explain away:  搪塞；把…解释过去
explain oneself:  为自己辩解；解释自己的行为
explain to: 向…解释
explain something to someone: 向某人解释某事
explain it all/everything: 解释一切


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esplaner (Modern French expliquer), from Latin explanare “to make level, flatten, spread out, explain,” from ex- “out” + planare “to make plain, level,” from planus “flat, level.”

注意事项:
美式和英式发音相同 /ɪkˈspleɪnz/
