overcleaning:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːnɪŋ/
noun
过度清洁
The act of cleaning something too much, often to the point of damaging it or removing beneficial elements.
Excessive cleaning can strip the skin of its natural oils. 过度清洁会剥夺皮肤的天然油脂。
over-sanitizing, over-washing
undercleaning
n.
