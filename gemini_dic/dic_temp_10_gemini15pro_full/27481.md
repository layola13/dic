campanologist:/ˌkæmpəˈnɒlədʒɪst/
n.
敲钟人；钟学家
A person who studies or practices the art of bell-ringing.
The campanologist meticulously adjusted the ropes for the perfect chime.  这位钟学家一丝不苟地调整绳索，以达到完美的钟声。
Synonyms: bell-ringer
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-bell-ringer")
Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *campanologia, from campana (“bell”) + -logia (“study of”).  Ultimately from Latin campana (“bell of Campania”), referring to the region where bells were first made.
