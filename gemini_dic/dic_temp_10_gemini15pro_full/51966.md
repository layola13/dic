diacoustic:/ˌdaɪəˈkuːstɪk/
adjective
双耳声的，透过两耳听到的
Relating to the difference in sound intensity between the two ears.  Pertaining to the use of two ears.
Example: Diacoustic hearing allows us to locate the source of a sound. 双耳听觉使我们能够定位声源。

Example: The diacoustic effect is crucial for spatial awareness. 双耳效应对于空间感知至关重要。

近义词: binaural, stereophonic
反义词: monaural
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dia- (Greek: through, across) + acoustic (Greek: akoustikos, relating to hearing).
