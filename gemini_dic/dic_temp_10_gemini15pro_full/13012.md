astounded: /əˈstaʊndɪd/ | adj. | 震惊的，惊骇的 | Feeling or showing great surprise or wonder; amazed. |  I was astounded by the news of his sudden death. 我被他突然去世的消息震惊了。

| The beauty of the Grand Canyon astounded me. 大峡谷的美丽令我惊叹。

| 近义词: amazed, astonished, shocked, surprised, stunned, dumbfounded | 反义词: unsurprised, indifferent, unimpressed | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Middle English *astoned, *astoundid, past participle of astounen, astounden (“to stun, daze, confound”), from Anglo-Norman estoner (“to stun, daze”), from Old French estoner (“to stun, daze, confound”), from Vulgar Latin *extonare (“to thunder at, stun with thunder”), from Latin ex (“out of, from”) + tonare (“to thunder”).
