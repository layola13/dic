homelesses:/ˈhoʊmləsɪz/ | n. | 无家可归的人（复数，罕用）|  A plural form of "homeless," referring to people who lack permanent housing.  This form is considered non-standard and rarely used. The singular "homeless" or the phrase "homeless people" is generally preferred. |  (Rarely used) The homelesses gathered around the fire for warmth.  无家可归的人们聚集在火堆旁取暖。 |  homeless people, the homeless, people without homes | housed, sheltered | noun

homeless:/ˈhoʊmləs/ | adj. | 无家可归的 | Without a home. | The homeless man slept on a park bench.  这个无家可归的男人睡在公园的长凳上。 The shelter provides food and clothing for the homeless. 这家收容所为无家可归的人提供食物和衣服。 | destitute, vagrant, down-and-out | housed, sheltered | adjective
