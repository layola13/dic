condemningly:/kənˈdemɪŋli/
adverb
谴责地，批判地
in a way that strongly disapproves of something or someone.
He looked at her condemningly. 他谴责地看着她。
Synonyms: disapprovingly, critically, censoriously, reproachfully
Antonyms: approvingly, supportively, commendably
词性: 副词
