barbary:/ˈbɑːrbəri/ | n. | 巴巴里 |  A region of North Africa, historically comprising the coastal regions from Egypt to the Atlantic Ocean.  It was inhabited by the Berbers, and the name ultimately derives from that term. | The Barbary pirates operated out of the ports of the Barbary Coast.  巴巴里海盗在巴巴里海岸的港口活动。 |  North Africa, Maghreb | None | noun
Barbary:/ˈbɑːrbəri/ | adj. | 巴巴里的；巴巴利种的 | Relating to Barbary or its inhabitants. |  Barbary sheep are adapted to the harsh desert climate.  巴巴里绵羊适应了恶劣的沙漠气候。 | North African, Maghreb | None | adjective
