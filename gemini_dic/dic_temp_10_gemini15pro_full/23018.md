boulangism:/ˌbuːlɑ̃ˈʒɪzəm/
n.
布朗热主义（19世纪80年代法国的政治运动）
A late 19th-century French political movement characterized by extreme nationalism, anti-Semitism, and calls for social and economic reforms, led by General Georges Boulanger.
The rise of boulangism in the late 1880s threatened the stability of the Third Republic.  布朗热主义在19世纪80年代后期的兴起威胁到第三共和国的稳定。
Boulangism, while popular for a time, ultimately failed to overthrow the government. 布朗热主义虽然一度很受欢迎，但最终未能推翻政府。
Synonyms: nationalism, populism, chauvinism
Antonyms: internationalism, globalism
