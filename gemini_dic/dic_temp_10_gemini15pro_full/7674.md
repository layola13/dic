anodizer:/ˈænəˌdaɪzər/| n.|阳极氧化器，阳极氧化处理装置|A device or apparatus used for anodizing metals, typically aluminum, to create a protective oxide layer.|The company invested in a new anodizer to improve the durability of their aluminum products. 这家公司投资了一台新的阳极氧化器，以提高其铝制品的耐用性。|近义词:anodizing machine, anodizing equipment|反义词: N/A|noun

anodize:/ˈænəˌdaɪz/| vt.|阳极氧化，阳极化处理|To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process.|The aluminum parts were anodized to prevent corrosion.  这些铝制零件经过阳极氧化处理以防止腐蚀。|近义词:oxidize, plate|反义词: N/A|verb
