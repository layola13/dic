aristocraticalness:/əˌrɪstəˈkrætɪkəlnəs/| n.|贵族气质，贵族气派|The quality of being aristocratical; aristocratic character or conduct.|Her aristocraticalness was evident in her elegant manners and refined taste. 她的贵族气质在她优雅的举止和 refined 的品味中显露无遗。|Synonyms: nobility, refinement, elegance, grandeur, hauteur|Antonyms: commonness, vulgarity, coarseness, crudeness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aristocratical + -ness.  Aristocratical from aristocrat + -ical. Aristocrat from French aristocrate, from Greek aristokratia "rule of the best," from aristos "best" + -kratia "power, rule."  -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
