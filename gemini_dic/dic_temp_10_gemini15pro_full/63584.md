envisage: /ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ (UK) /ɛnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ (US) | v. | 设想，展望 | To form a mental picture of something, especially something that may happen or be possible in the future. |  The architect envisaged a magnificent domed structure.  建筑师设想了一个宏伟的圆顶结构。 | synonyms: conceive, imagine, visualize, contemplate, foresee | antonyms: disregard, ignore | verb
