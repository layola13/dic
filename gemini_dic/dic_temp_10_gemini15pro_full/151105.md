parasite:/ˈpærəsaɪt/ | n. | 寄生虫；寄生生物；食客；靠他人为生的人 | An organism that lives on or in a host organism and gets its food from or at the expense of its host.  A person who habitually relies on or exploits others. | Example 1: The tapeworm is a parasite that lives in the intestines of animals.  绦虫是一种寄生在动物肠道内的寄生虫。

Example 2: He's a parasite, always living off other people's generosity. 他是个寄生虫，总是靠别人的慷慨为生。 | 近义词：freeloader, leech, sponger | 反义词：host, symbiont | 词性: noun
