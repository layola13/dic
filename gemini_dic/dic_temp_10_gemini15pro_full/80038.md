go-west:/ˌɡoʊ ˈwɛst/| vi.| 向西走；衰落；死亡；|To travel or move towards the west; to decline or deteriorate; to die. |例句：Many pioneers went west in search of gold. 许多拓荒者向西寻找黄金。

例句：His career went west after the scandal.  丑闻之后，他的事业一落千丈。|近义词：decline, deteriorate, perish, die, expire |反义词:improve, flourish, thrive|verb

go-west:/ˌɡoʊ ˈwɛst/| n.| 西部；衰落；死亡；|The western region; a decline; death. |例句：The go-west of the Roman Empire was a slow process. 罗马帝国的衰落是一个缓慢的过程。

例句：His sudden go-west shocked everyone. 他的突然离世震惊了所有人。|近义词：decline, demise, fall, decay |反义词:rise, growth, ascent|noun
