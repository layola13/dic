gambroon:/ɡæmˈbruːn/
n.
暗褐色, 棕灰色, 甘布隆布料, 一种波斯猫
a dark brownish gray color; a twilled fabric of wool or wool and cotton, used for dresses, curtains, etc.; a breed of long-haired domestic cat, believed to have originated in Persia.
The curtains were made of a heavy gambroon. 窗帘是用厚重的甘布隆布料做的。
近义词:gray, brown, drab, dun, taupe
反义词:bright, colorful
词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *gambrun*, altered (by influence of *brun* "brown") from *cambroun*, from *Cambrai* (modern Cambrai, France), where the fabric was originally made.
