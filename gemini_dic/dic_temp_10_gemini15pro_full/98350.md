insipid:/ɪnˈsɪpɪd/| adj. |平淡无味的；乏味的；枯燥的|Lacking flavor or zest; dull. |The soup was insipid and needed more seasoning. 这汤淡而无味，需要加些调料。|Synonyms: bland, dull, vapid, flat, lifeless|Antonyms:  zestful, piquant, exciting, vibrant, flavorful|adjective
