equatorwards:/ɪˈkweɪtə(r)wədz/
adv.
向赤道
towards the equator
例句：Birds migrate equatorwards in the winter. 鸟类在冬天向赤道迁移。

例句：The warm currents flow equatorwards. 暖流向赤道流动。
近义词：southwards (在北半球), northwards (在南半球)
反义词：polewards
词性：副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From equator + -wards.  "-wards" is a suffix indicating direction.  "Equator" comes from Late Middle English, from Latin *aequator (diei et noctis) ‘equalizer (of day and night),’ from aequare ‘make equal.’
