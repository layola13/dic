nontranscriptive:/ˌnɒntrænˈskrɪptɪv/ | adj. | 非转录的；非抄写的 | Not involving or based on transcription; not pertaining to or resulting from transcription.  A nontranscriptive approach to language learning focuses on spoken communication rather than written texts. |  The teacher used a nontranscriptive approach, emphasizing conversational skills. 老师采用了一种非转录式教学方法，强调会话技能。 |  non-recording, oral, unwritten | transcriptive, written, recorded | adjective
