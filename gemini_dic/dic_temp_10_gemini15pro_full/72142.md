floodplain:/ˈflʌdpleɪn/ | n. | 洪泛区，河漫滩 | An area of low-lying ground adjacent to a river, formed mainly of river sediments and subject to flooding. | The houses built on the floodplain were severely damaged during the hurricane.  洪水期间，建在洪泛区的房屋遭到严重破坏。 |
floodplain:/ˈflʌdpleɪn/ | n. | 洪泛平原 |  A flat or nearly flat land area along a river or stream that is naturally subject to periodic flooding. |  The floodplain provided fertile soil for agriculture. 洪泛平原为农业提供了肥沃的土壤。 |
floodplain:/ˈflʌdpleɪn/ | n. | 泛滥平原 | The area bordering a river channel that has the potential to flood. |  The government restricted development on the floodplain. 政府限制在泛滥平原上的开发。 |

ETYMOLOGY->
From *flood* (n.) + *plain* (n.).  The word "flood" comes from Old English *flōd*, meaning "a flowing, stream, flood, mass of water."  The word "plain" comes from Old French *plain*, from Latin *planus*, meaning "flat, level, even, plain."
