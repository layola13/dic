outbitched:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtʃt/
verb
胜过，比...更泼妇 (informal)
To surpass someone in malicious or spiteful behavior, typically used to describe women.
She outbitched her rival in the boardroom meeting, securing the promotion for herself. 她比她在董事会议上的对手更泼妇，为自己争取到了晋升。
He felt completely outbitched by his ex-wife during the custody battle. 他觉得在监护权争夺战中被前妻彻底打败了。
synonyms: outmaneuvered, outsmarted, defeated, triumphed over
antonyms: defeated, lost to
词性: 动词
