antiexpressionistic:/ˌæntiɪkˈspreʃənɪstɪk/
adjective
反表现主义的
Opposed to or rejecting expressionism, an artistic style that emphasizes the artist's subjective feelings and emotions rather than objective reality.
Her antiexpressionistic paintings focused on precise lines and geometric forms, a stark contrast to the emotionally charged works of her contemporaries.
近义词:formalist, classical, traditional
反义词:expressionistic
词性:adjective
