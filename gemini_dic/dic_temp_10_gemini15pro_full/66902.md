expedition:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃən/| n. |远征；探险；考察；迅速|A journey undertaken by a group of people with a particular purpose, especially that of exploration, research, or war. |例句：The expedition to the South Pole was a perilous undertaking.  去南极的探险是一项危险的任务。

例句：She joined an expedition to explore the Amazon rainforest. 她参加了一次探索亚马逊雨林的探险活动。|近义词：journey, voyage, quest, adventure, trip|反义词：stay, inaction|词性:noun
