ambivalence:/æmˈbɪvələns/ | n. |矛盾心理，矛盾情绪|The state of having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something or someone.|She felt a deep ambivalence towards him, attracted to his charisma yet repelled by his ruthlessness. 她对他有着深深的矛盾心理，被他的人格魅力所吸引，却又被他冷酷无情所排斥。|近义词：uncertainty, doubt, hesitation, indecision|反义词：certainty, conviction, decisiveness|词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin ambi- "both" + valentia "strength"
