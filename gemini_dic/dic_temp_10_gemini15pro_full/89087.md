holsteins:/ˈhoʊlˌstaɪnz/ | n. | 荷斯坦牛（复数）| Holsteins are a breed of dairy cattle known for their high milk production.  They are black and white and originate from the Netherlands and northern Germany. | The farmer milks his Holsteins twice a day.  这个农民每天给他的荷斯坦牛挤两次奶。|  The Holsteins grazed peacefully in the pasture. 荷斯坦牛在牧场上平静地吃草。 | synonyms: Holstein Friesians, dairy cows | antonyms: bulls (if referring specifically to female Holsteins) | noun
