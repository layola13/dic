aerographical:/ˌɛəroʊˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
航空绘图的;空气的
Relating to aerography, the study of the atmosphere.  Also, pertaining to the making of representations of the earth's surface from aerial photographs.
The aerographical survey provided valuable data for urban planning. 这项航空绘图测量为城市规划提供了宝贵的数据。

The pilot studied the aerographical charts before takeoff.  飞行员在起飞前研究了航空图表。
近义词:atmospheric, aerial
反义词:terrestrial
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (air) + -graphical (relating to writing or drawing).  Ultimately from Greek aēr (air) and graphein (to write).

This word is not commonly used in everyday conversation.  It is primarily used in technical or scientific contexts.
