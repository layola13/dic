entry-level:/ˈɛntri ˈlɛvəl/| adj.|入门级的；初级的|Suitable for someone starting their career.  Designed for or suitable for beginners or those with little experience.|She got an entry-level job at a law firm. 她在一间律师事务所获得了一份入门级的工作。|Synonyms: beginner, basic, introductory, junior|Antonyms: advanced, senior, experienced, expert|adjective
