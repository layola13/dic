nontactic:/nɒnˈtæktɪk/
adjective
非战术的; 非策略的
Not involving or relating to tactics, especially military or strategic tactics.
The CEO's nontactic approach to the merger confused the board members.  首席执行官对合并案的非战术性做法让董事会成员感到困惑。
The artist's nontactic brushstrokes gave the painting a unique and spontaneous feel.  艺术家非策略性的笔触赋予了这幅画独特而自然的感觉。
近义词:nonstrategic, untactical
反义词:tactical, strategic
词性:adjective
