hamstring:/ˈhæmstrɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhæmstrɪŋ/ (UK)| n. vt.| 腿筋；束缚；使残废|A tendon at the back of the knee.  To severely restrict the activity or effectiveness of someone or something.|
She pulled a hamstring during the race.  她在比赛中拉伤了腿筋。

She felt hamstrung by the lack of resources. 她感到缺乏资源束缚了她的手脚。

近义词:cripple, disable, incapacitate, restrict|
反义词:aid, assist, enable, help|
词性:noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hamm-þēoh (literally "ham-thigh"), where hamm refers to the back of the knee and þēoh means "thigh."
