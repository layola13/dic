metaphil:/ˈmɛtəfɪl/
n.
爱好哲学的人; 哲学爱好者
A lover of philosophy; a student of philosophy.
He considers himself a metaphil, always pondering the big questions. 他认为自己是一个哲学爱好者，总是思考着重大的问题。
近义词: philosopher, philomath
反义词: philistine
词性: noun

词源:
From Greek *μετα- (meta-) "after, among, with" + *φιλεῖν (philein) "to love."  Meaning "lover of philosophy, student of philosophy."
