blunderers:/ˈblʌndərərz/| n. | 犯错者，笨拙的人 | People who make mistakes or act clumsily. | The blunderers tripped over the equipment, causing chaos. 这些笨手笨脚的人被设备绊倒，造成了混乱。|  The team of blunderers lost the game due to a series of silly mistakes. 这支笨手笨脚的队伍因为一系列愚蠢的错误输掉了比赛。 | synonyms: bunglers, klutzes, incompetents | antonyms: experts, professionals | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From blunder (n.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns).  Blunder itself comes from Middle English blunderen, of uncertain origin, possibly Scandinavian.
