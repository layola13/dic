birthnight: /ˈbɜːrθnaɪt/
n.
生日之夜; 出生之夜
the night of one's birth.  The evening of the day on which someone was born.
例句：We celebrated her birthnight with a quiet dinner. 我们以一顿安静的晚餐庆祝了她的生日之夜。

例句：He remembered little of his birthnight. 他对出生的那个夜晚几乎没有什么记忆。

近义词：birthday, natal day
反义词：deathday
