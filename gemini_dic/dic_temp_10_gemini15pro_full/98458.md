inspiredly:/ɪnˈspaɪərɪdli/
adverb
充满灵感地，受鼓舞地
In a manner showing or feeling inspiration.
She played the piano inspiredly, her fingers dancing over the keys. 她充满灵感地弹着钢琴，手指在琴键上飞舞。
近义词: enthusiastically, imaginatively, creatively
反义词: dully, unimaginatively, listlessly
词性: 副词

词源:  From "inspired" + "-ly". "Inspired" comes from the Latin "inspirare," meaning "to breathe into," hence to influence or give inspiration.
