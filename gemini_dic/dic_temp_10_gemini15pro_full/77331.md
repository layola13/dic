gattern:/ˈɡætərn/ | n. | 水闸，堰，鱼梁 | A gate or weir used to control the flow of water, especially in a stream or river, often used for catching fish. | The old gattern was crumbling and needed repair.  旧的水闸已经破损，需要修理。|  Salmon were leaping over the gattern. 鲑鱼跳过鱼梁。| synonyms: weir, dam, sluice, fish weir | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "open channel") | noun

gattern:/ˈɡætərn/ | v. | 用水闸拦截 | To obstruct or control the flow of water with a gattern. | They gatteredned the stream to create a small pond. 他们用水闸拦住溪流，形成了一个小池塘。|  The river was gatteredned for irrigation purposes. 这条河被筑坝用于灌溉。 | synonyms: dam, impound, obstruct | antonyms: release, free | verb
