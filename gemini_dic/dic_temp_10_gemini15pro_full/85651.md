hausen:/ˈhaʊzən/| n. | 豪森（德国地名常用后缀）| A common suffix in German place names, indicating a settlement or house.  It's derived from the Old High German word "hūs," meaning "house."|例句：Neuenhausen is a beautiful town in Germany.  纽恩豪森是德国一个美丽的城镇。

例句：Many German towns end in -hausen. 很多德国城镇的名字都以 -hausen 结尾。|近义词: -heim, -dorf (similar German place name suffixes)|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词后缀
