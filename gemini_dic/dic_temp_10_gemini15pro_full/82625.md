gubernate:/ˈɡuːbərneɪt/ /ˈɡʌvərneɪt/| v. |统治，支配；担任总督|To govern; to act as a governor. |例句：He was appointed to gubernate the newly acquired territory. 他被任命管理新获得的领土。

例句：She gubernated the province with wisdom and fairness. 她以智慧和公平管理该省。|近义词：govern, rule, administer, control|反义词：follow, obey, submit|词性：动词

gubernatorial:/ˌɡuːbərˈnɔːriəl/ /ˌɡʌbərˈnɔːriəl/| adj. |总督的；与总督有关的|Relating to a governor or governorship. |例句：The gubernatorial election is scheduled for next year. 总督选举定于明年举行。

例句：He made a gubernatorial visit to the flood-stricken area. 他对洪灾地区进行了总督视察。|近义词：governing|反义词：N/A|词性：形容词
