meibomian: /maɪˈboʊmiən/ | adj. | 睑板的；迈博姆腺的 | Relating to the meibomian glands, which are sebaceous glands in the eyelids that secrete an oily substance to lubricate the eyes. | Meibomian gland dysfunction can cause dry eyes. 睑板腺功能障碍会导致干眼症。 |  synonym: tarsal | antonym: N/A | adjective
