bikkies:/ˈbɪkiz/
n.
饼干 (英式非正式)
Biscuits, cookies (informal British English).
I fancy a cuppa and some bikkies. 我想喝杯茶，吃点饼干。
近义词：biscuits, cookies, crackers
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of food)
词性: Noun

bikkies:/ˈbɪkiz/
Etymology: Shortened form of "biscuits," influenced by Australian and New Zealand English.
词源：biscuit的缩写形式，受澳大利亚和新西兰英语的影响。
