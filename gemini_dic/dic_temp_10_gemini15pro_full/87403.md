herringlike:/ˈherɪŋˌlaɪk/| adj.|像鲱鱼的，鲱鱼似的|Resembling a herring in appearance or characteristics; herring-shaped or herring-flavored.|例句：The small, silvery fish were herringlike in appearance. 这些小的银色鱼看起来像鲱鱼。|例句：The dish had a distinct, herringlike aroma. 这道菜有一种明显的鲱鱼香味。|近义词：herring-shaped, herring-flavored, clupeiform|反义词:|形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From *herring* + *-like*. *Herring* comes from Old English *hæring*, from Proto-Germanic *haringaz*, likely related to a root meaning "grey" or "army" (perhaps referencing the large schools they form). *-like* is a common suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
