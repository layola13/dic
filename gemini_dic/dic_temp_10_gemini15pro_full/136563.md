nonsalaried:/ˌnɒnˈsælərid/ /ˌnɑːnˈsælərid/
nonsalaried| n.|非受薪人员|A person who is not receiving a regular salary.| The nonsalaried interns worked long hours.|Synonyms: volunteer, unpaid worker|Antonyms: employee, salaried worker|noun
