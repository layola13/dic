counter-extension:/ˌkaʊntər ɪkˈstenʃən/| n.|反向伸展，对抗伸展|The act of extending a limb or other body part in the opposite direction to another limb or body part, typically to provide stability or leverage.|The gymnast used counter-extension of her arms to maintain balance on the beam.|近义词：counterbalance, counterpoise|反义词：flexion|noun
