constitute:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ (British), /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːt/ (American)| vt.|组成，构成；设立，建立；任命；构成…的要件|Seven days constitute a week. 七天构成一个星期。|Synonyms: form, compose, comprise, make up, establish, create, appoint|Antonyms: dissolve, destroy, abolish|verb

constitute:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ (British), /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːt/ (American)| n.|构成；建立；任命|The constitution of the committee was controversial. 委员会的组成备受争议。|Synonyms: establishment, formation, composition|Antonyms: dissolution, destruction, abolition|noun
