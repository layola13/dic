ocher:/ˈoʊkər/ (US) /ˈəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|赭色，赭石|A moderate orange yellow.  A natural earth pigment containing hydrated iron oxide, ranging in color from light yellow to deep orange or brown.|The artist used ocher to create the warm tones in the painting. 艺术家用赭石来创作画作中的暖色调。|Synonyms: ochre, yellow ocher, sienna, umber|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a color, but could consider contrasting colors like blue or green)|noun
ocher:/ˈoʊkər/ (US) /ˈəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|赭色的|Having the color of ocher.|The walls were painted a warm ocher color. 墙壁被漆成了温暖的赭色。|Synonyms: ochreous, brownish-yellow|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a color, but could consider contrasting colors like blue or green)|adjective
