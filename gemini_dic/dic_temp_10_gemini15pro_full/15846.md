ballasting:/ˈbæləstɪŋ/ | n. | 压载，镇重物 | The act of providing stability to something, typically a ship or airship, by adding weight. |  The ship was undergoing ballasting before its transatlantic voyage.  这艘船在横渡大西洋之前正在进行压载。 | synonyms: weighting, stabilizing, counterbalancing | antonyms: lightening, unbalancing | noun


ballast:/ˈbæləst/ | n. vt.| 压舱物，镇重物；使稳定 | Heavy material, such as gravel, sand, or iron, placed low in a vessel to give it stability.  | They used rocks as ballast for the hot air balloon. 他们用岩石作为热气球的压舱物。 | synonyms: counterweight, stabilizer | antonyms:  | noun, verb
