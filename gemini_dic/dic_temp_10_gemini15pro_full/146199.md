overexpansions:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænʃənz/
noun
过度扩张；过度膨胀
Excessive expansions, often beyond a sustainable or healthy limit.
The company's overexpansions into new markets led to financial difficulties.  公司过度扩张到新市场导致了财务困难。
The rapid overexpansions of the empire ultimately resulted in its collapse.  帝国的快速过度扩张最终导致了它的崩溃。
synonyms: overextensions, overgrowth, proliferation, spread, burgeoning
antonyms: contraction, reduction, downsizing, shrinking
n.
