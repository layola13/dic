co-attest:/ˌkoʊəˈtɛst/
|v.|共同证明；联署；联合作证|To certify or confirm something together with another person or persons. |The document was co-attested by two witnesses.  这份文件由两名证人共同作证。|The lawyers co-attested the signing of the will. 律师们共同见证了遗嘱的签署。|Synonym: corroborate, verify, authenticate|Antonym: deny, contradict, refute|verb

co-attest:/ˌkoʊəˈtɛst/
|n.|共同证明；联署；联合作证|The act of certifying or confirming something together with another person or persons.|The co-attestation of the document ensured its validity. 文件的共同认证确保了它的有效性。|The will required the co-attestation of two independent witnesses. 这份遗嘱需要两名独立证人的联署证明。|Synonym: corroboration, verification, authentication|Antonym: denial, contradiction, refutation|noun
