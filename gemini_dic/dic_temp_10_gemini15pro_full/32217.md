chicest:/ˈʃiːkɪst/
adjective
最时尚的，最优雅的
Most stylish and fashionable; elegantly and tastefully smart.
She wore the chicest outfit at the party. 她在派对上穿了最时尚的衣服。
Near Synonyms:  stylish, fashionable, trendy, elegant, sophisticated
Antonyms:  unfashionable, dowdy, frumpy, tacky
词性: adjective
