half-clearly:/ˌhɑːfˈklɪrli/
adv.
半清晰地，不太清晰地
Partly or incompletely clear; in a way that is not fully understood or explained.
The instructions were half-clearly written, leading to confusion. 说明书写得半清晰，导致了混乱。
The message was conveyed half-clearly, so I'm still unsure what they meant.  这条信息传达得不太清晰，所以我仍然不确定他们的意思。
近义词: vaguely, obscurely, ambiguously
反义词: clearly, distinctly, explicitly
词性: 副词

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "clearly" (from clear + -ly)
