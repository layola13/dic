churchgoing:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌɡəʊɪŋ/ /ˈtʃɝːtʃˌɡoʊɪŋ/
adj.
经常去教堂的；虔诚的
Regularly attending church; devout.
She comes from a very churchgoing family. 她来自一个非常虔诚的家庭。
Near synonyms: devout, pious, religious
Antonyms: irreligious, impious, secular
Etymology:  church + going (present participle of go)
Word type: Adjective
