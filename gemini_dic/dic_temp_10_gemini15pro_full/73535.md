foresightful:/ˈfɔːrsaɪtfəl/ /ˈfɔːrˌsaɪtfəl/| adj.|有远见的；深谋远虑的|Having or showing foresight; able to anticipate future needs or possibilities.|例句：She made a foresightful decision to invest in renewable energy. 她做了一个有远见的决定，投资可再生能源。

例句：His foresightful planning saved the company from bankruptcy. 他深谋远虑的计划使公司免于破产。

近义词：prescient, visionary, provident, anticipatory|反义词：shortsighted, myopic, improvident|词性: adjective
