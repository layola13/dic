codecs:/ˈkoʊdɛks/| n. |编解码器|A codec is a device or computer program capable of encoding or decoding a digital data stream or signal.  It's a portmanteau of "coder-decoder".|Example 1: The video file uses a modern codec that provides excellent compression.  这个视频文件使用了一种提供出色压缩的现代编解码器。

Example 2:  I need to install the right codec to play this audio file. 我需要安装正确的编解码器来播放这个音频文件。|Synonym: encoder, decoder|Antonym: N/A|Noun
