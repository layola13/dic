epigrammatical:/ˌepɪɡrəˈmætɪkl/
adjective
警句的；格言式的
Relating to or resembling an epigram; concise, pointed, and often paradoxical.
His epigrammatical style makes his writing memorable. 他的警句式风格使他的作品令人难忘。
synonyms: pithy, concise, terse, succinct, pointed, aphoristic, sententious
antonyms: verbose, prolix, rambling
