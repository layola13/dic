indexical:/ɪnˈdɛksɪk(ə)l/| adj. n.|指示的，指示词|Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used.|The word "here" is an indexical expression, as its meaning changes depending on the speaker's location.|近义词：deictic, demonstrative|反义词:non-indexical
