isorcin:/ˌaɪsoʊˈrɪsɪn/
noun
异苔色酸; 3,5-二羟基甲苯
A crystalline dihydric phenol isomeric with orcin. It is found in certain lichens.
Isorcin is used in some chemical syntheses. 异苔色酸用于某些化学合成。
近义词: dihydroxytoluene, methylresorcinol
反义词: None
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From iso- + orcin.
