deliverness: /dɪˈlɪvərnəs/
n.
交付能力；实现能力
The quality or state of being deliverable or achievable; ability to deliver or achieve.
The project's success hinges on the team's deliverness.  这个项目的成功取决于团队的交付能力。
The company's deliverness on its promises is crucial for building trust. 公司兑现承诺的能力对于建立信任至关重要。
近义词: capability, ability, competence, effectiveness
反义词: inability, incompetence, ineffectiveness
词性: 名词
