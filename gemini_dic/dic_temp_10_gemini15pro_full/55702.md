diurnalist:/ˌdaɪˈɜːrnəlɪst/ | n. | 日报记者；白天活动的人或动物 | A journalist who writes for a daily newspaper; a person or animal active during the day. |  The diurnalist filed his story just before deadline.  这位日报记者在截止日期前提交了他的报道。|  The diurnalist squirrel scurried up the tree. 这只白天活动的松鼠 scurried up the tree. | synonyms: reporter, journalist, daily writer;  daytime animal | antonyms: nocturnalist, night worker | noun

diurnal:/daɪˈɜːrnəl/ | adj. | 白天的；每日的 |  Of or relating to the daytime; daily; occurring every day. | Diurnal animals are active during the day. 白天活动的动物在白天很活跃。 | The hospital recorded her diurnal temperature changes. 医院记录了她每天的体温变化。| synonyms: daily, daytime | antonyms: nocturnal, nightly | adjective
