gazebos:/ɡəˈziːboʊz/ /ɡəˈziːbəʊz/
n.
凉亭；观景亭
A freestanding, roofed, open-sided structure, often hexagonal or octagonal, providing shade and a place to relax in a garden or park.
We sat in the gazebo, enjoying the shade and the view of the lake. 我们坐在凉亭里，享受着树荫和湖景。
The wedding ceremony was held in a beautifully decorated gazebo. 婚礼仪式在一个装饰精美的凉亭里举行。

近义词:pavilion, pergola, summerhouse
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "enclosed building" could be considered a contrasting concept)
词性:noun (plural)

gazebo:/ɡəˈziːboʊ/ /ɡəˈziːbəʊ/
n.
凉亭；观景亭
A freestanding, roofed, open-sided structure, often hexagonal or octagonal, providing shade and a place to relax in a garden or park.
The gazebo offered a welcome respite from the midday sun. 凉亭提供了正午阳光下受欢迎的休憩场所。
They built a gazebo overlooking the ocean. 他们在可以俯瞰大海的地方建造了一个凉亭。

近义词:pavilion, pergola, summerhouse
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun (singular)


词源:
From the 18th century, of uncertain origin. Possibly a humorous pseudo-Latin formation, perhaps on the model of Latin verbs ending in "-ibo," suggesting a fanciful meaning like "I shall gaze."
