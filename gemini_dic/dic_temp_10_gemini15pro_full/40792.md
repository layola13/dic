convocation's:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃənz/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
会议的; 召集的
belonging to or associated with a convocation (a large formal assembly of people)
The convocation's decision to increase tuition fees was met with student protests.  会议决定增加学费，遭到学生抗议。
synonyms: assembly's, gathering's, meeting's
antonyms: individual's, private's, solitary's
词性: noun (possessive form)
