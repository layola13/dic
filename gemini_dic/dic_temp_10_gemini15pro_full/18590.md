benignities:/bɪˈnɪɡnɪtiz/
n.|仁慈, 善举, 恩惠|acts of kindness or generosity; a gracious disposition.
|Her reign was marked by numerous benignities towards her subjects.|她统治时期以对臣民的无数仁慈行为而著称。
|Synonyms: kindnesses, generosities, favors, graces, blessings|
|Antonyms: cruelties, severities, harshness, malevolence|
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


benignity:/bɪˈnɪɡnɪti/
n.|仁慈, 善举, 恩惠|the quality of being kind, gentle, and harmless.
|He showed great benignity towards his former enemies.|他对以前的敌人表现出极大的仁慈。
|Synonyms: kindness, gentleness, mildness, compassion, benevolence|
|Antonyms: cruelty, severity, harshness, malevolence|
词性: 名词 (noun)
