episyllogism:/ˌɛpɪˈsɪləˌdʒɪzəm/
n.
附加三段论，间接三段论
A syllogism in which the conclusion of one syllogism becomes the premise of another.
The philosopher constructed a complex argument using a series of episyllogisms, each building upon the previous one. 哲学家运用一系列附加三段论构建了一个复杂的论证，每个论证都建立在前一个论证的基础上。
近义词:polysyllogism, chain syllogism
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider a simple, single syllogism as a contrasting concept)
词性:noun
