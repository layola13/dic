parashoth:/pærəˈʃɔːθ/| n.|红雀科鸟类（尤指雄鸟）; (犹太教)律法注释|A small, brightly colored finch-like bird, typically the male, of the family Emberizidae.  (Judaism) A commentary on Jewish law, especially on the Pentateuch.|The parashoth are often studied in synagogues. 这些律法注释经常在犹太教堂里被学习。|Near synonyms: cardinal, finch (for the bird); commentary, exegesis (for the Jewish law meaning)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the bird. For the commentary, perhaps) original text|Noun

注意事项:

“parashoth” 作为鸟类名称的用法较为罕见，主要指律法注释。  这个词来源于希伯来语 פָּרָשַׁת (parashah)，意思是“部分”或“段落”。  复数形式可以是 parashoth 或 parashot。 我这里采用parashoth。
