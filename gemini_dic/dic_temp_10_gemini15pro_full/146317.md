overfeels:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːlz/| v. |过度 احساس; 过度反应|To experience or express emotions too intensely, often in an exaggerated or dramatic way. It implies an excessive display of feelings, usually in response to something relatively minor.  It's often used online and in informal settings.|例句：She overfeels everything, even small setbacks. 她对每件事都反应过度，即使是小挫折也是如此。

例句：He tends to overfeel when watching sad movies. 他看悲伤电影时容易过度感伤。|近义词：overreact, exaggerate, dramatize|反义词：underreact, downplay|词性:动词
