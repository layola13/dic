exstrophy:/ˈekstrəfi/| n.|外翻，翻出|The congenital malformation characterized by the bladder lying open and exposed on the surface of the abdomen.|Bladder exstrophy is a rare birth defect that occurs early on in pregnancy when the bladder doesn't form properly outside the abdomen.|近义词：eversion, prolapse|反义词:inversion|词性:名词

Etymology: From Modern Latin *exstrophia, from Greek ἐκστροφή (ekstrophḗ, “a turning out, eversion”), from ἐκστρέφω (ekstréphō, “I turn out”), from ἐκ- (ek-, “out”) + στρέφω (stréphō, “I turn”).
