hyperconcentrations:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. | 超高浓度；过度集中 |  The state of being present in excessively high concentrations.  This often refers to substances dissolved in a liquid or particles suspended in a medium, exceeding normal or expected levels. | The hyperconcentrations of pollutants in the river caused a massive fish kill. 河流中污染物的超高浓度导致大量鱼类死亡。 |  The study examined the effects of hyperconcentrations of nutrients on plant growth. 这项研究调查了营养物质过度集中对植物生长的影响。| high concentrations, excessive levels | low concentrations, dilution | noun
