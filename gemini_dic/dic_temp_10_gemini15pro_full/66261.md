exclusive: /ɪksˈkluːsɪv/ (US) /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ (UK) | adj. n. | 独有的，排外的；独家新闻；独家经营的商品 | Not admitting of something else; limited to a particular person, group, or area. | This room is for the exclusive use of guests.  这个房间是供客人专用。|  近义词: sole, private, restricted, select | 反义词: inclusive, general, public | 词性: 形容词

exclusive: /ɪksˈkluːsɪv/ (US) /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/ (UK) | n. | 独家新闻；独家经营的商品 | An item or story published or broadcast by only one source. | The newspaper ran an exclusive on the mayor's resignation. 这家报纸刊登了市长辞职的独家新闻。| 近义词: scoop | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin exclusivus, from excludere ‘to shut out’.
