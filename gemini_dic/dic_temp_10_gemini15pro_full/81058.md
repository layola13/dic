grain:/ɡreɪn/| n.|谷物，颗粒，少量，纹理|A small, hard piece of something like sand or salt.  The seeds of plants (such as wheat, corn, and rice) that are used for food. A very small amount of something. The pattern of lines on the surface of wood or cloth.|The farmer scattered grain for the chickens. 农民把谷物撒给鸡吃。|Synonyms: seed, kernel, particle, bit, speck|Antonyms: whole, mass|noun

grain:/ɡreɪn/| n.|木纹，纹理，气质|The direction of the lines of wood, cloth, etc. The natural character of someone or something.|The wood has a beautiful grain. 这块木头的纹理很漂亮。|Synonyms: texture, fiber, nature, temperament|Antonyms: none|noun
