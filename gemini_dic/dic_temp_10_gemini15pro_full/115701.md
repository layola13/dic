marginalization:/ˌmɑːrdʒɪnəˌlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|边缘化；排斥|The process of treating a person or group as insignificant or peripheral.|例句：The marginalization of women in the workplace is a serious issue. 职场女性的边缘化是一个严重的问题。|近义词：exclusion, ostracism, sidelining, isolation|反义词：centralization, integration, inclusion, empowerment|词性:名词


词源: From margin + -al + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin *margin-, related to margo ("border, edge").
