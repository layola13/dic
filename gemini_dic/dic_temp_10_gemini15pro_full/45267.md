cultivated:/ˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/| adj.|耕种的，栽培的；有修养的，有教养的|Refined and well-educated.  Showing good manners and taste.|She has a cultivated manner and speaks several languages fluently. 她举止优雅，能流利地说几种语言。|Synonyms: refined, cultured, educated, polished, sophisticated|Antonyms: uncultured, uncultivated, crude, coarse, vulgar|adjective

cultivate:/ˈkʌltɪveɪt/| vt.|耕作，栽培；培养，陶冶|To prepare and use land for crops or gardening; to acquire or develop a skill or quality.|The farmer cultivated the land before planting the seeds. 农民在播种前耕耘土地。 He cultivated an appreciation for classical music. 他培养了对古典音乐的欣赏。|Synonyms: nurture, foster, develop, grow, raise|Antonyms: neglect, abandon, ignore|verb
