antiskeptic:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪk/
n.|反怀疑论者; 反怀疑论的 (少见)|A person who opposes skepticism or a particular skeptical argument.|He considered himself an antiskeptic, believing strongly in the power of faith and intuition.|Near synonyms: believer,  proponent|Near antonyms: skeptic, doubter|noun
antiskeptic:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪk/
adj.|反怀疑论的; 反对怀疑论的 (少见)|Opposed to skepticism or a particular skeptical argument.|His antiskeptic stance often put him at odds with his more empirically-minded colleagues.|Near synonyms: believing,  trusting|Near antonyms: skeptical, doubting|adjective
