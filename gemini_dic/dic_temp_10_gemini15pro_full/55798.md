divertimento:/ˌdɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛntoʊ/ /ˌdaɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛntoʊ/
| n. |嬉游曲，娱乐性乐曲|A light and entertaining musical composition, typically one in several movements, for a small group of instruments.|Mozart wrote several divertimentos for strings and wind instruments. 莫扎特创作了几首弦乐和管乐的嬉游曲。|
|Synonym: serenade, cassation, divertimento|Antonym: dirge, lament|noun|
divertimenti:/ˌdɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛntiː/ /ˌdaɪvɜːrtɪˈmɛntiː/
