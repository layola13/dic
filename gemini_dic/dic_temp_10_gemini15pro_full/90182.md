hotshot:/ˈhɑːtʃɑːt/| n.|能手，高手，自命不凡的人|A person who is very skilled and successful at something, often in a showy or arrogant way. |例句：He thinks he's a real hotshot lawyer.  他认为自己是一个真正的高手律师。

例句：The company hired a hotshot young executive to turn the business around. 公司聘请了一位精明能干的年轻主管来扭转业务局面。|近义词：expert, ace, whiz, prodigy, star, big shot|反义词：amateur, beginner, novice, incompetent|词性：noun
