muriate:/ˈmjʊəriət/ /ˈmʊəriˌeɪt/ | n. | 氯化物；盐酸盐 |A chloride of a metal.  Specifically and archaically: a crystalline salt containing chlorine. Formerly used especially of the chlorides of the alkali metals and ammonium.|Potassium muriate is used as a fertilizer. 氯化钾被用作肥料。| synonym: chloride, hydrochlorate | antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider compounds not containing chlorine) | noun
