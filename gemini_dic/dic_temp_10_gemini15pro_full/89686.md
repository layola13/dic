hoot:/huːt/| n. vi. vt.|猫头鹰叫声，汽笛声；嘲笑；起哄|A short, loud, high sound made by an owl.  Also, a shout or cry expressing disapproval or scorn.|The owl hooted from the tree. 猫头鹰在树上鸣叫。|近义词：screech, howl, jeer, boo|反义词: cheer, applaud|noun, verb
