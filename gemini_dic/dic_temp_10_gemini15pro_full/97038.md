infects:/ɪnˈfɛkts/| v. |感染，传染|To contaminate with a disease-producing germ or agent; to affect in a contagious way.|The virus infects the respiratory system. 病毒感染呼吸系统。|近义词：contaminates, transmits, spreads|反义词：cures, heals|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *īnficere*, from *īn-* (in, into) + *facere* (to make, do).  Originally meant "to put something into," later evolving to mean "to taint" or "corrupt," and finally acquiring its modern meaning of causing infection.
