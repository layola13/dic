all-inclusiveness:/ɔːlɪnˈkluːsɪvnəs/| n.|包容性，全包性|The quality or state of including everything or everyone; comprehensiveness.|The company prides itself on its all-inclusiveness, embracing employees from all walks of life.|公司以其包容性为荣，接纳来自各行各业的员工。|inclusivity, comprehensiveness, universality|exclusivity, restrictiveness|noun

all-inclusive:/ɔːlɪnˈkluːsɪv/| adj.|包罗万象的，全包的|Including everything or everyone; comprehensive.|We booked an all-inclusive holiday resort, so all meals and drinks were included in the price.|我们预订了一个全包的度假酒店，所以所有的餐食和饮料都包含在价格内。|comprehensive, inclusive, all-embracing|exclusive, restrictive|adjective
