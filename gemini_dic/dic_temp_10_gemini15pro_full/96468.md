individualize:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪz/| v. |使个性化；个别处理；个别对待|To make something suitable for individual people or things; to treat someone or something as different from others.|例句：The teacher tries to individualize instruction for each student. 老师努力让每个学生的教学个性化。

例句：They offer individualized training programs for athletes of all levels.  他们为各级运动员提供个性化的训练计划。|近义词：personalize, customize, tailor, adapt|反义词：standardize, generalize|词性:动词
