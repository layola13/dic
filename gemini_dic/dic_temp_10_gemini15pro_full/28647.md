caribs:/ˈkærɪbz/ | n. |加勒比人|A member of an indigenous people of the Caribbean islands and parts of adjacent South America, now largely extinct.  Originally inhabiting the Lesser Antilles, they were noted as fierce warriors who expanded into the Greater Antilles.|Columbus encountered Caribs on his second voyage in 1493. 哥伦布在1493年的第二次航行中遇到了加勒比人。

The Caribs were skilled navigators and boat builders. 加勒比人是熟练的航海家和造船师。|近义词：Island people, Amerindians|反义词: None (No direct antonym for an ethnic group)|词性: Noun
