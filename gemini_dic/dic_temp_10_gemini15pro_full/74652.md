free-spirited:/ˌfriːˈspɪrɪtɪd/ | adj. | 自由奔放的，无拘无束的 | Not bound by conventional rules or expectations; independent and unconventional in thought and action. |  She's a free-spirited artist who lives life on her own terms. 她是一位自由奔放的艺术家，按照自己的方式生活。

He has always been a free-spirited adventurer, traveling the world without a plan. 他一直是一个自由奔放的冒险家，漫无目的地环游世界。 | 近义词：independent, bohemian, unconventional, adventurous, uninhibited | 反义词：conventional, conformist, inhibited, restrained, traditional | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From free + spirited.  "Free" meaning unrestrained and "spirited" referring to having a lively, independent character. The combination emphasizes a personality that is not limited by social norms.
