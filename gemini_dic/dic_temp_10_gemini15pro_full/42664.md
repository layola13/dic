counterstrategist:/ˈkaʊntərˈstrætɪdʒɪst/
n.
反战略家；对策专家
One who devises counterstrategies.  A person who specializes in developing strategies to counteract the strategies of an opponent.
The general hired a team of counterstrategists to anticipate the enemy's next move. 将军聘请了一组反战略家来预测敌人的下一步行动。
The company's counterstrategist formulated a plan to outmaneuver the competition. 公司的反战略家制定了一个计划来智胜竞争对手。
Synonyms: tactician, strategist, planner, analyst
Antonyms:  N/A
