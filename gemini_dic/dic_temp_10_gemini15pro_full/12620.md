assassinate:/əˈsæsɪneɪt/ | vt. | 暗杀，行刺 | To murder (an important person) in a surprise attack for political or religious reasons. |  Example: The president was assassinated by a lone gunman. 总统被一名枪手暗杀了。

Example:  They plotted to assassinate the king. 他们密谋暗杀国王。

| 近义词: murder, kill, execute, eliminate, liquidate | 反义词: protect, defend, save | 词性: 动词
