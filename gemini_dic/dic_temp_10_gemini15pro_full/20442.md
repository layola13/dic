bittered:/ˈbɪtərd/| adj.|痛苦的，怨恨的，愤懑的|Made resentful or cynical; embittered.  Feeling or expressing bitterness.|例句：He was a bittered old man, full of resentment towards the world. 他是一个痛苦的老人，对世界充满了怨恨。

例句：She felt bittered by the betrayal of her closest friend. 她被最亲密朋友的背叛弄得很痛苦。|近义词：resentful, embittered, cynical, disillusioned|反义词：happy, content, joyful, optimistic|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From bitter + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bitraz.
