concessives:/kənˈsesɪvz/ | n. | 让步状语从句；让步 | Clauses that express an idea that contrasts with the main idea of the sentence, but does not prevent it. |  Although it was raining, we went for a walk. 尽管下雨了，我们还是去散步了。| synonyms: concessions, allowances | antonyms: restrictions, prohibitions | noun

concessive:/kənˈsesɪv/ | adj. | 让步的 | Having the power of conceding or yielding. | He made a concessive gesture, allowing her to speak first. 他做了一个让步的姿势，让她先发言。 | synonyms: yielding, accommodating | antonyms: unyielding, inflexible | adjective
