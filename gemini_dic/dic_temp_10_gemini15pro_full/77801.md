generalizer: /ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzər/ | n. | 概括者；推广者；泛化器 |  Someone or something that generalizes, forming general concepts from specific instances. |  He's a generalizer, preferring to talk about broad trends rather than specific details. 他是一个概括者，喜欢谈论广泛的趋势，而不是具体的细节。| synonyms: abstractor, synthesizer, theorist | antonyms: specialist, particularizer | noun


generalize: /ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪz/ | v. | 概括；推广；普及 | To make a general statement or form a general concept from specific instances; to extend a particular instance to a more general conclusion. |  It is dangerous to generalize from a single example. 从单个例子进行概括是危险的。| synonyms: abstract, synthesize, theorize | antonyms: specialize, particularize | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *generalizāre, from Latin generālis (“general”) + -izāre, equivalent to English general + -ize.
