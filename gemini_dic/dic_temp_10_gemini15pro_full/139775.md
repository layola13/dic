octane:/ˈɒkteɪn/ (UK) /ˈɑːkteɪn/ (US)| n.|辛烷|A saturated hydrocarbon found in petroleum, used as a fuel and solvent. It is also used as a standard in determining the octane rating of gasoline.|High-octane gasoline is more efficient.  高辛烷值汽油效率更高。|Synonyms: alkane, paraffin|Antonyms: N/A|noun

octander:/ɒkˈteɪndər/ (UK) /ɑːkˈteɪndər/ (US)| n.|辛烷值测定仪|An instrument used to measure the octane rating of gasoline.|The mechanic used an octander to test the fuel. 机械师用辛烷值测定仪测试燃料。|Synonyms: octane rating tester, octane analyzer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
