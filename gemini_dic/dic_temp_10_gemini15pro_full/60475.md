ejecta:/ɪˈdʒɛktə/
noun
喷出物，排出物
Matter thrown out from a volcano or crater.
The ejecta from the volcano covered the surrounding area. 火山的喷出物覆盖了周围地区。
synonyms: debris, discharge, emission, outflow
antonyms: intake, inflow

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ējectus, past participle of ēicere (to throw out).  From ex- (out) + jacere (to throw).
