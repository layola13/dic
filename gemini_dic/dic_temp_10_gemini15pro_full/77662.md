gelid:/ˈdʒɛlɪd/| adj.|极冷的；冰冷的|Extremely cold; icy.  Characterized by coldness, especially in a way that suggests lack of emotion or sympathy.|The gelid wind whipped across the frozen plains. 冰冷的风吹过冰冻的平原。|Synonyms: frigid, icy, frosty, freezing, glacial|Antonyms: warm, hot, tropical, balmy|adjective
