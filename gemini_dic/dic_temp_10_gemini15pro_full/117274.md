mechanizable:/ˌmɛkəˈnaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可机械化的 | Capable of being mechanized; able to be done by machines. |  Example 1: Farming has become increasingly mechanizable, reducing the need for manual labor. 农业越来越机械化，减少了对体力劳动的需求。

Example 2:  Many repetitive tasks in manufacturing are mechanizable. 制造业中的许多重复性工作可以机械化。 | Synonyms: automated, automatic, machinable | Antonyms: manual, hand-operated | adjective
