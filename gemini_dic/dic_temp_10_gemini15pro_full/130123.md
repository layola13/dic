non-rationalist:/nɒnˈræʃənəlɪst/ | adj. n. | 非理性主义的；非理性主义者 | Not based on or guided by reason or logic; rejecting rationalism. |  He took a non-rationalist approach to the problem, relying more on intuition. 他用非理性主义的方法处理这个问题，更多地依赖直觉。| 近义词:irrational, illogical, emotional | 反义词: rational, logical, reasonable | 词性:形容词, 名词


non-rationalist:/nɒnˈræʃənəlɪst/ | n. | 非理性主义者 | A person who rejects rationalism. | The non-rationalist argued that experience and feeling were more important than logic.  这位非理性主义者认为经验和感觉比逻辑更重要。 | 近义词: irrationalist | 反义词: rationalist | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin *non* meaning "not"
rationalist: from Late Latin *rationalis* "of or belonging to reason," from *ratio* "reason, calculation, reckoning."  The suffix *-ist* denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system.
