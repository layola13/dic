miniaturizing:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
小型化，微型化
the process of making something much smaller than it usually is
例句：Miniaturizing electronic components has revolutionized the computer industry.  电子元件的小型化彻底改变了计算机行业。

例句：They are working on miniaturizing the device to make it more portable.  他们正在努力使该设备小型化，使其更便携。
近义词：shrinking, reducing, downsizing
反义词：enlarging, expanding, magnifying
词性: verb (present participle)
