hyomandibular:/haɪ.əʊ.mænˈdɪb.jʊ.lər/ | adj. n.| 舌颌的，舌颌骨 |Relating to the hyomandibular bone or cartilage, which is a bone or cartilage element supporting the jaws of certain vertebrates. In most fishes, it connects the jaw to the skull. It is derived from the second pharyngeal arch. | The hyomandibular bone plays a crucial role in jaw suspension. | 近义词: (as a noun) hyomandibula | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun

hyomandibula:/haɪ.əʊ.mænˈdɪb.jʊ.lə/ | n.| 舌颌骨 | The bone or cartilage that connects the jaw to the skull in most fishes and in some amphibians. It is derived from the second pharyngeal arch. | The hyomandibula is a key element in the evolution of the vertebrate jaw. | 近义词: (as an adjective) hyomandibular | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
