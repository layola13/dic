excellent:/ˈɛksələnt/| adj.|优秀的，卓越的，极好的|Of the highest quality; outstanding.|Her performance was excellent. 她的表演非常出色。|近义词：superb, outstanding, magnificent, wonderful, first-rate, top-notch, exceptional|反义词: poor, bad, terrible, awful, inferior|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin excellentem (nominative excellens) "surpassing, excelling, distinguished, prominent," present participle of excellere "to rise above, surpass, excel," from ex "out, forth" + -cellere "to rise, be high," related to celsus "high."
