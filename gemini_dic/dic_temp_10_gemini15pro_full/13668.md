attractingly:/əˈtræktɪŋli/
adverb|迷人地，有吸引力地|in a way that attracts attention or interest; pleasantly|She was attractingly dressed in a bright red dress. 她穿着一件鲜红色的连衣裙，非常迷人。|近义词：charmingly, appealingly, enticingly, alluringly|反义词：repulsively, unattractively, displeasingly|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From attract + -ing + -ly.  Attract comes from the Latin *attractare*, frequentative of *attrahere* "to draw to oneself," from *ad-* "to" + *trahere* "to draw."
