break up: /ˈbreɪk ˈʌp/ (US) /ˈbreɪk ʌp/ (UK)| v. (phrasal verb) | 分手；解散；破裂；结束|They broke up after two years of dating.  他们约会两年后分手了。|近义词：separate, split, disband, dissolve|反义词：unite, reconcile, merge|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrasal verb "break up," composed of the verb "break" (Old English *brecan, from Proto-Germanic *brekaną) and the adverb/particle "up" (Old English up, uppe, from Proto-Germanic *upp).  The noun form emerged later, likely in the 19th century.
