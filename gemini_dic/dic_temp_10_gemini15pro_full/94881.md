in-charge:/ɪn ˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
adjective|负责的，主管的|Being in control or having responsibility over something or someone.|例句：She is in charge of marketing. 她负责市场营销。|近义词：responsible, leading, managing, supervisory|反义词:subordinate, junior|词性:adjective

in charge:/ɪn ˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
adverb|主管，负责|In a position of authority or control.|例句：Who's in charge here?  这里谁负责？|近义词：in command, in control, at the helm|反义词:subordinate, powerless|词性:adverb

in-charge (hyphenated as an adjective):
From "in" (preposition indicating position or state) + "charge" (noun from Old French "charge" meaning load, burden, expense, or office).  The sense of "in charge" evolved from the idea of being entrusted with a responsibility or duty.
