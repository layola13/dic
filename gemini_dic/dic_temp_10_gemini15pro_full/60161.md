effeminate: /ɪˈfɛmɪnət/ | adj. v. | 柔弱的，女人气的；使柔弱 | Having or showing qualities or characteristics more often associated with women than men. | He had an effeminate manner that some people found off-putting. 他有一种女人气的举止，有些人觉得很不舒服。|  He effeminated his brother by forcing him to wear dresses. 他强迫弟弟穿裙子，使他女性化。 | Synonyms: womanish, unmanly, sissy | Antonyms: manly, masculine, virile | adjective, verb


effeminates: /ɪˈfɛmɪneɪts/ | v. | 使柔弱，使女性化 | To make someone or something appear or behave more like a woman. |  Constant teasing effeminates boys and makes them insecure.  不断的戏弄会使男孩变得女性化，并使他们缺乏安全感。| Synonyms: emasculate, weaken, soften | Antonyms: strengthen, masculinize, harden | verb
