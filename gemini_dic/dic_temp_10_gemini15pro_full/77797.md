generalization:/ˌdʒɛnərəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 概括；普遍化；归纳；推广 |A general statement or concept obtained by inference from specific cases.|  He made a sweeping generalization about women. 他对女性作了一个笼统的概括。 | synonyms: abstraction, concept, induction, summary | antonyms: specification, particularization | noun

generalization:/ˌdʒɛnərəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 泛化；普遍化 |The process of formulating general concepts by abstracting common properties of instances.| The generalization of this mathematical principle is complex. 这个数学原理的推广很复杂。| synonyms: extension, expansion, broadening | antonyms: restriction, limitation, narrowing | noun
