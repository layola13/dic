nonimmunity:/ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. |无免疫力，不免疫|The state of not being immune to something, especially a disease.|  Her nonimmunity to chickenpox meant she had to avoid contact with infected individuals. 她对水痘没有免疫力，这意味着她必须避免与感染者接触。|Synonyms: vulnerability, susceptibility, defenselessness|Antonyms: immunity, resistance, protection|Noun
