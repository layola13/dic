leprosy: /ˈlɛprəsi/ (US), /ˈlɛprəsi/ (UK)| n. |麻风病|A chronic, curable infectious disease mainly causing skin lesions and nerve damage.|例句：Leprosy is a disease that can be cured with multidrug therapy. 麻风病是一种可以通过多药治疗治愈的疾病。

例句：In the past, people with leprosy were often ostracized. 过去，麻风病人经常被排斥。

近义词：Hansen's disease (汉森氏病)|反义词: health (健康)|词性: noun

leper: /ˈlɛpər/ (US), /ˈlɛpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |麻风病人|A person afflicted with leprosy.|例句：In biblical times, lepers were considered unclean. 在圣经时代，麻风病人被认为是不洁净的。

例句：The leper colony was located on a remote island. 麻风病疗养院位于一个偏远的岛屿上。

近义词：Hansen's disease patient (汉森氏病患者)|反义词: healthy person (健康的人)|词性: noun
