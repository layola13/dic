crustaceologist:/krʌˈsteɪʃənɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 甲壳动物学家 | A scientist who studies crustaceans. |例句：The crustaceologist spent years studying the migration patterns of crabs.  这位甲壳动物学家花了数年时间研究螃蟹的迁徙模式。

例句：My uncle is a crustaceologist, and he knows everything about lobsters.  我的叔叔是一位甲壳动物学家，他对龙虾了如指掌。|近义词：marine biologist, zoologist |反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From crustacean + -ologist.  "Crustacean" comes from the Latin "crusta" meaning "shell, rind, bark." "-ologist" denotes "one who studies" and derives from the Greek "logos" meaning "word, reason, study."
