concierge:/kɒnˈsjɛːʒ/ (UK) /kɑːnˈsjɛːrʒ/ (US)| n.|门房，服务员|A hotel employee who assists guests with various tasks such as making reservations, arranging tours, and providing information.|The concierge helped us book a table at a popular restaurant.|Synonyms: porter, attendant, doorman, receptionist|Antonyms: guest, customer|noun

concierge:/kɒnˈsjɛːʒ/ (UK) /kɑːnˈsjɛːrʒ/ (US)| adj.|礼宾的，提供全方位服务的|Relating to or denoting a service that provides personalized assistance with various tasks.|We stayed at a concierge hotel with excellent service.|Synonyms: full-service, personalized, bespoke|Antonyms: basic, standard|adjective
