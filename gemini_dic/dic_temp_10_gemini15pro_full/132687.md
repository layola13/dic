nondisruptive:/ˌnɑːndɪsˈrʌptɪv/ | adj. | 无中断的，不扰乱的 | Not causing disruption or disturbance; allowing something to continue without interruption or interference. |  The software update was nondisruptive, allowing users to continue working without interruption.  该软件更新是无中断的，允许用户继续工作而不会中断。

The construction crew worked nondisruptively, minimizing noise and inconvenience to the residents. 建筑施工人员以无干扰的方式工作，最大限度地减少了对居民的噪音和不便。 | 近义词: seamless, smooth, unobtrusive, nonintrusive | 反义词: disruptive, disturbing, interfering, intrusive | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- (not) + disruptive (causing disruption).  Disruptive comes from the Latin disruptus, past participle of disrumpere (to break apart).
