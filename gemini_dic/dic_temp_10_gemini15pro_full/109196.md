leguleian:/ləˈɡuːliən/
adjective
迂腐的；吹毛求疵的；讼棍似的
Excessively concerned with the letter or technicalities of the law; pettifogging.
He responded with a leguleian argument about the wording of the contract. 他用一个关于合同措辞的迂腐的论点来回应。
The leguleian details of the case bored the jury.  案件中吹毛求疵的细节让陪审团感到厌烦。
近义词:pettifogging, legalistic, quibbling, caviling
反义词:straightforward, simple, uncomplicated
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *leguleius "pettifogger," perhaps from lex (genitive legis) "law"
