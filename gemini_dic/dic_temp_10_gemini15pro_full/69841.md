ferried:/ˈferid/
verb
运送，摆渡
to transport people or goods across a body of water in a boat or ship.
The passengers were ferried across the river.  乘客们被渡过了河。
The supplies were ferried to the island by boat.  物资用船运到了岛上。
transport, convey, carry, ship
-
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From ferry + -ed, past participle suffix.  Ferry comes from Old English *ferian "to carry, convey," from Proto-Germanic *farjanan, from Proto-Indo-European *per- "to transport, pass over."
