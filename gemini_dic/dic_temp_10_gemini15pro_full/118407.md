merriest:/ˈmeriɪst/| adj.|最快乐的，最愉快的|Superlative form of merry.  Expressing the highest degree of cheerfulness and joy.|例句：We wish you the merriest Christmas ever! 我们祝你圣诞快乐！|近义词：jolliest, happiest, most cheerful|反义词：saddest, gloomiest|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *merriest, superlative of *merrie, from Old English myrġe (“pleasant, sweet, agreeable, delightful, merry, joyful”), from Proto-Germanic *murgijaz (“short, brief, pleasant”), from Proto-Indo-European *mréǵʰus (“short”). Cognate with Dutch merg (“marrow”), German Mark (“marrow”), Old Norse mergr (“marrow”). More at marrow. The superlative suffix -est comes from Old English -est.
