connexion:/kəˈnɛkʃ(ə)n/| n.|联系，连接，关系|A connexion is a link or relationship between things or people.|例句：There is a clear connexion between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间有明显的联系。

例句：She benefited from her family's connexions in the business world.  她在商界得益于其家族的关系。|近义词：connection, link, relationship, association|反义词:separation, disconnection|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French connexion, from Latin connexio (“a binding together”), from connexus, past participle of connectere (“to bind together”), from com- (“together”) + nectere (“to bind”).  The spelling with an x is now less common, especially in American English.
