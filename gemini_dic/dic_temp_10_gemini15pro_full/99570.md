interindependences:/ˌɪntərɪndɪˈpɛndənsɪz/ | n. |相互依存，互相依赖 |The state or quality of being interindependent; mutual dependence between things.  It signifies a reciprocal relationship where elements rely on each other for functioning or existence. | The interindependences of the global economy make international cooperation crucial. 全球经济的相互依存使得国际合作至关重要。

The ecological interindependences within a forest are complex and delicate. 森林内的生态相互依存关系复杂而微妙。| 近义词：interconnectedness, interdependence, interrelationship | 反义词：independence, autonomy | 词性: 名词

词源: "inter-" (between) + "in-" (in, within) + "dependence" (reliance on something else).  The prefix "inter-" emphasizes the mutual or reciprocal nature of the dependence.
