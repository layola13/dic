hardenability:/ˌhɑːrdənəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 硬化能力，淬透性 | The hardenability of a metal determines its ability to be hardened by heat treatment.  金属的淬透性决定了它通过热处理硬化的能力。 | The hardenability test results will inform the appropriate heat treatment process. 淬透性测试结果将为合适的热处理工艺提供信息。| synonyms: hardening potential,  tempers | antonyms: softness, ductility | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From harden (verb) + -ability (suffix). "Harden" comes from Old English "heardian," meaning "to make hard," related to "heard" (hard). "-ability" comes from Old French "-ableté" and ultimately from Latin "-abilis," meaning "capable of."
