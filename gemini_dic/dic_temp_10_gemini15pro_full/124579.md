mournings:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋz/
n.
悲痛，哀悼；丧服
Expressions of grief or sorrow, especially after someone's death; clothes worn to express grief.
The queen wore black mournings for her late husband. 女王为她已故的丈夫穿了黑色的丧服。
Synonyms: grief, sorrow, bereavement, lament, mourning clothes, weeds
Antonyms: celebration, rejoicing, joy, festivity
noun
