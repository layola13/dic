half-consumed:/ˌhæf kənˈsuːmd/
| adj. | 半消耗的，吃了一半的 | Partially consumed; not fully finished or used up. |  The half-consumed bottle of wine sat on the table. 桌上放着一瓶喝了一半的葡萄酒。| Partly eaten, partially used | Fully consumed, untouched | adjective


half-consumed:/ˌhæf kənˈsuːmd/
| n. |  吃了一半的东西 | Something that has been partially consumed. | He threw away the half-consumed sandwich. 他把吃了一半的三明治扔掉了。| Leftovers, remnants | Whole, complete | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" from Old English "healf," meaning "one of two equal parts."
"consumed" from Latin "consumere," meaning "to use up completely, devour, destroy."
