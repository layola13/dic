by-rote:/baɪ ˈroʊt/
| adv.| 死记硬背地，机械地 | learned or done by rote; memorized by repetition, often without understanding. | She learned her multiplication tables by rote. 她靠死记硬背学会了乘法表。| 近义词: mechanically, automatically | 反义词: thoughtfully, analytically | 词性: 副词
by-rote:/baɪ ˈroʊt/
| adj.| 死记硬背的，机械的 | done by rote; memorized by repetition, often without understanding. |  His by-rote recitation of the poem lacked feeling.  他死记硬背的诗歌朗诵缺乏感情。 | 近义词: mechanical, automatic | 反义词: thoughtful, analytical | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Middle English *bi rote*, from *bi* ("by") + *rote* ("rote").  The word "rote" itself comes from Old French *rote* ("hurdy-gurdy," hence "repetition"), ultimately from Vulgar Latin *rota* ("wheel"). The idea is of something turning repeatedly, like a wheel.
