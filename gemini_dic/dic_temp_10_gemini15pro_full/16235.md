ban:/bæn/
noun
禁令，禁止
An official or legal prohibition.
例句：There is a ban on smoking in public places.  公共场所禁止吸烟。
例句：The government imposed a ban on the import of certain goods. 政府禁止进口某些商品。
近义词：prohibition, embargo, restriction
反义词：allowance, permission

ban:/bæn/
verb
禁止，取缔
To officially or legally prohibit.
例句：The government banned the sale of alcohol to minors. 政府禁止向未成年人出售酒精。
例句：The book was banned for its controversial content. 这本书因其有争议的内容而被禁。
近义词：prohibit, forbid, outlaw
反义词：allow, permit
