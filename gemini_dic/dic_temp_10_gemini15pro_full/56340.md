dogshore:/ˈdɒɡʃɔːr/ (UK), /ˈdɔːɡʃɔːr/ (US)
noun
海滨散步场所；海滨公园
A place for walking along the seashore, especially a paved promenade.
The dogshore was crowded with people enjoying the sunshine.  海滨公园里挤满了享受阳光的人们。
promenade, esplanade, beachfront, waterfront
hinterland, inland


Etymology:
dog + shore
"dog" in this context likely refers to a dog's habit of walking along the shore.  "shore" refers to the land bordering a body of water.
