nonbeatifically: /nɒnˌbiːətɪfɪkəli/
词性: adverb
中文翻译: 不快乐地，不幸福地，不狂喜地
英文解析: In a manner that is not beatific; without bliss or extreme joy.  It describes actions or states that lack the characteristic of supreme happiness.
例句: He sighed nonbeatifically, realizing his vacation was over. 他不快乐地叹了口气，意识到他的假期结束了。

例句: The news was delivered nonbeatifically, reflecting the somber mood.  这个消息传达得并不令人愉快，反映了沉闷的气氛。


近义词: unhappily, joylessly, miserably, dismally, gloomily
反义词: beatifically, blissfully, joyfully, ecstatically, happily


词源:  The word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "beatifically," which is the adverbial form of "beatific." "Beatific" comes from the Late Latin "beatificus," from Latin "beatus" (blessed) + "-ficus" (making).
