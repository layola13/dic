bobsled:/ˈbɑːbsled/ (US) /ˈbɒbsled/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|雪橇，雪车，乘坐雪橇|A long racing sled for two or more people, typically steered by a person in the front and braked by a person at the rear.|例句：The team trained hard for the bobsled competition.  这个队伍为雪橇比赛刻苦训练。

例句：They bobsledded down the icy track at incredible speeds. 他们以惊人的速度乘坐雪橇滑下冰道。|近义词：sled, luge|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词，动词


Etymology:  From bob (verb, meaning "to move quickly up and down") + sled.  The sport originated in Switzerland in the late 19th century.
