microdegree:/ˈmaɪkroʊdɪɡriː/
n.
微学位，微证书
A mini-qualification, less extensive than a full degree, that demonstrates competency in a specific professional area.
She earned a microdegree in data analysis to enhance her job prospects. 她获得了数据分析的微学位，以提高她的就业前景。
He completed a microdegree in project management online. 他在线完成了项目管理的微学位。
近义词:nanodegree, certification, mini-qualification
反义词:degree, diploma
词性:noun
