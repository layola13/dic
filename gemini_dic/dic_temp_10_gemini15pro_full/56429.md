dollies:/ˈdɑːliz/ /ˈdɒliz/| n.|手推车；玩偶；移动摄影车|Small wheeled platforms for moving heavy objects, or child's toys resembling small humans.|The stagehands used dollies to move the scenery.  舞台工作人员用手推车搬运布景。| The little girl loved playing with her dollies. 小女孩喜欢玩她的洋娃娃。|近义词：carts, platforms, puppets, dolls|反义词:(No clear antonym)|noun
