gardant:/ˈɡɑːrdənt/
adj.
警戒的，注意的; (纹章学)守护的，(指动物)直立的，面向观察者的
Full face; vigilant; watchful.  (Heraldry)  Said of a beast depicted full-faced.
The lion gardant on the shield symbolized courage and vigilance. 盾牌上的直立雄狮象征着勇气和警惕。
近义词:watchful, vigilant, alert
反义词:inattentive, negligent, careless
词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *gardant (Modern French gardant), present participle of garder (“to guard, watch, keep”), from Frankish *wardōn (“to watch, guard”), from Proto-Germanic *wardōną (“to watch, guard”), from Proto-Indo-European *wer- (“to perceive, watch out for”).
