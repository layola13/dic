lipaemia:/laɪˈpiːmiə/ /lɪˈpimiə/
noun
血脂过多，脂血症
The presence of excess lipids (fats) in the blood.
High levels of triglycerides in the blood, sometimes causing it to appear milky.
例句：Lipaemia can interfere with some blood tests.  脂血症会干扰某些血液测试。

例句：The patient's blood sample showed marked lipaemia.  该病人的血液样本显示出明显的脂血症。
近义词：hyperlipidemia, hyperlipemia
反义词:  hypolipidemia (although not a direct opposite, it represents the opposite condition of low blood lipids)
词性: noun
