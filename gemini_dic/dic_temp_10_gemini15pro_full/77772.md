generability:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈbɪləti/
n.|可生成性，可产生性|The capability of something to be generated or produced.  The quality of being generalizable.|The model demonstrated high generability, producing a variety of outputs.|Synonym: productivity, fecundity, fertility|Antonym: sterility, barrenness|noun

generability:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈbɪləti/
n.|泛化性，概括性|The extent to which findings can be applied to a wider population or context.|The study's small sample size limited its generability.|Synonym: generalizability, applicability, universality|Antonym: specificity, particularity|noun
