adulterously: /əˈdʌltərəsli/
adverb
通奸地，不忠地
In a way that involves adultery, unfaithfully.
He behaved adulterously throughout his marriage.  他整个婚姻期间都行为不忠。
She admitted to having acted adulterously. 她承认有不忠的行为。

近义词: unfaithfully, disloyally, treacherously
反义词: faithfully, loyally

词性: adverb (副词)

词源:
From Late Latin *adulterōsus, from Latin adulter, adulterer.

USAGE->
She adulterously pursued a relationship with her co-worker. 她不忠地与同事发展关系。
He lived adulterously for years, deceiving both his wife and his mistress. 他多年来一直过着不忠的生活，欺骗了他的妻子和情妇。
