dismissal:/dɪsˈmɪsəl/ | n. | 解雇，免职，解散，驳回 |  The act of sending someone away or allowing them to leave; the act of refusing to consider something. | The company announced the dismissal of 100 employees. 公司宣布解雇100名员工。 | He received a dismissal notice last week. 他上周收到了解雇通知。| synonyms: discharge, termination, sacking, redundancy, rejection | antonyms: hiring, employment, retention, acceptance | noun
