epipubis:/ˌɛpɪˈpjuːbɪs/
n.
耻骨前骨，前耻骨
A pair of bones projecting forward from the pelvic girdle in marsupials and some other mammals.
Marsupials have epipubic bones.  有袋动物有耻骨前骨。
近义词:  prepubic bone (更偏向专业术语)
反义词:  无
词性: 名词
