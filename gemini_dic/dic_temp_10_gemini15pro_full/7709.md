anomie: /ˈænəmi/ (US), /ˈænəmi/ (UK)| n. | 社会失范，道德沦丧 | A condition of instability resulting from a breakdown of standards and values or from a lack of purpose or ideals. |  The rapid social changes led to a period of anomie.  社会急剧变化导致了一段时间的社会失范。

| Example: The sudden wealth experienced by some during the gold rush created a sense of anomie in the boomtowns.  淘金热期间一些人突然暴富，在兴盛的城镇里造成了一种社会失范感。| Synonym: lawlessness, normlessness, alienation, social disintegration | Antonym: social cohesion, order, stability | Noun
