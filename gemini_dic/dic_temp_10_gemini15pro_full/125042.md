mulberry:/ˈmʌlˌberi/ (US), /ˈmʌlbəri/ (UK)| n.|桑葚；桑树|A tree that bears mulberries, or the edible fruit of this tree.  The fruit is typically dark purple or red when ripe.|The birds feasted on the ripe mulberries. 鸟儿尽情享用成熟的桑葚。|Synonyms: blackberry, raspberry|Antonyms: None|Noun

mulberry:/ˈmʌlˌberi/ (US), /ˈmʌlbəri/ (UK)| adj.|桑葚色的；紫红色的|Of a dark reddish-purple colour.|She wore a mulberry-coloured dress. 她穿着一件紫红色的连衣裙。|Synonyms: purple, maroon|Antonyms: None|Adjective
