go-begging:/ɡəʊ ˈbeɡɪŋ/ | idiom | 乏人问津；无人理会；待价而沽 | to be unwanted or available but not taken |  The house has been going begging for months. 这房子几个月来一直无人问津。| unsold, unwanted, unclaimed | sought-after, desirable | idiom

go-begging:/ɡəʊ ˈbeɡɪŋ/ | idiom | 乞求；恳求 | to ask for something humbly or persistently |  He went begging for a second chance. 他恳求再给他一次机会。 | plead, implore, beseech | demand, insist | idiom
