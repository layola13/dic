bleaberry:/ˈbliːˌbɛri/| n.|越橘，欧洲越橘|A small, dark blue, edible berry, similar to the bilberry, that grows on heathland.  A common name for several species of low-growing shrubs of the genus Vaccinium.|I picked a handful of bleaberry while hiking in the Scottish Highlands. 我在苏格兰高地徒步旅行时摘了一把越橘。|近义词：bilberry, whortleberry, huckleberry|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
词源: From Middle English *bleberie, probably a compound of Old Norse *blaber (dark blue, livid) and Old English berie (berry).
