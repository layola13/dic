carriageway:/ˈkærɪdʒweɪ/| n.|车道，行车道|The part of a road intended for vehicles, not pedestrians. |Drive carefully on the carriageway. 在行车道上小心驾驶。|近义词：roadway, lane, highway|反义词:pavement, sidewalk|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cariage wei*, from *cariage* (carriage) + *wei* (way).  The word originally referred to any road or way suitable for carriages, but later came to specifically mean the part of the road dedicated to vehicular traffic.
