misstyled:/ˌmɪsˈstaɪld/
adjective
样式错误的，称呼错误的
incorrectly or inappropriately styled or named
The misstyled "expert" made several factual errors.  这位所谓的“专家”犯了几个事实性错误。
The document referred to him by a misstyled title.  这份文件用一个错误的称号称呼他。
近义词:misnamed, wrongly designated, incorrectly labeled
反义词:correctly styled, appropriately named
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- + styled, past participle of style.  Mis- 表示“错误地”、“不适当地”。 Style 在这里指“命名”、“称呼”或“设计样式”。
