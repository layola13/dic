flaily:/ˈfleɪli/| adj.|挥舞的，乱动的|Moving or waving energetically in an uncontrolled way.  Characterized by uncontrolled or erratic movements.|Her flaily arms nearly knocked over a vase. 她乱挥的手臂差点把花瓶打翻了。|近义词：wild, erratic, uncontrolled, jerky|反义词:controlled, steady, calm|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From the verb "flail" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Flail" comes from Old French "flaiel" (threshing tool), ultimately from Latin "flagellum" (whip, scourge).
