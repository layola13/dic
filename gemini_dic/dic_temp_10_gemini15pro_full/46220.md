cystidea:/sɪˈstɪdiə/| n. |海蕾纲动物；囊胞动物|An extinct class of echinoderms, related to blastoids and crinoids, that existed from the Ordovician to the Devonian periods and were characterized by a saclike body enclosed in plates.|Fossil cystidea were found in the Ordovician strata. 在奥陶纪地层中发现了海蕾纲动物化石。|近义词:blastoid, crinoid|反义词:None|noun
