hippiehood:/ˈhɪpihʊd/| n.|嬉皮士身份，嬉皮士的时代|The book offers a nostalgic look back at his years in hippiehood. 这本书怀旧地回顾了他嬉皮士时代的生活。|近义词:hippiedom|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑用 mainstream society 主流社会)|词性:名词

Etymology:  From hippie + -hood.  "Hippie" itself is likely a back-formation from "hipster," which emerged in the 1940s jazz scene, referring to those who were "hip" or aware of the latest trends.  "-hood" is a suffix denoting a state, condition, quality, or period of being.
