incomposed:/ˌɪnkəmˈpoʊzd/ | adj. | 不镇定的，不安的，慌乱的 | Not composed; disturbed; agitated. |  He appeared incomposed at the podium, fidgeting with his notes. 他在讲台上显得不安，不停地摆弄着笔记。|  The witness gave an incomposed account of the accident, her voice trembling. 目击者慌乱地讲述了事故经过，声音颤抖。 | 近义词: disturbed, agitated, flustered, ruffled, disconcerted | 反义词: calm, composed, serene, tranquil | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From in- (not) + composed (calm and self-possessed).  The word "composed" ultimately comes from the Latin "componere" (to put together).
