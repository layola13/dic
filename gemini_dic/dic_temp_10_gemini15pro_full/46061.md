cyclograph: /ˈsaɪkləɡræf/ /ˈsaɪkloʊɡræf/
noun
循环描记器，振动描记器
An instrument that records vibrations or motions in a cyclical pattern.
The engineer used a cyclograph to analyze the vibrations of the bridge. 工程师使用循环描记器来分析桥梁的振动。

近义词: oscillograph, vibrograph
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider words related to static measurement like "micrometer" if context allows)
词性: n.
