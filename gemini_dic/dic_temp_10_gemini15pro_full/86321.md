hebrewism:/ˈhiːbruːɪzəm/ | n. |希伯来语用法；希伯来风格|A word, phrase, or idiom derived from Hebrew or characteristic of the Hebrew language or culture.  |The use of "eye for an eye" is a common hebrewism.  “以眼还眼”的用法是一种常见的希伯来语用法。|近义词：hebraism |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun

hebrewism:/ˈhiːbruːɪzəm/ | n. |希伯来主义；犹太风格|A custom, practice, or mode of thought characteristic of the Hebrews or Jews. |Celebrating the Sabbath is a key hebrewism in Jewish culture. 庆祝安息日是犹太文化中一个重要的希伯来主义。|近义词：judaism |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
