flecklessly:/ˈflɛklɪsli/
adjective
无瑕疵地，完美无瑕地
without any flecks or spots; immaculately clean or pure
Her skin was flecklessly smooth. 她的皮肤光滑无瑕。
近义词:immaculately, flawlessly, spotlessly
反义词:spotted, blemished, flawed
词性:adjective

flecklessly:/ˈflɛklɪsli/
词源: From fleckless + -ly. Fleckless is from fleck + -less. Fleck is from Old Norse flekkr "spot, stain."
英文解析: In a manner free from flecks or spots; perfectly clean or pure.  It describes something that is entirely free of any marks, blemishes, or imperfections.
例句:The newly polished car gleamed flecklessly in the sunlight.  新抛光的汽车在阳光下闪闪发光，一尘不染。

例句:She presented a flecklessly clean house to the potential buyers. 她向潜在买家展示了一尘不染的房子。
近义词:immaculately, flawlessly, spotlessly, perfectly
反义词:spotted, blemished, stained, flawed, imperfect
词性:adverb
