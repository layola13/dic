federal:/ˈfɛdərəl/ | adj. | 联邦的；同盟的 | Relating to a system of government in which several states form a unity but remain independent in internal affairs. | The federal government is responsible for national defense.  联邦政府负责国防。|  national, central | local, state | adjective

federal:/ˈfɛdərəl/ | n. | 联邦主义者；同盟盟员 | A supporter of a federal system of government. | He was a staunch federal in the early days of the republic. 他是共和国早期坚定的联邦主义者。| unionist | separatist | noun
