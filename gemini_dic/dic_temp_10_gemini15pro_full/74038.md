foryete:/fɔːrˈjɛtə/ | v. |忘记|To lose the remembrance of; to let slip from the memory.|He foryete his promise. 他忘记了他的承诺。|例句：She completely foryete about our meeting. 她完全忘记了我们的会议。|近义词：forget, overlook, disregard|反义词: remember, recall|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *foryeten, from Old English *forġietan, from Proto-Germanic *farʒetaną (“to forget”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰed- (“to leave behind, lack”). Cognate with Scots foryet (“to forget”), West Frisian ferjitte (“to forget”), Dutch vergeten (“to forget”), German vergessen (“to forget”), Swedish förgäta (“to forget”).
