friskiest:/ˈfrɪskiɪst/
adjective
最活泼的，最欢跃的
Most playful and lively; full of energy and high spirits.
The friskiest puppy in the litter was chasing its tail.  这窝小狗中最活泼的那只正在追着自己的尾巴跑。
近义词:liveliest, most energetic, most playful
反义词:calmest, quietest, most subdued
词性:adjective


frisky:/ˈfrɪski/
adjective
活泼的，欢跃的
Playful and lively; full of energy and high spirits.
The lambs were frisky in the spring sunshine. 春天的阳光下，小羊羔们欢快地跳跃着。
近义词:playful, lively, spirited
反义词:lethargic, sluggish, subdued
词性:adjective
