chloroform: /ˈklɔːrəfɔːrm/ (US) /ˈklɒrəfɔːm/ (UK)| n.|氯仿|A colorless, volatile, sweet-smelling liquid used as a solvent and formerly as a general anesthetic.|例句：Chloroform was once widely used as an anesthetic. 氯仿曾被广泛用作麻醉剂。

例句：He used a chloroform-soaked rag to subdue his victim. 他用一块浸泡了氯仿的破布制服了他的受害者。|近义词：anesthetic, solvent|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps stimulant)|词性:noun
