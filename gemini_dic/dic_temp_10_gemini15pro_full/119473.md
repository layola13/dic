micromillimeter:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmɪlɪˌmiːtər/| n. |微毫米，百万分之一毫米|A unit of length equal to one millionth of a millimeter (10^-9 meters).  It is also called a nanometer.|The wavelength of visible light is measured in micromillimeters (nanometers). 可见光的波长是用微毫米（纳米）来测量的。|Synonym: nanometer|Antonym: kilometer|noun
