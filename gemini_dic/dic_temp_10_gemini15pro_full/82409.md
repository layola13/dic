grudges:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪz/| n. |怨恨，不满（grudge的复数形式）|plural form of "grudge"|He holds no grudges against anyone. 他对任何人都不怀恨在心。|近义词：resentments, animosities|反义词：pardons, mercies|noun
