cockneyer:/ˈkɒkniə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkniər/ (US)| n.|伦敦东区人，操伦敦东区口音的人|A native or inhabitant of the East End of London, traditionally one born within earshot of the Bow Bells; also, a person who speaks with a Cockney accent.|例句：He's a true cockneyer, born and bred in the East End. 他是一个地地道道的伦敦东区人，在东区出生长大。

例句：You can tell she's a cockneyer by her accent. 你可以从她的口音听出她是一个伦敦东区人。|近义词：East Ender, Londoner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone from outside London/rural areas)|词性:noun
