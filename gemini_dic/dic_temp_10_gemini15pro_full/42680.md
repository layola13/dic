countersuggestion:/ˈkaʊntər səˈdʒɛstʃən/ /ˈkaʊntɚ səˈdʒɛstʃən/
noun
反建议，对策建议
an alternative suggestion made in response to a previous suggestion
例句：
He offered a countersuggestion to their proposal.
他针对他们的提议提出了一个反建议。

Her countersuggestion was to postpone the meeting.
她的反建议是推迟会议。

近义词：alternative, counterproposal, rebuttal
反义词：suggestion, proposal
词性：n.
