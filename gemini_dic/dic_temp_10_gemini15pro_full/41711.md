corroborative:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtɪv/
adjective
确证的，证实的
serving to support or confirm
The witness provided corroborative evidence that supported the victim's testimony.  证人提供了确凿的证据，支持了受害人的证词。
corroborating, confirmatory, supporting, validating
contradictory, conflicting, opposing, refuting
