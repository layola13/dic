atoningly:/əˈtoʊnɪŋli/| adv.|赎罪地，补偿地|In a way that makes amends or expresses remorse for a wrong or injury.|He gazed at her atoningly. 他赎罪似地凝视着她。|近义词: remorsefully, penitently, apologetically|反义词: unrepentantly, defiantly|词性:副词

Etymology: From "atone" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Atone" comes from "at one" meaning to bring into agreement or harmony.
