nonpreservative:/ˌnɒnprɪˈzɜːvətɪv/ | adj.|无防腐剂的|Not containing or using preservatives.  A substance that prevents decay.  Often used to describe food or cosmetics.| This jam is nonpreservative, so it needs to be refrigerated.  这种果酱不含防腐剂，所以需要冷藏。| 近义词:preservative-free| 反义词:preserved, with preservatives| 词性:形容词

nonpreservative:/ˌnɒnprɪˈzɜːrvətɪv/ | n.|非防腐剂|A substance that does *not* act as a preservative.|Some natural ingredients act as nonpreservatives, actually accelerating spoilage.  一些天然成分起到了非防腐剂的作用，实际上加速了腐败变质。| 近义词: (As an adjective) preservative-free | 反义词:preservative | 词性:名词
