contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/
verb
违反，违背，抵触
to do something that is not allowed by a law or rule
例句：The company knew its actions contravened international law. 该公司知道其行为违反了国际法。

例句：Some portions of the bill may contravene state law. 该法案的某些部分可能违反州法律。


近义词：violate, breach, infringe, disobey, defy
反义词：obey, comply with, uphold, observe
词性：v.
