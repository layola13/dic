out-hissing:/ˌaʊtˈhɪsɪŋ/
verb
发出比...更大的嘶嘶声
Producing a hissing sound louder or more forcefully than another.
The steam engine out-hissed the escaping gas. 蒸汽机发出的嘶嘶声盖过了泄漏的气体的声音。
The angry goose out-hissed the cat. 那只愤怒的鹅发出比猫更大的嘶嘶声。
近义词: out-sounding, overpowering
反义词: hushed, muted
词性: verb (present participle)

词源:
"Out-" (prefix) + "hiss" (verb)

"Out-"  as a prefix often implies exceeding or surpassing something else. "Hiss" comes from the imitative sound of air escaping or snakes making a threatening sound.  Therefore, "out-hissing" literally means hissing louder than something else.
