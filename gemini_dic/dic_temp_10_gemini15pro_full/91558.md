hyper-nutrition:/ˌhaɪpərnuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |过度营养，营养过剩 |A condition of excessive intake of nutrients, often leading to health problems. | Example 1:  Hyper-nutrition can contribute to obesity and other health issues. 过度营养会导致肥胖和其他健康问题。

Example 2:  The child's hyper-nutrition was evident in his rapid weight gain.  这个孩子营养过剩的迹象很明显，他的体重增长很快。| Near Synonyms: overnutrition, excessive nutrition | Antonyms: malnutrition, undernutrition | noun

Etymology: From 'hyper-' (Greek: 'over, excessive') + 'nutrition' (Latin: 'nourishment').
