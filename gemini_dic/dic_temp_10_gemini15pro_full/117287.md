mechanurgy:/məˈkænərdʒi/ | n. | 机械魔术，机械魔法 | The art or practice of constructing and operating mechanical devices, often imbued with magical or supernatural qualities in fictional contexts.  It combines mechanics and magic, often involving the creation of automatons, golems, or other artificial beings. |  Example 1: The dwarves of the mountain fortress were renowned for their mechanurgy, crafting intricate clockwork guardians.  (山间堡垒的矮人以其机械魔术而闻名，他们制造了复杂的钟表守卫。)

Example 2:  Her novel explored the fascinating world of mechanurgy, where magic powered complex machines. (她的小说探索了迷人的机械魔法世界，在那里魔法驱动着复杂的机器。) | Near Synonyms:  thaumaturgic engineering, magic engineering, technomagic | Antonym:  natural magic | noun
