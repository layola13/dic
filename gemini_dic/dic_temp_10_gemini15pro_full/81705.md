greenhouses: /ˈɡriːnhaʊsɪz/ | n. | 温室；暖房 | A structure with walls and roof made chiefly of transparent material, such as glass, in which plants requiring regulated climatic conditions are grown. | The greenhouses were filled with exotic plants.  温室里充满了异国情调的植物。|  The botanist cultivated rare orchids in his greenhouses.  植物学家在他的温室里培育珍稀的兰花。 | 近义词: hothouse, glasshouse, conservatory | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑outdoor garden) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From "green" + "house."  The "green" refers not to the color of the structure but to the greenery grown within.
