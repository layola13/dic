demarcation:/ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃn/ |n.|界限，分界；划分|The demarcation between the two countries is a river. 这两个国家的分界线是一条河。|The demarcation between right and wrong isn't always clear. 正确与错误之间的界限并不总是清晰的。|近义词：boundary, division, separation, distinction|反义词：union, connection, integration|词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *demarcatio(n-), from Latin demārcare “to mark out, define,” from de- “out, completely” + mārcare “to mark,” from marca “mark, boundary.”
