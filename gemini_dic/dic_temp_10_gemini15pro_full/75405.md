fructification:/ˌfruːktɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 结果实；子实体；成就；实现 |The process of bearing or producing fruit; the state of being fruitful; the fruiting body of a fungus; the realization of something, such as a plan or project. | The fructification of his plans took many years. 他的计划的实现花了许多年。| Near Synonyms: fruiting, ripening, fruition, consummation, achievement, realization | Antonyms: barrenness, sterility, failure | noun

fructification:/ˌfruːktɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 结果实；子实体；成就；实现 | The appearance of fruiting bodies, especially of fungi. | We observed the fructification of several different mushroom species. 我们观察了几种不同蘑菇的子实体。 | Near Synonyms: fruiting body, sporocarp | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps mycelium - the vegetative part of a fungus) | noun
