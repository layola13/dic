diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ /ˌdaɪəˈriːɑː/| n.|腹泻|frequent passage of loose, watery stools.  A condition in which feces are discharged from the bowels frequently and in a liquid form.|He had a bout of diarrhea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后腹泻了一阵。|近义词：the runs, the trots, loose bowels|反义词: constipation|词性:noun
diarrhoea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ | n.|腹泻 (英式拼写)|British spelling of diarrhea.  Frequent passage of loose, watery stools. |She suffered from diarrhoea during her trip.  她在旅途中腹泻了。|近义词：the runs, the trots, loose bowels|反义词: constipation|词性:noun
