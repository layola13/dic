homogeneity:/ˌhoʊmədʒəˈniːɪti/ /ˌhɒmədʒəˈneɪəti/ | n. | 同质性，同种性，均匀性 | The state or quality of being homogeneous; similarity in kind or nature throughout. |  The researcher noted the homogeneity of the sample group. 研究人员注意到样本组的同质性。| synonyms: uniformity, consistency, similarity, sameness | antonyms: heterogeneity, diversity, variety | noun

homogenous:/həˈmɒdʒɪnəs/ /həˈmɑːdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 同质的，同类的，均匀的 |  Of the same kind or nature; similar in structure or composition throughout. | The mixture was homogenous, with no lumps or variations in color. 这种混合物是均匀的，没有块状物或颜色变化。| synonyms: uniform, consistent, similar, alike | antonyms: heterogeneous, diverse, varied | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin homogenes, from Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs, “of the same kind”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”).
