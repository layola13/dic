cullet:/ˈkʌlɪt/
n.
碎玻璃，玻璃碎片，回收的碎玻璃
Broken or waste glass, especially that recovered for remelting.
The factory uses cullet to make new bottles.  这家工厂用碎玻璃制造新瓶子。
synonyms:glass shards, broken glass, waste glass
antonyms: new glass, intact glass
noun
