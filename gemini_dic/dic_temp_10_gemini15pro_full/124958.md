muffling:/ˈmʌflɪŋ/| n. v.| 闷声；消音；抑制；包裹 |The act of wrapping or covering something to deaden its sound or conceal it.  The act of suppressing or restraining something. | The muffling of the drums made the music sound distant.  鼓声的消音使音乐听起来很遥远。|近义词：stifling, deadening, suppressing, muting, quieting|反义词: amplifying, enhancing, revealing|词性: noun, present participle of muffle

muffle:/ˈmʌfl/| v. n.|捂住；使声音降低；抑制；裹住|To wrap or cover for warmth or protection. To make a sound quieter or less distinct. To suppress or restrain.|She muffled her cough with her hand. 她用手捂住咳嗽。|近义词：stifle, deaden, suppress, mute, quiet|反义词: amplify, enhance, reveal|词性: verb, noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *muffelen,  frequentative of muffen, from Old French moufler "to cover the mouth," perhaps of imitative origin.
