degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/| v. |降低；贬低；使堕落；分解；降解|to reduce someone to a lower rank, position, or condition; to make something worse in quality; to cause something to break down into simpler substances|Exposure to sunlight degrades many plastics. 阳光照射会使许多塑料降解。|近义词：debase, demean, deteriorate, worsen, decompose, break down|反义词：enhance, improve, elevate, upgrade|词性:动词
