inaccuracies:/ɪnˈækjərəsiz/ | n. | 不准确，误差，错误 | plural of inaccuracy | The report was riddled with inaccuracies.  这份报告充满了错误。 | synonyms: errors, mistakes, fallacies, discrepancies | antonyms: accuracies, precision, correctness | noun

Etymology:
From in- (not) + accuracy. Accuracy from Latin accuratus "prepared with care, exact," past participle of accurare "to take care of," from ad- "to" + curare "take care of."
