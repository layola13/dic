nonfragilities:/ˌnɒnfrəˈdʒɪlətiz/ | n. | 非脆弱性；反脆弱性 |  The quality of not just resisting change but thriving and improving because of stressors, volatility, and randomness. It's the opposite of fragility, where things break under pressure.  | The book "Antifragile" explores the concept of nonfragilities in various systems, from financial markets to personal health.  | 近义词: resilience, robustness, antifragility | 反义词: fragility, vulnerability, weakness | 词性: noun
