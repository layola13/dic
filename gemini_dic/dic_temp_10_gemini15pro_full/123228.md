monocotyledonous:/ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ /ˌmɑːnəˌkɑːtɪˈliːdənəs/ | adj. | 单子叶的 |  Relating to or denoting plants of the class Monocotyledonae (or Liliopsida), characterized by having only one cotyledon in the seed, and typically parallel-veined leaves, fibrous roots, and floral parts in multiples of three. |  Examples:  Corn is a monocotyledonous plant. 玉米是一种单子叶植物。  Most grasses are monocotyledonous. 大多数草本植物是单子叶植物。 | 近义词: monocot | 反义词: dicotyledonous, dicot | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mono- (one) + cotyledon (seed leaf) + -ous (forming adjectives).  The term "cotyledon" comes from the Greek kotylēdōn, meaning "cup-shaped cavity."
