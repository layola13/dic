fuju: /ˈfuːdʒuː/ | n. | 符咒;护身符 | A Japanese amulet or charm believed to possess magical powers, often inscribed with Buddhist or Shinto prayers and symbols, and carried for protection, good luck, or other benefits. |  She always carries a small fuju in her purse for good luck. 她总是在钱包里放一个小符咒以求好运。| synonym: amulet, charm, talisman | antonym: curse, jinx | noun
