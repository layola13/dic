bimana:/baɪˈmeɪnə/
n.
two-handed mammals;  (especially) humans
Two-handed or two-footed mammals, specifically referring to humans as the only extant members of this group.
Humans are considered bimana. 人类被认为是两手动物。
Bimana are characterized by their opposable thumbs.  两手动物的特点是具有对生拇指。
近义词: hominid, human
反义词: quadruped
词性: noun
