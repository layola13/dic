diversifying:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 多样化，使多样化 |  The company is diversifying its product range to appeal to a wider market.  该公司正在使其产品范围多样化，以吸引更广阔的市场。 | Example: They are diversifying their investments to reduce risk. 他们在分散投资以降低风险。 | Synonyms: varying, broadening, expanding, branching out | Antonyms: specializing, focusing, narrowing, concentrating | verb

diversified:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 多样化的，多元化的 |  She has a diversified portfolio of investments. 她拥有多元化的投资组合。 | Example: The museum has a diversified collection of art from around the world.  这家博物馆收藏了来自世界各地的各种艺术品。 | Synonyms: varied, assorted, multifaceted, heterogeneous | Antonyms: specialized, uniform, homogeneous, single | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin diversus "different" + -ify.  Ultimately from Latin divertere "to turn aside," from dis- "apart" + vertere "to turn."
