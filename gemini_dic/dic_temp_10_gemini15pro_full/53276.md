dirk:/dɜːrk/| n.|匕首，短剑|A long, straight-bladed dagger. A dirk is a type of dagger with a long, straight blade. It is often associated with Scottish Highland dress.|He drew his dirk and defended himself against the attacker. 他拔出匕首，抵抗袭击者。|近义词：dagger, knife, blade|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "sheath")|词性:noun
