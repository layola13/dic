co-proprietor:/ˌkoʊ prəˈpraɪətər/| n. |共同所有人，共同业主|A co-proprietor is someone who owns something jointly with one or more other people.|John and Mary are co-proprietors of the bakery.  约翰和玛丽是这家面包店的共同所有人。| The siblings are co-proprietors of a large estate. 这几个兄弟姐妹是一个大庄园的共同业主。 |近义词：joint owner, co-owner|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + proprietor.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "jointly," "together," or "mutually." "Proprietor" comes from the Latin word "proprietarius," meaning "owner."
