drow:/draʊ/ | n. |  黑暗精灵 | A malevolent elf-like creature from underground, often associated with Lolth, the spider goddess. | The drow emerged from the caves, their eyes glowing in the dim light. 黑暗精灵从洞穴中出来，他们的眼睛在昏暗的光线下闪闪发光。| The drow warriors attacked the unsuspecting village. 黑暗精灵战士袭击了毫无防备的村庄。 |  Near synonyms: dark elf, deep elf | Antonyms: surface elf, high elf | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *draugr, meaning "phantom" or "ghost." While the modern usage specifically refers to the dark elves of Dungeons & Dragons, the original meaning relates to supernatural beings. The shift in meaning occurred within the context of D&D lore.
