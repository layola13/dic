gauntnesses:/ˈɡɔːntnəsɪz/
noun
消瘦；憔悴；荒凉
The state of being gaunt; thinness, especially from hunger or suffering.
His gauntnesses were a testament to the hardships he had endured. 他的消瘦证明了他所经历的苦难。
synonyms:  emaciation, thinness, leanness, boniness, slenderness, skinniness
antonyms:  plumpness, stoutness, robustness, chubbiness
