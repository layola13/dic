munificent:/mjuːˈnɪfɪs(ə)nt/
adjective
慷慨的；丰厚的
Characterized by great generosity or lavishness.
He made a munificent donation to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。

The university received a munificent gift from an anonymous donor. 这所大学收到了一位匿名捐赠者慷慨的捐赠。

近义词:generous, lavish, bountiful, philanthropic
反义词:stingy, miserly, parsimonious, meager
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin munificus (“bountiful, liberal, generous”), from munus (“gift, service, duty, office”) + -ficus (making, causing).
