gorgeous:/ˈɡɔːrdʒəs/ (US) /ˈɡɔːdʒəs/ (UK)| adj.|华丽的，灿烂的，极好的|Very beautiful or pleasant; giving great pleasure.|例句：She looked gorgeous in that dress. 她穿那件衣服看起来真漂亮。|例句：We had a gorgeous meal at the restaurant. 我们在那家餐馆吃了一顿美味佳肴。|近义词：beautiful, stunning, splendid, magnificent, lovely|反义词：ugly, hideous, unattractive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *gorgayous, *gorgious, from Old French gorgias (“elegant, fashionable”), from gorgias (“ruff, wimple”), from gorge (“throat, neck”). The sense development is probably by way of the rich adornment of neck and throat.
