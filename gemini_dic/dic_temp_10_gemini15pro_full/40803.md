convoking:/kənˈvoʊkɪŋ/
verb
召集，召开
The act of calling a group of people together for a meeting or assembly.
The president is convoking a meeting of his cabinet. 总统正在召集内阁会议。
assemble, summon, convene
dismiss, adjourn
v.
