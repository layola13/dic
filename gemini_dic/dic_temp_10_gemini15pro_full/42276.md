counter-disengagement:/ˈkaʊntər dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/
noun
反脱离接触；对抗性脱离接触
The act of resisting or opposing a disengagement, especially in a military or political context.  This implies an active effort to prevent or reverse a separation or withdrawal.
The government’s counter-disengagement strategy involved increasing troop presence in the region. 政府的反脱离接触战略包括增加在该地区的军队部署。
After the initial disengagement, there was a period of counter-disengagement as both sides tested each other's resolve. 在最初的脱离接触之后，双方都测试了对方的决心，出现了一段反脱离接触时期。
Synonyms: counter-withdrawal, re-engagement, counter-offensive
Antonyms: disengagement, withdrawal, retreat
