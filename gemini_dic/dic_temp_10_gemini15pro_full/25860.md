burka's:/ˈbʊrkəz/
noun
波卡（复数形式，指不止一件波卡）
A burka is a full-body garment worn by some Muslim women, covering the entire body including the face, with a mesh screen for vision.  Adding 's makes it possessive, referring to something belonging to a burka or burkas.
The burka's blue color was unusual.  这件波卡的蓝色很不寻常。
Several burka's were hanging in the shop.  几件波卡挂在商店里。
Synonym: burqas (alternative plural form), niqab (similar but not identical garment)
Antonym: (No direct antonym, as it is a specific garment.  One could consider "Western clothing" a broad contrast.)
