bandobast:/ˈbændoʊˌbɑːst/| n.|安排，组织，准备（尤指复杂的）; (印度英语)警力部署|An arrangement or organization, especially a complex one; (Indian English) the organization and deployment of police forces.|例句：The bandobast for the festival was impressive.  节日安排令人印象深刻。

例句：The police bandobast for the political rally was extensive.  这次政治集会的警力部署非常广泛。|近义词：arrangement, organization, preparation, plan, management, logistics, administration, system, scheme|反义词:disorganization, chaos, disorder|词性:名词

Etymology: From Urdu بندوبست (bandobast), from Persian بندوبست (bandobast, “arrangement, settlement, regulation, management”), from بند (band, “bond, tie”) + و (o, “and”) + بست (bast, “bound, tied, fastened”).
