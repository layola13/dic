hummocked:/ˈhʌməkt/ | adj. |  圆丘状的，小丘状的 | Covered with or resembling small mounds; bumpy. | The hummocked terrain made hiking difficult.  圆丘状的地形使徒步旅行变得困难。 | Example: The glaciers left behind a hummocked landscape. 冰川留下了一个圆丘状的景观。|  Synonyms: bumpy, hilly, undulating, rolling, uneven | Antonyms: flat, smooth, level | Adjective


hummock:/ˈhʌmək/ | n. | 圆丘，小丘 | A small, rounded hill or mound. |  We climbed to the top of the hummock for a better view. 我们爬到小丘顶上，以便更好地观看。 | Example:  The field was dotted with hummocks of earth. 田野上点缀着土堆。 | Synonyms: mound, knoll, hill, hillock | Antonyms: valley, hollow, depression | Noun
