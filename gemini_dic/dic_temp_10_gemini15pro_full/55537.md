distributist:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪst/ | n. | 分配主义者 | An advocate of distributism, a socio-economic ideology asserting that property ownership should be widely distributed among the general populace, rather than concentrated in the hands of a few. |  Example: The distributist argued for policies that would empower small businesses and family farms. 分配主义者主张推行赋能小型企业和家庭农场的政策。 | Example:  She considered herself a distributist, believing that widespread ownership fostered a more just and equitable society. 她自认为是一个分配主义者，相信广泛的所有权能促进一个更加公正公平的社会。 | Synonyms: decentralist, localist | Antonyms: centralist, collectivist | noun
