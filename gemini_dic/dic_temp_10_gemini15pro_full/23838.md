breadboarding:/ˈbrɛdbɔːrdɪŋ/
n.|面包板接线，线路实验|The process of creating and testing electronic circuits on a breadboard.|Engineers often use breadboarding to prototype their designs before creating a permanent circuit. 工程师经常在制作永久电路之前使用面包板来制作原型设计。|prototyping, circuit design|permanent circuit design|-|noun

breadboard:/ˈbrɛdbɔːrd/
n.|面包板|A solderless device for temporarily connecting electronic components.|I used a breadboard to build a simple LED circuit. 我用面包板搭建了一个简单的LED电路。|circuit board, perfboard, prototyping board|-|-|noun
vt.|在面包板上接线|To assemble an electronic circuit on a breadboard.|He breadboarded the circuit to test its functionality. 他在面包板上接线以测试其功能。|prototype, test, assemble|-|-|verb
