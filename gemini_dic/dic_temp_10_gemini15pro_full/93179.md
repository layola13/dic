ignobler:/ɪɡˈnoʊblər/ | adj. | 更卑鄙的，更不光彩的 | More ignoble; characterized by baseness or meanness. | He committed an even ignobler act of betrayal. 他犯下了更卑鄙的背叛行为。|  The politician's ignobler motives were soon revealed. 这位政客更不光彩的动机很快就暴露出来了。 | 近义词: baser, meaner, viler, more contemptible | 反义词: nobler, more honorable, more virtuous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *ignoblere, comparative of ignoble, from Old French ignoble, from Latin ignobilis (“not noble”), from in- (“not”) + nobilis (“noble”).
