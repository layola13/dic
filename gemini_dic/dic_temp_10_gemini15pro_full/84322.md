halfannoyingly:/ˌhɑːfəˈnɔɪɪŋli/
| adv. |有点恼人地；略微烦人地|Halfway between annoying and not annoying.  It suggests a mild irritation. |Her constant humming was halfannoyingly distracting. 她不停地哼歌，让人有点恼火地分心。|近义词: slightly annoying, mildly irritating, a bit bothersome |反义词: pleasantly, soothingly|词性:副词

Etymology:
"half" + "annoying" + "-ly"
"half" (Old English healf) meaning one of two equal parts.
"annoying" from "annoy" (Old French anoier, from Latin inodiare "to make loathsome") meaning to irritate.
"-ly" adverbial suffix.
