gibraltar: /dʒɪˈbrɔːltər/ | n. | 直布罗陀 | A British Overseas Territory located at the southern tip of the Iberian Peninsula. |  Gibraltar is known for its strategic location. 直布罗陀以其战略位置而闻名。 |  The Rock of Gibraltar is a famous landmark.  直布罗陀巨岩是一个著名的地标。 | 近义词: stronghold, fortress | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 inland  内陆地区) | 词性: noun

词源: From Spanish Gibraltar, from Arabic Jabal Ţāriq  ( جبل طارق ), meaning "Tariq's mountain," named after Tariq ibn Ziyad, the Berber general who conquered the Iberian Peninsula in 711 AD.
