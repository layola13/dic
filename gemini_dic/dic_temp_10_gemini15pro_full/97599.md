|v.|居住；栖息|Present participle of "inhabit".  The act of living in or occupying a place.|例句：The species inhabiting the island is unique.  栖息在这个岛上的物种是独一无二的。|近义词：occupying, residing, dwelling, populating|反义词：vacating, deserting, leaving|词性: Verb (present participle)

inhabit:/ɪnˈhæbɪt/
|v.|居住；栖息|To live in or occupy a place.|例句：Many different species inhabit the rainforest. 许多不同的物种栖息在雨林中。|近义词：occupy, reside, dwell, populate|反义词：vacate, desert, leave|词性: Verb


Etymology:
From Middle English inhabiten, from Old French enhabiter, from Latin inhabitō (“I dwell in”), from in (“in”) + habitō (“I dwell, inhabit”), frequentative of habeō (“I have, hold”).
