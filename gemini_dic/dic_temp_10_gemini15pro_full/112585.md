lovesick:/ˈlʌvsɪk/| adj.|相思病的，害单相思的|Experiencing overwhelming romantic love, often accompanied by sadness or anxiety, especially when unrequited or when separated from the beloved.|She was lovesick for him after he moved away. 她在他搬走后得了相思病。|Near Synonyms: infatuated, smitten, besotted|Antonyms: indifferent, apathetic|Word Type: adjective

lovesick:/ˈlʌvsɪk/| adj.|害相思病的|Affected with lovesickness; yearning for a loved one.|He was so lovesick he couldn't concentrate on his work. 他害了相思病，无法集中精力工作。|Near Synonyms: love-struck, lovestruck |Antonyms: uncaring, detached|Word Type: adjective
