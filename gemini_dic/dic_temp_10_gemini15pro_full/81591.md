greasily:/ˈɡriːzɪli/ | adv. | 油腻地，油污地，滑溜溜地 | in a greasy manner | He greasily smiled at her. 他油腻腻地对她笑了笑。| 近义词：oily, fatty, slickly | 反义词：cleanly, dryly | 词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->

From grease + -ly.  Grease comes from Old French *gresse, from Late Latin *crassa, from Latin crassus "thick, fat."
