misprints:/ˌmɪsˈprɪnts/
n.
印刷错误，错印
an error in printing.
The book was full of misprints. 这本书里到处都是印刷错误。
synonyms: typographical error, typo, erratum, misprint, printing error
antonyms: correct printing
