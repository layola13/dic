extravascularly:/ˌekstrəˈvæskjələrli/
adverb
血管外地
Outside of a blood vessel or the vascular system.
The drug was administered extravascularly.  这种药物被血管外给药。
The fluid accumulated extravascularly, causing swelling. 液体在血管外积聚，导致肿胀。
Synonyms: extravasally
Antonyms: intravascularly
词性: 副词
