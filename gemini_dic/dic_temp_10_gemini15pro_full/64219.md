equilibrist:/ɪˈkwɪlɪbrɪst/ | n. | 平衡艺术家；走钢丝者 | A performer who maintains balance while performing feats of agility, often involving walking on a tightrope or other narrow or unstable surface. |  The equilibrist walked across the high wire with grace and precision. 平衡艺术家优雅而精准地走过高空钢索。

The equilibrist amazed the audience with his daring stunts. 平衡艺术家用他大胆的特技让观众惊叹不已。

| 近义词: acrobat, tightrope walker, funambulist | 反义词: clumsy person, klutz | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *équilibriste*, from *équilibre* (“balance”), from Latin *aequilibrium*, from *aequus* (“equal, level”) + *libra* (“balance, scales”).
