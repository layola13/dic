The company was found to be amerceable for polluting the river.  这家公司被发现应为污染河流而受罚。

词性: adjective

词源: From Anglo-French *amercier, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + merci ‘mercy, discretion’ (from Latin merces, merced- ‘reward, wages’).  The word originally meant ‘to punish by a discretionary fine’, but later came to mean simply ‘to fine’.
