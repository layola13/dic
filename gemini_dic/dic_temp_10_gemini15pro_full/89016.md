hollowness:/ˈhɒləʊnəs/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊnəs/ (US)| n.|空虚，空洞；虚伪|An emotional state characterized by a lack of purpose, meaning, or connection.  A feeling of emptiness or superficiality.|例句：The hollowness of his victory left him feeling strangely unsatisfied. 他的胜利的空虚让他感到异常的不满足。

例句：She felt a deep hollowness after her children left home. 孩子们离开家后，她感到深深的空虚。|近义词：emptiness, void, vacuum, superficiality, insincerity|反义词:fullness, substance, sincerity, genuineness|词性:noun
