arthropod:/ˈɑːrθrəpɒd/ (US) /ˈɑːrθrəpɑːd/ (UK)| n. |节肢动物|An invertebrate animal of the large phylum Arthropoda, such as an insect, spider, or crustacean. Characterized by a segmented body, jointed limbs, and an exoskeleton.|例句：Insects, spiders, and crabs are all arthropods. 昆虫、蜘蛛和螃蟹都是节肢动物。|例句：Arthropods are the most diverse group of animals on Earth. 节肢动物是地球上最多样化的动物群体。|近义词：invertebrate|反义词:vertebrate|词性:noun
