deerstalkers:/ˈdɪərstɔːkəz/ | n. |猎鹿帽；侦探帽|A soft, woolen hat with visors front and back, typically worn by hunters, and famously associated with Sherlock Holmes. | Sherlock Holmes is often depicted wearing deerstalkers.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯经常被描绘成戴着猎鹿帽。|  He donned his deerstalker and set off into the fog. 他戴上猎鹿帽，走进了雾中。 | 近义词：hunting cap, Sherlock Holmes hat | 反义词：baseball cap | 词性:noun


deerstalker:/ˈdɪərstɔːkə(r)/ | n. |猎鹿者；追踪鹿的人|A person who stalks deer, typically for hunting. | The deerstalker moved stealthily through the forest. 猎鹿者悄悄地穿过森林。| Experienced deerstalkers know the best places to find their prey.  经验丰富的猎鹿者知道在哪里可以找到猎物。| 近义词：hunter | 反义词：conservationist | 词性:noun
