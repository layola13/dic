mildewing:/ˈmɪlˌdjuːɪŋ/ /ˈmɪlˌduːɪŋ/ | n. |发霉，长霉|The process of becoming covered with mildew; the state of being mildewed. | The damp climate caused mildewing on the books. 潮湿的气候导致书籍发霉。 | synonyms: moulding, rotting, decaying | antonyms: flourishing, thriving | noun

mildew:/ˈmɪlˌdjuː/ /ˈmɪlˌduː/ | n. v. |霉，霉菌；使发霉，使长霉|A superficial growth of fungi that appears on plants or damp organic matter.  As a verb, to cause or become affected with mildew. | The old bread had mildew on it.  旧面包上长了霉。 The dampness mildewed the walls. 潮湿使墙壁发霉。 | synonyms: mould, fungus, rot (n.); rot, decay (v.) | antonyms:  blossom, bloom (v.) | noun, verb
