clankingly:/ˈklæŋkɪŋli/| adv.|叮当地；发出铿锵声地|Making a loud, metallic, ringing sound, typically of something heavy.|The robot moved clankingly across the floor.  机器人叮叮当当地走过地板。|noisily, loudly, resonantly|quietly, silently|adverb

Etymology: From the verb "clank" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Clank" originates from imitative origin, echoing the sound itself.
