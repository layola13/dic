multitudes:/ˈmʌltɪtjuːdz/ | n. |群众；大量；众多|A large number of people or things.|例句：Multitudes gathered in the square to protest. 众多群众聚集在广场上抗议。

例句：There are multitudes of stars in the night sky. 夜空中有无数的星星。|近义词：crowds, masses, hosts, swarms, legions|反义词：few, handful, minority|词性:名词

multitude:/ˈmʌltɪtjuːd/ | n. |大量；众多|A large number of things.|例句：A multitude of problems plagued the project.  这个项目受到众多问题的困扰。

例句：He faced a multitude of choices. 他面临着众多的选择。|近义词：abundance, plethora, profusion, myriad|反义词：scarcity, lack, dearth|词性:名词
