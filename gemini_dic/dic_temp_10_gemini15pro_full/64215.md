equilibration:/ˌiːkwɪlɪˈbreɪʃən/ /ˌɛkwɪlɪˈbreɪʃən/
n.
平衡，均衡
The process of achieving a state of balance or equilibrium.
The equilibration of supply and demand. 供求均衡。

The government is trying to achieve an equilibration between the needs of industry and the environment. 政府正在努力在工业需求和环境之间取得平衡。

stabilization, balancing, adjustment
destabilization, imbalance
