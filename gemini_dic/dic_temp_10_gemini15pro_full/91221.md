hydrobromide:/ˌhaɪdrəˈbroʊmaɪd/| n. |氢溴酸盐；溴化氢|A salt formed by the reaction of hydrobromic acid with an organic base.|例句：The hydrobromide salt of the drug is more stable.  这种药物的氢溴酸盐更稳定。|例句：Morphine hydrobromide is used as a pain reliever.  氢溴酸吗啡用作止痛药。|近义词：bromide salt|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
