belial:/ˈbiːliəl/| n.|邪恶; 恶魔; 败类; 堕落|A Hebrew word signifying worthlessness, and hence recklessness, lawlessness.  It later became personified as the name of a demon.  Represents wickedness and ungodliness. |例句：His heart was full of Belial. 他的内心充满了邪恶。

例句：He acted like a son of Belial. 他的行为像个败类。|近义词：Satan, devil, Lucifer, evil, wickedness|反义词：God, good, righteousness|词性：名词
