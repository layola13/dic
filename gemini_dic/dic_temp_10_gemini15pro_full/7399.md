animaters:/ˈænɪmeɪtərz/| n.|动画师，动画制作人|People who create moving images, especially in cartoons or computer graphics.  They bring characters and stories to life through animation techniques.|The animaters worked tirelessly to finish the film on schedule.  动画师们不知疲倦地工作，以按时完成电影。|synonyms: cartoonists, animators|antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym)|noun
