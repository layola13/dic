disagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ /ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/| vi.|不同意，有分歧|To have a different opinion or to not approve of something.|例句：I disagree with his assessment of the situation. 我不同意他对情况的评估。|近义词：differ, object, dissent|反义词：agree, concur, consent|verb
disagree:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/ /ˌdɪsəˈɡriː/| n.|分歧，争论 (较少用作名词)|A failure or refusal to agree; difference of opinion.|例句：There was some disagreement about the best course of action.  对于最佳行动方案存在一些分歧。|近义词：discord, dispute, contention|反义词：agreement, concord, harmony|noun
