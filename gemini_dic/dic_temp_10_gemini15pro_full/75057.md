frightening:/ˈfraɪtnɪŋ/| adj.|令人恐惧的，骇人的|Causing fear or alarm; terrifying.|The thunder and lightning were frightening. 雷声和闪电很吓人。|近义词：scary, terrifying, alarming, horrifying|反义词：comforting, reassuring, calming|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *frightenen, from Old English *fryhtnian (“to frighten”), from Proto-Germanic *frihtjaną (“to frighten”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)bʰreyǵʰ- (“to be excited, tremble”). Cognate with West Frisian frygje (“to be afraid”), Dutch frezen (“to fear”), German fürchten (“to fear”), dialectal German frighten (“to be scared”). Related to fright.
