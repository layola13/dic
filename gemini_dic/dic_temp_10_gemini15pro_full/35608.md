coastal:/ˈkoʊstl/ (US) /ˈkəʊstl/ (UK)| adj.|沿海的，海岸的|Located on or near or bordering on a coast.  Relating to or characteristic of a coast.|例句：The coastal scenery is breathtaking. 沿海的风景令人叹为观止。|例句：Coastal erosion is a serious problem in some areas.  海岸侵蚀在某些地区是一个严重的问题。|近义词：shoreline, littoral, seaside|反义词：inland, interior|词性:形容词

词源: From coast + -al. Ultimately from Latin costa "rib, side, flank".
