chichi:/ˈʃiːʃiː/| adj. n.| شیک的，装饰的；乳房；虚饰；俗丽的东西|Stylish or decorative in a contrived or excessive way; a woman's breast; frills or elaborate ornamentation; something considered pretentious or overly decorative.|例句：Her dress was a little too chichi for my taste. 她的裙子对我来说有点太花哨了。|近义词：ostentatious, pretentious, flamboyant, fussy, ornate|反义词：simple, plain, understated, unpretentious|词性：形容词, 名词

chichi:/ˈʃiːʃiː/| n.| 乳房 |A woman's breast (often used in the plural).|例句：The dancer’s chichis bounced rhythmically.  舞者的乳房有节奏地跳动着。|近义词：breasts, boobs (informal), tits (vulgar)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
