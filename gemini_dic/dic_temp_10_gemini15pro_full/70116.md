feyer:/ˈfeɪər/| n.| 仙女; 小精灵|A mythical being of folk tales, typically having magical powers.|例句：The feyer granted her a wish. 仙女满足了她的愿望。

例句：Feyers are often depicted with wings. 仙女通常被描绘成有翅膀的样子。|近义词：fairy, sprite, pixie, elf|反义词:ogre, troll|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *feie, *fayerye, from Old French faerie (“fairy, enchantment, magic”), from fae (“fairy”), from Vulgar Latin *fata, from Latin Fāta (“the Fates”), plural of Fātum (“fate, destiny”). Doublet of fate.
