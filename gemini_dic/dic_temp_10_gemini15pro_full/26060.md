bushism: /ˈbʊʃɪzəm/ | n. | 布什主义（指美国前总统乔治·W·布什的口误或独特措辞） | A characteristically awkward or unusual verbal expression by George W. Bush, often involving malapropisms, unusual grammar, or nonsensical phrasing. |  "Rarely is the question asked: Is our children learning?" is a classic Bushism.  “很少有人问：我们的孩子在学习吗？”是一个典型的布什主义。| 近义词: gaffe, blunder, malapropism | 反义词: eloquence, articulation | 词性: 名词
