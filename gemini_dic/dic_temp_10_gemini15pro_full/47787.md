decapitate:/dɪˈkæpɪteɪt/
v.
斩首
To cut off the head of; behead.
The executioner decapitated the prisoner.  刽子手将囚犯斩首。

近义词:behead, execute, guillotine
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用spare, pardon)
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin decapitatus, past participle of decapitare "to behead," from Latin de- "off, down, from" + caput (genitive capitis) "head."
