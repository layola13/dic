asterophyllite:/ˈæstərəfɪlaɪt/| n.|星叶石|A brown or golden-yellow hydrous potassium iron titanium silicate mineral, occurring as lamellar or foliated masses in granitic pegmatites.|Asterophyllite is a relatively rare mineral found in some granitic pegmatites. 星叶石是一种相对稀有的矿物，存在于一些花岗伟晶岩中。|Synonyms: astrophyllite (variant spelling)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
