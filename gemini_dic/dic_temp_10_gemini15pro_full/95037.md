inaction:/ɪnˈækʃən/| n.|不作为，无行动，怠惰|Lack of action or activity; the state of doing nothing.|例句：Her inaction cost the company millions. 她的不作为使公司损失了数百万美元。

例句：The government's inaction on climate change is alarming. 政府在气候变化问题上的不作为令人担忧。|近义词：inactivity, idleness, passiveness, dormancy|反义词:action, activity, movement, operation|词性:名词
