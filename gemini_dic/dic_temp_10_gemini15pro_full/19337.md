bibliolater: /ˌbɪbliˈɒlətər/ (US) /ˌbɪbliˈɒleɪtər/ (UK)| n. |书籍崇拜者；过度尊崇书本的人|A person who excessively reveres or worships books, often to the point of valuing the physical object over its content.|He was a bibliolater, more concerned with collecting first editions than actually reading them. 他是一个书籍崇拜者，更关心收藏初版书而不是真正阅读它们。|
