cc'ed:/ˌsiːˈsiːd/ | v. | 抄送 | past participle of "cc," meaning to send a copy of an email or letter to someone else.  It comes from "carbon copy," referring to the old method of making copies using carbon paper. | I cc'ed my boss on the email to keep him informed. 我把邮件抄送给了我的老板，让他随时了解情况。|  copied, sent a copy to | N/A | verb
