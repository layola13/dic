mealier:/ˈmiːliər/ | adj. | 更多肉的；更像肉的；更丰盛的 | Containing more meal; resembling meal; more substantial. | The stew was mealier than usual, with chunks of potatoes and carrots.  这道炖菜比平时更丰盛，有大块的土豆和胡萝卜。| The bread was surprisingly mealier than I expected.  这面包出乎意料地比我想象的更像肉。|  近义词:fleshier, meatier | 反义词: leaner | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mely*, from Old English *melu* (meal, flour), from Proto-Germanic *melwą* (meal, flour), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (to grind, mill).  The comparative form "mealier" is formed by adding the suffix "-ier."
