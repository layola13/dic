nonexperimentally:/ˌnɑːnɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəli/ | adv. | 非实验地，非实验性地 |  Without the use of experiments; in a way that does not involve conducting an experiment. | The researcher studied the phenomenon nonexperimentally, relying on observational data. 研究人员非实验性地研究了这一现象，依靠的是观察数据。| The effects of the policy were assessed nonexperimentally through analysis of existing records.  该政策的效果是通过对现有记录的分析进行非实验性评估的。 | 近义词: observationally, empirically | 反义词: experimentally | 词性: 副词
