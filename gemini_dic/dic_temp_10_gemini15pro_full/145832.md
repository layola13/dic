overdeliberating:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 过度深思熟虑的，过度斟酌的 | Engaging in excessive deliberation; thinking too much or too long about something. | He was overdeliberating about the decision, causing him to miss the deadline. 他对这个决定过度深思熟虑，导致错过了截止日期。 |  She was paralyzed by overdeliberating and couldn't make a simple choice. 她因为过度斟酌而不知所措，无法做出一个简单的选择。 | synonyms: overthinking, agonizing, ruminating | antonyms: decisive, impulsive, spontaneous | adjective
