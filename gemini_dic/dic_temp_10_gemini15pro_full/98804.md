integumentation:/ɪnˌtɛɡjʊmɛnˈteɪʃən/
|n.|外皮形成，体被形成，覆盖，被覆|The process of forming or providing a natural covering, such as skin, shell, or rind.  The integumentation of insects provides protection and support.|The beetle's hard integumentation protects it from predators.| 近义词: covering, coating, skin, shell, rind|反义词: exposure, uncovering|noun
