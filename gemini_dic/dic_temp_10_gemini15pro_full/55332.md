dissuading:/dɪˈsweɪdɪŋ/
verb
劝阻; 劝退; 阻止
trying to stop someone from doing something
例句：
She dissuaded him from joining the army. 她劝他不要参军。

He tried dissuading her from leaving. 他试图劝她不要离开。

近义词：deterring, discouraging, preventing
反义词：encouraging, persuading, promoting
词性：现在分词 (Present Participle)
词源：From Latin dissuadere, from dis- 'apart, away' + suadere 'advise'.
