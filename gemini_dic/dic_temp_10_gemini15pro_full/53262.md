direst:/ˈdaɪərɪst/ | adj. | 最需要的 | most needed |  They are in direst need of medical supplies. 他们急需医疗用品。 | critical, urgent, pressing | unimportant, trivial | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dirrest, superlative of dire.  Ultimately from Latin dīrus (“terrible, dreadful”).
