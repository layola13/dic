cross-beam:/ˈkrɒs biːm/ (US) /ˈkrɒs biːm/ (UK)| n. |横梁，横桁 |A horizontal beam or bar, typically used as a structural support in a building or other construction.| The old barn's cross-beams were starting to sag.  旧谷仓的横梁开始下垂了。| The cross-beams support the roof. 横梁支撑着屋顶。| synonym: girder, joist, transom | antonym: support (can be considered an opposite in function, although not a direct antonym) | noun
