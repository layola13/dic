overrationalize: /ˌoʊvərˈræʃənəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度合理化；过分理性化 | To explain or justify (one's own or another's behavior or attitude) excessively or illogically with reasons that are obviously not the real or most important ones. | He tried to overrationalize his failure, blaming everyone but himself. 他试图过度合理化自己的失败，责怪除了自己以外的所有人。|  She overrationalized her decision to quit her job, citing minor inconveniences as the main reasons. 她过度合理化她辞职的决定，把一些小不便作为主要原因。 | synonyms: overjustify, overexplain, rationalize excessively | antonyms:  accept, acknowledge, admit,  | verb
