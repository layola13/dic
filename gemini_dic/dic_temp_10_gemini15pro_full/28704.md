carlot: /ˈkɑːrlɑːt/ (US), /ˈkɑːlɒt/ (UK)
noun
一火车皮货物；货车停车场
A large area where cars are parked, especially for sale or storage.
The dealer's carlot was full of new models.  经销商的停车场里摆满了新型号的汽车。
Synonyms:  car park, parking lot, lot
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct antonym for a place to store cars)
词性: noun

carlot: /ˈkɑːrlɑːt/ (US), /ˈkɑːlɒt/ (UK)
noun
一火车皮（尤指农产品）
A shipment of goods filling a railroad car, especially of produce.
The farmer shipped a carlot of watermelons.  农民运送了一火车皮的西瓜。
Synonyms: shipment, consignment, load
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct antonym for a unit of shipment)
词性: noun
