n.
添加物，补充物
Something added, especially to food; an addition.
The chef added a secret additament to the sauce.  厨师在酱汁里加了一种秘密添加剂。

The additament gave the dish a unique flavor.  这种添加物赋予了这道菜独特的风味。

近义词: additive, supplement, addition, ingredient
反义词: base, foundation

词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin additamentum, from Latin additāre, frequentative of addere "to add" (from ad- "to" + -dere, combining form of dare "to give").
