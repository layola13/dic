mirksome: /ˈmɜːksəm/ | adj. | 阴暗的，忧郁的 | Gloomy, dark, and cheerless.  Characterized by a depressing or sullen atmosphere. | The mirksome weather reflected his somber mood.  阴沉沉的天气反映了他忧郁的心情。|  The mirksome alley seemed to swallow the light. 阴暗的小巷似乎吞噬了所有的光线。 | 近义词: gloomy, dark, somber, dismal, dreary | 反义词: bright, cheerful, sunny, light | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *mirk + -some, from Old Norse myrkr (“dark”) + Old English -sum.
