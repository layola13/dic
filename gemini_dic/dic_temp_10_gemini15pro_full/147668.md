overpositiveness:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːzɪtɪvnɪs/ | n. | 过分积极；过度乐观 | The state of being excessively positive or optimistic, often to an unrealistic or impractical degree. |  His overpositiveness about the project, despite all the warning signs, ultimately led to its failure.  他尽管看到了所有的警示信号，却仍然对这个项目过度乐观，最终导致了项目的失败。 |  optimism, sanguineness, buoyancy | pessimism, negativity | noun
