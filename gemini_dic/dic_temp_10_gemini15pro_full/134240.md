noninfecting:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/
adjective
非感染性的
Not causing or capable of causing infection.
This strain of the virus is noninfecting.  这种病毒株是非感染性的。
The wound was cleaned with a noninfecting solution.  伤口用非感染性溶液清洗。
synonyms: non-contagious, sterile, aseptic
antonyms: infecting, contagious, infectious
词性: adjective
