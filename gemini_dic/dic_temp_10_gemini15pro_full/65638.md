evangelizes:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzɪz/
verb
传福音；使皈依；劝服
to preach the gospel to; to convert to Christianity; to persuade someone to believe in something
例句：
The missionary evangelizes in remote villages.
这个传教士在偏远的村庄传福音。

例句：
He evangelizes the benefits of a vegetarian diet.
他积极宣扬素食的好处。

近义词：proselytize, preach, convert, promote
反义词：dissuade, discourage, oppose
词性:v.
