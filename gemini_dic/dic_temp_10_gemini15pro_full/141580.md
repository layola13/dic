oppressing: /əˈprɛsɪŋ/
verb
压迫；压制；使（心情）沉重
keeping someone in subservience and hardship, by unjust exercise of authority.  Making someone feel worried and uncomfortable.
The dictator was oppressing the people.  独裁者压迫人民。
The humidity was oppressing me. 湿气让我感到很沉闷。
Synonyms: subjugating, suppressing, tyrannizing, crushing, burdening
Antonyms: liberating, freeing, empowering, supporting
v.
