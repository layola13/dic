over-assumption:/ˌoʊvərəˈsʌmpʃən/
n.|过度假设，过分臆断|The act of assuming too much; making an assumption without sufficient evidence.|His over-assumption about her wealth led to embarrassment. 他对她财富的过度臆断导致了尴尬。|presumption, supposition|under-assumption, cautiousness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + assumption.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Assumption" comes from the Latin word "assumptio," meaning "a taking up."
