nonlegislative:/ˌnɒnˈledʒɪslətɪv/
adjective
非立法的
Not involved in making or enacting laws.
The committee has a nonlegislative function, focusing on research and analysis.  该委员会具有非立法职能，专注于研究和分析。
The organization engages in nonlegislative advocacy to influence policy.  该组织从事非立法倡导以影响政策。
near synonyms:  non-statutory, administrative, executive
opposite: legislative, statutory
词性: adjective
