gyrant:/ˈdʒaɪrənt/
adjective
旋转的; 回旋的
rotating; revolving.
The gyrant motion of the top fascinated the child. 旋转的陀螺使孩子着迷。
近义词:rotating, revolving, spinning, whirling
反义词:stationary, static, motionless
词性:adjective

gyrant:/ˈdʒaɪrənt/
noun
旋转体
a rotating or revolving object.
The gyrant stabilized the spacecraft. 旋转体稳定了航天器。
近义词:rotor, spinner
反义词:stator
词性:noun
