elviate: /ɪˈliːvieɪt/ | v. | 减轻，缓解 | To make (pain, suffering, etc.) less severe or serious. |  The medication helped to elviate the pain.  这药帮助减轻了疼痛。

The government is trying to elviate the suffering of the refugees. 政府正在努力减轻难民的痛苦。 | 近义词: alleviate, lessen, reduce, mitigate, ease | 反义词: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate, intensify | 词性: 动词


词源: From Late Latin *eleviatus, past participle of elevare “to lift up,” from Latin e- (variant of ex-) “out, up” + levis “light.”
