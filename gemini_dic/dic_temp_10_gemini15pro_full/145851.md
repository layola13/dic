overdepress:/ˌoʊvərdiˈprɛs/| v. |过度压抑，过度抑制|To depress excessively; to cause someone to become excessively depressed.|His constant negativity overdepressed me. 他持续的消极情绪让我非常压抑。| The market crash overdepressed the economy. 股市崩盘过度抑制了经济。 |近义词：overwhelm, overburden, oppress|反义词：encourage, uplift|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "depress" (from Latin *deprimere* "to press down")
