dfid:/ˈdiːfɪd/ | n. (abbr.) |  英国国际发展部 (Department for International Development) |  An abbreviation for the former UK government department responsible for administering overseas aid. This department was merged with the Foreign and Commonwealth Office in 2020 to create the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO). | The DFID provided funding for many development projects in Africa.  英国国际发展部为非洲的许多发展项目提供资金。 |  Synonym: (formerly) UK aid department | Antonym: N/A | abbreviation
