manichord:/ˈmænɪkɔːrd/ | n. | 古钢琴，击弦古钢琴 | A musical instrument resembling a spinet, but with metal strings struck by hammers. It produces a metallic sound. |  He played a mournful melody on the manichord. 他用古钢琴演奏了一曲悲伤的旋律。 |  Synonyms: clavichord, virginal, spinet | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern piano) | noun
