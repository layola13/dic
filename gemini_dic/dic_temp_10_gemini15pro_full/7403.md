animatic:/ˌænɪˈmætɪk/ | n. | 动画脚本，故事板动画 | A preliminary version of a film, produced by shooting drawings or photographs of the storyboards and adding a soundtrack. | The director showed the animatic to the producers to get their feedback on the pacing of the film. 导演向制片人展示了动画脚本，以获得他们对影片节奏的反馈。 |  The animatic helped the team visualize the action sequences before starting the expensive animation process.  动画脚本帮助团队在开始昂贵的动画制作过程之前将动作序列可视化。 | synonyms: storyboard, previsualization, mockup | antonyms: final cut, finished film | noun
