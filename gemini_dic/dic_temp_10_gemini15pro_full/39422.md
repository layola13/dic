connaturalness:/kənˈnætʃrəlnəs/
n.
自然性；天赋；同质性
The state of being connatural; participation in the same nature; natural affinity.
His connaturalness with the forest allowed him to navigate it with ease. 他与森林的天然联系使他能够轻松地在森林中穿行。
近义词：innateness, naturalness, inherentness
反义词：artificiality, unnaturalness
词性: noun


connatural:/kənˈnætʃrəl/
adj.
同性质的；天然的；生来的
Existing or originating in something as a natural and inseparable quality or characteristic.
Music is connatural to the human spirit.  音乐是人类精神的固有属性。
近义词：innate, inherent, natural
反义词：acquired, unnatural
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *connaturalis, from Latin com- (“together”) + naturalis (“natural”).
