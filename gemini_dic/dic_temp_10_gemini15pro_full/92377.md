hypoconid:/ˌhaɪpəˈkoʊnɪd/ | n. | 下内尖 | A cusp on the distal-lingual side of a lower molar tooth.  It is the main occlusal cusp on the talonid. |  The hypoconid of the lower molar was worn down. 下臼齿的下内尖磨损了。 | The hypoconid is a prominent feature on the lower molar. 下臼齿的下内尖是一个显著的特征。 |  protoconid, metaconid | N/A | noun
