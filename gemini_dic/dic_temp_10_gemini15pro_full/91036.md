huskiness:/ˈhʌskɪnɪs/| n.|沙哑，嘶哑|A rough or hoarse quality in the voice, typically caused by illness or overuse.|Her voice had a noticeable huskiness after the concert.  演唱会后，她的嗓音明显沙哑了。|近义词：hoarseness, raspiness, roughness|反义词: clarity, smoothness|词性:noun

Etymology: From husky + -ness.  "Husky" itself likely comes from "husk," referring to something dry and rough, like the outer covering of a grain.
