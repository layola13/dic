paratha:/pəˈrɑːtə/ /pəˈrɔːtə/| n. |印度煎饼；烤饼|A layered flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent, made from dough that's often layered with ghee or oil, then cooked on a griddle.| I had a delicious paratha for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了一块美味的印度煎饼。|  Parathas are often served with yogurt or chutney.  印度煎饼通常与酸奶或调味酱一起食用。|近义词：flatbread, roti, naan|反义词: None|词性:noun
