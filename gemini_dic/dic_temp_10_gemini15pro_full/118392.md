meroblast:/ˈmɛrəˌblæst/| n. | 部分卵裂的卵；部分分裂的胚胎 | An embryo in which cleavage is incomplete and restricted to a portion of the cytoplasm, typically because of the presence of a large quantity of yolk.  The yolky part of the egg remains undivided. | The zebrafish embryo is a meroblast.  斑马鱼胚胎是部分卵裂的。|  partial cleavage embryo | holoblast | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *méros* "part" + *blastós* "germ, bud, sprout"
