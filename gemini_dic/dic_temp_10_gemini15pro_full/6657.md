anaglyptographic:/ˌænəˌɡlɪptəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
雕刻的，浮雕的
Relating to or produced by anaglyptography, a process of producing images or relief printing plates by engraving or embossing.
Anaglyptographic images appear three-dimensional when viewed through special glasses with red and blue lenses.  雕刻图像通过戴着红色和蓝色镜片特殊眼镜观看时呈现三维效果。

The museum displayed several anaglyptographic prints of ancient coins.  博物馆展出了几幅古代硬币的雕刻版画。

Synonyms: embossed, engraved, relief, raised
Antonyms: flat, smooth, two-dimensional
词性: adjective
