paintworks:/ˈpeɪntwɜːrks/
noun
油漆厂；油漆作坊；绘画作品
A place where painting is done, especially of vehicles or machinery.  Can also refer to the paintings themselves.
The old paintworks has been converted into luxury apartments.  旧的油漆厂已被改建成豪华公寓。
The artist's paintworks were displayed in the gallery.  这位艺术家的绘画作品在画廊展出。
synonyms: paint shop, body shop, studio, artwork, paintings
antonyms: N/A
