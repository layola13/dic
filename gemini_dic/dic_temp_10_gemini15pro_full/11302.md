ardentness:/ˈɑːrdəntnəs/
n.
热情；热烈；热心
the quality of being ardent; enthusiasm or passion.
Her ardentness for the cause inspired others to join. 她对这项事业的热情激励了其他人加入。
近义词：passion, fervor, zeal, enthusiasm
反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness
词性：名词

ardent:/ˈɑːrdənt/
adj.
热情的；热烈的；热心的
very enthusiastic or passionate.
He was an ardent supporter of the team. 他是球队的热情支持者。
近义词：passionate, fervent, zealous, enthusiastic
反义词：apathetic, indifferent, cold
词性：形容词


词源：
From Middle French *ardentnesse*, from Old French *ardant* (“burning, glowing, passionate”) + *-esse*.  Ultimately from Latin *ardēns*, present participle of *ardēre* (“to burn”).
