nonreasoning:/nɒnˈriːzənɪŋ/
adjective
非推理的，无推理能力的
Not using or involving reason; instinctive.
例句：Animals exhibit nonreasoning behavior in many situations. 动物在很多情况下表现出非推理行为。

例句：A nonreasoning fear gripped him. 一种无端的恐惧攫住了他。
近义词：instinctive, irrational, unreasoning, illogical
反义词：reasoning, rational, logical
词性: adjective
