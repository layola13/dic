mensch:/mɛnʃ/ | n. | 人；正派的人；好人；有担当的人 | A person of integrity and honor. | He's a real mensch, always helping others. 他是一个真正的好人，总是帮助别人。 |  近义词: good person, decent person, stand-up guy | 反义词: jerk, scoundrel, villain | 词性: 名词

词源: Yiddish מענטש (mentsh), from Middle High German mensch, from Old High German mennisco, from Proto-Germanic *manniskō, ultimately from the same Proto-Indo-European root as man.
