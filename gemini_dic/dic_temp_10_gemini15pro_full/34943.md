clock-in:/ˈklɑːk ɪn/ (US), /ˈklɒk ɪn/ (UK)| n. vi.|打卡上班，上班登记|The act of recording the time one arrives at work, typically by means of a time clock or electronic system. |Example:  My clock-in time is 9 am every day.  我每天的上班打卡时间是早上9点。

Example: Don't forget to clock-in when you arrive. 到达时别忘了打卡。|近义词：sign in, punch in|反义词: clock-out, sign out|词性: 名词, 动词


clock-in:/ˈklɑːk ɪn/ (US), /ˈklɒk ɪn/ (UK)| vi.|打卡上班，上班登记|To record the time of one's arrival at work. |Example:  I clocked in at 8:55 this morning.  我今天早上8:55打卡上班。

Example:  She forgot to clock in yesterday and got a warning. 她昨天忘记打卡上班，收到了警告。|近义词：sign in, punch in|反义词: clock-out, sign out|词性: 动词

词源：  "Clock" 源自中世纪拉丁语 "clocca"，指钟。 "In" 在这里表示进入或开始。  "Clock-in" 最初指使用机械打卡钟记录到达工作时间，现在也用于电子系统。
