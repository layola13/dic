baking:/ˈbeɪkɪŋ/ | n. |烘焙，烘烤 |The act of cooking food by dry heat in an oven. |  I love the smell of baking bread. 我喜欢烤面包的味道。

The cake needs more baking.  这个蛋糕需要再烤一会儿。


|近义词：roasting, cooking |反义词:freezing |词性:noun


baking:/ˈbeɪkɪŋ/ | adj. |烘烤的 |Used for baking. |We use a baking dish for lasagna. 我们用烤盘烤千层面。

The baking sun made the day very hot. 灼热的阳光使天气非常炎热。


|近义词：roasting |反义词:freezing |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bakken*, from Old Norse *baka* (“to bake”), from Proto-Germanic *bakōną* (“to bake”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰōgʷ- (“to roast, bake”).
