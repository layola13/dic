missionaries:/ˈmɪʃənɛriz/| n.|传教士|A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country.|例句：The missionaries traveled to remote villages to spread their faith. 传教士们前往偏远村庄传播他们的信仰。

例句：The missionaries built schools and hospitals in addition to preaching. 除了传教，传教士们还建造了学校和医院。

近义词：apostles, evangelists, preachers|反义词:None|词性:Noun
