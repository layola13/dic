citizen: /ˈsɪtɪzn/ | n. |公民，市民|A legally recognized subject or national of a state or commonwealth either native or naturalized.|She's a British citizen but lives in France. 她是一位英国公民，但住在法国。|Example: All citizens have the right to vote. 所有公民都有投票权。|Synonyms: national, subject, inhabitant, resident, dweller|Antonyms: alien, foreigner, non-citizen|noun
