missions:/ˈmɪʃənz/| n.|任务，使命，代表团|An important assignment carried out for political, religious, or commercial purposes, often involving travel.|The diplomat was sent on a peacekeeping mission to the war-torn country.  这位外交官被派往饱受战争蹂躏的国家执行维和任务。|Synonyms: assignments, tasks, duties, errands, expeditions, delegations|Antonyms: idleness, inaction|noun

missions:/ˈmɪʃənz/| n.|布道，传教，传教区|A group or committee of missionaries.|The missions in Africa provided medical care as well as religious instruction.  非洲的传教团提供医疗服务以及宗教指导。|Synonyms: ministries, churches, parishes|Antonyms: secularism|noun
