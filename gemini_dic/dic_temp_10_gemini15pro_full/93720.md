immanence:/ˈɪmənəns/| n. |内在性；固有性；临在|The state of existing or operating within; inherent existence.  The divine presence within the created world.|The artist explored themes of immanence and transcendence in his work.  这位艺术家在他的作品中探索了内在性和超越性的主题。|Synonyms: inherence, indwelling, presence, pervasion|Antonyms: transcendence, absence, exteriority|noun

immanent:/ˈɪmənənt/| adj. |内在的；固有的；临在的|Existing or operating within; inherent.  (of God) permanently pervading and sustaining the universe.|The divine is often seen as immanent in nature.  神圣通常被视为内在于自然。|Synonyms: inherent, intrinsic, indwelling, pervasive|Antonyms: transcendent, extrinsic, external|adjective
