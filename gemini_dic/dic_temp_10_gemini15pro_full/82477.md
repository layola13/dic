grungeman:/ˈɡrʌndʒmæn/| n. |垃圾摇滚乐迷；邋遢的人|A fan of grunge music, often characterized by a messy or unkempt appearance.  A person who is dirty or untidy in appearance.| He was a true grungeman, with ripped jeans and unwashed hair. 他是一个真正的垃圾摇滚乐迷，穿着破洞牛仔裤，头发蓬乱。|近义词：slacker, slob|反义词：neatnik, dandy|词性:noun
