clingy:/ˈklɪŋi/ | adj. | 粘人的，依恋的 | Tending to cling, especially to another person; overly dependent or emotionally attached.  |  The child was very clingy and wouldn't let go of his mother's leg.  这个孩子很粘人，不肯放开妈妈的腿。 |  needy, attached, dependent | independent, detached, aloof | adjective

clingy:/ˈklɪŋi/ | adj. | 紧身的 | Fitting tightly; close-fitting. | She wore a clingy dress that showed off her figure.  她穿着一件紧身连衣裙，展现了她的身材。 | tight-fitting, close-fitting, snug | loose-fitting, baggy, oversized | adjective
