DCOM:/ˈdiːkɒm/ (dee-kom)| n. |分布式组件对象模型 (Distributed Component Object Model)|A technology that allows software components to communicate directly over a network, especially a Microsoft network.|Example: DCOM allows clients to request services from objects located on other computers in a network. 分布式组件对象模型允许客户端向位于网络中其他计算机上的对象请求服务。|Synonym: RPC (Remote Procedure Call), ORPC (Object RPC)|Antonym: Local Procedure Call|noun
