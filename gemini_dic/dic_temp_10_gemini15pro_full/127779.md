neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə(r)/ (UK)| n. |邻居；邻国；邻近的事物|A person who lives next to or near another.|例句：My neighbor is a doctor. 我的邻居是一位医生。

例句：We have friendly neighbors. 我们有友好的邻居。


近义词：resident, dweller|反义词:stranger, foreigner|词性:名词


neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə(r)/ (UK)| adj. |邻近的；相邻的|Living near or next to.|例句：We visited the neighboring town. 我们参观了邻近的城镇。

例句：Neighboring countries often have close ties.  邻国通常关系密切。



近义词：adjacent, adjoining|反义词:distant, remote|词性:形容词


neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə(r)/ (UK)| v. |与…为邻；靠近|To live or be situated next to or near.|例句：Their house neighbors a beautiful park. 他们的房子毗邻一个美丽的公园。


例句：Canada neighbors the United States. 加拿大与美国接壤。


近义词：adjoin, abut|反义词: - |词性:动词

词源：From Old English *nēahgebūr* (near dweller), from *nēah* (near) and *gebūr* (dweller, farmer). Cognate with German *Nachbar*.
