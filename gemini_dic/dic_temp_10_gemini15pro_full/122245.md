miszoning:/ˌmɪsˈzəʊnɪŋ/ | n. | 错误分区 | The act of assigning a property to an incorrect zoning classification. | The miszoning of the land led to a lengthy legal battle. 这块土地的错误分区导致了漫长的法律诉讼。| The miszoning of the property prevented the owner from building the planned apartment complex.  这处房产的错误分区阻止了业主建造计划中的公寓大楼。 | 近义词: incorrect zoning, zoning error | 反义词: proper zoning, correct zoning | 词性: 名词

词源: "mis-" (prefix meaning wrong or bad) + "zoning" (noun referring to the division of a town or city into zones)
