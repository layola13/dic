meltwater:/ˈmeltwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈmeltwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|融水|Water formed by the melting of snow or ice, especially from a glacier or ice sheet.|The rising sea levels are partly due to meltwater from glaciers. 海平面上升的部分原因是来自冰川的融水。|近义词：thaw water, glacial runoff|反义词: ice, snow|名词
