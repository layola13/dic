drawing-roomy: /ˈdrɔːɪŋˈruːmi/
adjective
像客厅的；舒适的；宽敞的
Resembling or suggestive of a drawing room, especially in being spacious, comfortable, and elegant.
The new apartment has a drawing-roomy feel with its high ceilings and large windows. 这套新公寓有着高高的天花板和大窗户，感觉就像客厅一样。
The hotel suite was surprisingly drawing-roomy, perfect for relaxing after a long day.  酒店套房出奇地宽敞舒适，非常适合在漫长的一天后放松身心。

近义词: spacious, comfortable, ample, commodious, elegant
反义词: cramped, small, uncomfortable, poky
