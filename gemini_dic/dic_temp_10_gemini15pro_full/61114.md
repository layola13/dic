elogy: /ˈelədʒi/ (US), /ˈɛlədʒi/ (UK) | n. | 颂词；悼词；赞美 | A speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died. |  The minister delivered a moving eulogy at the funeral. 牧师在葬礼上致了一篇感人的悼词。| synonyms: tribute, panegyric, encomium, accolade | antonyms: criticism, condemnation, denunciation | noun

elogy: /ˈelədʒi/ (US), /ˈɛlədʒi/ (UK) | v. | 称赞；颂扬 (Less common as a verb) | To praise someone or something highly in speech or writing. | The poet eulogized the beauty of nature in his latest work. 这位诗人在他的最新作品中赞美了自然之美。| synonyms: extol, laud, praise, commend | antonyms: criticize, condemn, denounce | verb
