afflicting:/əˈflɪktɪŋ/
verb (present participle of afflict)
使痛苦；折磨；困扰
Causing pain, suffering, or distress.
The disease is afflicting many people in the region.  这种疾病困扰着该地区的许多人。
Severe drought is afflicting the region. 严重的干旱正在折磨着这个地区。

近义词:distressing, troubling, tormenting, paining, harrowing, agonizing
反义词:comforting, soothing, relieving, assuaging

词性:v.
