aatar: /ˈɑːtɑːr/ | n. | زعتر (阿拉伯语), 一种中东香料混合物，通常由烤芝麻、漆树、牛至、马郁兰和盐组成。| A Middle Eastern spice blend typically made with toasted sesame seeds, sumac, oregano, marjoram, and salt. It is used to flavor meats, vegetables, and breads. |  I sprinkled aatar on the hummus before serving. 我在上桌前在鹰嘴豆泥上撒了点儿阿拉伯香料。

The lamb was marinated in aatar and olive oil. 羊肉是用阿拉伯香料和橄榄油腌制的。


近义词: za'atar, zatar | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以根据具体用法找到味道相反的香料) | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic زعتر (zaʿtar), referring to both the herb and the spice blend. Ultimately from Akkadian *ṣaṭāru.

USAGE->
This spice mix is called aatar.  这种混合香料叫做阿拉伯香料。

Aatar is commonly used in Middle Eastern cuisine. 阿拉伯香料常用于中东菜肴。


PHRASE->
aatar blend = 阿拉伯香料混合物
aatar spice = 阿拉伯香料
sprinkle with aatar = 撒上阿拉伯香料
aatar seasoning = 阿拉伯香料调味料
