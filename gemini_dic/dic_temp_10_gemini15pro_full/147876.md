overreacted:/ˌoʊvərriˈæktɪd/| v.|反应过度，过激反应|To have reacted more strongly than was necessary or appropriate.|She overreacted to his comment and stormed out of the room. 她对他的评论反应过度，怒气冲冲地离开了房间。|Example:  He overreacted to the news and started shouting. 他对这个消息反应过度，开始大喊大叫。|Synonyms: overcompensated, overdid, went overboard|Antonyms: underreacted, downplayed|verb
