disillusionment:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənmənt/ |n.| 失望，醒悟 |A feeling of disappointment resulting from the discovery that something is not as good as one believed it to be. |The election results led to widespread disillusionment with the political system. 选举结果导致人们对政治制度普遍失望。| synonyms: disappointment, disenchantment, letdown | antonyms: hope, optimism, faith | noun

disillusion:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒən/ |v.| 使幻想破灭，使醒悟 |To free from illusion or misconception; disenchant. |The harsh realities of war disillusioned many young soldiers. 战争的残酷现实使许多年轻士兵的幻想破灭。| synonyms: disenchant, undeceive, disabuse | antonyms: enchant, delude, beguile | verb
