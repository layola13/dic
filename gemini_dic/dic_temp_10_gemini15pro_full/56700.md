doomiest:/ˈduːmiɪst/ | adj. |最悲观的，最凄惨的，最注定要失败的 |  Superlative form of "doomy": most characterized by a sense of impending doom or disaster.  Expressing the most pessimistic or negative view of a situation. | The doomiest predictions about the economy have not yet come true.  经济最悲观的预测还没有成真。|  The movie had the doomiest ending I've ever seen.  这部电影的结局是我看过的最凄惨的。| synonyms: bleakest, gloomiest, most pessimistic, most hopeless | antonyms: most optimistic, most hopeful, brightest, cheeriest | adjective
