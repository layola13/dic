felony:/ˈfɛləni/ | n. | 重罪 | A serious crime, typically one involving violence, and usually punishable by imprisonment for more than one year or by death. |  He was convicted of a felony. 他被判犯有重罪。 |  Example: The judge sentenced him to ten years in prison for the felony. 法官因其犯有重罪而判处他十年监禁。|Synonyms: serious crime, major crime, indictable offense, grave offense | Antonyms: misdemeanor, petty offense | noun
