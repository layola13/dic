painlessly:/ˈpeɪnlɪsli/| adv.|无痛地，轻松地|Without pain; easily and without difficulty.|The dentist extracted the tooth painlessly. 牙医无痛地拔掉了那颗牙。|Synonyms: easily, effortlessly, smoothly, comfortably|Antonyms: painfully, agonizingly|adverb

painlessly:/ˈpeɪnlɪsli/| adv.|无痛地，轻松地|Without any physical or emotional pain; in a way that causes no trouble or difficulty.|The procedure was performed painlessly.  这个手术进行得很顺利，没有痛苦。|Synonyms: effortlessly, smoothly, without difficulty|Antonyms: painfully, with difficulty|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From pain + -less + -ly.  "Pain" comes from the Old French "peine," ultimately from the Latin "poena" (punishment, penalty, suffering). "-less" is a suffix meaning "without," and "-ly" is an adverbial suffix.
