antireactive:/ˌæntiːriˈæktɪv/| adj.|抗反应的，非反应性的|Reducing or preventing a reaction, especially an allergic or immune reaction.  It describes something that works against or inhibits a reaction.|This new antireactive drug significantly reduces the symptoms of allergies.|近义词:inhibitory, preventative, suppressive|反义词:reactive, responsive|词性:形容词
