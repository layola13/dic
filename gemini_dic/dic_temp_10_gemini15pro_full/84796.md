handsomer:/ˈhændsəmər/ | adj. | 更英俊的 | Comparative form of handsome.  Used to describe someone (typically male) as more attractive than another person or than they were previously. | He's handsomer than his brother. 他比他哥哥更英俊。|  more attractive, better-looking, finer | less attractive, plainer, uglier | adjective
