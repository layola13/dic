caregivings:/ˈkɛərˌɡɪvɪŋz/
n.
照护；护理；照顾
the practice or profession of providing care for someone who needs it, especially an elderly person or someone with a disability or illness
Her elderly mother required extensive caregivings. 她年迈的母亲需要大量的照护。
care, support, assistance
neglect, abandonment
