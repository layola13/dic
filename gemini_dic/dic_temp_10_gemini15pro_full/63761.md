epicleidium:/ˌɛpɪˈkliːdiəm/
n.
外种皮，上果皮
The outermost layer of the seed coat in certain plants.
The epicleidium protects the seed from damage. 外种皮保护种子免受损害。
Synonyms: outer seed coat, testa
Antonyms: endocarp
noun
