optimizers:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzəz/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzərz/ (US)| n.|优化器，优化程序|A person or thing that optimizes something; a program or algorithm designed to improve the efficiency or performance of a system, process, or design. |Example: The company hired optimizers to improve their supply chain. 公司聘请了优化人员来改进他们的供应链。|Example:  Gradient descent is a common optimizer used in machine learning. 梯度下降是机器学习中常用的优化器。|Synonyms: improvers, enhancers, refiners|Antonyms: degraders, compromisers|Noun

optimize:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/ (US)| v.|优化，使最优化|Make the best or most effective use of (a situation, opportunity, or resource).|Example: We need to optimize our website for mobile devices. 我们需要优化我们的网站以适应移动设备。|Example:  The company is constantly optimizing its production process. 公司不断优化其生产流程。|Synonyms: improve, enhance, refine, perfect|Antonyms: worsen, degrade, compromise|Verb

optimal:/ˈɒptɪməl/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪməl/ (US)| adj.|最佳的，最优的|Best or most favorable; optimum.|Example: The optimal temperature for growing this plant is 25 degrees Celsius.  这种植物的最佳生长温度是25摄氏度。|Example: We are looking for the optimal solution to this problem. 我们正在寻找这个问题的最佳解决方案。|Synonyms: best, ideal, perfect, prime|Antonyms: worst, poorest, suboptimal|Adjective
