disloyalty's:/dɪsˈlɔɪəltiz/
noun
不忠诚，背叛
The possessive form of "disloyalty," referring to something that belongs to or is a characteristic of disloyalty.
His disloyalty's consequences were severe. 他的不忠带来的后果很严重。
Near Synonyms: treachery's, betrayal's, perfidy's, infidelity's
Antonyms: loyalty's, faithfulness's, fidelity's
