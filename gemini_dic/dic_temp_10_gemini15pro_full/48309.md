decrepitnesses:/dɪˈkrɛpɪtnəsɪz/
n.
衰弱，破旧
The state of being decrepit; feebleness from age; dilapidated condition.
The decrepitnesses of the old buildings made them unsafe. 这些老建筑的破败使得它们不再安全。
近义词:frailty, infirmity, debility, weakness, feebleness, decay, dilapidation
反义词:strength, robustness, vigor, soundness, sturdiness
