outsighed:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/
verb
叹息胜过；比...叹息更深
to sigh more deeply or for a longer time than
She outsighed the wind in the trees. 她比树上的风叹息得更深沉。
近义词: outwail, outmourn
反义词: rejoice, celebrate
词性: verb
