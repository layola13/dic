compositors:/kəmˈpɒzɪtərz/ | n. |排字工人，作曲家，合成器|A person who sets type, especially by hand; a person who writes music, especially as a profession; a device used in electronic music for generating and modifying sounds. |The compositors worked diligently to arrange the type for the newspaper. 排字工人孜孜不倦地为报纸排版。|Synonyms: typesetter, composer, musician, synthesizer |Antonyms: listener, audience | Noun
