palimpsest: /ˈpælɪmpsɛst/ | n. | 重写本，羊皮纸手稿，(经历多次修改或重塑的)事物 | something reused or altered but still bearing visible traces of its earlier form; a manuscript or piece of writing material on which the original writing has been effaced to make room for later writing but of which traces remain. | The city is a palimpsest of different cultures and historical periods. 这座城市是不同文化和历史时期的重写本。| synonyms: manuscript, parchment, tablet, document; overlay, patchwork | antonyms: blank slate, tabula rasa | noun

palimpsest: /ˈpælɪmpsɛst/ | adj. |  (尤指经历多次修改或重塑的)事物 | resembling a palimpsest; having been reused or altered but still bearing visible traces of its earlier form | The old building's walls were palimpsest, layered with peeling paint revealing glimpses of past colors. 这栋老建筑的墙壁就像重写本一样，层层剥落的油漆展现了过去的色彩。 | synonyms: layered, multi-layered, stratified, composite | antonyms: uniform, homogenous, consistent | adjective
