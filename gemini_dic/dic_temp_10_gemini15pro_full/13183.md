at-heels:/æt ˈhiːlz/
adjective
紧随其后；亦步亦趋
close behind; following closely
The dog followed at his heels. 狗紧随其后。
Synonyms: close behind, following closely, on someone's heels
Antonyms: far behind, distant, lagging
词性: 形容词
