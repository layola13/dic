grungeish:/ˈɡrʌndʒɪʃ/
adjective
邋遢的，颓废的，垃圾摇滚风格的
Resembling or characteristic of grunge, a style of rock music characterized by distorted electric guitars and lyrics expressing disillusionment and alienation.
Her grungeish outfit consisted of ripped jeans, a faded band t-shirt, and combat boots. 她那身邋遢的打扮包括破洞牛仔裤、褪色的乐队T恤和作战靴。
The band's sound was distinctly grungeish, with heavy guitars and angsty lyrics.  这支乐队的音乐风格明显是垃圾摇滚，带有沉重的吉他声和充满焦虑的歌词。
近义词:grungy, scruffy, messy, unkempt, slovenly
反义词:clean, tidy, neat, immaculate, pristine
词性:adjective

词源:
From grunge + -ish.  Grunge itself likely originated as a slang term in the Pacific Northwest of the United States, possibly from "grubby" or "grimy."
