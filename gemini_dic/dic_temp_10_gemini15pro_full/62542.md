endyma:/ɛnˈdaɪmə/ | n. | 室管膜 | The membrane lining the ventricles of the brain and the central canal of the spinal cord. | The endyma plays a crucial role in cerebrospinal fluid circulation. 室管膜在脑脊液循环中起着至关重要的作用。| The endyma can become inflamed in certain conditions. 在某些情况下，室管膜会发炎。 | 近义词: ependymal lining | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *éndumon*, neuter of *éndumos* "inner garment," from *en* "in" + *dyein* "to enter, put on."
