emulsive:/ɪˈmʌlsɪv/ | adj. | 乳化的；使乳化的；乳状的 | Having the power to emulsify or form an emulsion.  |  This emulsifying agent is commonly used in food production.  这种乳化剂常用于食品生产。 | synonyms: emulsifying, emulgent | antonyms: demulsifying, separating | adjective

emulsive:/ɪˈmʌlsɪv/ | adj. | 易乳化的；容易形成乳液的 | Tending to emulsify or form an emulsion. | Oil and water don't mix easily, but with an emulsifier, they become an emulsive substance. 油和水不容易混合，但有了乳化剂，它们就变成了乳状物质。 | synonyms: blendable, mixable | antonyms: immiscible, unblendable | adjective
