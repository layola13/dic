earwig:/ˈɪərˌwɪɡ/| vt.|使...不安；挑拨离间|To fill someone's mind with doubts and suspicions; to worry or annoy persistently.|She's been earwigging him all day about the meeting. 她整天都在为会议的事烦扰他。|Synonyms: badger, pester, annoy, harass|Antonyms: soothe, comfort, reassure|Verb
