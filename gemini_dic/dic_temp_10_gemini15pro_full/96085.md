indefectibility:/ˌɪndɪˌfɛktɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.|无错误；无缺点；不会衰退|The doctrine of papal infallibility asserts the indefectibility of the Pope's pronouncements on matters of faith and morals.  教皇无谬论断言教皇在信仰和道德问题上的声明是不会出错的。|The constitution guarantees the indefectibility of the nation's sovereignty. 宪法保障国家主权的不可动摇。|
近义词:infallibility, impeccability, soundness
反义词:fallibility, imperfection, defectiveness
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin indefectibilitas, from indefectibilis ("incapable of failing"), from in- ("not") + defectibilis ("liable to fail"), from deficere ("to fail, desert, abandon"), from de- ("down, away") + facere ("to do, make").
