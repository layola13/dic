mushed:/mʌʃt/ | adj. | 糊状的；软烂的；压碎的 | Reduced to a soft, pulpy consistency by crushing or pressure.  | The potatoes were overcooked and mushed. 土豆煮过头了，都糊了。 | Synonyms: pulpy, mashed, squishy | Antonyms: firm, solid | Adjective

mushed:/mʌʃt/ | v. |  把…压成糊状；捣碎 | To reduce something to a soft, pulpy consistency by crushing or pressure. | He mushed the bananas with a fork. 他用叉子把香蕉捣碎了。| Synonyms: mash, pulp, crush | Antonyms: solidify, harden | Verb

PHRASE->
mush something up: 把…捣烂  She mushed up the strawberries for the topping. 她把草莓捣烂做配料。
get all mushed up:变得非常感伤或情绪化  He got all mushed up when he saw the old photos.  看到老照片，他变得非常感伤。
all mushed up: 糊状的；烂糟糟的 The food was all mushed up after being in the backpack all day.  食物在背包里放了一整天，都糊了。
mushy peas:  豌豆泥  Mushy peas are a popular side dish in the UK.  豌豆泥在英国是很受欢迎的配菜。


词源:  From the Middle English *moshen*, from Old French *mossier*, meaning "to soften," ultimately from Latin *mollire*, "to soften."
