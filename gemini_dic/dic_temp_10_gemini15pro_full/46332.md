dacha:/ˈdɑːtʃə/ (US), /ˈdætʃə/ (UK)| n.|乡间别墅，别墅；(尤指俄国的)避暑别墅|A dacha is a Russian country house or cottage, often used as a second or vacation home.|例句：They spent their summers at their dacha outside Moscow. 他们在莫斯科郊外的别墅里度过了夏天。

例句：The family enjoyed relaxing at their dacha on weekends.  这家人喜欢在周末去他们的别墅放松。

|近义词：cottage, cabin, villa, country house|反义词:|noun
