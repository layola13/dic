enticements:/ɪnˈtaɪsmənts/
n.
诱惑；诱惑物
something that attracts or tempts by offering pleasure or advantage.
The store offered various enticements to attract customers, such as discounts and free gifts.  这家商店提供各种诱惑来吸引顾客，例如折扣和免费赠品。

The travel brochure was full of enticing pictures of tropical beaches. 旅行手册上满是诱人的热带海滩图片。

近义词: temptations, lures, attractions, inducements
反义词: deterrents, discouragements
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:  From Old French enticier "to incite, provoke," from en- "in" + a lost word probably meaning "set on fire" from Latin titio "firebrand."
