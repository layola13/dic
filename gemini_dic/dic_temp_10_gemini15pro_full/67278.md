exsanguineous: /ˌɛksˈsæŋɡwɪnɪəs/
adjective
失血的；苍白的
Having lost a great deal of blood; bloodless; pale.
The exsanguineous patient was rushed into surgery.  这位失血过多的病人被紧急送往手术室。
The victim lay exsanguineous on the floor. 受害者脸色苍白地躺在地板上。
Synonyms: anemic, ashen, bloodless, pale, pallid
Antonyms: flushed, ruddy, sanguine
词性: adjective
