ounce:/aʊns/| n.|盎司；少量|A unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound (avoirdupois) or 480 grains (28.35 grams). Also, a fluid ounce (29.57 milliliters).|The recipe calls for six ounces of chocolate.  这个食谱需要六盎司巧克力。|Synonyms:  unit, measure, bit, drop, dash|Antonyms:  ton, pound, kilogram|noun

ounce:/aʊns/| n.|雪豹|Snow leopard|We saw an ounce in the Himalayas. 我们在喜马拉雅山看到了雪豹. |Synonyms:  snow leopard, irbis|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *unce*, from Latin *uncia* "twelfth part, ounce," from Proto-Italic *ounkia*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*oynos* "one".
