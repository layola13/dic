anabasis:/əˈnæbəsɪs/ | n. |远征；进军；(尤指)溯河而上；(生理学)上升期；(病的)加剧期 | An expedition, especially a military advance up from the coast into the interior of a country; a difficult or arduous undertaking. | Xenophon's *Anabasis* recounts the Greek mercenaries' journey through Persia.  色诺芬的《远征记》讲述了希腊雇佣兵穿越波斯的旅程。

The anabasis of the salmon up the river to spawn is an incredible feat of nature. 鲑鱼溯河而上产卵是一项不可思议的自然壮举。 | 近义词：expedition, journey, ascent, advance | 反义词：descent, retreat, withdrawal | 词性: 名词

词源: From Ancient Greek ἀνάβασις (anábasis, “a going up”), from ἀναβαίνω (anabaínō, “I go up, mount”), from ἀνά (aná, “up”) + βαίνω (baínō, “I go”).
