computer:/kəmˈpjuːtər/ (US) /kəmˈpjuːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 计算机，电脑 |A programmable electronic device designed to accept data, perform prescribed mathematical and logical operations at high speed, and display the results of these operations.  | I use my computer for work and entertainment. 我用我的电脑工作和娱乐。|Synonyms: PC, laptop, desktop, machine|Antonyms: abacus, calculator (in terms of complexity and capability) | noun
