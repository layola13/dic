browner:/ˈbraʊnər/| adj.|更棕色的，更褐色的|Comparative form of brown.  Used to describe something that has a deeper or more intense brown color than something else being compared.|Her hair is browner than mine. 她的头发比我的更棕。|Near Synonyms: darker, deeper, richer|Antonyms: lighter, paler|adjective


browner:/ˈbraʊnər/| n.|晒黑的人，使…晒黑的人或物|Someone or something that makes things brown, or someone who has become tanned.|The sun is a natural browner. 太阳是天然的晒黑者。|Near Synonyms: tanner, bronzer|Antonyms: whitener, bleacher|noun
