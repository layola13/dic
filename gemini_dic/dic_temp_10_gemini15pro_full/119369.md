microcosm: /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌkɒzəm/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊˌkɑːzəm/ (US)| n. |缩影，微观世界|A miniature world or universe; a group or system viewed as the epitome of a larger one.|例句：The village is a microcosm of traditional English society.  这个村庄是英国传统社会的缩影。

例句：The school is a microcosm of the wider community. 这所学校是更广泛的社区的缩影。|近义词：epitome, miniature, model, representation|反义词：macrocosm|词性：名词
