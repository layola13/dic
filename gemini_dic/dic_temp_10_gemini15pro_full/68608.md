fantast:/ˈfæntæst/ | n. | 狂热爱好者；空想家；幻想家 | A person who is enthusiastic about something; a visionary; a dreamer. | He's a real film fantast, he watches movies every day. 他是个真正的电影迷，每天都看电影。 | 近义词：enthusiast, fanatic, visionary, dreamer | 反义词：pragmatist, realist | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Fanatiker*, ultimately from Latin *fanaticus* "inspired by a deity, frenzied, enthusiastic," literally "of a temple," from *fanum* "temple, shrine."
