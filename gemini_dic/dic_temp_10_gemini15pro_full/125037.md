mulatto:/məˈlætoʊ/ (US), /mjuˈlætoʊ/ (UK)| n. |混血儿（尤指白人和黑人的混血儿）|A person of mixed white and Black ancestry, especially a person with one white and one Black parent.  This term is now widely considered offensive due to its origins in slavery and its connotations of racial impurity.|例句：The novel tells the story of a mulatto woman passing for white in 19th-century America. 这部小说讲述了一位混血女性在19世纪的美国冒充白人的故事。

例句：Historically, the term "mulatto" was used to categorize people based on a racist hierarchy.  从历史上看，“mulatto”一词曾被用来根据种族主义等级制度对人进行分类。|近义词：mixed-race, biracial (preferred), person of color|反义词: N/A (no direct antonym for a racial designation)|词性:noun
