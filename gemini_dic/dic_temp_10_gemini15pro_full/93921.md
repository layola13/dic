immunise:/ˈɪmjʊnaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɪmjəˌnaɪz/ (US)| vt.|免疫，使免疫|To make someone immune to a disease, typically by vaccination.|例句：The doctor immunised the children against measles. 医生给孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。|例句：They are trying to immunise the population against the flu. 他们正努力使民众对流感免疫。|近义词：vaccinate, inoculate|反义词:infect, expose|词性:动词
词源:Late 16th century: from French immuniser, from immunité ‘immunity’.
