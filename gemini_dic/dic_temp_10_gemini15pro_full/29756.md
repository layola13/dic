catril: /ˈkætrɪl/
noun
摇纱筒；卷布辊；卷线轴
A device used in textile mills for winding yarn or thread onto a spool or bobbin.
The catril spun rapidly as the yarn was wound onto it.  摇纱筒快速旋转，将纱线缠绕在上面。
The weaver expertly used the catril to gather the threads.  织工熟练地使用卷线轴收集线。
synonyms: spool, bobbin, reel, winder
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "unwinder" if referring to the action)
