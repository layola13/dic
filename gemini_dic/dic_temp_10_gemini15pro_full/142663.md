ostracoidea:/ˌɒstrəˈkɔɪdiə/ | n. |介形亚纲；介形虫纲|A subclass or class of small crustaceans, typically enclosed in a bivalved carapace. They are abundant in both marine and freshwater environments.| Ostracoidea fossils can be used to date sedimentary rocks. 介形虫化石可以用来确定沉积岩的年代。|Synonyms: ostracods, seed shrimps|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
