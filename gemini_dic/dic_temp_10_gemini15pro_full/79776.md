glutinous:/ˈɡluːtɪnəs/| adj.|粘性的；胶状的；黏的|sticky; gluey; resembling glue|The rice was glutinous and difficult to eat with chopsticks. 这米很黏，很难用筷子吃。|近义词：sticky, gummy, adhesive, viscous|反义词：non-sticky, dry, runny|adjective

glutinous:/ˈɡluːtɪnəs/| adj.|含麸质的 (主要指小麦)|containing gluten (referring primarily to wheat)|Glutinous wheat is used to make bread.  含麸质的小麦被用来制作面包。|近义词：glutenous|反义词：gluten-free|adjective
