odorless:/ˈəʊdələs/ (US) /ˈoʊdərləs/ (UK)| adj.|无味的，无臭的|Having no odor; without a smell.|例句：The gas is odorless and therefore dangerous. 这种气体无色无味，因此很危险。|例句：The flowers are beautiful but odorless. 这些花很漂亮，但是没有香味。|近义词：scentless, unscented, inodorous|反义词：odorous, fragrant, aromatic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'odor' + '-less'. 'Odor' comes from Old French 'odor' and ultimately from Latin 'odor' meaning "smell, scent." '-less' is a suffix meaning "without."
