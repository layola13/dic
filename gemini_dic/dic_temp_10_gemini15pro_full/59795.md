ecotypic:/ˌiːkəˈtɪpɪk/ |adj.|生态型的，生态类型的|Relating to an ecotype, a distinct form or race of a plant or animal species occupying a particular habitat.  A genetically differentiated population within a species, adapted to specific environmental conditions.|The researchers studied the ecotypic variation in the plant's growth rate. 研究人员研究了这种植物生长速度的生态型变异。|Synonyms:  locally adapted, ecogeographical, specialized|Antonyms:  generalized, cosmopolitan, widespread|adjective


ecotype:/ˈiːkəʊˌtaɪp/|n.|生态型|A distinct form or race of a plant or animal species occupying a particular habitat.|The ecotype found in the mountains is more resistant to cold than the one found in the lowlands. 在山区发现的生态型比在低地发现的生态型更耐寒。|Synonyms: subspecies, race, variety, form|Antonyms: species (in the broad sense)|noun
