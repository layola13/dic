negating:/ˈnɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
否定，否认
Making something have no effect or validity; denying the truth of something.
He negated all the progress we had made. 他否定了我们取得的所有进展。
近义词: denying, contradicting, invalidating, refuting
反义词: affirming, confirming, validating, supporting
词性: verb (present participle of negate)

negate:/nɪˈɡeɪt/
verb
否定，使无效
To deny the existence, evidence, or truth of (something); to cause (something) to be ineffective.
Alcohol negates the effects of the medicine. 酒精会抵消药物的作用。
近义词: nullify, invalidate, cancel, counteract
反义词: affirm, confirm, validate, enhance
词性: verb
