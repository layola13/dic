nondiscrimination:/ˌnɒndɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 不歧视，无歧视 | The absence of discrimination; the practice of treating everyone equally, regardless of race, religion, gender, etc. |  Our company has a strict policy of nondiscrimination in hiring and promotion. 我们公司在招聘和晋升方面有严格的非歧视政策。| synonyms: equality, fairness, impartiality | antonyms: discrimination, prejudice, bias | noun
