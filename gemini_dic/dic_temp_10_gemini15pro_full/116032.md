Example 2: Marsupites are highly sought after by fossil collectors. 海百合化石是化石收藏家梦寐以求的藏品。|近义词：crinoid fossil 海百合化石|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "living crinoid" 現存的海百合)|词性:noun
