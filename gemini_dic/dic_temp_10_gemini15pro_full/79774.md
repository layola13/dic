glutinosities:/ˌɡluːtɪˈnɒsɪtiz/
|n.|粘性；含谷蛋白的特性；
|The glutinosities of different types of flour can affect the texture of baked goods.|不同类型面粉的粘性会影响烘焙食品的质地。
|例句：The recipe called for a flour with high glutinosity for a chewy bread.  这个食谱需要一种高粘性的面粉来制作有嚼劲的面面包。

|例句：The glutinosities of the dough made it difficult to knead. 面团的粘性使得它很难揉捏。
|近义词：stickiness, adhesiveness, viscosity
|反义词：slipperiness
|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From glutinous + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns denoting state, quality, or condition) + -es (suffix forming plural nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *glūtinōsus (sticky), from glūten (glue).
