dungyard:/ˈdʌŋjɑːrd/
n.
粪场，堆肥场
A place where dung is collected and stored, often for use as manure.
The farmer wheeled the barrow across the dungyard.  农民用手推车穿过粪场。
近义词：manure heap, compost heap
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
From 'dung' (animal excrement) + 'yard' (an enclosed area).  "Dung" comes from Old English *dung, and "yard" comes from Old English geard (enclosure).
