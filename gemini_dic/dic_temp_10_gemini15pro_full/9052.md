antimedicine:/ˌæntiˈmɛdəsɪn/ | n. | 反医药，反医学 | Opposition to the use of conventional medicine; a belief that medicine is harmful. | Example 1:  The antimedicine movement gained traction during the pandemic.  反医药运动在大流行期间获得了关注。

Example 2: Her antimedicine stance prevented her from seeking treatment for her illness. 她反对医学的立场使她无法寻求治疗她的疾病。| Near synonyms: alternative medicine (can be contrasted), unorthodox medicine, holistic medicine (can be contrasted) | Antonyms: conventional medicine, traditional medicine, allopathic medicine | noun
