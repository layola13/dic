cohabitant:/koʊˈhæbɪtənt/ | n. |同居者；共同居住者|A person who lives with another, especially in a sexual relationship, but not necessarily married to them.|例句：The landlord was unaware that the tenants were cohabitants.  房东不知道租客是同居关系。

例句：My cohabitant and I share the household chores.  我和我的同居伴侣分担家务。
