interfering:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋ/| adj.|干涉的，干扰的|Preventing something from working effectively or from developing successfully.  Characterized by interfering in the affairs of others.|例句：Interfering noise made it hard to hear the speaker. 干扰噪音使得很难听到演讲者的声音。|近义词：meddling, intrusive, obstructive|反义词:helpful, assisting|adjective

interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/| v.|干涉，干扰|To involve oneself in a situation when one's involvement is not wanted or is not helpful.|例句：Please don't interfere in my personal life. 请不要干涉我的私生活。|近义词：meddle, intervene, intrude|反义词:assist, support|verb
