astringe:/əˈstrɪndʒ/| v.|收敛；使收缩|To cause to shrink or constrict, especially by the action of an astringent substance.|The cold air astringed her skin. 冷空气使她的皮肤收缩。|Synonyms: constrict, tighten, contract|Antonyms: relax, loosen, expand|verb
astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/| adj. n.|收敛的；收敛剂|Having a tightening effect on living tissue; harsh or severe.|The witch hazel is a natural astringent. 金缕梅是一种天然的收敛剂。|Synonyms: constricting, contracting, styptic|Antonyms: relaxing, loosening, dilating|adjective, noun
