drillability:/ˌdrɪləˈbɪləti/| n.|可钻性|The ease with which a material can be drilled.  This depends on factors like hardness, brittleness, and structure.|The drillability of the rock formation was a key factor in determining the cost of the project.  该岩层的可钻性是决定项目成本的关键因素。

The drillability of certain plastics is enhanced by preheating them.  某些塑料的可钻性可以通过预热来提高。|近义词：machinability, workability|反义词: intractability,  resistance to drilling|noun
