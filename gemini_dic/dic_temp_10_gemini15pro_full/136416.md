nonreticently:/ˌnɒnˈretɪsəntli/ /ˌnɑːnˈretɪsəntli/| adv. |不隐瞒地，坦率地| In a manner that is not reticent; frankly and openly. | She spoke nonreticently about her struggles with depression. 她坦率地谈论了她与抑郁症的斗争。|  He nonreticently shared his opinions on the controversial topic. 他坦率地分享了他对这个有争议话题的看法。 | frankly, openly, candidly, unreservedly | reticently, reservedly, guardedly | adverb
