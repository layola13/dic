anticonsumer:/ˌænti kənˈsuːmər/
adjective
反消费主义的；抵制消费主义的
Opposed to consumerism; characterized by a rejection of consumer culture.
The anticonsumer movement encourages people to buy less and value experiences over material possessions. 反消费主义运动鼓励人们少买东西，重视体验而非物质财富。
She has adopted a more anticonsumer lifestyle, choosing to repair clothes rather than buy new ones. 她过着一种更加反消费主义的生活方式，选择修补衣服而不是买新的。
近义词: anti-materialistic, non-materialistic
反义词: consumerist, materialistic
词性: adjective
