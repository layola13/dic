overmeeknesses:/ˌoʊvərˈmiːknəsɪz/| n. | 过分的温顺，过度的谦卑 | Excessive meekness; humility to an excessive degree. |  His overmeeknesses made him seem weak and indecisive. 他的过分温顺使他看起来软弱优柔寡断。 |  The overmeeknesses of the applicant made the interviewer question his suitability for the role.  申请人过度的谦卑让面试官怀疑他是否适合这个职位。 | 近义词：submissiveness, humility, docility | 反义词：assertiveness, boldness, confidence | 词性: 名词

词源:  "overmeeknesses" is derived from "overmeekness," which combines "over-" (meaning excessive) and "meekness" (meaning humility or gentleness). "Meekness" itself comes from the Old Norse word "mjukr," meaning "soft."
