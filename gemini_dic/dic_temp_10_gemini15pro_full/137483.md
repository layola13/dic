nontoned: /nɒnˈtəʊnd/ /nɑːnˈtoʊnd/
adjective
无调的；非声调的
Not having tones, as in some languages.  Lacking musical tone or inflection.
The language is nontoned, relying on word order for meaning.  这门语言是非声调语言，依靠词序表达意义。
Her voice was flat and nontoned, betraying no emotion. 她的声音平淡无调，没有流露出任何情感。
近义词: atonal, uninflected
反义词: toned, inflected
词性: adjective
