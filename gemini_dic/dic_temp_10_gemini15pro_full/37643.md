commute:/kəˈmjuːt/ (US), /kɒˈmjuːt/ (UK)| v. n.|通勤，上下班，减刑|To travel regularly between work and home.|She commutes to London every day. 她每天通勤到伦敦。|近义词：travel, journey|反义词:stay, reside|词性:动词,名词
commute:/kəˈmjuːt/ (US), /kɒˈmjuːt/ (UK)| n.|通勤，上下班的路程|The journey between work and home.|My commute takes about an hour. 我的通勤时间大约是一个小时。|近义词：journey, trip|反义词:stay|词性:名词

词源：From Late Latin *commūtāre, from Latin commutare (“to change thoroughly, exchange”), from com- (“together”) + mutare (“to change”).


注意事项:  英国英语中，commute 还可以指将刑罚减轻。 例如：His death sentence was commuted to life imprisonment. 他的死刑被减为无期徒刑。
