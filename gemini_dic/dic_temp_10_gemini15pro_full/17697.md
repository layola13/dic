bedabble:/bɪˈdæbəl/
verb
弄湿，溅湿，浸湿
to wet or sprinkle by splashing; to moisten
The child bedabbled his clothes playing in the puddles.  孩子在水坑里玩耍，把衣服弄湿了。
近义词：splash, spatter, drench, soak
反义词：dry
词性：v.


bedabble:/bɪˈdæbəl/
verb
使混乱，使困惑
to confuse or bewilder
He was bedabbled by the complex instructions. 他被复杂的指示弄糊涂了。
近义词：confuse, bewilder, muddle
反义词：clarify
词性：v.
