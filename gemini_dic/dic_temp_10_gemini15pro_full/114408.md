make-free-with:/meɪk friː wɪð/| phrasal verb |  擅自使用；随意处置；不客气地对待 | To use something without permission or treat something carelessly, often implying a lack of respect. | He made free with the company's stationery, taking reams of paper home for his own use. 他擅自使用了公司的文具，把大量的纸带回家自己用。

|例句：She made free with my car while I was away. 我不在的时候，她擅自用了我的车。|近义词：help oneself to, take liberties with|反义词：respect, ask permission|词性：动词短语
