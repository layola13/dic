mousily:/ˈmaʊsɪli/ | adv. | 偷偷摸摸地，胆怯地 | In a quiet and timid manner, like a mouse. | She mousily crept into the room, hoping not to wake anyone. 她偷偷摸摸地溜进房间，希望不会吵醒任何人。|She mousily handed him the note. 她胆怯地把纸条递给他。| 近义词：timidly, shyly, furtively, stealthily | 反义词：boldly, confidently, brazenly | 词性: 副词

词源: From mouse + -ly.  Derived from the noun "mouse," referring to the quiet and timid nature of a mouse.
