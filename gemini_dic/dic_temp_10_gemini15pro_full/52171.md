diapirism:/ˈdaɪəpɪrɪzəm/
n.
底辟作用，底辟现象
The process by which a mobile, less dense rock mass, such as salt or magma, intrudes into or deforms overlying denser rocks.
Salt diapirism is a common phenomenon in the Gulf of Mexico. 墨西哥湾的盐底辟作用是一种常见现象。

The diapirism of the magma formed a dome-shaped mountain. 岩浆的底辟作用形成了一个圆顶形的山。


近义词:intrusion, uplift
反义词:subsidence
词性:noun
