close-fitting:/kloʊsˈfɪtɪŋ/| adj.|紧身的，贴身的|Fitting very closely to the body; tight.|She wore a close-fitting dress. 她穿着一件紧身连衣裙。|例句：The cyclist wore a close-fitting jersey.  骑自行车的人穿着一件紧身运动衫。

例句：The gloves were close-fitting to allow for maximum dexterity.  手套很贴身，以便最大限度地灵活。|近义词：tight, snug, form-fitting|反义词：loose, baggy, loose-fitting|词性:形容词
