overcover: /ˈoʊvərˌkʌvər/ | adj. | 过度覆盖的，覆盖过多的 | Covered excessively; having too much covering. | The plant died because it was overcovered with mulch.  这株植物死了，因为它被覆盖了太多的覆盖物。 | The cake was overcovered in frosting. 蛋糕上覆盖了过多的糖霜。 | 近义词: overspread, smothered | 反义词: uncovered, exposed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "cover" (verb meaning "to put something over or on top of something else").  由表示“过度”的前缀"over-"和动词"cover"（意为“覆盖”）组成。
