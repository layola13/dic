hippogriff:/ˈhɪpəˌɡrɪf/ | n. | 鹰头马身有翼兽 |A mythical creature with the head and wings of a griffin and the body of a horse. |例句：The hippogriff soared through the air, its powerful wings beating rhythmically.  鹰头马身有翼兽翱翔天际，强壮的翅膀有节奏地拍打着。

例句：Harry learned to ride Buckbeak, the hippogriff, in his third year at Hogwarts. 哈利在霍格沃茨的第三年学会了骑鹰头马身有翼兽巴克比克。|近义词：griffin (partly), mythical creature |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology:
From Italian *ippogrifo*, from *ippo-*, from Latin *equus* "horse," + *grifo* "griffin."  Ultimately from Greek *híppos* "horse" + *grýps* "griffin."
