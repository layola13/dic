intenable:/ɪnˈtɛnəbl/ | adj. | 无法防守的，站不住脚的 | Not able to be maintained or defended against attack or objection. |  Their position had become militarily untenable. 他们的阵地已在军事上无法坚守。|  untenable: Their argument was untenable and quickly fell apart. 他们的论点站不住脚，很快就瓦解了。 | 近义词: indefensible, unsustainable, unjustifiable | 反义词: defensible, justifiable, maintainable | 词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin intenabilis, from in- ‘not’ + tenabilis ‘able to be held’.
