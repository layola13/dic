donor:/ˈdoʊnər/ (US), /ˈdəʊnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|捐赠者，赠与人|A person or organization that gives something, especially money, to a charity or other cause.|The donor wished to remain anonymous.  这位捐赠者希望保持匿名。|The hospital relies heavily on organ donors. 这家医院严重依赖器官捐赠者。|synonyms: contributor, benefactor, giver, patron|antonyms: recipient, receiver|noun
