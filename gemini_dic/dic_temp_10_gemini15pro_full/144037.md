outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|舷外支架，突出物，支腿|A framework or float attached to the side of a boat or canoe to stabilize it. |The canoe glided smoothly across the water, its outrigger preventing it from tipping. 独木舟平稳地滑过水面，它的舷外支架防止它倾覆。|Synonyms: stabilizer, support, float|Antonyms: N/A|noun

outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|(比喻)外围，分支机构|An outlying or subsidiary part of an organization or system.|The company's outrigger in Asia is responsible for marketing their products in the region. 该公司在亚洲的分支机构负责在该地区销售其产品。|Synonyms: branch, subsidiary, offshoot|Antonyms: headquarters, center, core|noun
