icei: /ˈaɪsiː/
adjective
冰冷的；冷淡的；覆盖着冰的
Covered with ice; extremely cold; unfriendly and unwelcoming.
例句：The wind was icei and biting. 风冰冷刺骨。

例句：She gave him an icei stare. 她冷冷地瞪了他一眼。

近义词：frosty, frigid, glacial, icy, cold
反义词：warm, hot, friendly, cordial
