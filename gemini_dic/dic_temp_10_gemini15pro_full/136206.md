nonrelieving:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈliːvɪŋ/
adjective
未缓解的，不减轻的
Not providing relief; not alleviating pain or discomfort.
The patient reported nonrelieving chest pain despite taking medication. 病人报告说，尽管服用了药物，胸痛仍未缓解。
The doctor prescribed a stronger analgesic for the nonrelieving headache. 医生为未缓解的头痛开了更强的止痛药。
近义词: unrelieved, persistent, continuing
反义词: relieving, alleviating, soothing
词性: adjective
