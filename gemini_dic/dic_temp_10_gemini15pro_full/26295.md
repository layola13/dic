butyraceous:/ˌbjuːtɪˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
奶油状的；似奶油的；含奶油的
Resembling or containing butter; buttery.
The butyraceous texture of the frosting was delightful.  糖霜的奶油质地令人愉悦。
The avocado had a smooth, butyraceous consistency. 牛油果的质地光滑，像奶油一样。
synonyms: buttery, creamy, unctuous
antonyms: dry, gritty, coarse
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *butyrāceus, from Latin butyrum (“butter”) (from Ancient Greek βούτυρον (boútyron, “butter”)) + -āceus.
