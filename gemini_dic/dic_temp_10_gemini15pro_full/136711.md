nonselective:/ˌnɒnsɪˈlektɪv/ | adj. | 非选择性的，不加选择的 | Not selective; indiscriminate.  Applying to all or most members of a category, not restricted to a particular group. | This herbicide is nonselective and will kill any plant it touches. 这种除草剂是非选择性的，会杀死任何接触到的植物。| The bombing was nonselective and killed many civilians. 这次轰炸是不加选择的，杀死了许多平民。 | 近义词: indiscriminate, general, universal | 反义词: selective, specific, discriminating | 词性: adjective
