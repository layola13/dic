economically:/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkli/ /ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪkli/
adverb
经济地；从经济角度
In a way that relates to economics or finance.  Relating to the process or system by which goods and services are produced, sold, and bought.
例句：The project is not economically viable.  这个项目在经济上不可行。
例句：They are struggling economically. 他们在经济上很困难。
近义词：financially, thriftily, frugally
反义词：extravagantly, wastefully
词性：副词

Etymology:
From economic + -ally.  "Economic" derives from the French économique, from Greek oikonomikos, from oikonomia "household management," from oikos "house" + nemein "to manage."
