orcharded:/ˈɔːrtʃərdɪd/
adjective
果园化的；种满果树的
Planted with fruit trees; resembling an orchard.
The valley was orcharded with apple and pear trees.  山谷里种满了苹果树和梨树。
The hills were orcharded with rows of peach trees.  山上种满了成排的桃树。
Synonyms:  planted, cultivated, fruitful
Antonyms: barren, desolate
词性: adjective
