instep:/ˈɪnstɛp/| n.|脚背，足弓|The top of the foot between the toes and the ankle.|He kicked the ball with the instep of his right foot. 他用右脚的脚背踢球。|近义词: arch of the foot |反义词: (无明确反义词) |词性: 名词

Etymology: From in + step, literally "in the step" referring to the arched part of the foot within the line of the stepping motion.
