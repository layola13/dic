cassava:/kəˈsɑːvə/ |n.|木薯，树薯|A tropical plant with thick roots that are eaten as a vegetable.  Cassava is a major source of carbohydrate in many tropical countries.|Cassava is a staple food in many parts of Africa. 木薯是非洲许多地区的 staple food。

Cassava flour is used to make bread. 木薯粉用来做面包。|Synonyms: manioc, yuca|Antonyms: None|Noun
