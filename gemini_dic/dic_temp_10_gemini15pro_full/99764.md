intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/ | adj. n.|中间的，中级的；中间体，媒介物|lying or occurring between two extremes; at a stage between two others|He is learning an intermediate level of English. 他正在学习中级英语。|synonyms: middle, medium, average|antonyms: beginner, advanced, elementary|adjective

intermediate:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/ | n.|中间体，媒介物|a substance formed during a chemical process|This chemical acts as an intermediate in the reaction. 这种化学物质在反应中充当中间体。|synonyms: intermediary, mediator|antonyms: N/A|noun
