nondefiner:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪnər/ | n. | 非定义者，不确定者 |  Someone or something that does not define or categorize easily.  A person who doesn't fit neatly into a particular social group, belief system, or category.  It can also refer to something that lacks clear definition or boundaries. |  He's a bit of a nondefiner, not easily pigeonholed into any one group. 他有点非定义者，不容易被归类到任何一个群体。  The term 'nondefiner' itself is a nondefiner, encompassing a broad range of individuals and concepts.  “非定义者”这个词本身就是一个非定义者，包含了广泛的个人和概念。 | 近义词: nonconformist, individualist, maverick | 反义词: conformist, traditionalist | 词性: 名词


词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "definer" (from the verb "define"). This word is relatively recent and not yet fully established in standard dictionaries.  非 (前缀，意思是“不”) + 定义者 (来自动词“定义”)。这个词是相对较新的，在标准词典中还没有完全确立。
