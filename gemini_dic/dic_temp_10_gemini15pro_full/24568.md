brocading:/brəˈkeɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 织锦, 锦缎, 织锦工艺 | The act or process of weaving or embroidering brocade.  The creation of a rich, raised design on fabric. |  The brocading on her dress was exquisite. 她礼服上的织锦精美绝伦。 | synonyms: embroidery, weaving, ornamentation | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

brocade:/brəˈkeɪd/ | n. | 织锦, 锦缎 | A rich fabric woven with a raised design, often of gold or silver thread. | The curtains were made of a heavy brocade. 窗帘是用厚重的织锦做的。| synonyms: tapestry, damask, satin | antonyms: muslin, calico | noun

brocade:/brəˈkeɪd/ | v. | 用织锦装饰, 织出 | To weave or embroider with a raised design. |  She brocaded the fabric with intricate floral patterns.  她用复杂的图案织出了织锦。| synonyms: embroider, weave, ornament | antonyms: simplify, strip | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brocade*, from Italian *broccato*, past participle of *broccare* "to stud, adorn with nails," from *brocco* "small nail, sprout, shoot," perhaps of Germanic origin.
