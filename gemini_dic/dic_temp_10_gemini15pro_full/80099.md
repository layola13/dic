gobbing:/ˈɡɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| n. |吐痰;口水;大量|The act of spitting, or the saliva/sputum produced.  It can also refer to a large quantity of something.|He saw a disgusting gobbing of phlegm on the sidewalk. 他看到人行道上有一口令人作呕的痰。|Synonyms: spitting, expectoration, slobbering, drooling, a lot, heaps, masses|Antonyms: swallowing, keeping (saliva in one's mouth), little, few|noun


gobble:/ˈɡɒbl/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːbl/ (US)| v. |狼吞虎咽;贪婪地吃|To eat something quickly and greedily.|He gobbled down his dinner in five minutes. 他五分钟就狼吞虎咽地吃完了晚饭。|Synonyms: devour, gulp, guzzle, wolf down|Antonyms: nibble, savor|verb

gobble up (phrasal verb) = quickly use all of something  快速用完某物

注意事项：
“gobbing”主要作为名词使用，指吐痰或痰本身，而“gobble”作为动词则指狼吞虎咽地吃。 虽然两者词形相似，但含义和用法截然不同。 注意区分。
