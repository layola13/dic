gillless:/ˈɡɪllɪs/| adj.|无鳃的，没有鳃的|Lacking gills, the respiratory organs of fish and some amphibians.|例句：The gillless salamander breathes through its skin.  这种无鳃的蝾螈通过皮肤呼吸。

例句：Scientists discovered a new species of gillless fish in a deep-sea cave.  科学家们在一个深海洞穴中发现了一种新的无鳃鱼类。|近义词：abranchial|反义词：branchiate|词性：形容词
