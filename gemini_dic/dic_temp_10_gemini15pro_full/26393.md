by-the-bye:/ˌbaɪ ðə ˈbaɪ/
| adv. |顺便提一句；附带说说|Incidentally; by the way.  Used to introduce a remark or point not closely connected with the main topic of conversation.|By-the-bye, have you seen John lately?  顺便问一下，你最近见过约翰吗？|Synonyms: incidentally, by the way, apropos|Antonyms: purposefully, intentionally|adverb
by-the-bye:/ˌbaɪ ðə ˈbaɪ/
| n. |题外话；附带的话|An incidental remark; a digression.|His speech was full of amusing by-the-byes. 他的演讲充满了有趣的题外话。|Synonyms: digression, aside, incidental remark|Antonyms: main point, central theme|noun
