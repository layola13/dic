colds:/koʊldz/ | n. |感冒| An instance of a common viral infection, marked by sneezing, coughing, a runny nose, etc. |  I've had three colds already this winter.  我今年冬天已经感冒三次了。|  近义词: sniffles, flu, illnesses | 反义词: health, wellness |  noun

Etymology: From Old English *ceald, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kaldaz ("cold").  The plural form "colds" refers to multiple instances of this illness.
