hypercautiously:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəsli/ | adv. | 过分谨慎地，极度小心地 |  In an excessively cautious manner; with extreme or exaggerated care. | He stepped hypercautiously across the icy sidewalk.  他小心翼翼地走过结冰的人行道。| Example: She hypercautiously approached the stray dog. 她非常谨慎地靠近那只流浪狗。 | Synonyms: overcautiously, excessively cautiously, unduly cautiously | Antonyms: recklessly, carelessly, rashly | 词性: 副词

hypercautiously:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəsli/ | adv. | 过分谨慎地，极度小心地 |  Characterized by excessive caution; extremely careful. |  The government proceeded hypercautiously with the economic reforms. 政府极其谨慎地进行经济改革。 | Example:  He drove hypercautiously in the heavy rain. 他在大雨中极其小心地开车。 | Synonyms:  gingerly, warily, circumspectly | Antonyms: boldly, daringly, impetuously | 词性: 副词
