harrying:/ˈhæriɪŋ/ | v. | 持续不断地骚扰，侵扰 |  The constant noise was harrying her. |  The army was harrying the retreating enemy. | 近义词: harassing, pestering, troubling, tormenting | 反义词: comforting, soothing | 词性: 动词(present participle of harry)
