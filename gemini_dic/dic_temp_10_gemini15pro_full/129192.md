nobilitas:/noʊˈbɪlɪtɑːs/| n.|贵族, 高贵, 高尚|The quality of being noble in character, mind, birth, or rank.  Refers to the nobility as a class or the state of being noble.|He demonstrated true nobilitas in his selfless act of bravery.|Synonyms: nobility, aristocracy, grandeur, magnificence, eminence, distinction|Antonyms: baseness, commonness, ignobility, lowness, vulgarity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nobilitas*, from *nobilis* ("noble, well-known, famous, high-born, excellent"), from *nosco* ("I get to know").  Related to "noble", "know".
