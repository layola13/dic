explosiveness:/ɪkˈsploʊsɪvnəs/| n. |爆炸性，爆发力，易爆炸性|The explosiveness of the situation was evident. 局势的爆炸性显而易见。|例句：The explosiveness of the volcano surprised everyone. 火山的爆发力使所有人感到惊讶。  The explosiveness of the chemical mixture made it dangerous to handle. 这种化学混合物的易爆性使其处理起来很危险。|近义词：volatility, instability, reactivity|反义词：stability, inertness|词性:名词
词源: From explosive + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin explodere "to drive out by clapping," from ex- "out" + plaudere "to clap."
