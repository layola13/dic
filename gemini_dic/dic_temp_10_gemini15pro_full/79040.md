give-the-lie-to:/ɡɪv ðə laɪ tə/
|v.| 揭穿；证明…是假的；反驳|To show that something is false; to contradict.|Her calm demeanor gave the lie to her nervous inner state. 她平静的举止掩盖了她内心的紧张。|Synonyms: disprove, refute, debunk, contradict, expose|Antonyms: confirm, corroborate, verify, substantiate|Verb

give-the-lie-to:/ɡɪv ðə laɪ tə/
|phrasal verb| 揭穿；证明…是假的；反驳|To show that something is false; to contradict.|His confident boasts were given the lie to by his poor performance. 他自信的吹嘘被他糟糕的表现所揭穿。|Synonyms: disprove, refute, debunk, contradict, expose|Antonyms: confirm, corroborate, verify, substantiate|Phrasal Verb
