extractives:/ɪkˈstræktɪvz/| n.|提取物，萃取物|Substances extracted from natural materials, such as plants or wood.|例句：The extractives in some woods can be used for dyes.  一些木材中的提取物可以用来做染料。

例句：Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of extractives from rainforest plants. 研究人员正在研究雨林植物提取物的药用特性。|近义词：extracts, concentrates, essences|反义词: residues, remainders|词性:noun
