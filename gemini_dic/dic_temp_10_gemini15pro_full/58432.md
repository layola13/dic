duckbill:/ˈdʌkbɪl/ | n. |鸭嘴，鸭嘴兽|A bill shaped like that of a duck, especially the horny bill of a platypus.  The duckbill platypus is a unique mammal native to Australia. |例句：The duckbill platypus uses its bill to detect food underwater. 鸭嘴兽用它的鸭嘴在水下探测食物。

例句：The duckbill is sensitive to electrical signals from prey. 鸭嘴对来自猎物的电信号很敏感。 |近义词：platypus bill |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

duckbill:/ˈdʌkbɪl/ | adj. |鸭嘴状的|Having a bill shaped like that of a duck. |例句：The duckbill valve prevents backflow of blood. 鸭嘴瓣防止血液回流。
