anergy:/ˈænərdʒi/ | n. | 无反应性；能量缺失 | Absence of an immune response to a specific antigen; lack of energy. | The patient exhibited anergy to the skin test, indicating a suppressed immune system.  病人对皮肤测试表现出无反应性，表明免疫系统受到抑制。 |  The prolonged illness resulted in anergy, leaving him feeling weak and listless.  长期患病导致他精力缺失，感到虚弱无力。 | 近义词: inactivity, lethargy, unresponsiveness | 反义词:  activity, energy, responsiveness | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek *ἀν- (an-) "not, without" + *ἔργον (ergon) "work".
