myzostomata:/ˌmaɪzoʊˈstɒmətə/ | n. | 海羽星纲 |A class of marine annelid worms that are obligate symbionts or parasites of echinoderms, particularly crinoids (sea lilies and feather stars).| Myzostomata are fascinating creatures that exhibit a range of symbiotic relationships with their hosts. 海羽星纲是与宿主表现出一系列共生关系的迷人生物。|  Myzostomata often have highly modified body plans reflecting their parasitic lifestyle. 海羽星通常具有高度改良的身体结构，反映了它们的寄生生活方式。|Synonyms: myzostomes (informal)| Antonyms: N/A | noun
