cerebral:/ˈsɛrɪbrəl/ (US), /səˈriːbrəl/ (UK)| adj.|大脑的；理智的；智力的|Relating to the cerebrum or the brain; intellectual rather than emotional or physical.|She’s a very cerebral writer, more interested in ideas than action. 她是一位非常理性的作家，对思想比对行动更感兴趣。|His work is too cerebral for the average reader. 他的作品对于普通读者来说过于深奥。|近义词：intellectual, mental, cognitive|反义词：emotional, instinctive, visceral|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin cerebrālis, from Latin cerebrum (“brain”).
