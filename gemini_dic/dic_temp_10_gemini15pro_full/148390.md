oversqueamishly: /ˌoʊvərˈskwiːmɪʃli/ | adv. | 过分拘谨地，过分洁癖地 | In an excessively squeamish manner; too easily disgusted or shocked. |  He oversqueamishly avoided touching the raw meat. 他过分拘谨地避免接触生肉。|  She oversqueamishly turned away from the sight of blood. 她看到血后过分洁癖地转过身去。 | 近义词: fastidiously, prudishly, primly | 反义词:  brazenly, boldly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From over- + squeamish + -ly.  "Squeamish" itself comes from the Old English *swēamig, related to "swoon."
