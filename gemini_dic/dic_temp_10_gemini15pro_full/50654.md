derive:/dɪˈraɪv/ | v. | 源于；获得 | To get something from something else. |  Many English words derive from Latin. 许多英语单词源自拉丁语。 |  He derives a lot of pleasure from reading. 他从阅读中获得了很大的乐趣。 | synonyms: obtain, glean, extract | antonyms: originate, create | verb
