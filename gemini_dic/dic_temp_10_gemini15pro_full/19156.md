bewailing:/bɪˈweɪlɪŋ/
verb
悲叹，哀悼
Expressing deep sorrow or regret about something.
The women were bewailing the death of their husbands. 妇女们在哀悼她们丈夫的去世。
synonyms: lamenting, mourning, grieving
antonyms: rejoicing, celebrating
词性: 动词
