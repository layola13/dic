malingering:/məˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| n.|装病，诈病|The act of feigning illness, especially to avoid work or duty. |He was accused of malingering to get out of military service. 他被指控装病以逃避兵役。|近义词：feigning, faking, simulating, sham, pretending|反义词:genuinely ill, truly sick|noun
