caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ (NAmE) /ˈkærɪbjuː/ (BrE)| n.|驯鹿；北美驯鹿|A large deer with long legs, large hooves, and antlers in both males and females, found in the northern parts of North America, Europe, and Asia.  It is closely related to the reindeer of Europe.|例句：The caribou migrate south for the winter. 驯鹿冬天向南迁徙。

例句：Caribou are an important food source for many indigenous peoples. 驯鹿是许多土著居民的重要食物来源。|近义词：reindeer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French Canadian caribou, from the Mi'kmaq qalipu "pawer, scraper," referring to its habit of pawing through the snow to find food.
