monetarian:/ˌmʌnɪˈtɛəriən/
| adj. n.|货币的，货币主义者的；货币主义者|Relating to money or the monetary system; a supporter of monetarism (the theory that control of the money supply is the most important way to influence the economy).|例句：Monetarian policies often focus on controlling inflation. 货币主义政策通常侧重于控制通货膨胀。|近义词：financial, fiscal, monetary|反义词: non-monetary|adjective, noun

monetarist:/ˈmʌnɪtərɪst/
| n. adj.|货币主义者；货币主义的|A person who believes in monetarism; relating to monetarism.|例句：The monetarist argued for tighter control of the money supply.  这位货币主义者主张更严格地控制货币供应。|近义词：monetarian|反义词: Keynesian|noun, adjective
