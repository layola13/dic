flacking:/ˈflækɪŋ/| n.|公关宣传；吹捧；为…做广告宣传；(尤指体育明星的)经纪人工作|The act of providing publicity or acting as a publicist, especially for a sports star or celebrity.  Derived from "flack," meaning press agent or publicist.|The company hired a PR firm for flacking their new product.  这家公司聘请了一家公关公司为他们的新产品做宣传。|synonyms: publicizing, promoting, advertising, hyping |antonyms: concealing, suppressing, downplaying|noun
flacking:/ˈflækɪŋ/| v.|担任公关；做广告宣传；吹捧|Acting as a publicist; promoting or publicizing something, often excessively. Derived from "flack," meaning press agent or publicist.|He was flacking for a new movie. 他在为一部新电影做宣传。|synonyms: publicizing, promoting, advertising, hyping|antonyms: concealing, suppressing, downplaying|verb
