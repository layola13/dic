newfashioned:/ˌnjuːˈfæʃənd/| adj.|新式的，新潮的|Modern; recently come into fashion.  Characterized by a new fashion or style, often implying a departure from traditional ways.|例句：She prefers newfashioned furniture to antiques. 她喜欢新式家具胜过古董。|近义词：modern, contemporary, trendy, stylish|反义词：old-fashioned, outdated, traditional|词性:形容词
词源:  Compound of "new" and "fashioned" (past participle of "fashion," meaning "to make, shape, or form"). The word implies something recently created or styled.
