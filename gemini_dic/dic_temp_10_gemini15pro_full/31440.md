charities:/ˈtʃærətiz/ | n. | 慈善机构；慈善事业；施舍物 |  Organizations or institutions that provide help and support to those in need, or the act of giving help and support.  | Many people donate to charities during the holiday season. 许多人在节日期间向慈善机构捐款。| The hospital relies heavily on charities to fund its research. 这家医院的研究经费很大程度上依赖于慈善机构的资助。| synonyms: philanthropy, alms-giving, donations, benevolence | antonyms: selfishness, greed, avarice | noun
