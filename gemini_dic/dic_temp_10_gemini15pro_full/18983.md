bestirred:/bɪˈstɜːrd/
verb
奋起；激励；行动起来
To rouse oneself to action; become active.
He finally bestirred himself to go to work. 他终于奋起去上班了。
近义词: rouse, activate, energize, stir, galvanize
反义词: rest, relax, be idle, be still, procrastinate
词性: verb

词源:
From be- + stirred, past participle of stir.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely," so bestir literally means "to be thoroughly stirred or roused."
