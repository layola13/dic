macropetalous: /ˌmækroʊˈpɛtələs/
| adj. | 大瓣的；大花瓣的 | Having large petals. |  The macropetalous hibiscus flower is a vibrant sight in the garden.  花园里大瓣的芙蓉花是一道充满活力的景象。 |  large-petaled, long-petaled | micropetalous, small-petaled | adjective
Etymology->
From macro- (large) + -petalous (having petals), ultimately from Greek makros (large) and petalon (leaf, petal).
