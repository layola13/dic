aguelike:/ˈeɪɡlaɪk/| adj.|类似疟疾的，类似流行性感冒的|Resembling or characteristic of ague, a malarial or influenza-like fever characterized by chills, fever, and sweating.|She felt aguelike symptoms coming on after being exposed to the cold rain. 在淋了冷雨之后，她感觉像是要发疟疾了。|近义词：flulike, feverish, chilly|反义词: healthy, well|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From ague +‎ -like.  Ague comes from the Old French ague, from the Latin acuta (febris) “(sharp (fever)),” feminine of acutus (“sharp”).
