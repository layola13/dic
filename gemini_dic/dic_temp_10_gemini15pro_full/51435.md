detest:/dɪˈtest/ | v. | 憎恶，厌恶 | To feel intense dislike or hatred for. |  He detested hypocrisy. 他憎恨虚伪。| synonyms: abhor, loathe, despise, hate | antonyms: adore, love, cherish | verb

detested:/dɪˈtestɪd/ | adj. | 令人厌恶的；憎恨的 |  Feeling intense dislike or hatred for. |  The detested tyrant was finally overthrown.  令人憎恶的暴君终于被推翻了。| synonyms: hated, despised, loathed, abhorred | antonyms: beloved, cherished, adored, loved | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin detestari "to curse while calling a god to witness; to execrate, denounce, abhor," from de- "down, completely" (see de-) + testari "to bear witness, testify," from testis "witness" (see testament).
