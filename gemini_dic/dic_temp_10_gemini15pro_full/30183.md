celestes:/səˈlɛstiːz/| n. |天蓝色，天蓝色颜料；天蓝色衣服|A shade of light, airy blue, evocative of a clear sky.  It can refer to the color itself, pigments of that color, or items colored celeste.|The artist mixed a delicate celestes for the sky in his painting.  艺术家在他的画中调制了一种精致的天蓝色来描绘天空。|近义词：sky blue, azure, cerulean|反义词:dark blue, navy|词性:noun


Etymology->
From Italian *celeste* "sky-colored," from Latin *caelestis* "heavenly," from *caelum* "sky, heaven."
