nonassumed:/ˌnɒnəˈsjuːmd/ | adj. | 未假设的；非假定的；未认定的 | Not assumed; not taken for granted; not supposed. |  The nonassumed risks in this venture are substantial.  这个风险投资中未假设的风险是巨大的。| Examples:  The nonassumed liabilities of the company were a major concern for investors. 该公司未承担的债务是投资者主要关注的问题。 The report detailed the nonassumed consequences of the proposed policy change. 该报告详细说明了拟议的政策变更的非假定后果。 | Synonyms: unassumed, unpresumed, unexpressed, implicit, tacit | Antonyms: assumed, presumed, explicit, express | Word type: adjective
