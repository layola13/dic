nonreproductively:/ˌnɑːnˌriːprəˈdʌktɪvli/
| adv. |非繁殖地，非生殖地| In a manner that does not involve reproduction.  Not capable of being reproduced or replicated.| Some fungi spread nonreproductively through the growth of their mycelium.  一些真菌通过菌丝的生长进行非繁殖性传播。|Synonym: asexually, vegetatively|Antonym: reproductively, sexually|词性:副词
