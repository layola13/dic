overexaggerate: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 过分夸大；夸张 | To exaggerate to an excessive degree. | He tends to overexaggerate his accomplishments. 他倾向于过分夸大他的成就。| She overexaggerated the severity of the injury to get more sympathy. 她为了获得更多同情，夸大了伤势的严重程度。| 近义词: exaggerate, overstate, embellish, amplify | 反义词: understate, downplay | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + exaggerate.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Exaggerate" comes from the Latin word "exaggerare," meaning "to heap up, increase."
