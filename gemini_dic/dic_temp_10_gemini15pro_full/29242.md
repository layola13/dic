casseroled:/ˈkæsəˌrəʊld/| v. |用砂锅炖煮|To cook or bake (food) in a casserole.  The chicken was casseroled with vegetables.|例句：She casseroled the beef with potatoes and carrots. 她用砂锅炖了牛肉、土豆和胡萝卜。

例句：The chicken was casseroled to perfection. 鸡肉用砂锅炖得恰到好处。|近义词：baked, stewed, braised|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 fried, grilled)|词性: 动词
