/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/ |pron.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Which, who|N/A|pronoun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *what*, *hwat*, *quat*, from Old English *hwæt* (“what”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat* (“what”), from Proto-Indo-European *kʷód* (“what”). Cognate with Scots *what* (“what”), West Frisian *wat* (“what”), Dutch *wat* (“what”), German *was* (“what”), Danish *hvad* (“what”), Swedish *vad* (“what”), Icelandic *hvað* (“what”), Gothic *𐍈𐍀𐍀* (ƕap, “what”).


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you listening to? = 你在听什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has he done to his hair? = 他对他的头发做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 在做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next year? = 明年这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the century? = 到本世纪末会发生什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
