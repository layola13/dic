interlays:/ˌɪntərˈleɪz/ | v. | 交织，穿插；插入；镶嵌 | To place between or among other things; intersperse. | The artist interlays gold leaf within the painting. 艺术家在画中镶嵌了金箔。 | Synonyms: interweave, intertwine, intermingle, insert, interpolate | Antonyms: separate, divide, disjoin | verb


interlay:/ˈɪntərleɪ/ | n. | 交织物，夹层；插入物 | Something interlaid; a layer placed between other layers. | A thin interlay of silk separated the pages of the ancient book. 一层薄薄的丝绸隔开了这本古书的书页。| Synonyms: layer, interleaf, insert, filling | Antonyms: exterior, surface, outside | noun
