moods:/muːdz/
|n.| 心情，情绪，气氛
|A temporary state of mind or feeling.|
|She's been in a bad mood all day. 她一整天心情都不好。|
|Synonym: feelings, temper, humor, spirits|
|Antonym:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 positive feelings, cheerfulness 等表达相反的状态)|
|noun|


moods:/muːdz/
|n.| (动词 mood 的复数形式)| 语气
|(Grammar) A set of verb forms that indicate whether the action or state expressed by the verb is a fact, a command, a question, a condition, or a wish or possibility.|
|The subjunctive mood is rarely used in modern English.  虚拟语气在现代英语中很少使用。|
|Synonym: mode, form|
|Antonym: (没有直接的反义词)|
