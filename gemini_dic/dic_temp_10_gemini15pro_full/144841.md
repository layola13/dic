over-mixed:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪkst/ | adj. | 过度混合的 | Mixed too much; excessively blended. | The cake was over-mixed and dense.  蛋糕过度混合，很硬实。| The batter was over-mixed, resulting in a tough texture. 面糊过度混合，导致口感很硬。| 近义词：overbeaten, over-processed | 反义词: undermixed | 词性:形容词


over-mix:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪks/ | vt. | 过度混合 | To mix ingredients for too long, typically resulting in a less desirable texture. | Be careful not to over-mix the dough.  小心不要过度揉面。| I over-mixed the batter and the cake turned out tough. 我过度混合了面糊，导致蛋糕很硬。| 近义词：overbeat, over-process | 反义词: undermix | 词性:动词


词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "mix" (verb). "Over-" signifies "too much" or "excessively." "Mix" comes from Old English "mixian," related to "miscian," meaning "to combine."
