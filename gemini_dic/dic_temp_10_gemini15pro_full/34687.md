clerkless:/ˈklɜːrkləs/
adjective
无职员的；无人服务的
Without a clerk or clerks; operating without the assistance of clerks.
The clerkless store operates using a self-checkout system.  这家无人商店使用自助结账系统运营。
Many businesses are exploring clerkless options to reduce labor costs. 许多企业正在探索无人值守的选项以降低劳动力成本.
近义词: automated, self-service, unmanned
反义词: staffed, manned
词性: adjective

词源: clerk + -less.  "Clerk" comes from Old French "clerc" meaning "clergyman, scholar," ultimately from Latin "clericus."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
