idyll:/ˈɪdɪl/ | n. | 田园诗; 牧歌; 浪漫插曲 | A short poem or other piece of writing describing a peaceful and pleasant scene, especially in the countryside; a happy, peaceful, or romantic experience or period. |  例句：The book evokes an idyllic childhood spent on a farm. 这本书唤起了一个在农场度过的田园诗般的童年。

例句：Their honeymoon was a brief idyll. 他们的蜜月是一段短暂的浪漫插曲。 | 近义词：pastoral, romance, paradise | 反义词：nightmare, ordeal | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *idylle*, from Late Latin *idyllī*, plural of *idyllīon*, from Ancient Greek *εἰδύλλιον* (eidúllion, “short poem, descriptive piece”), diminutive of *εἶδος* (eîdos, “form, shape, figure”).
