internist:/ɪnˈtɜːrnɪst/ | n. |内科医生|A physician who specializes in internal medicine.  A doctor who focuses on the diagnosis and nonsurgical treatment of diseases of the internal organs (such as the heart, lungs, kidneys, and digestive system). |例句：My family doctor referred me to an internist for further evaluation. 我的家庭医生让我去看内科医生做进一步检查。

例句：The internist diagnosed her with a thyroid condition. 内科医生诊断她患有甲状腺疾病。|近义词：physician, doctor, general practitioner|反义词: surgeon, specialist (in a surgical field)|词性:noun
