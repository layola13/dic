overagitates:/ˌoʊvərˈædʒɪˌteɪts/
verb
过度搅动，过度激动
to stir, shake, or mix something too much, especially a liquid; to make someone excessively nervous or excited
The washing machine overagitates the clothes, causing them to wear out faster.  洗衣机过度搅动衣物，导致它们磨损得更快。
Her constant worrying overagitates her and makes it difficult for her to sleep. 她不停地担心使她过度激动，难以入睡。
Synonyms: overstimulate, overexcite, perturb, agitate excessively
Antonyms: calm, soothe, pacify, tranquilize
词性: 动词
