dickers:/ˈdɪkərz/ | v. | 讨价还价；争论；闲聊 | To bargain or haggle over the price of something; to argue or dispute about a minor matter; to chat or engage in light conversation. |  He dickered with the street vendor over the price of a souvenir. 他与街头小贩就纪念品的价钱讨价还价。

| 近义词: bargain, haggle, negotiate, quibble, chat | 反义词: agree, concede, acquiesce | 词性: verb


dicker:/ˈdɪkər/ | n. | 讨价还价；交易 | A bargain or deal; an act of haggling or negotiating. |  After much dickering, they finally reached an agreement. 经过一番讨价还价，他们终于达成了协议。

| 近义词: bargain, deal, negotiation | 反义词: agreement, settlement | 词性: noun
