amoeboid:/əˈmiːbɔɪd/ | adj. | 变形虫状的；阿米巴样的 | Resembling an amoeba, especially in having a shapeless form and in changing shape and moving by means of pseudopodia. |  The single-celled organism exhibited amoeboid movement across the petri dish.  单细胞生物在培养皿中呈现变形虫状运动。 |  Like an amoeba, the creature displayed amoeboid characteristics, constantly shifting its shape. 像变形虫一样，该生物表现出变形虫的特征，不断地改变其形状。 | 近义词：amorphous, protoplasmic | 反义词：static, rigid | 词性: 形容词
词源: From  Amoeba + -oid  ("resembling").  Amoeba comes from the Greek word *amoibē* meaning "change, alteration."
