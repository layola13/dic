crayfish:/ˈkreɪfɪʃ/ (US) /ˈkreɪfɪʃ/ (UK)| n.|小龙虾；螯虾|A freshwater crustacean resembling a small lobster, with a hard shell and large claws.|例句：We caught some crayfish in the creek. 我们在小溪里抓了一些小龙虾。

例句：Crayfish are a popular delicacy in many parts of the world. 小龙虾在世界许多地方都是一种受欢迎的美食。|近义词：crawfish, crawdad, yabby|反义词: None|词性: noun
