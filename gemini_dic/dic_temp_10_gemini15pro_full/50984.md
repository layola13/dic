desiredly:/dɪˈzaɪərəbli/ | adv. | 理想地，如愿地 | In a desirable manner; in a way that is wished for. |  The project progressed desiredly, finishing on time and under budget.  项目进展顺利，按时并在预算内完成。| She desiredly landed the lead role in the play. 她如愿以偿地获得了该剧的主角。 | 近义词：preferably, ideally, favorably | 反义词：undesirably, unfavorably | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "desirable" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Desirable" comes from the verb "desire" + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives). "Desire" ultimately derives from Latin "desiderare" meaning "to long for, wish for."
