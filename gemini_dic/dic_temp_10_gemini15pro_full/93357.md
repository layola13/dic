illegibility:/ɪˌlɛdʒɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
不可读性；字迹不清
the quality of being difficult or impossible to read.
His handwriting is notorious for its illegibility. 他的笔迹以难以辨认而闻名。
illegibleness, unclarity, unreadability
legibility, clarity, readability
noun
