hydrogode:/ˈhaɪdrəˌɡəʊd/ | n. | 水翼船，水翼艇 | A hydrofoil boat; a boat that lifts out of the water on hydrofoils at speed. |  Example 1: The hydrogode skimmed across the waves. 水翼艇掠过波浪。

Example 2:  They took a hydrogode to the island. 他们乘坐水翼艇去了岛上。 | Synonym: hydrofoil,  foilboat | Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym exists, perhaps a conventional displacement hull boat) | noun
