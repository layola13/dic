overeater:/ˌoʊvərˈiːtər/ | n. |暴食者；吃得过多的人|A person who habitually eats too much food.| He's always been an overeater, struggling with portion control. 他一直是个暴食者，很难控制食量。|She became an overeater after her divorce. 她离婚后变成了一个暴食者。|近义词: glutton, gourmand |反义词: anorexic, light eater |词性: noun

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + eater (noun meaning "one who eats")
