multigrid:/ˈmʌltɪɡrɪd/| adj. n.|多重网格的，多重网格|A multigrid method is an algorithm for solving differential equations using a hierarchy of discretizations.|Multigrid methods are commonly used in computational fluid dynamics. 多重网格方法常用于计算流体动力学。|近义词:multilevel, hierarchical|反义词:single-grid|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: multi- + grid.  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "Grid" comes from Middle English "griddle" referring to a grating.
