nonadorner:/nɒnəˈdɔːrnər/| n.|非装饰者；朴素的人|A person who does not adorn or decorate, someone who prefers simplicity and avoids embellishment.|She was a nonadorner, preferring simple clothes and a minimalist lifestyle. 她是一个不爱装饰的人，喜欢简单的衣服和极简的生活方式。|The architect, a known nonadorner, designed the building with clean lines and no ornamentation.  这位建筑师，一位著名的不爱装饰的人，设计的建筑线条简洁，没有任何装饰。|近义词：minimalist, purist, ascetic|反义词:decorator, embellisher, ornamentalist|词性:noun

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "adorn" (from Old French "adorner," meaning "to decorate") + "-er" (suffix indicating a person who performs an action).
