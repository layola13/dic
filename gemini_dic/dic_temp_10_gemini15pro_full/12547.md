aspirant:/ˈæspərənt/ (US), /əˈspaɪərənt/ (UK)| n. adj.|有抱负的人；有野心的人；雄心勃勃的|A person who has ambitious hopes or plans, especially for advancement or success.  A person who aspires to something higher or more important.|例句：He was an aspirant to the throne. 他是王位的觊觎者。|例句：She's an aspirant writer, hoping to publish her first novel soon. 她是一位有抱负的作家，希望很快能出版她的第一部小说。|近义词：ambitious, aspiring, hopeful, candidate|反义词：unambitious, complacent, satisfied|词性:名词/形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French aspirant, present participle of aspirer ‘aspire’, from Latin aspirare ‘breathe on, strive towards’.
