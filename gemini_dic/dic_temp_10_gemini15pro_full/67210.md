expressively:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 富有表现力地，意味深长地 |  In a way that clearly shows your feelings or thoughts. | She smiled expressively. 她意味深长地笑了笑。| expressively:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 富有表现力地，意味深长地 |  He played the piano expressively, conveying a deep sense of emotion. 他弹钢琴富有表现力，传达了深刻的情感。| 近义词:meaningfully, significantly, eloquently, vividly | 反义词:blankly, dully, impassively, inexpressively | 词性:副词

词源: From the adjective "expressive" + "-ly". "Expressive" comes from the Latin "expressus," past participle of "exprimere," meaning "to press out" or "represent."
