exhilarating:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人兴奋的；使人愉快的 | Making one feel very happy, animated, or elated; thrilling. | The roller coaster ride was exhilarating. 过山车真刺激。| synonyms: thrilling, exciting, stimulating, invigorating | antonyms: boring, tedious, dull, monotonous | adjective

exhilarate:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪt/ | verb | 使高兴；使兴奋 | To make (someone) feel very happy, animated, or elated; thrill. | The news exhilarated everyone.  这个消息使每个人都很兴奋。 | synonyms: thrill, excite, stimulate, invigorate | antonyms: bore, tire, depress, discourage | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exhilaratus, past participle of exhilarare (“to gladden, cheer”), from ex- (“out, from”) + hilarare (“to make cheerful”), from hilaris (“cheerful”).
