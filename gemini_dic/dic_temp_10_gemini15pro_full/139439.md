obsignate:/ˈɒbsɪɡneɪt/ /ˈɑːbsɪɡneɪt/
verb
密封，盖章；批准
to ratify or confirm something, often by affixing a seal or signature
The king obsignated the treaty, officially ending the war. 国王批准了条约，正式结束了战争。
The document was obsignated with the official seal.  这份文件盖上了官方印章。
近义词: seal, ratify, confirm, endorse, approve
反义词: reject, refuse, deny, disapprove
词性: verb

obsignate:/ˈɒbsɪɡneɪt/ /ˈɑːbsɪɡneɪt/
词源: From Latin obsignare (to seal, to mark), from ob- (towards, against) + signare (to mark, to sign).
词性: verb
