materialized:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/ /məˈtɪriəlaɪzd/
|adj.|实现的，实体化的，突然出现的|Having become real or actual; having taken on physical form. |The ghost materialized in front of our eyes. 鬼魂出现在我们眼前。|近义词：appeared, emerged, manifested, concretized|反义词：disappeared, vanished, dematerialized|词性: adjective
materialize:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ /məˈtɪriəlaɪzd/
|v.|实现，实体化，突然出现|To become real or actual; to take on physical form. |Her dreams of owning a house finally materialized. 她拥有房子的梦想终于实现了。|近义词：appear, emerge, manifest, concretize|反义词：disappear, vanish, dematerialize|词性: verb
