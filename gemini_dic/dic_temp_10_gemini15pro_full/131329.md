nonchaotic:/ˌnɒnkeɪˈɒtɪk/
adjective
非混沌的; 不混乱的
Not chaotic; exhibiting or characterized by predictable or regular behavior.
The system, despite its complexity, remains nonchaotic, allowing for accurate predictions of its future states.  这个系统尽管很复杂，但仍然是非混沌的，可以准确预测其未来的状态。
The steady, nonchaotic rhythm of the waves was calming.  稳定、非混沌的波浪节奏令人平静。
Synonyms: orderly, regular, predictable, stable, organized
Antonyms: chaotic, turbulent, disordered, unpredictable, random
