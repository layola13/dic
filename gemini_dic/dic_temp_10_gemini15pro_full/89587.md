hoodedness:/ˈhʊdɪdnəs/
n.
戴兜帽的状态；覆盖的状态；隐蔽性
The state of being hooded or covered; the quality of being hidden or concealed.
The hoodedness of the figure made it difficult to identify.  这个身影戴着兜帽，难以辨认。
The monk's hoodedness gave him an air of mystery.  僧侣戴着兜帽，使他显得神秘莫测。
concealment, covering, disguise
openness, exposure
noun
