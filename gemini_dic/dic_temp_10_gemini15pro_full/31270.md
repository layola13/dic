chapeau:/ʃæˈpoʊ/ (US), /ʃæˈpəʊ/ (UK)| n.|帽子；(尤指)女帽；(比喻)敬意，祝贺|A hat, especially a woman's hat. Figuratively, an expression of admiration or congratulations.|She wore a wide-brimmed chapeau to the races. 她戴着一顶宽边帽去赛马场。|He doffed his chapeau in greeting. 他脱帽致意。|synonyms: hat, bonnet, millinery|antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical hat, but for the figurative meaning: criticism, disapproval)|noun
