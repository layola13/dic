diffission:/dɪˈfɪʃən/ | n. | 扩散，弥散; 分裂生殖 | The process of spreading or scattering something over a wide area; reproduction by means of fission. | The diffission of light through a prism creates a rainbow. 光线通过棱镜的扩散形成了彩虹。| synonyms: dispersion, scattering, dissemination, propagation, fission | antonyms: concentration, gathering, accumulation | noun

diffission:/dɪˈfɪʃən/ | n. | (生物)分裂生殖 | Reproduction by means of fission. | Amoeba reproduce by diffission.  变形虫通过分裂生殖。 | synonyms: fission, binary fission, schizogony | antonyms: fusion, sexual reproduction | noun
