owing:/ˈoʊɪŋ/| adj.|欠的，应付的；由于，因为|due; as a result of; because of|例句：The money is owing to him. 这笔钱欠他的。|近义词：due, payable, outstanding|反义词:paid|词性:形容词

owing:/ˈoʊɪŋ/| prep.|由于，因为|because of|例句：Owing to the rain, the game was cancelled. 由于下雨，比赛取消了。|近义词：because of, due to, on account of|反义词:despite|词性:介词
