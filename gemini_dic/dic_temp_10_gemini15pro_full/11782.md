arquebusade:/ˌɑːrkɪbəˈseɪd/| n.|火枪水，火枪的洗液; (旧时)一种治疗枪伤的药水|A medicinal drink, originally believed to cure wounds caused by an arquebus (an early type of firearm).  It was typically made of wine or brandy with various herbs and spices.|The wounded soldier was given an arquebusade.  这名受伤的士兵被给予了火枪水。|Synonyms:  remedy, elixir, potion|Antonyms: poison, toxin|noun

arquebusade:/ˌɑːrkɪbəˈseɪd/| n.|火枪，早期火器|An early type of firearm, also known as a "hackbut" or "hook gun". This definition is less common than the medicinal one.|The arquebusade was an important weapon in 16th-century warfare.  火绳枪是16世纪战争中的一种重要武器。|Synonyms: firearm, gun, musket|Antonyms: peace, diplomacy|noun
