distressful:/dɪˈstrɛsfʊl/
adjective
令人痛苦的；令人苦恼的
Causing distress or worry; upsetting.
The news of her accident was deeply distressful. 她发生事故的消息令人深感不安。
近义词: upsetting, worrying, disturbing, agonizing, harrowing
反义词: comforting, reassuring, calming, soothing
词性: adjective

adjective

Causing distress or worry; upsetting.
It was a distressful time for the whole family. 这对全家来说是一段痛苦的时期。

ETYMOLOGY->
From distress + -ful.  Distress itself comes from Old French destresse, from Vulgar Latin *districtia, from Latin districtus, past participle of distringere "to draw tight, stretch apart, pull asunder, distract," from dis- "apart" + stringere "to draw tight."
