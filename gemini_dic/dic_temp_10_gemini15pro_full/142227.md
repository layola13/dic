ornithotomist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒtəmɪst/ | n. | 鸟类解剖学家 | A person who dissects birds; one who practices ornithotomy.  |  The ornithotomist carefully examined the bird's wing structure.  鸟类解剖学家仔细检查了鸟的翅膀结构。 |  bird anatomist, avian anatomist |  None | noun
Etymology: From Greek *ornitho-* (bird) + *-tomos* (cutting) + *-ist* (one who practices).
