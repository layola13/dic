inscrutable:/ɪnˈskruːtəbl/| adj.|难以捉摸的，高深莫测的|not readily investigated, interpreted, or understood|The ancient ruins held an inscrutable mystery.  古老的废墟隐藏着一个高深莫测的秘密。|例句：He stared at her with an inscrutable gaze. 他以一种难以捉摸的目光注视着她。|近义词：impenetrable, incomprehensible|反义词:comprehensible, understandable, clear|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inscrutabilis, from Latin in- "not" + scrutari "to search, examine"  (from PIE root *skreu- "to cut, scratch").
