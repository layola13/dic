orthognathism: /ˌɔːrθəɡˈnæθɪzəm/ | n. | 正颌；颌骨正常 | The condition of having straight jaws and a normal occlusion, where the upper and lower teeth meet correctly. |  Her orthognathism contributed to her perfect smile. 她颌骨的正常让她拥有完美的笑容。| synonym: normal occlusion | antonym: prognathism, retrognathism | noun
