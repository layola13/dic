five-a-side:/ˈfaɪv ə ˈsaɪd/| adj. n.|五人制的，五人制足球|A game of football played with five players on each team. Typically played on a smaller pitch than a standard football game.| We played five-a-side last night. 我们昨晚踢了五人制足球。|近义词：small-sided football, futsal|反义词:eleven-a-side, full-sided football|词性:adjective, noun
five-a-sides:/ˈfaɪv ə ˈsaɪdz/| n.|五人制足球比赛|Games of five-a-side football.|The local sports centre runs five-a-sides every Tuesday evening. 当地体育中心每周二晚上都举办五人制足球比赛。|近义词：small-sided games, futsal matches|反义词:eleven-a-side matches, full-sided games|词性:noun
