delphi:/ˈdelfaɪ/ /ˈdɛlfaɪ/| n. |德尔菲, 德尔斐|A town in ancient Greece, on the slopes of Mount Parnassus. It was the site of the Temple of Apollo and the oracle of Delphi, which was considered the center of the world by the ancient Greeks.|例句：The oracle at Delphi was famous throughout the ancient world. 德尔菲的神谕在古代世界闻名遐迩。

例句：Many pilgrims traveled to Delphi to consult the oracle. 许多朝圣者前往德尔菲咨询神谕。|近义词：oracle|反义词:|noun
