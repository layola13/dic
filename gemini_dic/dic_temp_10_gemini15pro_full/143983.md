outrank:/ˌaʊtˈræŋk/| vt.|超过，级别高于|To have a higher rank or position than someone or something. |例句：He outranks me in the army. 他在军队里的级别比我高。

例句：The new law outranks all previous legislation on this matter. 这项新法律的效力高于所有之前关于此事的立法。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, better, top|反义词:be outranked by, be subordinate to|词性:动词
