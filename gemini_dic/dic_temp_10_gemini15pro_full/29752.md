catosaurus: /ˌkætəˈsɔːrəs/
n.
猫龙 (一种虚构的生物)
A fictional creature, a blend of "cat" and "saurus" (dinosaur), implying a feline-like dinosaur.
The catosaurus stalked its prey through the jungle, its sleek, striped fur rippling in the dappled sunlight.  猫龙在丛林中跟踪猎物，其光滑的条纹皮毛在斑驳的阳光下荡漾。
The children imagined a catosaurus as their loyal pet, purring and playing fetch with giant bones. 孩子们把猫龙想象成他们忠诚的宠物，发出呼噜声，并用巨大的骨头玩取物游戏。
近义词:  gryphon, dragon (loosely)
反义词: (None, as it is a fictional creature)
