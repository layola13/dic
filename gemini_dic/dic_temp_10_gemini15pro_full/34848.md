clinkant:/ˈklɪŋkənt/ | adj. | 铿锵的，叮当响的 |  (of sounds) like the sound of metal striking metal; metallic-sounding. | The clinkant sound of the blacksmith's hammer filled the air. 铁匠锤子的铿锵声充满了空气。| The spurs made a clinkant noise as he walked. 马刺在他走动时发出叮当声。 | 近义词：clinking, jangling, metallic, resonant | 反义词：dull, muted, soft | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *clinken, from Old English *clincian, frequentative of clincan (“to clink, ring, tinkle”). Cognate with Dutch klinken (“to clink, ring, sound”), German klingen (“to ring, sound, resound”), Icelandic klingja (“to ring, tinkle, clink”).
