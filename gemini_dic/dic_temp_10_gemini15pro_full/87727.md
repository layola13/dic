hexest:/ˈhɛksɪst/| adj.| 最十六进制的，十六进制的 |  Relating to or based on the number sixteen or the hexadecimal system.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered a neologism formed by adding "-est" to "hex" (short for hexadecimal). |  The hexest value of this byte is FF.  这个字节的十六进制最大值是FF。 | Near Synonyms: hexadecimal | Antonym: decimal, binary | Adjective
