noninheritable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/| adj.|不可继承的，非遗传的|Not able to be inherited; not passed on from parent to offspring genetically.|This disease is caused by environmental factors and is noninheritable.  这种疾病是由环境因素引起的，不具有遗传性。|Synonym: non-hereditary, acquired|Antonym: inheritable, hereditary, genetic|Adjective

noninheritable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/| n.|不可继承之物|Something that cannot be inherited.|Certain learned behaviors are noninheritables.  某些习得行为是不可遗传的。|Synonym: non-hereditary trait|Antonym: inheritance, heritage|Noun
