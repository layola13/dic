co-founder:/ˌkoʊˈfaʊndər/| n.|共同创始人；联合创始人|A person who establishes an organization or business with one or more other people.|Example 1: Steve Wozniak was the co-founder of Apple with Steve Jobs. 史蒂夫·沃兹尼亚克和史蒂夫·乔布斯是苹果公司的共同创始人。

Example 2: The co-founders of the charity work tirelessly to raise funds.  这家慈善机构的联合创始人不知疲倦地筹集资金。|近义词：co-creator, joint founder|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:
From co- + founder. Co- comes from the Latin prefix "com-" meaning "together" or "with."  "Founder" comes from the Old French "fondre," meaning "to melt" or "to cast,"  later evolving to mean "to establish" or "to lay the foundation of."
