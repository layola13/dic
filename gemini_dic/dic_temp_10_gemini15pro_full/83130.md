gut-wrenchingly: /ˈɡʌtˌrɛntʃɪŋli/
| adv. | 令人心碎地，极其痛苦地 |  In a way that causes great emotional pain, as if one's stomach were being twisted.  It combines "gut" (referring to the stomach or intestines, often associated with strong emotions) and "wrenching" (meaning twisting violently and painfully). The "-ly" suffix turns it into an adverb. | The news of his death was gut-wrenchingly sad.  他去世的消息令人心碎。 | The film portrayed the family's suffering gut-wrenchingly. 这部电影极其痛苦地描绘了这个家庭的苦难。 |  heartbreaking, devastating, agonizing, harrowing | comforting, soothing | adverb
