defenced:/dɪˈfɛnst/ |v. | 防御，保卫 | Protected or guarded against attack. |  The city was well defenced against invaders.  这座城市防御入侵者非常牢固。| 近义词: protected, guarded, fortified | 反义词: attacked, vulnerable | 词性: 动词

defenced:/dɪˈfɛnst/ |adj. |设防的 | Protected or guarded against attack. |  The defenced city withstood the siege. 这座设防的城市抵挡住了围攻。| 近义词: protected, guarded, fortified | 反义词: undefended, exposed | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Middle English *defensen, from Anglo-Norman defendre, from Latin dēfendere (“to ward off, defend”).

注意事项:

"defenced"虽然在某些字典中可以找到，但"defended"是更常见的拼写和用法，尤其是在美式英语中。在现代英语中，"defended" 几乎总是首选。

这个单词作为形容词的用法比较少见，通常用动词的过去分词形式 "defended" 来表示“设防的”的意思。
