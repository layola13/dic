nonrepeaters:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈpiː.təz/ | n. | 中继器 | Devices or systems that do not repeat or amplify signals.  Specifically in telecommunications, circuits or lines that directly connect two points without intermediate amplification or regeneration. |  The old telephone system used nonrepeaters for short distances. 旧电话系统使用中继器进行短距离通信。|  Direct lines, point-to-point connections | Repeaters, amplifiers | noun
