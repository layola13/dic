malleability:/ˌmæliəˈbɪləti/
n.|延展性，可塑性，顺从|The malleability of gold makes it easy to shape into jewelry.  黄金的延展性使它易于塑造成珠宝。|近义词：ductility, plasticity, flexibility, suppleness|反义词：rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness|词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *malleabilitatem, from Latin malleabilis (“that can be hammered”), from malleare (“to hammer”), from malleus (“hammer”).
