clamberer:/ˈklæmərər/ (US), /ˈklæmbərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |攀登者，攀爬者|A person who clambers; someone who climbs with difficulty, using both hands and feet.|The clamberers finally reached the summit, exhausted but triumphant. 攀登者们最终到达了山顶，筋疲力尽但欣喜若狂。|近义词：climber, mountaineer, scaler|反义词:descender|词性:noun
