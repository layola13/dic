ferriprussiate:/ˌfɛriˈprʌsiˌeɪt/ | n. | 铁氰化物；赤血盐(尤指铁氰化钾) |A salt containing the ferricyanide anion. ferriprussiate:/ˌfɛriˈprʌsiˌeɪt/ | adj. | 铁氰化物的 |Containing or relating to the ferricyanide anion. | Ferriprussiate solutions are light sensitive.  铁氰化物溶液对光敏感。 | synonym: ferricyanic | antonym: ferroprussiate (similar to the noun form, not a true opposite) | adjective
