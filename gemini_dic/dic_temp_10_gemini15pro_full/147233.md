overmedicates:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪts/
verb
过度用药
To prescribe or administer too much medication to someone.
The doctor overmedicated the patient, leading to adverse side effects.  医生给病人开了过多的药物，导致了不良副作用。
He was concerned that his elderly mother was being overmedicated. 他担心他年迈的母亲被过度用药。
overdose, overprescribe
undermedicate
v.
