copperhead: /ˈkɑːpərˌhed/ (US) /ˈkɒpəhed/ (UK)| n.|铜斑蛇; (美)铜头蝮蛇 (一种毒蛇); (美，旧，贬)北方同情南方的人 (尤指在南北战争时期);|A copperhead is a venomous snake found in North America.  铜斑蛇是一种在北美发现的毒蛇。|例句：The hiker was bitten by a copperhead.  这名徒步旅行者被铜斑蛇咬了。

例句：During the Civil War, copperheads were Northerners who sympathized with the South. 在南北战争期间，北方同情南方的人被称为“铜头蝮”。|近义词：viper, rattlesnake (for the snake); peace Democrat, Doughface (for the historical political meaning)|反义词: (For the historical political meaning) Unionist, abolitionist |词性:noun
