moonstone:/ˈmuːnˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈmuːnˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |月光石；月长石|A semiprecious stone, typically of a pearly white or bluish-white color, exhibiting a schiller effect. It is a variety of feldspar.|She wore a beautiful moonstone pendant. 她戴着一个漂亮的月光石吊坠。|Synonyms: adularia, hecatolite |Antonyms: N/A |Noun
