dumbfoundering:/dʌmˈfaʊndərɪŋ/
adjective
令人震惊的，使人目瞪口呆的
extremely surprising or shocking
The news of his sudden death was utterly dumbfoundering. 他突然去世的消息令人震惊。
近义词: astonishing, astounding, bewildering, shocking, stunning
反义词: unsurprising, expected, predictable
词性: adjective

dumbfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
惊呆的，目瞪口呆的
so surprised and confused that you cannot speak
He was dumbfounded by the news.  他被这个消息惊呆了。
近义词: astonished, astounded, bewildered, speechless, stunned
反义词: unsurprising, expected, predictable
词性: adjective

词源:
From dumbfound, from dumb (unable to speak) + confound (to confuse and surprise).  由dumb（无法说话）+ confound（使困惑和惊讶）组成。
