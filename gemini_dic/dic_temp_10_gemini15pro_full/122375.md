mking: This is likely a misspelling of "making."  Assuming this is the intended word, the following analysis applies:

making: /ˈmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. adj.| 制作；制造；构成；创造；（产品的）制造；（钱的）赚取；性格；气质 | The process of creating or forming something; the way in which something is made;  the qualities that form someone's character. |  例句：The making of the film took two years.  这部电影的制作花了2年时间。| 近义词：creation, production, formation, construction | 反义词：destruction, demolition | 词性：名词；形容词


making: /ˈmeɪkɪŋ/ | adj.|  （产品的）制造；性格；气质 | Used to describe something related to the process of creation or the qualities that form someone's character | 例句： This is a wine-making region. 这是一个葡萄酒产区。 | 近义词：formative, constitutive | 反义词：destructive | 词性：形容词



词源: From Old English 'macian,'  meaning "to make, form, build, do, perform."  Related to Old Norse 'maka' and Old Frisian 'makia.'
