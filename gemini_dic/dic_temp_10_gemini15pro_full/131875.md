noncontagious:/ˌnɒnkənˈteɪdʒəs/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|非传染性的|Not capable of being transmitted by contact.  Not spreading from one person to another by contact.|The doctor assured me that the rash was noncontagious.|Synonyms: noncommunicable, noninfectious|Antonyms: contagious, infectious, communicable|adjective
