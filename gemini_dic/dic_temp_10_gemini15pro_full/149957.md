palls:/pɔːlz/| v. pall:/pɔːl/| n.| 棺罩；阴郁的气氛| a cloth spread over a coffin, hearse, or tomb.|例句：A pall of smoke hung over the city. 城市上空笼罩着一层烟雾。|近义词：covering, shroud, blanket|反义词：brightness, light|词性:名词
