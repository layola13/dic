nanoscience:/ˈnænoʊsaɪəns/| n. |纳米科学|The study of structures and materials on an ultra-small scale, at the level of atoms and molecules.  Dealing with dimensions between approximately 1 and 100 nanometers.|Scientists are making significant breakthroughs in nanoscience. 科学家们在纳米科学领域取得了重大突破。|Near synonyms: nanotechnology, nanomaterials science|Antonym: macroscience|noun
