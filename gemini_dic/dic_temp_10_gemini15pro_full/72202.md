florae:/ˈflɔːriː/
noun
植物群
the plants of a particular region or period
例句：The florae of the Amazon rainforest are incredibly diverse. 亚马逊雨林的植物群极其多样。

例句：Studying the florae of ancient times can provide clues about the climate. 研究古代的植物群可以提供有关气候的线索。

近义词：plants, vegetation
反义词：fauna (动物群)
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flōriae*, plural of *flōria*, meaning "flowers," related to *Flōra*, the Roman goddess of flowers and spring.
