encrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/ /ɛnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/
n.
结壳；硬壳；外壳；包壳
A hard coating or deposit on a surface.
The kettle was covered in an encrustation of limescale. 水壶上覆盖着一层石灰垢。
crust, coating, deposit, covering
