nonseraphic:/ˌnɒnsəˈræfɪk/ | adj. | 非天使的，不似天使的，世俗的 | Not angelic; not resembling or suggesting an angel; earthly. |  Her nonseraphic laughter filled the room, shattering the solemn atmosphere. 她不似天使的笑声充满了房间，打破了庄严的气氛。

The politician's nonseraphic concerns were focused on re-election. 这位政客世俗的关注点集中在连任上。 | 近义词：earthly, mundane, worldly, secular, profane | 反义词：seraphic, angelic, divine, heavenly | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + seraphic (from seraph, an angel of the highest order).
