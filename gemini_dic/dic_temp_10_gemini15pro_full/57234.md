downheartedly:/ˌdaʊnˈhɑːrtɪdli/| adv.|沮丧地，灰心地|In a manner showing discouragement and low spirits.  Feeling or expressing sadness and lack of hope.|He walked downheartedly away after hearing the bad news.  听到坏消息后，他沮丧地走开了。|近义词：dejectedly, despondently, sadly, glumly|反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "downhearted" + "-ly."  "Downhearted" is formed from "down" (meaning low in spirits) + "hearted."
