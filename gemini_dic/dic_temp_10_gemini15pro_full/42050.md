cosurfactants:/koʊˈsərˈfæktənts/ | n. | 助表面活性剂 | Substances added to surfactant solutions to enhance or modify the properties of the surfactant system. Often used to increase the solubility of surfactants or to reduce interfacial tension further than the surfactant alone can achieve. | Example: Cosurfactants are often used in microemulsion formulations to stabilize the system. 助表面活性剂常用于微乳液配方中以稳定体系。

Example: The addition of cosurfactants can improve the wetting properties of the cleaning solution. 添加助表面活性剂可以改善清洁溶液的润湿性能。 | Synonym: cosolvent, cosolute | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but a high concentration could destabilize a system instead of enhance it) | noun
