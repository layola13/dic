footprints:/ˈfʊtprɪnts/| n. |脚印，足迹|Marks left by a foot or shoe, often indicating a path or presence.|The children's footprints were visible in the fresh snow. 孩子们的脚印在新鲜的雪地上清晰可见。|Synonyms: tracks, impressions, marks|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "erasure" in a metaphorical sense)|Noun

footprints:/ˈfʊtprɪnts/| n. |(比喻) 痕迹，影响|The impact or evidence of something.|The company's footprints can be seen in communities across the country.  这家公司的影响力在全国各地都能看到。|Synonyms:  legacy, impact, influence|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|Noun
