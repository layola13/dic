obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
verb
阻碍，阻塞，妨碍
to block or get in the way of something or someone, thus preventing movement or progress
The fallen tree obstructed the road.  倒下的树阻塞了道路。
block, impede, hinder
facilitate, aid, assist
词性: 动词


obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/
noun
阻碍，障碍物
something that blocks or gets in the way, preventing movement or progress
The construction work is causing an obstruction to traffic. 施工造成了交通阻塞。
obstacle, barrier, impediment
assistance, aid, clearance
词性: 名词


obstructive:/əbˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
阻碍的，妨碍的
causing or tending to cause obstruction
His obstructive behavior made it difficult to reach a decision. 他阻挠的行为使我们难以做出决定。
hindering, impeding, blocking
helpful, cooperative, facilitating
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstruere*, from *ob-* ("against, in the way of") + *struere* ("to pile up, build").
