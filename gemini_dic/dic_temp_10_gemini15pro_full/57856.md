drest:/drɛst/ | adj. | 穿着盛装的；打扮的 | Dressed is the past participle of the verb "dress," meaning to put clothes on. It is often used as an adjective to describe someone who is wearing clothes, especially in a particular way.  | She was drest for the occasion in a beautiful gown. 她穿着漂亮的礼服出席了这个场合。 | The children were already drest and ready to go. 孩子们已经穿好衣服准备出发了。 | 近义词：attired, clad, clothed, garbed | 反义词：undressed, naked | 词性：形容词

词源：From Middle English *dressed*, *drest*, past participle of *dressen*, from Old French *dresser* (to arrange, prepare, straighten), from Vulgar Latin *dīrectāre*, frequentative form of Latin *dīrigere* (to guide, arrange).
