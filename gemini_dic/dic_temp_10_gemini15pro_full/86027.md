healings:/ˈhiːlɪŋz/
noun
治愈，痊愈；治疗；康复
the process of becoming well again, especially after a cut or other injury
The clinic specializes in spiritual healings.  这家诊所专门从事精神治疗。

近义词:recoveries, cures, remedies
反义词:injuries, harms, ailments
词性:n. (noun)


Etymology: From Old English *hæling, from Proto-Germanic *hailiŋą (“healing”), from *hailjaz (“whole, healthy”).
