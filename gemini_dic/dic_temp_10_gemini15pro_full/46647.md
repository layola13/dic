damnification:/ˌdæmnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
损害，伤害；赔偿
The act of causing damage or loss; the state of being damaged or injured; compensation for damage or loss.
The damnification caused by the flood was extensive. 洪水造成的损害是巨大的。

近义词: damage, harm, loss, injury, detriment, prejudice
反义词: benefit, advantage, improvement

词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin damnificātiō, from damnificāre  "to cause loss or damage," from Latin damnificus "causing loss," from damnum "loss, damage" + -ficus "-making."
