earthworm:/ˈɜːθwɜːrm/ (US) /ˈɜːθwɜːm/ (UK)| n.|蚯蚓|A burrowing annelid worm that lives in the soil. Earthworms play an important role in aerating and enriching the soil.|例句：The earthworm burrowed deep into the ground. 蚯蚓钻到地里深处去了。

例句：Earthworms are beneficial to gardens. 蚯蚓对花园有益。|近义词：night crawler, angleworm|反义词: None|词性: noun
