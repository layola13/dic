extrovert: /ˈekstrəvɜːrt/ (US) /ˈekstrəvɜːt/ (UK)
n. | 外向的人 |  A person who is sociable and outgoing and enjoys being with other people. |  She's a real extrovert, always the life of the party. 她真是个外向的人，总是聚会的焦点。 |  gregarious, outgoing, sociable | introvert, shy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *extravertere "to turn outward," from extra- "outside" + vertere "to turn."  Coined by Carl Jung.
