mitigator:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtər/| n.| 减轻者，缓和者，缓解因素 |Something that mitigates or lessens the severity of something else. | The judge considered her youth as a mitigating factor in sentencing. 法官认为她的年轻是量刑时的一个减轻罪责的因素。| synonyms: alleviator, moderator, softener | antonyms: aggravator, intensifier | noun

mitigate:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/| v.| 减轻，缓和，缓解 | To make less severe, serious, or painful. |  The medicine helped to mitigate the pain.  这药有助于减轻疼痛。| synonyms: alleviate, lessen, reduce, ease | antonyms: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate | verb
