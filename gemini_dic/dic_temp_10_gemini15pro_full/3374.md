affront:/əˈfrʌnt/| vt. n.|冒犯，侮辱；面对|To insult, especially to someone's face; an open or intentional insult.|She was affronted by his rude remarks. 她被他的粗鲁言论冒犯了。|He stared at her, his gaze an affront. 他盯着她，眼神中充满了冒犯。|Synonyms: insult, offend, provoke, outrage|Antonyms: compliment, praise, honor|verb, noun
