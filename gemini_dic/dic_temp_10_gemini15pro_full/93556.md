imagineers:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈnɪərz/
n.|幻想工程师；创意工程师|A person who combines imagination and engineering skills, especially in the design and construction of theme parks and attractions.|Walt Disney Imagineers are the creative minds behind Disney's theme parks.  华特迪士尼幻想工程师是迪士尼主题公园背后的创意人才。|
近义词: designers, creators, innovators
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (plural)


imagineer:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈnɪər/
n.|幻想工程师；创意工程师|A person who combines imagination and engineering skills, especially in the design and construction of theme parks and attractions.|He's an imagineer at Universal Studios. 他是环球影城的幻想工程师。|
近义词: designer, creator, innovator
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (singular)


Etymology:  A portmanteau of "imagine" and "engineer," coined by Walt Disney. 由“imagine”和“engineer”组合而成，由华特·迪士尼创造。
