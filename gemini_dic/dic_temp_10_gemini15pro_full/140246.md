offprinted:/ˈɔːfˌprɪntɪd/ | adj. |  抽印的；分开印刷的 | Printed as a separate item from a larger work, such as a journal or book. | This article was originally published in Nature and has been offprinted for wider distribution. 这篇文章最初发表在《自然》杂志上，现已抽印出来以便更广泛地发行。 | offprint, reprint, separate | original | adjective

offprint:/ˈɔːfˌprɪnt/ | n. | 抽印本；单印本 | A separately printed copy of an article, chapter, or other excerpt from a larger publication. |  I requested an offprint of the professor's groundbreaking research paper. 我请求一份教授那篇开创性研究论文的抽印本。 | reprint, pre-print | original, manuscript | noun
