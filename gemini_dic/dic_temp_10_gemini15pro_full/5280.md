allophone:/ˈæləfoʊn/ (US) /ˈæləfəʊn/ (UK)| n. |音位变体；同位异音|One of two or more variants of the same phoneme.  An allophone is a phonetic variant of a phoneme in a particular language.  Phonemes are abstract units, while allophones are the actual sounds produced.|The aspirated 'p' in 'pin' and the unaspirated 'p' in 'spin' are allophones of the same phoneme /p/. 送气的'p'在'pin'中和不送气的'p'在'spin'中是音位/p/的同位异音。

The dark 'l' in 'pull' and the clear 'l' in 'lip' are allophones of the phoneme /l/. 'pull'中的暗'l'和'lip'中的清晰'l'是音位/l/的同位异音。|近义词: variant, variation|反义词: phoneme|词性:noun
