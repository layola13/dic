holdall:/ˈhəʊldɔːl/ (UK) /ˈhoʊldɔːl/ (US)| n.| 手提旅行包，旅行袋|A large bag, typically made of strong fabric or leather, used for carrying clothes and other personal belongings when traveling.|She packed her clothes in a holdall for the weekend trip. 她把衣服装进旅行袋准备周末旅行。|近义词：duffel bag, travel bag, carryall|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "suitcase" for a more structured alternative)|noun
