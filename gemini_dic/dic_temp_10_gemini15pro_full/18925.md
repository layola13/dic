bespatters:/bɪˈspætərz/| vt.|溅污，玷污|To spatter something with a liquid or with many small objects. |The car bespattered the pedestrians with mud. 汽车溅了行人一身泥。|近义词：splash, splatter, spray, douse|反义词:clean, wipe|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + spatter.  "Spatter" comes from a Middle Low German word "spattern" meaning "to splash."
