felsic:/ˈfɛlsɪk/| adj.|长英质的，富硅酸盐的|Relating to or denoting igneous rocks composed chiefly of feldspars and silica, typically light in color.|Granite is a felsic rock. 花岗岩是一种长英质岩石。|The volcano erupted felsic lava. 这座火山喷发了长英质熔岩。|近义词：silicic, acidic|反义词：mafic, basic|词性:形容词

词源:
Formed from fel(dspar) + si(lica) + -ic. 由feldspar（长石）+ silica（二氧化硅）+ -ic组成.
