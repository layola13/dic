cocultures:/ˈkoʊkʌltʃərz/
noun
共同培养;共培养物
The practice of growing two or more different kinds of cells together.
例句：Cocultures of epithelial cells and fibroblasts were used to study cell-cell interactions.  上皮细胞和成纤维细胞的共培养被用来研究细胞间的相互作用。

例句：The researchers used cocultures of bacteria and fungi to investigate the effects of microbial interactions. 研究人员使用细菌和真菌的共培养来研究微生物相互作用的影响。

近义词：mixed cultures, combined cultures
反义词: monoculture
词性:n.
