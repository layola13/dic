botanical:/bəˈtænɪkəl/ /bɑːˈtænɪkəl/| adj.|植物学的；植物的|Relating to plants; their study, or the study of the structure, properties, and uses of plants.|The botanical gardens are a beautiful place to visit. 植物园是一个美丽的地方。|近义词：floral, plant, vegetal|反义词:zoological, animal|adjective

botanical:/bəˈtænɪkəl/ /bɑːˈtænɪkəl/| n.|植物；草药|A plant, especially one used for medicinal purposes or its study.|He is studying the effects of various botanicals. 他正在研究各种植物的功效。|近义词：herb, plant|反义词: N/A |noun
