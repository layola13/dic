assayers:/əˈseɪərz/
n.
化验员；鉴定者
People who examine and test metals or ores to determine their ingredients and quality.
The assayers carefully examined the gold nuggets. 化验员仔细检查了金块。
synonyms: testers, analysts, examiners, valuators
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)
noun
