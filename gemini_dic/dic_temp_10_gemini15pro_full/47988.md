decimate: /ˈdɛsɪmeɪt/ | vt. | 大量毁灭，严重削弱 | to destroy a large part of something; to kill or destroy a large number of something | Disease decimated the population. 疾病毁灭了大部分人口。| synonyms: destroy, devastate, ruin, wreck | antonyms: build, construct, create, repair | verb

decimate: /ˈdɛsɪmeɪt/ | vt. | (古罗马)每十人杀一人 | to kill one in every ten of (a group of soldiers or others) as a punishment for mutiny or other serious offenses (historical) | The Roman army decimated the rebellious legion. 罗马军队对叛乱的军团处以十抽一的惩罚。| synonyms: slaughter, execute, punish | antonyms: pardon, spare, forgive | verb
