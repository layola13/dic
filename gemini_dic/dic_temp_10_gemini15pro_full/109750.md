leviable:/ˈlɛviəbl/| adj.|可征收的，可课税的|Subject to a levy; that can be levied.  Something that can be imposed or collected as a tax or fee.|The government announced that the new tax was leviable on all imported goods. 政府宣布新税将对所有进口商品征收。|Synonyms: taxable, assessable, chargeable|Antonyms: non-taxable, exempt|adjective
