interlinks:/ˈɪntərˌlɪŋks/ | n. | 互相连接，相互关联 |  Connections or relationships between different parts of something, often a system or network. | The website's interlinks make it easy to navigate.  这个网站的相互链接使其易于浏览。 | synonyms: connections, links, relationships | antonyms: disconnections, separations | noun


interlink:/ˌɪntərˈlɪŋk/ | v. | 互相连接，相互关联 |  To connect or link things together. | The different departments are interlinked to ensure smooth operation.  不同的部门相互关联，以确保顺利运作。 | synonyms: connect, link, join, integrate | antonyms: disconnect, separate, divide | verb
