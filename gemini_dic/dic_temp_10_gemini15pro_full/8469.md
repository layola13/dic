antiassimilations:/ˈæntiəˌsɪməˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 反同化，反同化作用 | The process by which sounds become less similar to each other.  It's the opposite of assimilation, where sounds become more alike. |  Antiassimilations are common in some languages, preventing adjacent sounds from merging. 反同化在某些语言中很常见，可以防止相邻的声音合并。| dissimilation | assimilation | noun
