carvery: /ˈkɑːvəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrvəri/ (US)| n.|雕刻店，切肉处，自助餐馆|A restaurant or shop where cooked meat is carved and served to customers.  A place where meat is carved, especially a buffet-style restaurant. |例句：We went to the carvery for Sunday lunch. 我们去自助餐馆吃了周日午餐。

例句：The hotel carvery offers a wide selection of roast meats. 这家酒店的切肉处提供各种各样的烤肉。|近义词：buffet, cafeteria, smorgasbord|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a la carte restaurant)|词性:noun
