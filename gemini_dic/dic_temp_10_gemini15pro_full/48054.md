declaimers:/dɪˈkleɪmərz/
n.
朗诵者；演讲者；高谈阔论者
A person who speaks rhetorically or dramatically, often without sincerity or true conviction.
The declaimers filled the hall with their passionate speeches, but few seemed genuinely moved. 那些慷慨激昂的演讲者挤满了大厅，但很少有人真正感动。
近义词: orators, speakers, rhetoricians
反义词: listeners, audience

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From the Latin verb "declamare" meaning "to cry out, proclaim, declare."
