effectually:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəli/
adverb
有效地；彻底地
in a way that produces the intended result or a successful result.
The problem was effectually dealt with.  这个问题得到了有效的处理。
The rumor was effectually quashed.  这个谣言被有效地平息了。
近义词: effectively, successfully, completely, thoroughly
反义词: ineffectually, unsuccessfully, partially
词性: adverb
词源: From Late Latin *effectualis, from Latin effectus (“effect”) + -alis.
