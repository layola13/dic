horseshoe:/ˈhɔːrsʃuː/ (US) /ˈhɔːʃuː/ (UK)| n.|马蹄铁；马蹄形物|A U-shaped metal plate nailed to the bottom of a horse's hoof to protect it.|The blacksmith carefully shaped the horseshoe. 铁匠仔细地塑造马蹄铁。|synonyms:  horseshoe iron,  ferrule (for tool handles)|antonyms: N/A|noun
horseshoe:/ˈhɔːrsʃuː/ (US) /ˈhɔːʃuː/ (UK)| adj.|马蹄形的|Shaped like a horseshoe.|The horseshoe bend in the river was a popular fishing spot. 河流的马蹄形弯道是一个受欢迎的钓鱼点。|synonyms: U-shaped, arcuate|antonyms: straight, linear|adjective
