indulgingly: /ɪnˈdʌldʒɪŋli/
adverb
纵容地，放纵地，溺爱地
in a way that allows someone to have or do whatever they want
She smiled indulgingly at her grandson. 她对着孙子纵容地笑了笑。
Near Antonyms: sternly, strictly, disapprovingly
Near Synonyms: leniently, permissively, tolerantly
词性: 副词
