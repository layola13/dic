erythrin:/ˈɛrɪθrɪn/| n.|刺桐素; 刺桐属植物|A crystalline compound, C₂₀H₂₂O₁₀, extracted from the leguminous tree *Erythrina variegata*. It is poisonous, causing paralysis.|Erythrin is a powerful toxin found in certain coral reef fishes.|近义词:coral tree toxin, ichthyotoxin|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially antidote or antitoxin)|noun
