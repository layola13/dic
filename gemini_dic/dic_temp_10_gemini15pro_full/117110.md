mealtimes:/ˈmiːltaɪmz/
n.
用餐时间
The times at which meals are regularly eaten.
We try to have regular mealtimes. 我们尽量定时用餐。
近义词：eating times, meal periods
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "snack times" or "between meals")
词性: Noun
