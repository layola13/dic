ornamentality:/ˌɔːrnəmənˈtælɪti/
n.
装饰性，观赏性
The quality of being ornamental; serving or intended as an ornament.
The architect emphasized the building's ornamentality over its functionality.  这位建筑师强调建筑的装饰性胜过其功能性。
The ornamentality of the cake was impressive, but it tasted a bit dry.  蛋糕的装饰性令人印象深刻，但尝起来有点干。
近义词:decorativeness, embellishment, adornment
反义词:plainness, simplicity, austerity
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ornamentalite, from Middle French ornamentalité, from Latin ornamentum (“equipment, adornment, embellishment”), from ornare (“to furnish, equip, adorn, embellish”).
