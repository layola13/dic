ensample:/ɪnˈsæmpəl/ |n.|榜样，例子，样本|An example or model, especially one that is intended to be imitated.|The teacher used the student's essay as an ensample for the rest of the class. 老师用这个学生的文章作为全班其他学生的榜样。|Synonyms: example, model, paradigm, exemplar|Antonyms: warning, deterrent|noun

ensample:/ɪnˈsæmpəl/ |n.|警告，前车之鉴|A warning or cautionary example.|The ruins of the city served as an ensample of the dangers of unchecked greed. 这座城市的废墟是贪婪失控的危险的前车之鉴。|Synonyms: warning, caution, admonition|Antonyms: encouragement, incentive|noun
