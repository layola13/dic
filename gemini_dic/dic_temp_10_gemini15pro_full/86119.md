heartburn:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/| n.|胃灼热，烧心|A burning sensation in the chest, caused by acid refluxing from the stomach.|例句：I ate too much spicy food and now I have heartburn. 我吃了太多辛辣的食物，现在胃灼热。|例句：He often suffers from heartburn after a large meal.  他经常在吃完大餐后感到烧心。|近义词：acid indigestion, pyrosis|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
