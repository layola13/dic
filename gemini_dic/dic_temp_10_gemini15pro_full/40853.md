cooee:/kʊˈiː/
| n. vi. vt.|呼喊声；招呼声；用呼喊声招呼|A loud, clear call, especially used in the Australian bush to attract attention. |例句：He cooeed to his friends across the valley. 他在山谷对面向他的朋友们呼喊。|近义词：shout, yell, call, holler|反义词:whisper, murmur
