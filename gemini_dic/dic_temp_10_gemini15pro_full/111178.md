lithoglyphic: /ˌlɪθəˈɡlɪfɪk/
adjective
石刻的；在石头上雕刻的
Relating to lithoglyphy, the art of engraving on gems or stones.
The museum boasts a fascinating collection of lithoglyphic artifacts, showcasing intricate designs carved into various stones.  博物馆收藏了大量迷人的石刻文物，展示了雕刻在各种石头上的复杂图案。
The artisan's lithoglyphic skills were evident in the delicate details of the cameo.  这位工匠的石刻技艺在浮雕的精致细节中显而易见。
近义词: engraved, carved, inscribed
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) uncarved, plain, smooth
词性: adjective
