alloxan:/əˈlæksæn/ | n. | 阿脲，四氧嘧啶 | A crystalline oxidation product of uric acid, C₄H₂N₂O₄, that produces experimental diabetes.  It is a heterocyclic compound. | Alloxan can be used to induce diabetes in laboratory animals. 阿脲可以用来诱导实验动物的糖尿病。 |  synonyms: tetraoxopyrimidine | antonyms: None (No direct chemical antonym exists) | noun
