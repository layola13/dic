hypertoxicities:/ˌhaɪpərˌtɒkˈsɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 过度毒性；超毒性 |  The condition of being excessively toxic; extreme toxicity.  This refers to instances where a substance exhibits toxicity far beyond what is typical or expected. | The patient experienced severe hypertoxicities as a result of the overdose.  病人由于药物过量而经历了严重的过度毒性。|  The new chemotherapy drug showed unexpected hypertoxicities in clinical trials. 这种新的化疗药物在临床试验中显示出意想不到的过度毒性。 | Near synonyms: extreme toxicities, excessive toxicities | Antonyms:  low toxicity, mild toxicity | Noun
