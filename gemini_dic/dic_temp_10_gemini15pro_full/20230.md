birectangular:/ˌbaɪrɛkˈtæŋɡjʊlər/
adjective
双矩形的; 有两个直角的
Having two right angles.  Specifically, in spherical geometry, a spherical triangle with two right angles.
A birectangular triangle is a triangle with two 90 degree angles.
一个双矩形三角形是一个有两个90度角的三角形。
A birectangular quadrilateral has two right angles.
一个双矩形四边形有两个直角。
Near Synonyms: right-angled, orthogonal
Antonyms: oblique, acute, obtuse
词性: adjective
