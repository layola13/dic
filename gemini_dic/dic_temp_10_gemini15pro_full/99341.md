interdebating:/ˌɪntərdiˈbeɪtɪŋ/
|adj.|互相辩论的，在…之间辩论的| Engaging in debates between or among different groups or individuals.| The two teams were interdebating the merits of different economic policies.  两个队正在互相辩论不同经济政策的优劣。|近义词: discussing, arguing, debating amongst |反义词: agreeing, concurring|词性: 形容词


词源： "inter-" (prefix) + "debating" (present participle of "debate").  "Inter-" comes from Latin and means "between, among, mutually, reciprocally." "Debate" comes from Old French "debatre" meaning "to fight, contend."
