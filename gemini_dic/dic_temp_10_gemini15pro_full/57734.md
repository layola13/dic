dreadnesses:/ˈdrɛdnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
可怕的事物（复数）
The state of being dreadful; extreme fear or apprehension.  The accumulation of dreadful things or situations.
He spoke of the dreadnesses of war. 他讲述了战争的恐怖。
Near Synonyms: terrors, horrors, fears, anxieties
Antonyms: joys, delights, comforts, assurances
词性: 名词


dreadfulness:/ˈdrɛdflnɪs/
noun
可怕; 恐怖
The quality of being dreadful; terribleness.
She was struck by the dreadfulness of the situation. 她被情况的可怕性震惊了。
Near Synonyms: awfulness, terribleness, frightfulness, ghastliness
Antonyms: pleasantness, agreeableness, wonderfulness, delightfulness
词性: 名词


dreadful:/ˈdrɛdfəl/
adjective
可怕的; 令人恐惧的
Causing or involving great suffering, fear, or unhappiness; extremely bad or serious.
The weather has been dreadful all week.  整个星期天气都糟透了。
Near Synonyms: terrible, awful, frightful, appalling
Antonyms: wonderful, excellent, delightful, pleasant
词性: 形容词


词源：
From Middle English *dredful, from Old English *drēadfull, equivalent to dread +‎ -ful.
