over-optimistic: /ˌəʊvərˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过分乐观的 | Excessively optimistic; expecting a more favorable outcome than is realistic.  Characterized by or displaying excessive optimism. | He was over-optimistic about his chances of winning the lottery. 他对赢得彩票的机会过于乐观。| Synonyms:  excessively hopeful, unduly sanguine,  Pollyannaish | Antonyms: pessimistic, realistic, cynical | Adjective
