edictal:/iːˈdɪktəl/
adjective
法令的；布告的
Relating to or delivered by edict; decreed.
The edictal pronouncements of the king were met with resistance. 国王的法令声明遭到了抵制。

The edictal ban on public gatherings was strictly enforced.  禁止公众集会的法令得到了严格执行。

synonym: decreed, ordained, proclaimed, mandated
antonym: unproclaimed, undeclared, unauthorized
词性: adjective
