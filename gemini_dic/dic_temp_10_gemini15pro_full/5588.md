alterability:/ˌɔːltərəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 可变性，可更改性 |The alterability of the plan allows for adjustments based on changing circumstances.  该计划的可变性允许根据情况变化进行调整。|例句：The alterability of the software makes it adaptable to various needs.  该软件的可更改性使其能够适应各种需求。

例句：The alterability of the metal's shape made it suitable for crafting jewelry.  这种金属的可塑性使它适合制作珠宝。|近义词：mutability, changeability, modifiability|反义词：immutability, fixity, permanence|词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Middle English *alterable + -ity, from Latin alterābilis (“changeable”) + -ity.  Ultimately from Latin alter (“other”).
