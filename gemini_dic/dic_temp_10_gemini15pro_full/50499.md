depressurizes:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使减压；使降压
To release pressure from something that has been pressurized.
The pilot depressurized the cabin before landing.  飞行员在着陆前降低了机舱压力。
The spacecraft depressurizes before re-entry.  航天器在重返大气层之前会减压。
synonyms: decompress, vent, release pressure
antonyms: pressurize, compress
词性: 动词
