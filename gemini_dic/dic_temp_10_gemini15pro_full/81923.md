grilling:/ˈɡrɪlɪŋ/| n. v.|烧烤；拷问；严厉盘问|The act of cooking food over direct heat, typically on a grill.  It can also refer to intense questioning.|We enjoyed a grilling of sausages and burgers. 我们享用了烤香肠和汉堡。|近义词：barbecuing, broiling, roasting|反义词: chilling, freezing|词性: noun, verb (present participle)
Etymology: From the verb "grill," which comes from the Old French "griller" meaning "to broil." Ultimately from the Latin "craticula," meaning "small gridiron."
