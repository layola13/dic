affixion:/əˈfɪkʃən/| n.|痛苦，折磨；固定，附着|The affixion of his signature to the document made it legally binding.  他的签名附在文件上使其具有法律约束力。|例句：The constant pain and discomfort caused him great affixion.  持续的疼痛和不适给他带来了巨大的折磨。

例句：The affixion of the label to the product is done automatically.  标签贴到产品上是自动完成的。|近义词：suffering, distress, attachment, fastening|反义词:relief, comfort, detachment|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *affixio, from Latin affixus, past participle of affigere (“to fasten to, attach”), from ad- (“to”) + figere (“to fix, fasten”).
