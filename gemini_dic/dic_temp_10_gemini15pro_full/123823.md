mora:/ˈmɔːrə/ | n. | 音节；拍；[语言学]时量单位 | A unit of sound in some languages, such as Japanese, which determines syllable weight.  A mora is roughly equivalent to the duration of a short vowel. |  Japanese words are often analyzed in terms of morae. 日语单词通常按音拍进行分析。|  The word "koko" (here) has two morae.  “koko”（这里）有两个音拍。 | syllable, rhythmic unit |  (No commonly used antonym) | noun
