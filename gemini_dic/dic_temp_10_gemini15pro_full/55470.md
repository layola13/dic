distortingly:/dɪˈstɔːrtɪŋli/
adverb|扭曲地，歪曲地|in a way that distorts something, such as facts or sound|The media distortingly portrayed the events, creating a false narrative. 媒体歪曲地描绘了事件，制造了虚假的叙述。|Synonyms: misleadingly, falsely, inaccurately|Antonyms: accurately, truthfully, faithfully|词性: 副词

distort:/dɪˈstɔːrt/
verb|扭曲，歪曲|to pull or twist out of shape; to give a misleading or false account or impression of|The loud music distorted the sound of her voice.  嘈杂的音乐扭曲了她的声音。|Synonyms: twist, warp, misrepresent|Antonyms: clarify, represent accurately|词性: 动词

distortion:/dɪˈstɔːrʃən/
noun|扭曲，变形，失真|the action of distorting or the state of being distorted|The painting suffered distortion from the damp. 这幅画因潮湿而变形。|Synonyms: deformation, warping, misrepresentation|Antonyms: accuracy, clarity, truth|词性: 名词
