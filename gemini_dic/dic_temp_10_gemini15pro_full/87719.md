hexdecyl:/ˌhɛksˈdɛsɪl/ | adj. | 十六烷基的；鲸蜡基的 |  Relating to or containing hexadecane, a saturated hydrocarbon with 16 carbon atoms. Often used to describe a chemical group or radical derived from hexadecane, particularly in the context of organic chemistry and surfactants.  |  Hexdecyltrimethylammonium bromide is a common cationic surfactant.  十六烷基三甲基溴化铵是一种常见的阳离子表面活性剂。| Synonyms: cetyl, palmityl | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
