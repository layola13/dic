manslaughtered:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərd/ |v. |被过失杀害的|Killed unlawfully but without malice aforethought.|He was charged with manslaughter after a bar fight. 他在酒吧斗殴后被指控过失杀人。|Synonyms: killed, slain|Antonyms: saved, rescued|verb

manslaughter:/ˈmænˌslɔːtər/ |n. |过失杀人|The crime of killing a human being without malice aforethought, or otherwise in circumstances not amounting to murder.|The jury found him guilty of manslaughter. 陪审团认定他犯有过失杀人罪。|Synonyms: homicide, killing|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "life-saving")|noun
