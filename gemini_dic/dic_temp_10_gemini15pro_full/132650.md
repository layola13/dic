nondiscursive:/ˌnɒndɪˈskɜːsɪv/ | adj. | 非论证的；非推论的；直观的 | Not proceeding by reasoning or argument; intuitive.  |  Example:  Art is a nondiscursive medium, conveying meaning through images and emotions rather than logical argument. 艺术是一种非论证的媒介，它通过图像和情感而不是逻辑论证来传达意义。

Example:  Mystical experiences are often described as nondiscursive, involving direct apprehension of truth rather than intellectual understanding.  神秘体验通常被描述为非论证性的，涉及对真理的直接理解，而不是智力上的理解。

| 近义词: intuitive, immediate, direct, non-rational | 反义词: discursive, rational, logical, reasoned | 词性: adjective
