inconcinnity:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪnɪti/
n.
不协调，不和谐，不相称
lack of harmony or suitability; incongruity
The inconcinnity of his loud laughter in the somber atmosphere of the funeral was jarring.  他在葬礼庄严肃穆的气氛中大声笑，显得格格不入。
近义词：disharmony, incongruity, incompatibility, discordance
反义词：harmony, congruity, suitability, agreement
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inconcinnitas, from Latin inconcinnus (“unbecoming, inelegant, awkward”), from in- (“not”) + concinnus (“elegant, neat, apt”).
