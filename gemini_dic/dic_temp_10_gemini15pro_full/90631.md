humanists:/ˈhjuːmənɪsts/| n.| 人文主义者| People who believe in humanism, a philosophical stance that emphasizes the value and agency of human beings, individually and collectively, and generally prefers critical thinking and evidence (rationalism and empiricism) over acceptance of dogma or superstition.|The group of humanists met to discuss the ethical implications of the new technology. 这群人文主义者开会讨论新技术的伦理意义。|Synonyms:  humanitarian, freethinker, rationalist, unbeliever, skeptic|Antonyms:  dogmatist, fanatic, zealot|Noun


humanist:/ˈhjuːmənɪst/| adj.| 人文主义的| Relating to or characteristic of humanism.|He took a humanist approach to education, focusing on the individual student's needs. 他采取了一种人文主义的教育方法，专注于每个学生的 individual needs。|Synonyms:  humanistic, anthropocentric|Antonyms:  divine, spiritual, religious|Adjective
