icing:/ˈaɪsɪŋ/ (US), /ˈaɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |糖霜，结冰，(困境等的)表面掩饰 |A sweet coating made of sugar and water, often used to cover cakes. Also refers to the formation of ice on a surface, or a superficial covering that hides an unpleasant reality.|The cake had a thick layer of chocolate icing.  蛋糕上有一层厚厚的巧克力糖霜。|The airplane's wings were covered with a dangerous layer of icing. 飞机的机翼上覆盖着一层危险的冰。|Synonyms: frosting, glaze, coating, covering|Antonyms: thawing, melting|Noun
