leprously:/ˈlɛprəsli/| adv.|麻风病地；像麻风病人一样地；(道德上)堕落地|In a manner resembling leprosy; affected with leprosy; morally corrupt.  It describes something that looks or behaves like it is affected by leprosy, or something morally reprehensible.|His skin looked leprously white. 他的皮肤看起来像麻风病人一样苍白。|The corrupt politician acted leprously, betraying the public's trust.  这个腐败的政客行为堕落，背叛了公众的信任。|Near Synonyms: diseasedly, corruptly, wickedly|Antonyms: healthily, virtuously, purely|词性:副词
