carvel-built:/ˈkɑːrvəlˌbɪlt/ | adj. | 卡拉维尔式建造的；（尤指船）用卡拉维尔式建造的 | Built in the style of a caravel, a small, highly maneuverable sailing ship used by the Portuguese and Spanish in the 15th-17th centuries.  This typically means a vessel with a broad bow, a narrow stern, and lateen sails. |  The carvel-built ship was faster and more agile than its predecessors. 这艘卡拉维尔式建造的船比它的前辈更快更灵活。|  Synonym: carvel-constructed | Antonym: clinker-built | adjective
