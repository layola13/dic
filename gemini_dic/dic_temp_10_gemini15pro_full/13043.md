astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/
adjective
收敛的；止血的；尖刻的
causing the contraction of body tissues, typically of the skin; sharp or severe in manner or style.
The witch hazel is a natural astringent.  金缕梅是一种天然的收敛剂。
Her astringent wit alienated many. 她尖刻的才智疏远了很多人。
近义词:styptic, sharp, harsh, severe, caustic, biting
反义词:gentle, mild, soothing, emollient
词性:adjective

astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/
noun
收敛剂；止血药
a substance that causes the contraction of body tissues, typically of the skin.
You can use an astringent to help close your pores. 你可以使用收敛剂来帮助收缩毛孔。
After shaving, he applied an astringent to his face. 刮胡子后，他在脸上涂了收敛剂。
近义词:styptic, toner
反义词:emollient
词性:noun


词源:
From Late Latin *astringent-, from Latin astringēns, astringent-, present participle of astringere ‘to bind fast,’ from ad- ‘to’ + stringere ‘to draw tight.’
