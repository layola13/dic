binturong:/bɪnˈtʊərɒŋ/ (UK), /bɪnˈtʊrɔːŋ/ (US)| n. |熊狸|A bearcat, a nocturnal mammal native to South and Southeast Asia. It has a prehensile tail and a musky odor.|The binturong is often mistaken for a civet or a small bear. 熊狸经常被误认为是麝猫或小熊。

The zoo's binturong enclosure mimics its natural rainforest habitat.  动物园的熊狸围栏模仿了它的自然雨林栖息地。
