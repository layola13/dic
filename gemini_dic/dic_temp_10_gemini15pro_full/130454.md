nonabsorbency:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbənsi/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɔːrbənsi/| n. |不吸水性，非吸收性|The nonabsorbency of the material makes it ideal for raincoats.  这种材料的不吸水性使它成为雨衣的理想选择。|Example: The nonabsorbency of this fabric is its main selling point.  这种织物的不吸水性是它的主要卖点。|Synonyms: impermeability, impenetrability|Antonyms: absorbency, permeability|Noun

Etymology:
From non- + absorbent + -cy.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Absorbent comes from the Latin absorbēns, present participle of absorbēre "to swallow up."  -cy is a suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or state.
