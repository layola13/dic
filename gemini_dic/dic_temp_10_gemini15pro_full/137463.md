nontinted: /ˌnɒnˈtɪntɪd/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈtɪntɪd/ (American)| adj.| 未着色的，无色的| Not having been tinted; clear or transparent.|  She prefers nontinted sunscreen for her sensitive skin. 她敏感的皮肤更喜欢使用无色的防晒霜。|  He bought nontinted glasses for better clarity. 他为了看得更清晰买了无色眼镜。|近义词：clear, transparent, colorless, undyed|反义词: tinted, colored, dyed|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- + tinted

non-:  prefix meaning "not" (from Latin *non*)
tinted: past participle of tint (from Old French *teindre*, from Latin *tingere*) meaning "to color slightly"
