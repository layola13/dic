exponent:/ɪkˈspoʊnənt/ (US), /ɪkˈspəʊnənt/ (UK)| n.|指数；倡导者；代表人物|A symbol or number placed above and after another symbol or number to denote the power to which the latter is to be raised.|The exponent of 3 in the expression 3² is 2.  3²表达式中 3 的指数是 2。|Synonym: advocate, champion, representative|Antonym: opponent, critic|noun

exponent:/ɪkˈspoʊnənt/ (US), /ɪkˈspəʊnənt/ (UK)| n.|倡导者；代表人物|A person who supports, explains, or speaks for something or someone.|She is a leading exponent of free trade. 她是一位自由贸易的主要倡导者。|Synonym: advocate, champion, representative|Antonym: opponent, critic|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exponent-, *exponentem, present participle of exponere “to put forth, expound,” from ex- “out” + ponere “to put, place.”
