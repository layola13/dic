bartended:/bɑːrˈtɛndɪd/
verb
做酒保工作，担任酒保
worked as a bartender; served drinks.
He bartended at a local pub to pay his way through college.  他为了支付大学学费，在当地一家酒吧做酒保。
近义词: served, mixed drinks, worked behind the bar
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) abstained, refrained from serving
词性: verb (past tense of "bartend")
