harassed:/həˈræst/ /həˈræst/| adj.| 受骚扰的；烦恼的 | subjected to aggressive pressure or intimidation.|  She felt harassed by the constant phone calls. 她觉得不断地接到电话骚扰。| synonyms: bothered, pestered, troubled, tormented, persecuted | antonyms: peaceful, calm, tranquil | adjective

harass:/ˈhærəs/ /həˈræs/| vt.| 骚扰；使烦恼 | subject to aggressive pressure or intimidation.| The paparazzi constantly harassed the celebrity. 狗仔队不断骚扰这位名人。| synonyms: bother, pester, trouble, torment, persecute | antonyms: soothe, comfort, placate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *harasser "tire out, exhaust (a horse), harry, raid,"  probably from Old Norse *harða "to harden" or a similar Germanic source related to hard. The sense evolution is perhaps via the notion of "making things hard" for someone.
