anurous:/əˈnʊərəs/ |adj.|无尾的，无尾目动物的|Relating to or belonging to the Anura, an order of amphibians that comprises the frogs and toads, characterized by the absence of a tail in the adult form.|Frogs and toads are anurous amphibians. 青蛙和蟾蜍是无尾两栖动物。|The scientist specialized in anurous species. 这位科学家专门研究无尾目物种。|Near synonyms: tailless, frog-like, toad-like|Antonyms: caudate, tailed|adjective
