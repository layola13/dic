agrostologic:/ˌæɡrəˈstɒlədʒɪk/
adjective
草本学的，牧草学的
Relating to agrostology; the study of grasses.
Agrostologic research is crucial for developing sustainable pasture management practices. 草本学研究对于发展可持续的牧场管理实践至关重要。
The botanist specialized in agrostologic studies, focusing on the identification and classification of various grass species.  这位植物学家专门从事草本学研究，专注于各种草种的鉴定和分类。

反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, could consider terms related to other plant studies like "bryological" (study of mosses) or "pteridological" (study of ferns))
