communitarian:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈtɛəriən/
adjective|社群主义的；社群主义者的|Relating to or supporting the ideology of communitarianism, which emphasizes the importance of community and social responsibility.|The senator's communitarian values were reflected in his policies supporting local businesses.  这位参议员的社群主义价值观体现在他支持当地企业的政策中。|近义词：collective, social, communal|反义词：individualistic, libertarian|词性: adjective
communitarian:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈtɛəriən/
noun|社群主义者|A person who believes in or supports the ideology of communitarianism.|Many communitarians advocate for stronger social safety nets. 许多社群主义者主张加强社会安全网。|近义词：collectivist, socialist|反义词：individualist, libertarian|词性: noun

Etymology:
From "community" + "-arian" (a suffix forming nouns and adjectives denoting adherence to a particular system or doctrine).  "Community" ultimately derives from Latin "communis" (common, shared).
