auteurist:/ˌɔːtəˈrɪst/
n.
auteur理论拥护者; 作者论者
A filmmaker whose personal influence and artistic control over a movie are so great that the filmmaker is regarded as the author of the movie.
Example:  The French New Wave critics championed many directors as auteurs.  法国新浪潮的评论家们拥护许多导演作为作者论者。

Example:  He is considered an auteurist because of his unique cinematic style. 他因其独特的电影风格而被认为是作者论者。
近义词:director, filmmaker
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "studio executive" in a contrasting sense)
词性:noun
