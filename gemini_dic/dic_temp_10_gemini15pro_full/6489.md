amrita:/əˈmrɪtə/ /ˈæmrɪtə/| n. |甘露;长生不老药;仙酒|A mythical drink of the gods in Hinduism, believed to confer immortality.|The gods drank amrita to maintain their immortality. 神仙喝甘露以保持长生不老。|She felt a surge of amrita-like joy. 她感到一阵甘露般的喜悦。|synonyms: nectar, elixir, ambrosia|antonyms: poison, venom|noun
