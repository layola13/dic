Example 2:  The lexis of legal documents can be difficult to understand. 法律文件的词汇可能难以理解。 | Synonyms: vocabulary, lexicon, wordstock | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a body of words, but one could consider concepts like "silence" or "non-verbal communication" as contrasting ideas) | Noun
