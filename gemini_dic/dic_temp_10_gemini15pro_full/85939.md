headman:/ˈhedmən/| n. |酋长；头目；工头|A person who is the leader of a tribe or village, or the leader of a group of workers. | The headman called a meeting of the villagers. 酋长召集村民开会。|Synonyms: chief, leader, foreman, boss|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|noun

headman:/ˈhedmən/| n. |矿工; (尤指) 采煤工人|A miner, especially a coal miner. |The headman and his crew worked tirelessly in the dark and dangerous mine. 工头和他的队员们在黑暗危险的矿井里不知疲倦地工作。|Synonyms: miner, collier|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps surface worker)|noun
