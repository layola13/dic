enneandria:/ɛnˈiːændriə/
| n. |九雄蕊植物；九雄蕊纲|A plant having nine stamens;  A class of plants characterized by having nine stamens in each flower.|The Enneandria class includes plants like the bay laurel.|近义词: N/A (highly specific botanical term)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἐννέα (ennéa, “nine”) + ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”), with the latter word used in botany to refer to a stamen.
