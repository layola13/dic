fearless:/ˈfɪərləs/| adj.|无畏的，勇敢的|Without fear; brave and determined.|She showed fearless determination in the face of danger. 她面对危险表现出无畏的决心。|近义词：brave, courageous, intrepid, valiant, bold|反义词：fearful, timid, cowardly, apprehensive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *fǣrlēas, from fǣr (“fear”) + -lēas (“-less”).  Cognate with German furchtlos.
