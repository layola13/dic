bleeper:/ˈbliːpər/| n.|哔哔声；电子设备发出的短促的嘟嘟声；（尤指旧时游戏机的）简单电子音；寻呼机|A bleeper is a small electronic device that you carry so that people can contact you by sending a signal to it that makes a beeping sound.  A bleeper can also refer to the sound itself.|The doctor was called away by his bleeper. 医生被他的寻呼机叫走了。|Synonyms: beeper, pager|Antonyms: None.|noun

bleep:/bliːp/| n. v.|哔哔声；嘟嘟声；（用电子设备）发出哔哔声|A bleep is a short, high-pitched electronic sound. As a verb, it means to make such a sound.|The microwave bleeped when the food was ready.  微波炉在食物做好时发出哔哔声。  She bleeped him on his pager. 她用寻呼机给他发了哔哔声。|Synonyms: beep, peep|Antonyms: None.|noun, verb
