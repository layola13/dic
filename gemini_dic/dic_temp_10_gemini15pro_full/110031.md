lice:/laɪs/
noun
虱子
Small, wingless, parasitic insects that live on the skin of mammals and birds and feed on their blood.
The dog was infested with lice. 这只狗身上长满了虱子。
近义词：parasites
反义词：(无直接反义词)
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lȳs (plural *lȳs), from Proto-Germanic *lūs (plural *lūsiz), from Proto-Indo-European *lewH- (“louse”). Cognate with Scots louse, West Frisian luus, Dutch luis, German Laus, Danish lus, Swedish lus.
