acrobacy: /ˈækrəˌbæsi/
n.
杂技；特技；高难度动作
The performance featured incredible feats of acrobacy.
这场表演以惊人的杂技技艺为特色。

Acrobacy requires great strength, balance, and coordination.
杂技需要很大的力量、平衡和协调。

英文解析: The art or skill of performing acrobatic feats, involving agility, balance, and coordination.  Often involves difficult physical acts such as tumbling, balancing, and aerial maneuvers.

例句：
The circus acrobacy was breathtaking. = 马戏团的杂技表演令人叹为观止。

The gymnast's acrobacy on the balance beam was flawless. = 体操运动员在平衡木上的杂技表演完美无瑕。


近义词: gymnastics, aerobatics, stunts, feats
反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acrobatie*, from Ancient Greek ἀκροβατέω (akrobatéō, “I walk on tiptoe, I go aloft”), from ἄκρος (ákros, “highest, outermost”) + βαίνω (baínō, “I go, I walk”).
