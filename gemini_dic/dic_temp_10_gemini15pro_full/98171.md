inscribing:/ɪnˈskraɪbɪŋ/
v.
刻写，题写
The act of writing, engraving, or carving words or symbols onto a surface.
He was inscribing his name on the trophy. 他正在奖杯上刻写自己的名字。
inscribing:/ɪnˈskraɪbɪŋ/
v.
题献（书籍等）
To write a dedication or short message in or on (a book or other object), especially as a gift.
She inscribed the book to her best friend.  她把这本书题献给了她最好的朋友。

近义词:engraving, carving, etching, writing, dedicating
反义词:erasing, deleting
