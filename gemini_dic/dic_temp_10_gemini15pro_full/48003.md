decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/| v. |破译，解读|To convert from a code or cipher into ordinary language; to find the meaning of something unclear or difficult to understand.|The archaeologist deciphered the ancient hieroglyphs. 考古学家破译了古代象形文字。|
|近义词：decode, interpret, solve, unravel|反义词：encode, encrypt, conceal|verb|

decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/| n. |解密，译文|The act of deciphering something; the meaning of something that has been deciphered.|The decipherment of the code took weeks.  密码的破译工作花了几个星期。|
|近义词：interpretation, translation, solution|反义词：encryption, encoding|noun|
