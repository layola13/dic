catchword:/ˈkætʃwɜːrd/ (US), /ˈkætʃwɜːd/ (UK)| n.| 流行语；醒目标题；标语；（字典页左上角的）引导词|A word or phrase that is used repeatedly and becomes a slogan or motto.  A key word printed at the top of a page of a dictionary or other reference book to indicate the first or last word entry on that page.|The politician's catchword was "change."  这位政治家的口号是“变革”。|Synonyms: slogan, motto, byword, keyword, tagline|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "jargon" or "obscure term")|noun

catchword:/ˈkætʃwɜːrd/ (US), /ˈkætʃwɜːd/ (UK)| adj.| 引人注目的| Designed to attract attention; catchy.|The advertisement used catchword phrases to attract young consumers. 这则广告使用引人注目的短语来吸引年轻消费者。|Synonyms: catchy, attention-grabbing, memorable|Antonyms:  unremarkable, forgettable, dull|adjective
