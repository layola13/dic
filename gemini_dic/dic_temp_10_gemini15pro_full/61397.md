emblematize:/ˈɛmbləˌmaɪz/
verb
象征；体现；作为…的象征
to stand as a symbol of something; to typify
The dove emblematizes peace. 鸽子象征和平。
synonyms: symbolize, represent, typify, embody
antonyms: misrepresent, belie
词性:动词
