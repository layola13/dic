anadem: /ˈænəˌdɛm/ | n. | 花冠，花环；王冠 | A garland or wreath for the head; a crown. | The queen wore a jeweled anadem at her coronation. 女王在加冕典礼上戴着镶有珠宝的花冠。| 近义词: crown, garland, wreath, diadem | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *anadema, from Greek anádēma (“band, fillet”), from anadein (“to bind up”), from ana- (“up”) + dein (“to bind”).
