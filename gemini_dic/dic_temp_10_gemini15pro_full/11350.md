adjective

Composed of or containing sand or sandstone.  Relating to or resembling arenite.
The arenilitic nature of the soil made it difficult for plants to grow. 土壤的砂质特性使植物难以生长。
The geologist examined the arenilitic formations in the canyon. 地质学家检查了峡谷中的砂岩地层。
Synonyms: sandy, arenaceous
Antonyms: clayey, silty
