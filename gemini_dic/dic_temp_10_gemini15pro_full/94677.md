imprinters:/ɪmˈprɪntərz/
n.
压印机; 印刷机; 印记制造者; 盖印者
People or devices that impress or imprint something.  Those who create a lasting impression or mark.
The imprinters carefully stamped the company logo onto each leather bag.  这些压印机小心地将公司标志印在每个皮包上。
The experience left a lasting mark on her, making her one of the most dedicated imprinters of social change. 这段经历在她身上留下了持久的印记，使她成为社会变革中最专注的倡导者之一。
近义词: printers, stampers, markers
反义词: erasers
