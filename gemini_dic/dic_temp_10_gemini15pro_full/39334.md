conjecturers:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərərz/
|n.|推测者，猜想者|People who form conjectures or guesses; those who speculate or hypothesize.|The conjecturers were divided on the likely outcome of the experiment. 这些推测者对实验的可能结果意见不一。|近义词：speculators, theorists, guessers, hypothesizers|反义词：knowers, experts|词性: 名词

conjecture: /kənˈdʒɛktʃər/
|n.|推测，猜想|An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information.|His conjecture about the stock market proved to be correct. 他对股市的推测被证明是正确的。|近义词：guess, speculation, theory, hypothesis|反义词：fact, certainty, knowledge|词性: 名词
conjecture: /kənˈdʒɛktʃər/
|v.|推测，猜想|Form an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information.|He conjectured that the painting was a forgery. 他推测这幅画是赝品。|近义词：guess, speculate, theorize, hypothesize|反义词：know, ascertain, prove|词性: 动词
