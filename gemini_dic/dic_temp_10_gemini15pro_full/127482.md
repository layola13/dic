necessitous:/nəˈsesɪtəs/| adj.|贫困的， нуждающийся|Being in a state of poverty; needing essential goods for survival.  Characterized by need or poverty; indigent.|The necessitous family relied on the food bank for their meals.  这个贫困的家庭依靠食物银行来吃饭。|Synonyms: impoverished, indigent, needy, destitute, penurious|Antonyms: wealthy, affluent, prosperous|adjective
