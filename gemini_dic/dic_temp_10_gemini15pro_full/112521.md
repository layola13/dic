lov:/lʌv/ | n. | 爱，热爱；喜爱；情人，爱人 |  A feeling of strong attachment induced by that which delights or commands admiration; preeminent kindness or devotion to another; affection. |  I have a deep love for music. 我对音乐有着深深的热爱。 | 近义词: affection, fondness, adoration | 反义词: hate, hatred | 词性: 名词

lov:/lʌv/ | v. | 爱；热爱；喜爱；喜欢 | To have a great affection or liking for; to feel tender and passionate affection for. | I love spending time with my family. 我喜欢和家人待在一起。 | 近义词: adore, cherish, like | 反义词: hate, despise | 词性: 动词
