noncarrier:/ˌnɒnˈkæriər/ /ˌnɑːnˈkæriər/
noun
非携带者，非运营商
A person, organism, or substance that does not harbor or transmit a particular disease or characteristic, especially a recessive gene.
例句：
He is a noncarrier for the cystic fibrosis gene.
他不是囊性纤维化基因的携带者。

She was relieved to find out she was a noncarrier for the disease.
得知自己不是这种疾病的携带者，她松了一口气。

近义词：
non-transmitter
unaffected individual

反义词：
carrier

词性
n.
