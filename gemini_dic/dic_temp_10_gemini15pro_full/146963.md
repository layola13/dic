overissue:/ˌoʊvərˈɪʃuː/| n. v.|过度发行；超额发行；过量发行|An overissue occurs when more of something, typically currency or securities, is issued than is authorized or needed.|The overissue of company stock diluted the value of existing shares.|近义词：overproduction, surplus, glut|反义词：undersupply, shortage|n.
overissue:/ˌoʊvərˈɪʃuː/| v.|过度发行；超额发行；过量发行|To issue more of something than is authorized or needed, especially currency or securities.|The central bank overissued currency, leading to inflation.|近义词：overproduce|反义词：underproduce|v.
