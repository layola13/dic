hookah:/ˈhʊkə/ (US), /ˈhʊkɑː/ (UK)| n. |水烟袋，水烟壶|A hookah is a water pipe used to smoke specially made tobacco that comes in different flavors, such as apple, mint, or cherry. The smoke passes through a bowl of water before being inhaled through a long tube.|
Example: We smoked hookah at the cafe last night. 我们昨晚在咖啡馆抽了水烟。

Example: The hookah bar offers a wide variety of flavored tobaccos.  这家水烟吧提供各种口味的烟草。

|近义词：water pipe, narghile, shisha|反义词: None|词性:noun|
