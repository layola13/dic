malamute: /ˈmæləˌmjut/ | n. | 马拉缪特犬; 阿拉斯加雪橇犬 | A breed of large, powerful Arctic sled dog originating in Alaska, known for its endurance and thick coat. |  A team of malamutes pulled the sled across the frozen wasteland. 一群马拉缪特犬拉着雪橇穿过冰冻的荒原。

| 近义词: husky, sled dog | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a breed of dog) | 词性: noun
