epineural:/ˌɛpɪˈnʊrəl/
adjective|神经外膜的|Relating to the outermost layer of connective tissue surrounding a peripheral nerve.|The epineural sheath protects the nerve from external damage.|近义词:perineural|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "endoneural" which refers to the innermost layer)|词性:adjective

epineurium:/ˌɛpɪˈnʊəriəm/
noun|神经外膜|The outermost layer of connective tissue surrounding a peripheral nerve.|Damage to the epineurium can disrupt nerve function.|近义词: (No exact synonym, but "outer nerve sheath" is descriptive)|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "endoneurium" which refers to the innermost layer)|词性:noun
