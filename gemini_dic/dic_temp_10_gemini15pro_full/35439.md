co-regent:/ˌkoʊˈriːdʒənt/
n.
共同摄政者；共同统治者
A person who rules jointly with another.
Example:  The queen acted as co-regent with her son until he came of age. 女王与儿子共同摄政，直到他成年。

Example:  The two brothers served as co-regents after their father's death. 父王去世后，两兄弟共同摄政。
Synonym: joint ruler, co-ruler
Antonym: subject, subordinate
noun
