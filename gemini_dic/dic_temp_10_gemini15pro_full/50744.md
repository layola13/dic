derring-do:/ˈdɛrɪŋ ˈduː/
noun
勇敢的行为；大胆的举动
valiant deeds in adventure or warfare; daring action
例句：Tales of derring-do are always exciting.  勇敢的行为的故事总是令人兴奋的。

例句：He was known for his derring-do on the battlefield. 他以在战场上的英勇行为而闻名。

近义词：bravery, courage, valor, audacity, heroism
反义词：cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *derring do*, literally "daring to do".  A combination of derring (present participle of *dar*, "to dare") and do.
