distancing:/ˈdɪstənsɪŋ/ | n. | 疏远；保持距离 | the act of becoming or keeping yourself emotionally or physically further away from somebody/something |  Social distancing is important to prevent the spread of the virus. 保持社交距离对于防止病毒传播很重要。 | synonyms: separation, detachment | antonyms: closeness, intimacy | noun

distanced:/ˈdɪstənst/ | adj. | 疏远的；有距离的 | emotionally or physically separated or far away |  He felt distanced from his family after years of living abroad.  在国外生活多年后，他感到与家人疏远了。 | synonyms: remote, aloof, withdrawn | antonyms: close, intimate | adjective
