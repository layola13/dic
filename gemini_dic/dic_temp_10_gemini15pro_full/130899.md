nonartist:/ˌnɒnˈɑːtɪst/ |/ˌnɑːnˈɑːrtɪst/| n. |非艺术家；没有艺术才能的人|A person who is not an artist or does not practice art. |例句：He's a nonartist, but he appreciates good art. 他不是艺术家，但他欣赏好的艺术。

例句：The nonartist may not understand the technical aspects, but can still enjoy the beauty of a painting.  非艺术家可能不理解技术方面，但仍然可以欣赏绘画的美。|近义词：layperson, amateur |反义词: artist, professional | 词性: 名词
