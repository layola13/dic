dilatorily:/ˈdɪləˌtɔːrɪli/ /ˈdaɪləˌtɔːrɪli/
adverb|拖延地，缓慢地|in a way that shows that you are not willing to do something quickly|He worked dilatorily, taking frequent breaks. 他工作拖沓，经常休息。|The project was completed dilatorily, missing several deadlines.  这个项目完成得拖拖拉拉，错过了好几个截止日期。|slowly, tardily, sluggishly, leisurely|promptly, quickly, expeditiously|adverb

dilatory:/ˈdɪləˌtɔːri/ /ˈdaɪləˌtɔːri/
adjective|拖延的，缓慢的|slow to act or respond to something|She was dilatory in replying to my emails. 她回复我的电子邮件很慢。|The government has been dilatory in addressing the housing crisis. 政府在解决住房危机方面一直拖拖拉拉。|slow, tardy, sluggish, lagging|prompt, quick, expeditious|adjective
