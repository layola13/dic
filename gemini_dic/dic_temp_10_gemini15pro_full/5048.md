all-out:/ɔːlˈaʊt/| adj.|全力以赴的，竭尽全力的|Characterized by maximum effort or intensity; using all available resources and energy.|The team made an all-out effort to win the championship.  团队全力以赴赢得冠军。|近义词：whole-hearted, full-blown, exhaustive, intensive|反义词：half-hearted, perfunctory, restrained|词性:形容词
词源: From "all out," originally a military term meaning to attack with all available forces.
