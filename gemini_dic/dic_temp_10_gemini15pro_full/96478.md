individuating:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒueɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |使个性化的，赋予个性的|Distinguishing or marking as individual; giving individuality to.|Her unique style individuates her from her peers. 她独特的风格使她与同龄人区分开来。|近义词: distinguishing, differentiating, characterizing|反义词: generalizing, homogenizing|词性: adjective
individuate:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒueɪt/
| v. |使个性化，赋予个性|To make or become distinct or individual; to differentiate.|The artist individuated each piece of pottery with a unique design.  这位艺术家用独特的设计使每件陶器都具有个性。|近义词: distinguish, differentiate, characterize|反义词: generalize, homogenize|词性: verb
individuation:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuˈeɪʃn/
| n. |个性化，赋予个性|The process of becoming distinct or individual.|Jungian psychology emphasizes the process of individuation as a key to mental health. 荣格心理学强调个性化过程是心理健康的关键。|近义词: differentiation, distinction, characterization|反义词: generalization, homogenization|词性: noun
