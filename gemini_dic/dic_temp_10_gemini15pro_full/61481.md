embrawn:/ɪmˈbrɔːn/
verb
赋予力量；使强壮
to give strength to; to make strong or muscular
The weightlifting program embrawns the athletes.  举重训练使运动员强壮起来。
近义词:strengthen, fortify, empower
反义词:weaken, debilitate
词性:v. (verb,动词)
词源:From em- (variant of en- meaning "in" or "into") + brawn (muscular strength).  由em-（en- 的变体，意为“在...中”或“进入”）+ brawn（肌肉力量）组成。
