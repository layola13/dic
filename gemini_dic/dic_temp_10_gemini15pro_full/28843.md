carpentered:/ˈkɑːrpəntərd/ | adj. | 木工制作的，木工工艺的 | Made by or involving a carpenter; constructed of wood. | The room had beautifully carpentered shelves. 房间里有做工精美的木制架子。|  The carpentered staircase was a masterpiece. 木工制作的楼梯是一件杰作。| 近义词：wooden, timbered, handcrafted | 反义词: prefabricated, factory-made | 词性: 形容词

carpenter:/ˈkɑːrpəntər/ | n. | 木匠，木工 | A person whose job is to make and repair wooden objects and structures. | The carpenter built a beautiful deck for our house. 木匠为我们的房子建造了一个漂亮的露台。| The experienced carpenter carefully crafted the furniture. 经验丰富的木匠精心制作了家具。| 近义词：woodworker, joiner | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: From Middle English carpenter, from Old French carpentier, from Late Latin carpentārius (“cartwright, wagon-maker”), from Latin carpentum (“wagon, cart”).  The meaning shifted from wagon-maker to builder of wooden structures.
