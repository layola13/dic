dianoetic:/ˌdaɪəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
理智的；思维的；认知的
Relating to the intellect, the understanding, or the rational faculty; intellectual.  Pertaining to or characterized by the exercise of reason.
例句：Dianoetic processes are essential for problem-solving.  理智的思考过程对于解决问题至关重要。

例句：She engaged in dianoetic contemplation of the universe's mysteries. 她对宇宙的奥秘进行了理智的思考。
近义词：intellectual, cognitive, rational, reasoning
反义词：irrational, emotional, instinctive
词性：adjective

词源: From Greek *dianoētikos*, from *dianoeîsthai* "to think," from *dia-* "through" + *nous* "mind."
