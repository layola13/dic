cinnamene:/ˈsɪnəmiːn/| n. |苏合香烯，苯乙烯|A colorless, flammable liquid hydrocarbon, C8H8, with a strong aroma, made from ethylbenzene and used in making polystyrene resins, synthetic rubber, and other products. It's also known as styrene.|Cinnamene is an important building block for many types of plastic. 苏合香烯是许多类型塑料的重要组成部分。|Cinnamene is readily polymerized. 苏合香烯很容易聚合。|近义词：styrene, vinylbenzene, phenylethylene|反义词:|noun
