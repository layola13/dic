hookworms:/ˈhʊkwɜːrmz/
n.
钩虫
parasitic nematode worms that inhabit the small intestine of humans and other mammals, causing hookworm disease.
例句：Hookworms can cause anemia and malnutrition. 钩虫会导致贫血和营养不良。

例句：Infection with hookworms is common in areas with poor sanitation. 在卫生条件差的地区，钩虫感染很常见。

近义词：parasites, nematodes
反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑用 "healthy gut flora" 健康的肠道菌群)
词性: 名词 (plural)
