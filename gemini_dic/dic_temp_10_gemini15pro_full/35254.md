clunky:/ˈklʌŋki/ | adj. | 笨重的，粗陋的 | Clumsy, awkward, or unwieldy; difficult to use or handle. | The software is a bit clunky and needs some improvement. 这个软件有点笨重，需要一些改进。| synonyms: awkward, cumbersome, unwieldy, ungainly | antonyms: sleek, streamlined, graceful, elegant | adjective
