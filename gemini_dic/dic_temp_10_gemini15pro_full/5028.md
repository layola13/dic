all-in-all:/ˌɔːl ɪn ˈɔːl/
|adv.|总而言之，总的来说|Considering everything; in summary. |All in all, it was a successful trip. 总而言之，这是一次成功的旅行。|例句：All in all, I think it was a good decision. 总的来说，我认为这是一个不错的决定。|近义词：overall, on the whole, altogether, in conclusion, in summary, to sum up|反义词:partially, partly|副词
