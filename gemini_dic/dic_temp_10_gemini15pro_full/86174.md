heartswelling:/ˈhɑːrtswɛlɪŋ/| adj.|令人心潮澎湃的，感人的|Causing a feeling of swelling emotion in one's chest, as with happiness, love, or pride.|Seeing my daughter graduate was a heartswelling moment.  看到女儿毕业是一个令人心潮澎湃的时刻。|近义词：moving, touching, heartwarming, inspiring|反义词：depressing, disheartening|词性:形容词

Etymology:  heart (n.) + swelling (adj. from swell)
"heart" comes from Old English "heorte,"  related to Dutch "hart" and German "Herz."
"swell" comes from Old English "swellan,"  related to Old Norse "svella." The combination "heartswelling" describes the feeling of emotion causing a metaphorical swelling in the heart.
