overpeopling:/ˌoʊvərˈpiːpəlɪŋ/
n.
人口过剩，过度拥挤
The condition of having too many people in a certain area.
The overpeopling of the planet is a serious concern.  地球人口过剩是一个严重的问题。
Overpopulation in the city led to a housing shortage. 城市人口过多导致住房短缺。
