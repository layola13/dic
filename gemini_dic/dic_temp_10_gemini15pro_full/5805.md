amaryllises: /ˌæməˈrɪlɪsiːz/
n.
朱顶红（复数）
Amaryllises are bulbous plants known for their large, showy trumpet-shaped flowers, typically red, pink, or white.
The amaryllises in her garden were a vibrant splash of color. 她花园里的朱顶红是一片鲜艳的色彩。

Amaryllis (singular): /ˌæməˈrɪlɪs/
n.
朱顶红（单数）
The amaryllis is a popular choice for a winter-blooming houseplant. 朱顶红是冬季开花的室内植物的热门选择。

近义词:
belladonna lily, Jersey lily, knight's-star-lily (这些都是特定种类朱顶红的名称，并非完全同义)
反义词:
无明确的反义词。

词性:
noun (名词)


词源:
From Latin *amarysllis, from Greek *ἀμαρύσσω (amarýssō, “to sparkle, glitter”).
