blightingly:/ˈblaɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 毁灭性地，有害地 | In a way that causes blight; destructively. | The frost blightingly affected the crops. 霜冻对农作物造成了毁灭性的影响。| 近义词: destructively, ruinously, devastatingly | 反义词: beneficially, helpfully | 词性: 副词

blight:/blaɪt/ | n. | 枯萎病; 祸害; 不良影响 |  A disease that causes plants to wither and die; something that spoils or damages something. | Urban blight has affected many parts of the city. 城市衰败影响了城市的许多地方. | 近义词: affliction, curse, plague | 反义词: blessing, boon | 词性: 名词
