consopiation: /kənˌsōpɪˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 密谋，共谋；串通 | The act of conspiring together; a conspiracy.  |  The consopiation to overthrow the government was discovered.  政府被推翻的阴谋被发现了。 | 近义词: conspiracy, collusion, plot, intrigue | 反义词: cooperation, collaboration | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *consopiātiō, from Latin consopiāre (“to lull to sleep”), from com- (“together”) + sopiāre (“to lull to sleep”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)wep- (“to sleep”).
