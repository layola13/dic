encephalitogenicity:/ɛnˌsefəˌlaɪtədʒəˈnɪsɪti/
noun
脑炎致病性，致脑炎性
The capacity of a substance to cause encephalitis (inflammation of the brain).
The encephalitogenicity of the virus was determined by injecting it into mice.  这种病毒的致脑炎性是通过将其注射到小鼠体内来确定的。
neurovirulence, neuropathogenicity
non-encephalitogenicity (This is not a standard word, but represents the lack of encephalitogenicity)
n.
