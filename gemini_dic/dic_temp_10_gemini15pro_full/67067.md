explodable:/ɪkˈsploʊdəbl/
adjective
可爆炸的;会爆炸的
Capable of exploding; that can be exploded.
The old dynamite was highly explodable.  这旧炸药非常容易爆炸。
The lab contains several explodable chemicals.  实验室里有一些可爆炸的化学品。
近义词: explosive, flammable, combustible
反义词: non-explosive, inert, stable
词性: adjective
词源: From "explode" + "-able". "Explode" comes from Latin "explodere" (to hiss off the stage, drive out by clapping), from "ex-" (out) + "plaudere" (to clap). "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being".
