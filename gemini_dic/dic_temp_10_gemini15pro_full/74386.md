ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *framen, from Old French *framer (“to construct, fashion, build”),  from Frankish *framjan (“to benefit, be useful”), from Proto-Germanic *framjaną (“to further, promote, be useful”).  Related to Old High German fremmen, Old English fremian (“to further, promote”).  The meaning “to enclose in a frame” is from late Middle English.
