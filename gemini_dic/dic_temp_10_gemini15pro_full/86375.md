heddling:/ˈhɛdlɪŋ/ | n. |综线, 穿综| The process of threading yarn through the heddles of a loom to create a woven pattern. | The weaver spent hours heddling the loom before starting to weave.  织布工在开始织布前花了几个小时穿综。 | synonyms: warping, threading | antonyms: N/A | noun

heddle:/ˈhɛdl/ | n. |综丝, 综眼| A wire or string with an eye in the middle, used to guide the warp threads on a loom. |  Each heddle controls a specific warp thread. 每个综丝控制一根特定的经纱。 | synonyms: thread guide | antonyms: N/A | noun
