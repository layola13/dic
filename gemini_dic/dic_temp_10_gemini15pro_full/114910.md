manacled: /ˈmænəkld/ | adj. | 被铐住的，被束缚的 | Restrained with manacles; shackled.  Figuratively, it can also mean restricted or hampered. | The prisoner was manacled to the wall.  囚犯被铐在墙上。|  The company felt manacled by outdated regulations.  这家公司感觉受到了过时规定的束缚。 |  shackled, chained, bound, restrained, fettered | free, liberated, unrestrained | adjective
