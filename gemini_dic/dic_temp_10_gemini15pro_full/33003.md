chorisis:/kɔːˈraɪsɪs/
noun
分离，分隔；[医]脉络膜裂开
The separation of parts normally joined, especially the separation of parts of the eye.  指通常连接在一起的部分的分离，尤指眼球各部分的分离。
Chorisis can occur in various parts of the eye, such as the iris or the choroid.  脉络膜裂开可能发生在眼睛的各个部位，例如虹膜或脉络膜。
synonyms: separation, division, disunion, fission
antonyms: union, fusion, joining, connection
