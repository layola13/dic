oden:/ˈoʊdən/| n.|关东煮，杂烩菜肴|A Japanese winter dish consisting of various ingredients such as boiled eggs, processed fish cakes, and daikon radish, simmered in a light, soy-flavored dashi broth.|I had a bowl of hot oden for dinner on a cold night. 我在一个寒冷的晚上吃了一碗热腾腾的关东煮。|近义词：nimono (炖菜), hot pot|反义词: (无直接反义词)|名词
