convolvulaceous: /kənˌvɒlvjʊˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
旋花科的；像旋花科植物的
Relating to or resembling plants of the family Convolvulaceae, typically having trumpet-shaped flowers and twining stems.
The garden was filled with convolvulaceous flowers of various hues.  花园里开满了各种颜色的旋花科植物的花朵。
The vine had a distinctly convolvulaceous appearance, with its twining stem and funnel-shaped flowers. 这藤蔓有着明显的旋花科植物的外观，有着缠绕的茎和漏斗状的花。
Synonyms: twining, climbing, winding
Antonyms: erect, upright
词性: adjective
