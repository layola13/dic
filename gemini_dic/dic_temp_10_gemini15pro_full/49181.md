Delaney: /dəˈleɪni/ | n. | 德莱尼（姓氏或名字） | An Irish surname, also used as a given name.  It is derived from the Gaelic Ó Dubhshláine, meaning "descendant of Dubhsláine". Dubhsláine is a personal name meaning "black challenge" or "dark defiance".  | Delaney is a popular name in Ireland and the United States.  德莱尼在爱尔兰和美国是一个很受欢迎的名字。 | 近义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 反义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 词性: 名词
