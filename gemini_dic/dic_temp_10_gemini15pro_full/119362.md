microcomputer:/ˈmaɪkroʊkəmˌpjuːtər/ (US) /ˈmaɪkrəʊkəmˌpjuːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|微型计算机|A small computer that is based on a microprocessor and is designed for individual use.|I used my microcomputer to write the report. 我用我的微型计算机写了报告。|Synonyms: personal computer, PC, desktop, laptop|Antonyms: mainframe, supercomputer|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + computer.  "Micro-" comes from the Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Computer" originates from the Latin "computare" meaning "to calculate, sum up, compute".
