annexationist:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃənɪst/
n.
吞并主义者；主张兼并者
Someone who advocates or supports annexation, especially of territory.
The annexationist policies of the government led to increased tensions with neighboring countries. 政府的吞并主义政策导致与邻国的紧张关系加剧。
Synonyms: expansionist, imperialist, jingoist
Antonyms: pacifist, anti-imperialist
noun


annexationist:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃənɪst/
adj.
吞并主义的；主张兼并的
Relating to or supporting annexation, especially of territory.
The annexationist rhetoric of the politician fueled public support for the war.  这位政客的吞并主义言论激起了公众对战争的支持。
Synonyms: expansionist, imperialist, jingoist
Antonyms: pacifist, anti-imperialist
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From annexation + -ist.  Annexation comes from the Late Latin annexionem,  from annexus, past participle of annectere "to bind, tie, or fasten to," from ad- "to" + nectere "to bind."
