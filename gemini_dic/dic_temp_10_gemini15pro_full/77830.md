generativity:/ˌdʒɛnəˈrævɪti/
n.|慷慨，大方；创造力|the quality of being kind, understanding, and not selfish; the ability to produce or create something new|例句：Her generosity touched everyone. 她的慷慨感动了每个人。

例句：His artistic generativity seemed to increase with age.  他的艺术创造力似乎随着年龄的增长而增加。
|近义词：liberality, bounty, munificence, creativity, productivity, fecundity|反义词：stinginess, meanness, selfishness, barrenness, sterility|词性：名词
