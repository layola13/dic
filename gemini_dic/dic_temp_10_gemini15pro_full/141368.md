operativeness:/ˌɒpərətɪvnəs/ | n. |有效性，可操作性|The operativeness of the new system is yet to be determined.  The quality of being operative or in effect; practicality.|例句：The operativeness of the plan depends on the weather.  该计划的有效性取决于天气。|例句：We questioned the operativeness of the new strategy. 我们质疑新战略的可行性。|近义词：effectiveness, efficacy, feasibility, practicality|反义词：ineffectiveness, impracticality|词性:名词

词源: From operative + -ness.  Operative from Old French operatif or directly from Late Latin operātīvus “effective,” from past participle stem of operārī “to work,” from Latin opera “work.”
