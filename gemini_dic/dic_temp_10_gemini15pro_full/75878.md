functorial:/fʌŋkˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|函子的，函子性的|Of or relating to a functor; behaving like a functor.  A functorial construction is one that works in a compatible way across different categories.  It preserves relationships between objects and morphisms.|The construction of the dual space is functorial.|Near synonyms:  natural, canonical, compatible|Antonym: non-functorial|词性:形容词
