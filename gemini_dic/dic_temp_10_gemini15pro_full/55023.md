disqualify:/dɪsˈkwɑːlɪfaɪ/
v.
取消…的资格，使不合格
To declare someone or something ineligible or unfit for a position, competition, or activity.
He was disqualified from driving for a year. 他被取消驾驶资格一年。
Her lack of experience disqualifies her for the job. 她缺乏经验，不适合这份工作。
近义词: bar, preclude, prohibit
反义词: qualify, enable, permit
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not, opposite of) + qualify (to make eligible or fit). Ultimately from Latin qualis (of what kind)
