open-airishnesses:/ˌoʊpənˈɛərɪʃnɪsɪz/
|n.|露天开放性；户外活动的性质|The quality of being suitable for or suggestive of open-air activities.  A feeling or atmosphere of being outdoors.|The festival had a delightful open-airishness about it.  这个节日有一种令人愉悦的户外氛围。|近义词:outdoorsiness, al fresco quality|反义词:indoorsiness, confinement|词性:noun

Etymology:
From "open-air" + "-ish" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "somewhat like") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  "Open" comes from Old English "open"; "air" from Old French "air"; "-ish" and "-ness" are native English suffixes.
