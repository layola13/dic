alternating:/ˈɔːltərˌneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 交替的，轮流的 | Occurring in turn repeatedly; first one and then the other. | The salad had alternating layers of tomatoes and mozzarella. 沙拉里有交替出现的番茄和马苏里拉奶酪层。| The traffic lights were alternating between red and green. 交通信号灯在红绿之间交替变换。 |  Synonyms: rotating, switching, cyclical, reciprocal | Antonyms: continuous, constant, unchanging | adjective

alternating:/ˈɔːltərˌneɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 交替，轮流 | The act of alternating. |  The alternating of day and night is a fundamental aspect of life on Earth. 地球上昼夜交替是生命的一个基本方面。| The alternating of seasons brings changes in weather and temperature.  季节的交替带来了天气和温度的变化。| Synonyms: rotation, interchange | Antonyms: continuity, constancy | noun
