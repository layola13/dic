limp:/lɪmp/| v.  n.  adj.|跛行，柔软的，无力的|To walk with difficulty, typically because of a damaged or stiff leg or foot.  A tendency to limp. Lacking firmness; not stiff or strong.|例句：He limped off the field after twisting his ankle. 他扭伤脚踝后一瘸一拐地离开了场地。|近义词：hobble, falter, flexible, flaccid|反义词:firm, strong, stride|词性:verb, noun, adjective

limp:/lɪmp/| n.|跛行|The uneven gait of someone who limps.|例句：She walked with a pronounced limp. 她走路明显跛脚。|近义词：hobble|反义词:stride|词性:noun

limp:/lɪmp/| adj.|柔软的，无力的|Lacking firmness; not stiff or strong.|例句：The lettuce had gone limp in the heat.  生菜在高温下变得软塌塌的。|近义词：flexible, flaccid|反义词:firm, strong|词性:adjective
