lorded:/ˈlɔːrdɪd/| v.|作威作福，俨然像个主人，耀武扬威|To act in a superior and domineering manner, often in a way that is inappropriate or offensive.|He lorded it over his employees, constantly reminding them of his position. 他对他的员工作威作福，不断地提醒他们他的地位。|She lorded her wealth over her less fortunate relatives. 她向她不太富裕的亲戚炫耀她的财富。|Synonyms: domineer, tyrannize, swagger, boast, brag|Antonyms: submit, obey, defer|verb
