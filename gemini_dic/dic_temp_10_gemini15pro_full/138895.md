nutcracker:/ˈnʌtˌkrækər/ | n. |胡桃夹子；坚果钳 |A tool used to crack the shells of nuts.  A nutcracker is typically made of metal and has two hinged levers that are squeezed together to apply pressure to the nut.  Some nutcrackers are decorative figures, often shaped like a person. |She used a nutcracker to open the walnuts. 她用胡桃夹子打开了核桃。|Synonyms: nut cracker, nut opener |Antonyms: N/A |noun

nutcracker:/ˈnʌtˌkrækər/ | n. |胡桃夹子(芭蕾舞剧) |The Nutcracker is a two-act ballet, originally choreographed by Marius Petipa and Lev Ivanov with a score by Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky. |We went to see The Nutcracker ballet during the holidays. 我们在假期去看了芭蕾舞剧《胡桃夹子》。|Synonyms:  N/A (referring to the ballet) |Antonyms: N/A |noun
