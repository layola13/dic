pangenesis:/ˌpænˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
泛生论
The theory that all parts of the body contribute to the formation of reproductive cells.
Darwin's theory of pangenesis attempted to explain heredity. 达尔文的泛生论试图解释遗传。
近义词:  none (no direct synonym, related concepts include:  inheritance, heredity, germ plasm theory)
反义词:  none (no direct antonym, opposing theory would be Weissmann's germ plasm theory)
词性: noun
