inoculable:/ɪˈnɒkjʊləbl/
adjective
可接种的
Capable of being inoculated; susceptible to inoculation.
The virus is inoculable into mice.  这种病毒可以接种到老鼠身上。
inoculative, transmissible
non-inoculable, resistant
