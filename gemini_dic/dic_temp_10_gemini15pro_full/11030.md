arcadianism:/ɑːrˈkeɪdiənɪzəm/
n.
田园主义；田园风；田园诗
A literary style that idealizes rural life and nature.  A behavior or attitude that is charmingly simple and unsophisticated.
The poem was full of arcadianism, depicting shepherds and shepherdesses in a peaceful, idealized setting. 这首诗充满了田园主义，描绘了牧羊人和牧羊女在一个和平、理想化的环境中。
He maintained a certain arcadianism in his lifestyle, preferring the simple pleasures of gardening and fishing. 他在生活中保持着某种田园主义，偏爱园艺和钓鱼的简单乐趣。
近义词：pastoralism, bucolicism, idyllicism, rusticity
反义词：sophistication, urbanity, modernity
词性:noun
