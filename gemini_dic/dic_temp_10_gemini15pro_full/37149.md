come-to-hand:/kʌm tə hænd/
adjective
 readily available; easily found.
 This information came to hand just when I needed it. 这份资料正好在我需要的时候出现了.
 The tools I needed came readily to hand. 我需要的工具很容易就找到了.
近义词:available, accessible, handy, convenient
反义词:unavailable, inaccessible, scarce
词性:adjective

come-to-hand:/kʌm tə hænd/
verb phrase
to be received; to become available.
The documents came to hand yesterday. 文件昨天收到了.
When the opportunity comes to hand, I'll seize it. 当机会来临的时候，我会抓住它.
近义词:arrive, appear, materialize
反义词:disappear, vanish
词性:verb phrase

词源: "Come to hand" is a phrasal verb. "Come" indicates arrival or becoming available, and "hand" refers to possession or close proximity.  The phrase likely originated from the physical act of receiving something into one's hand.
