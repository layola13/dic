meatinesses:/ˈmiːtinɪsɪz/
noun
肉質豐富;多肉;實質性
The quality of being meaty; substance or significance.
The meatinesses of the steaks were impressive.  這些牛排的肉質令人印象深刻。
The meatinesses of the discussion surprised everyone. 討論的實質性讓每個人都感到驚訝。
Synonyms: substance, richness, depth, solidity
Antonyms: insignificance, superficiality, shallowness
