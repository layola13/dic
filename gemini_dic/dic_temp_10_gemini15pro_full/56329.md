dogmatism: /ˈdɒɡmətɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡmətɪzəm/ (US)| n. |教条主义；武断；独断|The tendency to lay down principles as incontrovertibly true, without consideration of evidence or the opinions of others.|He was known for his dogmatism and unwillingness to compromise. 他以教条主义和不愿妥协而闻名。|例句：Her dogmatism on the subject made any real discussion impossible. 她在这个问题上的教条主义使得任何真正的讨论都不可能。

例句：The professor's dogmatism alienated many of his students.  教授的武断疏远了许多学生。


近义词：rigidity, inflexibility, doctrinaireism, authoritarianism|反义词:open-mindedness, flexibility, relativism|词性:noun
