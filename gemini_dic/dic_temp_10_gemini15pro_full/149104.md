oxfly:/ˈɒksflaɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːksflaɪ/ (US)| n. |牛虻|A biting fly of the family Tabanidae, found in Africa and which sucks the blood of mammals, especially cattle. |The oxfly is a pest that transmits diseases to livestock. 牛虻是一种害虫，会传播疾病给牲畜。| Oxfly bites can be very painful. 牛虻叮咬会很痛。 |Synonyms: gadfly, horsefly, cleg| Antonyms: None| Noun
