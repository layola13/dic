flavonoid:/ˈfleɪvənɔɪd/| n. |类黄酮，黄酮类化合物|A large group of plant pigments that are responsible for many of the colors in fruits, vegetables, and flowers. They are also known for their antioxidant properties and potential health benefits.|例句：Flavonoids are found in a wide variety of foods, including berries, grapes, and citrus fruits. 类黄酮存在于各种各样的食物中，包括浆果、葡萄和柑橘类水果。

例句：Research suggests that flavonoids may help protect against certain chronic diseases. 研究表明，类黄酮可能有助于预防某些慢性疾病。|近义词：bioflavonoid|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
