obstreperously:/əbˈstrɛpərəsli/ | adv. | 吵闹地；喧嚣地；不服管束地 | characterized by loud, unruly, and defiant behavior; boisterously and noisily | The children behaved obstreperously during the long car ride. 孩子们在漫长的车程中吵闹个不停。| synonym: boisterously, clamorously, vociferously | antonym: quietly, calmly, peacefully | 词性: 副词

obstreperous:/əbˈstrɛpərəs/ | adj. | 吵闹的；喧嚣的；不服管束的 | noisy and difficult to control | The obstreperous crowd booed the referee. 吵闹的观众对裁判发出嘘声。| synonym: unruly, boisterous, clamorous | antonym: quiet, calm, peaceful | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstreperus* "noisy, clamorous," from *obstrepere* "to make a noise against," from *ob-* "against" + *strepere* "to make a noise."
