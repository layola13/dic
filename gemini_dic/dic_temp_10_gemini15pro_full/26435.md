bypass:/ˈbaɪpɑːs/ (US), /ˈbaɪpɑːs/ (UK)| n. vt.| 旁路，绕过；分流术|A road that passes around a town or city to avoid traffic congestion.  |The new bypass will reduce traffic in the city center. 新的绕城公路将减少市中心的交通拥堵。|synonyms: detour, alternative route, shunt|antonyms: direct route, main road|noun
bypass:/ˈbaɪpɑːs/ (US), /ˈbaɪpɑːs/ (UK)| vt.| 绕过，避开|To avoid something by going around it. |They bypassed the congested highway and took a back road. 他们绕过了拥堵的高速公路，走了一条小路。|synonyms: circumvent, avoid, evade|antonyms: confront, face, encounter|verb
