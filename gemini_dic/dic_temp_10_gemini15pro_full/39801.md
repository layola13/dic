consortial:/kənˈsɔːrʃəl/
adjective
财团的；联合的；合伙的
Relating to or involving a consortium.
Example: The project was funded by a consortial agreement between several universities.  这个项目是由几所大学之间的联合协议资助的。

Example: Consortial lending is common in large infrastructure projects.  财团贷款在大型基础设施项目中很常见。

Synonym: joint, combined, collaborative, cooperative
Antonym: individual, separate, independent
词性: adjective
