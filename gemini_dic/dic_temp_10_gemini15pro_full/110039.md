licensable:/ˈlaɪsənsəbl/ | adj. | 可许可的，可授权的 | Something is licensable if it can be given a license or if a license is required for it. |  The software is licensable under a GNU General Public License.  这款软件在GNU通用公共许可证下获得许可。 | Synonyms: permittable, authorized, allowable | Antonyms:  unlicensable, prohibited, forbidden | word type: adjective
