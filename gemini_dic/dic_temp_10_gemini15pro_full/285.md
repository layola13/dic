abelian:/əˈbiːliən/| adj.|阿贝尔的，交换的|Of or relating to Niels Henrik Abel or his mathematical discoveries, especially in the area of commutative groups.|The group of integers under addition is abelian.  整数加法群是阿贝尔群。

Addition of real numbers is an abelian operation. 实数的加法是阿贝尔运算。|近义词：commutative|反义词:non-abelian, noncommutative|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Norwegian mathematician Niels Henrik Abel (1802-1829).  以挪威数学家尼尔斯·亨利克·阿贝尔（1802-1829）的名字命名。

USAGE->
An abelian group is a group in which the result of applying the group operation to two group elements does not depend on the order in which they are written. This is formalized by the commutative law.  阿贝尔群是一个群，其中将群运算应用于两个群元素的结果不取决于它们的写入顺序。这是由交换律规定的。


An abelian category is a category satisfying certain axioms which make it behave like the category of abelian groups.  阿贝尔范畴是一个满足特定公理的范畴，这些公理使其表现得像阿贝尔群的范畴。


In an abelian group, a * b = b * a for all a and b in the group. 在阿贝尔群中，对于群中的所有 a 和 b，a * b = b * a。


The concept of an abelian group is fundamental in abstract algebra and has applications in many areas of mathematics and physics.  阿贝尔群的概念是抽象代数的基础，并在数学和物理学的许多领域都有应用。


Non-abelian groups are groups that are not abelian.  非阿贝尔群是不满足交换律的群。


PHRASE->

abelian extension = 阿贝尔扩张
