chunkier:/ˈtʃʌŋkiər/| adj.|更粗的，更厚的，更结实的|Comparitive form of chunky: more chunky.  Meaning thicker, heavier, and more solid.|The stew had chunkier pieces of vegetables than I expected.  这道炖菜里的蔬菜块比我预想的要大。|Synonyms: thicker, heavier, stockier, beefier, stubbier|Antonyms: thinner, slimmer, slenderer|adjective

chunkier:/ˈtʃʌŋkiər/| adj.|更大块的，更厚的|Comparitive form of chunky: more chunky. Meaning made up of larger, thicker pieces.|I prefer chunkier peanut butter. 我更喜欢颗粒更大的花生酱。|Synonyms: thicker, lumpier|Antonyms: smoother, finer|adjective
