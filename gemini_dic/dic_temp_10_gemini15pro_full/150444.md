pantaloonery:/ˌpæntəˈluːnəri/
n.
滑稽可笑的服装；小丑服装；过时的服装
clownish or outdated clothing, especially trousers.
The old man's pantaloonery made him look like he'd stepped out of a time machine.  这位老人的滑稽服装让他看起来像是从时光机里走出来的。
The comedian's act involved outlandish pantaloonery and slapstick humor.  这位喜剧演员的表演包括古怪的服装和滑稽的幽默。
Synonyms: buffoonery, clowning, antics
Antonyms: formality, seriousness
noun
