词源: be- + slaver.  Be- is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over". Slaver means "to drool or slobber".  The original meaning was to cover someone with saliva, but it evolved to mean excessive flattery, likely due to the image of a dog licking its owner's hand.
