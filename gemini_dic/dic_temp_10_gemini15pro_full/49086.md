deictic:/ˈdaɪktɪk/| adj.|指示的|Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used, e.g., *here*, *you*, *tomorrow*.|例句：The word "here" is deictic, as its meaning depends on the speaker's location.  “这里”这个词是指示性的，因为它的含义取决于说话者的位置。

例句：Pronouns like "I" and "you" are deictic expressions. 像“我”和“你”这样的代词是指示性表达。|近义词：indexical, demonstrative|反义词:non-deictic


词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *deiktikos* "able to show, fit for demonstrating," from *deiknynai* "to show."
