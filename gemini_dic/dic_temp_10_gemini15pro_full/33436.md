churlishly:/ˈtʃɜːrlɪʃli/
| adv. |粗鲁地，粗暴地|In a rude and bad-tempered way. |He churlishly refused to offer any explanation for his behavior. 他粗暴地拒绝解释他的行为。|Synonyms: rudely, impolitely, ungraciously, surlily, boorishly|Antonyms: politely, courteously, graciously, pleasantly|词性:副词

词源：churlish来源于古英语 ceorlisc，意思是“属于平民的，粗俗的”。  它最初是指农民或下层阶级的人，后来演变为指粗鲁无礼的行为。
