ambivalent:/æmˈbɪvəl(ə)nt/ |adj.|矛盾的；有矛盾情绪的|Having mixed feelings or contradictory ideas about something or someone.|I felt ambivalent about leaving home. 我对离家感到矛盾。|近义词: uncertain, unsure, doubtful, hesitant, indecisive, equivocal |反义词: certain, sure, decided, resolute, unequivocal|adjective
