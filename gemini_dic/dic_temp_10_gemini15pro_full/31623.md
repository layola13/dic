chat-up: /ˈtʃæt ʌp/ (UK) /ˈtʃæt ʌp/ (US)
| adj. | 搭讪的 | Relating to or used for starting a casual conversation with a stranger, often with romantic intent. | He used a chat-up line on her. 他对她使用了搭讪的开场白。 | 近义词: pick-up | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词
