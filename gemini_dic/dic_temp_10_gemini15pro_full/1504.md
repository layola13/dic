acetimeter:/ˌæsɪˈtɪmɪtər/
noun
醋酸计；醋酸定量器
An instrument for determining the strength of vinegar or acetic acid.
The chemist used an acetimeter to measure the acidity of the vinegar.  化学家使用醋酸计测量醋的酸度。
The acetimeter showed that the vinegar's acidity was within the acceptable range. 醋酸计显示醋的酸度在可接受的范围内。

近义词:acidimeter
反义词:None
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French acétimètre, from acétique (“acetic”) + -mètre (“-meter”).  Ultimately from Latin acētum (“vinegar”) + Greek μέτρον (métron, “measure”).

USAGE->
The acetimeter is an essential tool for vinegar production. 醋酸计是醋生产中必不可少的工具。

The quality control department uses an acetimeter to ensure the consistency of the vinegar. 质量控制部门使用醋酸计来确保醋的浓度一致。
