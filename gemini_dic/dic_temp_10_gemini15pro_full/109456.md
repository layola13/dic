lepidodendron:/ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈdɛndrɒn/ | n. |鳞木| An extinct tree-like plant of the Carboniferous period, characterized by its scaly bark and large, cone-shaped fruiting bodies.  |  Example: Fossils of Lepidodendron are commonly found in coal deposits.  鳞木化石常见于煤矿床中。 | Example: The Lepidodendron could grow up to 40 meters tall. 鳞木可以长到40米高。 |Synonym: scale tree | Antonym: N/A | noun
