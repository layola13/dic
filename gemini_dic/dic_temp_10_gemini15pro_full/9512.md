antischolasticisms: /ˌæntiˌskɒləˈstɪsɪzəmz/ | n. | 反学院主义，反学究主义 |  Opposition to traditional or formal education, especially that which emphasizes intellectual pursuits over practical skills.  It often involves a rejection of intellectualism and academic elitism. |  His antischolasticisms led him to drop out of college and pursue a career in carpentry.  他反学院主义的思想使他从大学辍学，从事木工职业。 |  The artist's work reflected a strong antischolasticisms, preferring instinct and emotion over formal training.  这位艺术家的作品反映了强烈的反学院主义，更喜欢本能和情感而不是正规训练。 | Near synonyms: anti-intellectualism, philistinism | Antonyms: scholasticism, intellectualism | Noun
