masterfully:/ˈmæstərˌfʊli/| adv.|熟练地，巧妙地|In a way that shows great skill and control.|She played the piano masterfully. 她熟练地演奏了钢琴。|近义词：skillfully, expertly, adeptly, proficiently|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly|词性: 副词

词源:  From "master" (meaning a person highly skilled in something) + "-ful" (meaning full of) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
