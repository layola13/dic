enchanters:/ɪnˈtʃæntərz/
n.
魔法师；施魔法者；使人着迷的人
A person who enchants; a magician or sorcerer.  Someone who captivates or charms.
The enchanters of the old tales could transform princes into frogs. 古老故事中的魔法师可以把王子变成青蛙。
The actress's beauty and talent made her a true enchanter of the audience.  这位女演员的美貌和才华使她成为真正的观众迷。
Synonyms: magician, sorcerer, wizard, charmer, captivator
Antonyms: bore, dullard
