antipoetical:/ˌæntipoʊˈetɪkəl/
adjective
反诗意的；缺乏诗意的
Lacking poetic qualities; unpoetic.
His antipoetical language made it difficult for readers to connect with the beauty of nature he was describing. 他那缺乏诗意的语言使得读者难以与他所描述的自然之美产生共鸣。
The businessman's dry, antipoetical speech bored the audience.  这位商人枯燥乏味的、缺乏诗意的演讲让听众感到无聊。
近义词:prosaic, unpoetic, mundane
反义词:poetic, lyrical, romantic
词性:adjective
