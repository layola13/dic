milice:/miːˈliːs/ | n. | 民兵，民团，（尤指政治或宗教的）武装团体 | A body of citizen soldiers called upon to defend their country, or a private army raised by a political party or other unofficial organization.  |  The local milice was called out to quell the riot. 当地民兵被召集起来平息骚乱。 | Synonyms: militia, vigilantes, paramilitary | Antonyms: regular army, police force | noun
