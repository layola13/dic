chafferer:/ˈtʃæfərər/| n.|讨价还价者，讲价者|A person who haggles or bargains, especially persistently.|The chafferer spent hours at the market trying to get the best price for the rugs.|Synonyms: bargainer, haggler, trader, dealer|Antonyms: buyer, customer|noun
Etymology: From Middle English *chaffar*, from Old North French *chaf(f)er* "to trade, bargain, exchange," of uncertain origin.
