buzzingly:/ˈbʌzɪŋli/| adv.|嗡嗡地，嘈杂地|In a manner that produces a buzzing sound.  Describes an atmosphere or activity filled with a low, continuous humming sound, often suggesting excitement or activity.|例句：The bees were buzzingly around the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围嗡嗡作响。|近义词：hummingly, droningly|反义词:quietly, silently|词性:副词
buzz:/bʌz/| n. v.|嗡嗡声，嘈杂声；发出嗡嗡声|A low, continuous humming sound.|例句：The buzz of the city kept me awake. 城市的喧嚣让我无法入睡。|近义词：hum, drone|反义词:silence, quiet|词性:名词，动词

词源: From the verb "buzz," which is imitative of the sound.
