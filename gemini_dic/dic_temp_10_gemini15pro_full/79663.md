glottals:/ˈɡlɒtəlz/ (UK) /ˈɡlɑːtəlz/ (US)| n.| 声门音；喉音|Sounds produced using the glottis, the space between the vocal cords.  These sounds include the glottal stop. |例句：The glottal stop is a common feature of many dialects of English. 声门塞音是许多英语方言的共同特征。

例句：The word "uh-oh" often features a glottal stop between the two syllables.  “uh-oh”这个词的两个音节之间通常有一个声门塞音。|近义词：laryngeals|反义词:|noun
