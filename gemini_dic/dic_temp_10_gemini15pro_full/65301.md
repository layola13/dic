eudemonics:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪks/ | n. | 幸福论，快乐论 |  The study of human flourishing and well-being, focusing on what constitutes a good life and how to achieve it. |  She dedicated her life to the study of eudemonics, seeking ways to promote happiness and fulfillment. 她毕生致力于幸福论的研究，寻求促进幸福和成就的方法。 |  synonyms:  eudaemonism,  well-being,  flourishing,  happiness | antonyms:  misery,  unhappiness,  suffering | noun
