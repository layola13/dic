distastefulness:/dɪsˈteɪstfʊlnəs/| n.|令人厌恶；讨厌；反感|The distastefulness of the situation made everyone uncomfortable.  这种令人厌恶的情况让每个人都不舒服。|Synonyms: unpleasantness, offensiveness, repugnance, repulsiveness|Antonyms: pleasantness, agreeableness, attractiveness, appeal|noun
Etymology: From distasteful + -ness.  Distasteful from dis- (not) + tasteful. Ultimately from Latin gustus (taste).
