fabrile:/ˈfeɪbraɪl/ (US), /ˈfiːbraɪl/ (UK)| adj.|发热的，发烧的|Characterized by or caused by fever; feverish.|例句：The patient presented with febrile symptoms, including a high temperature and chills. 病人出现发热症状，包括高烧和寒战。

例句：A febrile illness swept through the school, affecting many students and staff. 一种发热性疾病席卷了整个学校，影响了许多学生和教职工。

近义词：feverish, pyretic|反义词:afebrile|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *febrilis, from Latin febris (“fever”).
