overwariness:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛrɪnəs/| n.|过度谨慎，过分小心|The overwariness of the investors led them to miss out on a lucrative opportunity. 投资者过度的谨慎让他们错失了一个获利丰厚的机会。|Example: His overwariness prevented him from taking any risks. 他的过分小心使他不愿承担任何风险。|Synonyms: caution, prudence, circumspection|Antonyms: recklessness, carelessness, imprudence|Noun

Etymology:
From over- + wariness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Wariness" comes from the Old English word "wær," meaning "aware" or "cautious."
