morally:/ˈmɔːrəli/ /ˈmɑːrəli/| adv.| 道德上地；合乎道德地| In a way that relates to the principles of right and wrong behavior. |例句：She acted morally by returning the lost wallet. 她拾金不昧，行为高尚。

例句：He was morally obligated to help his family. 他在道义上有义务帮助他的家人。


近义词：ethically, righteously, virtuously|反义词: immorally, unethically, wrongly|词性: 副词
