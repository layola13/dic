morgans:/ˈmɔːrɡənz/ | n. | 摩根（遗传学单位）; 摩根（货币单位） | A unit of distance between genes on a chromosome, representing the frequency of crossing-over between them. Also, a former monetary unit of France. |  The two genes are 10 morgans apart on the chromosome. 这两个基因在染色体上相距10摩根。|  centimorgan, map unit (遗传学); franc (货币单位) | - | noun
morgan:/ˈmɔːrɡən/ | n. | 马匹品种 | A breed of horse known for its versatility and gentle temperament. |  She owns a beautiful Morgan horse. 她拥有一匹漂亮的摩根马。|  horse, steed | - | noun
