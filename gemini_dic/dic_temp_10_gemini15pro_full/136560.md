nonsailor:/ˈnɒnˌseɪlə(r)/ /ˈnɑːnˌseɪlər/
noun
非航海者，不懂航海的人
A person who is not a sailor; someone who does not sail or know much about sailing.
例句：The nonsailor felt seasick within minutes of leaving the harbor.  这个不懂航海的人离开港口几分钟后就晕船了。

例句：The yacht club offered introductory courses for nonsailors.  游艇俱乐部为非航海者提供入门课程。

近义词：landlubber
反义词：sailor
词性：noun
