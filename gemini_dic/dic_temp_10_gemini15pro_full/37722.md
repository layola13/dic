compared:/kəmˈpeərd/ | adj. | 相比之下 |  Examined for similarities and differences. |  Her problems seemed insignificant compared with his. 与他的问题相比，她的问题显得微不足道。 | Synonyms: relative, comparative | Antonyms: absolute, independent | adjective
