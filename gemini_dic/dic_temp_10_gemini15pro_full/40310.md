contortion:/kənˈtɔːʃən/
| n. |扭曲，变形|a twisting or distortion of something, especially a body part, out of its natural shape.|The contortions of the dancer's body were amazing. 舞蹈演员身体的扭曲令人惊叹。|近义词：twist, distortion, deformation|反义词：straightening|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contortionatus, past participle of contortionare "to twist violently," from Latin contortus, past participle of contorquēre "to twist, wrench, distort," from com- "together" + torquēre "to twist."
