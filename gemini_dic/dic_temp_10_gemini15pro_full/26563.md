cablelike:/ˈkeɪbəlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像电缆的; cable-like
Resembling a cable in form or function.
The creature had a long, cablelike tail.  这个生物有一条长长的、像电缆一样的尾巴。
The thick, cablelike vines hung from the tree.  粗壮的、像电缆一样的藤蔓从树上垂下来。
近义词:ropelike, cordlike, wiry
反义词:thin, slender
