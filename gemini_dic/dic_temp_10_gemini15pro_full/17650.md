beck:/bek/ | v. | 召唤；示意 | to make a gesture with the hand, head, etc., typically to summon someone |  She beckoned him to follow. 她示意他跟着她。 | synonyms: summon, signal, call, invite, gesture | antonyms: dismiss, reject | verb
