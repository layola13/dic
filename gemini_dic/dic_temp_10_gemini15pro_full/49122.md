deiparous:/dɪˈɪpərəs/| adj.|神之母的，生神的|Bearing or bringing forth a god, specifically applied to the Virgin Mary.|The deiparous Virgin Mary is venerated by many Christians.  圣母玛利亚被许多基督徒崇敬。|Near Synonyms: divine, godly, holy|Antonyms:  profane, unholy|adjective

deiparous:/dɪˈɪpərəs/| adj.|生神的，神圣的|Giving birth to a god; of or relating to the Virgin Mary as the mother of God.  |The icon depicted the deiparous Mother of God. 圣像描绘了生神的圣母。|Near Synonyms:  theotokos, God-bearing|Antonyms: mortal, human|adjective
