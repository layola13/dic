axiomatic:/ˌæksiəˈmætɪk/| adj.|公理的；不言自明的|Self-evident; taken as a given; based on an axiom.|It is axiomatic that good health is essential for a happy life.  健康对幸福生活至关重要，这是不言而喻的。|Synonyms: self-evident, obvious, given, undisputed, accepted, fundamental|Antonyms: questionable, debatable, disputable, uncertain|adjective

axiomatically:/ˌæksiəˈmætɪkli/| adv.|公理地；不言而喻地|In a self-evident or axiomatic manner.|Axiomatically, any increase in taxation will be unpopular.  不言而喻，任何增税都会不受欢迎。|Synonyms: obviously, evidently, clearly, undoubtedly, indisputably|Antonyms: questionably, debatably, disputably|adverb
