hosannaed:/hoʊˈzænəd/
|v.|欢呼；高呼“和散那”；赞美|To have praised or acclaimed enthusiastically, especially with shouts of "Hosanna."|The crowd hosannaed the returning hero. 群众欢呼迎接凯旋的英雄。|Near synonyms: acclaimed, cheered, lauded, praised, celebrated|Near antonyms: condemned, criticized, denounced|Verb
