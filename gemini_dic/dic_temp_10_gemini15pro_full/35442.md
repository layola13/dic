co-ruler:/ˈkəʊˌruːlə(r)/ | n. |共同统治者; 共治者|A person who rules jointly with another or others.|The two emperors were co-rulers for ten years. 这两位皇帝共同统治了十年。|Synonyms: co-regent, joint ruler|Antonyms: subject, subordinate|noun
