call it quits:/ˈkɔːl ɪt kwɪts/
verb phrase
停止；结束；放弃
to agree with someone to stop doing something, especially after a disagreement or fight.  To stop an activity or end a relationship.
After twenty years of marriage, they decided to call it quits.  二十年的婚姻后，他们决定离婚。
They were losing money, so they decided to call it quits and close the business.  他们一直在亏钱，所以决定放弃并关掉生意。
近义词: give up, stop, end, quit, cease
反义词: continue, persist, persevere
词性: verb phrase

call:/kɔːl/
verb
叫，称呼；打电话；认为
To give a name to; to telephone; to consider
He called her "sweetheart." 他叫她“甜心”。
She called her mother every day. 她每天都给母亲打电话。
I call that a good day's work. 我认为那是今天工作出色。
近义词: name, telephone, consider
反义词: ignore, neglect
词性: verb

it:/ɪt/
pronoun
它
Used to refer to something previously mentioned or easily identified.
The dog wagged its tail. 狗摇着尾巴。
It is raining. 下雨了。
近义词: this, that
反义词: 无
词性: pronoun

quits:/kwɪts/
adjective
扯平，两清
Even or equal, especially after settling a debt or score.
We're quits now. 现在我们两清了。
近义词: even, square
反义词: uneven, unbalanced
词性: adjective
