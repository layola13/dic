focussed:/ˈfəʊkəst/ (British English), /ˈfoʊkəst/ (American English)| adj.| 集中的，专注的|Concentrated on something; attentively directed.|例句：She was focussed on her work and didn't hear the phone. 她专注于工作，没听到电话响。

例句：The laser beam is focussed on a small spot. 激光束聚焦在一个小点上。

近义词：concentrated, attentive, dedicated, intent|反义词：distracted, unfocused, scattered, inattentive|词性:形容词


focussed:/ˈfəʊkəst/ (British English), /ˈfoʊkəst/ (American English)| v.| (focus的过去式和过去分词) 使集中; 集中(注意力等)|past tense and past participle of "focus".  To concentrate attention or effort on something.|例句：He focussed his attention on the problem. 他把注意力集中在这个问题上。

例句：The camera focussed automatically. 相机自动对焦。

近义词：concentrated, honed, directed|反义词：blurred, diffused, scattered|词性:动词


词源: From the Latin "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace".  The meaning shifted to "center point" and then to the current senses of concentration and a point of convergence.
