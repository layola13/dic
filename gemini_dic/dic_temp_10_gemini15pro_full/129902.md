non-Israelites:/nɒnˈɪzreɪlaɪts/ | n. | 非以色列人 | People who are not Israelites, referring to those not descended from Jacob (also called Israel) and therefore not members of the twelve tribes of Israel. |  The Philistines were non-Israelites who often clashed with the Israelites. 非利士人是非以色列人，经常与以色列人发生冲突。

The laws regarding sacrifices were different for Israelites and non-Israelites.  关于祭祀的律法对以色列人和非以色列人有所不同。 | 近义词: Gentiles, foreigners | 反义词: Israelites, Jews | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): From Latin *non*, meaning "not".
Israelite (noun): From Late Latin *Israelita*, from Greek *Israēlítēs*, from Hebrew *Yiśrā’ēlī*, meaning "descendant of Israel (Jacob)".
