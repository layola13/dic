madrigalists:/ˈmædrɪɡəlɪsts/
n.| 牧歌作曲家; 牧歌演唱者|Composers or singers of madrigals.|The madrigalists of the Renaissance period created complex and beautiful vocal works. 文艺复兴时期的牧歌作曲家创作了复杂而优美的声乐作品。|近义词:composers, musicians, singers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "non-musicians")|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Italian *madrigalista*, from *madrigale* (madrigal) + *-ista* (a suffix denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something).  Ultimately from Late Latin *matricale* "rustic song," perhaps from *mater* "mother."
