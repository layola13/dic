overvalued:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuːd/| adj.|估值过高；高估的|Having a value placed on it that is too high; overpriced.|The stock market is thought to be overvalued by many analysts. 许多分析师认为股市估值过高。|Synonyms: overpriced, inflated, exorbitant|Antonyms: undervalued, bargain, cheap|adjective
