facultative:/ˈfækəlˌteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 兼性的；隨意的；可选择的 | Occurring optionally; not compulsory or automatic. | Some anaerobic bacteria are facultative, meaning they can live with or without oxygen.  一些厌氧细菌是兼性的，意味着它们可以在有氧或无氧环境下生存。|  This type of training is facultative. 这种培训是可选的。 | 近义词: optional, elective, discretionary | 反义词: obligatory, compulsory, mandatory | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *facultātīvus, from facultās (“capability, opportunity, power”), from facilis (“easy”).
