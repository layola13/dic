luxurious: /lʌɡˈʒʊəriəs/ /lʌɡˈʒɜːriəs/ | adj. | 奢华的，豪华的 | Extremely comfortable and expensive; giving pleasure by satisfying the senses. |  She stayed in a luxurious hotel suite. 她住在一个豪华的酒店套房里。|  The car has a luxurious interior with leather seats. 这辆车内部很豪华，有真皮座椅。| 近义词: opulent, lavish, sumptuous, deluxe | 反义词: basic, simple, austere, modest | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *luxuriosus*, from *luxuria* ("luxury, abundance, extravagance"), from *luxus* ("dislocation, excess"), related to *lucere* ("to shine, be light").
