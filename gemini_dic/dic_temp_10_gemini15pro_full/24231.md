bridgeworks:/ˈbrɪdʒwɜːrks/
noun
桥梁工程，桥梁建筑
The construction or engineering of bridges.
The bridgeworks are nearing completion. 桥梁工程即将竣工。
近义词: bridge construction, bridge building
反义词: bridge demolition

ETYMOLOGY->
"bridgeworks" is a compound word formed from "bridge" and "works."
"Bridge" comes from Old English "brycg," meaning "bridge, causeway."
"Works" comes from Old English "weorc," meaning "work, labor, action."
