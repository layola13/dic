out:/aʊt/| adv. adj. n. prep. v.| 出去；在外；熄灭；公开；出局|Out of sight, out of mind. 眼不见，心不烦。|近义词：outside, away, off, extinct, revealed|反义词：in, inside, on, alight, hidden|词性：副词/形容词/名词/介词/动词
Etymology: From Old English *ūt (adverb, preposition, adjective) and *ūte (adjective), from Proto-Germanic *ūt (adverb, preposition), *ūtiz (adjective), from Proto-Indo-European *ud-/*ut- (out, away).
