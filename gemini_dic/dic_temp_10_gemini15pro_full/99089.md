interbreed:/ˌɪntərˈbriːd/
verb
杂交，异种交配
to breed or cause to breed with members of another breed or group
例句：
Wolves rarely interbreed with coyotes.
狼很少与土狼杂交。

Different varieties of plants can be interbred.
不同品种的植物可以杂交。

近义词：crossbreed, hybridize
反义词：purebreed
词性：v.
