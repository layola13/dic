encomium:/ɪnˈkəʊmiəm/ |n.|赞美; 颂词; 赞颂; 称颂|A formal expression of high praise; a eulogy.|He received encomiums from literary critics for his first novel. 他因第一部小说受到了文学评论家的赞扬。|Synonyms: praise, tribute, eulogy, panegyric, accolade|Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure|noun
