ambitions:/æmˈbɪʃənz/
n.
雄心，抱负；野心
a strong desire to do or to achieve something, typically something important, impressive, or difficult to do.
She has great ambitions to become a doctor. 她有成为一名医生的雄心壮志。

My ambition is to travel the world. 我的抱负是环游世界。

近义词: aspirations, goals, aims, objectives
反义词: apathy, indifference, inactivity

词性: 名词 (plural)
