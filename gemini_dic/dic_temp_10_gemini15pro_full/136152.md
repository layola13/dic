nonregenerating: /ˌnɒnriːˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非再生性的，不能再生的 | Not able to regenerate or be regenerated; incapable of renewal or restoration. |  The nonregenerating tissue was damaged beyond repair.  非再生组织受损严重，无法修复。|  The patient suffered from a nonregenerating nerve injury.  病人患有无法再生的神经损伤。| 近义词: irreparable, irreversible, permanent | 反义词: regenerating, renewable, restorative | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  
* non-:  Latin prefix meaning "not"
* regenerating: from Latin regeneratus, past participle of regenerare "to bring forth again," from re- "again" + generare "to beget"
