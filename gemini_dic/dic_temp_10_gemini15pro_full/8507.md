antibureaucratic:/ˌæntibaɪərəˈkrætɪk/
adjective
反官僚主义的
Opposed to or designed to prevent excessive bureaucracy or rigid adherence to rules and regulations.
The new mayor promised to implement antibureaucratic reforms to streamline city services. 新市长承诺实施反官僚主义改革，以简化城市服务。
The group advocated for antibureaucratic measures to make the government more responsive to the people's needs. 该团体倡导反官僚主义措施，使政府更能响应人民的需求。
Near Synonyms:  anti-red tape, nonbureaucratic, deregulated
Antonyms: bureaucratic, red-tape
