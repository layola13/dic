duplicitous:/duːˈplɪsɪtəs/| adj.|欺骗的；奸诈的；表里不一的|Deceptive in words or action, especially by speaking or acting in two different ways to different people about the same matter; two-faced.|例句：The duplicitous salesman sold the same car to two different people.  这个奸诈的推销员把同一辆车卖给了两个人。

例句：Her duplicitous nature made it hard to trust her. 她表里不一的性格让人很难信任她。|近义词：deceptive, dishonest, two-faced, deceitful, cunning, treacherous, insidious|反义词：honest, sincere, truthful, straightforward, candid|词性:形容词
词源: Late 16th century: from late Latin duplicitas ‘doubleness’, from duplex, duplic- ‘double’.
