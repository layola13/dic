liberalised:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzd/ | adj. |自由化的，放宽管制的 |Made or become more liberal, especially in social or political views; freed from or opposed to traditional restrictions. |例句：The government has liberalised the media. 政府已经放宽了对媒体的管制。|近义词：deregulated, freed, loosened |反义词: restricted, controlled |词性:形容词

liberalised:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzd/ | v. |使自由化，放宽…的管制 (liberalised的动词形式，过去分词) | To make or become liberal; remove or loosen restrictions on (something, typically an economic or political system).|例句：Trade has been liberalised. 贸易已经被自由化了。|近义词：deregulated, freed, loosened |反义词: restricted, controlled |词性:动词

Etymology: From liberal + -ise + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *liberalis "noble, generous, pertaining to a free man," from liber "free."
