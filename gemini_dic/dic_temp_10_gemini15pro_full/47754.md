decalogist: /dɪˈkælədʒɪst/
noun
十诫专家，精通十诫的人
An expert in the Decalogue (the Ten Commandments).
The decalogist explained the nuances of each commandment to the congregation. 十诫专家向会众解释了每条诫命的细微之处。
Near Synonyms: theologian, biblical scholar, scriptural expert
Antonyms: heretic, unbeliever, infidel
