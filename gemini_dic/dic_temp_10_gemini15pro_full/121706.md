misprescribed:/ˌmɪsprɪˈskraɪbd/
adjective
错误地开处方；处方不当
incorrectly prescribed, usually medication.
The patient suffered adverse effects from the misprescribed medication.  病人因处方错误的药物而遭受不良反应。
Misprescribed antibiotics can lead to antibiotic resistance.  错误开出的抗生素会导致抗生素耐药性。
近义词: wrongly prescribed, inappropriately prescribed
反义词: correctly prescribed, appropriately prescribed
词性: adjective

词源:
mis- (prefix): wrong, bad, wrongly  错误的，坏的，不正确地
prescribe (verb): (of a medical practitioner) advise and authorize the use of (a medicine or treatment) for someone, especially in writing.  （指医生）建议并授权某人使用（药物或治疗），尤指以书面形式。
