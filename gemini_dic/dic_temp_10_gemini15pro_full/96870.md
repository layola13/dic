inexhaustibly:/ɪnɪɡˈzɔːstəbli/ | adv. |取之不尽地，不知疲倦地 |  In a manner that cannot be exhausted; tirelessly. |  He worked inexhaustibly on the project, driven by his passion. 他不知疲倦地致力于这个项目，受他的热情驱使。 |  tirelessly, endlessly, incessantly, perpetually |  exhaustingly, finitely, intermittently | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + exhaustible + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Exhaustible comes from exhaust + -ible (capable of being). Exhaust ultimately derives from Latin exhaurire "to draw out completely," from ex- "out" + haurire "to draw, drain."
