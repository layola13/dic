foothold:/ˈfʊthəʊld/ (British) /ˈfʊthoʊld/ (American)| n.|立足点；稳固地位；根据地|A secure position from which further progress can be made.  A place where a foot can be firmly placed in climbing or walking.|例句：The climbers struggled to find a foothold on the icy rock face. 攀登者在结冰的岩壁上努力寻找立足点。

例句：The company gained a foothold in the European market. 这家公司在欧洲市场站稳了脚跟。|近义词：footing, base, foundation, bridgehead, toehold|反义词: instability, insecurity|词性: noun
