hydrotherapist:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɛrəpɪst/| n.|水疗师|A person who provides hydrotherapy. A healthcare professional specializing in the use of water for pain relief and treatment.|例句：The hydrotherapist recommended a series of warm water exercises for my back pain. 水疗师建议我进行一系列温水练习来缓解背痛。

例句：My grandmother sees a hydrotherapist regularly to help with her arthritis. 我的祖母定期去看水疗师以帮助治疗她的关节炎。|近义词：aquatic therapist, physical therapist (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:
hydro- + therapist
hydro-: from Greek *hudōr "water"
therapist: from Greek therapeutēs "attendant, servant," later "healer, physician"
