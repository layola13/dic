intermixture:/ˌɪntərˈmɪkstʃər/ | n. | 混合，混合物 | A substance made by mixing other substances together. | The rock is an intermixture of different minerals. 这块岩石是不同矿物质的混合物。|  The cake was a delicious intermixture of flavors.  这个蛋糕是各种口味的美味混合。 | synonyms: blend, mixture, combination, composite, amalgamation | antonyms: separation, division, segregation | noun

intermix:/ˌɪntərˈmɪks/ | v. | 混合，混杂 | To mix together. |  Oil and water don't intermix easily. 油和水不容易混合。 |  The different cultures intermixed over time. 不同的文化随着时间的推移相互融合。 | synonyms: blend, mingle, combine, merge | antonyms: separate, divide, segregate | verb
