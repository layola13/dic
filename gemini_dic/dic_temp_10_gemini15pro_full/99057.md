interartistic:/ˌɪntərɑːrˈtɪstɪk/
adjective
艺术间的，跨艺术的
Existing or occurring between different art forms.
Interartistic collaborations often lead to innovative and exciting new works.  跨艺术合作常常带来创新和激动人心的新作品。
The festival showcased a range of interartistic projects, blending music, dance, and visual art.  这个节日展示了一系列跨艺术项目，融合了音乐、舞蹈和视觉艺术。
近义词:interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary, multidisciplinary
反义词:specialized, single-discipline
词性:adjective
