hearse:/hɜːrs/ | n. |灵车；柩车|A vehicle for conveying a coffin to a church or cemetery. |例句：The hearse slowly made its way to the cemetery. 灵车缓缓地驶向墓地。

例句：A black hearse carried the coffin to the graveyard. 一辆黑色的灵车将棺材运送到墓地。

近义词：casket wagon, funeral car|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French herse "harrows," later "framework for candles placed over a coffin." From Late Latin hercia "harrow," from Frankish *hersia, from Proto-Germanic *harjō "harrow," from PIE *ḱeres- "to scratch, scrape." The meaning shifted from "framework over a coffin" to "carriage for the dead" by the 17th century.
