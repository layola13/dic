humdrum:/ˈhʌmdrʌm/| adj. n.|单调的，乏味的；单调乏味的事情|Lacking excitement or variety; dull; monotonous.  A tedious, monotonous routine.|例句：He lived a humdrum life, working at the same job for thirty years.  他过了三十年单调乏味的生活，做着同样的工作。|例句：She longed to escape the humdrum of suburban life. 她渴望逃离郊区单调乏味的生活。|近义词：monotonous, dull, tedious, boring, mundane|反义词：exciting, interesting, stimulating, thrilling, vibrant|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology->
From the reduplication of *hum*, imitating a monotonous sound, influenced by *drum*.  The word first appeared as an adverb in the 16th century, later evolving into an adjective and noun.
