enshrines:/ɪnˈʃraɪnz/ | vt. |  enshrine, enshrining, enshrined | 珍藏；铭记；供奉 |  To enclose in or as if in a shrine; cherish as sacred. To preserve or cherish (something, such as a right, tradition, or idea) as sacred or inviolable.  | The Constitution enshrines the basic rights of all citizens. 宪法 enshrines 所有公民的基本权利。

The museum enshrines the memory of the town's founder. 这座博物馆 enshrines 了该镇创始人的记忆。

| 近义词：preserve, cherish, protect, sanctify, consecrate | 反义词：desecrate, profane, violate, disregard | 词性：动词
