counter-surveillance:/ˌkaʊntər sərˈveɪləns/ | n. |反监视，反侦察|The act of detecting and preventing surveillance.  It involves identifying and neutralizing attempts by others to gather information or monitor activities.| The company invested heavily in counter-surveillance technology to protect its trade secrets.  这家公司大力投资反侦察技术来保护其商业机密。|Synonyms: counterintelligence, counterspying|Antonyms: surveillance, espionage|noun

counter-surveillance:/ˌkaʊntər sərˈveɪləns/ | adj. |反监视的，反侦察的| Relating to or used for counter-surveillance. |  He used counter-surveillance techniques to avoid being followed. 他使用反侦察技术来避免被跟踪。|Synonyms: counterintelligence, counterspying|Antonyms: surveillance, espionage|adjective
