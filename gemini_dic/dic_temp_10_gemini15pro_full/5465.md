alog: /ˈeɪlɒɡ/ (British English), /ˈeɪlɔːɡ/ (American English) | n. | 后记，结尾，收场白 | A section at the end of a book or play that serves as a conclusion or epilogue. |  Example: The author added an alog to the second edition of her novel, reflecting on the feedback she received. 作者在小说的第二版中添加了后记，反思了她收到的反馈。

Example:  The play's alog provided a sense of closure to the otherwise ambiguous ending.  这出戏的结尾为原本模棱两可的结局提供了一种完结感。  | Synonym: epilogue, afterword, postscript | Antonym: prologue, preface, foreword | Noun
