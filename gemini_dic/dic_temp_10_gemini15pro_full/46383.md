dadoing:/ˈdeɪdoʊɪŋ/
verb
镶嵌墙裙；给…装护墙板
The act of adding a dado, which is a decorative or protective covering applied to the lower portion of a wall.
He spent the weekend dadoing the dining room. 他整个周末都在给餐厅装护墙板。
The workers were dadoing the hallway with oak paneling.  工人们正在用橡木板给走廊装护墙板。
近义词: wainscoting, paneling
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "stripping" if removing a dado)
词性: verb (动名词)

Etymology: From "dado" (noun), which comes from the Italian "dado" meaning "die" or "cube," referring to the cubic form of the base of a pedestal or column. The "-ing" suffix transforms it into a present participle/gerund, indicating the action of applying a dado.
