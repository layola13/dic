galvanoscopic:/ˌɡælvənəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 电流计的；电流镜的 | Relating to or detected by a galvanoscope (an instrument for detecting the presence, direction, and strength of a small electric current). | The galvanoscopic deflection indicated a weak current. 电流计的偏转表明电流微弱。 |  The galvanoscopic readings were consistent throughout the experiment. 在整个实验过程中，电流计的读数是一致的。 |  Near synonyms:  electrometric, galvanometric | Antonyms:  None | adjective
