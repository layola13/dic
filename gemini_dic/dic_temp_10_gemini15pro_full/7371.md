animal:/ˈænɪməl/ (US), /ˈænɪml/ (UK)| n. |动物|A living being that is not a plant, typically able to move independently and feed on organic matter.|例句：The lion is a wild animal. 狮子是一种野生动物。

例句：She studies animal behavior. 她研究动物行为。

近义词：beast, creature, fauna|反义词:plant

词性:noun
