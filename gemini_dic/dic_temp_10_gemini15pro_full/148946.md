overwrote:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊt/ | v. | 覆盖，重写 | To write over something, especially computer data, replacing the original content. | I accidentally overwrote the file with an older version. 我不小心用旧版本覆盖了文件。| Synonym: replaced, superseded | Antonym: preserved, retained | verb

overwrote:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊt/ | v. |  （无意中）写得过多 | To write more than is necessary or appropriate. | The author overwrote the scene, making it overly dramatic. 作者把这个场景写得太多了，显得过于戏剧化。| Synonym: elaborated excessively, embellished | Antonym: summarized, condensed | verb
