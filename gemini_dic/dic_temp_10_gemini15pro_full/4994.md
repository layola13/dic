alkermes:/ælˈkɜːrmɪz/ | n. |胭脂虫红; 胭脂红; 一种甜酒|A liqueur, traditionally bright red, flavored with spices and often containing kermes.  A vivid crimson to purplish-red natural dye formerly obtained from kermes insects.| The cake was decorated with bright red alkermes.  蛋糕用鲜红色的胭脂虫红装饰。 | 近义词:carmine, crimson, scarlet | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle French alkermes, from Medieval Latin alchermes, from Arabic al-qirmiz (“the kermes”), from qirmiz, from ultimately from Sanskrit कृमिज (kṛmija, “produced by worms”).  Related to crimson and kermes.
