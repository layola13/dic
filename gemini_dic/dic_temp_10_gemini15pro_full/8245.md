anti-indemnity:/ˌæntiɪnˈdɛmnɪti/ | adj. | 反赔偿的，反保障的 |  Relating to a provision or clause that prohibits indemnification or compensation for loss or damage.  It means one party is specifically prevented from seeking reimbursement from another party for certain liabilities. | The contract contained an anti-indemnity clause, preventing the supplier from seeking compensation for any losses incurred due to the buyer's negligence.  合同中包含反赔偿条款，防止供应商因买方疏忽而寻求任何损失赔偿。 | The lease had an anti-indemnity provision, shifting the responsibility for property damage back to the landlord. 租约有反赔偿条款，将财产损失的责任转移回房东。 | Near Synonyms:  non-indemnifiable, uncompensated | Antonyms: indemnifying, compensatory | adjective
