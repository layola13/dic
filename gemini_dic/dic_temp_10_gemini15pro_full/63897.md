epinicion:/ɛpɪˈnɪkiən/ | n. | 颂歌，凯歌 | A song or poem celebrating a victory, especially in an athletic contest.  |  The poet composed an epinicion for the victorious athlete.  诗人为获胜的运动员创作了一首颂歌。 | synonyms: ode, hymn, paean, triumphal song | antonyms: dirge, lament, elegy | noun

Etymology: From Greek ἐπινίκιος (epinikios), from ἐπί (epi, “upon, at”) + νίκη (nikē, “victory”) + -ιος (-ios, adjectival suffix).
