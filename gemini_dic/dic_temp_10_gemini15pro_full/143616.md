outjest:/aʊtˈdʒɛst/
verb
胜过…的玩笑；在玩笑上超过
To surpass in jesting; to outdo in joking or wit.
He tried to outjest the comedian, but failed miserably. 他试图在玩笑上超过喜剧演员，但惨败而归。
The two friends constantly tried to outjest each other, creating a lively and humorous atmosphere. 这两个朋友不断地试图在玩笑上胜过对方，营造出一种活泼幽默的气氛。
Synonyms: outwit, surpass, outdo
Antonyms:  be outdone, lose to
v.
