nonrevivalist:/ˌnɒnriˈvaɪvəlɪst/
n.|非复兴主义者|A person who is not a revivalist; someone who does not advocate or participate in a revival, especially a religious revival.|The nonrevivalist pastor focused on social justice issues rather than emotional conversions.|近义词:traditionalist, conservative|反义词:revivalist, evangelist|词性:名词
nonrevivalist:/ˌnɒnriˈvaɪvəlɪst/
adj.|非复兴主义的|Not pertaining to or characteristic of a revival, especially a religious revival.|The nonrevivalist church service was quiet and contemplative.|近义词:traditional, conventional|反义词:revivalist, evangelical|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
revivalist (noun): from revive (verb) + -ist (suffix forming agent nouns)
revive (verb): from Old French revivre, from Latin revivere "to live again," from re- "again" + vivere "to live"
