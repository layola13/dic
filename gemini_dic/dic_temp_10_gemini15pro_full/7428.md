animosity:/ˌænɪˈmɒsəti/ | n. |敌意，仇恨|A strong feeling of dislike or hatred.|例句：There was a long-standing animosity between the two families. 这两个家族之间长期存在着仇恨。|例句：He felt no animosity towards his opponent. 他对他的对手没有敌意。|近义词：hostility, antagonism, antipathy, rancor, enmity|反义词：friendship, goodwill, amity, affection|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *animositas, from Latin animosus (“spirited, courageous, full of animosity”), from animus (“spirit, mind, courage, wrath”).
