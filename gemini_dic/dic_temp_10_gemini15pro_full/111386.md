liveware:/ˈlaɪvweər/
n.|人员；活体器件；活件|The human element in a system, as opposed to hardware or software.  指系统中的人员因素，与硬件或软件相对。|Pilots are the liveware of the aviation system. 飞行员是航空系统中的活件。|近义词: personnel, staff, human element|反义词: hardware, software|词性:名词
