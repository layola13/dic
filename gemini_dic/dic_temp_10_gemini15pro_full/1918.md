actinism:/ˈæktɪnɪzəm/
noun
光化性; 光化作用; 光能
The property of radiation, especially light, to produce chemical changes, as in photography or sunburn.
The actinism of the sun's rays caused the paint to fade.  太阳光线的化学作用导致油漆褪色。

SYNONYMS->
chemical action, photochemical activity, radiation

ANTONYMS->
inertness, inactivity

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aktinos (ray) + -ism.

USAGE->
The actinism of the sun can cause sunburn. =  阳光的光化作用会导致晒伤。

The photographer used the actinism of light to develop the film. = 摄影师利用光的光化作用来冲洗胶片。
