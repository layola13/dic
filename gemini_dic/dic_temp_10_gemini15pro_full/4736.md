alexic:/əˈlɛksɪk/| adj.|失语的，与词汇有关的|Relating to words or vocabulary; unable to use language due to a brain disorder.  Derived from Greek "a-" (without) + "lexis" (speech, word).|He suffered an alexic aphasia after the stroke, struggling to find the right words. 他在中风后患上了失语症，难以找到合适的词语。|近义词：aphasic, anomic|反义词:verbal, eloquent|词性:adjective
