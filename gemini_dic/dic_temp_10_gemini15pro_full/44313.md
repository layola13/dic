croons:/kruːnz/
verb
低吟浅唱，轻哼
To sing or hum in a soft, sentimental voice.
He crooned a lullaby to his baby daughter. 他低声哼唱摇篮曲哄他的宝贝女儿。
sings softly, hums, murmurs
shouts, yells
