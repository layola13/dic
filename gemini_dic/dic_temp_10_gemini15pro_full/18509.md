benedicite:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪteɪ/ | int. | 祝福，保佑；哎呀，我的天哪 | An archaic or literary exclamation of blessing, astonishment, or polite surprise. | Benedicite! What a wonderful surprise!  哎呀，我的天哪！真是个惊喜！ | 近义词: bless, my goodness, heavens | 反义词: curse | 词性:感叹词
Etymology: From Latin *benedicite*, the second person plural present active imperative of *benedicere* "to bless," from *bene* "well" + *dicere* "to speak."
