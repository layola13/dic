mile:/maɪl/| n.|英里|A unit of linear measure equal to 5,280 feet, or 1,760 yards (approximately 1.609 kilometers).|He ran a mile in four minutes. 他在四分钟内跑了一英里。|
近义词:kilometer, league|反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用short distance短距离)|词性:noun
