nondelivery:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪvəri/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈlɪvəri/
noun
未送达；未交付
failure to deliver something that has been promised or ordered.
The customer complained about the nondelivery of his order. 顾客投诉他的订单未送达。

The nondelivery of the promised goods resulted in a breach of contract.  承诺的货物未交付导致违约。

近义词:non-shipment, failure to deliver, default in delivery
反义词:delivery, shipment

词性:noun
