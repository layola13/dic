giveth:/ˈɡɪvɛθ/| v.|给予，赐予 (古英语，现在时第三人称单数)|This archaic form of "gives" emphasizes the act of bestowing.  这个古老的“gives”形式强调了赠予的行为。|The Lord giveth and the Lord taketh away. 上帝赐予，上帝收回。|近义词：gives, bestows, grants|反义词：taketh, receives|Verb (archaic)
