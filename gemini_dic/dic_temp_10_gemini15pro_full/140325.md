ogresses:/ˈoʊɡrɪsɪz/
n.
女食人魔；母夜叉
A female ogre, a cruel or monstrous woman.
The ogresses in the fairy tale imprisoned the prince. 童话故事里的女食人魔囚禁了王子。

The ogresses threatened to eat the children. 女妖怪们威胁要吃掉孩子们。

近义词:giantesses, monsters, witches
反义词:princesses, fairies, angels
词性:noun (plural)
