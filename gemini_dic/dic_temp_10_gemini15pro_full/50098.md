dentigerous:/dɛnˈtɪdʒərəs/
adjective
含牙的；生牙齿的
Containing teeth or having teeth erupt in unusual locations.
A dentigerous cyst is a fluid-filled sac that forms around the crown of an unerupted tooth.  含牙囊肿是一种在未萌出牙齿的牙冠周围形成的充满液体的囊肿。
The tumor was dentigerous, containing multiple small teeth.  这个肿瘤是含牙的，包含多个小牙齿。
Synonyms:  odontigenous
Antonyms:  edentulous
词性: adjective
