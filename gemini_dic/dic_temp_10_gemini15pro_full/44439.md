cross-refers:/ˌkrɒs rɪˈfɜːz/ (British) /ˌkrɔːs rɪˈfɜːrz/ (American)| v.| 交叉引用，相互参照|  To refer to another text or part of a text, especially one that provides further information or clarification.| This section cross-refers to Chapter 3 for a more detailed explanation. 本节交叉引用第三章以获得更详细的解释。| The documentation cross-refers to other relevant sections.  该文档交叉引用了其他相关章节。| synonyms:  refer, cite, link, connect| antonyms:  isolate, separate| verb
