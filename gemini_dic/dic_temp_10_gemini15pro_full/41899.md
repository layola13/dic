cosmetic:/kɒzˈmɛtɪk/ (UK) /kɑːzˈmɛtɪk/ (US)| n.|化妆品|A substance used to enhance or alter the appearance, especially of the face.|She uses a lot of cosmetics. 她使用很多化妆品。|近义词：makeup, toiletry|反义词: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cosmétique*, from Greek *kosmētikos*  "skilled in ordering or arranging," from *kosmos* "order, ornament, world."
