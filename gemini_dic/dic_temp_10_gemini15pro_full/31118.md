chanced:/tʃɑːnst/ /tʃænst/| v.|偶然发生，碰巧|To happen by chance; occur by accident.  It means something happened unexpectedly or without planning. | I chanced to meet an old friend in the street. 我碰巧在街上遇到了一位老朋友。| synonyms: happened, occurred, stumbled upon | antonyms: planned, intended, deliberate | verb

chanced:/tʃɑːnst/ /tʃænst/| v.|冒险|To take a risk; venture. It implies taking a chance on something uncertain with the possibility of failure. | He chanced his luck and invested all his money in the stock market.  他冒险把所有的钱都投资了股市。| synonyms: risked, gambled, ventured | antonyms: played safe, avoided risk | verb
