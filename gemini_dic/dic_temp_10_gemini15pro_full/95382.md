enchant:/ɪnˈtʃænt/ |ɪnˈtʃɑːnt/| vt.|使着迷，使陶醉|To fill (someone) with great delight; charm.|The magician enchanted the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法使观众着迷。|近义词:charm, captivate, fascinate, bewitch|反义词:disenchant, repel, disgust|verb

enchant:/ɪnˈtʃænt/ |ɪnˈtʃɑːnt/| adj.(古用法, 现已少用)|被施魔法的|placed under a spell by charms or incantations|The enchanted forest was full of magical creatures. 被施魔法的森林里充满了神奇的生物。|近义词:magical, charmed|反义词:mundane, ordinary|adjective
