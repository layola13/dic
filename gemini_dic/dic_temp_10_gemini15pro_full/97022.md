infecter:/ɪnˈfɛktər/ | n. | 传染者，感染者 | Someone or something that infects. |  The mosquito is the infecter of malaria.  蚊子是疟疾的传播者。| 近义词:carrier, transmitter, contaminant | 反义词: healer, purifier | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "infect," which comes from the Latin "inficere," meaning "to dip in, stain, taint, corrupt."  The suffix "-er" denotes the agent or one who performs the action.
