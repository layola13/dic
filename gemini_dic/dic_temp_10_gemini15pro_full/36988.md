columbarium:/kɒləmˈbɛːriəm/ /kəˈlʌmˌbɛriəm/ | n. |骨灰存放处；骨灰龛；灵堂|A structure, typically in a cemetery or church, with niches for storing urns containing cremated remains. |例句：The family visited the columbarium to pay their respects.  家人前往骨灰存放处表达敬意。

例句：The columbarium was a peaceful and serene place. 骨灰龛是一个宁静祥和的地方。|近义词：mausoleum, crypt, ossuary |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "cemetery" for burying whole bodies) |词性:noun
