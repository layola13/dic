cicerone: /ˌsɪsəˈroʊni/ (US), /ˌtʃɪtʃəˈrəʊni/ (UK)| n.|导游，向导；尤指对古迹或艺术品非常了解的讲解员|A guide, especially one who conducts tours and explains the history and details of ancient monuments, works of art, etc.|例句：Our cicerone at the museum was extremely knowledgeable. 我们博物馆的导游知识非常渊博。

例句：He acted as our cicerone during our trip to Rome. 在我们罗马之旅中，他担任了我们的导游。

近义词：guide, docent, escort, conductor|反义词:follower, tourist|词性:noun
