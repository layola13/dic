ETYMOLOGY->
From imperceptible + -ness.
Imperceptible: from im- (not) + perceptible (able to be perceived).
Perceptible: from Latin percept-, past participle stem of percipere (to perceive), from per- (thoroughly) + capere (to seize, take).
