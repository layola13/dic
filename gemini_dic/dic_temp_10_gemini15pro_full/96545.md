indoor:/ˈɪndɔːr/ (US); /ˈɪnˌdɔːr/ (UK)| adj.|室内的，户内的|Located, done, or used inside a building. |例句：Indoor sports are popular during the winter. 冬季室内运动很受欢迎。|近义词：inside, interior|反义词:outdoor, outside|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From in- + door.  The word "in" traces back to Proto-Germanic *in, and ultimately to Proto-Indo-European *en "in".  "Door" comes from Old English dor, from Proto-Germanic *duriz, meaning "door, gate," and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dhwer- "door, doorway."
