cyclometer:/saɪˈklɒmɪtər/ (UK), /saɪˈklɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |自行车里程表；车轮转数表|An instrument attached to a bicycle wheel that measures and records the distance travelled.|I checked my cyclometer after the ride and saw I'd cycled 50 miles. 我骑完后查看了我的里程表，发现我骑了50英里。|Synonyms: odometer, milometer|Antonyms: None|Noun
