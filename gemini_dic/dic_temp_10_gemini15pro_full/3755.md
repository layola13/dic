agistment:/əˈdʒɪstmənt/ | n. |寄养; 寄养费; 寄养地 |The act of agisting livestock; the fee charged for agisting; the pasture or other land on which livestock are agisted. | The agistment fee was reasonable, considering the quality of the pasture.  考虑到牧场的质量，寄养费是合理的。 | synonyms: pasturage, grazing | antonyms: confinement | noun
