collectedly:/kəˈlɛktɪdli/
adverb
共同地；集体地
in a collective manner; together.
The villagers collectedly decided to rebuild the school. 村民们共同决定重建学校。
The students collectedly submitted their petition. 学生们一起提交了请愿书。
近义词: jointly, together, unitedly, concertedly
反义词: individually, separately, singly
词性: 副词

词源:
From collect + -ed + -ly.  "Collect" comes from the Latin "colligere," meaning "to gather together."  The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle, and "-ly" transforms it into an adverb.
