outbulk:/ˌaʊtˈbʌlk/
verb
超过…的体积; 比…体积更大
to exceed in bulk; to be larger than in bulk
The new warehouse outbulks the old one by a significant margin.  新仓库的体积比旧仓库大很多。
The cruise ship outbulked all the other vessels in the harbor. 这艘游轮的体积比港口里的所有其他船只都大。
surpass, exceed
be smaller than, be dwarfed by
v.
