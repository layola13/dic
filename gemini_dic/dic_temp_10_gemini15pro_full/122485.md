moca:/ˈmoʊkə/ (US), /ˈmɒkə/ (UK)| n.|摩卡咖啡，摩卡壶|A type of coffee made with espresso, chocolate, and milk.  Also refers to a type of coffee pot.|例句：I ordered a moca latte this morning. 我今天早上点了一杯摩卡拿铁。

例句：She makes delicious moca in her new moca pot.  她用她的新摩卡壶煮了美味的摩卡咖啡。|近义词：latte, cappuccino|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps espresso if contrasting with the chocolate and milk additions)|词性:noun
