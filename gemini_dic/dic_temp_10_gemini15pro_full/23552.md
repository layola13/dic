brains: /breɪnz/
n.|大脑，智力，脑力|The center of the nervous system enclosed in the skull of vertebrates, controlling and coordinating the mental and physical actions. Also refers to intelligence or intellectual capacity. |He has got great brains. 他很有头脑。|Synonyms: intellect, mind, intelligence, wit, wisdom|Antonyms: stupidity, foolishness|noun


brains: /breɪnz/
n.| (复数) 脑袋，智囊|Intellectual ability; cleverness or intelligence. Often used in plural form to refer to a clever person or group of clever people.|The brains behind the operation was a young woman. 这次行动的幕后策划者是一位年轻女性。|Synonyms: intellect, mind, intelligence, wit|Antonyms: stupidity, foolishness|noun
