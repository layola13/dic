lighthouses:/ˈlaɪtˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
灯塔
A tower or other structure containing a beacon light to warn or guide ships at sea.
The lighthouse warned ships away from the dangerous rocks.  灯塔警告船只远离危险的岩石。

lighthouses:/ˈlaɪtˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
灯塔(复数)
Plural form of lighthouse.
There are several lighthouses along the coast.  沿着海岸线有几座灯塔。

近义词: beacon, pharos
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *lighthous*, from Old English *līhthūs* ("lighthouse"), equivalent to *līht* ("light") + *hūs* ("house").
