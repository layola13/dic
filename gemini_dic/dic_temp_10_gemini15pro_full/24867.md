browsable:/ˈbraʊzəbl/| adj.|可浏览的，可随意翻阅的|Suitable or intended for browsing; easily browsed.  Able to be looked through or examined, typically in a casual or leisurely way.|The online archive is browsable by keyword.  这个在线档案馆可以通过关键词浏览。|近义词：searchable, accessible, navigable|反义词：unsearchable, inaccessible, restricted|词性:形容词

Etymology: From "browse" + "-able."  "Browse" comes from Old French *broster (to sprout, put forth shoots), from which the sense of animals feeding on young shoots developed.  The modern sense of casually looking through something arose in the 19th century.
