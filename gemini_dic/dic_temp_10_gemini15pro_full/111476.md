loader:/ˈloʊdər/ (US) /ˈləʊdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|装载机；装填手；加载程序|A machine or person that loads something, especially a large machine used for loading earth or other materials into a truck, or a program that loads data or software into a computer.|The farmer used a front-end loader to move the hay bales. 农民用前端装载机搬运干草捆。|Synonyms: excavator, digger, shovel, crane,  program, application |Antonyms: unloader, downloader|noun
