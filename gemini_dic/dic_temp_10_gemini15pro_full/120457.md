minored:/ˈmaɪnərd/ | v. |辅修；次要|To study a subject as a secondary area of specialization, in addition to a major subject. | She majored in Biology and minored in Chemistry. 她主修生物，辅修化学。| I minored in history while I was getting my degree in English. 我在获得英语学位的同时辅修了历史。 | Near synonyms: dabbled in, took a few courses in | Near antonyms: majored in, specialized in | verb
