convenient:/kənˈviːniənt/ /kənˈvinjənt/| adj.|方便的，便利的|Suitable for one's needs; making life easier or more comfortable.|The store is conveniently located near my house.  这家商店位置便利，靠近我家。|Synonyms: easy, handy, suitable, accessible, opportune|Antonyms: inconvenient, awkward, troublesome, difficult|adjective
