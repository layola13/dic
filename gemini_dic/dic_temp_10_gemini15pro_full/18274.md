belittlingly:/bɪˈlɪtlɪŋli/
adverb
轻蔑地，贬低地
in a way that makes someone or something seem unimportant
He belittlingly referred to his colleague's idea as "childish." 他轻蔑地称同事的想法为“幼稚”。
She belittlingly dismissed my concerns. 她轻蔑地驳回了我的担忧。

近义词:contemptuously, disdainfully, scornfully
反义词:respectfully, admiringly
词性:adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From belittle + -ing + -ly.  Belittle itself comes from be- (making a verb) + little.
