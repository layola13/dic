overslavish:/ˌoʊvərˈsleɪvɪʃ/
adjective
过度奴性的，过分顺从的
Excessively submissive or devoted; too eager to please or obey.
She was criticized for her overslavish devotion to her boss. 她因对老板过分顺从而受到批评。
He offered an overslavish imitation of the master's style. 他对大师的风格进行了过分模仿。
synonyms: subservient, obsequious, fawning, servile, sycophantic
antonyms: independent, assertive, rebellious, defiant
词性: adjective
