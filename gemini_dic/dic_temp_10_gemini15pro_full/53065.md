n.
界定；限定；区分；区别
The act or process of defining or distinguishing. The act of marking out boundaries or limits.
Diorism is essential in scientific classification, allowing us to differentiate between species. 界定在科学分类中至关重要，使我们能够区分不同物种。
Precise diorism is crucial for effective legal arguments.  精确的界定对于有效的法律论证至关重要。
近义词：definition, delimitation, distinction, differentiation
反义词：confusion, ambiguity, vagueness
词性: noun

词源: From Greek *διορίζειν (dihorízein, “to define, delimit”), from *ὅρος (hóros, “boundary, limit”).
