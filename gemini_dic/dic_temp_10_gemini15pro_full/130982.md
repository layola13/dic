nonathletic:/ˌnɒnæθˈlɛtɪk/
adjective
不擅长运动的；非运动型的
Not good at or interested in sports; not related to athletics.
He's always been a bit nonathletic. 他一直都不太擅长运动。
近义词:unathletic, unsporting, inactive
反义词:athletic, sporty, active
词性:adjective
