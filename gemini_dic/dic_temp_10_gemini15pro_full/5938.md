ambling:/ˈæmblɪŋ/ (US), /ˈæmblɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. vi.|漫步的，缓行的；闲逛|walking or moving in a slow, relaxed way|例句：He was ambling along the beach. 他在沙滩上漫步。|例句：The river ambles through the valley. 这条河缓缓流过山谷。|近义词：strolling, sauntering, meandering|反义词：rushing, hurrying, sprinting|词性: adjective, verb (present participle)
Etymology: From Middle English *amblen, from Old French *ambler, from Latin *ambulare (“to walk”), frequentative of ambire (“to go around”).
