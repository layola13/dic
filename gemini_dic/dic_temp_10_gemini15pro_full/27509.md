camphine:/ˈkæmˌfaɪn/| n.|莰芬, 松节油精|A volatile, flammable liquid hydrocarbon mixture, C10H16, obtained from turpentine and formerly used as a fuel in lamps and as a solvent.|The old lamp used camphine as fuel.|近义词:turpentine, oil of turpentine|反义词: None|noun
