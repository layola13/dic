overtilt:/ˈoʊvərˌtɪlt/ | v. | 过度倾斜，过度倾覆 | To tilt or cause to tilt too far. | The helicopter overtilted and crashed. 直升机过度倾斜坠毁了。| The strong winds overtilted the sailboat. 强风使帆船过度倾斜。| 近义词: capsize, overturn, tip over | 反义词: right, stabilize | 词性: 动词

overtilt:/ˈoʊvərˌtɪlt/ | n. | 过度倾斜，过度倾覆 | The state of being tilted too far. | The overtilt of the truck caused the cargo to spill. 卡车的过度倾斜导致货物洒落。|  The overtilt of the tree made it vulnerable to strong winds.  树木的过度倾斜使其容易受到强风的影响。| 近义词:  capsizing, overturning | 反义词: stability, uprightness | 词性: 名词
