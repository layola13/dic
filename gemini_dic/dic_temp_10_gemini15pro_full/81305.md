granulose:/ˈɡrænjuˌloʊs/| adj.|颗粒状的，粒状的| Composed of or appearing to be made up of granules; granular.|The granulose layer of the ovary contains developing follicles. 卵巢的颗粒层包含正在发育的卵泡。|近义词：granular, grainy|反义词:smooth, homogeneous|词性:形容词
granulosa:/ˌɡrænjuˈloʊsə/| n.|颗粒层|The granulosa layer surrounds the oocyte within the ovarian follicle.|The granulosa cells play a crucial role in follicle development. 颗粒细胞在卵泡发育中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：stratum granulosum|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *grānulōsus*, from grānulum (“little grain”) + -ōsus.
