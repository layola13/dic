oxidizable:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzəbl/ (US) /ˈɒksɪdaɪzəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可氧化的，易氧化的|Susceptible to oxidation; able to be oxidized.|例句：Iron is an oxidizable metal. 铁是一种易氧化的金属。|近义词：oxidative, combustible, flammable|反义词：non-oxidizable, inert, stable|词性:形容词
oxidize:/ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (US) /ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (UK)| v.|氧化，生锈|To combine or become combined chemically with oxygen.|例句：The metal oxidized, forming a layer of rust. 金属氧化，形成一层锈。|近义词：rust, corrode, tarnish|反义词：reduce|词性:动词
oxidation:/ˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃn/ (US) /ˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|氧化，生锈|The process or result of oxidizing or being oxidized.|例句：The oxidation of iron produces rust. 铁的氧化产生锈。|近义词：rusting, corrosion|反义词：reduction|词性:名词
