brontolite:/ˈbrɒntəˌlaɪt/| n. | 雷电矿石，闪电熔岩 |A fulgurite, a glassy rock formed by lightning striking sand or rock.|The brontolite displayed a branching, tubular structure, evidence of the lightning's path through the sand.  这块雷电矿石呈现出分枝的管状结构，证明了闪电穿过沙子的路径。|近义词：fulgurite|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *brontē* (thunder) + *-lite* (stone, mineral).
