equitant:/ˈɛkwɪtənt/
adjective
重叠的，套叠的；骑马式的
Overlapping in such a way as to resemble a rider on horseback; especially designating leaves or bracts so arranged that the bases of the outer enclose those of the inner.
The plant has equitant leaves. 这种植物的叶子是套叠的。
近义词: overlapping, imbricate, stacked
反义词: separate, disjunct
词性: adjective

equitant:/ˈɛkwɪtənt/
adjective
骑马的
Riding on horseback.
The equitant figure in the tapestry is thought to be a king. 挂毯上的骑马人物被认为是一位国王。
近义词: mounted, riding
反义词: dismounted, walking
词性: adjective
