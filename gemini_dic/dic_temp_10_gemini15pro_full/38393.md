conceptualities:/kənˌsɛptʃuˈælɪtiz/ | n. | 概念性，概念框架 |  A complex of abstract thoughts; general notions.  It often refers to the collection or system of concepts related to a particular subject or field. | The artist explored complex conceptualities of time and space in their work.  这位艺术家在他们的作品中探索了时间和空间的复杂概念。| synonyms: concepts, notions, ideas, abstractions, frameworks, theories | antonyms: concretes, specifics, particulars, realities | noun

conceptuality:/kənˌsɛptʃuˈælɪti/ | n. | 概念性 | The state or quality of being conceptual; abstractness. | The conceptuality of the project made it difficult to grasp initially. 这个项目的概念性使得它一开始很难理解。| synonyms: abstractness, generality | antonyms: concreteness, specificity | noun
