criticize: /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/ (US) /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/ (UK)| v. |批评，批判|To express disapproval of someone or something, to find fault with.|He criticized her handling of the situation. 他批评了她对局势的处理。|Synonyms: condemn, censure, denounce, reprehend, reprove|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, extol|verb
