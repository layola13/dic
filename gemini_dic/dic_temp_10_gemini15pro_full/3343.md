affray:/əˈfreɪ/| n.| 争吵，斗殴；骚乱|a noisy, public fight or quarrel|Several people were injured in the affray.  有几个人在骚乱中受伤。|Synonyms: brawl, fight, fray, melee, rumpus, scrap, scuffle |Antonyms: peace, calm, tranquility|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esfreer "to frighten, break up," from Latin ex- "out" + fragor "crash, din." The meaning shifted from "fright" to "public fight or noisy disturbance."
