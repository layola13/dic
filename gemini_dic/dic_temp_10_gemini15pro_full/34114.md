clachan:/ˈklækən/ | n. |小村庄，小村落|A small village, especially a Highland village.|例句：The clachan nestled snugly in the valley. 小村庄依偎在山谷里。|例句：We visited a remote clachan in the Scottish Highlands. 我们参观了苏格兰高地一个偏僻的小村庄。|近义词：village, hamlet, settlement|反义词：city, metropolis|词性:名词

Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic *clachan*, from Old Irish *clachán* (“group of stones, monastic settlement”), diminutive of *cloch* (“stone”). Related to Welsh *clachan*.
