brachytypous:/brækɪˈtaɪpəs/
adjective
短头型的，短吻的
Having a short or broad head, especially with a short facial region.
Brachytypous breeds of dogs, like pugs and bulldogs, are prone to breathing problems. 短头型犬种，如哈巴狗和斗牛犬，容易出现呼吸问题。
short-headed, short-faced, short-nosed
long-headed, long-nosed, dolichocephalic
