objectors:/əbˈdʒɛktərz/
n.
反对者；抗议者；异议者
A person who expresses or feels disapproval or opposition to something.
例句：The objectors to the new highway plan voiced their concerns at the town meeting.  新的公路计划的反对者在镇民大会上表达了他们的担忧。

例句：Several objectors raised their hands in protest against the proposed development.  一些反对者举手抗议拟议中的开发项目。

近义词：protestors, dissenters, opponents
反义词：supporters, proponents, advocates
词性：名词(plural)

objector:/əbˈdʒɛktər/
n.
反对者；抗议者；异议者
A person who expresses or feels disapproval or opposition to something.
例句：He was a conscientious objector during the war.  在战争期间，他是一个出于良心拒服兵役者。

例句：The objector to the marriage filed a legal challenge.  对这桩婚姻的反对者提出了法律挑战。


近义词：protestor, dissenter, opponent
反义词：supporter, proponent, advocate
词性：名词(singular)
