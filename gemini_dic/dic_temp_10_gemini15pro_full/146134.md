overemploying: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/
verb
过度雇佣，雇佣过多员工
Employing more workers than is necessary or efficient.
The company is overemploying staff and needs to downsize.  这家公司雇佣的员工过多，需要裁员。
The factory was overemploying workers, leading to decreased productivity per employee.  这家工厂过度雇佣工人，导致每位员工的生产力下降。

Synonyms: overstaffing, overmanning
Antonyms: understaffing, short-staffing
词性: 动词 (Verb)
