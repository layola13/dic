cwm:/kʊm/ | n. | 环状陡壁，冰斗 | A steep-walled hollow in a mountain, especially one formed by glacial erosion. |  The hikers reached a cwm nestled high in the mountains.  登山者到达了高山上一个环状陡壁。| synonym: cirque, corrie | antonym: peak, summit | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Welsh *cwm*, meaning "valley" or "hollow."  Related to the Breton word *komm*.
