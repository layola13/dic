glandless:/ˈɡlændləs/
adjective
无腺的; 无油腺的
lacking glands, especially oil glands.
Glandless cotton is preferred because its seeds can be used for food.  无腺棉花是首选，因为它的种子可以用来做食物。
seedless, gland-free
glandular
adjective
