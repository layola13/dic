glaciation:/ˌɡleɪsiˈeɪʃən/| n. |冰川作用；冰河作用；冰川期|The formation, movement, and recession of glaciers.  The process, condition, or result of being covered by glaciers or ice sheets.|例句：The glaciation of the valley carved out a deep fjord.  山谷的冰川作用形成了一个深邃的峡湾。

例句：The last glaciation ended about 10,000 years ago.  上一次冰河期大约在一万年前结束。|近义词：glacial period, ice age|反义词:interglacial|词性:noun
