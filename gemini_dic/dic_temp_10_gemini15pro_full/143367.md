outfawn:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːn/
verb
谄媚；拍马屁；超过…的谄媚
To surpass someone in flattery or fawning behavior.
He outfawned all the other courtiers to gain the king's favor. 他比其他朝臣更会拍马屁，以获得国王的青睐。
近义词:outflatter, outdo
反义词:criticize, condemn
词性:verb

outfawn:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːn/
动词
谄媚；拍马屁；超过…的谄媚
To surpass someone in flattery or fawning behavior.

近义词:kowtow to, grovel before
反义词:disrespect, defy
