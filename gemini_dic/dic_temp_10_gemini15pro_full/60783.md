electromechanical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊməˈkænɪkəl/ | adj. | 机电的；电动机械的 | Relating to or operated by both electricity and mechanics. |  The car's electromechanical steering system failed, causing the accident. 汽车的机电转向系统失灵，导致了事故。 | The electromechanical brake is more efficient than traditional hydraulic brakes.  机电刹车比传统的液压刹车更高效。 | 近义词: electro-mechanical, mechatronic | 反义词: manual, mechanical | 词性: 形容词

词源: electro- (electric 电的) + mechanical (机械的)
