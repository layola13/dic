extends:/ɪkˈstɛndz/| v.|延伸，延长，扩展，提供，给予|The garden extends to the river.  花园一直延伸到河边。|近义词：stretch, lengthen, expand, offer, give|反义词: shorten, contract, limit, withhold|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *estendre*, from Latin extendere (to stretch out, spread out), from ex- (out) + tendere (to stretch).
