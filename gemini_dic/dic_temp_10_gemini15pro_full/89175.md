homeopathy:/ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθi/ | n. |顺势疗法|A system of treating diseases using very small amounts of substances that, in a healthy person, would produce symptoms like those of the disease. |例句：She opted for homeopathy to treat her allergies. 她选择顺势疗法来治疗她的过敏。

例句：Homeopathy is considered by many to be pseudoscience. 许多人认为顺势疗法是伪科学。|近义词：alternative medicine, naturopathy |反义词:conventional medicine, allopathy |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek words "homoios" (similar) and "pathos" (suffering or disease).
