hyposulphurous:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈsʌlfərəs/| adj.|次硫酸的；含低价硫的|Of or relating to hyposulphurous acid; containing sulphur at a lower oxidation state than in sulphurous compounds.|Hyposulphurous acid is a very unstable compound. 次硫酸是一种非常不稳定的化合物。|Synonym:dithionous|Antonym:persulfuric|adjective
