auto-suggestive:/ˌɔːtəʊ səˈdʒestɪv/| adj.|自我暗示的|Influencing or tending to influence one's own attitudes or behavior by other than conscious methods; based on or using suggestion. |例句：Auto-suggestive techniques can be helpful for improving self-confidence. 自我暗示技巧有助于提高自信心。

例句：The therapist used auto-suggestive therapy to help the patient overcome their fear of flying.  治疗师使用自我暗示疗法帮助病人克服飞行恐惧。|近义词：self-hypnotic, autosuggestion|反义词：N/A|词性：adjective
