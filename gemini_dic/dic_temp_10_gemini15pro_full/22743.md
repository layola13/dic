borosilicate:/ˌbɔːroʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
adjective|硼硅酸盐的|Made of a type of glass that contains boric oxide and silica, making it resistant to heat and chemical changes.|例句：Borosilicate glass is commonly used in laboratory equipment. 硼硅酸盐玻璃常用于实验室设备。|近义词: heat-resistant glass, Pyrex (trademark)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soda-lime glass" as a contrasting type)|词性:adjective
borosilicate:/ˌbɔːroʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
noun|硼硅酸盐玻璃|A type of glass containing boric oxide and silica, known for its durability and resistance to thermal shock.|例句：This beaker is made of borosilicate. 这个烧杯是由硼硅酸盐玻璃制成的。|近义词: heat-resistant glass, Pyrex (trademark)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soda-lime glass" as a contrasting type)|词性:noun
