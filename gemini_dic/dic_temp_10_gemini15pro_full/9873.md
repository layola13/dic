apar:/əˈpɑːr/ | adj. | 相隔的，分开的，不同的 | Apart from a few scratches, the car was undamaged. 除了几处划痕外，汽车没有损坏。 | The twins are so alike it's hard to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎长得太像了，很难区分他们。|  separate, distinct, different | together, joined, connected | adjective

Etymology->
From Old French *apart, from Latin *apartem, accusative of *apars, from ab "from, away from" + pars "part".
