overblow:/ˌoʊvərˈbloʊ/| v. |夸大；过度吹嘘；（管乐器）用力过度吹奏|To exaggerate or inflate the importance of something; to blow (a wind instrument) too forcefully.| The media overblew the significance of the incident. 媒体夸大了事件的重要性。|He overblew the trumpet, producing a harsh sound. 他用力过度吹奏小号，发出了刺耳的声音。|近义词：exaggerate, overstate, inflate, overemphasize|反义词: understate, downplay|动词


overblow:/ˈoʊvərbloʊ/| n. |（管乐器）用力过度吹奏；过度；夸大|The act of overblowing a wind instrument or exaggerating something.| The overblow caused the flute to squeak. 用力过度吹奏导致长笛发出吱吱声。|The overblow of the economic crisis led to panic. 对经济危机的夸大导致了恐慌。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement|反义词: understatement|名词
