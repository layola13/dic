depressurizer:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzər/ | n. | 减压器，卸压装置 | A device used to reduce pressure, typically in a pressurized system or environment.  | The pilot activated the cabin depressurizer before landing. 飞行员在着陆前启动了机舱减压器。|  The engineers checked the depressurizer to ensure it was functioning properly. 工程师检查了减压器以确保其正常工作。 | synonyms: pressure reducer, pressure relief valve | antonyms: pressurizer | noun
