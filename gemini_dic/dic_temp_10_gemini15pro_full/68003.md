factitiousness:/ˌfæktɪˈʃəsnəs/
n.
人为性; 不自然; 虚假
the quality of being produced artificially rather than by natural forces.
Her outburst of grief seemed a bit factitious. 她突然爆发的悲痛显得有些不自然。
Synonyms: artificiality, unnaturalness, spuriousness, contrivedness, feigning, pretense
Antonyms: genuineness, authenticity, naturalness, sincerity
noun


factitious:/fækˈtɪʃəs/
adj.
人为的; 不自然的; 虚假的
artificially created or developed.
a factitious demand for the product. 对该产品人为制造的需求。
Synonyms: artificial, unnatural, spurious, contrived, feigned, pretended
Antonyms: genuine, authentic, natural, sincere
adjective
