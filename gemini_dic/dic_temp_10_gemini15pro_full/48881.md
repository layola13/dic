defragmenter:/ˌdiːfræɡˈmɛntər/ |n.|碎片整理程序；磁盘碎片整理程序|A utility that reorganizes files on a disk drive to improve performance. |I ran the disk defragmenter to speed up my computer. 我运行了磁盘碎片整理程序来加快我的电脑速度。|Synonyms: optimizer, defragger|Antonyms: fragmenter (less common)|noun


defragment:/ˌdiːfræɡˈmɛnt/ |v.|整理碎片；对（磁盘）进行碎片整理|To reorganize files on a disk drive so that each file occupies a contiguous area of storage. |I need to defragment my hard drive. 我需要整理我的硬盘碎片。|Synonyms: optimize, consolidate|Antonyms: fragment|verb
