paralogical:/ˌpærəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 似是而非的，貌似合理的 | Resembling or simulating logical reasoning but not actually logical. Appearing superficially logical or reasonable, but based on a flawed premise or containing internal contradictions.  |  His argument seemed paralogical, convincing at first but ultimately flawed.  他的论证看似合理，起初令人信服，但最终却有缺陷。

His paralogical statements made it difficult to understand his point. 他似是而非的陈述使得他的观点难以理解。 | 近义词：fallacious, specious, sophistical | 反义词：logical, rational, sound | 词性: adjective
