mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/ (US) /ˈmɔːtɪs/ (UK)| v. n. adj.|榫眼，榫接；固定；接合|A hole or recess cut into a piece of timber or other material into which a tenon is inserted to form a joint.|The carpenter carefully mortised the two pieces of wood together. 木匠小心地将两块木头榫接在一起。|近义词：joint, connect, fasten, secure|反义词：separate, disconnect, loosen|词性: verb, noun, adjective

mortised:/ˈmɔːrtɪst/ (US) /ˈmɔːtɪst/ (UK)|adj.|榫接的，有榫眼的|Joined by a mortise and tenon.|The mortised joint was strong and secure.  这个榫接的接头很牢固。|近义词：joined, connected, fastened|反义词：separated, disconnected|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mortoise "notch, hole," from Late Latin *mortacium, of uncertain origin.
