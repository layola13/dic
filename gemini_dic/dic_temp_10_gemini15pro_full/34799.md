climb-the-walls:/klaɪm ðə wɔːlz/ | idiom | 焦躁不安，极度烦躁 | To be extremely restless, anxious, or frustrated. |  I've been waiting for hours, I'm climbing the walls! 我已经等了好几个小时了，我都快疯了！

| 近义词: go crazy, lose one's mind, be frantic, be restless | 反义词: be calm, be patient, be relaxed | 词性: idiom


She was climbing the walls with worry when her son didn't come home on time. 当她的儿子没有按时回家时，她急得像热锅上的蚂蚁。
