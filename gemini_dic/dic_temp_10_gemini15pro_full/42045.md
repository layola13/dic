costumier:/ˈkɒstjuːmər (UK), kɑːˈstuːmər (US)| n.|服装设计师，戏服设计员|A person or company that makes or supplies theatrical costumes.|The costumier created stunning dresses for the lead actress.  这位服装设计师为女主角设计了惊艳的礼服。|Synonyms: costume designer, wardrobe master, outfitter|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps) plainclothes supplier, everyday clothing provider|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "costumier," ultimately from Italian "costume" (custom, habit, dress), from Latin "consuetudo" (custom).
