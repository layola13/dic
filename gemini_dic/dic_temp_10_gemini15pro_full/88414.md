hirundo:/hɪˈrʌndoʊ/ /haɪˈrʌndoʊ/ | n. |燕子；燕属|A swallow (bird of the Hirundo genus).|例句：The hirundo built a nest under the eaves. 燕子在屋檐下筑了个巢。

例句：Several species of hirundo migrate south for the winter.  几种燕子会南迁过冬。|近义词：swallow|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *hirundō*, from Proto-Italic *swirundō*, from Proto-Indo-European *swén-/*swón- (“to sound, to ring”). Cognate with Sanskrit स्वनति (svánati, “it sounds”).
