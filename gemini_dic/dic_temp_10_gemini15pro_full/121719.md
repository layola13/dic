misproceeding: /ˌmɪsprəˈsiːdɪŋ/
noun
错误的程序；不当行为
an act of proceeding wrongly or improperly, especially in a legal context.
The lawyer argued that the judge's misproceeding had prejudiced the jury.  律师辩称法官的不当行为使陪审团产生了偏见。
Near Synonyms:  misconduct, malpractice, wrongdoing, impropriety
Antonyms:  correct procedure, proper conduct
词性: 名词
