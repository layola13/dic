cockatoo:/ˌkɒkəˈtuː/ (UK), /ˌkɑːkəˈtuː/ (US)| n.|凤头鹦鹉|A parrot with a large, brightly coloured crest and curved beak, native to Australasia.|例句：The cockatoo squawked loudly. 凤头鹦鹉大声地叫着。

例句：We saw a flock of cockatoos in the rainforest. 我们在雨林中看到了一群凤头鹦鹉。|近义词：parrot|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
