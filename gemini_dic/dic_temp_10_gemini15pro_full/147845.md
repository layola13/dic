overqualifies:/ˌoʊvərˈkwɑːlɪfaɪz/
v. | 使资历过高；使学历过高 | to have more qualifications than are needed for a particular job |  He overqualifies for this position, having a PhD when only a bachelor's degree is required. 他资历过高，这个职位只需要学士学位，而他拥有博士学位。 | overqualified,  exceeds the requirements | underqualified, lacks the requirements | verb


overqualified:/ˌoʊvərˈkwɑːlɪfaɪd/
adj. |资历过高的；学历过高的| having more qualifications than are necessary for a particular job | She felt overqualified for the administrative assistant position. 她觉得担任行政助理的职位大材小用。| excessively qualified, overly qualified | underqualified, unqualified | adjective
