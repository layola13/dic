goniatite:/ˈɡoʊniəˌtaɪt/| n. | 戈尼亞特菊石 | An extinct marine mollusk of the order Goniatitida, related to the ammonites, having a shell with simple, angular suture lines. | The museum displayed a well-preserved goniatite fossil from the Devonian period.  博物馆展出了一块保存完好的泥盆纪戈尼亞特菊石化石。| Synonym: ammonoid | Antonym: None | noun
