chiaro:/ˈkjaro/ (Italian)| adj.|清晰的，明亮的，浅色的|Clear, bright, light (in color).  It can refer to clarity of sound, light, or understanding, as well as to a light or pale color.|The soprano had a chiaro and resonant voice. 女高音的声音清晰而洪亮。|Synonyms: limpido, chiaro, luminoso, sereno,  (depending on context)|Antonyms: scuro, opaco, confuso|adjective

chiaro:/ˈkjaro/ (Italian)| adv.|clearly, brightly|Clearly, brightly.  Used to modify verbs, indicating a clear or bright manner.|She sang chiaro and beautifully. 她唱得清晰而优美。|Synonyms: chiaramente, limpidamente|Antonyms: oscuramente, indistintamente|adverb
