antiphonic:/ˌæn.tɪˈfɒn.ɪk/ | adj. | 交互唱和的，轮唱的 |  Of or relating to antiphony; performed alternately by two groups, especially of singers. | The choir sang an antiphonic anthem.  唱诗班演唱了一首轮唱的圣歌。 | synonyms: responsive, alternating, reciprocal | antonyms: unison, monophonic | adjective


antiphony:/ænˈtɪf.ə.ni/ | n. | 交互轮唱，轮流吟诵 | Music or verses performed alternately by two groups of singers or instruments.  |  The antiphony of the two choirs created a beautiful and moving effect.  两个唱诗班的轮唱营造了一种美丽动人的效果。 | synonyms: response, alternation | antonyms: unison, monologue | noun
