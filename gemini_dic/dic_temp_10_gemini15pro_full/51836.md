dewretting:/diːˈrɛtɪŋ/
n.
沤麻；浸渍
The process of separating the bast fibers of flax, hemp, jute, or ramie from the woody tissue of the stem by partial rotting.
Dewretting is a crucial step in linen production.  沤麻是亚麻生产的关键步骤。
The farmer dewretted the flax stalks in the pond.  农民在池塘里沤麻杆。
retting, decortication
