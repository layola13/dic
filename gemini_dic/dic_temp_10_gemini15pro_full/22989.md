boudoirs:/ˈbuːdwɑːrz/ (US), /ˈbuːdwɑːz/ (UK)| n. |闺房，化妆室|A woman's private sitting room or bedroom, often elegantly furnished.|She retreated to her boudoir to read and relax. 她回到自己的闺房读书放松。|Example:  The actress prepared for her performance in her opulent boudoir.  这位女演员在她华丽的闺房里为表演做准备。|Synonyms: bedroom, dressing room, private room|Antonyms: public room, living room|Noun
