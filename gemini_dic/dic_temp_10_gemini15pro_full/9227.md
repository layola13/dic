antiparallels:/ˌæntiˈpærəˌlelz/ | n. |反平行线，反平行边| Lines or sides that are opposite but not parallel, often referring to geometric figures where corresponding angles are equal but the lines/sides themselves are not parallel. | The antiparallels of a cyclic quadrilateral are equal.  一个圆内接四边形的反平行边相等。|

antiparallel:/ˌæntiˈpærəˌlel/ | adj. |反平行的|  Not parallel but having corresponding angles equal, often used in geometry to describe lines or sides of a figure. |The antiparallel sides of the trapezoid were equal in length.  梯形的反平行边长度相等。|近义词: opposite |反义词: parallel |词性: 形词
