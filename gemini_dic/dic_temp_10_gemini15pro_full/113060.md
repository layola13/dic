lumpiest:/ˈlʌmpiɪst/
adjective
最块状的；最疙瘩的
superlative adjective of lumpy: having or forming lumps.
The lumpiest mashed potatoes I've ever seen. 我见过最块状的土豆泥。
The track was the lumpiest and most uneven I’d ever ridden on.  这条赛道是我骑过的最颠簸不平的。
近义词:  bumpy, knobbly, uneven
反义词: smooth, even
词性: adjective
