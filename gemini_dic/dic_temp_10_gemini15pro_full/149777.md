palaver:/pəˈlævə(r)/ /pəˈlɑːvər/ | n. vi. | 闲谈，空谈； palabrería, cháchara | prolonged and often tedious fuss or discussion |  After much palaver, they finally agreed on a price. 经过一番冗长的讨价还价，他们终于商定了一个价格。 | synonyms: discussion, talk, chat, conference, negotiation | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun
palaver:/pəˈlævə(r)/ /pəˈlɑːvər/ | vi. | 闲聊，空谈 | to talk unnecessarily at length | They spent hours palavering about nothing important. 他们花了几个小时闲聊一些无关紧要的事情。 | synonyms: chatter, gossip, prattle | antonyms: be silent, listen | verb
