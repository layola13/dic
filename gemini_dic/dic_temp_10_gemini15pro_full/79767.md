glutens:/ˈɡluːtənz/| n. |谷蛋白；麸质|A mixture of proteins found in wheat and related grains, especially wheat, rye, and barley. It gives elasticity to dough, helping it rise and keep its shape, and often gives the final product a chewy texture.|例句：People with celiac disease must avoid glutens. 乳糜泻患者必须避免麸质。

例句：The bread is made with a mixture of glutens. 这种面包是用谷蛋白混合物制作的。|近义词：gluten|反义词: None|词性: Noun
