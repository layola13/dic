copious:/ˈkoʊpiəs/| adj.|丰富的，大量的，多产的|existing or being produced in large quantities; abundant.|例句：The storm produced a copious amount of rain. 这场暴风雨带来了大量的雨水。|近义词：abundant, plentiful, ample, profuse|反义词:scarce, meager, scant|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Latin copiosus, from copia ‘plenty’.
