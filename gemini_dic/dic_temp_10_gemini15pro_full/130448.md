nonabsolutistically:/ˌnɒnˌæbsəluːˈtɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 非绝对地 | In a way that is not absolute; without asserting or implying the existence of absolutes or universal truths. |  He approached the issue nonabsolutistically, considering all perspectives. 他以非绝对的方式处理这个问题，考虑了所有观点。 | She interpreted the rules nonabsolutistically, understanding that there could be exceptions. 她对规则的解释是非绝对的，理解可能存在例外。 | relativistically, contextually | absolutely | adverb
