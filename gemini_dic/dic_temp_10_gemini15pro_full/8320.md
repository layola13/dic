anti-parliamentary:/ˌæntiˌpɑːrləˈmentri/ |adj.|反议会的，妨碍议会工作的|Opposing or hindering the proper functioning of a parliament or legislative body.  It describes actions or language deemed disrespectful or disruptive to the established rules and procedures of parliamentary debate. |The speaker ruled her remarks as anti-parliamentary and demanded she withdraw them.  议长裁定她的言论妨碍议会工作，要求她收回。

The opposition leader was ejected from the chamber for using anti-parliamentary language. 反对党领袖因使用妨碍议会工作的语言而被逐出会议厅。 |Synonyms:  unparliamentary, obstructive, disruptive| Antonyms:  parliamentary, respectful, compliant|adjective
