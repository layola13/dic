incoming:/ˈɪnkʌmɪŋ/| adj. n.|到来的，传入的；收入|Something that is incoming is arriving or coming in.|例句：Incoming flights are delayed due to bad weather. 由于恶劣天气，入境航班延误。|近义词：arriving, approaching, entering|反义词:outgoing, departing|词性:形容词

incoming:/ˈɪnkʌmɪŋ/| n.|收入，收益|Money that is earned from business activities or investments.|例句：The company's incoming has increased by 20% this year. 公司的收入今年增加了20%。|近义词：revenue, earnings, income|反义词:outgoing, expenditure|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in + coming, present participle of come.
