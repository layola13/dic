action-packed:/ˈækʃənˌpækt/| adj.|充满动作的，惊险刺激的|Full of action or excitement.|We watched an action-packed movie last night. 我们昨晚看了一部充满动作的电影。
The weekend was action-packed, with hiking, camping, and kayaking. 这个周末充满了活动，有徒步旅行、露营和皮划艇。|近义词：exciting, thrilling, adventurous|反义词：boring, dull, uneventful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
action (n.) + packed (adj.)

action (n.) from Old French *acion "action, process, battle," from Latin actionem (nominative actio) "a doing, a driving, a performance; a process at law," from past participle stem of agere "to drive, lead, act,"  from PIE root *ag- "to drive, draw out or forth, move."

packed (adj.)  past participle of pack (v.) from Middle Dutch or Middle Low German pakken "to pack, press tightly," probably of imitative origin.


USAGE->
She planned an action-packed itinerary for the trip. 她为这次旅行计划了一个充满活动的行程。
The action-packed game kept the audience on the edge of their seats.  这场充满动作的比赛让观众一直处于紧张状态。
