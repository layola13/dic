enshrine:/ɪnˈʃraɪn/ | vt. | 珍藏；铭记；把…奉为神圣 | To enclose in or as if in a shrine; cherish as sacred. | The Constitution enshrines the basic rights of all citizens. 宪法 enshrines 所有公民的基本权利。

The document enshrined the rights of the individual.  这份文件 enshrines 个人的权利。

| 近义词：cherish, preserve, sanctify, consecrate | 反义词：desecrate, profane, violate | 词性: verb (动词)
