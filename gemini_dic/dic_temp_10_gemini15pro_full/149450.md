paddies:/ˈpædiz/| n.|稻田；(尤指东南亚的)种植水稻的农民|A small field where rice is grown, especially in southeast Asia. Also, (often offensive) a person, especially a man, from southeast Asia.|The rice paddies stretched as far as the eye could see. 远处，稻田一望无际。|Synonyms: rice fields, rice paddies|Antonyms: (for rice field meaning) dry fields, uplands|(for person meaning:  no direct antonym, avoid using this sense due to offensive potential)
paddies:/ˈpædiz/| n.|稻田；(尤指东南亚的)种植水稻的农民|Many paddies work long hours in the fields. 许多农民在田里长时间工作。(This usage can be offensive, try to avoid it.)|Synonyms: rice farmers, cultivators|Antonyms: (for farmer meaning) landowners, landlords|(for person meaning: no direct antonym, avoid using this sense due to offensive potential)
