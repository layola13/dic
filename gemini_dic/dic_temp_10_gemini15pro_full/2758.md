adulthood: /ˈædʌlthʊd/ (US) /ˈædʌlthʊd/ (UK)| n.|成年; 成年期|The period of time in a person's life after they have become an adult.|She reached adulthood at 18. 她18岁成年。
Responsibilities come with adulthood. 成年后要承担责任。
|近义词:maturity, majority|反义词:childhood, infancy, adolescence|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ēaldhād (unattested), from ēald (“old”) + -hād (a suffix forming abstract nouns denoting a state or condition). Cognate with Old Saxon ēldhēd, Old High German altihēit.

USAGE->
Reaching adulthood is a significant milestone. =  达到成年是一个重要的里程碑。
He spent most of his adulthood in London. = 他成年后的大部分时间都在伦敦度过。
