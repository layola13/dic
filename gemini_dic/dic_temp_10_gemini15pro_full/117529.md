USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I meet my friends every weekend.  我每个周末都和朋友见面。
She meets her family for dinner every Sunday. 她每个星期天都和家人一起吃晚饭。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am meeting with my boss right now. 我现在正在和我的老板开会。
They are meeting to discuss the project. 他们正在开会讨论这个项目。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have met many interesting people in my life. 我一生中遇到过很多有趣的人。
We have met our sales target for this quarter. 我们已经完成了本季度的销售目标。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been meeting with clients all day. 我一整天都在和客户开会。
She has been meeting with her tutor to prepare for the exam. 她一直在和她的导师见面，准备考试。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I met him at a conference last year. 我去年在一个会议上遇到了他。
They met for lunch yesterday. 他们昨天一起吃了午饭。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was meeting with a client when you called. 你打电话的时候我正在和客户开会。
She was meeting with her professor to discuss her thesis. 她当时正在和她的教授讨论她的论文。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had met her once before at a party. 我以前在一个派对上见过她一次。
They had met several times before they started dating. 他们在开始约会之前已经见过几次面了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been meeting with her regularly before she moved away. 在她搬走之前，我一直定期和她见面。
They had been meeting secretly for months. 他们秘密会面了好几个月。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will meet you at the airport tomorrow. 我明天去机场接你。
We will meet to discuss the next steps. 我们将开会讨论接下来的步骤。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

By the time she graduates, she will have been meeting with her advisor regularly for four years. 到她毕业的时候，她将定期与她的导师会面四年了。

*Note:* The word "meeten" is archaic and no longer commonly used.  The modern form "meet" is preferred.  The examples provided use "meet" in various tenses to demonstrate usage, as examples using "meeten" would sound unnatural in modern English.
