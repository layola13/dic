leucitic:/luːˈsɪtɪk/| adj.|白榴的，含白榴石的|Containing leucite, a white or grayish mineral.  A rock is leucitic if it contains leucite.|The lava flow was found to be leucitic. 这股熔岩流被发现含有白榴石。|Synonyms: leucitic-bearing |Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like leucite-free or non-leucitic)|adjective
