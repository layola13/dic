centriscoid: /sɛnˈtrɪskoɪd/
| adj. n.| Centriscoid的，centriscoids(复数)，centriscoid鱼，鹬嘴鱼科鱼类 |Relating to or denoting fishes of the family Centriscidae, which have a long tubular snout and compressed, knifelike bodies.  These fish are often found in coastal waters and are known for their unusual swimming posture, with their heads pointed downwards.|The centriscoid fish hovered near the coral, its body held vertically.  这条鹬嘴鱼在珊瑚附近盘旋，身体垂直竖立。| The divers observed several centriscoids during their exploration of the reef. 潜水员在探索珊瑚礁时观察到几条鹬嘴鱼。|近义词：snipefish, razorfish, shrimpfish |反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group) |词性：adjective, noun
