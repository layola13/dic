mountainboard:/ˈmaʊntɪnbɔːrd/| n. |山地滑板，全地形滑板|A board similar to a skateboard but designed for off-road use, with large pneumatic tires and bindings for the feet.|He went mountainboarding down the grassy slopes. 他滑着山地滑板从草坡上滑了下来。|近义词：all-terrain board, dirtboard|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑skateboard if contrasting with paved use)|名词
