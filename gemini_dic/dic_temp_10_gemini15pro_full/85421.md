harvestry:/ˈhɑːrvɪstri/
n.
收获季节; 收获期; 收获的庄稼; 收获物
The time of harvest. The crops gathered.
例句：The harvestry was bountiful this year. 今年的收成很丰盛。

例句：The farmers celebrated the harvestry with a feast. 农民们用盛宴庆祝丰收。

近义词：harvest, crop, yield
反义词：planting, sowing

词性：名词

词源：From "harvest" + "-ry" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a place, condition, or practice).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *harbistaz.
