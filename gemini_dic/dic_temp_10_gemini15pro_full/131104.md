nonbilious:/ˌnɒnˈbɪliəs/ | adj. | 非胆汁性的 | Not containing or producing bile; not marked by the presence of bile. |  The patient presented with nonbilious vomiting. 患者表现为非胆汁性呕吐。 |  The doctor determined the vomiting was nonbilious. 医生确定呕吐物是非胆汁性的。 | 近义词: abilious | 反义词: bilious | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): Latin, meaning "not"
bilious (adjective): From Late Latin biliosus, from Latin bilis "bile".
