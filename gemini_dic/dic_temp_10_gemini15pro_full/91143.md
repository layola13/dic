hybridizes:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪz/
verb
杂交，混合
combines or causes to combine to form a hybrid.
The plant hybridizes easily with other species.  这种植物很容易与其他物种杂交。
Geneticists have hybridized the two species to create a new variety. 遗传学家将这两个物种杂交以创造一个新品种。
synonyms: crossbreed, interbreed, cross, mix, blend, merge, combine, fuse, coalesce, intermingle, amalgamate
antonyms: separate, divide, isolate
v.
