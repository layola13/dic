dyspnea:/dɪˈspniːə/ /dɪsˈpniːə/
n.
呼吸困难，气促
Difficult or labored breathing.
The patient presented with dyspnea and chest pain. 病人表现出呼吸困难和胸痛。
近义词: shortness of breath, breathlessness
反义词: eupnea (normal breathing)
词性: 名词

dyspnea: /dɪˈspniːə/ /dɪsˈpniːə/
词源:  From Greek *dýspnoia, from dýspnoos “breathing with difficulty,” from dys- “bad, difficult” + pneo- “to breathe.”
词性: 名词
