bite-your-tongue:/ˈbaɪt jɔːr tʌŋ/ | idiom | 忍住不说；保持沉默 | To refrain from speaking, especially when tempted to say something critical or inappropriate. |  I wanted to tell her she was wrong, but I bit my tongue. 我想告诉她她错了，但我忍住了。

I almost told him the truth, but I bit my tongue at the last minute. 我差点告诉他真相，但在最后一刻我忍住了。 | 近义词: hold your tongue, keep silent, refrain from speaking | 反义词: speak out, speak your mind | idiom
