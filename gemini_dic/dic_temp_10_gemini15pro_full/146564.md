overgraded:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdɪd/
adj. | 过度分级的，级别过高的 | Assigned a grade or rank that is too high.  | The ore was overgraded, leading to an inflated estimate of the mine's value. 矿石被过度分级，导致对矿石价值的估计过高。| overestimated, overvalued | undervalued, undergraded | adjective


overgrade:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪd/
vt. | 过度分级，给予过高的等级 | To assign a grade or rank that is too high. | The teacher accidentally overgraded several of the essays. 老师不小心给几篇作文打了过高的分数。| overestimate, overvalue | underestimate, undervalue | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
