emeritus:/ɪˈmɛrɪtəs/ | adj. |退休的，荣誉退休的 | Having retired but retaining an honorary title corresponding to that held immediately before retirement. | Example 1: Professor Smith is now emeritus professor of history. 史密斯教授现在是历史系荣誉退休教授。

Example 2: She became emerita professor of physics upon her retirement. 她退休后成为了物理系荣誉退休教授。 | Near synonyms: retired, honorary | Antonyms: active, current | Word Type: Adjective
