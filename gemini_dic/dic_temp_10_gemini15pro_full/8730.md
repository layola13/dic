antidemocracies:/ˌæntidɪˈmɒkrəsiːz/
|n.|反民主国家；非民主国家|A system of government in which supreme power is concentrated in the hands of one person or a small elite and exercised in a manner inconsistent with democratic principles.|Many antidemocracies use propaganda to control their citizens.|近义词:dictatorships, totalitarian states, tyrannies|反义词:democracies|词性:名词
