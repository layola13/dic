europeanising:/ˌjʊərəˈpiːənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
欧洲化
The act of making something more European in character or style.
Many countries in Eastern Europe went through a process of europeanising after the fall of the Soviet Union.  许多东欧国家在苏联解体后经历了欧洲化的过程。
The candidate promised to europeanise the country's laws and regulations.  这位候选人承诺将使国家的法律法规欧洲化。
近义词: westernizing, modernizing
反义词: traditionalizing, localizing
词性:v.
