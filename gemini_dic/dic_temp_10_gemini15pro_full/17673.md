becoming:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋ/| adj.|合适的，相称的；好看的，漂亮的|Suitable or appropriate; attractive or pleasing.|That dress is very becoming on you. 那件衣服你穿起来很漂亮。|近义词：suitable, appropriate, flattering, attractive|反义词：unsuitable, inappropriate, unflattering, unattractive|adjective

becoming:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋ/| n.|行为，举止|the way one acts or appears; conduct; behavior|Her becoming was graceful and elegant. 她的举止优雅大方。|近义词：demeanor, conduct, behavior|反义词：misbehavior, misconduct|noun

A smile is becoming on her.  微笑很适合她。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is becoming more and more confident. 她正变得越来越自信。（描述逐渐变化的过程，并非“举止”的进行时）
He is looking becoming in that suit. 他穿那套西装看起来很帅气。（并非“举止”的进行时, 而是look的进行时）

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

That style has become her signature look.  那种风格已经成为她的标志性造型。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The dress became her perfectly.  这件衣服非常适合她。
His silence became awkward after a while.  过了一会儿，他的沉默变得很尴尬。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
She was becoming increasingly anxious as the deadline approached. 随着截止日期的临近，她越来越焦虑。（描述过去逐渐变化的过程）
The situation was becoming more and more complicated.  情况变得越来越复杂。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The dress had become too small for her after she gained weight.  她体重增加后，这件衣服变得太小了。
His behavior had become a cause for concern. 他的行为已经成为令人担忧的原因。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

He will have been becoming more and more knowledgeable about the subject. 他将对这个主题越来越了解。



PHRASE->
is becoming of = ...变得怎么样了 (e.g., What is becoming of the younger generation? 年轻一代变得怎么样了？)
ever becoming = 总是很得体
not becoming = 不合适的，不得体的
increasingly becoming = 越来越合适/流行/重要
become of = 发生在...身上; ...的结果是 (e.g., What will become of us? 我们将会变成什么样?)
