homoeomerical:/ˌhɒmiːəˈmɛrɪkəl/ | adj. | 由同类部分组成的; 同质的 | Composed of similar parts; homogeneous |  The ancient philosopher Anaxagoras believed that bone was homoeomerical, meaning made entirely of small "bone seeds."  古希腊哲学家阿那克萨哥拉认为骨头是同质的，意思是完全由小的“骨种子”组成。

The theory proposed that the universe was homoeomerical, with every part containing all the elements in the same proportion. 该理论认为宇宙是同质的，每一部分都包含相同比例的所有元素。 |  homogeneous, uniform | heterogeneous, diverse | adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὁμοιομερής (homoiomerēs, “having similar parts”), from ὁμοῖος (homoios, “similar”) + μέρος (meros, “part”).
