noncontemporaneously:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsli/
adverb
不同时地；非同时期地
occurring at different times
The two events happened noncontemporaneously, one in the 18th century and the other in the 21st. 这两件事发生在不同的时期，一个在18世纪，另一个在21世纪。
The artist blended noncontemporaneously styles in his work.  这位艺术家在他的作品中融合了不同时期的风格。
近义词: asynchronously, at different times
反义词: contemporaneously, simultaneously
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix): not
contemporary (adjective): existing, occurring, or living at the same time
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
