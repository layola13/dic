floorcloth:/ˈflɔːrklɒθ/ (US) /ˈflɔːklɒθ/ (UK)| n. |地板布，油布|A piece of heavy, waterproof material, such as canvas or oilcloth, used to cover and protect a floor.|She used a colorful floorcloth to brighten up the kitchen. 她用一块色彩鲜艳的地板布让厨房更明亮。|Synonyms: linoleum, oilcloth, rug, mat|Antonyms: carpet (sometimes, depending on context)|noun
