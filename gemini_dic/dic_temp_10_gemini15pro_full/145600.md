overconfident: /ˌoʊvərˈkɒnfɪdənt/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkɒnfɪdənt/ (UK) | adj. | 过于自信的；自负的 | Having or showing too much confidence; excessively confident. | He was overconfident going into the final match and ended up losing. 他在决赛前过于自信，结果输了。 |  She was so overconfident that she didn't even bother to study for the exam. 她太自信了，甚至懒得为考试学习。 | 近义词: arrogant, presumptuous, conceited | 反义词: diffident, insecure, modest | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "confident" (from Latin "confidentem," present participle of "confidere" meaning "to trust").
