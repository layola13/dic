nutritionally:/njuːˈtrɪʃənəli/
adverb
营养地；营养学上地
in a way that relates to nutrition
例句：This product is nutritionally balanced.  这款产品营养均衡。

例句：The food is nutritionally poor. 这食物营养不良。

近义词：dietarily, nutritiously
反义词: unhealthily

词性: adverb
