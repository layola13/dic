calcitrate: /ˈkælsɪtreɪt/ | n. | 柠檬酸盐; 钙的柠檬酸盐 |A salt or ester of citric acid.  It refers to any of the salts of citric acid, but often to calcium citrate specifically, which is commonly used as a food additive (E333) and dietary supplement. | Calcium citrate is often used as a calcium supplement.  柠檬酸钙常被用作钙补充剂。|Synonyms: citrate (especially in context), calcium salt (more general)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

calcitrate: /ˈkælsɪtreɪt/ | v. | 用柠檬酸处理；形成柠檬酸盐 |To treat or combine with citric acid to form a citrate. | The milk was calcitrated to improve its shelf life.  牛奶经过柠檬酸盐处理以延长保质期。|Synonyms: citrate|Antonyms: N/A|verb
