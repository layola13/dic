outdesign:/ˌaʊtˈdɪˈzaɪn/
verb
胜过…的设计；设计得比…更好
to design something that is more effective, attractive, etc. than something else
例句：They’ve completely outdesigned their competitors. 他们彻底在设计上胜过了竞争对手。

例句：The new iPhone outdesigns all previous models with its sleek lines and innovative features. 新款iPhone凭借其流畅的线条和创新的功能，在设计上超越了所有以前的型号。

近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip
反义词：be outdesigned by

词性:v.
