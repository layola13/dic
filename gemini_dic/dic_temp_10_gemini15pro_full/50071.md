dentalism: /ˈdɛntəlɪzəm/
noun
齿音现象；齿音化
The pronunciation of sounds with the tongue against the teeth.  This refers to sounds being articulated with the tongue touching the teeth, more than is typical for a given language.
Dentalism is common in some languages.  齿音化在某些语言中很常见。
The speaker's dentalism made it difficult to understand them.  说话者的齿音化使他们难以理解。
近义词: dentalization
反义词:  (No clear antonym exists, perhaps  velarization - 腭音化 -  as it involves a different part of the mouth)
词性: noun
