agelessly:/ˈeɪdʒləsli/
adv.
永恒地，不衰老地
In a way that does not age or appear to age.
She danced agelessly across the stage. 她永恒地舞动在舞台上。
近义词: eternally, timelessly, forever
反义词: ephemerally, transiently
