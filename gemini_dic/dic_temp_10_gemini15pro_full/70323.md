field-tested:/ˈfiːldˌtestɪd/
adj.
经过实地测试的；经过现场检验的
Proven or evaluated through practical use in real-world conditions, rather than just theoretical testing.
This new field-tested technology has proven to be highly effective.  这项经过实地测试的新技术已被证明非常有效。
The army is using field-tested equipment.  军队正在使用经过实地测试的装备。
近义词: proven, tried, tested, validated
反义词: untested, theoretical, unproven
词性: adjective
