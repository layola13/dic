hospitably:/ˈhɒspɪtəbli/ /hɑːspɪtəbli/| adv.|热情好客地，殷勤地|in a welcoming and friendly way, especially to guests or strangers|例句：They received us hospitably. 他们热情地接待了我们。|例句：The villagers hospitably offered us food and shelter. 村民们热情好客地为我们提供食物和住所。|近义词：warmly, graciously, kindly, generously|反义词：inhospitably, coldly, unwelcomingly|词性：副词

hospitable:/ˈhɒspɪtəbl/ /hɑːspɪtəbl/| adj.|热情好客的；适宜的|friendly and welcoming to guests or strangers; (of an environment) pleasant and favorable for living in|例句：The local people were very hospitable to us. 当地人对我们非常热情好客。|例句：The climate is hospitable to various types of plants. 这种气候适宜各种植物生长。|近义词：welcoming, friendly, warm, genial, agreeable|反义词：inhospitable, unfriendly, hostile|词性：形容词

hospitality:/ˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti/ /ˌhɑːspɪˈtælɪti/| n.|热情好客，殷勤招待|the friendly and generous reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers|例句：We were overwhelmed by the hospitality of the local people. 当地人的热情好客让我们受宠若惊。|例句：The hotel is renowned for its excellent hospitality. 这家酒店以其优质的服务而闻名。|近义词：welcome, friendliness, warmth, generosity|反义词：inhospitality, hostility|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *hospitable, from Latin hospitālis (“of a guest, hospitable”), from hospes (“guest, host”).
