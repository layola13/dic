coati:/koʊˈɑːti/ | n. |南美浣熊，蜜熊|A mammal (Nasua narica or a related species) of tropical and subtropical America, having a long, mobile snout, a long, ringed tail, and strong claws.|例句：We saw a coati foraging for food in the rainforest. 我们看到一只南美浣熊在雨林中觅食。

|近义词：coatimundi, nasua|反义词:|noun

coatis:/koʊˈɑːtiːz/ | n. |南美浣熊（复数）|Plural form of "coati".|例句：The coatis were playful and curious, sniffing around our campsite. 这些南美浣熊很好奇又爱玩耍，在我们的营地周围嗅来嗅去。|近义词：coatimundis, nasuas|反义词:|noun
