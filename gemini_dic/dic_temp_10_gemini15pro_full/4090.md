ailment:/ˈeɪlmənt/| n. |疾病，小病|An illness, typically a minor one.|例句：He was suffering from a mysterious ailment. 他患了一种神秘的疾病。|例句：She complained of various ailments, including headaches and fatigue. 她抱怨各种各样的病痛，包括头痛和疲劳。|近义词：illness, sickness, disease, malady|反义词:health, wellness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aille, from Latin *agere "to drive, lead, do, act". The original meaning was "trouble, difficulty".
