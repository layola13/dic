gazumper:/ɡəˈzʌmpər/| n.|抬价抢购者，截胡者|Someone who offers a higher price for something (especially a house) than someone else has already offered, in order to get it for themselves.|The gazumper offered £10,000 more than the original buyer. 这个抬价抢购者比原买家多 offered 1万英镑。|Synonyms: outbidder, snipper|Antonyms: underbidder|noun
