bacciform:/ˈbæsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
浆果状的; 类似浆果的
Having the form of a berry.
The botanist identified the strange fruit as a bacciform structure, unlike any he had seen before.  植物学家将这种奇怪的水果鉴定为浆果状结构，这与他以前见过的任何水果都不同。
The plant produces small, bacciform fruits that are attractive to birds.  这种植物会结出吸引鸟类的小浆果状果实。
近义词: berry-like, berry-shaped
反义词: (No clear antonym exists, perhaps "non-bacciform")
词性: adjective
