hankered:/ˈhæŋkərd/| v.|渴望，渴求|To have a strong desire for something.|She hankered for a return to her homeland. 她渴望回到故乡。|近义词：yearned, longed, craved|反义词:disliked, abhorred|verb

hankering:/ˈhæŋkərɪŋ/| n.|渴望，渴求|A strong desire for something.|He had a hankering for a big slice of chocolate cake. 他很想吃一大块巧克力蛋糕。|近义词：yearning, longing, craving|反义词:aversion, dislike|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hanker*, perhaps of imitative origin, expressing a gasping or panting sound.
