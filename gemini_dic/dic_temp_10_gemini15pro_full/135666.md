nonpregnant:/ˌnɒnˈprɛɡnənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈprɛɡnənt/| adj.|未怀孕的|Not pregnant; not carrying a developing fetus.|例句：The study focused on nonpregnant women. 这项研究的重点是非怀孕妇女。|近义词：not pregnant|反义词:pregnant|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + pregnant.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Pregnant" comes from the Latin "praegnans" meaning "being with child".
