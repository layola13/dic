blowtorching:/ˈbloʊˌtɔːrtʃɪŋ/
n.|喷灯灼烧；喷灯切割|The act of using a blowtorch to heat, cut, or weld something.  This involves directing a very hot flame onto a surface.|例句：The blowtorching of the metal created a shower of sparks.  喷灯灼烧金属产生了一阵火花。|近义词:welding, cutting, searing, scorching|反义词:cooling, extinguishing|词性:名词

blowtorching:/ˈbloʊˌtɔːrtʃɪŋ/
v.|用喷灯灼烧；用喷灯切割|To use a blowtorch to heat, cut, or weld something.|例句：He was blowtorching the old paint off the gate. 他正在用喷灯烧掉大门上的旧油漆。|近义词:welding, cutting, searing, scorching|反义词:cooling, extinguishing|词性:动词
