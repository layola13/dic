concretively:/kɒnˈkriːtli/ /kɑːnˈkriːtli/| adv.|具体地，具体而言|Making something concrete or solid; relating to an actual, specific thing or instance.  It implies a focus on practical details and tangible results, rather than abstract ideas.|She explained the concept concretively, using real-world examples. 她用现实世界的例子具体地解释了这个概念。|Synonyms: specifically, particularly, definitely, explicitly, tangibly|Antonyms: abstractly, vaguely, generally, indefinitely|Adverb
