chrysophane:/ˈkrɪsəˌfeɪn/| n.| 金黄素; 1,8-二羟基-3-甲基蒽醌 |A yellow crystalline substance found in various plants, including rhubarb and senna, used as a fungicide and cathartic.|Chrysophane is found in the roots of rhubarb and has laxative properties.|近义词:rhein, emodin|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源: From Greek *khrusophanes* "appearing golden", from *khrusos* "gold" + *phainein* "to appear."
