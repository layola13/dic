bellicosely:/ˌbelɪˈkoʊsli/ | adv. | 好战地，挑衅地 | In a hostile or aggressive manner; showing a willingness to fight. | He spoke bellicosely, threatening to retaliate against any perceived slight. 他好战地说着，威胁要报复任何感觉到的轻蔑。|  The general bellicosely addressed the troops, urging them to conquer the enemy. 将军气势汹汹地对部队讲话，敦促他们战胜敌人。 | 近义词: aggressively, belligerently, pugnaciously, contentiously | 反义词: peacefully, amicably, diplomatically | 词性: 副词

词源: From Latin "bellicosus" (warlike), from "bellum" (war).
