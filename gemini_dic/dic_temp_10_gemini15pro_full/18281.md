bell:/bel/ | n. |铃铛；钟声；钟形物|A hollow metal object, usually shaped like a cup, that makes a ringing sound when struck. |The church bells rang out across the valley. 教堂的钟声响彻山谷。|Synonyms: chime, gong, ringer |Antonyms: silence, mute |noun

bell:/bel/ | v. |鸣钟；装铃|To attach a bell to; to ring a bell. |They belled the cat to warn the birds. 他们在猫身上系了铃铛，好让鸟儿小心。|Synonyms: ring, chime, toll |Antonyms: silence, muffle |verb
