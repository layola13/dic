malfeasance:/mælˈfiːzəns/
n.
渎职；不法行为；玩忽职守
wrongdoing, especially by a public official
The mayor was accused of malfeasance in office. 市长被指控在职期间渎职。

malfeasance:/mælˈfiːzəns/
词性: 名词
中文翻译: 渎职；不法行为；玩忽职守
英文解析: wrongdoing, especially by a public official
例句：The investigation uncovered widespread financial malfeasance within the company. 调查揭露了公司内部普遍存在的财务违规行为。
例句：His malfeasance as a police officer led to his dismissal from the force. 他作为一名警官的不法行为导致他被解雇。
近义词：misconduct, wrongdoing, corruption, dereliction of duty
反义词：good conduct, propriety

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *malfaisance, from malfaisant (“misdoing, evildoing”), from mal- (“badly”) + faisant (“doing”), present participle of faire (“to do”). Equivalent to mal- + -feasance.
