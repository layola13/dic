masquers:/ˈmæskərərz/
n.
戴面具的人；伪装者；参加化装舞会的人
People who wear masks, especially at a masquerade ball.
The masquers danced through the night, their identities hidden behind elaborate masks.  戴面具的人整夜跳舞，他们的身份隐藏在精致的面具后面。
The masquers unveiled themselves at midnight.  伪装者在午夜揭开了他们的面纱。
synonyms: maskers, mummers, revelers
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "non-participants," "observers," or individuals who are "open and honest")
