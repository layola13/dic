alighting:/əˈlaɪtɪŋ/
v. |下车；下飞机；飞落；偶然发现|The passengers are alighting from the train.  乘客们正在下火车。|
例句：The bird was alighting on a branch. 鸟儿落在树枝上。
例句：We alighted from the bus at the next stop. 我们在下一站下了公共汽车。
近义词：landing, disembarking, getting off
反义词：boarding, embarking, taking off
词性：动词(present participle)

alighting:/əˈlaɪtɪŋ/
n. |下车；下飞机；飞落；偶然发现|The alighting of the passengers was delayed. 乘客下机被延误了。|
例句：The alighting of the eagle was a majestic sight.  老鹰的降落是一幅雄伟的景象。
例句：Her alighting on the solution was completely unexpected. 她偶然发现这个解决方案完全出乎意料。
近义词：landing, disembarkation
反义词：boarding, embarkation
词性：名词(gerund)

词源：From Middle English *alighten, from a- + light (verb).  Ultimately from Old English *ālīhtan, from ā- + līhtan (“to alight”).
