leapable:/ˈliːpəbl/| adj.|可跳跃的，可跨越的|Something that is leapable can be leaped over.  It describes something that is capable of being jumped or vaulted over.|The stream was narrow enough to be leapable. 这条小溪很窄，可以跳过去。|近义词：jumpable, vault-able|反义词:unleapable, impassable|词性:adjective
词源:From "leap" (v.) + "-able" (suffix).  "Leap" comes from Old English *hlēapan, of Germanic origin.
