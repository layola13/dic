misconceives:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvz/
verb
误解，错误地理解
to interpret or understand (something) wrongly
She completely misconceives the meaning of the poem. 她完全误解了这首诗的含义。
misunderstands, misinterprets, misconstrues
understands, comprehends, grasps
词性: 动词
