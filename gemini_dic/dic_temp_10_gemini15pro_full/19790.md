billycans:/ˈbɪlikænz/| n.|水壶，饭盒 (尤指金属制的，常用于户外活动)|A metal container used for cooking or carrying water, especially when camping or hiking. |例句：We boiled water in the billycans over the campfire. 我们在篝火上用水壶烧水。

例句：He packed his lunch in his trusty billycan. 他把午饭装在他可靠的饭盒里。|近义词：canteen, mess kit, cookware|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


词源:
From the Australian Aboriginal word "billa," meaning "water," and "can."  源于澳大利亚土著语“billa”，意思是“水”，和“can”（罐）。
