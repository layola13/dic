escutcheon:/ɪˈskʌtʃən/
n.
盾形纹章，饰板，孔罩
A shield or emblem bearing a coat of arms.  A decorative plate or shield around a keyhole, light switch, or other fitting.
The knight's escutcheon displayed a golden lion on a field of red. 骑士的盾形纹章上绘有一只在红色背景上的金狮子。
The escutcheon around the doorknob was tarnished. 门把手周围的饰板已经失去光泽了。
Synonym: shield, crest, insignia, plate
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "blank space" in context of a decorative plate)
