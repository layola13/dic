mesocoracoid: /ˌmɛzoʊˈkɔːrəkɔɪd/ | n. | 中乌喙骨 | A small bone or cartilaginous element located ventral to the coracoid in the pectoral girdle of some vertebrates, particularly birds and reptiles. |  The mesocoracoid provides additional support to the shoulder joint. 中乌喙骨为某些脊椎动物（尤其是鸟类和爬行动物）肩带中位于喙骨腹侧的小骨或软骨元件，为肩关节提供额外的支撑。| synonym:  coracoid process (in mammals - analogous structure) | antonym: None (anatomical term, no direct opposite) | noun
