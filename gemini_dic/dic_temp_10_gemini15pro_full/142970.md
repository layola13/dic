outbabbling:/ˌaʊtˈbæblɪŋ/
|v. | 比...说得更多，说得比...更流畅; 压过...的声音 |To talk longer, more fluently, or more loudly than someone else.| The stream outbabbled the birds. 小溪的声音盖过了鸟叫声。 | outtalk, drown out | be quiet, listen | verb

outbabbling:/ˌaʊtˈbæblɪŋ/
|adj. | 比...说得更多，说得比...更流畅的 |Characterized by talking longer, more fluently, or more loudly than someone else.  |  The outbabbling brook created a peaceful atmosphere.  潺潺的小溪营造了一种宁静的氛围。|  loquacious, garrulous | quiet, reserved | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "babble" (meaning to talk rapidly and continuously in a foolish, excited, or incomprehensible way).  "Babble" ultimately comes from the imitative Proto-Indo-European root *baba-.
