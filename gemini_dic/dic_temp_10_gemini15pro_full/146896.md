overinsistent:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstənt/| adj.|过分坚持的，过于执着的|persisting too much; demanding something repeatedly or excessively|She was overinsistent that we stay for dinner, even after we explained we had other plans. 她过分坚持让我们留下吃晚饭，即使在我们解释了我们有其他计划之后也是如此。

The salesman was overinsistent, bordering on aggressive, in his attempts to close the deal.  这个推销员过于执着，近乎咄咄逼人，试图完成交易。|近义词：persistent, insistent, tenacious, pertinacious|反义词：yielding, compliant, acquiescent|词性:形容词
