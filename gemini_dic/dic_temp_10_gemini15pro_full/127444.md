nebula:/ˈnɛbjələ/ (US), /ˈnɛbjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |星云|A cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter.|例句：The Orion Nebula is a popular target for amateur astronomers.  猎户座星云是业余天文学家的热门目标。

例句：Astronomers study nebulae to learn about the life cycle of stars.  天文学家研究星云以了解恒星的生命周期。|近义词：cloud, haze|反义词: void|词性:noun
