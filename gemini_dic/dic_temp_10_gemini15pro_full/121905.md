missed-out-on:/mɪst ˈaʊt ɑːn/
phrasal verb
错过，失去（机会）
Failing to take advantage of an opportunity or experience.
I missed out on the promotion because I was on maternity leave. 我因为休产假错过了升职的机会。
She really missed out on a great opportunity to travel the world. 她真的错过了环游世界的大好机会。
近义词: lose out on, forgo, miss
反义词: capitalize on, seize, take advantage of
词性: 短语动词
