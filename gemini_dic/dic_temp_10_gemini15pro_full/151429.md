parodistic:/ˌpærəˈdɪstɪk/ | adj. | 滑稽模仿的，戏仿的 | Imitating the style of something or someone else for comic effect, often by exaggerating certain features. |  His parodistic portrayal of the politician was hilarious. 他对这位政客的滑稽模仿令人捧腹。 | synonyms: satirical, mocking, burlesque, ironic, farcical | antonyms: serious, respectful, reverent | adjective

Etymology:
From French *parodique*, from Ancient Greek *παρωδία* (parōidía, “burlesque poem, parody”), from *παρά* (pará, “beside, beyond”) + *ᾠδή* (ōidḗ, “song, ode”).
