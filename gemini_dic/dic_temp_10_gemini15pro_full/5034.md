all-inclusive:/ɔːlɪnˈkluːsɪv/ | adj. | 包罗万象的；全包的 | Including everything or everyone; comprehensive. |  We booked an all-inclusive holiday to the Caribbean. 我们预订了一个去加勒比海的全包假期。| Example: The price is all-inclusive, covering flights, accommodation, and meals.  价格是全包的，包括航班、住宿和餐食。| Synonyms: comprehensive, complete, full, inclusive | Antonyms: limited, restricted, partial, exclusive | adjective
