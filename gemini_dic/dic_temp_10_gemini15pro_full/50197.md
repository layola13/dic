deoxygenize:/diːˈɒksɪdʒənaɪz/ /diːˈɑːksɪdʒənaɪz/
verb
脱氧，除去氧气
To remove oxygen from something.
例句：The process deoxygenizes the water, making it unsuitable for fish.  这个过程会除去水中的氧气，使水不适合鱼类生存。

例句：Scientists are exploring ways to deoxygenize Mars' atmosphere to make it habitable. 科学家们正在探索使火星大气脱氧以使其适宜居住的方法。
近义词：deoxidize, reduce
反义词：oxygenate, oxidize
词性：v.
