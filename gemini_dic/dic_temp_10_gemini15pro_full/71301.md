fixtureless:/ˈfɪkstʃərlɪs/
adj.
无固定装置的；无固定比赛的
lacking fixtures, especially permanent fixtures.  Also, in sports, not having a fixed schedule or opponent.
The apartment was completely fixtureless, not even a lightbulb. 这套公寓完全没有固定装置，甚至连灯泡都没有。
The team had a fixtureless week due to the tournament cancellation. 由于锦标赛取消，这个团队一周都没有固定比赛。
近义词: unfurnished, unequipped
反义词: furnished, equipped
词性: adjective
