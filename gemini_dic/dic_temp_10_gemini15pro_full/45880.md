cutes:/kjuts/ | n. |可爱的人或事物（复数）| Plural form of "cute," referring to multiple things or people that are endearing or charming. |  Those puppies are so cutes! 那些小狗太可爱了! | 近义词: cuties, adorable things, sweet things | 反义词: uglies, unpleasant things | 词性: 名词

cute:/kjut/ | adj. |可爱的；漂亮的；迷人的|Attractive, especially in a dainty way; pleasingly pretty.|  She has a cute little nose. 她有一个可爱的鼻子。| 近义词: adorable, charming, lovely | 反义词: ugly, unattractive, hideous | 词性: 形容词
