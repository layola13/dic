grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/ (US), /ˈɡræpəl/ (UK)| n. v.|抓住；格斗；扭打；钩爪；抓斗|A struggle or fight, especially a hand-to-hand one; a grappling hook or iron; to engage in a close fight or struggle without weapons; to try to deal with or understand a difficult problem or subject.|例句：The two wrestlers grappled with each other. 两个摔跤选手扭打在一起。|近义词：wrestle, contend, struggle, tackle, confront|反义词：release, surrender, yield|词性: 名词, 动词
