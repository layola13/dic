meanness:/ˈmiːnɪs/| n.|卑鄙；吝嗇；卑劣|The meanness of his actions shocked everyone. 他的卑鄙行径震惊了所有人。|Example: He showed surprising meanness by refusing to help the homeless man. 他拒绝帮助那个无家可归的人，表现出惊人的吝啬。|Synonyms: stinginess, pettiness, cruelty, unkindness|Antonyms: generosity, kindness, compassion, benevolence|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *menenesse*, from Old English *gemǣnnes* (“commonness, community, fellowship”), from Proto-Germanic *gamainiz* (“common”). More at mean (adjective). The sense shifted from "commonness" to "lowliness, baseness" and then to "stinginess, unkindness".
