negrohood:/ˈniːɡroʊhʊd/
n.
黑人居住区；黑人社会；黑人身份
The state or quality of being a Negro; Negro character or nature.  A neighborhood inhabited predominantly by Negroes.
He spent his childhood in the negrohood of Harlem. 他在哈莱姆的黑人社区度过了童年。
近义词:black neighborhood,  African American community
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
From negro + -hood.  "Negro" is an outdated and offensive term for a person of black African descent.  "-hood" is a suffix denoting a state, quality, condition, or character.  Therefore, "negrohood" literally means the state of being a Negro.
