meteor:/ˈmiːtiər/ (US), /ˈmiːtiɔːr/ (UK)| n.|流星;陨星|A small body of matter from outer space that enters the earth's atmosphere, becoming incandescent as a result of friction and appearing as a streak of light.|A meteor flashed across the sky. 一颗流星划过天空。|Synonyms: shooting star, falling star|Antonyms: N/A|noun
