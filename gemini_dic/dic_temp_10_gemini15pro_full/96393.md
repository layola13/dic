indiscrimination:/ˌɪndɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |不加区别，不分皂白，滥杀滥伤|Lack of careful thought or choice; treating all things or people the same, often in a negative way.|The indiscriminate bombing of the city caused many civilian casualties.  对该市不加区别的轰炸造成了许多平民伤亡。|Synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, blindness, nonselectivity|Antonyms: discrimination, discernment, selectivity|noun
