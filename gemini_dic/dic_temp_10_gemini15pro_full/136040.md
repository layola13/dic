nonreasoners:/ˌnɑːnˈriːzənərz/| n.|非理性者，不讲道理的人|People who don't use or are incapable of using reason or logic.|These nonreasoners simply refuse to acknowledge the facts.|近义词：illogical people, irrational people|反义词:rational people, logical thinkers|词性:Noun (plural)

Etymology->
From non- + reasoner.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" and "reasoner" is derived from the verb "to reason," which comes from Old French "raisonner" and ultimately from Latin "ratio" meaning "reckoning, calculation, reason."
