depletions:/dɪˈpliːʃənz/
|n.|消耗，减少，损耗|The depletions of natural resources are a serious concern. 自然资源的消耗是一个严重的问题。|
|The ozone layer has suffered severe depletions. 臭氧层遭受了严重的损耗。|
|近义词:reductions, decreases, losses, diminutions|
|反义词:increases, gains, accumulations|
|词性:名词(plural)|
|词源:From deplete + -ion. From Latin deplēre to empty out, from de- + plēre to fill.  Ultimately from the Indo-European root *pele- (1) to fill.|
