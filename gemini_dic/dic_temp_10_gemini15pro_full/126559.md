nadirs:/ˈneɪdɪrz/ /ˈneɪdərz/ | n. |最低点；最底点；最坏的时刻；|The nadir of his career came when he was fired. 他事业的最低谷是他被解雇的时候。| English Explanation: The lowest point; the point of greatest adversity or despair.  It can refer to a literal low point, like the nadir of a satellite's orbit, or a figurative low point, like the nadir of someone's fortunes. |例句：The stock market reached its nadir in March. 股市在三月份跌到了最低点。

例句：Her performance was the nadir of the evening. 她的表演是当晚最糟糕的。

近义词：low point, bottom, rock bottom |反义词: zenith, peak, apex | 词性: noun
