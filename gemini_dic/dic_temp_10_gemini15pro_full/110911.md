lipids:/ˈlɪpɪdz/| n. |脂质，类脂|Any of a class of organic compounds that are fatty acids or their derivatives and are insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents. They include many natural oils, waxes, and steroids.|例句：Cholesterol is a type of lipid. 胆固醇是一种脂质。

例句：Lipids are essential for cell membrane structure. 脂质对细胞膜结构至关重要。|近义词：fats, oils|反义词:|noun
