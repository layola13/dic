collude:/kəˈluːd/| v. |共谋，勾结|To conspire or plot secretly, especially for an illegal or deceitful purpose.|The companies colluded to fix prices. 这些公司串通一气操纵价格。|近义词：conspire, plot, scheme|反义词：cooperate, collaborate|词性：动词

Etymology: From Latin "colludere" (to play together), from "com-" (together) + "ludere" (to play).  The meaning shifted from "playing together" to "playing a deceitful game together."
