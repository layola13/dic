delator:/dɪˈleɪtər/| n.|告密者，告发者|A person who informs on another, especially one who betrays a trust; an informer.|The delator was ostracized by his former friends after his betrayal was revealed.  告密者被揭发背叛后，被他以前的朋友们排斥了。|Synonyms: informer, accuser, betrayer, snitch, tattletale|Antonyms: loyalist, protector, defender|noun

delator:/dɪˈleɪtər/| n.|控告者（尤指在法庭上）|Someone who accuses another person, especially in a court of law.|The delator presented evidence that incriminated the defendant. 控告者提供了证明被告有罪的证据。|Synonyms: prosecutor, plaintiff, complainant|Antonyms: defendant, accused|noun
