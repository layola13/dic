kpop:/ˈkeɪpɒp/ (US), /ˈkeɪpɒp/ (UK)| n. |韩国流行音乐|Korean popular music, an abbreviation of Korean pop. It refers to a genre of pop music originating in South Korea.|例句：Kpop has become a global phenomenon. 韩国流行音乐已成为一种全球现象。|例句：Many teenagers are obsessed with kpop idols. 许多青少年痴迷于韩国流行偶像。|近义词：K-pop|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider genres like J-pop or C-pop as alternatives, though not opposites.)|词性:noun
