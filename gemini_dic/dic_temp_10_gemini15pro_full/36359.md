coincidence:/koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ /kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns/| n. |巧合，一致，同时发生|a situation in which events happen at the same time in a way that seems surprising or unusual|例句：It was pure coincidence that we were both in Paris at the same time. 我们俩同时在巴黎纯属巧合。

例句：By coincidence, we both ended up wearing the same outfit. 碰巧的是，我们最终都穿了同一套衣服。

近义词：chance, accident, fluke, concurrence|反义词:intention, plan, design|词性:名词
