gelled:/dʒeld/ | v. | 凝胶化；团结；成功 | to become set or solidified into a gel; to become a cohesive unit; to work well together |  The gelatin gelled in the refrigerator. 果冻在冰箱里凝固了。

The team really gelled during the tournament.  团队在锦标赛中真正地团结起来了。 | 近义词：solidified, congealed, united, clicked | 反义词：liquefied, separated, disunited | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *gel* (n.) + *-ed* (past participle suffix).  *Gel* comes from Latin *gelu* "frost, ice, coldness."
