misfashioned:/ˌmɪsˈfæʃənd/
adjective
畸形的，拙劣的，式样不好的
badly or awkwardly fashioned; misshapen.
The dress was misfashioned and ill-fitting. 这件衣服做得不好，而且不合身。
Near Synonyms: deformed, distorted, misshapen, malformed, grotesque
Antonyms: well-made, shapely, well-formed
词性: adjective
