biscotin:/bɪsˈkɒtɪn/ |/bɪˈskɑːtɪn/| n. |小饼干，小脆饼|A small, crisp, twice-baked cookie or biscuit. |She offered me a delicious biscotin with my tea. 她给我一块美味的小饼干配茶。|The bakery is known for its delicate biscotins. 这家面包店以其精致的小饼干而闻名。|近义词：biscuit, cookie, cracker|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *biscotin*, diminutive of *biscoter*, "to bake twice," from Old French *bescoit*.  Ultimately from Latin *bis* "twice" + *coctus* "cooked," past participle of *coquere* "to cook."
