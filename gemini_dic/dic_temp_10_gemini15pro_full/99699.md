interlocks:/ˌɪntərˈlɒks/ (UK), /ˌɪntərˈlɑːks/ (US)| n. v.|联锁,互相扣住,连结|A device or system that prevents something from operating unless certain conditions are met; the act or state of being interlocked or interconnected.|The safety interlocks prevent the machine from starting if the guard is not in place. 安全联锁装置会在防护装置未到位时阻止机器启动。|Synonyms: connects, joins, links, meshes, engages|Antonyms: disconnects, separates, disengages|noun

interlocks:/ˌɪntərˈlɒks/ (UK), /ˌɪntərˈlɑːks/ (US)| v.|联锁,互相扣住,连结|To connect or lock together, typically in a way that prevents independent operation.|The gears interlock to transfer power smoothly. 齿轮相互啮合以平稳地传递动力。|Synonyms: connects, joins, links, meshes, engages|Antonyms: disconnects, separates, disengages|verb
