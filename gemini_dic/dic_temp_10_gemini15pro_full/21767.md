boatswain:/ˈboʊsən/ (US), /ˈbəʊsn/ (UK)| n. | 水手长；甲板长 | A warrant officer on a warship or other vessel, responsible for the maintenance of the ship's hull, rigging, anchors, cables, and boats, and for summoning the crew to their duties. | The boatswain piped the crew to quarters. 水手长吹哨召集船员到岗位。| 近义词：deck officer, petty officer | 反义词: N/A | noun
