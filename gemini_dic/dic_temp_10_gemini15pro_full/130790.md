nonanimation:/ˌnɒnˌænɪˈmeɪʃn/
n.
无生命; 非动画
lack of animation or liveliness.
The nonanimation of the puppets made the show less engaging. 木偶的呆板使得演出缺乏吸引力。

Synonyms: lifelessness, inertness, stillness
Antonyms: animation, liveliness, vibrancy
词性: 名词
