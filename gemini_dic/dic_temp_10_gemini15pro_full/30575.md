ceratohyal: /ˌsɛrətoʊˈhaɪəl/ /ˌsɛrətəˈhaɪəl/ | n. | 角舌骨 | A bone or cartilage element in the hyoid apparatus of many vertebrates, typically connecting the epihyal and hypohyal.  It is part of the skeletal structure supporting the tongue and other structures in the throat region. | The ceratohyal is a small bone located in the hyoid apparatus. 角舌骨是位于舌骨器中的一块小骨。 |  The ceratohyal of the frog is particularly prominent. 青蛙的角舌骨特别突出。 | synonyms: hyoid bone element (more general),  | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific bone) | noun
