nonascendency:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsi/
n.|非优势；非支配地位|Lack of dominance or controlling influence.  The state of not being in a position of power or authority.|The treaty ensured the nonascendency of any single nation. 该条约确保了任何单一国家都不会占据支配地位。|Near synonyms: subordination, subjection, subservience|Antonyms: dominance, supremacy, ascendancy
word class: noun
