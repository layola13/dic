industrious:/ɪnˈdʌstriəs/ | adj. | 勤奋的，勤劳的 | Characterized by hard work and perseverance.  |  She is an industrious student, always studying late into the night. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是学习到深夜。

例句：
The industrious farmer worked from dawn till dusk. 勤劳的农民从早到晚地工作。
He is an industrious worker, always completing his tasks efficiently. 他是一个勤奋的员工，总是高效地完成任务。

近义词：hardworking, diligent, assiduous, laborious | 反义词：lazy, idle, indolent, slothful | 词性：形容词


词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French industrieux, from Latin industrius ‘diligent’, from industria (see industry).
