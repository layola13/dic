nonterritorially:/ˌnɒnˌtɛrɪˈtɔːriəli/
adverb|非地域性地，不限于领土地|Existing or applying in a way that is not limited to a particular territory. |例句：The internet allows businesses to operate nonterritorially. 互联网允许企业进行非地域性运营。|例句：Digital nomads often work nonterritorially, moving from country to country. 数字游民经常非地域性地工作，在各国之间迁徙。|近义词：globally, internationally, ubiquitously|反义词:locally, regionally|词性:副词
词源: non- (not) + territorially (related to territory)
