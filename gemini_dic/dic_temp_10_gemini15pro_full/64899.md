essential: /ɪˈsɛnʃəl/ | adj. n. | 必要的，本质的；必需品 | Something that is essential is extremely important or absolutely necessary to a particular subject, situation, or activity. |  Regular exercise is essential for good health.  规律的锻炼对健康至关重要。| synonyms: necessary, vital, crucial, indispensable, fundamental | antonyms: nonessential, unnecessary, superfluous, optional | adjective

essential: /ɪˈsɛnʃəl/ | n. | 必需品 | Something that is essential, especially something that you need to take with you or have available. |  Food, water, and shelter are the essentials of life.  食物、水和住所是生活的必需品。| synonyms: necessity, requirement, requisite, must-have | antonyms: nonessential, luxury | noun
