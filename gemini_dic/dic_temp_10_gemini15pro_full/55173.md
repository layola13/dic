disseminations:/dɪˌsemɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|传播；散布；宣传|The dissemination of information is crucial in a democracy. 信息的传播在民主社会中至关重要。|例句：The dissemination of fake news online is a serious problem.  网上虚假新闻的传播是一个严重的问题。|近义词：distribution, spreading, circulation, propagation|反义词：suppression, concealment, withholding|词性:名词

disseminations:/dɪˌsemɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|散播；撒播（种子等）|The dissemination of seeds is essential for plant reproduction.  种子的散播对于植物繁殖至关重要。|例句：Wind plays a crucial role in the dissemination of dandelion seeds. 风在蒲公英种子的传播中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：scattering, dispersal|反义词：collection, gathering|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin disseminatio, from disseminare "to sow, scatter," from dis- "widely" + seminare "to sow," from semen "seed."
