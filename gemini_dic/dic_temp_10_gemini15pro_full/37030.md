comales:/koʊˈmɑːleɪs/
n.
煎饼锅，烤盘 (复数)
A flat griddle, typically made of clay or metal, used in Mexican cooking for making tortillas and other foods.
She cooked the tortillas on the comales. 她用煎饼锅做了玉米饼。
近义词:griddle, hotplate
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a cooking implement)
词性:名词
