catboat:/ˈkætˌboʊt/| n.| 帆船，平底帆船|A sailboat with a single mast set well forward, a large mainsail, and no jib.|例句：The catboat sailed smoothly across the bay. 平底帆船平稳地驶过海湾。

例句：He learned to sail in a small catboat. 他在一艘小平底帆船上学航海。|近义词：sailboat, dinghy|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑motorboat 机动船)|词性:noun
