conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/ | v. | 密谋，共谋，协力 | to secretly plan with someone to do something harmful or illegal |  They conspired to overthrow the government. 他们密谋推翻政府。 |  plot, scheme, collude | cooperate, oppose | verb

conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/ | v. | (指事件或情况)凑在一起造成(坏结果) |  appear to be working together to create an unwanted effect | Everything conspired to make her journey a nightmare. 所有的事情都凑在一起，让她不胜其烦。 | combine, coincide | clash, differ | verb
