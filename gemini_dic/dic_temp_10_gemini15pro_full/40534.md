controlr: /kənˈtroʊlər/ (US), /kənˈtrəʊlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|控制器，控制者|A person or device that controls or regulates something.|The air traffic controller guided the plane to the runway.  空中交通管制员引导飞机到跑道。|近义词：regulator, director, manager|反义词：subject, follower|noun

controlr: /kənˈtroʊlər/ (US), /kənˈtrəʊlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|控制者，管理者|A person who manages or directs something, especially a business or department.|The financial controller is responsible for the company's budget.  财务总监负责公司的预算。|近义词：administrator, executive, supervisor|反义词：subordinate, employee|noun



词源:  "controlr" seems to be a misspelling of "controller".  "Controller" comes from the verb "control", which ultimately derives from the Old French word "controle" (a counterpart or duplicate register used to verify accounts).  The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or one who performs an action.
