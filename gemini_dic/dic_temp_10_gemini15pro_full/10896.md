arafat:/ˈærəfæt/ | n. | 阿拉法特（地名）| Arafat, also known as Mount Arafat, is a plain about 20 km (12 mi) east of Mecca, in the Makkah Province of Saudi Arabia. It is a granite hill, about 70 m (230 ft) high, which is also known as the Mount of Mercy (Jabal ar-Rahmah).  |  Muslims gather on the plain of Arafat during the Hajj pilgrimage.  穆斯林在朝觐期间聚集在阿拉法特平原。 | Synonyms: Mount Arafat, Jabal ar-Rahmah | Antonyms: N/A | noun
