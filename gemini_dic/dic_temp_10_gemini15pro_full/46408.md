daffy:/ˈdæfi/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的|Silly or foolish, mildly eccentric.|He made a daffy remark about the weather. 他对天气做了一个愚蠢的评论。|近义词：silly, foolish, goofy, wacky, zany|反义词：sensible, wise, intelligent|词性:形容词
词源：Originated in the early 20th century, likely as a playful alteration of "daft," meaning "silly" or "insane."
