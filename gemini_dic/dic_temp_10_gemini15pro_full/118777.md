metallography: /ˌmɛtəˈlɒɡrəfi/ (US) /ˌmɛtəˈlɒɡrəfi/ (UK)| n. |金相学|The study of the structure of metals and alloys, typically using microscopic examination.|例句：Metallography revealed the presence of micro-cracks in the weld. 金相分析揭示了焊缝中存在微裂纹。

例句：The metallography of the failed component showed evidence of fatigue. 失效部件的金相分析显示出疲劳迹象。


|近义词：materialography|反义词: None|词性: noun
