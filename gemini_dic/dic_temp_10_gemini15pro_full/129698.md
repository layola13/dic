non-doctrinal:/ˌnɑːnˈdɑːktrɪnəl/
adj.
非教义的，非教条的
Not relating to or based on the established doctrines of a religion, political party, or other group.
例句：The group holds non-doctrinal meetings open to people of all faiths.  这个团体举行非教义的会议，对所有信仰的人开放。

例句：Their approach to spirituality is non-doctrinal and emphasizes personal experience.  他们对灵性的方法是非教条的，强调个人体验。

近义词：nondogmatic, undogmatic, non-prescriptive
反义词：doctrinal, dogmatic, orthodox
词性：adjective
