overdesigned:/ˌoʊvərdiˈzaɪnd/
adjective
过度设计的
Having too many features or details, resulting in a product or system that is unnecessarily complex or difficult to use.
The software is overdesigned and confusing for new users. 这个软件设计过度，让新用户感到困惑。
overdesigned:/ˌoʊvərdiˈzaɪnd/
adjective
过于繁复的
Characterized by excessive or unnecessary complexity in design.
The website's interface is overdesigned and cluttered.  这个网站的界面设计过于繁复且杂乱。

overdesigned:/ˌoʊvərdiˈzaɪnd/
adjective
华而不实的
Designed with an excessive focus on style or features at the expense of practicality.
The car's interior is overdesigned and impractical. 这辆车的内饰华而不实。


overdesigned:/ˌoʊvərdiˈzaɪnd/
adjective
设计过度的
Designed with more features or complexity than is necessary or desirable.
The new phone is overdesigned, with features that most users will never use.  这款新手机设计过度，有很多功能大多数用户永远都不会用到。



overdesigned:/ˌoʊvərdiˈzaɪnd/
adjective
过分设计的
Designed beyond what is necessary or appropriate; excessively elaborate or intricate.
The watch was overdesigned, making it difficult to read the time. 这款手表设计过分，使得读取时间变得困难。
