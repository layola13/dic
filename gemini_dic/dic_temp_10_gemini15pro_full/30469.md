centrifuges:/ˈsɛntrɪˌfjuːdʒɪz/
noun
离心机
A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.
The lab uses centrifuges to separate blood components.  实验室使用离心机分离血液成分。
synonyms: separator, extractor
antonyms: integrator, mixer
n.


centrifuge:/ˈsɛntrɪˌfjuːdʒ/
noun
离心机
A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.
The lab uses a centrifuge to separate blood components. 实验室使用离心机分离血液成分。
synonyms: separator, extractor
antonyms: integrator, mixer
n.


centrifuge:/ˈsɛntrɪˌfjuːdʒ/
verb
离心分离
To rotate something at high speed in a centrifuge to separate its components.
They centrifuged the blood samples to isolate the plasma. 他们对血液样本进行离心分离以提取血浆。
synonyms: spin, whirl, rotate
antonyms: combine, mix, integrate
v.
