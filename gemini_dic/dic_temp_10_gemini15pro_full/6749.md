analyzabilities:/ˌænəˌlaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |可分析性(复数) | The plural form of "analyzability," referring to the capacity of something to be analyzed or the qualities that make something amenable to analysis.  This word acknowledges that different things might have different levels or types of analyzability. | The analyzabilities of these different data sets will determine the appropriate statistical methods. 这些不同数据集的可分析性将决定合适的统计方法。 |The analyzabilities of the various market segments need to be considered. 需要考虑各个细分市场的可分析性。 |  analyzability,  amenability to analysis | resistance to analysis | noun
