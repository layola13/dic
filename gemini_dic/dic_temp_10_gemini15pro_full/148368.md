overspend: /ˌoʊvərˈspend/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈspend/ (UK)
verb
过度消费，超支
To spend more than one has budgeted or can afford.
She overspent on her credit card last month and now has a large bill to pay. 她上个月信用卡透支了，现在有一大笔账单要付。
He tends to overspend when he goes on vacation. 他度假时往往会超支。
近义词: splurge, squander
反义词: save, budget
词性: verb
