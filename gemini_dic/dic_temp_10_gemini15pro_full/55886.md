divisiveness:/dɪˈvaɪsɪvnəs/| n.| 分裂性，争议性 |The divisiveness of the issue threatened to split the party. 这个问题的争议性有可能分裂该党。| The political climate was marked by a high degree of divisiveness. 政治气氛的特点是高度分裂。|近义词：discord, contention, disunity, polarization|反义词：unity, harmony, agreement, consensus|词性: noun
Etymology: From divisive + -ness.  "Divisive" comes from the Latin "dividere" (to divide) + "-ive" (forming adjectives).
