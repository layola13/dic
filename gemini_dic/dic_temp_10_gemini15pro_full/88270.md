hinr:/hɪnər/ | n. | 提示, 线索 | A slight or indirect indication or suggestion. |  The detective followed the hinr that led him to the culprit. 侦探顺着线索找到了罪犯。 | 近义词: clue, hint, tip, indication, suggestion | 反义词:  information, proof, evidence | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a misspelling or variant of "hint."  "Hint" comes from Old English *hentan "to seize," related to Old Norse henda "to seize, grasp." The meaning shifted from "seizing" to "alluding to" and "suggesting."
