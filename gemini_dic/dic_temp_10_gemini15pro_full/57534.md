drainable:/ˈdreɪnəbl/
adjective
可排水的，可放干的
Able to be drained; capable of having liquid removed.
The sink is drainable, so you can easily clean it. 水槽可以排水，所以你可以很容易地清洗它。
The field is drainable, which is essential for growing crops. 这块田地可以排水，这对种植作物至关重要。
近义词:emptyable, exhaustible
反义词:undrainable, non-drainable
词性:adjective

词源:
From drain (v.) + -able, from Old English *drēhnian "to make dry," related to dry.
