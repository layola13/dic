diabetes mellitus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ˈmɛlɪtəs/ | n. | 糖尿病 | A group of metabolic disorders characterized by hyperglycemia resulting from defects in insulin secretion, insulin action, or both.  It is a chronic condition affecting how your body turns food into energy.|  My grandfather has diabetes mellitus and has to monitor his blood sugar levels carefully. 我的祖父患有糖尿病，必须仔细监测他的血糖水平。 | synonyms: diabetes, sugar diabetes | antonyms: normoglycemia (正常血糖) | noun
