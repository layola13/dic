circumambulate: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbjʊleɪt/ /ˌsɜːrkəˈmæmbjəleɪt/
| v. | 绕行，环行 | To walk around something, especially a sacred object or place, as part of a ritual. |  Pilgrims circumambulate the Kaaba in Mecca. 朝圣者绕麦加的天房克尔白环行。|
| v. | |  The tourists circumambulated the ancient monument. 游客们绕着古老的纪念碑走了一圈。 |
|Synonym:|walk around, circle, orbit,绕行|
|Antonym:|stand still, stay put, remain, 静止不动|
|verb|
