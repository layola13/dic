felicitousnesses:/fɪˌlɪˈsɪtəsnəsɪz/
noun
幸福，恰当，巧妙
the quality or state of being felicitous; appropriateness; aptness.
Her felicitousness of expression made her poems a joy to read. 她恰如其分的表达使她的诗歌读起来令人愉悦。
Synonyms: aptness, appropriateness, suitability, fittingness
Antonyms: inappropriateness, unsuitability, infelicity
