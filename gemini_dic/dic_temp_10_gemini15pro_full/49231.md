delegator:/ˈdɛlɪɡeɪtər/| n. |委托人，授权人|A person who delegates a task or responsibility to another.  A person who gives someone else the authority to represent them or act on their behalf.| The delegator must ensure the delegatee understands the task fully. 委托人必须确保被委托人完全理解任务。| synonyms: representative, envoy, commissioner, agent, deputy, proxy | antonyms: recipient, receiver, assignee | noun

delegator:/ˈdɛləˌɡeɪtər/| n. |代表，会议代表|A person sent or authorized to represent others, in particular an elected representative sent to a conference.|  She was a delegator at the national convention. 她是全国代表大会的代表。| synonyms: representative, delegate, envoy, emissary | antonyms: constituent | noun
