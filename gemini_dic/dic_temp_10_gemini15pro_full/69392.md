feeder:/ˈfiːdər/| n.|喂食器；饲养者；供给者；支线；进给器|A device or person that supplies food or other materials to something or someone.|The bird feeder was empty.  鸟食槽空了。|The farmer is a feeder of cattle.  这位农民是养牛的人。|Synonyms: supplier, provider, nourisher|Antonyms: consumer, user, taker|noun

feeder:/ˈfiːdər/| adj.|支线的；馈送的|Relating to or functioning as a feeder.|This is a feeder road to the highway. 这是一条通往高速公路的支线。|The feeder pipe was blocked.  供给管堵塞了。|Synonyms: tributary, subsidiary, auxiliary|Antonyms: main, primary, principal|adjective
