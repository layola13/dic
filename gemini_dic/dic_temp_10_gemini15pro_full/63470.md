entropic:/ɛnˈtrɒpɪk/ |adj.|熵的；混乱的；无序的|Relating to entropy; tending towards disorder or randomness.|例句：The entropic nature of the universe suggests that everything eventually decays. 宇宙的熵性表明一切最终都会衰败。

例句：The data showed an entropic trend, with increasing randomness over time. 数据显示出一种熵趋势，随着时间的推移随机性增加。|近义词：chaotic, disordered, random|反义词：ordered, organized, structured|词性: 形容词
