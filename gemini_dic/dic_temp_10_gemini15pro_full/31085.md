champer:/ˈtʃæmpər/| v. | 咬；嚼；鸣响；渴望 | To bite or chew on something noisily; to make a champing sound, often with the bit of a bridle; to show impatient eagerness. | The horse champered at the bit, eager to start the race.  这匹马嚼着马衔，急于开始比赛。| 近义词：chomp, munch, gnaw, crave | 反义词: release | 词性: 动词

champer:/ˈtʃæmpər/| n. |  (动物)咬；嚼；(马嚼马衔的) 鸣响；渴望 | The act of champing or chewing noisily; a champing sound. | The champer of the horse's bit echoed in the stable. 马嚼马衔的声音在马厩里回荡。| 近义词：chomping, munching, gnawing | 反义词: silence | 词性: 名词
