nonmaritally:/ˌnɑːnˈmærɪtəli/
adverb
非婚地，未婚地
outside of marriage; in an unmarried state.
She lived with him nonmaritally for five years before they decided to get married.  她和他在结婚前同居了五年。
Synonym:  out of wedlock
Antonym: maritally
词性: 副词
