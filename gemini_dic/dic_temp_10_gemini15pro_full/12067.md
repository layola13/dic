artificialities:/ˌɑːrtɪfɪʃiˈælɪtiz/ | n. | 人工制品；人为事物；矫揉造作；不自然 |  The quality of being produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally; affected or insincere behavior. | The painting was spoiled by the artificialities of the composition. 这幅画由于构图的矫揉造作而被破坏了。 |  artificialness, affectation, pretense, contrivance | naturalness, genuineness, sincerity, authenticity | noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *artificialitas, from Latin artificialis (“made by art”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
