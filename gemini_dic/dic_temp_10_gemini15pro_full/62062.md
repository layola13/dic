encashed:/ɪnˈkæʃt/
verb
兑现，变现
to exchange a check, bond, etc. for cash
He encashed his savings bonds to pay for the trip. 他兑现了他的储蓄债券来支付旅行费用。
synonyms: cash, redeem, convert
antonyms: deposit, invest
