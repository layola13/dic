dwindle:/ˈdwɪndl/| vi.|减少，缩小，衰落|To become steadily less; shrink.  To decrease gradually in size, amount, or strength.|例句：The town's population has dwindled to a few hundred.  这个镇的人口已经减少到几百人。|例句：Her hopes of winning dwindled as the race went on.  随着比赛的进行，她获胜的希望越来越渺茫。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, decline, lessen|反义词：increase, grow, expand, rise|词性:动词
