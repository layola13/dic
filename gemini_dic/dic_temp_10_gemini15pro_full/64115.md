eps:/iːpiːˈɛs/| n. |每股收益(Earnings Per Share)|Earnings per share is the portion of a company's profit allocated to each outstanding share of common stock.  |The company reported EPS of $2.50 for the quarter.  该公司本季度每股收益为2.50美元。|synonyms: earnings per share, profit per share|antonyms: N/A|noun
