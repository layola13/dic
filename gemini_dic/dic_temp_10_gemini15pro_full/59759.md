economies:/ɪˈkɑːnəmiz/ /iˈkɒnəmiz/| n.|经济；节约；经济体|The state of a country or region in terms of the production and consumption of goods and services and the supply of money.  A system of production and exchange.|例句：The global economy is facing a downturn. 全球经济正面临衰退。|近义词：finances, resources, thrift, frugality|反义词: extravagance, waste, profusion|noun

economies:/ɪˈkɑːnəmiz/ /iˈkɒnəmiz/| n.|节俭；节省|Careful management of available resources.|例句：He always practices economies in his household. 他在家总是很节俭。|近义词：saving, thriftiness, frugality|反义词: extravagance, waste|noun
