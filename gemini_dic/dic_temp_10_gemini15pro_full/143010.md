outbent:/ˌaʊtˈbɛnt/| adj.|向外弯曲的；极其弯曲的|Curved or bent outward; extremely bent.|The branches of the old tree were outbent under the weight of the snow. 老树的树枝在雪的重压下向外弯曲。|The outbent legs of the chair looked precarious. 椅子向外弯曲的腿看起来很不稳固。|近义词：arched, curved, bowed|反义词:straight, inbound|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"out-" + "bent"  "out-" 表示“向外”，"bent" 是 "bend" 的过去分词，意为“弯曲”。
