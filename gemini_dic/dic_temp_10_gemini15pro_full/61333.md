embattled:/ɪmˈbæt(ə)ld/| adj.|陷入困境的，被围困的|Involved in or beset by a conflict or struggle.|The embattled CEO faced pressure from shareholders to resign.  陷入困境的首席执行官面临股东要求他辞职的压力。|Example: The embattled city held out against the siege for months.  这座被围困的城市抵御了数月的围攻。|Synonyms: besieged, struggling, troubled, under fire, under siege|Antonyms: safe, secure, unchallenged|adjective

embattled:/ɪmˈbæt(ə)ld/| adj.|备受争议的|Subject to much contention or controversy.|The embattled policy was eventually withdrawn. 这项备受争议的政策最终被撤销。|Example:  The embattled mayor faced accusations of corruption.  这位备受争议的市长面临腐败指控。|Synonyms: controversial, disputed, contested|Antonyms: uncontroversial, undisputed, agreed upon|adjective
