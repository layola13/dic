overobvious:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːbviəs/| adj.|极其明显的|Something that is so obvious it is almost annoying.|His attempt to hide his guilt was overobvious. 他试图掩盖自己的罪行，但表现得太过明显了。|Synonyms: transparent, overt, glaring|Antonyms: subtle, nuanced, hidden|adjective
