drivepipe: /ˈdraɪvpaɪp/ | n. | 驱动管，打入管 | A heavy steel pipe driven into the earth to provide a casing for a well or to support foundations. |  The workers used a pile driver to install the drivepipe. 工人们用打桩机安装驱动管。

The drivepipe prevented the well from collapsing. 驱动管防止了井的坍塌。 | 近义词: casing, piling | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "liner" in some contexts if considered as an alternative) | 词性: noun
