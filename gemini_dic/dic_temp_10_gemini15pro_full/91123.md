hyblaean:/hɪˈbleɪən/ | adj. |  海布拉的；西西里的；蜂蜜般甜美的；充满诗意的 |  Relating to Hybla, a mountain or town in ancient Sicily famed for its honey. Also used figuratively to describe something sweet or poetic. | The hyblaean honey was renowned throughout the ancient world.  海布拉蜂蜜在古代世界闻名遐迩。|Synonyms: sweet, mellifluous, lyrical, Sicilian | Antonyms: bitter, harsh, discordant | Adjective
