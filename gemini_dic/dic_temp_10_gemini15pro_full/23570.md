braise:/breɪz/| v. | 焖，炖 | To cook (food) by browning it in fat and then simmering it in a covered pot with a small amount of liquid. |  She braised the beef in red wine for three hours. 她用红酒炖牛肉三个小时。

The short ribs were braised to perfection.  牛小排炖得恰到好处。

| 近义词：stew, simmer, pot-roast | 反义词: fry, roast, grill | 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "braiser," from "braise" (live coals), from Frankish "*brasa," from Proto-Germanic "*brasǭ" (burning embers). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root "*bhreu-" (to boil, bubble, effervesce, burn).
