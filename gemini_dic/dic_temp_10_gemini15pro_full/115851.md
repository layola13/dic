marks:/mɑːrks/ /mɑːrks/| n. |分数；标记；痕迹；特征；标志|A visible impression or stain, a sign or indication of something, a grade or score, a characteristic, a target.|

例句：
He got high marks in all his exams. 他所有考试都得了高分。

The flood left its marks on the furniture. 洪水在家具上留下了痕迹。


近义词：grades, scores, signs, indications, traces, characteristics, symbols, emblems

反义词:blanks (in the context of exams), unmarked


词性:noun


/mɑːrks/ /mɑːrks/| v. |做标记；打分；表明；庆祝|To make a visible impression on something, to grade or score, to indicate or signify, to celebrate or commemorate.|

例句：
Please mark your answers clearly on the answer sheet. 请在答题纸上清楚地标记你的答案。

We marked the occasion with a special dinner. 我们以一顿特别的晚餐来庆祝这个时刻。

近义词：label, score, indicate, denote, celebrate, commemorate

反义词: erase, unmark

词性:verb
