nonsentiently:/nɒnˈsɛnʃəntli/ | adv. | 无感觉地，无知觉地 | In a way that lacks awareness or consciousness; without experiencing sensation or feeling. | The robot arm moved nonsentiently, performing its programmed task.  机器人手臂无意识地移动，执行其编程任务。| The computer nonsentiently processed the data.  计算机无感觉地处理数据。 | 近义词: insensibly, unconsciously, mindlessly | 反义词: consciously, sentiently, feelingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From non- + sentient + -ly.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Sentient means "able to perceive or feel things." -ly is a suffix that forms adverbs.
