ewry:/ˈjuəri/
n.
水罐架，酒器架
A place where ewers and basins are kept; often a recess or cupboard.
The ewry was filled with silver pitchers and basins. 酒器架上摆满了银质的水罐和盆。
近义词:  washstand, basin stand
反义词:  None (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old French *euwier, from ewe (water) + -ier (suffix denoting a place for something). Ultimately from Latin aqua (water).
