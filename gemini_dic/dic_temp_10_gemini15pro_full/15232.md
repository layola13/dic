backfilling:/ˈbækˌfɪlɪŋ/
n.|回填，回填土，回填料|The process of refilling a hole or trench with excavated material or other suitable material.|例句：The backfilling of the trench was completed yesterday. 壕沟的回填昨天完成了。|近义词：refilling, filling|反义词：excavating|词性:名词

backfill:/ˈbækˌfɪl/
v.|回填，回填土，回填料|To refill a hole or trench with excavated material or other suitable material.|例句：They backfilled the hole with gravel. 他们用砾石回填了那个洞。|近义词：refill, fill|反义词：excavate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "back" (meaning behind or returning) + "fill" (meaning to make full).  The term originally referred to filling in a hole or trench after something had been removed or constructed.
