nonliquidating: /ˌnɒnˈlɪkwɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非清算的 | Not involving or requiring liquidation; not converting assets into cash.  A nonliquidating distribution from a partnership is one that does not require the partnership to sell off assets to make the distribution. |  The company made a nonliquidating distribution of stock to its shareholders. 公司向其股东进行了非清算的股票分配。 | The partners received a nonliquidating distribution of profits.  合伙人获得了非清算的利润分配。 | 近义词: non-cash,  | 反义词: liquidating | 词性: 形容词
