overdedication:/ˌoʊvərˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度奉献，过度投入 | Excessive dedication, often to the detriment of other aspects of life. |  Her overdedication to her work led to burnout. 她对工作的过度投入导致了倦怠。 |  His overdedication to the project cost him his health. 他对这个项目的过度投入让他失去了健康。 | 近义词：overcommitment, workaholism | 反义词：balance, moderation | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "dedication" (from Latin "dedicare," meaning "to dedicate")
