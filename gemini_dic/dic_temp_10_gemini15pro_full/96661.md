industrializing:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
工业化
The process of becoming industrial; developing industries in a country or region on a wide scale.
例句：Many developing nations are rapidly industrializing. 许多发展中国家正在迅速工业化。

例句：The government implemented policies to encourage industrializing the rural areas. 政府实施政策鼓励农村地区工业化。

近义词：developing, modernizing, advancing
反义词：deindustrializing, stagnating, regressing
词性：verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From industrialize + -ing.  Industrialize comes from industrial + -ize. Industrial stems from industry + -al.  Industry ultimately derives from the Latin "industria" meaning "diligence, activity."
