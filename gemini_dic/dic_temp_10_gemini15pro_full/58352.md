dtp:/ˌdiː tiː ˈpiː/ | n. | 桌面出版 | Desktop publishing, the production of printed matter by means of a desktop computer system that combines text and graphics. |  Example: DTP software has revolutionized the publishing industry. 桌面出版软件彻底改变了出版业。 | Example: He learned DTP to create his own brochures. 他学习桌面出版来制作自己的宣传册。 | Synonym: electronic publishing, computer typesetting | Antonym: traditional typesetting | noun
