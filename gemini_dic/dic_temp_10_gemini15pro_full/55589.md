disunion:/dɪsˈjuːnjən/
n.
分裂，不团结
lack of unity or agreement; separation
The Civil War was a result of the disunion between the North and the South. 南北战争是南北分裂的结果。

近义词:discord, division, separation, schism
反义词:unity, harmony, concord, agreement
词性:名词

disunite:/ˌdɪsjuˈnaɪt/
v.
使分裂，使不和
to cause to be separate or not united
Political differences can disunite a nation. 政治分歧会使一个国家分裂。

近义词:divide, separate, sever, disjoin
反义词:unite, join, connect, unify
词性:动词


词源:
From Late Latin *disunio, equivalent to dis- dis- + unio union.
