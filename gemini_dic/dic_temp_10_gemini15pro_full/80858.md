gourmandize: /ˈɡɔːrməndaɪz/ /ˈɡʊrməndaɪz/
verb
暴饮暴食；贪吃
To indulge in excessive eating; to eat greedily or to excess.
He gourmandized on the buffet, piling his plate high with every dish. 他在自助餐上大吃特吃，把盘子堆得满满的，每道菜都拿了一些。
She tends to gourmandize when she's stressed.  她压力大的时候容易暴饮暴食。
近义词: overeat, gorge, binge, indulge
反义词: diet, fast, abstain
词性: verb


gourmandize: /ˈɡɔːrməndaɪz/ /ˈɡʊrməndaɪz/
noun
暴饮暴食；贪吃
The act or practice of eating greedily or to excess.
His gourmandize led to significant weight gain.  他的暴饮暴食导致体重显著增加。
The holiday season is a time of gourmandize for many. 对很多人来说，假期是暴饮暴食的时期。
近义词: gluttony, overeating, voracity
反义词: moderation, abstinence
词性: noun

词源: From French *gourmandise*, from *gourmand* "glutton," of uncertain origin.
