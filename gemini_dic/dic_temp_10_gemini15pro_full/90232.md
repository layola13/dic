house:/haʊs/| n. v.|房子；住宅；容纳；收藏|A building for human habitation, especially one that consists of a ground floor and one or more upper storeys.|例句：They bought a new house in the suburbs. 他们在郊区买了一栋新房子。|例句：The museum houses a vast collection of ancient artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的古代文物。|近义词：home, residence, dwelling, abode, accommodate, shelter|反义词:outside, exterior|词性:名词，动词
Etymology: From Middle English hous, from Old English hūs, from Proto-Germanic *hūsaną, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱews- (“to cover, conceal”). Cognate with West Frisian hûs, Dutch huis, German Haus.
