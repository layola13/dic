logoed:/ˈloʊɡoʊd/ | adj. | 印有商标的；带有标志的 | Bearing a logo or other identifying mark. |  例句：She was wearing a logoed T-shirt. 她穿着一件印有商标的T恤。

例句：All employees are required to wear logoed apparel. 所有员工都必须穿着印有公司标志的服装。

近义词：branded, trademarked, imprinted | 反义词：unbranded, plain, unmarked | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Derived from the noun "logo" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  The word "logo" itself comes from Greek "logos" meaning "word, reason, speech."
