attune:/əˈtjuːn/| v. |使协调，使和谐；使合调|To bring into harmony or agreement; to adjust or accustom. |The musician attuned the strings of his guitar. 音乐家调了他的吉他弦。|近义词：harmonize, adjust, adapt, acclimate|反义词：disharmonize, mismatch|verb

attune:/əˈtjuːn/| adj. |协调的，和谐的 (罕用) |In harmony; adjusted or accustomed. |Although rare as an adjective, one might say "an attuned ear" for someone with a good sense of musical pitch. 虽然作为形容词很少见，但人们可能会用“attuned ear”来形容一个对音高有良好感觉的人。|近义词：harmonious, adjusted, adapted, acclimated|反义词：disharmonious, mismatched|adjective
