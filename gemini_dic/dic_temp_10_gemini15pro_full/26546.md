cabinetries:/ˈkæbɪnətriz/
n.
橱柜; 橱柜制造业
The quality of the kitchen cabinetry is excellent. 厨房橱柜的质量非常好。
The company specializes in custom cabinetry. 这家公司专门定制橱柜。
Synonyms: cabinets, cupboards
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the physical object, perhaps "open shelving" could be considered an opposite concept)
noun


Etymology:
From cabinet + -ry.  Ultimately from Old North French *cabinet, diminutive of cabane "cabin".
