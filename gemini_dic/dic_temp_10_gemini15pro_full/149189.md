oxygenate:/ˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/ | v. | 充氧，氧化 | To supply, treat, or combine with oxygen. |  The blood is oxygenated in the lungs. 血液在肺部被充氧。

The machine oxygenates the patient's blood. 这台机器给病人的血液充氧。


|近义词: aerate, oxidize |反义词: deoxygenate |词性: verb

oxygenate:/ˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/ | n. | 含氧化合物 | A substance that contains oxygen. |  This oxygenate is used as an additive in gasoline. 这种含氧化合物用作汽油添加剂。

Many oxygenates are used in industrial processes. 许多含氧化合物用于工业过程。

|近义词: oxide |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider reducing agents) |词性: noun
