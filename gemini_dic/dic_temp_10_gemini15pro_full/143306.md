outduel:/ˌaʊtˈduːəl/
verb
战胜，击败（尤指通过技巧或策略）
To defeat someone in a duel or contest, especially by using skill or strategy.
He outdueled his opponent in the final round of the competition. 他在比赛的最后一轮战胜了对手。
近义词: outwit, outmaneuver, defeat, conquer, vanquish
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb

outduel:/ˌaʊtˈduːəl/
verb
比…更胜一筹
To surpass or excel someone in a duel or contest.
She outdueled all the other candidates for the job. 她在求职竞争中胜过所有其他候选人。
近义词: surpass, excel, outdo, outperform
反义词: be outdone by, be surpassed by
词性: verb
