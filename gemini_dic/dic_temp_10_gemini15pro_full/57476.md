draftsmanly:/ˈdræftsmənli/
adj.
绘图员的；精于制图的；一丝不苟的
like a draftsman; meticulous and precise.
His work is very draftsmanly, with clean lines and precise details. 他的作品很有绘图员的风范，线条清晰，细节精准。
His draftsmanly skills were evident in the detailed blueprints. 他精湛的绘图技巧在详细的蓝图中显而易见。
近义词: meticulous, precise, accurate, careful, painstaking
反义词: careless, sloppy, inaccurate, imprecise
词性: adjective
词源: draftsman + -ly.  From draftsman (a person who makes drawings, especially technical drawings) + -ly (forming adjectives). Ultimately from draft (a preliminary version of a piece of writing) + man.
