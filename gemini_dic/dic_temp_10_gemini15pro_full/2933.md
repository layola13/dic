advocates:/ˈædvəkɪts/| n.|拥护者，提倡者|A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.  Someone who pleads the cause of another, especially in a court of law.|She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。

The lawyer advocated for his client's release. 律师主张释放他的客户。|近义词：supporter, proponent, champion, backer|反义词：opponent, critic, adversary|词性:noun


advocates:/ˈædvəkeɪts/| v.|提倡，主张，拥护|Publicly recommend or support.|He advocates for stricter gun control laws. 他主张更严格的枪支管制法。

She advocated a cautious approach. 她主张采取谨慎的态度。|近义词：support, promote, champion, endorse|反义词：oppose, condemn, denounce|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French avocat, based on Latin advocatus, past participle of advocare ‘to summon’, from ad- ‘to’ + vocare ‘to call’.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advocates for free speech. = 他倡导言论自由。
She advocates a healthy lifestyle. = 她提倡健康的生活方式。

一般过去时:
The group advocated for change in the past. = 该组织过去曾倡导变革。
They advocated a different approach to the problem. = 他们主张用不同的方法来解决这个问题。

现在完成时:
He has advocated for this policy for years. = 多年来，他一直倡导这项政策。
She has advocated the use of renewable energy. = 她一直提倡使用可再生能源。

过去完成时:
They had advocated for this reform before the new law was passed. = 在新法律通过之前，他们就已经倡导这项改革了。
He had advocated a more aggressive strategy before the crisis. = 在危机爆发之前，他一直主张采取更积极的策略。

将来时:
The organization will advocate for improved working conditions. = 该组织将倡导改善工作条件。
She will advocate the importance of education. = 她将倡导教育的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If I were in charge, I would advocate for a different approach. = 如果我负责，我会提倡一种不同的方法。
I wish they would advocate more strongly for their rights. = 我希望他们能更强烈地维护自己的权利。


被动语态:
A new policy was advocated by the committee. = 委员会提倡一项新政策。
Further research is being advocated. = 正在提倡进一步的研究。


疑问句:
Do you advocate for animal rights? = 你支持动物权利吗？
What changes do you advocate? = 你提倡哪些改变？


否定句:
I do not advocate violence. = 我不提倡暴力。
They did not advocate for this change. = 他们没有提倡这种改变。


PHRASE->
advocate for = 为...辩护/提倡
advocate change = 提倡变革
a strong advocate = 坚定的拥护者
a vocal advocate = 直言不讳的拥护者
advocate a policy = 提倡一项政策
