outarm: /ˌaʊtˈɑːrm/
verb
胜过；制服；以更长的武器胜过
to defeat or subdue (someone) by having a longer reach or more powerful weapons.
The knight outarmed his opponent with his longer lance.  骑士用他更长的长矛击败了他的对手。
The larger army outarmed the smaller rebel force. 更大的军队用更强大的武器制服了较小的叛军。
近义词: overpower, subdue, conquer, vanquish
反义词: yield, surrender, submit
词性: verb

outarm: /ˌaʊtˈɑːrm/
noun (rare)
（比喻）优势；长处
(figurative) An advantage or superiority.
His experience gave him an outarm in the negotiations.  他的经验使他在谈判中占了上风。
Her education was a significant outarm in the job market. 她的教育背景在就业市场上是一个显著的优势。
近义词: advantage, edge, upper hand
反义词: disadvantage, handicap
词性: noun
