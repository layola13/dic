forceps:/ˈfɔːrsɛps/| n. |镊子；钳子|A hinged instrument used for grasping and holding objects, especially in surgery or childbirth.|The surgeon used forceps to remove the splinter.  外科医生用镊子取出了碎片。|The baby was delivered with the help of forceps.  婴儿是用镊子接生的。|synonyms: tongs, pincers, pliers|antonyms: N/A|noun
