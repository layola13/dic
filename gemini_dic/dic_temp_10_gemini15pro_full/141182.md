ooidal:/oʊˈɔɪdəl/| adj.|卵形的，卵石状的|Resembling or consisting of ooids; composed of small, round grains resembling fish eggs.|The ooidal limestone formed in shallow, warm waters.|近义词：spherical, granular, rounded|反义词:angular, irregular|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From oo- + -idal.  Oo- comes from Greek *ōión* "egg".  -idal is a suffix meaning "like" or "pertaining to".
