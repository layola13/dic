ostrogothic:/ˌɒstrəˈɡɒθɪk/ /ˌɑːstroʊˈɡɑːθɪk/
adjective
东哥特式的
Relating to the Ostrogoths, an East Germanic people who established a kingdom in Italy.
The Ostrogothic kingdom was eventually conquered by the Byzantines. 东哥特王国最终被拜占庭人征服。
The church features stunning Ostrogothic architecture. 这座教堂拥有令人惊叹的东哥特式建筑。
近义词:East Germanic
反义词:Visigothic
词性:adjective
