octroi:/ˈɒktrwɑː/ (UK), /ˈɑːktrwɑː/ (US)| n.| 地方税; 入市税; |A tax or duty levied on goods brought into a city or town.|The octroi on imported goods increased the cost of living in the city. 入市税增加了城市的生活成本。|近义词：duty, tariff, levy, tax|反义词: subsidy|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *octroi*, from Old French *octroier* "to grant, concede," from Late Latin *auctorizare* "to authorize," from Latin *auctor* "author."  The sense shifted from the granting of the right to collect the tax to the tax itself.
