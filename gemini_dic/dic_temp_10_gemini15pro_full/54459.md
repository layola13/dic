disintegrable:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡrəbl/
adjective
可分解的，可瓦解的
Capable of being disintegrated; breaking apart into smaller pieces or components.
The tablet is disintegrable in water.  这种药片在水中可分解。
disintegrable packaging  可降解包装
synonyms: dissolvable, decomposable, breakable, fragile
antonyms: indestructible, unbreakable, indivisible
词性: adjective
