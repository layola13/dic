earthship:/ˈɜːrθʃɪp/
n.
地球船；生态住宅
A type of passive solar house that is made of natural and recycled materials.
Earthships are designed to be off-grid and self-sufficient.
例句：The earthship was built using recycled tires and cans.  这艘地球船是用回收的轮胎和罐头建造的。

例句：Living in an earthship allows for a sustainable lifestyle.  住在地球船里可以实现可持续的生活方式。

近义词：eco-home, sustainable house, off-grid home
反义词: conventional house, traditional house
词性: 名词
