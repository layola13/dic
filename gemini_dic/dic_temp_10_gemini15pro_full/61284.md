embalmers:/ɪmˈbɑːmərz/| n.|防腐员，尸体防腐师|A person who embalms corpses, especially as a profession.|例句：The embalmers prepared the body for the funeral. 防腐员为葬礼准备了遗体。

例句：The family hired embalmers to preserve the body for viewing. 家人雇佣了防腐员来保存遗体以便瞻仰。


|近义词：mortician, undertaker|反义词: None|词性:Noun
