boos:/buːz/ /buːs/| n.|嘘声；喝倒彩|A loud sound expressing disapproval or contempt.|The comedian received boos from the audience after his offensive joke.  喜剧演员讲了个冒犯性的笑话后，观众席上传来一片嘘声。|近义词：jeers, catcalls, hisses|反义词：cheers, applause|noun
