miryachit:/ˈmɪriætʃɪt/ | n. | 肌阵挛性跳跃症; 肌阵挛性抽搐; 鬼脸抽搐 | A syndrome found primarily in adolescent females characterized by obsessive compulsive-like behaviors, including excessive blinking, grimacing, shoulder shrugging, sniffing, and throat clearing, often exacerbated by stress. | Example:  She was diagnosed with miryachit after experiencing uncontrollable facial tics. 她被诊断出患有肌阵挛性抽搐，因为她经历了无法控制的面部抽搐。

Example:  Stress seemed to trigger her miryachit symptoms. 压力似乎引发了她的肌阵挛性跳跃症症状。 | Synonym:  tic disorder, involuntary movement disorder | Antonym:  motor control, stillness | noun
