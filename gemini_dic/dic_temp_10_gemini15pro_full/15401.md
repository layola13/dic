backwoodsier:/ˌbækˈwʊdziər/| adj.|更偏僻的，更闭塞的|Comparative form of backwoods: more backwoods.  Meaning even more remote and isolated, characteristic of sparsely populated areas far from cities and towns.|He lives in an even backwoodsier part of the state than I do. 他住在这个州比我住的地方更偏僻的地方。|近义词：remoter, more isolated, more rural|反义词：more urban, more cosmopolitan, more developed|词性:形容词


backwoods:/ˌbækˈwʊdz/| n. adj.|偏远地区，偏僻的|Remote, undeveloped rural areas.|They live in the backwoods of Montana. 他们住在蒙大拿州的偏远地区。 |近义词：hinterland, wilderness, bush|反义词：city, metropolis, urban area|词性:名词,形容词

词源:  From back + wood + -s, originally referring to the wooded areas far from settlements.  The term gained its figurative meaning of "uncultured" or "unsophisticated" later.
