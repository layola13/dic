neocene:/ˈniː.əˌsiːn/ | n. | 新近纪 |  The current geological epoch, proposed as the successor to the Holocene, marked by significant human impact on Earth's geology and ecosystems. | The debate about formally recognizing the Neocene epoch is ongoing.  新近纪是否正式被承认还在争论中。| The effects of climate change are a key feature of the Neocene.  气候变化的影响是新近纪的一个主要特征。 | synonyms: Anthropocene (often used interchangeably) | antonyms: Holocene, Pleistocene | noun
