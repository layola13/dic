immoderacy:/ɪˈmɑːdərəsi/ | n. |  无节制，过度，放纵 | Lack of moderation; excess. |  His immoderacy in eating led to health problems.  他饮食无度导致健康问题。 | 近义词: excess, intemperance, overindulgence | 反义词: moderation, temperance, restraint | 词性: 名词

immoderate:/ɪˈmɑːdərət/ | adj. | 无节制的，过度的，放纵的 | Not moderate; excessive. | The immoderate use of alcohol can be dangerous. 过量饮酒可能很危险。 | 近义词: excessive, extreme, inordinate | 反义词: moderate, reasonable, temperate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin immoderatus, from in- ‘not’ + moderatus (see moderate).
