maquette:/mæˈkɛt/| n.| 模型，草图，原型 |A preliminary model of something, often smaller scale, from which a finished work is produced.  A small model or rough draft of a planned or unfinished work.| The architect presented a maquette of the proposed skyscraper to the city council. 建筑师向市议会展示了拟建摩天大楼的模型。 The sculptor created a maquette in clay before starting on the marble. 雕塑家在开始大理石雕刻之前，先用黏土制作了一个模型。| synonym: model, prototype, mockup, miniature | antonym: finished product | noun
