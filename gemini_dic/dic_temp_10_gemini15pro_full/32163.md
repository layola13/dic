chewy:/ˈtʃuːi/| adj.|耐嚼的，有嚼劲的，粘性的|Something that is chewy requires effort to bite through and chew. |This caramel is very chewy.  这种焦糖很耐嚼。|The steak was tough and chewy. 这块牛排又硬又难嚼。|近义词：gummy, tough, rubbery|反义词:tender, soft, smooth|词性:形容词
Etymology: From chew + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *cēowan.
