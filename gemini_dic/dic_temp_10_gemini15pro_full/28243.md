capsulitis:/kæpsjʊˈlaɪtɪs/
n.|关节囊炎|Inflammation of a capsule, especially the capsule of a joint.|He was diagnosed with adhesive capsulitis in his shoulder. 他被诊断出患有肩关节粘连性关节囊炎。|近义词:frozen shoulder (特指肩周炎), periarthritis (特指关节周围炎)|反义词:N/A (炎症没有确切的反义词)
词性:noun
