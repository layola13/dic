determinability:/dɪˌtɜːrmɪnəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可确定性，可判定性| The quality or state of being determinable; capable of being determined or decided.  |The determinability of the case depends on the evidence.  这个案件的可判定性取决于证据。|Synonyms: decidability, resolvability, ascertainability |Antonyms: indeterminacy, uncertainty| noun
