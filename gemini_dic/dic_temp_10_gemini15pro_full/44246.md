crocused: /ˈkroʊ.kəst/ | adj. | 开满番红花的；像番红花一样的 | Covered with crocuses; resembling a crocus. | The meadow was crocused with purple and white blooms. 草地上开满了紫色和白色的番红花。| The hills were crocused with the first blooms of spring. 山丘上点缀着早春盛开的番红花。 | 近义词: blooming, blossoming | 反义词: barren, desolate | 词性:形容词

词源:  From crocus + -ed.  Crocus comes from the Greek κρόκος (krokos), referring to saffron, a spice derived from a type of crocus flower.
