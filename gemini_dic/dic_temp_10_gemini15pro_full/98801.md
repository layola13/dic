integumentation: /ɪnˌtɛɡjʊˈmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. |外皮形成，外被形成|The process of forming or developing an integument, a natural covering of an organism, such as skin, shell, or rind.  |The integumentation of insects provides protection and support. 昆虫的外皮形成提供了保护和支撑。|近义词：covering, coating, skin formation|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly)  exposure, vulnerability|词性: noun
