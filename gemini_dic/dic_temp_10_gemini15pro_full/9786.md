antsier: /ˈæntsiər/ (comparative adjective) |更焦虑的，更急切的| more anxious or eager | He seemed antsier than usual about the upcoming meeting. 他似乎比平时更焦虑即将到来的会议。|  She was antsier to leave than I was. 她比我更急着离开。|Synonyms: more restless, more uneasy, more impatient| Antonyms: calmer, more relaxed, more patient| adjective


Etymology: From "antsy" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Antsy" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "ants in one's pants."
