heterosis:/ˌhɛtərˈoʊsɪs/ | n. | 杂种优势，杂交优势 | The increased vigor or other superior qualities arising from the crossbreeding of genetically different plants or animals.  | Example 1: The farmer relied on heterosis to improve the yield of his corn crop.  农民依靠杂交优势来提高玉米作物的产量。

Example 2:  Heterosis is a key factor in the success of many modern agricultural practices. 杂交优势是许多现代农业实践成功的关键因素。 | Synonym: hybrid vigor, outbreeding enhancement | Antonym: inbreeding depression | noun
