overemphases:/ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪz/
|n.| 过分强调；过分重视|The act of placing too much importance or emphasis on something.|His overemphasis on the importance of grades worried his parents. 他过分强调成绩的重要性，让他的父母很担心。|overstress, overplay|underemphasize, downplay|noun

overemphasizes:/ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪz/
|v.| 过分强调；过分重视|To place too much importance or emphasis on something.|The media overemphasizes the negative aspects of the story. 媒体过分强调了故事的负面因素。|overstress, overplay|underemphasize, downplay|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + emphasize.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "too much" or "excessively." "Emphasize" comes from the French "emphasiser" and ultimately from the Greek "emphasis" meaning "significance" or "display."
