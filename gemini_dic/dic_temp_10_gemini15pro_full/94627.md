impresario:/ɪmprɛˈsɑːrioʊ/ /ɪmprɛˈsɑːriːəʊ/
n.
演出经理；演出承办人；(尤指)歌剧院、芭蕾舞团等的经理
A person who organizes and often finances concerts, plays, or operas.
The impresario brought many famous singers to our city.  这位演出经理把很多著名的歌手带到了我们的城市。
Synonyms: manager, producer, director, organizer
Antonyms: audience, spectator
