dandily:/ˈdændɪli/| adv.|衣着华丽地，讲究地；潇洒地|In a dandy manner; smartly or stylishly.|He was dressed dandily in a pinstripe suit and a bowler hat. 他穿着细条纹西装，戴着圆顶礼帽，打扮得十分讲究。|Near Synonyms: smartly, stylishly, fashionably, nattily, sprucely|Near Antonyms: sloppily, shabbily, carelessly|词性: 副词

词源: From dandy + -ly.  "Dandy" originated in the late 18th century, possibly from a Scottish pet form of Andrew or a diminutive of "Jack-a-dandy," a figure in children's rhymes.
