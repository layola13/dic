adventitiously: /ˌædvenˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
偶然地；意外地
Happening or appearing by chance; accidental.
The plant adventitiously took root in a crack in the wall.  这种植物偶然地在墙上的裂缝中扎了根。

The family adventitiously discovered a hidden room in their new house.  这家人意外地在他们的新房子里发现了一个密室。

近义词: accidentally, incidentally, unexpectedly, fortuitously
反义词: deliberately, intentionally, purposely
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adventitius (“coming from outside”), from adventus (“arrival”), past participle of advenīre (“to arrive”), from ad- (“to”) + venīre (“to come”).

USAGE->
This word is primarily used as an adverb.  Here are some examples in different contexts:

Describing an unexpected event:
The error occurred adventitiously during the experiment. = 这个错误是在实验过程中偶然发生的。
They adventitiously met an old friend while on vacation. = 他们在度假时意外地遇到了一位老朋友。


Describing an unintended consequence:
The drug adventitiously caused a severe allergic reaction. = 这种药物意外地引起了严重的过敏反应。
The construction project adventitiously damaged a nearby historical site. = 这个建筑项目意外地损坏了附近的一个历史遗址。



PHRASE->
While "adventitiously" doesn't typically form set phrases, it can be used with adverbs like "quite" or "purely":

quite adventitiously =  很偶然地
purely adventitiously =  纯粹偶然地
completely adventitiously = 完全偶然地
almost adventitiously = 几乎偶然地
entirely adventitiously = 完全偶然地
