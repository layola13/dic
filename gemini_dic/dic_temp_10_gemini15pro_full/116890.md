mayduke: /ˈmeɪˌdjuːk/ | n. |五月公爵（一种早熟的蓝莓品种）| A variety of blueberry known for its early ripening season. |例句：The mayduke blueberries are ready to pick in early June. 五月公爵蓝莓在六月初就可以采摘了。

例句：Mayduke blueberries are popular for their sweet flavor. 五月公爵蓝莓因其甜美的味道而受欢迎。|近义词：blueberry, earlyblue (other early-season blueberry varieties)|反义词: late-season blueberry (e.g., Elliott)|词性: noun
