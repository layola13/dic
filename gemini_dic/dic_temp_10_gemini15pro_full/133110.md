nonerudite:/ˌnɒnˈɛrʊdaɪt/
adjective
非博学的，不博学的
Not erudite; lacking in scholarly knowledge.
He gave a nonerudite, but heartfelt speech. 他做了一个没有学问的，但发自内心的演讲。
Synonyms: unscholarly, unlearned, ignorant, illiterate, uneducated
Antonyms: erudite, scholarly, learned, knowledgeable, educated
词性: 形容词
