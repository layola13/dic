moonwalking:/ˈmuːnwɔːkɪŋ/ | n. | 月球漫步 | The act of dancing in a way that creates the illusion of walking backwards, as if on the moon. | Michael Jackson's moonwalking was iconic.  迈克尔·杰克逊的太空步是标志性的。| synonyms: gliding, sliding | antonyms: walking, running | noun

moonwalking:/ˈmuːnwɔːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 月球漫步的 | Relating to or resembling the dance move moonwalking. | He gave a moonwalking performance. 他表演了月球漫步。 | synonyms: gliding, sliding | antonyms: walking, running | adjective
