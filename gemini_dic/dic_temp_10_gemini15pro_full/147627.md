overplotting:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːtɪŋ/
n.
过度绘图，重叠绘图
The phenomenon where data points overlap in a visualization, obscuring the underlying distribution.
Many points plotted on top of each other, making it difficult to see the actual density of the data.
The scatter plot suffered from severe overplotting, making it hard to discern any patterns.
overplotting makes it difficult to interpret the true distribution of the data.
overlap, occlusion
clarity, separation
noun
