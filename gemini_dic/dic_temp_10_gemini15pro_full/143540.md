outgushed: /aʊtˈɡʌʃt/
verb
涌出，喷出
(of liquid) to flow out suddenly and forcefully.
Tears outgushed from her eyes.  眼泪从她的眼中涌出。
The blood outgushed from the wound.  鲜血从伤口喷涌而出。
Synonyms: spurt, gush, stream, pour, jet
Antonyms: trickle, seep, dribble
词性: 动词
