nonremedially:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈmiː.di.ə.li/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈmiː.di.ə.li/
adverb
非补救地；不需补救地
Not in a remedial way; in a way that does not require remediation.
Students who pass the placement test are placed nonremedially in freshman English. 通过分班考试的学生将直接进入大学一年级英语课程，无需补习。
Synonyms: directly, straightforwardly, immediately
Antonyms: remedially, correctively
词性: 副词

非补救的；不需补救的
Not requiring remediation.
Nonremedial classes are offered to students who demonstrate proficiency.  为精通的学生提供非补救课程。
Synonyms: advanced, proficient, competent
Antonyms: remedial, basic, elementary
词性: 形容词
