macrodactyl: /ˌmækroʊˈdæktɪl/ | n. | 巨趾动物 | An animal with abnormally large fingers or toes. |  Macrodactyls are often found in specific lemur species.  巨趾动物常见于特定的狐猴物种。 | Synonyms: megapod | Antonyms: brachydactyl | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  *μακροδάκτυλος (makrodáktulos)  meaning “long-fingered,” from  μακρός (makrós, “long”) + δάκτυλος (dáktulos, “finger, toe”).
