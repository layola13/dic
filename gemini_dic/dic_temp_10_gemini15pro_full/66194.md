excise:/ˈɛksaɪz/ (US), /ɪkˈsaɪz/ (UK)| n. vt.|消费税；货物税；切除；删去|A tax levied on certain goods and commodities produced or sold within a country,  also refers to the act of removing something.|例句：The government imposed a new excise on gasoline. 政府对汽油征收了新的消费税。|近义词：tax, duty, levy, remove, delete|反义词:retain, include|n.

excise:/ˈɛksaɪz/ (US), /ɪkˈsaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|切除；删去|To remove something, especially by cutting.|例句：The surgeon excised the tumor. 外科医生切除了肿瘤。|近义词：remove, delete, expunge|反义词:insert, add|v.
