fainest:/ˈfeɪnɪst/ | adj. | 最好的，最优秀的；最乐意的 |  Superlative form of "fain": most pleased or willing; most excellent or desirable. |  She wore her fainest gown to the ball. 她穿着她最漂亮的礼服去参加舞会。|  Synonyms: best, finest, choicest, prime, top-notch | Antonyms: worst, poorest, inferior | adjective

fain:/feɪn/ | adj. | 高兴的；乐意的；不得不 | Pleased or willing under the circumstances; compelled by necessity.  He was fain to admit defeat. 他不得不承认失败。 | Synonyms: willing, glad, happy, pleased, content, obliged, compelled | Antonyms: unwilling, reluctant, disinclined | adjective
