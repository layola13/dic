anti-conservationist:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 反环保主义者；反对自然资源保护的人 | Someone who opposes environmental conservation efforts. |  The anti-conservationist lobby argued against the new national park proposal. 反对自然资源保护的游说团体反对新的国家公园提案。| The anti-conservationist's actions threatened the endangered species.  反环保主义者的行为威胁到濒危物种。 | 近义词: environmental opponent,  opponent of conservation | 反义词: conservationist, environmentalist | 词性: noun

anti-conservationist:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/ | adj. | 反对自然资源保护的 | Opposed to environmental conservation efforts. | The anti-conservationist stance of the company drew criticism. 该公司反对自然资源保护的立场引来了批评。 |  He made some anti-conservationist remarks that angered many people. 他发表了一些反环保的言论，激怒了许多人。 | 近义词: environmentally damaging, ecologically destructive | 反义词: pro-conservation, pro-environment | 词性: adjective


词源：

"anti-" (prefix): from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of"
"conservationist": from "conservation" + "-ist"
"conservation": from Late Latin "conservationem" meaning "preservation, keeping," from Latin "conservare" meaning "to preserve, keep," from "con-" (together) + "servare" (to keep)
"-ist": a suffix forming agent nouns, meaning "one who does or makes."
