child-proofing: /ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːfɪŋ/
| n. |儿童防护，防儿童措施|The act of making something safe for children, by preventing them from accessing dangerous items or situations.| We need to do some serious child-proofing before the baby starts crawling. 我们需要在宝宝开始爬行之前做好一些重要的儿童防护措施。|近义词: child safety, baby-proofing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hazard-creating")|词性: noun


child-proof: /ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/
| adj. |防儿童的，对儿童安全的|Designed to prevent children from accessing or operating something dangerous.|  Is your medicine cabinet child-proof? 你的药柜是防儿童的吗？|近义词: child-resistant, child-safe|反义词: unsafe (for children)|词性: adjective


child-proof: /ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/
| v. |进行儿童防护|To make something safe for children.| We need to child-proof the entire house. 我们需要对整个房子进行儿童防护。|近义词: secure, protect (for children)|反义词: endanger|词性: verb
