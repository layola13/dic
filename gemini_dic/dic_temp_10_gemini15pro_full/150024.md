palpable:/ˈpælpəbl/| adj.|可感知的，明显的，易觉察的|Readily or plainly seen, heard, perceived, etc.; obvious; evident.|The tension in the room was palpable. 房间里的紧张气氛 palpable. |近义词：tangible, perceptible, noticeable, apparent, manifest, distinct, evident, obvious|反义词：imperceptible, intangible, insubstantial|词性:形容词

palpable:/ˈpælpəbl/| adj.|可触知的，可摸到的|(Of a feeling or atmosphere) so intense as to seem almost tangible.|Her excitement was palpable. 她的兴奋 palpable. |近义词：tangible, perceptible, noticeable|反义词：intangible, imperceptible|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin palpabilis "that can be touched," from Latin palpare "to touch gently, stroke," related to palpus "feeler."
