mammiferous:/məˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
哺乳动物的
Bearing or suckling young; characteristic of mammals.
例句：Humans, dogs, and whales are all mammiferous animals. 人类、狗和鲸鱼都是哺乳动物。

例句：The platypus, although it lays eggs, is a mammiferous creature due to its milk production.  鸭嘴兽虽然产卵，但由于产奶，它是一种哺乳动物。

近义词：mammalian
反义词：None (There isn't a direct antonym for "mammiferous")
词性：adjective
