nationalistic:/ˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
民族主义的；国家主义的
Having strong patriotic feelings, especially believing that your own country is better than others.  Characterized by or displaying patriotism, especially of an aggressive or exaggerated kind.
His nationalistic sentiments led him to support protectionist trade policies. 他的民族主义情绪使他支持贸易保护主义政策。
synonyms: patriotic, chauvinistic, jingoistic
antonyms: internationalist, cosmopolitan, globalist
词性: adjective
