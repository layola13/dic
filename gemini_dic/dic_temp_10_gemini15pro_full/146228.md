overexploiting: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度开发，过度利用
exploiting something excessively, beyond its sustainable capacity.
The overexploiting of natural resources is a serious threat to the environment. 过度开发自然资源是对环境的严重威胁。

Fishermen are overexploiting the ocean's fish stocks. 渔民们正在过度捕捞海洋鱼类资源。

近义词: overusing, abusing, depleting
反义词: conserving, preserving, protecting
词性: verb (present participle)


overexploit: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪt/
verb
过度开发，过度利用
to exploit something excessively, beyond its sustainable capacity.
Many companies overexploit their workers by making them work long hours for low pay. 许多公司过度剥削工人，让他们长时间工作却只付低工资。

The mining company was accused of overexploiting the land and polluting the local water supply.  这家矿业公司被指控过度开发土地并污染当地水源。

近义词: overuse, abuse, deplete
反义词: conserve, preserve, protect
词性: verb


词源:
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "exploit" (from Old French "exploitier," meaning "to make use of, to profit from").
