debenture:/dɪˈbɛntʃər/ | n. | 债券；信用债券；无担保债券 | A debenture is a type of bond or other debt instrument that is unsecured by collateral. It is a certificate of agreement of loans that is given under the company’s stamp and carries an undertaking that the debenture holder will get a fixed return (interest) and the principal amount whenever the debenture matures. |  The company issued debentures to raise capital for its expansion project.  该公司发行债券为其扩张项目筹集资金。

The interest rate on the debentures is 8% per annum.  这些债券的年利率为 8%。 |  bond, loan, security | shares, stocks | noun
