orthotopic:/ˌɔːrθəˈtɑːpɪk/| adj.|原位的，同位移植的|Located in the normal or usual place.  Relating to or involving transplantation of tissue or an organ to its normal position in the body.|例句：The surgeon performed an orthotopic liver transplant.  外科医生进行了原位肝移植。|例句：Orthotopic heart transplantation is the standard procedure. 原位心脏移植是标准程序。|近义词：autochthonous, indigenous|反义词：heterotopic|词性:形容词
