nonfragile:/ˌnɒnˈfrædʒaɪl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfrædʒəl/| adj.|非脆弱的，坚固的，抗损的|Not fragile; able to withstand shocks or stress without breaking or being damaged.|This case is made of nonfragile plastic.  这个盒子是由不易碎的塑料制成的。|The servers are designed to be nonfragile and highly available. 这些服务器设计得非常坚固且高度可用。|近义词：robust, resilient, durable, tough, strong|反义词:fragile, brittle, weak, delicate|词性:形容词

词源:
The prefix "non-" means "not."  The word "fragile" comes from the Latin word "fragilis," meaning "breakable."  Therefore, "nonfragile" literally means "not breakable."
