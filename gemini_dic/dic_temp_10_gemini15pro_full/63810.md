epidermeous:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmiəs/| adj.|表皮的，生长于表皮上的|Growing on or relating to the epidermis (the outer layer of skin or other covering).|The epidermeous layer of the plant protected it from the harsh sun.  这种植物的表皮层保护它免受烈日的伤害。|Synonyms: epidermal, cutaneous|Antonyms: subcutaneous, internal|adjective
