off-year:/ˈɔːfˌjɪr/ /ˈɑːfˌjɪr/| n. adj.|非大选年，淡季|A year in which major elections, such as presidential or midterm elections, are not held.  It can also refer to a period of less activity or success than usual.|例句：Voter turnout is typically lower in an off-year election. 非大选年的投票率通常较低。

|近义词：non-election year|反义词：election year|词性:名词，形容词
