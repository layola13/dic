indifferent:/ɪnˈdɪfrənt/| adj.|漠不关心的；无关紧要的；中立的；平庸的|Having or showing no interest or concern; neither good nor bad; neutral; mediocre.|She remained indifferent to his pleas. 她对他的恳求无动于衷。|Synonyms: apathetic, uninterested, unconcerned, neutral, mediocre|Antonyms: interested, concerned, passionate, biased, exceptional|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin indifferentem (nominative indifferens) "not different, impartial," from in- "not" + differentem (nominative differens), present participle of differre "to differ."
