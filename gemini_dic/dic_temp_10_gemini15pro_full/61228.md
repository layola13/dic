emaciation:/ɪˌmeɪʃiˈeɪʃən/ /ɪˌmeɪsiˈeɪʃn/| n. |消瘦；憔悴；衰弱|The state of being abnormally thin or weak, often because of illness or lack of food.|The emaciation of the prisoners was horrifying.  囚犯们的消瘦令人震惊。|Synonyms: thinness, wasting, atrophy, gauntness, starvation|Antonyms: obesity, plumpness, stoutness|noun


emaciated:/ɪˈmeɪʃiˌeɪtɪd/ /ɪˈmeɪsiˌeɪtɪd/| adj. |消瘦的；憔悴的；衰弱的|Abnormally thin or weak, especially because of illness or lack of food.|The refugees arrived at the camp looking emaciated and exhausted.  难民们到达营地时，看起来消瘦不堪，疲惫至极。|Synonyms: thin, gaunt, skeletal, wasted, haggard|Antonyms: obese, plump, stout, robust|adjective
