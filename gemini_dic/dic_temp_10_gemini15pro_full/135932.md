nonpunishable:/ˌnɒnˈpʌnɪʃəbəl/ | adj. | 不可惩罚的，免受处罚的 | Not subject to or deserving punishment. |  The act, though morally reprehensible, was technically nonpunishable. 虽然这种行为在道德上应受谴责，但严格来说是不可惩罚的。|  His actions were deemed nonpunishable under the current law. 根据现行法律，他的行为被认为是不可惩罚的。 | synonyms:  immune, exempt, unpunished,  | antonyms: punishable, culpable, guilty | adjective
