epicerastic:/ˌɛpɪsəˈræstɪk/ | adj. | 愉悦的，令人高兴的；使人心情舒畅的 |  Relating to or characterized by pleasure or enjoyment; tending to cheer or exhilarate. |  The epicerastic effects of the music lifted everyone's spirits.  音乐令人愉悦的效果振奋了每个人的精神。| Synonyms: cheering, exhilarating, gladdening, joyous, pleasant | Antonyms: depressing, saddening, gloomy, disheartening | adjective

Etymology: From Greek *epikērastos* "delightful," from *epi-* "upon" + *kērastos* "mixed," from *kerannysthai* "to mix." The original sense likely referred to a well-blended or harmonious mixture, hence pleasant.
