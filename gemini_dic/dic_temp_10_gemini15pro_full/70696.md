finally:/ˈfaɪnəli/| adv.|最后，终于|After a long wait, we finally received our visas.  经过漫长的等待，我们终于拿到了签证。|例句：Finally, the rain stopped.  最后，雨停了。|近义词：eventually, ultimately, at last|反义词：initially, at first|词性:副词

Etymology: From Middle English *finalie, from Old French *finalment, from Latin fīnāliter, from fīnālis (“final”).
