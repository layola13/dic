hordock:/ˈhɔːrdɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrdɑːk/ (US)| n. |牛蒡|A biennial plant of the daisy family, with large leaves and prickly heads of purplish flowers. The long taproot of the common burdock (Arctium lappa) is edible, and the plant has medicinal properties.|The boy picked a hordock burr and threw it at his sister.  男孩摘了一朵牛蒡毛刺，扔向了他的妹妹。|burdock, cocklebur, burr| |noun
English explanation: Hordock is a variant spelling of burdock, a robust biennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family (daisy family).  It's characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and prickly flower heads that are typically purplish in color. The plant is known for its edible taproot and has a history of use in traditional medicine.

近义词: burdock (most common and preferred), cocklebur, burr
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant. Could consider antonyms for its qualities, e.g., smooth (opposite of prickly))
词性: noun
