coccosphere:/ˈkɒkəsfɪə(r)/ | n. | 球石藻; 球状颗石藻;  | A single-celled planktonic alga (belonging to the coccolithophores) covered with small calcareous plates called coccoliths. |  Coccolithophores, including their coccosphere structures, play a significant role in the ocean's carbon cycle. 球石藻，包括它们的球石结构，在海洋碳循环中起着重要作用。 |  Coccospheres can be observed under a microscope.  可以在显微镜下观察球石藻。 | synonyms: coccolithophore (sometimes used interchangeably, though technically the organism itself, not just the sphere), phytoplankton | antonyms:  N/A (lacks a direct opposite) | noun
