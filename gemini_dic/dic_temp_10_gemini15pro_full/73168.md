forbiddance:/fərˈbɪdəns/| n. |禁止，禁令|The act of forbidding something; prohibition.|The forbiddance of smoking in public places is becoming more common.  公共场所禁止吸烟越来越普遍。|近义词：prohibition, ban, interdiction|反义词：permission, allowance|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *forbedence, from Old French forbedeance, from forbeder "to forbid," from Latin forbedēre, from for- "away, off" + bedēre "to bid, command."
