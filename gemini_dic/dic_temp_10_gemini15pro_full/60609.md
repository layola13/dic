elderberry:/ˈɛldərˌbɛri/ (US), /ˈɛldəˌbɛri/ (UK)| n.|接骨木果，接骨木浆果|A small, dark purple berry that grows in clusters on elderberry bushes.  These berries are often used to make jams, wines, and other food products, and are also believed to have medicinal properties.|Elderberry wine is quite popular in some regions. 接骨木酒在某些地区很受欢迎。|Synonyms: elder, black elder, European elder|Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a type of berry)|noun

elderberry:/ˈɛldərˌbɛri/ (US), /ˈɛldəˌbɛri/ (UK)| adj.|接骨木的|Made from or relating to elderberries.|She made a delicious elderberry pie. 她做了一个美味的接骨木派。|Synonyms: elder|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
