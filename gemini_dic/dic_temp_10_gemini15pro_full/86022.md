healer:/ˈhiːlər/ | n. |治疗者，治愈者|A person who heals or attempts to heal, especially by non-scientific methods. |例句：The healer used herbs and massage to treat the patient.  治疗师用草药和按摩来治疗病人。

例句：Many people sought the healer's advice on spiritual matters. 很多人在精神问题上寻求治疗师的建议。

近义词：therapist, doctor, physician, shaman, medicine man/woman |反义词：none (no direct antonym, could consider 'harmer' or 'injurer' in some contexts) |词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English helere, from Old English hǣlere (“healer, savior”), from Proto-Germanic *hailjaną (“to heal”), from Proto-Indo-European *kail- (“healthy, whole, uninjured”).  Cognate with German Heiler (“healer”), Dutch heler (“healer”), Gothic 𐌷𐌰𐌹𐌻𐌾𐌰𐌽 (hailjan, “to heal”).
