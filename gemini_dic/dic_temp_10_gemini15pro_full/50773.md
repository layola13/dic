descale:/diːˈskeɪl/ /dɪˈskeɪl/| v. |除垢，去除水垢；降低…的规模|To remove scale or limescale from something, like a kettle or pipe.  To reduce the size or scope of something, especially a business or industry. |例句：I need to descale the kettle. 我需要给水壶除垢。

例句：The company was forced to descale its operations due to the economic downturn. 由于经济衰退，公司被迫缩减业务规模。|近义词：clean, remove, reduce, downsize|反义词:scale up, enlarge, expand|词性：动词
