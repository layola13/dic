frolickers:/ˈfrɒlɪkəz/ /ˈfrɑːlɪkərz/ | n. |嬉戏者，玩耍者 | People who are playing and having fun in a lively, carefree way.  Those engaged in frolicking. | The beach was full of frolickers enjoying the sunshine.  沙滩上满是享受阳光的嬉戏者。

The park was alive with the sound of children frolicking. 公园里充满了孩子们嬉戏的声音。 | 近义词:revellers, merrymakers, partygoers | 反义词:mourners, grievers | 词性:Noun (plural)

Etymology: From the verb "frolic," which comes from the Middle Dutch "vrolyc" meaning "joyful, merry."
