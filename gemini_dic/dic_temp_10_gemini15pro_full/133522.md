nonfibrous:/nɒnˈfaɪbrəs/
adjective
非纤维的；无纤维的
Not composed of or resembling fibers.
Cartilage is a nonfibrous connective tissue. 软骨是一种非纤维结缔组织。
The tumor was described as nonfibrous and dense.  肿瘤被描述为无纤维且致密的。
non-fibrous, nonfibrillous
fibrous, filamentous
