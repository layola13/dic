fuzed:/fjuːzd/| adj.|熔化的，熔合的|Melted or joined together by heat; fused.|例句：The two pieces of metal were fuzed together. 这两块金属熔合在一起了。|例句：The electrical circuit fuzed, causing a power outage. 电路熔断了，导致停电。|近义词：fused, melted, blended, merged|反义词：separated, divided|词性:形容词
fuse:/fjuːz/| n. v.|保险丝，熔断器；熔合|A safety device that melts and breaks an electric circuit if the current becomes too strong; to melt or join together by heat.|
词源: From Latin *fūsus*, past participle of *fundere* "to pour, melt".
