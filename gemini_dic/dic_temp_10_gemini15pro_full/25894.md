burlingtons:/ˈbɜːrlɪŋtənz/
n.
Burlington商店; Burlington品牌服装; Burlington（地名）
A chain of department stores in the United States known for discounted clothing and home goods. Often used as shorthand for "Burlington Coat Factory."  Can also refer to places named Burlington.
I bought this coat at Burlingtons. 我在这家Burlington商店买的这件外套。
The Burlingtons in my town just got a new shipment of shoes.  我们镇上的Burlington商店刚进了一批新鞋。
近义词:Burlington Coat Factory, department store, retailer
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
