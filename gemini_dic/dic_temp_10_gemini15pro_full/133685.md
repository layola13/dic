nonfraudulently:/ˌnɑːnˌfrɔːdʒələntli/
adverb
非欺诈地
In a manner that is not fraudulent; honestly.
He conducted his business affairs nonfraudulently. 他以非欺诈的方式处理他的业务。
She reported her income nonfraudulently. 她如实申报了她的收入。
Synonym: honestly, legitimately, truthfully, aboveboard
Antonym: fraudulently, deceitfully, dishonestly, corruptly
词性: 副词
