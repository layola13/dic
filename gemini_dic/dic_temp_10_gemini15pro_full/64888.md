essayistical:/ˌɛs eɪˈɪstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
散文式的；随笔体的
characteristic of an essay; resembling or suggestive of an essay in style or form.
Her writing style is quite essayistical, full of personal anecdotes and reflections. 她的写作风格很像散文，充满了个人轶事和思考。
近义词: discursive, rambling, digressive
反义词: concise, succinct
词性: adjective

词源: From essay + -istic + -al.  Ultimately from the French essai "attempt".
