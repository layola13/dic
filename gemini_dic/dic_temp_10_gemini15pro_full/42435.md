counterdeclarations:/ˈkɑʊntərˌdekləˈreɪʃənz/| n. |反声明，反宣言，对抗性声明|Statements made in opposition to previous declarations; formal denials or contradictions of something previously declared.|The government issued counterdeclarations to the accusations of corruption. 政府发表了反声明，驳斥腐败指控。|The company released counterdeclarations against the claims made by the former employee. 公司发布了反声明，驳斥前雇员提出的索赔。|synonyms: rebuttals, denials, contradictions|antonyms: affirmations, confirmations, corroborations|noun
