digestedness:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪdnəs/
n.|消化率，可消化性|The degree to which a food is digested and absorbed by the body.  指食物被身体消化和吸收的程度。|The digestedness of this protein is very high. 这种蛋白质的消化率很高。|近义词: digestibility|反义词: indigestibility|词性: 名词
Etymology: From digest + -ed + -ness.  来自 digest (消化) + -ed (动词过去分词后缀) + -ness (名词后缀，表示状态或性质).
