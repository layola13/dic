fugue:/fjuːɡ/| n.|赋格曲；神游症；精神错乱|A musical form in which a theme or subject is introduced by one voice or instrument and is imitated by others, each in turn.|The composer's last fugue is considered his masterpiece. 作曲家最后的赋格曲被认为是他的杰作。|Synonyms: canon, round|Antonyms: discord, dissonance|noun

fugue:/fjuːɡ/| n.|神游；精神恍惚|A state of altered consciousness in which a person performs acts they do not later remember, often involving travel or relocation.|After the accident, he experienced a fugue state and wandered off. 事故发生后，他出现了神游状态，四处游荡。|Synonyms: amnesia, blackout|Antonyms: awareness, lucidity|noun
