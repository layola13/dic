dendrochronologists:/ˌdɛndroʊkrəˈnɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
树木年代学家
Scientists who study tree rings to determine the age of trees and past climatic conditions.
Dendrochronologists can determine the age of a tree by counting its rings. 树木年代学家可以通过计算年轮来确定树的年龄。
The dendrochronologists dated the ancient ruins using wood samples. 树木年代学家使用木材样本确定了古代遗址的年代。
近义词: tree-ring analysts, tree-ring daters
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps archaeologist if contrasting dating methods)
词性: Noun (plural)
