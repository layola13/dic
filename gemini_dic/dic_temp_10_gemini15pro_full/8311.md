anti-oxygenation:/ˌæntiˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ | n. |抗氧化，抗氧化作用|The process of preventing or slowing down oxidation, especially in the body or in food.|Anti-oxygenation is crucial for maintaining cell health. 抗氧化对于维持细胞健康至关重要.|Synonyms: antioxidation|Antonyms: oxidation|noun

anti-oxygenation:/ˌæntiˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ | adj. |抗氧化的|Relating to or characterized by anti-oxygenation.|The anti-oxygenation properties of vitamin C are well-known.  维生素C的抗氧化特性是众所周知的。|Synonyms: antioxidant|Antonyms: pro-oxidant|adjective
