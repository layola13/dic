braai:/brɑːɪ/ (South African), /ˈbraɪər/ (elsewhere)
n.|烤肉架，烧烤野餐|A metal barbecue, typically a cylindrical container on legs, used for cooking food over an open fire.|We had a braai in the garden last weekend. 我们上周末在花园里烤肉。|近义词：barbecue, grill|反义词:|名词

braai:/brɑːɪ/ (South African), /ˈbraɪər/ (elsewhere)
v.|烧烤|To cook food on a braai.|We braaied some steaks and sausages. 我们烤了一些牛排和香肠。|近义词：barbecue, grill|反义词:|动词

Etymology: From Afrikaans *braaivleis*, from *braai* "to roast, grill" (related to Dutch *braden*) + *vleis* "meat".  The verb form is a back-formation from the noun.
