exit:/ˈɛksɪt/ (US), /ˈeksɪt/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|出口，离去，退出|An exit is a way out of a building or enclosed area.  To exit is to leave a place.|例句：The emergency exit is clearly marked. 紧急出口有明确标记。|近义词：departure, egress, leave, way out|反义词：entrance, entry, ingress|词性:名词,动词

exit:/ˈɛksɪt/ (US), /ˈeksɪt/ (UK)| vi. vt.|退出，离去|To exit is to leave a place, especially in a formal or dramatic way. It can also be used in computing to close a program or window.|例句：He exited the stage to thunderous applause. 他在雷鸣般的掌声中离开了舞台。|近义词：depart, leave, quit, withdraw|反义词：enter, arrive, join|词性:动词
