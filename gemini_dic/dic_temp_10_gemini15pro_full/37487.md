committedness:/kəˈmɪtɪdnəs/| n. |承诺，奉献，尽责|The state of being committed to a cause, activity, etc.  A strong dedication or loyalty.|例句：Her committedness to the project was evident in her long hours. 她对这个项目的奉献精神在她长时间的工作中显而易见。|近义词：commitment, dedication, devotion, loyalty|反义词：apathy, indifference, irresponsibility|词性:名词

Etymology:  From commit + -ed  + -ness.  "Commit" comes from the Latin "committere," meaning "to join together, entrust."  The "-ed" suffix creates the past participle (used here adjectivally), and "-ness" forms a noun expressing a state or quality.
