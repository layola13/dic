overlengthen:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛŋθən/
verb
过度延长；使过长
to make something too long or longer than necessary
The skirt was overlengthened and dragged on the floor. 这条裙子太长了，拖在地上。
The meeting was overlengthened by unnecessary discussions.  会议因为不必要的讨论而拖得太长了。
近义词: prolong, extend excessively
反义词: shorten, curtail
词性:v.


overlengthen:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛŋθən/
adjective
过长的
excessively long
The overlengthened rope tripped him. 过长的绳子把他绊倒了。
The film felt overlengthened and could have been edited down. 这部电影感觉太长了，可以剪辑一下。
近义词: too long, excessively long
反义词: short, concise
词性:adj.

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning excessively) + "lengthen" (verb meaning to make or become longer)  由前缀“over-”（表示过度地）加上动词“lengthen”（表示使或变得更长）构成。
