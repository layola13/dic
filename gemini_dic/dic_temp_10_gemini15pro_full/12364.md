ashkenazic:/ˌæʃkəˈnɑːzɪk/

ashkenazic:/ˌæʃkəˈnɑːzɪk/
noun|阿什肯纳兹犹太人|A Jew of central or eastern European descent.|例句：Many Ashkenazic have a genetic predisposition to certain diseases. 许多阿什肯纳兹犹太人对某些疾病有遗传易感性。|近义词：Ashkenazi|反义词:Sephardi|词性:noun
