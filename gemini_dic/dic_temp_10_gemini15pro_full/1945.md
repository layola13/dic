action: /ˈækʃən/ | n. | 行动，行为；作用；情节；诉讼 |  The time for action has arrived. 行动的时间到了。 |  activity, deed, operation | inaction, inactivity | noun
action: /ˈækʃən/ | n. | 战斗，作战 | He saw action in the Vietnam War. 他参加了越南战争。 | combat, fighting, warfare | peace | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *accioun, from Old French accion, from Latin actiō (“a doing, act, action, performance”), from agō (“I do, act, drive”).

USAGE->
Present Tense:
The company takes action to protect its employees. = 公司采取行动保护其员工。
He always actions his plans efficiently. = 他总是有效地执行他的计划。


Past Tense:
They actioned the decision immediately. = 他们立即执行了这项决定。
The government actioned the new policy last year. = 政府去年实施了这项新政策。


Present Perfect Tense:
The team has actioned all the necessary steps. = 团队已经执行了所有必要的步骤。
She has actioned the request for more information. = 她已经执行了索取更多信息的请求。


Past Perfect Tense:
They had actioned the plan before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前执行了该计划。
He had actioned the emergency procedures before the fire spread. = 在火势蔓延之前，他已经执行了紧急程序。


Future Tense:
We will action this request as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快处理此请求。
The board will action the recommendations in the report. = 董事会将执行报告中的建议。


Passive Voice:
The plan was actioned immediately. = 该计划立即得到了执行。
The recommendations are being actioned by the committee. = 委员会正在执行这些建议。
