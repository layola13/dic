motionlessness:/ˈmoʊʃənləsnəs/| n.|静止，不动|The motionlessness of the air was unnerving. 空气的静止令人不安。|例句：The motionlessness of the deer allowed the hunter to take a clear shot.  鹿的静止不动让猎人可以清楚地瞄准射击。

例句：The painting captured the motionlessness of the sleeping child perfectly. 这幅画完美地捕捉了熟睡孩子静止不动的姿态。|近义词：stillness, immobility, inactivity|反义词：movement, motion, activity|词性:名词

Etymology: From motion + -less + -ness.  Motion comes from Old French "mociun" ultimately from Latin "movere" (to move).
