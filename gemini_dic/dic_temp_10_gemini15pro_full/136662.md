nonseasonal:/ˌnɒnˈsiːzənəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsiːzənəl/
adjective
非季节性的；不受季节影响的
Not dependent on or influenced by the seasons; occurring or active throughout the year.
Nonseasonal unemployment persists regardless of the time of year.  非季节性失业全年都存在。
The nonseasonal nature of their work allows them to operate year-round. 他们的工作不受季节影响，可以全年运营。
continuous, year-round, all-year, perennial
