democracy: /dɪˈmɒkrəsi/ (UK) /dɪˈmɑːkrəsi/ (US)| n.|民主|A system of government by the whole population or all the eligible members of a state, typically through elected representatives.|例句：The United States is a democracy. 美国是一个民主国家。

例句：Many countries are striving for democracy. 许多国家都在争取民主。|近义词：republic, self-government, self-rule|反义词：dictatorship, tyranny, autocracy|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French democratie (14c.), from Medieval Latin democratia (13c.), from Greek dēmokratia "popular government," from dēmos "common people," originally "district" + -kratia "power, rule," from kratos "power, strength."
