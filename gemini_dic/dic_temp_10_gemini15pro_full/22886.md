botchery:/ˈbɒtʃəri/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtʃəri/ (US)| n. |拙劣的工作；粗制滥造|A piece of work that is done badly and clumsily. |The painting was a complete botchery, with uneven brushstrokes and mismatched colors. 这幅画完全是粗制滥造，笔触不均匀，颜色也不协调。

The repair job on the car was a botchery; it looks worse than before. 汽车的修理工作做得太糟糕了，看起来比以前更糟。|近义词：bungling, mess, bungle, hash|反义词：masterpiece, craftsmanship, workmanship|词性:名词
