adrenal:/əˈdriːnəl/| adj.|肾上腺的；刺激的|Relating to the adrenal glands or the hormones they produce.  Also, by extension, exciting or stimulating.| The adrenal glands produce adrenaline. 肾上腺产生肾上腺素。

The movie was an adrenal rush. 这部电影令人兴奋。|近义词：suprarenal, stimulating, exciting|反义词：calming, soothing|词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adrenalis*, from *ad* (to) + *renes* (kidneys).
