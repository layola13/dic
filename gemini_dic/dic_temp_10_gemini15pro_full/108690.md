leakily:/ˈliːkɪli/| adv. | 泄漏地，渗漏地 | In a manner that allows leaks or seepage.  | The roof was leakily constructed and water dripped in during the storm. 屋顶建造得很漏水，暴风雨期间水滴了进来。 |  The old pipe leakily dripped water onto the floor.  旧水管漏水滴到地板上。 | 近义词:  permeably,  porously | 反义词: tightly, impermeably | 词性: 副词

词源:  From leak (n.) + -ily (adverbial suffix).  "Leak" comes from Old Norse "leka" meaning "to drip, leak."
