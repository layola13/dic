ideologue:/ˈaɪdiːəlɔːɡ/ |ˈɪdiːəlɑːɡ/ | n. | 思想家；理论家；意识形态的拥护者 | A person who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas, especially political ones, and tries to put them into practice. | Example 1: The ideologue refused to compromise on his principles.  这位理论家拒绝在原则上妥协。

Example 2:  She was known as a passionate ideologue for her unwavering commitment to social justice. 她以对社会正义坚定不移的承诺而闻名，是一位充满激情的理想主义者。| Near Synonyms: theorist, doctrinaire, zealot | Near Antonyms: pragmatist, moderate, centrist | noun
