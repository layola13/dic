donnism: /ˈdɒnɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈdɑːnɪzəm/ (US) | n. | 多尼主义 |  The heresy, originating with Donatus, Bishop of Carthage (4th century), that maintained the validity of sacraments depended on the moral character of the minister.  It also held that the church must consist exclusively of saints. | The Donatist schism weakened the church in North Africa.  多尼特派的分裂削弱了北非的教会。 | 近义词: heresy, sectarianism | 反义词: orthodoxy | 词性: noun
