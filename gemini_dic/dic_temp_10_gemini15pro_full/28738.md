canary:/kəˈnɛəri/ | n. |金丝雀；淡黄色；女歌手|A small, brightly colored finch, often yellow, originally from the Canary Islands, popular as a cage bird. Also, a bright yellow color; a female singer, especially in a nightclub. |例句：The canary in the cage sang a beautiful song.  笼子里的金丝雀唱了一首优美的歌。

例句：She wore a dress the color of canary. 她穿了一件淡黄色的连衣裙。|近义词：finch, songbird (for the bird); yellow (for the color); vocalist, chanteuse (for the singer) |反义词: (No direct antonym for the bird or the singer)  |词性:noun


canary:/kəˈnɛəri/ | adj. |淡黄色的|Having a bright yellow color. |例句：The walls were painted a canary yellow. 墙壁被漆成了淡黄色。

例句：She bought a canary yellow scarf. 她买了一条淡黄色的围巾。|近义词：yellow, lemon-colored|反义词: dark, black |词性:adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *canaria (insula) "(island) of dogs," from Latin canis "dog."  The islands were named for the large dogs found there, not the birds.  The birds were later named after the islands.
