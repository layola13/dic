eas:/iːz/ | n. | 轻松，容易，舒适 | ease refers to a state of being free from difficulty, pain, or constraint. It can also mean freedom from worry or awkwardness. |  The medicine provided ease from the pain.  这药缓解了疼痛。| synonyms: comfort, relief, relaxation | antonyms: difficulty, hardship, discomfort | noun

eas:/iːz/ | v. | 减轻，缓和，使舒适 | to make something less difficult, painful, or severe |  The government's measures eased the financial burden on families. 政府的措施减轻了家庭的经济负担。| synonyms: alleviate, lessen, mitigate | antonyms: worsen, aggravate, exacerbate | verb
