nondivisiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈvaɪsɪvnəs/
noun
无分裂性；团结；和谐
The state or quality of being not divisive; promoting unity and harmony.
The candidate's message of nondivisiveness resonated with voters. 候选人团结一致的信息引起了选民的共鸣。
The nondivisiveness of the community was its greatest strength.  这个社区的团结是它最大的优势。
Synonyms: unity, harmony, solidarity, cohesion, concord
Antonyms: divisiveness, discord, disunity, conflict, friction
词性: 名词
