devotionalism:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
虔诚；宗教信仰；献身；挚爱
Religious fervor; piety; the quality of being devotional.
She approached her studies with a quiet devotionalism. 她以默默的虔诚治学。
Near Synonyms: piety, devoutness, religiousness, godliness, holiness
Near Antonyms: irreverence, impiety, ungodliness, secularism
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From devotional + -ism.  Devotional from devote + -ional. Devote from Latin dēvōtus (dedicated, devoted).
