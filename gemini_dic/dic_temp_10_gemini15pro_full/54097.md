disembody:/dɪsɪmˈbɑːdi/ | v. | 使脱离肉体；使脱离实体；使抽象化 | To separate (something, such as a soul or spirit) from the body.  To divest of a body; make incorporeal or immaterial. |  The novel attempts to disembody the human experience. 这部小说试图将人类的经验抽象化。| Synonyms: spiritualize, dematerialize, etherealize | Antonyms: embody, incarnate, materialize | verb

disembodied:/dɪsɪmˈbɑːdid/ | adj. | 脱离肉体的；脱离实体的；抽象的 | Separated from or existing without the body. | He heard a disembodied voice. 他听到一个空灵的声音。| Synonyms: incorporeal, immaterial, spectral | Antonyms: embodied, corporeal, physical | adjective
