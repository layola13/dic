arbiters:/ˈɑːrbɪtərz/ (US), /ˈɑːbɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|仲裁者，裁决人|A person who settles a dispute or has ultimate authority in a matter.|The arbiters of fashion decided that pink was the color of the season.  时尚界的仲裁者认定粉色是本季的流行色。|Synonyms: judge, mediator, referee, umpire|Antonyms: disputant, litigant|Noun


arbitrator:/ˈɑːrbɪtreɪtər/ (US), /ˈɑːbɪtreɪtə/ (UK)| n.|仲裁人，调停人|A person chosen to settle a dispute.|The arbitrator listened to both sides of the story before making a decision. 仲裁人听取了双方的陈述后才做出决定。|Synonyms: arbiter, judge, mediator, referee, umpire|Antonyms: disputant, litigant|Noun
