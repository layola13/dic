convokers:/kənˈvoʊkərz/| n.|召集人，召集者|People who convene or assemble a meeting or group.  Those who call others together for a particular purpose.|The convokers of the conference announced the agenda. 会议的召集人宣布了议程。|synonyms: organizers, conveners, assemblers|antonyms: attendees, participants|noun
