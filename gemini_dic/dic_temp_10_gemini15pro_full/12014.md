arthropodan:/ˈɑːrθrəpɒdən/ (US) /ˈɑːrθrəpɒdn/ (UK)| n. adj.|节肢动物|An invertebrate animal having an exoskeleton (external skeleton), a segmented body, and jointed appendages.  Belonging or relating to the phylum Arthropoda.|例句：The lobster is an arthropodan. 龙虾是一种节肢动物。|例句：Arthropodan exoskeletons are made of chitin. 节肢动物的外骨骼是由几丁质构成的。|近义词：invertebrate, arthropod|反义词: vertebrate|词性: noun, adjective

词源:
From Greek *ἄρθρον (arthron, “joint”) + *πούς (pous, “foot”).
