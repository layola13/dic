collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/ | v. | 合作，协作 | to work jointly with others or together especially in an intellectual endeavor | Example:  The two companies collaborated on the project.  两家公司合作开展这个项目。

Example:  Researchers are collaborating to find a cure for the disease.  研究人员正在合作寻找治疗这种疾病的方法。 | 近义词: cooperate, partner, team up, work together | 反义词: compete, oppose | 词性: 动词

collaboration:/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 合作，协作 | the action of working with someone to produce or create something | Example: The project was a collaboration between several artists.  这个项目是几位艺术家合作的成果。

Example:  The two universities have a long history of collaboration.  这两所大学有着悠久的合作历史。| 近义词: cooperation, partnership, teamwork | 反义词: competition, opposition | 词性: 名词

collaborative:/kəˈlæbərətɪv/ | adj. | 合作的，协作的 | produced or conducted by two or more parties working together | Example: The team adopted a collaborative approach to problem-solving.  团队采取了合作的方式来解决问题。

Example: The collaborative effort resulted in a successful outcome. 协同努力取得了圆满成果。| 近义词: cooperative, joint, shared | 反义词: individual, independent, solo | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin collaborare, from com- ‘together’ + laborare ‘to work’.  来自拉丁语 collaborare，由 com- “一起” + laborare “工作” 组成。
