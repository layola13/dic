nonprosaicnesses:/ˌnɒnproʊˈzeɪɪknəsɪz/
noun
非散文性；非平凡性；非寻常性
The state or quality of not being prosaic; imaginativeness, originality.
Her poetry is full of nonprosaicnesses, defying easy categorization. 她的诗歌充满了非同寻常之处，难以归类。
The artist's nonprosaicnesses are evident in his vibrant and unconventional use of color.  这位艺术家的非凡之处体现在他对色彩的鲜明和非传统的运用上。
近义词: originality, imaginativeness, creativity, unconventionality, uniqueness
反义词: prosaicness, ordinariness, commonness, banality, dullness
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From non- (not) + prosaic (dull, ordinary) + -ness (state or quality of being).  Ultimately from Latin prosa "straightforward discourse".
