nasalizable:/ˈneɪzələɪzəbl/| adj.|可鼻音化的|Able to be nasalized (pronounced with air passing through the nose).  This typically refers to sounds like vowels or consonants that can be produced with a nasal quality.|The vowel in "man" is nasalizable in some dialects of English.|近义词:nasal|反义词:oral|词性:adjective
nasalization:/ˌneɪzələɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|鼻音化|The act or process of making a sound nasal, or the quality of being nasal.|Nasalization is a common feature of French pronunciation.|近义词:nasality|反义词:non-nasality|词性:noun
nasalize:/ˈneɪzəlaɪz/| v.|使鼻音化; 用鼻音发音|To pronounce (a sound) with air passing through the nose.|In French, vowels are often nasalized before nasal consonants.|近义词:pronounce nasally|反义词:pronounce orally|词性:verb
