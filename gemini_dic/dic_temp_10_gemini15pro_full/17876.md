beekeeper:/ˈbiːkiːpər/| n.|养蜂人|A person who keeps bees, especially for the production of honey.|例句：The beekeeper carefully inspected the hives for signs of disease. 养蜂人仔细检查蜂箱是否有疾病迹象。

例句：My grandfather was a beekeeper and made the most delicious honey. 我的祖父是一位养蜂人，酿造出最美味的蜂蜜。|近义词：apiarist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bee hunter" if focusing on taking honey rather than cultivating bees)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bee +‎ keeper,  "keeper" comes from Middle English kepere, from Old English -cēpere, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kopijaną.
