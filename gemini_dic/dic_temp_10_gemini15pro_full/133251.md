nonexercisable:/ˌnɒnˈɛksəsaɪzəbəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈɛksəsaɪzəbl/ (UK)
adjective
不可行使的；不可执行的
Not capable of being exercised or put into effect; (especially of a stock option) not giving the holder the right to buy or sell shares.
The contract contained a nonexercisable clause relating to early termination.  该合同包含一项关于提前终止的不可执行条款。
He held a large number of nonexercisable stock options. 他持有大量不可行使的股票期权。
近义词: non-enforceable, invalid, unusable
反义词: exercisable, valid, usable
词性: adjective

nonexercisable:/ˌnɒnˈɛksəsaɪzəbəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈɛksəsaɪzəbl/ (UK)
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "exercisable" (from "exercise" + "-able" suffix indicating capability).  The root "exercise" ultimately comes from Latin "exercitium" meaning "practice," "training."
词性: adjective
