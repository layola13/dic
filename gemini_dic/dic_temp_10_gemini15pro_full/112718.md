loyalest:/ˈlɔɪəlɪst/
adjective
最忠诚的
Superlative form of loyal: most loyal.  Showing firm and constant support or allegiance to a person or institution.
He was the king's loyalest supporter. 他是国王最忠诚的支持者。
loyal, truest, steadfast, devoted
faithless, disloyal, treacherous
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Middle English *loyalest, a superlative of *loyal, from Anglo-Norman leial, loial (“loyal, faithful, legitimate”), from Latin lēgālis (“legal, lawful”), from lex (“law”).
