deconfliction:/ˌdiːkənˈflɪkʃən/
n.
冲突消除，冲突消解
The process of resolving or mitigating potential conflicts or overlaps between different activities, plans, or operations, especially in military or aviation contexts.
The deconfliction meeting aimed to prevent accidental clashes between the two air forces. 冲突消除会议旨在防止两国空军之间的意外冲突。
The software helps with the deconfliction of airspace. 该软件有助于消除空域冲突。
Synonyms: coordination, harmonization, integration, synchronization
Antonyms: conflict, clash, interference
noun
