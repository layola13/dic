coparcener:/ˌkəʊˈpɑːsɪnər/| n. |共同继承人；遗产共同继承人|A coparcener is a person who inherits property jointly with others, especially under common law.|例句：The three sisters were coparceners of their father's estate. 这三个姐妹是她们父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：Under the old laws, daughters were often coparceners. 根据旧法律，女儿通常是共同继承人。|近义词：joint heir, coheir|反义词: sole heir |词性:noun
