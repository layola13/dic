noncriminality:/ˌnɑːnˌkrɪmɪˈnælɪti/ | n. | 无罪; 非犯罪性 |The noncriminality of his actions was confirmed by the court. 法庭确认了他的行为的无罪性。|  The report attested to the noncriminality of the organization. 报告证明了该组织的非犯罪性质。 | 近义词: innocence, lawfulness, legality | 反义词: criminality, guilt, illegality | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From non- + criminality.  "Criminality" comes from the Late Latin "criminalitas,"  from Latin "criminalis" (criminal), ultimately from "crimen" (crime).
