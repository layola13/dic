hip-huggers:/ˈhɪpˌhʌɡərz/| n. |低腰裤|Low-rise trousers or jeans that sit low on the hips, often below the natural waistline.|She wore a pair of hip-huggers and a crop top. 她穿了一条低腰裤和一件露脐上衣。|Synonyms: low-rise jeans, low-riders|Antonyms: high-waisted trousers, high-rise jeans|noun

hip-huggers:/ˈhɪpˌhʌɡərz/| n. |低腰裤|Trousers designed to be worn low on the hips, emphasizing the hip line. |Hip-huggers were fashionable in the 1960s and made a comeback in the early 2000s. 低腰裤在20世纪60年代很流行，并在21世纪初再次流行。|Synonyms: low-slung jeans, low-rise trousers|Antonyms: high-waisted pants, high-rise jeans|noun
