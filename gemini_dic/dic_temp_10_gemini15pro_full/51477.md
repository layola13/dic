detoxification:/ˌdiːˌtɑːksɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 解毒；戒毒；净化 | the process of removing toxic substances or qualities. |  The clinic offers a detoxification program for drug addicts.  这家诊所为吸毒者提供戒毒方案。| synonyms: cleansing, purification, purging | antonyms: contamination, pollution | noun

detoxify:/ˌdiːˈtɑːksɪfaɪ/ | v. | 解毒；使洁净 | to remove toxic substances or qualities. | The liver detoxifies the blood. 肝脏净化血液。| synonyms: cleanse, purify, purge | antonyms: contaminate, pollute | verb
