disempower:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpaʊər/ | v. | 使失去权力，剥夺权力；使丧失能力，使无能为力 | To deprive of power; make weak, helpless, or ineffective. | The new law disempowers local governments.  新法律剥夺了地方政府的权力。| 近义词: weaken, undermine, disable | 反义词: empower, enable, strengthen | 词性: 动词

词源:  dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + empower (from Old French pouvoir "to be able")
