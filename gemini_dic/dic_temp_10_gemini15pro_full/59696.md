echoscope:/ˈɛkəˌskoʊp/ | n. | 超声波诊断仪；回声探测器 | An instrument using ultrasonic waves for diagnostic or therapeutic purposes, especially for visualizing internal body structures. | The doctor used an echoscope to examine the patient's heart. 医生用超声波诊断仪检查病人的心脏。| synonym: ultrasound, sonograph | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps X-ray as an alternative imaging technique) | noun
