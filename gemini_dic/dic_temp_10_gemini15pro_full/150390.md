panoply:/ˈpænəpli/| n. |全套披挂；盛装；炫耀；壮丽的展示；华丽的陈列|A complete or impressive collection of things. A splendid display.|The knight wore a full panoply of armor. 骑士身穿全套盔甲。|Synonyms: array, display, collection, show, exhibition|Antonyms: lack, deficiency, scarcity|noun

panoply:/ˈpænəpli/| n. |全副武装；全套装备|A complete set of arms or suit of armor.|The museum displayed a panoply of medieval weaponry.  博物馆展出了一整套中世纪武器。|Synonyms:  armament, equipment, gear|Antonyms: disarmament|noun
