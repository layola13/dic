mittys:/ˈmɪtiz/| n.|米蒂综合症；白日梦；空想|A state of being dominated by fantasies, especially about achieving success or recognition, often as a way of escaping from reality.  Derived from the character Walter Mitty in James Thurber's short story "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty".|He retreated into his mittys whenever his boss criticized him. 每当老板批评他时，他就躲进自己的白日梦里。|近义词：daydreams, fantasies, reveries|反义词：reality, actuality|noun
