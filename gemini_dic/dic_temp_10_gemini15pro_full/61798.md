emphatic:/ɪmˈfætɪk/ (US), /emˈfætɪk/ (UK)| adj.|强调的；着重的；断然的|Done or said in a strong way to show importance or certainty.|She was emphatic that she did not want to go. 她坚决表示她不想去。|近义词：forceful, assertive, insistent, decided|反义词：hesitant, tentative, weak|词性:形容词

emphatic:/ɪmˈfætɪk/ (US), /emˈfætɪk/ (UK)| n.|强调语气；加强语气词|A word or phrase used to emphasize a statement.|The use of "very" in this sentence is an emphatic.  在这个句子中，“very”的使用是一种强调语气。|近义词：stress, accent|反义词：understatement|词性:名词 (较少使用)
