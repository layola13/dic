onwardly:/ˈɒnwədli/ /ˈɑːnwərdli/| adv.|向前地，前进地；在精神上，在思想上|Moving or directed forward; also, in a forward direction of thought or development.|He pressed onwardly despite the obstacles. 他不顾障碍地继续前进。|近义词：forward, ahead, progressively|反义词:backward, behind|词性:副词

词源: From "onward" + "-ly." "Onward" comes from Old English "onweard," meaning "turned toward, facing, directed toward."
