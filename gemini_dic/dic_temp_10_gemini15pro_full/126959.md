narrow-mindedly:/ˌnæroʊˈmaɪndɪdli/
|adv.|狭隘地；心胸狭窄地；固执地|In a narrow-minded way; with intolerance or prejudice.  Showing a lack of acceptance or understanding of different perspectives.|He narrow-mindedly dismissed all new ideas. 他狭隘地拒绝了所有新想法。|
|She narrow-mindedly refused to consider any other viewpoints. 她固执地拒绝考虑任何其他观点。|
|Synonyms: intolerantly, bigotedly, prejudicially, dogmatically|
|Antonyms: open-mindedly, liberally, tolerantly, broadly|
|词性: 副词 (adverb)|


ETYMOLOGY->
From narrow-minded + -ly.  "Narrow-minded" itself comes from "narrow" (limited in extent, range, or scope) + "minded" (having a mind of a specified character).
