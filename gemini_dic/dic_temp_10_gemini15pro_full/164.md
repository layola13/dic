abate: /əˈbeɪt/
verb
减轻，减少，缓和
to become less strong; to make something less strong
The storm finally abated.  暴风雨终于减弱了。

The floodwaters began to abate. 洪水开始消退。

近义词: subside, lessen, diminish, decrease, decline, wane, moderate, mitigate
反义词: increase, intensify, escalate, aggravate

词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *abatre, from a- (from Latin ad 'to, at') + batre 'to beat down' (from Latin battuere, frequentative of batere 'to beat').

USAGE->
