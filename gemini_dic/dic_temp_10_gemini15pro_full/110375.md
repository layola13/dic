lignin:/ˈlɪɡnɪn/ |n.|木质素|A complex organic polymer deposited in the cell walls of many plants, making them rigid and woody.|Lignin makes up a significant portion of wood. 木质素构成木材的重要组成部分。

Lignin is difficult to break down. 木质素很难分解。|近义词:wood fiber|反义词:None|词性:noun
