nonstory:/ˈnɒnˌstɔːri/ /ˈnɑːnˌstɔːri/
noun
无价值的故事；不值得报道的事情
a report or account of events that are felt to be insignificant or uninteresting.
The media dismissed the scandal as a nonstory. 媒体认为这件丑闻不值一提。
Synonyms: trifle, nothing, bagatelle
Antonyms: scoop, blockbuster, sensation
词性: 名词
