fatalist:/ˈfeɪtəlɪst/| n.|宿命论者；听天由命的人|Someone who believes that events are determined by fate and cannot be changed. |例句：He's a fatalist and believes there's nothing he can do to change his destiny. 他是一个宿命论者，认为自己无力改变命运。

例句：As a fatalist, she accepted her misfortune without complaint.  作为一个宿命论者，她默默地接受了自己的不幸。|近义词：determinist, pessimist|反义词：optimist, pragmatist|词性: noun
