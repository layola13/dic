nonconservational:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非保守的，非守恒的 | Not tending to conserve; not conserving, especially of a physical quantity or effect.  Relating to a process or interaction in which a quantity (such as energy, momentum, or electric charge) is not conserved.  |  The nonconservational nature of the system led to a gradual loss of energy.  这个系统的非守恒性导致能量逐渐损失。 | Synonyms: dissipative, non-conservative | Antonyms: conservative, conservable | word property: adjective
