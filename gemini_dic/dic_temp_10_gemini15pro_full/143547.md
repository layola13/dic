outhasten:/aʊtˈheɪsən/
verb
超过，赶超，比...更快
to hasten more than or to a greater degree than another
He outhastened his rivals to reach the finish line first. 他超过了对手，第一个到达终点线。
近义词: outstrip, surpass, outpace
反义词: lag behind, fall behind
词性: verb

Etymology:
From out- + hasten.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing. "Hasten" comes from Middle English *hasten*, from Old Norse *heista*, meaning "to chase, pursue".
