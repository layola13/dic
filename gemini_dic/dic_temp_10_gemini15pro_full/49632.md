demigration: /ˌdiːmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. | 迁出；人口外移 | The demigration from rural areas to cities is a common phenomenon in developing countries.  农村地区向城市的人口迁出是发展中国家的普遍现象。|  The government is trying to slow the demigration of skilled workers to other countries. 政府正试图减缓熟练工人向其他国家的外移。 | 近义词：emigration, exodus | 反义词：immigration | 词性：名词

Etymology: From de- (down, away) + migration (movement from one place to another). Ultimately from Latin migrare (to move).
