adamantean: /ˌæd.ə.mænˈtiː.ən/ | adj. | 坚硬无比的，固执的 |  Made of or having the qualities of adamant; unyielding. | His adamantean will could not be broken.  他坚定的意志无法动摇。

The fortress had adamantean walls.  这座堡垒有着坚不可摧的城墙。| 近义词：implacable, inflexible, unyielding, resolute, firm | 反义词：yielding, flexible, pliable | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adamanteus, from Latin adamas, adamant- ‘adamant’, from Greek adamas, adamant- ‘invincible’.

USAGE->
Attributive Use (used before a noun):

He displayed an adamantean refusal to compromise. = 他表现出坚决不妥协的态度。
The team faced adamantean opposition from the local community. = 该团队面临当地社区的坚决反对。


Predicative Use (used after a verb):

Her stance on the issue was adamantean. = 她在这个问题上的立场很坚定。
He remained adamantean in his belief. = 他仍然坚定地坚持他的信仰。

PHRASE->
adamantean will = 坚强的意志
adamantean spirit = 坚定不移的精神
adamantean resolve = 坚定的决心
adamantean refusal = 坚决的拒绝
adamantean opposition = 坚决的反对
