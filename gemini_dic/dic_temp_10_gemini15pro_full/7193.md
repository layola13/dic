angiospermatous:/ˌændʒiəˈspɜːrmətəs/
adjective

Relating to or denoting a plant having its seeds enclosed in an ovary; a flowering plant.
Roses are angiospermatous plants. 玫瑰是被子植物。
Angiospermous, flowering
