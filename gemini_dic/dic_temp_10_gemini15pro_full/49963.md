Etymology: From Old French *deneier (unattested), from Latin denegare (“to deny, refuse”), from de- + negare (“to deny”).

注意事项:  Denial can refer to both a statement that something isn't true and a psychological defense mechanism where a person refuses to acknowledge a painful or difficult reality.  This distinction is important for understanding the nuances of the word.
