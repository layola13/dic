noncontentious:/ˌnɒnkənˈtenʃəs/
adjective
无争议的；不引起争论的
Not causing or likely to cause an argument; not disputed.
The meeting was noncontentious and productive.  会议没有争议，富有成效。
The bill passed through parliament in a noncontentious manner.  该法案在议会顺利通过，没有引起争议。
近义词:uncontroversial, undisputed, peaceful, amicable
反义词:contentious, disputed, controversial, argumentative
词性:adjective

noncontentiously:/ˌnɒnkənˈtenʃəsli/
adverb
无争议地；不引起争论地
In a way that does not cause or is not likely to cause an argument.
The issue was resolved noncontentiously.  这个问题得到了和平解决。
They noncontentiously agreed to the terms of the contract. 他们毫无争议地同意了合同条款。
近义词:amicably, peacefully, harmoniously
反义词:contentiously, controversially
词性:adverb

noncontentiousness:/ˌnɒnkənˈtenʃəsnəs/
noun
无争议；和谐
The state or quality of not being contentious.
The noncontentiousness of the meeting allowed for quick decisions. 会议的和谐氛围使得能够快速做出决定。
The lawyers praised the noncontentiousness of the divorce proceedings. 律师们赞扬了离婚程序的顺利进行。
近义词:harmony, agreement, peace, accord
反义词:contention, disagreement, dispute, discord
词性:noun


词源:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + contentious (adjective meaning "likely to cause an argument").  The root of "contentious" is the Latin "contendere," meaning "to strive, contend."
