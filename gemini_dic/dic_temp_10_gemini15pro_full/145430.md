overcharitablenesses:/ˌoʊvərˌtʃærɪtəblənəsɪz/
n.
过度仁慈，过度宽容
The state of being excessively charitable or forgiving, often to a fault.
His overcharitablenesses led him to be taken advantage of. 他过分的仁慈导致他被利用。
Synonyms: over-forgiveness, excessive leniency, undue tolerance
Antonyms: ruthlessness, mercilessness, severity
Noun
