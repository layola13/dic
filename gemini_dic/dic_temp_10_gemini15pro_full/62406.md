endogenetic:/ˌendəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 内生的，内成的 | Formed or originating from within an organism or system; produced internally.  |  Example 1: Endogenetic rocks are formed by processes within the Earth. 内生岩石是由地球内部的进程形成的。

Example 2:  The doctor explained that the disease was endogenetic, not caused by external factors. 医生解释说这种疾病是内生的，而不是由外部因素引起的。 | 近义词: internal, inherent, intrinsic | 反义词: exogenous, external | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *endo-*, meaning "within," and *genetikos*, meaning "originating."
