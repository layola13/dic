metacinnabarite:/ˌmɛtəsɪnəˈbɑːraɪt/| n. |黑辰砂|A black, cubic form of mercury sulfide (HgS), a mineral often found in mercury deposits.  It is a polymorph of cinnabar (the red form of HgS).|Metacinnabarite is often found alongside cinnabar in ore deposits. 黑辰砂常与辰砂一起出现在矿床中。

Metacinnabarite is a less common form of mercury sulfide than cinnabar. 黑辰砂是一种比辰砂少见的硫化汞形式。|近义词:cinnabar (in terms of chemical composition), mineral|反义词:None (no direct opposite for a mineral)|词性:noun
