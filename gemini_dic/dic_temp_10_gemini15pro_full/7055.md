anemography:/ˌænɪˈmɒɡrəfi/ | n. |风力测定法；风速记录；风向记录|The study and description of winds, specifically the methods of recording wind speed and direction. | Meteorologists use anemography to study wind patterns and predict future weather conditions. 气象学家使用风力测定法来研究风型并预测未来的天气状况。|Synonyms: anemology |Antonyms: N/A |noun


anemograph:/ˈænɪməɡrɑːf/ | n. |风速风向记录仪|An instrument that records the speed and direction of the wind. | The anemograph on the roof of the weather station constantly monitors wind conditions.  气象站屋顶上的风速风向记录仪持续监测风力状况。|Synonyms: wind gauge, wind recorder |Antonyms: N/A |noun
