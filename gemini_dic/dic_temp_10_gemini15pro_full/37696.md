companioning:/kəmˈpænɪənɪŋ/
n.|陪伴；伴随；做伴|The act of being a companion to someone, providing support and presence.|She found great comfort in the companioning of her close friends during a difficult time. 她发现朋友在她困难时期的陪伴给了她莫大的安慰。|Synonyms: accompanying, attending, supporting|Antonyms: abandoning, deserting|noun
