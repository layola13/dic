hoveled:/ˈhʌvəld/ /ˈhɑːvəld/| v. |居住在小屋里；挤在一起|Lived in or crowded together in a small, simple dwelling. |The refugees hoveled in makeshift shelters.  难民们挤在临时搭建的棚子里。|
hoveled:/ˈhʌvəld/ /ˈhɑːvəld/| v. |把…赶入小屋|To drive or force into a hovel. |The farmer hoveled the sheep during the storm. 农民在暴风雨期间把羊赶进了小屋。|
hoveled:/ˈhʌvəld/ /ˈhɑːvəld/| adj. |简陋的|Resembling a hovel; mean or wretched. |They lived in hoveled conditions. 他们住在简陋的环境中。|


近义词: sheltered, huddled, crowded, crammed
反义词: spacious, luxurious
词性: verb, adjective (past tense and past participle of "hovel")


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hovel" + "-ed."  "Hovel" comes from Middle English "hovel,"  meaning a shed or shelter for animals, perhaps from Middle Low German "hovil,"  meaning a small house.
