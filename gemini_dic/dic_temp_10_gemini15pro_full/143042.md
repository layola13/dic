outbluffed: /ˌaʊtˈblʌft/
verb
以虚张声势胜过，以更大的虚张声势压倒
to defeat someone by bluffing more successfully than they do.
He outbluffed his opponent in the poker game. 他在扑克游戏中以虚张声势战胜了对手。
Synonyms: outsmart, outmaneuver, outwit
Antonyms:  be outbluffed, concede, yield
词性: 动词
