high-risk:/ˌhaɪ ˈrɪsk/| adj.|高风险的；高危的|Involving a high level of risk or danger.|Investing in the stock market is a high-risk venture. 投资股票市场是一项高风险的企业。|High-risk individuals are more likely to develop heart disease.  高危人群更容易患心脏病。|近义词：dangerous, hazardous, perilous|反义词:safe, secure, low-risk|词性:形容词
Etymology: "high" (Old English *hēah) + "risk" (from Old French risqué, past participle of risquier "to run a risk," from Italian rischio "danger")
