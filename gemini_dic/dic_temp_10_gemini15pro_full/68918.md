fat-free:/ˈfætˈfriː/
adjective
无脂的；脱脂的
Containing no fat.
例句：I'm trying to eat more fat-free foods.  我正在尝试吃更多无脂食物。

例句：She always buys fat-free yogurt. 她总是买脱脂酸奶。


近义词：nonfat, low-fat
反义词：full-fat, high-fat
词性：adjective
