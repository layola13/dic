inpatient:/ˈɪnˌpeɪʃənt/ | n. adj. |住院病人；住院的|A person who stays in a hospital while receiving treatment.|例句：My grandmother was an inpatient at the hospital for a week after her surgery. 我祖母手术后在医院住院一周。|近义词：hospitalized person, resident patient |反义词: outpatient |词性:名词, 形容词
