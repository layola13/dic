outbuildings:/ˈaʊtˌbɪldɪŋz/
noun
附属建筑；外屋
A structure detached from but located near a main building, such as a shed or barn.
The outbuildings included a stable, a workshop, and a chicken coop. 附属建筑包括一个马厩、一个工作室和一个鸡舍。
synonyms: outhouse, annex, extension, dependency
antonyms: main building, primary structure
