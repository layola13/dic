hardier:/ˈhɑːrdiər/ | adj. | 更强壮的，更坚强的，更耐寒的 | More robust, more resilient, able to withstand difficult conditions better. | These hardier plants can survive even in harsh winters. 这些更耐寒的植物即使在严冬也能存活。| The new breed of tomato is hardier and produces more fruit.  这种新品种的西红柿更强壮，产量更高。 | synonyms: tougher, stronger, more robust, more resistant, more resilient | antonyms: weaker, frailer, more delicate, more vulnerable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hardy*, from Old French *hardi* (brave, bold), from Frankish *hardjan* (to make hard, strengthen), from Proto-Germanic *harduz* (hard).  Related to English "hard."
