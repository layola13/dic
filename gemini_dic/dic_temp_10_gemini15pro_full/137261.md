nonsymbolic:/ˌnɒn.sɪmˈbɒl.ɪk/ | adj. | 非象征性的；非符号的 | Not symbolic; not using or involving symbols or symbolism. |  Many early forms of communication were nonsymbolic, relying on gestures and sounds.  许多早期的交流形式是非象征性的，依靠手势和声音。

| Example 1: The artist's later work became increasingly nonsymbolic, focusing on pure abstraction.  这位艺术家后期的作品越来越非象征性，专注于纯粹的抽象。

| Example 2:  Her nonsymbolic paintings evoked a sense of calm and tranquility. 她的非象征性绘画唤起了一种平静和安宁的感觉。


| Synonyms: literal, concrete, denotative, representational | Antonyms: symbolic, figurative, abstract, metaphorical | Word Type: Adjective
