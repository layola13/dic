crazes:/ˈkreɪzɪz/
noun
狂热，风靡一时的事物
An enthusiasm for a particular activity or object which appears suddenly and achieves widespread but short-lived popularity.
Example: The latest fitness craze sweeping the nation. 席卷全国的最新健身热潮。

Example:  He collects old records and other crazes from the past. 他收集旧唱片和过去的其他流行玩意儿。

Synonym: fads, trends, fashions, rages, manias
Antonym: dislikes, indifferences, apathy

词性: noun (名词)
