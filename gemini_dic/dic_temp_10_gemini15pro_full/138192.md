nosebanded:/ˈnoʊzˌbændɪd/| adj.|戴着鼻羁的，受限制的|Having a noseband (a strap that goes around the nose of a horse, usually part of a bridle).  Figuratively, it can also mean restricted or controlled.|例句：The nosebanded horse trotted calmly around the ring.  戴着鼻羁的马平静地在场地里小跑。|例句：Feeling nosebanded by the strict rules, she longed for freedom.  受到严格规定的限制，她渴望自由。|近义词：restrained, restricted, controlled|反义词:free, unrestrained|词性:形容词
