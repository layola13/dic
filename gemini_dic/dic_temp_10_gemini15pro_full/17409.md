beamer:/ˈbiːmər/| n.|投影仪；投影机|A device that projects images onto a screen, especially one used for presentations.|例句：The beamer in the conference room wasn't working. 会议室里的投影仪坏了。

例句：She used a beamer to present her research findings. 她用投影仪展示了她的研究成果。|近义词：projector, overhead projector|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
