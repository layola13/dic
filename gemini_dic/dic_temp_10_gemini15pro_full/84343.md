halfpace:/ˈhæfˌpeɪs/ | n. | 半步，慢步 | A short, slow step; a pace between a walk and a full stride. |  He took a halfpace forward, hesitant to approach. 他向前迈了半步，犹豫着要不要靠近。| 近义词: short step, small step, mincing step | 反义词: stride, large step | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
"half" + "pace"
"half" (from Old English healf) meaning one of two equal parts.
"pace" (from Old French pas, ultimately from Latin passus) meaning a step.
