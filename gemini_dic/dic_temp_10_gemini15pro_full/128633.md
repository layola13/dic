nickelled:/ˈnɪkəld/ | adj. | 镀镍的；镍制的；（俚语）破产的；身无分文的 | Coated with nickel; made of nickel; (slang) broke, penniless | The car had nickelled bumpers. 这辆车的保险杠是镀镍的。 | synonyms: nickel-plated, broke, penniless | antonyms: wealthy, rich | adjective

nickelled:/ˈnɪkəld/ | v. | 镀镍；（俚语）偷窃；诈骗 | To coat with nickel; (slang) to steal, to cheat |  He nickelled the car's trim for a more polished look. 他给汽车的饰边镀了镍，使其看起来更有光泽。 | synonyms: nickel-plate, steal, swindle | antonyms: give, donate | verb

Etymology: From the noun "nickel," referring to the metallic element. The verb sense likely arose from the idea of coating something with a thin, valuable layer, and then extended metaphorically to stealing or cheating.
