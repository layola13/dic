baby-faced:/ˈbeɪbiˌfeɪst/| adj.|娃娃脸的，童颜的|Having a face that looks young and innocent like a baby's.  Characterized by a youthful or innocent appearance, typically with smooth skin and rounded features.|He's a baby-faced 40-year-old. 他是一位有着娃娃脸的40岁男子。|
She has a baby-faced look that makes her seem younger than she is. 她长着一张娃娃脸，让她看起来比实际年龄更年轻。|近义词：youthful, cherubic, innocent-looking|反义词：mature-looking, haggard, world-weary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From baby + faced.  "Faced" acts as a suffix here, meaning "having a face of a particular kind." The term became popular in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
