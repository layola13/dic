mammer:/ˈmæmər/ | v. | 犹豫；结巴；支吾；mumble | To speak hesitantly and indistinctly. | She mammered something about needing to leave. 她支支吾吾地说她需要离开。| He mammered an apology. 他结结巴巴地道歉了一句。 | 近义词: stammer, stutter, hesitate, falter | 反义词: articulate, enunciate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mameren, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *mumra (“to mumble”).  First recorded use c. 1400.
