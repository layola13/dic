backpacker:/ˈbækˌpækər/| n.|背包客；背包旅行者|A person who travels or hikes carrying their belongings in a backpack.|She met a group of backpackers while hiking the Appalachian Trail.  她在徒步穿越阿巴拉契亚山径时遇到了一群背包客。|Synonyms: hiker, trekker, globetrotter|Antonyms: tourist, traveler (in a more general sense)|noun

backpacking:/ˈbækˌpækɪŋ/| n.|背包旅行|The activity of travelling or hiking with a backpack.|Backpacking through Southeast Asia is a popular choice for gap year students.  背包游东南亚是间隔年学生的热门选择。|Synonyms: hiking, trekking, rambling|Antonyms:  luxury travel, guided tour|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From back + pack + -er.  "Backpack" itself comes from "back" and "pack," referring to a pack carried on the back.
