babier:/ˈbeɪbiər/| adj.|更像婴儿的；更幼稚的|More babyish; more infantile.|例句：He's acting babier than usual today. 他今天表现得比平时更幼稚。|近义词：more infantile, more childish, younger|反义词：more mature, older, more grown-up|词性:形容词

babier (comparative form of babyish): This word is not standard English. It's a colloquial or informal comparative form of "babyish," meaning resembling or characteristic of a baby, especially in a childish or immature way.  While grammatically understandable, it’s generally better to use "more babyish" in formal writing.
