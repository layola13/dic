heterogenesis:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. |异种生殖，异源发生| The development of dissimilar organisms, one from the other, as in alternation of generations.  The production of offspring different in form from the parent.| Example 1:  Heterogenesis is observed in certain insects, where the larval stage is drastically different from the adult form. 异种生殖在某些昆虫中可以观察到，它们的幼虫阶段与成虫形态截然不同。

Example 2:  The biologist studied the complex process of heterogenesis in aphids.  这位生物学家研究了蚜虫复杂的异种生殖过程。 | 近义词: abiogenesis, xenogenesis | 反义词: homogenesis | 词性: noun
