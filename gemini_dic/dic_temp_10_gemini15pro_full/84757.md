handmaiden:/ˈhændˌmeɪdən/| n. |侍女，女仆；助手，帮手|A female servant or attendant, especially one serving a woman of high rank; something that helps or promotes something else.|The queen's handmaiden brushed her hair. 女王的侍女为她梳头。|Synonyms: servant, attendant, maid, assistant, helper|Antonyms: master, mistress, lord, employer|noun

handmaiden:/ˈhændˌmeɪdən/| n. |(比喻)推动…的东西，…的工具|Something that helps or promotes something else.|Public relations is often the handmaiden of commercial interests. 公共关系常常是商业利益的工具。|Synonyms: tool, instrument, vehicle, catalyst|Antonyms: obstacle, hindrance, impediment|noun
