flag-waving:/ˈflæɡˌweɪvɪŋ/
flag-waving:adj.
flag-waving:爱国主义的(尤指夸张的)；炫耀爱国主义的
flag-waving:Showing excessive patriotism, especially of a superficial kind.
flag-waving:The politician's flag-waving speech didn't impress many voters. 这位政客的爱国演讲并没有打动很多选民。

flag-waving:n.
flag-waving:挥舞旗帜；爱国主义(尤指夸张的)；炫耀爱国主义
flag-waving:The act of waving a flag, especially as a display of patriotism; ostentatious patriotism.
flag-waving:There was a lot of flag-waving at the Fourth of July parade. 七月四日游行中有很多挥舞国旗的活动。


近义词:patriotic, nationalistic, jingoistic
反义词:unpatriotic, disloyal

词源:flag + waving (present participle of wave)
词性:形容词, 名词
