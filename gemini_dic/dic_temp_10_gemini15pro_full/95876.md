increase:/ɪnˈkriːs/ (UK), /ɪnˈkriːs/ (US)| n. v.|增加，增长|an act or process of becoming larger or greater in size, amount, or degree|例句：There has been a significant increase in sales this year. 今年销售额显著增长。|近义词：rise, growth, expansion, augmentation|反义词:decrease, decline, reduction|词性:名词,动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *encreiss-, present participle of encreistre “to grow, increase,” from Latin increscere “to grow in, into; grow larger, increase,” from in- “in” + crescere “to grow.”
