outlay:/ˈaʊtleɪ/| n.|支出，费用；经费|An amount of money spent on something, especially as a first investment.|The company had a large outlay on new equipment.  该公司在新设备上投入了巨资。|近义词：expenditure, expense, cost, spending|反义词：income, revenue|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *outeley, from out- + lay (verb).  Ultimately from Old English *ūt- + lecgan (“to lay, put, place”).
