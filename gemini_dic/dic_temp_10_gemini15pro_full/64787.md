esloin:/ɪˈsloɪn/ | v. | 移除；使疏远；隔绝 | To remove or separate, especially to take away illicitly; to withdraw or become remote; to isolate or estrange. | He was esloined from his family by the war. 他因战争而与家人分离。 | The thief esloined the jewels and fled the country. 小偷偷走了珠宝并逃离了该国。 | 近义词: remove, separate, withdraw, alienate, estrange | 反义词: unite, join, connect | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *esloignier (Modern French éloigner), from Vulgar Latin *exlongare, from Latin ex- 'out of' + longus 'long'.
