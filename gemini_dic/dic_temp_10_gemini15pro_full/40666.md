convergingly:/kənˈvɜːrdʒɪŋli/
adverb
会聚地，收敛地
in a converging manner; towards a common point.
The lines on the graph are convergingly approaching the same value. 图表上的线条正趋于相同的值。
The two rivers flow convergingly towards the sea.  两条河流汇聚流向大海。
近义词: convergently, concurrently
反义词: divergently, separately
词性: adverb

词源: From converge + -ing + -ly.  Converge comes from Latin convergere "to incline together," from com- "together" (see com-) + vergere "to bend, turn, incline."
