bethought:/bɪˈθɔːt/
verb
想起，考虑，思考
To think about something; to consider or reflect upon something.
She bethought herself of a solution to the problem. 她想出了一个解决问题的办法。
He bethought himself that he had left his keys at home. 他想起他把钥匙忘在家里了。
consider, remember, recollect
forget, ignore
v.
