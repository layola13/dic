medium-sized:/ˈmiːdiəm saɪzd/
adjective
中等大小的
Of a size that is neither large nor small.
The company is looking for a medium-sized office space.  这家公司正在寻找一个中等大小的办公空间。
The dog is medium-sized, a bit bigger than a terrier but smaller than a collie. 这只狗中等大小，比梗犬稍大一些，但比柯利牧羊犬小。
近义词:average-sized, mid-sized
反义词:small, large, oversized, tiny
词性:adjective
