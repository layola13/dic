anelectrode:/ænˈɛlɪktroʊd/
n.
阳极
A positive electrode.
The anelectrode is connected to the positive terminal of the power supply.  阳极连接到电源的正极。
The anelectrode attracts negatively charged ions. 阳极吸引带负电荷的离子。
anode, positive electrode
cathode, negative electrode
noun
