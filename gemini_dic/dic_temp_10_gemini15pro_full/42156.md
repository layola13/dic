couchee:/ˈkuːʃeɪ/
noun
睡衣派对; 就寝时间
A social gathering held in the evening, typically for women, often involving pajamas or nightgowns.  Also, bedtime.
She invited all her girlfriends to a sleepover/couchee. 她邀请了她所有的女朋友来参加睡衣派对。
It's time for my little one's couchee. 我的小家伙该睡觉了。
Synonyms: sleepover, pajama party, bedtime
Antonyms: waking time, daytime
n.

Etymology->
From French "coucher," meaning "to put to bed" or "to lie down."
