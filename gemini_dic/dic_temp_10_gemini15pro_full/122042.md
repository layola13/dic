mist:/mɪst/| n.|薄雾，水汽，迷蒙|A thin fog, a cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the air, something that obscures understanding.|The morning mist hung heavy over the valley. 早晨的薄雾笼罩着山谷。|Near synonyms: fog, haze, vapor|Opposite: clarity, clearness|noun
