enshrouded:/ɪnˈʃraʊdɪd/
adjective
被笼罩的，被遮蔽的
covered or enveloped completely, as if by a shroud
The mountain peak was enshrouded in mist. 山峰被薄雾笼罩。
Synonyms: veiled, cloaked, obscured, covered, enveloped
Antonyms: revealed, exposed, uncovered, bare
词性:形容词
