boogie:/ˈbuːɡi/| vi. n.|布吉舞, 跳布吉舞|To dance to rock music; a lively dance to rock music.|例句：Let's go boogie! 我们去跳布吉舞吧！

例句：He played a boogie on the piano. 他用钢琴演奏了一段布吉舞曲。|近义词：dance, groove, jive|反义词:stand still|词性:verb, noun

Etymology:  Probably imitative of the rhythm and movement of the dance.  The word first emerged in the 1920s, initially associated with boogie-woogie piano music.
