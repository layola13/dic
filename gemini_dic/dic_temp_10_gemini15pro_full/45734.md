curvilinearity:/ˌkɜːrvɪlɪniˈærɪti/ | n. | 曲线性，曲线性，非线性 |The state or character of being curvilinear; consisting of or bounded by curved lines.  |The artist explored the curvilinearity of the human form in his sketches.  艺术家在他的素描中探索了人体的曲线性。|Synonyms: curvature, nonlinearity | Antonyms: linearity, straightness | noun

curvilinear:/ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniər/ | adj. | 曲线的，弯曲的 | Consisting of or bounded by curved lines; represented by a curved line. | The architect favored curvilinear designs, incorporating sweeping arches and rounded walls.  这位建筑师喜欢曲线设计，融合了拱形和圆形墙壁。 | Synonyms: curved, bending, sinuous | Antonyms: straight, linear, rectilinear | adjective
