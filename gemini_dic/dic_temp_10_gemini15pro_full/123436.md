monopolizable:/məˈnɒpəlaɪzəbl/ /məˈnɑːpəlaɪzəbl/
adjective
可垄断的
Capable of being monopolized; susceptible to monopoly control.
The production of this rare mineral is easily monopolizable due to limited sources.  这种稀有矿物的生产很容易被垄断，因为来源有限。
The company's innovative technology makes its market share highly monopolizable.  该公司创新的技术使其市场份额极易被垄断。
Synonyms: cornerable, controllable, dominable
Antonyms: sharable, competitive, open
词性: adjective
