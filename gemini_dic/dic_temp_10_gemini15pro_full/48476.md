deep-sea:/ˌdiːpˈsiː/| adj.|深海的|Of or relating to the deeper parts of the sea; pelagic.  Relating to or denoting the deepest parts of the sea, typically below 1,000 fathoms (1,800 metres).|例句：Deep-sea creatures are fascinating. 深海生物令人着迷。

例句：Deep-sea fishing can be a rewarding experience. 深海捕鱼可以是一次有益的经历。|近义词：pelagic, abyssal|反义词：shallow, coastal|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From deep + sea.  First recorded in the late 16th century.
