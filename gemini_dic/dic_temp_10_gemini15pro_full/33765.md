circulation:/ˌsɜːrkjəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 流通，循环，发行量 |The movement of blood through the body.  The passing of something from one person or place to another. The number of copies of a newspaper or magazine sold. |例句：The circulation of the newspaper has increased in recent years. 这家报纸的发行量近年来有所增加。

例句：Good circulation is essential for healthy organs. 良好的血液循环对器官健康至关重要。|近义词：distribution, flow, dissemination|反义词：stagnation, suppression|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *circulationem,  from Latin circulatus, past participle of circulare "to make round, to go around in a circle," from circulus "small ring, circle," diminutive of circus "circle."
