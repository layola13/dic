overboldness:/ˌoʊvərˈboʊldnəs/| n.|过度大胆；鲁莽；冒失|An act or instance of excessive boldness; audacity.  The quality or state of being overbold.|His overboldness in approaching the bear led to a dangerous encounter. 他过于大胆地靠近熊，导致了一次危险的遭遇。|近义词：audacity, temerity, rashness, presumption|反义词: timidity, caution, hesitancy|词性:名词

词源:
From over + boldness.  "Boldness" comes from Middle English *boldnes*, from Old English *bealdnes*, from *beald* (bold) + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). "Over-" is a common prefix indicating excess.
