by-jove:/baɪ dʒoʊv/
interjection
哎呀；天哪
An exclamation of surprise or emphasis.
By Jove, that's a splendid idea! 哎呀，这真是个好主意！
近义词: by George, by God, goodness me, my goodness, my word
反义词:  None (Interjections typically lack direct antonyms)
词性: interjection

词源:
From the oath "by Jove," where "Jove" is another name for Jupiter, the chief god in Roman mythology.  Using his name in an oath invoked his power and authority.  Over time, the oath became less religious and more a mild exclamation of surprise or emphasis.
