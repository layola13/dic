filmer:/ˈfɪlmər/| n.|拍摄者，电影制作者|A person who makes films, especially amateur films or home movies.|例句：He's an amateur filmer who posts his videos online. 他是一位业余电影制作人，在网上发布他的视频。

例句：The filmer captured the entire event on his phone.  这位拍摄者用手机录下了整个事件。|近义词：filmmaker, videographer, cinematographer|反义词:viewer, audience|词性:noun
