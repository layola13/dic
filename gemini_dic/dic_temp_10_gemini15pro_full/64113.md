eprint:/ˈiːprɪnt/ | n. |电子预印本，电子版预印本| An electronic version of a preprint, typically available online. |  The researcher posted the eprint on arXiv before submitting it to a journal.  研究人员在将论文提交给期刊之前，先将其电子预印本发布在arXiv上。 | 近义词: preprint, online preprint | 反义词:  printed version, hard copy | 词性: 名词

eprint:/ˈiːprɪnt/ | v. |以电子方式预印，发布电子预印本| To publish a preprint electronically, typically online. | The author eprinted their latest research on a preprint server. 作者在预印本服务器上发布了他们最新的研究成果的电子预印本。 | 近义词: post online, publish online | 反义词: print, publish in print | 词性: 动词
