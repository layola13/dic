obituarize: /əˈbɪtʃuəˌraɪz/
verb
撰写讣告；为…写讣告
to write an obituary for; to commemorate or mark the death of (someone or something) in a formal way, often in an obituary.
The local newspaper obituarized the beloved teacher who had served the community for over 50 years. 当地报纸为这位服务社区50多年的敬爱的老师撰写了讣告。
Several newspapers obituarized the famous author after his sudden death.  几位著名作家突然去世后，几家报纸都刊登了他的讣告。
近义词: memorialize, commemorate
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "celebrate" in the context of life rather than death)
词性: verb
