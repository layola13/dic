gauzy:/ˈɡɔːzi/| adj.|薄纱似的，朦胧的|Thin and translucent, like gauze; also, vague or unclear.|例句：The gauzy curtains allowed a soft light to filter into the room. 薄纱窗帘让柔和的光线透进房间。

例句：Her memories of childhood were gauzy and indistinct. 她对童年的记忆是朦胧而不清晰的。|近义词：sheer, transparent, filmy, hazy, vague|反义词：opaque, thick, clear, distinct|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From gauze + -y. Gauze comes from Middle French gaze, from Gaza, a city in Palestine where the fabric was originally made.
