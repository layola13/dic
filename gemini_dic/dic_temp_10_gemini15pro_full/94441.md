imploringly:/ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋli/
adverb
恳求地，哀求地
In a way that shows you are begging someone to do something.
She looked at him imploringly. 她恳求地看着他。
He raised his hands imploringly. 他恳求地举起双手。
近义词:beseechingly, pleadingly, supplicatingly
反义词:demandingly, assertively
词性:adverb

词源:
From implore + -ing + -ly.  Implore comes from Old French *implorer, from Latin implōrāre "to invoke with tears," from in- "on, upon" + plōrāre "to weep, cry out."
