noncosmopolitanisms: /nɒnˌkɒzməˈpɒlətənɪzəmz/ | n. | 非世界主义，非世界性 | The lack of cosmopolitan attitudes or perspectives; a focus on local or national concerns rather than global ones.  It can also refer to the specific ideologies or practices that reject cosmopolitanism. |  The rise of noncosmopolitanisms in various countries poses a challenge to international cooperation. 各国非世界主义的兴起对国际合作构成挑战。

The book explored the historical roots of various noncosmopolitanisms. 这本书探讨了各种非世界主义的历史根源。 | 近义词: parochialism, insularity, provincialism | 反义词: cosmopolitanism, globalism, internationalism | 词性: noun
