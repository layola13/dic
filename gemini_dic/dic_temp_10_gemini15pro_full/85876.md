headbanging: /ˈhɛdbæŋɪŋ/
n.| 甩头，撞头 (一种伴随着强劲音乐节奏摇晃或摆动头部的行为)|The act of shaking or banging one's head, typically in time with loud, heavy music.|He was headbanging so hard I thought he would injure himself. 他甩头甩得那么厉害，我以为他会伤到自己。
|近义词：moshing|反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑 stillness 静止)|词性:名词


headbang: /ˈhɛdbæŋ/
v.| 甩头，撞头|To shake or bang one's head, typically in time with loud, heavy music.|They were headbanging to their favorite metal band. 他们随着最喜欢的金属乐队的音乐甩头。
|近义词：mosh|反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑 stand still 站着不动)|词性:动词


词源:  Compound of *head* and *bang*.  First recorded use in the 1970s, referring to the practice among fans of hard rock and heavy metal music. 由 *head* 和 *bang* 组成。 首次记录使用于 20 世纪 70 年代，指的是硬摇滚和重金属音乐爱好者的这种做法。
