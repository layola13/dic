commix:/kəˈmɪks/ | v. | 混合，掺杂 | To mix together; to blend. | The chef commixed various spices to create a unique flavor. 厨师混合各种香料，创造出独特的风味。| The artist commixed different painting styles in her latest work.  这位艺术家在她的最新作品中混合了不同的绘画风格。 | 近义词: mix, blend, combine, mingle | 反义词: separate, divide, sort | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *commiscere, from Latin com- 'together' + miscere 'to mix'.
