enrichments:/ɪnˈrɪtʃmənts/
| n. |丰富；充实；改进；浓缩|The enrichments to the museum's collection have made it even more prestigious.  博物馆藏品的丰富使其更加负盛名。|These educational enrichments are designed to broaden students' horizons. 这些教育性的充实旨在拓宽学生的视野。|近义词: enhancements, improvements, additions, supplements|反义词: depletions, reductions, impoverishments|词性:名词


Etymology:
From Old French enrichment, from enricher “to enrich,” from en- “in” + riche “rich.”  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *rīkijaz “powerful, mighty, rich.”
