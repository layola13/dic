fipple:/ˈfɪpəl/| n.|吹口，笛塞，塞子|A small plug or stopper, especially one in the mouthpiece of a recorder or other fipple flute, against which the player's breath is directed.|The fipple on my recorder is cracked. 我的竖笛的吹口裂了。|近义词：block, plug, stopper|反义词:|noun
