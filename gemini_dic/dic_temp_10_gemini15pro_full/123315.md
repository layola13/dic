monogrammous: /məˈnɒɡrəməs/ | adj. | 一夫一妻制的，单配偶制的 | Having only one mate at a time. | Swans are known for being monogamous birds, mating for life. 天鹅以一夫一妻制而闻名，终生交配。| The gibbons are monogamous and mate for life. 长臂猿是一夫一妻制的，终生交配。 | 近义词: monandrous, uniparous | 反义词: polygamous, polyandrous | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *monos* "single, alone" + *gamos* "marriage"
