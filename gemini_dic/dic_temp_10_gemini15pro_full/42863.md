courtesies: /ˈkɜːrtəsiːz/ | n. | 礼貌；客气；礼节；好意 | Politeness or good manners; acts of kindness or respect. |  She treated everyone with courtesy and respect. 她以礼貌和尊重对待每个人。

The hotel offers a range of courtesies, including free Wi-Fi and airport shuttle. 这家酒店提供一系列的优待服务，包括免费无线网络和机场接送。

近义词: civilities, manners, politeness, respect, kindness

反义词: rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect, discourtesy

词性: noun (plural of courtesy)

词源: From Old French "courtoisie," derived from "court," meaning "court" or "royal household," referring to the refined manners practiced there.
