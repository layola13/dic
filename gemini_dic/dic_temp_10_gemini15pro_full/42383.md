counterblockade:/ˌkaʊntərblɑːˈkeɪd/ | n. |反封锁，对封锁的封锁|An act of blockading a place that has already blockaded oneself.|The British navy established a counterblockade against the French ports. 英国海军对法国港口实施了反封锁。| 近义词: countersiege, retaliation | 反义词: blockade, siege | noun


counterblockade:/ˌkaʊntərblɑːˈkeɪd/ | v. |反封锁，对…进行反封锁|To blockade a place that has already blockaded oneself.|The country counterblockaded its aggressive neighbor. 该国对其侵略的邻国进行了反封锁。| 近义词: retaliate, countersiege | 反义词: blockade, siege | verb
