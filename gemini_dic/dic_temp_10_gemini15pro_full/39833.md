conspire:/kənˈspaɪər/ | v. | 密谋，共谋，协力 | To secretly plan with someone else to do something bad or illegal. |  They conspired to overthrow the government. 他们密谋推翻政府。

The prisoners conspired to escape. 囚犯们密谋越狱。

| 近义词: plot, scheme, collude, intrigue | 反义词: disagree, oppose | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *conspirare* "to agree, unite, plot together," literally "to breathe together," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *spirare* "to breathe" (see spirit (n.)).
