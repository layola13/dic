crinklier:/ˈkrɪŋkliər/| adj.|更皱的，更多皱纹的|Comparative form of 'crinkly', meaning having more small folds or wrinkles.|The old woman's face was crinklier than a dried prune. 老妇人的脸比干梅子还要皱。|近义词：more wrinkled, more creased|反义词:smoother, flatter|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From crinkle + -ier (comparative suffix).  Crinkle itself likely originates from Middle Dutch *krinckelen, frequentative of *krincken "to bend, twist," related to Old English crincan "to shrink, bend, yield."
