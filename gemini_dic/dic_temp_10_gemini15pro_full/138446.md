nova's:/ˈnoʊvəz/| n.|新星的|Possessive form of nova, meaning belonging to or associated with a nova.  A nova is a star that suddenly increases greatly in brightness and then slowly returns to its original state over a period of weeks or months.|Example: The nova's light illuminated the surrounding nebula.|Synonym: star's (in a specific context)|Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "dwarf star's" for contrast)|noun
