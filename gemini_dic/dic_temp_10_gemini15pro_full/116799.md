mawkishness:/ˈmɔːkɪʃnəs/| n.|多愁善感, 感伤, 令人作呕的伤感|Excessive sentimentality or emotionality, often to the point of being sickening or insincere.|Her mawkishness made it difficult to take her seriously. 她的多愁善感让人很难认真对待她。|sentimentality, emotionalism, sappiness|unsentimentality, pragmatism|noun


mawkish:/ˈmɔːkɪʃ/| adj.|多愁善感的，令人作呕的|Excessively sentimental or emotional, often to the point of being sickening or insincere.|He gave a mawkish speech about his dead dog. 他就他死去的狗发表了一通感伤的演讲。|sentimental, emotional, sappy|unsentimental, pragmatic|adjective
