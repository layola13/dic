officiation: /əˌfɪʃiˈeɪʃən/
n.
主持；执行；司仪；裁判
the act of performing a religious ceremony or other public duty
例句：The officiation at the wedding was conducted by the groom's uncle. 婚礼由新郎的叔叔主持。

例句：Her officiation at the conference was praised by all attendees. 她在会议上的主持工作受到了所有与会者的好评。

近义词：administration, conducting, presiding, refereeing
反义词：non-participation, absence

词性: noun
