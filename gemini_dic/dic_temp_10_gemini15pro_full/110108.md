lickings:/ˈlɪkɪŋz/
n.
舔；打败；鞭打
A beating or thrashing.  An act of licking something.  A small amount of something.
The losing team took a real licking in the final game.  The cat gave its paw a quick licking.  Just a licking of butter, please.
defeats, beatings, thrashings, whippings, floggings
victories, triumphs, wins
noun
