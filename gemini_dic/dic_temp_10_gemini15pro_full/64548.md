erraticism:/ɪˈrætɪsɪzəm/
n.|反复无常，不稳定，古怪行为|The erraticism of the stock market makes it difficult to predict. 股市的反复无常使其难以预测。|
例句：
The erraticism of his behavior worried his friends. 他的行为反复无常，让他的朋友们很担心。
Her erraticism made it hard to rely on her. 她的反复无常使人难以依赖她。
|近义词：unpredictability, instability, capriciousness, volatility|反义词：stability, consistency, predictability|词性：名词


erratic:/ɪˈrætɪk/
adj.|不稳定的，反复无常的，古怪的|His erratic driving habits made his passengers nervous. 他不稳定的驾驶习惯使他的乘客感到紧张。|
例句：
The weather has been erratic lately. 最近天气变化无常。
He's always been an erratic character. 他一直是个古怪的人。
|近义词：unpredictable, inconsistent, capricious, volatile|反义词：stable, consistent, predictable|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *erraticus, from Latin errare "to wander, stray, err," from Proto-Italic *ersare, from Proto-Indo-European *ers- "to move, roam."
