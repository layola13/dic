half-barrel:/ˈhæf ˌbærəl/ | n. | 半桶 | A barrel with half the usual capacity.  A unit of liquid measure equal to half a barrel. | The brewery delivered ten half-barrels of beer for the festival. 酒厂为节日运送了十个半桶啤酒。| The recipe calls for a half-barrel of water.  这个配方需要半桶水。| synonym: keg, cask | antonym: full barrel | noun

Etymology:
"half" (Old English healf) + "barrel" (Old French barel)
