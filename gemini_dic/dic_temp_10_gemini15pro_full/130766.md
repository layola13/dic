nonanalogous:/ˌnɒnəˈnæləɡəs/
adjective
非类似的；不同的
Not analogous; dissimilar or unlike in certain respects.
The two cases are nonanalogous and should be treated separately.  这两个案例不相似，应该分开处理。
The situation in this country is nonanalogous to anything we've seen before. 这个国家的情况与我们以前见过的任何情况都不同。
synonym: dissimilar, different, unlike, disparate, heterogeneous
antonym: analogous, similar, alike, comparable, parallel
词性: 形容词
