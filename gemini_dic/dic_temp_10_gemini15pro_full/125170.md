multiblades:/ˈmʌltiˌbleɪdz/
n.
多刀片; 多叶片
Multiple blades; blades in an array or set.
The multiblades of the wind turbine spun rapidly.  风力涡轮机的多叶片快速旋转。
近义词: multiple blades, array of blades
反义词: single blade
词性: 名词
