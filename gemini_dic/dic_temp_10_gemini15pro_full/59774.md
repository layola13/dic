ecophene:/ˈiːkəˌfiːn/ | n. |生态型，生态表型|A population or group of organisms within a species that has adapted to a specific set of environmental conditions; a form of a species resulting from adaptation to particular environmental conditions.| The ecophenes of this plant species vary greatly in size depending on the altitude at which they grow.  这种植物的生态型在大小上差异很大，这取决于它们生长的海拔高度。|Synonyms: ecotype, morphotype | Antonyms: N/A | noun
