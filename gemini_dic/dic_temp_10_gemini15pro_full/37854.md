competitor:/kəmˈpɛtɪtər/ (US), /kəmˈpɛtɪtə/ (UK)| n. |竞争者，对手|A person, team, or company that competes against others.|例句：Our main competitors are other online retailers. 我们的主要竞争对手是其他在线零售商。

例句：She finished ahead of her closest competitor in the race. 她在比赛中领先于她最接近的竞争对手。

近义词：rival, opponent, contender, challenger|反义词：ally, partner, collaborator|词性：名词
