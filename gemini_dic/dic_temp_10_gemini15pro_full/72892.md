follower:/ˈfɒləʊər/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊər/ (US)| n.|追随者，信徒，支持者|A person who follows someone or something, especially a leader, teacher, or belief.|例句：She has a large following on social media. 她在社交媒体上有很多粉丝。|例句：He is a follower of the Buddhist faith. 他是佛教的信徒。|近义词：supporter, adherent, disciple, fan, devotee|反义词：leader, opponent, critic|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *folewer*, from Old English *folgere*, from *folgian* ("to follow").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fulgijaną* ("to follow").
