chastising: /ˈtʃæstaɪzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈtʃæstaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)
verb (present participle)
责备，惩罚
rebuking or reprimanding severely
例句：He spent the entire meeting chastising his team for their poor performance. 他在整个会议上都在斥责他的团队表现不佳。

例句：She was chastising her son for lying. 她正在责备她儿子说谎。

近义词：reprimanding, scolding, rebuking, berating, upbraiding
反义词：praising, commending, lauding, applauding
词性: verb (present participle)
