amortize: /ˈæmərˌtaɪz/ (US), /əˈmɔːtɪz/ (UK) | v. | 摊销，分期偿还 | To gradually reduce or write off the cost of an asset over a period of time. | The company amortizes its equipment purchases over five years.  这家公司在五年内摊销其设备采购成本。 |
Synonyms: depreciate, write off, expensed | Antonyms: appreciate, increase | verb
