cutting's:/ˈkʌtɪŋz/
noun
插枝，剪报，剪下的东西
Pieces of something, such as a plant, that have been cut off.  Also, articles cut from newspapers or magazines.
The gardener planted the rose cuttings in pots. 园丁把玫瑰插枝种在花盆里。
She kept a scrapbook of newspaper cuttings about her favorite band. 她保存了一本剪贴簿，里面都是关于她最喜欢的乐队的剪报。
synonyms: clippings, snippets, offcuts
antonyms: whole, uncut, complete
