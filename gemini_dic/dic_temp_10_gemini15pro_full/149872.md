palimony: /ˈpælɪmoʊni/
n.
赡养费（指未婚同居伴侣分手后的经济补偿）
Compensation resembling alimony but paid to a former cohabitant after separation.
Example: After their long-term relationship ended, she sued him for palimony.  她与他长期同居关系结束后，起诉他要求支付赡养费。

Example: The concept of palimony is relatively new in legal terms.  就法律术语而言，“分手费”的概念相对较新。

近义词: alimony, support payment
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific legal term)
词性: Noun
