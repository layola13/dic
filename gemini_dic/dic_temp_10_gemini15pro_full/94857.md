in-a-hole:/ɪn ə hoʊl/| adj.|处于困境中，负债累累|Being in a difficult or disadvantageous situation, especially financially.  Often implies being in debt or behind on payments.|He's in a hole after losing his job and accumulating medical bills. 他失业后又积累了医疗费用，现在陷入了困境。|Synonyms: in trouble, in debt, behind, struggling|Antonyms:  ahead, affluent, prosperous|adjective

in-a-hole:/ɪn ə hoʊl/| n.|困境，债务|A difficult or disadvantageous situation, especially financial. Often implies being in debt or behind on payments.|Her gambling addiction put her in a deep hole. 她的赌瘾让她陷入了深深的困境。|Synonyms: predicament, mess, difficulty, debt|Antonyms: advantage, prosperity|noun
