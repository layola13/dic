caudicula:/kɔːˈdɪkjʊlə/
noun
小尾；花粉块柄
A slender stalk attaching the pollen masses of an orchid to the viscidium.
The caudicula of the orchid is delicate and easily broken.  兰花的粘盘柄很脆弱，容易断裂。

近义词: stalk, pedicel, peduncle
反义词: N/A
词性: n.


Etymology:
From Latin *caudicula, diminutive of cauda (“tail”).
