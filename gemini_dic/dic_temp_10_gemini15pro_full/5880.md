ambassage:/ˈæmbəsɪdʒ/ /ˈæmbəsɪdʒ/
noun
大使的职务；使馆；外交使命
An ambassage is a mission to a foreign government.
The king sent an ambassage to the neighboring kingdom to negotiate a peace treaty. 国王派遣使节前往邻国谈判和平条约。
mission, embassy, legation
n/a
noun
