nondebilitations: /nɒndɪˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |  无衰弱；未削弱的状态 | The absence of debilitation; the state of not being weakened.  |  His quick recovery demonstrated a surprising lack of nondebilitations after the surgery.  他术后恢复迅速，显示出术后惊人的未衰弱状态。 | Near synonyms: strength, vigor, robustness | Antonyms: debilitation, weakness, frailty | noun

Etymology:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "debilitation" (from Late Latin *debilitationem, from Latin debilitare "to weaken," from debilis "weak").
