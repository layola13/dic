cremate:/krɪˈmeɪt/ | v. |火化，焚烧（尸体）|To reduce (a dead body) to ashes by fire.|His remains were cremated. 他的遗体被火化了。|Synonyms: incinerate, burn |Antonyms: bury, inter |verb

cremation:/krɪˈmeɪʃn/ | n. |火化，焚烧|The act of cremating a body.|Cremation is becoming increasingly popular. 火葬越来越普遍。|Synonyms: incineration |Antonyms: burial, interment |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cremātus*, past participle of *cremāre* (“to burn”), from *cremō*, *cremāre*, *cremāvi*, *cremātum* (“to burn”).
