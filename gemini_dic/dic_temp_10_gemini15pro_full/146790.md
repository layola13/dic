overillustrated:/ˌoʊvərˈɪləstreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度插图的
Having too many illustrations; excessively illustrated.
The children's book was overillustrated, with a picture on nearly every page.  这本儿童读物插图过多，几乎每一页都有一幅图。
