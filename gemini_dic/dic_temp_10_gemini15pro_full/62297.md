endangering:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒərɪŋ/ | adj. | 危害的，使受到危险的 | Putting oneself in harm's way, exposing to peril, jeopardizing. |  The construction project is endangering the local wildlife habitat.  这个建筑项目正在危及当地的野生动物栖息地。| 近义词: jeopardizing, imperiling, threatening | 反义词: protecting, safeguarding | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From "endanger" + "-ing" suffix.  "Endanger" comes from Old French "en dangier" (in danger). Ultimately from Latin "dominus" (lord, master) implying being under someone's power or control, thus in danger.
