mediatonic: /ˌmiːdiəˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 半音的，介于全音和半音之间的 | Relating to or involving a mediant; intermediate in pitch between a whole tone and a semitone.  | The mediatonic scale has several variations. 半音阶有几种变体。|  Near synonyms:  chromatic, diatonic | Antonyms: whole tone | adjective
