bacterin:/ˈbæktərɪn/
n.
Bacterial vaccine; a suspension of killed or attenuated bacteria used as a vaccine.
A bacterin is a vaccine prepared from killed bacteria.
Example: The farmer vaccinated his cows with a bacterin to protect them from brucellosis.  农民给他的牛接种了细菌疫苗以保护它们免受布鲁氏菌病的侵害。
Example:  The vet recommended a bacterin for the kennel cough outbreak. 兽医建议使用细菌疫苗来应对犬舍咳疫情。
Synonyms: vaccine, immunogen
Antonyms: None. (There isn't a direct antonym for a preventative treatment)
