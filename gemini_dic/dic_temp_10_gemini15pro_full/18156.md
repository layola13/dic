It is considered dialectal or informal. | Being-as-how it's raining, I'll stay home. 因为下雨，我将待在家里。| Synonym: since, because, seeing that, inasmuch as | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a conjunction, but contrasting conjunctions could be "although," "though," "even though") | conjunction

(While "being-as-how" doesn't typically form set phrases, it's used in informal speech to introduce clauses. It's less about specific phrases and more about its function as a conjunction.)  Here are some examples showing how it's used in sentences:

Being-as-how it's late, let's go home. = 因为很晚了，我们回家吧。
Being-as-how you're here, can you help me? = 既然你在这里，你能帮我一下吗？
I'm going to bed early, being-as-how I have to wake up early tomorrow. = 我要早点睡觉，因为我明天必须早起。
We should bring an umbrella, being-as-how the forecast calls for rain. = 我们应该带把伞，因为天气预报说要下雨。
Being-as-how he's not feeling well, he won't be coming to the party. = 因为他感觉不舒服，所以他不会来参加聚会了。


注意事项:
"Being-as-how" is considered informal and dialectal.  Avoid using it in formal writing.
