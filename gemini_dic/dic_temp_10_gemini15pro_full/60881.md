elementality:/ˌelɪmenˈtælɪti/
n.
元素性，基本性；自然力
The state or quality of being elemental or fundamental.
The painting captured the raw elementality of the landscape. 这幅画捕捉到了景色的原始元素。
n.
自然力，元素精灵
A being or force thought to embody or represent a natural element.
Ancient cultures believed in elementalities that controlled the weather. 古代文化相信控制天气的元素精灵。

近义词:nature, essence, fundamental, basic, rudimentary, primal, primeval,  elemental force, elemental spirit
反义词:complexity, sophistication, artificiality
词性:noun
