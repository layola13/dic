geometrical:/ˌdʒiːəˈmetrɪkl/
adjective
几何的，几何学的
relating to geometry, or according to its methods.
例句：The building has a geometrical design.  这座建筑物具有几何图案。

例句：Geometric patterns are visually appealing. 几何图案在视觉上很吸引人。

近义词：geometric
反义词: non-geometrical, irregular

词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *geometricalis, from Latin geometricus (“geometrical”), from Ancient Greek γεωμετρικός (geōmetrikós, “of or pertaining to geometry”), from γεωμέτρης (geōmétrēs, “geometer, land-measurer”), from γῆ (gê, “earth”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
