burble:/ˈbɜːrbl/ (US) /ˈbɜːb(ə)l/ (UK)| n.|潺潺声；喋喋不休|A continuous murmuring sound like that of water flowing over pebbles. Lengthy excited or enthusiastic talk about something trivial.|The burble of the stream was soothing. 溪流的潺潺声令人舒缓。

Her constant burble about her vacation was getting tiresome. 她不停地唠叨她的假期，让人厌烦。|近义词：babble, gurgling, murmur, chatter|反义词:silence, quiet|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *burbelen, frequentative of burblen (“to bubble”), from Old French *borboler, from Latin *burbulare, from bulla (“bubble”).
