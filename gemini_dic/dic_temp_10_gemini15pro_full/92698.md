icehouse:/ˈaɪsˌhaʊs/| n.|冰窖；冰库；寒冷的地方|A building used to store ice, especially for commercial use.  A place that is very cold.|例句：The icehouse kept the food cold during the summer. 冰窖在夏天保持食物的低温。

例句：The cabin felt like an icehouse in the middle of winter.  冬天里，小屋感觉像个冰窖。|近义词：cold storage, freezer, refrigerator|反义词:oven, furnace|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *yshows*, from Old English *īshūs* (“ice house”), from *īs* (“ice”) + *hūs* (“house”).  The word is a compound of two very old Germanic roots.
