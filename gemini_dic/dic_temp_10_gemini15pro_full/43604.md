creams:/kriːmz/
n.|奶油；乳霜；面霜；精华；(颜色)米色|A thick, white or pale yellow fatty liquid which rises to the top of milk, used in cooking or eaten as a dessert.  Also, a smooth, thick substance rubbed on the skin to make it soft or to treat a skin problem.|She whipped the cream until it was thick. 她把奶油打到浓稠。|近义词：lotions, ointments, emulsions|反义词：liquids|词性:名词

creams:/kriːmz/
v.|结成奶油状；(使)起泡沫；提取精华|To form cream; to become covered with cream; to take the best part of something.|The soup creamed as it cooled. 汤冷却后结成了奶油状。|近义词：skim, top|反义词：thin, dilute|词性:动词
