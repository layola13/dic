differentiatedness:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪeɪtɪdnəs/| n. |区别性，差异性|The state or quality of being differentiated; the extent to which something is distinct or separate from other things.|The differentiatedness of the product line allows the company to target various consumer segments.|近义词：distinctiveness, uniqueness, individuality, separateness|反义词：similarity, sameness, homogeneity|词性:noun
Etymology: From differentiate + -ed + -ness.  Differentiate ultimately derives from Latin *differentiāre, from differēns (“different”).
