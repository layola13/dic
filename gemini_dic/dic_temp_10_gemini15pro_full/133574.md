nonflakier:/ˌnɑːnˈfleɪkiər/
adjective
更稳定可靠的; 不那么古怪的
less flaky; more reliable and consistent
例句：He's become a nonflakier employee since his promotion.  自从升职后，他成为了一个更稳定可靠的员工。

例句：This new version of the software is nonflakier and performs better.  这个新版本的软件更稳定，性能更好。
近义词：reliable, dependable, consistent, stable
反义词：flaky, unreliable, erratic, unpredictable
词性: adjective

词源:
"Nonflakier" is a combination of "non-" (a prefix indicating negation) and "flakier" (the comparative form of "flaky"). "Flaky" originally referred to something that breaks apart easily into flakes, like pastry.  Over time, it developed a figurative meaning of "unreliable" or "eccentric."  Therefore, "nonflakier" means "less flaky" or "more reliable."
