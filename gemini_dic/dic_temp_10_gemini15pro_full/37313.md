commeasurable:/kəˈmɛnʃərəbl/ /kəˈmɛʒərəbl/
adjective
可比的，可测量的，相称的
able to be measured or compared, especially in terms of quantity or quality.  Something is commensurable with something else if it can be measured or compared with it using the same standard or unit.
例句：Their achievements are hardly commensurable. 他们的成就很难相提并论。

例句：The two quantities are commensurable. 这两个量是可以比较的。


近义词：comparable, proportionate, similar, analogous, equivalent
反义词：incommensurable, disparate, dissimilar, different, unequal
词性：adjective
