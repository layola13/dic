destructive:/dɪˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
破坏性的；毁灭性的
Causing destruction or damage.
The hurricane was highly destructive.  飓风具有极强的破坏力。
The destructive power of nuclear weapons is immense. 核武器的破坏力是巨大的。
synonyms: damaging, harmful, detrimental, ruinous
antonyms: constructive, beneficial, helpful
词性:形容词
