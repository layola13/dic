daemonic:/dɪˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. |恶魔的；魔鬼似的|Possessed by or as if by a demon; fiendish. |例句：He had a daemonic gleam in his eyes. 他的眼中闪着恶魔般的光芒。

例句：The daemonic laughter echoed through the halls. 恶魔般的笑声回荡在大厅里。|近义词：diabolical, fiendish, satanic, infernal, hellish|反义词：angelic, divine, heavenly|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *daemonicus, from Ancient Greek δαιμονικός (daimonikós), from δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, spirit”).
