multifunctional:/ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adjective
多功能的
Having or fulfilling several different functions.
This multifunctional device serves as a printer, scanner, and copier.  这个多功能设备可用作打印机、扫描仪和复印机。
This room is multifunctional, serving as both a bedroom and a study. 这个房间是多功能的，既可以用作卧室，也可以用作书房。

synonym: versatile, all-purpose, adaptable
antonym: single-purpose, specialized, limited

ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (meaning "many") + functional (meaning "performing a function").  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many." "Functional" comes from the Late Latin "functionalis,"  ultimately from Latin "functio"  meaning "performance, execution."
