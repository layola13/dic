disshroud:/dɪˈʃraʊd/
verb
揭开；使露出；揭露
To uncover or reveal something that was previously hidden or concealed.
The morning mist disshrouded the mountains.  晨雾散去，群山显露出来。
The investigation disshrouded a complex web of deceit. 调查揭露了一个复杂的欺骗网。
近义词: unveil, uncover, reveal, expose
反义词: shroud, conceal, hide, cover
词性: verb
