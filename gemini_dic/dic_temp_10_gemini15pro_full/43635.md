creationist:/kriˈeɪʃənɪst/
n.|神创论者，创造论者|A person who believes that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.|He's a staunch creationist and rejects all evidence for evolution. 他是一个坚定的神创论者，拒绝所有进化论的证据。|
synonyms:anti-evolutionist,  proponent of creationism|
antonyms:evolutionist, Darwinist|
noun


creationist:/kriˈeɪʃənɪst/
adj.|神创论的，创造论的|Relating to the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation.|He holds creationist views. 他持有神创论的观点。|
synonyms:anti-evolutionary|
antonyms:evolutionary, Darwinian|
adjective
