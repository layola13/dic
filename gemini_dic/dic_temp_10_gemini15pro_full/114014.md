magmatically:/mæɡˈmætɪkli/ | adv. |岩浆地；以岩浆的方式| In a magmatic manner; by means of magma.  | The rocks were formed magmatically deep within the earth. 这些岩石在地球深处以岩浆的方式形成。| Synonym:  igneously, volcanically | Antonym: sedimentarily |词性:副词

magmatic:/mæɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. |岩浆的；由岩浆产生的| Relating to, resembling, or derived from magma. | The magmatic rocks were analyzed for their mineral content.  对岩浆岩的矿物含量进行了分析。| Synonym:  igneous, volcanic | Antonym: sedimentary |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *magmatique* and directly from Modern Latin *magmaticus*, from Ancient Greek *μάγμα* (*magma*, "kneaded mass, salve")  + *-ikos* (suffix forming adjectives of appurtenance).
