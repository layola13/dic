fireside:/ˈfaɪərsaɪd/| adj.|家庭的，炉边的|Relating to or suitable for a fireside; domestic, cozy, informal.|Fireside chats create a warm and intimate atmosphere. 炉边谈话营造了一种温暖亲切的氛围。|近义词：homey, cozy, domestic|反义词: outdoorsy|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fire + side.  The word "fireside" dates back to the late 16th century. "Fire" comes from Old English "fȳr," and "side" from Old English "sīde."  The combined word literally refers to the side of a fire.
