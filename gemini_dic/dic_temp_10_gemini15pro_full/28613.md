caressing:/kəˈrɛsɪŋ/
v.
爱抚；抚摸
touching or rubbing someone or something gently and affectionately.
She was caressing the soft fur of the cat. 她正在抚摸猫咪柔软的皮毛。
He caressed her cheek tenderly. 他温柔地抚摸着她的脸颊。
stroking, fondling, petting
hitting, striking
verb
