desilted:/diːˈsɪltɪd/
verb
去除淤泥的，清淤的
Cleared of silt; having had silt removed.
The harbor was desilted to allow larger ships to enter. 港口清除了淤泥，以便更大的船只进入。
近义词: dredged, cleared
反义词: silted, clogged
词性: Verb


Etymology:
From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + silt + -ed (past participle suffix).  The word "silt" comes from Middle English, possibly from Old Norse or Old Low German.
