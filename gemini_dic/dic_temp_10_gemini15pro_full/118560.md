mesopodium:/ˌmesəˈpoʊdiəm/ | n. | 中足, 腹中段 | The mesopodium is the middle part of the foot in some animals. |  The lizard's mesopodium allows it to grip surfaces effectively.  蜥蜴的中足使它能够有效地抓住表面。 | near synonyms: midfoot, central foot segment | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider acropodium (front part of foot) or metapodium (hind part of foot)) | noun

mesopodium:/ˌmesəˈpoʊdiəm/ | n. |中膜, 中隔 | In botany, the mesopodium refers to the middle layer of the  pericarp (fruit wall). | The mesopodium of the fruit is fleshy and sweet. 这种水果的中果皮肉质甜美。 | near synonyms: middle layer, mesocarp | antonyms: exocarp (outer layer), endocarp (inner layer) | noun
