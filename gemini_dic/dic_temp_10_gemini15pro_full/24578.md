brochettes:/brɒˈʃɛts/ /broʊˈʃɛts/| n. |烤肉串，串烧|Small pieces of meat, fish, or vegetables threaded onto skewers and grilled or roasted.|例句：We had chicken brochettes for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了鸡肉串。

例句：The restaurant is known for its delicious lamb brochettes. 这家餐厅以美味的羊肉串而闻名。|近义词：kebabs, skewers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "stew" or "casserole" if considering the method of cooking)|词性:noun
