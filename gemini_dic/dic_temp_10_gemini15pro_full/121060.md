misconnects:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkts/
| n. v. |错误连接，连接失败，误解|A failure to connect properly or a misunderstanding.|
|The phone call suffered from frequent misconnects, making conversation difficult.  电话经常断线，使谈话变得很困难。|
|The misconnect between the marketing team and the product development team resulted in a poorly received product. 营销团队和产品开发团队之间的沟通不畅导致产品反响不佳。|
|近义词：misunderstandings, disconnections, failures|
|反义词：connections, links, agreements|
|词性: 名词, 动词|
|词源:  From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + connect. |
