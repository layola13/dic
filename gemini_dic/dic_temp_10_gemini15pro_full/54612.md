dismaying:/dɪsˈmeɪɪŋ/| adj.|令人沮丧的，令人失望的|Causing dismay; disheartening.  Feeling or showing discouragement, disappointment, or alarm.|The news of the factory closing was dismaying to the workers. 工厂倒闭的消息令工人们感到沮丧。|近义词：disheartening, discouraging, upsetting, alarming, distressing|反义词：encouraging, heartening, cheering, uplifting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dismay + -ing. Dismay comes from Old French "desmayer," meaning "to lose courage."
