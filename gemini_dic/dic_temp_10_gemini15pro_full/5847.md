amazement:/əˈmeɪzmənt/| n.|惊讶，惊奇|A feeling of great surprise or wonder.|She stared in amazement at the magician's trick. 她惊讶地盯着魔术师的戏法。|近义词：astonishment, wonder, surprise, awe|反义词：indifference, apathy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *amasement, *amasment, from Anglo-Norman amasement, amesment, from Old French amasement, esmaiement (“stupefaction, bewilderment”), from esmaier (“to dismay, to bewilder”), from Vulgar Latin *exmagāre, from Latin ex (“out of”) + magus (“magician, wizard”) (ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *magh- (“to be able, to have power”)).
