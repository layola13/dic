blimp:/blɪmp/| n. |小型飞船，软式飞艇|A non-rigid airship.  A small airship without a rigid structure, relying on internal pressure to maintain its shape.|例句：The blimp floated silently over the stadium.  小型飞船静静地飘浮在体育场上空。

例句：The company uses a blimp for advertising.  这家公司使用小型飞船做广告。|近义词：airship, dirigible|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "airplane" as a contrasting form of air travel) |词性:noun
