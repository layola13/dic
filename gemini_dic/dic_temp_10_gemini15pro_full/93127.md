PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样  What if it rains tomorrow? 如果明天下雨怎么办？
what for = 为了什么  What did you buy that for? 你买那东西干什么？
no matter what = 无论如何  No matter what happens, I'll be there for you. 无论发生什么事，我都会在你身边。
what's more = 而且；更重要的是 What's more, it's free!  而且，它是免费的！
what about = ...怎么样 What about going to the movies tonight? 今晚去看电影怎么样？
