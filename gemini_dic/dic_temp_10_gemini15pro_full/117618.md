megarian: /məˈɡɪəriən/ /məˈɡɛəriən/
| adj. n.|麦加拉的；麦加拉学派的；极端吝啬的；麦加拉学派的人|Relating to Megara, an ancient Greek city-state, or its philosophical school, known for its emphasis on logic and dialectic. Also used to describe someone extremely parsimonious.|The Megarian school of philosophy was influential in ancient Greece. 麦加拉学派在古希腊很有影响力。|Synonyms: parsimonious, stingy, miserly|Antonyms: generous, prodigal|adjective, noun

megarian: /məˈɡɪəriən/ /məˈɡɛəriən/
| n.|麦加拉人|A native or inhabitant of Megara.|He was a Megarian by birth. 他是麦加拉人。|Synonyms: inhabitant, resident, citizen|Antonyms: foreigner, alien|noun
