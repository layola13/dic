gemmate:/ˈdʒɛmeɪt/ | adj. v. |发芽的，萌芽的；发芽，萌芽 |Reproducing by the formation of buds; having or resembling buds.  |例句：The gemmate leaf fell to the ground and began to grow. 发芽的叶子落到地上开始生长。

例句：Some plants gemmate in the spring. 有些植物在春天发芽。|近义词：bud, sprout, germinate, burgeon |反义词: wither, die |词性: adjective, verb

词源: From Latin *gemmatus, past participle of gemmare (“to bud”), from gemma (“bud”).
