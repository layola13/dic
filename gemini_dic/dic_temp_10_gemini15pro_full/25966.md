burrower:/ˈbɜːroʊər (US), ˈbʌrəʊər (UK)| n.|穴居动物；挖洞者|An animal that makes a burrow or hole in the ground to live in. |例句：The badger is a nocturnal burrower. 獾是一种夜行穴居动物。

例句：The mole is a common burrower in gardens. 鼹鼠是花园里常见的穴居动物。|近义词：excavator, digger|反义词: - |词性: noun
