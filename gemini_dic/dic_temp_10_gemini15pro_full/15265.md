backing:/ˈbækɪŋ/| n. |支持，赞助；背景，衬背；伴奏|The project has the backing of several major investors. 这个项目得到了几位主要投资者的支持。|support, sponsorship, endorsement|opposition, resistance|noun
backing:/ˈbækɪŋ/| adj. |支持的，伴奏的|The backing vocals were excellent. 和声非常出色。|supporting, accompanying|opposing|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "to back," which comes from the Old English "bac" meaning "back." The original sense was literal, referring to the back of something.  The figurative sense of "support" developed later.
