stipulation:/ˌstɪpjʊˈleɪʃn/
n.
规定，约定，条款
A stipulation is a condition or requirement that is specified or demanded as part of an agreement.
The contract included a stipulation that the work must be completed within six months.  合同中规定，工作必须在六个月内完成。
The loan was granted with the stipulation that it be repaid within two years.  这笔贷款获准的条件是必须在两年内偿还。
Synonyms: condition, requirement, provision, clause, term
Antonyms: flexibility, leniency

词性: noun
