nonmitigatory:/ˌnɒnˈmɪtɪɡətɔːri/
adjective
非减轻的，不缓和的
Not serving to mitigate, alleviate, or lessen.
The government's response to the crisis was criticized as nonmitigatory. 政府对危机的反应被批评为没有起到缓解作用。
The defendant argued that his actions were nonmitigatory, as they did not lessen the severity of the crime.  被告辩称，他的行为并没有减轻罪行的严重性，因此是非减轻性的。
Near Synonyms:  non-alleviating, non-remedial, exacerbating, aggravating
Antonyms: mitigatory, alleviating, remedial, extenuating
