colable:/ˈkoʊləbl/| adj.|可乐色的，焦炭色的|Having the dark brown color of cola.  Suitable for being coked (converted into coke).|例句：The liquid had a colable hue.  这种液体呈可乐色。

The coal is of good quality and highly colable. 这种煤质量好，非常适合炼焦。|近义词：brown, dark brown, caramel-colored|反义词:colorless, transparent|词性:adjective
