campanili:/ˌkæmpəˈniːliː/ /ˌkæmpəˈniːlaɪ/
n.
钟楼；（尤指意大利教堂的）独立式钟楼
A bell tower, especially one freestanding from the main church building (especially in Italy).
The campanili of St. Mark's square in Venice are iconic. 威尼斯圣马可广场的钟楼是标志性的建筑。
The earthquake damaged several of the city's ancient campanili. 地震损坏了这座城市几座古老的钟楼。
近义词：bell tower, belfry
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical structure)
词性: noun
