haphazard:/ˌhæpˈhæzərd/| adj.|偶然的，随意的，杂乱无章的|marked by lack of plan, order, or direction|The books were arranged in a haphazard manner on the shelves. 书架上的书摆放得很随意。|synonyms: random, arbitrary, unsystematic, chaotic, disorganized|antonyms: planned, organized, systematic, methodical|adjective

haphazard:/ˌhæpˈhæzərd/| n.|偶然事件；偶然性；任意性|chance; accident|The discovery was made by pure haphazard.  这个发现纯属偶然。|synonyms: chance, accident, fluke, serendipity|antonyms: intention, plan, design|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hap (n.) "chance, fortune" + hazard (n.) "a game of chance."  Originally meant “characterized by chance,” later acquired the sense of “disorganized, random.”
