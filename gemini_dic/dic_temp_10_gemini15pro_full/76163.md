fusillade:/ˌfjuːzɪˈleɪd/ | n. v. | 枪击，(批评、问题等的)连发，大量倾泻 | A fusillade is a rapid and continuous discharge of firearms or something resembling this, such as questions or criticisms. |  The troops were met with a fusillade of bullets. 部队遭到一连串的子弹射击。| 近义词：barrage, volley, bombardment | 反义词:trickle, dribble | 词性:名词, 动词

fusillade:/ˌfjuːzɪˈleɪd/ |  v. |  以密集火力攻击，大量倾泻 | To attack with a fusillade. | The speaker was fusilladed with questions from the audience. 演讲者遭到观众连珠炮似的提问。| 近义词：bombard, pepper, shower | 反义词: withhold, suppress | 词性:动词

词源: From French *fusillade*, from *fusiller* "to shoot," from *fusil* "musket."
