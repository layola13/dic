flourishing:/ˈflɜːrɪʃɪŋ/ /ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
繁荣的，兴旺的
Growing or developing successfully; thriving.
例句：The company is flourishing under the new management.  这家公司在新管理层下蓬勃发展。

例句：The arts are flourishing in this city.  艺术在这个城市蓬勃发展。

近义词：thriving, prosperous, booming, successful
反义词：declining, failing, struggling, withering
词性：adjective


flourishing:/ˈflɜːrɪʃɪŋ/ /ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋ/
noun
繁荣，兴旺
A period of growth and success.
例句：The 1920s were a time of great flourishing for the arts. 20世纪20年代是艺术蓬勃发展的时期。

例句：The city experienced a flourishing of trade in the 18th century.  这座城市在18世纪经历了贸易的繁荣。

近义词：prosperity, boom, success, growth
反义词：decline, failure, struggle, decay
词性：noun


Etymology:  From Middle English *florisshing, *flourisshing, present participle of florisshen, flourisshen (“to flourish, blossom, bloom, prosper”), from Anglo-Norman florir, flurir, from Old French florir (“to blossom, flower, bloom, flourish”), from Latin flōrēre (“to bloom, blossom, flower, flourish”), from flōs (“flower”).
