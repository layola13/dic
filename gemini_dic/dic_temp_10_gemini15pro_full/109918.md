libeled:/ˈlaɪbəld/ (US) /ˈlaɪbəld/ (UK)| v. |诽谤，中伤|To make false and damaging statements about someone. |例句：The newspaper libeled the politician with accusations of corruption. 这家报纸诽谤了这位政治家，指控他腐败。

例句：He claimed he had been libeled in a magazine article. 他声称自己在一篇杂志文章中被诽谤了。|近义词：defamed, slandered, calumniated|反义词：praised, lauded|词性：动词

libel:/ˈlaɪbəl/ (US) /ˈlaɪbəl/ (UK)| n. |诽谤，中伤|A false and damaging statement about someone. |例句：The article was a vicious libel against her character. 这篇文章是对她人格的恶意诽谤。

例句：He sued the newspaper for libel. 他起诉了这家报纸，控告其诽谤。|近义词：defamation, slander, calumny|反义词：praise, commendation|词性：名词
