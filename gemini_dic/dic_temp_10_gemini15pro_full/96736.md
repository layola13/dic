inelegant:/ɪnˈɛlɪɡənt/| adj.|不雅的，粗俗的，笨拙的|Lacking elegance, grace, or refinement; not pleasing in appearance or style.|Her inelegant solution to the problem caused more issues than it solved. 她对这个问题笨拙的解决方法造成了更多的问题，而不是解决了问题。|The code was functional but inelegant. 这段代码有效，但不优雅。|近义词：awkward, clumsy, graceless, ungainly, unrefined|反义词：elegant, graceful, refined, stylish|词性:形容词

词源: in- (not) + elegant (from Latin *elegans*, present participle of *elegere* "to choose")
