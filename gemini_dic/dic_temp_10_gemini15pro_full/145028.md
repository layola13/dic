overaffirming: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɜːrmɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度肯定的；过分赞同的 |  The act of affirming something excessively or to an unwarranted degree.  It implies an affirmation that goes beyond what is reasonable or justified. | Her overaffirming parenting style resulted in a child who lacked self-reliance. 她过度肯定的育儿方式导致孩子缺乏自立能力。 |  He was overaffirming in his praise of the mediocre performance. 他对平庸的表演给予了过分的赞扬。 | 近义词: overpraising, excessively supportive | 反义词: critical, disapproving | 词性: adjective

词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "affirming" (present participle of "affirm")
"Over-" denotes excess or going beyond a limit.
"Affirm" comes from the Latin "affirmare," meaning "to strengthen, confirm."
