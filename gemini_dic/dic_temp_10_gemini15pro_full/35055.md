closemouthed:/ˌkloʊzˈmaʊðd/ /ˌkləʊzˈmaʊθt/| adj.|沉默寡言的，嘴巴紧的|Not inclined to speak; reticent.  Reserved in speech; uncommunicative.|He was closemouthed about his plans. 他对自己的计划守口如瓶。|近义词：reticent, taciturn, tight-lipped|反义词：talkative, loquacious, garrulous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From close + mouthed.  "Close" in this context means "shut" or "closed".
