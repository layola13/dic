ordinative:/ˈɔːrdɪnətɪv/
adjective
序列的，顺序的
relating to or denoting a grammatical case expressing order or succession.
The ordinative case is used to indicate the order in which things happen. 序列格用来表示事情发生的顺序。
The second task is ordinative to the first. 第二个任务排在第一个任务之后。
近义词:sequential, ordered
反义词:unordered, random
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *ōrdinātivus, from ōrdinātus (“arranged, ordered”), past participle of ōrdinō (“I arrange”), from ōrdō (“order, row, rank”).
