antisplenetic:/ˌæntisplɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
消除怒气的；令人愉快的；治疗脾脏病的
Relieving ill humor, cheerful; good for the spleen.
The comedian's antisplenetic jokes lifted the audience's spirits.  喜剧演员消除怒气的笑话让观众精神振奋。
Her antisplenetic nature made her a joy to be around. 她令人愉快的性格使人乐于和她相处。
synonym
cheering, jovial, merry, mirthful, pleasant, amiable
antonym
depressing, gloomy, melancholic, sullen, morose
Etymology
From Late Latin *antispleneticus, from Ancient Greek ἀντισπληνικός (antisplēnikós, “good for the spleen”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + σπλήν (splḗn, “spleen”).  The spleen was once believed to be the seat of melancholy or anger.
