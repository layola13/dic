demitasse:/ˌdɛmiˈtæs/ /ˌdɛmɪˈtæs/ | n. |小咖啡杯；小咖啡杯的容量|A small cup for serving strong black coffee, typically holding about 2-3 ounces.  A serving of strong black coffee in such a cup. | She ordered a demitasse after dinner. 她晚饭后点了一小杯浓咖啡。|  He poured himself a demitasse of Turkish coffee. 他给自己倒了一小杯土耳其咖啡。|Synonyms: espresso cup, small coffee cup | Antonyms: mug, large coffee cup | noun

demitasse:/ˌdɛmiˈtæs/ /ˌdɛmɪˈtæs/ | adj. |(of a coffee cup) small; (of coffee) served in a small cup. |  A demitasse cup is perfect for a strong espresso.  一个小咖啡杯非常适合盛浓缩咖啡。| She prefers her coffee demitasse. 她喜欢喝小杯咖啡。|Synonyms: small, miniature | Antonyms: large, oversized | adjective
