besetments:/bɪˈsɛtmənts/
|n.|困境，围攻，困扰|troubles, difficulties, or worries that affect someone or something severely.|The company was beset by besetments on all sides, from falling sales to rising costs.  这家公司面临着来自各方面的困境，从销售额下降到成本上升。|
|例句：The small town was beset by besetments, including a failing economy and a rise in crime.  这个小镇被各种困境所困扰，包括经济衰退和犯罪率上升。|
|例句：His life seemed full of besetments, one after another. 他的生活似乎充满了接踵而至的困扰。|
|近义词：troubles, difficulties, problems, afflictions, tribulations, worries, anxieties|
|反义词：advantages, benefits, blessings, fortunes|
|词性：名词|


词源：
From  beset (v.) + -ment.  Beset comes from Old English besettan "to surround, set upon," from be- "around" + settan "to set."
