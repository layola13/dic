holmium:/ˈhəʊlmiəm/ (US) /ˈhoʊlmiəm/| n. |钬|A chemical element, atomic number 67, a silvery-white metal of the lanthanide series. It is used in some lasers and in nuclear reactors.|Holmium is used in certain medical lasers. 钬用于某些医用激光器。|Synonyms: Ho (symbol), element 67 |Antonyms: None (as it is a chemical element)|noun

holmium oxide:/ˈhəʊlmiəm ˈɒksaɪd/ (US) /ˈhoʊlmiəm ˈɑːksaɪd/| n. |氧化钬|A compound of holmium and oxygen, often used as a yellow or red colorant in glass and ceramics.|Holmium oxide gives a distinctive color to certain types of glass. 氧化钬赋予某些类型的玻璃独特的颜色。|Synonyms: holmia|Antonyms: None (as it is a chemical compound)|noun
