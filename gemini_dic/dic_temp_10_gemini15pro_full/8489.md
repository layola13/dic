antibiblical:/ˌæntibaɪblɪkəl/
adjective
反圣经的，违反圣经的
Opposing or contradicting the Bible or its teachings.
His antibiblical stance caused controversy within the church.  他反圣经的立场在教会内引起了争议。
The group held antibiblical beliefs, rejecting many of the core tenets of Christianity.  这个团体持有反圣经的信仰，拒绝基督教的许多核心信条。
Synonyms:  unscriptural, irreligious, profane, blasphemous
Antonyms:  biblical, scriptural, religious, pious
