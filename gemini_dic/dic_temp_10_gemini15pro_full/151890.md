passportless:/ˈpɑːspɔːrtlɪs/
adjective
无护照的；没有护照的
Without a passport.
He tried to cross the border passportless. 他试图无证越境。
Synonyms: undocumented, unauthorized
Antonyms: documented, authorized
词性: 形容词
