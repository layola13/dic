insectile:/ɪnˈsɛktaɪl/
adjective
昆虫的；昆虫似的
Resembling or suggesting an insect, especially in a creepy or unpleasant way.
The creature had long, insectile legs.  这个生物有着细长的、像昆虫一样的腿。
Her insectile movements made me shudder. 她像昆虫一样的动作让我不寒而栗。

近义词:insectlike, buggy
反义词:mammalian, avian
词性:adjective
