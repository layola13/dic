clumpiest:/ˈklʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最块状的，最笨重的，最结块的|Superlative form of "clumpy," meaning having many clumps or lumps.  It describes something that is composed of or covered in many thick, compact masses.|The clumpiest mashed potatoes I've ever seen were served at that restaurant. 我在那家餐厅吃到的土豆泥是我见过最结块的。

The hiker's boots were the clumpiest, covered in mud and leaves.  徒步旅行者的靴子最笨重，上面沾满了泥巴和树叶。|近义词：lumpy, bumpy, thick, dense|反义词:smooth, even, fine, thin|词性:adjective

Etymology: From "clump" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Clump" likely comes from a Scandinavian source, related to Danish "klump" (lump, clod).
