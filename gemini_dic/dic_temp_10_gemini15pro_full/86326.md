hecatomb: /ˈhɛkəˌtuːm/, /ˈhɛkəˌtɑːm/ | n. | 大屠杀，百牲祭 | A great public sacrifice, originally of a hundred oxen; hence, a general slaughter or massacre. | The king ordered a hecatomb to appease the gods. 国王下令进行百牲祭以安抚众神。 |  The dictator's rise to power resulted in a hecatomb of his political opponents. 独裁者上台导致他的政治对手遭到大屠杀。| 近义词: massacre, slaughter, carnage | 反义词: peace, amnesty | 词性: 名词
