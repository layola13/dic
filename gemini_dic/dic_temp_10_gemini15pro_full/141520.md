Oppenheim: /ˈɒpənhaɪm/ (UK), /ˈoʊpənhaɪm/ (US)| n. |奥本海姆|A surname of German origin, derived from a place name meaning "open home" or "open house". It is also associated with the German-Jewish banking family, Oppenheim & Co.|E.g., Max von Oppenheim was a German diplomat and archaeologist. 马克斯·冯·奥本海姆是一位德国外交官和考古学家。|Synonym: (As a surname, no direct synonym) |Antonym: (As a surname, no direct antonym) |Proper noun

Oppenheim: /ˈɒpənhaɪm/ (UK), /ˈoʊpənhaɪm/ (US)| adj. |奥本海姆的|Relating to or associated with the Oppenheim family or the town of Oppenheim.|E.g., The Oppenheim Collection of Mesopotamian artifacts is renowned. 奥本海姆收藏的美索不达米亚文物很有名。|Synonym: (Context-dependent) |Antonym: (Context-dependent) |Adjective
