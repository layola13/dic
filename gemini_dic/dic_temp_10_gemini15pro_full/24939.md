brunsick:/ˈbrʌnzˌwɪk/ | n. |不伦瑞克, 德国下萨克森州一城市|Brunswick is a city in Lower Saxony, Germany. It is known for its rich history and cultural heritage.|例句：I visited Brunswick last summer.  我去年夏天去了不伦瑞克。

例句：Brunswick is famous for its historic architecture. 不伦瑞克以其历史悠久的建筑而闻名。|近义词：Braunschweig (German name)|反义词: N/A |词性: 名词

Etymology: From German *Brunswik*, derived from *Bruno* (a personal name) + *wik* (a trading settlement).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bruniz* (brown) and *wīks* (village, trading post).
