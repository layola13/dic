fluocerite:/ˈfluːəˌsɛraɪt/| n.|氟铈矿|A mineral, a fluoride of cerium metals, typically light yellow to reddish brown in color.  It is a source of rare earth elements.|Fluocerite is found in association with other rare earth minerals. 氟铈矿与其他稀土矿物伴生。|The specimen contained a significant amount of fluocerite.  该标本含有大量的氟铈矿。|Synonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral name),  rare earth mineral|Antonyms: None|noun
