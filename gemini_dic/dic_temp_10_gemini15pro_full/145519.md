overcold:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊld/| adj.| 过于寒冷的；过度冷藏的| Excessively cold; chilled beyond a desirable or appropriate degree.  It describes something that is much colder than it should be or than is comfortable.|The dessert was overcold and the ice cream was rock hard. 这甜点太冰了，冰淇淋硬邦邦的。|The meat was overcold and tough. 这肉太冷了，而且很硬。|近义词：freezing, frigid, icy|反义词: warm, hot|词性:形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "cold" (adjective meaning low temperature)
