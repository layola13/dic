faithlessnesses:/ˈfeɪθləsnɪsɪz/
n.
不忠;背信弃义;无信仰
The state or quality of being faithless; instances of disloyalty or infidelity.
His repeated faithlessnesses eventually led to the breakdown of their marriage.  他屡次的不忠最终导致了他们婚姻的破裂。
近义词:infidelities, treacheries, betrayals, disloyalties
反义词:loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness
词性:名词

faithlessness:/ˈfeɪθləsnɪs/
n.
不忠;背信弃义;无信仰
The state or quality of being faithless; lack of faith or belief.
Her faithlessness to her principles shocked her colleagues. 她对自身原则的不忠让她的同事感到震惊。
近义词:infidelity, treachery, betrayal, disloyalty, unbelief
反义词:loyalty, fidelity, faithfulness, belief
词性:名词

faithless:/ˈfeɪθləs/
adj.
不忠的;背信弃义的;没有信仰的
Not true to duty or obligation; disloyal; untrue to one's promises or vows.
He was a faithless friend who betrayed everyone's trust. 他是一个不忠的朋友，背叛了所有人的信任。
近义词:disloyal, treacherous, unfaithful, untrue
反义词:loyal, faithful, true
词性:形容词


词源:  From "faithless" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). "Faithless" itself comes from "faith" + "-less" (suffix meaning "without").  Ultimately from Latin "fides" (faith, trust).
