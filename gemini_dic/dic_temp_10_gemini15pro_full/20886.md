blazoner:/ˈbleɪzənər/ | n. |纹章官；纹章描述者| A person who designs, describes, or paints coats of arms. | The king employed a skilled blazoner to create a new coat of arms for his family. 国王雇佣了一位技艺精湛的纹章官为他家族设计新的盾形纹章。 |  herald, armorist |  - | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *blasonier (Modern French blasonneur), from blason (“coat of arms”) + -ier (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Frankish *blaso (“horn, trumpet”).  Coats of arms were originally proclaimed by heralds using trumpets.
