indorsee:/ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiː/ /ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiː/
n.
被背书人，受让人
The person to whom a check, bill of exchange, or other negotiable instrument is endorsed.
The indorsee will receive the payment.  被背书人将收到付款。
近义词：beneficiary, assignee, transferee
反义词：indorser
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *endosse, past participle of endosser “to endorse,”  from en- “on, upon” + dos “back,” from Latin dorsum.
