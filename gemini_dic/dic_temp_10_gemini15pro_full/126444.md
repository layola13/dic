mystified: /ˈmɪstɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 困惑的，迷惑不解的 |  Made to feel confused or bewildered because something is difficult to understand. | Example: I was mystified by her sudden change in behavior. 我对她突然改变的行为感到困惑不解。 | Example: The detectives were mystified by the lack of evidence.  侦探们对缺乏证据感到迷惑不解。|  Synonyms: puzzled, bewildered, perplexed, baffled | Antonyms: clear, understanding, enlightened | adjective
