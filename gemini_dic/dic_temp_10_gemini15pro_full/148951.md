overzealousness:/ˌoʊvərˈzɛləsnəs/| n.|过度热情，过分热心|Excessive zeal; fanatical devotion.  Being too enthusiastic and eager, often to the point of being annoying or intrusive.|His overzealousness in enforcing the rules alienated his colleagues. 他执行规则时过分热心，疏远了他的同事。|近义词：fanaticism, extremism, excessiveness|反义词: apathy, indifference, moderation|noun
