holis:/ˈoʊlis/| interjection |你好；嗨 (主要用于拉丁美洲，尤其是在线聊天)|A Spanish slang greeting, short for "hola" which means "hello". It's often used online and conveys a friendly, informal tone, particularly in Latin American communities. |Example: Holis, ¿cómo estás? (Hi, how are you?)|Synonym: hola, hey, hi|Antonym: goodbye, farewell|Part of Speech: Interjection
