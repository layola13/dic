get-the-message:/ɡet ðə ˈmesɪdʒ/ |v. (phrasal verb)|理解；领会；明白|To understand the meaning or implication of something, often something not explicitly stated.|She didn't say anything directly, but I got the message that she wasn't happy. 她没有直接说什么，但我明白她不高兴。|

He kept glancing at his watch, and I got the message – it was time to go. 他不停地看手表，我明白了——该走了。|近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, catch on|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|词性：动词短语
