mysterial: /mɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. | 神秘的，不可思议的 | Relating to or suggesting mystery; enigmatic. |  The old house had a mysterial atmosphere. 这座老房子有一种神秘的气氛。 |  The mysterial disappearance of the ship remains unsolved.  这艘船神秘失踪至今仍未解开。 | 近义词: mysterious, enigmatic, puzzling, inscrutable | 反义词: clear, obvious, transparent | 词性: 形容词


词源: From mystery + -al.  Ultimately from Latin *ministerium* "service, office, duty."
