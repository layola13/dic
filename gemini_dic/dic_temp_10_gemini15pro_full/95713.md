incongruousnesses:/ˌɪnkənˈɡruːəsnəsɪz/
noun
不协调，不一致，不和谐
The quality or state of being incongruous; inappropriateness.  Lack of harmony or agreement.
His brightly colored tie was an incongruousness with his somber suit. 他那条颜色鲜艳的领带与他那套深色的西装很不协调。
incongruity, disharmony, incompatibility, inconsistency
harmony, agreement, appropriateness, congruity
