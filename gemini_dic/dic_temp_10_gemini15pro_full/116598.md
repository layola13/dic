matriculator:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪtər/
n.
注册员，登记员
A person who matriculates students or enters them on a register or matricula.
The matriculator processed the new student's paperwork. 注册员处理了新生的文件。
registrar, enroller
N/A
noun

matriculate:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/
v.
入学，注册
To enroll or register, especially as a student in a college or university.
She matriculated at Harvard University last fall. 她去年秋天在哈佛大学注册入学。
enroll, register
withdraw, drop out
verb
