gagers:/ˈɡeɪdʒərz/
n.
测量员，计量员
People who measure things, especially the contents of casks or other containers.
The gagers checked the volume of wine in each barrel. 计量员检查了每个桶中葡萄酒的体积。
近义词:measurers, surveyors, inspectors
反义词: N/A
词性:noun


gager:/ˈɡeɪdʒər/
n.
测量员，计量员
A person who measures things, especially the contents of casks or other containers.
The gager certified the amount of oil in the tanker. 计量员核准了油轮中的石油量。
近义词:measurer, surveyor, inspector
反义词: N/A
词性:noun

词源:
From the verb "gauge," which comes from Old North French "gauger," meaning "to measure." Ultimately from Frankish "*galgo," a gauging rod.
