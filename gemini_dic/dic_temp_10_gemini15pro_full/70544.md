filiety:/fɪˈliːɪti/ | n. | 忠诚，忠实 |  Loyalty or faithfulness, especially to a person, cause, or belief.  Derived from Old French "fileté" and ultimately from Latin "fidelitas" meaning "faithfulness". |  He swore filiety to the king. 他宣誓效忠国王。 |  The dog's filiety to its owner was remarkable. 这只狗对主人的忠诚令人瞩目。 | 近义词: loyalty, faithfulness, devotion, allegiance | 反义词: disloyalty, treachery, infidelity | 词性: 名词
