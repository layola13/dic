aciculate:/əˈsɪkjʊleɪt/
adjective
针状的，有针的
Slender and pointed, like a needle.
The aciculate leaves of the pine tree help it conserve water. 松树针状的叶子有助于它保存水分。

The cactus has aciculate spines for protection. 仙人掌有针状的刺以保护自己。

近义词: needle-like, spiny, prickly, sharp, pointed
反义词: blunt, rounded, obtuse
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin acicula ("small needle"), diminutive of acus ("needle").

USAGE->
The aciculate crystals were clearly visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下，针状晶体清晰可见。

The plant's aciculate leaves deterred grazing animals.  这种植物针状的叶子阻止了食草动物的啃食。
