crowns:/kraʊnz/
n.|王冠，皇冠；花冠；顶点|A symbolic headwear worn by monarchs.|The queen wore a dazzling crown at her coronation. 女王在加冕典礼上戴着耀眼的王冠。|近义词：coronet, diadem, tiara|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
crowns:/kraʊnz/
v.|为…加冕；表彰，授予荣誉|To place a crown on someone's head as a symbol of sovereignty; to honor or reward.|He was crowned king after his father's death. 他在父亲去世后被加冕为国王。 The team was crowned champion after their victory.  这个团队在获胜后被加冕为冠军。|近义词：honor, reward, recognize|反义词:dishonor|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "corone," from Latin "corona" meaning "garland, crown, wreath,"  related to "cornu" meaning "horn."  The sense of "topmost part of the head" developed later.
