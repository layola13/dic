mazourka:/ˌmæzˈɜːrkə/ /ˌmɑːˈzʊərkə/| n. |玛祖卡舞曲；玛祖卡舞|A lively Polish folk dance in triple time, characterized by accents on the second or third beat.  It is also a piece of music composed in this rhythm.| Chopin composed many beautiful mazourkas.  肖邦创作了许多优美的玛祖卡舞曲。|Synonyms:  mazurka, dance, Polish dance|Antonyms:  None|Noun


mazourkas:/ˌmæzˈɜːrkəz/ /ˌmɑːˈzʊərkəz/| n. |玛祖卡舞曲（复数）；玛祖卡舞（复数）|Plural form of mazourka.  Refers to multiple pieces of music or dances in the mazurka style.|He played several mazourkas at the recital.  他在独奏会上演奏了几首玛祖卡舞曲。|Synonyms: mazurkas, dances, Polish dances|Antonyms:  None|Noun
