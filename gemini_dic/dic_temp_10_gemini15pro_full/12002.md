arthrodic:/ɑːrˈθrɒdɪk/ | adj. | 节肢动物的 | Relating to or characteristic of arthropods, a phylum of invertebrate animals that includes insects, arachnids, crustaceans, and myriapods. | Arthropods, such as insects and spiders, are characterized by their arthrodic exoskeletons. 节肢动物，如昆虫和蜘蛛，以其节肢外骨骼为特征。|  The lobster's arthrodic appendages allow it to manipulate food and move efficiently. 龙虾的节肢附属物使其能够有效地操纵食物和移动。 | 近义词: segmented, jointed | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps unsegmented) | 词性: adjective


Etymology: From Greek *ἄρθρον* (arthron, “joint”) + *-ικός* (-ikos, adjectival suffix).
