columbier:/kəˈlʌmbiər/ |n.|鸽舍，鸽房|A dovecote; a pigeon house. | The old columbier in the courtyard housed dozens of pigeons.  院子里的旧鸽舍养着几十只鸽子。|Synonym: dovecote, pigeon house | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps aviary for contrasting bird housing) | noun

columbier:/kəˈlʌmbiər/ |n.|（旧时法国的一种）大纸张|A large size of French paper. |  The artist preferred working with columbier paper for its generous dimensions.  这位艺术家喜欢用大开纸作画，因为它尺寸很大。|Synonym:  paper sheet (general), Grand Aigle (similar size) | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a smaller paper size like quarto) | noun
