duodecimo:/ˌduːəˈdɛsɪmoʊ/ /ˌdjuːəˈdɛsɪmoʊ/
| n. adj.|十二开本；十二开大的|A size of book page resulting from the folding of each printed sheet into twelve leaves (24 pages).|This rare book was printed in duodecimo.|近义词：12mo|反义词:folio|词性:名词, 形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *duodecimus* (twelfth), from *duodecim* (twelve).
