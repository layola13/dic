mugle:/ˈmʌɡəl/| n.|麻瓜（指不会魔法的普通人）|A person who is not skilled in a particular activity or art, especially magic (in the Harry Potter universe).  A non-magical person.|Example 1:  In the world of Harry Potter, muggles are unaware of the existence of magic. 在哈利波特的世界里，麻瓜们不知道魔法的存在。

Example 2: He's a complete muggle when it comes to computers.  说到电脑，他完全是个门外汉。|Synonyms: non-wizard, layman, amateur, novice|Antonyms: wizard, witch, expert, professional|noun
