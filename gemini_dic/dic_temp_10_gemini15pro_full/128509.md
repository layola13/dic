newsworthily:/ˌnjuːzˈwɜːrðɪli/| adv.|有新闻价值地，值得报道地|In a manner worthy of being reported as news.  It describes actions or events that are significant or interesting enough to be reported in the news.|The event was reported newsworthily, focusing on the key details.  这事件被很有新闻价值地报道了，重点关注了关键细节。|Synonyms: significantly, notably, remarkably|Antonyms: insignificantly, trivially|adverb
