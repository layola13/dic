country: /ˈkʌntri/ (US) /ˈkʌntri/ (UK) | n. | 国家；乡村；故乡 | A nation with its own government, occupying a particular territory.  A rural area. |  例句：My country is known for its beautiful mountains. 我的国家以美丽的山脉闻名。

例句：I grew up in the country, surrounded by nature. 我在乡村长大，被大自然包围。

近义词：nation, land, state, countryside, rural area

反义词：city, urban area

词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *cuntrée* (Modern French *contrée*), from Vulgar Latin *contrata*, feminine past participle of *contrare* "to lie opposite, to be situated opposite," from Latin *contra* "against, opposite."  Originally referring to a tract of land spread out before one's view, hence "region, district."
