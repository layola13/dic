biorhythmicity:/ˌbaɪoʊˌrɪðˈmɪsɪti/
n.
生物节律性
The inherent rhythmic fluctuations in biological processes.
The biorhythmicity of sleep is regulated by the circadian clock.  睡眠的生物节律性是由生物钟调节的。
The study of biorhythmicity can help us understand jet lag. 对生物节律性的研究可以帮助我们理解时差。
