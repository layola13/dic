cocaptaining:/ˌkoʊˈkæptənɪŋ/ | n./v. | 共同担任队长；共同领导 | The act of sharing the captaincy of a team or group with another person. |  Example: Cocaptaining the team brought out the best in both leaders. 共同担任队长让两位领导者的优势都得以发挥。 | Example: They are cocaptaining the debate team this year. 今年他们共同担任辩论队的队长。 | Near Synonyms:  joint leadership, shared captaincy, co-leading | Antonyms: sole leadership, single captaincy | word type: noun/verb (present participle)

cocaptain:/ˈkoʊˌkæptən/ | n. | 共同队长 | One of two or more people who share the captaincy of a team or group. | Example:  Both cocaptains agreed on the game strategy. 两位共同队长就比赛策略达成了一致。 | Example:  She was elected cocaptain of the volleyball team. 她被选为排球队共同队长。 | Near Synonyms: joint captain, co-leader | Antonyms: sole captain, single leader | word type: noun
