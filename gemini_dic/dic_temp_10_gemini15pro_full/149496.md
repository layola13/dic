paeanisms:/ˈpiːənɪzəmz/
n.
赞美歌;颂歌;赞美之词
A paean is a song of praise or triumph. Paeanisms are expressions of enthusiastic praise or joy.
The returning soldiers were greeted with paeanisms and cheers.  归来的士兵受到赞美和歌颂的欢迎。
近义词: praises, accolades, encomiums, tributes
反义词: criticisms, condemnations, denunciations
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek παιάν (paiān, “hymn to Apollo”), from the refrain ἰὴ παιάν (iḕ paián), literally “heal, Paean”. Apollo was a healing god, and Paean was originally a name for Apollo as the healer.
