citified:/ˈsɪtɪfaɪd/
adjective
都市化的；城市化的
Having the characteristics of a city; made urban in character.
The citified young man had lost his rural roots.  这个都市化的年轻人已经失去了他的乡村根基。
The once small town had become thoroughly citified. 这个曾经的小镇已经完全城市化了。
近义词:urban, urbanized, metropolitan
反义词:rural, rustic, country
词性:adjective

citified:/ˈsɪtɪfaɪd/
adjective
世故的; 老练的
Sophisticated, worldly-wise. (Often slightly derogatory, implying a loss of natural simplicity.)
She looked down on his citified manners. 她瞧不起他那世故的举止。
He had a citified air about him that made him seem out of place in the small village. 他身上有一种都市气息，让他在这个小村庄里显得格格不入。
近义词:sophisticated, worldly, polished
反义词:naive, unsophisticated, simple
词性:adjective


词源: city + -fied.  From "city" (都市) and the suffix "-fied" (使...化), meaning "made into a city" or "having the characteristics of a city."
