mockingstock:/ˈmɑːkɪŋstɑːk/ /ˈmɒkɪŋstɒk/| n.|笑柄，嘲笑对象|A person or thing that is ridiculed or made fun of.|例句：After his disastrous presentation, he became the mockingstock of the entire company.  他糟糕的演讲过后，成了全公司的笑柄。

例句：The old, rusted car was a mockingstock in the neighborhood.  那辆老旧生锈的汽车成了邻居们的笑柄。|近义词：laughingstock, butt of jokes, object of ridicule|反义词: example, model|词性:noun
