glitziest: /ˈɡlɪtsiɪst/ | adj. | 最闪耀的，最华丽的 |  Superlative form of glitzy: having a fashionable showiness or glamour, often associated with wealth or celebrity. |  The Oscars are arguably the glitziest event in Hollywood. 奥斯卡颁奖典礼可以说是好莱坞最闪耀的盛事了。|  The singer wore one of her glitziest outfits for the performance.  这位歌手在演出时穿了她最华丽的服装之一。| 近义词：most glamorous, most dazzling, most sparkling | 反义词：dullest, plainest, most understated | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From glitz + -y + -est.  "Glitz" itself is a relatively recent word, first appearing in the mid-20th century, likely originating as imitative of the flashing of bright lights.
