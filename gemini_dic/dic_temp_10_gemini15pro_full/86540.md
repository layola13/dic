helder:/ˈhɛldər/ | adj. | 更清晰的，更明亮的，更响亮的 | Clearer, brighter, or louder.  More easily understood. | The photo became helder after some editing.  照片经过一些编辑后变得更清晰了。 | The explanation he gave was much helder than the one in the book. 他给出的解释比书上的更清楚。 | 近义词: clearer, brighter, louder, distinct, lucid | 反义词: dimmer, fainter, muffled, obscure, unclear | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle Dutch *heldere, comparative of *helde (“clear, bright”), from Proto-Germanic *haldiz (“inclined, sloping”).  Cognate with Dutch helder (“clear, bright”).  The meaning "clear, bright" is the primary meaning.  The sense of "loud" and "easily understood" developed from this.
