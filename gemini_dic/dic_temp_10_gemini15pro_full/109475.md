leprechaun: /ˈlɛprəkɔːn/ (UK), /ˈlɛprɪˌkɑːn/ (US)| n. |小妖精，矮妖|A small, mischievous sprite in Irish folklore, typically depicted as a bearded man dressed in green, who is said to possess hidden treasure.|例句：Legend says that if you catch a leprechaun, it will grant you three wishes. 传说如果你抓住一个小妖精，它会满足你三个愿望。

例句：The leprechaun disappeared in a puff of smoke. 小妖精消失在一缕烟雾中。

近义词：sprite, fairy, pixie, elf|反义词:giant|词性:noun
