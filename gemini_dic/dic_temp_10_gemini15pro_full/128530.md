nextg:/ˈnɛkst ʒiː/ (US) /ˈnɛkst ʒiː/ (UK)| n. |下一代 (技术，通常指移动通信)|Refers to the next generation of technology, often used in the context of mobile communications, succeeding 5G. It is characterized by significantly faster speeds, lower latency, and greater capacity.|Example: NextG networks are expected to revolutionize the way we connect and communicate.  下一代网络有望彻底改变我们连接和沟通的方式。

Example:  The development of NextG is crucial for the future of the internet of things.  下一代技术的开发对于物联网的未来至关重要。|近义词：6G, sixth generation, next-generation |反义词: previous generation, last generation|词性:名词
