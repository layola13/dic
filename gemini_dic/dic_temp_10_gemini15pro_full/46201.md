cypseliform:/sɪpˈsɛlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
形似瘦果的，菊果形的
Resembling a cypsela, a type of dry, single-seeded fruit characteristic of the Asteraceae (daisy) family.  It is formed from a compound inferior ovary and does not open at maturity.
The dandelion seed has a cypseliform structure that allows it to be easily dispersed by wind. 蒲公英种子具有瘦果状结构，使其易于被风散播。
cypseloid, achene-like
