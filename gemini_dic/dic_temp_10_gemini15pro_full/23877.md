break-forth:/breɪk ˈfɔːrθ/| vi.|爆发，突然出现，迸发|To suddenly start doing something; to emerge powerfully or violently.|例句：Cheers broke forth from the crowd.  人群中爆发出欢呼声。

例句：The volcano broke forth with a tremendous roar. 火山爆发出一阵巨大的轰鸣声。|近义词：erupt, burst out, explode, emerge|反义词：subside, die down|词性:动词
