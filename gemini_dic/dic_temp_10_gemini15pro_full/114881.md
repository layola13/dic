mammoths:/ˈmæməθs/ (US) /ˈmæmɒθs/ (UK)| n.|猛犸象（复数）|Large, extinct elephant-like mammals of the Pleistocene epoch, typically hairy with a sloping back and long curved tusks.|例句：Mammoths roamed the earth during the Ice Age. 猛犸象在冰河时代漫游地球。

例句：Scientists are studying the DNA of mammoths. 科学家正在研究猛犸象的DNA。|近义词：mastodon, behemoth (figuratively)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
