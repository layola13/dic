nontalkatively:/ˌnɒnˈtɔːkətɪvli/ | adv. | 不说话地，沉默地 |  In a manner that avoids conversation; silently. | She worked nontalkatively, focusing intently on her task. 她默默地工作，专注于她的任务。 | The children played nontalkatively, absorbed in their game. 孩子们静静地玩耍，沉浸在他们的游戏中。 | quietly, silently, reservedly | talkatively, loquaciously | adverb

Etymology->
From non- + talkative + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "talkative" is an adjective meaning "inclined to talk," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
