asininely:/ˌæsɪˈnaɪnli/
adverb|愚蠢地；像驴一样地|In an asinine manner; stupidly.|He behaved asininely at the meeting, interrupting everyone and making silly jokes. 他在会议上的行为愚蠢至极，打断每个人，还讲一些无聊的笑话。|stupidly, foolishly, idiotically|wisely, intelligently|adverb

Etymology:
From asinine + -ly.  Asinine comes from the Late Latin *asinīnus, from Latin asinus (“ass, donkey”).
