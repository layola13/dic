noncertitudes:/nɒnˈsɜːtɪtjuːdz/
noun
不确定性；无法确定的事情
Lack of certainty; things that are not certain or definite.
The report highlighted the noncertitudes surrounding the future of the economy. 报告强调了围绕经济前景的不确定性。
Near Synonyms: uncertainties, ambiguities, doubts, indeterminacies
Antonyms: certainties, assurances, definites
