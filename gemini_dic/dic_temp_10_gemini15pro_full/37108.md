comboloio:/ˌkɒmboˈlɔɪoʊ/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmboʊˈlɔɪoʊ/ (US)
noun
火车，列车（尤指长途慢车）
A long, slow train, especially one carrying freight or passengers over a long distance.
例句：The comboloio chugged slowly through the countryside.  这列长途慢车缓缓地穿过乡村。

例句：We took the comboloio from Rome to Milan. 我们乘坐火车从罗马到米兰。
synonym: train, railway car
antonym: airplane, car
n.
