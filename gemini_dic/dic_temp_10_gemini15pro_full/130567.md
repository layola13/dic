nonactivated:/ˌnɒnˈæktɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. |未激活的，未活化的|Not activated; in an inactive state.  It refers to something that has not been switched on, triggered, or started.  It can be used in various contexts, often scientific or technical, to describe something that is in a dormant or latent state.|The nonactivated alarm system failed to detect the intruder. 未激活的警报系统未能检测到入侵者。| The nonactivated enzyme is unable to catalyze the reaction. 未激活的酶无法催化反应。|inactive, dormant, latent|activated, active|adjective
