incorporating: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 包含，合并，组成，吸收 | present participle of incorporate | The new car design incorporating several innovative features.  这款新车设计融合了几项创新功能。 | Synonyms: integrating, combining, including, assimilating | Antonyms: excluding, separating, dividing | verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin incorporatus, past participle of incorporare "to embody," from in- "in" + corporare "to form into a body," from corpus "body."
