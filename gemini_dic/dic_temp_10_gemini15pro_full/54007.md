discriminatorily:/dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪtərɪli/ | adv. |歧视地，差别对待地 | In a manner that shows discrimination or prejudice against someone based on their race, religion, sex, etc.  |  They were treated discriminatorily because of their ethnicity.  他们因为种族而受到歧视。 | Synonyms: unfairly, prejudicially, biasedly | Antonyms: fairly, justly, equitably | part of speech: adverb

discriminatorily:/dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪtərɪli/ | adv. |有差别地 | In a way that distinguishes or differentiates between things. | The scholarships are awarded discriminatorily based on academic merit.  奖学金是根据学业成绩有区别地颁发的。 | Synonyms: distinctively, differently | Antonyms: similarly, uniformly | part of speech: adverb
