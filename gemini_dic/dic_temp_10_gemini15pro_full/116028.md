marsupials:/mɑːrˈsuːpiəlz/ | n. | 有袋动物 | Mammals whose females have a pouch (called a marsupium) containing the mammary glands where their young are carried and suckled.  |  例句：Kangaroos and koalas are marsupials.  袋鼠和考拉是有袋动物。

例句：Australia is famous for its diverse marsupial fauna. 澳大利亚以其多样化的有袋动物群而闻名。 | 近义词：pouched mammals | 反义词：placental mammals | 词性: noun
