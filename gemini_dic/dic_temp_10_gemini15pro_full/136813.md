nonshoppers:/ˈnɒnˌʃɒpəz/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌʃɑːpərz/
n.
非购物者；不购物的人
People who do not engage in shopping or do so infrequently.
Retailers are trying to find ways to attract nonshoppers.  零售商正在想方设法吸引不购物的人。
nonshoppers avoid crowded malls during the holiday season. 非购物者在假期期间会避开拥挤的商场。
近义词: non-buyers, non-consumers
反义词: shoppers, consumers
词性: noun
