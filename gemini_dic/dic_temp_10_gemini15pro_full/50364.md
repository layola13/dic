depopulate:/diːˈpɒpjʊleɪt/
verb
减少人口
To reduce the population of (a place), especially by a large amount or by force.
The Black Death depopulated much of Europe. 黑死病使欧洲许多地方人口减少。
depopulate rural areas  使农村地区人口减少
Synonyms:devastate, deplete, decimate, empty
Antonyms:populate, overpopulate
词性: 动词


depopulation:/diːˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃn/
noun
人口减少；人口稀少
A reduction in population; the state of having lost a large part of the population.
The depopulation of the countryside is a serious problem. 农村人口减少是一个严重问题。
rapid depopulation 人口迅速减少
Synonyms: decline, decrease, reduction
Antonyms:increase, growth, rise
词性: 名词
