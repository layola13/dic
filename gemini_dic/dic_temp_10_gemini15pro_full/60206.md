efflorescence:/ˌɛflɔˈrɛsəns/ | n. |风化，开花，繁荣|The state or a period of flowering.|The efflorescence of youth. 青春的繁荣。|Synonyms: blooming, blossoming, flowering, flourishing |Antonyms: decline, decay, withering |noun

efflorescence:/ˌɛflɔˈrɛsəns/ | n. |（盐类的）析出，晶华|A powdery deposit of salts or other crystalline substances on a surface, formed by efflorescence or by crystallization from a solution.|The walls showed signs of efflorescence. 墙壁上出现了盐析出的迹象。|Synonyms: deposit, precipitate, coating |Antonyms: solution, dissolution |noun
