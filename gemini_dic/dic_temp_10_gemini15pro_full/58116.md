dropperfuls:/ˈdrɒpəfʊlz/ (US) /ˈdrɒpəfʊlz/ (UK)| n. |滴管满的量; 一滴管; 数滴|The quantity contained in a dropper; several drops. |She added two dropperfuls of the tincture to the mixture. 她往混合物里加了两滴管的酊剂。|The recipe called for three dropperfuls of vanilla extract.  食谱要求加三滴管香草精。|近义词: drops, few drops |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "dropper" + "-ful".  "Dropper" originates from the verb "drop," meaning to fall or let fall in small drops. "-ful" is a suffix denoting the amount that fills something. Thus, "dropperful" refers to the amount that fills a dropper.
