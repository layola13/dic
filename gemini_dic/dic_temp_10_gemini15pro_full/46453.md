daguerreotyping:/dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪpɪŋ/
n.|银版照相术，银版摄影|The process of creating a daguerreotype, an early type of photograph. This involves creating an image on a silver-coated copper plate.|Example: Daguerreotyping was a popular method of portraiture in the mid-19th century.  19世纪中期，银版照相术是一种流行的肖像摄影方法。|Synonym: photography, silver plate photography|Antonym: digital photography
词性: noun
