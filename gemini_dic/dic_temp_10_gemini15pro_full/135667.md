nonprehensile:/ˌnɒnpriˈhensəl/ | adj. | 非缠绕性的；不能抓握的 | Not adapted for seizing or grasping, especially by wrapping around. | The panda's paws are nonprehensile, unlike a monkey's. 熊猫的爪子不像猴子的爪子那样能抓握。 |  A horse's tail is nonprehensile and cannot be used to grasp objects. 马的尾巴不能抓握，不能用来抓东西。 |  Near Antonyms: prehensile, grasping | Antonyms:  prehensile | adjective
