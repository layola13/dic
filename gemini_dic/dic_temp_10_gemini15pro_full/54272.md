disgruntledly:/dɪsˈɡrʌntldli/
adverb
不满地，不高兴地
in a disgruntled or discontented manner
He crossed his arms disgruntledly and stared out the window.  他很不高兴地交叉双臂，盯着窗外。
The employee disgruntledly performed his duties after being denied a promotion.  员工在被拒绝升职后，很不情愿地履行职责。
近义词: sullenly, resentfully, moodily, peevishly
反义词: cheerfully, happily, contentedly, willingly
词性: adverb

disgruntled:/dɪsˈɡrʌntld/
adjective
不满的，不高兴的
Feeling or showing dissatisfaction or resentment
The disgruntled customers demanded a refund. 不满的顾客要求退款。
He was disgruntled by the lack of recognition for his work.  他对自己的工作缺乏认可感到不满。
近义词: dissatisfied, discontented, resentful, unhappy
反义词: satisfied, content, happy, pleased
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + gruntle (to grumble, complain), from Middle English gruntlen.
