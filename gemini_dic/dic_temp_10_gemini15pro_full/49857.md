demotes:/dɪˈmoʊts/| v. |降级，降职| To lower in rank or position; to relegate to a lower grade or status.| The army demoted him for disobeying orders.  他因违反命令而被军队降级。|synonyms: downgrade, relegate, reduce, lower| antonyms: promote, elevate, advance, upgrade|verb

demote:/dɪˈmoʊt/| v. |降级，降职| To lower in rank or position; to relegate to a lower grade or status.|  The company demoted her from manager to assistant manager. 公司把她从经理降为助理经理。 |synonyms: downgrade, relegate, reduce, lower| antonyms: promote, elevate, advance, upgrade|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *demovere, from Latin demovēre ("to move down, remove"), from de- ("down") + movēre ("to move").
