odinism:/ˈoʊdɪnɪzəm/ | n. |奥丁主义;北欧异教|A modern religious movement based on the pre-Christian beliefs and practices of the Norse and Germanic peoples, focusing on the worship of gods like Odin, Thor, and Freya. |例句：Odinism is a polytheistic religion. 奥丁主义是一种多神教。

|例句：He began practicing Odinism after studying Norse mythology.  他在学习了北欧神话之后开始信奉奥丁主义。|近义词：Heathenry, Asatru, Norse Paganism |反义词:Christianity, Islam, Judaism|词性:noun
