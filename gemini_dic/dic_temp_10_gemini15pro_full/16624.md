barong: /ˈbɑːrɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːrɔŋ/ (US) | n. | 巴隆舞服，巴隆刀 | A traditional embroidered formal shirt worn by men in the Philippines, often accompanied by a sash and sometimes worn with a sword or dagger also called a barong.  It represents Filipino national dress.| He wore his finest barong to the wedding. 他穿着他最好的巴隆舞服去参加婚礼。|  The barong tagalog is a symbol of Filipino pride. 巴隆他加禄衫是菲律宾人的骄傲象征。 | synonyms: camisa de chino, barong tagalog | antonyms: N/A | noun
