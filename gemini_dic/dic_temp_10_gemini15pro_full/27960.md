cantopop:/ˈkæntoʊpɑːp/
n.
粤语流行音乐;香港流行音乐
A genre of popular music originating from Hong Kong, sung in Cantonese.
Cantopop has influenced music throughout Asia. 粤语流行曲影响了整个亚洲的音乐。
Cantopop is a vibrant and dynamic music scene. 粤语流行乐坛充满活力，变化万千。
Synonym: HK-Pop, Cantonese pop
Antonym: Mandopop (though not a direct opposite, it represents a distinct genre)
词性: 名词
