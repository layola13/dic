hypapophysis:/ˌhaɪpəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
noun
垂体突; 鞍底下垂体
A ventral down growth of the infundibulum of the brain that forms part of the pituitary gland in some vertebrates.
The hypapophysis is a ventral outgrowth of the brain that contributes to the pituitary gland. 垂体突是大脑的腹侧生长物，有助于垂体腺的形成。
The hypapophysis in certain species plays a crucial role in hormonal regulation. 在某些物种中，垂体突在激素调节中起着至关重要的作用。
Synonyms:  infundibular process, pituitary process (less common)
Antonyms: N/A (anatomical structures generally lack direct antonyms)
