deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/
n.
深思熟虑；审议；讨论
Careful consideration or discussion of a subject.
The jury reached its verdict after hours of deliberation. 陪审团经过数小时的审议后作出了裁决。
近义词: consideration, contemplation, reflection, thought, discussion
反义词: impulsiveness, recklessness
