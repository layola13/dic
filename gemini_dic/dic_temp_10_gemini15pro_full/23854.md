breadroot:/ˈbredˌruːt/ | n. | 面包根 |  A starchy root of a North American plant (Psoralea esculenta) of the pea family, formerly used as food by Native Americans. |  The nomadic tribes relied on breadroot as a staple food during the winter months. 游牧部落在冬季依靠面包根作为主食。| synonym: prairie turnip, Indian breadroot | antonym: (No direct antonym, possibly cultivated crops) | noun
