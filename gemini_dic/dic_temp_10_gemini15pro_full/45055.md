cubeb:/ˈkjuːbɛb/ | n. |荜澄茄，爪哇胡椒|A dried, unripe fruit of the pepper family, used as a spice and in medicine.|例句：Cubeb berries have a warm, slightly bitter flavor. 荜澄茄浆果有一种温暖的、略带苦味的味道。

例句：Cubeb is sometimes used in traditional medicine to treat respiratory ailments. 荜澄茄有时用于传统医学治疗呼吸系统疾病。|近义词：tailed pepper, Java pepper|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *cubebe*, from Medieval Latin *cubeba*, from Arabic *kabāba*. Ultimately from a Dravidian language (possibly Kannada *kabābu*).
