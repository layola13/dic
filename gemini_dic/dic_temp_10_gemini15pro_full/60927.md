eleutheromaniac: /ɪˌluːθərəˈmeɪniæk/
n.
狂热追求自由的人; 痴迷于自由的人
A person with a strong or excessive desire for freedom.
He was an eleutheromaniac, constantly rebelling against any form of authority. 他是一个痴迷于自由的人，不断反抗任何形式的权威。
Synonyms: libertarian, freedom fighter, nonconformist, rebel
Antonyms: conformist, traditionalist, authoritarian, dictator
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *eleuthero-* (free) + *-maniac* (obsessed with).
