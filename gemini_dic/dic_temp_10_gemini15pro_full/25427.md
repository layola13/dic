bulkier:/ˈbʌlkiər/| adj.|更笨重的，更庞大的|Comparative form of bulky: more bulky.  Meaning larger, heavier, and more difficult to carry or manage.|The new phone is bulkier than my old one. 这款新手机比我的旧手机更笨重。|The package was bulkier than I expected.  这个包裹比我预期的要大得多。|近义词：larger, heavier, bigger, more massive|反义词：smaller, lighter, more compact|词性:形容词

词源: From bulk + -y + -er.  Bulk ultimately comes from Old Norse *bulki, meaning "cargo, heap."
