directionality:/ˌdaɪˌrɛkʃəˈnæləti/ | n. |方向性；定向性|The property of having a direction or being directed.  The directionality of light waves is crucial in many optical applications.| The directionality of the antenna ensured the signal reached the intended recipient. 天线的方向性确保了信号到达预期的接收者。| synonyms: orientation, directedness | antonyms: randomness, non-directional | noun

directionality:/ˌdaɪˌrɛkʃəˈnæləti/ | n. |方向性；定向性|The quality of exhibiting direction; the tendency to move or develop in a specific direction.|  The growth of plants often shows a strong directionality towards light. 植物的生长通常表现出强烈的趋光性。| synonyms: tendency, trend, inclination| antonyms: aimlessness, lack of direction | noun
