centralists:/ˈsɛntrəlɪsts/| n.|中央集权主义者|A person who advocates centralizing political power.|The centralists believed that a strong national government was essential for the country's stability. 中央集权主义者认为强大的中央政府对国家的稳定至关重要。

|近义词：authoritarians, unitarians|反义词: federalists, decentralists|词性:noun


centralist:/ˈsɛntrəlɪst/| adj.|中央集权的|Relating to or advocating the concentration of power or control in a central authority.|The centralist government implemented policies that affected the entire nation. 中央集权政府实施了影响全国的政策。

|近义词：unified, centralized|反义词: federal, decentralized|词性:adjective
