balefully:/ˈbeɪlfəli/| adv.|恶狠狠地，不祥地|In a threatening or menacing way; ominously.  It suggests an expression or atmosphere of evil or impending doom. |The dog stared balefully at the intruder.  那条狗恶狠狠地盯着入侵者。

The dark clouds gathered balefully overhead.  乌云不祥地聚集在头顶。|近义词：menacingly, sinisterly, ominously, malevolently|反义词：benevolently, kindly, cheerfully|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "bale" (evil, woe, sorrow) + "-ful" (full of) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word "bale" comes from Old English "bealu,"  and is related to Old Norse "bǫl" (evil, calamity).
