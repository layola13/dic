miscount:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊnt/ | v. n. |数错；误算；错误的计数|To count incorrectly; to make a mistake in counting. | I miscounted the number of people attending the party and we ran out of food. 我数错了参加聚会的人数，结果食物不够了。|The cashier miscounted my change and gave me too much money. 收银员数错了我的零钱，多给了我钱。|近义词：miscalculate, undercount, overcount |反义词: count correctly, enumerate accurately |词性:动词/名词

miscount:/ˌmɪsˈkaʊnt/ | n. |错误的计数|An incorrect count. | There was a miscount in the election results, leading to a recount. 选举结果计数错误，导致重新计票。|The inventory miscount resulted in a shortage of supplies. 库存计数错误导致物资短缺。|近义词：miscalculation, error |反义词: accurate count |词性:名词

词源:  From mis- (meaning "wrongly, badly") + count.
