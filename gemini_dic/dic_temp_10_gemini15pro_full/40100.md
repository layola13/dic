contamination:/kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |污染，玷污，混淆|The act of contaminating, or the state of being contaminated; pollution; defilement. | The water supply was tested for contamination. 水供应经过了污染测试。| 近义词: pollution, taint, adulteration, infection | 反义词: purification, cleansing, sterilization | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *contaminatio, from Latin contaminare (“to contaminate”), from com- (“together”) + *tagmen (“touch”), from tangere (“to touch”).
