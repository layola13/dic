matriculatory: /məˈtrɪkjələˌtɔːri/ /məˈtrɪkjələˌtəri/
adjective
入学的；录取的
Relating to matriculation or the process of being enrolled in a college or university.
The matriculatory examination is very difficult. 入学考试非常难。
The matriculatory ceremony was held in the grand hall.  入学典礼在大厅举行。
Synonyms:  enrollment, admission, registration
Antonyms:  withdrawal, expulsion
词性: adjective

matriculatory: /məˈtrɪkjələˌtɔːri/ /məˈtrɪkjələˌtəri/
noun
新生入学考试；录取名单
An examination or other requirement for matriculation.
A list of students who have matriculated.
The university published its matriculatory list. 大学公布了新生录取名单。
Passing the matriculatory is a prerequisite for entering the university. 通过入学考试是进入大学的先决条件。
Synonyms: entrance examination, admission test, roster
Antonyms: graduation list, expulsion list
词性: noun
