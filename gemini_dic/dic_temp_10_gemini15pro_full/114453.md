makeshiftness:/ˈmeɪkʃɪftnəs/
n.
权宜之计；临时凑合；凑合
The quality of being makeshift; temporary and not intended to last.
Due to the unexpected rain, we had to resort to the makeshiftness of using a tarp as a shelter.  由于突如其来的大雨，我们不得不临时用防水布搭建一个避难所。
The makeshiftness of the stage was apparent, but the performance was still enjoyable. 舞台的简陋显而易见，但演出仍然很精彩。
synonyms: temporariness, impermanence, expediency
antonyms: permanence, durability, stability
noun
