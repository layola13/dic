/ˌdʒuːtərəʊkəˈnɒnɪkəl/ | /ˌduːtərəʊkəˈnɑːnɪkəl/ | adj. | 次经的; 旧约次经的 | Relating to or constituting the deuterocanon (a collection of books accepted as canonical by the Roman Catholic Church but not by most Protestant churches).  |  The deuterocanonical books are included in Catholic Bibles but not in most Protestant Bibles. | 近义词: apocryphal | 反义词: canonical | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:

From Greek *deuterokanonikos*, from *deuteros* (second) + *kanonikos* (canonical).
