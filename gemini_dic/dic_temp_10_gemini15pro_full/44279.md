cronies:/ˈkroʊniz/| n. |密友，亲信，狐朋狗友|Close friends or companions, especially those perceived as unscrupulous or benefiting from their association. |He was surrounded by his cronies at the country club. 他在乡村俱乐部里被他的亲信们包围着。|The mayor appointed his cronies to powerful positions. 市长任命他的亲信担任要职。|近义词：associates, companions, pals, confidants|反义词：enemies, adversaries|Noun
