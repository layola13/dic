hexametrist:/hɛkˈsæmɪtrɪst/
n.
六音步诗人；六步格诗人
A writer of hexameter verse.
Homer was a renowned hexametrist of ancient Greece. 荷马是古希腊著名的六音步诗人。
Virgil was a master hexametrist. 维吉尔是一位精通六音步诗的大师。
poet, versifier
prosaist
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek hexametros (six-measure) + -ist.
