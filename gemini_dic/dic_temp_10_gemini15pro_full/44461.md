cross-vaulting: /ˈkrɒs ˈvɔːltɪŋ/ /ˈkrɑːs ˈvɔːltɪŋ/
|n.| 交叉拱顶，十字拱顶|A vault formed by the intersection of two barrel vaults of the same diameter which cross or intersect at right angles.|The cathedral boasts impressive cross-vaulting.|Synonym: groin vault, quadripartite vault|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps flat ceiling)|noun
