clap:/klæp/| n. v.|鼓掌，拍手，啪的一声|A sudden loud noise, like the sound of two hard surfaces hitting each other.  The act of striking the palms of one's hands together repeatedly to express approval or appreciation.|The audience clapped enthusiastically after the performance.  演出结束后，观众热情地鼓掌。|近义词：applaud, slap, bang|反义词:criticize|noun, verb
