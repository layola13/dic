eructation: /ɪˌrʌkˈteɪʃən/ /iˌrəkˈtāSHən/
n.
打嗝，嗳气
the act of expelling gas from the stomach through the mouth.
After drinking the soda, he let out a loud eructation.  喝完汽水后，他大声地打了个嗝。
The volcano's eructation of ash covered the surrounding villages. 火山喷出的火山灰覆盖了周围的村庄。(比喻用法)
synonyms: belch, burp
antonyms: none (The absence of eructation isn't typically lexicalized.)
noun
