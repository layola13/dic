memsahib:/ˈmɛmˌsɑːɪb/ | n. | 女主人，太太；（尤指旧时在印度的）欧洲妇女 | A term of respect used in colonial India to address or refer to a European woman, especially the wife of a European official. |  She behaved like a real memsahib, ordering the servants around. 她像个十足的太太，对仆人们颐指气使。|Synonyms: madam, lady, mistress | Antonyms: servant, maid | Noun
