objection:/əbˈdʒekʃən/ | n. | 反对，异议 | An expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition; a reason for disagreeing. |  The main objection to the plan is its cost.  该计划的主要反对意见是其成本。| synonyms: protest, challenge, opposition, dissent, disapproval | antonyms: agreement, approval, acceptance, consent | noun
