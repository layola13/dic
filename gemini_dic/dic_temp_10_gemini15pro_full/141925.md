ordinariest:/ˌɔːrdɪˈnɛriɪst/
adjective
最普通的，最平凡的
Most ordinary; most commonplace.
She led the ordinariest life imaginable. 她过着最平凡的生活。
Synonyms: commonplace, mundane, humdrum, pedestrian, usual, typical, run-of-the-mill, common, average, everyday, workaday, unexceptional, unremarkable, unmemorable, undistinguished, nondescript, characterless, colourless, uninteresting, dull
Antonyms: extraordinary, unusual, remarkable, special
