chaplets:/ˈtʃæplɪts/
n.
花环，念珠
A string of beads, flowers, or leaves worn around the head or neck.
The bride wore a chaplet of white roses in her hair.  新娘头上戴着白色玫瑰花环。
She fingered the chaplet of beads, saying a silent prayer. 她拨动着念珠，默默地祈祷。
近义词:garland, wreath, rosary
反义词:(none specific -  could be "crown" if contrasting headwear)
词性:noun
