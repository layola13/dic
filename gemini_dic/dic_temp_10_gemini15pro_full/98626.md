instrumentals:/ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntlz/
n.
器乐曲；伴奏音乐
A piece of music or a recording consisting of only musical instruments, without vocals.
I prefer listening to instrumentals while I work, as lyrics can be distracting.  我喜欢工作的时候听纯音乐，因为歌词会让我分心。
近义词:instrumental music, background music
反义词:vocal music, songs with lyrics
词性:名词
