disallowances:/dɪsəˈlaʊənsɪz/
n.
扣除额；不允许的事项；不许可；拒绝给予
Amounts of money deducted, things not permitted, or the act of refusing to grant something.
The company's disallowances for travel expenses were very strict.  该公司对差旅费的扣除非常严格。
Due to excessive wear and tear, the disallowances on the car rental agreement were substantial. 由于过度磨损，汽车租赁协议中的扣除额很大。
近义词:deductions, rejections, prohibitions
反义词:allowances, permissions, approvals
词性:noun
