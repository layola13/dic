chicos:/ˈtʃiːkoʊs/ (US), /ˈtʃiːkɒs/ (UK)| n.|伙计们；孩子们；小伙子们|A Spanish word used to address a group of boys or young men, similar to "guys" or "fellas" in English. It can also be used more generally to refer to children, especially boys.|例句：¡Hola, chicos! How are you doing? 嗨，伙计们！你们好吗？

例句：The chicos were playing football in the park. 小伙子们正在公园里踢足球。|近义词：guys, fellas, lads, boys, children|反义词:chicas (girls)|Noun
