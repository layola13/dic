dissolution:/ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən/ | n. | 解散；溶解；分解；终止；毁灭 | The dissolution of the company was announced yesterday. 昨天宣布公司解散。|  The dissolution of sugar in water is a physical process. 糖在水中溶解是一个物理过程。 | Example: The dissolution of the partnership was inevitable due to irreconcilable differences.  合伙关系的解除由于不可调和的分歧而成为必然。

Example:  The dissolution of the tablet in water created a fizzy drink.  药片在水中溶解，产生了一种冒泡的饮料。

近义词: disbanding, disintegration, termination, decomposition, dissolving
反义词: formation, creation, solidification, union, fusion
词性: 名词
