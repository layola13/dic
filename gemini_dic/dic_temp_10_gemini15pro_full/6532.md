amygdala:/əˈmɪɡdələ/
|n.|
|杏仁核；扁桃腺；扁桃体|
|An almond-shaped mass of gray matter inside each cerebral hemisphere, involved with the experiencing of emotions.|
|例句1：The amygdala plays a crucial role in processing fear and other emotions. 杏仁核在处理恐惧和其他情绪方面起着至关重要的作用。|
