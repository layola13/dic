outbluster: /ˌaʊtˈblʌstər/
verb
大声呵斥，以咆哮压倒
to shout down or silence someone by being louder and more forceful
The angry customer tried to outbluster the manager. 愤怒的顾客试图用咆哮压倒经理。
近义词: outshout, drown out, overpower
反义词:  submit, yield, concede
词性: v.

outbluster: /ˌaʊtˈblʌstər/
noun
大声呵斥，咆哮
the act of shouting down or silencing someone by being louder and more forceful
His outbluster intimidated the other witnesses.  他的大声呵斥吓倒了其他证人。
近义词: shouting, ranting, blustering
反义词: silence, quiet, concession
词性: n.


词源：out- + bluster  "out-" 表示超过，胜过； "bluster" 指咆哮，大声吹嘘
