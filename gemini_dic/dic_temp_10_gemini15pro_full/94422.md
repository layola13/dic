implied:/ɪmˈplaɪd/| adj.|暗示的，隐含的|suggested but not directly expressed; implicit|Her silence implied consent. 她的沉默暗示着同意。|His implied criticism of the project upset the team. 他对这个项目的含蓄批评让团队感到不安。|近义词：implicit, suggested, tacit, unspoken, inferred|反义词：explicit, stated, expressed|词性: adjective
词源: From the past participle of *imply*, from late Middle English *implien*, from Old French *implier*, from Latin *implicare* "to involve, entangle," from *in-* "in" + *plicare* "to fold."
