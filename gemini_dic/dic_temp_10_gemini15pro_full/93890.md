immortalising: /ɪˈmɔːtəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ɪˈmɔːrtəlɪzɪŋ/
Verb
使永生,使不朽
The act of making something or someone immortal, either literally (granting eternal life) or figuratively (ensuring lasting fame or remembrance).
This photograph immortalises a moment of pure joy. 这张照片使纯粹的快乐永恒。
His bravery was immortalised in a song. 他的勇敢被一首歌曲所歌颂。
Synonyms: commemorating, memorializing, preserving, enshrining
Antonyms: forgetting, obscuring, erasing
词性:v.

immortalise: /ɪˈmɔːtəlaɪz/ /ɪˈmɔːrtəlɪz/

Synonyms: commemorate, memorialize, preserve, enshrine
Antonyms: forget, obscure, erase
