nonsoluble:/nɒnˈsɒljʊbl/ /nɑːnˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|不溶的，不能溶解的|Not capable of being dissolved.|例句：The precipitate formed was nonsoluble in water. 形成的沉淀物不溶于水。

例句：Nonsoluble fiber is important for digestive health.  不溶性纤维对消化系统健康很重要。
