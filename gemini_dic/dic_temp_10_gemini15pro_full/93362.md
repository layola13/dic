illegitimacy: /ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪməsi/
n.
非法; 私生; 不合法
the state of being illegitimate.  The condition of being born to parents who are not married to each other.
The report highlighted the social stigma associated with illegitimacy in the 19th century.  这份报告强调了19世纪与非婚生子女相关的社会污名。
His illegitimacy made it difficult for him to inherit his father's estate. 他的私生子身份使他难以继承父亲的遗产。
Synonyms: bastardy, unlawfulness, invalidity
Antonyms: legitimacy, legality, validity
词性: 名词
