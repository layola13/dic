explaining:/ɪkˈspleɪnɪŋ/| v.| 解释，说明 |present participle of the verb "explain".  Giving reasons for something, making something clear or easy to understand.|He was explaining the rules of the game. 他正在解释游戏规则.| synonyms: clarifying, elucidating, interpreting, illustrating| antonyms: confusing, obscuring, concealing| verb
