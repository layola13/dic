llms:/ˈɛl ɛl ɛm ɛs/ | n. | 大型语言模型 (Large Language Models) | Large Language Models (LLMs) are advanced AI systems capable of understanding and generating human-like text. They are trained on massive datasets of text and code, allowing them to perform a wide range of tasks, including translation, summarization, and question answering. |  Many companies are now exploring the use of LLMs to improve customer service. 许多公司现在正在探索使用大型语言模型来改善客户服务。

LLMs can generate creative text formats, like poems, code, scripts, musical pieces, email, letters, etc. 大型语言模型可以生成创造性的文本格式，例如诗歌、代码、脚本、音乐作品、电子邮件、信件等。| 近义词: AI models, language models | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially "rule-based systems") | 词性: noun
