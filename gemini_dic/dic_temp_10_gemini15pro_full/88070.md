high-level:/ˈhaɪˈlɛvəl/| adj.|高级的，高层的|of or involving the upper levels of management or other groups : relating to or dealing with general principles or broad issues rather than details|例句：High-level talks are taking place between the two governments. 两国政府正在进行高层会谈。|近义词：senior, top-level, strategic, executive|反义词:low-level, junior, detailed, operational|adjective

high level:/ˈhaɪˈlɛvəl/| n.|高水平，高层次|a high position or rank : an advanced stage of development or skill|例句：The company operates at a high level of efficiency. 该公司运营效率很高。|近义词：top level, upper echelon|反义词:low level, bottom rung|noun
