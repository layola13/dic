exhume:/ɪɡˈzjuːm/ /ɛksˈhjuːm/ |v. |掘出; 发掘;  |To dig out (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth. |The body was exhumed for further investigation. 尸体被挖掘出来进行进一步调查。|Synonyms: disinter, unearth, uncover |Antonyms: bury, inter, entomb |verb

exhumation:/ˌɛkshjuːˈmeɪʃən/ /ˌɛɡzjuːˈmeɪʃən/ |n. |掘尸; 发掘  |The act of exhuming, especially a corpse. |The exhumation of the body revealed new evidence. 尸体的发掘揭示了新的证据。|Synonyms: disinterment |Antonyms: burial, interment |noun
