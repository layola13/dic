interconfounds:/ˌɪntər.kənˈfaʊndz/
| v. |相互混淆，互相干扰|To mix up or confuse (different variables or factors) so that it becomes difficult to determine their individual effects.| The study's results were interconfounded by the presence of multiple uncontrolled variables, making it impossible to isolate the impact of the treatment.  这项研究的结果由于存在多个不受控制的变量而相互混淆，无法分离出治疗的影响。 |近义词：confuse, confound, muddle, complicate|反义词：clarify, distinguish, separate|词性:动词

interconfounds:/ˌɪntər.kənˈfaʊndz/
| n. |相互混淆，互相干扰（的情况）|The state of being mixed up or confused, especially in research, so that it's difficult to determine individual effects. |The interconfounds in the data made it challenging to interpret the findings accurately. 数据中的相互混淆使得准确解释研究结果变得具有挑战性。|近义词：confusion, complication|反义词：clarity, distinction|词性:名词
