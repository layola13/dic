nipple:/ˈnɪpəl/| n. |乳头；奶嘴；(某些器具的) 喷嘴|A nipple is a small projection on the breast or udder of a mammal, from which milk is drawn by the young. It can also refer to the rubber teat of a baby's bottle or a nozzle-like fitting on various equipment.|The baby latched onto its mother's nipple. 婴儿吸住了妈妈的乳头。

The baby's bottle has a new nipple.  婴儿的奶瓶有一个新的奶嘴。

The garden hose has a leaky nipple.  花园水管的喷嘴漏水了。|近义词：teat, nozzle|反义词: None|词性: Noun
