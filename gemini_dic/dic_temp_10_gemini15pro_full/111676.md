lockability:/ˌlɒkəˈbɪləti/ /ˌlɑːkəˈbɪləti/
noun
可锁性；上锁的可能性
The quality of being able to be locked.
This door's lockability is questionable; the lock seems quite flimsy.  这扇门的可锁性值得怀疑；锁看起来很脆弱。
The lockability of the windows is a key security feature. 窗户的可锁性是一项重要的安全特性。
securability, lockableness
unlockability
