doodles:/ˈduːdlz/| n.|涂鸦，胡写乱画|Meaning: absentminded scribbles or drawings, often done while preoccupied.  A doodle can be simple shapes or more elaborate drawings.|Example: He covered the margins of his notebook with doodles. 他在笔记本的空白处画满了涂鸦。|Example:  She doodled a flower while on the phone.  她打电话时信手涂鸦了一朵花。|近义词：scribbles, sketches|反义词:masterpiece, artwork|词性:noun
