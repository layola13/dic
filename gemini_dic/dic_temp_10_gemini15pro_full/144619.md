outwin:/ˌaʊtˈwɪn/
verb
胜过，赢得更多
to win more than (someone or something)
例句：Team A outwon Team B by a score of 5 to 2. A队以5比2的比分战胜了B队。

例句：She outwon her competitors in the sales contest. 她在销售竞赛中胜过她的竞争对手。
近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, defeat, beat
反义词：lose to
词性：v.
