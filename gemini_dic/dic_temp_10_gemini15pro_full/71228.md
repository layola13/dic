fitnesses:/ˈfɪtnəsɪz/
n.|健康状况，适合性|The state of being physically fit and healthy.  The quality of being suitable for a particular purpose.|例句：The trainer assessed the fitnesses of the new recruits. 教练评估了新成员的健康状况。

例句：The fitnesses of the candidates for the job were carefully considered.  候选人对这份工作的适合性得到了仔细考虑。|近义词：suitability, appropriateness, aptitude, capability|反义词：unsuitability, inappropriateness, incapability|词性:名词(复数)
