berserkness:/bərˈsɜːrknəs/
n.
狂暴，疯狂，暴怒
A state of violent or destructive rage.
He flew into a berserkness and started smashing things. 他勃然大怒，开始砸东西。
近义词：fury, rage, frenzy, madness
反义词：calmness, tranquility, peacefulness
词性: 名词

词源: From Old Norse *berserkr, meaning "bear-shirt" or "wolf-shirt" (referring to warriors who fought in animal skins) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).
