fugitivity:/fjuːdʒɪˈtɪvɪti/
n.
逃亡，逃亡状态；难以捉摸；短暂，易逝
The state or quality of being a fugitive; the act of fleeing.  The quality of being fleeting or elusive.
He lived a life of fugitivity, constantly moving from town to town. 他过着逃亡的生活，不断地从一个城镇搬到另一个城镇。
The fugitivity of youth is something we all lament. 青春的短暂是我们都感到惋惜的。
近义词：flight, escape, evanescence, transience
反义词：permanence, stability
