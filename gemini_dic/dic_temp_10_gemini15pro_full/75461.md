frumenty:/ˈfruːmənti/ | n. |粥，麦粥|A thick, porridge-like dish made from boiled hulled wheat, often with milk, sugar, and spices. |例句：Frumenty was a popular dish in medieval Europe.  中世纪欧洲，麦粥是一种很受欢迎的菜肴。


例句：She prepared a delicious frumenty for breakfast. 她准备了一份美味的麦粥作为早餐。|近义词：porridge, gruel |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a modern, processed breakfast cereal) |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *frumentee, from Latin frumentum ("grain").
