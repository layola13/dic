medica:/ˈmɛdɪkə/| n. |医疗的；医学的 (通常用作后缀) |  Relating to medicine; medical.  It is most commonly used as a suffix. |Medicaid is a healthcare program for low-income individuals. 医疗补助是一项针对低收入人群的医疗保健计划。|  The term "biomedica" refers to the application of biological principles to medicine.  “生物医学”一词指的是将生物学原理应用于医学。 |近义词: medical, therapeutic |反义词: non-medical |词性: 形容词 (adjective - used as a suffix)
