mangle:/ˈmæŋɡl/ (US), /ˈmæŋɡəl/ (UK)| v. n.| 损坏，毁坏；乱砍；轧布| To severely damage or disfigure by cutting, tearing, crushing, etc.; to ruin something, especially a text or a piece of music, by making mistakes or unnecessary changes.| The dog mangled the toy. 狗把玩具咬坏了。|  The editor mangled the story with his changes. 编辑的修改把故事搞砸了。| synonyms: mutilate, damage, ruin, distort, butcher| antonyms: repair, fix, improve, perfect|verb

mangle:/ˈmæŋɡl/ (US), /ˈmæŋɡəl/ (UK)| n.| 轧布机；洗衣机轧干装置| A machine for smoothing or pressing fabrics or clothes, usually using heated rollers; A machine with rollers for squeezing water out of washed clothes.|  She put the wet sheets through the mangle. 她把湿床单放进轧干机里。| The laundry mangle was a common appliance in the past. 洗衣机轧干装置在过去是一种常见的家用电器。| synonyms: wringer, flatwork ironer, calender| antonyms: N/A|noun
