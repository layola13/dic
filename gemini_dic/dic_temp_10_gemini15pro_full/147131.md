Characterized by reasoning that is excessively detailed or meticulous, often to the point of being impractical or pedantic. | He took an overlogical approach to the problem, missing the bigger picture. 他对这个问题采取了过于逻辑的方法，忽略了全局。 |  She's so overlogical that she can't even enjoy a simple movie without analyzing it to death. 她过于理性，甚至连一部简单的电影都不能好好欣赏，非要分析得天花乱坠。|  overscrupulous, overanalytical, hyperrational | impractical, intuitive, emotional | adjective
