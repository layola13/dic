hung-around:/ˌhʌŋ əˈraʊnd/| phrasal verb |闲逛，徘徊，逗留|To spend time idly or aimlessly; to loiter.|例句：He hung around the park all afternoon. 他整个下午都在公园闲逛。|近义词：loiter, linger, mill about|反义词:leave, depart|词性:动词短语

词源: "Hang" comes from Old English "hangian," meaning "to suspend." "Around" comes from Old English "on ymbhringe," meaning "in a circle."  The combination "hang around" evolved to mean remaining in a place without a specific purpose.
