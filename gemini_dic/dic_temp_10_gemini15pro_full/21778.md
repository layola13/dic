bobber:/ˈbɒbə(r) (UK), ˈbɑːbər (US)| n.|浮子，钓鱼浮标，短发|A float, especially one used in fishing; a person with bobbed hair (typically short).|The bobber dipped below the surface, signaling a bite. 浮标沉入水面以下，表明有鱼咬钩了。|近义词：float, buoy, cork, bob|反义词:sinker, weight|noun

bobber:/ˈbɒbə(r) (UK), ˈbɑːbər (US)| v.|上下浮动，剪短头发|To move up and down lightly and repeatedly; to cut hair short.|The cork bobbed gently on the water.  软木塞在水面上轻轻地上下浮动。|近义词：bounce, float, bob up and down|反义词:sink, submerge|verb
