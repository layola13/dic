caballo:/kɑːˈbɑːjoʊ/ (US), /kəˈbɑːjoʊ/ (UK)| n.|马|A domesticated, hoofed mammal used for riding, racing, and as a work animal. From Spanish *caballo*, ultimately from Late Latin *caballus* "horse," replacing classical Latin *equus*.|He rode his caballo across the plains. 他骑着他的马穿过平原。|近义词：horse, steed, mount|反义词: N/A|名词
