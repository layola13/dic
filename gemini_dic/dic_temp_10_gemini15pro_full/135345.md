nonpenitents:/ˌnɒnˈpɛnɪtənts/
|n.|
|未悔改者；不忏悔者|
|Those who do not feel or express regret or remorse for their sins or misdeeds.|
|The preacher's words seemed to fall on deaf ears among the nonpenitents.|
|牧师的话似乎对那些不悔改的人不起作用。|

|The nonpenitents continued their sinful ways despite the warnings.|
|尽管有警告，不忏悔者仍然继续他们的罪恶行径。|
|unrepentant, impenitent, remorseless|
|penitent, contrite, repentant|
|noun|
