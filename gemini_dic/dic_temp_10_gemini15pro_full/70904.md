fire-guard:/ˈfaɪərˌɡɑːrd/
n.|防火罩，火炉围栏|A metal or other framework placed in front of a fireplace to prevent sparks or embers from falling out.  It also serves to protect young children and pets from burns.|例句：The fire-guard prevented the sparks from setting the carpet alight. 防火罩防止了火星点燃地毯。|例句：We bought a new fire-guard for the fireplace. 我们买了一个新的壁炉防火罩。|近义词：fire screen, spark guard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fire hazard" in a loose sense)
词性: 名词
