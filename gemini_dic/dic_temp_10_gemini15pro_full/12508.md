aspersion:/əˈspɜːrʒən/
|n.|诽谤，中伤|An aspersion is a false or misleading statement that is intended to harm someone's reputation. |The politician's opponent cast aspersions on his character.  这位政治家的对手诽谤了他的人格。|Synonyms: defamation, slander, calumny, slur|Antonyms: praise, commendation, compliment|Noun
