mores:/ˈmɔːreɪz/ /ˈmɔːriz/
n.|习俗，道德观念|The customs, values, and behaviors that are considered acceptable and important by a particular social group.  A society's unwritten rules about what is considered right or wrong.|The mores of that society dictated that women should not work outside the home.  那个社会的习俗规定妇女不应外出工作。|customs, traditions, conventions|taboos, improprieties|noun
