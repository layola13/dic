discarding:/dɪˈskɑːrdɪŋ/
|v. (present participle)|丢弃，抛弃|The act of getting rid of something unwanted or useless. |They are discarding old furniture. 他们正在丢弃旧家具。|
discarding:/dɪˈskɑːrdɪŋ/
|adj.|丢弃的，抛弃的|Relating to the act of discarding something.|The discarding of industrial waste is regulated. 工业废料的丢弃受到管制。|
discarding:/dɪˈskɑːrdɪŋ/
|n.|丢弃，抛弃(行为)|The act of getting rid of something.|The discarding of evidence is a serious crime. 丢弃证据是严重的犯罪行为。|


Etymology: From the Old French "descarter," meaning "to discard," from "des-" (meaning "away") and "carte" (meaning "card"). Originally referred to discarding a playing card.

Synonyms:  jettisoning, dumping, scrapping, rejecting
Antonyms: keeping, retaining, saving, hoarding
