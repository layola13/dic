dieters:/ˈdaɪətərz/
n.
节食者，减肥者
People who are trying to lose weight by following a restricted diet.
Many dieters find it difficult to stick to their diet plans. 许多节食者发现很难坚持他们的饮食计划。
synonyms:  weight-watchers, slimmers
antonyms:  overeaters, gluttons
noun
