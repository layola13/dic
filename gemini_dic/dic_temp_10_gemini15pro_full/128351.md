neutralizes:/ˈnuːtrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使无效；中和；抵消
to make something ineffective or harmless by applying an opposite force or effect
Baking soda neutralizes stomach acid. 小苏打可以中和胃酸。
synonyms: counteract, offset, balance, cancel out
antonyms: activate, enhance, strengthen
词性: 动词
