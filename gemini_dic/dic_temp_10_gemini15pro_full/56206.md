dodgy:/ˈdɒdʒi/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒi/ (US)| adj.|狡猾的，躲闪的，不可靠的，危险的|Dishonest or unreliable; of questionable quality or legality; involving risk or danger.|例句：That guy looks a bit dodgy.  那个家伙看起来有点狡猾。|例句：The wiring in this old house is a bit dodgy. 这栋老房子的线路有点问题。|近义词：suspicious, shady, risky, unreliable, precarious|反义词：reliable, trustworthy, safe, secure|词性:形容词
dodgy:/ˈdɒdʒi/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒi/ (US)| n.|狡猾的人，骗子|A dishonest or unreliable person.|例句：He's known to be a bit of a dodgy. 他是出了名的狡猾。|例句：Watch out for dodgies in this area.  在这个地区要小心骗子。|近义词：con man, swindler, fraudster|反义词: honest person|词性:名词
