moldiest:/ˈməʊldiɪst/| adj.|最发霉的|Superlative form of moldy; covered with or smelling of mold to the greatest degree.|The basement was dark and damp, and the bread in the cupboard was the moldiest I'd ever seen. 地下室又黑又潮，橱柜里的面包是我见过的最发霉的。|近义词：mustiest, fustiest, stalest|反义词：freshest, newest|词性:形容词
