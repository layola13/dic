canebrake:/ˈkeɪnˌbreɪk/ | n. | 甘蔗丛；甘蔗田 | A thicket of canes, especially giant cane (Arundinaria gigantea). |  The runaway slaves found refuge in the dense canebrake. 逃亡的奴隶在茂密的甘蔗丛中找到了避难所。 | synonyms: thicket, brake, cane field | antonyms: open field, meadow | noun
