correspondent:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/ | n. | 通讯员，记者，通信者 | A person who writes letters to another person or a newspaper, especially one who is employed by a newspaper to report news from a particular place. |  Our foreign correspondent filed this report from Baghdad. 我们的驻外记者从巴格达发回了这篇报道。 | synonyms: reporter, journalist, writer, contributor, communicator | antonyms: recipient, receiver | noun

correspondent:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/ | adj. | 相应的，一致的，符合的 | Corresponding in character, form, or function. | The punishment should be correspondent to the crime. 惩罚应该与罪行相符。 | synonyms: equivalent, analogous, similar, comparable, matching | antonyms: different, dissimilar, contrasting, disparate | adjective
