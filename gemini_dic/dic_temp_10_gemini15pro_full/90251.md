houseboys:/ˈhaʊsbɔɪz/| n. |男仆，佣人|A male servant who performs domestic duties, typically in a large household.  This term is now considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its colonial connotations.|He employed two houseboys to maintain his large estate. 他雇了两个男仆来维护他的大庄园。|Servants, domestics| Butler, Maid | Noun
