boogieing:/ˈbuːɡiɪŋ/
verb
跳舞；狂欢
dancing energetically to rock music.
例句：They were boogieing the night away at the disco. 他们在迪斯科舞厅狂欢了一整夜。

例句：The band started playing and everyone was boogieing. 乐队开始演奏，每个人都跳起了舞。

近义词：dancing, grooving, jiving
反义词：sitting, standing still
词性：动词 (verb)

词源：Boogieing comes from the noun "boogie," which originated in the 1920s as "boogie-woogie," a style of blues piano music.  The verb "to boogie" then developed, meaning to dance to this music.  Over time, "boogieing" has come to mean dancing energetically to rock music in general.
