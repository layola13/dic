carbanil:/ˈkɑːrbənɪl/ |n.| 氨基甲酸酯; 苯氨基甲酰基 |A univalent radical derived from carbamic acid by removal of the hydroxyl group.  Specifically, the phenylcarbamoyl group, C6H5NHCO. |The melting point of the carbanil derivative was used to confirm the identity of the compound.  氨基甲酸酯衍生物的熔点用于确认化合物的身份。|Synonyms: carbamate, phenylcarbamoyl |Antonyms: N/A | Noun
