boding:/ˈboʊdɪŋ/| adj.|预兆的，凶兆的|Portending evil; ominous.|例句：The dark clouds were a boding sign of the storm to come.  乌云是即将到来的暴风雨的凶兆。|例句：There was a boding silence before the announcement.  宣布之前是一片不祥的寂静。|近义词：ominous, foreboding, portentous|反义词：auspicious, promising|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English bodian "to proclaim, announce, foretell," from Proto-Germanic *bod- (source also of Old Saxon bodian "to announce," Old Norse boda "to announce, foretell," Old Frisian bodia "to preach, announce," Middle Dutch boden "to announce," Dutch booden, German bieten "to offer").
