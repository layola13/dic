nonabsorbability:/ˌnɒnəbˌzɔːrbəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可吸收性；非吸收性 | The nonabsorbability of the material makes it suitable for use in wet environments.  这种材料的不可吸收性使其适用于潮湿环境。 |  The coating has high nonabsorbability, preventing water damage.  这种涂层具有很高的不可吸收性，可以防止水损害。 | 近义词: impermeability, impenetrability | 反义词: absorbability, permeability | 词性: noun | 词源: non- + absorbability, from absorb (Latin absorbere) + -ability
