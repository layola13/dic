crank-out:/ˈkræŋkˈaʊt/| v. |大量生产；粗制滥造|to produce something quickly and in large amounts, especially something of low quality|The factory cranks out thousands of these toys every day.  这家工厂每天生产成千上万的这种玩具。|The studio cranked out three movies last year.  该工作室去年制作了三部电影。|近义词：churn out, mass-produce, produce rapidly|反义词: craft, meticulously create|词性:动词

crank-out:/ˈkræŋkˈaʊt/| n. |(俚语)粗制滥造的作品|Something that is produced quickly and in large amounts, especially something of low quality|(This usage is less common) This song is just another crank-out from the pop music machine. 这首歌不过是流行音乐机器的又一次粗制滥造。|(This usage is less common) The movie was a predictable crank-out. 这部电影是一部可以预见的粗制滥造的作品。|近义词：potboiler|反义词: masterpiece|词性:名词
