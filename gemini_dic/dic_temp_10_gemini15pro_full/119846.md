mildly:/ˈmaɪldli/| adv.|轻微地；温和地；适度地|In a mild manner; to a slight or moderate degree. |The weather was mildly cold. 天气有点冷。|She smiled mildly. 她微微一笑。|近义词：slightly, gently, moderately|反义词：severely, harshly, extremely|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *myldeliche, from Old English *mīldelice (attested as mildelice), equivalent to mild +‎ -ly.
