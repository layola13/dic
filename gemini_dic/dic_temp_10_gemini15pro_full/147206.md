overmanages:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒɪz/
verb
过度管理,管理过细
to manage too much; to control or regulate excessively
The company overmanages its employees, stifling creativity and innovation.  这家公司过度管理员工，扼杀了创造力和创新。
Micromanaging and overmanaging can lead to employee burnout.  事无巨细的管理和过度管理会导致员工倦怠。
Synonym: micromanage, overcontrol
Antonym: undermanage, neglect
词性: 动词
