flimflammeries:/ˌflɪmˈflæməriz/
noun
欺骗；花招；胡说八道
nonsense, deceitful or foolish behavior, often intended to deceive
The salesman's promises were just a bunch of flimflammeries.  这个推销员的承诺只是一堆胡说八道。
His presentation was full of flimflammeries and empty promises. 他的演讲充满了花言巧语和空洞的承诺。
synonyms:  tricks, deceptions, shenanigans, chicanery, humbug
antonyms:  truths, realities, honesty, sincerity
词性: 名词(plural noun)
