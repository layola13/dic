edh: /ɛð/ | n. | (古英语/古冰岛语)诗歌; 灵感; 诗意 |  A term used primarily in Old English and Old Norse poetry, referring to poetic inspiration, skill, or art. It can also refer to a poetic composition itself. | Example: The poet's edh was evident in his beautiful verses. 这位诗人的诗意在他的优美的诗句中显而易见。  | Synonym: inspiration, creativity, poetry, verse | Antonym: dullness, lack of inspiration | noun
