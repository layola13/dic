alitrunk: /ˈælɪˌtrʌŋk/
n.
融合胸节；前体
The alitrunk is the fused thorax and first abdominal segment of hymenopteran insects, particularly ants, bees, and wasps.
The ant lifted a crumb many times its size with its powerful alitrunk.  这只蚂蚁用它强壮的前体举起了一块比它自身大许多的面包屑。
The queen bee's alitrunk was noticeably larger than the worker bees'.  蜂王的前体明显比工蜂的大。
Synonym: mesosoma
Antonym: gaster (referring to the abdomen following the alitrunk)
词性: noun
