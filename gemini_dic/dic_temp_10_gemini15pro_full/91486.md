hymenophore:/haɪˈmɛnəfɔːr/
n.
子实层体，菌膜
The part of a fungal fruiting body that bears the hymenium (the spore-bearing surface).
The gills of a mushroom are a type of hymenophore.  这种蘑菇的菌褶是一种子实层体。
The pores on the underside of a bolete are another example of a hymenophore. 牛肝菌下面的孔是子实层体的另一个例子。
近义词: hymenium, fruiting body
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ὑμήν (humḗn, “membrane”) + -phore
