fumblingness:/ˈfʌmblɪŋnəs/| n. |笨拙，笨手笨脚|The state of being clumsy or awkward in one's movements or actions.  Characterized by dropping things, tripping, or generally lacking coordination.|His fumblingness with the controls caused the machine to malfunction. 他对控制装置的笨拙操作导致机器故障。|Near Synonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness, maladroitness|Antonyms: dexterity, skillfulness, adroitness, deftness|Noun

fumble:/ˈfʌmbl/| v. n. |摸索，笨拙地处理|1.(v.) To handle something clumsily or awkwardly; to grope for or search blindly. 2.(n.) An act of fumbling; a clumsy mistake.|She fumbled in her purse for her keys. 她在钱包里摸索着找钥匙。|Near Synonyms: grope, fumble around/about, blunder|Antonyms: grasp, seize, manage skillfully|Verb, Noun
