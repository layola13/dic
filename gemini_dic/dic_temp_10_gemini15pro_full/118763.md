metallics: /məˈtælɪks/
noun|金属色，金属漆，金属制品|Metallic colors, paints, or products.  Metallics are substances that have a shiny or lustrous appearance, resembling metal. They can refer to actual metals or materials that mimic the look of metal.|She wore a dress with metallic threads woven into the fabric. 她穿了一件织有金属线的连衣裙。|Synonyms:  iridescent, shimmering, lustrous, glittering|Antonyms: matte, dull|n.
