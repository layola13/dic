bardine:/bɑːrˈdiːn/ | n. |巴丁鱼;巴丁鱼(欧洲产淡水鱼的一种)|A small European freshwater fish (Barbus barbus) of the carp family, with four barbels around its mouth. |例句：The bardine is a common fish in European rivers. 巴丁鱼是欧洲河流中常见的鱼。|例句：He caught a large bardine while fishing in the Danube. 他在多瑙河钓鱼时钓到了一条大巴丁鱼。| 近义词：barbel, barb | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bardine*, diminutive of *bar*, from Latin *barba* "beard," referring to the barbels around the fish's mouth.
