buildup:/ˈbɪldʌp/ | n. | 积累，增多，增强；宣传；准备工作 | An increase in something over a period of time.  A gradual increase in the amount of something, or the process of increasing something gradually.  Publicity and advertising for a product, event, etc., before it appears or takes place. | The media’s buildup to the election was excessive. 媒体对选举的宣传铺天盖地。

There was a gradual buildup of tension between the two countries. 两国之间的紧张关系逐渐升级。

The company launched a massive marketing buildup before the product's release.  公司在产品发布前进行了大规模的市场推广。 | synonyms: accumulation, increase, growth, intensification, escalation, promotion, hype | antonyms: decrease, reduction, decline, diminution | noun
