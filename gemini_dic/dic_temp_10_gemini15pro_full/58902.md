durio:/ˈdʊəriˌoʊ/ | n. | 榴莲 | A large, oval, spiny fruit containing a creamy, pungent pulp.  It is native to Southeast Asia. |  The durian is known as the "king of fruits". 榴莲被称为“水果之王”。

The smell of durian is quite overpowering. 榴莲的味道很浓烈。


|近义词:  (No direct synonym for the fruit itself, perhaps "jackfruit" in terms of being a large tropical fruit) |反义词: None | 词性: noun
