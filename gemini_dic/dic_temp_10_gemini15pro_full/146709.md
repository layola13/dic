overhigh:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪ/| adj.|过高的，过度的|Excessively high; higher than is desirable or necessary.  It combines the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "too much") with the adjective "high."|The overhigh price of houses in this city makes them unaffordable for many people. 这个城市过高的房价让很多人负担不起。|The company's overhigh expectations led to disappointment.  这家公司过高的期望导致了失望。|近义词：excessive, exorbitant, unreasonable|反义词：low, reasonable, moderate|词性:形容词

词源:
From "over-" + "high". "Over-" is from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across." "High" is from Old English "hēah."
