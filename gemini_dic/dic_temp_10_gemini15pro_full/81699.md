greenhood:/ˈɡriːnhʊd/| n. |绿兜兰；绿头巾|A terrestrial orchid of the genus *Pterostylis*, which has a green, hood-like flower.  It is native to Australia and New Zealand.|I saw a beautiful greenhood orchid in the Royal Botanic Gardens. 我在皇家植物园看到一株美丽的绿兜兰。|Synonyms: orchid, slipper orchid |Antonyms: N/A|noun

greenhood:/ˈɡriːnhʊd/| n. |新手；缺乏经验的人|A person who is new or inexperienced in a particular activity or field.|He's a greenhood when it comes to investing. 他在投资方面是个新手。|Synonyms: novice, beginner, tyro|Antonyms: expert, veteran, professional|noun
