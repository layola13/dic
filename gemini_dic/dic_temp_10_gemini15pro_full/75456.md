fruits: /fruːts/ | n. | 水果 | The sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seed and can be eaten as food. |  She bought a variety of fruits at the market.  她在市场上买了各种各样的水果。| synonyms: produce, harvest | antonyms: vegetables, meats | noun

fruits: /fruːts/ | v. (third-person singular simple present fruits, present participle fruiting, simple past and past participle fruited) | 结果实 | To bear or yield fruit. | The apple tree fruited heavily this year. 今年苹果树结了很多果实。 | synonyms: bear, yield, produce | antonyms: wither, die | verb
