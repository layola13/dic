immediateness:/ɪˈmiːdiət.nəs/| n.|直接性；即时性；紧迫性|The quality of being immediate; directness.  The state of being urgent or pressing.|The immediateness of the threat was apparent to everyone.  威胁的紧迫性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|近义词：urgency, proximity, closeness, directness|反义词：remoteness, distance, delay|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From immediate + -ness.  "Immediate" comes from Late Latin immediatus "without a mediator," from in- "not" + mediatus "mediated," past participle of mediare "to be in the middle," from Latin medius "middle."
