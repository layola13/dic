antimoralist:/ˌæntiˈmɔːrəlɪst/
noun
反道德主义者
Someone who opposes or rejects traditional morality or moral principles.
He's not so much immoral as antimoralist; he simply doesn't believe morality exists. 他与其说不道德，不如说是反道德主义者；他根本不相信道德的存在。
近义词: amoralist, immoralist, libertine
反义词: moralist, puritan
