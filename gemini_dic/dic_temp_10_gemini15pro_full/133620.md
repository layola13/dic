nonforbearances:/ˌnɒnfɔːrˈbɛərənsɪz/
n.
不宽容，不 forbearance 的行为或事例
The bank's nonforbearances on late payments resulted in several foreclosures.  这家银行对逾期付款的不宽容导致了几次丧失抵押品赎回权。
nonforbearance relates to the act of not showing patience or leniency, especially in demanding the repayment of a debt.  不宽容指的是不表现出耐心或宽容的行为，尤其是在要求偿还债务时。
The government's nonforbearance on illegal immigration has led to stricter border controls. 政府对非法移民的不宽容导致了更严格的边境管制。
Near Synonyms:  intransigence, inflexibility, rigor
Antonyms: forbearance, leniency, tolerance
Noun
