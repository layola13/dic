cholas:/ˈtʃoʊləz/ (US), /ˈtʃəʊləz/ (UK)| n. | 奇卡娜女孩，拉丁裔女孩（尤指打扮时尚、具有叛逆精神的）|A subculture of young Latinas, particularly Mexican-American women, often associated with a distinct style of dress, makeup, and attitude.  Often characterized by their bold fashion choices, including baggy pants, bandanas, and dark lipstick.|例句：The cholas at the party were dancing to hip-hop music.  派对上的奇卡娜女孩们在跳嘻哈舞。

例句：The movie portrays a group of cholas navigating life in East Los Angeles.  这部电影描绘了一群在东洛杉矶生活的奇卡娜女孩。|近义词：homegirls, chicas|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "preppy" in terms of style contrast)|词性: Noun
