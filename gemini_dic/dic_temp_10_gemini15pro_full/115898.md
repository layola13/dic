marmorean:/mɑːrˈmɔːriən/| adj.|大理石的；似大理石的；冷酷无情的|Made of or resembling marble; smooth, cold, and white like marble; figuratively, cold and unfeeling.|例句：The marmorean statue stood proudly in the town square.  大理石雕像傲然屹立在城镇广场上。

例句：He had a marmorean expression, showing no emotion. 他面无表情，像大理石一样冰冷。|近义词：marble, stony, impassive, aloof|反义词：warm, expressive, compassionate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin "marmoreus," from "marmor" (marble). 源自拉丁语 "marmoreus"，来自 "marmor"（大理石）。
