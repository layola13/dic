nontransparently:/ˌnɒntrænsˈpærəntli/ | adv. | 不透明地，不公开地 | In a way that is not transparent; obscurely or opaquely.  The decision-making process was conducted nontransparently, leaving many stakeholders feeling excluded. |  The government operated nontransparently, concealing crucial information from the public. | 近义词: opaquely, obscurely, secretly, covertly | 反义词: transparently, openly, clearly, publicly | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
transparent (adjective): from Latin trans- "through" + parere "appear"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
