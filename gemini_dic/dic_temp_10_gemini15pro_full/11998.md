arthroderm:/ˈɑːrθrəˌdɜːrm/
n.
节肢动物外骨骼,节肢动物的硬皮
the hard, jointed outer covering of an arthropod.
Trilobites, like other arthropods, periodically shed their arthroderm as they grow.  三叶虫像其他节肢动物一样，随着它们的生长周期性地蜕掉它们的外骨骼。
The arthroderm provides protection and support for the arthropod.  外骨骼为节肢动物提供保护和支撑。
exoskeleton, cuticle
shell, carapace
