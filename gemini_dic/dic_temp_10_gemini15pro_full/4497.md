albinos:/ælˈbaɪnoʊz/
noun
白化病患者（复数）
A person or animal with albinism, a condition characterized by a lack of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.
Albino rabbits are quite rare. 白化兔子很罕见。
Synonyms:  albinistic people,  people with albinism
Antonyms:  pigmented individuals
