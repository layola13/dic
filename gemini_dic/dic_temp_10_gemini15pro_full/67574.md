extrapolatory:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtəri/
adjective
外推的，推断的
based on extending a trend or data set beyond the area or period for which there is evidence
例句：The extrapolatory projections suggest a significant increase in sales next year.  这些外推预测表明明年销售额将大幅增长。

例句：Making extrapolatory predictions about the future can be risky. 对未来做出推断性的预测可能会有风险。

synonym:  projected, inferred, deduced, conjectural
antonym:  interpolatory, observed, factual
词性: adjective
