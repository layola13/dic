mysticality:/ˌmɪstɪˈkælɪti/ | n. | 神秘性，神秘感 | The quality of being mystical; a mysterious or spiritual quality. | The mysticality of the ancient ruins drew many visitors. 古老遗址的神秘性吸引了许多游客。 | synonyms: mystery, spirituality, otherworldliness, enigma, magic | antonyms: mundanity, ordinariness, clarity, concreteness | noun

mystical:/ˈmɪstɪkl/ | adj. | 神秘的 | Having a spiritual meaning or reality that is neither apparent to the senses nor obvious to the intellect; relating to or characteristic of mysticism. |  The mystical experience left her feeling deeply connected to the universe.  这次神秘的经历让她感到与宇宙深深地联系在一起。| synonyms: spiritual, esoteric, occult, supernatural, transcendental | antonyms: mundane, ordinary, rational, scientific | adjective
