elaterite:/ɪˈlætəraɪt/
n.
弹性沥青，韧性沥青
A dark brown, naturally occurring, elastic asphalt.
Elaterite is sometimes used as a sealant. 弹性沥青有时用作密封剂。
近义词:mineral caoutchouc, elastic bitumen
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider hard asphalt or brittle bitumen as contrasting materials)
词性:noun
