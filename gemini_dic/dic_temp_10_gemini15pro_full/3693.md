aggregated:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 合计的，总的，聚合的 | Formed by adding together several amounts or things. |
The aggregated score of all the students was impressive. 所有学生的总分令人印象深刻。
The aggregated data from multiple sources provided a comprehensive overview. 来自多个来源的汇总数据提供了一个全面的概述。 |
近义词: combined, total, collective, cumulative |
反义词: individual, separate, disaggregated |
词性: 形容词

aggregated:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/ | v. | 合计，总计，汇总（aggregate的过去式和过去分词） |  Combined into a single whole or total. |
The researcher aggregated the data from various experiments. 研究人员汇总了来自不同实验的数据。
All the donations were aggregated into a single fund. 所有的捐款都汇总到一个基金中。 |
近义词: combined, totaled, summed, amassed |
反义词: separated, divided, dispersed |
词性: 动词

词源: From Latin aggregatus, past participle of aggregare "to add to a flock," from ad- "to" + grex "flock."
