ineffervescence:/ˌɪnɪfərˈvɛsəns/| n. |沉闷；无生气；不活跃|lack of effervescence; the quality of being flat and dull.|The ineffervescence of the meeting made it hard to stay awake. 会议的沉闷让人昏昏欲睡。|近义词：dullness, flatness, inactivity, lifelessness, torpor|反义词: effervescence, liveliness, vibrancy, animation, exuberance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + effervescence (from Latin effervescere, to boil over).  The "in-" prefix negates the meaning of effervescence, which describes a bubbly or lively quality.
