attractable:/əˈtræktəbl/
adjective
可吸引的；有吸引力的
Capable of being attracted; attractive.
Iron filings are attractable to a magnet. 铁屑会被磁铁吸引。
Synonyms: appealing, alluring, charming, magnetic, captivating
Antonyms: repellent, repulsive, unappealing, unattractive
词性: adjective

attractable:/əˈtræktəbl/
词源: From attract + -able.  "Attract" comes from the Latin "attrahere," meaning "to draw to."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being."
中文翻译: 可吸引的；有吸引力的
英文解析: Able to be attracted; having qualities that attract or draw someone or something.  Often used in a physical sense, referring to magnetic or gravitational forces, but can also be used figuratively to describe things that draw interest or attention.
例句：
The colorful flowers were attractable to bees. 这些五颜六色的花朵吸引了蜜蜂。
His charisma made him attractable to many people. 他的魅力使他吸引了很多人。

近义词: appealing, alluring, magnetic, captivating, charming
反义词: repellent, repulsive, unappealing, unattractive
词性: adjective
