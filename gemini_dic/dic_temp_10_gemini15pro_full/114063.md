magnetize:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/ |v. |磁化，使具有磁性|To make something magnetic; to attract strongly, as if by magnetism.|例句：The iron filings were magnetized by the bar magnet. 铁屑被条形磁铁磁化了。

例句：His charisma magnetized people to him. 他的人格魅力深深地吸引着人们。|近义词：attract, draw, influence|反义词：demagnetize|词性：动词
