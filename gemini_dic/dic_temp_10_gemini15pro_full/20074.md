biorhythmicist:/ˌbaɪoʊˈrɪðmɪsɪst/| n. |生物节律学家|A person who studies or specializes in biorhythms, which are cyclical patterns in physiological and psychological states believed by some to affect human behavior.|The biorhythmicist predicted a challenging day based on my emotional cycle.|Synonyms: biorhythm expert, biorhythm analyst|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps skeptic or critic of biorhythms)|noun
