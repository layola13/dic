gashest:/ˈɡæʃɪst/
adjective
最深的，最大的（伤口）
deepest or largest (wound)
The soldier had the gashest wound I'd ever seen.  这个士兵的伤口是我见过的最深的。
The earthquake left a gashest scar across the landscape. 地震在地表留下了一道巨大的伤疤。
近义词:deepest, largest, most severe, widest
反义词:smallest, shallowest, slightest
词性:adjective

词源:
From *gash* + *-est* (superlative suffix). *Gash* comes from Old French *garser* "to scarify, gash."
