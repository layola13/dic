dhobi:/ˈdoʊbi/| n.|洗衣工，洗衣人|A person who washes clothes, especially one who does so as a profession, traditionally in South Asia.|例句：The dhobi collected the laundry this morning. 今早洗衣工来收衣服了。

例句：My grandmother used to take our clothes to the dhobi ghat. 我祖母过去常常把我们的衣服拿到公共洗衣处。|近义词：launderer, washerman, laundryman|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a profession)|词性: Noun
