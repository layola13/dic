initiated:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪd/
v.|开始，创始，发起；使初步了解；接纳（新成员）|to begin something; to teach someone about the basic facts of a subject; to formally accept someone as a member of a group or organization|The government initiated a new program to combat poverty. 政府启动了一项新的扶贫计划。|began, started, commenced|ended, terminated|verb

initiated:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪd/
adj.|已开始的；已入会的；有经验的|having been introduced to something; having been accepted as a member; experienced|He is an initiated member of the secret society. 他是这个秘密社团的正式成员。|experienced, knowledgeable, sophisticated|inexperienced, naive, uninitiated|adjective
