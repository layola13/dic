eldercare:/ˈeldərˌker/| n.|老年护理，老年人护理|The demand for eldercare services is increasing as the population ages.  随着人口老龄化，对老年护理服务的需求正在增加。|Eldercare is a growing industry. 老年护理是一个不断发展的行业。|近义词：geriatric care, senior care|反义词: childcare,  pediatric care|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
elder (Old English "eldra" comparative adjective meaning "older") + care (Old English "caru, cearu" meaning "anxiety, sorrow, grief," later meaning "attention, heed, charge")
