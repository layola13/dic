interpealed:/ˌɪntərˈpiːld/ | v. |  上诉，提出申诉 | To make a formal appeal to a higher authority or court against a decision. |  The defendant interpealed against the judge's decision. 被告对法官的判决提出了上诉。 | appeal, contest, challenge |  accept, concede | verb
Etymology: From Late Latin *interpellare, from Latin inter- 'between' + appellare 'to address, call upon.'
