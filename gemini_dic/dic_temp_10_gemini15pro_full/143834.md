outperformed: /ˌaʊt.pɚˈfɔːrmd/ | v. | 超过，胜过 | performed better than; exceeded the performance of | Our new product has significantly outperformed the competition in sales this quarter.  我们公司的新产品在本季度的销售额上显著超过了竞争对手。| The company outperformed market expectations. 该公司的业绩超出了市场预期。 | 近义词: excelled, surpassed, beat, triumphed over | 反义词: underperformed, lagged behind | 词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (超过) + "perform" (执行，表现)
