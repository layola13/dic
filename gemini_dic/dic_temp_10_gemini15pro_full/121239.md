miserably:/ˈmɪzərəbli/| adv.|悲惨地，痛苦地，非常糟糕地|In a way that causes or involves great unhappiness or discomfort.  Feeling or showing great unhappiness.|例句：She failed miserably. 她惨败了。|近义词：wretchedly, woefully, poorly, terribly|反义词：happily, joyfully, splendidly|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *miserabely, from Old French miserablement, from Latin miserabilis (“pitiable, wretched”) + -ment. Equivalent to miserable +‎ -ly.
