chauvinism:/ˈʃoʊvɪnɪzəm/ | n. | 沙文主义，盲目爱国主义 | Excessive or prejudiced loyalty or support for one's own group or cause. | Example 1: His chauvinism led him to believe that his country was superior to all others. 他的沙文主义使他相信他的国家比所有其他国家都优越。

Example 2:  Her cultural chauvinism made it difficult for her to appreciate other cultures. 她的文化沙文主义使她难以欣赏其他文化。 | 近义词: jingoism, bigotry, fanaticism | 反义词: tolerance, cosmopolitanism | 词性: 名词

chauvinistic:/ˌʃoʊvɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 沙文主义的，盲目爱国的 | Feeling or displaying aggressive or exaggerated patriotism. | Example 1:  His chauvinistic remarks offended many people at the conference. 他沙文主义的言论冒犯了会议上的许多人。

Example 2:  The chauvinistic attitude of the company made it difficult for women to advance. 该公司沙文主义的态度使女性难以晋升。 | 近义词: jingoistic, bigoted, fanatical | 反义词: tolerant, cosmopolitan | 词性: 形容词
