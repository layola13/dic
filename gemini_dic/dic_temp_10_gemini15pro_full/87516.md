例句：The artist's heterodoxal approach to painting challenged traditional techniques. 这位艺术家非正统的绘画方法挑战了传统技法。

近义词：unorthodox, unconventional, dissenting, heretical, iconoclastic|反义词：orthodox, conventional, traditional, conformist|词性：形容词
