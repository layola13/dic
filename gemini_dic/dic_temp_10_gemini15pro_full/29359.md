castrated:/ˈkæstreɪtɪd/
adjective
阉割的; 去势的
deprived of the testicles or ovaries; emasculated
例句：The castrated bull became docile. 这头被阉割的公牛变得温顺了。

例句：The farmer castrated the piglets to prevent unwanted breeding.  农民阉割了小猪以防止意外繁殖。

近义词：gelded, neutered, spayed
反义词：intact, uncastrated
词性: adjective
