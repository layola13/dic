overtensions:/ˌoʊvərˈtɛnʃənz/
| n. |过电压；超电压|An overvoltage or overtension is a voltage that is higher than what it should be. It can damage electrical equipment.|The power surge caused overtensions that fried the computer's motherboard. 电涌导致过电压，烧坏了电脑的主板。|
The overtensions damaged several appliances in the house. 房屋里的几个电器由于过电压而损坏。|
Synonyms: overvoltage, surge, spike|
Antonyms: undervoltage, brownout|
