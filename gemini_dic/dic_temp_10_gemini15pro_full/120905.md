miscaptioning:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃənɪŋ/
n.
错误的字幕，配错的标题
The act of providing an inaccurate or misleading caption for an image, video, or other media.
The photo of the dog playing fetch was miscaptioned as a cat chasing a mouse. 这张狗狗玩接球的照片被错误地标注为猫咪追老鼠。
mislabeling, mistitling
correct captioning
noun
