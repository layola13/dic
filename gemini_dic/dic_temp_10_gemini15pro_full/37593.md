communicable:/kəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/ | adj. | 可传达的；可沟通的；传染性的 | Able to be communicated or transmitted, especially of diseases. | Measles is a highly communicable disease. 麻疹是一种高度传染性疾病。 |  The information is not readily communicable to the public.  这些信息不容易向公众传达。 | Synonyms: contagious, infectious, transmissible | Antonyms: non-communicable, non-contagious | adjective
