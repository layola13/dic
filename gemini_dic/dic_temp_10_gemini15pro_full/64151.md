equalize:/ˈiːkwəlaɪz/ /ˈikwəˌlaɪz/| v. |使相等，使平等，扳平|to make things or groups equal|The government is trying to equalize opportunities for all children. 政府正在努力使所有儿童的机会均等。|They scored a goal in the last minute to equalize the game.  他们在最后一分钟进球扳平了比分。|近义词：balance, level, even out|反义词:differentiate, distinguish, unbalance|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *equalisen*, from Old French *equaliser*, from Late Latin *aequalizāre*, from Latin *aequālis* (“equal”).
