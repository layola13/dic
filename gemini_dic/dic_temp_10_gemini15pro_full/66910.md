expeditiousness:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃəsnəs/
n.
迅速；敏捷；效率
the quality of acting or working quickly and efficiently
The expeditiousness of the delivery service was impressive.  快递服务的效率令人印象深刻。
近义词: speed, swiftness, rapidity, promptness, efficiency
反义词: slowness, sluggishness, delay, inefficiency
词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *expeditus*, past participle of expedire "extricate, set free, prepare, make ready," from ex- "out" + ped- "foot"
