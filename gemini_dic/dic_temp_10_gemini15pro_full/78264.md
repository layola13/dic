germanicism:/ˈdʒɜːrmənɪsɪzəm/ /ˈdʒɜːrmənɪˌsɪzəm/
n.|日耳曼语言的特征；德语词语(或表达方式)；日耳曼习俗|A word, expression, or grammatical construction characteristic of the German language, or a custom typical of German people.|The use of "rucksack" instead of "backpack" is a germanicism. 用“rucksack”（背包）而不是“backpack”是德语用法。|loanword, borrowing|None|noun
