embarkments: /ɪmˈbɑːrkments/ | n. | 堤岸，码头 |  A structure built along a river or coastline to prevent flooding or to provide a landing place for ships.  It's the plural form of "embarkment." | The city's embarkments were reinforced after the flood.  这座城市的堤防在洪水过后得到了加固。|

The new embarkments provided safe mooring for the fishing boats.  新的码头为渔船提供了安全的停泊处。 | Synonyms: wharves, piers, quays, levees, dikes | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: open shore, unprotected coast) | noun
