giantly:/ˈdʒaɪəntli/| adv.|巨大地；庞大地|In a giant-like or enormous manner.  It describes actions or qualities performed or possessed in a way resembling a giant.|The tree grew giantly, overshadowing the entire house.  这棵树长得巨大，遮蔽了整栋房子。|Synonyms: enormously, hugely, immensely, massively|Antonyms: minutely, slightly, tinily|adverb
