emphyteusis:/ˌɛmfɪˈtjuːsɪs/
n.
永佃权；土地长租权
A long-term lease of land or property, typically for agricultural purposes, where the tenant holds the land in perpetuity or for a very long period, subject to certain conditions, including the payment of an annual rent and the obligation to improve the property.
The farmer held the land under emphyteusis, paying a modest annual rent to the landowner.  这位农民以永佃权持有这块土地，每年向地主支付少量的租金。
The emphyteusis agreement allowed the family to cultivate the land for generations.  永佃权协议允许这家人世代耕种这块土地。
近义词:long-term lease, perpetual lease
