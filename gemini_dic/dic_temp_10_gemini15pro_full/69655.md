fence-mending:/ˈfɛnsˌmɛndɪŋ/
|n.|修补栅栏；弥合关系；和解|The act of repairing a fence, often used metaphorically to describe the act of repairing a damaged relationship.|After their argument, they engaged in some serious fence-mending.  争吵之后，他们认真地进行了和解。|近义词：reconciliation, rapprochement, appeasement|反义词：estrangement, alienation|noun
