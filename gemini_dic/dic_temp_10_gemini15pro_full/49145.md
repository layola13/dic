dejected:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
Sad and depressed; low in spirits.
She looked dejected after failing the exam. 她考试不及格后看起来很沮丧。
近义词:downcast, disheartened, despondent, depressed, gloomy
反义词:cheerful, elated, happy, joyful
词性:adjective

dejected:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪd/
词源:From Latin dejectus, past participle of dejicere "to throw down," from de- "down" + jacere "to throw."
词性:adjective
中文翻译:沮丧的，灰心的
英文解析:Feeling or showing sadness and discouragement.  Characterized by low spirits.
例句:He walked home with dejected shoulders after losing the game.  他输了比赛后垂头丧气地走回家。

例句:The team was dejected after their unexpected defeat.  球队意外失利后非常沮丧。

近义词:dispirited, downcast, disheartened, depressed, gloomy
反义词:cheerful, elated, happy, joyful, optimistic
