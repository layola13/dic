nonclandestine:/ˌnɑːnklænˈdɛstɪn/
adjective
公开的，非秘密的
Not clandestine; open; not secret.
The meeting was nonclandestine and open to the public. 这次会议是公开的，公众可以参加。
The negotiations were nonclandestine and transparent. 谈判是公开透明的。

近义词:open, public, overt, aboveboard
反义词:clandestine, secret, covert, hidden
词性:adjective
