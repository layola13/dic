congregating:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ (US)| v.|聚集，集合|The act of coming together in a group or crowd.  People were congregating outside the stadium before the concert.|例句：Fans were congregating around the stage door. 歌迷们聚集在舞台门口。

例句：Birds are congregating on the telephone wires. 鸟儿聚集在电话线上。

近义词：gathering, assembling, amassing, clustering|反义词：dispersing, scattering, separating|词性：动词现在分词
