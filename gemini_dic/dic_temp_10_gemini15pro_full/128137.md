netify:/ˈnɛtɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使净值化; 使净值增加 | To increase the net value of something; to make something more profitable by reducing costs or increasing revenue. |  Example: The company netified its assets by selling off underperforming divisions. 公司通过出售表现不佳的部门使其资产净值化。

Example:  The new software helped to netify the business by automating several key processes.  新软件通过自动化几个关键流程帮助企业提高了净值。| 近义词: optimize, streamline, improve, enhance | 反义词: worsen, deteriorate, decline | 词性: verb
