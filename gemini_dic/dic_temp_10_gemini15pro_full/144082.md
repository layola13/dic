outrushing:/ˈaʊtrʌʃɪŋ/| adj.|向外奔涌的，涌出的|A sudden outrushing of air from the punctured tire.  从被刺破的轮胎里突然涌出一股气体。|例句：The outrushing tide carried the debris out to sea. 退潮将碎片带到了海里。|近义词：gushing, surging, streaming, effusive|反义词：inflowing, incoming, receding|词性:形容词

outrush:/ˈaʊtrʌʃ/| n.|涌出，流出|The outrush of water from the dam was powerful.  大坝里的水流汹涌而出。|例句：The outrush of fans after the concert caused a traffic jam.  音乐会结束后歌迷的涌出造成了交通堵塞。|近义词：gush, surge, stream, efflux|反义词：inflow, influx|词性:名词

词源:  out- (prefix) + rush (verb/noun)  "out-" 前缀表示“向外”，"rush" 表示“冲，涌”。
