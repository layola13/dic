dissipatedly:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪtɪdli/
adverb
放荡地；挥霍无度地；沉迷酒色地
In a dissipated manner; characterized by excessive indulgence in pleasure or debauchery.
He lived dissipatedly, squandering his fortune on wine, women, and song. 他过着放荡的生活，把自己的财产挥霍在酒、女人和歌曲上。
Synonyms:  profligately, debauchedly, riotously, wantonly, licentiously
Antonyms:  temperately, moderately, abstemiously, prudently, chastely
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissipatus, past participle of dissipare ("to scatter, disperse"), from dis- ("apart, asunder") + sipare ("to throw, scatter").  The adverbial form is derived by adding the suffix "-ly."
