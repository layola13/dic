affordable:/əˈfɔːrdəbl/| adj.|负担得起的，买得起的|Something that is affordable is reasonably priced and not too expensive, so that people can afford to buy it.|例句：The new apartments are affordable for young families. 这些新公寓对年轻家庭来说价格实惠。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, reasonable, budget, modest|反义词:expensive, costly, unaffordable, exorbitant, pricey|词性:形容词
词源:From afford + -able.  "Afford" comes from Old English *geforðian "to carry out, accomplish, perform," from forð "forth, forward."
