locomobile:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊbiːl/| n.|蒸汽动力汽车；早期汽车|A self-propelled road vehicle powered by steam, especially one of the early types.|例句：The locomobile was a popular mode of transportation in the early 20th century.  20世纪初，蒸汽动力汽车是一种流行的交通工具。

例句：His grandfather owned a Stanley Steamer locomobile. 他祖父拥有一辆斯坦利蒸汽动力汽车。|近义词：automobile, car, motorcar, horseless carriage|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *locus* ("place") + *mobilis* ("movable"). Coined in the late 19th century.
