churchwardenism:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdənɪzəm/ | n. |教区执事主义；狭隘的保守主义|An attitude of parochial and petty conservatism, often associated with interference in the affairs of others, especially in matters of morality or religious observance.  It derives from the stereotype of the overzealous churchwarden interfering in the lives of parishioners.|The vicar's churchwardenism led him to criticize the length of women's skirts. 牧师的教区执事主义导致他批评妇女的裙子太长。|Near Synonyms: parochialism, narrow-mindedness, prudery, interference, moralizing |Antonyms: liberalism, open-mindedness, tolerance|noun
