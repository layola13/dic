birthless:/ˈbɜːrθlɪs/| adj.|无生的，未出生的，非由生育产生的|Not born; having no birth; not produced by birth.  Existing without having been born.  Often used figuratively to describe something that seems to have always existed, or something not created through traditional means.|The ancient gods were often described as birthless beings.  古代的神通常被描述为无生的存在。

The universe, in some cosmologies, is considered birthless and eternal.  在某些宇宙学中，宇宙被认为是无生的和永恒的。|近义词：unborn, eternal, ageless, uncreated|反义词：born, created, mortal|词性:形容词
