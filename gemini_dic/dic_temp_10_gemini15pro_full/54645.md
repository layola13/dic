disnaturalize:/dɪsˈnætʃrəlaɪz/
verb
剥夺公民权；使变性；使失去自然属性
To deprive of the rights and privileges of a citizen or national; to make unnatural or artificial.
The government disnaturalized him for treason. 政府以叛国罪剥夺了他的公民权。
The processed food had been completely disnaturalized.  加工食品已经完全失去了自然属性。
近义词:denaturalize, expatriate
反义词:naturalize
词性:v.
