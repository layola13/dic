iguanodont:/ɪˈɡwɑːnədɒnt/ /ɪˈɡwɑːnəˌdɑːnt/| n. |禽龙|A large herbivorous dinosaur of the Cretaceous period, with a spiked thumb and hooflike claws.|The museum displayed a complete iguanodont skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具完整的禽龙骨架。|Synonyms: dinosaur, ornithopod|Antonyms: N/A|noun
Etymology->From Modern Latin *Iguanodon*, from *iguana* + Greek *odous* tooth.  Ultimately from Taino *iwana* and Ancient Greek *odoús*.|
