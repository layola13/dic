inorganization:/ɪnˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.| 缺乏组织; 组织混乱|Lack of organization or a state of being disorganized.  It refers to a situation where things are not arranged in a systematic or orderly way.| The office was in a state of complete inorganization after the flood. 办公室在洪水过后一片混乱，毫无组织。|The inorganization of the event led to a lot of confusion. 活动的组织混乱导致了很大的困惑。|近义词：disorder, chaos, mess, disarray|反义词：organization, order, tidiness, system|词性:名词
