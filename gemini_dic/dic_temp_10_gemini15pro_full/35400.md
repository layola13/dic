co-oping:/koʊˈɑːpɪŋ/ | n. | 合作公寓；合作社；合作教育 | A type of housing where residents own shares in a corporation that owns the building, or a program where students work in jobs related to their field of study. |  My friend lives in a co-oping in the city. 我的朋友住在市里的一套合作公寓里。|  She's doing a co-oping program this semester and working at a tech company. 她这学期在参加一个合作教育项目，在一家科技公司工作。| synonyms: cooperative housing, cooperative education, internship | antonyms:  private ownership, traditional education | noun
