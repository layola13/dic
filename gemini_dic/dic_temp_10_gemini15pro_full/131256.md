noncash:/ˌnɑːnˈkæʃ/
adjective
非现金的
Not involving or requiring cash; paid for by means other than cash.
The company offers a noncash incentive program for its employees.  公司为员工提供非现金激励计划。
The museum accepts noncash donations such as artwork or antiques.  博物馆接受艺术品或古董等非现金捐赠。
Synonyms: cashless, non-monetary
Antonyms: cash, monetary
