carpellum:/ˈkɑːrpələm/ (US), /kɑːˈpɛləm/ (UK)| n. |心皮|The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of an ovary, a style, and a stigma. It may occur singly or as one of a group.|The flower has five carpels fused together. 这朵花有五个心皮融合在一起。|Synonym: pistil (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically the pistil can be composed of multiple carpels)|Antonym: stamen|noun
