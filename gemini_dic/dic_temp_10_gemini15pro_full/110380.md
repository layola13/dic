lignitic:/lɪɡˈnɪtɪk/| adj.|褐煤的；含褐煤的|Of or relating to lignite; containing lignite.  Resembling lignite in properties or appearance.|The power plant burns lignitic coal.  这家发电厂燃烧褐煤。|近义词：brown coal, earthy brown coal|反义词：anthracitic, bituminous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lignitique*, from *lignite* + *-ique*. Ultimately from Latin *lignum* (wood).
