misbalances:/ˌmɪsˈbælənsɪz/
noun
失衡，不平衡
lack of balance or symmetry; disproportion
The current misbalances in the global economy are causing concern. 全球经济目前的失衡状况令人担忧。

misbalance:/ˌmɪsˈbæləns/
noun
失衡，不平衡
lack of balance or symmetry; disproportion
The report highlighted the misbalance between investment in education and healthcare. 该报告强调了教育和医疗保健投资之间的不平衡。
近义词: imbalance, disparity, disproportion, inequality
反义词: balance, equilibrium, symmetry, parity
词性:n.


misbalance:/ˌmɪsˈbæləns/
verb
使失衡，使不平衡
to cause to be unbalanced or out of proportion
Rapid industrialization has misbalanced the country's economy.  快速的工业化使该国的经济失衡。
近义词: unbalance, upset, destabilize
反义词: balance, stabilize, equilibrate
词性:v.
