grainednesses:/ˈɡreɪndnɪsɪz/| n. |粒度，颗粒性|The degree to which something is composed of grains or particles; the texture or coarseness resulting from this.|The grainednesses of the different sands varied considerably. 不同沙子的粒度差异很大。|Synonyms: granularity, texture, coarseness|Antonyms: smoothness, fineness|noun
