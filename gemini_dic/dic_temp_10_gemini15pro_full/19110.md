betto:/ˈbɛtoʊ/
noun
马夫，赌马经纪人
a person who takes bets on horse races or manages racehorses for their owner
He worked as a betto at the racetrack. 他在赛马场当马夫。
synonyms: bookmaker, turf accountant
antonyms: gambler, punter
n.


Etymology->
From Japanese 賭場 (bakuba, “gambling place, racetrack”) + 夫 (tto, “husband, man”).
