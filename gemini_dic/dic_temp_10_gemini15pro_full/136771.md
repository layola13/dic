nonsequentially:/ˌnɒn sɪˈkwɛnʃəli/ /ˌnɑːn sɪˈkwɛnʃəli/| adv.|非顺序地，不连续地|Not in a sequential or logical order.|The topics were presented nonsequentially, jumping from one subject to another with no clear connection.  这些主题的呈现方式不连贯，从一个主题跳到另一个主题，之间没有明确的联系。|The chapters are arranged nonsequentially, allowing readers to choose their own path through the material. 章节排列是非顺序的，允许读者选择自己的阅读路径。|randomly, haphazardly, disconnectedly|sequentially, consecutively|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + sequentially (in a sequence).  Sequentially comes from sequence, which ultimately derives from the Latin word *sequī* (to follow).
