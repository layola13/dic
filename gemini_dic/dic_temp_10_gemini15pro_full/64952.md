esthesiometer:/ˌesθiːziˈɒmɪtər/ /ˌesθiːzioʊˈmɪtər/| n. | 触觉计，感觉计 | An instrument for measuring tactile sensitivity, especially the minimum distance at which two points of contact can be perceived as distinct. | The doctor used an esthesiometer to assess the patient's nerve damage. 医生用触觉计评估病人的神经损伤。

The researchers used an esthesiometer to map the sensitivity of different areas of the skin. 研究人员使用触觉计绘制皮肤不同区域的敏感度图。 | 近义词:esthesiometry,tactile stimulator| 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *aisthēsis* "sensation, perception" + *-meter* "measure."
