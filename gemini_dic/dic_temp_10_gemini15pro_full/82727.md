guidepost:/ˈɡaɪdpoʊst/ | n. |路标；指导原则；指标|A sign or other marker that shows the way or gives directions.  Something that serves as a guide or indicator for future action or planning.| The weathered guidepost pointed towards the hidden trail.  风化的路标指向隐藏的小径。| synonyms: signpost, marker, beacon, landmark, indicator | antonyms: misdirection, distraction | noun

guidepost:/ˈɡaɪdpoʊst/ | n. |路标；指导原则；指标|A principle or policy that serves as a guide for future action or planning.  Something that helps to direct or influence decisions.| The company's core values serve as guideposts for its employees.  公司的核心价值观是其员工的指导原则。| synonyms: principle, policy, guideline, rule, standard | antonyms: ambiguity, uncertainty | noun
