dittoed:/ˈdɪtoʊd/ | adj. | 同上的，同上，重复的 |  Marked with ditto marks ("); repeated or duplicated.  Derived from the Italian word "detto" meaning "said". It signifies that something is the same as what precedes it. | The figures for 2024 are dittoed from the 2023 projections. 2024年的数据与2023年的预测相同。 | synonyms: repeated, duplicated, copied, identical | antonyms: different, unique, distinct | adjective

dittoed:/ˈdɪtoʊd/ | v. | 重复，复制 |  To mark with ditto marks; to repeat or duplicate. |  He simply dittoed the previous entry in the ledger. 他只是重复了账本上之前的条目。 | synonyms: repeat, duplicate, copy, reproduce | antonyms:  vary, differentiate, alter | verb
