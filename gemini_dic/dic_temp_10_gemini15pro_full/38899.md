confervous:/kənˈfɜːrvəs/
adjective
紧张不安的，慌乱的
A blend of "confused" and "nervous," describing a state of being both disoriented and anxious.
She felt confervous before her big presentation, unsure of her material and worried about the audience's reaction. 她在大报告之前感到紧张不安，对自己的材料不确定，也担心观众的反应。
近义词: anxious, apprehensive, flustered
反义词: calm, composed, confident
词性: adjective
