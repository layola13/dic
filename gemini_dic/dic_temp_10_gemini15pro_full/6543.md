amylic:/əˈmɪlɪk/ | adj. | 戊基的，淀粉的 | Relating to or derived from amyl alcohol; pertaining to starch. | Amylic alcohol is a colorless liquid with a disagreeable odor. 戊醇是一种无色的液体，气味难闻。|  Amylic fermentation produces various byproducts. 淀粉发酵会产生各种副产品。| 近义词:pentyl | 反义词: N/A | 词性:形容词


词源: From amyl + -ic.  Amyl comes from Latin *amylum, from Greek ἄμυλον (amylon), meaning "starch," because amyl alcohol was originally obtained from starch.
