ciderish:/ˈsaɪdərɪʃ/| adj.|似苹果酒的；微酸的|Resembling cider, especially in taste or smell; slightly tart or acid.|The drink had a ciderish tang to it.  这饮料有一股苹果酒的味道。|近义词：tart, tangy, acidic|反义词:sweet, bland|词性:形容词
Etymology: From cider + -ish.  源自 cider + -ish.
