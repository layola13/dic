dictative:/ˈdɪkteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 专横的；命令式的；独裁的 | Inclined to dictate; overbearing; imperious.  Characterized by or expressing commands. |  "He had a dictative manner that made it hard to work with him." 他有一种专横的态度，让人很难与他共事。 | Synonyms: authoritarian, autocratic, bossy, domineering, imperious, peremptory | Antonyms: democratic, submissive, yielding | word type: adjective

dictative:/ˈdɪkteɪtɪv/ | n. |  命令；指令 (罕见用法) |  A decree or command (archaic usage).  | "The king issued a dictative regarding taxes." 国王发布了一项关于税收的指令。（古语用法，现代英语中很少使用） | Synonyms: decree, edict, mandate, order | Antonyms: request, plea, suggestion | word type: noun
