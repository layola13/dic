freeholder:/ˈfriːhoʊldər/| n.|自由保有地产者；完全保有地产者|A freeholder is someone who owns a freehold property, meaning they own the land and the building on it outright, not just for a limited time.|例句：The freeholder is responsible for maintaining the structure of the building. 自由保有地产者负责维护建筑物的结构。

例句：As a freeholder, she has complete control over the property. 作为完全保有地产者，她完全控制着该房产。|近义词：landowner, property owner|反义词:leaseholder, tenant|词性:noun

freehold:/ˈfriːhoʊld/| n. adj.|永久产权；自由保有地产|Freehold refers to the ownership of land and the building on it outright, not just for a limited time.  As an adjective, it describes property held in this way.|例句：They purchased the property freehold. 他们购买了该房产的永久产权。

例句：Freehold property is generally more valuable than leasehold property. 永久产权的房产通常比租赁产权的房产更有价值。|近义词：fee simple|反义词:leasehold|词性:noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *frēoheald, from frēo "free" + heald "holding, tenure."  The term reflects the historical distinction between those who held land freely (without feudal obligations) and those who held it under a lord.
