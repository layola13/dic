integrate:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪt/ /ˈɪntəˌɡreɪt/| v. |整合，集成；融入；使完整|To combine two or more things into a whole; to become part of a group or society.|例句：The company is integrating its new software with existing systems. 公司正在将其新软件与现有系统集成。|近义词：combine, merge, unify, incorporate, assimilate|反义词：segregate, separate, divide|词性:动词

integrate:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪt/ /ˈɪntəˌɡreɪt/| adj. |整合的，完整的| Formed or united into a whole.|例句：An integrated circuit. 集成电路|近义词：combined, unified, whole|反义词：segregated, separated|词性:形容词
