entrance's:/ˈɛntrənsɪz/
noun
入口（处）的；进入（权）的
Possessive form of "entrance," referring to something belonging to or associated with an entrance.
The entrance's archway was beautifully decorated. 入口处的拱门装饰精美。
The security guard stood by the entrance's gate.保安站在入口的大门旁。
Synonyms: doorway's, entryway's, gate's
Antonyms: exit's, departure's
