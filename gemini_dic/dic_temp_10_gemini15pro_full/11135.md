archegonial: /ˌɑːrkɪˈɡoʊniəl/
adjective
颈卵器的；产生颈卵器的
Relating to or resembling an archegonium, the female reproductive organ in mosses, ferns, and some other plants.
The archegonial neck is a canal leading to the egg. 颈卵器颈部是一条通往卵子的管道。
The archegonial venter contains the egg cell. 颈卵器腹部包含卵细胞。
近义词:  female reproductive (related to female reproduction)
反义词: antheridial (relating to the male reproductive organ)
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *ἀρχή (arkhḗ, “beginning”) + γόνος (gónos, “offspring”).
