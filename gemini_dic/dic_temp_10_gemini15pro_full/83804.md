hairsplitter:/ˈhɛərˌsplɪtər/| n.|吹毛求疵的人；钻牛角尖的人| someone who makes petty or trivial distinctions; someone who argues about small, unimportant details|例句：He's such a hairsplitter that it's impossible to have a productive discussion with him. 他真是个吹毛求疵的人，跟他根本无法进行有效的讨论。

例句：Don't be a hairsplitter; the difference between the two options is negligible. 不要钻牛角尖了；这两个选项之间的区别微不足道。|近义词：nitpicker, quibbler, pedant|反义词:easygoing person,  broad-minded person|词性:noun
