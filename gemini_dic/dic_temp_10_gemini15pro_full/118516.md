mesencephalon: /ˌmɛsɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/ /ˌmɪdˈbrɪn/ | n. | 中脑 | The mesencephalon, also known as the midbrain, is the portion of the brainstem that connects the hindbrain and forebrain. It plays important roles in motor movement, particularly movements of the eye, and in auditory and visual processing. | The mesencephalon is responsible for several important functions. | 近义词: midbrain | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *μέσος (mésos, “middle”) + *ἐγκέφαλος (enképhalos, “brain”).
