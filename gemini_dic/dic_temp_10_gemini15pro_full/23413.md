bracings:/ˈbreɪsɪŋz/
noun
支撑物，加固物
Supports or reinforcements that add strength or stability.
The old barn needed new bracings to withstand the strong winds.  旧谷仓需要新的支撑物来抵御强风。

The engineer designed bracings for the bridge. 工程师为桥梁设计了加固装置。

近义词: supports, reinforcements, struts
