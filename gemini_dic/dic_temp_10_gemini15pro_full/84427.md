hallucinates:/həˈluːsɪneɪts/| v. | 幻觉，产生幻觉 | To experience hallucinations; to see, hear, or feel things that are not really there. |  He hallucinates when he has a high fever. 他发高烧时会产生幻觉。

The patient began hallucinating after taking the new medication. 服用新药后，病人开始出现幻觉。 | 近义词:  imagine, fantasize, envision, delude | 反义词: perceive, realize | 词性: verb
