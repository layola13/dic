freshwater:/ˈfreʃwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈfreʃwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.|淡水的，淡水生物；淡水|Relating to, living in, or consisting of water that is not salty; water that is not salty.|The Amazon is a freshwater river. 亚马逊河是一条淡水河。| freshwater fish live in lakes and rivers. 淡水鱼生活在湖泊和河流中。|近义词：fresh, inland|反义词：saltwater, marine|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology:
Old English *ferscwater (attested only in compounds such as ferscwaterfisc “freshwater fish”), from fersc "fresh" + wæter "water".  The loss of -c- in the compound is regular.
