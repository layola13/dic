debars:/dɪˈbɑːrz/
verb
禁止，阻止，排除
To officially prevent someone from doing something, especially from entering a place or taking part in an activity.
The judge debarred him from holding public office. 法官禁止他担任公职。
He was debarred from the club for unacceptable behavior.  他因为行为不检点而被禁止进入俱乐部。
Synonyms:  prohibit, forbid, exclude, preclude, bar
Antonyms: permit, allow, admit, include
词性: 动词
