bicycle:/ˈbaɪsɪkəl/| n. |自行车（单数）|A two-wheeled vehicle powered by pedals.|I ride my bicycle to work every day. 我每天骑自行车上班。|近义词：bike|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bicyclette*, from *bi-* (two) + *cycle* (circle, wheel).  The word "cycle" ultimately comes from the Greek *kyklos* meaning "circle" or "wheel."
