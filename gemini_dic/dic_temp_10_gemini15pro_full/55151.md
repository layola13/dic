dissed:/dɪst/ | v. | 怠慢，轻视，侮辱 | To treat someone with disrespect or contempt; to insult. | He dissed me in front of all my friends. 他当着我所有朋友的面羞辱了我。|  She felt dissed when he didn't call her back.  当他没有回电话时，她感到被轻视了。| 近义词: insulted, snubbed, slighted, disrespected | 反义词: respected, honored, praised | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
Shortened form of disrespect, ultimately from dis- (expressing negation) + respect.
