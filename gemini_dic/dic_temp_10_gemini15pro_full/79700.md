gloze:/ɡlɒʊz/ (British) /ɡloʊz/ (American)| v. n.|掩饰，注释，解释；光泽，光滑的表面|To give a plausible but often specious explanation for; to explain away; to gloss over. Also, a comment or explanation, especially in a difficult or obscure passage of text.|He glozed over the company's financial difficulties. 他掩饰了公司的财务困难。|Synonyms: gloss over, explain away, whitewash, varnish, palliate|Antonyms: expose, reveal, uncover|verb

gloze:/ɡlɒʊz/ (British) /ɡloʊz/ (American)| n.|光泽，光滑的表面|A smooth, shiny surface.|The gloze of the polished wood was beautiful.  抛光木材的光泽很漂亮。|Synonyms: sheen, gloss, luster, polish|Antonyms: dullness, roughness|noun
