nonspontaneous:/ˌnɒnspɒnˈteɪniəs/ (US) /ˌnɑːnspɑːnˈteɪniəs/| adj.|非自发的，不自发的|Not occurring or able to occur without external intervention or stimulus; not spontaneous.|Rusting of iron is a spontaneous process, but the conversion of iron ore to iron metal is nonspontaneous.|近义词：forced, induced, unnatural|反义词：spontaneous, natural|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + spontaneous.  "Spontaneous" comes from Late Latin *spontaneus, from Latin sponte "of one's own accord, voluntarily."
