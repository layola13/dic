manifesto:/ˌmænɪˈfɛstoʊ/ (US) /ˌmænɪˈfɛstəʊ/ (UK)| n. |宣言，声明|a public declaration of policy and aims, especially one issued before an election by a political party or candidate|The party released its election manifesto. 该党发布了其选举宣言。|近义词：declaration, platform, proclamation|反义词:denial, refutation|noun
