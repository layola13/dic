bisecting:/baɪˈsɛktɪŋ/
adjective|二等分的，平分的|dividing something into two equal parts|The line bisecting the angle creates two congruent angles. 这条线将角平分，形成了两个全等角。|
近义词: dividing, halving|反义词: joining, uniting|词性: adjective
bisect:/baɪˈsɛkt/
verb|平分，二等分|to divide something into two equal parts|The road bisects the town. 这条路把小镇一分为二。|
近义词: divide, halve |反义词: join, unite|词性: verb
