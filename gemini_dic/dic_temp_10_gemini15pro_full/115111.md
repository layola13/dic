manger:/ˈmeɪndʒər/ | n. | 马槽；饲料槽 | A long open box or trough in a stable or barn, from which horses or cattle eat. | The horses were feeding at the manger. 马儿们正在马槽里吃东西。| Near synonyms: crib, trough, feedbox | Antonyms: None | Noun


manger:/ˈmɑːnʒeɪ/ (chiefly British) /ˈmeɪndʒər/ (US) | n. | (尤指宗教场景中的) 马槽 |  Specifically, a trough or open box in which food is laid for horses or cattle; in the Christian tradition, associated with the birth of Jesus. |  She placed the baby Jesus in the manger. 她把婴儿耶稣放在马槽里。 | Near synonyms: crib, trough, feedbox | Antonyms: None | Noun
