explication:/ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 解释；说明；阐述 | a detailed explanation of something, such as a piece of writing or a work of art | The professor's explication of the poem helped the students understand its meaning. 教授对这首诗的讲解帮助学生理解了它的含义。 | explanation, interpretation, elucidation | obscurity, confusion | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin explicatio, from Latin explicare "to unfold, explain," from ex- "out" + plicare "to fold."
