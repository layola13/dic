cerberean:/sərˈbɪəriən/| adj.|地狱三头犬的；凶猛的；警觉的|Relating to Cerberus, the three-headed dog of Greek mythology that guarded the entrance to the underworld; hence, vigilant, watchful, or fiercely guarding.|The cerberean security system made it nearly impossible to break into the vault.|近义词：watchful, vigilant, alert, guarded, fierce|反义词：negligent, careless, inattentive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *Cerbereus*, from Greek *Kérberos*.
