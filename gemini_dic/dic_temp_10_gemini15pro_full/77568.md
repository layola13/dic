gearheads:/ˈɡɪərˌhɛdz/| n.|汽车迷；机械爱好者|A person who is enthusiastic about cars, motorcycles, or other machinery, especially one who enjoys working on them.|My brother is a real gearhead; he spends all his free time tinkering with his car. 我弟弟是个十足的汽车迷，他所有的空闲时间都用来摆弄他的车。|Synonyms: motorhead, car enthusiast, mechanic, tinkerer|Antonyms: technophobe|noun
