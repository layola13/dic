cadence:/ˈkeɪdns/| n.|节奏，韵律，抑扬顿挫|A sequence of notes or chords comprising the close of a musical phrase.|The speaker delivered his speech with a slow, deliberate cadence. 演讲者以缓慢而从容的节奏发表了演讲。|synonyms: rhythm, tempo, beat, measure, flow|antonyms: arrhythmia, discord, dissonance|noun
