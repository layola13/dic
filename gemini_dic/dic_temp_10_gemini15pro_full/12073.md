artificious:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəs/| adj.|做作的，不自然的，矫揉造作的|Made or contrived in a way that seems artificial and unnatural; lacking naturalness or spontaneity. |Her artificious laughter grated on my nerves. 她那做作的笑声让我神经紧张。|Synonyms: affected, contrived, feigned, pretended, artificial, unnatural, stilted, mannered,  ostentatious|Antonyms: natural, genuine, sincere, unaffected, artless, simple|adjective

artificious:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəs/| adj.|狡猾的，诡计多端的|(less common)  Characterized by craftiness or trickery. |He used artificious arguments to convince the jury. 他用狡猾的论证来说服陪审团。|Synonyms: cunning, crafty, wily, deceitful, devious|Antonyms: honest, straightforward, ingenuous|adjective
