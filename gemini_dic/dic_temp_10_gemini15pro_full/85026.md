harassment:/ˈhærəsmənt/ /həˈræsmənt/| n.|骚扰，烦扰|The act of repeatedly bothering or attacking someone.|He faced harassment from his coworkers after reporting the incident.  举报事件后，他面临来自同事的骚扰。|
近义词：bullying, badgering, pestering|
反义词：protection, respect|
词性: noun


词源: Late Middle English (as a legal term): from Anglo-Norman French harassement, based on harasser ‘tire out, devastate’, of unknown origin.
