operameter:/ˌɒpəˈræmɪtər/ | n. | 运转时间计，运行时间记录器 | An instrument that records the total operating time of a machine or device.  It measures and displays the cumulative running time of equipment. | The operameter showed that the engine had been running for over 1000 hours.  运转时间计显示发动机已经运行了1000多个小时。| Synonyms:  running time meter, hour meter, elapsed time meter | Antonyms: N/A | noun
