gravitater:/ˈɡrævɪˌteɪtər/
n.|趋向者，被吸引者|Someone or something that gravitates towards something else; a person or thing that is attracted to something.|Moths are gravitaters to light. 飞蛾趋光。|
Synonym: attractee, converger
Antonym: repeller, diverger
词性: noun
