overoffensively: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɛnsɪvli/ | adv. | 过于冒犯地，过分攻击性地 |  In a manner that is excessively or unnecessarily offensive.  It implies going beyond what is considered acceptable or appropriate in causing offense. |  He behaved overoffensively, making personal attacks and insulting everyone present.  他表现得过于冒犯，进行人身攻击并侮辱了在场的所有人。 |  aggressively, offensively, antagonistically, belligerently |  diplomatically, tactfully, politely, respectfully | adverb
