citronlike:/ˈsɪtrənˌlaɪk/
adj.
类似香橼的,柠檬色的
Resembling a citron in appearance, taste, or smell.
The cake had a delicate, citronlike fragrance. 这蛋糕有一种淡淡的类似香橼的香味。
近义词:citrusy, lemonlike, lemony
反义词:bland
