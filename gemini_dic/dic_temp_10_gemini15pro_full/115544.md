mappable:/ˈmæpəbl/| adj.|可绘制的，可映射的|Something that is mappable can be represented on a map.  It can refer to physical areas or abstract concepts that can be visually organized.|The terrain is easily mappable due to its clear landmarks.  这片地形由于地标清晰，很容易绘制地图。|Synonyms: chartable, plotable, representable|Antonyms: unmappable, unchartable|Adjective
