hills:/hɪlz/| n.|小山丘，丘陵地带|A naturally raised area of land, smaller than a mountain.|We hiked over the rolling hills. 我们徒步翻越了绵延起伏的丘陵。|近义词：knolls, mounds, slopes|反义词:valleys, plains|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hyll (variant of *heall), from Proto-Germanic *hulniz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (to rise, to be prominent).  Cognate with Dutch heuvel, German Hügel, Swedish höjd.
