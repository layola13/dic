incisor:/ɪnˈsaɪzər/| n. |门牙，切牙|A tooth at the front of the mouth, adapted for cutting.|例句：Rabbits use their incisors to gnaw on carrots. 兔子用门牙啃胡萝卜。

例句：The dentist examined the patient's incisors. 牙医检查了病人的门牙。|近义词：front tooth, cutting tooth|反义词:molar|词性:noun
