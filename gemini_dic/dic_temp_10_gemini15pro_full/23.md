a little bird:/ə ˈlɪtl bɜːrd/ | idiom | 小道消息；秘密来源 | A source of secret information; often used to avoid revealing the source. |  A little bird told me you're getting a promotion.  有人私下告诉我你要升职了。

A little bird told me it's your birthday today. 今天有小道消息告诉我今天是你生日。 | 近义词: rumor, grapevine, inside information | 反义词: official announcement, public knowledge | 词性: idiom

little:/ˈlɪtl/ | adj. | 小的；少量的；幼小的 | Small in size, amount, or degree; young. | The little boy played with his toy car. 小男孩玩他的玩具汽车。

She has very little patience. 她没什么耐心。 | 近义词: small, tiny, miniature, slight, young | 反义词: big, large, huge, enormous, much, many, old, elder | 词性: adjective

bird:/bɜːrd/ | n. | 鸟 | A feathered, winged, egg-laying animal. | The bird sang a beautiful song. 鸟儿唱了一首优美的歌。

I saw a flock of birds flying overhead. 我看到一群鸟从头顶飞过。 | 近义词: fowl | 反义词: (no direct opposite) | 词性: noun
