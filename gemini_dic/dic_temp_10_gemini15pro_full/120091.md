mime:/maɪm/ | n. v. | 模仿；哑剧；哑剧演员 | A performer who portrays characters and situations by mime, i.e., by facial expressions and gestures rather than words.  |  例句：The mime entertained the children with his silent performance.  哑剧演员用他的无声表演娱乐了孩子们。 | 近义词：impersonator, mimic | 反义词：speaker | 词性: noun, verb

mime:/maɪm/ | v. | 模仿；演哑剧 | To use mime, i.e., represent or act out (a situation, feeling, etc.) by facial expressions and gestures rather than words. | 例句：He mimed eating a banana. 他模仿吃香蕉的动作。 | 近义词：imitate, mimic | 反义词：speak | 词性: verb
