bidirectional:/ˌbaɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəl/| adj.|双向的，双向性的|Operating or moving in two directions. |The new highway is bidirectional, allowing traffic to flow smoothly in both directions. 这条新高速公路是双向的，使交通可以双向顺畅流动。|例句：This software supports bidirectional communication between devices.  这个软件支持设备之间的双向通信。|近义词：two-way, reciprocal, mutual|反义词：unidirectional, one-way|词性:形容词
