outspirit:/ˌaʊtˈspɪrɪt/
verb
胜过；在精神上压倒
To surpass in spirit or courage; to demoralize or dishearten.
He outspirited his opponents with his unwavering optimism. 他以坚定不移的乐观精神战胜了对手。
The team was outspirited after their crushing defeat.  球队在惨败后士气低落。
近义词:demoralize, dishearten, subdue, conquer, vanquish
反义词:encourage, hearten, inspire
词性:v.
