australasia:/ˌɔːstrəˈleɪʒə/ /ˌɑːstrəˈleɪʒə/| n. |澳大拉西亚|A geographical region comprising Australia, New Zealand, and adjacent islands.|例句：Australasia is known for its unique wildlife. 澳大拉西亚以其独特的野生动物而闻名。

例句：Many species of birds are endemic to Australasia. 许多鸟类是澳大拉西亚特有的。|近义词：Oceania|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin australis (“southern”) + Asia.  Coined in 1756 by Charles de Brosses in his Histoire des navigations aux terres australes.
