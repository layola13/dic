nonendorsement:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/
n.
不认可，不背书
lack of endorsement; absence of support or approval.
The candidate's nonendorsement by the union was a significant setback.  该候选人未获得工会的支持，这是一个重大的挫折。
The company issued a statement of nonendorsement regarding the controversial product.  该公司就这款备受争议的产品发表了不予认可的声明。
近义词:disapproval, rejection, opposition
反义词:endorsement, approval, support
词性:名词

nonendorsement:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "endorsement" (from endorse, meaning "to give support or approval to").  "Endorse" comes from Old French "endoser" meaning "to write on the back of".
用法说明: Nonendorsement signifies the absence of explicit support or approval. It can imply neutrality or even tacit disapproval.  Often used in formal contexts, especially in legal, political, or business settings.
