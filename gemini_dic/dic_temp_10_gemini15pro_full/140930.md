ondoyant:/ˈɒndɔɪənt; ˈɑːndwɑːjɑːnt/
adjective
波动的，起伏的; 闪光的，闪烁的
Having a wavy motion or appearance; shimmering.
The ondoyant surface of the lake reflected the moonlight beautifully. 波光粼粼的湖面优美地反射着月光。
Synonyms: undulating, shimmering, rippling, waving, flickering
Antonyms: still, motionless, steady, fixed, constant
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ondoyant*, present participle of *ondoyer* "to wave, undulate," from *onde* "wave," from Latin *unda* "wave."
