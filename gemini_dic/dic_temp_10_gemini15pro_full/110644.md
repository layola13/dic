limuloidea:/lɪˌmjʊlɔɪˈdiːə/ | n. |鲎科|A taxonomic superfamily of marine arthropods, commonly known as horseshoe crabs.  They are not true crabs, but are more closely related to spiders and scorpions.|Horseshoe crabs, members of the Limuloidea superfamily, have been around for millions of years.|Synonym: Xiphosura, Xiphosurida|Antonym: N/A|noun
