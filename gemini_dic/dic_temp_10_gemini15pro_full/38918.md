confessionally:/kənˈfɛʃənəli/
| adv. |忏悔地，坦白地|In a way that relates to confession or involves confessing something.  Relating to a religious confession or system of confessions.|He spoke confessionally about his past mistakes. 他忏悔地讲述了他过去犯下的错误。|Synonym: frankly, openly, candidly|Antonym: secretively, evasively|Adverb
