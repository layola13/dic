graptolites: /ˈɡræptəˌlaɪts/ | n. | 笔石 | Extinct marine colonial animals of the Paleozoic era, forming branching, fan-shaped or other colonies of tiny cups.  Their fossils are important index fossils for dating rocks. | Graptolites are useful for dating rocks from the Ordovician and Silurian periods. 笔石对于测定奥陶纪和志留纪的岩石年代非常有用。| synonyms: none (as they are a unique group) | antonyms: none | noun

graptolite: /ˈɡræptəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 笔石 | A single organism or zooid within a graptolite colony.  | This graptolite fossil is remarkably well-preserved.  这块笔石化石保存得非常好。 | synonyms: none | antonyms: none | noun
