businesswoman: /ˈbɪznəsˌwʊmən/ (US), /ˈbɪznɪsˌwʊmən/ (UK)
noun
女商人；商界女性
A woman who works in business, especially one who has a high position.
She's a very successful businesswoman. 她是一位非常成功的商界女性。
The businesswoman negotiated a lucrative deal.  这位女商人谈成了一笔利润丰厚的交易。

近义词:  executive, entrepreneur, professional
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym, could consider words like homemaker, unemployed, depending on context)
词性: noun
