medusoid:/məˈdjuːsɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 水母状的，水母体的；水母体 | Resembling a medusa; having the form or characteristics of a medusa. |  例句：The medusoid stage of the jellyfish is free-swimming. 水母的水母体阶段是自由游动的。

例句：The creature had a medusoid appearance. 这个生物有着水母般的外形。 | 近义词：jellyfish-like, medusa-like | 反义词: polyp-like | 词性: 形容词，名词


medusa:/məˈdjuːzə/ | n. | 水母 | The free-swimming form of a coelenterate, such as a jellyfish, typically shaped like an umbrella or bell and having tentacles around the edge. | 例句：The medusa drifted gracefully through the water. 水母在水中优雅地漂流。

例句：The sting of a medusa can be painful. 水母的刺痛会很疼。| 近义词：jellyfish | 反义词: polyp | 词性: 名词
