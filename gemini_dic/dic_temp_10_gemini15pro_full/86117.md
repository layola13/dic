heartburn:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrn/ (US) /ˈhɑːtbɜːn/ (UK)| n. |妒忌，嫉妒|A feeling of envy or resentment.|He felt a pang of heartburn when he saw his rival's success.  看到对手的成功，他感到一阵妒忌。|Synonyms: jealousy, envy, resentment|Antonyms: admiration, goodwill|noun
