glucinic:/ɡluːˈsɪnɪk/| adj.|葡萄糖酸的；铍的|Relating to or derived from glucose or glucinum (an old name for beryllium).|Glucinic acid is a crystalline acid obtained from glucose. 葡萄糖酸是一种从葡萄糖中提取的结晶酸。|The glucinic deposits were found near the beryllium mine. 铍矿附近发现了葡萄糖酸盐沉积物。|Synonyms: beryllate (for beryllium-related meaning), glucosate|Antonyms: None|adjective
