advertisable: /ˌædvərˈtaɪzəbl/
adjective
可宣传的；适于做广告的
Suitable for being advertised.
This new product is highly advertisable due to its unique features.  这款新产品由于其独特的特点非常适合做广告。

The company's eco-friendly image is very advertisable.  这家公司环保的形象很适合宣传。

synonym
promotable, marketable, publicizable
antonym
unmarketable, unpromotable
词性
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -able.  "Advertise" comes from the Latin "advertere" meaning "to turn towards".  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.

USAGE->
This product is easily advertisable to a young audience.  这个产品很容易向年轻受众宣传。

The charity's work is highly advertisable and attracts many donations.  这个慈善机构的工作非常适合宣传，吸引了很多捐款。

PHRASE->
highly advertisable = 非常适合做广告的
easily advertisable = 容易做广告的
readily advertisable = 容易宣传的
not easily advertisable = 不容易做广告的
widely advertisable = 可以广泛宣传的
