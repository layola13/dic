bichromatize:/baɪˈkroʊmətaɪz/
verb
使成双色，用两种颜色染色
to color something with two colors.
The artist bichromatized the painting, using only shades of blue and green.  艺术家用蓝色和绿色的阴影使这幅画成为双色。
The fabric was bichromatized with red and yellow dyes.  这块布料用红色和黄色的染料染成了双色。
近义词: two-tone, two-color
反义词: multicolor, polychromatic
词性: verb
词源: bi- (two) + chroma (color) + -ize (to make)
