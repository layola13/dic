millennialism:/mɪˈlɛniəlɪzəm/| n. |千禧年主义，千年至福说|The belief that Christ will reign on earth for a thousand years, usually preceded by a period of great tribulation.|Many cults embrace millennialism as a central tenet of their faith. 许多邪教将千禧年主义奉为其信仰的核心原则。|The preacher's sermons often focused on themes of millennialism. 牧师的布道经常集中在千禧年主义的主题上。|近义词：chiliasm|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *millenarismus, from Latin mille (thousand) + -enarius (containing a thousand) + -ismus.
