emphaticness:/ɪmˈfætɪknəs/| n.|强调，重点，坚决|The emphaticness of his denial convinced the jury of his innocence. 他坚决的否认使陪审团相信他是无辜的。|近义词：emphasis, forcefulness, insistence, vehemence|反义词：hesitancy, indecision, vagueness, weakness|noun

Etymology:
From emphatic + -ness.  Emphatic comes from the Late Latin *emphaticus, from the Greek emphatikos "expressive," from emphainein "to exhibit, display," from en- "in" + phainein "to show."
