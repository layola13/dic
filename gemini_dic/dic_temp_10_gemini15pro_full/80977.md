graciousness:/ˈɡreɪʃəsnəs/| n.|亲切；和蔼；殷勤；优雅|The quality of being kind, polite, and generous, especially in a way that is intended to make other people feel welcome and comfortable.  A quality of being pleasantly polite, courteous, or considerate.|She accepted his apology with graciousness. 她宽厚地接受了他的道歉。|Synonyms: courtesy, politeness, kindness, generosity, charm, affability, cordiality, amiability, good grace|Antonyms: rudeness, unkindness, hostility, impoliteness|noun
