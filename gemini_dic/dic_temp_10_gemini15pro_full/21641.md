blurs:/blɜːrz/ /blɜːrz/
| n. | 模糊；模糊的东西 | something that is not clearly seen or defined; a blurred effect or image | The photograph was a blur of colors. | 近义词: smudges, fuzziness | 反义词: clarity, sharpness | noun
blurs:/blɜːrz/ /blɜːrz/
| v. | (使)模糊；(使)看不清 | to make or become unclear or less distinct | The rain blurred the view from the window.  | 近义词: obscure, cloud | 反义词: sharpen, clarify | verb
