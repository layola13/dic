paludine:/pælˈuːdaɪn/ /ˈpæljudɪn/ | adj. |沼泽的；生长在沼泽中的；沼泽生物的 | Relating to, inhabiting, or thriving in marshes or swamps. |  The paludine flora and fauna are unique. 沼泽的动植物群落是独一无二的。

The paludine environment is home to many amphibians. 沼泽环境是许多两栖动物的家园。

|近义词：marshy, swampy, boggy|反义词：xeric, arid, dry|词性：形容词


Etymology: From Latin *palūdīnus, from palūs (“marsh, swamp”).
