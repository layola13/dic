overfilters:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪltərz/ /ˈəʊvəˌfɪltəz/ | v. (Third-person singular simple present overfilters, present participle overfiltering, simple past and past participle overfiltered) | 过度过滤 | To filter excessively, removing desirable elements along with undesirable ones. | The photographer overfiltered the image, making it appear washed out. 摄影师过度过滤了图像，使其看起来褪色。| 近义词: overprocess, overdo | 反义词: underfilter | 词性: 动词


overfilters:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪltərz/ /ˈəʊvəˌfɪltəz/ | n. (plural overfilters) | 过度过滤器(指过度过滤的工具或装置) | A filter that filters excessively.  This is less common than the verb form. |  He used an overfilters that bleached out all the color. 他使用了一个过度过滤的滤镜，漂白了所有的颜色。 | 近义词: excessive filter | 反义词: weak filter | 词性: 名词
