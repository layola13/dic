coelodont:/ˈsiːləˌdɒnt/| adj. n.|腔齿类动物的，腔齿类动物|Having hollow teeth; a mammal having hollow teeth, especially a member of an extinct group of herbivorous ungulates.|The coelodont fossils were discovered in the Eocene strata.  腔齿类动物化石是在始新世地层中发现的。|Coelodonts were a diverse group of herbivores. 腔齿类动物是多种多样的食草动物。|近义词:ungulate|反义词:|adjective, noun
