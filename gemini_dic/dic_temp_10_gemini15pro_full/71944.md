flied:/flaɪd/
| v. |飞，飞行（fly的过去式和过去分词）|The bird flied high in the sky. 鸟儿在空中高高飞翔。|
| 英文解析:Past tense and past participle of the verb "fly".  Indicates the action of moving through the air using wings, or traveling by aircraft. |
| 例句：He flied to London last week. 他上周飞往伦敦。|
| 例句：She has flied across the Atlantic many times. 她多次飞越大西洋。|
| 近义词：soared, winged, flew (simple past)|
| 反义词： landed, descended |
| 词性：动词 |

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Birds fly south for the winter.  鸟儿冬天飞往南方。
The airplane flies at a high altitude.  飞机在高空飞行。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The bird is flying towards the tree. 鸟正朝着树飞去。
The plane is flying over the ocean. 飞机正在飞越海洋。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The birds have flown south for the winter. 鸟已经飞往南方过冬了。
She has flown to Paris several times. 她已经飞往巴黎好几次了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The birds have been flying all day. 鸟儿们整天都在飞。
They have been flying for hours. 他们已经飞了好几个小时了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The bird flied over the house. 鸟儿飞过了房子。
They flied to New York yesterday. 他们昨天飞往纽约。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The birds were flying south when the storm hit.  暴风雨来袭时，鸟儿们正往南飞。
He was flying a kite when I saw him. 我看到他时，他正在放风筝。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The birds had flown south before the first snow. 初雪之前，鸟儿们已经飞往南方了。
She had flown to London before she went to Paris. 在去巴黎之前，她已经飞往伦敦了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The birds had been flying for hours before they landed. 鸟儿们飞了几个小时才着陆。
They had been flying all day before they reached their destination. 他们在到达目的地之前已经飞了一整天。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The birds will fly south next month. 鸟儿们下个月将飞往南方。
They will fly to Hawaii for their vacation. 他们将飞往夏威夷度假。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time tomorrow, the birds will be flying over the mountains. 明天这个时候，鸟儿们将在飞越群山。
Next week, they will be flying to Europe. 下周，他们将飞往欧洲。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, the birds will have flown thousands of miles. 到下周，鸟儿们将已经飞行了数千英里。
By the end of the year, she will have flown around the world. 到年底，她将已经环游世界了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, the birds will have been flying south for the winter for centuries. 到明年，鸟类将已经飞往南方过冬数个世纪了。（此处用fly的进行时态不太自然，因为迁徙是一个持续的习惯，而不是一个具体的持续动作。）
By the time she graduates, she will have been studying biology for four years. 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习生物学四年了。（此例句更符合进行时态的用法）

虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would fly around the world. 如果我有翅膀，我会飞遍全世界。
I wish I could fly like a bird. 我希望我可以像鸟一样飞翔。


被动语态:
The plane was flown by an experienced pilot. 这架飞机由一位经验丰富的飞行员驾驶。
The kite was being flown high in the sky. 风筝在空中高高飘扬。


疑问句:

否定句:
