discography:/dɪˈskɒɡrəfi/ /dɪˈskɑːɡrəfi/
noun
唱片目录；全部唱片
A list of all the recordings made by a particular musician or singer.
The band's discography includes ten studio albums and numerous singles.  这支乐队的唱片目录包括十张录音室专辑和许多单曲。
synonyms: catalog, list, record
antonyms: N/A
n.
