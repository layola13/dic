overcorrupted:/ˌoʊvərˈkərʌptɪd/ | adj. |过度腐败的，极度腐败的| Excessively corrupted; corrupted to an extreme degree.  This implies a level of corruption beyond what is typically considered bad. It suggests a complete and thorough perversion of integrity. | The overcorrupted system was beyond repair. 这个过度腐败的系统已经无法修复了。| The overcorrupted officials were finally brought to justice. 这些极度腐败的官员最终被绳之以法。 | 近义词: extremely corrupt, thoroughly corrupt, deeply corrupt | 反义词: honest, incorruptible, uncorrupted | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively," "too much") + "corrupt" (from Latin *corruptus, past participle of corrumpere "to destroy, spoil, bribe").
