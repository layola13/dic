parsimonious:/ˌpɑːrsɪˈməʊniəs/ | adj. |吝啬的，节俭的|Unwilling to spend money or use resources; stingy or frugal.|例句：She is parsimonious in her spending habits. 她花钱很节俭。|近义词：stingy, frugal, thrifty, miserly|反义词：generous, extravagant, lavish|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *parsimonia* "frugality," from *parcere* "to spare."
