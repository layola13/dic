incensor:/ɪnˈsensər/ /ɪnˈsɛnsər/| vt.|煽动，激怒|to stir up, provoke or inflame (violent feelings)|The politician's speech was intended to incensor the crowd.  这位政治家的演讲旨在煽动群众。|The media incensed public opinion against the government. 媒体激起了公众对政府的反对情绪。|近义词：inflame, provoke, agitate, incite, instigate|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify, appease|词性：动词

词源:
From Late Latin incensare "to set on fire," from in- "in" + censare "to assess, judge, punish."  The original sense was "to kindle, light," but the metaphorical sense of "to inflame with anger" emerged later.
