megaparsecs:/ˈmeɡəˌpɑːrˌseks/ | n. | 百万秒差距 | A unit of distance equal to one million parsecs (about 3.26 million light-years). It is used to measure very large distances, such as those between galaxies. | The Andromeda Galaxy is about 0.78 megaparsecs from Earth. 仙女座星系距离地球约 0.78 百万秒差距。

The most distant known galaxy is several megaparsecs away. 已知最遥远的星系距离我们有数百万秒差距。| 近义词: None (specific unit) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mega- + parsec. "Mega-" is a combining form meaning "one million," derived from Greek megas "great." "Parsec" is a unit of astronomical distance based on parallax and equal to about 3.26 light-years.
