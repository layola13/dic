animals:/ˈænɪməlz/ | n. | 动物 | Living creatures that are not plants, typically able to move independently and feed on organic matter. |例句：The zoo has many different kinds of animals. 动物园里有许多不同种类的动物。|例句：We must protect endangered animals. 我们必须保护濒危动物。| 近义词：creatures, beasts, fauna | 反义词：plants | noun

Etymology: From Old French *animal (modern French animal), from Latin animal, neuter of animalis (“having breath, living, animate”), from anima (“breath, soul”).
