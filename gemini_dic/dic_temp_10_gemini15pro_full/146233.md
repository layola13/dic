overexposing:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊzɪŋ/ |v.|过度曝光|Exposing something, especially photographic film, to too much light.|He overexposed the film and the pictures were too bright. 他过度曝光了胶卷，照片太亮了。|Synonyms: overexpose|Antonyms: underexpose|verb

overexposed:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊzd/ |adj.|过度曝光的|Having been exposed to too much light, especially of photographic film.|The overexposed photos were washed out and pale. 这些过度曝光的照片褪色且苍白。|Synonyms:  oversaturated, bleached (in the context of photos)|Antonyms: underexposed|adjective

overexposure:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊʒər/ |n.|过度曝光|The act of overexposing something, or the state of being overexposed.|The overexposure ruined the photograph. 过度曝光毁了这张照片。|Synonyms:  oversaturation|Antonyms: underexposure|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "expose" (from Latin "exponere," meaning "to put out, expose").
