nonpalpable:/nɒnˈpæl.pə.bəl/ (UK) /nɑːnˈpæl.pə.bəl/ (US)| adj.|难以察觉的，无法触知的，无形的|Not able to be felt by touching; intangible.|The tension in the room was almost nonpalpable. 房间里的紧张气氛几乎难以察觉。|The tumor was so small as to be nonpalpable.  这个肿瘤太小了，以至于无法触及。|近义词：imperceptible, intangible, insubstantial|反义词：palpable, tangible, perceptible|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + palpable, from Late Latin palpabilis, from Latin palpare "to touch gently, stroke."
