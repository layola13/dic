bro's:/broʊz/| n.|兄弟们；伙计们|Informal, plural form of "bro," short for "brother." Used to address a group of male friends or acquaintances. |例句：Hey bro's, what are you up to tonight? 嘿，兄弟们，今晚你们打算做什么？

例句：I'm going to the game with my bro's. 我要和我的兄弟们去看比赛。|近义词：guys, dudes, pals, buddies|反义词: enemies, foes|词性:名词
