bedecks:/bɪˈdɛks/
verb
装饰，点缀
to decorate or adorn something, typically with numerous small or colorful items.
The hall was bedecked with flowers and ribbons for the wedding.  大厅为了婚礼用鲜花和彩带装饰了起来。
The Christmas tree was bedecked with twinkling lights and ornaments. 圣诞树上装饰着闪烁的灯光和饰品。

近义词:adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, garnish
反义词:strip, denude, bare
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bedeken*, from be- + decken (“to cover”).  Cognate with Dutch bedekken (“to cover”), German bedecken (“to cover”), Swedish betäcka (“to cover”).
