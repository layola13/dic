hibernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃn/
n.
冬眠
A state of inactivity and metabolic depression in endotherms.
The bear went into hibernation for the winter. 熊在冬天进入了冬眠。

The chipmunk is in hibernation. 花栗鼠在冬眠。


近义词:dormancy, sleep, torpor
反义词:activity, wakefulness
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Latin *hibernationem, from hibernō (“I winter”), from hibernus (“of winter”), from hiems (“winter”).
