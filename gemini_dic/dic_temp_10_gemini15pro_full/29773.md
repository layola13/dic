cattiness:/ˈkætɪnɪs/| n. |刻薄；恶毒；刁钻|The cattiness of her remarks surprised everyone.  她的言辞刻薄，让每个人都感到惊讶。|例句：Her cattiness made her unpopular. 她的刻薄让她不受欢迎。|近义词：spitefulness, malice, bitchiness|反义词：kindness, generosity|词性：名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From catty (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Catty itself comes from cat (referring to a female cat) and is associated with the perceived spiteful or malicious nature of female cats.  最终来源于cat（指雌猫）并与人们认为的雌猫记仇或恶意的天性有关。
