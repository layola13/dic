institution:/ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən/ |n.| 机构，制度，习俗|An established organization or system, especially one dedicated to education, public service, or culture.  A custom or practice that has become established and accepted.|例句：The university is a respected institution of higher learning. 这所大学是一所受人尊敬的高等教育机构。

例句：Marriage is an important social institution. 婚姻是一项重要的社会制度。|近义词：organization, establishment, foundation, custom, practice|反义词：disorder, chaos, informality


institution:/ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃən/ |n.| 建立，设立|The act of establishing something.|例句：The institution of new safety regulations has improved working conditions. 新安全规定的建立改善了工作条件。

例句：The institution of a national holiday commemorated the historic event.  设立一个全国性的节日来纪念这一历史事件。|近义词：establishment, foundation, creation|反义词：abolition, termination
