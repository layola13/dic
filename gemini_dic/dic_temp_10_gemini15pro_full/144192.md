outsidernesses:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdərˌnəsɪz/| n. |局外人身份，局外状态（复数）|The state or quality of being an outsider, especially in multiple contexts or to varying degrees.|Her outsidernesses in both the academic and social spheres made it difficult for her to connect with others. 她既是学术界的局外人，也是社交圈的局外人，这让她很难与他人建立联系。|近义词：alienation, estrangement, otherness|反义词：belonging, inclusion, insiderness|词性:名词

词源：From outsider + -ness (suffix indicating a state, quality, or condition).  "Outsider" itself comes from "outside" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons or things associated with a particular place or activity).
