fertilization:/ˌfɜːrtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌfɝːtɪləˈzeɪʃn/
n. |受精，施肥|The process of combining the male and female gametes to form a zygote, or the process of adding fertilizer to soil to enrich it.|例句：The fertilization of the egg by the sperm is the beginning of new life. 精子和卵子的结合是新生命的开始。|近义词：conception, impregnation, enriching, manuring|反义词：sterility, barrenness|词性：名词

fertilize:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/ /ˈfɝːtɪləˌaɪz/
vt. |使受精，施肥|To make fertile; to cause fertilization to occur, or to add fertilizer to soil.|例句：Farmers fertilize their fields to improve crop yields. 农民给田地施肥以提高作物产量。|近义词：impregnate, enrich, manure|反义词：sterilize|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fertilisatio, from fertilis (fertile) + -atio (suffix forming nouns of action).  The root "fertilis" comes from "ferre" (to bear, carry, produce).
