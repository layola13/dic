countermatch:/ˈkaʊntərˌmætʃ/ | n. | 对抗赛，对等比赛 | A contest or game played between two teams or individuals that have previously competed against each other. |  The countermatch was scheduled for next week after the first game ended in a tie.  对抗赛安排在第一场比赛打平后的下周举行。| synonym: rematch, return match | antonym: first match | noun

countermatch:/ˈkaʊntərˌmætʃ/ | v. | 安排对抗赛，与...进行对抗赛 | To arrange or participate in a countermatch. | They decided to countermatch against their rivals after the initial defeat. 在最初的失败后，他们决定与对手进行对抗赛。| synonym: rematch, replay | antonym: forfeit | verb
