anhydride:/ænˈhaɪdraɪd/ | n. |酸酐 |A non-metallic oxide that reacts with water to form an acidic solution; any compound (especially an organic compound) that can be regarded as having been formed by removing water from another compound.| Acetic anhydride is a common industrial chemical. | 醋酸酐是一种常见的工业化学品。|  synonym: acid anhydride | antonym:  hydrate | noun
