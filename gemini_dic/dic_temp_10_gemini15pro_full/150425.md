panspermatist:/pænˈspɜːrmətɪst/ | n. | 泛种论者 | A believer in the theory of panspermia, which holds that life exists throughout the universe and is distributed by space dust, meteoroids, asteroids, comets, etc.|  Many scientists dismiss the idea, but some prominent researchers consider themselves panspermatists. 许多科学家驳斥这种观点，但一些杰出的研究人员认为自己是泛种论者。 |Synonyms:  proponent of panspermia | Antonyms:  opponent of panspermia | noun


panspermia: /pænˈspɜːrmiə/ | n. | 泛种论 | The theory that life exists throughout the universe and is distributed by space dust, meteoroids, asteroids, comets, etc.|  Panspermia suggests that life on Earth may have originated elsewhere. 泛种论表明地球上的生命可能起源于其他地方。 |Synonyms:  cosmogenesis, exogenesis | Antonyms:  abiogenesis (specifically on Earth) | noun
