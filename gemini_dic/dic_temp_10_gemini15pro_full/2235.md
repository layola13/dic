adduced:/əˈdjuːst/ | v. | 引用，举证 | To cite or offer as evidence or support for a claim or argument. | The lawyer adduced evidence that proved the defendant's innocence. 律师引用证据证明被告无罪。

The prosecutor adduced several witnesses to corroborate the victim's testimony.  检察官引用了几位证人来证实受害者的证词。


| 近义词：cited, quoted, alleged, presented, offered | 反义词：refuted, denied, contradicted | 词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adducere*, from *ad-* ("to, toward") + *ducere* ("to lead").  The word literally means "to lead to" or "to bring forward."

USAGE->
Present Tense:
He adduces evidence to support his claim. = 他引用证据来支持他的主张。
She adduces several examples to illustrate her point. = 她引用了几个例子来说明她的观点。

Past Tense:
The defense attorney adduced new evidence that challenged the prosecution's case. = 辩护律师提出了新的证据，对控方的案件提出了质疑。
They adduced testimony from several expert witnesses. = 他们引用了几位专家证人的证词。

Present Perfect Tense:
Researchers have adduced compelling evidence to support their theory. = 研究人员已经引用了令人信服的证据来支持他们的理论。
The historian has adduced numerous documents to back up his claims. = 这位历史学家引用了大量文件来支持他的说法。

Past Perfect Tense:
The witness had adduced crucial evidence before the trial was adjourned. = 在审判休庭之前，证人已经提供了关键证据。
She had adduced several reasons for her decision before changing her mind. = 在改变主意之前，她已经列举了几个做出这个决定的理由。

Future Tense:
The prosecution will adduce further evidence tomorrow. = 控方明天将提供进一步的证据。
The expert witness will adduce his findings to the court next week. = 专家证人下周将向法庭提交他的调查结果。

Passive Voice:
New evidence was adduced in court today. = 今天法庭上提出了新的证据。
Several arguments were adduced in support of the proposal. = 为了支持这项提议，人们提出了几个论点。


PHRASE->
adduce evidence (引用证据)
adduce reasons (引用理由)
adduce examples (引用例子)
adduce arguments (引用论点)
adduce testimony (引用证词)
