ductilimeter:/ˌdʌktɪˈlɪmɪtər/ | n. | 延性计，延展性测量仪 | An instrument used to measure the ductility of a material, especially metals.  | The ductilimeter showed that the alloy had a high degree of ductility. 延性计显示该合金具有很高的延展性。 | Example: The metallurgist used a ductilimeter to test the wire's ability to be drawn into a thinner strand.  冶金学家使用延性计测试金属丝被拉成细丝的能力。 | Synonyms: tensometer, extensometer | Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for an instrument) | noun
