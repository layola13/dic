gardens: /ˈɡɑːrdnz/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːrdnz/ (US)| n.|花园；菜园；公园|A piece of ground, often near a house, used for growing flowers, fruit, or vegetables.|例句：The children were playing in the gardens. 孩子们在花园里玩耍。

例句：She spends hours tending her gardens. 她花费数小时照料她的花园。|近义词：yards, grounds, parks|反义词：wilderness|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English gardin, from Old North French gardin, jardin, from Frankish *gardin, from Proto-Germanic *gardan. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gher- (“to grasp, enclose”).
