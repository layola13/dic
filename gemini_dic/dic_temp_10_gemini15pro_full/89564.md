honorary:/ˈɒnərəri/ (UK) /ˈɑːnəreri/ (US)| adj.|荣誉的；名誉的|Conferred as an honour, without the usual requirements or duties.|She received an honorary doctorate from the university. 她获得了大学的荣誉博士学位。|近义词：titular, nominal|反义词：earned, substantive|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin honorarius ‘conferring honor,’ from Latin honor.
