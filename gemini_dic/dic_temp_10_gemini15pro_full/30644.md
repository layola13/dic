cereal:/ˈsɪriəl/ (US), /ˈsɪəriəl/ (UK)| n. |谷物；谷类食物；麦片|A grain used for food, such as wheat, oats, or corn.  Often eaten as a breakfast food in processed form.|I eat cereal for breakfast every morning. 我每天早上都吃麦片。|Synonyms: grain, cornflakes, muesli|Antonyms: meat, fruit, vegetable|noun

cereal:/ˈsɪriəl/ (US), /ˈsɪəriəl/ (UK)| adj. |谷物的；谷类制成的|Made of or relating to cereal.|Cereal crops are important for the world's food supply. 谷类作物对世界粮食供应很重要。|Synonyms: grain,  |Antonyms: non-grain |adjective
