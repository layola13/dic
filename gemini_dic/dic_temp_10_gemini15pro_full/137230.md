nonsurrenders:/nɒn.səˈrɛn.dərz/ | n. | 不投降者；不屈服的人 |  People who refuse to surrender or give up. | The nonsurrenders held their ground despite overwhelming odds.  不投降者即使面对压倒性的劣势也坚守阵地。| 近义词: resisters, holdouts | 反义词: quitters, collaborators | 词性: 名词

nonsurrenders:/nɒn.səˈrɛn.dərz/ |  n. | 未放弃之物；未投降之物 | Things that have not been surrendered or given up. | The nonsurrenders included key territories and valuable resources. 未放弃之物包括关键领土和宝贵资源。 | 近义词: possessions, holdings | 反义词: losses, forfeitures | 词性: 名词


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "surrenders" (plural of "surrender," from Old French "surrendre," meaning "to give over").
