nuttish:/ˈnʌtɪʃ/| adj.|略带疯狂的，有点古怪的，傻傻的|Slightly crazy or eccentric; silly. |例句：He has a nuttish sense of humor. 他有一种傻傻的幽默感。

例句：She's been acting a bit nuttish lately. 她最近表现得有点古怪。|近义词：crazy, eccentric, wacky, zany, daft, silly, foolish|反义词：sane, sensible, rational|词性:形容词

nuttish:/ˈnʌtɪʃ/| adj.|坚果味的；坚果似的|Having the flavor or consistency of nuts.|例句：This cake has a delicious nuttish flavor. 这款蛋糕有美味的坚果味。

例句：The sauce had a rich, nuttish aroma.  这酱汁有浓郁的坚果香味。|近义词： nutty|反义词： bland (平淡的) |词性:形容词
