excandescent:/ˌɛkskænˈdɛsənt/| adj.|白炽的，发出白热光的；容光焕发的，热情洋溢的|Emitting light as a result of being heated; characterized by glowing or brilliance, especially in a metaphorical sense.|The excandescent filament in the light bulb glowed brightly. 白炽灯泡中的灯丝发出明亮的光芒。

The speaker's excandescent performance captivated the audience. 演讲者热情洋溢的表演吸引了观众。|近义词：glowing, radiant, luminous, brilliant, passionate, fervent|反义词：dull, dim, lackluster, apathetic|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "ex-" (out) + "candēscere" (to become white-hot), from "candēre" (to glow white).  The word literally means "to glow white-hot from within."
