convergent:/kənˈvɜːrdʒənt/| adj.|汇聚的，收敛的|Tending to move toward one point or to approach each other; converging.|The two rivers are convergent at this point. 这两条河流在这一点汇合。|Synonyms: converging, meeting, uniting, joining, merging, coalescing|Antonyms: divergent, diverging, separating, parting, branching|adjective

converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/| v.|汇聚，集中|To tend to meet in one point or line; incline toward each other.|The roads converge just outside the city.  这些道路就在城外汇合。|Synonyms: meet, intersect, join, merge, unite|Antonyms: diverge, separate|verb

convergence:/kənˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n.|汇聚，收敛|The act of converging; the state of being convergent.|The convergence of the two rivers creates a powerful current. 两条河流的汇合形成一股强大的水流。|Synonyms: meeting, junction, confluence, union, merging, coalescence|Antonyms: divergence, separation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *convergere*, from com- "together" + vergere "to incline, to turn."
