inhaul:/ˈɪnhɔːl/ (US), /ˈɪnhɔːl/ (UK)| n. vt.|拉进，收进；(尤指帆船的)收帆索|A rope used to haul in a sail or part of a sail, especially the inner corner of a headsail.|The sailor tightened the inhaul to flatten the sail.|近义词：sheet, rope, halyard|反义词:outhaul|noun, verb
