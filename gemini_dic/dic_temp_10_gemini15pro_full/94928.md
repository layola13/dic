in-manner:/ɪnˈmænər/ | adv. | 以...方式 | In the manner of; like.  | He spoke in a calm manner. 他以平静的方式讲话。 | 近义词: like, similarly, likewise | 反义词: unlike, differently | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Middle English *in manere, from Old French en maniere, from Latin in (preposition) + maniera (manner).
