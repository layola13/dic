incautiousnesses:/ɪnˌkɔːʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.|粗心大意，轻率|Lack of caution; imprudence.|His incautiousness led to the accident. 他的粗心大意导致了这场事故。|incautiousness:  Her incautiousness nearly cost her the game. 她的粗心差点让她输掉比赛。|Synonyms: carelessness, recklessness, imprudence, indiscretion|Antonyms: caution, carefulness, prudence, discretion|noun

incautiousness:/ɪnˈkɔːʃəsnəs/
|n.|粗心大意，轻率|The state of being incautious; lack of care or attention.|Her incautiousness in crossing the road almost caused an accident. 她过马路时的粗心大意差点酿成事故。|incautiousness: Due to his incautiousness, he left the front door unlocked. 由于他的粗心，他没锁前门。|Synonyms: carelessness, negligence, recklessness|Antonyms: caution, prudence, vigilance|noun
