concern-with:/kənˈsɜːrn wɪð/| phrasal verb | 牵涉; 与…有关 | To involve or affect someone or something. | The investigation concerns several high-ranking officials.  这项调查牵涉到几名高级官员。| synonym: involve, affect, impact | antonym: exclude, separate | phrasal verb
