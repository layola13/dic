disaster's:/dɪˈzæstərz/
noun
灾难的；灾祸的
belonging to or associated with a disaster.
The disaster's impact on the local economy was devastating. 这场灾难对当地经济的影响是毁灭性的。
The disaster's aftermath left the town in ruins. 灾难过后，小镇变成了一片废墟。

synonyms: calamity's, catastrophe's, cataclysm's
antonyms: triumph's, success's
