conditionate:/kənˈdɪʃənˌeɪt/ | v. | 使习惯于；使适应；调节；制约 | To cause someone or something to behave or react in a particular way by training or repeated experience. | Pavlov conditioned his dogs to salivate at the sound of a bell. 巴甫洛夫训练他的狗一听到铃声就流口水。|  Synonyms: train, habituate, accustom, adapt | Antonyms: decondition, unlearn | verb

conditionate:/kənˈdɪʃənˌeɪt/ | adj. |  有条件的;受…限制的 | Subject to or depending on conditions. | The offer is conditional upon you accepting the terms of the contract.  这个提议取决于你是否接受合同条款。 | Synonyms: conditional, dependent, contingent, provisional | Antonyms: unconditional, absolute, independent | adjective
