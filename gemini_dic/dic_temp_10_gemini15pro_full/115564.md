maracas:/məˈrækəz/ | n. | 沙槌，响葫芦 | A pair of maracas are percussion instruments traditionally made from dried gourds or coconut shells filled with beans or seeds.  They produce a rattling sound when shaken. | The musician shook the maracas to create a rhythmic beat. 音乐家摇动沙槌，创造出有节奏的节拍。|  The child happily played with a pair of colorful maracas.  孩子快乐地玩着一对色彩鲜艳的沙槌。 | synonyms: rattles, shakers | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun
