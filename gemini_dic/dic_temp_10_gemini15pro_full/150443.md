pantaloon: /ˌpæntəˈluːn/ (US), /ˌpæntəˈluːn/ (UK)
n.
滑稽丑角的紧身裤；老朽
tight-fitting trousers, usually brightly colored and patterned, worn by a comic character, especially in Italian commedia dell'arte; a foolish old man
例句：The pantaloon in the play was hilarious. 剧中的丑角滑稽可笑。

例句：He's become a doddering pantaloon in his old age.  他老了，变得像个老糊涂。
近义词：buffoon, clown, fool
反义词：youth, hero
词性：noun
