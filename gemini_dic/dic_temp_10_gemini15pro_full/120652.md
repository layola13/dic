mis-sorted:/ˌmɪsˈsɔːrtɪd/
adjective
分类错误的
Incorrectly sorted or categorized.
The mis-sorted mail was returned to the post office. 分类错误的邮件被退回邮局。
The warehouse was full of mis-sorted products. 仓库里堆满了分类错误的产品。
Synonym: misplaced, wrongly categorized, incorrectly filed
Antonym: correctly sorted, properly categorized, accurately filed
词性: 形容词
