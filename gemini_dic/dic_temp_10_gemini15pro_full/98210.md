insectlike:/ˈɪnsektˌlaɪk/| adj.|像昆虫的，昆虫似的|Resembling or characteristic of an insect.  This creature has an insectlike appearance with its segmented body and multiple legs.  这种生物有着昆虫般的外形，身体分节，多足。|例句：The robot had an insectlike body, allowing it to navigate tight spaces.  这个机器人有一个像昆虫一样的身体，可以穿过狭窄的空间。

例句：The alien's insectlike eyes blinked slowly. 外星人的昆虫般的眼睛慢慢地眨着。|近义词：insectoid, insectan|反义词:mammalian, vertebrate|词性:adjective
