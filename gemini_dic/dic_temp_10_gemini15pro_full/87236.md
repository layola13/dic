heretic:/ˈherɪtɪk/ /ˈhɛrətɪk/| n. |异教徒，异端邪说者|A person believing in or practicing something different from established religious doctrine.| Galileo was condemned as a heretic for his astronomical theories. 伽利略因他的天文学理论被谴责为异端。|Synonyms: dissenter, nonconformist, apostate, schismatic|Antonyms: conformist, orthodox, believer|Noun


heretical:/hɪˈretɪkl/ /həˈrɛtɪkl/| adj. |异端的，异教的|Characterized by departure from accepted beliefs or standards.|His heretical views led to his expulsion from the church. 他的异端观点导致他被逐出教会。|Synonyms: unorthodox, dissenting, iconoclastic, heterodox|Antonyms: orthodox, conventional, conformist|Adjective
