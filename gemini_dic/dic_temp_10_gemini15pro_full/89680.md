hooray:/hʊˈreɪ/ /həˈreɪ/| int.|好啊！万岁！|An exclamation of joy, triumph, or applause.|例句：Hooray! We won! 好啊！我们赢了！

例句：Hooray! The weekend is finally here! 万岁！周末终于到了！

近义词：hurrah, yay, yes, cheers|反义词:boo, alas, oh no|词性:感叹词
