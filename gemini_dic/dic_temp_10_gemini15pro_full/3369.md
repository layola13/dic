affronted: /əˈfrʌntɪd/
affronted: adj.
affronted: 受辱的，感到冒犯的
affronted: Feeling or showing resentment or indignation at having been insulted or offended.
affronted:  She felt affronted by his rude remarks. 她觉得他的粗鲁言论是对她的冒犯。
affronted: He looked affronted when I questioned his decision. 当我质疑他的决定时，他看起来很生气。

affronted: insulted, offended, aggrieved, piqued
affronted: pleased, delighted, flattered, honored
affronted: adjective
