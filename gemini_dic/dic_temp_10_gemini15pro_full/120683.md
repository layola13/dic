misadjustment:/ˌmɪsəˈdʒʌstmənt/
|n.| 失调；错位；不适应
|A failure to adjust or adapt properly.  A state of being out of harmony or balance.|
|The misadjustment of the gears caused the car to break down.  齿轮失调导致汽车抛锚。|
|maladjustment, imbalance, disharmony, discord, incompatibility|
|adjustment, adaptation, harmony, balance, compatibility|
|noun|
