overdilating: /ˌoʊvərdaɪˈleɪtɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvədaɪˈleɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度扩张，过度放大
causing something to dilate excessively.
The doctor warned against overdilating the pupils with eye drops. 医生警告不要用眼药水过度放大瞳孔。
Overdilating the blood vessels can lead to a dangerous drop in blood pressure. 过度扩张血管会导致血压危险下降。

近义词: overexpanding, overstretching
反义词: constricting, contracting
词性: verb (present participle)
