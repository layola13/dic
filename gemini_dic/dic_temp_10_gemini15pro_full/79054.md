Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned so far? = 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has he been up to lately? = 他最近在忙些什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading all day? = 她一整天都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about earlier? = 她之前在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
