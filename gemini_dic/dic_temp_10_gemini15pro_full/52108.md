diamond-like:/ˈdaɪməndˌlaɪk/
adjective
像钻石的，坚硬的
Resembling a diamond in hardness, brilliance, or other qualities.
The material has a diamond-like hardness.  这种材料具有类似钻石的硬度。
The screen protector has a diamond-like coating.  屏幕保护膜有一层类金刚石涂层。
synonyms: diamond-hard, adamantine, brilliant, sparkling
antonyms: soft, dull, opaque
