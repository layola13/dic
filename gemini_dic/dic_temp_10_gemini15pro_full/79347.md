glee:/ɡliː/
n.
欢乐，高兴，欣喜
A feeling of great happiness, especially from triumph or satisfaction.
The children squealed with glee as they opened their presents. 孩子们打开礼物时高兴地尖叫。
joy, delight, elation
sadness, sorrow
noun


glees:/ɡliːz/
n.
