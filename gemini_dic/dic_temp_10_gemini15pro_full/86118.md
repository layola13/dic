heartburned:/ˈhɑːrtˌbɜːrnd/| adj. n.|胃灼热的，烧心的；妒忌的|A burning sensation in the chest, caused by acid refluxing into the esophagus.  Also, a feeling of jealousy or resentment.|例句：I ate too much spicy food and now I'm heartburned. 我吃了太多辣的食物，现在烧心了。

|例句：He felt heartburned seeing his rival succeed. 看到对手成功，他感到妒忌。|近义词：heartburn (n.), jealous (adj.), envious (adj.)|反义词: contented, satisfied|词性:形容词, 名词
