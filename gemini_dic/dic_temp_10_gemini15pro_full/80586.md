goofy:/ˈɡuːfi/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的，笨拙的|Silly or foolish, often in a playful or amusing way.  Originating possibly from the cartoon character Goofy, known for his clumsiness and lack of intelligence.|He made a goofy face and we all laughed. 他做了个傻乎乎的表情，我们都笑了。|近义词：silly, foolish, clumsy, awkward, ridiculous|反义词：serious, sensible, intelligent, clever|词性:形容词

goofy:/ˈɡuːfi/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|(Informal) A silly or foolish person.|Don't be such a goofy! 别这么傻！|近义词：fool, idiot, nitwit|反义词：genius, intellectual|词性:名词
