outtalking:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːkɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的讲话；比…说得更多
speaking longer or more fluently than someone else
He outtalked his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中说得比对手多。
Synonyms: outspeak, outargue
Antonyms: be quiet, listen
