circumcursation: /ˌsɜːrkəmkərˈseɪʃən/ /ˌsɝːkəmkərˈseɪʃən/
n.
迂回说法; 绕圈子说话
the use of many words where fewer would do, especially in a deliberate attempt to be vague or evasive.
He was known for his circumcursation and tendency to avoid direct answers. 他以说话绕圈子、避免直接回答问题而闻名。
The lawyer's circumcursation seemed designed to confuse the jury. 律师的迂回其词似乎是为了迷惑陪审团。
synonyms: circumlocution, periphrasis, verbosity, prolixity
antonyms: conciseness, brevity, directness
noun
