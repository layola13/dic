open-airish:/ˌoʊpənˈɛərɪʃ/
adjective
露天式的，户外式的
Resembling or suggestive of the open air; somewhat open to the air.  Characterized by a feeling of being outdoors.
The restaurant had an open-airish feel, with large windows and a breezy patio.  这家餐厅有一种露天式的感觉，有大窗户和通风的露台。
The stage had an open-airish design, allowing the audience to feel connected to the natural surroundings. 舞台采用露天式设计，让观众感受到与自然环境的联系。
synonyms: airy, breezy, outdoorsy, al fresco
antonyms: stuffy, closed-in, indoors, enclosed
词性: adjective
