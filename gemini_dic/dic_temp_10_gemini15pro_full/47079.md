dats:/dæts/ | pron. | that's 的非正式拼写，意思是 that is 或 that has |  It's a contraction of "that is" or "that has", often used in informal speech or writing. | Dats cool.  那很酷。 | 近义词: that's | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，取决于 that's 后面的内容) | contraction


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Dats a good idea. = 那是个好主意。
Dats what I thought. = 我也是这么想的。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Dats happening right now. = 那就是现在正在发生的。
Dats what she's doing. = 那就是她正在做的。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Dats what has happened. = 那就是已经发生的。
Dats been a long day. = 今天真是漫长的一天。(that has been)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Dats what I've been saying. = 那就是我一直说的。
Dats what they've been doing all day. = 那就是他们一整天都在做的。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Dats what he said. = 那就是他说的。
Dats how it happened. = 事情就是这样发生的。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Dats what I was doing. = 那就是我当时在做的。
Dats what she was thinking. = 那就是她当时在想的。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Dats what had happened. = 那就是已经发生的事。
Dats what they had discussed. = 那就是他们之前讨论过的。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Dats what I'd been doing. = 那就是我一直做的。(that's what I had been doing)
Dats what they'd been talking about. = 那就是他们一直在谈论的。(that's what they had been talking about)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Dats what I'll do. = 那就是我将要做的。(that's what I will do)
Dats what will happen. = 那就是将要发生的。(that's what will happen)


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Dats what I'll be doing tomorrow. = 那就是我明天将要做的。(that's what I will be doing tomorrow)
Dats what they'll be discussing. = 那就是他们将要讨论的。(that's what they will be discussing)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Dats what I'll have done by then. = 那就是我到那时将已经完成的。(that's what I will have done by then)
Dats what will have changed. = 那就是到那时将已经改变的。(that's what will have changed)

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Dats what I'll have been doing for a year. = 那就是我将已经做了一年的。(that's what I will have been doing for a year)
Dats what she'll have been studying. = 那就是她将已经学习的。(that's what she will have been studying)
