lux:/lʌks/ | n. | 勒克斯（光照度的单位）; 奢侈, 豪华 | a unit of illumination equal to one lumen per square meter;  great comfort and extravagant living | The room was bathed in 300 lux.  房间沐浴在300勒克斯的光线下。| The hotel offers guests the ultimate in luxury. 这家酒店为客人提供极致的奢华。 | synonyms: brightness, brilliance, splendor, opulence | antonyms: darkness, gloom, austerity, poverty | noun

lux:/lʌks/ | adj. | 豪华的 | luxurious |  a lux hotel  一家豪华酒店 | synonyms: luxurious, opulent, lavish | antonyms: basic, simple, austere | adjective
