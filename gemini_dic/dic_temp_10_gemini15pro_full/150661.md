paprika:/pəˈpriːkə/ /ˈpæprɪkə/| n. |辣椒粉；甜椒|A powdered spice made from dried sweet red peppers.  It is commonly used in Hungarian cuisine and other dishes.| I added a teaspoon of paprika to the goulash for color and flavor. 我在炖牛肉里加了一茶匙辣椒粉来增色提味。|Synonyms:  sweet pepper, bell pepper (for the vegetable); Hungarian pepper|Antonyms: chili pepper, cayenne pepper (for spicier peppers)|noun

paprikas:/pəˈpriːkəz/ /ˈpæprɪkəz/| n. |辣椒粉（复数）；甜椒（复数）|Plural form of paprika, referring to multiple paprika spices or sweet peppers.|The recipe calls for two different paprikas: sweet and smoked.  这个食谱需要两种不同的辣椒粉：甜的和烟熏的。|Synonyms: sweet peppers, bell peppers (for the vegetable); Hungarian peppers|Antonyms: chili peppers, cayenne peppers (for spicier peppers)|noun
