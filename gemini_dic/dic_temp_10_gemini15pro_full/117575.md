megalithic:/ˌmɛɡəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.| 巨石的，巨石建造的|Relating to or denoting prehistoric monuments made of large stones.|例句：Stonehenge is a megalithic monument. 巨石阵是一座巨石建筑。|例句：The megalithic tombs are fascinating remnants of a bygone era. 巨石墓是过去时代令人着迷的遗迹。|近义词：massive, monumental, cyclopean|反义词: small, miniature|词性:形容词
megalith:/ˈmɛɡəlɪθ/| n.| 巨石|A large stone that forms a prehistoric monument (either alone or with others).|例句：The megaliths were transported over considerable distances. 这些巨石是从很远的地方运来的。|例句：Researchers are still unsure how the ancient people moved the megaliths. 研究人员仍然不确定古代人是如何移动这些巨石的。|近义词：monolith, boulder|反义词: pebble, grain|词性:名词
