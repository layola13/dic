confinable:/kənˈfaɪnəbl/
adjective
可限制的，可 confinement 的
able to be limited or contained within a particular area or space.
The virus is highly contagious, but thankfully confinable with proper quarantine measures. 这种病毒传染性很强，但幸好通过适当的隔离措施可以限制它。
confine, restrict, contain
uncontrollable, limitless
词性: adjective
