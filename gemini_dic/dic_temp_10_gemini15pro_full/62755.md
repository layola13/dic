engorges:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒɪz/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒɪz/
|v.|使充盈；使充血；狼吞虎咽|to cause to swell with blood; to devour greedily|The river, engorged by melting snow, overflowed its banks.  融化的雪使河水暴涨，漫过了河岸。|近义词：swell, distend, bloat, gorge, stuff, cram|反义词：shrink, contract, deflate|词性:动词

engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒd/
|adj.|充盈的；充血的|swollen with blood; filled to excess|The tick was engorged with blood after feeding on the dog.  这只蜱虫吸了狗的血后鼓胀起来。|近义词：swollen, distended, bloated|反义词：shrunken, contracted, deflated|词性:形容词
