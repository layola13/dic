-ify:/ɪfaɪ/
suffix|使...化，转变为...|
English explanation:  A suffix that forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be," "to become," "to invest with the character of," or "to treat with or as if with," from Middle English -ifien, from Old French -ifier, from Latin -ificare, from -ificus "-making" + -are "to make."|
Example: Simplify the process. 简化流程。|
Synonyms: convert, transform|
Antonyms: N/A|
Word type: suffix

-ify:/ɪfaɪ/
suffix|使...化，转变为...|
English explanation: Creates adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "tending to." |
Example: A justify text alignment.  两端对齐的文本格式。|
Synonyms: characterized by, tending to|
Antonyms: N/A|
Word type: suffix
