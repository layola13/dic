housewife:/ˈhaʊsˌwaɪf/ (US), /ˈhaʊswɪf/ (UK)| n.|家庭主妇|A married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and home.|例句：She was a dedicated housewife and mother. 她是一位尽职尽责的家庭主妇和母亲。

例句：Being a housewife can be a demanding job. 做家庭主妇可能是一项要求很高的工作。|近义词：homemaker, stay-at-home mom|反义词:working woman, career woman|词性:noun
