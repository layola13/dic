interdigitation:/ˌɪntərˌdɪdʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 交错，指状交叉 | The interdigitation of the muscle fibers allows for a stronger connection.  肌肉纤维的交错使连接更牢固。| The complex interdigitation of the two companies' operations made it difficult to separate them.  两家公司运营的复杂交错使得很难将它们分开。| 近义词：interlocking, dovetailing, meshing | 反义词：separation, disengagement | noun

interdigitate:/ˌɪntərˈdɪdʒɪteɪt/ | v. | 交错，使…指状交叉 | The fingers interdigitate when you clasp your hands.  当你双手紧握时，手指会交错。| The two mountain ranges interdigitate, forming a complex landscape. 两座山脉交错，形成了复杂的地貌。| 近义词：interlock, intertwine, mesh | 反义词：separate, disengage | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *interdigitatus*, past participle of *interdigitare* "to interlock the fingers," from *inter-* "between" + *digitare* "to touch with the finger," from *digitus* "finger."
