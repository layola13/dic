balmier:/ˈbɑːmiər/ (US), /ˈbɑːmiə/ (UK)| adj.|更温和的，更芳香的|Mild and pleasant; characterized by a soothing warmth and often a pleasant fragrance.|The weather is balmier today than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更温和。|The air was balmier and filled with the scent of flowers. 空气更加温和，充满了花香。|近义词：milder, warmer, more fragrant|反义词：colder, harsher, less fragrant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From balm + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin balsamum, meaning "balsam."
