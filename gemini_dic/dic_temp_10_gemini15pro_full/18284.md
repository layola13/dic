bell-curve: /ˈbel kɜːrv/ (US) /ˈbel kɜːv/ (UK)| n.|钟形曲线; 正态分布曲线|A bell curve is a graph of a normal distribution, which is a symmetrical distribution with the mean, median, and mode all coinciding at the peak.|The results of the IQ test formed a bell curve.|Synonyms: normal distribution, Gaussian distribution|Antonyms: skewed distribution|noun

bell-curves: /ˈbel kɜːvz/ (US) /ˈbel kɜːvz/ (UK)| n.|钟形曲线; 正态分布曲线（复数）|Plural form of "bell curve," referring to multiple instances of this type of graph.|The professor showed us several bell-curves representing different data sets.|Synonyms: normal distributions, Gaussian distributions|Antonyms: skewed distributions|noun
