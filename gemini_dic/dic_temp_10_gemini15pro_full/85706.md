havildar:/ˈhɑːvɪldɑːr/ (UK), /ˈhɑːvəldɑːr/ (US)| n. |下士；准尉|A rank in the Indian and Pakistani armies, equivalent to a sergeant or staff sergeant.|The havildar barked orders at the new recruits. 下士对着新兵大喊命令。|近义词：sergeant, staff sergeant|反义词:private, recruit|词性:noun

Etymology: From Urdu हविलदार (havaldār), from Persian هَوالدار (havāldār, “one in charge”), from هَواله (havāle, “charge, custody”) + دار (dār, “holder”).
