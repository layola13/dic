durries:/ˈdʌriz/| n. |粗棉地毯，粗毛毯|A heavy cotton fabric, often woven with stripes or other patterns, used for making rugs, carpets, or wall hangings, especially in India, Pakistan, and the Middle East. |We bought some colourful durries for the patio. 我们为露台买了一些色彩鲜艳的粗棉地毯。|Synonyms: rugs, carpets, mats, floor coverings|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bare floor")|Noun
