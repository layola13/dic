childminder:/ˈtʃaɪldˌmaɪndər/ (UK) /ˈtʃaɪldˌmaɪndər/ (US)| n. |儿童保育员|A person who looks after other people's children in their own home, for payment.|例句：She works as a registered childminder. 她是一名注册儿童保育员。|近义词：babysitter, nanny, daycare provider|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps parent)|词性:noun
