hyperinvolution:/ˌhaɪpərɪnvəˈluːʃən/ | n. |过度退化，过度复旧|Excessive or abnormal involution, especially of the uterus after childbirth.  It refers to a process of shrinkage beyond the normal physiological limits.  This term is sometimes used more broadly to describe any exaggerated or excessive regression.|Her doctor diagnosed her with hyperinvolution of the uterus after delivery.  她医生诊断她在分娩后子宫过度退化。|Synonym: superinvolution|Antonym: subinvolution|noun
