dandy's:/ˈdændiz/| n.| 花花公子的（东西）;纨绔子弟的（东西）| Something that belongs to or is associated with a dandy. |  That cane is a real dandy's accessory. 那根手杖真是花花公子的装饰品。| 近义词: fop's, coxcomb's | 反义词: slob's | 词性: 名词 (noun), 所有格

词源: From "dandy," a term originating in the late 18th century, possibly related to the Scottish name Andrew, or perhaps from a slang term for something excellent. The possessive form simply adds -'s.
