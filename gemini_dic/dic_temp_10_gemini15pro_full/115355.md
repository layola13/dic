manorial:/məˈnɔːriəl/
adjective
庄园的；领地的
Relating to a manor or its lord.  Of or pertaining to the system by which land was held under the feudal system.
The historian specialized in manorial records of medieval England.  这位历史学家专门研究中世纪英格兰的庄园记录。
近义词:feudal, landed, estate
反义词:non-feudal, urban
