glump:/ɡlʌmp/ | n. | 闷闷不乐；沮丧；忧郁 | A state of low spirits; sulkiness; gloom. |  He sat there in a glump, refusing to speak. 他闷闷不乐地坐在那里，不肯说话。

glump:/ɡlʌmp/ | v. | 闷闷不乐；感到沮丧 | To sulk; to be in a glump. |  Don't glump about it, things will get better.  别为此闷闷不乐，事情会好转的。


glump:/ɡlʌmp/ | adj.(非正式) | 闷闷不乐的; 沮丧的 | Sulky; gloomy. | He had a glump look on his face. 他脸上带着沮丧的表情。


近义词:sulk, mope, pout, frown, gloom, dejection, depression
反义词:cheerfulness, happiness, joy, elation
词性: 名词, 动词, 形容词 (主要用作名词和动词)

词源:  Likely of imitative origin, perhaps related to words like "clump" and "lump," suggesting a heavy, inert feeling.  可能源于拟声词，或许与“clump（块）”和“lump（团）”等词有关，暗示一种沉重、迟钝的感觉。
