chatline: /ˈtʃætlaɪn/ | n. | 聊天热线；聊天室 | A telephone number or internet service that people can call or connect to in order to have a conversation with others, often about a particular subject or with a particular purpose. |  She met him through a dating chatline. 她通过约会聊天热线认识了他。|  She works for a psychic chatline. 她为一家灵媒聊天热线工作。 | synonyms: party line, hotline, help line | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "face-to-face conversation" or "in-person meeting") | noun
