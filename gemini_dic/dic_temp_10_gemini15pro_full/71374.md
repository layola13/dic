flageolet:/ˌflædʒəˈlɛt/ | n. | 六孔竖笛；哨笛；嫩菜豆 | A small fipple flute; a type of small, tender green bean. | He played a sweet tune on his flageolet. 他用六孔竖笛吹奏了一首甜美的曲子。| The chef prepared a dish of flageolet beans with garlic and herbs. 厨师准备了一道用嫩菜豆、大蒜和香草做成的菜。 | 近义词：recorder, flute, haricot vert | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *flageol, diminutive of flageol (“pipe, flute”), from Vulgar Latin *flautiolus, diminutive of Latin flatus (“blown”), from flare (“to blow”).
