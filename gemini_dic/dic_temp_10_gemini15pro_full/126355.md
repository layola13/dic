myrialitre:/ˌmɪriəˈliːtər/ /ˌmɪriəˈliːtɚ/ | n. |百万升；一千万升|A unit of volume equal to one million litres or ten thousand cubic metres.  Especially used in scientific contexts.|The reservoir's capacity is measured in myrialitres. 水库的容量以百万升来衡量。

The annual rainfall in this region is several myrialitres. 这个地区的年降雨量是几百万升。|近义词: megalitre (one million litres)|反义词: microlitre, nanolitre|noun
