monochronic:/ˌmɒnəˈkrɒnɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈkrɑːnɪk/
adjective
单时制的；一次只做一件事的
Characterized by a preference for doing one thing at a time and adhering to schedules and deadlines.
Monochronic cultures value punctuality and efficiency.

Monochronic people tend to focus on one task at a time and prefer to complete it before starting another.


She has a monochronic work style, preferring to focus on one task at a time.
她有着单时制的工作风格，喜欢一次只专注于一项任务。

He's very monochronic and gets stressed when he has to multitask.
他非常单时制，当他不得不进行多任务处理时会感到压力很大。

近义词:linear, sequential
反义词:polychronic
词性:adjective
