acritochromacy: /əˌkrɪtəʊˈkrəʊməsi/
noun
全色盲
Complete color blindness; total absence of color vision.
The patient's acritochromacy made it difficult for him to distinguish between traffic lights.  这位病人的全色盲让他难以区分交通信号灯。
A mutation in the CNGA3 gene can lead to acritochromacy. CNGA3基因的突变会导致全色盲。
近义词: monochromacy, achromatopsia
反义词: trichromacy (正常三色视觉)
词性: n.
