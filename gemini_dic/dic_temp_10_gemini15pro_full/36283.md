coheir:/ˌkoʊˈɛər/| n.|共同继承人|A person who inherits property along with one or more other people.|例句：She and her brother are coheirs to their father's estate. 她和她的兄弟是她父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：The two sisters were named coheirs in the will. 这两姐妹在遗嘱中被指定为共同继承人。|近义词：joint heir, fellow heir|反义词:|noun
