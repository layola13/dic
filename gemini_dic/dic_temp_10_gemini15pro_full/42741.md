contour:/ˈkɒntʊə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːntʊr (US)| n. v.|轮廓，等高线；勾勒|A line that represents an imaginary line of constant or equal value on a map or chart, such as elevation or atmospheric pressure. Also refers to the general outline or shape of something.|The contour of the hills was softened by the mist. 山丘的轮廓被薄雾柔和了.|outline, shape, profile|formlessness, shapelessness|noun, verb
