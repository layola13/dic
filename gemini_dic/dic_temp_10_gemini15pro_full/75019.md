friends: /frendz/ /frɛndz/ | n. | 朋友；友人；支持者；赞助者 | People whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection, typically exclusive of sexual or family relations. |  I went to the movies with my friends. 我和我的朋友们去看电影了。| companions, pals, mates, buddies | enemies, foes | noun

Etymology: Old English *frēond, from frēon (“to love”), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vriend, German Freund.
