liegedom: /ˈliːdʒdəm/
n.
臣服；领地；效忠的义务
The territory or domain of a liege lord.  A liege lord's area of control or authority. The obligation or duty of allegiance owed to a liege lord.
The knight swore fealty to his liegedom.  这位骑士向他的领主宣誓效忠。
synonyms:  domain, territory, fief, realm,  allegiance, fealty, loyalty, obedience
antonyms:  rebellion, treachery, disloyalty, insubordination
noun
