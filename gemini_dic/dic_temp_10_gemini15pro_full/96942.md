inextricable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstrɪkəbl/ /ˌɪnɛkˈstrɪkəbl/
adjective
无法解脱的；纠缠不清的；难解的
impossible to disentangle or separate.
例句：The two families were linked by an inextricable bond of friendship. 这两个家庭因牢不可破的友谊而联系在一起。

例句：She found herself in an inextricable web of lies. 她发现自己陷入了无法解脱的谎言之中。

近义词：inseparable, tangled, intertwined, involved
反义词：separable, extricable, simple, straightforward
词性：adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from Latin inextricabilis, from in- ‘not’ + extricare (from ex- ‘out’ + tricae ‘perplexities’).
