mispaint:/ˌmɪsˈpeɪnt/
verb
错误地涂漆；涂错颜色
to paint something badly or incorrectly
The artist mispainted the portrait, giving the subject an orange complexion.  艺术家把肖像画画错了，把人物的肤色画成了橙色。
The wall was mispainted a hideous shade of green. 墙被错误地涂成了难看的绿色。
misapply, miscolor
paint correctly
v.
