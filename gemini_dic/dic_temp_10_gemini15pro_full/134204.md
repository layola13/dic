nonindependence:/ˌnɒnɪndɪˈpɛndəns/
noun
非独立性；不独立；非独立
Lack of independence; the state of not being independent.  The condition where two or more events or variables are related or affect each other.  This can be due to shared underlying factors, causal relationships, or other forms of dependence.
The nonindependence of the samples led to biased results in the study.  样本的非独立性导致研究结果出现偏差。
The researchers failed to account for the nonindependence of the variables, which invalidated their conclusions. 研究人员未能考虑到变量的非独立性，这使得他们的结论无效。
synonyms: dependence, interdependence, correlation, relatedness
antonyms: independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency
