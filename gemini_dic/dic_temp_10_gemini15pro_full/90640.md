humanizations:/ˌhjuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.| 人性化；赋予人性；人文主义化|The process of making something more human or giving it human qualities.  The increasing humanizations of robots is a fascinating area of study.|The recent humanizations of artificial intelligence have made interacting with technology more intuitive and user-friendly. 最近人工智能的人性化，使得与技术的互动更加直观和用户友好。|近义词：anthropomorphism, personification|反义词: dehumanization|noun
