antagonization:/ænˌtæɡənaɪˈzeɪʃn/| n. |对抗，敌对，引起敌对的行为|The antagonization of the neighboring country led to war.  与邻国的敌对导致了战争。|例句：His constant criticism resulted in the antagonization of his colleagues. 他不断的批评导致了同事们的敌对。

The policy of antagonization towards the minority groups caused widespread unrest. 针对少数群体的对抗政策引起了广泛的不安。|近义词：alienation, estrangement, hostility, opposition|反义词: conciliation, appeasement, propitiation|词性:noun
