angelos:/ˈændʒələs/| n.|天使; 使者; 报信者|A spiritual being believed to act as an attendant, agent, or messenger of God, conventionally represented in human form with wings and a long robe.|She prayed to her guardian angelos. 她向她的守护天使祈祷。|Synonyms: angel, seraph, cherub|Antonyms: demon, devil|Noun
