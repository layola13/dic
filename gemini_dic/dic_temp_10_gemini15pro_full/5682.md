aludel:/əˈluːdəl/
n.
蒸馏器，升华器
A pear-shaped earthenware vessel used in sublimation.
The alchemists used aludels to purify substances.  炼金术士使用蒸馏器来提纯物质。
The alchemists heated the mixture in the aludel. 炼金术士在蒸馏器中加热混合物。
近义词:alembic, retort, still
反义词:None
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Medieval Latin *aludel, from Arabic *al-’u<u>th</u>āl.
