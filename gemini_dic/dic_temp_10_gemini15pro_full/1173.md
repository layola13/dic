acclimated: /əˈklaɪmətɪd/
adjective
适应的，习惯的
Adjusted to a new climate or environment; used to.
The climbers acclimated themselves to the high altitude before attempting the summit. 登山者在尝试登顶之前先让自己适应了高海拔。

She quickly acclimated to the hot and humid weather. 她很快就适应了炎热潮湿的天气。

synonym
adapted, adjusted, habituated, inured
antonym
unaccustomed, unacclimated

ETYMOLOGY->
From French acclimater, from à (to) + climat (climate).  Ultimately from Greek klima (region, slope, zone).

USAGE->

The plant is acclimated to the desert environment. = 这株植物已经适应了沙漠环境。
He is acclimated to the cold weather. = 他已经适应了寒冷的天气。

一般过去时:
The explorers acclimated quickly to the jungle. = 探险家们很快适应了丛林。
They acclimated to the new culture within a few months. = 他们在几个月内就适应了新的文化。

现在完成时:
She has acclimated to living in a big city. = 她已经适应了大城市的生活。
The dog has acclimated to its new home. = 这只狗已经适应了它的新家。


过去完成时:
