foveolate:/ˈfɒvɪəleɪt/ | n. | 小窝 | A small pit or depression.  (This usage is less common) |  Microscopic examination revealed foveolates on the insect's exoskeleton. 显微镜检查显示昆虫的外骨骼上有小窝。 | Synonyms: pit, depression, alveolus | Antonyms: protrusion, bump | Noun
