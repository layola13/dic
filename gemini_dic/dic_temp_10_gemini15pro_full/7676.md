anodizing:/ˈænəˌdaɪzɪŋ/
noun
阳极氧化
An electrochemical process that forms a protective oxide layer on a metal surface, typically aluminum.
The anodizing of the aluminum parts increased their resistance to corrosion.  铝制零件的阳极氧化增加了它们的耐腐蚀性。
anodisation, anodic oxidation

anodize:/ˈænəˌdaɪz/
verb
阳极氧化（处理）
To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process.
The company anodizes aluminum parts for various industries. 这家公司为各行各业阳极氧化铝制零件。
plate, coat
strip, remove
