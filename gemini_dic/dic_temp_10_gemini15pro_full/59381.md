earplugs:/ˈɪərˌplʌɡz/ | n. | 耳塞 | Small plugs inserted into the ears to reduce noise. | I always wear earplugs when I fly to block out the engine noise. 我坐飞机的时候总是戴耳塞以隔绝引擎噪音。|  I use earplugs to help me sleep when my neighbors are noisy. 当我的邻居很吵时，我用耳塞帮助我入睡。 | Synonyms: ear defenders, hearing protectors | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps: noise amplifiers) | Noun
