buttermilk:/ˈbʌtərˌmɪlk/ | n. |酪乳，白脱牛奶|A tangy, milky liquid remaining after butter has been churned from cream, or a cultured milk product similar in flavor and consistency. | I used buttermilk to make the pancakes extra fluffy. 我用酪乳做煎饼，让它们格外蓬松。| synonyms: cultured milk, sour milk | antonyms: cream, sweet milk | noun
