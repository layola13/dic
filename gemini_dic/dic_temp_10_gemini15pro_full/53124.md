diploetic:/ˌdɪploʊˈɛtɪk/| adj.|板障的，顱骨板間的|Relating to or situated in the diploe, the spongy layer of bone between the inner and outer tables of the skull.| The diploetic veins are thin-walled and numerous.  板障静脉壁薄且数量众多。|近义词: cancellous, spongy|反义词: compact, dense|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek *diplóos* "double" + *-ikos* "-ic"
