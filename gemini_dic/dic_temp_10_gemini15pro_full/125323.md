multijugate:/ˌmʌltɪˈdʒʊɡət/ | adj. | 多偶的，多对轭的 | Having many pairs of leaflets; pinnate.  Describing a leaf that is pinnately compound with many pairs of leaflets. | Example: The multijugate leaves of the fern provided ample shade. 这种蕨类植物的多偶叶子提供了充足的荫凉。| Example: The botanist identified the plant by its distinctive multijugate leaf structure.  植物学家通过其独特的多偶叶结构识别了这种植物。| 近义词:  pinnately compound, multifoliate | 反义词: simple (leaf), unifoliate | 词性: 形容词
