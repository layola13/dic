haggish:/ˈhæɡɪʃ/| adj.|像老巫婆的；丑陋的；脾气坏的|Resembling or characteristic of a hag; ugly and bad-tempered.  Having the unpleasant qualities associated with a hag, such as being ugly, bad-tempered, or unpleasant.|例句：The old woman had a haggish appearance, with a hooked nose and warts on her chin.  这位老妇人有着一副巫婆般的外表，鹰钩鼻，下巴上还有疣。|例句：Her haggish demeanor made her unpopular with her neighbors. 她那副丑陋的举止让她在邻居中不受欢迎。|近义词：witchlike, shrewish, unpleasant, disagreeable|反义词：beautiful, pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hag + -ish.  Hag ultimately comes from Old English hægtesse "witch," related to Old High German hagzissa "nightmare, fury."
