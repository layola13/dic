chauvinist: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪst/ | n. | 沙文主义者；盲目爱国者 | A chauvinist is someone who believes that their own group (e.g., country, race, sex) is superior to others. |  He's such a male chauvinist! 他真是个大男子主义者！| synonyms: bigot, jingoist, sexist | antonyms: egalitarian | noun

chauvinists: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪsts/ | n. | 沙文主义者；盲目爱国者（复数） |  Chauvinists often discriminate against people they perceive as inferior. | The chauvinists refused to acknowledge the contributions of other cultures. 这些沙文主义者拒绝承认其他文化的贡献。| synonyms: bigots, jingoists, sexists | antonyms: egalitarians | noun
