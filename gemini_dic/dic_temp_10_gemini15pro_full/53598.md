discal:/ˈdɪskəl/| adj.|盘状的，圆盘状的|Relating to or resembling a disc or disk; shaped like a flat, circular plate.|The discal area of a leaf is its main surface area, excluding the petiole and margins.|近义词：disc-shaped, discoid, circular, round|反义词：linear, elongated|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "discus" (disk, quoit) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).
