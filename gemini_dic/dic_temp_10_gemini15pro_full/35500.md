coadjuting:/koʊˈædʒuːtɪŋ/
adjective
辅助的，协助的
acting as an assistant or helper.
The coadjuting bishop assisted the elderly bishop in his duties.  辅助主教协助年迈的主教履行职责。
近义词: assisting, helping, aiding, supporting
反义词: hindering, obstructing, impeding
词性: adjective

coadjutor:/koʊˈædʒuːtər/
noun
助手，副手，助理
a person who assists another, especially a bishop.
The bishop appointed a coadjutor to help him manage the diocese. 主教任命了一名助手来帮助他管理教区。
近义词: assistant, helper, aide, deputy
反义词: opponent, adversary
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *coadiutor, from Latin coadiuvāre ‘to assist,’ from co- ‘together’ + adiuvāre ‘to help.’
