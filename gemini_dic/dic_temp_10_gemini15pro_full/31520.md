chart:/tʃɑːrt/ (US) /tʃɑːt/ (UK)| n. vt.|图表；排行榜；航海图；绘制图表；记录；跟踪|A graphical representation of data, often using lines, bars, or circles, to show trends or comparisons.  Also, a map used for navigation, especially at sea.  As a verb, it means to make a chart of something or to plan a course of action.|
The company's sales chart showed a steady increase in profits.  公司销售图表显示利润稳步增长。
She charted the progress of her students throughout the semester.  她记录了学生们整个学期的进步。|近义词：graph, diagram, map, plot, record|反义词: (n.) none; (v.) ignore|noun, verb
