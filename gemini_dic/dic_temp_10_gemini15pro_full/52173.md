diapophysial:/ˌdaɪəpəˈfɪziəl/| adj.|横突的，椎体横突的|Relating to or associated with a diapophysis (a transverse process of a vertebra).  It describes structures related to the transverse processes of the vertebrae, which are bony projections extending laterally from the vertebral bodies.|The diapophysial joints are important for spinal stability. 横突关节对脊柱稳定性很重要。|近义词:transverse|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "axial" in some contexts)|词性:adjective
