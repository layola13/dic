nonextortive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɔːtɪv/
adjective
非勒索的，非强取的
Not involving extortion; not obtained by extortion.
The contract was nonextortive, ensuring both parties benefited fairly.  这份合同是非勒索性的，确保双方都能公平受益。
The negotiation process was nonextortive and transparent. 谈判过程是非勒索性的，而且透明。
近义词: fair, just, equitable, reasonable
反义词: extortive, coercive, exploitative
词性: adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "extortive" (related to extortion, from Latin "extorquere" - to twist out, to extort)
