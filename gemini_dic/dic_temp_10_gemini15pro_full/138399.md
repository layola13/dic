notturno:/nɒkˈtʊərnəʊ/ (UK), /nɑːkˈtɜːrnoʊ/ (US)| n.|夜曲；夜景画|A musical composition of a dreamy or pensive character, typically for piano, suggestive of night; a painting of a night scene.|例句：Chopin's nocturnes are some of his most beloved works. 肖邦的夜曲是他最受欢迎的作品之一。

例句：The art gallery featured several nocturnes by the artist.  这家美术馆展出了这位艺术家的几幅夜景画。|近义词：serenade, lullaby|反义词:aubade, diurnal|noun
