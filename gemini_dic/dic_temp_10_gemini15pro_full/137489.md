nontotalitarians:/ˌnɒntəʊˌtælɪˈtɛəriənz/ | n. | 非极权主义者 | People who do not adhere to or support totalitarian principles or systems of government. |  Many nontotalitarians actively protested the regime's oppressive policies. 许多非极权主义者积极抗议该政权的压迫政策。| synonyms: liberals, democrats, libertarians | antonyms: totalitarians, authoritarians, fascists | noun

nontotalitarian:/ˌnɒntəʊˌtælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. | 非极权主义的 | Not characteristic of or related to a totalitarian system of government. | The country transitioned from a totalitarian regime to a nontotalitarian democracy.  这个国家从极权主义政权过渡到非极权主义民主国家。 | synonyms: democratic, liberal, free | antonyms: totalitarian, authoritarian, despotic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "totalitarian" (from "total," referring to complete control).
