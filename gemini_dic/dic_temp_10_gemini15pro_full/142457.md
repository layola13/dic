oscines:/ˈɑːsaɪnz/ | n. |鸣禽|A suborder of perching birds (Passeriformes) characterized by a complex syrinx, allowing for varied and elaborate songs.  They are also known as songbirds.|例句：Oscines are known for their beautiful songs. 鸣禽以其优美的歌声而闻名。

例句：The robin is a common example of an oscine bird. 知更鸟是鸣禽中常见的例子。|近义词：songbirds|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially non-oscine birds like suboscines)|词性:noun
