eczema: /ˈɛɡzɪmə/ /ɪkˈziːmə/ | n. | 湿疹 | An inflammatory condition of the skin characterized by redness, itching, and dryness, sometimes with blisters or weeping lesions. |  My baby has eczema on his cheeks. 我的宝宝脸颊上有湿疹。

Eczema can be triggered by allergies. 湿疹可能是由过敏引起的。

| 近义词: dermatitis, rash | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a medical condition) | 词性: noun
