chrysoberyl:/ˈkrɪsəbəˌrɪl/ | n. |金铍石|A hard, yellow-green gemstone consisting of beryllium aluminate.  It is a variety of beryl that is valued for its hardness and brilliance, and is often used in jewelry.|The chrysoberyl in her ring shimmered in the candlelight. 她戒指上的金铍石在烛光下闪烁。|Synonyms: alexandrite (a variety of chrysoberyl), cat's eye (cymophane, another variety), gem, gemstone |Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | noun
