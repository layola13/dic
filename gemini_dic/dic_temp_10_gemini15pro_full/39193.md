congenialnesses:/kənˈdʒiːniəlɪsnɪsɪz/
| n. |宜人性；意气相投；合情合理|The state or quality of being congenial; agreeableness; pleasantness.  It refers to a feeling of compatibility and shared interests or values. |The congenialnesses of the group made the project a pleasure to work on.  这个团队的融洽氛围使得这个项目开展得非常愉快。|近义词：agreeableness, pleasantness, compatibility, affability, cordiality, geniality|反义词：disagreeableness, unpleasantness, incompatibility, hostility|词性: noun

congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/
| adj. |意气相投的；性格相似的；适宜的；一致的| (of a person) pleasant because of a personality, qualities, or interests that are similar to one's own.  Also, (of a thing) pleasant or agreeable because suited to one's taste or inclination. |We spent a relaxed evening with congenial friends. 我们与志趣相投的朋友们度过了一个轻松的夜晚。 The atmosphere was congenial to creative work.  这个氛围有利于进行创造性工作。|近义词：agreeable, pleasant, compatible, suitable, favorable|反义词：disagreeable, unpleasant, incompatible, unsuitable, unfavorable|词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *congeniālis*, from com- "together" + geniālis "of the same genius/kind," ultimately from gignere "to beget".
