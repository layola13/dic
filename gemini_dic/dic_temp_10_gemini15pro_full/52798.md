diligentness:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəntnəs/
n.
勤奋；勤勉；用功
The quality of being diligent; constant and earnest effort to accomplish what is undertaken; persistent exertion of body or mind.
Her diligentness in her studies resulted in excellent grades. 她学习勤奋，成绩优异。
近义词: assiduity, industry, sedulousness, perseverance
反义词: laziness, indolence, negligence, apathy


diligent:/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/
adj.
勤奋的；勤勉的；用功的
Having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties.
He is a diligent student who always completes his homework. 他是一个勤奋的学生，总是完成作业。
近义词: assiduous, industrious, sedulous, hardworking
反义词: lazy, indolent, negligent, remiss
