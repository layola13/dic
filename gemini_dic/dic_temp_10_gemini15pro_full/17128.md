battering-ram:/ˈbætərɪŋ ræm/| n.|攻城锤; 重型撞门锤|A battering ram is a large, heavy object used to break down walls or doors.|The police used a battering ram to break down the door of the suspected drug dealer's apartment. 警方用攻城锤撞开了嫌疑毒贩公寓的门。|Example: The castle's gate finally gave way under the repeated blows of the battering ram. 城堡的大门最终在攻城锤的反复撞击下倒塌了。|Synonyms: ram, siege engine|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'defense' or 'fortification')|Noun
