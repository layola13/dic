obstructionist:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪst/
noun|妨碍者，阻挠者|a person who deliberately delays or prevents progress, especially in politics.|The senator was accused of being an obstructionist by his opponents.|近义词:opponent, antagonist, blocker, thwarter|反义词:supporter, advocate, facilitator|n.

obstructionist:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪst/
adjective|阻挠的，妨碍的|relating to or characterized by obstructionism.|The opposition party took an obstructionist stance on the new legislation.|近义词:impeding, hindering, blocking|反义词:helpful, cooperative, facilitating|adj.
