fractional:/ˈfrækʃənəl/ (US), /ˈfrækʃnəl/ (UK)| adj.|部分的，分数的，小数的|Relating to or expressed as a fraction; constituting or involving a part or the parts of a whole, rather than the whole.|The price increase was only fractional. 价格上涨只是很小一部分。|Synonyms: partial, incomplete, fragmented, piecemeal|Antonyms: whole, complete, entire|adjective


fractional:/ˈfrækʃənəl/ (US), /ˈfrækʃnəl/ (UK)| n.|分数，小数|A number that is not a whole number, expressed as a ratio or decimal.|The fractional value was less than one.  这个分数的值小于一。|Synonyms: fraction, fragment, portion|Antonyms: whole, integer|noun
