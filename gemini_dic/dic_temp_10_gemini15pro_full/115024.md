mandibulated:/ˈmændɪbjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
有下颌骨的; 具有咀嚼器的
Having mandibles; jawed.
Mandibulated insects such as grasshoppers have strong mouthparts for chewing.  有下颌骨的昆虫，例如蚱蜢，有强壮的口器用于咀嚼。
Synonyms: jawed
Antonyms: unjawed, jawless
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mandibulatus, from mandibula (“jaw, mandible”) + -atus (suffix forming adjectives).
