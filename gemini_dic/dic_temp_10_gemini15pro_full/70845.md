finiteness:/ˈfʌɪnɪtnəs/
n.
有限性；有限；限度
The finiteness of resources is a major concern for environmentalists. 资源的有限性是环保主义者关注的主要问题。
Finiteness of life makes every moment precious.  生命的有限性使得每一刻都弥足珍贵。
近义词: limitedness, finitude, boundedness
反义词: infiniteness, infinity, endlessness
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From finite + -ness.  "Finite" comes from the Latin "fīnītus," past participle of "fīnīre" (to limit, bound, end), from "fīnis" (end, limit, boundary).
