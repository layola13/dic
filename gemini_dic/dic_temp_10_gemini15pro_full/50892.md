desertifies:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪz/
verb
使沙漠化;使荒漠化
To transform (land) into desert by removing vegetation and topsoil, as by excessive farming, overgrazing, or drought.
Overgrazing desertifies pastureland. 过度放牧使牧场沙漠化。
synonyms:aridify, make barren, turn into desert
antonyms:reforest, irrigate
词性:动词
