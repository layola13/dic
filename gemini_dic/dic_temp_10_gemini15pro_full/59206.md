dysphoric:/dɪsˈfɔːrɪk/ /dɪsˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 难受的，烦躁不安的 |  Characterized by dysphoria, a state of unease, dissatisfaction, or general unhappiness.  It often involves feelings of anxiety,  depression, or irritability. | She felt dysphoric after receiving the bad news. 她收到坏消息后感到烦躁不安。|  unhappy, depressed, uneasy, anxious, irritable, melancholic | euphoric, happy, content, comfortable | adjective

dysphoria:/dɪsˈfɔːriə/ /dɪsˈfɔːriə/ | n. | 烦躁不安，精神不适 | A state of profound unease, dissatisfaction, or general unhappiness. It is more than just feeling sad or down; it's a deeper sense of discomfort and emotional distress. | Gender dysphoria is the feeling of discomfort or distress that might occur in people whose gender identity differs from the sex they were assigned at birth. 性别焦虑是指性别认同与其出生时被指定的性别不同的个体可能会产生的不适感或痛苦感。| unease, anxiety, depression, malaise, dissatisfaction | euphoria, happiness, contentment, well-being | noun
