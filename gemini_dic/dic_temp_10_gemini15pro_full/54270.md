disgruntle:/dɪsˈɡrʌnt(ə)l/
verb
使不高兴，使不满
to make someone annoyed and unhappy about something
The employees were disgruntled by the company's new policies.  员工们对公司的新政策感到不满。
The long delays at the airport disgruntled the passengers. 在机场长时间的延误让乘客们很不高兴。
近义词: annoy, irritate, displease, upset
反义词: please, satisfy, content
词性: verb

disgruntled:/dɪsˈɡrʌnt(ə)ld/
adjective
不高兴的，不满的
annoyed and unhappy about something
Many disgruntled customers complained about the poor service. 许多不满的顾客抱怨服务差劲。
He gave a disgruntled sigh. 他不满地叹了口气。
近义词: discontented, dissatisfied, grumpy
反义词: happy, satisfied, contented, pleased
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- + gruntle, frequentative of grunt.  来自dis- + gruntle，grunt的重复形式。  最初的意思是“发出咕哝声”，后来演变成表示不满的情绪。
