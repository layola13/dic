nitrites:/ˈnaɪˌtraɪts/ | n. |亚硝酸盐|A salt or ester of nitrous acid.  Nitrites are often used as preservatives in processed meats.| Sodium nitrite is commonly used in cured meats. 亚硝酸钠通常用于腌制肉类。| synonyms:  None (as a specific chemical compound) | antonyms: None (as a specific chemical compound) | noun
