meditate: /ˈmedɪteɪt/ (US), /ˈmedɪteɪt/ (UK) | v. | 冥想，沉思，考虑 | to think deeply or focus one's mind for a period of time, in silence or with the aid of chanting, for religious or spiritual purposes or as a method of relaxation. |  He meditated for an hour every morning. 他每天早上冥想一个小时。

| She meditated on the meaning of life. 她沉思生命的意义。 |  contemplate, ponder, reflect, muse  |  rush, neglect | verb
