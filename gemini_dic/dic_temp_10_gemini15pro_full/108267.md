laundered:/ˈlɔːndərd/ /ˈlɑːndərd/
| adj. | 洗过的；洗白的（尤指非法所得的金钱） | Concealed the source of illegally obtained money |  The laundered money was then invested in legitimate businesses.  洗白的钱随后被投资于合法生意。 | cleaned, cleansed, purified | dirty, soiled | adjective

launder:/ˈlɔːndər/ /ˈlɑːndər/
| vt. | 洗涤；清洗；洗黑钱 | To wash and iron clothes; to conceal the source of illegally obtained money | He laundered his shirts and socks. 他洗了衬衫和袜子。 She was accused of laundering drug money. 她被指控洗钱。 | clean, cleanse, wash | soil, dirty | verb
