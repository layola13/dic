drinkers:/ˈdrɪŋkərz/| n.|饮酒者；酒鬼|People who consume alcoholic beverages.  Those who habitually drink alcoholic beverages, especially to excess.|例句：The bar was full of drinkers. 酒吧里挤满了喝酒的人。

例句：Heavy drinkers are at increased risk of liver disease.  酗酒者患肝病的风险增加。|近义词：alcoholics, imbiber, tipplers, topers|反义词:teetotalers, abstainers|noun
