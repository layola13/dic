afforded:/əˈfɔːrdɪd/
|v.|提供;给予(机会);买得起|Past tense of afford.  The past participle of afford.|
例句：The program afforded her the opportunity to study abroad.  这个项目让她有机会出国留学。

例句：His salary afforded him a comfortable lifestyle. 他的薪水让他过上了舒适的生活。
|近义词：provide, offer, grant, supply, furnish, give, allow, permit, enable, have the means for, be able to buy|
反义词：deny, refuse, withhold, lack, be unable to afford|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aforten*, from Old French *afurter*, from *a-* (to) + *furter* (to furnish, provide). Ultimately from Latin *fortis* (strong). The original meaning was "to strengthen," later evolving to "to supply" and then "to be able to bear the cost of."
