croon:/kruːn/| v. n.|哼唱；低吟；轻柔地唱|To sing or hum in a soft, sentimental voice.|例句：He crooned a lullaby to his baby daughter. 他轻哼摇篮曲哄他的宝贝女儿。

例句：The singer crooned the romantic ballad. 歌手轻柔地唱着浪漫的民谣。|近义词：hum, sing softly, murmur|反义词：shout, yell|词性：动词，名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scottish and Northern English *crone*, "to utter a low murmuring sound," of imitative origin.
