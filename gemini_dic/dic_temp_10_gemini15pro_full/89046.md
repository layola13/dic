holocrystalline:/ˌhoʊloʊˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | adj. | 全晶质的 | Composed entirely of crystals; having a completely crystalline texture. | The granite sample was determined to be holocrystalline, containing no glassy matrix.  这块花岗岩样本被确定为全晶质的，不包含任何玻璃质基质。 | The rock is holocrystalline, comprised entirely of interlocking crystals. 这块岩石是全晶质的，完全由相互连接的晶体组成。 | crystalline, phaneritic | hypocrystalline, glassy, amorphous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From holo- (Greek: "whole," "entire") + crystalline ("composed of crystals").
