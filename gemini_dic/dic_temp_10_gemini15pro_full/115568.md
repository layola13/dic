marago:/ˈmærəɡoʊ/| n.|马拉戈湖，一种委内瑞拉的朗姆酒|A type of rum from Venezuela, often associated with Lake Maracaibo. |I sipped a glass of aged marago, enjoying its smooth flavor. 我啜饮了一口陈年马拉戈朗姆酒，享受着它顺滑的口感。|近义词：rum, liquor|反义词:None|词性:名词

词源: Marago derives its name from the Maracaibo region of Venezuela, specifically Lake Maracaibo, where sugarcane used in its production is grown.  While not as globally recognized as other rum varieties, it holds local significance.
