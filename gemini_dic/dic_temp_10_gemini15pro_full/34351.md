classers:/ˈklæsərz/ | n. | 分类员，分类器 | People who classify things or people into different categories. | The classers sorted the apples by size and color. 分类员按大小和颜色对苹果进行了分类。|  synonyms: classifiers, sorters, categorizers | antonyms: mergers, combiners | noun
