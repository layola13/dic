distance-education:/ˈdɪstəns ɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |远程教育|Education that takes place without the student being physically present in a traditional classroom setting. It often involves online learning, correspondence courses, or other methods of remote instruction.| Many universities now offer distance-education programs. 许多大学现在都提供远程教育课程。| Online learning, e-learning, correspondence education | Traditional education, face-to-face learning | noun

distance-educations: This is not a standard English word. The word "distance education" is usually used in the singular form, even when referring to multiple programs or instances.  It's grammatically incorrect to pluralize it.
