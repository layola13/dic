autosuggestibility:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪləti/ | n. | 自我暗示性 | The process or condition of uncritically accepting ideas or beliefs, especially about oneself, often leading to changes in behavior or physical state.  The susceptibility to accepting suggestions originating within oneself. |  His remarkable recovery is attributed to his high degree of autosuggestibility.  他显著的康复归功于他高度的自我暗示性。

He used autosuggestibility to overcome his fear of public speaking. 他用自我暗示来克服对公开演讲的恐惧。 | 近义词：self-suggestion, suggestibility | 反义词：resistance to suggestion, skepticism | 词性：noun
