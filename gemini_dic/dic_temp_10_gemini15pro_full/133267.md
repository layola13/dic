nonexhibitions:/ˌnɒnɛksɪˈbɪʃənz/
noun
非展览；非展出（通常用复数形式）
The absence of exhibitions or displays; instances where something is not exhibited or displayed.
Due to the pandemic, many planned nonexhibitions led to financial losses for artists. 由于疫情，许多计划中的非展出给艺术家带来了经济损失。
Museums sometimes store artifacts in nonexhibitions for preservation purposes. 博物馆有时会将文物存储在非展出区以进行保存。
