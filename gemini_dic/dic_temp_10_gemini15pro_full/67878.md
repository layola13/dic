fabricatress:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtrɪs/
n.
女制造者；女编造者
A woman who fabricates or constructs something, especially by assembling parts or materials.
She was a skilled fabricatress of intricate jewelry. 她是一位技艺精湛的复杂珠宝的制作者。
Synonyms: maker, producer, constructor, manufacturer, inventor
Antonyms: destroyer, demolisher
noun
