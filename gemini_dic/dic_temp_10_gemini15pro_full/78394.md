get-around:/ɡɛt əˈraʊnd/ | phrasal verb |四处走动；克服；回避；传播 |To move from place to place, to overcome a problem or obstacle, to circumvent a rule or restriction, or to spread (information or news). |例句：He gets around town on his bicycle. 他骑自行车在城里四处走动。|例句：She found a way to get around the problem. 她找到了解决这个问题的方法。| 近义词：circumvent, bypass, evade, travel, commute, spread | 反义词：stay put, confront, face, suppress | 词性：动词短语

get-around:/ɡɛt əˈraʊnd/ | noun |解决方法；变通方案 | A solution or workaround to a problem. |例句：There's always a get-around if you're clever enough. 如果你足够聪明，总会有解决方法的。|例句：The company found a get-around for the import restrictions. 公司找到了绕过进口限制的办法。 |近义词：workaround, solution, loophole | 反义词：obstacle, problem | 词性：名词
