anarchic: /ænˈɑːrkɪk/ | adj. | 无政府主义的；混乱的；无法无天的 | Lacking order and control; chaotic. |  The country descended into an anarchic state after the coup.  这个国家在政变后陷入了无政府状态。

The classroom was anarchic without a teacher.  没有老师，教室里一片混乱。 | 近义词: lawless, chaotic, disorderly, ungoverned, tumultuous | 反义词: orderly, organized, controlled, lawful, peaceful | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From French *anarchique*, from Ancient Greek *ἀναρχία* (anarkhía, “lack of a ruler”), from *ἀν-* (an-, “without”) + *ἀρχή* (arkhé, “rule, beginning”).
