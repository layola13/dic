diploid:/ˈdɪplɔɪd/ | adj. | 二倍体的 | Having two sets of chromosomes. |  The diploid cell contains two copies of each chromosome. 二倍体细胞包含每条染色体的两个副本。 | synonyms: euploid | antonyms: haploid, monoploid | adjective
