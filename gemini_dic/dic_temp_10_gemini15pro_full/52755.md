dilatation:/ˌdaɪləˈteɪʃn/ /ˌdɪləˈteɪʃn/ | n. |扩张；扩大；膨胀|The widening of something; expansion.  The action of stretching or enlarging an opening or part of the body.|The doctor performed a dilatation of the pupil. 医生进行了瞳孔扩张。|Synonyms: expansion, enlargement, widening, stretching, distension|Antonyms: contraction, compression, constriction|noun

dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ /dɪˈleɪt/ | v. |（使）扩大，（使）膨胀，（使）扩张|Make or become wider, larger, or more open.|Her eyes dilated with fear. 她的眼睛因恐惧而睁大。|Synonyms: expand, enlarge, widen, stretch, distend|Antonyms: contract, compress, constrict|verb
