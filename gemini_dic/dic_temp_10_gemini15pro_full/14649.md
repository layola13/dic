avow:/əˈvaʊ/ | v. |公开承认；公开声明；断言|To declare or admit openly and boldly. |He avowed his love for her. 他公开承认爱她。|近义词：declare, admit, confess, affirm, assert |反义词：deny, disavow, repudiate |词性:动词
词源:From Middle English *avowen, from Old French avoer (“to avow, acknowledge, confess, admit, approve, allow”), from Latin advocare (“to summon, call to witness”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + vocare (“to call”).
