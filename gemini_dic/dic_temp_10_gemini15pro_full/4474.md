albertite: /ˈælbərˌtaɪt/ | n. | 阿尔伯特沥青 | A black, asphalt-like hydrocarbon mineral, a variety of asphaltum, named after Albert County, New Brunswick, Canada, where it was first found.  It is jet-black in color, has a brilliant lustre, and is brittle and readily fusible. | Albertite was an important source of kerosene in the 19th century. 阿尔伯特沥青在19世纪是煤油的重要来源。| Albertite deposits are now largely depleted.  阿尔伯特沥青矿床现在已基本枯竭。 | 近义词: asphaltum, gilsonite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
