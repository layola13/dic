pantograph:/ˈpæn.tə.ɡræf/ /ˈpæn.t̬ə.ɡræf/| n.|缩放仪，受电弓，比例画器|An instrument for copying a drawing or plan on a different scale or for transmitting motion.|The artist used a pantograph to enlarge the sketch.  这位艺术家使用缩放仪放大草图。

The high-speed train's pantograph connected with the overhead wires. 高铁的受电弓与架空电线连接。|近义词: scaler, enlarger, copier (for the drawing instrument); collector (for the train component)|反义词: N/A|noun
