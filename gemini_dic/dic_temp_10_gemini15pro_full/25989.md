bursitis:/bərˈsaɪtɪs/
noun
滑囊炎
Inflammation of a bursa, a small fluid-filled sac that cushions joints.
Example:  She was diagnosed with bursitis in her shoulder. 她被诊断出患有肩部滑囊炎。

Example:  His knee bursitis flared up after he ran the marathon.  他跑完马拉松后膝盖滑囊炎发作了。

近义词:inflammation, tendinitis
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a specific medical condition)
词性:noun
