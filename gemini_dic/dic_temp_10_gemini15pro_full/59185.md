dyslysin:/dɪsˈlaɪsɪn/
n.
消化不良素, 難溶解的樹脂狀物質
A resinous substance formed in the intestine by the decomposition of bile.
Dyslysin is formed in the intestine through bacterial action on bile acids.  消化不良素是由腸道細菌作用於膽汁酸而形成的。
Dyslysin is a poorly understood component of feces. 消化不良素是一種人們知之甚少的糞便成分。
synonym: coprostanol, stercobilin
antonym: None (Dyslysin doesn't have a direct opposite.)
noun
