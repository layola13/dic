multitone:/ˈmʌltiˌtəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmʌltiˌtoʊn/ (US)| adj. n.|多音的，多音调的；多音信号；|Having, using, or producing more than one tone.  A multitone signal is a telecommunications signal composed of multiple frequencies.|The composer used a multitone scale in his latest piece.  作曲家在他的最新作品中使用了多音阶。|The modem transmitted a multitone signal.  调制解调器传输了一个多音信号。|近义词：polyphonic, multifrequency|反义词：monotone, single-tone|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: multi- (many) + tone (sound)
