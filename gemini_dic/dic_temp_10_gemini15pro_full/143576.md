outhousing:/ˈaʊtˌhaʊzɪŋ/
n.
外屋；附属建筑；户外厕所
A building such as a shed or barn that is detached from the main house.
The outhouse needs a new coat of paint. 外屋需要重新粉刷一下。

近义词: outbuilding, shed, annex
反义词: main house, primary residence
词性: 名词
