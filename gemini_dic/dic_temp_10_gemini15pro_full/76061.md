furniment: /ˈfɜːnɪmənt/ | n. | 家具，设备 |  Furnishings; equipment, especially of a house or room.  | The old house lacked furniment and felt empty. 这座老房子缺少家具，感觉空荡荡的。| 近义词: furniture, fittings, furnishings | 反义词: emptiness, bareness | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *furniment, from Anglo-Norman furnement, Old French forneiment, from fornir (“to furnish”).  Ultimately from Frankish *frumjan (“to provide, supply”), from Proto-Germanic *frumjaną (“to perform, accomplish, promote”).
