nonbindingnesses:/ˌnɒnˈbaɪndɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
无约束力；非强制性
The state of being nonbinding; lack of obligation.
The nonbindingnesses of the agreement allowed both parties to withdraw.  该协议的非约束性允许双方退出。
The contract's nonbindingnesses made it essentially useless.  这份合同的非约束性使其基本上毫无用处。
Synonym: optionality, flexibility
Antonym: obligation, commitment
n.
