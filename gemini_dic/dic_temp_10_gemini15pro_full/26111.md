buss:/bʌs/ | n. v.| 公共汽车；亲吻 |A large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road, typically one operating on a fixed route.|  The children took the buss to school. 孩子们坐公共汽车去上学。|  He bussed her on the cheek. 他亲吻了她的脸颊。| 近义词：(n.) coach, bus (v.) kiss, peck | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词, 动词

词源:
(n.) Shortening of omnibus, from Latin *omnibus "for all," dative plural of omnis "all."
(v.)  Back-formation from bus (n.).
