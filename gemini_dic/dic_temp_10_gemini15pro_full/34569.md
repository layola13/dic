cleare: /klɪər/ | adj. v. | 清澈的，清晰的；清除，放晴 |  free from cloudiness or mist; easily seen through; easy to understand; remove any obstruction | The water in the stream was so cleare that you could see the bottom. 小溪里的水清澈见底。| 近义词：transparent, lucid, distinct, obvious, evident,  remove, eliminate, disburden | 反义词：opaque, muddy, unclear, vague, obscure, clutter, fill | 词性: 形容词，动词

词源:  From Middle English *clere, from Old French cler, from Latin clārus (“clear, bright, loud”).
