downlinking:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋkɪŋ/
n.|下行链路；下行传输|The process of transmitting data from a satellite or spacecraft to a ground station.|The satellite was experiencing problems with downlinking data. 卫星在下行数据方面遇到了问题。|近义词:transmission, download|反义词:uplinking|词性:名词

downlink:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋk/
n.|下行链路|A communications link used to transmit data from a satellite or spacecraft to a ground station.|The downlink from the spacecraft was weak. 来自航天器的下行链路很弱。|近义词:transmission channel|反义词:uplink|词性:名词

downlink:/ˌdaʊnˈlɪŋk/
v.|下行传输|To transmit data from a satellite or spacecraft to a ground station.|The scientists downlinked the images from the Hubble telescope. 科学家们将哈勃望远镜的图像下行传输。|近义词:transmit, download|反义词:uplink|词性:动词

词源:  "Downlink" is a combination of "down" and "link".  "Down" indicates the direction of the transmission (from a higher altitude to a lower one), and "link" refers to the communication connection.
