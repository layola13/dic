centrifugence: /ˌsɛntrɪˈfjuːdʒəns/ | n. | 离心力，离心作用 | The process of separating substances of different densities by the application of centrifugal force. |  The centrifuge uses centrifagence to separate blood components. 离心机利用离心力分离血液成分。| synonym: centrifugation | antonym: centripetence | noun

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ | n.  v. | 离心机，离心分离机；用离心机分离 | A machine that spins rapidly to separate substances of different densities.  To separate substances using a centrifuge. |  The lab technician used a centrifuge to separate the plasma from the blood. 实验室技术员使用离心机从血液中分离血浆。 The mixture was centrifuged at high speed. 混合物被高速离心。| synonym: separator, whirl | antonym: N/A | noun, verb
