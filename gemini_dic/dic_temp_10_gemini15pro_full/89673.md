hoopless:/ˈhuːpləs/| adj.|没有希望的，绝望的|Without hope; despairing.  Derived from "hoop" (referring to a ring or circle, symbolizing completeness or success) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  The original sense likely related to games involving hoops, where missing the hoop meant failure.|The team was hoopless after losing their star player.  该队在失去他们的明星球员后彻底绝望了。|hopeless, desperate, despairing|hopeful, optimistic|adjective
