panelist:/ˈpænəlɪst/| n.|陪审员|A member of a jury.|The panelists deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict. 陪审员商议了几个小时才达成裁决。|synonyms: juror, jury member|antonyms: judge, defendant|noun
