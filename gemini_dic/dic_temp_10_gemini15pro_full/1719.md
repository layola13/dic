acquaint:/əˈkweɪnt/| vt.|使熟悉，使认识|To make someone aware of or familiar with something.|I acquainted myself with the rules of the game before playing. 我在玩游戏之前熟悉了游戏规则。

I acquainted him with the details of the case. 我让他了解了案件的细节。|近义词：familiarize, inform, introduce|反义词: estrange, alienate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acointer*, from Vulgar Latin *accognitare*, frequentative of Latin *cognoscere* "to get to know," from *co-* "together" + *gnoscere* "to know."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I acquaint myself with new technologies. = 我使自己熟悉新技术。
He acquaints his students with the history of art. = 他让他的学生了解艺术史。

一般过去时:
She acquainted me with her family. = 她把我介绍给了她的家人。
They acquainted themselves with the local customs. = 他们熟悉了当地的风俗习惯。

现在完成时:
I have acquainted myself with the new software. = 我已经熟悉了这个新软件。
We have acquainted him with the terms of the contract. = 我们已经让他了解了合同条款。

过去完成时:
He had acquainted himself with the city before moving there. = 在搬去之前，他已经熟悉了这座城市。
They had acquainted us with their plans before the meeting. = 在会议之前，他们已经把他们的计划告诉了我们。

将来时:
I will acquaint you with the details later. = 我稍后会告诉你细节。
She will acquaint herself with the procedures before starting the job. =  她在开始工作之前会熟悉一下程序。


被动语态:
The new employees were acquainted with the company's policies. = 新员工被告知了公司的政策。
I was acquainted with the news by a friend. = 我是由一个朋友告知这个消息的。

PHRASE->
acquaint oneself with = 使自己熟悉…
acquaint someone with something = 使某人了解某事
be acquainted with = 熟悉…
become acquainted with = 开始熟悉…
get acquainted with = 认识；熟悉
