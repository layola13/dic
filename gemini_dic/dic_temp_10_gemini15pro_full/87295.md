hermitary:/ˌhɜːrmɪˈtɛri/
adjective
隐居的；遁世的；隐士的
Relating to or characteristic of a hermit; solitary; secluded.
The hermitary life of a monk.  僧侣隐居的生活。
Synonyms: reclusive, secluded, solitary, withdrawn
Antonyms: gregarious, sociable, outgoing
词性: adjective
