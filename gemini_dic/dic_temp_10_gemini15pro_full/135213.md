nonoverheads:/ˌnɒnˈəʊvəˌhedz/ | n. | 非间接费用 | Costs that are not related to general business expenses like rent, utilities, or administrative salaries.  These are typically direct costs related to a specific project or product. | The nonoverheads for this project include materials and labor.  这个项目的非间接费用包括材料和人工。| direct costs, specific expenses | overheads, indirect costs | noun
