downer:/ˈdaʊnər/| n.|令人沮丧的事; 沉闷的经历; 镇静剂|Something that causes a depressed feeling; a depressing experience; a sedative.|The news of the layoffs was a real downer. 裁员的消息真是令人沮丧。|Synonyms: depressant, bummer, drag|Antonyms: upper, pick-me-up|noun

downer:/ˈdaʊnər/| adj.|(非正式)令人沮丧的| (informal) depressing or disappointing|The rainy weather made the whole trip a downer.  阴雨天气让整个旅程都很令人沮丧。|Synonyms: depressing, disappointing|Antonyms: uplifting, encouraging|adjective
