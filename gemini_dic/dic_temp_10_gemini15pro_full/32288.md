chiefry:/ˈtʃiːfri/
noun
领地;管辖范围;统治权
A position of authority or control over a certain area or domain.
The king's chiefry extended throughout the land. 国王的统治范围遍及全国。
dominion, sovereignty, authority
subservience, dependence
