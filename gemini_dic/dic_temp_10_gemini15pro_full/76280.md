USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么值得这个奖？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie started? 电影开始前他们吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the century? 到本世纪末会发生什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？
