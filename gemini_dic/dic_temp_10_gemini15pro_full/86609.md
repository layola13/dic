heliosphere:/ˈhiːliəsfɪər/ | n. | 日球层 | The region of space, encompassing the solar system, in which the solar wind has a significant influence. |  The Voyager 1 spacecraft crossed the boundary of the heliosphere and entered interstellar space. 旅行者1号宇宙飞船穿越了日球层的边界，进入了星际空间。

The heliosphere protects the planets from much of the harmful radiation from interstellar space. 日球层保护行星免受星际空间的大部分有害辐射。| 近义词: None (This is a specific scientific term)| 反义词: interstellar space 星际空间 | 词性: noun
