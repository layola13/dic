houndier:/ˈhaʊndɪər/| adj.|更像猎犬的；更热衷于追捕的|More like a hound; more eager to hunt or pursue relentlessly.|The police were houndier than ever after the robbery. 抢劫案发生后，警方比以往任何时候都更加穷追不舍。|近义词：more persistent, more tenacious, more relentless|反义词：less persistent, less tenacious, less relentless|词性:形容词

Etymology: From hound + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hundaz.
