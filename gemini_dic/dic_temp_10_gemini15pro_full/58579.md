dulcify:/ˈdʌlsɪfaɪ/ /ˈdʊl-/| vt.|使甜蜜；使悦耳；使温和；减轻|to make something sweeter or more pleasant|例句：The government tried to dulcify the bad news with promises of tax cuts. 政府试图以减税的承诺来缓和这个坏消息。

例句：He dulcified the bitter medicine with honey. 他用蜂蜜使苦药变甜。

|近义词：sweeten, mellow, soften, mitigate|反义词：exacerbate, worsen, aggravate|词性：动词
