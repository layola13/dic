erosion:/ɪˈroʊʒən/ (US) /ɪˈrəʊʒən/ (UK)| n.|侵蚀，腐蚀|The gradual destruction or diminution of something.  The process by which the surface of the earth is worn away by the action of water, glaciers, winds, waves, etc.|The erosion of the coastline is a serious problem. 海岸线的侵蚀是一个严重的问题。|近义词：corrosion, attrition, wearing away, degradation|反义词：accretion, deposition, building up|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *erosioun*, from Late Latin *erosiō*, from Latin *erodere* "to gnaw away," from *e-* "out" + *rodere* "to gnaw."
