expensive: /ɪkˈspɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 昂贵的，花钱多的 | Costing a lot of money. |  This car is very expensive. 这辆车很贵。

The diamond ring was too expensive for me. 这枚钻戒对我来说太贵了。| 近义词: costly, dear, pricey | 反义词: cheap, inexpensive, affordable | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French expensif, -ive, from Latin expens- ‘paid out’, from the verb expendere.
