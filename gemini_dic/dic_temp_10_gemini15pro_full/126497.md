myxoedema:/ˌmɪksɪˈdiːmə/ (US) /ˌmɪksɔɪˈdiːmə/ (UK)| n. |粘液性水肿|A condition characterized by swelling of the skin and underlying tissues, giving a waxy consistency, due to severe prolonged hypothyroidism. It's often marked by sluggishness, slow speech, mental impairment, and sensitivity to cold.|例句：The patient presented with classic symptoms of myxoedema, including a puffy face and slow reflexes.  病人表现出典型的粘液性水肿症状，包括面部浮肿和反射迟钝。

例句：Myxoedema can be a serious complication of untreated hypothyroidism. 粘液性水肿可能是未经治疗的甲状腺功能减退症的严重并发症。|近义词：hypothyroidism (though this is the underlying cause, not the condition itself), non-pitting edema|反义词: hyperthyroidism (opposite underlying cause)|词性:noun
