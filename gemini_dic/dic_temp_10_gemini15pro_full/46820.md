dantesque:/dænˈtesk/ | adj. | 但丁式的，地狱般的，可怕的 | Resembling or suggestive of the Inferno of Dante, especially in its grimness or grotesqueness.  Characterized by nightmarish imagery, suffering, and moral or spiritual corruption, often on a grand scale. | The film portrayed a dantesque vision of a post-apocalyptic world. 这部电影描绘了世界末日后地狱般的景象。 | The refugees described dantesque scenes of suffering and despair in the war-torn city. 难民们描述了饱受战争蹂躏的城市里如同地狱般痛苦和绝望的景象。 |  apocalyptic, infernal, hellish, nightmarish | pleasant, heavenly, idyllic | adjective
