nonhospitals:/nɒnˈhɒspɪtəlz/ |n.| 非医院机构 | Institutions that provide healthcare services outside of a traditional hospital setting.  These can include clinics, nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, and other facilities. | Many patients now receive care in nonhospitals due to lower costs and greater convenience. 许多病人现在在非医院机构接受治疗，因为成本更低，也更方便。| synonyms: outpatient facilities, community health centers, alternative care facilities | antonyms: hospitals | noun
