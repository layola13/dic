blinkers:/ˈblɪŋkərz/ (US), /ˈblɪŋkəz/ (UK)| n.| 遮眼罩；转向灯|pieces of leather etc that are put beside a horse's eyes so that it can only see forwards|The horse was fitted with blinkers so it wouldn't be startled by the crowds.|Synonyms: blinders (US), winkers (UK)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

blinkers:/ˈblɪŋkərz/ (US), /ˈblɪŋkəz/ (UK)| n.| 局限性；狭隘的观点|something that prevents someone from noticing or understanding something|He needs to take off his blinkers and see the bigger picture.|Synonyms: limitations, blind spots|Antonyms: open-mindedness, broad perspective|noun
