maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ (US), /məˈnuːvə/ (UK)| n. v.|策略；调动；操控|A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care.|The driver maneuvered the car into a tight parking space. 司机熟练地把车停进了一个狭小的停车位。|Synonyms: tactic, strategy, operation, ploy, scheme|Antonyms: inaction, idleness|noun, verb

maneuvers:/məˈnuːvərz/ (US), /məˈnuːvəz/ (UK)| n. |策略；调动；操控（复数）|A series of movements or actions, especially in military or naval contexts, designed to achieve a specific objective.|The army conducted complex maneuvers in the desert. 军队在沙漠中进行了复杂的演习。|Synonyms: tactics, strategies, operations|Antonyms: inaction, idleness|noun
