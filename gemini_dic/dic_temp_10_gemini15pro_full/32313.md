chigoe:/ˈtʃɪɡoʊ/ | n. |沙蚤，沙蝇|A small flea-like insect (Tunga penetrans) found in tropical and subtropical regions, especially in Africa and South America. The female burrows into the skin, causing intense itching and inflammation. | The chigoe burrowed into her foot, causing a painful sore.  沙蚤钻进了她的脚里，引起疼痛的溃疡。 |Synonyms: jigger, sand flea|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

chigoe:/ˈtʃɪɡoʊ/ | n. |沙蚤叮咬，沙蚤病|The skin irritation and inflammation caused by a chigoe. | He suffered from a severe case of chigoe after walking barefoot on the beach.  他在沙滩上赤脚行走后，得了严重的沙蚤病。|Synonyms: jigger bite, tungiasis|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
