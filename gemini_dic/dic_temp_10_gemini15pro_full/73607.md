foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/ (US) /ˈfɔːwəd/ (UK)| n.|前言，序言|An introductory section of a book, typically written by someone other than the author.|Example:  I always read the foreword to get a sense of the book's purpose. 我总是读前言来了解这本书的意图。

Example: The foreword was written by a renowned expert in the field.  这本前言是由该领域一位著名专家撰写的。|近义词：preface, introduction, prologue|反义词: afterword, epilogue|词性:名词
