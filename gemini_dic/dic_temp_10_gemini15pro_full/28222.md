capsaicin:/kæpˈseɪsɪn/ | n. | 辣椒素 | A colorless, crystalline, pungent compound, C18H27NO3, found in various capsicums, especially chili peppers, and used medicinally as a counterirritant and analgesic.  It is the active component of chili peppers, which are plants belonging to the genus Capsicum. | Capsaicin is the chemical that makes chili peppers hot. 辣椒素是使辣椒具有辣味的化学物质。

Capsaicin creams can be used to relieve muscle pain. 辣椒素乳膏可以用来缓解肌肉疼痛。 | synonyms: chili pepper extract,  capsicum oleoresin | antonyms: None (There isn't a direct antonym for a specific chemical compound.) | noun
