circuit:/ˈsɜːrkɪt/ (US), /ˈsɜːkɪt/ (UK)| n.|电路；回路；环行；巡回；赛车道|A complete path through which an electric current can flow.  A roughly circular line, route, or movement that starts and finishes at the same place. A regular series of places visited or travelled to, especially by a judge or preacher, or by a sports team. |The circuit board in my computer needs replacing. 我的电脑里的电路板需要更换。|synonyms: lap, loop, tour, track, course|antonyms: straight line|noun


circuit:/ˈsɜːrkɪt/ (US), /ˈsɜːkɪt/ (UK)| v.|绕...环行|To go around something. |The race car circuited the track at high speed. 赛车高速环绕赛道行驶。|synonyms: circle, circumnavigate, orbit|antonyms: stop, halt|verb
