misalleges:/ˌmɪsəˈlɛdʒɪz/ | v. |错误地断言；诬称；歪曲 |To assert incorrectly; to allege falsely; to misrepresent. |The lawsuit misalleges the facts of the case.  这起诉讼歪曲了案件的事实。

The defendant misalleges that the plaintiff was present at the scene of the crime. 被告错误地断言原告出现在犯罪现场。 |近义词：misrepresents, distorts, falsifies, misstates |反义词：states correctly, represents accurately, reports truthfully |词性：动词
