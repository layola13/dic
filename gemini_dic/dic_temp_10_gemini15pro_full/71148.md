fishwoman:/ˈfɪʃˌwʊmən/| n. |鱼贩，卖鱼的女人|A woman who sells fish, especially in a market.|例句：The fishwoman at the market had the freshest catch of the day.  市场上的鱼贩有当天最新鲜的渔获。

例句：The fishwoman's loud calls attracted customers to her stall.  鱼贩的大声叫卖吸引了顾客到她的摊位。|近义词：fishmonger, fish seller|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps fish buyer) |词性:noun
