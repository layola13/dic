barrenness:/ˈbærənnəs/
n.
不孕，贫瘠，荒芜
The state of being barren; unproductiveness; infertility; sterility.
The barrenness of the desert landscape was striking.  沙漠景观的荒芜令人印象深刻。

The farmer was concerned about the barrenness of his fields after the drought.  干旱过后，农民担心他的田地会变得贫瘠。

sterility, infertility, unproductiveness, aridity, desolation
fertility, fruitfulness, productiveness, fecundity
noun
