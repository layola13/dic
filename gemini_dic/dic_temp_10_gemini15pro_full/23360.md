brace:/breɪs/| n. vt.|支撑物，支架；牙套；括号；准备，预备|A device that clamps things tightly together or that gives support to a weak or injured body part.  To prepare yourself physically or mentally for something unpleasant or difficult.|The doctor put a brace on her injured knee. 医生在她的受伤膝盖上戴了一个支架。|He braced himself for the impact. 他做好准备迎接撞击。|近义词：support, prop, prepare, strengthen|反义词: weaken|noun, verb
