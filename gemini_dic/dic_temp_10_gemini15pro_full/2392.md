adiposity:/ˌædɪˈpɒsɪti/ /ˌædɪˈpɑːsɪti/
n.|肥胖，脂肪过多|The state of being fat; obesity.  The condition of having excessive body fat.|Childhood adiposity is a growing problem. 儿童肥胖是一个日益严重的问题。

The study examined the link between adiposity and heart disease. 这项研究调查了肥胖与心脏病之间的联系。|近义词：obesity, corpulence, plumpness|反义词：leanness, thinness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adipōsitās*  ("fatness, fleshiness"), from *adipōsus* ("fatty, greasy"), from *adeps* ("fat, grease").

USAGE->
The word "adiposity" is primarily used in formal or scientific contexts.  Here are some examples of its usage in different sentence structures:

Statements:
Adiposity is a major risk factor for several chronic diseases. = 肥胖是几种慢性疾病的主要风险因素。

Increased adiposity is often associated with a poor diet and lack of exercise. =  肥胖增加通常与不良饮食和缺乏锻炼有关。


Questions:
Does childhood adiposity increase the risk of adult obesity? =  儿童肥胖会增加成年肥胖的风险吗？


What are the long-term health consequences of abdominal adiposity? = 腹部肥胖的长期健康后果是什么？


Negations:
The study found no significant correlation between adiposity and cognitive decline. =  该研究发现肥胖与认知能力下降之间没有显着相关性。

Not all individuals with increased adiposity develop metabolic complications. =  并非所有肥胖增加的个体都会出现代谢并发症。



Comparisons:
Visceral adiposity is considered more dangerous than subcutaneous adiposity. = 内脏脂肪比皮下脂肪更危险。

The prevalence of adiposity is higher in developed countries compared to developing countries. = 发达国家的肥胖率高于发展中国家。


Conditional Sentences:
If adiposity is not addressed, it can lead to serious health problems. = 如果不解决肥胖问题，可能会导致严重的健康问题。

Individuals with high levels of adiposity are more likely to develop type 2 diabetes. = 肥胖程度高的人更容易患上 2 型糖尿病。
