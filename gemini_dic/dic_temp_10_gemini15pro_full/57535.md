drainage:/ˈdreɪnɪdʒ/| n.|排水; 排水系统; 排出物; 排出液|The drainage system is crucial for flood control. 排水系统对防洪至关重要。|The drainage of the swamp helped reduce mosquito populations.  沼泽的排水有助于减少蚊子数量。|近义词:draining, outflow, runoff|反义词: flooding, inundation|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *drenage, from drainer (to drain), from Vulgar Latin *drenāre, from Latin drēnare (to drain), from dē- (down, away) + rīvus (stream).
