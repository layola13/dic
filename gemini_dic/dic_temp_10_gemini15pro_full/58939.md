dust-jacket: /ˈdʌst ˌdʒækɪt/
n.
书衣，护封，防尘套
A protective cover, usually made of paper, that fits over the binding of a book.
The dust-jacket was torn, but the book itself was in perfect condition. 书衣破了，但书本身完好无损。
synonym: book jacket, cover
antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unprotected binding")
词性: noun


dust-jacket: /ˈdʌst ˌdʒækɪt/
noun
书的护封；防尘套

He carefully removed the dust-jacket before reading the book. 他在读书前小心翼翼地取下护封。
synonym: book jacket
antonym: (No direct antonym)
