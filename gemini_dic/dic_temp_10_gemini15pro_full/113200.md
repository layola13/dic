lurker:/ˈlɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈlɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 潜伏者，窥探者 |Someone who reads the postings of an online discussion group, such as a newsgroup or an online forum, but rarely or never participates in the discussions.|He's a known lurker on several online forums. 他是几个在线论坛上有名的潜水者。|近义词：observer, onlooker, reader|反义词：participant, contributor|词性: noun

lurker:| n. | 潜伏者，窥探者 |A person who waits hidden in order to attack or do something harmful.|The police caught the lurker hiding in the bushes. 警察抓住了潜伏在灌木丛中的歹徒。|近义词：prowler, stalker|反义词： |词性: noun
