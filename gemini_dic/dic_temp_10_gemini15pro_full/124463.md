mottoes:/ˈmɑːtoʊz/
noun
座右铭；格言；箴言
A short sentence or phrase chosen as encapsulating the beliefs or ideals of an individual, family, or institution.
The school's motto is "Learn, Grow, Succeed." 学校的座右铭是“学习，成长，成功”。
Their family motto is "Honesty is the best policy".  他们家族的座右铭是“诚实是上策”。
