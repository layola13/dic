outcatches:/ˌaʊtˈkætʃɪz/
verb
胜过；捕获更多
Surpasses in catching; catches more than.
例句：The experienced fisherman outcatches his younger rival. 经验丰富的渔夫比他年轻的对手捕获更多。
例句：Their new fishing net outcatches all the others. 他们的新渔网比所有其他渔网捕获更多。
近义词：outperforms, surpasses, excels
反义词：loses to, is surpassed by
词性：v.
