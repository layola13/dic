hopple:/ˈhɒpəl/ (UK) /ˈhɑːpəl/ (US)| v. n.|捆绑（动物）的后腿，跳跃, 束缚 |To tie or fasten the legs of (an animal, especially a horse, dog, or sheep) together, typically with a short rope, to prevent it from straying or kicking; a rope or strap used for this purpose.  A hobble; a fetter.|The farmer hoppled the horse to prevent it from wandering off. 农民把马的腿捆起来，防止它走失。| 近义词：fetter, shackle, restrain, tie, tether |反义词: untie, release, free|词性: verb, noun


hopple:/ˈhɒpəl/ (UK) /ˈhɑːpəl/ (US)| n.|跳跃 |A hobble; an awkward, limping gait.|His injured ankle caused him to walk with a hopple. 他受伤的脚踝使他走路一瘸一拐。| 近义词：limp, hobble |反义词: stride, run|词性: noun
