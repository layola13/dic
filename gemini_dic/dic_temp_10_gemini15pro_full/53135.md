diplomacies: /dɪˈploʊməsiz/ | n. |外交，外交手段| the art and practice of conducting negotiations between nations; skill in handling affairs without raising hostility | The United States engaged in shuttle diplomacies to resolve the crisis. 美国通过穿梭外交来解决危机。 | synonyms: negotiation, tact, statesmanship | antonyms: warfare, hostility, aggression | noun

diplomacy: /dɪˈploʊməsi/ | n. |外交，外交手腕| the profession, activity, or skill of managing international relations, typically by a country's representatives abroad. |  She displayed considerable diplomacy in handling the sensitive situation. 她在处理敏感局势方面表现出了相当的外交手腕。| synonyms: tact, finesse, discretion | antonyms:  bluntness, clumsiness, indiscretion | noun
