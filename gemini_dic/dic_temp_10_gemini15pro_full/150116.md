pamperedly:/ˈpæmpərdli/
| adv. |娇惯地，溺爱地|In a pampered manner; being treated with excessive indulgence.|She grew up pamperedly, never having to lift a finger.| 近义词：indulgently, luxuriously|反义词：harshly, austerely|词性:副词


词源:  From the verb "pamper" (meaning to treat with excessive indulgence), which comes from the Middle Dutch word "pamperen" (to cram, stuff). The "-edly" suffix transforms the verb into an adverb.
