ether:/ˈiːθər/ (US) /ˈiːθə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 乙醚；以太；天空，苍穹 |A colorless, volatile, highly flammable liquid, C₂H₅OC₂H₅, used as an anesthetic and as a solvent.|The patient was given ether before the operation. 病人在手术前被给予了乙醚。|Synonyms: anesthetic, solvent|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ether:/ˈiːθər/ (US) /ˈiːθə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 以太；天空，苍穹|the upper regions of air beyond the clouds.|The rocket soared into the ether. 火箭飞入太空。|Synonyms: sky, heavens, firmament|Antonyms: earth, ground|noun
