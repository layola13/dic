chinkle:/ˈtʃɪŋkəl/| n.|鹅卵石，碎石|small, rounded pebbles, especially ones found on a beach or in a riverbed|The chinkle of the stream was soothing.  溪流中鹅卵石的叮当声令人舒缓。|Synonyms: pebble, gravel, stone|Antonyms: boulder, rock|noun


chinkle:/ˈtʃɪŋkəl/| v.|发出叮当声|to make a light, ringing sound, like small pebbles hitting together|The coins chinkled in his pocket. 硬币在他的口袋里叮当作响。|Synonyms: jingle, clink, tinkle|Antonyms: clang, bang|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely imitative of the sound of small pebbles hitting together.  Possibly related to "clink."
