fly-by:/ˈflaɪˌbaɪ/ | n. | 飞越；掠过；顺便访问 | A flight past a point, especially a close approach to a planet or moon by a spacecraft to obtain scientific information. |  The spacecraft made a fly-by of Jupiter and collected valuable data.  宇宙飞船飞越木星并收集了宝贵的数据。| 近义词: flypast, pass | 反义词: landing | 词性: 名词


fly-by:/ˈflaɪˌbaɪ/ | adj. |  顺便的；路过的；飞越的 | Done or provided while passing by a place. | We made a fly-by visit to our grandparents' house on our way home.  我们在回家的路上顺便拜访了祖父母家。 | 近义词: cursory, quick, brief | 反义词: thorough, detailed | 词性: 形容词
