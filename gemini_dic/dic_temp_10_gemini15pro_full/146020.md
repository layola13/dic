overdrained:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnd/
adjective
过度排水的；过度消耗的
Excessively drained of resources, especially water or nutrients; also, figuratively, exhausted or depleted.
The overdrained soil was unable to support the crops.  过度排水的土壤无法支撑农作物生长。
The team was overdrained after the grueling competition.  艰苦的比赛之后，团队精疲力竭。
Synonyms: depleted, exhausted, impoverished, spent
Antonyms: replenished, restored, invigorated
词性: 形容词
