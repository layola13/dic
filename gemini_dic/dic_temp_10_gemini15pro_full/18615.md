bent's:/bɛnts/
noun
癖好，倾向，天赋
a natural talent or inclination
He has a bent for music. 他有音乐天赋。
Synonym: inclination, aptitude, talent, gift, knack, propensity
Antonym: aversion, disinclination
n.

bent's:/bɛnts/
noun
(尤指古怪的) 爱好，嗜好
a liking or preference (especially for something unusual)
He has a bent for collecting old coins. 他有收集古钱币的嗜好。
Synonym: predilection, penchant, fondness, liking, taste
Antonym: dislike, aversion
n.
