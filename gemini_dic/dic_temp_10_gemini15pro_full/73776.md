formalists:/ˈfɔːməlɪsts/| n.|形式主义者|People who adhere rigidly to prescribed forms, rules, or conventions, often at the expense of substance or meaning.|The formalists criticized the poet for his unconventional use of rhyme.|近义词：sticklers, traditionalists, purists|反义词: innovators, rebels, nonconformists|noun
