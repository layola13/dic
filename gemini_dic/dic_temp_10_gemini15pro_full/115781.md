mariolater:/ˌmɛəriˈɒlətər/ | n. | 圣母崇拜者；过度崇敬圣母玛利亚的人 | A person who excessively venerates the Virgin Mary. | He was accused of being a mariolater for his extreme devotion to Mary. 他因其对玛利亚的极度虔诚而被指责为圣母崇拜者。| 近义词:Mariologist (a scholar of Mariology), devotee of Mary | 反义词: iconoclast, critic of Marian devotion | 词性: noun
