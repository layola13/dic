overcultivated: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
过度耕作的；过度开发的
Excessively cultivated; cultivated to the point of exhaustion of the soil.  Land that has been farmed too intensively, leading to reduced fertility.
The overcultivated fields yielded a poor harvest this year.  今年过度耕作的田地收成很差。
The once fertile valley is now overcultivated and barren.  曾经肥沃的山谷现在过度耕作，变得贫瘠。
近义词: overfarmed, exhausted, depleted
反义词:  uncultivated, fallow, fertile
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "cultivated" (past participle of "cultivate," from Latin "cultivare," meaning "to till, cultivate")
