absorption:/əbˈsɔːrpʃn/ /əbˈzɔːrpʃn/
noun
吸收; 吸收过程; 全神贯注; 专心致志
The process by which one substance absorbs or is absorbed by another.
Plants get their nutrients through absorption from the soil.  植物通过从土壤中吸收养分来获取营养。

The artist's complete absorption in his work. 艺术家完全沉浸在他的作品中。

近义词: assimilation, intake, uptake, incorporation
反义词: desorption, release, emission
词性: noun
