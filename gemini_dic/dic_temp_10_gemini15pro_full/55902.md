divorcible:/dɪˈvɔːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可离婚的，可解除婚姻的 | Capable of being divorced; allowing a divorce to take place. |  The marriage was deemed divorcible due to irreconcilable differences.  这桩婚姻由于不可调和的分歧被认为是可以离婚的。

The contract makes the partnership divorcible under certain conditions.  该合同规定，在某些条件下，合伙关系可以解除。 | synonyms: separable, dissolvable | antonyms: indissoluble, inseparable | adjective
