nonarchitectural:/ˌnɒnˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəl/
adjective
非建筑的；与建筑无关的
Not related to architecture, not having the characteristics of architecture, or not designed by an architect.
The museum's nonarchitectural exhibits, such as furniture and textiles, are equally fascinating.  博物馆的非建筑展品，例如家具和纺织品，同样引人入胜。
The garden featured a nonarchitectural sculpture made of recycled materials.  花园里有一个用回收材料制成的非建筑雕塑。
Synonyms: non-architectural, nonbuilding
Antonyms: architectural
词性: adjective
