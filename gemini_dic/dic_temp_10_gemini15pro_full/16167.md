bankeress:/ˈbæŋkərɪs/ | n. | 女银行家；女性银行从业人员 | A woman who works in or owns a bank. |  例句：The bankeress handled the large transaction with ease. 女银行家轻松地处理了这笔大额交易。

例句：Her grandmother was a renowned bankeress in the city. 她的祖母是城里一位著名的女银行家。

近义词：female banker, woman banker | 反义词：(No direct antonym, but could use "banker" if referring specifically to a male banker) | 词性：noun

Etymology:
From "banker" + "-ess" (a suffix traditionally used to form feminine nouns). "Banker" ultimately comes from Old Italian "banca" (bench, money-changer's table), from Old High German "bank" (bench).
