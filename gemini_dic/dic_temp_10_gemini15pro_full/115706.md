margined:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnd/| adj.|有边的，有边缘的；页边有空白的；（证券交易）保证金交易的|Having a margin or border; having blank space around the edge of a page; (of securities trading) transacted on margin.|The margined pages of the old book were yellowed with age. 这本古书有页边空白的页面已经泛黄了。|近义词：bordered, edged, framed, marginalia|反义词:unbordered, edgeless|词性:形容词
词源: From the noun "margin" + "-ed" suffix. Ultimately from Latin "margo" (margin, edge).
