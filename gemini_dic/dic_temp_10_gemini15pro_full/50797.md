descendingly:/dɪˈsendɪŋli/ | adv. | 下降地，递减地 | in a descending manner; from highest to lowest | The numbers are arranged descendingly from 100 to 1. 数字从100到1递减排列。| 近义词: decreasingly | 反义词: ascendingly, increasingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From descend (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Descend" comes from Latin "descendere" (to climb down), from "de-" (down) + "scandere" (to climb).
