dcms:/ˈdiː siː ɛmˈɛs/ | n. | 数据中心管理系统 (Data Center Management System) | A software suite used for managing and monitoring IT infrastructure within a data center.  It typically includes features like power and cooling management, asset tracking, and performance monitoring. |  The new dcms allows us to monitor server temperatures remotely.  新的数据中心管理系统允许我们远程监控服务器温度。 | synonyms:  data center infrastructure management (DCIM), IT infrastructure management | antonyms: N/A | noun
