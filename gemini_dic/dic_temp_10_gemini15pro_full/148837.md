overvote:/ˌoʊvərˈvoʊt/| n.|过度投票，超额投票|An overvote occurs when a voter chooses more candidates than permitted for a particular office. |Example: The ballot was rejected due to an overvote in the presidential race.  选票因总统竞选中的过度投票而被拒绝。|Example:  Overvotes can happen accidentally when a voter is confused by the ballot design. 过度投票可能会在选民对选票设计感到困惑时意外发生。|近义词:multiple votes, ballot error|反义词:undervote|noun
