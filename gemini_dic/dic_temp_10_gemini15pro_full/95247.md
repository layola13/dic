incandescent:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/| adj.|白炽的；灿烂的；发光的|emitting light as a result of being heated:  characterized by ardent emotion or intensity|The incandescent bulb glowed brightly. 白炽灯泡发出明亮的光芒。|近义词：glowing, luminous, radiant, bright, brilliant|反义词：dull, dark, dim|词性:形容词
incandescence:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsəns/| n.|白炽；炽热；光辉|the state of emitting light as a result of being heated: a state of emotional intensity|The incandescence of the molten metal was mesmerizing. 熔融金属的白炽状态令人着迷。|近义词：glow, luminosity, radiance, brightness, brilliance|反义词：dullness, darkness, dimness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin incandescere "to glow, become white-hot," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + candescere "to become white, glow," from candere "to shine, be white, glow."
