mundungus:/mʌnˈdʌŋɡəs/| n.|劣质烟草；令人讨厌的气味；可疑的人|A strong, offensive smell, typically of stale tobacco smoke;  A person of dubious character.|The room reeked of mundungus. 房间里充满了劣质烟草的味道。|Synonyms: stench, stink, reek, bad smell, unpleasant odor, foul odor, whiff, aroma (often used ironically), mephitis, effluvium|Antonyms: fragrance, perfume, scent, aroma (positive sense)|noun
mundungus:/mʌnˈdʌŋɡəs/| n.|可疑的人；不诚实的人|A person of dubious character, especially one who deals in stolen goods.|The shady mundungus slipped into the alleyway.  那个可疑的人溜进了小巷。|Synonyms: rogue, scoundrel, rascal, villain, miscreant, ne'er-do-well, good-for-nothing, lowlife, shady character|Antonyms: saint, paragon, role model, upright citizen|noun
