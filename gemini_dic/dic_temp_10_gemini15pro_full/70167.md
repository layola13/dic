fiberless:/ˈfaɪbərlɪs/
adjective
无纤维的；不含纤维的
Lacking fibers; having no fibers.
The fiberless meat was difficult to chew.  这种无纤维的肉很难咀嚼。
Near Synonyms: smooth, non-fibrous
Antonyms: fibrous, stringy
词性: 形容词
