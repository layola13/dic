nonostensible:/ˌnɒnɒˈstɛnsɪbəl/ | adj. | 不明显的，不外露的，不明示的 | Not readily apparent, observable, or demonstrable; inconspicuous. |  The spy's nonostensible communication methods allowed him to remain undetected.  这个间谍不外露的沟通方式让他没有被发现。 | 近义词: inconspicuous, unobtrusive, subtle, hidden, covert | 反义词: ostensible, obvious, apparent, conspicuous, overt | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + ostensible.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "ostensible" comes from the Latin "ostendere," meaning "to show."
