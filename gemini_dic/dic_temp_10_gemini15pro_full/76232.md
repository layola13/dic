futureless:/ˈfjuːtʃərləs/| adj.|没有未来的，没有前途的|Without a future; having no prospect of a future; hopeless.|He felt futureless, trapped in a dead-end job. 他感到没有未来，被困在一份没有前途的工作中。|例句：The futureless youth wandered the streets aimlessly.  没有未来的年轻人漫无目的地在街上游荡。|近义词：hopeless, bleak, desperate, despondent, pessimistic|反义词:promising, hopeful, optimistic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From future + -less.  "future" comes from Old French futur, from Latin futurus, future participle of esse "to be". "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without".
