mendicate:/ˈmɛndɪkeɪt/| v. |乞讨，行乞|To beg; to ask for alms or charity. |He was forced to mendicate on the streets to survive. 他被迫在街上乞讨以求生存。|Synonyms: beg, panhandle, implore, solicit|Antonyms: give, donate, provide, earn|verb

mendicant:/ˈmɛndɪkənt/| n. adj. |乞丐，行乞的|A beggar; someone who lives by begging. / Depending on charity; practicing begging.|The mendicant held out his hand for spare change. 乞丐伸出手来要零钱。/ The mendicant friars relied on the generosity of others. 托钵僧依靠别人的慷慨施舍。|Synonyms: beggar, panhandler, pauper|Antonyms: philanthropist, donor, benefactor|noun, adjective
