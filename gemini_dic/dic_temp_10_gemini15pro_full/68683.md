The sheer farcicalness of his excuse made it impossible to take him seriously. 他借口的滑稽程度让人无法认真对待他。|近义词：absurdity, ludicrousness, ridiculousness, preposterousness|反义词：seriousness, solemnity, gravity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From farcical + -ness.  Farcical comes from farce (a light dramatic work in which highly improbable plot situations, exaggerated characters, and often slapstick elements are used for humorous effect), from Old French farce "stuffing," from farcir "to stuff," from Latin farcire "to cram, stuff."
