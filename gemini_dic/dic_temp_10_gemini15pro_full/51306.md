detailer:/ˈdiːteɪlər/ (US), /ˈdiːteɪlə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 详细描述者，汽车美容师 |A person who pays attention to or provides small details. A person who cleans and polishes cars.| The detailer meticulously cleaned every inch of the car. 汽车美容师一丝不苟地清洁了汽车的每一寸。 The architect hired a detailer to finalize the blueprints. 建筑师聘请了一位详细描述者来完成蓝图。|Synonyms: finisher, specialist, expert |Antonyms: generalist, amateur| noun
