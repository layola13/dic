haloes:/ˈheɪloʊz/ (US) /ˈheɪləʊz/ (UK)| n. |光环，晕轮，神圣的光圈|A circle of light appearing to surround the sun or moon, or a similar effect around a saint's head in a religious painting.|The sun appeared with a bright halo around it. 太阳周围出现了一个明亮的光环。|Synonyms: aura, nimbus, ring, corona|Antonyms: darkness, shadow|Noun


haloes:/ˈheɪloʊz/ (US) /ˈheɪləʊz/ (UK)| n. |(halo的复数形式)|Plural form of halo.|The painting depicted several angels with haloes above their heads. 这幅画描绘了几个头顶有光环的天使。|Synonyms: auras, nimbi, rings, coronae|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plural noun)|Noun
