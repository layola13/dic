frequency:/ˈfriːkwənsi/| n.|频率，发生次数，出现率|The frequency of earthquakes in this area is increasing.  这个地区的地震频率正在增加。|例句：The radio operates at a frequency of 100 MHz. 这台收音机的工作频率是100兆赫。|近义词：rate, occurrence, incidence|反义词:rarity, infrequency|名词

etymology: From Latin *frequentia* "assembly, crowd, multitude," from *frequens* "crowded, numerous, repeated, common."
