eosphorite:/ˈiːəsfəˌraɪt/| n. |红磷铁矿|A hydrous manganese iron phosphate mineral, typically pink to reddish-brown in color. It is often found in granite pegmatites.|Eosphorite is a relatively rare mineral sought after by collectors. 红磷铁矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，受到收藏家的追捧。|Synonyms: none (as it's a specific mineral name)|Antonyms: none|noun
