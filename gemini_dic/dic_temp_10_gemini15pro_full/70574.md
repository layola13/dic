filleter:/fiːlˈteɪ.ər/ | n. | 过滤器; 滤纸 | A device used to remove solids from liquids or gases. |  The coffee machine uses a paper filleter. 咖啡机使用纸质过滤器。| synonyms: filter, strainer | antonyms: none | noun


filleter:/fiːlˈteɪ.ər/ | v. | 过滤 | To remove solids from a liquid or gas using a filter. | She filletered the water before drinking it. 她喝水之前先过滤了一下。| synonyms: filter, strain, sieve | antonyms: contaminate, pollute | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "filter", from Old French "filtre", from Medieval Latin "filtrum", of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Arabic "feltre" (felt), or Latin "filum" (thread).
