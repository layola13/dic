damnific:/dæmˈnɪfɪk/
adjective
损害的；造成损失的
causing or tending to cause damage or loss
The damnific effects of the storm were felt throughout the region. 暴风雨造成的损害影响了整个地区。
近义词: damaging, harmful, detrimental, injurious
反义词: beneficial, advantageous, helpful

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin damnificus, from damnificare (“to cause loss or harm”), from damnum (“loss, damage”) + facere (“to do, make”).
