hypozoic:/ˌhaɪpəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
隐生期的；生命出现以前的
Relating to the period in the earth's history before the first appearance of life.
The hypozoic era preceded the emergence of life on Earth.  隐生代早于地球上生命的出现。
The conditions during the hypozoic eon were not conducive to life. 隐生代的环境条件不利于生命的存在。
prebiotic, azoic
