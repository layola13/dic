overburn:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrn/| v. n.|过度燃烧，过度劳累|To burn excessively; to exhaust oneself by working too hard.|He overburned himself trying to meet the deadline. 他为了赶截止日期而过度劳累。|近义词：overwork, exhaust, burnout|反义词:relax, rest|词性:动词,名词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "burn" (verb meaning "to consume fuel and produce heat")
