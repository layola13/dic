basaltine:/ˈbæsəlˌtaɪn, ˈbeɪsəlˌtaɪn/
adjective
玄武岩的；似玄武岩的
Relating to, resembling, or containing basalt.
Basaltine rocks are common in volcanic areas. 玄武岩在火山地区很常见。
The cliff face was composed of dark, basaltine rock.  悬崖表面由深色的玄武岩构成。
近义词:basaltic
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider specifying a contrasting rock type, e.g., granitic)
词性:adjective
