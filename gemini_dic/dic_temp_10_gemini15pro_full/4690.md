alertest:/əˈlɜːtɪst/ | adj. | 最警觉的，最警惕的 |  Superlative form of alert: most alert.  Meaning "most watchful and ready to react." |  The alertest guard noticed the shadow moving in the alley.  最警觉的警卫注意到了小巷里移动的影子。 |  vigilant, watchful, observant, attentive |  inattentive, careless, oblivious, unaware | adjective

alertest:/əˈlɜːtɪst/ | adj. | 最机敏的，反应最快的 |  Superlative form of alert: most alert. Meaning "most mentally quick and responsive." | The alertest student in the class always answered the teacher's questions first. 班上最机敏的学生总是第一个回答老师的问题。 |  sharp, quick-witted, astute, perceptive |  slow, dull, unintelligent, unresponsive | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *alerte (modern French alerte)  "on the watch, watchful," from Italian all'erta "on the watch," literally "on the height, on the rise," from erta "watch, lookout," from a Vulgar Latin word *erta, from Latin erectus "upright," past participle of erigere "to erect," from ex- "out" + regere "to direct, to guide, to keep straight."
