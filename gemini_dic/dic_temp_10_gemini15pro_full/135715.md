nonpriestlier: /ˌnɒnˈprɪːstliər/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈpriːstliər/ (American) | adj. | 更不像牧师的；更缺乏牧师气质的 | Less priestly; having less of the qualities associated with a priest. |  He adopted a nonpriestlier tone in his later sermons.  他在后来的布道中采取了一种更不像牧师的语气。 |  less clerical, less ecclesiastical, more secular, more worldly | more priestly, more clerical, more ecclesiastical | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix meaning "not") + priestlier (comparative form of priestly)

priestlier: priest + -ly (suffix forming adjectives)

priest: From Old English *prēost, from Vulgar Latin *prester, from Late Latin presbyter, from Greek presbyteros "elder," comparative of presbys "old."
