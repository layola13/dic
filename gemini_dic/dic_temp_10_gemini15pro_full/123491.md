monotes:/ˈmɒnəʊtiːz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəˌtɪz/ (US)| n. |单音; 单音调; 单音音乐|A musical style or composition characterized by the use of a single melodic line, often without harmony.  It refers to music that uses only one note at a time, or music that emphasizes a single pitch or tone.|The ancient chant was a form of monotes. 这首古老的圣歌是一种单音音乐。| The drone of the bagpipes created a haunting monotes. 风笛的嗡嗡声营造出一种萦绕心头的单音调。 |近义词: monotone, unison|反义词: polyphony, harmony |词性: noun
