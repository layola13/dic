clutter:/ˈklʌtər/ | n. vt. | 杂乱，混乱；使凌乱 | A state of disorder, untidiness; to fill or cover with scattered or disordered things. | The attic was filled with clutter. 阁楼里堆满了杂物。 | My desk is cluttered with papers. 我的桌子堆满了文件。| synonyms: mess, disorder, chaos, confusion | antonyms: order, tidiness, neatness | noun

clutter:/ˈklʌtər/ | vt. | 使凌乱，塞满 | To fill or cover with scattered or disordered things. |  Too many pictures cluttered the wall. 太多的照片把墙弄得很乱。 | Don't clutter your mind with unnecessary worries. 不要让不必要的担忧扰乱你的思绪。| synonyms: mess up, disorganize, litter | antonyms: organize, tidy, declutter | verb
