nonverticalities:/ˌnɒnvɜːrtɪˈkælɪtiz/
|n.| 非垂直性；倾斜 |The nonverticalities of the leaning tower are quite pronounced.  斜塔的非垂直性非常明显。|
|英文解析| The state or quality of not being vertical; deviation from the vertical.  It refers to instances where something is not perfectly upright or perpendicular to the horizontal plane. |
|例句| The architect had to account for the nonverticalities of the terrain when designing the foundation. 建筑师在设计地基时必须考虑到地形的非垂直性。|
|例句| The nonverticalities in the old building's walls were a sign of structural damage. 老建筑墙壁的非垂直性是结构损坏的迹象。|
|近义词| inclinations, tilts, slants, leans |
|反义词| verticality, uprightness, perpendicularity |
|词性| noun (plural) |
