bring-to-book:/ˌbrɪŋ tə ˈbʊk/ |v. |追究责任；惩罚；责问|To call someone to account for their actions; to punish or reprimand someone for wrongdoing. | The company vowed to bring the perpetrators to book.  这家公司誓言要将肇事者绳之以法。|Synonyms: call to account, hold accountable, take to task, prosecute, punish | Antonyms: excuse, pardon, absolve | verb

bring-to-book:/ˌbrɪŋ tə ˈbʊk/ |v. |使受纪律处分| To subject someone to disciplinary action. |  The soldier was brought to book for disobeying orders.  这名士兵因不服从命令而受到纪律处分。|Synonyms: discipline, reprimand, censure | Antonyms: commend, praise | verb
