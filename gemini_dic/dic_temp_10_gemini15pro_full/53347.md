disadvantageous:/ˌdɪsˌædvənˈteɪdʒəs/
adjective
不利的，有害的
Presenting a disadvantage or creating unfavorable circumstances.
It would be disadvantageous to our country to reduce defense spending. 减少国防开支对我国是不利的。
Synonyms: unfavorable, detrimental, adverse, harmful, inimical
Antonyms: advantageous, beneficial, favorable, helpful, propitious
词性: 形容词
