amniota:/ˈæmniəʊtə/ (US) /ˈæmniətə/| n.|羊膜动物|Any of a group of vertebrates that comprises reptiles, birds, and mammals, characterized by having an amnion during embryonic development.|Reptiles, birds, and mammals are all amniotes, possessing an amnion that protects the developing embryo. 爬行动物、鸟类和哺乳动物都是羊膜动物，拥有保护发育中胚胎的羊膜。|近义词: amniotic vertebrate|反义词: anamniote|词性: 名词

amniote:/ˈæmniəʊt/ (US) /ˈæmniət/| adj.|羊膜的|Relating to or denoting amniotes.|The amniote egg is a key adaptation for terrestrial life. 羊膜卵是适应陆地生活的关键。|近义词: amniotic|反义词: anamniotic|词性: 形容词
