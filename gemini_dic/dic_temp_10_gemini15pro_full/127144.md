natters:/ˈnætərz/ | v. | 闲聊，唠叨 | to talk casually and at length about unimportant matters |  She natters on the phone for hours. 她煲电话粥好几个小时。| 近义词：chat, chatter, gossip, prattle | 反义词: be silent, be quiet | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *nateren, frequentative of *naten (“to use foolish or trifling words”), possibly from Old Norse *knatra (“to grumble”).
