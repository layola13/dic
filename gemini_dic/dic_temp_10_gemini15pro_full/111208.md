lithophane:/ˈlɪθəfeɪn/ | n. | 透光瓷板，透光石雕 | A translucent porcelain or thin wax plaque, usually with a design in relief on one side, which when lit from behind reveals a picture or scene. |  The lithophane showed a beautiful winter scene when held up to the light.  透光瓷板在对着光线时展现出一幅美丽的冬日景象。 | synonym: cameo, plaque | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opaque carving") | noun

lithophane:/ˈlɪθəfeɪn/ | n. | 透光瓷板，透光石雕 | A thin, translucent porcelain or wax plaque, often decorated with a design in relief, that reveals a picture or scene when backlit. | I bought a lithophane as a souvenir; it shows a picture of the castle when held up to the light. 我买了一块透光瓷板作为纪念品；对着光线时，它会显示出城堡的图像。 | synonym:  bas-relief, porcelain relief | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
