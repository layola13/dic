disquietous:/dɪsˈkwaɪətəs/
adjective
令人不安的，焦虑的
Causing anxiety or uneasiness; disturbing.
The disquietous news of the approaching storm made everyone nervous.  令人不安的暴风雨即将来临的消息让每个人都很紧张。
The constant dripping of the faucet was a disquietous sound that kept her awake.  水龙头不断滴水的声音令人不安，让她无法入睡。
近义词: unsettling, disturbing, worrying, troubling, alarming
反义词: calming, soothing, reassuring, peaceful, tranquil
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + quietous (obsolete form of quiet + -ous suffix).  由dis-（表示否定、反转）+ quietous（quiet的过时形式 + -ous后缀）构成。
