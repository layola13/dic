blubbery: /ˈblʌbəri/ (US) /ˈblʌbəri/ (UK)| adj.|肥胖的，臃肿的；哭哭啼啼的|Resembling or consisting of blubber; fat and flabby; tearful.  Characterized by excessive weeping or sentimentality.|The walrus has a thick, blubbery layer of fat to keep it warm in icy waters.  海象有一层厚厚的脂肪层，以使其在冰冷的水中保持温暖。

The child's blubbery face showed how upset he was.  孩子哭哭啼啼的脸表明他有多难过。|近义词：fat, obese, flabby, plump, chubby, tearful, weepy|反义词：thin, lean, muscular, stoic|词性:形容词
