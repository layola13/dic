outrageousnesses: /ˌaʊtˈreɪdʒəsnəsɪz/
noun
暴行；骇人听闻的行为；令人愤慨的事情
The state or quality of being outrageous; shocking or excessive behavior.
The outrageousnesses committed during the war are truly appalling.  战争期间犯下的暴行真是骇人听闻。
synonyms: atrocities, enormities, scandals, abuses, violations
antonyms: decencies, proprieties, kindnesses, courtesies
词性: 名词

outrageous: /ˌaʊtˈreɪdʒəs/
adjective
骇人听闻的；令人愤慨的；反常的
Shockingly bad or excessive; exceeding all bounds of decency or reasonableness.
The price of the dress was outrageous.  这件衣服的价格高得离谱。
synonyms: shocking, appalling, exorbitant, preposterous, monstrous
antonyms: reasonable, moderate, acceptable, decent, proper
词性: 形词

词源:
From outrage + -ous + -ness.  Outrage itself comes from Old French outrage, meaning "excess, violence."
