discourse's:/ˈdɪskɔːrsɪz/
|n.| 话语的；论述的|Belonging to or associated with discourse; possessive form of "discourse".|The discourse's main focus was on climate change.  这个话语的主要焦点是气候变化。|The discourse's central argument was compelling.  这篇论述的核心论点很有说服力。|近义词:conversation's, discussion's, dialogue's|反义词:silence's, quiet's|词性:名词所有格

词源: From Middle English *discours*, from Old French discours (“reasoning, speech, conversation”), from Late Latin discursus (“conversation”), from Latin discursus (“a running about”), past participle of discurrere (“to run about, to discuss”), from dis- (“apart”) + currere (“to run”).
