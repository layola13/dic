comparatively:/kəmˈpærətɪvli/| adv.|相对地，比较地|In relation to something else; by comparison.  It modifies an adjective or adverb to indicate a relative degree or extent.|例句：Comparatively speaking, the cost of living is low in this city. 相对而言，这个城市的生活成本较低。|近义词：relatively, rather, somewhat, fairly|反义词：absolutely, completely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *comparativus, from Latin comparare "to compare," from com- "together" + parare "to prepare, make ready, provide."
