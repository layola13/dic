pacified:/ˈpæsɪfaɪd/| adj.|平静的，安抚的，平定的|Brought to a state of peace or quiet; calmed, soothed, appeased.|例句：The pacified crowd dispersed quietly.  平息下来的群众安静地散开了。

例句：The treaty pacified the warring nations.  该条约使交战国恢复了和平。|近义词：calmed, soothed, appeased, tranquilized, quieted|反义词：agitated, disturbed, riled, provoked, angered|词性:形容词

pacify:/ˈpæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|平息，安抚，抚慰|To bring to a state of peace or quiet; to calm, soothe, or appease.|例句：The mother pacified her crying baby with a lullaby.  母亲用一首摇篮曲安抚了她哭闹的婴儿。

例句：The government tried to pacify the protesters with promises of reform. 政府试图以改革的承诺来安抚抗议者。|近义词：calm, soothe, appease, tranquilize, quiet|反义词：agitate, disturb, rile, provoke, anger|词性:动词
