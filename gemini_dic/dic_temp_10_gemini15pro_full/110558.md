limeyest:/ˈlaɪmiɪst/
adjective
最像英国人的；最具英国特色的
most resembling or characteristic of a British person, especially one from England.
He had the limeyest accent I'd ever heard. 他有着我听过的最英国的口音。
近义词:Britishmost, Englishmost
反义词:un-British, least English
词性:adjective
