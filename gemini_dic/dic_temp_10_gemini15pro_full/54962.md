disprofitable:/dɪsˈprɒfɪtəbəl/
adjective
无利可图的，不赚钱的
not profitable; incurring a loss or not yielding a worthwhile profit.
The company abandoned the unprofitable venture.  这家公司放弃了不赚钱的企业。
The investment proved to be unprofitable. 这项投资被证明是无利可图的。
近义词: loss-making, uneconomical, unprofitable, losing
反义词: profitable, lucrative, gainful, remunerative
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From dis- + profitable.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Profitable" comes from the Old French "profitable"  and ultimately from the Latin "proficere" meaning "to advance" or "to be useful."
