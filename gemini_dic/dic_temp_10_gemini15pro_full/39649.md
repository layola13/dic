conservationist:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/
n.
自然资源保护者，环保人士
A person who advocates or acts for the protection and preservation of the natural environment and its resources.
例句：The conservationist dedicated her life to protecting endangered species.  这位环保人士毕生致力于保护濒危物种。

例句：The conservationists are working to restore the damaged wetland habitat.  环保人士正在努力恢复受损的湿地栖息地。

近义词：environmentalist, preservationist, ecologist
反义词：developer, polluter

词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From conserve (v.) + -ation + -ist. Conserve comes from Old French conserver, from Latin conservare "to preserve," from com- "together" + servare "to keep, guard."
