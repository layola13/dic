parcenary:/ˈpɑːrsənəri/
n.
共同继承；共同继承权；共同继承的遗产
Joint heirship; the state of being a parcener; the estate held in parcenary.
The property was held in parcenary by the three sisters. 这处房产由三姐妹共同继承。
近义词: joint tenancy, co-heirship
反义词: sole ownership
词性: 名词
