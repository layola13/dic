artistic:/ɑrˈtɪstɪk/ /ɑːˈtɪstɪk/| adj.|艺术的；有艺术才能的|Having or revealing natural creative skill.|例句：She has an artistic temperament. 她有艺术家的气质。|例句：He comes from a very artistic family. 他来自一个很有艺术气息的家庭。|近义词：creative, imaginative, aesthetic, artful|反义词：inartistic, uncreative, unimaginative|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French artistique, from Latin artisticus, from ars, art- ‘art’.
