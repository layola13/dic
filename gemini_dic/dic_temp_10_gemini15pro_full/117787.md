meliaceous:/ˌmiːliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
楝科的
belonging to or relating to the mahogany family (Meliaceae) of tropical and subtropical trees and shrubs
The meliaceous tree produced fragrant flowers. 这棵楝科树开出了芬芳的花朵。
Synonyms: mahogany-like
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
