comrade:/ˈkɒm.reɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːm.ræd/ (US)| n.|同志，同伴，同事|A fellow socialist or communist (often used as a form of address).  A companion who shares one's activities or is a fellow member of an organization.|例句：The comrades planned the revolution together. 同志们一起计划了革命。

例句：He greeted his comrade with a firm handshake. 他和他的同志坚定地握手致意。

近义词：companion, friend, colleague, associate, partner, ally|反义词：enemy, opponent, foe, adversary|词性:noun

词源:
From late 18th century French camarade, from Spanish camarada ‘roommate’, from cámara ‘chamber’.


注意事项:
The word "comrade" has strong political connotations, particularly associated with communism and socialism.  Its use in everyday English is relatively uncommon and can sound dated or even ironic.  In some contexts, it might be perceived as humorous or even slightly subversive.
