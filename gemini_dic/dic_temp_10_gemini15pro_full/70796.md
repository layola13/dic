fingerling:/ˈfɪŋɡərlɪŋ/| n. |鱼苗，幼树|A young fish, especially one less than a year old, or a young tree.|例句：The pond was stocked with fingerlings.  池塘里放养了鱼苗。

例句：The forester planted rows of pine fingerlings. 护林员种下了成排的松树苗。|近义词：fry, seedling, sapling |反义词:mature fish/tree|noun
