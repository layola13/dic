aculeiform: /əˈkjuːliɪfɔːrm/
adjective
针状的，刺形的
Shaped like a needle or thorn; tapering to a point.
The aculeiform leaves of the yucca plant are sharp and dangerous. 丝兰植物的针状叶子又尖又危险。

The bee's aculeiform stinger delivered a painful dose of venom. 蜜蜂针状的毒刺带来了一阵剧痛。

近义词: needle-shaped, acicular, spiniform, pointed
反义词: blunt, rounded
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aculeus* (needle, sting) + *-formis* (shaped like).

USAGE->
The aculeiform structure of the cactus spines helps it to conserve water. 仙人掌刺的针状结构有助于其保存水分。

Many species of wasps have aculeiform ovipositors used to lay eggs in or on other insects. 许多种类的黄蜂都有针状的产卵器，用来在其他昆虫体内或体外产卵。
