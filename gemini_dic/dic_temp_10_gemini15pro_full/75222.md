frogmarching:/ˈfrɒɡˌmɑːtʃɪŋ/ | n. | 蛙跳式抬人; 蛙式押送 | The act of carrying someone face downwards by their arms and legs. |  The police frogmarched the protester out of the building. 警察蛙跳式地把抗议者抬出了大楼。|  They frogmarched him off the stage after his disruptive speech.  在他发表了破坏性言论后，他们把他蛙跳式地抬下了台。 | 近义词: carrying, manhandling | 反义词: releasing, freeing | 词性: 名词
