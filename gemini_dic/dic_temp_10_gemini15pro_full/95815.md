incorporality:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriˈælɪti/ /ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːrˈiːælɪti/
n.
无形性；非物质性；灵性
The state or quality of being incorporeal; lack of material body or form.
The concept of God's incorporality is central to many religions.  上帝的无形性是许多宗教的核心概念。
Near synonyms: immateriality, intangibility, bodilessness, spirituality, ethereality
Antonyms: corporeality, materiality, physicality, tangibility
noun
