fullmart:/ˈfʊlˌmɑːrt/ | n. |大型超市，量贩店 | A large supermarket, typically one with a wide range of goods, including groceries and other household items.  It suggests a store that is "full" of merchandise and operates like a "market". Although not a standard English word, it seems to be a blend of "full" and "market". |  We found everything we needed at the fullmart. 我们在大型超市找到了我们需要的所有东西。|  The fullmart had a great selection of fresh produce. 这家大型超市有种类繁多的新鲜农产品。 | Synonyms: supermarket, hypermarket, superstore | Antonyms: convenience store, small shop | Noun
