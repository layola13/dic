cry:/kraɪ/
n.|哭泣，叫喊|The baby's cry woke me up. 婴儿的哭声把我吵醒了。
|近义词:weep, sob, wail|反义词:laugh, chuckle|词性:名词

cry:/kraɪ/
v.|哭，喊，叫|She cried when she heard the bad news. 她听到坏消息哭了。
|近义词:weep, sob, yell|反义词:laugh, rejoice|词性:动词

cry:/kraɪ/
n.|喊声，叫声|I heard a cry for help. 我听到了呼救声。
|近义词:shout, yell, scream|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crien*, from Old French *crier*, from Latin *quiritare* "to shriek, scream" (of animals, especially birds), frequentative of *quirere* "to seek, ask."  The sense evolution from "shriek" to "weep" is unusual but not unparalleled.
