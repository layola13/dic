panorama: /ˌpænəˈrɑːmə/ /ˌpænəˈræmə/ | n. | 全景，全貌；概观；概述 | A wide and impressive view of a surrounding area; a comprehensive presentation of a subject. | From a mountain top, you can get a panorama of the whole city. 从山顶上，你可以看到整个城市的景色。 | synonyms: vista, overview, perspective, survey | antonyms: close-up, detail, myopia | noun

panorama: /ˌpænəˈrɑːmə/ /ˌpænəˈræmə/ | n. | 全景画，全景照片 | A wide photograph or picture that appears to surround the viewer. | The photographer stitched together several images to create a stunning panorama. 摄影师将几张照片拼接在一起，创造出一幅令人惊叹的全景图。 | synonyms: cyclorama, diorama | antonyms: snapshot | noun
