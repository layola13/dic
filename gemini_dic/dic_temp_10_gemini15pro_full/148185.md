overshade:/ˌoʊvərˈʃeɪd/| vt.|遮蔽，使失色，使相形见绌|To cast a shadow over; to darken or obscure.|The taller buildings overshadowed the park, making it feel damp and cold.  高楼遮蔽了公园，使它感觉潮湿阴冷。|The oak tree overshadowed the smaller plants in the garden. 橡树遮蔽了花园里较小的植物。|近义词：eclipse, obscure, overshadow|反义词：illuminate, highlight|verb
