cossack:/ˈkɒsæk/ (UK), /ˈkɑːsæk/ (US)| n.|哥萨克，哥萨克人|A member of a people of southern Russia and Ukraine, noted for their horsemanship and military skill, especially as cavalrymen.|例句：The Cossacks were famed for their horsemanship. 哥萨克人以其骑术而闻名。

例句：Many Cossacks served in the Tsar's army. 许多哥萨克人在沙皇军队服役。

近义词：horseman, cavalryman, warrior|反义词:civilian|词性:noun
