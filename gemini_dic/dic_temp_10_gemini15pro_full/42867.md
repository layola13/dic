courthouses:/ˈkɔːrtˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
法院；县政府所在地
A building that houses judicial courts.
The courthouse is located in the center of town.  法院位于市中心。
The lawyers met at the courthouse to discuss the case. 律师们在法院见面讨论案情。
synonyms: courts, halls of justice, judicial buildings
antonyms: jail, prison
noun
