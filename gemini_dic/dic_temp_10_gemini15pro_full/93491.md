illustrious:/ɪˈlʌstriəs/ | adj. | 著名的，杰出的，辉煌的 | Well known and respected, especially for past achievements.  |  例句：He had an illustrious career as a diplomat. 他有着辉煌的外交生涯。 | 近义词：distinguished, eminent, renowned, famed, celebrated | 反义词：obscure, unknown, undistinguished, inglorious | 词性: 形容词

词源：From Latin illustris ‘bright, clear, famous’, from in- ‘in, into’ + lustrare ‘to light up’.
