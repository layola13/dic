bestir:/bɪˈstɜːr/
verb
奋起；激励；唤醒
to rouse to action; stir up; exert oneself
He finally bestirred himself to go to work.  他终于振作起来去上班了。
近义词：rouse, stir, awaken, activate
反义词：idle, laze, procrastinate
词性：动词


bestir:/bɪˈstɜːr/
verb
使…行动；使…活跃
To cause to become active; rouse
The coach bestirred his players to try harder. 教练激励他的球员更加努力。
近义词：motivate, energize, galvanize
反义词：calm, soothe, pacify
词性：动词
