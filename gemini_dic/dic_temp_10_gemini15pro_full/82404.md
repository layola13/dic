grudgeless:/ˈɡrʌdʒləs/| adj.|无怨恨的，不记仇的|Free from resentment or ill will; forgiving.|She accepted his apology with a grudgeless smile. 她带着不记仇的微笑接受了他的道歉。|Example: He bore no grudgeless feelings towards his former rival.  他对以前的对手没有怀恨在心。|Synonyms: forgiving, unresentful, magnanimous, charitable|Antonyms: resentful, bitter, vindictive|adjective
