meditatingly:/ˈmɛdɪteɪtɪŋli/
adv.
沉思地，冥想地
in a meditating manner; thoughtfully; pensively.
She gazed meditatingly out the window. 她若有所思地凝视着窗外。
Synonyms: thoughtfully, pensively, contemplatively, reflectively
Antonyms: thoughtlessly, unreflectively, impulsively
词性: 副词
