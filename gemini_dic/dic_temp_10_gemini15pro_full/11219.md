architrave: /ˈɑːrkɪtreɪv/ | n. |  框缘，楣梁 | The molded frame of a doorway or window, especially the lower horizontal piece resting immediately on the columns. | The architrave of the temple was elaborately carved.  寺庙的框缘雕刻精美。 | synonyms: frame, molding, trim | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "gap," "opening" which represent the absence of a frame) | noun

architrave: /ˈɑːrkɪtreɪv/ | n. |  (尤指古典建筑的) 柱顶楣梁 |  In classical architecture, the architrave is the lintel or beam that rests on the capitals of columns. | The architrave of the Parthenon is remarkably well-preserved.  帕台农神庙的楣梁保存得非常完好。| synonyms: lintel, beam, epistyle | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
