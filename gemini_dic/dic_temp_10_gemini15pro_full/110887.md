lionship: /ˈlaɪənʃɪp/ | n. |雄狮身份，雄狮的威严，勇气，领导地位 |The quality of being a lion, especially with regard to courage, dominance, and majesty.  It can also refer to the position or status of a dominant male lion within a pride. | He showed true lionship in the face of danger.  他在面对危险时展现了真正的雄狮般的勇气。| synonyms: courage, bravery, valor, boldness, intrepidity, fearlessness, heroism, fortitude, mettle, nerve, pluck, grit, audacity, doughtiness, stoutheartedness, lionheartedness, leadership, dominance, mastery, control, supremacy, authority, power, ascendancy, sway, rule, sovereignty, dominion | antonyms: cowardice, fear, timidity, meekness, submissiveness, weakness | noun
