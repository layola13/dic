gumming:/ˈɡʌmɪŋ/| v. | 涂胶，用胶粘，分泌树胶，（机器）发生故障 |  covering or clogging with gum; becoming sticky or clogged; malfunctioning (especially of machinery) | The label was gumming up the printer.  标签把打印机弄粘住了。 | 近义词：sticking, clogging, jamming, adhering | 反义词: cleaning, unclogging, freeing | 词性: 动词 (present participle of *gum*)

gumming:/ˈɡʌmɪŋ/| n. |  涂胶，分泌树胶，（机器）发生故障 | the act of applying gum; the secretion of gum; malfunctioning | The gumming of the gears caused the clock to stop. 齿轮粘合导致时钟停止。| 近义词：sticking, adhesion, clogging | 反义词: cleaning, unclogging | 词性: 名词 (gerund of *gum*)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gommen*, from Old English *gōma* ("gum of the mouth, palate"), from Proto-Germanic *gōmaz* ("gum, palate"), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵeh₂m-/*ǵeh₂mbh- ("to yawn, gape").  Related to Dutch *gom*, German *Gaumen* ("palate").  The meaning shifted from "gum of the mouth" to other sticky substances.
