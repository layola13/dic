blazonments: /ˈbleɪzənmənts/
n.
纹章装饰; 紋章的描绘; 公开展示; 宣扬
A heraldic display; a showy or ostentatious display.  The act of describing or depicting armorial bearings.  Public display or proclamation.

The blazonments on the knight's shield told the story of his family's history.  骑士盾牌上的纹章讲述了他家族的历史。

The company's new logo was a prominent blazonment on all of their products. 该公司的新标志在所有产品上都醒目地展示。

The mayor's blazonment of the city's achievements was met with applause. 市长对城市成就的宣扬赢得了掌声。


Synonyms:  emblazonment, display, exhibition, proclamation, announcement, declaration, heraldry

Antonyms: concealment, hiding, suppression, secrecy
