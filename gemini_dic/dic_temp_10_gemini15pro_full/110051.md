licensure:/ˈlaɪsənʃər/ | n. |许可，执照|The act of granting a license, especially a professional one.  The state of being licensed. |例句：Doctors must obtain licensure before they can practice medicine. 医生必须获得许可才能行医。

|例句：The licensure requirements vary from state to state.  执照要求因州而异。|近义词：certification, accreditation, authorization|反义词：prohibition, forbiddance|词性：名词
