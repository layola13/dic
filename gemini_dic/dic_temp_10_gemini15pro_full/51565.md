deuteronomy:/ˌdjuːtəˈrɒnəmi/ | n. | 申命记 | The fifth book of the Old Testament, containing a recapitulation of the Mosaic Law.  It derives from Greek *Deuteronomion*, meaning "second law" or "repeated law".  |  The rabbi gave a sermon on Deuteronomy. 拉比就申命记做了一次布道。| synonym: Fifth Book of Moses | antonym: N/A | noun

deuteronomy:/ˌdjuːtəˈrɒnəmi/ | n. | 复述; 重申 | A restatement or repetition of something. | The politician's speech was a deuteronomy of his previous campaign promises. 政治家的演讲是他先前竞选承诺的复述。| synonym: recapitulation, reiteration | antonym: N/A | noun
