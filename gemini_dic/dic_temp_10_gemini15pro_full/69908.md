fertilizes:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
施肥，使肥沃，使受精
To supply with fertilizer; to make fertile or productive.  To cause the fertilization of (an egg, female animal, or plant).
The farmer fertilizes his fields every spring.  农民每年春天都给田地施肥。
Bees fertilize plants by carrying pollen from one flower to another. 蜜蜂通过将花粉从一朵花带到另一朵花来使植物受精。
Synonyms: enrich, nourish, inseminate, pollinate
Antonyms: deplete, sterilize
词性: 动词
