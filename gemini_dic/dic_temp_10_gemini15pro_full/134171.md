adjective

Not having a physical body; not embodied in flesh.
The nonincarnated spirit floated through the room.  非肉身的灵魂飘过房间。
The play explored the idea of nonincarnated beings interacting with humans.  这部戏剧探讨了非肉身的存在与人类互动。
近义词: disembodied, spiritual, incorporeal
反义词: incarnate, embodied, physical
词性: adjective
