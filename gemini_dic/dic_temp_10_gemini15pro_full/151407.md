parnassien:/pɑːrˈnæsɪən/ | n. adj. | 帕尔纳斯诗派的(诗人或作品); 追求唯美主义的 | A member of a school of French poets of the latter half of the 19th century, characterized by an emphasis on metrical perfection and detachment from social concerns; also, relating to or characteristic of this school. |  The poem is a perfect example of Parnassien aesthetics. 这首诗是帕尔纳斯诗派美学的完美典范。| 近义词: aesthetic, artistic, refined | 反义词: unrefined, crude, unpolished | 词性: 名词, 形容词

parnassien:/pɑːrˈnæsɪən/ | adj. |  唯美主义的; 讲究形式的 | Characterized by an emphasis on metrical perfection and detachment from social concerns. | His parnassien style focused on precise language and evocative imagery.  他追求唯美主义的风格注重精确的语言和唤起联想的意象。| 近义词: aesthetic, formal, precise | 反义词: informal, imprecise, unpolished | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *Parnassien*, from *Parnasse*, meaning "Parnassus," the mountain in Greece sacred to Apollo and the Muses.
