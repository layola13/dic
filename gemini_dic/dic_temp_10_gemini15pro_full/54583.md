dismalize:/dɪˈsməlaɪz/
verb
使沮丧；使忧郁
To make dismal; depress or discourage.
The rainy weather dismalized the picnickers.  阴雨天气使野餐的人们感到沮丧。
The news of the factory closing dismalized the entire town. 工厂倒闭的消息使整个镇子都陷入了低迷。
近义词: depress, sadden, deject, discourage
反义词: cheer, encourage, uplift, elate
词性: verb

dismal:/ˈdɪzməl/
adjective
阴沉的；凄凉的；忧郁的
Causing gloom or dejection; dreary; cheerless; melancholy.
The dismal weather kept everyone indoors. 阴沉的天气让大家都待在室内。
He had a dismal outlook on life. 他的人生观很悲观。
近义词: gloomy, dreary, bleak, depressing
反义词: cheerful, bright, sunny, optimistic
词性: adjective
