instable:/ɪnˈsteɪbl/| adj.|不稳定的，不稳固的|Not stable; likely to change or fail; unsteady.|The political situation is highly instable. 政治局势很不稳定。|The instable ladder wobbled dangerously. 不稳固的梯子摇摇晃晃，十分危险。|近义词：unstable, precarious, shaky, volatile|反义词：stable, firm, secure|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *instabilis, from in- "not" + stabilis "stable".
