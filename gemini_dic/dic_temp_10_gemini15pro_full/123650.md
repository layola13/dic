monumentalities:/ˌmɒnjʊmɛnˈtælɪtiz/
n.
不朽的事物；宏伟的品质；纪念性
The quality or state of being monumental; impressiveness, vastness.
The monumentalities of ancient Rome still inspire awe.  古罗马的不朽遗迹至今仍令人敬畏。
近义词: grandeur, immensity, vastness, magnificence
反义词: insignificance, triviality, smallness, mediocrity
词性: noun (plural)
