euphoniad:/juːˈfəʊniæd/ |n.|悦音词；优美的声音|A euphoniad is a word or phrase that is pleasing to the ear, often because of its smooth and harmonious sounds. | Her name, "Melliflua," was a euphoniad, rolling off the tongue like honey.| 近义词: euphony, melody, harmony | 反义词: cacophony, dissonance |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Greek *euphōnia* "euphony," from *eu- "good" + *phōnē* "sound, voice."  |词源：来自希腊语 *euphōnia* “悦耳之音”，源自 *eu-* “好的” + *phōnē* “声音，嗓音”。
