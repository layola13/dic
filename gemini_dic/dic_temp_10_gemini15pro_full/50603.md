dere:/dɪər/ | adjective | 亲爱的，可爱的 |  Used as an affectionate form of address, especially in older texts or dialects.  Similar to "dear" but often used before a name.  Derived from Old English "dēore". | Dere friend, how are you? 亲爱的朋友，你好吗？ | Synonyms: dear, beloved, precious | Antonyms: hated, despised | adjective

dere:/dɪər/ | noun | 亲爱的，爱人 |  A term of endearment, often used as a form of address. | My dere, I miss you. 我的爱人，我想你。| Synonyms: darling, sweetheart, love | Antonyms: enemy, foe | noun
