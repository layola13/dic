gestates:/ˈdʒɛsteɪts/
verb
孕育，酝酿，发展
develops gradually over a period of time
例句：The idea gestated in his mind for several years before he finally acted on it.  这个想法在他脑海中酝酿了几年，他才最终付诸行动。

例句：She gestated her first novel for over a decade. 她酝酿了十多年才写出第一部小说。

近义词：develops, matures, incubates, breeds, generates
反义词：wanes, diminishes, declines
词性:v.
