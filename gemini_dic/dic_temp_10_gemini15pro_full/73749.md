forlorn:/fərˈlɔːrn/
| adj.|绝望的，孤独凄凉的，被遗弃的|Feeling abandoned and hopeless; sad and lonely|例句：The forlorn figure on the park bench looked lost and alone. 公园长椅上那个孤单的身影看起来失魂落魄。|近义词：desolate, abandoned, lonely, miserable|反义词：happy, cheerful, hopeful, optimistic|词性:形容词

forlorn:/fərˈlɔːrn/
| adj.|无望的，注定要失败的|unlikely to succeed or be fulfilled; hopeless|例句：Their efforts to save the business were forlorn.  他们拯救企业的努力注定要失败。|近义词：hopeless, futile, vain|反义词：promising, hopeful|词性:形容词
