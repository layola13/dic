lauric:/ˈlɔːrɪk/| adj.|月桂酸的；十二烷酸的|Relating to or derived from laurel; specifically designating a white crystalline fatty acid, C12H24O2, found in many vegetable fats and oils, especially in coconut oil and palm kernel oil.|Lauric acid is a common ingredient in soaps and detergents. 月桂酸是肥皂和洗涤剂中的常见成分。|近义词:dodecanoic|反义词:None|词性:形容词
