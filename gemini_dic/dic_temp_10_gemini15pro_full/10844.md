arabesque:/ˌærəˈbɛsk/ | n. v. adj. |蔓藤花纹，阿拉伯式花纹；以蔓藤花纹装饰；蔓藤花纹的| An intricate, ornate design of intertwined lines suggesting flowers, foliage, or other vegetal motifs, characteristic of Islamic art and often used in Moorish architecture. As a verb, it means to decorate with arabesques or to move or pose with graceful, flowing lines. As an adjective, it describes something decorated with or resembling arabesques, or characterized by graceful, flowing movements. | The dancer arabesqued gracefully across the stage. 舞者在舞台上优雅地做着蔓藤花纹式的舞姿。| 近义词： flourish, ornamentation, embellishment, adornment, curlicue, scrollwork | 反义词: plainness, simplicity | 词性: noun, verb, adjective


Etymology: From French *arabesque*, from Italian *arabesco*, meaning "Arabic," from Late Latin *arabiscus*, ultimately from Arabic *ʻarabī* ("Arabic").
