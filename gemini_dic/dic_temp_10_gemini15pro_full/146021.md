overdraining:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnɪŋ/
| adj. |过度排水; 排水过度的|The overdraining of the soil has led to desertification. 土壤过度排水导致了荒漠化。|Excessive drainage, removing too much water. The overdraining of peat bogs releases large amounts of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere.泥炭沼泽的过度排水向大气中释放了大量的二氧化碳。|近义词：overdrying, excessive drainage|反义词: waterlogging, underdraining|词性:形容词
|Etymology: "over-" + "draining" (from "drain," from Old English *drēahnian)|
