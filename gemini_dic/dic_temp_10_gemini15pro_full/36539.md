collaborator: /kəˈlæbəreɪtər/| n. |合作者，协作者，通敌者|A person who works jointly on an activity or project; a person who cooperates traitorously with an enemy.|例句：She found a collaborator in her classmate for the science project. 她找到了一个同学作为科学项目的合作者。

例句：The writer thanked his collaborators in the acknowledgements section of his book. 作者在他的书的致谢部分感谢了他的合作者。|近义词：partner, associate, colleague, accomplice, confederate|反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin collaboratus, past participle of collaborare "to labor together," from Latin com- "together" + laborare "to labor."
