lyencephalous: /laɪɛnˈsɛfələs/
adjective
无脑的，缺少大脑的
lacking a brain; characterized by the absence of a cerebrum.  This is a serious developmental defect.
例句：The lyencephalous fetus was not viable.  无脑胎儿无法存活。

例句：Lyencephaly is a devastating birth defect. 无脑畸形是一种毁灭性的出生缺陷。

近义词：anencephalic
反义词: N/A (The opposite would imply having a normal brain, which is the default state.  There isn't a specific word for "having a brain".)
词性: adjective
