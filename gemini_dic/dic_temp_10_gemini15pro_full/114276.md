mainframe:/ˈmeɪnfreɪm/| n. |大型机，主机|A large, powerful computer system used primarily by large organizations for critical applications, bulk data processing, such as census, industry and consumer statistics, enterprise resource planning, and financial transaction processing.|The bank uses a mainframe to process millions of transactions daily.|近义词：supercomputer, main computer|反义词:microcomputer, minicomputer|词性:noun
Etymology: main (principal) + frame (structure), referring to the central processing unit's cabinet.
