farrago:/fəˈrɑːɡoʊ/ /fəˈreɪɡoʊ/
|n.|混杂，杂烩，大杂烩|A confused mixture; a medley.|The play was a farrago of clichés and predictable plot twists.  这出戏是陈词滥调和可预见的情节转折的大杂烩。|Synonyms: medley, mixture, mishmash, jumble, hodgepodge|Antonyms: order, organization, uniformity|noun


farragoes:/fəˈrɑːɡoʊz/ /fəˈreɪɡoʊz/
|n.|混杂，杂烩，大杂烩（farrago的复数形式）|Plural form of farrago, meaning a confused mixture or medley.|His library was a farrago of ancient texts and modern novels.  他的图书馆是古代文献和现代小说的杂烩。|Synonyms: medleys, mixtures, mishmashes, jumbles, hodgepodges|Antonyms: order, organization, uniformity|noun
