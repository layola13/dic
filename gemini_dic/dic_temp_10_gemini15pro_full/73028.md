footfaulting:/ˈfʊtfɔːltɪŋ/| n.|脚误，脚部犯规|A foot fault in tennis or volleyball occurs when a server's foot touches the baseline or an extension of the centre mark before the ball is struck.|The player was penalized for footfaulting on his second serve.  该球员因第二次发球脚误而受到处罚。|Synonyms: service fault, stepping over the line|Antonyms: legal serve|noun
