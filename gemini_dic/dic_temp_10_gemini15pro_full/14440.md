auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪlɪəri/ /ɔːɡˈzɪləri/| adj. n.|辅助的，附属的；助动词；辅助者|Providing supplementary or additional help and support.  Relating to something that acts as a reserve or addition to the main force or resource.  A person or thing that provides assistance. |The auxiliary engine kicked in when the main power failed.  备用发动机在主电源失效时启动了。|synonyms: supplementary, additional, supporting, reserve, backup|antonyms: main, primary, principal|adjective

auxiliary:/ɔːɡˈzɪlɪəri/ /ɔːɡˈzɪləri/| n.|辅助者，助手；附属机构|A person or thing that provides assistance. A supplementary or reserve source of something.  A group or organization that provides support to a larger one.|The hospital has a strong network of auxiliaries. 这家医院有一个强大的辅助机构网络。|synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, support staff, reserve|antonyms: primary force, main body|noun
