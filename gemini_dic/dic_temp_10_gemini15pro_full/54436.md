n.
通货膨胀率下降; 反通货膨胀

The central bank's policies are aimed at achieving disinflation. 中央银行的政策旨在实现反通货膨胀。
近义词: deceleration of inflation, slowing inflation
反义词: inflation, hyperinflation

disinflation:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfleɪʃən/
n.
通货膨胀率下降; 反通货膨胀
A decrease in the rate of inflation; a slowing of the rate at which prices are rising.
The government's efforts to control spending led to a period of disinflation. 政府控制支出的努力导致了一段时间的反通货膨胀。
近义词: deceleration of inflation, slowing inflation
反义词: inflation, hyperinflation
词性: 名词 (Noun)


词源:  "dis-" (not, opposite of) + "inflation" (a general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money).  "Inflation" comes from Late Latin "inflatio," meaning "swelling," from Latin "inflare" (to blow into, inflate).
