cosset:/ˈkɒsɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːsɪt/ (US)| n. vt.|宠儿，宝贝；溺爱，娇养|A person or animal that is cosseted is treated very indulgently and protected from anything unpleasant.|She cosseted her only child. 她溺爱她唯一的孩子。|近义词：pamper, indulge, spoil|反义词：neglect, ignore|词性: noun, verb
