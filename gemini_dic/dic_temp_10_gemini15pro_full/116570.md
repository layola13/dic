matless:/ˈmætləs/
adjective
无床垫的
Without a mattress.
She slept on the matless cot, finding it surprisingly comfortable. 她睡在没有床垫的帆布床上，发现它出奇地舒服。
The refugees had to sleep on matless bunks. 难民们不得不在没有床垫的铺位上睡觉。
Synonyms:  unpadded, hard, bare
Antonyms:  padded, cushioned, comfortable
词性: 形容词
