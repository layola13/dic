lethalnesses:/ˈliːθəlnɪsɪz/
noun
致命性，致死率
The plural form of lethalness, referring to multiple instances or degrees of being deadly or capable of causing death.
The lethalnesses of the various poisons were compared in the study.  这项研究比较了各种毒药的致命性。
The report detailed the lethalnesses of different strains of the virus.  报告详细说明了不同病毒株的致死率。
synonyms: deadlinesses, fatalities, mortalities
antonyms: harmlessnesses, innocuousnesses
n.
