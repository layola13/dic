nonsubliminal:/nɒnˌsʌbˈlɪmɪnəl/
adjective
非潜意识的;非阈下的
Not perceived by or affecting the mind subconsciously.
The advertising campaign used nonsubliminal messaging, clearly stating the product's benefits.  这广告活动使用了非潜意识的信息传递方式，清晰地陈述了产品的好处。
The message was nonsubliminal, displayed in large, bold letters.  这则信息并非潜意识的，它以醒目的大字显示。
Synonym: overt, explicit, conscious
Antonym: subliminal, subconscious, hidden
词性: 形容词
