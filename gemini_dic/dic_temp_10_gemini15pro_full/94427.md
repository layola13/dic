implodent:/ɪmˈploʊdənt/
adjective
内爆的；向内破裂的
bursting inward
The implodent pressure crushed the submarine. 内爆的压力压垮了潜艇。

近义词: collapsing, inward-bursting
反义词: exploding, outward-bursting
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin implodere "to burst in," from in- "in" + plaudere "to clap, applaud."  The "-ent" suffix forms adjectives from verbs.
