foment:/foʊˈment/ (US), /ˈfəʊment/ (UK)| v. |煽动，助长，激起|To instigate or stir up (an undesirable or violent sentiment or course of action).|例句：They accused him of fomenting political unrest. 他们指责他煽动政治动乱。

例句：The humid weather fomented the spread of the disease. 潮湿的天气助长了疾病的传播。

近义词：incite, instigate, provoke, abet, stir, encourage|反义词：quell, suppress, subdue, allay, mitigate|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French fomenter, from Latin fomentāre "to bathe with warm lotions, cherish, encourage," from fōmentum "a warm application, poultice," from fovēre "to warm, keep warm."
