coleslaw: /ˈkoʊlslɔː/ (US), /ˈkəʊlslɔː/ (UK)| n.|凉拌卷心菜沙拉|A salad of finely shredded raw cabbage, often mixed with mayonnaise and other ingredients such as carrots and onions.|Example: We had burgers and coleslaw for lunch. 我们午餐吃了汉堡和凉拌卷心菜沙拉。|Example: The coleslaw was creamy and tangy.  这凉拌卷心菜沙拉口感柔滑，味道浓郁。|近义词: cabbage salad|反义词: None|词性: noun
