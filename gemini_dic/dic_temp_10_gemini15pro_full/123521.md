monotoning:/məˈnɑːtənɪŋ/ | v. | 使单调乏味;  单调地说着 |Making something monotonous; speaking in a monotone. |The teacher's monotoning voice made the students sleepy. 老师单调的声音让学生们昏昏欲睡。|Synonyms: droning, repeating, chanting |Antonyms: varying, diversifying | verb


monotoning:/məˈnɑːtənɪŋ/ | adj. | 单调的; 乏味的 |Characterized by monotony; tedious. |The monotoning lecture seemed to go on forever. 这单调的讲座似乎永远也讲不完。|Synonyms: tedious, boring, dull |Antonyms: exciting, stimulating, interesting | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From monotone + -ing.  "Monotone" comes from the Greek "monotonos," meaning "having a single tone," from "monos" (single) + "tonos" (tone).
