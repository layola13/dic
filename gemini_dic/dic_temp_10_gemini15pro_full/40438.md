contrasted:/kənˈtrɑːstɪd/ | adj. | 对比的，对照的 |  Differing strikingly; compared to show differences.  | The artist contrasted light and shadow in the painting. 艺术家在画中对比了光线和阴影。| synonyms: compared, differentiated, juxtaposed | antonyms: similar, alike, uniform | adjective

contrasted:/kənˈtrɑːstɪd/ | v. | 对比，对照（contrast的过去分词）| Compared in such a way as to emphasize differences. |  His achievements contrasted sharply with his humble beginnings. 他的成就与他卑微的出身形成了鲜明的对比。 | synonyms: compared, differentiated, juxtaposed | antonyms: assimilated, harmonized, reconciled | verb
