例句：The grippleness of the story kept me reading until the very end.  故事的吸引力让我一直读到最后。|近义词：grip, traction, adhesion, appeal |反义词: slipperiness, smoothness|词性:noun

词源: From "gripple" + "-ness". "Gripple" is a variant of "grip," which comes from the Old English "gripa," meaning "handful, grasp." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
