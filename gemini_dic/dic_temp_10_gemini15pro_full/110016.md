libration:/laɪˈbreɪʃən/| n.| 天平动；振动；摇动| An apparent or real oscillation of a celestial body, especially of the moon, as seen from Earth, causing parts normally invisible to become visible.| The moon's libration allows us to see slightly more than half of its surface. 月球的天平动使我们能看到略多于一半的月球表面。|oscillation, wobble| fixity, stillness|noun
