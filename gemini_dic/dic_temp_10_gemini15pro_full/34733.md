clicky:/ˈklɪki/| adj.|发出咔哒声的;吸引人的;点击的|making a sharp clicking sound; attractive and interesting, often in a cheap or superficial way; relating to clicking a mouse button to select something on a computer screen|The keyboard was very clicky, which I found satisfying.  这个键盘咔哒声很响，我觉得很满意。|近义词：clickable, catchy, snappy|反义词:silent, dull|adjective

clicky:/ˈklɪki/| n.|点击;|an act of clicking a mouse button|Every clicky on the banner ad sends data back to the advertiser.  横幅广告上的每次点击都会将数据发送回广告商。|近义词：click|反义词:None|noun
