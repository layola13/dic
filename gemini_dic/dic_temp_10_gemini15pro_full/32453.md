chimneyless:/ˈtʃɪmni.ləs/
adjective
无烟囱的
lacking a chimney; without a chimney.
The chimneyless cabin relied on a wood stove vented through a wall.  这间没有烟囱的小屋依靠一个通过墙壁排气的柴炉。
Their chimneyless cottage was heated by a pot-bellied stove.  他们没有烟囱的小屋是用一个大肚炉取暖的。
Near synonyms: ventless, stackless
Antonyms: chimneyed, stacked
词性: adjective
