colonialism:/kəˈloʊniəlɪzəm/ | n. |殖民主义|The policy or practice of acquiring full or partial political control over another country, occupying it with settlers, and exploiting it economically.|The history of colonialism in Africa is complex and often brutal. 非洲的殖民主义历史复杂且通常残酷。|  The legacy of colonialism continues to affect many countries today. 殖民主义的遗留问题持续影响着当今的许多国家。 | imperialism, expansionism | anti-colonialism, independence | noun
