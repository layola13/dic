intercarrier:/ˌɪntərˈkæriər/| adjective|载波间的，相互间的|Relating to or involving communication between carriers (such as radio or television broadcasting stations). It specifically refers to a signal created by the difference between two carrier frequencies.|The intercarrier sound system is used in television broadcasting.|Synonyms: intermediate, connecting, linking|Antonyms: isolated, separate|adjective

intercarrier:/ˌɪntərˈkæriər/| noun|载波间信号，相互间的信号|A signal resulting from the difference between two carrier frequencies, particularly in television broadcasting, used for transmitting audio information.|The intercarrier signal carries the audio information in a TV broadcast.|Synonyms: difference signal, intermediate signal|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "primary signal" in a specific context)|noun
