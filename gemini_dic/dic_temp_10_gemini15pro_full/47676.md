debtor:/ˈdetər/ (US), /ˈdɛtə/ (UK)| n. | 债务人，欠债人 | A person or organization that owes money to another. |  The company is a major debtor to several banks.  这家公司是几家银行的主要债务人。

The debtor was unable to repay the loan.  这个债务人无力偿还贷款。| synonym: borrower, owing party | antonym: creditor, lender | noun
