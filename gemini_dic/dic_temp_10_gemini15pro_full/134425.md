noniridescently:/ˌnɒnɪrɪˈdɛsəntli/
adv.
无虹彩地，不闪光的
Not iridescent; without a play of colors like that of a rainbow.
The beetle's shell was noniridescently black.  这只甲虫的外壳是无虹彩的黑色。

The matte paint dried noniridescently.  这种哑光漆干后不反光。
Synonym: dully, plainly
Antonym: iridescently, brilliantly
词性: 副词
