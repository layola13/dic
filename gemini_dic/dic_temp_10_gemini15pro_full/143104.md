outbud:/ˌaʊtˈbʌd/| v.|发芽早于；比…早萌芽|To bud or sprout earlier than something else.  To grow or develop faster than others.|The roses outbudded the other flowers in the garden.  花园里的玫瑰比其他花发芽早。|近义词：outshoot, outgrow|反义词：lag behind|词性：动词
