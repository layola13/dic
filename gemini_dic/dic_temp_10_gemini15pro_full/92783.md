icily:/ˈaɪsɪli/ | adv. | 冰冷地；冷淡地 | In a cold, unfriendly, or unemotional manner. | She looked at him icily and turned away. 她冷冷地看了他一眼，然后转身走开了。|例句：He spoke icily to the reporters. 他冷冰冰地对记者说话。|近义词：coldly, frostily, frigidly |反义词：warmly, kindly, cordially |词性:副词
词源: From "icy" + "-ly". "Icy" comes from Old English "isig", from "is" (ice).
