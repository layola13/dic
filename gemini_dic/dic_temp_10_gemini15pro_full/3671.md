aggrade:/əˈɡreɪd/
verb
加积，堆积，使（河床）淤高
To build up (land) by deposition of sediment.
The river aggraded its bed with silt. 这条河用淤泥堆积河床。
synonyms: deposit, accumulate, build up, silt up
antonyms: erode, degrade, wear down
