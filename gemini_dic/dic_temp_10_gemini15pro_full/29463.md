catalyzes:/ˈkætəlaɪzɪz/
verb
催化; 促进
causes or accelerates a process or reaction (chemical or otherwise)
例句：The enzyme catalyzes the breakdown of starch into sugars.  这种酶催化淀粉分解成糖。

例句：The presence of a catalyst catalyzes the reaction. 催化剂的存在加速了反应。

近义词：accelerates, promotes, facilitates, speeds up, boosts
反义词：inhibits, hinders, retards, slows down
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *catalyser*, from Ancient Greek καταλύειν (katalúein, “to dissolve, annul, destroy”), from κατά (katá, “down”) + λύειν (lúein, “to loosen, untie”).
