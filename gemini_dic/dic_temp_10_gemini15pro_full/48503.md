deer:/dɪr/| n.|鹿|A hoofed ruminant mammal of the Cervidae family, characterized by the male having antlers.|例句：The deer grazed peacefully in the meadow. 鹿在草地上平静地吃草。|例句：We saw a herd of deer in the forest. 我们在森林里看到一群鹿。|近义词：hart (male deer), hind (female deer), stag (male deer), doe (female deer)|反义词:predator|词性:名词
