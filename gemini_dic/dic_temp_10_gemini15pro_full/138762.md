numerical: /njuːˈmɛrɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 数字的，数值的 | relating to or expressed in numbers |  Example: Numerical data is often presented in charts and graphs.  数值数据通常用图表和图形来表示。 | Example: The numerical value of pi is approximately 3.14159.  圆周率的数值约为3.14159。| Synonyms: numeric, quantitative | Antonyms: qualitative, non-numerical | word type: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *numeralis, from Latin numerus "number."
