estates:/ɪˈsteɪts/ | n. | 财产，遗产；房地产；庄园 |  A large area of land in the country, usually with a large house, owned by one person or family.  Also, all of someone's property and money, especially everything that is left after they die. | He inherited the family estates. 他继承了家族的财产。| synonyms: property, land, possessions, belongings, effects, chattels, holdings, assets, resources, wealth, fortune | antonyms: debts, liabilities | noun

estates:/ɪˈsteɪts/ | n. | (社会)阶层，等级 |  A social class or a group of people who share the same economic or social status.  This meaning is mostly used in historical contexts.  | The three estates of the realm were the clergy, the nobility, and the commoners. 王国的三个等级是神职人员、贵族和平民。 | synonyms: class, rank, order, level, stratum, echelon | antonyms: N/A | noun
