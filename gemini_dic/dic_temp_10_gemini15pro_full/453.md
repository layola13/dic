ables:/ˈeɪblz/| adj.|有能力的，能干的 (plural form of able)|Refers to people or things having the skill, power, or qualities to do something.  It is the plural form of "able."|The ables were selected for the challenging task.  这些有能力的人被选中来完成这项具有挑战性的任务。|Synonyms: capable, competent, skilled, qualified|Antonyms: incapable, incompetent, unskilled, unqualified|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ables*, plural of *able*, from Old French *hable, hable*, from Latin *habĭlis* (“apt, fit, suitable, handy, manageable, skillful”), from *habēre* (“to have, hold”). Doublet of *habil*.
