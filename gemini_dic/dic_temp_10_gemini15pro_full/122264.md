mitigable:/ˈmɪtɪɡəbl/
adjective
可减轻的，可缓和的
able to be mitigated or made less severe.
The risks associated with this project are mitigable.  这个项目相关的风险是可减轻的。
The negative impacts of climate change are potentially mitigable through global cooperation. 气候变化的负面影响有可能通过全球合作得到缓解。
近义词: reducible, lessenable, alleviatable, remediable
反义词: unmitigable, unavoidable, inexorable, irreversible
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin mitigabilis, from mitigare (“to soften, alleviate”), from mitis (“mild, soft”) + -igare (frequentative suffix).
