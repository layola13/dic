nonomission:/ˌnɒnəˈmɪʃən/| n. |非排放；不排放（的状态）|The state or quality of not emitting something, especially pollutants or greenhouse gases.  It refers to a process or activity that does not release harmful substances into the environment.| The company achieved nonomission status for its manufacturing process. 这家公司实现了其制造过程的非排放状态。|Near synonyms: zero emission, emission-free|Antonyms: emission, pollution|Noun
