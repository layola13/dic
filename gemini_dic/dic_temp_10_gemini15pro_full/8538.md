anticeremonialist:/ˌæntiˌserɪˈmoʊniəlɪst/
n.
反对繁文缛节者；不拘礼节的人
Someone who opposes or dislikes ceremonies, especially formal or elaborate ones.
He's a bit of an anticeremonialist, preferring a quiet wedding to a big, lavish affair. 他有点不拘小节，喜欢安静的婚礼，不喜欢大操大办。
The anticeremonialist scoffed at the elaborate rituals of the coronation.  这位反对繁文缛节者对加冕礼的繁琐仪式嗤之以鼻。

近义词:nonconformist, iconoclast, maverick, rebel
反义词:traditionalist, formalist, ritualist
词性:noun
