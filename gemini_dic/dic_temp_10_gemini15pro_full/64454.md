erinys:/ɪˈrɪnɪs/| n.|复仇女神，厄里倪厄斯|One of the Erinyes, female deities of vengeance in Greek mythology.  希腊神话中复仇女神之一。|The Erinyes pursued Orestes for the murder of his mother. 复仇女神因俄瑞斯忒斯弑母而追杀他。|近义词：fury, avenger|反义词:protector, guardian|名词

erinyes:/ɪˈrɪniːz/| n.|复仇女神们，厄里倪厄斯们|The Erinyes, also known as the Furies, were goddesses of vengeance.  厄里倪厄斯，也被称为复仇女神，是复仇女神。|The erinyes were feared for their relentless pursuit of justice.  人们害怕厄里倪厄斯无情地追求正义。|近义词：furies, avengers|反义词:protectors, guardians|名词
