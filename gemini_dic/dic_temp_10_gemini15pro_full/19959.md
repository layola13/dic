Antonyms: None
词性: adjective


bioenergetics:/ˌbaɪoʊˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪks/

The study of energy transformations in living organisms.
Bioenergetics is a complex field of study. 生物能学是一个复杂的研究领域。
Research in bioenergetics helps us understand how cells produce and use energy. 生物能学的研究帮助我们理解细胞如何产生和利用能量。
Synonyms: bioenergetic
Antonyms: None
