exosmosis:/ˌɛksoʊzˈmoʊsɪs/
noun
外渗，渗出
The movement of a solvent (usually water) from a solution of higher concentration to a solution of lower concentration through a semipermeable membrane.
The wilting of a plant placed in a concentrated salt solution is an example of exosmosis.  将植物放置在浓盐溶液中导致其枯萎是外渗的一个例子。
synonyms: outward osmosis, exfiltration
antonyms: endosmosis
