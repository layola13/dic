decapoda:/dɪˈkæpədə/ | n. | 十足目 | An order of crustaceans within the class Malacostraca, characterized by having ten legs, including shrimps, lobsters, crabs, and crayfish. |  Decapods are a vital part of the marine ecosystem. 十足目是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。 | Lobsters and crabs are decapods. 龙虾和螃蟹是十足目动物。| synonyms:  Malacostraca (partially), crustaceans (partially) | antonyms: None (Decapoda is a taxonomic classification, not a descriptive adjective with a direct opposite.) | noun
