engastrimuth:/ɛnˈɡæstrɪmʌθ/ | n. | 腹语术；腹语 | Ventriloquism; the art of speaking without moving the lips, creating the illusion that the voice is coming from elsewhere, often a puppet or dummy. | The magician amazed the audience with his engastrimuth, making the dummy seem to talk.  魔术师用他的腹语术使观众惊叹，让假人看起来像在说话。 | Synonyms: ventriloquism | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "clear speech" or "articulate speech" in contrast to the disguised voice) | noun
