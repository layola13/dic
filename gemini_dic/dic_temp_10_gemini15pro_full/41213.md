coracle: /ˈkɒrək(ə)l/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrək(ə)l/ (US) | n. | 小圆舟，轻便小船 | A small, round boat made of wickerwork covered with waterproof material, propelled with a paddle. |例句：The fisherman used a coracle to navigate the shallow river. 渔夫用小圆舟在浅河中航行。

例句：Coracles are still used in some parts of the world for fishing. 在世界的一些地方，小圆舟仍然被用于捕鱼。

近义词：boat, skiff, canoe | 反义词: ship, yacht | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Welsh *cwrwgl, from cwrwg ("frame, hull"), from Proto-Celtic *kʷorgos ("circle, round"). Cognate with Irish and Scottish Gaelic curach, Manx curragh.
