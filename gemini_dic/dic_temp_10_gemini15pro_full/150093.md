paludinous:/ˌpælʊˈdaɪnəs/| adj.|沼泽的，多沼泽的；疟疾的|Of or relating to marshes; marshy.  Also, relating to or characterized by malaria, formerly believed to be caused by marsh air. | The explorer trudged through the paludinous landscape, wary of mosquitoes.  探险家步履艰难地穿过沼泽地，提防着蚊子。| The paludinous air hung heavy and humid. 沼泽的空气沉闷而潮湿。 | marshy, swampy, boggy | arid, dry | adjective
