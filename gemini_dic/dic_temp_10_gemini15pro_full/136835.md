nonsilicates:/ˌnɒnˈsɪlɪkɪts/ | n. |非硅酸盐|Minerals that do not contain silicon-oxygen tetrahedra as their primary structural unit. These minerals are less common than silicates in the Earth's crust. |Garnets are examples of nonsilicates found in metamorphic rocks. 石榴石是在变质岩中发现的非硅酸盐的例子。|Non-silicon based minerals are examples of nonsilicates. 非硅基矿物是非硅酸盐的例子。| 近义词: non-silicate minerals | 反义词: silicates | 词性: noun
