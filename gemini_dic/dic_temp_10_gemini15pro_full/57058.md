doubtingnesses:/ˈdaʊtɪŋnəsɪz/
noun
疑惑，怀疑，不确定
The state of being full of doubt; uncertainty.
His doubtingnesses about the project caused delays. 他对项目的怀疑导致了延误。
Near Synonyms: uncertainties, hesitations, misgivings, reservations
Antonyms: certainties, convictions, assurances
词性: 名词

PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if he's right and we're wrong? 如果他是对的而我们是错的怎么办？
What if it rains tomorrow? 如果明天下雨怎么办？


what for = 为了什么
What did you do that for? 你为什么这么做？
What are you saving money for? 你存钱是为了什么？


no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what happens, I'll always be there for you. 无论发生什么事，我都会在你身边。
No matter what they say, I won't change my mind.  无论他们说什么，我都不会改变主意。


what's more = 而且；更重要的是
He's intelligent, and what's more, he's very kind. 他很聪明，而且很善良。
She's a talented musician, and what's more, she's a great teacher. 她是一位才华横溢的音乐家，而且是一位优秀的老师。


what about = ...怎么样
What about going to the cinema tonight? 今晚去看电影怎么样？
I'm going to the park. What about you? 我要去公园。你呢？
