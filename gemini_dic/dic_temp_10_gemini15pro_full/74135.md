foundational:/ˌfaʊnˈdeɪʃənl/| adj.|基础的，根本的|Forming a base or foundation; fundamental.|例句：A foundational principle of democracy is freedom of speech. 民主的基本原则是言论自由。

例句：These courses are foundational to understanding advanced concepts. 这些课程是理解高级概念的基础。|近义词：basic, fundamental, essential, primary, underlying|反义词：superficial, secondary, peripheral|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From foundation + -al.  "Foundation" comes from Old French "fondacion"  and ultimately from the Latin "fundare" (to found, establish, lay the bottom of), from "fundus" (bottom, base).
