delitigate:/diːˈlɪtɪɡeɪt/
verb
消弭诉讼;庭外和解
To resolve a dispute or conflict without going to court; to settle out of court.
The parties agreed to delitigate the matter through mediation. 双方同意通过调解来解决此事。
The company sought to delitigate the claim to avoid costly legal proceedings.  公司试图庭外和解，以避免昂贵的法律程序。
近义词:settle, arbitrate, mediate, compromise, negotiate
反义词:litigate, sue, prosecute
词性:v.

delitigation:/diːˌlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/
noun
消弭诉讼; 庭外和解
The process of resolving a dispute or conflict without going to court; out-of-court settlement.
The delitigation saved both parties time and money. 庭外和解为双方节省了时间和金钱。
The focus on delitigation has led to a rise in alternative dispute resolution methods.  对庭外和解的重视导致了替代性争议解决方法的兴起。
近义词:settlement, arbitration, mediation, compromise, negotiation
反义词:litigation, lawsuit, prosecution
词性:n.


词源:  "De-" (Latin prefix meaning "from, away, off, down, out, reverse, undo") + "litigate" (from Latin "litigare" - to dispute judicially).
