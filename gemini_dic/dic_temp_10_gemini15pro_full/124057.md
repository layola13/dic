morphogenetic:/ˌmɔːrfədʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 形态发生的，形态生成的 | Relating to morphogenesis, the biological process that causes an organism to develop its shape. |  Morphogenetic proteins play a crucial role in embryonic development. 形态发生蛋白在胚胎发育中起着至关重要的作用。

Morphogenetic fields are a controversial topic in developmental biology.  形态发生场是发育生物学中一个有争议的话题。 | 近义词:formative, developmental | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From  "morpho-" (Greek *morphē* meaning "form") + "genetic" (from Greek *genesis* meaning "origin, creation").
