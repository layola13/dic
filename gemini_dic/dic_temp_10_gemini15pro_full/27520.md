campiest:/ˈkæmpɪɪst/
adjective
最做作的，最夸张的
Exaggeratedly theatrical, artificial, or affected in a way that is intended to be entertaining.  Relating to or being camp. (Camp as a style is deliberately exaggerated and theatrical in style, typically for humorous effect.)
例句：That was the campiest movie I've ever seen. 那是我看过的最做作的电影。

例句：His campiest performance was as a flamboyant drag queen. 他最夸张的表演是扮演一个华丽的变装皇后。

近义词：theatrical, exaggerated, flamboyant, over-the-top, affected
反义词：understated, subtle, natural, restrained
词性：adjective
