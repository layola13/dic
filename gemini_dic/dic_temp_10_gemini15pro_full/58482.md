dudgeons:/ˈdʌdʒənz/ |n. |愤懑，愠怒；不高兴|A state of resentment or ill humor; sulky anger. | He left the meeting in high dudgeons. 他怒气冲冲地离开了会议。|Synonyms: resentment, indignation, pique, umbrage, huff | Antonyms: good humor, pleasure, contentment | noun

dudgeon:/ˈdʌdʒən/ |n. |柄，把柄；（尤指匕首的）木柄|The hilt of a dagger or other short weapon, especially a wooden one. | The dagger's dudgeon was intricately carved.  这把匕首的柄雕工精细。|Synonyms: hilt, grip, handle | Antonyms: blade, point | noun
