nonprocurement:/ˌnɑːnprəˈkjʊrmənt/ | n. | 非采购 |  The process of acquiring goods or services *other than* through traditional purchasing methods. This might include receiving donations, bartering, or using existing resources. | The organization relied heavily on nonprocurement methods to furnish its new office. 该组织严重依赖非采购方法来布置新办公室。 | Near synonyms:  alternative acquisition, non-purchase acquisition | Antonyms: procurement, purchasing | Noun
