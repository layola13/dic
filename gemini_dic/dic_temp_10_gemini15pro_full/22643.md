boragewort:/ˈbʌrɪdʒwɜːrt/ | n. |琉璃苣，星状花|A bristly European annual herb (Borago officinalis) having bright blue star-shaped flowers and hairy leaves with a cucumber-like aroma and taste, often used in salads or as a garnish.|例句：Boragewort is said to have medicinal properties. 琉璃苣据说有药用价值。

例句：She added boragewort flowers to her salad for a splash of color and a unique flavor. 她在沙拉里加了琉璃苣花，增添了一抹色彩和独特的风味。|近义词：borage, starflower|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
