lymphomatous:/lɪmˈfɒmətəs/ /lɪmˈfɑːmətəs/
adjective
淋巴瘤的；患淋巴瘤的
Relating to or affected with lymphoma, a type of cancer that originates in lymphocytes (white blood cells).
The patient presented with lymphomatous masses in the neck and chest.  病人颈部和胸部出现淋巴瘤肿块。
The biopsy revealed lymphomatous infiltration of the liver. 活检显示肝脏有淋巴瘤浸润。
近义词:lymphoblastoid, lymphoid
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 healthy, non-cancerous)
词性:adjective
