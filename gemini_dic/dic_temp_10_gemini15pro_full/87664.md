hexaemeric:/ˌhɛksəˈmɛrɪk/
|adj.|
六聚体的，由六个亚基组成的|
Composed of six subunits; specifically (Biochemistry) of a protein: composed of six monomeric subunits.|
Hexaemeric proteins are often involved in complex biological processes. 六聚体蛋白通常参与复杂的生物过程。
The virus has a hexaemeric capsid structure.  这种病毒具有六聚体衣壳结构。|
近义词: hexamerous, hexametric|
反义词: monomeric|
词性: adjective
