noncontrolling:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊlɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtroʊlɪŋ/
adjective
非控股的，无控制权的
Having or exerting no control.  Relating to a shareholder who does not have control over a company.
The noncontrolling interest in the subsidiary was sold last year.  去年，该子公司的非控股权益被出售了。
Minority interest, non-dominant
Controlling, majority
