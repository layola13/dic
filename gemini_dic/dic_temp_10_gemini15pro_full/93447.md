illuminee:/ɪˌluːmɪˈniː/
n.
被启迪者，被启蒙者
a person who has been enlightened or instructed spiritually or intellectually.
The illuminee experienced a profound sense of peace after the retreat.  这位被启迪者在静修之后体验到了一种深沉的平静。
The guru's illuminees followed his teachings devoutly.  这位大师的信徒虔诚地追随着他的教诲。
disciple, follower, student
opponent, critic
noun
