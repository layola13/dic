half-smiling:/ˌhæfˈsmaɪlɪŋ/
adjective
半笑着的，微微笑着的
Wearing a half-smiling expression, she greeted me at the door.  她半笑着在门口迎接我。
With a half-smiling face, he accepted the award. 他半笑着接受了奖项。
近义词: smirking, faintly smiling
反义词: frowning, scowling
词性: adjective

Etymology:
"half-" + "smiling"
"half-" Old English healf
"smiling"  from smile (v.) Old Norse smila "smile;"
