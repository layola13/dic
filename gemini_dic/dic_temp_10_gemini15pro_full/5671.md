altrices: /ˈæltrɪsiːz/
noun
female birds that are involved in raising young, especially those that are not their own offspring.
Female birds that help raise young, often not their own.  These birds assist the breeding pair in feeding and protecting the chicks.
The altrices diligently brought food to the nestlings.  这些雌鸟辛勤地给雏鸟带食物。
The experienced altrices played a crucial role in the survival of the young.  经验丰富的雌鸟在幼鸟的生存中扮演了至关重要的角色。
Synonyms: helpers, foster mothers
