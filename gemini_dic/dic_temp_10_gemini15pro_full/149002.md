ovulary: /ˈɒvjuːləri/ (US) /ˈəʊvjʊləri/ (UK)| n. |胚珠|The part of the ovary of seed plants that contains the female germ cell and after fertilization becomes the seed. | The ovulary of the flower develops into the fruit after pollination.  这朵花的胚珠在授粉后会发育成果实。|  The ovules are contained within the ovulary. 胚珠包含在胚珠内。 |synonyms:  megasporangium | antonyms: N/A | noun

ovule: /ˈɒvjuːl/ (US) /ˈəʊvjuːl/ (UK)| n. |胚珠|A small body in seed plants that contains the female germ cell and after fertilization becomes the seed. | The ovule develops into a seed after fertilization. 胚珠受精后发育成种子。 | Many ovules are present in the ovary of a flower.  一朵花的子房里有很多胚珠。| synonyms: seed bud | antonyms: N/A | noun
