hypnotising:/ˈhɪpnətaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 催眠的，令人着迷的 |  Exerting a compelling power, as if inducing hypnosis; fascinating.  | Her voice was soft and hypnotising. 她的声音轻柔而迷人。 | The hypnotising rhythm of the waves lulled me to sleep. 海浪催眠般的节奏让我昏昏欲睡。 | 近义词: mesmerizing, captivating, enchanting, spellbinding | 反义词: boring, tedious, dull, uninteresting | 词性: adjective | 词源: From hypnotize + -ing, from Greek hypnos "sleep".
