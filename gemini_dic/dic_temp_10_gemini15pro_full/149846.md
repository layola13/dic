pale:/peɪl/| adj. |苍白的，无血色的；暗淡的；微弱的| light in color or having little color|Her face was pale with fear. 她的脸色因恐惧而苍白。|Synonyms: wan, ashen, pallid|Antonyms: flushed, ruddy, vibrant|adjective

pale:/peɪl/| v. |变得苍白；使失色；相形见绌|to become pale; to lose color; to seem less important|Her face paled when she heard the news. 听到这个消息，她的脸色变得苍白。|Synonyms: blanch, fade, diminish|Antonyms: brighten, flush, intensify|verb
