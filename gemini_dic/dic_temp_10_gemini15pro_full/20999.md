blesbok:/ˈblɛsbɒk/ (UK), /ˈblɛsbɑːk/ (US)| n. | 角马, 白脸角马 | A South African antelope (Damaliscus pygargus phillipsi) related to the bontebok, having a distinctive white blaze on the face and a dark chocolate-colored coat. It is found in open grasslands. | Example 1: We saw a herd of blesbok grazing on the plains. 我们看到一群角马在平原上吃草。

Example 2: The blesbok is a common sight in South African national parks. 在南非国家公园里，角马很常见。 | 近义词:bontebok, antelope | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun

词源: From Afrikaans *blesbok*, from Dutch *bles* "blaze, white spot" + *bok* "buck, male antelope".
