bituminiferous:/bɪˌtjuːmɪˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含沥青的；产生沥青的
Containing or yielding bitumen.
The bituminiferous sandstone was a source of asphalt. 这种含沥青的砂岩是沥青的来源。
The quarry is known for its bituminiferous limestone. 这个采石场以其含沥青的石灰岩而闻名。
synonyms: asphaltic, bituminous
antonyms: non-bituminous


Etymology:
From Latin *bituminosus (“bituminous”), from bitumen (“bitumen, asphalt”) + -fer (“-bearing”), from ferō (“I bear, carry”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bheid- (“to split, cleave”).
