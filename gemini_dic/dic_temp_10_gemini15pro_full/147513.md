overpampers:/ˌoʊvərˈpæmpər/ |v.| 过分溺爱，过分娇惯 | to treat with excessive indulgence; to spoil. | She overpampers her dog, buying it gourmet food and designer clothes. 她过分溺爱她的狗，给它买美食和名牌衣服。| He overpampers his children, giving them everything they want. 他过分溺爱他的孩子，给他们想要的一切。| 近义词: spoil, coddle, indulge | 反义词: neglect, deprive |词性:动词
