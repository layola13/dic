overexplains:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪnz/
verb
过度解释；解释得过于详细
to explain something in too much detail, often in a way that is confusing or annoying
"He overexplained the joke, and it wasn't funny anymore." 他把笑话解释得太多了，一点也不好笑了。
Synonyms: elaborates excessively, belabors, goes on and on about
Antonyms: summarizes, simplifies, gets to the point
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "explain" (verb meaning "make something clear or easy to understand")
"over-" (表示“过度地”的前缀) + "explain" (动词，意为“使某事清晰易懂”)
