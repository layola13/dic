non-promotion:/ˌnɒn prəˈməʊʃn/ (US) /ˌnɑːn prəˈmoʊʃn/| n.|不晋升，未晋升|Lack of promotion or advancement to a higher position or rank.  The state of not being promoted.|Due to his poor performance, he faced non-promotion this year. 由于他表现不佳，他今年面临着不晋升。|Synonyms: stagnation, standstill|Antonyms: promotion, advancement|noun
