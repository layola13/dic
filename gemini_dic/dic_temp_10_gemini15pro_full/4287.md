airworthier:/ˈɛərwɜːrðiər/
adjective
更适航的
More suitable for air travel; safer or more reliable in flight.
The new design made the aircraft airworthier. 新的设计使飞机更适航。
近义词:safer, more reliable
反义词:less airworthy, unsafe
词性:adjective

airworthier:/ˈɛərwɜːrðiər/
adjective
更适航的
More suitable for air travel; safer or more reliable in flight.

近义词:more suitable for flight, sounder

词源:
From airworthy + -er (comparative suffix).  Airworthy itself comes from air + worthy.
