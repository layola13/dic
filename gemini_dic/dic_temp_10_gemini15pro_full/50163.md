deobstruct:/ˌdiːəbˈstrʌkt/
verb
疏通，清除障碍
To remove an obstruction or blockage from something.
The surgeon deobstructed the artery.  外科医生疏通了动脉。
The road was deobstructed after the landslide.  山体滑坡后道路被疏通了。
近义词:clear, unblock, unclog
反义词:obstruct, block, clog
词性:verb

词源:
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + obstruct (verb meaning "to block or fill with obstacles").  Obstruct comes from Latin obstructus, past participle of obstruere "to build in front of, block, bar," from ob "against" + struere "to pile, build."
