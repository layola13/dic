elaidate:/ɪˈleɪɪˌdeɪt/| v. | 反油酸酯化；使成为反油酸 | To convert into elaidic acid or an elaidate. |  The process elaidates the unsaturated fat.  这个过程将不饱和脂肪反油酸酯化。|Synonyms: isomerize, transform | Antonyms: hydrogenate, saturate | verb
elaidate:/ɪˈleɪɪˌdeɪt/| n. | 反油酸盐；反油酸酯 | A salt or ester of elaidic acid. | The elaidate formed a crystalline solid.  形成的反油酸酯是一种结晶状固体。 | Synonyms: trans isomer | Antonyms: cis isomer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
