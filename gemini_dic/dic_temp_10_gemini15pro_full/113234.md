lusted: /ˈlʌstɪd/ | v. | 渴望，垂涎，贪求 | To have felt a strong sexual desire for someone or something. | He lusted after her for years. 他对她垂涎多年。|  She lusted after the expensive sports car. 她渴望拥有那辆昂贵的跑车。 | 近义词: craved, yearned, desired, coveted | 反义词: abhorred, detested, loathed | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *lusten*, from Old English *lystan* (“to please, delight, cause pleasure, desire, lust”), from Proto-Germanic *lustjaną* (“to lust”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewdʰ- (“to grow, be exuberant”). Cognate with Dutch lusten (“to desire, lust”), German lüsten (“to desire, lust”), Icelandic lysta (“to desire, list”).
