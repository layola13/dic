hesperian:/hɛˈspɪəriən/
adjective
西方的；大 Hesperides 仙女花园的；晚熟的；关于晚上的
Relating to the west, or the evening; western.  Relating to the Hesperides, the nymphs of the evening in Greek mythology.
例句：The hesperian sky blazed with the setting sun. 夕阳西下，西方的天空一片火红。

例句：Hesperian fruits were said to grant immortality.  据说赫斯珀里得斯的果实能让人长生不老。
近义词：western, occidental
反义词：eastern, oriental
词性: adjective


hesperian:/hɛˈspɪəriən/
noun
西方人； Hesperides 仙女花园的居民
A person living in the west; an inhabitant of the Hesperides.
例句：The hesperian welcomed the travelers from the east. 西方人欢迎来自东方的旅客。

例句：The hesperians were said to guard the golden apples. 据说赫斯珀里得斯的居民守护着金苹果。
近义词：westerner
反义词：easterner
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Latin *Hesperius, from Ancient Greek Ἑσπέριος (Hespérios, “western, of the evening”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wespero- (“evening”).
