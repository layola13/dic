companiable:/kəmˈpeɪniəbl/ /kəmˈpænɪəbəl/
adjective
友善的；好相处的；适于做伴的
Enjoyable to be with; sociable.
She's a very companionable person. 她是一个非常友善的人。
近义词: sociable, friendly, amiable, gregarious, convivial, affable
反义词: unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, reserved, solitary
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From late Middle English: from Old French *compaignable*, from compaigne ‘companion’, from late Latin companio ‘companion’.
