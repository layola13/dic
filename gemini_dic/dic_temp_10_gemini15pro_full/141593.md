opprobrium:/əˈproʊbriəm/
n.
耻辱，责骂
harsh criticism or censure.
The politician faced opprobrium after the scandal broke.  这位政客在丑闻曝光后面临着骂名。
近义词:disgrace, shame, ignominy, obloquy, reproach
反义词:honor, praise, acclaim, respect
词性:名词


opprobriums:/əˈproʊbriəmz/
n.
耻辱，责骂（复数）
plural form of opprobrium.
He endured the opprobriums of his peers for his controversial views. 他因其有争议的观点而忍受了同龄人的责骂。
近义词:disgraces, shames, ignominies, obloquies, reproaches
反义词:honors, praises, acclaims, respects
词性:名词
