deductor:/dɪˈdʌktər/ | n. | 扣除者；减除者；推论者 | someone who deducts something, especially an amount of money | The deductor subtracted the expenses from the total income. 扣除者从总收入中减去了支出。 | synonyms: subtracter, inferrer, reasoner | antonyms: adder, contributor | noun

deduct:/dɪˈdʌkt/ | v. | 扣除，减去；推断 | to subtract or take away (an amount or part) from a total |  The company deducts taxes from your paycheck. 公司从你的工资中扣除税款。| synonyms: subtract, withdraw, infer | antonyms: add, contribute | verb

deductible:/dɪˈdʌktɪbl/ | adj. | 可扣除的；可减免的 | able to be deducted, especially from taxable income |  Medical expenses are often deductible. 医疗费用通常是可扣除的。 | synonyms: subtractable | antonyms: nondeductible | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Latin *deductor, from deduct- ‘led away’, from the verb deducere (see deduct).
