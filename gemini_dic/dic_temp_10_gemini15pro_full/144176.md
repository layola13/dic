outshrieks:/ˌaʊtˈʃriːks/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声
to shriek more loudly than
The child outshrieked his mother in the toy store.  这孩子在玩具店里比他妈妈的喊叫声还大。
近义词: outyells, drowns out
反义词: whispers, mumbles
词性: verb

Etymology:
From out- + shriek.  "Out-" prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding. "Shriek" comes from Middle English *shriken*, from Old Norse *skrǣkja*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *skrīkaną*.
