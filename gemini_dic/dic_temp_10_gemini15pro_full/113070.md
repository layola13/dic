lumpsucker:/ˈlʌmpsʌkər/| n. | 圆鳍鱼，䲢鱼|A type of fish (Cyclopterus lumpus) that has a sucking disc on its underside, allowing it to cling to rocks.|例句：The lumpsucker adhered to the rock, unmoved by the strong current.  圆鳍鱼附着在岩石上，不受强流的影响。

例句：Lumpsuckers are known for their adhesive discs. 圆鳍鱼以它们的吸盘而闻名。|近义词：sea snail, clingfish|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun
