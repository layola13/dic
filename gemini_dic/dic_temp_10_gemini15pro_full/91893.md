hyperkinetic:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɪnɛtɪk/ | adj. |过度活跃的，活动过度的|Characterized by excessive movement and activity; restless.|例句：The hyperkinetic child was constantly fidgeting in his seat.  这个过度活跃的孩子在他的座位上不停地扭来扭去。|例句：The teacher struggled to manage the hyperkinetic energy of the students. 老师努力控制学生们过度活跃的精力。|近义词：overactive, restless, energetic, fidgety|反义词：lethargic, sluggish, calm, quiet|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Greek *hyper* "over, above" + *kinetikos* "moving," from *kinein* "to move."
