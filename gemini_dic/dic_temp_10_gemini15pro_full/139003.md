nymphomany:/ˌnɪmfəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 女子淫狂，女性色情狂 |  A mental disorder marked by compulsive and excessive sexual desire in women.  Historically, a pejorative and unscientific term.  Modern psychology generally avoids this term, preferring diagnoses like hypersexuality or compulsive sexual behavior. | Her alleged nymphomania was the talk of the town. 她所谓的淫狂是镇上的谈资。 | 近义词: hypersexuality, compulsive sexual behavior | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider terms like frigidity or sexual aversion) | 词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *nymphomania*, from Greek *numphē* (bride, young woman) + *mania* (madness).
