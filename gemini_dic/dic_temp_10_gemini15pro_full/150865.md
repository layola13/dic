paragraphisms:/ˈpærəɡræfɪzəmz/| n.|段落主义，过分注重段落结构的写作风格|A style of writing that focuses excessively on paragraph structure, often at the expense of clarity and flow.  It can involve overly short or formulaic paragraphs, or an excessive concern with the visual appearance of the text.|His essay suffered from paragraphisms, with each point isolated in its own short paragraph, making it difficult to follow the overall argument.|近义词:formalism, rigidity|反义词:fluidity, naturalism|noun
