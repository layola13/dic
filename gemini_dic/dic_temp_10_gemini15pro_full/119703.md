midpoint:/ˈmɪdpɔɪnt/|n.|中点，正中央|The midpoint of the line is where it is divided exactly in half.  这条线的终点是它被精确地分成两半的地方。|例句：The midpoint of the journey is often a good time for a break. 旅途的中点通常是休息的好时机。

例句：The negotiators are trying to find a midpoint between the two sides' demands.  谈判者们正试图在双方要求之间找到一个中间点。|近义词：center, middle, halfway point|反义词:end, extremity|词性:n.

词源:mid (Middle English, from Old English midde) + point (Middle English, from Old French point, from Latin punctum, from pungere, to prick).
