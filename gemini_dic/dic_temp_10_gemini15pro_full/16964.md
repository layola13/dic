bast:/bæst/ | n. | 内皮，韧皮，编织材料 | The inner layer of the bark of a tree or other plant; fiber obtained from this inner bark and used for making rope, mats, etc. |  The bast of the tree was used to make rope.  这棵树的韧皮被用来制作绳索。| synonyms: fiber, phloem, inner bark | antonyms: xylem, heartwood | noun
