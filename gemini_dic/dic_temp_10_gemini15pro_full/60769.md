electrolytical:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈlɪtɪkəl/ | adj. |电解的；电解质的 | Relating to or involving electrolysis; produced by electrolysis. | The electrolytical process separates the hydrogen and oxygen in water. 电解过程将水中的氢和氧分离。|  The company uses an electrolytical method to refine copper.  这家公司使用电解法提炼铜。 |Synonyms: electrolytic | Antonyms: None | Adjective

electrolyte:/ɪˈlɛktrəlaɪt/ | n. | 电解质 | A substance that conducts electricity when dissolved in water or melted. | Sports drinks contain electrolytes to help athletes rehydrate. 运动饮料含有电解质，帮助运动员补充水分。 |  Sodium chloride is a common electrolyte. 氯化钠是一种常见的电解质。| Synonyms: ionic conductor | Antonyms: non-electrolyte | Noun
