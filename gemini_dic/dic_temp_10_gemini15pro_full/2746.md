adulterator: /əˈdʌltəreɪtər/
n.
掺杂者；伪造者；通奸者
A person who adulterates something, especially food or drink.
The food adulterator was fined heavily.  这个食品掺假者被处以重罚。

The adulterator was caught red-handed.  这个伪造者被当场抓获。

近义词:  debaser, falsifier, polluter, contaminator
反义词: purifier
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin adulterator, from Latin adulterare "to corrupt, debase," from ad- "to" + alter "other."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *al- "beyond."
