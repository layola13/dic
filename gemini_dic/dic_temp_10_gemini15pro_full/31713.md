chawg:/tʃɔːɡ/ | n. | 咀嚼；嚼碎的食物；一块粗糙的咀嚼物 | A piece of something chewed or intended for chewing.  The act of chewing. | The dog enjoyed his chawg on the bone. 狗很享受啃骨头。 | 近义词：chew, bite, gnaw | 反义词: swallow | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely imitative of the sound of chewing.  Possibly related to "chaw," a dialectal variant of "chew."
