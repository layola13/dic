overwild:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪld/ | adj. | 过于野生的，野性过度的 | Excessively wild; too wild.  It describes something that has become excessively wild, perhaps due to lack of human intervention or control.  | The overwild garden became a haven for local wildlife.  过于野生的花园成了当地野生动物的避难所。

The overwild horses were difficult to tame. 野性过度的马很难驯服。 | 近义词: feral, untamed, uncontrolled | 反义词: tame, domesticated, cultivated | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "wild" (adjective).  "Over-" comes from Old English "ofer," and "wild" comes from Old English "wilde."
