globosity:/ɡloʊˈbɑːsəti/
n.
球状；球形；圆形
The state or quality of being globular; roundness; sphericity.
The planet's globosity is slightly distorted at the poles.  这颗行星的球形在两极略有变形。

The apparent globosity of the moon. 月亮明显的球形。


近义词:roundness, sphericity, rotundity
反义词:flatness, angularity
词性:noun
