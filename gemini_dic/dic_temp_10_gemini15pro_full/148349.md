overspecify:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 过度指定，过分详细说明 | To provide more details or specifications than are necessary or useful, often making a task or problem more complex than it needs to be. | The instructions overspecified the procedure, confusing the new employees.  指令对程序作了过分详细的说明，把新员工都搞糊涂了。

The architect overspecified the materials, driving up the cost of the project.  建筑师对材料的要求过于具体，导致项目成本上升。 | 近义词: overdefine, overelaborate | 反义词: underspecify | 词性: 动词
