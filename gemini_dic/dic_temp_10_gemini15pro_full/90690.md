humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n. int.|骗局；欺骗；谎言；废话；（圣诞节吃的）薄荷硬糖|A deceptive or false statement or action, often intended to deceive or mislead; nonsense; a hard candy, typically peppermint flavored, associated with Christmas.|例句：The weight-loss advertisement was a complete humbug.  这个减肥广告完全是个骗局。

例句：He dismissed their promises as humbug.  他认为他们的承诺都是废话。|近义词：fraud, hoax, sham, nonsense, baloney|反义词：truth, honesty|词性:名词,感叹词
