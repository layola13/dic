babushka:/bəˈbʊʃkə/ (US), /bəˈbuːʃkə/ (UK)| n. | 老妇人；(俄罗斯)戴着头巾的老妇人；(套叠的)俄罗斯玩偶 |An elderly woman, especially one wearing a headscarf.  A set of traditional Russian nesting dolls.|My grandmother, a true babushka, always had a pot of tea brewing. 我的祖母，一个真正的俄罗斯老奶奶，总是煮着一壶茶。| The child loved playing with her set of brightly painted babushkas.  这个孩子喜欢玩她那套色彩鲜艳的俄罗斯套娃。|Synonyms: grandma, grandmother, granny, nan (informal), matriarch|Antonyms: child, youngster|Noun
