ochrea: /ˈɒkriːə/ (US) /ˈɒkriːə/ (UK)| n. |托叶鞘，膜质鞘|A membranous sheath formed from two stipules that surrounds the stem at the node in plants of the Polygonaceae family, such as rhubarb and knotweed.|The distinctive ochrea of the knotweed helps with identification.|近义词：sheath, stipular sheath|反义词: N/A|noun
