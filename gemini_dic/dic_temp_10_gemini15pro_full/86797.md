hemapophysis:/ˌhɛməˈpɒfɪsɪs/ | n. | 血骨突 | A bony process or outgrowth of a vertebra, arising from the centrum and representing a modified rib or the fusion of a rib with a vertebra. | The hemapophysis contributes to the protection of the spinal cord. 血骨突有助于保护脊髓。 |  synonyms: transverse process (in some contexts), costal process | antonyms: (no direct antonym, perhaps vertebral body if contrasting the outgrowth with the main body) | noun

hemapophysis:/ˌhɛməˈpɒfɪsɪs/ | n. | 血骨突 | A bony projection arising from the vertebral centrum, often representing a fused rib element. | In some fish, the hemapophysis is quite prominent. 在某些鱼类中，血骨突非常突出。 | synonyms:  parapophysis, pleurapophysis (in some contexts) | antonyms: (no direct antonym) | noun
