annualist:/ˈænjuəlɪst/| n.|年金领取者；年鉴编纂者|A person who receives an annuity or compiles annals.|例句：The annualist meticulously recorded the events of the year.  年鉴编纂者一丝不苟地记录了当年的事件。

例句：The annualist received a fixed sum of money each year. 年金领取者每年收到一笔固定金额的钱。|近义词：annuitant, chronicler, historian|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *annualista, from Latin annus (“year”) + -ista.
