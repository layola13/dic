foggiest:/ˈfɒɡiɪst/ (UK) /ˈfɑːɡiɪst/ (US)| adj.|最模糊的，最不清楚的|Superlative adjective of foggy.  Means extremely unclear, vague, or uncertain.|I haven't the foggiest idea what you're talking about. 我完全不知道你在说什么。|近义词：haziest, murkiest, vaguest, unclear, uncertain|反义词：clearest, most obvious, certain|词性:形容词
