epitomizes: /ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzɪz/
verb
体现；是…的缩影；概括
be a perfect example of a quality or type of thing
Her work epitomizes the struggle of artists against a hostile world. 她的作品体现了艺术家与充满敌意的世界斗争的缩影。
synonyms: typifies, embodies, exemplifies, represents, symbolizes
antonyms: misrepresents, distorts


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin epitomizare, from Ancient Greek ἐπιτομίζειν (epitomizein, “to abridge, summarize”), from ἐπιτέμνειν (epitemnein, “to cut off, abridge”), from ἐπί (epi, “upon, on”) + τέμνειν (temnein, “to cut”).
