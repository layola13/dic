clean slate:/kliːn sleɪt/
|n.|新的开始；清白的历史；干净的石板|A clean slate refers to a fresh start, as if one's past mistakes or debts have been erased.|He was given a clean slate after he apologized. 他道歉后得到了一个新的开始。|synonyms: fresh start, new beginning, blank page|antonyms: criminal record, bad reputation|noun

clean slate:/kliːn sleɪt/
|adj.|清白的；全新的|Clean slate can also function attributively, describing something brand new or unblemished. |This is a clean slate project. 这是一个全新的项目。|synonyms: pristine, spotless, immaculate|antonyms: tainted, sullied, blemished|adjective
