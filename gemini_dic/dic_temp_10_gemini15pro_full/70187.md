fibrination:/ˌfaɪbrɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
纤维化；纤维蛋白形成
The process of forming fibrin, an insoluble protein that is essential for blood clotting.
Excessive fibrination can lead to thrombosis. 过度的纤维蛋白形成会导致血栓形成。
近义词：coagulation, clotting
反义词：fibrinolysis
词性：名词
