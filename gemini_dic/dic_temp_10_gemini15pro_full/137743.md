nonvaporosity:/ˌnɒnˌveɪpəˈrɒsɪti/
n.|非挥发性；不挥发性|The nonvaporosity of the substance makes it suitable for use in high-temperature applications.  这种物质的非挥发性使其适用于高温应用。|
nonvaporosity: The nonvaporosity of the oil prevented it from evaporating quickly.  这种油的非挥发性防止它快速蒸发。
|Synonyms: nonvolatility, stability, permanence|Antonyms: volatility, evaporativeness, instability|noun
