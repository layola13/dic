fatalistic:/ˌfeɪtəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
宿命论的
Believing that all events are predetermined and therefore inevitable.
She had a fatalistic attitude toward the future, believing there was nothing she could do to change it. 她对未来抱有宿命论的态度，认为自己无力改变任何事情。

He accepted his fate fatalistically. 他宿命地接受了自己的命运。

近义词:resignation, acceptance,  defeatist, pessimistic
反义词:optimistic, hopeful, proactive

词性:adjective
