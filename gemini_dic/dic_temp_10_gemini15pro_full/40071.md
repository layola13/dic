contagioned:/kənˈteɪdʒənd/ | adj. |受感染的，受传染的 | Affected by contagion; infected. This word is archaic and rarely used.  Modern usage prefers "infected" or "contaminated." |  例句：The contagioned air spread the disease. 受污染的空气传播了疾病。(This example is archaic and sounds unnatural in modern English.) | 近义词：infected, contaminated, tainted | 反义词：uninfected, uncontaminated, pure | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *contagionatus, past participle of contagiare "to touch closely," from Latin contagio "a touching, contact," from contingere "to touch on all sides," from com- "together" + tangere "to touch."
