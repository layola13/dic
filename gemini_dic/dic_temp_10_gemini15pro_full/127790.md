neighbourer:/ˈneɪbərər/ (UK) /ˈneɪbərbər/ (US)| n.|邻居|A person who lives next door or very near to another.|例句：My neighbourer brought me a cake. 我的邻居给我送来了一块蛋糕。

例句：We had a barbecue with our neighbourers last weekend. 上周末我们和邻居一起烧烤了。|近义词：neighbor (US), next-door neighbor|反义词: stranger|词性:noun
