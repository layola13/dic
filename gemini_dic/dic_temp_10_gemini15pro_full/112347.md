lorication:/ˌlɔːrɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.| 装甲，鳞甲|The process of covering something with protective scales or plates, like the armor on a medieval knight or the scales of a fish.  It refers to the armor itself, too.|The knight's loricatio protected him in battle. 骑士的盔甲在战斗中保护了他。| 近义词: armor, armour, plating, scale, mail | 反义词: vulnerability, exposure
词性: 名词
