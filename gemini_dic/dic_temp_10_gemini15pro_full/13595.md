attenuation:/əˌtenjuˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 衰减；减弱；稀释 | the reduction of the force, effect, or value of something | The attenuation of the signal was significant over long distances. 信号在长距离传输中衰减得很厉害。| synonyms: weakening, reduction, diminution, decrease, lessening | antonyms: amplification, intensification, strengthening | noun

attenuate:/əˈtenjuˌeɪt/ | v. | 衰减；减弱；稀释 | to reduce the force, effect, or value of something |  Heavy curtains attenuated the sunlight. 厚重的窗帘减弱了阳光。| synonyms: weaken, reduce, diminish, decrease, lessen | antonyms: amplify, intensify, strengthen | verb
