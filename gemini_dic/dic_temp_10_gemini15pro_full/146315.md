overfeel:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːl/| v. |过分感觉；过度感受；反应过激|To experience or express a feeling with excessive intensity.  To react too strongly to something.| She overfelt the loss of her pet hamster, crying for days. 她对失去宠物仓鼠的悲伤过度，哭了好多天。|He overfelt the criticism and quit his job. 他对批评反应过激，辞掉了工作。|overreact, exaggerate, overdo|understate, downplay|verb
