breezed:/briːzd/
verb
吹微风；轻松完成；飘然而过
To move casually, easily, or quickly.
He breezed through the exam. 他轻松地完成了考试。
breeze through something
近义词:  sailed, glided, swept, drifted, floated
反义词: struggled, labored, toiled
词性: verb

breezed:/briːzd/
adjective
(非正式)凉爽通风的
A breezy place is pleasantly windy.
It was a breezy afternoon. 那是一个凉爽通风的下午。
近义词: airy, windy, fresh
反义词: stuffy, still, airless
词性: adjective
