mismatching:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃɪŋ/| adj.|不匹配的，不协调的|Not matching; incongruous or unsuitable.|The mismatching socks made her outfit look slightly off.  不匹配的袜子使她的服装看起来有点不对劲。|Examples: The curtains and carpet were mismatching, creating a jarring visual effect.  窗帘和地毯不匹配，造成了刺眼的视觉效果.  His mismatching tie and shirt clashed terribly. 他不匹配的领带和衬衫非常不协调。|Synonyms:  incompatible, conflicting, clashing, discordant, unsuited|Antonyms: matching, compatible, harmonious, coordinated, consistent|adjective

mismatch:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃ/| n. vt.|不匹配，配错；使不匹配|A failure to match or correspond; to match badly or inaccurately.|There was a mismatch between the skills required for the job and the candidate's qualifications. 这项工作所需的技能与候选人的资格不匹配。|Examples: The marriage was a mismatch from the start. 这桩婚姻从一开始就不合适。  The coach mismatched the players, resulting in a crushing defeat. 教练错误地安排了球员，导致惨败。|Synonyms: disparity, incongruity, incompatibility, inconsistency|Antonyms: match, compatibility, harmony, consistency|noun, verb
