barbizon:/ˈbɑːrbɪzən/ | n. | 巴比松，巴比松画派 |  A town and commune in north-central France, southeast of Paris. It was an important centre of landscape painting in the mid-19th century. |  The Barbizon painters were known for their depictions of rural life. 巴比松画派的画家以描绘乡村生活而闻名。 |  Synonyms: Barbizon school | Antonyms: N/A | noun

barbizon:/ˈbɑːrbɪzən/ | adj. | 巴比松风格的，巴比松画派的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Barbizon school of painting. |  The museum has a fine collection of Barbizon landscapes.  博物馆收藏了精美的巴比松风景画。 | Synonyms:  rustic, pastoral | Antonyms: urban, modern | adjective
