huswife:/ˈhʌsˌwaɪf/ (US) /ˈhʌzwɪf/ (UK)| n.|家庭主妇；针线盒；<古>节俭的持家人|A married woman whose primary occupation is managing the household.  A small container for needles, thread, and other sewing implements.  A thrifty and diligent homemaker (archaic).|例句：She was a dedicated housewife and mother. 她是一位尽职尽责的家庭主妇和母亲。

|例句：My grandmother kept her sewing needles in a silver housewife. 我的祖母把她的缝衣针放在一个银质的针线盒里。|近义词：homemaker, spouse, wife, (for sewing kit) sewing kit, needle case|反义词:career woman, working woman (for person); (for sewing kit) none|词性:noun
