albinotic:/ˌælbɪˈnɒtɪk/ | adj. | 白化的，患白化病的 | Affected by albinism, a genetic condition characterized by a lack of melanin pigment, resulting in white skin, hair, and eyes. |  The albinotic rabbit stood out against the dark grass.  白化的兔子在深色的草地上很显眼。 | synonyms: albinistic, amelanistic | antonyms: pigmented | adjective
