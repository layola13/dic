hyperintellectualnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
过度理智, 过度知识化
The state or quality of being excessively intellectual or overly concerned with intellectual pursuits.
His hyperintellectualnesses made him seem out of touch with reality.  他过度理智，让他看起来脱离现实。
Her hyperintellectualnesses often led to convoluted and impractical solutions. 她的过度知识化经常导致解决方案复杂且不切实际。
Near Synonyms:  overintellectualism, intellectualism, cerebrality
Antonyms:  simplicity, practicality, artlessness
