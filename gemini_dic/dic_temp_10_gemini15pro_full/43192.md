crabbers:/ˈkræbərz/
n.
捕蟹人，爱抱怨的人
People who catch crabs; people who complain a lot.
例句：The crabbers were out early this morning, hoping for a good catch.  今天早上捕蟹人很早就出去了，希望能捕到很多螃蟹。

例句：He's a real crabber, always finding something to complain about. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。
近义词：complainers, whiners, pessimists
反义词：optimists, positive thinkers
词性: 名词(plural)

crabber:/ˈkræbər/
n.
捕蟹人，爱抱怨的人
A person who catches crabs; a person who complains a lot.
例句：My uncle is a crabber and spends his summers on the Chesapeake Bay. 我叔叔是一个捕蟹人，夏天都在切萨皮克湾度过。

例句：Don't be such a crabber! Try to look on the bright side. 别这么爱抱怨！试着看看好的一面。
近义词：complainer, whiner, pessimist
反义词：optimist, positive thinker
词性: 名词(singular)
