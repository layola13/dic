marginalized:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪzd/ | adj. |边缘化的；处于社会边缘的|  Treated as insignificant or peripheral.  Put in a position of little or no power, influence, or importance.  |  Many indigenous communities feel marginalized by mainstream society. 许多原住民社区感到被主流社会边缘化。| synonyms: excluded, disadvantaged, dispossessed, ostracized, peripheral, voiceless | antonyms: central, included, mainstream, privileged, empowered | adjective

marginalize:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使边缘化；排斥 | To treat (a person, group, or concept) as insignificant or peripheral. |  Government policies have marginalized certain ethnic groups. 政府政策使某些族裔群体边缘化。 | synonyms: exclude, sideline, isolate, relegate, downplay | antonyms: include, integrate, empower, centralize | verb
