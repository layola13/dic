delphic:/ˈdɛlfɪk/| adj.|神谕的；含糊的；难以理解的|Of or relating to the ancient Greek oracle at Delphi; ambiguous and difficult to interpret.|例句：Her delphic pronouncements were open to various interpretations. 她的神谕式 pronouncements 可以有多种解释。

例句：The poem's ending is typically delphic, leaving the reader with more questions than answers. 这首诗的结尾典型的晦涩难懂，给读者留下了更多的问题而不是答案。|近义词：ambiguous, obscure, enigmatic, cryptic|反义词：clear, explicit, straightforward|词性:形容词

词源: From Delphi, the site of the famous oracle in ancient Greece.
