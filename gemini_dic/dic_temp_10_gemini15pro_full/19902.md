binoxalate:/baɪˈnɒksəleɪt/ /baɪˈnɑːksəleɪt/
noun|草酸氢盐；酸式草酸盐|A salt of oxalic acid containing the anion HC2O4− or the anion C2O42− with one hydrogen replaced by a metal cation.|Potassium binoxalate is used in metal cleaning and polishing. 草酸氢钾用于金属清洁和抛光。|近义词: hydrogen oxalate|反义词: N/A
词性: 名词
