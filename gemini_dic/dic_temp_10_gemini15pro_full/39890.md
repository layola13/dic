constituting:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪŋ/
verb
构成，组成
being the parts or elements of something; making up or forming something.
Twelve months constitute a year. 十二个月构成一年。
Synonyms: composing, forming, comprising, making up
Antonyms:  destroying, dismantling, dissolving
verb

constituting:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪŋ/
adjective
构成的，组成的
serving to form or compose something.
The constituting members of the committee. 委员会的组成成员。
Synonyms: component, elemental, integral
Antonyms:  nonessential, superfluous, extraneous
adjective
