cocainism:/koʊˈkeɪnɪzəm/
n.
可卡因瘾；可卡因中毒
The habitual use of cocaine, or the resulting condition.
He suffered from cocainism for many years. 他多年来饱受可卡因瘾的折磨。
Synonyms: cocaine addiction, cocaine dependence
Antonyms: sobriety, abstinence
