contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/| v. |违反，违背；抵触|to go against a law, rule, or agreement|The company's actions contravene international law. 该公司的行为违反了国际法。|Synonyms: violate, breach, infringe, disobey|Antonyms: comply with, obey, uphold, observe|verb

contravene:/ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/| v. |违反，违背；抵触|to go against a law, rule, or agreement|Building this structure here would contravene local planning regulations. 在这里建造这个建筑物会违反当地的规划条例。|Synonyms: violate, breach, infringe, disobey|Antonyms: comply with, obey, uphold, observe|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contravenīre, from Latin contrā (“against”) + venīre (“to come”).
