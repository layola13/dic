antichurch:/ˌæntiˈtʃɜːrtʃ/
adjective
反教会的；反对教会的
Opposed to or hostile towards the church or organized religion.
The antichurch sentiment grew during the revolution. 革命期间反教会情绪高涨。
近义词: irreligious, secular, anticlerical
反义词: religious, devout, pro-church
词性: adjective
