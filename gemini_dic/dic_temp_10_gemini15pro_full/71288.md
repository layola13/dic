fixednesses:/ˈfɪksɪdnəsɪz/
noun
固定性；僵化；固执
The state of being fixed or unchanging.  A tendency to be inflexible or rigid in thought or action.
His fixednesses on the issue prevented any compromise. 他对这个问题的固执阻碍了任何妥协。
Near Synonyms: rigidity, inflexibility, stubbornness, obstinacy
Antonyms: flexibility, adaptability, open-mindedness
