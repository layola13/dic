interparliamentary:/ˌɪntərˌpɑːrləˈmentəri/ | adj. |议会间的，各国议会间的|Existing or carried on between parliaments of different countries. |The Inter-Parliamentary Union promotes dialogue between nations. 各国议会联盟促进各国间的对话。|The conference focused on interparliamentary cooperation.  这次会议的重点是议会间的合作。|近义词: international, multinational |反义词: domestic, national |词性:形容词
Etymology: inter- (between) + parliamentary (pertaining to a parliament)
