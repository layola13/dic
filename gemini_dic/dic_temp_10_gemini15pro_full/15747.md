balderdash:/ˈbɔːldərdæʃ/| n.|胡说八道；废话|nonsense; senseless talk|He dismissed the report as utter balderdash. 他认为这份报告纯属胡说八道。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, gibberish, twaddle|反义词：sense, truth|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, perhaps related to Middle Dutch  *balderen "to roar, bellow"  and  dash  "to strike, smash."  First recorded in the mid 16th century.
