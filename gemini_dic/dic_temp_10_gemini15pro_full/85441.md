hash-out:/hæʃ ˈaʊt/| phrasal verb |  彻底讨论；详细讨论；商定；敲定 | To discuss something thoroughly, especially a disagreement, in order to reach an agreement or solution. | Example 1: We need to hash out the details of the contract before we sign it. 我们需要在签署合同之前敲定合同的细节。

Example 2: They spent hours hashing out their differences. 他们花了几个小时来讨论他们的分歧。 | 近义词: discuss, debate, thrash out, iron out, hammer out | 反义词: avoid, ignore, gloss over | 词性: 动词短语


hash-out:/hæʃ ˈaʊt/| n. |  彻底的讨论 | A thorough discussion, especially of a disagreement, aimed at reaching an agreement or solution. | Example 1: After a long hash-out, they finally reached a compromise.  经过长时间的彻底讨论，他们终于达成了妥协。

Example 2: The hash-out over the budget lasted several days. 关于预算的彻底讨论持续了几天。 | 近义词: discussion, debate, negotiation | 反义词: avoidance, silence | 词性: 名词 (较少使用)
