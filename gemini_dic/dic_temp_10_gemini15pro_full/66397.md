executer's:/ˈɛksɪˌkjutərz/
| n.|遗嘱执行人的|belonging to or associated with an executor.  An executor is someone named in a will to carry out the deceased person's wishes and manage their estate. This word typically refers to possessions or responsibilities of that person.|The executor's duties included distributing the assets of the estate. 遗嘱执行人的职责包括分配遗产的资产。|

|反义词:heir, beneficiary|
