dehumanized:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪzd/
adjective
非人化的，失去人性的
Deprived of human qualities, personality, or spirit; made to feel less than human.
The refugees were dehumanized by being treated like numbers instead of people. 难民们被当作数字而不是人来对待，这让他们失去了人性。
The prisoners were dehumanized by the harsh conditions and brutal treatment. 囚犯们在恶劣的环境和残酷的待遇下变得麻木不仁。

近义词:degraded, brutalized, objectified
反义词:humanized, personalized, individualized
词性:adjective
