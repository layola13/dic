bindingly:/ˈbaɪndɪŋli/
adv.
有约束力地；强制性地
in a way that legally or morally obligates someone to do something
The agreement is bindingly upon both parties.  该协议对双方都具有约束力。
近义词: obligatorily, compulsorily, mandatory
反义词: optionally, non-bindingly
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From bind (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Bind" comes from Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten."  The "-ing" suffix creates a present participle or gerund, and the "-ly" suffix transforms it into an adverb.
