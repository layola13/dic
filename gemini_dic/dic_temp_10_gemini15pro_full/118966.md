meterless:/ˈmiːtərləs/
adjective
无韵律的，没有格律的
Lacking metrical structure; not conforming to a regular rhythm or meter.
Her meterless poems were a fresh departure from traditional forms. 她无韵律的诗歌是对传统形式的一种全新突破。
The free verse poem was notably meterless and flowed organically. 这首自由诗明显没有格律，读起来很自然。
synonym: arrhythmic, nonmetrical, free verse
antonym: metrical, rhythmic, measured
词性: 形容词
