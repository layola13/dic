dahlia: /ˈdeɪljə/ (US) /ˈdæljə/ (UK)| n. |大丽花；大丽菊|A brightly colored flower with layered petals, originally from Mexico.|例句：The dahlias in her garden were a riot of color. 她花园里的大丽花五彩缤纷。

例句：He gave her a bouquet of dahlias for her birthday. 他送给她一束大丽花作为生日礼物。
