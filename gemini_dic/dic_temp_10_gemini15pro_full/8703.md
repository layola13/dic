anticreativenesses:/ˌæntiˌkriːeɪˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
反创造性，扼杀创造力的行为（复数）
The state or quality of being anticreative; actions or attitudes that stifle creativity.
His anticreativenesses stifled the team's innovative spirit. 他的反创造行为扼杀了团队的创新精神。
The company's rigid rules and procedures led to many anticreativenesses. 公司僵化的规章制度导致了许多扼杀创造力的行为。
Synonyms: uncreativeness, unimaginativeness, sterility
Antonyms: creativity, inventiveness, originality
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + creativeness (the quality of being creative).  Creativeness ultimately derives from the Latin creare "to create."
