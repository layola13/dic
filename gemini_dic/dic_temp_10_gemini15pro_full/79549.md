glome:/ɡloʊm/
noun
球，小球，肾小球
A rounded mass; specifically, a tuft or cluster, as of a flower. In anatomy, applied especially to a coil of blood vessels projecting into the capsule of a Malpighian body of the kidney.
The glome is a crucial part of the kidney's filtering system. 肾小球是肾脏过滤系统的重要组成部分。
synonyms:  tuft, cluster, ball, coil
antonyms:  line, strand, dispersion
