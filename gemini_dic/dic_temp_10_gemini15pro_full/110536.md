limehound:/ˈlaɪmˌhaʊnd/ | n. |猎犬（尤指追踪猎物用的嗅觉灵敏的狗）|A dog of a breed originally used for hunting by scent, especially a bloodhound. |例句：The limehound followed the deer's scent through the forest. 猎犬循着鹿的气味穿过森林。

例句：The experienced hunter used a limehound to track the wounded boar. 经验丰富的猎人用猎犬追踪受伤的野猪。

近义词：bloodhound, scent hound, tracker dog |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lymer*, *lymhour*, from Old French *liemier*, from *lier* "to leash" + *mier* "hound". The "lime" part is unrelated to the citrus fruit.
