covetiveness:/ˈkʌvɪtɪsnəs/
n.
贪婪，贪欲
The intense desire to possess something, typically something belonging to another.
His covetiveness led him to steal from his own family. 他的贪婪导致他偷窃自己家人的东西。
greed, avarice, rapacity, acquisitiveness
generosity, contentment, satisfaction
noun
