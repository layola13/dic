parallelistic: /ˌpærəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
平行的，并列的
Characterized by parallelism, especially in structure or style.  Relating to or characterized by the simultaneous performance of multiple operations.
The poem is full of parallelistic phrasing, creating a sense of rhythm and emphasis. 这首诗充满了并列的短语，创造了一种节奏感和强调感。
Her speech used parallelistic structure to drive home her main points. 她的演讲使用了并行结构来强调她的主要观点。
synonyms: parallel, analogous, similar, corresponding
antonyms: divergent, dissimilar, contrasting
