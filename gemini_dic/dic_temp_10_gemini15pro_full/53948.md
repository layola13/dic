discovering:/dɪˈskʌvərɪŋ/
词性:v.
单词属性:动名词/现在分词
中文翻译:发现，发觉
英文解析:Present participle of "discover".  The act of finding something unexpectedly or during a search.
例句：Discovering new music is one of my favorite hobbies.  发现新音乐是我最喜欢的爱好之一。
例句：He made a fortune by discovering gold. 他因发现黄金而发财。
近义词:finding, unearthing, exploring, detecting
反义词:hiding, concealing, ignoring, overlooking


词源:From Old French *descovrir, from des- "un-, dis-" + covrir "to cover."  Originally meant "to uncover."
