arts:/ɑːrts/
n.
艺术;文科;人文学科
The skills that are considered to involve refined taste and skill, such as painting, music, literature, and dance.  Also refers to humanities subjects as a field of study.
She has a degree in arts. 她拥有文科的学位。
The museum has a fine collection of decorative arts. 这家博物馆收藏了精美的装饰艺术品。
synonyms: humanities, fine arts, liberal arts, creative arts
antonyms: sciences, trades


arts:/ɑːrts/
n.
(art的复数)
技艺;技巧;技术
A skill at doing something, often one that takes practice.
The martial arts require discipline and dedication. 武术需要纪律和奉献精神。
He's learning the arts of negotiation and compromise. 他正在学习谈判和妥协的技巧。
synonyms: skills, crafts, techniques, abilities
antonyms: ineptitude, incompetence
