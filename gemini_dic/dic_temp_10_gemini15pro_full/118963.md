metered:/ˈmiːtərd/ | adj. | 按计量的，用计量器的 | Measured or controlled by a meter.  |  例句：The taxi fare was metered. 出租车费是用计价器计量的。 | 近义词：measured, gauged, calculated | 反义词: unmetered, unlimited | 词性: 形容词

metered:/ˈmiːtərd/ | v. | 用仪表测量 | To measure something with a meter. | 例句：The electricity company metered our usage. 电力公司用电表测量了我们的用电量。| 近义词：measure, gauge, calculate | 反义词: estimate, guess | 词性: 动词
