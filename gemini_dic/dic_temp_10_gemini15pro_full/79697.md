glowworm:/ˈɡloʊwɜːrm/ (US), /ˈɡləʊwɜːm/ (UK)| n. |萤火虫|A soft-bodied beetle whose larva and wingless adult female emit light.|例句：We saw glowworms twinkling in the forest at night. 我们晚上在森林里看到萤火虫闪烁。|例句：The children were fascinated by the glowworm's light. 孩子们被萤火虫的光芒迷住了。|近义词：firefly, lightning bug|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *glowworm, equivalent to glow + worm.
