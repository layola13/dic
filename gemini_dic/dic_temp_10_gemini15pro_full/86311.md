hebraician:/ˌhiːˈbreɪʃən/ | n. adj. |希伯来人,希伯来语的|A person who is a Hebrew, or relating to the Hebrew language or culture.|例句：He was a renowned Hebraician, fluent in ancient texts. 他是一位著名的希伯来语学者，精通古代文献。|例句：The Hebraician influence on Western culture is profound. 希伯来文化对西方文化的影响是深远的。|近义词：Hebrew, Israelite|反义词: Gentile|词性:名词,形容词

hebraist:/ˈhiːbreɪɪst/ | n. |希伯来语学者|A scholar of the Hebrew language or literature.|例句：She is a distinguished Hebraist, known for her translations of biblical texts. 她是一位杰出的希伯来语学者，以翻译圣经文本而闻名。|例句：The Hebraist offered a new interpretation of the ancient inscription.  这位希伯来语学者对古代铭文提出了新的解释。|近义词：Hebraician, Hebrew scholar|反义词: None|词性:名词


词源: From Late Latin Hebraicus, from Greek Hebraïkos, from Hebrew ʿivri.
