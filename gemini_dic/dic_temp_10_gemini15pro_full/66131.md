excepting:/ɪkˈsɛptɪŋ/

PHRASE->
excepting for = 除了
excepting that = 除了…之外
not excepting = 包括
always excepting = 总是除外
without excepting = 无一例外


词源:
From Middle English *exceptinge, present participle of excepten, from Old French excepter, from Latin exceptāre, present active infinitive of excipiō (“I take out, except, exclude”), from ex (“out of, from”) + capiō (“I take, seize”).


注意事项:  excepting 作为连词的用法不如作为介词常见，现在更多地用 except 或 unless 代替。
