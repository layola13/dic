cordovan:/ˈkɔːrdəvən/ /ˈkɔːrdəˌvæn/| n. adj.|科尔多瓦皮革；科尔多瓦色的|A type of smooth, glossy leather originally made in Cordoba, Spain, from goatskin tanned with alum and oils.|He wore cordovan shoes. 他穿了科尔多瓦皮革的鞋子。|Synonyms: leather, goatskin|Antonyms: synthetic leather|noun
cordovan:/ˈkɔːrdəvən/ /ˈkɔːrdəˌvæn/| adj.|科尔多瓦色的；深红色的|Having a rich, dark reddish-brown color.|The car had a cordovan leather interior. 这辆车的内饰是科尔多瓦色的皮革。|Synonyms: burgundy, maroon, oxblood|Antonyms: light, pale|adjective
