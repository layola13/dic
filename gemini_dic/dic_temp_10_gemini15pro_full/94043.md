impartial:/ɪmˈpɑːrʃəl/ | adj. | 公正的，无偏见的 | Not favoring one side or another; unbiased; neutral. | The judge must remain impartial throughout the trial. 法官在整个审判过程中必须保持公正。|  The newspaper gave an impartial report of the election. 这家报纸对选举进行了公正的报道。 | 近义词: unbiased, neutral, objective, fair, just, equitable | 反义词: biased, prejudiced, partial, unfair, unjust | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *imparcial*, from Anglo-Norman *imparcial*, from Old French *imparcial*, from Medieval Latin *impartialis*, from *in-* (not) + *partialis* (partial).
