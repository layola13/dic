exoccipital:/ˌɛksɒkˈsɪpɪtəl/ |adj. n.|外枕骨的；外枕骨|Relating to or denoting a bone forming the outer back part of the skull, just below the foramen magnum (the hole through which the spinal cord passes). In many vertebrates, it is a separate bone, but in mammals it fuses with the other occipital bones to form a single occipital bone.|The exoccipital bone forms part of the base of the skull. 外枕骨形成颅骨底部的一部分。|The exoccipital region of the skull is well preserved in the fossil.  该化石的外枕骨区域保存完好。|Synonym:occipital|Antonym:None|Noun, Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From exo- (outside, external) + occipital (relating to the back of the head).  The term "occipital" itself comes from the Latin "occiput," meaning "back of the head."
