installer:/ɪnˈstɔːlər/ (US) /ɪnˈstɔːlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|安装程序；安装工|A person or program that installs something, especially software.|The installer automatically configures the software. 安装程序会自动配置软件。|Example:  The installer made it easy to set up the new operating system. 安装程序使安装新的操作系统变得很容易。

Example:  I hired an installer to put in my new air conditioning unit. 我雇了一个安装工来安装我的新空调。|Synonym: setup program, fitter, technician|Antonym: uninstaller, remover|Noun
