gradeless:/ˈɡreɪdlɪs/| adj.|无等级的，不分等级的|Without grades or levels; not graded.  It refers to a system, especially in education, where performance is not assessed using traditional letter or number grades.|The school implemented a gradeless learning system focused on individual progress. 这所学校实施了一种不分等级的学习制度，注重个人进步。|Synonyms: ungraded, unmarked, non-graded|Antonyms: graded, ranked, assessed|Word Type: Adjective
