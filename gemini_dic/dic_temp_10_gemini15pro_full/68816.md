fasciitis:/ˌfæʃiˈaɪtɪs/| n.|筋膜炎|Inflammation of the fascia, a band of fibrous tissue that surrounds muscles, blood vessels, and nerves.|Plantar fasciitis is a common cause of heel pain. 足底筋膜炎是导致足跟疼痛的常见原因。|Near synonyms: myositis, tendinitis|Antonyms: None (inflammation doesn't have a direct opposite)|noun
