fuscous:/ˈfʌskəs/| adj.|暗褐色的；深色的；昏暗的|Of a dark brown or dusky colour, often tinged with grey or green.|The fuscous clouds hinted at an impending storm. 暗褐色的云暗示着即将到来的暴风雨。|Synonyms: dusky, murky, somber, dark, dull|Antonyms: bright, vibrant, light, luminous|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fuscus* "dark, brown, swarthy".
