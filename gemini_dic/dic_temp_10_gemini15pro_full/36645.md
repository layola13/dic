|Synonym: curatorships, positions, appointments|
|Antonym: disposals|
|Noun|


collectorship:/kəˈlektərʃɪp/
|n.|收藏；收藏品；收藏行为|His collectorship of antique clocks is well-known. 他对古董钟的收藏很有名。|
|She began her collectorship of stamps as a child. 她从小就开始集邮。|
|Synonym: collection, accumulation, hobby|
|Antonym: dispersal, distribution|
|Noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From collector + -ship.  "Collector" comes from the Latin "colligere" (to gather, collect), and "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
