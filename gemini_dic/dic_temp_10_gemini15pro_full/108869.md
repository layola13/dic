lecithin: /ˈlesɪθɪn/ (US), /ˈlesɪθɪn/ (UK) | n. | 卵磷脂 | A fat-like substance found in many plants and animals, especially in egg yolks and soybeans. It is important for the structure of cell membranes and is used in food processing and as a dietary supplement. |  Soybeans are a good source of lecithin. 大豆是卵磷脂的良好来源。

Lecithin is often used as an emulsifier in food products. 卵磷脂常在食品中用作乳化剂。 | synonym: phosphatidylcholine | antonym: None | noun
