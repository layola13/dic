cnidocil:/ˈnaɪdəʊsɪl/ /ˈnɪdəˌsɪl/ | n. | 刺细胞 | A modified cilium on a cnidocyte (stinging cell) of a cnidarian that acts as a trigger for the discharge of the nematocyst. |  Cnidocils are sensitive to touch and chemical stimuli. 刺细胞对触觉和化学刺激很敏感。

Cnidocils are essential for capturing prey and defending against predators. 刺细胞对于捕捉猎物和抵御捕食者至关重要。| 近义词: trigger hair | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly related concepts like "receptor" or "sensor" could be considered in a broader context.) | 词性: noun
