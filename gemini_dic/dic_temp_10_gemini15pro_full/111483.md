loads:/ləʊdz/| n.|大量，许多|A large number or amount of something.|例句：I've got loads of work to do. 我有很多工作要做。|例句：She has loads of friends. 她有很多朋友。|近义词：lots, plenty, tons, a lot, heaps|反义词:little, few|noun

loads:/ləʊdz/| v.|装载|To put a large quantity of something into or onto something else.|例句：They loaded the truck with boxes. 他们把箱子装上卡车。|例句：The ship was loaded with cargo. 这艘船装满了货物。|近义词：fill, pack, stack|反义词:unload, empty|verb
