incite:/ɪnˈsaɪt/| vt.|煽动，鼓动|To encourage or stir up (violent or unlawful behavior).|The politician's speech incited the crowd to riot.  这位政客的演讲煽动了人群暴动。|The news report incited fear and panic among the public. 这篇新闻报道在公众中引起了恐惧和恐慌。|Synonyms: provoke, instigate, agitate, spur, goad|Antonyms: soothe, calm, pacify, quell, suppress|verb
