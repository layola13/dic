alleges:/əˈlɛdʒɪz/ | v. |  断言，宣称，声称(尤指无证据) | to assert without proof | The newspaper alleges that the mayor accepted bribes.  该报声称市长收受贿赂。 | claims, asserts, contends | denies, refutes | verb

alleges:/əˈlɛdʒɪz/ | v. |  提出…作为理由 | to put forward as a reason or excuse | He alleged illness as the reason for his absence. 他声称自己生病了，以此作为缺席的理由。 | cites, adduces | denies, refutes | verb
