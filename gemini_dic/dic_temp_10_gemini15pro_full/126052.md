mustanged:/ˈmʌstæŋd/ | adj. | 野马似的；奔放的；狂野不羁的 | Resembling or characteristic of a mustang; wild and untamed. |  The mustanged spirit of the cowboy captivated her.  牛仔那野马般奔放的精神深深吸引了她。 | 近义词: wild, untamed, unruly, free-spirited | 反义词: tame, domesticated, subdued, restrained | 词性: 形容词

mustanged:/ˈmʌstæŋd/ | v. (past tense and past participle of mustang) |  (使)像野马一样；(使)变得狂野 | To make or become like a mustang; to make or become wild and untamed. | The harsh desert climate mustanged the horses. 恶劣的沙漠气候使这些马变得像野马一样狂野。| 近义词:  wild,  run wild | 反义词: tame, domesticate | 词性: 动词


词源:  Derived from "mustang" + "-ed". "Mustang" comes from the Spanish word "mestengo," meaning "stray animal," ultimately from "mixto," meaning "mixed."
