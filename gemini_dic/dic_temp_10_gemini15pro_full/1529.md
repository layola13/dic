ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Achāius*, from Ancient Greek *Ἀχαιός (Akhaios)*. Ultimately from Proto-Hellenic *Akʰajos*.

USAGE->
作为形容词:

The Achaean civilization flourished during the Bronze Age. = 阿凯亚文明在青铜时代蓬勃发展。

Achaean pottery is known for its distinctive style. = 阿凯亚陶器以其独特的风格而闻名。


作为名词:

The Achaeans were skilled seafarers. = 阿凯亚人都是熟练的航海家。

Many Achaeans settled in the western Mediterranean. = 许多阿凯亚人定居在地中海西部。
