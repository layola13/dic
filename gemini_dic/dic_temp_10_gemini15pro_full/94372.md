implanters:/ɪmˈplɑːntərz/
noun|植入者，移植者，灌输者|People or devices that implant something.  This usually refers to medical or technological contexts.|The implanters carefully inserted the device. 植入者小心地插入了设备。|synonyms: inserters, inductors, injectors|antonyms: extractors, removers|n.

implanters:/ɪmˈplɑːntərz/
noun|植入机|A machine designed to implant something, often in a manufacturing process like ion implantation.|The implanters were crucial for doping the semiconductors.  这些植入机对于半导体的掺杂至关重要。|synonyms: insertion machines, implantation devices|antonyms: extraction machines, removal devices|n.
