discussing:/dɪˈskʌsɪŋ/
v.
讨论，谈论
Talking about something with someone or a group of people, often to exchange ideas or opinions or to reach a decision.
They were discussing the latest news. 他们正在讨论最新的新闻。
The committee is discussing the proposal. 委员会正在讨论这项提案。
considering, debating, talking over
arguing, disputing
verb
