mental's:/ˈmɛntlz/
|adj.|精神的；智力的|Relating to the mind.  This is the possessive form of "mental," implying ownership or association with something related to the mind.  It's not standard usage and is generally avoided.  "Mental processes," not "mental's processes."|Example:  (Incorrect usage) The mental's state of the patient was concerning.  (Correct usage) The patient's mental state was concerning.|近义词:psychological, cognitive|反义词:physical, bodily|词性:adjective (possessive, non-standard)
