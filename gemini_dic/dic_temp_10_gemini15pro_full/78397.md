get away with:/ɡɛt əˈweɪ wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 逍遥法外；逃脱惩罚 | to do something wrong or illegal without being punished or suffering any bad consequences | He cheated on the exam and got away with it. 他考试作弊却没有受到惩罚。| synonyms: escape punishment, evade consequences, get off scot-free | antonyms: be punished, face consequences, suffer repercussions | verb phrase

get away with:/ɡɛt əˈweɪ wɪð/ | phrasal verb |  (勉强)过得去；(勉强)被接受 | to manage to do something even though it is not very good |  I got away with wearing jeans to the restaurant. 我穿着牛仔裤去了那家餐厅，居然也没事。 | synonyms: get by, manage, scrape through | antonyms: fail, be unacceptable | verb phrase
