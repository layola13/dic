flows:/floʊz/ (US) /fləʊz/ (UK)| v. n.|流动; 流出;  流畅; 涌出; (河水等)流向|The river flows to the sea. 这条河流入大海。|近义词：streams, runs, glides,  proceeds, emanates |反义词:stagnates, halts, ceases|verb, noun

Etymology: From Middle English *flowen*, from Old English *flōwan* (“to flow”), from Proto-Germanic *flōaną* (“to flow”), from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to flow, swim, float”). Cognate with Scots *flow* (“to flow”), West Frisian *floeie* (“to flow”), Dutch *vloeien* (“to flow”), German *fließen* (“to flow”), Danish *flyde* (“to flow”), Swedish *flyta* (“to float”). Related also to float.
