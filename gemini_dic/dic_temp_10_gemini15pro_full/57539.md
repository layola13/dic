drainer:/ˈdreɪnər/| n.|排水器，滤器，沥水器|A device used to remove excess liquid from something. |The drainer in the sink was clogged with food scraps. 水槽里的滤水器被食物残渣堵塞了。|The mechanic used a drainer to remove the old oil from the car engine.  机修工用排水器清除汽车发动机里的旧机油。| synonyms: strainer, colander, filter, sieve | antonyms:  filler| noun
