baltis:/ˈbɔːltɪs/| n.|巴尔蒂咖喱|A type of curry served in the United Kingdom, thought to have originated in Birmingham, England. It is characterized by a fairly mild flavor and a relatively thick sauce.|We went out for a baltis last night. 昨晚我们出去吃巴尔蒂咖喱了。|Synonyms: curry, Indian food|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps bland food)|noun
