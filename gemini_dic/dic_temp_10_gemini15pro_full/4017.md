ahkal: /ˈɑːkɑːl/ | n. | 永恒，不朽 |  A metaphysical concept referring to the timeless, immortal reality. It often appears in Sikhism, referring to the eternal, unchanging nature of God. |  The concept of ahkal is central to Sikh philosophy.  永恒的概念是锡克教哲学的核心。| Synonyms: eternity, immortality, timelessness | Antonyms: mortality, temporality | noun
