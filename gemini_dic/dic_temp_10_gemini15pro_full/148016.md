overripeness:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpnəs/| n. |过度成熟|The state of being excessively ripe, often past the point of ideal flavor or texture.  A condition of being too ripe.|The overripeness of the bananas made them mushy. 香蕉过度成熟，变得很软烂。|Synonyms: maturity, mellowness, ripeness (in early stages)|Antonyms: unripeness, immaturity, greenness|noun

overripe:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪp/| adj. |过度成熟的|Too ripe; past its prime.  Excessively ripe, often to the point of spoilage.|The overripe fruit attracted flies.  过度成熟的水果招来了苍蝇。|Synonyms:  rotten, decayed, putrid (in later stages), soft|Antonyms: unripe, underripe, firm, green|adjective
