bearers:/ˈbeərz/| n.|持有人，载体，承载者|People or things that carry or hold something else. |The pallbearers carried the coffin slowly into the church. 扶柩者缓缓地将棺材抬进教堂。|Synonyms: carriers, holders, supporters|Antonyms: receivers, takers|noun

bearers:/ˈbeərz/| n.|生育者|A woman who gives birth to a child. |She is the bearer of five children. 她生育了五个孩子。|Synonyms: mother, parent|Antonyms: child, offspring|noun
