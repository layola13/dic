buffering:/ˈbʌfərɪŋ/ /ˈbʌfəɹɪŋ/| n.|缓冲，缓存|The act of temporarily storing data in a buffer, typically to compensate for differences in the rates of data flow or to avoid interruptions during processing. |The video is buffering.  视频正在缓冲。|Synonyms: caching, loading, preloading|Antonyms: streaming, direct playback|noun

buffering:/ˈbʌfərɪŋ/ /ˈbʌfəɹɪŋ/| adj.|缓冲的，缓存的|Relating to or characterized by buffering. |The buffering process is taking longer than usual. 缓冲过程比平时要长。|Synonyms: caching, loading, preloading|Antonyms: streaming, direct playback|adjective
