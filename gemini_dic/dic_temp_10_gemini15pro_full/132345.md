nondegradable:/ˌnɒndɪˈɡreɪdəbəl/ | adj. | 不可降解的 | Not able to be broken down by natural processes, especially by the action of bacteria. |  Plastic bags are nondegradable and pollute the environment.  塑料袋是不可降解的，会污染环境。 | Example: Many plastics are nondegradable, posing a serious threat to the environment. 许多塑料是不可降解的，对环境构成严重威胁。| Synonyms: non-biodegradable, persistent, indestructible | Antonyms: biodegradable, decomposable, recyclable | Word Type: Adjective
