hypermanics:/ˌhaɪpərˈmænɪks/ | n. | 狂躁症患者；过度兴奋的人 | People experiencing a hypermanic episode may exhibit extreme energy, inflated self-esteem, and racing thoughts. |  During his hypermanic phase, he barely slept and made impulsive decisions. 他在狂躁期几乎不睡觉，而且会做出冲动的决定。 | 近义词: manics, overexcited individuals | 反义词: depressives, lethargic individuals | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From "hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér, "over, beyond") + "mania" (Greek: μανία, manía, "madness, frenzy").
