gastromancy: /ˈɡæstrəˌmænsi/ | n. | 胃卜 | Divination by means of sounds from the stomach, or by interpreting the motions of the stomach.  It's an ancient form of divination. |  Ancient cultures practiced gastromancy to predict the future. 古代文化中人们通过胃卜来预测未来。  Gastromancy, like other forms of divination, is not based on scientific evidence.  像其他占卜形式一样，胃卜并非基于科学证据。 |  Synonyms: divination, fortune-telling, soothsaying | Antonyms: science, logic | noun
