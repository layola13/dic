fugacious:/fjuːˈɡeɪʃəs/
adjective
短暂的，易逝的
lasting for a short time; fleeting.
例句：The fugacious nature of fame makes it a difficult goal to pursue.  名望的短暂性使它成为一个难以追求的目标。

例句：Summer romance is often fugacious, ending with the change of seasons.  夏日恋情通常很短暂，随着季节的更替而结束。

近义词：fleeting, ephemeral, transient, transitory, evanescent
反义词：permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual
词性: adjective
