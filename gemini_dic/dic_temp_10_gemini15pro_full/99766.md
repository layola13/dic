intermediated:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieɪtɪd/
v.|斡旋的，调解的，媒介的|acted as a link between people or things|The bank intermediated the loan between the borrower and the lender. 银行在借款人和贷款人之间进行了贷款中介。|mediated, negotiated, brokered|direct|Verb
Etymology: From "intermediate" + "-ed."  "Intermediate" comes from Late Latin intermediatus, past participle of intermediare "to be in the middle," from Latin inter "between" + mediare "to be in the middle."
