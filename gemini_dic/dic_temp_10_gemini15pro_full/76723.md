gamboge:/ˈɡæmbuːʒ/ | n. adj.| 藤黄，深黄色 | A gum resin obtained from various southeast Asian trees, used as a yellow pigment and, less commonly, as a purgative.  Also, a strong yellow color. |

例句：
The artist used gamboge to create a vibrant sunset in his painting. 艺术家使用藤黄在他的画中创造了一个充满活力的日落。

The walls of the room were painted a bright gamboge. 房间的墙壁被漆成了鲜艳的藤黄色。

近义词：
yellow, gold, ochre, saffron

反义词：
(As a color) blue, violet


词性:
Noun, Adjective
