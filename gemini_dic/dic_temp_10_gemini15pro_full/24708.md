brontosaur:/ˈbrɒntəsɔːr/ (UK), /ˈbrɑːntəsɔːr/ (US)| n. |雷龙|A large quadrupedal herbivorous dinosaur with a long neck and tail, now usually classified as Apatosaurus.  It was thought to be a distinct genus, but is now considered a synonym of Apatosaurus.|例句：The brontosaur lumbered across the prehistoric landscape.  雷龙缓缓地走过史前的景色。|例句：Many people still use the term brontosaur, even though it's scientifically outdated. 许多人仍然使用雷龙这个词，即使它在科学上已经过时了。|近义词：apatosaurus|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
