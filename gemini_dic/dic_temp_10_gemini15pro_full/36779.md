colms:/kɒmz/ | n. |煤尘, 煤末; (尤指矿井巷道中的)煤柱, 矿柱|Fine particles of coal or coal dust, especially those accumulating in mine passages, or pillars of coal left to support the roof of a mine. |The colms in the mine shaft posed a significant fire hazard. 矿井巷道中的煤尘构成了严重的火灾隐患。| The miners had to clear the colms regularly to maintain safe passage. 矿工们必须定期清理煤末以保持安全通道。| synonyms: coal dust, slack, duff, pillars | antonyms: (For pillars) void, cavity | noun
