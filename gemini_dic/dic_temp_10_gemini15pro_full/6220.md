PHRASE->
(Due to its informal and dialectal nature, "amn't" doesn't have many set phrases.  The examples below are contrived to illustrate possible usage, but they are not common phrases.)
I amn't sure what's up. 我不确定怎么了。
I amn't going, so what? 我不去，那又怎样？
I amn't doing it, no matter what. 无论如何，我都不做。
I amn't happy about it, what's more, I'm angry. 我对此不满意，而且，我很生气。
I amn't going, what about you? 我不去，你呢？



注意事项:

"amn't" is very informal and largely dialectal.  It is rarely used in formal writing or standard spoken English. While grammatically correct in certain dialects, it might sound unusual or incorrect to speakers of other dialects.  It is much more common to use "I'm not" or "I am not."
