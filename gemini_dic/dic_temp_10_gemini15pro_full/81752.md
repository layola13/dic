greeting:/ˈɡriːtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡriːtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|问候，致敬，祝贺|An act of communication in which one indicates recognition of another's arrival or presence.|Example: He gave a friendly greeting to his neighbor. 他对邻居友好的问候。|Synonyms: salutation, welcome, hello|Antonyms: farewell, goodbye|noun

greeting:/ˈɡriːtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡriːtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|问候的|Relating to or used as a greeting.|Example: a greeting card  一张贺卡|Synonyms: welcoming, salutatory|Antonyms: farewell, parting|adjective
