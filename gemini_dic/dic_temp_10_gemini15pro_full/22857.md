boswellian: /bɒzˈwɛliən/ | adj. n. | 博斯韦尔的，像博斯韦尔的 |  Relating to or characteristic of James Boswell, especially his biographical writing, marked by detailed and lively anecdotal accounts.  Can also refer to a person who resembles Boswell in these qualities. | His writing style is distinctly Boswellian, full of witty anecdotes and sharp observations. 他的写作风格明显是博斯韦尔式的，充满了诙谐的轶事和敏锐的观察。 |  Near Synonyms: anecdotal, biographical, descriptive, observant, witty | Antonyms:  incisive, terse, succinct | adjective

boswellian: /bɒzˈwɛliən/ | n. | 博斯韦尔式的作家或作品 | A writer or a piece of writing resembling that of James Boswell. |  He's a true Boswellian, always capturing the details of conversations and encounters. 他是一个真正的博斯韦尔式的人物，总是捕捉对话和相遇的细节。 | Near Synonyms: biographer, chronicler, diarist | Antonyms:  fiction writer, novelist | noun
