nonaccedence:/ˌnɒnəkˈsiːdəns/
n.
不接受；不赞同；拒绝
lack of acceptance or agreement; refusal.
His nonaccedence to the terms of the agreement caused the deal to fall apart. 他不接受协议条款导致交易破裂。

近义词: refusal, rejection, declination, dissent
反义词: acceptance, agreement, consent, approval
词性: 名词
