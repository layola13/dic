ignoramus:/ˌɪɡnəˈreɪməs/| n. |无知的人；笨蛋|A person who is ignorant or uninformed; a stupid person.|例句：He's such an ignoramus when it comes to art. 他对艺术一窍不通。

例句：Don't be an ignoramus; learn something new every day. 不要做一个无知的人，每天都要学习新东西。|近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, simpleton|反义词：expert, scholar, genius|词性: 名词
