draftiness:/ˈdræftinəs/
noun
通风；透风；有穿堂风
The state or quality of being drafty; characterized by currents of air.
The draftiness of the old house made it difficult to heat.  这栋老房子的通风使它很难被加热。
airiness, chilliness, ventilation
closeness, stuffiness, airtightness
