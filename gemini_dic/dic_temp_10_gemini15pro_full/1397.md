accuse:/əˈkjuːz/ | vt.|指责，控告|to say that someone has done something wrong or illegal|He accused her of stealing the money. = 他指控她偷了钱。

They accused him of murder. = 他们指控他谋杀。|近义词：blame, charge, indict|反义词：defend, excuse, pardon|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acuser*, from Latin *accusare* "to call to account," from *ad* "to" + *causa* "cause, reason."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police often accuse innocent people. = 警察经常指控无辜的人。
They accuse him of being lazy. = 他们指责他懒惰。

一般过去时:
She accused him of lying. = 她指责他说谎。
The prosecutor accused the defendant of theft. = 检察官指控被告盗窃。

现在完成时:
He has been accused of fraud. = 他已被指控犯有欺诈罪。
They have accused her of cheating on the exam. = 他们指控她在考试中作弊。

过去完成时:
He had been accused of the crime before the real culprit was found. = 在找到真正的罪犯之前，他一直被指控犯有这起罪行。
She had accused him of neglecting his duties before he resigned. = 在他辞职之前，她曾指责他玩忽职守。

将来时:
If you don't return the book, they will accuse you of stealing. = 如果你不归还这本书，他们会指控你偷窃。
The witness will accuse the suspect of committing the robbery. = 目击者将指控嫌疑人犯有抢劫罪。

被动语态:
He was accused of treason. = 他被指控犯有叛国罪。
The company is being accused of polluting the river. = 这家公司被指控污染了河流。

疑问句:
Who did they accuse of starting the fire? = 他们指控谁放火？
Why are you accusing me of this? = 你为什么指控我这个？

否定句:
I didn't accuse him of anything. = 我没有指控他任何事情。
She doesn't accuse anyone lightly. = 她不会轻易指责任何人。


PHRASE->
accuse someone of something = 指控某人某事
be accused of something = 被指控某事
wrongly accuse = 错误地指控
falsely accuse = 诬告
stand accused of = 被指控
