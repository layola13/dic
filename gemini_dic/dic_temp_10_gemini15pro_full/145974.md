overdiversities:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪtiz/ | n. | 过度多样化；过度多元化 |  The state of having too much diversity, often leading to fragmentation or inefficiency.  A somewhat controversial term, suggesting that excessive diversity can be detrimental. | The overdiversities of opinions within the committee stalled decision-making. 委员会内部意见过度多样化，导致决策停滞。|  The company struggled to manage the overdiversities of its product lines.  这家公司难以管理其产品线的过度多样化。|  excess diversity, overabundance of variety | lack of diversity, homogeneity | noun
