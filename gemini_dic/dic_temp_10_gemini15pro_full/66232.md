excito:/ɪkˈsaɪtoʊ/| adj. |兴奋性的；刺激性的 |  Relating to or denoting a neurotransmitter that excites or stimulates a nerve cell. | Excitotoxicity is a pathological process by which nerve cells are damaged and killed by the overactivation of receptors for the excitatory neurotransmitter glutamate. 兴奋性毒性是一种病理过程，神经细胞因兴奋性神经递质谷氨酸受体的过度激活而受损和死亡。| stimulating, exciting | inhibitory, suppressing | adjective
