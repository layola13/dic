nonplurality:/ˌnɑːnplʊˈrælɪti/
n.
非多数；非多数票
the fact or state of not constituting a plurality
In the election, the candidate won by a nonplurality, meaning they received the most votes but not a majority. 在选举中，候选人以非多数票获胜，这意味着他们获得了最多的选票，但没有超过半数。
lack of majority, minority
majority, plurality
noun
