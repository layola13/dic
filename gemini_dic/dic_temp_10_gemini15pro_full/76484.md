galactopoietic:/ɡəˌlæktəpɔɪˈɛtɪk/
adjective
催乳的，促进乳汁分泌的
Stimulating or increasing the flow of milk.
The herb fenugreek is known for its galactopoietic properties. 胡芦巴草因其催乳特性而闻名。
Mothers are often given galactopoietic medication after giving birth to help with breastfeeding.  产妇通常在分娩后服用催乳药物以帮助母乳喂养。
synonyms:lactogenic, milk-producing
antonyms:anti-galactopoietic, milk-inhibiting
词性: adjective
