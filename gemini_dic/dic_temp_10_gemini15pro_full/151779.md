pass away:/ˌpæs əˈweɪ/ | phrasal verb | 去世，亡故 | To die. It is a more polite or gentle way of saying that someone has died. | My grandfather passed away peacefully in his sleep last night. 昨晚我的祖父在睡梦中安详地去世了。|  His grandmother passed away last year after a long illness.  他祖母去年久病后去世了。 | Synonyms: die, expire, decease, perish | Antonyms: be born, live, survive | verb phrase
