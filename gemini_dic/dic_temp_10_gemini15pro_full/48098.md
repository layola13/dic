declinable:/dɪˈklaɪnəbl/| adj.|可变格的，可变化的|Able to be declined (grammatically).  Refers to words that can change their form to show different grammatical functions, such as case, number, gender, tense, etc.|The Latin word "rosa" is declinable, changing its form to "rosae" in the genitive case.|近义词：inflectable, variable|反义词:indeclinable, invariable|词性:adjective
