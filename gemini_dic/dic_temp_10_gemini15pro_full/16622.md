baronetize: /ˈbærəntaɪz/ | vt. | 授予男爵爵位；使成为男爵 | To elevate someone to the rank of baron. | The king decided to baronetize his loyal advisor. 国王决定授予他忠诚的顾问男爵爵位。

He was baronetized for his services to the crown. 他因效忠王室而被封为男爵。 | 近义词: ennoble, elevate | 反义词: degrade, demote | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From baron + -ize.  "Baron" comes from Old French, ultimately from a Frankish word *baro meaning "free man," "warrior."  "-ize" is a suffix of Greek origin forming verbs meaning "to make or become," "to treat with," "to subject to the action of."
