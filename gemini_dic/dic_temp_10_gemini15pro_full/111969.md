loment:/ˈloʊmənt/ | n. | 节荚；分节的荚果 | A dry fruit, such as that of some legumes, constricted between the seeds and breaking transversely into one-seeded segments. | The loment of the tick trefoil readily breaks apart into individual segments.  山蚂蝗的节荚很容易分裂成单独的节段。| Example: The botanist studied the loment of the plant, noting the distinct segments.  植物学家研究了这种植物的节荚，注意到明显的节段。| Synonym: pod, legume | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a non-segmented fruit) | noun
