argumentatious: /ˌɑːrɡjʊˈmɛnteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 好争辩的，爱争论的 | Given to argument; disputatious. Characterized by excessive arguing.  | He's become quite argumentatious since joining the debate team.  他自从加入辩论队后变得很喜欢争论。|  She was known for her argumentatious nature, always ready for a heated discussion. 她以好辩的性格而闻名，随时准备进行激烈的讨论。 | synonyms: disputatious, contentious, quarrelsome, combative | antonyms: agreeable, amenable, conciliatory, peaceable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *argumentatiosus, from Latin argumentari (to argue) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of," "prone to").
