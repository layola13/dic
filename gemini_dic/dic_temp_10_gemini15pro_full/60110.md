effable:/ˈefəbl/
adjective
可表达的，能说出的
capable of being expressed in words.
The experience was so profound and moving that it was barely effable. 这段经历如此深刻感人，几乎无法用语言表达。

近义词:expressible, utterable, communicable
反义词:ineffable, inexpressible, unutterable
词性:adjective

词源:From Late Latin *effabilis, from Latin ex- + fari “to speak.”  Literally, "able to be spoken."
