bullocks:/ˈbʊləks/ | n. | 公牛; (表示恼怒或惊讶的感叹词)胡说;废话 |Castrated male bovine animals, typically used for draught work. Also used as an exclamation of annoyance or surprise.|  He used a team of bullocks to plough the field. 他用一群公牛犁地。 | synonyms: oxen, steers, bull, rubbish, nonsense | antonyms: sense, truth | noun

bullocks:/ˈbʊləks/ | int. | 胡说八道; 废话; 鬼扯 (英国俚语，表示不相信、恼怒或轻蔑) | A mild oath or exclamation expressing disbelief, annoyance, or contempt. Primarily used in British English.| Bullocks! I don't believe a word of it.  胡说！我一个字都不信。 | synonyms: rubbish, nonsense, balderdash, fiddlesticks | antonyms: truth, fact | interjection
