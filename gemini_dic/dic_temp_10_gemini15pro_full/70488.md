filament:/ˈfɪləmənt/| n. |细丝，灯丝|A slender threadlike object or fiber, especially one found in animal or plant structures or used in some technical devices.|The spider spun a silken filament.  蜘蛛吐出一根丝线。|The light bulb's filament broke. 灯泡的灯丝断了。|近义词：fiber, thread, strand|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin *filamentum*, from *filum* "thread".
