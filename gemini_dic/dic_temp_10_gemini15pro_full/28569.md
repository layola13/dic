careen:/kəˈriːn/ | v. | 倾斜，侧倾；狂奔，疾驰 | To lean, sway, or tip to one side while in motion; to rush headlong or carelessly. | The car careened around the corner. 汽车在拐角处疾驰而过。| The ship careened violently in the storm. 船在暴风雨中剧烈地倾斜。 | 近义词：tilt, lean, list, sway, lurch, rush, speed, hurtle | 反义词：straighten, steady | 词性: 动词

careen:/kəˈriːn/ | n. | 倾斜，侧倾 | The act of careening. | The careen of the boat was alarming. 船的倾斜令人担忧。 | 近义词：tilt, lean, list, sway | 反义词：stability | 词性: 名词
