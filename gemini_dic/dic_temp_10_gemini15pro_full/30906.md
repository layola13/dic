chai:/tʃaɪ/| n.|印度奶茶，香料奶茶|A spiced tea beverage originating from India. It typically contains black tea infused with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves, often sweetened and mixed with milk.|I love a warm cup of chai on a chilly morning. 我喜欢在寒冷的早晨喝一杯热腾腾的印度奶茶。|Synonyms: masala chai, spiced tea|Antonyms: None|noun

chai:/tʃaɪ/| n.|茶|Tea, generally.|In some cultures, "chai" simply means "tea".|He offered me a cup of chai. 他给了我一杯茶。|Synonyms: tea|Antonyms: None|noun
