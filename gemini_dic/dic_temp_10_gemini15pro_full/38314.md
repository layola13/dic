concealing:/kənˈsiːlɪŋ/
verb (present participle of conceal)
隐藏，隐瞒
The thick fog was concealing the enemy's approach. 浓雾掩盖了敌人的逼近。
He was concealing his true feelings. 他在隐藏自己的真实感受。
Synonyms: hiding, disguising, masking, camouflaging, covering up
Antonyms: revealing, disclosing, exposing, unveiling, showing
verb

Etymology:
From Old French "conceler," from Latin "concelare," meaning "to hide, cover up," from "com-" (together, completely) + "celare" (to hide).
