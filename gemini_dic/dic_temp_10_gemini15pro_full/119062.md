metonymies:/ˌmɛtəˈnɪmiːz/
| n. | 转喻；借代 | A figure of speech in which a thing or concept is referred to by the name of something closely associated with that thing or concept. |  The White House released a statement today. (White House refers to the US presidential administration) 白宫今天发表了一份声明。（白宫指代美国总统行政部门）
