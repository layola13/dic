garages: /ɡəˈrɑːʒɪz/ /ɡəˈrɑːdʒɪz/
n.
车库；汽车修理厂
a building where a car is kept, or a place where cars are repaired
例句：The car is parked in the garage. 汽车停在车库里。

例句：He works at a garage. 他在一家汽车修理厂工作。

近义词：carports, workshops, service stations
