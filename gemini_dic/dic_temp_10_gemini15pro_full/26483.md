caan:/kæn/ | modal v. | 能, 可以, 会 |  Expresses ability, possibility, permission, or a polite request.  Derived from Old English *cunnan "to know." | I can speak English. 我会说英语。| synonyms: may, be able to | antonyms: cannot, unable to | verb

caan:/kɑːn/ | n. | 罐头，容器 | A sealed metal container used for preserving food. From Middle English canne, ultimately from Latin canna "reed, cane." |  We opened a can of beans. 我们打开了一罐豆子。| synonyms: tin, container | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
