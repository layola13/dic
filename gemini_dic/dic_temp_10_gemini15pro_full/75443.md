fruitiest:/ˈfruːtiɪst/ | adj. | 最有水果味的；最馥郁的 | Having the most pronounced fruit flavor; most luscious. |  The fruitiest wine I've ever tasted came from a small vineyard in Napa Valley. 我尝过最馥郁的葡萄酒来自纳帕谷的一个小葡萄园。 | 近义词: luscious, rich, flavorful, aromatic | 反义词: bland, tasteless, insipid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From fruit + -y + -est.  Derived from the noun "fruit" with the suffix "-y" to form the adjective "fruity," and then the superlative suffix "-est" to indicate the highest degree.
