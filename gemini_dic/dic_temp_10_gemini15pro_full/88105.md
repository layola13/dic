highwaymen:/ˈhaɪweɪmən/| n. |拦路强盗，公路响马|A robber who attacks travellers on a highway.|例句：Highwaymen were a common threat in the 18th century. 18世纪，拦路强盗是常见的威胁。

例句：The highwaymen robbed the stagecoach of all its valuables. 拦路强盗抢走了驿站马车上的所有贵重物品。

近义词：bandit, robber, outlaw, brigand|反义词:lawman, police officer|词性:noun
