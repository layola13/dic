opining:/əˈpaɪnɪŋ/
|v.|表达意见；发表看法|Expressing an opinion; stating a belief.|He was opining about the state of the economy. 他正在就经济状况发表意见。|Synonyms: commenting, stating, expressing, declaring|Antonyms: reticent, silent|verb

opining:/əˈpaɪnɪŋ/
|adj.|持有意见的|Holding or expressing an opinion.|He had an opining look on his face. 他脸上带着一种发表意见的表情。  (This usage is less common but still grammatically correct.)|Synonyms: opinionated, vocal|Antonyms: reserved, quiet|adjective
