dysfunctionally:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəli/
adv. |功能失调地，异常地|  In a way that is not performing its normal function or is exhibiting abnormal behavior. | The family was operating dysfunctionally due to a lack of communication.  这个家庭由于缺乏沟通而运作不良。|
近义词: abnormally, improperly, erratically, defectively
反义词: normally, properly, healthily, effectively
词性: 副词


dysfunctional:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adj. |功能失调的，异常的| Not operating normally or properly; exhibiting abnormal behavior. | The dysfunctional family struggled to cope with everyday life.  这个功能失调的家庭难以应付日常生活。|
近义词: broken, impaired, troubled, unhealthy
反义词: functional, healthy, normal, working
词性: 形容词


function:/ˈfʌŋkʃən/
n. |功能，作用| The purpose for which something is designed or exists; role. | The function of the heart is to pump blood. 心脏的功能是泵血。|
v. |运作，起作用| Work or operate in a particular way. | The machine functions perfectly. 这台机器运转完美。|
近义词: purpose, role, use, operate, work
反义词: malfunction, breakdown, failure
词性: 名词，动词

词源:  From Late Latin *dysfunctionalis*, from *dys-* (prefix meaning 'bad, difficult') + *functionalis* (related to *functio* 'performance, execution'). Ultimately from Latin *fungi* 'perform'.
