desensationalizing:/diːˌsɛnsəˈʃənəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|使失去轰动性，使不再引起强烈反应|The media tried to desensationalize the story by focusing on the facts. 媒体试图通过关注事实来淡化这个故事的轰动性。|例句：The government tried desensationalizing the news of the epidemic to avoid panic. 政府试图淡化疫情消息以避免恐慌. The documentary desensationalized the issue of homelessness by showing the human stories behind the statistics. 这部纪录片通过展示统计数据背后的人性故事，淡化了无家可归问题的轰动性。|近义词：downplay, de-emphasize, minimize, understate|反义词:sensationalize, dramatize, exaggerate, hype up|词性:动词

desensationalized:/diːˌsɛnsəˈʃənəˌlaɪzd/
|adj.|失去轰动性的，不再引起强烈反应的|The desensationalized report focused on the scientific evidence.  这份失去轰动性的报告着重于科学证据。|例句：The desensationalized account of the event helped people understand the true impact. 对该事件的淡化描述帮助人们了解了真正的影响。  The desensationalized approach to the topic made it easier to discuss calmly.  对该话题的淡化处理使其更容易冷静地讨论。|近义词：understated, low-key, muted|反义词:sensationalized, dramatic, exaggerated, hyped-up|词性:形容词
