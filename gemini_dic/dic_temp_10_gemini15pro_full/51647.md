deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/ /ˈdiːviˌeɪt/| v. |偏离，背离|To depart or swerve from a procedure, course of action, or acceptable norm.|The bus deviated from its usual route because of the road closure. 由于道路封闭，公共汽车偏离了通常的路线。|
deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/ /ˈdiːviˌeɪt/| n. |偏离者，背离者|One who departs from a norm; a deviant.|He was considered a deviate for his unconventional beliefs.  由于他不合常理的信仰，他被认为是一个背离者。|
deviated:/ˈdiːvieɪtɪd/ /ˈdiːviˌeɪtɪd/| adj. |偏离的，背离的|Having departed or swerved from a procedure, course of action, or acceptable norm.|The deviated septum made it difficult for him to breathe. 偏离的鼻中隔使他呼吸困难。|

近义词: stray, diverge, digress, swerve, veer
反义词: conform, adhere, comply, follow

词性: v. vi. vt.  (既可以做不及物动词，也可以做及物动词)
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin deviat- ‘gone astray’, past participle of deviare, from de- ‘away’ + via ‘way’.
