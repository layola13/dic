minidrama:/ˈmɪniˌdrɑːmə/ | n. |小型戏剧；短剧；小插曲|A short dramatic piece, often presented on television or radio, or a short dramatic incident in real life. | Example 1:  The couple's argument in the restaurant was a real-life minidrama.  这对夫妇在餐厅的争吵真是一场现实生活中的小插曲。

Example 2: The TV station aired a series of minidramas about life in the city.  电视台播放了一系列关于城市生活的短剧。| 近义词：short play, skit, vignette | 反义词: epic, full-length play | 词性: noun
