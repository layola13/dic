engulf:/ɪnˈɡʌlf/ | vt. | 吞没，席卷 | to completely surround or cover something, especially in a way that harms or destroys it | The flood waters engulfed the town. 洪水吞没了整个城镇。 | synonyms:  submerge, overwhelm, inundate | antonyms:  release, free, expose | verb
