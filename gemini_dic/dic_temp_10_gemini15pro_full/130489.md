nonacceding:/ˌnɒnəkˈsiːdɪŋ/
adjective
未加入的，未同意的，未批准的
Not agreeing or consenting to something; not a party to a treaty or agreement.
The nonacceding countries refused to sign the international trade agreement. 这些未加入的国家拒绝签署国际贸易协定。
Near Synonyms: dissenting, objecting, opposing, refusing, rejecting
Antonyms: agreeing, acceding, consenting, approving, ratifying
词性: adjective
