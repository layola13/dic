ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corneline, from Medieval Latin cornalia, from Latin cornus (“cornel cherry; dogwood”), from its resemblance to the color of the cornel cherry.  The spelling was influenced by Latin carneolus (“flesh-colored”).
