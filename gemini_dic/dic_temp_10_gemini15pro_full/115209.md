manifester:/ˈmænɪˌfɛstər/| n.| 宣言者，拥护者 |Someone who advocates or supports a particular cause or belief, often publicly declaring their support.|  She is a manifester of equal rights. 她是一位平等权利的拥护者。 | synonyms: advocate, supporter, proponent, champion | antonyms: opponent, adversary, critic | noun
