baddest:/ˈbædɪst/ | adj. | 最坏的，最差的；最厉害的，最棒的(俚语) |  Superlative form of "bad".  Can mean either the worst or, in slang, the best, coolest, or most impressive. | She's the baddest singer I've ever heard. (slang - meaning best) 她是我听过的最棒的歌手。

The traffic was the baddest I've ever seen. (meaning worst)  交通是我见过的最糟糕的。| 近义词：(meaning worst) worst, terrible, awful; (slang - meaning best) best, greatest, coolest | 反义词: (meaning worst) best, greatest; (slang - meaning best) worst, terrible | 词性:形容词
