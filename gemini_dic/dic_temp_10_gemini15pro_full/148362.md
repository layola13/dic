overspeedily:/ˌōvərˈspiːdɪli/ | adv. | 过速地，超速地 |  In a manner that exceeds the speed limit or a safe speed.  | The car sped overspeedily down the highway, ignoring the speed limit signs. 汽车超速行驶在高速公路上，无视限速标志。 |  He drove overspeedily through the residential area, endangering the children playing outside. 他超速行驶穿过居民区，危及在外面玩耍的孩子们。 |  rapidly, excessively fast, too quickly | slowly, carefully, moderately | adverb
