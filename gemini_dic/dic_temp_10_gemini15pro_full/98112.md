inrushes:/ˈɪnrʌʃɪz/
noun
涌入；涌进
A sudden rapid inward movement (of something, especially air, water, or people).
The inrush of cold air when the door opened made her shiver. 门打开时冷空气涌入，让她打了个寒颤。
influx, surge, rush
outflow, exodus
n.
