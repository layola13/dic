genericness:/dʒəˈnɛrɪknəs/ | n. | 普遍性；一般性；通俗性 | The genericness of the product made it unappealing to consumers looking for something unique.  该产品的普遍性使得它对寻求独特产品的消费者没有吸引力。| 例句：The genericness of the plot made the movie predictable.  情节的通俗性使得这部电影可以预见。| 近义词：generality, commonness, universality | 反义词：uniqueness, distinctiveness, specificity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From generic + -ness.  来自形容词 generic 加名词后缀 -ness.
