hyperalert:/ˌhaɪpərəˈlɜːrt/ | adj. |高度警觉的，极度警惕的 | Extremely alert and watchful; unusually sensitive to stimuli. |  The soldier on night watch was hyperalert, scanning the perimeter for any sign of movement.  士兵在夜间值班时高度警觉，扫视着周边地区，以防有任何动静。

| The dog, hyperalert to every sound, barked at the rustling leaves.  这只狗对每一种声音都高度警觉，对着沙沙作响的树叶吠叫。

| 近义词: vigilant, watchful, observant, attentive | 反义词: oblivious, unaware, inattentive, unobservant | 词性: 形容词
