fictionalise:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪz/ (UK), /ˈfɪkʃənəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v. |使小说化；虚构|To create a fictional account of something based on real events or people; to make something seem fictional. |例句：He fictionalized his experiences in the war for his new novel. 他将自己在战争中的经历写成小说。

|例句：The movie fictionalizes the life of a famous scientist.  这部电影将一位著名科学家的生平故事改编成了虚构作品。

|近义词：novelize, dramatize, invent, fabricate|反义词：document, report, factualize|词性:动词
