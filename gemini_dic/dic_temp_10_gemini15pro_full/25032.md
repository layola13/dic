bryological:/ˌbraɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌbraɪoʊˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
苔藓学的；苔藓学上的
Relating to bryology; the scientific study of mosses and liverworts.
Bryological research has led to a greater understanding of these fascinating plants.  苔藓学研究使人们对这些迷人的植物有了更深入的了解。
The bryological society held its annual meeting in the forest.  苔藓学会在森林里举行了年会。
近义词:  muscological
反义词:  N/A
词性: adjective
词源: From bryology + -ical.  Bryology from Greek bruon "moss" + -logy "study of".
