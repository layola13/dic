escheatage:/ɪsˈtʃiːtɪdʒ/
n.
无人继承的财产；归属权的转移
The reversion of property to the state in cases where there are no legal heirs.
The state claimed the escheatage of the abandoned property.  州政府声称拥有这块废弃土地的归属权。
近义词：escheat, forfeiture
反义词：inheritance
