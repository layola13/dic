feverishly:/ˈfiːvərɪʃli/| adv.|狂热地；兴奋地；发烧地|In a manner marked by intense emotion, excitement, or activity, often associated with a fever.  Resembling the state of having a fever, characterized by restlessness and heightened activity.|She worked feverishly to finish the project before the deadline. 她狂热地工作，以便在截止日期前完成项目。|Near Synonyms: excitedly, frantically, manically, intensely, passionately|Near Antonyms: calmly, placidly, apathetically|word property: adverb
