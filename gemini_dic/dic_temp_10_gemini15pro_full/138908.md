nutpecker:/ˈnʌtˌpɛkər/ (US), /ˈnʌtˌpɛkə/ (UK)| n. |啄木鸟|A bird with a strong beak that it uses to peck holes in trees, especially to find insects to eat.|The nutpecker pecked a hole in the tree trunk. 啄木鸟在树干上啄了一个洞。|Synonyms: woodpecker (more common), flickers, sapsuckers |Antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a specific bird)|noun

nutpecker:/ˈnʌtˌpɛkər/ (US), /ˈnʌtˌpɛkə/ (UK)| n. |坚果钳|A tool used to crack nuts.|He used a nutpecker to open the walnuts. 他用坚果钳打开了核桃。|Synonyms: nutcracker, nut wrench|Antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a tool)|noun
