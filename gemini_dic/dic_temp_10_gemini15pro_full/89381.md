homophonic:/ˌhɒməˈfɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌhoʊməˈfɑːnɪk/| adj.|同音的|Relating to or being homophones; sounding alike but having different meanings.|The words "to," "too," and "two" are homophonic.|近义词：homonymous, like-sounding|反义词：heterophonic|词性: 形容词

homophonic:/ˌhɒməˈfɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌhoʊməˈfɑːnɪk/| n.|同音词|A word that is pronounced the same as another word but differs in meaning, and may also differ in spelling.| "To," "too," and "two" are homophonics.|近义词：homonym, like-sounding word|反义词：heteronym|词性: 名词
