beggary: /ˈbeɡəri/ | n. | 乞讨；贫穷；乞丐身份 | The state of being a beggar; extreme poverty. | He was reduced to beggary after losing his job and home. 他失去工作和家后沦为乞丐。|  The family lived in abject beggary. 这家人生活赤贫。 | 近义词：poverty, destitution, indigence | 反义词：wealth, affluence | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *beggerie*, from Old French *begarie*, from *begar* "beggar". Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *beghardus*, from Middle Dutch *beggaert* ("beggar").
