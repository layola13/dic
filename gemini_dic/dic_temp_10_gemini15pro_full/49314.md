delighter:/dɪˈlaɪtər/
n.
令人高兴的人或事物
something or someone that gives great pleasure
例句：The new playground proved to be a real delighter for the children.  这个新游乐场证明是孩子们真正喜欢的地方。

例句：The magician's tricks were a delighter for the audience of all ages. 魔术师的戏法让所有年龄段的观众都感到高兴。

近义词：joy, pleasure, delight, treat
反义词：disappointment, displeasure, bore
词性: 名词
