misbalancing:/ˌmɪsˈbælənsɪŋ/
verb
使失衡；使不平衡
The act of causing something to be unbalanced or out of equilibrium.
Constant dieting can misbalance your metabolism.  持续节食会导致你的新陈代谢失调。
Overfishing is misbalancing the marine ecosystem. 过度捕捞正在破坏海洋生态系统的平衡。
Synonyms: destabilizing, upsetting, disrupting
Antonyms: balancing, stabilizing, harmonizing
verb

misbalance:/ˌmɪsˈbæləns/
noun
不平衡；失衡
A state of imbalance or disequilibrium.
There is a misbalance of power between the two countries. 这两个国家之间存在权力失衡。
The misbalance in the ecosystem led to a decline in biodiversity. 生态系统的不平衡导致生物多样性下降。
Synonyms: imbalance, disproportion, inequality
Antonyms: balance, equilibrium, harmony
noun
