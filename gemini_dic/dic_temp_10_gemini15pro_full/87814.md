hicker:/ˈhaɪkər/ | n. |徒步旅行者；远足者|A person who goes for long walks in the country, especially for pleasure or exercise. | Example: The hikers enjoyed the scenic views from the mountain trail. 徒步旅行者们欣赏着山间小径的优美景色。

Example:  Experienced hikers know the importance of carrying plenty of water. 有经验的徒步旅行者知道携带大量水的重要性。 | 近义词: walker, rambler, trekker | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "couch potato" informally) | 词性: noun
