反义词：dissimilar, different, unlike
词性：adjective


congener:/ˈkɒndʒɪnə(r)/
noun
同类，同属，同族
A person or thing of the same kind or nature. A member of the same genus.
例句：The tiger and the lion are congeners.  老虎和狮子是同类。

例句：Scientists are studying the effects of the chemical on its congeners. 科学家正在研究这种化学物质对其同类物质的影响。
近义词：relative, counterpart, analogue
反义词：N/A
词性：noun
