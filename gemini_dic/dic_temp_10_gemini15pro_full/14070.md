australian-aborigines:/ɔːˈstreɪliən ˌæbəˈrɪdʒəniz/ | n. |澳大利亚原住民|The indigenous peoples of the Australian continent, who have inhabited the land for tens of thousands of years.|例句：Australian Aborigines have a rich and complex culture. 澳大利亚原住民拥有丰富而复杂的文化。|近义词：indigenous Australians, First Nations people|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider non-Indigenous Australians)|noun
