fum:/fʌm/| n.|烟，气, 激怒|A strong smell of smoke or gas;  also used figuratively to represent anger or rage.|The room was filled with fum from the extinguished candle. 房间里充满了蜡烛熄灭后的烟。|近义词：smoke, fumes, vapor, reek|反义词:fragrance, perfume|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *fūmus*, smoke. Cognate with Spanish *humo*, French *fumée*, Italian *fumo*.
