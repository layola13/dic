over-compliant:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈplaɪənt/
adjective
过度顺从的，过分服从的
Excessively or unnecessarily compliant; adhering to rules or requests too strictly or rigidly.
The over-compliant employee agreed to work overtime every day without extra pay.  这个过度顺从的员工同意每天加班，却没有额外的报酬。
He was so over-compliant that he never questioned his boss's decisions, even when they were clearly wrong. 他过分顺从，以至于即使老板的决定明显错误，他也从不质疑。
Synonyms:  obsequious, subservient, docile, compliant
Antonyms:  rebellious, defiant, noncompliant, resistant
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "compliant" (adjective from the verb "comply," meaning "to act in accordance with a wish or command")  "over-"（前缀，意为“过度地，太多地”）+ "compliant"（形容词，源自动词 "comply"，意为“按照愿望或命令行事”）
