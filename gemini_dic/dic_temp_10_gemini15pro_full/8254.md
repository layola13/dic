anti-inflammatory:/ˌæntiɪnˈflæməˌtɔːri/ /ˌæntiɪnˈflæməˌtɔːri/
adjective|抗炎的；消炎的|Reducing inflammation; used to reduce pain and swelling.|This cream contains anti-inflammatory agents.  这款药膏含有抗炎成分。|Synonyms: antiphlogistic, nonsteroidal|Antonyms: inflammatory, pro-inflammatory
noun|消炎药|A drug or substance that reduces inflammation.|The doctor prescribed an anti-inflammatory to reduce the swelling. 医生开了消炎药来消肿。|Synonyms: anti-inflammatory agent, anti-inflammatory drug|Antonyms: inflammatory agent, pro-inflammatory agent
词性: adjective, noun
