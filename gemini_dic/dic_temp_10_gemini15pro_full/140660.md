ombudsman:/ˈɒmbʊdzmən/ (UK) /ˈɑːmbədzmən/ (US)| n. | 申诉专员；巡视员；调查官 | An official appointed to investigate individuals' complaints against maladministration, especially that of public authorities. |  The ombudsman received numerous complaints about the new policy. 申诉专员收到了许多关于新政策的投诉。

The financial ombudsman helps consumers resolve disputes with financial institutions. 金融申诉专员帮助消费者解决与金融机构的纠纷。

| 近义词：mediator, arbitrator, advocate, commissioner | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用词组表示，例如：biased official) | 词性: noun


ombudsmen:/ˈɒmbʊdzmən/ (UK) /ˈɑːmbədzmən/ (US)| n. | 申诉专员；巡视员；调查官 (ombudsman的复数形式) | The plural form of ombudsman, referring to multiple officials appointed to investigate complaints. | The ombudsmen investigated several cases of alleged misconduct.  几位申诉专员调查了几起涉嫌不当行为的案件。

Various ombudsmen across the country are working to improve public services. 全国各地的申诉专员正在努力改善公共服务。


| 近义词：mediators, arbitrators, advocates, commissioners | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Swedish *ombudsman*, meaning "representative,"  compounded from *umboð* "commission, representation" + *man* "man."
