inferrers:/ɪnˈfɜːrərz/
n.
推理者，推断者
People who make inferences; those who draw conclusions from evidence.
The inferrers concluded that the suspect was guilty based on the available evidence.  推理者根据现有证据得出结论，嫌疑人有罪。
Sherlock Holmes is a master of inferrers.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯是一位推理大师。
Synonyms:  deducers, interpreters, analysts
Antonyms:  guessers, speculators
