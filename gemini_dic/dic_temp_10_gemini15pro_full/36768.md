collusion:/kəˈluːʒən/
n.
共谋，勾结
Secret or illegal cooperation or conspiracy, especially in order to cheat or deceive others.
The investigation revealed a clear collusion between the two companies to fix prices. 调查揭露了这两家公司之间为操纵价格而进行的明显勾结。
Synonyms: conspiracy, connivance, collaboration, complicity
Antonyms: honesty, integrity, fairness
noun

collusions:/kəˈluːʒənz/
n. (plural)
共谋，勾结（复数）
Multiple instances of secret or illegal cooperation.
The report detailed several collusions between government officials and private businesses.  这份报告详细描述了政府官员和私营企业之间的几起勾结事件。
Synonyms: conspiracies, connivances, collaborations, complicities
Antonyms: honesties, integrities, fairnesses
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *collusio (“fraudulent agreement”), from Latin collūdō (“to play together, conspire”), from com- (“together”) + lūdō (“I play”).
