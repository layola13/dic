come to yourself:/kʌm tu jɔːrˈsɛlf/ |phrasal verb|恢复知觉；清醒过来；镇定下来|To regain consciousness or composure after fainting, shock, or a period of disorientation.|例句：After fainting, she slowly came to herself. 昏倒后，她慢慢地恢复了知觉。|例句：He took a deep breath and tried to come to himself after the frightening experience.  经历了可怕的遭遇后，他深吸一口气，努力让自己镇定下来。|近义词：regain consciousness, come around, come to, recover, compose oneself|反义词：faint, pass out, lose consciousness|词性:动词短语

come to your senses:/kʌm tu jɔːr ˈsɛnsɪz/ |phrasal verb|恢复理智；醒悟|To start thinking and behaving reasonably after a period of foolishness or irrationality.|例句：He finally came to his senses and realized the error of his ways. 他终于醒悟过来，意识到了自己的错误。|例句：You need to come to your senses and stop spending so much money! 你需要恢复理智，别再花这么多钱了！|近义词：see reason, regain one's sanity, become sensible|反义词：lose one's mind, go crazy|词性:动词短语

注意事项:
come to oneself 和 come to one's senses 虽然意思相近，但用法略有不同. come to oneself 更多指生理上的恢复知觉，而 come to one's senses 更多指心理上的醒悟或恢复理智。
