misperceives:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːvz/
verb
误解，错误地理解
interprets something incorrectly; understands wrongly.
She misperceives his silence as anger. 她误以为他的沉默是生气。
misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrue
correctly perceive, understand correctly
