nonforests:/ˈnɒnˈfɒrɪsts/ /ˈnɑːnˈfɔːrɪsts/| n. |非森林|Areas that are not classified as forests, including grasslands, deserts, urban areas, and agricultural lands.  They are distinguished from forests by their lack of tree cover or by having a tree canopy cover below a certain threshold. |例句：Nonforests play a crucial role in carbon cycling. 非森林在碳循环中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The expansion of agriculture has led to a significant decrease in nonforest areas. 农业扩张导致非森林面积大幅减少。|近义词：non-forest lands, non-wooded areas|反义词:forests, woodlands|词性:noun
