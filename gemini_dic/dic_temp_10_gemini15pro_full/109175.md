legitimising: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
使合法化；使正当化；给…以合法性
The process of making something legitimate or acceptable.  This is the present participle form of the verb "legitimise".
The government is legitimising the use of force. 政府正在将使用武力合法化。
New laws are legitimising same-sex marriage. 新法律使同性婚姻合法化。
近义词: validating, justifying, authorizing, sanctioning
反义词: invalidating, condemning, prohibiting
词性: verb (present participle)

legitimise: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/
verb
使合法化；使正当化；给…以合法性
To make something legitimate or acceptable.
The regime tried to legitimise its seizure of power. 该政权试图使其夺取政权的行为合法化。
His academic credentials legitimise his claim. 他的学术资历使其主张合法化。
近义词: validate, justify, authorize, sanction
反义词: invalidate, condemn, prohibit
词性: verb

词源: From French légitimiser, from légitime ‘legitimate’ (see legitimate).
