depleting: /dɪˈpliːtɪŋ/
adjective
消耗的，减少的
reducing the amount of something
The depleting ozone layer is a serious environmental concern.  消耗的臭氧层是一个严重的环境问题。
Synonyms: reducing, diminishing, exhausting, draining
Antonyms: increasing, replenishing, augmenting
词性: adjective


deplete: /dɪˈpliːt/
verb
耗尽，使减少
to reduce the amount of something
The prolonged drought has severely depleted the region's water reserves. 长时间的干旱严重耗尽了该地区的储水量。
Synonyms: exhaust, drain, consume, use up
Antonyms: replenish, refill, augment, increase
词性: verb


词源:  From Late Latin *depletāre, from Latin deplēre (“to empty”), from de- (“down, away”) + plēre (“to fill”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pele-  (“to fill”).
