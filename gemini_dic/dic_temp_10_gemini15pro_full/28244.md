capsulotomy:/ˌkæpsjʊˈlɒtəmi/ | n. | 晶状体囊切开术 | Surgical incision into the capsule of the lens of the eye, especially for cataract surgery. |  A capsulotomy was performed to remove the cataract. 晶状体囊切开术是为了去除白内障。| synonyms: capsulectomy | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *capsula* (capsule) + -tomy (surgical incision).  The suffix "-tomy" comes from Greek *τομή (tomḗ)*, meaning "a cutting."
