nonlitigation:/ˌnɒnlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |非诉讼，非诉讼程序| The resolution of disputes outside of the courtroom, such as through negotiation, mediation, or arbitration.  | Example:  The company prefers nonlitigation methods of dispute resolution to avoid costly court battles.  该公司更倾向于采用非诉讼方式解决纠纷，以避免代价高昂的法庭斗争。

| Example:  Nonlitigation options are often faster and less expensive. 非诉讼选择通常更快、更便宜。 | Synonym: alternative dispute resolution, ADR | Antonym: litigation | Noun
