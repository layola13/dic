enfeeblish:/ɪnˈfiːblɪʃ/| vt.|使衰弱，使虚弱|To make weak or feeble; to deprive of strength.|The long illness enfeebled him.  这场久病让他变得虚弱。|Synonyms: weaken, debilitate, enervate|Antonyms: strengthen, invigorate|verb

enfeeblish:/ɪnˈfiːblɪʃ/| vt.|使无力，使无效|To make something less effective or powerful.|The new regulations have enfeebled the power of the local government. 新规定削弱了地方政府的权力。|Synonyms: undermine, impair, cripple|Antonyms: empower, reinforce|verb
