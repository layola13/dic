northwester:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstər/ | n. adj.|西北风；来自西北的|A wind from the northwest; coming from the northwest|例句：A cold northwester blew across the plains. 一阵寒冷的西北风吹过平原。

例句：The northwester brought heavy rain. 西北风带来了大雨。|近义词：northwest wind|反义词：southeaster|词性: 名词, 形容词

northwestern:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstərn/ | adj.|西北方的，来自西北的|Situated in or coming from the northwest.|例句：The northwestern region of the country is known for its rugged beauty.  这个国家的西北地区以其崎岖的美丽而闻名。

例句：They lived in a small northwestern town. 他们住在一个西北小镇。|近义词：northwest|反义词：southeastern|词性: 形容词
