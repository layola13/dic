mise:/miːz/ | n. |  支出, 费用;  舞台布置; (尤指餐馆的) 上菜, 布菜; (尤指赌博的)赌注 | An arrangement of actors and scenery on a stage for a theatrical production.  Also, the stage setting or scenery of a play. In restaurants, it can refer to the placing of dishes on the table or the dishes themselves. It can also refer to a bet, especially a large one. | The mise en scène was breathtaking, transporting the audience to another world. 舞台布置令人叹为观止，将观众带到了另一个世界。| synonyms: setting, scenery, staging, layout, wager, stake | antonyms:  disarray, chaos (for setting); winnings (for wager) | noun
