antisocialists:/ˌæntiˈsoʊʃəlɪsts/ | n. | 反社会主义者；反对社会主义的人 | People who oppose socialism. |  Many antisocialists believe that socialism stifles individual freedom. 许多反社会主义者认为社会主义扼杀了个人自由。

Antisocialists argued against the nationalization of industries. 反社会主义者反对工业国有化。 | 近义词: conservatives, capitalists, right-wingers | 反义词: socialists, communists, left-wingers | Noun
