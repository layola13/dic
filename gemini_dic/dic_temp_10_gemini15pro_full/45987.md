cyanosite: /saɪˈænoʊsaɪt/ | n. | 蓝矾 |  A bright blue mineral consisting of hydrated copper sulfate, CuSO4·5H2O, used as a source of copper. | Cyanosite was once used as a pigment. 蓝矾曾经被用作颜料。 |  Gardeners sometimes use cyanosite to control algae. 园丁有时用蓝矾来控制藻类。 | 近义词: chalcanthite, blue vitriol, copper sulfate pentahydrate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
