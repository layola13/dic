bouncy:/ˈbaʊnsi/| adj.|有弹性的，活泼的|Something that is bouncy springs back easily when pressed or dropped. It can also describe someone lively and energetic.|The children were playing on a bouncy castle. 孩子们在一个充气城堡上玩耍。|近义词：elastic, resilient, lively, energetic|反义词:stiff, rigid, lifeless|adjective

bouncy:/ˈbaʊnsi/| n.|弹跳，活力|The quality of being bouncy; the ability to spring back easily.|The bouncy of the basketball made it easy to dribble.  篮球的弹性使它很容易运球。|近义词：elasticity, resilience, liveliness, energy|反义词:stiffness, rigidity, lifelessness|noun
