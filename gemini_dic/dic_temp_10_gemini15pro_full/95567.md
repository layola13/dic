incoherently:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərəntli/ | adv. | 不连贯地，语无伦次地 | in a way that is not clear and is difficult to understand | He mumbled incoherently, and I couldn't understand a word. 他含糊不清地咕哝着，我一个字也听不懂。| She spoke incoherently after the accident, clearly in shock. 事故发生后，她语无伦次地说着话，显然是受到了惊吓。| 近义词：unintelligibly, confusedly, ramblingly | 反义词：coherently, clearly, lucidly | 词性: 副词
