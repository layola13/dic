bowdlerize:/ˈbɑːdləraɪz/ /ˈbəʊdləraɪz/| vt.| 删节，净化，修订（通常指删除被认为不雅或冒犯性的内容）|To remove material that is considered improper or offensive from a text or account, especially with the result that the text becomes weaker or less effective.|  The publisher bowdlerized the book to avoid offending readers. 出版商删节了这本书，以免冒犯读者。|近义词：censor, expurgate, sanitize|反义词: unexpurgated, uncensored|动词

bowdlerized:/ˈbɑːdləraɪzd/ /ˈbəʊdləraɪzd/| adj.| 被删节的，被净化的|Having been edited to remove material considered improper or offensive.| The bowdlerized version of the play omitted several key scenes.  这个剧本的删节版省略了几个关键场景。|近义词：censored, expurgated, sanitized|反义词: unexpurgated, uncensored|形容词
