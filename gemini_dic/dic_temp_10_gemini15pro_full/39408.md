conking:/ˈkɒŋkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 梳理 (特指非裔美国人的一种发型)；(引擎)突然熄火；|The process of straightening tightly curled hair with a relaxer, often associated with African American men's hairstyles in the mid-20th century. Also, the hairstyle itself. Can also refer to an engine suddenly stopping.|例句：He spent hours perfecting his conk. 他花了几个小时梳理他的那种发型。

例句：The car's engine conked out on the highway. 汽车的引擎在高速公路上突然熄火了。|近义词：straightening (for hair), stalling (for engine) |反义词: curling (for hair)|词性:名词

conk:/ˈkɒŋk/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋk/ (US)| vi. vt. |(引擎)突然熄火；(重击)鼻子；| To suddenly stop working (usually of an engine).  To hit someone, especially on the nose.|例句：The engine conked out just as we reached the summit.  我们刚到达山顶，引擎就熄火了。

例句：He conked him on the nose. 他打了他的鼻子。|近义词：stall, die (for engine), hit, punch (for hitting)|反义词: start (for engine)|词性:动词

词源：
Conk (as in engine stalling) is likely imitative, echoing the sound of a sudden stoppage. Conk (as in hair straightening and the resulting hairstyle) is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "conch," referring to the shell's smooth, hard surface.
