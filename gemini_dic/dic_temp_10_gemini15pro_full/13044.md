astringently:/əˈstrɪndʒəntli/| adv.|涩地，严厉地|In an astringent manner; harshly or severely.  It refers to both a physical sensation of tightness or dryness and a metaphorical sense of severity or sharpness.|He spoke astringently about the need for fiscal responsibility.|近义词：harshly, severely, austerely, sternly|反义词:gently, mildly, kindly|词性:副词
