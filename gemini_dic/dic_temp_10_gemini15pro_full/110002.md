librae's:/ˈliːbriːz/ |n. (plural, possessive)|天秤座的；天秤座人的（东西）| This refers to something belonging to or associated with the constellation Libra, or a person born under that sign. |  These are librae's scales. 这些是天秤座的秤。| synonyms: Libran's | antonyms: (No direct antonym for possession) | possessive noun


Etymology:
From Latin "libra" (scales, balance), the name of the constellation Libra, representing a set of scales. The possessive form "librae's" indicates ownership or association with Libra.
