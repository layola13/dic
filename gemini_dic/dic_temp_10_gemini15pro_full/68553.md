fanfictions:/ˌfænˈfɪkʃənz/| n. |同人小说|Stories written by fans of a particular work (such as a movie, TV show, or book), using existing characters and settings.|She writes Harry Potter fanfictions in her spare time. 她空闲时间写哈利波特的同人小说。|近义词：fanworks, derivative works|反义词:original fiction|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
fan + fiction
"fan" shortened from "fanatic" (n.) "enthusiast," 1889, from fanatic + -ic;
"fiction" (n.) early 14c., "anything invented or imagined," from Old French ficcion "dissimulation, feigning; invention, fabrication" (12c.), from Latin fictionem (nominative fictio) "a fashioning or feigning," noun of action from past-participle stem of fingere "to shape, form, devise, feign," originally "to knead, form out of clay"
