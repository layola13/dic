airlessness:/ˈeərləsnəs/| n.|无空气；缺少空气；窒息|The airlessness of outer space makes it impossible to breathe without specialized equipment. 外太空的无空气状态使得在没有特殊设备的情况下无法呼吸。|例句：The airlessness of the moon makes it a hostile environment for life. 月球上没有空气，使其成为不适宜生命生存的环境。

The airlessness in the sealed chamber caused the candle to extinguish quickly. 密闭房间里的缺氧导致蜡烛迅速熄灭。|近义词：vacuum, suffocation|反义词:oxygenation, ventilation|词性:名词
