glandulose:/ˈɡlændjʊləs/| adj.|腺状的；有腺的；分泌腺的|Covered with or bearing glands; glandular.|The plant's leaves are glandulose, secreting a sticky resin. 这种植物的叶子有腺，会分泌一种粘稠的树脂。|例句：The stem is glandulose and hairy. 茎有腺且多毛。

例句：The glandulose trichomes produce a variety of compounds. 腺毛会产生各种化合物。|近义词：glandular, secretory|反义词:aglandular|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *glandulōsus, from glandula (“small acorn, gland”) + -ōsus.
