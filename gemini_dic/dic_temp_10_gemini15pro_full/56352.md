doh:/doʊ/ (US), /dəʊ/ (UK)
|interj.|唉！; 啊呀！（表示懊恼、沮丧、意识到犯错等）|An exclamation of frustration, dismay, or realization of a mistake, popularized by Homer Simpson.|例句：Doh! I forgot my keys! 唉！我忘记带钥匙了！|近义词：d'oh, oops, damn, shoot|反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation of this type)|interjection
