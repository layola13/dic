Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? 她最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:

What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before I arrived? 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the power went out? 停电前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  在她换专业之前，她一直在学什么？

What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
