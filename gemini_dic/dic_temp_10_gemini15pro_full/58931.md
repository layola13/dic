duskinesses:/ˈdʌskɪnɪsɪz/
noun
昏暗；幽暗；朦胧
The state or quality of being dusky; darkness or dimness.
The duskinesses of the forest made it hard to see the path. 森林的昏暗使道路难以辨认。
Synonyms: dimness, darkness, gloom, shadowiness, obscurity
Antonyms: brightness, lightness, brilliance, radiance
词性: 名词
