deobstruent:/ˌdiːəbˈstrʊənt/| adj. n.|通畅的，清除阻塞的；祛痰剂|A deobstruent substance or medication clears obstructions, particularly from the respiratory or digestive systems.  It facilitates the removal of mucus or other blockages.|The doctor prescribed a deobstruent for her congested cough. 医生给她开了祛痰药治疗她严重的咳嗽。|近义词：expectorant, decongestant, laxative|反义词：obstruent, constipating|词性: adjective, noun
词源：From Late Latin *deobstruēns, present participle of deobstruere (“to unblock, clear”), from de- + obstruere (“to obstruct”).
