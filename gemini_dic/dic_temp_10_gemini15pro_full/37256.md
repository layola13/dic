comicalnesses:/kəˈmɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
滑稽；诙谐；可笑
The comicalnesses of the situation were not lost on us.
The play was full of comicalnesses.
近义词: humor, funniness, drollery, wittiness
反义词: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From comical + -ness.  Comical comes from the Late Latin comicus, from Greek kōmikos "comic, of or for comedy," from kōmos "revel, merrymaking, komos (a drunken procession)," perhaps of imitative origin.
