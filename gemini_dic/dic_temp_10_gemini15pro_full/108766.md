lease:/liːs/
n.|租约，租赁；租期|A contract by which one party conveys land, property, services, etc., to another for a specified time, usually in return for a periodic payment.|They signed a lease for the apartment. 他们签署了公寓的租约。
lease:/liːs/
v.|出租，租赁|To grant the temporary possession or use of (lands, tenements, etc.) to another, usually in consideration of rent.|They leased the car for three years. 他们租了这辆车三年。
lease:/liːs/
n.|租期|The period of time for which a lease is valid.|The lease on the apartment is up next month. 公寓的租约下个月到期。

近义词:rent, hire, let
反义词:buy, purchase
词性:noun, verb
