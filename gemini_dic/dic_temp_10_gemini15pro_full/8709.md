anticruelty:/ˌæntiˈkruːəlti/
adjective
反虐待的
opposed to cruelty towards animals or people
The anticruelty society works to prevent animal abuse.  反虐待动物协会致力于防止虐待动物。
The organization promotes anticruelty laws.  该组织推动反虐待法。
近义词:humane, compassionate
反义词:cruel, inhumane
词性:adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix meaning "against") + cruelty (from Old French crualte, from Latin crudelitas)
