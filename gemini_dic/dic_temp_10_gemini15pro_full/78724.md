giggish: /ˈɡɪɡɪʃ/ | adj. | 轻浮的，傻笑的，爱笑的 | Given to giggling; frivolous and silly. | She had a giggish, almost childish, laugh. 她笑起来轻浮傻气，几乎像个孩子。|  He gave a giggish snort. 他傻笑了一声。 | 近义词：silly, frivolous, giddy, flighty | 反义词：serious, solemn, grave | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From giggler + -ish.  Ultimately from Middle Low German *gickelen.
