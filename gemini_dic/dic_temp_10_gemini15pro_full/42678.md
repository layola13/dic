countersued:/ˌkaʊntərˈsuːd/
verb
反诉，提起反诉
To file a countersuit against someone is to sue them after they have sued you.
例句：After being sued for breach of contract, the company countersued for damages.  在被控违约后，该公司提起反诉，要求赔偿损失。

例句：She countersued him for defamation after he sued her for theft.  在他起诉她盗窃之后，她反诉他诽谤。

近义词：reconvene, cross-claim, cross-sue
反义词：sue, prosecute
词性：verb
