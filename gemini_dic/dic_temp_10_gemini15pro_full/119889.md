militarising:/ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
军事化
The act of equipping and organizing a country, region, group, or other entity for military action; the act of increasing the influence of the military on a society or government.
The government is militarising the police force, providing them with military-grade weapons and training. 政府正在将警察部队军事化，为他们提供军事级别的武器和训练。
The ongoing conflict is militarising the region, leading to increased arms sales and military presence.  持续的冲突正在使该地区军事化，导致武器销售和军事存在增加。
