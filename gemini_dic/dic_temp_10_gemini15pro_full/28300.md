capybara:/ˌkæpiˈbɑːrə/ (US), /ˌkæpɪˈbɑːrə/ (UK)| n. |水豚|A large South American rodent with webbed feet, short brown fur, and a blunt muzzle. It is the largest living rodent.|例句：We saw a capybara swimming in the river. 我们看到一只水豚在河里游泳。

例句：The capybara grazed peacefully on the riverbank. 水豚在河岸上平静地吃草。
