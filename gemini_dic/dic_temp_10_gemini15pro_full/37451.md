commissar:/ˈkɒmɪsɑːr/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪsɑːr/ (US)| n. | 政委；委员；专员 | An official of the Communist Party, especially in the former Soviet Union, responsible for political education and the enforcement of party policy. | The commissar oversaw the political indoctrination of the soldiers. 政委监督士兵的政治灌输。|Synonyms: official, commissioner, agent, delegate, representative | Antonyms: civilian, non-official | Noun
