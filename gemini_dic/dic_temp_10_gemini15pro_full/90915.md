Etymology:
From Middle English *hur*, from Old Norse *hurr* (“pain, anguish”), from Proto-Germanic *hurza*, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“to hurt, harm”). Cognate with Icelandic *hurð* (“pain”), Swedish *hurd* (“pain, difficulty”).
