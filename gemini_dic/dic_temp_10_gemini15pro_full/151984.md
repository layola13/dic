ETYMOLOGY->
From "pasture" + "land." "Pasture" comes from Old French "pasture" (noun) and "pasturer" (verb), both from Latin "pastura"  "pasture, grazing," from "pascere"  "to feed, graze." "Land" comes from Old English "land"  "land, territory."
