arsine:/ˈɑːrsiːn/ |n.|胂，砷化三氢|A colorless, flammable, and highly poisonous gas (AsH3) formed by the action of acids on arsenides or by the action of nascent hydrogen on arsenic compounds.  It has a garlicky odor.|The accidental release of arsine gas resulted in several workers being hospitalized.|Synonyms: arsenic hydride, hydrogen arsenide|Antonyms:  N/A (lack of a direct chemical antonym)|noun
