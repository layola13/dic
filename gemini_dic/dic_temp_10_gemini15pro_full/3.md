a's:/eɪz/|n.|A 的（复数）|The plural form of "a," used to represent multiple instances of something denoted by "a."  It's often used informally or when the specific noun is understood from context.|She got straight A's this semester. 她这学期得了全A。|近义词: As (when referring to grades) |反义词: F's (when referring to grades)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the letter "a," with the plural suffix "-s."  The letter "a" itself ultimately derives from the Phoenician letter 'aleph, representing a glottal stop.

USAGE->
