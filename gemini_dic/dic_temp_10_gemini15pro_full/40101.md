contaminators:/kənˈtæmɪneɪtərz/
n.
污染者；沾染者
People who contaminate something.  Those who introduce impurities or pollutants into something, making it impure or unusable.
The contaminators were fined heavily for polluting the river. 污染河流的污染者被处以巨额罚款。
The contaminators were identified and prosecuted. 污染者被确认并被起诉。
polluters, spoilers, defilers
purifiers, cleaners
