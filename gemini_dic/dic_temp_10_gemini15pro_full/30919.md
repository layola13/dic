chaining:/ˈtʃeɪnɪŋ/| n. |链接; 链式;|The act or process of linking things together, often in a sequence or series.  It can refer to physical objects, like chains, or abstract concepts, like a chain of events.|He explained the chaining reaction in chemistry. 他解释了化学中的链式反应。|synonyms: linking, connecting, concatenation|antonyms: separating, disconnecting, detaching|noun

chaining:/ˈtʃeɪnɪŋ/| v. |链接;|Present participle of "chain," meaning to connect or fasten with a chain, either literally or figuratively.|They are chaining the prisoners together. 他们把囚犯用铁链锁在一起。|synonyms: linking, connecting|antonyms: separating, disconnecting|verb
