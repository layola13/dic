handcraft:/ˈhændkrɑːft/ |/ˈhændkræft/| n. v. | 手工艺品；手工制作 |To make something skillfully by hand. | 例句：She handcrafts beautiful wooden toys. 她手工制作漂亮的木制玩具。| 近义词：craft, make, fashion | 反义词：mass-produce, manufacture | 词性: 动词

词源: hand (手) + craft (工艺, 技巧).  From Old English *handcraft, from hand (hand) + craft (skill).
