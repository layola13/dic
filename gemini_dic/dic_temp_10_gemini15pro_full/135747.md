nonproducers:/ˌnɒnprəˈdjuːsərz/ /ˌnɑːnprəˈduːsərz/
| n. |非生产者；非生产性因素|People or things that do not produce goods or services.  Those who do not contribute to the economy through the creation of goods or services.  |The company laid off a number of nonproducers to cut costs. 公司为了削减成本解雇了一些非生产人员。

The government is trying to reduce the number of nonproducers in society. 政府正在努力减少社会上的非生产人员数量。|Near synonyms: idlers, dependents, consumers|Antonyms: producers, manufacturers, workers|Noun
