heathery:/ˈhɛðəri/| adj.|石楠丛生的，覆盖着石楠的，淡紫色的|Covered with heather; resembling the color of heather.|例句：The hills were heathery and desolate.  群山覆盖着石楠，一片荒凉。|例句：She wore a heathery sweater. 她穿着一件淡紫色的毛衣。|近义词：heather-clad, heather-covered|反义词: barren, bare|词性: adjective

词源: From *heather* + *-y*.
