nan:/næn/ | n. | 非数字（Not a Number）|  A value that is not a valid number. This often arises in computing when a mathematical operation produces an undefined or unrepresentable result. | Example: Dividing by zero in many programming languages results in NaN.  零在许多编程语言中相除会导致 NaN。 | Example: Trying to take the square root of a negative number can also produce NaN.  尝试取负数的平方根也会产生 NaN。| Synonym: undefined, indeterminate | Antonym: number, numeric | Noun
