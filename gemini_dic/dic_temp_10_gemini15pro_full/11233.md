archlute: /ˈɑːrtʃluːt/ | n. | 大键琴 | A large lute with additional bass strings. It was popular in the 16th and 17th centuries. | The archlute produced a rich, resonant sound. 大键琴发出丰富而洪亮的声音。|  The composer wrote several pieces specifically for the archlute.  这位作曲家专门为大键琴创作了几首曲子。|  Near synonyms: theorbo, chitarrone | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a modern instrument like guitar) | Noun
