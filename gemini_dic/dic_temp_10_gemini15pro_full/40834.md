convulsional: /kənˈvʌlʃənəl/ | adj. | 抽搐的，痉挛的 | Relating to or characterized by convulsions;  spasmodic. |  The child suffered convulsional seizures.  这个孩子患有抽搐性癫痫发作。 | Synonyms:  spasmodic, fitful, jerky, twitchy | Antonyms:  steady, smooth, controlled | adjective

convulsional: /kənˈvʌlʃənəl/ | n. | 抽搐 | A sudden, violent, irregular movement of a limb or of the body, caused by involuntary contraction of muscles; a fit or spasm. |  The patient experienced a convulsional episode. 病人经历了一次抽搐。| Synonyms: spasm, seizure, fit | Antonyms: stillness, calmness | noun (This usage is less common, and usually refers to something causing or related to convulsions.)
