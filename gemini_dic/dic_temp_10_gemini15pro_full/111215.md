lithophytic:/ˌlɪθəˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
岩生的；石生的
(of a plant) growing on rocks.
Example:  Lithophytic orchids are often found clinging to cliffs. 岩生兰花经常被发现 clinging to cliffs.
Example: Certain types of moss are lithophytic, thriving on bare rock surfaces.  某些类型的苔藓是岩生的，在裸露的岩石表面茁壮成长。
近义词:rock-dwelling, saxicolous
反义词:terrestrial, aquatic
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Greek *líthos* (stone) + *phytón* (plant).
