nonerratic:/ˌnɑːnɪˈrætɪk/
adjective
非不稳定的，规律的
Not erratic; regular or consistent.
The nonerratic heartbeat of the athlete indicated excellent cardiovascular health.  运动员规律的心跳表明他的心血管非常健康。
The clock ticked in a nonerratic rhythm.  时钟以稳定的节奏滴答作响。
近义词:regular, consistent, steady, stable, predictable
反义词:erratic, irregular, unstable, unpredictable
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + erratic.  Erratic comes from Late Latin erraticus, from Latin errare "to wander, stray, err."
