cinematically:/ˌsɪnəˈmætɪkli/| adv.|电影般地，在电影方面|In a way that relates to the making or showing of films, or resembles a film.  It describes something that looks or feels like it belongs in a movie, often emphasizing visual elements like dramatic lighting, sweeping shots, or intense emotions.|The story unfolds cinematically, with sweeping landscapes and dramatic close-ups.  故事以电影般的方式展开，伴随着辽阔的风景和戏剧性的特写镜头。

The director uses music cinematically to heighten the emotional impact of the scene. 导演巧妙地运用电影配乐来增强场景的情感冲击力。|近义词：visually, dramatically, strikingly, picturesquely|反义词：plainly, simply, uninterestingly|词性：副词
