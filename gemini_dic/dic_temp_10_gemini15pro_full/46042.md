cycled:/ˈsaɪkəld/| v.|骑自行车；循环|Past tense of "cycle".  Meaning to ride a bicycle or to move through a sequence of events or stages that repeat themselves. |I cycled to work yesterday. 我昨天骑自行车去上班了。

He cycled through the gears of the bike. 他循环换着自行车的档位。|近义词：biked, rode|反义词：walked, drove|词性: 动词
