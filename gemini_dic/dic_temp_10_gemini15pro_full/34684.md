clerkhood:/ˈklɜːrkhʊd/
n.
职员身份；职员生涯
The state or period of being a clerk.
He spent his entire adult life in clerkhood, working diligently at the bank.  他在银行勤奋工作，成年后一直在做职员。
近义词:clerkship
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider leadership, management, etc. depending on context)
