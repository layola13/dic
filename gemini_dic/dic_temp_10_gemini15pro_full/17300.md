bcc'ing: /ˌbiː siː ˈsiːɪŋ/ | v. | 密送；暗送 | The act of sending an email to someone using the "blind carbon copy" (bcc) field, so that the recipient's email address is not visible to the other recipients. |  I bcc'ed John on the email to keep him informed. 我把约翰密送了这封邮件，让他了解情况。 | Synonym: blind copying | Antonym: cc'ing | verb (present participle)

bcc'ing: /ˌbiː siː ˈsiːɪŋ/ | n. | 密送；暗送 (名词化用法，较少见) | The practice of using the bcc field in emails. | The bcc'ing of John on the email ensured his privacy.  将约翰密送邮件确保了他的隐私。| Synonym: blind copying | Antonym: cc'ing | noun (gerund)
