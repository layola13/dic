encomiastical: /ˌɛnkəˈmɪstɪkəl/ /ˌɑːnkəˈmɪstɪkəl/
adjective
赞美的，颂扬的
Expressing or containing high praise.
例句：He delivered an encomiastical speech about the retiring CEO.  他发表了歌颂退休CEO的演讲。

例句：The book review was highly encomiastical.  这本书评高度赞扬。

近义词：laudatory, complimentary, eulogistic, panegyrical
反义词：critical, derogatory, defamatory
词性: adjective
