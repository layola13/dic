dielytra: /daɪˈliːtrə/ | n. | 荷包牡丹 | A genus of herbaceous plants with heart-shaped flowers, native to Asia and North America.  Belongs to the poppy family (Papaveraceae). | Example: The dielytra in my garden are blooming beautifully this spring.  我花园里的荷包牡丹今年春天开得很漂亮。

Example:  She planted dielytra along the fence. 她沿着篱笆种植了荷包牡丹。| 近义词: bleeding heart (common name), Dicentra (synonym - scientific name) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek, dis, "twice" + elytron, "sheath," referring to the two spurs of the outer petals.
