hoker:/ˈhɔːkə(r)/| n.|小贩; 叫卖的小贩|A person who sells goods, typically food or drinks, from a stall or cart.|The hawker sold delicious noodles from his street cart.  这个小贩在他的街边小推车上卖美味的面条。|Synonyms: vendor, peddler, street vendor|Antonyms: buyer, customer|noun

hoker:/ˈhɔːkə(r)/| n.|兜售者; 推销员|Someone who tries to sell goods aggressively, often by going from house to house.|The hawker knocked on every door, trying to sell his cleaning products.  这个推销员挨家挨户地敲门，试图推销他的清洁用品。|Synonyms: salesman, canvasser, solicitor|Antonyms: buyer, customer|noun
