interlunar:/ˌɪntərˈluːnər/| adj.|月间的，新月之间的|Relating to the period between old and new moons, when the moon is invisible.|The interlunar period is a time of quiet in the night sky. 月亮不见的时期，夜空一片寂静。|The interlunar nights were particularly dark.  新月之间的那几个夜晚尤其黑暗。|Synonyms: dark moon, no moon|Antonyms: full moon|adjective
