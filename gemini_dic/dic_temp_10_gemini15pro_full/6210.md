ammonitish:/ˌæməˈnaɪtɪʃ/
adjective
像菊石的；含菊石的；菊石化石似的
resembling or containing ammonites; ammonoid
The cliffs were ammonitish. 这些悬崖富含菊石化石。
The rock formations were noticeably ammonitish. 这些岩层明显地含有菊石化石。
