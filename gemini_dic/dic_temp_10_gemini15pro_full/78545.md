ghostlike:/ˈɡoʊstˌlaɪk/| adj.|幽灵般的，鬼魅似的|Resembling or suggestive of a ghost; spectral.|The abandoned house had a ghostlike appearance. 这栋废弃的房子有一种鬼魅般的外观。|The fog rolled in, giving the forest a ghostlike quality. 雾气滚滚而来，使森林呈现出一种幽灵般的特质。|近义词：spectral, ghostly, phantom, eerie, unearthly|反义词：substantial, tangible, real, corporeal|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From ghost +‎ -like.
