coaster:/ˈkoʊstər (US), ˈkəʊstə(r) (UK)| n.|杯垫；过山车；沿海贸易船|A small mat or tray placed under a drink to protect the surface of a table.  A roller coaster. A ship that sails along a coast, carrying goods or passengers.|She put her glass on a coaster. 她把杯子放在杯垫上。

The kids rode the roller coaster five times. 孩子们坐了五次过山车。

The coaster delivered supplies to the seaside towns. 这艘沿海贸易船向海滨城镇运送物资。|近义词：mat, placemat, drink coaster (for the mat); roller coaster (for the amusement ride); coastal trader, coastal vessel (for the ship)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
