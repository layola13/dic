dunderheadedness:/ˌdʌndərˈhɛdɪdnəs/| n. |愚蠢，笨拙|The sheer dunderheadedness of the plan was astounding.  这个计划的愚蠢程度令人震惊。|例句：His dunderheadedness led him to make a series of poor decisions. 他的愚蠢导致他做出一系列糟糕的决定。

The level of dunderheadedness displayed in this project is simply unacceptable.  这个项目中表现出的愚蠢程度简直令人无法接受。|近义词：stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, obtuseness|反义词：intelligence, cleverness, brilliance, wisdom|词性:noun


Etymology:  From "dunderhead" + "-ness".  "Dunderhead" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "dunder" (a dialectal word for "thunder") or "dander" (anger, temper).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
