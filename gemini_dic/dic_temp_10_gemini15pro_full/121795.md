misrefer:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːr/
verb
错误地提及；错误地引用
to refer to (something or someone) incorrectly
He misreferred to the author in his bibliography. 他在他的参考书目中错误地引用了作者。
misquote, miscite
correctly cite
v.
