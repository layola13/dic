equivocating: /ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective
含糊其辞，支吾其词，模棱两可
using ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.
He was equivocating about the reasons for his resignation. 他对于辞职的原因含糊其辞。
近义词: prevaricating, hedging, evading, dodging, beating around the bush
反义词: forthright, direct, candid, straightforward
verb, adjective
