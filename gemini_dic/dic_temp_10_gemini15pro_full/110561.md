limicolae:/ˌlɪmɪˈkoʊliː/
n.
shorebirds; waders
A group of birds that live near water and wade in shallow water to find food.
例句：Limicolae, such as sandpipers and plovers, are often seen foraging along the coast.  滨鸟，例如矶鹬和鸻，经常在海岸边觅食。

例句：The limicolae population has declined due to habitat loss.  由于栖息地丧失，滨鸟的数量减少了。
近义词：waders, shorebirds
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)
词性: noun
