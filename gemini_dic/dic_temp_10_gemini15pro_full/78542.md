ghostless:/ˈɡoʊstləs/| adj.|无鬼的，没有鬼魂的|Without ghosts; not haunted.  Free from the influence or suggestion of ghosts.  Figuratively, lacking spirit or animation.|The scientist declared the house ghostless after a thorough investigation. 科学家在彻底调查后宣布这房子没有鬼魂。|Synonyms: unhaunted, spiritless, lifeless|Antonyms: haunted, ghostly, spirited|adjective
