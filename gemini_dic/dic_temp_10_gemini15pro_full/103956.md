judicious:/dʒuːˈdɪʃəs/
adjective
明智的；有见识的；审慎的
Having, showing, or done with good judgment or sense.
She made a judicious choice of career. 她做出了明智的职业选择。
sensible, wise, prudent, discerning
injudicious, unwise, imprudent, foolish
词性: 形容词
