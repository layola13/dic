brae:/breɪ/| n. |山坡，斜坡|A slope or hillside, especially one that is steep.  |例句：The sheep grazed peacefully on the brae. 羊群在山坡上平静地吃草。

例句：The cottage was nestled on the brae overlooking the valley. 小屋依偎在俯瞰山谷的山坡上。|近义词：hillside, slope, incline|反义词：valley, dale, lowland|词性:名词
