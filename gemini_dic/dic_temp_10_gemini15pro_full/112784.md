lubricative:/ˈluːbrɪkətɪv/ | adj. | 润滑的；滑溜的；油滑的；促进性交的 | Reducing friction; oily; slippery. Also, in a sexual context, making intercourse easier. |  The mechanic applied a lubricative oil to the gears. 机械师给齿轮涂上了润滑油。

The lotion has lubricative properties, making it ideal for massage.  这款乳液具有润滑性，非常适合按摩。| 近义词：slippery, oily, greasy, smooth | 反义词：rough, coarse, dry | 词性: adjective

lubricant:/ˈluːbrɪkənt/ | n. | 润滑剂；润滑油 | A substance, such as oil or grease, that reduces friction when applied to moving parts. | The engine requires a high-quality lubricant. 发动机需要高质量的润滑油。

Silicone-based lubricants are often used in medical procedures.  医用程序中经常使用硅基润滑剂。| 近义词：oil, grease | 反义词：abrasive | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *lubricativus, from Latin lubricus (“slippery”) + -ivus.
