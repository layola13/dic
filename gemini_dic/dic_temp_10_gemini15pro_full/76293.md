"Gabbier" is the comparative form of "gabby," which means talkative. "Gabby" likely originated from "gab," meaning idle talk or chatter, which dates back to the late 18th century.  The origin of "gab" is uncertain, but it may be related to the Old Norse word "gabba," meaning to mock or scoff.
