extirpatory:/ˈɛkstərpeɪtɔːri/
adjective
根除的，消除的
Serving to extirpate; tending to destroy completely.
The surgeon performed an extirpatory procedure to remove the cancerous tumor.  外科医生进行了根除手术以切除癌性肿瘤。
The government launched an extirpatory campaign against corruption. 政府发起了根除腐败的运动。
近义词: eradicative, destructive, eliminative
反义词:  constructive, restorative, preservative
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin extirpat-, past participle stem of extirpare "to root out, pluck up by the stem," from ex "out" + stirps "root, stock."
