parentalism: /ˈpɛərəntəlɪzəm/ | n. |家长式作风，家长主义| The act of treating people in a fatherly manner, especially by providing for their needs without giving them rights or responsibilities. |  Example 1: The government's paternalism stifled individual initiative. 政府的家长式作风扼杀了个人主动性。

Example 2:  She resented her boss's paternalism and wanted more autonomy. 她憎恨老板的家长式作风，想要更多的自主权。 |Synonyms:  condescension, patronage | Antonyms: autonomy, independence | noun
