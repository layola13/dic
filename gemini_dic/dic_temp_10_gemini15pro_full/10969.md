arbitrary: /ˈɑːrbɪtrɛri/ (US), /ˈɑːbɪtrəri/ (UK)| adj.|任意的；武断的；专横的|Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system.|The choice of restaurant was arbitrary, as no one had a preference. 餐厅的选择是任意的，因为没有人有偏好。|例句：The dictator made arbitrary arrests without any legal basis. 独裁者随意逮捕人，没有任何法律依据。

例句：The deadline seems arbitrary and doesn't relate to the project's actual needs.  这个截止日期看起来很随意，与项目的实际需求无关。


近义词：random, capricious, whimsical, despotic|反义词：reasonable, rational, reasoned, justifiable|词性:形容词
