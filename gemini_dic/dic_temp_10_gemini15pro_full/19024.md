beta-decays:/ˈbeɪtə dɪˈkeɪz/| n.|β衰变|A type of radioactive decay in which a beta particle (an electron or positron) is emitted from an atomic nucleus.|Carbon-14 beta-decays into nitrogen-14. 碳-14 β衰变为氮-14。|Synonyms: beta disintegration, radioactive decay|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider processes like nuclear fusion)|noun

beta-decay:/ˈbeɪtə dɪˈkeɪ/| v.|β衰变|Undergo beta decay; emit a beta particle from the nucleus.|Carbon-14 beta-decays with a half-life of 5,730 years. 碳-14以5730年的半衰期进行β衰变。|Synonyms: undergo beta disintegration|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider processes like nuclear fusion)|verb
