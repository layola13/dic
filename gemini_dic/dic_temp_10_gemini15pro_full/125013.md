mugwumpism:/ˈmʌɡˌwʌmpɪzəm/ | n. | 独立人士主义；骑墙派作风 | The attitude of a mugwump, especially neutrality or independent action in politics.  A person who remains aloof or independent, especially from party politics. |  The candidate's mugwumpism alienated both Democrats and Republicans.  这位候选人的独立人士主义疏远了民主党人和共和党人。 | Synonyms: neutrality, independence, aloofness | Antonyms: partisanship, factionalism | noun
