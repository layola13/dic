intercommunicate:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
verb
互相交流，互相沟通
To communicate with each other; exchange information or ideas.
The different departments need to intercommunicate more effectively.  不同的部门需要更有效地互相沟通。
The two computers can intercommunicate using a special cable.  这两台计算机可以使用专用电缆互相通讯。
近义词: communicate, interact, connect, network
反义词: isolate
词性: verb
