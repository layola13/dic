freckling:/ˈfrɛklɪŋ/ | n. | 雀斑，散布；点缀 | The act of becoming freckled or the state of being covered in freckles.  Small brownish spots on the skin caused by exposure to sunlight. |  She noticed a new freckling on her nose after a day at the beach.  她在海滩待了一天后注意到鼻子上出现了一块新的雀斑。 |  spotting, speckling |  clearing, blanching | noun

freckle:/ˈfrɛkl/ | n. v. | 雀斑；斑点；使生雀斑 | A small, brownish spot on the skin, typically caused by exposure to sunlight. To cover or become covered with freckles. | She has a few freckles across her nose. 她的鼻子上有一些雀斑。 |  spot, blemish, mole |  clear skin | noun, verb
