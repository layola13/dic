gerbil:/ˈdʒɜːrbɪl/| n.| 沙鼠；沙土鼠 |A small rodent with long hind legs, native to desert regions of Asia and Africa. It is often kept as a pet. | I bought a pair of gerbils for my daughter. 我给女儿买了一对沙鼠。|近义词：rodent, mouse, hamster|反义词: None|noun
