Etymology:
From non- + reverent.  "Reverent" comes from Old French *reverent (Modern French révérent), from Latin reverentem (nominative reverens), present participle of reverērī "to revere, stand in awe of," from re- "again, back" + verērī "to stand in awe of, fear, respect."
