charismatic:/ˌkærɪzˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 有魅力的；有感召力的 | Exercising a compelling charm which inspires devotion in others. |  He was a charismatic leader who inspired his followers. 他是一位富有魅力的领导者，激励着他的追随者。

She has a charismatic personality that draws people to her. 她具有超凡的魅力，吸引着人们靠近她。| 近义词：charming, magnetic, captivating, alluring | 反义词：uninspiring, dull, boring | 词性: adjective

charisma: /kəˈrɪzmə/ | n. |  魅力；感召力 | A compelling attractiveness or charm that can inspire devotion in others. | The candidate's charisma helped him win the election. 候选人的魅力帮助他赢得了选举。

The teacher's charisma kept the students engaged in the lesson. 老师的魅力使学生们积极参与课堂。| 近义词：charm, magnetism, allure, appeal | 反义词：dullness,  repulsiveness | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "khárisma" meaning "favor, divine gift,"  from "kharizesthai" meaning "to show favor," from "kháris" meaning "grace, favor."  The modern sense of personal magnetism developed in the early 20th century.
