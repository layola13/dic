enamellists:/ɪˈnæməlɪsts/
n.
珐琅匠；搪瓷匠；珐琅艺术家
Artists or craftspeople who work with enamel, creating decorative or artistic objects.
The enamellists of Limoges were famous for their intricate designs.  利摩日的珐琅匠以其复杂的设计而闻名。
The exhibition showcased the work of several contemporary enamellists.  展览展示了几位当代珐琅艺术家的作品。
近义词: enamelers
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of a destroyer of enamel work)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From enamel + -ist. Enamel itself comes from Old French *esmail (modern French émail), probably from a Frankish *smalt, ultimately from West Germanic *smeltan "to smelt."
