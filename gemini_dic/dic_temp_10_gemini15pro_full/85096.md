hardboards:/ˈhɑːrdbɔːrdz/
n.
硬纸板，纤维板
A stiff board made of compressed wood fibers.
The backs of some cheap furniture are made of hardboard.  一些廉价家具的背面是用硬纸板做的。
synonyms: fiberboard, pressed board, chipboard
antonyms: softwood, hardwood (these aren't strict opposites, but represent different materials)
noun
