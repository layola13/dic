lepidopter:/ˌlepɪˈdɒptər/ (US) /ˌlepɪˈdɒptə(r)/ (UK)| n. |鳞翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Lepidoptera, distinguished by having two pairs of large, typically brightly colored wings covered with minute overlapping scales.
