ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *abc*, from Old French *abece*, from Medieval Latin *abecedarium* ("alphabet"), ultimately a borrowing from the first four letters of the Phoenician alphabet: *ʾālep*, *bēt*, *gīmel*, *dālet*.
