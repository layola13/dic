estimableness:/ˈɛstɪməbəlnəs/| n.|可估量性；值得尊敬；可敬|The estimableness of a person's character is often judged by their actions.  一个人的品格是否值得尊敬通常由其行为来判断。|例句：Her estimableness made her a respected figure in the community. 她的可敬使她成为社区中受人尊敬的人物。

Her quiet dignity and estimableness shone through. 她安静的尊严和可敬闪耀着光芒。|近义词：worthiness, respectability, merit, value|反义词：unworthiness, disreputability|词性:noun


词源:
From estimable + -ness.  Estimable 源于 Late Middle English: from Old French estimable, from Latin aestimabilis, from aestimare ‘to estimate’.
