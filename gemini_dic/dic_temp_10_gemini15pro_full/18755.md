berkeleian:/ˌbɜːrkˈliːən/ | adj. n.| 贝克莱主义的，贝克莱主义者 | Relating to the philosophy of George Berkeley, especially his idealism.  A follower of George Berkeley's philosophy. |  His berkeleian arguments challenged the existence of matter. 他的贝克莱主义论点挑战了物质的存在。 | synonyms: idealist, immaterialist | antonyms: materialist, realist | adjective
berkeleian:/ˌbɜːrkˈliːən/ |  n.| 贝克莱主义者 | A follower of George Berkeley's philosophy. | He was a devout berkeleian, believing that only minds and ideas truly exist. 他是一位虔诚的贝克莱主义者，相信只有心灵和思想才是真正存在的。 | synonyms: idealist, immaterialist | antonyms: materialist, realist | noun
