incentive:/ɪnˈsɛntɪv/| n. |刺激；鼓励；动机|Something that motivates or encourages someone to do something.|例句：The government offers tax incentives for businesses to invest in renewable energy. 政府为企业投资可再生能源提供税收优惠。

例句：Bonus payments provide an incentive for employees to work harder. 奖金激励员工更加努力工作。|近义词：motivation, stimulus, encouragement, inducement|反义词：deterrent, discouragement|noun
