inattentiveness:/ˌɪnəˈtɛntɪvnəs/| n.| невнимательность; рассеянность; отсутствие внимания|The state of not paying attention; lack of attention.|His inattentiveness during the lecture resulted in a poor grade. 他上课不专心导致成绩很差。|近义词：distraction, heedlessness, negligence|反义词: attentiveness, mindfulness, concentration|词性:名词
Etymology: From in- (not) + attentive + -ness.  "Attentive" comes from Late Latin *attentīvus, from Latin attendere "to attend to," from ad- "to" + tendere "to stretch, extend."
