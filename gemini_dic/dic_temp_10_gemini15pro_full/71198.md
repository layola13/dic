fistularia:/ˌfɪstjʊˈlɛəriə/| n. |管口鱼；瘘管;|A slender, scaleless fish with a long tube-like snout.  一种细长的无鳞鱼，吻部呈长管状。|例句：The fistularia uses its long snout to suck up small crustaceans. 管口鱼用它长长的鼻子吸食小型甲壳类动物。

fistularia:/ˌfɪstjʊˈlɛəriə/| n. |瘘管;|An abnormal passage leading from an abscess or hollow organ to the body surface.  从脓肿或空心器官通向体表的异常通道。|例句：The surgeon had to operate to close the fistularia. 外科医生不得不进行手术来封闭瘘管。
