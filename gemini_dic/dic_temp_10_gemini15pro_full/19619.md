bigwig:/ˈbɪɡwɪɡ/| n.|大人物，要人，权贵|A person of importance or influence, especially in a particular sphere.|The company president and other bigwigs were at the meeting. 公司总裁和其他要员出席了会议。|近义词：VIP, notable, dignitary, high-up, heavyweight, leader, official, personage|反义词:nobody, underling|noun

词源:
The term "bigwig" originates from the 18th century when important men wore large, elaborate wigs as a symbol of status and power.  The larger the wig, the more important the person was perceived to be.
