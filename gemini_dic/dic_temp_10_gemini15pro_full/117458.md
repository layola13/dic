mediterranean:/ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən/| adj. n.|地中海的，地中海沿岸的；地中海；地中海气候|Relating to the Mediterranean Sea or the countries bordering it.  Characterized by warm, dry summers and mild, wet winters.  The Mediterranean Sea itself.|例句：The Mediterranean diet is considered very healthy. 地中海饮食被认为非常健康。

例句：We sailed across the Mediterranean. 我们航行了整个地中海。|近义词：midland, central|反义词：northern, arctic|词性:形容词, 名词

例句：The cruise ship toured the Mediterranean. 这艘游轮在地中海进行了巡游。|近义词：Mediterranean Sea|反义词：-|词性:名词
