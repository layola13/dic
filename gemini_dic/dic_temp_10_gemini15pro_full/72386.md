fluctuability:/ˌflʌktʃuːəˈbɪləti/
|n.|波动性；变异性|The quality of being likely to fluctuate.  The tendency of a characteristic to vary. |The fluctuability of the stock market makes investing risky. 股票市场的波动性使得投资有风险。|
|近义词：variability, instability, volatility|
|反义词：stability, constancy, steadiness|
|词性：noun|

fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/
|v.|波动；变动；涨落|To change continually; to rise and fall. |The price of gold fluctuates daily. 黄金价格每天都在波动。|
|近义词：vary, change, oscillate|
|反义词：stabilize, remain constant|
|词性：verb|
