clemency: /ˈklɛmənsi/ (US) /ˈklɛmənsi/ (UK)| n.|仁慈，宽恕，温和|Mildness or mercy, especially toward an offender or enemy; leniency.|The governor granted clemency to the prisoner sentenced to death.|近义词: mercy, leniency, compassion, forgiveness, pardon|反义词: severity, harshness, ruthlessness, cruelty, strictness|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle French *clemence* "mildness," from Latin *clementia* "mildness, gentleness," from *clemens* "mild, gentle."
