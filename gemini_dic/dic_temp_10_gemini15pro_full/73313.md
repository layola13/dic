foreclose:/fɔːrˈkloʊz/| v. |取消赎取权；排除；阻止|To take possession of a property from a mortgagor because of defaults on payments.|The bank foreclosed on their house after they missed several mortgage payments. 银行因他们多次未缴纳抵押贷款而取消了他们房屋的赎回权。|
Synonyms: repossess, seize, confiscate|Antonyms: allow, permit, grant|Verb|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *forclos, past participle of forclore “to shut out, exclude,” from fors “outside” (from Latin foris) + clore “to close” (from Latin claudere).
