digressing:/daɪˈɡrɛsɪŋ/
v. | 离题，跑题 | straying from the main subject; getting off topic |  The speaker kept digressing from the main point, making it hard to follow. 演讲者不断偏离主题，让人难以理解。 | Synonyms: deviating, rambling, straying | Antonyms: focusing, concentrating | verb


digress:/daɪˈɡrɛs/
v. | 离题，跑题 | to deviate or wander away from the main subject or purpose in speaking or writing |  He digressed from the topic to tell a personal anecdote. 他偏离主题，讲了一个个人轶事。 | Synonyms: deviate, ramble, stray | Antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb

Etymology: From Latin digressus, past participle of digredi "to go away, depart, digress," from di- "aside" + gradi "to walk, step."
