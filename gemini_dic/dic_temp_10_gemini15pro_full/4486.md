albiggeois:/ˌælbiʒɔɪˈwɑː/ /ˌælbiʒɔɪˈwɑː/ | n. / adj. | 阿尔比派教徒；阿尔比派的 | A person who belonged to a heretical Christian sect that flourished in southern France in the 12th and 13th centuries. Also used as an adjective to describe things related to this sect. | The Albigensian Crusade was a 20-year military campaign initiated by Pope Innocent III to eliminate the Cathar heresy in southern France.  阿尔比十字军东征是由教皇英诺森三世发起的一场为期20年的军事行动，旨在消除法国南部的卡特里异端。

The albiggeois beliefs were considered heretical by the Catholic Church. 阿尔比派的信仰被天主教会视为异端。 | 近义词：Cathar, heretic | 反义词：Catholic, orthodox | 词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Old French *albigeis, from Albi, a city in southern France, the center of the sect.  源自古法语 *albigeis，来自法国南部城市阿尔比，该教派的中心。
