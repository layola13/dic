nondeliberation:/ˌnɒndɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən/
n.
无深思熟虑，未加思考
The act or state of not deliberating; absence of deliberation.
Her nondeliberation led to a poor decision. 她未经深思熟虑就做出了一个糟糕的决定。
nonthinking, impulsiveness
deliberation, thoughtfulness
noun
