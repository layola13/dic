hamamelis:/ˌhæməˈmiːlɪs/ | n. |北美金缕梅;金缕梅属植物|A shrub or small tree of the witch hazel family, with fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The bark and leaves are used medicinally.|The hamamelis extract is used in skincare products.  金缕梅提取物用于护肤产品。|Synonyms: witch hazel|Antonyms: N/A|noun

hamamelis:/ˌhæməˈmiːlɪs/ | n. |金缕梅制剂|A medicinal preparation made from the bark and leaves of the hamamelis plant, typically used as an astringent.|I use hamamelis to soothe my skin after shaving. 我用金缕梅舒缓剃须后的皮肤。|Synonyms: witch hazel extract|Antonyms: N/A|noun
