overmild:/ˈoʊvərmaɪld/
adjective
过分温和的; 过于温和的
Excessively mild; not strong or severe enough.
The punishment was overmild considering the seriousness of the crime.  考虑到罪行的严重性，惩罚过于宽大了。
The winter has been overmild this year. 今年冬天过于暖和。
Synonyms: lenient, clement, indulgent, tolerant, permissive
Antonyms: harsh, severe, strict, stringent, rigorous
词性: 形容词
