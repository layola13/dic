bewley:/ˈbjuːli/ | n. | 茶馆，茶室 | A café or tea room. |  "Let's meet for tea at Bewley's."  让我们在Bewley's茶馆见面吧。 | synonym: tea room, café, coffee shop | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bar" or "pub" as a contrasting venue) | noun


Etymology:  Derived from the name of the Bewley family, founders of the famous Bewley's Oriental Cafés in Ireland.  The name has become synonymous with tea rooms in Ireland.  Ultimately, the surname Bewley is of Old English origin, from a place name meaning "beautiful meadow" or "beautiful clearing."
