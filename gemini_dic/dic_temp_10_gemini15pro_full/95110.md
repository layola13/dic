inaniloquous:/ˌɪnəˈnɪləkwəs/| adj.|空洞的，废话连篇的|Given to or characterized by  empty, idle, or senseless talk.  Characterized by speech that is empty, inane, or foolish.|His inaniloquous ramblings bored everyone in the room. 他空洞的漫谈使房间里的每个人都感到无聊。|The politician's inaniloquous speech offered no real solutions. 这位政客空洞无物的演讲没有提供任何真正的解决方案。|近义词：vapid, inane, senseless, foolish, empty, meaningless|反义词：meaningful, insightful, substantive, profound, concise|词性: adjective
