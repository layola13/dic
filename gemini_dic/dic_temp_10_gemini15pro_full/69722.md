feudality:/fjuːˈdælɪti/
n.
封建制度,封建主义
The social system that existed in Europe during the Middle Ages in which people received land and protection from a lord in exchange for working and fighting for him.
The history book described the  feudality of medieval Europe. 这本历史书描述了中世纪欧洲的封建制度。
近义词: feudalism, vassalage
反义词: democracy, republic
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feodalité*, from Medieval Latin *feodalitas*, from *feodum* ("fief").
