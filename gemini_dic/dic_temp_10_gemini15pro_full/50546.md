deputation:/ˌdɛpjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 代表团，派遣团 | A group of people appointed to represent others on a mission or at a conference. |  A deputation from the town went to Parliament to protest the new law. 该镇派出一个代表团到议会抗议新法律。| synonyms: delegation, mission, embassy | antonyms: individual, single person | noun

deputation:/ˌdɛpjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 委派，派遣 | The act of appointing a person or group of people to represent others. | The deputation of ambassadors to foreign countries is an important function of the government. 向外国派遣大使是政府的一项重要职能。| synonyms: assignment, appointment, commissioning | antonyms: recall, dismissal | noun
