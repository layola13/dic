bryozoa:/ˌbrɪəˈzoʊə/ | n. |苔藓虫, 苔藓动物|A phylum of aquatic invertebrate animals that form colonies. They are also known as moss animals or sea mats.| Bryozoa are often found encrusting rocks and seaweed in shallow waters. 苔藓虫经常在浅水区的岩石和海藻上结壳生长。|The bryozoan colony was attached to the underside of the boat.  苔藓虫群落附着在船底。|synonyms: moss animals, sea mats |antonyms: N/A |noun

bryozoan:/ˌbraɪəˈzoʊən/ | adj. n. |苔藓虫的, 苔藓动物的; 苔藓虫|Relating to or belonging to the phylum Bryozoa; a member of the phylum Bryozoa.|The bryozoan fossils were millions of years old. 这些苔藓虫化石有数百万年的历史。| Scientists discovered a new bryozoan species. 科学家发现了一种新的苔藓虫。|synonyms: moss animal, sea mat (when used as a noun) |antonyms: N/A |adjective, noun
