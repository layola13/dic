nosophen: /ˈnoʊsoʊfɛn/ | n. | 诺索芬（一种消毒剂） |  A disinfectant and antiseptic.  It's a combination of noso- (disease) + -phen (phenol, a common antiseptic). |  Nosophen was used to sterilize surgical instruments.  诺索芬曾用于消毒手术器械。 |  Synonym:  disinfectant, antiseptic, germicide | Antonym: contaminant | Noun
