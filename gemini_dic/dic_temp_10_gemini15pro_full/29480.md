cataphora:/kəˈtæfərə/ | n. |  前指 | The use of a word or phrase to refer ahead to another word or phrase in a sentence or text. |  After he bought it, John took the book home. (Here, "it" refers to "the book" which comes later in the sentence.) |  anaphor, antecedent |  - | noun
