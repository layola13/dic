bascule:/ˈbæskjuːl/ (US), /bæsˈkjuːl/ (UK)| n. | basculing bridge, tipping device | A type of bridge that pivots upwards to allow boats or other tall vessels to pass underneath. Also, any device that operates on a similar pivoting principle.|  The Tower Bridge in London uses a bascule mechanism to open and close.  伦敦塔桥使用一个翘板机构来打开和关闭。| synonym: drawbridge, lifting bridge, tilt bridge | antonym: fixed bridge | noun

bascule:/ˈbæskjuːl/ (US), /bæsˈkjuːl/ (UK)| v. | tilt, tip | To tilt or tip something, especially a bascule bridge. | The bridge operator basculed the span to allow the ship to pass.  桥梁操作员倾斜桥面以允许船只通过。| synonym:  raise, lift, tilt, pivot | antonym: lower, drop | verb
