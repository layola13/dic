alquifou: /ˈælkɪfuː/
noun
炼金家用具; 代指炼金术; (比喻)万能药
A vessel or apparatus used in alchemy; also, alchemy itself; figuratively, a panacea.
The alchemist searched for the perfect alquifou to transmute lead into gold.  炼金术士寻找完美的炼金用具，将铅变成金子。
The search for an alquifou to cure all ills has been a human endeavor for centuries.  几个世纪以来，寻找一种能治愈所有疾病的万能药一直是人类的努力方向。
Synonyms: alembic, cucurbit, retort, panacea, elixir
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but one could consider "poison" or "toxin" as contextual antonyms depending on the usage)
