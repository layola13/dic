catapulting:/ˈkætəˌpʌltɪŋ/
verb
投射，发射，推动
The novel catapulted her to fame. 这部小说使她一举成名。
The explosion catapulted the car across the street. 爆炸把汽车抛到街对面。
Synonyms: launching, projecting, propelling, thrusting, hurling
Antonyms: dropping, falling, lowering, descending
