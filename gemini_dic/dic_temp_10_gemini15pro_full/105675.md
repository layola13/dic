kings:/kɪŋz/
n.
国王们；君主们
Plural form of "king," meaning rulers of a kingdom or empire.
The kings of Europe met to discuss the treaty. 欧洲的国王们会面讨论条约。
近义词：monarchs, sovereigns, rulers
反义词：subjects, citizens, commoners
词性: 名词
