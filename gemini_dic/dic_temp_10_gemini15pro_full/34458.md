claviculate: /kləˈvɪkjʊleɪt/ /kləˈvɪkjʊlət/
adjective
锁骨状的；有锁骨的；似锁骨的
Having or resembling a clavicle; shaped like a small key.
The bird has a claviculate bone structure.  这只鸟有锁骨状的骨骼结构。
Synonyms: clavicular
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective


claviculate: /kləˈvɪkjʊleɪt/ /kləˈvɪkjʊlət/
verb
使成锁骨状
To form or shape like a clavicle.
The sculptor claviculated the figure's shoulders.  雕塑家把人物的肩膀塑造成锁骨状。
Synonyms: None
