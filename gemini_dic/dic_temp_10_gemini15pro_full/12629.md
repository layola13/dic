assassinous: /əˈsæsɪnəs/ | adj. | 凶残的，刺客般的 | Characterized by treachery and murder; having the qualities of an assassin; deadly. |  He gave me an assassinous look. 他给了我一个凶狠的眼神。|  The assassinous plot was foiled by the police.  警方挫败了这起暗杀阴谋。| 近义词: murderous, homicidal, deadly, lethal | 反义词: merciful, compassionate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French *assassinous, from assassin. 最初来自阿拉伯语 hashīshīn ‘hashish-eater,’ referring to a fanatical Muslim sect in Syria and Persia (11th–13th centuries) whose members were allegedly sent out on assassination missions.
