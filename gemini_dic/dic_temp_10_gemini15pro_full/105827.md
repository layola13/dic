kit-out:/ˈkɪtˌaʊt/
noun
装备; 全套装备
a set of clothes or equipment needed for a particular activity or purpose
The climbers had a full kit-out for the expedition. 登山者为这次探险准备了全套装备。
outfit, equipment, gear
lack of equipment
n.
