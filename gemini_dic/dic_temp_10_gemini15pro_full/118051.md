menaces:/ˈmɛnɪsɪz/ | n. | 威胁，恐吓 | A statement or action that indicates an intention to inflict harm or damage. |  The menacing clouds suggested a storm was coming. 乌云密布预示着暴风雨即将来临。

The gang's presence was a menace to the neighborhood.  该团伙的存在对社区构成威胁。 | 近义词：threat, intimidation, danger, peril | 反义词：safety, security | 词性: 名词 (plural of menace)


menace:/ˈmɛnɪs/ | n. | 威胁，危险人物，讨厌的人或物 | something that is likely to cause harm; a dangerous or troublesome person or thing | The hurricane is a menace to the coastal towns. 飓风对沿海城镇构成威胁。 | 近义词：threat, danger, peril, hazard | 反义词：safety, security, safeguard | 词性: 名词

menace:/ˈmɛnɪs/ | vt. | 威胁，恐吓 | To threaten, especially in a vague or indirect way. |  The rising floodwaters menaced the city. 上涨的洪水威胁着这座城市。 | 近义词：threaten, intimidate, endanger, jeopardize | 反义词：protect, safeguard | 词性: 动词


**词源:**

From Old French *manace, menace* "threat," from Late Latin *minacia* "a threat," from Latin *minax* "threatening," from *minari* "to threaten."
