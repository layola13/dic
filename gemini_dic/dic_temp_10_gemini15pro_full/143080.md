outbrawl: /ˌaʊtˈbrɔːl/
verb
大声叫嚷胜过; 在争吵中胜过
to shout louder than someone else in a quarrel or fight, or to defeat someone in a quarrel or fight
He outbrawled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以更大的嗓门压倒了对手。
Synonyms: outshout, outargue, defeat
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
