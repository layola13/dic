overclog:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːɡ/ | v. | 堵塞；阻塞 | To clog or obstruct excessively. | The pipes were overclogged with hair. 管道被头发堵塞了。 | The narrow roads were overclogged with traffic. 狭窄的道路被车辆堵得水泄不通。 | 近义词: block, obstruct, congest | 反义词: clear, unclog | 词性: 动词

overclogs:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːɡz/ | n. | 阻塞物 | Something that overclogs.  (Rarely used as a noun) | The overclogs in the drain caused a flood. 下水道里的阻塞物导致了洪水。(Rarely used) |  The mechanic removed the overclogs from the engine. 机械师清除了引擎中的阻塞物。(Rarely used) | 近义词: blockage, obstruction | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词 (Rare)
