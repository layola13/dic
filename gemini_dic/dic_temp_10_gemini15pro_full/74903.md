freshment: /ˈfreʃmənt/ | n. | 茶点，点心；清爽，新鲜感 |  The provision of refreshments will be much appreciated. 提供茶点将不胜感激。 |例句：We stopped for refreshments at a roadside cafe. 我们在路边咖啡馆停下来吃点心。| 近义词：snack, beverage, drink, food, refreshment, revitalization | 反义词: staleness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *fresshement, *freschement, from Anglo-Norman freſchement, freſchement, from Old French refreschement, refreschement, from refreschier, refreschir (“to refresh”).
