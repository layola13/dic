indignify: /ɪnˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/
verb
使愤慨；激怒
to make (someone) feel angry and disrespected
例句：The unfair treatment indignified the workers. 不公平的待遇激怒了工人。

例句：She was indignified by the rude remarks.  她被粗鲁的言论激怒了。

近义词：offend, insult, affront, provoke, anger, enrage
反义词：honor, respect, please, gratify
词性：v.
