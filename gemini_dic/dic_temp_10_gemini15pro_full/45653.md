curt:/kɜːrt/ (British) /kɝːt/ (American)| adj.|简短无礼的，唐突的|Rudely brief in speech or manner.|Example: He gave a curt reply and turned away.  他简短无礼地回答后就转身离开了。

Example:  Her curt tone made it clear she was annoyed. 她唐突的语气表明她很生气。


|近义词：abrupt, brusque, terse, short, blunt|反义词:polite, courteous, lengthy, expansive|词性:形容词
