cliques:/klɪks/ /kliːks/| n. |小圈子，派系|A small, exclusive group of people who tend to socialize only with each other and are often unfriendly towards outsiders.|例句：High school is full of cliques. 高中里有很多小圈子。

例句：It's hard to break into that clique. 很难打入那个小圈子。

近义词：groups, circles, gangs, factions|反义词:individuals, loners|词性:noun
