asymptotic:/ˌæsɪmˈtɒtɪk/ (US) /ˌeɪsɪmˈtɑːtɪk/| adj.|渐近的，渐近线的|Approaching a value or curve arbitrarily closely but never reaching it.|The graph of the function is asymptotic to the x-axis.  函数的图像与x轴渐近。|近义词:approaching, converging|反义词:diverging|词性:形容词
asymptote:/ˈæsɪmptəʊt/ (US) /ˈæsɪmptoʊt/| n.|渐近线|A line that a curve approaches, as it heads towards infinity, but never reaches.|The x-axis is an asymptote to the curve. x轴是这条曲线的渐近线。|近义词:limit|反义词:None|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *asymptōtos*, from *a-* (not) + *syn-* (together) + *ptōtós* (fallen).  Meaning "not falling together," referring to a line that doesn't intersect a curve.
