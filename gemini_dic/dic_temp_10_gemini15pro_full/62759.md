engraff:/ɪnˈɡræf/ /ɛnˈɡræf/ | vt. n.|嫁接；接枝；移植；(比喻)灌输|To insert or implant (a shoot or scion) as a graft.  To implant or fix deeply (as in the mind).|例句：
The gardener will engraff the apple tree with a new variety. 园丁将给苹果树嫁接一个新品种。

He tried to engraff his beliefs onto his children. 他试图将他的信仰灌输给他的孩子们。

|近义词：graft, implant, insert, inculcate|反义词:excise, remove|verb
engraff:/ɪnˈɡræf/ /ɛnˈɡræf/ | n.|嫁接；接枝；移植|The act or process of engrafting. A grafted plant.|例句：
The engraff took successfully, and the new branch began to grow. 嫁接成功了，新枝开始生长。

The farmer inspected the engraffs he had made earlier in the season.  农民检查了他在季节早期做的嫁接。

|近义词：graft, implant|反义词: - |noun
