anglicization:/ˌæŋɡlɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 英文化；英语化 | The process of making something more English in character or form. | The anglicization of Irish names began during the Tudor conquest.  爱尔兰名字的英语化始于都铎王朝的征服时期。

The restaurant's name underwent anglicization to appeal to a wider audience.  这家餐厅的名字经过英语化以吸引更广泛的顾客。 | synonyms: Anglicisation, englishing | antonyms: localization, vernacularization | noun

anglicize:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪz/ | v. | 使英语化；使英国化 | To make something more English in character or form. | Many Irish people anglicized their names during British rule. 许多爱尔兰人在英国统治时期将他们的名字英语化了。

They anglicized the spelling of the town's name. 他们把城镇名称的拼写英语化了。 | synonyms: Anglicise, english | antonyms: localize, vernacularize | verb
