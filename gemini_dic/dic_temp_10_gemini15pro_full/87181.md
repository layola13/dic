herdic: /ˈhɜːrdɪk/ | n. |  赫迪克式马车（一种早期出租马车） | A horse-drawn carriage that operated as a public conveyance, especially in Boston in the late 19th century.  It resembled a small omnibus with seats along the sides facing inward. | Example 1:  He hailed a herdic to take him to the theater. 他叫了一辆赫迪克式马车送他去剧院。

Example 2: Herdics were a common sight on the streets of Boston in the 1880s. 19 世纪 80 年代，赫迪克式马车在波士顿街头随处可见。 | 近义词: carriage, cab, hack | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps private carriage) | 词性: noun
