antischolasticism:/ˌæntiˌskɒˈlæstɪsɪzəm/| n.|反学院主义，反经院哲学|Opposition to traditional scholastic methods or philosophy.  A movement that emphasized practical experience and observation over the study of traditional authorities.|The rise of antischolasticism during the Renaissance led to new discoveries in science and art.  文艺复兴时期反学院主义的兴起导致了科学和艺术的新发现.|Synonyms:  anti-intellectualism, empiricism|Antonyms: scholasticism, intellectualism|noun
