gesse:/ˈdʒɛs/| n.|野豌豆，巢菜|A plant of the pea family, with tendrils and purple or white flowers, several kinds of which are grown as fodder crops.  A common name for several species of Lathyrus, especially Lathyrus sativus.|The field was sown with gesse.|近义词：vetch, chickling pea|反义词:|noun
