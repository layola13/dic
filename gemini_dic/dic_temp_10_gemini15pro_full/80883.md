governance:/ˈɡʌvərnəns/ | n. |治理；管理；统治|The process of making decisions and the capacity of government in a society to carry out its functions effectively.  |例句：The country's governance is improving.  这个国家的治理正在改善。|近义词：administration, management, rule |反义词:misrule, anarchy |noun

governance:/ˈɡʌvənəns/ | n. |支配；统治权|The act or manner of governing. | 例句：Corporate governance is crucial for investor confidence. 公司治理对投资者信心至关重要。| 近义词：control, authority, command | 反义词:submission, subservience |noun
