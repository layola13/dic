nonviscidities:/ˌnɒnvɪsˈkɒsɪtiz/
noun
无粘性；非粘性
The lack of viscosity or stickiness of a substance or substances.  Specifically referring to multiple instances of this property.
The nonviscidities of these new lubricants are remarkable, allowing for smoother operation of machinery. 这些新型润滑剂的无粘性非常出色，可以使机器运转更顺畅。
Near synonyms: fluidity, slipperiness
Antonyms: viscosities, stickiness, tackiness
n.
