hydroplaner:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪnər/ | n. |水上飞机; 水上滑板|A fast motorboat designed to skim over the surface of the water.|The hydroplaner zipped across the lake, leaving a white wake trailing behind it. 水上飞机快速地掠过湖面，留下一道白色的尾迹。|Synonyms: hydrofoil, speedboat, planing boat|Antonyms: submarine, submersible|noun

hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪn/ | v. |水上滑行|To skim over the surface of the water.|The speedboat hydroplaned across the waves. 快艇在波浪上滑行。|Synonyms: skim, plane, aquaplane|Antonyms: sink, submerge|verb

hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪn/ | n. |水上飞机; 水上滑板; 浮舟; 水翼|A fast motorboat designed to skim over the surface of the water; a seaplane.|He bought a new hydroplane. 他买了一艘新的水上飞机。|Synonyms: hydrofoil, speedboat, planing boat, seaplane|Antonyms: submarine, submersible|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + plane (to soar or glide).  Ultimately from Greek *húdōr (water) and Latin planus (flat).
