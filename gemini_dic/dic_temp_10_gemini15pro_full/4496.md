albinoism:/ˈælbɪˌnɪzəm/
n.|白化病|A genetic condition characterized by a deficiency or the absence of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.|People with albinism are more susceptible to sunburn and skin cancer.|近义词：achromasia, achromatosis|反义词：(None -  Albinism is a medical condition, not a descriptive adjective with a direct opposite)|词性:名词
