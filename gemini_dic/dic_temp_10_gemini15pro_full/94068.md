impassionate:/ɪmˈpæʃənət/| adj.|冷静的，不动感情的，没有热情的|Not feeling or showing emotion; dispassionate.|Her impassionate response to the news surprised everyone. 她对这个消息无动于衷的反应让每个人都很惊讶。|例句：He delivered an impassionate speech about the importance of education. 他发表了一场关于教育重要性的充满热情的演讲。

例句：She remained impassionate throughout the trial, never showing any emotion. 在整个审判过程中，她始终保持冷静，没有流露出任何情绪。|近义词：unemotional, apathetic, indifferent, stoic, detached|反义词：passionate, emotional, ardent, fervent, zealous|词性:形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from in- (expressing negation) + passionate.  The original sense was ‘free from passion (in the now archaic sense ‘suffering’),’ later ‘not subject to or easily moved by passion,’ hence ‘calm, impartial.’
