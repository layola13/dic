lovey-doveyness:/ˈlʌvi ˈdʌvinəs/| n.|卿卿我我；柔情蜜意；|A state of excessive affection and sentimentality between two people, often to the point of being nauseating to others.|例句：The lovey-doveyness of the honeymooners was obvious to everyone.  蜜月旅行者之间的卿卿我我是显而易见的。

例句：Their constant lovey-doveyness was a bit much for their friends. 他们不断的卿卿我我让他们的朋友有点受不了。|近义词：affection, tenderness, intimacy, romance|反义词：animosity, hostility, coldness, indifference|词性:名词

lovey-dovey:/ˈlʌvi ˈdʌvi/| adj.|卿卿我我的；柔情蜜意的；|Excessively affectionate and sentimental.|例句：They were being very lovey-dovey at the party. 他们在派对上卿卿我我。

例句：The lovey-dovey couple held hands throughout the movie. 这对卿卿我我的情侣在整部电影中都手牵着手。|近义词：affectionate, loving, romantic, sentimental|反义词：cold, distant, hostile, indifferent|词性:形容词
