glomuliferous:/ɡlɒˌmjuːˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
聚集的；多肾小球的
Bearing or producing glomeruli.  Characterized by the presence of glomeruli.
例句：The glomuliferous part of the kidney is essential for filtration.  肾脏中聚集肾小球的部分对于过滤至关重要。

例句：Glomuliferous plants are distinguished by their dense clusters of flowers.  多肾小球的植物以其密集的花簇为特征。
近义词：glomerular, clustered
反义词:  diffuse, scattered
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *glomerulus (diminutive of glomus "ball") + -ferous "bearing."
