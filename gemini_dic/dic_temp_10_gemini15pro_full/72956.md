food-cycle: /ˈfuːd ˌsaɪkəl/ | n. | 食物循环，食物链 | The natural process of food production, consumption, and decomposition, often with an emphasis on sustainability and reducing waste.  It encompasses the stages involved in growing, processing, distributing, consuming, and disposing of food, and the impact these stages have on the environment. | The restaurant composts food waste to complete the food-cycle. 这家餐厅将食物残渣堆肥，以完成食物循环。 | synonyms: food chain, food system, food web | antonyms: linear food system | noun
