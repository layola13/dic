nonsalvageable:/ˌnɒnˈsælvɪdʒəbl/
|adj.|
|不可挽救的，无法抢救的|
|Something that is nonsalvageable is so badly damaged that it cannot be repaired or saved.|
|The fire destroyed the building, rendering it completely nonsalvageable.|大火摧毁了这栋建筑，使它完全无法挽救。

|The car was so badly damaged in the crash that it was declared nonsalvageable.|这辆车在事故中损毁严重，被宣布无法修复。
|
|Synonym: irreparable, unrecoverable, ruined, beyond repair|
|Antonym:  salvageable, repairable, recoverable|
|word type: adjective|
