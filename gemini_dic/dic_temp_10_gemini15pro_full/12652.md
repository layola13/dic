assegai:/ˈæsɪˌɡaɪ/ /ˈæsɪɡaɪ/
n.
轻矛，投枪
A slender, iron-tipped spear, usually thrown by hand, used chiefly by Southern African peoples.
The Zulu warrior hurled his assegai with deadly accuracy.  祖鲁武士以惊人的准确度投掷他的轻矛。

近义词:spear, javelin, lance
反义词:(No direct antonym for a weapon.  Perhaps "shield" as a defensive counter.)
词性:noun
