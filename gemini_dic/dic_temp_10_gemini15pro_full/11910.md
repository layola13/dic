arrows:/ˈæroʊz/ (US), /ˈærəʊz/ (UK)| n.|箭；箭头；箭状物|A thin, straight stick with a sharp point at one end and feathers at the other, shot from a bow.  Also, a symbol or mark resembling an arrow, used to indicate direction or position.|The archer shot three arrows at the target.  弓箭手向目标射了三箭。|synonyms: darts, shafts, missiles|antonyms: shield, target|noun
