haemadromograph:/ˌhiːməˈdrɒməɡrɑːf/ /ˌhiːməˈdrɑːməɡræf/
noun
血流速度描记器
An instrument for recording the velocity of the blood current.
The haemadromograph measured a significant increase in blood flow after exercise. 运动后，血流速度描记器测量到血流量显著增加。
近义词:rheograph, blood flow meter
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a measurement device)
词性:n.

haem-/hem- (Greek): blood
-dromo (Greek): running, course
-graph (Greek): writing, recording
