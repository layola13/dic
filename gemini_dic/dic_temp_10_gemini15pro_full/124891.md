muddled:/ˈmʌdld/ (US), /ˈmʌdld/ (UK)| adj.|困惑的，糊涂的，混乱的|Being in a state of confusion; mixed up.|例句：He felt completely muddled after the long and complicated lecture.  他听了那场冗长而复杂的讲座后感到完全糊涂了。

例句：The instructions were so muddled that no one knew what to do.  说明书太混乱了，没有人知道该怎么做。|近义词：confused, bewildered, disoriented, unclear, messy|反义词：clear, lucid, organized, coherent|词性:形容词
