beelining:/ˈbiːlaɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
直奔，径直前往
Going directly and quickly towards something.
He beelined for the exit as soon as the meeting was over. 他会议一结束就直奔出口。
She beelined straight for the cake table. 她径直走向蛋糕桌。
近义词：heading straight for, making a beeline for, rushing towards
反义词：meandering, wandering, strolling
词性：v.

beelining:/ˈbiːlaɪnɪŋ/
noun
直奔，径直前往
The act of going directly and quickly towards something.
His beelining for the door made everyone curious. 他直奔门口的行为让每个人都很好奇。
Her beelining to the bar indicated she needed a drink. 她直奔酒吧表明她需要一杯饮料。
近义词：direct route, straight path
反义词：indirect route, detour
词性：n.


词源:  From "bee line," referring to the supposed straight flight of a bee returning to its hive. 源自“bee line”（蜂线），指的是蜜蜂返回蜂巢时假想的直线飞行。
