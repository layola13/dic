earliest:/ˈɜːrliɪst/| adj. |最早的|Happening or done before the usual or expected time; first.|例句：The earliest train leaves at 6 a.m. 最早的火车早上6点出发。|例句：We need to get the earliest possible flight. 我们需要乘坐最早的航班。|近义词：first, soonest, initial|反义词：latest, last|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *ǣrlicost, superlative of *ǣrlic (“early”), from *ǣr (“before, prior, former, early”) + -lic (“-ly”).  The root *ǣr is related to Old Frisian ēr (“before, formerly”), Old Saxon ēr (“before, formerly, early”), Old High German ēr (“before, formerly”), Old Norse ār (“early”), Gothic 𐌰𐌹𐍂 (air, “before, formerly”).
