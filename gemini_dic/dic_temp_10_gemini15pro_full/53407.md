disambiguating:/dɪsæmˈbɪɡjueɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.| 消歧的，消除歧义的| The process of identifying the intended meaning of a word or phrase that has multiple possible meanings.  This often involves considering the context in which the word or phrase is used.|  He is disambiguating the meaning of the word "bank." 他正在消除“bank”一词的歧义。 |  clarifying, specifying, distinguishing | confusing, obscuring | verb/adjective

disambiguate:/dɪsæmˈbɪɡjueɪt/| vt.| 消除歧义，澄清| To remove uncertainty or ambiguity from the meaning of something.|  The author used footnotes to disambiguate the archaic terms. 作者使用脚注来阐明古代术语。| clarify, specify, distinguish | confuse, obscure | verb


Etymology:
dis- (Latin) -  apart, asunder, away, utterly, completely.  Imparts a negative or reversing force.
ambiguus (Latin) - doubtful, uncertain.
-ate (suffix) - forms verbs from Latin adjectives.
-ing (suffix) - creates present participles and gerunds.
