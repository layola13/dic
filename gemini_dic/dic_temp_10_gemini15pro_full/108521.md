layship:/ˈleɪʃɪp/
noun
临时解雇;停工期
A period of temporary unemployment from one's usual work, especially in the shipping industry.
The shipyard workers faced a layship due to the lack of new orders.  由于缺乏新订单，造船厂的工人面临着停工。
furlough, layoff, redundancy
employment, work
n.
