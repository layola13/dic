meows:/miˈaʊz/| n. v.|喵喵叫声|The sound a cat makes. Also used as a verb, meaning "to make the sound of a cat."|The cat meows loudly when it's hungry. 猫饿的时候会大声喵喵叫。|近义词：purrs, caterwauls|反义词: (No true opposite, perhaps silence or barks)|词性: noun, verb
