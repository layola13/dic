bespattering:/bɪˈspætərɪŋ/
verb
溅污，泼溅
The action of splashing small drops of liquid or mud onto something.
The car was bespattering mud on the pedestrians as it sped through the puddle. 汽车飞快地穿过水坑，把泥溅到行人身上。
splashing, splattering, spotting, spraying
