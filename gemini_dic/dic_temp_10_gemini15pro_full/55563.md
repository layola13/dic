distune:/ˌdɪsˈtjuːn/ /ˌdɪsˈtuːn/
verb
使走调；扰乱；使失谐
to put out of tune; to upset or disturb
The loud music from next door distuned the radio reception. 隔壁传来的大声音乐干扰了收音机的接收。
近义词:unsettle, disturb, upset
反义词:tune, harmonize
词性:v.


distune:/ˌdɪsˈtjuːn/ /ˌdɪsˈtuːn/
adjective
走调的;失谐的
out of tune; discordant
The piano sounded distuned. 钢琴听起来走调了。
近义词:out of tune, discordant
反义词:tuned, harmonious
词性:adj.
