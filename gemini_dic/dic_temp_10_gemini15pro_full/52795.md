diligent:/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/ | adj.|勤奋的，勤勉的|Hardworking, conscientious, and persistent.|He is a diligent student who always completes his assignments on time.  他是一个勤奋的学生，总是按时完成作业。|近义词：industrious, assiduous, hardworking|反义词：lazy, negligent, slothful|词性:形容词
