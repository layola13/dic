depolished:/diːˈpɒlɪʃt/
adj.
失去光泽的，磨砂的
Having had the polish removed; matte.
The depolished granite countertop gave the kitchen a rustic look.  磨砂的花岗岩台面赋予厨房一种乡村气息。
近义词:matte, frosted, unpolished
反义词:polished, glossy, shiny
词性:形容词

depolish:/diːˈpɒlɪʃ/
vt.
去除光泽，使失去光泽
To remove the polish from.
The jeweler will depolish the ring before resetting the stone.  珠宝商会在重新镶嵌宝石之前去除戒指的光泽。
近义词:dull, tarnish
反义词:polish, burnish
词性:动词
