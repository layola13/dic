imperceptive:/ˌɪmpərˈseptɪv/| adj.|无感觉的，未察觉的，感觉迟钝的|Not perceptive; not able to perceive readily or fully. Failing to notice something.|The subtle changes in the painting were imperceptible to the untrained eye. 这幅画中细微的变化，没有受过训练的人是看不出来的。|Synonyms: unperceptive, unobservant, inattentive, oblivious, insensitive, unaware, numb, dull|Antonyms: perceptive, observant, attentive, aware, sensitive, keen|Word Origin: Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + perceptive|
imperceptible:/ˌɪmpərˈseptɪbl/| adj.|觉察不出的，难以察觉的，极细微的|So slight, gradual, or subtle as not to be perceived.|The improvement in his health was almost imperceptible. 他的健康状况略有好转，几乎难以察觉。|Synonyms: unnoticeable, undetectable, indistinguishable, invisible, inaudible|Antonyms: perceptible, noticeable, detectable, obvious, clear|Word Origin:  Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + perceptible|
