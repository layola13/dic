doctorize: /ˈdɒktəraɪz/ (British) /ˈdɑːktəraɪz/ (American)
|v.|使医学化；用医学术语表达；像医生一样对待；过分谨慎地对待|To treat something in a medical way, often excessively or unnecessarily; to make something seem like a medical issue; to explain something using medical jargon.|He tends to doctorize every little ache and pain. 他总是把每一点疼痛都当成病来看待。|overmedicalize, medicalize|Underestimate (perhaps, in some contexts)|verb
doctorize: /ˈdɒktəraɪz/ (British) /ˈdɑːktəraɪz/ (American)
|n.|医学化；用医学术语表达；像医生一样对待的行为|The act of doctorizing something.|The doctorization of everyday life can be detrimental. 日常生活的医学化可能是有害的。|medicalization, overmedicalization|Underestimation (perhaps, in some contexts)|noun
