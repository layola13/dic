biophysicist:/ˌbaɪ.oʊˈfɪz.ɪ.sɪst/| n. |生物物理学家|A scientist who studies the physics of living organisms and biological processes.|例句：The biophysicist used advanced imaging techniques to study cell behavior.  这位生物物理学家使用先进的成像技术来研究细胞行为。

例句：She's a renowned biophysicist specializing in protein folding. 她是一位著名的生物物理学家，专攻蛋白质折叠。|近义词：biological physicist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- + physicist
bio-: from Greek bios "life"
physicist: from physics + -ist
physics: from Greek physikē (epistēmē) "(knowledge) of nature"
