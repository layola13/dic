intercentral:/ˌɪntərˈsɛntrəl/
intercentral| adj.|中心间的，相互关联的|Located between or connecting different centers. Referring to communication or interaction occurring between different centers or central points.|The intercentral nervous system allows for rapid communication between different parts of the body.|近义词：interconnected, interlinked, networked|反义词:isolated, separate|词性:形容词
