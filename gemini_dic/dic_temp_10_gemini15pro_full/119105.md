metrologist:/ˌmiːtrɒlədʒɪst/ | /ˌmiːtrɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. |计量学家；度量衡学家| A specialist in metrology, the science of measurement. | The metrologist calibrated the instruments to ensure accurate readings. 计量学家校准了仪器以确保读数准确。|  Example: The metrologist carefully measured the dimensions of the artifact. 这位计量学家仔细测量了文物的尺寸。| Synonyms: measurement specialist, calibration expert | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
