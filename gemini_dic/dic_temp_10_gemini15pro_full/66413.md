executivenesses:/ɪɡˌzɛkjʊtɪvnəsəz/| n. |执行力，执行能力（复数）|The quality or state of being effective in carrying out plans or decisions; the ability to get things done.|The company's success is largely due to the executivenesses of its leadership team. 该公司的成功很大程度上归功于其领导团队的执行力。|近义词：effectiveness, efficiency, competence, capability|反义词：ineffectiveness, inefficiency, incompetence, inability|词性:名词

词源: From executive + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Executive" ultimately derives from Latin *exsequī*  meaning "to follow out, carry out".
