oatmeal: /ˈoʊtmˌil/ (US) /ˈəʊtmˌiːl/ (UK)| n. |燕麦片；燕麦粥|A meal made from ground oats, typically boiled with water or milk and served hot, often with added sugar, fruit, etc.|例句：I had a bowl of oatmeal for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一碗燕麦片。

例句：Oatmeal is a healthy and filling breakfast option. 燕麦片是一种健康且饱腹的早餐选择。

近义词：porridge, rolled oats|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider contrasting foods like) bacon, eggs |词性: noun
