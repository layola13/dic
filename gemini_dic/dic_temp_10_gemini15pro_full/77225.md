noun
胃炎（复数）
Inflammation of the lining of the stomach.  This plural form is less common than "gastritis."
Example: Chronic gastritises can lead to more serious complications.  慢性胃炎会导致更严重的并发症。
Example:  The doctor diagnosed multiple gastritises in the patient. 医生诊断出该患者患有多种胃炎。
Synonym: inflammations of the stomach (more formal and less common)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) healthy stomach lining, absence of inflammation
词性: noun
