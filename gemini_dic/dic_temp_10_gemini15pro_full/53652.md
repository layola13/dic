discharger's:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒərz/
noun
排放者（的）
belonging to or associated with a discharger (someone or something that discharges something).
The discharger's responsibility is to ensure proper waste disposal. 排放者的责任是确保妥善处理废物。
The discharger's permit was revoked due to repeated violations. 由于屡次违规，排放者的许可证被吊销了。
Synonyms: emitter's, releaser's
Antonyms: retainer's, holder's
