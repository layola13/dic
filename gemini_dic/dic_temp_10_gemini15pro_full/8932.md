antihierarchically:/ˌæntiˌhaɪərˈɑːrkɪkli/ | adv. | 反等级地；非等级地 | In a manner that opposes or rejects hierarchy, promoting equality and shared power. | The community was organized antihierarchically, with decisions made collectively.  这个社区的组织是非等级制的，决策是集体做出的。 | 近义词: egalitarianly, horizontally, collaboratively | 反义词: hierarchically, vertically, autocratically | 词性: 副词

antihierarchical:/ˌæntiˌhaɪərˈɑːrkɪkəl/ | adj. | 反等级的；非等级的 | Opposing or rejecting hierarchy; promoting equality and shared power. |  They adopted an antihierarchical approach to management. 他们采用了一种反等级的管理方法。| 近义词: egalitarian, horizontal, collaborative | 反义词: hierarchical, vertical, autocratic | 词性: 形容词

词源: "anti-" (against) + "hierarchical" (arranged in order of rank). "Hierarchical" comes from the Greek "hierarchia" (sacred rule).
