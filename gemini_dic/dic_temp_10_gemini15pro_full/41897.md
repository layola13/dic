cosines:/ˈkoʊsaɪnz/| n. |余弦|The plural form of cosine, which is a trigonometric function that represents the ratio of the adjacent side to the hypotenuse in a right-angled triangle.|The cosines of the angles in a triangle are important for calculating its area.|近义词: trigonometric ratios|反义词: (No direct antonym for a mathematical function)|词性:noun
