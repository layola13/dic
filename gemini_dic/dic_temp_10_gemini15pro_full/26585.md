cabriole:/ˈkæbriˌoʊl/ (US), /ˈkæbriəʊl/ (UK)| n. |跳跃，腾跃 (芭蕾舞)；(家具)弯腿 |A ballet leap in which the extended legs are beaten together in the air. A furniture leg that curves outward and then tapers inward just above the foot.|The dancer executed a perfect cabriole. 舞蹈演员完成了一个完美的跳跃。|The antique table had elegantly carved cabrioles. 这张古董桌有雕刻精美的弯腿。|近义词：leap, jump, bound (for the ballet move); curved leg, Queen Anne leg (for the furniture leg)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun

cabriole:/ˈkæbriˌoʊl/ (US), /ˈkæbriəʊl/ (UK)| v. |跳跃，腾跃|To perform a cabriole.|The horse cabrioled playfully in the field. 马在田野里欢快地跳跃。|近义词：leap, jump, bound|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb
