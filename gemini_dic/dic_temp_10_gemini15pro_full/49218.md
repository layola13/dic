delectus:/dɪˈlɛktəs/
n.
choice, selection, choosing
A careful and deliberate selection of something, especially something considered the best or most desirable.
The chef made a delectus of the finest ingredients for the meal.  厨师精心挑选了最好的食材来做这顿饭。
selection, choice, election, option
rejection, exclusion
