dilettante:/ˌdɪlɪˈtɑːntɪ/ /ˌdɪlɪˈtæntɪ/ | n. adj. | 业余爱好者；浅尝辄止的人 | A person who cultivates an area of interest, such as the arts, without real commitment or knowledge; a dabbler. |  She's a dilettante who dabbles in painting, but she's never produced anything worthwhile. 她是一个浅尝辄止的绘画爱好者，从未创作出任何有价值的作品。| synonym: amateur, dabbler, trifler | antonym: professional, expert, connoisseur | noun
