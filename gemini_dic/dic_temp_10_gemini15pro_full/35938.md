codak:/ˈkoʊdæk/| n.|柯达（公司名）|A major American technology company that primarily focused on photography related products and services. It is best known for its photographic film products.| Kodak used to be a giant in the film industry. 柯达曾经是胶卷行业的巨头。|Synonyms: Eastman Kodak Company|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
The name "Kodak" was coined by George Eastman, the founder of the company. He wanted a name that was short, easily pronounceable in any language, and not associated with anything else.  "K" was his favorite letter, and he experimented with various combinations until he settled on "Kodak."  The name has no specific meaning other than its association with the company.
