condemning:/kənˈdemɪŋ/
verb
谴责；宣告…有罪；迫使…接受困境
expressing strong disapproval of; officially declare (someone or something) to be guilty of a crime or wrong; force (someone) to endure something unpleasant or undesirable.
The judge is condemning the thief to five years in prison. 法官判处小偷五年徒刑。
近义词: denouncing, criticizing, censuring, blaming
反义词: praising, commending, approving, supporting
词性: Verb (present participle of condemn)

condemning:/kənˈdemɪŋ/
adjective
谴责的; 定罪的
serving to condemn.
She gave him a condemning look. 她用谴责的目光看着他。
近义词: damning, incriminating, accusatory
反义词: approving, commendatory, laudatory
词性: Adjective (present participle of condemn)
