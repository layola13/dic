nonepicurean:/ˌnɒnˌɛpɪkjʊˈrɪən/ | adj. | 非享乐主义的，禁欲的 | Not devoted to or indulging in sensual pleasures; ascetic. |  He led a nonepicurean life, shunning luxury and excess. 他过着禁欲的生活，避免奢侈和过度。|  She preferred a simple, nonepicurean meal. 她更喜欢简单、非享乐主义的餐食。| 近义词: ascetic, austere, abstemious | 反义词: epicurean, hedonistic, luxurious | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + epicurean.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "epicurean" refers to a follower of Epicurus, a Greek philosopher who advocated for pleasure as the highest good.  Thus, "nonepicurean" means "not like an epicurean."
