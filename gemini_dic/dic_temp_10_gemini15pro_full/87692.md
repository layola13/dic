hexametric:/hɛkˈsæmɪtrɪk/
adjective
六音步的；六步格的
Consisting of six metrical feet, especially of dactyls.  Relating to or composed in hexameter verse.
The poem was written in perfect hexametric form. 这首诗是以完美的六音步格写成的。
Homer's epics are written in dactylic hexameter. 荷马史诗是用抑扬六步格写成的。
近义词: six-foot, dactylic
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly iambic or pentameter depending on context)
词性: adjective
