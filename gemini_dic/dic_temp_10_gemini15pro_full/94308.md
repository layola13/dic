imperviousness:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəsnəs/
n.
不能渗透；无动于衷；不受影响
the state of not allowing something to pass through; the state of not being affected by something
The imperviousness of the rock to water erosion made it an ideal location for the dam. 这块岩石的不透水性使它成为建造水坝的理想地点。
His imperviousness to criticism is both a strength and a weakness. 他对批评无动于衷，这既是他的优点也是他的缺点。
近义词:impenetrability, resistance, immunity, insensitivity
反义词:permeability, vulnerability, sensitivity, susceptibility
词性:名词

impervious:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/
adj.
不能渗透的；无动于衷的；不受影响的
not allowing something to pass through; not affected by something
The material is impervious to water. 这种材料不透水。
He seemed impervious to the pain. 他似乎对疼痛无动于衷。
近义词:impenetrable, resistant, immune, insensitive
反义词:permeable, vulnerable, sensitive, susceptible
词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *impervius (“impassable”), from in- (“not”) + pervius (“passing through”), from per (“through”) + via (“way, road”).
