hyperexcitabilities:/ˌhaɪpərɪksaɪtəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|过度兴奋性，超兴奋性|A state of heightened sensitivity and reactivity in nerve cells or tissues, making them more prone to firing action potentials.|The patient's hyperexcitabilities led to seizures.  病人的过度兴奋性导致了癫痫发作。|Synonyms: irritability, sensitivity, reactivity|Antonyms:  inhibition, hypoexcitability|noun
