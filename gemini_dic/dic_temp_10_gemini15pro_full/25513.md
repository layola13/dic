bullish:/ˈbʊlɪʃ/| adj.| 看涨的；乐观自信的；上扬的 | Feeling or showing confidence that something will be successful or that prices will go up.  Characterized by rising stock prices. |  The stock market has been bullish recently. 最近股市看涨。 He's bullish about the company's future. 他对公司的前景很乐观。| 近义词：optimistic, confident, positive, hopeful | 反义词：bearish, pessimistic, negative | 词性: adjective

bullish:/ˈbʊlɪʃ/| n.| 牛市；看涨行情 | A period of rising prices, especially in the stock market. | The current bullish market is expected to continue for several months. 当前的牛市预计会持续几个月。  We're in a bullish period for real estate. 我们正处于房地产的牛市时期。 | 近义词：bull market, uptrend, boom | 反义词：bear market, downtrend, slump | 词性: noun (less common)

词源: From "bull," referring to the way a bull attacks by thrusting its horns upward.  源自“bull”（公牛），指的是公牛用牛角向上顶的方式攻击。
