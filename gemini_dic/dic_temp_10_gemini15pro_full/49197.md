delayed:/dɪˈleɪd/| adj. |延迟的，延误的| slowed down or held back; postponed | The flight was delayed due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班延误了。|近义词：postponed, late, overdue, tardy|反义词：prompt, timely, on-time|adjective

delayed:/dɪˈleɪd/| v. | 延迟，延误 (past tense of delay)| to make something happen at a later time than originally planned or expected |  He delayed his departure until the next morning. 他把出发时间推迟到第二天早上。 |近义词：postpone, defer, put off|反义词：expedite, hasten, advance|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French delaier, from de- + laier "to leave," from Latin laxare "to loosen, slacken."
