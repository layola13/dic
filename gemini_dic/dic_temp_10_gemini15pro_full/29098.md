casa:/ˈkɑːsə/ (US), /ˈkæzə/ (UK)| n.|房子，家|A building for human habitation, especially one that consists of a ground floor and one or more upper storeys.|例句：My casa is near the beach. 我的家在海滩附近。|例句：They returned to their casa after a long journey.  他们长途旅行后回到了家。|近义词：house, home, residence, dwelling|反义词:street, outside|noun
