例句：She told the joke with a humourless expression. 她面无表情地讲了这个笑话。


近义词：serious, solemn, earnest, grave, dour, stern, unsmiling, po-faced, stuffy|反义词:humorous, funny, witty, amusing, lighthearted, jovial|词性:形容词
