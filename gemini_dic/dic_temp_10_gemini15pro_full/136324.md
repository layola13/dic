nonrequisitions:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃənz/
n.
非申请; 未申请的物品; 非必要的物品
Absence of requisitions; items not requisitioned or not essential.
Due to budget cuts, the purchase of nonrequisitions was strictly prohibited. 由于预算削减，严禁购买非必需品。
The warehouse was overflowing with nonrequisitions. 仓库里堆满了非必需品。
近义词: nonessentials, superfluities, surplus
反义词:  requisitions, necessities, essentials
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, absence of  (来自拉丁语 non)
requisition (noun): a formal request for something, especially supplies  (来自拉丁语 requisitio, 要求)
-s (suffix):  plural marker
