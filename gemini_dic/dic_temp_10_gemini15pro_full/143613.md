outissued:/ˌaʊtˈɪʃud/
verb
发行过多；发行超过
having issued more (shares, currency, etc.) than is authorized or desirable.
The company outissued shares, diluting the value of existing stock.  这家公司发行了过多的股票，稀释了现有股票的价值。
outissued shares  超额发行的股票;
overissued, oversubscribed
underissued
v.
