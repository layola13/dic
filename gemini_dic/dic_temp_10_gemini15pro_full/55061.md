disrecommend:/ˌdɪsrɛkəˈmɛnd/
|vt.|不推荐，劝阻|To advise against; to suggest that something or someone is not suitable or desirable.|The hotel was so dirty that I would disrecommend it to anyone. 这家旅馆太脏了，我不会向任何人推荐它。|近义词：advise against, deprecate, discourage,  decry|反义词:recommend, advocate, endorse|词性:动词

Etymology:
dis- (prefix): expressing negation or opposition.  From Latin *dis- "apart, asunder, in different directions," from PIE *dis- "apart, asunder."
recommend (verb): from Latin commendare "to entrust, praise," from com- "together" (see com-) + mandare "to commit, entrust"
