esker:/ˈɛskər/| n. |蛇形丘，冰河脊|A long, winding ridge of stratified sand and gravel, deposited by a meltwater stream flowing within or beneath a glacier.|例句：The hikers followed the esker for miles, enjoying the elevated view.  徒步旅行者沿着蛇形丘走了数英里，欣赏着高处的景色。

例句：Eskers are important geological features that provide clues about past glaciation. 蛇形丘是重要的地质特征，为了解过去的冰川作用提供了线索。|近义词：ridge, kame, drumlin |反义词:valley|词性:noun
