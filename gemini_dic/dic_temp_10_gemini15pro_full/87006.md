henpeckings:/ˈhenˌpekɪŋz/| n. | 责骂，唠叨，压制 | The constant henpeckings from his wife made him miserable.  他妻子不断的唠叨让他很痛苦。 |  例句：He couldn't stand the henpeckings any longer and moved out. 他再也受不了唠叨，搬了出去。

He endured years of henpeckings before finally standing up for himself. 他忍受了多年的唠叨才最终为自己站起来。 | 近义词：nagging, scolding, carping, badgering | 反义词: praising, supporting | 词性: 名词


词源:  From "henpeck" + "-ings." "Henpeck" itself comes from "hen" (female chicken) + "peck" (to strike with the beak), suggesting a wife's constant, sharp criticism like a hen's pecking.
