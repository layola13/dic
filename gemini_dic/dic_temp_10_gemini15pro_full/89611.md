hoodwinkers:/ˈhʊdwɪŋkərz/
noun
欺骗者，蒙蔽者
People who deceive or trick others.
The hoodwinkers convinced the elderly woman to give them her life savings.  那些骗子说服了那位老妇人把她的毕生积蓄都给了他们。
Synonyms:  deceivers, tricksters, swindlers, charlatans, fraudsters
Antonyms:  truth-tellers, honest people
