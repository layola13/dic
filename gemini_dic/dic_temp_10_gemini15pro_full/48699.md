defibrillate: /ˌdiːˈfɪbrɪleɪt/ | v. | 除颤；电击 | To use a defibrillator to deliver an electric shock to (the heart) to restore a normal heart rhythm. |  The paramedics defibrillated the patient's heart.  医护人员对病人的心脏进行除颤。 | Synonyms: shock, resuscitate, revive | Antonyms:  kill, harm (in this context) | verb

defibrillated: /ˌdiːˈfɪbrɪleɪtɪd/ | adj. |  已除颤的; 接受过电击的 | Having been treated with a defibrillator.  | The patient was defibrillated twice before regaining consciousness.  病人被电击两次后才恢复意识。 | Synonyms:  revived, resuscitated (by defibrillation) | Antonyms: untreated (in this context) | adjective
