papa:/ˈpɑːpə/ (US), /pəˈpɑː/ (UK)| n.|爸爸，父亲 (child's word)；(sometimes offensive) 长者，(尤指)教皇|A child's word for father. Can sometimes be used offensively to refer to an older man, especially the Pope.|例句：My papa is the best in the world. 我的爸爸是世界上最好的。

例句：The little girl called out for her papa. 小女孩喊着要她的爸爸。


近义词：dad, daddy, father|反义词:mother, mom, mama|词性:noun
