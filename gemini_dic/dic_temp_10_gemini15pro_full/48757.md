definitiveness:/dɪˈfɪnɪtɪvnəs/| n.| 确定性，明确性 |The statement lacked definitiveness and left room for interpretation.  该声明缺乏确定性，留有解释的余地。|例句：The definitiveness of his answer surprised everyone. 他回答的肯定让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：certainty, conclusiveness, finality|反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, uncertainty|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From definite + -ness.  Definite traces back to Latin *dēfīnītus, past participle of dēfīnīre "to limit, define," from de- "completely" + fīnīre "to limit, bound," from fīnis "boundary, end."
