encroachment:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃmənt/ | n. |侵占，侵入，蚕食 | An act of slowly or gradually taking away someone else's rights, property, etc. |  The encroachment of the sea on the land is a serious problem.  海水对陆地的侵蚀是一个严重的问题。 | 近义词：intrusion, infringement, invasion | 反义词: retreat, withdrawal | 词性: 名词

encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ | v. |侵占，侵入，蚕食 | To advance beyond proper or former limits; trespass.  | The new shopping mall encroached on the local farmland. 新建的购物中心侵占了当地的农田。| 近义词：intrude, infringe, invade | 反义词: retreat, withdraw | 词性: 动词
