counterorders:/ˈkaʊntərˌɔːrdərz/
noun
反指令，取消命令
An order that cancels or reverses a previous order.
The general issued counterorders to stop the advance. 将军下达了停止前进的反指令。
synonyms: cancellation, revocation, recall, repeal
antonyms: order, command, instruction
n.
