arborescent:/ˌɑːrbəˈrɛsənt/| adj.|树状的，枝繁叶茂的|Resembling a tree in growth or appearance; branching like a tree.|The arborescent ferns created a lush, prehistoric feel in the garden.  这些树状的蕨类植物在花园里营造出一种繁茂的史前感觉。|近义词：treelike, branching, dendritic, ramified|反义词：shrubby, bushy|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *arborescent- (stem of arborscens), present participle of arborescere "to become a tree," from arbor "tree."
