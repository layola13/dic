gentility:/dʒɛnˈtɪləti/
n.
高贵的出身；绅士风度；优雅的举止；彬彬有礼
The quality of being genteel; refinement and courtesy of manners.
He treated everyone with the utmost gentility. 他待人极其彬彬有礼。
近义词: courtesy, politeness, refinement, grace, elegance, civility
反义词: rudeness, coarseness, vulgarity, incivility
