overromanticizes: /ˌoʊvərroʊˈmæntɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
过分浪漫化；过度理想化
Makes something seem more romantic than it actually is, often ignoring the negative aspects.
She overromanticizes the past, remembering only the good times and forgetting the hardships. 她把过去过度浪漫化了，只记得美好的时光，忘记了艰辛。
overidealizes, glorifies, sentimentalizes
debunks, degrades, understates
v.
