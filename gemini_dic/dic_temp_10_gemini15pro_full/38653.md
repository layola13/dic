concurring:/kənˈkɜːrɪŋ/
adjective
同时发生的
happening at the same time
例句：The concurring events led to a major crisis.  同时发生的事件导致了一场重大危机。

例句：Concurring opinions were presented at the meeting.  会上提出了相同的意见。

近义词：coincident, simultaneous, concurrent
反义词：separate, distinct, non-concurrent
词性: adjective


concurring:/kənˈkɜːrɪŋ/
adjective
表示赞同的;一致的
agreeing with something; expressing agreement
例句：Several concurring opinions were published alongside the main judgment.  除了主要判决外，还发表了几份表示赞同的意见。

例句：The judge wrote a concurring opinion, supporting the majority decision. 法官写了一份表示赞同的意见，支持多数人的决定。

近义词：agreeing, supporting, approving
反义词：dissenting, opposing, disagreeing
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concurrēns*, present participle of *concurrere* (“to run together, coincide, agree”), from *con-* (“together”) + *currere* (“to run”).
