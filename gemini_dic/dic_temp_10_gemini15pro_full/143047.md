outblundering: /ˌaʊtˈblʌndərɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
犯更大的错误；在失误方面胜过
making more or bigger mistakes than someone else
He outblundered his opponent in the chess match, losing in just a few moves. 他在棋赛中比对手犯了更大的错误，几步棋就输了。

He was outblundering his opponent in the negotiations. 他在谈判中犯的错误比对手更多。
outmaneuvering, outsmarting, outwitting
- (No clear single antonym;  perhaps "outplaying" or "outscoring" if the context suggests a competition where blunders are detrimental.)
