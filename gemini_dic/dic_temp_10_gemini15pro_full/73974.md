fortissimos:/fɔːrˈtɪsɪmoʊs/| adj.|极强的，最强音的|A musical direction indicating that a passage is to be played very loudly; the superlative of forte.|The fortissimos in the symphony were truly earsplitting. 交响乐中最强音的部分震耳欲聋。|近义词：loudest, very loud|反义词：pianissimo, quietest|词性:形容词
词源: Italian, superlative of *forte* "loud."
