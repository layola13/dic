overcommunicating:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
| v. |过度沟通；过分沟通|The act of communicating excessively, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency.  Sharing more information than is necessary or helpful.|例句：I think I'm overcommunicating with my team, sending them multiple updates a day. 我觉得我和我的团队沟通过度了，每天给他们发送多次更新。

例句：She tends to overcommunicate when she's nervous. 她紧张的时候往往会过度沟通。|近义词：overexplaining, oversharing|反义词: undercommunicating|词性:动词
