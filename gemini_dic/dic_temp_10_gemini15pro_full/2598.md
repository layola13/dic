adnominal:/ædˈnɒmɪnl/ /ædˈnɑːmɪnl/| adj.|定语的，作定语的|An adnominal word, phrase, or clause functions as an adjective, modifying a noun.|Example: In "red car," "red" is an adnominal adjective modifying "car".  在“红色汽车”中，“红色”是修饰“汽车”的定语形容词。

Example:  The phrase "walking down the street" is an adnominal phrase in the sentence "The man walking down the street is my uncle."  在句子“走在街上的那个人是我叔叔”中，“走在街上”是定语短语。|近义词:attributive|反义词:predicative|词性:adjective


USAGE->
The adnominal use of adjectives is the most common.  形容词的定语用法是最常见的。
Adnominal phrases can provide more detail about the noun they modify. 定语短语可以提供更多关于它们所修饰的名词的细节。
