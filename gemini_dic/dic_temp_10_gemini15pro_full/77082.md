garreting:/ˈɡærɪtɪŋ/
n.
阁楼房间；在阁楼居住
The act of living in a garret, especially as a poor artist or writer.
He took up garreting in Paris to pursue his painting career.  他为了追求绘画事业在巴黎的阁楼里居住。
Synonyms: attic living, loft living
Antonyms:  mansion living, luxury living
noun
