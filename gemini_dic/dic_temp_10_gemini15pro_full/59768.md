economized: /ɪˈkɒnəmaɪzd/ (US) /iˈkɒnəmaɪzd/ (UK) | v. | 节省，节约，精打细算 | To reduce expenses; to use sparingly or frugally. |  They economized on fuel by taking fewer trips. 他们通过减少出行来节省燃料。|  The company economized by reducing staff. 公司通过裁员来节省开支。 | synonyms: save, conserve, cut back, retrench | antonyms: waste, squander, splurge | verb
