inapprehensive:/ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 不担心的，不忧虑的 | Not worried or fearful; free from apprehension. |  She seemed surprisingly inapprehensive about the upcoming exam. 她似乎对即将到来的考试出奇地不担心。

The children were inapprehensive about starting at the new school. 孩子们对在新学校开始上学并不担心。

| 近义词: unconcerned, carefree, untroubled, relaxed, calm, composed, unworried | 反义词: apprehensive, anxious, worried, fearful, nervous | 词性: 形容词
