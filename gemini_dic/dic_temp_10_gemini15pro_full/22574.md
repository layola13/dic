booths:/buːðz/
n.
小隔间，摊位
a small enclosed space, such as one for selling goods or for voting
例句：We rented a booth at the craft fair. 我们在工艺品展览会上租了一个摊位。

例句：I went into the phone booth to make a call. 我走进电话亭打电话。

近义词：stalls, kiosks, cubicles
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps open space)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English bothe, from Old Norse búð (“dwelling, stall, booth”), from Proto-Germanic *bōþą (“dwelling”), from Proto-Indo-European *bhuH- (“to dwell, be”). Cognate with Danish bod, Swedish bod, Icelandic búð.
