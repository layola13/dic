barbel:/ˈbɑːrbəl/ (US), /ˈbɑːbl/ (UK)| n.|触须；倒刺；(某些鱼的) 须；杠铃|A fleshy filament growing from the mouth or snout of certain fishes, such as catfish, used for touch and taste.  A slender, beardlike process; a barb. A barbell.|The catfish uses its barbels to locate food in murky water.  鲶鱼用它的触须在浑浊的水中寻找食物。

The hook has a barb to prevent the fish from escaping.  鱼钩上有倒刺以防止鱼逃脱。

He lifted the barbel with ease. 他轻松地举起了杠铃。|近义词：whisker,  filament,  barb, weight|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning.  For "whisker," maybe "smooth." For "barb," maybe "point")|词性:noun
