近义词: crunchiness, firmness

crisp:/krɪsp/
adj.
脆的; 酥脆的; 新鲜的; 清晰的; 干冷的; 简短干脆的
Firm and brittle; fresh and invigorating; clear and distinct; (of the air) cold and dry; briskly decisive and efficient.
The air was crisp and cold. 空气又冷又脆。
She gave him a crisp, efficient reply. 她给了他一个干脆利落的答复。
近义词: crunchy, firm, fresh, clear, brisk
反义词: soft, soggy, stale, unclear, slow
词性: 形容词

crisp:/krɪsp/
v.
(使)变脆; (使)卷曲
To make or become crisp.
The cold air crisped the leaves. 冷空气使树叶变脆了。
I crisped the bacon in the pan. 我在锅里把培根煎脆了。
近义词:  fry, roast, toast
反义词: soften, soak
词性: 动词
