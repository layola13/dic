encroachingly: /ɪnˈkroʊtʃɪŋli/ | adv. | 侵入地，逐渐 encroaching 地 | Gradually and intrusively spreading or advancing into someone else's territory or domain. |  The city lights were encroachingly spreading into the countryside. 城市的灯光正逐渐侵入乡村。|  The darkness spread encroachingly, swallowing the last vestiges of daylight. 黑暗逐渐蔓延，吞噬了最后一丝日光。 | 近义词: intrusively, invasively, progressively, increasingly | 反义词: recedingly, retreatingly | 词性: 副词

encroach: /ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ | v. | 侵入，侵占 | Intrude on (a person's territory or a thing considered to be a right). |  The sea is encroaching on the land. 海水侵蚀着陆地。 |  Don't encroach on my privacy. 不要侵犯我的隐私。 | 近义词: intrude, infringe, invade | 反义词: retreat, withdraw | 词性: 动词
