mandore:/ˈmændɔːr/ (US), /ˈmændɔː/ (UK)| n.|曼陀林，一种欧洲的拨弦乐器|A plucked string instrument of the lute family, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries, with four to six pairs of strings and a flat back.|The mandore player skillfully plucked the strings, creating a delicate melody.|近义词：lute, theorbo, cittern|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a musical instrument)|noun
