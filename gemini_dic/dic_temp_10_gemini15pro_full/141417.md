ophiuchus:/ɒfiˈjuːkəs/ | n. | 蛇夫座 |A constellation straddling the celestial equator; it is not one of the traditional twelve constellations of the zodiac, although the ecliptic passes through it. |例句：Ophiuchus is a large constellation located near the center of the Milky Way galaxy. 蛇夫座是一个位于银河系中心附近的大星座。

例句：Some astrologers consider Ophiuchus to be the 13th sign of the zodiac. 一些占星家认为蛇夫座是黄道十二宫的第13个星座。|近义词：Serpent-bearer (字面意思: 蛇的持有者), the thirteenth constellation (第十三星座) |反义词:  None (没有反义词，星座名称通常没有反义词)| 词性: noun
