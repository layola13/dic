megastore:/ˈmeɡəˌstɔːr/ | n. | 大型商店，特大商店 | A very large store, typically one stocking a wide range of goods. |  Example 1: We bought all our furniture at the new megastore on the edge of town. 我们在城郊的新的大型商店买了所有的家具。

Example 2:  The megastore offers a wide variety of products at competitive prices. 这家大型商店以具有竞争力的价格提供各种各样的产品。 | Synonym: hypermarket, superstore | Antonym: small shop, boutique | Noun
