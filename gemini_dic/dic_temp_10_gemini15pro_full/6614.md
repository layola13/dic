anacrotism: /əˈnækrətɪzəm/ | n. | 上升支附加波；近心动脉波的异常上升支 | An additional small upward deflection in the ascending limb of the arterial pulse wave.  A notch on the upstroke of an arterial pulse tracing, sometimes associated with aortic stenosis. |  Anacrotism was observed in the patient's arterial pulse. 在病人的动脉脉搏中观察到上升支附加波。| Synonym: notch | Antonym: smooth upstroke | noun

anacrotic: /ˌænəˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 上升支附加波的；有关上升支附加波的 | Relating to or characterized by anacrotism.  | The anacrotic pulse suggested a potential issue with the aortic valve. 上升支附加波的脉搏提示主动脉瓣可能有问题。| Synonym: notched | Antonym: smooth | adjective
