holeyest:/ˈhoʊliɪst/| adj.|最神圣的，最圣洁的|Filled with holes and also most holy. A humorous or ironic usage.  This word is a neologism, combining "holy" and "holiest" with the suffix "-est" in a non-standard way, emphasizing the perforated nature of something while playfully invoking a sense of sacredness.|The holeyest socks I've ever seen were hanging on the clothesline, flapping in the wind like sacred banners. 我见过最破的袜子挂在晾衣绳上，在风中飘扬，就像神圣的旗帜一样。|近义词：sacred, divine, blessed|反义词：profane, unholy, cursed|词性:形容词
