editorialized:/ˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzd/
verb
发表社论；在…中发表个人意见
to express opinions rather than just report facts
The news report was clearly editorialized, revealing the author's bias. 这篇新闻报道明显带有社论色彩，暴露了作者的偏见。
editorialize, comment, opine
report
v.
