circumfusile:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfjuːsɪl/ | adj. | 可熔化的；可浇铸的；可铸造的 |  Capable of being poured or melted; molten.  Used especially of rocks formed from molten material.  Derived from Latin "circum" (around) and "fusus" (poured).  |  The circumfusile lava flowed down the mountainside.  可熔化的熔岩顺着山坡流了下来。 |  The circumfusile rock showed signs of having once been molten.  这种可浇铸的岩石显示出曾经熔化的迹象。 | Synonyms: fusible, molten, liquefiable | Antonyms: infusible, solid, solidified | 词性: 形容词
