georgettes:/ˌʒɔːrˈʒɛts/
noun
乔其纱（一种薄而透明的丝绸织物）
A sheer, lightweight crêpe fabric, typically made of silk or polyester.
She wore a beautiful georgette dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的乔其纱连衣裙去参加派对。
The curtains were made of a delicate georgette. 窗帘是用精致的乔其纱制成的。
synonyms: chiffon, crepe, voile
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a fabric type, could use thick, heavy fabrics as contrast)  brocade, velvet
n.
