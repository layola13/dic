outgreen:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːn/| v. | 比...更环保；在环保方面胜过 | To surpass someone in terms of environmental friendliness. |  Example 1: Company A outgreened its competitors by implementing a zero-waste policy.  A公司通过实施零浪费政策，在环保方面胜过了竞争对手。

Example 2:  She tried to outgreen her neighbors by installing solar panels and composting. 她试图通过安装太阳能电池板和堆肥来在环保方面胜过她的邻居。 | 近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse | 反义词: lag behind, fall behind | 词性: 动词
