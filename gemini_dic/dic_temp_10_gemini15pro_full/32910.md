chookies:/ˈtʃʊkiz/ | n. |小鸡; (尤指)小母鸡; 宝贝儿; 亲爱的| Chickens, especially young ones. Can be used as a term of endearment. |例句：The farmer had a coop full of chookies. 农场主有一个鸡舍，里面全是小鸡。

例句：She called her little daughter "my little chookie". 她叫她的小女儿“我的小宝贝”。|近义词：chickens, hens (for literal meaning); darling, sweetheart (for endearment)|反义词:rooster (for literal meaning, limited context) |词性:noun
