cadet-blues:/kəˈdɛt ˈbluːz/ | n. |  新生忧郁；军校忧郁 | A feeling of sadness, anxiety, or depression experienced by new cadets, especially during their initial period of adjustment to military life. |  The new cadet was experiencing a bout of the cadet-blues, missing home and struggling with the strict discipline. 新学员正经历着新生忧郁，想家，并难以适应严格的纪律。| synonyms: homesickness, freshman blues,  adjustment disorder | antonyms:  excitement, enthusiasm, contentment | noun
