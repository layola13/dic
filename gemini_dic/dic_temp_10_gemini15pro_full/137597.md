nontribesman:/ˌnɒnˈtraɪbz.mən/ /ˌnɑːnˈtraɪbz.mən/
noun
非部落成员；外族人
A person who is not a member of a particular tribe.
He was considered a nontribesman by the local indigenous people. 他被当地原住民视为外族人。
The nontribesman was not allowed to participate in the sacred rituals.  这个外族人不能参加神圣的仪式。
Synonyms: outsider, foreigner, alien
Antonyms: tribesman, native, insider
