augmenter:/ɔːɡˈmɛntər/ | n. | 增添者；增大器 | Something that augments; an increaser. | The new software is an augmenter of productivity.  这款新软件可以提高生产力。 |  近义词: enhancer, increaser, amplifier | 反义词: diminisher, reducer | noun

augmenter:/ɔːɡˈmɛnt/ | v. | 增加，增大，增强 | To make something greater by adding to it; increase. |  The company augmented its staff with recent graduates. 公司增加了应届毕业生来扩大员工队伍。 | 近义词: increase, enlarge, expand, enhance, boost | 反义词: decrease, reduce, diminish, lessen | verb
