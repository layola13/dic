aromas:/əˈroʊməz/
noun
香味，芳香
A distinctive, usually pleasant smell.
The aromas of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.  厨房里充满了新鲜出炉的面包的香味。
scents, fragrances
stenches, odors
n.
