colorman:/ˈkʌlərmən/| n. |颜料商，颜料工，(尤指)涂料工|A person who sells or manufactures colors, especially paints or dyes. |例句：The colorman mixed a custom shade of blue for the artist.  颜料商为艺术家调制了一种定制的蓝色。

例句：The colorman's shop was filled with vibrant pigments. 颜料工的店里摆满了鲜艳的颜料。|近义词：paint dealer, pigment seller, dye merchant|反义词: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "color" + "man".  "Color" comes from Old French "colur" (modern French "couleur"), ultimately from Latin "color". "Man" comes from Old English "mann".
