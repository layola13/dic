misregistrations:/ˌmɪsrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃənz/
|n.|错误登记，误登记|Instances where data or information has been recorded incorrectly.  The act of registering something wrongly.|The misregistrations in the voter database caused significant problems on election day. 选民数据库中的错误登记在选举日造成了重大问题。|misentries, incorrect recordings, inaccurate entries|correct registrations, accurate entries|noun
