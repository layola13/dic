acetonuria: /ˌæsɪtəˈnjʊəriə/ /ˌæsɪtoʊˈnjʊəriə/
noun
酮尿症
The presence of acetone bodies in the urine.  It is a sign of abnormal metabolism, often associated with diabetes.
The doctor diagnosed acetonuria based on the patient's symptoms and urine test results. 医生根据病人的症状和尿检结果诊断为酮尿症。

Acetonuria can be a sign of uncontrolled diabetes. 酮尿症可能是糖尿病未得到控制的迹象。

近义词: ketoaciduria
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider absence of acetonuria or normal urine)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aceto- + -uria.  Aceto- comes from Latin acētum "vinegar," related to acēre "to be sour." -uria comes from Greek ouron "urine."
