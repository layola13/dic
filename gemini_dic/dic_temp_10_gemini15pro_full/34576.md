clearing:/ˈklɪərɪŋ/| n.| 清理，清除；空地；票据交换；结算；光明|An act or process of making something clear, free from obstruction, or settling accounts.  A piece of land cleared of trees. |The workers were busy with the clearing of the debris after the storm. 工人们正忙着清理风暴后的残骸。|Synonyms: glade, opening, space,  settlement, resolution |Antonyms: obstruction, blockage, confusion|noun

clearing:/ˈklɪərɪŋ/| adj.| 晴朗的；清澈的；无障碍的 |Becoming clear or free from obstruction. |The sky is clearing after the rain. 雨后天空正在放晴。|Synonyms: bright, sunny, cloudless, transparent |Antonyms: cloudy, overcast, opaque|adjective
