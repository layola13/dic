barrage:/bəˈrɑːʒ/ /bəˈrɑːdʒ/| n.  v. |掩护炮火，弹幕，阻塞，连珠炮似的提问|A concentrated artillery bombardment over a wide area. A rapid, concentrated discharge of missiles, blows, or words. To bombard someone with something.|The soldiers were caught in a barrage of enemy fire. 士兵们陷入了敌人的炮火之中。

He faced a barrage of questions from the reporters. 他面临记者连珠炮似的提问。

The dam was built to withstand a barrage of water.  这座大坝是为了抵御洪水而建的。

The company barraged consumers with advertising. 这家公司用广告轰炸消费者。|近义词：bombardment, volley, deluge, onslaught|反义词:trickle, dribble|词性:名词, 动词
