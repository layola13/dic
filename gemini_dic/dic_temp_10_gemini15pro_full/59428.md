earthly:/ˈɜːrθli/ (US) /ˈɜːθli/ (UK)| adj.| 世俗的；尘世的；人间的；可能的| Relating to the earth, as opposed to heaven; worldly; material; possible; conceivable. |例句：She has no earthly possessions. 她没有任何世俗的财产。|例句：There's no earthly reason why he should object. 他没有理由反对。|近义词：worldly, mundane, temporal, material, secular, possible, conceivable |反义词：heavenly, spiritual, divine, impossible, inconceivable |词性:形容词

词源: Old English *eorþlīc (from eorþe 'earth' + -līc '-ly').
