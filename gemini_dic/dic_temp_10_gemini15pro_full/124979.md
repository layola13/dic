muggily:/ˈmʌɡɪli/| adv.|闷热地；潮湿地；郁闷地|In a muggy or oppressive manner; characterized by mugginess.  Describing an atmosphere that is hot and humid, often making people feel uncomfortable. Can also describe a person's mood as sullen or sulky.|The air hung muggily in the room, making it hard to breathe.  房间里的空气闷热潮湿，让人难以呼吸。|近义词：humidly, stickily, oppressively, sullenly|反义词：refreshingly, coolly, brightly|词性:副词
