paraglider:/ˈpærəˌɡlaɪdər/| n. |滑翔伞; 滑翔伞运动员|A light, steerable, fabric wing, inflated by air, used for gliding.|He took off from the hilltop with his paraglider. 他带着滑翔伞从山顶起飞。|近义词：glider, hang glider|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 airplane 飞机, helicopter 直升机 表示不同的飞行方式)|noun
