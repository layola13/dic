collisionless:/kəˈlɪʒənləs/
adjective
无碰撞的
Not involving or affected by collisions between particles.
In a collisionless plasma, the particles do not interact directly with each other through collisions. 在无碰撞等离子体中，粒子不会通过碰撞直接相互作用。
The collisionless Boltzmann equation describes the evolution of a particle distribution function in the absence of collisions. 无碰撞玻尔兹曼方程描述了在没有碰撞的情况下粒子分布函数的演化。
synonym: non-colliding, collision-free
antonym: collisional, colliding
词性: 形容词
