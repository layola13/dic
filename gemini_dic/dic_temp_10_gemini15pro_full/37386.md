commentatorial:/ˌkɒmənˈteəriəl/ | /ˌkɑːmənˈteriəl/ | adj. | 评论的；解说的；评述的 | Relating to or characteristic of commentary. |  The game was accompanied by insightful commentatorial analysis. 这场比赛伴随着富有洞察力的评论分析。 | The commentatorial style was dry and academic. 这种评论风格枯燥且学术化。 | 近义词: analytical, explanatory, expository, interpretive | 反义词: uncritical, biased, subjective | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From commentary + -al.  Ultimately from Latin commentārius (“commentary”), from commentārī (“to think upon, write down, compose”), from com- (“together”) + meditārī (“to meditate”).
