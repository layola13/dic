amianthiform:/ˌeɪmiˈænθɪfɔːrm/
adj.
石棉状的，似石棉的
Resembling amianthus; having a fine, fibrous, flexible structure.
The mineral exhibited an amianthiform texture, composed of delicate, interwoven fibers.  这种矿物呈现出石棉状结构，由纤细交织的纤维组成。
近义词:asbestiform, fibrous
反义词:compact, solid
词性:adjective
