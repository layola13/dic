desultory:/ˈdesəlˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 散漫的，断断续续的，不连贯的 | Lacking a plan, purpose, or enthusiasm; occurring randomly or occasionally. |  He made a desultory attempt to tidy his desk. 他漫不经心地整理了一下桌子。| synonyms: aimless, haphazard, erratic, random, casual, half-hearted, lukewarm, cursory | antonyms: methodical, systematic, focused, diligent, persistent, thorough | adjective

desultory:/ˈdesəlˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 散漫的，断断续续的，不连贯的 | Jumping from one topic to another; disconnected. | The conversation was desultory, jumping from politics to the weather to personal gossip.  这场谈话很散漫，从政治跳到天气，再跳到个人八卦。| synonyms: rambling, disjointed, incoherent, wandering | antonyms: coherent, focused, connected, organized | adjective
