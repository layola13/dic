outwiggled: /ˌaʊtˈwɪɡəld/
verb
扭动胜过，比…更灵活
To surpass or defeat someone by wiggling or maneuvering more effectively.
The dancer outwiggled her competitor, earning a perfect score.  这位舞者比她的对手扭动得更灵活，获得了满分。
The fish outwiggled the net and escaped.  这条鱼扭动着身体，逃出了渔网。
近义词: outmaneuver, outflank, surpass, defeat
反义词: be outmaneuvered, be defeated
