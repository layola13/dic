bobbers:/ˈbɒbəz (UK), ˈbɑːbərz (US)| n.|浮子，钓鱼用的浮标，短发|A bobber is a small floating object attached to a fishing line to indicate when a fish is biting. It can also refer to a short haircut, especially one that is chin-length and curled under.|The bobber dipped below the surface, signaling a bite. 浮标沉了下去，表明有鱼咬钩了。

|Synonyms: float, cork, bob, crop (haircut)|Antonyms: sinker (fishing), long hair|noun
