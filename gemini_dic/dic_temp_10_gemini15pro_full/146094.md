overeffusive:/ˌoʊvərɪˈfjuːsɪv/| adj.|过于热情洋溢的，过分热情的|Expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or excessive way.|She greeted me with an overeffusive hug and a barrage of compliments. 她以过分热情的拥抱和一连串的恭维迎接我。|Near Synonyms: gushing, exuberant, effusive, lavish, unrestrained|Antonyms: reserved, restrained, subdued, understated|Word Origin: From 'over-' (excessively) + 'effusive' (pouring out), from Latin 'effusus', past participle of 'effundere' (to pour out).
