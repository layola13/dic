nonresiliencies:/ˌnɒnriˈzɪliənsiz/
| n. | 非弹性，非韧性；缺乏适应力 | The nonresiliencies of the system were exposed during the crisis. 该系统的非弹性在危机期间暴露无遗。|
| 英文解析:  Lack of resilience; inability to recover quickly from difficulties or change.  It refers to the absence of the qualities that allow someone or something to bounce back from adversity or adapt to changing circumstances. |
| 例句：The city’s nonresiliencies in the face of natural disasters became tragically clear.  这座城市在面对自然灾害时的缺乏适应力变得异常明显。|

| 例句：The report highlighted the nonresiliencies of the global financial system.  这份报告强调了全球金融体系的脆弱性。|

| 近义词：fragilities, vulnerabilities, weaknesses, inflexibilities |
| 反义词：resiliencies, strengths, adaptability, flexibility |
| 词性: noun (plural) |


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + resilience.  "Resilience" comes from the Latin "resilire," meaning "to leap back, rebound." The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning, resulting in "nonresilience," the lack of ability to rebound or recover.
