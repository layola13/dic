incultivated:/ˌɪnˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
未开垦的，荒芜的；未耕作的；不文明的；没教养的
Not cultivated; undeveloped; unrefined.  Lacking culture or refinement; uncivilized.
The garden was incultivated and overgrown with weeds.  花园未经耕种，杂草丛生。
He showed incultivated manners at the dinner table.  他在餐桌上表现出没有教养的举止。
Synonyms:  uncultivated, unrefined, crude, coarse, uncivilized, barbarous, boorish, rude, rough, untamed, fallow, wild
Antonyms: cultivated, refined, civilized, cultured, polished, sophisticated, tame, domestic
词性: adjective
