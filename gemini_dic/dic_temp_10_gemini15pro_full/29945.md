caveator:/ˈkeɪviˌeɪtər/ | n. | 警告者; 抗辩者; 预告登记人 | A person who files a caveat, a formal notice to a court or other official body, warning them not to proceed with a particular action until the caveator has been heard. |  The caveator filed a notice to prevent the sale of the property.  预告登记人提交了一份通知，以阻止该房产的出售。 |  objector, challenger, appellant | supporter, proponent | noun
