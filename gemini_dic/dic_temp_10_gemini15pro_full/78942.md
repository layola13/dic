girders:/ˈɡɜːrdərz/| n. |大梁，桁梁|A large beam, typically of steel or concrete, used as a main horizontal support in a structure.|The bridge was supported by massive steel girders. 这座桥由巨大的钢梁支撑。|The new stadium is built with pre-stressed concrete girders.  这座新体育场是用预应力混凝土梁建造的。| synonyms: beams, joists, supports | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "supports," if considering removal) | noun
