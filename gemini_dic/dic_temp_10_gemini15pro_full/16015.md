banalizing:/bəˈnælaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|使...平庸化，使...陈腐|The overuse of clichés in writing can banalize even the most original ideas.  在写作中过度使用陈词滥调，即使是最有创意的想法也会变得平庸。 | The constant media exposure banalized the tragedy.  持续的媒体曝光使得这场悲剧变得平庸。|近义词：trivializing, vulgarizing, oversimplifying,  devaluing|反义词：originalizing, elevating, enhancing, dignifying|词性: 动词 (Verb)
词源: From French *banaliser*, from *banal* ("commonplace"), ultimately from Old French *ban* (“proclamation”), from Frankish *ban* (“command, jurisdiction”).  来自法语 *banaliser*，源于 *banal*（“平凡的”），最终来自古法语 *ban（“公告”），源于法兰克语 *ban（“命令，管辖权”）。
