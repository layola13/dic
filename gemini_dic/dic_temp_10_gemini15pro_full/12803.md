assistant-professorships: /əˈsɪstənt prəˈfɛsərˌʃɪps/ | n. |助理教授职位；助理教授的职称|The university offers several assistant-professorships in the engineering department. 这所大学在工程系提供几个助理教授职位。|Example: She was awarded one of the new assistant-professorships. 她获得了新的助理教授职位之一。|Synonym: assistant professorship, tenure-track position|Antonym: full professorship|Noun

Etymology:
assistant (from Old French "assistant," present participle of "assister" to stand by, from Latin "assistere" to stand near, from "ad-" to + "sistere" to cause to stand) + professor (from Late Latin "professor," meaning "one who professes, teacher," from "professus," past participle of "profiteri" to declare publicly, from "pro-" forth + "fateri" to confess) + -ship (Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office).
