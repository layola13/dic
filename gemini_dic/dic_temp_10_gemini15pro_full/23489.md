brahmaness:/ˈbrɑːmənɛs/
n.
婆罗门女子；婆罗门妇女
A female member of the Brahman caste, the highest Hindu caste, traditionally associated with priesthood and scholarship.
The brahmaness prepared the ritual offerings for the ceremony.  婆罗门女子为仪式准备了祭品。
The brahmaness chanted ancient hymns. 婆罗门女子吟唱着古老的赞美诗。
近义词:Brahmin woman, Brahmin female
反义词:(None -  lacks a direct opposite.  Could consider lower caste members, but not a direct antonym)
词性:noun
