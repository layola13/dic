campylotropous: /ˌkæmpɪˈlɒtrəpəs/
adjective
弯生胚珠的
(of an ovule) curved so that the micropyle is near the funicle.
例句：The campylotropous ovule is common in legumes.  弯生胚珠在豆科植物中很常见。

例句：Campylotropous ovules are characteristic of the Capparaceae family. 弯生胚珠是白花菜科植物的特征。

近义词：anatropous (partially), amphitropous (partially)
反义词：orthotropous
词性: adjective
