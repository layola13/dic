impassibleness:/ɪmˌpæsɪb(ə)lnəs/
n.
无动于衷，冷漠，不动声色
The state of being impassible; inability to suffer or feel pain; insensibility; apathy.
His impassibleness during the crisis was remarkable.  他在危机期间的镇定令人瞩目。
近义词：impassivity, apathy, indifference, stoicism
反义词：sensitivity, emotionality, responsiveness

impassible:/ɪmˈpæsɪb(ə)l/
adj.
无动于衷的，冷漠的，不动的
Not capable of suffering; incapable of feeling pain; insensible; apathetic.
He remained impassible throughout the trial. 他在整个审判过程中都面无表情。
近义词：unfeeling, emotionless, indifferent, stoic
反义词：sensitive, emotional, responsive

词源:
From Late Latin *impassibilis, from in- "not" + passibilis "capable of suffering," from pati "to suffer."
