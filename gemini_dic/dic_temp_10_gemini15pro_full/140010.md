ecumenical: /ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪk(ə)l/ /ˌiːkjuːˈmɛnɪk(ə)l/
adjective
普遍的，世界的；基督教各派大联合的
representing or promoting unity among all Christian churches; worldwide or general in extent or influence.
例句：The Pope called for greater ecumenical cooperation between Christian churches.  教皇呼吁基督教各教会之间加强大公合作。

例句：The conference attracted scholars with ecumenical interests from all over the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地具有普遍兴趣的学者。

近义词：universal, global, worldwide, general, catholic
反义词：parochial, sectarian, local, limited
词性: adjective
