engravable:/ɪnˈɡreɪvəbl/
adjective
可雕刻的
Suitable or ready to be engraved.
This pen is made of high-quality metal and is engravable. 这支笔由优质金属制成，可以雕刻。
The trophy was engravable with the winner's name.  奖杯上可以刻上获胜者的名字。

近义词:inscribable, carvable
反义词:non-engravable

词性:adjective
词源:From engrave + -able.  Engrave comes from the Old French engraver, ultimately from the Latin *in- + *gravare "to carve."
