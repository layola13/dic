half-wildnesses:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪldnɪsɪz/
n.
半野生状态; 略微荒凉的地方
Places that are partially wild or untamed, retaining some aspects of wilderness.
The garden had been neglected and returned to a state of half-wildness.  这个花园被忽视了，又回到了半荒芜的状态。
The area surrounding the cabin was a charming half-wildness, with overgrown flowers and visiting deer. 小屋周围的区域有一种迷人的半野生状态，野花丛生，还有鹿出没。
near synonyms: semi-wildness, partial wilderness, controlled wilderness
antonyms: cultivation, domestication, tameness
noun
