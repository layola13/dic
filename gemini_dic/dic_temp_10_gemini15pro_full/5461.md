aloe:/ˈæloʊ/ (US) /ˈæləʊ/ (UK)| n.|芦荟; 芦荟胶|A succulent plant with thick, fleshy leaves that is widely grown in warm climates. The leaves contain a gel that is used in cosmetics and medicine.|例句：I use aloe vera gel to soothe sunburn. 我用芦荟胶来舒缓晒伤。

例句：Aloe is a common ingredient in many skin care products. 芦荟是许多护肤产品的常见成分。|近义词：aloe vera|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
