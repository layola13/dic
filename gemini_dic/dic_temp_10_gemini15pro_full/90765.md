humoring:/ˈhjuːmərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərɪŋ/ (UK)|v. (present participle of humor)|迁就；迎合；顺某人的意|Indulging or going along with someone's whims or wishes, often to appease them or maintain a pleasant atmosphere. |例句：He was humoring his grandmother by pretending to enjoy her terrible cooking. 他假装喜欢祖母做的难吃的菜来哄她开心。

例句：She was humoring her child by letting him stay up late. 她迁就孩子，让他熬夜。

|近义词：indulging, appeasing, accommodating, pampering|反义词：opposing, contradicting, defying|词性:动词
