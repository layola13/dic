land: /lænd/
n.|土地，陆地|a piece of ground|The farmer worked his land. 农民耕种他的土地。
v.|着陆，登陆；获得|to come down to the ground; to acquire|The plane landed safely. 飞机安全着陆。|
近义词: ground, soil, territory, property (n.); arrive, obtain, secure (v.)
反义词: sea, sky (n.); depart, lose (v.)
词性: noun, verb
词源: From Old English *land, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch land and German Land.
