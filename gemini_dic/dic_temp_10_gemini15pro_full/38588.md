concordantly:/kənˈkɔːrdəntli/ | adv. | 一致地，和谐地 | in agreement, harmoniously | The two studies' results, concordantly, showed a marked improvement in patient outcomes. 这两项研究的结果一致表明，患者的预后明显改善。| The musicians played concordantly, creating a beautiful melody.  音乐家们演奏得十分和谐，创造出优美的旋律。 | 近义词: harmoniously, consistently, agreeably, uniformly | 反义词: discordantly, inconsistently, differently | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *concordant- (stem of concordāns), present participle of concordāre ‘to agree’, from con- ‘together’ + cord- ‘heart’.
