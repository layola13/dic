misanthropist:/ˈmɪsənθrəpɪst/| n.|厌世者，愤世嫉俗者|A person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society.|例句：He was a misanthropist who lived alone in the woods. 他是一个厌世者，独自住在树林里。

例句：The old misanthropist refused all visitors.  这位年老的厌世者拒绝所有访客。


近义词：cynic, pessimist, recluse|反义词:philanthropist, humanitarian|词性:noun
