bullhead:/ˈbʊlˌhed/ (US) /ˈbʊlhed/ (UK)| n.|牛头；鲶鱼的一种；固执的人|A freshwater fish of the catfish family, with a large head. Also, a stubborn or obstinate person. |例句：

He caught a bullhead while fishing in the river.  他河钓时钓到了一条牛头鱼。

He's such a bullhead; he never listens to anyone's advice. 他真是个固执的人；从来不听别人的劝告。

|近义词：catfish, stubborn person, obstinate person|反义词：compliant person, amenable person|noun


bullhead:/ˈbʊlˌhed/ (US) /ˈbʊlhed/ (UK)| adj.|固执的，顽固的|Stubborn or obstinate.|例句：

He has a bullhead attitude and refuses to compromise.  他态度顽固，拒绝妥协。

She's known for her bullhead determination to succeed. 她以其顽固的决心而闻名。


|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, headstrong|反义词：compliant, amenable, yielding|adjective
