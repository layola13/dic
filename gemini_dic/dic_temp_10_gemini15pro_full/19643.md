词源:
From Bikini Atoll, a small island in the Marshall Islands where atomic bomb tests were conducted. The swimsuit was named after the island because its revealing nature was considered as explosive as the bomb.
来自比基尼环礁，马绍尔群岛的一个小岛，在那里进行了原子弹试验。这款泳衣以该岛命名，因为它的暴露性质被认为与炸弹一样具有爆炸性。
