overspecializing:/ˌōvərˈspɛʃəˌlīzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)|过度专业化；过分专门化|The act of focusing too narrowly on a specific area of expertise, often to the detriment of broader knowledge or skills.|He worried about overspecializing in one narrow area of law. 他担心在法律的一个狭窄领域过度专业化。|近义词: narrowing, focusing, concentrating|反义词: diversifying, broadening, generalizing|词性:v.


overspecialization:/ˌōvərˌspɛʃələˈzeɪʃən/
noun|过度专业化；过分专门化|The state or result of focusing too narrowly on a specific area of expertise.|Overspecialization can sometimes limit career opportunities. 过度专业化有时会限制职业机会。|近义词: narrowness, focus, concentration|反义词: diversification, breadth, generalization|词性:n.
