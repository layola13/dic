overextracts:/ˌoʊvərˈekstrækt/ |v. (overextracted, overextracting)|过度提取，过度萃取|To extract an excessive amount from something, such as flavor, color, or resources.|The coffee was bitter because they had overextracted the grounds.|近义词: overbrew, overuse, deplete|反义词: underextract, conserve|词性:动词

overextracts:/ˌoʊvərˈekstrækt/ |n. (usually plural: overextracts)|过度提取物|The substance that has been extracted excessively.|The overextracts from the tea leaves made the drink too strong.|近义词: excess, surplus|反义词: deficiency, lack|词性:名词
