chowk:/tʃɔːk/ | n. |广场，市场，十字路口 | A public square or marketplace, especially in India or Pakistan.  It can also refer to a crossroads. |  The vendors set up their stalls in the bustling chowk.  小贩们在熙熙攘攘的广场上摆起了摊位。

The chowk was the heart of the old city.  这个广场是老城的中心。| 近义词: square, plaza, marketplace, crossroads | 反义词: cul-de-sac, dead end | 词性: 名词

词源: From Hindi and Urdu چوک (cauk), from Persian چوک (čauk, “four”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kʷetwṓr- (“four”).  The word reflects the four-way intersection often found at a chowk.
