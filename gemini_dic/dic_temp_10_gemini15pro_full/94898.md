in-flight:/ˈɪnflaɪt/ /ɪnˈflaɪt/ | adj. | 飞行中的，在飞行期间的 | Occurring or provided during an airplane flight. |  Passengers can enjoy in-flight entertainment. 乘客可以享受机上娱乐。 |  The in-flight meal was surprisingly good.  飞机餐出奇地好。 | Synonyms: airborne, aloft | Antonyms: grounded, landed | adjective

in-flight:/ˈɪnflaɪt/ /ɪnˈflaɪt/ | n. | (通常指飞机的)飞行，航程 | The act or process of flying, especially in an airplane. | The in-flight was smooth and uneventful. 航班平稳，一切正常。| The turbulence during the in-flight was quite unsettling. 飞行途中的湍流令人不安。 | Synonyms: flight, journey | Antonyms: landing, arrival | noun
