diurnal: /daɪˈɜːrnl/ | adj. | 白天的，昼行性的 | Happening or active during the day. | Bats are not diurnal creatures; they are nocturnal.  蝙蝠不是昼行动物；它们是夜行性的。| Diurnal animals are active during the daytime. 昼行动物在白天很活跃。 | 近义词: daily, daytime | 反义词: nocturnal, nightly | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin diurnālis, from Latin diurnus ‘daily’, from dies ‘day’.
