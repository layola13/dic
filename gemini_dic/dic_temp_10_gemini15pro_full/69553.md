fellowlike:/ˈfɛləʊˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像伙伴的；同志般的；友好的 | Resembling or characteristic of a fellow; companionable. | He had a fellowlike attitude towards his colleagues. 他对同事的态度很友好。|  Near synonyms: companionable, friendly, comradely, sociable, affable | Antonyms: unfriendly, unsociable, hostile, aloof, distant | adjective

fellowlike:/ˈfɛləʊˌlaɪk/ | n. | 同伴；伙伴 | A person who is similar to another in some way; a companion. (This usage is less common than the adjective form) | He was a fellowlike to me in our shared love of hiking. 他和我一样热爱徒步旅行，是我的同伴。(This usage is archaic and rarely used today) | Near synonyms: companion, comrade, friend, peer, associate | Antonyms: enemy, foe, opponent, adversary, rival | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "fellow" + "-like". "Fellow" comes from Old Norse "félagi" meaning "partner, shareholder," from "félag" meaning "partnership, company." "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
