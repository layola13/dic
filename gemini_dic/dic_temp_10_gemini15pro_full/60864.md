elegantly:/ˈelɪɡəntli/| adv.|优雅地；优美地；精致地|In a graceful or refined manner.|She danced elegantly across the stage. 她优雅地在舞台上翩翩起舞。|近义词：gracefully, stylishly, tastefully, exquisitely|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, crudely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Old French *elegantment, from Latin elegant-, elegantis + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin eligere "to choose, select," suggesting a refined taste.
