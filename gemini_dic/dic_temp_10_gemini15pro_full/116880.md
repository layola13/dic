mayapan:/ˌmaɪəˈpɑːn/
n.
玛雅潘（古玛雅城市）
A pre-Columbian Maya city located in the Yucatán Peninsula. It was the political and cultural capital of the Maya in the Late Post-Classic period (c. 1220–1440 AD).
The ruins of Mayapan are a popular tourist destination. 玛雅潘遗址是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。
近义词: Chichen Itza, Uxmal (other Maya cities)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific city)
词性: noun
