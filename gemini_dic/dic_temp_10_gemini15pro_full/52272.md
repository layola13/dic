dicey:/ˈdaɪsi/ (US), /ˈdaɪsi/ (UK)| adj.|危险的，不确定的，冒险的|Unpredictable and potentially dangerous or risky.|例句：The situation is a bit dicey at the moment. 目前的情况有点危险。

例句：Investing in that company seems rather dicey.  投资那家公司似乎风险很大。


近义词：risky, precarious, uncertain, dangerous, hazardous, perilous|反义词:safe, secure, certain, stable|词性:形容词
