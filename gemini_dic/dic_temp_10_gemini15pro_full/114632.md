maler:/ˈmɑːlər/ (UK), /ˈmeɪlər/ (US)| n.|画家；油漆工|A person who paints pictures, especially as a profession; a person whose job is to paint surfaces, such as walls and doors.|例句：The maler is known for his vibrant landscapes.  这位画家以其充满活力的风景画而闻名。

例句：We hired a maler to decorate the house. 我们雇了一个油漆工来装修房子。|近义词：artist, painter, decorator|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered as someone who destroys art or defaces things)|noun
