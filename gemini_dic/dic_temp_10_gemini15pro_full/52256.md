It's used to describe compounds where two butyl groups are attached to another atom or group.|Dibutyl phthalate is a common plasticizer.  邻苯二甲酸二丁酯是一种常见的增塑剂。|近义词:  (No direct synonyms, depends on the specific context.  Could be related to other alkyl prefixes like diethyl or dipropyl) |反义词: N/A|词性: Adjective
