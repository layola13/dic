freebies:/ˈfriːbiz/
noun
免费赠品，免费的东西
something that is given away free, especially by a company
例句：Everyone loves freebies!  每个人都喜欢免费赠品！
例句：The conference attendees received a bag full of freebies. 与会者收到了一袋免费赠品。
近义词：giveaways, free gifts, complimentary items
反义词：purchases, paid items
词性:n.
