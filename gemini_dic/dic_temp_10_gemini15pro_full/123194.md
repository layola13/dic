monocentric:/ˌmɒnəʊˈsɛntrɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəʊˈsɛntrɪk/
adjective
单中心的；以单一中心为基础的
Having a single center; organized around a single center.
The monocentric city is characterized by a dominant central business district.  这个单中心城市的特点是有一个主要的中心商务区。

The company adopted a monocentric organizational structure, with all decisions emanating from headquarters. 这家公司采用单中心组织结构，所有决策都来自总部。

synonym:  unifocal, single-centered
antonym:  polycentric, multicentered
词性: adjective
