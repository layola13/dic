dressmaking:/ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkɪŋ/
noun
裁缝; 女装裁制
The activity of making women's clothes.
She learned dressmaking from her grandmother. 她跟祖母学会了裁缝。
近义词: tailoring, sewing
反义词: None
词性: noun

dressmaking:/ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkɪŋ/
Etymology:  dress + making.  "dress" from Old French "dresser" meaning "to arrange, straighten, prepare." "making" from Old English "macian" meaning "to make, form, build, create, compose, do, perform."
The craft or activity of making women's clothes.  It involves designing, cutting, fitting, and sewing fabric to create garments.
She took a dressmaking course to learn how to make her own clothes. 她参加了一个裁缝课程来学习如何制作自己的衣服。
近义词: tailoring, sewing, garment-making
反义词: None
词性: noun
