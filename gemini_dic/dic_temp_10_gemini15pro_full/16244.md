bantams:/ˈbæntəmz/| n.|矮脚鸡；小型鸡；小而好斗的人|A breed of small domestic fowl, often having short legs and feathered feet. Also, metaphorically, a small but aggressive person.|例句：The bantams strutted around the farmyard. 矮脚鸡在农场里昂首阔步地走来走去。

例句：He was a bantam of a man, always ready for a fight. 他是个矮小但好斗的人，随时准备打架。|近义词：miniature, dwarf, midget (for fowl); feisty, scrappy (for people)|反义词：giant (for fowl); timid, meek (for people)|词性:名词

bantam:/ˈbæntəm/| adj.|小型的；矮小的|Small; miniature.|例句：She raises bantam chickens in her backyard. 她在后院养矮脚鸡。

例句：It was a bantam version of the original car. 这辆车是原版车的缩小版。|近义词：miniature, dwarf, small|反义词：large, giant|词性:形容词
