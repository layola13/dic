inebriety:/ɪnɪˈbraɪəti/
n.
醉酒，陶醉
Drunkenness; intoxication.
He stumbled into the room in a state of inebriety. 他醉醺醺地跌跌撞撞地进了房间。
近义词：drunkenness, intoxication, tipsiness
反义词：sobriety
词性：名词
