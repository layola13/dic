countercharge:/ˈkaʊntərˌtʃɑːrdʒ/ /ˈkaʊntərˌtʃɑːrdʒ/| n. v.|反击，反诉，反控|An accusation made in response to an earlier accusation.|例句：The defendant's countercharge was that he had been framed.  被告的反诉是他被陷害了。

例句：He responded to the allegations with a countercharge of corruption. 他以腐败的反控回应了这些指控。|近义词：counterattack, counterclaim, rebuttal, riposte|反义词: accusation, charge|词性:名词,动词
