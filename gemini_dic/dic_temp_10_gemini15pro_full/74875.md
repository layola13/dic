frescoes:/ˈfrɛskoʊz/ (US), /ˈfrɛskoʊz/ (UK)| n.|壁画|A painting done rapidly in watercolor on wet plaster on a wall or ceiling, so that the colors penetrate the plaster and become fixed as it dries.|The villa's walls were adorned with vibrant frescoes. 这栋别墅的墙壁上装饰着充满活力的壁画。|近义词：mural, painting|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *fresco* "fresh," from Latin *friscus*.  The technique refers to applying paint to fresh plaster.
