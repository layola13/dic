breve:/briːv/| n. adj.|短音符号，短音的|A diacritical mark (˘) placed over a vowel to indicate a short sound or a short or unstressed syllable.  As an adjective, it means short in duration or sound.|The word "breve" itself has a breve over the "e".  This word has a breve. |近义词:short, brief|反义词:long, macron|noun, adjective
