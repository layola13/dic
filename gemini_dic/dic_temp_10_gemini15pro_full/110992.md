liquidlike:/ˈlɪkwɪdlaɪk/
adjective
像液体的；似液体的
Resembling a liquid; having liquid properties.
The substance had a liquidlike consistency. 这种物质具有类似液体的稠度。
The molten metal flowed in a liquidlike manner. 熔化的金属以类似液体的形式流动。

近义词:fluid, runny, watery
反义词:solid, viscous, thick
词性:adjective
