lesotho:/ləˈsuːtuː/ /ləˈsoʊtuː/
n.
莱索托 (非洲南部国家)
A landlocked country in southern Africa. It is entirely surrounded by South Africa.
Lesotho gained independence from Britain in 1966. 莱索托于1966年脱离英国独立。
Lesotho is known as the "Kingdom in the Sky" due to its high altitude. 莱索托因其高海拔而被称为“天空王国”。
