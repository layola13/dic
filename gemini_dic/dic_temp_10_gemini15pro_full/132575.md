nondieting:/ˌnɒnˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | adj. | 非节食的，不限制饮食的 |  Relating to or advocating an approach to health and well-being that does not involve restrictive dieting.  It emphasizes intuitive eating, body acceptance, and focusing on overall health rather than weight loss. | She adopted a nondieting approach to food and felt much happier. 她采取了非节食的饮食方式，感觉快乐多了。|  Intuitive eating, mindful eating, healthy eating | restrictive dieting,  dieting | adjective
