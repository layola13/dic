out-print:/ˌaʊt əv ˈprɪnt/ | adj. |绝版的，已售完的| A publication is out of print when it is no longer being printed and copies are no longer available for sale from the publisher. |  This book is out of print, so it's hard to find. 这本书已经绝版了，所以很难找到。|The first edition is now out of print and highly collectible. 第一版现在已经绝版了，很有收藏价值。| 近义词: unavailable, sold out | 反义词: in print | 词性:形容词
