nondistributivenesses:/ˌnɒndɪˌstrɪbjʊˈtɪvnəsɪz/| n. |非分配性|The state or quality of being nondistributive.  A property of certain logical or mathematical operations where the operation applied to a disjunction of terms is not equivalent to the disjunction of the operation applied to each term individually.|The nondistributivenesses of multiplication over addition in some algebraic structures can lead to unexpected results.|近义词: non-distributivity|反义词: distributivity|词性: noun
