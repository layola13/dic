decry:/dɪˈkraɪ/
verb
公开谴责；诋毁
to publicly criticize or condemn something
The government's economic policies were decried by the opposition. 政府的经济政策受到了反对派的谴责。
The media decried the violence. 媒体谴责了暴力行为。
Synonym: denounce, condemn, criticize, disparage, vilify
Antonym: praise, laud, commend, extol
词性: 动词
