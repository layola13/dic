oreodon: /ˈɔːriːədɒn/ | n. |  奥利奥猪，一种已灭绝的北美哺乳动物 | An extinct mammal of the family Merycoidodontidae, abundant in western North America during the Oligocene and Miocene epochs.  |  The oreodon fossils were found in the Badlands. 奥利奥猪化石在荒地被发现。 |  The museum displayed a complete oreodon skeleton. 博物馆展出了完整的奥利奥猪骨架。 | 近义词: merycoidodon (严格来说是同义词，因为Merycoidodontidae科也叫Oreodontidae科) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *ὄρος* (óros, “mountain”) + *ὀδούς* (odoús, “tooth”).  "Mountain tooth", referring to the appearance of the animal's molars.
