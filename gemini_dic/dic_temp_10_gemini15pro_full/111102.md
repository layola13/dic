litenesses:/ˈlaɪtnɪsɪz/
n.
礼貌；客气；彬彬有礼(的举止)
plural of politeness.  The state or quality of being polite; refined courtesy; obliging attentions.
Her  litenesses charmed everyone. 她的礼貌举止迷住了所有人。
近义词：courtesies, civilities, pleasantries
反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy
词性: 名词(plural)

词源:
From Middle English *litenes, from Old French *liteté, from Latin lītetās ("smoothness, elegance"), from lītis ("smooth, polished"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ley-  ("to smear, anoint, stick").  The English word was re-borrowed from French politesse in the 17th century.
