hornsnake:/ˈhɔːrnˌsneɪk/ | n. | 角响尾蛇 | A venomous snake of the genus *Cerastes*, found in sandy regions of North Africa and the Middle East, characterized by a pair of horn-like scales above its eyes. |  Example: The hornsnake lay camouflaged in the sand, waiting for its prey. 角响尾蛇伪装在沙子里，等待着猎物。

Example:  Researchers are studying the venom of the hornsnake to develop new antivenoms. 研究人员正在研究角响尾蛇的毒液以开发新的抗蛇毒血清。 | 近义词: horned viper, sidewinder (some species) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
