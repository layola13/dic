drecks: /drɛks/
n.
渣滓，败类，人渣
Scum; despicable people.
He called the rioters a bunch of drecks. 他称那些暴徒是一群人渣。
近义词: scum, trash, riffraff, vermin
反义词: elite, aristocracy, gentry
词性: 名词

drecks: /drɛks/
n.
废物，垃圾
Rubbish; worthless things.
Just throw all that drecks away. 把那些垃圾都扔掉。
近义词: garbage, rubbish, trash, junk
反义词: treasure, valuables
词性: 名词


词源:
From German *Drecks*, plural of *Dreck* meaning "dirt, filth".
