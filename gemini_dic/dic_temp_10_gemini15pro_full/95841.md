incorrectly: /ˌɪnkəˈrɛktli/ | adv. | 不正确地，错误地 |  In a way that is not correct or accurate.  Derived from "in-" (not) + "correct" (right, free from error) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). The root "correct" comes from the Latin "correctus," past participle of "corrigere" (to make straight, to set right). | He spelled my name incorrectly. 他把我的名字拼错了。| He answered the question incorrectly. 他答错了这个问题。| 近义词: wrongly, mistakenly, erroneously | 反义词: correctly, accurately, rightly | 词性: 副词
