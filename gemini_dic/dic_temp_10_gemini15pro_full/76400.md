gag-reined:/ˈɡæɡˌreɪnd/
adjective
戴着嚼子的；被压制言论自由的
Having a gag (a device or piece of cloth put in or over someone's mouth to prevent them from speaking) and reins (straps used to control a horse).  Figuratively, it means silenced or prevented from speaking freely.
The gag-reined witness was unable to testify truthfully.  被压制言论自由的证人无法如实作证。
Synonyms: silenced, muzzled, suppressed, censored
Antonyms: outspoken, vocal, uncensored, free
Word type: adjective
