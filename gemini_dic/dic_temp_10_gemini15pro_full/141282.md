opelet: /ˈoʊpəˌlɛt/ | n. |轻歌剧; 小型歌剧|A short opera, usually on a light or humorous theme.|The students performed a charming opelet about a mischievous fairy. 学生们表演了一出关于顽皮仙女的迷人的轻歌剧。|近义词: operetta, musical|反义词: opera (in the sense of a grand opera, though not a strict opposite)|词性: noun

词源:  From French *opérette*, diminutive of *opéra* (opera), from Italian *opera*, literally "work".
