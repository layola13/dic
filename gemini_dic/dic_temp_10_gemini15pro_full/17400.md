beakless:/ˈbiːklɪs/| adj.|无喙的，没有喙的|Lacking a beak; having no beak.  This term describes a bird or other creature that normally has a beak but, for some reason (e.g., injury, birth defect), does not.|The beakless bird struggled to feed its young. 这只没有喙的鸟很难喂养它的幼鸟。|近义词: jawless|反义词: beaked|词性: 形容词
