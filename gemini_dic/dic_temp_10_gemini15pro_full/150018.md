palometa:/ˌpɑːloʊˈmeɪtə/ | n. |鲳鱼; 梭鱼; 蝴蝶鱼|A type of fish, often a pompano, permit, or butterflyfish, found in tropical and subtropical waters.  Specifically, in Cuba, it refers to the Atlantic threadfin (Polydactylus octonemus).|例句：The fisherman caught a large palometa. 渔夫捕到了一条大鲳鱼。

例句：Palometa is a popular dish in Caribbean cuisine. 鲳鱼是加勒比菜肴中的一道热门菜。|近义词：pompano, permit, butterflyfish|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of fish) |词性:noun
