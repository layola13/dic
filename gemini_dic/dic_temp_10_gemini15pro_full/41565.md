corporalship:/ˈkɔːrpərəlʃɪp/ |n.|下士职务；下士军衔|The position or rank of a corporal.  It refers to the collective body of corporals, or the term of service as a corporal.|He held the corporalship for two years before being promoted. 他担任下士职务两年后晋升了。|Synonyms: rank, position, grade|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider lower or higher ranks, e.g., private, sergeant)|noun
