aumery:/ˈɔːməri/ | n. |碗橱，餐具柜；圣器室 |A cupboard or small room, especially in a monastery or church, used for storing vessels, vestments, or other sacred objects. Also, a cupboard for storing dishes and household goods. | The aumery held the chalices and patens for the Mass. 圣器室里存放着弥撒用的圣杯和圣盘。

The aumery was well-stocked with plates and bowls. 碗橱里装满了盘子和碗。 |Synonyms: cupboard, pantry, larder, ambry | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "emptiness" in a figurative sense) | noun
