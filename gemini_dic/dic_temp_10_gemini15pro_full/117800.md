melini:/məˈlini/| n.|蜜糖；甜酒；(Melini)梅利尼（姓氏）|A sweet wine, often made from honey.  Melini is also a surname. |例句：She sipped the sweet melini. 她小口啜饮着甜美的蜜糖酒。

例句：Mr. Melini is a renowned winemaker. 梅利尼先生是一位著名的酿酒师。|近义词：honey wine, mead, nectar|反义词: vinegar,  bile |词性:noun
