groutiness:/ˈɡraʊtinəs/
|n.| 泥浆状；灰浆状；粗糙 |The groutiness of the mixture made it difficult to spread. 混合物呈泥浆状，难以涂抹。|
|近义词: coarseness, roughness, lumpiness|
|反义词: smoothness, fineness|
|词性: noun|

词源: From grout + -iness.  "Grout" comes from Old English "grūt" meaning "coarse meal."
