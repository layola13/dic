hilted:/ˈhɪltɪd/| adj.|带柄的，有柄的|Having a hilt (the handle of a sword, dagger, or other bladed weapon).|The knight drew his hilted sword and charged into battle. 骑士拔出他的带柄剑冲入战场。|Near synonyms: handled, gripped.  There isn't a direct opposite for "hilted," but you could consider "hiltless" (though rare).|adjective
