nestled:/ˈnesəld/| adj.|依偎的，舒适地坐落的|Positioned comfortably and snugly, often in a protected or sheltered spot.  Suggesting a feeling of security and comfort.|The cottage was nestled in the valley. 小屋依偎在山谷中。|近义词：snug, cozy, sheltered, tucked away|反义词:exposed, unsheltered|词性:形容词
Etymology: From nest + -le (frequentative suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *nestlian, frequentative of nestian “to build a nest.”
