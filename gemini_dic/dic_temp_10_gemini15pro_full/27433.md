camlet:/ˈkæmlɪt/| n.| 锦缎，骆驼毛织物 |A fine fabric made of wool, silk, or hair, often used for clothing or upholstery. Historically, it was often made with camel or goat hair, hence the name. |She wore a gown of shimmering camlet. 她穿着一件闪亮的锦缎长袍。| The antique chair was upholstered in a rich camlet. 这把古董椅子用华丽的锦缎装饰。 | 近义词: damask, satin, brocade | 反义词: burlap, canvas | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *camelot*, from Italian *camelotto*, probably ultimately from Arabic *ḵaml* meaning "camel". The fabric was originally made with camel hair.
