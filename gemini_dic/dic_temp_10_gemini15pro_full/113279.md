lutein:/ˈluːtiːn/| n.|叶黄素|A yellow carotenoid pigment found in the retina of the eye, egg yolk, and many plants, believed to protect the eye from damage.|例句：Lutein is important for maintaining eye health. 叶黄素对维持眼睛健康很重要。

例句：Foods rich in lutein may help prevent macular degeneration. 富含叶黄素的食物可能有助于预防黄斑变性。|近义词：xanthophyll|反义词:|noun
