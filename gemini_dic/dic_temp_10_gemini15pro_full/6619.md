anadromous:/əˈnædrəməs/
adjective
溯河产卵的；溯河洄游的
(of a fish) migrating up rivers from the sea to spawn.
Salmon are anadromous fish, swimming upstream to spawn. 鲑鱼是溯河产卵的鱼类，会逆流而上产卵。
The anadromous behavior of these fish is fascinating to observe. 观察这些鱼类的溯河洄游行为令人着迷。
近义词: potamodromous (migrating within fresh water only)
反义词: catadromous (migrating down rivers to the sea to spawn)
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek anadromos "running upward," from ana "up" + dromos "running."
