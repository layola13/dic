importable:/ɪmˈpɔːrtəbl/ | adj. | 可进口的，可输入的 | Able to be brought in from a foreign country; able to be transferred or copied into a computer system. |  The software makes data importable from various sources.  该软件可以从各种来源导入数据。

The country's importable goods include electronics and textiles.  该国的进口商品包括电子产品和纺织品。 |  Synonyms: admissible, transferable, acceptable  | Antonyms: non-importable, inadmissible, unexportable | adjective
