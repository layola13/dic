collocution:/ˌkɒləˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 搭配；词语搭配；惯用语 | A collocation is a combination of words that are commonly used together. It's about the way words naturally go together.  |  Example: "Heavy rain" is a common collocation, whereas "strong rain" sounds unnatural.  暴雨是一个常见的搭配，而强雨听起来不自然。

Example:  "Make a mistake" is a typical collocation.  “犯错误”是一个典型的搭配。 | 近义词: combination, phrase, expression | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
