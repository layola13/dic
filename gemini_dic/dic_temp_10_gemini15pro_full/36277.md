cohabitor: /koʊˈhæbɪtər/ (US) /kəʊˈhæbɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |同居者，同住人|A person who lives with another, especially a romantic partner, without being married.|例句：My cohabitor and I share the rent and household chores. 我和我的同居者分摊房租和家务。

例句：They were cohabitors for five years before they decided to get married.  他们在结婚前同居了五年。

近义词：housemate, roommate, partner, live-in partner|反义词: spouse, husband, wife |词性:noun
