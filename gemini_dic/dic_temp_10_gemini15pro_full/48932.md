defuelled:/diːˈfjuːəld/
verb
卸除燃料，清空燃料
To remove fuel from something, such as an aircraft or vehicle.
The aircraft was defuelled before maintenance.  飞机在维护前被卸除了燃料。
The racing car was defuelled after the race. 赛车在比赛结束后被清空了燃料。
synonym: drain, empty
antonym: fuel, refuel
verb
