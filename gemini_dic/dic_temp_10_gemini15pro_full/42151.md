couch: /kaʊtʃ/ | n. | 沙发；长椅；卧榻；诊察台 | A piece of furniture for seating two or more people, typically upholstered and with a back and arms. |  I fell asleep on the couch. 我在沙发上睡着了。 | sofa, settee | bed, chair | noun
