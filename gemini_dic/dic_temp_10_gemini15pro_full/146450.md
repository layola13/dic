overfreighting:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪtɪŋ/
noun
超载，过度装载
The act of loading a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle with more cargo than is safe or permitted.
The ship sank due to overfreighting. 这艘船因超载而沉没。
overloading, overburdening
underloading
