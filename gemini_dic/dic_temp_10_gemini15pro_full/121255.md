misestimates:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪts/
| v. |错误估计，低估，高估 |To estimate something wrongly or inaccurately. |She misestimated the cost of the project. 她错误估计了项目的成本。|overestimates, underestimates, miscalculates, misjudges | accurately estimates, correctly calculates |verb

misestimate:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/
| n. |错误的估计，估错 |An inaccurate or incorrect estimation. |The budget overruns were due to a misestimate of the project's complexity. 预算超支是由于对项目复杂性估计错误造成的。|overestimation, underestimation, miscalculation | accurate estimate, correct calculation |noun
