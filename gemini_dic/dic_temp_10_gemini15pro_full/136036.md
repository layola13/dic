nonreasonableness:/nɒnˈriːzənəblənəs/
noun
不合理，不讲理
lack of reasonableness; the state of being unreasonable
The nonreasonableness of his demands led to the breakdown of negotiations.  他的要求不合理导致谈判破裂。
Near Synonyms:  unreasonableness, irrationality, illogicality, absurdity
Antonyms: reasonableness, rationality, logic, sense
词性: 名词
