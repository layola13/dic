compensate:/ˈkɒmpənseɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪt/ (US)| v. |补偿，赔偿，弥补|to give something to someone in exchange for something they have lost or damaged or for some inconvenience |The company will compensate employees for any travel expenses. 公司将补偿员工的任何差旅费用。|synonyms: recompense, remunerate, indemnify, offset|antonyms: penalize, forfeit|verb
