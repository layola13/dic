explain:/ɪkˈspleɪn/ | v. | 解释，说明 | To make something clear or easy to understand by describing or giving information about it. |  I explained the rules of the game to her. 我向她解释了游戏规则。

She explained that she had been delayed by traffic. 她解释说她因为交通堵塞而迟到了。

|Synonyms: clarify, elucidate, interpret, expound|Antonyms: confuse, obscure, complicate|verb

PHRASE->
explain away:  搪塞；把…解释过去
He tried to explain away his mistake by blaming others. 他试图通过责怪别人来搪塞他的错误。

explain oneself:  解释自己的行为或言语
I demand that you explain yourself! 我要求你解释你的行为！

explain to someone: 向某人解释
Please explain to me why you were late. 请向我解释你为什么迟到。

explain something to someone: 向某人解释某事
Can you explain this concept to me? 你能向我解释一下这个概念吗？

explain that: 解释说
He explained that he had been stuck in traffic. 他解释说他堵车了。
