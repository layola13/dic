owelty:/ˈoʊəlti/| n.|均等金，平衡金，差价补偿金|A sum of money paid to one of the co-owners of property to equalize the shares, especially in the case of partition.|Example:  The court ordered the payment of owelty to ensure a fair division of the inherited land. 法院下令支付均等金，以确保公平分配继承的土地。

Example:  He received owelty as compensation for receiving the smaller portion of the property. 他收到差价补偿金，以弥补他获得较小部分财产的损失。|近义词：compensation, equalization payment|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps deficit or imbalance) |词性:noun
