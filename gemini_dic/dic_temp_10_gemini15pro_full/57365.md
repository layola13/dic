doxological:/dɒksəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adj.
赞美的，颂神的
Relating to or expressing praise, especially to God.
The congregation sang a doxological hymn.  会众唱了一首赞美神的圣诗。
近义词:laudatory, praising, hymnic
反义词:blasphemous, critical, profane
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek doxologia "a praising, glorification," from doxologos "giving glory," from doxa "glory, splendor, praise" + -logos "speaking."
