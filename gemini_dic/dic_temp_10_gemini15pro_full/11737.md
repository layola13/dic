aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 芳香的；芬芳的；有香味的 | Having a pleasant and distinctive smell. | The air was aromatic with the scent of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的香味。 | fragrant, sweet-smelling, perfumed | odorless, fetid | adjective

aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/ | n. | 芳香植物；芳香物质 | A plant, substance, or chemical that has a pleasant smell. | Many herbs and spices are aromatics. 许多草药和香料都是芳香植物。| fragrance, perfume, scent | stench, stink | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aromaticus, from Greek aromatikos (“fragrant, spicy”), from aroma (“spice, fragrant herb”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂er- (“to fit together”).
