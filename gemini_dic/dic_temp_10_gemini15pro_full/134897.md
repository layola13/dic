nonmulched:/ˌnɒnˈmʌltʃt/
adjective
未覆盖的，无护根物的
not covered with mulch (a protective layer of material spread on the ground around plants).
The nonmulched garden bed dried out quickly in the summer heat.  夏天的高温很快使未覆盖护根物的苗床干涸了。
The nonmulched soil was susceptible to erosion. 未覆盖护根物的土壤容易被侵蚀。
Synonyms: uncovered, bare, exposed
Antonyms: mulched, covered, protected
词性: 形容词
