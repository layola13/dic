lithofellic: /ˌlɪθəˈfɛlɪk/
adjective
亲岩的，喜岩的
Having an affinity for rocks; rock-dwelling.  Specifically refers to organisms that live in or on rocks.
Lichens are lithofellic organisms, thriving on the surfaces of rocks.  地衣是亲岩生物，在岩石表面茁壮生长。
Certain types of mosses are also lithofellic, finding a foothold on rocky terrain.  某些类型的苔藓也是亲岩的，在岩石地形上找到了立足点。
近义词:  rock-dwelling, rupicolous, saxicolous
反义词:  aquatic, terrestrial (in a broader sense)
词性: adjective
词源:  From Greek "litho-" (stone) + "-phile" (loving).
