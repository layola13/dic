mistrusters:/mɪsˈtrʌstərz/
n.
不信任者，怀疑者
People who mistrust others; those who lack trust or confidence.
The mistrusters questioned the motives behind the new policy.  不信任者质疑新政策背后的动机。
The group of mistrusters were hesitant to invest in the project. 这群怀疑者对投资该项目犹豫不决。
近义词: skeptics, doubters, cynics
反义词: believers, trusters
词性: 名词(plural)

mistrust:/mɪsˈtrʌst/
n.
不信任，怀疑
Lack of trust or confidence.
There is a growing mistrust of politicians. 对政客的不信任感日益加剧。
His mistrust of her was evident in his tone. 他对她的不信任在他的语气中很明显。
v.
不信任，怀疑
To regard with distrust or suspicion.
She mistrusted his motives for helping her. 她不信任他帮助她的动机。
He mistrusted anyone who offered him something for nothing.  他不信任任何给他免费东西的人。
近义词: suspicion, doubt, disbelief
反义词: trust, faith, confidence
词性: 名词, 动词

词源:  From Middle English *mistrusten, from mis- + trusten (“to trust”). Equivalent to mis- +‎ trust.
