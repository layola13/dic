gonophore:/ˈɡɒnəfɔːr/ /ˈɡɑːnəfɔːr/ | n. |生殖体；生殖枝|A specialized branch or structure bearing reproductive organs, especially in hydroids and siphonophores.|The gonophore of the hydra releases gametes into the water. 水螅的生殖体将配子释放到水中。|Synonym: reproductive organ, sporosac |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms related to non-reproductive structures, such as somatic cells) |noun

gonophore:/ˈɡɒnəfɔːr/ /ˈɡɑːnəfɔːr/ | n. |生殖枝|A specialized branch in certain plants, particularly some algae, that bears reproductive structures. |The researchers studied the development of gonophores in the seaweed species. 研究人员研究了海藻物种中生殖枝的发育。|Synonym: reproductive branch, fertile branch|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps vegetative branch)|noun
