archaeologically:/ˌɑːrkiəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb|考古学上地，从考古学的角度|
English Explanation: In a way that relates to archaeology, the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.
例句：The site was archaeologically significant.  这个遗址具有重要的考古学意义。

例句：The findings were interpreted archaeologically. 这些发现是从考古学角度进行解读的。

近义词：archaeologically
反义词:|无明确反义词|
词性:adverb
