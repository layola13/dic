nonpreferential:/ˌnɒnprɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 非优惠的，无差别待遇的 | Not showing or involving preference; not preferential.  Relating to trade or tariffs that are applied equally to all countries. |  Imports from the country are subject to nonpreferential tariffs.  该国进口商品需缴纳非优惠关税。 | The agreement ensures nonpreferential treatment for all members.  该协议确保所有成员国都享受无差别待遇。 | Synonyms: nondiscriminatory, impartial, unbiased, equitable | Antonyms: preferential, discriminatory, biased, partial | adjective
