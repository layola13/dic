hyperight: /haɪpəˈraɪt/ |adj.|过度兴奋的，极度正确的|Excessively or extremely right, often to the point of being self-righteous or intolerant of other viewpoints.|He was so hyperight about his political views that no one could have a reasonable discussion with him. 他对自己的政治观点如此固执己见，以至于没有人能和他进行合理的讨论。 |例句：She became hyperight about recycling after watching the documentary.  看完纪录片后，她对回收利用变得极其热衷。|近义词：dogmatic, inflexible, rigid, unyielding|反义词: open-minded, flexible, tolerant|词性:形容词

词源:  "Hyper-" (meaning "over, excessive") + "right" (correct).  This word is a relatively recent coinage and not yet widely established in standard dictionaries.  It carries a connotation of excessive righteousness, often to a fault.  "Hyper-" 来自希腊语，意为“超过，过度”；"right" 意为“正确的”。这个词是相对较新的造词，尚未在标准词典中广泛使用。它带有过度正义的含义，通常带有贬义。
