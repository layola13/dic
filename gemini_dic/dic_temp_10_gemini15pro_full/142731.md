n.
耳鼻喉科医生
A medical specialist who diagnoses and treats diseases of the ear, nose, and throat.
例句：I went to see an otolaryngologist about my persistent sore throat. 我去看耳鼻喉科医生治疗我持续的喉咙痛。

例句：The otolaryngologist performed surgery to remove the patient's tonsils.  耳鼻喉科医生进行了手术以切除患者的扁桃体。

近义词：ENT doctor, ENT specialist
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a medical specialist)
词性: noun
