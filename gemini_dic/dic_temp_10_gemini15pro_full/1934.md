actinophonic: /ˌæktɪnəˈfɒnɪk/
adjective
光音的，光致发声的
Relating to or exhibiting actinophonism; producing sound by the action of radiant energy, especially light.
The researchers developed an actinophonic device that could generate sound from light pulses.  研究人员开发了一种光音装置，可以从光脉冲中产生声音。

The actinophonic properties of the material were tested using a laser.  使用激光测试了该材料的光音特性。

synonym: photoacoustic, optoacoustic
antonym: N/A
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From actino- (relating to rays or radiation) + -phonic (relating to sound).  Actino- comes from the Greek *aktīn-, *aktīnos (ray). -phonic comes from the Greek phōnē (sound, voice).
