anteversion:/ˌæntiˈvɜːrʒən/
noun
前倾，前倾姿势
The abnormal forward tilting of an organ or part.
Example: Anteversion of the uterus is a normal anatomical variant. 子宫前倾是一种正常的解剖变异。
Example:  The doctor noted the patient's pelvic anteversion. 医生注意到病人的骨盆前倾。
Synonyms: tilting forward, forward inclination
Antonyms: retroversion, tilting backward
