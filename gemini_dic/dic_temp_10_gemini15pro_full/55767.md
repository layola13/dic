diversifiers:/dɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪərz/| n. |多元化因素，分散投资的对象|Something that diversifies, especially a business or investment that is intended to reduce risk by spreading it over different areas.|The company is looking at new diversifiers in the tech industry.  这家公司正在科技行业寻找新的多元化投资对象。|Synonyms: investments, assets, holdings, securities|Antonyms: concentrations, specializations|noun

diversify:/dɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/| v. |使多样化，分散投资|Make or become more diverse or varied.|The company is looking to diversify its product range.  这家公司正在寻求使其产品范围多样化。|Synonyms: vary, expand, broaden, branch out|Antonyms: concentrate, specialize, limit, restrict|verb

diversified:/dɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/ | adj. |多样化的，多元化的|Varied or different.|The company has a diversified portfolio of investments.  这家公司拥有多元化的投资组合。|Synonyms: varied, assorted, multifaceted, heterogeneous|Antonyms: homogeneous, uniform, concentrated, specialized|adjective
