girandole:/ˌʒɪrənˈdoʊl/ /ˌdʒɪrənˈdoʊl/| n.|枝形烛台；旋转焰火；簇状宝石饰物|A girandole is a branched candlestick or light fixture, often ornate and holding several candles or lamps. It can also refer to a revolving firework or a cluster of gemstones arranged in a similar branching pattern.|The dining room was lit by a magnificent silver girandole. 餐厅里点着一盏华丽的银枝形烛台。|Synonyms: candelabra, chandelier, sconce|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
