lichenology:/laɪkəˈnɒlədʒi/ /laɪkəˈnɑːlədʒi/
n.
地衣学
The study of lichens.
Lichens are fascinating organisms, and lichenology is a complex field.  地衣是迷人的生物体，地衣学是一个复杂的领域。
He is a renowned expert in lichenology. 他是地衣学领域的著名专家。
近义词:  phycology (藻类学), mycology (真菌学)
反义词:  N/A
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  Greek  *leikhēn* (lichen) + *-logia* (study of).
