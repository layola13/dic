cubed:/kjuːbd/
adjective|立方的，三次幂的|raised to the third power; having the form of a cube|例句：The recipe calls for a cup of cubed potatoes.  食谱需要一杯土豆丁。|近义词：diced, three-dimensional|反义词: (no direct antonym)
verb|切成小方块，使成立方形|to cut into cubes; to raise to the third power|例句：The chef cubed the watermelon.  厨师把西瓜切成丁。|近义词：dice, cut into cubes |反义词: (no direct antonym)
noun|立方体，三次方|the result of multiplying a number by itself three times|例句：The cubed of 5 is 125.  5的立方是125。|近义词：cube, third power|反义词: (no direct antonym)
词性|形容词，动词，名词
