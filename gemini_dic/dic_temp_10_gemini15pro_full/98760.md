intagliated:/ˌɪnˈtæliˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
雕刻的，阴刻的
Engraved or carved as a design into a surface, so that the design is below the surrounding surface level.
The intagliated design on the ring was intricate and beautiful.  戒指上雕刻的图案复杂而美丽。
The artist specialized in intagliated gemstones. 这位艺术家擅长雕刻宝石。
近义词:engraved, carved, incised, etched
反义词:raised, embossed, relief
词性:adjective
