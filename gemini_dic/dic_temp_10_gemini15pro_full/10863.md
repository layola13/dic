arbor:/ˈɑːrbər/ (US), /ˈɑːbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|凉亭，树荫处|A shady garden shelter, usually made of rustic wood or latticework and covered with climbing plants.|We sat in the arbor, enjoying the shade and the scent of honeysuckle. 我们坐在凉亭里，享受着树荫和金银花的香味。|近义词：pergola, gazebo, bower|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open area" or "exposed area")|名词
