misadventures:/ˌmɪs.ədˈvɛn.tʃəz/
|n.|不幸的遭遇；倒霉事；厄运|An unfortunate or slightly amusing incident.|例句：She recounted her misadventures in trying to find the right apartment. 她讲述了她寻找合适公寓时的种种不幸遭遇。

例句：His trip was filled with comic misadventures. 他的旅行充满了滑稽的倒霉事。
|近义词：mishaps, misfortunes, miscalculations|反义词：fortunes, successes|词性：名词（复数）
