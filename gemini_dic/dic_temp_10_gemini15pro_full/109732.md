lever:/ˈliːvər/ (US) /ˈliːvə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |杠杆；控制杆；手段，方法|A rigid bar resting on a pivot, used to help move a heavy or firmly fixed load with one end when pressure is applied to the other.|She used a lever to pry open the crate. 她用杠杆撬开了板条箱。|近义词: crowbar, handle, tool, mechanism, instrument, means|反义词: impediment, obstacle|n.
lever:/ˈliːvər/ (US) /ˈliːvə(r)/ (UK)| v. |撬动，撬起|To move (something) with a lever.|He levered the rock out of the hole. 他用杠杆把石头撬出了洞。|近义词: pry, lift, raise, heave|反义词: press, push, lower|v.
