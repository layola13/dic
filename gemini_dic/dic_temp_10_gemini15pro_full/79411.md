gling:/ɡlɪŋ/ | n. | 铃声， jingle (拟声词) |A high-pitched, resonant ringing sound. |The gling of the tiny bells on her bracelet was barely audible. 她手镯上的小铃铛的叮当声几乎听不见。|近义词：jingle, chime, ring, tinkling |反义词: silence, hush |词性:名词


词源:  Likely imitative or onomatopoeic, echoing the sound it represents.  类似于其他表示铃铛声的词，如 "jingle" 和 "tingle"，"gling" 很可能是拟声词，模仿了它所代表的声音。
