halogenic:/ˌhæləˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |卤素的| Relating to or containing a halogen. |例句：The halogenic compounds are widely used in industry. 卤素化合物在工业中被广泛使用。

例句：Chlorine is a halogenic element used in water purification.  氯是一种用于净水的卤素元素。

近义词：halogen, halide |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-halogenic) | 词性: adjective
