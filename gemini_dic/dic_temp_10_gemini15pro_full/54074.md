dissected:/daɪˈsektɪd/
adjective
解剖的，仔细分析的
Carefully divided into parts for analysis.
The frog was dissected in biology class.  青蛙在生物课上被解剖了。
The lawyer dissected the witness's testimony. 律师仔细分析了证人的证词。
Synonyms: analyzed, examined, scrutinized
Antonyms: synthesized, combined, whole
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin dissectus, past participle of dissecare "to cut apart," from dis- "apart" + secare "to cut."
