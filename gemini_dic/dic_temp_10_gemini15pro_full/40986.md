coordinating:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective|协调的，同等的|arranging something so that it works well together with other things; of equal importance or rank in a sentence|例句：Coordinating conjunctions connect words, phrases, or clauses of equal grammatical rank. 并列连词连接语法等级相同的词、短语或从句。|例句：She's responsible for coordinating the work of several committees. 她负责协调几个委员会的工作。|近义词：harmonizing, synchronizing, integrating, collaborating|反义词：conflicting, clashing|词性: adjective

coordinating:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb|协调，配合| (present participle of coordinate) to make different things work effectively as a whole|例句：We need someone to coordinate the whole campaign. 我们需要有人来协调整个活动。|例句：They appointed a new manager to coordinate the work of the team. 他们任命了一位新的经理来协调团队的工作。|近义词：organize, manage, harmonize, synchronize|反义词：disorganize, disrupt|词性: verb
