noncolorfast:/ˌnɒnˈkʌlərfɑːst/ | adj. | 不固色的，易褪色的 | Not resistant to fading or running of color, especially when wet or exposed to light. |  This shirt is noncolorfast, so wash it separately. 这件衬衫不固色，所以要单独洗。| The noncolorfast curtains faded quickly in the bright sunlight.  不固色的窗帘在强烈的阳光下很快就褪色了。 | 近义词: non-fast, fugitive (color) | 反义词: colorfast, colourfast | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- + color + fast.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Color" refers to the dye or pigment. "Fast" in this context means "firmly fixed or attached."
