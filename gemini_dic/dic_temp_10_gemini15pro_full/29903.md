cautionary:/ˈkɔːʃənəri/ | adj. | 警告的；告诫的；提醒注意的 | Serving as a warning. | The government issued a cautionary statement about the dangers of smoking. 政府发布了一份关于吸烟危害的警告声明。| The museum posted cautionary signs about the wet floor. 博物馆张贴了小心地滑的警告标志。 |  近义词: warning, admonitory, advisory | 反义词: encouraging, reassuring | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *cautionarius, from Latin cautio (“caution”).
