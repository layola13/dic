mushroomed:/ˈmʌʃ.ruːmd/ /ˈmʌʃ.rʊmd/| v. |迅速增长；迅速发展；如雨后春笋般涌现|To grow or expand rapidly, like mushrooms after a rain.|例句：The town's population mushroomed after the new factory opened.  小镇的人口在新工厂开业后迅速增长。

例句：Online businesses have mushroomed in recent years.  近年来，在线业务迅速发展。|近义词：proliferated, burgeoned, expanded, exploded, boomed|反义词：dwindled, decreased, shrunk, contracted|词性：动词
