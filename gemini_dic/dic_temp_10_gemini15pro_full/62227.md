encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ /ɛnˈkroʊtʃ/ | v. |侵入，侵占；蚕食|To advance gradually beyond usual or acceptable limits.|The new shopping mall encroached on the local wetlands. 新建的购物中心侵占了当地的湿地.|synonyms: intrude, infringe, trespass, impinge | antonyms: retreat, withdraw | verb

encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ /ɛnˈkroʊtʃ/ | n. |侵犯，侵占|An act of encroaching.|The encroachment of the sea is threatening the coastal village.  海洋的侵蚀正威胁着沿海村庄。|synonyms: intrusion, infringement, trespass | antonyms: retreat, withdrawal | noun
