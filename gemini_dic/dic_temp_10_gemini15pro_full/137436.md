nontheological:/ˌnɒnθiːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adj.
非神学的；非宗教的
Not related to or based on theology; secular.
His approach to the subject was strictly nontheological. 他对这个主题的探讨完全是非神学的。
近义词:secular, nonreligious, lay
反义词:theological, religious
词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, meaning "not".
theological (adjective): From Late Latin theologicus, from Greek theologikos, from theologia "theology".
