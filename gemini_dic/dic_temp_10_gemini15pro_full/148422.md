overstep:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛp/ | v. | 超越；逾越；超出…的限度 | to go beyond the limits of what is considered acceptable or appropriate |  He overstepped the mark when he criticized the boss. 他批评老板的举动过分了。

| Synonym: exceed, transgress, infringe | Antonym: observe, respect, adhere to | verb

overstep:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛp/ | n. | 超越；逾越 | An act of going beyond the limits of what is considered acceptable or appropriate. | His comments were a clear overstep of his authority. 他的评论明显超出了他的权限。| Synonym: transgression, violation, breach | Antonym: observance, compliance | noun
