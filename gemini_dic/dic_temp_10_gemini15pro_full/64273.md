equisetaceous:/ˌɛkwɪsɪˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
木贼科的；像木贼的
Resembling or relating to the horsetails (genus *Equisetum*), a group of vascular plants with hollow, jointed stems.
The fossil revealed a distinctly equisetaceous stem structure. 这块化石显示出明显的木贼科茎结构。
The swamp was dominated by tall, equisetaceous plants. 沼泽里主要生长着高大的木贼科植物。
synonyms: horsetail-like
antonyms: (No direct antonyms exist, but one could use descriptions like "non-equisetaceous" or describe a plant that is distinctly different, like "fern-like" or "moss-like" depending on the context.)
词性: adjective
