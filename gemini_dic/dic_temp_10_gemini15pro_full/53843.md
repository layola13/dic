disconsonant:/dɪsˈkɑːnsənənt/ /dɪsˈkɒnsənənt/ | adj. | 不和谐的，不一致的 | Lacking harmony; incongruous; clashing. |  The disconsonant chords clashed and created an unpleasant sound.  不和谐的和弦冲突，产生了刺耳的声音。| 近义词: discordant, inharmonious, incongruous, incompatible, jarring, clashing | 反义词: consonant, harmonious, agreeable, compatible, concordant | 词性: 形容词

disconsonance:/dɪsˈkɑːnsənəns/ /dɪsˈkɒnsənəns/ | n. | 不和谐，不一致 |  A lack of harmony or agreement; discord. | There was a disconsonance between her words and her actions. 她的言行不一致。 | 近义词: discord, disagreement, incongruity, incompatibility, dissonance | 反义词: consonance, harmony, agreement, compatibility, concord | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dissonant- ‘sounding differently’, from the verb dissonare, from dis- ‘apart’ + sonare ‘to sound’.
