foresights:/ˈfɔːrsaɪts/| n. |远见，先见之明，预见| the ability to predict or the action of predicting what will happen or be needed in the future. |例句：Her foresights in buying the land proved to be correct. 她当初买下这块地的远见卓识被证明是正确的。

例句：The company's lack of foresights led to its downfall. 该公司缺乏远见导致了它的衰败。|近义词：vision, foresight, prudence, anticipation, precaution |反义词:shortsightedness, myopia, imprudence |词性:noun (plural)
