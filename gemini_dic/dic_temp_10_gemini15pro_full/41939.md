cosmopolis:/kɒzˈmɒpəlɪs/ |/kɑːzˈmɑːpəlɪs/| n. |国际大都市；世界之都|A city inhabited by people from many different countries.  A center of culture, commerce, and intellectual activity, often characterized by a diverse and sophisticated population.|例句：New York is often described as a cosmopolis, a melting pot of cultures from around the world. 纽约常被描述为一个国际大都市，一个来自世界各地文化的熔炉。

例句：London, a true cosmopolis, attracts people from all walks of life.  伦敦，一个真正的世界之都，吸引着各行各业的人。|近义词：metropolis, megacity, global city|反义词：province, rural area|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe, order”) + πόλις (pólis, “city”).
