amorphy:/əˈmɔːrfi/ | n. |无定形，无定形性 |Lack of definite form; shapelessness. | The amorphy of the clay allowed the sculptor to mold it into any shape.  这块黏土的无定形性质使得雕塑家可以把它塑造成任何形状。|
|Synonyms: formlessness, shapelessness, indefiniteness | Antonyms: structure, form, crystallinity | noun
