crosswords:/ˈkrɒswɜːdz/ (UK), /ˈkrɔːswɜːrdz/ (US)| n.|纵横字谜; 纵横填字游戏|A puzzle in which words corresponding to numbered clues are to be fitted into a grid of horizontal and vertical squares.|I enjoy doing crosswords on the train. 我喜欢在火车上玩纵横字谜。|近义词：puzzle, word game|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From cross + word + -s.  "Cross-word" originally referred to a type of puzzle where the words crossed each other, and later evolved to specifically mean the type of word puzzle we know today.
