non-specialized:/nɒnˈspɛʃəlaɪzd/ /nɑːnˈspɛʃəlaɪzd/| adj.|非专门化的，非专业的，通用的|Not limited to a particular area of expertise or function; general.  Suitable for or used in a variety of situations or for general purposes.|This course offers a non-specialized introduction to computer science, suitable for students from any background.|近义词：general, generic, universal, all-purpose|反义词：specialized, specific, technical, professional|词性:adjective

词源:  Comes from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") added to "specialized," which itself derives from "special" with the suffix "-ized." Ultimately from the Latin "species" (kind, sort).
