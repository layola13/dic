crawfishing:/ˈkrɔːfɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 捕小龙虾；退缩，食言 | The act of backing out of a commitment; the act of catching crawfish. | His crawfishing made him look unreliable. 他的退缩让他看起来不可靠。 | reneging, retreating | commitment, follow-through | noun
