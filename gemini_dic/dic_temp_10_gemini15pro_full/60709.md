electro-coating:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈkoʊtɪŋ/| n.|电泳涂装，电沉积涂层|A process where electrically charged paint particles are deposited onto a conductive surface. |The car body undergoes electro-coating to prevent rust. 车身经过电泳涂装以防止生锈。|Synonyms: electrodeposition, electrophoretic deposition|Antonyms: N/A|noun
