biprism:/ˈbaɪˌprɪzəm/| n.|双棱镜|An optical device consisting of two thin prisms with their bases joined, used to produce interference patterns.  It effectively acts like two coherent light sources, similar to a double slit.|The scientist used a biprism to demonstrate the wave nature of light. 科学家用双棱镜演示了光的波动性。|Synonyms: Fresnel biprism|Antonyms: N/A|noun
