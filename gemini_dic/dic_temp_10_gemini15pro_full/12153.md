arut: /ˈɑːrʌt/ | n. | Root (植物的根) |The main part of a plant that grows underground, absorbs water and nutrients, and anchors the plant. | The arut of the tree was deep and strong.  这棵树的根又深又壮。 | synonyms: root, rhizome | antonyms: stem, branch, leaf | noun
