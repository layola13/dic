miry:/ˈmaɪri/| adj.|泥泞的；沾满泥的|Covered with deep, sticky mud.|The car got stuck in the miry lane. 汽车陷在了泥泞的小路上。|近义词：muddy, swampy, boggy|反义词：dry, firm, solid|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *mirie, from Old English *myrig, from Proto-Germanic *muzijaz (“soft, swampy”), from Proto-Indo-European *meug- (“slimy, slippery”). Cognate with Dutch mierig (“miry, mucky”), German Moder (“mould, decay”), Swedish mjäka (“to soften, make soft”).
