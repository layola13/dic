businesses:/ˈbɪznɪsɪz/
n.
企业，商业，生意
The act or process of producing, buying, and selling goods or services.
例句：
He runs three successful businesses. 他经营着三家成功的企业。

Many small businesses are struggling during the pandemic. 许多小企业在大流行期间举步维艰。


近义词：
companies, firms, enterprises, ventures, commerces, trades
反义词：
unemployment, idleness, inactivity


词性
名词 (plural of business)
