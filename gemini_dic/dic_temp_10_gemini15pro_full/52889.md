diminutivenesses:/dɪˌmɪnjətɪvnɪsɪz/| n. |微小，渺小（复数）|The state or quality of being extremely small.  It refers to the smallness of size or stature. |The diminutivenesses of the bonsai trees were striking. 这些盆景的微小令人惊叹。| The diminutivenesses of the figurines made them perfect for the dollhouse.  这些小雕像的微小使它们非常适合放在玩具屋里。 |Synonyms: smallness, tininess, minuteness, petiteness|Antonyms: largeness, hugeness, vastness, immensity| noun
