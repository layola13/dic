demi-tint:/ˈdɛmiˌtɪnt/
n.
半色调；浅色调
A pale or subdued tint or shade of a color.
The artist used a demi-tint to create a sense of depth in the painting.  艺术家用半色调来营造画面的深度感。
Synonyms: pastel, mid-tone, wash
Antonyms: vibrant, saturated, bold
noun

demi-tint:/ˈdɛmiˌtɪnt/
n.
半色调印刷；网目版印刷
A method of printing images using dots of varying sizes to simulate continuous tones.
Demi-tint printing was a revolutionary development in the history of graphic arts. 半色调印刷是平面艺术史上的一项革命性发展。
Synonyms: halftone
Antonyms: solid color printing
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French demi-teinte, from demi- "half" + teinte "tint". Ultimately from Latin dimidius "half" and tingere "to dye".
