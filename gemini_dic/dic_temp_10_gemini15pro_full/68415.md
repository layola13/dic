falsies:/ˈfɔːlsiz/
noun
假睫毛
Artificial eyelashes worn to make one's own lashes appear longer and thicker.
She wore falsies to enhance her eyes for the party. 她戴着假睫毛去参加派对，让自己的眼睛看起来更大更有神。
近义词:false eyelashes, eyelash extensions
反义词:natural eyelashes
词性:n.
