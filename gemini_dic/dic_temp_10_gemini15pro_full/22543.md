boorishness:/ˈbʊərɪʃnəs/| n. |粗鲁，粗野，缺乏教养|The boorishness of his behavior embarrassed everyone present. |例句：His boorishness was evident in his loud talking and interrupting others. 他的粗鲁表现在他的大声喧哗和打断别人讲话。

例句：Her boorishness made it difficult for her to make friends. 她的粗鲁让她很难交到朋友。|近义词：rudeness, incivility, crudeness, vulgarity|反义词: politeness, courtesy, refinement, civility|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From boorish + -ness.  Boorish comes from Middle English *borel, from Old French *burel "coarse woolen cloth," hence "person of low birth, peasant," from Late Latin *buralis "of coarse wool."
