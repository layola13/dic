aguila:/ˈɑːɡilə/ (US), /ˈæɡɪlə/ (UK)| n. |鹰|A large bird of prey with a hooked beak and powerful talons, known for its keen eyesight and soaring flight.  From the Latin *aquila*, meaning "eagle".|例句：The aguila soared high above the mountains.  鹰在群山上空翱翔。

The photographer captured a stunning image of the aguila in flight. 摄影师捕捉到了一张鹰在飞行中令人惊叹的图像。

|近义词：eagle, hawk, falcon|反义词:prey|词性:noun
