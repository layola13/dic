advise:/ədˈvaɪz/| vt.|建议，劝告，通知|To offer suggestions, recommendations, or guidance to someone.|I advise you to see a doctor. 我建议你去看看医生。

I strongly advise against going out alone at night. 我强烈建议你不要晚上一个人出去。|近义词：recommend, suggest, counsel, guide|反义词：dissuade, warn against|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aviser* "to consider, reflect, look at," from *a vis* "in view," from Latin *ad visum* "to sight."  The meaning shifted from "to see, consider" to "give an opinion based on consideration" and then to "counsel, recommend."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I advise him to study harder. = 我建议他更加努力学习。
She advises her clients on financial matters. = 她为客户提供财务方面的建议。

一般过去时:
He advised me to take a break. = 他建议我休息一下。
They advised against travelling during the storm. = 他们建议不要在暴风雨期间旅行。

现在完成时:
I have advised him several times to quit smoking. = 我已经多次劝他戒烟了。
She has advised many students on their career choices. = 她已经为很多学生提供了职业选择方面的建议。

过去完成时:
I had advised him to leave before it got dark. = 我曾建议他在天黑前离开。
They had advised against the merger before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻曝光之前，他们就建议不要合并。

将来时:
I will advise him about the best course of action. = 我会建议他采取最佳行动方案。
They will advise us on the legal implications of the contract. = 他们会就合同的法律含义向我们提供建议。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would advise him to reconsider. = 如果我是你，我会建议他重新考虑。
I wish I had advised her to be more careful. = 我希望我曾建议她更加小心。

被动语态:
He was advised to seek legal counsel. = 他被建议寻求法律顾问。
We were advised against making any hasty decisions. = 我们被建议不要做出任何仓促的决定。

疑问句:
Do you advise me to go? = 你建议我去吗？
What do you advise me to do? = 你建议我做什么？

否定句:
I do not advise you to do that. = 我不建议你那样做。
She did not advise us on this matter. = 她没有就此事给我们建议。


PHRASE->
advise against =  建议不要
advise on =  就…提供建议
seek advice = 寻求建议
give advice =  给予建议
take someone's advice = 采纳某人的建议
