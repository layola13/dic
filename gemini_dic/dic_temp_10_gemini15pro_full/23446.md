bradoon:/ˈbreɪduːn/| n.|马衔，轻勒|A bit, typically a snaffle, used in conjunction with a curb bit to control a horse.  A bradoon is a smaller bit used with a double bridle.|The young horse was trained using a bradoon and a cavesson.|近义词：snaffle, bit|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps curb bit as it's part of a contrasting system)|noun
