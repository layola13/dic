inbounded:/ɪnˈbaʊndɪd/| adj.|有界的，受限制的|Bounded or limited within certain limits.  It describes something that has boundaries or restrictions placed upon it.|The inbounded area was clearly marked with fences. 有界区域用栅栏清楚地标出。|近义词：limited, restricted, confined, bounded|反义词:unbounded, unlimited, infinite|词性:形容词
词源: "in-" (prefix meaning "in, within") + "bound" (verb meaning "to set limits to") + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives)
