administrate: /ədˈmɪnɪstreɪt/
verb
管理，支配
To manage or direct the affairs of an organization, institution, etc.
The government administers the social security system. 政府管理社会保障制度。

The manager administrates a large team. 经理管理一个大团队。

近义词: manage, direct, govern, oversee, supervise, control
反义词: neglect, mismanage
词性: verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The council administrates the local schools. = 委员会管理当地学校。
She administrates a large budget. = 她管理一大笔预算。

一般过去时:
He administrated the company for ten years. = 他管理这家公司十年了。
They administrated the project successfully. = 他们成功地管理了这个项目。

现在完成时:
She has administrated the department for five years. = 她管理这个部门已经五年了。
They have administrated several successful projects. = 他们已经管理了几个成功的项目。

过去完成时:
He had administrated the company before it was sold. = 在公司被出售之前，他曾管理过这家公司。
She had administrated the fund for many years before retiring. = 她在退休前管理这笔资金多年。

将来时:
She will administrate the new program. = 她将管理新的项目。
The team will administrate the project next year. = 该团队明年将管理这个项目。

被动语态:
The funds are administrated by a trust. = 这些资金由信托机构管理。
The program is administrated by the government. = 该项目由政府管理。
