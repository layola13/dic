anticonservation:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃən/ | n. |反保护主义，反保守主义| Opposition to the conservation of natural resources, cultural heritage, or traditional values. | Example:  The developer's anticonservation stance led to the destruction of a valuable wetland habitat.  开发商的反保护主义立场导致了宝贵湿地栖息地的破坏。

Example:  Her anticonservation views were met with strong opposition from environmental groups. 她的反保护主义观点遭到了环保组织的强烈反对。| Near Synonyms: exploitation, unsustainable development, vandalism (in some contexts) | Antonyms: conservation, preservation, environmentalism | noun
