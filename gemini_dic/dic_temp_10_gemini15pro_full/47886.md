decemvir: /dɪˈsɛmvər/ | n. | 十人委员会成员；十人执政官 | A member of a body of ten magistrates in ancient Rome. |  The decemvirs were appointed to codify Roman law. 十人委员会被任命编纂罗马法。 |  magistrate, official | autocrat, dictator | noun

decemvirate: /dɪˈsɛmvərət/ | n. | 十人委员会；十人执政官的职位或任期 | The office or term of office of a decemvir; government by a body of ten. | The decemvirate lasted for only two years. 十人执政只持续了两年。 | commission, council | monarchy, autocracy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *decemviri*, plural of *decemvir*, from *decem* ("ten") + *vir* ("man").
