call-outs:/ˈkɔːlˌaʊts/ | n. |标注；注释；插播；大声疾呼|Explanations or annotations added to an image, text, or diagram, often to highlight key features or provide additional information. Can also refer to a brief interruption during a broadcast for an important announcement or a public expression of strong disapproval or support.|The architect's plans included call-outs to explain the complex structural elements.|近义词：annotations, labels, captions, explanations, shout-outs|反义词:|n.

call-out:/ˈkɔːlˌaʊt/ | n. |出动；召唤；点名；挑战|An instance of calling someone out, such as for a task or duty, or to criticize or challenge them publicly.|The fire department received a call-out to a burning building.|近义词：summons, alert, challenge, denunciation|反义词:|n.
