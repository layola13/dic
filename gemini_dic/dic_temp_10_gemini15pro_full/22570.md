bootes:/boʊˈoʊˌtiz/| n.|牧夫座|A constellation in the northern hemisphere near Ursa Major, containing the bright star Arcturus.|例句：Bootes is easy to find in the night sky because of Arcturus. 由于大角星的存在，牧夫座在夜空中很容易被找到。

例句：Ancient Greeks visualized Bootes as a herdsman driving the bears (Ursa Major and Ursa Minor) around the celestial pole. 古希腊人将牧夫座想象成一位牧人，赶着熊(大熊座和小熊座)绕着天极旋转。|近义词：Herdsman (poetic)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
