crimes:/kraɪmz/| n.|罪行，犯罪行为|An act or omission that constitutes an offense that may be prosecuted by the state and is punishable by law. |The police are investigating a series of violent crimes. 警方正在调查一系列暴力犯罪案件。|近义词：offenses, violations, wrongdoings|反义词:good deeds, lawful acts|noun
