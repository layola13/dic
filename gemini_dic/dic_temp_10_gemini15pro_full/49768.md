demonetization:/diːˌmʌnɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.| 废除货币；取消货币的合法地位|The act of stripping a currency unit of its status as legal tender.|The demonetization of 500 and 1000 rupee notes caused chaos in India.  印度500和1000卢比纸币的废除造成了混乱。|devaluation, withdrawal, abolition|monetization, legalization|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "removal or reversal") + monetization (the act of establishing something as legal tender).  Ultimately from Latin moneta ("money"). 来自de-（前缀，表示“移除或逆转”）+ monetization（将某物确立为法定货币的行为）。最终来自拉丁语moneta（“货币”）。
