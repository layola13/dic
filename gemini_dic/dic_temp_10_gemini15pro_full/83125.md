gustoso:/ɡuˈstoʊsoʊ/| adj.|美味的，可口的|Pleasing to the taste; savory.  Derived from the Italian word "gusto" meaning taste or pleasure.|The pasta was gustoso, with a rich tomato sauce.  这道意大利面很美味，配有浓郁的番茄酱。|近义词：delicious, tasty, flavorful, savory, palatable|反义词：unappetizing, bland, tasteless|词性:形容词
词源:From Italian gustoso, from gusto ‘taste, pleasure’.
