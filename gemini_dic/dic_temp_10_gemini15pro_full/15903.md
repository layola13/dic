ballpark:/ˈbɔːlˌpɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbɔːlˌpɑːk/ (UK)| n. |棒球场；大致范围，约略估计|A ballpark is a stadium where baseball is played.  Figuratively, it refers to an approximate range or estimate.|The new stadium is a beautiful ballpark.  新体育场是一个漂亮的棒球场。|synonyms: stadium, arena, field,  approximation, estimate, range|antonyms:  precise figure, exact amount|noun

ballpark:/ˈbɔːlˌpɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbɔːlˌpɑːk/ (UK)| adj. |大概的，大致的|Used figuratively to describe something as approximate or within a general range.|Give me a ballpark figure for the cost of the project.  给我一个项目成本的大概数字。|synonyms: approximate, estimated, rough|antonyms: precise, exact, accurate|adjective
