depleter:/dɪˈpliːtər/| n.|消耗者，损耗者|Someone or something that depletes a resource or substance.  A person or thing that reduces the amount of something.|例句：The ozone layer has been severely damaged by depleters such as chlorofluorocarbons. 臭氧层已被氯氟烃等消耗物质严重破坏。

例句：Overfishing is a major depleter of fish stocks in the ocean. 过度捕捞是海洋鱼类资源的主要消耗因素。|近义词：consumer, user, reducer, exhauster|反义词：replenisher, restorer, supplier|词性: noun
