manslaughters:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərz/| n.|过失杀人(案件);|The unlawful killing of a human being without malice aforethought.  It’s a legal term referring to the taking of a human life without premeditation or malice.|The trial for the multiple manslaughters began this week. 这几起过失杀人案的审判本周开始。|Synonyms: homicide, killing, slaying |Antonyms:  preservation of life|Noun
