fools:/fuːlz/| n.|傻瓜，愚人；受骗者|A fool is a person who acts unwisely or makes foolish decisions.  A person who lacks good sense or judgment. |Those fools lost all their money gambling. 那些傻瓜赌博输光了所有的钱。|synonyms: idiot, moron, imbecile, dunce, simpleton|antonyms: genius, sage, wise person|noun


fools:/fuːlz/| v.|愚弄，欺骗|To trick or deceive someone.|He fooled me into thinking he was a millionaire. 他骗我以为他是个百万富翁。|synonyms: trick, deceive, dupe, cheat, hoax|antonyms: enlighten, inform, undeceive|verb
