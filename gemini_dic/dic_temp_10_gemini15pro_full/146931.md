overintensifications: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌtɛnsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度强化；过度增强 |  The act of intensifying something excessively, often to an unhealthy or counterproductive degree.  This can refer to emotions, behaviors, thoughts, or even physical sensations. |  The overintensifications of her emotions led to burnout.  她的情绪过度强化导致了倦怠。| intensification, exaggeration, amplification | underestimation, minimization | noun

Etymology:  "Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubari "over, above") + "intensification" ("intense" (from Latin intensus "stretched out, strained, violent, vehement, eager") + "-ification" (suffix forming nouns of action or result from verbs ending in -ify)).
