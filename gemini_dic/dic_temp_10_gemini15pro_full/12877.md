assubjugate:/əˈsʌbdʒʊɡeɪt/
|v.|征服， subjugate; 使服从，使屈服|To bring under complete control; conquer; to make someone or something subordinate.|The emperor assubjugated the neighboring countries.  皇帝征服了邻国。|Synonyms: conquer, subdue, vanquish, dominate, enslave|Antonyms: liberate, free, emancipate|verb
assubjugate:/əˈsʌbdʒʊɡeɪt/
|v.|使隶属，使 подчинять; to make subordinate.|The king assubjugated all the smaller kingdoms to his rule. 国王使所有的小王国都臣服于他的统治。|Synonyms: subordinate, subject|Antonyms: empower, elevate|verb
