geochemical:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈkɛmɪkəl/| adj.|地球化学的|Relating to the chemical composition of the Earth and the chemical changes that occur within it.  The study of the distribution and amounts of the chemical elements in minerals, ores, rocks, soils, water, and the atmosphere, and the circulation of the elements in nature.|例句：Geochemical analysis revealed the presence of gold in the rock sample. 地球化学分析揭示了岩石样本中存在金。|例句：The geochemical processes involved in the formation of oil are complex. 形成石油所涉及的地球化学过程非常复杂。|近义词：chemical, geological|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- (earth) + chemical.  "Geo" comes from the Greek word "gē" meaning "earth".
