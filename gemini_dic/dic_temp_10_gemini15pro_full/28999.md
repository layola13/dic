carthorse:/ˈkɑːrtˌhɔːrs/ | n. | 挽马；重型马；（指人）干苦力的人，做单调乏味工作的人 | A large, strong horse used for pulling carts or heavy loads.  A person who does a lot of hard, tedious work. | 例句：The carthorse strained under the weight of the logs. 这匹挽马在原木的重压下吃力地拉着。 | 例句：He felt like a carthorse, working long hours for little reward.  他觉得自己像一匹拉车的马，长时间工作却报酬很少。 | 近义词：workhorse, draught horse | 反义词: racehorse, thoroughbred | 词性: noun
