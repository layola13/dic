obesity:/oʊˈbiːsəti/ /əʊˈbiːsɪti/| n.|肥胖，肥胖症|The condition of being grossly fat or overweight.  A medical condition in which excess body fat has accumulated to the extent that it may have an adverse effect on health.|Obesity is a growing problem in many developed countries. 肥胖是许多发达国家日益严重的问题。|synonyms: corpulence, adiposity, stoutness, portliness, plumpness, chubbiness,  fatness, overweight|antonyms: thinness, slenderness, slimness, leanness|noun
