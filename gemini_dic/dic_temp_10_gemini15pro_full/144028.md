outreproduce:/ˌaʊtrɪprəˈdjuːs/
verb
过度繁殖；繁殖超过
to reproduce at a higher rate than
The rabbits outreproduced the foxes, leading to an imbalance in the ecosystem. 兔子繁殖的速度超过了狐狸，导致生态系统失衡。
outnumber, surpass, exceed
underperform, lag behind
词性:动词
