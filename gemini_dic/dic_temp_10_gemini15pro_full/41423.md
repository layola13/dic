cornering:/ˈkɔːrnərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɔːnərɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |转弯; 垄断; 囤积居奇|The act of driving or riding around a corner, especially at high speed. Also refers to controlling a market supply of a commodity or asset, often to manipulate prices.|He showed impressive skill in cornering at high speeds. 他高速转弯的技巧令人印象深刻。|近义词：turning, rounding, maneuvering, monopolizing, hoarding|反义词:straightening|noun

cornering:/ˈkɔːrnərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɔːnərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |(通常指车辆)转弯的|Relating to the act of turning a corner.|The car's cornering ability was exceptional. 这辆车的转弯性能非常出色。|近义词：turning, curving|反义词:straight|adjective
