fundraiser:/ˈfʌndreɪzər/| n.| 筹款人; 筹款活动;|A person or event involved in collecting money for a particular purpose, especially for a charity.|例句：The school held a fundraiser to build a new library. 学校举办了一场筹款活动，为建造新图书馆筹款。

例句：She's a skilled fundraiser for the Red Cross. 她是一位为红十字会工作的熟练的筹款人。|近义词：benefit, collection, drive|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用spender)|词性: noun
