flanging:/ˈflændʒɪŋ/
n.
镶边，凸缘；（声音的）镶边效果
a sound effect produced especially with electronically amplified music by slightly delaying part of the signal and combining it with the main signal
The song used flanging to create a psychedelic effect. 这首歌用镶边效果来营造迷幻效果。
近义词：phasing, chorus effect
反义词：(No direct antonym for this specific audio effect)
词性：noun

flange:/flændʒ/
n.
凸缘，法兰；轮缘
a projecting flat rim, collar, or rib on an object, serving to strengthen or attach it to something else.
The flange of the wheel prevents it from derailing.  车轮的轮缘防止它脱轨。
近义词：rim, edge, lip
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：noun

flange:/flændʒ/
v.
给…装凸缘；给…镶边
provide with a flange or flanges.
The pipes were flanged together. 管子用凸缘连接在一起。
近义词：rim, edge
反义词：(No direct antonym for this specific verb)
词性：verb
