attirement:/əˈtaɪərmənt/
n.|服装，衣着；盛装|Clothing, apparel; especially fine or decorative clothing.|She chose her attire carefully for the wedding. 她精心挑选了参加婚礼的服装。|Synonyms: clothing, apparel, garb, outfit, dress|Antonyms: undress, nudity|noun

attirement:/əˈtaɪərmənt/
n.|打扮，装饰|The act of attiring or dressing.|The queen's attire was magnificent. 女王的盛装华丽无比。|Synonyms: dressing, arraying|Antonyms: disrobing, undressing|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *atire,* from *atirer* "to equip, dress," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) "to" + *tirer* "to draw, pull," from Vulgar Latin *tirare,* from Latin *trahere* "to draw, pull."  The sense evolution is from "the act of dressing" to "the clothes themselves."
