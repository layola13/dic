chiliastic:/ˌkɪliˈæstɪk/| adj.|千禧年主义的，相信千禧年的|Relating to or believing in the millennium (a period of a thousand years, especially when associated with a future golden age of peace and prosperity).|例句：The group held chiliastic beliefs, expecting the imminent arrival of a utopian era.  这个团体持有千禧年主义信仰，期待着乌托邦时代的即将来临。

例句：The preacher's sermon was filled with chiliastic imagery, describing a world free from suffering.  牧师的布道充满了千禧年主义的意象，描述了一个没有苦难的世界。|近义词：millenarian, apocalyptic|反义词:secular, worldly|词性:adjective
