nonspecialist:/ˌnɒnˈspɛʃəlɪst/ /ˌnɑːnˈspɛʃəlɪst/| n. |非专家，门外汉|A person who does not have special knowledge or training in a particular subject.|This book is written for the nonspecialist.|近义词：layman, amateur, nonprofessional|反义词: specialist, expert, professional|词性:noun

Etymology: non- + specialist.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "specialist" comes from the Latin "specialis" meaning "pertaining to a species," later developing the sense of "particular" or "individual."
