masks:/mɑːsks/ /mæːsks/| n. |面具；口罩；掩饰|A covering for all or part of the face, worn for protection, disguise, performance, or other purposes. |例句：The surgeon wore a mask during the operation.  外科医生在手术过程中戴着口罩。

例句：They wore masks to conceal their identities. 他们戴着面具隐藏身份。|近义词：disguises, coverings, veils, facades|反义词:faces, realities|词性:noun


masks:/mɑːsks/ /mæːsks/| v. |掩盖；遮蔽|To cover or conceal something. |例句：She masked her disappointment with a smile. 她用微笑掩饰了她的失望。

例句：Makeup can be used to mask imperfections.  化妆可以用来掩盖瑕疵。|近义词：conceals, hides, covers, disguises|反义词:reveals, exposes, shows|词性:verb
