bellicose: /ˈbelɪˌkoʊs/ (US) /ˈbelɪkəs/ (UK)| adj.|好战的，好斗的|Inclined or eager to fight; aggressively hostile.|Example 1: His bellicose rhetoric stirred up the crowd. 他的好战言辞激怒了人群。

Example 2: The bellicose nation threatened its neighbors.  这个好战的国家威胁着它的邻国。|近义词：aggressive, hostile, pugnacious, belligerent, warlike|反义词：peaceful, pacifistic, conciliatory, amicable|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bellicosus*, from bellicus (“of war”), from bellum (“war”).
