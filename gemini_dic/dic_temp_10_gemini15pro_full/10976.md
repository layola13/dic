arbitrationist:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃənɪst/
noun
仲裁人，仲裁员
A person who acts as an arbitrator.  One who settles a dispute between two parties.
The arbitrationist listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making a decision. 仲裁员在做出决定之前仔细听取了双方的论点。
The arbitrationist's decision is legally binding. 仲裁员的决定具有法律约束力。
synonyms: arbitrator, arbiter, umpire, judge, mediator
antonyms: litigant, disputant
n.
