misdescribe:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪb/
verb
错误地描述；歪曲；描述失实
to describe something wrongly or inaccurately
The witness misdescribed the getaway car. 目击者错误地描述了逃逸车辆。
misrepresent, distort, falsify
describe accurately, portray truthfully
v.
