amorphous:/əˈmɔːrfəs/
adjective
无定形的，无组织的
Having no definite form or distinct shape; lacking crystalline structure.
例句：
The blob of jelly was amorphous.
这团果冻是无定形的。

Glass is an amorphous solid.
玻璃是一种无定形固体。

近义词：shapeless, formless, structureless, unorganized, indeterminate
反义词：crystalline, definite, structured, organized

词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *amorphus, from Ancient Greek ἄμορφος (ámorphos), from ἀ- (a-, “without”) + μορφή (morphḗ, “form, shape”).
