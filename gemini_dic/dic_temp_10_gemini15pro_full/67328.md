extemporize: /ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪz/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəraɪz/ | v. | 即兴表演，即兴创作 | To compose, perform, or produce something without preparation; improvise. |  He had to extemporize a speech at the wedding. 他不得不在婚礼上即兴发表讲话。

| 近义词: improvise, ad-lib, wing it | 反义词: prepare, rehearse, plan | 词性: verb

extemporization: /ɪkˌstɛmpəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ɛkˌstɛmpərɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 即兴创作，即兴表演 | The act of extemporizing. | His extemporization on the piano was breathtaking. 他在钢琴上的即兴演奏令人叹为观止。| 近义词: improvisation, ad-lib | 反义词: preparation, rehearsal | 词性: noun

extemporaneous: /ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ /ɛkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ | adj. | 即兴的，无准备的 | Done without preparation; improvised. | Her extemporaneous remarks were surprisingly insightful. 她即兴的评论出奇地有见地。 | 近义词: improvised, impromptu, off-the-cuff | 反义词: prepared, rehearsed, planned | 词性: adjective
