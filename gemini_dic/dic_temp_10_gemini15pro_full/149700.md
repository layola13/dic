palacelike: /ˈpælɪsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像宫殿的，宫殿般的
Resembling a palace in grandeur, size, or ornateness.
The mansion was palacelike in its splendor.  这座大厦金碧辉煌，像宫殿一般。
The hotel lobby was palacelike with its high ceilings and marble floors.  酒店大堂有着高高的天花板和大理石地板，像宫殿一样。
近义词: palatial, grand, magnificent, stately, opulent
反义词: humble, modest, simple, plain
