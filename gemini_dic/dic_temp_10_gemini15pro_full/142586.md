osteodystrophy: /ˌɒstiːəʊˈdɪstrəfi/ /ˌɑːstioʊˈdɪstrəfi/ | n. | 骨营养不良 | Defective bone formation or calcification, often due to disturbances in calcium and phosphorus metabolism. |  Renal osteodystrophy is a common complication of chronic kidney disease. 肾性骨营养不良是慢性肾脏病的常见并发症。 |  The child suffered from osteodystrophy due to a vitamin D deficiency.  由于维生素D缺乏，这个孩子患上了骨营养不良。 | synonyms: osteopathy, bone disease | antonyms: healthy bone development | noun
