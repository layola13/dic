competed:/kəmˈpiːtɪd/ | v. | 竞争，比赛 |  past tense and past participle of "compete," meaning to strive to gain or win something by defeating or establishing superiority over others.  |  She competed in the Olympics. 她参加了奥运会比赛。 | He competed against his brother for the scholarship. 他和他的兄弟竞争奖学金。 |  近义词: contended, vied, contested, rivaled | 反义词: cooperated, collaborated | 词性: 动词
