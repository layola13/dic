forelet:/ˈfɔːr.lɪt/
noun
前额带，额饰
A band worn on the forehead.
She wore a jeweled forelet as part of her costume. 她戴着宝石额饰作为她服装的一部分。
synonyms: headband, forehead band, frontlet
antonyms: N/A
