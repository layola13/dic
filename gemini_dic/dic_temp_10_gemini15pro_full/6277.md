amorality:/ˌeɪməˈræləti/
n.
lack of morality; absence of moral standards
The amorality of the business deal shocked investors. 这笔交易缺乏道德让投资者感到震惊。
His amorality is evident in his willingness to exploit others for personal gain. 他为了个人利益愿意剥削他人，这表明他缺乏道德。
unscrupulousness, immorality, dishonesty
morality, principle, virtue
noun
