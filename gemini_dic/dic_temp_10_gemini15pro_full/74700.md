freeing:/ˈfriːɪŋ/
adjective
释放的; 使人自由的
The act of liberating someone or something.  The experience can be emotionally freeing.
She described the experience as incredibly freeing. 她形容这段经历令人难以置信地自由。
liberating, emancipating, releasing
confining, restricting
adjective
