dambose:/dæmˈboʊs/ | n. | 糖醇，达木糖醇 | A crystalline, sweet, alcohol, C5H12O5, formed by the reduction of dambose. It is found in certain plants. | Dambose is a naturally occurring sugar alcohol. 达木糖醇是一种天然存在的糖醇。 | Dambose is used as a sweetener in some foods. 达木糖醇在一些食品中用作甜味剂。| synonyms: sugar alcohol, inositol | antonyms: sugar | noun
