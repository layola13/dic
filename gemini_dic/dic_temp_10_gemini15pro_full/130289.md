non-sustainability:/ˌnɒn səˌsteɪnəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 不可持续性 |The non-sustainability of current farming practices is a major concern.  当前农业实践的不可持续性是一个主要问题。| The report highlighted the non-sustainability of the project given its reliance on finite resources.  鉴于该项目依赖于有限的资源，报告强调了其不可持续性。 | unsustainability, non-viability| sustainability, viability | noun


Etymology->

From non- + sustainability.  Sustainability from sustain (from Old French *sostenir "to hold up," from Latin sustinere "hold up, hold upright; sustain, maintain, endure," from sub- "up from below" + tenere "to hold").
