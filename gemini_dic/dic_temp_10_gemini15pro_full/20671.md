blacktopped:/ˈblækˌtɑːpt/| adj.|铺上柏油的，柏油铺面的|Covered with a surface of asphalt or bitumen.|The driveway was freshly blacktopped.  车道是新铺的柏油路面。|The newly blacktopped road shimmered in the heat. 新铺的柏油路在高温下闪闪发光。|近义词:asphalted, paved, tarmacked|反义词:unpaved, dirt, gravel|词性:形容词

blacktop:/ˈblækˌtɑːp/| n.|柏油路面，沥青路面|A surface paved with asphalt or bitumen.|The children were playing basketball on the blacktop. 孩子们在柏油路面上打篮球。|The blacktop was hot enough to fry an egg on. 柏油路面热得可以在上面煎鸡蛋。|近义词:asphalt, pavement, tarmac|反义词:dirt road, gravel road|词性:名词


词源:  "blacktop" is a compound word formed from "black" and "top."  "Black" refers to the dark color of the asphalt, and "top" refers to the surface layer of the road.
