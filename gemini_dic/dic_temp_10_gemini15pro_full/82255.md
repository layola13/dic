groundednesses:/ˈɡraʊndɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
扎根，根基，稳重
The state of being grounded or well-founded.
Her groundedness in reality helped her through the difficult times.  她在现实中的根基帮助她度过了难关。
groundedness: stability, solidity, firmness
groundedness: flightiness, instability, capriciousness
