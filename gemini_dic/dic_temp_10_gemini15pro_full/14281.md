autograph: /ˈɔːtəɡræf/ (US) /ˈɔːtəɡrɑːf/ (UK)| n. vt.|亲笔签名；手稿；亲笔签名纪念品|A signature, especially that of a celebrity written as a memento for an admirer.  A manuscript in the author's handwriting.|例句：I got his autograph after the concert. 我在音乐会后得到了他的签名。|近义词：signature, handwriting|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 forgery 伪造)|词性: noun, verb

autographs: /ˈɔːtəɡræfs/ (US) /ˈɔːtəɡrɑːfs/ (UK)| n.  vt.|亲笔签名（复数）；手稿（复数）|Plural form of autograph.|例句：She collects autographs of famous athletes. 她收集著名运动员的签名。|近义词：signatures, handwritings|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French autographe, from Greek autos 'self' + graphein 'to write'.
