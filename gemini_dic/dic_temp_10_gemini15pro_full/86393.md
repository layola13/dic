hedgerows:/ˈhedʒˌroʊz/ (US), /ˈhedʒˌrəʊz/ (UK)
n.
树篱；灌木篱墙
A row of bushes or low trees forming a fence or boundary.
The hedgerows were bursting with blossom.  树篱上开满了花。

The birds nested in the hedgerows. 鸟儿在树篱里筑巢。

Synonyms: hedge, thicket, shrubbery
Antonyms: open field, clearing
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English heggerow, from Old English hegerēwe, from hege (“hedge”) + rāw (“row”).
