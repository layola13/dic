malts:/mɔːlts/ | n. |麦芽；麦芽酒；麦芽威士忌| Grain, typically barley, that has been germinated and dried, used in brewing and distilling.  It can also refer to beverages made with malt, like beer or whisky. | The distillery uses only the finest malts. 这家酿酒厂只使用最好的麦芽。 | synonyms: grains, barley (when referring to the grain itself), beer, whiskey, ale (when referring to the beverage) | antonyms: None (for the grain);  perhaps "spirits" or "liquor" in a very broad sense if referring to the beverage and contrasting with other alcoholic drinks. | noun

malts: /mɔːlts/ | v. (third-person singular simple present) | 将…制成麦芽；变成麦芽|  To convert grain into malt. | The brewery malts its own barley. 这家啤酒厂自己制作大麦麦芽。 | synonyms: germinate, sprout | antonyms: None | verb
