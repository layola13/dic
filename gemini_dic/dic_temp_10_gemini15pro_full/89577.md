hoo-hah:/ˈhuːhɑː/ /huːˈhɑː/

词源:
The origin of "hoo-hah" is uncertain. It's thought to be imitative, possibly echoing sounds of excitement or commotion.  It first appeared in the late 19th century, initially as a military slang term.


注意事项:
