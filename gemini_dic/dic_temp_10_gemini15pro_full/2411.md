adjectives:/ˈædʒɪktɪvz/| n.|形容词|A word that describes a noun or pronoun.  It tells you more about the noun or pronoun, like its size, color, shape, or quality.|The small cat sat on the mat.  (Small is an adjective describing the cat.)|Synonyms: descriptors, modifiers|Antonyms: N/A (Adjectives don't have direct antonyms;  opposites depend on the specific adjective.)|Noun


USAGE->
Descriptive Usage:
The red car sped down the street. =  红色的汽车沿着街道疾驰。
The tall building cast a long shadow. = 高大的建筑物投下了长长的阴影。

Limiting Usage:
Several people attended the meeting. =  几个人参加了会议。
This book is mine. = 这本书是我的。

Predicative Usage:
The food is delicious. = 食物很美味。
The weather seems pleasant today. = 今天天气似乎很宜人。

Attributive Usage:
The old house stood on a hill. =  老房子矗立在山上。
She wore a beautiful dress. = 她穿着一件漂亮的连衣裙。

With Linking Verbs:
The flowers smell fragrant. =  花闻起来很香。
He felt tired after the long journey. = 长途旅行后，他感到很累。
