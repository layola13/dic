mono-substituted:/ˌmɒnəʊsəbˈstɪtjuːtɪd/ | adj. | 单取代的 |  A molecule, especially an organic molecule, in which only one hydrogen atom has been replaced by another atom or group. |  Toluene is a mono-substituted benzene derivative. 甲苯是苯的单取代衍生物。| synonyms: monosubstituted | antonyms: polysubstituted, disubstituted | adjective
