interbelligerent:/ˌɪntərˈbɛlɪdʒərənt/
adjective
交战国之间的；战争期间的
Occurring between or involving parties engaged in warfare with each other.
例句：Interbelligerent agreements were signed to allow the exchange of prisoners.  交战双方签署了协议，允许交换战俘。

例句：The interbelligerent commission oversaw the repatriation of refugees. 交战国委员会监督难民的遣返。
synonym: warring, belligerent, hostile
antonym: peaceful, neutral, non-belligerent
词性: 形容词
