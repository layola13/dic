meitnerium:/ˌmaɪtˈnɪəriəm/ | n. |钅麦|A synthetic chemical element with symbol Mt and atomic number 109. It is an extremely radioactive synthetic element (an element not found in nature that can be created in a laboratory). It was first created in 1982.  |Element 109, meitnerium, was first synthesized in 1982.  109号元素钅麦于1982年首次合成。| 近义词: (As it's an element, it doesn't have direct synonyms, but other transuranium elements could be considered related)  transuranium elements| 反义词: None |词性:noun
