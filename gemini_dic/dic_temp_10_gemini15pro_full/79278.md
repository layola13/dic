glaucodot:/ˈɡlɔːkədɒt/| n. |灰钴矿|A mineral, cobalt arsenide sulfide (CoAsS), that typically occurs as orthorhombic crystals.  It is an important ore of cobalt.|Glaucodot is often found in association with other cobalt and nickel minerals. 灰钴矿经常与其他钴和镍矿物伴生。|The glaucodot crystals were small but well-formed. 灰钴矿晶体很小，但形状很好。|Synonyms: alloclasite (partially)|Antonyms: None|Noun
