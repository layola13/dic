excavator:/ˈɛkskəveɪtər/ (US), /ˈɛkskəveɪtə/ (UK)| n.|挖掘机；挖掘者|A large, powerful machine with a bucket and a long arm, used for digging and moving earth and rocks. |The excavator was used to dig the foundation for the new building. 这台挖掘机被用来挖掘新建筑的地基。|Synonyms: digger, backhoe, shovel|Antonyms: filler, backfiller|noun

excavate:/ˈɛkskəveɪt/ (US), /ˈɛkskəveɪt/ (UK)| v.|挖掘；发掘|To dig a hole or channel in the ground, especially with a machine; to uncover something by digging. |Archaeologists excavated the ancient city. 考古学家挖掘了这座古城。|Synonyms: dig, unearth, uncover|Antonyms: bury, fill, cover|verb
