highflier:/ˈhaɪflaɪər/| n.|高飞者；雄心勃勃的人；成功人士|A person who is very successful in their career or who is considered to have the potential to be very successful.|She's a real highflier in the advertising world. 她是一位广告界真正的成功人士。|近义词：achiever, go-getter, ambitious person|反义词：underachiever, failure|词性:名词

Etymology:
From high +‎ flyer, from fly +‎ -er.  Meaning "a bird that flies high" is from 1670s. The figurative sense of "an ambitious person" is from 1950s.
