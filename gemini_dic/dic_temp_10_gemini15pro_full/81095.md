grame:/ɡreɪm/ | n. | 克（质量单位） | A gram is a metric unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a kilogram. | Example:  The recipe calls for 200 grams of flour.  食谱需要200克面粉。

Example: A paperclip weighs about a gram. 一个回形针大约重一克。 | 近义词: gramme | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) | 词性: noun
