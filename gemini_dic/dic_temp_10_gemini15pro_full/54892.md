disponee:/dɪspɒˈniː/ /dɪspɑːˈniː/
noun
受让人；处置权受益人
a person or organization that receives something such as property or money from someone who is giving it away or selling it
The disponee of the estate will inherit a considerable fortune.  该遗产的受让人将继承一大笔财产。


Etymology:
From Anglo-French *dispoiné, past participle of dispoiner "to dispose of," from dis- "apart, away" + poiner "to put, place."
