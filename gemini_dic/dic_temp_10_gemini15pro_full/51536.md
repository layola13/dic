deturb:/dɪˈtɜːrb/ | v. |  扰乱；使不安；逐出；罢免 | To displace (soil, rock, or other material) from its original position. To remove from office or position; oust. | The construction project deturbed the local ecosystem.  这个建设项目扰乱了当地的生态系统。 The corrupt official was deturbed from his position.  腐败官员被罢免了。| synonyms: displace, dislodge, disturb, oust, remove, eject | antonyms: establish, settle, install, reinstate | verb
