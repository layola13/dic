autological:/ˌɔːtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 自指的，自体逻辑的 |  An autological word is a word that describes itself.  It is a word that expresses a property that it also possesses. |  "Short" is an autological word because it is a short word. | Synonyms: self-descriptive, self-referential | Antonyms: heterological | Adjective
