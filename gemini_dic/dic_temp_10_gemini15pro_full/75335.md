frostier:/ˈfrɔːstiər (UK), ˈfrɑːstiər (US)| adj.|更冷的，更冷淡的|Comparative form of frosty: more frosty.  Covered with frost, or appearing as if covered with frost; hence, cold, chilling. Also, figuratively, cold in manner, unfriendly.|The reception I received was even frostier than last time. 我受到的接待比上次更冷淡了。|The air grew frostier as the sun began to set. 随着太阳开始落山，空气变得更冷了。|近义词：colder, icier, chillier, less friendly, more aloof|反义词：warmer, friendlier, more cordial|词性:形容词

frostiest:/ˈfrɔːstiɪst (UK), ˈfrɑːstiɪst (US)| adj.|最冷的，最冷淡的|Superlative form of frosty: most frosty. Covered with frost, or appearing as if covered with frost; hence, cold, chilling. Also, figuratively, cold in manner, unfriendly.|This is the frostiest winter we've had in decades. 这是我们几十年来最冷的冬天了。|He gave me the frostiest glare I've ever seen. 他给了我我见过的最冷淡的一瞥。|近义词：coldest, iciest, chilliest, least friendly, most aloof|反义词：warmest, friendliest, most cordial|词性:形容词
