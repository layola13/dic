obloquy:/ˈɑːbləkwai/ (US), /ˈɒbləkwɪ/ (UK)| n. | 辱骂，诽谤，恶名 |Strong public criticism or verbal abuse. |例句：He endured years of obloquy for his controversial views.  他因其有争议的观点忍受了多年的诽谤。

例句：The politician resigned in disgrace, overwhelmed by the obloquy heaped upon him.  这位政客在耻辱中辞职，不堪重负的诽谤压垮了他。|近义词：disgrace, dishonor, shame, opprobrium, condemnation, censure, vituperation, calumny |反义词：praise, acclaim, honor, respect|词性:名词
