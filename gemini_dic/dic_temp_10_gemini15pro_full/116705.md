matwali: /mɑːˈtwɑːliː/
n.
醉酒的人；酒鬼；常醉酒的人
A person who habitually drinks alcohol to excess; a drunkard.
He staggered around like a matwali. 他像个醉汉一样踉踉跄跄地走来走去。
近义词: drunkard, alcoholic, inebriate, sot
反义词: teetotaler, abstainer
词性: 名词

matwali: /mɑːˈtwɑːliː/
adj.
醉酒的；陶醉的
Intoxicated with alcohol.
He became matwali after drinking several beers.  他喝了几瓶啤酒后就醉了。
近义词: drunk, intoxicated, inebriated
反义词: sober
词性: 形容词
