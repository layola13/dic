fetched:/fɛtʃt/| adj.|做作的；牵强的；不自然的；（价格）过高的；| Appearing contrived or artificial; not natural; strained.  Also, unusually high (of a price).|Her laughter seemed a bit fetched. 她的笑声显得有点做作。|The price they're asking is a bit fetched. 他们要价有点高。|artificial, contrived, forced, strained, unnatural|natural, genuine, unforced|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From the past participle of *fetch*, from Old English *fetian, feccan* "to fetch, bring, derive, obtain," of Germanic origin.
