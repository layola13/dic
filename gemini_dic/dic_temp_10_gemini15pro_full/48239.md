decontaminative:/diːkənˈtæmɪnətɪv/
adjective
去污的；净化性的
Having the ability to remove contaminants or make something safe by removing contaminants.
The decontaminative properties of the solution make it ideal for cleaning up hazardous spills.  这种溶液的去污特性使其成为清理危险泄漏物的理想选择。
The team used a decontaminative spray to neutralize the harmful chemicals.  该团队使用去污喷雾来中和有害化学物质。
近义词: cleansing, purifying, sanitizing
反义词: contaminating, polluting
