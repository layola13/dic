epistyle:/ˈɛpɪstaɪl/| n. |楣梁，额枋|A horizontal beam or stone resting on columns or pillars, especially the beam forming the lintel over a doorway or window.|The epistyle of the temple was elaborately carved.  神庙的楣梁雕刻精美。|Synonym: lintel, architrave|Antonym: foundation, base|noun
