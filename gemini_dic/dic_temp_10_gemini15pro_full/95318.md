incavation:/ˌɪnkəˈveɪʃən/
n.
内凹，凹陷，挖空
The process of making a hollow or cavity, or the hollow itself.
The archaeologist discovered an incavation in the rock face, possibly a burial site. 考古学家在岩壁上发现了一个凹陷，可能是一个墓地。
concavity, depression, hollow, pit, indentation
convexity, protuberance, bulge
noun
