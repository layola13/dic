conjoiner's:/kənˈdʒɔɪnərz/ | n. | 连接者(们)的；结合者(们)的 |  Belonging to or associated with a conjoiner (a person or thing that joins or connects). This word is the possessive form of "conjoiner". | The conjoiner's role is crucial. 连接者的作用至关重要。|  The conjoiner's tools were left on the table. 连接者的工具落在桌上了。 | connector's, joiner's, uniter's | divider's, separator's | noun (possessive)


词源:
From "conjoin" (verb) + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting agents) + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Conjoin" comes from the Latin "conjungere" (to join together).
