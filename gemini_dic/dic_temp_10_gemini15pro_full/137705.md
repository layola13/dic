nonusurpingly:/ˌnɒnjuːˈsɜːpɪŋli/
adverb
非篡夺地；不僭越地
In a manner that does not usurp or seize power or rights illegitimately.
The new king, nonusurpingly, inherited the throne from his father. 新国王合法地继承了他父亲的王位。
He nonusurpingly assumed the leadership role after the previous leader resigned.  前任领导辞职后，他名正言顺地担任了领导职务。
近义词:legitimately, rightfully, lawfully
反义词: usurpingly, illegally, wrongfully
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + usurping + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "usurping" is the present participle of "usurp" (meaning to seize and hold (a position, office, power, etc.) by force or without legal right), and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
