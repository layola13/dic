absentnesses: /ˈæbsəntnəsəz/
n.
缺席；心不在焉
instances of being absent; states of being preoccupied or inattentive.
His frequent absentnesses from class are worrisome.  他经常缺课令人担忧。

Her absentnesses during the meeting were noticeable. 会议期间她的心不在焉很明显。

近义词: absences, distractions, inattentiveness
反义词: presence, attentiveness, focus
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From absent (adj.) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Absent from Latin absens, present participle of abesse "to be away," from ab- "off, away from" + esse "to be."
