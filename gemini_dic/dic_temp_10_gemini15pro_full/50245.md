dependencies:/dɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. | 依赖性，依赖关系 |  The state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else. | The project's success has dependencies on several key factors.  这个项目的成功依赖于几个关键因素。 | synonyms: reliance,  requirement,  prerequisite | antonyms: independence, autonomy | noun

dependencies:/dɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. | 眷属，家属（尤指经济上依赖他人者） |  People who depend on someone else for financial support, typically family members. |  He has several dependencies, including his elderly parents and young children. 他有几个家属，包括他年迈的父母和年幼的孩子。 | synonyms: dependents, family | antonyms: none | noun
