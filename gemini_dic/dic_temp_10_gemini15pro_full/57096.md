doughtren: /ˈdɔːtrən/ | n. | daughter | An archaic or poetic term for "daughter". |  My doughtren fair, come hither unto me. 我的美丽的女儿，到我这里来。| Synonym: daughter, girl, child (female) | Antonym: son | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doughter, from Old English *dohtor, from Proto-Germanic *duhtēr, from Proto-Indo-European *dhugh₂tḗr. Cognate with Dutch dochter, German Tochter, Icelandic dóttir.
