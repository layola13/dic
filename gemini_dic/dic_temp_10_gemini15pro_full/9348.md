antiproductivity:/ˌæntiˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvəti/ | n. | 反生产力，效率低下 |The state of being unproductive or hindering productivity.  A tendency to engage in activities that distract from or actively work against achieving goals or completing tasks. | Excessive social media use can lead to antiproductivity. 过度使用社交媒体会导致效率低下。|  The company's outdated software contributed to antiproductivity. 公司过时的软件导致了生产力低下。 | 近义词: inefficiency, unproductiveness, ineffectiveness | 反义词: productivity, efficiency, effectiveness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposite of (from Greek *anti-)
productivity (noun): the state or quality of being productive (from productive + -ity)
