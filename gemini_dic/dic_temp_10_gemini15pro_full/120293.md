minibibliography:/ˌmɪniˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 小型参考书目；简要参考书目 | A minibibliography is a short list of books or articles related to a particular subject.  | The professor provided a minibibliography for further reading on the topic. 教授提供了一份关于该主题的简要参考书目。| synonyms: short bibliography, abbreviated bibliography | antonyms: extensive bibliography, comprehensive bibliography | noun
