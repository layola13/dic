diureide:/ˈdaɪjʊəˌraɪd/| n. |利尿酰胺，尿酸盐|A compound formed from the combination of two molecules of urea with a dibasic acid, such as uric acid found in the excrement of birds and reptiles.|Uric acid is a diureide of purine metabolism. 尿酸是嘌呤代谢的利尿酰胺。|近义词:urate|反义词:None|noun
