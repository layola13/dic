earthworms:/ˈɜːθwɜːrmz/ (US) /ˈɜːθwɜːmz/ (UK)| n. |蚯蚓|A tube-shaped, segmented worm that lives in the soil. They are beneficial to gardens as they aerate the soil and improve drainage.|例句：Earthworms play a vital role in soil health. 蚯蚓在土壤健康中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：angleworms, red wigglers|反义词:|Noun
