minibuses:/ˈmɪniˌbʌsɪz/ |n. | 小型公共汽车；中巴 |A small bus designed to carry a limited number of passengers. |例句：The minibuses run regularly between the airport and the city center. 小型公共汽车定期往返于机场和市中心。

例句：We hired a minibus to take us to the wedding. 我们租了一辆小型公共汽车送我们去参加婚礼。


|近义词：minivan, shuttle bus|反义词: coach, bus |词性: 名词
