overbrowse:/ˌoʊvərˈbraʊz/
verb
过度浏览；过度放牧
To browse excessively, consuming more than is sustainable or desirable.
The deer have overbrowsed the forest, leaving little vegetation for other animals. 鹿群过度啃食了森林，几乎没有给其他动物留下植被。
近义词：overgraze, deplete
反义词：conserve, protect
词性：v.

overbrowse:/ˌoʊvərˈbraʊz/
noun
过度浏览；过度放牧
The act of browsing excessively.
Overbrowse by deer can lead to deforestation. 鹿的过度啃食会导致森林砍伐。
近义词：overgrazing, depletion
反义词：conservation, protection
词性：n.


词源:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "browse" (verb meaning "to graze; to casually look through").
