autoclaving:/ɔːtəˈkleɪvɪŋ/
|n.|高压灭菌，高压蒸汽灭菌|The process of sterilizing equipment and supplies by subjecting them to high-pressure saturated steam at 121 °C (249 °F) for around 15–20 minutes depending on the size and content of the load.|Laboratory glassware was sterilized by autoclaving.|近义词：sterilization, steam sterilization|反义词:contamination|词性:名词

autoclave:/ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/
|n.|高压灭菌器；|A strong, heated container used for chemical reactions and other processes using high pressures and temperatures, e.g., steam sterilization.|The hospital uses an autoclave to sterilize surgical instruments.|近义词：sterilizer|反义词:|词性:名词
autoclave:/ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/
|vt.|用高压锅消毒，高压灭菌|To sterilize something using an autoclave.|We autoclaved the surgical instruments before the operation.|近义词：sterilize|反义词:contaminate|词性:动词
