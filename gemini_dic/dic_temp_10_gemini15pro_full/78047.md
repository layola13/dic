genuineness:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪnnəs/| n.| 真实性；真诚；真品|The genuineness of his remorse was evident. 他的悔恨是真诚的。|近义词：authenticity, sincerity, honesty|反义词：falseness, artificiality, insincerity|词性:名词
Etymology: From genuine + -ness.  Genuine comes from Latin *genuinus "innate, native, natural," from genus "birth, race, stock."
