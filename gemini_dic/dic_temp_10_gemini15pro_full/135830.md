nonprophetically:/ˌnɒnˌprɒˈfɛtɪkli/
adverb
非预言地；不带预言性地
in a way that does not involve prophesying or predicting the future.
The economist nonprophetically analyzed the current market trends.  这位经济学家以非预言性的方式分析了当前的市场趋势。
He spoke nonprophetically about the potential challenges. 他没有预言性地谈到了潜在的挑战。
近义词:objectively, empirically, realistically
反义词:prophetically, predictively
词性:adverb
