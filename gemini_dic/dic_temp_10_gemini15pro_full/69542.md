felloe: /ˈfɛloʊ/ (US) /ˈfɛləʊ/ (UK) | n. | 外轮辋，轮圈 | The outer rim of a wheel, traditionally made of wood and forming a circle to which the spokes are attached. | The blacksmith replaced a broken felloe on the wagon wheel. 铁匠更换了马车轮上断裂的外轮辋。| synonym: rim, wheel rim | antonym: hub, center | noun

felloe: /ˈfɛloʊ/ (US) /ˈfɛləʊ/ (UK) | n. | (集体名词)轮辋，轮圈 |  Felloes collectively; the entire rim of a wheel. | The felloes were warped from the intense heat.  轮辋因高温而变形。| synonym: rims | antonym: hubs | noun
