palpitatingly:/ˈpælpɪˌteɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 心跳加速地，悸动地 | In a way that suggests a rapid, strong, or irregular heartbeat.  This adverb describes actions or states occurring in a manner suggestive of palpitations. | Her voice shook palpitatingly as she confessed her love. 她表白的时候，声音颤抖着，仿佛心跳加速。|  The bird beat its wings palpitatingly against the cage.  那只鸟焦躁地在笼子里拍打着翅膀，心跳似乎很快。 | 近义词: excitedly, nervously, anxiously, flutteringly | 反义词: calmly, serenely, placidly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the verb "palpitate" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Palpitate" comes from the Latin word "palpitare," meaning "to throb" or "to flutter."
