autodidact: /ˌɔːtəˈdɪdækt/ | n. | 自学者 | A person who has taught themself a subject, skill, etc., rather than having lessons from a teacher. |  He was a brilliant autodidact, teaching himself several languages and mastering complex mathematical theories. 他是一位才华横溢的自学者，自学了几种语言，并掌握了复杂的数学理论。| synonymous: self-taught person, self-learner | antonymous: student, pupil | noun

autodidact: /ˌɔːtəˈdɪdækt/ | adj. | 自学的 |  Having taught oneself a subject, skill, etc., rather than having lessons from a teacher. |  His autodidact approach to learning allowed him to explore his interests at his own pace.  他自学的学习方法使他能够按照自己的节奏探索自己的兴趣。| synonymous: self-taught, self-educated | antonymous: taught, instructed | adjective
