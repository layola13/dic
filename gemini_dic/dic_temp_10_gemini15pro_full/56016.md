do-or-die:/ˌduː ɔːr ˈdaɪ/ | adj. | 不成功便成仁的，生死攸关的 | Involving a critical situation where one must succeed or face dire consequences.  |  It was a do-or-die situation; we had to win the contract to save the company.  这是生死攸关的局面；我们必须赢得合同才能挽救公司。 | crucial, critical, desperate, life-or-death | optional, inconsequential | adjective

do-or-die:/ˌduː ɔːr ˈdaɪ/ | n. | 孤注一掷，生死关头 | A desperate situation requiring a risky course of action.  | The final match was do-or-die for the team. 这场决赛对球队来说是生死攸关的。| crisis, showdown, last stand | safe bet, sure thing | noun
