metazooen: /ˌmɛtəˈzoʊən/ | n. | 后生动物 | An animal of the Metazoa division; a multicellular animal having cells differentiated into tissues and organs and usually a digestive cavity and nervous system. | Example: Sponges are among the simplest metazoans. 海绵是最简单的后生动物之一。

Example:  Metazoans exhibit a remarkable diversity in form and function. 后生动物在形态和功能上表现出显著的多样性。 | 近义词: animal, multicellular animal | 反义词: protozoan, single-celled organism | 词性: noun
