nursingly:/ˈnɜːrsɪŋli/
adverb
像护士一样地；细心地；体贴地
in a way that shows care and concern, like a nurse
She nursingly bathed the wound. 她细心地清洗伤口。
近义词:carefully, tenderly, attentively
反义词:roughly, carelessly, neglectfully
词性:副词

词源:
From nurse + -ing + -ly.  "Nurse" comes from Old French "norice" meaning "wet-nurse," ultimately from Latin "nutrire" meaning "to nourish."
