numismatics:/ˌnjuːmɪzˈmætɪks/ | n. |钱币学| The study or collection of coins, paper money, and other forms of currency. | He developed a keen interest in numismatics after inheriting his grandfather's coin collection. 他在继承了祖父的硬币收藏后，对钱币学产生了浓厚的兴趣。 | 近义词: coin collecting | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

numismaticses:  This word is not standard English. The correct plural is "numismatics" as it is an uncountable noun.
