letter:/ˈlɛtər/ (US), /ˈletə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|字母；信；证书；字面意义|A character representing one or more of the sounds used in speech; a written, typed, or printed communication, especially one sent in an envelope by mail or messenger.|例句：Please write the letter clearly. 请把字母写清楚。|例句：I received a letter from my friend yesterday. 我昨天收到了朋友的一封信。|近义词：character, note, message, correspondence|反义词: (letter as a character) symbol, sign, figure (no direct opposite for letter as mail)|词性: noun, verb


letter:/ˈlɛtər/ (US), /ˈletə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|用字母标明；写信给|To mark with letters; to write a letter to someone.|例句：The box was lettered with his initials. 盒子上标有他的姓名首字母。|例句：I will letter him about the details. 我会写信给他详细说明。|近义词：(for marking) label, inscribe, write (for sending a letter) write, contact, inform|反义词: (no direct opposite)|词性: verb
