bolar:/ˈboʊlɑːr/ | adj. | 似土的，粘土状的 | Resembling clay; clayey; consisting of or relating to bole. | The bolar deposits were rich in iron oxide. 这些似土的沉积物富含氧化铁。 |Example: The geologist examined the bolar sample, noting its reddish-brown hue. 地质学家检查了粘土状的样本，注意到它的红棕色调。| synonym: clayey, argillaceous | antonym: sandy, granular | adjective
