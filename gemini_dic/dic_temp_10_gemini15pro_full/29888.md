cautious: /ˈkɔːʃəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːʃəs/ (UK)| adj. |谨慎的，小心的|Careful to avoid potential problems or dangers. Showing or practicing caution.|She was cautious about crossing the busy street. 她过繁忙的街道时很小心。|近义词: careful, wary, prudent, circumspect|反义词: reckless, careless, impulsive, rash|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cautiōsus*, from *cautiō* ("caution"), from *caveō* ("beware, take heed").
