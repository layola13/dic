foolscap:/ˈfuːlsˌkæp/| n.|傻瓜；笨蛋|A person who acts unwisely or foolishly (archaic).|He was a foolscap for trusting her.|Synonyms: idiot, fool, simpleton|Antonyms: genius, sage, wise person|noun
