marriageableness:/ˌmærɪdʒəˈbɪlnəs/
n.
适婚性，可结婚性
The quality or state of being suitable for marriage.
Her marriageableness was enhanced by her beauty and wealth. 她的美貌和财富增加了她的适婚性。
Synonyms: eligibility, suitability
Antonyms: unsuitability, ineligibility
