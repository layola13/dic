endearment:/ɪnˈdɪərmənt/ /ɛnˈdɪərmənt/| n. |爱慕，钟爱；爱称，昵称|Something that inspires affection. An expression of affection.|例句：Terms of endearment, such as "honey" or "sweetie," are often used between close friends or romantic partners. 爱称，例如“亲爱的”或“甜心”，经常用于亲密的朋友或恋人之间。

例句：The child's constant endearments charmed her grandparents.  孩子不断的亲昵举动迷住了她的祖父母。|近义词：affection, fondness, tenderness, love, attachment |反义词: dislike, hatred, aversion, antipathy |词性:noun
