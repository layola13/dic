equiponderous:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndərəs/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndərəs/| adj.|等重的，相等的，均衡的|Of equal weight; having equal weight on each side; equally balanced.  Characterized by balance and equality in weight, force, or importance.|The two equiponderous weights balanced the scale perfectly.|近义词：equal, balanced, equivalent, commensurate|反义词:unequal, unbalanced, disproportionate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *aequiponderosus*, from *aequi-* (equal) + *ponderosus* (weighty), from *pondus* (weight).
