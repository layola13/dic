offensive: /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (US), /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (UK)| adj. n.|冒犯的；攻击性的；进攻的；攻势|Causing someone to feel resentful, upset, or annoyed; actively aggressive; relating to attack rather than defense.|例句：His comments were deeply offensive to many people. 他的评论对很多人来说非常冒犯。|近义词：insulting, abusive, aggressive, attacking|反义词：inoffensive, defensive, harmless|词性:形容词

offensive: /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (US), /əˈfɛnsɪv/ (UK)| n.|攻势；进攻|An aggressive military operation.|例句：The army launched a major offensive against the enemy. 军队对敌人发动了大规模进攻。|近义词：attack, assault, onslaught|反义词：defense|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *offensif (modern French offensif), from Latin offēnsīvus (“striking against”), from offēnsus, perfect passive participle of offendō (“I strike against, offend”).
