clubbing: /ˈklʌbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈklʌbɪŋ/ (US)
n.
杵状指(趾)
abnormal enlargement of the distal phalanges (fingers and toes) associated with certain heart and lung conditions.
例句：The doctor noticed clubbing of his fingers and suspected a lung problem. 医生注意到他的手指杵状变形，怀疑是肺部问题。

例句：Clubbing can be a sign of chronic hypoxia. 杵状指可能是慢性缺氧的迹象。

近义词：finger clubbing, digital clubbing
反义词： (No direct antonym, represents a medical condition)
词性：名词


词源:  From the verb "club" (meaning to hit with a club), originally referring to the shape of clubbed fingers resembling a club. The meaning related to nightclubs emerged later.
