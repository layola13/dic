lymphangitis:/ˌlɪmfænˈdʒaɪtɪs/
n.
淋巴管炎
Inflammation of the lymphatic vessels, typically caused by a bacterial infection.  The affected vessels become inflamed and appear as red streaks under the skin.
The patient presented with lymphangitis in his left arm after a minor scratch became infected.  病人左臂轻微擦伤后感染，出现淋巴管炎。
Lymphangitis is a serious condition and requires prompt medical attention. 淋巴管炎是一种严重的疾病，需要立即就医。
synonyms: lymph vessel inflammation
antonyms:  None (There isn't a direct antonym for a pathological condition like lymphangitis.)
