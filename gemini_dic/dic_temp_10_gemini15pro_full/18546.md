beneficent:/bɪˈnɛfɪsnt/
adjective
仁慈的；善行的；有益的
Doing good or causing good to be done; kindly in action or purpose.
例句：The beneficent king helped his people. 仁慈的国王帮助了他的人民。

例句：Sunshine is beneficent to plants. 阳光对植物有益。

近义词：benevolent, charitable, kind, generous, philanthropic
反义词：malevolent, cruel, harmful, detrimental
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *beneficens*, present participle of *benefacere* "to do good," from *bene* "well" + *facere* "to do."
