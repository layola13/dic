hyperpurely:/haɪpərˈpjʊərli/
adverb
超级纯净地；极度纯净地
Meaning: in an extremely pure manner.  This word emphasizes a level of purity that goes beyond typical standards. It suggests something is purified to an exceptional or excessive degree.
Example: The silicon crystals were hyperpurely refined for use in the advanced sensors. 这些硅晶体经过超纯提炼，用于先进的传感器。

Example:  The ingredients are hyperpurely sourced and tested for contaminants. 这些成分来源超纯，并经过污染物测试。

近义词: extremely purely, ultra-purely, exceptionally purely
反义词: impurely, contaminatedly
词性: adverb

词源: "Hyper-" (Greek prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive") + "purely" (from "pure" + "-ly").
