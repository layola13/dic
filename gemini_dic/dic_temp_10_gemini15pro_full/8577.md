anticlassicalism:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪzəm/| n.|反古典主义|Opposition to or a reaction against classicism in art, literature, music, or architecture, especially the classicism of the 18th century.|The rise of Romanticism marked a period of strong anticlassicalism. 浪漫主义的兴起标志着一个强烈的反古典主义时期。|The architect's designs reflected a clear anticlassicalism, favoring asymmetry and organic forms.  这位建筑师的设计体现了明显的反古典主义，偏爱不对称和有机形式。|近义词：romanticism, modernism|反义词：classicism|词性: noun
