nanocomposite:/ˈnænoʊkəmˈpɑːzɪt/ | n. |纳米复合材料|A material made of two or more constituent materials with at least one of the constituents having at least one dimension in the nanometer range (1-100 nm).|Researchers are exploring the use of nanocomposites in aerospace applications. 研究人员正在探索纳米复合材料在航空航天领域的应用。|Synonyms: nanomaterial, hybrid material|Antonyms: monolithic material|noun

nanocomposite:/ˈnænoʊkəmˈpɑːzɪt/ | adj. |纳米复合的|Made of or relating to a nanocomposite material.|The nanocomposite structure exhibits enhanced mechanical properties. 这种纳米复合结构表现出增强的机械性能。|Synonyms: nanostructured|Antonyms: conventional, traditional|adjective
