animism: /ˈænɪmɪzəm/ | n. |万物有灵论，泛灵论|The belief that natural objects, natural phenomena, and the universe itself possess souls.  The attribution of a soul to plants, inanimate objects, and natural phenomena.|例句：Animism is a common belief in many indigenous cultures.  万物有灵论在许多土著文化中是一种普遍的信仰。|例句：Her paintings reflect her belief in animism, portraying trees and rivers with human-like faces. 她的画作反映了她对万物有灵论的信仰，描绘了具有类人面孔的树木和河流。|近义词：pantheism |反义词: materialism, atheism |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *animisme*, from Latin *anima* (soul, life) + *-ismus* (forming nouns of action or condition).
