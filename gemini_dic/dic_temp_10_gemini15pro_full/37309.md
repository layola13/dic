commat:/ˈkɒmæt/ | n. | 逗号（用于电子邮件地址中，替代@符号）| A "commat" is a spoken representation of the "@" symbol, primarily used when verbally relaying an email address. It avoids confusion with the word "at". | Example: My email address is username commat domain dot com. 我的电子邮件地址是用户名逗号域名点com。 | Example: Please send the document to info commat example commat com. 请将文档发送至info逗号example点com。| 近义词: at symbol | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
