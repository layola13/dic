divorceable:/dɪˈvɔːrsəbl/
adj.
可离婚的
Capable of being divorced; permitting or allowing divorce.
Her marriage was deemed divorceable due to irreconcilable differences. 她的婚姻由于不可调和的分歧而被认为是可以离婚的。
Their relationship became so strained that it seemed divorceable. 他们的关系变得如此紧张，似乎可以离婚了。
近义词:separable, dissolvable
反义词:indissoluble
词性:adjective
