determinable:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/
adjective|可决定的，可确定的|Capable of being determined, ascertained, or established.|The exact date of the event is determinable.  这个事件的确切日期是可以确定的。|Synonyms: ascertainable, definable, calculable|Antonyms: indeterminable, uncertain, indefinite
