implementation:/ˌɪmplɪmɛnˈteɪʃən/| n. |实施，执行|The implementation of the new policy was delayed. 新政策的实施被推迟了。|例句：The successful implementation of the project requires careful planning.  这个项目的成功实施需要仔细的规划。

The implementation of the new software caused some initial problems. 新软件的实施初期出现了一些问题。

|近义词：execution, application, enactment, realization|反义词:non-implementation, neglect, abandonment|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin implementatio, from Latin implementum (“filling up, accomplishment”), from implere (“to fill up”), from in- (“in, into”) + plere (“to fill”).
