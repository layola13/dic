advocated: /ˈædvəkeɪtɪd/
verb
提倡；主张；拥护
Supported or recommended publicly.
The doctor advocated a healthy diet and regular exercise. 医生提倡健康饮食和规律运动。

She advocated for the rights of the disabled. 她倡导残疾人的权利。

近义词: supported, recommended, championed, promoted, endorsed
反义词: opposed, condemned, denounced
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *advocatus*, past participle of *advocāre* "to summon, to call to one's aid," from *ad-* "to" + *vocāre* "to call."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advocates for free speech. = 他提倡言论自由。
The organization advocates for animal rights. = 这个组织倡导动物权利。

一般过去时:
They advocated for peace during the war. = 战争期间，他们倡导和平。
She advocated a change in policy. = 她主张改变政策。

现在完成时:
The group has advocated for environmental protection for many years. = 该组织多年来一直倡导环境保护。
He has advocated this approach in several publications. = 他在几份出版物中都提倡这种方法。

过去完成时:
They had advocated for this change before the new law was passed. = 在新法律通过之前，他们就一直倡导这一改变。
She had advocated for his release from prison. = 她一直主张将他从监狱释放。

将来时:
We will advocate for better working conditions. = 我们将倡导更好的工作条件。
The union will advocate for a pay raise. = 工会将倡导加薪。

虚拟语气:
If they advocated for change, things might be different. = 如果他们倡导变革，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish they would advocate for a more sustainable solution. = 我希望他们能倡导一个更可持续的解决方案。

被动语态:
A new approach was advocated by the experts. = 专家们提倡一种新方法。
Further research is being advocated. = 人们正在倡导进一步的研究。

疑问句:
What changes are they advocating? = 他们正在倡导哪些改变？
Who is advocating for this policy? = 谁在倡导这项政策？

否定句:
They didn't advocate for violence. = 他们没有提倡暴力。
She doesn't advocate for that particular solution. = 她不提倡那个特定的解决方案。


PHRASE->
advocate for (something/someone) =  提倡（某事/某人）
advocate a change = 提倡改变
strongly advocate =  强烈主张
publicly advocate =  公开倡导
advocate a policy =  倡导一项政策
