enantiomorphous:/ɪˌnæntiəˈmɔːrfəs/| adj.|对映异构的，镜像的|Existing in two forms that are mirror images of each other but cannot be superimposed.|The two enantiomorphous crystals refracted light differently. 这两个对映异构的晶体以不同的方式折射光线。|近义词：chiral, mirror-image, non-superimposable|反义词：achiral, superimposable|词性:adjective
enantiomorph:/ɪˌnæntiəˈmɔːrf/| n.|对映体，镜像异构体|Either of a pair of molecules or crystals that are mirror images of each other but cannot be superimposed.|The two enantiomorphs have different optical properties. 这两个对映体具有不同的光学性质。|近义词：chiral isomer, mirror image isomer|反义词：-|词性:noun

词源: From Greek enantios "opposite" + morphē "form."
