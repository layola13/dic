inablement:/ɪˈneɪblmənt/| n.|赋能，使能，授权|The process of giving someone the ability or authority to do something.  The act of enabling.  It often refers to providing tools, resources, or training to empower individuals or groups.|例句：The company's focus on employee inablement has led to increased productivity.  公司对员工赋能的重视带来了生产力的提高。

例句：The inablement program provided the necessary resources for the community to thrive.  赋能计划为社区的繁荣提供了必要的资源。|近义词：empowerment, facilitation, enablement, authorization|反义词:restriction, hindrance, disablement|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "enable" + suffix "-ment."
"Enable" comes from the Old French "en-" (meaning "in" or "make") and "abler" (meaning "to make able").  The suffix "-ment" forms nouns from verbs, indicating the result or product of an action.
