jingoistic:/ˌdʒɪŋɡoʊˈɪstɪk/
adjective
侵略主义的，沙文主义的
Characterized by extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.
His jingoistic rhetoric stirred up the crowd. 他的沙文主义言论煽动了人群。
