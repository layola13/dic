micas: /ˈmaɪkəz/ | n. | 云母 | Mica is a shiny silicate mineral with a layered structure, found as minute scales in granite and other rocks, or as crystals. It is used as a thermal or electrical insulator. | The electrical components were insulated with mica.  这些电子元件用云母绝缘。| synonyms:  isinglass, vermiculite (in some contexts) | antonyms: N/A | noun
