edacity: /ɪˈdæsɪti/ | n. | 贪吃; 贪婪; 狼吞虎咽 |  A voracious appetite; greediness. It refers to the quality of being excessively eager for food or other things.  | He ate with remarkable edacity. 他吃得狼吞虎咽。 | 近义词: gluttony, voracity, rapacity | 反义词: abstinence, moderation | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *ēdācitās*, from ēdāx (“gluttonous”), from ēdere (“to eat”).
