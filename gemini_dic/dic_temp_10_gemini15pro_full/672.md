abruptnesses: /əˈbrʌptnəsɪz/
n.
突然，唐突，粗鲁
The abruptness of his departure surprised everyone.
他的突然离开让每个人都感到惊讶。
The abruptnesses of the terrain made hiking difficult.
地形的崎岖不平使得徒步旅行变得困难。
ETYMOLOGY: From abrupt + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *abruptus, past participle of abrumpere "to break off," from ab- "off, away from" + rumpere "to break."
SYNONYMS: suddenness, sharpness, bluntness, curtness, brusqueness
ANTONYMS: gradualness, smoothness, politeness, courtesy, gentleness
词性: 名词
