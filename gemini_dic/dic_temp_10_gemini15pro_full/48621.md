defensible:/dɪˈfɛnsɪbəl/
adjective
可辩护的；可防御的
Capable of being defended, justified, or protected.
His actions were completely defensible under the circumstances. 在这种情况下，他的行为是完全可以辩护的。

defensible:/dɪˈfɛnsɪbəl/
adjective
可防御的
Capable of being protected against attack.
The city walls were made more defensible. 城墙被修得更易于防守了。


Synonyms: justifiable, excusable, vindicable, maintainable, protectable
Antonyms: indefensible, unjustifiable, inexcusable, unsustainable
