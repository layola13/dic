oxytoluene:/ˌɒksɪˈtɒljʊiːn/ /ˌɑːksiˈtɑːljuˌiːn/| n.|甲苯酚，羟基甲苯|A compound derived from toluene by replacing one hydrogen atom of the methyl group with a hydroxyl group.  It is an isomer of benzyl alcohol.|Cresols are oxytoluenes used as disinfectants. 甲酚是羟基甲苯，用作消毒剂。|Synonyms: cresol, hydroxytoluene|Antonyms: None|noun
