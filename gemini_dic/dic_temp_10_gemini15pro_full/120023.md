n.
百万富翁的身份/地位
The state or condition of being a millionaire.
He achieved millionaireship through hard work and smart investments. 他通过努力工作和明智的投资获得了百万富翁的地位。
近义词: wealth, affluence, prosperity
反义词: poverty, destitution
词性: 名词

词源: millionaire + -ship.  Millionaire comes from French millionnaire, from million.  The suffix -ship denotes "state or condition of being."
