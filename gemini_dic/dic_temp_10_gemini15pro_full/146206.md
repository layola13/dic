overexpectantly:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspektəntli/
| adv. | 过于期待地；期望过高地 | In a manner that shows excessive expectation or anticipation. | She waited overexpectantly for his reply, her eyes shining with hope. 她满怀希望地等待着他的答复，眼里闪着光。|例句：The child looked overexpectantly at the closed gift box. 孩子过于期待地看着那个 بسته 的礼物盒。| 近义词: hopefully, eagerly, anxiously | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "expectant" (having or showing expectation) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Expectant" comes from the Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await).
