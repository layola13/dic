hemisection:/ˌhemɪˈsekʃn/
n.
半切; 半断
The surgical cutting of half of a transverse section, as of the spinal cord.
例句：Hemisection of the spinal cord can result in a characteristic pattern of symptoms.  脊髓半切可导致一种特有的症状模式。

例句：The hemisection of the nerve resulted in loss of sensation on one side of the body.  神经的半切导致身体一侧感觉丧失。

近义词：bisection, transection
反义词: None


词性: noun

词源:  From hemi- (half) + section (a cutting).  源自 hemi- （一半） + section （切割）。
