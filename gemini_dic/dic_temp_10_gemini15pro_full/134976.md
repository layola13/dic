nonnaturalism:/ˌnɒnˈnætʃrəlɪzəm/
n.
非自然主义
The philosophical view that ethical properties, such as goodness or rightness, are not reducible to natural properties, such as pleasure or evolutionary fitness.
Nonnaturalism holds that moral facts are not discoverable through scientific observation.  非自然主义认为道德事实无法通过科学观察发现。

Nonnaturalism is a key concept in meta-ethics. 非自然主义是元伦理学中的一个关键概念。

Synonyms: moral realism, intuitionism
Antonyms: naturalism, subjectivism
词性: 名词
