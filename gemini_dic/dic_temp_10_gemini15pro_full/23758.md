bravest:/ˈbreɪvɪst/| adj.|最勇敢的|Superlative form of brave: most brave.  It describes someone who possesses the highest degree of courage.|例句：He was the bravest soldier in the whole army. 他是全军最勇敢的士兵。

例句：She was the bravest person I'd ever met. 她是我见过的最勇敢的人。|近义词：most courageous, most valiant, most daring|反义词：most cowardly, most timid, most fearful|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *bravest, from Old English brævest, superlative of bræf (“brave”). Cognate with Scots bravest (“bravest”), West Frisian braafst (“bravest”), Dutch braafst (“bravest, best-behaved”), German brav (“good, well-behaved, brave”).
