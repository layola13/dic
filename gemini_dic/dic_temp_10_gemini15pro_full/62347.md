endered:/ˈɛndərd/
Verb
给予，赋予; 使成为；使变得
To cause someone or something to be in a particular state or condition.
例句：The long illness endered him weak. 长期患病使他变得虚弱。

例句：His actions endered him unpopular. 他的行为使他不受欢迎。

近义词：made, caused, rendered
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but consider words like "prevented" or "protected" depending on the context)
