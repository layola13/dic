befrill:/bɪˈfrɪl/
verb
给…加上褶边，装饰
to decorate something with a frill or frills
She befrilled the curtains with lace. 她给窗帘加上了蕾丝褶边。

近义词:decorate, adorn, ornament, embellish, trim
反义词:strip, denude

词性:v.

词源: be- + frill.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "to make or cause to be," and "frill" refers to a ruffled or gathered strip of fabric used as ornamentation. Thus, "befrill" means to add frills to something.
