microchemical:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈkɛmɪkəl/ |adj.|微化学的；微量化学的|Relating to chemical reactions or analysis involving very small quantities of substances.| Microchemical tests were conducted to identify the unknown compound. 对未知化合物进行了微量化学测试。|例句：Microchemical analysis is essential in forensic science.  微量化学分析在法医学中至关重要。

例句：Researchers used microchemical techniques to study the composition of the meteorite. 研究人员使用微量化学技术来研究陨石的成分。|近义词：microscopic, minute |反义词:macrochemical|词性:形容词
