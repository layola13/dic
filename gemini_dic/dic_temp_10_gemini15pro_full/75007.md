friendless:/ˈfrendləs/| adj.|无朋友的，孤单的|Without friends; lacking friendship.|He felt friendless in a new city. 他在一个新的城市感到孤单。|近义词：lonely, isolated, alone|反义词: sociable, friendly, gregarious|词性:形容词

词源: friend (朋友) + -less (无，没有)  From Old English *frēondlēas (unattested), from frēond "friend" + -lēas "-less."  Cognate with Dutch vriendloos, German freundlos.
