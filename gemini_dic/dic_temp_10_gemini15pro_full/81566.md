grazers:/ˈɡreɪzərz/| n.|食草动物；放牧者|Animals that graze, or people who graze livestock.  These animals eat grass and other plants in a field or pasture.|The grazers moved slowly across the savanna. 食草动物缓缓地穿过大草原。|The sheep grazers led their flock to a new pasture. 牧羊人把羊群带到新的牧场。|近义词：herbivores, pasture animals, livestock farmers|反义词:predators, carnivores|词性:noun (plural)
graze:/ɡreɪz/| v.|放牧；吃草；擦伤|To feed on growing grass or other plants; to lightly touch or scrape something in passing.|The cows grazed peacefully in the field. 奶牛在田野里平静地吃草。|His knee grazed the pavement when he fell. 他跌倒时膝盖擦伤了路面。|近义词：browse, nibble, scrape|反义词:fast, starve|词性:verb
