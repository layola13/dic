draping:/ˈdreɪpɪŋ/
n.|悬垂，褶皱; 布料覆盖; (尤指衣服的)悬垂性，褶皱; |The draping of the curtains was elegant. 窗帘的悬垂很优雅。|近义词:covering, hanging, arrangement |反义词:tightening, stretching
词性:名词

draping:/ˈdreɪpɪŋ/
v.|用布料覆盖; 悬挂，披; 使(布料)优雅地悬挂或垂落;  |She draped the scarf around her shoulders. 她把围巾披在肩上。|近义词:cover, hang, adorn |反义词:uncover, remove
词性:动词


词源: From drape + -ing, from Old French *draper "to drape," from drap "cloth," of Germanic origin.
