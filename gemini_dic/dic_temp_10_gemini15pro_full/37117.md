combustibleness:/kəmˈbʌstɪblənəs/
n.
可燃性
The quality of being combustible; flammability.
The combustibleness of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶的可燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。
flammability, ignitability
incombustibility, nonflammability
noun
