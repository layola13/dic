noun
儿媳（复数）
The plural form of "daughter-in-law," referring to the wives of one's sons.
My two daughters-in-law are both wonderful women. 我的两个儿媳都是很棒的女人。
His daughters-in-law often visit him. 他的儿媳们经常来看望他。

daughters-in-law: /ˈdɔːtərz ɪn lɔː/
noun
儿媳们
A term of relationship, referring to the wives of a person's son or sons.
Both my daughters-in-law are excellent cooks. 我的两个儿媳都擅长烹饪。
He introduced me to his daughters-in-law. 他把我介绍给了他的儿媳们。
近义词: (No single-word synonym, but can be expressed as "sons' wives")
反义词: (No antonym)
