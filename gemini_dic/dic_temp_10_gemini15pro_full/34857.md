clinodiagonal:/klaɪnoʊdaɪˈæɡənəl/ |adj.|斜角的，倾斜对角线的|Relating to or designating a diagonal line or plane inclined to some other line or plane.  A clinodiagonal is a diagonal line or plane that is inclined or slanted relative to another line or plane, often in a crystal or geometric structure.|The clinodiagonal axis is important in classifying monoclinic crystals.|近义词:oblique, inclined, slanted|反义词:orthogonal, perpendicular|adjective
