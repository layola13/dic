evocation:/ˌɛvəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 唤起，激起，召唤 | The novel's vivid descriptions produce a powerful evocation of childhood. 这部小说的生动描写唤起了人们对童年的强烈回忆。 | The magician's evocation of spirits thrilled the audience. 魔术师召唤灵魂的表演使观众激动不已。 | 近义词: summoning, conjuring, recall, suggestion | 反义词: suppression, repression | noun

词源: From Late Latin *evocatio(n-), from Latin evocatus, past participle of evocare "to call out, summon," from ex- "out" + vocare "to call."
