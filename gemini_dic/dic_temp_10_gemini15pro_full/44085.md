cripple:/ˈkrɪpəl/ (US), /ˈkrɪpl/ (UK)| n. v. adj.| 跛子，残疾人；严重削弱；使残废|A person who is unable to walk or move normally because of a permanent injury or disability. cripple:/ˈkrɪpəl/ (US), /ˈkrɪpl/ (UK)| n. v. adj.| 跛子，残疾人；严重削弱；使残废|Derived from Old English "crypel" and Old Norse "kryppill," meaning "to creep." Originally referred to someone who had to crawl, but the meaning broadened to include any physical disability affecting movement.  It is now considered offensive when used to refer to a person.  Use "person with a disability" instead.|例句：The sanctions crippled the country's economy. 制裁严重削弱了该国的经济。|近义词：disable, incapacitate, impair, damage, weaken|反义词：heal, cure, strengthen, repair|词性:名词,动词,形容词
