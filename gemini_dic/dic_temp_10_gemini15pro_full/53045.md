dionysus:/daɪəˈnaɪsəs/| n.|狄俄尼索斯|The Greek god of wine, fertility, theatre, and religious ecstasy.  He is associated with revelry, intoxication, and the primal forces of nature.|例句：Dionysus is often depicted holding a thyrsus. 狄俄尼索斯通常被描绘成手持酒神杖。

例句：The festival of Dionysus was a time of great celebration. 狄俄尼索斯节是一个盛大的节日。|近义词：Bacchus (Roman equivalent)|反义词: Apollo (representing order and reason)|词性:Noun
