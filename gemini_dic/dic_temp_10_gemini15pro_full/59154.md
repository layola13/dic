dynasties:/ˈdaɪnəstiz/ /ˈdɪnəstiz/| n. |王朝；朝代|A sequence of rulers from the same family, stock, or group.|The Ming dynasty ruled China for centuries. 明朝统治了中国数百年。|近义词：house, lineage, reign|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用词组，例如：period of instability 不稳定时期)|词性: noun

dynasties:/ˈdaɪnəstiz/ /ˈdɪnəstiz/| n. |(动植物等的)世系|A succession of groups or entities.|The dynasty of hummingbirds has evolved over millions of years. 蜂鸟的世系已经进化了数百万年。|近义词：lineage, ancestry, descent|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: noun
