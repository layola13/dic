narrownesses:/ˈnæroʊnɪsɪz/
noun
狭窄，狭隘；偏狭
The narrownesses of the mountain pass made it difficult for vehicles to pass.
这条山路的狭窄使得车辆难以通行。
The narrownesses of his views prevented him from seeing the bigger picture.
他观点的狭隘使他无法看到更大的局面。
Synonyms: limitations, restrictions, constraints, confinements
Antonyms: widths, breadths, expanses, opennesses
词性: 名词
