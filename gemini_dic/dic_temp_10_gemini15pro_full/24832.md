brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn bæɡ/| n. |自带午餐；自带午餐的聚会；|A lunch packed in a brown paper bag, typically brought from home to work or school.  Also refers to an informal meeting or seminar where attendees bring their own lunches.|例句：I brown-bag it every day to save money. 我每天自带午餐以省钱。

例句：We're having a brown-bag lunch meeting tomorrow to discuss the new project. 明天我们将举行自带午餐的会议来讨论新项目。|近义词：packed lunch, sack lunch|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "eating out" or "restaurant meal")|词性:noun


brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn bæɡ/| v. |自带午餐(去上班或上学)；|To bring one's own lunch, typically in a brown paper bag, to work or school.|例句：I've been brown-bagging it all week. 我这周一直自带午餐。

例句：She brown-bags her lunch to avoid the expensive cafeteria food. 她自带午餐以避免昂贵的自助餐厅食物。|近义词：pack a lunch, bring a lunch|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "eat out" or "buy lunch")|词性:verb
