hideousness:/ˈhɪdiəsnəs/ /ˈhaɪdiəsnəs/| n.|丑陋；可怕；可憎|The hideousness of the crime shocked the nation. 这起罪行的可怕震惊了全国。|近义词：ugliness, monstrosity, frightfulness, repulsiveness|反义词：beauty, attractiveness, loveliness|词性:名词

Etymology->
From hideous + -ness.  Hideous itself comes from Middle English hidous, from Anglo-French hidus, probably from Old Norse hȳðr "horror, shame, disgrace."
