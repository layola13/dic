happy-go-lucky:/ˌhæpi ɡoʊ ˈlʌki/| adj.|无忧无虑的；乐天派的|Cheerfully unconcerned about the future.| He has a happy-go-lucky attitude to life. 他对生活有一种无忧无虑的态度。|Synonyms: carefree, easygoing, lighthearted|Antonyms: anxious, worried, apprehensive|adjective

happy-go-lucky:/ˌhæpi ɡoʊ ˈlʌki/| n.|乐天派|A person who is cheerfully unconcerned about the future.|She's such a happy-go-lucky, always smiling and optimistic. 她真是个乐天派，总是面带微笑，乐观向上。|Synonyms: optimist, free spirit|Antonyms: pessimist, worrier|noun
