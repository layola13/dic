bragg:/bræɡ/| v. n.| 吹嘘，自夸；吹牛大王|To talk boastfully about oneself or one's achievements.  A person who boasts.|例句：He bragged about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。|近义词：boast, vaunt, crow, swagger|反义词：deprecate, belittle|词性：动词, 名词

braggart:/ˈbræɡɑːrt/| n.| 吹牛大王，自夸者|A person who brags a lot.|例句：He's such a braggart, always talking about how great he is. 他真是个吹牛大王，总是谈论自己有多伟大。|近义词：boaster, show-off|反义词：humble person|词性：名词
