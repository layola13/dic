fungicide:/ˈfʌndʒɪsaɪd/ | n. | 杀菌剂 | A substance that destroys fungi or inhibits their growth. |  Farmers often use fungicides to protect their crops. 农民经常使用杀菌剂来保护他们的作物。

The fungicide effectively controlled the spread of mildew.  这种杀菌剂有效地控制了霉菌的蔓延。

| 近义词：antifungal | 反义词:fungistat (抑菌剂, 此处相对而言) | 词性:noun
