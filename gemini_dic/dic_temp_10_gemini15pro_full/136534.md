nonrumination:/ˌnɒnˌruːmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 非反刍，不沉思，不反复思考 | The act of not ruminating, or dwelling on negative thoughts and feelings.  It implies a conscious effort to avoid obsessive or repetitive thinking. | Through meditation, he practiced nonrumination to improve his mental well-being.  他通过冥想练习不沉思，以改善心理健康。 | clarity, focus, mindfulness | rumination, obsession, brooding | noun


**Etymology:**

From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "rumination" (from Latin "ruminari," meaning "to chew over again," figuratively referring to pondering or meditating).
