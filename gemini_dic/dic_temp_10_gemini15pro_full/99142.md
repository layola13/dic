intercessional:/ˌɪntərˈsɛʃənəl/
adjective
休会的，闭会的
Occurring between sessions, especially between sessions of a legislature or other formal body.
The intercessional period allows committees to prepare for the next full session.  休会期间允许委员会为下次全体会议做准备。
The ambassador held intercessional meetings with representatives of both countries. 大国在休会期间与两国代表举行了会晤。
近义词: interim, between sessions
反义词: in session
词性: adjective
