paleographical:/ˌpeɪliəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
古文字学的
relating to paleography; of or pertaining to the study of ancient writings and inscriptions
例句：Paleographical analysis helped determine the age of the manuscript. 古文字分析帮助确定了手稿的年代。

例句：The paleographical features suggest the text dates back to the 15th century.  古文字学的特征表明该文本可以追溯到15世纪。
近义词：scribal, calligraphic
反义词: modern (in the context of writing styles)
词性:adjective
