nonmelting:/ˌnɒnˈmɛltɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɛltɪŋ/
adjective
不熔化的；耐熔的
Not melting; resistant to melting.
This material is nonmelting at high temperatures. 这种材料在高温下不熔化。
The spacecraft's heat shield is made of a nonmelting material.  宇宙飞船的隔热罩由不熔化的材料制成。
近义词:infusible, refractory
反义词:meltable, fusible
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not."
melting (present participle of melt): from Old English meltan, of Germanic origin.
