auncel:/ˈɔːnsəl/| n.|盎司（旧时重量单位，尤指用于称丝绸、药品等）;少量|A unit of weight, especially for silk or drugs, varying in different countries and at different periods.  It was usually one sixteenth of a pound avoirdupois.|The recipe called for an auncel of saffron.  这配方需要一盎司藏红花。|近义词:ounce, dram|反义词:ton, pound|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old North French *auncelle, *aucelle (compare Old French auncelle, aucelle (“unit of weight”)), from Latin uncia (“twelfth part, ounce”).  Doublet of *inch* and *ounce*.
