overflogging:/ˌoʊvərˈflɑːɡɪŋ/
n.
过度鞭打，过度责罚
Excessive flogging or whipping; punishment that is too harsh or severe.
The overflogging of the prisoner led to his death.  过度鞭打囚犯导致了他的死亡。
The public outcry against the overflogging of the sailors forced the navy to change its disciplinary practices.  公众对过度鞭打水手的强烈抗议迫使海军改变了其纪律措施。
近义词: excessive punishment, brutalization, cruelty
反义词: leniency, mercy, compassion
词性: 名词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "flog" (verb meaning "to whip or beat severely")
