curer:/ˈkjʊərər/ (US), /ˈkjʊərə(r)/ (UK)|n.|治疗者，治愈者|A person or thing that cures a disease or ailment. |例句：He is a renowned curer of cancer. 他是一位著名的癌症治疗专家。

例句：This herb is considered a curer for many illnesses. 这种草药被认为是治疗多种疾病的良药。|近义词：healer, therapist, physician|反义词:sickener, aggravator|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *cureor, from curer (“to cure”), from Latin cūrāre (“to take care of”).
