alaric:/ˈælərɪk/ | n. | 阿拉里克 |  A male given name, of Visigothic origin, meaning "all-ruler".  Refers most famously to Alaric I, king of the Visigoths (c. 370 – 410 AD), who sacked Rome in 410 AD. |  His parents named him Alaric after the Visigoth king.  他的父母以西哥特国王的名字给他取名为阿拉里克。 | 近义词: (No direct synonyms for a proper noun, could consider other Visigothic names like Ataulf or Theodoric) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
