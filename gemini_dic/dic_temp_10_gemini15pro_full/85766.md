haybird:/ˈheɪbɜːrd/| n. |乡巴佬，土包子|A derogatory term for a person from a rural area, implying simplicity or lack of sophistication.  It suggests someone is unsophisticated, naive, or easily fooled.|He called me a haybird, but I'm proud of my country roots.|近义词：yokel, hick, rube, country bumpkin|反义词:sophisticate, cosmopolitan|词性:noun
