get-down:/ˈɡet daʊn/ (US), /ɡet daʊn/ (UK)| phrasal verb |下来；使沮丧；开始认真做；写下；吞下|To descend; to depress or demoralize; to begin to work seriously; to write down; to swallow.|例句：The cat got down from the tree. 猫从树上下来了。|近义词：descend, depress, record, swallow|反义词：ascend, uplift|词性：动词短语

get-down:/ˈɡet daʊn/ (US), /ɡet daʊn/ (UK)| n. |沮丧；令人沮丧的事|A state of depression; something that causes depression.|例句：He's been in a real get-down since he lost his job. 他失业后一直很沮丧。|近义词：depression, blues|反义词：elation, happiness|词性：名词
