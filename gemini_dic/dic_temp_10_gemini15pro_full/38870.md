confederative:/kənˈfɛdərətɪv/
adj.
联盟的，联邦的
Relating to or based on a confederation; constituting a league or alliance.
The confederative structure of the Iroquois League allowed six Native American nations to coexist peacefully.  易洛魁联盟的联邦结构允许六个美洲原住民国家和平共处。
The newly formed nation adopted a confederative system of government. 这个新成立的国家采用了联邦制的政府体制。
近义词: federal, allied, federated, united
反义词: unitary, centralized
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *confederativus, from Latin confœderatus (past participle of confœderare ‘unite by treaty’), from con- ‘together’ + fœderare ‘make a treaty’, from fœdus ‘treaty’.
