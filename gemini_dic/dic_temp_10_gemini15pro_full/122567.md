moderateness:/ˈmɒdərɪtnəs/ /ˈmɑːdərɪtnəs/| n. |适度；温和；中庸|The quality of being moderate; avoidance of extremes.|He urged people to show moderateness and tolerance. 他敦促人们表现出适度和宽容。|近义词：moderation, temperance, restraint|反义词：extremism, immoderateness, excess|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French *moderesce, from late Latin moderantia, from moderant- ‘moderating’, from the verb moderari (see moderate).
