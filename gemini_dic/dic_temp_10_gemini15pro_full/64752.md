escritorial:/ˌɛskrɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective|书房的；著述的；写作的|Relating to writing or a place for writing; characteristic of a writer's study or work.|The room had an escritorial feel, with bookshelves lining the walls and a large wooden desk in the center. 这个房间有一种书房的感觉，墙上排列着书架，中间有一张大木桌。|近义词:literary, scholarly, academic|反义词:illiterate, unscholarly|词性:adjective

escritorial:/ˌɛskrɪˈtɔːriəl/
noun|书房；著作|A writing desk or study; the writings of a particular author.|He retreated to his escritorial to work on his novel. 他回到书房去写小说。|近义词:study, office, library|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *escritorial*, from *escritorio* ("writing desk"), ultimately from Latin *scriptorium* ("a place for writing").
