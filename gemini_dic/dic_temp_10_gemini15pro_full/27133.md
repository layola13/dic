calisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/| n. |健美操，徒手体操|Calisthenics are exercises that use your body weight for resistance training. 健美操是利用你的体重进行阻力训练的练习。|例句：She practices calisthenics every morning to stay fit. 她每天早上练习健美操以保持健康。

例句：Calisthenics is a great way to build strength and flexibility. 健美操是增强力量和柔韧性的好方法。|近义词：exercises, workout, bodyweight training|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Greek *kalos* "beautiful" + *sthenos* "strength".  源自希腊语 *kalos* “美丽” + *sthenos* “力量”。
