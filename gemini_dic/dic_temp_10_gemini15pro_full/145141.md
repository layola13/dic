overasserted:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪd/ | adj. | 过分自信的，过分武断的 | Excessively assertive; too confident or forceful in stating one's opinions or claims.  Characterized by excessive or inappropriate assertion. | He was often perceived as overasserted in meetings, interrupting colleagues and dominating discussions. 他在会议上经常被认为过于自信，打断同事并主导讨论。|  The overasserted salesman annoyed the customer with his pushy tactics.  这个过于自信的推销员用他咄咄逼人的策略惹恼了顾客。 | Synonyms: pushy, aggressive, domineering, overbearing, presumptuous | Antonyms: timid, shy, reserved, unassuming, modest | adjective
