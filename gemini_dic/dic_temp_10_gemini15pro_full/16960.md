bassorin:/ˈbæsərɪn/| n.|巴索林|A complex gummy carbohydrate found in gum tragacanth. It swells in water but does not dissolve.|Bassorin is used as a thickening agent in some foods and pharmaceuticals.|Bassorin absorbs water to form a gel.|近义词: tragacanth, gum tragacanth|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a dissolving agent?)|词性:noun
