overlier:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪər/| n.|异常值，离群值|A data point that differs significantly from other observations.|The overlier on the scatter plot suggested an error in data collection. 散点图上的异常值表明数据收集过程中存在错误。|Example:  Removing the overliers from the dataset gave a more accurate representation of the trend. 从数据集中移除异常值可以更准确地表示趋势。|Synonyms: anomaly, outlier, exception, aberration|Antonyms: norm, average, standard|Noun

overlie:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪ/| v.|覆盖，叠加|To lie on top of or cover something.|A thick layer of snow overlaid the fields. 厚厚的积雪覆盖了田野。|Example: Sedimentary rock often overlies older igneous rock. 沉积岩通常覆盖在较老的火成岩之上。|Synonyms: cover, overlay, blanket, coat|Antonyms: uncover, expose, reveal|Verb
