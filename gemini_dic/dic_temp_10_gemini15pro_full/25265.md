buffeting:/ˈbʌfɪtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbʌfɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 冲击，打击，（风雨的）袭击 | The continuous buffeting of the waves against the shore eroded the beach. 海浪不断冲击海岸，侵蚀了海滩。|例句：The small boat was buffeting by strong winds. 小船受到强风的冲击。 小船被强风冲击着。| 近义词：battering, pounding, thrashing | 反义词： protecting, shielding | 词性：名词

buffeting:/ˈbʌfɪtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbʌfɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 冲击的，打击的 | The buffeting winds made it difficult to walk.  猛烈的风刮得人难以行走。| 例句：The buffeting winds and rain made driving conditions treacherous.  狂风暴雨使驾驶条件变得危险。 狂风暴雨使得驾驶条件变得危险。|近义词：pounding, battering |反义词：calm, gentle |词性：形容词

词源: From the verb "buffet," from Old French "buffeter" meaning "to strike repeatedly," probably of imitative origin.
