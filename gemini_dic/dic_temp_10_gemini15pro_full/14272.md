autogenous:/ɔːˈtɒdʒɪnəs/ /ɔːˈtɑːdʒɪnəs/
adjective
自生的；自体的；自发的
(of a disease) originating within the body.  (of a material, such as steel or rocks) produced by natural causes, without external influence.  (of a graft) taken from the patient's own body.
例句：Autogenous infection can be difficult to treat.  自体感染可能难以治疗。

例句：The autogenous tissue graft was successful.  自体组织移植成功了。

近义词：self-generated, innate, indigenous
反义词：exogenous, acquired

词性: adjective
