duopolist:/duːˈɒpəlɪst/ /duˈɑːpəlɪst/
n.
双头垄断者；双寡头垄断者
One of two sellers in a duopoly.  
The two duopolists agreed to fix prices. 这两家双头垄断者同意固定价格。

近义词:oligopolist
反义词:monopolist
词性:noun


Etymology->
From duo- + -polist, from Greek pólēs "seller".  Coined in English, 1907.
