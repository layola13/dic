briquets:/brɪˈkɛts/
|n.|煤球，煤砖，引火块|A compressed block of flammable material, typically charcoal, sawdust, or coal dust, used for fuel.|He lit the barbecue with a few briquets. 他用几个引火块点燃了烧烤架。|Synonyms: briquette, block, cake |Antonyms: kindling (in some contexts, as briquets are a more processed form of kindling)|noun
briquette:/brɪˈkɛt/
|n.|煤球，煤砖，引火块|A compressed block of flammable material, typically charcoal, sawdust, or coal dust, used for fuel.|She used a briquette to start the fire in the fireplace. 她用一块煤砖在壁炉里生火。|Synonyms: briquet, block, cake|Antonyms: kindling (in some contexts, as briquettes are a more processed form of kindling)|noun
