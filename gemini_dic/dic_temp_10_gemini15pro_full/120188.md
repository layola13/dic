mindset:/ˈmaɪndset/ | n. | 心态；思维模式 | A person's way of thinking and their opinions, especially when these are shared by a group of people. | Example 1:  He has a very positive mindset. 他心态很积极。

Example 2:  It's important to have a growth mindset when learning new skills. 学习新技能时，拥有成长型思维至关重要。 | Synonym: attitude, outlook, mentality, frame of mind | Antonym: negativity, closed-mindedness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mind" (referring to mental processes) + "set" (a fixed or established state).  The term became popular in the late 20th century, largely due to the work of psychologist Carol Dweck.
