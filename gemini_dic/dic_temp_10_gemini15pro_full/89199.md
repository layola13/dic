homesick:/ˈhoʊmsɪk/ (US) /ˈhəʊmsɪk/ (UK)| adj.|想家的，思乡的|Experiencing a longing for one's home and family.|例句：She felt homesick after being away for only a week. 她离家才一周就感到想家了。|例句：He was so homesick that he called his parents every day. 他非常想家，每天都给父母打电话。|近义词：nostalgic, lonesome|反义词:content, happy|词性:adjective
