inevidence:/ɪn ˈɛvɪdəns/
adjective
明显的，显而易见的
clearly visible or obvious; easily noticeable
Her anxiety was in evidence as she paced back and forth. 她来回踱步，焦虑显而易见。
Synonyms: apparent, visible, noticeable, obvious, manifest, conspicuous, plain, clear
Antonyms: hidden, concealed, inconspicuous, unnoticeable
词性: adjective
