mwini: /ˈmwini/ | n. | owner, proprietor |  A Bantu word (primarily Chichewa/Nyanja) meaning "owner" or "proprietor". Refers to the person who possesses or has rights to something. | The mwini of the house welcomed the guests.  房子的主人欢迎客人们。| synonyms: owner, possessor, proprietor | antonyms: renter, tenant | noun

mwini: /ˈmwini/ | pron. | self, oneself (reflexive) | A Bantu word (primarily Chichewa/Nyanja) used as a reflexive pronoun, meaning "self" or "oneself." Often used with possessive pronouns to emphasize ownership. |  Anadziphikira mwini wake. She cooked for herself. 她为自己做饭。| synonyms: himself, herself, itself, themselves, myself, yourself (reflexive) | antonyms: N/A | pronoun
