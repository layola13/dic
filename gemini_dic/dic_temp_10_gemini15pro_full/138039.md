nordic:/ˈnɔːrdɪk/ | adj. |北欧的；北欧人的；斯堪的纳维亚的|Relating to Scandinavia, Finland, Iceland, and sometimes also Greenland and the Faroe Islands. |例句：She has a classic Nordic look. 她有着典型的北欧人的长相。|例句：Nordic countries are known for their high quality of life. 北欧国家以其高生活质量而闻名。|近义词：Scandinavian|反义词：Southern, Mediterranean|词性：形容词

词源: From Old Norse *norðr "north" + -ic.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nurþrą. Related to English north.
