balan:/ˈbælən/| n.| 平衡，余额，天平 |A state of equilibrium or equipoise; equality in amount or value.| The company's balan sheet shows a healthy financial position. 该公司的资产负债表显示其财务状况良好。|synonyms: equilibrium, stability, symmetry|antonyms: imbalance, instability, asymmetry|noun
balan:/ˈbælən/| v.| 使平衡，称重 |To bring into or maintain in a state of equilibrium; to weigh.|  He balanced the book on his head. 他把书顶在头上保持平衡。|synonyms: stabilize, steady, equalize|antonyms: destabilize, unbalance, upset|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "balance" (noun and verb), and directly from Late Latin "bilancia" meaning "balance, pair of scales," from Latin "bis" (twice) + "lanx" (plate, dish, pan of a balance).
