gern:/ɡɛrn/
adv.
willingly; gladly; readily; with pleasure
欣然地；乐意地；容易地；愉快地
She gern helps others. 她乐于助人。
Synonyms: readily, willingly, gladly
Antonyms: reluctantly, unwillingly
词性: 副词
