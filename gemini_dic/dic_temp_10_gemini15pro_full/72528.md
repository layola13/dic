flushness:/ˈflʌʃnɪs/| n.|齐平；平整；|The flushness of the surface is remarkable. 表面的平整度非常出色。|近义词：levelness, evenness, smoothness|反义词: unevenness, roughness|noun
Etymology-> From flush + -ness. Flush meaning level or even ultimately derives from the Latin "fluxus," meaning "a flowing." The "-ness" suffix transforms the adjective "flush" into the noun "flushness."
