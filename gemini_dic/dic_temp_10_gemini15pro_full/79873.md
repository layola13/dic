gnarlinesses:/ˈnɑːrlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
复杂性，棘手性；粗糙，多节
the quality or state of being gnarly, especially the complex, difficult, or challenging nature of something.  Also refers to the twisted, knotted, or rugged appearance of something.
The gnarlinesses of the old oak tree's branches were impressive. 这棵老橡树枝干的粗糙和多节令人印象深刻。
The gnarlinesses of the legal issues made the case difficult to resolve.  法律问题的复杂性使案件难以解决。
complexity, difficulty, intricacy, knottiness, ruggedness
simplicity, smoothness, easiness
noun
