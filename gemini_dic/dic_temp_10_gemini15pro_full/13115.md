astrut:/əˈstrʌt/| adj.|神气活泼的，昂首阔步的|Walking with a proud, confident gait, often with the head held high and chest out.  Showing off in a confident way.|She strutted across the stage, astrut with confidence. 她昂首阔步地走过舞台，神采飞扬。|Synonyms: swaggering, pompous, vain, conceited|Antonyms: humble, modest, shy, unassuming|adjective
