deltafication:/ˌdɛltɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
三角洲化，三角洲形成（作用）
The process by which a river forms a delta.
例句：Deltafication is a complex process influenced by factors such as river discharge, sediment load, and coastal processes. 三角洲化是一个复杂的过程，受河流流量、沉积物负荷和海岸过程等因素的影响。

例句：The deltafication of the Nile River has created fertile agricultural land for centuries. 尼罗河的三角洲化作用几个世纪以来创造了肥沃的农业用地。

近义词：delta formation, delta development
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider) erosion, coastal retreat
