fuguing:/ˈfjuːɡɪŋ/
adj.
赋格的，模仿的
Resembling or suggestive of a fugue, especially in having interwoven or overlapping parts.  Imitating the style of a fugue, a musical form characterized by the systematic imitation of a principal theme (the subject) in different voices.
The composer's fuguing style is evident in his later works.  这位作曲家赋格的风格在他的后期作品中很明显。
The fuguing melodies created a rich and complex tapestry of sound.  交织的旋律创造出丰富而复杂的音响织锦。
Synonyms:  interwoven, overlapping, imitative, contrapuntal
Antonyms:  simple, homophonic
