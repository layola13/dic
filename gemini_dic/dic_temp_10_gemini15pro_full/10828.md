aquitanian:/ˌæk.wɪˈteɪ.ni.ən/ | adj. n.|阿基坦的；阿基坦人|Relating to or characteristic of Aquitaine, a historical region in southwestern France.  A native or inhabitant of Aquitaine.|例句：The Aquitanian basin is known for its wine production. 阿基坦盆地以其葡萄酒生产而闻名。|例句：He is an Aquitanian by birth. 他是阿基坦人。|近义词：Gascon (sometimes), southwestern French|反义词:(No direct antonym; could use northern French, depending on context)|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Latin *Aquitanus*, from *Aquitani* (the people of Aquitaine), of unknown origin.
