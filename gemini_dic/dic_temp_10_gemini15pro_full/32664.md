chitterlings:/ˈtʃɪt(ə)rlɪŋz/| n. |猪小肠|The small intestines of a pig, usually fried and eaten as food.|My grandmother used to make the best fried chitterlings. 我祖母过去做的炸猪小肠是最好吃的。|Synonym: chitlins|Antonym: None|Noun


chitterlingses: This word is non-standard and grammatically incorrect.  The plural of "chitterlings" is simply "chitterlings."  There is no "chitterlingses."
