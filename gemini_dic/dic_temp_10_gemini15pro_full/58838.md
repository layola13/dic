duplexes:/ˈdjuːpleksɪz/ /ˈduːpleksɪz/| n.|复式公寓，双拼别墅|A duplex is a house divided into two apartments, with a separate entrance for each.|They're building new duplexes down the street. 他们在街上建造新的双拼别墅。|The duplexes offer spacious living for two families.  这些复式公寓为两个家庭提供了宽敞的居住空间。|近义词：two-family house, semi-detached house|反义词:single-family home|词性:noun

词源: From the French word "duplex," meaning "double" or "twofold."  Ultimately from Latin "duo" (two) + "plicō" (to fold).
