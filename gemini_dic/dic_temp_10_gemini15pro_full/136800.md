nonsexuals:/nɒnˈsɛkʃuəlz/ | n. | 无性恋者 |  People who do not experience sexual attraction or have little or no interest in sexual activity. |  Example:  The study focused on the experiences of nonsexuals in romantic relationships. 这项研究的重点是无性恋者在恋爱关系中的经历。

Example:  Nonsexuals can form deep and meaningful connections without sex. 无性恋者可以在没有性的情况下建立深刻而有意义的联系。 | 近义词: asexuals | 反义词: sexuals | 词性: 名词


词源: non- + sexual + -s
non-: prefix meaning "not"
sexual: relating to sex or sexuality
-s: plural suffix
