coagulatory:/koʊˈæɡjʊləˌtɔːri/
adjective
凝血的，促凝的
Causing coagulation; tending to coagulate.
The doctor prescribed coagulatory medicine to stop the bleeding. 医生开了促凝血药物来止血。
Coagulatory factors are essential for blood clotting. 凝血因子对血液凝固至关重要。

synonym: clotting, thickening
antonym: anticoagulant, thinning
