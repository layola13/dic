achieve: /əˈtʃiːv/ (US) /əˈtʃiːv/ (UK)
verb
实现，达到，完成
To successfully bring about or reach (a desired objective, level, or result) by effort, skill, or courage.
She worked hard and finally achieved her goal of becoming a doctor. 她努力工作，终于实现了成为一名医生的目标。

He achieved success through hard work and determination. 他通过努力工作和决心获得了成功。

近义词: accomplish, attain, gain, obtain, reach
反义词: fail, forfeit, lose, miss
词性: verb


USAGE->

He achieves his goals through hard work. = 他通过努力工作实现了他的目标。
