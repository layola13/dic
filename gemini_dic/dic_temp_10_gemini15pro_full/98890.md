intensative:/ɪnˈtɛnsətɪv/ | adj. | 加强语气的，强调的 |  An intensifying word or expression, used to give force or emphasis. |  "Really" is an intensative adverb.  “really”是一个加强语气的副词。| synonyms:  emphasizing, strengthening,  intensifying | antonyms: weakening, mitigating, softening | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intensivus, from Latin intendere "to stretch out, strain," from in- "toward" + tendere "to stretch."
