lupulinic:/ˌluːpjʊˈlɪnɪk/ | adj. |  啤酒花的，含蛇麻腺的 | Relating to or containing lupulin, the bitter, aromatic, yellowish powder found in the cones of the hop plant. | The lupulinic glands give beer its characteristic bitter flavor. 蛇麻腺赋予啤酒特有的苦味。 | The lupulinic aroma filled the brewery. 啤酒厂里充满了啤酒花的香味。 |  近义词: hoppy | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

lupulin:/ˈluːpjʊlɪn/ | n. | 蛇麻素，啤酒花苦味素 | A yellowish powder found in the cones of the hop plant, giving beer its characteristic bitter flavor. |  Lupulin is responsible for the bitterness and aroma of beer. 蛇麻素决定了啤酒的苦味和香味。 |  The brewers carefully added lupulin to the wort.  酿酒师小心地将蛇麻素加入麦芽汁中。 | 近义词: hop resin | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
