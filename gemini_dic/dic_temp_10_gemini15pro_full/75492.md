fruticant:/ˈfruːtɪkənt/| adj.|灌木状的，矮灌木的|Growing low and shrubby; producing fruit on small, woody stems.|例句：The fruticant plant thrived in the rocky terrain. 这种灌木状植物在岩石地形中茁壮成长。

例句：The botanist specialized in fruticant species of the region.  这位植物学家专门研究该地区的灌木物种。|近义词：shrubby, bushy, fruticose|反义词：arborescent, arboreal|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *fruticans*, present participle of *fruticare* "to put forth shoots, sprout," from *frutex* "shrub, bush."
