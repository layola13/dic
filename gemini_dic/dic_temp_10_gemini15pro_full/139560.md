obtuseness:/əbˈtuːsnəs/| n.|迟钝，愚钝|The state of being obtuse; lacking sharpness of intellect; dullness of perception or understanding.|His obtuseness to social cues made him awkward in conversations. 他对社交暗示的迟钝让他在谈话中显得笨拙。|Synonyms: dullness, insensitivity, stupidity, slowness, density|Antonyms: sharpness, acuity, intelligence, brilliance, astuteness|noun

obtuse:/əbˈtuːs/| adj.|迟钝的，愚钝的|Lacking sharpness of intellect; not quick or alert in perception, feeling, or reaction; not sensitive or observant; dull.|He was so obtuse that he didn't realize she was flirting with him. 他太迟钝了，竟然没意识到她在和他调情。|Synonyms: dull, insensitive, stupid, slow, dense|Antonyms: sharp, acute, intelligent, brilliant, astute|adjective
