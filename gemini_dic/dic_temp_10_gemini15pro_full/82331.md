groveling:/ˈɡrɒvəlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡrɑːvəlɪŋ/ (US)| adj. vi.|卑躬屈膝的，匍匐的；奴颜婢膝地行事|Acting in a subservient manner, behaving obsequiously, often to gain favor or forgiveness.|He was groveling at his boss's feet, begging for another chance. 他卑躬屈膝地跪在老板脚下，乞求再给他一次机会。|近义词：fawning, obsequious, servile, sycophantic|反义词：dignified, proud, upright|词性: adjective, verb (intransitive)
