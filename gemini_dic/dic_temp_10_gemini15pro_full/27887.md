cantabrigian: /kænˈtæbˈrɪdʒɪən/ | n. adj. |剑桥人，剑桥的|A native or inhabitant of Cambridge, England, or a member of the University of Cambridge.  Relating to Cambridge, England, or its university.|例句：He's a Cantabrigian, having studied at Trinity College. 他是剑桥人，曾在三一学院学习。|例句：The cantabrigian atmosphere is known for its intellectual rigor. 剑桥的氛围以其学术严谨而闻名。|近义词：Cambridge resident, Cambridge student, Cambridge graduate|反义词: N/A |词性: 名词，形容词


Etymology:  From Latin *Cantabrigia*, the medieval Latin name for Cambridge. Ultimately derived from Brittonic *Cantobrigia* meaning "bridge on the River Cam".
