octastyle: /ˈɒktəstaɪl/ (UK) /ˈɑːktəstaɪl/ (US)| adjective, noun | 八柱式的；有八根柱子的门廊 | Having or consisting of eight columns in the front, especially of a classical temple or building.  | The Parthenon is a well-known example of an octastyle temple.  帕特农神庙是一个著名的八柱式神庙的例子。| The architect designed an octastyle portico for the grand entrance.  这位建筑师为大入口设计了一个八柱式的门廊。 | 近义词: eight-columned | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider temples with different numbers of columns, e.g., hexastyle, tetrastyle) | 词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Greek *oktás 'eight' + *stûlos 'column'.
