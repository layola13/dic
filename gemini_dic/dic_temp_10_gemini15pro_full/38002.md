complimentaries:/ˌkɒmplɪˈment(ə)riz/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈment(ə)riz/
noun
免费赠送的物品（通常指酒店或航空公司提供的）; 赞美; 敬意
Items or services offered free of charge, typically by a hotel or airline; expressions of praise or admiration.
The hotel offers complimentary breakfast and Wi-Fi.  这家酒店提供免费早餐和无线网络。
She received numerous complimentaries on her performance. 她因表演而受到了无数的赞美。
Synonyms: freebies, perks, gifts, praises, accolades
Antonyms: payments, charges, criticisms, insults
