orthodox: /ˈɔːrθədɒks/ (UK) /ˈɔːrθədɑːks/ (US) | adj. | 正统的；传统的；规范的 | Conforming to what is generally or traditionally accepted as right or true; established and approved. |  The church's doctrines are very orthodox.  教会的教义非常正统。| synonyms: conventional, traditional, established, accepted, standard, customary, regular, normal, conservative | antonyms: unorthodox, unconventional, heterodox, heretical, radical, revolutionary | adjective

orthodox: /ˈɔːrθədɒks/ (UK) /ˈɔːrθədɑːks/ (US) | n. | 正统的人；正统的事物 |  A person who adheres to the accepted or established faith or beliefs.  | He is considered an orthodox in his field. 他在他的领域被认为是正统派。 | synonyms: conformist, traditionalist | antonyms: nonconformist, radical, maverick | noun
