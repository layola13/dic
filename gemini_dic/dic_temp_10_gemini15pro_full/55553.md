distringas:/dɪˈstrɪŋɡæs/| n.|扣押令，扣押财产的命令|A writ authorizing the seizure of someone's goods to compel appearance in court or to enforce payment of a debt.|The court issued a distringas against the defendant's assets.|Synonym: warrant, seizure, attachment|Antonym: release|noun

distringas:/dɪˈstrɪŋɡæs/| n.|停止过户通知书|A notice forbidding a company to allow the transfer of stock or shares held in a particular name.|The claimant obtained a distringas to prevent the defendant from selling the disputed shares.|Synonym: injunction, freeze, hold|Antonym: allowance, permission|noun
