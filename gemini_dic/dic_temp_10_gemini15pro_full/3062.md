aerogun: /ˈɛːroʊɡʌn/ | n. | 气枪，空气枪 | A gun that propels projectiles by means of compressed air. | He used an aerogun to shoot targets in the backyard. 他在后院用气枪射击靶子。

The boy was playing with his new aerogun.  这个男孩正在玩他的新气枪。| 近义词: air gun, air rifle, BB gun | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "firearm" as it uses a different propellant) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + gun.  "Aero" comes from the Greek word "aer" meaning "air".
