craftiest:/ˈkræftiɪst/| adj.|最狡猾的，最灵巧的|Superlative form of crafty: most crafty.  Having or showing skill in achieving one's ends by deceit or evasion.|She was the craftiest politician I'd ever met. 她是我见过的最狡猾的政治家。|近义词：sly, cunning, wily, artful, shrewd|反义词：honest, naive, guileless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crafti*, from Old English *cræftig* (“strong, powerful, skillful, cunning, crafty”), from *cræft* (“strength, skill, power”) + *-ig*. Cognate with Dutch *kraftig* (“powerful, strong”), German *kräftig* (“powerful, strong”), Swedish *kraftig* (“powerful, strong”).
