anaesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ (UK), /ˌænɪsˈθiːziə/ (US)| n.|麻醉；麻木|A loss of feeling or awareness, typically induced by drugs for medical purposes.|The patient was given general anaesthesia before the surgery. 病人在手术前进行了全身麻醉。|近义词：narcosis, numbness|反义词: sensibility, consciousness|词性:noun

anesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ (US)| n.|麻醉；麻木|A loss of feeling or awareness, typically induced by drugs for medical purposes.|The patient received local anesthesia for the dental procedure. 病人在牙科手术中接受了局部麻醉。|近义词：narcosis, numbness|反义词: sensibility, consciousness|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀναισθησία (anaisthesía, “insensibility”), from ἀν- (an-, “without, not”) + αἴσθησις (aísthēsis, “sensation, feeling”).
