industrialists:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪsts/
n.
工业家；实业家
A person who owns or controls large industrial enterprises.
The industrialists of the 19th century amassed great fortunes. 19世纪的工业家积累了巨大的财富。
The government offered tax breaks to attract industrialists to the region. 政府提供税收优惠以吸引工业家到该地区投资。
synonyms: manufacturer, tycoon, magnate, entrepreneur, business leader, captain of industry
antonyms: worker, laborer, employee

ETYMOLOGY->
From industrial + -ist.  "Industrial" comes from  the Late Latin "industrialis"  meaning "relating to industry". "-ist" is a suffix forming agent nouns.
