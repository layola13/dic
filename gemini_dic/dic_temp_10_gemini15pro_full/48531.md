deface:/dɪˈfeɪs/ | vt. | 污损，毁坏容貌，涂抹 | To mar the surface or appearance of; disfigure. | Vandals defaced the statue with graffiti. 故意破坏公物者用涂鸦污损了雕像。| synonyms: mar, spoil, damage, vandalize | antonyms: restore, repair, beautify | verb

deface:/dɪˈfeɪs/ | vt. | 损害，玷污（名誉等）| To injure or impair, as by effacing or obliterating something essential. |  The scandal defaced his reputation.  这件丑闻损害了他的名誉。| synonyms: tarnish, blemish, sully | antonyms: enhance, improve, elevate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desfacer, from des- (expressing removal, reversal) + face (face).  Ultimately from Latin facia ("face, form, appearance").
