nonjurancy:/nɒnˈdʒʊərənsi/
n.
拒绝宣誓效忠；不宣誓
The state of being a nonjuror; refusal to take an oath of allegiance.
The nonjurancy of the Scottish bishops caused a schism in the Church. 苏格兰主教的拒绝宣誓效忠导致了教会的分裂。
近义词：recusancy, dissent, refusal
反义词：allegiance, loyalty, obedience
词性：名词

nonjuror:/nɒnˈdʒʊərər/
n.
拒服誓者；不宣誓者
A person who refuses to take an oath, especially an oath of allegiance.
Many nonjurors were persecuted for their beliefs. 许多拒服誓者因其信仰而受到迫害。
近义词：recusant, dissenter, objector
反义词：loyalist, conformist
词性：名词
