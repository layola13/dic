inspectingly:/ɪnˈspɛktɪŋli/ | adv. | 检查地，审视地 | In a manner of close and critical examination. | He looked at the contract inspectingly before signing it.  他仔细审视了合同才签字。|  closely, carefully, critically, scrutinizingly | cursorily, casually | adverb

Etymology->
From "inspecting" + "-ly." "Inspecting" comes from the Latin "inspectus," the past participle of "inspicere," meaning "to look into."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
