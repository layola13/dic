hugeness:/ˈhjuːdʒnəs/ | n. |巨大；庞大|The hugeness of the task before them was daunting.  任务的艰巨性令他们感到畏惧。|  The sheer hugeness of the universe is difficult to comprehend. 宇宙的浩瀚难以理解。 | 近义词: immensity, vastness, enormity | 反义词: smallness, tininess, diminutiveness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *hugenesse, derived from huge + -ness.  Ultimately from Old Norse *haugr.
