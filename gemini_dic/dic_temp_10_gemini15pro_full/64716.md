eschatologists:/ˌɛskəˈtɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
末世论者，研究末世论的人
A person who studies eschatology (the part of theology concerned with death, judgment, and the final destiny of the soul and of humankind).
Many eschatologists believe that the world will end in fire. 许多末世论者认为世界将终结于火。
The eschatologists debated the meaning of the prophecy. 末世论者们争论着预言的含义。
Synonyms: millennialists, apocalypticists
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider those who deny or refute eschatological ideas)
Noun


Etymology->
From Ancient Greek ἔσχατος (éskhatos, “last, furthest, uttermost, extreme”) +‎ -logy.
