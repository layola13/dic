moult:/moʊlt/ (US), /məʊlt/ (UK)| v. n.|蜕皮; 换毛; 脱落|The process of shedding periodically feathers, hair, skin, or other outer covering, which is then replaced by a new growth.|The snake moults its skin. 这条蛇蜕皮。|近义词：shed, cast off, slough|反义词:grow|词性:动词, 名词

moult:/moʊlt/ (US), /məʊlt/ (UK)| n.|蜕皮; 换毛; 脱落|The act or process of moulting.|The bird is in its moult. 这只鸟正在换羽。|近义词：shedding, exuviation|反义词:growth|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mūtian, from Proto-Germanic *mūtijaną (“to change, exchange”), from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“to change, exchange”). Cognate with Old Norse møyta (“to moult”).
