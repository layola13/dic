leafletted:/ˈliːflɪtɪd/
verb
散发传单；发传单
having distributed leaflets.  The streets were leafleted with advertisements for the new store.  街道上到处都是新店的广告传单。
The area was heavily leafleted before the election. 选举前，这个地区散发了大量的传单。
Synonyms: distribute leaflets, hand out flyers, circulate pamphlets, canvas
Antonyms: collect, gather
