overcommercialize: /ˌōvər-kəˈmərʃəˌlīz/ | v. | 过度商业化 | To exploit or promote (something) excessively for profit, often at the expense of quality or value. |  Many critics argue that Christmas has been overcommercialized. 许多评论家认为圣诞节已经被过度商业化了。 | The band was accused of overcommercializing their music.  这支乐队被指责过度商业化他们的音乐。 |  overexploit, oversaturate | underpromote, undersell | verb
