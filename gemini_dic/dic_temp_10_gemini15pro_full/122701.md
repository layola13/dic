moghul:/ˈmoʊɡʌl/| n. |莫卧儿，大亨|A member of the Muslim dynasty of Mongol origin founded by Babur that ruled India from the 16th to the 19th century; a powerful or influential person.|例句：The Moghul emperors were great patrons of the arts. 莫卧儿皇帝是艺术的伟大赞助人。

例句：He's a real moghul in the business world. 他是商界真正的大亨。|近义词：magnate, tycoon, emperor|反义词:pauper, commoner|词性:noun
