cru:/kruː/
n.| 原产地; 生长地; 特级佳酿(尤指葡萄酒)|A vineyard or region considered of high quality for wine production, especially in the Bordeaux region of France. |The wine is a cru classé from Saint-Estèphe. 这酒是圣达使徒夫的特级佳酿。|近义词: growth, vineyard |反义词: N/A|词性:名词

cru:/kruː/
adj.|特级佳酿的; 未稀释的; 生的; 未煮过的|(of wine) of superior quality, especially from a specific vineyard; (of food) raw or uncooked. |a bottle of cru Beaujolais 一瓶特级博若莱葡萄酒|近义词: vintage, superior, raw, uncooked |反义词: cooked, diluted|词性:形容词
