exigent:/ˈɛksɪdʒənt/| adj.|紧急的；迫切的；苛求的|Pressing; demanding.  Characterized by urgent needs or demands.|例句：The exigent circumstances required immediate action. 紧急情况需要立即采取行动。

例句：The hospital faced an exigent need for blood donations. 医院急需血液捐赠。|近义词：pressing, urgent, critical, imperative, immediate|反义词：minor, trivial, unimportant, nonessential|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *exigent- (stem of exigēns), present participle of exigere "to demand, require, exact, drive out," from ex- "out" + agere "to drive, lead, act."
