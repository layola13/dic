buzz:/bʌz/| n. v.|嗡嗡声，嘈杂声；使嗡嗡叫，发出嘈杂声|A low, continuous humming or vibrating sound, like that of a bee; to make such a sound.|The bee buzzed around the flower. 蜜蜂绕着花嗡嗡叫。|近义词：hum, drone, whirr|反义词:silence, quiet|noun, verb

buzz:/bʌz/| n. v.|兴奋，激动；使兴奋，使激动|A feeling of excitement, energy, and activity; to fill with excitement and activity.|There's a real buzz about the new product. 新产品引起了真正的轰动。|近义词：excitement, thrill, hype|反义词:apathy, boredom|noun, verb
