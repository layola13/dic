diapasm:/ˈdaɪəpɔːzəm/| n. |滞育，休眠|A period of suspended development in an insect, other invertebrate, or plant, especially during unfavorable environmental conditions.|The insect entered diapause during the winter months.  这种昆虫在冬季进入了滞育期。|近义词：dormancy, quiescence, hibernation|反义词：activity, growth|noun
