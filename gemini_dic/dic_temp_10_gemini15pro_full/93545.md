imaginationalism:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
想象主义;  空想主义
A doctrine that emphasizes the power and importance of imagination.  A tendency to indulge in fantasy or unrealistic ideas.
Her artwork is a vibrant expression of imaginationalism, filled with fantastical creatures and impossible landscapes. 她的艺术作品是想象主义的生动表达，充满了奇幻的生物和不可能的风景。
His imaginationalism led him to believe he could fly. 他的空想主义使他相信自己能飞。
近义词:fantasy, idealism, daydreaming
反义词:realism, pragmatism
词性:noun
