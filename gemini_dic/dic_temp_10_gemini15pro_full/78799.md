gilt-edged:/ˌɡɪltˈɛdʒd/
adjective
镀金边的；金边的；优良的；高收益低风险的
of the highest quality and reliability, especially relating to investments.
例句：Gilt-edged securities are considered a safe investment. 镀金证券被认为是安全的投资。

例句：She comes from a gilt-edged family background. 她来自一个优良的家庭背景。
近义词：high-grade, top-quality, blue-chip, prime, first-class, premium
反义词：low-grade, inferior, risky, speculative
词性: adjective
