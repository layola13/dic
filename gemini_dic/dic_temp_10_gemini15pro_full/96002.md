incurrent:/ɪnˈkʌrənt/ |adj.| 流入的；吸入的 |Carrying or drawing inwards; afferent.  Relating to a current flowing inwards. |The sponge's incurrent pores allow water to flow into its body. 海绵的流入孔允许水流入体内。|The incurrent canals bring oxygenated water to the gills. 流入管将含氧的水带到鳃。|近义词: afferent, incoming, inward |反义词: excurrent, outgoing, outward |词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *incurrent-*, present participle of *incurrere* "to run into, run against," from *in-* "in, into" + *currere* "to run."
