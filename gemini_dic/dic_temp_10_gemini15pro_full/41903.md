cosmic: /ˈkɒzmɪk/ (UK), /ˈkɑːzmɪk/ (US) | adj. | 宇宙的；无比巨大的 | Relating to the universe or cosmos, especially in its vastness; immense. |例句：Cosmic rays bombard the Earth constantly. 宇宙射线不断轰击地球。|例句：The earthquake was a disaster of cosmic proportions. 这场地震是场巨大无比的灾难。| 近义词：universal, global, vast, immense, astronomical | 反义词：terrestrial, earthly, small, minuscule | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *cosmicus, from Greek kosmikos "of the world, universal," from kosmos "order, good order, orderly arrangement," later "the world, the universe."
