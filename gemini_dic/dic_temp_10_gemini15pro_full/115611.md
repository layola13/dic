marcassin:/mɑːrkæˈsɪn/ |n.|小野猪，幼野猪|A young wild boar. |例句：The hunters tracked the marcassin through the forest. 猎人追踪小野猪穿过森林。

例句：The marcassin darted into the undergrowth. 小野猪飞快地跑进了灌木丛。|近义词：piglet, shoat|反义词：boar (成年野猪)|词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *marcassin, diminutive of Old French *marcas (modern French *marcas, now only found in the word sanglier *marcas, “wild boar”), from Gaulish *marco-, “horse”. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *marko-, “horse”.
