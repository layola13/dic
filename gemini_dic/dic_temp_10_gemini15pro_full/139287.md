obliterative:/əˈblɪtəreɪtɪv/
adjective
毁灭性的，消除的，抹杀的
Relating to or causing obliteration; tending to obliterate.  Destroying utterly; wiping out.
The disease had an obliterative effect on her memory. 这种疾病对她的记忆有毁灭性的影响。
The government is pursuing an obliterative policy towards the opposition. 政府正在推行一项旨在消灭反对派的政策。
synonyms:destructive, eradicative, extinguishing, extirpative
antonyms: constructive, preservative, restorative
词性:adjective


obliterate:/əˈblɪtəreɪt/
verb
消灭，抹去，摧毁
Destroy utterly; wipe out.
The bombing raid obliterated the entire city.  轰炸袭击摧毁了整个城市。
All traces of the ancient civilization were obliterated by time.  所有古代文明的痕迹都被时间抹去了。
synonyms:destroy, erase, annihilate, expunge
antonyms:create, preserve, restore
词性:verb
