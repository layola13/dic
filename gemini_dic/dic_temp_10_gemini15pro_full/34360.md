classicalists:/ˈklæsɪsɪsts/| n.|古典主义者；古典学者|Scholars or enthusiasts of classical antiquity, especially ancient Greek and Roman literature, art, and culture.|The conference attracted classicalists from all over the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的古典学者。|Synonyms: classicists, humanists, scholars|Antonyms: modernist, contemporary|noun
