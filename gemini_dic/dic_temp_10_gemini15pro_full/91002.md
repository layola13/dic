husbandable:/ˈhʌzbəndəbəl/
adjective
可节约的，可管理的，可持久的
capable of being used or managed carefully and without waste; capable of being conserved
Farmers need to find more husbandable ways of irrigating their crops. 农民需要找到更节约的灌溉作物的方法。
The country's natural resources must be treated as a husbandable asset.  这个国家的自然资源必须被视为可持续利用的资产。
近义词:conservable, manageable, sustainable, thrifty
反义词:wasteful, extravagant, unsustainable
词性:adjective

词源:
From husband (v.) + -able.  The original sense was "capable of being managed or economized," specifically of a household.  The sense extended to other resources over time.
