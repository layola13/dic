endocrinologist:/ˌɛndəkrɪˈnɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 内分泌学家 | A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the endocrine system, which includes glands that produce hormones. |  My doctor referred me to an endocrinologist to manage my thyroid condition. 我的医生让我去看内分泌科医生来控制我的甲状腺疾病。

Endocrinologists treat hormonal imbalances. 内分泌学家治疗激素失衡。 |  Synonym: hormone specialist | Antonym: N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  endo- (within) + -crine (to separate) + -ologist (one who studies).  Ultimately from Greek endon (within) and krinein (to separate).
