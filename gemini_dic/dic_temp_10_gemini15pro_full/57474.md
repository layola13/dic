draftsman:/ˈdræftsmən/ /ˈdrɑːftsmən/ | n. | 制图员；起草人 | A person who draws plans and sketches, especially of technical things.  |  The draftsman prepared the blueprints for the new building.  这位制图员准备了新大楼的蓝图。 |  designer, architect, illustrator | writer, author (for drafting documents) | noun
Etymology: From draft + -s- + man.  Draft ultimately from Old Norse *drapta, "to draw, drag."
