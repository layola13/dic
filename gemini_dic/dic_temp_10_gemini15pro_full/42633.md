counterscarf: /ˈkaʊntərˌskɑːrf/ | n. | 对披巾; 领巾;  | A long scarf worn over the shoulders, often crossed and tied in front.  A decorative cloth draped over a piece of furniture, such as a piano or table. | She wore a beautiful counterscarf over her dress. 她穿着一件漂亮的对披巾在她的连衣裙外面。

The counterscarf on the piano protected it from dust. 钢琴上的巾子保护它免受灰尘侵袭。 | 近义词: scarf, shawl, wrap | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From counter- + scarf.  "Counter-" in this context likely refers to the scarf being worn "against" or "over" the clothing.
