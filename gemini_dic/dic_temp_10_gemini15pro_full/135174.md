nonoptically:/ˌnɒnˈɒptɪkli/
adverb
非光学地
not involving or relating to optics or the use of light
The data was transmitted nonoptically, using radio waves. 数据以非光学方式传输，使用的是无线电波。
近义词:electronically, mechanically
反义词:optically
词性:副词
