dermoskeleton:/ˈdɜːrməʊˌskelɪtən/ | n. | 外骨骼，皮骨骼 | An external skeleton, characteristic of many invertebrates, such as insects and crustaceans, that provides support and protection. | Many insects have a hard dermoskeleton made of chitin. 许多昆虫都具有由几丁质构成的坚硬外骨骼。 | exoskeleton, integument |  (No direct antonym) | noun
