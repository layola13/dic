formaldehyde:/fɔːrˈmældaɪhaɪd/ /fɔːrˈmæl.də.haɪd/| n. |甲醛|A colorless pungent gas in solution made by oxidizing methanol. It is used as a disinfectant and preservative, and in making synthetic resins.|Formaldehyde is used in the manufacture of building materials and many household products.|近义词:methanal|反义词:N/A|noun
