colostomy: /kəˈlɑːstəmi/ /kəˈlɒstəmi/
noun
结肠造口术
A surgical operation in which a part of the colon is brought through the abdominal wall to form an artificial anus.
The doctor recommended a colostomy due to the severity of his bowel disease.  医生由于他肠道疾病的严重程度建议进行结肠造口术。
After the colostomy, he had to learn how to manage the stoma.  结肠造口术后，他必须学习如何管理造口。
Synonym:  stoma surgery
Antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym for a surgical procedure)
