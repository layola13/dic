crepuscular:/krɪˈpʌskjʊlər/ | adj. | 暮色的；朦胧的；拂晓的；黄昏的 | Relating to twilight; dim; occurring or active during twilight. | Bats are crepuscular creatures, flying around at dusk and dawn.  蝙蝠是暮色动物，在黄昏和黎明时飞来飞去。 |  The crepuscular light cast long shadows across the lawn. 暮色使草坪上投下长长的影子。 | 近义词：twilight, dim, nocturnal | 反义词：diurnal, daylight | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *crepusculum* "twilight," diminutive of *creper* "dusky."
