cairnier:/ˈkɛərniər/ | adj. | 更像石堆的，更多石堆的 | More resembling a cairn; having more cairns. | The summit was cairnier than I expected, with piles of rocks marking the way. 山顶比我想象的更像石堆，一堆堆的石头标记着道路。|  The landscape became increasingly cairnier as we ascended the mountain.  随着我们登上山，地貌变得越来越像石堆。| 近义词: rocky, stony | 反义词: smooth, flat | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "cairn" (a man-made pile of stones) + "-ier" (comparative adjective suffix).  源自“cairn”（人造石堆）+“-ier”（比较级形容词后缀）。
