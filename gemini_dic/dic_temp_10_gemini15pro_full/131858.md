nonconsular:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːnsələr/
adjective
非领事的
Not relating to the duties or office of a consul.
A nonconsular passport is issued to individuals who are not diplomats or consular officials.  非领事护照发放给非外交官或领事官员的个人。
The nonconsular section of the embassy handles visa applications. 大使馆的非领事部门处理签证申请。
Synonyms: non-diplomatic, civilian
Antonyms: consular
词性: adjective
