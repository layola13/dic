eugenic:/juːˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
优生的；优生学的
Relating to or promoting the improvement of hereditary qualities in a given population, especially in humans.
例句：Eugenic policies were popular in the early 20th century.  20世纪初，优生政策很受欢迎。

例句：Some people believe that genetic engineering could be used for eugenic purposes.  有些人认为基因工程可以用于优生目的。
近义词：genetic, hereditary
反义词：dysgenic
词性：adjective
