dwindle:/ˈdwɪndl/| v. |减少，缩小，衰落|To become smaller and smaller; to decrease gradually in size, amount, or strength.|The town's population has dwindled to a mere 500.  这个镇的人口已经减少到只有500人了。

The snow dwindled away as the temperature rose. 随着温度升高，雪逐渐融化消失了。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, decline, lessen|反义词:increase, grow, expand, rise|词性:动词
