blottiest:/ˈblɒtɪɪst/| adj.|污渍最多的，墨迹最多的|Having the most blots or stains. Covered with the greatest number of marks or blemishes.|The blottiest page in the book was the one with the spilled coffee.|近义词：stained, spotted, marked, messy|反义词：clean, spotless, immaculate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "blot" (Old Norse "blottr," meaning a spot or stain) + "-y" (forming adjectives of quality) + "-est" (superlative suffix).
