colatitude:/koʊˈlætɪtjuːd/ /kəˈlætɪˌtjuːd/
n.
余纬；地心纬度
The angular distance of a point on a celestial sphere from the celestial equator, measured along the great circle passing through the point and the celestial poles.  It is equal to 90 degrees minus the point's declination. On Earth, it is the complement of the latitude.
The colatitude of the star is 30 degrees. 这颗恒星的余纬是30度。

近义词:polar distance
反义词:latitude
词性:noun
