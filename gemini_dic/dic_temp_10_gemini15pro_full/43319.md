cram:/kræm/| v. n.| 填塞；塞满；死记硬背；临时抱佛脚；挤满；（使）挤入；（使）塞满|To force a lot of things into a small space; to study hard in a short time just before an exam.| I crammed all my clothes into the suitcase. 我把所有衣服都塞进了手提箱。

He's cramming for his history exam. 他正在为历史考试临时抱佛脚。|近义词：stuff, pack, jam, squeeze, revise, study|反义词:empty, unpack|词性:动词,名词

cram:/kræm/| n.| 挤满；拥挤；死记硬背；填鸭式用功|A situation in which a lot of people or things are crowded together; the act of studying hard in a short time just before an exam.|The bus was a real cram. 公共汽车里真是拥挤不堪。

He had a last-minute cram before the test. 他在考试前最后关头突击了一下。|近义词：crush, crowd, jam|反义词:emptiness, space|词性:名词
