draftinesses:/ˈdræftinəsɪz/
n.
通风，透风处；冷风嗖嗖；穿堂风
the state of being drafty; full of drafts of air
The old house was full of draftinesses, making it uncomfortable in the winter.  这栋老房子到处都是穿堂风，冬天住起来很不舒服。
synonyms: drafts, airiness, chilliness
antonyms: airtightness, warmth, stuffiness
词性: 名词
