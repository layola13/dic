neutralize:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/ /ˈnuːtrəlaɪz/| vt.|中和，抵消，使无效|To make something neutral, especially by counteracting its effects.|The acid was neutralized by the alkali. 酸被碱中和了。|synonyms: counteract, offset, balance, cancel out|antonyms: activate, enhance, strengthen|verb

neutralize:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/ /ˈnuːtrəlaɪz/| vt.|使中立|To make a country or area neutral in a war.|The two countries agreed to neutralize the disputed territory.  这两个国家同意使争议领土中立化。|synonyms: disarm, demilitarize|antonyms: arm, militarize|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin neutralis + -ize.  Neutralis comes from Latin neuter, meaning "neither".
