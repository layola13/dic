ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anatomicus, from Ancient Greek ἀνατομικός (anatomikós, “pertaining to dissection”), from ἀνατέμνω (anatémnō, “I cut up, dissect”), from ἀνά (aná, “up”) + τέμνω (témnō, “I cut”).
