biga:/ˈbiːɡə/| n.|比加|A type of pre-ferment used in Italian baking, typically consisting of flour, water, and a small amount of yeast, left to ferment for a long period (typically 12-48 hours) at a cool temperature.  It develops flavor and improves the texture of the final bread.|The baker added the biga to the dough.|Synonym: pre-ferment, starter (in some contexts)|Antonym: N/A|noun
