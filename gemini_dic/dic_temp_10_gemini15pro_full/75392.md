frozenly:/ˈfrəʊzənli/| adv.|冰冷地，僵硬地|In a frozen or stiff manner; without warmth or emotion. |Her face was frozenly composed. 她的脸上毫无表情，僵硬冰冷。|She stared frozenly ahead. 她目光呆滞地直视前方。|近义词：rigidly, stiffly, icily|反义词：warmly, fluidly|词性:副词

词源: From "frozen" (past participle of "freeze") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Freeze" comes from Old English "freosan."
