antipatriotism:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/
n.
lack of patriotism; opposition to patriotism
The author explored the themes of alienation and antipatriotism in his novel.  这位作者在他的小说中探讨了疏离感和反爱国主义的主题。
antipatriotism is a sentiment that can manifest in various forms, from peaceful protests to violent acts of rebellion. 反爱国主义是一种可以以各种形式表现出来的情绪，从和平抗议到暴力反抗行为。
近义词: disloyalty, sedition, treason
反义词: patriotism, nationalism, loyalty
