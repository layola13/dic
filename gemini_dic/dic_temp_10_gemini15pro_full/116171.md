masculinising:/ˈmæskjʊləˌnaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 男性化的；使男性化的 | Causing something or someone to become more masculine in appearance, behavior, or characteristics.  |  Steroids can have a masculinising effect on women. 类固醇会对女性产生男性化的影响。 |  The gym promotes a masculinising workout regime.  这家健身房推广一种男性化的锻炼方案。 | Synonyms: virilizing,  androgenizing | Antonyms: feminizing, effeminizing | adjective


masculinise:/ˈmæskjʊləˌnaɪz/ | v. | 使男性化；男性化 | To make or become more masculine in appearance, behavior, or characteristics. |  Certain hormones can masculinise the female voice. 某些激素会使女性的声音男性化。 |  He felt pressure to masculinise his behavior to fit in. 他感到有压力要使自己的行为男性化以适应环境。 | Synonyms: virilize, androgenize | Antonyms: feminize, effeminize | verb


词源:

From French *masculinisant*, present participle of *masculinis*, from Latin *masculinus* ("masculine").
