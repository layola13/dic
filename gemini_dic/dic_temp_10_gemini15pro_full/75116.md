ETYMOLOGY->
"Fries" comes from the shortening of "French fries," believed to have originated either in Belgium or France.

"Frise" originates from the Old French word "frise," meaning "curly" or "napped," referring to the texture of the cloth or the appearance of the architectural decoration.
