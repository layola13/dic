equipment: /ɪˈkwɪpmənt/ | n. | 设备 | The set of tools, clothing, etc., used for a particular purpose. | The photographer had all the latest equipment. 摄影师拥有所有最新的设备。| 近义词: gear, apparatus, paraphernalia | 反义词: (None, as it's a category of items) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *equipament, ultimately from Late Latin *equipare ("to furnish, fit out, equip"), from Latin ex- + parare ("to prepare").
