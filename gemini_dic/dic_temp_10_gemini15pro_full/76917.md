gantry: /ˈɡæntri/ (US), /ˈɡæntri/ (UK)| n.|龙门架；高架移动平台|A framework supporting a crane, railway signal, etc., or carrying it overhead.|The gantry crane lifted the heavy container onto the ship. 龙门吊将沉重的集装箱吊到船上。|Synonyms: crane, framework, support, scaffold|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun


gantry: /ˈɡæntri/ (US), /ˈɡæntri/ (UK)| adj.|龙门式的|Resembling or relating to a gantry.|The gantry system allowed for precise movement of the camera.  龙门系统允许摄像机精确移动。|Synonyms: overhead, elevated|Antonyms: grounded, low-lying|adjective
