modest:/ˈmɑːdɪst/ | adj. | 谦虚的；适度的 | Unassuming or moderate in the estimation of one's abilities or achievements. Moderate in size, amount, or scope; not extreme or excessive. |  He's very modest about his achievements. 他对自己的成就非常谦虚。  They live in a modest house. 他们住在一个普通的房子里。 | Near Synonyms: humble, unassuming, unpretentious, self-effacing, restrained, moderate, simple, small, limited | Antonyms: boastful, arrogant, pretentious, ostentatious, flamboyant, immodest | adjective
