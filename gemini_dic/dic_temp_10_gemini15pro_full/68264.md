fajitas:/fəˈhiːtəz/ | n. | 烤肉丝，法吉塔 | Grilled strips of meat served with tortillas and various toppings.  A Tex-Mex dish consisting of strips of marinated skirt steak, chicken, or shrimp grilled and served with warm tortillas and various toppings such as salsa, guacamole, sour cream, and shredded cheese. | We ordered fajitas for dinner. 我们晚餐点了烤肉丝。|  Synonyms: tacos, burritos, quesadillas | Antonyms: None | Noun
