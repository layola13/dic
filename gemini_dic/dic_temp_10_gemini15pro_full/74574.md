fraulein: /ˈfrɔɪlaɪn/ (US), /ˈfrɔɪlaɪn/ (UK)| n.| 小姐 (未婚女子);| A German word for an unmarried woman, now largely replaced by "Frau".  It was traditionally used as a title of respect, similar to "Miss" in English. |例句：The fraulein served us tea.  这位小姐给我们上了茶。

例句：He addressed her as "Fraulein Schmidt". 他称呼她为“施密特小姐”。

近义词：Miss, Ms., Fräulein (modern German)|反义词: Frau (married woman)|词性:noun
