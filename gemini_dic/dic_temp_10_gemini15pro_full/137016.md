nonstainer:/nɒnˈsteɪnər/ | n. | 防污剂；不染色剂；不留污渍之物 | Something that does not stain or leave marks. | This carpet is a real nonstainer; even red wine doesn't mark it. 这块地毯真防污，即使红酒也不会留下痕迹。 |  The tablecloth is a nonstainer, so spills are no problem. 这桌布是不沾污的，所以洒东西没问题。| 近义词: stain-resistant material, non-staining product | 反义词: stainer | 词性: 名词

nonstaining:/nɒnˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不染色的；不留污渍的 | Not causing stains or marks. | This lipstick is nonstaining, so you don't have to worry about it getting on your teeth. 这款口红不染色，所以你不必担心它会沾到你的牙齿上。|  Use a nonstaining sunscreen to protect your skin without leaving white marks. 使用不染色的防晒霜来保护你的皮肤，不会留下白色痕迹。 | 近义词: stain-resistant, non-marking | 反义词: staining | 词性: 形容词
