circumrotatory:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈroʊtətɔːri/ /ˌsɜːkəmˈrɒʊtətri/
adjective
旋转的，环状旋转的
Rotating or moving in a circle, especially around a central point or axis.
The circumrotatory movement of the planets around the sun. 行星绕太阳的环状旋转运动。

The bacteria exhibited a circumrotatory swimming pattern. 这些细菌展现出一种环状旋转的游动模式。

synonym:rotary, circular, rotational
antonym:linear, straight
