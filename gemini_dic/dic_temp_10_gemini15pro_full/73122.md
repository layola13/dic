for-the-hell-of-it:/fɔːr ðə hɛl əv ɪt/ |adv. phrase| 为了好玩；一时兴起；就为了试试；纯粹为了高兴；| Just for fun, without any specific reason or purpose.  It implies a sense of spontaneity and a lack of serious motivation. |He climbed the mountain just for the hell of it.| Synonyms: for kicks, for the fun of it, on a whim, just because |Antonyms: purposefully, deliberately, for a reason| adverbial phrase
