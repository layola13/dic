coccinella:/ˌkɒksɪˈnɛlə/ (UK), /ˌkɑːksɪˈnɛlə/ (US)| n. |瓢虫|A small beetle with a rounded, often brightly colored, spotted body. It is often considered a symbol of good luck. |例句：A coccinella landed on my hand.  一只瓢虫落在了我的手上。
