cany:/ˈkæni/ | adj. | 峡谷的；像峡谷的 | Resembling a canyon; characterized by steep sides and a narrow bottom. | The road wound through a cany landscape. 这条路蜿蜒穿过峡谷般的景色。 | 近义词：canyonous, gorge-like | 反义词：flat, open, plain | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From canyon + -y.  Ultimately from Spanish *cañón "tube, cannon," because of the resemblance of some canyons to gun barrels.
