admit:/ədˈmɪt/ | vt. vi.| 承认；准许进入；可容纳 | To confess something, or to allow someone to enter a place. | I admit I made a mistake. = 我承认我犯了一个错误。

He was admitted to the hospital. = 他被送进了医院。 | 近义词：confess, acknowledge, concede, permit, allow | 反义词：deny, refuse, reject | verb

admit:/ədˈmɪt/ | n.| 承认；入场权 | An act of admitting or the right to be admitted. | His admit to the concert was revoked. = 他的音乐会入场券被取消了。 | 近义词：admission, entrance, access | 反义词：denial, refusal, rejection | noun
