altho:/ɔːlˈðoʊ/ (also /ɑːlˈðoʊ/)
conj.
虽然；尽管
An informal, shortened form of "although."  Used to introduce a subordinate clause that makes a concession.
Altho it was raining, we went for a walk. 虽然下雨了，我们还是去散步了。
近义词:although, though, even though, while, albeit
反义词: (No direct antonyms for conjunctions, consider antonyms for the idea expressed, such as) nevertheless, however, yet
词性: conjunction
