overweighting:/ˌoʊvərˈweɪtɪŋ/| n.|加权过重，过重配置|Giving too much importance or influence to something.|The portfolio suffered from an overweighting in technology stocks. 该投资组合因科技股配置过重而蒙受损失。|近义词：overemphasis, overrepresentation|反义词: underweighting|noun

overweight:/ˌoʊvərˈweɪt/| adj.|超重的，过重的|Weighing more than is considered normal or desirable.|He is overweight and needs to lose some pounds. 他超重了，需要减掉几磅。|近义词：obese, heavy|反义词: underweight, slim|adjective
