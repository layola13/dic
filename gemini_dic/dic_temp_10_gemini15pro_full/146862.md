overindustrialized: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度工业化的
Having developed too much industry, to the detriment of other aspects of society or the environment.
The city was overindustrialized and polluted.  这个城市过度工业化，污染严重。
The region suffers from being overindustrialized and undereducated. 该地区苦于过度工业化和教育不足。
近义词: overdeveloped, excessively industrialized
反义词: underdeveloped, agrarian
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + industrialized.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "industrialized" refers to the process of developing industries on a large scale.
