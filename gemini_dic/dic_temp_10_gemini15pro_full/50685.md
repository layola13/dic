dermatogen:/ˈdɜːrmətədʒɛn/| n. |表皮原|The outermost layer of cells of an apical meristem that gives rise to the epidermis of a plant.|The dermatogen is responsible for the formation of the plant's protective outer layer.|Synonym: protoderm|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with inner tissue layers like the ground meristem or procambium)|Noun
