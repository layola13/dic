accurse:/əˈkɜːrs/ /əˈkɜːrs/| vt.|诅咒，咒骂|To pronounce a curse upon; to call down evil or misery upon.  To condemn vehemently.|He accursed the day he was born. 他诅咒他出生的那一天。

The witch accursed the prince, turning him into a beast. 女巫诅咒了王子，把他变成了野兽。|近义词：curse, damn, condemn, execrate|反义词：bless, praise, commend|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *acursen*, from Old French *acurser*, from Latin *accursāre*, from *ad* (“to”) + *cursāre* (“to curse”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱers-* (“to run, to go”).
