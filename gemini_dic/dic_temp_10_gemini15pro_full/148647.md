overthronging:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋɪŋ/
verb, present participle
过度拥挤，挤满
The act of filling a place with too many people or things.
The market was overthronging with shoppers during the holiday season.  假日季节市场里挤满了购物者。
overthronging:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɒŋɪŋ/
adjective
过度拥挤的，挤满的
Excessively crowded or filled.
The overthronging streets made it difficult to navigate.  拥挤的街道使通行变得困难。


Synonyms: overcrowding, crowding, jamming, packing, swarming
Antonyms: deserted, empty, sparse, uncrowded
verb, present participle


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + thronging, present participle of throng (v.).
