noneligible:/ˌnɒnˈelɪdʒəbəl/
adjective
不合格的，没有资格的
Not eligible; not allowed or qualified to do or receive something.
例句：
He was declared noneligible for the competition due to his age.
他因年龄原因被宣布没有资格参加比赛。

Many low-income families are noneligible for government assistance.
许多低收入家庭没有资格获得政府援助。

近义词：ineligible, disqualified, unqualified
反义词：eligible, qualified
词性: adjective
