buttercupped:/ˈbʌtərˌkʌpt/| adj.|毛茛状的，像毛茛花的；金黄色的|Having a bright yellow colour like that of a buttercup flower; adorned with or resembling buttercups.|The field was buttercupped with sunshine.  田野里阳光灿烂，一片金黄。|Synonyms: golden, yellow, sunny|Antonyms: dark, dull, colorless|adjective
