bock: /bɑːk/ | n. | 博克啤酒; 公山羊 | A strong dark German beer, traditionally brewed in the winter and drunk in the spring. Also, a male goat. |  例句：I enjoyed a refreshing bock on a warm spring day. 我在一个温暖的春日里享用了一杯清爽的博克啤酒。

例句：The bock charged across the field. 那只公山羊冲过田野。 | 近义词：(for beer) doppelbock, weizenbock (for goat) billy goat, buck | 反义词: (no direct antonym for the beer sense) (for goat) nanny goat, doe | 词性: Noun
