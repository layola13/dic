airmen:/ˈɛəmən/ | n. |飞行员，空军人员|A member of an air force, especially one who is not an officer. |The airmen were preparing for a long mission. 飞行员们正在准备执行一项长期任务。|Example: The young airmen bravely defended their country. 这些年轻的飞行员勇敢地保卫了他们的国家。|Synonyms: pilot, aviator, flyer |Antonyms: civilian, ground crew |Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From air + man.  "Air" refers to the medium of flight, and "man" traditionally referred to a person of either sex.  The term became popular with the rise of powered flight in the early 20th century.
