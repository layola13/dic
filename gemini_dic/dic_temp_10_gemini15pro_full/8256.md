anti-inflationary:/ˌænti-ɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/
anti-inflationary| adj.|反通货膨胀的，抑制通货膨胀的|Tending to reduce or counteract inflation.  Designed to curb inflation.|The government introduced anti-inflationary measures to stabilize the economy. 政府采取了反通货膨胀措施来稳定经济。|
anti-inflationary|近义词: deflationary, counter-inflationary|反义词: inflationary|词性: adjective


anti-inflationary:/ˌænti-ɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/
anti-inflationary| n.|反通货膨胀政策（措施）|A policy or measure intended to reduce or counteract inflation.|The central bank's anti-inflationary was criticized for being too aggressive. 中央银行的反通货膨胀政策因过于激进而受到批评。|近义词: deflationary measure, counter-inflationary policy|反义词: inflationary policy|词性: noun (rarely used)


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite"
inflationary: from inflate + -ary (suffix forming adjectives). Inflate comes from Latin inflare "to blow into, puff up."
