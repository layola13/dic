beaklike:/ˈbiːklaɪk/| adj.|像鸟喙的，喙状的|Resembling a beak in shape or form.  Having a pointed, projecting shape like the beak of a bird.|The bird had a beaklike mouth.  这只鸟的嘴像鸟喙。|近义词：pointed, hooked, sharp|反义词:blunt, rounded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'beak' + '-like'. 'Beak' comes from the Middle English 'bek', from the Old English 'bēac'.  '-like' is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "similar to," from Old English '-līc'.
