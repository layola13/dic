insipidly:/ɪnˈsɪpɪdli/ | adv. | 平淡地；乏味地；无生气地 | In a way that lacks taste, zest, or interest. | She insipidly recounted the details of her vacation. 她平淡无奇地讲述了她的假期细节。| Synonyms: dully, blandly, vapidly, uninterestingly | Antonyms: interestingly, excitingly, vibrantly, zestfully | Adverb

insipid:/ɪnˈsɪpɪd/ | adj. | 平淡的；乏味的；无生气的 | Lacking taste, zest, or interest. | The soup was insipid and watery. 这汤淡而无味。| Synonyms: dull, bland, vapid, uninteresting | Antonyms: interesting, exciting, vibrant, zestful | Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insipidus*, from in- "not" + sapidus "tasty," from sapere "to taste."
