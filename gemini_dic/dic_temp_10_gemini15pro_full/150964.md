paramedical:/ˌpærəˈmedɪkəl/ | adj. n. |辅助医务人员的；护理人员|Relating to services or staff that assist medical professionals. |  She's studying to be a paramedical. 她正在学习成为一名辅助医务人员。|  Example:  He received paramedical training. 他接受了辅助医务人员的培训。 | 近义词: auxiliary, ancillary, supporting | 反义词: medical | 词性: adjective, noun


词源:  From para- (beside, alongside) + medical.
