ophidious:/oʊˈfɪdiəs/| adj.|阴险的；毒蛇似的；卑鄙的|Resembling or characteristic of a snake, especially in being sly or treacherous.  Having a snake-like quality, often referring to treacherous or deceitful behavior.|例句：He gave her an ophidious glance, making her feel uncomfortable. 他阴险地瞥了她一眼，让她感到很不舒服。

例句：The ophidious politician spread rumors to discredit his opponent.  这个阴险的政客散布谣言败坏他的对手的名誉。|近义词：treacherous, deceitful, insidious, cunning, sly, serpentine|反义词：honest, trustworthy, forthright, candid|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ophidius*, from *ophis* "snake" (from Greek *ophis* "snake") + *-ius* adjectival suffix.
