discrepancy: /dɪˈskrepənsi/ | n. | 差异，不一致，出入 | a difference between two things that should be the same | There's a discrepancy between the figures in the two reports. 这两份报告中的数字之间存在差异。 | synonyms: difference, inconsistency, disagreement, divergence, variation | antonyms: agreement, consistency, similarity, conformity | noun

discrepancies: /dɪˈskrepənsiz/ | n. | 差异，不一致，出入（复数）| differences between two or more things that should be the same |  There were several discrepancies between the two accounts of the accident.  关于事故的两份描述中存在若干处出入。 | synonyms: differences, inconsistencies, disagreements, divergences, variations | antonyms: agreements, consistencies, similarities, conformities | noun
