metameric:/ˌmetəˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. | 分节的，同分异构的 | Having or consisting of metameres; exhibiting metamerism.  Relating to or characterized by metamerism (the condition of being composed of metameres, or similar segments). In chemistry, relating to or denoting isomers having the same elements in the same proportions but different structural arrangements.| The earthworm has a metameric body. 蚯蚓有一个分节的身体。| The chemist studied the metameric properties of the compound. 化学家研究了该化合物的同分异构特性。 | segmented, isomeric | antonymous (in chemistry):  non-isomeric | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *meta* (μετά) "after, among, with" + *meros* (μέρος) "part, segment" + *-ikos* (-ικός) "pertaining to".
