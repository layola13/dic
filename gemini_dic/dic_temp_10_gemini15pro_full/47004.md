dash:/dæʃ/| n. v.|破折号，冲刺，少量，猛冲，泼溅|A punctuation mark (—) used to indicate a break in a sentence or to set off parenthetical material. Also, a sudden quick run or the act of running somewhere suddenly and quickly. As a verb, it means to move somewhere suddenly and quickly, or to throw or splash liquid on something.|例句：The rain dashed against the windows. 雨水拍打着窗户。

例句：Her hopes were dashed when she heard the news. 当她听到这个消息时，她的希望破灭了。|近义词：sprint, rush, hyphen, dash mark, shatter, ruin|反义词: crawl, stroll, build, create|noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *daska "to strike, beat," related to Danish daske "to slap, bang." The noun sense developed from the verb, referring to a sudden striking movement.
