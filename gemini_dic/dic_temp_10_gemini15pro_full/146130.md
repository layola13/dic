overempirical: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɪrɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于经验主义的；过分依赖经验的 | Relying excessively on observation and experiment, sometimes at the expense of theory or logic. | His research methodology was criticized for being overempirical, neglecting the theoretical framework. 他的研究方法被批评为过于经验主义，忽略了理论框架。| The overempirical approach ignored the established scientific principles.  这种过分依赖经验的方法忽略了已确立的科学原理。 | 近义词:  overly practical, excessively experimental | 反义词: theoretical, speculative | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "empirical" (from Greek "empeirikos" meaning "experienced").  "Over-"（表示“过度”的前缀）+ "empirical"（源自希腊语 "empeirikos"，意为“经验的”）。
