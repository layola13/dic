bulliest:/ˈbʊliɪst/| adj.|最欺凌弱小的；最恃强凌弱的|superlative adjective of bully: most given to or characterized by bullying.  This describes someone who engages in bullying behavior more than anyone else in a given group.|He was the bulliest kid in the entire school, picking on everyone smaller than him. 他是全校最爱欺负人的孩子，总是欺负比他弱小的人。|Synonyms: most intimidating, most domineering, most tyrannical|Antonyms: kindest, gentlest, most compassionate|Word Class: Adjective
