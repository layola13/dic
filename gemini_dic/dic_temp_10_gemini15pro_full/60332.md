egoistically: /ˌiːɡəʊˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 自私地，利己地 | In a selfish manner; motivated by concern for one's own advantage or welfare, regardless of others. | He acted egoistically, only thinking about his own gain. 他自私地行事，只想着自己的利益。|  He egoistically pursued his own interests, disregarding the needs of his family. 他自私地追求自己的利益，无视家人的需要。 | 近义词: selfishly, self-centeredly | 反义词: altruistically, selflessly | 词性: 副词

egoism: /ˈiːɡəʊɪzəm/ | n. | 利己主义，自我主义 |  A doctrine that individual self-interest is the actual motive of all conscious action. | His egoism prevented him from seeing other people's perspectives. 他的利己主义使他看不到别人的观点。| Her egoism made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她的利己主义让她在同事中不受欢迎。 | 近义词: selfishness, self-centeredness | 反义词: altruism, selflessness | 词性: 名词

egoist: /ˈiːɡəʊɪst/ | n. | 利己主义者，自我主义者 | A person who is preoccupied with their own interests. | He was an egoist who only cared about himself. 他是一个只关心自己的利己主义者。 |  The egoist only thought about how the situation would benefit him.  这个利己主义者只考虑这种情况会如何让他受益。 | 近义词: selfish person, narcissist | 反义词: altruist | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *égoïste* (now spelled *égoïste*), from Latin *ego* ("I").  最终源自拉丁语“ego”（意为“我”）。
