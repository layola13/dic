blacked: /blækt/ | v. | 使变黑; 涂黑; 弄脏; 抵制; 封锁 (信息); 失去知觉 | to make something black or dark; to censor or suppress information; to lose consciousness |  The windows were blacked out during the air raid.  窗户在空袭期间被涂黑了。| synonyms: darkened, obscured, censored, suppressed, fainted | antonyms: lightened, illuminated, revealed, published, conscious | verb

blacked: /blækt/ | adj. | 变黑的; 弄脏的; 抵制的 | having been made black or dark; censored or suppressed | The blacked-out city was eerily silent.  漆黑的城市一片寂静，令人毛骨悚然。 | synonyms: darkened, obscured, censored | antonyms: lightened, illuminated, revealed | adjective
