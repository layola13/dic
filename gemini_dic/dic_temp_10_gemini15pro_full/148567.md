overtaken:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪkən/ | v. (past participle of overtake) | 超越，超过；突然降临于；压倒 | overtaken is the past participle of the verb "overtake". It signifies being surpassed, caught up with, or overwhelmed by something. |  He was overtaken by a younger runner in the final lap. 他在最后一圈被一个更年轻的跑步者超越了。

The city was overtaken by the floodwaters. 城市被洪水淹没了。 | 近义词：surpassed, eclipsed, overwhelmed, overcome | 反义词: lag behind, fall behind | 词性: verb (past participle)

overtake:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪk/ | v. | 超越，赶上；（不愉快的事情）突然降临于 | To catch up with and pass, or for something unpleasant to happen suddenly. | The car overtook the truck on the highway. 这辆车在高速公路上超过了卡车。

A sudden storm overtook the hikers. 暴风雨突然降临到徒步旅行者身上。 | 近义词：pass, outstrip, surpass | 反义词: trail, lag behind | 词性: verb
