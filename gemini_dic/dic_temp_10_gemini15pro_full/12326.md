asepsis:/eɪˈsɛpsɪs/ /əˈsɛpsɪs/
n.
无菌，无菌状态
The absence of significant contamination.  The state of being free from pathogenic microorganisms.
The surgeon ensured asepsis throughout the operation.  外科医生确保整个手术过程无菌。
Sterility, hygiene
Sepsis, infection
noun
