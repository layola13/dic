madge:/mædʒ/
noun
女人，唠叨的女人 (informal, chiefly British)
A woman, especially a gossipy or talkative one.
例句：The old madges were gossiping by the fence.  那些老女人在篱笆边闲聊。

例句：Don't be such a madge, mind your own business! 别这么八卦，管好你自己的事！
近义词：gossip, chatterbox, busybody
反义词:quiet person, introvert
