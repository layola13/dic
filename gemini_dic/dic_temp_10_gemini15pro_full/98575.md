institutionalization:/ˌɪnstɪˌtjuːʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |制度化；机构化|The process of establishing something as a convention or norm in an organization or culture.  The act of committing someone to an institution such as a mental hospital or prison.|例句：The institutionalization of racism in the legal system had devastating consequences.  种族主义在法律体系中的制度化造成了毁灭性的后果。

例句：Her long institutionalization had made it difficult for her to adjust to life outside the hospital.  长期住院治疗使她难以适应院外的生活。|近义词：establishment, formalization, systematization|反义词:informalization, abolition|词性:noun
