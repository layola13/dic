aspire:/əˈspaɪər/ | v. |渴望，追求，有志于| To have a strong desire to achieve something high or great. | She aspired to be a doctor. 她渴望成为一名医生。| He aspired to the presidency. 他有志于当总统。| 近义词: desire, yearn, aim, strive | 反义词: disregard, neglect | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aspirare "to breathe on, blow gently upon," from Latin ad- "to" + spirare "to breathe."  The figurative sense of "to strive for something higher" arose from the notion of "breathing after" or "longing for" something.
