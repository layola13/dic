overgeniality:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒiːniˈælɪti/ | n. | 过分热情，过分亲切 | Excessive geniality; being too friendly or amiable, often to the point of insincerity. |  Her overgeniality made me suspicious. 她的过分热情让我起了疑心。| Synonyms: effusiveness, exuberance, heartiness | Antonyms: coldness, aloofness, unfriendliness | noun

overgenial:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 过分热情的，过分亲切的 | Excessively or insincerely friendly or amiable. | The overgenial salesman made me uncomfortable.  那个过分热情的推销员让我感到不舒服。 | Synonyms: effusive, exuberant, hearty | Antonyms: cold, aloof, unfriendly | adjective
