dingos:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊz/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡəʊz/ (UK)| n.|澳洲野狗|A wild or half-wild dog of Australia, reddish-brown in colour.|Dingoes are known for their cunning. 澳洲野狗以狡猾闻名。|Example:  Dingoes are a threat to livestock. 澳洲野狗对牲畜构成威胁。

Example:  The dingo fence was built to keep dingoes out of southeastern Australia.  澳洲野狗围栏是为了防止野狗进入澳大利亚东南部而建造的。|Synonyms: wild dog, canid|Antonyms: domesticated dog|Noun
