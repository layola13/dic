anglophone: /ˈæŋɡləˌfoʊn/ | n. adj. |英语使用者；英语国家的|A person who speaks English, especially as their first language, or whose culture is based on English.  A country where English is the main language. |例句：Canada is an anglophone country. 加拿大是一个英语国家。

例句：Many anglophones live in Quebec. 很多英语使用者居住在魁北克。|近义词：English speaker, English-speaking |反义词: Francophone |词性:名词，形容词
