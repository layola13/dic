nonracialism:/nɒnˈreɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.
非种族主义
The belief that race should not be a factor in social, political, or economic decisions.
Example 1: Nelson Mandela advocated for nonracialism in South Africa.  纳尔逊·曼德拉在南非倡导非种族主义。

Example 2:  The organization promotes nonracialism through education and community outreach.  该组织通过教育和社区推广来促进非种族主义。
Synonym:  anti-racism, equality, integration
Antonym: racism, segregation, discrimination
词性: 名词
