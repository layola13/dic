aerotherapentics:/ˌɛroʊθɛrəˈpjuːtɪks/
|n.|空气疗法|Treatment of disease by exposure to variations in climate or to modified air, such as that containing increased or decreased amounts of oxygen or carbon dioxide.|Patients with respiratory illnesses may benefit from aerotherapentics in a mountain environment. 呼吸系统疾病患者可能会受益于山区环境中的空气疗法。|Synonyms: climatotherapy, open-air treatment|Antonyms: chemotherapy, drug therapy|Noun
