nitrated:/ˈnaɪtreɪtɪd/| adj.|硝化的；用硝酸处理过的|Treated or reacted with nitric acid, resulting in the introduction of a nitro group (-NO2).|The nitrated cellulose is highly flammable. 硝化纤维素非常易燃。|近义词: treated with nitric acid |反义词: denitrated|词性:形容词
Etymology: From nitrate + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *nitrum "natron" (native sodium carbonate) + -ate (suffix forming verbs, nouns, and adjectives).
