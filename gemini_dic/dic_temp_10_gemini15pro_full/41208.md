coquimbite:/koʊˈkɪmbaɪt/| n. |水绿矾|A hydrated iron sulfate mineral, Fe₂(SO₄)₃·9H₂O, occurring in greenish-white or yellow masses. It is a secondary mineral formed by the oxidation of pyrite and other sulfide minerals.|Coquimbite is often found in arid regions. 水绿矾常在干旱地区被发现。|Synonyms: none specifically|Antonyms: none specifically|noun
