overneglecting:/ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛktɪŋ/
| adj. |过度忽视的，极度疏忽的 |  Failing to care for or give attention to something or someone to an excessive degree. | The overneglecting landlord allowed the building to fall into disrepair.  房东过度疏忽，导致建筑物失修。|Synonyms: seriously neglecting, grossly neglecting, severely neglecting | Antonyms: attentive, caring, diligent | verb (present participle)


overneglect:/ˌoʊvər nɪˈɡlɛkt/
| v. |过度忽视，极度疏忽 | To fail to care for or give attention to something or someone to an excessive degree. | He overneglected his studies and failed the exam. 他极度疏忽学业，考试不及格。|Synonyms: seriously neglect, grossly neglect, severely neglect | Antonyms: attend to, care for, be diligent with | verb
