filminesses:/ˈfɪlminɪsɪz/
n.
薄膜状物；薄膜状态
The state or quality of being filmy; resembling a thin film or membrane.
The artist captured the filminesses of the spider's web in his painting.  这位艺术家在他的画作中捕捉到了蜘蛛网的薄膜状.
thinness, flimsiness, delicacy, gauziness
thickness, density, solidity
noun
