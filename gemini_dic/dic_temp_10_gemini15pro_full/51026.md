词源:

"Deslum" isn't a standard English word. It seems to be a variant or misspelling of "dazzle," perhaps influenced by Romance languages like Spanish or Portuguese, which have similar-sounding words related to dazzling or amazing.  The English word "dazzle" comes from Middle English *dasen, of uncertain origin.
