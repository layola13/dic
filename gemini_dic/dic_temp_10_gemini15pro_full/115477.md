manumise:/ˈmænjʊˌmaɪz/ | vt. | 解放，释放（奴隶） | To release from slavery or servitude; to emancipate. |  The plantation owner finally manumised all his slaves.  种植园主最终释放了他所有的奴隶。

The government manumised the political prisoners. 政府释放了政治犯。 | 近义词：emancipate, liberate, free, release | 反义词：enslave, imprison, confine | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *manūmissus*, past participle of *manūmittere* ("to release from one's hand, set free"), from *manus* ("hand") + *mittere* ("to send, let go").
