chain-reacted:/ˈtʃeɪn riˈæktɪd/ | adj. | 连锁反应的，链式反应的 | A process in which one event automatically causes another, which in turn causes another, etc. | The explosion chain-reacted, causing widespread destruction. 爆炸引起了连锁反应，造成了大范围的破坏。|  The dominoes chain-reacted, falling one after another. 多米诺骨牌发生了连锁反应，一个接一个地倒下。| 近义词: cascading, consequential | 反义词: isolated, independent | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: chain (n.) + react (v.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives)
