agapes:/ˈæɡəˌpiːz/ /ˈæɡəˌpeɪz/
noun
爱餐；（基督教早期）圣餐；爱；盛宴
A love feast; a communal meal, especially one held by early Christians in token of fellowship and love, or the Eucharist.  A love; a feast.
The early church celebrated agapes. 早期教会举行爱餐。
The agape was a ritual meal shared by early Christians. 爱餐是早期基督徒共享的仪式性聚餐。

近义词: feast, banquet, meal, communion, Eucharist
反义词: fasting, starvation
