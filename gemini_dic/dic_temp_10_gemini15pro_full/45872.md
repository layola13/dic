cutch:/kʌtʃ/ | n. | 印度儿茶，儿茶浸膏，黑儿茶 | A reddish-brown extract from the acacia tree, used for tanning and dyeing. |  The leather was tanned using cutch.  这块皮革是用儿茶鞣制的。| Synonyms: catechu, tannin | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but bleaching agents could be considered opposite in effect) | Noun

cutch:/kʌtʃ/ | n. | (泥沼)泥，烂泥 |  Thick, sticky mud. | The wheels of the car got stuck in the cutch. 车轮陷在泥里了。| Synonyms: mire, muck, sludge | Antonyms:  solid ground | Noun
