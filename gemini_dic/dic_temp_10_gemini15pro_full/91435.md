hygienic:/haɪˈdʒiːnɪk/ /haɪˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|卫生的，清洁的|Clean and free of bacteria and other disease-causing organisms; conducive to health.|例句：It's important to maintain hygienic conditions in the kitchen.  在厨房保持卫生条件很重要。|近义词：clean, sanitary, sterile|反义词:unhygienic, insanitary, dirty|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *hygiénique*, from *hygiène* (“hygiene”), from Ancient Greek *ὑγιεινός* (*hygieinós*, “healthful”), from *ὑγιής* (*hygiḗs*, “healthy”).
