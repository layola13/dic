countertendencies:/ˌkaʊntərˈtɛndənsiz/| n.|反趋势，对抗趋势|A countertendency is a trend that goes against the prevailing trend.|The recent rise in vinyl record sales is a countertendency to the dominance of digital music. 最近黑胶唱片销量的上升是与数字音乐的主导地位相反的趋势。|Synonyms: opposing trend, reverse trend, contrary trend|Antonyms: prevailing trend, dominant trend|noun
