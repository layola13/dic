antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
n.|古董商；收藏家；古物研究者|A person who studies or collects old and valuable things, especially books and works of art.|He was a renowned antiquarian with a vast collection of medieval manuscripts. 他是一位著名的古董收藏家，收藏了大量中世纪的手稿。|synonyms: collector, connoisseur, archaeologist|antonyms: modernist, contemporary|noun
antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
adj.|古董的；古文物研究的|Relating to the study or collection of old and valuable things.|She had an antiquarian interest in Roman pottery. 她对罗马陶器有古文物研究的兴趣。|synonyms: ancient, antique, archaic|antonyms: modern, contemporary, new|adjective
