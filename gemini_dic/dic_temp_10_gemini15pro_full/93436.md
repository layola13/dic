illuminating:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |启发的，启迪的；照亮的|Making something clearer or easier to understand; shedding light on (literally or figuratively). |例句：The professor's lecture was illuminating. 教授的讲座很有启发性。

例句：The moon was illuminating the path ahead. 月亮照亮了前方的道路。|近义词：enlightening, insightful, revealing, clarifying, brightening |反义词：confusing, obscure, darkening, bewildering |词性:形容词


词源: From Latin "illuminare" (to light up, brighten), from "in-" (in, on) + "luminare" (to light), from "lumen" (light).
