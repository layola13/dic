imputer:/ɪmˈpjuːtər/ | n. | 估算器，插补器 | A person or thing that imputes; especially : a computer program that is used to supply missing values in a data set.  | Example: The imputer filled in the missing data points using the mean of the existing values.  估算器使用现有值的平均值填充缺失的数据点。

Example:  The researcher used an imputer to handle the missing data in their survey. 研究人员使用估算器来处理调查中缺失的数据。 | 近义词: estimator, filler | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From the verb "impute" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Impute" comes from Late Latin "imputare"  (to charge, ascribe), from "in-" (in, on) + "putare" (to think, reckon).
