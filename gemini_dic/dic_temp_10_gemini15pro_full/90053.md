hospitalized:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzd/ (US)| adj.| 住院的，住院治疗的 |Placed in a hospital for treatment.| He was hospitalized for a week with pneumonia. 他因肺炎住院一周。|近义词：admitted, in hospital, |反义词:discharged, out of hospital|adjective
hospitalized:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzd/ (US)| v.| 住院治疗 |To place someone in a hospital for treatment. (This is the past participle form used as an adjective, but the base form is a verb)|  Many car accident victims were hospitalized. 许多车祸受害者住院治疗。|近义词：admit, treat in hospital, |反义词:discharge, release|verb
