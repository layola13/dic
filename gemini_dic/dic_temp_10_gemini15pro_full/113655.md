machineries:/məˈʃiːnəri/| n.|机械、机器（总称）| Machinery refers to machines collectively or the parts of a machine. |The factory invested in new machinery to increase production. 工厂投资了新的机器以提高产量。|The complex machinery of the clock fascinated him. 钟表复杂的机械装置使他着迷。|近义词：equipment, apparatus, machines|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑manual labor 手工劳动)|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Middle English machinerie, from Old French machinerie, from machine + -erie. Ultimately from Latin machina (“contrivance, engine, machine”).
