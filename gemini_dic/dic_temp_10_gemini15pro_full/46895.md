darkener:/ˈdɑːrkənər/| n.|加深剂，使变暗之物|Something that darkens something else. |例句：She added a fabric darkener to the dye bath to achieve a deeper shade of blue. 她往染缸里加了一种织物加深剂，使蓝色更深。

例句：The clouds acted as a darkener, blocking out the sun. 云层遮住了太阳，使天色变暗。|近义词：dye, tint, pigment, stain|反义词：lightener, brightener|词性：名词

darken:/ˈdɑːrkən/| v.|（使）变暗，（使）变黑|To make or become dark or darker.|例句：The sky darkened as the storm approached. 随着暴风雨的临近，天空变暗了。

例句：He darkened his hair with dye. 他用染料把头发染黑了。|近义词：dim, obscure, shadow, blacken|反义词：lighten, brighten, illuminate|词性：动词
