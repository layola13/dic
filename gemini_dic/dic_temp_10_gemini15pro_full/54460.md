disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| n. | 瓦解，分解，碎裂（名词形式较少用）| The act of disintegrating. | The disintegration of the empire was a slow and painful process.  帝国的瓦解是一个缓慢而痛苦的过程。| synonyms: collapse, breakdown, decay | antonyms: unification, integration | noun
