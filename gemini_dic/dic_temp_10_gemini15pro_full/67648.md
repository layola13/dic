extrinsicism:/ˌɛkˈstrɪnsɪsɪzəm/
n.
外在主义; 外在性; 外因性
The doctrine that value or validity are conferred by something external.  A focus on external factors, rather than internal ones.
His art is a rejection of extrinsicism, focusing purely on the intrinsic qualities of the materials. 他de艺术是对外在主义的拒绝，纯粹关注材料的内在品质。
近义词: externalism, objectivism
反义词: intrinsicism, subjectivism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From extrinsic + -ism.  Extrinsic comes from Late Latin extrinsecus ("outward"), from Latin exter ("outside").
