embracery: /ɪmˈbreɪsəri/ /ɛmˈbreɪsəri/
n.
贿赂陪审团; 操纵陪审团
The act of attempting to corruptly influence a jury.
The lawyer was accused of embracery for attempting to bribe a juror.  这名律师因试图贿赂陪审员而被指控操纵陪审团。
The judge condemned the blatant act of embracery that threatened to undermine the entire trial.  法官谴责了这一公然操纵陪审团的行为，该行为有可能破坏整个审判。
近义词: jury tampering, bribery, corruption
反义词: justice, fairness, impartiality
词性: 名词
