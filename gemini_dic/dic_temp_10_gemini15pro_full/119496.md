microperthitic: /ˌmaɪkroʊpərˈθɪtɪk/
adjective
微斜长石的
Relating to or designating a perthite in which the lamellae of albite are only visible under a microscope.  (A perthite is a feldspar mineral composed of two endmembers: albite (NaAlSi3O8) and orthoclase (KAlSi3O8).)
The rock sample exhibited a microperthitic texture. 这块岩石样品呈现出微斜长石结构。
Near synonyms: perthitic, fine-grained, microscopic
Antonyms: macroperthitic, coarse-grained
