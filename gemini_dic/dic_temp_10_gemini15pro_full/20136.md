biped:/ˈbaɪped/| n.|两足动物|An animal that uses two legs for walking.|Humans are bipeds. 人类是两足动物。|The ostrich is a large, flightless biped. 鸵鸟是一种大型的不会飞的两足动物。|近义词：two-footed animal|反义词：quadruped|词性：noun

词源:
From Latin *bipēs* (two-footed), from *bi-* (two) + *pēs* (foot).
