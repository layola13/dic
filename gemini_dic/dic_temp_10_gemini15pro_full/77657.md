gelders:/ˈɡɛldərz/ | adj. n.| 海尔德兰的；海尔德兰人；海尔德兰语 | Relating to Gelderland, a province in the Netherlands; a person from Gelderland; the dialect spoken in Gelderland. |  He speaks Gelders, a dialect difficult for other Dutch speakers to understand. 他说海尔德兰语，其他荷兰语使用者很难听懂。| The Gelders landscape is known for its rivers and forests. 海尔德兰的景观以河流和森林而闻名。 | 近义词: Gelderlander, Guelders | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially other Dutch provinces, e.g., Brabantian, Frisian) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From the name of the Dutch province, Gelderland. The origin of "Gelderland" is uncertain, but it may be related to "Gelre," a historical region, possibly meaning "sandy area."
