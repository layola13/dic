grotesque:/ɡroʊˈtɛsk/ | adj. n. | 怪诞的，奇形怪状的；怪诞的图案（或雕塑）| Comically or repulsively ugly or distorted. |  The gargoyle on the building was grotesque.  这栋建筑上的滴水嘴很怪诞。| synonyms: bizarre, distorted, deformed, misshapen | antonyms: beautiful, attractive, normal, pleasing | adjective

grotesque:/ɡroʊˈtɛsk/ | n. | 怪诞的图案（或雕塑）| A style of decorative art characterized by fanciful or fantastic human and animal forms often interwoven with foliage or similar designs. | The artist specialized in grotesques.  这位艺术家专攻怪诞图案。 | synonyms: caricature, gargoyle | antonyms: beauty, elegance | noun
