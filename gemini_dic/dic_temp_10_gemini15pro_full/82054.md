gristmill:/ˈɡrɪstmɪl/| n.|磨坊，谷物磨坊|A building equipped with machinery for grinding grain into flour.|The old gristmill by the river has been restored and is now a museum. 河边的老磨坊已经修复，现在成了一个博物馆。|近义词：flour mill, mill|反义词: None|noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *gristmylle*, from Old English *gryst* (“grinding”) + *mylen* (“mill”).  The word *gryst* is related to *grind*.
