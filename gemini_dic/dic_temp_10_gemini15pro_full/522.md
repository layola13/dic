abomasum:/ˌæbəˈmeɪsəm/| n.|皱胃|The fourth and final stomach compartment in ruminants. It is the true stomach, secreting gastric juices as in non-ruminant mammals.|The abomasum is where the main part of digestion occurs in a cow. 牛的消化主要在皱胃中进行。

The vet found a blockage in the calf's abomasum. 兽医发现小牛的皱胃堵塞了。|近义词：stomach, ventriculus|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abomasum, from Latin ab- + omasum "tripe," perhaps from Greek ômos "raw, shoulder (of an animal)."
