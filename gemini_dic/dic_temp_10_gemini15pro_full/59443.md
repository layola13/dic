earthwards:/ˈɜːθwədz/ | adv. | 向地球；朝向地面 | Towards the earth; in a downward direction. | The meteor hurtled earthwards.  流星朝地球飞驰而来。| The apple fell earthwards from the tree. 苹果从树上掉落到地面。 | 近义词: downwards, groundwards | 反义词: upwards, skywards | 词性: 副词

词源:  "earth" + "-wards"  "earth" 指地球，"-wards" 是一个后缀，表示“朝向...的方向”。
