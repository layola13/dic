exorcisers:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzərz/| n. |驱魔人，驱邪者|A person who performs exorcisms; someone who casts out evil spirits. |例句：The exorcisers performed a ritual to cleanse the house. 驱魔师进行了一个仪式来净化这所房子。

例句：The team of exorcisers were called in to deal with the demonic presence.  这队驱魔人被叫来处理恶魔的存在。|近义词：shaman, priest, spiritualist|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
