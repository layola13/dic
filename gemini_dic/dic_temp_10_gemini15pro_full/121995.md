misspelled:/ˌmɪsˈspɛld/
adjective
拼写错误的
incorrectly spelled.
Example: The student misspelled several words on the test.  这个学生在测试中拼错了几个单词。
Example:  I misspelled "accommodate" again! 我又把“accommodate”拼错了！
Synonym: wrongly spelled, misspelled
Antonym: correctly spelled, spelled right
词性:adjective
