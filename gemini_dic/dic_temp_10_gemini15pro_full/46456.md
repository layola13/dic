daguerreotypy:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpi/
noun
银版照相法；银版照片
A photograph taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.
例句：The daguerreotypy captured a haunting image of the young woman.  银版照片捕捉到了这位年轻女子令人难忘的形象。

例句：Daguerreotypy was the first commercially successful photographic process.  银版照相法是第一个商业上成功的摄影工艺。
近义词：photograph, image, picture
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider modern photography processes)
词性:n.
