constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/| n. adj.|成分; 选民; 要素;|A constituent is a part of something larger, or a voter in a particular area represented by an elected official.  A constituent can also be an essential part or characteristic of something.|例句：The constituents of the mixture were carefully measured. 混合物的成分被仔细测量。|近义词：component, element, ingredient, voter, elector|反义词:whole, entirety|noun

constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/| adj.|组成的; 构成的;|Constituent means forming or being part of something.|例句：The constituent parts of the machine were all made in Germany.  这台机器的组成部件都是在德国制造的。|近义词：component, elemental, essential|反义词:nonessential, inessential|adjective
