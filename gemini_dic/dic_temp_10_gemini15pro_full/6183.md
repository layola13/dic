amitosis:/ˌeɪmaɪˈtoʊsɪs/ /ˌæmaɪˈtoʊsɪs/ | n. | 无丝分裂 | Direct cell division, characterized by simple cleavage of the nucleus without the formation of chromosomes or a spindle. |  Amitosis is a less common form of cell division than mitosis.  无丝分裂是一种比有丝分裂少见的细胞分裂形式。| synonym: direct cell division | antonym: mitosis, meiosis | noun
