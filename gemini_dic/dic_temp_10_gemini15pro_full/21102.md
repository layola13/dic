blip:/blɪp/| n.|短暂而尖锐的声音；（尤指雷达屏幕上的）光点，闪光；小差错，暂时的问题|A temporary, minor problem or interruption; a short, sharp sound or a spot of light on a radar screen.|The recent drop in sales was just a blip. 最近的销售额下降只是一个小插曲。|近义词：glitch, hiccup, anomaly, spike|反义词:trend, norm|noun

blip:/blɪp/| v.|发出哔哔声；短暂出现；快速按下（按钮）|To make a short, sharp sound; to appear briefly; to press (a button) quickly.|The radar blips every time a plane flies overhead. 每当有飞机飞过头顶时，雷达都会发出哔哔声。|近义词：beep, flash, blink|反义词:persist, continue|verb
