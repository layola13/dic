extemporizer:/ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪzər/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəˌraɪzər/| n. |即兴演说者；即兴表演者；即兴创作的人|A person who extemporizes, especially a speaker or performer who improvises.|He was a gifted extemporizer, able to deliver a compelling speech on any topic. 他是一位才华横溢的即兴演说家，能够就任何话题发表引人入胜的演讲。|近义词：improviser, ad-libber|反义词: planner, preparer|词性:noun

extemporize:/ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪz/ /ɛkˈstɛmpəˌraɪz/| v. |即兴创作；即兴表演；即兴演说|To compose, perform, or produce something such as music or a speech without preparation; improvise.|She had to extemporize a speech at the awards ceremony.  她在颁奖典礼上不得不即兴发表演讲。|近义词：improvise, ad-lib|反义词: prepare, rehearse|词性:verb

extemporaneous:/ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ /ɛkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/| adj. |即兴的；无准备的；临时的|Spoken or done without preparation.|His extemporaneous remarks were surprisingly insightful. 他即兴发表的评论出奇地有见地。|近义词：improvised, off-the-cuff|反义词: prepared, rehearsed|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ex tempore* "out of the time," from *ex* "out of" + *tempore* "time, season."
