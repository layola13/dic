essayical:/ɛˈseɪɪkəl/
adjective
散文式的，论文体的
resembling or characteristic of an essay; analytical or discursive
His writing style is very essayical, focusing on detailed analysis and thoughtful reflection. 他的写作风格非常散文式，注重详细的分析和深刻的反思。
近义词: discursive, analytical, expository
反义词: concise, succinct, brief
词性: adjective
