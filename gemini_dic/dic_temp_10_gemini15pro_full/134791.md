nonmetrical:/nɒnˈmɛtrɪkəl/ | adj. | 非韵律的，非格律的，不符合格律的 | Not conforming to a regular metrical pattern; not metrical. |  The poem is nonmetrical, relying on free verse instead of a set rhythm. 这首诗是非韵律的，依靠自由诗而不是固定的节奏。

The musician's improvisations were often nonmetrical, creating a sense of unpredictability.  这位音乐家的即兴演奏通常是非韵律的，营造出一种不可预测的感觉。 |Synonyms: arrhythmic, irregular, free verse | Antonyms: metrical, rhythmic, regular | adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "metrical" (from "meter" - a unit of rhythm in poetry).  词源：“non-”（前缀，意思是“不”）+“metrical”（来自“meter” - 诗歌中节奏的单位）。
