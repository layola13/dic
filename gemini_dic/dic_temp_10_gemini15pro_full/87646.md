hexacapsular:/ˌheksəˈkæpsjʊlər/| adj.|六囊的，有六个 капсул 的|Having six capsules or compartments.  A hexacapsular fruit has six compartments.|The poppy plant produces a hexacapsular fruit. 罂粟植物会结出六个果荚的果实。|multicapsular|unicapsular|adjective
词源:
From *hexa-* (six) + *capsular* (relating to a capsule). *Hexa-* comes from Greek *héx* (six). *Capsular* derives from Latin *capsula* (small box, capsule).
用法:
This term is primarily used in botany to describe fruits or seed pods that have six compartments or capsules.
