diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːz/ (adj.) /dɪˈfjuːs/ (v.)
adjective|弥漫的，散开的；冗长的，啰嗦的|Spread out over a large area; not concentrated. |The light was diffuse and soft. 光线弥漫而柔和。| widespread, dispersed, scattered|concentrated, localized, focused|adjective
diffuse:/dɪˈfjuːz/ (adj.) /dɪˈfjuːs/ (v.)
verb|扩散，传播；使分散|To spread or cause to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people.|The news diffused quickly throughout the town. 消息迅速传遍了全镇。|spread, disseminate, disperse|concentrate, gather, collect|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin diffusus, past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread out," from dis- "out" + fundere "to pour."
