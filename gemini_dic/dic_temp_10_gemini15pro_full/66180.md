exchanged:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒd/
verb
交换的; 兑换的
past tense and past participle of exchange
We exchanged gifts at Christmas.  我们在圣诞节交换了礼物。
They exchanged glances across the room. 他们隔着房间交换了一下眼神。
synonym: swapped, traded, bartered
antonym: kept, retained
