denature:/diːˈneɪtʃə(r)/
v.
使变性，使改变性质
to change the usual character of something, especially in order to make it less effective
Cooking denatures protein.  烹饪会使蛋白质变性。

近义词:alter, modify, change, transform
反义词:renature, restore

词性: verb

词源: From de- + nature, from Latin denaturare "to change the nature of," from de- "from, away from" + natura "nature"
