leaf-letting:/ˈliːfˌlɛtɪŋ/
n.
散发传单; 传单散发
the activity of distributing leaflets to people
例句：Leaf-letting is a common way to spread political messages.  散发传单是传播政治信息的一种常见方式。

例句：The group organized a leaf-letting campaign to raise awareness of the issue.  该组织发起了一场散发传单的活动，以提高人们对这个问题的认识。

近义词：distributing leaflets, handing out flyers, canvassing
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider "online campaigning" as a contrasting method)
