depravities: /dɪˈprævɪtiz/ | n. | 堕落; 腐败; 邪恶的行为 | Moral depravities such as corruption and violence were rampant in the city.  像腐败和暴力这样的道德败坏在这座城市猖獗。|  The novel explored the depths of human depravities. 这部小说探讨了人类堕落的深渊。 | 近义词: corruption, wickedness, immorality, perversion, degeneracy | 反义词: virtue, goodness, morality, decency | 词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From Late Latin *depravitatem, from Latin depravatus (past participle of depravare ‘to corrupt’), from de- ‘down’ + pravus ‘crooked, perverse’.
