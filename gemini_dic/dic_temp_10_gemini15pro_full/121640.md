misperceived:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːvd/ | adj. | 被误解的，被错误理解的 |  Past participle of misperceive, meaning to perceive incorrectly or wrongly. It describes something that has been understood or interpreted in a way that is not accurate. | The politician's words were misperceived by the media, leading to a public outcry.  这位政治家的话被媒体误解了，引起了公众的强烈抗议。| The artist felt misperceived by the critics, who didn't understand his work. 这位艺术家觉得评论家误解了他，他们不理解他的作品。| Synonyms: misinterpreted, misunderstood, misconstrued | Antonyms: understood, correctly perceived, accurately interpreted |Verb (past participle)
