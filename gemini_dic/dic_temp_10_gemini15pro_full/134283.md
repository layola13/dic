noninhabitant:/ˌnɒnɪnˈhæbɪtənt/
noun
非居民；不住在这里的人
A person who does not live in a particular place.
The noninhabitants of the island were surprised by the sudden influx of tourists. 岛上的非居民对游客的突然涌入感到惊讶。
The building was declared unsafe for noninhabitants. 这栋建筑被宣布为非居民不安全。
synonym: outsider, nonresident, alien
antonym: resident, inhabitant, dweller
n.
