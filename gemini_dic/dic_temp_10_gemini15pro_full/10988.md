arboral:/ˈɑːrˌbɔːrəl/ |adj.|树栖的，树木的|Living in trees; of or relating to trees.|例句：Monkeys are arboreal creatures. 猴子是树栖动物。

例句：The sloth is an arboreal mammal.  树懒是一种树栖哺乳动物。

近义词：arboreous, tree-dwelling, dendritic|反义词：terrestrial, ground-dwelling|词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *arborealis, from Latin arbor ("tree") + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).
