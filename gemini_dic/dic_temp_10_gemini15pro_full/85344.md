harquebus:/ˈhɑːrkəbəs/| n.|火绳枪|An early type of portable firearm with a long barrel, supported on a tripod or forked rest when fired.  A precursor to the musket.|The harquebus was a revolutionary weapon in its time, allowing infantry to inflict significant damage on enemy formations.|Synonyms:arquebus, handgun, firearm, gun|Antonyms:bow and arrow, sword, spear|noun
