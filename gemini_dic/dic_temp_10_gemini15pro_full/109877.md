what:/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/, hwʌt/ | pron. det. int. adv.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask for information specifying something.| 例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！| 近义词：which, how | 反义词:  (无明确反义词，意义取决于上下文) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？
What does she do for a living? = 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚晚餐做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week?  = 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do this weekend? = 你这周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next month? = 到下个月你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么多久了？
What will she have been working on for five years? = 到那时她将已经从事那项工作五年了？


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the hell? = 搞什么鬼！
What if we go to the beach? = 如果我们去海滩怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
No matter what, I'll be there for you. = 无论如何，我都会在你身边。
