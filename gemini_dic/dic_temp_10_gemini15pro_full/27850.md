canonistic:/kænəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
教会法的；符合教会法的
Relating to or based on canons, especially of the church.
The canonistic laws regarding marriage are complex.  教会法中关于婚姻的法律很复杂。
The scholar presented a canonistic interpretation of the historical document.  这位学者对这份历史文献提出了符合教会法的解读。
近义词:canonical, ecclesiastical
反义词:secular, lay
词性:adjective
