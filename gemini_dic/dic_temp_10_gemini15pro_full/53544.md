/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəl/ | /ˌdɪsəˈvaʊmənt/
n.|否认，拒绝承认，放弃|The act of denying responsibility for or connection with something or someone.|He issued a disavowal of the group's violent actions. 他发表声明，否认与该组织的暴力行为有任何关联。|
近义词：denial, rejection, repudiation, renunciation|
反义词：acknowledgment, acceptance, admission, avowal|
词性：noun
