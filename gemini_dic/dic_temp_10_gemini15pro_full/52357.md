dictatorian:/dɪktəˈtɔːriən/
| adj.|独裁的，独裁者的|Relating to or characteristic of a dictator or dictatorship; tyrannical. | The dictatorian regime severely restricted freedom of speech.  独裁政权严格限制言论自由。| The dictatorian government controlled all aspects of citizens' lives. 独裁政府控制了公民生活的方方面面。|近义词：autocratic, tyrannical, despotic, authoritarian|反义词：democratic, liberal, egalitarian|词性:形容词
dictatorian:/ˌdɪktəˈtɔːriənɪzəm/
| n.|独裁主义|The principles or practice of dictatorship. |Dictatorianism often leads to oppression and human rights violations. 独裁主义往往导致压迫和侵犯人权。| The rise of dictatorianism in the region was a cause for concern. 该地区独裁主义的兴起令人担忧。|近义词：autocracy, tyranny, despotism, authoritarianism|反义词：democracy, liberalism, egalitarianism|词性:名词
