overthrow: /ˌoʊvərˈθroʊ/ /ˌəʊvəˈθrəʊ/
verb
推翻；颠覆
to remove forcibly from power
例句：The rebels overthrew the government. 叛军推翻了政府。

例句：The dictator was overthrown in a coup. 独裁者在政变中被推翻。
近义词：overturn, topple, depose, subvert
反义词：establish, uphold, support
词性：v.

overthrow: /ˈoʊvərθroʊ/ /ˈəʊvəθrəʊ/
noun
推翻；颠覆
the act of overthrowing someone or something
例句：The overthrow of the monarchy led to a republic. 君主制的推翻导致了共和国的建立。

例句：The revolution resulted in the overthrow of the ruling class.  这场革命导致了统治阶级的垮台。
近义词：defeat, downfall, collapse
反义词：establishment, support
词性：n.
