overviolent: /ˌoʊvərˈvaɪələnt/ | adj. | 过于暴力的；极端暴力的 | Excessively violent; marked by extreme or unnecessary violence.  | The film was criticized for its overviolent scenes. 这部电影因其过于暴力的场景而受到批评。 |  The overviolent video game was banned in several countries.  这款过于暴力的电子游戏在几个国家被禁。 | 近义词: excessively violent, brutal, savage, ferocious | 反义词: peaceful, gentle, nonviolent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "violent" (adjective meaning "using or involving physical force intended to hurt, damage, or kill someone or something")
