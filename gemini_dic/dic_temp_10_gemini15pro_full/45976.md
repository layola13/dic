cyanin:/ˈsaɪənɪn/| n. |花青素，花色素苷|A water-soluble pigment found in plants, especially flowers and fruits, responsible for blue, purple, and red colors.|The cyanin in blueberries is a powerful antioxidant. 蓝莓中的花青素是一种强大的抗氧化剂。|Synonyms: anthocyanin, pigment|Antonyms: None|noun
