antidogmatisms: /ˌæntiˈdɒɡməˌtɪzəmz/ | n. | 反教条主义 |  Opposition to or rejection of dogma, especially in religion or philosophy.  It emphasizes independent thought and open-mindedness over strict adherence to established doctrines. |  His antidogmatisms led him to question everything he had been taught.  他的反教条主义让他质疑他所学到的一切。 | synonyms:  nonconformism, heterodoxy, freethinking, skepticism | antonyms: dogmatism, orthodoxy, conformism | noun
