diminutive:/dɪˈmɪnjətɪv/| adj. n.|微小的，小型的；爱称|Extremely or unusually small. A shortened form of a name, typically used informally.|She has diminutive hands for an adult.  她作为一个成年人，手非常小。|Synonyms: tiny, small, miniature, minuscule, petite|Antonyms: huge, enormous, gigantic, large, massive|adjective

diminutive:/dɪˈmɪnjətɪv/| n.|爱称，昵称|A shortened form of a name, typically used informally.|“Will” is a diminutive of “William”. “Will”是“William”的昵称。|Synonyms: nickname, sobriquet, pet name|Antonyms: full name, formal name|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French diminutif, -ive, from Latin diminutivus, from diminuere ‘to lessen’.
