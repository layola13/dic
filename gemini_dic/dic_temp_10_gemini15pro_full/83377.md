gyrose:/ˈdʒaɪˌroʊs/ /ˈdʒaɪrəʊs/| adj.|旋转的，回旋的；(植物学)螺旋形的，环状的|Marked by a circular or spiral arrangement or motion; sinuous; winding.  Specifically (Botany) coiled or convoluted.|The gyrose movements of the dancer were mesmerizing. 舞者的旋转动作令人着迷。|近义词：spiral, coiled, convoluted, winding, twisting, sinuous|反义词：straight, linear|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *gyrōsus, from Ancient Greek γῦρος (gûros, “circle, ring”) +‎ -ōsus.
