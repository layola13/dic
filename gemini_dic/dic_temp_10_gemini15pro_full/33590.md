cimbal:/ˈtʃɪmbəl/| n.|钦巴龙，扬琴|A cimbalom is a musical instrument of the dulcimer family, with strings struck by hammers.|I heard a beautiful melody played on a cimbalom. 我听到一首用钦巴龙演奏的优美旋律。|Synonym: dulcimer, hammered dulcimer|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|noun

Etymology:
From Hungarian *cimbalom*, from German *Zymbal*, from Italian *cembalo*, ultimately from Greek *kymbalon* "cymbal."
