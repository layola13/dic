infanthood:/ˈɪnfənthʊd/| n.|婴儿期，幼年时期|The period of a child's life from birth to about two years of age; early childhood.|She has few memories of her infanthood. 她对自己的婴儿期几乎没有记忆。|近义词：babyhood, infancy|反义词：adulthood, maturity|词性:名词
Etymology: From infant + -hood. Infant comes from the Latin infans, meaning "unable to speak," from in- "not" + fans "speaking."  -hood is a suffix denoting a state or condition.
