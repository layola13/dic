nine:/naɪn/ |num. adj.|九；第九|The number after eight and before ten.  A cardinal number.| There are nine planets in our solar system. 太阳系有九大行星。|  I live on the ninth floor. 我住在九楼。| 近义词: none (as in 'no one' in some dialects) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: numeral, adjective

Etymology: From Old English *nigon, from Proto-Germanic *newun, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁néwn̥. Cognate with German neun, Dutch negen, Latin novem, Greek ἐννέα (ennéa), Sanskrit नव (náva).
