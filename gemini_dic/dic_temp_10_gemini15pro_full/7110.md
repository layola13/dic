aneurysmal:/ˌænjuˈrɪzml/ | adj. | 动脉瘤的；像动脉瘤的；膨大的；不稳定的 | Relating to or resembling an aneurysm; exhibiting a sudden or dramatic expansion or enlargement.  |  The doctor discovered an aneurysmal dilation in the patient's aorta. 医生在病人的主动脉发现了动脉瘤扩张。 | Synonyms: bulging, dilated, enlarged, expanded, swollen, unstable, precarious | Antonyms: contracted, shrunken, stable | Adjective

aneurismal:/ˌænjuˈrɪzml/ | adj. | 危险的；随时可能破裂的 |  Likely to rupture or burst; highly unstable and dangerous.  | His aneurysmal temper made everyone nervous.  他随时可能爆发的脾气让每个人都很紧张。| Synonyms: dangerous, precarious, risky, unstable | Antonyms: safe, secure, stable | Adjective


Etymology:  From aneurysm + -al.  Aneurysm comes from the Greek "aneurysma," meaning "a widening."
