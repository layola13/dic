anecdotally:/ˌænɪkˈdəʊtəli/ | adv. |  轶事地；趣闻地 | Based on personal accounts or stories rather than on systematic research or scientific evidence. |  Many people claim anecdotally that the new medicine has helped them, but more research is needed.  很多人声称这种新药对他们有帮助，但这还需要更多的研究。| Synonyms: informally, unofficially, casually | Antonyms: scientifically, formally, empirically | 词性: 副词

anecdotally:/ˌænɪkˈdoʊtəli/ | adv. | 轶闻地；趣闻地 |  Relaying information based on casual observations or stories, not necessarily reliable data. |  He anecdotally mentioned that the local economy was improving. 他随意提到当地经济正在改善。 | Synonyms: narratively, descriptively, empirically (when referring to personal experience) | Antonyms: statistically, scientifically, objectively | 词性: 副词
