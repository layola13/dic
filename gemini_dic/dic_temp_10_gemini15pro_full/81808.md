gressorial:/ɡrɛˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
适于行走的，步行的
adapted for walking
The gressorial legs of the tiger beetle allow it to run quickly. 虎甲虫适于行走的腿让它可以快速奔跑。
近义词: ambulatory, cursorial
反义词: fossorial, aerial
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *gressorius*, from gradi "to walk," from PIE root *ghredh- "to walk, go."
