officiates:/əˈfɪʃieɪts/
verb
主持，执行，担任裁判
acts as an official in charge of something, especially a sporting event or religious ceremony.
The referee officiated the football game.  裁判主持了足球比赛。
The priest officiated at the wedding.  牧师主持了婚礼。
synonyms: preside, referee, umpire, judge, administer, conduct, direct, oversee, supervise, manage, run, control
antonyms: observe, spectate, witness

词性:v.
