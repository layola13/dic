embellishes:/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪz/| v.|装饰，修饰，润色|To make something more attractive or interesting by adding details, often decorative ones.  It implies making something already good even better or more elaborate.|She embellished the story with vivid descriptions of the landscape. 她用对景色的生动描述来润色这个故事。|Synonyms: adorn, decorate, enhance, ornament, enrich|Antonyms: simplify, strip, understate|verb
