overflying:/ˌoʊvərˈflaɪɪŋ/| adj. v. (present participle of overfly)|飞越的，越过的|Flying over a place, especially without permission.|例句：The overflying aircraft was identified as a military plane. 这架飞越的飞机被确定为军用飞机。

例句：They were concerned about the number of overflying planes. 他们担心飞越的飞机数量。|近义词：flying over, passing over|反义词:landing, underflying|词性:形容词, 动词(现在分词)
