loathsomely:/ˈloʊðsəmli/ | adv. | 讨厌地，令人作呕地 | In an extremely offensive or repugnant manner; disgustingly. | He stared loathsomely at the rotting food. 他厌恶地盯着腐烂的食物。| The wound oozed loathsomely. 伤口令人作呕地渗出脓液。 | 近义词: repugnantly, offensively, disgustingly, abhorrently, detestably | 反义词: pleasantly, attractively, appealingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From loathsome + -ly.  Loathsome comes from Middle English *lothsom, from Old English lāðsum (“hateful, loathsome”), from lāð (“hateful, hostile”) + -sum.
