frats:/fræts/
n.
兄弟会;  大学生联谊会
(informal) fraternities, especially those for male students.
例句：He joined one of the frats at college. 他在大学里加入了一个兄弟会。

例句：The frats are throwing a huge party this weekend.  兄弟会这周末要举行一个盛大的派对。

近义词：fraternities, brotherhoods
反义词：sororities (specifically for women)
词性：名词(plural noun)
