misgender: /ˌmɪsˈdʒɛndər/ | v. | 错误地称呼某人的性别 | To refer to someone using a word, especially a pronoun or form of address, that does not correctly reflect the gender with which they identify. |  She was constantly misgendered by her colleagues, which made her feel very uncomfortable. 她经常被同事称呼错误的性别，这让她感到很不舒服。

He accidentally misgendered his new classmate, and quickly apologized. 他不小心用错了新同学的性别称呼，并很快道歉了。 | 近义词: mispronoun, mislabel | 反义词:  N/A (Respecting someone's gender identity doesn't have a single antonym) | 词性: verb
