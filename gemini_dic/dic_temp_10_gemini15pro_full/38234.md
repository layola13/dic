词源：

This word is a humorous or ironic neologism, formed by adding the suffix "-doms" (meaning "domain" or "realm," often used ironically) to the word "computer."  It's not a standard dictionary word but is understandable in context.  The suffix "-dom" is seen in words like kingdom, Christendom, and dukedom.
