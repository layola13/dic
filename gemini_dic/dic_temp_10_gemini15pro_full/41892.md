cosigning:/ˌkoʊˈsaɪnɪŋ/
|n.| 共同签署，联署|The act of signing a loan or lease with another person, jointly assuming responsibility for repayment.|例句：Cosigning a loan can be risky if the primary borrower defaults.  共同签署贷款是有风险的，如果主要借款人违约的话。

例句：She helped her son by cosigning his car loan. 她通过共同签署汽车贷款来帮助她的儿子。
|近义词：guaranteeing, endorsing, vouching|反义词：refusing, declining|词性: noun (gerund)

cosign:/ˌkoʊˈsaɪn/
|v.| 共同签署，联署|To sign a loan or lease with another person, jointly assuming responsibility for repayment.|例句：I cosigned my daughter's student loan. 我共同签署了我女儿的学生贷款。

例句：He was reluctant to cosign for his friend's business loan. 他不愿意为朋友的商业贷款共同签署。

|近义词：guarantee, endorse, vouch|反义词：refuse, decline|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + sign. Co- means "together," "jointly," or "mutually." Sign means "to mark with one's signature." Therefore, cosign literally means "to sign together."
