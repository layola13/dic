parallelled: /ˈpærəˌleld/ | v. | 平行，与…相似，比得上 |  To be similar to something else, or to match something else in some way.  Also, to draw a parallel between two things. |  Her rise to fame has been paralleled by a decline in her personal life. 她的名声鹊起与她个人生活的衰落同时发生。

The police are paralleling this case with a similar murder last year. 警方正在将这起案件与去年发生的类似谋杀案进行比较。 | Synonyms:  matched, equated, compared, likened | Antonyms:  diverged, contrasted, differed | verb

parallelled: /ˈpærəˌleld/ | adj. | 平行的，类似的 |  Similar or analogous; corresponding.  | The development of the computer industry paralleled the growth of the internet. 计算机产业的发展与互联网的发展同步。

The two roads run paralleled for several miles. 这两条道路平行延伸了几英里。| Synonyms:  similar, analogous, corresponding, akin | Antonyms:  divergent, contrasting, different | adjective
