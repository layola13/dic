humanistic:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 人文主义的；人本主义的；人文精神的 |  Relating to or supporting the principles of humanism; emphasizing human potential and values. |  例句：Humanistic psychology emphasizes personal growth and self-actualization. 人本主义心理学强调个人成长和自我实现。

例句：The museum's collection reflects a humanistic approach to art.  博物馆的收藏反映了一种人文主义的艺术观。

近义词：human-centered, compassionate, empathetic | 反义词：inhuman, unfeeling, authoritarian | 词性：adjective
