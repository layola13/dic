evacuees:/ɪˌvækjuˈiːz/
n.
被疏散者，撤离者
People who have been evacuated from a dangerous place.
The evacuees were housed in temporary shelters.  被疏散者被安置在临时避难所。
The evacuees from the flooded town were given food and blankets.  从被洪水淹没的城镇撤离的人们得到了食物和毯子。
refugees, displaced persons
residents, inhabitants
noun
