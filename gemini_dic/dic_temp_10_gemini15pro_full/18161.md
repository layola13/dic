bejape:/bɪˈdʒeɪp/| vt.|欺骗，愚弄|To deceive or trick someone, often in a playful or mischievous way.|He bejaped his friend by hiding his car keys.|近义词: trick, deceive, fool, dupe, hoodwink|反义词: enlighten, inform, help|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *bejapen, from be- + japen (“to jest, joke, trick”).  More distantly related to jape.
