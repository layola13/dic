half-dressed:/ˌhæf ˈdrest/
adjective
半裸的，衣衫不整的
partially clothed; not fully dressed
例句：He answered the door half-dressed. 他衣衫不整地开了门。

例句：She ran out into the street half-dressed in her pajamas. 她穿着睡衣，衣衫不整地跑到街上。

近义词：partially clothed, scantily clad, underdressed
反义词：fully dressed, properly dressed
词性：adjective
