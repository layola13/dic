moraler:/ˈmɔːrələr/ (US) /ˈmɒrələr/ (UK)| n.|士气鼓舞者；振奋士气的人；激励者|Someone who boosts the morale of others.  A person who improves the spirits of others, often in a difficult situation.|例句：He was the team's moraler, always keeping spirits high. 他是球队的士气鼓舞者，总是保持高昂的士气。

例句：The coach acted as a moraler for the team after their devastating loss.  教练在球队惨败后充当了士气鼓舞者的角色。


近义词：motivator, inspirer, encourager|反义词: demoralizer, discourager|词性:noun
