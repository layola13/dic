charterers:/ˈtʃɑːrtərərz/
n.
承租人，租船人
A person or company that hires or leases a vessel or aircraft.
The charterers were responsible for the cargo. 承租人对货物负责。

charterers:/ˈtʃɑːrtərərz/
n.
包租人
A person or company that hires or leases something, especially a vehicle or vessel.
The charterers of the bus were a group of tourists. 巴士的包租人是一群游客。



近义词:lessees, hirers, renters
反义词:owners, lessors


词性:Noun (Plural)


词源: From the verb "to charter," which comes from the Old French "chartrer" (to hire), derived from the Late Latin "carraria" (cart).
