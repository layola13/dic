consortion:/kənˈsɔːrʃən/ /kənˈsɔːrtiən/
n.
财团，联合，合伙
a group of companies or organizations, especially financial institutions, that have joined together to undertake a large project
例句：The construction project was undertaken by a consortium of local businesses.  这个建筑项目是由当地企业组成的财团承办的。

例句：An international consortium was formed to finance the deal.  为了给这笔交易融资，成立了一个国际财团。

近义词：syndicate, alliance, coalition, partnership, association
反义词：None (No single antonym perfectly captures the opposite of a consortium)
词性：Noun
