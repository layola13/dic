dedicational:/ˌdedɪˈkeɪʃənəl/
adjective
奉献的；献辞的
Relating to or expressing dedication.  Showing or expressing devotion or commitment to a particular cause, person, or purpose.
The book contains a dedicational inscription to the author's parents. 这本书包含了一段献给作者父母的题词。
The museum hosted a dedicational ceremony for the new wing. 博物馆为新侧楼举行了落成典礼。
近义词: devoted, committed, dedicated
反义词: uncommitted, indifferent, apathetic
词性: adjective
词源: From "dedication" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin "dedicare" (to dedicate).
