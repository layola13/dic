over-clever:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛvər/ | adj. | 过于聪明的，自作聪明的 | Too clever for one's own good; excessively ingenious or cunning, often to the point of being impractical or counterproductive. | He came up with an over-clever plan that ultimately backfired. 他想出了一个过于聪明的计划，最终适得其反。 |  Example: His over-clever remarks alienated his colleagues. 他自作聪明的言论疏远了他的同事。| Synonyms: too clever, smart-alecky, cunning, shrewd, artful | Antonyms: naive, simple, unintelligent, foolish | word property: adjective
