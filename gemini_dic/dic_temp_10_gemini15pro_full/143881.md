outpolled:/ˌaʊtˈpoʊld/
verb
击败，在投票中胜过
To receive more votes than another candidate in an election.
The incumbent mayor was outpolled by a newcomer. 现任市长在选举中被一位新人击败。
The socialist candidate outpolled her conservative rival. 社会主义候选人在投票中胜过了她的保守派对手。
Synonyms: defeat, beat, surpass, outvote
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性:动词
