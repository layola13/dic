listings:/ˈlɪstɪŋz/| n.|清单，列表|A printed list of items for sale, especially of shares or property offered on a stock exchange or advertised for sale by an agent.  The act of including something in a list.|例句：The listings for apartments in the city center are extensive. 市中心的公寓房源清单很广泛。

例句：The company's listings on the stock exchange were suspended. 该公司在证券交易所的上市被暂停了。|近义词：catalog, register, inventory, directory|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
