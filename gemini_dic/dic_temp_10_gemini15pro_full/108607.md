leaded:/ˈlɛdɪd/ | adj. | 含铅的；加铅的 | Containing lead. | Leaded gasoline is harmful to the environment. 含铅汽油对环境有害。 | The walls were covered in leaded paint. 墙壁上涂满了含铅油漆。 | 近义词: lead-containing | 反义词: unleaded | 词性:形容词

leaded:/ˈlɛdɪd/ | adj. | (玻璃)镶嵌铅条的 | (of glass) having lead strips as a support or decoration. | The leaded windows of the church cast colorful patterns on the floor. 教堂的镶铅玻璃窗在地板上投下五彩缤纷的图案。| The artist created a beautiful leaded glass lampshade. 艺术家创作了一个漂亮的镶铅玻璃灯罩。 | 近义词: stained-glass (specifically for colored glass) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain glass") | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "lead" (to cover or fit with lead) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English "lēad" (lead).
