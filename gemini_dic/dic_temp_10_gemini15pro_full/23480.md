brags:/bræɡz/

brags:/bræɡz/
| n. (plural of brag) | 吹嘘，自夸 (常用复数形式，指多次吹嘘的行为) | Boastful statements or claims. |  All his brags about his wealth turned out to be lies. 所有关于他财富的吹嘘结果都是谎言。 | boasts, vauntings | apologies, understatements | noun
