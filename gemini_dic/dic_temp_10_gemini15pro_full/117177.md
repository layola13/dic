measles:/ˈmiːzəlz/| n. |麻疹|An infectious viral disease causing fever and a red rash, typically occurring in childhood.|例句：The child has come down with measles. 这个孩子得了麻疹。|例句：Measles is a highly contagious disease. 麻疹是一种高度传染的疾病。|近义词：rubeola|反义词: (None - diseases don't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *meseles*, from Old English *mēse*, a contraction of *mȳs* and *seocnes*. *Mēse* meaning *spot* or *stain*, related to German *Maser* (measles). *Seocnes*, from *seoc* meaning *sick*, is related to *seek*.  Therefore, measles literally meant "spotted sickness".
