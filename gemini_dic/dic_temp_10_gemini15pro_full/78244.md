geriatric: /ˌdʒɛriˈætrɪk/ | adj. n. | 老年医学的；老年人的；老年病学的 | Relating to old age, especially with regard to their healthcare and well-being.  |  例句：The hospital has a dedicated geriatric ward. 这家医院有一个专门的老年病房。 | 近义词：elderly, aged, senile | 反义词：young, youthful | 词性：形容词

geriatric: /ˌdʒɛriˈætrɪk/ | n. | 老年人 | An old person, especially one receiving special care. | 例句：The geriatric enjoyed the activities organized by the nursing home.  这位老年人很喜欢养老院组织的活动。| 近义词：senior citizen, oldster | 反义词：youngster, child | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
