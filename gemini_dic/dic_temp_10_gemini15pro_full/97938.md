innovation:/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃn/ | n. |革新，创新|The introduction of something new; a new idea, method, or device. |例句：Technological innovation is essential for economic growth. 技术创新对经济增长至关重要。

例句：The company encourages innovation in all departments.  这家公司鼓励所有部门进行创新。

近义词：novelty, invention, creativity, revolution, modernization,  advancement, breakthrough

反义词：tradition, stagnation, convention,  inertia, obsolescence

词性: noun
