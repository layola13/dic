equites:/ˈɛkwɪˌtiːz/
n.
骑士，骑兵，（古罗马）骑士阶级
Roman citizens who originally formed the cavalry of the Roman army and later developed into a wealthy social class.  They ranked below the senators but above the commoners.
The equites played a crucial role in Roman politics and commerce. 古罗马的骑士在政治和商业中扮演着至关重要的角色。
The equites were a powerful force in Roman society.  罗马骑士是罗马社会中一股强大的力量。

近义词:cavalry, horsemen, knights
反义词:plebeians, commoners
词性:noun (plural)


equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/
adj.
骑马的，马术的
Relating to horseback riding or horsemanship.
She demonstrated her equestrian skills at the competition. 她在比赛中展示了她的马术技巧。
He was a renowned equestrian artist, known for his lifelike depictions of horses. 他是一位著名的马术艺术家，以其栩栩如生的马匹描绘而闻名。
近义词:mounted
反义词:pedestrian
词性:adjective
