assimilatory:/əˈsɪmɪlətɔːri/
adjective
同化的，吸收的
Relating to or involving assimilation; causing assimilation.  Taking something in and absorbing it into a larger system or structure.
The assimilatory processes of the digestive system break down food. 消化系统的同化过程分解食物。
The government implemented assimilatory policies towards immigrants. 政府对移民实施了同化政策。
Synonyms: integrative, incorporative, absorptive
Antonyms: segregative, divisive, exclusionary
词性: adjective
