fuhrers:/ˈfʊərərz/| n.|元首（复数）; 领袖（复数）|A term, especially associated with Adolf Hitler, used to denote a political leader, particularly a dictator or authoritarian figure.  It is the plural form of "Fuhrer".|The history books recount the atrocities committed by the fuhrers of totalitarian regimes. 历史书籍记载了极权主义政权的元首们犯下的暴行。|Synonyms: leaders, dictators, rulers, chiefs|Antonyms: followers, subordinates|noun
