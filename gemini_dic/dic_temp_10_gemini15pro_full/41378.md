cormac: /ˈkɔːrmæk/ | n. | 科马克（男子名） |  An Irish Gaelic male given name, meaning "charioteer" or "raven". It is derived from the Old Irish name *Corbmac. | Cormac McCarthy is a famous American novelist. 科马克·麦卡锡是一位著名的美国小说家。|  Cormac is a popular name in Ireland. 科马克在爱尔兰是一个很受欢迎的名字. | 近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms, consider other Irish names like:  Conor,  Declan,  Finn) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
