cottagely:/ˈkɒtɪdʒli/| adj.|类似小屋的；乡村小屋风格的；质朴的|Characteristic of a cottage; rustic; simple. |The room had a cottagely charm with its exposed beams and fireplace. 房间里的裸露梁和壁炉有一种乡村小屋的魅力。| The tablecloth had a cottagely floral pattern.  桌布上有乡村小屋风格的花卉图案。 |近义词：rustic, rural, simple, homely|反义词：modern, sophisticated, urban|词性:形容词

词源:  From cottage + -ly.  "Cottage" comes from Old French "cotege" (sheepcote, hut), ultimately from "cote" (hut).  "-ly" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "like" or "characteristic of".
