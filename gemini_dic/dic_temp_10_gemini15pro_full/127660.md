词源:
From Middle English *nere, ner, from Old English nēar, nēah (“near, nigh, close; almost, nearly”), from Proto-Germanic *nēhwaz (“near”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁néǵʰ- (“near”). Cognate with Scots near (“near”), West Frisian near (“near”), Dutch naar (“near, to”), German nahe (“near”), Danish nær (“near”), Swedish nära (“near”), Icelandic nær (“near”). Related also to nigh.
