brickfielder:/ˈbrɪkˌfildər/| n.|砖匠风，砖厂风；热风，干燥的北风|A hot, dry, dusty northerly wind that blows in southern Australia, especially in spring and summer.  It originates in the arid interior of the continent and can cause a dramatic rise in temperature.|例句1：The brickfielder blew all day, coating everything in red dust. 砖匠风刮了一整天，把所有东西都蒙上了一层红尘。

例句2：The sudden brickfielder sent temperatures soaring. 突如其来的砖厂风使气温飙升。|近义词：dust storm, northerly, hot wind|反义词: southerly, cool breeze|noun
