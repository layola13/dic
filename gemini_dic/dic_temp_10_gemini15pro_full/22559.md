boot-cut:/ˈbuːtˌkʌt/
adjective
靴裤型的；喇叭形的
Slightly flared from the knee to the hem, designed to fit over boots.
例句：She wore a pair of dark wash boot-cut jeans. 她穿了一条深色水洗靴型牛仔裤。

例句：Boot-cut trousers are flattering on most body types. 靴裤对大多数体型的人都比较友好。

近义词：flared, bell-bottomed
反义词：straight-leg, skinny
词性：adjective
