obtuser:/əbˈtjuːsər/ /ɑːbˈtuːsər/
adjective
钝的；迟钝的；不敏感的
(of an angle) greater than 90° and less than 180°. More generally, lacking sharpness or quickness of sensibility or intellect.
例句：The obtuse angle of the roof made it difficult to walk underneath.  屋顶的钝角使得在下面行走很困难。

例句：He was obtuse to the subtle hints she was giving him. 他对她的暗示很迟钝。
近义词：blunt, dull, insensitive, slow-witted
反义词：acute, sharp, keen, perceptive
词性：adjective
