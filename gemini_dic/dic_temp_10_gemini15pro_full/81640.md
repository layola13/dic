greedsome: /ˈɡriːdsəm/ | adj. | 贪婪的，贪心的 | Excessively greedy; avaricious. | He had a greedsome desire for power. 他对权力有着贪得无厌的渴望。|  The greedsome landlord raised the rent again.  贪婪的房东又提高了租金。 | 近义词: greedy, avaricious, covetous, grasping | 反义词: generous, charitable, altruistic | 词性: 形容词

词源: From greed + -some,  influenced by words like "winsome" and "lovesome".  "Greed" comes from Old English "grǣdig," meaning "greedy."  "-some" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, verbs, and other adjectives, meaning "characterized by" or "given to."
