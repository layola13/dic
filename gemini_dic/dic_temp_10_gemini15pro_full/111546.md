lobbed:/lɑbd/ |/lɒbd/| v. |投掷；吊高球|To propel (something) slowly in a high arc.|He lobbed the ball over the fence. 他把球扔过篱笆。|近义词：tossed, pitched, flung, hurled |反义词: caught, received |动词

lobbed:/lɑbd/ |/lɒbd/| adj. |(球)吊高的|(of a ball or shot) hit so as to travel in a high arc.|a lobbed pass 一个吊高球传球|近义词：tossed, high-arcing |反义词: low, driven |形容词
