overindebtednesses:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdɛtɪdnəsɪz/
|n.|过度负债（复数）
|The state of owing more debt than one can reasonably repay.|
|Many developing countries are struggling with overindebtednesses.|许多发展中国家正在努力应对过度负债问题。
|Overindebtednesses can lead to financial ruin.|过度负债会导致经济崩溃。
|synonyms: excessive debt, insolvency, overleveraging|
|antonyms: solvency, financial stability|
|noun|


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "indebtedness" ("in-" (Latin in, meaning "in, into, on, upon, against") + "debt" (Old French dette, from Latin debitum "thing owed," past participle of debere "to owe") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).
