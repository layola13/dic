gin:/dʒɪn/ | n. | 杜松子酒；陷阱；轧棉机 | An alcoholic spirit distilled from grain or malt and flavored with juniper berries; a trap or snare; a machine for separating cotton from its seeds. |  I like a gin and tonic. 我喜欢杜松子酒加汤力水。 | synonyms: genever, snare, trap | antonyms: freedom, release | noun

gin:/dʒɪn/ | v. | 轧（棉花） | To separate cotton from its seeds with a gin. | The cotton was ginned before being shipped. 棉花在装船前被轧过。 | synonyms: separate, extract | antonyms: combine, mix | verb
