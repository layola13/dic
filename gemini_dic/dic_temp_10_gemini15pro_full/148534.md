oversusceptibility:/ˌōvərˌsəˌseptəˈbɪləti/
|n.|过度敏感，过度易感性|The state of being unusually sensitive or susceptible to something, especially to a disease or allergen.|People with allergies often have an oversusceptibility to certain substances. 过敏的人通常对某些物质过度敏感。|
|Synonym: hypersensitivity, vulnerability|Antonym: resistance, immunity|noun|

oversusceptible:/ˌōvərˌsəˈseptəbəl/
|adj.|过度敏感的，过度易感的|Unusually sensitive or susceptible to something, especially to a disease or allergen.|He is oversusceptible to colds. 他很容易感冒。|
|Synonym: hypersensitive, vulnerable|Antonym: resistant, immune|adjective|
