flue:/fluː/
noun
烟道，管道
a duct for smoke and waste gases, especially one leading from a fireplace to the top of a chimney.
The chimney flue needs cleaning. 烟囱的烟道需要清理。
近义词: chimney, vent, duct
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "intake")


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flewe*, *flue*, from Old French *flue* ("flowing, flood"), from Latin *fluō* ("I flow"). Related to flow, fluid, fluent.
