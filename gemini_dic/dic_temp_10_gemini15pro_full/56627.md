doni:/ˈdoʊni/| n.|多尼（印度尼西亚语，指一种传统的帆船）|A doni is a traditional Indonesian sailing vessel, typically with two masts and large, colorful sails. It is often used for fishing or inter-island transport.|We saw a beautiful doni sailing across the horizon. 我们看到一艘美丽的doni号帆船航行在地平线上。|近义词：perahu, boat, vessel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "land vehicle")|Noun
