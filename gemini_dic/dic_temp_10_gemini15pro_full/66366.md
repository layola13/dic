excuseless: /ɪkˈskjuːsləs/
adjective
无借口的，不可原谅的
Without excuse or justification; inexcusable.
His behavior was absolutely excuseless. 他的行为绝对不可原谅。
近义词: inexcusable, unjustifiable, unforgivable, indefensible
反义词: excusable, justifiable, forgivable, defensible
词性: adjective
词源:  From excuse + -less.  "Excuse" comes from Old French "excuser," from Latin "excusare" meaning "to free from blame."
