freeway:/ˈfriːweɪ/
n.
高速公路，免费公路
A controlled-access highway, especially one without tolls.
We took the freeway to Los Angeles. 我们走高速公路去了洛杉矶。
近义词：expressway, highway, motorway, thruway, turnpike
反义词:  street, road (These aren't true opposites, but represent different types of roads)
词性: 名词


freeway:/ˈfriːweɪ/
词源:  From *free* (meaning without cost, particularly tolls) + *way* (meaning road or path).  The term originated in the United States.
用法:  Primarily used in American English to refer to a controlled-access highway, often (but not always) without tolls.
