liquidnesses:/lɪˈkwɪdɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
流动性，变现能力
The degree to which an asset or security can be quickly bought or sold in the market without affecting the asset's price.  The ease with which something can be converted into cash.
The company's liquidnesses improved after selling some of its assets.  该公司出售了一些资产后，流动性得到了改善。
liquidity, fluidity
illiquidity, frozenness
noun
