overperemptorilyness:/ˌoʊvərˌpɛrˈɛmptərɪlinɪs/ | n. | 过于专横; 过于武断 | The state or quality of being overperemptory; excessive dictatorialness or authoritarianism. | His overperemptorilyness alienated his colleagues. 他的过于专横疏远了他的同事。 | 近义词：dictatorialness, authoritarianism, imperiousness, high-handedness | 反义词：humility, modesty, meekness | 词性: noun

词源: From overperemptory + -ness.  Overperemptory itself comes from over- + peremptory. Peremptory ultimately derives from Latin *peremptorius, from perimere "to destroy, kill," from per- "thoroughly" + emere "to take, buy."
