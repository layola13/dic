contently:/kənˈtɛntli/ | adv. | 满意地，心满意足地 | In a content and satisfied manner; happily. | She smiled contently after finishing her meal. 她吃完饭后满意地笑了。|  He sat contently by the fire, reading a book. 他心满意足地坐在火炉旁看书。 | 近义词: happily, cheerfully, peacefully, serenely | 反义词: discontentedly, unhappily, restlessly | 词性: 副词

词源: From content (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix).  Ultimately from Latin contentus, past participle of continere "to contain, hold together."
