bladderlike:/ˈblædərlaɪk/
adjective
像膀胱的；囊状的
Resembling a bladder; having the form or thin, flexible quality of a bladder.
The jellyfish had a bladderlike body, pulsating gently in the current.  水母有一个像膀胱一样的身体，在水流中轻轻地搏动着。
The plant's seed pods were bladderlike and filled with air. 这种植物的种子荚像膀胱一样，充满了空气。
近义词:saclike, pouchlike, cystlike
反义词:solid, rigid
词性:adjective
