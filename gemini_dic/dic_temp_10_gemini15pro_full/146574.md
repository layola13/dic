overgratification:/ˌōvərˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度满足；过分满足 | Excessive gratification of desires, often leading to negative consequences. | Example:  Overgratification in childhood can lead to a sense of entitlement later in life. 童年时期的过度满足会导致成年后产生一种权利感。

Example: The overgratification of his appetite led to health problems. 他对食欲的过度放纵导致了健康问题。 | 近义词: overindulgence, satiety, surfeit | 反义词: deprivation, frustration, self-denial | 词性: noun

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "gratification" (from Late Latin "gratificatio," meaning "pleasing, giving pleasure")
