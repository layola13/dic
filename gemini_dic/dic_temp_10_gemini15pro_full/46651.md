damningness:/ˈdæmɪŋnɪs/
n.
定罪性；该死程度；非常不利
The quality of being damning; the extent to which something is damning.
The damningness of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  这些证据非常不利，导致了迅速定罪。
The sheer damningness of his silence was palpable. 他沉默的该死程度显而易见。
近义词: incrimination, culpability, condemnation
反义词: exculpation, exoneration
词性: 名词

Etymology: From "damning" + "-ness".  "Damning" comes from the verb "damn," from Old English *dēman "to judge, deem," from Proto-Germanic *dōmijaną "to judge, set, think," from PIE *dʰeh₁- "to set, put."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
