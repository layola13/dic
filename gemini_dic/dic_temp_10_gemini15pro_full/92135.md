hypersensibilities:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/| n. |过敏反应；过度敏感|Excessive reactions of the immune system to certain substances.|  Example: He suffers from multiple hypersensitivities, including allergies to pollen and dust mites. 他患有多种过敏症，包括对花粉和尘螨过敏。

Example:  Food hypersensitivities can manifest in various ways, from mild rashes to severe anaphylaxis. 食物过敏反应的表现形式多种多样，从轻微的皮疹到严重的过敏性休克。|近义词:allergies, sensitivities|反义词: tolerances, immunities|词性:noun (plural)
