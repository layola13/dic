enrolling:/ɪnˈroʊlɪŋ/
verb
登记；注册；招收
The act of registering or signing up for something, such as a class, program, or service.
She is enrolling in a photography class. 她正在报名参加摄影课。
The college is enrolling students for the fall semester.  这所大学正在招收秋季学期的学生。
synonyms: registering, signing up, joining, admitting
antonyms: withdrawing, dropping out, unenrolling
