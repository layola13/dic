lithodome:/ˈlɪθəˌdoʊm/| n. |石蛏;穿石贝;石鳖|A marine mollusk of the family Lithophaginae that bores into rocks or shells.|The lithodome creates its burrow by chemical means, not by mechanical drilling. 石蛏通过化学方式而不是机械钻孔来建造它的洞穴。|近义词：rock borer, date mussel|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider free-swimming mollusk)|词性:noun
