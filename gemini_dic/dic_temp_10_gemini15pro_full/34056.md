civet:/ˈsɪvɪt/ | n. | 灵猫；灵猫香 |A slender nocturnal carnivorous mammal of tropical Asia and Africa, having a long body, short legs, and a bushy tail.  Also refers to a strong musky scent secreted by glands near the anus of the civet, used in perfumery. | Civets are known for their distinctive scent. 灵猫以其独特的气味而闻名。|The perfume contained a hint of civet. 这款香水带有一丝灵猫香。 | synonyms: civet cat, toddycat | antonyms: N/A | noun
