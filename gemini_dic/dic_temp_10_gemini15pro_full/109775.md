levitating:/ˈlɛvɪteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
悬浮的，飘浮的
rising or floating in the air, especially in a way that appears to be magical
The magician made the assistant appear to be levitating. 魔术师让助手看起来像是漂浮在空中。
近义词:floating, hovering, suspended
反义词:grounded, sinking, falling
词性:adjective

levitate:/ˈlɛvɪteɪt/
verb
使悬浮，使飘浮
to rise or float in the air, especially in a way that appears to be magical
The magician levitated the table. 魔术师使桌子漂浮起来。
近义词:float, hover, rise
反义词:fall, sink, drop
词性:verb


词源:
From Latin *levitatus, past participle of levitare (“to make light, lift up”), from levis (“light”).
