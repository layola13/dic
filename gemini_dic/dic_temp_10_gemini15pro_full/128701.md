nidra:/ˈnɪdrə/ | n. |睡眠，瑜伽睡眠|A state of conscious deep sleep, often induced through guided meditation in yoga practice.  It is a practice of pratyahara (withdrawal of the senses). |Example:  She practiced yoga nidra for an hour to reduce stress. 她练习了一小时瑜伽睡眠来减轻压力。
|Example: Yoga nidra helps me sleep deeper at night. 瑜伽睡眠帮助我晚上睡得更深。|Synonyms: sleep, slumber, repose, rest|Antonyms: wakefulness, insomnia|noun
