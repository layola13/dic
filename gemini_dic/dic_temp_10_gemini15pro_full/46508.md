daisied:/ˈdeɪzid/ | adj. |  雏菊覆盖的；饰以雏菊的 | Covered or decorated with daisies. | The field was daisied and bright. 田野上开满了雏菊，一片明亮。| The little girl wore a daisied dress. 小女孩穿着一条饰有雏菊图案的连衣裙。 | 近义词: flowery, floral | 反义词: barren, bare | 词性: 形容词

词源: From daisy + -ed.  "Daisy" comes from Old English dægeseage, meaning "day's eye".
