hypercarbureted:/ˌhaɪpərˈkärbjəˌreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度增碳的，过度碳化的
excessively carbureted; containing an excessive amount of carbon.  This term usually refers to a fuel mixture, implying it is overly rich in fuel and may not combust properly.
The engine sputtered and coughed, likely because the mixture was hypercarbureted.  发动机噼啪作响并且咳嗽，可能是因为混合物过度碳化了。
The hypercarbureted fuel caused the engine to run poorly. 过度碳化的燃料导致发动机运转不良。
近义词:over-rich, saturated
反义词:lean (mixture)
词性:adjective
