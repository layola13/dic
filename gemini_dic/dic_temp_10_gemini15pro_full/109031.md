leg-pulling:/ˈleɡˌpʊlɪŋ/
noun
开玩笑；戏弄；愚弄
The act of teasing or tricking someone in a playful way.
"He wasn't serious, it was just a bit of leg-pulling." 他不是认真的，只是开个玩笑。
Synonyms: teasing, joking, kidding, ribbing, joshing, bantering
Antonyms: seriousness, sincerity
n.
