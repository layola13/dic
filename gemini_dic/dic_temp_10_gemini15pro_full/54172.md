disentrance:/dɪsˈɛntrəns/ | n. | 迷惑，觉醒，清醒 |The act of disenchanting; freeing from illusion or false belief. A state of disillusionment or freedom from enchantment.  |  His disenchantment with politics led him to resign. 他对政治的觉醒导致他辞职。 | 近义词: disillusionment, awakening, sobriety | 反义词: enchantment, illusion, delusion | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From dis- + entrance (in the obsolete sense "a state of enchantment or ecstasy").  Ultimately from Latin *intransire "to enter into," from in- "into" + transire "to go across."
