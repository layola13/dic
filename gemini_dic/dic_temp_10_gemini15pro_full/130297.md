non-syllabic:/ˌnɒn.sɪˈlæb.ɪk/ /ˌnɑːn.səˈlæb.ɪk/
adjective
非音节的
Not forming or constituting a syllable.  A sound that doesn't form the nucleus of a syllable.
The "n" in "button" is a non-syllabic consonant.  “button”中的“n”是一个非音节辅音。
non-vowel, consonantal
