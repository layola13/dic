bifurcation:/ˌbaɪfərˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 分叉，分歧 | the division of something into two branches or parts | The bifurcation of the river created two distinct channels. 这条河的分叉形成了两条不同的河道。| synonyms: division, split, fork, branching | antonyms: convergence, union, merging | noun

bifurcate:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪt/ | v. | 分叉，分歧 | to divide into two branches or parts | The road bifurcates at the top of the hill. 这条路在山顶分叉。| synonyms: divide, split, fork, branch | antonyms: converge, unite, merge | verb
