hypernyms:/ˈhaɪpərˌnɪmz/| n.|上位词，上义词|A word with a broad meaning constituting a category into which words with more specific meanings fall; a superordinate.  Hypernyms are words that refer to a more general class or category than a given word.|Example: "Animal" is a hypernym of "dog," "cat," and "bird."  动物是狗、猫和鸟的上位词。|Synonyms: superordinate, generic term|Antonyms: hyponym, subordinate|Noun
