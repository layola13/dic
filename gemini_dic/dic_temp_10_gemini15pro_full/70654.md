filtrated:/ˈfɪltreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过滤的；滤过的 | Having passed through a filter.  | The filtrated water was clear and pure. 过滤后的水清澈纯净。|  The solution was filtrated to remove impurities.  溶液经过过滤以去除杂质。 | 近义词: filtered, strained, purified | 反义词: unfiltered, unstrained, impure | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "filtrate," which comes from the Latin "filtrum" meaning "felt" (used in early filters) + "-ate" (verb suffix).

PHRASE->
While less common than "filtered," "filtrated" can be used in phrases like:

* filtrated water (过滤水)
* filtrated solution (过滤溶液)
* filtrated air (过滤空气)
* a filtrated sample (过滤后的样品)
* the filtrated liquid (过滤后的液体)

注意事项:

"Filtered" is generally preferred in modern English. "Filtrated" is less common but still considered correct.
