bccs:/ˌbiː siː siːz/| n.|密送，副本抄送|Blind Carbon Copies, an email feature that lets you send a copy of the email to someone without the primary recipients knowing.|I sent the email to John and bcc'd Mary. 我把邮件发给了约翰，并密送给了玛丽。|近义词:carbon copy (cc)|反义词:to|noun
