architecturalist:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəlɪst/
n.
建筑师; 建筑学家; 建筑理论家
A person who designs buildings and other structures.  A person who studies the art and science of designing and constructing buildings. A person who theorizes about architecture.
例句：The architecturalist designed a stunning skyscraper.  这位建筑师设计了一座令人惊叹的摩天大楼。

例句：The architecturalist wrote a book on the history of Gothic architecture. 这位建筑学家写了一本关于哥特式建筑历史的书。

近义词：architect, designer, architectural theorist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
