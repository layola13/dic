biographical:/ˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
传记的;传记体的
Relating to the facts and events of a person's life.
例句：She wrote a biographical sketch of the artist. 她为这位艺术家写了一篇传记小品。

例句：The book is a biographical account of the president's life. 这本书是总统生平的传记。

近义词：autobiographical, life-story, life, memoir
反义词：fictional, invented
