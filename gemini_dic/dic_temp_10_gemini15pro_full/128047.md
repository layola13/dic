neri:/ˈnɛri/| adj.|黑色的；深色的；墨色的|Dark, black, or inky.  Relating to the Italian political party, Movimento Sociale Italiano - Destra Nazionale (Italian Social Movement - National Right).|例句：The neri ink stained the parchment. 墨水弄脏了羊皮纸。|例句：His neri eyes held a deep sadness. 他深邃的黑眼睛里充满了悲伤。|近义词：black, dark, dusky, ebony, inky, jet-black|反义词：white, light, pale, bright|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Italian *nero* (black), from Latin *niger* (black).  Also used to refer to the black shirts worn by supporters of Mussolini and later used as a nickname for the neo-fascist party MSI.
