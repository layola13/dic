odalisque:/ˈoʊdəlɪsk/ | n. | 女奴，侍妾 | A female slave or concubine in a harem, especially in the Ottoman Empire, often depicted in art as reclining sensuously. |  The artist painted a beautiful odalisque draped in silks. 艺术家画了一个穿着丝绸的美丽女奴。| synonyms: concubine, slave girl, harem girl | antonyms:  master, mistress | noun
