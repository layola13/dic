contriter:/kənˈtraɪtər/| adj.|更懊悔的，更悔恨的|Feeling or expressing remorse to a greater degree than before.|After seeing the consequences of his actions, he felt even more contrite.|近义词：remorseful, penitent, repentant|反义词：unrepentant, impenitent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *contrītus*, past participle of *contrīre* (“to rub together, grind, bruise”), from Latin *com-* (“together”) + *terere* (“to rub, grind”).
