fruitily:/ˈfruːtɪli/ | adv. | 丰硕地，富有成果地，甜美地 | In a manner resembling or suggesting fruit; productively; sweetly. | The garden fruitily produced a bounty of apples.  花园里结出了大量的苹果。 | 近义词：productively, abundantly, richly, sweetly | 反义词：barrenly, unproductively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From fruit + -ily.  Ultimately from Latin *fructus*.
