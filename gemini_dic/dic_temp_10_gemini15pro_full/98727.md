insurgently:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒəntli/
| adv. |叛乱地，暴动地；反抗地|In a rebellious or insurrectionary manner; characteristic of an insurgent. |The crowd reacted insurgently to the news.  人群对这个消息反应强烈。| 近义词: rebelliously, mutinously, seditiously | 反义词: obediently, submissively, passively |词性:副词

insurgently:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒəntli/
| adv. |反抗地；造反地|In a manner that opposes or challenges authority or established norms. |He insurgently questioned the company's policies. 他反叛地质疑了公司的政策。| 近义词: defiantly, resisting, opposing | 反义词: compliantly, obediently, submissively |词性:副词
