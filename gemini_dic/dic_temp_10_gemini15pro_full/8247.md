anti-inductive:/ˌæntiɪnˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 反归纳的，反诱导的 |  Contrary to or opposing induction, especially the process of reasoning from specific instances to general principles.  It often refers to situations where a pattern observed in the past does not reliably predict future behavior. | The stock market's behavior can be anti-inductive; past performance is not necessarily indicative of future results. 股票市场的行为可能是反归纳的；过去的表现不一定能预示未来的结果。

The anti-inductive nature of complex systems makes predictions challenging. 复杂系统的反归纳特性使预测变得具有挑战性。 | Near synonyms: non-inductive, counter-inductive | Near antonyms: inductive | 词性: 形容词
