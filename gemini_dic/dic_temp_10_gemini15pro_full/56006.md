do-gooder:/ˈduːˌɡʊdər/
n.
行善者；爱管闲事的人；不切实际的理想主义者
A person who does things they think are good, often without understanding the problems they are trying to solve or without other people wanting their help.
例句：He's seen as a do-gooder by some, but others admire his commitment.  他被一些人视为爱管闲事的人，但其他人钦佩他的奉献精神。

例句：The do-gooders were trying to raise money for the homeless. 这些行善者正试图为无家可归的人筹款。

近义词：altruist, philanthropist, humanitarian, reformer
反义词：malefactor, wrongdoer

词性: 名词
