marcato:/mɑːrˈkɑːtoʊ/ /mɑːˈkɑːtəʊ/
| adj. adv.|
| 强调的，突出的 (音乐术语) |
| Played or sung with each note sharply detached or accented.  用每个音符清晰分离或重音演奏或演唱。|
| The pianist played the passage marcato, giving each note a strong emphasis. 钢琴家以marcato的方式演奏这段乐句，每个音符都给予了强烈的强调。|
| The conductor instructed the strings to play the melody marcato. 指挥指示弦乐以marcato的方式演奏旋律。|
| 近义词: accented, emphasized, stressed |
| 反义词: legato, smooth |
| 词性: adjective, adverb |
