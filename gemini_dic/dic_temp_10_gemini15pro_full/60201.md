efflagitate:/ˌɛfləˈdʒɪteɪt/
verb
恳求，迫切请求
To demand or request earnestly or urgently.
She efflagitated the king for mercy. 她恳求国王开恩。
Synonyms: beseech, implore, entreat, supplicate
Antonyms: demand, command, order
词性: 动词
