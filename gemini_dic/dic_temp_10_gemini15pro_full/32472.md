chinatowns:/ˈtʃaɪnəˌtaʊnz/| n. |唐人街（复数）|A district of any non-Chinese city in which the population is predominantly of Chinese origin or ancestry.|We visited several Chinatowns on our trip. 我们在旅途中参观了几个唐人街。|Synonyms: Chinese quarters, Chinese district|Antonyms: None|noun
