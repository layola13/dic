cobelligerent:/koʊˈbɛlɪdʒərənt/ /kəʊˈbɛlɪdʒərənt/
n.|交战国，共同交战国|A nation or group allied with another in a war.|例句：During World War II, the United States and the United Kingdom were cobelligerents against Nazi Germany.  二战期间，美国和英国是共同对抗纳粹德国的交战国。|例句：Several smaller nations joined the war as cobelligerents.  几个小国作为共同交战国加入了战争。|近义词：ally, confederate|反义词：enemy, adversary|词性:名词
cobelligerent:/koʊˈbɛlɪdʒərənt/ /kəʊˈbɛlɪdʒərənt/
adj.|共同交战的|Fighting as allies; jointly belligerent.|例句：The cobelligerent forces launched a joint offensive. 共同交战的部队发动了联合攻势。|例句：Their cobelligerent stance surprised many.  他们共同交战的立场让很多人感到惊讶。|近义词：allied, joint|反义词：enemy, opposing|词性:形容词
