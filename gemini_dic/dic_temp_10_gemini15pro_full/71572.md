flashily:/ˈflæʃɪli/| adv.|炫耀地，华丽地|In a showy or ostentatious manner; gaudily.|She dressed flashily in a bright pink dress and gold jewelry. 她穿着一件亮粉色的连衣裙和金色的珠宝，打扮得花枝招展。|近义词：ostentatiously, gaudily, showily|反义词：simply, plainly, modestly|词性:副词

flashily:/ˈflæʃɪli/| adv.|俗气地|In a vulgar or tasteless manner.|He drove a flashily customized sports car. 他开着一辆俗气地改装过的跑车。|近义词：gaudy, tawdry|反义词：tastefully, subtly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From flashy + -ly. Flashy itself comes from flash (v.) + -y.  The verb "flash" originates from Old Norse *flasa "to dash, splash," related to Old English *flæscian "to strip off flesh."
