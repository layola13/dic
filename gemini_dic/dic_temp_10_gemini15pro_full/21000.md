bless:/blɛs/| vt. n.|祝福，保佑，赞美|to ask God to protect someone or something; to make something holy; something that brings happiness|May God bless you. 愿上帝保佑你。|Synonyms: consecrate, sanctify, praise|Antonyms: curse, damn|verb

bless:/blɛs/| n.|祝福，好事|something that is good or helpful|It was a bless that no one was hurt.  没有人受伤真是万幸。|Synonyms: boon, gift, advantage|Antonyms: curse, misfortune|noun
