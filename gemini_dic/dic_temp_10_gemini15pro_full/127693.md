negative: /ˈneɡətɪv/ | adj. n. |消极的，否定的；负数；底片|Something that is negative involves a bad or harmful quality or effect.  A negative answer or decision means "no."|The test came back negative. 测试结果呈阴性。|Synonyms: adverse, bad, unfavorable|Antonyms: positive, affirmative|adjective

negative: /ˈneɡətɪv/ | n. |否定词；负数；底片|A word or statement indicating denial, refusal, or disagreement; a photographic image in which the light and shade values are reversed.|She answered in the negative. 她给出了否定的回答。|Synonyms: refusal, denial|Antonyms: affirmative, positive|noun

negatives: /ˈneɡətɪvz/ | n. | 否定，消极方面；底片|The opposite of positives; aspects or features that are unfavorable, disadvantageous, or pessimistic.  Also refers to photographic film or plates that produce a reversed image.|The negatives of living in the city include high rents and noise. 住在城市的缺点包括高租金和噪音。|Synonyms: drawbacks, disadvantages, downsides|Antonyms: positives, advantages, upsides|noun
