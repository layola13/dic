ahorseback:/əˈhɔːrsbæk/| adv.|骑马地，在马背上|on horseback means riding a horse. It describes the state of being mounted on a horse. |He traveled across the plains ahorseback. 他骑马穿越了平原。|近义词：mounted, riding|反义词:on foot|词性:副词
ahorseback:/əˈhɔːrsbæk/| adj.|骑马的|Being on or related to horseback riding. |an ahorseback messenger 一个骑马的信使|近义词：mounted, equestrian|反义词:dismounted, pedestrian|词性:形容词
