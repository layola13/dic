consciousness-raising:/ˈkɒnʃəsnəs ˈreɪzɪŋ/
noun
提高觉悟；意识觉醒
The process of increasing people's awareness or understanding of a particular social or political issue.
Example 1: The feminist movement of the 1970s involved a lot of consciousness-raising about gender inequality.  20世纪70年代的女权运动中有很多关于性别不平等的意识觉醒活动。
Example 2:  The documentary was intended as a consciousness-raising exercise about the plight of refugees.  这部纪录片的目的是提高人们对难民困境的认识。
Synonym: awareness-raising, education, enlightenment
Antonym: suppression, ignorance, concealment
