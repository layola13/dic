mulligrubs:/ˈmʌlɪɡrʌbz/
n.
肚子疼; 心情不好; 郁闷
A feeling of uneasiness or discomfort, often in the stomach; a state of low spirits; the sulks.
He woke up with the mulligrubs and decided to stay in bed. 他醒来时感到肚子不舒服，决定待在床上。
She had the mulligrubs after her argument with her friend.  她和朋友吵架后心情不好。
近义词: blues, doldrums, dumps, gloom, melancholy, sulks
反义词: cheerfulness, happiness, joy
词性: 名词
