domicile: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməˌsaɪl/ (US) | n. vt. |住所，定居地；使定居|A person's domicile is the place where they live permanently or consider their home. |  例句：He established his domicile in France. 他在法国定居。| 近义词：residence, abode, dwelling, home | 反义词:  (没有确切的反义词，可以考虑用 temporary residence 临时住所) | 词性：名词，动词

domicile: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməˌsaɪl/ (US) | vt. |使定居| To domicile someone means to establish their permanent residence. | 例句：The company domiciled its new employees in temporary housing. 公司将新员工安置在临时住房中。| 近义词：settle, establish, locate | 反义词: (没有确切的反义词，可以考虑用 relocate 迁移) | 词性：动词
