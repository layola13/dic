bipinnatifid:/ˌbaɪpɪˈnætɪfɪd/| adj.|二回羽状分裂的|Twice pinnately divided; each leaflet being pinnatifid.|Example: The fern had delicate, bipinnatifid fronds.  这种蕨类植物有精致的二回羽状分裂的叶子。|Example:  Bipinnatifid leaves are common in certain fern species.  二回羽状分裂的叶子在某些蕨类植物中很常见。|Synonym: bipinnate, twice-pinnate|Antonym: entire, simple|word property: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + pinnate (feather-like arrangement) + -fid (divided).  Ultimately from Latin *pinnatus* (feathered) and *findere* (to cleave).
