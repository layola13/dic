calque:/kælk/ /kɑːlk/| n.|仿造词，直译词|A word or phrase borrowed from another language by literal translation.  A loan translation.|例句：The English term “skyscraper” is a calque of the German word “Wolkenkratzer”. 英文单词“skyscraper”（摩天大楼）是德语单词“Wolkenkratzer”的仿造词。

例句：The expression "flea market" is a calque from the French "marché aux puces". “跳蚤市场”这个表达方式是从法语“marché aux puces”直译过来的。|近义词：loan translation, borrowing|反义词:|词性:noun
