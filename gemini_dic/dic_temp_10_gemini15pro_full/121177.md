misdirectedness:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛktɪdnəs/
|n.|误导，方向错误|The misdirectedness of the marketing campaign led to disappointing sales. 市场营销活动的误导导致销售额令人失望。|The misdirectedness of his efforts resulted in wasted time and resources. 他努力方向的错误导致了时间和资源的浪费。|
|近义词：misguidance, misdirection, error, fallacy|
|反义词：guidance, direction, accuracy, correctness|
|词性：名词|

misdirect:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkt/
|vt.|误导，指错方向|The guide misdirected us, and we ended up lost in the woods. 导游给我们指错了方向，结果我们在树林里迷路了。|The manager misdirected his employees, leading to confusion and delays. 经理误导了他的员工，导致了混乱和延误。|
|近义词：misguide, mislead, misinform|
|反义词：guide, direct, inform|
|词性：动词|
