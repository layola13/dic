explorement:/ɪkˈsplɔːrmənt/
n.
探索,探究,勘探
The act of exploring something.
例句：His explorement of the Amazon rainforest led to the discovery of several new species. 他对亚马逊雨林的探索导致了几个新物种的发现。

例句：The explorement of new markets is crucial for the company's growth.  对新市场的探索对公司的发展至关重要。

近义词：exploration, investigation, inquiry
反义词：neglect, ignorance
词性: 名词


词源:  From the verb "explore" + suffix "-ment" (forming nouns denoting an action or process).  Explore comes from Old French explorer, based on Latin explorare "search out, investigate," from ex- "out" + plorare "to cry out, weep, wail, implore; to explore."
