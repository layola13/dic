inter-animation:/ˌɪntərænɪˈmeɪʃən/ |n.|动画间隙；动画之间的过渡|The inter-animation was seamless, making the transition between scenes smooth and natural.|动画间的过渡天衣无缝，使得场景之间的转换流畅自然。|近义词: transition, interval|反义词: continuation|词性: 名词

Etymology:
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "in the midst of"
"animation" (noun): from Latin "animare" (to give life to), from "anima" (soul, breath)
