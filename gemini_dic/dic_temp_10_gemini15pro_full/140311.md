ogler:/ˈoʊɡlər/| n. | 盯着看的人；色迷迷地看的人 | someone who stares, especially at women, in a way that suggests sexual interest |  He's such an ogler, always staring at women. 他真是个好色之徒，总是盯着女人看。 | synonyms: lecher, gawker, starer | antonyms: none | noun

ogle:/ˈoʊɡəl/| v. | 色迷迷地看；盯着看 | to stare at someone in a way that suggests sexual interest | He kept ogling the waitress. 他一直色迷迷地盯着女服务员看。 | synonyms: leer at, gawk at, stare at | antonyms: ignore, avert one's eyes | verb
