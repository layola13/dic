de-powering:/diːˈpaʊərɪŋ/
de-powering|v.|使失去权力，剥夺权力|The process of taking away power or authority from someone or something.  It can be literal, like taking away political power, or metaphorical, like reducing someone's confidence.|The new regulations are de-powering local governments. 新规定正在削弱地方政府的权力。|近义词:weakening, disempowering, undermining|反义词:empowering, strengthening|词性:动词

de-powering:/diːˈpaʊərɪŋ/
de-powering|adj.|使人失去权力的，削弱权力的|Describing something that takes away power or authority.|The de-powering effects of the legislation were felt immediately.  这项立法的削弱权力的影响立即显现出来。|近义词:weakening, disempowering|反义词:empowering, strengthening|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the prefix "de-" (meaning "remove, reduce, reverse") + "powering" (from "power" + "-ing").  The prefix "de-" has Latin origins. "Power" comes from Old French "pouvoir."
