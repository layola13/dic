nonignitible:/ˌnɑːnɪɡˈnaɪtəbl/
adjective
不可燃的
Not capable of being ignited; nonflammable.
This material is nonignitible and therefore safe to use near heat sources.  这种材料不可燃，因此可在热源附近安全使用。
The firefighters were relieved to find that the building materials were nonignitible. 消防队员发现建筑材料不可燃，感到松了一口气。
近义词:nonflammable, fireproof, incombustible
反义词:flammable, combustible, ignitable
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + ignitable.  来自non-（不） + ignitable（可燃的）。 Ignitable comes from ignite (点燃), which ultimately derives from the Latin ignis (fire).
