combating:/kəmˈbætɪŋ/ /kəmˈbɑːtɪŋ/
verb, present participle
打击；与…斗争
The act of fighting against something or struggling with it.
They are combating human trafficking. 他们正在打击人口贩卖。
The government is combating inflation. 政府正在努力控制通货膨胀。

synonyms: fighting, opposing, resisting, struggling against, tackling
antonyms: supporting, aiding, assisting, promoting, encouraging
词性: verb (present participle)
