intermundane: /ˌɪntərˈmʌndeɪn/
adjective
现世的，尘世的；存在于世界之间的
Existing or occurring between worlds or within the world; worldly.
The intermundane affairs of state kept the king busy. 国王的现世政务让他忙得不可开交。
Near Synonyms: earthly, worldly, temporal, mundane
Antonyms: unearthly, otherworldly, spiritual, celestial
词性: adjective
