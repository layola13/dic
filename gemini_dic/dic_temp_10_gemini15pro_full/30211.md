cellared:/ˈsɛlərd/| adj.|窖藏的，放在地窖中的|Stored in a cellar.  The wine is cellared for five years before being sold. 这葡萄酒在地窖中窖藏五年才出售。|例句：The cellared wine had a rich, complex flavor.  窖藏的葡萄酒味道浓郁而复杂。

例句：We found some old photographs cellared in the attic. 我们在阁楼里发现了一些放在那里的旧照片。|近义词：stored, underground|反义词：aboveground|词性：形容词

词源: From cellar (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).  "Cellar" comes from Old French "celier," ultimately from Latin "cellarium" (storehouse).
