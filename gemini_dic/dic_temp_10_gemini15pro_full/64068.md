epitrochlea:/ˌɛpɪˈtrɒkliə/ | n. | 内上髁 |  A small bony prominence on the inner aspect of the lower extremity of the humerus.  It serves as an attachment point for several muscles of the forearm. |  Pain in the epitrochlea can be a sign of golfer's elbow.  内上髁疼痛可能是高尔夫球肘的征兆。 | Synonym:  medial epicondyle | Antonym:  lateral epicondyle | noun
