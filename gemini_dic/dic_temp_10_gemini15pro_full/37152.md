come-to-naught:/kʌm tə ˈnɔːt/
verb phrase
化为乌有；失败；落空
to fail to achieve a desired result; to become worthless or ineffective
All his hard work came to naught when the project was cancelled. 他所有的辛勤工作都白费了，因为项目被取消了。
synonyms: fail, fall through, come to nothing, amount to nothing, end in failure
antonyms: succeed, prevail, triumph
