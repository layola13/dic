conspansion:/kənˈspænʃən/ | n. | 共同扩张; 共同膨胀 |  A simultaneous expansion or widening, often implying a shared growth or development.  It's a blend of "conspire" (to act together) and "expansion". | The conspansion of the two companies led to a significant increase in market share. 两家公司的共同扩张导致市场份额显著增加。 |  synergy, collaboration, convergence, growth | contraction, decline | noun
