operettas:/ˌɒpəˈrɛtəz/ /ˌɑːpəˈrɛtəz/| n. |轻歌剧（复数）|A short amusing opera with spoken dialogue.|例句：The theater company is known for its productions of Gilbert and Sullivan operettas.  这家剧团以制作吉尔伯特和萨利文的轻歌剧而闻名。

例句：She has a large collection of vintage operettas on vinyl. 她收藏了大量的黑胶唱片版本的轻歌剧。|近义词：musical comedies, light operas|反义词:tragedies, dramas|词性:noun
