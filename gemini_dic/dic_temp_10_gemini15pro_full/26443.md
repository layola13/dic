byroad:/ˈbaɪˌroʊd/ | n. | 迂回路线 | A roundabout or less direct route. |  He took a byroad to avoid the traffic. 他绕道而行以避开交通拥堵。| synonym: detour, indirect route | antonym: direct route | noun
