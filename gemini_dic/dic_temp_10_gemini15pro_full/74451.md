frangulic: /frænˈɡjuːlɪk/
adjective
frangulic acid的;鼠李酸的
Relating to or derived from frangula, a genus of shrubs including the alder buckthorn, or to frangulic acid.
Frangulic acid is derived from the bark of several species of the genus Rhamnus. 鼠李酸是从几种鼠李属植物的树皮中提取的。
The frangulic laxative effect is mild and predictable. 鼠李的泻药作用温和且可预测。
近义词: purgative, laxative
反义词: astringent, constipating
词性: adjective
