louvre:/ˈluːvər/ (US), /ˈluːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|百叶窗; 散热孔; 天窗|A set of angled slats in a door, window, or other opening, used to regulate airflow or light; a roof lantern or turret.|The louvres were adjusted to block the afternoon sun. 百叶窗被调整以遮挡下午的阳光。|Synonyms: shutter, blind, vent, grille|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider: solid wall, sealed opening)|noun


louvre:/ˈluːvər/ (US), /ˈluːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|罗浮宫|A famous art museum in Paris, France, housed in the former royal palace.|We spent hours admiring the masterpieces in the Louvre. 我们花了几个小时欣赏罗浮宫的杰作。|Synonyms: (No direct synonym for the building itself)|Antonyms: (Not applicable)|noun
