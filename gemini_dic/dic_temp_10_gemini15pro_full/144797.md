over-focused:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəst/ | adj. | 过于专注的，过度集中的 | Concentrated on one thing to an excessive degree, often to the detriment of other important things.  | He was so over-focused on the details that he missed the bigger picture. 他过于专注于细节，反而忽略了全局。 |  She became over-focused on her career and neglected her family. 她变得过度专注于事业，而忽略了家庭。 | 近义词: obsessed, fixated, preoccupied | 反义词: distracted, inattentive, unfocused | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "focused" (past participle of "focus," from Latin "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "focused," indicating an excessive degree of concentration.
