named:/neɪmd/| adj.|被命名的；指定的；有名的|Having a specified name; called.|例句：They have a daughter named Emily. 他们有一个名叫艾米莉的女儿。|例句：The award was named after a famous scientist.  这个奖项是以一位著名科学家的名字命名的。|近义词：called, designated, entitled, known as|反义词：unnamed, anonymous|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *namede*, past participle of *namen* (“to name”), from Old English *nemnan*, *namian* (“to name, call, pronounce, nominate, appoint”), from Proto-Germanic *namōjaną* (“to name”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁nómn̥* (“name”). Cognate with West Frisian *namje* (“to name”), Dutch *noemen* (“to name, call”), German *nennen* (“to name, call”), Danish *nævne* (“to name, mention”), Swedish *nämna* (“to name, mention”).
