corno:/ˈkɔːrnəʊ/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrnoʊ/ (US)| n.|角；号角；（意大利语）羊角面包|A hard, pointed, and often curved part that grows from the head of some animals; a brass musical instrument played by blowing into it; (Italian) a croissant.|He saw a deer with large cornos. 他看到一只长着大角的鹿。|The corno player hit a high note. 号角演奏者吹出了一个高音。|I had a corno for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了一个羊角面包。|近义词：horn, croissant (in the Italian context)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
