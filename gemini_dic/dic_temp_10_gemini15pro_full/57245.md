downlighter: /ˈdaʊnˌlaɪtər/ | n. | 筒灯，射灯 | A lamp fitting set into the ceiling so that the light is directed downwards. |  The downlighters in the kitchen provide good task lighting. 厨房里的筒灯提供了很好的工作照明。| synonyms: recessed light, can light | antonyms: uplighter | noun
