overaggravates:/ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪts/| v. |过分加重，过分恶化|To aggravate something excessively or unnecessarily; to make a bad situation worse by exaggerating its importance or severity.|His constant complaining only overaggravates the situation. 他不停的抱怨只会让情况更糟。|The media overaggravated the crisis, causing widespread panic. 媒体过分夸大了危机，引起了广泛的恐慌。|近义词：exacerbate, worsen, inflame|反义词: alleviate, improve, mitigate|词性:动词

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "aggravate" (from Latin aggravare, "to make heavier").  The prefix "ag-" intensifies the meaning of "gravare" (to burden).
