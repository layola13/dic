imbower:/ɪmˈbaʊər/| v. |使…深藏于树丛中；荫蔽；|To enclose or shelter, as in a bower; to surround with foliage.|The cottage was imbowered in roses. 小屋被玫瑰环绕。|近义词：enclose, shelter, surround, envelop|反义词：expose|词性:动词

Etymology:
From im- (variant of en-, meaning "in" or "into") + bower ("a shady place under trees or climbing plants").  The word ultimately derives from the Old English *būr, meaning "dwelling."
