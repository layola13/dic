non-conjunction:/nɒn-kənˈdʒʌŋkʃən/| n.|非合取, 非连词|A word or phrase that does not function as a conjunction, meaning it does not connect words, phrases, or clauses. It can also refer to a situation where expected conjunction is absent.|The use of "but" in that sentence is a non-conjunction, it should be "and".|近义词:non-connective|反义词:conjunction|词性:noun
