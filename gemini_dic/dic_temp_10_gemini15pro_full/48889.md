defraudation:/dɪˌfrɔːˈdeɪʃn/
n.
欺诈，诈骗
The act of illegally obtaining money or goods from someone by deception.
He was found guilty of defrauding investors of millions of dollars. 他被判犯有诈骗投资者数百万美元的罪行。
The company suffered huge losses due to employee defradation.  由于员工欺诈，公司遭受了巨额损失。

近义词: fraud, swindle, deceit, deception
反义词: honesty, integrity

词性: noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French defraudacion, from Latin defraudatio(n-), from the verb defraudare (see defraud).
