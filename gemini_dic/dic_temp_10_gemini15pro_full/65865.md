evokers:/ɪˈvoʊkərz/| n.|唤起者，召唤者|People or things that evoke a feeling, memory, or image.  Those who call forth or summon something, often spirits or magical entities.|The evokers chanted ancient words, hoping to summon a powerful spirit. 召唤者吟唱着古老的咒语，希望能召唤出一个强大的灵魂。|近义词：summoners, conjurers, callers|反义词: banishers, dispellers|noun
