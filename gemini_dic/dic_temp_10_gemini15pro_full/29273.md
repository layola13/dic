cassumunar:/ˌkæsəˈmuːnɑːr/ | n. |卡萨姆纳姜|A type of ginger (Zingiber cassumunar) native to Southeast Asia, used in traditional medicine and as a spice.|The cassumunar plant is known for its strong aroma. 卡萨姆纳姜植物以其浓郁的香气而闻名。| Cassumunar is a key ingredient in some Thai curries.  卡萨姆纳姜是某些泰国咖喱的关键成分。|Synonyms:  plai (Thai),  pungent ginger|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider mild spices like parsley or dill as contrasting flavors)|Noun
