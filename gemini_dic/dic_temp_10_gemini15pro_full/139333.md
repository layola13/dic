obovate:/ˈoʊboʊveɪt/
adjective
倒卵形的
Shaped like an egg with the narrow end at the base.
The obovate leaves of the plant are a distinctive feature.  这种植物的倒卵形叶子是一个显著特征。
The artist painted an obovate fruit in the still life.  艺术家在静物画中画了一个倒卵形的水果。

近义词:oval, ovate, egg-shaped
反义词:lanceolate, linear


词性:adjective

词源:From Latin *ōbovātus, from ōvum (“egg”) + -ātus
