N/A -  "Mosaically" is an adverb and doesn't typically form set phrases.  It describes *how* something is done, rather than forming a standalone unit of meaning with other words.


词源：From mosaic + -ally.  "Mosaic" itself comes from French *mosaïque*, ultimately from Late Latin *mosaicus*, "relating to the Muses."  This likely refers to the intricate and artistic nature of mosaics, fitting for works inspired by the Muses.
