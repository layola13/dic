mewl:/mjul/ | vi. | (猫)喵喵叫；(婴儿)发出微弱的哭声；哀鸣；抱怨 | to cry feebly, like a kitten; to complain weakly | The kitten mewled for its mother. 小猫喵喵地叫着找妈妈。 | 近义词: whimper, whine, cry | 反义词: roar, shout | verb

mewl:/mjul/ | n. | 喵喵叫声；微弱的哭声 | the feeble cry of a kitten or baby | The baby's mewl woke her up. 婴儿微弱的哭声把她吵醒了。 | 近义词: whimper, whine, cry | 反义词: roar, shout | noun
