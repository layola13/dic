nonpracticabilities:/ˌnɒnpræktɪkəˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
不可行性；不实用性
The contract was terminated due to unforeseen nonpracticabilities.  由于不可预见的不可行性，合同终止了。
The nonpracticabilities of the proposed solution led to its rejection.  提议的解决方案的不可行性导致它被拒绝。
Synonyms: impossibilities, infeasibilities, unworkabilities
Antonyms: feasibilities, possibilities, workabilities
