cachemia: /kəˈkiːmiə/
n.
恶病质，体液不调
A condition of general ill health and malnutrition marked by weakness and emaciation, usually associated with a serious disease such as cancer.
Cachemia is a debilitating condition that can significantly impact a patient's quality of life.  恶病质是一种使人衰弱的疾病，会严重影响患者的生活质量。
The doctor explained that the patient's cachemia was a result of the advanced cancer. 医生解释说，病人的恶病质是晚期癌症的结果。
近义词: cachexy, emaciation, wasting, inanition
反义词: robustness, vigor, health
词性: noun
