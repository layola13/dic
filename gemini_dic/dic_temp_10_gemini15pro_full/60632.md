eleatic:/ˌɛliˈætɪk/ | adj. n. | 伊利亚学派的，伊利亚学派的人 | Relating to Elea, a Greek colony in southern Italy, or to the pre-Socratic school of philosophy founded there, especially the doctrines of Parmenides and Zeno.  A member of the Eleatic school of philosophy. |  例句：Eleatic philosophy emphasizes the unity and immutability of being. 伊利亚哲学强调存在的统一性和不变性。

例句：Zeno's paradoxes are a classic example of Eleatic thought. 芝诺的悖论是伊利亚思想的典型例子。 | 近义词：Parmenidean, Monistic | 反义词：Pluralistic, Heraclitean | 词性: adjective, noun
