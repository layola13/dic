misasserts:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrts/
|v.|错误断言，虚报，不如实陈述|To state or declare incorrectly or falsely. |He misasserts the facts of the case. 他歪曲了案件的事实。|近义词：misrepresents, falsifies, distorts|反义词：asserts correctly, states truthfully, represents accurately|词性：动词
