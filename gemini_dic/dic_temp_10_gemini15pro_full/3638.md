agential:/eɪˈdʒɛnʃəl/
adjective
代理的；作用者的；施动者的

例句：The agential role of humans in climate change is undeniable.  人类在气候变化中的作用是不可否认的。

例句：Her agential capacities were limited by her illness.  她的疾病限制了她的行动能力。

近义词：active, operative, effective, causal
反义词：passive, inactive, inert
词性: adjective
