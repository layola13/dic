indiscipline:/ɪnˈdɪsəplɪn/| n.|缺乏纪律，不守纪律|Lack of discipline; uncontrolled behavior.|The teacher was frustrated by the students' indiscipline in the classroom. 老师对学生在课堂上的不守纪律感到沮丧。|近义词：disorder, misbehavior, disobedience, unruliness|反义词: discipline, order, obedience|词性:名词

indisciplined:/ɪnˈdɪsəplɪnd/| adj.|无纪律的，不守纪律的|Lacking in discipline; uncontrolled.|The coach benched the indisciplined player for arguing with the referee. 教练让不守纪律的球员坐冷板凳，因为他与裁判争吵。|近义词：disorderly, unruly, uncontrolled, wild|反义词: disciplined, orderly, controlled, well-behaved|词性:形容词

词源:  From in- "not, opposite of" + discipline.  Ultimately from Latin *discipulus "pupil," from discere "to learn."
