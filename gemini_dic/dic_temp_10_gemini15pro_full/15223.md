backend:/ˈbækɛnd/ | n. adj. | 后端，后台 |  The part of a computer system or application that is not directly accessible to the user, typically responsible for data storage, processing, and security. |  Our backend systems need an upgrade to handle the increased traffic. 我们的后端系统需要升级以处理增加的流量。| synonyms: server-side, back-office | antonyms: frontend, client-side | noun

backend:/ˈbækɛnd/ | adj. | 后端的，后台的 | Relating to or involving the backend of a computer system or application. | We're looking for a backend developer with experience in Java. 我们正在寻找一位有 Java 经验的后端开发人员。 | synonyms: server-side, back-office | antonyms: frontend, client-side | adjective
