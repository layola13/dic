paleography:/pælɪˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 古文字学；古文书法 | the study of ancient writing systems and the deciphering of historical manuscripts. | Example: Paleography helps us understand the evolution of writing.  古文字学帮助我们理解文字的演变。| Example: He specialized in the paleography of medieval manuscripts. 他专门研究中世纪手稿的古文字学。| Synonym: epigraphy | Antonym: N/A | Noun
