disexercise:/ˌdɪsˈɛksərsaɪz/
verb
取消锻炼; 停止锻炼
To stop exercising or training.
She disexercised her vocal cords after the surgery.  手术后，她停止了声带的锻炼。
Athletes often disexercise during the off-season.  运动员在休赛期经常停止训练。
近义词: detrain, lay off
反义词: exercise, train
词性: verb
