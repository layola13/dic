buffing:/ˈbʌfɪŋ/ | n. |抛光，磨光| The act of polishing or shining something, typically with a soft cloth or brush. | He spent hours buffing his car to a high sheen. 他花了几个小时把他的车擦得锃亮。|近义词：polishing, shining, burnishing |反义词:dulling, scratching |词性:名词

buffing:/ˈbʌfɪŋ/ | v. |抛光，磨光| To polish or shine something, typically with a soft cloth or brush. |  I need to buff these shoes before I wear them. 我需要把这些鞋擦亮再穿。|近义词：polish, shine, burnish |反义词:dull, scratch |词性:动词

词源: From buff (n.), from Old French bufle “wild ox,” referring to the soft leather made from its hide, used for polishing.
