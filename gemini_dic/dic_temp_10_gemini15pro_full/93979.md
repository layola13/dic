impacting:/ɪmˈpæktɪŋ/
verb
冲击，撞击; 产生影响
having a strong effect on someone or something.
The meteor impacting the earth caused widespread devastation.  陨石撞击地球造成了大范围的破坏。

The new policy is impacting businesses both large and small. 新政策对大小企业都产生了影响。

近义词: affecting, influencing, hitting, striking
反义词: missing, avoiding

词性: v. (动词现在分词)
词源: From impact (n.) + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin impāctus, past participle of impingere "to push, drive, or dash against."
