areosystyle:/ˌɛəriˈɒstɪˌstaɪl/ | adj. | 空气柱式的，具有空气柱风格的 | Characterized by columns that are widely spaced, allowing for air circulation. This term is specifically related to architectural styles, particularly in ancient Greek temples. |  The architect designed an areosystyle temple to take advantage of the cool breezes. 建筑师设计了一座空气柱式的寺庙，以利用凉爽的微风。| Near synonyms:  open, airy, spacious (in architectural context). | Antonyms:  cramped, closed, dense (in architectural context). | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀερόστυλος* (aerostylos), from *ἀήρ* (aēr, “air”) + *στῦλος* (stylos, “column”).
