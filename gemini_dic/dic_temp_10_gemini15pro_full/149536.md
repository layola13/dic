page-through:/peɪdʒ θruː/
verb
翻阅，浏览
to turn the pages of a book, magazine, etc., quickly, looking at them briefly
She page-through the magazine, looking for the recipe. 她快速翻阅杂志，寻找食谱。
skim, browse, flip through
pore over, scrutinize
v.
