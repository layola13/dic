ill-advised:/ˌɪl ədˈvaɪzd/
adjective
不明智的，欠考虑的
not wise, and likely to cause problems
例句：
Going out in this weather without a coat was ill-advised.
在这样的天气里不穿外套出去是不明智的。

It was ill-advised of him to invest all his money in one scheme.
他把所有的钱都投资在一个项目上是很不明智的。

近义词：unwise, imprudent, foolish, injudicious
反义词：wise, prudent, sensible, judicious
词性:adjective
