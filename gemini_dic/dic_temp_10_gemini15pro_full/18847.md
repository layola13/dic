bescumber:/bɪˈskʌmbər/
verb
阻碍，妨碍；拖累
To burden or weigh down; to encumber.
The travelers were bescumbered by their heavy backpacks. 旅行者被沉重的背包拖累了。
近义词:encumber, burden, hamper, hinder
反义词:aid, assist, help
词性:v.

bescumber:/bɪˈskʌmbər/
verb
（精神上）使困扰，使焦虑
To cause mental or emotional distress to.
He was bescumbered by worries about his family. 他被对家人的担忧所困扰。
近义词:trouble, worry, distress
反义词:comfort, soothe
词性:v.
