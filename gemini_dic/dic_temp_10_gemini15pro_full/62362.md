endingless:/ˈɛndɪŋlɪs/| adj.|无止境的，无尽的|Having no end; continuing indefinitely.|The endingless cycle of violence must stop. 无休止的暴力循环必须停止。|近义词:interminable, perpetual, ceaseless, incessant|反义词:finite, limited, temporary|词性:形容词
词源: "end" + "-ing" + "-less". "End" comes from Old English "ende".  "-ing" forms a present participle, often used to create adjectives describing a continuing action or state. "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking".
