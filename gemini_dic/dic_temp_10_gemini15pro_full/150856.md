paragrammatist:/ˈpærəˈɡræmətɪst/
n.
玩文字游戏的人; 善用双关语的人
A person who makes paragrams, especially one who does so skillfully and habitually.
Oscar Wilde was a brilliant paragrammatist, twisting words to create witty and surprising effects. 奥斯卡·王尔德是一位杰出的文字游戏大师，他巧妙地运用文字，创造出机智而令人惊讶的效果。

He entertained his friends with clever paragrams. 他用巧妙的双关语娱乐他的朋友们。

近义词:punster, wordplayer
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "literalist")

词性:noun
