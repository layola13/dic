inactivity:/ˌɪnækˈtɪvɪti/| n.|不活动; 闲散; 静止|The long period of inactivity weakened his muscles. 长时间的缺乏活动使他的肌肉变得虚弱。|例句：The volcano’s inactivity lasted for 20 years. 这座火山静止了20年。

例句：Her inactivity after the surgery slowed her recovery. 手术后她缺乏活动，延缓了康复。|近义词：idleness, dormancy, quiescence, stagnation|反义词：activity, action, movement|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + activity.  The word "activity" ultimately derives from Latin "actus" (a doing, a driving, an impulse).
