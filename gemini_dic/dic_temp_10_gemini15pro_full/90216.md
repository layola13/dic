houndy:/ˈhaʊndi/| adj.|像猎犬的，猎犬似的；卑鄙的，恶劣的|Having the characteristics of a hound; resembling a hound in behavior, especially in being tenacious or relentless; also, mean or unpleasant. |例句：He had a houndy look in his eyes as he pursued his target.  他追捕目标时，眼神里透着猎犬般的凶狠。

例句：That was a houndy trick to play on your friend.  你对你的朋友耍了个卑鄙的伎俩。|近义词：dogged, relentless, mean, nasty|反义词：kind, generous, pleasant|词性:形容词
