danaite:/ˈdeɪnaɪt/| n. |砷钴矿|A mineral, cobalt arsenide (CoAs), part of the cobaltite group. It is steel-gray to silver-white in color and occurs as granular to compact masses.|Danaite is a relatively rare mineral found in hydrothermal veins.|近义词:cobaltite, skutterudite|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性:noun
