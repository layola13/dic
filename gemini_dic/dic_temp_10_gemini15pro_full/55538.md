distributive:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪv/ | adj. | 分配的，分布的，散布的 | Relating to or denoting the distribution of resources or goods among a group or area;  characterized by or producing distribution. | The government implemented a distributive policy to ensure that all citizens had access to basic necessities. 政府实施了一项分配政策，以确保所有公民都能获得基本生活必需品。|  The company has a distributive network that spans across the entire country. 该公司的分销网络遍布全国。 | Synonyms: allocative, apportioning, dispensing | Antonyms: collective, cumulative, concentrated | adjective
