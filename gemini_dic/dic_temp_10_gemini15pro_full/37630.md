communities:/kəˈmjuːnətiz/| n.|社区，社群，群体|A group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common.|例句：The local communities welcomed the new park. 当地社区欢迎新公园的建成。|近义词：societies, groups, neighborhoods|反义词:individuals, isolation|Noun

communities:/kəˈmjuːnətiz/| n.|共有，共同体|a feeling of fellowship with others, as a result of sharing common attitudes, interests, and goals.|例句：There is a strong sense of community in this town.  这个小镇有很强的社区意识。|近义词：togetherness, solidarity, fellowship|反义词:division, discord|Noun
