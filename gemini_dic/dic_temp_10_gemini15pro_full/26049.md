bushhammer:/ˈbʊʃˌhæmər/| n. | Bush hammer; 石匠锤 |A heavy hammer with a rectangular head, one face of which is serrated or has many small pyramidal points, used for dressing stone.| The mason used a bushhammer to texture the granite surface. 石匠用石匠锤给花岗岩表面做了纹理。|Synonyms: mason's hammer, stone hammer, texturing hammer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
bushhammer:/ˈbʊʃˌhæmər/| v. | Bush hammer; 用石匠锤加工|To dress (stone) with a bushhammer.| They bushhammered the concrete to give it a rough finish. 他们用石匠锤加工混凝土，使其表面粗糙。|Synonyms: texture, dress, roughen|Antonyms: smooth, polish|verb
