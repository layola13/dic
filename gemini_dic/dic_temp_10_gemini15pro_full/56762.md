doozying:/ˈduːziɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人眼花缭乱的，令人晕眩的 | Extremely impressive, remarkable, or exciting, often in a way that is overwhelming or disorienting.  | The roller coaster ride was a doozying experience. 过山车是一次令人晕眩的经历。 |  The special effects in the movie were doozying.  这部电影的特效令人眼花缭乱。 | 近义词：amazing, stunning, breathtaking, overwhelming, intense | 反义词：boring, mundane, underwhelming, calming | 词性:形容词

词源: 源于名词"doozy,"  指"极好的东西"，可能源于20世纪早期的汽车品牌Duesenberg，以其高性能和奢华而闻名。
