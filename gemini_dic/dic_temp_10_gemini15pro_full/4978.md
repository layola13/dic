alkalizate: /ˈælkəlaɪˌzeɪt/
verb
碱化；使成碱性
to make (something) alkaline
例句：The process alkalizates the soil, making it more suitable for certain crops.  这个过程碱化了土壤，使其更适合某些作物。

例句：They alkalizated the solution by adding a base.  他们通过添加碱来碱化溶液。
近义词：neutralize, alkalify, basify
反义词：acidify, acidulate
词性：v.
