| |The young deer outleaped the fence with ease.  小鹿轻松地跳过了篱笆。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip|反义词：lag, trail, fall behind|词性：动词


词源：
"outleap" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "beyond" or "exceeding") and the verb "leap" (meaning "to jump").  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into its present participle form, "outleaping."
