dabble:/ˈdæbəl/| v.|涉猎，浅尝|engages in a superficial way, typically as a hobby or pastime|She dabbles in a bit of everything but doesn't really excel at anything.|近义词：tinkers, experiments, toys, dilettantes|反义词：specializes, masters, immerses|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *dabbelen, frequentative of dabben (“to strike, hit lightly”), from Proto-Germanic *dabjaną (“to dab, strike lightly”).  Related to Old English dabban (“to strike”).
