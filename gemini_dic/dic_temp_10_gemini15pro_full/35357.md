co-heritor:/ˌkoʊɪnˈherɪtər/ | n. |共同继承人|A person who inherits property jointly with one or more other people.|例句：She and her brother are co-heritors of their father's estate. 她和她哥哥是父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：The co-heritors disagreed on how to divide the property. 共同继承人在如何分割财产上存在分歧。

近义词：joint heir, fellow heir|反义词:|词性:noun
