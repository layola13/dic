damodar:/ˈdɑːmoʊdɑːr/| n.|达莫达尔河，黑天|A river in eastern India; also, a name of Krishna, meaning "one who has a rope around his belly."  It refers to an incident where Krishna was tied to a mortar by his foster mother, Yashoda.|Example: The Damodar River flows through the states of Jharkhand and West Bengal. 达莫达尔河流经贾坎德邦和西孟加拉邦。

Example:  Devotees often sing hymns praising Damodar, a form of Krishna.  信徒经常吟唱赞美黑天（克里希纳的一种形式）的赞美诗。|近义词:Krishna, dark one (as a name of Krishna)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
