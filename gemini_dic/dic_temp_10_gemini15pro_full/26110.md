busloads:/ˈbʌsloʊdz/| n.| 满载的公共汽车; 许多; 大量 |A large number or quantity of people or things, as much as a bus can carry. |Example 1: Busloads of tourists arrive in the city every day. 每天都有大量的游客涌入这座城市。

Example 2: They brought busloads of supplies to the refugees. 他们给难民带来了大量的物资。|Synonyms: crowds, lots, masses, heaps, scads |Antonyms: few, little, handful |noun
