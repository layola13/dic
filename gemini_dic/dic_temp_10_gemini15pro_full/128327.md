neuter:/ˈnjuːtər/ (US), /ˈnjuːtə(r)/ (UK)| adj. n. v. | 中性的；无性别的；阉割；中性词；变为无效 |A neuter word has no gender.  一个中性词没有性别。|The cat was neutered at six months old. 这只猫在六个月大的时候被阉割了。|近义词:neutral, asexual, castrate|反义词:masculine, feminine, sexual|词性:adjective, noun, verb
词源:From Latin *neuter, from *ne-ūter (“neither”), from ne (“not”) + ūter (“either, which of two”).
