hepatorenal:/ˌhɛpətəʊˈriːnəl/
adjective
肝肾的
Relating to the liver and the kidneys.
Hepatorenal syndrome is a serious complication of chronic liver disease. 肝肾综合征是慢性肝病的一种严重并发症。
The patient presented with hepatorenal failure.  这位病人出现肝肾衰竭。
Synonym:  hepatic-renal
Antonym: N/A
词性: adjective
