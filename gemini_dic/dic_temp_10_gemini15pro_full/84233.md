half-severed:/ˌhæf ˈsɛvəd/
|adj.|
|半切断的，部分切断的|
|Partially cut or separated, but not completely.  It implies that something is still partially attached.|
|The hiker found a half-severed rope dangling from the cliff face.|
|登山者发现一根半切断的绳子悬挂在悬崖峭壁上。|

|The butcher presented the half-severed carcass for inspection.|
|屠夫拿出半切断的屠体供检查。|

|partly severed, partially severed, incompletely severed|
|completely severed, fully severed, entirely severed|
|adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" (Old English healf) + "severed" (past participle of "sever," from Old French sevrer, ultimately from Latin separare "to separate")
