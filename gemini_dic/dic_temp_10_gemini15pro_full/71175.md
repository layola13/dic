fissural:/ˈfɪʃərəl/
adjective
裂隙的; 裂缝的
Relating to or occurring in a fissure or crack.
The fissural eruption of the volcano created a dramatic landscape. 火山的裂隙喷发创造了引人注目的景观。
The doctor identified a fissural lesion on the patient's skin. 医生在病人的皮肤上发现了一个裂隙性病灶。
近义词: cracked, cleft, split
反义词: sealed, closed
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fissūrālis, from fissūra (“fissure, cleft”) + -ālis (adjectival suffix).
