hipe:/haɪp/
n.
炒作；兴奋；大肆宣传
Intense publicity and promotion, typically of a product or idea.
The marketing team created a lot of hype around the new product launch.
营销团队为新产品的发布制造了很多宣传。

近义词:buzz, publicity, promotion, ballyhoo
反义词:understatement, downplaying

词性:名词

词源: Possibly from hyperbole, meaning exaggeration.  The exact origin is uncertain.
