formatter:/ˈfɔːrmætər/ | n. | 格式化程序，格式器 | A program or device that arranges data into a specified format. |  This formatter can convert text files to different output formats.  这个格式化程序可以将文本文件转换为不同的输出格式。 | synonym: styler, organizer | antonym: disorganizer, scrambler | noun

formatter:/ˈfɔːrmætər/ | v. | 格式化 | To arrange something according to a fixed style or design. | He formatted the hard drive before installing the new operating system. 他在安装新的操作系统之前格式化了硬盘。 | synonym: arrange, style, organize | antonym: disorganize, scramble | verb
