confidentness:/ˈkɒnfɪdəntnəs/ (US) /ˈkɑːnfɪdəntnəs/| n. |自信；把握|The state or quality of being confident; assurance.|She spoke with great confidentness about her ability to do the job. 她非常自信地谈论她完成这项工作的能力。|近义词：confidence, assurance, self-assurance, certitude|反义词：diffidence, insecurity, self-doubt|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From confident + -ness.  "Confident" comes from the Latin "confidentem" (nominative confidens), present participle of "confidere" "to trust firmly," from "com-" (together, with) + "fidere" (to trust).  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
