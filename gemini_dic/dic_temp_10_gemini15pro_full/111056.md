/lɪˈstiːz/ | n. | 登记人；注册者 | People who have registered or enrolled for something. |  The listees for the conference will receive a confirmation email.  会议的注册者将收到一封确认邮件。| synonyms: registrants, enrollees | antonyms: unregistered, unenrolled | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "list" + "-ee" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who is the object or recipient of the action of the verb).  The verb "list" ultimately comes from the Old French "liste," meaning "strip of cloth, catalog."
