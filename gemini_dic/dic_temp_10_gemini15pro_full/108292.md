laureated:/ˈlɔːrieɪtɪd/
adjective
授予桂冠的；获得荣誉学位的；享有殊荣的
crowned with laurel; having received a degree or other academic honor; distinguished or celebrated
The laureated poet read from his award-winning collection.  这位获奖诗人朗读了他的获奖作品集。
The university president congratulated the newly laureated graduates.  大学校长祝贺新毕业的获奖者。

synonyms: honored, distinguished, celebrated, acclaimed, award-winning, prize-winning, decorated
antonyms: undistinguished, unknown, obscure, uncelebrated
