aunties: /ˈæntiz/ | n. |阿姨们；姨妈们；姑妈们；伯母们；舅妈们|The plural form of "auntie," a term of affection for a woman who is not one's biological aunt but plays a similar role.  It can also refer to one's actual aunts collectively.|My aunties always give me the best birthday presents. 我的阿姨们总是送给我最好的生日礼物。|Synonyms: aunts (more formal), relatives|Antonyms: uncles|noun
