energizes:/ˈɛnərdʒaɪzɪz/
verb
使充满活力；给予…能量
Gives energy or vigor to.
The coach's speech energized the team. 教练的讲话使队员们充满了活力。
energize, invigorate, stimulate, enliven
drain, exhaust, weaken
verb
