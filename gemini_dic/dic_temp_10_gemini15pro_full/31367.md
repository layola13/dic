charbon:/ʃɑːrˈbɒn/ (UK), /ʃɑːrˈboʊn/ (US)| n. |木炭; 炭; 煤|A black or dark-brown residue consisting primarily of carbon obtained by removing water and other volatile constituents from animal and vegetation substances.|例句：The artist used charcoal to sketch the portrait. 艺术家用木炭画了肖像。

例句：They burned charcoal to keep warm in the winter. 冬天他们烧木炭取暖。
