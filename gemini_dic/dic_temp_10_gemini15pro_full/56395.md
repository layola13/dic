dolefully:/ˈdoʊlfəli/ | adv. | 悲哀地，忧郁地 | In a sad and mournful way. | She looked at him dolefully. 她悲哀地看着他。|  He shook his head dolefully. 他悲伤地摇了摇头。| 近义词: sadly, mournfully, sorrowfully | 反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From *doleful* + *-ly*.  *Doleful* comes from Middle English *doleful*, from *dole* (sorrow, grief) + *-ful*.  Ultimately from Old French *deuil* (mourning).
