frocking:/ˈfrɒkɪŋ/ | n. |  任命仪式; 穿法衣; 法衣 |  The act of ordaining someone to the Christian ministry, particularly by investing them with a frock. Also refers to the frock itself or the style of wearing it. |  The bishop's frocking was a grand ceremony. 主教的任命仪式是一场盛大的典礼。|  investiture, ordination, appointment | dismissal, removal | noun
