frowzy:/ˈfraʊzi/ | adj. | 邋遢的，不整洁的；（尤指房间）闷热的，有霉味的 | Having a stale, musty smell; unkempt or slovenly in appearance.  | The frowzy attic was filled with dusty furniture and old clothes. 闷热的阁楼里堆满了满是灰尘的家具和旧衣服。|  The frowzy old man hadn't shaved in days.  那个邋遢的老人好几天没刮胡子了。 | 近义词: musty, stuffy, untidy, slovenly, scruffy | 反义词: fresh, clean, tidy, neat | 词性: 形容词

frowzy:/ˈfraʊzi/ | adj. | （尤指女人）不整洁的，邋遢的 |  (especially of a woman) untidy and slovenly in appearance. | She looked rather frowzy after her long journey.  长途旅行后，她看起来很不整洁。| The frowzy woman shuffled down the street in her slippers. 那个邋遢的女人穿着拖鞋拖着脚走在街上。 | 近义词: unkempt, disheveled, frumpy | 反义词: neat, tidy, groomed | 词性: 形容词
