hypogeous:/ˌhaɪpəˈdʒiːəs/
adjective
地下生的;生长在地下的
Growing or living beneath the surface of the earth.
Truffles are hypogeous fungi.  块菌是地下生的真菌。
近义词: subterranean, underground
反义词: epigeous, aboveground
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὑπόγειος (hupógeios, “underground”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + γῆ (gê, “earth, land”).
