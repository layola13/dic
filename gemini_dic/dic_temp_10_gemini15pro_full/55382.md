distempered:/dɪsˈtɛmpəd/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的；失调的；患病的 |  Lacking composure; upset or disturbed; ill, especially with a disease of the respiratory system. | The distempered dog was quarantined. 这只患病的狗被隔离了。| 近义词：ill, sick, diseased, unstable, agitated, disturbed | 反义词：healthy, well, calm, composed, stable | 词性: 形容词

distempered:/dɪsˈtɛmpəd/ | adj. | (颜色)暗淡的，灰暗的 | (of a colour) not bright; dull. | The distempered walls of the old house gave it a gloomy atmosphere.  老房子灰暗的墙壁给它一种阴沉的气氛。 | 近义词：dull, drab, muted, subdued | 反义词：bright, vibrant, vivid | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Old French *destemprer, from des- (expressing reversal) + temprer ‘to temper’.
