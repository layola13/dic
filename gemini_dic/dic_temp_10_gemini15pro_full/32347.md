childishly:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃli/| adv.|幼稚地|In a manner resembling a child; in a naive, immature, or silly way.|He giggled childishly at the silly joke. 他幼稚地咯咯笑着那个愚蠢的笑话。|例句：She behaved childishly by stomping her feet. 她跺着脚，表现得很幼稚。|近义词：immaturely, foolishly, naively|反义词: maturely, responsibly, sensibly|词性:副词
childish:/ˈtʃaɪldɪʃ/ |adj.|幼稚的|Having or showing the behavior or characteristics of a child, especially in a negative way; immature.|His childish behavior embarrassed his parents. 他幼稚的行为让他的父母很尴尬。|例句：It was childish of him to refuse to share his toys. 他拒绝分享玩具，真是幼稚。|近义词：immature, puerile, infantile|反义词: mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
