forebear:/ˈfɔːrˌbɛər/ (US), /ˈfɔːˌbeə(r)/ (UK)| n.|祖先，祖辈|An ancestor, a person from whom one is descended.|My forebears came to this country from Ireland. 我的祖先来自爱尔兰。|近义词：ancestor, progenitor, antecedent|反义词：descendant, offspring|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *forbere*, from Old English *forebeara* "foregoer, ancestor," from *fore* "before" + *beara* "bearer, carrier."
