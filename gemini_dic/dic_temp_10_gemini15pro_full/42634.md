counterscientific:/ˌkaʊntərˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
反科学的；违反科学的
Opposing or contradicting science or scientific principles.
His counterscientific beliefs made it difficult for him to accept the evidence. 他反科学的信仰使他难以接受证据。
Near Synonyms:  antiscientific, pseudoscientific, unscientific
Antonyms: scientific, evidence-based, empirical
词性: 形容词
