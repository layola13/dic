babbitting:/ˈbæbɪtɪŋ/
n.
巴氏合金浇铸，巴氏合金衬垫
The process of lining or coating bearing surfaces with babbitt metal.  This creates a low-friction surface.
The babbitting of the engine's bearings was essential for smooth operation.  发动机的轴承巴氏合金浇铸对于平稳运行至关重要。
lining, bearing
n/a
noun

babbitt:/ˈbæbɪt/
n.
巴氏合金
A soft, white, non-ferrous alloy of tin, copper, and antimony used for bearing linings.
The babbitt in the bearing had worn down, causing a knocking sound.  轴承中的巴氏合金磨损了，导致发出敲击声。
alloy, bearing metal
n/a
noun
