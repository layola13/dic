deduce:/dɪˈduːs/ /dɪˈdjuːs/
verb
推断，演绎
To reach a conclusion from something known or assumed; infer.
The police deduced that the thief was left-handed. 警方推断窃贼是左撇子。
infer, conclude, reason, gather
surmise, conjecture, guess
v.
