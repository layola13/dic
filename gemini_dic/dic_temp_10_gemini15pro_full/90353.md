hovelling:/ˈhʌvəlɪŋ/ | n. |盘旋，徘徊；(尤指鸟类)翱翔；守候；(尤指为了攻击或乞讨而)逗留|The act of hovering, especially of a bird in flight, or of waiting or lingering, often with the intent to attack or beg.|例句：The hawk was hovelling above the field, searching for prey. 老鹰在田野上空盘旋，寻找猎物。

例句：He was hovelling near the buffet, waiting for the fresh food to be brought out. 他在自助餐附近徘徊，等着新食物被端出来。|近义词：hovering, lingering, waiting, dawdling|反义词：departing, leaving, going|词性:名词(动名词)


hovelling:/ˈhʌvəlɪŋ/ | adj. |盘旋的，翱翔的；守候的；逗留的|Describing the act of hovering, especially of a bird in flight, or of waiting or lingering, often with the intent to attack or beg.|例句：The hovelling helicopter provided an aerial view of the traffic jam. 盘旋的直升机提供了交通堵塞的鸟瞰图。

例句：A hovelling presence made her feel uneasy. 一个徘徊不去的身影让她感到不安。|近义词：hovering, lingering, waiting, dawdling|反义词：departing, leaving, going|词性:形容词


词源:  From the verb "hover," which comes from the Old English *hoforian, meaning "to hang suspended."
