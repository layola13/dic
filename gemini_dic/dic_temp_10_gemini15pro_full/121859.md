misrepresenting:/ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛntɪŋ/
|v.|歪曲；不如实地叙述；|present participle of misrepresent|He was accused of misrepresenting the facts. 他被指控歪曲事实。|Synonyms: distorting, falsifying, misconstruing|Antonyms: portraying accurately, representing truthfully|Verb

misrepresent:/ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛnt/
|vt.|歪曲；不如实地叙述；|to give a false or misleading account of something or someone|The advertisement misrepresents the product's capabilities.  这则广告歪曲了产品的功能。|Synonyms: distort, falsify, misstate|Antonyms: portray accurately, represent truthfully|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + represent (to present).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English, while "represent" comes from Old French representer, ultimately from Latin repraesentare.
