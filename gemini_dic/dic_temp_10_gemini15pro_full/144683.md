outyielding:/ˌaʊtˈjiːldɪŋ/
adjective
产量更高的，胜过的
Producing a greater yield than something else.
This new strain of wheat is outyielding all other varieties in the region. 这种新的小麦品种比该地区所有其他品种的产量都高。
Synonyms:  more productive, higher-yielding, surpassing
Antonyms: less productive, lower-yielding, underperforming
词性: 形容词
