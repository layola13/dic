bestows:/bɪˈstoʊz/
verb
授予，给予
confers or presents (an honor, right, or gift).
The queen bestowed a knighthood on him. 女王授予他骑士爵位。
synonyms: confer, grant, award, present, give, endow, donate, vouchsafe
antonyms: withhold, deny, refuse, revoke, withdraw
词性：动词
