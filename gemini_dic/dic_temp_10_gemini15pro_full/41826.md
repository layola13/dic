coruscating: adj.
coruscating: 闪耀的，闪烁的
coruscating:  Flashing brilliantly; sparkling.  It describes something that emits intermittent flashes of light, often in a dazzling or striking way.  It can also be used figuratively to describe brilliant or witty language.
coruscating: Her coruscating diamond necklace caught everyone's eye. 她的闪耀的钻石项链吸引了所有人的目光。

coruscating: The author's coruscating wit made the novel a delight to read. 作者闪耀的才智使这部小说读起来令人愉悦。

coruscating: 近义词: sparkling, glittering, shimmering, flashing, dazzling, brilliant, scintillating
coruscating: 反义词: dull, lackluster, dim, matte
