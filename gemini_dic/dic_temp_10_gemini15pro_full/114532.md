malamide:/ˈmæləmaɪd/ | n. | 马来酰胺 |A crystalline diamide of maleic acid, used especially as a stabilizer in nylon and other polymers.  It is also known as 2,5-pyrrolidinedione. |  Malamide is added to nylon to improve its heat stability. 马来酰胺被添加到尼龙中以提高其热稳定性。|Synonyms: 2,5-pyrrolidinedione | Antonyms:  (No commonly used direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | noun
