furthers:/ˈfɜːrðərz/| v.| 促进，推动，增进|To help the progress or development of something; to promote something. |例句：The new trade agreement furthers economic cooperation between the two countries.  新的贸易协定促进了两国之间的经济合作。

例句：She took a second job to further her career goals. 她为了进一步实现自己的职业目标而找了第二份工作。|近义词：promotes, advances, encourages, fosters, supports|反义词：hinders, obstructs, impedes, blocks|词性:动词


furthers:/ˈfɜːrðərz/| n.| 更多的，进一步的 (通常用复数形式)|Additional things; something more or extra. |例句：We need to discuss this matter at further length. 我们需要进一步地讨论这件事。

例句：Are there any further questions? 还有其他问题吗？|近义词：additional, more, extra, supplementary|反义词：less, fewer|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *fertheren, from Old English fyrðran, from furðor (“further”).
