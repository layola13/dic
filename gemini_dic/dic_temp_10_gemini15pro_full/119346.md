microchip:/ˈmaɪkroʊtʃɪp/| n. |微芯片，集成电路|A tiny wafer of semiconductor material, typically silicon, on which an integrated circuit is etched. |例句：The microchip revolutionized the electronics industry. 微芯片彻底改变了电子工业。

例句：Modern computers contain millions of microchips. 现代计算机包含数百万个微芯片。|近义词：integrated circuit, chip, semiconductor|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "discrete component")|词性:noun
