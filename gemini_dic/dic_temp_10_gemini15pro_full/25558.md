bulwark: /ˈbʊlwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈbʊlwək/ (UK)| n. |壁垒，防御工事，保障|A defensive wall or embankment raised as a protection; a person, institution, or principle that acts as a defense or safeguard.|例句：


The castle wall served as a bulwark against invaders.  城堡的城墙是抵御入侵者的壁垒。


Free speech is a bulwark against tyranny. 言论自由是抵御暴政的保障。


近义词：defense, rampart, safeguard, protection, bastion |反义词: vulnerability, weakness| 词性: 名词
