multiculturalists:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪsts/ | n. | 多元文化主义者 |  A person who believes in or supports multiculturalism, the idea that different cultures can coexist peacefully and equitably in a single society. |  The multiculturalists argue that diversity enriches a society. 多元文化主义者认为，多样性丰富了社会。| Synonyms:  diversity advocates, pluralists, inclusionists | Antonyms:  nativists, xenophobes, segregationists | Noun

multiculturalist:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | adj. | 多元文化的 | Relating to or supporting multiculturalism.  | The city has a multiculturalist policy that celebrates all ethnic groups.  这座城市有一项多元文化政策，庆祝所有民族群体。| Synonyms:  diverse, pluralistic, inclusive | Antonyms:  homogeneous, monolithic, exclusionary | Adjective
