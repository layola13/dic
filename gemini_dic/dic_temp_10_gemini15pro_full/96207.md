index-linking:/ˈɪndɛks lɪŋkɪŋ/| n.|指数挂钩|The act of connecting the value of something, like wages or prices, to a price index. This is done to adjust for inflation or other economic changes.|例句：Index-linking of pensions protects retirees from the erosion of their income due to inflation. 指数挂钩的养老金制度保护退休人员的收入免受通货膨胀的影响。

例句：Index-linking of government bonds can help to reduce the risk of inflation for investors. 指数挂钩的政府债券可以帮助投资者降低通货膨胀的风险。|近义词：inflation adjustment, price indexing|反义词: fixed rate|词性:noun
