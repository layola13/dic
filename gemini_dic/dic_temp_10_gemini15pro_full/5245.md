allocators:/ˈæləˌkeɪtərz/| n.|分配器，分配者|An allocator is something or someone that allocates resources (like memory, time, or money).  It typically refers to a software component responsible for managing the allocation and deallocation of memory. |The custom allocator improved performance significantly.  自定义分配器显著提高了性能。|近义词：distributor, assigner|反义词: collector, reclaimer|noun
