nonsectionally:/ˌnɑnˈsekʃənəli/
adv.
不分地域地；不分派别地
in a way that does not relate to or affect only one section of something; in a non-sectional way.
The policy was applied nonsectionally across the entire country. 这项政策在全国范围内不分地区地实施。
近义词:nationally, uniformly, across the board
反义词:sectionally, regionally, locally
词性:副词
