civilest:/ˈsɪvɪlɪst/
adjective
最有礼貌的；最文明的
Most polite; most courteous.
She gave the civilest response possible, despite being insulted. 即使受到了侮辱，她还是尽可能做出了最有礼貌的回应。
近义词: most courteous, most polite, most respectful
反义词: rudest, most impolite, most discourteous
词性: adjective

词源:
From civil + -est (superlative suffix).  The word "civil" comes from Old French "civil," ultimately from Latin "civilis" meaning "relating to citizens, courteous," derived from "civis" meaning "citizen."
