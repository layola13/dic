co-omnipotent:/koʊˌɑːmˈnɪpətənt/
| adj. |共同全能的，共同万能的 | Sharing omnipotence; equally all-powerful. |  The two deities were considered co-omnipotent, ruling the universe together. 这两位神被认为是共同全能的，共同统治着宇宙。 |  The co-omnipotent rulers shared absolute power. 共同全能的统治者共享绝对权力。 | 近义词: all-powerful, almighty, supreme | 反义词: powerless, impotent, weak | 词性:形容词

co-omnipotent:/koʊˌɑːmˈnɪpətənt/
| n. | 共同全能者 | One who shares omnipotence with another. | In this mythology, the sun and moon are co-omnipotents. 在这个神话中，太阳和月亮是共同全能的。 |  The story features two co-omnipotents battling for supremacy.  这个故事讲述了两个共同全能者争夺霸权。 | 近义词: all-powerful being, supreme being | 反义词: mortal, powerless being | 词性:名词


词源:

"co-" (prefix) from Latin, meaning "together," "jointly," "mutually."

"omnipotent" from Late Latin "omnipotentem" (nominative omnipotens), from "omni-" (all) + "potens" (powerful), present participle of "posse" (to be able).
