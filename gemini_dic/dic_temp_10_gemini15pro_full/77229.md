gastrocolic: /ˌɡæstrəˈkɒlɪk/ /ˌɡæstroʊˈkɑːlɪk/ | adj. | 胃结肠的 | Relating to the stomach and the colon. | The gastrocolic reflex is the increased motility of the colon after food enters the stomach.  胃结肠反射是指食物进入胃后结肠蠕动增加。|  Gastrocolic fistula is a rare but serious complication. 胃结肠瘘是一种罕见但严重的并发症。 |  gastrointestinal |  None | adjective
Etymology:  From gastro- (combining form meaning "stomach") + colic (relating to the colon).  词源：来自gastro-（组合形式，意思是“胃”）+ colic（与结肠有关）。
