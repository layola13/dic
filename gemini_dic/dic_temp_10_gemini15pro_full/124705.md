mows:/moʊz/ (US) /məʊz/ (UK)| v.| 割（草）；刈（草坪等）；扫射；|He mows the lawn every Saturday. 他每个星期六都修剪草坪。|例句：
The gardener mows the lawn weekly. 园丁每周修剪一次草坪。
The machine gun mowed down the enemy soldiers. 机枪扫射了敌军士兵。


|近义词：cuts, trims, shears, reaps, |反义词:grow, cultivate|词性:verb


mow:/moʊ/ (US) /məʊ/ (UK)| n.| 割草；干草堆；皱眉；鬼脸|The farmer stored the hay in the mow. 农民把干草储存在干草堆里。|例句：

The children played in the mow. 孩子们在干草堆里玩耍。
He made a mow of disgust. 他做了个厌恶的鬼脸。

|近义词：grimace, frown|反义词:smile|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *māwan (attested only in the past participle māwen), from Proto-Germanic *mēaną (“to mow”), from Proto-Indo-European *meh₁- (“to cut, reap”). Cognate with Old Frisian māwa (“to mow”), Middle Dutch maeyen, maaien (“to mow”), German mähen (“to mow”), Old Norse meya (“to mow”).
