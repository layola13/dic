nonpreventive:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈvɛntɪv/
adjective
非预防性的
Not able to prevent something; not preventive.
The study showed that the measures taken were nonpreventive and did not reduce the incidence of the disease. 这项研究表明，所采取的措施是非预防性的，并没有降低疾病的发病率。
Near Synonyms:  non-prophylactic, remedial, therapeutic
Antonyms: preventative, prophylactic, protective
词性: adjective
