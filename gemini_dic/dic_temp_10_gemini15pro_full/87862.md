hideouts:/ˈhaɪdaʊts/| n. |藏身处，隐匿处|A place where someone hides, especially from the police or from other people who are looking for them.  A secret place; a refuge.|The police raided several hideouts used by the gang. 警察突袭了该团伙使用的几个藏身处。|近义词：hideaway, refuge, shelter, sanctuary|反义词:exposure, open area|noun
