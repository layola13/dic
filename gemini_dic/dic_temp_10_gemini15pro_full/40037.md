consuming:/kənˈsuːmɪŋ/
adjective
消耗的；强烈的
very intense
The heat was consuming.  酷热难耐。
近义词: intense, overwhelming, devouring, absorbing
反义词: mild, moderate, insignificant
词性: adjective

consuming:/kənˈsuːmɪŋ/
verb
消耗；消费；吃；喝
eat, drink, or ingest (food or drink).
He was consuming vast quantities of chips. 他吃了大量的薯片。
近义词: devour, eat, drink, ingest
反义词: produce, create, abstain
词性: verb present participle
