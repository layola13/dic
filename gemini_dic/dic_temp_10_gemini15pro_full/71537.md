flar:/flɑːr/| n.| 闪光; 照明弹; 信号弹; 耀斑|A sudden brief burst of bright flame or light. A magnesium flar is used for taking photographs in poor light or at night.|The ship sent up a flar to signal for help. 这艘船发射了一枚信号弹以寻求帮助。|Synonyms: flash, blaze, glare, burst|Antonyms: darkness, gloom|noun
