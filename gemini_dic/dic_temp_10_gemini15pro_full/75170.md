frivolity:/frɪˈvɒləti/| n. |轻浮；轻佻；无聊的事；琐事|Lack of seriousness; behaviour that is silly and not serious.|She spent her days in a whirl of parties and frivolities. 她终日沉迷于派对和无聊的琐事中。|Synonyms:  levity, flippancy, silliness, triviality, lightheartedness|Antonyms: seriousness, gravity, solemnity|noun


frivolous:/ˈfrɪvələs/| adj. |轻浮的；轻佻的；无聊的；琐碎的|Not having any serious purpose or value.|He made some frivolous remarks. 他说了一些轻佻的话。|Synonyms:  flippant, silly, trifling, trivial, superficial|Antonyms: serious, important, significant|adjective
