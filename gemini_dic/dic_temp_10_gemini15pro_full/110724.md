linerless:/ˈlaɪnərlɪs/
adj.
无衬垫的，无内衬的
Without a liner; typically used in the context of labeling.  This refers to labels that don't require a backing paper or liner.
Linerless labels are more sustainable.  无衬垫标签更具可持续性。
Linerless labels reduce waste.  无衬垫标签减少浪费。
synonym: liner-free
antonym: lined, with liner
词性: 形容词
