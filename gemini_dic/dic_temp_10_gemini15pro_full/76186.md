fussinesses:/ˈfʌsɪnəsɪz/
noun
过度讲究；挑剔；小题大做
instances of being fussy; excessive attention to minor details.
The child's fussinesses about food worried his mother.  这个孩子对食物的挑剔让他的母亲很担心。
近义词：pickiness, fastidiousness, choosiness, finickiness
反义词：easygoingness, tolerance, adaptability


fussiness:/ˈfʌsɪnəs/
noun
过度讲究；挑剔；小题大做
the quality or state of being fussy; excessive attention to minor details.
Her fussiness about the wedding arrangements was getting on everyone's nerves.  她对婚礼安排的挑剔让每个人都很紧张。
近义词：pickiness, fastidiousness, choosiness, finickiness
反义词：easygoingness, tolerance, adaptability


fussy:/ˈfʌsi/
adjective
爱挑剔的；难取悦的；过分讲究的
too concerned or worried about details or standards, especially unimportant ones.
He's a very fussy eater.  他是一个非常挑剔的食客。
近义词：picky, fastidious, choosy, finicky
反义词：easygoing, tolerant, adaptable

词源:
From fuss + -y.  来自fuss + -y。 Fuss 最早的意思是“过度的活动或努力”，可能与中古英语 fissen（快速移动）有关。
