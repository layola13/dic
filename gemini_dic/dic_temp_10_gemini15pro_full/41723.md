corrodent:/kəˈrəʊdənt/ | adj. n. | 腐蚀性的；腐蚀剂 |  (adj.) Having the power to gradually wear away or destroy, especially by chemical action. (n.) A substance that corrodes. |  Rust is a common corrodent of iron. 铁锈是铁的常见腐蚀剂。| 近义词：destructive, decaying | 反义词：inert | 词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corrodent-, present participle stem of corrodere "to gnaw to pieces," from com- "together" (see com-) + rodere "to gnaw" (from PIE root *red- "to scrape, scratch, gnaw").
