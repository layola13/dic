bodyguard:/ˈbɑːdiˌɡɑːrd/ /ˈbɒdiˌɡɑːd/| n.|保镖|a person employed to protect someone from attack|The celebrity hired several bodyguards for the event.  这位名人为这次活动雇佣了几名保镖。|近义词：protector, guardian, escort|反义词: assailant, attacker|noun

bodyguard:/ˈbɑːdiˌɡɑːrd/ /ˈbɒdiˌɡɑːd/| vt.|保护，护卫|to act as a bodyguard for someone|Two police officers bodyguarded the witness. 两名警察保护证人。|近义词：protect, guard, defend|反义词: attack, endanger|verb
