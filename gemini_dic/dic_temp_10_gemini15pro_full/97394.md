infrastructural:/ˌɪnfrəˈstrʌktʃərəl/
adjective
基础设施的
Relating to the basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g. buildings, roads, power supplies) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise.
The country needs to invest heavily in infrastructural improvements to support economic growth.  这个国家需要大力投资基础设施建设以支持经济增长。
The project will require significant infrastructural changes.  这个项目将需要重大的基础设施改变。
Synonyms: basic, fundamental, essential, underlying
Antonyms: superficial, cosmetic
词性: adjective
