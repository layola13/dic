measurelessness:/ˈmɛʒərˌlɪsnəs/| n.|无度，无限，不可估量|The measurelessness of the universe is awe-inspiring. 宇宙的浩瀚令人敬畏。|近义词：immensity, vastness, boundlessness, infinity|反义词: finiteness, limitation, boundedness|noun

Etymology->
From measure + -less + -ness.  "Measure" comes from Old French "mesure" ultimately from Latin "mensura" meaning "a measuring." "-less" is a suffix indicating lack or deprivation. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
