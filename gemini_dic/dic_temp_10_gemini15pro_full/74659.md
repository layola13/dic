free-wheel:/ˈfriːwiːl/ /ˈfriːhwil/| vi. n.|自由旋转，滑行；无拘无束地行动；空转；飞轮|To move along freely without using power or effort, especially on a bicycle; to act without restraint or planning.|例句：He free-wheeled down the hill. 他沿着山坡滑行下去。

例句：The car free-wheeled to a stop. 汽车空转着停了下来。

|近义词：coast, drift, glide, roll, sail|反义词：pedal, strive, struggle|v.

free-wheel:/ˈfriːwiːl/ /ˈfriːhwil/| n.|飞轮；自由轮|A mechanism, especially on a bicycle, that allows a wheel to rotate freely in one direction while transmitting power in the other.|例句：The bicycle's free-wheel needs to be replaced. 这辆自行车的飞轮需要更换。

例句：The free-wheel mechanism allows the cyclist to coast downhill.  飞轮装置使骑自行车的人可以下坡滑行。|近义词：freewheel sprocket, ratchet wheel|反义词：fixed gear|n.
