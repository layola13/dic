convicted:/kənˈvɪktɪd/ | adj. | 被定罪的；已被判刑的 |  Having been declared guilty of a criminal offense by the verdict of a jury or the decision of a judge in a court of law. | He was convicted of murder. 他被判犯有谋杀罪。 | Synonyms: condemned, sentenced, judged guilty | Antonyms: acquitted, exonerated, cleared | adjective

convicting:/kənˈvɪktɪŋ/ | v. | 定罪；证明…有罪 | Proving or declaring guilty of an offense, especially by the verdict of a jury or the decision of a judge in a court of law. |  The evidence was insufficient to convict him. 证据不足以给他定罪。 | Synonyms: incriminating, indicting, condemning | Antonyms: acquitting, exonerating, clearing | verb
