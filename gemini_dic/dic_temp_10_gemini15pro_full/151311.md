parigenin: /pəˈrɪdʒənɪn/ | n. | 副甙元; 洋地黄毒甙元 | A cardiac glycoside obtained from the seeds of Digitalis purpurea. It is used in the treatment of congestive heart failure. | Parigenin is a key component in some heart medications.  副甙元是一些心脏药物的关键成分。|  Parigenin is derived from the foxglove plant. 副甙元是从毛地黄植物中提取的。 |Synonyms: digitoxigenin, digitalis glycoside| Antonyms: None | Noun
