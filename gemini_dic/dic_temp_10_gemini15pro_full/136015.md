nonreactor:/ˌnɒnriˈæktər/ | n. | 非反应堆；非反应者 |  Something or someone that does not react or cause a reaction.  A person, substance, or entity that does not participate in a chemical or nuclear reaction. | The scientist identified the substance as a nonreactor in the experiment. 科学家在实验中确定该物质为非反应物。

The control group in the study consisted of nonreactors to the allergen.  研究中的对照组由对过敏原无反应的人组成。| 近义词:inert substance, non-respondent | 反义词: reactor, reactant | 词性: 名词
