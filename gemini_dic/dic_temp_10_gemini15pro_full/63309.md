enticement:/ɪnˈtaɪsmənt/| n.|诱惑，诱因|Something that attracts or tempts someone.|The advertisement was an enticement to buy the product. 这则广告是对购买该产品的诱惑。|近义词：attraction, lure, temptation, allurement|反义词：deterrent, discouragement|noun

entice:/ɪnˈtaɪs/| vt.|诱惑，引诱|To attract or tempt someone by offering something pleasant or desirable.|The smell of freshly baked bread enticed me into the bakery.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味把我引进了面包店。|近义词：tempt, lure, attract, seduce|反义词：deter, discourage, repel|verb
