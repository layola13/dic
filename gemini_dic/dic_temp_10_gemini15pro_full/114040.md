magnet:/ˈmæɡnɪt/ | n. |磁铁，磁石；有吸引力的人或物 |A piece of iron or other material that has its component atoms so ordered that the material exhibits properties of magnetism, such as attracting other iron-containing objects or aligning itself in an external magnetic field.| The fridge magnet fell on the floor. 冰箱磁铁掉在地上了。| synonyms: lodestone, electromagnet | antonyms: non-magnet | noun
