metronome: /ˈmɛtrəˌnoʊm/ (US) /ˈmɛtrəˌnəʊm/ (UK) | n. | 节拍器 | A device that marks time at a selected rate by emitting regular ticks or beeps, used by musicians to practice playing at a steady tempo. | The pianist used a metronome to practice the difficult passage.  钢琴家用节拍器练习困难的段落。 |  timer, clock |  - | noun
