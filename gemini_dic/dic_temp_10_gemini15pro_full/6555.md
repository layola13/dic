amylose:/ˈæmɪloʊs/| n. |直链淀粉|A linear polysaccharide composed of glucose units linked by alpha-1,4 glycosidic bonds. It is a component of starch.|Amylose is less soluble in water than amylopectin.|近义词:starch component|反义词:amylopectin|词性:noun
