chlorinated:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. |氯化的；加氯的|Treated or infused with chlorine.  This process is often used for disinfection, especially of water. | The swimming pool was filled with chlorinated water. 游泳池里装满了氯化水。

The city supplies chlorinated drinking water. 城市提供氯化饮用水。|近义词：sanitized, disinfected|反义词: unchlorinated, untreated|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From chlorine + -ated.  Chlorine comes from the Greek word "khlōros" meaning "pale green," referring to the element's color. The suffix "-ated" means "treated with" or "containing."
