nephew:/ˈnefjuː/ (US), /ˈnevjuː/ (UK)| n. |侄子；外甥|A son of one's brother or sister or of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law.|My nephew is graduating from college next year. 我的侄子明年大学毕业。|Synonym:  No single perfect synonym, but related terms include kinsman, relative. |Antonym: No direct antonym.|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *neveu (modern French neveu), from Latin nepōtem (nominative nepōs), meaning "grandson, nephew".  The meaning shifted from "grandson" to primarily "nephew" in Vulgar Latin.
