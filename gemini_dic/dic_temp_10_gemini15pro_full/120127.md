min:/mɪn/| n. | 分钟；最少量；最小值 | A unit of time equal to 60 seconds.  The smallest amount or value. | Example: It takes 5 mins to walk there.  走到那里要5分钟。

Example: This is the min price I can offer. 这是我能提供的最低价格。 | Synonym: minimum, least | Antonym: max, maximum | noun


min:/mɪn/| adj. | 最小的；最低的| Smallest in size, amount, degree, or importance. | Example: The min age for voting is 18.  投票的最低年龄是18岁。

Example: What's the min wage in this country? 这个国家的最低工资是多少？ | Synonym: smallest, least | Antonym: maximum, largest | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mynute*, from Old French *minute* (noun), *minut* (adjective), from Latin *minūta*, feminine of *minūtus* (“small, minute”), past participle of *minuō* (“I lessen, diminish”).
