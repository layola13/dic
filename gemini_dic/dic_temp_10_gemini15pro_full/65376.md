eunuchry: /ˈjuːnəkəri/ | n. | 阉割；太监身份；无男子气概 |The practice of castration, especially to produce eunuchs; the state or condition of being a eunuch; a group or body of eunuchs.  The emasculation or castration of a male; also, the condition of being a eunuch. |  The eunuchry in the Ottoman Empire held significant power. 奥斯曼帝国的太监群体拥有显著的权力。 Historical accounts detail the brutal practices of eunuchry in ancient China.  历史记载详细描述了古代中国残酷的阉割习俗。 | 近义词: castration, emasculation | 反义词: virility, masculinity | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *eunuchia, from Greek eunoucheia, from eunouchos (eunuch).
