anthrenus:/ænˈθriːnəs/
n.
Carpet beetle, Museum beetle
A genus of beetles in the family Dermestidae, known as carpet beetles or museum beetles.  These beetles are common pests of households and museums, feeding on animal products such as wool, fur, feathers, and insect collections.
The anthrenus beetle infested the museum's collection of taxidermied birds.  标本甲虫侵扰了博物馆的动物标本收藏。
Synonyms:  carpet beetle, museum beetle, dermestid beetle
