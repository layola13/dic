muny:/ˈmʌni/ | n. | 钱; 资金 |  Slang, primarily used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE), for "money."  It's a shortened, informal version of the word "money." |  "I need to get my muny right." 我需要把我的钱弄好。 | synonyms: cash, dough, bread, loot | antonyms: debt, poverty | noun
