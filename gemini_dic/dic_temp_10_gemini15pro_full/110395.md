ligule:/ˈlɪɡjuːl/ /ˈlaɪɡjuːl/
n.
薄膜,叶舌,舌状物
A membranous appendage, especially a strap-shaped outgrowth at the junction of leaf and leafstalk in grasses and sedges.  Also, a similar structure in other plants or animals.
The ligule of the grass was prominent.  这棵草的叶舌很明显。

近义词:strap, appendage, scale

词性:noun
