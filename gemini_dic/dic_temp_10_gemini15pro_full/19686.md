bilection: /baɪˈlɛkʃən/
n.
双重选举; 两次选举
A bilection refers to two elections held within a short period, often for different offices or due to special circumstances.
Due to the resignation of the mayor, the city held a bilection to fill the vacancy and elect a new city council. 由于市长辞职，该市举行了两次选举以填补空缺并选举新的市议会。
The country held a bilection for both president and parliament. 这个国家举行了总统和议会两项选举。
