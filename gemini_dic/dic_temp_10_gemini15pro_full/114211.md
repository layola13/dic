maidling:/ˈmeɪdlɪŋ/ | n. | 少女，年轻女子 | A young unmarried woman; a girl. |  例句：The fair maidling caught the eye of the young knight.  年轻美丽的少女吸引了年轻骑士的目光。

例句：The maidling blushed when complimented on her beauty. 少女因被赞美美丽而脸红。 | 近义词：maiden, lass, damsel, girl | 反义词: matron, crone | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *maidlinge, a diminutive of maiden, from Old English mægden "maiden, virgin," of Germanic origin.
