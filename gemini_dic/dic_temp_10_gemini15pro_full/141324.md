openers:/ˈoʊpənərz/
n.
开场白；开局者；开幕式；开瓶器；开启者
People who start conversations or activities, things that begin something, or tools used for opening.
The openers for the concert were a local band. 这场音乐会的开场嘉宾是一个当地乐队。

The chess player surprised everyone with his unconventional openers. 这位棋手以其非传统的开局方式震惊了所有人。


A good bottle opener is essential for a bartender.  一个好的开瓶器对调酒师来说是必不可少的。


The openers of the new store were very successful. 新店的开业非常成功。


近义词: beginnings, starts, initiators,  introductions
反义词: closers, endings, finishers
词性: 名词
