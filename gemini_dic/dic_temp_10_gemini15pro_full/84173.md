half-proletarian:/ˌhæfˌproʊlɪˈtɛəriən/| adj. n.|半无产者，半无产阶级的|A person who belongs to the half-proletariat, a social group between the proletariat and the peasantry, often having some land or means of production but also relying on wage labor.|The half-proletarians, owning small plots of land, were vulnerable to economic downturns. 半无产者拥有小块土地，容易受到经济衰退的影响。|Near synonyms: semi-proletarian, marginal worker, part-time worker|Near antonyms: bourgeois, capitalist, landowner|adjective/noun
