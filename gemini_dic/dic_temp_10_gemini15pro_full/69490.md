felicitate: /fəˈlɪsɪˌteɪt/ /fəˈlɪsɪteɪt/ | vt. | 祝贺，庆贺 | To express congratulations to someone; to compliment or praise for a happy event or occasion. |  I felicitated her on her promotion. 我祝贺她升职了。

|  The president felicitated the winning team. 总统祝贺了获胜的队伍。

| 近义词: congratulate, commend, applaud | 反义词: condole, commiserate | 词性: verb (动词)
