arrogantness:/ˈærəɡəntnəs/
n.
傲慢；自大
The quality of being arrogant; overbearing pride.
His arrogantness alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了他的同事。
Synonyms: haughtiness, superciliousness, disdain, pomposity
Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness
noun
