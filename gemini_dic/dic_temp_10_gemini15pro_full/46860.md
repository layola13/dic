dared:/derd/ | v. | 敢，胆敢 | past tense of *dare* - to have the courage to do something | He dared to challenge the champion. 他胆敢挑战冠军。 |  近义词: challenged, ventured, risked | 反义词: hesitated, flinched, cowered | verb


dared:/derd/ | adj. | 敢于...的; 被激将...的 | used to describe someone who has been challenged or provoked to do something |  She completed the dared task. 她完成了被激将的任务。 | 近义词: challenged, provoked | 反义词: unchallenged | adjective
