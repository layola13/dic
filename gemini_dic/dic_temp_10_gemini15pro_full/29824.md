cauliform:/ˈkɔːlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
菜花状的，花椰菜状的
Resembling a cauliflower in form.  Having a compact head like a cauliflower.
The fungus had a cauliform appearance. 这种真菌外观呈菜花状。
The coral exhibited a cauliform structure.  这块珊瑚呈现出菜花状的结构。
近义词: cauliflower-shaped, cauliflower-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider smooth, flat, etc. depending on context)
词性: adjective
