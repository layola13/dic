dazzling:/ˈdæzlɪŋ/ /ˈdæzəlɪŋ/| adj.|耀眼的，令人眼花缭乱的| Extremely bright, especially so as to blind the eyes temporarily. |The sunset was dazzling. 日落景色令人目眩。|近义词：blinding, brilliant, gleaming, shimmering, sparkling|反义词：dull, dim, lackluster|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *daze* (v.) + *-ling*, a suffix forming present participles.
