obiter:/ˈɒbɪtər/ (UK), /ˈoʊbɪtər/ (US)| adj. adv.|顺便提及的，附带的|Incidental; said in passing, usually in a legal context.  A judge's obiter dicta are remarks which are not binding precedent.|The judge made some interesting obiter remarks about the case.|近义词：incidental, passing, casual|反义词:essential, crucial|词性:adjective, adverb

obiter dictum:/ˈɒbɪtər ˈdɪktəm/ (UK), /ˈoʊbɪtər ˈdɪktəm/ (US)| n.|附带意见，顺便提及|A judge's expression of opinion uttered in court or in a written judgement, but not essential to the decision and therefore not legally binding as a precedent.|The judge's obiter dictum on that point has been widely cited.|近义词：remark, comment, aside|反义词:ruling, decision|词性:noun
