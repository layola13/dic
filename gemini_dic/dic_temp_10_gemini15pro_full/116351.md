masthead:/ˈmæstˌhed/ (US), /ˈmɑːstˌhed/ (UK)| n.|桅顶; 报头; (公司或组织的)领导层|The ship's masthead was visible from miles away.  这艘船的桅顶在几英里外都能看到。|The newspaper's masthead proudly proclaimed its founding date. 这家报纸的报头自豪地宣告了它的创刊日期。|The company's masthead includes the CEO and other top executives.  该公司的领导层包括首席执行官和其他高管。|synonyms: header, banner, title, leadership, top brass|antonyms: staff, employees, workers|noun
