frontignac: /ˌfrɒntiˈnjæk/ /ˌfrʌntiˈnjæk/ | n. | 麝香葡萄酒（一种甜白葡萄酒）| A sweet white wine made from the Muscat grape, originally from the Frontignan region of France. |  Example 1:  We enjoyed a glass of chilled Frontignac with dessert. 我们用甜点享用了一杯冰镇的麝香葡萄酒。

Example 2: The Frontignac had a rich, floral aroma.  这款麝香葡萄酒有着浓郁的花香。 | 近义词: Muscat, sweet wine | 反义词: dry wine | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Frontignan, a town in southern France where the wine originated.  源自法国南部的弗龙蒂尼昂镇，该葡萄酒的原产地。
