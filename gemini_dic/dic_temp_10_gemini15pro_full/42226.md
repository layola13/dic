counsels:/ˈkaʊnsəlz/
n.| 劝告，忠告，建议|advice or guidance given to someone.|The king sought the counsels of his wisest advisors. 国王寻求他最明智的顾问的建议。|Synonyms: advice, guidance, recommendations|Antonyms: dissuasion, discouragement|noun

counsels:/ˈkaʊnsəlz/
v.|劝告，忠告；提议|to give advice or guidance to someone.|She counsels young people on career choices. 她为年轻人提供职业选择方面的建议。|Synonyms: advise, guide, recommend|Antonyms: dissuade, discourage|verb


counsel:/ˈkaʊnsəl/
n.|律师，法律顾问|a lawyer or group of lawyers representing someone in a legal case.|The defense counsel presented a strong case. 辩护律师提出了强有力的论据。|Synonyms: lawyer, attorney, advocate|Antonyms: prosecutor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conseil*, from Latin *consilium* "a plan, counsel, deliberation, advice," from *consulere* "to consult, deliberate, ask advice," literally "to sit together," from com- "together" + *sedere* "to sit."
