minimise:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɪnəˌmaɪz/ (US)| v. |最小化，使减到最少|To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree.|The company is trying to minimise costs. 公司正在努力将成本降到最低。|近义词: reduce, decrease, lessen, curtail, diminish |反义词: maximise, increase, enlarge, amplify |verb

minimization:/ˌmɪnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌmɪnəməˈzeɪʃən/ (US)| n. |最小化|The act or process of minimizing something.|The minimization of risk is a key concern for investors. 将风险最小化是投资者们的主要关注点。|近义词: reduction, decrease |反义词: maximization, increase |noun
