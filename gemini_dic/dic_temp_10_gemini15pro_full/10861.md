arable:/ˈærəbl/| adj.|可耕种的；适于耕种的|Suitable for growing crops.|The land is rich and arable. 这片土地肥沃，适宜耕种。|近义词:cultivable, plowable, tillable|反义词:infertile, barren, uncultivable|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *arabilis, from arare (“to plow”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂erh₃- (“to plow”).
