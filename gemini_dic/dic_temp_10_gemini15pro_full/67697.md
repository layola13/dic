exulceratory:/ɪkˈsʌlsəreɪtəri/
adjective
使溃疡的; 加剧溃疡的; 刺激性的
Tending to cause or exacerbate ulceration; irritating.
His exulceratory remarks deepened the conflict between the two parties. 他那些刺激性的言论加剧了双方之间的冲突。

The medication had exulceratory side effects on his stomach.  这种药物对他的胃有刺激性副作用。

近义词:irritating, abrasive, caustic, corrosive
反义词:soothing, healing, lenitive, emollient
词性:adjective


词源:
From Latin *exulceratorius, from exulcerare (“to cause an ulcer”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + ulcerare (“to ulcerate”), from ulcus (“sore, ulcer”).
