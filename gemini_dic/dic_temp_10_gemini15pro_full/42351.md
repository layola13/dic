counteradvertisings:/ˌkaʊntərˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋz/
|n.|反广告，对抗性广告|Advertising that takes a position contrary to an advertising message that preceded it.  It can be used to refute claims made in an advertisement or to promote an opposing viewpoint. |Example 1: The company launched a series of counteradvertisings to address the misleading claims made by their competitor. 这家公司发起了一系列反广告，以回应竞争对手提出的误导性声明。

Example 2:  Health advocates used counteradvertisings to warn people about the dangers of smoking. 健康倡导者使用反广告来警告人们吸烟的危害。|Synonyms: counter-advertising, counter-message, rebuttal advertising|Antonyms:  advertising, promotion|noun
