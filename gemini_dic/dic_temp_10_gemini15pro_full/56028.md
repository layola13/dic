doabler:/ˈduːblər/| n.|替身演员，加倍器|A person who stands in for another, especially in a dangerous scene in a film or other performance.  A device or mechanism that doubles something.|The stunt doabler jumped from the building.  特技替身演员从楼上跳了下来。|近义词：stand-in, substitute, replacement, understudy, double|反义词: principal (actor)|词性: noun
