The haunted house had a hadish atmosphere.  这栋鬼屋有一种令人毛骨悚然的气氛。|近义词：infernal, hellish, ghostly, eerie, macabre, gruesome|反义词：heavenly, celestial, divine|词性:形容词
词源: From Hades (Ancient Greek ᾍδης), name of the Greek god of the underworld.
