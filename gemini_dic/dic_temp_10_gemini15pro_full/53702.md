disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/ | n. | 免责声明；否认；放弃 | A statement that denies something, especially responsibility. |  The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of the information provided. 该网站包含关于所提供信息的准确性的免责声明。| Example: The advertisement had a disclaimer stating that results may vary.  广告中有一份免责声明，说明结果可能会有所不同。|Synonyms: denial, rejection, renunciation | Antonyms: acceptance, acknowledgment, admission | noun
