digressed:/daɪˈɡrɛst/| v.|偏离，离题|to deviate or wander away from the main topic or purpose in speaking or writing|He digressed from the main point of his lecture to tell a personal anecdote. 他在演讲中偏离了主题，讲了一个个人轶事。|近义词：deviated, strayed, rambled|反义词:focused, stayed on topic|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin digressus, past participle of digredi, from di- "aside" + gradi "to walk, go".
