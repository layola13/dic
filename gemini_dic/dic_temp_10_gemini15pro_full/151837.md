passiflora:/ˌpæsiˈflɔːrə/ | n. |西番莲，百香果|A genus of chiefly tropical American climbing plants, including the passion flower and granadilla, cultivated for their showy flowers and edible fruit. |例句：The passiflora is a beautiful and exotic flower. 西番莲是一种美丽而奇特的植物。

例句：Passiflora edulis is the species that produces the passion fruit.  西番莲是生产百香果的品种。|近义词：passion flower, granadilla |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology->
From Latin *passio* (“suffering, passion”) + *flor-* (“flower”).  The name was given by Spanish missionaries in South America who saw symbolism of the Passion of Christ in the flower's structure.
