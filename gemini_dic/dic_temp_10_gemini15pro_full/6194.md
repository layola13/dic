ammiral:/ˈædmərəl/ | n. | 海军上将；旗舰；凤蝶 | A high-ranking officer in the navy. | Example 1: The admiral commanded the entire fleet. 海军上将指挥着整个舰队。

Example 2:  He was promoted to the rank of admiral. 他被提升为海军上将。 | Synonyms: commander, chief, captain | Antonyms: subordinate, seaman | noun
