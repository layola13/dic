hegge: /ˈhɛdʒ/ | n. | 树篱；篱笆 | A hedge is a fence or boundary formed by closely growing bushes or shrubs. |  The garden was surrounded by a thick hegge.  花园被厚厚的树篱包围着。| synonym: fence, barrier, hedgerow | antonym: opening, gap | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *hegge*, from Old English *hecg*, from Proto-Germanic *hagjō* (“hedge, enclosure”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱak-yos* (“fence, enclosure”).
