locale:/loʊˈkæl/ (US) /ləʊˈkɑːl/ (UK)| n.| 地点，场所；现场；区域设置 |A locale is a place where something happens or is set, or that has particular events associated with it.  It can also refer to the settings on a computer that determine language, currency, etc.|

例句：
The locale for the wedding was a beautiful vineyard.
婚礼的地点是一个美丽的葡萄园。

The novel's locale is a small town in the American South.
这部小说的地点是美国南部的一个小镇。


近义词：place, location, setting, venue, spot, area, region
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
