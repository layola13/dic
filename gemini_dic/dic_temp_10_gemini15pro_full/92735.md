ichthidin: /ɪkˈθɪdɪn/
n.
鱼精蛋白
A protamine found in the sperm of salmon and some other fish.
Salmon sperm contains a high concentration of ichthidin.  鲑鱼精子中含有高浓度的鱼精蛋白。
Synonyms: protamine, clupeine, salmine
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for this specific protein)
noun
