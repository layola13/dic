overmitigate: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/
verb
过度缓解；过度减轻
To mitigate something excessively, often to the point of negating the intended benefits or creating new problems.  The act of taking excessive measures to reduce or eliminate risk, often to the point of causing unintended negative consequences.
The government's attempt to overmitigate the economic downturn led to excessive spending and increased national debt. 政府试图过度缓解经济衰退，导致过度支出和国家债务增加。

The company's overmitigation of security risks resulted in a cumbersome and inefficient system. 公司对安全风险的过度缓解导致系统繁琐且效率低下。

近义词: overcompensate, overdo
反义词: undermitigate, neglect
词性: verb
