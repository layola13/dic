bonanza:/bəˈnænzə/| n.| 繁荣，兴旺；大量，丰富；意外的收获|A situation or event that creates a sudden increase in wealth, good fortune, or something that is highly desirable.  A rich mine or pocket of ore.|The gold rush was a bonanza for the town.  淘金热使这个镇繁荣起来。|Synonyms: windfall, godsend, boon, jackpot, prosperity|Antonyms: misfortune, adversity, hardship|noun

bonanza:/bəˈnænzə/| n.| 意外的收获，好运|A sudden, unexpected source of wealth or good fortune.|Discovering the antique in the attic was a real bonanza. 在阁楼里发现古董真是意外的收获。|Synonyms: windfall, stroke of luck|Antonyms: misfortune, bad luck|noun
