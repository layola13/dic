lionizers:/ˈlaɪənaɪzərz/
noun
奉承者，吹捧者
People who lionize someone treat them as a very important and famous person.
Example: The young author was lionized by the press after winning the prestigious award.  这位年轻的作家在获得这一殊荣后受到了媒体的追捧。
Example: He was lionized everywhere after his Olympic victory.  他在奥运会夺冠后到处受到追捧。
Synonyms: admirers, flatterers, worshippers, devotees
Antonyms: critics, detractors, skeptics
