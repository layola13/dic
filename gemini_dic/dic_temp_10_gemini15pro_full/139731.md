ochreate: /ˈoʊkriːt/ | adj. n. |鞘状的；托叶鞘 | A membranous sheath formed from two stipules that encircles the stem at a node, as in the Polygonaceae. |  The rhubarb plant has prominent ochreate stipules. 大黄植物有明显的鞘状托叶。|  The ochreate base of the leaf stalk is a key characteristic of this species. 叶柄基部的鞘状结构是该物种的一个关键特征。| synonyms: sheathing, stipular | antonyms:  exstipulate, nonsheathing | adjective, noun


ochrea: /ˈoʊkriːə/ | n. | 托叶鞘 | A tubular sheath formed from two stipules that surrounds the stem at a node, characteristic of the Polygonaceae (buckwheat family). | The ochrea is a distinguishing feature of plants like rhubarb. 托叶鞘是像大黄这样的植物的一个显著特征。| The brownish ochrea protects the delicate emerging leaves. 棕色的托叶鞘保护着娇嫩的新生叶子。| synonyms: ocrea, ochreate stipule | antonyms: N/A | noun
