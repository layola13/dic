nonattachment:/ˌnɒnəˈtætʃmənt/
n.
无执着，不执着，超脱
The state of not being emotionally attached to something or someone.
She practiced nonattachment to material possessions. 她练习对物质财富的无执着。
detachment, indifference, apathy
attachment, dependence, obsession
noun
