ETYMOLOGY->
From boy + -ish + -ly.  "Boy" comes from Middle English *boi, *boie, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Low German *boije "young man, servant." "-ish" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "of the nature of," "somewhat like," "rather," from Old English -isc.  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, from Old English -lice.
