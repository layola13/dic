grilles:/ɡrɪlz/ | n. |格栅，烤架，铁栅 |A grating or screen of metal bars or wires, placed in front of something as protection or to allow ventilation or passage of sound.| The speaker was hidden behind metal grilles.  扬声器藏在金属格栅后面。| Synonym: gratings, screens, lattices | Antonym: openings | noun

grilles:/ɡrɪlz/ | v. |(在…上)装格栅，烤(尤指肉类) |To broil food on a grill.|  We grilled some steaks for dinner. 我们晚餐烤了一些牛排。| Synonym: broil, barbecue, charbroil | Antonym: boil, stew | verb
