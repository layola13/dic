hyperventilating:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度换气，呼吸急促|breathing too rapidly, causing a decrease in carbon dioxide levels in the blood|She was hyperventilating from the shock of the news. 她被这个消息震惊得呼吸急促。|近义词：overbreathing, panting|反义词:hypoventilating|Verb

hyperventilation:/ˌhaɪpərˌvɛntɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |过度换气，呼吸急促|the condition of breathing too rapidly, causing a decrease in carbon dioxide levels in the blood|His hyperventilation was a symptom of his anxiety. 他的呼吸急促是焦虑的症状。|近义词：overbreathing|反义词:hypoventilation|Noun
