cuneiform:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. |楔形的；楔形文字|A system of writing first developed by the ancient Sumerians of Mesopotamia, characterized by wedge-shaped marks impressed on clay tablets. | The museum boasts a vast collection of cuneiform tablets.  博物馆收藏了大量的楔形文字泥板。|  The archaeologist carefully deciphered the cuneiform inscriptions. 考古学家仔细地破译了楔形文字铭文。| 近义词:wedge-shaped | 反义词: (no direct antonym) | 词性: adjective, noun

cuneiforms:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrmz/ | n. |楔形文字（复数）| Cuneiform characters or inscriptions considered collectively. | Many ancient cuneiforms remain undeciphered. 许多古代楔形文字仍未被破译。 | Researchers are studying these cuneiforms to learn more about ancient Mesopotamian culture. 研究人员正在研究这些楔形文字，以了解更多关于古代美索不达米亚文化的信息。 | 近义词: (no direct synonym, could use "cuneiform tablets" or "cuneiform inscriptions") | 反义词: (no direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cuneiformis*, from *cuneus* (wedge) + *forma* (form).
