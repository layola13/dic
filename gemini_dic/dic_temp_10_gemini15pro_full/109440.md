leotarded:/ˈliːəˌtɑːrdɪd/
|adj.|穿着紧身衣的|Wearing a leotard.  Covered or clothed in a leotard.|The leotarded gymnast performed a graceful backflip.|近义词:skin-tight, close-fitting|反义词:loose-fitting|词性:形容词
词源: From "leotard" (a close-fitting one-piece garment) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Leotard" is named after Jules Léotard (1838–1870), a French trapeze artist.
