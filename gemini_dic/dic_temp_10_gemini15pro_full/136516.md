nonrubbers:/ˌnɒnˈrʌbəz/
noun
非橡胶制品；非橡胶植物
materials or plants that are not made of or do not produce rubber
The factory specializes in nonrubbers.  这家工厂专门生产非橡胶制品。
Many desert plants are nonrubbers. 许多沙漠植物都不是橡胶植物。
synonyms: non-rubber materials, non-rubber plants
antonyms: rubbers, rubber plants
