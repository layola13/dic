covetously:/ˈkʌvɪtəsli/
adverb
贪婪地，垂涎地
In a way that shows a strong desire to possess something that belongs to someone else.
He looked covetously at his neighbor's new car. 他贪婪地看着邻居的新车。
近义词: greedily, enviously, jealously, avariciously
反义词: generously, charitably, selflessly
词性: adverb

covetous:/ˈkʌvɪtəs/
adjective
贪婪的，垂涎的
Having or showing a strong desire to possess something that belongs to someone else.
She was covetous of her sister's success. 她嫉妒她姐姐的成功。
近义词: greedy, envious, jealous, avaricious
反义词: generous, charitable, selfless
词性: adjective
