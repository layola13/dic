dacron:/ˈdeɪkrɒn/| n.|涤纶，达克纶|A synthetic polyester fiber used in clothing, sails, and other products, known for its durability, wrinkle resistance, and quick-drying properties.  It is a trademarked name owned by Invista.|This jacket is made of Dacron, so it's very lightweight and dries quickly. 这件夹克是涤纶做的，所以很轻便，干得也很快。|Synonyms: polyester, Terylene|Antonyms: cotton, wool|noun
