overshadowingly:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædəʊɪŋli/
adv.
压倒性地，使相形见绌地
in a way that overshadows something else; so as to overshadow something else.
Her success was overshadowingly impressive. 她的成功令人印象深刻，使其他一切黯然失色。
overshadowing, dominantly, overwhelmingly
inconspicuously, insignificantly
词性: 副词
