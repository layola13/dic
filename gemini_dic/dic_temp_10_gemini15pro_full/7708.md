anomia:/əˈnoʊmiə/| n.|失语症，命名障碍|A medical condition characterized by the inability to name objects or to recognize the written or spoken names of objects.  It's a form of aphasia.|She suffered from anomia after the stroke and struggled to recall the names of everyday items.|近义词:aphasia, nominal aphasia|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using "fluency of speech" or "normal language function")|noun
