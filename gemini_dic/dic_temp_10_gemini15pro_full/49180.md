delamination:/diːˌlæmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |分层，剥离|The process of separating into layers, or the state of being separated into layers. |The delamination of the plywood was caused by water damage.  胶合板的分层是由水损造成的。|Synonyms: separation, splitting, peeling, flaking |Antonyms: adhesion, bonding| noun

delaminate:/diːˈlæmɪneɪt/ | v. |分层，剥离|To separate into layers, or cause something to separate into layers. |The paint began to delaminate after only a few months.  油漆才几个月就开始剥落了。|Synonyms: separate, split, peel, flake |Antonyms: adhere, bond| verb
