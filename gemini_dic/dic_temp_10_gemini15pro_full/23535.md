brainiac: /ˈbreɪniæk/ | n. | 天才，智多星 |  A very intelligent person. |  例句：She's a real brainiac and always gets top marks. 她是个真正的天才，总是得最高分。

例句：He's such a brainiac, he can solve any puzzle. 他真是个智多星，任何谜题都能解开。

近义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind | 反义词：idiot, fool, dunce | 词性：名词

词源：Coined in the 1950s, likely influenced by the character "Brainiac" from DC Comics, a super-intelligent alien.  Derived from "brain" + "-iac" (a suffix suggesting obsession or characteristic).
