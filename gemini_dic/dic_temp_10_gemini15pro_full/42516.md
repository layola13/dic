counterinvasions: /ˌkaʊntərɪnˈveɪʒənz/ | n. |反入侵，反攻|Military actions taken to repel an invasion.  The act of invading a territory in response to a prior invasion.|例句：The counterinvasions were swift and decisive. 反攻迅速而果断。

例句：The general planned a series of counterinvasions to reclaim lost territory. 将军计划了一系列反攻以收复失地。|近义词：counterattacks, counteroffensives|反义词:invasions|词性:名词
