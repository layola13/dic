outvenoming:/ˌaʊtˈvɛnəmɪŋ/
verb
以毒胜过；用更强的毒液制服
The antidote successfully outvenomed the snake's poison.  解毒剂成功地战胜了蛇毒。
The spider outvenomed its prey.  蜘蛛用更强的毒液制服了它的猎物。
近义词:overpower, subdue, conquer
反义词:succumb, yield
