overregiment: /ˌoʊvərˈrɛdʒɪmənt/
|v.|过度管制，过度组织|To control or organize too strictly, often with excessive rules and regulations.|The new management team overregimented the staff, leading to low morale and decreased productivity.|近义词: overcontrol, micromanage, overorganize|反义词: empower, liberate, free|词性: 动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "regiment" (verb meaning "to organize into regiments," from French "régiment").  The original meaning referred to organizing troops into regiments, but the meaning has broadened to include any kind of excessive organization or control.
