cultivably:/ˈkʌltɪvəbli/
adjective
可耕种地
capable of being cultivated.
The land is cultivably rich and fertile. 这片土地肥沃，适宜耕种。
近义词: arable, tillable
反义词: uncultivable, barren
词性: adjective

词源:
From cultivate + -ably.  Cultivate comes from Late Middle English: from Latin cultivat- ‘tilled, cultivated’, from the verb colere.  -ably is a suffix meaning "in a manner that is specified"
