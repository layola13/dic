nuisance:/ˈnjuːsns/| n.|讨厌的人(或事物); 麻烦事; 妨害行为|Something or someone that annoys or causes trouble; a person, thing, or circumstance causing inconvenience or annoyance|The dog next door is a real nuisance, barking all night. 隔壁那条狗真讨厌，整夜吠个不停。|近义词：annoyance, bother, pest, inconvenience|反义词:pleasure, delight|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *nusance "harm, injury," from nuire "to harm, injure," from Latin nocēre "to hurt, harm."
