nonarmaments:/ˌnɒnˈɑːməmənts/
n.
裁军；非军备
the reduction or abolition of a nation's military forces and armaments.
The treaty called for complete nonarmaments. 该条约呼吁彻底裁军。
近义词：disarmament, demilitarization
反义词：armaments, militarization
词性：名词

nonarmaments:/ˌnɒnˈɑːməmənts/
词源： "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "armaments" (n. military weapons and equipment)
From Latin "armamenta" meaning "tools, equipment, tackle"
