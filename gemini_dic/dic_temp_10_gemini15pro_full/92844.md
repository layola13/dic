ict:/ˌaɪ siː ˈtiː/ | n. | 信息和通信技术 (Information and Communication Technology) |  ICT refers to all the technology used to handle information and aid communication.  This includes computer hardware and software, networks, satellite systems, and so on. |  The government is investing heavily in ICT infrastructure. 政府正在大力投资信息和通信技术基础设施。 |  The school's ICT resources are excellent.  学校的信息和通信技术资源非常棒。 | Synonyms: information technology, IT, telecommunications | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but perhaps) traditional methods, outdated technology | noun
