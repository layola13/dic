lunchrooms:/ˈlʌntʃruːmz, ˈlʌntʃrʊmz/
n.
午餐室，食堂
A room or area in a workplace or school where people eat lunch.
The employees gathered in the lunchrooms to eat and socialize.  员工们聚集在食堂里吃饭和社交。
The school lunchrooms were noisy and crowded. 学校食堂里很吵，也很拥挤。
近义词：cafeterias, dining halls
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps kitchen or office if considering location)
词性: noun
