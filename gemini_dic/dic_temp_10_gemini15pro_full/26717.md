cadetship:/kəˈdɛtʃɪp/
n.
学员身份；见习期
A period of training as a cadet, especially in the armed forces or merchant navy.
He completed his cadetship and became a full officer. 他完成了见习期，成为了一名正式军官。

He served a cadetship in the merchant navy. 他在商船队服役了见习期。

Synonym: apprenticeship, traineeship, internship
Antonym:  seniority, experience
词性: noun
