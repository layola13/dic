bling-blinging:/blɪŋ blɪŋɪŋ/
adjective
闪亮的；炫耀的；奢华的
Covered in sparkly, expensive jewelry or other flashy accessories.  Often used to describe a style characterized by ostentatious display of wealth.
She arrived at the party bling-blinging in diamonds. 她戴着闪耀的钻石来到派对。
blinged-out, flashy, ostentatious, glitzy, glamorous
understated, simple, modest, plain
词性:形容词


bling-blinging:/blɪŋ blɪŋɪŋ/
noun
闪亮的东西；炫耀的饰品
Sparkly, expensive jewelry or other flashy accessories.
Her bling-blinging caught everyone's eye. 她的闪亮饰品吸引了所有人的目光。
jewelry, diamonds, bling, ice
plain clothes, simple accessories
词性:名词


bling-bling:/ˈblɪŋ blɪŋ/
noun
闪亮的东西；炫耀的饰品；珠宝
Expensive, ostentatious jewelry or other decorative items.
He loves to wear his bling-bling. 他喜欢戴他的珠宝首饰。
jewelry, diamonds, ice, bling
plain clothes, simple accessories
词性:名词

词源:
Onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of light reflecting off shiny jewelry. Popularized in hip-hop culture in the 1990s.
