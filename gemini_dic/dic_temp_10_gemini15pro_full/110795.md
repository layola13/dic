linkage:/ˈlɪŋkɪdʒ/| n.|连接，联系，联动|The linkage between the two companies is strong. 这两家公司之间的联系很强。|Example: The mechanical linkage between the steering wheel and the wheels is essential for controlling the car.  方向盘和车轮之间的机械连接对于控制汽车至关重要。

Example: There is a clear linkage between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间有明显的联系。|Synonyms: connection, link, relationship, association|Antonyms: separation, disconnection, division|noun

linkage:/ˈlɪŋkɪdʒ/| n.|遗传连锁|The linkage between these two genes is well-established. 这两个基因之间的连锁关系已经确立。|Example:  Genetic linkage can be used to map the location of genes on chromosomes. 基因连锁可以用来绘制基因在染色体上的位置图。

Example:  The study demonstrated a strong linkage between the two genetic markers.  这项研究证明了这两个遗传标记之间有很强的联系。|Synonyms: genetic linkage|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but the concept of independent assortment in genetics contrasts with linkage)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from link + -age.
