outsnored:/ˌaʊtˈsnɔːrd/
verb
比…鼾声更大，胜过…的鼾声
outdo someone in snoring; snore more loudly than
例句：He outsnoared his wife last night. 他昨晚的鼾声比他妻子的大。
例句：She was outsnored by her dog.  她的鼾声被她的狗盖过了。
近义词：outdo, surpass
反义词：(None - difficult to have a direct antonym for this specific action)
词性: verb

词源:  A combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and the verb "snore."  "Out-" is from Old English "ūt," and "snore" is from Middle English "snoren," of imitative origin.
