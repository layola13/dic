lenes:/ˈliːnz/| n. |透镜，晶状体|A piece of glass or other transparent substance with curved sides for concentrating or dispersing light rays, used singly (as in a magnifying glass) or with other lenses (as in a telescope).|例句：The lenses of his glasses were thick. 他的眼镜镜片很厚。


例句：The camera's lens was scratched. 相机的镜头被刮花了。


近义词：lens (单数形式), glasses (眼镜), optic (光学器件)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun (名词)
