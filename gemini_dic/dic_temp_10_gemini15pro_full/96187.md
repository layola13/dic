indestructibly:/ˌɪndɪˈstrʌktɪbli/
adverb
不可摧毁地;牢不可破地
in a way that is impossible to destroy or break.
The data is encrypted and stored indestructibly. 数据经过加密并以不可摧毁的方式存储。
The bond between them was indestructibly strong.  他们之间的联系牢不可破。
近义词:permanently, enduringly
反义词:fragilely, precariously
词性:adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + destructible (capable of being destroyed) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Destructible comes from Latin *destructus*, past participle of destruere (to destroy).
