chargrilled:/ˈtʃɑːrɡrɪld/
adjective
烤的；炭烤的
cooked on a grill over or under a heat source
例句：We enjoyed some chargrilled vegetables. 我们享用了一些烤蔬菜。

例句：The chargrilled steak was perfectly cooked. 炭烤牛排做得恰到好处。

近义词：grilled, barbecued, broiled
反义词：(No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of methods like boiled, steamed, microwaved)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
char + grilled
char (v.) - to burn or scorch the surface of something
grill (v.) - to cook food over direct heat
