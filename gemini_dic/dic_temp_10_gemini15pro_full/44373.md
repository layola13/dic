cross-cousin:/ˈkrɒs ˌkʌzən/ (US) /ˈkrɒs ˌkʌzn/ (UK)| n. |表亲，堂亲（指父母一方的兄弟姐妹的子女）|A cross-cousin is the child of one's parent's opposite-sex sibling.  For example, the child of your mother's brother or your father's sister.|My mother's brother's daughter is my cross-cousin. 我母亲的兄弟的女儿是我的表妹。

My father's sister's son is my cross-cousin. 我父亲的姐妹的儿子是我的表弟。|近义词：cousin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
