graphemically:/ɡræˈfɪmɪkli/ | adv. | 拼写地，以拼写方式 |  Relating to or concerned with the written or printed representation of language. |  The word is spelled graphemically correctly, but its pronunciation is unusual.  这个词拼写上是正确的，但它的发音很不寻常。 | synonym: orthographically | antonym: phonetically | adverb
