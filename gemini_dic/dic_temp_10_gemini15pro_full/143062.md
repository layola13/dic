outbound:/ˈaʊtbaʊnd/| adj.|向外的；离开的；外出的；出站的|Traveling away from a particular place, especially returning home.|例句：Outbound flights are often cheaper than return flights. 出境的航班通常比返程航班便宜。|近义词：departing, outgoing, egressing|反义词:inbound, incoming|词性:形容词

outbound:/ˈaʊtbaʊnd/| n.|外发邮件；发送|The sending of messages or data, especially over the internet.|例句：The company's outbound marketing strategy focuses on email campaigns. 该公司的对外营销策略侧重于电子邮件活动。|近义词：dispatch, transmission|反义词:inbound|词性:名词
