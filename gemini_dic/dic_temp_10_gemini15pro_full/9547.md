antisepsis:/ˌæntiˈsɛpsɪs/ | n. |防腐, 抗菌|The prevention of sepsis by preventing or inhibiting the growth of causative microorganisms.|Doctors ensured strict antisepsis during surgery. 医生在手术过程中确保了严格的防腐。|Synonyms: disinfection, sterilization |Antonyms: infection, contamination |noun

antiseptic:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/ | adj. n. |防腐的，抗菌的；防腐剂，抗菌剂|Preventing or arresting sepsis.|The nurse cleaned the wound with an antiseptic solution. 护士用防腐溶液清洗伤口。|Synonyms: disinfectant, bactericidal |Antonyms: septic, infectious |adjective, noun
