bargainor:/ˈbɑːrɡɪnər/| n.|卖方；让与人；契约者|The bargainor is responsible for delivering the goods as agreed. 卖方有责任按约定交付货物。|Example: The bargainor agreed to sell the land for $1 million.  卖方同意以100万美元的价格出售土地。|Synonyms: seller, vendor, transferor|Antonyms: bargainee, buyer, purchaser|noun
