chalcography: /kælˈkɒɡrəfi/ /kælˈkɑːɡrəfi/
noun
铜版雕刻术；铜版画
The art or process of engraving on copper or brass, especially in intaglio, or the impression taken from such an engraving.
Chalcography is a less common term for engraving.  Copperplate engravings are highly detailed and valued by collectors. 铜版雕刻是一种不太常见的雕刻术语。铜版雕刻非常精细，受到收藏家的重视。
The museum has a large collection of 18th-century chalcography.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的18世纪铜版画。
Synonyms: engraving, etching, copperplate
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could consider digital printing as a modern contrast)
