anti-pacifism:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪzəm/
noun
反和平主义
Opposition to pacifism; the belief that war or violence is sometimes necessary or justifiable.
Anti-pacifism grew during the rise of Nazi Germany.  在纳粹德国崛起期间，反和平主义抬头。
The general's speech was a clear statement of anti-pacifism. 将军的演讲清楚地表明了反和平主义的立场。
近义词:bellicosity, militarism, hawkishness
反义词:pacifism, peacefulness
词性:noun
