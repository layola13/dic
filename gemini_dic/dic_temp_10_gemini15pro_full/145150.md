overassess:/ˌoʊvərəˈses/
verb
过高评估；过高估计
To assess something at too high a value or level.
The company overassessed the value of its assets.  这家公司高估了其资产的价值。

The tax assessor overassessed our property.  税务评估员对我们的房产估价过高。

近义词: overestimate, overvalue
反义词: underestimate, undervalue
词性: verb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "assess" (from Old French "assesser," ultimately from Latin "assidere" - to sit beside, assist in a judgment)
