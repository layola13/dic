diagraphical:/ˌdaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
用二合字母书写的；与二合字母相关的
Relating to or consisting of digraphs (a combination of two letters representing one sound, such as 'sh' or 'ch').
The digraph 'ch' can represent different sounds, as in 'chair' and 'choir'.  'ch'二合字母可以代表不同的发音，例如在'chair'和'choir'中。

The study of digraphs is important for understanding the orthography of a language.  研究二合字母对于理解一门语言的拼写法很重要。

synonym:  digraphic
antonym: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "monographic" relating to single letters representing sounds)
词性: 形容词
