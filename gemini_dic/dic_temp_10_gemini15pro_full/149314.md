pacan:/ˈpeɪkən/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋 | A foolish or stupid person. | He's such a pacan, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个傻瓜，又把钥匙锁在车里了。 | Synonyms: fool, idiot, dolt, simpleton | Antonyms: genius, sage, wise person | Noun

Etymology:  From Middle French *pacan*, possibly from *paquier* "to graze," suggesting someone who grazes like an animal and thus is unintelligent.  This etymology is uncertain and debated.
