enpierce:/ɪnˈpɪərs/ | v. | 刺穿，穿透 | To pierce or penetrate something, often with a sharp object; to deeply affect or move someone emotionally. | The knight's lance enpierced the dragon's hide. 骑士的长矛刺穿了龙的皮。|  The sad news enpierced her heart.  这悲伤的消息刺痛了她的心。 | Synonyms: pierce, penetrate, transfix, stab, impale | Antonyms:  extract, remove, withdraw | verb
