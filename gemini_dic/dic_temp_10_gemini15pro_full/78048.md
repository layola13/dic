genus:/ˈdʒiːnəs/| n. |属，类|A principal taxonomic category that ranks above species and below family, and is denoted by a capitalized Latin name, e.g. *Leo*.|例句：The lion belongs to the genus *Panthera*. 狮子属于豹属。|例句：Scientists have discovered a new genus of plant in the rainforest. 科学家在雨林中发现了一个新的植物属。|近义词：kind, sort, type, category, class|反义词: (Genus has no direct antonym as it's a taxonomic category.)|noun
