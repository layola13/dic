liquidator:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtər/| n. | 清算人；破产清算人；公司资产清理人 | A person appointed to wind up the affairs of a company, especially one that is insolvent, and to distribute its assets. | The liquidator sold the company's assets to pay off its debts. 清算人出售了该公司的资产以偿还债务。 | Synonyms: receiver, administrator, trustee | Antonyms: founder, establisher | noun

liquidator:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪtər/| n. | 清盘人；(尤指)破产清盘人 | A person appointed to carry out the closing down of a company and deal with its assets and debts. | The liquidator was appointed to oversee the closure of the bankrupt company. 清盘人被任命监督这家破产公司的关闭。 | Synonyms: insolvency practitioner, official receiver | Antonyms: incorporator | noun
