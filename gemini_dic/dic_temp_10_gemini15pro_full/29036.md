cartoonists:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪsts/
noun
漫画家
A person who creates cartoons.
The newspaper employed several talented cartoonists. 这家报纸雇佣了几位才华横溢的漫画家。
近义词:comic strip artists, animators, illustrators
反义词: 无
词性:n.
