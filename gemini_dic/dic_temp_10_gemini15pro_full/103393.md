jinking:/ˈdʒɪŋkɪŋ/
noun
快速移动;躲闪;欺骗
the act of moving quickly and suddenly in different directions, especially in order to avoid being hit or seen
The rabbit's jinking run made it hard to shoot.  兔子的躲闪跑动使它很难被射中。
dodging, weaving, zigzagging

(苏格兰)  恶作剧;戏弄
