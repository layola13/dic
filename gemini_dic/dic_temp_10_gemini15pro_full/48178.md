decomposabilities:/ˌdiːkəmˌpəʊzəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可分解性；可被分解的性质|The decomposabilities of different materials vary widely. 不同材料的可分解性差异很大。|例句：The decomposabilities of organic waste are crucial for composting.  有机废物的可分解性对于堆肥至关重要。

例句：Researchers are studying the decomposabilities of various plastics to find more environmentally friendly alternatives.  研究人员正在研究各种塑料的可分解性，以寻找更环保的替代品。|近义词：degradabilities, biodegradabilities|反义词：inertness, stability|词性:noun (plural)
