corroborated:/kəˈrɑːbəreɪtɪd/
verb
证实的，确凿的
confirmed or supported by other evidence
The witness's statement was corroborated by the security camera footage.  目击者的陈述得到了监控录像的证实。
近义词: confirmed, verified, substantiated, validated
反义词: contradicted, refuted, denied, challenged
词性: past participle (of corroborate)

corroborate:/kəˈrɑːbəreɪt/
verb
证实，确凿
to confirm or support something with additional evidence
The new evidence corroborates the initial theory. 新证据证实了最初的理论。
近义词: confirm, verify, substantiate, validate
反义词: contradict, refute, deny, challenge
词性: verb
