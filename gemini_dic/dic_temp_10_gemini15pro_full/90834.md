hundreder:/ˈhʌndrədər/
n.
一百美元；百元钞票；能跑一百码的选手；得分过百的选手
A hundred dollar bill; someone or something that scores one hundred or more points, or runs one hundred yards or more.
He paid with a hundreder. 他用一张一百美元的钞票付了款。
The sprinter was a renowned hundreder.  这名短跑运动员是著名的百码赛跑健将。
synonym:  Benjamin, C-note (for $100 bill), century maker (for scoring 100)
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "small bill," "low scorer," etc. depending on the context)
词性:名词
