despecificate:/diːspɛsɪfɪkeɪt/
v.
去除特性；使不具体；概括化
To deprive of specific characteristics; to make less specific; to generalize.
The politician despecificated the details of his plan, avoiding any firm commitments.  这位政治家将他的计划细节概括化，避免任何确定的承诺。
The artist despecificated the image, blurring the lines and removing detail.  艺术家将图像概括化，模糊线条并去除细节。
近义词: generalize, abstract, summarize
反义词: specify, detail, particularize
词性: 动词
