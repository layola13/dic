fasciitis:/ˌfæʃiˈaɪtɪs/
noun
筋膜炎
Inflammation of the fascia, a band of fibrous tissue that surrounds muscles, blood vessels, and nerves.
Plantar fasciitis is a common cause of heel pain. 足底筋膜炎是足跟痛的常见原因。
