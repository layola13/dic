nollie:/ˈnɒli/ (UK) /ˈnɑːli/ (US)| n.| 豚跳, 豚翻,  诺利(滑板技巧)|A skateboarding trick where the rider uses the nose of the board to propel the board and themselves into the air, without using their back foot to pop the tail.  It is essentially the opposite of an ollie.|He landed a nollie down the five stairs.|近义词: noseflip (sometimes)|反义词: ollie|词性:noun
