overdescribes:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskraɪbz/
verb
过度描述，过分描述
describes something in too much detail, often unnecessarily.
The author overdescribes the setting, making the story drag. 作者过度描述了场景，使得故事显得拖沓。
近义词：overelaborates, embellishes, expounds
反义词：understates, summarizes, sketches
词性：动词
