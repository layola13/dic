hydrazine:/ˈhaɪdrəziːn/ |n.|肼|A colorless, flammable, corrosive liquid, N₂H₄, used as a rocket fuel and reducing agent.|Hydrazine is a powerful reducing agent. 肼是一种强还原剂。|

Hydrazine is used as a propellant in rocket engines. 肼用作火箭发动机的推进剂。|

Near synonyms: diamidogen, nitrogen hydride|Antonyms: oxidant|noun
