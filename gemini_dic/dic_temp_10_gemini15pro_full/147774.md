overpromises: /ˌoʊvərˈprɒmɪsɪz/ | v. | 过度承诺，承诺过多 | To promise more than one can or will deliver. | The company overpromises and underdelivers, leaving customers frustrated.  这家公司总是承诺过多而交付不足，让客户感到沮丧。| overcommits, exaggerates | underdelivers, underperforms | verb


overpromise: /ˌoʊvərˈprɒmɪs/ | n. | 过度承诺，过度的承诺 |  A promise that is excessive or unrealistic. | The salesman's overpromise led to disappointment when the product failed to live up to expectations.  销售人员的过度承诺导致产品未能达到预期，令人失望。| exaggeration, overcommitment | understatement, underperformance | noun
