foregoing:/fɔːrˈɡoʊɪŋ/| adj.|前述的，前面的|preceding; previously mentioned|The foregoing examples illustrate the point. 前面的例子说明了这一点。|The foregoing arguments prove the theory is flawed. 前面的论证证明这个理论是有缺陷的。|近义词：previous, preceding, aforementioned, above-mentioned|反义词：following, subsequent|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *forgoinge*, present participle of *forgon* (“to go before, precede”), from Old English *forgān* (“to go before, precede”), equivalent to *for-* + *gān* (“to go”).  The sense shifted from active "going before" to passive "having gone before" over time.
