oligarchization: /ˌɒlɪɡɑːkɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌoʊlɪɡɑːrkɪˈzeɪʃən/
| n. |寡头政治化，寡头统治化|The process by which power becomes concentrated in the hands of a small group of individuals or entities, especially in a way that is seen as detrimental to society.  权力集中在一小群个人或实体手中的过程，尤指以一种被认为对社会有害的方式。|The oligarchization of the media has led to a decline in journalistic integrity. 媒体的寡头政治化导致新闻诚信的下降。|Synonyms: concentration of power, plutocracy,  cartelisation|Antonyms: democratization, decentralization|noun

oligarchization: /ˌɒlɪɡɑːkɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌoʊlɪɡɑːrkɪˈzeɪʃən/
| n. |寡头政治化，寡头统治化| The increasing influence of a small group of wealthy and powerful individuals in a society or industry.  一小群富有和有权势的个人在社会或行业中日益增长的影响力。|The oligarchization of the tech industry raises concerns about competition and innovation. 科技行业的寡头垄断化引发了人们对竞争和创新的担忧。|Synonyms: consolidation of power, monopolization|Antonyms: diversification, distribution of power|noun
