draftsperson:/ˈdræftspɜːrsən/ /ˈdræftspɝːsən/
n.
绘图员; 制图员
A person who makes drawings, especially technical drawings, plans, etc.
The draftsperson meticulously rendered the blueprints for the new skyscraper.  绘图员一丝不苟地绘制了新摩天大楼的蓝图。

近义词: draughtsman, draftsman, drawer, designer
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "client" or "architect" in certain contexts)
词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From draft +‎ -s- +‎ person.  "Draft" in this context means a preliminary version of a writing or drawing.  "-s-" is a linking element.  "Person" denotes an individual carrying out the action.
