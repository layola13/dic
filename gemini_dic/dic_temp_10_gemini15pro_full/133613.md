nonfluorescent:/ˌnɒnfluˈɒrɛsənt/ /ˌnɑːnfluˈɔːrɛsənt/| adj.|不发荧光的，无荧光性的|Not exhibiting fluorescence; not emitting light when exposed to radiation or other stimulation.|The control group was treated with a nonfluorescent dye.|近义词:nonluminescent, nonphosphorescent|反义词:fluorescent, luminescent|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + fluorescent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "fluorescent" refers to the emission of light by a substance that has absorbed light or other electromagnetic radiation.
