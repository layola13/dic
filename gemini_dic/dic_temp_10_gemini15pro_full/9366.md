antiputrescent:/ˌæntiˌpjuːˈtrɛs(ə)nt/
adjective|抗腐的，防腐的|Preventing or slowing down the process of decay or putrefaction.|This antiputrescent cream is good for minor burns. 这种抗腐烂的药膏对轻微烧伤很有用。|Synonyms: antiseptic, preservative, disinfectant|Antonyms: putrescent, decaying, rotting|adj.
