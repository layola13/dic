hoopla:/ˈhuːplɑː/
n.|喧闹，热闹，炒作|A lot of excitement and activity surrounding an event.|例句：There was a lot of hoopla surrounding the movie's premiere. 这部电影的首映式周围有很多宣传炒作。

例句：They launched the product with a great deal of hoopla. 他们大张旗鼓地推出了这款产品。

近义词：fuss, commotion, fanfare, excitement, buzz
反义词：quiet, calm, stillness
词性: 名词
