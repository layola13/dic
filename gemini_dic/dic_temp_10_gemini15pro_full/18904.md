besmirching: /bɪˈsmɜːrtʃɪŋ/ | v. |玷污，败坏，损害（名誉）| To damage the reputation of (someone or something) in the opinion of others by saying or doing something unpleasant or disapproving. | The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire company.  这桩丑闻玷污了整个公司的声誉。 | synonyms: sully, tarnish, taint, smear, defile, disgrace | antonyms: honor, praise, extol, elevate, enhance | verb

Etymology:
From be- + smirch, from Middle English smerchen, probably of Scandinavian origin; compare Old Norse smerkja (“to smear”).
