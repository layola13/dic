bazid:/ˈbeɪzɪd/| adj.|担子菌的|Relating to or characteristic of basidia or the Basidiomycetes.|The mushroom has a bazidial layer on its gills.|Near synonyms: basidiomycetous, basidiosporous|Antonym: (No direct antonym, possibly ascomycetous relating to a different fungal group)|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *Basidium, from  Latin basis "base" + Greek -idion  "small, diminutive suffix".  Referring to the club-shaped structure bearing spores.
