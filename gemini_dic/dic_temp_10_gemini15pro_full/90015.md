hosanna:/hoʊˈzænə/ | n. int. |和散那；欢悦，赞美 | An exclamation of praise and adoration. |例句：The crowd cried "Hosanna!" as Jesus entered Jerusalem. 群众喊着“和散那！”，当耶稣进入耶路撒冷。

例句：They sang hosannas to the returning hero. 他们向凯旋的英雄唱起了赞歌。|近义词：praise, acclaim, adoration|反义词：criticism, condemnation|词性：名词,感叹词

词源: From Hebrew הוֹשִׁיעָה־נָּא  (hôšîʿâ-nā) meaning  "save, we pray," later used as a shout of joy or adoration.
