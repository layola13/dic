nonbiliously:/ˌnɒnbɪliəsli/
adjective
非胆汁性地; 无胆汁分泌地
Without bile or the vomiting of bile.
The patient vomited nonbiliously after the surgery.  手术后病人呕吐物中没有胆汁。
He experienced nonbilious nausea throughout the day. 他整天都感到恶心，但没有吐出胆汁。
近义词: abilious
反义词: bilious
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non "not."
bilious (adjective): relating to bile  来自拉丁语 bilis "bile."
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives;  来自古英语 -lice.
