flinging:/ˈflɪŋɪŋ/ | v. | 投掷，猛扔，抛 | The act of throwing something forcefully or carelessly. | He was flinging his clothes into the suitcase. 他正把衣服扔进行李箱里。| The children were flinging snowballs at each other. 孩子们互相扔雪球。 | hurling, tossing, casting | catching, gathering | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flingen, from Old Norse *flingja (“to throw, hurl, cast”), from Proto-Germanic *flinganą (“to throw”). Cognate with Swedish slunga (“to sling”), Danish slynge (“to sling”), Dutch slingeren (“to fling, sling”).
