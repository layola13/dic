annunciatory:/əˈnʌnsiəˌtɔːri/
adjective
预示的，预告的
Of or relating to an announcement; serving to announce.
The president's annunciatory tone suggested important news was forthcoming. 总统的预示性语气表明即将发布重要新闻。
The dark clouds were annunciatory of a coming storm. 乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。
synonyms:  heralding, proclaiming, predictive, premonitory
antonyms: concealing, hiding, suppressing
词性: adjective
