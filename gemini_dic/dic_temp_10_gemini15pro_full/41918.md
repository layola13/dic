cosmogony:/kɒzˈmɒɡəni/ /kɑːzˈmɑːɡəni/
noun
宇宙起源学; 宇宙进化论
A theory or story about the origin and development of the universe.
例句：Ancient Greek cosmogonies often involved the creation of the world from chaos. 古希腊的宇宙起源论通常涉及从混沌中创造世界。

例句：Modern cosmogonies are based on scientific observations and theories like the Big Bang. 现代宇宙起源论基于科学观测和理论，例如宇宙大爆炸理论。

近义词：creation myth, origin story, universe evolution
反义词: N/A
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κοσμογονία (kosmogonía), from κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe, order”) + γονή (gonḗ, “generation, birth”).
