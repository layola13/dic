family:/ˈfæməli/ | n. | 家庭；家人；家族 | A group of one or more parents and their children living together as a unit.  Can also refer to all the descendants of a common ancestor. | My family is very important to me. (我的家庭对我来说很重要。)  We have a large family gathering every year. (我们每年都会举行一次大型家庭聚会。) | 近义词: relatives, kin, household | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可能用strangers, foes) | 词性: noun
