eucalyptic:/ˌjuːkəˈlɪptɪk/
adjective
桉树的；有桉树气味的
Of or relating to a eucalyptus.  Having a smell reminiscent of eucalyptus.
The air was thick with a eucalyptic aroma. 空气中弥漫着桉树的香味。
The koala's fur smelled faintly eucalyptic.  考拉的皮毛散发着淡淡的桉树味。
近义词: eucalyptus-scented, eucalyptus-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: unscented, odorless)
词性: adjective
