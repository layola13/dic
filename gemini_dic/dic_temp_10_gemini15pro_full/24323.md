brilliantines:/ˌbrɪljənˈtiːnz/
n.
润发油；亮发剂
Oily or greasy hairdressing used to make the hair shiny.
He slicked back his hair with brilliantine. 他用润发油把头发往后梳得油光发亮。
近义词：pomade, hair oil, hairdressing
反义词：(None -  lack of a single antonym, consider "dry shampoo" or "matte styling product" as contextually opposite)
词性: noun


brilliantine:/ˌbrɪljənˈtiːn/
n.
光泽;光辉
Gloss; brilliance.
The brilliantine of the polished wood. 抛光木材的光泽。
近义词：shine, luster, sheen, gloss
反义词：dullness, mattness
词性: noun
