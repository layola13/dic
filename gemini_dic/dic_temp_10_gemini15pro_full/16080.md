bandoleer:/ˌbændəˈlɪər/| n.|子弹带，班多利尔|A belt worn over the shoulder and across the chest, typically used to carry ammunition.|The soldier slung his bandoleer across his chest. 士兵把子弹带挎在胸前。|Synonyms: cartridge belt, ammunition belt|Antonyms: None|noun
