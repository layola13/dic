angeleno's:/ˌændʒəˈlinoʊz/
noun
洛杉矶人的（名词，所有格）
A person from Los Angeles.  Specifically, it is the possessive form, indicating something belongs to or is associated with an Angeleno.
Example: The Angeleno's car was parked on Hollywood Boulevard.  洛杉矶人的车停在好莱坞大道上。
Example:  The Angeleno's love for the Dodgers is well-known. 洛杉矶人对道奇队的热爱是众所周知的。
Synonym: Los Angelan (although "Angeleno" is generally preferred)
Antonym: N/A (no direct opposite for a person from a specific place)
