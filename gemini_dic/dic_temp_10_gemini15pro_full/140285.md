oftens:/ˈɒf(ə)nz/ /ˈɔːf(ə)nz/
|adv.|经常，常常|Often used incorrectly as a plural form of "often".  "Oftens" is considered archaic or dialectal and is rarely used in modern standard English. It means "frequently" or "many times."  Modern usage favors "often."|He oftens goes to the library (archaic/dialectal). 他经常去图书馆。|近义词：frequently, often, regularly|反义词: rarely, seldom, infrequently
|词性: Adverb
