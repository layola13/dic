fissuration:/fɪʃəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 裂缝形成，龟裂 | The process of forming fissures or cracks. |  The drought caused fissuration in the dry lake bed. 干旱导致干涸的湖床出现裂缝。| 近义词： cracking, splitting, fracturing | 反义词： fusion, joining | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *fissurationem, from Latin fissus, past participle of findere "to split, cleave."
