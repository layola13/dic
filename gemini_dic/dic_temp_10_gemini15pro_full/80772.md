gossipper:/ˈɡɒsɪpə(r)/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːsɪpər/ (US)| n. |流言蜚语者；爱说闲话的人|A person who habitually spreads rumors or talks about the private matters of others.|She's such a gossipper, always talking about everyone behind their backs. 她真是个爱说闲话的人，总是在背后谈论每个人。|例句：The office gossipper spread rumors about the new employee. 办公室里爱传闲话的人散布关于新员工的谣言。|近义词：rumormonger, scandalmonger, busybody|反义词:discreet person, confidant|词性:noun
