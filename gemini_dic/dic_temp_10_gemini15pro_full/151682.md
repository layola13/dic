partitioning:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 分割，划分 | The act of dividing something into parts or sections. |  The partitioning of the hard drive created separate areas for different operating systems.  硬盘的分割为不同的操作系统创建了单独的区域。 | division, separation, segmentation | combination, unification | noun

partitioning:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənɪŋ/ | adj. | 分割的，划分的 |  Relating to or involving the act of partitioning.  |  A partitioning wall divided the room into two smaller spaces. 一堵隔墙把房间分成了两个较小的空间。 | dividing, separating, segmenting | combining, unifying | adjective
