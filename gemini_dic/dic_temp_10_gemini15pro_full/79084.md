glacial:/ˈɡleɪʃəl/ | adj. | 冰的，冰河的；冰冷的；极慢的 | Relating to glaciers or ice; extremely slow; unfriendly and unemotional. |  The glacial landscape was breathtaking. 冰川景观令人叹为观止。

The service at the restaurant was glacial.  这家餐馆的服务速度极慢。 | 近义词: icy, frigid, freezing, slow, sluggish | 反义词: warm, hot, rapid, speedy | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *glacialis*, from glacies "ice".
