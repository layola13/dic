pachyderm:/ˈpækɪˌdɜːrm/
n.
厚皮动物
A very large mammal with thick skin, such as an elephant, rhinoceros, or hippopotamus.
Elephants and rhinos are pachyderms. 大象和犀牛都是厚皮动物。
Synonyms: elephant, rhinoceros, hippopotamus
Antonyms: thin-skinned animal
