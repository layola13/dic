malaya:/məˈleɪə/ | adj. n.| 马来亚的；马来人；马来语 |Relating to Malaya, its people, or their language.  Formerly used as a noun to refer to the Malay Peninsula or the Federation of Malaya (now part of Malaysia).|  He speaks fluent Malaya. 他讲一口流利的马来语。| The Malaya tiger is endangered.  马来亚虎濒临灭绝。| synonyms: Malayan, Malaysian (in some contexts) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could be considered conceptually opposite to "non-Malayan") |adjective, noun
