decontaminations:/ˌdiːkənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|净化，去污，消毒|The process of removing or neutralizing contaminants from a person, object, or area.  The hospital carried out thorough decontaminations after the virus outbreak.|例句：The decontaminations of the lab took several hours.  实验室的净化工作花了几个小时。

例句：Multiple decontaminations were necessary to ensure the area was safe. 为了确保该区域的安全，需要多次去污。|近义词：cleansings, purifications, sanitizations|反义词: contaminations, pollutions|词性:名词

词源:  From de- (meaning "removal, reversal") + contamination (from contaminate, from Latin contaminare "to pollute, defile").
