bauxite:/ˈbɔːksaɪt/ | n. |铝土矿|A rock material that is the chief source of aluminum. It consists largely of hydrated alumina with variable proportions of iron oxides.|Bauxite is refined to produce alumina, which is then used to make aluminum. 铝土矿被提炼成氧化铝，然后用于制造铝。|Synonyms: aluminum ore, laterite |Antonyms: N/A |noun

bauxite:/ˈbɔːksaɪt/ | n. |铝土矿|A sedimentary rock consisting of aluminum hydroxide and aluminum oxide, the principal ore of aluminum.|Jamaica is rich in bauxite deposits. 牙买加铝土矿储量丰富。|Synonyms: aluminum ore |Antonyms: N/A |noun
