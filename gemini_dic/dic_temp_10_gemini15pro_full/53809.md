disconfirmatory:/ˌdɪskənˈfɜːrmətɔːri/
adjective
不证实的，反证的
Tending to disprove or invalidate a hypothesis or expectation.
The experiment yielded disconfirmatory results, challenging the prevailing theory.  这个实验产生了反证结果，对盛行的理论提出了挑战。

The disconfirmatory evidence led the scientists to abandon their original hypothesis. 反证证据使科学家们放弃了他们最初的假设。

contradictory, refuting, invalidating
confirmatory, corroborating, validating
adjective
