braziery: /ˈbreɪʒəri/ | n. | 铜匠工艺; 铜器; 铜器作坊 |  The art or craft of making articles of brass; also, brass articles collectively; a place where brass articles are made. | The museum had a fascinating exhibit on medieval braziery, showcasing intricate candlesticks and ornate helmets.  博物馆举办了一个引人入胜的中世纪铜器展览，展示了复杂的烛台和华丽的头盔。 | 近义词: brasswork, brassware | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与其制作材料相反的词汇，例如 ironwork 铁器) | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Old French "brasier," meaning "brazier" (a metal container for holding burning coals), from "braise" meaning "burning coals."  Ultimately from Latin "pruna" meaning "live coal."
