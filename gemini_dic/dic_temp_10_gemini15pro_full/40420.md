contraremonstrant:/ˌkɒntrəˈmɒnstrənt/ |ˌkɑːntrəˈmɑːnstrənt/| n. adj.|抗辩者，抗议者；抗辩的，抗议的|A person who protests or opposes something, especially a religious doctrine; protesting or opposing, especially a religious doctrine.  Derived from the Dutch "contra-remonstrant," meaning "against the Remonstrants," referring to a group who opposed the teachings of Jacobus Arminius in the early 17th century.|

例句：The contraremonstrants argued vehemently against the Remonstrants' views on predestination. 抗辩者强烈反对抗议者关于宿命论的观点。

例句：His contraremonstrant stance on the new policy surprised many. 他对新政策的反对立场让许多人感到惊讶。

近义词：opponent, objector, dissenter, protestor|
反义词：supporter, proponent, advocate|
词性：noun, adjective|
