lithoclast: /ˈlɪθəˌklæst/ | n. | 岩屑，碎屑岩 | A fragment of pre-existing rock. A lithoclast is a clast of rock. | Example: The conglomerate contained numerous lithoclasts of various sizes and compositions. 这块砾岩包含许多大小和成分各异的岩屑。

Example:  Lithoclasts in the sandstone indicate a nearby source area.  砂岩中的岩屑表明附近存在源区。| 近义词: rock fragment, clast | 反义词:  None (no direct opposite) | 词性: noun

词源: litho- (Greek, "stone") + -clast (Greek, "broken")
