flummox:/ˈflʌməks/| v. |使困惑，使狼狈|To confuse someone so much that they do not know what to do or say. |例句：The complicated instructions completely flummoxed me. 这些复杂的说明彻底把我弄糊涂了。

例句：I was flummoxed by his sudden question. 他的突然提问让我不知所措。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, perplex, baffle|反义词：clarify, enlighten|词性：动词
