half-rebellious:/ˌhæf rɪˈbɛljəs/
adjective
半叛逆的，略带叛逆的
Partially or slightly rebellious; showing some, but not complete, resistance to authority or convention.
She had a half-rebellious, half-curious look on her face. 她脸上带着一种半叛逆、半好奇的表情。
Near synonyms:  disobedient, defiant, resistant, unruly, insubordinate
Near antonyms: obedient, compliant, submissive, conforming, docile
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
half (Old English healf) + rebellious (from Old French rebelle + -ous)
