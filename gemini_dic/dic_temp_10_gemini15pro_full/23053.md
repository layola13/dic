bouncer:/ˈbaʊnsər/| n.|保镖，保安；(酒吧等的)门卫；跳跃的人或物|A person employed by a nightclub or similar establishment to prevent troublemakers from entering or to eject them from the premises.  A person or thing that bounces.|The bouncer wouldn't let them in.  保安不让他们进去。 The child loved the bouncy castle, acting like a little bouncer. 这孩子喜欢充气城堡，像个小弹跳者一样。|近义词：doorman, guard, security|反义词:customer, guest|noun
