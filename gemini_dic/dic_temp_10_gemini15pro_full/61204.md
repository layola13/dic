elvers:/ˈɛlvərz/| n. |幼鳗，鳗线|Young eels, especially those migrating upstream from the sea.|例句：The elvers are migrating upstream. 幼鳗正在洄游到上游。

例句：Fishermen catch elvers for food and aquaculture. 渔民捕捞鳗线用于食用和水产养殖。

近义词：young eels, glass eels|反义词:adult eels|词性:noun
