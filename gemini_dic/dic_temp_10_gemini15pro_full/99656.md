interlending:/ˌɪntərˈlɛndɪŋ/| n.|馆际互借|The act of lending and borrowing materials between libraries.|The university encourages interlending to provide students with access to a wider range of resources. 大学鼓励馆际互借，以便为学生提供更广泛的资源。|Synonyms: resource sharing, library cooperation|Antonyms: independent collection development|noun
