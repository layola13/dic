osteochondroma:/ˌɒstioʊkɒnˈdroʊmə/ | n. | 骨软骨瘤 | A benign tumor composed of bone and cartilage, usually occurring near the end of a long bone.  It is the most common benign bone tumor. |  He was diagnosed with an osteochondroma on his femur. 他被诊断出股骨骨软骨瘤。 | synonyms: exostosis, cartilage-capped bony projection | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific tumor type.  Could consider general antonyms for "benign" such as malignant or cancerous) | noun
