词源：
This term originated in American slang in the 1930s, derived from the popular radio crime drama "Gang Busters."  The show's fast-paced action and dramatic arrests led to the phrase being used to describe anything happening with great speed and success.
这个词起源于20世纪30年代的美国俚语，来源于当时流行的广播犯罪剧《Gang Busters》。该剧节奏快，逮捕行动戏剧性，因此这个短语被用来形容任何快速而成功的事情。
