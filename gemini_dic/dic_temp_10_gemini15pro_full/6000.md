amchitka: /æmˈtʃɪtkə/
proper noun
阿姆奇特卡岛
A small island in the Aleutian Islands of Alaska, known as the site of underground nuclear tests conducted by the United States.
The US government detonated three underground nuclear tests on Amchitka Island. 美国政府在阿姆奇特卡岛进行了三次地下核试验。
