boyo:/ˈbɔɪoʊ/| n.|小子，伙计 (称呼)|A term of address for a boy or young man, often used affectionately or playfully. It's particularly common in Ireland, Scotland, and parts of England.|例句：Alright, boyo, let's get to work. 好吧，小子，我们开始工作吧。

例句：That boyo's got some nerve! 那小子真有胆量！|近义词：lad, chap, fellow, mate|反义词:girl, lass (female equivalents, not true opposites)|Noun
