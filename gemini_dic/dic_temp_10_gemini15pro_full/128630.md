nickelite: /ˈnɪkəlaɪt/ | n. | 红砷镍矿 | A mineral consisting of nickel arsenide (NiAs), typically reddish in color. |  Nickelite is a common ore of nickel. 红砷镍矿是镍的常见矿石。| Near synonyms:  kupfernickel, nickel arsenide | Antonyms: None | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Nickel + -ite (a suffix used in mineralogy to denote salts or other derivatives of an element). Nickel is named after the German word Kupfernickel (literally 'copper devil'), which referred to a nickel ore that resembled copper but yielded no copper.
