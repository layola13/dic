doziness:/ˈdoʊzinəs/| n.|困倦，昏昏欲睡|A state of being drowsy; sleepiness.|例句：The doziness caused by the medication made it difficult to concentrate. 药物引起的困倦使人难以集中注意力。

例句：He fought off the doziness and tried to stay awake during the lecture. 他努力克服困倦，试图在讲座期间保持清醒。|近义词：drowsiness, sleepiness, lethargy, sluggishness|反义词：alertness, wakefulness, energy, vigor|词性:名词

词源: From doze + -ness.  Ultimately from Old Norse *dusa.
