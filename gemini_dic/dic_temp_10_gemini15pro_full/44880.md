crybaby:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/| n. |爱哭鬼，哭鼻子的人|A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains frequently. |例句：Stop being such a crybaby and deal with it! 别再像个爱哭鬼一样了，去面对它！

例句：He was teased as a crybaby throughout his childhood. 他整个童年时期都被嘲笑为爱哭鬼。|近义词：whiner, complainer, sook (Australian)|反义词: stoic, tough guy/girl|词性: noun
