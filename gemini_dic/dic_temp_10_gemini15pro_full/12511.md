aspersorium:/ˌæspərˈsɔːriəm/
n.
洒水器，圣水器
A vessel for holding holy water, especially one used for sprinkling.
The priest dipped his fingers in the aspersorium and sprinkled the congregation with holy water.  神父将手指浸入圣水器中，向会众洒圣水。
近义词:sprinkler, holy water vessel
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

aspersorium:/ˌæspərˈsɔːriəm/
Etymology: From Latin *aspersōrium*, from *aspersus*, past participle of *aspergō* (“to sprinkle”).  *Aspergō* is from *ad* (“to, towards”) + *spargō* (“to scatter, sprinkle”).
词源：源自拉丁语 *aspersōrium*，来自 *aspersus*，*aspergō*（“洒”）的过去分词。 *Aspergō* 来自 *ad*（“向，朝向”）+ *spargō*（“散布，洒”）。
