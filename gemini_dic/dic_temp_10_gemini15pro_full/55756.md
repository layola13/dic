diverse:/daɪˈvɜːrs/ (US), /daɪˈvɜːs/ (UK)| adj.|不同的，多样的，形形色色的|Showing a great deal of variety; of many different kinds.|The city has a diverse population.  这座城市人口构成十分多元。|例句：The company employs a diverse workforce.  这家公司雇佣了各种各样的员工。|例句：They offer a diverse range of products. 他们提供各种各样的产品。|近义词：varied, different, multiple, various|反义词：similar, homogeneous, uniform|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Middle English *divers*, borrowed from Old French *divers*, from Latin *dīversus* (“turned different ways, opposite, unlike, various”), past participle of *dīvertere* (“to turn aside, turn in different directions”), from *dī-* (“aside, apart”) + *vertere* (“to turn”).
