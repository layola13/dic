oversensitizing:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使过分敏感；使过敏
Making someone or something excessively sensitive to particular stimuli.
The constant criticism is oversensitizing her to any negative feedback.  不断的批评使她对任何负面反馈都过于敏感。
He worried that the training was oversensitizing the dog to loud noises. 他担心这种训练会使狗对巨大的噪音过于敏感。
近义词:sensitizing, exaggerating
反义词:desensitizing, numbing
词性:v.

oversensitive:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/
adjective
过分敏感的；过敏的
Excessively sensitive; easily offended or upset.
She's oversensitive to criticism. 她对批评过于敏感。
My skin is oversensitive to the sun. 我的皮肤对阳光过敏。
近义词:touchy, thin-skinned
反义词:insensitive, thick-skinned
词性:adj.

oversensitivity:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/
noun
过分敏感；过敏
The state of being excessively sensitive.
Her oversensitivity made it difficult to have an honest conversation. 她过于敏感，很难进行坦诚的交谈。
Oversensitivity to certain foods can cause allergic reactions. 对某些食物的过度敏感会导致过敏反应。
近义词:sensitivity, touchiness
反义词:insensitivity, indifference
词性:n.


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "sensitive" (from Latin "sentire" - to feel) + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
