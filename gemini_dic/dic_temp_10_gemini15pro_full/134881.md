nonmotion:/ˌnɑːnˈmoʊʃən/
noun
不动; 静止
lack of motion; stillness.
The photograph captured the absolute nonmotion of the sleeping cat.  这张照片捕捉到了熟睡的猫一动不动的状态。
The long exposure photograph blurred everything except the objects in nonmotion. 长时间曝光的照片模糊了除静止物体之外的所有东西。
synonyms: stillness, immobility, inactivity, quiescence
antonyms: motion, movement, activity, dynamism
