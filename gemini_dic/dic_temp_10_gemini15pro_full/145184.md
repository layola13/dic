overaward:/ˌoʊvərəˈwɔːrd/
verb
过度奖励；奖励过多
to give a greater reward than is deserved or appropriate.
The scholarship committee overawarded the student, giving her more money than was allocated for the prize. 奖学金委员会过度奖励了这个学生，给她的钱比奖金分配的要多。
The judge overawarded damages to the plaintiff. 法官判给原告的赔偿金过高。
synonyms: overcompensate, overpay
antonyms: underpay, undercompensate
v.

noun
过度奖励；奖励过多的情况
an award that is greater than deserved or appropriate.
The overaward was later rectified by the committee. 委员会后来纠正了过度奖励的情况。
The family contested the overaward of damages to the other party.  这家人对另一方获得过高的损害赔偿提出了异议。
synonyms: overpayment, excess payment
antonyms: underpayment, insufficient payment
n.
