ETYMOLOGY->
"Dreadlocks" originated in Jamaica and comes from "dread" (fear or awe) and "locks" (hair). Rastafarians wore dreadlocks as a symbol of their spiritual beliefs, and the hairstyle became associated with fear or respect.
