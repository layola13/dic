burdenless:/ˈbɜːrdənlɪs/
adjective
无负担的；轻松的
free from burdens; light
例句：She felt burdenless after confessing her secret. 她坦白了秘密后感到轻松。

例句：He walked with a burdenless stride. 他步伐轻快地走着。
近义词：untroubled, carefree, lighthearted, unencumbered
反义词：burdened, weighed down, oppressed
词性:adjective
