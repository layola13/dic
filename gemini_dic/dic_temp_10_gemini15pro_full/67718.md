exuscitate: /ˌɛksʌsɪˈteɪt/
verb
使苏醒；唤醒；激起
To rouse from inactivity or unconsciousness; to revive or stimulate.
The doctor tried to exuscitate the patient after the accident. 医生试图在事故后使病人苏醒。
The coach's speech exuscitated the team's fighting spirit. 教练的讲话激起了球队的斗志。

近义词: revive, awaken, arouse, stimulate, invigorate
反义词: suppress, subdue, deaden, calm, soothe
词性: verb
