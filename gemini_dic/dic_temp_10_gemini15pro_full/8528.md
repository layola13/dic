anticensoriousness:/ˌæntɪˌsɛnsɔːrˈiəsnɪs/
|n.| 不爱批评；宽容 |The anticensoriousness of the community allowed for a diversity of opinions.| 这个社区的宽容使得各种观点得以存在。|
|例句：His anticensoriousness made him a popular figure. 他不爱批评的性格使他很受欢迎。|
|例句：Her anticensoriousness was remarkable given her position of authority.  鉴于她的权威地位，她的宽容令人瞩目。|
|近义词：tolerance, forbearance, leniency, indulgence |
|反义词：censoriousness, criticalness, judgmentalness |
|词性: noun|

词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  反对，对抗
censorious (adjective): severely critical 苛刻的，吹毛求疵的
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives  构成名词的后缀
