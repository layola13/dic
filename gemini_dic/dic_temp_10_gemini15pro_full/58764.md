dunking:/ˈdʌŋkɪŋ/| n. v.|扣篮，浸泡|The act of dipping or submerging something into liquid.  Also, in basketball, the act of scoring by putting the ball through the hoop from above.|He showed off his dunking skills at the basketball game. 他在篮球比赛中展示了他的扣篮技巧。|近义词：dipping, immersing, submerging (for general use); slam dunk (for basketball)|反义词:lifting, removing|noun, verb

dunking:/ˈdʌŋkɪŋ/| n. v.|浸泡，泡|The act of soaking something in liquid.|She enjoys dunking biscuits in her tea. 她喜欢把饼干泡在茶里吃。|近义词：soaking, steeping|反义词:drying|noun, verb
