fanfiction:/ˌfænˈfɪkʃ(ə)n/
n.
同人小说
Fiction written by fans of a particular work (such as a movie, TV show, or video game), using existing characters and situations to create new stories.
She enjoys reading fanfiction based on her favorite book series. 她喜欢读她最喜欢的系列丛书的同人小说。
My friend writes fanfiction about her favorite K-pop group. 我的朋友写她最喜欢的韩国流行音乐组合的同人小说。
近义词:fandom, fan work
反义词:original fiction, canon
词性:noun
