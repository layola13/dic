endogenesis:/ˌɛndəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.|内生，内源|The development or origin of something within an organism, tissue, or cell.|例句：Endogenesis is the process of rock formation from within the Earth.  岩石的内生作用是指地球内部形成岩石的过程。|例句：The study focuses on the endogenesis of cancer cells. 这项研究的重点是癌细胞的内生。|近义词：internal development, internal formation, internal origin|反义词：exogenesis|词性: 名词
