deescalates:/ˌdiːˈɛskəˌleɪts/| v. | 缓和，降级，降低|  To reduce the intensity, scale, or scope of something, especially a conflict or crisis. | The police officer deescalated the situation by speaking calmly to the angry protesters. 警察通过平静地与愤怒的抗议者交谈，缓和了局势。| synonyms: lessen, mitigate, reduce, diminish, alleviate, calm down, cool down | antonyms: escalate, aggravate, intensify, worsen, exacerbate | verb
