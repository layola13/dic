bullishnesses:/ˈbʊlɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
看涨；乐观情绪；牛市
The state of being bullish; an optimistic outlook, especially about market conditions.
Investors are showing bullishnesses about the company's future prospects.  投资者对公司未来前景表现出乐观态度。
optimism, confidence, buoyancy
bearishness, pessimism, negativity
