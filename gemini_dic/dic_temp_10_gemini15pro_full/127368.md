ne'er:/nɛər/
|adv.| 从不，永不 |Archaic contraction of never.  It is rarely used in modern English except in poetry or for humorous effect.|例句：He ne'er will return. 他永不回来。|近义词：never|反义词:always|词性:副词

PHRASE->
Ne'er a word did he speak. = 他一句话也没说。
Ne'er have I seen such beauty. = 我从未见过如此美丽的东西。
Ne'er shall I forget that day. = 我永远不会忘记那一天。
Ne'er again will I make that mistake. = 我再也不会犯那个错误了。
Ne'er a moment did she hesitate. = 她一刻也没有犹豫。


词源:  From Middle English *nere, a contraction of *ne were (“were not”, “was not”). This in turn comes from Old English *ne wǣre.

注意事项:  Ne'er is archaic and mostly used in poetry or for stylistic effect.  Avoid using it in everyday conversation or formal writing as it sounds outdated.
