inhume:/ɪnˈhjuːm/ /ɪnˈjum/ | vt. |埋葬，安葬|To bury (a corpse) in the earth; inter.|例句：The king was inhumed with great ceremony. 国王被隆重安葬。

例句：They inhumed the treasure in a secret location. 他们把宝藏埋葬在一个秘密地点。

近义词：bury, inter, entomb|反义词:exhume, disinter|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inhumāre, from Latin in- (“in, into”) + humāre (“to bury, cover with earth”), from humus (“earth, ground”).
