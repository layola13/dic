neem:/niːm/| n.| 印楝，楝树|A fast-growing tropical evergreen tree (Azadirachta indica) native to India, having a wide-spreading crown, small white flowers, and smooth brown fruit.|The neem tree is highly valued for its medicinal properties.|近义词:margosa, Indian lilac|反义词:|noun
