heparin:/ˈhɛpərɪn/| n.|肝素|A substance found in the liver and other tissues which inhibits blood coagulation; it is used as an anticoagulant in the treatment of thrombosis.|The doctor prescribed heparin to prevent blood clots.|Synonyms: anticoagulant, blood thinner|Antonyms: coagulant, procoagulant|noun
