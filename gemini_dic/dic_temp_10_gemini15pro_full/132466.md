nondeprivations:/ˌnɒndɛprɪˈveɪʃənz/
noun
非剥夺; 未剥夺的状态
The state of not being deprived of something; the absence of deprivation.
The study focused on the nondeprivations experienced by the wealthy, highlighting their access to resources and opportunities. 这项研究的重点是有钱人未被剥夺的状态，突出了他们获得资源和机会的情况。
Near synonyms:  abundance, affluence, prosperity, wealth, privilege
Antonyms: deprivations, poverty, lack, want, destitution
词性: 名词
