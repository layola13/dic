longly:/ˈlɒŋli/ | adv. | 寂寞地，孤单地 | In a lonely manner; expressing or experiencing loneliness. | She stared longly out the window, missing her family. 她寂寞地凝视着窗外，思念着家人。|  He walked longly down the empty street. 他孤单地走在空旷的街道上。| 近义词: lonesomely, solitarily | 反义词: sociably, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From "long" (渴望) + "-ly" (副词后缀).  While grammatically correct, "longly" is considered archaic and rarely used in modern English.  "Loneliness" (名词) and "lonely" (形容词) are the preferred forms.
