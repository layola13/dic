aerophyte:/ˈɛəroʊˌfaɪt/ | n. | 气生植物；空气植物 | A plant that derives its moisture and nutrients from the air and rain; an epiphyte.  |  Certain orchids are aerophytes, clinging to branches high in the rainforest canopy.  某些兰花是气生植物，依附在雨林冠层高处的树枝上。

Aerophytes thrive in humid environments. 气生植物在潮湿的环境中茁壮成长。 | 近义词: epiphyte, air plant | 反义词: terrestrial plant | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + -phyte (from Greek φυτόν (phytón, “plant”)).
