From non- (not) + intermittent (occurring at irregular intervals) + -ness (forming nouns expressing state or quality).  The root "intermittent" comes from Latin "intermittentem" (present participle of intermittere), meaning "to discontinue, leave off, cease," from "inter-" (between) + "mittere" (to send, let go).
