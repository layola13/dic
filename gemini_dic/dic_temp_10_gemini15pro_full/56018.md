do someone's bidding:/duː ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈbɪdɪŋ/
|v. phrase|
|执行某人的命令；听从某人的吩咐；按某人的要求去做|
|Carrying out someone's orders or instructions; acting according to someone's wishes.|

|The soldiers did the general's bidding without question. 士兵们毫不迟疑地执行了将军的命令。|

|He spent his life doing his father's bidding. 他一生都在听从父亲的吩咐。|

|近义词: obey, comply with, follow orders, carry out instructions, execute commands|
|反义词: disobey, defy, refuse, resist|
|词性: 动词短语|
