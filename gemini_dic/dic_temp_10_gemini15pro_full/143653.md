outkissed:/ˌaʊtˈkɪst/
verb
胜过亲吻；吻得更多
To kiss someone more or better than someone else.
She outkissed him at the wedding, showing everyone how much in love they were.  她在婚礼上吻他吻得比他吻她还多，向所有人展示了他们有多么相爱。
He tried to outkiss her, but she playfully pushed him away. 他想吻得比她更多，但她开玩笑地把他推开了。
Synonyms: outdo in kissing, surpass in kissing
Antonyms: be outkissed, be surpassed in kissing
词性: 动词
