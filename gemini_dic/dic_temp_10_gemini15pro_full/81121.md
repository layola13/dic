grammaticism: /ɡrəˈmætɪsɪzəm/| n. |语法主义；语法癖|A strong adherence to grammatical rules, sometimes to an excessive or pedantic degree.  An instance of strict adherence to grammatical rules.|He was known for his grammaticism, correcting everyone's speech. 他以语法癖著称，总是纠正每个人的讲话。|  Her essay was filled with grammaticisms, making it sound stilted. 她的文章充满了语法主义，听起来很生硬。|近义词：pedantry, purism|反义词: colloquialism, informality|词性:noun
