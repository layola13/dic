oiler:/ˈɔɪlər/ | n. |加油工；注油器；油轮|A person or device that oils something.  A ship designed to carry oil. |例句：The oiler lubricated the machine's gears. 加油工给机器的齿轮上了润滑油。

例句：The oiler spilled some oil on the deck. 油轮在甲板上洒了一些油。|近义词：lubricator, tanker (for ship)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

oiler:/ˈɔɪlər/ | n. |滑油的动物|An animal that oils its fur or skin, such as a bird.|例句：The duck is an oiler, keeping its feathers waterproof. 鸭子是一种会涂油的动物，它能让羽毛防水。
