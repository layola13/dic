leptology:/lɛpˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | λεπτολογία (希腊语) | The study of small, thin, or slender things, especially scales, as in the scales of fish or reptiles.  It also refers to the excessive attention to minor details, often to the detriment of the overall picture. |  The scientist's leptology allowed him to identify the fish species from a single scale.  这个科学家对细微事物的研究使他能够从一片鱼鳞中识别出鱼的种类。| synonyms:  micrology, minutiae | antonyms:  summary, overview | noun
