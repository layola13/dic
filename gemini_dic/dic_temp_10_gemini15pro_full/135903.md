nonpsychics:/ˌnɒnˈsaɪkɪks/
noun
非通灵者
People who do not possess psychic abilities.
Many nonpsychics are skeptical of paranormal phenomena. 许多非通灵者对超自然现象持怀疑态度。
Nonpsychics often rely on logic and reason. 非通灵者通常依赖逻辑和理性。
Synonyms:ordinary people, regular people
Antonyms:psychics, mediums, clairvoyants
