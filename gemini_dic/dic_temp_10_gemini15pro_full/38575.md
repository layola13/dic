concomitantly:/kənˈkɒmɪtəntli/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtəntli/
adverb
同时地；伴随地
occurring or existing at the same time
The drug's side effects concomitantly cause nausea and headaches. 这种药物的副作用同时引起恶心和头痛。
Concomitantly with the rise in unemployment, crime rates also increased.  随着失业率的上升，犯罪率也随之增加。
近义词: simultaneously, concurrently, alongside, together with
反义词: separately, independently, individually
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin concomitantem (nominative concomitans), present participle of concomitari ("to accompany"), from com- ("together") + comitari ("to accompany").
