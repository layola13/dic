lithographies:/lɪˈθɒɡrəfiz/
n.
光刻技术；平版印刷术
the process of printing from a flat stone or metal plate on which the image areas are ink-receptive and the non-image areas are ink-repellent.
Modern microchip manufacturing relies heavily on advanced lithographies. 现代微芯片制造严重依赖先进的光刻技术。
synonyms:  printmaking, lithography, offset printing
antonyms:  N/A
noun
