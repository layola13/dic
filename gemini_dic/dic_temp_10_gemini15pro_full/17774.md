bedlike: /ˈbedlaɪk/
adjective
像床的; 适于做床的
Resembling a bed; suitable for use as a bed.
The sofa was so comfortable and bedlike that I fell asleep.  这个沙发如此舒适，像床一样，我睡着了。
The wide, flat rock formed a bedlike surface.  这块宽阔平坦的岩石形成了一个像床一样的表面。
近义词: bed-shaped, comfortable, cozy
反义词: uncomfortable, uninviting
词性: adjective
