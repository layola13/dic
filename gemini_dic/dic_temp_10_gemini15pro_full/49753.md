demoisturizing: /ˌdiːmɔɪstʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 去湿的，保湿的 | Removing moisture; having the effect of reducing moisture.  This word can be confusing, as it seems to contradict itself. While "moisturizing" adds moisture, "demoisturizing" removes it. Think of it as "de-" meaning "to remove" and "moisturizing" referring to the moisture being removed. | This demoisturizing cream helps control oil production.  这款去湿面霜有助于控制油脂分泌。|  drying, dehydrating | moisturizing, hydrating | adjective
