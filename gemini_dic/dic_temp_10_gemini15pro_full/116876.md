mayan: /ˈmaɪən/ | adj. n.|玛雅的；玛雅人|Relating to the Mayan civilization, an ancient Mesoamerican culture.  A member of an indigenous people of Yucatan and adjacent areas of Mexico, Guatemala, Belize, and Honduras, descendants of the Maya civilization.|例句：The Mayan calendar was incredibly complex. 玛雅历法非常复杂。|近义词：Mesoamerican|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: adjective, noun

mayan: /ˈmaɪən/ | n.|玛雅人|A member of a Mayan people.|例句：Many Mayans still speak Mayan languages. 许多玛雅人仍然说玛雅语。|近义词：indigenous people|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
