frigidities:/frɪˈdʒɪdɪtiz/| n. | 冷淡，冷漠，性冷淡|A state of coldness, especially emotional or sexual unresponsiveness.  It often refers to a lack of desire or pleasure in sexual activity.|Her emotional frigidities made it difficult to connect with her.|Synonyms: coldness, aloofness, indifference, frigidity|Antonyms: warmth, passion, ardor|noun

frigidities:/frɪˈdʒɪdɪtiz/| n. | 冷漠，缺乏热情|A state of lacking warmth or enthusiasm in any area, not just sexually. It can refer to a general lack of interest or engagement.|The frigidities of the political climate made it difficult to find common ground.|Synonyms: apathy, indifference, detachment|Antonyms: enthusiasm, fervor, passion|noun
