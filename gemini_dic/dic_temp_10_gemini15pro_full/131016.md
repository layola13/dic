nonauditory:/ˌnɒnˈɔːdɪtəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈɔːdɪtɔːri/
adjective
非听觉的
Not related to hearing or the sense of hearing.
The owl uses nonauditory cues, such as air pressure changes, to locate prey. 猫头鹰利用非听觉线索，例如气压变化，来定位猎物。

The brain processes nonauditory sensory information like touch and sight. 大脑处理非听觉感官信息，如触觉和视觉。

近义词: nonaural
反义词: auditory

词性: adjective
