overbake:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪk/| v. |烤过头，烘烤过久|To bake (food) for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.| I overbaked the cookies and they were hard as rocks. 我把饼干烤过头了，它们硬得像石头一样。|The cake was slightly overbaked. 蛋糕稍微烤过头了。|近义词：burn, scorch|反义词：underbake|词性:动词
