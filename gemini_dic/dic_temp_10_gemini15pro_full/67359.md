extensional:/ɪkˈstɛnʃənəl/| adj.|外延的，延展的|Denoting a relationship between a word and the set of entities it refers to, rather than the qualities or properties those entities share.  Relating to or based on the denotation of a term.|例句：An extensional definition lists the members of the class the word denotes.  一个外延定义列出了该词所指类的成员。

例句：Extensional properties, unlike intensional ones, are not preserved under logical equivalence.  外延属性不像内涵属性，在逻辑等价下不保留。|近义词：denotative, explicit|反义词：intensional, implicit|词性:adjective
