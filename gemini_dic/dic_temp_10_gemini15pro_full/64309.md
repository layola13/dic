equivocated: /ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtɪd/
verb
含糊其辞；模棱两可地说；说谎
used ambiguously or unclearly, often to mislead or avoid commitment
The politician equivocated when asked about his stance on the controversial issue.  这位政客在被问及他对争议问题的立场时含糊其辞。
He equivocated about the source of the funding. 他对资金来源含糊其辞。
近义词: prevaricated, evaded, hedged, waffled
反义词: stated clearly, affirmed, committed, asserted
词性: verb (past tense)


equivocate: /ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪt/
verb
含糊其辞；模棱两可地说；说谎
to use ambiguous or unclear language, often to mislead or avoid commitment
She equivocates whenever the discussion turns to money. 每当讨论转向金钱时，她总是含糊其辞。
Don't equivocate with me – tell me the truth!  别跟我含糊其辞——告诉我真相！
近义词: prevaricate, evade, hedge, waffle
反义词: be direct, be straightforward
词性: verb


词源:  From Late Latin aequivocatus, past participle of aequivocari (“to use words of double meaning”), from Latin aequus (“equal”) + vocare (“to call”).
