bloodletting: /ˈblʌdˌlɛtɪŋ/
noun
放血； кровопускание (Russian); 사혈 (Korean); Aderlass (German)
The practice of drawing blood from a patient to cure or prevent illness.
Bloodletting was a common medical practice in the past.  过去，放血是一种常见的医疗方法。
Bloodletting was used to treat a variety of ailments. 放血疗法曾被用于治疗各种疾病。
近义词: phlebotomy, venesection
反义词: blood transfusion, infusion
