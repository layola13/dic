heiress-ship:/ˈɛərɪsˌʃɪp/
n.
女继承人身份，继承权（尤指女性）
The state or condition of being an heiress; the right of inheriting property, rank, or title as an heiress.
She assumed the heiress-ship of her father's vast estate. 她继承了父亲的庞大遗产。
synonyms: inheritance, heritage, legacy, birthright
antonyms: disinheritance
noun
