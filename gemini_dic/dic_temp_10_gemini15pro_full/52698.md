digraph:/ˈdaɪɡræf/| n.|双字母，二合字母|A digraph is a pair of characters used to represent a single sound, such as "ph" in "phone" or "th" in "thin".|The digraph "ch" can represent different sounds, as in "church" and "character".  这两个字母ch可以代表不同的发音，例如在church和character中。|synonym: ligature|antonym: monograph|noun
