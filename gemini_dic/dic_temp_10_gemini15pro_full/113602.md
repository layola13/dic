macerate: /ˈmæsəreɪt/ | v. | 浸渍；软化；消瘦 | To soften or break down by soaking in liquid; to become thin and weak. | The chef macerated the fruit in wine overnight. 厨师将水果在葡萄酒中浸泡了一夜。

The strawberries were macerated in sugar and balsamic vinegar. 草莓在糖和香醋中浸渍。 | 近义词: soak, steep, soften, weaken | 反义词: harden, strengthen, dry | 词性: 动词

macerates: /ˈmæsəreɪts/ | v. | 浸渍；软化；消瘦（第三人称单数）|  Third-person singular present tense form of "macerate". | He macerates the dried herbs in warm water before using them in his tea. 他在将干草药用于茶之前，先用温水浸泡。

She macerates the chicken in a buttermilk brine before frying it. 她把鸡肉浸泡在酪乳盐水中，然后再炸。 | 近义词: soaks, steeps, softens, weakens | 反义词: hardens, strengthens, dries | 词性: 动词


词源: From Latin *maceratus*, past participle of *macerare* ("to make soft, steep, soak"), from *macer* ("lean, thin").
