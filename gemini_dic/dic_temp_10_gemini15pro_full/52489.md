dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. |营养师，营养学家 |A health professional who gives advice on diet and nutrition.  A dietitian helps people improve their health by recommending appropriate diets. |例句：The dietitian advised me to eat more fruits and vegetables. 营养师建议我多吃水果和蔬菜。

例句：My doctor referred me to a registered dietitian to help manage my diabetes. 我的医生将我推荐给了一位注册营养师来帮助我控制糖尿病。|近义词：nutritionist |反义词: (没有直接的反义词) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diététicien*, from *diététique* (“dietetics”),  from Greek *διαιτητικός (diaitētikos)*, from *δίαιτα (diaita)* (“manner of living, diet”).
