confirmedly:/kənˈfɜːrmɪdli/
adv.
确定地，无疑地
in a way that shows something is definitely true
He confirmedly stated his intention to resign. 他明确地表明了他辞职的意图。
近义词: definitely, certainly, undoubtedly, assuredly
反义词: doubtfully, uncertainly, questionably
词性: 副词

词源:  From "confirmed" + "-ly". "Confirmed" comes from the Old French "confirmer," ultimately from Latin "confirmare" (to strengthen, establish).

注意事项:

"Confirmedly" is considered somewhat formal and less common than its synonyms like "definitely" or "certainly."  It might be perceived as overly emphatic or even slightly archaic in everyday conversation.  It's more likely to be found in formal writing or legal contexts.
