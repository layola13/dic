gherao:/ɡɛˈraʊ/
n.|包围；围堵；(尤指印度的)集体纠察|A gherao is a form of protest used in India in which a group of people surround a person or building and refuse to let them leave until their demands are met. |The workers staged a gherao outside the factory gates. 工人们在工厂门口举行围堵抗议。|Synonyms: picket, blockade, siege, demonstration|Antonyms: release, liberation|noun

gherao:/ɡɛˈraʊ/
vt.|包围；围堵；(尤指印度的)对…集体纠察|To gherao someone is to surround them with a group of people and refuse to let them leave until they agree to your demands.|The protesters gheraoed the CEO for hours. 抗议者将CEO围堵了几个小时。|Synonyms: besiege, blockade, picket, surround|Antonyms: release, liberate|verb
