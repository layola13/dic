furial:/ˈfjʊəriəl/| adj.|狂怒的，激烈的|Characterized by or expressing fury; intensely angry or violent.|The furial storm raged through the night.  狂怒的风暴整夜肆虐。|近义词：furious, enraged, vehement, violent, fierce|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *furialis, from Latin furia (“fury, rage”) + -alis (adjective suffix).
