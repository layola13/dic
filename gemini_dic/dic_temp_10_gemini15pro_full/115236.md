manikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ | n. |人体模型；时装模特儿；侏儒|A jointed model of the human body, used especially for drawing or displaying clothes.  |例句：The dressmaker draped the fabric on the manikin.  裁缝把布料披在人体模型上。
