overadjustment:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌstmənt/
noun
过度调整；过度校正
An overadjustment is a correction that goes too far, resulting in a worse situation than the original problem.  It often refers to excessive compensation or reaction to a perceived imbalance.
The government's overadjustment of interest rates led to a recession. 政府对利率的过度调整导致了经济衰退。

The overadjustment of the thermostat made the room too cold.  恒温器的过度调节使房间太冷了。


近义词: overcompensation, overcorrection
反义词: underadjustment, undercorrection
词性: n.
