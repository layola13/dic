comeliness:/ˈkʌmlɪnɪs/
n.
美丽，漂亮，吸引力
The quality of being attractive in appearance; physical grace and beauty.
She possessed a natural comeliness that captivated everyone. 她拥有一种吸引所有人的自然美。
近义词：beauty, attractiveness, loveliness, prettiness
反义词：ugliness, homeliness, unattractiveness
词性：名词(singular)


词源:
From Middle English *comelynesse, from comely +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Old English *cȳmlīc (“comely, handsome, pretty, elegant, splendid, noble, glorious”), equivalent to cyme (“fitting, suitable”) +‎ -ly.  More at become.
