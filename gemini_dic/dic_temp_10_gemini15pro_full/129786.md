non-finite:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪt/ /nɑːnˈfaɪnaɪt/| adj.|非限定的，非限定形式的|A non-finite verb form is one that does not show tense.  It functions as a noun, adjective, or adverb, rather than as a main verb.  These forms are infinitives (with or without "to"), participles (present and past), and gerunds.|Reading is my favorite pastime. 阅读是我最喜欢的消遣。|近义词: infinite, unlimited|反义词: finite, limited|词性:形容词
