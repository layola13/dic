antiphony:/ænˈtɪfəni/ /ænˈtɪfoʊni/ | n. |对唱，轮唱，应答|A musical form in which two or more groups of singers or instruments perform alternately.  A responsive or antithetical utterance.| The choir sang in antiphony, their voices echoing back and forth across the cathedral.  唱诗班以对唱的方式演唱，他们的声音在教堂里回荡。|近义词：response, alternation, call and response |反义词:monophony, unison|noun

antiphony:/ænˈtɪfəni/ /ænˈtɪfoʊni/ | adj. |轮唱的，应答的|Of or relating to antiphony; responsive.|  The antiphonal chanting created a mystical atmosphere. 轮唱的圣歌营造了一种神秘的氛围。|近义词：responsive, alternating |反义词:monophonic, unisonal|adjective
