coriander:/ˌkɔːriˈændər/ /ˌkɔːriˈɑːndər/| n.|香菜，芫荽|An aromatic herb whose leaves and seeds are used as a culinary spice, especially in Asian and Mexican cooking.  The leaves are often called cilantro in North America.|I added some fresh coriander to the salsa. 我在莎莎酱里加了一些新鲜的香菜。|近义词：cilantro (especially in North America)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
