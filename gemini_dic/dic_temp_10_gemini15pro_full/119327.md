microbiology:/ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 微生物学 | The study of microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and algae. | Microbiology is a fascinating field of study. 微生物学是一个引人入胜的研究领域。| Microbiology helps us understand how diseases spread. 微生物学帮助我们理解疾病是如何传播的。 | 近义词: bacteriology | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
micro- (small) + bio- (life) + -logy (study of)
