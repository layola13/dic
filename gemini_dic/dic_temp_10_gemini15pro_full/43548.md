crayoning:/ˈkreɪənɪŋ/| n. |用蜡笔画画，蜡笔画|The act of drawing or coloring with crayons.  |The child's crayoning depicted a vibrant rainbow.  这个孩子用蜡笔画的画描绘了一道充满活力的彩虹。|近义词：drawing, coloring, sketching|反义词:erasing|词性:noun (gerund)

crayoning:/ˈkreɪənɪŋ/| adj. |用蜡笔画的|Created or done with crayons. |She admired the crayoning artwork on the wall. 她欣赏墙上用蜡笔画的艺术作品。|近义词：drawn, colored, sketched|反义词:erased|词性:adjective (participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From crayon + -ing.  Crayon comes from French "crayon," from "craie" (chalk), ultimately from Latin "creta" (chalk, Cretan earth).
