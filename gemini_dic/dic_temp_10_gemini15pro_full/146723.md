overhonestly:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnɪstli/
adverb
过于诚实地
in an excessively honest manner; more honestly than is necessary or desirable.
She overhonestly described her flaws in the interview, which might have cost her the job.  她在面试中过于诚实地描述了自己的缺点，这可能让她失去了这份工作。
overhonestly admitted his mistakes. 他过于诚实地承认了自己的错误。

近义词:excessively honestly, too frankly, unduly candidly
反义词:dishonestly, deceptively

词性:adverb

词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "honestly" (adverb form of "honest"). The word "honest" comes from Old French "honeste" and ultimately from Latin "honestus," meaning "honorable" or "respectable."
