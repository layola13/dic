five:/faɪv/
num.|五|The cardinal number between four and six. |There are five apples in the basket. 篮子里有五个苹果。|Synonyms: quintet, pentad|Antonyms: zero, none
adj.|五的|Relating to the number five. |She has five fingers on each hand. 她每只手上有五个手指。|Synonyms: quinary|Antonyms: none
n.|五个一组|A group of five. |A basketball team is a five. 一个篮球队是由五个人组成的。|Synonyms: quintet, pentad|Antonyms: none
