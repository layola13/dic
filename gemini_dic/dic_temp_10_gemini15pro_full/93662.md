imbue:/ɪmˈbjuː/ /ɪmˈbjuː/ | vt. | 灌输，使充满 | To inspire or permeate with a feeling or quality. |
例句：
The teacher imbued her students with a love of learning. 老师向她的学生灌输了对学习的热爱。

The old house was imbued with a sense of history.  这栋老房子充满了历史感。

近义词：infuse, instill, permeate, saturate, impregnate |
反义词：deprive, divest, drain |
词性：Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *imbuere, from Latin in- + buere "to drink, soak."
