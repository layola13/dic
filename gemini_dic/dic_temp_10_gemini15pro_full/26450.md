byssiferous:/bɪˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
产丝的，生丝的
Producing or bearing byssus (fine silky filaments secreted by certain bivalve mollusks, especially mussels).
The byssiferous glands of the mussel secrete the strong threads that attach it to rocks.  贻贝的产丝腺分泌强韧的丝线，使其附着在岩石上。

The byssiferous mussel anchored itself firmly to the piling.  这只会产丝的贻贝牢牢地把自己固定在桩子上。

近义词:silk-producing, filamentous
反义词:None

词性:adjective
