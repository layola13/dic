ban:/bæn/
verb
禁止，取缔
To officially or legally prohibit.
例句：The government banned smoking in public places. 政府禁止在公共场所吸烟。
例句：The book was banned in several countries. 这本书在几个国家被禁。
近义词：prohibit, forbid, outlaw
反义词：allow, permit
词性：动词


ban:/bæn/
noun
禁令
An official or legal prohibition.
例句：There is a ban on smoking in the building.  这栋楼里禁止吸烟。
例句：The ban on the chemical was lifted.  对这种化学品的禁令被解除了。
近义词：prohibition, embargo
反义词：allowance, permission
词性：名词
