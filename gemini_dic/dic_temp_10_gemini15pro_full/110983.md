liquidising:/ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪŋ/
v.
液化；(用液体搅拌机)把…打成糊状
The process of making something liquid, or of crushing something into a liquid.
She was liquidising some fruit for the baby. 她正在把一些水果打成糊状给婴儿吃。
liquefying, blending, pureeing
solidifying
verb
