lingism:/ˈlɪŋɪzəm/
n.
语言主义，语言歧视
Discrimination based on language, especially prejudice against speakers of a particular language or dialect.
Some argue that standardized language tests are a form of lingism, disadvantaging those who speak non-standard dialects.  一些人认为标准化的语言测试是一种语言歧视，对那些不说标准方言的人不利。
People who speak with a strong regional accent often face lingism in the workplace.  说话带有浓重地方口音的人在工作场所经常面临语言歧视。
近义词: language discrimination, linguistic discrimination, language prejudice
反义词: linguistic tolerance, language acceptance
词性: noun
