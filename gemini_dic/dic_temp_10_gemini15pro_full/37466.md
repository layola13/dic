commissioned: /kəˈmɪʃən/ | v. | 委托，委任；正式任命 | To give an order for or authorize the production of (something such as a building, piece of equipment, or work of art). To appoint (someone) to the rank of officer in the armed forces. |  They commissioned a new opera from the composer. 他们委托这位作曲家创作一部新歌剧。| Synonyms: authorize, contract, engage, appoint, entrust | Antonyms:  cancel, revoke, withdraw | 词性: 动词
