microcline:/ˈmaɪkrəˌklaɪn/| n. |微斜长石|A common feldspar mineral, KAlSi3O8, occurring in white or variously tinted triclinic crystals.|Microcline is a common ingredient in many ceramic glazes. 微斜长石是许多陶瓷釉料中的常见成分。|The geologist identified the mineral as microcline. 地质学家将这种矿物鉴定为微斜长石。|Synonyms: feldspar, orthoclase|Antonyms: N/A (Minerals typically don't have direct antonyms)|noun
