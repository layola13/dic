lay-down-law:/ˌleɪ daʊn ˈlɔː/ | adj. |  强制性的，明确规定的 |  An adjective describing a rule or law that is clearly stated and must be obeyed.  |  The company has a lay-down-law about not using personal phones during meetings.  公司有明确规定，会议期间不得使用个人手机。 | Synonyms: strict, stringent, inflexible, absolute | Antonyms: flexible, lenient, lax | adjective

lay-down-law:/ˌleɪ daʊn ˈlɔː/ | n. |  明确规定，强制性规定 |  A noun referring to a rule or law that is clearly stated and must be obeyed.  |  The lay-down-law in this house is no shoes on the carpet.  这栋房子的明确规定是不准在铺有地毯的地方穿鞋。 | Synonyms: rule, regulation, statute, ordinance | Antonyms: suggestion, recommendation, guideline | noun
