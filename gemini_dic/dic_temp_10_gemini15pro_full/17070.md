bathysphere:/ˈbæθɪsfɪr/| n. |深海潜水球，深海探测器|A manned submersible, usually spherical in shape, used for deep-sea exploration.|例句：The bathysphere allowed scientists to observe marine life at unprecedented depths. 深海潜水球让科学家们能够在史无前例的深度观察海洋生物。

例句：William Beebe and Otis Barton made historic dives in a bathysphere in the 1930s. 威廉·比比和奥蒂斯·巴顿在20世纪30年代乘坐深海潜水球进行了历史性的潜水。|近义词：submersible, underwater vehicle|反义词: (无明确反义词，可以考虑 surface vessel 水面船只)|词性:noun
