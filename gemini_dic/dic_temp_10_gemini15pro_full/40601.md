convection:/kənˈvekʃən/| n.|对流，传送，传递;运流|The transfer of heat by the circulation or movement of the heated parts of a liquid or gas.|Heat is transferred from the sun to the earth by convection.|近义词：circulation, flow, transmission|反义词:stagnation, stillness|noun

convections:/kənˈvekʃənz/| n.|对流（convection的复数形式）|Multiple instances of the transfer of heat through the movement of fluids (liquids or gases).|The atmospheric convections caused the storm clouds to form.|近义词：circulations, flows, transmissions|反义词:stagnations, stillnesses|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convectio*, from Latin convehere "to carry or bring together," from com- "together" + vehere "to carry."
