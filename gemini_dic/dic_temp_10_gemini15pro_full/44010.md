crimination: /ˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
控告; 罪责; 责备
the act of accusing someone of a crime or wrongdoing.
例句：The crimination of the innocent man shocked the community.  控告这个无辜的人震惊了整个社区。

例句：He responded to the crimination with a vehement denial. 他对这一指控予以强烈否认。

近义词：accusation, indictment, charge, incrimination
反义词：exoneration, acquittal, vindication
词性：名词
