counteractively:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktɪvli/
adv.|抵消地，对抗地|in a way that cancels out or opposes the effects of something else.|例句：His attempts to improve the situation counteractively made it worse. 他试图改善情况，却适得其反，使情况变得更糟。|例句：The medication worked counteractively, increasing his blood pressure instead of lowering it.  这种药物起了反作用，使他的血压升高而不是降低。|近义词：adversely, negatively, detrimentally|反义词：positively, beneficially, constructively|词性:副词
