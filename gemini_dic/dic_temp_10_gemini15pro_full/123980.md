mormoness:/ˈmɔːrmənnɪs/| n.|摩门教特质；摩门教信仰的程度|The degree to which someone embodies the principles and practices of Mormonism.  It can also refer to the quality of being characteristic of Mormons or their culture. |Her mormoness was evident in her commitment to family and community service. 她的摩门教特质体现在她对家庭和社区服务的奉献上。|Synonyms: Mormon character, Mormon identity, Mormon spirit|Antonyms: secularism, worldliness|noun
