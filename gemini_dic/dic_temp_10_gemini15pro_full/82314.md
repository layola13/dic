grousing: /ˈɡraʊsɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɡraʊzɪŋ/ (UK) | n. | 抱怨，牢骚 | the act of complaining or grumbling |  He was tired of his colleagues' constant grousing about the workload. 他厌倦了同事们不断抱怨工作量。 |  The soldiers' grousing about the food was understandable. 士兵们抱怨食物是可以理解的。 | synonyms: complaining, grumbling, whining, moaning | antonyms: praising, commending, applauding | noun


growse: /ɡraʊs/ (US) /ɡraʊz/ (UK) | v. | 抱怨，发牢骚 | to complain or grumble | He's always grousing about something. 他总是抱怨一些事情。 | She groused about the long hours she had to work. 她抱怨她不得不长时间工作。 | synonyms: complain, grumble, whine, moan | antonyms: praise, commend, applaud | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *growsen, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old North French *groucier or Anglo-Norman *groucer, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old French groucier "to murmur, grumble."
