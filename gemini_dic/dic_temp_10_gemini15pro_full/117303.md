medaillons:/məˈdæljənz/| n.|大奖章，纪念章，圆形装饰物，(尤指烹饪中)圆形鱼肉或肉片|A medallion is a large medal, especially one awarded as a prize. It can also refer to an ornamental plaque or disc, often bearing a portrait or other design, or in cookery, a small, round piece of meat or fish.|She wore a heavy gold medallion around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一个沉甸甸的金质大奖章。|The chef prepared veal medallions in a rich cream sauce. 厨师用浓郁的奶油酱汁烹制了小牛肉片。|Synonyms: medal, badge, plaque, fillet (in cookery)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
