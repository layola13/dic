cyclopean:/saɪkləˈpiːən/ | adj. | 巨大的，庞大的；独眼的 | Of or relating to the Cyclops, a race of one-eyed giants in Greek mythology; hence, huge, massive, gigantic. |  例句：The cyclopean walls of the ancient city still stood. 古城的巨大城墙依然屹立。 | 近义词：gigantic, colossal, immense, enormous | 反义词：tiny, small, miniature | 词性: 形容词

cyclopean:/saɪkləˈpiːən/ | adj. | 独眼的 | Having a single eye in the middle of the forehead, like a Cyclops. | 例句：The monster had a cyclopean stare.  这个怪物有一只独眼凝视着。| 近义词：one-eyed | 反义词：two-eyed | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Cyclopeus, from Latin Cyclops, from Ancient Greek Κύκλωψ (Kúklōps, “round-eyed, Cyclops”), from κύκλος (kúklos, “circle, wheel”) + ὤψ (ṓps, “eye, face”).
