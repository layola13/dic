half-section:/ˈhæfˌsɛkʃən/
n.
半段；半截；（尤指土地的）一半
A half-section is one half of a section, typically referring to a parcel of land.
The farmer sold a half-section of his land last year. 去年这位农民卖掉了自己一半的土地。
synonyms: half, moiety, portion
antonyms: whole, entirety
noun
