dryest:/ˈdraɪɪst/| adj.|最干燥的|Superlative form of dry; having the least amount of moisture.|The Atacama Desert is one of the dryest places on Earth. 阿塔卡马沙漠是地球上最干燥的地方之一。|The dryest month of the year is usually July. 一年中最干燥的月份通常是七月。|近义词：arid, parched, barren|反义词：wettest, dampest, humidest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *drūge, from Proto-Germanic *drūgaz.  Cognate with Dutch droog, German trocken.
