classify:/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v. |分类，归类|To arrange or organize according to class or category.|例句：The librarian will classify the new books. 图书管理员将对新书进行分类。|例句：Scientists classify animals into different groups. 科学家将动物分成不同的类别。|近义词：categorize, sort, arrange, group, order|反义词：randomize, jumble, mix, disorganize|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *classificare, from Latin classis "class" + -ficare (from facere "to make").
