genets:/ˈdʒɛnɪts/ | n. | 灵猫；遗传单位 | A genet is a small, cat-like mammal native to Africa, Europe, and the Middle East.  It is known for its spotted coat and long, ringed tail.  In biology, "genets" can also refer to genetic units or genes, though this usage is less common. |  Genets are nocturnal hunters. 灵猫是夜行动物猎人。  The researchers studied the genets of the fruit flies. 研究人员研究了果蝇的基因（遗传单位）。 | Near synonyms:  (for animal) civet, mongoose | Antonyms: (no direct antonym for the animal) | Noun
