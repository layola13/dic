depilatory:/dɪˈpɪlətɔːri/ /dəˈpɪləˌtɔri/
noun|脱毛剂|A substance used to remove hair.|She used a depilatory cream to remove unwanted hair. 她用脱毛膏去除不想要的毛发。
adjective|脱毛的|Having the power to remove hair.|The depilatory cream worked quickly and effectively.  这种脱毛膏作用迅速有效。
Synonym:hair remover, epilator
Antonym: hirsute, hairy
Etymology: From Late Latin *dēpīlātōrius, from Latin dēpīlāre (“to deprive of hair”), from dē- (“from, away from”) + pilus (“hair”).
