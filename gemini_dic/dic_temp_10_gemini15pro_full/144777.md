over-exacting:/ˌoʊvər ɪɡˈzæktɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分苛求的，过于严格的 | Demanding too much; excessively meticulous or demanding.  |  He was an over-exacting boss, making his employees work late every night. 他是一个过于苛刻的老板，让他的员工每晚都加班到很晚。

|  She has over-exacting standards for her children's academic performance. 她对孩子的学习成绩要求过高。

| 近义词: demanding, fastidious, meticulous, stringent, rigorous | 反义词: lenient, tolerant, easygoing, undemanding | 词性: 形容词
