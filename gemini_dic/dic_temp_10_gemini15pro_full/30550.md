cephalosome:/ˈsɛfələˌsoʊm/| n. | 头胸部 | The fused head and thorax of some arthropods, such as crustaceans and arachnids. |  The horseshoe crab's cephalosome is covered by a hard carapace.  鲎的头胸部被坚硬的甲壳覆盖。|Synonym: cephalothorax | Antonym: abdomen | noun

cephalosome:/ˈsɛfələˌsoʊm/| n. | 头胸部 | The anterior body region of many arthropods, comprising the fused head and thorax segments. | The spider's cephalosome bears the eyes, mouthparts, and legs. 蜘蛛的头胸部长有眼睛、口器和腿。|Synonym: prosoma | Antonym: opisthosoma | noun
