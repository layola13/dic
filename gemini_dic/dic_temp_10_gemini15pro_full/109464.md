lepidopterous:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərəs/| adj.|鳞翅目的；属于鳞翅目的|Relating to or belonging to the order Lepidoptera, which includes butterflies and moths.|Butterflies and moths are lepidopterous insects. 蝴蝶和飞蛾是鳞翅目昆虫。|The collector specialized in lepidopterous specimens.  这位收藏家专门收藏鳞翅目昆虫标本。|Synonyms: butterfly-like, moth-like|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

lepidopteran:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərən/| n. |鳞翅目昆虫|Any of numerous insects of the order Lepidoptera, having four wings covered with tiny scales, including the butterflies and moths.|The lepidopteran fluttered around the flower. 这只鳞翅目昆虫在花朵周围飞舞。|She studied the life cycle of the lepidopterans in the rainforest. 她在雨林中研究鳞翅目昆虫的生命周期。|Synonyms: butterfly, moth|Antonyms: N/A|noun
