nourishers:/ˈnɜːrɪʃərz/
词性:n.
中文翻译:滋养者；营养物
英文解析:Plural form of nourisher, referring to people or things that provide nourishment or sustenance.
例句：
The nourishers of the body include healthy foods and plenty of water.
身体的滋养物包括健康的食物和充足的水。

The community rallied around the nourishers of their local food bank.
社区团结起来支持当地食物银行的供应者。


近义词:providers, sustainers, supporters
反义词:depleters, destroyers
