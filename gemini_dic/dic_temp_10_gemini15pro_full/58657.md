dumdum:/ˈdʌmˌdʌm/ | n. | 傻瓜；笨蛋；低能儿 | A stupid or foolish person. | He acted like such a dumdum when he locked his keys in the car. 他把钥匙锁在车里，真是个傻瓜。|  Synonym: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile | Antonym: genius, intellectual | Noun


dumdum:/ˈdʌmˌdʌm/ | adj. | 愚蠢的；笨的 | Stupid or foolish. | That was a dumdum decision to make. 那是一个愚蠢的决定。 | Synonym: stupid, foolish, silly, idiotic | Antonym: smart, intelligent, wise | Adjective


Etymology:  From the name of Dum Dum Arsenal, near Calcutta, India, where a type of expanding bullet was manufactured. The bullet was considered inhumane, and by extension, the term came to refer to someone considered stupid or slow-witted.
