inhumanity:/ˌɪnhjuːˈmænəti/
n.
lack of compassion or pity; cruelty
lack of kindness, mercy, or compassion; barbarity
The inhumanity of the prison system is shocking.  监狱系统的残酷令人震惊。
The refugees suffered great inhumanity at the hands of their captors.  难民们遭受了俘虏他们的极大的不人道待遇。
近义词:cruelty, barbarity, brutality, savagery, wickedness
反义词:humanity, compassion, kindness, mercy, empathy
词性:noun

inhuman:/ɪnˈhjuːmən/
adj.
lacking human qualities of compassion and mercy; cruel and barbaric
not human; beastly
The inhuman treatment of the prisoners was condemned by international organizations.  囚犯的非人待遇受到国际组织的谴责。
The experiment involved subjecting animals to inhuman conditions.  这项实验让动物经受非人道的条件。
近义词:cruel, brutal, barbaric, savage, monstrous
反义词:human, humane, compassionate, kind, merciful
词性:adjective
