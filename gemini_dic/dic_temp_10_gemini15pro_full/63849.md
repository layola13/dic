epigrammatic:/ˌepɪɡrəˈmætɪk/| adj.|警句式的，简洁精辟的|Of the nature or in the style of an epigram; concise, pointed, and witty.|Her epigrammatic style made her a popular speaker. 她的简洁精辟的风格使她成为受欢迎的演说家。|Synonyms: concise, pithy, succinct, terse, witty, aphoristic|Antonyms: verbose, prolix, rambling, long-winded|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epigrammaticus*, from Greek *epigrammatikos*, from *epigramma* (epigram).
