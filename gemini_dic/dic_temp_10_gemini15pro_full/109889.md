liaises:/liˈeɪzɪz/
verb
联络；联系；接洽
acts as a link between two or more groups, organizations, etc.
The company liaises with customers through email and social media. 这家公司通过电子邮件和社交媒体与客户联系。
Our department liaises with other departments to ensure smooth operations.  我们的部门与其他部门联络，以确保运营顺利。
collaborates, coordinates, connects, communicates, networks
separates, isolates, disconnects
v.
