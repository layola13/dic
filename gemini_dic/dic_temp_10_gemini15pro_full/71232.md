fitted:/ˈfɪtɪd/| adj.|安装好的，合适的，合身的|Made to conform to the shape of something; having been altered to fit.|The tailor made a fitted suit for him.  裁缝为他做了一套合身的西装。|近义词:suitable, appropriate, tailored, adjusted, snug|反义词:loose, baggy, ill-fitting, unsuitable|adjective

fitted:/ˈfɪtɪd/| v.|安装，使…合身（fit的过去式和过去分词）|Past tense and past participle of "fit".  To be the right shape and size for.|The carpenter fitted the new cabinets. 木匠安装了新的橱柜。|近义词:installed, placed, attached, adjusted|反义词:removed, uninstalled, detached|verb
