The endless repetition of the task led to a feeling of inanitiation.  这项任务无休止的重复导致了一种空虚感。 | 近义词：emptiness, hollowness, futility, meaninglessness, vacuity | 反义词：meaningfulness, purpose, fulfillment | 词性: noun
