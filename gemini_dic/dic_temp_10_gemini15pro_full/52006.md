diagonal:/daɪˈæɡənəl/ | adj. n. |对角线的，斜线的；对角线，斜线|A diagonal line or movement goes from one corner to another, not along one of the sides.| The square is divided into two triangles by a diagonal. 这个正方形被一条对角线分成两个三角形。|例句：He crossed the square diagonally. 他斜穿过了广场。|近义词：slanting, oblique, transverse|反义词：horizontal, vertical|词性：形容词, 名词

diagonals:/daɪˈæɡənəlz/ | n. |对角线，斜线|Plural form of diagonal.| The diagonals of a rectangle bisect each other. 长方形的对角线互相平分。|例句：Draw all the diagonals of the pentagon. 画出五边形的所有对角线。|近义词：slanting lines, oblique lines|反义词：horizontal lines, vertical lines|词性：名词
