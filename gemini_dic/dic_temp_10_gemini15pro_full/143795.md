outnumbering:/ˌaʊtˈnʌmbərɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
数量上超过
The act of exceeding something or someone in number.
The enemy forces, outnumbering us three to one, launched a fierce attack.  敌军兵力三倍于我军，发动了猛烈进攻。
Our troops were heavily outnumbered, but they fought bravely.  我们的军队人数远远少于敌人，但他们英勇作战。
近义词: exceeding, surpassing, preponderating
反义词: outnumbered, fewer than
词性: verb (present participle)
