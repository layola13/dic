nonsaving:/nɒnˈseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
非储蓄的，不储蓄的
Not pertaining to saving; not characterized by saving; not accumulating savings.
例句：
Nonsaving behavior can lead to financial instability.
非储蓄行为会导致财务不稳定。

The nonsaving habits of some consumers contribute to the economy's reliance on credit.
一些消费者的非储蓄习惯导致了经济对信贷的依赖。

近义词：spending, consumptive
反义词：saving, thrifty
词性：adjective
