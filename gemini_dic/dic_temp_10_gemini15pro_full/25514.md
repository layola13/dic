bullishly:/ˈbʊlɪʃli/| adv.|看涨地，乐观地|In a bullish manner; optimistically, with an expectation of rising prices (especially of stocks).  Showing or expressing confidence or optimism.|例句：Investors are bullishly buying up tech stocks. 投资者正乐观地买入科技股。|近义词：optimistically, confidently, hopefully|反义词:bearishly, pessimistically|词性:副词
bullish:/ˈbʊlɪʃ/| adj.|看涨的，乐观的|Optimistic about something, especially about economic conditions or the prospects for a particular company or industry; characterized by rising prices.|例句：The stock market has been bullish for the past few months. 股市在过去几个月里一直看涨。|近义词：optimistic, positive, confident|反义词:bearish, pessimistic|词性:形容词

词源: From bull (n.) + -ish.  The connection with bulls may be due to their aggressive nature, or to their use in bull baiting, where their upward thrusting horns were associated with rising prices.
