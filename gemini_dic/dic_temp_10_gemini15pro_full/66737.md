exorcising:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
驱魔；去除（坏念头等）
The priest performed a ritual for exorcising the demons.  神父举行仪式驱除恶魔。
He tried to exorcise the painful memories from his mind. 他试图从脑海中去除痛苦的记忆。
近义词: purging, cleansing, purifying
反义词: summoning, invoking
词性: verb (present participle)
