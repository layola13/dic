overpainful: /ˌoʊvərˈpeɪnfəl/ | adj. | 过于痛苦的，极度痛苦的 | Excessively painful; causing extreme pain.  Formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "too much") to the adjective "painful." | The overpainful memories of the accident haunted her.  事故过于痛苦的回忆一直困扰着她。| The surgery was overpainful, even with anesthesia. 即使使用了麻醉剂，手术还是极其痛苦。 | 近义词: excruciating, agonizing, unbearable | 反义词: painless, comfortable | 词性: 形容词
