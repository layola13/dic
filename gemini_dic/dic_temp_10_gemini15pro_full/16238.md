banshee:/ˈbænʃiː/ (US), /ˈbænʃiː/ (UK)| n.|报丧女妖|A female spirit in Irish folklore whose wailing warns of an impending death in a family.|Legend says that if you hear a banshee wail, someone in your family will soon die.|Synonyms: wraith, spirit, phantom, apparition|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a supernatural entity.  One could consider "living being" or "mortal" as a contrasting concept.)|noun
