begrudgery:/bɪˈɡrʌdʒəri/
n.
勉强；不情愿；妒忌
Reluctance or resentment arising from a feeling that something is unfair.

He accepted the extra work with begrudgery.
他不情愿地接受了额外的工作。

She felt a pang of begrudgery when she saw her colleague's promotion.
看到同事升职，她感到一阵妒忌。

resentment, reluctance, envy, malice
willingness, generosity, goodwill
noun
