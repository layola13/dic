fissioning:/ˈfɪʃənɪŋ/
|n.|裂变; 分裂|The process of splitting the nucleus of an atom into two or more smaller nuclei, releasing large amounts of energy.|Nuclear fissioning is the process used in nuclear power plants.|近义词：splitting, dividing, breaking apart|反义词：fusion|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *fission* + *-ing*. *Fission* comes from Latin *fissio*, from *findere* "to split".
