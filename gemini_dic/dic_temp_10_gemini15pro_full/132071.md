noncranking:/nɒnˈkræŋkɪŋ/ (US) /nɒnˈkræŋkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|非启动的，非曲柄转动的|Not related to or involving the cranking of an engine (typically a starter motor attempting to turn the engine over).  This term often describes a state or condition where the engine is not being attempted to start or is not rotating. | The noncranking current draw was very high. 非启动电流消耗非常高。|The battery was completely dead, resulting in a noncranking condition. 电池完全没电了，导致发动机无法启动。|近义词：not starting, inert, inactive|反义词：cranking, starting|词性: adjective
