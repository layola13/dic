echoless:/ˈekəʊləs/ (UK) /ˈɛkoʊləs/ (US)| adj.|无回声的，没有回音的|Without an echo; not producing echoes.  Absorbing sound so that no echoes are produced.|The chamber was echoless, absorbing all sound.|Near synonyms: soundproof, anechoic, non-reverberant|Opposite: reverberant, resonant
词性: adjective
