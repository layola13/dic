oversophistication:/ˌoʊvər səˌfɪstɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度复杂化；过度精细化 | The state of being excessively sophisticated or refined, often to the point of being impractical or unnecessary. |  Her writing suffered from oversophistication; it was difficult to understand. 她的写作过于复杂，难以理解。

The oversophistication of the software made it difficult to use.  这个软件过于复杂，难以使用。

| 近义词: complexity, elaborateness, refinement, intricacy | 反义词: simplicity, straightforwardness, artlessness | 词性: noun
