noncosmopolitan:/ˌnɒnkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/ | adj. | 非世界性的，非国际性的；狭隘的 | Not cosmopolitan; not worldly or sophisticated; narrow or provincial in outlook. |  Many small towns have a distinctly noncosmopolitan atmosphere. 许多小镇都有一种明显的非世界性氛围。

| Example: His noncosmopolitan views made it difficult for him to adapt to life in a diverse city. 他狭隘的观点使他难以适应多元化城市的生活。| Synonym: provincial, insular, parochial, narrow-minded | Antonym: cosmopolitan, worldly, sophisticated, broad-minded | Adjective
