hungriness:/ˈhʌŋɡrɪnɪs/| n.|饥饿感；渴望|The state of being hungry; a strong desire or craving, especially for food.|Her hungriness for knowledge led her to read extensively. 她对知识的渴望使她广泛阅读。|Synonyms: hunger, ravenousness, craving, appetite, yearning|Antonyms: satiety, fullness, satisfaction|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hungry + -ness.  "Hungry" comes from Old English "hungrig," meaning "having a strong desire or craving for food." The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
