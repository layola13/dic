in-step:/ɪnˈstɛp/| adj. adv.|同步的，合拍的；步伐一致地|Moving or acting in unison or agreement, especially in keeping pace with others.|例句：The soldiers marched in-step. 士兵们步伐一致地前进。|近义词：in sync, coordinated, harmonious, rhythmical|反义词:out of step, out of sync, uncoordinated|词性:形容词, 副词
Etymology:  "in" + "step". "In" meaning "within" or "in accordance with," and "step" referring to a pace or rhythm.
