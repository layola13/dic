noncomposer:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpoʊzər/
noun
非作曲家
a person who does not compose music
He's a talented musician, but a noncomposer. 他是一位才华横溢的音乐家，但不是作曲家。
Near Synonyms: non-musician, amateur
Antonyms: composer, songwriter
词性: 名词
