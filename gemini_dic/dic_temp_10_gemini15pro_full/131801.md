nonconsecutive:/ˌnɒnkənˈsekjətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsekjətɪv/| adj.|非连续的，不连续的|Not following in a continuous sequence; separated by an interval or intervals.|The nonconsecutive numbers on the houses made it difficult to find the right address.  房屋上的门牌号不连续，很难找到正确的地址。|例句：The meetings were nonconsecutive, taking place over several weeks. 这些会议并非连续举行，而是持续了几个星期。|近义词：discontinuous, intermittent, sporadic, broken, interrupted|反义词：consecutive, continuous, successive|词性:形容词

nonconsecutive:/ˌnɒnkənˈsekjətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsekjətɪv/| n.|非连续数|A number that is not part of a consecutive sequence.|There are several nonconsecutives in this series of numbers. 在这一系列数字中，有几个非连续数。|例句：Identifying the nonconsecutives in the data set helped reveal a pattern. 找出数据集中非连续的数字有助于揭示一种模式。|近义词：(As a noun, it's less common and doesn't have direct synonyms.  One could use "gap" or "interruption" to describe the absence of consecutiveness.)|反义词：(Similarly, no direct antonym exists for the noun form.)|词性:名词 (rarely used)
