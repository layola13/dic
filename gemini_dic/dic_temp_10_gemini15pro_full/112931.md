luge:/luːʒ/ | n. |雪橇, 小型雪橇, 平底雪橇 |A small sled, typically used for racing down an ice track, ridden in a supine position with the feet first.|  The athlete expertly navigated the luge track.  这位运动员熟练地驾驶着雪橇滑下赛道。| Synonyms: sled, toboggan | Antonyms: None | Noun

luge:/luːʒ/ | v. |乘雪橇滑行 |To ride a luge.| He lugeed down the icy slope at breakneck speed. 他以惊人的速度乘雪橇滑下冰冷的斜坡。| Synonyms: sled, toboggan | Antonyms: None | Verb
