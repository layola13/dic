electro-acoustics: /ɪˌlektroʊ əˈkuːstɪks/ | n. |电声学| The branch of acoustics that deals with the production, control, transmission, reception, and effects of sound by electronic means. | Electro-acoustics plays a vital role in modern sound systems. 电声学在现代音响系统中起着至关重要的作用。| The concert hall had state-of-the-art electro-acoustics.  音乐厅拥有最先进的电声设备。| synonyms: audio engineering, sound engineering | antonyms: None | noun
