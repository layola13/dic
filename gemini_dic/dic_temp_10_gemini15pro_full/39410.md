con man:/ˈkɒnmæn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnmæn/ (US)| n. |骗子；行骗者|A man who cheats or tricks people by gaining their trust and persuading them to believe something that is not true.|She realized she'd been taken in by a con man. 她意识到自己被一个骗子骗了。|近义词：fraudster, swindler, cheat, trickster|反义词: honest person, philanthropist | noun


词源:  From "confidence man," shortened to "con man."  It refers to someone who gains the confidence of their victim in order to defraud them.
