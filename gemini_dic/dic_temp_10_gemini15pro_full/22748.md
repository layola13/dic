boroughmonger:/ˈbʌrəˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 选区操纵者，控制议会席位者 | A person who controls or manipulates the selection of candidates for political office, especially in a borough.  They often used corrupt practices to influence elections. |  In the 19th century, boroughmongers were notorious for their corrupt practices. 19世纪，选区操纵者因其腐败行为而臭名昭著。 The boroughmonger used his wealth and influence to secure a seat in Parliament for his son.  这个选区操纵者利用他的财富和影响力为他的儿子在议会中谋得了一个席位。 | 近义词: political fixer, manipulator, power broker | 反义词: reformer | 词性: noun
