arrestors:/əˈrɛstərz/
noun
避雷器；抑制器；制动器
A device used to protect electrical equipment from voltage surges, especially lightning strikes. Also used more generally to describe devices that stop or check something.
The power station was protected by lightning arrestors.  发电站受到避雷器的保护。
The train's emergency arrestors were activated. 火车的紧急制动器被启动了。
synonyms: surge protector, suppressor, lightning rod, brake
antonyms: accelerator, catalyst
