fatigate:/ˈfætɪɡeɪt/| v. |使疲劳，使疲乏|To make someone extremely tired, either physically or mentally.|The long journey fatigued the travelers.  漫长的旅程使旅行者疲惫不堪。

The constant stress of the job fatigued her.  工作的持续压力使她疲惫不堪。|近义词：tire, exhaust, weary, enervate|反义词：energize, invigorate, refresh|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin fatigatus, past participle of fatigare "to weary," from Latin fatisci "crack, gape, give way, grow weak," related to fatiscere "to gape, yawn."
