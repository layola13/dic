holocephali:/ˌhɒləʊˈsefəlaɪ/ /ˌhɑːloʊˈsefəlaɪ/| n. |全头亚纲|A subclass of cartilaginous fish (Chondrichthyes) characterized by a single gill opening on each side, a large head, and tooth plates rather than separate teeth. They include the chimaeras or ratfish.|例句：Holocephali are a fascinating group of deep-sea fish. 全头亚纲是一群迷人的深海鱼类。

Holocephali diverged from sharks millions of years ago. 全头亚纲在数百万年前与鲨鱼分化。|近义词：Chimaeriformes (sometimes used interchangeably)|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic classification)|词性:noun
