cloak:/kloʊk/ (US) /kləʊk/ (UK)| n. vt.| 斗篷；掩盖；遮掩物 |A loose outer garment, typically sleeveless and fastening at the neck, worn for warmth or protection. To cover or conceal something.|She wore a heavy cloak to protect herself from the cold. 她穿着一件厚厚的斗篷来御寒。|近义词：cape, mantle, cover, conceal, disguise|反义词：reveal, uncover|词性: noun, verb
