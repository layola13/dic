cohabiting:/koʊˈhæbɪtɪŋ/
adjective
同居的
Living together in a sexual relationship without being married.
例句：They were cohabiting for five years before they got married. 他们在结婚前同居了五年。

例句：Cohabiting couples are increasingly common in modern society.  同居伴侣在现代社会越来越普遍。

近义词：living together, cohabitating
反义词：married, single, separate
词性: adjective


cohabit:/koʊˈhæbɪt/
verb
同居
To live together in a sexual relationship without being married.
例句：They decided to cohabit before getting married. 他们决定在结婚前同居。

例句：Many young couples choose to cohabit these days. 现在很多年轻夫妇选择同居。

近义词：live together, cohabitate
反义词：separate, divorce
词性: verb
