She was accused of misprision of treason for hiding her brother, a known traitor. 她因窝藏她那臭名昭著的叛徒哥哥而被指控包庇叛国罪。 | 近义词: concealment, neglect, oversight, misunderstanding | 反义词: disclosure, attention, care, comprehension | 词性: 名词
