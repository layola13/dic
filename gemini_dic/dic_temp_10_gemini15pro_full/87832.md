hidability:/ˌhaɪdəˈbɪləti/
n.
可隐藏性，隐蔽性
The hidability of the bunker was its greatest asset.  这个掩体的隐蔽性是它最大的优势。
hidability:  The quality or state of being able to be hidden or concealed.  This refers to how easily something can be kept out of sight or unnoticed.
The spy valued the hidability of the microdot.  这个间谍很重视微型胶卷的可隐藏性。

近义词:concealment, secrecy, discretion
反义词:visibility, exposure, obviousness
词性:noun
