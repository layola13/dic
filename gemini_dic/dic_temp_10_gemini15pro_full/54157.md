disentangled:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡld/
|adj.|
|解开的，理清的，分开的|
|Free from entanglement; separated or untwisted.|
|The archaeologist carefully disentangled the delicate necklace from the skeleton's bones. 考古学家小心翼翼地从骸骨上解开精致的项链。|

|After hours of work, she finally disentangled the complex legal issues. 经过几个小时的工作，她终于理清了复杂的法律问题。|
|近义词: untangled, separated, resolved, clarified|
|反义词: tangled, intertwined, complicated, confused|
|词性: 形容词|

disentangle:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡl/
|vt.|
|解开，理清，分开|
|To free from entanglement; to separate or untwist.|
|He disentangled the fishing line from the seaweed. 他把鱼线从海藻里解开了。|

|It's difficult to disentangle the truth from the lies in this case. 在这个案件中，很难区分真相和谎言。|
|近义词: untangle, separate, resolve, unravel|
|反义词: tangle, intertwine, complicate, confuse|
|词性: 动词|
