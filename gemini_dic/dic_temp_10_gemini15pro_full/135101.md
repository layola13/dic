nonobservingly:/nɑːnəbˈzɜːrvɪŋli/
adverb
不观察地，不注意地
without observing or paying attention.
He walked past nonobservingly, lost in his thoughts. 他心不在焉地走过，沉浸在自己的思绪中。
She nonobservingly stepped on the ant. 她无意中踩到了蚂蚁。
近义词: inattentively, unmindfully, heedlessly
反义词: observantly, attentively, mindfully
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix) + observing (present participle of observe) + -ly (adverb suffix)
