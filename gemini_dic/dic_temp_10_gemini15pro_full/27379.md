came-in-for:/keɪm ˈɪn fɔːr/| phrasal verb | 遭受；受到（批评或责备）；| to receive something, usually criticism or blame | Example: The government came in for a lot of criticism over its handling of the economy. 政府因其处理经济的方式而受到很多批评。| Example:  He came in for some harsh words from his boss after missing the deadline. 他因为错过截止日期而受到老板的严厉批评。 | Synonyms: receive, experience, be subjected to,  encounter,  undergo | Antonyms: avoid, escape, evade | verb phrase

came-in-for:/keɪm ˈɪn fɔːr/| phrasal verb | 接受；获得；| to be given something | Example:  She came in for a large inheritance from her aunt. 她从她姑妈那里继承了一大笔遗产。| Example: The team came in for praise after their impressive win.  这个团队在取得令人印象深刻的胜利后获得了赞扬。 | Synonyms: receive, obtain, get, acquire, inherit | Antonyms: lose, forfeit, be deprived of | verb phrase
