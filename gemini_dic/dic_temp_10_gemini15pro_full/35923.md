cocultivates:/koʊˈkʌltɪveɪts/
verb
共同培养，共同栽培
To grow two or more different kinds of organisms together.
The researchers cocultivated the bacteria with human cells to study the interaction. 研究人员将细菌与人体细胞共同培养以研究它们的相互作用。
co-culture, cultivate together
monoculture
v.
