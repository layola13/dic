battalions:/bəˈtæljənz/
n.
营; 部队
A large body of troops ready for battle, especially an infantry unit forming part of a brigade typically commanded by a lieutenant colonel.
The general deployed three battalions to secure the perimeter. 将军部署了三个营来确保周边安全。

The battalions marched tirelessly through the night. 这些部队整夜不知疲倦地行军。


近义词:regiments, units, forces
反义词:(无直接的反义词，可以考虑 civilian 或 individual)
词性:名词
