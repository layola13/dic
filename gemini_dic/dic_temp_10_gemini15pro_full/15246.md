backgammoning:/ˌbækˈɡæmənɪŋ/
|n.|
|双陆棋残局; 在双陆棋中以大比分击败对手|
|The act of winning a game of backgammon by preventing your opponent from bearing off any of their checkers.|
|He gave his opponent a sound backgammoning.|他把对手打得落花流水（双陆棋）。|
|gammon, defeating, thrashing (informal)|
