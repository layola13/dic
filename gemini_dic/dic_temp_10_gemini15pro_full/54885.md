displosion:/dɪˈsploʊʒən/
n.
内爆; 向内爆炸
an explosion inwards
The demolition of the building was achieved by displosion. 这栋建筑是通过内爆拆除的。

Etymology:  Formed by analogy with *explosion*, from *dis-* (expressing reversal) + *-plosion* (from Latin *explodere*, "to drive out by clapping").

Synonyms: implosion, inward burst, collapse
Antonyms: explosion, outburst, eruption
noun
