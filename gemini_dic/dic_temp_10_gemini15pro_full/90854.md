hungerer:/ˈhʌŋɡərər/ | n. | 饥饿的人;渴望得到…的人 |Someone who is very hungry or who craves something. | The refugees were hungerers, desperate for food and water. 这些难民都非常饥饿，迫切需要食物和水。| Synonyms: starveling, glutton (when craving something), aspirant (when craving something abstract) | Antonyms: satisfied person, sated person | Noun

Etymology: From "hunger" (Old English *hungor, from Proto-Germanic *hungraz) + "-er" (agent suffix, from Old English -ere).
