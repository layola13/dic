no-hoper:/ˌnəʊˈhəʊpə(r)/
noun
无可救药的人; 没有希望的人
a person who is unlikely to be successful or to achieve anything worthwhile.
He was labelled a no-hoper at school. 他在学校被贴上了无可救药的标签。
synonyms: loser, failure, deadbeat, washout, good-for-nothing
antonyms: achiever, winner, success, high-flyer
n.
