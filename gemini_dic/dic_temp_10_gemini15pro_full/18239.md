belibel:/bɪˈlaɪbəl/ | adj. | 可信的，可信赖的 |  Worthy of belief or trust; credible. |  The witness gave a believable account of the accident.  目击者对事故的描述可信。| 近义词: credible, plausible, trustworthy | 反义词: unbelievable, implausible, untrustworthy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From be- + believable, from belief + -able. Ultimately from Latin *lēctus.
