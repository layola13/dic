macula:/ˈmækjʊlə/ | n. |斑点，污点；(视网膜)黄斑|A small discolored area of skin that is not raised above the surrounding skin.  A spot or blemish, especially on the retina of the eye.|The doctor examined the macula of his patient's eye. 医生检查了病人眼睛的黄斑。|Synonyms: spot, blemish, blotch, speck, patch|Antonyms: clear skin, clear area|noun


macula:/ˈmækjʊlə/ | n. |(天文)太阳黑子|A dark spot on the sun's surface.|Solar flares often erupt near maculae. 太阳耀斑经常在太阳黑子附近爆发。|Synonyms: sunspot|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "photosphere" as the general bright surface)|noun
