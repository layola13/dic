flummox:/ˈflʌməks/| vt.|使困惑，使不知所措|To confuse someone so much that they do not know what to do or say. |The complicated instructions completely flummoxed me. 这些复杂的指示让我完全不知所措。|Synonyms: bewilder, confuse, perplex, baffle|Antonyms: enlighten, clarify|verb

flummoxed:/ˈflʌməkst/| adj.|困惑的，不知所措的|Confused and bewildered. |I was completely flummoxed by the sudden change of plans.  计划的突然改变让我完全不知所措。|Synonyms: bewildered, confused, perplexed, baffled|Antonyms: clear-headed, composed|adjective
