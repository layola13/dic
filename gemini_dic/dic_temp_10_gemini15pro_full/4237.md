airmailing:/ˈeəmeɪlɪŋ/| n. |空邮, 航空邮寄|The act of sending something by airmail.|I chose airmailing for faster delivery. 我选择空邮以便更快送达。|近义词：air post, sending by air|反义词: surface mail|词性:noun (present participle of airmail)
Etymology: air + mail + -ing (present participle suffix)  "air" refers to the medium of transport (by airplane), and "mail" refers to postal service.  The "-ing" suffix creates a noun from the verb "airmail," indicating the action or process.
