chipped:/tʃɪpt/| v.| chipped; chipped; chipping|削(或凿)下(碎片)；打破；弄缺|He chipped a piece of glass from the bottle. 他从瓶子上削掉了一块玻璃。|Synonyms: break, crack, splinter, shatter|Antonyms: repair, mend, fix|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chippen*, frequentative of *choppen* (“to chop”), from Old English *cippan* (“to cut off, chop”), from Proto-Germanic *kippaną* (“to cut off, chop”), from Proto-Indo-European *geyp-* (“to cut”). Cognate with Dutch *kippen* (“to hatch, chip”), German *kippen* (“to tip over, tilt”).
