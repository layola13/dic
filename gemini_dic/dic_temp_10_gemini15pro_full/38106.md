compounder:/kəmˈpaʊndər/ (US), /ˈkɒmpaʊndə(r)/ (UK)| n.|配药师；调配员；混合器；加重者；<金融>复利计算者|A person who prepares medicines by combining ingredients; a person or thing that mixes substances; something that makes a problem or situation worse; <Finance> someone who calculates compound interest.|The compounder carefully measured the ingredients for the prescription. 配药师仔细地测量处方中的成分。|近义词：mixer, blender, aggravator, intensifier|反义词：separator, mitigator|词性: 名词

compounder:/kəmˈpaʊnd/ (US), /ˈkɒmpaʊnd/ (UK)| v.|混合；调配；加重；使恶化；<金融>以复利计算|To mix or combine ingredients; to make a problem or situation worse; <Finance> to calculate using compound interest.|Leaving the wound uncovered compounded the infection.  没有包扎伤口加重了感染。|近义词：mix, blend, worsen, aggravate|反义词：separate, alleviate|词性: 动词
