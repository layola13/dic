calumnize: /ˈkæləmnaɪz/ | v. | 诽谤，中伤 | To make false and malicious statements about someone in order to damage their reputation. | He was calumnized by his political opponents. 他被他的政敌诽谤了。|  The newspaper article calumnized the actor, falsely accusing him of drug use. 这篇报纸文章诽谤了这位演员，错误地指控他吸毒。| 近义词: slander, defame, libel, smear | 反义词: praise, commend, extol | 词性: 动词

calumny: /ˈkæləmni/ | n. | 诽谤，中伤 | A false and malicious statement designed to injure the reputation of someone else. |  The politician spread calumnies about his opponent. 这位政客散布关于他对手的诽谤。|  He was the victim of calumny and lies. 他是诽谤和谎言的受害者。 | 近义词: slander, defamation, libel | 反义词: praise, commendation | 词性: 名词
