overdeepened:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpənd/ | adj. | 过度加深的，过度挖掘的 |  Excavated or eroded to a depth greater than that which would normally be expected.  A glacial valley, for example, can be overdeepened by glacial erosion. | The fjord is an overdeepened valley carved by glaciers. 这峡湾是由冰川雕刻而成的过度加深的河谷。| The lake was overdeepened by the glacier.  这个湖被冰川过度加深了。 |  excavated, gouged, eroded | shallow | adjective
