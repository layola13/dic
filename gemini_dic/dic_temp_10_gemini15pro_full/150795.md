paradoxical:/ˌpærəˈdɒksɪkl/ (US) /ˌpærəˈdɑːksɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|似是而非的，矛盾的|Seemingly absurd or self-contradictory.|It is paradoxical that standing is more tiring than walking.  站着比走路更累，这似乎很矛盾。|The medicine had a paradoxical effect, making the patient sicker.  这药产生了矛盾的效果，使病人的病情加重了。|近义词：contradictory, ironic, illogical, inconsistent|反义词：logical, consistent, reasonable|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *paradoxalis*, from Ancient Greek παράδοξος (parádoxos, “contrary to expectation, incredible”), from παρά (pará, “beside, beyond, contrary to”) + δόξα (dóxa, “opinion, belief”).
