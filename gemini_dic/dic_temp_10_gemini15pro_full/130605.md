nonadherence:/ˌnɒnədˈhɪərəns/| n. |不依从，不坚持|Failure or refusal to follow rules, guidelines, or recommendations.  The patient's nonadherence to the prescribed medication regimen led to a worsening of his condition.|例句：Nonadherence to the treatment plan can have serious consequences. 不遵守治疗计划可能会导致严重后果。

例句：The doctor stressed the importance of nonadherence to smoking. 医生强调了不吸烟的重要性。|近义词：noncompliance, default, lapse|反义词：adherence, compliance, conformity|词性:名词

词源: non- (not) + adherence (sticking to)  From Latin adhaerere "to stick to."
