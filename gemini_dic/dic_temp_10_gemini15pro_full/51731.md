devoicing:/diːˈvɔɪsɪŋ/
noun
浊音弱化，清音化
The process of a voiced sound becoming voiceless, especially in a particular phonetic context.
In German, final devoicing is a common phonological process. 在德语中，词尾清音化是一种常见的语音现象。
近义词: unvoicing
反义词: voicing
词性: n.
