mispoint:/mɪsˈpɔɪnt/ | n. | 错误点; 误解点 | A point that is misunderstood or incorrectly interpreted. |  The main mispoint in your argument is that you assume everyone has the same background knowledge. 你论证中的主要错误点在于你假设每个人都有相同的背景知识。

The lawyer seized on the mispoint in the witness's testimony. 律师抓住了证人证词中的错误点。 | Near Synonyms: misunderstanding, misinterpretation, misconception, fallacy, error | Antonyms: accurate point, correct interpretation, understanding | noun
