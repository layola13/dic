adiactinic:/ˌeɪdaɪækˈtɪnɪk/
adjective
不透光化线的，防光化作用的
Not transmitting chemically active rays.  Preventing chemical changes caused by light.
The adiactinic glass protected the sensitive chemicals from degrading.  这种防光化玻璃保护了敏感的化学物质免于降解。
Adiactinic containers are used to store photosensitive materials. 使用防光化容器来储存光敏材料。
近义词:lightproof, opaque (to actinic rays)
反义词:translucent, transparent (to actinic rays)
词性:adjective
词源: a- (not) + dia- (through) + actinic (relating to chemical action of light) from Greek *aktis* meaning "ray"
