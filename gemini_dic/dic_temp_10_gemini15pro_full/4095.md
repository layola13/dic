aim-at:/ˈeɪm æt/ | phrasal verb | 瞄准；针对；以…为目标 | to point or direct something, usually a weapon, at a target; to intend something to influence or affect a particular group of people |  The hunter aimed at the deer. 猎人瞄准了鹿。

He aimed his remarks at the opposition. 他的话是针对反对派的。 | 近义词：target, direct, intend for | 反义词：miss, avoid | 词性: 动词短语
