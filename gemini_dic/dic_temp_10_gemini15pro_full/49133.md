deistically:/ˌdeɪˈɪstɪkli/
adverb
神论地; 自然神论地
In a manner characteristic of a deist; from the perspective of deism.
He argued deistically that God created the universe but does not intervene in its affairs.  他以自然神论者的观点争辩说，上帝创造了宇宙，但不干预宇宙的事务。
Synonym: naturalistically
Antonym: theistically
词性: 副词
