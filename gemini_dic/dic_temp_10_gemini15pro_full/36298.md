coherer:/koʊˈhɪrər/ |n.|粉末检波器，金属检波器|A device used to detect radio waves, consisting of a tube containing loosely packed metal filings whose electrical resistance decreases when electromagnetic radiation is incident, used especially in early radio receivers.|The coherer was a crucial component in early wireless telegraphy systems. 粉末检波器是早期无线电报系统中的关键部件。|Synonyms: detector, receiver|Antonyms: transmitter, emitter|noun
