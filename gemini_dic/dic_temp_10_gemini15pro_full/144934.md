overabounded:/ˌoʊvərəˈbaʊndɪd/
verb
过多；过剩；极其丰富
existed or occurred in excessive amounts
例句：Fish overabounded in the lake. 湖里的鱼极其丰富。
例句：Kindness overabounded in her heart.  她的心里充满了仁慈。
近义词：overflowed, teemed, proliferated, superabounded
反义词：lacked, dwindled, diminished
词性: v.
