overswim:/ˌoʊvərˈswɪm/ | v. | 游过；游得过远；过度游泳 | To swim across or beyond a certain point; to swim too far or for too long. | He overswam the designated area and was disqualified. 他游过了指定的区域，被取消了资格。


The child nearly overswam his strength and had to be rescued.  这孩子几乎游过了他的体力极限，不得不被救出来。


| 近义词: outswim | 反义词: - | 词性: 动词
