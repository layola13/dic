defend:/dɪˈfɛnd/ | v. | 保卫，防御；辩护；维护 | to protect someone or something from attack; to support someone or something that is being criticized |  The soldiers defended the city against the invaders. 士兵们保卫城市抵御侵略者。

She defended her decision to quit her job. 她为她辞职的决定辩护。 | 近义词: protect, guard, support, justify | 反义词: attack, oppose, prosecute | 词性: 动词

defend:/dɪˈfɛnd/ | n. | 防御；保卫（罕见）| the act of defending or protecting something |  His defend of the castle was heroic. 他对城堡的保卫是英勇的。| 近义词: protection, defense | 反义词: attack, offense | 词性: 名词 (罕用)

词源: From Middle English *defenden*, from Old French *defendre*, from Latin *dēfendere* (“to ward off, repel, protect”), from *dē* (“from, away from”) + *fendere* (“to strike, thrust”).
