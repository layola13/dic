overpiteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈpɪtiəsnəs/| n.|过度怜悯，过分同情|An excessive or exaggerated expression of pity or compassion.  It implies an almost cloying or insincere level of sympathy.|Her overpiteousness towards the stray cat was a bit much; it was just a scratch, not a broken leg.|近义词：oversentimentality, excessive pity, maudlin sympathy|反义词:  hard-heartedness, callousness, indifference|词性:noun
