engent: /ˈendʒənt/ | n. | 代理人；特工；产生者 | An engent is someone or something that produces a particular effect or result.  It can also refer to a person who acts on behalf of another. | The virus was the engent of the disease.  这种病毒是这种疾病的病原体。 The lawyer acted as her engent in the sale of the property. 律师在出售房产时担任她的代理人。|  近义词: agent, representative, factor, cause | 反义词: patient, recipient | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *engēns, present participle of engere “to beget, produce.” Related to ingenious.
