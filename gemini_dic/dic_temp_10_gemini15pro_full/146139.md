overemulated:/ˌoʊvərˈemjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度模仿的 | Imitated to an excessive or unnecessary degree.  Having been emulated too much. | The pop star's style, once unique, became overemulated by her fans and lost its distinctiveness.  这位流行歌星的风格，曾经独一无二，却被她的粉丝过度模仿，失去了它的特色。| 近义词：overcopied, overaped, overimitated | 反义词：original, unique, innovative | 词性：形容词
