nonethnics:/ˌnɒnˈɛθnɪks/ |n.|非特定种族群体；不属于特定种族的人群|People who do not identify with or belong to a particular ethnic group are considered nonethnics.  不认同或不属于特定种族群体的人被认为是非特定种族群体。|In a truly globalized society, the concept of nonethnics might become increasingly relevant. 在一个真正全球化的社会中，非特定种族群体的概念可能变得越来越重要。|近义词：non-ethnics, mixed-race, multiracial, cosmopolitan|反义词：ethnic group, specific race|词性：名词
