fictionalize:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪz/
verb
使小说化; 将…改编成小说
To make something fictional; to create a fictional account of something real.
The movie fictionalizes the life of a famous singer. 这部电影将一位著名歌手的生活改编成了小说。
The author fictionalized his own experiences in his latest novel.  作者在他最新的小说中将自己的经历小说化了。
Synonyms: novelize, dramatize, romanticize
Antonyms: document, factualize, historicize
词性: 动词
