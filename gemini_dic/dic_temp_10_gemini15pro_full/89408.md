homotaxis:/ˌhɒməˈtæksɪs/
n.
同源平行演化，平行进化
The development of similar structures in related organisms that do not share a common ancestor having that structure.
The wings of birds and bats are an example of homotaxis, as they evolved independently. 鸟类和蝙蝠的翅膀是同源平行演化的一个例子，因为它们是独立进化的。
近义词:parallel evolution, convergent evolution
反义词:homology
词性:noun
