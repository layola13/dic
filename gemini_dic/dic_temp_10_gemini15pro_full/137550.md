nontransgressive:/ˌnɒntrænsˈɡrɛsɪv/
adjective
非越界的，不违反的，不逾矩的
Not transgressing; not violating a law or moral code.
Her art is surprisingly nontransgressive, focusing on traditional landscapes. 她的艺术出人意料地没有突破传统，专注于传统的风景画。
The politician's speech was notably nontransgressive, avoiding any controversial topics.  这位政治家的演讲明显没有逾越常规，避免了任何有争议的话题。
Synonyms: conventional, orthodox, traditional, conformist
Antonyms: transgressive, rebellious, unconventional, radical
词性: 形容词
