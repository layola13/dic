highs:/haɪz/
n.
高点；高潮；最高记录；兴旺时期
a high level or amount; the highest level or amount something reaches
The stock market reached new highs this year. 股票市场今年创下新高。
近义词：peaks, crests, summits
反义词：lows, nadirs, bottoms
词性：名词复数

high:/haɪ/
adj.
高的；高级的；高尚的；高音的
measuring a long distance from the bottom to the top; of a superior standard or quality; noble, virtuous, or moral; (of a sound) pitched at a high frequency
He climbed a high mountain. 他爬了一座高山。
近义词：tall, lofty, elevated
反义词：low, short, base
词性：形容词

high:/haɪ/
adv.
高；高高地
at or to a high level or position
The birds flew high in the sky. 鸟儿在高空飞翔。
近义词：up, aloft, overhead
反义词：low, down, below
词性：副词

high:/haɪ/
n.
高水平；高处；天空；高压区
a high level, point, or position; the sky or heavens; an area of high atmospheric pressure
The temperature reached a new high yesterday. 昨天气温达到了新高。
近义词：peak, summit, crest
反义词：low, bottom, nadir
词性：名词
