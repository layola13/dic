louse:/laʊs/ (plural lice /laɪs/) | n. |虱子；讨厌的家伙|A small, wingless, parasitic insect that lives on the skin of mammals and birds and sucks their blood. |The dog was infested with lice. 这只狗身上长满了虱子。|Synonym: parasite, vermin|Antonym: None|Noun

louse (plural louses) /laʊz/ | vt. |去除…的虱子；彻底搜查|To rid of lice; to search thoroughly.|They loused the prisoner's hair. 他们仔细检查了囚犯的头发。|Synonym: delouse, inspect|Antonym: infest|Verb
