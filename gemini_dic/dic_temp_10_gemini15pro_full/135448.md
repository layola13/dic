nonpertinence:/ˌnɒnpɜːtɪnəns/ |n.| 无关紧要；不恰当；不相干|lack of pertinence; irrelevance.  Something that is nonpertinent is not related to the subject being discussed or considered.|His comments on the weather were met with stares, their nonpertinence to the serious discussion obvious. 他关于天气的评论招来大家注视，这些评论与严肃的讨论明显无关。|Synonyms: irrelevance, inappositeness, immateriality, extraneousness|Antonyms: relevance, pertinence, germaneness|noun

nonpertinent:/ˌnɒnpɜːtɪnənt/ |adj.| 无关紧要的；不恰当的；不相干的|Not pertinent; irrelevant. Not relating to the subject being discussed or considered.|The lawyer objected to the witness's nonpertinent remarks. 律师反对证人无关紧要的言论。|Synonyms: irrelevant, immaterial, extraneous, inapplicable|Antonyms: relevant, pertinent, germane, applicable|adjective
