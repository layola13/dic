antihistorical:/ˌæntiɪˈstɔːrɪkəl/ | adj. | 反历史的，歪曲历史的 | Opposing or distorting history; presenting a false or inaccurate picture of the past. |  This antihistorical account of the war glorifies violence and ignores the suffering of civilians.  这个反历史的战争叙述美化了暴力，忽略了平民的苦难。|  revisionist, ahistorical | historical | adjective

antihistorical:/ˌæntiɪˈstɔːrɪkəl/ | adj. | 与史实不符的 | Not in accordance with history; lacking historical basis. |  His claim that the earth is flat is antihistorical. 他声称地球是平的，这与史实不符。 | inaccurate, fallacious | factual, historical | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  (from Greek anti-)
historical (adjective): relating to history (from history + -ical)
