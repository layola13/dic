ineffableness:/ɪnˈɛfəbəlnəs/
n.
不可言喻；难以形容
The quality of being too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.
The ineffableness of the divine. 神圣的不可言喻。
Synonyms: inexpressibility, indescribability, unutterability
Antonyms: expressiveness, describability, communicativeness
词性: 名词

ineffable:/ɪnˈɛfəbəl/
adj.
不可言喻的；难以形容的
Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.
The ineffable beauty of the sunset.  日落难以形容的美。
Synonyms: inexpressible, indescribable, unutterable
Antonyms: expressible, describable, communicable
词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin ineffabilis, from in- "not" + effabilis "speakable," from effari "to utter," from ex- "out" + fari "to speak."
