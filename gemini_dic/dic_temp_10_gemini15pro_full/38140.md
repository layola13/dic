compress:/kəmˈprɛs/ | v. n. |压缩，浓缩；压迫|To press together; to reduce in size or volume.  |She compressed the files into a zip folder. 她把文件压缩成一个zip文件夹。|synonyms: condense, compact, squeeze, constrict | antonyms: expand, enlarge, decompress, stretch | verb

compress:/ˈkɑːmˌprɛs/ | n. |敷布；压缩物|A pad of absorbent material pressed onto a part of the body to relieve inflammation or stop bleeding. |The nurse applied a cold compress to his forehead. 护士在他的额头上敷了一块冷敷布。|synonyms: pad, dressing, poultice | antonyms: N/A | noun
