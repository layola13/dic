diverberate:/daɪˈvɜːrbəreɪt/ /dɪˈvɜːrbəreɪt/
verb
回响， reverberate, resound, echo
To resound; to be repeated by echoes. The sound of the explosion diverberated across the valley.
The chanting diverberated through the ancient temple.  寺庙里回荡着诵经声。
近义词:resound, echo, reverberate
反义词:muffle, dampen, silence
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diverberatus*, past participle of *diverberare*, "to strike apart, scatter," from *dis-* ("apart, asunder") + *verberare* ("to beat, strike").
