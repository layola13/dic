parasitical:/ˌpærəˈsɪtɪkl/ | adj. |寄生的，寄生虫似的；依赖他人生活的，食嗟来之食的|exploiting or living off another organism like a parasite; also, figuratively, living off another person or entity without giving anything back|例句：The parasitical plant weakened the host tree. 寄生植物削弱了寄主树。|例句：He led a parasitical existence, relying entirely on his parents for money. 他过着寄生虫般的生活，完全依赖父母的钱。|近义词：dependent, leeching, parasitic, sponging |反义词:independent, self-sufficient|词性:adjective

parasitic:/ˌpærəˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. |寄生的，寄生虫似的|Same as parasitical; sometimes used specifically to refer to biological parasites.|例句：The parasitic wasp lays its eggs inside the caterpillar. 寄生蜂把卵产在毛虫体内。|例句：A parasitic infection can be difficult to treat. 寄生虫感染可能很难治疗。|近义词：parasitical, dependent, leeching, sponging|反义词:independent, self-sufficient|词性:adjective
