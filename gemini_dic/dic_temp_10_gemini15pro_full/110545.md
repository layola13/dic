limelike:/ˈlaɪmˌlaɪk/| adj.|像石灰的，像酸橙的|Resembling lime in appearance, taste, or consistency.|The substance had a limelike texture, crumbly and white.|近义词：chalky, calcareous|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词

词源: lime + like.  Lime from Old French *lim, from Latin līmus (“mud, slime”).  Like from Old English *gelīc, from Proto-Germanic *galīkaz ("having the same form").
