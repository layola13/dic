nonobviousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈɒbviəsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 非显而易见性；非显而易见之处 |  The quality of not being easily seen or understood.  It refers to inventions or discoveries that are not readily apparent to a person having ordinary skill in the art. | The patent examiner rejected the application due to lack of nonobviousness. 专利审查员因缺乏非显而易见性而驳回了申请。|  The nonobviousnesses of the solution made it even more impressive.  这个解决方案的非显而易见性让它更加令人印象深刻。 | synonyms:  subtlety, ingenuity, novelty, originality | antonyms: obviousness, clarity, simplicity, evidentness | noun
