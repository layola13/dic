nonrelations:/ˌnɒnrɪˈleɪʃənz/
n.
没有关系；非亲属关系；无关的人或事物
lack of relationship; people or things not related
例句：There were strained nonrelations between the two families. 这两个家庭之间关系紧张，形同陌路。

例句：The company severed all nonrelations with the disgraced former CEO.  公司与声名狼藉的前首席执行官断绝了一切关系。
近义词：strangers, outsiders, non-kin
反义词：relations, relatives, family, kin
词性: 名词

nonrelation:/ˌnɒnrɪˈleɪʃən/
n.
没有关系的人；无关的人或事物
a person who is not related; something that is not connected
例句：He treated her as a complete nonrelation. 他把她当作一个完全无关的人。

例句：The topic of conversation was a complete nonrelation to the meeting's agenda.  这个话题与会议议程完全无关。
近义词：stranger, outsider, non-kin
反义词：relation, relative, family member, kin
词性: 名词
