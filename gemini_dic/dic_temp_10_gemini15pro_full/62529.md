endurer:/ɪnˈdjʊərər/| n. |忍受者，忍耐者|Someone who endures or suffers something patiently or for a long time.  A person who can tolerate hardship or pain.|例句：He was an endurer of great pain. 他是一个忍受巨大痛苦的人。|例句：She is known as an endurer of hardship. 她以能吃苦耐劳而闻名。|近义词：sufferer, tolerator, withstander|反义词:quitter, avoider|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *endurer, from Old French endureor, endurour (“one who endures”), from endurer (“to endure”),  from Latin indūrāre (“to harden, make hard, inure”), from in- (“in, into”) + dūrus (“hard”).
