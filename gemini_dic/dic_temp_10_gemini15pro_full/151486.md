parrel:/ˈpærəl/| n.|桶箍；水平杆；梯级；(尤指旧时船上的)梯子；|A horizontal bar or rung, especially one of a series forming a ladder, as on a ship's mast.|The sailor climbed the parrel to the crow's nest.|近义词：rung, crossbar, ladder|反义词:|n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *parel, from Old French parel, perel (“equal, similar, like”), from Latin parilis (“equal”).  The sense development likely arose from the parallel bars or rungs of a ladder.
