nonadventurousnesses:/ˌnɒnədˈvɛntʃərəsnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏冒险精神；不爱冒险
lack of adventurousness; unwillingness to take risks
His nonadventurousness kept him from trying new things. 他的不爱冒险让他不敢尝试新事物。

近义词: timidity, cautiousness, conservatism
反义词: adventurousness, daring, boldness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not
adventurous (adjective): willing to take risks or try new methods, ideas, or experiences
-ness (suffix): forms nouns expressing a state or quality
