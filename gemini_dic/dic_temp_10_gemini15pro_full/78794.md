gilot:/ˈʒiːloʊ/ | n. | 吉洛, (一种斜切刀) | A gilot is a knife with an oblique blade used for cutting leather.  It's also sometimes used in bookbinding. |  He used a gilot to trim the edges of the leather. 他用一把吉洛刀修剪皮革的边缘。

 The bookbinder skillfully used the gilot to pare the leather.  装订工人熟练地使用吉洛刀削薄皮革。 | 近义词:  knife, cutter | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
