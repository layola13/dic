agented:/ˈeɪdʒəntɪd/
adjective
代理的；由代理人处理的
Having an agent; represented by an agent.  Represented or arranged by a talent or booking agent.
The agented actor quickly landed a role in a major film.  这位有经纪人的演员很快就在一部大片中获得了一个角色。
She was agented by one of the top firms in Hollywood. 她由好莱坞的顶级公司之一代理。
represented, managed
unrepresented, independent
词性: 形容词
