luthern:/ˈluːθərn/| adj. n.|路德教的，路德宗信徒|Relating to Martin Luther, his teachings, or the Protestant churches that follow them. A follower of Martin Luther or the Lutheran Church.|例句：He was raised in a Lutheran household. 他在一个路德教家庭长大。

例句：The Lutheran church is holding a service this Sunday. 路德教堂本周日举行礼拜。|近义词：Protestant|反义词：Catholic|词性：形容词，名词

Etymology:  From the German *Lutherisch*, derived from the surname of Martin Luther (1483-1546), the German theologian who initiated the Protestant Reformation.  The suffix *-isch* forms adjectives meaning "pertaining to" or "of the nature of."
