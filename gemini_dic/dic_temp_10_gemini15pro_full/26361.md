by-election:/ˌbaɪ ɪˈlekʃən/ | n. | 补选 | A special election held between regular elections to fill a vacancy, usually caused by the death or resignation of the incumbent. |  A by-election was called after the local MP resigned.  当地议员辞职后举行了补选。 | synonyms: special election | antonyms: general election | noun

by-election:/ˌbaɪ ɪˈlekʃən/ | n. | 补缺选举 | An election held to fill a political office that has become vacant between general elections. | The by-election will be held next month. 补选将于下个月举行。 | synonyms: supplementary election | antonyms: general election | noun
