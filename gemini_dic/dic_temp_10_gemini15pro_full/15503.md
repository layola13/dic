baenopod: /ˈbeɪnoʊpɒd/
noun
贝诺足类动物 (一种已灭绝的节肢动物)
An extinct arthropod belonging to a group known as Baenopoda, characterized by their unique leg structure.
The baenopod fossil provided valuable insights into the evolution of arthropods.  贝诺足类动物化石为了解节肢动物的进化提供了宝贵的线索。
The museum displayed a well-preserved baenopod specimen.  博物馆展出了一件保存完好的贝诺足类动物标本。
近义词: arthropod 节肢动物
反义词: None (由于是已灭绝的特定物种，没有直接的反义词)
词性: n.
