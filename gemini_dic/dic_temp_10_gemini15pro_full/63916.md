epiphyseal: /ˌɛpɪˈfɪziəl/ | n. | 骨骺 | The epiphysis, the rounded end of a long bone. |  Damage to the epiphyseal can stunt growth. 对骨骺的损害会阻碍生长。| Synonyms:  physis, growth plate | Antonyms: diaphysis | noun
