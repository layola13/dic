debateful:/dɪˈbeɪtfʊl/
adjective
可争辩的，值得讨论的
Open to debate; debatable.  Characterized by or causing debate.
The merits of the new policy are debateful. 新政策的优点值得商榷。
近义词:debatable, arguable, disputable, controversial
反义词:undebatable, indisputable, unquestionable
词性:adjective
