interculture: /ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃər/ | n. | 跨文化，文化间的 |  The study of intercultural communication is becoming increasingly important in our globalized world.  跨文化交际研究在当今全球化的世界中变得越来越重要。 |  例句：Intercultural understanding is crucial for successful international business. 跨文化理解对于成功的国际业务至关重要。 | 近义词：cross-cultural, multicultural | 反义词: monocultural | 词性: 名词


intercultural: /ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 跨文化的，文化间的 |  Intercultural exchanges can broaden students' perspectives. 跨文化交流可以拓宽学生的视野。 |  例句：She is interested in intercultural relationships and the challenges they face. 她对跨文化关系及其面临的挑战感兴趣。 | 近义词：cross-cultural, multicultural | 反义词: monocultural | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + culture.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Culture" comes from the Latin word "cultura," meaning "cultivation" or "growing."  The word "interculture" therefore refers to the interaction or exchange between different cultures.
