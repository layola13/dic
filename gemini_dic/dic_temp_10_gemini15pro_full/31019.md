challenging:/ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/| adj.|挑战性的，考验人的，有挑战性的|Stimulating or demanding considerable effort and skill.  Requiring full use of one's abilities or resources.|Climbing Mount Everest is a challenging task. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项具有挑战性的任务。|近义词：difficult, demanding, arduous, strenuous, tough|反义词：easy, simple, straightforward, undemanding|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "challenge," which comes from the Old French "chalongier" (to dispute, accuse), ultimately from the Latin "calumniare" (to accuse falsely).  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into an adjective indicating a quality or characteristic.
