compotation:/ˌkɒmpəˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 欢饮；畅饮；共饮 | The act of drinking together; a convivial drinking bout. | We engaged in a compotation to celebrate our victory. 我们举杯畅饮庆祝胜利。 |  The king and his knights enjoyed a hearty compotation after the battle. 国王和他的骑士们在战斗后尽情畅饮。 | 近义词：carousal, revelry, bacchanal, debauch | 反义词：abstinence, sobriety | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *compotatio, from Latin compōtō (“I drink together”), from com- (“together”) + pōtō (“I drink”).
