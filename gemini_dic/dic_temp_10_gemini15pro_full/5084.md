allantoid: /əˈlæntɔɪd/ | n. adj. | 尿囊，尿膜的 |  A vascular fetal membrane formed as an outgrowth of the hindgut of the embryo. It is found in reptiles, birds, and mammals. In reptiles and birds, it stores metabolic wastes and performs gas exchange; in mammals, it forms part of the placenta. | The allantoid helps the developing embryo exchange gases and handle liquid waste. 尿囊帮助发育中的胚胎交换气体和处理液体废物。 |  chorion, amnion | yolk sac | noun, adjective

allantoic: /ˌælənˈtɔɪɪk/ | adj. | 尿囊的 | Relating to the allantois. |  The allantoic fluid provides cushioning for the embryo. 尿囊液为胚胎提供缓冲。 |  chorionic, amniotic |  | adjective
