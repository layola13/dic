maltobioses: /ˌmɔːltəʊˈbaɪoʊsiːz/ /ˌmɑːltəʊˈbaɪoʊsiːz/
| n. |麦芽二糖|A reducing disaccharide formed by the condensation of two glucose units linked through carbons 1 and 4.  It is a product of starch hydrolysis and is further broken down into glucose by the enzyme maltase.|Maltobioses are important intermediates in the digestion of starch.|近义词: disaccharide|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps polysaccharide as a contrasting concept)|词性: noun
