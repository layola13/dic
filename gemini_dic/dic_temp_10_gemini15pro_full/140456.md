old-buzzard:/oʊld ˈbʌzərd/ | n. |老家伙，老顽固（通常指男性）| An informal, often derogatory term for an old man, typically implying stubbornness or cantankerousness. | Example: That old buzzard refused to let the kids play on his lawn. 那个老家伙不让孩子们在他的草坪上玩。| Example:  He's a bit of an old buzzard, but he's got a heart of gold. 他有点顽固，但心地善良。 | Synonym: codger, curmudgeon, geezer | Antonym: youngster, youth | noun
