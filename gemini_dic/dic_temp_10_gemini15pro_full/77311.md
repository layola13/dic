gatewise:/ˈɡeɪtwaɪz/
adjective
逐个门的；按门的；通过门的
In a manner relating to or controlled by individual gates.
The security system checks each person gatewise.  安全系统逐个门地检查每个人。
The stadium was evacuated gatewise to avoid a stampede. 体育场按门疏散，以避免踩踏事件。
Near synonyms: individually, separately, one by one
Antonyms: all at once, simultaneously, together
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "gate" + "-wise" (suffix meaning "in the manner of," "in the direction of," or "with respect to").
