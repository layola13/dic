liberalizing: /ˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪŋ/
Verb, present participle
使自由化；放宽限制
Making something less strict or controlled, allowing more freedom.
The government is liberalizing trade policies. 政府正在放宽贸易政策。
The new regulations are liberalizing the market. 新规章正在使市场自由化。

近义词: loosening, relaxing, easing, deregulating
反义词: restricting, tightening, controlling, regulating
词性: Verb (present participle)
