anticommercialist:/ˈænti kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪst/| adj. |反商业主义的；反对商业化的|Opposed to commercialism or the pursuit of profit as the primary value.|She has a very anticommercialist attitude and refuses to buy anything advertised on television.|近义词：noncommercial, uncommercial|反义词: pro-business, commercial|词性:形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
commercial (adjective): related to commerce or trade
-ist (suffix): forming nouns and adjectives denoting a person who practices or adheres to a particular doctrine or system
