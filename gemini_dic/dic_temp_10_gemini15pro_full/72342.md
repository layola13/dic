flowerbed: /ˈflaʊərˌbed/ | n. |花坛，花圃 |A plot of ground where flowers are planted. |  The gardener planted roses in the flowerbed. 园丁在花坛里种了玫瑰。

The flowerbed was full of colorful tulips. 花坛里满是五颜六色的郁金香。

| 近义词: garden, border | 反义词:  (No direct opposite) | 词性: noun
