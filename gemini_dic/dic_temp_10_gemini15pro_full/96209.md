indexations:/ˌɪndɛkˈseɪʃənz/| n. |索引，标引，指数化|The process of creating an index or a series of indexes, often used in finance and economics to adjust values for inflation or other factors.  It can also refer to the indexes themselves.|例句：The government uses indexations to adjust pensions for inflation. 政府使用指数化来调整养老金以应对通货膨胀。

例句：The indexations of salaries helps to maintain purchasing power.  工资的指数化有助于维持购买力。|近义词：adjustments, calibrations, corrections|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
