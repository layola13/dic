charlatan:/ˈʃɑːrlətən/ | n. |江湖骗子；吹牛大王|A person falsely claiming to have a special knowledge or skill; a fraud.| He posed as a doctor, but he was really just a charlatan. 他装扮成医生，但实际上他只是一个骗子。|Synonyms: fraud, imposter, quack, fake, sham|Antonyms: expert, professional, genuine|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *charlatan*, from Italian *ciarlatano*, from *ciarlare* "to chatter," of imitative origin.
