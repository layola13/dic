mockage: /ˈmɒkɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːkɪdʒ/ (US) | n. | 嘲弄；愚弄；仿制品 | The act of mocking or deriding; something designed to ridicule or imitate something else in a derogatory way. |  His speech was met with mockage and laughter. 他的演讲遭到了嘲笑和讥讽。

The cheap knock-off was a mere mockage of the original design.  这个廉价的仿制品只是对原设计的拙劣模仿。 | 近义词：mockery, derision, ridicule, scorn, imitation, parody | 反义词：praise, admiration, respect, flattery | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *mockage, from Old French *mocage, from moquer (“to mock”), of uncertain origin.
