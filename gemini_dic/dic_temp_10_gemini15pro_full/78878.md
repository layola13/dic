ginkgo:/ˈɡɪŋkoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɪŋkɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. |银杏|A large Chinese tree with fan-shaped leaves and yellow fruit. Its nuts are edible and its extract is used in herbal medicine. |例句：The ginkgo tree in my yard turns a beautiful golden color in the fall. 我院子里的银杏树在秋天变成了美丽的金色。

例句：Ginkgo biloba extract is said to improve memory. 据说银杏叶提取物可以改善记忆力。|近义词：maidenhair tree|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Japanese *ginkyō* (later spelled *ichō*), from Chinese 銀杏 *yínxìng*, meaning "silver apricot".
