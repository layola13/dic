half-civil:/ˌhæfˈsɪvəl/
adjective
半文明的;勉强文明的;不太礼貌的
Partially civil; exhibiting a limited degree of civilized behavior; somewhat rude or impolite.
He gave a half-civil nod as he brushed past. 他擦肩而过时勉强点了点头。
Near synonyms:  rude, impolite, disrespectful, discourteous, uncivil, curt, brusque
Antonyms:  civil, polite, courteous, respectful, gracious
