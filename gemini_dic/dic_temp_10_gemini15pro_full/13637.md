attitudinarian:/ˌætɪtjuːdɪˈnɛəriən/
noun.
教条主义者；装腔作势者
A person who is excessively concerned with certain principles, especially in religion.  Someone who affects a particular attitude, often to impress others.
例句：He was known as an attitudinarian, always ready to debate the finest points of doctrine. 他以教条主义者而闻名，总是准备好辩论教义的细微之处。

例句：She struck me as an attitudinarian, more interested in appearing intellectual than actually being so.  她给我的印象是一个装腔作势的人，更在乎显得有学问而不是真的有学问。
近义词：dogmatist, doctrinaire, pedant, poseur
反义词：pragmatist, freethinker
