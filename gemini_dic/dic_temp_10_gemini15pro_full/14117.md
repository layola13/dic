authenticity:/ˌɔːθɛnˈtɪsɪti/ /ˌɑːθɛnˈtɪsɪti/
n.
真实性；可靠性；确实性
The quality of being authentic or genuine.
The painting’s authenticity has been questioned. 这幅画的真伪受到了质疑。
The museum guarantees the authenticity of all its exhibits.  博物馆保证其所有展品的真实性。
genuineness, veracity, validity
falsity, spuriousness, artificiality
