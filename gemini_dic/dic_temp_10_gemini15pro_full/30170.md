celebratory:/ˈsɛləbrətɔːri/ (US), /ˈsɛləbreɪtəri/ (UK)| adj.|庆祝的，庆贺的|Relating to, or marking a celebration.  Expressing or characterized by celebration.|例句：They held a celebratory dinner after winning the championship. 他们在赢得冠军后举行了一场庆祝晚宴。

例句：The atmosphere was celebratory as the graduates tossed their caps in the air. 毕业生们将帽子抛向空中，气氛一片欢腾。


近义词：festive, joyous, commemorating|反义词:mournful, somber|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *celebrātōrius, from Latin celebrātus (past participle of celebrāre ‘to frequent, celebrate’), from celeber ‘frequented, populous, famous’.
