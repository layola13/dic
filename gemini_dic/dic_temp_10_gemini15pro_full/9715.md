antitype:/ˈæntaɪtaɪp/| n.|原型，预表，对应物|Something that is represented by a type or symbol; a person or thing that corresponds to or fulfills a prefigurement.|The Passover lamb is a type, and Christ is the antitype.  逾越节的羔羊是一个预表，基督是它的原型。|The flood is seen by some as an antitype of baptism. 有些人认为洪水是洗礼的预表。|近义词:prototype, archetype, fulfillment|反义词:type, prefiguration|词性:noun
