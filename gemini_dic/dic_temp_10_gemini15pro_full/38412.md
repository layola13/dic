ETYMOLOGY->
From concerned + -ness.  "Concerned" comes from the Latin "concernere" meaning "to mix together," which evolved to mean "to pertain to" and later "to be anxious about."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
