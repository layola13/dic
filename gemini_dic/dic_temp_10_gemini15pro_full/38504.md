conciliary: /kənˈsɪliəri/ /kənˈsɪliɛri/
adjective
安抚的; 调解的; 怀柔的;  (教会)宗教会议的
Relating to or involved in the settlement of disputes.  Of or relating to a council, especially an ecclesiastical council.
The government adopted a conciliary approach to the rebels. 政府对叛乱分子采取了安抚政策。
The conciliary decree was issued by the Pope.  教皇颁布了宗教会议的法令。

近义词: appeasing, mediating, pacifying, irenic,  synodal
反义词: antagonistic, hostile, aggressive,  confrontational
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *conciliaris, from Latin concilium (“council, assembly”).
