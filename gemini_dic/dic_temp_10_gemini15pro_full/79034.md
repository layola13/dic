give ear:/ɡɪv ˈɪər/ |v. (phrasal verb)|倾听，注意 |To listen attentively; to pay heed.|例句：The king refused to give ear to the people's complaints. 国王拒绝听取人民的抱怨。

例句：Give ear to my words and you will understand.  留心听我说，你就会明白。|近义词：listen, heed, pay attention, attend|反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect|词性：动词短语

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *given ere*, from Old English *giefan ēare*  "to give ear", composed of *giefan*  "to give" and *ēare* "ear".  The meaning shifted from physically offering one's ear to listen to simply paying attention.
