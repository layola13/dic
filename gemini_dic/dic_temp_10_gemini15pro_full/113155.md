lupin:/ˈluːpɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpɪn/ (UK)| n.|羽扇豆，鲁冰花|A plant with tall spikes of brightly coloured flowers, belonging to the pea family.  It is also used to refer to the seed of this plant, which can be eaten.|Lupins grow well in sandy soil. 羽扇豆在沙质土壤中生长良好。

The lupin seeds are bitter but can be eaten after soaking. 羽扇豆的种子很苦，但浸泡后可以食用。|近义词：bluebonnet (especially for blue varieties) |反义词:|noun

lupine:/ˈluːpaɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpaɪn/ (UK)| adj.|像狼的，狼性的|Of or relating to wolves; wolflike.|He had a lupine hunger after his long journey.  他长途跋涉后，像狼一样饥饿。

She gave him a lupine stare, full of suspicion. 她像狼一样盯着他，充满了怀疑。|近义词：wolfish|反义词:|adjective
