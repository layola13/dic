ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *montambanco* (literally "mount on a bench"), from the practice of charlatans and vendors mounting a bench in a public place to sell their wares or perform their tricks.  This ultimately comes from *montare* ("to mount") and *banco* ("bench").
