backstreet:/ˈbækˌstriːt/ (US) /ˈbækˌstriːt/ (UK)| n. |后街；偏僻街道|A street away from the main thoroughfares, often narrow and quiet.|We walked down a quiet backstreet to avoid the traffic. 我们沿着一条安静的后街走，以避开交通。|近义词：alley, side street, lane|反义词：main street, avenue|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From 'back' + 'street'. 'Back' in this context implies secondary or less important, and 'street' refers to a public road in a city or town.
