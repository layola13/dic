buscon: /ˈbʌskən/ | n. | 侦探，密探；搜索者 | A person who searches diligently, especially for information or evidence.  A detective or investigator. | He worked as a buscon for a private investigation firm. 他在一家私家侦探公司当侦探。|  Synonyms: detective, investigator, sleuth, searcher | Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct antonym for a person who searches) | noun

Etymology: From Spanish *busca* ("search") +‎ *-ón* (augmentative suffix). Related to "buscar" (to search, to look for).
