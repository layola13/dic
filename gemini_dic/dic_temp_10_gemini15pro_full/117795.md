melilot:/ˈmelɪlɒt/ (US) /ˈmelɪlɒt/ (UK)| n.|香甜三叶草，草木樨|A plant of the pea family (Fabaceae), with small yellow or white flowers and a sweet scent, used as fodder and for making hay. It is also a source of coumarin, which gives it a vanilla-like fragrance and can be used in perfumes and flavorings.|The air was filled with the sweet scent of melilot.|近义词:sweet clover, yellow sweet clover, white sweet clover|反义词:|noun
