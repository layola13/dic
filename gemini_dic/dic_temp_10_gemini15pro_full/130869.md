nonarchitectures:/nɒnˈɑːrkɪtɛktʃəz/
|n.|非架构，非建筑；(通常指计算机)非体系结构|A term, often used in computing, to refer to systems or designs that lack a coherent or well-defined structure, or that deviate significantly from established architectural principles.  It can also refer to things that are not buildings or structures in a traditional sense.|The presentation covered various nonarchitectures, including ad-hoc networks and legacy systems. 演示涵盖了各种非架构，包括临时网络和遗留系统。 The artist's installation explored the concept of nonarchitectures in urban spaces. 这位艺术家的装置探索了城市空间中非架构的概念。|Near Synonyms: unstructured systems, informal designs, non-buildings|Antonyms: architectures, structured systems, formal designs, buildings|Noun
