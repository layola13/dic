ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *arsenic (modern French arsenic), from Medieval Latin arsenicum, from Greek arsenikon "arsenic trisulfide," related to arsenikos "masculine, potent," perhaps from Persian zarnikh "gold pigment, orpiment."
