audibilities:/ˌɔːdɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可闻度，可听度|The quality or state of being audible; the degree to which something can be heard. |The audibilities of the different instruments varied depending on their position on the stage.  不同乐器在舞台上的位置不同，它们的清晰度也不同。|近义词：clarity, distinctness, loudness|反义词：inaudibility, faintness|noun
