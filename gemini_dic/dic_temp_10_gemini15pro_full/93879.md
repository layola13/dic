immolators:/ˈɪməˌleɪtərz/
n.
祭祀者，自焚者
A person who kills as a sacrificial victim, especially by fire.
The immolators prepared the sacrifice for the ancient ritual. 祭祀者为古代仪式准备了祭品。

The monk was one of the immolators protesting the war.  这名僧侣是抗议战争的自焚者之一。

synonyms: sacrificers, victims (when self-inflicted)
antonyms: rescuers, savers
noun
