contriver:/kənˈtraɪvər/ | n. | 设计者，发明者，策划者 |  A person who cleverly invents or devises something. |  He was the contriver of a new type of engine. 他是新型引擎的设计者。 | inventor, designer, deviser, schemer, planner |  -- | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *contrive*, from Old French *controver* "to find, invent, compose," from Latin *controvere* "to come together, assemble, collect," from *com-* (together) + *trovare* (to find).
