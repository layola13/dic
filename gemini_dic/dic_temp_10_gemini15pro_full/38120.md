comprehendingly:/ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛndɪŋli/ | adv. | 理解地，领悟地 | In a way that shows understanding or comprehension. | She nodded comprehendingly as he explained the complex situation. 她在他解释复杂情况时理解地点了点头。| 近义词: understandingly, knowingly, perceptively | 反义词: uncomprehendingly, blankly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From comprehend (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Comprehend comes from the Latin "comprehendere," meaning "to grasp, seize, or understand."
