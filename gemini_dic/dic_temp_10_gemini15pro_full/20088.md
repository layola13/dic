bioscope:/ˈbaɪəskəʊp/ | n. |电影放映机；电影院|A device for viewing moving pictures; a cinema. |Example 1:  We went to the bioscope every Saturday afternoon. 我们每个星期六下午都去看电影。

Example 2: The old bioscope was replaced with a modern cinema. 老式的电影放映机被现代化的电影院取代了。|Synonym: cinema, movie theater, picture house |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "home theater" as a contrasting venue) | Noun
