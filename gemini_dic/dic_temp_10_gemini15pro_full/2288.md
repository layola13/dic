adenophyllous:/ˌædɪnəˈfɪləs/
adjective
腺叶的；腺体丰富的
Having or bearing glands, especially on the leaves.  Relating to or characterized by the presence of glands, especially oil glands, on the leaves or other parts of a plant.
The adenophyllous plant secreted a sticky resin.  这种腺叶植物分泌一种粘稠的树脂。
The botanist identified the species as adenophyllous due to the prominent glands on its leaves.  植物学家根据其叶子上明显的腺体将该物种鉴定为腺叶植物。
glandular, glanduliferous
eglandular
adjective
