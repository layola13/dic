farreation:/ˌfærɪˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 古罗马结婚仪式，谷物婚姻 | An ancient Roman form of marriage, the highest and most solemn of the three forms recognized by law, performed with a special ceremony involving the offering of a cake made of spelt (far) to Jupiter. | The farreation ceremony was reserved for patricians and involved a complex ritual with specific offerings and prayers. 古罗马的谷物婚姻仪式是为贵族保留的，它包含复杂的仪式，特定的祭品和祈祷。 |  The historical records suggest that farreation was a less common form of marriage even among the patrician class.  历史记录表明，即使在贵族阶级中，谷物婚姻也是一种不太常见的婚姻形式。 |  confarreatio, holy matrimony | divorce, annulment | noun
