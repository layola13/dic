drizzliest:/ˈdrɪzliɪst/| adj.|最细雨蒙蒙的，毛毛雨最大的|Characterized by the most frequent or heaviest drizzle.|It was the drizzliest day of the summer. 那是夏天毛毛雨最多的一天。|近义词：dampest, wettest|反义词：driest, sunniest|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From drizzle + -iest (superlative suffix). Drizzle comes from Old English *drisle.
