glitter:/ˈɡlɪtər/ (US), /ˈɡlɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|闪光；灿烂；亮粉|Small, sparkling pieces of metal or plastic used for decoration.|She sprinkled glitter on the Christmas tree. 她在圣诞树上撒了亮粉。|近义词：sparkle, shimmer|反义词：dullness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glitren, frequentative of *glīten (“to shine, glitter”), from Old Norse glita (“to glitter, shine”), from Proto-Germanic *glītaną (“to shine, gleam”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰlei- (“to shine”).
