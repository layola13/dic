ceviches:/səˈviːtʃeɪz/
n.
酸橘汁腌鱼(复数)
A seafood dish typically made from fresh raw fish cured in citrus juices, such as lemon or lime, and spiced with ají or chili peppers.  It is popular in the coastal regions of Latin America.
We ordered two ceviches, one with shrimp and one with scallops. 我们点了两份酸橘汁腌鱼，一份是虾的，一份是扇贝的。
