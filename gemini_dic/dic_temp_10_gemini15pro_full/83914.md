half-blindly:/ˌhɑːf ˈblaɪndli/ | adv. | 半盲目地，不完全清楚地 | Doing something with limited awareness or understanding, as if partially blind. | She half-blindly stumbled through the dark room, searching for the light switch. 她半盲目地在黑暗的房间里摸索，寻找电灯开关。|  He half-blindly signed the contract without reading it carefully. 他没有仔细阅读就稀里糊涂地签了合同。 | 近义词: partially sightedly, dimly, vaguely, unclearly | 反义词: clearly, knowingly, deliberately | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"half" (Old English healf) + "blind" (Old English blind) + "-ly" (Old English -lice, adverbial suffix)
