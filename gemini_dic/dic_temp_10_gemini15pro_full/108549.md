lazing:/ˈleɪzɪŋ/| v.| 懒散，无所事事 |spending time in a relaxed, inactive way; relaxing idly.|  He spent the afternoon lazing on the beach. 他整个下午都在海滩上懒洋洋地晒太阳。| 近义词：relaxing, lounging, idling | 反义词：working, toiling, striving | 词性: 动词(Present Participle of "laze")
词源: From the verb "laze," which comes from Middle English "lasen," meaning "to be idle."
