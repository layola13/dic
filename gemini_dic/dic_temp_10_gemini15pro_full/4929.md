aliquant:/ˈælɪkwɒnt/| adj.|不可整除的，包含余数的|An aliquant part of a number is a number that does not divide it evenly, leaving a remainder.|15 is an aliquant part of 100. 15是100的不可整除部分。

2. The aliquant sum is less than the total.  这个不可整除的总和小于总数。|近义词:uneven, incommensurable|反义词:aliquot, divisor|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *aliquantus, from Latin alius (“other”) + quantus (“how much”).
