hyperresponsiveness:/ˌhaɪpərɪspɑːnsɪvnəs/| n. |过度反应，高反应性|The state of being excessively or abnormally responsive to a stimulus.|例句：The child's hyperresponsiveness to loud noises suggests a sensory processing disorder.  这个孩子对巨大噪音的过度反应表明存在感觉处理障碍。

例句：Hyperresponsiveness to allergens can lead to severe allergic reactions. 对过敏原的过度反应会导致严重的过敏反应。


|近义词：hypersensitivity, oversensitivity|反义词:hyporeactivity, unresponsiveness|词性:noun
