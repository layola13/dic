nondiaphanousness:/ˌnɒndaɪəˈfænəsnəs/
n.
不透明性；非透明性
The nondiaphanousness of the frosted glass provided privacy.  毛玻璃的不透明性提供了私密性。
nondiaphanousness is the quality or state of being nondiaphanous, meaning not allowing light to pass through.  不透明性指的是不透光的性质或状态。
The nondiaphanousness of the curtain blocked out the morning sun. 窗帘的不透明性挡住了早晨的阳光。

近义词:opacity, opaqueness
反义词:transparency, translucence, diaphaneity
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + diaphanous + -ness.  Diaphanous comes from the Greek  "diaphanēs" meaning transparent, from "dia-" (through) + "phainein" (to show).
