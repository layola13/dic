memorist:/ˈmɛmərɪst/| n.|记忆大师，记忆力超群的人|A person with an exceptional memory, especially one who performs feats of memorization.|例句：The memorist could recite the entire phone book. 这位记忆大师能背诵整本电话簿。

例句：She was a renowned memorist, capable of recalling complex patterns and long strings of numbers. 她是一位著名的记忆大师，能够回忆起复杂的图案和长串数字。|近义词：mnemonist|反义词:forgetful person|词性:noun
