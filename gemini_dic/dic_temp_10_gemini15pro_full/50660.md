derivative:/dɪˈrɪvətɪv/ | adj. n. | 衍生的，派生的；衍生物，派生物 |Something that is derived from something else; not original.  A derivative is something which has been developed or obtained from something else.| The drug is a derivative of morphine. 这种药物是从吗啡衍生出来的。| synonyms: derived, unoriginal, secondary, copied | antonyms: original, primary, fundamental | adjective

derivative:/dɪˈrɪvətɪv/ | n. | 衍生物，派生物 |Something that is derived from something else; not original. | Many cleaning products are derivatives of petroleum. 许多清洁产品是石油的衍生物。| synonyms: by-product, offshoot, spin-off | antonyms: original, source | noun
