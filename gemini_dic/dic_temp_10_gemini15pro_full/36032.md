codiscover:/ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvər/| v. |共同发现|To discover something at the same time as someone else, often independently.| Marie and Pierre Curie codiscovered radium. 玛丽和皮埃尔·居里共同发现了镭。| Example:  Researchers from two different universities codiscovered a new species of frog in the Amazon rainforest. 两所不同大学的研究人员在亚马逊雨林共同发现了一种新的青蛙。| synonym: discover jointly, find together, unearth together| antonym: conceal, hide, ignore |verb
