/ˌɛm diː ˈtiː/ | abbreviation | Mountain Daylight Time | 山地夏令时 | Mountain Daylight Time (MDT) is observed in parts of North America during the summer months. It is 7 hours behind Coordinated Universal Time (UTC−07:00). |  Arizona does not observe daylight saving time, so during the summer it is on the same time as MDT. 亚利桑那州不遵守夏令时，所以在夏季它与山地夏令时相同。
