chili:/ˈtʃɪli/ (US) /ˈtʃɪli/ (UK)| n.|辣椒，辣椒粉，红辣椒|A small, spicy pepper or pod of a variety of capsicum used in cooking. Also refers to a dish made with chili peppers.|例句：I like my chili extra spicy. 我喜欢我的辣椒非常辣。

例句：Chili peppers are used in many cuisines. 辣椒用于许多菜肴中。|近义词：chilli, chile, pepper|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bland food")|词性:noun

chili:/ˈtʃɪli/ (US) /ˈtʃɪli/ (UK)| n.|辣椒酱|A dish of meat and beans seasoned with chili peppers.|例句：She made a big pot of chili for the game. 她为比赛做了一大锅辣椒酱。

例句：This chili is really flavorful. 这辣椒酱真够味。|近义词：chili con carne|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
