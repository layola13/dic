nonaccumulativeness:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːmjʊlətɪvnəs/
| n. |非累积性，非累加性|The property of not accumulating or building up over time.  This can refer to things like knowledge, skills, or effects.|The nonaccumulativeness of the drug means that it needs to be taken regularly.|近义词：noncumulative, transient, ephemeral|反义词:cumulative, additive, persistent|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + accumulative + -ness.  "Accumulative" comes from the Latin "accumulāre" (to heap up).
