overrationalizations:/ˌoʊvərˌræʃənələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度合理化 | The act of creating excessive or elaborate justifications for one's actions or beliefs, often to avoid facing underlying motivations or uncomfortable truths. |  He came up with a series of overrationalizations for his lateness. 他为自己的迟到编造了一系列过度合理的理由。|  Overjustifications, rationalizations |  Understatements, admissions | noun
