code-naming:/ˈkoʊd neɪmɪŋ/ |n.|代码命名，代号命名|The process of assigning code names to projects, operations, or individuals to maintain secrecy or for easier reference.|The code-naming of the project was "Phoenix."  该项目的代号是“凤凰”。|synonyms:  labeling, designating, classifying|antonyms: disclosing, revealing|noun

code-naming:/ˈkoʊd neɪmɪŋ/ |adj.|代码命名的|Used to describe something related to the process of assigning code names.|The code-naming convention used by the agency is highly complex. 该机构使用的代码命名惯例非常复杂。|synonyms: classified, clandestine|antonyms: public, open|adjective
