casco:/ˈkæskoʊ/ (US), /ˈkɑːskoʊ/ (UK)| n.|头盔，安全帽；(船的)船体；(水果等的)外壳，外皮|A protective helmet, especially one worn by cyclists, motorcyclists, or soldiers. Also refers to the hull of a ship or the outer covering of a fruit.|例句：He always wears a casco when he rides his motorcycle. 他骑摩托车时总是戴着头盔。|例句：The ship's casco was damaged in the storm. 船体在暴风雨中受损了.|近义词：helmet, shell, hull|反义词:|n.
