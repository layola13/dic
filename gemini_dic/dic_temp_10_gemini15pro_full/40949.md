cooperage:/ˈkuːpərɪdʒ/| n.|制桶业，制桶厂，桶|The cooperage is where barrels are made and repaired.  制桶厂是制造和修理桶的地方。|A traditional cooperage still uses handcrafted techniques.  一家传统的制桶厂仍然使用手工技术。|近义词：barrel making, barrel factory|反义词:|名词

Etymology->
From Middle English *coperage, from Old French coperie, from coper ("to make barrels"), from cupe ("barrel"). Ultimately from Latin cupa ("tub, cask, vat").
