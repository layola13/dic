dropa:/ˈdrɒpə/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpə/ (US)| n.| 投递员；送货员；（旧时）布商 |A person who delivers something, especially goods or mail; (archaic) a draper. | The dropa brought the groceries to my door. 送货员把食品杂货送到我家门口。| 近义词: delivery person, courier, mail carrier | 反义词: recipient, receiver | 词性: 名词

dropa (as a verb - archaic): /ˈdrɒpə/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpə/ (US)| v. | （旧时）经营布匹生意 | (archaic) To deal or trade in cloth or drapery. | He dropaed fine silks and linens in the market. 他在市场上经营精美的丝绸和亚麻布。| 近义词: sell, trade (in cloth) | 反义词: buy, purchase | 词性: 动词
