locoing:/ˈloʊkoʊɪŋ/
verb
发疯；失去理智；行为古怪
acting crazy or strange; behaving in an unusual or eccentric way
He's been locoing lately, staying up all night and talking to himself. 他最近有点疯疯癫癫的，整晚不睡觉，自言自语。
近义词:  crazing, going mad, acting up
反义词:  sane, rational, normal
词性: verb
