acturience: /ˈæktʃʊəriəns/
noun
强烈愿望; 渴望
A restless or intense desire to act.
He felt a surge of acturience, a desperate need to do something, anything, to change his situation.  他感到一阵强烈的冲动，迫切地想要做点什么，任何事，来改变他的处境。

The acturience of youth often leads to impulsive decisions. 年轻人的冲动往往导致他们做出冲动的决定。

近义词: eagerness, yearning, longing, zeal, fervor
反义词: apathy, indifference, lethargy

词性: noun (名词)

词源:  From Latin *acturientia, from acturus ("about to act") + -ientia (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  来自拉丁语 *acturientia，由 acturus（“即将行动”）+ -ientia（构成性质或状态名词的后缀）组成。
