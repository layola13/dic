cubical:/ˈkjuːbɪkl/| adj.|立方的，立方形的|Shaped like a cube; having six equal square sides.|The warehouse was filled with cubical boxes.|近义词：cubic, cube-shaped|反义词:spherical, round|词性:形容词
cubical:/ˈkjuːbɪkl/| n.|小隔间|A small partitioned area in a larger room, often used as an office space.|She worked in a small, windowless cubical.|近义词：booth, compartment|反义词:open space|词性:名词
