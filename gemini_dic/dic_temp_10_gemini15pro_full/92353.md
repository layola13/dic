hypocarp:/ˈhaɪpəʊkɑːrp/| n. |子房下位（花的子房位于花萼、花冠及雄蕊的下方）|The part of a flowering plant that bears the ovules and ripens into the fruit, especially one in which the receptacle surrounds the ovary and becomes part of the fruit.|The apple is a classic example of a fruit with a hypocarpous ovary. 苹果是子房下位果实的典型例子。|近义词: inferior ovary|反义词: epicarp, superior ovary|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek  ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + καρπός (karpós, “fruit”).
