blaxploitation:/ˌblæksˌplɔɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|黑人剥削电影（类型）|A genre of low-budget films made in the early 1970s, featuring primarily African American actors in stereotypical roles, often exploiting themes of crime, violence, and sexuality. These films were aimed at a Black audience and often showcased themes of Black power and resistance against a racist system, but were also criticized for their stereotypical portrayals and glorification of violence.|Example: "Shaft" is a classic example of a blaxploitation film.  《夏福特》是黑人剥削电影的经典例子。

Example: The blaxploitation era produced many iconic soundtracks. 黑人剥削电影时代产生了许多标志性的电影配乐。|近义词:genre, exploitation film|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
