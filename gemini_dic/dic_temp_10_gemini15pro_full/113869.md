madisterium:/mædɪˈstɪəriəm/| n. | 教师办公室，教师会议室 | A room or building used by teachers, especially for meetings or as a common work area. |  The madisterium was filled with the chatter of teachers preparing for the new school year. 教师办公室里充满了老师们为新学年做准备的喋喋不休的声音。| The headmaster called a meeting in the madisterium to discuss the new curriculum. 校长在教师会议室召集会议，讨论新课程。 | synonyms: staff room, common room, teachers' lounge, faculty room | antonyms: classroom, student lounge | noun
