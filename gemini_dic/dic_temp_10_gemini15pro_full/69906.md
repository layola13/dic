fertilizer:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n. |肥料|A substance added to soil or plants to provide nutrients essential for growth.|Farmers use fertilizer to enrich the soil.  农民用肥料来肥沃土壤。|The gardener sprinkled fertilizer around the roses. 园丁在玫瑰周围撒了肥料。|synonyms: manure, compost, plant food|antonyms: pollutant, contaminant|noun
