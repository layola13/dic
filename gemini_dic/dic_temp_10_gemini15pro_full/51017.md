deskills:/ˌdiːˈskɪl/
verb
使失去技能；降低…的技能
to reduce the level of skill required to do a job, or to reduce someone's level of skill
Automation has deskilled many factory jobs. 自动化使许多工厂工作变得不需要太多技能。
reduce the skills of, make unskilled
upskill
deprofessionalize
v.
