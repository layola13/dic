akimbo:/əˈkɪmboʊ/| adj.|叉腰的，两手叉腰的|With hands on the hips and elbows turned outward.  Characterized by having the hands on the hips and elbows bent outward.|She stood with arms akimbo, waiting impatiently. 她叉腰站着，不耐烦地等待着。|例句：The shopkeeper stood akimbo, surveying his domain.  店主叉着腰，审视着他的地盘。

例句：He stood with legs akimbo, a picture of defiance. 他叉开双腿站着，一副挑衅的样子。|近义词：askew, awry|反义词：straight, aligned|词性: adjective
