mastiff:/ˈmæstɪf/ | n. |獒犬，猛犬|A large, powerful breed of dog with a short coat, typically fawn, brindle, or apricot in color, known for its strength and historically used as a guard dog.| The mastiff barked deeply at the intruder.  这只獒犬对着入侵者狂吠。|Synonyms:  bulldog, guard dog|Antonyms:  lapdog, toy dog|Noun
