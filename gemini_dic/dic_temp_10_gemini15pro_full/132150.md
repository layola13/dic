noncuratively: /ˌnɒnˈkjʊərətɪvli/  /ˌnɑːnˈkjʊrətɪvli/
adverb
非治愈性地，姑息性地
in a way that is not intended to cure a disease, but only to reduce pain or other symptoms
The patient was treated noncuratively, focusing on pain management and comfort.  这位病人接受了非治愈性治疗，重点是疼痛管理和舒适度。
The doctor explained that the treatment was noncuratively, aimed at improving quality of life rather than curing the disease. 医生解释说，这种治疗是非治愈性的，旨在提高生活质量而不是治愈疾病。
近义词: palliatively
反义词: curatively
词性: adverb
