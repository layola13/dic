dakotahs:/dəˈkoʊtəz/
n.
达科他人（美国原住民部落）
Members of the Sioux tribes inhabiting North and South Dakota.
The Lakota, Dakota, and Nakota peoples are collectively known as the Dakotas. 拉科塔人、达科他人和纳科他人统称为达科他人。
Sioux, Lakota, Nakota
