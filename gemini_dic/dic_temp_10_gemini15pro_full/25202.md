buddleia: /ˈbʌdliə/ (US) /ˈbʌdliːə/ (UK)| n. |醉鱼草；大叶醉鱼草| A shrub with brightly colored, fragrant flowers that attract butterflies. | Example: The buddleia in my garden is covered in butterflies. 我花园里的醉鱼草上满是蝴蝶。| Example:  We planted a buddleia to attract pollinators to the yard. 我们种了一棵醉鱼草来吸引传粉者到院子里。 |Synonym: butterfly bush, summer lilac| Antonym: N/A | Noun
