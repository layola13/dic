mantas:/ˈmæntəz/
n.
蝠鲼（复数）
Large, flat, diamond-shaped fish with wing-like pectoral fins, often found in tropical and subtropical waters.
The mantas gracefully glided through the clear ocean water.  蝠鲼优雅地在清澈的海水中滑翔。
Mantas are filter feeders, consuming plankton and small fish.  蝠鲼是滤食动物，以浮游生物和小鱼为食。
Synonyms: devilfish, devil ray
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal)
