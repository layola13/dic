hyperstimulations:/ˌhaɪpərˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃənz/
|n.|过度刺激|Excessive stimulation, often to an unhealthy or undesirable degree.|The constant notifications on her phone led to hyperstimulation of her nervous system.|近义词: overstimulation, hyperarousal|反义词: hypostimulation, understimulation|词性: Noun
Etymology: From "hyper-" (Greek: over, beyond) + "stimulation" (from Latin: stimulatus, past participle of stimulare, to goad, incite).
