overflattening: /ˌoʊvərˈflætənɪŋ/ | n. |过度扁平化|Excessive flattening, often referring to economic or social structures, where differences or variations are minimized to an undesirable degree.|The overflattening of the organization led to a lack of specialized expertise. 公司组织的过度扁平化导致缺乏专业知识。| The overflattening of the income distribution has exacerbated social inequality. 收入分配的过度扁平化加剧了社会不平等。|近义词: homogenization, leveling, standardization |反义词: diversification, stratification, specialization |词性: 名词

词源: "Over-" (prefix denoting excess) + "flatten" (verb meaning to make flat) + "-ing" (suffix forming a noun from a verb).  The word is a relatively recent formation, gaining prominence in discussions of economic and organizational structures.
