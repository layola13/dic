limitate:/ˈlɪmɪteɪt/| v. |限制，限定|To restrict or confine within limits. |例句：The new law limitates the sale of alcohol. 新法律限制了酒精的销售。

例句：His limited experience limitates his job opportunities.  他有限的经验限制了他的工作机会。


|近义词：restrict, confine, limit, restrain, circumscribe|反义词:expand, enlarge, broaden, extend|词性:动词
