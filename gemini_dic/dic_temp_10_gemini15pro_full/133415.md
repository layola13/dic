/ˌnɑːnfəˈmɪliər/ | /ˌnɒnfəˈmɪliər/ | adj. | 不熟悉的，陌生的 | Not known or recognized; strange. |  例句：The street was nonfamiliar to me. 这条街对我来说很陌生。 | 近义词：unfamiliar, unknown, strange, foreign | 反义词：familiar, known, common | 词性: 形容词

nonfamiliar: 词源来自 non- (not) + familiar. "Non-" 是一个前缀，表示否定，"familiar" 源自拉丁语 "familiaris"，意思是 "of or belonging to a family or household"，引申为“熟悉的”。
