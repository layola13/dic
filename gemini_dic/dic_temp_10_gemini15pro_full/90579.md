hulkamania:/hʌlkəˈmeɪniə/

例句：The Hulkamania era was a significant period in professional wrestling.  霍克狂热时代是职业摔跤史上的重要时期。|近义词：fanaticism, craze, mania|反义词：apathy, indifference|词性:名词
