confesses:/kənˈfɛsɪz/
verb
承认，坦白
admits something reluctantly, especially wrongdoing
He confessed to stealing the money. 他承认偷了钱。
近义词:admits, acknowledges, owns up
反义词:denies, conceals
词性:v.
