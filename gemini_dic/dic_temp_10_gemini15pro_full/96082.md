indefatigable:/ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbl/
adjective
不知疲倦的；不屈不挠的
Tireless; persisting tirelessly.
例句：The indefatigable campaigner finally saw her efforts rewarded with a victory.  这位不知疲倦的活动家最终看到她的努力获得了胜利。

例句：Despite facing numerous setbacks, she remained indefatigable in her pursuit of justice. 尽管面临重重挫折，她在追求正义的道路上仍然不屈不挠。

近义词：tireless, unflagging, relentless, persistent, persevering
反义词：tired, weary, exhausted, fatigued, lethargic
词性: adjective
