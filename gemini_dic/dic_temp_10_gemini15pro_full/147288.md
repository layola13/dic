overmodernizations:/ˌoʊvərˌmɒdərnaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度现代化 | The act of modernizing something excessively, often to the detriment of traditional values or practices.  The process of making something too modern, often resulting in a loss of character or functionality. |  The overmodernizations of the city center led to the destruction of several historic buildings.  城市的过度现代化导致了几座历史建筑的破坏。

The overmodernizations in the education system prioritize standardized testing over creativity. 教育系统的过度现代化将标准化测试置于创造力之上。 | 近义词: excesses, extremes | 反义词:  underdevelopment, stagnation | 词性: 名词
