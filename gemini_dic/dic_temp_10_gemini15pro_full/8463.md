antiaristocratically:/ˌæntiˌærɪstəˈkrætɪkli/ | adv. | 反贵族地，平民化地 | In a manner opposed to aristocracy or its principles; showing disdain for aristocratic values or behaviors.  | He behaved antiaristocratically, refusing to acknowledge titles or inherited privilege. 他举止反贵族，拒绝承认头衔或继承特权。|  She dressed antiaristocratically in simple, unadorned clothes. 她穿着朴素，没有装饰，表现得反贵族。| 近义词: democratically, egalitarianly, unpretentiously | 反义词: aristocratically, snobbishly, elitistly | 词性: 副词
