desynchronized:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzd/
adjective
去同步的，不同步的
Out of synchronization; not occurring at the same time or rate.
The dancers' movements became desynchronized. 舞蹈演员的动作变得不同步了。
The audio and video are desynchronized. 音频和视频不同步。
近义词: asynchronous, out of sync, misaligned, uncoordinated
反义词: synchronized, in sync, aligned, coordinated
词性: adjective
词源: de- (prefix meaning "removal or reversal") + synchronize (from Greek syn- "together" + chronos "time") + -ized (suffix forming past participles and adjectives)
