interlocution:/ˌɪntərloʊˈkjuːʃən/ |n.|对话，交谈，问答|An instance of exchanging words; a conversation.|The interlocution between the two leaders was tense and brief. 两位领导人之间的对话紧张而简短。|Synonyms: dialogue, conversation, exchange, discourse|Antonyms: silence, monologue|noun

interlocutor:/ˌɪntərˈlɒkjʊtər/ |n.|对话者，交谈者|A person who takes part in a dialogue or conversation.|The professor acted as the interlocutor, guiding the discussion between the students.  教授充当对话者，引导学生之间的讨论。|Synonyms: speaker, conversationalist, participant|Antonyms: listener, audience|noun
