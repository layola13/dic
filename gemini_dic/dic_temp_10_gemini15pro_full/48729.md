defiler:/dɪˈfaɪlər/| n.|玷污者，亵渎者|Someone or something that defiles or makes impure; a corrupter.|The defiler of the temple was banished from the city.  玷污神庙的人被驱逐出城。|Synonyms: polluter, contaminator, profaner, desecrator|Antonyms: purifier, cleanser, sanctifier|noun


defile:/dɪˈfaɪl/| vt.|玷污，亵渎，弄脏|To make foul, dirty, or unclean; to corrupt the purity or sanctity of; to profane.|The oil spill defiled the pristine beach.  石油泄漏污染了原始的海滩。|Synonyms: pollute, contaminate, taint, corrupt, desecrate|Antonyms: purify, cleanse, sanctify|verb
