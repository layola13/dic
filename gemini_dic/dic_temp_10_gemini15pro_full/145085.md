overanxiety:/ˌoʊvəræŋˈzɑɪəti/ | n. | 过度焦虑 | Excessive anxiety; a state of being overly anxious or worried. | She suffered from overanxiety about her exams. 她对考试过度焦虑。| 近义词：apprehension, worry, solicitude, angst | 反义词：calmness, tranquility, serenity | 词性: noun

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "anxiety" (from Late Latin anxietatem (nominative anxietas) "anxiety, anguish," from Latin anxius "solicitous, uneasy, troubled," literally "choking, causing anxiety," present participle of angere "to strangle, torment")
