nonencyclopedic:/ˌnɒnɪnsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 非百科全书式的，不适合百科全书的 | Not pertaining to or characteristic of an encyclopedia; unsuitable for inclusion in an encyclopedia; lacking the qualities of objectivity, comprehensiveness, and neutrality expected of encyclopedic content. | This blog post is nonencyclopedic and focuses on personal opinions. 这篇博客文章是非百科全书式的，侧重于个人观点。|  The information provided was considered nonencyclopedic due to its biased nature.  由于其偏颇的性质，所提供的信息被认为是非百科全书式的。| 近义词: subjective, biased, informal, opinionated | 反义词: encyclopedic, objective, factual, neutral | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + encyclopedic (adjective derived from "encyclopedia")
