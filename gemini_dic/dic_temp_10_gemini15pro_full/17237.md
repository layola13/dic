bawn:/bɔːn/
n.
围场，畜栏，城堡外有围墙的庭院
A fortified enclosure for cattle or other livestock, especially one attached to a farmhouse or castle.
The children played in the castle's bawn. 孩子们在城堡的庭院里玩耍。
近义词: enclosure, courtyard, yard, pen, fold
反义词: open field
