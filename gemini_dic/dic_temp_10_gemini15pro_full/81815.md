grete:/ˈɡreɪt/ /ˈɡriːt/| adj.|伟大的，优秀的，重要的；愉快的，令人满意的；大量的，很多的|Great means large in size, extent, or amount. It can also mean of outstanding significance or importance, or excellent, wonderful.  It can also mean considerable or much.|例句：He was a great leader. 他是一位伟大的领袖。

例句：We had a great time at the party. 我们在聚会上玩得很开心。|近义词：large, big, huge, immense, vast, enormous, gigantic, colossal, tremendous, magnificent, wonderful, excellent, outstanding, remarkable, exceptional, extraordinary|反义词：small, insignificant, unimportant, poor, bad, terrible|词性：形容词
