decode:/diːˈkoʊd/ (US), /diːˈkəʊd/ (UK)| v. n.|解码，译码; 破译|To convert (a coded message) into intelligible language.  To discover the underlying meaning of.|例句：The spy decoded the secret message.  间谍破译了秘密信息。|近义词：decipher, interpret, decrypt|反义词：encode, encrypt|词性:动词, 名词

decode:/diːˈkoʊd/ (US), /diːˈkəʊd/ (UK)| n.|解码，译码; 破译|The act or process of decoding.|例句：The decode of the enemy's transmissions proved crucial. 对敌人传输信息的解码证明至关重要。|近义词：deciphering, interpretation, decryption|反义词：encoding, encryption|词性:名词
