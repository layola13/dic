dismettled:/dɪsˈmɛt(ə)ld/| adj.| 沮丧的，气馁的，灰心的 |  Deprived of spirit and courage; disheartened.  Derived from "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") and "mettled" (meaning "spirited" or "courageous").  The word implies a loss of motivation and enthusiasm. | The dismettled troops retreated after the devastating loss.  溃败的军队在遭受毁灭性损失后撤退了。

The dismettled student gave up on the difficult exam. 灰心的学生放弃了那场艰难的考试。 | Synonyms: disheartened, discouraged, demoralized, dejected | Antonyms: encouraged, heartened, spirited, emboldened | adjective
