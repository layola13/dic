logrolling:/ˈlɒɡˌrəʊlɪŋ/ | n. |互惠互利；互相吹捧；滚木运动|The practice of exchanging favors, especially in politics by reciprocal voting for each other's proposed legislation. Also refers to the sport of balancing on and maneuvering a floating log.|The two senators engaged in logrolling to get their bills passed. 这两位参议员互相交换利益以使他们的法案通过。|Synonyms: back-scratching, quid pro quo, collusion|Antonyms:  independence, impartiality|noun


logrolling:/ˈlɒɡˌrəʊlɪŋ/ | n. |滚木运动|The sport of balancing on and maneuvering a floating log.|Logrolling requires great skill and balance. 滚木运动需要高超的技巧和平衡能力。|Synonyms: birling |Antonyms: N/A|noun
