chinoiserie:/ˌʃiːnˈwɑːzəri/| n.|中国风，具有中国装饰风格的物品|A style of art, decoration, or architecture that imitates Chinese motifs and techniques.|The room was decorated with elaborate chinoiserie, featuring hand-painted wallpaper and lacquered furniture.  房间装饰着精致的中国风，特色是手绘壁纸和漆器家具。|Synonyms: orientalism, exoticism|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "Western style" or "European style")|Noun
