aperiodic:/ˌeɪpɪərɪˈɒdɪk/ | adj. | 非周期的，无周期的 | Occurring at irregular intervals; not periodic. |  The aperiodic nature of the signal made it difficult to analyze. 信号的非周期性使其难以分析。| The comet's aperiodic orbit makes it difficult to predict its return. 这颗彗星的非周期轨道使得难以预测它的回归。 |  irregular, non-periodic, nonsystematic, intermittent, sporadic | periodic, regular, cyclical | adjective
