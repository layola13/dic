overrestrictions:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstrɪkʃənz/
|n.|过度限制|The overrestrictions on children's activities can stifle their creativity. 对儿童活动的过度限制会扼杀他们的创造力。|Overrestrictions imposed by the government hampered economic growth. 政府施加的过度限制阻碍了经济增长。|近义词: limitations, constraints, regulations|反义词: freedoms, liberties|词性: 名词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "restriction"
"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively."
"restriction" comes from Late Latin "restrictionem," meaning "a holding back."
