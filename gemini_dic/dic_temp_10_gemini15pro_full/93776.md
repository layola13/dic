immense:/ɪˈmɛns/| adj.|巨大的，无限的|Extremely large or great in scale or degree.|The universe is immense and full of mysteries. 宇宙浩瀚无垠，充满了神秘。|The charity received an immense amount of donations.  该慈善机构收到了巨额捐款。|近义词：huge, vast, enormous, gigantic, colossal|反义词：tiny, small, minute, minuscule|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *immense*, from Latin *immensus* "immeasurable," from *in-* "not" (from PIE root *en* "in") + *mensus* "measured," past participle of *metiri* "to measure" (from PIE root *me-* "to measure").
