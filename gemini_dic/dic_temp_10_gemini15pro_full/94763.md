improvising: /ˈɪmprəvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
即兴创作，即兴表演
creating and performing something spontaneously, without preparation
The musicians were improvising a jazz melody.  音乐家们正在即兴创作爵士旋律。
He improvised a speech on the spot. 他当场即兴发表了一篇演讲。
synonym: ad-libbing, extemporizing, winging it
antonym: rehearsing, planning, preparing
词性: v. (动词)
