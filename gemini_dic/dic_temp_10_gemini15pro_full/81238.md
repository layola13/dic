grandstander: /ˈɡrændˌstændər/ (US), /ˈɡrænˌstændər/ (UK)| n.|哗众取宠者；卖弄者|A person who behaves in a showy or ostentatious way in an attempt to impress onlookers.|He's such a grandstander, always showing off his skills in front of a crowd. 他真是个爱出风头的人，总是在人群面前炫耀自己的技能。|
|近义词: show-off, exhibitionist, attention-seeker|反义词: wallflower, introvert|词性: noun|
