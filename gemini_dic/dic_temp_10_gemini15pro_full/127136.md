natrolite:/ˈneɪtrəˌlaɪt/| n.|钠沸石|A hydrous sodium aluminum silicate mineral, Na2Al2Si3O10·2H2O, occurring in slender, glassy crystals in cavities in basaltic rocks.|Natrolite crystals were found in the volcanic rock.|近义词：zeolite, mesolite, scolecite|反义词:|noun
