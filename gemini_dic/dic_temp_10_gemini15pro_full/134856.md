nonmodifying:/ˌnɒnˈmɒdɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 非修饰性的；不作修饰语的 |  Not able to function as a modifier, such as an adjective or adverb.  It describes a word or phrase that doesn't change the meaning of the word it relates to. |  "The" in "the book" is a nonmodifying word. “the”在“the book”中是一个非修饰词。 |  non-restrictive, attributive |  modifying, restrictive | adjective
