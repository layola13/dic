craftinesses:/ˈkræftinɪsɪz/
noun
狡猾；诡诈；熟练
the quality of being crafty; skill in deception or manipulation; dexterity.
He admired the craftinesses of the magician's tricks. 他很佩服魔术师戏法的精巧。
Synonyms: cunning, guile, slyness, wiliness, trickery, dexterity, adroitness, ingenuity
Antonyms: honesty, forthrightness, naiveté, artlessness, clumsiness
词性: 名词(plural)
