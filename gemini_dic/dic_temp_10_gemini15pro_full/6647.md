anaesthetize:/əˈniːsθətaɪz/ /æˈnɛsθəˌtaɪz/| v. |麻醉；使麻木|To induce a loss of sensation, especially pain, in all or part of the body.|The surgeon anaesthetized the patient before the operation. 外科医生在手术前给病人麻醉了。|The dentist anaesthetized my gums before pulling the tooth. 牙医在拔牙之前麻醉了我的牙龈。| 近义词:numb, drug, sedate|反义词:stimulate, awaken|词性:动词
anaesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ /ˌænɪsˈθiːziə/| n. |麻醉；麻木|Loss of sensation, especially pain, induced by drugs.|The patient was under general anaesthesia during the surgery. 病人在手术过程中处于全身麻醉状态。|Local anaesthesia is often used for minor procedures. 局部麻醉常用于小型手术。|近义词:numbness, insensibility|反义词:sensitivity, consciousness|词性:名词
anaesthetic:/ænɪsˈθɛtɪk/| n. adj. |麻醉剂；麻醉的|A substance that causes loss of sensation, especially pain.|The anaesthetic wore off after a few hours. 麻醉剂几个小时后就失效了。|The procedure was performed using a local anaesthetic.  这个手术是用局部麻醉剂进行的。|近义词:narcotic, opiate|反义词:stimulant|词性:名词,形容词
