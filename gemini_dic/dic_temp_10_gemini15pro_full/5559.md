already:/ɔːlˈredi/ /ɑːlˈrɛdi/| adv.|已经|By this or that time; previously.  Something has happened before now, sooner than expected, or by the time mentioned.|
I've already finished my homework. 我已经完成了我的家庭作业。

He had already left when I arrived. 当我到达时，他已经离开了。

|近义词：by now, previously, yet|反义词:not yet, still|词性:adverb
