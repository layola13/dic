ecotypically:/ˌiːkəˈtɪpɪkli/
adverb
生态典型地
In a way that is typical of the ecology of a particular organism or species.  Relating to an ecotype, which is a distinct form or population of a species occupying a particular habitat.
The plant ecotypically grows in shaded areas.  这种植物通常生长在阴凉地区。
Synonyms:  characteristically, typically, naturally
Antonyms:  atypically, unusually, abnormally
Part of Speech: adverb
