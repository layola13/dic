palaestra:/pəˈlɛstrə/ | n. | 角力学校，体育馆，运动场 | A place for exercise or athletic contests, especially wrestling; a gymnasium or wrestling school. | The athletes trained rigorously in the palaestra. 运动员们在体育馆里刻苦训练。

The palaestra was the center of physical and social life in ancient Greece.  角力学校是古希腊体育和社会生活的中心。 | 近义词: gymnasium, arena, stadium, training ground | 反义词: classroom, library | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From Latin *palaestra*, from Ancient Greek *παλαίστρα (palaístra)*, from *πάλαισμα (pálaisma)*, "wrestling", from *πάλλω (pállō)*, "to wrestle," "to brandish," "to quiver."
