fellow-feeling:| | |The word "fellow-feeling" combines "fellow," meaning a person sharing a particular activity, condition, etc., with another, and "feeling," meaning an emotional state or reaction.  It originally conveyed a sense of shared experience and emotion, evolving to emphasize compassion and sympathy.|例句：She expressed fellow-feeling for the victims of the earthquake. 她对地震灾民表示同情。|近义词：commiseration|反义词：hostility|词性：名词
