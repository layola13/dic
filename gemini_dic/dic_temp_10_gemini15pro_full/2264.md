adelphos: /əˈdɛlfɒs/ | n. | brother | From Ancient Greek ἀδελφός (adelphós, “brother”).  A male sibling. | He visited his adelphos in the hospital. 他去医院探望了他的兄弟。| synonym: brother, sibling | antonym: sister | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀδελφός (adelphós, “brother”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sm̥-bʰréh₂tēr (“brother”).  Cognate with Latin frater, Sanskrit bhrātā, Old Irish bráthair, Gothic brōþar, Old English brōþor (English brother).
