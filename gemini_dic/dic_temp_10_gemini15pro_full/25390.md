bulbule:/ˈbʌlbjuːl/ | n. |球茎；小球；水泡 |A small bulb, a rounded swelling, or a bubble-like structure.  Specifically refers to a small, rounded outgrowth on a plant stem, often involved in vegetative reproduction. Can also refer to a small anatomical swelling or a bubble. |  The plant produces new bulbles along its stems. 这种植物沿着茎长出新的球茎。| synonyms: bulb, nodule, swelling | antonyms: concavity, depression | noun
