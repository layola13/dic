heliometrical:/ˌhiːliəˈmetrɪkl/ | adj. | 日出同升的，与太阳同时升起的 | Relating to the measurement of the sun's diameter, or to the observation of a star's rising or setting simultaneously with the sun.  | Example 1: The heliometrical rising of Sirius was an important event in the ancient Egyptian calendar.  古埃及历法中，天狼星与太阳同升是一个重要的事件。

Example 2:  Heliometrical observations were used to determine the size and distance of celestial bodies.  日出同升观测被用来确定天体的大小和距离。 | Near Synonyms:  cosmical, heliacal | Antonyms:  N/A | Word type: Adjective
