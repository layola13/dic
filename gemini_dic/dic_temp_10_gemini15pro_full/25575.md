bumbled:/ˈbʌmbəld/ | v. | 笨手笨脚地做；结结巴巴地说 | to speak or move in a clumsy, awkward way; to stumble |  He bumbled through his speech, forgetting his lines several times. 他结结巴巴地做完演讲，好几次忘了台词。| synonyms: stumbled, fumbled, stammered, muttered | antonyms: articulated, enunciated, flowed, glided | verb

bumble:/ˈbʌmbəl/ | v. | 笨手笨脚地做；结结巴巴地说；踉跄；错误 | to speak or behave clumsily or falteringly; to stumble or blunder | She bumbled around in the dark, looking for the light switch.  她在黑暗中摸索着找电灯开关。 | synonyms: stumble, fumble, blunder, falter | antonyms: glide, sail, succeed, master | verb

词源:  From Middle English *bomben, probably of imitative origin.
