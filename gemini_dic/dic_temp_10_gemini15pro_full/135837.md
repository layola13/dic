nonproportionateness:/ˌnɒnprəˈpɔːʃənətnəs/ |n.| 不成比例性，不相称性 |The state or quality of being not proportionate; lack of proportion. The relationship between two things when they are not in proportion to each other.|The nonproportionateness of the punishment to the crime caused public outcry. 对罪行处以不相称的惩罚引起了公众的强烈抗议。| disproportion, imbalance, disparity, incongruity | proportionality, balance, symmetry | noun
