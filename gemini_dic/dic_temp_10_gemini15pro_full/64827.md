esparto:/ɪˈspɑːrtoʊ/
n.
西班牙草，麻 esparto grass
A strong, coarse grass native to Spain and North Africa, used for making ropes, mats, and paper.
The sandals were made of esparto.  这些凉鞋是用西班牙草做的。
