disunify: /dɪsˈjuːnɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使不统一，使分裂 | To cause (something) to no longer be united or uniform; to break up into separate parts or groups. | The new policy disunified the country, leading to regional tensions. 新政策使国家分裂，导致地区紧张局势。| The ongoing conflict has disunified the community. 持续的冲突使社区分裂。 | 近义词: divide, separate, fragment, disintegrate | 反义词: unify, unite, integrate, consolidate | 词性: 动词
