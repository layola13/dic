burden: /ˈbɜːrdən/ (US) /ˈbɜːdn/ (UK)| n. vt.|负担，重担；加重压于|Something that is carried, a load; something that is difficult to bear, a responsibility or worry. |The donkey struggled under its heavy burden. 驴子在重担下挣扎。|Synonyms: load, weight, encumbrance, responsibility, onus|Antonyms: relief, comfort, ease|noun
burden: /ˈbɜːrdən/ (US) /ˈbɜːdn/ (UK)| vt.|使负重担；加重压于|To load heavily; to trouble someone with something difficult to bear.|He was burdened with heavy debts. 他背负着沉重的债务。|Synonyms: load, weigh down, oppress, encumber|Antonyms: unburden, relieve, ease|verb
