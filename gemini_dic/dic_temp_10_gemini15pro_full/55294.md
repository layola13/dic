dissolubleness:/dɪˌsɒljəbəlnəs/
n.
可溶解性；易分解性；不稳定性
The quality of being dissoluble; capable of being dissolved, disintegrated, or broken up.
The dissolubleness of the compound made it unsuitable for long-term storage.  这种化合物的易分解性使其不适合长期储存。
Solubility, dissolvability, instability, fragility
Stability, permanence,  indissolubility
noun
