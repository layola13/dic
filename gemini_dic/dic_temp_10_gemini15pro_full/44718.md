cruelty: /ˈkruːəlti/ (US) /ˈkrʊəlti/ (UK) | n. | 残酷；残忍；残酷的行为 | The deliberate infliction of pain or suffering; inhuman treatment. |  The cruelty of the dictator shocked the world.  独裁者的残暴震惊了世界。

The animal shelter rescues pets from cruelty and neglect.  动物收容所从虐待和忽视中拯救宠物。

| 近义词: brutality, inhumanity, savagery, barbarity, viciousness | 反义词: kindness, compassion, mercy, humanity, gentleness | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crualte*, borrowed from Old French *crualte*, *cruelleté*, from Late Latin *crūdēlitās*, from Latin *crūdēlis* (“raw, unripe, cruel”), from *crūdus* (“raw, bloody”).
