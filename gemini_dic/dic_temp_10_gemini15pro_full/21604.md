blunderingly:/ˈblʌndərɪŋli/| adv.|笨拙地，莽撞地|in a blundering manner; clumsily and awkwardly.|He blunderingly walked into the room, knocking over a lamp. 他笨手笨脚地走进房间，撞翻了一盏灯。|She blunderingly tried to explain the situation, making it even more confusing. 她笨拙地试图解释情况，反而让事情更加混乱。|Synonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, stumblingly|Antonyms: skillfully, deftly, gracefully, adroitly|词性:副词

Etymology: From the verb "blunder" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Blunder" comes from the Scandinavian, likely Old Norse *blundra "to shut one's eyes," hence "to stumble."
