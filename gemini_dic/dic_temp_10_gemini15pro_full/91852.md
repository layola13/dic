hyperidealistically:/ˌhaɪpərˌaɪdiːəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 过分理想主义地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive or unrealistic idealism.  It implies a naive and impractical approach to achieving ideals, often disregarding practical considerations. | He hyperidealistically believed he could single-handedly solve world hunger. 他过分理想主义地认为他可以独自解决世界饥饿问题。 |  He approached the problem hyperidealistically, ignoring the complex realities on the ground. 他以过分理想化的方式处理这个问题，忽略了当地的复杂现实。 | 近义词: naively, impractically, utopianly, quixotically | 反义词: realistically, pragmatically, cynically | 词性: 副词
