blackwall:/ˈblækˌwɔːl/| n.|黑墙，黑壁，伦敦的一个地名|A dark, usually vertical rock face.  A district in London, England, historically associated with the maritime industry. |例句：The climbers ascended the sheer blackwall.  攀登者登上了陡峭的黑墙。

例句：He grew up in Blackwall, London. 他在伦敦的黑墙区长大。|近义词：cliff, precipice (for rock face); docklands (for the London district)|反义词:whitewall|词性:名词
