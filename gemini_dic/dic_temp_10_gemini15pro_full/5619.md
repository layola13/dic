alternative:/ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪv/ /ɑːlˈtɜːrnətɪv/| adj. n.|可供替代的；另类的；替代方案；选择|A choice limited to one of two or more possibilities, as of things, propositions, or courses of action.|The alternative is to stay home.|近义词：option, choice, substitute|反义词: compulsory, required, necessary|词性:形容词

alternative:/ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪv/ /ɑːlˈtɜːrnətɪv/| n.|可供选择的事物；替代方案|One of two or more available possibilities.|We had no alternative but to leave.|近义词：option, choice, substitute|反义词: compulsory, required, necessary|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alternātīvus, from Latin alternātus (past participle of alternāre “to do by turns, alternate”), from alternus “every other, one after the other,” from alter “other.”
