illiberalism:/ɪˈlɪbərəlɪzəm/
noun
非自由主义
A political ideology that rejects liberal principles, typically advocating for strong state control, nationalism, and restrictions on individual rights.
The rise of illiberalism is a concerning trend in global politics.  非自由主义的兴起是全球政治中一个令人担忧的趋势。
His illiberal views made him unpopular. 他的非自由主义观点使他不受欢迎。
近义词:authoritarianism, despotism, tyranny
反义词:liberalism, democracy
词性:n.

illiberal:/ɪˈlɪbərəl/
adjective
非自由的，狭隘的
Opposed to liberal principles; restricting freedom of thought or behavior.
The government's illiberal policies sparked widespread protests. 政府的非自由政策引发了广泛的抗议。
She holds some surprisingly illiberal views for someone who claims to be progressive. 对于一个自称进步的人来说，她持有一些令人惊讶的非自由主义观点。
近义词:intolerant, narrow-minded, bigoted
反义词:liberal, tolerant, open-minded
词性:adj.
