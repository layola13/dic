gtg:/ˈɡɒt tuː ɡəʊ/ (British) /ˈɡɑːt tuː ɡoʊ/ (American)| abbreviation | 我得走了 |  Short for "got to go". Used in informal online communication. | Example: Gotta finish this report, gtg. 我得完成这份报告，得走了。 | 近义词: gotta go, have to go, must go | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) staying, arriving | abbreviation
