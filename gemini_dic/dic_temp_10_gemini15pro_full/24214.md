bridgeable:/ˈbrɪdʒəbl/| adj.|可架桥的，可弥合的|Able to be bridged or overcome.  Something that can be connected or joined, often referring to a gap or difference.|例句：The cultural differences between the two countries are bridgeable. 这两个国家之间的文化差异是可以弥合的。

例句：The river is narrow enough to be bridgeable. 这条河很窄，可以架桥。|近义词：connectable, linkable, spannable|反义词：unbridgeable, impassable|词性:adjective
