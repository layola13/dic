overcultivate: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌltɪveɪt/
verb
过度耕作，过度种植
to cultivate (land) excessively, leading to depletion of soil nutrients.
The farmers overcultivated the land, resulting in poor yields the following year. 农民过度耕作土地，导致来年产量低。
Overcultivation of the soil led to erosion and desertification.  过度耕作导致土壤侵蚀和沙漠化。

近义词: overfarm, overwork
反义词: undercultivate
词性: verb
