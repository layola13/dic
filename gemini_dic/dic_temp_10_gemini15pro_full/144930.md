over-with:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪθ/ | adj. |  结束的，完结的 | Finished, completed, done with something unpleasant. | I'm glad that meeting is over-with.  我很高兴那次会议结束了。| The dentist appointment is finally over-with.  看牙医的预约终于结束了。| 近义词: finished, completed, done, concluded | 反义词: ongoing, continuing, unfinished | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Over" indicates completion, and "with" emphasizes finality and separation from something, often something unpleasant.  "Over"表示完成，“with”强调最终性和与某事的分离，通常是不愉快的事情。
