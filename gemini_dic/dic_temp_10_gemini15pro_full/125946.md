musicalities:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkælɪtiz/
noun
音乐性，音乐才能
The sensitivity to or expressiveness of music displayed by a performer or composer.
Her playing demonstrated great musicalities. 她的演奏展现了极高的音乐性。
musicality, artistry, musicianship
lack of musicality, tone-deafness
