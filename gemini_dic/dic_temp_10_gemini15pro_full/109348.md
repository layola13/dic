leninites: /ˈlɛnɪnaɪts/
| n. |列宁主义者；列宁主义的支持者|A follower or supporter of the political theories of Vladimir Lenin, especially those advocating the creation of a vanguard party and a dictatorship of the proletariat.|The leninites believed a revolution was necessary.|Synonyms: communist, Bolshevik, Marxist-Leninist|Antonyms: capitalist, anti-communist|Noun
