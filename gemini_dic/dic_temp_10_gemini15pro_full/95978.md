incumbent:/ɪnˈkʌmbənt/| n. adj.|在职者，现任者；负有义务的|A person who holds a particular office or position, especially a political one; obligatory.|The incumbent president faces a tough re-election campaign. 现任总统面临艰难的连任竞选。|Synonyms: holder, occupant, officeholder|Antonyms:  challenger, applicant, candidate|noun

incumbent:/ɪnˈkʌmbənt/| adj.|负有义务的，义不容辞的|Necessary for (someone) as a duty or responsibility.|It is incumbent upon all of us to protect the environment. 保护环境是我们所有人的责任。|Synonyms: obligatory, mandatory, required|Antonyms: optional, voluntary|adjective
