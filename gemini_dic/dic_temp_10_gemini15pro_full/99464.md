interfaith:/ˌɪntərˈfeɪθ/
adjective
不同信仰间的；跨宗教的
Relating to or involving people of different religious faiths.
例句：The interfaith dialogue promoted understanding between different religious communities.  不同信仰间的对话促进了不同宗教社群之间的理解。

例句：The interfaith service was attended by people from various religious backgrounds.  跨宗教的仪式有来自不同宗教背景的人参加。
近义词：interreligious, ecumenical
反义词：sectarian
词性：adjective
