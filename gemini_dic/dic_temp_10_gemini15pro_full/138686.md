nuisance:/ˈnjuːsns/ | n. | 讨厌的人（或事物）；麻烦事 | something or someone that annoys you or causes trouble |  The dogs next door are a real nuisance. 隔壁的狗真讨厌。 | synonyms: annoyance, bother, pest, problem | antonyms: pleasure, delight | noun

nuisances:/ˈnjuːsnsɪz/ | n. | 讨厌的人（或事物）；麻烦事（复数） |  something or someone that annoys you or causes trouble (plural) | These flies are a real nuisance. 这些苍蝇真烦人。 | synonyms: annoyances, bothers, pests, problems | antonyms: pleasures, delights | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *nusance "harm, injury," from nuire "to harm, hurt, injure," from Latin nocēre "to hurt, harm, injure."
