multiphotons:/ˌmʌltiˈfoʊtɑːnz/ | n. | 多光子 |  Relating to or involving multiple photons.  It describes processes that involve the simultaneous interaction of two or more photons with a target, such as an atom or molecule. |  Multiphoton microscopy allows for deeper imaging into tissues. 多光子显微技术允许对组织进行更深层次的成像。| synonyms:  multi-photon (sometimes used interchangeably) | antonyms: single-photon | noun
