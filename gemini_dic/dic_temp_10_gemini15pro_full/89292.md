homoeomerous:/ˌhəʊmiˈɒmərəs/| adj.|同质的，由相同部分组成的|Composed of similar parts; of uniform structure throughout.|例句：The ancient Greeks believed that the universe was homoeomerous, made of the same basic elements. 古希腊人认为宇宙是同质的，由相同的基元构成。

例句：Anaximander believed that the universe was composed of a homoeomerous substance called apeiron.  阿那克西曼德认为宇宙是由一种叫做“无定”的同质物质构成的。|近义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar|反义词:heterogeneous, dissimilar, varied|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὁμοιομερής (homoiomerēs, “having similar parts”), from ὁμοῖος (homoios, “similar”) + μέρος (meros, “part”).
