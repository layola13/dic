breaks:/breɪks/| v. n.|打破，中断，休息，破裂|The vase breaks easily. 花瓶很容易破碎。|近义词：shatters, fractures, cracks, pauses, rests|反义词:mends, repairs, fixes, continues|词性:动词/名词
Etymology: From Old English *brecan (attested only in the past participle brocen), from Proto-Germanic *brekaną, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreg-, meaning "to break".
