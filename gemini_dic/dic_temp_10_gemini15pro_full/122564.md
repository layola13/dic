moderate:/ˈmɑːdərət/ (US) /ˈmɒdərət/ (UK)| adj. v. n.|适度的，温和的；缓和；主持|Keeping a moderate pace is key to long-distance running. 保持适度的步速是长跑的关键。|近义词：mild, temperate, reasonable|反义词：extreme, excessive, immoderate|adjective

moderate:/ˈmɑːdərət/ (US) /ˈmɒdərət/ (UK)| v. |缓和，减轻；主持|The chairman moderated the debate. 主席主持了辩论。|近义词：mediate, lessen, diminish|反义词：intensify, aggravate, exacerbate|verb

moderate:/ˈmɑːdərət/ (US) /ˈmɒdərət/ (UK)| n. |持温和观点者|He is a political moderate. 他是一个政治上的温和派。|近义词：centrist|反义词：extremist|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin moderatus, past participle of moderari "to restrain, regulate," from Latin modus "measure, manner."
