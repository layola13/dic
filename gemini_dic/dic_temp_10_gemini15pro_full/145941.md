overdiscourages: /ˌoʊvər dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒɪz/
verb
过度劝退，极度打击
To discourage excessively; to dampen enthusiasm or hope to an extreme degree.
The constant criticism from his boss overdiscouraged him and eventually led him to quit his job.  他老板不断的批评过度打击了他，最终导致他辞职。
The coach's harsh words overdiscouraged the young athletes. 教练严厉的话极度打击了年轻的运动员。


Synonyms: demoralizes, disheartens, dispirits
Antonyms: encourages, motivates, inspires
词性: 动词
