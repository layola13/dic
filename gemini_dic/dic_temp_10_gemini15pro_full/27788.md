cannily:/ˈkænɪli/| adv.|精明地；狡猾地；出乎意料地|In a clever and shrewd way, often in a way that suggests great skill at noticing and using opportunities.|He cannily predicted the outcome of the election. 他精明地预测了选举结果。|Synonyms: astutely, shrewdly, cleverly, artfully, craftily|Antonyms: foolishly, stupidly, clumsily, ineptly|词性:副词

词源:
From canny + -ly.  Canny itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from Scots cannie "gentle, quiet, cautious, safe," or from a northern English variant of Middle English connyng "knowing, skillful, clever,"  from Old English cunnung "knowledge, skill."
