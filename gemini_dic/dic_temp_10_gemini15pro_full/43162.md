cpc:/ˌsiː piː ˈsiː/ | n. | Cost Per Click (每次点击成本) | Cost Per Click is a common online advertising model where the advertiser pays a fee each time one of their ads is clicked. |  Example 1: The CPC for this keyword is very high, meaning we'll pay a lot for each click.  这个关键词的每次点击成本非常高，意味着我们每次点击都要付出很多。

Example 2:  We need to optimize our campaign to lower our CPC and improve our ROI. 我们需要优化我们的广告系列以降低每次点击成本并提高投资回报率。 | synonyms: pay-per-click, PPC | antonyms: CPM (Cost Per Mille/Thousand), CPA (Cost Per Action) | noun
