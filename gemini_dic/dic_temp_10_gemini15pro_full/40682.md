conservationist:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 保育人士，环保人士 | A person who actively works to protect and preserve the natural environment. |  例句：The conservationist dedicated her life to saving endangered species.  这位环保人士毕生致力于拯救濒危物种。

例句：Conservationists are working to protect the rainforest from deforestation.  环保人士正在努力保护雨林免遭砍伐。 | 近义词：environmentalist, preservationist | 反义词：developer, polluter | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From conserve + -ation + -ist.  Conserve ultimately comes from Latin conservare "to keep, maintain, preserve," from com-, intensive prefix (see com-), + servare "to keep, guard" (from PIE root *ser- (1) "to protect").
