overplows:/ˌoʊvərˈplaʊz/ | v. | 耕翻过度，过度犁地 | To plow too much, to the point of damaging the soil. | The farmer overplowed his fields, which led to soil erosion.  农民过度耕作了他的田地，导致了水土流失。| 近义词: overcultivate, overfarm | 反义词: underplow | 词性: 动词

overplows:/ˌoʊvərˈplaʊz/ | n. | (过度耕作的) 土地 | Land that has been overplowed. | The overplows suffered from severe erosion. 被过度耕作的土地遭受了严重的侵蚀。 | 近义词: overworked land | 反义词:  (没有合适的反义词，可以用fertile land肥沃的土地来对比) | 词性: 名词

PHRASE->
(由于overplow较少用于词组搭配，以下列出一些包含"plow"的常用词组)
plow through:  费力地穿过;  He plowed through the deep snow. 他费力地穿过深雪。
plow into:  猛撞；投入； He plowed into the back of another car. 他撞上了另一辆车的尾部。
plow ahead:  继续进行； They decided to plow ahead with the project despite the difficulties. 尽管困难重重，他们还是决定继续推进这个项目。
plow back: 将（利润）再投资； The company plowed back its profits into research and development.  公司将利润再投资于研发。
under the plow:  耕作中； A large percentage of the land is under the plow.  很大一部分土地正在耕作中。


词源： over- + plow  "over-" 前缀表示 "过度"，"plow" 指的是耕地，因此 "overplow" 的意思是过度耕作。
