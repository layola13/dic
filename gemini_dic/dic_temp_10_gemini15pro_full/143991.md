outrate: /ˌaʊtˈreɪt/
verb
超过，胜过
to surpass or exceed in rate or degree.
Our sales figures outrated all our competitors this quarter. 我们的销售额在本季度超过了所有竞争对手。
The new car outrates the old one in speed and fuel efficiency. 这辆新车在速度和燃油效率方面都超过了旧车。
surpass, exceed, outstrip, outdo
lag behind, fall behind
v.
