mistressship:/ˈmɪstrɪsʃɪp/
n.
情妇身份；女主人身份；女教师身份
The state of being a mistress (in various senses).
She maintained her mistressship for five years before he left his wife. 她保持了五年的情妇身份，然后他才离开妻子。
近义词：concubinage, paramourship (for the illicit sense); ownership, proprietorship (for the sense of ownership); leadership, headship (for the sense of leadership)
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the meaning being used. For example, the opposite of mistress in the illicit sense could be wife.)
词性: Noun
