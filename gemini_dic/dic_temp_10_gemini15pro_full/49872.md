demotivational:/ˌdiːˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃənəl/
adjective
消极的; 令人沮丧的; 丧气的
reducing or destroying motivation
The constant criticism from his boss had a demotivational effect on him. 他老板不断的批评对他产生了消极的影响。

近义词:discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting
反义词:motivational, inspiring, encouraging
词性:adjective
