foolishest:/ˈfuːlɪʃɪst/| adj.|最愚蠢的|Superlative form of foolish: most foolish.  It describes something or someone exhibiting the greatest degree of foolishness.|That was the foolishest decision you could have made. 那可是你能做出的最愚蠢的决定了。|Synonyms: silliest, dumbest, most ridiculous|Antonyms: wisest, smartest, most intelligent|adjective
