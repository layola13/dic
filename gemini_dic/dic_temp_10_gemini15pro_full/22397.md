boogie-boarder:/ˈbuːɡiˌbɔːrdər/ | n. |趴板冲浪者；滑板冲浪者|A person who rides a boogie board. |例句：The boogie-boarder caught a great wave. 趴板冲浪者抓住了一个很棒的浪。

例句：Many boogie-boarders enjoy the waves at this beach. 许多趴板冲浪者喜欢这个海滩的浪。|近义词：bodyboarder, wave rider |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "swimmer" in a context where one chooses between boogie boarding and swimming) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From boogie board + -er.  "Boogie board" is a trademark for a type of small surfboard, from the dance boogie-woogie.
