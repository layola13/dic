dharnas:/ˈdɑːrnɑːz/
n.
抗议静坐，静坐示威
A form of non-violent protest in which participants sit and refuse to move.  It originates from the Sanskrit word "dharnā" meaning "to hold, maintain."
Thousands of farmers staged dharnas across the country to protest the new agricultural laws. 数千名农民在全国各地举行静坐示威，抗议新的农业法。
sit-in, demonstration, protest, picketing
counter-protest, opposition
