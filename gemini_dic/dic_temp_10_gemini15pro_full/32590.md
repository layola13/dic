chiromantic:/ˌkaɪrəˈmæntɪk/ | adj. n.| 手相的；手相术士 | Relating to or practicing chiromancy (palmistry). | She gave a chiromantic reading at the fair. 她在集市上进行了一次手相解读。 |  The chiromantic predicted a long and prosperous life. 手相师预测他会长寿且繁荣。 | 近义词: palmistic, palm-reading | 反义词:  None | 词性: adjective, noun

chiromancy: /ˈkaɪrəˌmænsi/ | n. | 手相术 | The art of telling fortunes by examining the lines and other features of the hand. | She learned chiromancy from her grandmother. 她跟祖母学习手相术。 | Chiromancy has been practiced for centuries. 手相术已经流传了几个世纪。 | 近义词: palmistry, palm-reading | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
