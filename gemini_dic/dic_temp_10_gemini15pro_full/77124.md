gas-fired:/ˈɡæsˌfaɪərd/| adj.|燃气的，烧气的|Powered or fueled by gas.|例句：They have a gas-fired power station. 他们有一座燃气发电站。|例句：Gas-fired boilers are more efficient than electric ones. 燃气锅炉比电锅炉效率更高。|近义词：gas-powered, gas-burning|反义词：electric, coal-fired|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"gas" (n.) - from late 17th century: perhaps from Greek *khaos 'empty space, vast chasm.'
"fired" (adj.) - from "fire" (v.) - from Old English fȳr, of West Germanic origin; related to Dutch vuur and German Feuer.
