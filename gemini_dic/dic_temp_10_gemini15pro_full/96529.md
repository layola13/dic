indolency:/ˈɪndələnsɪ/| n. |懒惰，惰性|Habitual laziness; a disposition to avoid exertion; sloth.|He was fired for indolency. 他因为懒惰被解雇了。|近义词：laziness, sloth, idleness, inertia|反义词：diligence, industry, activity|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French indolence, from Latin indolentia, from in- ‘not’ + dolere ‘to grieve’.  The original sense was ‘freedom from pain or trouble’, hence ‘ease’, later ‘inactivity’.
