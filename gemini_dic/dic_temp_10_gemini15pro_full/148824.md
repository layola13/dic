overvigorous: /ˌoʊvərˈvɪɡərəs/ | adj. | 过于精力旺盛的；过分剧烈的 | Excessively vigorous; too energetic or forceful. |  The overvigorous exercise routine left him exhausted. 过于剧烈的锻炼让他筋疲力尽。| Synonyms: excessive, immoderate, extreme, overzealous, hyperactive | Antonyms: gentle, moderate, mild, restrained, subdued |  adjective

overvigorous: /ˌoʊvərˈvɪɡərəs/ | adj. | 过于激烈的；过分用力的 |  Characterized by excessive energy or force. | Her overvigorous scrubbing damaged the delicate fabric. 她用力过猛的擦洗损坏了精致的布料。 | Synonyms: forceful, intense, powerful, strenuous | Antonyms: gentle, delicate, soft, tender | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + vigorous.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Vigorous" comes from the Latin "vigor" meaning "liveliness, activity, force".
