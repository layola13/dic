cooled:/kuːld/
adjective
冷却的，冷的
past participle of "cool" meaning having become less hot.
The soup cooled quickly in the breeze. 汤在微风中很快冷却了。
The engine cooled down after a long drive.  长时间驾驶后，发动机冷却下来了。
近义词:chilled, refrigerated
反义词:heated, warmed
词性:adjective


cool:/kuːl/
verb
冷却，使冷静
to make something less hot.
She cooled the soup by adding some ice. 她加了些冰块来冷却汤。
He splashed cold water on his face to cool down. 他往脸上泼冷水让自己冷静下来。
近义词:chill, refrigerate
反义词:heat, warm
词性:verb

cool:/kuːl/
adjective
凉爽的，冷静的
somewhat cold; not hot or warm.
It was a cool autumn evening.  那是一个凉爽的秋夜。
He remained cool under pressure. 他在压力下保持冷静。
近义词:chilled, refreshing
反义词:hot, warm
词性:adjective

cool:/kuːl/
noun
凉爽，凉爽的地方
a cool place or time.
The shade offered some cool from the midday sun.  树荫提供了正午阳光下的些许凉爽。
Let's go inside, it's cooler in there. 我们进去吧，里面凉快些。
近义词:chill, coolness
反义词:heat, warmth
词性:noun


词源:
From Middle English *colen, *kelen, from Old English cōlian (“to cool, make cool, become cool”), from Proto-Germanic *kōlijaną (“to cool”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵel- (“cold, to freeze”). Cognate with West Frisian koalje (“to cool”), Dutch koelen (“to cool”), German kühlen (“to cool”), Swedish kyla (“to cool”).
