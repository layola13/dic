crouke: /kraʊk/ | v. | 蹲伏，蜷缩 | To stoop or bend low; to crouch. | The weary traveler crouked by the fire to warm himself.  疲惫的旅行者蜷缩在火堆旁取暖。 |  He crouked down to avoid being seen. 他蹲下以避免被人看见。 | 近义词：crouch, squat, stoop | 反义词: stand, straighten | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crouken, from Old Norse *krjúka (“to bend, to crook”), from Proto-Germanic *kreukaną (“to bend, to crook”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreuǵ- (“to bend, to curve”). Cognate with Danish krøge (“to bend, to crook”), Swedish kröka (“to bend, to crook”), Icelandic krjúpa (“to crouch”).
