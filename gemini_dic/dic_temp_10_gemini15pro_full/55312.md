dissolvent:/dɪˈzɒlvənt/ | adj. n. | 溶解的；溶剂 | Having the power of dissolving; a liquid capable of dissolving another substance. |  A strong dissolvent is needed to remove this stain. 需要一种强力溶剂来去除这个污渍。| 近义词: solvent, dissolving | 反义词: insoluble, precipitate | 词性: adjective, noun

词源:
From Late Latin dissolvens, present participle of dissolvere "to dissolve," from Latin dis- "apart" + solvere "to loosen."
