exotom: /ˈɛksətɒm/ | n. | 外切体，外切组 |A taxonomic group that is excluded from another, more comprehensive group.  A group of organisms separated from a larger group on the basis of certain characteristics. |The study revealed that this species is an exotom of the original genus. 这项研究表明，该物种是从原始属中分离出来的一个外切组。 | The exotom displayed unique genetic markers not found in the main group. 这个外切组展现了在主要群体中未发现的独特的遗传标记。|  outgroup, subgroup | ingroup, main group | noun
