capricorns: /ˈkæprɪˌkɔːrnz/ | n. | 摩羯座；摩羯座的人 |  The plural form of "Capricorn," referring to people born under this astrological sign (December 22 - January 19) or the astrological sign itself.  It can also refer to ibex, a wild goat. | Capricorn is an Earth sign and is known for being practical and ambitious. 摩羯座是土象星座，以务实和雄心勃勃而闻名。|  Many Capricorns are known for their strong work ethic. 很多摩羯座的人以其强烈的职业道德而闻名。| synonyms: (for the sign) Goat; (for people) none specific, use traits like ambitious, practical, disciplined | antonyms: none specific | noun
