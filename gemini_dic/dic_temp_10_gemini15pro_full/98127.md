insanably:/ɪnˈseɪnəbli/
adjective
疯狂地，不可救药地
In a manner that suggests a severely disordered mind; madly.
He laughed insanably at the absurd situation. 他对着这荒谬的场景疯狂地大笑。
insanely, madly, uncontrollably, wildly
sanely, rationally, reasonably
