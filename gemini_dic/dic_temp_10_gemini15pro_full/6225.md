amnesic: /æmˈniːzɪk/ /æmˈniːzik/ | n. adj. | 失忆症患者，健忘的 |A person experiencing a loss of memory, or relating to amnesia.  |例句：The amnesic patient couldn't remember his own name. 这位失忆症患者记不起自己的名字了。

|例句：The blow to his head left him amnesic. 他头部受到的打击使他失忆了。|近义词：forgetful, memory-impaired |反义词: mindful, remembering |词性: noun, adjective

amnesics: /æmˈniːzɪks/ /æmˈniːzɪks/ | n. | 失忆症患者（复数） | Plural form of "amnesic," referring to multiple individuals experiencing amnesia.  |例句：The support group was specifically for amnesics and their families. 这个支持小组专门为失忆症患者及其家人设立。


|例句：The doctor specialized in treating amnesics with various causes of memory loss.  这位医生专门治疗各种原因导致失忆的患者。|近义词：people with amnesia, memory-impaired individuals|反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a group of people with a medical condition) |词性: noun
