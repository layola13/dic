hereditability:/ˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪləti/ /ˌhɛrɪdəˈbɪlədi/| n. |遗传性，遗传力|The proportion of variation among individuals that we can attribute to genes. The heritability of a trait may vary, depending on the range of populations and environments studied.|The heritability of intelligence has been a controversial topic for decades. 智力的遗传性几十年来一直是一个有争议的话题。|Synonyms: inheritability,  transmissibility|Antonyms: non-heritability|noun

heritable:/ˈhɛrɪtəbl/ /ˈhɛrɪdəbl/| adj. |可遗传的，可继承的|Able to be inherited.|Eye color is a heritable trait. 眼睛的颜色是一种可遗传的特征。|Synonyms: inheritable, transmissible, genetic|Antonyms: non-heritable, acquired|adjective
