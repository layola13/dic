helicoptered:/ˈhelɪkɒptəd/ (British) /ˈhelɪkɑːptərd/ (American)| v.|用直升机运送；乘直升机旅行|To travel or transport by helicopter. |The troops were helicoptered into the combat zone. 部队被直升机运送到战区。

The president was helicoptered to the hospital after the incident.  事件发生后，总统被直升机送往医院。|近义词：airlifted, flown|反义词: drove, walked |词性:动词
