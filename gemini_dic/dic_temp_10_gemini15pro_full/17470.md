bearding:/ˈbɪədɪŋ/| n.|蓄须，公然对抗，挑战|The act of confronting someone boldly, especially in their own territory, often as a form of protest or challenge.  It can also refer to the practice of keeping bees.|He showed his defiance by bearding the lion in its den. 他公然对抗，就像在狮穴里挑战狮子一样。|近义词：confronting, challenging, defying|反义词:avoiding, submitting|noun

bearding:/ˈbɪədɪŋ/| v.|蓄须，公然对抗，挑战|present participle of beard|He is bearding the CEO about the new policy. 他正在就新政策向CEO提出挑战。|近义词：confronting, challenging, defying|反义词:avoiding, submitting|verb
