free-enterprising: /ˌfriːˈɛntərˌpraɪzɪŋ/
| adj. | 自由创业的；富有进取心的；有创业精神的 | Characterized by initiative and willingness to undertake new ventures and business activities; entrepreneurial. |  She has a free-enterprising spirit and started her own bakery. 她有自由创业的精神，开办了自己的面包店。 |  He's a free-enterprising young man with lots of business ideas. 他是一个富有进取心的年轻人，有很多商业想法。 | 近义词: entrepreneurial, enterprising, ambitious, go-getting, driven | 反义词: unambitious, lazy, unmotivated, risk-averse | 词性: adjective
词源:  "free" (meaning unrestricted) + "enterprising" (from "enterprise," meaning a project or undertaking, ultimately from Old French "entreprendre," to undertake).
