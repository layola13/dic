fruitestere:/ˈfruːtˌtɛstər/ | n. | 水果品尝师 | A person who tastes fruit, especially to assess its quality or ripeness. | 例句：The fruitestere sampled each mango to ensure its sweetness. 水果品尝师品尝了每个芒果以确保其甜度。

例句：The fruitestere rejected the batch of strawberries due to their lack of flavor.  由于缺乏味道，水果品尝师拒绝了这批草莓。| 近义词：fruit taster, produce inspector | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
