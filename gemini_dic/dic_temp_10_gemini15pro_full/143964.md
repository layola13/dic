outquibbles:/ˌaʊtˈkwɪbəl/
verb
胜过诡辩; 在诡辩中胜过
to surpass or defeat someone in quibbling or clever argument.
He outquibbled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以诡辩胜过了对手。
Synonyms: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
