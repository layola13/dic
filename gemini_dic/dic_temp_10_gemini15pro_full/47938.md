dechristianize:/diːˈkrɪʃtʃənaɪz/
verb
使非基督教化，使世俗化
To eliminate the Christian character or influence of; secularize.
The French Revolution attempted to dechristianize France. 法国大革命试图使法国非基督教化。
The government's policies are slowly dechristianizing the nation. 政府的政策正在慢慢地使国家世俗化。
