deepnesses:/ˈdiːpnəsɪz/
n.
深度，深处；深奥，深刻
Great deepnesses of the ocean hide many unknown creatures. 海洋深处隐藏着许多未知的生物。
The deepnesses of her thoughts surprised everyone. 她思想的深邃让每个人都感到惊讶。
Synonyms: depths, profundities, abysses
Antonyms: shallows, superficiality
noun

Etymology: From Middle English *depnesses, from Old English dēpness (“deepness”), equivalent to deep +‎ -ness. Cognate with Scots depnes (“depth”), West Frisian djipnis (“depth”), Dutch diepte (“depth”), German Tiefe (“depth”). More at deep.
