lauds:/lɔːdz/ | n. | 赞扬，称赞；赞美诗 |  Praise and honor expressed in song or writing, often of a religious nature.  It can also refer to the service of morning prayer in some churches. | The priest offered lauds to the Almighty.  神父向上帝献上赞美。| synonyms: praise, acclaim, commendation, accolades, tributes,  eulogies, plaudits, kudos | antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure, reproach, denunciation | noun
