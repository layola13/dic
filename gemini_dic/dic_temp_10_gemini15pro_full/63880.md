epilogize:/ˈɛpɪˌlɒdʒaɪz/ | v. | 作结语；写结语；以结语结束 | to add or write an epilogue to (something) | He epilogized his speech with a call to action.  他以行动号召结束了他的演讲。 | 近义词: conclude, summarize, recap | 反义词: preface, introduce | 词性: verb

epilogize:/ˈɛpɪˌlɒdʒaɪz/ | v. | 为...写后记 | to provide an epilogue for something | The author epilogized the novel with a chapter describing the characters' later lives. 作者在小说结尾加了一章，描述了人物后来的生活。 | 近义词: append, supplement | 反义词:  preface, begin | 词性: verb
