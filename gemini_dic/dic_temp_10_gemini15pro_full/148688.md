overtint: /ˌoʊvərˈtɪnt/
noun
过度着色；过浓的色彩
A shade of color that is excessively or inappropriately tinted.
The photograph suffered from overtint, making the sky look unnatural.  这张照片过度着色，使天空看起来不自然。

The artist's use of overtint made the painting look garish. 艺术家过度使用浓重的色彩，使这幅画看起来很俗丽。
近义词: oversaturation, overcoloring
反义词: undertint, pale color
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "tint" (a shade of color).  "Tint" ultimately comes from the Old French "teindre," meaning "to dye."
