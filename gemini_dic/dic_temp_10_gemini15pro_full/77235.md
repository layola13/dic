gastroenteritic:/ˌɡæstroʊˌentəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
胃肠炎的
Relating to or affected by gastroenteritis.
He had gastroenteritic symptoms, including vomiting and diarrhea. 他有胃肠炎的症状，包括呕吐和腹泻。
近义词:intestinal, stomach
反义词:healthy


Etymology:
From gastro- (combining form meaning "stomach") + entero- (combining form meaning "intestine") + -itic (suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characterized by inflammation of").  The roots ultimately derive from Greek *gastēr* (belly, stomach) and *énteron* (intestine).
