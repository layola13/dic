dining:/ˈdaɪnɪŋ/
n.
用餐;进餐
the act of eating dinner
例句：The dining room has a beautiful view of the garden. 餐厅可以看到花园的美景。
例句：We had a pleasant dining experience at the new restaurant. 我们在新餐厅的用餐体验很愉快。
近义词：meal, repast
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可用fasting 禁食)
词性:名词


dinning:/ˈdɪnɪŋ/
n./adj.
(n.)喧嚣声，嘈杂声; (adj.)震耳欲聋的
(n.)a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise
(adj.)(of a noise) loud and unpleasant
例句：The dinning of the construction work made it hard to concentrate.  建筑工程的噪音使人难以集中注意力。
例句：The dinning noise of the traffic gave me a headache.  交通的喧嚣声让我头疼。
近义词：(n.) noise, clamor, racket (adj.) deafening, earsplitting
反义词：(n.) silence, quiet (adj.) quiet, soft
词性:名词/形容词

词源：
Dining 源于 dine (v. 用餐)，dine 来自古法语 disner，最终源于拉丁语 disjejunare “打破禁食”。
Dinning 源于 din (n. 喧嚣声)，din 可能源于拟声词。
