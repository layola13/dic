organon:/ˈɔːrɡənɒn/ (UK) /ˈɔːrɡənɑːn/ (US)| n.|工具，器官，体系，方法论|A means of reasoning or a system of logic.  A tool or instrument used to achieve a particular purpose.  In Aristotle's philosophy, an instrument of scientific investigation.| Aristotle's logical treatises were collectively known as the Organon.  The scientific method is an organon for understanding the natural world.  Language is an organon of thought.|近义词：tool, instrument, method, system, means|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on specific meaning)|noun
