expeller:/ɪkˈspɛlər/| n.|压榨机；排出器；驱逐者|A machine that presses or squeezes something to extract liquid or oil.  A person or thing that expels something.|The expeller pressed the seeds to extract the oil. 这台压榨机压榨种子以提取油。|近义词：press, extractor, ejector|反义词: retainer, holder|noun
