assume:/əˈsuːm/ (US) /əˈsjuːm/ (UK)| v. | 假定，假设；承担；呈现；装出 | To accept that something is true without proof.  To take on (a role or responsibility). To begin to have (a particular quality, appearance, etc.) | I assume you've already met my wife. 我想你已经见过我妻子了。| synonyms: suppose, presume, posit, take on, undertake | antonyms: doubt, question, disavow | verb
