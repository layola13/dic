downsizes:/ˈdaʊnsaɪzɪz/
verb
缩小规模，裁员
reduces the number of employees and/or the size of a company or organization
The company downsizes its workforce by 10%.  这家公司裁员10%。
Many companies are downsizing their operations to cut costs. 许多公司正在缩减业务以降低成本。
synonyms: reduces, cuts, streamlines, shrinks
antonyms: expands, increases, grows, enlarges
v.
