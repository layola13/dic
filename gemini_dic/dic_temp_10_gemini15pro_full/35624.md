coatee:/koʊˈtiː/ /kəʊˈtiː/
n.
短上衣，外套
A short, close-fitting coat.
The gentleman wore a stylish coatee to the opera.  这位先生穿着一件时髦的短上衣去看了歌剧。
jacket, coat
cloak, overcoat
noun
