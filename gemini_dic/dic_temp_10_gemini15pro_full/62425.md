endoneurium:/ˌɛndoʊˈnjʊəriəm/| n. |神经内膜|A delicate layer of connective tissue surrounding the myelin sheath of each nerve fiber within a fascicle.|The endoneurium protects individual nerve fibers from damage.|近义词:  (No direct synonym, but related terms include perineurium, epineurium)|反义词: (No antonym)|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *éndon* (ἔνδον) "within" + *neuron* (νεῦρον) "nerve".
