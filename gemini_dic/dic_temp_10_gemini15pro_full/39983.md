consular: /ˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/ (British) /ˈkɑːnsələr/ (American)| adj.| 领事的；领事馆的|Relating to a consul or consulate.  A consul is an official appointed by a government to live in a foreign city and protect the government's citizens and interests there.|The consular officer stamped my visa. 领事官员在我的签证上盖了章。|近义词: diplomatic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

consulary: /ˈkɒnsjʊləri/ (British) /ˈkɑːnsəleri/ (American)| n.| 领事馆；领事裁判权|The office or jurisdiction of a consul.|He went to the American consulary in Paris. 他去了位于巴黎的美国领事馆。|近义词: consulate|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
