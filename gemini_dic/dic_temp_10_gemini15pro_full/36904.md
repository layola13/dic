coloristic:/ˌkʌlərˈɪstɪk/
adjective
色彩的；着色的；用色的
Relating to or characterized by the use of color, especially in a striking or unusual way.
Her paintings are known for their bold, coloristic effects. 她的绘画以其大胆的色彩效果而闻名。
synonyms: colorful, vibrant, rich, vivid, chromatic
antonyms: drab, dull, monochrome, achromatic
adjective
