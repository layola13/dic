/ˈniːdɪd/ | adj. | 需要的，必需的 | required or wanted |  例句：A needed change. 必需的改变。 | 近义词：necessary, required, essential, requisite | 反义词：unnecessary, optional | 词性: 形容词

/ˈniːdɪd/ | v. | 需要 (need的过去式和过去分词) |  required or wanted | 例句：He needed help. 他需要帮助。 | 近义词：required, wanted | 反义词：unwanted | 词性: 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Is the car needed today? = 今天需要用车吗？
Do they need any help with the project? = 他们需要项目上的帮助吗？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am needing a break right now. = 我现在需要休息一下。(较不常见，一般用I need a break right now.)
Are you needing anything from the store? = 你需要从商店买什么东西吗？(较不常见，一般用Do you need anything from the store?)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Have you needed any assistance so far? = 到目前为止你需要任何帮助吗？
The company has needed to hire more staff recently. = 最近公司需要雇佣更多员工。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been needing a new printer for months. = 我们几个月来一直需要一台新打印机。
They have been needing extra support during this difficult time. = 在这段困难时期，他们一直需要额外的支持。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I needed your help yesterday. = 我昨天需要你的帮助。
She needed to finish the report by Friday. = 她需要在星期五之前完成报告。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was needing some advice when you called. = 你打电话时我正需要一些建议。(较不常见，一般用I needed some advice when you called.)
They were needing more time to complete the task. = 他们当时需要更多时间来完成任务。(较不常见，一般用They needed more time to complete the task.)

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had needed that information before the meeting. = 我在会议前就需要那些信息了。
She had needed to speak with him urgently. = 她当时急需和他谈谈。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
We had been needing a bigger house for a while before we moved. = 我们搬家之前就一直需要一套更大的房子。
They had been needing a solution to the problem for quite some time. = 他们一直需要一个解决方案来解决这个问题很长一段时间了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will need your help tomorrow. = 我明天将需要你的帮助。
They will need more resources to complete the project. = 他们将需要更多资源来完成项目。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be needing the car this afternoon. = 我今天下午需要用车。
They will be needing our support in the coming weeks. = 他们在接下来的几周里需要我们的支持。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have needed all the documents. = 到下周，我将需要所有文件。
By the end of the year, they will have needed to raise more funds. = 到年底，他们将需要筹集更多资金。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been needing this medication for a year. = 到下个月，我将需要这种药物一年了。
By the time the project is finished, they will have been needing our expertise for several months. = 到项目完成时，他们将需要我们的专业知识好几个月了。

虚拟语气:
If I needed help, I would ask you. = 如果我需要帮助，我会问你。
I wish I didn't need to work so much. = 我希望我不需要工作这么多。

被动语态:
More funding is needed for the project. = 该项目需要更多资金。
A solution was needed urgently. = 迫切需要一个解决方案。

疑问句:
Is anything else needed? = 还需要其他东西吗？
What is needed to complete the project? = 完成这个项目需要什么？

否定句:
You are not needed here anymore. = 你在这里不再被需要了。
He didn't need to go to the meeting. = 他不需要去参加会议。

词源：From Middle English *neden, from Old English nēodian (“to have need, be under necessity”), from Proto-Germanic *nauþijaną (“to compel, force, constrain”), from Proto-Indo-European *néudh- (“to force, compel, be distressed”).
