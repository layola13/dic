inappreciable:/ˌɪnəˈpriːʃəbl/ | adj. | 微不足道的，难以察觉的 | Too small to be measured or noticed. | The difference in taste was inappreciable. 味道上的差别难以察觉。|  The change in temperature was inappreciable. 温度的变化微不足道。 | synonyms: imperceptible, negligible, insignificant, undetectable, trivial | antonyms: appreciable, significant, noticeable, substantial | adjective
