mugwumps:/ˈmʌɡˌwʌmps/ | n. | 独立人士，骑墙派 | A person who remains aloof or independent, especially from party politics.  A person who affects superiority, especially in matters of taste and morals. |  The mugwumps refused to support their party's candidate. 那些独立人士拒绝支持他们党派的候选人。|  The mugwumps criticized the new art exhibit, claiming it lacked refinement. 这些自命不凡的人批评了新的艺术展览，声称它缺乏精致。| synonyms: independent, maverick, dissenter, nonconformist, highbrow | antonyms: conformist, follower, partisan | noun
