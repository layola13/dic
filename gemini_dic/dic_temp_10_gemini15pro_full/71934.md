flickering:/ˈflɪkərɪŋ/ | adj. | 闪烁的，摇曳的 | burning or shining unsteadily; wavering | The flickering candlelight created a spooky atmosphere. 摇曳的烛光营造出一种怪诞的氛围。| synonyms: glimmering, twinkling, flashing | antonyms: steady, constant | adjective

flicker:/ˈflɪkər/ | v. | 闪烁，摇曳 | to burn or shine unsteadily; to waver | The lights flickered and then went out. 灯光闪烁了几下然后熄灭了。| synonyms: glimmer, twinkle, flash | antonyms: steady, glow | verb

flicker:/ˈflɪkər/ | n. | 闪烁，摇曳 | a brief, unsteady light or flame | There was a flicker of hope in his eyes. 他的眼中闪过一丝希望。| synonyms: glimmer, twinkle, flash | antonyms: steadiness, constancy | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flicorian, frequentative of *flican "to move quickly," of Germanic origin.
