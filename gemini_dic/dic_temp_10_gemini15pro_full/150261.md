pandora's box:/pænˈdɔːrəz bɒks/| n.|潘多拉的盒子|A source of many unforeseen troubles; something that seems harmless but unleashes a multitude of troubles or evils.|Opening that file was like opening Pandora's box - it unleashed a virus that crashed the entire network. 打开那个文件就像打开了潘多拉的盒子——它释放了一种病毒，导致整个网络崩溃。|近义词:trouble, source of problems, can of worms|反义词:blessing, godsend|名词
