convexity:/kənˈvɛksɪti/| n.|凸性，凸度|The outward curve or swelling of a surface.  The degree to which a bond's price changes in response to interest rate changes.|The lens has a slight convexity.  这个镜片略微凸起。|近义词：bulge, curvature, prominence|反义词:concavity|noun
