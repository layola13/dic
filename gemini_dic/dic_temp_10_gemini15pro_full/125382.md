multimodalities:/ˌmʌltiˌmoʊˈdælɪtiz/ | n. |多模态| The combination of different modes of communication or representation, such as text, audio, images, animation, video, and interactive elements.  |  The online course utilizes multimodalities to enhance learning.  这门在线课程利用多模态来增强学习。 | synonyms: multimodal systems, multimedia approaches | antonyms: unimodal, single-mode | noun
