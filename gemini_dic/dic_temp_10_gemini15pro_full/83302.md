gynecocracy:/ˌɡaɪnɪˈkɒkrəsi/ /ˌɡaɪnəˈkɑːkrəsi/
n.
女权统治; 女王统治; 妇女当政
Government by women; a society ruled by women.
The Amazonian tribes were often cited as examples of gynecocracy in ancient literature.  古代文学中经常引用亚马逊部落作为女权统治的例子。
Some science fiction novels depict futuristic societies organized as gynecocracies. 一些科幻小说描绘了未来社会组织成女权统治的社会。
synonym: matriarchy, female rule
antonym: patriarchy, male rule
noun
