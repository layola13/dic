overwhelming:/ˌoʊvərˈwelmɪŋ/| adj.|势不可挡的，压倒性的，巨大的|Very great in amount, effect, or force; so strong as to be irresistible.|The evidence against him was overwhelming.  对他不利的证据是压倒性的。|近义词：powerful, intense, immense, overpowering|反义词:weak, insignificant, manageable|词性:形容词
词源:From over- + whelm, from Old English -wielman “to turn over.”  The original sense was thus "to capsize."
