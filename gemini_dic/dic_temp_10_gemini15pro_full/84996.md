hara-kiri: /ˌhɑːrəˈkɪəri/ /ˌhærəˈkɪri/ | n. |  (比喻)自我毁灭 |  Any action that results in self-destruction. | The company's decision to expand too quickly was hara-kiri.  这家公司决定过快扩张无异于自杀。 | Synonym: Self-destruction, suicide | Antonym: Self-preservation | noun
