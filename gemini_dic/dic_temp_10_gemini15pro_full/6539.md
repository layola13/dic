amylase:/ˈæmɪleɪs/ /ˈæmɪleɪz/| n.|淀粉酶|An enzyme that catalyses the hydrolysis of starch into sugars.|Saliva contains amylase, which begins the digestion of starch. 唾液中含有淀粉酶，它开始消化淀粉。|Synonym: diastase|Antonym: None|Noun
