forlornly:/fərˈlɔːrnli/ /fɔːrˈlɔːrnli/
adverb
孤零零地；凄凉地；绝望地
In a sad and lonely way; in a way that shows someone has no hope or is very unhappy.

She sat forlornly in the corner, staring out the window. 她孤零零地坐在角落里，凝视着窗外。

He wandered forlornly through the empty streets. 他在空荡荡的街道上凄凉地游荡。


近义词:desolately, miserably, sadly, wretchedly
反义词:happily, cheerfully, hopefully
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From forlorn + -ly.  Forlorn itself comes from Middle English forloren, from Old English forlēosan  "to lose completely,"  composed of for- "completely" + lēosan "to lose."  The sense shifted from "lost" to "abandoned, desolate" and then to "wretched, miserable."
