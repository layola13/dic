counsel:/ˈkaʊnsl/ (US), /ˈkaʊnsəl/ (UK)| n. v.|建议，忠告；法律顾问；商议，劝告|advice or guidance, especially given by someone qualified to do so; a lawyer or group of lawyers representing someone in court; the act of giving advice or guidance|例句：The company sought legal counsel on the matter. 公司就此事寻求了法律顾问。|近义词：advice, guidance, lawyer, attorney|反义词:misguide, dissuade|noun

counsel:/ˈkaʊnsl/ (US), /ˈkaʊnsəl/ (UK)| v.|劝告，忠告；提议|to give advice or guidance to someone|例句：He counseled her to be patient. 他劝她耐心点。|近义词：advise, recommend, suggest|反义词:dissuade|verb
