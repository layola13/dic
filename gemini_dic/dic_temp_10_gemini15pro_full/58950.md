dustiest:/ˈdʌstiɪst/ | adj. | 布满灰尘的；尘土最多的 | Covered with the most dust; most filled with dust. | The attic was the dustiest place in the whole house. 阁楼是整栋房子里灰尘最多的地方。 | The old books on the shelf were the dustiest I'd ever seen.  书架上那些旧书是我见过的布满灰尘最多的书。 | 近义词: dirtiest, filthiest | 反义词: cleanest, tidiest | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From dust + -y (suffix forming adjectives) + -est (suffix forming superlative adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English *dust.
