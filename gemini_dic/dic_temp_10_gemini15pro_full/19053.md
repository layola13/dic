bethrall:/bɪˈθrɔːl/| vt.|迷住，吸引住|to capture the attention and interest of someone completely; to fascinate or enchant|The magician's performance completely bethralled the audience. 魔术师的表演完全吸引住了观众。

The beauty of the sunset bethralled us. 日落的美丽使我们着迷。|近义词：captivate, enchant, fascinate, mesmerize|反义词：bore, repel, disgust|词性：动词

词源:

From Middle English *bethrallen*, from be- + thrall (“slave”).  The original meaning was "to enslave," reflecting the idea of being captivated as a kind of enslavement to beauty or charm.
