leucinic:/ˌluːˈsɪnɪk/ |adj.|亮氨酸的；与亮氨酸相关的|Relating to or derived from leucine, an essential amino acid.|Leucinic acid is a metabolite of leucine.|近义词:amino acid related, leucine-derived|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps consider terms related to deficiency like "leucine deficient")|词性:adjective

Etymology: From leucine + -ic.  Leucine itself comes from the Greek leukos, meaning "white," because the isolated amino acid appears as white crystals.
