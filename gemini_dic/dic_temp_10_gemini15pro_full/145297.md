overbravely:/ˈoʊvərˈbreɪvli/
adv.|过于勇敢地；鲁莽地|In a way that is too brave and shows a lack of good judgement.|He overbravely rushed into the burning building. 他过于勇敢地冲进了燃烧的建筑。|Synonyms: recklessly, rashly, foolishly, imprudently|Antonyms: cautiously, prudently, carefully|词性: 副词

overbravely:/ˈoʊvərˈbreɪvli/
adv.|过于勇敢地；鲁莽地|Acting with excessive bravery, often to the point of recklessness.|She overbravely confronted the armed robber. 她鲁莽地与持枪劫匪对峙。|Synonyms:  impetuously, audaciously, heedlessly|Antonyms: timidly, cautiously, discreetly|词性: 副词
