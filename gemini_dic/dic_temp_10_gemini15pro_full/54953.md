dispreader:/dɪˈsprɛdər/ | n. | 分散器，撒布器 | A device used to spread or disperse something, such as fertilizer, seeds, or sand. |  The farmer used a dispreader to distribute fertilizer evenly across his field. 农民用撒布器把肥料均匀地撒在田里。| synonyms: distributor, spreader, scatterer | antonyms: concentrator, collector | noun


dispreader:/dɪˈsprɛdər/ | n. | 散播者，传播者 (比喻义) | Someone or something that spreads or disseminates information, ideas, or disease. | Social media can be a powerful dispreader of misinformation. 社交媒体可以成为传播错误信息的强大工具。 | synonyms: disseminator, propagator, distributor | antonyms: suppressor, container | noun
