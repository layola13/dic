bulimia:/buˈlɪmiə/ | n. |贪食症；暴食症 |An eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by inappropriate methods of weight control, such as vomiting, fasting, or excessive exercise.| She struggled with bulimia for many years. 她多年来一直在与贪食症作斗争。| Synonyms: hyperphagia, gluttony | Antonyms: anorexia | noun


bulimia:/buˈlɪmiə/ | n. |暴食 |An excessive or insatiable appetite.|His bulimia led to significant weight gain. 他的暴食导致体重显著增加。| Synonyms: voracity, ravenousness | Antonyms: abstinence | noun
