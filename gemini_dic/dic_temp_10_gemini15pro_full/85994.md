headstock:/ˈhɛdstɒk (UK), ˈhɛdstɑːk (US)| n.|琴头；床头箱；主轴箱|The part of a machine tool, such as a lathe, that supports the main spindle and associated parts, or the tuning mechanism at the end of a stringed instrument's neck. |The headstock of his guitar was ornately carved. 他的吉他琴头雕刻精美。|Synonyms: tuning pegs (for instruments), spindle head, head box|Antonyms: tailstock (for lathes)|noun


headstock:/ˈhɛdstɒk (UK), ˈhɛdstɑːk (US)| n.|车床顶架；床头箱；（某些机器的）机头|The part of a machine, especially a lathe, that holds the part being worked on and rotated. The headstock of a lathe contains the spindle and gears. 车床的床头箱包含主轴和齿轮。|The headstock of the lathe was damaged in the accident. 车床的顶架在事故中受损。|Synonyms: head box, spindle head|Antonyms: tailstock|noun
