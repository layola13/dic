oysterish:/ˈɔɪstərɪʃ/ | adj. |  冷漠的，沉默寡言的；像牡蛎的；有牡蛎味道的 | Resembling or suggestive of an oyster, especially in being closed or uncommunicative. | He gave me an oysterish look, refusing to divulge any information. 他给了我一个冷漠的眼神，拒绝透露任何信息。| The soup had a distinctly oysterish flavor. 这汤有一种明显的牡蛎味。 | 近义词： taciturn, reticent, reserved, clamlike | 反义词： talkative, communicative, loquacious | 词性: adjective
