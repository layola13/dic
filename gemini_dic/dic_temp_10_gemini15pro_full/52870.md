diminishes:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪz/
verb
减少，降低，缩小
makes or becomes less
His influence has diminished with age. 他的影响力随着年龄的增长而减弱。
Synonyms: decrease, reduce, lessen, decline, weaken
Antonyms: increase, enhance, strengthen, grow, augment
词性: 动词
