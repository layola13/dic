butterer:/ˈbʌtərər/| n.|奉承者，拍马屁的人|Someone who flatters someone else, usually excessively, in order to gain favor.  A person who butters someone up. |He's a real butterer, always telling the boss how great he is. 他真是个马屁精，总是告诉老板他有多棒。|Synonyms: flatterer, sycophant, yes-man|Antonyms: critic, detractor|noun
