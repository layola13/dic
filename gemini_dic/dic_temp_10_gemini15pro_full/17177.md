battuta:/bɑːˈtuːtə/ /bætˈuːtə/ | n. | 拍击，敲击；（音乐）拍子；（尤指诙谐的）妙语，俏皮话 | A beat, stroke, or witty remark, especially in music or humor.  It comes from the Italian verb "battere," meaning "to beat." |  The conductor emphasized the final battuta of the symphony. 指挥强调了交响曲的最后一个节拍。| synonyms: beat, stroke, quip, witticism, joke | antonyms: silence, rest, seriousness | noun

battuta:/bɑːˈtuːtə/ /bætˈuːtə/ | n. | (Italian, fencing) A beat, a parry and immediate riposte. | A defensive action followed instantly by an attack in fencing. |  His battuta was so swift, his opponent couldn't react. 他的防御反击如此迅速，对手无法反应。| synonyms: parry and riposte, counterattack | antonyms: defense, parry | noun
