hominy:/ˈhɑːməni/ | n. |玉米粥，粗磨玉米粒|Kernels of corn that have been treated with an alkali, such as lye, in a process called nixtamalization. This process removes the pericarp (outer layer) of the kernels, making them easier to cook and digest, and also increases their nutritional value. Hominy can be eaten whole, ground into grits, or used to make masa for tortillas. |例句：She made hominy grits for breakfast. 她早餐做了玉米粥。

|例句：Hominy is a staple food in many parts of the Americas. 玉米粥是美洲许多地方的主食。|近义词：grits (when ground), posole (Mexican stew with hominy)|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Powhatan *rockahominy, meaning "parched, pounded corn." Borrowed into English in the 17th century.
