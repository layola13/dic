elevating:/ˈɛləveɪtɪŋ/
adj.
提高的，升高的；使人振奋的
Raising or lifting something up; morally or intellectually uplifting.
The elevating experience of listening to a beautiful symphony.  聆听一场优美的交响乐是一种令人振奋的体验。

近义词: uplifting, inspiring, exalting, raising, lifting
反义词: depressing, lowering, degrading

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *elevatus*, past participle of elevare (“to lift up”), from ex- (“out, up”) + levare (“to lift, lighten”).
