noncollections:/ˌnɒnkəˈlɛkʃənz/ | n. |非收藏品；未收款项| Items or debts that are not part of a collection or have not been collected. | The museum's noncollections include items deemed not historically significant.  博物馆的非收藏品包括那些被认为没有历史意义的物品。

The company wrote off the noncollections as bad debts. 该公司将未收款项作为坏账核销。 |近义词：uncollected items/debts, arrears (for debts) |反义词：collections, assets| 词性: 名词
