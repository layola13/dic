bibcock:/ˈbɪbkɒk/ (UK) /ˈbɪbkɑːk/ (US)| n.|屋外水龙头；室外水龙头|An outdoor faucet or tap, typically located on the exterior of a building, used for connecting a hose or drawing water.|The bibcock on the side of the house was leaking. 房子侧面的室外水龙头漏水了。|近义词：outdoor faucet, outside tap, hose bib, sillcock|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps indoor faucet)|noun
