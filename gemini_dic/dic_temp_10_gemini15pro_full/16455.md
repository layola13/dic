baren: /ˈbɛərən/ | adj. | 贫瘠的，荒芜的；不结果实的；缺乏的 | Too little rain makes the land barren. 雨水太少使得土地贫瘠。|例句：The desert landscape was barren and desolate.  沙漠的景色荒凉而凄凉。

The barren branches of the tree reached towards the sky. 光秃秃的树枝伸向天空。

|近义词：infertile, sterile, unproductive, desolate, arid |反义词：fertile, fruitful, productive, lush |词性：形容词
