court: /kɔːrt/ (British) /kɔːrt/ (American)| n. vt.| 法庭，法院；庭院；球场；宫廷；追求；试图获得|An enclosed area marked out for a game or sport.  A place where legal cases are heard. The residence of a sovereign or other ruler. Pay special attention to (someone) in an attempt to win their support or favour.|例句：The case will be heard in court next week.  下周将在法庭审理此案。

例句：They played tennis on a grass court. 他们在草地球场上打网球。

例句：He's been courting her for months. 他追求她已经好几个月了。

例句：The company is courting new investors. 这家公司正在试图吸引新的投资者。

近义词：courtroom, tribunal (for legal sense); yard, patio (for residence sense); field, pitch (for sports sense); woo, pursue (for courting sense)|反义词:  (No direct opposite for all senses. Could be "ignore," "reject" for the courting sense.)|词性: noun, verb
