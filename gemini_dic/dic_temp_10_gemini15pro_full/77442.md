gawkiness:/ˈɡɔːkɪnɪs/| n. |笨拙，迟钝，不雅观|The gawkiness of adolescence is a phase most people go through. 青春期的笨拙是大多数人都会经历的一个阶段。|例句：His gawkiness made him feel self-conscious. 他的笨拙让他感到不自在。

例句：She overcame her teenage gawkiness and became a graceful dancer. 她克服了青少年时期的笨拙，成为了一名优雅的舞者。

|近义词：awkwardness, clumsiness, ungainliness|反义词: grace, elegance, poise|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gawky + -ness.  Gawky itself likely comes from the obsolete word "gawk," meaning "to stare stupidly," possibly of Scandinavian origin.
