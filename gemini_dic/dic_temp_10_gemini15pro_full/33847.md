circumnavigator:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪtər/ | n. |环球航海家，环球旅行家|A person who travels completely around something, especially the world, by water.|Ferdinand Magellan was the first circumnavigator of the globe, though he didn't survive the entire journey. 费迪南·麦哲伦是第一个环球航行的人，尽管他没有完成整个旅程。|近义词:globetrotter, world traveler|反义词:landlubber|词性:名词

circumnavigate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/ | vt. |环球航行，绕...航行|To travel completely around something, especially the world, by water.|They circumnavigated the globe in a small sailboat. 他们驾驶一艘小帆船环游世界。|近义词:sail around, go around|反义词:stay put|词性:动词
