maidishnesses:/ˈmeɪdɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
少女般的品质或举止; 处女情结
The qualities or behavior characteristic of a young unmarried woman; excessive modesty or prudishness.
Her maidishnesses, such as blushing easily and being easily flustered, were endearing. 她少女般的姿态，例如容易脸红和容易慌乱，很可爱。
近义词: prudery, modesty, demureness, shyness
反义词: boldness, brazenness, immodesty
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From maidish + -ness. Maidish from maid + -ish. Maid from Old English mægden "maiden, virgin," from Proto-Germanic *magwiz.
