hyperproducer:/ˈhaɪpərprəˈdjuːsər/ /ˈhaɪpərprəˈduːsər/ | n. | 超级生产者，产量极高者 | Someone or something that produces a large amount of something, especially more than is usual or necessary. | The factory is a hyperproducer of microchips, churning out millions each day.  这家工厂是微芯片的超级生产商，每天生产数百万个。

This cow is a hyperproducer of milk. 这奶牛产奶量极高。 | 近义词：overproducer, high-yield producer | 反义词: low producer, underproducer | 词性: noun
