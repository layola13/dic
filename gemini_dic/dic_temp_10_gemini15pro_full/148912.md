overwillingly:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪlɪŋli/| adv.|过于乐意地，过分积极地|In a manner that is excessively eager or compliant.|He overwillingly agreed to help, not realizing the task would be so arduous. 他过分积极地答应帮忙，却没有意识到这项任务会如此艰巨。|Example: She overwillingly accepted the extra work, hoping to impress her boss. 她过于乐意地接受了额外的工作，希望能给老板留下好印象。|Synonyms: too eagerly, excessively readily, overzealously|Antonyms: reluctantly, hesitantly, unwillingly|词性:副词
