beast:/biːst/| n.|野兽，怪兽，凶残的人，令人厌恶的事物|A wild animal, especially a large, dangerous four-footed one. Also, a cruel, violent, or unpleasant person or thing.|The lion is a beast of prey. 狮子是食肉野兽。|synonyms: animal, monster, brute, creature|antonyms: human, beauty, angel|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *beste* (Modern French *bête*), from Latin *bēstia* ("beast, animal").
