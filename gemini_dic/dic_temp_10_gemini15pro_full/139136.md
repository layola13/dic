aberration: /ˌæbəˈreɪʃən/ (US), /ˌæbəˈreɪʃn/ (UK)| n. | 偏差，异常，失常 |A departure from what is normal, usual, or expected, typically an unwelcome one. | The sudden drop in temperature was an aberration from the normal summer weather. 气温骤降是夏季正常天气的一种异常现象。|  The politician's scandalous behavior was an aberration from his normally upright character.  这位政客的丑闻行为与他通常正直的性格相悖。 | synonyms: anomaly, deviation, irregularity, abnormality | antonyms: norm, standard, regularity | noun
