hoveringly:/ˈhʌvərɪŋli/
adverb
盘旋地；徘徊地；犹豫不决地
in a hovering manner; remaining in one place in the air.  Lingering or wavering indecisively.
The hummingbird hovered hoveringly over the flower.  蜂鸟在花朵上方盘旋。
He looked hoveringly at the menu, unable to decide. 他犹豫不决地看着菜单，无法决定。
Synonyms: lingeringly, hesitantly, waveringly, uncertainly
Antonyms: decisively, resolutely, firmly, steadily
词性: 副词
