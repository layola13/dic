assiento:/əˈsjɛntoʊ/ /æˈsiɛntoʊ/
noun
协议，合同；(尤指18世纪西班牙与其他国家签订的)黑奴贸易协定
a contract or agreement, especially one between Spain and another power in the 18th century relating to the slave trade.
The Asiento granted Britain the right to supply slaves to Spain's American colonies.  英国通过《阿西恩托条约》获得了向西班牙美洲殖民地提供奴隶的权利。
synonyms: agreement, contract, pact, treaty, accord
antonyms: disagreement, discord, refusal
n.
