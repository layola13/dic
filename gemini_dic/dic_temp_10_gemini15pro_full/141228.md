ootheca:/oʊ.əˈθiːkə/ |n.|卵鞘；卵囊|A case or capsule containing eggs, especially as produced by certain insects, mollusks, and other invertebrates.|The praying mantis’s ootheca can contain hundreds of eggs. 螳螂的卵鞘可以包含数百个卵。|egg case, egg mass| None|Noun
Etymology-> From Ancient Greek  ᾠόν (ōón, “egg”) + θήκη (thḗkē, “case, box”).
