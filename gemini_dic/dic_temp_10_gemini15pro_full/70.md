aan:/ɑːn/
determiner
一(个)；某一(个)
one; a certain
Aan Universiteit van Amsterdam = a University of Amsterdam 阿姆斯特丹大学
He's aan captain of the football team. = He's a captain of the football team. 他是足球队的队长之一。
近义词:a, one
反义词:none, no
词性:determiner


USAGE->
This word "aan" is primarily used in Dutch, not English.  The following examples demonstrate its usage in Dutch, with English translations.

Definite Article ("the"):

De man gaf aan boek aan de vrouw. = The man gave the book to the woman.
Het kind speelde met aan bal. = The child played with a ball.

Indefinite Article ("a/an"):

Ik zag aan vogel in de boom. = I saw a bird in the tree.
Ze kocht aan nieuwe fiets. = She bought a new bicycle.

Preposition ("to," "on," "at," etc.):

Hij gaf het boek aan mij. = He gave the book to me.
De schilderij hangt aan de muur. = The painting hangs on the wall.

Other usages:

Aan het werk = at work
Aan de slag = to get started

注意事项:

"Aan" is not an English word. It's a Dutch word with various functions, including serving as an article (like "the" or "a/an") and a preposition.  It does not have a direct equivalent in English, and its meaning depends on the context.
