mousing:/ˈmaʊsɪŋ/ | n. | 用鼠标操作；捕鼠 | The act of using a computer mouse or the act of a cat hunting mice. |  He spent hours mousing around the internet. 他花了几个小时在网上浏览。The cat was mousing in the garden. 猫在花园里捕鼠。 | 近义词：clicking, navigating (for computer use); hunting, stalking (for cats) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun

mousing:/ˈmaʊsɪŋ/ | adj. | (指猫)捕鼠的；(指人)用鼠标操作的 | Relating to the act of using a mouse or hunting mice. |  Her mousing skills are impressive. 她的鼠标操作技巧令人印象深刻。 The cat's mousing instincts kicked in. 猫的捕鼠本能被激发了。 | 近义词：(For cats) predatory, hunting | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | adjective
