auditorial:/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|听觉的，观众的，礼堂的|Relating to the sense of hearing, or to an audience or auditorium.|The auditorium was filled to capacity for the concert.  音乐厅里挤满了来听音乐会的人。|Near synonyms: acoustic, aural, auditory|Near antonyms: visual, olfactory|adjective

auditorium:/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəm/| n.|礼堂，观众席|A large room or hall used for meetings, performances, etc.|The school auditorium was used for assemblies and plays. 学校礼堂用于集会和演出。|Near synonyms: hall, theater, lecture hall|Near antonyms: backstage, behind the scenes|noun
