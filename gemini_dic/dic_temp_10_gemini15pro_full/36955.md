colouristically:/ˌkʌləˈrɪstɪkli/
adv.|色彩地；在色彩方面|Relating to the use of color, especially in a painting or other work of art.  It describes the way color is used to create a certain effect or mood.|The painting is colouristically rich and vibrant. 这幅画色彩丰富，充满活力。|Synonyms: chromatically, pictorially|Antonyms: monochromatically, dully|Adverb
