overexpressed:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛst/| adj.|过度表达的|Expressing a gene at a higher level than normal.  The gene produces more protein than usual.|The HER2 gene is often overexpressed in breast cancer cells.  HER2基因在乳腺癌细胞中经常过度表达。|Synonyms: upregulated, amplified|Antonyms: downregulated, suppressed|adjective

overexpress:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛs/| v.|过度表达|To express a gene at a higher level than normal.  This leads to an increased production of the protein encoded by the gene.|Some cancers overexpress certain growth factors. 一些癌症会过度表达某些生长因子。|Synonyms: upregulate, amplify|Antonyms: downregulate, suppress|verb
