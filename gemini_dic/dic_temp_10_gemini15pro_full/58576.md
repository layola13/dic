dulcification:/ˌdʌlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 甜化，变甜;  减轻; 缓和 |The dulcification of the tart cherries with sugar made them perfect for a pie. 用糖使酸樱桃变甜，非常适合做馅饼。|  sweetening, mitigation, alleviation |  exacerbation, aggravation | noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *dulcificātiō, from Latin dulcificō (“I sweeten”), from dulcis (“sweet”) + -ficō (from faciō (“I make, I do”)).
