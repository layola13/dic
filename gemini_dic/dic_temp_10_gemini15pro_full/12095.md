artiste:/ɑːrˈtiːst/ /ɑːrˈtɪst/ | n. |艺术家；艺人|A skilled performer, especially a singer, dancer, or actor, who emphasizes artistry over technical skill. A professional entertainer. |例句：The artiste captivated the audience with her soulful performance.  这位艺术家以其深情的表演吸引了观众。

|例句：He is a true artiste, dedicated to his craft. 他是一位真正的艺术家，致力于他的技艺。|近义词：performer, entertainer, musician, actor, singer, dancer|反义词：amateur, novice|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *artiste*, from Italian *artista*, from Latin *ars* (art). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ers-* (to fit together).
