paradiplomatic:/ˌpærədɪpləˈmætɪk/| adj.|准外交的，地方外交的|Relating to the international activities of subnational entities such as regions, provinces, states, and cities, involving activities undertaken by these entities independent of their central governments in pursuit of their own interests.|The province engaged in paradiplomatic efforts to attract foreign investment.|Synonyms: subnational diplomacy, multi-level governance|Antonyms: national diplomacy, centralized diplomacy|adjective
