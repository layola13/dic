immute:/ɪˈmjuːt/| adj.|不变的，不可改变的|Not subject or susceptible to change or alteration.|The laws of physics are immutable.|近义词：unchangeable, unalterable, fixed, permanent, constant, invariable|反义词：mutable, changeable, variable, alterable|词性:形容词
词源：From Latin *immutabilis*, from *in-* (not) + *mutabilis* (changeable), from *mutare* (to change). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (to exchange, change).
