imperious:/ɪmˈpɪəriəs/| adj.|专横的；傲慢的；迫切的|Assuming power or authority without justification; arrogant and domineering.|例句：The imperious queen demanded absolute obedience from her subjects.  专横的女王要求臣民绝对服从。

例句：He gave an imperious wave of his hand, dismissing the servant. 他傲慢地挥了挥手，把仆人打发走了。|近义词：domineering, arrogant, haughty, overbearing, dictatorial|反义词：humble, modest, subservient, meek|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *imperiosus*, from Latin imperium (“command, power, empire”).
