agonize:/ˈæɡənaɪz/| v. |感到极度痛苦；苦苦思索|To suffer great pain or anguish; to worry about something intensely.|She agonized over the decision for weeks. 她为这个决定苦苦思索了几个星期。| He agonized over whether to tell her the truth. 他非常苦恼要不要告诉她真相。|近义词：worry, fret, anguish, suffer|反义词:relax, celebrate|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *agonizare, from Greek agōnizesthai "contend for a prize," from agōn "contest."
