blowpipe:/ˈbloʊpaɪp/ (US) /ˈbləʊpaɪp/ (UK)| n. |吹管；吹箭筒|A small tube through which air is blown to direct a flame onto something or to propel a small projectile. |The glassblower used a blowpipe to shape the molten glass. 玻璃吹制工用吹管来塑造熔化的玻璃。| The tribesmen hunted with blowpipes and poisoned darts. 部落居民用吹管和毒镖狩猎。|synonyms: blowgun, tube |antonyms: N/A| noun
