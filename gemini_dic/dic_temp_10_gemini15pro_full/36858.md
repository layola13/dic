colophonite:/ˈkɒləfəˌnaɪt/ /ˈkɑːləfəˌnaɪt/
noun
树脂石，钙铁榴石的一种
A brown, yellow, or green variety of andradite garnet, usually with a resinous luster.  It is essentially a massive, granular grossular or andradite.
The jeweler showed her a beautiful necklace made with colophonite.  珠宝商给她看了一条用树脂石做的漂亮的项链。
Synonyms:  andradite, garnet
Antonyms:  N/A
