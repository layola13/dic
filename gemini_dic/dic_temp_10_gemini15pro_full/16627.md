baronian:/bəˈroʊniən/| adj.|巴罗尼亚的；巴罗尼亚风格的|Relating to or characteristic of Baronie, a historical region in southern Italy, or its people or culture.  It can also refer to the style of Baroque architecture and art that flourished in the region.|The baronial palace was a magnificent example of baronian architecture. 这座男爵宫殿是巴罗尼亚建筑的宏伟典范。|Near synonyms: aristocratic, noble, feudal|Antonyms: common, plebeian|adjective
