dethroning:/dɪˈθroʊnɪŋ/
n.|废黜，罢免|The dethroning of the king marked the end of an era. 国王的废黜标志着一个时代的结束。
v.|废黜，罢免|The rebels are planning on dethroning the current leader. 反叛者正计划废黜现任领导人。
近义词: ousting, removing, deposing
反义词: crowning, enthroning
词性: 动名词/现在分词


词源:  From de- + throne + -ing.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "down from".  "Throne" refers to the seat of a monarch.  Therefore, "dethroning" literally means removing someone from their throne.
