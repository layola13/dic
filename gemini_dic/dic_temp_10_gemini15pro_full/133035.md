nonenviableness:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəbəlnəs/
n.
不可羡慕；不值得羡慕
the state or quality of not being enviable
His nonenviableness was clear after he lost all his money. 他失去所有钱后，他的不值得羡慕显而易见。

近义词:undesirability, unattractiveness
反义词:enviableness, desirability
词性:noun
