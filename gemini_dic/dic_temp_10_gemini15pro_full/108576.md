leach's:/liːtʃɪz/| n.| 滤液; 沥出物; 浸滤液| Leach's refers to the liquid that has percolated through a substance, carrying dissolved or suspended matter.  It is the possessive form of "leach," indicating the liquid belonging to or produced by the leaching process. | The leach's from the mine contained harmful chemicals. 矿场的浸滤液含有有害化学物质。| synonyms:  percolate, filtrate, effluent | antonyms:  precipitate, sediment | noun

leach's:/liːtʃɪz/| n.| 沥滤物; 浸出物| Leach's can also refer to the material that has been removed from a substance by a percolating liquid.  This is less common than the liquid definition. | The leach's from the tea leaves stained the tablecloth. 茶叶的沥滤物弄脏了桌布。| synonyms: extract, residue | antonyms: original substance | noun
