denned:/dend/
v.|居住; 藏身|Used of animals, to live in or retreat to a den.  The foxes denned in the hillside. |例句：The bears denned up for the winter. 熊为了过冬躲进了洞穴。

例句：The wolves denned in a cave. 狼在一个山洞里安家。
|近义词：holed up, sheltered, nested|反义词:emerged, left|词性:动词

denned:/dend/
adj.|居住在兽穴中的; 藏在兽穴中的|Used of animals, having a den. The denned badger was difficult to spot. |例句：The denned fox was safe from the hunters. 藏在洞穴里的狐狸躲过了猎人的追捕。

例句：The denned animals were protected from the storm.  躲在洞穴里的动物免受了暴风雨的侵袭。
|近义词：sheltered, hidden|反义词:exposed, visible|词性:形容词
