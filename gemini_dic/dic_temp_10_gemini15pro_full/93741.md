immaterialness:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəlnəs/ | n. | 非物质性；无关紧要；无足轻重 |The immaterialness of worldly possessions.  世俗财产的无足轻重。 | Synonyms: insignificance, unimportance, irrelevance, triviality | Antonyms: importance, significance, relevance, materiality | noun

Etymology:  From immaterial + -ness.  Immaterial from im- (not) + material (matter).  Ultimately from Latin *materia* (matter).
