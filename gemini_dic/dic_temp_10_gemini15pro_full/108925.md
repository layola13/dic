leecher:/ˈliːtʃər/| n. |水蛭；寄生虫；吸血鬼；（尤指）利用网络下载资源但不上传的人|Someone who downloads or benefits from resources, information, or effort of others without contributing anything in return, especially in online communities.|He was labeled a leecher on the forum for constantly downloading files without ever uploading. 他因为经常下载文件却从不上传而被论坛上的人称为“吸血鬼”。|近义词：freeloader, parasite, sponge|反义词：contributor, sharer, seeder|noun
