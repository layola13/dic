heptyl:/ˈhep.təl/| adj. n.|庚基, 七（烷）基|Relating to or containing seven carbon atoms in a chain, especially of an alkyl radical.  A univalent radical containing seven carbon atoms.|例句：Heptyl alcohol is a colorless liquid. 庚醇是一种无色液体。

例句：A heptyl group is a seven-carbon alkyl substituent. 庚基是一个七碳烷基取代基。|近义词：C7,  heptane-based|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hepta* "seven" + *-yl*, a suffix used in forming the names of alkyl radicals.
