hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| adj. n. vt.|胡言乱语；花招；戏法；施魔法|Meaningless words used in conjuring or sleight of hand.  Also refers to trickery or deception.|The magician waved his wand and muttered some hocus-pocus before pulling a rabbit out of the hat. 魔术师挥舞着魔杖，嘴里念叨着咒语，然后从帽子里变出一只兔子。|synonyms:trickery, deception, sleight of hand, magic, conjuring, wizardry|antonyms:reality, truth, honesty|adjective, noun, verb

hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| n.|胡言乱语；花招；戏法|Nonsense words used by magicians.|All that talk about doubling your money is just hocus-pocus. 所有关于让你的钱翻倍的说法都是骗人的鬼话。|synonyms:nonsense, gibberish, mumbo jumbo|antonyms:sense, logic, reason|noun

hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| vt.|施魔法；欺骗|To trick or deceive someone.|He hocus-pocused the investors into believing his scheme. 他用花言巧语骗投资者相信他的计划。|synonyms:trick, deceive, bamboozle, hoodwink|antonyms:be honest, enlighten, inform|verb
