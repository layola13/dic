gentianine: /ˈdʒɛnʃəniːn/ | n. | 龙胆宁，龙胆碱 | A bitter crystalline alkaloid, C20H20N2O2, obtained from the rhizome and roots of the yellow gentian.  It is used as a tonic and stomachic. | Gentianine is extracted from the roots of the yellow gentian plant. 龙胆宁是从黄龙胆植物的根部提取的。|  Gentianine stimulates appetite and aids digestion. 龙胆宁刺激食欲并帮助消化。 | synonyms:  bitter principle,  gentian alkaloid | antonyms:  None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | noun
