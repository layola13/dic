lusitania:/luːsɪˈteɪniə/
noun
卢西塔尼亚号（邮轮）
A British passenger ship that was sunk by a German U-boat in 1915.
The sinking of the Lusitania was a major factor in the United States' entry into World War I. 卢西塔尼亚号的沉没是美国加入第一次世界大战的主要因素。
synonyms: ship, liner, passenger ship
antonyms: N/A
