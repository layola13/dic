bloatednesses:/ˈbloʊtɪdnəsɪz/
noun
膨胀；肿胀；臃肿
The state of being bloated, excessively full of liquid or gas.
The bloatednesses of his stomach after the feast was uncomfortable.  宴会后，他的肚子胀得不舒服。
近义词: swelling, distension, inflation
反义词: flatness, thinness
