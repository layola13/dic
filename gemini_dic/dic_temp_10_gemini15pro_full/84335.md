halfhazard:/ˌhæfˈhæzərd/ |adj.| 偶然的，随便的，杂乱无章的 |Characterized by lack of order or planning, random; done in a way that does not seem to be planned or organized. |  The books were piled on the shelves in a halfhazard way. 书架上的书堆放得杂乱无章。 | The investigation seemed halfhazard and incomplete. 调查似乎很草率，不完整。 |  random, careless, disorganized, haphazard |  organized, systematic, methodical, planned | adjective
