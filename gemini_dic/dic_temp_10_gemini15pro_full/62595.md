enfeebling:/ɪnˈfiːblɪŋ/
adjective
使人衰弱的；使衰弱的
Making someone or something very weak.
The illness had an enfeebling effect on her. 这种疾病对她有衰弱的作用。
The long illness left him enfeebling.  这场久病让他变得虚弱。

近义词:weakening, debilitating, incapacitating
反义词:strengthening, invigorating, empowering
词性:adjective
