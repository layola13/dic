defeating:/dɪˈfiːtɪŋ/
adjective
击败的，战胜的; 使人泄气的
causing someone to feel discouraged or disheartened.
例句：The team suffered a defeating loss.  该队遭受了惨败。

例句：It was a defeating experience. 这是一次令人沮丧的经历。

近义词：discouraging, disheartening, demoralizing, frustrating
反义词：encouraging, heartening, inspiring, uplifting
词性: adjective


defeating:/dɪˈfiːtɪŋ/
verb
击败，战胜 (present participle of defeat)
the action of winning a victory over someone or something in a game, war, or other competition.
例句：He is defeating his opponent in the chess game. 他正在国际象棋比赛中击败他的对手。

例句：They are defeating all expectations.  他们正在打破所有的预期。

近义词：conquering, overcoming, vanquishing, besting
反义词：losing to, succumbing to, yielding to
词性: verb (present participle)


词源: From Middle English *defete, from Anglo-Norman defeit, past participle of desfaire, from Old French desfaire (“to undo, destroy, defeat”), from des- (“dis-”) + faire (“to do, make”), from Latin facere.
