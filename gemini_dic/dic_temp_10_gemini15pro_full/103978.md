jugged:/dʒʌɡd/ | adj. | 炖的；(指兔子或野兔)在瓦罐里加调料焖熟的；被监禁的 | Stewed or cooked in a jug, especially of rabbit or hare; imprisoned. | The jugged hare was a rich and flavorful dish.  瓦罐焖野兔是一道浓郁美味的菜肴。 |  The poacher was caught and jugged for six months. 偷猎者被抓获并被监禁了六个月。 | 近义词: stewed, braised, imprisoned, jailed | 反义词: released, freed | 词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "jug" + "-ed," meaning "cooked or preserved in a jug."  The sense of "imprisoned" is likely a figurative extension, referring to being confined like something in a jug.
