multiplicator:/ˈmʌltɪplɪkeɪtər/| n.|乘数, 倍增器|A number by which another number is multiplied.  Something that multiplies or increases something else.|The government used a multiplicator of 2.5 to calculate the benefits of the project. 政府使用2.5的乘数来计算项目的效益。|Synonyms: factor, coefficient, multiplier|Antonyms: divisor, denominator|noun
