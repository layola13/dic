capitan:/kæpɪˈtæn/ | n. | 船长，队长 | A person who is in command of a ship or other vessel, or the leader of a team or group. | The capitan steered the ship through the storm. 船长驾驶着船只穿越了风暴。| The capitan of the football team gave an inspiring speech. 足球队队长发表了鼓舞人心的讲话。 |Synonyms: skipper, master, commander, leader, chief | Antonyms: crewman, subordinate, follower | noun
