flavoursome:/ˈfleɪvəsəm/| adj.|美味的，可口的|Having a pleasant and distinct flavour; tasty.|The chef prepared a flavoursome dish with a blend of spices. 厨师用混合香料调制了一道美味的菜。|近义词：tasty, delicious, savory, palatable|反义词：bland, tasteless, insipid|词性:形容词
词源: From flavour + -some.  Flavour itself comes from Old French *flaur, from Latin *flator, from flare "to blow." The suffix "-some" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "producing."
