balkier:/ˈbɔːlkɪər/ (US), /ˈbɔːlkɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更不愿合作的，更倔强的|More unwilling to cooperate or comply; more stubborn.|The mule became balkier as the day wore on, refusing to move even an inch.  这头骡子随着时间的推移变得越来越倔强，甚至一英寸也不肯移动。

The team was dealing with a balkier client who kept changing the project requirements.  团队正在与一位更不愿合作的客户打交道，他不断更改项目需求。|近义词：more stubborn, more obstinate, more recalcitrant|反义词：more cooperative, more compliant, more amenable|词性:形容词
balk:/bɔːlk/| v. n.| 阻碍，阻止；不愿做；畏缩不前| To refuse to proceed or to do something; hesitate or be unwilling.|The horse balked at the jump.  这匹马在跳跃前畏缩不前。

He balked at the idea of working on weekends. 他不愿在周末工作。|近义词：hesitate, resist, refuse|反义词：proceed, accept, comply|词性:动词/名词

词源：balk 最初可能来自中古英语 balken（意为“留下未耕种的田埂”），后来演变为指马或牛拒绝前进。这个词义逐渐扩展到指人犹豫或拒绝做某事。
