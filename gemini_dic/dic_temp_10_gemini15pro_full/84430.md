hallucination: /həˌluːsɪˈneɪʃn/ /həˌluːsɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 幻觉，妄想 | An experience involving the apparent perception of something not present. |  He began to experience hallucinations. 他开始出现幻觉。

The patient reported experiencing auditory hallucinations. 病人报告说经历了幻听。

近义词: delusion, illusion, mirage, vision | 反义词: reality, perception | 词性: noun
