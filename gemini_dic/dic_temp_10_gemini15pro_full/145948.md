overdiscriminatingly:/ˌoʊvərˌdɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋli/
| adv. | 过分歧视地，过度地区别对待地 |  Acting with excessive or unnecessary discrimination; making distinctions that are too fine or subtle, often to the point of being unfair or unreasonable. |  He overdiscriminatingly refused to hire anyone who hadn't graduated from an Ivy League school. 他过分歧视地拒绝雇佣任何没有从常春藤盟校毕业的人。 |  Example: She overdiscriminatingly analyzed the data, focusing on minute details that were ultimately irrelevant. 她过分细致地分析了数据，专注于最终无关紧要的微小细节。 | 近义词: excessively discriminating, hypercritical, over-particular | 反义词: indiscriminately, uncritically | 词性: 副词
