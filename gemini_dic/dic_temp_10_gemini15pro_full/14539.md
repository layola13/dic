averroist:/əˈvɛroʊɪst/ | n. adj. | 阿威罗伊主义者；阿威罗伊派的 | A follower of Averroes, a 12th-century Islamic philosopher who interpreted Aristotle's works.  Averroists often held views considered heretical by the Church, such as the concept of a single intellect shared by all humans. |  He was accused of being an Averroist because of his unorthodox philosophical views. 他因为非正统的哲学观点而被指责为阿威罗伊主义者。| Near synonyms: freethinker, rationalist, heretic (in the historical context) | Near antonyms: orthodox, scholastic (in the historical context) | noun, adjective
