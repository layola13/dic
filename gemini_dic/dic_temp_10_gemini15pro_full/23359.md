braccate:/bræˈkeɪt/
adjective
穿着裤子的；缚有绑腿的
Wearing trousers or breeches; having the legs covered.
The Roman soldiers, braccate and armed, marched through the streets.  罗马士兵穿着裤子，全副武装，在街上行进。
近义词: trousered, breeched
反义词: unbraccate, barelegged
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *braccatus*, from *braccae* (trousers, breeches).
