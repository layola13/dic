moneyball:/ˈmʌniˌbɔːl/| n.| 金钱球；《点球成金》（电影名）|A strategy in baseball (and later other sports) of using statistical analysis to identify undervalued players and assemble a competitive team on a limited budget.  It was popularized by the book and subsequent movie "Moneyball," which chronicled the Oakland A's use of the strategy.| Billy Beane's moneyball strategy helped the Oakland A's compete with richer teams. 比利·比恩的金钱球策略帮助奥克兰运动家队与更富有的球队竞争。|近义词：sabermetrics, analytics|反义词: traditional scouting|词性:名词
