brazzle:/ˈbræzəl/| n. |喧闹，吵闹；争吵；烤肉野餐|A noisy, boisterous celebration or social gathering, often involving drinking and feasting, especially a barbecue.  A brazzle can also refer to the act of roasting meat over an open fire.|They had a big brazzle in the park to celebrate the holiday. 他们在公园里举行了一场盛大的烤肉野餐来庆祝节日。|The family had a summer brazzle with all their relatives.  这家人和所有的亲戚一起举行了夏季烧烤聚会。|synonyms: barbecue, cookout, feast, revel, carousal|antonyms: quiet, silence|noun
