catechumenate: /ˌkætɪˈkjuːmɪnɪt/ | n. |慕道期，慕道者身份|The period of instruction and preparation for baptism and church membership, especially in the early Christian Church.  The state or condition of being a catechumen.| He completed his catechumenate and was baptized at Easter. 他完成了慕道期，并在复活节受洗。| Synonyms: preparation, instruction, apprenticeship, novitiate | Antonyms: membership, initiation (in the sense of already being initiated) | noun


catechumenate: /ˌkætɪˈkjuːmɪnɪt/ | n. |慕道者团体| A group of catechumens. | The catechumenate met weekly for instruction. 慕道班每周聚会学习。| Synonyms: class, group, cohort | Antonyms: congregation, membership (in the sense of already being members) | noun
