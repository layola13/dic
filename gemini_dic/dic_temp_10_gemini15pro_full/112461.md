loudspeaker:/ˌlaʊdˈspiːkər/ /ˌlaʊdˈspikər/| n.|扬声器；喇叭|A device that converts electrical signals into sound waves and broadcasts them at a higher volume.|The band used a powerful loudspeaker to amplify their music. 乐队使用了一个功率强大的扬声器来放大他们的音乐。|synonyms: speaker, amplifier, bullhorn|antonyms: microphone, receiver|noun
loudspeaker:/ˌlaʊdˈspiːkər/ /ˌlaʊdˈspikər/| n.|扩音器|A device that converts electrical signals into sound waves and broadcasts them at a higher volume, often used for public announcements.|The principal used a loudspeaker to address the students in the assembly hall. 校长用扩音器在礼堂里向学生们讲话。|synonyms: speaker, amplifier, megaphone, PA system|antonyms: microphone, receiver|noun
