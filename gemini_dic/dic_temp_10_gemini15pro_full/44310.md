crooners:/ˈkruːnərz/| n. |低吟歌手；轻柔的歌手|A singer, typically male, who sings sentimental songs in a soft, low voice.|例句：The crooners of the 1950s were incredibly popular.  20世纪50年代的低吟歌手非常受欢迎。

例句：He's a modern-day crooner with a velvety voice. 他是一位嗓音柔和的现代低吟歌手。|近义词：singer, vocalist, balladeer|反义词:shouter|词性:noun
