imputes:/ɪmˈpjuːts/| v. |归咎于，归罪于；认为…是…的起因|represents (something, especially something undesirable) as being done, caused, or possessed by someone; attribute.|The report imputes the rise in crime to high unemployment. 报告将犯罪率的上升归咎于高失业率。|synonyms: attribute, ascribe, assign, credit, accredit, chalk up to|antonyms: exculpate, absolve|verb

imputes:/ɪmˈpjuːts/| v. |估算，推算（缺失数据）|calculate or estimate the value of (something) using available data or information.|The program imputes missing values based on the average of the available data. 该程序根据可用数据的平均值估算缺失值。|synonyms: estimate, calculate, assess, evaluate, gauge|antonyms: N/A|verb
