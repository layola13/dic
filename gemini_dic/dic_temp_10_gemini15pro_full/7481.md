anklet: /ˈæŋklət/ | n. | 脚链 | An ornament worn around the ankle.  | She wore a delicate silver anklet on her left ankle. 她左脚踝上戴着一条精致的银脚链。 |  Example: The dancer's anklets jingled as she moved. 舞蹈演员的脚链随着她的舞动发出叮当声。 | Synonyms: ankle bracelet, ankle chain | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From ankle + -et (diminutive suffix).  The word "ankle" comes from Old English *ancleow, related to Old Norse ökkla and German Knöchel.
