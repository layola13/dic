antihumanists:/ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪsts/
n.|反人文主义者|People who oppose or critique the principles of humanism, particularly its emphasis on human reason, agency, and values.|The antihumanists argued that humanism was a naive and self-serving ideology.|近义词:critics of humanism, poststructuralists|反义词:humanists|词性:名词

antihumanist:/ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪst/
adj.|反人文主义的|Opposed to or critical of the principles of humanism.|His antihumanist stance led him to question the very foundations of Western thought.|近义词:anti-humanistic|反义词:humanist, humanistic|词性:形容词
