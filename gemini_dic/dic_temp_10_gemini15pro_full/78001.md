gentlemanlikeness:/ˈdʒɛntlmənlɪknəs/| n.|绅士风度，君子风范|The quality of being gentlemanlike; possessing the characteristics of a gentleman, such as courtesy, politeness, and integrity.|He was admired for his gentlemanlikeness and impeccable manners. 他因其绅士风度和无可挑剔的礼仪而备受赞赏。|近义词：chivalry, courtesy, gentility, politeness|反义词：rudeness, coarseness, vulgarity|词性:noun

Etymology:
From gentleman + -like + -ness.  Gentleman from gentle (meaning 'noble, high-born') + man.  Ultimately from Old French gentilhomme.
