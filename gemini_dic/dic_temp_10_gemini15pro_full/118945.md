meteorography:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒɡrəfi/
n.
气象学，气候学
The study of weather and atmospheric phenomena.
Meteorography is a crucial field in understanding and predicting weather patterns. 气象学是理解和预测天气模式的关键领域。
The advancements in meteorography have significantly improved weather forecasting accuracy. 气象学的进步显著提高了天气预报的准确性。
近义词: meteorology
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *météorographie*, from Ancient Greek *μετέωρος* (metéōros, “high in the air, lofty; raised from the ground, hanging”) + *-γραφία* (-graphía, “writing”).
