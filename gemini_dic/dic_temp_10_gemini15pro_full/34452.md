clavichords:/ˈklævɪkɔːrdz/
n.
古钢琴；击弦古钢琴；
A rectangular keyboard instrument producing a soft sound by means of metal tangents that strike the strings.
The clavichord was a popular instrument in the Renaissance and Baroque periods.  文艺复兴和巴洛克时期，古钢琴是一种流行的乐器。
The musician played a delicate melody on the clavichord.  音乐家在古钢琴上演奏了一曲优美的旋律。
