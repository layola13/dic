colloquialism:/kəˈloʊkwɪəlɪzəm/| n. |俗语；口语；白话|A colloquialism is a word or phrase that is used in informal conversation, but not in formal writing or speech.  It's characteristic of or appropriate to ordinary or familiar conversation rather than formal speech or writing.|Example: "Wanna" is a colloquialism for "want to". “Wanna”是“want to”的口语说法。

Example:  Using "kid" for "child" is a common colloquialism.  用“kid”代替“child”是一个常见的口语用法。|近义词：informalism, slang, idiom|反义词:formalism|词性:noun
