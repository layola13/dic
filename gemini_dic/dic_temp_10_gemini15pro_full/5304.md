allottable:/əˈlɒtəbl/
adjective
可分配的
able to be allotted or assigned.
例句：The allottable resources are limited. 可分配的资源是有限的。
例句：The company has an allottable budget for each department. 公司为每个部门都设有可分配的预算。
近义词：assignable, distributable, apportionable
反义词：unallotable, indivisible
词性: adjective

词源:
From allot + -able.  Allot comes from Old French *aloter "to divide by lots," from a- "to" + lot "portion, share."
