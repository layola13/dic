inattentions: /ˌɪnəˈtɛnʃənz/
| n. |疏忽，注意力不集中|The accident was caused by a series of inattentions on the part of the driver.  这起事故是由司机一连串的疏忽造成的。|
| n. |漫不经心|His inattentions during the meeting were noticed by the boss.  他在会议上的漫不经心被老板注意到了。|
|近义词：negligence, carelessness, oversight, distraction|
|反义词：attention, diligence, care, focus|
|词性：名词|


Etymology:
From in- (not) + attention.  The suffix -s indicates the plural form. 源于in-（不）+ attention（注意力）。 后缀-s 表示复数形式。
