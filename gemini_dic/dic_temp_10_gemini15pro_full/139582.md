obviating:/ˈɒbviˌeɪtɪŋ/
verb
排除；避免；使不必要
removing (a need or problem)
Installing a new lock will obviate the need for a key. 安装新锁将不再需要钥匙。
synonyms: preclude, prevent, remove, avoid, avert
antonyms: necessitate, require, cause, create
verb
