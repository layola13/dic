papal:/ˈpeɪpəl/| adj.|教皇的，教宗的|Of or relating to the Pope or the papacy.|The papal visit drew huge crowds. 教皇的访问吸引了大批群众。|近义词：pontifical, apostolic|反义词：secular|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *papal*, from Latin *papālis* (“of the pope”), from *papa* (“pope, bishop, father”).
