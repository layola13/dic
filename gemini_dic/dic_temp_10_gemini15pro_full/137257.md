nonsweeter:/nɒnˈswiːtər/ | adj. | 不更甜的，甜度不更高的 |  A comparative form of "nonsweet," meaning not sweet or less sweet. It implies a comparison between two things, where one is not sweeter than the other. | This apple is nonsweeter than that one. 这个苹果不比那个甜。| The new recipe is nonsweeter, which I prefer.  新的食谱不更甜，我更喜欢。| Near synonyms: less sweet, equally sweet, no sweeter | Antonyms: sweeter, more sweet | adjective
