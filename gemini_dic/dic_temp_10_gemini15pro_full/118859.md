metaplasm: /ˈmɛtəˌplæzəm/ | n. | 语音转换，变音 | A change in a word, such as the addition, omission, or transposition of sounds or letters. | Example 1: The word "amn't" is a metaplasm of "am not."  amn't是am not的变音。| Example 2:  The poet used metaplasm to create a rhyming effect. 诗人使用变音来创造押韵效果。| 近义词:  metathesis, alteration, modification | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *metaplasma, from Greek metaplasmos “transformation,” from metaplassein "to transform," from meta- “change” + plassein "to mold."
