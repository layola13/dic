odorate:/ˈəʊdəreɪt/| adj. v.|有气味的，使…有气味|Having a distinct odor; to impart an odor to.|The odorate flowers filled the room with a sweet fragrance. 芬芳的花朵使房间充满了甜美的香味。|The chemist odorated the air with a new compound. 化学家用一种新的化合物使空气变得有气味。|近义词：fragrant, aromatic, scented, perfumed|反义词：odorless, unscented|词性: adjective, verb
Etymology: From Latin *odoratus*, past participle of *odorare* "to give forth a smell," from *odor* "smell."
