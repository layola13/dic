nonlegislatively:/ˌnɒnˈledʒɪslətɪvli/
adverb
非立法地，非通过立法
without the involvement of a legislature or the enactment of laws.
The issue was resolved nonlegislatively, through negotiations between the parties.  这个问题是通过各方之间的谈判，以非立法的方式解决的。
The mayor attempted to address the problem nonlegislatively, using executive orders. 市长试图用行政命令以非立法的方式来解决这个问题。
近义词: informally, privately, extra-legally
反义词: legislatively, legally, by law
词性: adverb
