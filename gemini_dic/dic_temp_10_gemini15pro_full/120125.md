mimotannic: /mɪmoʊˈtænɪk/
adjective
含羞草鞣酸的; 属于含羞草鞣酸的
Relating to or derived from mimotannic acid, a tannin found in various plants, especially those of the genus Mimosa.
Mimosa bark contains mimotannic acid, which is used in tanning. 含羞草树皮含有含羞草鞣酸，用于鞣革。
Mimotannic acid is a powerful astringent. 含羞草鞣酸是一种强力的收敛剂。

近义词: tannic
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it describes a specific chemical compound.  One could consider antonyms related to its effects, like "relaxing" (opposite of astringent), but not for the compound itself.)
