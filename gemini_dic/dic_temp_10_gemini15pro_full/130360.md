non-understanding:/ˌnɒnʌndəˈstændɪŋ/ | adj. | 不理解的，缺乏理解的 | Lack of understanding; failure to understand. |  Her non-understanding of the situation led to further complications. 她对情况的不理解导致了进一步的复杂化。

| Example:  The teacher expressed her non-understanding of the student's poor performance. 老师表示不理解这个学生糟糕的表现。 | Synonyms:  incomprehension, misunderstanding, confusion, bewilderment | Antonyms: understanding, comprehension, grasp, apprehension | adjective


non-understanding:/ˌnɒnʌndəˈstændɪŋ/ | n. | 不理解，缺乏理解 | The state of not understanding something. | His non-understanding of the rules resulted in a penalty. 他对规则的不理解导致了惩罚。

| Example: There was a general non-understanding of the new policy. 大家普遍对新政策不理解。 | Synonyms:  incomprehension, lack of comprehension, confusion, bewilderment | Antonyms: understanding, comprehension, grasp, apprehension | noun
