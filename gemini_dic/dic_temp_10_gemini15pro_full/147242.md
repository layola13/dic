overmellowly:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛloʊli/| adv.|过度成熟地；过分伤感地；过分多愁善感地|Excessively mellow; overly sentimental or emotional.  It describes something done in a way that is too soft, sweet, or sentimental. It implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the mellowness is excessive and perhaps insincere or cloying.|He sang the love song overmellowly, making the audience cringe. 他唱情歌过于伤感，让观众感到尴尬。|近义词：oversentimentally, mawkishly, cloyingly|反义词：harshly, coldly, austerely|词性:副词

词源:

"over-" (prefix) + "mellow" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)

"over-" 表示“过度”、“过分”
"mellow" 源自古英语 "mearu"，意为“柔软的”、“成熟的”
"-ly" 将形容词转换为副词
