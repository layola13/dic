cowerers:/ˈkaʊərərz/ | n. | 畏缩者，胆怯者 | People who cower, showing fear or submission. | The cowerers hid in the basement during the bombing raid. 轰炸袭击时，胆小鬼们躲在地下室里。|  Synonyms:  timid souls, scaredy-cats, chickens | Antonyms: heroes, bravehearts | Noun

cower:/ˈkaʊər/ | v. | 畏缩，蜷缩 | To crouch down in fear. | The dog cowered under the table during the thunderstorm.  雷暴期间，狗蜷缩在桌子底下。| Synonyms: cringe, shrink, recoil | Antonyms: stand tall, face, confront | Verb
