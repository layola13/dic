musical: /ˈmjuːzɪkl/ (US) /ˈmjuːzɪkəl/ (UK) | adj. n. |音乐的；悦耳的；爱好音乐的；音乐剧| Relating to music; melodious; fond of music; a play or film in which singing and dancing play an essential part. |  
例句：She has a very musical voice. 她有一副很悦耳的嗓音。
例句：We went to see a musical last night. 我们昨晚去看了一场音乐剧。
近义词：melodious, harmonic, tuneful (adj.); opera, play (n.) |
反义词：unmusical, dissonant (adj.) |
词性：adjective, noun |
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin musicalis, from Latin musica ‘music’.
