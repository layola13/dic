comanagements:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒmənts/| n. |共同管理，共同经营|The act or process of managing something together with one or more other people or entities.|The comanagements of the project by the two companies ensured its success.  两家公司对项目的共同管理确保了它的成功。|Synonyms: joint management, shared management, collaborative management|Antonyms: sole management, independent management|noun
