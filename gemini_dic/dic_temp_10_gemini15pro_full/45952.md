CVV:/ˌsiː viː ˈviː/ | n. | Card Verification Value (信用卡安全码) | A three- or four-digit security code printed on the back of credit and debit cards. It is used to verify that the person making the purchase has physical possession of the card. | Example: Please enter the CVV code from the back of your card to complete the transaction.  请在卡背面输入CVV码以完成交易。 | Synonym: CVC, CID, security code | Antonym: N/A | noun
