ainu:/ˈaɪnuː/
n.|阿伊努人；阿伊努语|A member of an indigenous people of northern Japan, also called the Ezo,  and parts of Russia (Sakhalin and the Kuril Islands).  The Ainu language is considered a language isolate.|The Ainu have a rich culture with distinct traditions and ceremonies.|近义词:Ezo (historical)|反义词:None|词性:noun

ainu:/ˈaɪnuː/
adj.|阿伊努人的；阿伊努语的|Relating to the Ainu people or their language.|Traditional Ainu clothing is often decorated with intricate embroidery.|近义词:Ezo (historical)|反义词:None|词性:adjective
