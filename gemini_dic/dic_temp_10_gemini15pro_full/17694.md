bed-roll:/ˈbedˌroʊl/| n.|睡袋，铺盖卷|A portable bedding consisting of a mattress, blankets, or other sleeping gear rolled up for carrying. |He packed his bed-roll and set off on his camping trip. 他打包好睡袋，开始了他的露营之旅。|Synonym: sleeping bag, bedding|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fixed bed")|noun

bed-roll:/ˈbedˌroʊl/| v.|卷起铺盖|To roll up bedding into a bundle for carrying or storage. |She bed-rolled her sleeping bag and stowed it in the closet. 她把睡袋卷起来放进了壁橱里。|Synonym: roll up, bundle up|Antonym: unroll, unpack|verb
