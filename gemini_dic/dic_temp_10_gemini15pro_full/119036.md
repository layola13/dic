methylamine:/ˈmɛθəlˌæmiːn/ | n. | 甲胺 | A colorless, flammable, fishy-smelling gas, CH3NH2, the simplest primary amine, used in the manufacture of insecticides, surfactants, and pharmaceuticals. | Methylamine is used in the synthesis of various pharmaceuticals. 甲胺用于合成各种药物。| Methylamine has a strong, unpleasant odor similar to that of rotting fish. 甲胺有一种强烈的、难闻的气味，类似于腐烂的鱼。| synonyms: aminomethane | antonyms: N/A | noun
