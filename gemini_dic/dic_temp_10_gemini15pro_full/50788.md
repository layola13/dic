descendant:/dɪˈsendənt/ | n. | 后裔；子孙 | A person who is related to someone who lived a long time ago; someone who is descended from another person. |  Her descendants still live in the area. 她的后代仍然住在该地区。

| The painting was passed down to his descendants. 这幅画传给了他的后代。|近义词：offspring, heir, progeny |反义词: ancestor, forebear | noun
