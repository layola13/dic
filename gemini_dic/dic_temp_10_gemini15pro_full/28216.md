caprylic:/kəˈprɪlɪk/
adjective
辛酸的；羊脂质的；含有辛酸的
Relating to or derived from caprylic acid.  A saturated fatty acid with eight carbon atoms, found in the milk of some mammals and in coconut and palm oils.
Caprylic acid is used in the production of esters used in perfumery and also in the manufacture of dyes. 辛酸用于生产用于香料的酯，也用于制造染料。
Caprylic acid is found naturally in coconut oil and palm kernel oil. 辛酸天然存在于椰子油和棕榈仁油中。
synonym:octanoic
antonym:N/A
词性:adjective
