informed: /ɪnˈfɔːrmd/ /ɪnˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
知情的；见多识广的
Having or showing knowledge of a particular subject or situation.
例句：She made an informed decision after researching all the options. 她研究了所有选择后做出了明智的决定。

例句：He is an informed critic of modern art. 他是一位对现代艺术见多识广的评论家。

近义词：knowledgeable, aware, educated, enlightened
反义词：ignorant, unaware, uninformed, uneducated
词性：形容词
