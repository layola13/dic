archetypal:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtaɪpəl/ | adj. | 原型的；典型的 |  representing or embodying an original model or type after which other similar things are patterned; very typical |  The hero's journey is an archetypal story found in many cultures. 英雄之旅是一个在许多文化中都能找到的原型故事。 |  classic, quintessential, exemplary | atypical, unorthodox | adjective

archetype:/ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/ | n. | 原型；典型 | an original model or type after which other similar things are patterned; a perfect example of something |  The character of the wise old mentor is a common archetype in literature.  智慧老人的角色是文学中常见的原型。 |  prototype, model, epitome |  anomaly, aberration | noun
