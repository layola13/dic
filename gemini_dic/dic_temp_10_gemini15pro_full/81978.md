grindingly:/ˈɡraɪndɪŋli/| adv.| 缓慢而费力地，令人难以忍受地|  in a way that is slow, difficult, and unpleasant | The days passed grindingly slowly.  日子过得缓慢而煎熬。 | 近义词:laboriously, tediously, excruciatingly | 反义词:easily, quickly, effortlessly | 词性:副词
词源: From grind (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grindaną.
