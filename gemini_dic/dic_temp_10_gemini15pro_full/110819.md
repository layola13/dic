linnets:/ˈlɪnɪts/| n. |红雀，亚麻雀（复数）|A small brown finch, *Carduelis cannabina*, with a red breast and forehead in the breeding male.  The plural form of linnet. |例句：A flock of linnets flew over the field. 一群红雀飞过田野。

例句：The linnets were singing in the bushes. 红雀在灌木丛中歌唱。


|近义词：finch, redpoll|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
