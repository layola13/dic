overplowed:/ˌoʊvərˈplaʊd/ | adj. |过度耕作的；过度犁过的| Excessively plowed, often to the point of damaging the soil.  This leads to soil erosion and depletion of nutrients.| The overplowed fields were barren and dusty.  过度耕作的田地荒芜而尘土飞扬。| Synonyms: overtilled, overcultivated | Antonyms: undercultivated, fallow | adjective

overplowed:/ˌoʊvərˈplaʊd/ | v. |过度耕作；过度犁地 | To plow excessively, often to the point of damaging the soil. | The farmer overplowed the land, leaving it vulnerable to erosion. 农民过度耕作土地，使其容易受到侵蚀。| Synonyms: overtill, overcultivate | Antonyms: undercultivate | verb
