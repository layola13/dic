coresearchers:/ˌkoʊˈriːsɜːrtʃərz/ /ˌkəʊˈriːsɜːtʃərz/| n. |共同研究者；合作研究人员|People who conduct research together on a project.  A group of scientists working collaboratively on a study are coresearchers.|例句：The coresearchers published their findings in a prestigious journal. 共同研究者们在一份著名的期刊上发表了他们的发现。|例句：The coresearchers met weekly to discuss their progress. 这些共同研究人员每周开会讨论他们的进展。|近义词：collaborators, colleagues, fellow researchers|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "independent researcher")|词性:noun
