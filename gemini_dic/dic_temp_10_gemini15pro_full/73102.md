foppish:/ˈfɒpɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːpɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|浮夸的；纨绔子气的；矫揉造作的|Characteristic of a fop; affecting extreme elegance in dress and manner, excessively vain and concerned with fashion.|He wore a foppish hat and a ridiculously long scarf. 他戴着一顶浮夸的帽子和一条滑稽的长围巾.|Synonyms: dandyish, affected, pretentious, ostentatious, vain|Antonyms: modest, unassuming, down-to-earth|adjective
