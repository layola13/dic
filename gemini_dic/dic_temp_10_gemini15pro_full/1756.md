acquires:/əˈkwaɪərz/| v.|获得，取得，学到|To come into possession or control of something; to obtain or learn something.|She acquired a taste for fine wines during her time in France.  她在法国期间培养了对葡萄酒的品味。

The company acquired several smaller businesses last year.  这家公司去年收购了几家小企业。|近义词：obtain, gain, learn, earn, achieve|反义词: lose, forfeit, relinquish|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acquerre (Modern French acquérir), from Latin acquīrere "to acquire, get in addition, accumulate, procure," from ad- "to" + quaerere "to seek, look for."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acquires knowledge quickly. = 他学东西很快。
She acquires new clients through networking. = 她通过人际关系获得新客户。

一般过去时:
They acquired the property last year. = 他们去年获得了这处房产。
He acquired a new skill during his training. = 他在培训期间学到了一项新技能。

现在完成时:
We have acquired a significant amount of data. = 我们已经获取了大量数据。
She has acquired a reputation for excellence. = 她已经赢得了卓越的声誉。

过去完成时:
By the time he was 20, he had acquired a fortune. = 到他20岁的时候，他已经获得了一笔财富。
They had acquired the necessary permits before starting construction. = 他们在开始施工之前已经获得了必要的许可证。

将来时:
The company will acquire new assets next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度获得新资产。
He will acquire the necessary qualifications through this course. = 他将通过这门课程获得必要的资格。

虚拟语气:
If I were to acquire a new car, I would choose an electric one. = 如果我要买一辆新车，我会选择电动汽车。
I wish I could acquire fluency in another language. = 我希望我能流利地使用另一种语言。

被动语态:
The company was acquired by a larger corporation. = 这家公司被一家更大的公司收购了。
New skills are acquired through practice and dedication. = 新技能是通过实践和奉献获得的。

疑问句:
How did he acquire such wealth? = 他是如何获得如此财富的？
Where did she acquire that painting? = 她在哪里获得那幅画的？

否定句:
They didn't acquire the necessary funding for the project. = 他们没有获得该项目所需的资金。
He hasn't acquired the skills needed for the job. = 他还没有获得这项工作所需的技能。
