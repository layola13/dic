inbreeding:/ˈɪnbrɪːdɪŋ/ | n. |近亲繁殖；同系交配 |The practice of breeding from closely related people or animals, especially over many generations.|Excessive inbreeding has caused many genetic problems in this dog breed. 过度的近亲繁殖在这个犬种中造成了很多遗传问题。|近义词：endogamy, interbreeding (within a small group) |反义词：outbreeding, exogamy| noun

inbreed:/ˈɪnbrɪːd/ | vt. |（使）近亲繁殖；使…气质根深蒂固 |To breed from closely related individuals.|The royal families inbred for generations.  皇室家族几代人近亲结婚。|近义词: interbreed (within a small group)|反义词: outbreed, crossbreed| verb
