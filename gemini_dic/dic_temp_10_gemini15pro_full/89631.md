hookable:/ˈhʊkəbl/| adj.|可钩住的；易上瘾的；容易被吸引的|Able to be hooked or fastened with a hook; (figuratively) addictive or easily engaging.|The design features a hookable strap for easy carrying.  这个设计有一个可钩住的带子，便于携带。|The game is so hookable, I've been playing it for hours.  这个游戏太容易上瘾了，我已经玩了几个小时了。|近义词：addictive, captivating, engaging, gripping|反义词：boring, tedious, uninteresting|词性:adjective
词源: From hook + -able.  来自 hook（钩子） + -able（可...的）。
