immobile:/ɪˈmoʊbəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|不动的，静止的，固定的|Unable to move or be moved.|例句：The patient was completely immobile after the accident.  病人事故后完全无法动弹。

例句：The immobile car blocked the entire street.  那辆无法移动的车挡住了整条街。

近义词：motionless, static, fixed, stationary|反义词：mobile, movable, active|词性:形容词


immobility:/ˌɪməˈbɪləti/ (US), /ˌɪməˈbɪlɪti/ (UK)| n.|不动，静止，固定|The state of being unable to move.|例句：The immobility of the statue made it seem almost lifelike.  雕像的静止使它看起来栩栩如生。

例句：His immobility after the surgery was a concern for the doctors.  他手术后无法动弹让医生很担心。


近义词：motionlessness, stillness|反义词：mobility, movement, activity|词性:名词
