derelict:/ˈdɛrəlɪkt/ /ˈdɪrəlɪkt/| n. adj.|被遗弃的人/物，玩忽职守的|Something or someone abandoned or neglected.  As an adjective, it describes something in a state of disrepair or ruin due to neglect.|例句：The derelict ship slowly rusted in the harbor. 那艘废弃的船在港口慢慢生锈。|近义词：abandoned, forsaken, dilapidated, ramshackle|反义词：maintained, cared-for, preserved|词性: noun, adjective

derelict:/ˈdɛrəlɪkt/ /ˈdɪrəlɪkt/| adj.|玩忽职守的，疏忽职守的|Failing in what duty requires.|例句：The derelict officer was dismissed from the force.  玩忽职守的警官被解雇了。|近义词：negligent, remiss, irresponsible|反义词：responsible, diligent, conscientious|词性: adjective
