bewrap:/bɪˈræp/| v.|包裹，覆盖；使入迷，使着迷|To wrap or cover something completely; to engross or enthrall someone.|The climbers were bewrapped in fog. 登山者被大雾笼罩。  He was bewrapped in his studies. 他专心致志于他的学习。|近义词：envelop, enshroud, immerse, absorb|反义词：uncover, reveal|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + wrap.  "Be-" is a prefix of Germanic origin often used to form transitive verbs. "Wrap" comes from the Old English word "wræppan," meaning to cover or enclose.
