immixable:/ɪˈmɪksəbl/
adjective
不可混合的
not capable of being mixed
Oil and water are immixable liquids. 油和水是不可混合的液体。
The two chemicals proved immixable, remaining separate layers in the beaker.  这两种化学物质被证明是不可混合的，在烧杯中保持着分离的层。
近义词: non-miscible, unmixable
反义词: miscible, mixable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *immixibilis, from Latin in- "not" + miscibilis "mixable," from miscere "to mix."
