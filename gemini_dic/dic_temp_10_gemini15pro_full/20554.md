black-out:/ˈblækaʊt/| v.|使断电；使一片漆黑；封锁消息|To cause a loss of electricity or light.|The government blacked out all news of the disaster. 政府封锁了所有关于这场灾难的消息。|近义词：suppress, censor|反义词:reveal, publicize|动词
