demasculinization:/diːˌmæskjʊlɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去男性化，男性气质丧失|The process of depriving a male of his masculinity or male role.  The act of making something less masculine or less traditionally associated with men.|  Example:  The demasculinization of men in media is a topic of much discussion. 媒体中男性去男性化是一个备受讨论的话题。

Example: Some argue that certain educational practices contribute to the demasculinization of boys.  一些人认为某些教育实践导致了男孩的去男性化。 | 近义词: effeminization, feminization | 反义词: masculinization | 词性: noun
