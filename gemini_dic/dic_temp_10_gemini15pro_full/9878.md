aparta:/aˈparta/ | adj. | 分开的，隔开的 | Apart from its practical uses, the building is also a work of art.  除了其实用价值外，这座建筑也是一件艺术品。|  The twins are so alike it's hard to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎长得太像了，很难区分他们。 | separate, distinct, aside | together, joined | adjective

Etymology->
From Spanish *aparta*, from *apartar* "to set aside," from Latin *ad* "to" + *pars* "part."
