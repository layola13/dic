exsiliency:/ɪkˈsɪliənsi/ /ekˈsɪliənsi/
n.
弹性; 恢复力; 快速恢复的能力
The ability to recover quickly from setbacks or misfortune.
The exsiliency of the economy is remarkable considering the global crisis. 考虑到全球危机，经济的恢复力非凡。
The exsiliency of youth allows them to bounce back from disappointment quickly. 年轻人的快速恢复能力使他们能够迅速从失望中恢复过来。
resilience, elasticity, buoyancy, flexibility
rigidity, inflexibility, fragility
