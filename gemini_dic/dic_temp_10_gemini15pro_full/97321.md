information's:/ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. |信息的，情报的 (罕用，通常用information)|  Possessive form of information, rarely used and generally considered incorrect.  It's better to use "of information" instead. | The information's source is unknown. (Generally avoid this usage) 信息的来源未知。 | 近义词: data, intelligence, facts | 反义词: disinformation, misinformation | 词性: 名词 (possessive)

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *informacioun*, from Old French *informacion*, from Latin *informatio*,  "formation, conception, idea" from the verb *informare*, "to shape, form, instruct". The suffix -'s is the English possessive marker.
