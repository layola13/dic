gnattiest:/ˈnætiɪst/
adjective
最爱挑剔的；最吹毛求疵的
Most annoyingly persistent; most irritatingly fault-finding.
例句：She's the gnattiest customer I've ever had. 她是我遇到过的最挑剔的顾客。

例句：He kept asking gnattiest questions about every little detail. 他不停地就每个小细节问一些吹毛求疵的问题。

近义词：picky, fussy, nitpicky, persnickety
反义词：easygoing, tolerant, undemanding
词性: adjective
