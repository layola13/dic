gloar:/ɡlɔr/| n.|凝视，注视|A fixed stare; a gaze.  The cat sat in the window, gloaring at the birds outside.|例句：He stood gloaring at me after I spilled his coffee. 他在我把他的咖啡洒了之后怒视着我。

例句：She gave him a gloar that could curdle milk. 她狠狠地瞪了他一眼，足以让牛奶凝固。|近义词：glare, stare, scowl|反义词:glance|词性:名词
