grooming:/ˈɡruːmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡruːmɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|打扮；修饰；梳洗；（动物的）清洁；准备；培训|To clean and care for one's appearance. To prepare someone for a special role or purpose. |He was grooming his beard. 他正在修剪他的胡须。|近义词：preening, primping, preparing, training|反义词：neglecting|词性: verb


词源: From Old English *grūma "man," related to Old Frisian grōma "boy, male child." Originally referred to a boy or servant who attended to horses. The meaning broadened to include the care of horses and then to personal care and preparation in general.
