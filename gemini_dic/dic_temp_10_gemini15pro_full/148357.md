overspeculativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌspɛkjəˈleɪtɪvnəs/
|n.|过度推测，过度臆测|The overspeculativeness of the market led to a crash. 市场过度投机导致了崩盘。|Overspeculativeness in the housing market caused prices to skyrocket. 房地产市场的过度投机导致价格飞涨。|近义词：conjecture, speculation, surmise|反义词：caution, prudence, restraint|词性: noun

Etymology:
From over- + speculativeness.  "Speculative" comes from Late Latin *speculativus, from Latin speculatus, past participle of speculari "to observe, watch, spy," from specula "watchtower," from specere "to look at."  "Over-" simply intensifies the meaning, indicating an excessive amount.
