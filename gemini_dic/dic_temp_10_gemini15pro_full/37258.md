comics: /ˈkɑːmɪks/ (US), /ˈkɒmɪks/ (UK) | n. | 漫画；连环画 |  A form of entertainment and storytelling through sequential art, often combining words and images. Typically published as magazines, books, or online. | I enjoy reading comics in my spare time. 我喜欢在空闲时间看漫画。| synonyms: comic books, graphic novels, cartoons | antonyms: novels, textbooks | noun
