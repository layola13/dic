paramagnetic:/ˌpærəˌmæɡˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
顺磁性的
Having a small positive susceptibility to an external magnetic field. Paramagnetic materials are slightly attracted by a magnetic field and do not retain the magnetic properties when the external field is removed.
例句：
Oxygen is a paramagnetic substance.
氧气是一种顺磁性物质。

Aluminum is a paramagnetic metal.
铝是一种顺磁性金属。

近义词：
ferromagnetic (only weakly in comparison)

反义词：
diamagnetic
