errands:/ˈɛrəndz/
n.
差事，任务
short trips that you take to do something, such as buying or delivering things
例句：I have to run some errands this afternoon.  我今天下午要跑几趟差事。

例句：She asked her son to do a few errands for her. 她叫她儿子帮她做几件事。

近义词：tasks, chores, jobs
反义词: leisure, relaxation
词性: noun
