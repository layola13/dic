mystifiers:/ˈmɪstɪfaɪərz/
noun
迷惑者，神秘化的人
People who cause confusion or bewilderment.
The mystifiers behind the elaborate hoax were never caught.  那些精心策划骗局背后的迷惑者从未被抓到。
Synonyms:  puzzlers, confounders, bewilderers
Antonyms: clarifiers, explainers
n.
