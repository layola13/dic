cuffer:/ˈkʌfər/| n.|手铐；(尤指)戴手铐的人；袖口；能手；内行；<非正式>耳光；一巴掌|A metal ring that locks around a person's wrist, usually used in pairs by police to prevent someone from escaping; (especially) a person wearing handcuffs; the end of a sleeve; someone who is skilled or expert at something; a blow with the open hand, especially on the side of the head.|例句：The police officer placed the cuffer in the back of the patrol car. 警察把戴手铐的人放在巡逻车的后座。

例句：The cuffs of his shirt were frayed. 他的衬衫袖口磨损了。|近义词：handcuffs, manacles (for the restraint device); expert, professional (for a skilled person); slap, blow (for a hit)|反义词:amateur (for a skilled person)|词性:名词
