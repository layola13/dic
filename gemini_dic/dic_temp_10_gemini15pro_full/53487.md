disarray:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪ/| n. vt.|混乱，紊乱；使混乱，使紊乱|A state of disorganization or untidiness.|The room was in complete disarray after the party. 房间在聚会后一片狼藉。|Synonyms: disorder, confusion, chaos, mess|Antonyms: order, tidiness, organization|noun
disarray:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪ/| vt.|使混乱，使紊乱|To throw into disorder or confusion.|The sudden storm disarrayed the garden. 突如其来的暴风雨把花园弄得乱七八糟。|Synonyms: disorganize, disorder, confuse, muddle|Antonyms: organize, order, arrange|verb
