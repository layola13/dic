helmless:/ˈhɛlm.ləs/| adj.|无助的，没有方向的，没有领导的|Lacking a helm; without guidance or leadership; defenseless.|The helmless ship drifted aimlessly in the storm.  这艘无舵的船在暴风雨中漫无目的地漂流。|Example: After the captain died, the crew felt helmless and lost at sea. 船长死后，船员们感到茫然无措，在海上迷失了方向。|Synonyms: rudderless, adrift, leaderless, unguided, lost|Antonyms: guided, directed, led, controlled|Word type: adjective
