floweriness:/ˈflaʊərɪnɪs/| n. |华丽的词藻；辞藻华丽|The floweriness of his prose sometimes obscured its meaning. 他华丽的散文有时掩盖了它的含义。|例句：The speech was criticized for its excessive floweriness.  这篇演讲因其过分华丽的辞藻而受到批评。

例句：She disliked the floweriness of his writing style, preferring plain and simple language. 她不喜欢他写作风格的华丽辞藻，更喜欢简洁明了的语言。|近义词：ornateness, elaborateness, flamboyance|反义词：simplicity, plainness|词性:名词
