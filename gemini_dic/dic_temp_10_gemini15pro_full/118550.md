mesonephros:/ˈmɛzoʊˌnɛfrɒs/ | n. | 中肾 | The mesonephros is a type of kidney that functions in vertebrate embryos and in some adult fishes and amphibians.  It is the intermediate stage in the development of the kidney between the pronephros and the metanephros. | The mesonephros plays a vital role in the development of the urinary system. 中肾在泌尿系统发育中起着至关重要的作用。 | The mesonephric duct develops into parts of the male reproductive system. 中肾管发育成为男性生殖系统的一部分。 | synonyms: Wolffian body | antonyms:  None | noun
