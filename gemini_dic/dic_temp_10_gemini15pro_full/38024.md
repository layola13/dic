complyingly:/kəmˈplaɪɪŋli/
adverb|顺从地，服从地|In a way that obeys rules or requests.|She complyingly handed over her phone to the officer. 她顺从地把手机交给了警察。|例句：He complyingly agreed to her request. 他顺从地同意了她的请求。|近义词：obediently, submissively, meekly|反义词：defiantly, rebelliously|词性:副词
词源: From comply + -ing + -ly. Comply comes from Old French complir, from Latin complēre, meaning "to fulfill."
