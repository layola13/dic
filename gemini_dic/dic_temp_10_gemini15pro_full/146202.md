overexpansiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænsɪvnəs/
noun
过度扩张；过度膨胀
The state of being excessively expansive, especially in one's plans, ideas, or ambitions.
His overexpansiveness led him to invest in several risky ventures. 他的过度扩张导致他投资了几个风险很大的企业。
The company's overexpansiveness ultimately led to its downfall.  这家公司的过度扩张最终导致了它的垮台。

近义词:  excessiveness, extravagance, immoderation
反义词:  restraint, moderation, caution
词性: noun
