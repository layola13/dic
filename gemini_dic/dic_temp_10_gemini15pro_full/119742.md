midwife:/ˈmɪdˌwaɪf/ (US) /ˈmɪdwʌɪf/ (UK)| n.|助产士，接生婆|A person, typically a woman, who is trained to assist women during childbirth.|例句：The midwife helped deliver the baby safely. 助产士帮助安全地接生了婴儿。

例句：She decided to become a midwife after witnessing the miracle of birth. 在目睹了生育的奇迹后，她决定成为一名助产士。

近义词：nurse-midwife, birth attendant, accoucheur|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps obstetrician as a contrasting medical professional)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *midwif, from mid "with" + wif "woman".  Literally, "with woman".
