nonmotivated:/nɒnˈməʊtɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 没有动力的，缺乏积极性的 | Lacking motivation; not inspired or driven to action. |  He seemed nonmotivated and disengaged in class. 他在课堂上显得没有动力，心不在焉。|  The nonmotivated employees were eventually let go.  那些缺乏积极性的员工最终被解雇了。| 近义词: unmotivated, apathetic, listless, lethargic | 反义词: motivated, driven, enthusiastic, ambitious | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + motivated (adjective)

"non-" is a Latin prefix meaning "not."
"motivated" comes from the Latin word "motivus," meaning "moving," and the suffix "-ed," which forms adjectives.
