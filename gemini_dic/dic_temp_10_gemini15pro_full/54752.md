dispassionate:/dɪsˈpæʃənət/ | adj. | 冷静的，不带感情的，客观的 | Not influenced by strong emotion, and so able to be rational and impartial. |  The judge delivered a dispassionate summation of the evidence. 法官以冷静客观的口吻总结了证据。

She listened to his outburst with dispassionate calm. 她心平气和地听着他的咆哮。

| 近义词: unemotional, objective, impartial, detached, calm, cool, collected | 反义词: passionate, emotional, biased, prejudiced | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + passionate (having, showing, or caused by strong feelings or beliefs).  Ultimately from Latin passio (“suffering, enduring”), from patior (“I suffer, endure”).
