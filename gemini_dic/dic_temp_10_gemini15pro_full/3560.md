agamogenetic:/ˌeɪɡæmoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
无性生殖的
Relating to, produced by, or exhibiting agamogenesis.  Reproduction without the fusion of gametes.
例句：Agamogenetic reproduction is common in some plants and invertebrates.  无性生殖在某些植物和无脊椎动物中很常见。

例句：The agamogenetic propagation of this species allows it to spread rapidly.  这个物种的无性繁殖使其能够迅速传播。
synonym: asexual, vegetative
antonym: sexual, gamogenetic
词性: adjective
