exasperating: /ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪŋ/ /ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人恼怒的，使人烦躁的 | intensely annoying; very frustrating |  The exasperating delay made me miss my flight.  令人恼火的延误使我错过了航班。 | synonyms: annoying, irritating, frustrating, infuriating | antonyms: soothing, calming, pleasing, delightful | adjective

exasperate: /ɪɡˈzæspəreɪt/ /ɪɡˈzæspəreɪt/ | v. | 激怒，使恼怒 | irritate intensely; infuriate | His constant complaining exasperated me. 他不停的抱怨激怒了我。 | synonyms: annoy, irritate, provoke, infuriate | antonyms: soothe, calm, please, delight | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exasperatus, past participle of exasperare ("to roughen, irritate, provoke"), from ex- ("out, utterly") + asperare ("to make rough"), from asper ("rough, harsh").
