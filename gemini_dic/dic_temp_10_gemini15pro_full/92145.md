hypersensitizes:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪz/
verb
使…过度敏感；使…过敏
To make excessively sensitive.  To increase the sensitivity of something or someone.
The detergent hypersensitized her skin.  这种洗涤剂使她的皮肤过敏。
近义词:sensitize, aggravate
反义词:desensitize
词性:v.


hypersensitive:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/
adjective
过度敏感的；过敏的
Excessively sensitive; abnormally susceptible to something.
He's hypersensitive to criticism. 他对批评过度敏感。
近义词:sensitive, allergic
反义词:insensitive
词性:adj.


hypersensitivity:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/
noun
过度敏感；过敏性
Excessive sensitivity.
He suffers from hypersensitivity to sunlight. 他患有阳光过敏症。
近义词:sensitivity, allergy
反义词:insensitivity
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (excessive) + sensitive + -ize (to make).  The prefix "hyper-" comes from Greek meaning "over, beyond." "Sensitive" comes from Latin "sensus" meaning "feeling, sense."
