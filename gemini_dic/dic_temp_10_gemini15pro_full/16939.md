basset:/ˈbæsɪt/| n.|巴吉度猎犬; 短腿猎犬|A short-legged hound with long ears, a wrinkled face, and a keen sense of smell, originally bred in France for hunting small game.|The basset hound followed the scent with its nose to the ground.|近义词：hound, dog|反义词: -(not applicable to animal breeds)|Noun


basset:/ˈbæsɪt/| adj.|短腿的; 矮的|Having short legs; low to the ground.|The basset furniture was perfect for the small apartment.|近义词：short-legged, low-slung|反义词:tall, high|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *basset*, meaning "rather low", from *bas*, meaning "low". Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *bassus*.
