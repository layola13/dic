archeologically:/ˌɑːrkiəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 考古学地；从考古学的角度 |  In a way that relates to the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains. | The site was archeologically significant, revealing evidence of a previously unknown civilization.  这个遗址具有重要的考古学意义，揭示了一个以前不为人知的文明的证据。| Near Synonyms: archaeologically speaking, from an archaeological standpoint | Antonyms: non-archaeologically | word type: adverb
