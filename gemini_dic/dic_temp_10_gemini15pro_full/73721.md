fork-out:/ˈfɔːrk aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 付出(一大笔钱); 掏出(钱) | to pay a sum of money, especially reluctantly |  I had to fork out £500 for the repairs. 我不得不掏出500英镑来修理。|  shell out, cough up | - | verb phrase

fork-out:/ˈfɔːrk aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 提供(尤指不情愿地) | to provide something, especially reluctantly |  The company had to fork out for a new computer system. 公司不得不掏钱买一套新的计算机系统。|  provide, supply | withhold, refuse | verb phrase
