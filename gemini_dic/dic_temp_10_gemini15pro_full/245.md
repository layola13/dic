abdom:/ˈæbdəm/| n.|腹部，肚子|The doctor examined her abdom. 医生检查了她的腹部。|例句：He felt a sharp pain in his abdom. 他感到腹部一阵剧痛。|近义词：belly, stomach|反义词:back|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abdomen "belly, paunch."

USAGE->
医学描述:
The patient complained of pain in the lower abdom. = 病人抱怨下腹部疼痛。
The surgeon made an incision in the abdom. = 外科医生在腹部做了一个切口。

解剖学描述:
The abdom contains many vital organs. = 腹部包含许多重要器官。
The muscles of the abdom are important for core strength.  = 腹部的肌肉对核心力量很重要。

日常用语 (较为正式，不如belly或stomach常用):
He had a large abdom. = 他肚子很大。
She rubbed her abdom after a big meal. = 她吃完大餐后揉了揉肚子。

PHRASE->
abdominal pain = 腹痛
abdominal muscles = 腹肌
abdominal cavity = 腹腔
distended abdom = 腹部胀大
abdominal surgery = 腹部手术


注意事项:

*  abdom是abdomen的缩写，在医学或科技文献中更为常见。在日常生活中，人们更倾向于使用belly或stomach。
