corybantic:/ˌkɔːrɪˈbæntik/| adj.|狂热的，狂乱的，喧闹的|Characterized by or resembling the frenzied, ecstatic rites of the Corybantes, devotees of the Phrygian goddess Cybele.  Marked by unrestrained enthusiasm or wild excitement.|The music inspired a corybantic dance.  这音乐激起了狂热的舞蹈。|Synonyms: frenzied, ecstatic, wild, unrestrained, boisterous, exuberant, frantic|Antonyms: calm, peaceful, quiet, subdued, restrained|adjective

corybantic:/ˌkɔːrɪˈbæntik/| n.|狂热的舞蹈或行为|A wild, frenzied dance or behavior.|The corybantic at the concert was contagious.  音乐会上的狂热气氛很有感染力。|Synonyms: frenzy, ecstasy, revelry, bacchanalia|Antonyms: peace, quiet, tranquility|noun
