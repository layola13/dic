elastical:/ɪˈlæstɪkəl/| adj.|弹性的，有弹力的|Having the property of elasticity; springy; rebounding.|The elastical band snapped back into place.  这根橡皮筋弹回了原位。|近义词：elastic, resilient, flexible, springy|反义词:rigid, inflexible, stiff|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *elasticus, from Greek elastikos ‘propelling’, from elaunein ‘to drive, beat out’.
