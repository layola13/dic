bellybound:/ˈbeliˌbaʊnd/
adjective
裹腹的，肚子被束缚的
tightly bound or constricted around the belly.  This term often describes a feeling of tightness or restriction in the abdomen, sometimes due to clothing, or a medical condition.
She felt bellybound after eating a large meal. 她吃完一顿大餐后感到肚子被束缚。
constricted, tight, restrained
loose, free
