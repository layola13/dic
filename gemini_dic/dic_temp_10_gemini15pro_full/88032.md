highborn:/ˈhaɪbɔːrn/| adj.|出身高贵的，贵族出身的|Born into a family of high social rank; of noble birth.|She was a highborn lady, accustomed to a life of luxury. 她是一位出身高贵的女士，习惯了奢华的生活。|例句：The highborn prince was known for his generosity.  这位出身高贵的王子以慷慨大方而闻名。

例句：Highborn and wealthy, she had little experience of the hardships of ordinary life. 出身高贵且富有，她几乎没有经历过普通生活的艰辛。|近义词：noble, aristocratic, blue-blooded|反义词：lowborn, common, plebeian|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hihborn, equivalent to high + born.  Ultimately from Old English hēahgeboren (highborn)
