grooviness:/ˈɡruːvɪnəs/| n. |时髦，酷，绝妙|The state or quality of being groovy; fashionable, stylish, excellent.|Her outfit had a certain grooviness to it. 她的穿着有一种独特的时髦感。|近义词：coolness, hipness, trendiness, stylishness|反义词:squareness, uncoolness, lameness|词性:名词

词源:
From groovy + -ness.  "Groovy" originated in the 1960s slang, likely influenced by the "groove" of a vinyl record, implying something that fits perfectly and is therefore pleasing or cool.  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "groovy" into the noun "grooviness," indicating the state or quality of being groovy.
