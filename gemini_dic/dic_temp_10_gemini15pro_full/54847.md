displacer:/dɪˈspleɪsər/ | n. | 置换器; 排水器; 位移器 | something that displaces another thing | The piston acts as a displacer, moving the hydraulic fluid.  活塞充当置换器，移动液压油。| synonyms:  supplanter,  replacer | antonyms: retainer, keeper | noun


displace:/dɪˈspleɪs/ | v. | 取代; 移置; 驱逐 | to force something or someone out of its usual or original position | The rising floodwaters displaced thousands of people.  上涨的洪水使数千人流离失所。| synonyms: oust, remove, relocate | antonyms:  retain, keep | verb
