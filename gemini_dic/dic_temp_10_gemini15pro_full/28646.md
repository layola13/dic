caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ (US), /ˈkærɪbuː/ (UK)| n.|驯鹿|A large North American deer (Rangifer tarandus) with large antlers in both sexes, found in the arctic and subarctic regions.|例句：The caribou migrated south for the winter. 驯鹿向南迁徙过冬。


例句：Caribou are an important food source for many indigenous peoples. 驯鹿是许多土著居民的重要食物来源。

近义词：reindeer|反义词:|词性:noun
