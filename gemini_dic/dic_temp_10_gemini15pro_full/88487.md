historicisms:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəmz/
n.
历史主义；历史相对主义；过时的词语
An interpretation of the past in terms of its own values and beliefs, rather than in terms of present-day standards.  The tendency to judge past people and events by the standards of one's own time. Obsolete words or expressions that are no longer in common use.
The book is full of annoying historicisms. 这本书里充满了烦人的过时词语。
His approach to history is marked by strong historicisms. 他研究历史的方法带有强烈的历史主义色彩。
synonym: anachronism, archaism
antonym: modernism, presentism
noun

historicism:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəm/
n.
历史主义；历史相对主义；过时的词语
An interpretation of the past in terms of its own values and beliefs, rather than in terms of present-day standards.  The tendency to judge past people and events by the standards of one's own time. Obsolete words or expressions that are no longer in common use.
Using historicism, we can understand why people in the past acted the way they did.  通过历史主义，我们可以理解为什么过去的人们会那样做。
The author's historicism prevents him from making objective judgments about the past. 作者的历史主义使他无法对过去做出客观的判断。
synonym: anachronism, archaism
antonym: modernism, presentism
noun
