microculture:/ˈmaɪkroʊkʌltʃər/| n. |微型文化；亚文化|A small, distinct group within a larger culture, having its own particular values, beliefs, and customs.|例句：The company fostered a microculture of innovation within its research and development department.  这家公司在其研发部门内培养了一种创新微型文化。

例句：The student dormitory developed its own unique microculture. 学生宿舍发展出了自己独特的微型文化。|近义词：subculture, subgroup, niche culture|反义词:macroculture, mainstream culture|词性:noun
