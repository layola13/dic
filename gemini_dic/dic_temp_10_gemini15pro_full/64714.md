eschatologies: /ˌɛskətɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 末世论；来世论 |  The study of the end times, including beliefs about death, judgment, heaven, and hell.  It often encompasses various religious or philosophical perspectives on the ultimate destiny of humanity and the universe. | Many religions have their own eschatologies, offering different perspectives on the afterlife. 许多宗教都有自己的末世论，对来世有着不同的看法。

The book explores various eschatologies, from ancient prophecies to modern apocalyptic scenarios. 这本书探讨了各种末世论，从古代预言到现代的末日情景。| 近义词: end-time theories, apocalypticism | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun (plural)

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἐσχατολογία (eschatología), from ἔσχατος (éskhatos, “last, furthest, utmost, extreme”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
