employed:/ɪmˈplɔɪd/ | adj. | 受雇的；有工作的 | Having a job; working for someone |  She is currently employed as a teacher. 她目前受雇为教师。| 近义词: working, hired, occupied | 反义词: unemployed, jobless, idle | 词性:形容词

employed:/ɪmˈplɔɪd/ | v. (employ的过去式和过去分词) | 雇用；使用；运用 |  Give work to (someone) and pay them for it; make use of | The company employs over 500 people. 这家公司雇佣了500多人。| 近义词: used, utilized, applied | 反义词: dismissed, fired, laid off | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emploier "make use of, occupy (one's time)," from Latin implicare "to involve, entangle," from in- "in" + plicare "to fold"
