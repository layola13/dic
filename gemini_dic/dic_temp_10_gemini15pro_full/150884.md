paralalia:/ˌpærəˈleɪliə/ /ˌpærəˈleɪljə/| n.| 并语症，复述症|A speech disorder characterized by the involuntary repetition of words, phrases, or sentences.|The patient exhibited paralalia, repeating the doctor's questions instead of answering them. 病人表现出并语症，重复医生的问题而不是回答问题。|例句：His paralalia made conversation difficult. 他的并语症使对话变得困难。

例句：The therapist worked with the patient to manage his paralalia.  治疗师与病人一起努力控制他的并语症。|近义词：echolalia, palilalia|反义词:|noun
