dispensaries:/dɪˈspɛnsəriːz/
n.
药房，诊疗所
a place where medicine or medical treatment is dispensed
Many people rely on local dispensaries for affordable healthcare. 许多人依靠当地的诊疗所获得负担得起的医疗保健。
The dispensary provides free vaccinations for children. 这家诊疗所为儿童提供免费疫苗接种。
clinics, pharmacies, health centers
hospitals (sometimes)
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *dispensarie, from Anglo-Norman and Old French dispensarie, from Medieval Latin dispensāria, neuter plural of dispensārius (“dispensing, distributive”), from Latin dispensō (“I dispense, distribute”).
