frenziedly:/ˈfrɛnziːdli/ | adv. | 狂暴地，疯狂地 | In a frenzied and chaotic manner; with extreme agitation or wild excitement. |  He worked frenziedly to finish the project before the deadline. 他疯狂地工作，以便在截止日期前完成项目。

The shoppers grabbed the discounted items frenziedly. 购物者疯狂地抢购打折商品。

| 近义词: frantically, wildly, hysterically, maniacally | 反义词: calmly, peacefully, quietly, serenely | 词性: 副词
