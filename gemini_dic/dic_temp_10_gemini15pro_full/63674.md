epaulet: /ˈɛpəlɛt/ (US), /ˈɛpɔːlɛt/ (UK) | n. | 肩章，肩饰 | An ornamental shoulder piece on an item of clothing, typically on the coat or jacket of a military uniform. | The general's epaulets glittered in the sunlight. 将军的肩章在阳光下闪闪发光。|  The captain adjusted his epaulets before addressing the troops. 上尉在向部队讲话前调整了他的肩章。 | Synonyms: shoulder piece, shoulder strap, shoulder board | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
