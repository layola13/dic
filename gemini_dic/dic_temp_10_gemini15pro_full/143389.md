outfigured: /ˌaʊtˈfɪɡərd/
verb
计算得胜过；智胜
surpass or defeat in calculating or planning; outwit
He outfigured his opponents in the negotiations and secured a favorable deal. 他在谈判中智胜了对手，获得了一项有利的协议。
outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
outdone, outsmarted

verb
想出，理解(通常指困难的事)
to work out, understand (usually something difficult)
She finally outfigured the complex math problem. 她最终解出了这道复杂的数学题。
decipher, solve, understand
misunderstood, miscalculated

词性: v.
