myna:/ˈmaɪnə/ | n. | 八哥；椋鸟 | A bird of the starling family, especially of tropical Asia, some of which can mimic human speech. | 例句1：The myna mimicked the shopkeeper's voice perfectly. 八哥完美地模仿了店主的聲音。
