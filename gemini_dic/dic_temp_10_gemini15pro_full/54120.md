disenchanted:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃæntɪd/ | adj. | 失望的，不再抱有幻想的 |  No longer feeling enthusiasm or enchantment; disillusioned.  Derived from "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") and "enchanted" (meaning "filled with delight and wonder").  |  After working in the industry for a few years, she became disenchanted with the corporate world.  在行业工作了几年后，她对企业界不再抱有幻想。 | synonyms: disillusioned, disappointed, jaded, cynical | antonyms: enchanted, charmed, delighted, enthusiastic | word type: adjective
