hercynian:/hɜːˈsɪniən/
adjective
海西期的;海西山的
of or relating to a mountain-building period of the late Paleozoic Era, about 290 to 225 million years ago, during which mountains were formed in Europe, North Africa, and eastern North America.  Also, of or relating to the mountains formed in this period.
The Hercynian orogeny was a period of intense mountain building. 海西造山运动是一个剧烈的造山时期。
Hercynian mountains are found across Europe. 海西山脉遍布欧洲。
Synonyms: Variscan
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
