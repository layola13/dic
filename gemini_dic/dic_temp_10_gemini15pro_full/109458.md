lepidolite:/ləˈpɪdəˌlaɪt/| n. |锂云母|A lilac-gray or rose-colored mica containing lithium.  A relatively common source of lithium.|Lepidolite is often found in pegmatites. 锂云母常产于伟晶岩中。|Synonym: lithia mica, |Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *lepis* (scale) + *lithos* (stone), referring to its scaly appearance.
