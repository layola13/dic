gaudiness's:/ˈɡɔːdinəsɪz/
noun
俗丽；华而不实；炫耀
The quality of being gaudy; excessive showiness or ornamentation.
The gaudiness's of the decorations was overwhelming.  这装饰的俗丽让人难以忍受。
garishness, flashiness, tawdriness
simplicity, elegance, understatement
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From gaudy + -ness.  Gaudy itself comes from the Anglo-French gaudi, meaning "rejoicing, extravagant festivity," ultimately from Latin gaudere "to rejoice."
