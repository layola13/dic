liberalises:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使自由化，放宽管制
To make something less strict or controlled, often relating to laws, regulations, or social attitudes.
The government has liberalized trade regulations. 政府放宽了贸易管制。
liberalize, loosen, relax, ease
restrict, tighten, control
v.
