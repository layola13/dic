extinct:/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/
adjective
灭绝的，绝种的
(of a species, family, or other larger group) having no living members.
例句：The dodo is an extinct bird. 渡渡鸟是一种已灭绝的鸟类。

例句：Many languages once spoken in the region are now extinct. 该地区曾经使用的许多语言现在已经灭绝了。

近义词：exterminated, vanished, defunct, obsolete
反义词：extant, living, surviving
词性：adjective
