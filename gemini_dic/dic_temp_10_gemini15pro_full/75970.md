funkyass:/ˈfʌŋkiˌæs/ | adj. | 放克风格的，另类的，非常酷的 |  A slang term used to describe something as cool, stylish, or unconventional, often with a retro or vintage feel.  It combines "funky" (stylish and unconventional) with "ass," which intensifies the meaning.  It can also be used to describe someone as attractive in a unique or unconventional way. | That funkyass vintage car is so cool! 那辆酷毙了的复古车太棒了！| 近义词：cool, stylish, groovy, hip, rad | 反义词：boring, conventional, dull, unstylish | 词性: 形容词
