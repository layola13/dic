detractious:/dɪˈtrækʃəs/
adjective
贬低的，诽谤的，诋毁的
Tending to detract; disparaging; defamatory.  Given to making detracting, unfair, or belittling remarks about someone or something.
The detractious comments made by the rival candidate were seen as a sign of desperation.  竞争对手发表的贬低性评论被视为绝望的迹象。
