nonaudible:/ˌnɒnˈɔːdəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈɔːdəbl/| adj.|听不见的，不可闻的|Not able to be heard.|例句：The dog whistle emits a nonaudible frequency to humans.  这种狗哨发出的是人类听不见的频率。

例句：His whispers were nonaudible over the loud music.  在嘈杂的音乐声中，他的低语听不见。|近义词：inaudible, silent, unheard|反义词:audible, perceptible, hearable|词性:形容词
词源:  non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + audible (from Latin audibilis, "able to be heard," from audire, "to hear").
