eat-like-a-bird:/iːt laɪk ə bɜːrd/
idiom
吃得像小鸟一样（指吃得很少）
To eat a very small amount of food.
She eats like a bird – that's why she's so thin. 她吃得很少 - 这就是她这么瘦的原因。
近义词: have a small appetite, peck at one's food
反义词: eat like a horse, have a large appetite
词性: idiom (idiomatic phrase)


eat-like-a-bird:/iːt laɪk ə bɜːrd/
verb phrase
吃得像小鸟一样
To consume food in small quantities, similar to the way a bird pecks at food.
He's been eating like a bird ever since he got sick. 他自从生病以来就吃得很少。
近义词: nibble, pick at
反义词: gorge, devour
词性: verb phrase
