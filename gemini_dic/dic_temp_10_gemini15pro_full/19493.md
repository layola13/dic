bidental:/baɪˈdɛntl/ | adj.|双齿音的；用两齿发音的|Pronounced by placing the tongue tip between the upper and lower teeth.|The sound /θ/ as in "thin" is a bidental consonant.|近义词: interdental|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider providing contrasting articulation points like velar, labial, etc.)|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *bi* (two) + *dentalis* (of the teeth)
