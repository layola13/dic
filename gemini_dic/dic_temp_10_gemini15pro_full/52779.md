dilemma:/dɪˈlemə/ /daɪˈlemə/| n.|困境，进退两难|A situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones.|She faced a dilemma: should she tell the truth and risk her job, or lie and keep her position? 她面临着一个困境：是说出真相并冒着丢掉工作的风险，还是撒谎并保住自己的职位？

He was in a dilemma about whether to go to the party or stay home and study. 他左右为难，不知道是去参加聚会还是待在家里学习。

|近义词：predicament, quandary, problem, difficulty|反义词:solution, advantage, benefit|noun
