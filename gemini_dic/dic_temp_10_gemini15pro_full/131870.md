nonconsumptiveness:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmptɪvnəs/ | n. |非消耗性，非消费 |The nonconsumptiveness of knowledge means that one person's use of it doesn't diminish another's.  知识的非消耗性意味着一个人对知识的使用不会减少另一个人对它的使用。|  Nonconsumptiveness is a key characteristic of public goods. 非消耗性是公共物品的一个关键特征。|近义词: inexhaustibility, nonrivalrousness |反义词: consumability, rivalrousness | 词性: noun
