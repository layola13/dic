gusto:/ˈɡʌstoʊ/| n.|热情，兴致，滋味|Enthusiasm and enjoyment in doing something.|She sang with gusto. 她充满热情地歌唱。|近义词：relish, zest, enthusiasm, enjoyment, fervor, passion|反义词：apathy, indifference, reluctance|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *gusto* "taste, liking," from Latin *gustus* "a tasting, taste, flavor," related to *gustare* "to taste."
