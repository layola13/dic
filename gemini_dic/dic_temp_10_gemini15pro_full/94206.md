imperfectible:/ˌɪmpərˈfɛktɪbl/ | adj. | 不完美的，有缺陷的，无法臻于完美的 | Not capable of being made perfect or complete; necessarily flawed. |  Human beings are imperfect and therefore imperfectible. 人类是不完美的，因此也是无法臻于完美的。| The system, though complex, is not imperfectible. 这个系统虽然复杂，但并非无法改进。| 近义词: flawed, fallible, imperfect | 反义词: perfect, flawless, impeccable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imperfectibilis, from in- "not" + perfectibilis "perfectible," from perfectus, past participle of perficere "to complete, finish."
