moisteners:/ˈmɔɪsənərz/
noun
保湿剂
Substances that help retain or restore moisture.
Apply the moisteners to your face after washing.  洗完脸后，将保湿剂涂在脸上。
synonyms:  humectants, emollients, hydrators
antonyms:  drying agents, desiccants
n.
