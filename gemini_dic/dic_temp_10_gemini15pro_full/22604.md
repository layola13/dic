boottree:/ˈbuːttriː/
n.
boot tree; shoe tree
A device, typically made of wood or plastic, inserted into a boot or shoe to help it maintain its shape and prevent creasing.
The boot trees helped his expensive leather boots keep their shape.  鞋撑帮助他昂贵的皮靴保持形状。
synonyms: shoe tree, boot last, shoe form
antonyms: N/A
