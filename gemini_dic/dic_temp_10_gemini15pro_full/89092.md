holt:/həʊlt/| n. |树林，小树林; 隐蔽处; (獾等动物的)洞穴，窝|A small wood or thicket; a hiding place; the burrow or den of an animal, such as a badger.|The fox disappeared into its holt in the woods. 狐狸消失在树林里的洞穴中。|近义词：wood, copse, thicket, den, burrow|反义词: plain, field |noun
