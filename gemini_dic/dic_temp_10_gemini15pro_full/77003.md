gardyloo:/ɡɑːrdiˈluː/
interj.
小心泼水！；倒污水啦！
A warning cry formerly used in Scotland to alert passersby that wastewater was about to be thrown from a window.
Gardyloo! (A splash is heard.)  小心泼水！（听到泼水声。）
Gardyloo! I'm emptying the chamber pot. 倒污水啦！我要倒夜壶了。
近义词:Heads up!, Look out below!
反义词:None.
词性:Interjection
