formalizable:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzəbl/ /ˈfɔːrmələzəbl/
adjective
可形式化的
Capable of being formalized; able to be expressed in a precise, systematic, or rigorous way, often using mathematical or logical symbols.
The concept of justice is difficult to formalize.  正义的概念很难形式化。
His intuitive understanding of the problem was later formalized into a theorem. 他对这个问题的直观理解后来被形式化为一个定理。
近义词:systematizable, definable, expressible
反义词:unformalizable, vague, ambiguous
词性:adjective
