bourses:/ˈbʊərsɪz/ /ˈbʊərsəz/ | n. | 奖学金；助学金；交易所 | A grant of financial aid awarded to a student for further education; a stock exchange. |  The student received several bourses to help fund her studies.  这个学生获得了多项奖学金来资助她的学习。

The Paris Bourse is one of the oldest stock exchanges in the world. 巴黎证券交易所是世界上最古老的证券交易所之一。 | 近义词：scholarships, grants, fellowships, stock market, exchange | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 loan 贷款) | 词性: 名词
