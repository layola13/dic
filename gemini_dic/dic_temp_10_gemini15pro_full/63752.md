epicenisms:/ɪˈpɪsɪnɪzəmz/
|n.|通性词;  无性词|A word that has only one form for both masculine and feminine genders.|The word "author" is an epicene, as it can refer to both male and female writers.|Synonyms: gender-neutral terms, unisex words|Antonyms: gender-specific terms|Noun
