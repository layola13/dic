gesticulatingly:/dʒɛsˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪŋli/
| adv. |做着手势地，以手势表达地|In a manner characterized by expressive hand gestures.  She spoke gesticulatingly, her hands flying around as she tried to explain the complex concept. 她做着手势地说着，她的双手在她试图解释这个复杂的概念时四处飞舞。|例句：He described the scene gesticulatingly, waving his arms to mimic the action. 他绘声绘色地描述了那个场景，挥舞着手臂模仿动作。

例句：The conductor gesticulatingly directed the orchestra with passion and precision.  指挥家充满激情和精准地做着手势指挥乐队。|近义词：animatedly, expressively, emphatically|反义词：calmly, quietly, impassively|词性:副词

词源: From the verb "gesticulate" + "-ing" + "-ly." "Gesticulate" comes from the Latin "gesticulārī," frequentative of "gesticulāre," meaning "to make gestures," from "gestus," meaning "gesture," from "gerere," meaning "to bear, carry, perform."
