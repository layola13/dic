inscriptional:/ɪnˈskrɪpʃənl/
adjective
铭文的，刻写的
relating to or consisting of an inscription
The inscriptional evidence supports the theory. 铭文证据支持了这一理论。
近义词: engraved, inscribed
反义词: unwritten, blank
词性: adjective
