gleba:/ˈɡliːbə/| n. |孢体，菌髓|The fertile, spore-bearing inner mass of certain fungi, such as puffballs and stinkhorns.  某些真菌（如马勃菌和鬼笔菌）内部肥沃的、带有孢子的物质。|The gleba of the puffball is initially white and firm, but it turns brown and powdery as the spores mature.  马勃菌的孢体最初是白色且坚实的，但随着孢子成熟，它会变成棕色且呈粉状。|The stinkhorn's gleba is a dark, slimy mass that attracts insects. 鬼笔菌的孢体是一团黑色的粘液，可以吸引昆虫。|synonym: sporophore, fruiting body|antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical term)
词性: noun
