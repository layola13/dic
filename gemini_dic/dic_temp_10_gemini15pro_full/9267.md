antiperistaltic:/ˌæntiˌpɛrɪˈstɔːltɪk/ /ˌæntiˌpɛrɪˈstɑːltɪk/
antiperistaltic| adj.|逆蠕动的|Relating to or characterized by reversed peristalsis, the wavelike muscular contractions that move food through the digestive tract.|Antiperistaltic waves can cause vomiting. 逆蠕动波会导致呕吐。|reverse peristalsis, retrograde peristalsis|peristaltic|adjective
antiperistaltic| n.|逆蠕动|The reversed movement of the contents of the alimentary canal, opposite to the normal direction of peristalsis.|Severe antiperistaltic action can be painful.  严重的逆蠕动会很痛。|reverse peristalsis, retrograde peristalsis|peristalsis|noun
