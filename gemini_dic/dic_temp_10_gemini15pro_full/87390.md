herpetology:/ˌhɜːrpɪˈtɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌhɜːrpəˈtɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n.|爬虫学|The study of reptiles and amphibians.|Example 1: Herpetology is a fascinating branch of zoology. 爬虫学是动物学中一个引人入胜的分支。

Example 2: She's studying herpetology at university. 她在大学学习爬虫学。|近义词: - |反义词: - |词性:noun


Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἑρπετόν (herpetón, “reptile; creeping thing”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
