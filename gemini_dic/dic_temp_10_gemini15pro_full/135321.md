nonpayments:/ˌnɒnˈpeɪmənts/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈpeɪmənts/
n.
未付款，欠款
The act or state of not making payments.  Failure to pay a sum of money that is owed.
The company is facing legal action due to repeated nonpayments.  这家公司因屡次拖欠付款而面临法律诉讼。
nonpayment, default, delinquency, arrears
payment, settlement
