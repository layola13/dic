farle:/ˈfɑːrl/| n.|法雷饼（一种康沃尔郡的甜点）|A farle is a type of flat, round cake traditionally made in Cornwall, England. It's often served with clotted cream and jam.|We enjoyed freshly baked farles with cream and jam for afternoon tea. 我们享用了新鲜出炉的法雷饼配奶油和果酱作为下午茶。|Example:  My grandmother makes the best farles in the village. 我祖母做的法雷饼是村里最好吃的。|Synonym: scone, cake|Antonym: N/A|noun
