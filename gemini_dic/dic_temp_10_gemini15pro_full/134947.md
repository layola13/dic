nonnarcissism:/nɒnˈnɑːsɪsɪzəm/
n.
非自恋
Lack of narcissism; the state of not being narcissistic.
例句：Her nonnarcissism was refreshing in a world obsessed with selfies. 在一个痴迷于自拍的世界里，她的不以自我为中心令人耳目一新。

例句：He displayed a healthy nonnarcissism, always putting others' needs before his own. 他表现出一种健康的非自恋，总是把别人的需要放在自己的前面。
近义词：humility, modesty, selflessness
反义词：narcissism, egotism, self-centeredness
词性: 名词
