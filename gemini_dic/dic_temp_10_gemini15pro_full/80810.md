gothicnesses:/ˈɡɒθɪknɪsɪz/
n.
哥特式风格；哥特式特征
The quality or state of being Gothic; Gothic characteristics.
The castle's gothicnesses were evident in its pointed arches and gargoyles. 这座城堡的哥特式风格在其尖拱和滴水嘴兽中显露无疑。
Near synonyms: Gothicism, Gothic qualities
Antonyms: Modernism, minimalism
noun
