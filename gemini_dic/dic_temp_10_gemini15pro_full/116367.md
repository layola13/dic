masticators:/ˈmæstɪkeɪtərz/| n. |咀嚼肌；咀嚼器| Muscles or devices used for chewing or grinding food.  A person or animal that chews food.|The cow’s strong masticators allow it to grind tough grasses. 牛强壮的咀嚼肌使其能够磨碎坚韧的草。|  The industrial masticator easily pulverized the concrete.  这台工业粉碎机轻而易举地将混凝土粉碎了。| synonyms:  chewers, grinders, jaws, mandibles| antonyms: N/A| noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *masticator, from Latin masticatus, past participle of masticare "to chew," from Greek mastikhan "to gnash the teeth."
