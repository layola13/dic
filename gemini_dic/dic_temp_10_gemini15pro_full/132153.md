noncurdling:/ˌnɒnˈkɜːdlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不凝结的，抗凝结的 | Not curdling; resistant to curdling.  This describes something, usually a liquid or substance, that doesn't solidify or separate into curds and whey, like milk does when it sours. | This recipe uses a noncurdling milk substitute.  这个食谱使用了一种不凝结的牛奶替代品。 | The noncurdling yogurt was perfect for the smoothie.  这种不凝结的酸奶非常适合做冰昔。|  non-coagulating, stable | curdling, coagulating | adjective
