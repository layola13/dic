chevroned:/ˈʃɛvrənd/
adjective
人字形的，V字形的
decorated with a chevron or chevrons.
例句：The sergeant's chevroned sleeve indicated his rank. 中士袖子上的人字形图案表明了他的军衔。

例句：The building's facade was decorated with a chevroned pattern.  这栋建筑的正面装饰着人字形图案。

近义词：V-shaped, zigzag, angled
反义词：straight, linear
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chevronné*, from *chevron* ("rafter"), from Vulgar Latin *capriōnem*, accusative singular of *capriō* (“rafter”), from Latin *caper* (“goat”).  So called because the shape resembles a pair of goat's horns.
