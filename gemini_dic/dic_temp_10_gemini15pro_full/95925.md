incriminatory:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪnətɔːri/ /ɪnˈkrɪmɪˌneɪtɔːri/
adjective
控告的；显示有罪的；牵连的
Making someone appear guilty of a crime.
例句：The police found incriminatory evidence in his car. 警方在他的车里发现了显示他有罪的证据。

例句：His incriminatory remarks led to his arrest. 他那些牵连自己的言论导致他被捕。


近义词：accusatory, implicating, compromising
反义词：exculpatory, exonerating
词性：adjective
