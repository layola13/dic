ambidextrousnesses:/ˌæmbɪdɛkˈstrʌsnəsɪz/
n.
左右开弓；双手灵巧
The state of being ambidextrous; ability to use both hands equally well.
Her ambidextrousness was a great advantage in playing the piano. 她的双手灵巧在弹钢琴方面是一个很大的优势。
Synonyms: versatility, adaptability, two-handedness
Antonyms: one-handedness, clumsiness
noun
