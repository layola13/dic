modif:/ˈmɒdɪf/ /ˈmɑːdɪf/| v. |修改，更改|To make partial or minor changes to something, typically so as to improve it or to make it less extreme.|例句：The software needs to be modified to fit our needs.  这个软件需要修改以适应我们的需求。|例句：She modified her language to suit her audience. 她调整了措辞以适应听众。|近义词：alter, change, adjust, amend, revise|反义词：preserve, maintain, keep, retain|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *modificāre, from Latin modus (“measure, limit”) + -ficāre, a combining form from facere (“to make, to do”).
