hummingbird:/ˈhʌmɪŋbɜːrd/ | n. | 蜂鸟 | A small, brightly colored bird with a long, thin beak and the ability to hover in the air by rapidly flapping its wings. |  Example: The hummingbird zipped from flower to flower, sipping nectar.  蜂鸟在花丛中飞来飞去，吸食花蜜。
| Example:  I saw a beautiful hummingbird hovering near the feeder. 我看到一只美丽的蜂鸟在喂食器附近盘旋。| Synonyms: hummer | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the humming sound made by their rapidly beating wings. "Humming" + "bird".
