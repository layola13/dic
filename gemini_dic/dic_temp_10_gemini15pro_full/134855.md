nonmoderns:/nɒnˈmɒdənz/ | n. | 非现代人；非现代主义者 | People who are not considered modern, often referring to those who lived before the modern era or those who reject modern values. |  The museum's exhibit contrasted the art of the moderns with that of the nonmoderns.  博物馆的展览将现代主义者的艺术与非现代主义者的艺术进行了对比。| Synonyms: traditionalists, pre-moderns | Antonyms: moderns, contemporaries | noun

nonmoderns:/nɒnˈmɒdənz/ | adj. | 非现代的 | Not modern; belonging to or characteristic of a period before the modern era. |  The building's nonmodern architecture clashed with the surrounding skyscrapers. 这座建筑的非现代建筑与周围的摩天大楼格格不入。 | Synonyms: traditional, antiquated, archaic | Antonyms: modern, contemporary | adjective
