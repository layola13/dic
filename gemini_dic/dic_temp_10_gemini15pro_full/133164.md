nonetto:/noʊˈnɛtoʊ/
n.
九重奏，九重唱
A composition for nine voices or instruments.
The composer wrote a beautiful nonetto for strings and woodwinds.  作曲家写了一首优美的弦乐和木管九重奏。
nonet, nine-part music
octet, solo
