alligator-pear: /ˈælɪˌɡeɪtər pɛər/ | n. |鳄梨，牛油果 | A fruit with dark green or purplish skin, smooth, oily, green flesh, and a large, round stone.  It is native to tropical America and is now widely cultivated in warm climates. The term "alligator pear" is an older name, now largely superseded by "avocado." |  Alligator pears were once considered a luxury item. 鳄梨曾经被认为是奢侈品。|  They used to call avocados alligator pears. 他们过去把鳄梨叫做鳄梨梨。 | synonym: avocado | antonym: N/A | noun
