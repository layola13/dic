harengiform:/ˌhɛrɪŋdʒɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. |鯡形目的；鯡形目的鱼|Relating to or denoting fishes of the order Clupeiformes, which includes herrings, sardines, and anchovies.  These fish are typically small, silvery, and found in large schools.|例句：The herring is a harengiform fish. 鲱鱼是一种鲱形目鱼。|例句：Harengiform fishes are important commercially. 鲱形目鱼具有重要的商业价值。|近义词：clupeiform|反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)|词性：adjective, noun
