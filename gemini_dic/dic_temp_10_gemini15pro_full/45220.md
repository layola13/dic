culmiferous:/kʌlˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
有秆的；产生秆的
Bearing culms or stalks, especially those bearing grain.
The culmiferous plants, such as wheat and barley, are essential for food production.  像小麦和大麦这样的有秆植物对粮食生产至关重要。
Synonyms: stalk-bearing, stem-bearing, gramineous
Antonyms: acaulescent (无茎的), stemless
