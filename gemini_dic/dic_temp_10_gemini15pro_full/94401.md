impliable:/ɪmˈplaɪəbl/ | adj. | 易受影响的，可塑的，顺从的 | Easily influenced, persuaded, or molded; readily adaptable. |  The young artist's impliable mind quickly absorbed new techniques.  年轻艺术家的可塑性很强的头脑很快吸收了新技术。| 近义词: malleable, pliable, flexible, adaptable, tractable | 反义词: inflexible, rigid, obstinate, stubborn, unyielding | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *implicabilis, from Latin implicare "to involve, entangle," from in- "in" + plicare "to fold."  The sense development is from "easily folded or bent" to "easily influenced."
