hummocks:/ˈhʌməks/ | n. | 小丘，土墩，冰丘 | A small, rounded hill or mound of earth or ice. |  The tundra was dotted with hummocks of frozen earth. 苔原上点缀着一个个冻土小丘。

The ship navigated carefully through the icy hummocks. 船小心地在冰丘中航行。 | 近义词: mounds, knolls, hillocks | 反义词: hollows, depressions | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *hummoke, possibly a variant of *hummocke, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to hump.
