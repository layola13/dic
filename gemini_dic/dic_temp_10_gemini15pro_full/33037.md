chosennesses:/ˈtʃoʊzənɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
被选中状态；被选择性
The state of being chosen; the quality of having been selected.
The chosennesses of the few individuals for the prestigious award sparked controversy.  这几个人被选中获得这个享有盛誉的奖项引发了争议。
近义词: selections, choices
反义词: rejections
