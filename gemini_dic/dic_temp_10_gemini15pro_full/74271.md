foyer:/ˈfɔɪər/ (US), /ˈfɔɪjeɪ/ (UK)| n. |门厅，休息室，大厅|An entrance hall or lobby, especially of a theater, hotel, or other public building.|例句：The hotel foyer was grand and impressive.  酒店的门厅富丽堂皇，令人印象深刻。

例句：We met in the foyer of the theatre before the performance. 我们在演出前在剧院的门厅见面。|近义词：lobby, entrance hall, vestibule, antechamber|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *foyer* "fireplace, hearth," from Latin *focus* "hearth, fireplace."  The meaning shifted from "fireplace" to "lobby near the fireplace" and then to "entrance hall."
