allogamy: /əˈlɒɡəmi/ /əˈlɑːɡəmi/ | n. |异花授粉 | Fertilization of a plant by pollen from another plant. |例句：Allogamy is essential for maintaining genetic diversity in many plant species. 异花授粉对于维持许多植物物种的遗传多样性至关重要。

例句：Allogamy contrasts with autogamy, or self-fertilization. 异花授粉与自花授粉形成对比。|近义词：cross-fertilization, cross-pollination |反义词: autogamy, self-fertilization |词性: noun
