crater-like:/ˈkreɪtərˌlaɪk/
adjective
像火山口的;坑状的
Resembling a crater, especially in being a bowl-shaped depression.  A surface marked by or full of craters.
The meteorite impact left a crater-like depression in the ground. 陨石撞击在地面上留下了一个像火山口一样的凹陷。
The moon's surface is crater-like. 月球表面坑坑洼洼。
Synonyms:  cratered, pitted, hollowed, concave
Antonyms: smooth, flat, level
词性: 形容词
