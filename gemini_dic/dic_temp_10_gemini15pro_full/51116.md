despise:/dɪˈspaɪz/| vt.|鄙视，蔑视|To feel a strong dislike for someone or something considered unworthy of respect or regard.|She despises hypocrisy. 她鄙视虚伪。|He despised lying above all things. 他最痛恨撒谎。|近义词：loathe, detest, abhor, scorn|反义词：admire, respect, esteem|verb
