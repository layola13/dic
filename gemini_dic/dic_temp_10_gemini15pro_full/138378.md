notochord: /ˈnoʊtəkɔːrd/ | n. | 脊索 | A flexible rodlike structure that forms the main support of the body in the lowest chordates and lowest vertebrates and in embryos of higher vertebrates. |  The notochord is the defining characteristic of chordates. 脊索是脊索动物的定义特征。|  The embryo's notochord develops into the vertebral column. 胚胎的脊索发育成脊柱。| synonyms: backbone, spine | antonyms: None | noun
