disappearer:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪərər/
n.
消失的人; 使…消失的人或物
a person or thing that disappears.
The magician was a master disappearer, making rabbits vanish into thin air.  魔术师是个消失大师，能让兔子凭空消失。
The fog was a disappearer of ships, swallowing them whole.  雾是船只的消失者，将它们整个吞噬。
近义词:vanisher
反义词:appearer, materializer
