bare: /ber/ | adj. v. | 赤裸的，空的，露出，仅仅 | Uncovered, exposed, simple, or merely. |  The child ran outside with bare feet.  孩子光着脚跑到了外面。

The room was bare except for a table and a chair. 房间里除了一张桌子和一把椅子外空无一物。


| 近义词: naked, uncovered, exposed, empty, plain, mere | 反义词: covered, clothed, full, adorned, complex | 词性: 形容词，动词


bare: /ber/ | v. |  露出，暴露 | To uncover or expose. | He bared his chest to the sun. 他袒胸露乳晒太阳。

The dog bared its teeth aggressively. 狗凶狠地露出了牙齿。



| 近义词: expose, reveal, uncover | 反义词: conceal, cover, hide | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "bær" (meaning naked, bare), from Proto-Germanic "*baraz" (meaning bare, naked), from Proto-Indo-European "*bʰoso-" (meaning bare, naked).
