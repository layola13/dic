inseparable:/ɪnˈsɛpərəbəl/| adj.|不可分的，形影不离的|not able to be separated|例句：The two friends were inseparable. 这两个朋友形影不离。|近义词：indivisible, attached, close, united|反义词:separable, divisible, detached|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin inseparabilis, from in- 'not' + separabilis 'separable'. Ultimately from Latin separare 'to separate'.
