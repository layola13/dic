europhobic:/ˌjʊərəˈfəʊbɪk/| adj.|恐欧的，惧欧的|Having or showing an aversion to Europe or European things; characterized by fear of or prejudice against Europe or European culture.|例句：His europhobic sentiments led him to oppose joining the European Union. 他的恐欧情绪导致他反对加入欧盟。

例句：The politician's europhobic rhetoric stirred up controversy.  这位政客的恐欧言论引发了争议。|近义词：anti-European|反义词:pro-European|词性:形容词
