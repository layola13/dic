monophthong:/ˈmɒnəfθɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnəfθɔːŋ/ (US)| n.|单元音|A vowel sound that is pronounced with a single, unchanging mouth position, unlike a diphthong which glides from one sound to another.|The word "bet" contains the monophthong /e/.  "赌注"这个词包含单元音/e/。|Near synonyms: pure vowel|Antonym: diphthong|Noun
