feudatary:/ˈfjuːdəˌtɛri/
n.|封臣，诸侯；附属国|A person or state that holds land from a feudal lord in return for homage and service.  A vassal or dependent.|The small kingdom was a feudatary of the larger empire.|近义词:vassal, liegeman, subject, tributary|反义词:lord, sovereign, overlord|词性:名词

feudatary:/ˈfjuːdəˌtɛri/
adj.|封建的，诸侯的|Of or relating to a feudatory; held by feudal tenure.|The feudatary lord held considerable power within his own domain.|近义词:feudal, vassal, dependent|反义词:independent, sovereign|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feudataire*, from Medieval Latin *feudatarius*, from *feudum* ("fief").
