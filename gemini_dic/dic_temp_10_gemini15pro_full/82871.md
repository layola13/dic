gulling:/ˈɡʌlɪŋ/ | n. | 欺骗，愚弄 | The act of deceiving or cheating someone. | His gulling of the investors resulted in a huge loss for them. 他对投资者的欺骗导致他们损失惨重。|  synonyms: deceiving, cheating, tricking, duping, defrauding, swindling | antonyms: honesty, truthfulness | noun
gulling:/ˈɡʌlɪŋ/ | adj. | 易受骗的 | Easily deceived or cheated.  | He was so gulling that he believed every word the con man said. 他太容易受骗了，以至于相信了骗子说的每一句话。 | synonyms: gullible, naive, credulous, unsuspecting | antonyms: astute, discerning, perceptive, shrewd | adjective
