fairiest:/ˈfeəriɪst/| adj.|最美丽的，最迷人的|superlative form of  fairy; most like a fairy; most enchanting or magical|例句：She had the fairiest voice I'd ever heard. 她拥有我听过最美妙的声音。|近义词：magical, enchanting, delightful, whimsical|反义词：ugly, hideous, unpleasant, mundane|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *faierie, from Old French faerie (“fairyland, enchantment”), from feie (“fairy”), from Late Latin fata (“fate, destiny; fairy”), from Latin fatum.
