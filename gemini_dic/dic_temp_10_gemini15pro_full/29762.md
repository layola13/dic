catstitch:/ˈkætˌstɪtʃ/ |n.| 猫步缝合，锁边缝合 |A type of stitch used in sewing, resembling the tracks a cat makes when walking through soft ground; a blind stitch used to close an opening or join two edges invisibly.  It is also known as a blind stitch, slip stitch, or ladder stitch.|She used a catstitch to close the opening in the pillow. 她用猫步缝合缝合了枕头的开口。|Synonyms: blind stitch, slip stitch, ladder stitch |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific stitch type) |noun
