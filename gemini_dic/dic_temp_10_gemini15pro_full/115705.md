marginated:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
边缘化的；记入页边的
Having a margin; placed in or relegated to a lower or outer edge, as of society or a group.  Being pushed to the side, excluded, or ignored.
The marginalized communities often lack access to basic healthcare. 边缘化社区通常缺乏获得基本医疗保健的机会。
The report focuses on the experiences of marginalized youth in the city. 这份报告关注的是城市边缘青年的经历。
近义词:peripheral, excluded, disadvantaged, marginalized, voiceless
反义词:central, included, privileged, mainstream
词性:adjective

marginate:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/
verb
使边缘化；记入页边
To place in or relegate to a lower or outer edge, as of society or a group.
Social inequalities often marginate certain groups. 社会不平等常常使某些群体边缘化。
His controversial views have marginated him within the academic community. 他备受争议的观点使他在学术界被边缘化。
近义词:exclude, isolate, marginalize, ostracize
反义词:include, integrate, centralize
词性:verb
