fitzroy:/ˈfɪtsrɔɪ/| n.|菲茨罗伊; 菲茨罗伊河; 菲茨罗伊山|A surname and placename derived from the Norman French personal name "Fitz Roy", meaning "son of the king".  It is most commonly associated with Mount Fitz Roy in Patagonia, Argentina.|We climbed Mount Fitz Roy last summer. 我们去年夏天攀登了菲茨罗伊山。|近义词: (For the mountain) peak, mountain, summit|反义词: (For the mountain) valley, plain|词性:noun

fitzroy:/ˈfɪtsrɔɪ/| adj.|菲茨罗伊的|Relating to or located in Fitzroy, a suburb of Melbourne, Australia.|The Fitzroy bars are known for their live music. 菲茨罗伊的酒吧以现场音乐而闻名。|近义词: (If referring to location) Melburnian|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
