go-getter:/ˈɡoʊˌɡɛtər/
n.|实干家；积极进取的人|A go-getter is someone who is very energetic, determined to be successful, and able to deal with new or difficult situations easily.|She's a real go-getter and I'm sure she'll do well in her new job. 她是一个真正的实干家，我相信她会在新工作中做得很好。|synonyms: high-flier, achiever, self-starter, dynamo|antonyms: slacker, underachiever|noun
