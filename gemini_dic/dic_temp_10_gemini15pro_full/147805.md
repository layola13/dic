overprovided:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvaɪdɪd/
adjective
供应过剩的，过度提供的
having more of something than is needed or wanted
The market is overprovided with low-quality goods.  市场上充斥着劣质商品。
The charity was overprovided with donations after the disaster. 灾难过后，慈善机构收到了过多的捐赠。
近义词:oversupplied, glutted, saturated
反义词:underprovided, lacking, deficient
词性:adjective

over:/ˈoʊvər/
adverb, preposition, adjective
在...之上，超过，结束
above, across, beyond, finished
The meeting is over. 会议结束了。
He jumped over the fence. 他跳过了栅栏。
近义词:above, across, beyond, finished
反义词:under, below
词性:adverb, preposition, adjective

provided:/prəˈvaɪdɪd/
verb, past participle
提供，规定
to give something that is needed or wanted; to state as a condition
The company provided us with all the necessary equipment. 公司为我们提供了所有必要的设备。
Provided that you work hard, you will succeed. 只要你努力工作，你就会成功。
近义词:supply, furnish, offer, stipulate
反义词:withhold, deprive
词性:verb, past participle
