one-sidedly:/ˌwʌnˈsaɪdɪdli/ | adv. | 片面地，单方面地 |  In a way that shows only one side of an issue or situation; unfairly.  |  The issue was presented one-sidedly, ignoring the opposing viewpoint.  这个问题被片面地呈现，忽略了相反的观点。 | Synonyms: unfairly, biasedly, partially | Antonyms: fairly, impartially, objectively | Adverb

one-sided:/ˌwʌnˈsaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 片面的，单方面的 | Showing only one side of an issue or situation; unfair.  | The media coverage was very one-sided. 媒体的报道非常片面。 | Synonyms: unfair, biased, partial | Antonyms: fair, impartial, objective | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "one-sided" + "-ly."  "One-sided" is from "one" + "sided." "Side" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *sīdō.  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
