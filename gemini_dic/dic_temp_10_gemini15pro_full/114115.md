magot:/ˈmæɡət/ | n. |猕猴；巴巴利猕猴；财富；积蓄|A tailless monkey (Macaca sylvanus) of North Africa and Gibraltar.|例句：The magot is the only macaque species found outside of Asia. 巴巴利猕猴是唯一一种在亚洲以外地区发现的猕猴。

例句：The pirates buried their magot on the deserted island. 海盗们把他们的宝藏埋在了荒岛上。|近义词：macaque, monkey, treasure, hoard, savings|反义词：debt, expenditure|词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *magot, of uncertain origin, perhaps from Arabic makhāzin 'stores', plural of makhzan 'storehouse'.
