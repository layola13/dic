bridgeless:/ˈbrɪdʒləs/| adj.|无桥的，没有桥梁的|Lacking a bridge or bridges; not connected by a bridge.|The river was bridgeless, making it difficult to cross.|近义词：unbridged, unconnected|反义词:bridged, connected|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "bridge" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  "Bridge" comes from Old English "brycg." "-less" comes from Old English "-lēas."
