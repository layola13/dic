mpor 不是一个有效的英文单词。 你可能是想问 "import"。如果是这样，以下是 "import" 的解析：

import: /ɪmˈpɔːrt/ (UK) /ˈɪmpɔːrt/ (US) | n. vt. | 进口; 重要性; 意义; 输入 | To bring (goods or services) into a country from abroad for sale.  The meaning or significance of something, especially when not directly stated. To copy (data) from one computer system to another. |  例句：The US imports a lot of oil. 美国进口大量的石油。  The import of his words was clear.  他话里的含义很清楚。 We need to import the data into the new system. 我们需要将数据导入到新系统中。 | 近义词：bring in, introduce, significance, meaning | 反义词: export | 词性: noun, verb
