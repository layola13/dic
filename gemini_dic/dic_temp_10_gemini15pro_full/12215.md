asaphus:/əˈseɪfəs/ | n. | 阿萨弗斯，三叶虫的一种 | An extinct genus of trilobite that lived during the Ordovician period, characterized by a large head shield and prominent eyes. | Example: The Asaphus kowalewskii is known for its exceptionally long eye stalks.  阿萨弗斯k Kowalewskii以其异常长的眼柄而闻名。
