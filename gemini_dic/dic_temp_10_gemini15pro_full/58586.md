dulcino:/dʊlˈtʃiːnoʊ/ /dʌlˈtʃiːnoʊ/
noun
甜食；甜酒；甜心；亲爱的
a sweet food or drink; a sweetheart; a term of endearment
He brought her a box of dulcinos from the bakery. 他从面包店给她带了一盒甜点。
She called him her dulcino in a love letter.  她在情书中称他为她的甜心。
synonyms:sweet, sweetie, darling, honey, dear
antonyms:none (This word doesn't have a direct opposite.)
