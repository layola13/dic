hydronephrosis:/ˌhaɪdrɵnɪˈfroʊsɪs/| n. |肾积水|The dilation of the renal pelvis and calyces of one or both kidneys, caused by the obstruction of the flow of urine.|The patient presented with symptoms suggestive of hydronephrosis.|近义词:  urinary obstruction, renal stasis|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider normal kidney function)|词性:noun
