nonparishioners:/ˌnɒn pəˈrɪʃənəz/ (US) /ˌnɒn pəˈrɪʃənəz/ (UK)| n. |非教区居民|People who do not belong to a particular parish.  Those who live outside the boundaries of a parish or do not attend its church.| The church welcomed nonparishioners to the Christmas service. 教堂欢迎非教区居民参加圣诞节礼拜。|synonyms: outsider, non-member|antonyms: parishioner, member|noun
