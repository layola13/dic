immobilises:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使不动；固定；使不能动弹
To prevent something or someone from moving or operating.
The police immobilised the vehicle by clamping its wheels. 警察用车轮夹锁使车辆无法移动。
His broken leg immobilised him for weeks.  他的断腿使他数周不能动弹。
synonyms: restrict, restrain, disable, paralyze
antonyms: mobilize, activate, free, release
词性: 动词


immobilises:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzɪz/
verb
固定（资金、资产等）
To prevent assets or funds from being readily used or transferred.
The government immobilised the company's assets after the fraud was discovered. 政府在发现欺诈行为后冻结了公司的资产。
Large amounts of capital are immobilised in property. 大量的资金被固定在房地产中。
synonyms: freeze, block, tie up
antonyms: release, free up, liquidate
词性: 动词
