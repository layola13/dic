oversupplies:/ˌoʊvər səˈplaɪz/ /ˌəʊvə səˈplaɪz/| n. |过量供应，供应过剩|An amount of something that is more than is needed or wanted.|例句：Oversupplies of oil have led to a drop in prices. 石油供应过剩导致价格下跌。|近义词：surplus, glut, excess|反义词: shortage, deficit, undersupply|词性:名词

oversupply:/ˌoʊvər səˈplaɪ/ /ˌəʊvə səˈplaɪ/| vt. |过度供应，供应过剩|To provide more of something than is needed or wanted.|例句：The market is oversupplied with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|近义词：glut, saturate, flood|反义词: undersupply|词性:动词
