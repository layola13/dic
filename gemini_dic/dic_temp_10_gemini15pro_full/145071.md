overanalyze:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪz/| v.|过度分析，过分解读|To analyze something excessively or in too much detail, often to the point of losing clarity or becoming needlessly critical.|She tends to overanalyze every situation, which can make her anxious. 她倾向于过分解读每一种情况，这会让她焦虑。|He overanalyzed the email from his boss and convinced himself he was going to be fired. 他过分解读了老板的邮件，确信自己要被解雇了。|近义词：overthink, dissect, scrutinize|反义词：underthink, skim, glance|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + analyze.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Analyze" comes from the French analyser, from Greek analyein "to loosen up, dissolve," from ana- "up" + lyein "to loosen."
