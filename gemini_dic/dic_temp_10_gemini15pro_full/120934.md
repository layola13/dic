miscellany: /ˌmɪsəˈleɪni/ | n. | 杂项；混合物；文集 | A collection of different items or a mixture of various things. |  例句：The museum housed a miscellany of artifacts from different eras.  博物馆收藏了来自不同时代的各种文物。

| 例句：Her book is a miscellany of poems, essays, and short stories. 她的书是诗歌、散文和短篇小说的杂集。 | 近义词：assortment, collection, mixture, variety | 反义词: uniformity, homogeneity | 词性: noun


miscellaneous: /ˌmɪsəˈleɪniəs/ | adj. | 各种各样的；混杂的 |  Consisting of diverse things or members. | 例句：The attic contained a miscellaneous collection of old furniture and toys. 阁楼里堆放着各种各样的旧家具和玩具。

| 例句：He had a miscellaneous collection of stamps from around the world. 他收集了来自世界各地的各种邮票。 | 近义词：varied, diverse, assorted, sundry | 反义词: uniform, homogeneous, specific | 词性: adjective
