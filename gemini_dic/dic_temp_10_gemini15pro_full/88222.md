hinderling:/ˈhɪndərlɪŋ/| n. | 妨碍者；碍手碍脚的人；落后者；无用之人 |A person or thing that hinders or obstructs; a nuisance. Someone who lags behind or is incompetent. |The slow hinderling was holding up the entire group.  这个行动迟缓的落后者拖了整个团队的后腿。|  He considered his younger brother a hinderling to his success. 他认为他的弟弟是他成功的绊脚石。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, nuisance, straggler, laggard|反义词：help, aid, asset, advantage|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hinder (v.) + -ling (suffix forming nouns denoting persons or things characterized by the action or quality of the verb).  Hinder itself comes from Middle English hindren, from Old English hindrian "to keep back, prevent, restrain," related to hinder (adj.) "situated behind."
