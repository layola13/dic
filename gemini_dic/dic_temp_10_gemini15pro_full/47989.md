decimate:/ˈdɛsɪmeɪt/| v. | 大量毁灭，严重削弱 | To destroy or kill a large proportion of.  Originally, to kill one in every ten (as a punishment in Roman armies). | The population was decimated by disease.  这种疾病使人口大量减少。 | synonyms: destroy, devastate, ravage, ruin, wreck | antonyms:  build, create, construct, establish,  protect | verb


decimation:/ˌdɛsɪˈmeɪʃən/| n. | 大量毁灭，严重削弱 | The act of decimating; widespread destruction. | The decimation of the rainforest is a serious environmental problem.  雨林遭到大面积破坏是一个严重的环境问题。 | synonyms: destruction, devastation, ruin | antonyms: construction, creation, preservation | noun
