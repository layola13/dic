all-for-the-best:/ɔːl fɔːr ðə best/
|adj.|
|一切为了更好；出于好意；到头来都是好事|
|Used to say that although something seems bad or unpleasant now, it will have a good result in the future.|
|It was probably all for the best that he didn't get the job.|他没得到那份工作或许反倒是件好事。|
|beneficial, advantageous, fortunate|
|disadvantageous, unfortunate, unfavorable|
|adjective phrase|
