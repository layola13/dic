correlates:/ˈkɔːrəleɪts/| n. |相关物，相关联的事物|Something that correlates; a variable that is correlated with another.|例句：Stress levels and heart disease are often correlates. 压力水平和心脏病通常是相关联的。|近义词：connections, relationships, correspondences|反义词:disconnects, disparities|noun

correlates:/ˈkɔːrəleɪts/| v. |相互关联，使…相关联|Have a mutual relationship or connection, in which one thing affects or depends on another.|例句：Researchers found that income correlates with education level. 研究人员发现收入与教育水平相关。|近义词：relate, correspond, match, connect|反义词:differ, diverge, contrast|verb
