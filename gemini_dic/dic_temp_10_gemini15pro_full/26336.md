buzzards:/ˈbʌzərdz/ (US), /ˈbʌzədz/ (UK)| n. |秃鹫；贪婪的人|Large birds of prey that feed on carrion (dead animals). Can also refer to people who exploit others. |例句：The buzzards circled overhead, waiting for the dying animal. 秃鹰在头顶盘旋，等待着垂死的动物。

例句：He was known as a buzzard in the business world, always looking for a quick profit. 在商界，他被认为是一个贪婪的人，总是想快速获利。|近义词：vultures, hawks, profiteers |反义词: doves (symbolically for peaceful people) |词性:noun
