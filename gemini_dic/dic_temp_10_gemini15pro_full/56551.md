domineering:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 专横的，跋扈的 | Trying to control other people without considering their feelings or opinions. |  She has a very domineering personality and always wants to be in charge. 她性格很专横，总是想掌控一切。

The domineering manager made all the decisions without consulting his team.  这个专横的经理没有咨询他的团队就做出了所有决定。

| 近义词: bossy, overbearing, tyrannical, autocratic, imperious | 反义词: submissive, meek, compliant, accommodating | 词性: 形容词
