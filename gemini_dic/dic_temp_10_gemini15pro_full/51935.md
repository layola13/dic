diabetic:/ˌdaɪəˈbetɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪk/| adj. n.|糖尿病的，糖尿病患者|Relating to or suffering from diabetes, a disease in which the body cannot properly control the amount of sugar in the blood.|My grandmother is diabetic and has to watch her sugar intake. 我的祖母患有糖尿病，必须注意糖的摄入量。|近义词：hyperglycemic|反义词:non-diabetic|词性:形容词

diabetic:/ˌdaɪəˈbetɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪk/| n.|糖尿病患者|A person who has diabetes.|The diabetic needs to take insulin regularly.  这个糖尿病患者需要定期注射胰岛素。|近义词：sufferer of diabetes|反义词:N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diabeticus, from Greek διαβητικός (diabētikos, “passing through”), from διαβαίνειν (diabainein, “to pass through”), from διά (dia, “through”) + βαίνειν (bainein, “to go, walk”).  The term refers to excessive urination, a common symptom of diabetes.
