homegirls:/ˈhoʊmˌɡɜːrlz/ (US) /ˈhəʊmˌɡɜːlz/ (UK)| n.|闺蜜，女性好友（尤指来自同一社区或背景的）|A term of endearment and solidarity among women, particularly those from the same neighborhood, social group, or cultural background. It signifies close friendship, loyalty, and shared experiences.|例句：My homegirls and I went shopping yesterday. 我和我的闺蜜们昨天去购物了。

例句：She's been my homegirl since we were kids. 她从小就是我的好闺蜜。|近义词：best friend, close friend, gal pal, bestie|反义词:enemy, rival|Noun
