chambray: /ˈʃæmbreɪ/ (US), /ˈʃɒmbreɪ/ (UK) | n. |  青年布，一种类似牛仔布的棉织物 | A lightweight cotton fabric woven with a colored warp (often blue) and a white filling, giving it a mottled appearance similar to denim. |  She wore a chambray shirt with white jeans. 她穿着一件青年布衬衫和白色牛仔裤。| 近义词: denim, gingham | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps silk or satin for contrasting texture) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Cambrai, a town in northern France where the fabric originated.
