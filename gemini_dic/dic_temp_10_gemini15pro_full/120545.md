miracles:/ˈmɪrəkəlz/ (US), /ˈmɪrəklz/ (UK)| n. |奇迹；令人惊奇的人（或事）|An event that appears inexplicable by the laws of nature and so is attributed to a supernatural or divine agency.  A remarkable and unexpected event, thing, or achievement.|例句：The doctors said her recovery was a miracle. 医生说她的康复是个奇迹。|近义词：wonder, marvel, phenomenon|反义词：disaster, calamity|Noun
