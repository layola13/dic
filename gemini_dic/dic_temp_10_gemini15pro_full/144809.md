over-hardens:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnz/
|v.|过度硬化；硬化过度|To make something excessively hard, beyond what is desirable or necessary.  The process often makes the material brittle and prone to cracking.|Repeated heating and quenching can over-harden the steel, making it brittle. 反复加热和淬火会使钢过度硬化，使其变脆。|The potter over-hardened the clay, causing it to crack during firing. 陶工把粘土烧得过硬，导致在烧制过程中开裂。|over-stiffen, over-cure|soften, temper|verb
