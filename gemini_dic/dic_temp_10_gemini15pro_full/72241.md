floridity:/flɔːˈrɪdəti/ /flɔːˈrɪdɪti/
noun
华丽；绚丽；辞藻华丽；花哨
the quality of being florid, especially excessively so.
Her prose is known for its floridity. 她的散文以其华丽的辞藻而闻名。

The floridity of his language made the speech difficult to understand.  他华丽的辞藻使得演讲难以理解。

近义词:ornateness, elaborateness, flamboyance, extravagance
反义词:simplicity, plainness
词性:noun
