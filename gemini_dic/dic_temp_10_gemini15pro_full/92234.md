hypertragic:/ˌhaɪpərˈtrædʒɪk/
adjective
极其悲惨的，过分悲剧的
Exaggeratedly or excessively tragic.  Marked by extreme or exaggerated tragedy.  Beyond the usual range of tragedy; extremely tragic.
His hypertragic pronouncements about the state of the economy were met with skepticism. 他关于经济状况的极其悲观的言论遭到了质疑。
The play was so hypertragic it bordered on parody.  这出戏过于悲惨，近乎滑稽模仿。
近义词:over-the-top, melodramatic, excessively tragic
反义词:comic, lighthearted, humorous
词性:adjective

词源:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek *uper "over, beyond"
tragic: from Late Latin tragicus, from Greek tragikos "pertaining to tragedy," from tragoidia "tragedy"
