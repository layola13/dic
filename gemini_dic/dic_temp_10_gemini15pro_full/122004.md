misspent:/ˌmɪsˈspɛnt/
adjective
浪费的，滥用的
incorrectly or unwisely used (especially of money or time)
He regretted his misspent youth. 他后悔自己虚度了青春。
近义词: wasted, squandered, frittered away
反义词: well-spent, productive, fruitful
词性: adjective

词源:
From mis- + spent, past participle of spend.  The prefix "mis-" signifies "badly" or "wrongly."  "Spend" comes from Old English *spendan, meaning "to expend, pay out."
