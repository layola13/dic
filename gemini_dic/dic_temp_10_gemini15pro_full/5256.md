allodial:/əˈloʊdiəl/
adjective
自主保有土地的;自由保有财产的;完全拥有的
Of land, owned absolutely; free of any rent or service due to a superior.  Relating to the absolute ownership of real property.
例句：He owned the land allodially, free and clear of any feudal obligations. 他完全拥有这片土地，没有任何封建义务。

例句：Allodial title is the most complete form of ownership. 完全所有权是最完整的拥有形式。
synonym:freehold, outright
antonym:leasehold, feudal
词性:adjective
