clued-in:/ˌkluːdˈɪn/
adjective
知情的；了解内情的；消息灵通的
Well-informed and aware of what is happening.
例句：She's very clued-in about the latest fashion trends. 她对最新的时尚潮流非常了解。

例句：He's not very clued-in when it comes to office politics.  说到办公室政治，他不太了解情况。

近义词：informed, knowledgeable, aware, in the know, up to speed
反义词：unaware, uninformed, out of the loop, clueless, ignorant
词性: adjective
