antiradars:/ˌæntiˈreɪdɑːrz/
| n. |反雷达装置，反雷达技术|Devices or techniques designed to counter or evade radar detection.|The plane was equipped with antiradars to avoid enemy detection. 这架飞机配备了反雷达装置以避免被敌人侦测到。|Synonyms: radar jammers, radar countermeasures|Antonyms: radar detectors|noun
