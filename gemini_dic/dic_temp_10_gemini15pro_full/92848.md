icteritous:/ˌɪktəˈrɪʃəs/
adjective
黄疸的；患黄疸病的
Affected by jaundice, which causes yellowing of the skin and eyes.
The patient's skin had an icteritous tinge, suggesting liver problems. 病人的皮肤呈黄疸色，表明有肝脏问题。

The newborn baby's icteritous skin worried the new parents.  新生儿黄疸的皮肤让新手父母很担心。

synonym: jaundiced
antonym: healthy, rosy

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *icterus* (jaundice) + *-ous* (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Greek *ikteros* (jaundice).
