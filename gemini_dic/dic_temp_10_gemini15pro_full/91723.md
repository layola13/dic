过度神化的；极度神圣化的
Excessively deified; treated as a god to an extreme degree.  Built from "hyper-" (meaning "over, excessive") and "deified" (meaning "treated as a god").
The hyperdeified ruler demanded absolute obedience from his subjects.  这位被过度神化的统治者要求臣民绝对服从。

Antonyms: vilified, demonized, denigrated
词性: adjective
