enthean:/ɛnˈθiːən/ | adj. | 狂喜的，受神灵启示的；灵感迸发的 | Inspired by a god, or divinely inspired; relating to divine possession or frenzy.  | The poet felt an enthean surge of creativity as he wrote. 这位诗人写作时感到一股神灵般的创作冲动。 | 近义词：inspired, rapturous, ecstatic, divine | 反义词：mundane, ordinary, uninspired | 词性: 形容词

enthean:/ɛnˈθiːən/ | n. | 受神灵启示的人；狂热者 | A person inspired by a god; an enthusiast. | The ancient oracles were considered entheans, capable of communicating with the gods. 古代的神谕者被认为是受神灵启示的人，能够与神交流。 | 近义词：prophet, seer, visionary | 反义词：skeptic, doubter | 词性: 名词

词源: From Greek *entheos* "inspired, possessed by a god," from *en* "in" + *theos* "god."
