hubbard:/ˈhʌbərd/| n. |大南瓜; 南瓜状的东西; (Hubbard)哈伯德(姓氏)|A type of large, round winter squash with a dark green, bumpy rind.  A thing shaped like a hubbard squash. A surname.|例句：We roasted a hubbard squash for dinner. 我们烤了一个大南瓜作为晚餐。

例句：The old car looked like a giant hubbard squash. 那辆旧车看起来像一个巨大的南瓜。|近义词：squash, pumpkin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
