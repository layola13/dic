dumpiest:/ˈdʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最矮胖的|Superlative form of dumpy: most dumpy.  Describes someone short and stout in a less than flattering way.|例句：He was the dumpiest little man I'd ever seen. 他是我见过的最矮胖的小个子男人。|例句：Of all the kittens, she picked the dumpiest one. 在所有的小猫中，她挑了最矮胖的一只。|近义词：chubbiest, stoutest, stockiest|反义词：tallest, slimmest, thinnest|词性:形容词
词源: From 'dump' (meaning short and stout) + '-y' (forming an adjective) + '-est' (superlative suffix). Ultimately from a Low German word meaning "short and thick."
