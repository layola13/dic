dysphagia:/dɪsˈfeɪdʒə/ (US), /dɪsˈfeɪʒɪə/ (UK)| n.|吞咽困难|Difficulty or discomfort in swallowing, as a symptom of disease.|The stroke patient experienced dysphagia and required a feeding tube.  中风病人出现吞咽困难，需要插胃管。|

例句：
His dysphagia made it difficult for him to eat solid foods. 他的吞咽困难使他难以进食固体食物。

The doctor diagnosed her with dysphagia after she complained of difficulty swallowing.  医生在她抱怨吞咽困难后诊断她患有吞咽困难。
