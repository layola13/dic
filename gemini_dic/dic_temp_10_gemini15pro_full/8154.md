anti-cosmetics:/ˌænti kɒzˈmɛtɪks/ |n.| 反化妆品；反对化妆品 |Products or a philosophy that opposes the traditional ideals of cosmetics, often emphasizing natural beauty or rejecting the societal pressures associated with makeup.|  She's part of the anti-cosmetics movement and embraces her natural look. 她参与了反化妆品运动，并拥抱她的自然容貌。|  He wrote a blog post about the anti-cosmetics trend. 他写了一篇关于反化妆品趋势的博文。| Near synonyms: natural beauty movement, pro-aging | Antonyms: cosmetics, makeup | noun

anti-cosmetic:/ˌænti kɒzˈmɛtɪk/ |adj.| 反对化妆品的；反化妆的| Opposing or rejecting the use of cosmetics. | She has an anti-cosmetic stance, believing makeup is superficial. 她持有反化妆品的立场，认为化妆是肤浅的。 |  The article presented an anti-cosmetic viewpoint, arguing that makeup masks true beauty. 这篇文章提出了一个反化妆的观点，认为化妆掩盖了真正的美。| Near synonyms:  natural, unadorned | Antonyms: cosmetic, pro-makeup | adjective
