DVD-ROM: /ˌdiː viː diː ˈrɒm/ (British) /ˌdiː viː diː ˈrɑːm/ (American)| n. |数字多功能光盘只读存储器|A type of optical disc that can store large amounts of data, but the data can only be read and not written to.  It stands for "Digital Versatile Disc - Read-Only Memory".|I have a collection of movies on DVD-ROM. 我收藏了很多DVD-ROM电影。|Near synonyms: optical disc, CD-ROM|Antonym: DVD-RW (DVD-ReWritable)|Noun
