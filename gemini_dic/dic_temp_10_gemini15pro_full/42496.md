counterincentives:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɛntɪvz/| n.|反激励，抑制因素|Something that discourages a particular action or behavior.  It acts against the intended incentive.|High taxes on cigarettes are a counterincentive to smoking.  香烟的高税收是对吸烟的一种反激励。|disincentives, deterrents|incentives, inducements|noun
