overreduces:/ˌoʊvərɪˈduːsɪz/ | v. | 过度简化，过度还原 | Simplifies to an excessive degree; reduces too much. |  The model overreduces the complexity of the situation, ignoring key factors.  这个模型过度简化了情况的复杂性，忽略了关键因素。

The explanation overreduces the scientific principles involved.  这个解释过度简化了所涉及的科学原理。 | 近义词: oversimplifies, minimizes | 反义词: complicates, expands | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "reduce" (from Latin "reducere," meaning "to lead back, bring back, restore; diminish, lessen").
