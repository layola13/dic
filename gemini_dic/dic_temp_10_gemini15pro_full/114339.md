majestic:/məˈdʒɛstɪk/| adj.|雄伟的；壮丽的；庄严的|Having or showing impressive beauty or dignity.|The majestic mountains loomed over the valley. 雄伟的山脉俯瞰着山谷。|例句：The eagle soared majestically through the air.  老鹰雄赳赳地翱翔在空中。

例句：The majestic cathedral stood tall against the sky. 壮丽的大教堂高耸入云。|近义词：grand, magnificent, stately, imposing, august|反义词：humble, insignificant, paltry|词性:形容词
