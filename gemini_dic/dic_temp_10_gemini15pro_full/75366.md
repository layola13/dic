frounce:/fraʊns/ | v. | 皱起；使皱褶；使起皱纹 | To gather or contract (cloth or the features) into wrinkles or folds. | She frounced her eyebrows in disapproval. 她不赞成地皱起了眉头。

The dress was frounced around the neckline. 这件衣服在领口周围有褶皱。 | 近义词：wrinkle, crease, gather, pucker, crumple | 反义词：smooth, flatten, iron | 词性: 动词

frounce:/fraʊns/ | n. | 皱褶；褶皱 | A wrinkle or gathering in cloth. | The frounces in the curtains added a touch of elegance. 窗帘上的褶皱增添了一丝优雅。 | 近义词：wrinkle, crease, gather, pucker, fold | 反义词：smoothness, flatness | 词性: 名词
