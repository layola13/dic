monograph on (关于...的专著): He is writing a monograph on the history of photography. 他正在写一本关于摄影史的专著。
a monograph about (关于...的专著):  She published a monograph about the life of Marie Curie. 她出版了一本关于居里夫人生平的专著。
monograph dealing with (处理...的专著):  This monograph deals with the complexities of quantum physics. 这本专著探讨了量子物理学的复杂性。
publish a monograph (出版专著): He hopes to publish a monograph on his research findings. 他希望出版一本关于他的研究成果的专著。
research for a monograph (为专著进行研究): She is conducting research for a monograph on the history of medicine. 她正在为一本关于医学史的专著进行研究。
