oxbow:/ˈɒksbəʊ/ (UK) /ˈɑːksboʊ/ (US)| n.|牛轭湖；河迹湖|A U-shaped bend in a river or the land enclosed by it, formed by a change in the river's course.|The farm was located near an oxbow of the Mississippi River.  这个农场位于密西西比河的一个牛轭湖附近。

The oxbow lake was a haven for wildlife.  这个牛轭湖是野生动物的避难所。|近义词：meander, bend, loop|反义词:straight (stretch of river)|词性:noun
