inconformity:/ˌɪnkənˈfɔːrməti/
n.
不一致，不符合；不整合
lack of conformity or agreement; geological unconformity
The unconformity between the two rock layers suggests a significant gap in geological time. 这两层岩石的不整合表明地质时期存在着显著的差距。
nonconformity, discordance, disagreement, disharmony, variance
conformity, agreement, harmony, concordance
noun
