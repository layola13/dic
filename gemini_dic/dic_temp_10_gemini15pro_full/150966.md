parament:/ˈpærəmənt/ /ˈpærəmɛnt/| n. |圣服，装饰品|A rich or decorative hanging or covering, especially one used in a church or temple.  Specifically, it refers to the vestments or robes worn by clergy during religious services. |The altar was covered with a crimson parament.  祭坛上覆盖着深红色的圣服。|近义词：vestment, drapery, adornment|反义词:plainness, simplicity|词性:noun

parament:/ˈpærəmənt/ /ˈpærəmɛnt/| v. |装饰，修饰|To adorn or decorate, especially with rich hangings or ornaments. (This usage is less common.) |They paramented the hall for the festival.  他们为节日装饰了大厅。|近义词：adorn, decorate, embellish|反义词:strip, denude|词性:verb
