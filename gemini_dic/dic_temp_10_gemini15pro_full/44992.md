crystallize around something:  围绕…形成；以…为中心  Public opinion quickly crystallized around the issue.  公众舆论迅速围绕这个问题形成。
crystallize one's thoughts/ideas:  使某人的想法/思想明确  She needed time to crystallize her thoughts. 她需要时间来理清思路。
