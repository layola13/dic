crept:/krɛpt/
| v.|爬行，蹑手蹑脚地走，(植物)蔓延，(感觉)渐渐产生|Past form of creep. 过去式动词 creep|The cat crept silently towards the bird. 猫悄悄地向鸟爬去。|近义词：crawled, sneaked, inched, slithered|反义词：ran, strode, charged|词性: 动词


Etymology:
From Middle English crepen, from Old English crēopan (“to creep, crawl”), from Proto-Germanic *kreupaną (“to creep”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”). Cognate with Scots creip (“to creep”), West Frisian krûpe (“to creep”), Dutch kruipen (“to crawl, creep”), German kriechen (“to crawl, creep”), Danish krybe (“to creep, crawl”), Swedish krypa (“to creep, crawl”), Icelandic krjúpa (“to creep, crawl, crouch”).
