betoken:/bɪˈtəʊkən/ | v. |预示，表示，象征 |To be a sign of; indicate:|Dark clouds betoken rain. 乌云预示着要下雨。|近义词：indicate, signify, portend, foreshadow, presage|反义词：conceal, hide, mask|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *bitokenen*, from Old English *betācnian* (“to signify, betoken, portend, indicate, show, demonstrate, designate, appoint, assign, prescribe”), from *be-* + *tācnian* (“to mark, show, indicate”).  Cognate with Dutch *betekenen* (“to mean, signify”), German *bezeichnen* (“to mark, denote”).
