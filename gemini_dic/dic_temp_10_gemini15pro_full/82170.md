ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *grāpian "to grasp, seize," from Proto-Germanic *grapōjanan (source also of Old Frisian grāpa "to grasp, grope," Middle Dutch gropen "to grasp, grope," Old High German grappon "to seize, grasp," German grapschen "to grab").
