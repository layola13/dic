ocellary:/ˈɒsələri/ /ˈɑːsəleri/| adj.|眼状斑的，单眼的|Relating to, or resembling, an ocellus; marked with eyelike spots.|The butterfly's wings had ocellary markings that resembled eyes, deterring predators. 蝴蝶的翅膀上有类似眼睛的眼状斑，可以吓退捕食者。|近义词：eyed, eyelike, ocellated|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "unmarked" or "plain")|词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *ocellus* (little eye), diminutive of *oculus* (eye).
