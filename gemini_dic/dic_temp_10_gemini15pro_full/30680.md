cert:/sɜːrt/ /sɝːt/| n.|证书，证明|A document attesting to the truth of something, typically to the qualifications or abilities of a person.|She finally got her teaching cert. 她终于拿到了教师资格证书。|Synonym: certificate, license, diploma|Antonym: disqualification|noun

cert:/sɜːrt/ /sɝːt/| abbreviation.|certain|Used informally for 'certain'.|It's a cert that he'll win. 他肯定会赢。|Synonym: sure, definite, positive|Antonym: uncertain, doubtful|abbreviation
