olympiadic: /əˌlɪmpiˈædɪk/ | adj. | 奥林匹克的；与奥林匹克运动会相关的 | Relating to the Olympic Games or resembling the Olympic Games in scale or importance. |  The olympiadic spirit inspires athletes worldwide. 奥林匹克精神激励着全世界的运动员。| The city underwent olympiadic preparations for years before hosting the games.  这座城市在举办奥运会之前进行了多年的奥运筹备工作。 | 近义词: Olympic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From French *olympiadique*, from Olympie "Olympia" (the site of the ancient Olympic Games in Greece) + -ique "-ic". Ultimately from Ancient Greek  Ὄλυμπος (Ólympos, “Mount Olympus”).
