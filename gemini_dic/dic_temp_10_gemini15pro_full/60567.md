elastician:/ɪˌlæstɪˈʃən/
n.
弹性力学专家，研究弹性物质的科学家
A scientist who specializes in the study of elasticity.
The elastician calculated the stress on the bridge supports.  弹性力学专家计算了桥梁支撑的应力。
近义词:physicist, engineer, scientist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

elasticity:/ɪˌlæˈstɪsəti/
n.
弹性，弹力
The ability of a material to return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed.
Rubber has high elasticity. 橡胶具有高弹性。
近义词: flexibility, resilience, springiness
反义词: rigidity, stiffness
词性: noun
