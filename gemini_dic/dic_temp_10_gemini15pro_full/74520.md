fraternalisms:/frəˈtɜːrnəlɪzəmz/
n.
兄弟情谊，博爱精神
The spirit or feeling of brotherhood; fraternal relationship.  A system of beliefs and practices based on the idea of brotherhood.
College fraternities are known for their fraternalisms.  大学兄弟会以其兄弟情谊而闻名。
近义词：brotherhood, camaraderie, fellowship
反义词：animosity, hostility, antagonism
词性: 名词


fraternalisms:/frəˈtɜːrnəlɪzəmz/

兄弟主义
Doctrines or practices of fraternal organizations.
The fraternalisms of the Freemasons are well-documented. 共济会的兄弟主义有详细的记载。
