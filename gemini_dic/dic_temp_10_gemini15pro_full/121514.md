misliving:/ˌmɪsˈlɪvɪŋ/
noun
错误的生活方式;不道德的生活
living wrongly or immorally
He regretted his years of misliving. 他后悔自己多年来的不道德生活。
近义词: debauchery, dissipation, vice
反义词: virtue, righteousness
词性:n.


Etymology:
From mis- + living,  "mis-" being a prefix of negative or wrong sense, and "living" referring to one's way of life.
