mysticalnesses:/ˈmɪstɪkəlnəsɪz/| n. |神秘性；神秘感；不可思议|The quality or state of being mystical; a mysterious or awe-inspiring quality.|The mysticalnesses of the ancient rituals captivated the audience. 古老仪式的奥秘深深吸引了观众。|近义词：mystery, enigma, otherworldliness, spirituality|反义词：mundanity, ordinariness|词性: noun

词源: From mystical + -ness. "Mystical" comes from Late Latin *mysticalis*, from Greek *mystikos* "secret, mystic," from *mystes* "one initiated into mysteries."
