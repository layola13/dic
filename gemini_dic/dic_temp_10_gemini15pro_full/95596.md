incommixture:/ˌɪnkɒmˈmɪkstʃər/ |n.|混合，混合物|The incommixture of different cultures created a vibrant new society. 不同文化的混合创造了一个充满活力的新社会。|例句：The recipe called for an incommixture of spices.  这食谱需要混合各种香料。

The incommixture of different styles of music created a unique sound. 不同音乐风格的混合创造了一种独特的声音。|近义词：mixture, blend, mingling, amalgamation, combination|反义词：separation, division, segregation|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (meaning "in, into, on, upon, not, without") + commix (meaning "to mix together") + -ture (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *in- + commixtus (past participle of commiscere, "to mix together").
