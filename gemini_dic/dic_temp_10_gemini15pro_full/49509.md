demagogueries: /ˌdɛməˈɡɒɡəriːz/
noun
煽动性言论，蛊惑人心的言辞
Inflammatory rhetoric or appeals designed to arouse emotions and prejudices, often used by political leaders to gain popular support.
His campaign was filled with demagogueries about immigrants stealing jobs. 他的竞选活动充斥着关于移民抢走工作的煽动性言论。
The senator's demagogueries about the dangers of socialism resonated with his base. 参议员关于社会主义危险的煽动性言论引起了他的支持者的共鸣。
synonyms: rabble-rousing, incendiary rhetoric, appeals to prejudice, fear-mongering
antonyms: reasoned discourse, logical argumentation, factual presentation
plural noun
