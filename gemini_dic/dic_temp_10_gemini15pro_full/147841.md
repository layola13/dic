overpurchasing:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrtʃəsɪŋ/
noun
过度购买，超额采购
The act of buying more than is needed or can be used.
They regretted their overpurchasing of food for the party, as much of it went to waste.  他们后悔为聚会过度采购了食物，因为很多都浪费了。
Overpurchasing can lead to financial difficulties. 过度购买会导致财务困难。
synonyms: overbuying, excessive buying
antonyms: underpurchasing, insufficient buying
n.
