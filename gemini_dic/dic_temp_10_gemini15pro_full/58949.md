dustier:/ˈdʌstiər/ | adj. | 更有灰尘的；满是灰尘的 | Covered with more dust than something else; more covered in dust than before. | The attic was much dustier than I remembered. 阁楼比我记忆中满是灰尘。| The old books on the shelf were dustier than the rest.  架子上的旧书比其他的更积灰。 | 近义词: dirtier, grimier | 反义词: cleaner, tidier | 词性: 形容词

词源: From dust + -y (suffix forming adjectives) + -er (comparative suffix).  来自dust（灰尘） + -y（形容词后缀） + -er（比较级后缀）。
