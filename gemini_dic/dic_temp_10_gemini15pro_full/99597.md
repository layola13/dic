interiors:/ɪnˈtɪəriərz/
n.
内部；内饰；室内
the inside part of something, especially a building; the inside parts of a building, especially the walls, floors, and ceilings
例句：The interiors of the house were beautifully decorated.  这房子的内部装饰很漂亮。

例句：She specializes in designing stylish interiors for modern homes. 她专门为现代住宅设计时尚的内饰。
近义词：inside, interior design, inner parts
反义词：exterior, outside
词性：名词

interior:/ɪnˈtɪəriər/
n.
内部；内饰；室内
the inside part of something, especially a building; the inside parts of a building, especially the walls, floors, and ceilings
例句：The car's interior was spacious and comfortable. 这辆车的内部宽敞舒适。

例句：The interior of the church was dimly lit. 教堂内部光线昏暗。
近义词：inside, inner, internal
反义词：exterior, outer, external
词性：名词

interior:/ɪnˈtɪəriər/
adj.
内部的；里面的
inside; inner
例句：The interior walls were painted a soft blue. 内墙被漆成柔和的蓝色。

例句：The interior design of the office was modern and minimalist. 办公室的室内设计是现代简约风格的。
近义词：inner, inside, internal
反义词：exterior, outer, external
词性：形容词


词源：From French intérieur, from Latin interior, comparative of inter 'within'.
