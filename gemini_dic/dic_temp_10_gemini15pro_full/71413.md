flail:/fleɪl/| n. v. |连枷，打谷机; 挥动; 乱动 |A tool used for threshing grain, consisting of a wooden staff with a short, free-swinging stick attached to it. / To wave or swing wildly.| The farmer used a flail to separate the wheat from the chaff.  农民用连枷把麦粒从麦糠中分离出来。/  His arms flailed wildly as he fell. 他摔倒时双臂胡乱挥舞。 | 近义词: thrash, beat, flog, swing, wave | 反义词:  control, steady | 词性: 名词，动词


flails:/fleɪlz/| v. |挥动; 乱动 (flail的第三人称单数形式)|Third person singular present tense form of the verb "flail". | He flails his arms around when he's excited. 他激动的时候会挥舞手臂。/ The swimmer flails in the water, desperate to reach the shore.  游泳者在水中拼命挣扎，想要游到岸边。 | 近义词: thrashes, beats, flogs, swings, waves | 反义词: controls, steadies | 词性: 动词
