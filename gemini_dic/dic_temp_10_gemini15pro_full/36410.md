cojuror:/ˌkoʊˈdʒʊrər/ | n. | 共谋者; 帮凶; 共同宣誓者 | A person who swears an oath with another; a joint swearer.  A person who assists another in performing magic tricks. | The magician's cojuror helped him with the disappearing act. 魔术师的助手帮他完成了消失的表演。 |  accomplice, confederate, collaborator, assistant | opponent, adversary, enemy | noun
