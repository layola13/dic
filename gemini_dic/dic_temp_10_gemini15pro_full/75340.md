frosting:/ˈfrɒstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstɪŋ/ (US)| n.|糖霜，糖衣|A sweet creamy mixture used to cover or fill cakes, cookies, etc.|例句：The cake was covered in a thick layer of chocolate frosting. 蛋糕上覆盖着一层厚厚的巧克力糖霜。

例句：She piped pink frosting onto the cupcakes. 她把粉红色的糖霜挤到纸杯蛋糕上。

近义词：icing, glaze|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "filling" if contrasting with a cake's inside)|词性:noun


frosting:/ˈfrɒstɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstɪŋ/ (US)| n.|结霜，冰冻|The formation of frost.|例句：The car windows were covered with frosting. 车窗上覆盖着霜。

例句：The frosting on the ground made it slippery. 地面上的霜使地面很滑。

近义词：frost, ice|反义词:thawing, melting|词性:noun
