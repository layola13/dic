cellepore: /ˈsɛləˌpɔːr/ | n. | 小孔；微孔；（尤指珊瑚的）多孔结构 | A porous structure, especially in coral. |  Coral cellepores allow water to flow through. 珊瑚的多孔结构允许水流通过。 | 近义词: pore, opening, aperture | 反义词: solid | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Cellepore" likely originates from a combination of "celle" (from Latin "cella," meaning "small room" or "cell") and "pore" (from Latin "porus," meaning "passage" or "channel").  This reflects the structure of cellepores as small openings or chambers within a larger structure.
