what:/wɒt/(UK) /hwɑːt/(US) |pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| something, anything | nothing | pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast?  你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在看什么电视节目？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What did she say to you? 她对你说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before dinner? 晚饭前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你打算做什么？
What will she study in college? 她上大学后会学什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next year? 明年这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be wearing to the wedding? 她婚礼上会穿什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will she have learned by the end of the course? 到课程结束时她将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying for five years by graduation? 到毕业时她将已经学习五年什么？
