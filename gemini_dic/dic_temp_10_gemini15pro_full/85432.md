hasakura:/hɑːsɑːˈkʊrə/ | n. | 早樱; 初樱 |  A Japanese term combining "hasa" (early) and "sakura" (cherry blossom), referring to early-blooming cherry blossom varieties. |  The hasakura are already in bloom, heralding the arrival of spring. 早樱已经盛开，预示着春天的到来。 | The park is famous for its beautiful hasakura. 这个公园以美丽的早樱而闻名。 | synonyms: early-blooming cherry blossoms, | antonyms: late-blooming cherry blossoms | noun
