installed:/ɪnˈstɔːld/ | adj. | 已安装的，已设置的 | put (something) in position ready for use. | The plumber installed the new sink. 水管工安装了新的水槽。|  近义词：setup, established, implemented | 反义词: uninstalled, removed | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "install," which comes from Old French "installer," from Late Latin "installare," meaning "to place in a stall or seat."
