apprising:/əˈpraɪzɪŋ/
verb
通知；告知
informing someone about something
The lawyer is apprising his client of the latest developments in the case.  律师正在告知他的客户案件的最新进展。
He apprised me of the dangers of the journey. 他告知我旅途的危险。
近义词: informing, notifying, advising, telling
反义词: concealing, withholding, hiding
词性: verb
