galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/| vt.|激励；刺激；电镀；镀锌|To stimulate or excite as if by electric shock; to coat (iron or steel) with zinc.|The coach's speech galvanized the team into action. 教练的讲话激励了队员们行动起来。|近义词：stimulate, excite, arouse, invigorate, energize, electrify, jolt, shock, startle, animate, inspire, motivate, spur, prod, goad|反义词:demotivate, discourage, depress, calm, soothe|词性:动词

galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/| vt.|镀锌|To coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc.|The steel beams were galvanized to prevent rust. 钢梁镀锌以防生锈。|近义词：zinc-coat, plate|反义词:N/A|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *galvaniser*, from the name of Italian scientist Luigi Galvani (1737-1798), whose experiments with frogs' legs led to the discovery of galvanism (electricity produced by chemical action). The term was originally used in a physiological sense, referring to the stimulation of muscles by electricity.
