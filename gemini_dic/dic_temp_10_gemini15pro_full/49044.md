dehairs:/dɪˈhɛərz/
verb
去除毛发
removes hair from something, typically an animal hide.
The tannery dehairs cowhides before processing them into leather.  制革厂在将牛皮加工成皮革之前先去除毛发。
The machine efficiently dehairs the sheepskins.  这台机器能有效地去除羊皮上的毛。
近义词:  depilates, unhairs
反义词:  N/A
词性: verb

dehairing:/dɪˈhɛərɪŋ/
noun
脱毛;去毛
the process of removing hair from something, typically an animal hide.
The dehairing process is crucial in leather production. 脱毛过程在皮革生产中至关重要。
The dehairing of the rabbit pelts was done carefully. 兔皮的脱毛过程做得非常仔细。
近义词: depilation
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
