myrmicine:/maɪˈrmaɪsɪn/| adj. n.|蚁亚科的；蚁亚科动物|Relating to or belonging to the Myrmicinae, a large subfamily of ants.|例句：The myrmicine ants are known for their diverse behaviors. 蚁亚科的蚂蚁以其多样的行为而闻名。

例句：Researchers studied the myrmicine ant's complex social structure. 研究人员研究了蚁亚科蚂蚁复杂的社会结构。|近义词：ant (general term)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Modern Latin *Myrmicinae*, from Ancient Greek μύρμηξ (múrmēx, “ant”) +‎ -inae (subfamily suffix).
