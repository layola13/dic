historify:/hɪˈstɔːrɪfaɪ/
verb
使…成为历史；将…记载于史册
to make something historical or to record something in history.
The company aims to historify its founder's journey.  这家公司旨在将其创始人的历程记载于史册。
They are historifying the events of the war. 他们正在将战争的事件记录下来，使其成为历史。
chronicle, record, document
