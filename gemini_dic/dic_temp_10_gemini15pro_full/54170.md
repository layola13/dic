disentrail:/dɪsɛnˈtreɪl/ | vt. |  取出内脏; 剖腹 | To remove the entrails or intestines from; disembowel. |  The hunter disentrailed the deer. 猎人取出鹿的内脏。 | 近义词: disembowel, eviscerate | 反义词: None | 词性: 动词

词源:

From dis- + entrail.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," or indicating reversal or negation. "Entrail" refers to the internal organs, especially the intestines.
