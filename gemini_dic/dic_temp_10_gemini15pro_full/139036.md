oaky:/ˈəʊki/| adj.|橡木味的，像橡木的|Having the flavor or aroma of oak, especially oak barrels used in aging wine or spirits.  Relating to or resembling oak wood.|The wine has a pleasant oaky flavor. 这款酒有怡人的橡木味。|Synonyms: woody, oak-like|Antonyms: fruity, sweet|adjective
