disperser:/dɪˈspɜːrsər/ /dɪˈspɝːsɚ/| n.|分散器，散播器，驱散器|A device or agent that disperses something.|The farmer used a seed disperser to plant his crops.  农民用播种机播种庄稼。|synonyms: distributor, spreader, scatterer|antonyms: concentrator, collector, gatherer|noun


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ /dɪˈspɝːs/| v.|分散，散开，驱散|To distribute or spread over a wide area.|The crowd dispersed after the concert.  音乐会结束后人群散去。|synonyms: scatter, dissipate, disseminate|antonyms: gather, collect, assemble|verb
