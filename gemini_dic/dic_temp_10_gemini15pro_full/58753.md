dungmeer:/ˈdʌŋ.mɪər/
n.
粪海，粪堆
A large accumulation of animal dung.
The farmer had to clear the dungmeer in the field.  农民不得不清理田里的粪堆。
dung heap, manure pile
N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Afrikaans *dungmeer*, from Dutch *dung* (dung) + *meer* (lake, sea).  So literally "dung sea" or "dung lake".
