gaudless:/ˈɡɔːdləs/
adjective
无装饰的，朴素的
Lacking gaudiness or ostentation; plain or simple.
The gaudless room was sparsely furnished with only a bed and a chair.  朴素的房间里只有sparsely furnished一张床和一把椅子。
Her gaudless attire reflected her humble nature. 她朴素的着装反映了她谦逊的性格。
近义词:simple, plain, unadorned, austere, undecorated
反义词:gaudy, ornate, flamboyant, ostentatious
词性:adjective

词源:
From gaud + -less.  Gaud comes from the Old North French gaude, "jest, trick, adornment".
