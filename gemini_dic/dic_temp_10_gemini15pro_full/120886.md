misbuilding:/ˌmɪsˈbɪldɪŋ/
n.
错误的建筑; 构建错误;
A building that is poorly constructed or designed.  The act of building something incorrectly.
The misbuilding of the bridge led to its collapse.  这座桥的错误建造导致了它的倒塌。
misconstruction, faulty construction
correct construction, sound building
noun
