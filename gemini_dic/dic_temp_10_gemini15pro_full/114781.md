maltobiose: /ˌmɔːltəˈbaɪoʊs/ | n. |麦芽二糖|A disaccharide formed from two glucose units joined by an α-1,4 glycosidic bond. It is a reducing sugar. It is formed during the digestion of starch by amylase and is further hydrolyzed to glucose by maltase.|Maltobiose is a product of starch hydrolysis.|近义词: disaccharide |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific sugar) | noun
