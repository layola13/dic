merism:/ˈmɛrɪzəm/ | n. | 部分列举 | A figure of speech in which a single thing is referred to by a conventional phrase that enumerates some of its parts, or which lists several synonyms or related things |  "Lock, stock, and barrel" is a merism for a complete rifle.  “枪机、枪托和枪管”是用部分列举来指代完整的步枪。 |  synecdoche, enumeration | N/A | noun
