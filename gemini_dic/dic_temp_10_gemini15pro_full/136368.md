nonresponders:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːn.dərz/ | n. | 未答复者，无应答者 | People who do not respond to a survey, request, or treatment. |  The researchers sent out 300 surveys, but 50% were returned by nonresponders. 研究人员发出了300份调查问卷，但50%被退回，因为收件人没有回复。|  Example: The nonresponders to the survey skewed the results.  这项调查的无应答者扭曲了结果。| Synonyms: non-participants,  no-shows | Antonyms: responders, participants | noun
