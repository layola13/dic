granitelike:/ˈɡrænɪtˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像花岗岩的；坚硬的 | Resembling granite in hardness, texture, or appearance.  | The cliff face was granitelike in its solidity.  悬崖表面坚硬得像花岗岩。 | Near synonyms: granitic, stony, rocklike, hard | Near antonyms: soft, yielding, pliable | adjective
