panellists:/ˈpænəlɪsts/ | n. | 专家小组成员 | A group of specialists assembled to offer advice or make judgments. | The panellists judged the entries in the art competition. 专家小组成员评判了艺术比赛的参赛作品。 | synonyms: experts, judges, assessors | antonyms: amateurs, novices | noun
