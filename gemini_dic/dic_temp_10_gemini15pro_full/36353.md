coinable:/ˈkɔɪnəbl/| adj.|可铸造的，可创造的（新词语等）|Something that is coinable can be made into coins (of metal) or can be invented (of words, phrases, etc.).|例句：A new word for this concept is easily coinable.  这个概念很容易造出一个新词。

例句：Gold is a coinable metal. 黄金是一种可以铸造硬币的金属。|近义词：mintable, creatable, inventable|反义词：uncoined, nonexistent|词性:adjective
