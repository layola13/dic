acetabulum: /ˌæsəˈtæbjʊləm/ (US), /ˌæsɪˈtæbjʊləm/ (UK)| n. | 髋臼；臼窝 |The acetabulum is the socket of the hipbone, into which the head of the femur fits. | The surgeon repaired the fracture in the acetabulum.  外科医生修复了髋臼的骨折。

The ball-and-socket joint of the hip is formed by the head of the femur fitting into the acetabulum.  股骨头与髋臼嵌合形成髋关节的球窝关节。| 近义词: hip socket | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially the head of the femur as the complementary structure) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acētabulum* "small vinegar cup," from *acētum* "vinegar" (from *ācēre* "to be sour") + *-bulum*, a suffix denoting an instrument or place.  The shape of the hip socket resembled a small Roman vinegar cup.
