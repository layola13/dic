lunitidal:/ˌluːnɪˈtaɪdəl/
adjective
月潮的，受月球潮汐影响的
Relating to or caused by the combined action of the moon and tides; influenced by the moon's gravity and the tides.
The lunitidal interval is the time between the moon's transit and the next high tide. 月潮间隔是指月亮凌日和下次涨潮之间的时间。
The study of lunitidal influences on coastal ecosystems is crucial for understanding their dynamics. 研究月潮对沿海生态系统的影响对于理解其动态至关重要。
synonym: tidal, moon-influenced
antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "non-tidal" or "terrestrial" in certain contexts.)
词性: adjective
