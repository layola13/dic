burned:/bɜːrnd/ (UK) /bɝnd/ (US)| v. adj.|烧伤的，烧焦的；燃烧的|past tense and past participle of burn.  The process of something being consumed by fire, or showing the effects of fire.|The house burned down last night. 昨晚房子烧毁了。|近义词：scorched, singed, charred, ablaze|反义词:extinguished, unburned|词性:动词(过去式/过去分词), 形容词


burned:/bɜːrnd/ (UK) /bɝnd/ (US)| adj.|烧伤的，烧焦的|damaged or injured by burning.|He suffered burned hands in the fire. 他的手在火灾中被烧伤了。|近义词：scalded, seared|反义词:unburned, healed|词性:形容词


Etymology: From Middle English *brennen*, from Old English *bærnan* (“to burn, kindle”), from Proto-Germanic *brannjaną* (“to burn, shine”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“bright, high”).
