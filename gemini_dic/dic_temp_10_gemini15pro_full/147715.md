overpressure:/ˈoʊvərˌprɛʃər/| n.|超压，过压|An increase in pressure above the normal or expected pressure.  This can refer to various contexts, from physical systems like pipelines to geological formations or even psychological stress.|The overpressure in the pipeline caused a rupture. 管道中的超压导致了破裂。|synonyms: excess pressure, high pressure, overinflation|antonyms: underpressure, low pressure|noun
