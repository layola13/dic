manning-up:/ˈmænɪŋ ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 振作起来；拿出男子气概；承担责任 | To act bravely or responsibly, typically in a way traditionally associated with men. It implies overcoming fear or hesitation and taking charge of a situation. | Example: He finally manned up and apologized for his mistake. 他最终振作起来，为自己的错误道歉。

Example:  She manned up and confronted the bully. 她勇敢地面对了恶霸。 | Near Synonyms: toughen up, step up, take responsibility, be brave, show courage | Near Antonyms: back down, chicken out, wimp out | Verb Phrase
