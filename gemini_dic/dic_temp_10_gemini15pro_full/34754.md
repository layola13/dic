cliff-like:/ˈklɪfˌlaɪk/
adjective
像悬崖的；峭壁似的
Resembling a cliff; steep and rugged.
The coastline was cliff-like, with sheer drops into the ocean.  海岸线像悬崖一样，陡峭地落入海洋。
The building's cliff-like facade was imposing.  这座建筑像悬崖一样的正面雄伟壮观。

近义词:precipitous, sheer, steep
反义词:gentle, sloping, gradual
词性:adjective
