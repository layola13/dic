overthoughtfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈθɔːtfəlnəs/
n.
过度思虑，想得太多
The state of thinking too much about something, often to the point of anxiety or inaction.
Her overthoughtfulness often prevented her from making decisions. 她过度思虑经常让她无法做出决定。
overthinking, rumination
decisiveness, spontaneity
noun
