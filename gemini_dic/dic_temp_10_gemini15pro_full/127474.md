necessitated:/nəˈsesɪteɪtɪd/
verb
需要；使成为必需
made necessary; required
The increase in population necessitated a bigger school. 人口增长使得需要一所更大的学校。
Synonyms: required, demanded, called for
Antonyms: obviated, precluded
词性: 动词
