filially:/ˈfɪliəli/ | adv. |孝顺地，子女似地 | In a manner befitting a son or daughter; dutifully. | She cared for her aging parents filially. 她孝顺地照顾年迈的父母。|  He visited his mother filially every week. 他每周都孝顺地去看望他的母亲。 | 近义词: dutifully, obediently, respectfully | 反义词: disobediently, disrespectfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *filialiter, from Latin filius (“son”).
