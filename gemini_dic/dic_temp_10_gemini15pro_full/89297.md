homoeopathic:/ˌhəʊmiəˈpæθɪk/ (also homeopathic) | adj. |顺势疗法的|Relating to or characterized by homeopathy, a system of medicine based on the principle that "like cures like." | Example: She opted for a homoeopathic remedy for her allergies. 她选择了顺势疗法来治疗她的过敏。| Example:  He's a strong believer in homoeopathic medicine. 他坚信顺势疗法。| Synonym: homeopathic | Antonym: allopathic, conventional | word type: adjective

homoeopathy:/ˌhəʊmiˈɒpəθi/ (also homeopathy) | n. |顺势疗法|A system of treating disease by the administration of minute doses of a remedy that would in larger amounts produce in healthy persons symptoms similar to those of the disease. | Example:  She turned to homeopathy after conventional medicine failed to help. 在传统医学未能奏效后，她转向了顺势疗法。 | Example: The effectiveness of homeopathy is still debated. 顺势疗法的有效性仍在争论中。| Synonym:  homeopathy | Antonym: allopathy, conventional medicine | word type: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek homoeopathēs, from homoios 'similar' + pathos 'suffering'.
