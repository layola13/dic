biogases:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɡæsɪz/| n. |沼气|A mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide, produced by the breakdown of organic matter in the absence of oxygen.  It is often used as a fuel.| Biogases from the landfill site are used to generate electricity. 垃圾填埋场的沼气被用来发电。| synonyms: biogas, methane, landfill gas | antonyms:  (Biogases doesn't have a direct antonym.
