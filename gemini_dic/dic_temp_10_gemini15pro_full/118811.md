metamorphist:/ˌmetəˈmɔːrfɪst/
n.
变质学家；变质岩学者
A scientist who studies metamorphism and metamorphic rocks.
The metamorphist examined the thin section of the rock under the microscope.  变质学家在显微镜下检查了岩石的薄片。
