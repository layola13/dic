handman:/ˈhændmæn/| n. |杂工，修理工|A person employed to do various small jobs, typically around a house or business.|例句：We hired a handyman to fix the leaky faucet. 我们雇了一个杂工来修理漏水的水龙头。

例句：The handyman repaired the broken fence and mowed the lawn.  修理工修理了坏了的栅栏，还修剪了草坪。

近义词：repairman, fixer, odd-job man, maintenance man|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑specialist专家)|词性:noun
