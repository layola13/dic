outfroth: /ˌaʊtˈfrɒθ/ (British) /ˌaʊtˈfrɔːθ/ (American)
|v.| 吐出泡沫；胜过，超过 (比喻) |To emit or discharge like froth; to surpass or exceed in frothing or effervescence; figuratively, to surpass or excel. | The volcano outfrothed molten lava and ash. 火山喷涌出熔岩和火山灰。

| | | The competitor outfrothed everyone else in the beer-chugging contest.  在啤酒大赛中，这位选手胜过了所有其他人。 | 近义词: surpass, excel, outdo, outperform, exceed | 反义词:  lose to, be inferior to | 词性: 动词
