evasively:/ɪˈveɪsɪvli/| adv.|逃避地，推诿地，含糊其辞地|In a manner that is not direct or clear, tending to avoid commitment or answering questions fully.|He answered evasively when asked about his plans. 当被问及他的计划时，他含糊其辞。|近义词：ambiguously, indirectly, vaguely|反义词：directly, frankly, openly|词性:副词
Etymology: From evade + -ive + -ly.  Evade comes from Late Latin ēvādere "to escape," from ex- "out" + vādere "to go."
