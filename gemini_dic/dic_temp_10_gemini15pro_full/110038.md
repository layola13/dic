license:/ˈlaɪsns/ (US), /ˈlaɪsəns/ (UK)| n. vt.|许可证，执照；特许；准许；放肆|A license is an official document that gives you permission to do, use, or have something.|例句：Do you have a driver's license? 你有驾驶执照吗？

例句：He received a license to operate a radio station. 他获得了经营广播电台的许可证。|近义词：permit, authorization, certificate|反义词:prohibition, ban|词性:名词, 动词

license:/ˈlaɪsns/ (US), /ˈlaɪsəns/ (UK)| vt.|许可，授权；特许|To license someone is to give them official permission to do, use, or have something.|例句：The software is licensed under the GNU General Public License.  这款软件是在GNU通用公共许可证下授权的。

例句：They licensed the company to produce the drug. 他们授权该公司生产这种药物。|近义词：authorize, permit, allow|反义词:forbid, prohibit|词性:动词
