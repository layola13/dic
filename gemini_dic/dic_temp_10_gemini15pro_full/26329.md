buzz-cut:/ˈbʌzkʌt/ | n. |寸头, 平头 |A very short haircut in which the hair is clipped close to the head.| He got a buzz-cut for the summer.  他夏天剪了个寸头。| 近义词:crew cut, short haircut | 反义词:long hair | noun

buzz-cut:/ˈbʌzkʌt/ | v. | 剪寸头 |To cut hair very short.|  He buzz-cut his hair every month. 他每个月都剪寸头。| 近义词:crop, shave | 反义词:grow (hair) | verb

词源:  The term "buzz-cut" is onomatopoeic, referring to the buzzing sound made by electric clippers used to create this style.
