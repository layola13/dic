modulo:/ˈmɒdjʊləʊ/ (UK), /ˈmɑːdʒʊloʊ/ (US)| prep. conj. n. |模; 模数; 以...为模|A mathematical operation that finds the remainder after division of one number by another (called the modulus of the operation).  In computing, it is often represented by the symbol %.|

例句：
15 modulo 4 is 3.
15 模 4 等于 3。

The calculation is done modulo 7.
计算以 7 为模。

近义词：mod, remainder (when used as a noun)
反义词: N/A
词性: preposition, conjunction, noun
