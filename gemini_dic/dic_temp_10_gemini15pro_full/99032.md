interalliance: /ˌɪntərəˈlaɪəns/
n.
联盟; 结合; 结盟;
An alliance between different groups or organizations, especially between nations.
例句：The interalliance between the two companies strengthened their market position.  两家公司之间的联盟巩固了它们的市场地位。

例句：The wartime interalliance proved crucial for victory.  战时的联盟对胜利至关重要。

近义词：alliance, coalition, partnership, union
反义词：division, separation, disunity

词性: 名词


词源:  From "inter-" (between) + "alliance" (a union or association formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries or organizations). "Alliance" comes from Old French "aliance,"  from "alier" (to ally), from Latin "alligare" (to bind to).
