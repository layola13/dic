legislating:/ˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪŋ/
v.
立法；制定法律
The act of making or enacting laws.
Congress is responsible for legislating on national issues. 国会负责制定国家问题的法律。
legislative, lawmaking, enacting, ordaining
repealing, abolishing, nullifying
