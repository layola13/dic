antipredator:/ˌæntiˈprɛdətər/ | adj. | 反捕食者的，反掠夺者的 | Relating to or denoting defenses or adaptations evolved to reduce the risk of being eaten by predators. |  Many animals have developed antipredator adaptations, such as camouflage or toxins. 许多动物进化出了反捕食者的适应性，例如伪装或毒素。| synonyms: defensive, protective | antonyms: predatory, offensive | adjective

antipredator:/ˌæntiˈprɛdətər/ | n. | 反捕食者 | An animal that has developed defenses or adaptations to reduce the risk of being eaten by predators. | The poison dart frog is a well-known antipredator due to its bright colors and toxic skin. 箭毒蛙因其鲜艳的颜色和有毒的皮肤而成为著名的反捕食者。 | synonyms: prey animal, defender | antonyms: predator, attacker | noun
