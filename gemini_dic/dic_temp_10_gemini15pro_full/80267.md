goggly:/ˈɡɒɡli/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːɡli/ (US)| adj.|瞪眼看的，凸眼的|Having large, staring, and slightly protruding eyes, often as a result of surprise or excitement.|例句：He gave me a goggly look when I told him the news. 当我告诉他这个消息时，他瞪大了眼睛看着我。

例句：The children were goggly-eyed with wonder at the magic show. 孩子们惊奇地瞪大了眼睛看着魔术表演。


近义词：wide-eyed, staring, pop-eyed|反义词: unexpressive, impassive|词性:形容词
