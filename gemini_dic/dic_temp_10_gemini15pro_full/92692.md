icebreaker:/ˈaɪsˌbreɪkər/| n.|破冰游戏，破冰活动；破冰船|Something that is done or said to relieve tension or get conversation started, especially at a social gathering where people do not know each other well.  Also, a ship designed to break through ice.|We played a few icebreakers to get everyone relaxed. 我们玩了一些破冰游戏，让大家放松一下。|synonyms: conversation starter, warm-up activity, introductory game|antonyms: awkward silence, tension builder|noun
