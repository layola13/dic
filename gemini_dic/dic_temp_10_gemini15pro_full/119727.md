midsummery:/ˌmɪdˈsʌməri/ | adj. | 仲夏的，典型的仲夏的 | Suggestive of midsummer; characteristic of midsummer. |  The air was warm and fragrant, a truly midsummery scent. 空气温暖芬芳，弥漫着真正的仲夏气息。

| The weather was glorious and midsummery. 天气晴朗，正是仲夏时节。 | 近义词: summery, estival | 反义词: wintry, hibernal | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From midsummer + -y.  Midsummer comes from Middle English *mydsomer*, from Old English *middesumer*, from *midde* ("middle") + *sumor* ("summer").
