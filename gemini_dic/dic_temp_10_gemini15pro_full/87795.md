hibiscus: /hɪˈbɪskəs/ | n. |木槿；芙蓉；玫瑰茄| A shrub or small tree of the mallow family, bearing large, showy, variously colored flowers. The flowers are used in salads and to make beverages. |  例句：The hibiscus flowers in her garden were a vibrant red. 她花园里的木槿花是鲜艳的红色。

例句：Hibiscus tea is a popular drink in many cultures.  木槿茶是许多文化中流行的饮品。| 近义词：rose mallow | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词
