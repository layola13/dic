ogham:/ˈoʊəm/ /ˈɒɡəm/
noun
欧甘文
An ancient alphabet used to write the early Irish language, consisting of a series of strokes and notches cut across a central stem line.
The Ogham inscription on the stone was difficult to decipher.  这块石头上的欧甘铭文很难破译。
近义词: script, alphabet
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific alphabet)
词性: n.

Etymology:
From Old Irish *ogam, perhaps related to og- "point, edge".
