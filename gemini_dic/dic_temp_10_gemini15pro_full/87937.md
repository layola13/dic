heist: /haɪst/ | n. |抢劫, 盗窃| An act of robbery, especially a large or daring one. |例句：The bank heist was planned meticulously.  银行抢劫案经过了精心策划。

例句：They pulled off a daring art heist. 他们成功地进行了一次大胆的艺术品盗窃。|近义词：robbery, theft, burglary |反义词: donation, gift |词性: noun
