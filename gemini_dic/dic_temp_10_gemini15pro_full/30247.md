celluloid: /ˈsɛljʊlɔɪd/
noun | 赛璐珞; 电影胶片 | A flammable plastic material made from nitrocellulose and camphor, formerly used for photographic film. | Old movies are sometimes referred to as "celluloid dreams."  老电影有时被称为“赛璐珞之梦”。| synonyms: filmstock, film | antonyms: digital media | n.

celluloids: /ˈsɛljʊlɔɪdz/
noun | 赛璐珞制品; 电影 | Items made from celluloid, especially old movies. |  Many early celluloids have been lost due to their flammable nature. 许多早期的赛璐珞电影因为其易燃性而失传。 | synonyms: films, movies | antonyms: digital films | n.
