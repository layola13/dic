hygiene:/ˈhaɪdʒiːn/ | n. |卫生；保健法|Conditions or practices conducive to maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness.|例句：Good hygiene is essential for preventing the spread of infection. 良好的卫生习惯对于预防感染的传播至关重要。|例句：He has very poor personal hygiene. 他的个人卫生很差。|近义词：cleanliness, sanitation|反义词：filth, dirtiness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *hygiène*, from Late Latin *hygieina*, from Greek *hygieinē (tekhnē)* “art of health”, from *hygies* "healthy, sound," ultimately from PIE *ugi- "vigorous, strong".
