demonstration:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃn/ | n. | 示范，演示；游行示威；证明，表明 | An act of showing how something works or how to do something; a public display of group opinion, often a march or rally; proof or evidence. | The salesperson gave a demonstration of the new vacuum cleaner.  售货员演示了新的吸尘器。| synonyms: display, exhibition, presentation, protest, march, rally, proof, evidence | antonyms: concealment, suppression, refutation | noun
