benevolentness:/bəˈnevələntnəs/
n.
仁慈；善心；慈善
The quality of being well meaning and kindly.
He treated his employees with benevolentness and understanding. 他以仁慈和理解的态度对待他的员工。
Her benevolentness extended to all creatures, great and small. 她的仁慈惠及所有生物，无论大小。
kindness, generosity, charity, compassion, goodwill
malevolence, cruelty, hostility, unkindness
noun
