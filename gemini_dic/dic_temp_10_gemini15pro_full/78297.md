germinators:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtərz/
n.
发芽器；使发芽的东西
A device or environment that encourages seeds to sprout.
The farmer placed the seeds in germinators to ensure a higher success rate.  农民将种子放入发芽器中以确保更高的成功率。
The warm, damp cloth acted as a simple germinator for the beans.  温暖潮湿的布充当了豆子的简单发芽器。
incubators, propagators
inhibitors
noun
