auxometer:/ɔːkˈsɒmɪtər/ | n. | 膨胀计，生长计 | An instrument for measuring linear expansion or growth, especially of plant parts. |  The botanist used an auxometer to track the growth of the seedling. 植物学家使用膨胀计来追踪幼苗的生长。|  The auxometer provided precise measurements of the stem's elongation. 膨胀计提供了茎伸长率的精确测量。 |  dilatometer, extensometer | antonym: None (lack of a direct antonym for a measuring instrument)| noun
