ossuarium:/ˈɒsjuːɛːriəm/ /ˈɑːsjuːɛːriəm/
noun
骨灰存放处；藏骨堂
A container or room in which the bones of dead people are placed.
The archaeologist discovered an ossuarium containing the remains of hundreds of individuals. 考古学家发现了一个藏骨堂，里面有数百人的遗骸。

The ossuarium held the bones of monks who had died over several centuries.  这个藏骨堂存放着几个世纪以来去世的僧侣的骨头。

synonym: charnel house, bone house, crypt
antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps cemetery or graveyard as a place where bodies, not just bones, are interred.)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ossuarium, from Latin os "bone" + -arium "-ary."
