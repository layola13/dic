fissionability:/ˌfɪʃənəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可裂变性；裂变能力 | The capability of a substance, especially a heavy atom's nucleus, to undergo nuclear fission. |  The fissionability of uranium-235 makes it suitable for nuclear reactors. 铀-235的可裂变性使其适用于核反应堆。| synonym: fissile, cleavable | antonym: non-fissile, stable | noun
