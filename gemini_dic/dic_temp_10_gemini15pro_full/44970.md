crystalliform:/krɪˈstælɪfɔːrm/
adjective
水晶形的，晶状的
Resembling crystal in form. Having the external form of a crystal.
The mineral deposit exhibited a beautiful crystalliform structure. 矿床呈现出美丽的晶状结构。
The cave walls were covered in crystalliform formations. 洞穴墙壁上覆盖着晶状结构。
近义词: crystalline, crystal-like, crystalloid
反义词: amorphous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "crystal" + "-iform" (a suffix meaning "having the form of").  "Crystal" comes from the Greek "krystallos," meaning "ice," later applied to quartz crystal, as it was believed to be permanently frozen ice.
