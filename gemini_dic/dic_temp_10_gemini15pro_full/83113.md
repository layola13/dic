gusted:/ˈɡʌstɪd/ | v. | (风)猛烈地吹; (感情)迸发 | (of wind) blow in gusts; (of feelings) burst forth suddenly and intensely. | Anger gusted through him.  怒火在他心中迸发。| Synonyms: surge, rush, flare | Antonyms: subside, wane | verb
