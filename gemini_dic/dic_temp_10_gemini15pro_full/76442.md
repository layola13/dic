gaingiving:/ˈɡeɪnˌɡɪvɪŋ/
n.
收益分配; 利益共享
The gaingiving plan allows employees to share in the company's profits.  利益共享计划允许员工分享公司的利润。
Gaingiving is a way to motivate employees and improve productivity. 收益分配是激励员工和提高生产力的一种方式。
