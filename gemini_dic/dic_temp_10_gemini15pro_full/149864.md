palfrey:/ˈpælfʊri/ | n. | 骑乘的马；游侠的马；女士的马 | A horse suitable for riding, especially one used by women or for leisurely riding.  A gentle horse for ordinary riding, especially for a woman. |  She rode her palfrey through the forest. 她骑着她的马穿过森林。| The knight's palfrey was a magnificent white steed. 骑士的坐骑是一匹壮丽的白马。| synonyms: steed, courser, charger | antonyms: nag, jade | noun
