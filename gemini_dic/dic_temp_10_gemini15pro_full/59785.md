ecotok: /ˈiːkəˌtɒk/ (US) /ˈiːkəˌtɒk/ (UK) | n. | 生态象征; 生态标记 |  A symbol or mark that signifies a product or service is environmentally friendly or sustainable. It can be a certification, label, or logo. |  The company uses an ecotok on its packaging to show its commitment to sustainability.  这家公司在其包装上使用生态标记，以表明其对可持续发展的承诺。 | Synonyms: ecolabel, green label, environmental certification | Antonyms:  None (a direct antonym is conceptually difficult; perhaps unsustainable label) | Noun
