exorcismal:/ˈɛksɔːsɪzməl/ |adj.|驱魔的，驱邪的|Relating to or characteristic of exorcism; used in exorcism.|The priest performed the exorcismal ritual.  神父执行了驱魔仪式。|Example: The exorcismal rites were performed in Latin.  驱魔仪式是用拉丁语进行的。|Synonyms: purifying, cleansing, ritualistic|Antonyms:  blessing, consecrating|adjective


exorcism:/ˈɛksɔːsɪzəm/ |n.|驱魔，驱邪|The act or practice of expelling evil spirits from a person or place, especially by means of prayers or religious rituals.|The family sought an exorcism for their troubled son.  这家人为他们陷入困境的儿子寻求驱魔。|Example: The exorcism was conducted by a local priest.  驱魔仪式由当地一位牧师主持。|Synonyms: purification, cleansing, expulsion|Antonyms:  blessing, consecration|noun
