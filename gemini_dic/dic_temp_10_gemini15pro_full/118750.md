metallic:/məˈtælɪk/| adj.|金属的；金属般的；有金属特性的|Made of, containing, or resembling metal; having the sheen or luster of metal.|例句：The car has a metallic blue paint. 这辆车有金属感的蓝色油漆。|近义词：metal, silvery, steely|反义词:nonmetallic, dull|词性:形容词
词源:From French *métallique*, from Late Latin *metallicus*, from Latin *metallum* (“metal”).
