inequidistant:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɪdɪstənt/
adjective
不相等的；不等距的
Not equally distant.
The points plotted on the graph were inequidistant, reflecting the irregular intervals of data collection.  图表上标出的点是不等距的，反映了数据收集的不规则间隔。
The fence posts were inequidistant, giving the fence a slightly uneven appearance.  栅栏柱的间距不相等，使栅栏看起来略有不平。
近义词: uneven, unequal, irregular, nonuniform
反义词: equidistant, uniform, regular, even
词性: adjective
词源: in- (not) + equi- (equal) + distant
