fluxible:/ˈflʌksɪbl/ |adj.|易变的，可变通的| readily or constantly changing : likely to change|The political situation is highly fluxible. 政治局势高度易变。|例句：The market is fluxible and prices change daily. 市场变化无常，价格每天都在变动。|近义词：changeable, variable, adaptable, flexible, fluid|反义词：inflexible, rigid, unchangeable, fixed, stable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin fluxibilis, from fluere "to flow".
