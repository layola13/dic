crustacea:/krʌˈsteɪʃə/ | n. | 甲壳纲动物 |A large subphylum of arthropods, typically having a segmented body, a hard exoskeleton, and paired, jointed limbs.  They include crabs, lobsters, shrimps, barnacles, and woodlice.| Lobsters and crabs are types of crustacea.  龙虾和螃蟹是甲壳类动物。 | synonyms: shellfish, seafood | antonyms: None | noun

crustacean:/krʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 甲壳纲动物 |An arthropod of the large, mainly aquatic subphylum Crustacea, such as a crab, lobster, shrimp, or barnacle.| The shrimp is a small crustacean.  虾是一种小型甲壳类动物。 | synonyms: shellfish, seafood | antonyms: None | noun
