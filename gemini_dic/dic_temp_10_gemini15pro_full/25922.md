burnings:/ˈbɜːrnɪŋz/
n.|燃烧；烧伤；烧灼感|The act of something being on fire, or the feeling of being burned.|The burnings from the fire were severe. 火灾造成的烧伤很严重。|近义词：fires, blazes, combustion, scorchings|反义词:extinguishing, cooling|词性:名词


Etymology:  From the Old English *byrning*, present participle of *bærnan* "to burn," from Proto-Germanic *brennaną*.
