housemasterships:/ˈhaʊsˌmæstərʃɪps/
|n.|舍监职位；舍监职务|The responsibilities and duties associated with being a housemaster, often in a boarding school context.  This refers to the position itself, not the person holding it.|The school had several housemasterships available.|Synonym: housemaster positions, residential faculty positions|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "student body" or "pupil roles" as contrasting concepts)|noun
