alliterative:/əˈlɪtərətɪv/| adj.|押头韵的；头韵的|Containing or characterized by alliteration (the repetition of initial consonant sounds).|Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers is an alliterative tongue twister.|近义词：consonantal, rhyming|反义词:non-alliterative|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin alliterativus, from Latin ad- 'to' + littera 'letter'.
