handbrake:/ˈhændbreɪk/| n.|手刹; 手闸|A lever used to apply the brakes of a vehicle, typically a car, usually by mechanical linkage.  It is often used to prevent the vehicle from rolling when parked.|I always put the handbrake on when parked on a hill. 我在山上停车时总是拉上手刹。|Synonyms: parking brake, emergency brake|Antonyms: accelerator, gas pedal|noun
