mumps:/mʌmps/| n.|腮腺炎|An infectious disease characterized by swelling of the salivary glands, especially the parotid glands. |例句：He caught the mumps from his classmate. 他从同学那里感染了腮腺炎。

例句：Mumps is a highly contagious viral illness. 腮腺炎是一种高度传染的病毒性疾病。|近义词：parotitis|反义词:|noun
