adaptations:/ˌædæpˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 适应; 改编; 改作 | The process of change by which an organism or species becomes better suited to its environment.  A film, television drama, or stage play that has been adapted from a novel or other source. |  Desert animals have remarkable adaptations for dealing with the harsh environment. 沙漠动物对严酷环境有着非凡的适应能力。

The movie is an adaptation of a popular novel. 这部电影是由一部畅销小说改编的。 | synonyms: adjustments, modifications, alterations, conversions | antonyms: maladaptations | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin adaptātiōn- (stem of adaptātiō ), from adaptāre ‘to adapt’
