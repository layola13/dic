corroboratively:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtɪvli/
adverb
确证地，证实地
in a way that confirms or supports a statement, theory, or finding.
The witness corroborated the victim's account.  证人证实了受害人的说法。
The forensic evidence corroboratively supported the eyewitness testimony. 法医证据确证地支持了目击者的证词。
Synonyms: confirmingly, supportively, substantively
Antonyms: contradictorily, refutingly
词性: 副词
