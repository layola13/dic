blurriest:/ˈblɜːriɪst/ | adj. | 最模糊的 | Superlative form of blurry; most blurry.  Most out of focus; most indistinct visually. | The photo was so blurry, the blurriest I'd ever seen. 这张照片非常模糊，是我见过的最模糊的。| The CCTV footage was the blurriest, making identification impossible. 闭路电视录像最模糊，无法辨认。 | 近义词: fuzziest, haziest, indistinctest, vaguest | 反义词: clearest, sharpest | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From *blurry* + *-est*. *Blurry* comes from *blur* (verb), which likely originates from a Scandinavian source, related to Old Norse *blunda* "to close one's eyes." The suffix *-est* is a common superlative ending in English.
