ovicell: /ˈoʊvɪˌsɛl/ | n. | 卵胞 | A small chamber in a bryozoan colony that houses the embryo during development.| The ovicell protects the developing bryozoan larvae.  卵胞保护发育中的苔藓虫幼虫。|  brood chamber,  |  | noun
Etymology: From Latin *ōvicella*, diminutive of *ōviculum*, from *ōvum* (egg).
