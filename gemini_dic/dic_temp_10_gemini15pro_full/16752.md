barton:/ˈbɑːrtn/| n. |农场；谷仓；牲畜棚|A barton is a farmyard, or the buildings of a farm.  It originates from Old English "bere-tun" meaning "barley farm".|
The children played in the barton while the farmers worked. 孩子们在农场里玩耍，农民们在干活。

The old barton was converted into luxury apartments.  旧农场被改建成了豪华公寓。|近义词：farmyard, homestead, grange|反义词:city, town|词性:noun
