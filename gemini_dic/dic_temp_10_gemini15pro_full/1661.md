acknowledging: /əkˈnɒlɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. |承认，认可；感谢，答谢|The company acknowledged receiving the complaint.  该公司承认收到了投诉。|例句：He acknowledged his mistake. 他承认了他的错误。

例句：She acknowledged the applause with a gracious bow. 她优雅地鞠躬答谢掌声。

近义词：admitting, recognizing, confessing, thanking, appreciating|反义词：denying, rejecting, ignoring, disregarding|词性：动词

词源：From Middle English *knowlechen, from Anglo-Norman *conuiss-, present participle stem of conoistre “to know,” from Latin cognōscere (perfect active cognovi, supine cognitum) “to get to know, become acquainted with, learn,” from com- “together” + gnōscō “I come to know”.  The prefix ac- is a variant of ad-.
