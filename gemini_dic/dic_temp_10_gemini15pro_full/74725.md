freer:/ˈfriːər/| adj.|更自由的，更无拘无束的|Comparative form of "free," meaning less restricted, less constrained, or having more liberty.|例句：The bird felt freer outside its cage. 这只鸟在笼子外面感觉更自由了。|近义词：more liberated, more unrestricted, looser|反义词:more restricted, more confined, tighter|词性:形容词

freer:/ˈfriːər/| n.|释放者，解放者 (古语，罕用)|One who frees or releases from captivity or bondage.|例句：He acted as a freer of the slaves. 他充当了奴隶的解放者。|近义词：liberator, emancipator|反义词:captor, enslaver|词性:名词
