nonmutually:/nɒnˈmjuːtʃuəli/ | adv. | 非互相地，不相互地 | Not mutually; in a way that is not shared or reciprocal. | The two events influenced each other nonmutually; one significantly impacted the other, but not vice-versa. 这两个事件相互影响是非相互的；一个事件显著地影响了另一个事件，但反之则不然。 |  The agreement benefited both parties nonmutually; one gained significantly more than the other. 该协议对双方的利益并非互惠互利；一方获得的利益明显大于另一方。| 近义词: unilaterally, independently | 反义词: mutually, reciprocally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"Non-" (prefix): From Latin "non" meaning "not".
"Mutually": From Late Middle English "mutualy", from Old French "mutuel", ultimately from Latin "mutuus" meaning "reciprocal, exchanged, lent, borrowed".
