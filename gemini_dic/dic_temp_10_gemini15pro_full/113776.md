macroscopically:/ˌmækrəˈskɑːpɪkli/ | adv. | 宏观地；肉眼可见地 |  From a large-scale perspective; visible to the naked eye.  | The tumor was macroscopically evident.  肿瘤肉眼可见。 | Synonyms: visibly, observably, noticeably | Antonyms: microscopically, invisibly | adverb

macroscopical:/ˌmækrəˈskɑːpɪkəl/ | adj. | 宏观的；肉眼可见的 | Relating to large-scale properties or phenomena; visible without magnification. |  The macroscopical examination revealed no abnormalities.  宏观检查未发现异常。 | Synonyms: visible, observable, gross | Antonyms: microscopic, invisible | adjective
