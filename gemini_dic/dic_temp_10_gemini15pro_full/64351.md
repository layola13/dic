erasion:/ɪˈreɪʒn/ | n. |消除，抹去，磨损|The erosion of the coastline is a serious problem. 海岸线的侵蚀是一个严重的问题。|The wind and rain caused the erosion of the rock face. 风和雨造成了岩壁的侵蚀。|近义词：abrasion, corrosion, deterioration, wearing away|反义词：accumulation, building, construction, accretion|词性:名词
词源: From Latin *ērāsiōn- (stem of ērāsiō ), from ērās- , past participle stem of ēradere  "to scrape out, erase," from ex- "out" + radere "to scrape."
