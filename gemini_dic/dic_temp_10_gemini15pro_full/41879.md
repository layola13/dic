cosh:/kɒʃ/ (UK), /kɑːʃ/ (US)| n.|双曲余弦|Hyperbolic cosine, a mathematical function.  It is defined as (e^x + e^-x)/2, where e is the base of the natural logarithm and x is the argument.|The cosh of 0 is 1.  双曲余弦0等于1。
