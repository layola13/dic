convictively:/kənˈvɪktɪvli/
adv.
令人信服地，有说服力地
in a manner that produces a firm belief in the truth of something
He argued his case convictively. 他令人信服地辩论了他的案子。
近义词: persuasively, convincingly
反义词: unconvincingly, implausibly
