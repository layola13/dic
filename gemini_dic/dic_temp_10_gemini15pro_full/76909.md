ganoidian:/ˈɡænɔɪdiən/ | adj. | 硬鳞鱼类的；硬鳞的 | Relating to, resembling, or characteristic of a ganoid fish, having thick bony scales. | The ganoidian scales of the gar provided excellent protection.  这种雀鳝的硬鳞提供了极佳的保护。|  近义词: bony, scaled | 反义词: soft-scaled, cartilaginous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From French *ganoïde* (not attested in this form) and directly from Ancient Greek γάνος (*gános*, “brightness, sheen”) +‎ *-oid*.  The French word *ganoïde* was itself coined by Louis Agassiz.
