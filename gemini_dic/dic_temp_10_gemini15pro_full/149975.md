palmatisected: /ˌpælmətɪˈsektɪd/ | adj. |  掌状深裂的 | Divided into lobes like a hand with fingers spread; palmately cleft almost to the base or midrib. | The leaves of the Japanese maple are palmatisected, giving it a delicate, lacy appearance. 日本枫树的叶子是掌状深裂的，赋予它精致的蕾丝般的外观。| Synonyms: palmately lobed, palmately cleft, dissected | Antonyms: entire, undivided | word type: adjective
