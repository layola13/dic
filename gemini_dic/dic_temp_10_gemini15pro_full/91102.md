hyades:/ˈhaɪəˌdiːz/ | n. |毕宿星团; 雨星|A V-shaped cluster of stars in the constellation Taurus, considered to bring rain. |The Hyades are visible to the naked eye.  毕宿星团肉眼可见。| The rising of the Hyades marked the start of the rainy season for the ancient Greeks. 对古希腊人来说，毕宿星团的升起标志着雨季的开始。 | synonyms:  Taurus (as a constellation), star cluster | antonyms: N/A | noun
