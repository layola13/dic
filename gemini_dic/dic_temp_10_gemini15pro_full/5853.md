amazingly:/əˈmeɪzɪŋli/
adverb
令人惊奇地，惊人地
in a way that causes surprise or wonder.
She played amazingly well. 她演奏得异常出色。
近义词:surprisingly, remarkably, astonishingly, incredibly
反义词:ordinarily, commonly, unsurprisingly
词性:副词
