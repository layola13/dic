gongle:/ˈɡɒŋɡəl/ (British), /ˈɡɑːŋɡəl/ (American)| n.|锣，鸣锣声，喧闹声|A large metal disc with a turned-down rim, giving a resonant note when struck, typically used in pairs in an orchestra or to signal the start of a boxing round.|The gongle of the starting bell signaled the beginning of the race. 发令钟的锣声标志着比赛的开始。|近义词：gong, chime, clang,  |反义词:silence, quiet|noun
