dispossessed:/ˌdɪspəˈzɛst/ | /ˌdɪspəˈzɛst/ | adj. | 被剥夺的；失去产业的 | Deprived of homes, possessions, or land, especially unjustly. |  The dispossessed farmers migrated to the cities.  被剥夺土地的农民迁移到城市。| synonyms: deprived, expropriated, evicted, ousted | antonyms:  entitled, privileged, endowed | adjective

dispossess:/ˌdɪspəˈzɛs/ | /ˌdɪspəˈzɛs/ | vt. | 剥夺；使失去财产 | To deprive of land, property, or other possessions. | The government dispossessed the natives of their ancestral lands. 政府剥夺了原住民的祖传土地。 | synonyms: deprive, expropriate, divest, strip | antonyms: provide, grant, endow, bestow | verb
