cadew: /ˈkæduː/ (Welsh pronunciation - approximate English equivalent)
n.
gift, present
A cadew is a gift or present, particularly in the Welsh language.
例句：He gave her a cadew for her birthday. 他送给她一份生日礼物。

例句：She received many cadewiau for her wedding. 她收到了很多结婚礼物。

近义词：gift, present, offering
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

词源：From Welsh *cadeu, from Proto-Brythonic *kado-, from Proto-Celtic *kaddo- “battle, fight”, from Proto-Indo-European *kad- (“to fall”).  The semantic shift is from "spoils of war" to "gift".  (This etymology is complex and somewhat debated.)
