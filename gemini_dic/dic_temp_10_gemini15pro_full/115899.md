marmoretum:/ˌmɑːrməˈriːtəm/ | n. | 大理石收藏馆，石材标本园 | A place where a collection of marbles, especially specimens of different kinds, are exhibited. |  The university boasts a magnificent marmoretum showcasing marbles from around the world.  这所大学拥有一个宏伟的大理石收藏馆，展示了来自世界各地的大理石。 | synonyms: marble collection, stone garden | antonyms: N/A | noun

marmoretum:/ˌmɑːrməˈriːtəm/ | n. | 大理石样品库 | A place where samples of marble are kept for display or study. |  The architect visited the marmoretum to select the perfect marble for the new building. 建筑师参观了大理石样品库，为新建筑挑选了完美的大理石。 | synonyms: marble repository, marble archive | antonyms: N/A | noun
