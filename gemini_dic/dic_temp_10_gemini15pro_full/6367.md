|adj.|

|Open to more than one interpretation; having two or more possible meanings.|

|The oracle's amphibological prophecy could be interpreted in several ways.|神谕模棱两可的预言可以用几种方式来解释。

|词性：adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *amphibologicus*, from Ancient Greek ἀμφιβολία (amphibolía, “ambiguity”) +‎ -logicus (“-logical”).  ἀμφιβολία is derived from  ἀμφί (amphí, “around, about”) + βάλλω (bállō, “I throw, cast”).
