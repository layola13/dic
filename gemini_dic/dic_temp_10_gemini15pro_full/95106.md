inane:/ɪˈneɪn/ | adj. | 空洞的；愚蠢的；无意义的 | Empty, silly, or meaningless. |  Her inane chatter bored me to tears. 她空洞的喋喋不休让我烦得要命。| synonyms: silly, foolish, absurd, pointless, vapid | antonyms: sensible, intelligent, meaningful, profound | 词性:形容词

inane:/ɪˈneɪn/ | n. | 空洞的事物 | Something that is empty, silly, or meaningless. | His speech was full of inanities. 他的演讲充满了空洞的内容。 | synonyms: nonsense, drivel, twaddle | antonyms: sense, wisdom, substance | 词性:名词 (较少使用)
