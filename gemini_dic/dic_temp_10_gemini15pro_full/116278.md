masses:/ˈmæsɪz/ (US), /ˈmæsɪz/ (UK)| n.|群众；民众；大量；众多|A large number of people or things; the majority of people.|例句：The masses gathered in the square to protest. 群众聚集在广场上抗议。

例句：He has masses of work to do. 他有很多工作要做。|近义词：crowds, throngs, multitudes, lots, abundance|反义词：few, minority, elite|Noun
