paladin: /ˈpælədɪn/ | n. | 圣骑士；勇士；维护者 | A knight renowned for heroism and chivalry.  A champion of a noble cause. |例句：He was a true paladin, always defending the weak. 他是一位真正的圣骑士，总是保护弱者。

|例句：The paladin rode forth to battle the dragon. 圣骑士骑马出征与恶龙搏斗。| 近义词：knight, champion, hero, warrior | 反义词：villain, coward, traitor | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *paladin*, from Italian *paladino*, ultimately from Late Latin *palatīnus* "official of the palace," from Latin *palātium* "palace." Originally referred to the twelve peers of Charlemagne's court.
