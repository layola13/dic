parochially:/pəˈroʊkiəli/
adjective
狭隘地；地方观念地
limited to a narrow outlook or scope, especially a local or provincial one; concerned only with local or narrow issues.
He viewed the issue parochially, failing to see its national implications. 他狭隘地看待这个问题，没有看到它的国家意义。
narrowly, provincially, locally, restrictedly
broadly, globally, universally
adjective
