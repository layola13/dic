lenis:/ˈliːnɪs/| adj.|弱的，松弛的|Characterized by weakness or lack of force, especially in the articulation of consonants; unaspirated.|The phoneme /b/ in Spanish is lenis, while the /p/ is fortis.|近义词：weak, soft, lax|反义词：fortis, strong, tense|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *lēnis, meaning "smooth, gentle, mild".
