bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/ | v. | 欺骗，迷惑，哄骗 | To trick or deceive someone, often in a playful way. |  He bamboozled me into lending him money. 他骗我借钱给他。

The magician bamboozled the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他巧妙的戏法迷惑了观众。


| 近义词: trick, deceive, hoodwink, dupe, swindle, cheat, con, defraud | 反义词: enlighten, clarify, be honest | 词性: 动词
