motorpathic:/ˌmoʊtərˈpæθɪk/| adj.|运动病的，运动障碍的|Relating to or affected by motorpathy, a condition characterized by disordered motor function or impaired coordination.|He exhibited motorpathic symptoms, struggling with basic movements like walking and grasping objects. 他表现出运动病症状，连走路和抓东西等基本动作都很困难。|近义词：dyskinetic, ataxic|反义词：coordinated, healthy|词性:形容词

词源:
From "motor" (relating to movement) + "-pathic" (relating to disease or suffering).  "Motor" ultimately derives from Latin "movēre" (to move). "-pathic" comes from Greek "pathos" (suffering).
