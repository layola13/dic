fibriform:/ˈfaɪbrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
纤维状的，纤维形的
Resembling or composed of fibers; fibrous.
The fungus had a fibriform appearance. 这种真菌呈纤维状外观。
Synonyms: fibrous, filamentous, threadlike
Antonyms: compact, solid, dense
词性: 形容词
