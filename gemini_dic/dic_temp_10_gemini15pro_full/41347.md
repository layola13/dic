corinthian: /kəˈrɪnθiən/ | adj. n. |科林斯的；科林斯式的；科林斯人|Relating to or characteristic of ancient Corinth, its people, or its style of architecture, especially its ornate order of columns.  A native or inhabitant of ancient Corinth. |例句：The Corinthian columns of the temple were magnificent.  寺庙的科林斯式圆柱宏伟壮观。|例句：He was a wealthy Corinthian merchant. 他是一位富有的科林斯商人。|近义词：ornate, elaborate, decorated |反义词：plain, simple |词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: From Latin *Corinthius*, from Greek *Kórinthos* (Κόρινθος).
