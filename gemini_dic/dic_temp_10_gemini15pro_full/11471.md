arguably:/ˈɑːrɡjuəbli/
adv.
可论证地，可以说是
Used to say that something is possible or likely true, although other people might not agree.
Arguably, he is the best football player in the world.  可以说是，他是世界上最好的足球运动员。
近义词:possibly, probably, conceivably
反义词:undeniably, indisputably
词性:副词
