asiphonata:/eɪsaɪfəˈneɪtə/ | n. | 无管类 | A subclass of bivalve molluscs characterized by the absence of a siphon.  These are typically freshwater mussels. |  Asiphonata mussels are found in many rivers. 无管类贻贝在许多河流中都能找到。 | synonym: Eulamellibranchia (in older classifications, though not a perfect synonym as the classifications have shifted) | antonym: Siphonata | noun
