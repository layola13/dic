marquee:/mɑːrˈkiː/
n.|大帐篷，华盖；（剧院等的）遮檐；跑马灯|A large tent used for social or commercial functions. A canopy projecting over an entrance, especially of a theater or hotel. A lighted display sign that scrolls text or images.|The wedding reception was held in a grand marquee on the lawn. 婚礼招待会在草坪上的一个大帐篷里举行。| synonyms: canopy, awning, tent, shelter | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "interior" or "indoors") | noun
marquee:/mɑːrˈkiː/
adj.|（尤指演员、运动员）最重要的，最著名的；领衔主演的|Leading, preeminent, especially of performers or athletes.|The marquee player on the team is injured. 球队中最重要的球员受伤了。|synonyms: star, leading, principal, top, main | antonyms:  minor, supporting, secondary | adjective
