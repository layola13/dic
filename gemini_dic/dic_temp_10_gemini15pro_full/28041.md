caparison: /kəˈpærɪsən/ /kəˈpɛrəsən/
n. vt.|装饰; 服饰; 马具|Ornamental coverings or trappings, especially for a horse; rich clothing or decoration.|The knight's horse was clad in elaborate caparison.|近义词: adornment, decoration, ornamentation, finery, array, regalia|反义词: plainness, simplicity
词性: 名词，动词

caparison: /kəˈpærɪsən/ /kəˈpɛrəsən/
vt.|披上华丽的装饰; 盛装打扮|To cover with rich decorative coverings or trappings; to dress up or decorate elaborately.|The general's horse was caparisoned in blue and gold.|近义词: adorn, decorate, bedeck, ornament, embellish|反义词: strip, denude
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *caparasson, from Spanish caparazón "covering," from capa "cape." Ultimately from Late Latin cappa.
