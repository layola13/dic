looplight: /ˈluːplaɪt/ | n. | 环形灯，环状灯 | A light source that forms a loop, often used for photography or video recording, providing even illumination. | Example: The photographer used a looplight to eliminate shadows on the model's face. 摄影师用环形灯消除模特脸上的阴影。

Example:  The makeup artist prefers looplights for their soft, diffused light.  化妆师更喜欢环形灯柔和、漫射的光线。
