overgeneralised:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒenrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度概括的，过分泛化的 | Characterized by making generalizations that are too broad or sweeping, often based on limited evidence. |  He made an overgeneralised statement about all politicians being corrupt. 他对所有政客都腐败的说法过于笼统。| synonyms: overbroad, sweeping, indiscriminate | antonyms: specific, precise, nuanced | adjective
