bastardises:/ˈbæstərdaɪzɪz/
verb
使…品质降低；窜改；歪曲
corrupts, debased, or adulterates something, typically something valuable or of high artistic merit.
The movie bastardizes the original novel. 这部电影歪曲了原著小说。
synonyms: corrupts, debases, adulterates, degrades, perverts, distorts, falsifies, misrepresents, vulgarizes, cheapens
antonyms: purifies, refines, enhances, improves, elevates


bastardisation:/ˌbæstərdaɪˈzeɪʃn/
noun
窜改；歪曲；降低品质的行为或结果
The act or process of corrupting, debasing, or adulterating something.
The bastardization of the language is appalling. 对这门语言的窜改令人震惊。
synonyms: corruption, debasement, adulteration, degradation, perversion, distortion, falsification, misrepresentation, vulgarization, cheapening
antonyms: purification, refinement, enhancement, improvement, elevation
