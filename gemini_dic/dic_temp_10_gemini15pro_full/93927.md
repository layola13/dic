immunization:/ˌɪmjənɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 免疫，免疫接种 |The process by which a person or animal becomes protected against a disease. This term is often used interchangeably with vaccination or inoculation.|  Children should receive their routine immunizations. 孩子们应该接受常规免疫接种。| synonyms: vaccination, inoculation, injection | antonyms: infection, vulnerability | noun

immunize:/ˈɪmjənaɪz/ | vt. | 使免疫，接种 | To make a person or animal immune to infection, typically by inoculation. |  The vaccine immunizes against measles. 这种疫苗可以预防麻疹。 | synonyms: vaccinate, inoculate, protect | antonyms: infect, expose | verb
