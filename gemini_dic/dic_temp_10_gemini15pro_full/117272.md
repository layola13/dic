mechanistic:/ˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
机械论的; 机制的
Relating to theories that explain phenomena in purely physical or deterministic terms.  Characterized by a view of nature as a machine whose workings can be fully understood in terms of its component parts and their interactions.
The scientist proposed a mechanistic explanation for the origin of life.  这位科学家提出了对生命起源的机械论解释。
The report offered a mechanistic analysis of the economy, ignoring social and political factors. 报告提供了一个对经济的机械论分析，忽略了社会和政治因素。
Synonyms: deterministic, reductionist, physicalist
Antonyms: holistic, vitalistic, teleological
词性: 形容词
