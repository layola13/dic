breaker:/ˈbreɪkər/| n.|断路器，打破者，破碎机，浪花|A device that interrupts the flow of electricity in a circuit.  A person or thing that breaks something. A wave that breaks on the shore.|The circuit breaker tripped and the lights went out. 断路器跳闸，灯灭了。|Synonyms: interrupter, disconnector, surge protector|Antonyms: connector, closer|noun

breaker:/ˈbreɪkər/| n.|打破常规者，创新者|Someone who challenges conventions or breaks new ground.|She's a real breaker in the fashion industry. 她是时尚界的真正创新者。|Synonyms: innovator, pioneer, trailblazer|Antonyms: follower, conformist|noun
