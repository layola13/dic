destabilising:/diːˈsteɪbəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使不稳定的；破坏稳定的
tending to make a government, area, or political or economic system less strong or safe, by causing changes and problems
例句：The constant threat of war had a destabilising effect on the region.  持续的战争威胁对该地区造成了不稳定的影响。

例句：The release of the confidential documents had a destabilising influence on the stock market.  机密文件的发布对股票市场产生了不稳定的影响。

近义词：disruptive, unsettling, undermining
反义词：stabilizing, strengthening, reinforcing
词性：adjective
