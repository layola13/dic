out-think:/ˌaʊtˈθɪŋk/
verb
智胜；在思考上胜过
to defeat someone by thinking more cleverly than they do
He out-thought his opponents and won the contract. 他智胜了对手，赢得了合同。
outsmart, outmaneuver
- (This verb doesn't typically have an antonym, as it describes a specific action of intellectual superiority.)
