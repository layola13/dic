disarticulate:/dɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
使脱节; 拆开
To separate at the joints; disjoint.
The butcher disarticulated the chicken.  屠夫把鸡切成了块。

disarticulate:/dɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
(使言语)不连贯
To make (speech) disconnected or unintelligible.
His anger caused him to disarticulate his words.  他的愤怒使他的话语含糊不清。

disarticulate:/dɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
adjective
不连贯的; 语无伦次的
Disconnected; unintelligible.
His presentation was disarticulated and confusing. 他的陈述语无伦次，令人费解。


Etymology:  From Latin dis- "apart" + articulatus "jointed," past participle of articulare "to joint," from articulus "small joint," diminutive of artus "joint."

Synonym:  disconnect, separate, disjoint, incoherent, rambling, mumbled (for speech)
Antonym: connect, join, articulate, coherent, clear (for speech)
Word Type: Verb, Adjective
