axmen's:/ˈæksmənz/
noun
伐木工的; 斧头工的
belonging to or associated with axmen.  It is the possessive form of "axmen."
The axmen's competition was fierce. 伐木工的比赛很激烈。
Synonym: lumberjacks', woodcutters'
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a possessive.  Could consider something like "conservationists'")
词性: 名词所有格


ETYMOLOGY->
From "axman" + "-'s". "Axman" comes from "ax" (Old English æx) + "man" (Old English mann).
