overimaginatively: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmædʒɪnətɪvli/
adverb
过度想象地; 过于 fanciful 地
Having or showing too much imagination, especially about unreal or unpleasant things.  Characterized by excessive use of the imagination, often to the point of being unrealistic or impractical.
She overimaginatively described the monster under her bed as a seven-headed dragon with laser eyes. 她过度想象地把她床下的怪物描述成一只长着激光眼的七头龙。

Synonyms: fancifully, whimsically, fantastically, elaborately
Antonyms: realistically, practically, pragmatically
词性: 副词
