nonrivals:/ˌnɒnˈraɪvəlz/
n.|非竞争性物品，非竞争品|Goods or services that can be consumed by one person without preventing another person from consuming them.|例句：Public parks are considered nonrivals because one person's enjoyment doesn't diminish another's.  公园被认为是非竞争性物品，因为一个人的享受不会减少另一个人的享受。|例句：Street lighting is a classic example of a nonrival good. 路灯是非竞争性物品的典型例子。|近义词：non-excludable, public goods|反义词：rivalrous, private goods|词性:名词(通常用作复数)
