intercoordinated:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
| adj. |相互协调的，互相配合的|arranged or structured so that different parts work effectively together.|The various departments need to be better intercoordinated to improve efficiency. 各个部门需要更好地相互协调以提高效率.|Example 2: The intercoordinated movements of the dancers were mesmerizing. 舞者们相互协调的动作令人着迷。|近义词：coordinated, synchronized, integrated, harmonized|反义词：uncoordinated, disjointed, fragmented|词性:形容词


Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among, mutually, reciprocally (from Latin)
coordinate (verb): to bring into a common action, movement, or condition (from Latin co- "together" + ordinare "to arrange")
-ed (suffix): forming the past participle of verbs
