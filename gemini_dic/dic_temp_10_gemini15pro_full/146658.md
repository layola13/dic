overhastens:/ˌōvərˈheɪsənz/ | v. | 加速；过快地完成；操之过急 | To hasten excessively; to do something too quickly or hurriedly, often with negative consequences. | He overhastened the project and ended up making costly mistakes. 他为了赶工期，结果犯了代价高昂的错误。

The government overhastened the economic reforms, leading to instability. 政府操之过急地进行经济改革，导致了不稳定。 | 近义词: rush, hurry, precipitate | 反义词: delay, postpone, procrastinate | 词性: 动词

词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "hasten" (to hurry). "Hasten" comes from Middle English "hasten," from Old Norse "hasta."
