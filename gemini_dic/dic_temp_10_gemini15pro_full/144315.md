outspringing:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪŋɪŋ/
|n.|涌出;  爆发;  起源;
|The outspringing of new ideas from the brainstorming session was impressive.  头脑风暴会议上涌现出的新想法令人印象深刻。|
|例句：The outspringing of creativity in the art class was inspiring. 艺术课上创造力的迸发令人鼓舞。|
|近义词：emergence, outburst, eruption, origin|
|反义词：suppression, decline, disappearance|
|词性：名词|
