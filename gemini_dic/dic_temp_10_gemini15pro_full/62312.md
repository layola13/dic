endearingly:/ɪnˈdɪərɪŋli/ | adv. | 可爱地，讨人喜欢地 | In a way that inspires affection. | She smiled endearingly at her grandfather. 她对她祖父露出了可爱的微笑。|  He patted the dog endearingly. 他亲昵地拍了拍小狗。 | 近义词: charmingly, sweetly, lovingly, adorably | 反义词: offensively, repulsively, annoyingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From endear + -ing + -ly.  "Endear" comes from the Old French "endaierer," meaning "to make dear."
