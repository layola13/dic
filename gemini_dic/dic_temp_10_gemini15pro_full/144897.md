over-skepticalness's:/ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 过度怀疑的态度 |  The state of being excessively skeptical; extreme doubtfulness.  It is formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning excessive) to "skeptical" (inclined to doubt) and then adding the suffix "-ness" to create a noun denoting the state of being skeptical. The final 's is the possessive apostrophe s. | Her over-skepticalness's impact on the team's morale was noticeable. 她的过度怀疑对团队士气的影响很明显。|  He attributed his over-skepticalness's development to past experiences of being deceived. 他将自己过度怀疑态度的形成归因于过去被欺骗的经历。 | cynicism, distrust, doubt, suspicion | credulity, gullibility, naivety | noun
