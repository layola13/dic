brierier:/ˈbraɪəriər/| adj.|更棘手的，更多荆棘的|Comparative form of briery: having more briers|The path became brierier as we went deeper into the woods.  这条小路在我们越往森林深处走就越棘手。|近义词：thornier, pricklier, spikier|反义词:smoother, less thorny|词性:形容词
brier:/ˈbraɪər/| n.|荆棘，野蔷薇|A thorny shrub or plant.|The roses were entangled with the briers. 玫瑰与荆棘缠绕在一起。|近义词：thorn, bramble|反义词: (no direct opposite, perhaps smooth plant)|词性:名词
