airdromes:/ˈeərdroʊmz/
n.
飞机场，机场
A place where aircraft regularly land and take off, with buildings and facilities for passengers.
Many international airdromes have been built in recent years. 近年来建造了许多国际机场。

The old airdrome is now a museum.  这个旧机场现在是一个博物馆。

近义词:airports, airfields
反义词:None
词性:noun
