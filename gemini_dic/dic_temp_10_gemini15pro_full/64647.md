erythrolein:/ɪˌrɪθroʊˈliːɪn/
noun
赤油精；丙烯醛
An oily liquid, C₃H₄O, the aldehyde of allyl alcohol, acrolein, obtained by the dehydration of glycerin.  It is a highly flammable liquid with a pungent odor.
Erythrolein is a toxic compound found in cigarette smoke. 赤油精是一种在香烟烟雾中发现的有毒化合物。
Erythrolein can cause irritation to the eyes and respiratory tract. 赤油精会刺激眼睛和呼吸道。
Synonyms: acrolein, propenal
Antonyms: None specifically for this chemical compound.
