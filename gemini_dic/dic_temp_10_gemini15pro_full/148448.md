overstoring:/ˌoʊvərˈstɔːrɪŋ/ | n. | 过度存储，过量存储 | The act of storing more than is necessary or desirable. |  Many people are guilty of overstoring food, which often leads to waste. 很多人对于食物过度存储，这经常导致浪费。 | 近义词：hoarding, accumulating, stockpiling | 反义词: purging, discarding | 词性:名词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "storing" (present participle of "store")
