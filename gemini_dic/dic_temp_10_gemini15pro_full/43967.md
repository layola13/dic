cricketlike:/ˈkrɪkɪtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像蟋蟀的，蟋蟀似的|Resembling or characteristic of a cricket, especially in sound or appearance.|The insect made a cricketlike chirping sound.  这种昆虫发出像蟋蟀一样的鸣叫声。|The toy made a cricketlike clicking noise. 这个玩具发出咔哒咔哒的像蟋蟀一样的声音。|近义词：chirping, clicking|反义词: silent, quiet|词性:形容词

词源: cricket + like.  "Cricket" comes from Old North French "criquet," imitative of the insect's sound.  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-lic."
