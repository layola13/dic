lithophotography:/ˌlɪθəfəˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |石版摄影|The process of creating images on stone using photographic methods. It combines the principles of lithography and photography. |Early experiments in lithophotography were crucial for the development of offset printing. 早期的石版摄影实验对于胶印技术的发展至关重要。|Lithophotography is a historical photographic process that wasn't widely adopted. 石版摄影是一种历史悠久的摄影工艺，但并未被广泛采用。|synonyms: photolithography|antonyms: N/A|noun
