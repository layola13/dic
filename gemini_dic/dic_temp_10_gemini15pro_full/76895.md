gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/ | n. |舷梯；通道；过道 |A raised platform or passageway, often portable, used for boarding or leaving a ship or aircraft; a passage between rows of seats, as in a theater or airplane. |例句：The passengers boarded the ship via the gangway.  乘客通过舷梯登上了船。

例句：Excuse me, could you please move out of the gangway?  不好意思，请您让一下过道好吗？|近义词：passage, aisle, walkway, corridor |反义词: barrier, obstacle |词性: noun

gangways:/ˈɡæŋweɪz/ | n. |舷梯（复数）；通道（复数）；过道（复数）|Plural form of gangway. |例句：The crew lowered the gangways so the passengers could disembark. 船员放下了舷梯以便乘客下船。
