frequentative:/ˈfriːkwənteɪtɪv/
adjective|频繁的，重复的|Characterized by frequent repetition.|He has a frequentative cough. 他经常咳嗽。
|近义词：repeated, recurrent, habitual, iterative|反义词：infrequent, occasional, rare|词性: adjective

frequentative:/ˈfriːkwənteɪtɪv/
noun|重复词，频次动词|A word, especially a verb, that expresses frequent repetition of an action.|The word "flutter" is a frequentative of "flit."  "flutter"是"flit"的重复词。
|近义词：iterative|反义词：N/A|词性: noun
