noviceship: /ˈnɒvɪsʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈnɑːvɪsʃɪp/ (US)
n.
学徒期，新手期，见习期
The state or period of being a novice; the time one spends as a beginner learning a skill or trade.
He completed his noviceship in carpentry and became a journeyman. 他完成了木工的学徒期，成为了一名熟练工人。

The noviceship program lasts for two years.  这个见习项目持续两年。

近义词: apprenticeship, traineeship, internship, probation
反义词: mastery, expertise, experience, proficiency
词性: noun
