aliferous:/əˈlɪfərəs/| adj.|有翼的；产生…的|Bearing or producing wings; producing or yielding something (specified by the initial element).|例句：The aliferous insect took flight.  这只长翅膀的昆虫飞走了。

例句：The carboniferous period is known for its coal deposits.  石炭纪以其煤炭矿藏而闻名。|近义词：winged, producing|反义词：wingless, barren|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ala ("wing") + -ferous (from Latin -fer ("bearing")).  *ala is related to avis ("bird").
