celebraters: /ˈsɛləˌbreɪtərz/
n.
庆祝者，庆祝的人们
People who celebrate something.
The celebraters gathered in the town square.  庆祝者聚集在城镇广场。
The celebraters cheered and sang songs. 庆祝者们欢呼和歌唱。
近义词: revelers, partygoers
反义词: mourners
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From the verb "celebrate" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Celebrate" comes from Latin "celebrare"  meaning "to frequent, honor, praise."
