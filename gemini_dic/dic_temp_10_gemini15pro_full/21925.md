bogan:/ˈboʊɡən/ (AU) /ˈbɒɡən/ (UK)| n.|粗俗的人，没文化的人|A person who is considered unfashionable, uncultivated, or of a lower social class, often associated with particular stereotypes of clothing, music, and behavior.  This term is primarily used in Australia and New Zealand.|He's a bit of a bogan, always wearing those flannelette shirts.|近义词：redneck, chav, yob|反义词:sophisticate, cultured person|词性:noun
