ignoramus:/ˌɪɡnəˈreɪməs/ | adj. | 无知的；愚昧的 | Lacking knowledge; uneducated. | His ignoramus comment offended everyone in the room. 他愚蠢的评论冒犯了房间里的每个人. | Synonyms: uneducated, unlearned, illiterate, uninformed | Antonyms: knowledgeable, educated, learned, informed | adjective
