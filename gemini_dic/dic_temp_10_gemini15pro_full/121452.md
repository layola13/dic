misitemized:/ˌmɪsˈaɪtəmaɪzd/
verb
错误列出的，条目错误的
listed incorrectly or with errors.
The expenses were misitemized, leading to an inaccurate budget.  费用被错误列出，导致预算不准确。
The invoice was misitemized, charging us for products we didn't order.  发票项目错误，向我们收取了我们没有订购的产品的费用。
近义词:misstated, miscalculated, misreported
反义词:correctly itemized, accurately listed
词性:verb
