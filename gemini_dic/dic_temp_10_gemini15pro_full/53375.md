disaggregation:/dɪsˌæɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 分解；分类；分散 | The process of separating something into its constituent parts.  |  The disaggregation of the data revealed interesting trends in different demographics. 数据的分类揭示了不同人群中的有趣趋势。 |  Disaggregation of the company's financial reports showed losses in several divisions.  公司财务报告的分解显示几个部门出现亏损。 |  breakdown, separation, analysis | aggregation, combination, integration | noun
