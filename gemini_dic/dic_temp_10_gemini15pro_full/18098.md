behaviourists: /bɪˈheɪvjərɪsts/ | n. | 行为主义者 |  A person who adheres to behaviorism, a school of psychology that emphasizes the study of observable behavior rather than internal mental processes. |  例句：The behaviourists believed that all behavior is learned through conditioning. 行为主义者认为所有行为都是通过条件反射学习的。

例句：Many behaviourists conduct experiments on animals to study learning processes. 许多行为主义者对动物进行实验来研究学习过程。| 近义词：behaviorists | 反义词: cognitivists, mentalists | 词性: noun
