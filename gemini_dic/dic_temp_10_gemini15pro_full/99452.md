interests:/ˈɪntrəsts/ (US), /ˈɪntrests/ (UK)| n.|兴趣，利益，利息|Something that concerns, involves, draws the attention of, or excites the curiosity of a person; something in which a person has a stake, share, or involvement.|She has diverse interests, including painting, hiking, and playing the piano. 她有多种兴趣爱好，包括绘画、徒步旅行和弹钢琴。|synonyms: hobbies, passions, pursuits, concerns, stakes|antonyms: disinterests, apathy|noun

interests:/ˈɪntrəsts/ (US), /ˈɪntrests/ (UK)| vt.|使…感兴趣|To arouse the curiosity or attention of; to involve.|The documentary about space exploration deeply interested him.  这部关于太空探索的纪录片深深地吸引了他。|synonyms: fascinate, intrigue, engage, attract|antonyms: bore, repel, disgust|verb
