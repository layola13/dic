nonprohibitive:/ˌnɒnprəˈhɪbɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非禁止性的；价格适中的；可承受的 | Not prohibitive; moderate, affordable.  It implies that something is permitted or allowed and, often additionally, that the cost is reasonable or manageable. | The cost of the tickets was nonprohibitive, so we all went to the concert.  票价不高，所以我们都去听了音乐会。|  The entry fee is nonprohibitive, encouraging a large number of participants. 入场费不高，鼓励了大量参与者。| Synonyms: affordable, reasonable, moderate, permissible, acceptable | Antonyms: prohibitive, exorbitant, expensive, unaffordable, unacceptable |词性: adjective
