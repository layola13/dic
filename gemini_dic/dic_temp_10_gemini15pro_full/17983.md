beg:/beɡ/ | v. |乞求；请求；恳求 | to ask for something in a humble or urgent way | He begged her for forgiveness. 他乞求她原谅他。| synonyms: implore, plead, beseech | antonyms: demand, command | verb

beg:/beɡ/ | v. |乞讨 | to ask for money or food from people in the street |  The homeless man begged for money.  这个无家可归的人乞讨钱财。| synonyms: panhandle, solicit | antonyms: give, donate | verb

beg:/beɡ/ | v. |(用于客气地请求)请 | used to ask politely for something | I beg your pardon?  对不起，您说什么？| synonyms: request | antonyms: refuse | verb
