antihypertension:/ˌæntiˌhaɪpərˈtɛnʃən/ | n. |抗高血压|A medication or other therapy used to treat hypertension (high blood pressure).|例句：The doctor prescribed an antihypertension medication to lower his blood pressure. 医生给他开了抗高血压药来降低血压。

例句：Regular exercise can be a natural form of antihypertension.  规律的运动可以是一种天然的抗高血压方式。|近义词：antihypertensive |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but conceptually: hypertensive agent) |词性:noun
