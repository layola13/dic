outsallied:/ˌaʊtˈsælid/
verb
突袭而出，出击胜过
To sally forth more successfully or effectively than another; to surpass in sallying.
The besieged troops outsallied the enemy and drove them back.  被围困的部队突袭而出，击退了敌人。
outsallied the enemy in a daring nighttime raid. 在一次大胆的夜间突袭中胜过敌人。
近义词: outflank, outmaneuver, surpass
反义词: retreat, withdraw
