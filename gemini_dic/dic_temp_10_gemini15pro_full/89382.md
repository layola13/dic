homophonous:/ˌhɒməˈfəʊnəs/ (UK) /ˌhoʊməˈfoʊnəs/ (US)| adj.|同音的|Having the same pronunciation as another word but a different meaning, origin, or spelling.|例句：The words "to," "too," and "two" are homophonous.  单词"to"、"too"和"two"是同音异义词。

例句：The words "their," "there," and "they're" are homophonous, which can lead to confusion. 单词 "their," "there," and "they're" 是同音词，容易混淆。|近义词：homophonic|反义词:heterophonous|词性:adjective
