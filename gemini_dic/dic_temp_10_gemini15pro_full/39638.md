consequentialness:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəlɪsnɪs/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃəlɪsnɪs/
n.
重要性；重大；随之而来的结果
the state or quality of being consequential; the state of producing significant results or effects.
The consequentialness of the decision was immediately apparent.  这个决定的重要性立刻显现出来。
The president stressed the consequentialness of the upcoming elections. 总统强调了即将举行的选举的重要性。
significance, importance, gravity, weight, moment
insignificance, unimportance, triviality
noun
