daguerreotypist:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpɪst/ | n. | 达盖尔摄影师 | A person who makes daguerreotypes, an early type of photograph. | Louis Daguerre's invention revolutionized portraiture, and skilled daguerreotypists were highly sought after. 路易斯·达盖尔的这项发明彻底改变了肖像画，技艺精湛的达盖尔摄影师也因此备受追捧。 | synonym: photographer | antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *daguerréotypiste*, from *daguerréotype* (daguerreotype) + *-iste* (-ist).  The word *daguerreotype* itself is named after Louis-Jacques-Mandé Daguerre, the inventor of the process.
