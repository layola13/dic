outwrestle:/ˌaʊtˈrɛsəl/| v. |以摔跤击败，胜过|To defeat someone by wrestling; to overcome or surpass someone or something through greater strength, effort, or determination.|He outwrestled his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮以摔跤击败了他的对手。| The company outwrestled its competitors for the lucrative contract.  这家公司在竞争中胜过对手，赢得了利润丰厚的合同。 |近义词：outmaneuver, outfight, overcome, surpass|反义词：lose to, be defeated by|词性:动词

词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "wrestle" (verb meaning "to engage in wrestling")
