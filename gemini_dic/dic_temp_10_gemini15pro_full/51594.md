devanagaris:/ˌdeɪvəˈnɑːɡəriːz/ | n. | 天城体，梵文 | Devanagari is an abugida writing system used for over 120 languages including Hindi, Marathi, Nepali, and Sanskrit. 天城体是一种元音附标文字，用于包括印地语、马拉地语、尼泊尔语和梵语在内的120多种语言。| The Devanagari script is written from left to right. 天城文字是从左到右书写的。| synonym: Devanagari script | antonym: N/A | noun
