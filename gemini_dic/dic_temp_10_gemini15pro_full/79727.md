gluey:/ˈɡluːi/ (US) /ˈɡlʊːi/ (UK)| adj.|胶状的；粘的；粘糊糊的|Covered with or resembling glue; sticky.|The cake was still gluey in the middle.  蛋糕中间还是粘糊糊的。|The child's hands were gluey from the melted candy.  孩子的手因为融化的糖果而粘糊糊的。|近义词：sticky, gummy, adhesive, viscous|反义词：dry, non-sticky|adjective

Etymology->
From glue + -y.  From Middle English glu, from Anglo-Norman glu, from Late Latin glus, glūten, from Latin glūs.
