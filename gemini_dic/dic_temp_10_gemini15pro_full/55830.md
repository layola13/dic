dividend: /ˈdɪvɪdend/ (US), /ˈdɪvɪdənd/ (UK)| n. |红利；股息；回报；被除数|A sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves).  Also refers to the number being divided in a division problem.| The company announced a dividend of $2 per share.  公司宣布每股派发2美元的红利。| synonyms:  payout, distribution, return, share, bonus | antonyms:  loss, debit, expenditure | noun

dividend: /ˈdɪvɪdend/ (US), /ˈdɪvɪdənd/ (UK)| n. |被除数|The number that is being divided in a division problem.| In the equation 10 / 2 = 5, 10 is the dividend. 在等式10 / 2 = 5中，10是被除数。| synonyms:  numerator | antonyms: divisor, denominator | noun
