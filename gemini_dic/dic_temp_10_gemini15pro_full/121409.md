misincline:/ˌmɪsɪnˈklaɪn/
|v.|使不感兴趣；使不愿|To make someone unwilling or reluctant to do something; to discourage. |The rainy weather misinclined me to go for a walk.  下雨天让我不想去散步。| 近义词: discourage, deter, disaffect | 反义词: incline, encourage, motivate |词性:动词

misincline:/ˌmɪsɪnˈklaɪn/
|adj.|不愿意的；不情愿的|Unwilling or reluctant.  (Less common usage)|He seemed misinclined to offer any help. 他似乎不愿意提供任何帮助。|近义词: unwilling, reluctant, disinclined | 反义词: willing, eager, keen |词性:形容词
