ganoine:/ˈɡænoʊɪn/| n. |硬鳞质，闪光质|A hard, shiny, enamel-like substance that forms the outer layer of the scales of certain primitive fishes.|The ancient fish's scales were covered in ganoine, giving them a shimmering appearance.  古代鱼的鳞片上覆盖着硬鳞质，使它们闪闪发光。|近义词:enamel, dentin|反义词:|noun

词源:
From French *ganoïde*, from Greek *gános* "brightness, sheen" + *-oeidēs* "-oid."
