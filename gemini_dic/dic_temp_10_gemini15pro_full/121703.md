mispredicting:/ˌmɪsprɪˈdɪktɪŋ/
verb
错误预测，预测失误
Making an incorrect prediction about a future event or outcome.
The economist was mispredicting the market trends and lost a lot of money. 这位经济学家错误地预测了市场趋势，损失了很多钱。
The weather forecast was mispredicting the storm's path. 天气预报错误地预测了风暴的路径。

近义词:forecasting incorrectly, wrongly predicting, miscalculating
反义词:predicting accurately, correctly forecasting

词性:verb (动名词形式)
词源:mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + predicting (present participle of "predict," from Latin praedict-, past participle stem of praedicere "foretell," from prae- "beforehand" + dicere "to say").
