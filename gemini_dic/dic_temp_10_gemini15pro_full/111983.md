例句：Many Londoners commute to work by underground. 很多伦敦人乘地铁上下班。


近义词：London residents, London inhabitants | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑乡村居民 rural residents) | 词性: 名词


词源: London + -er + -s

London:  From Middle English *Lunden, from Old English Lunden, Lundenceaster (“London”), from Proto-Germanic *Lundun, of uncertain origin.

-er:  A suffix forming nouns designating persons from their place of origin, residence, or activity, or from other nouns.

-s:  plural marker
