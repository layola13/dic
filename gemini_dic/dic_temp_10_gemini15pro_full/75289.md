fronted:/ˈfrʌntɪd/ | adj. | 前置的，前端的 | Moved to the front or beginning; having a front or facade. | The fronted adverbial phrase adds emphasis. 前置的状语短语增加了强调。| The house has a newly fronted porch. 这房子有一个新装修的门廊。 | 近义词:foremost, leading, initial, preposed | 反义词: rear, back, hindmost | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "front" + "-ed" suffix, meaning "having been fronted." "Front" itself comes from the Old French "front," meaning "forehead, brow," ultimately from Latin "frons" (genitive "frontis").
