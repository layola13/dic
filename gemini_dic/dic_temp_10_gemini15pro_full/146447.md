overfreely:/ˌoʊvərˈfriːli/| adv.|过度自由地，过于随便地|Acting or speaking with excessive freedom or lack of restraint.|He expressed his opinions overfreely, offending several people. 他过度自由地表达了自己的观点，冒犯了几个人。|She dressed overfreely for the occasion, wearing a very revealing outfit. 她在那个场合穿着过于随便，穿了一件非常暴露的衣服。|近义词：too freely, excessively freely, unrestrainedly|反义词：reservedly, cautiously, discreetly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "freely" (adverbial form of "free").  "Free" comes from Old English "freo," meaning "free, exempt, noble."  The prefix "over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, over, beyond."
