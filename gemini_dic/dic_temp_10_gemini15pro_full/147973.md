overrestricted:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstrɪktɪd/
adjective
过度限制的
subject to too many restrictions; excessively restricted.
The overrestricted diet left him feeling weak and deprived.  过度限制的饮食让他感到虚弱和被剥夺。
Her social life was overrestricted by her parents' strict rules.  她的社交生活受到父母严格规定的过度限制。
Synonyms: excessively restricted, overly limited, too confined, unduly constrained
Antonyms: unrestricted, unlimited, free, open
词性: 形容词
