eventh:/ˈiːvənθ/ |adj.|第七的|Relating to the number seven in a series; coming after six others. | This is her seventh attempt at baking the cake. 这是她第七次尝试烤蛋糕。| Example: The seventh day of the week is Sunday.  一周的第七天是星期天。|Synonyms: ordinal, seventh-place |Antonyms: first, last | Word Class: Adjective
