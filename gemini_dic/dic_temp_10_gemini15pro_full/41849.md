corymbose:/kɔːˈrɪmboʊs/| adj.|伞房花序的，聚伞花序的|A corymbose inflorescence is a type of flowering plant arrangement where the individual flower stalks arise from different points on the main stem, but all reach roughly the same level, creating a flat-topped or slightly domed cluster.|The yarrow has a corymbose head of tiny white flowers.|近义词：cymose|反义词:|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corymbosus, from Ancient Greek κόρυμβος (kórymbos, “cluster of ivy berries, top of a plant”).
