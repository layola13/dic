flowerless:/ˈflaʊərlɪs/| adj.|无花的，不开花的|Without flowers.  Not producing flowers.  |Ferns and mosses are flowerless plants. 蕨类植物和苔藓植物是无花植物。|The flowerless cactus stood stark against the desert landscape.  这株无花的仙人掌在沙漠景观中显得格外醒目。|Near synonyms: nonflowering, bloomless, aphyllous (in some contexts)|Antonyms: flowering, blooming, floriferous|adjective
