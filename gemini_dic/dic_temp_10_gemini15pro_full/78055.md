geocacher:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃər/| n.|寻宝者，地理藏宝玩家|A person who participates in the activity of geocaching, which involves using a GPS receiver or mobile device to hide and seek containers (called "caches") at specific locations marked by coordinates.|例句：The geocacher spent hours searching for the hidden cache.  这位寻宝者花了几个小时寻找隐藏的宝藏。

例句：Many geocachers enjoy the challenge of finding difficult caches.  许多寻宝者喜欢寻找困难的宝藏的挑战。|近义词：treasure hunter, cacher|反义词:|noun
