overgorge: /ˌoʊvərˈɡɔːrdʒ/ | v. |  暴饮暴食；使…过度饱食；充斥；使…充满 | To eat too much; to fill to excess. | He overgorged himself on Thanksgiving dinner and felt sick afterwards. 他在感恩节晚餐上暴饮暴食，之后感到恶心。| The river overgorged its banks and flooded the surrounding fields. 河水漫过河岸，淹没了周围的田野。 | 近义词: stuff, cram, glut, satiate | 反义词: starve, deprive | 词性: 动词

词源:
From over- + gorge (n.) "throat, stomach," from Old French gorge "throat, gullet," from Latin gurges "whirlpool, abyss, throat," perhaps related to gula "gullet."  The verb sense "to swallow greedily" is from c. 1300.  The meaning "to fill to excess" is from late 14c.
