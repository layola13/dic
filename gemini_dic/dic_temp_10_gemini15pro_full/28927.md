carrageen: /ˈkærɪɡiːn/ | n. | 角叉菜；爱尔兰苔藓 | A type of red seaweed (Chondrus crispus) that grows along the coasts of the North Atlantic, notable for its use in making a thickening agent for foods and other products. |  Carrageen is commonly used in desserts like puddings and ice cream. 角叉菜通常用于布丁和冰淇淋等甜点中。

Carrageen moss is harvested and dried for commercial use. 角叉菜被收集起来晒干以供商业用途。 | 近义词: Irish moss, seaweed | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "land plant" as a broad contrast) | 词性: noun
