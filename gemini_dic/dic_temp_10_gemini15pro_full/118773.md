metallogist:/məˈtælədʒɪst/ | n. | 冶金学家；金属学家 | A scientist or engineer who specializes in the study of metals, their properties, and their extraction and processing. | The metallogist analyzed the composition of the alloy. 冶金学家分析了合金的成分。|  The metallogist determined the best method for extracting the ore. 冶金学家确定了提取矿石的最佳方法。 | Synonyms: metallurgist, materials scientist | Antonyms: N/A | noun
