flashback:/ˈflæʃbæk/| n.|闪回；倒叙|A sudden and vivid memory of a past event, typically an emotionally charged one. |例句：The film uses flashbacks to tell the story of the protagonist's childhood. 这部电影用闪回讲述了主人公童年的故事。

例句：She experienced a flashback to the car accident. 她突然闪回到车祸发生的那一刻。|近义词：retrospection, reminiscence|反义词: foresight, premonition|词性:noun
