froe:/froʊ/
n.
劈木楔，劈木斧
A wedge-shaped tool with a handle on the back, used for splitting wood.
He used a froe to split the logs into firewood. 他用劈木楔把木头劈成柴火。
近义词：wedge, cleaver, splitter
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "joiner" as it performs the opposite function)
词性: noun


froe:/froʊ/
v.
用劈木楔劈开
To split with or as if with a froe.
He froed the log into two halves. 他用劈木楔把木头劈成两半。
近义词：split, cleave, rive
反义词: join, unite
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frowe, from Old English *frō, from Proto-Germanic *frōaną (“to rub, fray”), from Proto-Indo-European *preh₂- (“to rub, grind”). Cognate with Old High German frōn (“to rub, fray”), Old Norse frá (“from”).
