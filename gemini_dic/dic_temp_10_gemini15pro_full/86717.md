helminthagogue:/ˌhɛlmɪnˈθæɡɒɡ/ | n. | 驱肠虫药 | A medicine that expels parasitic worms from the intestines. |  The doctor prescribed a helminthagogue to treat the patient's intestinal worms. 医生给病人开了驱虫药来治疗肠道寄生虫。| synonyms: anthelmintic, vermifuge | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider concepts like "parasite promoter" - though not a real word) | noun
