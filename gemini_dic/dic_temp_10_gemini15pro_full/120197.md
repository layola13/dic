minefields:/ˈmaɪnˌfiːldz/
noun
雷区；危险地带
An area of land or water that contains mines. A situation presenting unseen hazards.
例句：The negotiators are proceeding cautiously through a minefield of conflicting interests. 谈判代表们在相互冲突的利益雷区中谨慎行事。

例句：The political landscape is a minefield for any new candidate. 对于任何新候选人来说，政治格局都是一个雷区。
近义词：danger zones, hazardous areas, perilous regions
反义词：safe zones, secure areas
词性:n.
