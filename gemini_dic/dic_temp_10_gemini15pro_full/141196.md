oompahed:/ˌuːmpəˈhɛd/ |v. |伴奏(尤指铜管乐);  (发出)低沉的喇叭声| to play a loud accompaniment to (something, especially singing) on a brass instrument;  to make a loud, low-pitched sound like a brass instrument|The band oompahed their way through the song. 乐队用铜管乐器为这首歌伴奏。| The tuba oompahed loudly. 低音号发出低沉的喇叭声。 | synonyms: accompany,  play,  blast,  toot | antonyms: silence, mute | verb
