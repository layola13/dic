nonesoterically:/ˌnɒnˌiːsəˈtɛrɪkli/ | adv. | 非神秘地；通俗地；不故弄玄虚地 |  In a way that is not esoteric; plainly and straightforwardly.  Explaining complex ideas in a nonesoteric way makes them accessible to a wider audience. | She explained the complex scientific theory nonesoterically, so even I could understand it. 她用通俗易懂的方式解释了这个复杂的科学理论，所以连我都听得懂。| synonym: plainly, simply, clearly, understandably | antonym: esoterically, obscurely, mystically | Adverb
