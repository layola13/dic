facilitates:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪts/
|v.|促进，使便利|Makes an action or process easier.|The new software facilitates communication between departments.|近义词：aids, assists, eases, expedites, promotes, simplifies, streamlines|反义词：hinders, obstructs, impedes, blocks|verb

Etymology:
From Latin *facilitāre*, present active infinitive of *facilitō* (“I make easy”), from *facilis* (“easy”).
