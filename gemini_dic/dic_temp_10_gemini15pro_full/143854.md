outplays:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪz/
verb
胜过，以技巧取胜
To perform better than another player in a game or competitive situation, especially through superior skill or strategy.
例句：She completely outplayed her opponent in the chess match. 她在棋赛中完全胜过了她的对手。

例句：The team outplayed their rivals and secured a convincing victory.  该队技高一筹，战胜了对手，取得了令人信服的胜利。

近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, defeat, beat
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
