autosave:/ˈɔːtoʊseɪv/| n. |自动保存|The act of automatically saving data to a file, usually at regular intervals, to prevent data loss in case of a system crash or power outage.|The autosave feature saved my work when the computer crashed.|近义词：automatic save, backup|反义词:manual save|noun
