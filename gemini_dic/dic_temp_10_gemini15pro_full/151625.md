participial:/ˌpɑːrtɪˈsɪpɪəl/
adjective
分词的
A participial is a word formed from a verb and used as an adjective or a noun.
The crying baby needed attention.  哭闹的婴儿需要关注。
Smiling, she accepted the award.  她微笑着接受了奖项。
近义词:verbal
反义词: (No direct antonym for a grammatical term)
词性:adjective
