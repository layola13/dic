horseplay:/ˈhɔːrspleɪ/ (US), /ˈhɔːspleɪ/ (UK)| n.| 胡闹，嬉戏，玩闹|Rough or boisterous play.|The children were engaging in horseplay in the backyard. 孩子们在后院嬉闹玩耍。|Synonyms: romping, frolicking, skylarking, clowning, antics|Antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, calmness|noun

Etymology:
From *horse* + *play*.  Originally referred to rough, boisterous play resembling the movements of a horse.
