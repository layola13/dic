morph:/mɔːrf/ | v. & n. | 变形，变体 | To change smoothly from one image to another by small gradual steps using computer animation techniques.  | Example: The caterpillar will morph into a butterfly. 毛毛虫会变成蝴蝶。 | Synonyms: transform, change, mutate, evolve, transmute, metamorphose | Antonyms: remain, stay, stagnate | verb

morph:/mɔːrf/ | n. | 变体，词素变体 | A form that a word takes depending on its grammatical function.  | Example:  "Cats" is a morph of "cat". “Cats”是“cat”的变体。 | Synonyms: variant, form, version, alteration | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for this noun sense) | noun
