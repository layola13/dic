diverging:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective
分叉的，岔开的，发散的
moving, extending, or spreading in different directions from a common point
例句：The diverging paths led to two different villages. 这条岔路通向两个不同的村庄。

例句：Their opinions on the matter are diverging. 他们在这个问题上的意见存在分歧。
近义词：separating, branching, forking, splitting
反义词：converging, meeting, uniting, joining
词性: adjective
