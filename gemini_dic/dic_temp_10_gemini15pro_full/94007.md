impala:/ɪmˈpɑːlə/ | n. |黑斑羚 |A medium-sized African antelope (Aepyceros melampus) with lyre-shaped horns, reddish-brown coat, and white underparts. It is known for its graceful leaping ability. | We saw a herd of impala grazing in the savanna. 我们看到一群黑斑羚在热带草原上吃草。|  A lone impala leaped across the road in front of our jeep. 一只孤独的黑斑羚在我们吉普车前的路上跳跃而过。 | synonyms: antelope, gazelle | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | noun
