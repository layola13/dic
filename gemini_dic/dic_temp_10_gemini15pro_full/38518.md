conciliatory:/kənˈsɪliətɔːri/ | adj. | 调解的；安抚的；和解的 | Intended or likely to placate or pacify. |  Example: She took a conciliatory approach to the dispute, hoping to avoid further conflict. 她对这场争端采取了和解的态度，希望避免进一步的冲突。

Example:  His conciliatory tone helped to calm the angry crowd.  他安抚的语气帮助平息了愤怒的人群。


| 近义词：appeasing, pacifying, mollifying, soothing, placatory | 反义词：antagonistic, belligerent, provocative, inflammatory | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conciliatorius, from Latin conciliare (“to bring together, unite, win over”), from concilium (“council, meeting, assembly”), from com- (“with, together”) + calare (“to call, summon”).
