digressingly:/daɪˈɡrɛsɪŋli/
adv. | 离题地，跑题地 | In a manner that deviates or wanders away from the main topic or point of discussion. |  He spoke digressingly about his childhood, losing the thread of his main argument. 他离题地谈起了他的童年，失去了主要论点的线索。 |  Example 2: She digressingly mentioned a funny anecdote during the serious presentation. 在严肃的演讲中，她跑题讲了一个有趣的小故事。 | synonym: ramblingly, tangentially, discursively | antonym: concisely, directly, relevantly | 词性:副词
