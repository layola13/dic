impetration:/ˌɪmpɪˈtreɪʃn/ | n. |恳求，祈求 | The act of obtaining something by earnest request or prayer.  It implies a persistent and sometimes insistent pleading. | The king's impetration for divine guidance went unanswered. 国王祈求神圣指引的恳求没有得到回应。|  The desperate farmer made an impetration for rain. 绝望的农民祈求下雨。 | synonyms: supplication, entreaty, plea, petition | antonyms: demand, command, order | noun
