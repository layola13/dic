legitimizes:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
使…合法化；使…正统化
To make something legal or acceptable; to justify or validate something.
The government legitimizes the use of force in self-defense. 政府认可自卫使用武力的合法性。
The election legitimizes his presidency.  这次选举使他的总统职位合法化。
Synonyms: validates, justifies, authorizes, sanctions
Antonyms: invalidates, delegitimizes, condemns, denounces
词性: 动词


legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adjective
合法的；正当的；合理的
Conforming to the law or to rules; able to be defended with logic or justification; genuine.
He had a legitimate reason for being late. 他迟到是有正当理由的。
Is this a legitimate business? 这是合法生意吗？
Synonyms: legal, valid, lawful, justifiable, genuine
Antonyms: illegal, invalid, unlawful, illegitimate, unjustified
词性: 形容词


legitimacy:/ləˈdʒɪtɪməsi/
noun
合法性；正统性；合理性
The quality or state of being legitimate.
The legitimacy of the election results is being questioned. 选举结果的合法性受到质疑。
The government's legitimacy is derived from the consent of the governed. 政府的合法性来源于被统治者的同意。
Synonyms: legality, validity, lawfulness, justifiability, genuineness
Antonyms: illegality, invalidity, unlawfulness, illegitimacy
词性: 名词


词源:  From Late Latin legitimatus, past participle of legitimare "to make lawful," from Latin legitimus "lawful," from lex (genitive legis) "law."
