boned:/boʊnd/| adj.|带骨的；除去骨头的；有…根骨头的|Having bones, or having had the bones removed.  From bone + -ed.|例句1：He ordered a boned chicken for dinner. 他晚餐点了一份去骨鸡肉。

例句2：The fish was boned before being grilled.  这条鱼在烤之前被去骨了。|近义词：bony, skeletal, osseous|反义词:boneless|词性:形容词
