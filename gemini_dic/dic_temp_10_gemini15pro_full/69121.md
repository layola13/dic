favonian:/fəˈvəʊniən/ /fəˈvoʊniən/| adj.|温和的，宜人的；西风的|Relating to the west wind; mild and gentle; favorable to growth.|The favonian breeze gently rustled the leaves. 温和的微风轻轻地吹动树叶。|The favonian climate made the island a popular destination. 宜人的气候使该岛成为热门目的地。|近义词：mild, gentle, balmy, pleasant|反义词：harsh, severe, inclement|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *favonius*, from Favonus, the Roman god of the west wind, equivalent to the Greek Zephyrus.
