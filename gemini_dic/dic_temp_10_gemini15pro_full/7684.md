anoint:/əˈnɔɪnt/| vt.|涂油；施以涂油礼；任命；圣化|To smear or rub with oil, especially as part of a religious ceremony; to consecrate or ordain to a sacred office; to choose for a special purpose.|The priest anointed the baby's forehead with holy water. 牧师用圣水涂抹婴儿的额头。|近义词：consecrate, ordain, bless, sanctify|反义词:desecrate, profane|verb
