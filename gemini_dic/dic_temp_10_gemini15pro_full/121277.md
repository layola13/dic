misfashion:/ˌmɪsˈfæʃən/
n.
过时的样式；不合时宜的打扮；不恰当的行为
An outdated or inappropriate style, manner, or practice.
Her dress was a complete misfashion. 她的裙子完全过时了。
近义词: faux pas, solecism, impropriety
反义词: fashion, vogue, style
词性: 名词
