bocardo:/boʊˈkɑːrdoʊ/
noun
驳卡多式三段论，一种逻辑谬误
A syllogism of the third figure in which the major premise is a particular affirmative proposition and the minor premise and conclusion are universal negative propositions.  A logical fallacy.
例句：The argument presented was a classic bocardo, failing to prove its conclusion.  提出的论点是一个典型的驳卡多式三段论，未能证明其结论。

例句：She recognized the bocardo in his reasoning and quickly refuted it. 她在他的推理中认出了驳卡多式三段论，并迅速反驳了它。

近义词：fallacy, error in reasoning, invalid argument
反义词：valid argument, sound reasoning
词性:n.
