parage:/ˈpærədʒ/| n.|分段，段落; lineage, descent; extraction.|A distinct section or subdivision of a written or printed work, usually dealing with a particular point or idea and separated from other sections by a space or indentation.  A lineage; descent.  Extraction.|The second parage of the document outlines the terms of the agreement.  该文件的第二段概述了协议条款。 He was of noble parage. 他出身名门。|近义词：paragraph, section, passage, excerpt, lineage, ancestry, descent|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *parage* (“lineage, extraction”), from *parer* (“to prepare, arrange, adorn”), from Latin *paro*, *parare* (“to prepare”). Doublet of *pare*.
