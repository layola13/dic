denouement: /ˌdeɪnuːˈmɑːŋ/ (US), /deɪˈnuːmɒŋ/ (UK) | n. |结局，收场|The denouement of the play was surprisingly predictable. 这部戏的结局出人意料地在意料之中。|The detective novel had a thrilling denouement. 这部侦探小说有一个惊险的结局。| 近义词: conclusion, resolution, outcome, climax | 反义词: beginning, start, introduction | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French dénouement, from dénouer (“to untie, unravel”), from des- (“un-”) + nouer (“to tie, knot”).
