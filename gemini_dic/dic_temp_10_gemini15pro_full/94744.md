improvidentially:/ˌɪmprɒvɪˈdɛnʃəli/
adverb
中文翻译：缺乏远见地；不谨慎地；鲁莽地
英文解析：In a way that lacks foresight or careful planning; rashly or imprudently.
例句：He improvidentially invested all his savings in a single, risky venture. 他不谨慎地把所有积蓄都投资到一项单一的、高风险的企业中。

例句：She improvidentially left the house without her keys or wallet. 她粗心地出门，没带钥匙和钱包。

近义词：rashly, imprudently, thoughtlessly, carelessly, recklessly
反义词：prudently, wisely, cautiously, carefully, thoughtfully
词性：adverb

词源:  From Latin *improvidentialis, from improvidens (“imprudent, unforeseeing”) + -alis.  Improvidens is from in- (“not”) + providens, present participle of providēre (“to foresee, provide”).  Ultimately from pro- (“before”) + vidēre (“to see”).
