out-toil: /ˌaʊtˈtɔɪl/
verb
超越; 胜过 (通过更辛苦地工作)
to work harder and longer than someone else
例句：He out-toiled his competitors and won the race. 他比他的竞争对手更加努力，赢得了比赛。

例句：She out-toiled everyone in the office, often staying late into the night. 她在办公室里比任何人都努力，经常加班到深夜。

近义词：outwork, surpass, exceed
反义词：be outworked, be surpassed

词性: v.
