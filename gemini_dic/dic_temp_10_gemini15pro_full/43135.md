coypu:/ˈkɔɪpuː/ (US) /ˈkɔɪpjuː/ (UK)| n.|海狸鼠，河狸鼠|A large, South American, aquatic rodent with webbed hind feet and dense, reddish-brown fur, introduced to other parts of the world for its fur.|Coypus are considered an invasive species in many areas. 海狸鼠在许多地区被认为是入侵物种。|
Coypus are native to South America and are now found in North America, Europe, and Asia. 海狸鼠原产于南美洲，现在在北美、欧洲和亚洲都有发现。
|synonyms: nutria, swamp beaver|antonyms: None|noun
