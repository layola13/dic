overofficiously: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 过于殷勤地，过分 officious 地 | Characterized by excessive eagerness in offering unwanted services or advice.  He overofficiously rearranged the furniture, even though the homeowner had asked him not to. 他过于殷勤地重新摆放了家具，即使房主已经要求他不要这样做。 | 近义词: officiously, meddlesomely, intrusively | 反义词: discreetly, unobtrusively, respectfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "officious" (offering unwanted services) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Officious" comes from Latin "officiosus" (dutiful, obliging), from "officium" (duty, service).
