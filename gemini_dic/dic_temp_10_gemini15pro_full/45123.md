cucumiform: /kjuːˈkjuːmɪfɔːrm/
adjective
黄瓜形的；瓜形的
shaped like a cucumber.
例句：The virus particles are cucumiform in shape. 病毒颗粒呈黄瓜状。

例句：The fruit of this rare plant is cucumiform and covered in small spines.  这种稀有植物的果实呈黄瓜状，并覆盖着小刺。
近义词：cucumber-shaped, oblong, elongated
反义词: round, spherical
词性: adjective
