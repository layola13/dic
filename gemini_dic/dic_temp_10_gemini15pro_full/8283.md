anti-libertarian:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/| adj. n.| 反自由主义的，反自由主义者| Opposed to libertarianism; a person who opposes libertarianism. |  The anti-libertarian argued for stronger government regulation.  反自由主义者主张加强政府监管。|  He wrote an anti-libertarian article criticizing deregulation. 他写了一篇反自由主义的文章，批评放松管制。|近义词：authoritarian, statist, collectivist|反义词：libertarian, individualist|词性：形容词, 名词


Etymology:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposed to (from Greek *antí)
libertarian (noun/adjective):  advocating individual liberty (from Latin *lībertās "freedom")
