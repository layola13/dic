archon:/ˈɑːrkɒn/ (UK) /ˈɑːrkɑːn/ (US)| n. |执政官，统治者|A ruler or magistrate in ancient Greece, especially in Athens.  A powerful figure who held a leading position in government. |The archon made a decree that all citizens must pay taxes. 执政官颁布法令，所有公民都必须纳税。| synonym: ruler, magistrate, governor | antonym: subject, citizen | noun
