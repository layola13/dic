infantilize: /ˈɪnfəntɪlaɪz/ /ɪnˈfæn.t̬əl.aɪz/ | vt. | 幼稚化；像对待小孩一样对待；使幼儿化 | To treat (someone) as a child or in a way that denies their maturity. | He accused her of infantilizing him by constantly reminding him to take his vitamins.  他指责她总是提醒他吃维生素，把他当孩子对待。 |  The marketing campaign infantilized women by portraying them as obsessed with shopping. 这场营销活动将女性描绘成购物狂，从而使她们幼稚化。 | synonyms: patronize, condescend, coddle, baby | antonyms: empower, mature, respect, treat as an adult | verb
