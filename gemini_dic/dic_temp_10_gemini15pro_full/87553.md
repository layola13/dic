heteromyaria:/ˌhetəroʊmaɪˈɛəriə/ | n. | 异鼠类 | A suborder of rodents characterized by external fur-lined cheek pouches used for carrying food.  They are native to North and South America. | The kangaroo rat is a member of the Heteromyaria. 袋鼠鼠是异鼠类的一员。|  Synonym: Heteromyidae (sometimes used interchangeably) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym as it's a taxonomic classification;  could possibly contrast with other rodent suborders like Sciuromorpha or Myomorpha) | noun
