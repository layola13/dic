noncredible:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɛdəbəl/
adjective
不可信的
not believable; lacking credibility.
例句：The witness's testimony was deemed noncredible due to inconsistencies in his story.  证人的证词由于故事中的前后矛盾被认为不可信。

例句：The politician's noncredible promises led to a decline in his popularity.  这位政客不可信的承诺导致他的支持率下降。

近义词：unbelievable, implausible, dubious, questionable
反义词：credible, believable, plausible, trustworthy
词性：adjective
