nonbuddings:/ˌnɒnˈbʌdɪŋz/
n.
不出芽的，不萌芽的
organisms or cells that do not undergo budding (a form of asexual reproduction).
Nonbudding yeast strains are preferred in some industrial processes.  不出芽的酵母菌株在某些工业过程中是首选的。
These nonbudding cells were observed under the microscope.  这些不出芽的细胞在显微镜下被观察到。
近义词:non-germinating, non-sprouting
反义词:budding, germinating, sprouting
词性:名词


Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "budding" (present participle of "bud," meaning to sprout or produce buds).  From Old English “budda”
