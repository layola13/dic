annunciator:/əˈnʌnsiˌeɪtər/| n.|通报器，指示器，播音员|A device or person that announces something, typically arrival, departure, or an emergency.  A device that displays information or signals. |The annunciator flashed, indicating a problem in engine room three.|近义词：indicator, display, announcer, signaler|反义词: None|noun
