haliotoid:/ˌhælɪˈɔɪtɔɪd/ | adj. n. |鲍壳状的，鲍鱼状的；鲍鱼状贝壳 |Resembling an abalone or its shell, especially in being ear-shaped and iridescent.  |The haliotoid shell shimmered with rainbow hues.  鲍鱼状的贝壳闪耀着彩虹般的色调。| The craftsman created a haliotoid ornament from polished wood.  工匠用抛光木头制作了一个鲍鱼状的装饰品。 |Synonyms:  ear-shaped, iridescent, nacreous | Antonyms: dull, matte | adjective, noun
