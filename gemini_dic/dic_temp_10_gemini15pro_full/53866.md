discontinuee:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuˈiː/| n.|中止者，停止使用者|A person who has ceased using a product or service.|The company sent out surveys to its discontinuees to understand why they left.|Synonym: ex-user, former customer|Antonym: user, customer|noun

discontinue:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/| vt.|停止，中断|To stop doing something; to cease.|The company decided to discontinue the production of this model.|Synonym: cease, stop, terminate, abandon|Antonym: continue, start, commence|verb
