depolarizer:/diːˈpoʊləˌraɪzər/ |n.|去极化剂，消偏振器|A substance that removes polarization, especially from a nerve or cell membrane, enabling repolarization to occur.|The depolarizer in this battery helps maintain a consistent voltage.  这种电池中的去极化剂有助于保持稳定的电压。|Example:  The scientist added a depolarizer to the solution to counteract the polarization effect. 科学家在溶液中加入了去极化剂以抵消极化效应。|Synonyms:  neutralizer, counteragent|Antonyms:  polarizer|noun
