absist:/æbˈsɪst/| v. |戒除，禁绝|To voluntarily refrain from something, typically a habit or practice.|He decided to absist from alcohol after years of heavy drinking. 他决定在多年酗酒后戒酒。

The doctor advised him to absist from smoking. 医生建议他戒烟。

近义词：refrain, abstain, forbear, desist|反义词：indulge, partake|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abstinere "to hold back from," from abs- "from" + tenere "to hold."
