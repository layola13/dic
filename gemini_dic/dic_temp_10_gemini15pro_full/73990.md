fortresslike:/ˈfɔːrtrəsˌlaɪk/| adj.|像堡垒一样的；坚固的|Resembling a fortress, especially in being strongly fortified and difficult to penetrate.|The house, with its high walls and barred windows, had a fortresslike appearance.  这栋房子，高墙和带栅栏的窗户，看起来像个堡垒。|The building had a fortresslike exterior.  这栋楼的外观像个堡垒。|近义词：impregnable, fortified, secure, invulnerable|反义词:vulnerable, weak, exposed, defenseless|词性:形容词

词源:
From "fortress" (n.) + "-like" (suffix).  "Fortress" comes from Old French "forteresce," from "fort" (strong) + "-esse" (suffix forming nouns of quality).  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.  由名词"fortress"和后缀"-like"构成。"Fortress"源于古法语"forteresce"，由"fort"（坚固的）和"-esse"（构成名词的后缀）组成。"-like"是一个常见的用于构成形容词的后缀，表示相似。
