The child overresisted taking the medicine, making it a traumatic experience.  孩子过度抵抗吃药，使其成为了一种创伤性经历。 | 近义词: overreact, oppose excessively | 反义词: yield, submit, comply | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "resist" (from Latin resistere, "to stand back, withstand").
