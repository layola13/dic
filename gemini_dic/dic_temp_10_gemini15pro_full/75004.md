friend's:/frendz/ | n. |朋友的，朋友的(东西)|  Something that belongs to a friend. | This is my friend's car. 这是我朋友的车。| My friend's house is very big. 我朋友的房子很大。 |近义词: pal's, mate's, buddy's |反义词: enemy's, foe's |词性:noun (possessive)

词源:
From Old English *frēond, from Proto-Germanic *frijōndz (“lover, friend”), from *frijō (“to love”) + *-jōndz (“-ing”). Cognate with Old Saxon friund, Old Frisian friond, Dutch vriend, Old High German friunt, German Freund, Old Norse frændi (“kinsman, relative”).
