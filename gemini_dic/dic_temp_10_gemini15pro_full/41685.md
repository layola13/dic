corresponding:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndɪŋ/ | adj. |相应的，对应的；符合的，一致的|being similar or analogous; matching or agreeing|例句：The corresponding period last year saw a significant increase in sales. 去年同期销售额显著增长。|近义词：equivalent, similar, analogous, matching, related, parallel|反义词：different, dissimilar, unrelated, contrasting|词性:形容词

corresponding:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndɪŋ/ | n. |对应，通信|the activity of writing letters or emails to someone|例句：I have a lot of corresponding to do today. 我今天有很多信件要回复。|近义词：communication, correspondence|反义词：None|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *correspondent*, from Old French *correspondant*, present participle of *correspondre*, from Latin *correspondēre* (“to correspond”), from *com-* (“with, together”) + *respondēre* (“to answer, respond”).
