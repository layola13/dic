castilians:/kæˈstɪliənz/
n.
卡斯蒂利亚人；卡斯蒂利亚语使用者
People from Castile, a region in Spain, or those who speak Castilian Spanish.
Many Castilians migrated to the Americas during the colonial period. 许多卡斯蒂利亚人在殖民时期移民到美洲。
synonyms: Castilian Spanish speakers, people from Castile
