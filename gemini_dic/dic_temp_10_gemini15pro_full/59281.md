eagles:/ˈiːɡlz/ | n. |老鹰（复数）|A large, strong bird of prey with a hooked beak and powerful talons, known for its keen eyesight and soaring flight.  The plural form of "eagle". |例句：The eagles soared high above the mountains. 老鹰在群山之上翱翔。

例句：We saw a pair of bald eagles nesting near the river. 我们看到一对白头鹰在河边筑巢。|近义词：raptors, birds of prey |反义词:prey |词性:noun
