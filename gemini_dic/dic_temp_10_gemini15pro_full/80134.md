gobstopper:/ˈɡɒbstɒpə(r)/ (US: /ˈɡɑːbstɑːpər/) | n. |  高布斯托珀糖，彩虹糖，一种硬糖 | A large, hard, spherical candy that changes colors and flavors as it is sucked or licked. | Example: The child loved the gobstopper because it lasted a long time and changed flavors. 孩子喜欢这种硬糖，因为它可以吃很久而且味道会变。 | Example:  He kept the gobstopper in his mouth for hours, enjoying the changing flavors.  他把硬糖含在嘴里好几个小时，享受着不断变化的味道。| Synonym: jawbreaker, everlasting gobstopper | Antonym: N/A | Noun
