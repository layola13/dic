nonembryonal:/ˌnɑːnˈɛmbriːənəl/
adjective
非胚胎的
Not derived from an embryo; not characteristic of an embryo.  Relating to tumors that arise from tissues other than embryonic tissues.
Nonembryonal carcinomas of the testis are less common than embryonal carcinomas.  睾丸非胚胎性癌不如胚胎性癌常见。
Near synonyms: nonepithelial, extraembryonic
Opposite: embryonal
词性: adjective
