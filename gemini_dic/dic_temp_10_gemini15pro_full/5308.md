allotters: /əˈlɒtərz/
n.
分配者，分配人
People who allot something, those responsible for distributing or assigning shares or portions.
The allotters decided to give each family a plot of land. 分配者决定给每个家庭一块土地。
The allotters ensured everyone received a fair share of the resources.  分配者确保每个人都得到公平的资源份额。
近义词: distributors, assigners, allocators
反义词: recipients, receivers
