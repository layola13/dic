convincement:/kənˈvɪnsmənt/
n.
说服;信服;确信
The act of convincing, or the state of being convinced.
His convincement of their innocence led him to defend them. 他确信他们是无辜的，所以为他们辩护。
近义词:conviction, persuasion, assurance
反义词:doubt, uncertainty
词性:名词

词源: From convince + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin convincere "to overcome, convict, prove wrong."
