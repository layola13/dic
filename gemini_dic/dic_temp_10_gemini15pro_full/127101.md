nationalistically:/ˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪkli/
adverb
民族主义地；国家主义地
In a way that shows strong support for the interests of one's own nation and belief that it is superior to other nations.
He spoke nationalistically about the country's glorious past. 他以民族主义的口吻谈论了国家光荣的过去。
The government is acting nationalistically to protect its own industries. 政府正以民族主义的方式保护其本国产业。
Synonyms: patriotically, chauvinistically
Antonyms: internationally, globally
词性: 副词

nationalistic:/ˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
民族主义的；国家主义的
Having strong patriotic feelings, especially believing that your country is better than others.
His nationalistic sentiments led him to believe his country was always right. 他的民族主义情绪使他相信他的国家总是正确的。
The party adopted a more nationalistic stance in its foreign policy. 该党在其外交政策中采取了更具民族主义的立场。
Synonyms: patriotic, chauvinistic
Antonyms: international, cosmopolitan
词性: 形容词

nation:/ˈneɪʃən/
noun
国家；民族
A large body of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular country or territory.
The nation mourned the death of its beloved leader. 全国人民哀悼他们敬爱的领袖的逝世。
Several nations competed in the international sporting event.  几个国家参加了这项国际体育赛事。
Synonyms: country, state, land
Antonyms: world, globe
词性: 名词
