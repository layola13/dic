loyalnesses:/ˈlɔɪəltɪnɪsɪz/
n.
忠诚，忠心
The state or quality of being loyal; faithfulness.
His loyalnesses to the company were unwavering. 他对公司的忠诚毫不动摇。
近义词: fidelities, allegiances
反义词: treacheries, betrayals
词性: 名词

词源：From Middle English *loyalnesse, from Old French loialte + -nesse. Equivalent to loyal + -ness.
