convictions:/kənˈvɪkʃənz/
n.
信念，确信；
A firmly held belief or opinion.
例句：He has strong political convictions. 他有坚定的政治信念。

例句：She spoke with conviction about the need for change. 她充满确信地谈到变革的必要性。

近义词：beliefs, opinions, principles, persuasions
反义词：doubts, uncertainties, skepticism

n.
定罪，判罪；
A formal declaration that someone is guilty of a criminal offense, made by the verdict of a jury or the decision of a judge in a court of law.
例句：He had two previous convictions for theft.  他之前有两项盗窃罪的定罪记录。

例句：The conviction was overturned on appeal.  上诉后，判决被推翻了。

近义词：sentence, judgment, verdict
反义词：acquittal, exoneration


词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  From Late Latin convictio, from Latin convincere "to overcome, prove wrong, convict," from com- "together" + vincere "to conquer."
