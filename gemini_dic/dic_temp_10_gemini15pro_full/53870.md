discontinuing:/dɪskənˈtɪnjuːɪŋ/
verb
停止，中止
The act of ending something; ceasing to do, use, or produce something.
They are discontinuing the production of this model. 他们将停止生产这款型号。
近义词: ceasing, stopping, abandoning, halting, terminating
反义词: continuing, starting, commencing, initiating, resuming
词性: Verb (Present Participle)
