aeromechanics: /ˌɛroʊmɪˈkænɪks/
n.
空气力学
The study of the forces and motion of air and other gases, especially in relation to their interaction with solid bodies.
Aeromechanics plays a crucial role in aircraft design.  空气力学在飞机设计中起着至关重要的作用。

The principles of aeromechanics are essential for understanding bird flight.  空气力学原理对于理解鸟类飞行至关重要。

近义词: gas dynamics, fluid dynamics
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + mechanics (the study of forces and motion).  Aero- comes from Greek ἀήρ (aēr, "air"). Mechanics comes from Greek μηχανική (mēkhanikē, "contrivance").
