falls:/fɔːlz/| v. |落下; 跌倒; 降低; 成为; 属于|To descend freely by the force of gravity. To lose one's balance and collapse. To become lower in amount, degree, or intensity. To come under a particular classification or category.|例句：The rain falls softly on the roof. 雨轻轻地落在屋顶上。
