creasy:/ˈkriːsi/ | adj. | 有皱纹的；易皱的 | Covered with or characterized by creases; wrinkled. | The old map was creasy and torn. 这张旧地图又皱又破。| 近义词: wrinkled, crumpled, folded | 反义词: smooth, ironed | 词性: 形容词

creasy:/ˈkriːsi/ | adj. | (非正式) 疯狂的；神经错乱的 | (Informal) Insane; mentally unstable. | He went a little creasy after losing his job. 他失业后有点精神错乱。| 近义词: crazy, insane, mad | 反义词: sane, rational | 词性: 形容词
