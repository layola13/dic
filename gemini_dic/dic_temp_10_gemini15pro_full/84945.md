hanyang: /ˈhɑːnˌjɑːŋ/ | n. | 汉阳 | Hanyang is a district of Wuhan, a major city in central China.  It is situated on the west bank of the Han River, opposite the Wuchang and Hankou districts, with which it forms the tripartite metropolitan area of Wuhan.  Historically, Hanyang was one of the three towns that merged to form modern Wuhan. | Hanyang played a crucial role in the 1911 Revolution. 汉阳在1911年的辛亥革命中扮演了至关重要的角色。 |  Wuhan, Hankou, Wuchang | N/A | noun
