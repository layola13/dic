panel:/ˈpæn(ə)l/ (US) /ˈpænl/ (UK)| v. |镶嵌板;  组成专家小组 |To cover or decorate with panels.| The walls were paneled with dark wood. 墙壁镶嵌着深色木板。| synonyms: cover, decorate | antonyms: uncover, strip | verb
