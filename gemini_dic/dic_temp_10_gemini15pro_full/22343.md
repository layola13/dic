bonobo:/bəˈnoʊboʊ/ (US), /bəˈnɒbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|倭黑猩猩|A small, endangered ape found in the rainforests of the Democratic Republic of Congo, closely related to the chimpanzee and known for its peaceful and matriarchal social structure. It is also known for its frequent sexual activity, used for social bonding and conflict resolution.|例句：The bonobo is known for its intelligence and social skills. 倭黑猩猩以其智力和社交技巧而闻名。

例句：Researchers study bonobos to learn more about primate behavior. 研究人员研究倭黑猩猩以了解更多关于灵长类动物的行为。|近义词：ape, chimpanzee|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
