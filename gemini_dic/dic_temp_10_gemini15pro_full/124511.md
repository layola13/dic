mounding:/ˈmaʊndɪŋ/ | n. | 堆积，隆起 | The process of forming something into a mound or the state of being mounded.  A rounded mass projecting from a level surface. | The mounding of earth around the base of the tree helped protect its roots.  树根周围的土堆有助于保护它的根部。 | synonyms: heaping, piling, accumulating | antonyms: leveling, flattening | noun

mounding:/ˈmaʊndɪŋ/ | adj. | 隆起的，堆积的 |  Rising or swelling into a rounded shape. |  The mounding waves crashed against the shore.  隆起的波浪拍打着海岸。 | synonyms:  rounded, swelling, bulging | antonyms: flat, level | adjective
