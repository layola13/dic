blastiest:/ˈblæstiɪst/
adjective
最该死的；最讨厌的；最糟糕的
(superlative of) blasty: extremely unpleasant or annoying.  Expressing annoyance or disapproval.
例句：That was the blastiest movie I've ever seen. 那是我看过的最烂的电影。

例句：He's the blastiest person I know; always complaining. 他是我认识的最讨厌的人，总是抱怨。

近义词：damnedest, cursed, infernal, worst
反义词：best, greatest, finest
词性:adjective
