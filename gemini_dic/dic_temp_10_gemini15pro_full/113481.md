lyricism: /ˈlɪrɪsɪzəm/| n.|抒情性；抒情手法；富有诗意|The quality of being lyrical, expressing deep personal emotion or observations in a beautiful and imaginative way.  The state or quality of being lyric; specifically, the character of song or poetry as distinguished from other forms of literature.|Her prose is marked by a rare lyricism. 她的散文以罕见的抒情性为特征。|synonyms: poetry, melodiousness, musicality, rhythmicality, tunefulness, euphony, harmony, sweetness, mellifluousness; expressiveness, emotion, passion, feeling, pathos, intensity;  poeticness, lyricality, romanticism.|antonyms: prose, dullness|noun
