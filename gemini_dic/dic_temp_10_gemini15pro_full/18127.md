beholder:/bɪˈhəʊldər/ (US) /bɪˈhoʊldər/| n.|观看者；旁观者|A beholder is someone who sees or observes something.  A person who looks on or observes; a spectator.|The beauty of the sunset was appreciated by every beholder.  日落的美丽被每一位观看者所欣赏。|synonyms: observer, spectator, onlooker, witness|antonyms: participant, performer|noun
