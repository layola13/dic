archivolt:/ˈɑːrkɪvɒlt/| n.|拱门饰带，券面|An archivolt is an ornamental molding or band following the curve of an arch.  It is the decorated molding framing an arch, especially in Romanesque and Gothic architecture.|The archivolt of the cathedral entrance was intricately carved with biblical scenes.| 近义词: arch molding, extrados|反义词: (No direct antonym)| noun
