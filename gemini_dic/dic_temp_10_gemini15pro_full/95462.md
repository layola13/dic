incitant:/ɪnˈsaɪtənt/ | n. adj.| 刺激物；刺激的 |Something that incites or stimulates; an incentive.  Can also be used as an adjective meaning stimulating or provocative.| The politician's fiery speech acted as an incitant to the crowd.  这位政治家的慷慨激昂的演讲煽动了人群。| 近义词：stimulus, stimulant, provocation, incentive | 反义词：depressant, sedative, deterrent |词性: 名词, 形容词
词源: From Late Latin incitant- ‘inciting,’ from the verb incitare, from in- ‘into’ + citare ‘rouse.’
