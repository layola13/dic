deoxidizer:/diːˈɒksaɪdaɪzər/ | n. | 脱氧剂，除氧剂 | A substance that removes oxygen from a material or system. |  Aluminum is a powerful deoxidizer used in steelmaking. 铝是炼钢中一种强力脱氧剂。

The deoxidizer prevented the oxidation of the metal.  这种脱氧剂防止了金属的氧化。| 近义词: oxygen scavenger, reducing agent | 反义词: oxidizer, oxidizing agent | 词性: noun

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove") + oxidize + -er (suffix forming agent nouns)  来自前缀 de-（意为“去除”）+ oxidize（氧化）+ 后缀 -er（构成表示“使…的人或物”的名词）
