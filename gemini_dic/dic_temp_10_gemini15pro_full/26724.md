cadge:/kædʒ/
verb
乞讨，索取
to ask for or get something from someone, often without intending to pay for it or return it
He cadged a cigarette from me. 他向我讨了一根烟。
近义词：beg, scrounge, sponge
反义词：give, provide, earn
词性: verb

词源:
Originates from the early 19th century, possibly from Scottish or northern English dialect.  Its precise origin is uncertain, but it may be related to words meaning "peddler" or "carrier."
