antimusics:/ˌæntiˈmjuːzɪks/
n.
反音乐，非音乐
Experimental music or sound that challenges traditional notions of musicality.
John Cage's 4'33" is often cited as an example of antimusics.
约翰·凯奇的《4分33秒》常被认为是反音乐的例子。
experimental music, noise music, avant-garde music
traditional music, classical music
