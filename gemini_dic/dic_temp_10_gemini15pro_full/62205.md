encourage:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/ /ɛnˈkɝːɪdʒ/| vt.|鼓励，激励，促进|To give support, confidence, or hope to (someone).|My teacher encouraged me to apply for the scholarship. 我的老师鼓励我申请奖学金。|
The coach encouraged the team to play their best. 教练鼓励队员们发挥最佳水平。|
近义词：inspire, motivate, support, promote|反义词:discourage, hinder, deter|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *encoragier, from en- "in" + corage "courage," from Latin cor "heart."
