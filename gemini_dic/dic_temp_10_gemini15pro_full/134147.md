nonimpregnated: /ˌnɒnɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未浸渍的，未渗透的 | Not impregnated; not saturated or permeated with a substance. | The researchers used nonimpregnated wood in the control group. 研究人员在对照组中使用了未浸渍的木材。|  The fabric was nonimpregnated and therefore susceptible to water damage. 这块布料未经浸渍，因此容易受到水的损害。 | 近义词: untreated, unprocessed, pure | 反义词: impregnated, saturated, permeated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "impregnated" (from Latin "impraegnare," meaning "to make pregnant," later broadened to mean "to saturate").
