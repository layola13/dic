adumbrate: /ædˈʌmbreɪt/
verb
预示;  遮蔽;  勾勒;  略述
to suggest, disclose, or outline partially
The architect adumbrated his plans for the new building.  建筑师概述了他对新建筑的计划。

The recent economic downturn adumbrates a more serious recession. 最近的经济衰退预示着更严重的衰退即将到来。

近义词: foreshadow, prefigure, intimate, suggest, outline, sketch
反义词: reveal, clarify, illuminate, elaborate


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adumbrare (“to sketch, outline”), from ad- (“to”) + umbra (“shadow”)
