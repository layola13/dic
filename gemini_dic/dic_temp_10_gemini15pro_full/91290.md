hydrologist:/haɪˈdrɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 水文学家 | A scientist who studies the distribution, circulation, and properties of water on the earth and in the atmosphere. |  The hydrologist studied the river's flow rate.  水文学家研究了河流的流速。

The hydrologist predicted the flood. 水文学家预测了洪水。| 近义词: water scientist, limnologist (specifically for freshwater) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From hydro- (water) + -logy (study of) + -ist (one who studies)  Ultimately from Greek "hydōr" (water) and "logos" (study).
