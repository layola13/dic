hyperresponsivenesses:/ˌhaɪpərɪsˈpɑːnsɪvnəsɪz/ | n. |过度反应|The state of being excessively or abnormally responsive to stimuli.  It refers to a heightened or exaggerated reaction to sensory input or other stimuli.|
The child's hyperresponsivenesses to loud noises made family outings difficult.  这孩子对噪音的过度反应让家庭出游变得困难。|
Her hyperresponsivenesses to certain foods suggested a possible allergy. 她对某些食物的过度反应表明可能有过敏症。|
近义词: hypersensitivity, oversensitivity |反义词: hyposensitivity, unresponsiveness |词性: 名词

词源:
hyper- (Greek): over, above, excessive
response (Latin respondere): to answer, reply
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives
-ness (suffix): forming nouns from adjectives
-es (suffix): plural form
