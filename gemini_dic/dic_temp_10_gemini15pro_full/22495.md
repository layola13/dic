bookworms:/ˈbʊkwɜːrmz/
n.
书虫；酷爱读书的人
A person who spends a great deal of time reading.
She's a real bookworm and always has her nose in a book. 她是个真正的书虫，总是埋头读书。
My daughter is such a bookworm; she reads at least a book a week. 我的女儿真是个书虫，她每周至少读一本书。

近义词:bibliophile, avid reader, voracious reader
反义词:illiterate, non-reader
