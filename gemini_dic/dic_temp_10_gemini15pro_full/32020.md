chequing:/ˈtʃɛkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃekɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|支票的，支票账户|Relating to or involving the use of cheques; a checking account.  A chequing account is a bank account that allows you to write cheques.|I deposited the money into my chequing account. 我把钱存入了我的支票账户。|Synonym: checking (US), current (UK)|Antonym: savings|adjective, noun
