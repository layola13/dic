microlite:/ˈmaɪkrəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 微晶；微镜下的小晶体 | A microscopic crystal, especially in a glassy matrix. | The volcanic rock contained numerous microlites. 这块火山岩包含许多微晶。| synonyms: microcrystal, crystallite | antonyms: macrocrystal, megacrystal | noun

microlite:/ˈmaɪkrəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 微晶石 | A fine-grained igneous rock. | Microlite is a type of volcanic rock. 微晶石是一种火山岩。 | synonyms: volcanic rock, igneous rock | antonyms: sedimentary rock, metamorphic rock | noun
