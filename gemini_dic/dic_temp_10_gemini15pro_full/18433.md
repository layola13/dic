belvedere:/ˈbɛlvɪdɪər/ | n. |凉亭，观景楼；美景|A building, or part of a building, situated so as to command a fine view; a structure designed to take advantage of a scenic view.|From the window of my belvedere, I could see the entire city. 从我的观景楼的窗户，我能看到整个城市。|Synonyms: gazebo, pavilion, lookout, vista, panorama|Antonyms: dungeon, basement|noun
