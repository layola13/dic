necrological:/ˌnɛkrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
讣告的；死亡的；关于死亡的
Relating to or resembling an obituary.
The newspaper published a necrological notice about the deceased scientist.  这家报纸刊登了一篇关于这位已故科学家的讣告。
The historian unearthed some necrological records from the 18th century. 这位历史学家发掘了一些18世纪的死亡记录。
近义词:obituary, funerary, mortuary
反义词:vital, living
