countermobilization: /ˌkaʊntərˌmoʊbɪlɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌkaʊntəˌməʊbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 反动员，对抗动员 | The act of organizing and preparing forces to oppose a mobilization.  It can refer to military preparations but also to social or political movements organized in opposition to another. |  The government's countermobilization efforts included deploying troops to the border. 政府的反动员工作包括向边境部署军队。

The union launched a countermobilization campaign to oppose the proposed layoffs. 工会发起了一场反动员运动，反对拟议中的裁员。 | 近义词: counter-offensive, countermeasure | 反义词: mobilization | 词性: 名词
