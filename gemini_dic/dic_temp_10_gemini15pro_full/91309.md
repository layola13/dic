hydrometer:/haɪˈdrɒmɪtər/ (US) /haɪˈdrɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|液体比重计；浮秤|An instrument used for measuring the relative density of liquids. |Example 1: The brewer used a hydrometer to measure the specific gravity of the wort.  酿酒师用液体比重计测量麦芽汁的比重。

Example 2:  A hydrometer is essential for checking the battery acid's charge. 液体比重计对于检查电池酸液的充电状态至关重要。|近义词：densitometer, areometer|反义词: None|词性:noun


Etymology->
From hydro- + -meter.  "Hydro-" comes from the Greek "hydōr" (water), and "-meter" comes from the Greek "metron" (measure).
