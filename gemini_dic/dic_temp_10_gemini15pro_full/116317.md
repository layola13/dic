masterdom:/ˈmæstərdəm/ | n. | 支配；统治；精通；掌握 | The state of being a master; control; mastery. | He achieved masterdom over his craft after years of dedicated practice.  他经过多年的刻苦练习，精通了他的手艺。 | 近义词：mastery, dominance, command, expertise | 反义词：subservience, weakness, incompetence | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "master" + "-dom" (suffix forming nouns meaning "state or condition of being," "domain," "rank," or "dignity").
