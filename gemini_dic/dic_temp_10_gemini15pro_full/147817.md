overprovoke: /ˌōvərprəˈvōk/
verb
过度激怒；过分挑衅
To provoke excessively; to incite beyond reasonable limits.
His constant insults finally overprovoked her into slapping him. 他不断的侮辱最终让她忍无可忍，给了他一巴掌。

近义词:  inflame, aggravate, exasperate, goad
反义词:  placate, appease, soothe, calm
词性: verb
