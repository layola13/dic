bluesy:/ˈbluːzi/ | adj. | 忧郁的，悲伤的；布鲁斯风格的 | Feeling or expressing sadness, typically with a slow tempo and minor key, characteristic of blues music. |  The song has a bluesy feel to it. 这首歌有一种忧郁的感觉。 | Synonyms: melancholic, mournful, wistful, plaintive, sad, blue | Antonyms: cheerful, happy, upbeat, joyful | adjective


bluesy:/ˈbluːzi/ | n. | 布鲁斯音乐 | Blues music. (Informal) | He played some bluesy on the piano. 他在钢琴上演奏了一些布鲁斯音乐。| Synonyms: blues | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider other genres like pop, rock, classical) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "blues" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Blues" originated in African American communities in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, expressing melancholy and hardship.
