chattery: /ˈtʃætəri/ (US) /ˈtʃætəri/ (UK)| adj. n.|喋喋不休的，饶舌的； 闲谈，聊天|Characterized by frequent and informal conversation, often about unimportant things;  light informal conversation.|The room was filled with the chatter of excited children. 房间里充满了孩子们兴奋的喋喋不休。|近义词：talkative, loquacious, garrulous|反义词：quiet, silent|词性:形容词,名词
chatter: /ˈtʃætər/ (US) /ˈtʃætər/ (UK)| v. n.|喋喋不休，唠叨； 咔哒声|Talk rapidly or incessantly about unimportant matters; rapid clicking sounds.|The monkeys chattered excitedly in the trees. 猴子在树上兴奋地吱吱喳喳叫。|近义词：prattle, babble, jabber|反义词：be quiet, be silent|词性:动词,名词
