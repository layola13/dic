hygrometrical:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
湿度测量的;测湿的
Of or relating to hygrometry; used to measure humidity.
A hygrometrical instrument is used to measure the humidity of the air. 一种湿度测量仪器被用来测量空气的湿度。
The researchers used a hygrometrical sensor to monitor the moisture content of the soil. 研究人员使用湿度传感器来监测土壤的湿度。
近义词:hydroscopic, hygroscopic
反义词:None
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hygros* (wet) + *metron* (measure)
