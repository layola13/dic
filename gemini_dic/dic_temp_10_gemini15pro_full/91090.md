huzza:/həˈzɑː/ |int. n.|欢呼；加油；万岁|A shout of encouragement, triumph, or applause. | The crowd cried "Huzza!" as the team scored.  人群在球队得分时高呼“万岁！” | 近义词: cheer, hooray, hurrah | 反义词: boo | 词性:感叹词, 名词

Etymology: From the exclamation "Huzza!" itself, likely influenced by similar cheers.  Its origins are uncertain, possibly related to a nautical term.
