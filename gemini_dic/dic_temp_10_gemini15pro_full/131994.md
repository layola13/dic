nonconvivially:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɪviəli/
adverb
不 convivial 地；不歡樂地；不友好地
In a manner that is not convivial; unsociably; unfriendly.
He behaved nonconvivially, refusing to join in the festivities. 他表现得不友好，拒绝参加庆祝活动。
The meeting ended nonconvivially, with both parties storming out. 会议不欢而散，双方都愤然离场。
Near Synonyms: unsociably, unfriendly, inhospitably, unpleasantly
Antonyms: convivially, sociably, amiably, pleasantly, hospitably
词性: 副词
