captionless:/ˈkæpʃənləs/
adjective
无字幕的;没有标题的
Lacking a caption or title.
The video was captionless, so I didn't know what it was about. 这段视频没有字幕，所以我不知道它是关于什么的。
captionless photographs 无标题照片
synonyms: untitled, unlabeled, undescribed
antonyms: captioned, titled, labeled
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From caption + -less.  "Caption" comes from the Latin "captio" meaning a taking or seizing, later evolving to mean a heading or title. "-less" is a suffix indicating absence or lack of something.
