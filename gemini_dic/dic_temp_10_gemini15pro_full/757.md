absolutism: /ˈæbsəlʊtɪzəm/
n.
专制主义，绝对主义
A political system in which a ruler has complete power and authority.
The king ruled by divine right, enforcing his absolutism across the land. 国王凭借神权统治，在全国实行专制主义。

Absolutism was the prevailing political theory in 17th-century France.  专制主义是17世纪法国盛行的政治理论。

近义词: autocracy, despotism, tyranny
反义词: democracy, liberalism
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *absolutisme*, from *absolu* (absolute) + *-isme* (-ism).  Ultimately from Latin *absolutus* (absolute).

USAGE->
The principle of absolutism is often discussed in political science courses.  专制主义的原则经常在政治学课程中讨论。

Historical examples of absolutism include the reign of Louis XIV of France.  专制主义的历史例子包括法国路易十四的统治。

PHRASE->
political absolutism = 政治专制主义
monarchical absolutism = 君主专制主义
religious absolutism = 宗教专制主义
the age of absolutism = 专制主义时代
the rise of absolutism = 专制主义的兴起
