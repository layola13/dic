carcinogens:/kɑːrˈsɪnədʒənz/
|n.|致癌物|A substance capable of causing cancer in living tissue.|Exposure to certain carcinogens, such as asbestos and tobacco smoke, increases the risk of cancer. 接触某些致癌物，如石棉和烟草烟雾，会增加患癌症的风险。|Synonyms: cancer-causing agents, cancerogens|Antonyms: anti-carcinogens, cancer inhibitors|noun
