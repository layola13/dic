dickensian:/dɪˈkɛnziən/
adjective
狄更斯式的；狄更斯作品风格的
Characteristic of or reminiscent of the novels of Charles Dickens, especially in suggesting the poor social conditions or comically repulsive characters that they portray.
The Dickensian scene of poverty and hardship unfolded before their eyes. 他们眼前展现出一幅狄更斯式贫困和苦难的景象。
Synonyms: impoverished, squalid, grim, bleak, depressing, poverty-stricken
Antonyms: prosperous, affluent, comfortable, cheerful, optimistic

dickensians:/dɪˈkɛnziənz/
noun
狄更斯迷；狄更斯作品研究者
People who are admirers or scholars of the works of Charles Dickens.
The Dickensians held their annual conference in London. 狄更斯迷们在伦敦举行了他们的年度会议。
Synonyms: admirers, enthusiasts, scholars, devotees, fans
Antonyms: critics, detractors
