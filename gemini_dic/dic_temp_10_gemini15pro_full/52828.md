dilutors:/daɪˈluːtərz/
|n.|稀释剂，稀释器|A substance used to dilute something, typically a solvent added to reduce the concentration of a solution.|The lab technician used several dilutors to prepare the samples for analysis. 实验室技术员使用了几种稀释剂来制备待分析的样品。|synonyms: thinners, diluents|antonyms: concentrators, thickeners|noun

|n.|稀释剂，稀释器|A substance used to dilute something, typically a solvent added to reduce the concentration of a solution.|The chemist carefully added the dilutor to the concentrated solution. 化学家小心地将稀释剂添加到浓缩溶液中。|synonyms: thinners, diluents|antonyms: concentrators, thickeners|noun
