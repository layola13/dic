harpoonlike:/ˈhɑːrˌpuːnˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像鱼叉的；似鱼叉的 | Resembling a harpoon in shape or function; barbed and pointed. |  The creature's teeth were harpoonlike, perfect for catching fish.  这个生物的牙齿像鱼叉一样，非常适合捕鱼。 | similar to a harpoon, barbed, pointed | blunt, rounded | adjective

词源:
harpoon (n.) + -like (suffix)

harpoon (n.):  from Middle French *harpon, from Old French harpon "grappling iron, harpoon," probably of Germanic origin.

-like (suffix): from Old English -lic, related to German -lich.  Forms adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
