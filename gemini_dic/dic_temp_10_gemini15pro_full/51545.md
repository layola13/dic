例句：The kid yelled, "I gotta go deucer!"  那孩子喊道：“我要去拉臭臭！”|近义词：number two, runner-up, second-in-command (for person); two bucks (for money); two points (for score) |反义词: winner, champion, leader (for person)|词性:名词
