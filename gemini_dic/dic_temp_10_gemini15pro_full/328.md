abeyant: /əˈbeɪənt/
adjective
中止的，暂停的，暂搁的
Temporarily inactive or suspended, but capable of being revived.
The project is currently abeyant, but we hope to restart it next year.  这个项目目前处于暂停状态，但我们希望明年重新启动它。

The volcano had been abeyant for centuries before its sudden eruption. 这座火山在突然爆发之前已经休眠了几个世纪。

synonym: dormant, inactive, latent, quiescent
antonym: active, ongoing, in progress
词性: 形容词
