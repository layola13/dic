insurrectional:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənəl/
adjective
暴动的；起义的；叛乱的
Of, relating to, or tending toward insurrection.
例句：The insurrectional movement was quickly suppressed by the government. 政府迅速镇压了叛乱运动。

例句：Insurrectional activities are a threat to national security. 叛乱活动对国家安全构成威胁。

近义词：rebellious, revolutionary, mutinous, seditious
反义词：loyal, obedient, submissive
词性：adjective
