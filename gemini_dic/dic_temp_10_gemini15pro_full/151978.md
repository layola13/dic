pasturage:/ˈpɑːstʃərɪdʒ/ /ˈpæstʃərɪdʒ/| n.|牧场；牧草；放牧|Land covered with grass and other low plants suitable for grazing animals; the action or practice of grazing livestock.|The sheep were put out to pasturage in the spring. 羊群在春天被放出去吃草。|近义词：grazing, pasture, herbage|反义词：barrenness, desert|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Old French *pasturage, from pasturer (“to pasture”), from pasture (“pasture”), from Latin pastūra (“pasture”), from pascere (“to feed, graze”), from pāscō (“to feed”).
