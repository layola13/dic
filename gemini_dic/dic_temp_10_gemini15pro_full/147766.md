overprolixness:/ˌoʊvərproʊˈlɪksnəs/| n. | 过于冗长，啰嗦 | The overprolixness of his writing made it difficult to follow his argument.  他写的东西过于冗长，很难理解他的论点。 | Example: The overprolixness of the legal document made it almost incomprehensible to the average person.  这份法律文件过于冗长，让普通人几乎无法理解。|Synonyms: verbosity, wordiness, prolixity | Antonyms: conciseness, brevity, succinctness | noun

Etymology: From "over" + "prolix" + "-ness". "Prolix" comes from Latin "prolixus," meaning "extended," from "pro-" (forth) + "liquere" (to flow).
