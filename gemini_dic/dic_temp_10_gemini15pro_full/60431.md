eigen:/ˈaɪɡən/| adjective| 特征的，本征的| Relating to or denoting a quantity (such as an eigenvector or eigenvalue) that remains unchanged when multiplied by a given matrix or operator. | The eigenvector of a matrix is a non-zero vector that changes by only a scalar factor when that linear transformation is applied to it. |一个矩阵的特征向量是一个非零向量，当该线性变换应用于它时，它只改变一个标量因子。|Synonyms: characteristic, inherent, intrinsic| Antonyms: extrinsic, external| adjective
