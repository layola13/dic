miskicked: /ˌmɪsˈkɪkt/
verb
踢偏，踢失误
kicked badly or inaccurately
He miskicked the penalty and the ball went wide. 他罚点球踢偏了，球偏出了球门。
近义词: mishit, flub
反义词: score, succeed
词性: verb

词源:
From mis- + kicked, past participle of kick.  The prefix "mis-" indicates something done wrongly or badly.
