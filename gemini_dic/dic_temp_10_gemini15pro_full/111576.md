lobosa:/loʊˈboʊsə/ | n. | 叶足纲，变形虫的一纲 |A class of amoebae characterized by lobose pseudopods, which are blunt, finger-like projections of cytoplasm used for movement and capturing prey.  Lobosa are found in a variety of habitats, including freshwater, marine, and soil environments.| Amoebae of the Lobosa group were observed in the sample. 在样本中观察到了叶足纲的变形虫。| synonyms: Amoebida (sometimes used interchangeably, but can also refer to a specific order within Lobosa), Tubulinea | antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification) | noun
