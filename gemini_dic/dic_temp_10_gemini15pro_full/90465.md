hubbies:/ˈhʌbiz/| n.|丈夫们（复数，非正式）|Informal plural of "hubby," which itself is an informal term for "husband."  It refers to more than one husband, and is generally used in a lighthearted or affectionate way.|例句：My hubby is taking me out to dinner. 我的老公要带我出去吃饭。

The hubbies are getting together for a poker night.  老公们要聚在一起打扑克。|近义词：husbands, spouses (more formal)|反义词:wives (opposite gender)|词性:noun
