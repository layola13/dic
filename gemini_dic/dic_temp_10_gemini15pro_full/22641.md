borage:/ˈbʌrɪdʒ/ /ˈbɔːrɪdʒ/| n.|琉璃苣|A bristly European annual herb (Borago officinalis) of the borage family, cultivated for its blue star-shaped flowers and edible leaves.  It is also used medicinally.|例句：Borage flowers are often used as a garnish in salads and cocktails. 琉璃苣花常被用作沙拉和鸡尾酒的装饰。

例句：Borage oil is believed to have anti-inflammatory properties. 琉璃苣油被认为具有抗炎特性。|近义词：starflower|反义词:|noun
