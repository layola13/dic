antiauthoritarianism:/ˌæntiɔːˌθɒrɪˈtɛəriənɪzəm/
n.|反专制主义|The belief or practice of opposing authoritarianism.  反对专制主义的信念或实践。|The rise of antiauthoritarianism in the region is a positive sign. 该地区反专制主义的兴起是一个积极的信号。|近义词：anti-despotism, libertarianism|反义词：authoritarianism, totalitarianism|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) + authoritarianism
  anti-: from Greek anti- "against, opposite of"
  authoritarian: from authority + -arian
    authority: from Old French auctorité, from Latin auctoritas "official power, influence, dignity,"  from auctor "author, originator, founder"
    -arian: suffix forming nouns and adjectives meaning "pertaining to, of the nature of," from Latin -arius
