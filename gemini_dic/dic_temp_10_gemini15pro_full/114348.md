majolica:/məˈdʒɒlɪkə/ | n. | 彩色陶器 | A type of Italian pottery glazed with an opaque white tin glaze and decorated with bright colors. | The museum has a stunning collection of majolica.  博物馆收藏了令人惊叹的彩色陶器。 |  The artist specialized in creating intricate designs on majolica. 这位艺术家擅长在彩色陶器上创作复杂的图案。| synonyms: faience, delftware | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps plain pottery) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *maiolica*, from Majorca, the largest of the Balearic Islands, from which the pottery was believed to have been originally imported into Italy.
