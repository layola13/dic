legitimatenesses:/ləˌdʒɪtɪmɪtnəsɪz/
|n.|合法性，正统性|The state of being legitimate; lawfulness.  The quality of being genuine and conforming to recognized standards or principles.|The president's legitimatenesses was questioned after the election. 总统的合法性在选举后受到质疑。|Synonyms: validity, lawfulness, legality, authenticity, genuineness|Antonyms: illegitimacy, unlawfulness, invalidity, spuriousness|noun
