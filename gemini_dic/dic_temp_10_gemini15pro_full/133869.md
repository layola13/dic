nongravitation:/ˌnɒnˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃən/
adjective
非引力的；非重力的
Relating to or involving forces other than gravity.
The nongravitational forces acting on the asteroid were significant.  非引力对小行星的作用是显著的。
The spacecraft's trajectory was affected by nongravitational perturbations. 航天器的轨道受到了非引力扰动的影响。
近义词: agravitational
反义词: gravitational
词性: adjective
