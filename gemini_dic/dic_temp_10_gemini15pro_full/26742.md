ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cadmia, from Greek *kadmeia, meaning "calamine," a zinc ore that often contains cadmium.  Ultimately related to the name of the legendary Cadmus, founder of Thebes, as calamine was supposedly mined near Thebes.
