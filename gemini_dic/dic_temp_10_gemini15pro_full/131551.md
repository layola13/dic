noncommittal:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪt(ə)l/ /ˌnɑːnkəˈmɪtl/ | adj. | 不置可否的，不表态的 | Not expressing or revealing commitment to a definite opinion or course of action. |  The politician gave a noncommittal answer to the reporter's question. 这位政治家对记者的提问给出了一个不置可否的回答。

Her response to my invitation was noncommittal. 她对我的邀请没有明确表态。

近义词: evasive, vague, ambiguous, indifferent, neutral

反义词: definite, committed, decisive, clear, forthright

词性: adjective

词源: non- (not) + committal (expressing commitment)
