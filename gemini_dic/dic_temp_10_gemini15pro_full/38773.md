conducibility:/kənˌduːsɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
传导性，传导率
The quality or state of being conducible.  The capacity to transmit something, such as electricity or heat.
The conducibility of copper makes it ideal for wiring. 铜的导电性使其成为理想的电线材料。
近义词：conductivity, conductance
反义词：resistance, nonconductivity
词性：名词

conducible:/kənˈdjuːsɪbl/
adj.
有助于…的；导致…的；有益于
Tending to promote or assist; contributive; helpful.
Regular exercise is conducible to good health. 规律的运动有助于身体健康。
近义词：beneficial, advantageous, helpful, conducive
反义词：detrimental, harmful, unhelpful
词性：形容词
