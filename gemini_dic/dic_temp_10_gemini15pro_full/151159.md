paraxial:/ˌpærˈæksiəl/| adj.|近轴的|Close to the axis, especially of a lens or mirror system; relating to rays of light that are nearly parallel to the optical axis.|The paraxial approximation is used in Gaussian optics to simplify calculations.|Near-axis, close to the axis|Off-axis, peripheral|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From para- (beside, near) + axial (relating to an axis).
