aquiparous: /əˈkwɪpərəs/
adjective
胎生的；卵胎生的
Giving birth to live young that have developed inside eggs within the mother's body, as in certain reptiles and fishes.
The aquiparous lizard gave birth to three live young. 这只卵胎生的蜥蜴产下了三只幼崽。
近义词: ovoviviparous
反义词: oviparous, viviparous
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *aquiparus, from aqua (“water”) + parere (“to give birth to”).  The term reflects an older misunderstanding of the reproductive process, assuming a connection with water.  Modern scientific terms like ovoviviparous are more precise.
