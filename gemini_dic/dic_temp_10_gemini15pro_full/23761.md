bravingly:/ˈbreɪvli/| adv.|勇敢地，无畏地|In a brave manner; courageously.|She bravingly faced the angry mob. 她勇敢地面对愤怒的暴民。|courageously, valiantly, boldly, fearlessly|cowardly, timidly|adverb
词源: From Middle English *bravely, from Old French *bravement, from brave. Ultimately from Latin barbarus.
