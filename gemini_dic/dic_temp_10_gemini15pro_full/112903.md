ludificatory:/ˌluːdɪˈfɪkətɔːri/
adjective
戏弄的，嘲弄的
Designed to mock or ridicule; making a mockery of something.
His ludificatory remarks about her singing made her feel embarrassed. 他对她歌声的嘲弄性言辞让她感到尴尬。

Her ludificatory tone made it clear she wasn't taking the situation seriously. 她嘲弄的语气表明她没有认真对待这种情况。


近义词:mocking, derisive, scornful, satirical, sardonic
反义词:respectful, serious, sincere, complimentary
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lūdificātōrius*, from *lūdificārī* "to mock, make sport of," from *lūdus* "game, play, sport" + *-ficārī* (from *facere* "to make, do").
