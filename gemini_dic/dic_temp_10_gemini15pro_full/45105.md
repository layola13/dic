cuckoldy: /ˈkʌkəldi/ | adj. |  cuckolded的形容词形式，戴绿帽的，妻子有外遇的 | Relating to or characteristic of a cuckold; having a wife who commits adultery. |  He felt cuckoldy and humiliated after discovering his wife's affair. 他发现妻子有外遇后感到自己被戴了绿帽子，倍感羞辱。|  The play explored the cuckoldy husband's themes of jealousy and revenge.  这部戏剧探讨了被戴绿帽子的丈夫的嫉妒和复仇主题。| Synonyms: cheated on, betrayed, dishonored | Antonyms: faithful, loyal | adjective

Etymology: From cuckold + -y.  Cuckold comes from Old French cucuault, from cuculus "cuckoo," referring to the cuckoo's habit of laying its eggs in other birds' nests.
