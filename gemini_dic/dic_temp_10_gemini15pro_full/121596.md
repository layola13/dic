misology:/mɪˈsɒlədʒi/ /mɪˈsɑːlədʒi/
n.
厌恶推理；憎恨理性
Hatred of reasoning; hatred of argument or discussion.
Misology can lead to the rejection of scientific evidence. 厌恶推理会导致对科学证据的排斥。
The politician's misology prevented any meaningful debate from taking place.  这位政客对理性的厌恶阻碍了任何有意义的辩论的进行。
synonyms: anti-intellectualism, irrationalism
antonyms:  rationalism, logic
noun
