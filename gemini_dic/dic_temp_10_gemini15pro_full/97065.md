inferiority:/ɪnˌfɪəriˈɔːrəti/ | n. | 自卑，劣等 | a feeling that you are not as good, intelligent, etc. as other people |  He had a deep sense of inferiority because of his stutter. 他因为口吃而有很深的自卑感。 | synonyms: inadequacy, inferiority complex | antonyms: superiority, confidence | noun

inferior:/ɪnˈfɪəriər/ | adj. |  次等的，较差的，下级的 | lower in rank, status, or quality |  These products are inferior to those we bought last year. 这些产品比我们去年买的要差。| synonyms: lesser, subordinate, second-rate | antonyms: superior, excellent | adjective
