nonethnic:/ˌnɒnˈɛθnɪk/ | adj. | 非种族的，不限种族的 |  Not relating to or characteristic of a particular ethnic group. |  The organization promotes nonethnic policies to ensure equal opportunities for everyone.  这个组织推行非种族政策，以确保人人享有平等的机会。|  Synonyms: nonracial, multiethnic, interracial | Antonyms: ethnic, racial | adjective

nonethnic:/ˌnɒnˈɛθnɪk/ | n. | 非特定种族的人 | A person not belonging to a particular ethnic group (often used in a statistical context). | The survey included both ethnic and nonethnic individuals.  这项调查包括了特定种族和非特定种族的人。 | Synonyms: non-racial individual | Antonyms: ethnic individual | noun
