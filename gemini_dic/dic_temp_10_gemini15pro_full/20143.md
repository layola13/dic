bipennate:/baɪˈpɛneɪt/ | adj. | 双羽状的 | Having a feather-like arrangement with parts arising from both sides of a central axis, like a feather.  Specifically referring to muscles where fibers attach obliquely to both sides of a central tendon. | The rectus femoris muscle is an example of a bipennate muscle.  股直肌是双羽肌的一个例子。|  bipinnate | unipennate, multipennate | adjective
Etymology: From Latin *bi- (two) + pennatus (feathered), from penna (feather).
