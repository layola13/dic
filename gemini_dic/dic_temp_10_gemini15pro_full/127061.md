natalities: /nəˈtælɪtiz/
noun
出生人数
The number of births; birth rate.
The natalities in the country have been declining steadily over the past decade.  这个国家过去十年出生人数一直在稳步下降。
Synonyms: births, birth rate
Antonyms: mortalities, death rate
