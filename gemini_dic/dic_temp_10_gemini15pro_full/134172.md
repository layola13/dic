nonincentive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsɛntɪv/ | adj. | 非激励性的，没有激励作用的 | Not providing any incentive; discouraging. |  The company's nonincentive pay structure led to low morale. 该公司没有激励作用的薪酬结构导致士气低落。 | The nonincentive nature of the task made it difficult to find volunteers. 这项任务缺乏激励性，很难找到志愿者。 | 近义词: discouraging, demotivating, counterproductive | 反义词: incentivizing, motivating, encouraging | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "incentive" (from Latin "incentivus" meaning "inciting, setting the tune").
