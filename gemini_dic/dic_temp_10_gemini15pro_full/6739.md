analyst: /ˈænəlɪst/ (US) /ˈænəlɪst/ (UK)| n.|分析师，分析员|A person who examines something in detail in order to understand it or explain it.|The financial analyst predicted a rise in stock prices.  金融分析师预测股价会上涨。|synonyms: examiner, investigator, interpreter, evaluator|antonyms: synthesizer, compiler|noun
