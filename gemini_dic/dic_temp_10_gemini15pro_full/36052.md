codrives:/ˈkoʊˌdraɪvz/
verb
共同驾驶，共同开车
Shares the driving with someone on a long journey or trip.
例句：We codrove to Los Angeles. 我们共同开车去了洛杉矶。

例句：They codrove across the country. 他们共同开车穿越了全国。

近义词：share the driving, alternate driving
反义词：(none specific - perhaps "drives alone")
词性：verb
