arcaded:/ɑːrˈkeɪdɪd/
adj.
拱形的，有拱廊的
having arches or arcades
The street was arcaded, providing shade from the sun.  这条街有拱廊，遮挡了阳光。
The courtyard was arcaded on all four sides.  庭院四面都有拱廊。
近义词: arched, vaulted
反义词: open, flat
词性: adjective

arcaded:/ɑːrˈkeɪdɪd/
n.
拱廊，拱廊街
a covered passageway with arches and shops
We strolled through the arcaded shopping street. 我们漫步在拱廊购物街上。
The city is known for its elegant arcaded squares. 这座城市以其优雅的拱廊广场而闻名。
近义词: arcade, colonnade, gallery
反义词: open street, plaza
词性: noun


词源：From French *arcadé*, past participle of *arcader* (“to arch, vault”), from Italian *arcare* (“to arch”), from Latin *arcus* (“arch, bow”).
