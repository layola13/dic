basaltic:/bəˈsɔːltɪk/ /bəˈsɑːltɪk/
adjective
玄武岩的；由玄武岩构成的
Relating to, formed from, or containing basalt.
Basaltic lava flows are common in volcanic regions. 玄武岩熔岩流在火山地区很常见。
The island is composed of basaltic rock.  这个岛屿由玄武岩构成。
synonyms: igneous, volcanic
antonyms: sedimentary, metamorphic
词性: adjective
