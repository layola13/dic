enragedness:/ɪnˈreɪdʒdnəs/| n. |愤怒，暴怒|The state of being enraged; extreme anger.|His enragedness at the injustice was palpable. 他对不公正的愤怒显而易见。|近义词：fury, rage, wrath, ire|反义词：calmness, tranquility|词性:名词
Etymology: From enrage + -ed + -ness. Enrage comes from the Old French enrager, from en- "in" + rager "to rage," from Latin rabies "rage, madness."
