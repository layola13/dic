bear-off:/ˈbeər ɒf/ (US) /ˈbeər ɒf/ (UK)| v.|拿掉棋子; 驶离; 偏离航线|To remove one's checkers from the board in backgammon after all have reached the home board. Also, to steer away. |Example 1: He bore off all his checkers and won the game. 他拿掉了所有棋子，赢得了比赛。

Example 2: The ship bore off to avoid the rocks. 船偏离航线以避开岩石。|近义词：remove, steer away, depart|反义词: approach, add|词性: 动词
