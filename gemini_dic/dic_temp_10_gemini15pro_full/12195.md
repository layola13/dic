as-of:/ˌæz ˈʌv/ | prep. |截至；到...为止 |Indicates a point in time up to which something is true or valid. |The price list is valid as of June 1st.  价目表自6月1日起生效。

The agreement is terminated as of today.  协议从今天起终止。|Synonyms: from, effective, starting, beginning | Antonyms: after, beyond, since | preposition
