anticapitalists:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪsts/
n.|反资本主义者|People who oppose capitalism.  Those who believe that capitalism is inherently exploitative and advocate for alternative economic systems.|Many anticapitalists believe that the pursuit of profit leads to social inequality.|近义词：socialists, communists, radicals, leftists|反义词: capitalists, conservatives|词性:noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek _anti_)
capitalist (noun): a person who uses or invests capital in business (from _capital_ + _-ist_)
