emetic:/ɪˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. n. | 催吐的；催吐剂 | Causing vomiting; a medicine or other substance which causes vomiting. | The doctor prescribed an emetic to help the patient expel the poison. 医生给病人开了催吐剂以帮助排出毒物。|  She accidentally swallowed some cleaning fluid and was given an emetic. 她不小心吞下了一些清洁液，被给予了催吐剂。 | 近义词: vomitive, nauseant | 反义词: antiemetic | 词性: adjective, noun

emetics:/ɪˈmɛtɪks/ | n. | 催吐剂 |  Substances that induce vomiting. | The nurse administered emetics to the child who had swallowed a coin. 护士给吞了硬币的孩子服用了催吐剂。 |  Certain plants contain natural emetics. 某些植物含有天然的催吐剂。 | 近义词: vomitives, nauseants | 反义词: antiemetics | 词性: noun
