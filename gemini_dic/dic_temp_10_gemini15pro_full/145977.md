overdoctrinaire: /ˌoʊvərˌdɒktrɪˈnɛər/ | adj. | 过于教条的，过于理论化的 | Excessively attached to a doctrine or theory, especially in a rigid or inflexible way.  Applying doctrine or theory without sufficient regard for practical considerations or individual circumstances. |  His overdoctrinaire approach to economics alienated many of his colleagues.  他过于教条的经济学方法疏远了他的许多同事。 | The party's overdoctrinaire stance on immigration lost them many voters.  该党在移民问题上过于教条的立场使他们失去了许多选民。 | 近义词: dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, doctrinaire, authoritarian | 反义词: pragmatic, flexible, adaptable, open-minded, practical | 词性: adjective | 词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + doctrinaire (from French, meaning "doctrinal")
