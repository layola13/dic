disrate:/dɪsˈreɪt/
verb
降低…的等级; 剥夺…的等级(或级别、军衔)
To reduce in rank or rating.
The soldier was disrated for insubordination.  这个士兵因不服从命令而被降级。
He was disrated from sergeant to private. 他从军士降级为列兵。
synonyms: degrade, demote, downgrade, reduce in rank
antonyms: promote, upgrade, elevate
词性: 动词
