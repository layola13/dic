nonresilient:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈzɪ.li.ənt/ | adj. | 非弹性的，缺乏韧性的，脆弱的 | Not resilient; unable to recover quickly from difficulties, shock, or change. | The economy proved nonresilient in the face of the global crisis.  这个经济体在面对全球危机时显得脆弱。| The nonresilient bridge collapsed under the weight of the snow.  这座缺乏韧性的桥在大雪的重压下坍塌了。| 近义词：fragile, brittle, vulnerable, weak | 反义词：resilient, strong, robust, tough | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + resilient.  "Resilient" comes from the Latin "resilire," meaning "to leap back" or "rebound." The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning of "resilient."
