eugenic: /juːˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 優生的，優生學的 | Relating to or promoting the production of fine offspring, especially in humans, by controlled breeding. |  Eugenics was a popular idea in the early 20th century. 優生學在20世紀初是一個流行的觀念。| Synonyms:  none | Antonyms: dysgenic | adjective

eugenics: /juːˈdʒɛnɪks/ | n. | 優生學 | The study of or belief in the possibility of improving the qualities of the human species or a human population, especially by such means as discouraging reproduction by persons having genetic defects or presumed to have inheritable undesirable traits (negative eugenics) or encouraging reproduction by persons presumed to have inheritable desirable traits (positive eugenics).  | The history of eugenics is a dark chapter in the 20th century. 優生學的歷史是20世紀的黑暗篇章。 | Synonyms:  none | Antonyms:  none | noun
