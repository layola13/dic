glareless:/ˈɡlɛərlɪs/| adj.|无眩光的，不刺眼的|Free from glare; not causing glare.  It describes something that doesn't produce or reflect a harsh, bright light that can be uncomfortable or blinding.|The glareless headlights made driving at night much safer. 无眩光的前灯使夜间驾驶更加安全。|Synonyms: nonglaring, soft, diffused|Antonyms: glaring, dazzling, bright|adjective
