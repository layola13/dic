demurrer:/dɪˈmɜːrər/| n.|异议，反对，抗辩|An objection that an opponent's point is irrelevant or invalid, especially in law.|The lawyer filed a demurrer, arguing the case should be dismissed.|Synonyms: objection, protest, challenge, exception|Antonyms: acceptance, agreement, approval|noun

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/| v.|提出异议，反对，犹豫|To raise doubts or objections or show reluctance.|She demurred at his suggestion to travel by train.|Synonyms: object, protest, hesitate, waver|Antonyms: agree, accept, assent|verb
