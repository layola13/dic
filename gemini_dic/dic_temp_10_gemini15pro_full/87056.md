heptagonal:/hɛpˈtæɡənəl/
adjective
七边形的；七角形的
Having seven sides and seven angles.
A heptagonal prism has two heptagonal bases and seven rectangular sides. 一个七角棱镜有两个七角形的底面和七个长方形的侧面。
The fortress was built on a heptagonal plan.  这座堡垒是按照七边形的平面图建造的。
近义词:septagonal
反义词:(No direct antonym, but consider terms like circular, triangular, quadrilateral for contrasting shapes)
词性:adjective
