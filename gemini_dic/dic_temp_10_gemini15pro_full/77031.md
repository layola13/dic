garish:/ˈɡɛərɪʃ/| adj.|炫耀的，俗丽的，过于艳丽的|Excessively bright and showy, typically involving a clash of colours.|The interior of the house was garish, with bright pink walls and a lime green sofa. 这房子的内部装修很俗气，亮粉色的墙壁配上黄绿色的沙发。|近义词：gaudy, flashy, lurid, showy, loud, ostentatious|反义词：subtle, understated, elegant, refined, subdued|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier *garnish, from Middle English garnysshen, from Old French garniss-, stem of garnir “to equip, furnish, adorn,” from Frankish *warnjan, of Germanic origin; related to Old High German warnōn “to provide, warn.”  The sense shifted from "furnished" to "showily dressed" to "overly bright" in color.
