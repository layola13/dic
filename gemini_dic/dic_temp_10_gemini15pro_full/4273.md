airtightly:/ˈɛərtʌɪtli/
adverb
密封地，不透气地
In such a manner as to be airtight; so as not to permit the passage of air.
The jar was sealed airtightly to preserve the contents.  这个罐子被密封地盖好以保存里面的东西。
Food was stored airtightly to prevent spoilage.  食物被密封保存以防止腐败变质。
近义词: hermetically, securely, tightly
反义词: loosely, openly
