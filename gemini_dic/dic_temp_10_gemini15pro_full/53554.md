disbark:/dɪsˈbɑːrk/
verb
卸下（尤指从船上）；使登陆
To unload from a ship, especially troops or cargo.  To go ashore from a ship.
The troops were disbarked at dawn.  黎明时分，部队登陆了。
The passengers were disbarked at the port.  乘客们在港口登陆了。
近义词: disembark, land, unload
反义词: embark, load
词性: verb
