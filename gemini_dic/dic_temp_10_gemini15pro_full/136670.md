nonsecludedness:/ˌnɒnsɪˈkluːdɪdnəs/ |n.| 非隐蔽性；开放性 |The nonsecludedness of the park made it a popular spot for gatherings.  公园的开放性使它成为聚会的热门地点。|English Explanation: The state or quality of not being secluded or hidden; openness or accessibility.  |Example 1:  The nonsecludedness of the beach made it less appealing to those seeking privacy.  海滩的开放性使得它对那些寻求隐私的人来说不那么有吸引力了。

Example 2: They enjoyed the nonsecludedness of the cafe, where they could people-watch. 他们喜欢咖啡馆的开放性，在那里他们可以观察人群。 |Synonym: openness, accessibility, publicness |Antonym: seclusion, privacy, isolation |noun
