infusive:/ɪnˈfjuːsɪv/| adj.|注入的；灌输的；充满的|Having the quality of infusing or being infused; tending to infuse.  Characterized by or resulting from infusion.|例句：The tea had an infusive quality, spreading its warmth throughout my body.  这茶有一种注入般的力量，将它的温暖散布到我的全身。

例句：Her teaching style was infusive, inspiring her students with a love of learning.  她的教学风格充满激情，激发了学生对学习的热爱。|近义词：inspiring, stimulating, permeating, pervasive|反义词：vapid, dull, uninspiring|词性: adjective
