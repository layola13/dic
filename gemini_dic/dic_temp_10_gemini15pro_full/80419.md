gomphosis:/ɡɒmˈfəʊsɪs/ /ɡɑːmˈfoʊsɪs/
noun
嵌合；钉状缝
A type of fibrous joint in which a conical process is inserted into a socket-like portion.  This joint is characterized by immobility.
The gomphosis joint, exemplified by the attachment of a tooth to its socket, demonstrates a remarkable stability. 牙齿与其牙槽的连接，即嵌合关节，展现了非凡的稳定性。
synonym: articulation, joint, synarthrosis
antonym: diarthrosis (freely movable joint)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek γόμφος (gómphos, “peg, bolt”) +‎ -osis.
