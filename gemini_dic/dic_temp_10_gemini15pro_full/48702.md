defibrillation: /diːˌfɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/ /diːˌfaɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/
noun
除颤；电击复律
The process of restoring the heart's normal rhythm by applying a controlled electric shock.
The patient received defibrillation and regained consciousness. 病人接受了电击除颤后恢复了意识。
synonyms: cardioversion, countershock, electric shock therapy
antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a medical procedure)
n.
