hit-the-roof:/hɪt ðə ruːf/| idiom (v.)|勃然大怒，暴跳如雷|To become extremely angry.  It implies a sudden and intense outburst of anger.|例句：When I told my dad I crashed his car, he hit the roof. 当我告诉我爸爸我撞了他的车时，他勃然大怒。

例句：She hit the roof when she saw the phone bill. 当她看到电话账单时，她暴跳如雷。|近义词：go ballistic, blow one's top, fly off the handle, lose one's temper|反义词：stay calm, remain composed|词性 idiom
