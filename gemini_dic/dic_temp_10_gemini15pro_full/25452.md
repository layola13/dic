bulldoggers:/ˈbʊlˌdɔːɡərz/
n.
牛仔竞技者，牧牛者，摔牛者
People who compete in bulldogging, a rodeo event where a rider on horseback chases a steer, grabs it by the horns, and wrestles it to the ground.
The bulldoggers were getting ready for their event at the rodeo. 牛仔竞技者们正准备参加牛仔竞技表演中的摔牛比赛。
The bulldoggers displayed incredible skill and strength. 摔牛者展现了惊人的技巧和力量。
synonyms: steer wrestlers, rodeo cowboys
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym)
