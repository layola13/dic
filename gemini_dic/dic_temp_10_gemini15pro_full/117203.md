measurer:/ˈmɛʒərər/| n. |测量者；测量器|A person or device that measures something. |例句：The measurer carefully calculated the dimensions of the room. 测量员仔细地计算了房间的尺寸。

例句：A rain gauge is a type of measurer used to determine how much rain has fallen. 雨量计是一种用来测量降雨量的测量器。|近义词：gauger, calibrator, surveyor, meter|反义词: None|词性:noun
