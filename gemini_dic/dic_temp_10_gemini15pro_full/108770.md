leasee:/ˌliːˈsiː/ /ˌleɪˈsiː/
n.
承租人，租赁人
A person or company that holds a lease of something, especially real estate.
The lessee is responsible for maintaining the property during the lease term. 承租人有责任在租赁期内维护房产。
The lessee agreed to pay a monthly rent of $2,000. 承租人同意每月支付2000美元的租金。
tenant, renter
lessor, landlord
noun
