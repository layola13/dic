furthered:/ˈfɜːrðərd/| v.|促进，推进，增进|To help the development or progress of something; to promote or advance.|His education was furthered by his travel abroad. 他的旅行开阔了他的眼界，增进了他的学识。|近义词：advanced, promoted, fostered, facilitated|反义词:hindered, impeded, obstructed|词性:动词

词源:  From the verb "further," which comes from Old English "furthor," meaning "more forward, farther." The "-ed" ending indicates the past tense or past participle form.
