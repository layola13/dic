overdedications:/ˌoʊvərˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
过度奉献；过分投入
Excessive dedication or commitment, often to the detriment of other aspects of life.
She suffered from overdedications to her work, neglecting her family and health. 她因过度投入工作而身心俱疲，忽略了家人和健康。
近义词:overcommitment, overzealousness
反义词:negligence, apathy
词性:名词
