orthoclastic:/ˌɔːrθəˈklæstɪk/ | adj. | 直节理的，正交解理的 |  Relating to or exhibiting orthoclasis, a type of rock fracture where the breaks are at right angles to the pre-existing structure. |  The orthoclastic texture of the rock indicated it had undergone significant stress.  岩石的直节理结构表明它经历了巨大的压力。 |  rectangular, perpendicular | oblique, inclined | adjective
