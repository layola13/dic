compeer:/kəmˈpɪr/ | n. | 同事，同伴，同辈 | A person of equal standing; a peer. | He discussed the issue with his compeers at the university. 他和大学里的同事讨论了这个问题。 |  She valued the opinions of her compeers in the legal profession. 她重视她在法律界同行的意见。 | 近义词: peer, colleague, associate, equal | 反义词: inferior, subordinate | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *comper, from Late Latin *compar, from Latin compar "equal, like," from com- "with, together" + par "equal."
