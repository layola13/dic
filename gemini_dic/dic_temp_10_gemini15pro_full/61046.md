elizabethanism:/ɪˌlɪzəˈbiːθənɪzəm/
n.
伊丽莎白时代风格；伊丽莎白时代精神
The characteristics of the culture of England during the reign of Queen Elizabeth I (1558-1603), including its literature, music, and architecture, marked by a flourishing of the arts.
The Globe Theatre is a perfect example of Elizabethanism in architecture.  地球剧院是伊丽莎白时代建筑的完美典范。
近义词:Renaissance, Elizabethan era
反义词:Modernism, Victorianism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Elizabeth + -an + -ism.  Elizabeth 指的是英国女王伊丽莎白一世， -an 表示“属于...的”， -ism 表示“...主义”或“...风格”。
