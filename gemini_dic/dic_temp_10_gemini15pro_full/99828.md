interministerial: /ˌɪntərˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. | 部际的；跨部委的 | Involving or relating to more than one government ministry or department. | The issue required interministerial cooperation to resolve.  这个问题需要部际合作才能解决。 | Example: An interministerial committee was formed to address the economic crisis.  成立了一个部际委员会来应对经济危机。 | Synonyms: interdepartmental, cross-departmental, collaborative, joint | Antonyms:  intradepartemental, within-departmental | word type: adjective
