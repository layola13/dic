effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/ | adj. | 兴奋的；活跃的；冒泡的 | Vivacious and enthusiastic; giving off bubbles of gas. | The effervescent child couldn't stop giggling.  这个兴奋的孩子咯咯地笑个不停。 |  Synonyms: bubbly, lively, sparkling, animated | Antonyms: flat, dull, listless | adjective

effervescence:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsəns/ | n. | 兴奋；活跃；冒泡 | Vivacity and enthusiasm; the process of giving off bubbles of gas. | The effervescence of the crowd was contagious.  人群的兴奋情绪很有感染力。| Synonyms: bubbliness, liveliness, sparkle, animation | Antonyms: flatness, dullness, listlessness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin effervescent-, present participle stem of effervescere, from ex- + fervescere "to begin to boil," from fervere "to boil, glow."
