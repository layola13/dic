impulsivenesses:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
冲动性，一时兴起，鲁莽行为(复数)
The state of being impulsive; a tendency to act on a whim.  This word is rarely used in the plural.
His impulsivenesses often led him into trouble. 他的冲动行为经常给他带来麻烦。
impulsivity, rashness, recklessness
