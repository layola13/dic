counterweighting:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪtɪŋ/
noun
平衡，抗衡，抵消
the act of adding a counterweight to something in order to balance it.
The crane uses a system of counterweighting to lift heavy loads.  起重机使用平衡系统来提升重物。
The government introduced tax cuts as a counterweighting measure to stimulate the economy. 政府引入了减税作为一项刺激经济的平衡措施。
counterbalance, offset
increase, addition


counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/
noun
平衡重，砝码
a weight that balances another weight.
The counterweight on the crane prevents it from tipping over. 起重机上的平衡重防止它翻倒。
He acted as a counterweight to the more radical members of the group. 他扮演了平衡团队中激进成员的角色。
balance, counterpoise
imbalance
