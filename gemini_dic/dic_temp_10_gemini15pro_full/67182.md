expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/ | v. | 阐述，解释，详细说明 | to explain something in detail |  She expounded her theory on the origins of the universe. 她阐述了她关于宇宙起源的理论。 | Synonyms: explain, elucidate, interpret, clarify | Antonyms: obscure, confuse, conceal | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *expounen*, from Old French *espondre*, from Latin *exponere* ("to put out, set forth, explain"), from *ex-* ("out") + *ponere* ("to put, place").
