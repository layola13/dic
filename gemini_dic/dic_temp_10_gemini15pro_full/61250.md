emanatory:/ˈemənətɔːri/
adjective
散发出的，流露出的
Proceeding or flowing forth; issuing; emanating.  Something that issues or originates from a source.
The emanatory fragrance of the flowers filled the room. 花散发出的香味充满了整个房间。
emanating, issuing, exuding, radiating
absorbing, retaining
adjective
