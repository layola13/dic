greekless:/ˈɡriːklɪs/| adj.|不懂希腊语的，对希腊文化不了解的|Lacking knowledge of Greek language or culture.  Derived from "Greek" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).|He was completely greekless and couldn't understand the inscriptions. 他完全不懂希腊语，无法理解那些铭文。|Synonyms: unlettered in Greek, ignorant of Greek, unversed in Greek|Antonyms: knowledgeable about Greek, versed in Greek, fluent in Greek|adjective
