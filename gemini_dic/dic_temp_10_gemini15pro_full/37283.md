commandeer:/ˌkɒmənˈdɪə(r)/ | v. | 征用，强占 | To seize (something, especially a vehicle) for military or official use. | The army commandeered all the trucks to transport supplies. 军队征用了所有的卡车来运输补给。 | The government commandeered the building for use as a temporary hospital. 政府征用了这栋楼作为临时医院。 | synonyms: seize, confiscate, appropriate, expropriate | antonyms: return, release, surrender | verb
