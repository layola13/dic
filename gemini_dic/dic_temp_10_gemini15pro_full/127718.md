negligee:/ˈnɛɡlɪʒeɪ/ /ˈnɛɡləˌʒeɪ/
noun
女士睡衣；轻薄的晨衣
A woman's light, loose dressing gown or robe, typically made of sheer or delicate fabric.
She slipped into a silk negligee. 她穿上一件丝绸睡衣。

近义词:nightgown, robe, peignoir
反义词:daywear, outerwear

词性:n.
