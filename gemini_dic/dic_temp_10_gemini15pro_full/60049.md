educatory:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɔːri/ /ˈɛdʒʊkətɔːri/
adjective
教育的；有教育意义的
serving to educate; instructive
例句：The museum offers a range of educatory programs for children.  博物馆为孩子们提供一系列教育项目。

例句：The trip was both enjoyable and educatory.  这次旅行既愉快又有教育意义。

近义词：educational, instructive, informative, enlightening
反义词：unedifying, uninformative

词性: adjective
