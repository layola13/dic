amerce:/əˈmɜːrs/| vt.|处以罚金；惩罚|To punish by inflicting a fine, especially an arbitrary one.|The judge amerced the defendant $500 for contempt of court. 法官因被告藐视法庭而对其处以500美元的罚款。|Synonyms: fine, penalize, punish|Antonyms: reward, compensate|verb

amerce:/əˈmɜːrs/| vt.|剥夺；强制放弃|To deprive or take away (something, such as a right or privilege).|The king amerced the traitor of his lands and titles. 国王剥夺了叛徒的土地和爵位。|Synonyms: deprive, divest, strip|Antonyms: grant, bestow, award|verb
