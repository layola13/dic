nonunited:/ˌnɒnjuːˈnaɪtɪd/
adj.
不团结的，不统一的
Not united; disunited; lacking unity or cohesion.
The nonunited tribes were easily conquered.  不团结的部落很容易被征服。
The political party was nonunited on the issue of healthcare reform.  该政党在医疗改革问题上意见不统一。
近义词:disunited, divided, fragmented, separated
反义词:united, unified, cohesive, integrated
词性:形容词

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "united" (joined together)
