gatefold:/ˈɡeɪtfəʊld/ (UK) /ˈɡeɪtfold/ (US)| adj. n.|折叠式的；折叠页；|A gatefold is a record cover, book cover, or other folded item with two flaps that fold inwards, meeting in the middle. It's often used for larger artwork or additional information.|The vinyl record came in a beautiful gatefold sleeve.|近义词：foldout, bifold|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "single-sleeve")|词性: adjective, noun
