conciseness:/kənˈsaɪsnəs/| n. |简洁，简明|The conciseness of her writing style made it easy to understand. 她的写作风格简洁明了，易于理解。|例句：She expressed her ideas with admirable conciseness. 她以令人钦佩的简洁表达了她的想法。|近义词：brevity, terseness, succinctness|反义词：verbosity, prolixity, wordiness|词性:名词
concise:/kənˈsaɪs/| adj. |简洁的，简明的|A concise summary of the report is needed. 需要一份简明的报告摘要。|例句：His concise explanation left no room for misunderstanding. 他简洁的解释没有留下任何误解的余地。|近义词：brief, succinct, terse|反义词：verbose, prolix, wordy|词性:形容词
