overseasons:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːzənz/ | v. |过冬，过季; (尤指运动员)在一个地方待一个休赛期| spends the winter in a particular place; (especially of an athlete) spends an off-season in a particular place | The baseball player overseasons in Florida.  这位棒球运动员在佛罗里达过冬。| 近义词: winters, spends the off-season | 反义词: migrates |词性: 动词

overseason:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːzən/ | v. |过度调味| to season excessively | I think I overseasoned the soup; it's too salty. 我想我给汤加太多调料了；太咸了。 | 近义词: oversalt, overspice | 反义词: underseason |词性: 动词
