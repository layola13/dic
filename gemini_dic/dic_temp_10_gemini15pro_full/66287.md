excommunicatory:/ˌɛkskəˈmjuːnɪkətɔːri/
adjective
逐出教会的；开除教籍的
Relating to or denoting excommunication.
The priest issued an excommunicatory decree.  神父发布了开除教籍的命令。
The council published an excommunicatory bull against him.  委员会发布了逐他出教的诏书。
近义词: condemnatory, denunciatory
反义词: absolving, forgiving
词性: adjective
