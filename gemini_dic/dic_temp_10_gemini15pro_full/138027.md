noosers:/ˈnuːzərz/
noun
傻瓜，笨蛋，失败者
A person who loses, especially habitually or ignominiously.  Often used in a derogatory way.
He called the other team a bunch of noosers after they lost the game. 他在另一队输了比赛后称他们为一群失败者。
