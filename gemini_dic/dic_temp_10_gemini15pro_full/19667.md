bilateralness:/ˌbaɪˌlætərəlnəs/
n.
双边性，两侧性
The state or quality of being bilateral; having two sides or parts.
Bilateralness is essential for balanced movement in many animals.  双边性对于许多动物的平衡运动至关重要。

The treaty emphasized the bilateralness of the agreement. 该条约强调了协议的双边性。

近义词: two-sidedness, symmetry
反义词: unilaterality, asymmetry
词性: noun

bilateral:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəl/
adj.
双边的，两侧的
Involving two sides, parties, or countries.
The two countries signed a bilateral trade agreement. 这两个国家签署了一项双边贸易协定。
They have a bilateral agreement for sharing resources. 他们有一个双方资源共享协议。
近义词: mutual, reciprocal
反义词: unilateral, one-sided
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lateral (side) + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *bi- (two) and latus (side).
