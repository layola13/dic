incline-plane:/ˈɪnklaɪn pleɪn/| n. |斜面|A sloping surface, such as a ramp, that makes it easier to move objects up or down.|We used an incline-plane to load the heavy boxes onto the truck. 我们用斜面把沉重的箱子装到卡车上。|Synonym: ramp, slope, gradient|Antonym: decline, descent|noun

incline-planes:/ˈɪnklaɪn pleɪnz/| n. |斜面（复数）|Multiple sloping surfaces used for raising or lowering objects.|The ancient Egyptians used incline-planes to build the pyramids. 古埃及人用斜面建造金字塔。|Synonym: ramps, slopes, gradients|Antonym: declines, descents|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *plan incliné*, from Latin *inclīnāre* (to incline, bend) and *plānus* (flat, level).
