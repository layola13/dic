crocin: /ˈkroʊsɪn/ | n. | 番红花苷，藏红花素 | A carotenoid chemical compound that is the main chemical component giving saffron its color. It is also responsible for many of saffron's medicinal properties. | Saffron is a source of crocin, a compound believed to have antioxidant properties. 藏红花是番红花苷的来源，这种化合物被认为具有抗氧化特性。 | Crocin is extracted from saffron and used as a natural food coloring.  番红花苷从藏红花中提取出来，用作天然食用色素。 | synonyms: none (highly specific chemical compound) | antonyms: none | noun
