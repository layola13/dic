antinationalization:/ˌæntiˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 反国家化，反对国有化 | The act of opposing or reversing the process of nationalization, which is the transfer of privately owned assets or businesses to government ownership or control. |  The government faced strong antinationalization protests from business owners. 政府面临来自企业主的强烈反国家化抗议。

The party's platform included a commitment to antinationalization policies. 该党的纲领包括承诺奉行反国家化政策。 | 近义词: privatization, denationalization | 反义词: nationalization | 词性: 名词
