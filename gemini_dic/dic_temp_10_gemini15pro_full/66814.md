expansional:/ɪkˈspænʃənəl/ | adj. |扩张的，膨胀的|Relating to or characterized by expansion; tending to expand. |The country experienced an expansional period of economic growth.  这个国家经历了一段经济扩张时期。| The company adopted an expansional monetary policy. 公司采取了扩张性货币政策。 | 近义词: expanding, growth-oriented, inflationary | 反义词: contractionary, deflationary, recessive | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From expansion + -al.  Expansion comes from Late Latin expansionem,  from expandere "to spread out," from ex- "out" + pandere "to spread."
