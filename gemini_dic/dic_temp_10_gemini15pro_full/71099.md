fishability:/fɪʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
可钓鱼性；适于垂钓；可捕捞性
The fishability of a river depends on several factors, including water quality and fish population.
一条河流的可钓鱼性取决于几个因素，包括水质和鱼类种群。
The new dam has greatly reduced the fishability of the lake.
新建的大坝大大降低了湖泊的可捕捞性。
Near synonyms:  angling potential, fishing suitability
Antonyms: unfishable
