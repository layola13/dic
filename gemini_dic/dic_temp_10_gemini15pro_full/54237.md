disgarrison:/ˌdɪsˈɡærɪsən/
verb
撤离驻军，解除驻防
To remove a garrison from a fortified place.
The troops were disgarrisoned from the fort after the war ended.  战争结束后，部队从要塞撤离了。
The commander decided to disgarrison the outpost due to the impending attack.  由于即将到来的袭击，指挥官决定撤离前哨。
近义词: evacuate, withdraw, remove
反义词: garrison, fortify, occupy
词性: verb
