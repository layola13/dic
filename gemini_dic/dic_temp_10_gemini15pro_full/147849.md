overquell:/ˌoʊvərˈkwɛl/ | v. | 征服；压倒；抑制；（感情）充满；（液体）溢出 | To overcome or suppress completely; to overwhelm with emotion. |  Her joy overquelled her and she burst into tears. 她喜极而泣。| 近义词：overwhelm, subdue, conquer, overcome | 反义词：yield, succumb | 词性：动词

Etymology: From over- + quell.  "Quell" comes from Old English *cwellan "to kill, murder, execute," from Proto-Germanic *kwaljanan "to torment, kill"
