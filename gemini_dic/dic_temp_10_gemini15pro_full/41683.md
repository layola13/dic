correspondentship:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndəntʃɪp/
n.
通信；通信关系；记者职位
the state or fact of being a correspondent; the relationship between correspondents; the position of a correspondent
She maintained a correspondentship with several leading scientists. 她与几位顶尖科学家保持着通信联系。
His correspondentship with the newspaper ended abruptly. 他与那家报纸的记者职位突然终止了。
synonym: correspondence, communication, exchange,  relationship, association
antonym: silence, isolation, disconnect
noun
