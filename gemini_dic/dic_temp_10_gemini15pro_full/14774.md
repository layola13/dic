awl:/ɔːl/ | n. | 锥子；尖钻 | A small pointed tool used for piercing holes, especially in leather or wood. |  He used an awl to make holes in the leather for the stitching. 他用锥子在皮革上钻孔以便缝合。| synonym:  bradawl, piercer, gimlet | antonym: - | noun
