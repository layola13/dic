confocal:/ˈkɒnfəkəl/ (UK) /kənˈfoʊkəl/ (US)| adj.|共焦的，共焦点的|Relating to or denoting a microscope that uses a laser beam to illuminate a single point of a specimen at a time, and a pinhole aperture to reject out-of-focus light, producing a high-resolution three-dimensional image.|Confocal microscopy allows for detailed visualization of cellular structures. 共焦显微镜可以对细胞结构进行详细的可视化。|Near Synonyms:  high-resolution, 3D imaging|Antonyms: wide-field|adjective


confocal:/ˈkɒnfəkəl/ (UK) /kənˈfoʊkəl/ (US)| n.|共焦显微镜|A confocal microscope.|The confocal revealed intricate details of the neuron's morphology. 共焦显微镜揭示了神经元形态的复杂细节。|Near Synonyms: confocal microscope|Antonyms: conventional microscope|noun
