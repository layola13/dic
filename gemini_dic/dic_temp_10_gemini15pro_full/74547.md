fratted:/ˈfrætɪd/
adjective
兄弟会的；兄弟会式的；过着兄弟会生活的
Characterized by or resembling the social life and activities of a fraternity, especially involving excessive drinking and partying.
He lived a fratted life in college, filled with parties and pranks. 他在大学里过着兄弟会式的生活，充满了派对和恶作剧。
近义词：fraternal, collegiate
反义词：studious, intellectual
词性: adjective
