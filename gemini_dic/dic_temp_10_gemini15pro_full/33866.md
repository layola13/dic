circumscript:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌskrɪpt/ | adj. v. | 外接的，限制的；限定 | to draw a line around; to limit; to restrict | The king's power was circumscribed by the constitution. 国王的权力受宪法限制。|  The circumscribed area allowed for limited expansion.  划定的区域限制了扩张。| 近义词：restrict, confine, limit, bound | 反义词：expand, enlarge, broaden | 词性: adjective, verb

circumscript:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌskrɪpt/ | v. |  限制；限定| to draw a line around; to limit; to restrict |  They tried to circumscribe the spread of the disease. 他们试图限制疾病的传播。 |  His authority was circumscribed by the board of directors. 他的权力受到了董事会的限制。 | 近义词：restrict, confine, limit, bound | 反义词：expand, enlarge, broaden | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin  *circumscrībere*  (to draw a line around, encircle, limit), from *circum* (around) + *scrībere* (to write, draw).
