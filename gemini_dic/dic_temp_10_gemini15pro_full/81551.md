graying:/ˈɡreɪɪŋ/| adj.|变灰白的，头发变白的|Becoming gray; turning gray.  The process of becoming gray. |例句：His graying hair made him look distinguished. 他那花白的头发使他看起来很尊贵。

例句：The graying of the population is a growing concern for many countries. 人口老龄化是许多国家日益关注的问题。|近义词：aging, greying, silvering, whitening|反义词：darkening, blackening|词性:形容词

词源: From gray + -ing.  "Gray" ultimately comes from the Proto-Germanic *grēwaz.
