dumbfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
惊呆的，目瞪口呆的
So amazed or surprised that one is unable to speak; astonished.
She was dumbfounded by the news. 她被这个消息惊呆了。
近义词: astonished, speechless, stunned, bewildered
反义词: unsurprised, calm, composed
词性: adjective


dumbfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
惊呆的，目瞪口呆的
So amazed or surprised that one is unable to speak; astonished.
He was completely dumbfounded when he won the lottery. 当他赢得彩票时，他完全惊呆了。
近义词: astounded, shocked, flabbergasted
反义词: expecting, anticipating
词性: adjective


词源:  From dumb (unable to speak) + confound (to confuse and surprise).  Essentially, it means "struck dumb by confusion and surprise."
