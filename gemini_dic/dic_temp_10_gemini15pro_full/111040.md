lisp:/lɪsp/| n. vi.| 咬舌不清，口齿不清; Lisp (一种编程语言)|A speech defect that involves pronouncing "s" and "z" like "th". Also, a family of computer programming languages.|She speaks with a slight lisp. 她说话有点咬舌。 He's learning to program in Lisp. 他正在学习Lisp编程。|近义词：slur, stammer|反义词: articulate, clear speech|n., vi.
