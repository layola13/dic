overconscientious:/ˌoʊvərˌkɒnʃiˈɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 过分认真的，过分谨慎的 | Characterized by excessive conscientiousness; overly scrupulous and meticulous. | He was so overconscientious that he checked his work three times. 他过分认真，把自己的工作检查了三遍。 | 近义词：scrupulous, meticulous, punctilious | 反义词：careless, negligent | 词性: 形容词

词源:
over- (prefix) + conscientious + -ness (suffix)
"over-" 表示 "过度"， "conscientious" 源自拉丁语 "conscientia" (意为 "良心")，"-ness" 是一个名词后缀，用于构成抽象名词。
