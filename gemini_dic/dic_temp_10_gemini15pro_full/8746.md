antidiarrheal: /ˌæntiˌdaɪəˈriːəl/ | adj. | 止泻的 | Having the property of preventing or relieving diarrhea. |  The antidiarrheal properties of the medication helped to solidify his stool.  这种药物的止泻特性帮助他巩固了大便。|  She was advised to drink plenty of fluids and take antidiarrheal medicine. 她被建议多喝水并服用止泻药。| Synonyms: antidiarrhetic | Antonyms: laxative, purgative | Adjective


Etymology:  "anti-" (Greek, against) + "diarrhea" (from Greek "diarrhoia," meaning "flowing through").
