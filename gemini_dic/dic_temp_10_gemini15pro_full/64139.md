equaled: /ˈiːkwəld/ (US), /ˈiːkwəld/ (UK)
verb
等于；比得上
made equal; amounted to.
The revenues equaled the expenses. 收入等于支出。
synonyms: matched, reached, amounted to
antonyms: differed from, surpassed, fell short of
词性: verb

equaled: /ˈiːkwəld/ (US), /ˈiːkwəld/ (UK)
adjective
相等的；比得上的 (This usage is less common and usually appears as "equalled" in British English)
being the same in quantity, size, degree, or value.
Their scores were equaled in the final round.  他们的分数在最后一轮打平了。 (Less common usage)
synonyms: equivalent, identical, uniform
antonyms: unequal, dissimilar, different
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *equalen*, from Latin *aequalis* (“equal, even, like”), from *aequus* (“level, even, equal, just”).
