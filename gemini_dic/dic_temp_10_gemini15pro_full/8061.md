anthropomancy:/ˌænθrəpəˈmænsi/
n.
占卜; 人体占卜
Divination by the inspection of human entrails, especially the liver.  The practice of attempting to foretell the future by examining human bodies, especially the intestines of sacrificed victims.
Ancient Greeks sometimes practiced anthropomancy to predict the outcome of battles. 古希腊人有时会进行人体占卜来预测战争的结果。
Anthropomancy was a brutal and superstitious practice. 人体占卜是一种残忍且迷信的做法。
近义词: divination, augury, necromancy
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "scientific prediction")
