oversecured:/ˌoʊvər səˈkjʊərd/
adjective
过度担保的；过分安全的
protected by too much security, or having borrowed less money than the value of the assets used as collateral.
The loan was oversecured, meaning the collateral was worth significantly more than the amount borrowed. 这笔贷款过度担保，意味着抵押品的价值远远超过借款金额。
The bank felt comfortable lending the money because the loan was oversecured by the property. 因为这笔贷款由房产过度担保，所以银行放心地贷出了这笔钱。
近义词: overcollateralized, excessively secured
反义词: undersecured, insecure
