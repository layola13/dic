consonants:/ˈkɑːnsənənts/| n.|辅音|A basic speech sound in which the breath is at least partly obstructed and which can be combined with a vowel to form a syllable.|The word "strength" has several consonants clustered together.|近义词:non-vowels|反义词:vowels|noun
