citron:/ˈsɪtrən/ | n. | 香橼; 香橼树;  |A pale yellow, thick-rinded citrus fruit similar to a lemon but larger and less acidic. citron:/ˈsɪtrən/ | adj. |  柠檬黄色的 |Having a pale yellow color like that of a citron. |She wore a citron dress to the party. 她穿着一件柠檬黄色的连衣裙去参加聚会。 | The walls were painted a soft citron hue. 墙壁被漆成了柔和的柠檬黄色。 | 近义词: lemon-yellow, pale yellow | 反义词: N/A | 形容词
