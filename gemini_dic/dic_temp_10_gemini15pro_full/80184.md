godhead:/ˈɡɒdˌhed/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdˌhed/ (US)| n.|神性，神格，神位|The state or condition of being a god; divinity.  It can also refer to the divine nature, especially the Trinity in Christian theology.|例句：The painting depicted the godhead in all its glory.  这幅画描绘了神性的全部荣耀。

例句：They believed in the godhead of the emperor. 他们相信皇帝的神性。|近义词：divinity, deity, godhood|反义词:mortality|词性:noun
