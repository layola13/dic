hearer:/ˈhɪrər (US), ˈhɪərə(UK)|
n.|听者，听众|A person who hears something; a member of an audience.|The speaker tried to engage the hearers with an emotional story. 演讲者试图用一个感人的故事来吸引听众。|
近义词:listener, audience, auditor|
反义词:speaker, talker|
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English herer, from Old English hȳrere, from Proto-Germanic *hauzijaną (“to hear”).  Cognate with Dutch hoorder (“hearer”), German Hörer (“listener”).
