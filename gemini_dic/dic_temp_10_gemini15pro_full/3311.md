affluency: /ˈæfluənsi/ | n. | 富裕，富足 |  The state of having a great deal of money; wealth. |  The family lived in affluency for generations.  这个家族几代人都过着富裕的生活。| synonyms: wealth, prosperity, opulence, riches | antonyms: poverty, destitution, indigence | noun

Etymology:  From Latin *affluentia, from affluēns (“flowing to, abounding”), present participle of affluō (“I flow to, abound”).
