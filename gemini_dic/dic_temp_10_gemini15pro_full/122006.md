misstamped:/ˌmɪsˈstæmpt/
adjective
盖错邮戳的；印错戳的
marked with a wrong stamp or improperly stamped.
The misstamped envelope was returned to sender.  盖错邮戳的信封被退回给寄件人。
The passport was misstamped, causing delays at the border.  护照盖错了章，导致在边境延误。
近义词:incorrectly stamped, wrongly stamped
反义词:correctly stamped
词性:adjective
词源:mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + stamped (past participle of "stamp")
