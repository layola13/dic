incrementable:/ɪnkrɪˈmɛntəbl/ | adj. |可增量的，可递增的|Able to be increased or added to, typically in small amounts.|The software allows for incrementable adjustments to the volume.  该软件允许对音量进行递增调整。|例句：The value is incrementable by one.  该值可以递增1。|近义词：adjustable, adaptable, modifiable|反义词：fixed, unchangeable|词性:adjective
词源: From "increment" + "-able". "Increment" comes from Latin "incrementum," meaning "increase" or "growth."
