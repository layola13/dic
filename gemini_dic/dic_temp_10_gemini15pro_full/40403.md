contraindicate:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkeɪt/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈɪndɪkeɪt/| vt.|禁忌；显示…的禁忌症|To make (a treatment or procedure) inadvisable.|This medication is contraindicated in patients with kidney disease. 这种药物禁用于肾病患者。|近义词：proscribe, forbid, preclude|反义词:indicate, suggest, advise|verb

contraindication:/ˌkɒntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|禁忌症|A factor that makes a particular treatment or procedure inadvisable.|A history of heart disease is a contraindication for this type of surgery. 心脏病史是这种手术的禁忌症。|近义词：objection, deterrent|反义词:indication|noun
