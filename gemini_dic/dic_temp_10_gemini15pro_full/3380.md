affront:/əˈfrʌnt/| n. |侮辱，冒犯|An action or remark that causes outrage or offense.|His refusal to shake hands was a deliberate affront. 他拒绝握手是故意的侮辱。|近义词：insult, offense, slight|反义词:compliment, praise|noun
affront:/əˈfrʌnt/| vt. |公开侮辱，冒犯|To offend the dignity or self-respect of.|I was most affronted by his comments. 我被他说的那些话给气坏了。|近义词：insult, offend, provoke|反义词:honor, respect|verb
