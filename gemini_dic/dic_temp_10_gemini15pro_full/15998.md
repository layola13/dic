bamboozlers:/ˌbæmbuːzlərz/ | n. | 骗子；欺诈者 | People who deceive or trick others, often through confusing or misleading tactics. | Those bamboozlers sold me a fake antique. 那些骗子卖给我一件假古董。| The bamboozlers convinced her to invest in a nonexistent company. 这些骗子说服她投资一家根本不存在的公司。 |  Synonyms: swindlers, cheats, con artists, fraudsters, tricksters | Antonyms: honest people, truth-tellers | noun
