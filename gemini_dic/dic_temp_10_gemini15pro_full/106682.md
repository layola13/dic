如果您想了解“what”的用法，我可以提供如下分析：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么 | used to ask for information about something |

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？

近义词： which, how
反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually do on weekends? = 你周末通常做什么？
What does she think about the new policy? = 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing right now? = 你现在正在做什么？
What is he working on at the moment? = 他现在正在做什么工作？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon? = 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has he been working on for the past few months? = 他过去几个月一直在做什么工作？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party last night? = 昨晚的派对发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before I arrived? = 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 中断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
