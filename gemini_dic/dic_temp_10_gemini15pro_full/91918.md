hypermanic:/ˌhaɪpərˈmænɪk/| adj.|躁狂的，过度兴奋的|Showing excessive excitement and energy; extremely manic.|He was hypermanic, staying up for days and spending money recklessly. 他处于过度兴奋的状态，几天没睡觉，花钱大手大脚。|例句：

His hypermanic episode led to impulsive decisions.
他的过度兴奋导致了冲动的决定。

She became hypermanic after starting the new medication.
她开始服用新药后变得过度兴奋。|近义词：manic, frenzied, frantic, overexcited|反义词：depressed, lethargic, calm|词性:形容词
