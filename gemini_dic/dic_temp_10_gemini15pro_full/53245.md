directorships:/dəˈrɛktərˌʃɪps/ |n. |董事职位；董事身份|The position of being a director of a company or organization.  A person may hold multiple directorships simultaneously. |She holds several directorships on the boards of major corporations.| 近义词: board memberships, board seats | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "unemployment" in a broad sense) | 词性: 名词

词源:
From "director" + "-ship".  "Director" comes from the Latin "dirigere" (to direct). "-ship" is a suffix denoting status, condition, or office.
