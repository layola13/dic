incredulity:/ˌɪnkrəˈduːləti/ | n. |怀疑；不相信；难以置信|the state of being unwilling or unable to believe something | He stared at me in incredulity. 他难以置信地盯着我。| 近义词: disbelief, skepticism, doubt | 反义词: belief, faith, trust | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incredulitas, from Latin incredulus (unbelieving) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns).  The Latin incredulus is from in- (not) + credulus (believing), which comes from credere (to believe).
