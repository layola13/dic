disbars:/dɪsˈbɑːrz/
verb
取消律师资格
to expel a lawyer from the bar; to deprive (a lawyer) of the right to practice law
The lawyer was disbarred for misconduct.  这名律师因行为不端被取消了律师资格。
近义词：suspend, expel
反义词：reinstate, admit
词性：v.

disbars:/dɪsˈbɑːrz/
verb
禁止，阻止
to prevent or forbid
The new rules disbar anyone under 18 from entering the club. 新规定禁止18岁以下的人进入俱乐部。
近义词：prohibit, forbid, exclude
反义词：allow, permit, include
词性：v.
