daunted:/ˈdɔːntɪd/ (US), /ˈdɔːntɪd/ (UK)| adj.| 畏惧的，胆怯的| Feeling intimidated or discouraged by the size or difficulty of a task.|  The climbers were undaunted by the sheer cliff face. 攀岩者们并没有被陡峭的崖壁吓倒。|  She was daunted by the prospect of public speaking. 她对公开演讲的前景感到胆怯。 | synonyms: intimidated, discouraged, disheartened, overwhelmed, apprehensive | antonyms: undaunted, fearless, bold, confident, courageous | adjective
