globy:/ˈɡloʊbi/| adj.|球形的，球状的；圆胖的|Resembling a globe; spherical; round and plump. |The child had a globy face.  这个孩子的脸圆嘟嘟的。|The planet appeared globy through the telescope. 通过望远镜，这颗行星看起来呈球形。|近义词：round, spherical, rotund, plump|反义词：flat, thin, slender|词性:形容词
词源: From globe + -y.  来自 globe（球体）+ -y（形容词后缀）。
