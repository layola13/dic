grasslike: /ˈɡræsˌlaɪk/ | adj. |像草的；草似的 | Resembling grass, especially in appearance or texture. | The plant had long, grasslike leaves. 这种植物长着长长的、像草一样的叶子。 | similar to grass, grassy | bushy, shrubby | adjective

Etymology: grass + like. From "grass" (Old English græs) + "like" (Old English līc).
