nonburdensome: /ˌnɒnˈbɜːrdənsəm/ | adj. | 无负担的，不繁重的 | Not burdensome; easy to bear or manage. | The application process was surprisingly nonburdensome. 申请流程出乎意料地简单。|  This new software is nonburdensome and easy to use.  这个新软件使用起来很轻松，没有负担。 | 近义词: easy, simple, lightweight, manageable, undemanding | 反义词: burdensome, onerous, difficult, demanding, cumbersome | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "burdensome" (difficult to bear)
