overjocularly: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɒkjʊləli/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈdʒɒkjʊləli/ (UK)| adv. | 过于滑稽地，过于诙谐地 | In a manner that is excessively or inappropriately jocular.  Derived from "over" (excessive) + "jocular" (humorous) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). | He overjocularly slapped his friend on the back, causing him to spill his drink. 他过于滑稽地拍了他朋友的背，导致他洒了饮料。|  He overjocularly announced the bad news, making light of the situation. 他过于轻佻地宣布了这个坏消息，对情况轻描淡写。| 近义词：facetiously, flippantly, jokingly | 反义词：seriously, solemnly | 词性: 副词
