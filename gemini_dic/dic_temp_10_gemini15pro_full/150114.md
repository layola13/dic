pamper:/ˈpæmpər/| vt.|纵容，娇惯；精心护理；使…过得舒服|to treat with extreme or excessive kindness or care; to indulge|She pampers her dog with the finest treats and toys. 她用最好的零食和玩具娇惯她的狗。|近义词：indulge, spoil, coddle, cosset|反义词：neglect, deprive|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *pamperen, frequentative of pampen “to cram, stuff.”  Originally meant "to cram with food."
