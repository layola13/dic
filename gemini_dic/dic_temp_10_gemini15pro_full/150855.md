paragrammatical:/ˌpærəɡrəˈmætɪkəl/
adj.
准语法的；半语法的；近乎语法的
Relating to an utterance that deviates slightly from grammatical norms but is still comprehensible.
Example:  The child's utterance, "I goed to the park," is paragrammatical.  孩子说的“我去了公园”这句话是准语法的。

Example:  His writing style is sometimes paragrammatical, but his meaning is clear. 他的写作风格有时不完全符合语法规范，但他的意思很清楚。
Near synonyms:  ungrammatical,agrammatical,solecistic
Antonyms: grammatical, correct
词性: 形容词
