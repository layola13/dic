nonattacking:/ˌnɒnəˈtækɪŋ/
adjective
非攻击性的；不互相攻击的
Not attacking or involving attack.
In chess, two queens are nonattacking if they cannot capture each other. 在国际象棋中，如果两个皇后不能互相俘获，则它们是非攻击性的。
The two groups agreed to a nonattacking stance while negotiations continued.  在谈判继续进行的同时，两组同意采取不互相攻击的姿态。
近义词:peaceful, harmless, nonaggressive
反义词:aggressive, attacking, hostile
词性:adjective
