corsets:/ˈkɔːrsɪts/ | n. |紧身胸衣|A close-fitting undergarment or outer garment worn to support and shape the waist and torso, typically extending from below the bust to the hips. |例句：Women in the Victorian era often wore corsets.  维多利亚时代的女性经常穿紧身胸衣。

例句：She laced up her corset tightly. 她紧紧地系上了她的紧身胸衣。|近义词：stays, girdle, bodice|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 loose clothing 宽松的衣服)|词性: noun


corset:/ˈkɔːrsɪt/ | v. |穿紧身胸衣|To dress or furnish with a corset. |例句：She corseted herself tightly for the ball. 她为了参加舞会紧紧地穿上了紧身胸衣。

例句：The actress was corseted for the period drama.  这位女演员为这部时代剧穿上了紧身胸衣。|近义词：constrict, cinch|反义词： loosen, free|词性: verb


词源: From Old French *cors, diminutive of corps "body," from Latin corpus.
