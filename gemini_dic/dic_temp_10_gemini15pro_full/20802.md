blasé:/blɑːˈzeɪ/
adjective
厌倦的，漠不关心的
unimpressed or indifferent to something because one has experienced or seen it so often before.
He was blasé about the dangers of the trip, having done it several times. 他对这次旅行的危险漠不关心，因为他已经做过好几次了。

blasé:/blɑːˈzeɪ/
adjective
玩腻了的，无动于衷的
bored or unimpressed by things because of excessive indulgence or enjoyment.
She became blasé about expensive restaurants after dining in so many.  在去过这么多高档餐厅吃饭后，她对它们变得无动于衷了。


ETYMOLOGY->
From French blasé, past participle of blaser "to satiate," perhaps ultimately from Old High German blasan "to blow."


SYNONYMS->
apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned, uninterested, world-weary, jaded

ANTONYMS->
enthusiastic, excited, interested, passionate
