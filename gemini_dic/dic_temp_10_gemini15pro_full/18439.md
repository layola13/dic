bema:/ˈbiːmə/| n.|讲台，高台，审判席|A raised platform used as a judgment seat or for public speaking, especially in ancient Greece or Rome. |The speaker addressed the crowd from the bema. 演讲者在讲台上向人群发表讲话。|The emperor judged the prisoner from his bema.  皇帝从他的审判席上审判囚犯。|近义词：platform, podium, dais|反义词:floor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βῆμα (bêma, “step, pace, gait; base, foundation; stride; tribunal”), from βαίνω (bainō, “I go, walk, step”).
