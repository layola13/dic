campagna:/kæmˈpɑːnjə/ /kæmˈpeɪnjə/
n.
campaign; countryside; rural area
A series of military operations intended to achieve a particular objective, confined to a particular area, or involving a specified type of fighting. Also, a planned series of activities designed to achieve a particular social, commercial or political aim; the surrounding countryside.
The Italian army launched a campagna against the Austrians. 意大利军队对奥地利人发动了一场战役。
We spent a relaxing weekend in the campagna. 我们在乡下度过了一个轻松的周末。
近义词：campaign, countryside, rural area
反义词：city, urban area
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian campagna (“countryside, level country, plain, field; campaign”), from Late Latin campania (“plain, level country”), from Latin campus (“field, plain”). Doublet of champion and champ.
