n.
鸡皮疙瘩；毛骨悚然
The sudden chill caused horripilation.
突如其来的寒意使我起了鸡皮疙瘩。
A feeling of horripilation ran down his spine.
一阵毛骨悚然的感觉沿着他的脊椎向下蔓延。
Synonyms: goosebumps, gooseflesh, chills, shivers
Antonyms: calmness, composure

Etymology: From Latin *horripilatio*, from *horripilare* "to bristle up, stand on end," from *horrere* "to bristle, shudder" + *pilus* "hair."
