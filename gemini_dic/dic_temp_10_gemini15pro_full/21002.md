blesseder:/ˈblesədər/ (less common variant of 'blesser')| n.|祝福者 (較少用的變體)|A person who bestows blessings or good fortune.  This form is archaic and rarely used.  The modern equivalent is "blesser".  The term has also taken on a more contemporary, often negative, connotation in some cultures, referring to a wealthy older person who provides financial support to a younger person in exchange for companionship or sexual favors. |Example 1: In olden times, the blesseder of the village was revered. (古时候，村庄里的祝福者受到尊敬。)

Example 2:  (Archaic Usage) The blesseder laid hands upon the child. (祝福者把手放在孩子身上。) |近义词：benefactor, patron|反义词：curse, jinx|词性: noun
