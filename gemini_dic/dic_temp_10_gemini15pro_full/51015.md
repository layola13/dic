deskilled:/diːˈskɪld/
| adj. |去技能化的；降低技术含量的|Reduced in skill level, often due to technological advancements that simplify tasks or replace skilled workers with machines.|Many factory workers have been deskilled by the introduction of automated machinery. 许多工厂工人因自动化机械的引入而技能降低。|例句：The new software deskilled many traditional design jobs. 新的软件降低了许多传统设计工作的技能要求。

例句：The rise of automation has deskilled many jobs in the manufacturing industry. 自动化的兴起降低了制造业中许多工作的技能要求。


|近义词：downgraded, simplified, automated|反义词：upskilled, enhanced, specialized|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce") + skilled.  The term gained prominence in the late 20th century with discussions of the impact of technology on labor. 源自de-（前缀，意为“移除”或“减少”）+ skilled。该词在20世纪后期随着关于技术对劳动力影响的讨论而变得突出。
