nothingism:/ˈnʌθɪŋɪzəm/
n.
虚无主义；认为人生无意义的哲学
The philosophy that life is meaningless and without objective purpose.
He embraced nothingism after the loss of his family.  在失去家人之后，他接受了虚无主义。
近义词：nihilism, pessimism
反义词：optimism, existentialism
词性: 名词
