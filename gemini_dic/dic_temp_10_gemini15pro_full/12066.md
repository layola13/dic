artificialism:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.|人为论；人工主义|The belief that natural phenomena are caused by the intentional acts of a god or other supernatural being.  The belief that everything is a product of human creation or artifice.|
例句：The child's artificialism was evident in his belief that the wind was caused by someone blowing very hard.  这个孩子的人为论很明显，因为他相信风是由某人大力吹造成的。

例句：Her artificialism led her to think that the mountains were built by giants. 她的人为论让她认为山脉是巨人建造的。
近义词：anthropomorphism|
反义词:naturalism|
词性:noun
