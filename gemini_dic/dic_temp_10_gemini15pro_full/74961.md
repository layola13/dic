fribbler:/ˈfrɪblər/| n.| 轻浮的人，玩弄感情的人；浪费时间的人 |A person who trifles or wastes time on frivolous pursuits; one who engages in flirtatious behavior without serious intent.|He was known as a fribbler, never serious about his work or relationships. 他被认为是一个轻浮的人，对工作和感情都不认真。|近义词：dabbler, trifler, philanderer, flirt|反义词：earnest person, dedicated individual|词性: 名词

词源:  Likely from the verb "fribble," meaning to trifle or act frivolously, which in turn likely comes from a frequentative form of an obsolete verb "frib," also meaning to trifle.  The "-er" suffix denotes a person who performs the action.
