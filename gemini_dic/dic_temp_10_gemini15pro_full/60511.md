elaborate: /ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ (verb) /ɪˈlæbərət/ (adjective)
verb|阐述；详细说明；精心制作|To explain or describe something in detail.|She elaborated on her plans for the future. 她详细阐述了她对未来的计划。|expand, develop, expound|simplify, condense|v.
elaborate: /ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ (verb) /ɪˈlæbərət/ (adjective)
adjective|复杂的；详尽的；精心的|Detailed and complicated; carefully prepared and finished.|He had prepared an elaborate excuse for his absence. 他为自己的缺席准备了一个详尽的借口。|complex, intricate, detailed|simple, basic|adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *elaboratus, past participle of elaborare (“to work out, produce by labor”), from Latin e- (“out”) + laborare (“to labor”).
