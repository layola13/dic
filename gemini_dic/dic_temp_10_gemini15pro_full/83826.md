haka:/ˈhɑːkə/ | n. |哈卡舞，一种毛利人的传统战舞 | A traditional ancestral war cry, dance, or challenge from the Māori people of New Zealand. It is often performed by a group, with vigorous movements and rhythmic shouting. |  The All Blacks performed a powerful haka before the rugby match. 全黑队在橄榄球比赛前表演了一场气势磅礴的哈卡舞。


The haka is more than just a dance; it's an expression of cultural pride. 哈卡舞不仅仅是一种舞蹈；它还是一种文化自豪感的表达。 | 近义词: war dance, challenge, ritual | 反义词: peace dance, surrender | 词性: noun
