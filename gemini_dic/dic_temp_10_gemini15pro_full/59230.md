doom:/duːm/
n.|厄运；毁灭；死亡；判决；|A statement of what will certainly happen to someone, especially something unpleasant.|例句：The newspapers predicted doom and disaster for the economy. 报纸预测经济将会出现厄运和灾难。|例句：He seemed to be heading for financial doom. 他似乎正走向经济毁灭。|近义词：ruin, destruction, demise, fate|反义词：fortune, luck, blessing|词性:名词
