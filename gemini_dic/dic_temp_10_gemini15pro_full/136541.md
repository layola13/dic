nonrurally:/ˌnɒnˈrʊrəli/
| adv. |非农村地，在城市地|Not in a rural area; in a city or town. |  Many young people prefer to live nonrurally, drawn by the amenities and opportunities of urban life. 许多年轻人更喜欢居住在城市，被城市生活的便利设施和机会所吸引。|Synonyms: urbanly, in the city, in town |Antonyms: rurally, in the country, in the countryside |adverb

Etymology:
* non- : a prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*
* rural : from Late Middle English, from Old French *rural*, from Latin *ruralis* "of the countryside," from *rus, ruris* "countryside," of uncertain origin.
* -ly : adverbial suffix
