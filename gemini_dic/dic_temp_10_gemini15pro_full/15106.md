baccivorous: /ˌbæsɪˈvɔːrəs/
adjective
食浆果的
Feeding on berries.
The baccivorous bird flitted from branch to branch, feasting on the ripe berries.  这只食浆果的鸟从一个树枝飞到另一个树枝，尽情享用成熟的浆果。
The researchers observed the baccivorous habits of the rare bird species. 研究人员观察了这种稀有鸟类的食浆果习性。
近义词: frugivorous
反义词: carnivorous, herbivorous
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *bacc(a) "berry" + -vorous.
