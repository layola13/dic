expatriation:/ˌɛksˌpeɪtriˈeɪʃən/ | n. |  移居国外；放弃国籍 | The act of leaving one's native country to live elsewhere, often permanently, and sometimes including the renunciation of citizenship. | Many Americans choose expatriation for tax purposes. 许多美国人为了避税而选择移居国外。| emigration, exile | repatriation, naturalization | noun
