bafflement:/ˈbæflmənt/| n.|困惑，迷惑，不解|The complexity of the situation caused widespread bafflement. 这种情况的复杂性引起了广泛的困惑。|She stared at him in bafflement. 她困惑地盯着他。|近义词：confusion, bewilderment, perplexity|反义词：clarity, understanding|词性:名词
词源: From baffle + -ment, from Old French bafler "to muzzle, deceive."
