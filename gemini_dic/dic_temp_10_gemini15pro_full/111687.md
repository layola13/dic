locket:/ˈlɒkɪt/ (US) /ˈlɒkɪt/ (UK)| n.| 小盒式吊坠，纪念品盒|A small ornamental case, typically made of gold or silver, worn on a chain around the neck and containing a portrait or other memento.|She wore a locket containing a picture of her grandmother. 她戴着一个装着她祖母照片的吊坠盒。|Example: He gave her a locket with a picture of himself inside. 他送给她一个里面装着自己照片的吊坠盒。|Synonyms: pendant, medallion|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
