frump:/frʌmp/
n.|邋遢的女人；古板的人|A dull, old-fashioned, and unattractive woman. |She looked like a frump in that old dress. 她穿着那件旧衣服看起来像个邋遢女人。|近义词：dowdy, drab, unfashionable|反义词：stylish, fashionable, chic|词性: 名词

frump:/frʌmp/
v.|使显得邋遢；使显得古板|To make someone look dull, old-fashioned, or unattractive.|The old hat frumped her appearance. 那顶旧帽子使她显得邋遢。|近义词：dowdy, drab|反义词：stylish, fashionable|词性: 动词
