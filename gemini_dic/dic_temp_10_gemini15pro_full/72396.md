flued:/fluːd/
noun|烟道；管道；有烟道的 |A duct or passage for smoke, especially from a fireplace or furnace, typically made of brick or metal. |The chimney flued the smoke from the fireplace efficiently.  烟囱有效地将壁炉里的烟排了出去。|Synonym: chimney, vent, duct|Antonym: inlet|n.


flued:/fluːd/
adjective|有烟道的；有管道的 |Having a flue or flues. |The house is flued for a wood-burning stove. 这房子有烧木柴炉子的烟道。|Synonym: vented|Antonym: unvented|adj.
