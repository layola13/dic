overfavors: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪvərz/
verb
过分偏袒，过分宠爱
To show excessive favoritism towards someone or something.
He overfavors his youngest child, often giving her more privileges than his other children.  他过分偏袒他最小的孩子，经常给她比其他孩子更多的特权。
She tends to overfavor certain employees, leading to resentment among the staff. 她倾向于过分偏袒某些员工，导致员工之间的怨恨。
近义词: spoil, pamper, indulge, coddle
反义词: neglect, ignore, slight
词性: verb

Etymology:
"Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "favor" (from Old French "faveur" and Latin "favor," meaning "goodwill, kindness")
