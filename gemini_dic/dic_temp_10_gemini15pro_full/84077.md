half-hypnotized:/ˌhæf ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzd/
adjective
半催眠的，处于半催眠状态的
Being in a state between wakefulness and hypnosis; dazed or entranced.
She sat staring at the flickering candle flame, half-hypnotized by its movement. 她坐在那里盯着闪烁的烛光，被它的移动弄得半催眠状态。
The rhythmic sound of the waves had a half-hypnotizing effect on me.  海浪有节奏的声音对我产生了半催眠的效果。
近义词: mesmerized, entranced, spellbound, captivated
反义词: alert, awake, conscious
