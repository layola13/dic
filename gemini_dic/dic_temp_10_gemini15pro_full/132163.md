noncursive:/nɑːnˈkɜːrsɪv/
adjective
非草书的，印刷体的
Not written in a joined-up or flowing style; printed.
Her noncursive handwriting was very neat and easy to read. 她的印刷体字迹非常整洁易读。
The teacher asked the students to write their names in noncursive letters. 老师要求学生用印刷体写名字。
近义词:print, block
反义词:cursive, joined-up
词性:adjective
