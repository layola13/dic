discardment:/dɪˈskɑːrdmənt/
n.|抛弃，丢弃|The discardment of old traditions can lead to both positive and negative consequences.  抛弃旧传统可能会导致正面和负面的后果。|The city council approved the discardment of hazardous waste. 市议会批准了对有害废物的丢弃。|近义词：rejection, disposal, abandonment|反义词：acceptance, retention, keeping|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "discard" + suffix "-ment." "Discard" comes from the Old French "escarde," meaning a discarded card.
