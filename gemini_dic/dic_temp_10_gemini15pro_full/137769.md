nonvascular:/nɒnˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非维管的 |  Lacking vascular tissue; not containing vessels that conduct fluids such as blood or sap.  |  Mosses are nonvascular plants. 苔藓是非维管植物。 | Non-vascular plants include mosses, liverworts, and hornworts. 非维管植物包括苔藓、地钱和角苔。| synonyms: avascular | antonyms: vascular | adjective
