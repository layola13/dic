injucundity:/ˌɪndʒʊˈkʌndɪti/
n.
不愉快，不高兴
The state or quality of being unpleasant or disagreeable.
His injucundity made it difficult to be around him. 他的不愉快让人很难和他相处。
近义词：unpleasantness, disagreeableness, dourness, sullenness, moroseness
反义词：pleasantness, agreeableness, cheerfulness, joviality
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *injucunditas, from Latin injucundus (“unpleasant”), from in- (“not”) + jucundus (“pleasant”).
