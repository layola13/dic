amphitrocha:/ˈæmfɪˌtroʊkə/| n.|两端轮虫，双环轮虫|A type of trochophore larva in which the preoral and postoral ciliated bands (prototroch and telotroch) are continuous.  It is characteristic of many annelids and mollusks. |The amphitrocha larva is a common feature of many marine invertebrates.  双环轮虫幼虫是许多海洋无脊椎动物的共同特征。|The marine snail develops from an amphitrocha larva.  这种海螺是由双环轮虫幼虫发育而来的。|近义词：trochophore larva|反义词:|noun
