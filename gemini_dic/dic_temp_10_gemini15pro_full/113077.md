lunacy:/ˈluːnəsi/| n.|疯狂，精神错乱|The idea of swimming across the Atlantic in a bathtub seemed like sheer lunacy.  在浴缸里横渡大西洋的想法看起来完全是疯了。|例句：He was prone to bouts of lunacy. 他容易发作精神错乱。

例句：Her plan to hike Mount Everest in flip-flops bordered on lunacy. 她穿着人字拖攀登珠穆朗玛峰的计划近乎疯狂。|近义词：insanity, madness, dementia|反义词:sanity, lucidity|词性:名词
