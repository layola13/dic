longhorns:/ˈlɔːŋhɔːrnz/
noun
长角牛; (尤指)德克萨斯长角牛
A breed of cattle with long horns, especially the Texas Longhorn.
The longhorns grazed peacefully on the ranch. 长角牛在牧场上平静地吃草。
Longhorns are a symbol of the American West. 长角牛是美国西部的象征。
Synonyms: cattle, steers, oxen
Antonyms: N/A

ETYMOLOGY->
From "long" + "horn," referring to the animal's characteristically long horns.  The word first appeared in the late 19th century.
