guilting:/ˈɡɪltɪŋ/| v.|使内疚，使愧疚|The act of making someone feel guilty.  Making someone feel responsible for something bad, whether or not they actually are.|She's always guilting me about not visiting her enough. 她总是让我因为没有经常去看她而感到内疚。|Synonyms: blaming, reproaching, accusing|Antonyms: praising, commending|verb
