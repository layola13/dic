mobilising:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪˌlaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)| v. (present participle)|动员，调动|The act of making something movable or capable of action; assembling and preparing something, especially resources or people, for action or use.|The government is mobilising troops for the war. 政府正在为战争动员军队。|近义词：activating, assembling, deploying, marshaling, rallying|反义词: demobilising, disbanding|verb

mobilisation:/ˌmoʊbɪlɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US) /ˌməʊbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK)| n. |动员，调动|The act or process of mobilising something or someone.|The mobilisation of the army took several weeks.  军队的动员花了几个星期。|近义词：activation, assembly, deployment|反义词: demobilisation, disbandment|noun
