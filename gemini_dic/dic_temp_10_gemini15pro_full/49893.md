demurrage:/dɪˈmɜːrɪdʒ/ | n. |滞期费，延滞费| A charge payable to the owner of a chartered ship on failure to load or discharge the ship within the time agreed.|The charterer had to pay a substantial amount of demurrage due to port congestion.| Synonyms: detention charge, waiting charge | Antonyms: dispatch money,  expedition bonus | noun

demurrage:/dɪˈmɜːrɪdʒ/ | n. |延滞，滞留|The act of delaying or hindering.|There was a demurrage in the project due to unforeseen circumstances. | Synonyms: delay, hindrance | Antonyms: expedition, acceleration | noun
