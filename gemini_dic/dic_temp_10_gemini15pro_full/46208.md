cyriologic: /ˌsɪriəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 蜘蛛学的；与蜘蛛研究相关的 | Relating to the study of spiders. | Cyriologic research has led to new discoveries about spider venom. 蜘蛛学研究导致了关于蜘蛛毒液的新发现。 |  Cyriologic studies are crucial for understanding the biodiversity of these fascinating creatures. 蜘蛛学研究对于理解这些迷人生物的生物多样性至关重要。 | 近义词: arachnological | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Greek *κυριος (kyrios, “lord, master”) + -logic.  The name likely refers to the imposing nature of some spiders.  *κυριος is also the root of "cyriopagus," referring to conjoined twins joined at the sacrum.
