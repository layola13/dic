endothelial:/ˌɛndəˈθiːliəl/ | adj. | 内皮的 | Relating to or consisting of endothelium. |  The endothelial cells lining the blood vessels regulate blood flow. 内皮细胞排列在血管内壁，调节血液流动。|  The drug improved endothelial function.  这种药物改善了内皮功能。 | 近义词: lining, vascular | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

endothelium:/ˌɛndəˈθiːliəm/ | n. | 内皮 | A thin layer of cells that lines the inner surface of blood vessels and lymphatic vessels. | Damage to the endothelium can lead to blood clots. 内皮损伤会导致血栓。 | The endothelium plays a crucial role in regulating vascular tone. 内皮在调节血管张力方面起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: lining | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From endo- (within) + -thelium (nipple, tissue layer).  Ultimately from Greek endon (within) and thēlē (nipple).
