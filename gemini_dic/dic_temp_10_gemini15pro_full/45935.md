cutty:/ˈkʌti/| adj. n.|短的，矮的；短裤；短袜|short, shortened (especially of clothing or a person's stature); a short, stubby thing, particularly a short-handled tool or a short-legged animal; (Scottish) a short clay pipe|The farmer wore cutty trousers. 那个农民穿着短裤。|近义词：short, stubby, cropped, brief|反义词：long, lengthy, extended|词性:形容词,名词

cutty sark:/ˌkʌti ˈsɑːrk/| n.|卡蒂萨克号帆船（19世纪著名的快速帆船）|a famous and very fast clipper ship built in the 19th century|The Cutty Sark is a historic sailing ship. 卡蒂萨克号是一艘历史悠久的帆船。|近义词：clipper ship|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
