ominousness:/ˈɒmɪnəsnəs/
noun
不祥的预感；预兆；凶兆
The quality of being ominous; giving the impression that something bad or unpleasant is going to happen.
The ominousness of the dark clouds gathering overhead suggested a storm was coming.  乌云密布天空中不祥的预兆表明暴风雨即将来临。

The silence was filled with a sense of ominousness.  寂静中充满了不祥的预感。

近义词:foreboding, portent, threat, danger, menace
反义词: auspiciousness, hopefulness, positivity
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ominous + -ness.  Ominous derives from Latin *ominōsus, from ōmen (“omen”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives).
