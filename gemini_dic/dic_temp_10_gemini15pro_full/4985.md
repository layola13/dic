alkane:/ˈælkeɪn/ | n. | 烷烃 | A saturated hydrocarbon with the general formula CnH2n+2.  A hydrocarbon containing only single covalent bonds. | Methane is the simplest alkane. 甲烷是最简单的烷烃。 |  Propane is an alkane commonly used as fuel. 丙烷是一种常用的烷烃燃料。 | synonym: paraffin | antonym: alkene, alkyne | noun
