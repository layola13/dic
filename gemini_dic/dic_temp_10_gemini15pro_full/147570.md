overperforming:/ˌoʊvərˈpərˌfɔːrmɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvəpəˈfɔːmɪŋ/| adj.|表现优异的，超出预期的|Performing better than expected or above the required standard.|例句：The company's stock is overperforming this quarter.  这家公司的股票本季度表现优异。|例句：The student is overperforming in all of his classes.  这个学生在他的所有课程中都表现优异。|近义词：excelling, thriving, flourishing|反义词：underperforming, lagging, failing|词性:形容词

overperform:/ˌoʊvərˈpərˌfɔːrm/ /ˌəʊvəpəˈfɔːm/| v.|表现优异，超出预期|To perform better than expected or above the required standard.|例句：The company overperformed in the last quarter, exceeding expectations.  该公司在上个季度表现优异，超出了预期。|例句：She consistently overperforms in her sales role.  她在销售职位上一直表现出色。|近义词：excel, surpass, exceed|反义词：underperform, disappoint|词性:动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, beyond") + "perform" (verb meaning "carry out, execute, fulfill")
