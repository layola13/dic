overirrigate: /ˌoʊvərˈɪrɪɡeɪt/
verb
过度灌溉
To supply (land or crops) with too much water.
The farmer overirrigated his fields and damaged the crops. 农民过度灌溉了他的田地，损坏了庄稼。
The constant rain has overirrigated the garden.  持续的降雨使花园过度灌溉了。
