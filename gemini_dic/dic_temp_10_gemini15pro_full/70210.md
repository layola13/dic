fibrous:/ˈfaɪbrəs/| adj.|纤维的；纤维状的|Composed of or containing fibers; resembling fiber.|例句：The plant has a fibrous root system.  这种植物有纤维状的根系。

例句：Fibrous tissue connects bones and muscles. 纤维组织连接骨骼和肌肉。|近义词：stringy, filamentous, threadlike|反义词: non-fibrous, smooth|词性: adjective
