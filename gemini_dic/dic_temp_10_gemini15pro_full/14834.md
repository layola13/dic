axiomatize:/ˈæksiəməˌtaɪz/ | v. | 公理化；以公理为基础 | To base a system (as of knowledge or belief) on axioms; to reduce to axioms; to treat axiomatically. |  Mathematicians axiomatized Euclidean geometry. 数学家将欧几里得几何学公理化了。|  formalize, systematize, postulate |  - | verb
axiomatization:/ˌæksiəmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 公理化 | The act or process of axiomatizing. | The axiomatization of set theory is a complex undertaking. 集合论的公理化是一项复杂的工作。 | formalization, systematization | - | noun
