manifoldnesses:/ˈmænɪfoʊldnɪsɪz/
noun
多种多样，多样性
The state or quality of being manifold; numerousness; variety.
Her paintings capture the manifoldnesses of nature's beauty. 她的画作捕捉了自然美景的多样性。


近义词:diversity, variety, multiplicity, plurality
反义词:uniformity, sameness, homogeneity
词性:noun


词源: From manifold + -ness. Manifold itself comes from Middle English manyfold, from Old English manigfeald, from manig (many) + feald (fold).
