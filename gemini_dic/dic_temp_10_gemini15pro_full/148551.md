overswell:/ˌoʊvərˈswɛl/| v.| 泛滥，过度膨胀，（感情）压倒，淹没 | To swell over or beyond limits; to overwhelm emotionally. | The river overswelled its banks and flooded the town. 河水泛滥，淹没了城镇。| synonyms: overflow, inundate, overwhelm, submerge | antonyms: recede, diminish, shrink | verb

overswell:/ˌoʊvərˈswɛl/| n.| 泛滥，过度膨胀 | An excessive swelling or overflowing. | The overswell of the river caused significant damage. 河水泛滥造成了重大损失。| synonyms: overflow, deluge, inundate | antonyms: ebb, decline, decrease | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + swell.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating excess or going beyond a limit. "Swell" comes from Old English *swellan, meaning to expand or increase in size.
