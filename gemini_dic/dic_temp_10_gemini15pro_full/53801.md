disconcertion:/ˌdɪskənˈsɜːrʃn/
n.
不安；慌乱；扰乱
A feeling of unease or embarrassment; confusion.
The sudden announcement of the change in plans caused widespread disconcertion among the travelers.  突如其来的计划变更通知引起了旅客们的普遍不安。
Her silence caused him some disconcertion. 她的沉默让他有些不安。
近义词: unease, anxiety, perturbation, confusion, dismay
反义词: composure, calmness, tranquility, equanimity
