flaky:/ˈfleɪki/| adj.|薄片的；易剥落的；不稳定的；古怪的；不靠谱的|Coming apart easily into small thin pieces; unreliable; eccentric.|The pastry was light and flaky. 这糕点又轻又酥。 He's a flaky guy, always changing his mind. 他是个反复无常的家伙，总是改变主意。|Synonyms: brittle, crumbly, unreliable, eccentric, capricious|Antonyms: stable, reliable, consistent|adjective

flaky:/ˈfleɪki/| adj.|古怪的；容易激动的| behaving in a strange or unusual way, especially in a way that is unreliable|He's a bit flaky, but he's a good artist. 他有点古怪，但他是个好艺术家。 She gets flaky under pressure.  她在压力下容易激动。|Synonyms: eccentric, erratic, temperamental|Antonyms: normal, conventional, steady|adjective
