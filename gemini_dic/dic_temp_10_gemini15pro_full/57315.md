downtime:/ˈdaʊntaɪm/| n.|停工期，故障时间，休息时间|A period of time when a system or machine is not working or is not available for use because it is not working properly. |The server experienced some downtime yesterday. 服务器昨天出现了一些故障时间。|近义词：outage, inactivity, idle time|反义词:uptime, working time|noun
