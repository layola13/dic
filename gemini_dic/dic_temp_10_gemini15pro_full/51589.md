devalue:/diːˈvæljuː/
verb
贬值；降低…的价值；贬低
to reduce the value of a currency against other currencies or a standard; to make something less valuable or less important
The government decided to devalue the currency. 政府决定将货币贬值。
They devalued the contribution of women in society. 他们贬低了女性在社会中的贡献。
synonym: depreciate, undervalue, downgrade, cheapen
antonym: appreciate, enhance, upgrade, increase value
词性:动词
