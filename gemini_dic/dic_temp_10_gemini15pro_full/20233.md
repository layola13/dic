birettas:/bɪˈrɛtəz/
n.
Square academic caps, often with a tuft on top.
Square caps worn by graduates and clergy, typically black with a tuft.
The graduating class wore their birettas proudly. 毕业生们自豪地戴着他们的学位帽。
The bishops all donned their birettas for the ceremony. 主教们都戴上了他们的方帽参加仪式。
synonyms:mortarboards, academic caps
antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly casual hats or no hat)
noun
