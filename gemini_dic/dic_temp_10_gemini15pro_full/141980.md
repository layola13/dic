organ-grinder:/ˈɔːrɡənˌɡraɪndər/ | n. |街头手摇风琴演奏者；卖艺人|A person who plays a barrel organ, especially one who performs in the street for money.|The organ-grinder cranked the handle, and the monkey danced for coins. 街头手摇风琴演奏者转动把手，猴子随着音乐跳舞以乞讨钱币。|Synonym: busker, street musician, street performer |Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "concert musician" for contrast) | noun

organ-grinder:/ˈɔːrɡənˌɡraɪndər/ | n. |比喻义：重复单调工作的人|Someone who performs repetitive or monotonous tasks. |He felt like an organ-grinder, churning out the same reports every week. 他觉得自己像个手摇风琴演奏者，每周都在重复地写着同样的报告。|Synonym: drone, drudge |Antonym: innovator, creator | noun
