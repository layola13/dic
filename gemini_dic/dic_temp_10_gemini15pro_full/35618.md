coasts:/kəʊsts/ (British) /koʊsts/ (American)| n.|海岸，海滨，滑行|The ship sailed along the coasts of France. 这艘船沿着法国海岸航行。|例句：The Pacific coasts are beautiful. 太平洋海岸很美。|近义词：shores, beaches, seaside|反义词:inland|noun
coast:/kəʊst/ (British) /koʊst/ (American)| v.|滑行，不费力地前进|The car coasted down the hill. 汽车滑下山坡。|例句：He coasted to victory in the election. 他在选举中轻松获胜。|近义词：glide, drift, freewheel|反义词:pedal, struggle|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coste (Modern French côte), from Latin costa (“rib, side, flank”), which also yielded Italian costa and Spanish costa.
