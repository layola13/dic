morice:/ˈmɒrɪs/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrɪs/ (US)| n.|Morris dance; a Moorish dance; a kind of helmet|莫里斯舞；一种摩尔式舞蹈；一种头盔|A traditional English folk dance involving men with blackened faces, bells on their legs, and waving handkerchiefs or sticks. It's often performed around May Day.  The word also refers to a type of helmet.|例句：The village celebrated May Day with a lively morice dance. 这个村庄以一场热闹的莫里斯舞庆祝五朔节。

例句：The knight wore a morice as part of his armor.  这位骑士戴着头盔作为盔甲的一部分。|近义词：Morris dance, mummers' play (for the dance), helmet (for the headwear)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
