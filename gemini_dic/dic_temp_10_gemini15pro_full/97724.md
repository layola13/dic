iniquitous:/ɪˈnɪkwɪtəs/
adjective
邪恶的；不公正的
grossly unfair and morally wrong
例句：The iniquitous system of apartheid was finally dismantled.  邪恶的种族隔离制度最终被废除。

例句：It is an iniquitous law that allows such cruelty. 这是一项允许这种残酷行为的邪恶法律。

近义词：wicked, evil, unjust, immoral, sinful, nefarious
反义词：righteous, just, virtuous, moral
词性：adjective
