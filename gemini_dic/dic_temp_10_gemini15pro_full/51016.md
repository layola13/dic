deskilling:/diːˈskɪlɪŋ/
n.
减少技能; 技能退化
The reduction of skill needed to perform a job, typically as a result of automation or technological change.
Automation has led to deskilling in many manufacturing industries. 自动化导致了许多制造业的技能退化。
The introduction of new machinery resulted in the deskilling of many traditional craftsmen. 新机器的引进导致许多传统工匠的技能退化。
近义词: downgrading, degradation
反义词: upskilling, reskilling
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'reduce' or 'remove') + skill + -ing (suffix forming a noun from a verb).
