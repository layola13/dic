oversecularizing:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛkjələrˌaɪzɪŋ/
verb
过度世俗化
Making something excessively secular; focusing too much on non-religious aspects of life and neglecting or minimizing religious or spiritual considerations.
The government's policies are oversecularizing the education system, removing any mention of religious holidays or traditions. 政府的政策使教育系统过度世俗化，删除了任何关于宗教节日或传统的提及。
Some critics argue that the media is oversecularizing society by constantly promoting materialistic values. 一些批评人士认为，媒体不断宣传物质主义价值观，使社会过度世俗化。
近义词: secularizing excessively, overemphasizing secularism
反义词: sacralizing, emphasizing religiosity
词性: verb (present participle)
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "secularize" (verb meaning "to make secular") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
