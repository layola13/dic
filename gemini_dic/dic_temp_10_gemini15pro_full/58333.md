drysuit: /ˈdraɪsuːt/ | n. | 防水服，干式潜水服 | A watertight garment worn by divers, boaters, etc., that keeps the wearer dry. | Example 1: The diver wore a drysuit to protect himself from the cold water. 潜水员穿了一件干式潜水服来保护自己免受冷水的侵袭。

Example 2:  Drysuits are essential for diving in extremely cold conditions. 在极冷的条件下潜水，干式潜水服是必不可少的。 | 近义词: diving suit, immersion suit | 反义词: wetsuit | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dry + suit.  Relatively modern, emerging in the mid-20th century with advancements in waterproof materials and diving technology.
