cadge's:/kædʒɪz/ | n. | 乞讨者的东西; 乞讨所得之物 |  Belonging to someone who cadges (begs or obtains something by persuading someone).  It refers to the possessions or things obtained through cadging. | The cadge's meager belongings were tied up in a bandana. 乞丐微薄的家当都捆在一块大手帕里。 | synonyms: beggar's belongings, scrounger's possessions | antonyms:  earned possessions, honestly acquired goods | noun (possessive form)


cadge:/kædʒ/ | v. | 乞讨；讨要；蹭 | To ask for or obtain (something) without paying for it or offering anything in return. | He cadged a cigarette from a stranger. 他向一个陌生人讨了一根烟。 | synonyms: beg, sponge, scrounge, mooch | antonyms: earn, buy, provide | verb
