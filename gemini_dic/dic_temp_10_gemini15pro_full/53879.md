disconvenience:/ˌdɪskənˈviːniəns/
n.|不便，麻烦|An inconvenience or trouble.|The flight delay was a major disconvenience for the travelers.  航班延误给旅客带来了极大的不便。|近义词：inconvenience, trouble, bother, hardship|反义词:convenience, ease, comfort|词性:名词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + convenience.
