commissioners:/kəˈmɪʃənərz/
n.
委员；专员；行政长官
A person appointed to an official position, typically to carry out a specific task or to administer a particular area.
The city commissioners voted to approve the new budget. 市政委员投票通过了新的预算。
The commissioners of the baseball league decided to suspend the player. 棒球联盟的委员们决定暂停该球员的比赛。
Synonyms: officials, administrators, delegates, representatives, agents
Antonyms: subordinates, employees (in some contexts)
