devastating:/ˈdevəsteɪtɪŋ/| adj.|毁灭性的，破坏性的|Causing widespread destruction or damage; highly destructive.  Extremely effective in a destructive way.|The earthquake had a devastating impact on the city.  地震对这座城市造成了毁灭性的影响。|The loss of his job had a devastating effect on his family. 失去工作对他的家庭造成了毁灭性的打击。|近义词：destructive, ruinous, catastrophic, calamitous|反义词：beneficial, helpful, constructive|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From devastate + -ing. Devastate comes from the Latin word "devastatus," the past participle of "devastare," meaning "to lay waste, make desolate."  "Devastare" is formed from "de-" (completely) + "vastare" (to lay waste).
