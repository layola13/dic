locusting: /ˈloʊkəstɪŋ/ | n. |蝗灾；像蝗虫一样席卷；(尤指)短时间内在多地旅行；|The act of swarming or traveling like locusts, often involving short trips to multiple locations, especially for leisure or consumption.|  Locusting has become a popular trend among young travelers. 年轻游客中，蝗虫式旅游成为一种流行趋势。| 近义词: swarming, overrunning, descending | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 staying put, remaining, settling down 表示待在原地) | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from "locust," from Old French "locuste," ultimately from Latin "locusta" (locust, lobster). The "-ing" suffix transforms the noun into a gerund or present participle, indicating the act of behaving like a locust.
