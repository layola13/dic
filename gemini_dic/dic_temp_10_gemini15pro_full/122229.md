misworshiper:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːrʃɪpər/
n.
错误崇拜者，误拜者
Someone who worships falsely or improperly; someone who worships a false god or idol.
The misworshiper knelt before the statue of a pagan god.  这个错误崇拜者跪在一个异教神像前。
He denounced the sect as misworshipers, claiming they followed a false prophet. 他谴责该教派为错误崇拜者，声称他们追随的是假先知。
Synonyms: idolater, heretic, infidel
Antonyms: believer, devotee, faithful
noun
