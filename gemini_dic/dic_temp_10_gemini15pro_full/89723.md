hopeless:/ˈhoʊplɪs (US)  /ˈhəʊpləs (UK)/
adjective
绝望的；无望的
Having no hope; giving no cause for hope.
The situation seemed hopeless.  情况似乎毫无希望。
They were in a hopeless situation. 他们处于绝望的境地。

近义词:despairing, desperate, incurable
反义词:hopeful, promising, optimistic
词性:adjective
