nonhedonistic:/ˌnɒnˌhiːdənˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非享乐主义的 | Not motivated by or focused on the pursuit of pleasure; characterized by a lack of emphasis on pleasure or enjoyment. |  She adopted a nonhedonistic lifestyle, prioritizing service to others over personal gratification. 她采取了一种非享乐主义的生活方式，将服务他人置于个人满足之上。|  He lived a simple, nonhedonistic life, finding fulfillment in his work and relationships. 他过着简朴的、非享乐主义的生活，在他的工作和人际关系中找到了满足感。 |  ascetic, austere, self-denying | hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, indulgent | adjective
