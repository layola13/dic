nonrationalism:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
非理性主义
The rejection of rationalism, especially the belief that intuition, instinct, or feeling are more important than reason in decision-making.
Nonrationalism emphasizes the role of emotions and intuition in understanding the world.  非理性主义强调情感和直觉在理解世界中的作用。
The artist's work was a celebration of nonrationalism, embracing the chaotic and the intuitive.  这位艺术家的作品是对非理性主义的颂扬，拥抱混乱和直觉。
近义词:irrationalism, anti-rationalism
反义词:rationalism
词性:noun
