estovers:/ˈɛstəvərz/
n.
必需品;生存必需品;燃料木;木柴;
The right of a tenant to take necessary wood from the landlord's property for fuel, repairs, etc.  Specifically, wood that a tenant is allowed to take from leased property for fuel, repairs, fences, and other agricultural needs.
例句：The tenant had the right of estovers to gather wood for heating.  租户有权收集木柴取暖。

例句：The ancient deed granted estovers, allowing the farmer to take timber for repairing his barn.  古老的契约授予了必需品权，允许农民取木材修理他的谷仓。

近义词: necessities, provisions, firewood, fuel
反义词: luxuries, superfluities
