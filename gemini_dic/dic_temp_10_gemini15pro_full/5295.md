allotrope:/ˈæləˌtroʊp/ (US) /ˈælətrəʊp/ (UK)| n.|同素异形体|One of two or more different physical forms in which an element can exist.  Graphite and diamond are allotropes of carbon.|Diamond and graphite are allotropes of carbon. 钻石和石墨是碳的同素异形体。|synonym: polymorph, isomer|antonym: N/A|noun
