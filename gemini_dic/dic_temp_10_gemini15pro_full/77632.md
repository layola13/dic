gelatification:/dʒɛˌlætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
胶凝化, 胶凝作用
The process of becoming a gel.  The transformation of a sol to a gel.
The gelatification of the agar solution occurred upon cooling.  琼脂溶液冷却后发生胶凝。
gelation, solidification
liquefaction, melting
noun
