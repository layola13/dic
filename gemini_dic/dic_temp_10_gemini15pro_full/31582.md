chasselas:/ʃæsəˈlɑː/ /ʃæsəˈlɑːs/
noun
莎斯拉（一种白葡萄品种）
A white grape variety primarily grown in the Valais region of Switzerland, known for producing dry, aromatic wines with notes of citrus, almond, and honey.
This Chasselas has a lovely floral aroma. 这款莎斯拉葡萄酒有可爱的花香。
近义词:Gutedel (its German name)
反义词:(No direct antonym for a grape variety. Could consider a contrasting variety like Pinot Noir if discussing wine styles.)
词性:n.
