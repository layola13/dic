lynchian:/ˈlɪntʃiən/| adj.|林奇式的，大卫·林奇风格的|Characteristic of the surreal, violent, and often darkly humorous style of filmmaker David Lynch.| The movie had a distinctly Lynchian atmosphere, with dreamlike sequences and unsettling characters. 这部电影有一种明显的林奇式氛围，充满了梦幻般的场景和令人不安的角色。|例句：The unsettling music added to the Lynchian feel of the scene. 令人不安的音乐增加了场景的林奇式感觉。|例句：Her dream was filled with Lynchian imagery, a bizarre mix of the mundane and the terrifying. 她的梦充满了林奇式的意象，一种将世俗和恐怖怪诞地混合在一起的景象。|近义词：surreal, bizarre, eerie, unsettling|反义词：mundane, ordinary, conventional|词性:形容词
