blinder:/ˈblaɪndər/ (US), /ˈblaɪndə/ (UK)| n.|眼罩；障眼物；令人眼花缭乱的事物；<非正式>绝妙的东西，极好的事物|Something that prevents someone from seeing something or understanding the truth; something exceptionally good. |The horse was wearing blinders so it wouldn't be startled by the crowds. 这匹马戴着眼罩，这样它就不会被人群吓到。

blinder:/ˈblaɪndər/ (US), /ˈblaɪndə/ (UK)| vt.|使失明；使眼花缭乱；使思维混乱|To make someone unable to see, either physically or metaphorically. |The bright lights blinded me momentarily.  强光使我一时失明。

His success has blinded him to the struggles of others. 他的成功使他看不到别人的挣扎。|近义词：dazzle, obscure, confuse|反义词：enlighten, clarify|词性:动词
