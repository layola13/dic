expositional:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənəl/
adjective
说明性的，解释性的，阐述的
serving to expound or set forth; explanatory
The first chapter is expositional, laying out the basic concepts of the theory.  第一章是说明性的，阐述了该理论的基本概念。
The lecture was highly expositional, clearly explaining the complex topic.  讲座具有很强的解释性，清晰地解释了这个复杂的话题。
synonyms: explanatory, illustrative, descriptive, elucidative
antonyms:  implicit, implied, suggested, allusive
词性: 形容词
