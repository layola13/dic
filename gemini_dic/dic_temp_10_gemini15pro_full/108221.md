lauders:/ˈlɔːdərz/ | n. | 赞美者；称赞者 | People who express praise or admiration.  Those who applaud or commend someone or something. | The lauders of the new policy believe it will boost the economy. 新政策的赞美者认为它会促进经济发展。 |  The artist's lauders filled the gallery opening. 艺术家的赞美者挤满了画廊的开幕式。 |  admirers, supporters, proponents | critics, detractors | noun

Etymology:  From the verb "laud," which comes from Middle English *lauden*, from Old French *loer*, and ultimately from Latin *laudare* "to praise."
