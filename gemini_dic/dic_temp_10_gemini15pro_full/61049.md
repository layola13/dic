elk:/ɛlk/ | n. | 麋鹿 | A large deer with big antlers, found in North America and eastern Asia. |  We saw a herd of elk grazing in the meadow. 我们看到一群麋鹿在草地上吃草。 | Elk are known for their majestic antlers. 麋鹿因其雄伟的鹿角而闻名。 | 近义词: wapiti, moose (in some contexts) | 反义词: N/A | noun
