encumberingly:/ɪnˈkʌmbərɪŋli/
adverb
沉重地；累赘地
in a way that makes movement or activity difficult or awkward.
The actor moved encumberingly across the stage in the heavy costume.  演员穿着沉重的戏服在舞台上笨拙地移动。
The rules and regulations encumberingly restricted the company's growth.  繁琐的规章制度限制了公司的发展。
近义词:awkwardly, clumsily, cumbersomely
反义词:easily, freely, smoothly
词性:adverb

encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/
verb
阻碍；妨碍；拖累
restrict or impede (someone or something) in such a way that free action or movement is difficult.
She was encumbered by a large, heavy suitcase. 她被一个又大又重的行李箱拖累了。
The company is encumbered with debt.  这家公司负债累累。
近义词:hinder, obstruct, impede
反义词:facilitate, aid, assist
词性:verb

cumber:/ˈkʌmbər/
verb
阻碍；塞满；使混乱
to burden or hinder with difficulties; trouble with something cumbersome or useless
The room was cumbered with old furniture.  房间里堆满了旧家具。
He was cumbered with anxieties.  他焦虑不安。
近义词:burden, overload, clutter
反义词:clear, unburden, empty
词性:verb
