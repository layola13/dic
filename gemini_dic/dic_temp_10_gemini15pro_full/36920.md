colossal:/kəˈlɑːsl/ /kəˈlɒsl/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|Extremely large or great.|例句：The statue was colossal in size. 这座雕像体积庞大。

例句：They suffered a colossal defeat in the election. 他们在选举中惨败。

近义词：enormous, gigantic, huge, immense, massive, vast|反义词：tiny, small, minuscule|词性:形容词

colossus:/kəˈlɑːsəs/ /kəˈlɒsəs/| n.|巨人；巨像；庞然大物|Something extremely large or powerful.|例句：The company is a colossus in the computer industry. 这家公司是计算机行业的巨头。

例句：The dam was a colossus of engineering. 这座大坝是工程学的奇迹。

近义词：giant, behemoth, leviathan|反义词：dwarf, pygmy|词性:名词
