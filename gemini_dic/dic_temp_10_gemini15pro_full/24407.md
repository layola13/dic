briquetted:/brɪˈkɛtɪd/
adjective|成块的，压块的，制成煤砖的|Converted into briquettes; formed into a briquette.|The factory briquetted the coal dust into usable fuel.  工厂把煤屑压制成可用的燃料块。|compacted, compressed, consolidated|loose, scattered
词性:adjective

briquette:/brɪˈkɛt/
noun|煤砖，燃料块|A block of compressed coal dust or other combustible material used for fuel.|We used briquettes to start the fire in the fireplace. 我们用煤砖在壁炉里生火。|block, cake, lump|dust, powder
词性:noun

词源: From French *briquette*, diminutive of *brique* (“brick”), from Italian *bricca*, ultimately from a Germanic source akin to Old High German *brihha* (“fragment, potsherd”).
