grand-slammers:/ˈɡrænd slæmərz/| n.| 大满贯选手；赢得所有四大满贯赛事的人|A grand slammer is someone who has won all four major championships in a particular sport, typically tennis or golf. | Serena Williams is one of the greatest grand slammers of all time.  塞雷娜·威廉姆斯是有史以来最伟大的大满贯选手之一。|Synonyms: champion, winner, victor|Antonyms: loser, runner-up|Noun
grand-slammer:/ˈɡrænd slæmərz/| n.| （棒球）满垒本垒打|In baseball, a grand slam is a home run hit with all three bases loaded, resulting in four runs being scored.|He hit a grand slam in the bottom of the ninth inning to win the game. 他在第九局下半场击出一记满垒本垒打，赢得了比赛。|Synonyms: home run, four-bagger|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
