cereous:/ˈsɪəriəs/ /ˈsiːriəs/ | adj. |蜡状的；蜡质的；苍白的|Resembling wax in appearance, consistency, or color; waxy; pale.|The plant had thick, cereous leaves.  这种植物有厚厚的、蜡质的叶子。|The corpse had a cereous complexion.  这具尸体面色苍白如蜡。|近义词:waxy, waxlike, pale|反义词:ruddy, flushed, healthy|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *cereus*, from *cera* "wax".
