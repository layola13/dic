archaeography:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒɡrəfi/
n.
考古学; 古文书学
The study of ancient documents, especially non-literary texts such as inscriptions, papyri, and wax tablets, and the methods used to decipher and interpret them.
Archaeography helps us understand the daily lives of people in the past. 考古学帮助我们了解过去人们的日常生活。
Archaeography revealed the trade routes of ancient civilizations. 考古学揭示了古代文明的贸易路线。
paleography, epigraphy
