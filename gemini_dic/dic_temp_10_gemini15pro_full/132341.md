nondegenerate:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʒɛnərət/| adj.|非退化的，非简并的|Not degenerate; having a full set of linearly independent eigenvectors (in the context of matrices) or satisfying certain other non-singularity conditions depending on the context.  In physics, it often refers to a system where energy levels are distinct and well-defined.|The matrix is nondegenerate, so it has an inverse.  这个矩阵是非退化的，所以它有逆矩阵。|Near Synonyms: nonsingular, invertible, distinct|Antonyms: degenerate, singular|Adjective
