buttresslike:/ˈbʌtrəsˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像扶壁一样的 | Resembling a buttress, a projecting support built against a wall.  | The cliff face was buttresslike, providing a natural defense. 悬崖面像扶壁一样，提供了天然的防御。 | The massive tree's roots spread out in a buttresslike formation.  这棵大树的树根以扶壁状的形态向外蔓延。 | 近义词：supporting, bolstering, reinforcing | 反义词：weak, unsupported | 词性: adjective


buttress:/ˈbʌtrəs/ | n. vt. | 扶壁；支持；加强 | A projecting support of stone or brick built against a wall; to support or reinforce. | The cathedral walls were reinforced with massive buttresses.  大教堂的墙壁用巨大的扶壁加固。| The government buttressed the economy with financial aid. 政府用财政援助支撑经济。| 近义词：support, prop, reinforce | 反义词：weaken, undermine | 词性: noun, verb

like:/laɪk/ | prep. adj. adv. n. v. | 像；喜欢；相似；例如；可能 | similar to; enjoy; resembling; such as; probably |  He looks like his father. 他看起来像他的父亲。 | I like swimming. 我喜欢游泳。 | 近义词：similar to, such as, enjoy | 反义词：unlike, dislike | 词性: preposition, adjective, adverb, noun, verb

词源: From Middle English butres, from Old French boterez "flying buttress," from bouter "to thrust, push," of Germanic origin.  The suffix "-like" is Old English in origin, meaning "having the characteristics of."
