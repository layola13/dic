homophily:/ˌhɒməˈfɪli/ (US) /ˌhoʊməˈfɪli/ (UK)| n. |同质性，同类相聚|The tendency of individuals to associate and bond with similar others.|Birds of a feather flock together is a good example of homophily in action. 物以类聚，人以群分就是同质性作用的很好例子。

|近义词：association, similarity, likeness, affinity|反义词:heterophily|词性:noun
