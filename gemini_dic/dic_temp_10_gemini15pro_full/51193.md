destigmatize: /diːˈstɪɡmətaɪz/ | vt. | 去污名化；消除耻辱 | To remove the stigma or negative connotations associated with something. |  例句：
Mental health advocates are working to destigmatize mental illness. 
心理健康倡导者正在努力消除精神疾病的污名。

They are trying to destigmatize seeking help for addiction.
他们正在努力消除寻求成瘾帮助的耻辱感。

| 近义词：normalize, legitimize, decriminalize, validate | 反义词：stigmatize, condemn, ostracize | 词性：动词

Etymology:  From de- + stigmatize.  "Stigmatize" comes from the Greek word "stigma" meaning a mark or brand, often used to identify criminals or slaves. The prefix "de-" means to remove or reverse.
