indivision:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪʒən/
n.
不可分割；整体；未分割状态
The state of being undivided; unity.
The property remained in indivision among the heirs.  这处房产仍然由继承人共同持有，未分割。
Near synonyms: unity, entirety, wholeness
Antonyms: division, separation, partition
词性：名词
