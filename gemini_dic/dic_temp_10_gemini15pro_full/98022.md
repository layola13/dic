inoppressive:/ˌɪnəˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. | 不压迫的，不沉重的，不严厉的 | Not oppressive; not burdensome or harsh. | The atmosphere in the meeting was inoppressive, encouraging open discussion. 会议的气氛轻松，鼓励公开讨论。| The new manager's style is inoppressive and collaborative. 新经理的风格不压迫人，而且善于合作。| 近义词: gentle, mild, lenient, tolerant, easygoing | 反义词: oppressive, harsh, severe, tyrannical, burdensome | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "inoppressive" comes from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") added to "oppressive," which derives from the Latin "oppressus," the past participle of "opprimere" (to press against, suppress).
