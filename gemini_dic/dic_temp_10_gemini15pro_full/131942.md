noncontradictory:/ˌnɑːnkɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/ |adj.|无矛盾的，不矛盾的，一致的|Not contradictory; without contradiction; consistent.|The witness's statement was noncontradictory to the evidence presented. 证人的陈述与提出的证据没有矛盾。|His explanations were clear and noncontradictory. 他的解释清晰且没有矛盾。|近义词：consistent, harmonious, compatible, congruent|反义词: contradictory, inconsistent, conflicting, incompatible|词性: 形容词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + contradictory (adjective from contradict, from Latin contra- "against" + dicere "to speak")
