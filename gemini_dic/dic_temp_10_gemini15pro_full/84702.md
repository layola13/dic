handholds:/ˈhændhoʊldz/
n.
扶手，支撑点
(places to put your hands when climbing)  A climber needs secure handholds. 攀岩者需要稳固的支撑点。
The path was steep and there were few handholds. 这条路很陡峭，几乎没有可以抓握的地方。
supports, grips, holds
obstacles, impediments
noun
