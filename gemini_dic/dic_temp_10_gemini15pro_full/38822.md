coneless:/ˈkoʊnˌlɛs/| adj.|无锥体的，没有球果的|Lacking cones, typically referring to plants that would normally bear cones.|The coneless variety of pine tree is unusual.  这种无锥体的松树很不寻常。|近义词:aconical|反义词:coniferous, cone-bearing|词性:形容词

词源:  "cone" + "-less"

"cone" 源自古法语 "cone,"  最终源自拉丁语 "conus,"  意为 "cone" (锥体)。

"-less" 是一个古英语后缀，表示 "without" (没有)。
