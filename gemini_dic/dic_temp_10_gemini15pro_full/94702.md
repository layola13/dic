improgressive:/ɪmˈprəˈɡrɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非进行体的，非持续体的 | Not progressive; not denoting action in progress.  Describes verbs that cannot be used in continuous tenses. |  The verb "know" is improgressive.  你不能说 "I am knowing the answer". |  stative, non-continuous | progressive, continuous | adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I know the answer. = 我知道答案。
She believes in him. = 她相信他。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Incorrect: I am knowing the answer.  (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)
Incorrect: She is believing in him. (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have known the answer for a while. = 我已经知道答案一段时间了。
She has believed in him since they met. = 自从他们见面以来，她就一直相信他。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Incorrect: I have been knowing the answer. (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)
Incorrect: She has been believing in him. (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I knew the answer yesterday. = 我昨天就知道答案了。
She believed him when he told her the story. = 当他给她讲故事时，她相信了他。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Incorrect: I was knowing the answer. (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)
Incorrect: She was believing in him at the time. (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had known the answer before the test. = 在考试之前我就已经知道答案了。
She had believed in him for many years. = 她已经相信他很多年了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Incorrect: I had been knowing the answer. (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)
Incorrect: She had been believing in him all along. (Improgressive verbs cannot be used in continuous tenses.)

The word "improgressive" is rarely used in phrases. It's primarily a grammatical term.  Instead of phrases, here are some examples of verbs that are improgressive:

know = 知道
believe = 相信
understand = 理解
own = 拥有
love = 爱
