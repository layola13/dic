bosberaad:/ˈbɒsbəˌrɑːt/| n.|战略会议，务虚会|A meeting, typically held in a secluded location, for intensive discussion and planning, especially by government or business leaders.  It's an Afrikaans word adopted into English mainly in South Africa.|The party leaders held a bosberaad to discuss their election strategy. 该党领导人举行了一次务虚会，讨论他们的选举策略。|近义词：retreat, conference, summit, think tank|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Afrikaans *bosberaad*, from *bos* ("bush") + *beraad* ("consultation, deliberation").
