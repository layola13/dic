anagoge:/ˌænəˈɡoʊdʒi/ | n. | 神秘的解释，灵意的解释，(尤指圣经的)寓意解经 | A mystical or spiritual interpretation (especially of Scripture), a method of interpretation in which the literal or historical meaning of a text is seen as a symbol of a higher spiritual or moral truth. |  The preacher's anagoge of the story of Jonah emphasized the importance of obedience to God's will.  牧师对约拿故事的灵意解释强调了服从上帝旨意的重要性。

The anagoge of the Exodus story sees it as a metaphor for the soul's journey to God. 出埃及记的灵意解释将其视为灵魂通往上帝之旅的隐喻。 | 近义词: allegory, spiritual interpretation, mystical interpretation | 反义词: literal interpretation | 词性: noun
