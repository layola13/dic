crocein:/ˈkroʊsiɪn/| n. |藏红花素，藏红T，酸性黄73|A water-soluble synthetic yellow dye, C₁₆H₁₀N₂Na₂O₇S₂, used as a biological stain and coloring agent for food.|The scientist used crocein to stain the cells for microscopy. 科学家用藏红花素给细胞染色以便显微镜观察。|Synonyms: Acid Yellow 73, Crocus sativus dye|Antonyms: N/A|noun

crocein:/ˈkroʊsiɪn/| adj. |藏红花色的|Having the color of saffron; deep orange-yellow.|The fabric was dyed a vibrant crocein hue. 这块布料被染成了鲜艳的藏红花色。|Synonyms: saffron-colored, orange-yellow|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
