parotitis:/ˌpærəˈtaɪtɪs/| n. |腮腺炎|Inflammation of a parotid gland, especially, mumps.|例句：The child was diagnosed with parotitis.  这个孩子被诊断出患有腮腺炎。

例句：Parotitis can cause painful swelling in the cheeks. 腮腺炎会导致脸颊疼痛肿胀。|近义词：mumps|反义词: (None, as it's a disease)|词性:noun
