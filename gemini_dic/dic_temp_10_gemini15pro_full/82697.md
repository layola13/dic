guffaws:/ɡəˈfɔːz/ /ɡwɑːˈfɔːz/
| n. |哄笑，狂笑|A loud, unrestrained burst of laughter.|The comedian's joke elicited guffaws from the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话引起了观众的哄堂大笑。|
|Synonyms: roars of laughter, howls of laughter, belly laughs|
|Antonyms:  chuckles, giggles, snickers|
|noun|

guffaw:/ɡəˈfɔː/ /ɡwɑːˈfɔː/
| v. |哄笑，狂笑|To laugh loudly and boisterously.|He guffawed at his own silly joke. 他对自己愚蠢的笑话大笑起来。|
|Synonyms: roar with laughter, howl with laughter|
|Antonyms: chuckle, giggle, snicker|
|verb|
