creatine:/ˈkriːətiːn/ /ˈkriːətɪn/ | n. |肌酸|A nitrogenous organic acid that occurs naturally in vertebrates and helps to supply energy for muscle contraction. |Creatine supplements are popular among athletes. 运动员普遍服用肌酸补充剂。|Synonyms:  phosphocreatine |Antonyms: None |Noun


creatinine:/kriˈætɪniːn/ /kriˈætɪˌnin/ | n. |肌酐|A compound formed in protein metabolism and excreted in the urine.  Elevated levels of creatinine in the blood may indicate impaired kidney function. |Doctors monitor creatinine levels to assess kidney health. 医生监测肌酐水平以评估肾脏健康。|Synonyms: None |Antonyms: None |Noun
