glistens:/ˈɡlɪsənz/| v.|闪烁，闪耀|shines with a soft reflected light.|The dew glistens on the grass. 露珠在草地上闪闪发光。|sparkles, shimmers, shines|dulls, fades|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glisnian, frequentative of glisian "to shine, glisten," from Proto-Germanic *glis-, from Proto-Indo-European *ghel- "to shine, glitter, be yellow or green."
