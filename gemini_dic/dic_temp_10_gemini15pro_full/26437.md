bypath:/ˈbaɪˌpæθ/ | n. | 小路，僻径；间接途径 | A less-traveled path, often a shortcut or a route that avoids the main road; figuratively, an unconventional or indirect way of achieving something. |  We took a bypath through the woods to avoid the traffic on the main road. 我们抄小路穿过树林，避开了主干道上的交通。| synonyms: shortcut, back road, side road, detour, indirect route | antonyms: highway, main road, direct route | noun

bypath:/ˈbaɪˌpæθ/ | n. | 偏差，歧途 | A deviation from a right or correct course of action. | His ambition led him down a bypath of dishonesty. 他的野心使他走上了不诚实的歧途。| synonyms: deviation, digression, diversion | antonyms: straight path, correct path | noun
