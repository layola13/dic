ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *eminence*, from Latin *eminentia* ("a standing out, projection"), from *eminentem* (nominative *eminens*), present participle of *eminēre* ("to stand out, project"), from *ex-* ("out of, from") + *minēre* ("to project, jut out").
