overcapacity:/ˌoʊvər kəˈpæsɪti/| n.|生产过剩，产能过剩|A situation in which the capacity to produce something is greater than the demand for it.|The factory was operating at overcapacity to meet holiday demand. 工厂为了满足节日需求而超负荷运转。|近义词：glut, surplus, excess capacity|反义词:undercapacity, shortage|noun
