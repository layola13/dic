hypersalivation:/ˌhaɪpərˌsælɪˈveɪʃən/
n.
过度流涎，唾液分泌过多
Excessive production of saliva.
The patient experienced hypersalivation as a side effect of the medication.  这个病人因为药物的副作用而流口水过多。
Hypersalivation can be a symptom of certain medical conditions. 过度流涎可能是某些疾病的症状。
近义词:sialorrhea, ptyalism
反义词:xerostomia (口干)
词性:noun

词源:
From hyper- + salivation, ultimately from Latin salivare "to salt, pickle, produce saliva."
