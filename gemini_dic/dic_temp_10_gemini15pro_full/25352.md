PHRASE->
build up:  逐渐增强；积累  He needs to build up his strength after his illness. 他病后需要增强体力。
build on:  基于…；以…为基础  We need to build on our previous successes. 我们需要在以往的成功基础上再接再厉。
build in:  使成为固定装置；使成为组成部分 The oven is built in to the kitchen units. 烤箱是嵌在橱柜里的。
build out:  扩建；扩展  They plan to build out the existing office space. 他们计划扩建现有的办公空间。
build around:  围绕…构建  The entire story is built around a single event. 整个故事都围绕着一个事件展开。
