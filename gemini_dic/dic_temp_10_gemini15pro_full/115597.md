marblelike:/ˈmɑːrblˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像大理石的；坚硬冰冷的；纹理像大理石的 | Resembling marble in appearance, texture, or coldness. | The statue had a marblelike smoothness. 这座雕像有着像大理石一样光滑的表面。| 近义词: marbleous, stony, cold, smooth | 反义词: rough, warm, porous | 词性: 形容词

词源: marble (n.) + like (suffix)

marble: from Old French marbre, from Latin marmor, from Greek marmaros, meaning "crystalline rock, shining stone."
like: from Old English -lic, meaning "having the qualities of, resembling."
