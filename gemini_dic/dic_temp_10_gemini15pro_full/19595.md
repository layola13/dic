bigha:/ˈbiːɡə/ | n. | 比加（印度土地面积单位） | A unit of land area used in India, varying in size from region to region but commonly about one-third of an acre.  | Example 1: His family owns five bighas of land.  他的家族拥有五比加的土地。

Example 2:  The price of a bigha of land has increased significantly. 一比加土地的价格显著上涨了。 | 近义词: acre (英亩), hectare (公顷) (These are approximate equivalents and the exact conversion depends on the local definition of bigha) | 反义词: N/A (Units of measurement don't typically have antonyms.) | 词性: noun
