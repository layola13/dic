devotee:/ˌdɛvəˈtiː/
n.|信徒，爱好者，热心者|A devotee is a person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something.|例句：He is a devotee of classical music. 他是古典音乐的爱好者。

例句：She is a devotee of the yoga. 她是一位瑜伽爱好者。

近义词：follower, admirer, enthusiast, fan, aficionado
反义词：critic, detractor, opponent
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dévot*, from Latin *dēvōtus* "set apart, dedicated," past participle of *dēvovēre* "to vow, dedicate, curse," from *de-* "from" + *vovēre* "to vow."
