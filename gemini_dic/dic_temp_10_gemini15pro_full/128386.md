new:/njuː/ /nuː/ | adj.|新的；新鲜的；最近的；不熟悉的；现代的|Something recently made, discovered, or introduced; not existing before. |例句：I bought a new car. 我买了一辆新车。|近义词：recent, modern, fresh, novel|反义词：old, ancient, used, familiar|词性:形容词

new:/njuː/ /nuː/ | n.|新的东西；新闻|Something recently made, discovered, or introduced; not existing before. |例句：What's the new with you? 你有什么新鲜事？|近义词：innovation, novelty|反义词：old, tradition|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *newe*, from Old English *nīew* (“new, fresh, recent, unusual, strange”), from Proto-Germanic *newjaz* (“new”), from Proto-Indo-European *néwos* (“new”). Cognates include West Frisian *nij*, Dutch *nieuw*, German *neu*, Danish *ny*, Swedish *ny*, Icelandic *nýr*, Latin *novus*, Greek *νέος* (néos), Sanskrit *नव* (náva), and Persian *نو* (now).
