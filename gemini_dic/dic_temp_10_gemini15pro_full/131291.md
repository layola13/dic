noncensoriousnesses:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈsɔːrɪəsnɪsɪz/
n.
不苛求；宽容；不吹毛求疵
lack of censoriousness; tolerance; absence of criticism.
Her noncensoriousness made her a beloved friend and confidante. 她的宽容使她成为一个受人爱戴的朋友和知己。
Synonyms: tolerance, forbearance, leniency, indulgence, acceptance
Antonyms: censoriousness, judgmentalness, criticism, disapproval, condemnation
词性: 名词
