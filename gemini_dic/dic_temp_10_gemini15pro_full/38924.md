confessor:/kənˈfɛsər/| n.|忏悔者；告解者；承认者|A person who confesses their sins to a priest; someone who admits to having done something wrong.|例句：The confessor listened patiently to the penitent. 忏悔者耐心地听着忏悔者的忏悔。

例句：He became a confessor to his crimes. 他承认了自己的罪行。|近义词：penitent, admitter|反义词: denier, liar|词性:noun

confessor:/kənˈfɛsər/| n.|（尤指基督教的）神父，听忏悔的神父|A priest who hears confessions and gives absolution.|例句：The confessor absolved him of his sins. 神父赦免了他的罪。

例句：She went to see her confessor every week. 她每周都去见她的神父。|近义词：priest, minister|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
