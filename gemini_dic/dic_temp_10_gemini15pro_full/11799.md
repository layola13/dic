arranged: /əˈreɪndʒd/ | adj. | 安排好的，整理好的 |  put in a certain order; organized; agreed upon | The flowers were beautifully arranged in a vase.  花被漂亮地插在花瓶里。| 近义词: organized, planned, ordered, settled | 反义词: disorganized, chaotic, unplanned, random | 词性: adjective

词源: From the Old French "arengier," meaning "to put in a row," ultimately from the Frankish "*hring," meaning "circle" or "ring."
