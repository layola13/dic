embellishing: /ɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪŋ/
embellishing: v.
embellishing: 装饰；润色；对…加以渲染
embellishing:  The act of making something more beautiful or interesting by adding details or decorations.  It comes from the Old French word "embellir," meaning "to make beautiful."
embellishing: He was embellishing his story with details that weren't true. 他在故事中添加了一些不真实的细节来润色它。
embellishing:  adorning, decorating, ornamenting, enhancing
embellishing: simplifying, minimizing, understating
embellishing: verb
