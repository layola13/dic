pangolin:/ˈpæŋɡəlɪn/ (US), /ˈpæŋɡəlɪn/ (UK)| n. |穿山甲|A scaly mammal that feeds on ants and termites.|The pangolin is the only mammal wholly covered in scales. 穿山甲是唯一全身覆盖鳞片的哺乳动物。|Pangolins are nocturnal animals. 穿山甲是夜行动物。|近义词:scaly anteater|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Malay *pengguling, literally “one that rolls up” referring to their defense mechanism.
