naturally:/ˈnætʃrəli/ /ˈnætʃərəli/| adv.|自然地，天然地；当然，理所当然地|In a natural manner; as expected.|She naturally assumed that he would help her. 她自然而然地认为他会帮助她。|近义词：normally, instinctively, of course, certainly|反义词：artificially, unnaturally, surprisingly|词性:副词

naturally:/ˈnætʃrəli/ /ˈnætʃərəli/| adj.(古语，罕用)|自然的，天然的|Existing in or caused by nature; not made or caused by humankind.|Her naturally curly hair. 她天生的卷发。|近义词：innate, inherent|反义词：artificial, unnatural|词性:形容词
