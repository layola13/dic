headships:/ˈhɛdʃɪps/| n.|领导地位，首脑职位；领导权|The position or role of being a head, leader, or chief, especially in a family, organization, or community.  It refers to the authority and responsibility associated with that position.|He assumed the headship of the family after his father's death. 他在父亲去世后承担起了家庭的领导责任。|Synonyms: leadership, dominance, authority, control, supremacy|Antonyms: subordination, subservience|noun
