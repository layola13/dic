loverlike:/ˈlʌvərlaɪk/
adjective
像情人的; 充满爱意的; 情人似的
Resembling or characteristic of a lover; affectionate.
She gave him a loverlike look. 她给了他一个充满爱意的眼神。
Near Synonyms: affectionate, loving, amorous, tender, devoted, passionate, ardent
Antonyms: cold, unloving, indifferent, apathetic
词性: 形容词
