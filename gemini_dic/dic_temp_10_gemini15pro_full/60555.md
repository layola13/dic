elapsing:/ɪˈlæpsɪŋ/
verb
流逝，消逝
passing or going by (of time)
Five years elapsed before they met again. 五年过去了，他们才再次相遇。

elapsing:/ɪˈlæpsɪŋ/
adjective
消逝的，流逝的
(of time) passing or going by
With each elapsing day, the situation grew worse. 随着一天天过去，情况变得更糟。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin elapsus, past participle of elabi "to slip away," from e- "out" + labi "to slip, glide."

SYNONYMS->
passing, going by, slipping away, gliding by, expiring

ANTONYMS->
remaining, staying, enduring, persisting
