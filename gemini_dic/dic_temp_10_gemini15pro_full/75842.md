fumy:/ˈfjuːmi/ | adj. | 烟雾弥漫的；有烟味的 | producing or filled with fumes; smelling of fumes | The air was thick and fumy from the fire. 空气因火灾而变得浓烟滚滚，有烟味. |例句：
The basement was dark and fumy. 地下室又黑又有烟味。

The factory emitted a fumy odor. 工厂排放出刺鼻的烟味。

| 近义词：smoky, smoky-flavored | 反义词：clear, fresh | 词性: 形容词
