colleterial:/kɒləˈtɪəriəl/ | adj. | 胶状的；粘性的；由粘液腺分泌的 | Relating to or denoting colleters, which are plant glands that secrete a sticky substance, often covering buds. |  Colleterial secretions protect the developing bud from desiccation and insect attack. 胶状分泌物保护发育中的芽免受干燥和昆虫侵袭。 |  The colleterial glands on the sepals produced a resinous substance. 萼片上的粘液腺产生了一种树脂状物质。 | adhesive, gummy, mucilaginous | dry, non-adhesive | adjective

colleterial:/kɒləˈtɪəriəl/ | n. | (昆蟲)粘腺 | A colleterial gland in insects, especially in certain Hymenoptera. | The colleterial gland secretions are used to glue eggs to surfaces or form protective coatings. 粘腺分泌物用于将卵粘在表面或形成保护层。 | The wasp uses its colleterial to attach its egg to a caterpillar. 黄蜂用它的粘腺把卵粘在毛虫身上。 | gland | N/A | noun
