marxisms:/ˈmɑːrksɪzəmz/
noun
马克思主义（复数）
The different interpretations and applications of Karl Marx's theories.
Many different Marxisms developed in the 20th century.  20世纪发展出许多不同的马克思主义。
synonyms: Marxist doctrines, Marxist philosophies
antonyms: capitalism, liberalism
