decrown:/diːˈkraʊn/
verb
废黜；革除…的王位；剥夺…的冠军称号
to remove a crown from; to depose from royal power; to dethrone.
The king was decrowned and exiled. 国王被废黜并流放了。
synonyms: dethrone, unking, depose, oust
antonyms: crown, enthrone, invest
词性:动词
