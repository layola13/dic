nonhouseholders:/ˈnɑːnˈhaʊshəʊldərz/ (US), /ˈnɒnˈhaʊshəʊldərz/ (UK)| n. |非户主，非家庭户成员|People who do not maintain their own households, but live in group quarters like college dormitories, military barracks, or nursing homes.  They are not considered the head of a household.|The census counted a significant number of nonhouseholders living in the city center.  人口普查显示市中心有大量的非户主居住。|Synonyms: non-heads of household, group quarters residents|Antonyms: householders, heads of household|noun
