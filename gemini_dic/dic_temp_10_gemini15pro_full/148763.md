overturns:/ˌōvərˈtɜːrnz/
verb
推翻；倾覆；翻倒
to cause something to turn upside down or on its side
The car overturned and landed in a ditch. 汽车翻倒在沟里。
近义词: capsizes, upsets, topples, overthrows
反义词: stabilizes, upholds, supports
词性: verb

overturn:/ˌōvərˈtɜːrn/
noun
推翻；倾覆；翻倒
an act of overturning something; the state of being overturned
The overturn of the government was a surprise. 政府的垮台令人意外。
近义词: overthrow, upset, reversal
反义词: stabilization, upholding, support
词性: noun

overturn:/ˌōvərˈtɜːrn/
verb
(law) to reverse a legal decision
The Supreme Court overturned the lower court's ruling. 最高法院推翻了下级法院的裁决。
近义词: reverse, annul, invalidate
反义词: uphold, affirm, sustain
词性: verb


词源: From Middle English *overturnen, from Old English *oferturnian, from ofer- (over) + turnian (to turn).
