frush:/frʌʃ/
adjective
脆的; 易碎的; 酥脆的;
easily broken; brittle.  Similar to friable but implies also a dryness and brittleness.
例句：The frush pastry crumbled as soon as I touched it.  这酥脆的糕点我一碰就碎了。

例句：The leaves were dry and frush after the long, hot summer.  漫长炎热的夏天过后，树叶变得干燥易碎。
近义词：brittle, crumbly, friable, crisp
反义词：tough, pliable, flexible
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a variant of "friable" influenced by words like "crush" and "brush."  No definitive etymology exists, and it's considered a relatively rare and possibly dialectal word.
