menology: /mɪˈnɒlədʒi/ /məˈnɑːlədʒi/
n.
圣人传;  月志;  月历;
A calendar or register of saints' days or other holidays; a martyrology.  A monthly record of events or a calendar.
The church published a menology commemorating the lives of its saints. 教堂出版了一本纪念其圣徒生平的圣人传。
The historical society keeps a detailed menology of important local events.  历史学会保存着一份详细记载当地重要事件的月志。
synonym: calendar, martyrology, register, annals, chronicle
antonym: N/A
noun
