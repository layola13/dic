misrelate:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪt/
verb
错误地联系；不正确地叙述
to relate incorrectly or inappropriately
He misrelated the facts of the case. 他错误地叙述了案件的事实。
misrepresent, misstate
relate correctly, represent accurately
词性:动词
