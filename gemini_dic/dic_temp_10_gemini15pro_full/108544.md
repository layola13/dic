lazies:/ˈleɪziz/
n.
懒虫，懒汉 (通常用复数形式)
People who are unwilling to work or exert themselves.
The lazies were still in bed at noon. 那些懒虫中午还在床上。
近义词: idlers, loafers, slackers
反义词: workers, doers, go-getters
词性: 名词

Etymology: From "lazy" + "-ies" (plural suffix). "Lazy" comes from Middle English *lazi*, from Old English *læsig*.
