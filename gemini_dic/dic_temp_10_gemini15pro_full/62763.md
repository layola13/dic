engraftment:/ɪnˈɡræftmənt/ /ɛnˈɡræftmənt/| n. |移植,植入,嫁接|The process of transplanted tissue becoming established in its new location.  The successful integration of a transplanted organ or tissue into the recipient's body.|The doctor monitored the patient closely for signs of engraftment. 医生密切监测病人是否有移植成功的迹象。|Bone marrow engraftment typically takes several weeks. 骨髓移植通常需要几周时间。|Synonyms: implantation, transplantation, integration|Antonyms: rejection|noun
