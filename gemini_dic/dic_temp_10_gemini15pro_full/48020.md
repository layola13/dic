decisional:/dɪˈsɪʒənəl/
adjective
决策的;关于作决定的
relating to making decisions
The CEO has the decisional authority in the company. 首席执行官拥有公司的决策权。
Her decisional process was very thorough. 她的决策过程非常周密。
近义词: decisive, conclusive
反义词: indecisive
