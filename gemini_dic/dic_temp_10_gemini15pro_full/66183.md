exchange:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/
|vt.| 交换；交流；交易|I exchanged my old car for a new one. 我把我的旧车换了一辆新的。|
|例句：We exchanged gifts at Christmas. 我们在圣诞节交换了礼物。|
|例句：They exchanged phone numbers before parting ways.  他们在分别前交换了电话号码。|
|近义词：trade, swap, barter|
|反义词：retain, keep|
|词性: verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "eschangier" (also "changier"), from Late Latin "excambiare"  "to exchange," from "ex-" (out) + "cambiare" "to barter, exchange."  The Latin "cambiare" is of uncertain origin, possibly from a Celtic source.
