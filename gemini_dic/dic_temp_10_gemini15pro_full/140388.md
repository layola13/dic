oilnut:/ˈɔɪlnʌt/ | n. | 油桐树，油桐果 | A tree (Aleurites fordii) cultivated in eastern Asia for the oil obtained from its seeds, used in varnishes and paints. |  The oilnut tree is native to China. 油桐树原产于中国。

The oilnut is pressed to extract its valuable oil. 油桐果被压榨以提取其珍贵的油。 | 近义词：tung tree, tung oil tree | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
