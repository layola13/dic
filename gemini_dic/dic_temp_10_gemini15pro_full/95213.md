inboards:/ˈɪnbɔːrdz/
n.
内部构件; 内部系统; (尤指船的)舷内发动机
The inner parts or systems, especially the engines, of a boat or ship.
The boat's inboards were recently serviced. 这艘船的舷内发动机最近进行了检修。
近义词:internal components, internal systems, inboard motors
反义词:outboards
词性:名词


inboard:/ˈɪnbɔːrd/
adv.
在船内; 向船内
Toward the center of a ship or boat.
The captain ordered the crew to move inboard. 船长命令船员向船内移动。
近义词:inside, inwards
反义词:outboard
词性:副词


inboard:/ˈɪnbɔːrd/
adj.
(尤指船的发动机)舷内的
Located inside the hull of a ship or boat, especially of an engine.
The yacht has an inboard motor. 这艘游艇有一个舷内发动机。
近义词:internal, inside
反义词:outboard
词性:形容词
