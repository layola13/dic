non-silicate:/ˌnɒnˈsɪlɪkeɪt/ | adj. n. | 非硅酸盐的；非硅酸盐 | Not composed of or containing silicate.  A mineral that does not contain silicon and oxygen as the principal components of its chemical structure. | This rock is composed of non-silicate minerals. 这块岩石由非硅酸盐矿物组成。 | The museum has a large collection of non-silicate minerals.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的非硅酸盐矿物。 | 近义词: non-siliceous | 反义词: silicate | 词性: adjective, noun
