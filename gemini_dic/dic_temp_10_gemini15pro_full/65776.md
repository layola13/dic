everyone's:/ˈevriwʌnz/| determiner|每个人的，大家的|It's a contraction of "everyone is" or "everyone has".  It indicates possession or belonging to every individual in a group. | Everyone's opinion matters.  每个人的意见都很重要。| Everyone's contribution was appreciated. 每個人的貢獻都受到讚賞。|近义词：everybody's|反义词: no one's|词性:determiner (代词性所有格)

注意事项:

"Everyone's" is always treated as singular, even though it refers to multiple people.

While less common, "everyone's" can also be a contraction of "everyone has,"  as in "Everyone's been there."
