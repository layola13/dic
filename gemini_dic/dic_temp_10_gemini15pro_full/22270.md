bonell:/boʊˈnɛl/| adj. n.|博内尔弹簧（床垫的一种），博内尔弹簧结构的|A type of coil spring system used in mattresses, characterized by hourglass-shaped coils connected by helical wires.|This mattress uses a bonell spring system. 这款床垫使用博内尔弹簧系统。|Near synonyms: coil spring, innerspring|Antonym: pocket spring|Noun, Adjective
