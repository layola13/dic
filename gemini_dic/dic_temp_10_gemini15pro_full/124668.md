moveable:/ˈmuːvəbl/ (US), /ˈmuːvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可移动的，活动的|Something that is moveable can be moved from one place to another.|The furniture in this apartment is all moveable. 这套公寓里的家具都是可移动的.|The museum exhibit featured moveable parts that visitors could interact with.  博物馆的展览以可移动的部件为特色，参观者可以与之互动。|近义词：mobile, portable, transportable|反义词：fixed, immovable, stationary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moevable*, from Old French *moevable*, from Late Latin *movabilis*, from Latin *movēre* (to move).
