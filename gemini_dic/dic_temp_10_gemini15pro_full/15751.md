baldies:/ˈbɔːldiz/| n.|秃头的人；秃顶的人|A slang term, often considered insensitive, for people who are bald or have very little hair.  It is the plural of "baldy". |"The baldies in the front row got sunburnt." 前排的秃顶们被晒伤了。|近义词：bald men, baldheaded men|反义词: hairy men|Noun
