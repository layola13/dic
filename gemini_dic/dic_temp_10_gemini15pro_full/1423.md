ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *as (modern French as), which is from Latin as, meaning "a unit, a single spot on dice."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He's an ace tennis player. = 他是一名优秀的网球运动员。
She aces all her exams. = 她所有考试都得满分。

一般过去时:
He aced the history test. = 他的历史考试得了满分。
She served an ace to win the match. = 她发球得分赢得了比赛。

现在完成时:
He has aced every test this semester. = 他这学期每场考试都得了满分。
She has served several aces in this game. = 她在这场比赛中已经发球得了好几分。

过去完成时:
He had already aced the test before I even finished studying. = 我还没读完他就已经考了满分。
She had aced the serve twice before her opponent realized what was happening. = 在她的对手意识到发生了什么之前，她已经两次发球得分了。

将来时:
If he studies hard, he will ace the exam. = 如果他努力学习，考试就能拿满分。
She will ace her serve and win the point. = 她将发球得分并赢得这一分。

虚拟语气:
If I were an ace pilot, I would fly around the world. = 如果我是王牌飞行员，我会环游世界。
I wish I could ace every test. = 我希望每次考试都能得满分。


被动语态:
The test was aced by the entire class. = 全班都考了满分。
The serve was aced by the player. = 球员发球得分了。


疑问句:
Is he an ace pilot? = 他是王牌飞行员吗？
Did she ace the exam? = 她考试得满分了吗？


否定句:
He isn't an ace at math. = 他数学不是很好。
She didn't ace the exam. = 她考试没得满分。
