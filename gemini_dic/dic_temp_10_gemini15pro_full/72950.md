fontanelle:/ˌfɒntəˈnɛl/ (US) /ˌfɑːntəˈnɛl/| n. |囟门|A space between the bones of the skull in an infant or fetus, where ossification is not complete and the sutures not fully formed. |The pediatrician checked the baby's fontanelle during the examination.|Synonyms: soft spot, cranial suture (incompletely formed)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fused skull bones")|Noun
