endermatic:/ˌɛndərˈmætɪk/
adjective
经皮的；透皮的
Relating to or denoting the application of a medicine through the skin, typically by rubbing in an ointment or lotion.
The doctor prescribed an endermatic cream for the patient's rash. 医生给病人开了经皮使用的药膏来治疗皮疹。
Synonyms: transdermal, percutaneous
Antonyms: oral, intravenous
词性: adjective
