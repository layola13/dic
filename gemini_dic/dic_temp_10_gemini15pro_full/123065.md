money-grubbing:/ˈmʌniˌɡrʌbɪŋ/
adjective
贪婪的，唯利是图的
Excessively interested in acquiring money.
例句：He was a money-grubbing landlord who always raised the rent. 他是一个贪婪的房东，总是提高租金。

例句：Her money-grubbing attitude made her unpopular. 她唯利是图的态度让她不受欢迎。

近义词：avaricious, greedy, mercenary, grasping, rapacious
反义词：generous, altruistic, philanthropic
词性：adjective
