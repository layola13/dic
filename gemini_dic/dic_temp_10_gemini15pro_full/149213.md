oxyhemoglobin:/ˌɒksiˈhiːməˌɡləʊbɪn/ /ˌɑːksiˈhiːməˌɡloʊbɪn/ | n. |氧合血红蛋白|A compound formed by the combination of hemoglobin with oxygen, present in arterial blood.|例句：The blood test measured the level of oxyhemoglobin.  血液测试测量了氧合血红蛋白的水平。|例句：Oxyhemoglobin is responsible for the bright red color of arterial blood. 氧合血红蛋白是动脉血液鲜红色的原因。|近义词：oxygenated hemoglobin|反义词：deoxyhemoglobin|词性:noun

Etymology->
oxy- + hemoglobin

oxy-: from Greek oxys "sharp, acid, pungent," also "swift, keen, eager," of unknown origin.

hemoglobin: from hemo- + globin.

hemo-:  from Greek haima (genitive haimatos) "blood"

globin: from globe + -in
