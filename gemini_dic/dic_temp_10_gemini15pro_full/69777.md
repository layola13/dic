fermentability:/fɜːrmɛntəˈbɪləti/ /ˌfɜːrˌmɛntəˈbɪlədi/| n.|可发酵性，发酵能力|The fermentability of a sugar is a key factor in brewing beer.|糖的可发酵性是酿造啤酒的关键因素。|Synonyms: digestibility, decomposability|Antonyms: non-fermentability, indigestibility|noun


Etymology->

From ferment + -ability.  "Ferment" ultimately derives from Latin *fervere "to boil, seethe."
