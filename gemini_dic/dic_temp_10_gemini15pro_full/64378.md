erective:/ɪˈrɛktɪv/| adj.|勃起的，竖起的，使直立的|Characterized by or causing erection, typically of the penis or clitoris; tending to cause erection.  Relating to or involved in erection.|例句：The drug has erective properties. 这种药有勃起功能。|例句：He was experiencing erective dysfunction. 他正在经历勃起功能障碍。|近义词：raised, upright, rigid|反义词:flaccid, limp|词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *ērectīvus, from Latin ērigō (“I raise, set upright”) + -īvus.
