cantest:/kənˈtest/ | n. | 比赛，竞赛；争夺，竞争 | A competition or struggle for supremacy or some prize. |  The two teams held a cantest to see who could raise the most money.  这两个团队举行了一场比赛，看谁筹集的资金最多。 | 近义词: competition, match, contest, tournament | 反义词: cooperation, agreement, harmony | 词性: 名词

cantest:/kənˈtest/ | vt. | 争辩，质疑；辩赛 | To oppose, challenge, or dispute. | The lawyer contested the evidence presented by the prosecution.  律师对控方提出的证据提出质疑。 | 近义词: challenge, dispute, oppose, question | 反义词: accept, concede, agree, admit | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "contester," from Latin "contestari," meaning "to call to witness, invoke as witness," from "com-" (together) and "testari" (to bear witness).  The sense of "to dispute" arose from the idea of calling witnesses to support one's side in a dispute.
