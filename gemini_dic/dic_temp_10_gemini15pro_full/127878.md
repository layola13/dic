neoconservative:/ˌniːoʊkənˈsɜːrvətɪv/
n./adj.|新保守主义者/新保守主义的|A political ideology characterized by an emphasis on free-market capitalism, a assertive foreign policy, and a more traditional approach to social issues.  It emerged in the United States in the 1970s, primarily among liberal intellectuals who became disillusioned with the perceived excesses of the 1960s counterculture and the leftward drift of the Democratic Party.|
He's considered a neoconservative. 他被认为是新保守主义者。
The article presented a neoconservative view of foreign policy. 这篇文章提出了新保守主义的对外政策观点。
|近义词：hawk, right-winger|反义词：liberal, dove|词性：名词/形容词
