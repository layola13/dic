eve-teasing:/ˈiːvˌtiːzɪŋ/
|n.|调戏，挑逗（尤指对女性的骚扰）|The act of making unwanted sexual remarks or advances towards women in public places.|Eve-teasing is a serious problem in some societies. 在一些社会中，调戏女性是一个严重的问题。|
|例句：The police are trying to crack down on eve-teasing. 警方正试图打击调戏妇女的行为。|
|例句：She was subjected to constant eve-teasing on her way to work.  她在上班路上经常受到骚扰。|
|近义词：sexual harassment, street harassment, catcalling|
|反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider) respectful behavior, appropriate conduct|
|词性：noun|
