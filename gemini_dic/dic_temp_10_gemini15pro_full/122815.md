molebut: /ˈmoʊlˌbʌt/ | n. |鼴鼠的臀部;  比喻微不足道的东西;  |A mole's buttocks. Figuratively used to refer to something insignificant or of little value.  | The reward for finding my lost key was a molebut – practically nothing! 找到我丢失的钥匙的奖励简直就是鼹鼠的屁股——几乎什么也没有！| 近义词: trifle, nothing, peanuts | 反义词: fortune, a lot | 词性: 名词

词源:  This word is a humorous and somewhat vulgar colloquialism. It combines "mole," referring to the small burrowing mammal, with "but," meaning buttocks.  It plays on the small size of a mole's rear end to emphasize insignificance.
