overpersecuting:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrsɪˌkjuːtɪŋ/
|v.|过度迫害|The act of persecuting someone excessively or beyond reasonable limits.|The government was accused of overpersecuting the minority group for their religious beliefs. 政府被指控过度迫害少数族裔的宗教信仰。|overzealously prosecuting, harassing relentlessly|tolerating, protecting|verb


overpersecuting:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrsɪˌkjuːtɪŋ/
|adj.|过度迫害的|Characterized by excessive or unreasonable persecution.|The overpersecuting regime eventually fell from power.  这个过度迫害的政权最终垮台了。|overzealous, tyrannical|lenient, tolerant|adjective


Etymology:

"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "persecute" (from Latin persecutus, past participle of persequi "to follow persistently, pursue; to hunt down, prosecute," from per- "thoroughly" + sequi "to follow") + "-ing" (present participle suffix).
