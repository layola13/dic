biometry:/baɪˈɒmɪtri/ | n. | 生物统计学；生物测量学 | The study of measurement and statistical analysis of biological data, such as height, weight, and lifespan.  It encompasses the development and application of statistical methods to solve problems in biology, especially in genetics, ecology, and medicine. |  Biometry is used in various fields like genetics and ecology. 生物统计学应用于遗传学和生态学等多个领域。

Biometry plays a crucial role in modern drug development. 生物统计学在现代药物研发中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: biostatistics, biometrics | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  bio- (life) + -metry (measurement) from Greek "bios" (life) and "metron" (measure).
