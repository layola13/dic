overcoached:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtʃt/
adjective
过度训练的；被过度指导的
Having been coached excessively, often to the point of detriment.  The athlete's performance declined because they were overcoached.  运动员的表现下降是因为他们被过度指导了。
例句：The team was overcoached and burned out by the end of the season.  这个团队在赛季结束时被过度训练，筋疲力尽。

例句：The young pianist felt overcoached and lost her passion for music.  这位年轻的钢琴家感到被过度指导，失去了对音乐的热情。
近义词：overtrained, overmanaged
反义词：undertrained, undercoached
词性: adjective
