parked:/pɑːrkt/ | adj. | 停放的 |  (of a vehicle) left standing in a particular place.  | The car was parked outside the house. 汽车停在房子外面。 |  stationary, immobile | moving, in motion | adjective

parked:/pɑːrkt/ | v. | 停放（车辆） |  past tense and past participle of “park” - to maneuver (a vehicle) into a parking space or other designated area. | I parked the car in the garage. 我把车停在车库里。 | leave, position | retrieve, move | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "park" (verb), from Middle English "parken," from Old North French "parquer" (to enclose, impound). Ultimately from a Germanic source related to Old High German "pfarrih" (parish). The original meaning referred to enclosing animals or setting up a camp.
