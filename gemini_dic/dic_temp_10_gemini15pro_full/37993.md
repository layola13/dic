complicit:/kəmˈplɪsɪt/ | adj. | 有同谋关系的，串通的 | Involved with others in an illegal activity or wrongdoing. |  She was complicit in the robbery. 她参与了抢劫。 |  involved, implicated, accessory | innocent, uninvolved | adjective

complicit:/kəmˈplɪsɪt/ | adj. | 默许的，纵容的 | Implicitly involved in or associated with someone or something bad. | He remained silent, appearing complicit in their deception. 他保持沉默，似乎默许了他们的欺骗行为。 |  conniving, colluding, acquiescent | objecting, opposing | adjective
