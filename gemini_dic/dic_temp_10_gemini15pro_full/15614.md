bailiff: /ˈbeɪlɪf/ | n. | 法警；执行官；区镇治安官 | A bailiff is an officer of the court who is responsible for enforcing court orders, such as serving legal documents, evicting tenants, and seizing property. | The bailiff served the eviction notice on the tenant. 法警向房客送达了驱逐通知。

The bailiff seized the debtor's car. 法警扣押了债务人的汽车。

Synonym: officer, sheriff, marshal | Antonym: defendant, plaintiff | noun
