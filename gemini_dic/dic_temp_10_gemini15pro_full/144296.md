outspins:/ˌaʊtˈspɪnz/
verb
胜过，以旋转胜过
surpasses or defeats someone by spinning faster or more effectively.
The new model of the turbine outspins the older one, generating more energy.  这个新型涡轮机比旧的转得更快，产生的能量也更多。
The figure skater outspun her rival and won the gold medal.  这位花样滑冰运动员旋转胜过她的对手，赢得了金牌。
近义词:outperforms, surpasses, beats, defeats
反义词:loses to, is outdone by
词性:v.
