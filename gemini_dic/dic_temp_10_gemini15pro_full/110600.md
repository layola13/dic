limn:/lɪm/
verb
描绘，绘画（尤指景色或人物）
to represent or describe something in words or pictures, especially in a detailed and attractive way
例句：The artist limned the dancer's graceful movements. 艺术家描绘了舞者优雅的动作。

例句：The novel limns a vivid picture of life in the 19th century. 这部小说生动地描绘了19世纪的生活。
近义词：depict, portray, describe, paint, sketch
反义词：obscure, confuse
词性：v.
