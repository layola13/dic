bimo:/ˈbaɪmoʊ/| n.|双月刊；两月一次的活动|A publication issued every two months; an event occurring every two months.|The club publishes a bimo with news and updates for its members.  俱乐部每两个月出版一份双月刊，其中包含会员的新闻和最新信息。
The bimo meeting is scheduled for the first Monday of every other month.  双月会议定于每隔一个月的第一个星期一举行。|近义词:bi-monthly publication, bi-monthly event|反义词:N/A|名词
