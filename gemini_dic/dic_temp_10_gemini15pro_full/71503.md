ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *flanc, from Frankish *hlanka, from Proto-Germanic *hlankō ("haunch, flank"), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)leng- ("to bend, turn"). Cognate with Old High German hlanca ("flank"), Old English hlanc ("slender").
