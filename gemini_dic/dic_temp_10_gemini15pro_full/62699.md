engineer:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/ | n.  |工程师；设计师；机师；轮机员；策划者|A person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or public works.|例句：He is a talented engineer. 他是一位才华横溢的工程师。|例句：She's an engineer for a large construction company.  她是一家大型建筑公司的工程师。|近义词：designer, architect, constructor, mechanic, planner|反义词: N/A | noun

engineer:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/ | vt. |设计；建造；策划；安排；操纵|To design and build (a machine or structure).
