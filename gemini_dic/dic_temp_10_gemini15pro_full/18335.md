bellied:/ˈbelɪd/| adj.|大肚子的，鼓起的|Having a belly of a specified kind.  Characterized by having a prominent belly.|例句：A pot-bellied pig. 一只大肚猪。|例句：The big-bellied stove glowed warmly. 大肚子的火炉发出温暖的光芒。|近义词：protuberant, bulging|反义词：flat, concave|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'belly' + '-ed'. 'Belly' comes from Old English 'belg', meaning "bag, purse, bellows," related to Old Norse 'belgr' "bag, skin."  The '-ed' suffix is used to form adjectives indicating possession or having the characteristic of the root word.
