antimorality:/ˌæntiˌmɔːˈræləti/
n.
反道德；非道德
Opposition to or rejection of traditional moral principles or beliefs.
He argued that the book promoted antimorality and encouraged deviant behavior.  他认为这本书宣扬反道德，鼓励越轨行为。
Synonyms: immorality, wickedness, depravity
Antonyms: morality, virtue, righteousness
noun
