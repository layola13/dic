butcherly:/ˈbʊtʃərli/
adjective
残忍的；屠夫似的；笨拙的
Resembling or characteristic of a butcher; cruel and bloody; clumsily performed.
The dictator's butcherly regime was responsible for countless deaths.  独裁者的残暴统治导致了无数人的死亡。
The surgery was performed in a butcherly manner, leaving a large, unsightly scar.  手术做得非常粗糙，留下了一个又大又难看的疤痕。

反义词: gentle, kind, humane, skillful, deft
词性: adjective
