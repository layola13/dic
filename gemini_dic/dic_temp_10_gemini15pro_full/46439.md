dagon:/ˈdeɪɡɒn/ | n. | 达贡（非利士人的鱼神）| A Philistine god, often represented as half man, half fish. |  The temple of Dagon was destroyed when Samson pushed down its pillars.  参孙推倒了达贡神庙的柱子，摧毁了它。 | synonyms: deity, god, idol | antonyms:  None (as it's a proper noun) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hebrew דָּגוֹן (dāgōn), related to Hebrew דָּג (dag, “fish”).  Ultimately of uncertain origin. Some speculate a connection to the Akkadian word *daganu, meaning "grain," suggesting a connection to fertility.
