cultch:/kʌltʃ/
n.|贝壳碎片；牡蛎壳；育珠蚌的壳；（牡蛎等的）养殖场|Oyster shells or other material used to establish a bed for oyster propagation.  A place where oysters are bred.|The oyster farmer scattered cultch on the seabed to encourage the growth of new oysters. 牡蛎养殖者将贝壳碎片撒在海床上以促进新牡蛎的生长。|Synonyms: oyster bed, shellfish bed|Antonyms: None|Noun
