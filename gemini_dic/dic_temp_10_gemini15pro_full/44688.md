crudded:/ˈkrʌdɪd/
| adj. | 覆盖着污垢的；脏的；粗糙的；未加工的 | Covered with or containing crud; dirty; rough; unrefined. | The table was crudded with the remnants of dinner. 桌子上沾满了晚餐的残渣。|The engine was crudded with oil and grease.  发动机上满是油污。| 近义词:dirty, filthy, grimy, soiled | 反义词:clean, pristine, immaculate | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From crud + -ed.  "Crud" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "curd."
