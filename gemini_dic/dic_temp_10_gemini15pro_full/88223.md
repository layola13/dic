hindermost:/ˈhɪndərməʊst/ (UK) /ˈhaɪndərməʊst/ (US)| adj.|最后面的，最后方的|Furthest back or last in order. |例句：The hindermost car in the procession was decorated with flowers.  队伍中最后面的那辆车装饰着鲜花。

例句：He was always the hindermost in the race. 他总是在比赛中跑最后。

近义词：rearmost, last, hindmost|反义词：foremost, first, leading|词性：形容词
