hypercolour:/ˈhaɪpərˌkʌlər/ (US), /ˈhaɪpəˌkʌlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|超彩色的，热变色的|A type of fabric that changes colour with temperature.  The term "Hypercolor" was a trademark of Generra Sportswear, but is often used generically.|例句：I had a hypercolour t-shirt in the 90s that changed from blue to pink. 我90年代有一件超彩色的T恤，可以从蓝色变成粉色。

例句：The hypercolour trend was short-lived but memorable. 超彩色潮流虽然短暂，但却令人难忘。|近义词：thermochromic, colour-changing|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
