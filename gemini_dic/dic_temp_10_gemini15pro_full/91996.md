hypernym:/ˈhaɪpərˌnɪm/| n.|上位词，上义词|A word that is more general than another word.  A hypernym encompasses a range of hyponyms (more specific words). For example, "animal" is a hypernym of "dog", "cat", and "bird".|例句： "Animal" is a hypernym of "dog."  “动物”是“狗”的上位词。

例句："Fruit" is a hypernym of "apple", "orange", and "banana". “水果”是“苹果”、“橙子”和“香蕉”的上位词。|近义词：superordinate|反义词:hyponym, subordinate|词性:noun
