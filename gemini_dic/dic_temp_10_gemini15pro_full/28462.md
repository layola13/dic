carburization:/ˌkɑːrbjʊrɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
渗碳；碳化；增碳
The process of increasing the carbon content of the surface of steel by exposing it to a carbonaceous atmosphere at elevated temperatures.
The carburization process hardened the surface of the steel. 渗碳工艺使钢的表面硬化。
cementation, carbonization
decarburization
noun
