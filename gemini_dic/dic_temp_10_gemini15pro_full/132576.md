nondieting's:/ˌnɒnˈdaɪətɪŋz/ |n.| 非节食的（状态、行为或原则）|  Relating to or characteristic of a lifestyle that doesn't involve dieting.  It usually refers to an approach focused on intuitive eating and body acceptance rather than restrictive food plans. | Example: Nondieting's focus on self-care is refreshing. 非节食关注自我保健令人耳目一新。| Example:  The book explored the principles of nondieting's philosophy. 这本书探讨了非节食哲学的原则。 | Synonyms:  anti-dieting, intuitive eating, mindful eating,  | Antonyms:  dieting, restrictive eating |noun
