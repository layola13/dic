butyryl:/ˈbjuːtɪrɪl/| adj. n.|丁酰基，丁酰|A univalent radical, C4H7O, derived from butyric acid.|Butyryl chloride is a highly reactive compound. 丁酰氯是一种高活性化合物。|近义词:butanoyl|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From butyric (from Latin butyrum "butter") + -yl (chemical suffix denoting a univalent radical).
