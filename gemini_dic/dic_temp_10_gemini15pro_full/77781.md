generalises:/ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪzɪz/
verb
概括，归纳，推广
Forms a general statement or concept from specific instances; infers a general conclusion from particular facts.
This study generalises the findings to a wider population. 这项研究将结果推广到更广泛的人群。
The author generalises about the impact of social media on teenagers. 作者概括了社交媒体对青少年的影响。
generalize, extrapolate, broaden, expand, infer
specify, particularize, restrict, limit
词性:动词
