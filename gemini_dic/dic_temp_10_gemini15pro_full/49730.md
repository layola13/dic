democratizers:/dɪˈmɒkrəˌtaɪzərz/
noun
民主化者，推动民主的人或事物
People or things that promote or cause democratization.
The internet has been hailed as one of the great democratizers of information.  互联网被誉为信息 democratization 的伟大推动者之一。
The fall of the Berlin Wall was seen by many as a victory for democratizers around the world. 柏林墙的倒塌被许多人视为全世界民主化者的胜利。
synonyms: reformers, liberals, advocates, promoters
antonyms: authoritarians, dictators, oppressors
