panstereorama: /ˌpænˌstɛrɪəˈrɑːmə/ | n. | 全景立体图; 全景画 | A panoramic image or representation, often enhanced to create a three-dimensional effect. It can be considered a precursor to virtual reality. |  They created a panstereorama of the battlefield to give viewers a sense of being there. 他们创作了一幅战场的全景立体图，让观众有身临其境的感觉。 | synonym: panorama, diorama, cyclorama | antonym: close-up | noun

etymology:  "pan" (Greek: all) + "stereo" (Greek: solid) + "orama" (Greek: sight)
