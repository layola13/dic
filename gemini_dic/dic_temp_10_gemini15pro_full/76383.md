gadzookery:/ˌɡædˈzʊkəri/
noun
古怪；滑稽古怪的行为；奇特的玩意儿
A whimsical affectation of archaic language or style; outlandish or bizarre behavior or attire.
例句：His gadzookery and flamboyant attire made him the center of attention. 他的古怪行为和华丽的服装使他成为众人瞩目的焦点。

例句：The play was full of gadzookery, with characters speaking in rhyming couplets and wearing outlandish costumes.  这出戏充满了古怪滑稽，角色们用押韵的对句说话，穿着奇装异服。

近义词：eccentricity, quirkiness, oddity, peculiarity
反义词：normality, conventionality
词性:n.
