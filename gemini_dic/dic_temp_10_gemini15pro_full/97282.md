influentials:/ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəlz/
n.|有影响力的人（复数）|People who have significant influence, especially in business, politics, or media. |Marketing campaigns often target influentials to reach a wider audience. 营销活动通常以有影响力的人为目标，以覆盖更广泛的受众。|近义词：leaders, opinion-makers, trendsetters|反义词：followers, ordinary people|词性：名词

influential:/ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl/
adj.|有影响力的|Having or exerting influence. |He is an influential figure in the art world. 他是艺术界举足轻重的人物。|近义词：powerful, important, significant|反义词：insignificant, powerless|词性：形容词
