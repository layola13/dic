marabou:/ˈmærəbuː/| n.|秃鹳，鹳毛|A large African stork with a naked head and neck and a large pouch.  A large wading bird of the stork family, native to Africa. Its soft tail feathers are used for trimming garments and hats.|The milliner adorned the hat with marabou feathers.|近义词:stork, adjutant|反义词:|noun
