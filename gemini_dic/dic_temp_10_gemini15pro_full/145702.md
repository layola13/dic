overcounting:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊntɪŋ/ | n. | 过度计数，重复计数 | The act of counting something more than once, leading to an inflated total. |  We avoided overcounting by carefully checking off each item as it was tallied. 我们通过仔细核对每一项来避免重复计数。| Overcounting led to an inaccurate inventory. 重复计数导致库存不准确。 | double counting, multiple counting | undercounting | noun
