manurially:/məˈnjʊəriəli/
adverb
用肥料地;以施肥方式
in a way that involves using manure as fertilizer.
The field was manurially treated to enhance soil fertility. 这块田地用肥料处理以增强土壤肥力。
Synonyms:  with manure, using fertilizer, by fertilization
Antonyms: chemically, synthetically
词性: 副词
