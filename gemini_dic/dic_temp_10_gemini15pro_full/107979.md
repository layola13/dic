lash:/læʃ/| n. vt. vi.| 睫毛; 鞭打; 讽刺; 猛击; 捆扎; 冲击; 挥动; 迅速摆动;|A swift stroke, as with a whip or the like; a sudden brief spell of vigorous activity.
