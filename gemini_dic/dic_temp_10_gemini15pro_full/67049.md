"an expletory outburst"

例句：Her expletory reaction showed her true feelings about the situation. 她愤怒的反应显示了她对这种情况的真实感受。

近义词：curse, swear, cuss, profane, blasphemous
反义词：polite, respectful, reverent
词性 adjective
