despondency:/dɪˈspɒndənsi/ /dɪˈspɑːndənsi/| n.|沮丧，绝望，意气消沉|A state of low spirits caused by loss of hope or courage.|例句：He was plunged into despondency after failing the exam.  他考试不及格后陷入了沮丧。

例句：Her despondency was evident in her slumped posture and tear-stained face. 她的沮丧从她颓丧的姿势和泪痕斑斑的脸上显而易见。|近义词：dejection, despair, hopelessness, gloom, melancholy|反义词：hope, optimism, cheerfulness, joy|词性:名词
