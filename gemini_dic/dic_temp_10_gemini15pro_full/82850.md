词源: From Persian گلستان (golestân, “rose garden, flower garden”), from گل (gol, “flower, rose”) + -ستان (-estân, location suffix).


注意事项:  The word "gulist" is not a common English word.  It's a loanword from Persian and is used primarily in specialized contexts related to gardens and horticulture. You are more likely to encounter "rose garden" in everyday English.
