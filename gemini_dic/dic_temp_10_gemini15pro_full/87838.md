hiddenite:/ˈhɪdnɪtaɪt/
n.
翠绿色的锂辉石, 隐藏石
A transparent, emerald-green variety of spodumene, used as a gemstone.
Hiddenite is a rare and beautiful gemstone. 隐藏石是一种稀有而美丽的宝石。
This ring features a stunning hiddenite stone. 这枚戒指上镶嵌着一颗令人惊艳的隐藏石。
近义词:spodumene, emerald
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after William Earl Hidden (1853-1918), American mineralogist, who discovered it in North Carolina, USA in 1879.
