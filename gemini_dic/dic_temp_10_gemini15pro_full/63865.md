epigraphy:/ɪˈpɪɡrəfi/ | n. | 碑文研究；铭文研究；碑铭学 | The study of inscriptions, especially those on stone or metal, as a means of understanding past cultures and events. |  Example 1: The epigraphy of the ancient tomb revealed the pharaoh's name and lineage. 古墓的碑文研究揭示了法老的名字和血统。

Example 2:  Modern epigraphy relies on advanced techniques like 3D scanning to document inscriptions accurately. 现代碑文研究依靠诸如3D扫描之类的先进技术来准确记录铭文。 | 近义词: inscription study, paleography | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
