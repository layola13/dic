consoled:/kənˈsoʊld/
verb
安慰; 慰藉
past tense and past participle of console
He consoled her after her father's death.  在她父亲去世后，他安慰了她。
She was consoled by the kind words of her friends. 朋友们友善的话语安慰了她。

comfort, soothe, solace
distress, upset, grieve
v.
