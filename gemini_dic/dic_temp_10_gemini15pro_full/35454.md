co-workers:/ˈkoʊˌwɜːrkərz/ (US), /ˈkəʊˌwɜːkəz/ (UK)| n. |同事|People you work with, especially those at a similar level.|

例句：
My co-workers and I often go out for lunch together.
我和我的同事经常一起出去吃午饭。

I have a great relationship with my co-workers.
我和我的同事关系很好。

近义词：colleagues, associates, peers|
反义词: boss, supervisor, manager |
词性: noun
