foraminated:/ˌfɒrˈæmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
有孔的，穿孔的
pierced with holes; perforated
The foraminated shell allowed water to flow through.  这个有孔的贝壳可以让水流过。
The sponge is foraminated to increase its absorbency.  海绵是穿孔的，以增加其吸收性。
近义词: perforated, porous, pierced
反义词: solid, impermeable, nonporous
词性: adjective

foraminate:/fəˈræmɪneɪt/
verb
穿孔，使成多孔
to pierce with a hole or holes; to perforate
The worker foraminated the metal sheet for ventilation.  工人给金属板穿孔以便通风。
They foraminated the leather to create a decorative pattern.  他们在皮革上穿孔，以创造出一种装饰图案。
近义词: perforate, pierce, puncture
反义词: seal, close, block
词性: verb

词源:  From Latin *foraminatus, past participle of *foraminare ‘to pierce, make a hole,’ from foramen ‘hole, opening.’
