panetto:/pæˈnɛtoʊ/
noun
小块（尤指食物，如奶酪、黄油等）; a small block of something, especially food such as cheese or butter.
The chef carefully grated the panetto of Parmesan.  厨师小心地磨碎了那小块帕玛森干酪。
近义词:piece, block, chunk, wedge
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "whole" or "large quantity")
词性:n.


panetto:/pæˈnɛtoʊ/
noun
(意大利语) 一小块（通常指面包或面团）;(Italian) a small loaf or portion of dough, typically for bread.
The baker shaped the panetto before letting it rise. 面包师在让它发酵之前先把小面团揉成形。
近义词:loaf, portion, roll
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "large loaf" or "bulk dough")
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *panetto*, diminutive of *pane* ("bread").  来自意大利语 *panetto*，是 *pane*（“面包”）的小称。
