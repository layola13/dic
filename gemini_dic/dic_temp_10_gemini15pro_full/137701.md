nonusuriously:/nɒnjuːˈʒʊəriəsli/
adv.
不放高利贷地; 非高利贷地
without charging excessive or exorbitant interest; in a manner that does not involve usury.
He lent the money nonusuriously, asking only for the principal back. 他没有放高利贷，只要求归还本金。
近义词: fairly, justly, reasonably, ethically
反义词: usuriously
词性: 副词
