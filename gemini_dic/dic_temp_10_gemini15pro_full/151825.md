passer-by:/ˈpɑːsəˈbaɪ/ (US) /ˈpɑːsəˈbaɪ/ (UK)| n. |过路人；路人|A person who happens to be going past a place, especially one who is on foot.|例句：A passer-by saw the accident and called the police. 一个过路人看到了事故并报了警。|例句：The passer-by offered to help the lost child. 路人主动帮助迷路的孩子。|近义词：pedestrian, onlooker, bystander|反义词:resident, inhabitant|noun
