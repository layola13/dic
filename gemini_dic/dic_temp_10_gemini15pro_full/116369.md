mastich: /ˈmæstɪk/ (US), /ˈmæstɪk/ (UK)| n.|乳香，胶，油灰，乳香树|A resin obtained from the mastic tree, used in varnishes, adhesives, and chewing gum.  Also, a putty-like sealant.|The painter used mastic to seal the window. 画家用油灰封住窗户。|Synonyms: resin, gum, sealant, putty|Antonyms: solvent (for some meanings)|noun

mastich: /ˈmæstɪk/ (US), /ˈmæstɪk/ (UK)| adj.|乳香树的|Made of or containing mastic.|Mastic trees are native to the Mediterranean region. 乳香树原产于地中海地区。|Synonyms: resinous, gummy|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
