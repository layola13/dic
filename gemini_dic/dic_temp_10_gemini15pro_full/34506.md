clean-shaven:/ˌkliːnˈʃeɪvən/ | adj. | 剃干净胡须的，刮过脸的 | Having the beard or stubble shaved off smoothly. | He looked younger, clean-shaven and alert. 他看起来更年轻了，胡子刮得干干净净，而且很机灵。| 近义词: smooth-shaven, beardless | 反义词: bearded, unshaven | 词性: 形容词

词源: clean + shaven (shave的过去分词).  "Clean" here means completely, thoroughly. "Shaven" is the past participle of the verb "to shave," meaning to remove hair with a razor.
