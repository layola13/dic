graphics: /ˈɡræfɪks/ | n. | 图形，图解，制图法;  (尤指) 计算机图形学 |  The use of diagrams, photographs, etc. in books, magazines, etc. to illustrate or decorate them.  Especially:  The images that are shown on a computer screen and the way they are created. | The graphics in this video game are amazing.  这款电子游戏中的画面令人惊叹。 | synonyms: visuals, imagery, illustrations | antonyms: text, words | noun

graphics: /ˈɡræfɪks/ | n. | 图形，图表 |  A diagram or other visual representation of information, especially statistical information.  |  The graphics clearly show the increase in sales. 图表清楚地显示了销售额的增长。| synonyms: charts, diagrams, figures | antonyms:  narration, description | noun
