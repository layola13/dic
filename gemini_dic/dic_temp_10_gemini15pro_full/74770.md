freeze-framed:/ˈfriːz freɪmd/ | adj. | 冻结帧的，定格的 |  Appearing as if frozen in a single frame, like a photograph.  Describes something that seems to be paused or stopped in time. | The movie freeze-framed on the actor's shocked expression.  这部电影定格在演员震惊的表情上。| The photograph captured a freeze-framed moment of joy.  这张照片捕捉到了一个定格的喜悦瞬间。| 近义词：frozen, static, still, motionless | 反义词：moving, dynamic, fluid | 词性：形容词

freeze-frame:/ˈfriːz freɪm/ | n. | 冻结帧，定格画面 | A single frame of a film or video, or a photograph that captures a moment in time. | The director used a freeze-frame to emphasize the character's sadness. 导演用一个定格画面来强调角色的悲伤。 | The freeze-frame of the winning goal was shown repeatedly on the news.  新闻反复播放了制胜球的定格画面。 | 近义词：still, snapshot, still image | 反义词：video, film, animation | 词性：名词

freeze-frame:/ˈfriːz freɪm/ | v. |  (使)定格，(使)冻结画面 | To pause a film or video on a single frame. |  He freeze-framed the video to examine the suspect's face. 他暂停了视频以检查嫌疑人的脸。 | The software allows you to freeze-frame any part of the recording.  该软件允许您冻结录制中的任何部分。| 近义词：pause, stop, hold | 反义词：play, continue, resume | 词性：动词
