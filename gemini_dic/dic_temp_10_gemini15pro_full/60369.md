egrimony:/ˈɛɡrɪməni/| n. |悲痛，悔恨，懊悔|A feeling of bitter regret or remorse.|例句：He felt deep egrimony for his past mistakes. 他对过去的错误感到深深的悔恨。

例句：She expressed egrimony for the pain she had caused. 她对造成的痛苦表示悔恨。|近义词：remorse, regret, penitence, contrition|反义词：joy, happiness, satisfaction, complacency|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *egrimonie, from Late Latin *aegrimonia, from Latin aeger ("sick, ill").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *āeghs- ("pain, disease").
