circumforaneous:/ˌsɜːrkəmˌfɒrˈeɪniəs/
adjective
巡回的；流动的
traveling from place to place, especially working or based in various places for relatively short periods.
The circumforaneous preacher moved from town to town spreading his message.  这位巡回传教士辗转于各个城镇传播他的信息。
The circus was a circumforaneous entertainment, delighting audiences in one town before moving on to the next.  这个马戏团是一个流动的娱乐节目，在一个城镇让观众高兴之后，又转移到下一个城镇。
近义词: itinerant, traveling, peripatetic, nomadic
反义词: settled, stationary, resident, fixed, local
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin circum "around" + foraneus "outside," from foris "out of doors."
