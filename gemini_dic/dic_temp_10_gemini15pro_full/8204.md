anti-elite:/ˌæntiɪˈliːt/| adj. n.| 反精英的，反精英人士 | Opposed to or hostile towards elites, especially those seen as wielding excessive power or influence. |  The candidate's anti-elite rhetoric resonated with many voters.  这位候选人的反精英言论引起了许多选民的共鸣。|  The anti-elite movement is gaining traction. 反精英运动正在 gaining traction.| 近义词：populist, egalitarian | 反义词：elitist, aristocratic | 词性: 形容词, 名词

词源:
"anti-" (Greek origin) meaning "against" + "elite" (French origin, from "élire" meaning "to choose")
