almeh:/ˈɑːlmeɪ/ /ˈælmeɪ/
|n.|歌女，舞女|A professional female entertainer in Egypt and other Arab countries, trained to sing and dance for male audiences. |She was a celebrated almeh, known for her beauty and her voice. 她是一位著名的歌女，以她的美貌和嗓音而闻名。|Synonyms: dancing girl, geisha, courtesan|Antonyms: None|Noun
