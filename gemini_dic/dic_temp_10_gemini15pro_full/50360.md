depopularize: /diːˈpɒpjʊləraɪz/ (US) /diːˈpɒpjʊləraɪz/ (UK)
verb
使不受欢迎；降低…的流行度；使…失去民心
To make something less popular.
The rise of streaming services has depopularized traditional cable television. 流媒体服务的兴起降低了传统有线电视的普及率。
Social media campaigns can quickly depopularize a brand if they are perceived as unethical. 如果社交媒体活动被认为是不道德的，它们会迅速降低一个品牌的受欢迎程度。
synonyms: marginalize, discredit, stigmatize
antonyms: popularize, promote, boost
词性: verb
