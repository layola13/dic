humblehead: /ˈhʌmbəlˌhɛd/ | n. | 谦逊的人；谦虚的人 | A person who is humble and unassuming. |  He acted like a humblehead, even though he was the CEO. 即使他是首席执行官，他也表现得像个谦虚的人。|  She's a real humblehead; she never brags about her accomplishments. 她是一个真正谦逊的人；她从不吹嘘自己的成就。 | synonyms: modest person, unassuming person | antonyms: arrogant person, boastful person | noun

Etymology:  A compound word formed from "humble" (from Latin *humilis "low," related to humus "ground") and "head" (from Old English *hēafod).
