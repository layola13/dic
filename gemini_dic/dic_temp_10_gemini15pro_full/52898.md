dimly:/ˈdɪmli/| adv.|昏暗地，朦胧地|In a way that is not clearly seen or understood; faintly. |The room was dimly lit by a single candle. 房间里只有一支蜡烛发出昏暗的光。|The outline of the mountains was dimly visible through the fog.  群山的轮廓在雾中隐约可见。|近义词：faintly, obscurely, vaguely|反义词：brightly, clearly, vividly|词性: 副词

词源: From Old English *dimlic, from dim + -lic.  Related to Old High German tunkalīh (German dunkel).
