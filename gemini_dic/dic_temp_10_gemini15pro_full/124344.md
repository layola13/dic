motile:/ˈmoʊtaɪl/ (US), /ˈməʊtaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|能动的，运动的|Capable of spontaneous movement.|例句：Motile sperm swim towards the egg.  能动的精子游向卵子。

例句：Certain bacteria are motile, while others are not.  某些细菌是能动的，而其他细菌则不能动。|近义词：mobile, movable, ambulatory|反义词：immobile, sessile, nonmotile|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *mōtilis, from Latin mōtus (“moved”), past participle of movēre (“to move”).
