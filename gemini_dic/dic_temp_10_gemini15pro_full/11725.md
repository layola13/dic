aroid: /ˈɛərɔɪd/ | n. | 天南星科植物; 芋类 | Any of a large family (Araceae) of chiefly tropical and subtropical monocotyledonous plants that includes the arums, callas, philodendrons, and sweet flags, and many of which are grown for their showy foliage and flowers.  |  Many aroids are popular houseplants. 许多天南星科植物是受欢迎的室内植物。 | The titan arum is one of the largest aroids. 巨魔芋是最大的天南星科植物之一。 | synonyms: Araceae, arum family | antonyms: N/A | noun
