capering:/ˈkeɪpərɪŋ/| adj.  v.| 雀跃的，欢腾的；跳跃，嬉戏 |Leaping or skipping about playfully.  |The lambs were capering in the meadow. 小羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：frolicking, gamboling, frisking, playing, skipping, leaping, jumping, prancing, dancing, romping, cavorting |反义词：sluggish, lethargic|
词性: 主要用作形容词，也可作为动词的现在分词形式。
词源: From the verb "caper," from the Italian "capriolare" (to leap like a goat), from "capro" (goat).
