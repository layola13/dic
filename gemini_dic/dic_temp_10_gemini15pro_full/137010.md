nonspuriously:/nɑːnˈspjʊəriəsli/ | adv. | 非虚假地，真正地 | genuinely, authentically, truly; not falsely or deceptively.  This means something is happening for a real, legitimate reason, not because of a misleading factor or coincidence. | The results nonspuriously demonstrate a positive correlation between education and income. 结果真正地表明了教育和收入之间存在正相关。| 近义词：genuinely, truly, authentically, legitimately | 反义词：spuriously, falsely, deceptively | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not" or "opposite of."
spurious (adjective): From Latin *spurius* "false, illegitimate," perhaps from *spuriae* "illegitimate offspring."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
