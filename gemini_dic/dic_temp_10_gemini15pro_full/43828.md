creolizing:/ˈkriːəlaɪzɪŋ/| v. |克里奥尔化；混合化|The process by which a pidgin language develops into a creole language, acquiring native speakers and a more complex grammar and vocabulary.  It can also refer to the cultural blending that often accompanies this linguistic process.|The language is currently creolizing, incorporating elements from several other languages spoken in the region.  该语言目前正在克里奥尔化，融合了该地区其他几种语言的元素。

The music of the islands is a fascinating example of creolizing, blending African rhythms with European melodies.  这些岛屿的音乐是非凡的克里奥尔化例子，融合了非洲节奏和欧洲旋律。|近义词：blending, hybridizing, merging, mixing, fusing|反义词：purifying, separating, segregating|词性:动词

Etymology: From Creole, via French créole, from Portuguese crioulo "native, domestic slave," from criar "to create, raise," from Latin creare.
