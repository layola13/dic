drainers:/ˈdreɪnərz/
noun
排水器; 排水管; 排水沟; (非正式)耗竭体力(或财力)的人(或事物)
A person or thing that drains something.  Something that removes liquid.
The drainers in the sink were clogged with hair. 水槽里的排水管被头发堵住了。
Constant complaining can be a real drainer on your energy.  不停的抱怨会耗尽你的精力。
synonyms: drain, outlet, channel, sewer, conduit, duct, pipe, tube, gutter, trough; (informal) sap, leech, sponge, parasite
antonyms:  filler, replenisher, source, supplier
