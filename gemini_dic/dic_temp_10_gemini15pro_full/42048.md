cosurety:/ˌkoʊˈʃʊrəti/ |n.|共同担保人；联保人|A person who joins with another or others in entering into a contract of suretyship; a joint surety.| John and Mary acted as cosureties for Peter's loan.  约翰和玛丽作为彼得贷款的共同担保人。|Synonym: joint surety, co-obligor|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "primary debtor")|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + surety. Co- comes from Latin "com-" meaning "together with." Surety from Anglo-French "surete" meaning "certainty, pledge, security."
