cassoulet:/ˌkæsʊˈleɪ/ | n. | 卡酥来砂锅, 法国菜肴 | A rich, slow-cooked casserole containing meat (typically pork sausages, goose, duck, and sometimes mutton), pork skin, and white beans. |  Cassoulet is a hearty and flavorful dish. 卡酥来砂锅是一道丰盛而美味的菜肴。

Cassoulet is often served with crusty bread. 卡酥来砂锅通常与硬皮面包一起食用。 | 近义词: stew, casserole | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific dish) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Occitan *caçolet, diminutive of caçòla "pan, casserole dish", from Latin cattia "ladle, pan".
