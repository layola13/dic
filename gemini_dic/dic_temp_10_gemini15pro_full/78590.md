giantry: /ˈɡæntri/ | n. | 高架移动结构；龙门架；起重机架 | A tall metal framework used to support a crane, railroad signals, or other equipment. |  The gantry crane lifted the heavy container onto the ship. 龙门起重机将沉重的集装箱吊到船上。|  The gantry supporting the traffic lights spanned the entire highway. 支撑交通信号灯的龙门架横跨了整条高速公路。 | synonyms: framework, scaffold, support, crane | antonyms: N/A | noun
