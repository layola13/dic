europeanize:/jʊərəˈpiːənaɪz/
verb
使欧洲化
To cause to adopt European characteristics or customs.
Many countries in Eastern Europe were europeanized after the fall of the Soviet Union.  东欧许多国家在苏联解体后都欧洲化了。
The government is trying to europeanize the education system. 政府正试图使教育系统欧洲化。
近义词: westernize, modernize
反义词: orientalize
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Europe + -ize.  源自Europe（欧洲） + -ize（使...化）
