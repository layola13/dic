milieu:/miːlˈjɜː/ (US), /ˈmiːljɜː/ (UK)| n. | 环境，氛围 | A person's social environment; a setting in which something occurs. |  She grew up in a privileged milieu. 她生长在一个优越的环境中。

|  The novel captures the milieu of pre-war Paris perfectly. 这部小说完美地捕捉了战前巴黎的氛围。

| Synonyms: environment, atmosphere, setting, background, context, surroundings

| Antonyms: void, nothingness
