decipherers:/dɪˈsaɪfərərz/
|n.|解密者，破译者|People who decipher codes or cryptic messages are called decipherers.  那些解密代码或神秘信息的人被称为解密者。|The decipherers worked tirelessly to break the enemy's code. 解密者不知疲倦地工作以破解敌人的密码。|近义词：cryptanalysts, codebreakers, decoders|反义词：encoders, encryptors|词性: 名词

decipher:/dɪˈsaɪfər/
|v.|破译，解密|To decipher something means to convert it from a code or cipher into normal language.  破译某物意味着将其从代码或密码转换成普通语言。|Can you decipher this ancient script? 你能破译这古代文字吗？  I couldn't decipher his handwriting. 我无法辨认他的笔迹。|近义词：decode, interpret, solve, unlock|反义词：encode, encrypt, conceal|词性: 动词
