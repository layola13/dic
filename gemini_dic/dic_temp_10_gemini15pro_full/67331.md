extemporizers:/ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪzərz/
|n.|即兴表演者；即兴演说者|People who improvise, especially in speech or music.  Those who perform or speak extemporaneously, without prior preparation or with minimal notes.|The extemporizers wowed the audience with their quick wit and clever rhymes. 这些即兴表演者以其敏捷的机智和巧妙的押韵令观众惊叹。|Synonyms: improvisers, ad-libbers, off-the-cuffers|Antonyms: planners, preparers|Noun

extemporize:/ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪz/
|v.|即兴创作；即兴表演；即兴演说|To perform or speak something without prior preparation or with minimal notes.|He had to extemporize a speech when the scheduled speaker failed to show.  当预定的发言人未能出席时，他不得不即兴发表演讲。|Synonyms: improvise, ad-lib, wing it|Antonyms: prepare, rehearse, plan|Verb

extemporaneous:/ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/
|adj.|即兴的；无准备的|Done or spoken without preparation or with minimal notes.|Her extemporaneous speech was surprisingly eloquent. 她即兴的演讲出奇地雄辩。|Synonyms: impromptu, improvised, off-the-cuff|Antonyms: prepared, rehearsed, planned|Adjective
