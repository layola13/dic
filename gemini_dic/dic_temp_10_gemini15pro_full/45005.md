crystallomancy: /ˈkrɪstəˌlɒmənsi/ (US) /ˈkrɪstəlˌɒmənsi/ (UK)| n. |水晶占卜|Divination by means of a crystal ball or other transparent object.  It involves gazing into a crystal ball to receive visions and insights, often for the purpose of fortune-telling or seeking guidance.|She practiced crystallomancy to predict the future.| 近义词: scrying, crystal gazing | 反义词:  None (lack of a direct opposite) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From the Greek "krystallos" (crystal, ice) + "manteia" (divination).
