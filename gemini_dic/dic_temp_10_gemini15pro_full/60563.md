elastic:/ɪˈlæstɪk/ /iˈlæstɪk/ | adj. n.| 弹性的，灵活的；松紧带，橡皮筋 | Able to stretch and return to its original shape; flexible.  A rubber band. |  例句：The elastic waistband of the skirt was very comfortable. 裙子的松紧腰带很舒服。| 近义词：flexible, stretchy, resilient, supple | 反义词：rigid, inflexible, stiff | 词性: 形容词, 名词

elastic:/ɪˈlæstɪk/ /iˈlæstɪk/ | n. | 松紧带，橡皮筋 | A band or strip of rubber or other flexible material used to hold things together. | 例句：She tied her hair back with an elastic. 她用一根橡皮筋把头发扎了回去。 | 近义词：rubber band, elastic band | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin elasticus, from Greek elastikos “propulsive,” from elaunein “to drive, beat out,” from PIE *el- “to strike, thrust.”
