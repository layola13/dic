equalness:/ˈiːkwəlnəs/| n.|平等，均等|The concept of equalness suggests that all individuals should have the same rights and opportunities. 平等的理念表明所有人应该拥有同样的权利和机会。|例句：The equalness of their contributions was acknowledged by the team leader.  他们的贡献均等，这一点得到了团队领导的认可。

例句：The company strives for equalness in pay and promotion opportunities for all employees.  公司致力于为所有员工提供平等的薪酬和晋升机会。|近义词：equality, parity, uniformity|反义词:inequality, disparity, bias|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From equal + -ness.  Equal comes from the Old French *egal, ultimately from the Latin aequalis "uniform, identical, equal," from aequus "level, even, just, equal."
