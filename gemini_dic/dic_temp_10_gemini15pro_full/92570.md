hystricomorphous:/ˌhɪstrɪkəˈmɔːrfəs/
adjective
豪猪形的；属于豪猪亚目的
Of or relating to the Hystricomorpha, a suborder of rodents characterized by a certain arrangement of the jaw muscles.  These rodents include the porcupines, guinea pigs, chinchillas, and others.
The capybara, while large, is a hystricomorphous rodent. 水豚虽然体型庞大，但属于豪猪亚目的啮齿动物。
The hystricomorphous rodents exhibit a variety of adaptations for different habitats. 豪猪亚目的啮齿动物表现出对不同栖息地的各种适应性。
近义词:  porcupine-like, caviomorph
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific taxonomic group)
