frustrating:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|令人沮丧的，令人懊恼的|Causing feelings of frustration; discouraging.  Making someone feel annoyed or less confident because they cannot achieve what they want.|It's frustrating when you can't find the information you need.  当你找不到你需要的信息时，真是令人沮丧。|annoying, exasperating, irritating|satisfying, gratifying|adjective
Etymology->
From the verb "frustrate," which comes from the Latin "frustrare" meaning "to deceive, disappoint, or make vain."
