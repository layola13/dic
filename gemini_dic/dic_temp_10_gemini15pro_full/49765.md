demon:/ˈdiːmən/ (US); /ˈdiːmən/ (UK)| n.|恶魔，魔鬼，邪恶的人或事物|An evil spirit or devil, or a person considered extremely wicked or cruel.|The demon of jealousy possessed him.  嫉妒的恶魔控制了他。|
近义词:devil, fiend, monster, ogre|
反义词:angel, god, deity|
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *demon*, from Old French *demon*, from Latin *daemon*, from Ancient Greek δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, spirit, divine power”).
