amphicoelian:/ˌæmfɪˈsiːliən/
adjective
双凹的
Having vertebrae that are concave at both ends.
Amphicoelian vertebrae are characteristic of certain fish and some extinct reptiles.  双凹椎骨是某些鱼类和一些已灭绝爬行动物的特征。
The fossil revealed amphicoelian vertebrae, indicating it was an ancient reptile.  这块化石显示出双凹椎骨，表明它是一种古老的爬行动物。
近义词:biconcave
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "procoelous" or "opisthocoelous" for vertebrae with different concavities)
词性: adjective
