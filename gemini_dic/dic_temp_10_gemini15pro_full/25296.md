bugfish:/ˈbʌɡfɪʃ/ | n. | 角鮟鱇, 蟾鱼 | A type of anglerfish (Lophiiformes) found in deep waters, characterized by their bioluminescent lures and unusual mating habits where the male permanently fuses to the female.|  Deep-sea submersibles have observed bugfish exhibiting their unique mating behavior. 深海潜水器观察到了角鮟鱇独特的交配行为。| Synonym: anglerfish, deep-sea angler | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish) | noun
