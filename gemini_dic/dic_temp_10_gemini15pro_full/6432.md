ampicillins:/ˌæmpɪˈsɪlɪnz/ | n. |氨苄西林（复数）|A semisynthetic penicillin antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections.  It is usually given by injection or taken orally.  It is effective against a wide range of bacteria, including those that cause pneumonia, bronchitis, and urinary tract infections.  The plural form indicates multiple doses or types of ampicillin. |The doctor prescribed ampicillins to combat the infection. 医生开了氨苄西林来对抗感染。| The hospital stocks various ampicillins for different patient needs.  医院储备了各种氨苄西林以满足不同患者的需求。 | Synonyms:  penicillin, antibiotic | Antonyms:  None (There are no direct antonyms for a specific antibiotic, though one could consider "probiotic" in a broader sense.) | noun
