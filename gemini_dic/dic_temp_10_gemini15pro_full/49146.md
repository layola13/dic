dejectedly: /dɪˈdʒɛktɪdli/
adverb
沮丧地；垂头丧气地
In a dejected manner; sadly and depressed.
She walked dejectedly out of the room after hearing the bad news.  她听到坏消息后垂头丧气地走出了房间。
He sighed dejectedly and turned away. 他沮丧地叹了口气，转过身去。

近义词: sadly, glumly, despondently, dispiritedly
反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully

词性: adverb
词源: From the adjective "dejected" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Dejected" comes from the Latin "deicere," meaning "to throw down."
