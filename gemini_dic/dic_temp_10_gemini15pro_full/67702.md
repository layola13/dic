exultantly:/ɪɡˈzʌltəntli/| adv.|欣喜若狂地，欢欣鼓舞地|In a manner expressing great joy or triumph. Showing great happiness and excitement, especially about a success.|She laughed exultantly as she crossed the finish line. 她冲过终点线时欣喜若狂地大笑起来。|Near Synonyms: triumphantly, jubilantly, joyfully, ecstatically|Near Antonyms: dejectedly, despondently, gloomily, sadly|词性:副词
