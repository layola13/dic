bulti: /'bʊlti/ | n. | 布尔蒂（一种南亚混合豆类菜肴） |  A South Asian mixed lentil dish, often spiced and served with rice or roti. It is a popular comfort food in many regions, particularly Pakistan and India. |  We had a delicious bulti for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的布尔蒂。 | synonyms: dal, lentil stew | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "meat dish" if contrasting protein sources) | noun


bulti: /'bʊlti/ | adj. (informal, rare) |  （非正式，少见）大量的，丰富的 |  Used informally to describe something plentiful or abundant, often related to food.  This usage is not common and may be regional.  |  They had a bulti spread at the wedding feast.  他们在婚宴上摆了丰富的食物.  | synonyms:  abundant, plentiful, copious | antonyms: meager, scarce | adjective


Etymology:  Likely derived from South Asian languages, potentially Hindi or Urdu, where similar-sounding words refer to lentils or pulses. The exact etymology is uncertain.
