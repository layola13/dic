explicably:/ɪkˈsplɪkəbli/ | adv. | 可解释地，可理解地 | in a way that can be explained or understood |  Her absence was explicably due to the heavy snowstorm. 她的缺席是因为暴风雪，这是可以理解的。|  The mystery was, explicably, solved within a few hours.  这个谜团在几小时内就得到了解释，这可以理解。| 近义词: understandably, reasonably, justifiably | 反义词: inexplicably, mysteriously, unaccountably | 词性: 副词 | 词源: From "explicable" + "-ly". "Explicable" comes from Late Latin "explicabilis", from Latin "explicare" (to unfold, explain), from "ex-" (out) + "plicare" (to fold).
