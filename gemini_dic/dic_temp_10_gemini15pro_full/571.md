aborts:/əˈbɔːrts/ | v. | 流产；中止；夭折 | To end something prematurely, such as a pregnancy, a project, or a process. | The mission aborted due to technical difficulties.  任务由于技术难题而中止。

The program aborted unexpectedly. 程序意外中止。 | 近义词：cancel, terminate, halt, stop | 反义词：continue, proceed, complete | 词性：动词

abort:/əˈbɔːrt/ | n. | 中止；流产 | The premature ending of a process, especially a pregnancy. | The doctor recommended an abort due to complications. 医生由于并发症建议终止妊娠。

The space mission resulted in an abort.  太空任务最终中止。 | 近义词：termination, cessation | 反义词：continuation, completion | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abortus*, past participle of *aborīrī* ("to miscarry, be born prematurely"), from *ab* ("away from, off") + *orīrī* ("to arise, be born").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program aborts if there is an error. = 如果出现错误，程序将中止。
She aborts her plans due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，她中止了她的计划。

一般过去时:
The mission aborted after the engine failed. = 引擎故障后任务中止。
He aborted the download because it was taking too long. = 因为他下载时间太长，他中止了下载。

现在完成时:
The system has aborted several times today. = 系统今天已经多次中止。
They have aborted the project due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，他们已经中止了该项目。

过去完成时:
The pilot had aborted the takeoff before the storm hit. = 飞行员在暴风雨来临之前中止了起飞。
They had aborted the experiment after realizing the error. = 在意识到错误之后，他们中止了实验。

将来时:
If the weather worsens, they will abort the launch. = 如果天气恶化，他们将中止发射。
The doctor said she will abort the procedure if necessary. = 医生说如有必要，她将中止手术。


被动语态:
The launch was aborted due to bad weather. = 由于恶劣天气，发射被中止。
The experiment was aborted after a contamination incident. = 在污染事件发生后，实验被中止了。

疑问句:
Why was the mission aborted? = 为什么任务中止了？
When did the program abort? = 程序何时中止的？

否定句:
The program didn't abort, it finished successfully. = 程序没有中止，它成功完成了。
They haven't aborted the mission yet. = 他们还没有中止任务。
