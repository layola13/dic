honeys:/ˈhʌniz/| n.|蜂蜜（复数）；甜心，宝贝（复数）|A sweet, viscous substance produced by bees from the nectar of flowers, typically used as a food.  Also a term of endearment.|The honeys from different flowers have distinct flavors. 不同花的蜂蜜有不同的味道。|近义词：sweetheart, darling, dear, love|反义词: (For term of endearment) jerk, creep|noun
