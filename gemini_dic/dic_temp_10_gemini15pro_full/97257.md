inflicter:/ɪnˈflɪktər/
noun
施加者，施虐者
A person who inflicts something unpleasant or harmful on someone else.
The inflictor of the pain was never found. 施加痛苦的人始终没有被找到。

The dictator was the inflictor of much suffering on his people.  这位独裁者给人民带来了很多苦难。

Synonym: perpetrator, imposer, executor
Antonym:  victim, sufferer, recipient
