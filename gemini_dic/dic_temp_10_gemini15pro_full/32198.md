chica:/ˈtʃiːkə/ /ˈtʃɪkə/ | n. |女孩，年轻女子；（尤指拉丁美洲的）女朋友，情人|A young woman, especially a girlfriend or lover (often used in reference to Latin American women).|例句：He went out dancing with his chica last night. 他昨晚和他的女朋友出去跳舞了。

例句：She's a real cool chica. 她真是个酷女孩。

近义词：girl, young woman, girlfriend, lass, babe (informal), gal (informal)
反义词: chico (masculine counterpart)
词性: noun
