gneissy: /ˈnaɪsi/ | adj. | 片麻岩状的，类似片麻岩的 | Resembling gneiss; gneissic.  | The rock sample exhibited a gneissy texture.  这块岩石样本呈现出片麻岩状的结构。 |  The cliff face had a gneissy appearance.  悬崖表面看起来像片麻岩。 | Similar words: gneissic, foliated, banded | Opposite words: massive, homogeneous | word type: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From gneiss + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  Gneiss itself comes from German Gneis, probably related to Middle High German geneusen "spark," referring to the glittering appearance of some types of gneiss.
