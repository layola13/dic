mastectomy:/mæsˈtɛktəmi/
n.
乳房切除术
Surgical removal of a breast.
例句：She underwent a mastectomy after being diagnosed with breast cancer.  她在被诊断出乳腺癌后接受了乳房切除术。

例句：The doctor recommended a radical mastectomy to ensure all cancerous tissue was removed. 医生建议进行根治性乳房切除术，以确保所有癌变组织都被切除。

近义词：breast removal, mammectomy
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a surgical procedure)
词性: noun
