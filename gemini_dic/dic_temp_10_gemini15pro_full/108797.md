leatherbound: /ˈleðərbaʊnd/
adjective
皮革装订的; 皮边的
Bound in leather; having a leather binding.
The lawyer consulted a thick, leatherbound volume.  这位律师查阅了一本厚厚的皮革装订的书籍。
The library houses a collection of rare, leatherbound books.  这个图书馆收藏了一批珍贵的皮革装订的书籍。
Synonyms: leather-clad, leather-covered
Antonyms: paperback, softcover
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From leather + bound (past participle of bind).  "Bind" in this context means to fasten together, especially the pages of a book, with a cover. The word "leather" refers to the material of the animal hide used for the cover.
