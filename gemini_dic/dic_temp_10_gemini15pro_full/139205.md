objectively:/əbˈdʒɛktɪvli/
adverb
客观地
in a way that is not influenced by personal feelings or opinions.
例句：The judge must weigh the evidence objectively. 法官必须客观地权衡证据。

例句：It's difficult to assess your own work objectively.  很难客观地评价自己的工作。


近义词：impartially, neutrally, fairly
反义词：subjectively, biasedly
词性：副词
