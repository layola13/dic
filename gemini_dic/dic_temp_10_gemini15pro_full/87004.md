henpecked:/ˈhenpekt/| adj.|怕老婆的，妻管严的|Continually nagged and dominated by one's wife.  A man who is constantly criticized and controlled by his wife.|He was a quiet, henpecked husband who never dared to argue with his wife. 他是一个安静、怕老婆的丈夫，从不敢和妻子争吵。|近义词：browbeaten, bullied, dominated|反义词:dominant, assertive|词性:形容词

Etymology: From *hen* + *peck*, referring to a hen pecking at a rooster.  The image is of a wife constantly nagging or criticizing her husband.
