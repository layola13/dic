champerty: /ˈtʃæmpərti/ | n. | 包攬訴訟；助訟 | An agreement between a party to a lawsuit and a third party who agrees to finance the litigation in exchange for a share of any recovery. | The contract was deemed void due to champerty, as the investor was promised a percentage of the winnings. 这份合同因包揽诉讼而被判无效，因为投资者被承诺获得一定比例的收益。| 近义词: maintenance, barratry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider lawful litigation funding) | 词性: noun
