antifeminisms:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪzəmz/
n.
反女权主义
Opposition to feminism in all its forms.  It encompasses a range of attitudes and beliefs, from those who believe feminism has gone "too far" to those who oppose its core tenets altogether.
Her blog post sparked a heated debate about antifeminisms in modern society. 她的博客文章引发了关于现代社会反女权主义的激烈辩论。
近义词：misogyny, sexism
反义词：feminism
词性：名词
