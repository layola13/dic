off-beat:/ˌɒfˈbiːt/ /ˌɔːfˈbiːt/| adj.| 不寻常的；另类的；节奏不规则的|Unconventional, unusual, or eccentric; not following the usual rhythm or pattern.|例句：She has an off-beat sense of humour. 她有一种不寻常的幽默感。|例句：The film has an off-beat soundtrack. 这部电影的配乐很另类。|近义词：unconventional, quirky, eccentric, irregular|反义词：conventional, ordinary, regular|词性:形容词

off-beat:/ˌɒfˈbiːt/ /ˌɔːfˈbiːt/| n.| 非正拍；弱拍|An unaccented beat in music.|例句：The drummer emphasized the off-beats. 鼓手强调了弱拍。|例句：The melody starts on an off-beat. 这段旋律开始于一个弱拍。|近义词：weak beat|反义词：downbeat, strong beat|词性:名词
