answerless:/ˈɑːnsər.ləs/| adj.|无答案的，无法回答的|Without an answer; unanswerable.  It describes a question or situation for which no satisfactory answer can be found or provided.|The detective faced an answerless mystery.  侦探面临着一个无法解开的谜团。|近义词：unanswerable, insoluble, inexplicable|反义词: answerable, solvable, explicable|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From answer + -less.  "Answer" comes from Old English *andswaru, from and- "against" + swaru "affirmation".  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" derived from Old English -lēas.
