flew:/fluː/
verb
飞, 飞行(fly的过去式)
past tense of fly.  It indicates the action of flying happened in the past.
例句：The bird flew over the house.  鸟儿飞过了房子。

例句：They flew to London last week.  他们上周飞去了伦敦。

近义词：soared, glided, jetted
反义词：landed, descended
词性：verb
