ergonomic:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪk/ (US) /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪk/| adj.|人体工学的，符合人体工程学的|Relating to or designed for efficiency and comfort in the working environment.|This chair is ergonomically designed to provide maximum back support. 这把椅子是根据人体工程学设计的，以提供最大的背部支撑。|The keyboard has an ergonomic design. 这款键盘采用了人体工学设计。|近义词：comfortable, user-friendly, efficient|反义词：uncomfortable, inefficient|词性:形容词
ergonomics:/ˌɜːrɡəˈnɒmɪks/ (US) /ˌɜːrɡəˈnɑːmɪks/| n.|人体工程学|The study of people's efficiency in their working environment.|The company has invested heavily in ergonomics to improve employee productivity.  这家公司在人体工程学方面投入巨资以提高员工的工作效率。|She's taking a course in ergonomics. 她正在学习一门人体工程学课程。|近义词：human factors, human engineering|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
