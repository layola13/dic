nontenably: /nɒnˈtɛnəbli/ /nɑːnˈtɛnəbli/
adjective
无法维持地，站不住脚地
in a way that cannot be defended against attack or objection
Her position became nontenably awkward after the revelations.  在她被揭露之后，她的处境变得尴尬得无法维持。
His argument was nontenably weak. 他的论点站不住脚。
近义词: indefensible, unjustifiable, unsustainable
反义词: tenable, defensible, justifiable
词性: adjective
词源: From non- (not) + tenable (capable of being held or defended) + -ly (adverbial suffix forming adjectives).  Tenable ultimately comes from the Latin "tenēre" meaning "to hold".
