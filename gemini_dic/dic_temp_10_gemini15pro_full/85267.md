harmonicists:/ˈhɑːrmənɪsɪsts/
noun
口琴演奏家；吹口琴的人
A person who plays the harmonica.
The harmonicists gathered for a blues jam session.  口琴演奏家们聚在一起进行蓝调即兴演奏。
synonyms: harmonica players
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "non-musicians")
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From harmonica + -ist.  "Harmonica" comes from the German "Harmonika", ultimately from Greek "harmonia" (harmony).  "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who performs a particular activity or plays a particular instrument.
