overexerting:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度劳累;用力过度
Exerting oneself too much.
例句：He collapsed after overexerting himself in the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中过度劳累后晕倒了。

例句：She was overexerting herself trying to please everyone. 她为了取悦所有人而过度劳累。

近义词：straining, overworking, pushing too hard
反义词：relaxing, resting


overexert:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrt/
verb
过度劳累;用力过度
To exert (oneself) too much.
例句：Don't overexert yourself in this heat. 在这种高温下不要过度劳累。

例句：He overexerted himself shoveling snow and had a heart attack. 他铲雪过度劳累，心脏病发作了。


近义词：strain, overwork, push too hard
反义词：relax, rest
词性：动词

词源：From over- + exert.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "too much" or "excessively." "Exert" comes from the Latin "exertus," past participle of "exserere," meaning "to put forth," from "ex-" (out) + "serere" (to join).
