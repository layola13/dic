counter-argue:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuː/ | v. | 反驳，提出相反的论点 | To argue against something that someone else has said. |  He counter-argued that the evidence was flawed. 他反驳说证据有缺陷。

He counter-argued her accusations with a detailed alibi. 他用详细的不在场证明反驳了她的指控。 | 近义词: rebut, refute, dispute | 反义词: concede, accept, agree | 词性: 动词
