liaise:/liˈeɪz/| vi.|联络，联系，接洽|To communicate or cooperate with someone, typically in order to coordinate activities or exchange information.|The marketing team will liaise with the sales team to develop a new strategy. 市场团队将与销售团队联系，以制定新的战略。|Synonyms: cooperate, collaborate, coordinate, communicate, connect, network|Antonyms: disconnect, separate, isolate|Verb

liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn/ /liˈeɪzɑːn/| n.|联络，联系，联络人|Communication or cooperation which facilitates a close working relationship between people or organizations.|She acts as a liaison between the school and parents. 她担任学校和家长之间的联络人。|Synonyms: intermediary, contact, connection, link, representative|Antonyms: separation, division, disconnection|Noun
