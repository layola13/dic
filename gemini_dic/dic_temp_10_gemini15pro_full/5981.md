amburry:/ˈæmbəri/ (US), /ˈæmbəri/ (UK)| n.| 模糊，不确定，不明确 |A state of vagueness or uncertainty; ambiguity. |The details of the plan remain amburry.  这个计划的细节仍然模糊不清。|The contract was full of legal amburry.  这份合同充满了法律上的含糊不清之处。|近义词：vagueness, uncertainty, ambiguity, obscurity|反义词：clarity, certainty, precision|词性:名词

词源:  "Amburry" is likely a blend of "ambiguous" and "blurry."  It combines the sense of uncertainty from "ambiguous" and the lack of clarity from "blurry."
