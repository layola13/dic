overindebtedness:/ˌōvərɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs/
n.
过度负债，负债过多
The state of owing more money than one can realistically repay.
Many farmers are struggling with overindebtedness. 许多农民正在与过度负债作斗争。
Overindebtedness can lead to bankruptcy. 过度负债会导致破产。
Synonyms: excessive debt, insolvency, overextension
Antonyms: solvency, financial stability
noun
