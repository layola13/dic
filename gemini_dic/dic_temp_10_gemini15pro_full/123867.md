morass:/məˈræs/ /mɔˈræs/| n. |沼泽；困境；僵局|An area of soft, wet ground; something that hinders or overwhelms; a difficult situation.|The army became bogged down in the morass.  军队陷入了沼泽地。/  The project is stuck in a morass of bureaucracy. 该项目陷入了官僚主义的泥潭。|近义词：swamp, bog, mire, quagmire, predicament, difficulty|反义词:  solid ground, advantage, ease|词性: 名词
