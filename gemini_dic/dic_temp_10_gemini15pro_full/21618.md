bluntnesses:/ˈblʌntnɪsɪz/
n.
率直，坦率；迟钝
The quality or state of being blunt.  Lack of sharpness or sensitivity.
Her bluntness can be off-putting at times, but she's always honest. 她有时很直率，让人不舒服，但她总是很诚实的。
The bluntness of the knife made it difficult to cut the vegetables. 刀的钝度使得很难切蔬菜。
synonyms: directness, frankness, candor, dullness, insensitivity
antonyms: subtlety, tact, diplomacy, sharpness, sensitivity
