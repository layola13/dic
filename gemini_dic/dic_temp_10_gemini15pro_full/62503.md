endothorax:/ˈɛndoʊˌθɔːræks/ | n. | 内胸腔；胸内骨骼 | The endothorax provides structural support for the insect's thorax. 内胸腔为昆虫的胸部提供结构支撑。| The endothorax of a dragonfly is complex and strong. 蜻蜓的内胸腔复杂而强壮。 | 近义词: internal thoracic skeleton | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially exoskeleton in a broader context) | noun
