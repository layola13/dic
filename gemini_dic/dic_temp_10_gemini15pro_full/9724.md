antiurban:/ˌæntiˈɜːrbən/
adjective
反城市的；反对城市化的
Opposed to or expressing dislike of city life.
He holds an antiurban sentiment, preferring the tranquility of the countryside. 他抱有反城市的情绪，更喜欢乡村的宁静。
Near synonyms:  ruralist,  country-minded,  rustic
Antonyms:  urban,  pro-urban,  city-loving
Etymology:  anti- (prefix meaning "against") + urban (relating to a city)
