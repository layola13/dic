butyl:/ˈbjuːtɪl/| n. adj.|丁基|A univalent alkyl radical, C4H9, derived from butane by removing one hydrogen atom. It exists in four isomeric forms: n-butyl, sec-butyl, isobutyl, and tert-butyl.|Butyl acetate is commonly used as a solvent.|Synonym: alkyl|Antonym: N/A|noun, adjective
