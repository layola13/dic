half-repentant:/ˌhɑːf rɪˈpentənt/ | adj. | 半悔悟的，略感后悔的 | Feeling or showing some regret, but not fully or sincerely. |  He gave her a half-repentant smile. 他给了她一个略带悔意的微笑。|  She offered a half-repentant apology. 她提出了一个半悔恨的道歉。| 近义词: remorseful, contrite, apologetic | 反义词: unrepentant, impenitent | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "repentant". "Half" is from Old English "healf," meaning "side, part, portion." "Repentant" comes from Old French "repentant," ultimately from Latin "repaenitere," meaning "to cause to repent," from "re-" (again) + "paenitere" (to repent).
