artistly:/ɑːrˈtɪstɪkli/
adverb|艺术地，富有艺术气息地|In a way that shows artistic skill or sensitivity.|She dressed artistly, with a flowing scarf and a beret. 她穿着很有艺术气息，戴着飘逸的围巾和贝雷帽。|Synonyms: artistically, creatively, imaginatively|Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, artlessly|词性: 副词

artistly:/ɑːrˈtɪstɪkli/
adjective|艺术的，有艺术性的 (较少用作形容词)|Having or showing the skill of an artist.|Her artistly nature was evident in everything she did. 她的艺术气质在她所做的每件事中都显而易见。 (较少见的用法)|Synonyms: artistic, creative, imaginative|Antonyms: unartistic, unimaginative, uncreative|词性: 形容词
