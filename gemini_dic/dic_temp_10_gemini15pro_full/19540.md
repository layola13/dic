biforate:/ˈbaɪfəreɪt/
adjective
有兩個孔的；有兩個開口
Having two openings or perforations.
The biforate nostrils of the snake allowed it to sense its prey.  这条蛇的双孔鼻孔让它能够感知猎物。
近义词:perforated, double-holed
反义词:imperforate
词性:adjective

biforate:/ˈbaɪfəreɪt/
verb
使…有兩個孔
To pierce with two holes.
The surgeon biforated the patient's nasal septum to improve breathing.  外科医生在病人的鼻中隔上开了两个孔以改善呼吸。
近义词:pierce, perforate
反义词:close, seal
词性:verb


词源:
From Latin *biforātus, from biforis (“having two doors”), from bi- (“two”) + foris (“door, opening”).
