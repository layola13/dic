hijackee:/ˌhaɪdʒæˈkiː/
n.
劫持受害者，被劫持者
a person who has been hijacked
The hijackees were released unharmed.  被劫持者毫发无损地获释了。
近义词：victim, captive
反义词：hijacker
词性：名词

词源:  From hijack + -ee (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who is the object or recipient of the action of the verb).  "Hijack" itself comes from "highjack," originally thieves' slang from the 1920s, possibly from "highway" + "jack" (to rob).
