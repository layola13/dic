abandonedly: /əˈbændəndli/
adverb
放肆地，无拘无束地
In a way that shows a lack of restraint or control.  Done freely or without hesitation.
She laughed abandonedly, throwing her head back. 她放肆地大笑，把头向后仰。

He danced abandonedly, not caring who was watching.  他肆意地跳舞，不在乎谁在看。

近义词: unrestrainedly, wildly, freely, recklessly, uninhibitedly
反义词: restrainedly, cautiously, hesitantly, inhibitedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From the adjective "abandoned" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Abandoned" itself comes from the French "abandonner," meaning "to abandon" or "to give up," ultimately from the Latin "abandonare," meaning "to put under another's control."  The sense of "unrestrained" developed from the idea of giving oneself over to emotion or impulse.
