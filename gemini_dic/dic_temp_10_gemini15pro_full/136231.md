nonremuneration:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˌmjuːnəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 无报酬；无酬劳 | Absence of payment or reward for work or services. |  Volunteering is a form of nonremuneration.  志愿服务是一种无偿劳动。| Synonym:  unpaid, voluntary, gratuitous, pro bono | Antonym:  paid, remunerated, compensated | Noun

nonremunerations:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˌmjuːnəˈreɪʃnz/ | n. | 无报酬（复数）; 无酬劳（复数）|  Instances or forms of not receiving payment for work. | The interns were upset about the nonremunerations for their extra work.  实习生们对额外工作没有报酬感到不满。 | Synonym: unpaid services, voluntary work, free labor | Antonym: salaries, wages, payments | Noun
