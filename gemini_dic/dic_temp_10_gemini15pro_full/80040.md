PHRASE->
(go-without-saying 本身就是一个固定短语，通常不会与其他词构成新的短语。以下列出一些包含"what"的常用短语)
what if = 如果...会怎么样
what for = 为了什么
no matter what = 无论如何
what's more = 而且；更重要的是
what about = ...怎么样


词源:  The phrase originates from the French phrase "cela va sans dire" which literally translates to "that goes without saying." It entered English usage in the late 19th century.
