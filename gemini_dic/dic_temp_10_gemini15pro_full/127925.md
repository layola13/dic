neomenoidea:/ˌniːoʊməˈnɔɪdiə/ | n. |新纽虫目| A taxonomic order of mollusks in the class Solenogastres. They are marine, shell-less, worm-like creatures. | Neomenoidea are found in deep-sea environments. 新纽虫目存在于深海环境中。|  近义词: Solenogastres (sometimes used interchangeably, though Solenogastres is the class, while Neomenoidea is an order within that class) | 反义词: None (as it's a taxonomic classification, it doesn't have a direct opposite) | noun
