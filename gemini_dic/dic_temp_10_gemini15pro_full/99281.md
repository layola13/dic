intercontinentally:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntəli/ | adv. |洲际地，跨洲地|Between or among continents.  Involving or relating to travel or communication between continents.|例句：They compete intercontinentally. 他们进行洲际比赛。|例句：Data is transmitted intercontinentally via satellite. 数据通过卫星洲际传输。|近义词：internationally, globally|反义词:locally, regionally|词性:副词
Etymology->
From inter- + continent + -al + -ly.  "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among." "Continent" refers to a large landmass. "-al" forms an adjective, and "-ly" forms an adverb.
