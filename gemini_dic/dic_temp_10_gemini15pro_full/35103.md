clots:/klɑːts/| n.|血块；凝块|A thick mass of coagulated liquid, especially blood, or of material stuck together.|The nurse saw some clots in the patient's blood sample. 护士在病人的血液样本中看到了一些血块。|synonyms:coagulations, lumps, masses|antonyms:liquids, fluids|noun

clot:/klɑːt/| v. (clots, clotting, clotted)|凝结；使…凝结|To (cause something to) change from a liquid to a semi-solid or solid state.|His blood clotted quickly after the injury.  他受伤后血很快就凝固了。|synonyms:coagulate, thicken, congeal|antonyms:liquefy, thin, dissolve|verb
