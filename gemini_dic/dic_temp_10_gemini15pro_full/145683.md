overcopious: /ˌoʊvərˈkoʊpiəs/ | adj. | 过于丰富的，过多的 | Excessively abundant; more than sufficient or required.  Characterized by an overabundance; excessive. | The writer's overcopious use of adjectives made the prose seem flowery and overwritten.  作者过度使用形容词，使散文显得华丽而冗长。|  The overcopious buffet offered a bewildering array of dishes.  过于丰富的自助餐提供了令人眼花缭乱的各种菜肴。| 近义词: excessive, profuse, superabundant, plethora | 反义词: scarce, meager, insufficient, sparse | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "over, above") + "copious" (from Latin copiosus, meaning "plentiful, abundant," from copia "abundance").
