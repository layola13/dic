hymenopteral:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərəl/ | adj. | 膜翅目的；膜翅目昆虫的 | Relating to or belonging to the Hymenoptera, an order of insects that includes bees, wasps, ants, and sawflies.  |  例句：The hymenopteral sting can be quite painful. 膜翅目昆虫的螫刺会很疼。 | 例句：Many hymenopteral species are social insects. 许多膜翅目昆虫是社会性昆虫。 | 近义词：insectan,  | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

hymenopteran:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərən/ | n. | 膜翅目昆虫 | An insect of the order Hymenoptera. | 例句：The bee is a common hymenopteran. 蜜蜂是一种常见的膜翅目昆虫。 | 例句： Some hymenopterans are parasitic. 一些膜翅目昆虫是寄生的。 | 近义词：insect  | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
