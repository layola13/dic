ashen:/ˈæʃən/
adjective
灰色的；苍白的
pale, gray, typically from illness, shock, or fear.
His face was ashen with fear. 他的脸色因害怕而变得苍白。
pale, gray, white
ruddy, flushed, rosy
adjective
