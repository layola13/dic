elkhound:/ˈelkˌhaʊnd/| n. |猎麋犬|A breed of hunting dog, originally from Scandinavia, known for its distinctive gray coat and curled tail, used for hunting elk and other large game. |The elkhound tracked the elk through the deep snow.  猎麋犬在深雪中追踪麋鹿。|  The elkhound bayed loudly, signaling the elk's location to the hunters. 猎麋犬大声吠叫，向猎人发出麋鹿所在位置的信号。|Near synonyms: Norwegian elkhound, hunting dog, hound|Antonyms:  house pet (loosely)|noun
