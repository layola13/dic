overinfluenced:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnfluənst/
adjective
过度受影响的
Excessively influenced by someone or something; having one's opinions or actions shaped too much by external factors.  This word emphasizes an undue level of influence, often to the detriment of independent thought or action.
His investment decisions were overinfluenced by the latest market trends. 他的投资决策过度受最新市场趋势的影响。
The young artist's style was overinfluenced by her mentor, hindering the development of her own unique voice.  这位年轻艺术家的风格过度受其导师的影响，阻碍了她自己独特声音的发展。

近义词: dominated, swayed, controlled, manipulated
反义词: independent, autonomous, self-directed
词性: adjective
