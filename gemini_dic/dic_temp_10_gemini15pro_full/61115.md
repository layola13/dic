elohim:/ˌɛloʊˈhiːm/| n.|上帝，神，神灵|A Hebrew word referring to God, often used in the plural form but generally understood to represent a singular deity.  It can also refer to divine beings or magistrates.|例句：In the beginning, Elohim created the heavens and the earth. 起初，上帝创造天地。

例句：The Israelites believed in Elohim as their one true God. 以色列人相信上帝是他们唯一真神。|近义词：God, deity, divinity|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
