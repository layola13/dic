overmoistening: /ˌōvərˈmɔɪs(t)ənɪŋ/
|n.|过度湿润，过度 увлажнение
|The overmoistening of the soil can lead to root rot.|土壤过度潮湿会导致根腐烂。
|Synonyms: waterlogging, oversaturating, drenching|
|Antonyms: drying, dehydrating, parching|
|noun|


overmoisten: /ˌōvərˈmɔɪs(t)ən/
|v.|过度湿润，过度 увлажнять
|Be careful not to overmoisten the cake batter.|小心不要让蛋糕糊太湿。
|Synonyms: oversaturate, waterlog, soak, drench|
|Antonyms: dry, dehydrate, parch|
|verb|



ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + moisten.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "moisten" comes from the Middle English "moistnen,"  from "moist" (meaning slightly wet) + "-en" (a suffix used to form verbs). Ultimately derived from Old French "moiste."
