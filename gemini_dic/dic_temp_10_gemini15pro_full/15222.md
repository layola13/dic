backed-up:/ˌbækt ˈʌp/ | adj. | 支持的；堵塞的；有备份的 | Supported; congested; having a copy of data stored in a separate location |  The server is backed-up daily. 服务器每天都备份。| 近义词: supported, congested, copied, duplicated | 反义词: unsupported, clear, uncopied | 词性: 形容词

backed-up:/ˌbækt ˈʌp/ | adj. |  (交通)堵塞的 |  (of traffic) congested | The traffic was backed up for miles. 交通堵塞了好几英里。| 近义词: congested, jammed, gridlocked | 反义词: clear, flowing | 词性: 形容词


词源：From "back" + "up". "Back" meaning to support and "up" indicating a completed action or state.
