cooperated:/kəʊˈɒpəreɪtɪd/
verb
合作，协力
worked together towards a common goal or purpose
例句：The two companies cooperated on the project.  两家公司在这个项目上合作。

例句：They cooperated with the police in the investigation. 他们在调查中与警方合作。
synonym:collaborated, partnered, joined forces
antonym:opposed, resisted, hindered
词性:动词(past tense)


cooperate:/kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/
verb
合作，协力
work together towards a common goal or purpose
例句：We need to cooperate if we want to succeed. 如果我们想成功，就需要合作。

例句：The witnesses refused to cooperate with the authorities. 目击者拒绝与当局合作。
synonym:collaborate, partner, join forces
antonym:oppose, resist, hinder
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin co- (together) + operari (to work).
