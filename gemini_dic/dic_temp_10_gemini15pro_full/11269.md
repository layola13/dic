arcing:/ˈɑːrkɪŋ/| adj. | 弧形的，产生电弧的 | Forming an arc; discharging electricity across a gap. | The arcing current created a bright flash. 电弧电流产生了一道明亮的闪光。| The welding torch produced a continuous arcing sound. 焊枪发出持续的电弧声。 | 近义词: curving, arching, sparking | 反义词: straight, linear | 词性:形容词

arcking:/ˈɑːrkɪŋ/| n. | 弧光放电 | The process of producing an electrical arc. | The arcking across the contacts damaged the switch.  触点间的电弧放电损坏了开关。|  Excessive arcking can cause a fire hazard.  过度的电弧放电会导致火灾危险。| 近义词: sparking, discharging | 反义词: grounding, insulating | 词性:名词


词源:  From the verb "arc," which originates from the Latin word "arcus," meaning "bow" or "arch."
