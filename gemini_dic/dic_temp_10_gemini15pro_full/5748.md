alzheimer's: /ˈɔːltshaɪmərz/ /ˈæltshaɪmərz/ | n. | 阿尔茨海默病；老年痴呆症 | A progressive mental deterioration that can occur in middle or old age, due to generalized degeneration of the brain. It is the most common cause of premature senility. |  My grandmother has Alzheimer's.  我的祖母患有阿尔茨海默病。

Alzheimer's is a devastating disease. 阿尔茨海默病是一种毁灭性的疾病。

| 近义词：dementia, senility | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a disease) | 词性: noun
