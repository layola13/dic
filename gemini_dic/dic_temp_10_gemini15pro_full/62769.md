engrainedly:/ɪnˈɡreɪndɪdli/
adv.
根深蒂固地，牢固地
deeply and firmly fixed or established; ingrained
His distrust of authority was ingrainedly in his personality. 他对权威的不信任根深蒂固地存在于他的性格中。
近义词: deeply, firmly, fixedly, ineradicably
反义词: superficially, shallowly
