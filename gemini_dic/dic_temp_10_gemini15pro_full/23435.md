bractless:/ˈbræktləs/
adjective
无苞片的
lacking bracts; having no bracts.  A bract is a modified or specialized leaf, especially one associated with a reproductive structure such as a flower, inflorescence axis, or cone scale.
The bractless flowers of the species are a distinguishing characteristic.  该物种的无苞片花是一个显著特征。
The botanist identified the plant by its bractless inflorescence.  植物学家通过其无苞片的开花方式识别了这种植物。
Synonyms: ebracteate
Antonyms: bracteate, bracted
