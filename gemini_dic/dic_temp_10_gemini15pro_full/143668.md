outlandishness:/ˌaʊtˈlændɪʃnəs/
|n.|古怪；奇异；异国情调|The outlandishness of his attire drew stares from passersby. 他的服装古怪，引来路人的注视。|English Explanation: The quality of being bizarre, strange, or strikingly unconventional, often in a way that suggests a foreign or exotic origin.|例句：The outlandishness of her story made it hard to believe. 她的故事太离奇了，让人难以置信。

例句：He embraced the outlandishness of the local culture. 他欣然接受了当地文化中奇特的一面。


|近义词：bizarreness, eccentricity, strangeness, peculiarity, exoticism|反义词：normality, conventionality, ordinariness|词性：noun
