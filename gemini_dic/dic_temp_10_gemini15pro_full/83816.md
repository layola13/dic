hairy:/ˈhɛəri/ (US), /ˈheəri/ (UK)| adj.|毛茸茸的，多毛的|Covered with hair; having a lot of hair. |例句：The dog was big and hairy. 这只狗又大又毛茸茸的。|例句：He has hairy arms. 他的胳膊毛茸茸的。|近义词：furry, shaggy, woolly|反义词：bald, smooth, hairless|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *hǣrig, from hǣr (hair) + -ig (suffix forming adjectives).  Cognate with German haarig.
