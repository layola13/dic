handywork:/ˈhændiwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈhændɪwɜːk/ (UK)| n. | 手工；手工艺品；（尤指拙劣的）作品 | Something made by hand, especially something showing skill; often used ironically to refer to something clumsily done. |  例句：The intricate carvings are a testament to the artisan's handywork.  这些精美的雕刻证明了工匠的手艺。

|例句：The broken vase was clearly the handywork of the children.  破碎的花瓶显然是孩子们干的“好事”。| 近义词：craft, handiwork, workmanship, creation | 反义词： (No direct antonym, possibly destruction) | 词性: noun
