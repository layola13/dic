career-women's:/kəˈrɪər ˌwɪmɪnz/
|adj.|职业女性的| Belonging to or associated with women who are focused on their careers. | This is a career-women's networking event. 这是一个职业女性的社交活动。| 近义词: professional women's, working women's | 反义词: homemaker's, housewife's | 词性: 形容词


词源:
"career" comes from the French word "carrière" which originally referred to a racecourse for horses and chariots. It later evolved to mean a course of life or profession.
"women" comes from Old English "wifmann," meaning "female human being."
"'s" is a possessive marker, indicating belonging to career women.
