glugged:/ɡlʌɡd/
verb
发出汩汩声；咕嘟咕嘟地喝
make a sound like liquid being poured from a bottle
He glugged down his milk. 他咕咚咕咚地喝下了牛奶。
近义词: gulped, chugged, swallowed
反义词: sipped
词性: verb


glugging:/ˈɡlʌɡɪŋ/
noun
汩汩声；咕嘟咕嘟地喝
the sound of liquid being poured from a bottle
The glugging of the water in the pipes was annoying. 水管里汩汩的流水声很烦人。
近义词: gurgling, sloshing
反义词: dripping
词性: noun
