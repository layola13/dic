obsessed:/əbˈsɛst/| adj.|痴迷的，着迷的|Preoccupied with or constantly thinking about something or someone.|例句：She is obsessed with her weight. 她痴迷于自己的体重。|例句：He's obsessed with the idea of becoming a millionaire. 他一心想成为百万富翁。|近义词：fixated, preoccupied, consumed, captivated|反义词：indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin word *obsessus*, the past participle of *obsidere*, meaning "to besiege" or "to occupy."  The original meaning implied being besieged by evil spirits.
