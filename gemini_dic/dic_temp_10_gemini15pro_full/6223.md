amnesias:/æmˈniːʒəz/
n.
失忆症（复数）
loss of memory, especially of recent events; a period during which someone cannot remember things
例句：He suffered from recurring amnesias after the accident. 事故发生后，他反复出现失忆症。

例句：The amnesias were a side effect of the medication. 这些失忆症是药物的副作用。

近义词：memory loss, forgetfulness
反义词：remembrance, recollection
词性：名词

amnesia:/æmˈniːʒə/
n.
失忆症
loss of memory, especially of recent events; a period during which someone cannot remember things
例句：She suffered from amnesia after the trauma.  她在遭受创伤后患上了失忆症。

例句：The movie explored the theme of amnesia and identity. 这部电影探讨了失忆和身份的主题。

近义词：memory loss, forgetfulness
反义词：remembrance, recollection
词性：名词

词源: From Greek *amnēsia* "forgetfulness," from *amnēs* "forgetful," from *a-* "without" + *mnēmē* "memory."
