condolence:/kənˈdoʊləns/ /ˈkɒndələns/| n.| 慰问，吊唁|An expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death.|We offer our sincere condolences to the family. 我们向家人表示诚挚的慰问。|Synonyms: sympathy, commiseration, compassion|Antonyms: congratulations, felicitations|Noun
