mineralized:/ˈmɪnərəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 矿化的，含矿物质的 | Containing or impregnated with minerals; converted into a mineral substance. | The bones are mineralized, making them strong and rigid. 骨骼被矿化，使它们坚固而僵硬。|  The soil is mineralized with iron. 土壤中富含铁矿物质。 | 近义词: fossilized, petrified, ossified | 反义词: demineralized | 词性: adjective

词源: From mineral + -ize + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *minera "mine, ore."
