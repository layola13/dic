nonproducing:/ˌnɒn prəˈdjuːsɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːn prəˈduːsɪŋ/
adjective
非生产性的；不生产的
Not producing or yielding results, profits, or anything else.
例句：The company decided to sell its nonproducing assets.  公司决定出售其非生产性资产。

例句：The nonproducing oil well was eventually capped. 这口不产油的油井最终被封住了。

近义词：unproductive, barren, sterile, infertile, idle
反义词：productive, fertile, fruitful
词性：adjective
