moniliform:/ˈmɒnɪlɪfɔːm/ /ˈmɑːnɪlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|念珠状的，串珠状的|Resembling a string of beads; arranged like a necklace.|The bacteria formed moniliform chains under the microscope. 在显微镜下，细菌形成了念珠状的链。|Synonyms: beaded, chain-like, necklace-like|Antonyms: continuous, unbroken|adjective

moniliform:/ˈmɒnɪlɪfɔːm/ /ˈmɑːnɪlɪfɔːrm/| n.|念珠状结构|A structure resembling a string of beads.|The moniliform of the root helped identify the plant. 根部的念珠状结构帮助鉴定了这种植物。|Synonyms: beaded structure, chain-like structure|Antonyms: smooth structure, unbroken structure|noun
