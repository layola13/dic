manganiferous:/ˌmæŋɡəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含锰的
Containing manganese.
The manganiferous rock formations are rich in valuable minerals. 这些含锰的岩层富含珍贵的矿物质。
Near the volcano, they discovered manganiferous soil. 在火山附近，他们发现了含锰的土壤。
Synonyms: manganese-bearing, manganese-rich
Antonyms: manganese-deficient, manganese-poor
