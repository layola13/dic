casern:/ˈkæzərn/ /ˈkɑːzərn/| n. |兵营，营房|A building or group of buildings used to house soldiers.|The soldiers returned to their casern after a long day of training. 士兵们经过一天漫长的训练后回到了他们的营房。|Example: The old casern was converted into apartments.  这座旧兵营被改建成了公寓。|Synonyms: barracks, quarters, garrison|Antonyms: home, residence|Noun
