impatientness:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənsnəs/| n. |不耐烦，急躁|The state of being impatient; lack of patience.  The inability or unwillingness to wait.|His impatientness was evident in his constant fidgeting. 他的不耐烦表现在他不停地坐立不安。|Synonyms: restlessness, eagerness, anxiety, hastiness |Antonyms: patience, tolerance, forbearance|noun
