ballet: /ˈbæleɪ/ (US) /ˈbæleɪ/ (UK)| n.|芭蕾舞|A theatrical art form using stylized dance movements and music, usually telling a story.  It originated in the Italian Renaissance courts of the 15th century and later developed in France and Russia as a concert dance form.|She studied ballet for ten years. 她学习芭蕾舞十年了。| dance, performance |opera, play|noun
