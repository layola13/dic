concedes:/kənˈsiːdz/| v.|承认，让步，割让|admits something is true or valid often unwillingly. |例句：He conceded defeat. 他承认失败。

例句：She conceded that she had made a mistake. 她承认自己犯了一个错误。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, yield, surrender|反义词：deny, dispute, refuse, resist|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *conceden*, from Old French *conceder*, from Latin *concedere* ("to yield, grant"), from *con-* ("together, with") + *cedere* ("to go, yield, withdraw").
