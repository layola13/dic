mudslide:/ˈmʌdslaɪd/| n.|泥石流；山体滑坡|A flow of mud and debris down a slope, typically caused by heavy rain or melting snow.|The heavy rain triggered a devastating mudslide that destroyed several homes. 暴雨引发了一场毁灭性的泥石流，摧毁了几座房屋。|例句：The mudslide buried the entire village. 泥石流掩埋了整个村庄。|近义词：landslide, debris flow, avalanche|反义词:(None -  No direct opposite for a natural disaster)|词性:noun
