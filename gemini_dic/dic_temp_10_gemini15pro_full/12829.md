associate:/əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪt/ (US) /əˈsəʊʃieɪt/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|伙伴，同事；联想；联合的|A person united with another or others in an enterprise, interest, or common purpose. ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *associatus, past participle of associare (“to join, unite, attach”), from Latin ad- (“to, towards”) + socius (“companion, ally”).
