allots:/əˈlɒts/ | v. | 分配，拨给 | to give something to someone as a share of a total amount, to use a particular amount of time for something, or to decide that something will be used for a particular purpose | The government has allotted $10 million for education spending this year. 政府今年拨款 1000 万美元用于教育支出。| assigns, distributes, apportions | withholds, denies | verb
allot:/əˈlɒt/ | v. | 分配，拨给 | to give something to someone as a share of a total amount |  They allotted everyone a separate desk. 他们给每个人都分配了一张单独的桌子。| allocate, assign, distribute | withhold, deny | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aloter (recorded only in the past participle aloté), from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + lot ‘share, portion’.
