alternatives:/ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪvz/ | n. | 替代方案，选择 |  Something that can be chosen instead of something else. | We have three alternatives: stay here, go home, or go to the movies. 我们有三个选择：留在这里，回家，或者去看电影。 |  options, choices, possibilities |  limitations, restrictions | noun

alternatives:/ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪvz/ | n. | 备选方案 |  A different plan or method you can use if you do not want to use the first one. |  The alternative was to close the factory. 备选方案是关闭工厂。 | backups, substitutes, replacements  |  primary, main | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from late Latin *alternativus, from Latin alternatus ‘alternating’, from alternare (see alternate).
