overroughnesses:/ˈoʊvərˈrʌfnəsɪz/
noun
过度粗糙；过多的粗糙部分
The excessive roughness or unevenness of a surface.  This refers to a surface having more roughness than is desirable or typical.
The overroughnesses of the road surface made for a bumpy ride. 路面的过度粗糙导致颠簸的行驶。
The overroughnesses on the casting needed to be smoothed out. 铸件上过多的粗糙部分需要磨平。
Synonyms: unevenness, roughness, bumpiness, irregularity
Antonyms: smoothness, evenness, flatness
