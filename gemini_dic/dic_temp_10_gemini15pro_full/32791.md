ETYMOLOGY->
From choiceness + -es (plural suffix).  Choiceness is derived from choice + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives). Choice comes from Old French chois, ultimately from Latin cadere "to fall," with a sense of "that which falls to one's lot."
