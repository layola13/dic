cartable: /kɑːrˈtɑːbl/ (UK), /kɑːrˈteɪbl/ (US)
n.
schoolbag, satchel
A bag used by schoolchildren to carry books and other supplies.
例句：
He packed his cartable with books and pencils.
他把书和铅笔装进了书包。

例句：
She forgot her cartable at home.
她把书包忘在家里了。

近义词：schoolbag, satchel, backpack, book bag
反义词: None (No single antonym for a physical object like a schoolbag)
词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cartable*, from *carte* (card, paper) + *-able* (suffix indicating suitability for).  Originally referred to a portfolio for carrying papers.
