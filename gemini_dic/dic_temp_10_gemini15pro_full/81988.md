gringos:/ˈɡrɪŋɡoʊz/ (US), /ˈɡrɪŋɡəʊz/ (UK)| n. |外国佬；老外（尤指讲英语的外国人，通常带有贬义）|A term, often derogatory, used in Latin America and parts of the southwestern United States to refer to foreigners, especially English speakers, and particularly from the United States.|The locals called the tourists "gringos." 当地人称那些游客为“外国佬”。|synonyms: foreigners, outsiders, aliens (more formal), yankees (specifically for US citizens)|antonyms: locals, natives|noun
