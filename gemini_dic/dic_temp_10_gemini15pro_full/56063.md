docilely:/ˈdɒsɪlli/ (US) /ˈdɑːsɪlli/ (UK)| adv.|温顺地，驯服地，顺从地|in a way that shows you are willing to be taught or controlled; obediently|The horse docilely accepted the bridle. 这匹马温顺地接受了缰绳。|Synonyms: obediently, submissively, meekly, compliantly|Antonyms: rebelliously, defiantly, stubbornly|Part of Speech: Adverb

docile:/ˈdɒsaɪl/ (US) /ˈdɒsaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|温顺的，驯服的，顺从的|ready and willing to be taught or controlled; obedient|She has a docile nature. 她性情温顺。|Synonyms: obedient, submissive, meek, compliant|Antonyms: rebellious, defiant, stubborn|Part of Speech: Adjective
