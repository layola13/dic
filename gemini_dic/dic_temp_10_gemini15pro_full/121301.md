misfitting:/ˌmɪsˈfɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不合身的; 不合适的; 格格不入的
not fitting properly; unsuitable; out of place.
The suit was misfitting and made him look awkward. 这套西装不合身，让他看起来很笨拙。
近义词: ill-fitting, unsuitable, inappropriate, incongruous, out of place
反义词: fitting, suitable, appropriate, congruous, belonging

misfitting:/ˌmɪsˈfɪtɪŋ/
noun
格格不入的人; 不适应环境的人
a person who is not accepted by a particular group of people or by society in general.
He felt like a misfitting in the corporate world. 他觉得自己在企业界格格不入。
近义词: outcast, outsider, nonconformist, maverick
反义词: conformist, insider

词性: 可以作形容词和名词

词源:  From mis- + fitting, from fit (v.).
