awesomely:/ˈɔːsəmli/ | adv. | 极好地，非常地，令人惊叹地 | In a way that inspires awe.  She sang awesomely at the concert. |例句：He played awesomely in the game. 他在比赛中表现得非常出色。

例句：The mountains were awesomely beautiful.  群山壮丽无比。| 近义词：incredibly, amazingly, wonderfully, fantastically | 反义词：terribly, awfully, poorly | 词性: 副词

词源: From "awesome" + "-ly." "Awesome" comes from "awe" (from Old Norse "agi," meaning "fear, terror") + "-some" (a suffix meaning "causing, characterized by").  The meaning shifted over time from inspiring fear to inspiring wonder.
