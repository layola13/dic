outdeliver:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈlɪ.vər/
verb
超越交付；在交付方面胜过
To deliver more or better than someone else.
Our company outdelivered our competitors by a wide margin this quarter. 本季度我们公司的交付量远远超过了竞争对手。
近义词: outperform, surpass, exceed
反义词: underperform, lag behind
词性: verb

There are not many common set phrases that use "outdeliver".  It's primarily used as a standalone verb. However, you could see it used in phrases like:

Consistently outdeliver the competition  持续超越竞争对手
