longings:/ˈlɒŋɪŋz/ (US) /ˈlɒŋɪŋz/ (UK)| n. |渴望， yearning; 愿望，desire |A feeling of intense desire or yearning for someone or something.|She felt a deep longing for her homeland. 她对故乡充满了渴望。|近义词：yearnings, desires, wishes, cravings, hankerings|反义词：indifference, apathy|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *longyng, from Old English *langung, from the verb langian (“to yearn, long for”). Cognate with West Frisian langing (“longing”), Dutch langing (“longing”), German Langung (“longing”).
