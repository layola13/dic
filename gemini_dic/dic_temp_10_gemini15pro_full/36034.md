codiscoverer:/ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvərər/| n. |共同发现者|A person who discovers something along with another person or people.| Marie Curie and her husband, Pierre, were codiscoverers of radium. 玛丽·居里和她的丈夫皮埃尔是镭的共同发现者。|collaborator, joint discoverer|  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "independent discoverer")|noun
