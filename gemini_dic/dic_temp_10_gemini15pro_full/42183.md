couleur:/kuːlœʁ/ (French)| n.| 颜色 |A French word meaning "color". It is often used in English in artistic or literary contexts to convey a more nuanced or sophisticated sense of color.| The painting captured the subtle couleur of the twilight sky. 这幅画捕捉到了暮色天空微妙的色彩。 | 近义词: color, hue, shade, tint, tone | 反义词: (No direct opposite for color itself; could consider concepts like colorless, achromatic, monochrome) | 词性: Noun

词源:
From Old French colour, from Latin colōrem (nominative color), accusative of color "color, complexion, appearance".
