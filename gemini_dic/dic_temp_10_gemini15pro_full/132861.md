noneffusively:/ˌnɒnɪˈfjuːsɪvli/
adverb
不热情地，冷淡地
In a way that is not effusive; restrained or reserved in expression of feeling.
She thanked him noneffusively for his help. 她冷淡地感谢了他的帮助。
近义词: reservedly, restrainedly, undemonstratively, quietly
反义词: effusively, enthusiastically, warmly
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + effusive + -ly.  "Effusive" comes from the Latin word "effusus," meaning "poured out" or "shed forth."  Therefore, "noneffusively" means "not poured out" or "not shed forth" in terms of emotion.
