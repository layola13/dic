disquietness:/dɪsˈkwaɪətnəs/
n.
不安; 焦躁; 忧虑
A state of uneasiness or anxiety.
The disquietness in her voice betrayed her fear. 她声音里的不安暴露了她的恐惧。
uneasiness, anxiety, restlessness, perturbation, distress
calm, peace, tranquility, composure
noun

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/
vt.
使不安; 使焦虑
To make someone worried or uneasy.
The news disquieted her. 这消息使她不安。
n.
不安; 焦虑
A feeling of worry or unease.
She felt a growing disquiet about the situation. 她对局势越来越感到不安。
worry, disturb, upset, bother
calm, soothe, reassure
