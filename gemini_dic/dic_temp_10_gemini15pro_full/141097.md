onshore:/ˈɑːnʃɔːr/ /ˈɒnʃɔːr/
adjective
陆上的；向岸的；在岸上的
Located or occurring on land; returning or coming to the shore.
The onshore wind blew the sand towards the houses. 陆上的风把沙子吹向房屋。

onshore:/ˈɑːnʃɔːr/ /ˈɒnʃɔːr/
adverb
向岸地；在岸上
Towards the shore; on land.
The lifeboat struggled onshore. 救生艇艰难地向岸边驶去。

onshore:/ˈɑːnʃɔːr/ /ˈɒnʃɔːr/
noun
国内，陆地
the land
Onshore investments are considered safer. 国内投资被认为更安全。

Etymology:
From on + shore.  "Shore" comes from Old English "scora," meaning "shore, coast, cliff."

近义词:coastal, inland
反义词:offshore

词性: adjective, adverb, noun
