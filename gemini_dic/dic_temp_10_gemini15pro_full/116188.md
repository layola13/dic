mashie:/ˈmæʃi/| n. | 马什杆（一种高尔夫球杆）; 土豆泥 |A golf club with a short, lofted head, used for short approaches to the green; mashed potatoes. |He used his mashie to chip the ball onto the green. 他用他的马什杆将球切上果岭。| 近义词: lofted iron, niblick (for golf club), mashed potatoes (for food) | 反义词: driver (loosely, as opposite type of golf club) | 词性:noun


mashie:/ˈmæʃi/| n. |土豆泥| Mashed potatoes, often served as a side dish. | We had steak and mashie for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了牛排和土豆泥。| 近义词: mashed potatoes | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for the food meaning) | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *mashie*, perhaps a diminutive of *mash*.  Originally referred to a tool for mashing, and then to a golf club with a lofted head used for lofting the ball like mashed potatoes.
