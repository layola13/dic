creakily:/ˈkriːkɪli/| adv.|吱吱作响地，嘎吱嘎吱地|Making a harsh, grating sound, like that of an old door or wooden floor.|The old stairs creaked ominously as she climbed them. 她爬上楼梯时，老旧的楼梯发出不祥的吱吱声。|The rusty gate creakily swung open. 生锈的大门嘎吱嘎吱地打开了。|近义词：gratingly, squeakily, shrilly|反义词:smoothly, silently|词性:副词
Etymology: From creaky + -ly.  "Creaky" comes from the Middle English "creken," of imitative origin.
