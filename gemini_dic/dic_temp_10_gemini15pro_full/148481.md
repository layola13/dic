overstrew:/ˌoʊvərˈstruː/ /ˌəʊvəˈstruː/ | vt. | 散布，撒满 | To scatter or spread something over a surface; to cover something with something else. | The ground was overstrewn with leaves. 地上撒满了树叶。

The table was overstrewn with papers. 桌子上堆满了文件。

| 近义词：scatter, sprinkle, strew, cover | 反义词：clear, gather | 词性：动词
