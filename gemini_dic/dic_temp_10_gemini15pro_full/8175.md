anti-darwinist:/ˌæntiˈdɑːrwɪnɪst/ | n. | 反达尔文主义者；反对达尔文进化论的人 |  A person who opposes or rejects Darwin's theory of evolution by natural selection. |  He was a staunch anti-Darwinist, believing in creationism. 他是一位坚定的反达尔文主义者，信仰神创论。 |  opponent of evolution, creationist |  Darwinist, evolutionist | noun

anti-darwinist:/ˌæntiˈdɑːrwɪnɪst/ | adj. | 反达尔文主义的；反对达尔文进化论的 | Relating to or characteristic of opposition to Darwin's theory of evolution. |  His anti-Darwinist views were often met with criticism. 他的反达尔文主义观点经常遭到批评。|  creationist, anti-evolutionary |  Darwinian, evolutionary | adjective
