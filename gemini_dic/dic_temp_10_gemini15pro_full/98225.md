insecution:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 迫害；烦扰 | The constant insecution from the paparazzi drove the actress to seclusion.  狗仔队不断的骚扰迫使这位女演员隐居。|  例句：The refugees fled their homeland due to religious insecution. 这些难民因宗教迫害逃离了祖国。

例句：He suffered years of insecution for his political beliefs.  他因其政治信仰遭受了多年的迫害。 | 近义词：persecution, harassment, torment | 反义词：protection, support | 词性: 名词


词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin  *insecution- ‘pursuit’, from the verb  insequi, from in- ‘into, upon’ + sequi ‘to follow’.
