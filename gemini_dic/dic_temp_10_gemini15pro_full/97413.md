infringing:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
侵犯的，违反的
Actively violating a law or right.
例句：The company was accused of infringing on its competitor's patents.  该公司被指控侵犯其竞争对手的专利。

例句：His use of the copyrighted material was deemed infringing. 他对受版权保护的材料的使用被认为是侵权的。

近义词：violating, breaching, trespassing, contravening
反义词：respecting, upholding, complying with
词性: adjective


infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/
verb
侵犯，违反
To act so as to intrude on or violate something.
例句：They infringed on his personal space. 他们侵犯了他的私人空间。

例句：Building the factory here would infringe on environmental regulations. 在这里建厂会违反环境法规。

近义词：violate, breach, trespass, contravene
反义词：respect, uphold, comply with
词性: verb

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French enfringier, based on Latin infringere ‘break’, from in- ‘into’ + frangere ‘break’.
