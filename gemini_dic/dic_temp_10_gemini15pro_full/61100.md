elocution:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃən/ |n.|雄辩；演讲术；朗诵法|The art of clear and expressive speech, especially of distinct pronunciation and articulation.|His elocution was impeccable, captivating the audience with every word. 他的演讲技巧无可挑剔，每个字都吸引着观众。|Synonyms: oratory, rhetoric, articulation, delivery|Antonyms: inarticulacy, mumbling|noun

elocutionary:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɛri/ |adj.|雄辩的；口才好的|Relating to or characterized by elocution; expressive.|Her elocutionary skills impressed everyone at the debate. 她的演讲技巧给辩论中的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|Synonyms: articulate, eloquent, fluent, expressive|Antonyms: inarticulate, mumbled, unclear|adjective
