blamed: /bleɪmd/ | v. | 指责，责备 |  to say or think that someone or something did something wrong or is responsible for something bad happening | He blamed his poor performance on lack of sleep. 他把糟糕的表现归咎于睡眠不足。 |  accused, criticized, faulted | praised, commended | verb

blamed: /bleɪmd/ | adj. | 被责备的 |  Used to describe someone or something that has been assigned responsibility for a fault or wrongdoing. This usage is less common than the verb form but appears frequently in passive constructions.  | The blamed party denied any involvement. 被指责的一方否认有任何参与。 | accused, criticized, faulted | praised, commended | adjective
