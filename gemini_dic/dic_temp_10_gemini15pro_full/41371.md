corkscrewing:/ˈkɔːrkskruːɪŋ/| adj.  n. |螺旋形的，开瓶器式的; 螺旋式运动|Moving in a spiral or twisting motion, like a corkscrew.  The plane began corkscrewing downwards.|近义词：spiralling, twisting, whirling, twirling|反义词:straightening, uncoiling|词性: adjective, noun (gerund/present participle)
Etymology: From "corkscrew" + "-ing". "Corkscrew" itself comes from "cork" + "screw".

corkscrew:/ˈkɔːrkskruː/| n. v. |开瓶器；螺旋钻; 用开瓶器打开|A tool for removing corks from bottles; to open a bottle with a corkscrew.  He used a corkscrew to open the wine. / The plane corkscrewed through the air. |近义词：bottle opener (n.), spiral, helix (n.)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb
