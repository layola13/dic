mitigant:/ˈmɪtɪɡənt/| adj. n.|减轻的，缓和的；缓和剂|Something that mitigates, or makes less severe.  A mitigating factor or circumstance.|The judge took his age into account as a mitigating factor in sentencing. 法官将他的年龄作为量刑中的一个减轻罪行的因素。|近义词：alleviating, palliative, assuaging, relieving|反义词：aggravating, exacerbating|词性:adjective, noun

mitigate:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/| v.|减轻，缓和|To make less severe, serious, or painful.|The government is trying to mitigate the effects of the recession. 政府正在努力减轻经济衰退的影响。|近义词：alleviate, lessen, reduce, diminish|反义词：aggravate, worsen, exacerbate|词性:verb


词源:
From Latin mitigatus, past participle of mitigare (“to soften, make mild”), from mitis (“mild, soft”) + agere (“to do, drive, act”).
