outdistancing:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstənsɪŋ/
verb
超越，把…远远抛在后面
surpassing someone or something by a large margin in a race, competition, or other endeavor.
The Kenyan runner outdistanced his rivals in the marathon.  肯尼亚选手在马拉松比赛中远远超过了他的对手。
outpace, surpass, outrun, leave behind
lag behind, trail, fall behind
v.
