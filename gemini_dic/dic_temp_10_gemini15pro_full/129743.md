non-essential:/ˌnɒnɪˈsenʃəl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnɪˈsenʃəl/ (US)| adj. n.|非必要的；不重要的；非必需品|Not absolutely necessary; not vital.  Also used as a noun to refer to something that is non-essential.|例句：During the lockdown, only essential businesses were allowed to open.  封锁期间，只有必要的企业才被允许营业。

例句：Luxury items are considered non-essentials. 奢侈品被认为是非必需品。|近义词：unnecessary, noncritical, dispensable, inessential|反义词：essential, necessary, crucial, vital|词性:形容词, 名词
