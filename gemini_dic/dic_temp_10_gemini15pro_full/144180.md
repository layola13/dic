outshrills:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪlz/
verb
发出比…更大的喊叫声；盖过…的喊叫声
to shout louder than someone or something else
The protestors outshrilled the police sirens. 抗议者的喊叫声盖过了警笛声。
近义词: outshout, drown out
反义词: be drowned out, be outshouted
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + shrill.  "Out-" prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing. "Shrill" meaning high-pitched and piercing.
