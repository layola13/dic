devisee:/ˌdɛvɪˈziː/ /ˌdiːvaɪˈziː/
n.
受遗赠不动产的人
a person who inherits real property under a will
例句：The devisee of the estate is her youngest son.  这片地产的受遗赠人是她的幼子。

例句：The devisee was surprised to learn about the extent of the property she inherited. 受遗赠人惊讶地得知她继承的财产范围。

近义词：heir, beneficiary, inheritor
反义词:testator (遗嘱人)
词性:noun
