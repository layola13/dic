hyperimmunization:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪmjənɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. | 超免疫 | The process of inducing a heightened immune response, typically by administering repeated and increasing doses of an antigen.  This results in unusually high antibody levels.| The patient underwent hyperimmunization for rabies after being bitten by a wild animal.  病人被野生动物咬伤后接受了狂犬病超免疫治疗。| 近义词: immunization, vaccination | 反义词: immunosuppression | 词性: noun

hyperimmunization:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪmjənɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. | 超免疫 | The act of producing a very high level of immunity in a person or animal, usually by giving repeated injections of an antigen.  This process is often used to create antisera for therapeutic use. |  Hyperimmunization of horses is used to produce antivenom.  对马进行超免疫以产生抗蛇毒血清。 | 近义词: immunization, vaccination | 反义词: immunosuppression | 词性: noun
