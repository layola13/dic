nonirritableness:/ˌnɒnɪrɪtəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
无刺激性; 不易怒性; 温和
lack of irritability; gentleness.
The doctor praised the patient's nonirritableness despite the painful procedure. 尽管手术很痛苦，医生还是称赞了病人的温和。
Synonyms: calmness, placidity, tranquility, gentleness, mildness, patience
Antonyms: irritability, irascibility, excitability, petulance, testiness, impatience
noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + irritability + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Irritability itself comes from Latin irritabilis "irritable," from irritare "to excite, provoke," from irritus "invalid, void."
