nonprophetical:/ˌnɑːnprəˈfɛtɪkəl/
adjective
非预言的，不具预言性质的
Not prophetic; not containing or resembling prophecy.
Her novel is a nonprophetical account of future possibilities. 她的小说是对未来可能性的非预言性描述。
Near Synonyms: nonpredictive, secular, mundane
Antonyms: prophetic, predictive, apocalyptic
词性: 形容词
