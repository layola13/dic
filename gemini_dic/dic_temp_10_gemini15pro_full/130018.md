non-onerous:/ˌnɒnˈəʊnərəs/ | adj. | 非繁重的，不费力的 | Not burdensome or troublesome; easy. |  This job is non-onerous and leaves me plenty of free time. 这份工作不繁重，让我有很多空闲时间。| The terms of the agreement were non-onerous and easily met. 协议的条款并不繁重，很容易满足。| 近义词: easy, light, simple, undemanding, manageable | 反义词: onerous, burdensome, difficult, strenuous, demanding | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + onerous.  "Onerous" comes from Late Latin onerosus "burdensome," from Latin onus "burden."
