dandiest:/ˈdændiɪst/| adj.|最衣冠楚楚的，最花哨的|Superlative form of dandy: most dandy.  Meaning extremely fashionable and stylish, often in a flamboyant or showy way.|He was the dandiest man at the party, with a bright purple suit and a feather in his hat. 他是派对上最衣冠楚楚的男人，穿着一套亮紫色的西装，帽子里插着一根羽毛。|近义词：smartest, most fashionable, most stylish|反义词：scruffiest, shabbiest|词性:形容词
