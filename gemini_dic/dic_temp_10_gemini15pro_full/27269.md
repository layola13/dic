cals:/kælz/ | n. | calories (卡路里) |  A unit of energy, often used to measure the energy content of food.  It's short for "calories".  Specifically, it refers to a kilocalorie (kcal), which is the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of one kilogram of water by one degree Celsius. | I'm trying to cut down on cals.  我正在努力减少卡路里摄入。|  calories, kcal | kJ (kilojoules) | noun
