cellule:/seˈlul/ /sɛˈlul/| n. |细胞; 小房间; 小组; 电池|A small cavity or compartment, as in a honeycomb or a plant or animal.  The basic structural and functional unit of all organisms; the smallest unit of life. A small group forming the nucleus of a larger political or religious movement. A small unit of an organization, especially a clandestine one.  |例句：The biologist studied the cellule under the microscope.  生物学家在显微镜下研究细胞。

例句：The monk lived in a small cellule.  僧侣住在一个小房间里。|近义词：cell, compartment, chamber, cavity, unit, group|反义词: organism (in the sense of a whole being)|noun
