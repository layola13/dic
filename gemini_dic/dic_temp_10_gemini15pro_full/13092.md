astronomers:/əˈstrɒnəməz/
n.
天文学家
Scientists who study celestial objects, space, and the physical universe as a whole.
Astronomers have discovered a new planet. 天文学家发现了一颗新的行星。
Astronomers use telescopes to observe distant galaxies. 天文学家使用望远镜观察遥远的星系。
近义词:astrophysicists, cosmologists
反义词:N/A
词性:Noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English astronomere, from Old French astronomien, from Latin astronomus, from Ancient Greek ἀστρονόμος (astronomos, “astronomer”), from ἄστρον (astron, “star”) + νόμος (nomos, “law, custom; district, province; melody, song”).
