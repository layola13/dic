crossbarred:/ˈkrɒsbɑːrd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbɑːrd/ (US)| adj.| 交叉的，有横档的，有格子的| Marked with bars crossing each other; having crossbars. | The window was crossbarred. 窗户上有横档。| The old jail had crossbarred doors. 老监狱的门上有横木。|近义词：barred, grated, latticed|反义词: open, unobstructed|词性: adjective

crossbar:/ˈkrɒsbɑːr/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbɑːr/ (US)| n.| 横木，横档| A horizontal bar, beam, or piece. | The goalkeeper leaped and tipped the ball over the crossbar. 守门员跳起来，把球拨过了球门横梁。| The crossbar on the gate was broken. 大门上的横木断了。|近义词：bar, beam, rail|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross- + barred, past participle of bar (“to fasten with a bar”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *barra (“bar”).
