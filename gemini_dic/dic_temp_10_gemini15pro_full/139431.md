obsessives:/əbˈsɛsɪvz/
noun
强迫症患者；痴迷者
People who are obsessives are preoccupied with something or someone.
He's one of those fitness obsessives who goes to the gym every day. 他是那种每天都去健身房的健身狂。
synonyms: fanatics, addicts, zealots
antonyms: moderates, casuals
词性: 名词
