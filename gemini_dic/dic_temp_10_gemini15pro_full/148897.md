overwhelm: /ˌoʊvərˈwelm/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈwelm/ (UK)
verb
(使)不知所措，(使)难以承受；压倒；淹没
to affect someone very strongly, especially with a negative feeling, so that they cannot think clearly
The beauty of the landscape overwhelmed me.  这片美景让我不知所措。
He was overwhelmed by grief when his wife died.  妻子去世后，他悲痛欲绝。
The city was overwhelmed by the floodwaters.  城市被洪水淹没了。
synonym: overpower, subdue, engulf, inundate
antonym: underwhelm, encourage, support
词性: 动词
