geostationary:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsteɪʃəneri/| adj.|地球静止的，对地静止的|Relating to a satellite that orbits the Earth at the same speed as the Earth rotates, so that it seems to stay in the same place above the Earth.|例句：A geostationary satellite remains fixed in the sky relative to a point on the earth's surface. 一颗地球静止卫星相对于地球表面上的一个点固定在天空中。

例句：Geostationary orbits are useful for communication satellites. 地球静止轨道对通信卫星很有用。|近义词：geosynchronous|反义词:|词性:adjective
