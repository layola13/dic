nonentomologic:/ˌnɒnˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪk/
adjective
非昆虫学的
Not pertaining to entomology; not related to the study of insects.
The museum's nonentomologic collection includes minerals and fossils.  博物馆的非昆虫学藏品包括矿物和化石。
Near synonyms: non-insectological
Antonyms: entomological
词性: adjective
