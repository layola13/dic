macrameed:/ˌmæk.rəˈmiːd/ | adj. | 用绳结编织的 | Made using the macrame technique, which involves knotting cords or strings to create decorative items. |  She wore a macrameed vest. 她穿着一件绳结编织的背心。| 近义词: knotted, woven, braided | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider: unknotted, plain) | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From macrame (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives from nouns). Macrame itself comes from Turkish *makrama, from Arabic migramah 'embroidered veil'.
