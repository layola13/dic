forthputting:/fɔːrθˈpʊtɪŋ/ | adj. | 直率的；外向的；敢于表达的 |  Forthputting describes someone who is direct, outspoken, and assertive in expressing their opinions and feelings.  It implies a willingness to communicate openly and honestly, sometimes even to the point of being blunt. | She was forthputting in her criticism of the new policy. 她对新政策的批评直言不讳。

He's not very forthputting, so you'll have to ask him directly what he thinks. 他不太坦率，所以你得直接问他的想法。 | 近义词: outspoken, frank, direct, candid, assertive | 反义词: reserved, reticent, shy, timid | 词性: adjective
