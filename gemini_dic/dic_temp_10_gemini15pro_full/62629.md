enforcers: /ɪnˈfɔːrsərz/ | n. | 执行者；强制执行人；打手 |  A person who enforces something, such as a law, rule, or contract, or who uses force to persuade someone to do something. | The company hired enforcers to collect debts. 公司雇佣了执行者来收债。|  The government sent enforcers to ensure the regulations were followed. 政府派执法人员确保法规得到遵守。 | Synonyms: executor, implementer, officer, agent, thug, muscle | Antonyms: lawbreaker, violator | noun
