concentricity:/ˌkɒnsɛnˈtrɪsɪti/ /ˌkɑːnsɛnˈtrɪsɪti/| n.|同心度，同轴度|The state or condition of having a common center; the quality of being concentric.  The degree to which two or more objects share the same center.|The machinist checked the concentricity of the gears. 机械师检查了齿轮的同心度。|Synonyms: coaxiality, alignment, centeredness|Antonyms: eccentricity, off-centeredness|noun
