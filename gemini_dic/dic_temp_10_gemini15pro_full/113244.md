lustier:/ˈlʌstiər/ | adj. | 更强壮的，更健康的，更有活力的，更旺盛的 |  Comparative adjective of lusty: more lusty.  Having or showing strong sexual desires; vigorous and healthy. |  The plants looked lustier after the rain. 雨后植物看起来更有生机。 | 近义词:healthier, stronger, more vigorous, robust, sturdier | 反义词:weaker, feebler, less vigorous, sickly | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *lustiere, comparative of lusty (“pleasant, beautiful, joyful; vigorous, strong, healthy; lustful”), from Old French lastif, lusti (“joyful”), from Vulgar Latin *lustīvus, from Latin laetus (“happy, joyful”) + -īvus (suffix forming adjectives).
