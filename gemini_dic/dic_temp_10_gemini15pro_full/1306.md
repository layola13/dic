accoutre:/əˈkuːtər/| vt.|装扮，打扮，装备|To clothe or equip, typically in something noticeable or impressive.|The knights were accoutred in shining armor. 骑士们穿着闪亮的盔甲。

The actress was accoutred in a stunning gown.  这位女演员穿着一件惊艳的礼服。|近义词：attire, equip, outfit, array, garb, deck out|反义词：undress, disrobe|动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acoustrer*, from *coustre* "sewing," from Latin *consuere* "to sew together."
