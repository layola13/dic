chanukah:/ˈhɑːnəkə/ /ˈxɑːnəkə/| n. |光明节|An eight-day Jewish festival commemorating the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem at the time of the Maccabean Revolt against the Seleucid Empire.| We celebrate Chanukah by lighting the menorah. 我们通过点燃烛台来庆祝光明节。|Synonym:Festival of Lights|Antonym:N/A|noun
