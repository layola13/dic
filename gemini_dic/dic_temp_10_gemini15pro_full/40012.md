consultum:/kənˈsʌltəm/ | n. | decree, decision, resolution, thing deliberated upon |  A formal decision, opinion, or decree, often reached after deliberation.  It comes from the Latin verb "consulere," meaning "to consult." | The consultum of the council was to declare war.  议会的决议是宣战。 | The Senate issued a consultum on the matter. 参议院就此问题发布了一项决议。 |  decision, resolution, decree, judgment, finding, verdict | ruling, order | noun
