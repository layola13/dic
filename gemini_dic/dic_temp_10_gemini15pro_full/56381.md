dolce:/ˈdoltʃeɪ/ /ˈdɔːltʃeɪ/| adj. n.| 甜的，甜点；甜蜜，温柔|Sweet, soft; a dessert or sweet.  A term used in music direction to indicate a sweet and soft manner of performance.|The dessert was dolce and creamy. 这道甜点香甜柔滑。|近义词:sweet, mellow, soft, gentle|反义词:bitter, harsh, sour|词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *dolce*, from Latin *dulcis* "sweet," from PIE root *dlk-wi-* "sweet."
