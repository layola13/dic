forswearers:/fɔːrˈswɛːrərz/
n.
放弃者；发誓戒绝者
People who have sworn to renounce or give up something, especially formally or under oath.
The forswearers of violence gathered to protest the war.  放弃暴力的人聚集起来抗议战争。
He was amongst the forswearers of alcohol. 他是戒酒者之一。
近义词：renouncers, abstainers, repudiators
反义词：advocates, supporters, adherents
词性: 名词


词源:
From Middle English *forswēren, from Old English forswęrian (“to swear falsely, perjure oneself; abjure, renounce”), from for- + swęrian (“to swear”).
