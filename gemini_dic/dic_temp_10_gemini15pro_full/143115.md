outbulked:/aʊtˈbʌlkt/
verb
超越，在体积上超过
surpass in bulk; exceed in volume or mass.
China has outbulked the US in steel production. 中国的钢铁产量已经超过了美国。
outweigh, outstrip, surpass, exceed
--- (no clear antonym, perhaps be smaller than)
