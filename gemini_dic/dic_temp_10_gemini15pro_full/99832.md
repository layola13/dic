intermission:/ˌɪntərˈmɪʃn/ | n. | 中场休息；幕间休息；暂停；间歇 | A pause or break in activity, especially between parts of a performance or game. | The play had a 15-minute intermission.  这出戏有15分钟的幕间休息。|  The teacher called for a five-minute intermission. 老师要求休息五分钟。 | synonyms: break, pause, interval, recess | antonyms: continuation, performance, play | noun
