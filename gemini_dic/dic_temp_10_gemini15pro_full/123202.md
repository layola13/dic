monochromatic:/ˌmɑːnəkrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. |单色的，单频的| Containing or using only one color or shades of one color.  Characterized by the presence of radiation of only one wavelength.|  The artist created a monochromatic painting using only shades of blue.  这位艺术家创作了一幅只用蓝色调的单色画。| synonyms: monotone, single-colored, one-color | antonyms: polychromatic, multicolored, varicolored | adjective

monochrome:/ˈmɑːnəkroʊm/ | n. adj. |单色，单色的| A photograph or picture developed or executed in black and white or in varying tones of only one color.  Relating to or consisting of one color or shades of one color. | The photographer specialized in monochrome portraits. 这位摄影师擅长拍摄单色肖像。 | synonyms: monotone, single-colored, one-color | antonyms: polychrome, multicolored, varicolored | noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mónos* (single, alone) + *khrôma* (color).
