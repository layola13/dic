mallards:/ˈmælərdz/| n.|野鸭（复数）|A common wild duck (Anas platyrhynchos) known for its iridescent green head (males) and mottled brown plumage (females).|例句：A flock of mallards landed on the pond. 一群野鸭落在池塘上。|近义词：ducks, wild ducks|反义词:|noun

mallard:/ˈmælərd/| n.|野鸭（单数）|A common wild duck (Anas platyrhynchos) known for its iridescent green head (males) and mottled brown plumage (females).|例句：The mallard dabbled in the shallows. 野鸭在浅滩上觅食。|近义词：duck, wild duck|反义词:|noun
