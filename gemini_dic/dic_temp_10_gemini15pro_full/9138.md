antinarrative:/ˌæntiˈnærətɪv/
|n.|反叙事，非叙事|A type of narrative that deliberately avoids or subverts traditional narrative conventions, such as plot, character development, and linear timelines.|The film's antinarrative structure made it challenging to follow, but also incredibly intriguing. 这部电影的反叙事结构让它难以理解，但也非常引人入胜。|
|近义词：non-narrative, unconventional narrative, experimental narrative|反义词：narrative, conventional narrative, linear narrative|词性：名词|

antinarrative:/ˌæntiˈnærətɪv/
|adj.|反叙事的，非叙事的|Characterized by the avoidance or subversion of traditional narrative conventions.|The director's antinarrative style is evident in his fragmented and dreamlike films. 导演的反叙事风格体现在他支离破碎和梦幻般的电影中。|近义词：non-narrative, unconventional, experimental|反义词：narrative, conventional, linear|词性：形容词|
