deader:/ˈdɛdər/| adj.|更死的，更加没有生气的|Comparative form of dead; more dead.  Indicates a greater degree of lifelessness, often used hyperbolically.|例句：After that workout, I felt deader than a doornail.  在那次锻炼之后，我觉得比门钉还死。

例句：This town is even deader than it was last year. 这个镇比去年更加死气沉沉了。|近义词：more lifeless, more inanimate, deceased, expired|反义词：more alive, livelier, more vital|词性：形容词

词源：From Old English *deadra, comparative of dead.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *daudaz.
