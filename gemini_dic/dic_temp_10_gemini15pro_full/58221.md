druggiest:/ˈdrʌɡiɪst/| adj.|最沉迷于毒品的；最像吸毒者的|Most resembling or characteristic of a drug addict; most suggestive of drug use.  Relating to or involved with the illicit use of drugs, especially in a way that is perceived as excessive or problematic.| He had the druggiest look in his eyes. 他的眼神看起来非常像吸毒者。|Near Synonyms: strung-out, doped-up, high, stoned, wasted. |Antonyms: sober, clean.|adjective
