ferreted:/ˈferɪtɪd/
verb
搜寻出;  ferret out
He ferreted out the information from the files. 他从文件中搜寻出了信息。
近义词: unearthed, discovered, uncovered, hunted down, rooted out
反义词: concealed, hid, obscured
词性: verb

ferreted:/ˈferɪtɪd/
adjective
(having been) searched out
搜寻出的
The ferreted information proved crucial to the case.  搜寻出的信息对案件至关重要。
近义词: discovered, found, unearthed
反义词: hidden, concealed
词性: adjective


词源: From the noun "ferret," a small, weasel-like carnivorous mammal used for hunting rabbits and rats, from Old French *furet, diminutive of furon "thief," from Latin fur "thief."  The verb sense developed from the ferret's behavior of hunting and driving out prey from burrows.
