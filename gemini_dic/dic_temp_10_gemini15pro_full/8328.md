anti-pestilence:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪləns/ | adj. | 防疫的，抗瘟疫的 | Preventing or combating pestilence (a deadly epidemic disease). |  The anti-pestilence measures included quarantining the infected. 防疫措施包括隔离感染者。| synonyms: anti-epidemic, prophylactic, preventative | antonyms: pestilential, contagious, infectious | adjective

anti-pestilence:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪləns/ | n. | 防疫措施，抗瘟疫物 | Something that prevents or combats pestilence. | The doctor recommended various anti-pestilences, including herbs and good hygiene. 医生推荐了各种防疫措施，包括草药和良好的卫生习惯。 | synonyms: preventative measure, prophylactic, remedy | antonyms: contagion, infection, disease | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- "against" + pestilence "deadly epidemic disease."  "Pestilence" comes from Old French pestilence, from Latin pestilentia "plague, pestilence," from pestilens "unhealthy, noxious," present participle of pestere "to annoy, trouble."
