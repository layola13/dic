noncorruptibilities:/ˌnɒnkərʌptɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
廉洁性，不可腐蚀性
The quality or state of being incorruptible; incapable of being corrupted, bribed, or morally compromised.
The judge was known for his noncorruptibility and impartiality.  这位法官以其廉洁公正而闻名。
The system's noncorruptibility ensured fair elections. 该系统的不可腐蚀性确保了选举的公平。
近义词:incorruptibility, integrity, probity, honesty, uprightness
反义词:corruptibility, dishonesty, venality
词性:名词(plural)
