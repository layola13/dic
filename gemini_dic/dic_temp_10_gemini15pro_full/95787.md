incontrovertible:/ˌɪnkɒntrəˈvɜːtɪbl/ | /ˌɪnkɑːntrəˈvɜːrtəbl/ | adj. | 不容置疑的；无可辩驳的 | Not able to be denied or disputed. |  The evidence against him was incontrovertible.  他面临的证据是无可辩驳的。

The photographs provided incontrovertible proof of his guilt.  这些照片提供了无可辩驳的罪证。 | 近义词: indisputable, undeniable, irrefutable, unquestionable | 反义词: disputable, debatable, questionable, dubious | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *incontrovertibilis, from in- "not" + controversus "disputed, turned against," past participle of controvertere "to turn against, dispute," from contro- "against" + vertere "to turn."
