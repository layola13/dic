outtoil:/ˌaʊtˈtɔɪl/
verb
超越；在劳动上胜过
to work harder and longer than someone else
Example: He outtoiled everyone on the team. 他比队里的任何人都更努力工作。
Example:  She outtoiled her competitors and won the contract. 她比竞争对手更加努力，赢得了合同。
Synonym: outwork, surpass, outperform
Antonym:  underperform, lag behind
词性: 动词
