albugineous:/ˌælˌbjuːˈdʒɪniəs/
adjective
白色光滑的；似蛋白的；坚韧的
Resembling the white of an egg; white and glistening.  Relating to the albuginea.
例句：The albuginea is a dense, albuginous layer of connective tissue.  白膜是一层致密的、蛋白状的结缔组织层。

例句：The surgeon carefully incised the albuginous tunic of the testicle.  外科医生小心地切开了睾丸的白色光滑外膜。

近义词：white, glistening, pearly, opaque
反义词：transparent, translucent

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *albuginosus, from albus (“white”) + -ginosus (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘full of’ or ‘abounding in’).
