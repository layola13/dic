evasiveness:/ɪˈveɪsɪvnəs/| n. |逃避，躲避，推诿|The quality of being evasive; a tendency to avoid commitment or self-revelation, especially by responding indirectly.|His evasiveness only increased her suspicions. 他的躲躲闪闪反而增加了她的怀疑。|近义词：elusiveness, slipperiness, indirectness|反义词：directness, forthrightness, openness|词性:名词
Etymology: From evasive + -ness. Evasive comes from Late Latin ēvādere "to evade," from ex- "out" + vādere "to go."
