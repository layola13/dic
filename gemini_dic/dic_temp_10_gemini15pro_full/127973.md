neoterically:/ˌniːoʊˈtɛrɪkli/
|adv.|新近地；现代地；最近地|In a recent or modern manner; characteristic of present times.|The museum has been neoterically redesigned.  博物馆最近进行了现代化改造。|Synonyms: recently, modernly, newly, contemporarily|Antonyms: anciently, archaically, traditionally, old-fashionedly|词性: 副词

neoteric:/ˌniːoʊˈtɛrɪk/
|adj.|新近的；现代的；最近的|Recent in origin; modern.|The architect incorporated neoteric design elements into the building.  这位建筑师将现代设计元素融入到建筑中。|Synonyms: recent, modern, new, contemporary|Antonyms: ancient, archaic, traditional, old-fashioned|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *neotericus, from Ancient Greek νεωτερικός (neōterikós, “new, recent, modern”), from νέος (néos, “new, young, recent”).
