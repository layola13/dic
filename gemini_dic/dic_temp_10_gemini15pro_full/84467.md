halotrichite:/həˈloʊtrɪkaɪt/ | n. | 毛矾 | A hydrous sulfate of iron and aluminum, occurring in silky, fibrous masses, usually as an efflorescence on pyrite or other sulfide minerals.  A mineral consisting of hydrated iron aluminum sulfate. | Halotrichite is often found in caves. 毛矾常在洞穴中发现。| Halotrichite can be a sign of pyrite oxidation. 毛矾可以作为黄铁矿氧化的标志。 | synonyms: feather alum, iron alum | antonyms: N/A | noun
