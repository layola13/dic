bueno:/ˈbwenoʊ/ (US) /ˈbweːnoʊ/ (UK)| adj.|好的，不错的|Good, fine, pleasant, agreeable.  It's used primarily in Spanish and adopted in some English dialects, particularly in the Southwestern US.|Example: The food was bueno.  食物很好吃。|Example:  "That's a bueno idea." 那是个好主意。|Synonym: good, fine, excellent, great, terrific|Antonym: bad, terrible, awful, poor|Adjective
