deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/| vt.|耗尽，减少|To reduce the amount of something, especially a resource or supply.  To empty or use up (something).|The prolonged drought has severely depleted the region's water reserves. 长时间的干旱严重耗尽了该地区的水资源。

The long journey depleted our supplies of food and water.  长途旅行耗尽了我们的食物和水。|近义词：exhaust, drain, diminish, reduce, consume|反义词：replenish, augment, increase|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēplētus*, past participle of *dēplēre* "to empty," from *dē* "from, out of" + *plēre* "to fill."
