dekko:/ˈdɛkoʊ/| n.|一瞥，一看|A quick look or glimpse.|I had a quick dekko at the new car. 我快速地看了一眼那辆新车。|近义词：glance, peek, look|反义词:stare (长时间凝视)|noun

dekko:/ˈdɛkoʊ/| v.|看一看，瞧一瞧 (主要用于英式非正式英语)|To take a quick look.|Let's have a dekko at your new phone. 让我们看看你的新手机。|近义词：look, see, glance, peek|反义词:ignore, avoid (避免看)|verb
