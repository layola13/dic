bedrizzle: /bɪˈdrɪzəl/
verb
轻微地淋湿，沾湿
To sprinkle or cover lightly with droplets, as of dew or rain.
The flowers were bedrizzled with dew. 花上沾满了露水。
The grass was bedrizzled with rain. 草地上沾着雨水。
近义词: drizzle, sprinkle, moisten
反义词: dry, parch
词性: verb

词源：
"be-" prefix + "drizzle"  The prefix "be-" is often used to create transitive verbs from nouns or adjectives, meaning "to make or cause to be something." In this case, "be-" combines with "drizzle" (light rain) to form "bedrizzle," meaning "to cover with drizzle."
