lissome:/ˈlɪsəm/ | adj. | 轻盈的，柔软的，敏捷的 | Easily bent; supple; agile and graceful. |  The dancer's lissome body moved with effortless grace.  舞者的轻盈身躯优雅地舞动着。 |  The lissome willow tree swayed in the breeze. 轻盈的柳树在微风中摇曳。| 近义词: supple, lithe, flexible, agile, nimble, graceful | 反义词: stiff, rigid, inflexible, clumsy, awkward | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *lissom, from Old English *liðsum (“flexible, pliant”), equivalent to lithe +‎ -some. Cognate with Dutch lijfzaam (“supple”), German leibsam (“pleasant, agreeable”).
