humdrummery:/hʌmˈdrʌməri/
noun
单调乏味；无聊；平庸
The routine of office work can sometimes feel like endless humdrummery.
办公室工作的日常有时会让人觉得无休止的单调乏味。
Etymology:  From humdrum + -ery.  Humdrum itself is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
Synonyms: monotony, tedium, dullness, boredom, banality
Antonyms: excitement, thrill, stimulation, interest, novelty
词性: 名词
