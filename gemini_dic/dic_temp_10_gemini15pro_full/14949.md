azyme:/ˈeɪzaɪm/ |adj. n.|无酵的，无酵饼|Unleavened; a type of unleavened bread, especially used in certain religious ceremonies.|The Passover meal includes azyme bread. 逾越节餐包括无酵饼。|近义词:unleavened|反义词:leavened|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology: From Old French *azyme*, from Latin *azymos*, from Greek *άζυμος (azymos)*, meaning "unleavened". Ultimately from the privative prefix *a-* and *ζύμη (zyme)*, "leaven".
