diffusively:/dɪˈfjuːsɪvli/
adverb
弥漫地，扩散地
in a way that spreads widely or thinly
The light diffused diffusively through the frosted glass. 光线弥漫地透过磨砂玻璃。
近义词: widely, broadly, extensively, pervasively
反义词: narrowly, locally, concentratedly
词性: adverb

词源: From diffuse + -ive + -ly.  Diffuse ultimately comes from the Latin word "diffundere," meaning "to pour out" or "to spread."
