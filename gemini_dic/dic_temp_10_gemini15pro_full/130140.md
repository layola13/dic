non-referential:/ˌnɒnˌrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|非指称的，无指代对象的|A non-referential expression doesn't refer to a specific entity in the real world.|The word "it" in "It's raining" is non-referential.|Synonyms: dummy, expletive, pleonastic|Antonyms: referential|词性:形容词
