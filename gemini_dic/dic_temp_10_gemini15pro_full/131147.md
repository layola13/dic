nonbodily:/nɒnˈbɒdɪli/ /nɑːnˈbɑːdəli/
adjective
非物质的; 非肉体的
Not of or relating to the body; incorporeal.  Relating to something that does not have a physical form.
His poetry explores nonbodily experiences, focusing on emotions and thoughts. 他的诗歌探索非物质的体验，关注情感和思想。
The judge awarded damages for nonbodily injury, such as emotional distress. 法官判决赔偿非肉体伤害，例如精神损害。
近义词: intangible, incorporeal, spiritual, mental
反义词: physical, bodily, corporeal, material
词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "bodily" (adjective meaning "of or relating to the body").  "Bodily" comes from "body" + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives).
