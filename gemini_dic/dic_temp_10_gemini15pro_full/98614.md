instructorships:/ɪnˈstrʌktərʃɪps/
n.
教职；讲师职位
The university offers several instructorships in the English department.
这所大学的英语系提供几个讲师职位。
Synonyms: teaching positions, lectureships
Antonyms: studentships, apprenticeships
词性: 名词


instructor:/ɪnˈstrʌktər/
n.
指导者；教练；讲师
He is an instructor at a local community college.
他是当地一所社区大学的讲师。
Synonyms: teacher, tutor, coach, trainer
Antonyms: student, pupil


ship:/ʃɪp/
n.
后缀，表示状态，职位，技能，身份等等
His leadership was questioned.
他的领导能力受到了质疑。
Synonyms: state, quality, skill
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a suffix)
词性: 后缀


Etymology:
"instructor" comes from the Late Latin word "instructor," meaning "builder, arranger, teacher," from the verb "instruere," meaning "to build, equip, arrange, teach." "ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
