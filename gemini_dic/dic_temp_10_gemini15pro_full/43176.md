cpri:/ˈsiːpriː/| abbreviation|通用公共无线接口|Common Public Radio Interface,  a standardized interface for the connection between the distributed units (DUs) and radio units (RUs) in Open RAN architectures.  It allows for interoperability between different vendors' equipment.|The new 5G network utilizes the CPRI standard for its fronthaul connection.|近义词:fronthaul interface|反义词:N/A|n.
