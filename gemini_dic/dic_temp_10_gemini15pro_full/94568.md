impound:/ɪmˈpaʊnd/| v. |扣押，没收，拘留|To seize and hold in the custody of the law; confiscate.|The police impounded his car after the accident. 警方在事故后扣押了他的车.|近义词：confiscate, seize, impound, appropriate|反义词:release, return|verb

impounder:/ɪmˈpaʊndər/| n. |扣押者，没收者|A person or authority that impounds something.|The city is the impounder of stray animals.  该市是流浪动物的扣押者。|近义词：confiscator, seizer|反义词:releaser|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French empoundre, from en- (“in”) + poundre (“to put, place”). Ultimately from Latin ponere (“to put, place”).
