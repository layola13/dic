ludicrously:/ˈluːdɪkrəsli/| adv.|滑稽地，荒谬地|in a way that is extremely silly and unreasonable, deserving to be laughed at|He was ludicrously dressed as a chicken. 他滑稽地 dressed as a chicken.|近义词：absurdly, ridiculously, preposterously, comically|反义词：sensibly, reasonably, seriously|词性:副词
Etymology:  From ludicrous + -ly.  Ludicrous comes from the Latin word *lūdicrus*, meaning "sportive, playful," from *lūdus*, "play, game, sport."
