/ˈhɪər/ /ˈhɪr/
pron.|这里，在这里|Used to refer to this place or position.|例句：I live here. 我住在这里。|近义词：this place, this spot|反义词:there|词性:代词

/hɪər/
v.|听见，听到|To perceive with the ear the sound made by (someone or something).|例句：I can hear you. 我能听到你说话。|近义词：listen, perceive (aurally)|反义词:not hear, be deaf to|词性:动词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I hear the birds singing. 我听见鸟儿在唱歌。
She hears the music playing. 她听见音乐在播放。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am hearing a strange noise. 我正在听到一个奇怪的声音。(较少用，通常用I can hear)
Are you hearing this? 你能听到这个吗？(较少用，通常用Can you hear)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have heard that story before. 我以前听过那个故事。
Have you heard the news? 你听说了这个消息吗？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been hearing strange noises all night. 我整晚都在听到奇怪的声音。(较少用)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I heard a loud bang. 我听到一声巨响。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

They had heard the alarm but ignored it. 他们听到了警报，但没有理会。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been hearing good things about the movie before I saw it. 在我看这部电影之前，我一直听到关于它的好评。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
