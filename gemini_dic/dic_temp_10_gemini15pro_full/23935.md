breamed:/briːmd/
v. |充满，洋溢|To be filled to the brim; overflow.  To be full of a particular quality or emotion. |Her eyes breamed with happiness. 她的眼里充满了幸福。|近义词：overflowed, brimmed, teemed, abounded|反义词：lacked, emptied|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *brēamian "to be radiant, shine," from brēme "famous, glorious, renowned."  Related to Old High German brāmen "to roar, rage," and Old Norse brǣma "to roar." The sense shift from "shine" to "overflow" likely occurred through the image of a full container shining or gleaming.
