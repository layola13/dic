cubbridge:/ˈkʌbrɪdʒ/| n.| 小桥;涵洞;阴沟 |A small bridge, typically one over a stream or ditch. | We crossed the little cubbridge over the babbling brook. 我们穿过小溪上的小桥。| The old cubbridge collapsed under the weight of the truck. 老涵洞在卡车的重压下坍塌了。 | 近义词: bridge, culvert, footbridge | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  Likely a blend of "cub" (possibly suggesting smallness) and "bridge."  No definitive etymological source found. The word is not common and may be dialectal or regional.
