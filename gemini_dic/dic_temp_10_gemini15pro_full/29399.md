catacaustic:/ˌkætəˈkɔːstɪk/
noun
焦散线；焦散面
A curve or surface formed by the focusing of rays of light or other radiation reflected or refracted by another curve or surface.
The catacaustic curve of a spherical mirror reflecting parallel rays is a nephroid. 球面镜反射平行光线的焦散曲线是肾脏线。
Synonyms: caustic, focal curve
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "diffuse reflection")
词性: noun
