melain:/ˈmelɪn/ | n. |黑色素|A dark brown to black pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. It is responsible for tanning of skin exposed to sunlight.| The sun stimulates the production of melanin in the skin.  太阳刺激皮肤产生黑色素。|  Melanin protects the skin from the harmful effects of UV radiation. 黑色素保护皮肤免受紫外线辐射的 harmful effects. | pigment, coloring |achromia| noun
