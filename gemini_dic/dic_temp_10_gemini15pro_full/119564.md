microtome:/ˈmaɪkrəˌtoʊm/| n. |切片机|An instrument used to cut extremely thin sections of material, especially for microscopic examination.|The microtome is an essential tool in histology.  切片机是组织学中必不可少的工具。

The pathologist used a microtome to prepare tissue samples for analysis. 病理学家使用切片机准备组织样本进行分析。|近义词: sectioning instrument|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- (small) + -tome (cutting instrument).  From Greek *mikrós* (small) + *témnein* (to cut).
