contraflow:/ˈkɒntrəˌfləʊ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrəˌfloʊ/ (US)| n. |逆流，逆向车流|The movement of traffic against the normal flow, especially on a highway or motorway.| Due to the accident, the police set up a contraflow system on the highway. 由于事故，警方在高速公路上设置了逆行车道。|synonyms: reverse flow, opposite direction flow |antonyms: normal flow, with-flow|noun
