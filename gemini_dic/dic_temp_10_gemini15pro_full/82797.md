guineans:/ˈɡɪniənz/
n.
几内亚人；豚鼠
People from Guinea or things related to Guinea. Also, a common name for guinea pigs.
Many Guineans speak French. 许多几内亚人说法语。
Guinea pigs are sometimes called guineans. 豚鼠有时被称为几内亚猪。
synonyms: Guinean people, guinea pigs
antonyms: N/A
