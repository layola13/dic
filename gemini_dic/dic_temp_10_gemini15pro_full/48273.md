decoupage:/ˌdeɪkuːˈpɑːʒ/
n.|剪纸装饰；拼贴画；|The art of decorating surfaces by gluing on cutouts of paper or fabric and coating them with varnish or lacquer.|她正在学习剪纸装饰这门艺术。She is learning the art of decoupage.|近义词:collage, montage|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *découper* (to cut out), from *couper* (to cut).
