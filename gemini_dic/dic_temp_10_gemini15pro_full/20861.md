blathering: /ˈblæðərɪŋ/ | adj. n. | 喋喋不休的，胡说八道的；废话，胡扯 | Talking foolishly at length; long-winded nonsense. | He was blathering on about nothing in particular. 他一直在胡扯些无关紧要的事情。 | 近义词：babbling, rambling, chattering, prattling | 反义词：concise, succinct | 词性: adjective, noun (gerund)

blather: /ˈblæðər/ | v. n. | 喋喋不休，胡说；废话，胡扯 | To talk foolishly at length; to utter nonsense. |  She blathered on for hours about her latest diet. 她喋喋不休地讲了几个小时她最新的节食方法。 | 近义词：babble, ramble, chatter, prattle | 反义词：be quiet, be silent | 词性: verb, noun

词源:  From Middle English *blateren, of imitative origin.  Related to Old Norse blaðra (“to talk nonsense”).
