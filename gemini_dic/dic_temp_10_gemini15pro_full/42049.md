cosurfactant:/koʊˈsɜːrˌfæktənt/| n. |助表面活性剂|A substance that is added to a surfactant to enhance its properties, particularly its ability to reduce surface tension and form micelles.|Example: The cosurfactant helps to stabilize the emulsion.  助表面活性剂有助于稳定乳液。

Example:  A short-chain alcohol was used as a cosurfactant in the formulation.  该配方中使用了一种短链醇作为助表面活性剂。|近义词：cosolvent, additive|反义词: None|词性:noun
