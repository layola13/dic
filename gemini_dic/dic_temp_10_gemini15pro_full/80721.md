gormandizer:/ˈɡɔːrməndaɪzər/| n. |贪吃的人；暴食者|A person who eats greedily and excessively.|He's such a gormandizer; he ate the entire cake by himself. 他真是个贪吃鬼，一个人把整个蛋糕都吃掉了。|Synonyms: glutton, overeater, gourmand|Antonyms: ascetic, moderate eater|noun
