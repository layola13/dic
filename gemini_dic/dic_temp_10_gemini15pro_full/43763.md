creepy:/ˈkriːpi/| adj.|令人毛骨悚然的；令人害怕的；怪异的|causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease.|The old abandoned house was creepy. 那栋被遗弃的老房子令人毛骨悚然。|近义词：eerie, spooky, unsettling, disturbing|反义词：comforting, reassuring|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *crepen, from Old English *crēopan (“to creep, crawl”). Related to creep.
