interdash:/ˌɪntərˈdæʃ/ | n. |破折号，连接号 |A punctuation mark (–) used to connect parts of a compound word or between the parts of a sentence. It's longer than a hyphen. | The word "user-friendly" uses a hyphen, while "pre–World War II" uses an interdash.  用户友好的单词使用连字符，而“二战前”使用破折号。| synonym: dash, em dash | antonym: hyphen | noun
