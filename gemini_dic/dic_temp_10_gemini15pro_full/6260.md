amok:/əˈmɒk/ (US) /əˈmɒk/ (UK)| adv. & adj.| 狂乱地；发狂地 | In a frenzied or uncontrolled state; in a murderous rampage.  Out of control.| He ran amok with a knife. 他拿着刀子发狂似的乱跑。|近义词：berserk, frenzied, wild, uncontrolled|反义词：calm, peaceful, controlled|词性: 副词 & 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Malay *amuk* ("rushing in a frenzy"). Ultimately from Sanskrit *āmoka* ("unguided," "reckless").
