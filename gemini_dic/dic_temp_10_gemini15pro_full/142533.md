ossify:/ˈɒsɪfaɪ/ (British), /ˈɑːsɪfaɪ/ (American)| v. |骨化；僵化；墨守成规|To become bone or bony; to harden or become rigid like bone; to become inflexible in habits or beliefs.|例句：The cartilage will eventually ossify into bone. 软骨最终会骨化成骨。|例句：His political views had ossified over the years. 他的政治观点多年来已经僵化。|近义词：harden, solidify, rigidify, fossilize|反义词:soften,liquefy|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *ossificare, from Latin os (genitive ossis) "bone" + -ficare "-fy."
