nonpliablenesses:/ˌnɒnˈplaɪəbəlˌnɛsɪz/
n.
不顺从；固执；僵硬
The state or quality of being nonpliable; inflexibility; stubbornness.
Her nonpliableness made negotiations difficult.  她的固执使得谈判变得困难。
近义词: inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy
反义词: flexibility, pliability, compliance, docility
词性: noun (plural)


nonpliable:/ˌnɒnˈplaɪəbəl/
adj.
不柔顺的；不易弯曲的；固执的
Not easily bent or folded; inflexible; stubborn.
The material was nonpliable and difficult to work with.  这种材料不易弯曲，很难加工。
近义词: inflexible, rigid, stiff, unyielding
反义词: flexible, pliable, supple, yielding
词性: adjective
