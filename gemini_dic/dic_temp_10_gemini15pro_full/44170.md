criticizes: /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 批评，批判 | Expresses disapproval of someone or something, finding fault. | He criticizes her work ethic. 他批评她的职业道德。|  The report criticizes the government's handling of the crisis. 该报告批评了政府对这场危机处理方式。 | synonyms: condemns, denounces, reprimands, censures | antonyms: praises, commends, lauds | verb (third-person singular simple present)
