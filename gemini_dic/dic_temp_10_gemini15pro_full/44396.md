cross-fixed:/ˈkrɒs fɪkst/ | adj. | 交叉固定的，双重固定的 | Fixed in two directions or at two points. This term is often used in finance, particularly related to exchange rates or interest rates. It can also be used in a more general sense to describe something secured in two places. | The exchange rate was cross-fixed to both the dollar and the euro.  汇率与美元和欧元双向挂钩。 | The scaffolding was cross-fixed for extra stability. 脚手架被交叉固定以增加稳定性。 |  synonym:  dual-fixed, doubly secured | antonym:  unfixed, floating (especially in financial contexts) | adjective
