leveret: /ˈlɛvərɪt/ /ˈlevərət/ | n. | 幼兔，小兔子 | A young hare in its first year. |  例句：The leveret froze, hoping the fox wouldn't see it. 小兔子一动不动，希望狐狸不会看到它。

例句：The photographer captured a beautiful image of a leveret in the morning dew. 摄影师捕捉到了一张沾着晨露的美丽小兔子的照片。| 近义词：young hare, bunny | 反义词: adult hare | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *leverett, from Old French levret, diminutive of levre ("hare"), from Latin lepus ("hare").
