calcareo:/kælˈkɛːrioʊ/
adj.
钙质的，含钙的
Containing calcium carbonate; chalky.
The soil in this region is calcareo and supports specific types of vegetation.  这个地区的土壤是钙质的，支持特定类型的植被。
近义词：calcareous, chalky
反义词：siliceous


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calcarius* (of lime), from *calx* (lime, limestone).
