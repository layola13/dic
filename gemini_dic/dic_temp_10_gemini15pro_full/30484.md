centrode:/ˈsɛntrɔʊd/| n. |质心，形心|The point at which the total mass of a body may be considered to be concentrated.|The centrode of the triangle is where the medians intersect. 三角形的形心是中线相交的地方。|Synonyms: centroid, center of mass, center of gravity|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *centroïde*, from *centre* (center) + *-oïde* (suffix forming nouns resembling something).  Ultimately from Latin *centrum* (center).
