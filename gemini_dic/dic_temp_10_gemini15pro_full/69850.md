ferrites:/ˈferˌaɪts/ | n. | 铁氧体 | A ceramic-like material containing iron oxide combined with other metallic oxides, often used in magnets and electronic components. | Ferrite magnets are commonly used in speakers. 铁氧体磁铁通常用于扬声器中。| synonyms: ceramic magnets, magnetic oxides | antonyms: paramagnets, diamagnets | noun


ferrite:/ˈferˌaɪt/ | n. | 铁素体 | A form of pure iron that is magnetic and relatively soft.  | The microstructure of the steel revealed a ferrite and pearlite structure. 钢的微观结构显示出铁素体和珠光体结构。 | synonyms: alpha iron | antonyms: austenite | noun
