carburizations: /ˌkɑːrbjʊrɪˈzeɪʃənz/ |n.|渗碳（处理），增碳（作用）|The process of increasing the carbon content of the surface of steel by heating it in the presence of a carbonaceous material. |The carburizations of the steel components increased their surface hardness.|Synonyms: carbonization, cementation|Antonyms: decarburization|noun
