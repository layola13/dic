dunkables:/ˈdʌŋkəb(ə)lz/
noun
可蘸食物；适合蘸酱的食物
Foods that are suitable for dipping into a sauce or other liquid.
Example: The party platter included a variety of dunkables, such as carrot sticks, celery, and chips. 派对拼盘里包含了各种可以蘸酱的食物，比如胡萝卜条、芹菜和薯片。
Example:  These chicken nuggets are perfect dunkables for kids. 这些鸡块是孩子们完美的蘸酱食物。
Synonyms: dippers, finger foods
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider)  soup, stew, main course
