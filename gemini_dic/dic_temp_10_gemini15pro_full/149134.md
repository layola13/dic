oxidising:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|氧化的；氧化性的|Causing oxidation; having the ability to oxidize other substances.  Relating to or involving oxidation.|例句：The oxidizing agent accepts electrons. 氧化剂接受电子。|近义词：corrosive, reactive|反义词：reducing|词性:形容词
oxidise:/ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdaɪz/ (US)| v.|氧化|To combine or become combined chemically with oxygen.|例句：Iron oxidises to form rust. 铁氧化形成锈。|近义词：corrode, rust|反义词：reduce|词性:动词
oxidation:/ˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌɑːksɪˈdeɪʃən/ (US)| n.|氧化；氧化作用|The process or result of oxidizing or being oxidized.|例句：The oxidation of iron produces rust. 铁的氧化产生铁锈。|近义词：corrosion, rusting|反义词：reduction|词性:名词
