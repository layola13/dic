paganism: /ˈpeɪɡənɪzəm/ | n. | 异教，异教信仰 | A religion other than one of the main world religions, specifically a non-Christian or pre-Christian religion.  It often refers to polytheistic religions, nature worship, or indigenous spiritual practices. |  Example 1:  The rise of Christianity led to the decline of paganism in Europe. 基督教的兴起导致了欧洲异教的衰落。

Example 2:  Many ancient paganism rituals involved celebrating the solstices and equinoxes. 许多古代异教仪式都涉及庆祝冬至和春分。 | Synonyms: heathenism, idolatry | Antonyms: Christianity, Islam, Judaism | noun
