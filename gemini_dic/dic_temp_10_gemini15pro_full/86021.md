healed:/hiːld/
|adj. |已痊愈的，已康复的|cured; restored to health or soundness.|The wound has healed completely.  伤口已经完全愈合了。|近义词：cured, recovered, mended|反义词:injured, wounded, broken|词性:形容词
healed:/hiːld/
|v. |(使)痊愈，(使)康复|(cause to) become healthy again|The doctor healed the patient's broken leg. 医生治好了病人的断腿。|近义词：cure, mend, restore|反义词:injure, wound, harm|词性:动词

词源: Old English *hælan "to make whole, sound, or well," from Proto-Germanic *hailjan "to heal," from PIE root *kailo- "whole, uninjured, of good omen."
