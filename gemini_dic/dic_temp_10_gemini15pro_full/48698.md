defiatory:/dɪˈfaɪəˌtɔːri/ /dɪˈfaɪəˌtɔːri/
adjective
挑衅的；蔑视的；挑战的
Showing defiance; boldly resistant or challenging.
His defiant stare dared anyone to question him. 他挑衅的凝视，挑战任何人质疑他。
The defendant remained defiant throughout the trial.  被告在整个审判过程中都保持着蔑视的态度。
synonyms: rebellious, resistant, disobedient, insubordinate, challenging, provocative, obstinate, unyielding, contumacious
antonyms: submissive, obedient, compliant, yielding, respectful, deferential
词性: 形容词
