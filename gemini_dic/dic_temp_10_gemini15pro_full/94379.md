implausiblenesses:/ɪmˌplɔːzəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
| n. |难以置信；不可信；不合理|The implausiblenesses of his story made it difficult to believe.  他的故事的不可信之处让人难以相信。|The sheer implausiblenesses of the coincidences stretched credulity. 这些巧合的难以置信之处让人难以置信。|近义词：improbabilities, unlikelihoods|反义词：plausibilities, probabilities|词性:名词

Etymology:
From implausible + -ness.  Implausible is from im- (not) + plausible (believable, seeming reasonable). Plausible comes from Latin plausibilis, from plaudere (to applaud).
