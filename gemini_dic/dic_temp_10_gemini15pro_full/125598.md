multivagous:/ˌmʌltɪˈveɪɡəs/ | adj. | 多食性的，杂食性的 | Feeding on a wide variety of foods; omnivorous.  | Example: Multivagous insects are less susceptible to environmental changes affecting specific food sources. 多食性昆虫不易受影响特定食物来源的环境变化的影响。

Example: The multivagous diet of the bear allows it to thrive in various habitats. 熊的多食性饮食使它能够在各种栖息地茁壮成长。 | 近义词: polyphagous, omnivorous | 反义词: monophagous, oligophagous | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *multivagus*, from *multi-* (many) + *vagus* (wandering, roving).  The term suggests an animal that "wanders" or "roves" among many different food sources.
