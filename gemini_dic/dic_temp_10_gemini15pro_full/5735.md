alveus:/ˈælviəs/ | n. | 槽，管，导管 | A channel or tube, especially in the body. Specifically, a layer of white matter located in the hippocampal sulcus of the brain. | The alveus contains axons that connect the hippocampus to other parts of the brain. 海马的槽包含连接海马体和大脑其他部分的轴突。| synonyms: canal, duct, channel | antonyms: none | noun

alveus:/ˈælviəs/ | n. | (蜂巢的)巢房 | A cell of a honeycomb. | The bees filled the alvei with honey. 蜜蜂用蜂蜜填满了蜂巢。| synonyms: cell, compartment | antonyms: none | noun
