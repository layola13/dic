mow:/moʊ/ (US) /məʊ/ (UK)| v. n.|割；修剪；扫射；大量杀死|To cut down (grass, grain, etc.) with a blade or machine.  To knock down or destroy something in great numbers as if by cutting down.|He mowed the lawn every Saturday.  他每个星期六都修剪草坪。|The machine gun mowed down the enemy soldiers. 机枪扫射了敌军士兵。| synonyms: cut, trim, clip, shear, reap, harvest| antonyms: grow, plant, cultivate| verb

mow:/moʊ/ (US) /məʊ/ (UK)| n.|割草；干草堆|The act of mowing; the grass or other growth that has been mowed.|The mow was freshly cut and smelled sweet.  新割的草堆散发着甜甜的香味。| synonyms:  swathe, windrow| antonyms: N/A| noun
