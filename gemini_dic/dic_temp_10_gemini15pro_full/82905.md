gumboot:/ˈɡʌmbuːt/ (UK) /ˈɡʌmbut/ (US)| n. |胶靴；雨靴|A waterproof boot, typically made of rubber, extending to the knee or higher.|例句：He pulled on his gumboots before going out into the rain. 他出门到雨中之前穿上了胶靴。

例句：The children splashed in puddles wearing their bright yellow gumboots.  孩子们穿着鲜黄色的雨靴在水坑里嬉戏。

近义词：Wellington boot, rain boot, rubber boot|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly sandals or slippers for contrasting footwear)|词性:noun
