be-in-for:/biː ˈɪn fɔːr/
verb phrase
即将面临，即将经历
be about to experience something, typically something unpleasant
例句：I think we're in for a storm. 我想我们即将面临一场暴风雨。

例句：You're in for a treat! 你将会有一个惊喜！
近义词：expect, anticipate, about to face
反义词:  avoid, escape
词性:  verb phrase
