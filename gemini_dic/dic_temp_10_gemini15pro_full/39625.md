consequence:/ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəkwɛns/ (US)| n.|结果，后果；重要性；推论|a result or effect of an action or condition.|例句：The consequence of his actions was a loss of trust. 他的行为导致了信任的丧失。|例句：She accepted the consequences of her decision. 她接受了她决定的后果。|近义词：result, outcome, effect, ramification|反义词：cause, origin|词性:名词
