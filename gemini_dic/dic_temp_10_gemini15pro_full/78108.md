geologist:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.|地质学家|A scientist who studies the earth, its structure, and its history. |The geologist examined the rock formations for signs of ancient life.  地质学家检查了岩层中古代生命的迹象。|Synonym: earth scientist, geoscientist|Antonym: N/A|noun


geological:/ˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkl/
adj.|地质的；地质学的|Relating to the study of the earth, its structure, and its history. |The geological survey revealed valuable mineral deposits. 地质调查发现了宝贵的矿藏。|Synonym: earthy, terrestrial|Antonym: N/A|adjective


geologically:/ˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adv.|地质学上|In a way that relates to geology. |Geologically speaking, this area is very interesting. 从地质学角度来说，这个地区非常有趣。|Synonym: earthily, terrestrially|Antonym: N/A|adverb
