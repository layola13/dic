liers:/ˈlaɪərz/| n.|骗子，说谎者|People who tell lies are called liars. 说谎的人被称为骗子。|The detective quickly identified the liars in the witness group. 侦探迅速识别出证人组中的骗子。|近义词：deceivers, prevaricators, fibbers|反义词：truth-tellers, honest people|词性：名词

词源:
From Middle English *lier, from Old French liere ("to bind, tie"), from Frankish *leogan, from Proto-Germanic *leuganą ("to lie, tell a lie"). Cognate with Old English lēogan ("to lie"), German lügen ("to lie"), Dutch liegen ("to lie"), Swedish ljuga ("to lie"). The noun is derived from the verb.
