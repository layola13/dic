commotion:/kəˈmoʊʃən/ | n. |骚动，混乱，喧闹 |a state of confused and noisy disturbance|  The sudden commotion startled everyone in the library. 突然的骚动吓到了图书馆里的所有人。|  There was a great commotion when the fire alarm went off. 火警响起时，一片混乱。|  近义词：disturbance, uproar, tumult, chaos, pandemonium |  反义词：calm, peace, quiet, tranquility | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commotiōn-, from Latin commovēre "to move violently," from com- "together" + movēre "to move."
