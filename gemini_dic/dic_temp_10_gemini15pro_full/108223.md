lauding:/ˈlɔːdɪŋ/ | v. | 赞扬，称赞 | Praising or acclaiming someone or something highly. |  The critics are lauding the new film as a masterpiece. 评论家们称赞这部新电影是杰作。

The mayor lauded the firefighters for their bravery. 市长赞扬了消防员们的勇敢。


近义词: praising, extolling, applauding, commending, celebrating
反义词: criticizing, condemning, denouncing, disparaging
词性: 动词 (Present Participle of "laud")


词源:
From Late Middle English *lauden, from Latin laudāre (“to praise”), from laus (“praise, glory”).
