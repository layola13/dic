gravely:/ˈɡreɪvli/| adv.|严重地；严肃地；沉重地|in a serious or solemn manner; significantly|例句：He looked at me gravely. 他严肃地看着我。

例句：The situation is gravely serious. 局势非常严峻。|近义词：seriously, solemnly, soberly, severely|反义词：lightly, frivolously, cheerfully|词性：副词

词源: From Middle English *graveli, from Old French *gravelement, from grave (“serious, weighty”) + -ment.  Ultimately from Latin gravis (“heavy, weighty, serious”).
