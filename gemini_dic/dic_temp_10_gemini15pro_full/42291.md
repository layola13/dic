counter-indication:/ˌkaʊntərˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|禁忌症，禁忌证|A factor that makes it inadvisable to prescribe or administer a particular drug or employ a particular procedure or treatment.|The patient's history of bleeding disorders is a counter-indication for this medication.  该病人的出血性疾病史是服用这种药物的禁忌症。|contraindication, reason against|indication|noun
