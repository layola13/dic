birthweight:/ˈbɜːθweɪt/| n.|出生体重|The weight of a baby at birth.  It's an important indicator of a baby's health.|例句：The baby's birthweight was a healthy 7 pounds.  这个婴儿的出生体重是健康的7磅。

例句：Low birthweight can be a serious health concern. 低出生体重可能是一个严重的健康问题。|近义词：neonatal weight, birth weight|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *birth* + *weight*.  *Birth* comes from Old English *birþ*, meaning "the act of being born."  *Weight* comes from Old English *wiht*, meaning "amount of heaviness."
