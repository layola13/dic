flag-down:/ˌflæɡ ˈdaʊn/ | v. | 挥手示意停车；招呼…停下 | To signal (a vehicle, especially a taxi) to stop by waving one's hand. |  I flagged down a taxi on the busy street. 我在繁忙的街道上招了一辆出租车。

| He flagged down a passing motorist to ask for directions. 他挥手示意一个路过的司机停下来问路。 | synonyms: hail, signal, wave down | antonyms: let pass, ignore | verb

flag-down:/ˌflæɡ ˈdaʊn/ | n. | (挥手)示意停车 | The act of signaling a vehicle to stop by waving. | The taxi driver noticed my flag-down immediately. 出租车司机立刻注意到了我的挥手示意。

|  A quick flag-down got the waiter's attention.  一个快速的挥手示意引起了服务员的注意。| synonyms: signal, wave | antonyms: N/A | noun
