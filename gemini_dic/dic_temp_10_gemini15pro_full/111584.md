lobotomization:/ˌloʊbəˈtɒməˌzeɪʃən/ /ˌlɒbɒˈtɒmaɪˌzeɪʃən/| n.|脑叶切除术|A surgical operation in which a lobe of the brain is cut into in order to treat mental illness.|The patient underwent a lobotomization in the 1950s.  这位病人在20世纪50年代接受了脑叶切除术。|Synonyms: leucotomy, prefrontal lobotomy|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "psychotherapy" or "medication" as representing alternative treatments)|noun

lobotomy:/ləˈbɑtəmi/ /ləˈbɒtəmi/| n.|脑叶切断术|Surgical incision into a lobe of the brain, especially the prefrontal lobe, formerly used to treat mental illness.|Lobotomies were a controversial treatment for mental illness. 脑叶切断术曾是治疗精神疾病的一种备受争议的方法。|Synonyms: leucotomy, prefrontal lobotomy|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "psychotherapy" or "medication" as representing alternative treatments)|noun
