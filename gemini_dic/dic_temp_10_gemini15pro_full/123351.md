monolobular:/ˌmɒnəˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɑːbjʊlər/
|adj.|单叶的；单瓣的|Having a single lobe or resembling a single lobe. |The monolobular leaves of the plant are its distinguishing feature.  这种植物的单叶是它的显著特征。|unilobular, single-lobed|multilobular|adjective


Etymology->
mono- + lobular

mono-:  from Greek *monos "single, alone"

-lobular: from lobe + -ular. 

lobe: from Latin lobus "pod, husk,"  from Greek lobos "lobe of the ear, pod."

-ular:  suffix forming adjectives meaning "of, relating to, resembling," from French -ulaire and directly from Latin -ularis.
