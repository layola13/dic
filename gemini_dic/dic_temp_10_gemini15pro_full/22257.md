bone-headed:/ˈboʊnˌhɛdɪd/
adjective
愚蠢的，笨头笨脑的
stupid; lacking intelligence or good judgment
That was a bone-headed decision to make.  那是一个愚蠢的决定。
He made a bone-headed move by quitting his job. 他辞职是一个愚蠢的举动。
近义词:stupid, foolish, idiotic, silly, unwise, dumb
反义词:smart, intelligent, wise, clever, brilliant
词性:adjective

词源:
From "bonehead" (n.), which originated in the late 19th century as slang for "a stupid person."  "Bonehead" literally refers to someone with a head made of bone, implying a lack of brains.
