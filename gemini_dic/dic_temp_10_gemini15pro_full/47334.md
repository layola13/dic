de-seeding:/diːˈsiːdɪŋ/

de-seed:/diːˈsiːd/
|v.|去籽，除籽|To remove the seeds from a fruit or vegetable.|例句：I de-seeded the watermelon before serving it. 我在上西瓜之前把籽去掉了。|近义词：remove seeds, pit (for stone fruits)|反义词：seed|verb
