nondecadent:/nɒnˈdekədənt/ | adj. |非颓堕的，非衰败的| Not characterized by decadence; resisting or rejecting decadence. | The nondecadent architecture of the building stood in stark contrast to the crumbling structures around it. 这座建筑的非颓废建筑与其周围摇摇欲坠的建筑形成鲜明对比。 | Example 2: Her nondecadent lifestyle, focused on simplicity and hard work, was admired by many. 她非颓废的生活方式，注重简单和努力工作，受到许多人的钦佩。 | 近义词: conservative, traditional, austere | 反义词: decadent, degenerate | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (not) + "decadent" (decaying, declining morally or culturally). "Decadent" comes from the French "décadent" and ultimately from the Latin "cadere" (to fall).
