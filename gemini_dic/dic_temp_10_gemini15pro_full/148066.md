oversanguinenesses:/ˌoʊvərˈsæŋɡwɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
过度乐观
Excessive optimism; the state of being too hopeful or confident.
His oversanguineness about the project's success led to disappointment. 他对项目成功的过度乐观导致了失望。
近义词：optimism, hopefulness, buoyancy
反义词：pessimism, cynicism, despair
词性:名词

n.

Excessive optimism; the state of being too hopeful or confident.
Her oversanguineness about the election results proved to be misplaced. 她对选举结果的过度乐观被证明是错误的。
近义词：optimism, hopefulness, buoyancy
反义词：pessimism, cynicism, despair
词性:名词


词源:  From over- + sanguine + -ness.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Sanguine" comes from Latin "sanguis" meaning "blood," originally referring to a healthy, reddish complexion thought to indicate a cheerful disposition.  "-ness" forms a noun indicating a state or quality.
