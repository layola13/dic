inexplosive:/ˌɪnɪkˈsploʊsɪv/
adjective
非爆炸性的；不易爆炸的
Not explosive; unable to explode.
例句：
The liquid is classified as inexplosive.
这种液体被归类为非爆炸性液体。

They transported the inexplosive chemicals by truck.
他们用卡车运送了这些非爆炸性化学品。

近义词：non-explosive, inert, stable
反义词：explosive, volatile, unstable
词性：adjective
