overcautious:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/| adj.|过度谨慎的，过分小心|Excessively cautious; too careful.|She was overcautious in her investments, missing out on some good opportunities. 她投资过于谨慎，错过了一些好机会.|Synonyms: careful, prudent, wary, circumspect|Antonyms: reckless, careless, impulsive, rash|adjective

overcautious:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/| adj.|过度谨慎的，过分小心|Excessively cautious; too careful.|He was so overcautious about crossing the street that he held up traffic. 他过马路过于小心，以至于阻碍了交通.|Synonyms: apprehensive, timid, hesitant|Antonyms: daring, bold, adventurous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + cautious.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."  "Cautious" comes from the Latin word "cautus," meaning "careful" or "wary."
