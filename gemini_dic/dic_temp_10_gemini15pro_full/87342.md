heroes:/ˈhɪəroʊz/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊz/ (UK)| n.|英雄;勇士|A hero is a person who is admired or idealized for courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities.|例句：The firefighters are true heroes. 消防员是真正的英雄。

例句：Many people consider Nelson Mandela a hero. 很多人认为纳尔逊·曼德拉是一位英雄。

近义词：champion, idol, legend, icon|反义词：villain, coward|词性：名词

词源：From Old French *heroe, from Latin heros, from Ancient Greek ἥρως (hḗrōs, “demi-god, hero”).
