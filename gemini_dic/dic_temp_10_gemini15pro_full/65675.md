even-though:/ˈiːvən ðoʊ/| conjunction|即使；虽然；尽管|Used to introduce a subordinate clause expressing something that contrasts with the main clause.|Even though it was raining, we went for a walk. 即使下雨了，我们还是去散步了。|Even though I was tired, I finished the report. 虽然我很累，但我还是完成了报告。|近义词：although, though, despite, in spite of|反义词:|conjunction
