endo:/ˈendoʊ/
prefix
内部的;在内的
Located inside; within.
Endoscopy is a procedure that involves inserting a thin, flexible tube with a camera on the end into the body to examine the internal organs. 内窥镜检查是一种将末端带有摄像头的细而灵活的管子插入体内以检查内脏的程序。
近义词: intra-, inner
反义词: ecto-, exo-
词性:前缀
