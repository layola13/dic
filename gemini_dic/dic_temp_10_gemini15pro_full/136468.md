nonrevivalists:/ˌnɑːnriˈvaɪvəlɪsts/
n.
非复兴主义者
People who do not support or participate in a revival, especially a religious one.
The nonrevivalists in the community maintained their traditional practices. 社区里的非复兴主义者保持着他们的传统习俗。
The nonrevivalists questioned the emotional fervor of the meetings. 非复兴主义者质疑会议上的情绪狂热。
近义词:traditionalists, conservatives
反义词:revivalists, reformers
词性:名词


nonrevivalist:/ˌnɑːnriˈvaɪvəlɪst/
adj.
非复兴主义的
Not pertaining to or characterized by revivalism.
The nonrevivalist church service focused on quiet contemplation. 非复兴主义的教堂礼拜侧重于安静的沉思。
They took a nonrevivalist approach to worship. 他们采取了一种非复兴主义的崇拜方式。
近义词:traditional, conservative
反义词:revivalist, reformist
词性:形容词


词源:
non- (prefix) + revival + -ist (suffix)

"non-"  means "not" or "opposite of".
"revival" comes from the Late Middle English word "revivalle," meaning "act of reviving."  It ultimately derives from the Latin "revivere," "to live again."
"-ist" denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine, belief, or practice.
