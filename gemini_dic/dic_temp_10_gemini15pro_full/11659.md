armisonant: /ɑːrmɪˈsənənt/
adjective
发出武器碰撞声的; 铿锵的
Producing or characterized by the sounds of clashing arms.
The armisonant clang of swords echoed through the valley. 剑的铿锵声回荡在山谷中。
近义词: clangorous, resounding, echoing
反义词: quiet, silent
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *armisonus ("sounding of arms"), from arma ("arms") + sonus ("sound").
