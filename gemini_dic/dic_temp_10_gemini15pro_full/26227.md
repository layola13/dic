butterine: /ˈbʌtərˌin/ (US) /ˈbʌtərɪn/ (UK)| n.|人造奶油；植物黄油|A cheap substitute for butter, made from vegetable oils or animal fats.|例句：They used butterine instead of butter for baking. 他们用人造奶油代替黄油烘焙。

例句：The butterine was spread thickly on the toast.  人造奶油厚厚地涂在吐司上。


|近义词：margarine, oleo|反义词: butter|词性: noun
