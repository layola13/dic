nonshipping:/ˌnɒnˈʃɪpɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈʃɪpɪŋ/| adj.|非航运的，不涉及航运的|Relating to or denoting services or goods not involving transportation by sea.|The company’s nonshipping logistics division handles trucking and warehousing.  这家公司的非航运物流部门负责货车运输和仓储。|Synonyms: nonmaritime, inland|Antonyms: shipping, maritime|adjective

nonshipping:/ˌnɒnˈʃɪpɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈʃɪpɪŋ/| n.|非航运业务|Business activities not related to shipping.|The company expanded its nonshipping operations to include warehousing. 这家公司扩大了其非航运业务，将仓储纳入其中。|Synonyms: nonmaritime operations, inland business|Antonyms: shipping business, maritime operations|noun
