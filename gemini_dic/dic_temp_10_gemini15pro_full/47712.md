decacuminated:/diːkəˈkjuːmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
削尖的；渐尖的；突然变尖的
Having a long pointed apex, abruptly narrowed; tapering to a slender point.
The decacuminated spire of the church was visible for miles. 教堂削尖的尖顶在几英里外都能看到。
The leaf had a decacuminated tip. 这片叶子的尖端是渐尖的。
近义词: acuminate, cuspidate, pointed, sharp
反义词: obtuse, rounded, blunt
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin 'de' (down, away from) + 'cacumen' (summit, peak, point) + '-ated' (forming an adjective).  Essentially meaning "taken away from the peak/point," implying a tapering to a point.
