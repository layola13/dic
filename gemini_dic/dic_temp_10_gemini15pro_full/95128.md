inappetence:/ˌɪnˈæpɪtəns/| n. |食欲不振；厌食|Lack or loss of appetite.|The patient exhibited inappetence after the surgery. 病人在手术后表现出食欲不振。|Synonyms: anorexia, loss of appetite|Antonyms: appetite, hunger|noun

inappetency:/ˌɪnˈæpɪtənsi/| n. |欲望缺失; 冷淡|Lack of desire or inclination.|He showed inappetency for any form of exercise. 他对任何形式的运动都缺乏兴趣。|Synonyms: apathy, indifference, listlessness|Antonyms: eagerness, enthusiasm, desire|noun
