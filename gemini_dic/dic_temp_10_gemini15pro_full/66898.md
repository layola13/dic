expeditely:/ˌɛkspəˈdaɪtli/| adv.|迅速地，快速地|In a quick and efficient manner; with speed and promptness.  This word emphasizes efficiency and the removal of obstacles to achieve speed.|The package was delivered expeditely.  包裹被迅速送达。|The company worked expeditely to resolve the customer's issue. 公司迅速解决了客户的问题。|近义词：quickly, rapidly, swiftly, promptly, efficiently|反义词：slowly, tardily, deliberately|词性:副词
词源: From Latin expedire ("to disentangle, set free, prepare, make ready"), from ex- ("out") + pes, ped- ("foot").  The sense of freeing one's feet led to the meaning of "to facilitate, hasten".
