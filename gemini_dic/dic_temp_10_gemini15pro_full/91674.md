hypercivilized:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɪvɪlaɪzd/| adj.|超级文明的，过度文明的|Excessively civilized; having a civilization that is perceived as overly developed, sophisticated, or refined, potentially to the point of decadence or artificiality.|例句：The hypercivilized society had lost touch with nature. 这个超级文明的社会已经失去了与自然的联系。|例句：Their hypercivilized manners seemed artificial and insincere. 他们过度文明的举止显得虚假且不真诚。|近义词：advanced, sophisticated, refined, overrefined, decadent|反义词：primitive, undeveloped, barbaric, unrefined|词性：形容词
