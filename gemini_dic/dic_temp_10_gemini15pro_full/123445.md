monopoloid:/ˈmɒnəˌplɔɪd/| adj. n.|单倍体，单倍体生物|Having a single set of chromosomes in each cell, half the usual diploid number.  A cell or organism having a single set of chromosomes.|例句：Monopoloid cells are often used in genetic research. 单倍体细胞常用于基因研究。

例句：The monopoloid plant was more susceptible to disease.  这种单倍体植物更容易生病。|近义词：haploid|反义词：diploid, polyploid|词性: adjective, noun
