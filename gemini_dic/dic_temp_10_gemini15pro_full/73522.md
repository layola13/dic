foreshortened:/fɔːrˈʃɔːrtnd/ /fɔːrˈʃɔːrtnɪd/
adjective
缩短的; (绘画中)透视缩短的
appearing shorter than it really is because it is pointing towards you
The artist skillfully foreshortened the outstretched arm in the painting.  画家巧妙地将画中伸出的手臂进行了透视缩短。
shortened, compressed, contracted
elongated, extended, lengthened
词性: adjective
