hiss:/hɪs/| n. vi. vt.|嘶嘶声，发出嘶嘶声|A sharp sibilant sound, like that made by a snake or a gas escaping under pressure.  The sound itself can also be referred to as a "hiss". To make such a sound.|The snake hissed at me. 那条蛇对着我嘶嘶叫。|近义词：sizzle, fizz, wheeze, whisper|反义词: roar, shout|noun, verb
