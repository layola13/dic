overfoolish:/ˌoʊvərˈfuːlɪʃ/| adj.|过于愚蠢的，非常愚蠢的|Excessively or extremely foolish; more foolish than usual or expected.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) with "foolish".|That was an overfoolish decision, and he's going to regret it. 那是一个非常愚蠢的决定，他会后悔的。|He made the overfoolish mistake of trusting a known con artist. 他犯了一个极其愚蠢的错误，相信了一个臭名昭著的骗子。|近义词：extremely foolish, ridiculously foolish, absurdly foolish, excessively foolish|反义词：wise, sensible, prudent|词性:形容词
