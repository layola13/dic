hortulan:/hɔːrˈtʌlən/ | n. | 园丁，园艺工人 | A person who works in a garden, especially one employed to take care of a large garden or estate. | The hortulan meticulously pruned the rose bushes. 园丁细致地修剪了玫瑰丛。|  The hortulan planted rows of colorful tulips in the garden. 园丁在花园里种了成排的五颜六色的郁金香。 | 近义词: gardener, groundskeeper | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *hortulānus*, from *hortulus* ("small garden"), diminutive of *hortus* ("garden").
