adversifoliate: /ˌædvɜːrsiˈfəʊliɪt/
adjective
对生叶的; 对叶的
Having leaves arranged in pairs on opposite sides of the stem.  Leaves are positioned directly across from each other at each node.
The adversifoliate arrangement of the leaves allowed for maximum sunlight exposure.  对生叶的排列方式使叶子能够最大限度地暴露在阳光下。

The botanist identified the plant by its adversifoliate leaves and distinctive flowers.  植物学家通过其对生叶和独特的 花朵识别了这种植物。

synonym: opposite
antonym: alternate, spiral
词性: adjective
