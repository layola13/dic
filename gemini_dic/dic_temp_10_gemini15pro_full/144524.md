outvalued:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuːd/
verb
超过…的价值
to exceed in value.
Her contributions to the company outvalued his. 她对公司的贡献超过了他的价值。
Synonyms: surpass, exceed, outstrip, outweigh
Antonyms: undervalue, underestimate
词性:动词
