maked: /meɪkt/ | v. (archaic) | made的过去式和过去分词 |  past tense and past participle of "make" |  He maked a cake for her birthday. (He made a cake for her birthday.) 他为她生日做了一个蛋糕。 | 近义词: created, produced, constructed | 反义词: destroyed, demolished | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *maken, from Old English macian (“to make, do, perform, build, create, compose, fashion, prepare, cause, bring about, institute, appoint, establish, hold, observe, celebrate, gain, earn, acquire, get, receive, obtain, capture, seize, take, win, conquer”), from Proto-Germanic *makōną (“to make, fit”), from Proto-Indo-European *magʰ- (“to be able, to have power”).  Cognate with Scots mak (“to make”), West Frisian meitsje (“to make”), Dutch maken (“to make”), German machen (“to make, do”), Danish mage (“to be able”), Swedish må (“may”), Icelandic mega (“may, can”).
