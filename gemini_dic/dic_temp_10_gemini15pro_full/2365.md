-adic:/ˈædɪk/
adjective
与…有关的；…进制的
Relating to or based on a particular number or numerical system, typically a prime number.
This is a 2-adic number system. 这是一个二进制数系。
The researcher is exploring 5-adic analysis.  这位研究员正在探索五进制分析。
近义词: related, based on
反义词: unrelated
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin -adicus, from Greek -αδικός (-adikos). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *-o-diko-.

USAGE->
As an adjective:
The properties of p-adic numbers are fascinating. = p进数的性质令人着迷。
2-adic analysis has applications in computer science. = 2进数分析在计算机科学中有着应用。


PHRASE->
p-adic numbers = p进数
p-adic analysis = p进数分析
2-adic system = 二进制系统
10-adic system = 十进制系统
p-adic valuation = p进赋值
