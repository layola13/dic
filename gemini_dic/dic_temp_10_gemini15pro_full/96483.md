individuity: /ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒʊˈælɪti/ | n. | 个体性；个性；独特之处 | The quality or state of being an individual; separate nature or existence; distinctive character. |  She expressed her individuality through her unique sense of style. 她通过独特的风格表达了她的个性。| synonyms: uniqueness, singularity, distinctiveness, originality, peculiarity | antonyms: conformity, uniformity, similarity, sameness | noun

Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin individualitas, from individuus ‘indivisible’ (see individual).
