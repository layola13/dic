lonelinesses:/ˈloʊnlinɪsɪz/
n.
孤独，寂寞（loneliness 的复数形式）
The poem explores the lonelinesses of modern life. 这首诗探讨了现代生活的孤独感。
lonelinesses of modern life. 现代生活的孤独感。
近义词：isolations, solitudes
反义词：togethers, companionships
词性：名词


Etymology:
From loneliness + -es (plural suffix). Loneliness itself comes from lonely + -ness.  Lonely traces back to Old English `ānlic` meaning "alone, solitary," from `ān` ("one") and `-lic` ("-ly").
