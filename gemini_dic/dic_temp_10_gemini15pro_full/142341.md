orthogonally:/ɔːrˈθɒɡənəli/ | adv. | 正交地，垂直地 |  Relating to or involving right angles; perpendicular.  It also describes things that are statistically independent or conceptually unrelated. | The two lines intersect orthogonally. 这两条线正交相交。 | synonym: perpendicularly, vertically, at right angles | antonym: obliquely, diagonally | adverb

orthogonal:/ɔːˈθɒɡənəl/ | adj. | 正交的，垂直的 |  Relating to or involving right angles; perpendicular.  It also describes things that are statistically independent or conceptually unrelated. | The two vectors are orthogonal. 这两个向量是正交的。 | synonym: perpendicular, vertical, at right angles | antonym: oblique, diagonal | adjective
