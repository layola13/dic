mismatched:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃt/ | adj. | 不匹配的，不相配的 | Not matching or corresponding; unsuitable or incongruous. | The curtains and carpet were mismatched. 窗帘和地毯不搭。| synonyms: incompatible, incongruous, unsuitable, unsuited, ill-matched, discordant | antonyms: matched, compatible, harmonious, suitable, appropriate | adjective

mismatched:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃt/ |  v. | 错配，配错 | To match (people or things) incorrectly or inappropriately.  |  The dating app mismatched me with someone twenty years older.  这个约会软件把我跟一个比我大二十岁的人配对了。 | synonyms: mispair, mismatch | antonyms: match, pair | verb
