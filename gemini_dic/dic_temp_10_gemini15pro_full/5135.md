allegretto:/ˌæləˈɡrɛtoʊ/ | adj. adv. n. | 小快板，稍快的 | Moderately fast, but not as fast as allegro.  Used chiefly as a direction in music. | The second movement of the sonata is marked allegretto.  奏鸣曲的第二乐章是小快板。 | 近义词:moderately fast, brisk | 反义词:lento, adagio | 词性:adjective, adverb, noun
词源: Italian, diminutive of allegro (cheerful, brisk), from Latin alacer (lively, cheerful).
