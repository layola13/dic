erased:/ɪˈreɪst/ | adj. | 已擦掉的，被删除的 | Removed, obliterated, wiped out.  | The data was erased from the hard drive. 数据已从硬盘中删除。 |  My childhood memories of that place have been erased by time.  时间已经抹去了我对那个地方的童年记忆。 | 近义词: deleted, removed, obliterated, expunged | 反义词:  recorded, saved, preserved, restored | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *ērasus, past participle of ēradere "to scrape out," from ex- "out" + radere "to scrape."
