blow-dried: /ˈbloʊˌdraɪd/ (US), /ˈbləʊˌdraɪd/ (UK)
adjective
吹干的
dried with a hairdryer
Her blow-dried hair shone in the sunlight. 她的吹干的头发在阳光下闪闪发光。
His blow-dried hair looked very stylish. 他吹干的头发看起来很时尚。
近义词: dried, styled
反义词: wet, damp
词性: adjective

blow-dry: /ˈbloʊˌdraɪ/ (US), /ˈbləʊˌdraɪ/ (UK)
verb
吹干
to dry something, especially hair, using a hairdryer
She blow-dries her hair every morning. 她每天早上都吹干头发。
He blow-dried his hair quickly before going out. 他出门前快速地吹干了头发。
近义词: dry
反义词: wet
词性: verb

词源:  "blow-dry" is a compound verb formed from "blow" (meaning to expel air) and "dry."  The adjective "blow-dried" is derived from this verb.
