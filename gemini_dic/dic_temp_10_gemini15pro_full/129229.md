noctidial: /nɒkˈtɪdiəl/
adjective
夜间活动的；夜间发生的
Active at night; nocturnal.
例句：The noctidial owl hunts for prey under the cover of darkness.  夜间活动的猫头鹰在夜幕的掩护下捕猎。

例句：Noctidial animals often have heightened senses of hearing and smell. 夜行动物通常具有更强的听觉和嗅觉。
近义词：nocturnal, nightly
反义词：diurnal
词性：adjective

noctidial: /nɒkˈtɪdiəl/
noun
夜行动物
An animal that is active at night.
例句：Bats are classic examples of noctidials.  蝙蝠是夜行动物的典型例子。

例句：Many desert creatures are noctidials, avoiding the scorching daytime heat. 许多沙漠生物都是夜行动物，以避开白天灼热的温度。
近义词：nocturnal animal
反义词：diurnal animal
词性：noun
