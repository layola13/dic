anacanths:/ˈænəˌkænθs/
n.
棘鱼类，棘鳍类鱼
spiny-finned fishes; an extensive group of teleost fishes having fins supported by sharp inflexible spines
Anacanths include cod, hake, and haddock. 棘鳍类鱼包括鳕鱼、无须鳕和黑线鳕。
近义词:acanthopterygians, teleosts
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider cartilaginous fishes)
词性:noun
