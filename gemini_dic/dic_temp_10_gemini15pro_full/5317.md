allowables:/əˈlaʊəbəlz/
|n.| 允许值，容许量，许可项目| Amounts or items that are permitted, usually within a set of rules or regulations. | The company's allowables for travel expenses are clearly stated in the employee handbook. 公司员工手册中明确规定了公司允许的差旅费用。| synonyms:  permitted amounts,  accepted expenses,  authorized items | antonyms: disallowed amounts,  prohibited items,  unaccepted expenses | noun


allowable:/əˈlaʊəb(ə)l/
|adj.| 允许的，可接受的，容许的| Permitted, acceptable, or permissible according to a set of rules or standards. |  Is this expense allowable under company policy?  根据公司政策，这笔费用是允许的吗？ | synonyms: permissible, acceptable, legitimate, authorized | antonyms: disallowed,  prohibited, unacceptable, unauthorized | adjective


Etymology:
From "allow" (verb) + "-able" (suffix).  "Allow" comes from Old French "alouer," ultimately from Latin "allaudare" meaning "to praise."  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "liable to be."
