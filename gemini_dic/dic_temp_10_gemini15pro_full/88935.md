holding-out:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ aʊt/| n.|坚持，抵抗；拖延，保留；希望，期盼|The holding-out of the defenders was heroic. 防守者的坚持是英勇的。|近义词：resistance, perseverance, delay, withholding|反义词: surrender, yielding, release|noun
holding-out:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ aʊt/| v.|坚持，抵抗；拖延，保留；希望，期盼|They are holding out for a better offer. 他们在坚持要求更好的报价。|近义词：resist, persevere, delay, withhold|反义词: surrender, yield, release|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb phrase "hold out," composed of "hold" (from Old English "haldan," meaning "to grasp, keep, possess") and "out" (from Old English "ūt," meaning "outward, outside").  The meaning of "resisting" or "persisting" developed from the physical act of holding something out or extending it.
