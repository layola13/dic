firebreak:/ˈfaɪərbreɪk/| n.|防火道，隔离带，阻火器|A barrier intended to stop the progress of a fire.|Firefighters bulldozed a firebreak to contain the wildfire. 消防队员推土机开辟了一条防火道以控制野火。|The government introduced a firebreak lockdown to try and halt the spread of the virus. 政府实施了防火墙式的封锁，试图阻止病毒的传播。|近义词：firestop, barrier, buffer zone|反义词:fuel, accelerant|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "fire" + "break," meaning a break or interruption in a fire.  The term was originally used in forestry and land management.
