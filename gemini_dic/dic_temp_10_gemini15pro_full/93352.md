illegalize: /ɪˈliːɡəlaɪz/ | v. | 使非法；宣布…为非法 | To make something illegal; to prohibit something by law. | The government decided to illegalize the sale of certain drugs. 政府决定将某些药物的销售非法化。

The city council voted to illegalize smoking in public parks. 市议会投票决定禁止在公园内吸烟。| 近义词: prohibit, ban, outlaw | 反义词: legalize, permit, allow | 词性: 动词

词源:  From il- (not) + legal + -ize (to make).  The prefix "il-" becomes "illegal-" before "l".  Essentially meaning "to make not legal."
