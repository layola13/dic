metastasized: /məˈtæs.tə.saɪzd/ | v. | 转移（尤指癌细胞）| (of a cancer) spread to other sites in the body. | The cancer had metastasized to her lungs. 癌症已经转移到她的肺部了。| synonyms: spread, diffused, disseminated | antonyms: localized, contained | verb


metastasis: /məˈtæs.tə.sɪs/ | n. | 转移 | the development of secondary malignant growths at a distance from a primary site of cancer. | The patient unfortunately showed signs of metastasis.  很不幸，病人出现了转移的迹象。| synonyms: spread, diffusion, dissemination | antonyms: localization, containment | noun
