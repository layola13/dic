idleby:/ˈaɪdəlˌbaɪ/ | n. | 闲人；懒汉 |  A person who spends their time doing nothing or being unproductive. | He was known as an idleby, always lounging around the village square.  他被称为懒汉，总是在村里的广场上闲逛。 | 近义词: loafer, slacker, good-for-nothing | 反义词: worker, busybody | 词性: 名词

词源:  A compound word combining "idle" (inactive) and "by" (near, beside), suggesting someone who stands idly by.
