calumniously: /kəˈlʌmniəsli/
adverb
诽谤地；中伤地
in a calumnious manner; falsely and maliciously.
He was calumniously accused of theft. 他被诬告偷窃。
近义词: falsely, slanderously, maliciously, defamatory, libelously
反义词: truthfully, honestly, fairly
词性: adverb

calumny: /ˈkæləmni/
noun
诽谤；中伤
the making of false and defamatory statements in order to damage someone's reputation; slander.
He spread calumnies about his rivals. 他散布关于他对手的诽谤。
近义词: slander, defamation, libel, vilification, aspersion
反义词: praise, acclaim, commendation
词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *calumniosus, from Latin calumnia (“trickery, chicanery, false accusation”), from calvi (“to deceive”).
