paramagnetism:/ˌpærəˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/| n.|顺磁性|A form of magnetism whereby certain materials are weakly attracted by an externally applied magnetic field, and form internal, induced magnetic fields in the direction of the applied magnetic field.|Liquid oxygen is paramagnetic.|近义词: magnetism|反义词:diamagnetism|noun
