haunts:/hɔːnts/ | v. | 萦绕；经常出没；鬼魂出没；缠扰 | To be persistently in the mind of someone.  To visit (a place) regularly. To appear as a ghost or apparition. | The memory of that day still haunts me. 那一天的记忆仍然萦绕着我。| synonyms: troubles, plagues, besets, disturbs | antonyms: comforts, soothes | verb

haunt:/hɔːnt/ | n. | 常去的地方；栖息地 | A place frequented by a specified person or group of people. | The cafe was a favorite haunt of artists and writers. 这家咖啡馆是艺术家和作家最喜欢的去处。 | synonyms: hangout, resort, retreat, den | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "unfamiliar place" or "rarely visited location") | noun
