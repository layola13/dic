ablatively:/əˈbleɪtɪvli/
adverb
烧蚀地；通过烧蚀；以消融方式
In a manner that involves ablation, typically by melting or vaporization.  通过烧蚀的方式，通常是通过熔化或汽化。
The heat shield protected the spacecraft ablatively during re-entry.  隔热罩在重返大气层时以烧蚀方式保护了航天器。

The surface of the comet was ablatively eroded by the solar wind. 彗星表面被太阳风以烧蚀的方式侵蚀。
synonym: by ablation, through ablation
antonym: (No direct antonym, consider "accretively" which means by adding material)
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "ablatus" (removed, carried away), past participle of "auferre" (to carry away), from "ab-" (away) + "ferre" (to carry).  来自拉丁语 "ablatus"（移除，带走），"auferre"（带走）的过去分词，源自 "ab-"（离开）+ "ferre"（带）。


USAGE->
This word is primarily used in scientific or technical contexts.  这个词主要用于科学或技术语境。

The material degrades ablatively, protecting the underlying structure. =  这种材料以烧蚀方式降解，保护下面的结构。
The spacecraft was designed to re-enter the atmosphere ablatively. = 航天器被设计成以烧蚀方式重返大气层。


PHRASE->
ablatively cooled = 烧蚀冷却
ablatively protected = 烧蚀保护
