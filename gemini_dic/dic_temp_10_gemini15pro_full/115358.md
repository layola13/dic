/mænɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |  庄园化；形成或发展成庄园制度 | The process by which land ownership and agricultural production become organized around the manor, a self-sufficient economic and social unit. | The manorialization of medieval Europe had a profound impact on social structures. 中世纪欧洲的庄园化对社会结构产生了深远的影响。 |  feudalization,  enfeoffment | decentralization, fragmentation | noun

/ˌmænɔːriəˈlaɪz/ | v. | 庄园化；使成为庄园 | To organize (land, an estate, etc.) into a manor or manors. | The king manorialized vast tracts of land, granting them to his loyal followers. 国王将大片土地庄园化，授予他的忠实追随者。| feudalize, enfeoff |  decentralize, fragment | verb
