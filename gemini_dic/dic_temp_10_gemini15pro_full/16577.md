barnier:/ˈbɑːrniər/ | adj. | 更困难的，障碍更大的 | Creating a more inclusive environment will be barnier than some anticipate. |  The negotiations became barnier after the new proposals were introduced. | 近义词: tougher, more difficult, more challenging, more obstructive | 反义词: easier, simpler, less demanding | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Derived from the noun "barrier" (meaning obstacle), with the addition of the comparative suffix "-er".  Ultimately from Old French "barre" (bar).
