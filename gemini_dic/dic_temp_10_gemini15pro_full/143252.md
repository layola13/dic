outdespatch:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈspætʃ/ | vt. | 超越，胜过；更快地发送 | To send something faster or more efficiently than someone or something else. |  They outdespatched their rivals by using a more streamlined delivery system. 他们通过使用更精简的交付系统超越了竞争对手。

The company outdespatched its competitors and delivered the goods first.  这家公司比竞争对手更快地发货，率先交付了货物。

| 近义词：outstrip, surpass, exceed, better, outdo | 反义词：lag behind, fall behind | 词性：动词

outdespatch:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈspætʃ/ | n. | (古旧用法)  发送; 派遣; 调遣 | The act of sending something out quickly or efficiently. | The outdespatch of the troops was swift and efficient. 部队的派遣迅速而有效。 (这个用法现在很少见)

The urgent outdespatch of the medicine saved the patient's life.  紧急发送的药物挽救了病人的生命。(这个用法现在很少见)

| 近义词：dispatch, sending | 反义词：delay, withholding | 词性：名词

词源： out- + despatch.  "Out-" prefix indicates exceeding or surpassing.  "Despatch" means to send off quickly.  Therefore, "outdespatch" combines these meanings to represent surpassing in speed of sending or doing something.
