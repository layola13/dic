bearberry:/ˈberˌberi/ /ˈbɛrˌbɛri/| n. |熊果，熊葡萄|A low-growing evergreen shrub (Arctostaphylos uva-ursi) with leathery leaves, small pink or white flowers, and red berries. It is found in arctic and subarctic regions and on mountains farther south.  The leaves are sometimes used medicinally.|Bears love to eat the berries of this plant, hence the name bearberry. 熊喜欢吃这种植物的浆果，因此得名熊果。|Synonyms: kinnikinnick, uva-ursi|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bearberry:/ˈberˌberi/ /ˈbɛrˌbɛri/| adj. |熊果的|Made from or relating to the bearberry plant.|Bearberry extract is used in some skincare products. 熊果提取物用于一些护肤品中。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
