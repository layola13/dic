overmonopolized: /ˌoʊvər məˈnɑpəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度垄断的 |  Dominated by a monopoly to an excessive or harmful degree.  It signifies a situation where a single entity or a small group controls a market so completely that it stifles competition and potentially harms consumers. | The overmonopolized telecommunications industry resulted in high prices and limited choices for consumers. 电信行业过度垄断导致价格高昂，消费者选择有限。| The overmonopolized tech sector stifled innovation and limited consumer choice. 过度垄断的科技行业扼杀了创新，限制了消费者的选择。 |  monopolistic, overconcentrated | competitive, diverse | adjective
