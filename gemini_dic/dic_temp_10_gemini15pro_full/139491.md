obstructers:/əbˈstrʌktərz/
noun
阻碍者，妨碍者
A person or thing that obstructs something.
The obstructers were removed from the road.  道路上的障碍物被清除了。
Protesters acted as obstructers, blocking the entrance to the building. 抗议者充当了阻碍者，堵住了大楼的入口。
Synonyms: hinderers, blockers, impediments
Antonyms: facilitators, enablers
