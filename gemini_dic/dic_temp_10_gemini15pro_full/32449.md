chimney-stack:/ˈtʃɪmni stæk/| n.|烟囱，烟囱组|A vertical structure containing one or more flues for conveying smoke or exhaust gases from a fireplace, furnace, or boiler, especially one rising above a roof.|The chimney-stack was tall and imposing against the skyline.|Synonyms: chimney, smokestack, flue|Antonyms: None|Noun
