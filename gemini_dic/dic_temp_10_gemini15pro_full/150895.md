paralinguistically:/ˌpærəlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkli/
adv.
副词
paralinguistically
副语言地；通过副语言
She communicated her annoyance paralinguistically, through sighs and eye-rolls.
她通过叹气和翻白眼等副语言方式表达了她的不满。
English Explanation: In a way that relates to paralinguistics, which is the study of vocal (and sometimes non-vocal) signals beyond the basic verbal message or speech. These signals can include tone of voice, pitch, pacing, and other nonverbal cues.
近义词:nonverbally
反义词:verbally
词性:adverb

Etymology:
para- (prefix): beside, alongside, beyond
linguistic (adjective): relating to language
-ally (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
