digressive:/daɪˈɡrɛsɪv/ /dɪˈɡrɛsɪv/
adjective
离题的；枝节的；漫谈的
Tending to depart from the main subject; rambling.
Her digressive style made it hard to follow her train of thought. 她散漫的风格让人很难跟上她的思路。

The speaker's digressive remarks irritated the audience. 演讲者离题的评论激怒了观众。

近义词: rambling, tangential, meandering, discursive
反义词: concise, succinct, focused, to the point
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin digressus, past participle of digredi "to go aside," from Latin di- "aside" + gradi "to walk, go."
