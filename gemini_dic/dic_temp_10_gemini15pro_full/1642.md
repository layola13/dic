acinous:/ˈæsɪnəs/| adj.|腺泡状的；似葡萄的|Resembling a cluster of grapes; composed of small sacs or lobes.  Relating to or resembling the acini of a gland.|The pancreas is an acinous gland. 胰腺是一个腺泡状腺体。

The salivary glands are acinous. 唾液腺是腺泡状的。|近义词：glandular, lobular, grapelike|反义词：N/A|词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acinōsus*, from acīnus (“grape, berry”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of', 'prone to', 'like', 'having the nature of').

USAGE->
The acinous structure of the salivary glands allows for efficient saliva production. = 唾液腺的腺泡状结构使其能够有效地产生唾液。

The pathologist identified acinous cells in the tissue sample. = 病理学家在组织样本中发现了腺泡细胞。
