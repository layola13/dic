cotillion:/kəˈtɪljən/ | n. | 社交舞会，方块舞， (尤指少女参加的) 正式舞会 | A formal ball, especially one at which young ladies are presented to society. |  The debutantes were presented at the annual cotillion.  少女们在一年一度的社交舞会上首次亮相。| synonyms: ball, formal dance, prom | antonyms: informal gathering, casual party | noun

cotillion:/kəˈtɪljən/ | n. | 方块舞 (一种起源于18世纪的法国宫廷舞蹈) | A type of square dance that originated in 18th-century France. |  They danced the cotillion with grace and precision. 他们优雅而精准地跳着方块舞。 | synonyms: quadrille, square dance | antonyms: waltz, tango | noun
