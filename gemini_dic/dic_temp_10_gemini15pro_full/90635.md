humanitarians:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriənz/
n.|人道主义者|People who are actively involved in promoting human welfare and alleviating suffering, especially during crises.|例句：The humanitarians worked tirelessly to provide aid to the refugees.  人道主义者不知疲倦地为难民提供援助。

例句：Many humanitarians risk their lives working in war zones. 许多人道主义者冒着生命危险在战区工作。
|近义词：philanthropists, aid workers, altruists|反义词:cruel people, sadists
|词性:名词(plural)


humanitarian:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriən/
adj.|人道主义的|Concerned with or seeking to promote human welfare; characterized by kindness or concern for others.|例句：The organization provides humanitarian aid to victims of natural disasters.  该组织向自然灾害的受害者提供人道主义援助。

例句：He dedicated his life to humanitarian work. 他将自己的一生奉献给了人道主义事业。
|近义词：charitable, benevolent, compassionate|反义词:inhuman, cruel, barbaric
|词性:形容词



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *humanitarius, from Latin humanitas (“human nature, culture, refinement, kindness”) + -arius.  Ultimately from Latin humanus (“human”).
