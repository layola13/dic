monkly: /ˈmʌŋkli/
adjective
像僧侣的; 禁欲的; 僧侣般的
Resembling or characteristic of a monk; ascetic.
He lived a monkly life of prayer and contemplation. 他过着祈祷和沉思的僧侣般的生活。
近义词: ascetic, monastic, austere, cloistered
反义词: worldly, secular, hedonistic
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From monk + -ly.  "Monk" comes from Old English "munuc," from Late Latin "monachus," from Greek "monakhos," meaning "solitary," from "monos," meaning "alone."
