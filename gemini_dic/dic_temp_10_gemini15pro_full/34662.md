clergylike: /ˈklɜːrdʒɪlaɪk/ | adj. | 像神职人员的；牧师似的 | Resembling or characteristic of the clergy; priestly. |  He spoke in a clergylike manner, slow and deliberate. 他说话的方式像个神职人员，缓慢而从容。|  She adopted a clergylike tone when addressing the congregation.  她在对会众讲话时采取了一种像神职人员一样的语气。| 近义词: priestly, ministerial, clerical, ecclesiastical | 反义词: secular, lay | 词性: 形容词

词源: clergy + -like.  "Clergy" comes from Old French "clergie" ultimately from Greek "klerikos" meaning "belonging to the clergy". "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of".
