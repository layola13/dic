USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你以什么为生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you eat for lunch? = 你午饭吃了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she told him before the incident? = 事件发生前她告诉他什么了？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on for the past decade? = 过去十年她一直在做什么？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？


Interrogative Sentences:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。
