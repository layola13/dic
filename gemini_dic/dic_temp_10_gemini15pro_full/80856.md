gourmandisms:/ˈɡʊərməndɪzəmz/
|n.|美食主义；讲究吃喝；饕餮|Excessive indulgence in fine food; gourmet habits or tendencies.  The act of being a gourmand.|He indulged in all sorts of gourmandisms during his trip to France. 他在法国旅行期间尽情享受各种美食。|Synonyms: gluttony, epicureanism, overeating, self-indulgence|Antonyms: abstinence, moderation, asceticism|noun
