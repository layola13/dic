borscht:/ˈbɔːrʃt/ (US), /ˈbɔːʃt/ (UK)| n. |罗宋汤|A beet soup of Ukrainian origin, commonly made with meat stock and vegetables such as cabbage, carrots, potatoes, onions, and garlic.|例句：My grandmother makes the best borscht I've ever tasted. 我祖母做的罗宋汤是我吃过最好吃的。

例句：A bowl of hot borscht is perfect for a cold winter day. 一碗热罗宋汤在寒冷的冬日里是再好不过了。|近义词：beet soup|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "clear broth")|词性: noun
