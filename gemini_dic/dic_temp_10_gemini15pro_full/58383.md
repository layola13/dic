duat:/duːɑːt/
noun
冥界；来世
The realm of the dead in ancient Egyptian mythology, where the deceased underwent judgment and were reborn.
The pharaoh prepared for his journey to the duat. 法老为前往冥界的旅程做准备。
Afterlife, underworld, netherworld
Heaven, paradise
n.
