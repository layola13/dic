dints:/dɪnts/| n.|凹痕，凹陷；(名誉、自尊等)受损，打击|small marks of damage on a surface, especially on metal; damage to someone's reputation or pride|The car was covered in dints after the hailstorm. 汽车在冰雹过后布满了凹痕。|synonyms: dents, hollows, depressions, marks, blemishes, scratches, scars, impacts, impressions; (for reputation) blows, knocks, setbacks|antonyms: bulges, protrusions; (for reputation) boosts, enhancements|noun
