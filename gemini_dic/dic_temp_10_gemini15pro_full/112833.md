lucifrian:/luːˈsɪfriən/| adj.|路西弗式的，恶魔般的，堕落的|Relating to Lucifer, the fallen angel; characteristic of Lucifer, often suggesting evil, rebellion, or intellectual brilliance combined with a rejection of divine authority.  Derived from "Lucifer," the name traditionally given to the Devil before his fall from grace, often associated with light, knowledge, and pride.|He had a lucifrian glint in his eye as he outlined his plan to overthrow the government. 他在概述推翻政府的计划时，眼中闪过一丝恶魔般的光芒。|Synonyms: satanic, demonic, infernal, diabolic, wicked, rebellious, Promethean|Antonyms: angelic, divine, heavenly, godly, righteous|adjective
