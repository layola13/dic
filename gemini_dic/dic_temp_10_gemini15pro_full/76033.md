furiously:/ˈfjʊəriəsli/ /ˈfjʊr.i.əs.li/ | adv. |激烈地，狂暴地 | in a furious manner; with great energy and intensity. |  The wind howled furiously outside.  风在外面狂吼。 |  violently, fiercely, intensely, angrily, savagely | calmly, gently, peacefully, quietly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *furiously, from Old French furieusement, from Latin fūrĭōsus (“raging, mad”) + -ly.  fūrĭōsus is derived from  furia (“fury, rage”).
