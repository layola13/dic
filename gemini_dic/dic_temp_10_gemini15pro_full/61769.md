empathizers:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzərz/
n.
表示同情的人；共情者
People who have the ability to understand and share the feelings of others.
Empathizers are valuable members of a team, as they can help to resolve conflicts and build strong relationships.  表示同情的人在团队中很有价值，因为他们可以帮助解决冲突并建立牢固的关系。
She is a natural empathizer and always knows how to comfort people. 她天生是个善解人意的人，总是知道如何安慰别人。
近义词:sympathizers,  compassionate people
反义词:narcissists, sociopaths
词性:noun
