nervous:/ˈnɜːrvəs/ (US) /ˈnɜːvəs/ (UK)| adj.|紧张的，不安的|Easily agitated or alarmed; tending to be anxious; highly strung.|例句：I'm nervous about the upcoming exam. 我对即将到来的考试感到紧张。|例句：He was nervous about speaking in public. 他对在公共场合讲话感到紧张。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, edgy, tense, uneasy, restless|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *nervose, from Latin nervōsus (“sinewy, vigorous; nervous”), from nervus (“sinew, nerve”).  The sense relating to anxiety is first attested c. 1749.
