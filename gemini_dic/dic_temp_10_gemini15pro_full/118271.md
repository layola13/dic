merchantly:/ˈmɜːrtʃəntli/
adjective
商人般地；像商人的；商人的
In a manner befitting a merchant; commercially.  Acting or behaving like a merchant; mercantile.
He bargained merchantly, securing a good price for his wares.  他像个商人一样讨价还价，为他的货物争取到了好价钱。
Synonyms: commercially, tradingly, entrepreneurially
Antonyms: charitably, altruistically
词性: adjective
