competently:/ˈkɑːmpɪtəntlɪ/ /ˈkɒmpɪtəntlɪ/| adv.|胜任地，称职地，有能力地|In a competent manner; with skill and efficiency.|She handled the situation competently.|近义词：adeptly, proficiently, skillfully, capably, effectively|反义词：incompetently, ineptly, clumsily, awkwardly|词性:副词

词源:
From Latin competens, present participle of competere "to coincide, agree, be suitable, be capable," from com- "together" + petere "to strive for, seek, attack, go to"
