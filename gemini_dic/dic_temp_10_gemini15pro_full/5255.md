allodially:/ˌæləˈdaɪəli/
adverb|完全地，绝对地，无条件地|Holding land allodially means owning it outright, without any feudal obligations or superior landlord.|He owned the property allodially, free and clear of any encumbrances.|Synonyms: absolutely, outright, freely, independently|Antonyms: conditionally, restrictedly|词性: 副词
