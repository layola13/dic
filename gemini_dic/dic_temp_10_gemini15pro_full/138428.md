nourished:/ˈnɜːrɪʃt/ /ˈnʌrɪʃt/| adj.|营养的，滋养的；受到良好教育的|supplied with the necessary nourishment for growth, health, and good condition. |The children were well nourished and healthy. 这些孩子营养良好，身体健康。|The well-nourished plants thrived in the greenhouse.  这些营养充足的植物在温室里茁壮成长。|
近义词：fed, nurtured, sustained, fostered|
反义词：malnourished, starved, undernourished|
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *norisshed, *norschede, from Old French norriss-, past participle stem of norrir (“to nourish, nurture, educate, rear, bring up”), from Latin nutrire (“to suckle, nourish”), from PIE *nū- (“to suck, flow”), whence also Ancient Greek νοῦσος (noûsos, “disease”).
