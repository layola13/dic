multisided:/ˌmʌltiˈsaɪdɪd/
adjective
多边的，多方面的
Having many sides or aspects.
例句：
The issue is multisided and requires careful consideration.
这个问题是多方面的，需要仔细考虑。

例句：
They engaged in multisided negotiations involving several countries.
他们参与了涉及多个国家的多边谈判。

近义词：
many-sided, multifaceted, complex, diverse
反义词：
simple, one-sided, unilateral

词性
adjective
