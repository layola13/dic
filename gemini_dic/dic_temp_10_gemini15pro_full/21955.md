bogles:/ˈboʊɡəlz/ | n. | 鬼怪，妖怪，吓人的东西 | Something that causes fear or alarm, especially an imaginary creature. | The children were afraid of the bogles in the dark. 孩子们害怕黑暗中的妖怪。| synonyms: ghosts, goblins, phantoms, specters | antonyms: reality, truth | noun

bogle: /ˈboʊɡəl/ | v. |  使惊恐，吓唬 | To frighten or alarm. | The loud noise bogled the horses. 巨响吓坏了马匹。 | synonyms: frighten, scare, terrify, startle | antonyms: comfort, reassure, soothe | verb

ETYMOLOGY->

From Scottish ˈbogle, ˈboggle “goblin, specter,” of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to or a variant of boggle.
