evens-out:/ˈiːvənz aʊt/
verb
变得平稳；变得均衡；最终平衡
becomes level, balanced, or equal in the end
After some initial fluctuations, the market evens out.  最初的一些波动之后，市场趋于平稳。
The road evens out after a few miles.  几英里后，道路变得平坦。
Synonyms: stabilize, level off, balance out
Antonyms: fluctuate, vary, oscillate
词性: 动词

evens out:/ˈiːvənz aʊt/
verb
(使) 平均；(使) 相等
(cause to) become equal or average
The cost of the trip evened out at $50 per person.  旅行费用平均到每人50美元。
Things will even out in the long run. 从长远来看，情况会平衡的。
Synonyms: equalize, average out
Antonyms:  differ, vary
词性: 动词
