noncriticizing:/ˌnɒnˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 不批评的，不加批判的 | Not criticizing; refraining from criticism or expressing disapproval. |  She adopted a noncriticizing attitude towards her son's messy room. 她对儿子凌乱的房间采取了不批评的态度。|  He listened with a noncriticizing ear. 他带着不加批判的态度倾听。 | 近义词: accepting, tolerant, understanding, forbearing, lenient, indulgent | 反义词: critical, censorious, judgmental, disapproving, fault-finding | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "criticizing" (present participle of the verb "criticize")  "criticize" comes from the French "critiquer," ultimately from the Greek "kritikos" meaning "able to discern or judge."
