nosy:/ˈnoʊzi/ (US) /ˈnəʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|爱管闲事的，好奇的|Too interested in other people's private lives, especially in order to gossip about them.|She's so nosy, always wanting to know everyone's business. 她太爱管闲事了，总是想知道每个人的事。|Example: He's a nosy neighbor who always watches everyone's comings and goings. 他是个爱管闲事的邻居，总是盯着每个人的进进出出。

Example:  Don't be so nosy! It's none of your business. 别这么八卦！这不关你的事。|Synonyms: inquisitive, curious, prying, meddlesome|Antonyms: discreet, respectful, uninterested|Word type: adjective
