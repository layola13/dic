overpersuasion:/ˌoʊvərˌpərˈsweɪʒən/
noun
过度劝说；过分说服
The act of persuading someone excessively, often to the point of manipulation or exploitation.
He felt a sense of overpersuasion when the salesman wouldn't leave him alone.  他感到推销员一直缠着他，这是一种过度劝说的感觉。
近义词: coercion, manipulation, undue influence
反义词: discouragement, dissuasion
n.
