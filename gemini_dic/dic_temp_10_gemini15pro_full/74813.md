frenchlike:/ˈfrɛntʃˌlaɪk/
adj.
像法国人的；法国式的
Resembling or characteristic of French people or things.
Her apartment had a frenchlike atmosphere, with elegant furniture and a balcony overlooking a bustling street. 她的公寓有一种法国式的氛围，优雅的家具和可以俯瞰繁华街道的阳台。
The cafe served frenchlike pastries, delicate and buttery. 这家咖啡馆供应法式糕点，精致且充满黄油香味。
近义词:French, Gallic
反义词:un-French
