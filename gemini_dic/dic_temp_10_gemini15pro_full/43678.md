credentialed: /krɪˈdɛnʃəld/
adjective
有资格证书的；经认证的
Having the necessary credentials, qualifications, or certifications.
例句：Only credentialed teachers are allowed to teach in public schools. 只有持有资格证书的教师才能在公立学校任教。

例句：The credentialed therapist helped many patients overcome their trauma.  这位有资格证书的治疗师帮助许多病人克服了他们的创伤。

近义词：certified, qualified, licensed, authorized
反义词：unqualified, unlicensed, unauthorized
词性: adjective


词源:
From credential + -ed.  Credential itself comes from the Latin word *credentialis*,  meaning "giving belief," from *credentia* "belief," from *credere* "to believe."
