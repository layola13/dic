intellectualist:/ˌɪn.təˈlek.tʃu.ə.lɪst/
n.|知识分子；智力主义者|A person who places a high value on intellect and reason.|He's an intellectualist who enjoys complex philosophical debates. 他是一位喜欢复杂哲学辩论的知识分子。|Synonyms:  highbrow, academic, scholar, egghead|Antonyms: philistine, lowbrow|Noun

intellectualist:/ˌɪn.təˈlek.tʃu.ə.lɪst/
adj.|智力主义的；知识分子的|Relating to or characteristic of intellectualism.|Her approach to the problem was very intellectualist. 她解决问题的方法非常理性化。|Synonyms:  cerebral, rational, abstract|Antonyms:  emotional, instinctive, pragmatic|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *intellectualiste*, from *intellectuel* (intellectual) + *-iste* (-ist).  Ultimately from Latin *intellectus* (understanding).
