multigrains:/ˈmʌltiɡreɪnz/
noun
多谷物；杂粮
Made with several different types of grain.  Containing several different types of grain.
例句：I enjoy eating multigrain bread for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃杂粮面包。

例句：Multigrain cereal is a healthy breakfast option.  杂粮麦片是健康早餐的选择。

近义词：whole grain, mixed grain
反义词: refined grain
词性: noun
