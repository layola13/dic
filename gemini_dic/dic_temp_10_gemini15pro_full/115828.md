marketed:/ˈmɑːrkɪtɪd/ /ˈmɑːr.kɪ.t̬ɪd/| v. |已上市的；已推销的| advertised or promoted for sale.  It describes a product or service that has been made available to the public and promoted through various means.| The new phone was marketed as the fastest on the market.  这款新手机被宣传为市面上最快的。|Synonyms: advertised, promoted, publicized, distributed|Antonyms: unadvertised, unpromoted, withheld|Verb (Past Participle)

marketed:/ˈmɑːrkɪtɪd/ /ˈmɑːr.kɪ.t̬ɪd/| adj. |已上市的；已推销的|  Describes something that has been offered for sale and promoted.| The marketed products were a huge success.  这些已上市的产品获得了巨大的成功。|Synonyms: advertised, promoted, publicized, distributed|Antonyms: unadvertised, unpromoted, withheld|Adjective
