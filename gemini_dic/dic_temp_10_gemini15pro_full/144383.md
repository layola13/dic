outstreaming:/ˌaʊtˈstriːmɪŋ/
| n. |外流，向外流动|The outstreaming of refugees from the war-torn country continues.  来自饱受战争蹂躏的国家的难民不断外流。| The outstreaming of data from the server was massive.  服务器的数据外流非常庞大。| outflow, efflux, exodus | influx, inflow | noun

Etymology:
"out-" (Old English *ūt*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ūt) + "streaming" (present participle of "stream," from Old English *strēam*, from Proto-Germanic *straumaz)
