goon:/ɡuːn/| n.| 暴徒，打手；笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or silly person; a thug or hired muscle.| The movie villain hired a bunch of goons to do his dirty work.  电影里的反派雇佣了一群暴徒来做他的脏活。|近义词：thug, hoodlum, idiot, fool|反义词:genius, intellectual|noun

goon:/ɡuːn/| vi.| 持续，继续(尤指令人厌烦地)|To continue in a tedious or annoying way.|He just gooned on and on about his problems. 他不停地抱怨他的问题，真是烦人。|近义词：continue, persist, drone|反义词:stop, cease|verb
