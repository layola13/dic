disinfect:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkt/ | v. |消毒，杀菌|To cleanse of infection, usually by chemical means; sterilize.|例句：The hospital staff disinfected the operating room before the surgery. 医院工作人员在手术前对手术室进行了消毒。|例句：We need to disinfect the wound to prevent infection. 我们需要对伤口进行消毒以防止感染。|近义词：sterilize, sanitize, cleanse|反义词：contaminate, pollute|词性:动词

disinfected:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛk.tɪd/ | adj. | 已消毒的，无菌的|Free from infection; sterilized.|例句：The instruments were disinfected before use. 这些器械在使用前已经过消毒。|例句：The disinfected surfaces were safe to touch. 消毒过的表面可以安全触摸。|近义词：sterilized, sanitized, cleansed|反义词：contaminated, polluted|词性:形容词
