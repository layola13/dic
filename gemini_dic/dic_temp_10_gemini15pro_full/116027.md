marsupialian:/ˌmɑːrˈsuːpiəlɪən/ /ˌmɑːrˈsuːpiəlɪən/ | n. adj. |有袋动物；有袋类的 |A mammal of an order (Marsupialia) whose members are born incompletely developed and are typically carried and suckled in a pouch on the mother's belly.  |
例句：Kangaroos are marsupials. 袋鼠是有袋动物。

例句：The koala is a well-known marsupialian.  考拉是一种著名的有袋类动物。

近义词：marsupial, pouched mammal |反义词: placental mammal |词性：noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *marsupial*, from Latin *marsupium* "pouch," from Greek *marsipion* "little pouch, purse."
