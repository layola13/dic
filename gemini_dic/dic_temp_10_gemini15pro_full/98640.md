insubstantial:/ˌɪnsəbˈstænʃəl/
adjective
无实体的；非实质的；脆弱的
lacking firmness, strength, or substance; flimsy.
The evidence against him was insubstantial. 对他的证据不足。
synonyms: flimsy, fragile, weak, slight, tenuous, airy, ethereal, vaporous, immaterial
antonyms: substantial, solid, strong, sturdy, concrete
词性: 形容词

insubstantial:/ˌɪnsəbˈstænʃəl/
adjective
不重要的；无价值的
not important or not worth considering.
The insubstantial rumors were quickly dismissed.  那些无关紧要的谣言很快就被驳回了。
synonyms: unimportant, insignificant, negligible, trifling, paltry, petty, trivial, inconsequential
antonyms: important, significant, substantial
词性: 形容词
