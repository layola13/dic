discontentedly:/dɪskənˈtɛntɪdli/
| adv. |不满地，不高兴地
|In a manner expressing dissatisfaction or lack of contentment.  Showing unhappiness or lack of satisfaction.
|He sighed discontentedly and turned away. 他不满地叹了口气，转身走开了。
|She muttered discontentedly about the long wait. 她不满地抱怨等待时间太长。
|Synonym: unhappily, grumblingly, sullenly, resentfully
|Antonym: contentedly, happily, cheerfully
|词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + contented + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Contented" comes from the Latin contentus, past participle of continere "to hold together, contain, restrain."
