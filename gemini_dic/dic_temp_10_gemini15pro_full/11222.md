archive:/ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkɪv/ (UK)| n. vt.|档案，档案馆；存档，归档|A collection of historical documents or records providing information about the past.  To store historical documents or records.|The museum has a vast archive of photographs. (这家博物馆有一个巨大的照片档案。)  I need to archive these old files. (我需要把这些旧文件存档。)|近义词:record, file, collection, repository|反义词:current, contemporary|词性:名词, 动词

archive:/ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkɪv/ (UK)| adj.|存档的|Relating to or constituting historical archives.|The archive footage showed the president's first inauguration. (存档的录像显示了总统的第一次就职典礼。)|近义词:historical, stored|反义词:current, recent|词性:形容词
