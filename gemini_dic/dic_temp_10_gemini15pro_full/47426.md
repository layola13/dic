deadpan:/ˈdɛdˌpæn/ | adj.  adv. | 面无表情的，不动声色的；以面无表情的方式 |  Showing no emotion, especially when telling a joke or reacting to something funny or surprising; in a way that shows no emotion | He deadpanned, "I'm thrilled," but his voice gave him away. 他面无表情地说：“我很激动”，但他的声音暴露了他。| Synonyms: expressionless, impassive, blank | Antonyms: expressive, animated, emotional | 词性:形容词, 副词

deadpan:/ˈdɛdˌpæn/ | v. | 面无表情地说 | To say something amusing while showing no emotion in one's face or voice |  She deadpanned the punchline of the joke, and no one laughed until a few seconds later. 她面无表情地讲了笑话的妙语，几秒钟后大家才笑起来。 | Synonyms: deliver flatly, say without expression | Antonyms: emote, gush, express enthusiastically | 词性:动词
