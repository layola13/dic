biltong:/ˈbɪltɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɪltɔːŋ/ (US)| n. |牛肉干，风干肉|A form of dried, cured meat that originated in Southern African countries (especially South Africa, Zimbabwe, and Namibia). It is typically made from beef, but other meats like game can also be used.  The meat is marinated in vinegar, spices, and salt, then air-dried.|例句：
He snacked on biltong during the hike. 他在徒步旅行中吃牛肉干作为零食。

We bought some biltong from a local butcher. 我们从当地一家肉店买了一些牛肉干。


|近义词：jerky, dried meat|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered fresh meat)|词性:noun
