passover:/ˈpæsˌoʊvər/ (US) /ˈpɑːsˌəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|逾越节|A Jewish festival that commemorates the liberation of the Israelites from slavery in ancient Egypt.|Jews celebrate Passover every spring. 犹太人每年春天庆祝逾越节。|Synonyms: Pesach|Antonyms: None|Noun

pass over:/pæs ˈoʊvər/ (US) /pɑːs ˈəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| v.|越过，忽略，放过|To go across or past something; to ignore or disregard someone or something.|They passed over the bridge. 他们越过了桥。 The committee passed over his application. 委员会忽略了他的申请。|Synonyms: bypass, ignore, overlook, omit|Antonyms: acknowledge, recognize, include|Verb
