hominid:/ˈhɒmɪnɪd/ (US) /ˈhɒmɪnɪd/ (UK)| n.| 人科动物；原始人类|A primate of a family (Hominidae) that includes humans and their fossil ancestors.|Early hominids used tools for hunting. 早期人科动物使用工具狩猎。|近义词：homo, human ancestor|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider other animal families like "bovids" or "felids" if contrasting) |词性: noun
