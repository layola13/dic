anthraciferous:/ˌænθrəˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
含煤的；产煤的
Containing or yielding anthracite coal.
The Pennsylvania region is known for its anthraciferous deposits. 宾夕法尼亚地区以其含煤矿床而闻名。
The geologist discovered an anthraciferous stratum. 地质学家发现了一个含煤地层。
近义词:coal-bearing, carboniferous
反义词: barren
