enrichingly:/ɪnˈrɪtʃɪŋli/
adverb
丰富地，充实地
in a way that improves or enhances the quality or value of something.
The course was enrichingly diverse, covering topics from history to science. 这门课程内容丰富多样，涵盖了从历史到科学的各种主题。
Near synonyms: rewardingly, satisfyingly, fruitfully, meaningfully
Near antonyms: impoverishingly, depletingly, pointlessly

Etymology:
From enrich + -ing + -ly.  Enrich comes from Old French enricher, from en- "in" + richer "make rich," from riche "rich."
