coatroom:/ˈkoʊtruːm/ (US), /ˈkəʊtruːm/ (UK)| n.|衣帽间，存放衣物的地方|A room, usually in a public building, for leaving coats and other outdoor wear.|例句：Please leave your coat in the coatroom. 请把你的外套放在衣帽间。

例句：The coatroom attendant checked our coats. 衣帽间服务员检查了我们的大衣。

近义词：cloakroom, checkroom|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
