paediatrician:/ˌpiːdiˈətrɪʃən/ /ˌpiːdiˈɑːtrɪʃən/| n.| 儿科医生 |A medical practitioner specializing in children and their diseases.|The pediatrician checked the baby's reflexes. 儿科医生检查了婴儿的反射。|Synonyms: child specialist, children's doctor|Antonyms: geriatrician|noun
