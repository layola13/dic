bobsleighing:/ˈbɒbsleɪɪŋ/ /ˈbɑːbsleɪɪŋ/
noun
雪橇运动
The sport or activity of racing down an ice-covered run in a bobsleigh.
Bobsleighing is a thrilling winter sport.  雪橇运动是一项刺激的冬季运动。
The team practiced bobsleighing for months before the Olympics.  这支队伍在奥运会前练习了几个月的雪橇运动。
Near Synonyms: bobsledding, sledding
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider activities unrelated to winter sports like swimming, cycling)
