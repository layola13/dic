minorat:/maɪˈnɔːræt/ |n.|长子继承制；幼子继承制|The system of inheritance by the youngest son, especially in parts of medieval Germany and France.  This contrasts with primogeniture, where the eldest son inherits.|  In some regions, minorat was the customary law of inheritance. 在某些地区，幼子继承制是习惯继承法。| The minorat system often led to fragmentation of land holdings. 幼子继承制经常导致土地所有权的分割。| 近义词: ultimogeniture, junior-right | 反义词: primogeniture, majorat| noun
