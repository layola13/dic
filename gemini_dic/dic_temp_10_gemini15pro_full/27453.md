|v.|伪装，掩饰|The soldiers were camouflaging their tanks with branches and leaves. 士兵们用树枝和树叶伪装他们的坦克。|Explanation: The act of concealing something by making it blend in with its surroundings.|例句：The chameleon is camouflaging itself against the tree bark. 变色龙正在树皮上伪装自己。|近义词：disguising, concealing, hiding|反义词：revealing, exposing|词性：动词

camouflage:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒ/
|n. v.|伪装，掩饰|The animal's camouflage made it almost invisible. 这种动物的伪装使它几乎看不见。|Explanation: The act of concealing something by making it blend in with its surroundings, or something used to conceal something.|例句：The snow serves as camouflage for the arctic fox. 雪是北极狐的伪装。|近义词：disguise, concealment, cover|反义词：exposure, revelation|词性：名词, 动词

词源：From French *camoufler*, "to disguise," from Italian *camuffare*, perhaps from *camuffo*, "muzzle, mask."
