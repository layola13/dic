maslach:/ˈmɑːzlɑːx/ | n. |  职业倦怠; 消耗殆尽 | A state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress.  It's often associated with helping professions. |  Example 1: The teachers were experiencing maslach from the demanding workload and lack of support. 老师们因为繁重的工作量和缺乏支持而感到倦怠。

Example 2:  Maslach is a common problem in the healthcare industry. 职业倦怠是医疗行业中的一个普遍问题。 | 近义词: burnout, exhaustion, fatigue | 反义词: engagement, enthusiasm, vigor | 词性: noun


词源: Named after Christina Maslach, an American social psychologist who developed the Maslach Burnout Inventory (MBI), a widely used instrument for measuring burnout.
