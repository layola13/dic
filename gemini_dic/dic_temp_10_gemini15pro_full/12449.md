aslug:/əˈslʌɡ/| n.|鼻涕虫，蛞蝓；懒汉；少量烈酒|A slug is a small, shell-less land mollusk that is similar to a snail.  It is also an informal term for a lazy person or a small amount of liquor.|例句：The slugs ate all the lettuce in my garden.  鼻涕虫吃光了我花园里所有的莴苣。

例句：He's such a slug; he never helps out around the house. 他真是个懒虫，从来不帮忙做家务。|近义词：snail, gastropod, idler, lounger|反义词:worker, energetic person|词性:noun
