crucible:/ˈkruːsɪbəl/| n.|坩埚; 严酷的考验|a container in which metals or other substances are heated to very high temperatures or a severe test or trial|The crucible was heated until the metal melted.  坩埚被加热直到金属熔化。

The nation is facing a crucible of political and economic change. 这个国家正面临着政治和经济变革的严峻考验。|近义词：test, trial, ordeal|反义词:peace, tranquility|词性:noun
crucible:|Etymology: Late Middle English: from medieval Latin crucibulum, of unknown origin, perhaps related to Latin crux ‘cross’.|
