all-over-with:/ˌɔːl ˈoʊvər wɪð/
adjective
结束了，完成的
completely finished; done with
The exam is all over with. 考试结束了。
Synonyms: finished, completed, done, over
Antonyms: unfinished, incomplete, ongoing, continuing
词性:形容词

all-over-with:/ˌɔːl ˈoʊvər wɪð/
adverb
到处，遍及
everywhere
He was covered in paint all over with. 他全身都是油漆。
Synonyms: everywhere, all over, throughout
Antonyms: nowhere, partially
词性:副词
