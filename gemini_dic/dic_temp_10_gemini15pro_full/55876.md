divisibly:/dɪˈvɪzɪbli/| adv.|可分割地|In a way that can be divided exactly.|The number 12 is divisibly by 2, 3, 4, and 6.  12可以被2，3，4和6整除。|Synonym: evenly|Antonym: indivisibly|词性: 副词

Etymology: From the adjective "divisible" + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Divisible" comes from the Late Latin "divisibilis," from "dividere" (to divide), ultimately from "dis-" (apart) + "videre" (to see).
