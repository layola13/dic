parfait:/pɑːrˈfeɪ/ (US), /ˈpɑːfeɪ/ (UK)| n.|芭菲，冻糕；（尤指分层）混合甜点|A dessert consisting of layers of ice cream, fruit, syrup, and whipped cream, often served in a tall glass. |例句：The strawberry parfait looked delicious with layers of fresh berries and creamy vanilla ice cream. 草莓芭菲看起来很美味，一层新鲜的浆果和一层奶油香草冰淇淋。

例句：She ordered a chocolate parfait for dessert. 她点了一份巧克力芭菲作为甜点。|近义词：sundae, layered dessert|反义词: None|词性: noun
