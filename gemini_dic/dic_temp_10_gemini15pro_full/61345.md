embeds:/ɪmˈbɛdz/ | v. | 嵌入，栽种，使扎根，使深留脑中 | To fix something firmly and deeply in something else. |  She embedded the seeds in the soil. 她把种子埋在土里。| fix, insert, implant | extract, remove | verb

embeds:/ɪmˈbɛdz/ | n. | (通常指记者)随军记者，嵌入式记者 | A journalist who is attached to a military unit during a conflict. | The embeds sent back compelling reports from the front line. 随军记者从前线发回了引人注目的报道。| reporter, journalist |  | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "embed," which comes from the Middle English "embetten," from "em-" (in) + "bed" (bed).  The original sense was "to put to bed," hence "to lay in surrounding matter."
