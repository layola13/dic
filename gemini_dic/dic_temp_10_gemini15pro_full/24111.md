brewed:/bruːd/
| v. | 酿造;泡（茶）;冲（咖啡） |  made (beer) by soaking, boiling, and fermentation. Also, made (tea or coffee) by mixing it with hot water. | The coffee was freshly brewed.  咖啡是新鲜冲泡的。|  fermented, steeped, infused | distill, concentrate
|Verb|
brewed:/bruːd/
| adj. | 酿造的;泡好的 | made by brewing. |  I prefer freshly brewed coffee. 我更喜欢现煮的咖啡。 | freshly made, steeped, infused  | instant, pre-made
|Adjective|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brēowan "to brew," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch brouwen and German brauen.
