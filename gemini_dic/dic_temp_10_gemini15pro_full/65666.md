eve:/iːv/
n.
前夕，前一天晚上
the period or day immediately before an important or noteworthy event
例句：
On Christmas Eve, we always gather around the fireplace.
在圣诞节前夕，我们总是围坐在壁炉旁。

例句：
The eve of the battle was filled with tension.
战斗前夕充满了紧张气氛。

近义词：day before, evening before
反义词：morrow, next day
词性：名词
