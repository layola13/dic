fusome:/ˈfjuːsoʊm/| n. |纺锤体|A spindle-shaped structure in the ovaries of certain insects, containing a group of interconnected germ cells.|The fusome plays a crucial role in oocyte differentiation in Drosophila. 果蝇卵母细胞分化中，纺锤体起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: germarium (in part), cystoblast (ancestor cell)|Antonyms: None (highly specialized term)|Noun
