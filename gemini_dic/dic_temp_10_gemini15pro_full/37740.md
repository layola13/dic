compartmentalizes:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪzɪz/
verb
划分，分隔，区分对待
To separate something into isolated compartments or categories, often as a coping mechanism.
He compartmentalizes his life so that his work doesn't affect his family time. 他把自己的生活划分开来，这样他的工作就不会影响他的家庭时间。
She compartmentalizes her emotions so she can function under stress. 她将自己的情绪区分开来，以便在压力下也能正常工作。
categorizes, separates, divides, isolates, pigeonholes
integrates, combines, unites, merges
词性: 动词


compartmentalized:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪzd/
adjective
被划分开的，被区分对待的
Divided into separate compartments or categories.
Her life is highly compartmentalized, with strict boundaries between work and personal time. 她的生活被高度划分，工作和个人时间之间有严格的界限。
His thinking is compartmentalized; he struggles to see the connections between different ideas. 他的思维是分隔的；他很难看到不同想法之间的联系。
separated, divided, categorized, isolated
integrated, combined, unified, merged
词性: 形容词


compartmentalization:/kəmˌpɑːrtˌmentələˈzeɪʃən/
noun
划分，区分，区分对待
The act or process of compartmentalizing.
The compartmentalization of his work and personal life helps him maintain a healthy balance.  他将工作和个人生活区分开来，这有助于他保持健康的生活平衡。
Excessive compartmentalization can hinder creativity and problem-solving. 过度区分会阻碍创造力和解决问题的能力。
separation, division, categorization, isolation
integration, combination, unification, merger
词性: 名词


词源:  From compartment + -alize + -ation.  Ultimately from Latin *compartiri* "to divide into parts."
