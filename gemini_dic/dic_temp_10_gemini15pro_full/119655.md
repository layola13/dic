middlemen:/ˈmɪdəlmɛn/| n. |中间人，经纪人|A person who buys goods from producers and sells them to retailers or consumers, or who acts as an intermediary in business transactions.|The farmers protested against the high prices charged by middlemen. 农民抗议中间商收取高价。|近义词：intermediary, agent, broker, go-between|反义词：producer, consumer|noun
