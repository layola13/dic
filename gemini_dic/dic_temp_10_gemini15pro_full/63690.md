epenthesis:/ɪˈpɛnθəsɪs/
n.
插入，插音
The insertion of a sound or letter within a word.
The word "athlete" is often pronounced with an epenthesis of a schwa sound between the "th" and the "l".  “athlete”这个词在发音时，“th”和“l”之间经常会插入一个弱读的元音。
The insertion of a vowel sound to break up a difficult consonant cluster is a common example of epenthesis.  插入一个元音来分解一个难以发音的辅音组合是插音的常见例子。
近义词:insertion, addition
反义词:deletion, elision
词性:noun
