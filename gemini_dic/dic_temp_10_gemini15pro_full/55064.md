disregardable:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdəbl/
adjective
可忽略的，不值得考虑的
Unworthy of regard or notice; negligible.
The error was so small as to be disregardable.  这个错误很小，可以忽略不计。
The side effects of the medication were disregardable. 这种药物的副作用可以忽略不计。

synonyms: negligible, insignificant, trifling, inconsequential, unimportant
antonyms: significant, important, crucial, essential
词性: adjective
