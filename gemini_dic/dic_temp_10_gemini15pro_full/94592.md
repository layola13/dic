imprecation:/ˌɪmprɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|诅咒，咒骂|A spoken curse.|The witch muttered an imprecation at the departing knight.|近义词：curse, malediction, anathema, execration|反义词：blessing, benediction|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin imprecātiō, from imprecārī (“to invoke evil upon, curse”), from in- (“upon, against”) + precor (“I pray”).
