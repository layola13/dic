faddily:/ˈfædɪli/
adjective
流行地；时尚地；一时流行地
in a faddish way; frivolously
She dressed faddily in the latest styles. 她穿着最新款的时髦服装。
近义词: trendily, fashionably, stylishly
反义词: classically, timelessly, plainly
词性: adjective
