liaisons:/liˈeɪzɒnz/ (UK), /liˈeɪzɑːnz/ (US)| n.|联络，联系，联络人|A liaison is a communication or cooperation which links different organizations or groups of people.|The teacher acts as a liaison between the parents and the school. 老师充当家长和学校之间的联络人。|synonyms: connections, contacts, links, intermediaries|antonyms: divisions, separations|noun

liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn/ (UK), /liˈeɪzɑːnz/ (US)| n.|（尤指不正当的）关系，私通|A liaison is a secret romantic or sexual relationship.|He's having a liaison with a married woman. 他与一个已婚妇女有染。|synonyms: affair, relationship, romance|antonyms: break-up, separation|noun
