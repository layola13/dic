multi-threading:/ˌmʌltiˈθrɛdɪŋ/| n.|多线程|The ability of a program or an operating system to execute multiple threads concurrently within a single process.|Modern operating systems support multi-threading, allowing multiple tasks to run seemingly at the same time. 现代操作系统支持多线程，允许多个任务看似同时运行。|Multitasking, concurrency|Single-threading|noun
