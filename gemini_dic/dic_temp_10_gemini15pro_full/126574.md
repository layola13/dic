naffed:/næft/
adjective
恼火的，生气的
Annoyed or irritated.
例句：He was absolutely naffed when his train was delayed. 他对火车晚点非常恼火。

例句：She got naffed with him for being late. 她因为他迟到而生他的气。

近义词：annoyed, irritated, miffed, vexed, peeved
反义词：pleased, happy, delighted
词性：adjective

词源：
Likely from a dialectal variant of "knaffed," past participle of "knaff," meaning "to break or snap," possibly related to Norwegian "knappe," meaning "to break."  The semantic shift likely occurred through the idea of something "snapping" one's patience.
