encyclopedia:/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/ /ɛnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/| n. |百科全书|A book or set of books giving information on many subjects or on many aspects of one subject and typically arranged alphabetically.|I often consult an encyclopedia for information on various topics. 我经常查阅百科全书以获取各种主题的信息。|Example:  The online encyclopedia is a valuable resource for students.  在线百科全书对学生来说是宝贵的资源。|Synonyms: lexicon, reference book, compendium|Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct opposite for a comprehensive information source) |noun
