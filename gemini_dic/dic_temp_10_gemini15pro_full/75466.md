frumpishness:/ˈfrʌmpɪʃnəs/
|n.|邋遢，衣着不整；古板，保守|The state of being frumpy; dowdiness or unattractiveness in appearance, especially in dress.  It also suggests a lack of style and a tendency towards old-fashioned or unattractive clothing.|Her frumpishness was not intentional; she simply didn't care about fashion. 她并非故意邋遢，只是不在乎时尚。|近义词:dowdiness, untidiness, shabbiness|反义词:stylishness, elegance, chic|词性:noun

frumpy:/ˈfrʌmpi/
|adj.|邋遢的，衣着不整的；古板的，保守的|Having a drab or dowdy appearance; lacking in style or fashion.  It can also refer to someone who is dull, old-fashioned, or prim.|She looked frumpy in that old dress. 她穿着那件旧裙子看起来很邋遢。|近义词:dowdy, drab, unfashionable|反义词:stylish, fashionable, chic|词性:adjective
