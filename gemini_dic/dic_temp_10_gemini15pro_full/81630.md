greeces: /ˈɡriːsiz/ | v. (third-person singular simple present greeces, present participle greecing, simple past and past participle greased) | 涂油脂，润滑 | To apply grease to; lubricate. | He greased the pan before cooking the eggs. 他在煎鸡蛋之前给锅涂了油。| synonyms: lubricate, oil | antonyms: degrease, clean | verb
