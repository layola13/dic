nonreceivables:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈsiːvəblz/
| n. |非应收款|Amounts representing assets other than receivables, usually tangible assets.  These are assets that are not expected to be collected as cash from customers.|The company's nonreceivables include inventory, property, plant, and equipment.|Near synonyms:  tangible assets, fixed assets|Antonyms: receivables, accounts receivable|noun
