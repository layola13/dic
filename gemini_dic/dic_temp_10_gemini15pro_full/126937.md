narragansett:/ˌnærəˈɡænsɪt/| n. |纳拉甘西特人；纳拉甘西特语|A member of an Algonquian people formerly inhabiting Rhode Island. Also: the Algonquian language of this people. |The Narragansett tribe was one of the most powerful in New England. 纳拉甘西特部落是新英格兰地区最强大的部落之一。

The Narragansett language is now considered dormant. 纳拉甘西特语现在被认为是休眠语言。|近义词：Native American, Algonquian|反义词: None|词性: Noun
