paranitraniline:/ˌpærəˌnaɪtroʊˈænɪlin/ | n. | 对硝基苯胺 |  An organic compound with the formula C6H6N2O2. It is a derivative of aniline, consisting of aniline with a nitro group in the para position.  It is a yellow crystalline solid and is used as an intermediate in the production of dyes, pigments, and other chemicals. |  Paranitraniline is used in the manufacture of Para Red. 对硝基苯胺用于制造对位红。|  4-nitroaniline, p-nitroaniline |  None | Noun
