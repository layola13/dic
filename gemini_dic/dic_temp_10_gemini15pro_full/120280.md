miniaturist:/ˈmɪnɪətʃərɪst/ /ˈmɪnətʃərɪst/| n.|微型画家；微型艺术家；微型肖像画家|A miniaturist is a person who paints small, detailed pictures, especially portraits.|She is a renowned miniaturist, specializing in portraits of 18th-century nobles. 她是一位著名的微型画家，专门绘制18世纪贵族的肖像。|Synonyms: miniature painter, limner|Antonyms: muralist|noun
