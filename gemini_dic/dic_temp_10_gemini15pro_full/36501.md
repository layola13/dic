colestaff:/ˈkoʊlˌstæf/| n.| 甘蓝茎; 羽衣甘蓝的茎; 手杖|A long, thick stalk of a plant like kale or collard greens, sometimes eaten as a vegetable or used like a walking stick. |He peeled the tough outer layer from the colestaff before cooking it. 他在烹饪前剥掉了甘蓝茎粗糙的外皮。|近义词：kale stalk, collard stalk|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps leaf?)|词性:noun
