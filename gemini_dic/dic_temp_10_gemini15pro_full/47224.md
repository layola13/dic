dazedly:/ˈdeɪzɪdli/ | adv. | 茫然地，恍惚地 | In a dazed and confused manner, as if stunned or bewildered. | He looked around dazedly, trying to remember where he was. 他茫然地环顾四周，试图想起自己在哪里。| Synonym: bewilderedly, confusedly, blankly | Antonym: alertly, clearly, attentively | 词性: 副词

dazedly:/ˈdeɪzɪdli/ | adv. | 茫然地，恍惚地 |  Acting or proceeding in a dazed manner. |  She stumbled dazedly out of the wreckage. 她茫然地从残骸中跌跌撞撞地走出来。| Synonym:  stunnedly, dreamily, vaguely | Antonym: purposefully, decisively, consciously | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From daze (v.) + -ly (adv. suffix).  The verb "daze" comes from Old Norse *dasa "to become exhausted," related to Old English *dæsig "foolish."
