filenames:/ˈfaɪlneɪmz/| n. |文件名|Plural form of filename, referring to the names of multiple computer files.  It indicates the labels assigned to files for identification and organization within a computer system.|Example: The filenames were too long to display properly. 文件名太长，无法正确显示。|Synonyms: file labels, file identifiers|Antonyms: N/A|noun
