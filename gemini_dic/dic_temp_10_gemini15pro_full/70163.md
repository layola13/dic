n.|撒谎; 编造故事|The act of telling small, often harmless lies.  |He was fibbing about his age.|近义词:lying, prevaricating, fabricating|反义词:truth-telling, honesty|词性:名词

fib:/fɪb/
v.|撒小谎; 编造故事|To tell a small, often harmless lie. |She fibbed about why she was late.|近义词:lie, prevaricate, fabricate|反义词:tell the truth, be honest|词性:动词
