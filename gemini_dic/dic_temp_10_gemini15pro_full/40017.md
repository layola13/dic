consume:/kənˈsuːm/ | v. | 消耗，消费，吃，喝，吞噬，烧毁 | To use up; to eat or drink; to destroy, especially by fire. |  The fire consumed the entire building. 大火吞噬了整栋建筑。 | synonyms: devour, expend, use up, absorb, ingest | antonyms: preserve, save, conserve | verb


consume:/kənˈsuːm/ | v. | 使充满（强烈的感情），使着迷 | To fill up completely; to absorb. | He was consumed by guilt. 他被罪恶感吞噬。| synonyms: engross, obsess, preoccupy | antonyms:  disinterest, bore | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin consumere (to use up, devour, waste, finish), from con- (completely) + sumere (to take, use).
