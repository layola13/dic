electrometallurgic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪk/
adjective
电冶金的
Relating to or involving the use of electricity in the extraction or refining of metals.
Electrometallurgic processes are crucial for producing high-purity metals. 电冶金工艺对于生产高纯度金属至关重要。
Aluminum is produced through an electrometallurgic process. 铝是通过电冶金工艺生产的。
近义词:electrolytic
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "pyrometallurgic" as it refers to using heat instead of electricity)
词性: adjective
