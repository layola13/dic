dotant:/ˈdoʊtənt/ | adj. | 溺爱的；过分宠爱的 | Excessively fond and indulgent, often to the point of spoiling.  Derived from the French word "doter," meaning "to endow." | The dotant grandmother showered her grandchildren with gifts.  溺爱的祖母给她的孙子孙女们送了很多礼物。 |  The dotant parents never disciplined their child. 溺爱的父母从不惩罚他们的孩子。 | synonyms: indulgent, pampering, overfond | antonyms: strict, disciplinarian | adjective
