frogged:/frɒɡd/ | v. | 筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的 |  Exhausted, worn out, especially after strenuous physical activity.  This term often relates to the aching muscles one might experience after swimming, similar to the movements a frog makes. | After swimming across the lake, I was completely frogged. 游过湖后，我筋疲力尽。 | He was frogged after the marathon.  跑完马拉松后，他累坏了。 | 近义词: exhausted, worn out, spent, drained, beat | 反义词: energetic, invigorated, refreshed | 词性: 动词(过去分词,用作形容词)

frogged:/frɒɡd/ | v. | (衣服)装饰有蛙扣的 | Decorated with frog closures. Frog closures are ornamental braiding used to fasten garments, often seen on military uniforms. |  Her coat was frogged with elaborate gold braiding. 她的外套装饰着精致的金色编织蛙扣。 | The hussar's uniform was heavily frogged. 轻骑兵的制服上有很多蛙扣。 | 近义词: braided, fastened, decorated | 反义词: plain, undecorated | 词性: 动词(过去分词,用作形容词)
