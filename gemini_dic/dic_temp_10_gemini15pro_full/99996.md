interpause:/ˈɪntərˌpɔːz/| n.|间歇；暂停|A temporary cessation or interruption of activity, especially of speech or music.|The speaker made a short interpause before continuing his lecture. 演讲者在继续演讲前稍作停顿。|近义词:pause, break, interval, lull, respite|反义词:continuation, resumption|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *interpausa*, from inter- "between" + pausa "pause".
