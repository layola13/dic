advantageously:/ˌædvənˈteɪdʒəsli/
adverb
有利地，有效地
In a way that is advantageous; to someone's advantage.
She was advantageously placed to observe the whole scene. 她所处的位置有利于观察整个场面。
The firm is advantageously located near the station. 该公司位置优越，靠近车站。

近义词: beneficially, profitably, favorably, conveniently, opportunely
反义词: disadvantageously, unfavorably, poorly, badly

词性: adverb

词源:
From advantage + -ous + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin abante "from before" + agere "to do, drive, lead, act.”


USAGE->
She used her knowledge of the local market advantageously. 她利用她对当地市场的了解获得了优势。
He advantageously positioned himself near the exit. 他有利地将自己置于出口附近。

He invested his money advantageously. 他投资了他的钱，并获得了收益。
The house is advantageously situated near good schools. 这所房子地理位置优越，靠近好学校。
