overrunning:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌnɪŋ/| v. n.|泛滥，蔓延；超出，超过；侵占，蹂躏|To spread over and occupy or take control of (a place) in large numbers, especially by military force; to exceed (a limit, especially of time or cost).|The project is overrunning its budget.  这个项目超出了预算。|近义词：overwhelm, invade, exceed, surpass|反义词：underwhelm, retreat, fall short of|词性:动词现在分词/名词

overrunning:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌnɪŋ/| adj.|泛滥的，蔓延的；超出的，超过的|Spreading over and occupying or taking control of a place in large numbers; exceeding a limit, especially of time or cost.|The overrunning weeds choked the garden. 蔓延的杂草阻塞了花园。|近义词：rampant, excessive, overflowing|反义词：contained, limited, moderate|词性:形容词

词源: From "overrun," a combination of "over" and "run."  "Over" implies excess or beyond a limit, while "run" suggests rapid movement or spread.
