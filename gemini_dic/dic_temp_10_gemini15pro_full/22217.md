bonapartist:/ˈboʊnəpɑːrtɪst/ | n. adj. |波拿巴主义者；波拿巴主义的|A supporter or admirer of Napoleon Bonaparte or his policies; relating to Napoleon Bonaparte or his policies. |例句：He was a staunch Bonapartist, believing in Napoleon's vision for France. 他是一位坚定的波拿巴主义者，相信拿破仑对法国的愿景。

例句：The Bonapartist regime was marked by both military successes and authoritarian rule. 波拿巴政权的特点是军事上的成功和专制统治。|近义词：imperialist, authoritarian |反义词:republican, democrat |词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology: From the name of Napoleon Bonaparte + -ist.  Originating in the late 18th/early 19th century.
