myolemma:/ˈmaɪoʊˌlɛmə/| n. |肌膜|The plasma membrane of a muscle cell, especially of a muscle fiber.  It is the site of the neuromuscular junction.|The action potential travels along the myolemma. 动作电位沿着肌膜传播。|Synonyms: sarcolemma|Antonyms: None|noun
