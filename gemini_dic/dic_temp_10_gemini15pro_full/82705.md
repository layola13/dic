guia:/ˈɡiːə/ /ˈɡaɪə/| n.|指南; 指导; 旅行指南; 导游; |A guide is a person who shows tourists around a place. |例句：The guide led us through the ancient ruins. 导游带我们穿过古老的废墟。

例句：I bought a guide to the best restaurants in Paris. 我买了一本巴黎最佳餐厅指南。|近义词：guidebook, handbook, manual, directory, leader, conductor, escort |反义词:follower|词性:名词


词源: From Portuguese *guia*, from Old French *guie*, from Frankish *witan* "to show the way". Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *witanan*. Related to English *wise*.
