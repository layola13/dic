backspace:/ˈbækspeɪs/| n. v.|退格键，删除键|A key on a computer keyboard that moves the cursor one position to the left, deleting the character in that position. |She hit the backspace key to correct her typo. 她按了退格键来纠正她的打字错误。|近义词：delete key|反义词: None|词性: noun, verb

backspace:/ˈbækspeɪs/| v.|退格，删除|To use the backspace key. |He backspaced to erase the extra letter. 他按下退格键删除了多余的字母。|近义词：delete|反义词: insert, type|词性: verb
