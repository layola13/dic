nopal:/noʊˈpɑːl/| n.|仙人掌(尤指食用仙人掌)|A cactus, especially the prickly pear, common in Mexico and the southwestern US, whose fleshy pads are eaten as a vegetable.|We had nopal salad for lunch. 我们午餐吃了仙人掌沙拉。|Synonyms: prickly pear, cactus|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


nopales:/noʊˈpɑːleɪs/ (plural form of nopal)| n.|仙人掌(尤指食用仙人掌，复数形式)|The plural form of nopal, referring to the pads of the prickly pear cactus.|Nopales are a common ingredient in Mexican cuisine. 仙人掌是墨西哥菜肴中常见的食材。|Synonyms: prickly pears, cacti|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
