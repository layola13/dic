decreers:/dɪˈkriːərz/
n.|法令颁布者；裁决者|Those who issue decrees; those who make authoritative pronouncements.
|The decreers of the new law faced much opposition. 新法律的颁布者面临很多反对。
|The decreers decided the fate of the kingdom. 裁决者决定了王国的命运。
|Synonym: legislators, judges, arbitrators|Antonym: followers, subjects|Noun
