outhissed:/ˌaʊtˈhɪst/
verb
发出更大的嘘声盖过
to hiss louder than
The crowd outhissed the unpopular speaker.  人群发出更大的嘘声盖过了不受欢迎的演讲者。
近义词:outboo, drown out
反义词:applaud, cheer
词性:verb

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "hiss" (verb meaning "to make a sibilant sound expressing disapproval")
