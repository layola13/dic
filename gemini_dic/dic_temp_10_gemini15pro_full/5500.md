aloud:/əˈlaʊd/| adv.|大声地，出声地|in a voice that can be heard; not silently|例句：She read the story aloud to her children. 她把故事大声地读给她的孩子们听。

例句：He laughed aloud at the joke. 他听到笑话大笑出声。

近义词：audibly, loudly|反义词：silently, quietly|词性：副词

词源：From Middle English al out, from Old English on hlūde (“loudly”), from on (“on”) + hlūde (“loudly”).  The word gradually became aloude, and eventually aloud.
