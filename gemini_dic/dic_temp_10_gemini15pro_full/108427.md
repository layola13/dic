lawsuit: /ˈlɔːsuːt/ (US) /ˈlɔːsjuːt/ (UK)| n. |诉讼，官司|A case in a court of law.|She filed a lawsuit against her former employer for wrongful dismissal. 她对她的前雇主提起诉讼，指控其不当解雇。|Synonyms: case, action, suit, litigation|Antonyms: settlement, agreement|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lawe sute*, from Old French *lawe suite* "pursuit of the law," from *lawe* (from Latin *lex*, *legis* "law") + *suite* "following, pursuit" (from Latin *sequi* "to follow").
