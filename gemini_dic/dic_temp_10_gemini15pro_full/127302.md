navik: /ˈnævɪk/ | n. | 领航员，航海家 | A person who navigates a ship or aircraft. |  The navik skillfully guided the ship through the treacherous waters. 领航员熟练地引导船只穿过危险的水域。

|  The experienced navik ensured the aircraft reached its destination safely. 经验丰富的领航员确保飞机安全抵达目的地。

| 近义词: navigator, pilot, helmsman | 反义词: passenger | 词性: 名词

词源: From Hindi and Sanskrit nāvika "sailor, boatman," from nāu "ship, boat."
