conqueress:/ˈkɒŋkərɪs/ /ˈkɑːŋkərɪs/ | n. |女征服者|A woman who conquers; a female victor.|例句：The conqueress claimed the land as her own. 女征服者宣称这片土地是她的。

例句：History remembers her as a ruthless conqueress. 历史记载着她是一位冷酷无情的女征服者。

近义词：victor, champion, winner, heroine |反义词:loser, vanquished |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *conqueresse*, from Anglo-Norman *conqueresse*, *conqueresce*, feminine of *conquerour*, from Old French conquerere, feminine of conquerre (“to conquer”). Equivalent to conquer +‎ -ess.
