objective:/əbˈdʒɛktɪv/ /ɑːbˈdʒɛktɪv/| adj. n.|客观的，目标，目的|Something that you are working towards achieving. ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *objectivus, from Latin objectum ("something thrown before, presented," noun use of neuter of objectus, past participle of obicere "to throw before, oppose, present"), from ob- ("toward, against") + iacere ("to throw").
