ectolecithal:/ˌɛktoʊˈlɛsɪθəl/
adjective
外卵黄的
Having the yolk concentrated at the surface of the egg, as in certain arthropods.  The yolk is external to the embryo.
例句：Ectolecithal eggs are characteristic of some insects.  外卵黄的卵是一些昆虫的特征。

例句：The ectolecithal development of the insect allows for rapid development. 昆虫的外卵黄发育可以使其快速发育。
近义词：centrolecithal (somewhat similar in that yolk is concentrated, but centrally)
反义词：endolecithal
词性: adjective
