disincorporate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/
verb
解散（公司）；使脱离法人资格
To deprive of corporate powers and privileges; to dissolve or break up (a corporation).
The company voted to disincorporate after years of financial struggles.  这家公司在多年的财政困难后投票决定解散。
The state legislature disincorporated the failing bank. 州立法机关取消了这家倒闭银行的法人资格。
近义词: dissolve, liquidate, break up
反义词: incorporate, establish, found
词性: verb
