aurocephalous:/ˌɔːrəˈsɛfələs/
adjective
金头的，金冠的
Having a golden or gilded head.  Often used in mythology and heraldry to describe creatures or figures.
The aurocephalous statue of the goddess gleamed in the sunlight. 女神金头的雕像在阳光下闪闪发光。
The aurocephalous eagle was a prominent symbol in the ancient empire.  金冠鹰是古代帝国的重要象征。
近义词:golden-headed, gilded
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *aurocephalus*, from aurum ("gold") + -cephalus ("-headed"), from Greek kephalē ("head").
