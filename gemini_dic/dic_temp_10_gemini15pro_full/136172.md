nonreimbursed:/ˌnɑːnriːɪmˈbɜːrst/
adjective
未报销的，未补偿的
Not reimbursed; not compensated for expenses or losses.
例句：
The nonreimbursed expenses put a strain on his finances.
未报销的费用使他的财务状况紧张。

She kept track of all her nonreimbursed business travel costs.
她记录了所有未报销的商务旅行费用。

近义词：uncompensated, unreimbursed, out-of-pocket
反义词：reimbursed, compensated
词性：adjective
