melty:/ˈmɛlti/| adj.|融化的，熔化的，快要融化的|Likely to melt or partially melted.  Having a texture like something melted. |例句：The ice cream is melty. 冰淇淋融化了。

The cheese on the pizza was perfectly melty. 比萨饼上的奶酪融化得恰到好处。|近义词：molten, melted, gooey, soft|反义词:frozen, solid, hard|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *melt + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  *Melt comes from Old English *meltan, of Germanic origin.
