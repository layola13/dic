aare: /ˈɑːrə/ (US), /ˈɑːrə/ (UK)
n.
阿勒河 (瑞士)
A river in Switzerland.
We swam in the Aare during our trip to Bern. = 我们在伯尔尼旅行期间在阿勒河游泳。
The Aare flows through the city of Bern. = 阿勒河流经伯尔尼市。
近义词: river, stream
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 land, earth)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old High German *Ahar, from Proto-Germanic *Ahurō, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éǵʰurō, meaning "swift water."
