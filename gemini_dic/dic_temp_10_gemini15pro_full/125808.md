murky:/ˈmɜːrki/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrki/ (US)| adj.|黑暗的，浑浊的，模糊的，暧昧的|Dark and gloomy, especially due to thick mist or fog; also, unclear, obscure, or morally questionable.|The murky water of the lake made it impossible to see the bottom. 湖水浑浊，根本看不到底。|近义词：dark, gloomy, obscure, unclear, shady|反义词：clear, bright, transparent, lucid|词性:形容词
