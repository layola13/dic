conscientiousness:/ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəsnəs/ /ˌkɑːnʃiˈenʃəsnəs/| n.|认真；尽责；一丝不苟|The quality of wishing to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly. |She approached her studies with commendable conscientiousness. 她学习非常认真刻苦。|近义词：diligence, meticulousness, scrupulousness|反义词：negligence, carelessness|词性: noun
Etymology:  From Late Latin conscientia (“consciousness, inner knowledge”),  + -ous (forming adjectives) + -ness (forming nouns). Ultimately from Latin conscire (“to be conscious of, to know with”), from com- (“with, together”) + scire (“to know”).
