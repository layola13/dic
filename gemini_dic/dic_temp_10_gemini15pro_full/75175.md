friz:/frɪz/
verb
卷曲，使卷曲
to form tight curls or make something form tight curls.
Her hair naturally frizzes in humid weather.  潮湿的天气里，她的头发自然卷曲。
The humidity made my hair friz. 湿度让我的头发卷曲了。
synonyms: curl, kink, crinkle
antonyms: straighten, smooth
词性:动词

friz:/frɪz/
noun
卷发，毛躁
tight curls or a mass of tight curls.
She woke up with a terrible friz. 她醒来时头发乱糟糟的。
The humidity gave me such a friz. 潮湿的天气让我的头发毛躁不堪。
synonyms: curl, kink, crinkle
antonyms: straight hair
词性:名词

词源:  Likely a variant of *friss, from Middle English *frisen, probably of Scandinavian origin; compare with Old Norse frýsa (“to freeze, bristle”).  可能是 *friss 的变体，来自中古英语 *frisen，可能源自斯堪的纳维亚语；与古挪威语 frýsa（“冻结，竖立”）比较。
