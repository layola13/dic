fatalists:/ˈfeɪtəlɪsts/| n. |宿命论者|People who believe that events are predetermined and inevitable, regardless of human actions.|例句：The fatalists accepted their fate without complaint. 宿命论者毫无怨言地接受了他们的命运。

例句：He's a fatalist about the future of the planet. 他对地球的未来持宿命论的观点。|近义词：determinists, predestinarians|反义词:optimists, free-will advocates|词性:noun
