nonmystic:/ˌnɑːnˈmɪstɪk/
adjective
非神秘的，不神秘的
Not mystical; not relating to or characterized by mysticism.
She offered a nonmystic explanation for the strange events. 她对这些奇怪的事件提供了一个非神秘的解释。
non-mystical
mystical, occult
