noncontributories:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjʊtɔːriz/ | n. | 非缴费人员；不缴费会员；无供款者 | People who are not making contributions to a particular fund or system, such as a pension scheme or social security. |  The noncontributories will not receive the same benefits as those who have paid into the system. 非缴费人员将无法获得与已缴费人员相同的福利。


The company's pension plan covers both contributories and noncontributories. 公司的养老金计划涵盖了缴费人员和非缴费人员。


| 近义词: non-payers, dependents | 反义词: contributors, payers | 词性: noun (plural)
