chameleon-like:/kəˈmiːliənˌlaɪk/| adj.|变色龙似的，善变的|Resembling a chameleon, especially in its ability to adapt or change; readily changing one's opinions or behavior.|例句：He has a chameleon-like ability to blend in with any social group. 他有一种变色龙般的能力，可以融入任何社会群体。|例句：Her chameleon-like personality made it hard to know where she stood on any issue. 她善变的性格让人很难知道她在任何问题上的立场。|近义词：adaptable, versatile, fickle, changeable|反义词：inflexible, rigid, constant, steadfast|词性:形容词
Etymology: From chameleon +‎ -like.
