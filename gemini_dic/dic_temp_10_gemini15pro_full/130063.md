non-porous:/ˌnɒnˈpɔːrəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɔːrəs/| adj. |无孔的，不透气的|Not allowing liquid or air to pass through.|This type of container is non-porous and will keep the contents fresh.  这种容器是无孔的，可以保持里面的东西新鲜。|The non-porous surface is easy to clean.  这种无孔的表面很容易清洁。|近义词：impermeable, airtight, waterproof|反义词: porous, permeable|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".
porous (adjective): from Late Latin porosus "having pores," from Latin porus "pore, passage," from Greek poros "passage, pore."
