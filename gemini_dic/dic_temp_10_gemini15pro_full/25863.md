burkha's:/ˈbɜːrkəz/ |n.| 布卡（的）| A burkha is a long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to feet, worn in public by many Muslim women.  Burkha's indicates possession, meaning "belonging to a burkha."| The burkha's fabric was torn. 这件布卡的布料破了。|Synonyms: veil, niqab, chador| Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing)| noun

Etymology: From Urdu and Persian بُرقع (burqaʿ), from Arabic بُرْقُع (burquʿ, “face veil”).
