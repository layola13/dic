enfeebler:/ɪnˈfiːblər/
verb
使衰弱，使虚弱
to make someone or something weak
The illness enfeebles the body's immune system. 这种疾病会削弱人体的免疫系统。
The long drought enfeebles the crops. 长期干旱使农作物变得衰弱。
近义词: weaken, debilitate, enervate
反义词: strengthen, invigorate, fortify
词性: verb

enfeeblement:/ɪnˈfiːblmənt/
noun
衰弱，虚弱
the state of being weakened
The enfeeblement of the elderly is a natural process, but it can be exacerbated by certain diseases. 老年人的衰弱是一个自然过程，但某些疾病会加剧衰弱。
The prolonged drought resulted in the enfeeblement of the once-fertile farmland. 长期干旱导致曾经肥沃的农田变得贫瘠。
近义词: weakness, debility, frailty
反义词: strength, vigor, robustness
词性: noun
