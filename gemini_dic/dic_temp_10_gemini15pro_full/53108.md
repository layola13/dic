diphthong:/ˈdɪfθɔːŋ/ (US) /ˈdɪpθɒŋ/ (UK)| n.|双元音|A sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves toward another (as in coin, loud, and side ).|The word "loud" has a diphthong.|Synonyms: vowel blend, gliding vowel|Antonyms: monophthong|noun
