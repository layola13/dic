half-great:/ˌhæf ˈɡreɪt/
| adj. |不太好的；平庸的；二流的 | Moderately good; mediocre.  A blend of "half" and "great," indicating something that is not fully great. | The movie was half-great; some parts were good, but others were boring. 这部电影一般般；有些部分不错，但其他部分很无聊。|Synonyms: mediocre, so-so, average, middling, fair | Antonyms: excellent, superb, outstanding, magnificent | Adjective

half-great:/ˌhæf ˈɡreɪt/
| n. |平庸之物；不太好的人或事 | Something that is moderately good; a mediocre person or thing. |  The restaurant was a half-great; the food was decent but the service was slow. 这家餐馆很一般；食物还不错，但服务很慢。 | Synonyms: mediocrity | Antonyms: excellence, masterpiece| Noun
