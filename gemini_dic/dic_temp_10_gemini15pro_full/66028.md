exanimate:/ɪɡˈzænɪmət/| adj.|无生命的；失去知觉的；没精神的|Lifeless; deprived of life; spiritless.  Showing no sign of life; inanimate.|The explorers discovered an exanimate body buried in the ice. 探险家们发现了一具埋在冰里的尸体。|近义词：lifeless, inanimate, dead, inert|反义词：living, animate, lively, vibrant|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *exanimatus*, past participle of *exanimare* ("to deprive of life, to kill"), from *ex-* ("out of, from") + *anima* ("breath, life, soul").
