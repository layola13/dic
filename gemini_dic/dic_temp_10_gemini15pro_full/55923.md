divvying:/ˈdɪviɪŋ/
|v.|分摊，分配|The act of dividing or sharing something among a group of people.|They were divvying up the profits from the sale. 他们正在分配销售利润。|Synonyms: sharing, distributing, allocating, apportioning|Antonyms: hoarding, keeping, centralizing|Verb

divvy:/ˈdɪvi/
|n.|一份，份额|A share or portion of something.|Everyone got their fair divvy of the cake. 每个人都得到了公平的一份蛋糕。|Synonyms: share, portion, allotment|Antonyms: whole, total|Noun
