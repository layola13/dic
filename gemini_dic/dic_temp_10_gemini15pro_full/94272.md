impersonalness:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənəlnəs/| n.|非个人性；客观性；冷淡|lack of human warmth or expression; objectivity.|The impersonalness of the hospital environment made her feel isolated.  医院环境的冷淡让她感到孤立。|Example: The impersonalness of the automated phone system was frustrating. 自动电话系统的非人情味令人沮丧。|Synonyms: detachment, objectivity, aloofness, coldness, formality|Antonyms: warmth, intimacy, friendliness, personality, subjectivity|noun
