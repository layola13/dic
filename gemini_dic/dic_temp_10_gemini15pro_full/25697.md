bunkhouse:/ˈbʌŋkhaʊs/ | n. | 宿舍，集体宿舍 | A simple building providing sleeping accommodations for a number of people, typically workers. | The cowboys slept in the bunkhouse after a long day's work. 牛仔们辛苦工作了一天后在集体宿舍里睡觉。|  The miners lived in a bunkhouse near the mine. 矿工们住在矿井附近的一个集体宿舍里。| synonyms: dormitory, barracks, hostel | antonyms: apartment, house, mansion | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bunk (a built-in bed or sleeping berth) + house
