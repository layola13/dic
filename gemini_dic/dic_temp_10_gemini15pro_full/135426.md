nonperseverances:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrsɪˈvɪərənsɪz/
|n.| 非坚持，缺乏毅力 |Lack of perseverance; inability or unwillingness to persist in an activity or task.|  His nonperseverance led to the failure of the project.  他的缺乏毅力导致了项目的失败。|例句：The student's nonperseverance in his studies resulted in poor grades.  这个学生学习不坚持导致成绩很差。

| | | | Her nonperseverance made it difficult for her to learn a new language. 她缺乏毅力，很难学习一门外语。| 近义词: inconstancy, fickleness, instability, vacillation | 反义词: perseverance, persistence, tenacity, steadfastness| 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (not) + perseverance (persistence)  来自拉丁语 perseverantia，意为“坚持不懈”。
