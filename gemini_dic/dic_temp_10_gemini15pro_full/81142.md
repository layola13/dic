granadilla:/ˌɡrænəˈdɪlə/ | n. | 百香果；西番莲果 | A tropical fruit with a hard shell and many small seeds, covered with a juicy pulp.  The term "granadilla" can refer to several different species of passion fruit. | I love the sweet and tangy flavor of granadilla. 我喜欢百香果酸甜的味道。 | Granadilla juice is a refreshing drink on a hot day. 在炎热的天气里，百香果汁是一种提神的饮料。 | 近义词: passion fruit, maracuja | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a fruit) | 词性: noun
