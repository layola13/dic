hyphened:/ˈhaɪfənd/ | adj. | 带连字符的，有连字符的 | Connected by a hyphen. |  Example:  The compound word "well-being" is hyphened.  复合词“well-being”是由连字符连接的。

Example: He used a hyphened phrase in his writing. 他在写作中使用了一个带连字符的短语。 |  Synonyms: hyphenated | Antonyms: unhyphenated | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyphen + -ed.  Ultimately from Greek ὑφ᾽ ἕν (hyph' hen, “under one”).
