baked:/beɪkt/| adj.|烤的，烘培的|Cooked by dry heat in an oven.|例句：I love the smell of freshly baked bread. 我喜欢新鲜出炉的面包的香味。|例句：The baked potatoes were fluffy and delicious. 烤土豆蓬松可口。|近义词：roasted, cooked|反义词:raw, uncooked|词性:形容词


Etymology->
From Middle English *baken, from Old English *bacen (past participle of bacan (“to bake”)), from Proto-Germanic *bakaną (“to bake”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂g- (“to bake, roast”). Cognate with West Frisian bakke (“to bake”), Dutch bakken (“to bake”), German backen (“to bake”), Danish bage (“to bake”), Swedish baka (“to bake”).
