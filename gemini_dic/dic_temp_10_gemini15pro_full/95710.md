incongruous:/ɪnˈkɒŋɡruːəs/ (US) /ɪnˈkɑːŋɡruəs/| adj.|不协调的，不一致的，不合适的|Not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something; out of place.|例句：Her bright pink dress looked incongruous against the somber background of the funeral. 她鲜艳的粉红色连衣裙在葬礼的阴沉背景下显得格格不入。

例句：The modern sculpture looked incongruous in the ancient temple.  现代雕塑在古老的寺庙里显得格格不入。

近义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, out of place, incompatible, clashing, discordant|反义词：appropriate, suitable, fitting, harmonious, consistent, congruent|词性:形容词
