outsearching:/aʊtˈsɜːrtʃɪŋ/
verb
超越搜索；比…搜索得更彻底
Searching more extensively or effectively than someone or something else.
例句：The new search engine is outsearching all its competitors, indexing more pages and providing more relevant results.  这个新的搜索引擎比所有竞争对手搜索得都更彻底，索引了更多页面并提供了更相关的结果。

例句：She outsearched the entire team, uncovering crucial evidence that had been missed. 她比整个团队搜索得都更彻底，发现了被遗漏的关键证据。
近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, surpass in searching
反义词：being outsearched, losing in a search competition
词性：动词
