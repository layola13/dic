outmoved:/ˌaʊtˈmuːvd/
verb
超越，在…方面胜过
surpass in emotion; affect more deeply than another.
He was outmoved by his opponent's passion. 他被对手的热情所感动，胜过了自己。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, transcend
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
