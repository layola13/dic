illegitimatize:/ɪˌlɪˈdʒɪtɪmətaɪz/
verb
使不合法；宣布…为非法；剥夺…的合法地位；认定…为私生子
To make something illegitimate; to deprive of legal status.
The new law illegitimatized all previous claims to the land. 新法律使所有先前对这片土地的主张都变得非法。
synonyms: bastardize, disinherit, outlaw, delegitimize
antonyms: legitimize, validate, authorize
词性: 动词

illegitimate:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adjective
非法的；私生的；不合逻辑的
Not authorized by law; born out of wedlock; not in accordance with logic.
The illegitimate government was overthrown by the people.  非法的政府被人民推翻了。
synonyms: unlawful, illegal, illicit, bastard, spurious
antonyms: legitimate, legal, lawful, valid
词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin illegitimatus, from in- "not" + legitimatus "legitimate".
