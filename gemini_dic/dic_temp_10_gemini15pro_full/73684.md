forgetting:/fərˈɡetɪŋ/
n.|遗忘，忘记|The act of losing the ability to remember something.  |My biggest problem is forgetting people's names. 我最大的问题就是记不住别人的名字。|近义词：oblivion, amnesia|反义词：remembering, recollection|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old English *forgietan "to forget," from for- "away, amiss" + gietan "to get."
