attemperate:/əˈtɛmpərɪt/| adj.|温和的，适度的；调和的|To make or become less intense or extreme; moderate.  To adapt to a particular situation or environment.|The attemperate climate of the Mediterranean coast is ideal for growing olives.| 近义词: moderate, temperate, mild, gentle|反义词: extreme, immoderate, excessive, harsh|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *attemperatus*, past participle of *attemperare* "to temper, moderate," from Latin *ad* "to" + *temperare* "to mix, combine, moderate," from *tempus* "time, season."
