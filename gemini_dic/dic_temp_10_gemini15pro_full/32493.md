chinju: /ˈtʃɪndʒuː/
n.
镇守;守护神;守护灵
A guardian spirit or deity, often associated with a specific place or object, particularly in Korean folklore.
Chinju are believed to protect the place they inhabit.  镇守被认为会保护他们居住的地方。

The mountain had a powerful chinju that protected the villagers. 这座山有一位强大的镇守保护着村民们。

The old tree was revered as the chinju of the forest. 这棵老树被尊为森林的守护神。


Synonym: guardian, protector, spirit
Antonym:  demon, evil spirit
词性: noun
