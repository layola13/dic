exporters:/ɪkˈspɔːrtərz/
noun
出口商；输出国
People or companies that sell goods to other countries.
China is one of the world's largest exporters of manufactured goods.  中国是世界上最大的制成品出口国之一。
The exporters are facing increasing competition in the global market.  出口商在全球市场上面临日益激烈的竞争。
synonyms:  sellers, vendors, shippers, traders
antonyms: importers, buyers
词性: 名词
