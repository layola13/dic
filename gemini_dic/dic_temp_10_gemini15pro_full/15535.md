bagels:/ˈbeɪɡəlz/ (US), /ˈbeɪɡlz/ (UK)| n. |贝果，百吉圈|A dense, ring-shaped bread roll, first boiled then baked.  Traditionally eaten with cream cheese or lox (smoked salmon).|例句：I had a bagel with cream cheese for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个涂奶油奶酪的贝果。

|例句：The bakery sells a variety of bagels, including poppy seed and everything bagels.  这家面包店出售各种各样的贝果，包括罂粟籽贝果和全麦贝果。|近义词：roll, bun|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific food item)|词性:noun
