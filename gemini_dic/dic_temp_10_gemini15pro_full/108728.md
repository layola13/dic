leap-years:/ˈliːp jɪərz/| n.|闰年|A year, occurring once every four years, which has 366 days including February 29th as an intercalary day.|例句：2024 is a leap year. 2024年是闰年。

|例句：Leap years help keep our calendar aligned with the Earth's orbit. 闰年有助于使我们的日历与地球的轨道保持一致。|近义词：bissextile year|反义词:common year|noun
