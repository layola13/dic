comportance: /kəmˈpɔːrtəns/ 
n. | 行为；举止；风度； demeanor |  Comportance is the way someone behaves, especially in formal situations. |  He maintained a dignified comportance throughout the ceremony. 他在整个仪式中保持着庄重的举止。 |  demeanor, bearing, conduct, behavior |  misconduct, impropriety | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "comporter" (to behave) + "-ance" (suffix forming nouns of action or state) Ultimately from Latin "comportare" (to carry together, collect)
