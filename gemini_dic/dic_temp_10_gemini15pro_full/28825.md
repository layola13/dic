carousingly:/kəˈraʊzɪŋli/
adverb
狂欢地，欢闹地
In a manner characterized by revelry and boisterous merriment.
They celebrated carousingly after winning the championship. 他们赢得冠军后狂欢庆祝。
The pirates spent their loot carousingly, drinking and singing all night. 海盗们狂欢地花掉他们的战利品，整夜喝酒唱歌。
Synonyms: boisterously, uproariously, festively, merrily, convivially
Antonyms: solemnly, quietly, soberly, seriously
词性: 副词
