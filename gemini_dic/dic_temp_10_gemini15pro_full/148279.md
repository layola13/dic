oversoaking:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊkɪŋ/
|n.|过度浸泡|The act of soaking something for too long, typically resulting in damage or an undesirable outcome.|Leaving the laundry oversoaking can make it smell musty.  把衣物浸泡太久会使它发霉。|oversaturation, waterlogging|undersoaking|noun
|v.|（使）过度浸泡|(often passive) To soak something for too long.|The rice was oversoaked and became mushy.  米饭被浸泡太久了，变得糊状了。|oversaturate, waterlog|undersoak|verb
