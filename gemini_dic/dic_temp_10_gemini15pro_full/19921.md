biocentric:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 生物中心主义的 | Considering all forms of life as having intrinsic value.  Believing that human beings are no more important than other living things. |  Example 1:  A biocentric worldview emphasizes the interconnectedness of all living organisms.  生物中心主义的世界观强调所有生物体的相互联系。

Example 2:  She holds a biocentric perspective, arguing against the exploitation of animals for human benefit. 她持有生物中心主义的观点，反对为了人类利益而剥削动物。| 近义词：ecocentric, nature-centered | 反义词：anthropocentric | 词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
bio- (life) + centric (centered)
From Greek "bios" (life) +  Latin "centrum" (center).
