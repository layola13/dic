osteographer: /ɒstiˈɒɡrəfər/ /ɑːstiˈɑːɡrəfər/
n.
骨骼学家，骨骼描述者
A person who describes or studies bones.
The osteographer carefully examined the skeletal remains.  骨骼学家仔细检查了骨骼遗骸。
The museum hired a skilled osteographer to catalog the dinosaur bones. 博物馆聘请了一位熟练的骨骼学家来编录恐龙骨骼。
Synonym: bone specialist, skeletal anatomist
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a general term like "soft tissue specialist")
词性: noun

Etymology:
From osteo- (bone) + -grapher (writer, describer).  From Greek *osteon* (bone) + *-graphos* (writer).
