fracted:/ˈfræktɪd/| adj.|破碎的，分裂的|Broken or split into fragments, either literally or figuratively.  Can also refer to a disrupted or disjointed state.|The glass was fractured into a thousand pieces. 玻璃碎成了一千片。|synonyms: fragmented, splintered, shattered, broken, divided, disunited|antonyms: whole, intact, unified, complete|adjective

fracted:/ˈfræktɪd/| adj.|（精神、组织等）分裂的，不完整的|Figuratively, describes something that is disrupted, disjointed, or lacking unity.|After the war, the country's political system was fractured. 战后，该国的政治体系支离破碎。|synonyms: fragmented, disjointed, incoherent, disorganized|antonyms: coherent, unified, organized, integrated|adjective
