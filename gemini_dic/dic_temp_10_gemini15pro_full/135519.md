nonplausibly:/ˌnɒnˈplɔːzɪbli/
adverb
难以置信地；不足信地
not in a way that seems reasonable or probable
Her story seemed nonplausibly optimistic. 她的故事乐观得令人难以置信。

The witness nonplausibly claimed to have seen the entire event from a mile away.  证人难以置信地声称从一英里外看到了整个事件。

近义词:improbably, unbelievably, unconvincingly
反义词:plausibly, believably, convincingly
词性:adverb
