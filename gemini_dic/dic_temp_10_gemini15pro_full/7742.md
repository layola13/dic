anoraks: /ˈænəræks/ (US) /ˈænəræks/ (UK)| n. | 防水防风夹克；(尤指对某事狂热)的爱好者 | A hooded, waterproof jacket, typically with a front pouch. Also, a person who has an obsessive interest in a particular subject or activity. | He wore his anorak to protect himself from the rain. 他穿着他的防水防风夹克以防雨淋。 |  He's a real anorak when it comes to trains.  说到火车，他真是个狂热爱好者。 | 近义词: jacket, coat, enthusiast, buff | 反义词: (For clothing) none; (For person) amateur, dabbler | 词性: noun
