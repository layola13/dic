inharmoniously:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəsli/ | adv. |不和谐地，不协调地 | Not in a harmonious or agreeable manner; discordantly. | The instruments played inharmoniously, creating a jarring sound.  乐器演奏得极不和谐，发出刺耳的声音。 | The colors clashed inharmoniously. 这些颜色不协调地冲突着。| 近义词: discordantly, dissonantly, jarringly, clashingly | 反义词: harmoniously, concordantly, agreeably | 词性: 副词

inharmonious:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəs/ | adj. |不和谐的，不协调的 | Not harmonious; discordant. | The inharmonious sounds of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地不和谐的声音使人难以集中注意力。 |  The meeting was marked by inharmonious debate. 这次会议的特点是争论不休。 | 近义词: discordant, dissonant, jarring, clashing | 反义词: harmonious, concordant, agreeable | 词性: 形容词


词源:
From in- (not) + harmonious + -ly (adverbial suffix). "Harmonious" ultimately derives from Greek "harmonia" (agreement, concord).
