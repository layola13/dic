inequable:/ɪnˈiːkwəbəl/
adjective
不稳定的，易变的，不均匀的
not consistent; changeable; uneven
The inequable climate made farming difficult.  这种不稳定的气候使耕作变得困难。
His inequable temper made him difficult to work with. 他反复无常的脾气使他难以共事。
近义词:variable, fluctuating, unstable, erratic, inconsistent, uneven
反义词:stable, consistent, steady, uniform, even
词性:adjective

词源:
Late 16th century: from Latin *inaequabilis, from in- ‘not’ + aequabilis (from aequare ‘make equal’).
