footnotes:/ˈfʊtnəʊts/ /ˈfʊtnoʊts/| n. |脚注，注释|A note at the bottom of a page that gives more information about something mentioned in the text.|The book is full of footnotes, making it a scholarly work. 这本书充满了脚注，使其成为一部学术著作。|synonyms: annotation, endnote, note|antonyms: text, body (of text)|noun
