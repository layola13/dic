/wɒt/ (UK) /hwʌt/ /wʌt/ (US)|pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something; used to express surprise or disbelief.|

例句：
What is your name?
你叫什么名字？

What did you do yesterday?
你昨天做了什么？

近义词：which|反义词: N/A|词性: pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb
