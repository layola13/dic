aimer's: /ˈeɪmərz/
|n.| 爱好者; 情人; 仰慕者; |Someone who loves or admires someone or something.  A person who has a strong affection for someone or something.|  Example: The artist's aimers flocked to the gallery opening. 这位艺术家的仰慕者蜂拥至画廊开幕式。| 近义词: admirer, fan, devotee, enthusiast | 反义词: critic, detractor, hater | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the French word "aimer" (to love), with the English possessive suffix "-'s".  The word "aimer" itself comes from the Latin "amare" (to love).
