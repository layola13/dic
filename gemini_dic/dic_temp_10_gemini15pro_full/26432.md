bypassed:/ˌbaɪˈpæst/ /ˌbaɪˈpæst/| v. | 绕过，避开；忽视，不顾 | to go around or avoid something |  The surgeon bypassed the blocked artery. 外科医生绕过了阻塞的动脉。

The new highway bypassed the town, reducing traffic congestion. 新建的高速公路绕过了城镇，减少了交通拥堵。| 近义词：circumvent, avoid, detour, ignore | 反义词：confront, address, encounter | 词性: 动词

bypassed:/ˌbaɪˈpæst/ /ˌbaɪˈpæst/| adj. | 被绕过的 | having been bypassed |  The bypassed town suffered economically. 被绕过的城镇经济萧条。

The bypassed section of the river was calm and peaceful. 河流被绕过的部分平静而安宁。| 近义词：circumvented, avoided | 反义词：confronted, addressed | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "by-" (meaning "near, past") + "pass" (to go by).
