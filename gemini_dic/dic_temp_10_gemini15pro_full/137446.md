nonthermal:/ˌnɑːnˈθɜːrməl/ |adj.|非热力的；非温度的|Not relating to or caused by heat; occurring at temperatures low enough that heat is not a major factor.|例句：Nonthermal plasma is used for sterilization. 非热等离子体用于灭菌。|例句：Nonthermal food processing methods preserve nutrients. 非热食品加工方法可以保存营养。|近义词：athermal|反义词：thermal|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + thermal.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Thermal" comes from the Greek word "thermē" meaning "heat".
