antispiritualist:/ˌæntiˈspɪrɪtʃuəlɪst/
noun
反唯灵论者；反对唯灵论的人
A person who opposes spiritualism.
He was a staunch antispiritualist, believing only in what could be scientifically proven. 他是一个坚定的反唯灵论者，只相信可以用科学证明的东西。
The antispiritualist argued that the séance was a hoax.  这个反唯灵论者认为降神会是个骗局。

近义词:materialist, skeptic
反义词:spiritualist, mystic
词性:n.
