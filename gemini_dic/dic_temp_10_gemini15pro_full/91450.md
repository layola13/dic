hygrophanous:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈfænəs/ | adj. |吸湿变色的 | Appearing to change color as it loses or absorbs moisture, as the flesh or cap of certain fungi. | The mushroom's cap was hygrophanous, turning from a dark brown when wet to a pale buff as it dried. 这种蘑菇的菌盖具有吸湿变色性，潮湿时呈深棕色，干燥后变成浅黄色。| synonyms: color-changing, moisture-sensitive | antonyms: unchanging, non-hygrophanous | adjective

hygrophanous:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈfænəs/ | adj. |吸水变色的| Appearing to change color with variations in moisture content. | The hygrophanous nature of the fungus made identification challenging.  这种真菌的吸水变色特性使得鉴定具有挑战性。| synonyms: water-sensitive, deliquescent (in some contexts) | antonyms: unchanging, stable | adjective
