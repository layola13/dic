cross-accent:/ˌkrɒs ˈæksənt/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈæksent/ | adj. |交叉重音的|  Relating to or characterized by a rhythmic pattern in which stresses fall on syllables other than those normally accented. This often occurs when words from different languages are combined, or when musical rhythms clash with natural speech rhythms.| The song had a peculiar cross-accent rhythm that was hard to follow.|  synonym: syncopated, off-beat | antonym: regular, conventional| adjective


cross-accent:/ˌkrɒs ˈæksənt/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈæksent/ | n. |交叉重音|  A rhythmic pattern in which stresses fall on syllables other than those normally accented.| The cross-accent in the poem created a sense of unease.| synonym: syncopation, off-beat | antonym: regular rhythm | noun
