infrequency:/ɪnˈfriːkwənsi/ | n. | 不常发生; 罕见; |lack of frequency; rarity.  The infrequency of his visits made us wonder if he'd moved. 他很少来访，让我们怀疑他是否搬走了。 | 近义词: rarity, uncommonness, rareness | 反义词: frequency, commonness, prevalence | 词性: noun
infrequent:/ɪnˈfriːkwənt/ | adj. | 罕见的; 不常发生的; |not occurring often.  Infrequent rainfall has led to a drought. 降雨稀少导致了干旱。 | 近义词: rare, uncommon, unusual | 反义词: frequent, common, usual, regular | 词性: adjective
