megacity:/ˈmegəsɪti/| n. |大城市，特大城市|A very large city, typically one with a population of over ten million people.|Tokyo is a bustling megacity with a rich culture. 东京是一个充满活力的文化丰富的大都市。|The rapid growth of megacities presents unique challenges. 特大城市的快速发展带来了独特的挑战。|近义词：metropolis, megalopolis|反义词:small town, village|词性:noun

Etymology:  From mega- +‎ city.  "Mega-" comes from Greek μέγας (mégas, “great, large”). "City" comes from Old French cité  and ultimately from Latin civitas (“citizenship, state, city”).
