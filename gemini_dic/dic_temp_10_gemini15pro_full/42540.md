countermemo:/ˈkaʊntərˈmɛmoʊ/
noun
反备忘录，复函
A memo written in reply to a previous memo, often presenting a contrasting viewpoint or refutation.
The CEO issued a countermemo outlining the reasons for the recent layoffs, directly addressing the concerns raised by employees in their initial memo. 首席执行官发布了一份反备忘录，概述了最近裁员的原因，直接回应了员工在最初备忘录中提出的担忧。
He penned a sharp countermemo to the board of directors, refuting their accusations of mismanagement. 他写了一份措辞强烈的反备忘录给董事会，驳斥了他们对其管理不善的指控。
Synonyms: reply, response, rebuttal, rejoinder
Antonyms:  original memo, initial memo
