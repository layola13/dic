allays:/əˈleɪz/| v. |减轻，缓和，使平静|reduces or relieves; calms or pacifies|The medicine allays the pain.  这药缓解了疼痛。|例句：Her gentle words allayed their fears. 她温柔的话语减轻了他们的恐惧。|近义词：soothe, mitigate, alleviate, relieve, lessen, diminish, ease, calm, pacify|反义词：aggravate, exacerbate, intensify, worsen, increase|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *aleien, *aleggen, from Old French *aleger, *alager (“to alleviate, lighten”), from Latin *alleviāre, from ad- (“to”) + levis (“light”).
