bisect:/baɪˈsɛkt/ | v. | 平分，二等分 | To divide something into two equal parts. | The line bisects the angle.  这条线平分这个角。| synonyms: divide, halve, split | antonyms: join, unite, combine | verb

bisected:/baɪˈsɛktɪd/ | adj. | 被平分的，被二等分的 | Divided into two equal parts. | The circle is bisected by the diameter. 这个圆被直径二等分。| synonyms: divided, halved, split | antonyms: joined, united, combined | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bisectus*, past participle of *bisecare* "to cut in two," from *bi-* "two" + *secare* "to cut."
