nasopalatine:/ˌneɪzoʊˈpælətaɪn/| adj.|鼻腭的|Relating to the nose and palate.  It describes structures or locations associated with both the nasal cavity and the hard palate of the mouth.|The nasopalatine nerve supplies sensation to the front part of the roof of the mouth.|Synonym: nasopalatal|Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, as this is an anatomical descriptor)|adjective

nasopalatine:/ˌneɪzoʊˈpælətaɪn/| n.|鼻腭管|A duct or canal that passes through the incisive foramen, connecting the nasal cavity with the oral cavity.|A nasopalatine cyst can develop within the nasopalatine duct.|Synonym: incisive canal|Antonym: (No direct antonym exists, as this is an anatomical descriptor)|noun
