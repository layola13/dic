colliders:/kəˈlaɪdərz/| n.|对撞机|A machine that accelerates particles to high speeds and smashes them together, allowing physicists to study the resulting interactions.|The Large Hadron Collider is the world's largest and most powerful particle collider. 大型强子对撞机是世界上最大、功率最强的粒子对撞机。|synonyms: accelerator, smasher|antonyms: N/A|noun

colliders:/kəˈlaɪdərz/| n.|碰撞器|Something that causes or is involved in a collision.|The two cars were the colliders in the accident. 这两辆汽车是事故中的碰撞物。|synonyms: impactor, hitter|antonyms: N/A|noun
