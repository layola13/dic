monocormic:/ˌmɒnəˈkɔːrmɪk/ | adj. | 单茎的；单轴的 | Having a single stem or axis; not branching. | Example 1: The monocormic palm tree stood tall and slender.  这棵单茎棕榈树又高又细。
Example 2:  The researchers studied the growth patterns of monocormic plants. 研究人员研究了单茎植物的生长模式。 | Near synonyms: unbranched, single-stemmed | Antonyms: branching, multi-stemmed | Word type: adjective
