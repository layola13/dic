cleanings:/ˈkliːnɪŋz/
noun
清洁；清洗；清除；打扫（cleaning的复数）
The act of making something clean.  The results of a cleaning process.
例句：The spring cleanings left the house spotless. 春季大扫除后，房子一尘不染。

例句：The dry cleanings are ready to be picked up.  干洗的衣服可以取了。

近义词：tidying, cleansing, purifying, purging
反义词：dirtying, soiling, messing

词性:n.
