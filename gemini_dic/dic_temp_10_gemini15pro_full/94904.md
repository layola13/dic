in full swing:/ɪn fʊl swɪŋ/
adjective
全面展开；如火如荼
at the height of activity; fully active
The party was in full swing by the time we arrived. 我们到达时，派对正进行得如火如荼。
The construction project is now in full swing.  这个建筑项目现在正在全面展开。
synonyms: underway, in progress, at its height, bustling, lively
antonyms: inactive, stalled, slow, quiet
词性: adjective
