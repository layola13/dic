horticulturist: /ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. | 园艺家；园艺师 | A person who practices or studies horticulture; a gardener, especially one skilled in the science of plant cultivation. |  My grandmother, a skilled horticulturist, could grow anything. 我的祖母是一位技艺娴熟的园艺家，什么都能种出来。

|  The horticulturist grafted the apple tree with a new variety.  这位园艺师将苹果树嫁接了一个新品种。 | 近义词: gardener, grower, cultivator | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From horticulture + -ist.  Horticulture comes from the Latin words "hortus" (garden) and "cultura" (cultivation).
