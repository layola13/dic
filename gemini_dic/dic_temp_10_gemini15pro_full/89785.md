horizontal:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntl/ /ˌhɑːrɪˈzɑːntl/| adj. n.|水平的；地平线的；横的|Parallel to the horizon; at right angles to the vertical.|The ship’s mast was horizontal to the water. 船的桅杆与水面平行。|Synonyms: level, flat, even|Antonyms: vertical, upright, perpendicular|adjective

horizontal:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntl/ /ˌhɑːrɪˈzɑːntl/| n.|水平线；水平面；水平位置|A horizontal line, plane, or position.|Draw a horizontal line across the page. 在页面上画一条水平线。|Synonyms: level, plane|Antonyms: vertical|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *horizontalis, from Latin horizontem (nominative horizon), "bounding circle, horizon," from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horizōn) (present participle of ὁρίζειν (horizein), "to bound, limit"), from ὅρος (horos), "boundary, limit."
