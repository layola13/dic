inseminate:/ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪt/| vt.|使受精，使怀孕；灌输，注入|To introduce semen into the vagina or uterus of a female animal or plant, or to introduce something that influences something else.|The farmer inseminated the cows artificially. 农民给母牛做了人工授精。|Synonyms: impregnate, fertilize, infuse|Antonyms: sterilize, castrate|verb

insemination:/ɪnˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|授精，受精；灌输|The act or process of inseminating.|Artificial insemination is a common practice in modern agriculture. 人工授精是现代农业中常见的做法。|Synonyms: fertilization, impregnation, infusion|Antonyms: sterilization|noun
