balefire:/ˈbeɪlˌfaɪər/| n. |魔火，毁灭之火，净化之火|A magically kindled fire, especially one with destructive or purifying properties.  It often appears in fantasy literature and often leaves no trace of what it burns.|The balefire consumed the evil sorcerer, leaving not even ashes. 魔火吞噬了邪恶的巫师，甚至连灰烬都没留下。|近义词：wildfire, inferno, conflagration|反义词: gentle flame, controlled burn|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *balefyr*, from bale (evil, woe, sorrow) + fire. The term gained popularity through its use in J.R.R. Tolkien's works.
