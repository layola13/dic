incompletion:/ˌɪnkəmˈpliːʃn/ | n. |未完成，不完整|The incompletion of the project caused significant delays.  这个项目未完成导致了严重的延误。|例句：The feeling of incompletion haunted him. 未完成的感觉萦绕着他。|近义词：lack, deficiency, unfinishedness|反义词:completion, fulfillment, achievement|词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + completion (the act of completing).  Ultimately from Latin complēre (to fill up).
