councilist: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪst/
n.
市政主义者；地方议会议员的支持者
A person who advocates for or supports the role of local councils and council members in government.
Councillists believe that local government is best placed to understand and address the needs of communities. 市政主义者认为地方政府最能理解和解决社区的需求。
The councilist argued for greater local autonomy in decision-making.  这位市政主义者主张在决策过程中给予地方更大的自主权。

近义词: localist, municipalist
反义词: centralist

词性: noun

词源: From "council" + "-ist" (suffix forming agent nouns).
