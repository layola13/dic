benchmark:/ˈbɛntʃmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbɛntʃmɑːk/ (UK)| n. vt.|基准；标准；衡量|A standard or point of reference against which things may be compared or assessed.|The company's profits this year serve as a benchmark for future performance.  这家公司今年的利润可作为衡量未来业绩的基准。|synonyms: standard, criterion, gauge, yardstick|antonyms:  deviation, anomaly|noun
