aristotelians:/ˌærɪstəˈtiːliənz/
noun
亚里士多德学派的人；亚里士多德主义者
Followers or students of the philosophy of Aristotle.
Many Aristotelians believe in the importance of empirical observation. 许多亚里士多德主义者相信经验观察的重要性。
synonyms:Peripatetics, Aristotelian philosophers
antonyms:Platonists
n.
