haters:/ˈheɪtərz/ (US), /ˈheɪtəz/ (UK)| n.| 憎恨者，仇恨者；讨厌…的人|A person who greatly dislikes someone or something. |例句：The singer ignored her haters and focused on her fans.  这位歌手无视她的黑粉，专注于她的粉丝。

例句：He’s used to dealing with haters online. 他习惯了在网上与黑子打交道。

近义词：critics, detractors, adversaries, opponents|反义词:fans, supporters, admirers|词性:名词
