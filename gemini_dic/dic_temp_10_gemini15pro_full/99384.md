interdict:/ˌɪntərˈdɪkt/| v. |禁止，阻止|To prohibit or forbid (something) officially.|The government interdicted the sale of the drug. 政府禁止了这种药物的销售。|Synonyms: prohibit, forbid, ban, proscribe|Antonyms: permit, allow, authorize|verb

interdict:/ˈɪntərˌdɪkt/| n. |禁令，阻止|An official or authoritative prohibition.|The church placed an interdict on the king. 教会对国王下达了禁令。|Synonyms: prohibition, ban, proscription|Antonyms: permission, allowance, authorization|noun
