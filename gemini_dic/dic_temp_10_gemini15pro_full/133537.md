nonfilamentous:/ˌnɒnˌfɪləˈmentəs/ | adj. | 非丝状的，无丝的 | Not filamentous; lacking filaments.  A nonfilamentous bacterium does not form long thread-like structures. |  The bacteria observed under the microscope were nonfilamentous.  显微镜下观察到的细菌是非丝状的。 | The fungus is nonfilamentous, growing as single cells.  这种真菌是非丝状的，以单细胞的形式生长。 | 近义词: nonfibrous, unicellular (for bacteria/fungi) | 反义词: filamentous, fibrous | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  Latin, meaning "not"
filamentous (adjective):  From Latin "filum" (thread) + "-ous" (suffix forming adjectives)
