megastructure:/ˈmeɡəˌstrʌktʃər/| n. |巨型结构，超大规模结构|A very large artificial structure, such as a Dyson sphere, often envisioned in science fiction.|例句：The Dyson sphere is a hypothetical megastructure that could enclose a star. 戴森球是一个假设的巨型结构，可以包围一颗恒星。

例句：Building a megastructure in space presents enormous engineering challenges. 在太空中建造巨型结构面临巨大的工程挑战。|近义词：massive structure, colossal structure|反义词:miniature structure, small structure|词性:noun
