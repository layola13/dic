doyen:/ˈdɔɪən/ (US), /ˈdɔɪən/ (UK)| n.|院长；系主任；学部主任；（尤指在某领域）资深人士，泰斗|A senior member of a group, especially one who is highly respected because of their long experience in a particular field. |例句：He is considered the doyen of political commentators. 他被认为是政治评论员中的泰斗。

例句：She's the doyen of the fashion industry. 她是时尚界的元老。|近义词：veteran, expert, master, authority, leader|反义词：novice, beginner, apprentice|词性:noun
