nongeminates:/nɒnˈdʒemɪnɪts/ | n. |非重叠音，非叠音|A nongeminate is a speech sound that is not pronounced with a prolonged or doubled articulation.  It is a single, short sound, unlike geminates which are held longer. In phonetics, these are often represented as single symbols as opposed to double symbols for geminates.| The word "better" contains the nongeminate consonant /t/. "Better" 中包含非叠音辅音 /t/。 | 近义词: singleton (in some contexts)|反义词: geminates| 词性: Noun
