antisyndicalism:/ˌæntiˌsɪndɪkəlɪzəm/
n.
反工团主义，反工会主义
Opposition to trade unions; the belief that trade unions are harmful to society.
The government's antisyndicalism led to the suppression of several labor organizations. 政府的反工会主义导致几个劳工组织遭到压制。
The rise of antisyndicalism threatened the workers' rights. 反工会主义的兴起威胁到工人的权利。
近义词:anti-unionism
反义词:pro-unionism
词性:noun
