enchant:/ɪnˈtʃænt/ |v.|使着迷，使陶醉|To fill someone with great delight and wonder.|The magician enchanted the children with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法使孩子们着迷。|近义词:captivate, charm, fascinate, bewitch|反义词:disenchant, repel, bore|Verb

enchanting:/ɪnˈtʃæntɪŋ/ |adj.|迷人的，令人陶醉的|Delightfully charming and attractive.|The view from the mountaintop was enchanting. 从山顶望下去的景色令人陶醉。|近义词:charming, captivating, delightful, magical|反义词:disenchanting, repulsive, boring|Adjective
