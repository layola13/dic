homogeneity:/ˌhɒmədʒəˈniːɪti/ /ˌhoʊmədʒəˈniːɪti/ | n. | 同质性，同种，均匀性 | The homogeneity of the sample is important for accurate results.  样本的同质性对于获得准确的结果至关重要。|  例句：The scientist tested the homogeneity of the mixture. 科学家测试了混合物的同质性。 | 近义词：uniformity, consistency, similarity | 反义词：heterogeneity, diversity, variety | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *homogeneitas*, from Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs, “of the same kind”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”).
