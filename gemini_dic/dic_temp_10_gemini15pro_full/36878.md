coloratura:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊərə/ /ˌkʌlərəˈtʊrə/ | n. | 花腔；炫技的歌唱；花腔女高音 |A rapid melodic passage, often with ornamentation, requiring great vocal agility, or a soprano specializing in such passages. |The soprano's coloratura in the aria was breathtaking.  这位女高音在咏叹调中的花腔令人叹为观止。|  She is a coloratura soprano known for her impressive range. 她是一位以音域宽广著称的花腔女高音。 | 近义词: ornamentation, embellishment, flourish | 反义词: simplicity | 词性: 名词

coloraturas:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊərəz/ /ˌkʌlərəˈtʊrəz/ | n. | 花腔（复数）；炫技的歌唱（复数）；花腔女高音（复数）|  Plural form of "coloratura," referring to multiple instances of rapid melodic passages or multiple singers specializing in them. | The opera featured dazzling coloraturas in the second act.  这部歌剧的第二幕以令人眼花缭乱的花腔为特色。|  The coloraturas in the choir blended beautifully. 合唱团中的花腔女高音配合得天衣无缝。| 近义词: ornamentations, embellishments, flourishes | 反义词: simplicities | 词性: 名词
