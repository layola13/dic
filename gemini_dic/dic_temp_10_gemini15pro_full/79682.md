glow:/ɡloʊ/| n. vi.|发光，光辉；喜悦，热情|A steady light emitted by a heated body; a feeling of warmth or happiness.|The embers still had a faint glow. 余烬仍然发出微弱的光芒。|近义词：shine, gleam, radiate, beam|反义词：dullness, darkness|noun
