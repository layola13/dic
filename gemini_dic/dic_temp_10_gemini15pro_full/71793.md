flector:/ˈflektər/| n.|屈肌；反射器；反射镜|A muscle that bends a part of the body, such as a limb.  A surface that reflects light, heat, or sound.|例句：The flector muscles in her leg contracted, allowing her to bend her knee. 她腿上的屈肌收缩，使她能够弯曲膝盖。

例句：The bicycle's flector made it visible to oncoming cars at night. 自行车的反光镜使夜间迎面而来的汽车能够看到它。|近义词：flexor (for muscle), reflector|反义词:extensor (for muscle)|词性:noun
