chicaning:/ʃɪˈkeɪnɪŋ/
v.
耍花招；诡辩；欺骗
using trickery to deceive someone
The lawyer was accused of chicaning to win the case.  这个律师被指控为了赢得案件而耍花招。
present participle of chicane
近义词: deceiving, tricking, manipulating, outmaneuvering, defrauding
反义词:  being honest, being forthright, being straightforward


chicane:/ʃɪˈkeɪn/
n.
诡计；花招；欺骗
trickery or deception
He resorted to chicane to get what he wanted. 他为了得到他想要的东西 resorted to chicane.
n.

The chicane caused several accidents during the race.  这个急弯在比赛中造成了几起事故。
v.
耍花招；诡辩；欺骗

He chicaned his way into a position of power. 他耍花招获得了权力职位。
词源: From French *chicane*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Provençal *chicana*, meaning "squabble, dispute."
近义词: trickery, deception, subterfuge, ruse, wile
反义词: honesty, forthrightness, straightforwardness
