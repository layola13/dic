boviform:/ˈboʊvɪfɔːrm/
adjective
牛形的；似牛的
Resembling an ox or cow in form or appearance.
The creature had a boviform appearance, with its large, stocky body and short legs.  这生物有着牛的形状，有着庞大壮硕的身体和短小的腿。
The artist sculpted a boviform figure from clay.  艺术家用粘土塑造了一个牛形的雕像。
近义词:ox-like, cow-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "slender," "graceful," or antonyms specific to the animal being compared, e.g., "equine" if contrasted with a horse)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *bovinus (“of or pertaining to oxen or cows”) + -form.  Ultimately from Latin bōs (“ox, cow”).
