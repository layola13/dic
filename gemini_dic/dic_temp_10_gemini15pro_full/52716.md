digynia:/daɪˈdʒɪniə/
noun
二雌蕊植物
A plant having two pistils or styles in each flower.
Example: The flower exhibits digynia, meaning it has two styles. 这朵花展现了二雌蕊植物的特征，意味着它有两个花柱。
