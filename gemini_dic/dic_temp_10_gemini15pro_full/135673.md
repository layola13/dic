nonpremiums:/ˌnɒnˈpriːmiəmz/
n.| 非溢价商品/服务；非赠品；普通商品/服务 | Products or services offered at a standard price, without any extra incentives or bonuses. | The store offered a range of nonpremiums during the sale.  这家商店在大甩卖期间提供了一系列普通商品。| synonyms: standard products, regular goods, basic offerings | antonyms: premium products, bonus items, luxury goods | noun

nonpremium:/ˌnɒnˈpriːmiəm/
adj.| 非溢价的；非赠品的；普通的 |  Not of premium quality; not offered as a bonus or incentive. |  They opted for the nonpremium version of the software. 他们选择了该软件的非高级版本。 | synonyms: standard, regular, basic | antonyms: premium, bonus, luxury | adjective
