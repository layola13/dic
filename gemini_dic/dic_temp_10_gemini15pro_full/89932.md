horsefly:/ˈhɔːrsflaɪ/ (US), /ˈhɔːsflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |马蝇|A large fly that bites horses, cattle, and sometimes people. It is known for its painful bite.|The horseflies were swarming around the horses in the field.  马蝇在田野里成群地围着马转。


The farmer sprayed his cows with insecticide to protect them from horseflies. 农场主给他的牛喷洒杀虫剂以保护它们免受马蝇的叮咬。


|近义词：gadfly, cleg, breeze fly|反义词: N/A |noun|
