enoint:/əˈnɔɪnt/ |v. |涂油；施以涂油礼；指定；选定|To smear or rub with oil, especially as part of a religious ceremony; to designate or choose for an office or position.|The priest anointed the king's forehead with holy oil.  神父用圣油涂抹国王的前额。|The board anointed her as the new CEO. 董事会指定她为新的首席执行官。| synonyms: consecrate, ordain, appoint, designate | antonyms:  dismiss, reject|verb
