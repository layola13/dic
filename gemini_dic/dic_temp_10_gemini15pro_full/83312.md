gynocracy:/ˌɡaɪˈnɒkrəsi/ |n.| 女性统治；女权政治 |A government ruled by women; female dominance, especially in a political or social context. |The Amazons of Greek mythology were often cited as an example of a gynocracy.  亚马逊人经常被引用为女性统治的例子。|The science fiction novel depicted a gynocracy where men were relegated to secondary roles. 这部科幻小说描绘了一个女性统治的社会，男性被 relegated to 次要角色。|Synonyms: matriarchy, female rule |Antonyms: patriarchy, male rule|noun
