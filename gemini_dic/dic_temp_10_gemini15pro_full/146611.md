overgrow:/ˌoʊvərˈɡroʊ/ /ˌəʊvəˈɡrəʊ/ | v. |过度生长，蔓生，覆盖|To grow over something so as to cover it completely; to become covered with plants that have been allowed to grow wild. |The garden is overgrown with weeds. 花园里杂草丛生。|Synonyms: overrun, engulf, smother, choke|Antonyms: prune, trim, cultivate|verb

overgrow:/ˌoʊvərˈɡroʊn/ /ˌəʊvəˈɡrəʊn/ | adj. |杂草丛生的，过度生长的|Covered with plants that have been allowed to grow wild. |The path was overgrown and difficult to follow. 小路杂草丛生，难以通行。|Synonyms: weedy, jungly, rampant|Antonyms: manicured, cultivated, trimmed|adjective
