hitch:/hɪtʃ/| n. vt.|故障，障碍；搭便车；套住，拴住|A hitch is a temporary problem or delay.  As a verb, it means to fasten something to something else, often a vehicle.|例句：There was a hitch in the plan. 计划中出现了一个小故障。|例句：We hitched the trailer to the car. 我们把拖车拴在汽车上。|近义词：(n.) snag, problem, glitch, obstacle (v.) fasten, attach, tie|反义词：(n.) solution, advantage (v.) detach, untie|词性:名词，动词
