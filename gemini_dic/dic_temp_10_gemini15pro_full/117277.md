mechanize:/ˈmɛkəˌnaɪz/| v.|机械化，使机械化|To equip with machinery, especially in order to replace human or animal labor.  |例句：The factory was mechanized to increase production.  工厂机械化以提高产量。


例句：They are mechanizing the process of sorting mail. 他们正在使邮件分拣过程机械化。

近义词：automate, motorize|反义词:manualize|词性:动词
