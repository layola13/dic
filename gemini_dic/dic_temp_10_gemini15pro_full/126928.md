narkier:/ˈnɑːrkiər/
adj.
更爱告密的;更爱抱怨的
More inclined to inform on others or complain, especially about trivial matters.
例句：He's become much narkier in his old age. 他年纪大了，变得更爱抱怨了。
例句：The new rules have made some employees narkier. 新的规定让一些员工更爱告密了。
近义词：whiny, complaining, tattling, gossiping
反义词：tolerant, understanding, forgiving
