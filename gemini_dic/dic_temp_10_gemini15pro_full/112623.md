low-grade:/ˌloʊˈɡreɪd/| adj.|低等级的；低劣的；低度的|Of inferior quality; not of the highest standard.  Relatively mild or undeveloped.|例句：He was diagnosed with low-grade fever. 他被诊断为低烧。

例句：The mine produced low-grade ore. 这座矿出产低品位矿石。|近义词：inferior, poor, substandard, second-rate, mediocre|反义词:high-grade, superior, excellent, premium|词性:adjective
