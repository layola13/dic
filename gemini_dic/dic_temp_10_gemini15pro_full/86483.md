heightener:/ˈhaɪtnər/| n.|增强器，增高剂，加剧者|Something that increases height, intensity, or effect.|The use of chili flakes is a common flavor heightener in many Asian cuisines. 辣椒片在许多亚洲菜肴中是一种常见的增味剂。|近义词：intensifier, amplifier, enhancer|反义词：reducer, diminisher, weakener|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From height + -en (verb suffix meaning "to make or become") + -er (noun suffix forming agent nouns).  The word "height" ultimately comes from the Proto-Germanic *hauhitjaz, meaning "high."
