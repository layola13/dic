nematelmia:/ˌnɛməˈtɛlmiə/
n.
线虫纲; 线虫动物门
A phylum of unsegmented, usually microscopic, worms with a long, slender, cylindrical body pointed at both ends. They are abundant in soil, fresh water, and marine environments, and some are parasitic.
例句：Nematelmia are a diverse group of animals found in many different environments. 线虫动物门是一个多样化的动物群体，存在于许多不同的环境中。

例句：Some species of Nematelmia are important agricultural pests. 一些线虫纲物种是重要的农业害虫。
近义词：Nematoda, roundworms
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a biological classification)
词性: noun
