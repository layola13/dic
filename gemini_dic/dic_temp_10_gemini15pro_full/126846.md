naphthylamine:/ˈnæfθəlˌæmiːn/ /ˈnæpθəlˌəmiːn/ | n. |萘胺|An aromatic amine derived from naphthalene by replacing one or more hydrogen atoms with amino groups.  A colorless crystalline solid, some forms of which are carcinogenic.|Naphthylamine is used in the production of certain dyes.|近义词: aminoaphthalene |反义词: None | noun
