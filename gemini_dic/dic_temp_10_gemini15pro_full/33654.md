cinematographer: /ˌsɪnəməˈtɒɡrəfər/ (UK) /ˌsɪnəməˈtɑːɡrəfər/ (US)| n. |电影摄影师，摄影指导|A person who is responsible for the art and technique of film photography, including the choice of cameras, lenses, film stock, lighting, composition, and camera movement.|例句：The cinematographer used a wide-angle lens to capture the vast landscape. 摄影师使用了广角镜头来捕捉广阔的风景。

例句：Roger Deakins is a highly acclaimed cinematographer. 罗杰·狄金斯是一位备受赞誉的电影摄影师。|近义词：director of photography, DP, cameraman|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From French *cinématographiste*, from cinématographe (“motion picture camera and projector”) + -iste.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κίνημα (kínēma, “movement”) + γράφω (gráphō, “write, record”).
