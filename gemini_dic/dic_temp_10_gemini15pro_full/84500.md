halwa:/ˈhɑːlvə/ /ˈhæl və/| n.|哈尔瓦（一种甜点）|A dense, sweet confection made from semolina, flour, butter, sugar, and often nuts or dried fruit. It's popular in many parts of the Middle East, South Asia, North Africa, and the Balkans.|We had a delicious halwa for dessert. 我们餐后甜点吃了美味的哈尔瓦。|Synonyms: sweet, confection, dessert|Antonyms: savory dish, main course|noun
