dischargees:/ˌdɪsˈtʃɑːrˈdʒiːz/
n.
被解雇者；被释放者；被排出者
People who have been released or discharged from a place or situation, such as a hospital, prison, or the military.
The hospital dischargees were given instructions on how to care for themselves at home.  医院的出院病人得到了在家如何照顾自己的指示。
The prison dischargees often struggle to reintegrate into society.  出狱的人员往往难以重新融入社会。
近义词:ex-patients, ex-inmates, veterans, graduates
反义词:patients, inmates, recruits
