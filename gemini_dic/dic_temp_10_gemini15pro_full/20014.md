bioluminescent:/ˌbaɪoʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsənt/| adj.|生物发光的|Producing and emitting light as a result of a chemical reaction within a living organism.  The light is produced by a chemical reaction between a light-emitting molecule called luciferin and an enzyme called luciferase.|Fireflies are bioluminescent insects. 萤火虫是生物发光的昆虫。|
The deep sea is home to many bioluminescent creatures. 深海是许多生物发光生物的家园。|近义词：light-emitting, phosphorescent|反义词:non-luminescent|词性:adjective
bioluminescence:/ˌbaɪoʊˌluːmɪˈnɛsəns/| n.|生物发光|The biochemical emission of light by living organisms such as fireflies and deep-sea fishes.|Bioluminescence is used by some animals for camouflage. 有些动物利用生物发光来伪装。|The bioluminescence of the plankton created a magical glow in the water. 浮游生物的生物发光在水中创造了一种神奇的光芒。|近义词：phosphorescence|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + luminescent.  "Bio-" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life."  "Luminescent" comes from the Latin "lumen" meaning "light."
