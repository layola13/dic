inarticulateness:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjələtnəs/
| n. |表达不清，不善言辞|The state of being unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.|His inarticulateness made it difficult to understand his point. 他的表达不清使得很难理解他的观点。|近义词：speechlessness, tongue-tiedness, inability to express oneself|反义词：articulateness, eloquence, fluency|词性:名词

inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjələt/
| adj. |表达不清的，不善言辞的|Unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.|He became inarticulate with rage. 他气得说不出话来。|近义词：speechless, tongue-tied, halting|反义词：articulate, eloquent, fluent|词性:形容词
