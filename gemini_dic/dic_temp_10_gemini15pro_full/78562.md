ghoulishly:/ˈɡuːlɪʃli/| adv.| 可怕地，令人毛骨悚然地 |In a gruesome or macabre manner, suggestive of ghouls.  |He grinned ghoulishly at the sight of the fake blood. 他看到假血，露出了令人毛骨悚然的笑容。|She described the scene ghoulishly, relishing the gory details. 她绘声绘色地描述了现场，津津乐道于血腥的细节。|Synonym: gruesomely, macabrely, morbidly, creepily |Antonym: pleasantly, cheerfully, delightfully|adverb

Etymology:
From ghoul + -ish + -ly. Ghoul comes from Arabic غول (ghūl), a desert demon believed to rob graves and feed on corpses.
