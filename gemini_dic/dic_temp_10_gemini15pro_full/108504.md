layman:/ˈleɪmən/| n.|外行，门外汉；俗人|a person who is not trained or ordained as a minister or priest; a person without professional or specialized knowledge in a particular subject.|The doctor explained the complex procedure in layman's terms so that I could understand it. 医生用通俗易懂的语言解释了这个复杂的手术过程，以便我能理解。|synonyms: nonprofessional, amateur, novice|antonyms: expert, professional, specialist|noun
