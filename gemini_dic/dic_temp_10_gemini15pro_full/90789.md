humouring:/ˈhjuːmərɪŋ (UK) / /ˈhjumərɪŋ (US)/
verb
中文翻译：迁就；迎合；顺着(某人)
英文解析：The present participle of humour, meaning to comply with the whims or fancies of (someone) to keep them content, however unreasonable.
例句：I was just humouring him when I agreed to his ridiculous plan. 我同意他那荒谬的计划只是在迁就他。

例句：She humoured her grandfather by pretending to listen to his long, rambling stories. 她假装听她祖父冗长、不着边际的故事来哄他开心。

近义词：indulging, appeasing, accommodating, pandering to
反义词：opposing, contradicting, defying
词性：v. (verb)
