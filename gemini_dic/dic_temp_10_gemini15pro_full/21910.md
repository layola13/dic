boeotian:/biːˈoʊʃən/| adj. n.|迟钝的，愚笨的；古波奥提亚人|A dull, stupid, or obtuse person; an inhabitant of ancient Boeotia.|He dismissed his critics as boeotian philistines. 他把批评他的人都斥为愚钝的庸人。|近义词：stupid, dull, obtuse, unintelligent|反义词：intelligent, bright, clever, sharp|词性:形容词, 名词

boeotian:/biːˈoʊʃən/| n.|古波奥提亚人|An inhabitant of ancient Boeotia, a region of ancient Greece.|The Boeotians were known for their agricultural prowess. 波奥提亚人以其农业实力而闻名。|近义词：Boeotians (plural)|反义词：None (proper noun)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Boeotia, a region of ancient Greece north of Attica, whose inhabitants were considered by the Athenians to be dull and rustic.  Ultimately from Greek *Boiōtoi*.
