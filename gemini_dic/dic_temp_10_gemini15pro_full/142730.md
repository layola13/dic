耳鼻喉科医生
A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the ear, nose, and throat.
例句：I need to see an otolaryngologist about my persistent sore throat.  我需要去看耳鼻喉科医生看看我持续的喉咙痛。
