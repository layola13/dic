objet:/ˈɒbdʒeɪ/ (UK), /ˈɑːbdʒeɪ/ (US)| n.|物体；对象；目标；客体|An objet is a material thing that can be seen and touched.  An objet can also be the focus of attention or action, or something mental or physical towards which thought, feeling, or action is directed.|The museum displayed several valuable objets d'art.|近义词：item, thing, article, object, target, aim, goal|反义词: subject|noun
