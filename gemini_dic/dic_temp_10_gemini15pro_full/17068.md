bathymetry:/bəˈθɪmətri/ | n. | 水深测量；海底地形 | The study of underwater depth of lake or ocean floors.  It is the underwater equivalent to hypsometry or topography. |  Scientists used sonar to create a bathymetry map of the ocean floor. 科学家利用声纳绘制了海底地形图。|  hypsography, topography | - | noun
Etymology:  From Ancient Greek βαθύς (bathús, “deep”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
