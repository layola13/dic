musty's:/ˈmʌstiz/ | n. | 发霉的东西; 发霉的气味(通常用复数形式) |  Usually used in the plural to refer to things that smell or taste musty, or the musty smell/taste itself. | The old books in the attic had a distinct musty's about them. 阁楼里的旧书有一股明显的霉味。| 近义词: mustiness, moldiness, dampness | 反义词: freshness, cleanness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "musty" (adjective) + "'s" (possessive suffix, indicating a quality or characteristic).  "Musty" comes from Middle English *musti*, probably related to Old French *moisi* "moldy."
