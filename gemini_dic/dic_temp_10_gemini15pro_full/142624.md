osteosclerosis: /ˌɒstioʊskləˈrəʊsɪs/ (US) /ˌɒstiəʊskləˈrəʊsɪs/ (UK)| n. | 骨硬化 |Abnormal hardening of bone tissue.  |Example: The X-ray revealed osteosclerosis in his hip. X光显示他的髋部有骨硬化。|Synonym: osteopetrosis, hardening of bone|Antonym: osteomalacia, softening of bone|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  *ὀστέον (ostéon, “bone”) +  *σκληρός (sklērós, “hard”) + *-ωσις (-ōsis, abstract noun suffix).
