momentary:/ˈmoʊmənˌteri/ (US) /ˈməʊmənˌtri/ (UK)| adj.|短暂的，瞬间的|Lasting for a very short time; brief.|例句：The pain was momentary, but intense. 疼痛短暂但剧烈。|近义词：brief, fleeting, transient, ephemeral|反义词：lasting, enduring, permanent|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *momentarius, from Latin momentum "movement, motion; importance, weight, influence; instant, moment of time," from movere "to move."
