hogwash:/ˈhɒɡwɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːɡwɑːʃ/ (US)| n. |废话；胡说八道；无聊的东西|Nonsense; meaningless or insincere talk or writing.  It often implies that something is deceptive or misleading.|例句：That's a load of hogwash!  那是一派胡言！

例句：He dismissed the report as complete hogwash. 他把这份报告斥为一派胡言。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, balderdash, bunk, claptrap, twaddle|反义词：truth, fact, reality|词性:名词
