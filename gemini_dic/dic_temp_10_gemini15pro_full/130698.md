nonaggression:/ˌnɒnəˈɡrɛʃən/ |n.|不侵略，互不侵犯|The two countries signed a pact of nonaggression. 这两个国家签署了互不侵犯条约。|Synonyms: peace, truce, armistice |Antonyms: aggression, hostility, war|noun

nonaggressions:/ˌnɒnəˈɡrɛʃənz/ |n.|不侵略（复数）|Multiple nonaggressions treaties were signed to prevent further conflict. 多项互不侵犯条约的签署是为了防止进一步的冲突。|Synonyms: peaces, truces, armistices |Antonyms: aggressions, hostilities, wars|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + aggression.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Aggression" comes from the Latin "aggressio," meaning "attack" or "assault."
