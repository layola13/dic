deviltry:/ˈdevəltri/ | n. | 恶作剧；捣蛋；胡闹 | Mischievous or malicious behavior; playful tricks or pranks, often with a hint of wickedness. |  例句：The children's deviltry kept their parents constantly on their toes. 孩子们的恶作剧让他们的父母一刻不得闲。

例句：His eyes twinkled with deviltry as he planned his next prank. 他计划着下一个恶作剧时，他的眼睛里闪烁着调皮的光芒。

| 近义词：mischief, pranks, shenanigans, naughtiness, roguery | 反义词：obedience, goodness, virtue | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *develrye, from devel (“devil”) + -rye, equivalent to devil + -ry.
