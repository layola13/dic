accrescent:/əˈkrɛsənt/
adjective
增长的，渐增的
Increasing in size with age, especially after flowering, as a calyx or other part of a plant.
The sepals of the flower are accrescent, enlarging and enclosing the fruit as it matures.  这朵花的花萼是增长的，随着果实的成熟而增大并包围果实。

The plant has accrescent leaves that continue to grow throughout the summer.  这种植物的叶子会持续生长整个夏天。
近义词: enlarging, expanding, growing, increasing
反义词: decreasing, diminishing, shrinking
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin accrescens, present participle of accrescere ("to grow larger"), from ad- ("to, towards") + crescere ("to grow").

USAGE->
The accrescent calyx is a key characteristic of this species. = 增长的花萼是该物种的一个关键特征。

Some plants have accrescent fruits that continue to swell even after ripening. =  一些植物的果实即使在成熟后也会继续膨胀。
