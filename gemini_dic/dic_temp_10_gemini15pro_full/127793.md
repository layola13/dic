neighbourhood:/ˈneɪbərˌhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n. |街区，邻近地区|A district or area with its own identity, character, or community.|例句：We live in a friendly neighbourhood. 我们住在一个友好的街区。|近义词：community, area, vicinity, district|反义词:remoteness, isolation|noun

neighbourhood:/ˈneɪbərˌhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n. |邻里关系|The state of being neighbours; neighbourliness.|例句：There's a strong sense of neighbourhood here. 这里邻里关系很融洽。|近义词：neighborliness, camaraderie, fellowship|反义词:hostility, animosity|noun
