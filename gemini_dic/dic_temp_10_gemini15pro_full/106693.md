/kwɪ/ |pron. |什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| which, who |  |pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she doing right now? = 她现在正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you been up to lately? = 你最近都在做什么？
What has she learned from this experience? = 她从这次经历中学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been thinking about all this time? = 他一直以来都在想什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when you saw her? = 你看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 中断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们讨论了好几个小时，都在讨论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将会取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get there? = 等我们到那里的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2032? = 到2032年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？


PHRASE->

So what? = 那又怎样？
