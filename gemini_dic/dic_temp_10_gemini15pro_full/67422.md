extillation:/ɛkstɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 蒸馏; 提炼; 精华 |  The process of extracting the essential meaning or most important aspects of something.  The product of distilling. | The extillation of his years of research is a groundbreaking new theory. 他多年研究的结晶是一个开创性的新理论。 | 近义词: distillation, refinement, essence, quintessence | 反义词:  dilution, coarseness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin extillatio, from extillare ‘to drip out,’ from ex- ‘out of’ + stillare ‘to drip.’
