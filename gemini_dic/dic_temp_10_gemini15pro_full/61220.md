elytra:/ˈɛlɪtrə/ (US) /ˈɛlɪtrə/ (UK)| n. |鞘翅；翅鞘 (elytron的复数形式)|The hard, horny forewings of beetles and some other insects, serving to protect the hindwings when folded. |  The iridescent elytra of the beetle shimmered in the sunlight. 甲虫的彩虹色鞘翅在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词:forewings, wing cases | 反义词:hindwings | noun

词源: From Modern Latin *ēlytron, from Ancient Greek ἔλυτρον (élutron, “sheath, covering”), from ἐλύω (elúō, “I wrap, roll up”).
