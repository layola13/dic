camara:/ˈkæmərə/ /ˈkɑːmərə/| n.|房间，室；照相机；法官办公室|A small room; a chamber, especially one used for a specific purpose. Also, an old-fashioned term for a camera. |例句：The judge retired to his camara to consider the verdict. 法官回到他的办公室去考虑判决。

例句：He took a picture with his old camara. 他用他的旧相机拍了一张照片。|近义词：room, chamber, apartment, camera|反义词:hall, corridor|词性:noun
