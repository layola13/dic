militate:/ˈmɪlɪteɪt/
verb
(不利地)影响，作用
to have weight or effect (usually followed by *against*)
His criminal record militated against him getting the job. 他的犯罪记录对他获得这份工作产生了不利影响。
近义词:count against, work against, operate against, tell against, argue against, go against, weigh against, prejudice, bias, disadvantage
反义词:favor, support, benefit, help, aid, assist
词性:v.
