epitomizer:/ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪzər/| n. | 典型，缩影，概括者 |A person or thing that is a perfect example of a particular quality or type. |  Nelson Mandela was the epitomizer of courage and resilience. 纳尔逊·曼德拉是勇气和韧性的典范。

The new sports car is the epitomizer of luxury and performance. 这款新的跑车是奢华和性能的缩影。 | 近义词: embodiment, personification, exemplar, quintessence | 反义词: antithesis, opposite | 词性: noun

epitomize:/ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪz/| v. |  是…的典型；概括；成为…的缩影 |To be a perfect example of something; typify. |  Her work epitomizes the struggles of immigrant families. 她的作品是移民家庭奋斗的缩影。

His latest film epitomizes everything that is wrong with Hollywood. 他的最新电影集中体现了所有好莱坞的问题。| 近义词: represent, embody, exemplify, symbolize | 反义词: contradict, negate | 词性: verb
