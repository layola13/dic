optimist:/ˈɒptɪmɪst/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmɪst/ (US)| n.|乐观主义者；乐天派|A person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future or the success of something.|例句：She's a born optimist and always looks on the bright side. 她天生是个乐观主义者，总是看到光明的一面。

例句：Even in the face of adversity, he remained an optimist. 即使面对逆境，他仍然保持乐观。|近义词：positive thinker, hopeful person|反义词:pessimist|词性:名词


optimistic:/ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/ (UK) /ˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/ (US)| adj.|乐观的；乐观主义的|Hopeful and confident about the future.|例句：She felt optimistic about the outcome of the meeting. 她对会议的结果感到乐观。

例句：He had an optimistic outlook on life. 他对生活持乐观态度。|近义词：positive, hopeful, sanguine|反义词:pessimistic|词性:形容词


optimistically:/ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪkli/ (UK) /ˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪkli/ (US)| adv.|乐观地|In a hopeful and confident way.|例句：She optimistically predicted that the project would be a success. 她乐观地预测该项目将会成功。

例句：He spoke optimistically about the future of the company. 他乐观地谈论了公司的未来。|近义词：hopefully, positively|反义词:pessimistically|词性:副词
