lipa:/ˈliːpə/| n. |菩提树; 椴树|A deciduous tree with heart-shaped leaves and fragrant yellowish flowers.  The lime tree is also a source of lime blossom tea.|The lipa tree in the town square is hundreds of years old. 城镇广场上的菩提树已经有几百年的历史了。|synonyms: lime tree, linden, basswood|antonyms: N/A|noun
