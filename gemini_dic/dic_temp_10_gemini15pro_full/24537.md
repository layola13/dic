broadline:/ˈbrɔːdlaɪn/| adj.|广谱的，广泛的；（商品）种类齐全的|A broadline retailer or wholesaler stocks a wide range of different types of goods.|This supermarket is a broadline retailer, selling everything from groceries to clothing.|近义词：wide-ranging, comprehensive, diversified|反义词：specialized, niche, limited|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
"broad" (wide) + "line" (range of products).  The term originated in the retail and wholesale trade to describe businesses offering a wide variety of goods.
