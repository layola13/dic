paraglide:/ˈpærəɡlaɪd/| v. n.|滑翔伞运动，滑翔伞|To fly using a paraglider.  The act of flying a paraglider. |He wanted to paraglide over the Alps. 他想滑翔伞飞越阿尔卑斯山。|The paraglide offered breathtaking views of the coastline. 滑翔伞提供了令人惊叹的海岸线景色。|近义词：glide, soar, parachute|反义词：(没有明确的反义词，可以考虑land, descend)|词性: verb, noun


Etymology->
From para- (meaning "nearby, alongside") + glide.  The "para-" prefix in this case refers to the parachute-like canopy.
