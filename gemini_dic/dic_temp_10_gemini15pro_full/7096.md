anesthetists:/əˈnɛsθətɪsts/ | n. |麻醉师|A medical doctor trained to administer anesthetics and manage patients' pain and vital signs during surgery and other medical procedures.|The anesthetists monitored the patient's heart rate throughout the operation. 麻醉师在整个手术过程中监测病人的心率。|The anesthetists explained the risks of general anesthesia before the procedure. 麻醉师在手术前解释了全身麻醉的风险。|Synonyms: anesthesiologist |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "patient" in a specific context)|Noun
