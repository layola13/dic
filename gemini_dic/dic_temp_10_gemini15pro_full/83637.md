haemoglobins:/ˌhiːməˈɡloʊbɪnz/ /ˌhiːməˈɡləʊbɪnz/ | n. |血红蛋白|A red protein responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood of vertebrates. It contains iron, to which the oxygen binds. |例句：The doctor checked her haemoglobin levels. 医生检查了她的血红蛋白水平。

例句：Haemoglobins are essential for carrying oxygen throughout the body. 血红蛋白对于在全身输送氧气至关重要。

近义词：- (No direct single-word synonym, but related terms include:  erythrocytes (red blood cells), oxygen carriers)

反义词：- (No direct antonym)

词性：noun
