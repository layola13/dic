buy-in:/ˈbaɪɪn/| n.|支持，同意，赞同；买进，入股；(尤指公司) 首次公开募股|Acceptance of and willingness to actively support and participate in something (especially a plan).  The purchase of a stake in a business (especially during an initial public offering).|例句：We need to get buy-in from all stakeholders before proceeding. 我们在继续进行之前需要得到所有利益相关者的支持。

例句：The company's buy-in on the stock market was successful.  这家公司在股票市场的首次公开募股很成功。|近义词：agreement, acceptance, approval, support, backing, investment, stake|反义词：opposition, disagreement, resistance, rejection, divestment|词性:noun
