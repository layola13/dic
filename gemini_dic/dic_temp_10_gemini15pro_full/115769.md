marinations:/ˌmærɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|腌泡汁；腌制法|The process of soaking foods in a seasoned liquid before cooking, or the liquid itself.  指在烹饪前将食物浸泡在调味液体中的过程，或指这种液体本身。|The chef prepared three different marinations for the chicken. 厨师为鸡肉准备了三种不同的腌泡汁。|近义词：sauces, dips|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑：dry rubs)|词性:名词


词源:
From French "marinade," from "mariner" (to pickle in seawater), from "marin" (sea).  源自法语 "marinade"，来自 "mariner"（用海水腌制），来自 "marin"（海洋）。
