basseting:/ˈbæsɪtɪŋ/
n.
垂耳; 低矮; 垂耳状;
The act of breeding or developing basset hounds, or the characteristic of having drooping ears.
Basseting is a prominent feature of the breed.  垂耳是这个品种的显著特征。
近义词:drooping, sagging
反义词:erect, upright
词性:名词

basseting:/ˈbæsɪtɪŋ/
v.
使垂下; 使低矮;
The act of causing something to droop or sag.
The weight of the snow was basseting the branches. 雪的重量使树枝下垂。
近义词:lowering, drooping
反义词:raising, lifting
词性:动词
