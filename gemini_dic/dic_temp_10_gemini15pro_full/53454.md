disapprovals: /ˌdɪsəˈpruːvəlz/
noun
不赞成，反对；非难
Expressions of disapproval; censures.
The board expressed their disapprovals of the CEO's decision. 董事会表达了他们对CEO决定的反对。
Her silence was taken as a sign of disapproval. 她的沉默被认为是不赞成的表示。
synonyms: objections, censures, criticisms, condemnations
antonyms: approvals, endorsements, sanctions, commendations
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
