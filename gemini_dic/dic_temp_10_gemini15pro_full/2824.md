advents:/ˈædˌvɛnts/| n.| 到来，出现；基督降临节|The advents of new technologies have dramatically changed our lives. 新技术的到来极大地改变了我们的生活。|例句：The advent of the internet marked a new era in communication. 互联网的出现标志着通讯领域的新纪元。

The four Sundays before Christmas are known as Advent. 圣诞节前的四个星期天被称为基督降临节。|近义词：arrivals, beginnings, onsets|反义词：departures, endings|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avent, from Latin adventus (“arrival”), from advenīre (“to arrive”), from ad- (“to”) + venīre (“to come”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷem- (“to come; to go”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advent of spring brings warmer weather. = 春天的到来带来了温暖的天气。
His advent changes the whole situation. = 他的到来改变了整个局面。

一般过去时:
The advent of the automobile revolutionized transportation. = 汽车的出现彻底改变了交通运输。
The advent of electricity transformed daily life. = 电的出现改变了日常生活。


现在完成时:
The advent of social media has connected people worldwide. = 社交媒体的出现将世界各地的人们联系起来。
The advent of new medical treatments has increased life expectancy. = 新医疗手段的出现提高了预期寿命。


过去完成时:
Before the advent of computers, calculations were done by hand. = 在计算机出现之前，计算都是手工完成的。
The advent of the printing press had revolutionized communication before the advent of the internet. = 在互联网出现之前，印刷机的出现已经彻底改变了通讯方式。


将来时:
The advent of artificial intelligence will likely reshape the job market. = 人工智能的到来可能会重塑就业市场。
The advent of new energy sources will be crucial for a sustainable future. = 新能源的出现对于可持续的未来至关重要。



虚拟语气:
If the advent of this technology were to occur sooner, it would greatly benefit society. = 如果这项技术能早点出现，将会极大地造福社会。
Were the advent of peace to happen, the world would celebrate. = 如果和平到来，世界将会欢庆。


被动语态:
The advent of the digital age was widely celebrated. = 数字时代的到来受到了广泛的庆祝。
