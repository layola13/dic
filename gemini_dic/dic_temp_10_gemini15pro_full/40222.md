contextualist:/kənˈtekstʃuəlɪst/ | n. | 语境主义者；上下文主义者 |  A person who believes that the meaning of words, actions, or texts depends on the context in which they occur. |  The contextualist argued that the author's intentions were irrelevant to the meaning of the text. 语境主义者认为作者的意图与文本的意义无关。 | synonyms: interpretivist, pragmatist | antonyms: formalist, literalist | noun

contextual:/kənˈtekstʃuəl/ | adj. | 取决于上下文的；与上下文有关的 | Depending on or relating to the context. | The meaning of the word is highly contextual.  这个词的含义很大程度上取决于上下文。 | synonyms: circumstantial, contingent, relative | antonyms: absolute, inherent, intrinsic | adjective
