lithophosphoric:/ˌlɪθoʊfɒˈsfɒrɪk/ /ˌlɪθoʊfɑːˈsfɔːrɪk/
adjective
磷酸锂的; 含磷酸锂的
Relating to or containing lithium phosphate (Li3PO4).
The lithophosphoric compound showed promising results in battery testing.  这种磷酸锂化合物在电池测试中显示出良好的结果。
The researchers synthesized a new lithophosphoric material for use in solid-state batteries. 研究人员合成了一种新的磷酸锂材料，用于固态电池。
近义词: lithium-phosphate-containing
反义词: (No direct antonym, could be context-specific, e.g., non-lithophosphoric)
词性: adjective
