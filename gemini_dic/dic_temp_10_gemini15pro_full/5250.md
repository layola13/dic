allocution:/ˌæləˈkjuːʃən/
n.
a formal speech
a formal speech giving advice or a warning.
The president's allocution to the nation was broadcast on all major networks. 总统向全国发表的讲话在所有主要电视网络上播出。
The Pope delivered his weekly allocution to the assembled crowd in St. Peter's Square. 教皇在圣彼得广场向聚集的人群发表了每周讲话。
address, speech, oration, discourse
chat, dialogue
