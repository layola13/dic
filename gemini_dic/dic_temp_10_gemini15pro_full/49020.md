degras:/deɪˈɡrɑː/ | n. | 油脂，脱脂物 |  Residue from the process of oil-tanning, containing oxidized fish oil, animal fats, and water. Used as a leather dressing. |  The saddle maker used degras to soften the leather.  鞍具制造商用油脂软化皮革。 | synonyms:  dubbin,  currier's grease | antonyms: N/A | noun
degras:/deɪˈɡrɑː/ | n. | 油渣 | The solid residue left after extracting oil from animal fats, sometimes used in fertilizers. | The degras from the rendering process was used as fertilizer. 提炼过程中的油渣被用作肥料。 | synonyms:  greaves,  cracklings | antonyms: N/A | noun
