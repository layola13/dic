decoding: /diːˈkoʊdɪŋ/ (US), /diːˈkəʊdɪŋ/ (UK)
|n.|解码，译码|The process of converting code into ordinary language.  The decoding of the enemy's messages provided vital intelligence.  |例句：The decoding of the ancient script revealed surprising insights into their culture.  对古代文字的解码揭示了他们文化中令人惊讶的见解。

例句：The spy was skilled in decoding secret messages.  这个间谍擅长解码秘密信息。
|近义词：deciphering, interpreting, translating|反义词：encoding, encrypting|词性: 名词

de-coding: /diːˈkoʊdɪŋ/ (US), /diːˈkəʊdɪŋ/ (UK)
|v.|解码，译码|To convert (a coded message) into intelligible language. |例句：Researchers are working to decode the human genome. 研究人员正在努力解码人类基因组。

例句：He quickly decoded the message hidden in the numbers. 他迅速地将隐藏在数字中的信息解码。
|近义词：deciphering, interpreting, translating|反义词：encoding, encrypting|词性: 动词
