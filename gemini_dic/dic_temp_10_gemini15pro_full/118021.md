memoryless:/ˈmeməriˌles/| adj.|无记忆的，无记忆性的|Lacking memory; specifically (of a stochastic process) having the property that the probability of an event occurring in the future is independent of the past.|The exponential distribution is a common example of a memoryless process.|近义词：Markovian, amnesiac|反义词：retentive, mindful|词性:形容词
