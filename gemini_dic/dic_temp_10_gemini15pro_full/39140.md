confusingness:/kənˈfjuːzɪŋnɪs/| n. |困惑，混乱|The confusingness of the instructions led to many errors.  说明书的混乱导致了很多错误。|The confusingness of the situation made it difficult to decide what to do.  情况的混乱让人难以决定该怎么做。|近义词：confusion, bewilderment, perplexity|反义词：clarity, clearness, simplicity|词性:noun
Etymology: From confuse (v.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and verbal nouns) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin confundere "to pour together, mix up," from com- "together" + fundere "to pour."
