diplomatism:/dɪˈpləʊməˌtɪzəm/ | n. |外交手腕，交际手腕，策略|The situation required tact and diplomatism.|例句：He handled the delicate situation with great diplomatism.  他以极高的外交手腕处理了这个微妙的局面。

例句：Her diplomatism smoothed the way for a successful negotiation. 她的交际手腕为成功的谈判铺平了道路。


|近义词：tact, diplomacy, finesse, skill, discretion|反义词:clumsiness, bluntness, tactlessness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French diplomatisme, from diplomate (diplomat) + -isme. Ultimately from Greek diploma, meaning "folded document."
