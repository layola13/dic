contributary: /kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ /kənˈtrɪbjəteri/
contributary: n. | 支流；附属物；促成因素 | A stream that flows into a larger stream or river.  Something that contributes to a larger whole. | The Ohio River is a major tributary of the Mississippi River.  俄亥俄河是密西西比河的主要支流。 Stress is a major contributory factor to many illnesses. 压力是许多疾病的主要诱因。 |近义词: affluent, branch, feeder | 反义词: distributary | 词性: 名词

contributary: /kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ /kənˈtrɪbjəteri/
contributary: adj. | 贡献的；附属的；支流的 | Contributing to a larger whole; subsidiary.  Relating to a tributary. |  Several contributory factors led to the accident.  几个促成因素导致了这起事故。 The contributory streams make the main river wider.  支流使主河道更宽。 | 近义词: contributing, subsidiary, accessory | 反义词: main, primary | 词性: 形容词
