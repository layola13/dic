diners:/ˈdaɪnərz/| n.|用餐者；餐车式小餐馆|People who eat a meal, typically in a restaurant.  A small, informal restaurant, often resembling a railway dining car. |The diners enjoyed their meals. 用餐者们享受着他们的美食。|The diner down the street serves the best breakfast. 街角那家小餐馆的早餐最棒。|近义词：customers, patrons, eateries, restaurants|反义词:| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dine (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting persons performing an action), ultimately from Latin dis- (apart) + jejunare (to fast). The sense of "dining car" arose from their use in railway dining cars.
