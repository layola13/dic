/wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) |pron.| 什么 | Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | which, who | --- | pronoun

/wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) |det. | 什么，哪一个，哪一些 | Used to ask for information specifying something. | What color is your car? 你的车是什么颜色的？ | which, who | --- | determiner

/wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) |interj.| 什么 | Expressing surprise or disbelief. | What! You didn't tell me! 什么！你没告诉我！ | --- | --- | interjection

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, nominative and accusative neuter singular of hwā (“who”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷód. Cognate with Scots quhat, what, West Frisian wat, Dutch wat, German was, Danish hvad, Swedish vad, Icelandic hvað.

USAGE->

Simple Present:
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for breakfast? = 她早餐想吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚晚餐做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had she done before she left? = 她离开前做了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will have changed by the next election? = 到下次选举会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将已经做了什么？


PHRASE->

What for? = 为了什么？/  干什么用？
