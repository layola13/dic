overcleverly:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛvərli/
adjective|过于聪明地；自作聪明地|In a way that is excessively or inappropriately clever, often resulting in a contrived or artificial effect.|He overcleverly worded his response, making it sound insincere.| 近义词: too cleverly, excessively cleverly, pretentiously, artfully|反义词: simply, plainly, artlessly|词性: adverb

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "cleverly" (from "clever" (Middle English *clever, from uncertain origin) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix))
