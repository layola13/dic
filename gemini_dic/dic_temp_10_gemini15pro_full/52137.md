diapente:/daɪəˈpɛnti/| n.|五度音程；(诗歌)五音步诗行；|A perfect fifth in music.  在音乐中指纯五度。|The diapente interval creates a pleasing harmony. 五度音程创造了一种悦耳的和声。|近义词：fifth, perfect fifth|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diapente, from Greek διάπεντε (diápente, “through five”), from διά (diá, “through”) + πέντε (pénte, “five”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pénkʷe (“five”).
