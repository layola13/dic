misgrounded:/ˌmɪsˈɡraʊndɪd/
adjective
根据错误的理由；理由不充分的；没有根据的
based on incorrect reasons or information; poorly founded; unjustified
His argument was completely misgrounded. 他的论点完全没有根据。
Synonyms: unfounded, baseless, groundless, unwarranted, unjustified, fallacious
Antonyms: well-founded, justified, valid, sound, legitimate
词性: adjective
