concordal:/kənˈkɔːrdl/| adj.|一致的，和谐的，符合的|Being in agreement or harmony; corresponding.  Relating to or involving a concordance.|The concordal passages in the Gospels tell the same story. 福音书中一致的段落讲述了同一个故事。|近义词：harmonious, agreeing, consistent, consonant, corresponding|反义词：discordant, disagreeing, inconsistent, dissonant|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin concordalis, from concordia ‘agreement’ (see concord).
