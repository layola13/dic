out-of-the-blue:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈbluː/
adjective
突然地，意外地
Happening suddenly and unexpectedly.
The job offer came completely out of the blue.  这份工作机会完全是突然出现的。
He showed up at my door out of the blue. 他突然出现在我的门口。
Synonyms: unexpectedly, suddenly, without warning
Antonyms: expectedly, predictably
词性: adjective
