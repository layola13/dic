outhector:/ˌaʊtˈhɛktər/
verb
以威吓方式战胜; 咆哮; 压倒;
to intimidate or subdue by noise, threats, or bluster.
He outhectored his opponent into submission. 他咆哮着使对手屈服。
The bully tried to outhector the smaller children. 恶霸试图恐吓比他小的孩子们。
近义词: browbeat, bully, intimidate, cow
反义词: cajole, coax, persuade
