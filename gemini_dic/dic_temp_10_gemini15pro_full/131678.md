nonconciliatory:/nɒnkənˈsɪliətəri/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈsɪliətɔːri/
adjective
不妥协的，不让步的，不和解的
Not willing to compromise, make concessions, or come to an agreement.
例句：His nonconciliatory attitude made negotiations difficult. 他的不妥协态度使得谈判变得困难。

例句：The government took a nonconciliatory stance on the issue. 政府在这个问题上采取了不让步的立场。

近义词：inflexible, unyielding, intransigent, uncompromising
反义词：conciliatory, accommodating, flexible, yielding
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + conciliatory (from conciliate, from Latin conciliare "to bring together, unite, win over," from concilium "council").
