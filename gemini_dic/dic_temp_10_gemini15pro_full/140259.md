offshore:/ˌɒfˈʃɔːr/ (UK) /ˌɔːfˈʃɔːr/ (US)| n.|近海, 离岸|The area of sea that is near to the coast, but not close to it.|Oil platforms are often located in the offshore.  石油平台通常位于近海。|近义词：offshore waters|反义词:inshore|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From off + shore.  "Off" indicating separation or distance and "shore" referring to the land bordering a body of water.
