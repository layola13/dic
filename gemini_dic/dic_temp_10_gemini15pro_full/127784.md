neighborly:/ˈneɪbərli/| adj.|友好的，睦邻的，像邻居的|Showing kindness and friendliness to one's neighbors; helpful and sociable.|例句：She was always neighborly, offering help whenever she could. 她总是很友善，随时乐于助人。|例句：It was a neighborly gesture to bring over a plate of cookies.  带一盘饼干过来是一种睦邻友好的姿态。|近义词：friendly, amiable, kind, helpful, sociable, cordial, amicable|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, unsociable, aloof, cold|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From neighbor + -ly.  "Neighbor" comes from Old English *nēahgebūr, from *nēah (near) + gebūr (dweller).
