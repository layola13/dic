chicle:/ˈtʃɪkəl/ /ˈtʃiːkl/| n. |口香糖原料，天然树胶|A milky latex tapped from sapodilla trees, used as the basis of chewing gum.|例句：Chicle was the original base for chewing gum.  天然树胶是口香糖最初的基料。

例句：The ancient Mayans chewed chicle long before modern chewing gum was invented.  早在现代口香糖发明之前，古代玛雅人就嚼天然树胶了。|近义词：gum base, latex|反义词: None|词性:noun
