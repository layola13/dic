Etymology:
* non-:  a prefix meaning "not," "opposite of," "lack of," from Latin *non*.
* development: from the Old French *desveloper*, meaning "to unwrap, unfold."  Relates to the process of growth and maturation.
* -al: suffix forming adjectives from nouns.
* -ly: suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
