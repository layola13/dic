oscine:/ˈɑːsaɪn/ /ˈɒsaɪn/ | adj. n. |鸣禽，鸣禽类的|Of or relating to the suborder Oscines, characterized by complex vocalizations.  A bird of the suborder Oscines. |例句：The oscine birds are known for their beautiful songs. 鸣禽以其优美的歌声而闻名。

例句：The robin is an oscine bird common in many gardens. 知更鸟是一种在许多花园中常见的鸣禽。|近义词：songbird, passerine|反义词: (no direct antonym, potentially non-passerine)|词性: adjective, noun
