camelbacked:/ˈkæməlbækt/| adj.|驼背的，弓形的|Having a hump or a rounded shape like a camel's back.  Resembling the back of a camel; humped.|The camelbacked bridge offered a picturesque view of the river.  这座驼背桥提供了如画的河景。|Synonyms: humped, hunched, arched, curved|Antonyms: flat, straight|adjective
