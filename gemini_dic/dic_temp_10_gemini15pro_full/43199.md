crabbish:/ˈkræbɪʃ/| adj.|坏脾气的；暴躁的；令人不愉快的|Unpleasant, irritable, or bad-tempered.|He was in a crabbish mood all day after his meeting was cancelled.  他一整天都心情不好，因为他的会议被取消了。|The crabbish old man yelled at the children playing in the street.  那个脾气暴躁的老人对着在街上玩耍的孩子们大喊大叫。|近义词：grumpy, irritable, cantankerous, bad-tempered, surly|反义词：good-natured, pleasant, amiable, cheerful|词性:形容词
