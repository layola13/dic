bibliotheke:/ˌbɪbliəˈθɛki/ /ˌbɪbliəˈθiːki/ | n. | 图书馆，藏书室 | A library, especially a large or scholarly one.  Derived from the Greek words 'biblion' (book) and 'theke' (case, box, or place for storing). It refers to a place where books and other written materials are stored and made available for reading or borrowing. |  The ancient bibliotheke at Alexandria was a marvel of its time.  亚历山大港的古老图书馆在其时代是一个奇迹。

The bibliotheke holds a vast collection of medieval manuscripts.  这个图书馆收藏了大量的中世纪手稿。 | synonyms: library, archive | antonyms:  None (Lack of a single direct antonym.  One could consider concepts like "emptiness," "ignorance," or "book burning" as opposing ideas, but not direct antonyms.) | noun
