nonradical:/nɒnˈrædɪkəl/ /nɑːnˈrædɪkəl/| adj.|非激进的，温和的；非自由基的|Not extreme or revolutionary; moderate.  Also, in chemistry, not relating to or containing a chemical radical.|例句：He proposed a nonradical solution to the problem. 他提出了一个温和的解决方案。

例句：The reaction involved nonradical intermediates. 该反应涉及非自由基中间体。|近义词：moderate, conservative, gradual, reformist|反义词：radical, revolutionary, extremist|词性:形容词

Etymology->
non- + radical.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Radical" comes from Late Latin "radicalis," meaning "of a root."  In a political sense, it refers to those who advocate for fundamental change. In chemistry, it refers to a group of atoms behaving as a unit.
