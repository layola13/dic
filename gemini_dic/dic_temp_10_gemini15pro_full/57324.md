downtrodden:/ˌdaʊnˈtrɒdn/ (UK) /ˌdaʊnˈtrɑːdn/ (US)| adj.|被践踏的，被压迫的|Oppressed or treated badly by people in power.|The downtrodden masses yearn for freedom. 被压迫的群众渴望自由。|Synonyms: oppressed, subjugated, suppressed, tyrannized|Antonyms: empowered, liberated, free, privileged|adjective

downtrodden:/ˌdaʊnˈtrɒdn/ (UK) /ˌdaʊnˈtrɑːdn/ (US)| n.|被压迫的人，受欺压者|People who are oppressed or treated badly by those in power.|The downtrodden often have no voice in society. 受压迫的人通常在社会上没有发言权。|Synonyms: the oppressed, the disadvantaged, the underprivileged|Antonyms: the privileged, the elite, the ruling class|noun
