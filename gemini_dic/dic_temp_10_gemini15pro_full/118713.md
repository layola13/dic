metadiscoidal:/ˌmɛtədɪˈskɔɪdəl/ | adj. | 盘状后期的，后盘状的 |  Relating to or denoting a later stage of discoidal cleavage in which the blastoderm is a multilayered disc.  It describes a type of egg development where the embryo forms as a disc on top of the yolk. | The zebrafish embryo exhibits metadiscoidal cleavage.  斑马鱼胚胎表现出盘状后卵裂。|  The development of the avian blastoderm is metadiscoidal. 鸟类囊胚层的发育是盘状后期的。 |  discoidal | ameridiscal | adjective
