guernica:/ɡərˈniːkə/ | n. | 格尔尼卡 | A town in northern Spain, the historic capital of the Basques, bombed by German planes on April 26, 1937, during the Spanish Civil War; also, a large oil painting by Pablo Picasso depicting the suffering brought by the bombing. |  I saw Picasso's Guernica at the Reina Sofia Museum in Madrid. 我在马德里的索菲亚王后国家艺术中心博物馆看到了毕加索的《格尔尼卡》。 | synonym: masterpiece, mural | antonym: peace (as a concept contrasting the devastation depicted) | noun
