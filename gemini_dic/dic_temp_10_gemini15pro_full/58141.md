drosophilist: /drəʊˈsɒfɪlɪst/ | n. | 果蝇学家；果蝇研究者 | A biologist who specializes in the study of Drosophila, especially Drosophila melanogaster (the common fruit fly). | Example 1: The drosophilist spent years studying the genetic mutations in fruit flies.  这位果蝇学家花了数年时间研究果蝇的基因突变。

Example 2:  Many drosophilists have made significant contributions to our understanding of genetics. 许多果蝇学家对我们理解遗传学做出了重大贡献。 | 近义词: geneticist, biologist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Drosophila (the genus of fruit flies) + -ist (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something). Drosophila comes from Greek *drósos* "dew" + *phílos* "loving."
