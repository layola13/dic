cannibalize:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/| v. |蚕食，内部竞争，拆用配件|To reduce the sales of one product by introducing a similar, newer product; to take parts from one machine to repair another.|The company's new phone cannibalized sales of its older model. 这家公司的新手机蚕食了其旧款手机的销量。|Synonyms: undermine, erode, eat into|Antonyms: boost, support, enhance|verb

cannibalizes:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪzɪz/| v. |蚕食，内部竞争，拆用配件|Third-person singular present tense form of "cannibalize".|The new product line cannibalizes the sales of the existing one.  这条新的产品线蚕食了现有产品的销售。|Synonyms: undermines, erodes, eats into|Antonyms: boosts, supports, enhances|verb
