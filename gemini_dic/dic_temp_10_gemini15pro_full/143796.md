outnumbers:/ˌaʊtˈnʌmbərz/
verb
在数量上超过
to be more numerous than
例句：The girls outnumber the boys in our class. 我们班女生比男生多。

例句：Their troops outnumber ours by two to one. 他们的军队在数量上以二比一超过我们。
近义词：exceeds, surpasses, outweighs
反义词：is outnumbered by
词性:v.
