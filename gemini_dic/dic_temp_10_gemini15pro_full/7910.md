antenniferous:/ˌæntɪˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
有触角的；长着触角的
Bearing antennae.
例句：The antenniferous beetle scurried across the leaf.  这只长着触角的甲虫匆匆爬过树叶。

例句：Antenniferous insects communicate through their antennae.  有触角的昆虫通过触角交流。

近义词: antennate
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "antenna-less")
词性: adjective
