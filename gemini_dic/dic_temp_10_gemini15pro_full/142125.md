origenist:/ˈɔːrɪdʒənɪst/| n. |奥利金主义者；起源论者|A follower of the theological doctrines of Origen, a 3rd-century Christian theologian.  Origen believed in the pre-existence of souls and universal salvation, doctrines later declared heretical.|Origenist beliefs were widespread in the early church. 奥利金主义信仰在早期教会中很普遍。|近义词:follower, disciple|反义词:opponent, critic|词性:noun
