factionally:/ˈfækʃənəli/
adverb
派系地；小派系地
In a way that relates to factions or involves factionalism.
The party was factionally divided on the issue of tax reform.  该党在税制改革问题上存在派系分歧。
The company was run factionally, with different departments constantly vying for power.  这家公司以派系方式运营，不同部门不断争夺权力。
近义词: divisively, schismatically
反义词: unitedly, harmoniously
词性: adverb
