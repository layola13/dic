hyper-sophisticatedly:/ˌhaɪpər səˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪdli/
adverb
高度复杂地；超级精密地
in a way that is extremely sophisticated; in a manner exhibiting excessive refinement and complexity.
Her apartment was hyper-sophisticatedly decorated with minimalist art and designer furniture. 她的公寓以极简主义艺术和名牌家具装饰得极其精致。
The software is hyper-sophisticatedly designed to handle complex data analysis.  这个软件设计得非常精密，可以处理复杂的数据分析。
近义词: elaborately, intricately, complexly
反义词: simply, crudely, plainly
词性: adverb

词源:
"Hyper-" (prefix): from Greek "hyper" (over, beyond, excessive)
"Sophisticated": from Late Latin "sophisticatus" (past participle of "sophisticare"), meaning "to make artificial, adulterate, tamper with," from Latin "sophisticus" (sophistical), from Greek "sophistikos" (clever, skillful), from "sophistēs" (sophist, wise man).
"-ly" (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
