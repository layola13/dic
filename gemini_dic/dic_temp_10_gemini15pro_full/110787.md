liniment: /ˈlɪnɪmənt/ | n. | 药油；涂剂 | A liquid or semiliquid preparation for rubbing on the skin, as for sprains or bruises. |  She rubbed liniment on her sore muscles. 她将药油涂抹在酸痛的肌肉上。 |  The vet prescribed a liniment for the horse's leg. 兽医给马腿开了药油。 | 近义词: balm, ointment, salve | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *linimentum, from Latin linere "to smear," from PIE root *leik- "to leave a trace."
