cream-puff:/ˈkriːm pʌf/ | n. |奶油泡芙；懦夫，胆小鬼 | A light pastry shell filled with whipped cream or custard.  A person who is easily intimidated or who avoids difficult situations. |  例句：I bought a box of cream puffs from the bakery. 我从面包店买了一盒奶油泡芙。

例句：He's such a cream puff, he's afraid of everything. 他真是个胆小鬼，什么都怕。| 近义词：(for pastry) éclair, profiterole; (for person) coward, wimp | 反义词：(for person) hero, brave person | 词性: noun
