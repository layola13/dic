fluey:/ˈfluːi/ | adj. |  患流感的；像流感的 | Having or showing symptoms of influenza. |  I'm feeling a bit fluey today. 我今天感觉有点像得了流感。|  近义词：ill, sick, unwell | 反义词：well, healthy | 词性: 形容词

fluey:/ˈfluːi/ | adj. |  患流感的；像流感的 | Resembling or characteristic of influenza. | She had a fluey cough and a sore throat. 她咳嗽得像得了流感，嗓子也疼。|  近义词：ailing, poorly, indisposed | 反义词：fit, strong | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From *flu* (influenza) + *-y* (suffix forming adjectives).  The word "flu" is a shortened form of "influenza," which ultimately comes from Italian *influenza*, meaning "influence,"  referring to the supposed astrological influence on health.
