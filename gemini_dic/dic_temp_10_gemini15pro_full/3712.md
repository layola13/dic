aggressively:/əˈɡrɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 侵略地，攻击地，有进取心地 |  In a manner that is forceful, assertive, and sometimes hostile.  |  He aggressively pursued the business deal.  他积极地争取这笔生意。 | forcefully, assertively, vigorously, offensively | passively, defensively, meekly | adverb

aggressively:/əˈɡrɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 积极地，进取地 | In a determined and energetic manner, often to achieve a goal. | She marketed her new product aggressively. 她积极地推销她的新产品。| proactively, ambitiously, dynamically | passively, lazily, indifferently | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From aggressive + -ly. Aggressive ultimately derives from Latin *aggredi, present active infinitive of aggredī ("to attack"), from ad- ("to, towards") + gradi ("to step, walk").
