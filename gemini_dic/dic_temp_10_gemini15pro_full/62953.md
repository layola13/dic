enneaspermous:/ˌɛniˈəspɜːrməs/
adjective
九籽的；有九颗种子的
Having nine seeds.
The enneaspermous fruit is a unique characteristic of this plant species.  这种植物的九籽果实是其独特的特征。
The botanist identified the plant as enneaspermous due to the presence of nine seeds in its fruit.  植物学家根据其果实中有九颗种子而将其鉴定为九籽植物。
Near Synonyms: nine-seeded
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use terms like "few-seeded," "oligospermous" (having few seeds), but these are not exact opposites)
Etymology:  From Greek "ennea-" (nine) + "sperma" (seed)
