fish-or-cut-bait:/ˈfɪʃ ɔːr kʌt ˈbeɪt/| idiom|要么钓鱼，要么切鱼饵；要么行动，要么放弃|This idiom means to either take action or give up, to stop delaying and make a decision.|The boss told us to fish or cut bait: either start selling more product or leave the company. 老板告诉我们要么行动，要么放弃：要么开始销售更多产品，要么离开公司。|近义词：make up one's mind, take action, decide, get on with it|反义词：procrastinate, delay, hesitate|词性 idiom
