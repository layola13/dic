affluence:/ˈæfluəns/| n.|富裕，丰富|The state of having a great deal of money or property; wealth.  A great quantity of something.|They live in an area of great affluence. 他们生活在一个非常富裕的地区。|近义词：wealth, prosperity, opulence, richness|反义词：poverty, indigence, destitution|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *affluentia*, from affluentem (nominative affluens) "abounding, copious, affluent," present participle of affluere "to flow to, abound," from ad- "to" + fluere "to flow."
