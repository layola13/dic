angel:/ˈeɪndʒəl/ (US), /ˈeɪndʒl/ (UK)| n.|天使;守护神;善良的人|A spiritual being believed to act as an attendant, agent, or messenger of God, conventionally represented in human form with wings and a halo.|She looked like an angel in her white dress. 她穿着白色的连衣裙，看起来像个天使。|Synonyms: seraph, cherub, guardian angel, divine messenger|Antonyms: devil, demon, fiend|Noun
