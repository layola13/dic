mephistophelian:/ˌmefɪstəˈfiːliən/
adjective
梅菲斯特式的;恶魔般的;邪恶的
Diabolically evil or cruel; suggestive of or resembling Mephistopheles.  Mephistopheles is a demon featured in German folklore. He is often portrayed as a cunning and seductive tempter who offers worldly pleasures in exchange for one's soul.
He had a mephistophelian smile that made her shudder. 他脸上带着梅菲斯特式的笑容，让她不寒而栗。
Synonyms: diabolic, demonic, satanic, evil, wicked, fiendish, devilish, infernal
Antonyms: angelic, benevolent, virtuous, saintly
词性: adjective

mephistophelian:/ˌmefɪstəˈfiːliən/
noun
梅菲斯特式的人;恶魔般的人
A person who is diabolically evil or cruel.
He was a true mephistophelian, delighting in the suffering of others. 他是一个真正的恶魔，以他人的痛苦为乐。
Synonyms: devil, fiend, villain, miscreant
Antonyms: angel, saint
词性: noun
