butcheries:/ˈbʊtʃəriːz/
n.
屠宰场；肉店；大屠杀
places where animals are slaughtered for meat; shops that sell meat; acts of mass killing, especially of people
The town had two butcheries, both family-run.  这个镇上有两家肉店，都是家族经营的。
The war resulted in countless butcheries. 这场战争导致了无数的大屠杀。
synonyms: slaughterhouses, abattoirs, massacres, carnage, bloodbaths
antonyms: sanctuaries, havens
noun

butchery:/ˈbʊtʃəri/
n.
屠宰；肉店；大屠杀
the slaughtering of animals for food; a shop selling meat; an act of cruel and indiscriminate killing
The butchery took place early in the morning. 屠宰是在清晨进行的。
He learned the butchery trade from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了屠宰业。
The rebels were responsible for the brutal butchery of innocent civilians. 叛军应对无辜平民的野蛮屠杀负责。
synonyms: slaughter, carnage, massacre
antonyms: preservation, conservation
noun
