overcontributing:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ/
| adj. |过度贡献的，贡献过多的|Contributing too much, often to the detriment of oneself.|She was overcontributing to the project, working late nights and weekends, and neglecting her own well-being. 她对这个项目过度投入，经常加班到深夜和周末，忽略了自己的健康。|He felt he was overcontributing to the household chores and resented his roommates. 他觉得自己承担了过多的家务，对室友感到不满。|近义词：overworking, overexerting, overdoing|反义词: undercontributing, neglecting|词性:形容词

overcontribute:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtrɪbjuːt/
| v. |过度贡献，贡献过多|To contribute more than is necessary or healthy, often to the detriment of oneself.|She overcontributed to the charity, donating more than she could afford. 她为慈善机构捐款过多，超过了她所能承受的范围。|He overcontributed his time and energy to the project, leaving him exhausted. 他在这个项目上投入了过多的时间和精力，导致精疲力竭。|近义词：overwork, overexert, overdo|反义词: undercontribute, neglect|词性:动词
