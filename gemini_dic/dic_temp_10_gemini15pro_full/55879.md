divisionalization:/dɪˌvɪʒənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |部门化，分权化|The act or process of dividing a company or organization into separate divisions, each responsible for a particular product, market, or geographic area.  It's a form of decentralization.| The company underwent divisionalization to improve efficiency.  这家公司进行了部门化改革以提高效率。| Synonyms: decentralization, departmentalization, segmentation | Antonyms: centralization, consolidation | noun

divisionalize:/dɪˌvɪʒənəlaɪz/ | v. |部门化，分权化|To divide a company or organization into separate divisions. |They divisionalized the company into three separate business units. 他们将公司分成三个独立的业务部门。| Synonyms: decentralize, departmentalize, segment | Antonyms: centralize, consolidate | verb
