dichotomous:/daɪˈkɑːtəməs/
adj.
二分的，二歧的
divided or dividing into two sharply distinguished parts or classifications.
The survey offered a dichotomous choice: agree or disagree.  这项调查提供了一个二元选择：同意或不同意。
The philosopher presented a dichotomous view of good and evil. 这位哲学家提出了善恶二元论的观点。
近义词: binary, dual, twofold
反义词: multiple, diverse, varied
词性: adjective
