hellful:/ˈhɛlfl̩/
adj.
充满地狱的; 极糟糕的; 非常困难的
Of or relating to hell; extremely unpleasant or difficult.
例句：The traffic was hellful this morning. 今早交通状况糟透了。

例句：It was a hellful experience trying to get the visa.  办签证的经历真是太难了。

近义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, nightmarish, infernal
反义词：heavenly, wonderful, delightful, pleasant
