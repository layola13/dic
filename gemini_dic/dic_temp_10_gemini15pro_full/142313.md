orthodoxality:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksəlɪti/
n.|正统性，正统观念|The state or quality of being orthodox; adherence to established doctrines, especially in religion.|His orthodoxality made him resistant to new ideas. 他的正统观念使他抵制新思想。|Synonyms: orthodoxy, conventionality, traditionalism|Antonyms: heterodoxy, unconventionality, unorthodoxy|noun
