misunderstandable:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændəbl/| adj.|可被误解的，容易引起误会的|Likely to be misunderstood; not clearly expressed.|Her handwriting is so messy, it's completely misunderstandable. 她的字迹太潦草了，完全让人无法理解。|近义词：confusing, ambiguous, unclear|反义词：clear, unambiguous, easy to understand|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (expressing wrongness or lack) + understandable.  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss". "Understandable" comes from "understand" which originates from Old English "understandan"  (to comprehend).
