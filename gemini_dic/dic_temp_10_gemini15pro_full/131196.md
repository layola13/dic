nonburdensomely:/ˌnɒnˈbɜːrdənsəmli/
adverb
轻松地，不费力地
in a way that is not burdensome; easily.
The process was completed nonburdensomely.  这个过程轻松地完成了。
The new software updates nonburdensomely in the background. 新软件在后台轻松更新。
Synonyms: easily, effortlessly, lightly, simply
Antonyms: arduously, laboriously, strenuously, difficultly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  not, absence of. From Latin *non*
burdensome (adjective): difficult to carry or deal with. From *burden* (n.) + *-some* (suffix)
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives.
