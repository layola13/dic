dipolar:/daɪˈpoʊlər/ | adj. |  偶极的；双极的 | Having two poles or extremities with opposite characteristics, such as electric charge or magnetic force. | Water is a dipolar molecule because it has a positive end and a negative end.  水是偶极分子，因为它有一正一负两端。 | synonyms: polar, bipolar | antonyms: nonpolar, apolar | adjective

dipole:/ˈdaɪpoʊl/ | n. | 偶极子；双极子 | A pair of equal and oppositely charged or magnetized poles separated by a distance. | The antenna acts as a dipole, radiating electromagnetic waves. 天线充当偶极子，辐射电磁波。| synonyms: dipole antenna, doublet | antonyms: N/A | noun
