ligula:/ˈlɪɡjʊlə/| n.|舌状物，小舌，叶舌|A strap-shaped structure, particularly a small appendage at the junction of the leaf blade and sheath in grasses, or a similar structure in other plants, or a strap-shaped extension of the corolla of a ray floret in the Compositae (daisy family).|The botanist examined the ligula of the grass to identify the species.  植物学家检查了草的叶舌以确定其种类。

The ligula of the daisy was a vibrant yellow.  雏菊的舌状花瓣是鲜艳的黄色。|Synonyms: strap, appendage, tongue|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
