Göbekli Tepe: /ɡøbeˈkli teˈpe/ | n. | 哥贝克力石阵 |  A Neolithic archaeological site in southeastern Turkey, considered the world's oldest known megalithic structure. It predates pottery, metallurgy, and the invention of writing or the wheel.  |  Example: Göbekli Tepe has revolutionized our understanding of the Neolithic period. 哥贝克力石阵彻底改变了我们对新石器时代的理解。 | Example:  Visiting Göbekli Tepe is a truly awe-inspiring experience. 参观哥贝克力石阵是一次真正令人敬畏的经历。| Synonym:  (No direct synonym, but related terms could include) prehistoric site, megalithic temple | Antonym:  (No antonym) | Noun


Etymology:
Turkish:  *göbekli* "potbellied," from *göbek* "belly, navel" + *-li* (adjective-forming suffix)
*Tepe* "hill"
