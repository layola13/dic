crystallogeny:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɒdʒəni/ | n. | 晶体发生，结晶学 | The study of the origin and formation of crystals, including their growth, external shape, and internal structure. | The crystallogeny of this mineral is complex and not yet fully understood. 这种矿物的晶体发生过程很复杂，尚未完全了解。| 近义词: crystallography | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos, “ice, crystal”) + -geny (from Ancient Greek -γενής (-genḗs) or -γενεία (-geneía), both meaning “birth, production, origin”).
