fourierist:/ˈfʊrieɪrɪst/ | n. | 傅立叶主义者；空想社会主义者 | A follower of the French socialist theorist Charles Fourier (1772-1837), who advocated a cooperative social order in which people lived and worked in communities called phalansteries. |  He was a committed Fourierist, believing in the potential of phalansteries to create a more just and equitable society. 他是一位坚定的傅立叶主义者，相信和谐社会能够创造一个更加公正公平的社会。 | synonyms: utopian socialist,  communitarian | antonyms: capitalist, individualist | noun
