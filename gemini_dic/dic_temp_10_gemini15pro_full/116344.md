masterstroke:/ˈmɑːstərstroʊk/| n. |妙计；高招；神来之笔|A masterstroke is a very skillful and successful action or plan, especially one that is a clever way of achieving an aim.|例句：His resignation was a masterstroke. 他的辞职是一着妙棋。

例句：Signing that young player was a real masterstroke by the manager.  签下那位年轻球员是经理的真正高招。|近义词：coup, triumph, stroke of genius, brilliant move|反义词:blunder, mistake, error|词性:名词
