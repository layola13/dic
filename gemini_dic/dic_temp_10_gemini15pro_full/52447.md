died: /daɪd/
verb
死亡，逝世
past tense of "die" - ceased to live.
My grandfather died last year. 我的祖父去年去世了。
The flowers died because I forgot to water them.  这些花枯萎了，因为我忘记浇水了。
近义词: perished, expired, deceased
反义词: lived, survived
词性: verb
