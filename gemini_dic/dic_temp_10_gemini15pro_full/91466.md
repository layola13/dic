hylobate:/ˈhaɪləˌbeɪt/ | n. | 猿；长臂猿 | An ape of the family Hylobatidae, characterized by long arms and arboreal habits.  It includes gibbons and siamangs.|  例句：The hylobate swung effortlessly through the trees. 长臂猿轻松地在树间荡来荡去。 | 近义词：gibbon, ape | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially large terrestrial mammals) | 词性:noun

hylobate:/ˈhaɪləˌbeɪt/ | adj. |  长臂猿的；类长臂猿的 | Relating to or resembling a hylobate. | 例句：The hylobate skeleton is adapted for brachiation. 长臂猿的骨骼适合臂行。 | 近义词：gibbon-like | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性:adjective
