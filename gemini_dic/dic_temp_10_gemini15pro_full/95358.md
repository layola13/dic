incerative: /ɪnˈsɛrətɪv/ | adj. | 不确定的，犹豫的，不果断的 | uncertain or hesitant; not resolute |  He seemed incerative about accepting the job offer. 他似乎对接受这份工作犹豫不决。|  Example: Her incerative steps showed her lack of confidence. 她犹豫的步伐显示出她缺乏自信。 | Synonyms: hesitant, tentative, unsure, wavering, vacillating | Antonyms: decisive, resolute, confident, certain, firm | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
