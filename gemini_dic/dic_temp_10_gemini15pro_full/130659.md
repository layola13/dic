nonadventitiously:/ˌnɒnˌædvənˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
非偶然地；非外来地；固有地
Not adventitiously; in a way that is not accidental or introduced from outside; inherently.
The plant is nonadventitiously present in the region, meaning it's native and not introduced.  这种植物非外来地存在于该地区，这意味着它是本地植物，而不是引进的。
The trait developed nonadventitiously, arising naturally within the species.  这种特性是非偶然地产生的，是在物种内部自然产生的。
Synonym: inherently, naturally, intrinsically
Antonym: adventitiously, accidentally, externally
词性: adverb
