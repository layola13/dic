macrocephalous: /ˌmækroʊˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 巨头畸形的，大头的 | Having an abnormally large head. |  例句：The child was diagnosed as macrocephalous.  这个孩子被诊断为巨头畸形。

例句：Macrocephalous individuals may experience various developmental challenges. 巨头畸形的人可能会经历各种发育挑战。


| 近义词：large-headed | 反义词: microcephalous | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek  *makros* "large" + *kephalē* "head"
