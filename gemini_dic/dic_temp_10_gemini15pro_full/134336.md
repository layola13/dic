nonintegers:/ˌnɒnˈɪntɪdʒəz/ | n. | 非整数 | Numbers that are not integers, including fractions, decimals, and irrational numbers. |  Example: Pi (π) is a noninteger. π是一个非整数。

Example: 1/2 is a noninteger. 1/2是一个非整数。 | 近义词: non-whole numbers, fractions, decimals | 反义词: integers, whole numbers | 词性: noun
