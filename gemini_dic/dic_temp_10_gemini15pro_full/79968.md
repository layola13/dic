go-bad:/ɡoʊ bæd/ | v. | 变坏，腐败，变质 | To deteriorate in quality, especially of food or drink; to become corrupt or morally reprehensible. | The milk went bad after being left out in the sun. 牛奶在太阳下放了一天后变质了。| synonyms: spoil, decay, deteriorate, rot, fester, decline, worsen | antonyms: improve, enhance, ameliorate, recover, flourish, thrive | verb

go-bad:/ɡoʊ bæd/ | adj. |  (informal)  in a bad state; rotten or decayed. |  The meat had gone bad and smelled awful.  肉已经坏了，闻起来很糟糕。| synonyms: rotten, spoiled, decayed, putrid, rancid | antonyms: fresh, good, wholesome | adjective
