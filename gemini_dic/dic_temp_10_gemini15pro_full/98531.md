instigate: /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 教唆，煽动，激起 | to cause an event or situation to happen by making a set of actions or a formal process begin | The government instigated a program of economic reform. 政府启动了一项经济改革计划。|  The protests were instigated by a small group of activists. 抗议是由一小撮激进分子煽动的。 | synonyms: incite, provoke, goad, abet, foment | antonyms: deter, discourage, prevent, suppress | verb
