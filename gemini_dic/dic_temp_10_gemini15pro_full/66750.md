exorhiza:/ˌɛksoʊˈraɪzə/ | n. | 外生根 |A root that grows from a stem above ground level, as in some mangroves and palms. |例句：The mangrove's exorhiza provided stability in the soft mud.  红树林的外生根在软泥中提供了稳定性。

例句：The exorhiza of the palm tree helped anchor it to the ground. 棕榈树的外生根帮助它固定在地面上。|近义词：aerial root, prop root|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subterranean root) |词性: noun
