co-respondent:/ˌkəʊrɪˈspɒndənt/ (US: /ˌkoʊrɪˈspɑːndənt/) | n. | 共同被告；婚外情对象 |  In divorce proceedings, a person cited as having committed adultery with the respondent (the person against whom the divorce petition is filed).  | The co-respondent in the divorce case denied all allegations of an affair.  离婚案中的共同被告否认了所有关于婚外情的指控。| synonyms: paramour, lover, mistress, other woman/man | antonyms: spouse, partner | noun

co-respondent:/ˌkəʊrɪˈspɒndənt/ (US: /ˌkoʊrɪˈspɑːndənt/) | n. | 通信者；共同通讯者 | A person who exchanges letters with another. |  She maintained a regular correspondence with her co-respondent overseas. 她与海外的通信者保持着定期通信。 | synonyms: correspondent, pen pal, communicator | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider non-communicative person) | noun
