harmonise:/ˈhɑːmənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ (US)| v. |使和谐；协调；相配|To bring into or cause to be in harmony; to make things consistent or compatible.|The designer harmonised the colours in the room. 设计师协调了房间里的颜色。|近义词：coordinate, blend, match, agree, conform|反义词：clash, disagree, conflict|词性:动词
harmonise:/ˈhɑːmənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ (US)| n. |协调；和谐|The combination of elements to create a pleasing effect.|The harmonise of the music was beautiful. 音乐的和谐很美妙。|近义词：harmony, concord, agreement|反义词：discord, conflict, dissonance|词性:名词
