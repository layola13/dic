grooms:/ɡruːmz/| n. v.|新郎；马夫；(动物) 美容师|A man who is about to get married or has just got married. / Someone who takes care of horses or other animals. / To clean and brush an animal.|例句：The groom looked handsome in his tuxedo. 新郎穿著燕尾服看起来很帅。

The groom carefully brushed his horse's coat. 马夫仔细地刷着马的皮毛。|近义词：(n.) bridegroom, stableman, (v.) brush, clean, tend|反义词: bride (n.)|词性: noun, verb

grooms:/ɡruːmz/| v.|打扮；准备；培养；使适合|To prepare someone or something for a particular purpose or event.|例句：She is being groomed for a leadership position. 她正在被培养成为领导。

He groomed his son to take over the family business. 他培养他的儿子接管家族企业。|近义词：prepare, train, prime|反义词: neglect|词性: verb
