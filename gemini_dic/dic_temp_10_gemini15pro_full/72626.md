flying-out:/ˈflaɪɪŋ aʊt/| n.|外出飞行；出差；飞离|The act of flying away from a location, often for a temporary period, especially for business or a specific event.  Often refers to sending something or someone by air.|例句：His flying-out to the conference was delayed due to bad weather. 他因恶劣天气推迟了飞往会议地点。

例句：The company arranged a flying-out for their top executives to attend the international summit. 公司安排其高管飞往参加国际峰会。|近义词：departure, flight, trip, business trip|反义词：flying-in, arrival, return|词性:名词
