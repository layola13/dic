ethanes:/ˈɛθeɪnz/| n. |乙烷|A colorless, odorless, gaseous alkane, C2H6, that occurs as a constituent of natural gas and petroleum and is used as a fuel and refrigerant.|Ethane is the second simplest alkane after methane. 乙烷是仅次于甲烷的第二简单的烷烃。|Ethane is used primarily as a feedstock for ethylene production. 乙烷主要用作乙烯生产的原料。|propane, butane|methane|noun
