iion: /ˈaɪɒn/ | n. | 离子 | An atom or molecule with a net electric charge due to the loss or gain of one or more electrons. |  A sodium ion (Na+) has a positive charge. 钠离子带正电荷。

The chloride ion (Cl-) has a negative charge. 氯离子带负电荷。| 近义词: charged particle | 反义词: neutral atom | 词性: noun
