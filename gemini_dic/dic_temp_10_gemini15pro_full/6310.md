amortize: /ˈæmərtaɪz/ (US), /əˈmɔːtaɪz/ (UK) | v. | 摊销，分期偿还 | To gradually reduce or write off the cost of an asset over a period of time. | The company is amortizing the cost of new equipment over five years.  这家公司正在将新设备的成本摊销五年。|  Example:  We are amortizing the loan over 15 years. 我们正在15年内分期偿还贷款。 | Synonyms: depreciate, write off, expensed | Antonyms: appreciate, increase | verb
