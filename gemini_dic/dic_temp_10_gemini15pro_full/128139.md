netiquette:/ˈnetɪkɛt/ /ˈnetɪket/| n.|网络礼仪|The set of rules and guidelines for polite behavior online.|Please observe netiquette and avoid using all caps in your emails. 请遵守网络礼仪，避免在电子邮件中全部使用大写。|近义词: online etiquette, cyber etiquette|反义词: bad manners, rudeness|词性: noun
Etymology:  A blend of "net" (from "internet" or "network") and "etiquette".
