incognizant:/ɪnˈkɒɡnɪzənt/ /ɪnˈkɑːɡnɪzənt/| adj.|未察觉的，不知道的，未意识到的|Not aware; lacking knowledge or consciousness of something.|He was completely incognizant of the danger he was in. 他完全没有意识到自己身处险境。|Example:  She was incognizant of his presence. 她没有注意到他在场。|Synonyms: unaware, oblivious, ignorant, insensible|Antonyms: aware, conscious, mindful|Word type: Adjective

incognizance:/ɪnˈkɒɡnɪzəns/ /ɪnˈkɑːɡnɪzəns/| n.|无知，不知情|Lack of awareness or consciousness.|Her incognizance of the situation was surprising. 她对情况的无知令人惊讶。|Example:  He acted in incognizance of the facts. 他在不了解事实的情况下采取了行动。|Synonyms: unawareness, obliviousness, ignorance|Antonyms: awareness, consciousness, mindfulness|Word type: Noun
