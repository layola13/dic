forsake:/fərˈseɪk/ | vt. |放弃，抛弃，摒弃|To abandon or leave someone or something, especially when they need you.|He forsook his family and went to live in another country. 他抛弃了家人，去了另一个国家生活。|She forsook her career to raise her children. 她放弃了事业来抚养孩子。|近义词：abandon, desert, renounce, relinquish|反义词：cherish, embrace, keep, maintain|词性:动词

词源:
Old English *forsacan "oppose, refuse, renounce, deny, resist," from for- "away, against" + sacan "strive, contend, dispute," related to Old High German sahhon "to accuse, dispute." Meaning "to abandon, leave" emerged by late 12c.
