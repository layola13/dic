ditheism:/ˈdaɪθiːɪzəm/
n.
二神论，二神教
The belief in two gods, especially two equal and opposing gods.
Ditheism posits two equally powerful gods, one good and one evil.  二神论假设存在两个同样强大的神，一个善一个恶。
Ancient Zoroastrianism is sometimes considered a form of ditheism.  古代琐罗亚斯德教有时被认为是二神论的一种形式。
近义词:dualism
反义词:monotheism, polytheism
词性:noun
