nonaccruals:/ˌnɒnəˈkruːəlz/
noun
未累计收益，未 accrual 债务
Loans that are not accruing interest are called nonaccruals.  未累计利息的贷款被称为未累计收益。
Nonaccrual loans represent a significant risk for banks. 未累计收益贷款对银行构成重大风险。
Synonyms: nonperforming loans, bad debts, delinquent loans
Antonyms: performing loans, accruals
词性: 名词
