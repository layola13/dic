eyepatches:/ˈaɪˌpætʃɪz/
n.
眼罩
A small patch worn over one or both eyes, typically for medical or cosmetic reasons.
The pirate wore an eyepatch.  海盗戴着眼罩。

eyepatches:/ˈaɪˌpætʃɪz/
n.
眼膜
A pad saturated with a solution applied to the skin under the eyes, often used for cosmetic treatments.
She uses eyepatches to reduce puffiness. 她使用眼膜来减少浮肿。

近义词:eye mask, eye shield, blindfold
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly uncovered eyes)
词性:noun
