outthrown:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊn/
adjective
投掷出去的；抛出的；超越的
Thrown further or with greater force than something else.  Surpassing or exceeding in throwing.
The quarterback's pass was outthrown by the wind, landing harmlessly out of bounds. 四分卫的传球被风吹得更远，落在了界外。
The javelin thrower outthrew his personal best by a significant margin. 标枪运动员的投掷成绩大大超过了他个人的最好成绩。
近义词: outdistanced, surpassed, exceeded
反义词: short, underthrown
词性: adjective
