encoding:/ɪnˈkoʊdɪŋ/ /ɛnˈkəʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 编码，编码方式 | The process of converting information from one form to another, such as from text to binary code. | Example: The encoding of the message was complex and difficult to decipher.  这条信息的编码很复杂，难以破译。| Example:  UTF-8 is a common character encoding used on the web. UTF-8是一种常用的网络字符编码。 | Synonyms: coding, ciphering | Antonyms: decoding, deciphering | Noun
