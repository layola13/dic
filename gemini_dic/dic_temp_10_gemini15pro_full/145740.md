overcrowd:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊd/ /ˌəʊvəˈkraʊd/| vt. |使过度拥挤；挤满|To fill a space with too many people or things.|The train was overcrowded with commuters. 火车里挤满了通勤者。|近义词：overfill, pack, cram|反义词：depopulate, empty|verb

overcrowded:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪd/ /ˌəʊvəˈkraʊdɪd/| adj. |过度拥挤的；挤满的|Filled with too many people or things.|The overcrowded bus was uncomfortable.  过度拥挤的公交车很不舒服。|近义词：overfilled, packed, crammed|反义词：empty, spacious|adjective


词源:

From over- + crowd.  由over-（过度）+ crowd（人群）组成.
