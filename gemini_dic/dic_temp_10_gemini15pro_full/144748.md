over-browned: /ˌoʊvərˈbraʊnd/
adjective
过度烤焦的，烤糊的
cooked for too long, resulting in a darker color than desired, often with a burnt taste.
The toast was over-browned and tasted slightly burnt. 吐司烤焦了，尝起来有点糊味。
The cookies were a little over-browned, but still edible. 饼干有点烤焦了，但还可以吃。
Synonyms: burnt, scorched, overcooked
Antonyms: undercooked, underdone, raw
word type: adjective
