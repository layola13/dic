nondeclarative:/ˌnɒndɪˈklærətɪv/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈklærətɪv/| adj.|非陈述性的，非声明性的|Not relating to or expressed by a declaration; not declarative. It often refers to a type of memory that is not consciously recalled, such as skills, habits, and conditioned responses.|Muscle memory is a form of nondeclarative memory.  肌肉记忆是一种非陈述性记忆。|Synonyms: implicit, procedural, non-explicit|Antonyms: declarative, explicit|adjective
