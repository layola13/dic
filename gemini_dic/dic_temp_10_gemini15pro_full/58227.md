drugless:/ˈdrʌɡləs/| adj.|无药的；不使用药物的；不用麻醉剂的|Without drugs; not using or involving drugs.  Relating to or advocating treatment by methods other than the use of drugs.|例句：She opted for a drugless approach to managing her pain. 她选择了一种不用药物的方法来控制疼痛。|例句：He believes in drugless healing. 他相信不用药物的治疗方法。|近义词：medication-free, pharmaceutical-free, natural|反义词：medicated, pharmaceutical|词性:形容词
