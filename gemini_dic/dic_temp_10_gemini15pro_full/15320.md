backshish:/ˈbækʃiːʃ/ /ˈbɑːkʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，回扣，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in expectation of or in return for a service or favour.  Often implies a slightly dubious or underhand transaction.|Example: He offered the official a small backshish to speed up the process. 他向官员提供了一小笔回扣，以加快办理进程。

Example:  She slipped the waiter a generous backshish. 她偷偷塞给服务员一笔丰厚的小费。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback|反义词:|词性:noun
