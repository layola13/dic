menthol:/ˈmɛnθɒl/, /ˈmɛnθɔːl/ | n. |薄荷醇，薄荷脑| A crystalline alcohol with a peppermint taste and odor, found in peppermint oil and other natural oils. It is used in flavorings, perfumes, and medicines.|  Menthol is often used in cough drops. 薄荷醇经常用于止咳糖。| synonyms: peppermint camphor, mint camphor | antonyms: None | noun
