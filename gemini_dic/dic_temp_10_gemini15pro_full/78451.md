geth:/ɡɛθ/| n.| 获取，检索 (在以太坊语境中)|A command-line interface for running a full Ethereum node implemented in Go.  It is the most common way to interact with the Ethereum blockchain.|Example: Use `geth` to connect to the Ethereum network. 使用 `geth` 连接到以太坊网络。|近义词:retrieve, fetch, obtain (in the context of data)|反义词:send, put, store (in the context of data)|名词
