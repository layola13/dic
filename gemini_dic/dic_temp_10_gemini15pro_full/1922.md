actinogram: /ækˈtɪnəɡræm/
noun
放线菌图；光化记录
A graphic record of sunlight, especially ultraviolet radiation.  It is produced by the action of sunlight on sensitized paper.
The actinogram showed a high level of UV radiation that day. 那天的光化记录显示紫外线辐射水平很高。

The researchers used an actinogram to measure the intensity of sunlight. 研究人员使用光化记录来测量阳光的强度。

近义词:  heliogram
反义词:  None
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From actino- (relating to rays or radiation) + -gram (something written or recorded).  "Actino-" comes from the Greek "aktīs" (ray). "-gram" comes from the Greek "gramma" (something written).
