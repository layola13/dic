outechoing:/ˌaʊtˈekəʊɪŋ/
verb
胜过回响，盖过回声; 比...回声大
surpassing or exceeding in echoing; louder than an echo
The sound of the waterfall outechoing the birdsong. 瀑布的声音盖过了鸟鸣。
近义词:out-sounding, drowning out
反义词:muffled, subdued
词性:v.


词源:
"outechoing" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) and the verb "echoing" (meaning to produce an echo).  Therefore, it literally means to echo louder than something else, or to surpass in echoing.
