elms:/ɛlmz/| n.|榆树|A tall deciduous tree that typically has rough serrated leaves and propagates from suckers.  The wood is tough and hard-wearing.|例句：The elms lined the avenue, creating a shady canopy. 榆树排列在林荫道上，形成了一个阴凉的树冠。

例句：The old elms in the park were damaged by the storm. 公园里古老的榆树被暴风雨损坏了。|近义词：tree, hardwood|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific tree type) |词性:noun
