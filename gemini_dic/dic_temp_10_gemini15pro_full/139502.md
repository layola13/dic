obstructive:/əbˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
阻碍的；阻塞的
Tending to obstruct or block; hindering.
His obstructive tactics were hindering the progress of the project. 他的阻挠策略阻碍了项目的进展。

The obstructive behaviour of the opposition party delayed the passing of the bill. 反对党的阻挠行为拖延了法案的通过。

近义词: hindering, blocking, impeding, hampering
反义词: helpful, facilitating, aiding, assisting
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstructus*, past participle of obstruere (“to build in front of, block up, obstruct”), from ob (“against, in front of”) + struere (“to pile, build”).
