metaphrasis:/ˌmɛtəˈfreɪsɪs/ | n. |逐字翻译，直译| A translation that renders the text literally, word for word. |  The metaphrasis of the ancient poem lost some of its original poetic beauty.  这首古诗的直译失去了一些原有的诗意。| Synonyms: literal translation, word-for-word translation | Antonyms: paraphrase, free translation | noun
