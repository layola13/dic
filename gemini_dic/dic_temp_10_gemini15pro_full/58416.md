dubstep:/ˈdʌbstɛp/| n. |电子舞曲的一种|A genre of electronic dance music characterized by syncopated rhythms, heavy bass and sub-bass lines.|例句：I went to a dubstep concert last night. 昨晚我去听了一场电子舞曲音乐会。

例句：Dubstep originated in South London. 电子舞曲起源于伦敦南部。|近义词：electronic dance music, EDM, grime, bass music|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider genres like classical music or pop) |词性:noun
