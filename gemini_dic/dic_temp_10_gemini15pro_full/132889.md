nonelectivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
非选择性；非自愿性
The state or quality of being nonelective.  This refers to situations, positions, or procedures that are not chosen or voted for but are assigned or mandatory.
The nonelectivenesses of the committee assignments caused some resentment among the members. 委员会的任命并非选举产生，这引起了一些成员的不满。
Near Synonyms:  inevitability, necessity, compulsion, obligation, requirement
Opposite:  choice, optionality, voluntariness
