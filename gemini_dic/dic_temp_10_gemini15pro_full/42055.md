cot:/kɑːt/ /kɒt/| n.|婴儿床，小屋，帆布床|A small bed with high sides for a baby or young child. A small, simple shelter. A narrow bed, especially on a ship, train, or aircraft.|例句：The baby slept soundly in her cot. 婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。

例句：He spent the summer in a fishing cot on the lake. 他在湖边的一个钓鱼小屋里度过了夏天。|近义词：crib, cradle, cabin, hut|反义词: (无明确反义词)
词性:名词

cot:/kɑːt/ /kɒt/|n.|余切 (数学)|In mathematics, the cotangent of an angle is the ratio of the length of the adjacent side to the length of the opposite side.|例句：The cotangent of 45 degrees is 1. 45度的余切是1.

例句：The cotangent function is the reciprocal of the tangent function.  余切函数是正切函数的倒数。|近义词：(无，这是一个数学函数)|反义词: tangent (在数学关系上)|
词性:名词
