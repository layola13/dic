mastabah:/ˈmɑːstəbə/ | n. | 埃及平顶金字塔, 梯形坟墓 | A flat-roofed, rectangular Egyptian tomb with sloping sides. | Example 1: The mastaba is an ancient Egyptian tomb form, preceding the pyramids.  埃及平顶金字塔是一种古埃及墓葬形式，早于金字塔。

Example 2:  Archaeologists unearthed a well-preserved mastaba containing valuable artifacts. 考古学家发掘了一座保存完好的平顶金字塔，里面有珍贵的文物。 | Near synonyms: tomb, sepulcher, burial chamber | Antonyms:  N/A (No direct opposite for a type of tomb) | Noun
