facia:/ˈfeɪʃə/ (US), /ˈfeɪʃɪə/ (UK)| n.| 面板，饰带，筋膜|A surface or plate, often decorative, forming the front of something; a board or band used to cover the ends of rafters or other building components; a thin layer of connective tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.|The building's facia was adorned with intricate carvings.  这栋建筑的饰带装饰着复杂的雕刻。|synonyms: facade, front, covering, cladding|antonyms: back, rear|noun
