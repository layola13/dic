binominal:/baɪˈnoʊmiəl/| adj. n.|二项式的；二项式|Relating to or consisting of two names or terms, especially in taxonomic nomenclature.|The binomial nomenclature system uses two Latin names to identify each species. 双名法用两个拉丁名来识别每个物种。|近义词:binomial|反义词:trinomial (in the context of three terms)|词性:adjective, noun
