nonfallaciously:/ˌnɒnfəˈleɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 无谬误地，正确地 |  Without logical fallacies; in a way that is valid and sound. |  He argued nonfallaciously, presenting a well-reasoned and evidence-based case.  他辩论得无懈可击，提出了一个论证充分、有理有据的案例。 | synonyms: validly, soundly, correctly, accurately, logically | antonyms: fallaciously, erroneously, invalidly, unsoundly | adverb
