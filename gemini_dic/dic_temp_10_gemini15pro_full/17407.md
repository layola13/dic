beambird:/ˈbiːmbɜːrd/| n. | 光束鸟，同步辐射装置中的光束线|A beambird is a slang term used at synchrotron facilities to refer to a beamline scientist or technician responsible for setting up and maintaining the experimental equipment on a beamline.  It's a person who "herds" the photon beam. |The beambird helped us optimize the X-ray beam for our experiment. 光束鸟帮助我们优化了实验用的X射线束。|近义词：beamline scientist, beamline technician|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
