lost-to:/lɒst tuː/ /lɑːst tuː/| adj.|输给，败给；被…夺走；|A term used primarily in sports contexts to indicate a defeat against a particular opponent. It implies a direct competition where one side loses to the other. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something being taken away by something else.|例句：The team lost-to their rivals in the final.  该队在决赛中输给了他们的对手。|近义词：defeated by, beaten by, succumbed to|反义词:victorious over, triumphed over|词性:形容词
