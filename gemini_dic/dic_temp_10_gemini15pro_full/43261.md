cradlers:/ˈkreɪdlərz/| n.|摇篮里的人; 抱着…的人;  创始者; 发祥地|People who cradle something or someone, or those who are in a cradle. Also can metaphorically refer to the founders or birthplace of something. |The mothers were the cradlers of their newborn babies.|近义词：holders, supporters, founders, originators|反义词: destroyers, opposers|Noun
