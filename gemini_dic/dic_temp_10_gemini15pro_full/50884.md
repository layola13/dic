desertedness:/dɪˈzɜːtɪdnəs/
n.
荒凉；无人居住；被遗弃的状态
The state of being deserted; emptiness and loneliness.
The desertedness of the island was eerie.  这个岛的荒凉令人毛骨悚然。
loneliness, isolation, desolation, barrenness
population, liveliness, bustle
noun
