environmentalists:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentəlɪsts/| n.|环保主义者；环境保护者|People who are concerned about and advocate for the protection of the environment.|例句：Environmentalists are protesting the construction of the new pipeline. 环保主义者正在抗议修建新的输油管道。|例句：Many environmentalists believe that climate change is the biggest threat facing humanity. 许多环保主义者认为气候变化是人类面临的最大威胁。|近义词：conservationists, ecologists, nature lovers|反义词：polluters, destroyers of nature|词性:noun

词源: environment (环境) + -al (形容词后缀) + -ist (表示“从事…的人”的后缀)
