encomiast:/ɪnˈkoʊmiæst/| n.|赞美者，颂扬者|A person who praises someone or something publicly and enthusiastically. |The king's encomiast wrote a lengthy poem praising his reign. 国王的赞美者写了一首长诗赞美他的统治。|近义词：eulogist, panegyrist, praiser, lauder|反义词：critic, detractor, denouncer|noun
