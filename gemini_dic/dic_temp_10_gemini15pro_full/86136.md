hearth:/hɑːrθ/ | n. | 熔炉 |The floor of a furnace or oven where intense heat is produced for smelting or baking.| The blacksmith worked the metal on the hearth of his forge. 铁匠在他的熔炉炉膛上加工金属。 | synonyms: furnace, forge, oven | antonyms: cooler, refrigerator | noun
