indexation:/ˌɪndɛkˈseɪʃən/ | n. |指数化|The act of linking the value of something (e.g., wages, prices, benefits) to an index, often to account for inflation or other economic changes. |例句：Wage indexation protects workers' purchasing power during periods of inflation. 工资指数化在通货膨胀时期保护工人的购买力。

例句：Indexation of pensions ensures that retirees' income keeps pace with rising prices. 养老金指数化确保退休人员的收入跟上物价上涨的步伐。|近义词：linking, adjustment, escalation|反义词:stagnation, fixed rate|noun
