outmove:/ˌaʊtˈmuːv/| v. |胜过；以策略击败|To surpass or defeat by maneuver; to outmaneuver.|She outmoved her opponent in the chess match by anticipating his every move. 她在棋局中预判了对手的每一步棋，从而胜过对手。|近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, defeat, overcome|反义词：lose to, be defeated by|词性:动词

词源: "out-" (超越) + "move" (移动)
