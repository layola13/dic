elapsed:/ɪˈlæpst/| adj.|过去的，逝去的，流逝的|past (time):  Having passed or slipped by.  Referring to a period of time that has already happened.|Three years have elapsed since we last met. 自从我们上次见面以来已经过去了三年。|Synonyms: passed, gone by, transpired, bygone|Antonyms: future, upcoming|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *elapsus, past participle of elabi (“to slip away, glide by”), from ex (“out of”) + labi (“to slip, glide”).
