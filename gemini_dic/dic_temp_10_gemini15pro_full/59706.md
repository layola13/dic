eclampsias:/ɪˈklæmpsiəz/
n.
子痫
A serious condition in pregnancy characterized by convulsions and coma.
Eclampsias can be life-threatening for both mother and baby. 子痫对母亲和婴儿都可能危及生命。
Severe pre-eclampsia can develop into eclampsias. 严重的先兆子痫会发展成子痫。
近义词:toxemia, convulsions
反义词: (No direct antonym for a medical condition like this.  One could consider "health" or "wellness" a broad antonym.)
词性: Noun
