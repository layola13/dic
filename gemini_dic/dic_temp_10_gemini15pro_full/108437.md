laxation:/lækˈseɪʃn/
n.
放松；松弛；减轻；缓和
The act of loosening or relaxing, or the state of being loosened or relaxed.
Muscle relaxation is important for athletes.  肌肉放松对运动员很重要。
近义词：loosening, easing, slackening, alleviation, mitigation
反义词：tension, tightening, constriction
词性：名词

laxation:/lækˈseɪʃn/
n.
通便，排便；泻药
The emptying of the bowels, or a medicine that causes this.
The doctor recommended a mild laxation for the patient. 医生建议病人服用温和的泻药。
近义词：bowel movement, defecation, purgative, aperient
反义词：constipation
词性：名词
