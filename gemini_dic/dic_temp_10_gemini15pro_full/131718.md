nonconfederations: /ˌnɒnkənˈfɛdəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.| 非联盟; 未结盟的国家或组织|A group of entities that are not united in a confederation.  The term refers to states or organizations that choose to remain independent and not join a larger political alliance or union.|  Example: The historian studied the various nonconfederations during the 19th century, analyzing their reasons for remaining independent.|Synonyms: non-alliances, independent states, unaffiliated entities|Antonyms: confederations, alliances, unions|Noun
