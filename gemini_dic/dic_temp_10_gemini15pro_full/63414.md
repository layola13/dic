entrancement:/ɪnˈtrænsmənt/ | n. | 着迷，狂喜，入迷 | A state of being completely captivated or enchanted. |  She watched the ballet in complete entrancement. 她完全着迷地观赏着芭蕾舞。

| Example: The magician's performance held the audience in a state of entrancement.  魔术师的表演让观众如痴如醉。| Synonym: fascination, rapture, captivation, enchantment | Antonym: boredom, disinterest, apathy | noun

entrancement:/ɪnˈtrænsmənt/ | n. |  施魔法，催眠 | The act of inducing a state of entrancement. |  The shaman's entrancement of the tribe was part of the healing ritual.  萨满对部落的催眠是治疗仪式的一部分。

| Example:  The hypnotist's methods of entrancement were quite effective.  催眠师的催眠方法相当有效。 | Synonym: hypnotism, spellbinding | Antonym: disenchantment | noun
