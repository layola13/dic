catholicness:/ˌkæθəˈlɪknɪs/
n.
天主教性；普遍性；包容性
The quality of being catholic, especially in terms of breadth of knowledge, taste, or interests.  The catholicness of her reading is impressive.
She embraces the catholicness of world cuisine. 她欣然接受世界各地美食的包容性。
universality, comprehensiveness, breadth, ecumenism
narrowness, sectarianism, exclusiveness
