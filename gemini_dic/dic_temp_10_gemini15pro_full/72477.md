fluo:/ˈfluːoʊ/ (US), /ˈfluːəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|荧光的，荧光剂|A shortened form of "fluorescent," referring to a bright, vivid, often neon color.  It can also refer to a fluorescent lamp or light.|The club was decorated with fluo paint.  这家俱乐部用荧光漆装饰。| The fluo lights made the room look like a disco. 荧光灯让房间看起来像个迪斯科舞厅。|近义词：fluorescent, neon, bright, vivid|反义词：dull, muted, dark|词性: adjective, noun

词源:  Shortened from "fluorescent," which ultimately derives from "fluorspar," a mineral that exhibits fluorescence.
