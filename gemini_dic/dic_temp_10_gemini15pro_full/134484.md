nonlaminating:/ˌnɒnˈlæmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非层压的，非叠层的
Not forming or tending to form laminae or layers.
The doctor discovered a nonlaminating tumor in the patient's lung. 医生在病人的肺部发现了一个非层状肿瘤。
The paint created a smooth, nonlaminating surface.  这种油漆形成了光滑、无分层的表面。
non-layered, non-stratified
laminating, layered, stratified
