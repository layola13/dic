ordinarily:/ˌɔːrdɪˈnɛrɪli/ /ˌɔːrˈdɪnərəli/
adverb
通常地，一般地，平常地
Usually; in a normal way.
Ordinarily, I leave for work at 8 am. 我通常早上八点去上班。

He ordinarily dresses casually. 他通常穿得很休闲。


近义词:usually, normally, generally, typically, commonly
反义词:unusually, rarely, exceptionally, infrequently
词性:adverb
