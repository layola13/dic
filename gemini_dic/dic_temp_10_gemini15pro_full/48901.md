defrayal:/dɪˈfreɪəl/ | n. | 支付，支付款，付款 | the act of paying the cost of something | The company will handle the defrayal of all travel expenses. 公司将负责支付所有旅行费用。 | 近义词: payment, settlement, discharge | 反义词: charge, billing, accrual | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *desfraier, from des- (dis-) + fraier (“to spend, consume”).  Ultimately from Latin dis- + fractus, past participle of frangere (“to break”).  The sense development is from "breaking up" a debt or expense.
