localizability: /ˌloʊkələˈzaɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可本地化性，本地化能力 | The localizability of software is crucial for international markets.  软件的可本地化性对于国际市场至关重要。|  localizability refers to the ease with which a product, especially software, can be adapted for different locales, including translation of text and modifications for cultural differences.  可本地化性指的是一个产品，特别是软件，适应不同地区的容易程度，包括文本翻译和针对文化差异的修改。 | Example: The game's high localizability made it a success worldwide.  这款游戏的高本地化程度使其在全球范围内取得了成功。

Example:  Improving the localizability of the website is a top priority.  提高网站的本地化程度是当务之急。 | synonyms: adaptability, translatability, internationalization readiness | antonyms: non-localizability, untranslatability | noun
