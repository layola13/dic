false:/fɔːls/ (British), /fɑːls/ (American)| adj.|假的，错误的，不真实的，人造的，虚伪的|Not true or correct; artificial; deceitful.  Derived from Old English *fals, from Proto-Germanic *falsoz, meaning "deceitful, treacherous." Related to Old Norse fals "false, wrong," and Dutch vals "false."|例句：The statement is false. 这句话是假的。

例句：He gave false testimony in court. 他在法庭上作伪证。


近义词：untrue, incorrect, erroneous, fake, artificial, spurious, bogus|反义词:true, real, genuine, authentic|词性:adjective
