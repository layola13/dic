epiphytical:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪtɪkəl/
adjective
附生的
(of a plant) growing on another plant but not parasitic.
例句：Epiphytical orchids thrive in the humid rainforest canopy.  附生兰花在潮湿的雨林冠层中茁壮成长。

例句：Many bromeliads are epiphytical, obtaining their nutrients from the air and rain. 许多凤梨科植物是附生的，从空气和雨水中获取养分。
近义词：airborne, nonparasitic
反义词：terrestrial, parasitic
词性：adjective

epiphyte:/ˈɛpɪˌfaɪt/
noun
附生植物
a plant that grows on another plant, especially one that is not parasitic, such as many orchids and ferns.
例句：The tree was covered in epiphytes, creating a miniature hanging garden.  这棵树上覆盖着附生植物，形成了一个微型空中花园。

例句：Orchids and ferns are common examples of epiphytes. 兰花和蕨类植物是附生植物的常见例子。
近义词：air plant
反义词：terrestrial plant
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *epi-* (upon, on) + *phyton* (plant).
