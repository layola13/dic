artefact:/ˈɑːtɪfækt/ (US) /ˈɑːtɪfɑːkt/ (UK)| n. |人工制品，史前古器物，人为现象|An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest.  Something observed in a scientific investigation or experiment that is not naturally present but occurs as a result of the preparative or investigative procedure.|The museum has many interesting artefacts from ancient Egypt.  博物馆收藏了许多来自古埃及的有趣文物。

The scientists discovered an artefact in their data that skewed the results. 科学家们在他们的数据中发现了一个人为造成的现象，它扭曲了结果。|近义词: relic, object, artifact (US spelling), article, item, specimen|反义词: natural object|词性:noun
