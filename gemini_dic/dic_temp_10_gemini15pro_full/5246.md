allocatur:/ˈæləkeɪtər/| n.| 分配令，拨款令| A formal authorization, especially a judicial order allowing a sum of money to be paid.| The judge issued an allocatur for the payment of legal fees. 法官签发了支付律师费的拨款令。|Synonyms: authorization, warrant, order, grant |Antonyms: denial, refusal| noun
Etymology->
From Late Latin *allocātur, third-person singular present passive indicative of allocō (“I assign, allot”), from ad (“to”) + locō (“I place”).
