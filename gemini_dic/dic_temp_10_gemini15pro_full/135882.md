nonprovincial:/ˌnɒnprəˈvɪnʃəl/ /ˌnɑːnprəˈvɪnʃəl/
adjective
非狭隘的；见识广的；不守旧的
Not limited in outlook; sophisticated and broad-minded.
Her nonprovincial tastes in art made her an interesting conversationalist.  她对艺术的品味并不狭隘，这使她成为一个有趣的健谈者。
He had a nonprovincial attitude, embracing ideas from all cultures.  他有一种不狭隘的态度，包容来自所有文化的思想。
近义词: cosmopolitan, sophisticated, broad-minded, open-minded, worldly, urbane
反义词: provincial, narrow-minded, insular, parochial

词性
adjective
