craftsmanlike:/ˈkræftsmənˌlaɪk/| adj.|技艺精湛的；匠心独运的；精工细作的|Resembling or characteristic of a skilled craftsperson; exhibiting careful workmanship and attention to detail.|The furniture was crafted with craftsmanlike precision. 这件家具制作得非常精细，体现了精湛的工艺。|近义词：skillful, adept, expert, proficient, meticulous, precise|反义词：clumsy, amateurish, shoddy, careless|词性:形容词
词源: craftsman + like. Craftsman comes from Old English cræft (skill, strength) + man.
