composed:/kəmˈpoʊzd/ (US) /kəmˈpəʊzd/ (UK)| adj.|沉着的；镇定的；平静的；由…组成（构成）的|Made up of different parts or elements; calm, tranquil, and self-possessed.|例句：She appeared calm and composed. 她显得平静而沉着。|近义词：calm, tranquil, serene, collected, self-possessed|反义词：agitated, nervous, anxious, distraught|词性:形容词

composed:/kəmˈpoʊzd/ (US) /kəmˈpəʊzd/ (UK)| v.|创作（乐曲、诗歌等）；组成；构成|Past participle of "compose," meaning to create or constitute.|例句：The committee is composed of ten members. 委员会由十名成员组成。|近义词：create, write, form, constitute, make up|反义词：decompose, dismantle, break down|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *compos*, past participle of *composer*, from Latin *componere* "to put together," from *com-* "together" + *ponere* "to put, place."
