astrodome: /ˈæstrəˌdoʊm/ | n. | 天文馆，太空穹顶 | A large dome-shaped building, often with a transparent roof, used for observing the stars and planets or for presenting shows about space.  A transparent dome or other structure resembling a dome, especially one on top of an aircraft. |  We visited the astrodome to see a show about the constellations. 我们参观了天文馆，观看了一场关于星座的演出。| The pilot had a clear view from the astrodome of the helicopter. 飞行员可以从直升机顶部的透明穹顶清楚地看到外面。 | synonyms: planetarium, observatory, dome | antonyms: N/A | noun
