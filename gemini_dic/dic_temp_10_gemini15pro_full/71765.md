fleak:/fliːk/| n. |小斑点，雀斑；微粒；少量|A tiny speck; a freckle.  A small particle or bit. A small quantity.|例句：I noticed a fleak of dust on the lens. 我注意到镜片上有一粒灰尘。

例句：She had a few fleaks of freckles across her nose. 她的鼻梁上有一些雀斑。|近义词：speck, particle, bit, spot, dot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *flek*, akin to Old Norse *flekkr* (spot, patch) and Middle High German *vlëc* (spot, freckle).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flekkaz*.  Related to Old English *fliece* (fleece).
