diamine:/ˈdaɪəˌmiːn/|n.|二胺|An organic compound containing two amino groups (-NH2).  These groups can be attached to the same carbon atom or to different carbon atoms within the molecule. Diamines are important building blocks in the synthesis of various polymers, such as nylon.|Hexamethylenediamine is used in the production of nylon. 六亚甲基二胺用于生产尼龙。|近义词: diamino compound|反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|词性: noun
