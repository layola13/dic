graced:/ɡreɪst/| v. adj.|使…增光添彩；使…更优雅；被…装饰；  优美的；优雅的；有风度的|To add beauty or charm to something; to be an attractive presence in or on something;  showing grace or elegance.|Her presence graced the occasion. 她的出席使这个场合增光添彩。|近义词：adorned, embellished, honored, dignified, elegant|反义词：disgraced, marred, tarnished, ungainly, awkward|词性:动词（过去式/过去分词）; 形容词

graced:/ɡreɪst/| adj.|优美的；优雅的；有风度的|Having or showing grace or elegance.|She moved with a graced elegance. 她举止优雅。|近义词：elegant, graceful, refined, polished, poised|反义词：awkward, clumsy, ungainly, graceless, inelegant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *gracier "to thank, grant favor, pardon," from Latin gratia "grace, favor, thanks."
