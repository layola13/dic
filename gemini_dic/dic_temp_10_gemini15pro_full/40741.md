conveyancings:/kənˈveɪənsɪŋz/
n.
财产转让；不动产转让手续；转让证书
The legal process of transferring property ownership from one person to another.  This includes all the documents and actions involved.
The conveyancing of the property took several weeks.  这处房产的转让手续花了几个星期。
synonyms: property transfer, transfer of ownership, transfer of title, legal transfer
antonyms: acquisition, purchase


conveyancing:/kənˈveɪənsɪŋ/
n.
运输；运送
The act of transporting something from one place to another. (This meaning is less common than the legal meaning.)
The conveyancing of goods by rail is efficient.  铁路运输货物效率很高。
synonyms: transport, transportation, carriage, haulage
antonyms:  None for this meaning



词性: noun (名词)
