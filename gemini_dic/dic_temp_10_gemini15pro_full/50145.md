reduced in number; numbered.
The denumbered ranks of the army struggled to hold the line.  这支数量减少的军队艰难地坚守着防线。
The boxes were denumbered for easy identification. 这些箱子被编号以便于识别。
synonyms: reduced, lessened, diminished, numbered, counted
antonyms: increased, augmented, expanded
