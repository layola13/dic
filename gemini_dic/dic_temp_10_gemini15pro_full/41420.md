cornerstone:/ˈkɔːrnərˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈkɔːnəstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|基石，奠基石，基础，根本|A cornerstone is a stone that forms part of a corner in a building, especially one that is laid in a special ceremony. Figuratively, it refers to something of fundamental importance.|The freedom of speech is the cornerstone of a democratic society. 言论自由是民主社会的基础。|synonyms: foundation, base, bedrock, underpinning|antonyms: apex, peak, summit|noun
