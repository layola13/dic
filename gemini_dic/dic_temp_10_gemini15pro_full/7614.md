annualize: /ˈænjuəlaɪz/ | v. | 按年计算，年化 | To calculate or adjust something, such as earnings or interest rates, to show what it would be if it continued for a full year. | The company annualized its quarterly earnings to project its yearly profits. 公司将其季度收益按年计算以预测其年度利润。| The bank annualized the interest rate on the loan. 银行将贷款利率年化。 | synonym: extrapolate, project | antonym: - | verb
