fatherly:/ˈfɑːðərli/| adj.|父亲的；慈父般的|Characteristic of or befitting a father; kind and protective.|He gave me a fatherly hug. 他给了我一个慈父般的拥抱。|近义词：paternal, avuncular|反义词：unfatherly, unpaternal|词性:形容词

词源: From father + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" transforms nouns into adjectives, indicating resemblance or characteristic of the noun.
