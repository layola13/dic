antimilitarist:/ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪst/| n. adj.|反军国主义者，反战主义者|A person who opposes militarism or war.|例句：The antimilitarist argued passionately against the war.  这位反军国主义者强烈反对战争。

例句：She is a known antimilitarist and peace activist. 她是一位著名的反战主义者和和平活动家。


|近义词：pacifist, peacemaker, dove|反义词: warmonger, hawk, militarist|词性: noun, adjective


antimilitarism:/ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪzəm/| n.|反军国主义|Opposition to militarism; the belief that war and the preparation for war are wrong.|例句：Antimilitarism was a growing sentiment among the population. 反军国主义情绪在民众中日益增长。

例句：The group promoted antimilitarism through peaceful protests.  该团体通过和平抗议来宣扬反军国主义。|近义词：pacifism|反义词: militarism|词性: noun
