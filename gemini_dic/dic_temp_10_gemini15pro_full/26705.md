cadences:/ˈkeɪdənsɪz/ (US), /ˈkeɪdənsiz/ (UK)| n.|节奏，韵律，抑扬顿挫|A sequence of notes or chords comprising the close of a musical phrase, section, or movement.|The cadences of the music lulled the baby to sleep.  这首音乐的节奏使婴儿入睡了。|The speaker's cadences were mesmerizing. 演讲者的抑扬顿挫令人着迷。|synonyms: rhythms, beats, pulsations, measures, tempos|antonyms: monotony, uniformity|noun
