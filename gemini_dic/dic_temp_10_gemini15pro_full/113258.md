lustreless:/ˈlʌstələs/| adj.|无光泽的，暗淡的|Lacking lustre or shine; dull.  It describes something that is not shiny or reflective.|The old painting had become lustreless over time. 这幅古老的画随着时间的推移变得暗淡无光。|近义词：dull, mat, lacklustre, dim, drab|反义词：lustrous, shiny, glossy, gleaming, radiant|词性:形容词

词源:
From lustre + -less.  Lustre itself comes from Old French lustre "brightness, brilliance," from Italian lustro, from Latin lustrare "to brighten, purify," from PIE root *leuk- "light, brightness."
