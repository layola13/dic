cynanche:/sɪˈnæŋki/ | n. | 肉苁蓉; | A parasitic plant of the genus Cistanche, native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia and the Mediterranean.  Used in traditional Chinese medicine. |  Cistanche deserticola, a species of cynanche, is known for its medicinal properties. 肉苁蓉的一种，荒漠肉苁蓉，以其药用价值而闻名。| synonym: cistanche, desert ginseng | antonym: N/A | noun
