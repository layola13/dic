acinaciform: /əˈsɪnəsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
弯刀形的，似弯刀的
Curved like a scimitar; shaped like a saber.
The acinaciform leaves of the plant were a distinctive feature.  这种植物的弯刀形叶子是一个显著特征。
The bacteria had an acinaciform shape under the microscope. 在显微镜下，这种细菌呈弯刀状。
近义词: falcate, curved, sickle-shaped
反义词: straight, linear
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acinacēs* (a short, curved sword of Persian origin), + *-formis* (meaning "having the form of").  Ultimately from Greek *ἀκινάκης (akinákēs)*.
