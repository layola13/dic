emissivity:/ˌiːmɪˈsɪvɪti/| n. |发射率；辐射率|The ratio of the radiant flux emitted by any body to that emitted by a blackbody at the same temperature. |The emissivity of the metal surface is 0.8.  金属表面的发射率为0.8。|Synonyms: radiation coefficient,  |Antonyms: absorptivity|noun
