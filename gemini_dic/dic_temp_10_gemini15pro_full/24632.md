broker-dealer:/ˈbroʊkər ˈdiːlər/ | n. | 经纪自营商；证券经纪自营商 | A firm that buys and sells securities for its own account and also acts as a broker for its clients.  A broker-dealer trades securities for its own account, as well as buying and selling securities for its customers. |  Example 1: The broker-dealer facilitated the trade of the company's stock. 这家经纪自营商促成了该公司股票的交易。

Example 2: Many large financial institutions act as broker-dealers. 许多大型金融机构充当经纪自营商。 | Synonyms: securities firm, brokerage firm, investment bank | Antonyms:  N/A | Noun
