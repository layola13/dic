brachiosaurus:/ˌbrækiəˈsɔːrəs/
n.
腕龙
A genus of large sauropod dinosaurs that lived during the Late Jurassic period.  They are known for their long necks and front legs that were longer than their hind legs.
The brachiosaurus is one of the most iconic dinosaurs. 腕龙是最具标志性的恐龙之一。
Brachiosaurus browsed on trees high above the ground. 腕龙吃的是高高树上的叶子。
近义词:sauropod, dinosaur
反义词:None (It's a specific genus of dinosaur, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性:noun
