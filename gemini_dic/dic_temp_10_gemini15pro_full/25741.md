buoying:/ˈbɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人振奋的，令人鼓舞的 | making someone feel more cheerful or confident |  The good news was buoying.  好消息令人振奋。 | 近义词: encouraging, uplifting, heartening | 反义词: depressing, disheartening, discouraging | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From the verb "buoy," meaning "to keep afloat" or "to support," from the Old French "boie," ultimately from the Dutch "boei." The sense of "raising spirits" developed figuratively from the idea of something being kept afloat or lifted up.
