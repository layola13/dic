nuclear-pile:/ˈnjuːkliər paɪl/
n.|核反应堆|A structure containing fissile material arranged in such a way that a controlled chain reaction can be produced.  A nuclear reactor.|The first nuclear-pile was built at the University of Chicago.|Synonym: nuclear reactor, atomic pile|Antonym: None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite.)|noun

nuclear-pile:/ˈnjuːkliər paɪl/
n.|核反应堆|A structure of fissile material and other substances arranged in such a way that a controlled chain reaction can occur. |They built a nuclear-pile for research purposes.|Synonym:  reactor, atomic pile|Antonym: None|noun
