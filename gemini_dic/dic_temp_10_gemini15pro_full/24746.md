brooding-over:/ˈbruːdɪŋ ˈoʊvər/
adjective
沉思的，忧思的
Deeply absorbed in thought, especially in a melancholy or anxious way.
He spent the afternoon brooding over his problems. 他一下午都在沉思他的问题。
近义词:mulling over, pondering, dwelling on, contemplating
反义词:carefree, lighthearted
词性:adjective
