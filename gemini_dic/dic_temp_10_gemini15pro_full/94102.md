impeachable:/ɪmˈpiːtʃəbl/
adjective
可弹劾的
Subject to impeachment; capable of being impeached.  Liable to be called to account.
The president is impeachable for high crimes and misdemeanors. 总统可以因严重罪行和轻罪被弹劾。
The governor's actions were deemed impeachable by the state legislature. 州立法机关认为州长的行为是可以弹劾的。
近义词: indictable, accusable, chargeable
反义词: unimpeachable, irreproachable, blameless
词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from impeach + -able.
