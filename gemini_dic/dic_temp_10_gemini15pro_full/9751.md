antl:/ˈæntəl/ | n. |  ANTLR (ANother Tool for Language Recognition) | A parser generator that uses LL(*) parsing. It is commonly used to build compilers, interpreters, and other language processing tools.  | Example:  I used ANTLR to generate a parser for my new programming language. 我使用ANTLR为我的新编程语言生成一个解析器。

| Synonym: parser generator, compiler-compiler | Antonym: N/A | 词性: noun
