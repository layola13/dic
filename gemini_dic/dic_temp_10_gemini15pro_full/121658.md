mispicture: /ˌmɪsˈpɪktʃər/
verb
歪曲；误绘；对…产生错误印象
to represent inaccurately
The media mispictured the event, making it seem far more serious than it actually was. 媒体歪曲了这一事件，使其看起来比实际情况严重得多。
misrepresent, distort
represent accurately, portray truthfully
词性: 动词
