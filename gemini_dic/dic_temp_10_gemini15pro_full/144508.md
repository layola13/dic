outtrading:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪdɪŋ/
verb
胜过，在交易中超过
surpassing someone in trading; making better deals or earning more profit than competitors.
He outtraded his rivals and secured the best deal. 他在交易中胜过对手，获得了最好的交易。
The company outtraded all its competitors in the region.  这家公司在该地区的交易中超过了所有竞争对手。
近义词: outmaneuver, outperform, outsmart
反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outdone
词性: verb
