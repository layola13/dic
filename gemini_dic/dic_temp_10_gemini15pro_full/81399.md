grasses:/ˈɡræsɪz/ | n. |草，草地；禾本科植物|A wide expanse of grasses covered the prairie.  广阔的草原上覆盖着草。|例句：The horses grazed on the lush green grasses. 马匹在茂盛的绿草地上吃草。

The grasses swayed gently in the breeze. 草在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：grasslands, vegetation, turf, sod|反义词：barren land, desert|词性: 名词 (plural of grass)

Etymology:
From Middle English *grasess, *gresses, plural of gras, gresse (“grass”), from Old English græs, gærs (“grass, herb, plant”), from Proto-Germanic *grasan (“grass”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰreh₁- (“to grow, become green”). Cognate with Scots gress (“grass”), West Frisian gers (“grass”), Dutch gras (“grass”), German Gras (“grass”), Swedish gräs (“grass”), Danish græs (“grass”).
