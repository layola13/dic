overdeliciousness: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈlɪʃəsnəs/ | n. | 极度的美味，非常好吃 |  A state of being extremely delicious, beyond ordinary deliciousness. | The overdeliciousness of the chocolate cake made me want to eat the whole thing. 这块巧克力蛋糕的极度美味让我想要把它全部吃掉。| synonyms: exquisite deliciousness, supreme deliciousness, heavenly flavor, ambrosial taste | antonyms: blandness, unappetizingness, unpleasant taste | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix): From Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively."
"deliciousness" (noun): From "delicious" + "-ness."
"delicious" (adjective): From Old French "delicieux," from Latin "deliciosus," meaning "delightful to the senses, luxurious," from "deliciae" (plural noun) "pleasures, delights."
