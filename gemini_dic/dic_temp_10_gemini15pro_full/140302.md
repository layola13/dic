ogham: /ˈoʊəm/ (US), /ˈɒɡəm/ (UK)| n.|欧甘文|An ancient alphabet used primarily by the Irish and Picts, typically consisting of a series of strokes branching off a central stem line.|The inscription on the stone was written in ogham.|近义词: ancient script, alphabet|反义词: modern alphabet|noun
