interlot:/ˈɪntərˌlɒt/ (US) /ˈɪntəˌlɒt/ (UK)| n.|空地；地块之间的空地|A piece of land between lots, especially one that is undeveloped or used as a common area. |The children used the interlot as a playground. 孩子们把空地当作游乐场。|Synonym: vacant lot, open space, interstitial space|Antonym: developed land|noun
