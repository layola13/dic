outachieving:/ˌaʊtəˈtʃiːvɪŋ/
| adj. | 超越；胜过；在成就上超过 | Performing or achieving at a higher level than others, especially those in a similar category. |  She's consistently outachieving her peers in mathematics. 她一直在数学方面超越同龄人。|  He was always outachieving his older brother in school. 他在学校总是比他哥哥成绩好。 | synonyms: outperforming, surpassing, excelling, exceeding | antonyms: underperforming, failing, lagging | adjective
