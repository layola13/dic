hematinometric:/ˌhiːmətɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
血红素测定的
Relating to the measurement of hematin or hemoglobin.
Hematinometric methods are used to determine the concentration of hemoglobin in blood samples.  血红素测定法用于测定血液样本中血红蛋白的浓度。
This hematinometric assay provides accurate results. 这种血红素测定法可提供准确的结果。
近义词: hemoglobinometric
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider terms related to *not* measuring hematin/hemoglobin)
