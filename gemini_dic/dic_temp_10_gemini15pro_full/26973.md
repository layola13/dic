calcinate:/ˈkælsɪneɪt/| v. |煅烧，焙烧|To heat a substance to a high temperature but below the melting or fusing point, causing loss of moisture, volatile matter, or oxidation.|The bones were calcinated in the fire.  这些骨头在火中被烧成了灰。|The limestone is calcinated to produce lime. 石灰石经过煅烧生成石灰。|近义词：burn, roast, bake|反义词：hydrate|词性：动词
