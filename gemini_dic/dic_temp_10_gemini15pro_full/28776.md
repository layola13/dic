carob:/ˈkærəb/| n.|角豆；角豆树；角豆荚|A sweet, edible pod from a Mediterranean tree, used as a chocolate substitute.|例句：I use carob powder instead of cocoa in my baking. 我在烘焙时用角豆粉代替可可粉。

例句：The carob tree thrives in warm, dry climates. 角豆树在温暖干燥的气候中茁壮成长。|近义词：locust bean, St. John's bread|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *carobe*, from Arabic خَرُّوبٌ (*ḵarrūb*), from Aramaic ܟܪܘܒܐ (*kharubha*), ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly borrowed from Akkadian *kharubu*.
