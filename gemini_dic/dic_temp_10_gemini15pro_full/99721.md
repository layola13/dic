interlude:/ˈɪntərluːd/| n. |插曲，间奏；幕间休息|A period of time between two events, or an activity that is different from what comes before and after it.|The musical interlude provided a welcome break from the speeches.  音乐插曲让演讲之间有了个受欢迎的休息时间。|Synonyms: break, interval, pause, respite|Antonyms: continuation,  persistence|Noun

interlude:/ˈɪntərluːd/| n. |插曲，间奏；幕间休息|A short piece of instrumental music played between the parts of a song, play, or religious service.|The pianist played a short interlude before the next act began.  钢琴家在下一幕开始前演奏了一段简短的间奏曲。|Synonyms: intermezzo,  bridge|Antonyms: main piece,  principal performance|Noun
