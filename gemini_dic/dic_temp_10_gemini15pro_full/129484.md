non-analogous:/ˌnɒn əˈnæləɡəs/ | adj. | 非类似的，不同的 | Not analogous; dissimilar; not corresponding. | The two situations are non-analogous and require different solutions. 这两种情况并不相似，需要不同的解决方案。 |  The economic crisis of 2008 is non-analogous to the Great Depression. 2008年的经济危机与大萧条并不相似。 | synonyms: dissimilar, different, unlike, disparate, divergent | antonyms: analogous, similar, alike, comparable, parallel | adjective
