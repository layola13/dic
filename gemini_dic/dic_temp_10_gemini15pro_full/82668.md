guessing:/ˈɡesɪŋ/| n. |猜测，推测| the process of trying to find the right answer or information about something without being sure of all the facts | I had no idea what the answer was, so I was just guessing. 我不知道答案是什么，所以我只是在猜测。|近义词：conjecture, supposition, surmise, speculation|反义词:knowing, certainty|noun
