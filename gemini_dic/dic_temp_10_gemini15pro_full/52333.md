dicrotism:/ˈdaɪkrətɪzəm/| n. |双脉搏; 重搏|A condition in which the pulse is perceived as having two distinct beats for each cardiac cycle.|The doctor detected dicrotism in the patient's pulse, suggesting a possible cardiovascular issue. 医生在病人的脉搏中检测到重搏，表明可能存在心血管问题。|近义词:double beat pulse|反义词:|noun
