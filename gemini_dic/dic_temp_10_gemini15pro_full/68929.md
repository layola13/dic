fatalities:/fəˈtælɪtiz/
noun
死亡人数，死亡，致命事故
deaths resulting from an accident or disaster.
The earthquake caused thousands of fatalities. 地震造成了数千人死亡。
近义词:deaths, casualties, losses
反义词:survivors, living
