desertless:/ˈdɛzətlɪs/
adjective
无沙漠的; 没有沙漠的
Lacking deserts; having no deserts.
The planet is surprisingly desertless, covered mostly in lush vegetation.  这个星球令人惊讶地没有沙漠，大部分覆盖着茂密的植被。
The terrain was remarkably desertless, dominated by rolling green hills.  这片地形非常奇特，没有沙漠，主要由起伏的绿色山丘组成。
近义词:arid-free, non-desert
反义词:desert, arid, barren
词性:adjective
