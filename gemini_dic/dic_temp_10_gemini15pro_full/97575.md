ingulf:/ɪnˈɡʌlf/ /ɛnˈɡʌlf/
verb
吞没， engulf
to flow over and cover something completely
The tsunami engulfed the coastal villages. 海啸吞没了沿海村庄。
engross, overwhelm, submerge
extricate, free, release
v.
