mulishness:/ˈmjuːlɪʃnəs/ /ˈmʊlɪʃnɪs/| n. |固执，倔强|The state or quality of being mulish; stubbornness. |His mulishness prevented a compromise. 他的固执阻碍了妥协。|近义词：stubbornness, obstinacy, intransigence, pigheadedness|反义词:compliance, acquiescence, docility, flexibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mule + -ishness.  Ultimately from Latin mulus (mule).  The mule's reputation for stubbornness led to the adjective "mulish" and then to the noun "mulishness."
