gemmy:/ˈdʒɛmi/| adj.|宝石般的，似宝石的，光彩夺目的|Resembling a gem; sparkling; brilliant.  Having the nature or appearance of gems.|The cave was filled with gemmy crystals.  洞穴里充满了宝石般的晶体。|近义词：sparkling, brilliant, glittering, luminous, radiant|反义词：dull, lackluster, opaque|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From gem + -y.  Ultimately from Latin gemma "bud, jewel."
