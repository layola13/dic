atrocity:/əˈtrɒsəti/ /əˈtrɑːsəti/| n.|暴行，残暴行为|An act of extreme cruelty and wickedness, especially involving physical violence or injury.|The atrocities committed during the war shocked the world. 战争期间犯下的暴行震惊了世界。|近义词：barbarity, savagery, brutality, wickedness, outrage, enormity, abomination, horror, crime, evil, inhumanity, violation|反义词：kindness, compassion, benevolence, humanity|词性: 名词
