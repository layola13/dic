merchandises:/ˈmɜːrtʃənˌdaɪzɪz/
|n.| 商品，货物|Goods that are bought and sold. |The store offers a wide variety of merchandises. 这家商店提供各种各样的商品。|Synonyms: goods, wares, commodities, products|Antonyms: services|noun

merchandise:/ˈmɜːrtʃənˌdaɪz/
|v.| 推销，销售|To promote the sale of (goods), especially by their presentation in retail outlets. |The company merchandised its new product line aggressively. 该公司积极推销其新的产品线。|Synonyms: market, promote, sell, retail|Antonyms: buy, purchase|verb
