multi-access:/ˌmʌltiˈækses/ | adj. | 多路访问的，多址访问的 | Allowing multiple users or devices to access a system or resource simultaneously. |  This server supports multi-access technology, enabling numerous users to connect at the same time. 这台服务器支持多路访问技术，允许多个用户同时连接。

The multi-access network allows everyone in the office to use the internet. 多路访问网络允许办公室里的每个人都使用互联网。


|近义词：multiple-access, shared access |反义词：single-access, exclusive access | 词性: 形容词
