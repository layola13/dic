innovators:/ˈɪnəveɪtərz/ | n. |创新者，革新者 |  A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products. |  例句：The company is looking for innovators who can help them stay ahead of the competition.  这家公司正在寻找能够帮助他们在竞争中保持领先地位的创新者。

例句：Innovators are often the driving force behind technological advancements. 创新者通常是技术进步背后的驱动力。 | 近义词：pioneers, inventors, creators, developers, groundbreakers | 反义词：followers, imitators, traditionalists | 词性：名词

词源：Late Middle English: from Latin innovator, from innovat- ‘renewed’, from the verb innovare, from in- ‘in’ + novus ‘new’.
