outshouted:/ˌaʊtˈʃaʊtɪd/
verb
大声喊过，盖过…的喊叫声
past participle of outshout: to shout louder than someone else
例句：The angry protesters were outshouted by the supporters of the new policy.  愤怒的抗议者被新政策的支持者的喊声盖过了。

例句：He was outshouted by the crowd and couldn't make himself heard. 他被人群的喊声盖过，无法让别人听到他的声音。

近义词：drowned out, overshouted
反义词：
词性：verb (past participle)

词源：out- + shout + -ed
"out-" prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing
"shout" verb meaning to utter a loud cry
"-ed" past participle suffix
