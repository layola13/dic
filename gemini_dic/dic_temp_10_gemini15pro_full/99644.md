interlayered:/ˌɪntərˈleɪərd/
adjective
交错层的，夹层的
arranged in or consisting of alternating layers of two or more substances.
The rock formation was interlayered with shale and sandstone. 这块岩层由页岩和砂岩交错而成。
Synonyms: laminated, stratified, layered
Antonyms: homogeneous, uniform
词性: adjective
