on-hold:/ɑːn ˈhoʊld/ /ɒn ˈhəʊld/
adjective|暂停的，搁置的|Suspended, delayed, or interrupted temporarily, typically referring to a phone call or a project.|例句：My application is currently on hold. 我的申请目前处于搁置状态。|例句：The project was put on hold due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被暂停了。|近义词：suspended, pending, delayed|反义词：active, in progress, ongoing|词性:adjective
