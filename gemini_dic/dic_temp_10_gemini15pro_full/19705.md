bilinear:/ˌbaɪˈlɪniər/| adj.|双线性的，双一次的|Relating to or involving two lines or variables.|The bilinear interpolation algorithm is commonly used in image processing. 双线性插值算法常用于图像处理。|linear, multiple|nonlinear|adjective
