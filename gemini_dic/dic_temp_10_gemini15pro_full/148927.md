overwise:/ˈoʊvərwaɪz/| adj.|过分的,过度的,过度的; 不明智的; 夸张的|Excessively or unnecessarily large, elaborate, or detailed.  Unwise or unreasonable.|His reaction was a little overwise. 他的反应有点过激了。|The decorations were a bit overwise for such a small party.  对于这样一个小聚会来说，这些装饰有点过分了。|近义词：excessive, extravagant, immoderate, undue, unreasonable|反义词：moderate, reasonable, appropriate, sensible|词性:形容词

词源:
From "over-" + "-wise," meaning excessively wise, hence foolishly so.  The word has fallen out of common usage.
