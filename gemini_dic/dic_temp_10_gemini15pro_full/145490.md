overclinically:/ˌoʊvərˈklɪnɪkli/ | adv. | 过于临床地，过分注重临床地 |  Excessively or overly focused on the clinical aspects of a situation, often neglecting the emotional or personal aspects. | The doctor's approach was criticized for being too overclinically detached, lacking empathy for the patient's emotional distress. |  近义词: impersonally, coldly, detachedly | 反义词: empathetically, compassionately | 词性: 副词

overclinically:/ˌoʊvərˈklɪnɪkli/ | adj. | 过于临床的，过分注重临床的| Excessively or overly focused on the clinical aspects of a situation, often neglecting the emotional or personal aspects. | The report was written in an overclinically dry style, focusing solely on the medical data and ignoring the patient's perspective. | 近义词: impersonal, cold, detached | 反义词: empathetic, compassionate | 词性: 形容词


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "clinically" (adverb derived from "clinical," ultimately from Greek "klinikos" meaning "of or pertaining to a bed," referring to medical treatment).
