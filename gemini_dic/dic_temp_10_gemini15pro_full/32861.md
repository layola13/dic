cholinergic:/ˌkəʊlɪˈnɜːrdʒɪk/ | adj. n.| 胆碱能的；胆碱能神经| Relating to or denoting nerve cells that release acetylcholine as a neurotransmitter.  |  例句：Cholinergic drugs mimic the action of acetylcholine. 胆碱能药物模仿乙酰胆碱的作用。 | 近义词:acetylcholine-releasing, parasympathetic | 反义词: adrenergic | 词性:形容词, 名词


cholinergic:/ˌkəʊlɪˈnɜːrdʒɪk/ | n.| 胆碱能物质| A substance that resembles acetylcholine or mimics its action. | 例句：This drug acts as a cholinergic at certain receptor sites.  这种药物在某些受体部位充当胆碱能物质。| 近义词: acetylcholine agonist | 反义词: anticholinergic | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *cholē* (bile) + *erg(o)-* (work) + *-ic* (adjective suffix).  The term refers to the action of acetylcholine, which was first isolated from bile.
