overlooked:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊkt/| adj.|被忽视的，被忽略的|Not noticed or given attention to.|The potential side effects of the new drug were overlooked in the initial study.  这种新药的潜在副作用在最初的研究中被忽略了。|例句：The small detail was overlooked in the contract.  合同中忽略了这个小细节。

例句：Her contributions to the project were overlooked by her manager.  她的经理忽略了她对项目的贡献。|近义词：ignored, neglected, missed, disregarded|反义词：noticed, observed, recognized|词性:形容词

overlook:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/| vt.|俯瞰，忽视，宽恕|1. To have a view from above. 2. To fail to notice or consider something. 3. To forgive or ignore (a fault or offense).|例句：Our hotel room overlooked the ocean. 我们的酒店房间俯瞰大海。

例句：I overlooked a crucial step in the instructions. 我忽略了说明书中的一个关键步骤。|近义词：ignore, neglect, miss, excuse, pardon|反义词：notice, observe, scrutinize, punish|词性:动词


词源: From Middle English *overloken, from Old English oferlōcian (“to look over, look down upon, despise, neglect”), from ofer (“over”) + lōcian (“to look”).
