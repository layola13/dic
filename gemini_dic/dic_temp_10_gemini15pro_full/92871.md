idea:/aɪˈdɪə/ | n. |想法，主意，概念|a thought or suggestion as to a possible course of action|I have a great idea for a new business. 我有一个很棒的创业想法。|
近义词：thought, concept, notion, plan|反义词：reality, actuality|词性: noun

idea:/aɪˈdɪə/ | n. |意见，见解|an opinion or belief|He has some strange ideas about education. 他对教育有一些奇怪的见解。|
近义词：opinion, belief, view, perspective|反义词：fact, truth|词性: noun

idea:/aɪˈdɪə/ | n. |模糊印象，感觉|a vague impression or feeling|I had no idea it was so late. 我不知道已经这么晚了。|
近义词：impression, feeling, sense, inkling|反义词：certainty, knowledge|词性: noun


词源: From Late Latin idea, from Ancient Greek ἰδέα (idéa, “form, pattern, appearance; idea, mental picture”), from ἰδεῖν (ideîn, “to see”).
