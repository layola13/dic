fusillade:/ˌfjuːsɪˈleɪd/ /ˌfjuːsəˈlɑːd/
noun
一阵齐射；(批评、问题等的)猛烈攻击；连发
A fusillade is a large number of shots fired rapidly or a large number of things that happen quickly one after the other.

例句：
The troops were met by a fusillade of bullets.  部队遭到一阵猛烈的弹雨。

The speaker was greeted with a fusillade of questions from the audience. 演讲者遭到观众一连串的提问。

近义词：
barrage, volley, salvo, bombardment

反义词：
trickle, dribble

词性
n.
