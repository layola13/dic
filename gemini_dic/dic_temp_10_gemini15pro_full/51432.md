detestably:/dɪˈtestəbli/| adv.|讨厌地；可憎地|in a detestable manner; arousing or meriting intense dislike.|He behaved detestably towards his guests. 他对他的客人表现得很讨厌。|近义词：abominably, hatefully, loathsomely, offensively, repugnantly|反义词：admirably, delightfully, pleasantly, wonderfully|词性:副词
词源：From detest (v.) + -able (adj.) + -ly (adv.).  Ultimately from Latin detestari "to curse, denounce, abhor."
