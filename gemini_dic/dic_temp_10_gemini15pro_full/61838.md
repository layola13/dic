emplecton:/ɛmˈplɛktən/ | n. |  嵌入;嵌入物;嵌入体 | Something embedded, especially in rock.  A fragment of one rock enclosed within another rock. | The geologist identified the emplecton as a fragment of volcanic rock within the sedimentary layer. 地质学家将嵌入体鉴定为沉积层中的一块火山岩碎片。 |  inclusion, insert, implant | extraction, removal | noun
