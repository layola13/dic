craggednesses:/ˈkræɡɪdnəsɪz/
n.
崎岖不平，险峻
The state of being cragged; full of crags; ruggedness.
The craggednesses of the mountain range made it a challenging climb.  这山脉的崎岖不平使攀登成为一项挑战。
近义词: ruggedness, roughness, unevenness
反义词: smoothness, flatness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From cragged + -ness.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *kragjo- (“hook, crooked”).
