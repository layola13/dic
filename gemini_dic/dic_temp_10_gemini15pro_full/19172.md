bewilder:/bɪˈwɪldər/| vt.|使迷惑，使不知所措|to confuse or puzzle someone completely|The complex instructions bewildered her.|近义词：confuse, perplex, puzzle|反义词：clarify, enlighten|verb

bewilderment:/bɪˈwɪldərmənt/| n.|迷惑，困惑|a feeling of being confused and disoriented|She stared at him in bewilderment.|近义词：confusion, perplexity|反义词：clarity, understanding|noun

bewildered:/bɪˈwɪldərd/| adj.|迷惑的，不知所措的|feeling confused and disoriented|He gave me a bewildered look.|近义词：confused, perplexed|反义词：clear, understanding|adjective
