frustrately:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪdli/| adv.| 沮丧地，懊恼地 |In a manner that shows frustration; in a way that expresses or indicates feelings of discouragement or dissatisfaction.|She sighed frustratedly and threw her pen on the desk. 她沮丧地叹了口气，把笔扔在桌子上。|He pounded the table frustratedly when he couldn't solve the puzzle.  当他解不开谜题时，他懊恼地捶着桌子。|近义词: angrily, disappointedly, despairingly, impatiently|反义词: patiently, calmly, cheerfully|词性:副词
词源: From frustrate (verb) + -ly (adverb suffix).  Ultimately from Latin frustrare "to deceive, disappoint, make vain."
