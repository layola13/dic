hafted:/ˈhæftɪd/
adjective
装了柄的；带柄的
Having a haft or handle.
The archaeologist unearthed a hafted axe from the Bronze Age. 考古学家出土了一把青铜时代的带柄斧头。
The museum displayed beautifully hafted spears and arrowheads. 博物馆展出了做工精美的带柄矛和箭头。
近义词:handled, hilted
反义词:haftless


词性:adjective

词源:From Old English *hæfted, past participle of hæftan (“to fasten, fix, attach”), from Proto-Germanic *haftōną (“to fasten, seize, grasp”).  Related to *haft (“handle, hilt”).
