deglorified:/diːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪd/
adj.
失去荣耀的，被贬低的
Deprived of glory or honor; reduced in dignity or esteem.
The deglorified king was forced to live in exile.  被贬黜的国王被迫流亡国外。
The once-grand hotel now stood deglorified and abandoned. 曾经辉煌的酒店现在破败不堪，无人问津。
近义词:dishonored, humbled, shamed, degraded
反义词:glorified, exalted, honored, celebrated
词性:形容词

deglorify:/diːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/
vt.
使失去荣耀，贬低
To deprive of glory or honor; to reduce in dignity or esteem.
The scandal deglorified the politician's career.  这桩丑闻使这位政治家的职业生涯蒙羞。
The constant criticism deglorified her achievements.  不断的批评贬低了她的成就。
近义词:dishonor, humble, shame, degrade
反义词:glorify, exalt, honor, celebrate
词性:动词


词源:
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reduce") + glorify (from Latin glorificare, "to make glorious").
