icebird:/ˈaɪsbɜːrd/ | n. | 冰鸟 | A fictional or mythical bird associated with ice or cold environments.  It doesn't refer to a specific, real-world bird species. |  The legend tells of an icebird that brings winter's first snow.  传说中有一种冰鸟带来冬天的第一场雪。 | 近义词:  frostbird (fictional), snowbird | 反义词: firebird | 词性: noun
