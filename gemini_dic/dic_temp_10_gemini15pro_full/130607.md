nonadherent:/ˌnɒnədˈhɪərənt/ (US) /ˌnɑːnədˈhɪrənt/ (UK)| adj.|非粘附的，不依附的|Not sticking to something; not adhering.  Relating to a substance that does not stick to another substance, or a person who does not support a particular person, group, or belief.|The surgeon used a nonadherent dressing to cover the wound.  外科医生用非粘附性敷料覆盖伤口。|Synonyms: nonadhesive, nonsticky, unbound, unattached, independent|Antonyms: adherent, adhesive, sticky, clinging, attached|adjective

nonadherent:/ˌnɒnədˈhɪərənt/ (US) /ˌnɑːnədˈhɪrənt/ (UK)| n.|不依附者，非粘附物|A person who does not support a particular person, group, or belief; a substance that does not stick.|The nonadherents of the political party formed their own independent group.  该政党的不依附者组成了他们自己的独立团体。|Synonyms: dissenter, independent, maverick, rebel|Antonyms: adherent, follower, supporter, loyalist|noun
