architrave: /ˈɑːrkɪtreɪv/ | n. |  框缘;楣梁;线脚 | The molded frame around a doorway or window, especially the lower beam resting on the columns. | The architrave of the temple was intricately carved.  寺庙的框缘雕刻精美。 | synonyms: molding, frame, trim | antonyms: N/A | noun


architrave: /ˈɑːrkɪtreɪv/ | n. | 主框; (尤指) 门框,窗框 | The main beam resting across the tops of columns, forming the lowest part of an entablature. | The weight of the roof was supported by the strong architrave. 屋顶的重量由坚固的主框支撑。| synonyms: lintel, beam, girder | antonyms: N/A | noun
