emotive:/ɪˈmoʊtɪv/ /iˈməʊtɪv/| adj.| 感情激动的；表达感情的；引起情绪的|Arousing or able to arouse intense feeling.|Her emotive speech brought tears to many people's eyes. 她充满感情的演讲让许多人热泪盈眶。|近义词：emotional, affecting, moving, poignant|反义词：unemotional, apathetic, impassive|词性:形容词

词源: Late 19th century: from emotion + -ive.
