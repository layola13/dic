nonsupporting:/ˌnɒnsəˈpɔːtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnsəˈpɔːrtɪŋ/
adjective
不支持的；不赞助的；无支撑的
not providing support, either financially or emotionally; not giving evidence to confirm something
例句：The nonsupporting beams caused the roof to collapse.  没有支撑的梁导致屋顶坍塌。

例句：He faced criticism for his nonsupporting stance on the new policy. 他因为不支持新政策的立场而面临批评。
近义词：unsupportive, uncooperative, unhelpful
反义词：supportive, helpful, cooperative
词性: adjective
