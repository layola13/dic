nonpredatory:/ˌnɒnˈprɛdətəri/ | adj. | 非掠夺性的；非捕食性的 | Not predatory; not inclined to prey on or exploit others. |  Many herbivores are nonpredatory animals. 许多食草动物是非掠夺性的。 |  The fund invests in nonpredatory lending practices. 该基金投资于非掠夺性的贷款业务。 | 近义词: non-exploitative, harmless, benign | 反义词: predatory, exploitative, rapacious | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not"
predatory (adjective):  from Late Latin *praedatorius, from Latin praedator "plunderer," from praedari "to plunder," from praeda "booty, plunder"
