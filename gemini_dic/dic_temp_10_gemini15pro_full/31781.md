checkerboarding:/ˈtʃɛkərˌbɔːrdɪŋ/| n.|棋盘格状，交错排列|The arrangement of elements in a checkerboard pattern, alternating between two different states or types. checkerboarding:/ˈtʃɛkərˌbɔːrdɪŋ/| v.|使成棋盘格状，交错排列|To arrange something in a checkerboard pattern.
