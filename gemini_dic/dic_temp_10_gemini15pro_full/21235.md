blogs:/blɑːɡz/ /blɒɡz/
n.
博客；网络日志
A website on which an individual or group of users record opinions, information, etc. on a regular basis.
She regularly updates her blog with travel photos and stories. 她定期在她的博客上更新旅行照片和故事。
blog, weblog, online journal, internet diary
newspaper, printed journal
noun
