edentate:/iːˈdɛnteɪt/ | adj. n. | 无齿的，贫齿目动物 | Lacking teeth; belonging to the former mammalian order Edentata (now Xenarthra), which includes sloths, anteaters, and armadillos. |  The anteater is an edentate mammal. 食蚁兽是一种无齿的哺乳动物。 | Sloth, armadillo, anteater |  Toothed | adjective, noun
