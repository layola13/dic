declinator:/ˈdɛklɪˌneɪtər/| n. |赤纬调节器，磁偏角校正器|An instrument used to measure or correct for magnetic declination, especially in a compass.|The surveyor used a declinator to adjust his compass readings. 测量员使用赤纬调节器来调整他的指南针读数。|Synonyms: magnetic declination compensator, magnetic variation corrector|Antonyms: N/A|noun

declination:/ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |磁偏角，衰退，倾斜|1. The angular distance of a celestial body north or south of the celestial equator. 2. A downward slope or bend. 3. A decline or deterioration.|The declination of the star was measured using a telescope.  这颗恒星的赤纬是用望远镜测量的。|Synonyms: descent, dip, deterioration, deviation|Antonyms: ascent, incline, improvement|noun

decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/| v. n.|下降，衰退，婉拒|1. (v.) To politely refuse. 2. (v.) To decrease in quantity or importance. 3. (n.) A gradual decrease.|He declined the invitation to the party. 他婉拒了参加聚会的邀请。|Synonyms: refuse, decrease, diminish, reject|Antonyms: accept, increase, rise|verb, noun
