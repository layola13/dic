for-the-most-part:/fɔr ðə moʊst pɑːrt/| adv.|大体上，基本上，通常|Mostly; generally; in most cases.  It signifies that something is true or applies in the majority of instances, but not necessarily all.|The food at the restaurant is, for the most part, quite good.|Synonyms: mostly, generally, largely, primarily, chiefly, mainly|Antonyms: rarely, seldom, infrequently|adverb
