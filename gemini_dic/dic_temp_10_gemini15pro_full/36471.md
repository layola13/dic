coldnesses:/ˈkoʊldnəsɪz/
n.
寒冷；冷淡；冷漠
The state or quality of being cold in temperature or manner.
The coldness of his stare made her shiver. 他的眼神冰冷，让她不寒而栗。
近义词: frigidity, iciness, aloofness, indifference
反义词: warmth, friendliness, cordiality
词性: 名词(plural)
