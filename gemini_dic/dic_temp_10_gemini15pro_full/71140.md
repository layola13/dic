fishskin:/ˈfɪʃˌskɪn/| n. |鱼皮|The skin of a fish. It can be used in various ways, including for leather, food, or as a byproduct of fish processing.|The fishskin was surprisingly tough and durable.|synonyms: fish hide, scale (when referring to individual scales)|antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "fish flesh" or "fish fillet" as contrasting elements)|noun
