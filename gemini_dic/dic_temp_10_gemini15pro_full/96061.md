indecisiveness:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/| n.|优柔寡断，犹豫不决|The state of being unable to make decisions quickly and effectively.|His indecisiveness cost him the opportunity. 他的优柔寡断让他错失了机会。|近义词：hesitancy, vacillation, irresolution|反义词: decisiveness, resolution, determination|词性:noun

Etymology: From indecisive + -ness.  "Indecisive" comes from the French indécisif, from in- "not" + décider "to decide," ultimately from Latin decidere "to cut off, determine."
