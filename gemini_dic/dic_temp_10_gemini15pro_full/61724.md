emotionlessly:/ɪˈmoʊʃənləsli/| adv.|毫无感情地，冷漠地|Without emotion; in an impassive manner.  Derived from "emotion" + "-less" (lacking) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The root word "emotion" comes from Old French "emouvoir" meaning "to stir up."|She delivered the news emotionlessly, her face betraying no sign of grief. 她面无表情地宣布了这个消息，脸上没有流露出任何悲伤。|He watched the demolition of his childhood home emotionlessly. 他冷漠地看着儿时住所被拆除。|近义词：apathetically, indifferently, blankly, stoically|反义词：emotionally, passionately, expressively|词性: 副词
