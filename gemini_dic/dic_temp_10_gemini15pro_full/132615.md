nondisabled:/ˌnɒndɪsˈeɪb(ə)ld/
adj.
非残疾的
Not having a disability; able-bodied.
例句：The nondisabled often fail to understand the challenges faced by people with disabilities.  非残疾人往往无法理解残疾人面临的挑战。

例句：The architect designed the building to be accessible to both nondisabled and disabled individuals.  建筑师设计的建筑物既方便非残疾人，也方便残疾人。
近义词：able-bodied, non-disabled
反义词：disabled, handicapped
词性：adjective
