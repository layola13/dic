multinationalisms:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/| n.|多国主义（现象或意识形态）|The advocacy of or support for the interests of multiple nations or nationalities within a single political entity, or the existence of multiple nations or nationalities within a single political entity.|The rise of multinationalisms in the region has led to increased political instability.| 近义词: cosmopolitanism, internationalism, globalism | 反义词: nationalism, parochialism | 词性: Noun

Etymology:
From multinational + -ism.  "Multinational" comes from multi- (many) + national (relating to a nation).  "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns that refer to doctrines, beliefs, principles, or practices of a particular kind.
