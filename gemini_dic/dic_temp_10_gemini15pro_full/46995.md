dash-off:/ˈdæʃ ɒf/ (UK) /ˈdæʃ ɔːf/ (US)| v. n.|匆匆完成；草草写成；即兴之作|To write or create something quickly and easily, often without much care or attention to detail.  Also, something produced quickly and without much effort.|I dashed off a quick email before leaving for the meeting. 我在去开会之前匆匆写了一封电子邮件。|synonyms: scribble, jot down, whip up, throw together|antonyms: elaborate, painstakingly create|verb

dash-off:/ˈdæʃ ɒf/ (UK) /ˈdæʃ ɔːf/ (US)| n.|匆匆完成的作品；即兴之作|Something that has been written or created quickly and easily.|Her painting was a quick dash-off, but it still captured the essence of the scene. 她的画是即兴之作，但仍然抓住了场景的精髓。|synonyms: sketch, draft|antonyms: masterpiece, finished work|noun
