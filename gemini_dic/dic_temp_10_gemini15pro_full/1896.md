act-on:/ˈækt ɑːn/| phrasal verb | 采取行动，对…起作用，按照…行事 | The medicine acts on the central nervous system. 这种药物作用于中枢神经系统。 The enzyme acts on the protein. 这种酶对蛋白质起作用。 | 近义词：affect, influence, impact | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, depends on context) | verb phrase


USAGE->
Present Simple:
The police are acting on a tip-off. = 警方正在根据线报采取行动。
This drug acts on the inflammation. = 这种药对炎症起作用。

Present Continuous:
The committee is acting on the complaints it has received. = 委员会正在处理收到的投诉。
The doctor is acting on the test results. = 医生正在根据测试结果采取行动。


Present Perfect:
The government has acted on the advice of its scientific advisors. = 政府已经根据其科学顾问的建议采取了行动。
We have acted on your suggestions. = 我们已经根据您的建议采取了行动。

Past Simple:
They acted on their lawyer's advice. = 他们按照律师的建议行事。
The company acted on the customer's feedback. = 公司根据客户的反馈采取了行动。


Past Continuous:
The team was acting on the instructions from the manager. = 团队正在按照经理的指示行事。
He was acting on his best instincts. = 他凭着自己最好的直觉行事。

Past Perfect:
They had acted on the information before it was too late. = 他们在为时已晚之前根据信息采取了行动。
We had already acted on the complaint when he called. = 他打电话时我们已经处理了投诉。


Future Simple:
We will act on this information immediately. = 我们会立即根据此信息采取行动。
The board will act on the recommendations next week. = 董事会下周将根据建议采取行动。

Future Continuous:

The government will be acting on the report's findings in the near future. = 政府将在不久的将来根据报告的调查结果采取行动。
