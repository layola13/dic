overfurnishes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃɪz/
verb
过度装修；放置过多家具
to furnish (a room, house, etc.) with too much furniture
The room was dark and overfurnished.  这个房间很暗，而且家具太多。

近义词: overcrowd, clutter
反义词: underfurnish

词性: verb
