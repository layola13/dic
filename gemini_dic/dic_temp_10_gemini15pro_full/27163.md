callable:/ˈkɔːləbl/| adj. n.|可调用的，可赎回的；（债券）可提前兑付的|Something that is callable can be called, summoned, or easily brought into action.  In finance, it refers to a type of security (like a bond) that can be redeemed by the issuer before its maturity date.|例句：The bonds are callable after five years. 这些债券五年后可以赎回。|近义词：redeemable, due, payable|反义词:noncallable, irredeemable|词性:形容词

callable:/ˈkɔːləbl/| n.|可调用对象|In programming, a callable is an object that can be called like a function, such as a function, method, class, or lambda expression.|例句：In Python, functions and classes are callables. 在Python中，函数和类都是可调用对象。|近义词：invocable|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
