genuflecting:/ˈdʒenjʊˌflektɪŋ/
| adj. | 跪拜的，屈膝的 | The act of bending one knee to the ground, typically in worship or as a sign of respect. |  He was genuflecting before the altar. 他在祭坛前屈膝。 | 近义词：kneeling, bowing | 反义词：standing, erect | 词性：形容词（present participle）

genuflect:/ˈdʒenjʊˌflekt/
| vi. | 跪拜，屈膝 | To bend one knee to the ground, typically in worship or as a sign of respect. |  She genuflected before the queen. 她在女王面前屈膝。| 近义词：kneel, bow | 反义词：stand | 词性：动词

词源: From Late Latin *genuflectere, from Latin genu 'knee' + flectere 'to bend.'
