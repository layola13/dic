chancers:/ˈtʃɑːnsəz/ (UK), /ˈtʃænsərz/ (US)
n.|投机分子；碰运气的人|People who take risks, especially in business or financial matters, hoping to gain something even if the chances of success are small.  They often lack skill or experience but rely on luck. |Those chancers thought they could get rich quick in the stock market.|近义词: gamblers, risk-takers, opportunists|反义词:  cautious people, planners|词性:名词复数
