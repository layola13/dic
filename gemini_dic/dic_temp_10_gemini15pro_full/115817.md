markednesses:/ˈmɑːrkɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.|显著性；标记性（通常指语言特征的显著性或非典型性）|The markednesses of certain grammatical features can make them difficult for learners to acquire.  某些语法特征的显著性会使学习者难以习得。|例句：The plural form of nouns is marked by the addition of -s, while the singular form is unmarked.  名词的复数形式通过添加 -s 来标记，而单数形式则未标记。|近义词：salience, prominence, distinctiveness|反义词：unmarkedness, typicality|词性:名词
