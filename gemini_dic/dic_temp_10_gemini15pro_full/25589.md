bummalo:/ˈbʌməloʊ/
n.
鰹魚乾，煙燻鰹魚
dried, smoked bonito (a type of tuna).
Maldivian fishermen traditionally make bummalo by smoking and sun-drying skipjack tuna. 馬爾地夫漁民傳統上通過煙熏和曬乾鰹魚來製作鰹魚乾。
Fishermen sell their catch of the day and preserve the rest by making bummalo. 漁民會賣掉當天捕獲的魚，剩下的則製成鰹魚乾保存。
近义词:smoked fish, dried fish
反义词:fresh fish
词性:noun
