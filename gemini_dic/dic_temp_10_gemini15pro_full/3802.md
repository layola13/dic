agnoiology:/ˌæɡnɔɪˈɒlədʒi/
n.
不可知论
The study of what is inherently unknowable.
Agnoiology explores the limits of human knowledge and the nature of the unknowable.  不可知论探讨了人类知识的极限和不可知事物的本质。
Agnoiology is a fascinating field for philosophers and theologians.  对于哲学家和神学家来说，不可知论是一个引人入胜的领域。
近义词: skepticism, nescience
反义词: gnosticism, epistemology
词性: noun
