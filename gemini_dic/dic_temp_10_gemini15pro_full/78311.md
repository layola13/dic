geronimo:/dʒəˈrɒnɪməʊ/ (UK) /dʒəˈrɑːnɪmoʊ/ (US)| interj. n.| 振奋人心的呐喊；杰罗尼莫（美国土著阿帕奇部落领袖）|Used as a battle cry, especially when jumping from a great height. Also the name of a famous Apache leader.|Example 1: He shouted "Geronimo!" and jumped out of the plane. 他大喊一声“杰罗尼莫！”然后跳出了飞机。

Example 2: Geronimo led his people in resistance against the US Army. 杰罗尼莫带领他的人民抵抗美国军队。|近义词：battle cry, war cry|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|词性: interjection, noun
