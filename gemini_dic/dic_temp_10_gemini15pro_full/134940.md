nonmystics:/ˌnɒnˈmɪstɪks/ | n. | 非神秘主义者 |  People who do not believe in or practice mysticism.  Those who focus on practical or rational approaches rather than spiritual or intuitive ones. |  The nonmystics in the group focused on the scientific data rather than personal experiences.  这个团体中的非神秘主义者专注于科学数据而不是个人经验。 | Synonyms: pragmatists, rationalists, skeptics | Antonyms: mystics, spiritualists | Noun
