comanage:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒ/
verb
共同管理，协同管理
To manage something together with one or more other people.
We decided to comanage the project to share the workload and responsibility. 我们决定共同管理这个项目，以分担工作量和责任。
The IT department and the security team comanage the company's network infrastructure. IT部门和安全团队共同管理公司的网络基础设施。
近义词: co-administer, jointly manage, share management
反义词: solely manage, independently manage
词性: verb

co- + manage
co-: prefix meaning "together," "jointly," "mutually"
manage: from Old French *maneger "to handle," especially horses, from Italian maneggiare "to handle," especially a horse, from mano "hand," from Latin manus "hand."
