eurosceptical: /ˌjʊərəʊˈskɛptɪkəl/ | adj. | 欧洲怀疑主义的 | Opposed to increasing the powers of the European Union. |  The UK has a reputation for being eurosceptical. 英国以其对欧盟持怀疑态度而闻名。|  Many eurosceptical voters opted to leave the EU. 许多对欧盟持怀疑态度的选民选择离开欧盟。| synonyms: anti-EU, anti-European | antonyms: pro-European, Europhile | adjective
