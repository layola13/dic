ectental:/ɛkˈtɛntəl/
adjective
外延的；可延伸的
Relating to or designating the outer limits of something; capable of being extended.
The ectental membrane of the cell began to expand.  细胞的外延膜开始扩张。

近义词:extensive, expandable, outward
反义词:internal, limited, inward
词性:adjective


词源:
From Latin *ectentus, from ex- ‘out’ + tentus ‘stretched, extended’ (past participle of tendere ‘to stretch’). Coined in the late 19th or early 20th century.
