hyperdevelopments:/ˌhaɪpərˌdɪˈvɛləpmənts/| n. |过度发展；超常发展|Excessive or accelerated development, often beyond what is considered normal or sustainable.  It implies a rapid and intense growth, often with negative connotations related to imbalance or unsustainable practices.|The city's hyperdevelopments led to traffic congestion and a shortage of affordable housing. 城市的过度发展导致交通拥堵和经济适用房短缺。| The coastal hyperdevelopments have destroyed natural habitats.  沿海地区的过度发展破坏了自然栖息地。 |近义词：overdevelopment, excessive growth, rapid expansion|反义词:underdevelopment, stagnation|词性:名词

Etymology:  "Hyper-" (Greek) meaning "over, excessive" + "development" (from Old French "developper").
