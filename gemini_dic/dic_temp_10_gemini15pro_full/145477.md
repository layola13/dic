overcleanness:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛn.nɪs/
n.|过度清洁；洁癖|Excessive cleanliness, often to an unhealthy or obsessive degree. |She suffered from overcleanness, washing her hands dozens of times a day.|近义词:fastidiousness, meticulousness, overscrupulousness|反义词:dirtiness, uncleanliness, filthiness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "cleanness" (the state of being clean).  "Clean" comes from Old English "clæne," meaning "free from dirt or defilement."
