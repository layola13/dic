/ˈbruːzɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人痛苦的，费力的，伤痕累累的 |  Causing physical or emotional pain; requiring a lot of effort. /ˈbruːzɪŋ/ | n. |  擦伤; 挫伤 (Usually used in plural form "bruisings") | An injury where the skin is discolored, but not broken. | He had several bruisings on his legs after the fall. 他摔倒后腿上有多处擦伤。| contusion, abrasion | healing | noun
