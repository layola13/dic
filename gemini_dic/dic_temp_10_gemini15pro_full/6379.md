amphictyons:/æmˈfɪktiənz/| n. |同盟代表；神圣同盟成员|A member of an amphictyony, a league of ancient Greek tribes or states formed for the protection of a religious center.  These leagues often had political and military functions as well.|The amphictyons met twice a year to discuss matters of mutual interest, including religious festivals and trade.|近义词：delegates, representatives, council members|反义词: (No clear antonym, perhaps "enemies" in a specific context)|noun
