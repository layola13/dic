duetto:/duˈɛtoʊ/ /djuˈɛtoʊ/
n.
二重唱，二重奏
A piece of music written for two performers or singers.
The opera featured a beautiful duetto between the two main characters. 这部歌剧以两位主角之间美丽的二重唱为特色。
synonyms: duet, duo
antonyms: solo, chorus
noun
