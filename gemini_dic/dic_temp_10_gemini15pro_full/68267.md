fakeer:/fəˈkɪər/| n. |托钵僧；苦行僧|A Muslim or Hindu religious mendicant or ascetic.  A person who lives by begging, especially one who claims to have magical powers.|例句：The fakeer sat cross-legged on the ground, meditating.  托钵僧盘腿坐在地上冥想。

例句：He gave a few coins to the wandering fakeer. 他给了那个流浪的托钵僧几个硬币。|近义词：mendicant, ascetic, beggar, dervish|反义词:rich man, wealthy person|词性:noun
