hydrargyrism:/ˌhaɪdrɑːrˈdʒɪrɪzəm/
n.
汞中毒，水银中毒
Mercury poisoning, a condition caused by exposure to mercury or its compounds.
He suffered from hydrargyrism after working in a mercury mine for many years. 他在汞矿工作多年后患上了汞中毒。
近义词: mercury poisoning, mercurialism
反义词: (None, as it's a specific medical condition)
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  hydr- +‎ argyr- +‎ -ism.  Hydr- comes from Greek  ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”), referring to mercury's liquid state.  Argyr- comes from Greek ἄργυρος (árguros, “silver”), referring to mercury's silvery appearance.  -ism denotes a condition or doctrine.
