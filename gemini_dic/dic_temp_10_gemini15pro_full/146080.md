overeditorialized: /ˌoʊvərˌedɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度编辑的
Having been edited too much, often to the point of being excessive or unnecessary.
The article was overeditorialized and lost the author's original voice. 这篇文章被过度编辑，失去了作者原本的声音。

The manuscript was so overeditorialized that it felt sterile and lifeless.  这份手稿被过度编辑，以至于感觉枯燥无味。

近义词: overedited, overworked
反义词: underedited

词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "editorialize" (verb meaning "to express opinions rather than just report facts") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
