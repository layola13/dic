mayonnaise:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪz/ | n. |蛋黄酱; 千岛酱 |A thick, creamy dressing made from oil, egg yolks, lemon juice or vinegar, and seasonings. |例句：I like mayonnaise on my sandwich. 我喜欢在三明治里加蛋黄酱。

例句：She made a delicious mayonnaise from scratch. 她亲手做了一份美味的蛋黄酱。|近义词：aioli |反义词: (没有直接的反义词) |词性: noun
