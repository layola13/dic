devoting:/dɪˈvoʊtɪŋ/
verb
奉献，致力于
giving all or a large part of one's time or resources to (a person, activity, or cause).
She is devoting her life to helping others. 她将自己的一生都奉献给了帮助他人。
He is devoting his time and energy to his new business. 他将自己的时间和精力都投入到他的新事业中。
dedicating, committing, allotting
neglecting, ignoring
verb
