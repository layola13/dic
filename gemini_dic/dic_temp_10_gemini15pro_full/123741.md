moonstricken:/ˈmuːnˌstrɪkən/| adj.|月光下的，受月神影响而精神错乱的，呆呆的| Affected by the moon, especially in a way that suggests madness or romantic enchantment.  Literally "struck by the moon."|She wandered the moors, a moonstricken figure lost in her dreams. 她漫步在荒野上，一个月光下的身影沉浸在她的梦中。| The moonstricken lovers whispered sweet nothings to each other.  沉醉于月色的情侣互相低语着甜言蜜语。|近义词：lovesick, dreamy, dazed, moonstruck|反义词：lucid, rational, clear-headed|词性:形容词
