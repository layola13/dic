forespeaking:/fɔːrˈspiːkɪŋ/
verb
预言；预示；征兆；预定；先于…说话
anticipating or indicating something in advance; betokening; predestining; speaking before another person does.
The dark clouds forespoke a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。
His nervousness forespoke his failure in the exam. 他的紧张预示了他考试的失败。

synonym
predict, foretell, presage, portend, augur, foreshadow
antonym
retrospect

verb
提前说；先于…说
speaking before another person does
She forespoke his name before he introduced himself. 在他自我介绍之前，她先说了他的名字。
The chairman forespoke the main points of the meeting. 主席预先说了会议的要点。

synonym
precede, anticipate
antonym
follow
