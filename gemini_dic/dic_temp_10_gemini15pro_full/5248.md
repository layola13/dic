allochroite:/ˈæləˌkroʊaɪt/
n.
翠榴石，异色榴石
A variety of andradite garnet, usually brownish red and containing small amounts of chromium.  A variety of andradite garnet, characterized by its brownish-red color often due to chromium content.
Allochroite is a rare and beautiful gemstone sought after by collectors. 翠榴石是一种稀有而美丽的宝石，受到收藏家的追捧。
This allochroite displays a distinctive brownish red hue. 这颗翠榴石展现出独特的棕红色调。
Synonyms: andradite, garnet
