misgracious:/mɪsˈɡreɪʃəs/
adjective
不礼貌的，没教养的，粗鲁的
lacking graciousness or courtesy; rude and unpleasant.
例句：He gave a misgracious response to her kind offer. 他对她的好意作出了粗鲁的回应。
例句：It was misgracious of him to ignore her greeting. 他无视她的问候，真是失礼。
近义词：rude, impolite, ungracious, discourteous, uncivil
反义词：gracious, polite, courteous, civil
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- (expressing wrongness or lack) + gracious.  The word "gracious" comes from the Old French "gracieux," ultimately derived from the Latin "gratia" meaning "grace" or "favor."
