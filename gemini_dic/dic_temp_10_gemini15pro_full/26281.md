buttress:/ˈbʌtrəs/ (US), /ˈbʌtrɪs/ (UK)| n. vt.|支撑，扶壁；支持，加强|A structure, typically of brick or stone, built against a wall to strengthen or support it.  A source of defense or support.|The cathedral walls are supported by huge stone buttresses.  这座大教堂的墙壁由巨大的石质扶壁支撑。|Synonyms: support, prop, reinforcement, brace, shore, underpinning|Antonyms: weaken, undermine|noun

buttress:/ˈbʌtrəs/ (US), /ˈbʌtrɪs/ (UK)| vt.|支持，加强|To strengthen or support something.|The government's economic policy was buttressed by strong support from the business community. 政府的经济政策得到了商界的大力支持。|Synonyms: support, reinforce, strengthen, bolster, fortify, underpin|Antonyms: weaken, undermine|verb
