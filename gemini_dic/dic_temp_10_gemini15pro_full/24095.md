brevity:/ˈbrevəti/ | n. |简洁，简短|The quality of being brief or concise.|The brevity of her speech was appreciated by the audience. 她的演讲简短，受到了观众的赞赏。|The brevity of his response surprised me. 他回答的简短让我感到惊讶。|近义词:conciseness, terseness, succinctness|反义词:length, prolixity, verbosity|词性:名词

词源:
From Latin *breviatus*, past participle of *breviare* "to shorten," from *brevis* "short."
