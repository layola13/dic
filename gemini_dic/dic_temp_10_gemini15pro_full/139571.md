obvention:/ɒbˈvɛnʃən/ /ɑːbˈvɛnʃən/ | n. |意外的收入；临时收入；偶然的收入；（尤指教会的）俸禄，收入；（旧时）阻止，防止|A church obvention is a payment made to a church, especially by someone who is not a member of it, for a particular service.  An obvention can also be any incidental or occasional payment, gain, or revenue.| The small church relied heavily on obventions from weddings and funerals. 这个小教堂严重依赖婚礼和葬礼的临时收入。 |Synonyms: income, revenue, proceeds, receipts, earnings, takings, remuneration, compensation, emolument, perquisite|Antonyms: expenditure, expense, cost, outlay|noun
