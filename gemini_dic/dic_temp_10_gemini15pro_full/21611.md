blunted:/ˈblʌntɪd/| adj.|变钝的，迟钝的，减弱的|Having a dull edge or point; not sharp.  Also, weakened or less intense.|The knife was blunted from overuse.  这把刀因为过度使用而变钝了。|近义词：dull, weakened, subdued, muffled|反义词:sharp, keen, intense, heightened|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *blunten*, from Old English *blunt* (“blunt, obtuse”), from Proto-Germanic *bluntaz* (“blunt”), of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse *blunda* (“to close one's eyes, doze”).
