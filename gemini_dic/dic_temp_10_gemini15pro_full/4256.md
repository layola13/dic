airship:/ˈeəʃɪp/ (US), /ˈɛːʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|飞艇|A lighter-than-air aircraft that can be steered and propelled through the air using engines. It consists of a large gas-filled bag or envelope, which provides buoyancy, along with a gondola or cabin for passengers and crew, and propellers or other propulsion systems.|The airship floated gracefully across the sky. 飞艇优雅地飘过天空。

The Hindenburg was a famous, but ill-fated, airship.  兴登堡号是一艘著名的但命运多舛的飞艇。
