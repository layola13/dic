outwitted:/ˌaʊtˈwɪtɪd/
verb
以智取胜，智胜
defeated someone by being more clever or cunning than them
He outwitted his opponents by anticipating their next move. 他预料到对手的下一步行动，从而以智取胜。
近义词: outsmarted, tricked, deceived, fooled
反义词: outmaneuvered,  defeated


词性: verb (past tense and past participle of outwit)

词源:  "out-" (meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "wit" (meaning "intelligence," "cleverness")
