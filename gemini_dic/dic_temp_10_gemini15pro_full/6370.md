amphiboly:/æmˈfɪbəli/ | n. |  歧义，模棱两可 | A statement or phrase that can be interpreted in more than one way due to its grammatical structure. |  The sentence "I saw the man with the telescope" is an example of amphiboly; did I use the telescope to see the man, or did the man have the telescope?  我用望远镜看到了那个人，还是那个人拿着望远镜？ | ambiguity, equivocation | clarity, precision | noun
