endorhizous:/ɛn.doʊˈraɪ.zəs/
| adj. | 内生菌根的 | Growing or living within the roots of the host plant; specifically designating a type of mycorrhiza in which the fungus penetrates the cortical cells of the roots. |  The study focuses on endorhizous fungi and their role in nutrient uptake.  这项研究的重点是内生菌根真菌及其在营养吸收中的作用。| Synonyms: endomycorrhizal | Antonyms: ectomycorrhizal | Adjective


endorhizous:/ɛn.doʊˈraɪ.zəs/
| adj. | 内生菌根的 |  Characterized by the presence of endomycorrhizae, a type of symbiotic relationship between fungi and plant roots where the fungus grows inside the root cells. |  Endorhizous fungi form a mutually beneficial relationship with their host plants. 内生菌根真菌与其宿主植物形成互惠互利的关系。 | Synonyms: endomycorrhizal | Antonyms: ectomycorrhizal | Adjective
