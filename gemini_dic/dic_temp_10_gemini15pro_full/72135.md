floodings:/ˈflʌdɪŋz/
noun
洪水，泛滥
An overflowing of water onto land that is normally dry.
The heavy rains caused severe floodings in the region. 大雨导致该地区严重洪水。

近义词:floods, inundations, deluges
反义词:droughts
词性:noun
