disproportion:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃən/ |n.| 不均衡，不成比例，不相称 |A lack of balance or equality between things; an instance of this.|  The report highlights the disproportion between the number of hours worked and the salaries paid.  这份报告强调了工作时间和工资支付之间的不均衡。| synonyms: imbalance, inequality, disparity, asymmetry | antonyms: balance, equality, proportion, symmetry | noun


disproportions:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənz/ |n.| 不均衡，不成比例，不相称（复数）| Instances of a lack of balance or equality between things. | There are huge disproportions in funding between different schools. 不同学校之间的资金分配存在巨大差异。| synonyms: imbalances, inequalities, disparities, asymmetries | antonyms: balances, equalities, proportions, symmetries | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (not, opposite of) + proportion (from Latin proportio, meaning "comparative relation, analogy, portion," from pro "for" + portio "share, portion").
