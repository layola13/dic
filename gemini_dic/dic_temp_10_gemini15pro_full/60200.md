effing:/ˈɛfɪŋ/
adjective
该死的；他妈的；讨厌的
A euphemism for "fucking," used to avoid saying the actual word, but still conveying a strong sense of annoyance or anger.
例句：That effing computer keeps crashing!  这该死的电脑老是崩溃！

例句：Where's the effing remote control?  那讨厌的遥控器在哪儿？

近义词：bloody, damn, blasted
反义词：wonderful, excellent
