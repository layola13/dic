husten:/ˈhuːstən/ (German)| v. |咳嗽|To cough; to expel air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound.|例句：Er musste husten.  He had to cough.

例句：Ich huste die ganze Nacht. I've been coughing all night.


近义词：cough|反义词: (None for a bodily function)|词性: verb
