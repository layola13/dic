enology:/iːˈnɑːlədʒi/ /iːˈnɒlədʒi/
n.
葡萄酒酿造学; 葡萄酒学
The science and study of all aspects of wine and winemaking, excluding viticulture (the cultivation of grapevines).
Enology is a complex subject, encompassing everything from grape growing to fermentation. 葡萄酒酿造学是一门复杂的学科，涵盖了从葡萄种植到发酵的所有内容。
He studied enology in France. 他在法国学习葡萄酒酿造学。
近义词:viniculture, winemaking
反义词: N/A
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *œnologie*, from Ancient Greek *οἶνος* (*oînos*, “wine”) + *-λογία* (*-logía*, “-logy”).
