misdialing:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəlɪŋ/
n.
误拨，拨错号码
The act of dialing a telephone number incorrectly.
I keep misdialing his number. 我一直拨错他的号码。
misdial, wrong number
correct dialing
noun

misdial:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəl/
v.
拨错（电话号码）
To dial a telephone number incorrectly.
I misdialed and got a wrong number. 我拨错号码了，打到了一个陌生人那里。
dial incorrectly, dial wrong, get a wrong number
dial correctly
verb
