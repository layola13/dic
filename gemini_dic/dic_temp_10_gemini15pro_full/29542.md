catch-up:/ˈkætʃ ʌp/ (US) /ˈkætʃ ʌp/ (UK)| n.|追赶；赶上；弥补；补课|An act of catching up, especially in work or studies.  A meeting or conversation to exchange information.|I need to have a catch-up with my team to discuss the project's progress.  我需要和我的团队开个会，讨论一下项目的进展。|近义词：update, review, briefing|反义词:fall behind|noun


catch-up:/ˈkætʃ ʌp/ (US) /ˈkætʃ ʌp/ (UK)| adj.|追赶的；弥补的|Designed to allow someone to catch up.|I attended a catch-up class after missing a week of school. 我缺课一周后参加了补习班。|近义词：remedial, supplementary|反义词:advanced, ahead|adjective
