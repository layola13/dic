antifeudalism:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪzəm/| n. |反封建主义|Opposition to or the system of abolishing feudalism, characterized by a hierarchical society based on land ownership and the services of serfs.|The French Revolution was a period of intense antifeudalism.|Synonyms: anti-feudalism, counter-feudalism|Antonyms: feudalism|Noun
