blur:/blɜːr/ /blɜːr/| v. n.| 模糊，污点| The rain blurred the view from the window. 雨水模糊了窗外的景色。|  The events of that night were a blur. 那晚的 events 很模糊。|近义词：obscure, dim, soften, fuzz|反义词：sharpen, focus, clarify|词性: 动词/名词


词源: From Middle English *blouren, *bluren (“to dim, make unclear”), from *blure (“a dimness, a mist”), probably of imitative origin.
