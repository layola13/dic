newspaperless:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpər.ləs/ /ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər.ləs/
adjective
无报纸的；不用报纸的
Existing or operating without newspapers.  A future where news is consumed digitally.
The library is aiming for a newspaperless future, providing all its news sources online. 图书馆的目标是未来不再使用报纸，所有新闻来源都将在线提供。
Several companies are exploring newspaperless offices to reduce paper waste and promote sustainability.  一些公司正在探索无纸化办公，以减少纸张浪费并促进可持续发展。
近义词:digital, online, electronic
反义词:paper-based, print
词性:adjective
