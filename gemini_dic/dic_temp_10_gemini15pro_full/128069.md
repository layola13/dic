nerves:/nɜːrvz/| n.|神经；勇气；紧张|a whitish fiber or bundle of fibers that transmits impulses of sensation to the brain or spinal cord, and impulses from these to the muscles and organs.|例句：The dentist touched a nerve and I flinched. 牙医碰到了我的神经，我畏缩了一下。

例句：It takes a lot of nerve to stand up to a bully.  对抗恶霸需要很大的勇气。|近义词：courage, fortitude, anxiety, tension|反义词:calmness, composure|Noun
