blameless:/ˈbleɪmləs/| adj.|无罪的；无辜的；无可指责的|Free from blame or guilt; innocent.|He was found to be blameless in the incident. 他被发现与此事件无关，是无辜的。|近义词：innocent, guiltless, irreproachable, impeccable|反义词：guilty, culpable, blameworthy|adjective

blameless:/ˈbleɪmləs/| adj.|无瑕疵的；完美的|Without fault or defect; perfect.|Her performance was blameless. 她的表现无可挑剔。|近义词：perfect, flawless, immaculate, faultless|反义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blameles*, from Old English *blāmlēas* (“blameless, innocent, without spot”), equivalent to blame +‎ -less. Cognate with Dutch *blaamloos* (“blameless”), German *blamalos* (“blameless”).
