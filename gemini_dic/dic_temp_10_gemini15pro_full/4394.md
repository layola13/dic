alamo:/ˈæləmoʊ/| n.|阿拉莫|A Spanish mission in San Antonio, Texas, that was the site of a famous battle in 1836.  It is now a museum and a symbol of Texan independence.|Remember the Alamo!  记住阿拉莫！|例句：The Alamo is a popular tourist destination. 阿拉莫是一个热门的旅游目的地。

例句：The battle of the Alamo was a pivotal moment in Texas history. 阿拉莫之战是德克萨斯州历史上的关键时刻。|近义词：mission, fort, fortress|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *álamo* "poplar tree," ultimately from Latin *alnus*.  The mission was named after a nearby grove of cottonwood trees, which are a type of poplar.
