naggers:/ˈnæɡərz/
n.
爱唠叨的人；吹毛求疵的人
People who nag; persistent complainers.
My aunt is one of those naggers who always finds something to criticize. 我的姑姑是那种总是能找到东西批评的爱唠叨的人。

The coach was a real nagger, constantly reminding the team to work harder.  教练真是个爱唠叨的人，不断提醒队员们要更加努力。

近义词: complainers, critics, faultfinders
反义词:  approvers, praisers
词性: 名词 (plural)
