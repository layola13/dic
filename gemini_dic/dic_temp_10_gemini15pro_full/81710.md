greenish:/ˈɡriːnɪʃ/| adj.|浅绿色的，略带绿色的|Slightly green in color.|The water in the pond had a greenish tinge. 池塘里的水略带绿色。|近义词：light green, olive, jade|反义词:reddish, bluish|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From green + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" forms adjectives meaning "somewhat" or "slightly".  The word "green" comes from Old English "grēne," of Germanic origin, related to "grow."
