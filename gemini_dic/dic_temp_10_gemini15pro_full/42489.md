countergovernments:/ˌkaʊntərˈɡʌvərnmənts/
n.
反政府；影子内阁；对立政府
a government established in opposition to another, usually existing one.
The rebels formed a countergovernment in the occupied territory.  叛军在占领区成立了一个反政府。
The exiled political party established a countergovernment. 流亡的政党建立了一个影子内阁。

Synonyms: shadow government, rival government, opposition government
Antonyms: legitimate government, incumbent government
