na:/nɑː/ | interj. | 不, 没有 | Used to express negation or refusal, especially in informal contexts. | Na, I don't want to go. 不，我不想去。| 近义词: no, nope | 反义词: yes, yeah | 词性: interjection

na:/nɑː/ | adj. | 没有的，缺乏的 | Lacking or deficient in something. (Scottish English and dialectal) | He's na use to me. 他对我没用。| 近义词: lacking, deficient | 反义词: having, possessing | 词性: adjective
