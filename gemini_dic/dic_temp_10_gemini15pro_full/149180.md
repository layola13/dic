oxycaproic:/ˌɒksɪkəˈproʊɪk/ | adj. | 氧己酸的；6-羟基己酸的 |  Relating to or designating 6-hydroxyhexanoic acid, a cyclic ester-forming hydroxy acid. It forms the monomer for polycaprolactone. |  Oxycaproic acid is used in the production of biodegradable polymers. 氧己酸用于生产可生物降解的聚合物。 | Synonyms: 6-hydroxyhexanoic acid | Antonyms: None | adjective
