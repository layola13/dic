interannual:/ˌɪntərˈænjuəl/
adjective
年际的
Occurring between years; relating to variation over a period of years.
The interannual variability of rainfall can impact agricultural yields.  年际降雨量的变化会影响农业产量。
Scientists are studying the interannual changes in sea surface temperature. 科学家正在研究海面温度的年际变化。
Near synonyms: multiyear, year-to-year
Antonyms: intra-annual, seasonal
Word Type: Adjective
