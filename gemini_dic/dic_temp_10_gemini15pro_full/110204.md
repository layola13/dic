life-giver:/ˈlaɪfˌɡɪvər/| n.|生命给予者；赋予生命者|Someone or something that gives life, often used metaphorically.  It can refer to a deity, a natural force, or even a person who brings positive change and inspiration.|The sun is often seen as a life-giver to our planet. 太阳通常被视为我们星球的生命给予者。

The doctor who performed the life-saving surgery was hailed as a life-giver.  实施了救命手术的医生被誉为生命给予者。|近义词：creator, originator, source of life|反义词：destroyer, taker of life|词性：名词
