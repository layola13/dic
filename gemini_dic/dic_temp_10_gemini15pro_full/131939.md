noncontractual:/ˌnɒnkənˈtræktʃuəl/
adjective
非契约的，非合同的
Not based on or regulated by a formal contract.
This is a noncontractual arrangement between the two parties.  这是双方之间的一个非契约性安排。
The agreement was noncontractual, based solely on trust.  该协议是非契约性的，完全基于信任。
informal, unwritten, nonbinding
contractual, formal, binding
adjective
