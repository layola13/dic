echopractic:/ˌekəʊˈpræktɪk/ | adj. | 模仿实践的，模仿练习的 | Relating to or involving the practice of imitating or repeating sounds or actions. |  The echopractic exercises helped the students improve their pronunciation. 模仿练习帮助学生提高了他们的发音。|  The therapist used echopractic methods to help the child with speech difficulties. 治疗师使用模仿练习的方法来帮助有语言障碍的孩子。| Near synonyms: imitative, mimetic | Antonyms: original, creative | word type: adjective
