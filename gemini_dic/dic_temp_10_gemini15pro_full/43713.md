creditworthy: /ˈkrɛdɪtˌwɜːrði/ | adj. | 值得信赖的；信用可靠的；有信誉的 |  Able to be trusted to pay back money that is owed.  A creditworthy individual or company is considered a good risk for lending or extending credit. |  Example 1: The bank determined that the business was creditworthy and approved their loan application.  这家银行认定该企业信用良好，并批准了他们的贷款申请。

Example 2:  Only creditworthy customers are offered our premium credit card. 只有信用可靠的客户才能获得我们的高级信用卡。 | Synonyms: reliable, dependable, trustworthy, solvent | Antonyms: untrustworthy, unreliable, insolvent, risky | Word type: Adjective
