homilist:/ˈhɑːməlɪst/ | n. |布道者；讲道者| A person who delivers homilies or sermons; a preacher. |  The homilist delivered a powerful message about forgiveness. 布道者发表了一篇关于宽恕的强有力的信息。

The young homilist inspired the congregation with his passion.  年轻的讲道者以他的热情激励了会众。| 近义词: preacher, minister, sermonizer | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *homilista, from Ancient Greek ὁμιλητής (homilētḗs, “one who converses, companion”), from ὁμιλέω (homiléō, “I converse, associate with”). Ultimately from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + ἵλη (hílē, “troop, band”).
