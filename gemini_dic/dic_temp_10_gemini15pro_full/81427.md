gratefullest:/ˈɡreɪtfəlɪst/ | adj. |最感激的|  Superlative form of grateful, meaning feeling or showing an appreciation of kindness; thankful.  While grammatically correct, it is considered non-standard and awkward. "Most grateful" is generally preferred. | Example: She sent the gratefullest letter she could muster. 她寄出了她能写出的最感激的信。 | Example: Of all his friends, he felt gratefullest to her. 在他所有的朋友中，他对她最感激。 | 近义词: most grateful, most thankful, most appreciative | 反义词: ungrateful, unthankful, unappreciative |词性: 形容词
