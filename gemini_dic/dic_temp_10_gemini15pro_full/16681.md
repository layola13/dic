barrenest:/ˈbærənɪst/
adjective
最贫瘠的，最荒芜的
Superlative adjective of barren: most barren.  It describes something that is completely unproductive; unable to support life or growth.
The barrenest landscapes are often found in deserts.  最贫瘠的景观通常出现在沙漠中。
The farmer struggled to cultivate the barrenest field he had ever seen.  这位农民努力耕种着他见过的最贫瘠的田地。
近义词: desolate, infertile, sterile, unproductive
反义词: fertile, productive, fruitful, lush
词性: adjective

barren:/ˈbærən/
adjective
贫瘠的，荒芜的
Too poor to produce much or any vegetation; unproductive; unfruitful.  Can also refer to something that is lacking in interest or ideas.
The desert landscape was barren and lifeless.  沙漠景观荒芜而毫无生气。
The writer struggled with a barren imagination.  这位作家苦于缺乏想象力。
近义词: desolate, infertile, sterile, unproductive
反义词: fertile, productive, fruitful, lush
词性: adjective
