nonprescient:/nɑːnˈprɛʃɪənt/ /nɒnˈprɛʃɪənt/
adjective
非先知的，没有预见的
lacking prescience; not having foreknowledge.
The nonprescient CEO failed to anticipate the market crash.  这位没有预见的首席执行官未能预料到市场崩盘。
Synonyms: unforeseeing, unprophetic, myopic
Antonyms: prescient, prophetic, farsighted
词性: 形容词
