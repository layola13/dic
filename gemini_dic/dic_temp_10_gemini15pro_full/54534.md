disloyal:/dɪsˈlɔɪəl/
adjective
不忠诚的，背信弃义的
Not loyal; faithless.
He was accused of being disloyal to the company. 他被指控对公司不忠。
synonyms: faithless, treacherous, unfaithful, perfidious, traitorous, false, untrue
antonyms: loyal, faithful, true, steadfast, constant
词性: 形容词
