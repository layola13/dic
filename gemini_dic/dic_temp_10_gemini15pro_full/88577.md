hither-and-yon:/ˈhɪðər ən ˈjɒn/ (British), /ˈhɪðər ən ˈjɑːn/ (American)| adv.|到处；四处； hither and thither|Moving hither and yon, the butterflies flitted through the garden. 蝴蝶到处飞舞，在花园里翩翩起舞。|The children ran hither and yon, exploring the park. 孩子们四处奔跑，探索着公园。|近义词：here and there, everywhere, back and forth, to and fro|反义词: nowhere, in one place|词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Middle English *hider and yon*, equivalent to *hider* (“hither”) + *and* + *yon* (“yon”).
