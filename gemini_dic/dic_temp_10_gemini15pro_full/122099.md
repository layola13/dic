mistiming:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪmɪŋ/
n.
错误的时机;不合时宜
the act of doing something at the wrong time.
His mistiming of the announcement caused confusion. 他宣布的时机不对，造成了混乱。
miscalculation, bad timing, wrong time
good timing, opportune moment
noun
