depicted:/dɪˈpɪktɪd/
verb
描绘的，描写的
represented by a drawing, painting, or other visual medium; portrayed.
The painting depicted a scene of rural life. 这幅画描绘了一幅乡村生活的景象。
Synonyms: portrayed, represented, illustrated, pictured, shown, visualized, rendered
Antonyms: misrepresented, distorted
词性:v.
