crafted:/ˈkræftɪd/ | adj. | 精心制作的，精巧的 | Made skillfully, usually by hand. |  This beautifully crafted table is made of solid oak. 这张做工精美的桌子是用实心橡木制成的。

The story is crafted with great care and attention to detail.  这个故事写得非常用心，注重细节。| 近义词：handmade, hand-crafted, well-made, skillfully made, artistic, elaborate | 反义词：crude, rough, poorly made, mass-produced | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "craft" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Craft" comes from Old English "cræft," meaning "strength, skill, art."
