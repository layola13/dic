equitancy:/ˈɛkwɪtənsi/ | n. |共同遗产继承权；年金；年金保险|The state of having joint ownership or right, especially to an annuity.  A fixed sum of money paid to someone each year, typically for the rest of their life.| He purchased an equitancy that provided him with a steady income stream. 他购买了一份年金，为他提供了稳定的收入来源。|Synonyms: annuity, pension, allowance|Antonyms: debt, liability|noun


equitable:/ˈɛkwɪtəbl/ | adj. |公平的；公正的|Fair and impartial.| The judge made an equitable decision. 法官做出了公平的决定。|Synonyms: fair, just, impartial, unbiased|Antonyms: unfair, unjust, biased, partial|adjective
