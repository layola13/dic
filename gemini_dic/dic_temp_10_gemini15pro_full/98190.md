insearch:/ɪnˈsɜːrtʃ/ | adv. | 寻找；探求 |  Actively seeking or looking for something. |  He was insearch of a better life. 他在寻找更好的生活。 |  She traveled the world insearch of enlightenment. 她周游世界寻找启迪。 | 近义词: seeking, searching, looking for, questing | 反义词: ignoring, neglecting, avoiding | 词性: 副词

词源: "in" (在...之中) + "search" (寻找).  This is a relatively modern construction, likely influenced by phrases like "in pursuit of."
