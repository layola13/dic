gossiper:/ˈɡɑːsɪpər/ (US), /ˈɡɒsɪpə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 流言蜚语者；爱说闲话的人 |A person who habitually spreads rumors or talks about the private matters of others.| She's such a gossiper; she can't keep a secret to save her life. 她真是个爱说闲话的人；她根本守不住秘密。| synonyms: rumormonger, scandalmonger, busybody | antonyms: confidant, discreet person | noun
