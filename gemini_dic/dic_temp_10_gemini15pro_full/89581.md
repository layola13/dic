hooches:/ˈhuːtʃɪz/| n. |私酒窝点；(尤指破旧的)房子，住所|A usually small, rundown, and often illicit dwelling.  It often implies a place where alcohol is sold illegally. |They found him living in one of those hooches down by the river. 他们在河边那些破旧的小屋里找到了他。|Synonyms: shack, shanty, dive, joint, den |Antonyms: mansion, palace|noun

hooch:/huːtʃ/| n. |私酒；劣质酒|Cheap, inferior, or illicit liquor. |He offered me a swig of some nasty hooch he'd brewed himself. 他给了我一口他自己酿的难喝的私酒。|Synonyms: booze, liquor, rotgut, moonshine |Antonyms: fine wine, vintage wine|noun
