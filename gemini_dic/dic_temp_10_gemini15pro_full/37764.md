compassionless:/kəmˈpæʃənləs/
adj.
无情的，没有同情心的
Lacking compassion; feeling or showing no sympathy for others.
He displayed a compassionless attitude towards the homeless. 他对无家可归的人表现出无情的态度。

The CEO's compassionless decision to lay off hundreds of workers just before Christmas sparked outrage.  这位首席执行官在圣诞节前解雇数百名工人的无情决定引发了众怒。

近义词:unsympathetic, merciless, callous, inhumane, cold-hearted
反义词:compassionate, sympathetic, merciful, kind, humane
词性:adjective
