anamnestic:/ˌænəmˈnɛstɪk/ | adj. | 记忆的；回忆的；基于记忆的 | Relating to or based on memory, especially of past experiences; assisting the memory. |  The patient showed anamnestic responses to the images of his childhood home. 病人对童年住所的图像表现出记忆反应。 | The anamnestic effects of the medication helped her recall suppressed memories. 药物的助忆作用帮助她回忆起被压抑的记忆。 |  recollective, mnemonic | amnesic, forgetful | adjective

anamnesis: /ˌænæmˈniːsɪs/ | n. |  回忆；anamnesis | The process of recalling past events or experiences, especially in a medical context. | The doctor took a detailed anamnesis of the patient's symptoms. 医生详细询问了病人的症状。| The anamnesis revealed a history of childhood trauma.  回忆揭示了童年创伤的经历。| recollection, reminiscence | amnesia, forgetting | noun
