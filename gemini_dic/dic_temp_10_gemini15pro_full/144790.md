over-flattened:/ˈoʊvərˈflæt(ə)nd/| adj.|过度扁平化的|Excessively flattened; flattened to an excessive degree.  This implies that something has been flattened more than is desirable or necessary.|The dough was over-flattened and couldn't hold its shape. 面团被压得太扁了，无法保持形状。|The over-flattened cushions were uncomfortable to sit on.  这些被压得太扁的垫子坐起来不舒服。|overly flattened, excessively flattened|slightly flattened, under-flattened|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

"over-" (Old English *ofer) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "flatten" (from flat + -en) meaning "to make flat."
