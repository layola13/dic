ferries:/ˈferɪz/
n.
渡船; 渡运
boats or ships that regularly carry passengers and vehicles across a body of water
The ferries run every half hour. 渡轮每半小时一班。

近义词:boats, ships
反义词:None (Ferries don't have a direct opposite.)
词性:noun
