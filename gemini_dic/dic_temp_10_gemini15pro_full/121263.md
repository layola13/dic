misevaluation:/ˌmɪsɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/
n.|错误评估，估错|An inaccurate or incorrect assessment of something's worth, significance, or quality.|The misevaluation of the company's assets led to significant financial losses. 公司资产的错误评估导致了重大财务损失。|misjudgment, underestimation, overestimation|correct evaluation, accurate assessment|noun
