grew:/ɡruː/
verb
长大; 成长;  (数量或规模)增加, 增多; 发展; 变得;
past simple of grow. It means to increase in size, number, or strength.
The city grew rapidly in the 19th century.  这个城市在19世纪快速发展。
The company grew from a small startup to a multinational corporation. 这家公司从小小的初创企业发展成为一家跨国公司。
近义词: developed, increased, expanded, enlarged
反义词: shrink, decrease, diminish, contract
词性: verb
