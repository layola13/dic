**Oblong:**

**Etymology of Rectangle:**

From Late Latin *rectangulum*, from Latin *rectus* (straight, right) + *angulus* (angle).

**Etymology of Oblong:**

From Latin *oblongus* "somewhat long, rather long," from *ob* "toward, against, before" + *longus* "long."
