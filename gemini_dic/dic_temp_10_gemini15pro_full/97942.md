innovative: /ˈɪnəveɪtɪv/ | adj. | 创新的，革新的 | Introducing something new; characterized by new methods or ideas. |  Example 1: The company is known for its innovative designs. 这家公司以其创新的设计而闻名。

Example 2:  They came up with an innovative solution to the problem. 他们想出了一个创新的解决方案。 | Synonyms:  creative, original, groundbreaking, pioneering, ingenious, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art | Antonyms: traditional, conventional, outdated, old-fashioned | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *innovativus, from innovare "to renew, alter," from in- "into" + novus "new".
