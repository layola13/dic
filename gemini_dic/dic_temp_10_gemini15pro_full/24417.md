briskest:/brɪskɪst/| adj.|最轻快的，最活泼的，最兴旺的|Superlative form of brisk: quick and energetic.|The briskest trade was done in the early morning hours.  清晨的交易最为活跃。|近义词：quickest, liveliest, most active|反义词：slowest, dullest, most sluggish|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *brisk*, from Anglo-Norman *briske*, of uncertain origin, possibly from Old French *bruisquer* "to shatter, break," or from Old Low Franconian *briska* "brushwood, twigs."
