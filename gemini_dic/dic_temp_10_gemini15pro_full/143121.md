outbuy: /ˌaʊtˈbaɪ/
verb
胜过购买；以更高的价格买下
To purchase something at a higher price than someone else, effectively outbidding them.
The company outbought its rivals for the rights to the new technology.  这家公司以更高的价格胜过了竞争对手，获得了这项新技术的权利。
The government was outbought by private collectors at the auction. 政府在拍卖会上被私人收藏家以更高的价格买走了藏品。
Synonyms: outbid, overbid
Antonyms: underbid
词性: 动词
