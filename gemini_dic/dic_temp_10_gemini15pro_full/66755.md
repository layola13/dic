exosculate: /ˈɛksɒskjʊleɪt/ (UK) /ˈɛksɑːskjəleɪt/ (US)
verb
接吻; (尤指) 深吻
To kiss, especially passionately.
例句：The lovers exosculated under the moonlight. 恋人们在月光下深情拥吻。

例句：They exosculated for a long time, lost in each other's embrace. 他们长时间地拥吻，沉浸在彼此的怀抱中。

近义词：kiss, smooch, canoodle
反义词: abstain

词性: v.
