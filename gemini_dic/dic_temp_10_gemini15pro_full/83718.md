hagseed:/ˈhæɡsiːd/| n. |女巫的种子; 丑老太婆; (尤指) 脾气暴躁的老妇人;  哈格西德（小说《暴风雨》中的人物）; 令人讨厌的女人|A hagseed is an unpleasant or bad-tempered old woman.  It also refers to a character in Margaret Atwood's novel "Hag-Seed," a retelling of Shakespeare's "The Tempest".|Felix was a hagseed, bitter and twisted inside. 菲利克斯是一个脾气暴躁、内心扭曲的丑老太婆。

The play features a character named Hag-Seed, based on Prospero from Shakespeare's The Tempest.  这出戏有一个名为哈格西德的角色，以莎士比亚《暴风雨》中的普洛斯彼罗为原型。|近义词：witch, crone, shrew, harridan|反义词:beauty, charmer|词性:noun
