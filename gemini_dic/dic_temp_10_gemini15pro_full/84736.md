handkerchieves:/ˈhæŋkərtʃiːvz/
n.
手帕（handkerchief的复数）
A square of cotton or other finely woven material, typically carried in one's pocket and intended for blowing one's nose or wiping one's face.
She dabbed her eyes with a lace handkerchief. 她用一条蕾丝手帕擦了擦眼睛。

近义词:tissues, hankies
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object. Could consider alternatives like "facial tissue" which is a modern replacement.)
