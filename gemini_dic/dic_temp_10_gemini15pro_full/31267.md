chapattis:/tʃəˈpɑːtiːz/
noun
印度薄饼;恰巴提
Thin, flat unleavened bread made from whole-wheat flour, cooked on a griddle.
We had chapattis with our curry. 我们咖喱配着恰巴提吃。
Synonyms:roti, flatbread
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific type of bread)
n.
