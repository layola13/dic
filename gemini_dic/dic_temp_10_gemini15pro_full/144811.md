over-head's: /ˌoʊvərˈhɛdz/ | n. |经常性费用; 管理费用| Regular business expenses, such as rent, electricity, and salaries, not directly related to producing goods or services. | The overheads for running this office are very high.  经营这间办公室的经常性费用很高。 | synonyms: operating expenses, indirect costs, fixed costs | antonyms: revenue, income | noun

over-head's: /ˌoʊvərˈhɛdz/ | adj. |头顶上的; 高架的| Located above one's head. | The overhead lights were too bright. 顶灯太亮了。 | synonyms: above, aloft | antonyms: below, underneath | adjective
