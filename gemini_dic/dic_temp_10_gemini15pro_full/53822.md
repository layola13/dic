disconnecting:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪŋ/
adjective|断开的，分离的|Not connected; separated.|例句：The disconnecting cable caused the internet to go down. 断开的电缆导致互联网中断。|近义词：disconnected, unattached, separate|反义词：connected, attached, joined|adjective
