crustation:/krʌˈsteɪʃən/| n.|甲壳类动物|A crustacean is an animal belonging to the large group of arthropods, which also includes insects, spiders, and myriapods.  Crustaceans typically have a hard outer shell or exoskeleton, segmented bodies, and jointed limbs.  Many crustaceans live in water, such as crabs, lobsters, and shrimps.|例句：Lobsters and crabs are types of crustaceation. 龙虾和螃蟹是甲壳类动物。
