misaddresses:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛsɪz/ | v. | 写错地址；称呼错误 | to write the wrong address on (an envelope, package, etc.); to use the wrong name or title when speaking to someone | The letter was misaddressed and never reached its destination. 这封信写错了地址，从未到达目的地。|  misdirects, mislabels | correctly addresses, addresses properly | verb
misaddress:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛs/ | n. | 写错地址；称呼错误 | the act of writing the wrong address or using the wrong name or title |  The misaddress caused a significant delay in delivery. 地址错误导致投递严重延误。|  misdirection, mislabeling | correct address, proper address | noun
