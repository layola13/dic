hereditably:/hɪˈrɛdɪtəbli/
adverb
可遗传地，遗传性地
in a way that is passed on from parent to offspring through genes.
例句：The disease is hereditably transmitted. 这种疾病是遗传性的。

例句：Some traits are hereditably determined. 一些性状是由遗传决定的。

近义词：genetically, inheritedly
反义词：acquired

词性：副词
