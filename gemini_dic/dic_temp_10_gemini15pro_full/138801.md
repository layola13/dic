nummulite:/ˈnʌmjʊˌlaɪt/ | n. |货币虫，钱币石 |A lenticular fossil shell of a foraminifer of the genus Nummulites, common in the Eocene and Oligocene periods, resembling a small coin. |The limestone cliffs were rich in nummulites. 这些石灰岩悬崖富含货币虫化石。| The pyramids of Egypt are built of limestone containing nummulites. 埃及的金字塔是由含有货币虫的石灰岩建造的。 |Synonyms: foraminifer, fossil |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific fossil type) | noun
