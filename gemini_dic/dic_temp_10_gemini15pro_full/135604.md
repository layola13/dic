nonpossessive:/ˌnɑːnpəˈzɛsɪv/| adj.|非所有格的，非占有的|Not indicating possession; not possessive.  Relating to or being a grammatical case that indicates a relationship other than possession.|The pronoun "mine" is possessive, while "me" is nonpossessive.|近义词:non-possessing, absolute|反义词:possessive|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non, meaning "not."
possessive (adjective): from Late Middle English possessif, from Old French possessif, from Latin possess-, past participle stem of possidēre "to possess."
-ive (suffix): from Latin -īvus.
