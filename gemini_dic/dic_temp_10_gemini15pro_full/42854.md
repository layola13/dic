courteous:/ˈkɜːrtɪəs/ | adj. | 有礼貌的，谦恭的 | Polite and respectful; considerate of others. | She was unfailingly courteous to everyone she met. 她对她遇到的每个人都非常有礼貌。| He gave a courteous nod to the doorman. 他礼貌地向门卫点了点头。| 近义词: polite, respectful, gracious, civil | 反义词: rude, impolite, disrespectful, discourteous | 词性: adjective
