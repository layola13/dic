actresses:/ˈæktrɪsɪz/
n.
女演员
Plural form of actress; female performers in plays, movies, or television.
The actresses took a bow after the final curtain.  剧终谢幕后，女演员们鞠了一躬。
The actresses all auditioned for the lead role. 所有的女演员都参加了主角的试镜。
近义词: performers, stars, artists
反义词: audience, viewers
词性: noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French actrice + -ess (feminine suffix). Actress is the feminine form of actor, which comes from Latin actor "agent, doer, performer," from actus "a doing, a driving," past participle stem of agere "to do, drive, lead, act."  The -ess suffix was added later to denote the female gender.
