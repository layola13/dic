floribunda:/ˌflɔːrɪˈbʌndə/
n.|丰花月季；多花月季|A modern shrub rose derived from hybrid tea and polyantha roses, characterized by its profuse clusters of relatively small flowers.|The floribunda roses in my garden are blooming beautifully. 我花园里的丰花月季开得很漂亮。|Synonyms: cluster rose, polyantha rose|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps single-flowered rose)|Noun
