guff:/ɡʌf/
noun
废话，胡说，空话
Empty or foolish talk, nonsense.
He gave me a load of guff about how busy he was. 他跟我说了很多关于他有多忙的废话。
近义词: nonsense, rubbish, baloney, bunk
反义词: sense, truth
