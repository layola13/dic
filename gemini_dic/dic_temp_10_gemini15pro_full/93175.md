ignobility:/ˌɪɡnəˈbɪləti/
n.
卑鄙，下流
the quality of being ignoble; baseness.
His ignonility was evident in his willingness to betray his friends for personal gain.  他的卑鄙在他为了个人利益而背叛朋友的意愿中显而易见。
ignobleness, baseness, vileness, meanness, lowness
nobility, honor, goodness, virtue
noun
