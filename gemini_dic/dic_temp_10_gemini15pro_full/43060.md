cowlings:/ˈkaʊlɪŋz/
n.
整流罩；发动机罩；引擎罩
A removable metal covering for an aircraft engine.
The mechanic removed the cowlings to inspect the engine.  机械师取下整流罩检查发动机。
coverings, panels, nacelles
(无常用反义词)
