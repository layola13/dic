ETYMOLOGY->
From the boxing term "down" (knocked to the canvas) and "out" (unconscious or unable to rise before the count of ten), extended to a general sense of being defeated and helpless in life.  源于拳击术语“down”（击倒在画布上）和“out”（失去知觉或无法在数到十之前站起来），引申为在生活中被击败和无助的普遍意义。
