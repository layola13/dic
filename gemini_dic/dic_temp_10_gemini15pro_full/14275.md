autographal:/ˌɔːtəˈɡræfɪkl/
adjective
亲笔的；自传的
Written by a person about their own life, or relating to such a work.
Example:  An autographal letter.  一封亲笔信。

Example:  Her latest book is highly autographal. 她最新的书是高度自传性质的。

synonym:  autobiographical, self-written, personal
antonym:  biographical (about someone else), fictional
