filtrations:/fɪlˈtreɪʃənz/| n. |过滤; 滤液|The process of filtering a liquid or gas, or a system used for doing this.  The action or process of filtering something.|
例句：The filtrations system removes impurities from the water.  这个过滤系统去除水中的杂质。
