multifaceted:/ˌmʌltiˈfæsɪtɪd/ | adj. | 多方面的，多层面的 | Having many different aspects or features. |  This is a multifaceted problem with no easy solutions. 这是一个多方面的问题，没有简单的解决办法。| Example: The diamond was multifaceted and sparkled brilliantly. 这颗钻石有多个刻面，闪耀着光芒。 | Synonyms: complex, diverse, varied, many-sided | Antonyms: simple, basic, one-dimensional | Adjective
