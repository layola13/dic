pashmina:/pæʃˈmiːnə/ /pɑːʃˈmiːnə/ | n. | 羊绒，山羊绒围巾 | A fine type of wool made from the undercoat of a Himalayan goat, or a scarf or shawl made from this wool. | 例句：She wrapped a luxurious pashmina around her shoulders. 她把一条奢华的羊绒披肩围在肩上。

例句：The pashmina felt incredibly soft against her skin. 这羊绒在她皮肤上感觉 incredibly soft against her skin.

近义词：cashmere, shawl, wrap | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑粗糙的材质例如：coarse wool) | 词性: noun


pashmina:/pæʃˈmiːnə/ /pɑːʃˈmiːnə/ | adj. | 羊绒制的 | Made from pashmina wool. | 例句：She wore a pashmina shawl to the opera. 她戴着一条羊绒披肩去歌剧院。

例句：He bought her a beautiful pashmina scarf for her birthday. 他给她买了一条漂亮的羊绒围巾作为生日礼物。

近义词：cashmere | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: adjective
