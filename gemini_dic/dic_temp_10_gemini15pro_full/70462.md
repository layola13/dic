figures:/ˈfɪɡjərz/ (US), /ˈfɪɡəz/ (UK)| n. |数字; 图形; 人物; 身材; (v. 计算; 认为)|A numerical symbol. / A diagram or illustrative drawing. / A person, especially a well-known or notable one. / The shape of a person's body. / (v.) To calculate or work out. / To think or consider.|

例句：The latest figures show a decline in sales. 最新数据显示销售额下降。
The book contains many figures and tables. 这本书包含许多图表。

例句：She's a leading figure in the fashion industry. 她是时尚界的领军人物。
He has a very athletic figure. 他身材很健美。

例句：I figured it would cost about $100. 我估计大约要花费 100 美元。
I figured that he would be late. 我认为他会迟到。

近义词：(n.) numbers, statistics, diagrams, illustrations, individuals, personalities, forms, shapes; (v.) calculate, compute, estimate, believe, consider
反义词：(n.) words, text; (v.)  miscalculate, overestimate, underestimate, doubt
词性: noun (primarily), verb
