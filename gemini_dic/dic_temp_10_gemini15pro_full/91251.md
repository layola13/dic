hydroelectricity:/ˌhaɪdroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsɪti/| n.|水力发电|Electricity generated by the conversion of the energy of running water.|Hydroelectricity is a clean and renewable energy source. 水力发电是一种清洁的可再生能源。|The dam provides hydroelectricity for the entire region.  这座大坝为整个地区提供水力发电。|Synonyms: hydropower, water power|Antonyms: fossil fuels, nuclear power|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + electricity.  "Hydro-" comes from the Greek "hydōr" (water).
