histopathology:/ˌhɪstoʊpəˈθɒlədʒi/ /ˌhɪstəpəˈθɒlədʒi/| n. |组织病理学|The study of diseased tissues at the microscopic level.  It involves examining thin slices of tissue under a microscope to identify abnormalities and diagnose diseases.|例句：The histopathology report confirmed the presence of cancer cells. 组织病理学报告证实了癌细胞的存在。

例句：Histopathology is crucial for accurate diagnosis of many diseases.  组织病理学对于许多疾病的准确诊断至关重要。|近义词：microscopic anatomy, cellular pathology|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
