flashguns:/ˈflæʃɡʌnz/
n.
闪光灯（尤指用于照相机的电子闪光灯）
A flashgun is an electronic flash unit used in photography to produce a brief, intense burst of light.
The photographer used a flashgun to illuminate the dark room. 摄影师用闪光灯照亮了黑暗的房间。
synonyms: flash, speedlight, strobe
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "natural light")
noun
