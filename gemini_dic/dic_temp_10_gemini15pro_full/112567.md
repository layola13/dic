lovelornness:/ˈlʌvlɔːrnɪs/| n.|失恋的痛苦，单相思的苦恼|The state of being without love, especially due to unrequited love or the loss of a loved one. It's a deep feeling of sadness and loneliness caused by romantic troubles.|She suffered from a deep lovelornness after her boyfriend left her. 她男友离开她之后，她陷入了深深的失恋痛苦之中。|Synonyms: heartache, lovesickness, pining, desolation, despair|Antonyms: happiness, joy, contentment|Noun
