overcontented:/ˌōvər kənˈtɛntɪd/ | adj. | 过分满足的，过于自满的 | Excessively satisfied or complacent; too easily pleased. | He seemed overcontented with his mediocre achievements. 他似乎对自己的平庸成就过于满足了。|  The overcontented cat lay basking in the sun.  那只过分满足的猫躺在阳光下晒太阳。 | 近义词: complacent, self-satisfied, smug | 反义词: dissatisfied, discontented, restless | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + contented (adjective from content, meaning "satisfied")
