governorates:/ˈɡʌvərnərɪts/
n.
省份；地方行政区；管辖区域
A governorate is a principal administrative division of a country or empire.
Egypt is divided into 27 governorates. 埃及被划分为27个省。
The governorates have considerable autonomy. 这些省份拥有相当大的自治权。
近义词:provinces, regions, districts, states
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly "municipality" for a smaller division)
词性:noun
