overseas:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːz/ /ˌəʊvəˈsiːz/ | adv.| 在海外，向海外 | To or in a foreign country across the sea. |  He travelled overseas for business. 他出国出差了。 | They shipped the goods overseas.  他们把货物运到了海外。 | 近义词：abroad | 反义词: domestically, locally | 词性: adverb
