discorrespondent:/ˌdɪskɒrɪˈspɒndənt/ | adj. | 不一致的，不符的，不相称的 | Not corresponding; disagreeing; incongruous.  | The witness's testimony was discorrespondent with the physical evidence. 目击者的证词与物证不符。 |  Near Synonyms:  inconsistent, discrepant, conflicting, divergent, dissimilar | Antonyms: consistent, corresponding, congruent, harmonious, accordant | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + correspondent (agreeing, similar). Ultimately from Latin *correspondēre* (to correspond).
