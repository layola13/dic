batholiths: /ˈbæθəlɪθs/
n.
岩基
A very large igneous intrusion extending deep in the earth's crust.
The Sierra Nevada batholith is a huge mass of granite.  内华达山脉岩基是一个巨大的花岗岩体。

batholith: /ˈbæθəlɪθ/
n.
岩基

The geologists studied the formation of the batholith. 地质学家研究了岩基的形成。

反义词: (No direct antonym for a geological formation)
