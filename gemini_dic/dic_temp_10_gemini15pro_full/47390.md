deadener:/ˈdɛdnər/| n.|减震器，消音器，抑制剂|Something that deadens or reduces sound, vibration, or feeling.|The carpenter used a special deadener between the floors to reduce noise. 木匠在地板之间使用了特殊的隔音材料来减少噪音。|Synonyms: muffler, silencer, damper, suppressant|Antonyms: amplifier, intensifier|noun
