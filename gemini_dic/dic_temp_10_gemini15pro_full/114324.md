maisonette:/ˌmeɪzəˈnɛt/ | n. | 小复式公寓；小型公寓 |A small apartment, often on two levels, within a larger building, typically with its own entrance. | Example:  They live in a charming maisonette in the heart of the city. 他们住在市中心一间迷人的小复式公寓里。

Example: The maisonette has a private balcony overlooking the park.  这套小型公寓有一个可以俯瞰公园的私人阳台。| Synonym: apartment, flat, duplex | Antonym: house, mansion | noun
