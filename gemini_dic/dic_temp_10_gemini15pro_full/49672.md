demissionary:/ˌdɛmɪʃəˈnɛri/
adjective
辞职的，即将离任的
Relating to or characteristic of resignation from office.
The demissionary cabinet continued to govern until the new government was formed.  即将离任的内阁继续执政，直到新政府成立。
The prime minister offered his demissionary letter to the president.  首相向总统递交了辞呈。
近义词: resigning, outgoing, caretaker
反义词: incoming, succeeding
词性: adjective

词源:
From French *démissionnaire*, from démissionner (“to resign”), from dé- + mission (“mission, duty”).
