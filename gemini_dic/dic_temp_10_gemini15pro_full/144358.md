outsteal:/ˌaʊtˈstiːl/
verb
超过…的偷窃; 比…偷得更多
To surpass someone in stealing; to steal more than someone else.
The notorious art thief managed to outsteal his rivals, making off with a priceless painting.  这个臭名昭著的艺术窃贼设法比他的对手偷得更多，带走了一幅无价的画。
outsteal his competitors by acquiring the rare artifact. 他通过获得稀有文物，在偷窃方面胜过他的竞争对手。
近义词: outdo, surpass, outmaneuver
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb
