fehling:/ˈfeɪlɪŋ/ |adj. n.|费林, 指费林试剂或费林溶液| Fehling's solution, a chemical reagent used to differentiate between water-soluble carbohydrate and ketone functional groups, and as a test for reducing sugars and non-reducing sugars, supplementary to the Tollens' reagent test. Also used as a test for glucose in urine.|The Fehling test was positive, indicating the presence of reducing sugars.|Synonyms: Fehling's solution, Fehling's reagent|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical reagent. Could consider tests for different substances as indirect antonyms.)|noun, adjective
