buttercuppy:/ˈbʌtərˌkʌpi/| n. |毛茛; 金凤花|A small, bright yellow wildflower, also known as buttercup.  It belongs to the Ranunculus genus. |The field was covered in buttercuppies.  田野里开满了金凤花。| The children were making daisy chains and picking buttercuppies. 孩子们正在用雏菊编花环，采摘金凤花。|Synonyms: buttercup, crowfoot|Antonyms: N/A|noun
