featherbone:/ˈfeðərbəʊn/ (UK) /ˈfeðərbown/ (US)| n. |鲸须；鲸骨；用鲸须做的女装紧身胸衣|A flexible, lightweight material made from baleen (whalebone) used in corsets and other garments in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
