kidnappee:/ˌkɪdnæˈpiː/
n.
被绑架者，被劫持者
A person who has been kidnapped.
The kidnappers demanded a ransom for the kidnappee. 绑匪要求为被绑架者支付赎金。
The kidnappee was found safe and sound. 被绑架者被找到，安然无恙。
近义词:hostage, captive
反义词:kidnapper
词性:名词
