interfoliate:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊliˌeɪt/
|v.|插入；交错；（使）散布|To insert between leaves or folds; to interleave.|She interfoliated the tissue paper between the pages of the old book to protect them. 她将薄页纸插入旧书页之间以保护它们。|interleave, insert, intercalate|separate, remove|Verb
interfoliate:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊliˌeɪt/
|adj.|散布的；交错的|Arranged in layers or interspersed.|The artist created an interfoliate sculpture using metal and glass. 艺术家使用金属和玻璃创作了一个散布式雕塑。|interspersed, layered, interleaved|separate, solid|Adjective
