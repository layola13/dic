mitrailleur:/ˌmiːtrɑːˈjɜːr/ /ˌmiːtrəˈjɜːr/ | n. | 机枪手；机枪 | A soldier who operates a mitrailleuse (a type of machine gun).  A mitrailleur is a person who uses a mitrailleuse, an early type of machine gun. The word itself can also refer to the weapon itself, although this usage is less common in modern English. | The mitrailleur kept the enemy pinned down with suppressing fire. 机枪手用压制火力压制住了敌人。| The regiment's mitrailleurs were positioned on the hilltop.  该团的机枪手位于山顶。 | synonyms: machine gunner, gunner | antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym for a person operating a weapon) | noun


mitrailleuse:/ˌmiːtrɑːˈjɜːz/ /ˌmiːtrəˈjɜːz/ | n. | 机枪（尤指早期的一种）| A type of early machine gun, typically a volley gun with multiple barrels.  This word is less commonly used than "machine gun" in modern English.  It refers to a specific type of early rapid-firing gun. | The French army used mitrailleuses during the Franco-Prussian War. 法国军队在普法战争中使用了米特留斯机枪。| The mitrailleuse proved to be less effective than anticipated. 事实证明，这种机枪的效果不如预期。 | synonyms: machine gun, Gatling gun | antonyms: N/A | noun
