blub:/blʌb/
n.|哭泣，呜咽；泡，水泡|A blub is the act of crying noisily or a bubble of air or gas in a liquid.|The child blubbed uncontrollably after scraping his knee.  孩子擦伤膝盖后哭个不停。|近义词：sob, weep, cry, bubble|反义词:laugh, giggle|词性:名词，动词

blub:/blʌb/
v.|哭泣，呜咽|To blub means to cry noisily.|He blubbed all the way home. 他一路哭着回家。|近义词：sob, weep, cry|反义词:laugh, giggle|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
Likely imitative of the sound of crying.  Possibly related to Dutch "blob" (bubble).
