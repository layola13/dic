deviceless:/dɪˈvaɪslɪs/
adjective
无设备的；无需设备的
Not requiring a physical device, particularly in the context of computing or technology; operating or accessible without the need for a dedicated device.
Many companies are now offering deviceless access to their services. 许多公司现在提供无需设备即可访问其服务。
Deviceless authentication is becoming increasingly popular. 无设备身份验证正变得越来越流行。
近义词: device-free, device-independent
反义词: device-dependent, device-based
词性: adjective
词源: "device" + "-less"  "Device" comes from Old French "devis," meaning "division, arrangement, wish, desire." "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
