hosts:/hoʊsts/ (US) /həʊsts/ (UK)| n. |主人，主持人，东道主，大量，许多|A person who receives or entertains other people as guests.|The hosts of the party were very welcoming. 聚会的主人非常热情好客。|近义词：hostess, emcee, multitude, crowd, swarm |反义词:guest, visitor|noun


hosts:/hoʊsts/ (US) /həʊsts/ (UK)| v. |主持，主办，当主人招待|To act as host at an event or to provide the space and other things necessary for an event.|She hosted a dinner party for her friends. 她为朋友们举办了一个晚宴。|近义词：entertain, present, organize|反义词:attend|verb
