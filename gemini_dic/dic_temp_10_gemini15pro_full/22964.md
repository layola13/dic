bottomlessly:/ˈbɒtəmləsli/
adv.
无底地，无限地，深不可测地
In an immeasurable or inexhaustible way.
She stared bottomlessly into the night sky. 她茫然地凝视着夜空。
Synonyms: infinitely, endlessly, limitlessly, immeasurably, inexhaustibly
Antonyms: finitely, limitedly, measurably
词性: 副词

bottomless:/ˈbɒtəmləs/
adj.
无底的，无限的，深不可测的
Having no bottom; very deep.  Seeming to be without limit.
He fell into a seemingly bottomless pit. 他掉进了一个看似无底的深坑。
Synonyms: deep, profound, immeasurable, infinite, vast
Antonyms: shallow, finite, limited
词性: 形容词

词源:
From "bottomless" + "-ly."  "Bottomless" comes from "bottom" (Old English "botm") + "-less" (Old English "-lēas").
