murderesses: /ˈmɜːrdərɪsɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrdərəsɪz/ (US)
n.
女凶手们，女性杀人犯
A woman or women who commit murder.
The murderesses were apprehended shortly after the crime.  这些女凶手在犯罪后不久就被逮捕了。
The trial of the murderesses attracted national attention.  对这些女凶手的审判引起了全国的关注。
近义词: killers, assassins, homicide perpetrators
反义词: victim(s)
词性: Noun (Plural)

Etymology:
From Middle English *murdresse*, from Anglo-Norman *murd(e)resse*, feminine of *murdrere*, *murderour*, from Old French *murderres*, *murderere* "murderer." Ultimately from Germanic.
