outfaces: /ˌaʊtˈfeɪsɪz/ | v. |胜过，以更大胆的态度对待| surpasses or confronts with a bolder attitude | He outfaced his accusers and maintained his innocence. 他大胆地面对原告，坚持自己无罪。| 近义词: outbraves, defies, withstands | 反义词: submits, yields | 词性: 动词

词源:  From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "face" (meaning to confront).  Essentially, to face someone or something with more boldness or confidence.
