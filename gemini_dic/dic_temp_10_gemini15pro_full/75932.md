fungicides:/ˈfʌndʒɪsaɪdz/| n.|杀菌剂|Substances that kill fungi or inhibit their growth.  They are used to control fungal diseases in plants, animals, and humans.|例句：The farmer sprayed his crops with fungicides to prevent mildew.  农民给作物喷洒杀菌剂以防止霉变。

例句：Fungicides are important for protecting crops from fungal diseases. 杀菌剂对于保护作物免受真菌病害至关重要。|近义词：antifungal agents|反义词: (No direct antonym, but potentially) fungal growth promoters|noun
