monarchial:/məˈnɑːrkiəl/
adjective
君主的；君主政体的；拥护君主政体的
Of or relating to a monarch or monarchy.  Characterized by or favoring monarchy.
例句：The country retains a monarchial system of government.  这个国家保留了君主立宪制。

例句：She expressed monarchial sentiments, believing a king could unify the nation. 她表达了拥护君主政体的观点，认为国王可以统一国家。

近义词：royal, regal, kingly, queenly
反义词：republican, democratic
词性：adjective

monarchy:/ˈmɒnəki/
noun
君主制；君主国
A form of government with a monarch at the head.
例句：The United Kingdom is a constitutional monarchy. 英国是君主立宪制国家。

例句：The monarchy was abolished in 1918. 君主制在1918年被废除。

近义词：kingdom, empire, sovereignty
反义词：republic, democracy
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *monarchialis, from Latin monarcha (“monarch”), from Ancient Greek μονάρχης (monárkhēs, “sole ruler”), from μόνος (mónos, “alone, single”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule”).
