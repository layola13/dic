doted:/ˈdoʊtɪd/| adj.|溺爱的；宠爱的；过分喜爱的|excessively fond of; adoring. Showing excessive fondness or love.|She doted on her grandchildren, showering them with gifts and attention. 她溺爱她的孙子孙女，给他们送了很多礼物，非常关心他们。|近义词：adored, cherished, loved, fond, indulgent|反义词：neglectful, indifferent|词性:形容词
词源:From the verb "dote," which comes from Middle English *doten*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Dutch *doten* "to be foolish, to rave."
