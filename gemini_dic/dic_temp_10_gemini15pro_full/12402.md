asicus: /ˈæsɪkəs/
adjective| 无柄的；固着的 |Sessile; attached directly by the base; stalkless. |The asicus barnacle attaches directly to the whale's skin.  这种无柄藤壶直接附着在鲸鱼的皮肤上。|Sessile, attached, fixed|Stalked, pedunculate|adjective
Etymology: From Latin *asicus, possibly related to āsitus (“placed near”).
