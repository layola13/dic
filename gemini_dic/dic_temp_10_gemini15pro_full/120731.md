misallotting:/ˌmɪsæˈlɒtɪŋ/
verb
错误分配；分配不当
The act of allocating resources or funds incorrectly or inappropriately.
The committee was accused of misallotting funds to projects that benefited their own members. 委员会被指控将资金错误分配给了使其自身成员受益的项目。
The government was criticized for misallotting resources to less important projects. 政府因将资源错误分配给不太重要的项目而受到批评。
misallocate, misuse, misappropriate
allocate correctly, appropriate correctly
