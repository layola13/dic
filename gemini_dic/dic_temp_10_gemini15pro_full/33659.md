cinephilia:/ˌsɪnəˈfɪliə/
n.
热爱电影；影迷癖
A great love of films; passion for cinema.
She has a pronounced cinephilia, spending hours watching and discussing films. 她有明显的电影癖，花费数小时观看和讨论电影。
近义词:film enthusiasm, movie love
反义词:film aversion
