odontoid:/ˈɒdənˌtɔɪd/| adj. n.| 齿状的，齿突 |Resembling a tooth; tooth-shaped.  Also, relating to the odontoid process (also called the dens), a tooth-like projection from the second cervical vertebra (axis) upon which the first cervical vertebra (atlas) rotates, allowing head movement. |The odontoid process is crucial for head rotation. 齿突对于头部旋转至关重要。|Synonyms: dens, tooth-like|Antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun
