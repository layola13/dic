monotrochal: /ˌmɒnəˈtrəʊkəl/ | adj. | 单轮的；单鞭毛的 | Having a single flagellum at one end (of a cell or microorganism). |  Example: The bacteria is monotrochal, possessing only one flagellum for movement. 这种细菌是单鞭毛的，只有一个鞭毛用于运动。

Example: Monotrochal bacteria are commonly found in aquatic environments. 单鞭毛细菌常见于水生环境。 | 近义词: uniflagellate | 反义词: peritrichous, lophotrichous, amphitrichous | 词性: 形容词
