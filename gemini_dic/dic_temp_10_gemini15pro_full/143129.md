outcarolled:/ˌaʊtkəˈrɒld/ | v. |胜过歌声；以更优美的歌声盖过 |To sing more beautifully or loudly than someone or something else. | The nightingale outcarolled all the other birds in the forest. 夜莺的歌声盖过了森林里所有其他鸟类的歌声。 |  The choir outcarolled the other competing choirs.  唱诗班的歌声胜过了其他参赛唱诗班。 | surpass in singing, outsing | - | verb
词源: out- (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + carol (verb meaning "to sing joyfully")
