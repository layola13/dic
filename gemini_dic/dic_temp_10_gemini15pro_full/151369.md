parlando:/pɑːrˈlɑːndoʊ/ | adv. |  似说白似唱歌地，用说话的语气 |  In a speaking manner; in a conversational style, especially as a direction in music. | The soprano delivered the aria parlando, creating a dramatic effect. 女高音用说话的语气演唱了咏叹调，营造出戏剧性的效果。 | synonyms: declamatory, recitative | antonyms: cantabile, lyrical | adverb

Etymology->
From Italian *parlando*, present participle of *parlare* "to speak," from Latin *parabolare* "to speak," literally "to throw words beside" from *para-* "beside" + *-bolare*, related to *fari* "to speak."
