homogeneal:/ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. |同类的，同质的，均匀的 | Of the same kind or nature; consisting of similar parts or elements; uniform in composition or structure. |  The mixture was homogeneal, with no lumps or variations in color.  这种混合物是均匀的，没有结块或颜色变化。|  The scientist studied the homogeneal properties of the metal alloy. 科学家研究了这种金属合金的均匀特性。 | 近义词: homogeneous, uniform, consistent, similar | 反义词: heterogeneous, dissimilar, varied, diverse | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin homogeneus, from Greek homogenēs, from homos "same" + genos "kind, race."
