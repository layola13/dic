articulation:/ɑːrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |清晰度；发音；连接；关节 | the formation of clear and distinct sounds in speech; the state of being jointed or joined |  The speaker's articulation was so clear that everyone could understand him. 演讲者的发音非常清晰，每个人都能听懂。| synonyms: pronunciation, enunciation, expression, joint, connection | antonyms: slurring, mumbling, inarticulateness, disconnection | noun

articulation:/ɑːrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 表达；阐述 | the act of expressing something clearly in words |  He struggled with the articulation of his complex ideas. 他难以表达他复杂的想法。 | synonyms: expression, formulation, voicing | antonyms: suppression, repression, silence | noun
