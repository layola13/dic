desirous:/dɪˈzaɪrəs/
adjective
渴望的，想往的
Having or characterized by desire; wanting something.
She was desirous of a promotion. 她渴望升职。
近义词: eager, keen, avid, longing, yearning
反义词: indifferent, apathetic, unenthusiastic
词性: adjective
词源: From Old French *desiros, from Latin dēsīderōsus (“full of longing”), from dēsīderō (“long for, desire greatly”), from dē- + sīderō (“consider, observe”), literally “observe from the stars”.
