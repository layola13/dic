frontwards:/ˈfɔːrwərdz/ (US) /ˈfɔːwədz/ (UK)| adv.|向前地，朝前地|Moving or facing towards the front.|She stumbled forwards and fell. 她向前踉跄了一下，摔倒了。|近义词：ahead, onward, forth|反义词:backwards, backward|副词
