cyclostome:/ˈsaɪkləˌstoʊm/ | n. | 圆口纲动物 | An agnathan, jawless vertebrate fish of the class Cyclostomata, such as a lamprey or hagfish.  Characterized by a circular, sucker-like mouth. | Lampreys are cyclostomes that are parasitic on other fish. 七鳃鳗是寄生在其他鱼类身上的圆口纲动物。| synonym: agnathan | antonym: gnathostome | noun


cyclostome:/ˈsaɪkləˌstoʊm/ | adj. | 圆口的 | Having a circular mouth; pertaining to the Cyclostomata. |  The cyclostome mouth of the lamprey allows it to attach to its host. 七鳃鳗的圆形口器使其能够附着在宿主身上。| synonym: circular-mouthed | antonym: jawed | adjective
