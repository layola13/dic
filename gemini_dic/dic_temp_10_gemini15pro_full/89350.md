homologous:/həˈmɒləɡəs/ /hoʊˈmɑːləɡəs/| adj.|同源的，相似的|Having the same relation, relative position, or structure, in particular.|The wings of a bat and the arms of a human are homologous structures. 蝙蝠的翅膀和人类的胳膊是同源结构。|synonyms: analogous, corresponding, similar|antonyms: heterologous, dissimilar|adjective

homologous:/həˈmɒləɡəs/ /hoʊˈmɑːləɡəs/| n.|同系物|A thing that is homologous with something else.|In chemistry, methane and ethane are homologues. 在化学中，甲烷和乙烷是同系物。|synonyms: counterpart, equivalent|antonyms: N/A|noun
