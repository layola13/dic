papyraceous:/ˌpeɪpəˈreɪʃəs/
adjective

thin, dry, and papery
The dried leaves were papyraceous and crumbled easily. 这些干枯的叶子像纸一样薄，很容易碎。

The petals of the flower are papyraceous.  这朵花的花瓣像纸一样薄。

Synonym: papery, chartaceous
Antonym: thick, fleshy
