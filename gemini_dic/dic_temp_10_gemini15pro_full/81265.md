granitoids: /ˈɡrænɪˌtɔɪdz/ | n. |花岗岩类；类花岗岩|A group of intrusive igneous rocks similar to granite in composition and texture, including granite, granodiorite, quartz monzonite, and tonalite.  They are phaneritic (coarse-grained) and typically light-colored.|The geologist identified the sample as granitoids. 地质学家将该样本鉴定为花岗岩类。|Synonyms: plutonic rocks, intrusive rocks|Antonyms: volcanic rocks, extrusive rocks|noun
