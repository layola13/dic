fastback:/ˈfæstˌbæk/| n. adj.|快背式汽车，快背式的|A car with a sloping back that continues in a smooth curve from the roof to the rear bumper.|例句：He just bought a new fastback sports car. 他刚买了一辆新的快背式跑车。

例句：The fastback design improves the car's aerodynamics. 快背式设计提高了汽车的空气动力学性能。|近义词：hatchback, coupe, sports car|反义词:sedan, saloon|词性:名词, 形容词
词源: "fast" (meaning aerodynamically efficient) + "back" (referring to the sloping rear)
