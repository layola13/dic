bicker:/ˈbɪkər/| vi. n.|争吵，斗嘴|To argue about petty and trivial matters.|The couple bickered about who would do the dishes. 这对夫妇为谁洗碗而争吵。|
They bickered constantly over minor details. 他们不断地为一些小细节争吵。|
近义词：quarrel, squabble, argue, wrangle|
反义词：agree, concur, harmonize|
