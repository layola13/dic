fortuning:/ˈfɔːrtʃuːnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
算命，预测命运
The act of telling someone's fortune, usually by examining their palm, tarot cards, or other methods.
She was fortuning by reading tea leaves. 她正在通过茶叶占卜算命。
He made his living by fortuning wealthy clients. 他靠为富有的客户算命为生。
