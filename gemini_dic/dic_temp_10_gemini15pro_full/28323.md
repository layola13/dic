caracul: /ˈkærəkəl/ | n. | 卡拉库尔羔皮，卡拉库尔羔羊 |  A type of curly, glossy fur obtained from the very young lambs of a particular breed of Central Asian sheep.  Also refers to the sheep itself. | The coat was made of luxurious caracul. 这件外套是用奢华的卡拉库尔羔皮制成的。|  Synonyms: broadtail, persian lamb | Antonyms: None | Noun

caracul: /ˈkærəkəl/ | adj. | 卡拉库尔的 | Made from caracul fur. |  She wore a caracul hat. 她戴着一顶卡拉库尔帽子。| Synonyms: broadtail, persian lamb | Antonyms: None | Adjective
