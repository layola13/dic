overemotes:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊts/| v. |过度表达情感；表演过火|To express emotions in an exaggerated or excessive way, often in a theatrical manner.  It implies artificiality or insincerity.|He overemotes in every scene, making his character seem unbelievable. 他在每个场景中都表演过火，使得他的角色显得不可信。|Synonyms: overact, exaggerate, dramatize|Antonyms: underplay, downplay|verb
