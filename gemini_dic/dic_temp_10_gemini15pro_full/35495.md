coadapted:/ˌkoʊəˈdæptɪd/
adjective
共同适应的
Having adapted to each other or to the same environment.  Two species are said to be coadapted when they evolve together, each influencing the other's evolution.
The bees and the flowers they pollinate are coadapted. 蜜蜂和它们授粉的花朵是共同适应的。
The fig wasp and the fig tree have a coadapted relationship. 无花果蜂和无花果树具有共同适应的关系。
Synonyms: coevolved, mutually adapted
Antonyms: maladapted, unadapted
词性: adjective
