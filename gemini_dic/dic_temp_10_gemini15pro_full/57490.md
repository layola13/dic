dragantine:/ˈdræɡəntiːn/ | adj. | 龙形的；凶猛的 | Resembling or suggestive of a dragon; fierce or formidable. | The dragantine gargoyle perched menacingly on the cathedral roof.  龙形的滴水嘴兽威风凛凛地蹲坐在大教堂的屋顶上。| 近义词: dragonish, draconian, ferocious | 反义词: gentle, meek | 词性: adjective

dragantine:/ˈdræɡəntiːn/ | n. |  (古)一种快速帆船 | A type of swift sailing ship, especially one used in the Mediterranean. | The dragantine sliced through the waves, its sails billowing in the wind. 快速帆船破浪前进，船帆在风中鼓起。 | 近义词: galleon, caravel | 反义词: (无明确反义词，可考虑与之速度对比的船只例如 barge) | 词性: noun


词源: From Italian *dragantino*, ultimately from Latin *draco* ("dragon"). 源自意大利语 *dragantino*，最终源自拉丁语 *draco*（“龙”）。
