consumes:/kənˈsuːmz/
verb
消耗，消费，吞噬
uses up, eats, drinks, or destroys something
The car consumes a lot of fuel. 这辆车很耗油。
近义词:devours, expends, uses, spends
反义词:produces, creates, conserves, saves
词性:v.


consumes:/kənˈsuːmz/
verb
充满，使…着迷
to fill somebody’s mind completely so that they cannot think of anything else
He was consumed with guilt after the accident. 事故发生后，他充满了内疚。
近义词:engrosses, absorbs, preoccupies
反义词:bores, disinterests
词性:v.
