dovishness:/ˈdʌvɪʃnəs/| n.|鸽派性，温和的立场|The candidate's dovishness on foreign policy appealed to many voters.  这位候选人在外交政策上的鸽派立场吸引了许多选民。|例句：The president's dovishness on the issue surprised many. 总统在这个问题上的温和立场让很多人感到惊讶。

The dovishness of the new administration is a welcome change. 新政府的温和立场是一个受欢迎的变化。|近义词：pacifism, moderation,conciliation|反义词:hawkishness, belligerence|词性:noun

Etymology->
From dove (n.) + -ish (adj. suffix) + -ness (n. suffix).  "Dove" has symbolized peace since ancient times, contrasted with the "hawk" which represents war.
