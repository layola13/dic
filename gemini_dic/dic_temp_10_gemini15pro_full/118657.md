mestling:/ˈmestlɪŋ/ | n. | 幼鸟；雏鸟 | A young bird that has left the nest but is not yet fully fledged and able to fly well. | The mestling hopped clumsily on the branch.  雏鸟笨拙地在树枝上跳来跳去。 | fledgling, chick | adult bird | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *mestling, from Old English *mestlung, from mest "nest".
