extra-essential:/ˌekstrə ɪˈsɛnʃəl/
adj.
极其必要的，非常重要的
more essential than essential; absolutely necessary
例句：During a pandemic, hand sanitizer becomes extra-essential. 在疫情期间，洗手液变得极其重要。

例句：Access to clean water is extra-essential for survival. 获得清洁用水对于生存至关重要。

近义词：crucial, vital, indispensable, paramount
反义词：unimportant, nonessential, trivial
词性：形容词

词源：
"extra-" (prefix) from Latin, meaning "outside, beyond"
"-essential" from Late Latin essentialis, from essentia "being, essence," from esse "to be"
