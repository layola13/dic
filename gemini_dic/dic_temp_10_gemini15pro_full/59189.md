dysodile:/ˈdɪsəˌdaɪl/ | n. | 页岩，油页岩 | A laminated, bituminous shale, often containing fossil fish remains, which emits a fetid odor when burned. |  The dysodile found in Scotland is known for its high oil content.  苏格兰发现的油页岩以其高含油量而闻名。

| Synonym: oil shale, bituminous shale | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps anthracite coal as it burns cleanly) | noun
