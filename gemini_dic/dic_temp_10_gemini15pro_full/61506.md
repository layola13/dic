embroil:/ɪmˈbrɔɪl/ | v. | 使卷入；使陷入；纠缠 | to involve someone deeply in an argument, conflict, or difficult situation | The two countries were embroiled in a bitter dispute over territory. 这两个国家陷入了关于领土的激烈争端。|  The CEO found himself embroiled in a scandal. 首席执行官发现自己卷入了一场丑闻。| synonyms: entangle, involve, implicate | antonyms: extricate, disentangle | verb
