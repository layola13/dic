osiered:/ˈoʊʒərd/| adj.|柳条编制的，用柳条围成的|Made of or lined with osiers (willow twigs).|The riverbank was bordered by an osiered fence. 河岸边围着一圈柳条篱笆。|近义词：wicker, woven|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unfenced", "open")|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ōsrig, from ōs "osier" + -ig.  The word "osier" itself comes from Proto-Germanic *ōz-riją- "willow twig used for weaving."
