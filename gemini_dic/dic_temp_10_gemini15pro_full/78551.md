ghosts:/ɡoʊsts/| n.|鬼魂，幽灵|The apparition of ghosts is a common theme in horror stories. 鬼魂的出现是恐怖故事中的常见主题。|例句：The old house is said to be haunted by ghosts. 据说这栋老房子闹鬼。

例句：She claims to have seen ghosts in her house. 她声称在她家里看到过鬼魂。

|近义词：spirits, phantoms, specters, apparitions|反义词:reality, physicality|词性:名词
