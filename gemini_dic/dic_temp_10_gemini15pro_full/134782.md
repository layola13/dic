nonmetaphorically:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtəˈfɒrɪkli/ | adv. | 非比喻地，字面上地 | In a literal sense; without using metaphors.  Describing something exactly as it is, without any figurative language. | He explained the situation nonmetaphorically, using plain and simple language. 他用简洁明了的语言，非比喻地解释了情况。

He nonmetaphorically hit the roof when he heard the news. 当他听到这个消息时，他真的气得跳脚了。（这里hit the roof本是比喻义“勃然大怒”，但nonmetaphorically限定了其字面意思） | literally, directly | figuratively, metaphorically | adverb
