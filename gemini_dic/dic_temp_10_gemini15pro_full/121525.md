mismade:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 做得不好的；做坏了的；畸形的 | Badly or incorrectly made; deformed. | The dress was mismade and had to be returned. 这件衣服做坏了，必须退货。| The mismade toy had one leg shorter than the other.  这个做坏了的玩具有一条腿比另一条短。 | 近义词: faulty, defective, flawed, imperfect | 反义词: well-made, perfect, flawless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From mis- + made, past participle of make.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating something wrong or incorrect.
