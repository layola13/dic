martially:/ˈmɑːrʃəli/ | adv. | 以军事方式地，好战地 | In a martial or warlike manner; aggressively. |  The troops were martially arrayed. 部队以战斗队形排列。| 近义词: militarily, воинственно, warlike, combatively | 反义词: peacefully, pacifistically | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Middle French *martial*, from Latin martiālis (“of Mars, warlike”), from Mars (“Mars, the Roman god of war”).
