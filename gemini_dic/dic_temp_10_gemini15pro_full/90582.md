hulks:/hʌlks/| n.| （绿巨人）浩克，笨重的人或物|plural of hulk, referring to large, strong people or things, often with a clumsy or destructive connotation.  It can also refer to multiple instances of the Marvel character, The Hulk.|The incredible hulks smashed through the city.  难以置信的浩克们砸穿了城市。|Synonyms: behemoths, giants, monsters|Antonyms: dwarfs, midgets|Noun

hulk:/hʌlk/| n.|  笨重的人或物，（船的）残骸|a large, heavy, clumsy person or thing; the abandoned wreck or shell of a ship, especially a large one.|The old hulk of a ship lay rusting in the harbor. 那艘船的旧残骸躺在港口里生锈。|Synonyms: behemoth, giant, monster, wreck|Antonyms: dwarf, midget|Noun
