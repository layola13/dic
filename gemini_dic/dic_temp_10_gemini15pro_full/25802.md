burgee:/ˈbɜːrdʒiː/
n.
a small flag
A small flag, typically triangular or swallow-tailed, used on boats.
The yacht club's burgee snapped in the wind.  游艇俱乐部的三角旗在风中啪啪作响。
近义词: pennant, flag
