mesaticephalic:/ˌmɛsɪtɪˈsɛfəlɪk/ |adj.| 中头型的 |Having a head of medium proportions; having a cephalic index between 75 and 80.  A mesaticephalic skull is considered typical or average in size and shape for humans. |Her skull was classified as mesaticephalic. 她的颅骨被归类为中头型的。|  |brachycephalic (短头型的), dolichocephalic (长头型的)|adjective
mesocephalic:/ˌmɛsoʊˈsɛfəlɪk/ |adj.| 中脑的 |Relating to the midbrain. |Damage to the mesocephalic area can cause a variety of neurological problems. 中脑区域的损伤会导致各种神经系统问题。|midbrain| |adjective
