bridegrooms:/ˈbraɪdɡruːmz/ | n. |新郎（复数）|A man who is about to get married or has just got married. | The bridegrooms looked nervous but happy. 新郎们看起来很紧张但很开心。| The bridegrooms exchanged rings. 新郎们交换了戒指。| 近义词:grooms | 反义词:brides | 词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *bridegome, from Old English *brȳdguma (“bridegroom”), from *brȳd (“bride”) + *guma (“man”). Cognate with Old Frisian brēdegom, Old Saxon brūdigomo, Old High German brūtigomo, Gothic 𐌱𐍂𐌹𐌳𐌲𐌿𐌼𐍃 (bridgums).
