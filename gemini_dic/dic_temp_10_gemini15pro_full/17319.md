be-a-law-unto-oneself:/bi ə lɔː ˈʌntuː wʌnˈsɛlf/
idiomatic phrase
我行我素；不受约束
to behave in a way that is independent and not controlled by rules or other people
He's always been a law unto himself, doing whatever he pleases. 他总是独断独行，想做什么就做什么。
independent, unconventional, maverick, nonconformist
conformist, conventional, obedient
