nonreceipts:/ˌnɒnriˈsiːts/
|n.|未收到款项；收据缺失|The nonreceipts of several invoices caused a discrepancy in the accounts. 几张发票的未收到导致了账目差异。|
|n.|未收到货物|The nonreceipts of the ordered materials delayed the project.  订购材料的未收到延迟了项目。|
|Etymology:|non- (prefix) meaning "not" + receipt (noun) meaning "the act of receiving something" |
|Synonyms:|non-payment, outstanding payment, lack of payment, absence of receipts|
|Antonyms:|receipts, payments, received funds|
|Word type:|noun|
