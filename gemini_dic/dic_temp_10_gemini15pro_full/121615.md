misorients:/ˌmɪsɔːriˈɛnts/ | v. | 使迷失方向，使失去方向感 | To cause (someone) to lose their bearings; to confuse or disorient. | The dense fog misoriented the hikers, and they wandered off the trail. 浓雾使徒步旅行者迷失了方向，他们偏离了小路。

The complex city streets misoriented her. 复杂的城市街道让她迷失了方向。 | 近义词: disorient, confuse, bewilder, perplex | 反义词: orient, guide, direct | 词性: 动词
