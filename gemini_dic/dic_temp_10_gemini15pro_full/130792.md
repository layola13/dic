nonannexable:/ˌnɒnəˈnɛksəbl/ /ˌnɑːnəˈnɛksəbl/
adjective
不可兼并的；不能吞并的
Not capable of being annexed.  Something that cannot be incorporated into another territory or property.
The small island nation declared itself nonannexable, determined to maintain its independence.  这个小岛国宣布自己不可兼并，决心保持独立。
The tribe's ancestral lands were considered nonannexable by the neighboring kingdom.  这个部落的祖先土地被邻国视为不可吞并的。
近义词:inseparable, indivisible, unattachable
反义词:annexable
词性:adjective
