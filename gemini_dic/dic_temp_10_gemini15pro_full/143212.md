outcrops:/ˈaʊtkrɑːps/ | n. |露出地面的岩层；露头|A visible exposure of bedrock or other stratum at the earth's surface. |The geologist studied the outcrops in the canyon to determine the age of the rocks.| synonyms: exposure, outcropping, protrusion | antonyms: subsurface, underground | noun

outcrop:/ˈaʊtkrɑːp/ | v. |露出地面；显露|To appear or become visible at the surface of the earth. |Veins of gold outcropped in the hills. | synonyms: emerge, appear, surface | antonyms: disappear, submerge, vanish | verb
