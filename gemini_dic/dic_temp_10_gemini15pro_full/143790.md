outname:/ˌaʊtˈneɪm/| n. |外号，别名|An outname is a name other than a person's legal name, often used in a particular context or community.| He earned the outname "Speedy" because of how fast he ran. 他跑得很快，因此得了个“飞毛腿”的外号。|synonym:nickname, alias, sobriquet|antonym:legal name|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'out' (meaning outside or beyond) + 'name' (a word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known or referred to).  Indicates a name used outside of formal or official contexts.
