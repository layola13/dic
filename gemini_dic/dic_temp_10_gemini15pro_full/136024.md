nonrealities:/ˌnɑːnriˈælɪtiz/ | n. | 非现实，虚构的事物 |  Things that are not real or do not exist; fantasies or illusions. |  He lives in a world of nonrealities, constantly daydreaming. 他生活在一个非现实的世界里，不断地做着白日梦。 |  The author skillfully blends realities and nonrealities in her latest novel. 作者在她的最新小说中巧妙地融合了现实和虚构。 | synonyms: fantasies, illusions, fabrications, inventions, figments, chimeras | antonyms: realities, facts, truths | noun

Etymology:
From non- + reality + -ities.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Reality" comes from the Late Latin "realitas," from "realis" meaning "real," ultimately from the Latin "res" meaning "thing."  "-ities" is a suffix used to form plural nouns.
