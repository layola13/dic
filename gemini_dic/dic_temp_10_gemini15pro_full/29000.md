carthusian:/kɑːrˈθuːʒən/ /kɑːrˈθuːziən/
carthusian| n. adj.|卡尔特教派的修士；卡尔特教派的；禁欲的，苦行的|A member of a strict monastic order founded in 1084; of or relating to the Carthusian order.|例句：The Carthusian monks live a life of silence and solitude. 卡尔特教派的修士过着沉默和孤独的生活。|近义词：ascetic, austere, monastic, reclusive|反义词：hedonistic, worldly|词性:名词,形容词
