cambrians: /ˈkeɪmbriənz/
noun|寒武纪生物；寒武纪的人们|organisms that lived during the Cambrian period; people who lived during the Cambrian period|The Cambrian explosion saw a rapid diversification of life. 寒武纪生命大爆发见证了生命的快速多样化。|The museum has a fascinating exhibit on Cambrian fossils.  这家博物馆有一个关于寒武纪化石的引人入胜的展览。|近义词: Cambrian period organisms, Cambrian era inhabitants|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可考虑后期地质时代，例如) Mesozoic organisms, Cenozoic organisms|词性:n.

Etymology:
From "Cambria," the Latin name for Wales, where rocks from this period were first studied, + "-an," a suffix forming adjectives and nouns.  源自“Cambria”，威尔士的拉丁名，该时期的岩石最初是在那里研究的，加上“-an”，一个构成形容词和名词的后缀。
