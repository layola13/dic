abrasional:/əˈbreɪʒənəl/| adj.|磨损的，擦伤的|Relating to or caused by abrasion; tending to abrade.|The abrasive action of the sand smoothed the surface of the rock. 沙子的磨蚀作用使岩石表面光滑。|The constant rubbing caused abrasional wear on the fabric. 不断的摩擦导致织物磨损。|近义词：abrasive, erosive, grinding|反义词：smooth, polished|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From abrasion + -al. Abrasion from Latin abradere, from ab- + radere "to scrape".
USAGE->
The abrasional wear on the gears needs attention. = 齿轮上的磨损需要注意。

The archaeologist discovered abrasional marks on ancient tools. = 考古学家在古代工具上发现了磨损痕迹。

PHRASE->
abrasional wear = 磨损
abrasional damage = 磨损损伤
abrasional resistance = 耐磨性
abrasional forces = 磨蚀力
abrasional process = 磨蚀过程
