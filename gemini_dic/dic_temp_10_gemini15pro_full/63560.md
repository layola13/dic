envidia:/enˈviðiə/ | n. | 羡慕，嫉妒 | A feeling of discontented or resentful longing aroused by someone else's possessions, qualities, or luck. | She felt a pang of envidia when she saw her friend's new car. 她看到朋友的新车时感到一阵嫉妒。|  Example: His success aroused the envidia of his colleagues. 他的成功引起了同事们的嫉妒。| Synonyms: jealousy, envy, covetousness | Antonyms: admiration, goodwill | noun
