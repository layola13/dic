noncompetitivenesses:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏竞争力；无竞争力
The state or quality of not being competitive; lack of competitiveness.
The report highlighted the noncompetitivenesses of several industries in the region.  报告强调了该地区几个行业的缺乏竞争力。
The company's noncompetitivenesses ultimately led to its downfall.  该公司的缺乏竞争力最终导致了它的垮台。
Synonyms: uncompetitiveness, lack of competitiveness
Antonyms: competitiveness,  competitivenesses


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix meaning "not") + competitiveness + -es (plural suffix)
"Competitiveness" comes from "competitive" + "-ness". "Competitive" comes from "compete" (from Latin competere "to strive in common") + "-itive".
