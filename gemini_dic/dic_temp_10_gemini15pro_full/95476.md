incivil:/ɪnˈsɪvəl/ | adj. |不文明的，粗鲁的，失礼的 | Not civilized; rude or impolite. |  He made some very incivil remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌做了一些非常不礼貌的评论。

| Example: His incivil behavior at the dinner party embarrassed his wife. 他在晚宴上不文明的行为让他的妻子很尴尬。| Synonyms: rude, impolite, disrespectful, discourteous, uncivil, boorish, churlish | Antonyms: polite, courteous, respectful, civil, gracious | Word Class: Adjective
