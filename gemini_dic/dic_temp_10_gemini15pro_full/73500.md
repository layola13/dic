foreseeability:/fɔːrˌsiːəˈbɪləti/
n.
可预见性
the ability to anticipate that something might happen in the future
The accident was caused by a lack of foreseeability on the part of the driver.  由于驾驶员缺乏预见性，导致了事故。
近义词：predictability, anticipation
反义词：unforeseeability, unexpectedness
词性: noun

foreseeable:/fɔːrˈsiːəbl/
adj.
可预见的
able to be foreseen or predicted
The consequences of the decision were foreseeable.  这个决定的后果是可以预见的。
近义词：predictable, anticipated
反义词：unforeseeable, unexpected
词性: adjective
