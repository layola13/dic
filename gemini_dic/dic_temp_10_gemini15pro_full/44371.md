cross-country:/ˈkrɒsˈkʌntri/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˈkʌntri/ (UK)| n.|越野赛跑|A race across fields or open country.|例句：She won the cross-country championship. 她赢得了越野锦标赛。|近义词：off-road race|反义词：track race|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cross + country.  "Cross," in this context, means "across" or "over," referring to traversing the countryside. "Country" refers to open land or rural areas.
