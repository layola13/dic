dimity: /ˈdɪmɪti/ | n. | 斜纹布，薄棉布 | A strong, sheer cotton fabric with a corded surface, typically white or pale-colored. |  She wore a dress of white dimity. 她穿着一件白色斜纹布连衣裙。 | The curtains were made of a delicate dimity. 窗帘是用精致的薄棉布做的。 | 近义词: muslin, lawn, batiste | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something heavy like) velvet, corduroy | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle French *dimité, from Medieval Latin *dimitum, alteration of Latin *dimitton, from Greek δίμιτος (dímitos, “double-threaded”), from δίς (dís, “twice”) + μίτος (mítos, “thread”).
