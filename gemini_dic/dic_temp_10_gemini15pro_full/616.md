abraid:/əˈbreɪd/| vt.|磨损，擦伤；责骂|To rub or wear away by friction; to scold severely.|The harsh rope abraided the climber's hands. 粗糙的绳子擦伤了登山者的手。

The coach abraided the team for their poor performance. 教练因队员表现不佳而严厉斥责他们。|近义词：chafe, graze, erode, scold, berate, reprimand|反义词：soothe, mollify, praise|词性：动词

USAGE->

The rough fabric abraids the skin. = 粗糙的布料磨损皮肤。
His constant criticism abraids her confidence. = 他不断的批评打击了她的自信心。
