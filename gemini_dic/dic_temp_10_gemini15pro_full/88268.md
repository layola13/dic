hinny:/ˈhɪni/| n.|骡;宝贝;亲爱的|A sterile hybrid offspring of a female donkey and a male horse. Also a term of endearment.|例句：The farmer used his hinny for plowing the fields. 农民用他的骡子耕地。

例句：She called her child "hinny," a term of endearment common in her region. 她叫她的孩子“宝贝”，这是她所在地区常用的爱称。|近义词：mule, darling, sweetheart, honey|反义词: (No direct antonym for a hybrid animal.
