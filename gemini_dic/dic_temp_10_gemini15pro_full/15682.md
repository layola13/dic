baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/
|n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in some Asian and Middle Eastern countries; also, a bribe.|Example: He offered the official a baksheesh to expedite the process. 他向官员提供了一笔赏钱以加快办理进度。

Example:  She gave the porter a small baksheesh for carrying her luggage. 她给了搬运工一小笔小费来搬运行李。|近义词:tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps penalty if referring to a bribe)|词性:noun
