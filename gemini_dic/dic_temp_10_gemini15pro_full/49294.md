delicatessen: /ˌdɛlɪkəˈtɛsən/ | n. | 熟食店，熟食 | A store selling prepared foods, such as cheeses, salads, and cooked meats.  |  例句：I bought some pastrami at the delicatessen. 我在熟食店买了一些熏牛肉。

例句：The delicatessen offers a wide variety of imported cheeses. 这家熟食店提供各种各样的进口奶酪。

近义词：deli | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

delicatessen: /ˌdɛlɪkəˈtɛsən/ | n. |  美味，佳肴 (plural: delicatessen or delicatessens) |  A choice or expensive food. | 例句： Smoked salmon is considered a delicatessen. 熏鲑鱼被认为是一种美味佳肴。

例句：They served a variety of delicatessens at the party.  他们在聚会上提供了各种各样的美味佳肴。

近义词：delicacy, gourmet food | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Delikatessen*, plural of *Delikatesse*, from French *délicatesse* "delicacy," from *délicat* "delicate".
