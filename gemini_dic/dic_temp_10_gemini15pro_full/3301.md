afflicters:/əˈflɪktərz/| n. |折磨者，使痛苦的人|Those who inflict pain or suffering on others. |The afflicters showed no remorse for their actions.  折磨者对自己的行为毫无悔意。|Synonyms: abusers, bullies|Antonyms: saviors, rescuers|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *afflictere*, from Old French *aflicter*, from Latin *afflictāre*, from *afflictus*, past participle of *affligere* ("to strike down, damage, weaken"), from *ad-* ("to, towards") + *fligere* ("to strike").
