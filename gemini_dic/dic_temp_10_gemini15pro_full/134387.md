nonintimidating:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不吓人的，不令人生畏的 | Not causing fear or intimidation; approachable and reassuring. |  The teacher created a nonintimidating atmosphere in the classroom. 老师在教室里营造了一种轻松的氛围。|  The interview process was nonintimidating and friendly. 面试过程轻松友好。 | 近义词: approachable, friendly, welcoming, reassuring, easygoing | 反义词: intimidating, threatening, daunting, scary, formidable | 词性: adjective

词源:  Comes from "non-" (meaning "not") + "intimidating" (from the verb "intimidate," which comes from the Late Latin "intimare" meaning "to make afraid, frighten").
