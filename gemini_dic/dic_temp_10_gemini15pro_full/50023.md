denominationalist:/dɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪst/
n.
宗派主义者；教派主义者
A person who strongly supports the beliefs and practices of a particular religious denomination and often opposes cooperation or unity with other denominations.
The denominationalist refused to participate in the interfaith service.  这位宗派主义者拒绝参加跨宗教的礼拜。
Denominationalists sometimes prioritize their own doctrines over broader Christian unity.  宗派主义者有时将他们自己的教义置于更广泛的基督教团结之上。
synonyms: sectarian, partisan
antonyms: ecumenist, interdenominational
noun


denominational:/dɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adj.
教派的；宗派的
Relating to or characteristic of a particular religious denomination.
Denominational differences sometimes lead to conflict. 教派差异有时会导致冲突。
The school offered denominational religious education classes.  这所学校提供教派宗教教育课程。
synonyms: sectarian, factional
antonyms: ecumenical, nondenominational, interdenominational
adjective
