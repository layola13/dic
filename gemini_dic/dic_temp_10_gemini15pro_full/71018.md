firmed:/fɜːmd/ | v. | 坚挺，稳固，变坚实；确定，固定 |  became stronger, more stable, or more definite | The prices firmed after the initial drop. 价格在最初下跌后趋于稳定。| 近义词: strengthened, stabilized, solidified | 反义词: weakened, destabilized, softened | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From the verb "firm" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The verb "firm" comes from the Middle English *fermen*, from Old French *ferme*, ultimately from Latin *firmus* meaning "strong, steadfast, durable."
