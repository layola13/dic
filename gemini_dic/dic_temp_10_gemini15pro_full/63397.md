entozoic:/ˌɛntəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
体内寄生的；肠内寄生的
Living within the body of an animal, especially in the intestines.
例句：Entozoic parasites can cause a range of health problems. 体内寄生虫会导致一系列健康问题。

例句：The study focused on the entozoic fauna of the host organism. 这项研究的重点是宿主生物体内的寄生动物群。
近义词：endoparasitic, internal
反义词：ectozoic, external
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *entos* (within) + *zōikos* (of animals, from *zōion*, animal).
