greetingly: /ˈɡriːtɪŋli/ | adv. | 问候地，致敬地 | In a way that expresses greetings or welcome. | She smiled greetingly as he entered the room. 她在他进房间时，带着问候的微笑。 |  She waved greetingly at the newcomers. 她向新来的人问候地挥了挥手。| 近义词: warmly, cordially, affably | 反义词: coldly, unwelcomingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From greeting + -ly.  "Greeting" comes from the Old English "grētan," meaning "to approach, address, salute."
