non-normal:/nɒnˈnɔːməl/ | adj. | 非正常的，异常的 | Not conforming to a standard; unusual or atypical. | The non-normal distribution of the data skewed the results. 数据的非正态分布使结果出现了偏差。 | The recent weather patterns have been non-normal. 最近的天气模式异常。 | 近义词: abnormal, atypical, irregular, deviant | 反义词: normal, typical, standard, regular | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + normal (from Latin normalis, meaning "according to a carpenter's square," hence "standard, regular")
