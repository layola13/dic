hoi:/hɔɪ/ | int. | 嗨，你好 | An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey".  It's primarily used in South Africa, the Netherlands, and Belgium. | Hoi, how's it going? 嗨，你好吗？ | Hi, hey, hello | Goodbye, farewell | Interjection

USAGE->

Hoi, what's your name? = 嗨，你叫什么名字？
Hoi, where do you live? = 嗨，你住在哪里？

Hoi, what are you doing? = 嗨，你在做什么？
Hoi, are you watching TV now? = 嗨，你现在在看电视吗？

Hoi, have you finished your homework? = 嗨，你完成作业了吗？
