dimplier:/ˈdɪmplɪər/| adj.|酒窝更深的，笑起来更迷人的|Having or showing more or deeper dimples.  A comparative adjective of "dimply". It describes someone who has more pronounced dimples than before or than someone else.|Her smile was dimplier than usual, radiating genuine happiness. 她的笑容比平常更迷人，散发着真正的幸福。|近义词：more dimpled|反义词:less dimpled|词性:adjective

dimply:/ˈdɪmpli/| adj.|有酒窝的，酒窝深的|Having or showing dimples, especially in the cheeks or chin.|The dimply child giggled at the clown's antics. 那个有酒窝的孩子咯咯地笑着小丑的滑稽动作。|近义词：dimpled|反义词:smooth, unblemished|词性:adjective

词源: dimple (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives)  "dimple" 源自古英语 "dympel,"  可能模仿了小凹陷的形状。
