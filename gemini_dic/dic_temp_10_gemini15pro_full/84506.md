ham-fisted:/ˌhæmˈfɪstɪd/
adjective
笨手笨脚的；粗鲁的；拙劣的
clumsy, awkward, and insensitive; lacking finesse or skill. Especially in handling things physically.
He made a ham-fisted attempt to fix the vase, but only made it worse. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理花瓶，结果反而弄得更糟了。
Near Synonyms:clumsy, awkward, inept, maladroit, gauche, ungainly
Antonyms:skillful, deft, adroit, dexterous
词性:adjective
