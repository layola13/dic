padlocks:/ˈpædlɒks/ (US) /ˈpædlɒks/ (UK)| n. |挂锁|A lock that has a U-shaped bar hinged at one end and passing through the other, fastened by a spring bolt that can be opened by a key.|He secured the shed with a sturdy padlock. 他用一把结实的挂锁锁住了棚屋。|近义词：lock, bolt, clasp|反义词:key, unlock|noun
