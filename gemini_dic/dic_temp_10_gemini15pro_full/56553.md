domineeringness:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋnəs/| n.|专横，跋扈，盛气凌人|The quality of being domineering; an overbearing manner.  A tendency to control or dictate others' actions or thoughts.|Her domineeringness made it difficult for anyone to offer suggestions.|近义词：bossiness, imperiousness, arrogance, high-handedness|反义词:submissiveness, humility, meekness|词性:noun

domineering:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋ/| adj.|专横的，跋扈的，盛气凌人的|Trying to control other people without considering their opinions or feelings.|His domineering personality alienated many of his colleagues.|近义词：bossy, imperious, overbearing, tyrannical|反义词:submissive, humble, meek|词性:adjective

词源: From domineer + -ing + -ness. Domineer comes from the Dutch domineren, meaning "to dominate," ultimately from Latin dominus "lord, master."
