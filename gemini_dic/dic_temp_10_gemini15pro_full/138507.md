PHRASE->
*Novus ordo seclorum* -  "New order of the ages" - A Latin phrase found on the Great Seal of the United States.
A *novus homo* - A "new man" - In ancient Rome, a man who was the first in his family to achieve senatorial rank.
*Novus codex* -  A "new code" or "new law."
Embrace the *novus* -  To welcome the new or innovative.
*Terra novus* - "New land."  Often used figuratively to describe a new field of study or endeavor.
