lumiere:/luːˈmjɛər/ | n. | 光，光明，启迪 |  A French word meaning "light." Often used in English to refer to the early days of filmmaking, specifically the Lumière brothers, Auguste and Louis, who were among the first filmmakers. | The Lumiere brothers' invention revolutionized entertainment.  卢米埃尔兄弟的发明彻底改变了娱乐业。| Synonyms: light, illumination, brilliance | Antonyms: darkness, obscurity | noun
