illegitimates:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪməts/
| n. |私生子，庶子（illegitimate 的复数形式）|Plural form of illegitimate, referring to children born out of wedlock.| 例句：Historically, illegitimates faced social stigma.  历史上，私生子面临着社会耻辱。

词源:
From Late Latin illegitimatus, from in- ‘not’ + legitimatus ‘legitimate’ (from legitimus ‘lawful,’ from lex ‘law’).
