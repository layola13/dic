claret:/ˈklærət/ | n. | 紫红色，红葡萄酒 | A dry red wine, originally and chiefly from Bordeaux in France.  A deep purplish-red color. |  例句：He ordered a bottle of claret with dinner.  他点了一瓶红葡萄酒佐餐。

例句：Her dress was a beautiful shade of claret. 她的裙子是美丽的紫红色。 | 近义词：burgundy, red wine, crimson | 反义词: white wine (for the beverage sense),  pale (for the color sense) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *claret, from Old French claret (“clear, light-colored wine”), from clar (“clear”), from Latin clārus.  Originally referred to a lighter-colored red wine than is now usual.
