failings:/ˈfeɪlɪŋz/
noun
缺点，弱点，不足之处
shortcomings, weaknesses or defects in a person's character or in something they do
Her greatest failings were impatience and a lack of attention to detail. 她最大的缺点是不耐烦和缺乏对细节的关注。
synonyms: shortcomings, weaknesses, defects, flaws, faults, imperfections
antonyms: strengths, virtues, merits
n.
