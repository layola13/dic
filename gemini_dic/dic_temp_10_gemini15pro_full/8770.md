antidromous:/ænˈtɪdrəməs/ | adj. | 逆流的，逆向的 | Directed or moving against the usual direction of flow or movement, especially of a nerve impulse. |  Salmon are antidromous fish, migrating from saltwater to freshwater to spawn. 鲑鱼是逆流性鱼类，它们从咸水洄游到淡水产卵。| synonyms: upstream, retrograde | antonyms: orthodromous | adjective
