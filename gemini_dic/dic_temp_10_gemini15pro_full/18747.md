beriberi:/ˌberɪˈberɪ/ | n. | 脚气病 | A disease caused by a deficiency of vitamin B1 (thiamine), characterized by neurological symptoms, cardiovascular problems, and edema. |  He suffered from beriberi due to his poor diet. 他因为饮食不良患上了脚气病。 |  Synonyms: thiamine deficiency | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a disease) | noun
