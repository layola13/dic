all-set:/ɔːl ˈsɛt/| adj.|准备好的，齐全的，一切就绪的|Indicates that everything is ready or prepared for a particular action or event.  It implies a state of completion or readiness.|例句：We're all set for the picnic. 我们野餐的一切都准备好了。|例句：Are you all set to leave? 你准备好出发了吗？|近义词：ready, prepared, organized|反义词:unprepared, unready|词性:形容词
