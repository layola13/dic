flare:/flɛər/ /fleər/| n. vi. vt.|喇叭裤，逐渐张开|Something that spreads gradually outward, such as the bottom of a skirt or pants leg. To gradually become wider at one end.|The skirt flared out at the bottom. 裙子下摆呈喇叭状。|近义词：widen, expand, spread|反义词：narrow, contract|noun, verb
