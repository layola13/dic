compatible:/kəmˈpætəbl/ | adj. | 兼容的，相容的，可共存的 | Able to exist or occur together without conflict.  |  This software is compatible with most operating systems.  这个软件与大多数操作系统兼容。 |  The new printer is compatible with both Mac and PC. 新打印机兼容 Mac 和 PC。 | synonyms: consistent, harmonious, agreeable, adaptable | antonyms: incompatible, conflicting, discordant | adjective
