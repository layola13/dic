dikes:/daɪks/| n.|堤坝，堤防|A structure built to prevent flooding, typically an embankment of earth or other material.|The dikes protected the city from the rising river. 堤坝保护城市免受河水上涨的侵袭。|近义词：levees, embankments, barriers|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 breach (决口))|noun
dikes:/daɪks/| n.|沟渠|A ditch or water channel.|The farmer dug dikes to irrigate his fields. 农民挖沟渠来灌溉他的田地。|近义词：ditches, channels, canals|反义词:ridges, mounds|noun
