outtrades:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪdz/
verb
胜过；在交易中胜过
to surpass someone in trading or bartering.
例句：He outtraded his competitors and secured the best deal. 他在交易中胜过了他的竞争对手，获得了最好的交易。

例句：She outtraded all the other merchants at the market. 她在市场上胜过了所有其他商人。

近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass
反义词：be outdone, be outsmarted

词性：v.
