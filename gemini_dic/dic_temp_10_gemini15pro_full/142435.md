The action of oscillating; vibration or fluctuation.|The oscillancy of the market makes it difficult to predict future trends. 市场波动使得难以预测未来趋势。|近义词：fluctuation, variation, oscillation, vacillation|反义词：stability, constancy, steadiness|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Latin *oscillans*, present participle of *oscillare* "to swing," from *oscillum* "swing."
