manipulate:/məˈnɪpjəleɪt/ /məˈnɪpjʊleɪt/
verb
操纵，控制；(熟练地)操作
to control something or someone to your advantage, often unfairly or dishonestly
例句：The politician was accused of manipulating public opinion.  这个政客被指控操纵公众舆论。

例句：He's very good at manipulating the media. 他非常擅长操纵媒体。

近义词：control, influence, exploit, maneuver
反义词：help, assist, support
词性：verb
