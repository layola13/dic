attemperance: /ˈætɛmpərəns/ | n. | 节制；戒酒；温和 | moderation or self-restraint, especially in eating and drinking; abstinence from alcoholic drink. |  He preached attemperance and a simple life. 他提倡节制和简朴的生活。 | 近义词: moderation, sobriety, abstinence, self-control | 反义词: intemperance, excess, indulgence | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *attemperantia, from Latin attemperāns, present participle of attemperāre (“to moderate, temper, qualify”), from ad- + temperāre (“to mix in due proportion, temper, moderate”), from tempus (“time, season”).
