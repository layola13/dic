agroforestry:/ˈæɡroʊˈfɔːrɪstri/ | n. | 农林复合经营，农林业 | A land use management system in which trees or shrubs are grown around or among crops or pastureland. This intentional combination of agriculture and forestry has diverse benefits, such as increased biodiversity, improved soil health, and reduced erosion. | Example 1: Agroforestry practices can help farmers diversify their income and increase their resilience to climate change.  农林复合经营实践可以帮助农民实现收入多元化，并增强他们对气候变化的适应能力。

Example 2:  The agroforestry system implemented on the farm included planting fruit trees amongst the rows of coffee plants.  农场实施的农林复合系统包括在咖啡树行间种植果树。 | Near synonyms:  silvopastoral system, integrated farming, sustainable agriculture | Antonyms: monoculture, intensive agriculture | Noun
