neologize: /niˈɒlədʒaɪz/ (UK) /niˈɑːlədʒaɪz/ (US)
verb
创造新词；采用新词
To coin or use new words.
例句：The author neologized extensively, creating a unique vocabulary for his fantasy world.  这位作者大量创造新词，为他奇幻世界构建了独特的词汇。

例句：Scientists often neologize to describe new discoveries or phenomena. 科学家经常会创造新词来描述新的发现或现象。
synonym: coin, invent, create, fabricate
antonym: archaize
词性: verb
