atomizes:/ˈætəˌmaɪzɪz/
verb
使…雾化；使…原子化
reduces (something) to fine particles or spray.
The perfume atomizes as it sprays from the bottle. 香水从瓶子里喷出来时雾化了。
近义词:vaporizes, sprays, mists
反义词:condenses, solidifies
词性:v.
