orca:/ˈɔːrkə/ (US), /ˈɔːkə/ (UK)| n.|虎鲸，逆戟鲸|A large, black and white toothed whale that lives in the ocean and is known for its intelligence and hunting abilities.  It is the largest member of the dolphin family.|例句：The orca breached, its black and white body gleaming in the sun. 虎鲸跃出水面，黑白相间的身体在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：Orcas are highly social animals, living in pods. 虎鲸是高度社会化的动物，群居生活。|近义词：killer whale|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "prey" in a specific context)|词性:noun
