decontaminator:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtər/| n. |去污剂；净化器；消毒器|A substance or device used to remove contaminants, such as radioactive materials, chemicals, or germs, from a person, object, or area.|The workers used a special decontaminator to clean the area after the chemical spill. 工人们在化学品泄漏后使用了一种特殊的去污剂来清洁该区域。|近义词：cleaner, purifier, sanitizer, sterilizer|反义词:contaminant, pollutant|noun
