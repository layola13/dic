militarizing:/ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
军事化，使军事化
The act of equipping or organizing for military purposes.  Increasing the military presence or influence in an area or a sphere of activity.
The government is militarizing the police force. 政府正在将警察部队军事化。
The island nation began militarizing in response to the growing threat.  这个岛国开始军事化，以应对日益增长的威胁。
arming, fortifying, equipping
demilitarizing, pacifying
v.
