andalusite:/ˈændəlʊˌsaɪt/ | n. | 红柱石 | A reddish-brown or gray-green mineral, Al2SiO5, occurring in metamorphic rocks. It is used as a refractory material and as a gemstone. | Andalusite is a common mineral found in metamorphic rocks. 红柱石是一种常见的变质岩矿物。

Andalusite is sometimes cut as a gemstone. 红柱石有时被切割成宝石。 | 近义词:chiastolite, mineral | 反义词: None | 词性:noun
