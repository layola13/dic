aumail:/ɔːˈmeɪl/ |n.| Chain mail; armor made of interlinked metal rings.|A knight wearing aumail rode into battle. 一位身穿锁子甲的骑士骑马投入战斗。|例句：The aumail protected the knight from sword blows. 锁子甲保护骑士免受剑击。

The museum displayed a suit of aumail from the medieval period.  博物馆展出了一套中世纪时期的锁子甲。|近义词：chain mail, mail, hauberk, byrnie|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) civilian clothing, unarmed|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *ausberc, *halsberc ("neck protection"), from Germanic *halsberg ("neck protection").  Related to Old High German *halsberg ("neck protection").  The "au" in aumail reflects a regular sound change in Old French.
