docketed:/ˈdɑːkɪtɪd/
adjective
已归档的，已登记的
Officially recorded and filed.
The case was docketed and scheduled for a hearing next month.  这个案子已登记在案，并安排在下个月举行听证会。
近义词: filed, registered, recorded
反义词: pending, unfiled


docket:/ˈdɑːkɪt/
noun
待办事项清单， docket
A list of cases to be heard in court.
The judge reviewed the docket for the day. 法官查看了当天的待办事项清单。
近义词: agenda, schedule, calendar
反义词: N/A

docket:/ˈdɑːkɪt/
verb
将…登记在案，将…列入待办事项清单
To officially record and file.
The clerk docketed the new case. 书记员将新案件登记在案。
近义词: file, register, record
反义词: remove, expunge
