monistically:/mɒnɪˈstɪkəli/
adv.
一元论地
in a way that is based on monism (the doctrine that mind and matter are formed from a single ultimate substance or principle of being).
Monism considers all of reality as ultimately one substance. Monistically holds that mind and body are one.
He argued monistically that all apparent differences are ultimately superficial. 他一元论地认为所有表面上的差异最终都是肤浅的。
近义词:singly, uniformly
反义词:pluralistically, dualistically
词性:副词
