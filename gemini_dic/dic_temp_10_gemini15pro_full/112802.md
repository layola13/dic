Lucayans: /luːˈkeɪənz/
| n. |卢卡亚人|The Lucayans were the indigenous inhabitants of the Bahamas before the arrival of Christopher Columbus.| The Lucayans were a peaceful people who lived off the land and sea. 卢卡亚人是一个爱好和平的民族，以耕种土地和捕鱼为生。|Synonyms: Taino people (related), islanders |Antonyms:  European colonizers|noun


Lucayans: /luːˈkeɪən/
| adj. |卢卡亚的；卢卡亚人的|Lucayan art is known for its intricate carvings. |The Lucayan culture was tragically destroyed by European colonization. 卢卡亚文化被欧洲殖民者摧毁，令人痛心。|Synonyms: Taino (related), Bahamian (modern context)|Antonyms: European|adjective
