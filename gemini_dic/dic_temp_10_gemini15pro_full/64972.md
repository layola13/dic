estimate: /ˈɛstɪmeɪt/ (US), /ˈɛstɪmeɪt/ (UK) | vt.| 估计; 估价; 评价 | To form an approximate idea of the value, number, quantity, or extent of something. | We estimated the cost of the project to be around $5,000. 我们估计这个项目的成本大约是5000美元。| calculate, assess, gauge | underestimate, overestimate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: *estimaten*, from Old French: *estimer*, from Latin: *aestimare* ‘to value, assess’, from *aes* ‘bronze, money’.
