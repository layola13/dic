enemy: /ˈenəmi/ (US) /ˈɛnəmi/ (UK) | n. | 敌人，仇敌 | A person who feels hatred for, and may try to harm, another. |  He saw her as his worst enemy. 他把她视为自己最大的敌人。

She made many enemies during her rise to power. 她在掌权的过程中树敌众多。

近义词: foe, adversary, opponent, rival

反义词: friend, ally, supporter


词性: noun

词源: From Old French enemi, from Latin inimicus (unfriendly, hostile), from in- (not) + amicus (friend).
