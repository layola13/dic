disfavorable:/dɪsˈfeɪvərəbl/
adjective
不利的，不适宜的
not favorable; unfavorable; tending to discourage or oppose; adverse.
The disfavorable weather conditions led to the cancellation of the outdoor concert.  恶劣的天气条件导致了户外音乐会的取消。
近义词: unfavorable, adverse, disadvantageous, inauspicious
反义词: favorable, advantageous, auspicious, propitious
词性: adjective
