monopyrenous:/ˌmɒnəˈpaɪrɪnəs/
adjective
单核的；单核花粉的
Having a single pyrene or kernel; (Botany) having pollen grains with a single nucleus.
Monopyrenous pollen is characteristic of certain plant families.  单核花粉是某些植物科的特征。
The botanist identified the pollen as monopyrenous. 植物学家将花粉鉴定为单核的。
Near Synonyms: uninucleate
Antonyms: polynucleate
词性: adjective
