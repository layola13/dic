bosun:/ˈboʊsən/ (US) /ˈbəʊsən/ (UK)| n.|水手长；甲板长|A petty officer on a ship, in charge of the deck crew and responsible for the ship's rigging, anchors, cables, and boats.  The bosun maintains discipline among the deck crew and relays the orders of the deck officers.|The bosun piped all hands on deck. 水手长吹哨召集全体船员到甲板上。

The bosun oversaw the loading of the cargo. 水手长监督货物的装载。|近义词：boatswain|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
