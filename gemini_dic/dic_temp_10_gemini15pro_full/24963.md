brushwork:/ˈbrʌʃwɜːrk/| n.|笔法；画法；笔触|The artist's brushwork is delicate and precise.  这位艺术家的笔触细腻而精准。|例句：The brushwork in this painting is very expressive. 这幅画的笔法非常有表现力。|近义词：technique, style, stroke, handling|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: From brush + work.  "brush" referring to the tool and "work" referring to the action or result of using it.
