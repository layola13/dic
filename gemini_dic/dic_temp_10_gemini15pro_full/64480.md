erminois:/ˌɜːrmɪˈnwɑː/
n.
金黄色;  饰有貂皮纹的金色
A tincture of gold bespangled with black ermine spots.
The shield was erminois, a gold field powdered with black ermine tails. 盾牌是金黄色的，上面点缀着黑色的貂尾。
Synonyms: gold, or
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a heraldic tincture)
