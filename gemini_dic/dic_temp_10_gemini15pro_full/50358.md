deponent:/dɪˈpoʊnənt/
n.|证人，宣誓作证者；（语法）去动词|A deponent verb is a verb that is passive in form but active in meaning. |The deponent testified in court. 证人在法庭上作证。|Synonyms: witness, testifier|Antonyms: defendant, accused|noun

deponent:/dɪˈpoʊnənt/
adj.|(语法)去动词的|Relating to a deponent verb. |Latin has many deponent verbs. 拉丁语有很多去动词。|Synonyms: (No direct synonym, describe as "passive in form but active in meaning")|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
