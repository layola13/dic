nonacute:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːt/
adjective
非急性的;非尖锐的
Not acute; not sharp or pointed; not severe or intense.
The patient presented with nonacute abdominal pain.  病人表现为非急性腹痛。
The angle is nonacute, meaning it is greater than or equal to 90 degrees.  这个角是非锐角，意味着它大于或等于90度。
obtuse, mild, chronic
acute, sharp, severe
