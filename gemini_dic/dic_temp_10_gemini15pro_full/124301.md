mothered:/ˈmʌðərd/
verb
母性的，像母亲的；溺爱的；呵护的
treated or protected someone in an excessive or overly careful way, like a mother.
She mothered her youngest son. 她溺爱她的小儿子。
近义词: nurtured, cosseted, pampered
反义词: neglected, abandoned
词性: Verb (past tense of "mother")


mothered:/ˈmʌðərd/
adjective
母性的，像母亲的；溺爱的；呵护的
Having the qualities of a mother; characterized by maternal care or protectiveness.  Can also describe someone treated with excessive care.
He felt rather mothered after his sister fussed over him.  他妹妹对他嘘寒问暖之后，他觉得有点被溺爱了。
近义词: maternal, nurturing, protective
反义词: uncaring, neglectful
词性: Adjective (past participle of "mother" used as an adjective)
