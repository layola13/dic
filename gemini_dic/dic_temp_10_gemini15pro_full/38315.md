concealingly:/kənˈsiːlɪŋli/
adv.
隐蔽地，偷偷摸摸地
in a manner that keeps something hidden or secret.
She smiled concealingly, hiding her true feelings. 她偷偷地笑了笑，掩饰着真实的感受。
Synonyms: secretly, furtively, stealthily, covertly
Antonyms: openly, publicly, visibly, blatantly
词性: 副词
