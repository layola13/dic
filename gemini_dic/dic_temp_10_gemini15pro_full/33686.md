cinnamons:/ˈsɪnəm ənz/| n.|肉桂（复数）|The plural form of "cinnamon," referring to multiple instances of the spice. Cinnamon is a spice obtained from the inner bark of several trees from the genus Cinnamomum. It is used in both sweet and savory foods.|I bought two different types of cinnamons at the market. 我在市场上买了两种不同类型的肉桂。|Synonyms: cinnamon sticks, cassia|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
