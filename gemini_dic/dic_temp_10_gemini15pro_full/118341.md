meridian: /məˈrɪdiən/ /məˈrɪdiən/ | n. |子午线，经线；顶点，最高点|A circle of constant longitude passing through a given place on the earth's surface and the terrestrial poles.|例句：The prime meridian passes through Greenwich. 本初子午线穿过格林威治。

例句：The sun was at its meridian. 太阳正当中天。|近义词：longitude, zenith, apex, peak, culmination |反义词：nadir, bottom |词性: 名词

meridian: /məˈrɪdiən/ /məˈrɪdiən/ | adj. | 子午线的，经线的；顶点的，最高的；中午的|Of or relating to a meridian; Relating to the highest point or culmination.|例句：The meridian sun beat down on us. 正午的阳光直射在我们身上。

例句：He was at the meridian of his career. 他正处于事业的巅峰。|近义词：highest, peak, culminating, midday |反义词：lowest, bottom, midnight |词性: 形容词
