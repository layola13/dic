long-range:/ˌlɔːŋˈreɪndʒ/ | adj. | 长期的，远程的 |  Covering an extended period. |  They made a long-range weather forecast.  他们做了一个长期的天气预报。


近义词:long-term, far-reaching, distant | 反义词:short-range, short-term, immediate | 词性:adjective

词源:  "long" (Old English "lang") + "range" (from Old French "renge," meaning "row, rank"). The combined meaning refers to something extending over a long distance or period.
