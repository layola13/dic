anthropology:/ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒi/ /ˌænθrəˈpɑːlədʒi/| n. |人类学|The study of humankind, in particular.  The comparative study of human societies and cultures and their development.| Anthropology helps us understand the diversity of human cultures. 人类学帮助我们理解人类文化的多样性。|近义词: ethnology, social science |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anthropologie*, from Ancient Greek *ἄνθρωπος* (*ánthrōpos*) meaning "human being" and *-λογία* (*-logía*) meaning "study of".
