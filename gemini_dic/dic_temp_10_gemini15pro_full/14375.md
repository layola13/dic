autopsy: /ˈɔːtɑːpsi/ (US) /ˈɔːtɒpsi/ (UK) | n. | 验尸，尸体解剖 | A postmortem examination to discover the cause of death or the extent of disease. | The autopsy revealed the cause of death to be a heart attack. 验尸结果显示死因是心脏病发作。|  The coroner ordered an autopsy to determine the exact cause of death. 验尸官下令进行尸检以确定确切的死因。 | synonyms: postmortem, necropsy | antonyms: N/A | noun
