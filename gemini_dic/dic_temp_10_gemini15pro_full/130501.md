nonaccessories:/nɒnəkˈsɛsəriz/ | n. | 非配件；非附件 | Items that are not considered accessories, meaning they are essential or integral parts rather than optional additions. | The engine is a nonaccessory; you can't drive the car without it. 发动机不是附件；没有它，你就无法驾驶汽车。| The frame of the bicycle is a nonaccessory, unlike the bell or the basket. 自行车车架不是附件，不像车铃或车筐。 | 近义词: essentials, necessities, integral parts | 反义词: accessories, add-ons, optional extras | 词性: noun

nonaccessory: /nɒnəkˈsɛsəri/ | adj. | 非配件的；非附件的 | Not pertaining to or constituting an accessory; essential or integral. | The nonaccessory parts of the computer include the motherboard and the CPU. 计算机的非附件部分包括主板和CPU。 |  The mechanic pointed out the nonaccessory damage to the car after the accident. 机械师指出了事故发生后汽车非附件部分的损坏。 | 近义词: essential, integral, necessary | 反义词: accessory, optional, supplementary | 词性: adjective
