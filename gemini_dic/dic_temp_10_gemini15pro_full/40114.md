contempt:/kənˈtempt/| n.|轻蔑，蔑视|a feeling that someone or something is not worthy of any respect or approval.|She looked at him with contempt. 她轻蔑地看着他。|近义词：scorn, disdain, disrespect|反义词:respect, admiration|noun


contemn:/kənˈtem/| v.|鄙视，蔑视|to consider someone or something to be worthless or deserving of scorn.|He contemned their hypocrisy. 他鄙视他们的虚伪。|近义词：despise, scorn, disdain|反义词:admire, respect|verb
