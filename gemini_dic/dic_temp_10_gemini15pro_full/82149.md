groomishly:/ˈɡruːmɪʃli/
adjective
精心打扮地，修饰整齐地
carefully or elaborately groomed; in a well-groomed manner.
He was groomishly dressed in a tailored suit. 他穿着一套剪裁得体的西装，打扮得十分整洁。
近义词：smartly, dapperly, nattily
反义词：slovenly, untidily, shabbily
词性: adjective

词源:  From groom (verb) + -ish (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "groom" originally referred to caring for horses, and later extended to mean caring for one's appearance.
