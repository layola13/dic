nuncupation:/nʌŋkjʊˈpeɪʃən/ | n. |口头遗嘱；口头指定；口头叙述|The act of declaring something orally, especially a will or testament.  It refers to the oral declaration of a will, usually made before witnesses, often in anticipation of imminent death.| He made a nuncupation of his wishes on his deathbed. 他在临终前口头交代了他的遗愿。|Synonyms: oral testament, verbal will, spoken declaration |Antonyms: written will, testamentary document| noun
