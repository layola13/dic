historicize:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsaɪz/ |v. | 历史化；将...置于历史背景下 |To place (something) in a historical context, especially by relating it to relevant historical events, social trends, or intellectual currents.  To interpret something with reference to its historical origin and development.| Historians historicize events to help us understand their significance. 历史学家将事件历史化，以帮助我们理解它们的意义。

The novel historicizes the struggles of immigrant families in the early 20th century. 这部小说将20世纪早期移民家庭的斗争历史化。| 近义词: contextualize, interpret | 反义词: decontextualize |词性: 动词
