instaure:/ˈɪnstəˌtʃʊr/ /ˈɪnstəˌtjʊər/ | vt. | 建立，设立，创始 | To establish or introduce something, such as a law, custom, or system. |  The government instaured a new policy on education. 政府制定了一项新的教育政策。

The new manager instaured a more collaborative working environment. 新经理建立了更具协作性的工作环境。


近义词：establish, institute, introduce, inaugurate | 反义词：abolish, terminate, discontinue | 词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *instaurer*, from Latin *instaurare* "to renew, restore, rebuild," from *in- "in, on" + *staurare* "to restore, re-establish."
