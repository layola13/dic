grail:/ɡreɪl/| n.|圣杯；渴望得到但难以得到的东西|Something that is eagerly pursued or sought after, often seen as unattainable or mythical.  The Holy Grail is the cup or platter used by Jesus at the Last Supper, said to possess miraculous powers.|The search for the Holy Grail is a recurring theme in Arthurian legend.|近义词:quest, prize, treasure|反义词:banality, commonplace|noun
