dalmatica:/dælˈmætɪkə/
n.|达尔马提卡长袍；达尔马提亚语|A wide-sleeved tunic worn by deacons in the Roman Catholic and some other churches; the language of Dalmatia.|The priest wore a beautiful dalmatica during the service.  神父在仪式中穿着一件漂亮的达尔马提卡长袍。|Synonyms: tunic, robe, vestment|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps casual wear)|noun

dalmatic:/dælˈmætɪk/
adj.|达尔马提亚的；达尔马提卡长袍的|Relating to Dalmatia or its language; relating to the dalmatica.|The dalmatic coast is known for its beauty. 达尔马提亚海岸以其美丽而闻名。|Synonyms: Dalmatian|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
