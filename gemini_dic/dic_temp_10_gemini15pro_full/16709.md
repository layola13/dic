barrister:/ˈbærɪstər/| n.|大律师，律师|A lawyer in England and Wales who has the right to argue cases in the higher courts of law.|例句：The barrister presented a compelling case to the jury.  大律师向陪审团提出了令人信服的案件。

例句：She hired a top barrister to represent her in court. 她聘请了一位顶级律师在法庭上代表她。|近义词：lawyer, advocate, counsel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps defendant if considering opposing roles in court) |词性:noun
