non-noble:/ˌnɒnˈnəʊbl̩/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈnoʊbl/| adj.|非贵族的，卑贱的，不贵重的|Not belonging to the nobility; not of high social rank; base or ignoble.|The non-noble metals are less resistant to corrosion.|近义词：base, common, ignoble, plebeian|反义词：noble, aristocratic, highborn|词性:形容词
词源:  From non- + noble.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "noble" comes from Old French, ultimately from Latin "nobilis," meaning "well-known, famous, of high birth."
