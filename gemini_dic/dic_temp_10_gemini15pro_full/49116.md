deinternationalized:/diːɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/
|v. |去国际化|To reverse the process of internationalization; to make something less international in character or scope.| Many companies are deinternationalizing their operations due to the current economic climate. 由于目前的经济气候，许多公司正在将其业务去国际化。| The government's new policies seem to be deinternationalizing the education system. 政府的新政策似乎正在使教育体系去国际化。 |localize, nationalize|globalize, internationalize|verb
