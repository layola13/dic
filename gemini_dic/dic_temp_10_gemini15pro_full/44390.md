cross-fertilizable:/ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可异花授粉的，可杂交的 | Able to be fertilized by pollen from a different plant or variety.  This allows for the mixing of genetic material and can lead to hybrid offspring. |  Many varieties of apples are cross-fertilizable. 许多种类的苹果都可以异花授粉。| synonyms: cross-pollinable, hybridizing, interbreeding | antonyms: self-fertilizing, self-pollinating | adjective

cross-fertilize:/ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ | v. | 异花授粉，杂交 | To fertilize a plant with pollen from another plant. In a broader sense, it can also mean to exchange ideas or methods between different groups or disciplines, leading to new developments. | The farmer cross-fertilized the two varieties of corn.  这位农民将两种玉米品种杂交。| synonyms: cross-pollinate, hybridize, interbreed | antonyms: self-fertilize, self-pollinate | verb
