dogmatics: /dɒɡˈmætɪks/ | n. | 教条，教条主义 | A set of beliefs that are accepted by the members of a group without being questioned or doubted.  |  Example 1: The dogmatics of the cult were strict and unyielding.  该邪教的教条是严格且不容改变的。

Example 2: He challenged the dogmatics of the political party. 他挑战了该政党的教条。 | 近义词: doctrines, tenets, dogma | 反义词: skepticism, doubt, heterodoxy | 词性: 名词

dogmatic: /dɒɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 教条的，武断的 | Expressing opinions or beliefs as if they were certain and unquestionable truths. | Example 1:  He has a dogmatic approach to teaching. 他有一种教条式的教学方法。

Example 2:  She was dogmatic in her belief that she was right. 她武断地相信自己是正确的。 | 近义词: assertive, opinionated, peremptory | 反义词: open-minded, flexible, receptive | 词性: 形容词
