nonschool:/ˌnɑːnˈskuːl/ /ˌnɒnˈskuːl/
adjective
非学校的；校外的
Not related to or connected with school.
例句：Nonschool activities are important for a child's development. 校外活动对孩子的发展很重要。

例句：She spent her nonschool hours volunteering at the local library.  她在课余时间在当地图书馆做志愿者。

近义词：extrascholastic, out-of-school
反义词：school, academic
词性：adjective
