constantia:/kɒnˈstænʃɪə/ /kɑːnˈstænʃɪə/
noun
坚定; 坚贞; 恒心
firmness of purpose; resoluteness; steadfastness
例句：Her constantia in the face of adversity was admirable.  她在逆境中的坚定令人钦佩。

例句：The emperor admired her constantia and unwavering loyalty. 皇帝钦佩她的坚定和 unwavering 忠诚。

近义词：perseverance, tenacity, fortitude, steadfastness, resolve
反义词：irresolution, vacillation, fickleness, instability
词性: 名词
