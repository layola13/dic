dodges:/ˈdɒdʒɪz/| n. v. (third-person singular simple present)|躲闪，闪避，伎俩|An act of evading something by moving quickly or suddenly. dodge:/dɒdʒ/| v. |躲闪，闪避，伎俩|To evade something by moving quickly or suddenly. It can also refer to using tricks or schemes.| He dodged the flying ball with ease. 他轻松地躲过了飞来的球。|近义词：evade, avoid, elude, parry, shirk, sidestep|反义词:face, confront, meet|词性:verb

词源: From Old French *doger, from a Germanic source related to Old English *dweorh "dwarf, stunted creature," the notion being of stooping or crouching to avoid something.
