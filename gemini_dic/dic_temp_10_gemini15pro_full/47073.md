datiscin:/ˈdeɪtɪsɪn/| n. |达替辛|A glucoside found in the root bark and stems of species of *Datisca*, hydrolyzing to datiscetin and rhamnose.  It has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.|Datiscin has been isolated from *Datisca cannabina*. 达替辛已经从大麻状达替斯中分离出来。|Datiscin shows promise as an anti-inflammatory agent. 达替辛有望成为一种抗炎药。|近义词:datiscoside|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
