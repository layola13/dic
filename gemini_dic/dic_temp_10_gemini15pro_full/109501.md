leptynite:/ˈlɛptɪˌnaɪt/| n. |细粒麻粒岩|A fine-grained metamorphic rock composed mainly of quartz and feldspar.|The geologist identified the sample as leptynite. 地质学家将该样品鉴定为细粒麻粒岩。|Synonyms: granulite, gneiss|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific rock type)|noun
Etymology: From German *Leptynit*, from Greek *λεπτός* (leptós, “thin, small, fine”) + *-ite* (suffix forming names of rocks).
