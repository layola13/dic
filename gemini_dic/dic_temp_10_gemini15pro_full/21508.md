bludhorn:/ˈblʌdˌhɔːrn/ | n. | 血角, 血角螺 | A large marine gastropod mollusk (Charonia tritonis) of the Indian and Pacific oceans, having a spiral, often brightly colored shell and valued as a trumpet. |  The bludhorn is a prized possession for shell collectors.  血角螺是贝壳收藏家的珍贵藏品。| The diver found a beautiful bludhorn during his exploration.  潜水员在探险过程中发现了一个美丽的血角螺。 | synonyms: triton's trumpet, trumpet shell | antonyms: N/A | noun
