neutrino:/njuːˈtriːnəʊ/ (British), /nuːˈtriːnoʊ/ (American)| n. |中微子|A subatomic particle with no electric charge and very little mass, that travels at almost the speed of light.|Scientists are studying neutrinos to learn more about the universe.|Synonyms: neutral particle (less precise)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific particle)|Noun
