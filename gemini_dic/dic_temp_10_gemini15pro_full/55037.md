disquieting:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪŋ/
adjective
令人不安的；令人忧虑的
Causing anxiety or uneasiness.
The news from the hospital was disquieting.  医院传来的消息令人不安。
There was a disquieting silence after he finished speaking. 他说完话后，一阵令人不安的沉默。
近义词: unsettling, disturbing, alarming, worrying
反义词: calming, reassuring, comforting
词性: adjective

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/
noun
不安；忧虑
A feeling of anxiety or worry.
A sense of disquiet settled over the crowd.  人群中弥漫着一种不安的感觉。
The disquiet about the future of the company is growing. 对公司未来的担忧正在加剧。
近义词: unease, anxiety, worry, apprehension
反义词: calm, peace, tranquility
词性: noun

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/
verb
使不安；使忧虑
To make someone feel anxious or worried.
The sudden change in his behavior disquieted her.  他行为的突然改变让她感到不安。
The economic downturn disquieted investors. 经济衰退令投资者感到不安。
近义词: disturb, worry, upset, bother
反义词: reassure, comfort, soothe
词性: verb
