guay:/ˈɡwaɪ/| adj.|好的，棒的，酷的|Something that is guay is considered cool, excellent, or fashionable, primarily used in informal settings in some Spanish-speaking regions.  It expresses approval or admiration.|That new motorcycle is really guay!  那辆新摩托车真酷！|近义词：cool, awesome, great, excellent, fantastic|反义词：bad, awful, terrible, lousy|词性:形容词
词源: From Caribbean Spanish *guásimo*, from Taíno *waisíma*. Refers to a type of tree with soft, light wood, perhaps implying something easy and pleasant.
