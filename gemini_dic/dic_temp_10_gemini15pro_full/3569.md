agape:/əˈɡɑːpeɪ/
n.
神的爱，无私的爱
A Greek word for love, specifically referring to a selfless, unconditional, and benevolent love.  It is often contrasted with eros (romantic love) or philia (brotherly love).
Agape is considered the highest form of love. 神的爱被认为是最高形式的爱。
近义词：unconditional love, selfless love, divine love
反义词：selfishness, hatred
词性: 名词 (n.)
