necessitude:/nɪˈsesɪtjuːd/
n.
变迁；沧桑；盛衰
the quality or state of being likely to change; mutability
例句：Life is full of vicissitude. 人生充满了变迁。
例句：The vicissitudes of fortune can be unpredictable. 命运的变迁是不可预测的。
近义词：change, mutability, variability, fluctuation, inconstancy
反义词：stability, constancy, permanence
词性：名词
