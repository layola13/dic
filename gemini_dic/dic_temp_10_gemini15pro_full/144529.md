outvanishes:/ˌaʊtvæˈnɪʃɪz/
verb
消失得比...更快；在消失方面胜过
To vanish or disappear more completely or quickly than something else.
The magician outvanished his rival, leaving the audience utterly bewildered.  魔术师比他的对手消失得更快，让观众完全迷惑了。
The morning mist outvanished the last stars. 晨雾比最后一颗星星消失得更快。
近义词:eclipse, surpass, outdo
反义词:appear, materialize
