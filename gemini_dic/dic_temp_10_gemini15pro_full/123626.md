monticle: /ˈmɒntɪk(ə)l/ (US) /ˈmɑːntɪk(ə)l/
n. | 小丘, 土墩 | A small hill or mound of earth. |  A monticle rose from the flat landscape.  一个小丘从平坦的地形中隆起。 | mound, hillock, knoll | mountain, peak | noun

Etymology:
From French *monticule*, from Latin *monticulus*, diminutive of *mons*, *montis* meaning "mountain".
