non-sentient:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnʃənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnʃənt/ | adj. |无知觉的，无感情的|Lacking the capacity for sensation or feeling; inanimate.|例句：Rocks and minerals are non-sentient objects. 岩石和矿物质是无知觉的物体。|近义词：inanimate, insensate, unconscious|反义词：sentient, conscious, aware|词性:形容词

non-sentient:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnʃənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnʃənt/ | n. |无知觉的事物|Something that lacks the capacity for sensation or feeling.|例句：AI, despite its advancements, remains a non-sentient entity.  尽管人工智能有所进步，它仍然是一个无知觉的实体。|近义词：inanimate object|反义词：sentient being|词性:名词
