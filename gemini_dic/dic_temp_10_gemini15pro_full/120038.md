millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ | n. |千足虫，马陆 |A small creature with a long body and many legs, belonging to the class Diplopoda. |例句：I saw a millipede crawling on the forest floor. 我看见一只千足虫在森林地面上爬行。

例句：Millipedes are detritivores, which means they eat decaying plant matter. 千足虫是食腐动物，这意味着它们吃腐烂的植物。|近义词：myriapod |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *millipeda, from Latin mille (“thousand”) + pes (“foot”).
