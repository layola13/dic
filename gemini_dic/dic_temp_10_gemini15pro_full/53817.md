disconnectedly:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪdli/ | adv. | 不连贯地，断断续续地 | In a disconnected or fragmented manner; lacking coherence or continuity. | She spoke disconnectedly, jumping from one topic to another. 她说话断断续续，从一个话题跳到另一个话题。 | He stammered disconnectedly, clearly still in shock. 他结结巴巴，语无伦次，显然还在震惊之中。 | 近义词：incoherently, disjointedly, ramblingly, fitfully | 反义词：coherently, connectedly, fluently, smoothly | 词性：副词
