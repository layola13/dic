climatically:/klaɪˈmætɪkli/
adv.|气候地，就气候而言|
英文解析: In a way that relates to climate.  It describes actions, events, or situations influenced by or related to the climate.
例句：Climatically, the region is prone to droughts.  这个地区气候上容易发生干旱。

例句：The project was climatically challenging due to extreme temperature variations.  由于极端的温度变化，该项目在气候方面具有挑战性。
近义词：environmentally, meteorologically
反义词: N/A
词性: adverb
