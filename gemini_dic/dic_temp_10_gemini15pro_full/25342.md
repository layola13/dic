PHRASE->
build up:  逐渐增强；建立  He's been building up his stamina for the marathon. 他一直在为马拉松比赛增强体力。
build on:  基于…；以…为基础  We need to build on our successes and learn from our mistakes. 我们需要在成功的基础上再接再厉，并从错误中吸取教训。
build in:  内置；包含  The oven has a built-in timer. 烤箱有一个内置计时器。
build out:  扩建；扩展  They are planning to build out their network infrastructure. 他们计划扩展他们的网络基础设施。
build around:  围绕…构建  The company's strategy is built around customer satisfaction. 该公司的战略是围绕客户满意度构建的。
