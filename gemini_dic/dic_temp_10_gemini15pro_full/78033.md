genu:/ˈdʒiːnuː/ /ˈdʒɛnjuː/
n.
knee
The part of the leg between the thigh and the shank.
The child fell and scraped her genu.  孩子摔倒擦伤了膝盖。

genu valgum:/ˈdʒiːnuː ˈvælɡəm/ /ˈdʒɛnjuː ˈvælɡəm/
n.
knock-knee
A condition in which the knees angle in and touch one another when the legs are straightened.
Genu valgum can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics and nutritional deficiencies.  膝内翻可能是由多种因素引起的，包括遗传和营养缺乏。

genu varum:/ˈdʒiːnuː ˈveərəm/ /ˈdʒɛnjuː ˈveərəm/
n.
bowleg
A condition in which the knees stay wide apart when the ankles are together.
Genu varum is common in toddlers but usually resolves itself as the child grows.  弓形腿在幼儿中很常见，但通常随着孩子的成长而自行解决。
