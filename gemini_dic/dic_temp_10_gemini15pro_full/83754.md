hair:/her/ (US); /heə(r)/ (UK)| n.|头发，毛发|A threadlike strand growing from the skin of humans and animals.|She has long, beautiful hair. 她有一头漂亮的长发。

hair:/her/ (US); /heə(r)/ (UK)| n.|毛，茸毛|A growth of such strands.|The cat's hair is very soft. 这只猫的毛很柔软。

hair:/her/ (US); /heə(r)/ (UK)| n.|[比喻] 微小数量，丝毫|A very small amount or degree.|He won the race by a hair. 他以微弱的优势赢得了比赛。

近义词：fur, fuzz, tresses, locks (for human hair)
反义词: baldness (state of having no hair)
词性: noun
