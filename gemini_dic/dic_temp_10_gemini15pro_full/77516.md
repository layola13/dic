gazumped:/ɡəˈzʌmpt/
verb
抬高要价(尤指在谈妥后);被抬价
To raise the price of something (especially a house) after agreeing on a lower price with another buyer, or to have the price of something raised on you in this way.
The seller gazumped us at the last minute by accepting a higher offer from someone else.  卖家在最后一刻抬高了要价，接受了别人的更高报价。
We were gazumped by a rival bidder. 我们被一个竞争的竞标者抬价了。
synonyms: outbid, overbid
antonyms: underbid
