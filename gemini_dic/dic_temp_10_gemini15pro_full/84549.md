hammer-down:/ˈhæmər daʊn/| adj. adv.|最终的，确定的；全力以赴地，坚决地|Refers to a final, firm decision or action, often in a negotiation or competition; also describes acting with great determination and force.|The CEO made a hammer-down decision to close the factory. 首席执行官做出了关闭工厂的最终决定。|近义词：final, conclusive, decisive, resolute, determined|反义词:tentative, hesitant, irresolute|词性:形容词/副词

hammer-down:/ˈhæmər daʊn/| n.|最终决定，最后期限|A final, non-negotiable decision or deadline.|The hammer-down on the project is next Friday.  该项目的最后期限是下周五。|近义词：deadline, ultimatum|反义词:proposal, suggestion|词性:名词
