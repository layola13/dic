dulcite:/ˈdʌlsaɪt/ | n. | 六碳糖醇; 半乳糖醇 | A sweet, crystalline, water-soluble alcohol, C6H14O6, derived from galactose and found in Madagascar manna. | Dulcite is less sweet than mannitol. 半乳糖醇不如甘露糖醇甜。| synonym: galactitol | antonym: None | noun

dulcite:/ˈdʌlsaɪt/ | adj. | 甜的 | Sweet; pleasant. | The dulcite tones of her voice were soothing. 她甜美的声音令人舒缓。| synonym: sweet, mellifluous | antonym: bitter, acrid | adjective
