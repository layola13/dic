idioelectric:/ˌɪdioʊɪˈlɛktrɪk/ | adj. | 特异电性的，自发电性的 |  Producing or generating its own electricity, typically referring to biological processes. | The electric eel possesses idioelectric organs that can generate powerful electric shocks. 电鳗拥有能够产生强大电击的特异电性器官。 | The pacemaker cells in the heart are idioelectric. 心脏中的起搏细胞是自发电性的。 | 近义词: electrogenic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

词源:  "idio-" (Greek) meaning "one's own," "peculiar," + "electric"
