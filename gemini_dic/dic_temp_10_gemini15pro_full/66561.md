exhibitionist:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪst/| n.|暴露狂，爱出风头的人|A person who behaves in a way intended to attract attention to themselves, especially in a flamboyant or dramatic way.  Someone who compels attention to themselves by public exposure of their body parts (usually genitals).|例句：He's a bit of an exhibitionist; he loves being the center of attention. 他有点爱出风头，喜欢成为关注的焦点。

例句：The exhibitionist was arrested for indecent exposure in the park.  那个暴露癖者因为在公园里猥琐暴露被逮捕了。|近义词：show-off, attention-seeker, flasher (specifically for indecent exposure)|反义词：introvert, wallflower|词性:noun
