bulu:/ˈbuːluː/ | n. | 绒毛，软毛，羽绒 |  Fine, soft hair or down, like that on a chick or some fruits. | The chick was covered in soft bulu. 小鸡身上覆盖着柔软的绒毛。| The durian fruit has sharp spikes, but the flesh inside is soft like bulu. 榴莲有尖刺，但里面的果肉像绒毛一样柔软。 | synonyms: down, fuzz, fluff | antonyms:  spikes, bristles | noun

bulu:/ˈbuːluː/ | n. |羽毛 | Feather, especially a large, soft feather. (Primarily used in Southeast Asia, especially Malay/Indonesian) | The bird's bulu were brightly colored. 这只鸟的羽毛颜色鲜艳。 |  The artist used bulu to decorate his masks.  艺术家用羽毛来装饰他的面具。| synonyms: plumage, feather, plume | antonyms: scale, skin | noun
