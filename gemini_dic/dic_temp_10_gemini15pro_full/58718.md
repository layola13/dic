duncehood:/ˈdʌnshʊd/
n.
愚蠢，笨拙；傻瓜的状态
the state of being a dunce; stupidity.
He was condemned to a lifetime of duncehood for his foolish mistakes. 他因愚蠢的错误而被判终身愚蠢。
Near synonyms: stupidity, foolishness, ignorance, dullness
Antonyms: intelligence, brilliance, wisdom, cleverness
noun
