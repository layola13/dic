longingness:/ˈlɒŋɪŋnəs/ /ˈlɔːŋɪŋnɪs/| n. |渴望，憧憬 |A state of intense yearning or desire. | She felt a deep longingness for her homeland. 她对故乡充满了深深的渴望。 | 近义词: yearning, desire, craving, ache, pining | 反义词: apathy, indifference, contentment | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From long + -ing + -ness.  "Longing" as a noun dates back to the late 14th century, meaning "yearning desire."  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "longing" into the abstract noun "longingness."
