indigo:/ˈɪndɪɡoʊ/ (US) /ˈɪndɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|靛蓝，靛青；深蓝色|A dark blue dye obtained from plants or produced synthetically.  A deep purplish-blue color.|例句：Her dress was a vibrant shade of indigo. 她的裙子是鲜艳的靛蓝色。|近义词：blue, sapphire, azure|反义词：yellow, orange|词性:名词, 形容词

indigo:/ˈɪndɪɡoʊ/ (US) /ˈɪndɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|靛蓝色的|Having a deep purplish-blue color.|例句：The indigo sky was dotted with stars. 靛蓝色的天空中点缀着星星。|近义词：blue, sapphire, azure|反义词：yellow, orange|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Late Latin *indicum, from Latin Indicum, meaning "Indian (dye)," from Greek indikon (φάρμακον) "(dye) from India." Ultimately from Sanskrit indī- (इन्दि)  "indigo plant."
