overexpressive:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛsɪv/| adj.|过度表达的，表情过分丰富的|Expressing emotions too openly or strongly.  Showing feelings in an exaggerated way.|Her overexpressive acting style didn't suit the subtle nuances of the play. 她过于夸张的表演风格不适合这部戏的微妙之处。

The child was overexpressive, constantly laughing and crying with great intensity.  这个孩子表情过于丰富，总是大笑或大哭，非常强烈。|近义词：demonstrative, effusive, exuberant, unrestrained|反义词：reserved, restrained, subdued, inhibited|词性:形容词

词源：over- + expressive.  "Over-"  is a prefix meaning "too much" or "excessively." "Expressive" comes from the Latin "expressus," past participle of "exprimere," meaning "to press out" or "represent."  Therefore, "overexpressive" literally means "expressing too much."
