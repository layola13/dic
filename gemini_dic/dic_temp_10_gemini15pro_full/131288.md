noncensorious:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈsɔːriəs/ | adj. | 不加评判的，不吹毛求疵的 | Not critical or judgmental; tolerant and accepting of different behaviors or opinions. |  She took a noncensorious approach to her friend's unconventional lifestyle. 她对朋友不同寻常的生活方式采取了不加评判的态度。

He offered noncensorious advice, focusing on solutions rather than blame. 他提出了不带评判的建议，侧重于解决问题而不是指责。 | 近义词：tolerant, accepting, open-minded, forgiving, understanding | 反义词：critical, judgmental, censorious, disapproving, moralistic | 词性：形容词
