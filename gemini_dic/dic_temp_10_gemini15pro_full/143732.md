outloose: /aʊtˈluːs/
adjective
自由自在的，无拘无束的
free from restraint or control; unrestrained.
The outloose dogs roamed the streets.  自由自在的狗在街上游荡。
The children were allowed to run outloose in the park. 孩子们被允许在公园里自由奔跑。
近义词: free, unrestrained, liberated, unfettered
反义词: restricted, confined, restrained, controlled
词性: adjective

词源:  The word "outloose" likely originates from the combination of "out" and "loose."  "Out" often implies a sense of freedom or release, while "loose" suggests a lack of restraint. Therefore, "outloose" combines these concepts to express unrestrained freedom.
