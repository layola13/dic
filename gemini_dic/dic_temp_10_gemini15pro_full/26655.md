cacophonic:/kəˈkɑːfənɪk/ /kækoʊˈfɑːnɪk/
adjective
刺耳的，不和谐的
Characterized by harsh or jarring sounds; discordant.
The cacophonic sounds of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地的刺耳声音使人难以集中注意力。
The band's music was so cacophonic that it gave me a headache.  乐队的音乐太刺耳了，让我头疼。
近义词:discordant, dissonant, jarring, inharmonious, raucous, strident
反义词:harmonious, melodious, euphonious, pleasant, soothing
词性:adjective

cacophony:/kəˈkɑːfəni/ /kæˈkɑːfəni/
noun
刺耳的声音，不和谐的声音
A harsh, discordant mixture of sounds.
The cacophony of the city streets can be overwhelming. 城市街道的嘈杂声可能令人难以忍受。
The sudden cacophony of car horns startled the pedestrians.  突然的汽车喇叭声吓到了行人。
近义词:din, racket, noise, clamor, hubbub
反义词:harmony, melody, quiet, silence, stillness
词性:noun

词源: From Greek *kakophōnos*  "ill-sounding," from *kakos* "bad" + *phōnē* "sound, voice."
