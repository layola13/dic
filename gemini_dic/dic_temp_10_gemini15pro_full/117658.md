megrim:/ˈmiːɡrɪm/ | n. | 偏头痛；怪念头；奇想 | A particularly severe headache, often accompanied by nausea and sensitivity to light and sound; a whim or fancy. |  She suffered from frequent megrims that left her incapacitated for days. 她经常偏头痛，每次都要好几天才能恢复。

She had a sudden megrim to paint the kitchen bright pink. 她突然心血来潮想把厨房漆成亮粉色。 | 近义词：migraine, headache, whim, caprice | 反义词:  (For headache meaning) well-being | 词性: noun
