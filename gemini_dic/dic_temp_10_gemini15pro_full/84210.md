half-rise:/ˈhɑːf raɪz/
noun
半上升; 半高; 半抬
A position where the dancer stands with knees bent, heels raised halfway.
The dancers performed a series of half-rises, transitioning gracefully between movements. 舞者们表演了一系列半上升动作，在不同动作之间优雅地过渡。
