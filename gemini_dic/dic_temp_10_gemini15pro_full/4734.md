alexia:/əˈleksiə/| n.|失读症|Inability to read, usually caused by brain damage.|Example 1: After the stroke, John developed alexia and could no longer read his favorite books. 中风后，约翰患上了失读症，再也无法阅读他最喜欢的书了。

Example 2:  Alexia can be a devastating condition, impacting a person's ability to work and participate in daily life. 失读症可能是一种毁灭性的疾病，会影响一个人工作和参与日常生活的能力。|近义词：word blindness, visual aphasia|反义词: literacy|词性:noun
