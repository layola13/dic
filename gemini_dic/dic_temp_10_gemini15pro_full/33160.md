chromatology:/ˌkroʊməˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 色谱学，色彩学 | The science that deals with colors, their properties, and their relations. | Chromatography is an important technique in chemical analysis. 色谱法是化学分析中的一种重要技术。|  Chromatography is used to separate and identify the components of a mixture. 色谱法用于分离和鉴定混合物的成分。|近义词: color science, colorimetry | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
