corroborating:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
确证的，证实的
supporting or confirming a statement, theory, or finding.
The witness's statement corroborating the victim's account was crucial to the case.  证人证实受害者描述的陈述对案件至关重要。
Police found corroborating evidence at the scene. 警方在现场发现了确凿的证据。
近义词: confirming, verifying, substantiating, validating
反义词: contradicting, refuting, denying
词性: adjective

corroborate:/kəˈrɒbəreɪt/
verb
证实，确证
confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding).
The evidence corroborates his version of events.  证据证实了他对事件的描述。
New DNA evidence corroborated the confession. 新的DNA证据证实了供词。
近义词: confirm, verify, substantiate, validate
反义词: contradict, refute, deny
词性: verb

词源:
From Late Latin corroboratus, past participle of corroborare "to strengthen," from Latin com- "together" + roborare "to strengthen," from robur "oak, strength."
