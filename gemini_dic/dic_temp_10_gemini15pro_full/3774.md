agitatorial:/ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənl/
adjective
煽动性的；鼓动的
Of, relating to, or characterized by agitation; inflammatory.
The agitatorial speeches roused the crowd to a frenzy.  煽动性的演讲使人群陷入狂热。
近义词:inflammatory, provocative, subversive, incendiary
反义词: calming, soothing, pacifying
词性:adjective

词源:
From agitate + -orial.  来自 agitate（煽动）+ -orial（形容词后缀）。
