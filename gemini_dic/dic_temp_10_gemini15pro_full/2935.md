advocates:/ˈædvəkɪts/| n. |拥护者，提倡者|A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.|She is a strong advocate for animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利倡导者。

The organization advocates for stricter environmental regulations.  该组织倡导更严格的环境法规。|近义词：supporter, proponent, champion|反义词：opponent, adversary|词性:名词

advocates:/ˈædvəkeɪts/| v. |提倡，主张，拥护|Publicly recommend or support.|The report advocates a ban on smoking in public places.  该报告主张禁止在公共场所吸烟。

He advocates for greater investment in education. 他主张加大教育投资。|近义词：support, promote, champion|反义词：oppose, condemn|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English advocat, from Old French avocat, from Latin advocatus (“one called to [one's aid], advocate, pleader”), past participle of advocare (“to summon, call to one's aid”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + vocare (“to call”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advocates for free healthcare. = 他倡导免费医疗。
She advocates a more balanced approach. = 她提倡一种更平衡的方法。

一般过去时:
They advocated for changes to the law. = 他们倡导修改法律。
The group advocated a peaceful solution. = 该团体主张和平解决。

现在完成时:
Scientists have advocated for more research funding. = 科学家们一直倡导增加研究经费。
She has advocated this policy for years. = 多年来她一直倡导这项政策。

过去完成时:
He had advocated for this change before the incident. = 在事件发生前，他就一直倡导这一改变。
They had advocated a different approach before the new management took over. = 在新管理层接手之前，他们一直提倡不同的方法。

将来时:
We will advocate for the rights of the disabled. = 我们将倡导残疾人的权利。
The organization will advocate for stricter regulations. = 该组织将倡导更严格的法规。


虚拟语气:
If I were president, I would advocate for universal healthcare. = 如果我是总统，我会倡导全民医疗保健。
I wish they would advocate more strongly for environmental protection. = 我希望他们能更强烈地倡导环境保护。

被动语态:
Further research is advocated by the scientists. = 科学家们提倡进一步研究。
A change in policy was advocated by the opposition party. = 反对党提倡改变政策。


疑问句:
Do you advocate for stricter gun control? = 你支持更严格的枪支管制吗？
What changes do they advocate? = 他们主张哪些改变？


否定句:
They do not advocate violence. = 他们不提倡暴力。
She doesn't advocate for this policy anymore. = 她不再倡导这项政策了。


PHRASE->
advocate for (something/someone) = 倡导（某事/某人）
advocate a policy = 提倡一项政策
a strong advocate = 坚定的拥护者
an advocate of change = 变革的倡导者
advocate on behalf of (someone) = 代表(某人) 倡导
