everyday:/ˈevriˌdeɪ/ | adj. | 日常的；普通的 | Happening or used each day; ordinary. | I wear my everyday shoes to work. 我穿我的日常鞋去上班。|  She's dressed in her everyday clothes. 她穿着她的日常服装。 | 近义词: daily, routine, ordinary, usual | 反义词: unusual, special, extraordinary, occasional | 词性: 形容词


every day:/ˈevri deɪ/ | adv. | 每天 | Each day; daily. | I go to work every day. 我每天都去上班。 | I exercise every day to stay healthy. 我每天锻炼以保持健康。 | 近义词: daily, each day | 反义词: never, rarely, occasionally | 词性: 副词


词源: From Middle English *everi day*, equivalent to *everi* + *day*.
