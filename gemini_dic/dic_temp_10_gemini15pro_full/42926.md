covelline:/ˈkɒvəlaɪn/ | n. | 铜蓝 |A dark indigo-blue mineral, copper sulfide (CuS), that is an important ore of copper. | Covelline is a valuable ore due to its high copper content. 铜蓝因其高铜含量而是一种有价值的矿石。| Covelline is often found in association with chalcopyrite. 铜蓝经常与黄铜矿伴生。 | Synonyms: indigo copper, copper glance | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
