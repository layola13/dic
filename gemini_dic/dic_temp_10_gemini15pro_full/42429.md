counterculture:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌltʃər/| n. |反文化|A culture, especially of young people, with values or lifestyles in opposition to those of the established culture.|The 1960s saw the rise of a counterculture that challenged traditional social norms. 20世纪60年代见证了挑战传统社会规范的反文化的兴起。|Synonyms: underground, alternative culture|Antonyms: mainstream culture, dominant culture|noun


counterculture:/ˈkaʊntərˌkʌltʃər/| n. |反主流文化|A way of life and set of attitudes opposed to or at variance with the prevailing social norm.|The hippie movement was a prominent example of a counterculture.  嬉皮士运动是反主流文化的一个突出例子。|Synonyms: nonconformism, rebellion|Antonyms: conformity, establishment|noun
