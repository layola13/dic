evangelizing: /ɪˈvændʒəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
传福音; 福音传道; 宣传; 劝服
The act of preaching the gospel or spreading a religious belief.  It also refers to the act of actively promoting something, trying to persuade others of its merits.
They are evangelizing in remote villages. 他们在偏远乡村传福音。
synonyms:  preaching, proselytizing, promoting, advocating
antonyms: suppressing, concealing, discouraging, dissuading
