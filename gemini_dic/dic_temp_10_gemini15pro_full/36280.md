coheaded:/koʊˈhɛdɪd/| adj.|共同领导的，共同负责的|Having two or more heads, or leaders; jointly led or managed.  Used to describe organizations, projects, or teams led by more than one person with equal authority.|The coheaded organization struggled with decision-making due to conflicting priorities. 这个共同领导的组织由于优先事项冲突而在决策方面遇到了困难。

The project was coheaded by two experienced managers. 这个项目由两位经验丰富的经理共同领导。|jointly led, jointly managed, co-directed|solely led, independently managed|adjective
