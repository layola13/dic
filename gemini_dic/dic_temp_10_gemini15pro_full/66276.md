excommune:/ˌɛksˈkɒmjuːn/
n.
前公社; 已脱离公社的人
A community, especially a commune, that formerly existed or from which someone has departed.
The farm was an excommune, established in the 70s, now privately owned.  这个农场以前是一个公社，建于70年代，现在是私有的。
Former commune members struggled to adapt to life outside the excommune.  以前的公社成员努力适应公社外的生活。
近义词: former commune, ex-collective
反义词: commune, collective
词性: 名词
