ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *haelnes*, from Old English *hælnes*, from *hāl* ("whole, sound, healthy") + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns). Cognate with Old Frisian *hēlnisse* (“health, salvation”), Old Saxon *hēli* (“health”), Old High German *heili* (“health, salvation”), Old Norse *heill* (“whole, healthy”).
