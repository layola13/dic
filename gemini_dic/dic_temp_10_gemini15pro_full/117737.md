melanite:/ˈmɛləˌnaɪt/
noun
黑榴石，钛榴石
A black variety of andradite garnet, containing titanium.
Melanite is a black gemstone variety of andradite garnet.  黑榴石是一种黑色的，属于钙铁榴石的宝石。
This necklace is made with beautiful melanite gemstones. 这条项链是用美丽的黑色榴石宝石制成的。
Synonym:andradite, black garnet, titanium garnet
