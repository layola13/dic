bidirectionally:/ˌbaɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəli/
| adv.|双向地，双方向地|Operating or moving in two directions. |Data flows bidirectionally between the two devices. 数据在两个设备之间双向流动。|The street allows traffic to flow bidirectionally. 这条街允许车辆双向通行。|近义词：reciprocally, mutually|反义词：unidirectionally|词性:副词
