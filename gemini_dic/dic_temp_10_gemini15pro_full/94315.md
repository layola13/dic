impetigo:/ɪmˈpɛtɪɡoʊ/ /ɪmˈpɪtaɪɡəʊ/
|n.|脓疱病|A common, contagious skin infection, characterized by sores and blisters that usually occur on the face, neck, hands, and diaper area. It is primarily caused by bacteria such as Staphylococcus aureus and Streptococcus pyogenes.|The doctor diagnosed the child with impetigo.|Synonyms: impetiginous eruption, pyoderma|Antonyms: healthy skin|noun
