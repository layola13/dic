hypersubtlety:/ˌhaɪpərˈsʌtəlti/| n. |极端的精妙; 过于微妙; 极致的细微|Extreme subtlety; excessive refinement; over-refinement.|Her writing is full of hypersubtleties that are lost on most readers. 她的作品充满了大多数读者难以理解的极端精妙之处。|The artist’s hypersubtlety in shading created a breathtaking realism.  这位艺术家在阴影处理上的极致细微之处营造出惊人的真实感。|近义词: nuance, refinement, sophistication|反义词: obviousness, crudeness, simplicity|词性:noun

Etymology->
From hyper- + subtlety.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," from Greek *hyper* "over, above." "Subtlety" comes from Old French *sotilte*, from Latin *subtilitas*, from *subtilis* "fine, delicate."
