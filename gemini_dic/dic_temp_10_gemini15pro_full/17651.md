becked:/bɛkt/
|v.|召唤，招手，点头示意|To signal with a nod or gesture, typically of the head, as a sign of command, greeting, or acknowledgment.|He becked me over with a slight nod of his head. 他微微点头示意我过去。|近义词：summoned, gestured, signaled, nodded|反义词：ignored, dismissed|词性:动词

becked:/bɛkt/
|adj.|被召唤的，被招手的|Summoned or signaled, typically by a nod or gesture.|The becked servant hurried to his master's side. 被召唤的仆人急忙跑到主人身边。|近义词：summoned, called, signaled|反义词：ignored, dismissed|词性:形容词

词源：From Old English *beacnian "to nod, make a sign," from Proto-Germanic *bauknjan "to beckon," related to Old Norse bauka "to beat."  The original sense was probably "to make a signal by nodding or waving."
