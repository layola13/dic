disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/
n.|不安, 焦虑, 担忧|A feeling of worry or unease.|例句：A deep disquiet settled over her. 她心中涌起深深的不安。|近义词：unease, anxiety, worry, apprehension|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/
vt.|使不安, 使担忧|To make someone worried or uneasy.|例句：The news disquieted her. 这消息让她感到不安。|近义词：worry, disturb, upset, bother|反义词：calm, soothe, reassure|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desquier, from des- (expressing negation, reversal) + quier (quiet). Ultimately from Latin quies (rest, quiet).
