basbleu:/ˌbæˈbluː/| n.|女学者，才女|A woman with considerable literary knowledge or intellectual ability.|例句：She was known as a basbleu in her circle, hosting lively literary discussions.  她在她的圈子里被称为才女，主持着生动的文学讨论。

例句：The basbleu impressed everyone with her insightful commentary on the latest novel.  这位才女对最新小说的深刻评论给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：intellectual, bluestocking, highbrow|反义词：philistine, lowbrow|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "bas bleu" (literally "blue stocking"), referring to the informal gatherings of learned women in 18th-century London, some of whom wore blue worsted stockings.
