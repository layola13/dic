cofinancing:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænsɪŋ/
noun
共同融资
The act of financing something together with another or others.
The project was completed thanks to cofinancing from several international organizations.  这个项目由于几个国际组织的共同出资才得以完成。
joint financing, shared financing
sole financing
n.
