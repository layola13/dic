avauntour:/əˈvɔːntʊər/ |n.| 冒险家，骑士 | An adventurer, especially a knight errant. | He was a true avauntour, seeking glory and fortune in distant lands. 他是一位真正的冒险家，在遥远的土地上寻求荣耀和财富。|  Example: The avauntour set off on a perilous journey.  这位冒险家开始了危险的旅程。| Synonyms: adventurer, knight-errant, wanderer, traveler | Antonyms: homebody, stay-at-home | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *avanturier "adventurer," from avant "before, forward" (from Latin abante "from before," from ab "from, away from" + ante "before") + -ier (agent suffix).
