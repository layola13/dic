hurting:/ˈhɜːrtɪŋ/ /ˈhɝːtɪŋ/| adj. v.|受伤的，疼痛的；令人痛苦的|Experiencing physical or emotional pain; causing distress or anguish.|例句：My leg is hurting. 我的腿疼。|近义词：aching, painful, distressing, agonizing|反义词:soothing, healing, comforting|词性:形容词

hurting:/ˈhɜːrtɪŋ/ /ˈhɝːtɪŋ/| v.|伤害，使疼痛；(感情上) 伤害|Causing physical or emotional pain to someone or something.|例句：She's hurting from the breakup. 她正为分手而伤心。|近义词：injuring, harming, wounding, upsetting|反义词:helping, aiding, comforting|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hurten*, from Old Norse *hurta* "to hurt, strike against," from Proto-Germanic *hurtjanan*, from Proto-Indo-European *kerd-* "heart."  The emotional sense developed later.
