euthanatizes:/ˌjuːθəˈnaɪzaɪz/
|vt.|对…实施安乐死|to kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness.|The veterinarian euthanatized the severely injured horse.|近义词: put down, put to sleep|反义词: resuscitate, revive
|verb|
