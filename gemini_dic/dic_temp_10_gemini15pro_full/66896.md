expedite:/ˈekspədaɪt/| v. |加快；促进；迅速完成|To make something happen more quickly.|例句：We need to expedite the shipping process. 我们需要加快运输流程。

例句：The company expedited the delivery of the goods. 公司加快了货物的交付。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, quicken, facilitate|反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expeditare*, from Latin expedītus “freed, unimpeded,” past participle of expedīre “to extricate, set free, disentangle, prepare, make ready.”
