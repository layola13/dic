misreliance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪəns/
noun
过度依赖；错误信赖
An act of depending too much on something or someone, especially when that thing or person is not reliable.
His misreliance on his GPS led him to drive into a lake. 他过度依赖GPS导航，结果开车掉进了湖里。
近义词: overreliance, overdependence
反义词: independence, self-reliance
词性: n.

mis-/mis/
prefix
表示“错误的”、“不正确的”、“坏的”
From Old French mes- (Modern French més-), from Latin minus "less, smaller".
Examples: misspell, misunderstand, misfortune


reliance:/rɪˈlaɪəns/
noun
依赖；依靠；信任
Dependence on or trust in someone or something.
The country's reliance on imported oil is a major concern. 该国对进口石油的依赖是一个主要问题。
