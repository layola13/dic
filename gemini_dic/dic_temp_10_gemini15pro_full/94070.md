impassionedly:/ɪmˈpæʃənɪdli/| adv.|热情地，充满激情地|With great emotion and fervor; ardently.|She impassionedly defended her beliefs in the debate.  她在辩论中热情地捍卫自己的信仰。|例句：He spoke impassionedly about the need for change. 他热情地谈到了变革的必要性。|近义词：fervently, ardently, passionately, zealously|反义词：apathetically, indifferently, coldly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From impassioned + -ly.  Impassioned from im- (variant of in- "into, upon, on, in") + passioned (past participle of passion "to arouse strong feelings in").  Ultimately from Latin passio "suffering, enduring."
