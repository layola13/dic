dialytic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|透析的；渗析的；辩证法的|Relating to dialysis (separation of substances in solution by means of their unequal diffusion through a semipermeable membrane). Also, pertaining to dialectic (a method of philosophical argument that involves some sort of contradictory process between opposing sides).|Dialytic treatment is often used for patients with kidney failure. 透析治疗常用于肾衰竭患者。

The philosopher engaged in a dialytic examination of the concept of truth. 这位哲学家对真理的概念进行了辩证的考察。|近义词：diffusive, osmotic (for the scientific sense); dialectical (for the philosophical sense)|反义词：synthetic (in some contexts)|词性:adjective
