cerebrally:/səˈriːbrəli/ /səˈribərəli/| adv.|理智地，在智力上|In a cerebral manner; intellectually.  Relating to the cerebrum or brain.|He approached the problem cerebrally, analyzing each aspect carefully. 他理智地处理这个问题，仔细分析了每个方面。|近义词：intellectually, mentally, rationally|反义词：emotionally, instinctively, viscerally|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cerebralis*, from Latin cerebrum (“brain”).
