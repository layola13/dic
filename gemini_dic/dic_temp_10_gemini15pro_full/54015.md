disculpate:/dɪˈskʌlpeɪt/ /dɪsˈkʌlpeɪt/
verb
为...开脱；证明...无罪
to clear from blame; prove innocent
The lawyer disculpated his client by providing a solid alibi.  律师通过提供确凿的不在场证明为他的客户开脱了罪责。

The evidence presented in court disculpated the accused of all charges.  法庭上提出的证据证明被告的所有指控均不成立。

近义词:absolve, exonerate, acquit, vindicate
反义词:incriminate, accuse, blame, convict
词性:verb
