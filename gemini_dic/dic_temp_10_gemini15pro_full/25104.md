buccaneered:/ˌbʌkəˈnɪərd/
verb
掠夺；海盗式地抢劫
acted like a buccaneer; robbed someone in the manner of a buccaneer.
The pirates buccaneered the Spanish galleon.  海盗们掠夺了西班牙大帆船。
The software company buccaneered the competitor's code. 这家软件公司盗用了竞争对手的代码。

synonyms: pirated, plundered, pillaged, robbed, marauded
antonyms: donated, gave, protected, guarded
