ouananiche:/ˌwɑːnəˈniːʃ/ | n. | 红大麻哈鱼，陆封型大西洋鲑 | A landlocked form of Atlantic salmon (Salmo salar) found in certain lakes of northeastern North America.  It is smaller than the typical Atlantic salmon. |  The ouananiche leaped from the water as it chased a fly.  红大麻哈鱼追逐一只苍蝇，跃出水面。 | Synonyms: landlocked salmon, sebago salmon | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
