ecorche:/eɪˈkɔːrʃeɪ/ | n. |  剥皮模型，肌肉解剖模型 | A model of a human or animal body with the skin removed, displaying the muscles underneath. It is used for anatomical study, especially by artists. |  The art students spent hours studying the ecorche to understand the human musculature. 艺术学生花了数小时研究剥皮模型来了解人体肌肉组织。| synonym: anatomical model, flayed figure | antonym: N/A | noun

ecorche:/eɪˈkɔːrʃeɪ/ | vt. |  剥皮，解剖 | To remove the skin of a body for anatomical study.  | The professor ecorched the cadaver to demonstrate the layers of muscle. 教授解剖了尸体来演示肌肉层。| synonym: flay, dissect | antonym:  N/A | verb
