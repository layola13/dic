depulp:/diːˈpʌlp/
v.|去浆; 除果肉|To remove the pulp from fruit or other produce. 将果肉从水果或其他农产品中去除。
|The machine depulps the fruit and separates the seeds. 这台机器可以去除果肉并分离种子。|
近义词:pulp, flesh
