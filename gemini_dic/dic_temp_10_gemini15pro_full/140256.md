offsetting:/ˌɒfˈsɛtɪŋ/ /ˌɑːfˈsɛtɪŋ/| adj. n.|抵消的，补偿的; 抵消，补偿|Balancing one influence against another, or compensating for something.|The company's losses in one area were offset by gains in another. 该公司在一个地区的损失被另一个地区的收益所抵消。|Synonyms: compensating, counterbalancing, neutralizing|Antonyms: increasing, adding, exacerbating|word property: adjective, noun

offset:/ˈɒfˌsɛt/ /ˈɑːfˌsɛt/| n. vt.|抵消，补偿; 偏移量; 分支; 弯管; 抵消，补偿; 开端; 出发|Something that counterbalances or compensates for something else.|The high cost of housing in the city was offset by higher salaries. 城市的高房价被高工资所抵消。|Synonyms: compensate, counterbalance, neutralize|Antonyms: increase, add, exacerbate|word property: noun, verb
