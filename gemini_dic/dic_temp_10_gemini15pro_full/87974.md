high-hats:/ˈhaɪ hæts/| n.|镲片（通常指架子鼓上的）|A pair of small cymbals mounted on a stand, played by striking them together with a foot pedal.|The drummer's high-hats were crisp and precise. 鼓手的镲片敲击清脆而精准。|Synonyms: cymbals|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun

high-hat:/ˈhaɪ hæt/| adj.|傲慢的，自大的|Snobbish, condescending.|She gave him a high-hat look. 她傲慢地看了他一眼。|Synonyms: arrogant, haughty, snooty|Antonyms: humble, modest|Adjective
