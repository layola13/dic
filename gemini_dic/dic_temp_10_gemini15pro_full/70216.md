fibs:/fɪbz/
n.
小谎; 无伤大雅的谎言;
A small and unimportant lie.
例句：She told a few fibs to avoid hurting his feelings. 她说了几个小谎，以免伤害他的感情。

例句：He told a fib about his age to get the job.  他为了得到那份工作，谎报了年龄。

近义词：lies, white lies, untruths
反义词：truths

词性: 名词


词源:  Possibly a shortened form of "fables," or from the verb "fib" (to tell a fib).
