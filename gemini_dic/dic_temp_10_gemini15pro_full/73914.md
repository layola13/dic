forswear:/fɔːrˈswer/| vt.|发誓放弃，作伪证|To make a false oath; to commit perjury.|He forswore himself in court. 他在法庭上作了伪证。|Synonyms: perjure, lie under oath|Antonyms: testify truthfully|verb

词源:
From Old English *forswerian (unattested), from for- "away, against" + swerian "to swear."  Cognate with Old High German farswerian, Old Norse forsverja.  The sense of "renounce, abandon" developed from the idea of swearing off something.
