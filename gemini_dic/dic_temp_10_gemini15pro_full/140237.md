offlet:/ˈɒflət/| n.|残次品，次品，瑕疵品|A product, usually of food, that is imperfect or below the usual standard and therefore sold at a reduced price. |The bakery sells its offlets at a discount.  这家面包店以折扣价出售它的残次品。|The supermarket has a special section for offlets. 这家超市有一个专门的残次品区。|seconds, rejects, imperfections|prime, perfect|noun
