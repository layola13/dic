bucket:/ˈbʌkɪt/| n. |桶; 一桶的量; 大量; (篮球运动的)投篮|A container with an open top and a handle, typically made of metal or plastic, used for carrying liquids or other materials.  Also, a large quantity of something.|例句：He filled the bucket with water. 他把桶装满了水。

例句：They scored buckets of points in the final quarter. 他们在最后一节得了很多分。|近义词：pail, container, vessel, a lot, loads|反义词:  (For quantity) little, few
