doughfaceism:/ˈdoʊfeɪsɪzəm/
n.
迎合奴隶主（尤指北方人）的行为；假仁假义
The practice of a northerner who sympathized with the South and its pro-slavery views before and during the Civil War.
Historians often criticize the doughfaceism of certain Northern politicians who were unwilling to challenge the power of the Southern slaveholders.  历史学家经常批评某些北方政客的迎合奴隶主行为，他们不愿挑战南方奴隶主的权力。
Synonym: appeasement, conciliation, truckling
Antonym: resistance, defiance, abolitionism
