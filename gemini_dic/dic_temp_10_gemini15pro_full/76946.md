gappiest:/ˈɡæpiɪst/
adjective
最快乐的
Most lighthearted; most cheerful.
She had the gappiest grin I'd ever seen. 她露出了我见过的最灿烂的笑容。
Synonyms: cheerful, merry, joyful, jolly, lighthearted, carefree, buoyant
Antonyms: saddest, glummest, most miserable
词性: 形容词
