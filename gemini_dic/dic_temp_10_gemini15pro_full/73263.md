fore-understanding:/ˌfɔːrʌndərˈstændɪŋ/ | n. | 预先理解；初步了解 |  An understanding or comprehension of something before it is fully explained or experienced. It implies a basic grasp of the key concepts or principles involved. | Before attending the lecture, it's helpful to have some fore-understanding of the topic.  在参加讲座之前，对主题有一些预先理解是很有帮助的。| prior knowledge, preliminary understanding, pre-conception | misunderstanding, ignorance | noun
