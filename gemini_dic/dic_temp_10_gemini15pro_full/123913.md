morel:/mɔːˈrɛl/| n.|羊肚菌|An edible mushroom (Morchella esculenta or other species of the genus Morchella) having a conical cap with a pitted surface.|I found a morel while foraging in the woods. 我在树林里觅食时发现了一个羊肚菌。|Synonyms: sponge mushroom, honeycombed morel|Antonyms: none|noun

morel:/mɔːˈrɛl/| n.|深褐色|A dark brown color.|Her dress was a beautiful morel color. 她的裙子是美丽的深褐色。|Synonyms: dark brown, sepia|Antonyms: light brown, beige|noun
