ministering:/ˈmɪnɪstərɪŋ/ |v. |服侍；照顾；给予帮助| Attending to the needs of others, especially in a religious or caring context. |例句：The nurses were ministering to the wounded soldiers. 护士们正在照顾受伤的士兵。

例句：The priest was ministering to the sick.  牧师正在照顾病人。

近义词：serving, tending, attending, caring, aiding, assisting |反义词：neglecting, ignoring|词性：动词现在分词

ministering:/ˈmɪnɪstərɪŋ/ |adj. | 服务性的；照顾的| Serving or caring for others. |例句：She had a ministering spirit, always ready to help. 她有一种服务精神，总是乐于助人。

例句：His ministering presence brought comfort to the grieving family. 他关怀备至的出现给悲痛的家庭带来了安慰。


近义词： helpful, supportive, caring, compassionate |反义词： uncaring, neglectful, indifferent|词性：形容词


词源：From Late Latin *ministrare, present active infinitive of *ministrāre, frequentative of Latin ministrāre “to serve, attend,” from minister “servant, attendant.”
