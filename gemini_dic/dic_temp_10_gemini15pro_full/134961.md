nonnationalization:/ˌnɒnˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 非国有化|The process of returning a nationalized industry or asset to private ownership and control.|The government pursued a policy of nonnationalization, preferring to encourage private enterprise.| 近义词: privatization, denationalization | 反义词: nationalization | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "nationalization" (from "nationalize,"  from "national" + "-ize" suffix). Ultimately from Latin "natio" (nation) and Greek "-izein" (to make).
