corundum:/kəˈrʌndəm/
n.
刚玉
A very hard crystalline form of aluminum oxide, Al₂O₃, used as an abrasive and in some gems.
Rubies and sapphires are varieties of corundum. 红宝石和蓝宝石是刚玉的变种。
Synonyms: alumina, emery
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a mineral)
