defaced:/dɪˈfeɪst/| adj.|污损的，毁容的，面目全非的|Damaged, spoiled, or disfigured, especially by removing or altering features.|The statue was defaced by vandals. 这座雕像被 vandals 污损了。|Synonyms: marred, spoiled, disfigured, mutilated|Antonyms: pristine, unblemished, immaculate|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (expressing removal or reversal) + face (meaning the surface or appearance of something).  The original meaning was "to mar the face or surface of something."
