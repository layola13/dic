evidently:/ˈevɪdəntli/| adv.|显然地，明显地|Clearly; obviously; it appears that.|例句：Evidently, she was very upset. 显然，她非常难过。|例句：He was evidently uncomfortable with the topic.  他显然对这个话题感到不舒服。|近义词：apparently, obviously, clearly, seemingly, visibly|反义词：doubtfully, questionably, uncertainly|词性：副词
词源: From Late Latin *ēvidentēr, from Latin ēvidēns (“evident”).
