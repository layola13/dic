defectless:/ˈdiːfektlɪs/| adj.|无瑕疵的，完美的|Free from defects; flawless.|The company prides itself on producing defectless products.  这家公司以生产无瑕疵的产品而自豪。| The surface was smooth and defectless. 表面光滑无瑕。|近义词：flawless, perfect, impeccable, immaculate|反义词：defective, flawed, imperfect, faulty|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "defect" (from Latin *dēfectus*, past participle of *dēficere* "to fail, desert, abandon") + "-less" (Old English *-lēas*).
