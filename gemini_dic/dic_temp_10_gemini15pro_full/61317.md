embarrass:/ɪmˈbærəs/| vt.|使尴尬，使窘迫|To cause someone to feel self-conscious, ashamed, or awkward.|His constant jokes embarrassed her. 他不停地开玩笑让她很尴尬。|近义词：shame, humiliate, mortify|反义词:comfort, reassure|verb
