Neiman:/ˈnaɪmən/| n.|尼曼 (通常指 Neiman Marcus)|Neiman usually refers to Neiman Marcus, a luxury department store chain in the United States.|She bought a new dress at Neiman Marcus. 她从尼曼·马库斯买了一件新裙子。|Synonym: Neiman Marcus|Antonym: N/A|noun

Etymology->
"Neiman" is a surname of German origin.  In the context of "Neiman Marcus," it refers to the founders, Herbert Marcus Sr., his sister Carrie Marcus Neiman, and her husband, A.L. Neiman.  The name combines the surnames of two of the founders.
