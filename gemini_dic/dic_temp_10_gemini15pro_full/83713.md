hagioscope:/ˈhæɡiəˌskoʊp/| n. |圣龛窥孔，小壁龛|A small opening or squint in the wall of a church, usually oblique, enabling a person in a side chapel or aisle to see the altar.|The hagioscope allowed those in the side chapel to view the elevation of the Host. 圣龛窥孔可以让侧殿里的人看到圣体升高的仪式。|近义词：squint, lychnoscope|反义词: N/A|名词
