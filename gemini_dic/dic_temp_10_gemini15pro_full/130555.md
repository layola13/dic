nonacquiescing:/ˌnɑːnˌækwiˈɛsɪŋ/
adjective
不服从的；不默许的；不顺从的
Not agreeing or consenting; refusing to accept or comply with something.
The nonacquiescing taxpayer refused to pay the disputed tax.  不服从的纳税人拒绝支付有争议的税款。
The government faced nonacquiescing citizens protesting the new law. 政府面临着抗议新法律的不顺从的公民。
近义词: dissenting, objecting, resisting, opposing
反义词: acquiescing, complying, agreeing, consenting
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + acquiescing (present participle of acquiesce), from Latin acquiescere (to rest, find repose in), from ad- (to) + quiescere (to be quiet).
