arthrology:/ˌɑːrˈθrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 关节学 | The study of the structure and function of joints. |  Arthrology is a complex subject, requiring detailed knowledge of anatomy and physiology. 关节学是一门复杂的学科，需要详细的解剖学和生理学知识。

Arthrology helps us understand how movement is possible. 关节学帮助我们理解运动是如何实现的。| 近义词: syndesmology | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider areas *not* focused on joints, like myology or osteology) | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Greek ἄρθρον (arthron, “joint”) + -λογία (-logia, “study of”).
