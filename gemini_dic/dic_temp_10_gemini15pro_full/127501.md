neckland: /ˈnɛklənd/
noun
颈部区域；颈饰
The area around the neck, sometimes referring to a necklace or other adornment worn around the neck.
She wore a beautiful neckland of pearls. 她戴着一条美丽的珍珠颈饰。
synonyms: necklace, choker, gorget
antonyms: bare neck, exposed neck
词性: 名词

Etymology:
"neckland" is a relatively rare word formed by combining "neck" (Old English *hēafod-necca, meaning "head-neck") and "land" (Old English land, meaning "territory, area").  It suggests the area of the body belonging to or surrounding the neck.
