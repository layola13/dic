overtone:/ˈoʊvərˌtoʊn/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˌtəʊn/ (UK)| n. | 寓意; 弦外之音; 暗示;泛音 |an additional meaning, implication, or quality that is not directly stated but can be understood from what someone says or does; a musical tone that is a part of the harmonic series above a fundamental note| The painting has political overtones. 这幅画带有政治寓意。|synonyms: implication, nuance, suggestion, undertone, connotation, hint, innuendo| antonyms: explicit statement, direct meaning| noun


overtone:/ˈoʊvərˌtoʊn/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˌtəʊn/ (UK)| n. | 泛音 |a musical tone that is a part of the harmonic series above a fundamental note|  The overtones of the flute created a haunting melody. 长笛的泛音营造出一种萦绕心头的旋律。|synonyms: harmonic, partial| antonyms: fundamental| noun
