haemodromometer:/ˌhiːməʊdrɒˈmɒmɪtər/ | /ˌhimədroˈmɑːmɪtər/ | n. | 血流速度计 | An instrument for measuring the velocity of the blood flow. |  The haemodromometer provided valuable data for the study. 血流速度计为这项研究提供了宝贵的数据。|  The doctor used a haemodromometer to assess the patient's circulation. 医生使用血流速度计来评估病人的血液循环。| synonyms: blood flow meter, rheometer | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:  From Greek *haima* "blood" + *dromos* "running, course" + *-meter* "measurer."
