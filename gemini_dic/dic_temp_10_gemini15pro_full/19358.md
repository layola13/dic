bibliopolar:/ˌbɪbliəˈpoʊlər/ | adj. | 贩书的；爱书的；藏书的 | Relating to booksellers or bookselling; also, of or relating to a love of books. |  He was a bibliopolar individual, with a vast collection of first editions. 他是一个爱书之人，收藏了大量的初版书籍。| Synonym:  bibliophilic, bookish | Antonym:  aphilic (rarely used, meaning dislike of books) | adjective

bibliopolar:/ˌbɪbliəˈpoʊlər/ | n. | 书商；爱书人 | A bookseller or a lover of books. | The bibliopolar frequented auctions and estate sales, always searching for rare volumes.  这位书商经常光顾拍卖会和房地产销售，总是在寻找珍本书。| Synonym:  bibliophile, bookman | Antonym:  aphilic (rarely used, meaning dislike of books) | noun
