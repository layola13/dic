mashlin:/ˈmæʃlɪn/ | n. | 亚麻布，混纺亚麻布 | A strong linen fabric, often mixed with cotton or other fibers. |  The mashlin sails billowed in the wind. 亚麻布帆在风中鼓起。| 近义词: linen, canvas | 反义词: silk, wool | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *maslin, from Old French *masle, from Late Latin *mascla, possibly from Arabic *maṣlaḥ "mixed material."
