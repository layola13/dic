conjunctivitises:/kənˌdʒʌŋktɪˈvaɪtiːsiːz/
n.
结膜炎（复数）
Inflammation of the conjunctiva, the membrane that lines the eyelids and covers the exposed surface of the eyeball.
Pink eye is a common form of conjunctivitis.
红眼病是结膜炎的一种常见形式。

Conjunctivitises can be caused by bacteria, viruses, or allergens.
结膜炎可以由细菌、病毒或过敏原引起。
近义词:pinkeye, red eye
反义词:(None -  a direct antonym for a disease doesn't exist)
词性:noun
