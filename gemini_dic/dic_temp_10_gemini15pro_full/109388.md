lenticular:/lɛnˈtɪkjʊlər/ /lenˈtɪkjəˌlɑr/| adj.|透镜状的；双凸透镜状的；晶状体形的|Resembling a lens in shape; biconvex.  Relating to or shaped like a lens.|The fish has lenticular eyes. 这条鱼有透镜状的眼睛。|Synonyms: lens-shaped, biconvex, lentiform|Antonyms: concave, flat|adjective

lenticular:/lɛnˈtɪkjʊlər/ /lenˈtɪkjəˌlɑr/| n.|透镜云|A lens-shaped cloud.|Lenticular clouds are often mistaken for UFOs. 透镜云经常被误认为是不明飞行物。|Synonyms: lens cloud|Antonyms: N/A|noun
