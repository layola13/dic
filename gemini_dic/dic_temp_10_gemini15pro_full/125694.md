mundivagant:/ˌmʌndiˈveɪɡənt/
adjective
漫游世界的；周游世界的
Wandering throughout the world; traveling over the whole world.
He led a mundivagant life, exploring different cultures and countries. 他过着周游世界的生活，探索不同的文化和国家。
The mundivagant scholar shared stories of his travels.  这位周游世界的学者分享了他的旅行故事。

Synonym:
peripatetic, nomadic, itinerant, wandering, roving

Antonym:
settled, stationary, static, resident

Etymology:
From Latin *mundivagus*, from *mundus* ("world") + *vagus* ("wandering").
