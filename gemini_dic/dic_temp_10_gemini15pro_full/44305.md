crookneck: /ˈkrʊkˌnek/ | n. |  歪脖南瓜，嫩葫芦 | A type of summer squash with a long, curved neck.  It is harvested when young and tender and has a mild, sweet flavor. |  Example: I added diced crookneck squash to the ratatouille.  我把切碎的歪脖南瓜加到蔬菜杂烩里。

Example: Crookneck squash is a versatile vegetable that can be grilled, roasted, or sauteed. 歪脖南瓜是一种用途广泛的蔬菜，可以烤、烧或炒。 | 近义词: yellow squash, summer squash | 反义词: winter squash | 词性: noun
