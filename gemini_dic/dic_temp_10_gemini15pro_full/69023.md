fattiness:/ˈfætɪnɪs/
noun
肥胖; 油腻
The quality of being fat; the proportion of fat in something.
The fattiness of the meat made it difficult to digest.  这块肉太肥了，很难消化。
近义词：obesity, plumpness, oiliness, greasiness
反义词：leanness, thinness
词性:n.


Etymology:
From *fat* (adjective) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns denoting a state, condition, or quality).  The word *fat* has Proto-Germanic origins, ultimately related to words meaning "fed" and "nourished."
