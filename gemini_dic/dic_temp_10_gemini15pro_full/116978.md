Macbeth: /məkˈbeθ/ | n. | 麦克白 |  The main character in Shakespeare's tragedy of the same name, a Scottish general who becomes king through murder. | Example: Macbeth is a complex character, driven by ambition and guilt. 麦克白是一个复杂的人物，受野心和罪恶感的驱使。 |  Example: The play Macbeth explores themes of ambition and the corrupting influence of power.  戏剧《麦克白》探讨了野心和权力腐蚀性影响的主题。 | Synonym:  tyrant, usurper | Antonym: hero, king (rightful) | noun
