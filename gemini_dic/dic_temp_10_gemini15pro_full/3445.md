afrikanerize:/æfˌrɪkɑːnəraɪz/
verb
使非洲化；赋予非洲特色
To make or become Afrikaner in character or culture; to adapt to Afrikaner culture or customs.
The government's policies sought to Afrikanerize the education system. 政府的政策试图使教育系统非洲化。
The family Afrikanerized their name after moving to South Africa.  这家人移居南非后，将他们的名字非洲化了。
Synonyms: Africanize
Antonyms: Internationalize
词性: 动词
