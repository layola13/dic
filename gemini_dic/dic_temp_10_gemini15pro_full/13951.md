aura:/ˈɔːrə/| n. |气氛，气场，光环|A distinctive atmosphere or quality that seems to surround and be generated by a person, thing, or place. |She had an aura of confidence about her. 她身上散发着自信的光环。|Synonyms: atmosphere, ambience, air, mood, feeling, vibe, emanation, quality, character, tone, spirit |Antonyms:  (Aura doesn't have a direct opposite, but one might consider words like "dullness," "blandness," or "neutrality" as contrasting concepts.)|noun

aura:/ˈɔːrə/| n. |预感，兆头|A supposed emanation surrounding the body of a living creature and regarded as an energy field.|He sensed an aura of danger surrounding the abandoned house. 他感觉到那栋废弃的房子周围有一种危险的气息。|Synonyms: premonition, foreboding, presentiment |Antonyms:  (Aura in this sense also lacks a direct antonym.)|noun
