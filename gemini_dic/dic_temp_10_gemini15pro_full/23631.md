branchiostege:/ˌbræŋkiˈɒstiːdʒ/| n. |鳃盖|The bony flap that covers and protects the gills of fishes.|The branchiostege of the fish was damaged, affecting its ability to breathe. 鱼的鳃盖受损，影响了它的呼吸能力。|Synonyms: operculum, gill cover|Antonyms: None|noun


Etymology:  From Greek *bránkhia* (gills) + *stégē* (roof, covering).
