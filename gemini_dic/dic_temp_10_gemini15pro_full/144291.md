outspending:/ˌaʊtˈspɛndɪŋ/
verb
花费超过（某人）
spending more money than (someone else)
The Democratic candidate is outspending his Republican rival by a wide margin. 民主党候选人在支出方面远远超过了他的共和党竞争对手。
近义词: outlaying, exceeding in expenditure
反义词: underspending
词性: verb


outspending:/ˌaʊtˈspɛndɪŋ/
noun
超支
the act of spending more than someone else
The campaign's outspending has become a major issue in the election. 该活动的超支已成为选举中的一个主要问题。
近义词: overspending, excessive expenditure
反义词: underspending, saving
词性: noun
