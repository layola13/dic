canthoplasty:/ˈkænθəˌplæsti/ | n. | 眼眦成形术 | Surgical repair of the canthus (corner of the eye).  This procedure can be used to correct drooping eyelids, widen the eye opening, or repair damage from trauma. |  The patient underwent canthoplasty to correct her drooping lower eyelids.  这位病人接受了眼眦成形术来矫正她下垂的下眼睑。|  Canthopexy, blepharoplasty |  N/A | noun
