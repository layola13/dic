obsignation:/ˌɒbsɪɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 签字；盖章；批准；确认 | The obsignation of the treaty marked the end of the war.  条约的签署标志着战争的结束。 |  The lawyer witnessed the obsignation of the document. 律师见证了文件的签署。 | 近义词：signature, seal, ratification, confirmation | 反义词：rejection, refusal | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Latin *obsignatio*, from obsignare (“to seal, sign”), from ob- (“towards, against, in the way, down”) + signare (“to mark, sign”).
