insentient:/ɪnˈsɛnʃənt/ | adj. | 无感觉的；无知觉的；没有生命的 | Lacking the power of sensation; inanimate. |  The artist carved an insentient piece of wood into a beautiful sculpture. 艺术家把一块没有生命的木头雕刻成了一座美丽的雕塑。

The robot, though highly advanced, remained insentient.  这个机器人虽然高度先进，但仍然没有知觉。 | 近义词：inanimate, lifeless, unconscious | 反义词：sentient, conscious, living | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *insentient-, from in- "not" + sentient- "feeling," present participle stem of sentire "to feel."
