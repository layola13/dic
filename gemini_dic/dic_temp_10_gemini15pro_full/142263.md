The system of caring for orphans in orphanages. |  The report criticized the widespread orphanotrophism in the country, arguing for more family-based care.  这份报告批评了该国普遍存在的孤儿院主义，主张更多以家庭为基础的照顾。| 近义词：orphan care, orphanage management | 反义词: family care, adoption | noun


Etymology:
From Greek *orphanotrophos* "one who brings up orphans," from *orphanos* "orphan" + *trophe* "nourishment, upbringing."
