droyle:/drɔɪl/| n.|权利，特权；（封建时代国王授予的）领地；附属国|A hereditary right or privilege, especially one attached to a feudal estate or lordship; a dependent territory or kingdom. |The duke enjoyed the droyle of hunting on the royal lands. 公爵享有在皇家领地上狩猎的权利。|近义词：right, privilege, prerogative, appanage, fief|反义词：duty, obligation|词性:noun

词源:
From Old French *droil, *dreuil (“right, law”), from Late Latin *drectum, from Latin directum (“straight, right”), neuter of directus, past participle of dirigō (“to direct, guide, arrange, govern, steer, aim”). Doublet of direct.
