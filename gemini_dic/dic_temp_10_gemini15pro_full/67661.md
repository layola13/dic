extrusive:/ɪkˈstruːsɪv/
adjective
喷出的；挤出的；火山喷出的
Relating to or denoting rock that has been extruded at the earth's surface as lava or other volcanic deposits.
The extrusive rocks were formed by the rapid cooling of lava. 这些喷出岩是由熔岩快速冷却形成的。
Volcanic activity resulted in the formation of extrusive igneous rocks. 火山活动导致了喷出火成岩的形成。

近义词:volcanic, eruptive
反义词:intrusive
词性:adjective
