orillon:/ɔːˈrɪljən/ | n. |耳墙，侧墙，包厢|A small projecting tower at the angle of a building, often flanking a curtain wall or located at the shoulder of a bastion. In theatres, a box or part of a gallery near the stage.|The orillons of the fortress provided excellent vantage points for archers.  堡垒的耳墙为弓箭手提供了绝佳的有利位置。|Synonyms:  flanking tower, wing wall, side tower|Antonyms:  central tower, main wall|noun


orillon:/ɔːˈrɪljən/ | n. |包厢|A box or part of a gallery near the stage, especially one that is partially enclosed.|We had excellent seats in the orillon, with a perfect view of the stage. 我们在包厢里有很好的座位，可以完美地看到舞台。|Synonyms:  box, loge|Antonyms:  backstage, wings|noun
