noneloquent:/ˌnɒnˈɛləkwənt/
adjective
不善言辞的，表达能力差的
lacking eloquence; not fluent or persuasive in speech or writing
His noneloquent speech failed to move the audience. 他不善言辞的演讲未能打动观众。
Synonyms: inarticulate, tongue-tied, uncommunicative
Antonyms: eloquent, articulate, fluent
词性: 形容词
