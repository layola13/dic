oversecurity:/ˌoʊvər sɪˈkjʊrɪti/ | n. | 过度安全；过分担心安全 | An excessive concern for safety and security, often leading to unnecessary precautions or restrictions. |  He installed three locks on his door out of oversecurity.  他因为过分担心安全，在门上装了三个锁。 | 近义词: overcautiousness, paranoia | 反义词: recklessness, carelessness | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "security" (noun meaning "the state of being free from danger or threat")
