nonsibilance: /nɒnˈsɪbɪləns/ | n. | 非咝音 | The absence of a hissing sound; a speech sound that is not a sibilant.  A non-sibilant is a speech sound which is not produced by forcing air through a narrow channel in the vocal tract. | The word "flow" contains a nonsibilance.  "流动"这个词包含一个非咝音。 |  近义词: non-fricative, non-sibilant | 反义词: sibilance, fricative | 词性: noun

nonsibilance: /nɒnˈsɪbɪlənt/ | adj. | 非咝音的 |  Not characterized by a hissing sound; not a sibilant. | The letter "f" represents a nonsibilant sound. 字母"f"代表一个非咝音。 | 近义词: non-fricative, non-sibilant | 反义词: sibilant, fricative | 词性: adjective
