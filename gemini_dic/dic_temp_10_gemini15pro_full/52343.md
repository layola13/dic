dictate:/ˈdɪkteɪt/ | v. |口述；命令；规定|to say words aloud for someone to write down or to type into a machine; to tell someone exactly what they must do or how they must behave |The CEO dictated a memo to his secretary. 首席执行官向他的秘书口述了一份备忘录。|synonyms: command, order, prescribe, impose|antonyms: suggest, request, comply, obey|verb

dictate:/ˈdɪkteɪt/ | n. |命令；指示|an order or principle that must be obeyed |The dictates of fashion. 时尚的规则。|synonyms: command, order, decree, mandate|antonyms: suggestion, request, plea|noun
