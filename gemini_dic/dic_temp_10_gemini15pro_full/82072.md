gritty:/ˈɡrɪti/| adj.|坚韧不拔的；有砂砾的；粗糙的|Showing courage and resolve in a difficult situation; containing or resembling grit; rough in texture.|The photographer captured the gritty reality of life in the slums.  这位摄影师捕捉到了贫民窟生活的残酷现实。|近义词：courageous, resolute, determined, sandy, grainy, coarse|反义词:smooth, refined, delicate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From grit (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  The noun "grit" comes from Old English "grytt," meaning "sand, gravel."
