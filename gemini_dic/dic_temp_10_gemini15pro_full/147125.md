overloath:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊθ/ /ˌəʊvəˈləʊθ/| adj.|非常不情愿的，极不愿意的|Extremely unwilling; reluctant.|例句：He was overloath to admit his mistake. 他极不愿意承认自己的错误。

例句：She was overloath to leave her family and move abroad. 她非常不情愿离开家人移居国外。

近义词：reluctant, unwilling, disinclined, averse|反义词：willing, eager, keen|词性：形容词

词源：From Middle English *overloth, from Old English *oferlāþ (“hateful, loathsome”), equivalent to over- + loath. Cognate with Dutch overleed (“deceased”). More at over-, loath.
