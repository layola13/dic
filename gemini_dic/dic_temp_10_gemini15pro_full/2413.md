adjoin:/əˈdʒɔɪn/| v.|毗邻；邻接；贴近；|To be next to or joined with something else.|Our house adjoins the supermarket. 我们的房子挨着超市。|近义词：border, abut, neighbor, connect, join|反义词:separate, divide|动词

adjoin:/əˈdʒɔɪn/| vt.|贴近；连接；毗连；|To add or attach something.|The dining room adjoins the kitchen. 餐厅与厨房相连。|近义词：attach, annex, append|反义词:detach, separate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Their garden adjoins ours. = 他们的花园与我们的花园相邻。
The two buildings adjoin each other. = 这两栋楼彼此相邻。

一般过去时:
His land adjoined the royal estate. = 他的土地与皇家庄园相邻。
The hotel room adjoined a small balcony. = 酒店房间连接着一个小阳台。

现在完成时:
The two countries have adjoined for centuries. = 这两个国家已经相邻几个世纪了。
Several new offices have been adjoined to the existing building. = 几间新的办公室已经连接到现有的建筑物上。

过去完成时:
The two properties had adjoined before the new fence was built. = 在新栅栏建成之前，这两处房产是相邻的。
The kingdoms had adjoined peacefully for many years before the war. = 在战争爆发之前，这两个王国已经和平共处多年。

将来时:
The new extension will adjoin the main building. = 新的扩建部分将与主楼相连。
Our new property will adjoin a nature reserve. = 我们的新房产将毗邻一个自然保护区。

被动语态:
The conference room is adjoined by a small kitchen. = 会议室旁边有一个小厨房。
The two rooms were adjoined by a secret passage. = 这两个房间由一条秘密通道相连。
