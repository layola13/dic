cracklings:/ˈkræklɪŋz/
noun
油炸猪皮；爆出的油渣；噼啪声
the crisp residue left after the rendering of lard; small pieces of crisp fried or roasted skin of a pig or other animal; a series of slight cracking sounds.
The crackling was crispy and salty. 油炸猪皮酥脆咸香。
The fire settled down to a soft crackling. 火势减弱，发出轻微的噼啪声。
synonyms: scraps, bits, fragments, chips, crisps, rinds
antonyms: smoothness, silence
词性:n.

crackle:/ˈkræk(ə)l/
verb
发出噼啪声
to make a series of slight cracking sounds.
The fire crackled merrily in the fireplace. 火在壁炉里欢快地噼啪作响。
The radio crackled with interference. 收音机受到干扰，发出噼啪声。
synonyms: sputter, snap, pop, crack
antonyms: hush, silence
词性:v.

crackle:/ˈkræk(ə)l/
noun
噼啪声
a series of slight cracking sounds.
The crackle of the fire was soothing. 火焰的噼啪声令人舒缓。
There was a crackle of static on the line. 电话线上有静电的噼啪声。
synonyms: sputtering, snapping, popping, cracking
antonyms: silence, hush
词性:n.
