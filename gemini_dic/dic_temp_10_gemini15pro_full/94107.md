impeaching:/ɪmˈpiːtʃɪŋ/
verb
弹劾；控告；质疑
charging someone with a serious crime or misdemeanor, especially a public official; calling into question the integrity or validity of something.
The House of Representatives is impeaching the president for abuse of power. 众议院正在弹劾总统滥用权力。
The lawyer is impeaching the witness's testimony. 律师正在质疑证人的证词。
Synonyms: accusing, indicting, challenging, questioning
Antonyms: exonerating, acquitting, defending, supporting
词性: 动词
