nariest:/ˈnɛəriɪst/ | adj. | 最可怕的，最恐怖的 |  Superlative form of "nary," meaning "not any" or "no," but used informally to mean "very" or "extremely" + scary.  Essentially, it means "the most scary." | That was the nariest haunted house I've ever been in. 那是我去过的最恐怖的鬼屋。| Synonyms: scariest, most frightening, creepiest | Antonyms: least scary, calming, comforting | adjective

nary:/ˈnɛəri/ | adj. | 没有，无 | Not any; no; not a; not one; (used with a singular noun).  While traditionally meaning "not any," it's often used informally as an intensifier. |  Nary a soul was in sight. 一个人影也没看见。(传统用法)  He showed nary a sign of fear.  他一点也不害怕。(非正式用法，强调“毫不”) | Synonyms: no, not any | Antonyms: some, any, a | adjective
