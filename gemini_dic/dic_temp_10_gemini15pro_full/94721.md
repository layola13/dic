impropriation:/ɪmˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/
|n.|拨用，侵占，私吞（尤指教会财产）|The act of taking possession of something, especially church property, for one's own use, typically without permission or proper authority.|The impropriation of church lands by the crown.|近义词：misappropriation, seizure, confiscation, appropriation|反义词:restitution, restoration|词性:名词
