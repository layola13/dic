ooze:/uːz/ | v. n. | 渗出，泄露；（慢慢地）流出，分泌；表现，流露 | to flow or leak out slowly, or to show a particular quality or feeling |  The wound oozed blood. 伤口渗出了血。| synonyms: seep, leak, exude, emanate | antonyms: gush, pour, absorb | verb

ooze:/uːz/ | n. |  软泥，淤泥 | soft, wet mud or slime, especially at the bottom of a river or lake | The riverbed was covered in thick ooze. 河床覆盖着厚厚的淤泥。| synonyms: mud, slime, sludge | antonyms: rock, solid | noun
