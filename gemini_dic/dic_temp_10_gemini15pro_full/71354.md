flacker:/ˈflækər/ | v. | 闪烁，摇曳，忽明忽暗 | To burn or shine with an unsteady light; to flicker. | The candle flackered in the wind.  蜡烛在风中摇曳。| synonyms: flicker, waver, flutter | antonyms: blaze, shine steadily | verb

flacker:/ˈflækər/ | n. | 闪烁，摇曳 | A flickering light or flame. | The flacker of the firelight created dancing shadows on the walls. 炉火的闪烁在墙上投下舞动的影子。 | synonyms: flicker, glimmer | antonyms: steady light, constant glow | noun
