cascarilla:/ˌkæskəˈrɪlə/ | n. |卡斯卡里拉树皮；卡斯卡里拉酒；一种雪茄|The aromatic bark of the cascarilla tree, used as a tonic and in flavoring. Also, a liqueur flavored with this bark, and a type of cigar.|例句：

He enjoyed a cascarilla after dinner.  他晚饭后享用了一杯卡斯卡里拉酒。


The cascarilla cigar had a unique, spicy flavor. 这支卡斯卡里拉雪茄有一种独特的辛辣味。

|近义词：cinchona (for the bark's medicinal use), aromatic, liqueur, cigar |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


cascarilla:/ˌkæskəˈrɪlə/ | adj. |卡斯卡里拉树皮的|Made from or relating to the bark of the cascarilla tree.|例句：

The cascarilla flavoring added a subtle complexity to the drink. 卡斯卡里拉香料为饮料增添了微妙的复杂性。


He preferred cascarilla cigars to Cuban ones.  比起古巴雪茄，他更喜欢卡斯卡里拉雪茄。

|近义词：aromatic, spicy|反义词: N/A |词性:adjective
