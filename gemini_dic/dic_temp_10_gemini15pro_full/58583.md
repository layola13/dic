dulcimores:/ˈdʌlsɪmɔːrz/ | n. | 洋琴，齐特琴 | A trapezoidal zither with metal strings which are struck with hammers held in the hands.  A dulcimer is a stringed instrument played by striking the strings with small hammers. |  例句：He played a haunting melody on the dulcimores. 他用洋琴演奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。

例句：The dulcimores added a unique sound to the folk ensemble. 洋琴为民间乐团增添了独特的音色。 | 近义词：zither, hammered dulcimer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | 词性: noun
