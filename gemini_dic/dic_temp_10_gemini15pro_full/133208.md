nonexaggerated:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪd/
adjective
未夸大的，真实的
Not exaggerated; accurate and true to life.
Her account of the incident was nonexaggerated and factual. 她对事件的描述未经夸大，而且是真实的。

Her response was a nonexaggerated account of what happened. 她的回应是对发生的事情的真实描述。
The witness gave a nonexaggerated account of the accident. 目击者对事故进行了未加夸大的描述。


近义词:unembellished, factual, realistic, truthful, accurate, veritable, unvarnished
反义词:exaggerated, overstated, embellished, inflated, amplified, hyped-up
词性:adjective
