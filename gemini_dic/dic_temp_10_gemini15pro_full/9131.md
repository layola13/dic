antimysticalness:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪkəlnəs/

mystical:/ˈmɪstɪkəl/
|adj.|神秘的，神秘主义的|The mystical experience left her feeling transformed.  这次神秘的经历让她感觉焕然一新。|He was drawn to the mystical teachings of the ancient texts. 他被古代文献中的神秘教义所吸引。|近义词: spiritual, esoteric, occult|反义词: rational, empirical, pragmatic|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

anti-:  a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against," "opposite of," "opposed to."

mystical: from Late Latin *mysticalis, from Latin mysticus “of secret rites,” from Greek mystikos “of mysteries, mystic,” from mystēs “one initiated into mysteries.”

-ness: a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
