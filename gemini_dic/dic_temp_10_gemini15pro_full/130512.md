nonaccommodatingness:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪtɪŋnəs/
noun
不妥协；不迁就；不通融
The state or quality of being unwilling to compromise or make concessions.
Her nonaccommodatingness made negotiations difficult. 她的不妥协使得谈判变得困难。
inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness
accommodatingness, flexibility, willingness to compromise
