matfelon: /ˈmætˌfɛlən/ | n. |  毒蝇伞 | A poisonous mushroom (Amanita muscaria) with a bright red cap with white warts. |  The matfelon, with its distinctive red cap, is easily recognizable but should never be eaten.  毒蝇伞有着独特的红色菌盖，很容易辨认，但绝对不能食用。| synonyms: fly agaric, fly amanita | antonyms: edible mushroom | noun
