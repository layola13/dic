bug:/bʌɡ/| n. v.|虫子；故障；窃听器；烦扰|A small insect. A fault or defect in a computer program, system, or machine. A concealed microphone or other listening device placed to eavesdrop on conversations.|例句：There's a bug in my soup! 我的汤里有只虫子！

|近义词：insect, glitch, error, defect, annoy, bother|反义词:feature (as in a software feature)|词性:名词,动词

bug:/bʌɡ/| v.|装窃听器；烦扰|To install a concealed microphone or other listening device. To annoy or bother.|例句：The FBI bugged the suspect's phone.  FBI窃听了嫌疑人的电话。

|近义词：annoy, pester, irritate|反义词:soothe, please|词性:动词
