heteromera:/ˌhɛtərˈɒmərə/
noun
异跗节类
A suborder of beetles characterized by having five tarsal segments on the front and middle legs, and four on the hind legs.
The heteromera include many familiar beetles, such as blister beetles and darkling beetles.
近义词: none (highly specific scientific term)
反义词: none
词性: n.


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + μέρος (méros, “part”).  Refers to the different number of tarsal segments.
