mochila: /moʊˈtʃiːlə/ (US), /məʊˈtʃiːlə/ (UK)| n. |背包|A bag, typically made of canvas or other sturdy material, carried on the back by straps over the shoulders, and used for carrying supplies or personal belongings, especially by hikers or campers.  It's derived from the Spanish word "mochila."|Example: I packed my mochila with everything I needed for the weekend hike. 我把周末徒步旅行所需的一切都装进了背包里。|Example:  She grabbed her mochila and headed out the door for school. 她抓起背包，出门上学去了。|Synonym: backpack, rucksack, knapsack|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "suitcase" or "briefcase" for different carrying purposes) |Noun
