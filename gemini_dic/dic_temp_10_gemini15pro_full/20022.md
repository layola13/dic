biomass:/ˈbaɪoʊmæs/| n. |生物量|The total mass of organisms in a given area or volume.|The biomass of the rainforest is incredibly diverse.  雨林的生物量极其多样化。|Synonyms: organic matter, living matter|Antonyms: inorganic matter|noun


biomasses:/ˈbaɪoʊˌmæsɪz/| n. |生物量（复数）|The total masses of organisms in given areas or volumes.|Different ecosystems have varying biomasses. 不同生态系统的生物量各不相同。|Synonyms: organic matters, living matters|Antonyms: inorganic matters|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + mass.  Bio- comes from Greek bios "life". Mass comes from Latin massa "lump, kneaded dough, barley cake".
