ooecium:/oʊˈiːsiəm/
n.
卵巢腔，卵室，育卵囊
A brood chamber or protective capsule enclosing the eggs of some bryozoans.
The ooecium protects the developing embryos. 卵巢腔保护正在发育的胚胎。
The structure of the ooecium varies among different bryozoan species. 不同苔藓虫物种的卵巢腔结构各不相同。
Synonym: brood chamber, gonocyst
Antonym: N/A
