bleary-eyed:/ˈblɪəri aɪd/
adjective
朦胧的，睡眼惺忪的
(of someone's eyes) unfocused or watery, especially from tiredness or sleep.
例句：He stumbled into the kitchen, bleary-eyed and disoriented. 他跌跌撞撞地走进厨房，睡眼惺忪，迷迷糊糊。

例句：She was bleary-eyed after studying all night.  她学习了一整夜，眼睛都模糊了。

近义词：sleepy, tired, exhausted, bloodshot
反义词：alert, awake, bright-eyed, refreshed
词性: adjective
