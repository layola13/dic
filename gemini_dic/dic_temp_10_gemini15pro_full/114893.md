man-boy:/ˈmænˌbɔɪ/| n.|男人孩子，介于男人和男孩之间的男性|A male who is physically mature, but retains some boyish qualities or behaviors.  He's a real man-boy, loves video games and still lives with his parents.|例句：He's a bit of a man-boy, still obsessed with superheroes. 他有点像个大男孩，仍然痴迷于超级英雄。

例句：Despite his age, he's still a man-boy at heart. 尽管他的年龄，他内心仍然是个大男孩。|近义词：adolescent, youth, young man, kidult |反义词: adult, grown-up|词性:名词
