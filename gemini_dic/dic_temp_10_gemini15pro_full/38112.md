compradors:/kəmˈprɑːdɔːrz/ | n. | 买办 | A person who acts as an agent for foreign organizations engaged in investment, trade, or economic or political exploitation.  A native manager of a foreign business in China or other Asian countries. | The compradors grew wealthy facilitating trade between foreign merchants and the local population. 买办通过为外国商人和当地居民之间的贸易提供便利而变得富有。 |  The British trading companies relied heavily on their compradors to navigate the complexities of the Chinese market.  英国贸易公司非常依赖他们的买办来应对中国市场的复杂性。 | synonyms: agents, intermediaries, middlemen, facilitators | antonyms: principals, owners, operators | noun
