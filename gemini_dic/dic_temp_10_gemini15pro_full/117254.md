mechanicalization:/ˌmɛkənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 机械化 | The process of introducing machines to do work that was previously done by hand or with animal power. | The mechanicalization of agriculture has greatly increased crop yields. 农业机械化极大地提高了农作物产量。| Synonyms: automation, industrialization | Antonyms: manual labor, handcraft | noun
