nondeterminants:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnənts/ | n. | 非限定词 | Words or phrases that do not function as determiners, typically including nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs.  They do not specify or limit the noun they modify in the same way that determiners do. |  The sentence "Cats sleep a lot" uses "cats" as a nondeterminant.  猫睡很多。| Synonyms: non-limiting words, open-class words | Antonyms: determiners, limiting words | noun

nondeterminant:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnənt/ | adj. | 非限定的 | Not serving to determine or limit; not functioning as a determiner. | A nondeterminant adjective doesn't specify which noun it refers to. 非限定形容词并不指定它指的是哪个名词。 | Synonyms: indefinite, non-specific | Antonyms: determinant, definite, specific | adjective
