nasals:/ˈneɪzəlz/| n.|鼻音，鼻音字母|Sounds produced by air passing through the nose, such as /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/.|例句：The word "sing" ends with a nasal sound.  单词"sing"以鼻音结尾。

例句：Many languages have nasal vowels. 许多语言都有鼻元音。|近义词：nasal consonants|反义词:oral sounds (oral stops, fricatives, etc.)|词性:noun
