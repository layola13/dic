ducker:/ˈdʌkər/| n.|潜水者；躲避者；低头弯腰的人；一种头部运动（尤指表示同意或顺从）；(棒球) 故意躲避投球的击球手|someone who ducks or dodges something, often by bending down quickly; someone who dives under water|The ducker managed to avoid being hit by the flying debris.|近义词：diver, dodger, evader|反义词: confronter|noun

ducker:/ˈdʌkər/| v.|快速低头；闪避；潜水；躲避；逃避|to lower the head or body suddenly, especially to avoid being seen or hit; to dive briefly under water; to avoid or evade something|He ducked under the low-hanging branch.|近义词：dodge, evade, avoid, dip, plunge|反义词: confront, face|verb
