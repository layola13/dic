interlaces:/ˌɪntərˈleɪsɪz/
verb
交织，交错
to weave or twine together; intermix
例句：The branches of the trees interlaced overhead.  树枝在头顶交错。

例句：She interlaced her fingers nervously. 她紧张地交叉着手指。
近义词：interweave, intertwine, braid, plait
反义词：separate, untangle, disentangle
词性:v.

interlace:/ˌɪntərˈleɪs/
noun
交织，交错
the act of interlacing; something that is interlaced
例句：The interlace of the branches created a dappled shade.  树枝的交错形成了斑驳的树荫。

例句：The intricate interlace of the Celtic knot design was beautiful.  凯尔特结设计的复杂交错很漂亮。
近义词：interweaving, intertwining, network, lattice
反义词：separation
词性:n.
