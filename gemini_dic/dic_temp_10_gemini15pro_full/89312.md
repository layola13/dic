homogangliate:/ˌhɒməʊˈɡæŋɡliɪt/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˈɡæŋɡliɪt/
adjective
同神经节的，具有同型神经节的
Having or characterized by ganglia of similar form and function.
Mollusks are typically homogangliate, meaning their ganglia are similar in structure and function.  软体动物通常是同神经节的，这意味着它们的神经节在结构和功能上相似。
This species exhibits a homogangliate nervous system. 该物种表现出同神经节的神经系统。
近义词: isoganglionic
反义词: heterogangliate
词性: adjective
