cornstarch:/ˈkɔːrnstɑːrtʃ/ (US) /ˈkɔːnstɑːtʃ/ (UK)| n. |玉米淀粉|A fine, white powder made from the endosperm of corn kernels, used as a thickening agent in cooking.|She used cornstarch to thicken the gravy.|近义词：cornflour (UK), tapioca starch, potato starch|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "thinning agent")|noun
