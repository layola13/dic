atonable:/əˈtoʊnəbəl/
adjective
可赎罪的;可弥补的
Capable of being atoned for; expiable.
His crimes were so heinous, they were not atonable.  他的罪行如此滔天，不可饶恕。
The damage done to their relationship was atonable through sincere apologies and changed behavior.  他们之间关系的损害可以通过真诚的道歉和改变行为来弥补。
近义词:excusable, forgivable, pardonable
反义词:unforgivable, inexpiable
词性:adjective
词源:From atone + -able.  来自atone + -able.
