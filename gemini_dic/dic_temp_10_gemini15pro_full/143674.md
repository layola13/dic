outlaugh:/ˌaʊtˈlæf/ | v. | 笑得比…更大声；以笑声胜过 | To laugh longer or louder than someone else. |  He outlaughed everyone in the room with his infectious guffaw. 他富有感染力的哄笑声盖过了房间里所有人的笑声。|Synonyms: outdo, surpass | Antonyms: be outdone, be surpassed | verb

outlaugh:/ˌaʊtˈlæf/ | n. |  胜过（某人）的笑声；持续时间更长的笑声 | An instance of laughing longer or louder than someone else. |  Her outlaugh was a clear sign of victory in the playful argument.  她持续的笑声清楚地表明了她在玩笑般的争论中取得了胜利。 | Synonyms: triumph, victory | Antonyms: defeat, loss | noun
