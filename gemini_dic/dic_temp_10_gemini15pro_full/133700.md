nonfricatives:/ˌnɒnˈfrɪkətɪvz/ |n.|非摩擦音|Sounds produced without friction in the vocal tract, such as vowels and nasals. These sounds are also called sonorants.|Example: The sounds /m/, /n/, /ŋ/, /l/, /r/, /w/, and /j/ are nonfricatives in English.  英语中的/m/, /n/, /ŋ/, /l/, /r/, /w/, 和 /j/ 都是非摩擦音。| Synonyms: sonorants, resonant consonants| Antonyms: fricatives |noun
