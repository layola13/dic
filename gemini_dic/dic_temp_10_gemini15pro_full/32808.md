choirs:/ˈkwaɪərz/ (US), /ˈkɔɪəz/ (UK)| n. |合唱团，唱诗班|A group of singers, especially one that sings in a church or performs regularly.|The church choirs sang beautifully at the Christmas service. 教堂唱诗班在圣诞节仪式上唱得非常优美。|Synonyms: chorus, ensemble, glee club|Antonyms: soloist, individual|Noun
