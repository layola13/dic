panoistic:/pænɔɪˈstɪk/ | adj. | 全腺的 |  Describing an exocrine gland in which the entire cell disintegrates to release its secretions. |  The panoistic glands of certain insects produce large quantities of pheromones. 某些昆虫的全腺体产生大量的费洛蒙。|  merocrine | holocrine | adjective
