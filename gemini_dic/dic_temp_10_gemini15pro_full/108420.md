lawny:/ˈlɔːni/| adj.|草坪似的，草地般的；绿草如茵的|Resembling a lawn; covered with grass like a lawn.  Characteristic of a lawn; like a lawn, especially in its smoothness and greenness.|The park boasts a lawny expanse perfect for picnics.  公园里有一片绿草如茵的开阔地，非常适合野餐。|近义词：grassy, verdant, lush|反义词：barren, arid|词性:形容词
词源: From lawn + -y.  "Lawn" comes from the Middle English *launde*, from Old North French *lande*, meaning "heath, moor".
