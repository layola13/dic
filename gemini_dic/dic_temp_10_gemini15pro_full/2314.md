ades: /eɪdz/
noun
(通常复数) 含糖饮料；果汁汽水
Sweetened drinks, typically made with fruit juice.
Lemonade and other fruit ades are popular in the summer. 柠檬水和其他水果汽水在夏天很受欢迎。

近义词: beverages, drinks, soft drinks
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑) alcoholic beverages, liquor


ETYMOLOGY->
From -ade, a suffix of Latin origin, denoting a fruit drink.  Originally, the word referred specifically to lemonade, but it has been extended to other fruit drinks.  The Latin root is *citreāta*, meaning "lemonade."
