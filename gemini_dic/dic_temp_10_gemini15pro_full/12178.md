as-best-one-can:/æz ˈbɛst wʌn kæn/ | adv. | 尽力地，尽可能地 |  To the best of one's ability, within one's limitations.  It implies doing something with effort, even if the outcome isn't perfect. |  She completed the task as best she could, given the limited resources.  她利用有限的资源，尽其所能地完成了任务。 |  as well as possible, to the best of one's ability, as much as possible |  half-heartedly, poorly, carelessly | adverb
