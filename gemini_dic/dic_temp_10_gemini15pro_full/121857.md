misrepresenter:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛntər/ | n. |歪曲事实者；误传者|Someone who misrepresents something; gives a false or misleading account of something. | He was accused of being a misrepresenter of the facts. 他被指控歪曲事实。| The advertising campaign was criticized for being a misrepresenter of the product's capabilities.  这个广告宣传活动因歪曲产品性能而受到批评。| synonyms: liar, falsifier, distorter, perverter | antonyms: truth-teller, honest person | noun
