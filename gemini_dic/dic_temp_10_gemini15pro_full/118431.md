mes:/meɪz/ (French pronunciation) | pron. | 我的 (plural) | My (plural).  Used in formal or literary contexts, often with a possessive sense. Derived from Old French "mei," "mi," or "moi," meaning "me." Ultimately from Latin "mihi," dative of "ego," meaning "I." |  Mes amis sont arrivés. My friends have arrived. |  Synonyms: my (in most contexts), our (if referring to a group including the speaker) | Antonyms: your, his, her, their, its | Pronoun
