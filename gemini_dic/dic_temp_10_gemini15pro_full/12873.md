assuagers:/əˈsweɪdʒərz/
| n. |慰藉者；缓和者；减轻者|People or things that assuage or lessen the intensity of something unpleasant.|The kind words of the chaplain were assuagers of her grief. 牧师的仁慈话语减轻了她的悲伤。|近义词：soothers, relievers, mitigators, alleviators|反义词：aggravators, intensifiers|Noun


assuage:/əˈsweɪdʒ/
| vt. |缓和；减轻；使满足|To make (something unpleasant or painful) less intense.|The medicine assuaged his pain.  这药减轻了他的疼痛。|近义词：relieve, ease, soothe, mitigate, alleviate|反义词：aggravate, intensify, worsen|Verb
