chickenshit:/ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪt/| n. adj.|懦夫，胆小鬼；胆小的，懦弱的|A cowardly or contemptible person. 词源：
This term is considered vulgar slang. It likely originates from the perceived timidity of chickens.  The first recorded use is from the early 20th century, particularly in military slang.

注意事项:
This word is considered highly offensive and should be used with extreme caution. It's generally inappropriate for formal settings.  Its usage can be perceived as aggressive and disrespectful.
