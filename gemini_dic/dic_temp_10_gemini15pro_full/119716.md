midships:/ˌmɪdˈʃɪps/| adv. adj. n.|在船中部；在船舯部|In or toward the middle of a ship. |The captain ordered the crew to gather midships. 船长命令船员到船中部集合。|Near, center, amidships|fore, aft|adverb, adjective, noun
Etymology: From Middle English *middeschippes*, from *midde* (middle) + *schippes* (genitive plural of *schip*, ship).  Meaning "in the middle of a ship" is from the 15th century.
