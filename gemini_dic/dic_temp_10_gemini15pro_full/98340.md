insinuation:/ɪnˌsɪnjuˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 暗示，含沙射影，曲解 |The insinuation that she was lying made her furious. 她被暗示在撒谎，这让她很生气。| insinuation is suggesting or implying something bad or unpleasant in an indirect way. |  Her continual insinuations about her co-workers finally got her fired. 她不断地含沙射影她的同事，最终导致她被解雇了。| implication, suggestion, innuendo |  direct statement, explicit statement | noun
