empaistic:/ɪmˈpeɪstɪk/ | adj. | 厚涂的，用颜料厚涂的 | Relating to the technique of impasto, where paint is laid on thickly so that it stands out from the surface. |  The artist's empaistic style, with its thick swirls of paint, gave the painting a three-dimensional quality. 艺术家厚涂的风格，加上厚厚的颜料漩涡，赋予了这幅画三维的质感。 | near synonyms: impasto, textured, thick, heavy | antonyms: smooth, flat, thin | adjective

empaistic:/ɪmˈpeɪstɪk/ | n. | 厚涂法 | The technique of laying on paint thickly. | The empaistic in this painting adds a dynamic texture to the canvas.  这幅画中的厚涂法为画布增添了动态的质感。 | near synonyms: impasto | antonyms: smooth finish | noun
