horse-trading:/ˈhɔːrsˌtreɪdɪŋ/
n.
讨价还价；政治交易
negotiation characterized by hard bargaining and shrewd compromises
例句：The budget agreement was the result of weeks of intense horse-trading.  预算协议是数周激烈讨价还价的结果。

例句：There was a lot of horse-trading before they finally reached a deal.  在他们最终达成协议之前，进行了大量的讨价还价。

近义词：bargaining, negotiating, compromising, dickering
反义词：cooperation, collaboration
