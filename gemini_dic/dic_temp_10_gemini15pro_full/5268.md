allomorphisms:/ˌæləˈmɔːrfɪzəmz/| n. |同质异形体；同质异象|Different forms of the same morpheme.  The variations are often predictable based on phonetic environment.|The plural suffix in English has several allomorphisms: /-s/, /-z/, and /-ɪz/.  英语复数后缀有几种同质异形体：/-s/、/-z/ 和 /-ɪz/。|Synonyms: allomorphs, variant forms|Antonyms: N/A|noun
