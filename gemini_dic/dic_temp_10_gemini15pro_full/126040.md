mustache:/ˈmʌstæʃ/ (US), /məˈstɑːʃ/ (UK)| n. |胡子，髭|A mustache is hair grown above the upper lip.|He twirled his mustache thoughtfully. 他若有所思地捻着他的胡子.|Synonyms: moustache (British spelling), whiskers, beard (when referring to facial hair in general)|Antonyms: clean-shaven, beardless|noun
