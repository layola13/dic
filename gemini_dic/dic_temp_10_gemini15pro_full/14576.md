aviator:/ˈeɪvieɪtər/| n.|飞行员；飞行家|A person who operates an aircraft.|例句：The aviator skillfully landed the plane during the storm.  飞行员在暴风雨中熟练地将飞机降落。

例句： Amelia Earhart was a pioneering aviator. 艾米莉亚·埃尔哈特是一位先锋飞行员。|近义词：pilot, flyer, airman|反义词:passenger|词性:noun

aviators:/ˈeɪvieɪtərz/| n.|飞行员太阳镜；飞行眼镜|Sunglasses with large, teardrop-shaped lenses, originally designed for pilots.|例句：He wore a pair of aviators to protect his eyes from the sun. 他戴着一副飞行员太阳镜来保护眼睛免受阳光照射。

例句：Aviators are a classic style of sunglasses. 飞行员太阳镜是一款经典款式的太阳镜。|近义词：sunglasses, shades|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
