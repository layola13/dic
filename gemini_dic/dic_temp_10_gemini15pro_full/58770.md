dunlin:/ˈdʌnlɪn/ | n. | 敦林; 三趾滨鹬 | A small wading bird with a long, slightly downcurved bill, found on coasts and estuaries.  It breeds in arctic regions and winters further south. | I saw a flock of dunlins probing the mudflats for food. 我看到一群敦林在泥滩上觅食。| Dunlins are known for their distinctive winter plumage. 敦林以其独特的冬羽而闻名。 | 近义词:sandpiper, stint | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
