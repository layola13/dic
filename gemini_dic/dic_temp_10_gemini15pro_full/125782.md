murexide:/ˈmjʊərɪksaɪd/ | n. | 紫脲酸铵;氨基嘌呤;紫色指示剂 | An ammonium salt of purpuric acid, used as an indicator in analytical chemistry. It forms a pink complex with calcium ions. |  Murexide is used as an indicator in the complexometric titration of calcium. 紫脲酸铵在钙的络合滴定中用作指示剂。

Murexide changes color from pink to purple in the presence of calcium ions.  紫脲酸铵在钙离子存在下会从粉红色变为紫色。 | 近义词: ammonium purpurate (化学名称) | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
