nonanimatingly:/ˌnɒnˈænɪmeɪtɪŋli/
| adv. | 无生气地，缺乏活力地 | In a manner that lacks animation or liveliness; dully. | He spoke nonanimatingly, barely making eye contact.  他说话毫无生气，几乎没有眼神交流。|
| The lecture was delivered nonanimatingly, causing many students to doze off.  讲座进行得死气沉沉，导致许多学生打瞌睡。 | 近义词：lifelessly, dully, spiritlessly, vapidly | 反义词：animatedly, lively, vibrantly, energetically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
animate (verb): from Latin animare "give breath to, quicken," from anima "breath, soul"
-ing (suffix): forms present participles and gerunds
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
