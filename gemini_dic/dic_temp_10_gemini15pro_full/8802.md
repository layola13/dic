antiempiricists:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪrɪsɪsts/
|n.|反经验主义者|Those who oppose empiricism, a theory that knowledge comes primarily from sensory experience.|The antiempiricists argued that reason, not experience, was the primary source of knowledge.|近义词:rationalists, idealists|反义词:empiricists|noun
