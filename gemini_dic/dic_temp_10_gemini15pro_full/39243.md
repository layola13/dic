congratulator:/kənˈɡrætʃəleɪtər/ | n. | 祝贺者，恭贺者 | A person who offers congratulations. |  Example: He was surrounded by congratulators after winning the race.  他赢得比赛后被祝贺者包围。

Example: Many congratulators sent him messages on social media. 许多祝贺者在社交媒体上给他发了信息。

| 近义词: well-wisher, felicitator | 反义词: critic, detractor | 词性: noun
