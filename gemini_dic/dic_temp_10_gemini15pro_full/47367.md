deactivated:/diːˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
已停用的，已关闭的
made inactive or no longer effective.
My old phone has been deactivated. 我的旧手机已被停用。
The security system was deactivated. 安全系统已被停用。
近义词:disabled, inactivated, off, unconnected
反义词:activated, enabled, on, connected
词性:adjective

deactivated:/diːˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
停用，使失效
past participle of deactivate
His account was deactivated due to inactivity. 他的帐户因不活动而被停用。
The bomb was deactivated before it could explode. 炸弹在爆炸前被拆除了。
近义词:disabled, inactivated, neutralized
反义词:activated, enabled, started
词性:verb (past participle)


词源:
From de- + activate + -ed.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove," and "activate" means to make something active. "-ed" is a suffix forming the past participle.
