diablo:/diˈɑːbloʊ/ /dɪˈæbloʊ/
noun
魔鬼，恶魔
A Spanish word meaning "devil".  Often used to refer to a powerful demon or evil spirit.
The legend of the diablo haunted the small village.  这个小村庄一直被魔鬼的传说所困扰。
The new video game features a powerful diablo as the main antagonist.  这款新的电子游戏以一个强大的恶魔作为主要反派。
synonyms: devil, demon, fiend, Satan
antonyms: angel, god, deity
