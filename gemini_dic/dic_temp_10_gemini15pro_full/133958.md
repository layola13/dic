nonhepatic:/ˌnɒnheˈpætɪk/ /ˌnɑːnhɪˈpætɪk/
adjective
非肝的；肝外的
Not relating to or originating in the liver.
例句：Nonhepatic causes of jaundice include hemolytic anemia and biliary obstruction.  非肝性黄疸的病因包括溶血性贫血和胆道阻塞。

例句：The doctor investigated nonhepatic sources for the patient's elevated bilirubin levels. 医生调查了病人胆红素水平升高的非肝脏来源。

近义词：extrahepatic
反义词：hepatic
词性：adjective
