anti-productivity:/ˌæntiˌprɒdʌkˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌæntiˌproʊdʌkˈtɪvɪti/| n.| 反生产力；反效率 |The concept of anti-productivity focuses on activities that seem enjoyable but ultimately hinder progress. 反生产力的概念集中于那些看起来令人愉悦但最终阻碍进步的活动。|例句：My constant social media scrolling is a form of anti-productivity. 我不断地浏览社交媒体是一种反生产力的行为。|近义词：unproductivity, inefficiency, ineffectiveness |反义词: productivity, efficiency, effectiveness |词性:名词

anti-productive:/ˌæntiprəˈdʌktɪv/ /ˌæntiprəˈdʌktɪv/| adj.| 反生产力的；反效率的| Engaging in anti-productive activities can lead to missed deadlines and increased stress. 从事反生产力的活动会导致错过截止日期和增加压力。|例句：That meeting was completely anti-productive; we didn't accomplish anything. 那次会议完全是反生产力的；我们什么也没完成。|近义词：unproductive, inefficient, ineffective |反义词: productive, efficient, effective |词性:形容词
