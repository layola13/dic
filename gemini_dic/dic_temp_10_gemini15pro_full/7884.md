antedate:/ˌæntiˈdeɪt/
| v. |先于，早于| to exist or happen before something else; to give something an earlier date than its real one | The church antedates the Norman Conquest. 这座教堂早于诺曼征服时期。| synonyms: precede, predate, pre-exist | antonyms: postdate, follow | verb
antedating:/ˌæntiˈdeɪtɪŋ/
| n. | 先于；早于；回溯 |The act of predating something; assigning an earlier date to something than its actual date.| The antedating of the document is suspicious. 这份文件的回溯日期很可疑。 | synonyms: predating, backdating | antonyms: postdating | noun
antedating:/ˌæntiˈdeɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 先前的；早于的 | Existing or occurring before something else in time.| An antedating event led to the current situation.  先前发生的事件导致了目前的局面。| synonyms:  prior, earlier, preceding | antonyms: subsequent, later, following | adjective
