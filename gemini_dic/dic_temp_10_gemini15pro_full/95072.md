inadvisabilities:/ˌɪnədˌvaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|不明智之举；不妥当的行为；不可取之处|The inadvisabilities of travelling alone in that region were stressed by the authorities. 当局强调了在该地区独自旅行的不妥之处。|English Explanation: Plural form of "inadvisability," referring to multiple instances of something being unwise or imprudent.|例句：The report highlighted the inadvisabilities of investing in such a volatile market. 报告强调了投资如此动荡的市场是不明智的。

例句：He ignored the inadvisabilities of confronting his boss directly. 他忽视了直接对抗老板的不妥之处。|近义词：imprudences, unwise actions, mistakes, errors in judgment|反义词: wisdom, prudence, advisabilities|词性:noun
