counterfeitnesses:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtnəsəz/| n. |伪造；假冒（罕用，通常用单数 counterfeitness）|The state or quality of being counterfeit; forgery.  This word is rarely used; counterfeitness (singular) is more common.| Due to the sheer number of counterfeitnesses, it was difficult to determine the authentic artifact. 由于大量的赝品，很难确定真正的文物。| Near synonyms: forgeries, falsifications, imitations | Antonyms: authenticities, genuinenesses | noun
