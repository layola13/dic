bicentenaries: /ˌbaɪsɛnˈtɛnəriːz/ /ˌbaɪsɛnˈtɛnəriːz/
n.
二百周年纪念; 二百周年纪念日
the two-hundredth anniversary of a significant event.
The city is celebrating its bicentenaries with a series of events.  这个城市正在举行一系列活动来庆祝其建市二百周年。
The museum held an exhibition to mark the bicentenaries of the artist's birth.  博物馆举办了一个展览来纪念这位艺术家的诞辰二百周年。
近义词: bicentennials
反义词:  (No direct antonym for an anniversary)
词性: 名词 (plural)
