antistrophon:/ænˈtɪstrəfən/ | n. | 反唱，轮唱 |  A rhetorical device in which the speaker turns an opponent's argument against them.  It involves taking the opponent's words and using them to support one's own position. |  "He accused me of being too aggressive, but it was he who started the fight – a perfect antistrophon." 他指责我太咄咄逼人，但偏偏是他先挑起的争端——一个完美的反唱。 |  reversal, refutation, retort, counterargument |  confirmation, agreement | noun
