daikon:/ˈdaɪkɒn/ (UK), /ˈdaɪkɑːn/ (US)| n.|萝卜，大根，白萝卜|A large, mild-flavored East Asian radish, white in color and often used in salads and as a garnish.|例句：I added some sliced daikon to my salad for extra crunch. 我在沙拉里加了一些萝卜片，使其更加爽脆。

例句：Daikon is a popular ingredient in Japanese cuisine. 白萝卜是日本料理中很受欢迎的食材。|近义词：radish, mooli, |反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Japanese 大根 (だいこん, daikon), literally "large root".
