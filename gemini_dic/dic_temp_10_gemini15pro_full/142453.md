oscilloscope:/əˈsɪləskəʊp/ (UK) /əˈsɪləˌskoʊp/ (US)| n. |示波器|An electronic instrument that allows observation of constantly varying signal voltages, usually as a two-dimensional plot of one or more signals as a function of time.|The engineer used an oscilloscope to analyze the signal's waveform.|Synonyms: scope, CRO (Cathode Ray Oscilloscope)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
