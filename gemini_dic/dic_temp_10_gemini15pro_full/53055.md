diopter:/daɪˈɑːptər/ (US)| n. |屈光度|A unit of measurement of the refractive power of a lens, equal to the reciprocal of the focal length in meters. 例句：A diopter is a unit of measurement for lens power. 屈光度是衡量镜片度数的单位。|近义词：dioptre (British spelling)|反义词: None|词性:noun
