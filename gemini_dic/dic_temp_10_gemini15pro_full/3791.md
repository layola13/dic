agminal:/ˈæɡmɪnəl/| adj.|成群的，聚集的|Of or relating to an agmen; clustered or growing in a dense cluster.  Referring to a group, especially a moving group,  like a procession or army.|The agminal behavior of the bees swarming around the hive was fascinating. 蜜蜂围绕蜂巢成群聚集的行为令人着迷。|近义词：gregarious, clustered, aggregated|反义词：scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词
agmen:/ˈæɡmən/| n.|行进的队伍，成群移动的动物|A body of people or animals moving forward in a line or group; a procession, troop, or swarm.|The slow agmen of the caterpillars made their way across the leaf. 毛毛虫缓慢地排成一队穿过树叶。|近义词：procession, troop, swarm|反义词：scattering, dispersal|词性:名词
