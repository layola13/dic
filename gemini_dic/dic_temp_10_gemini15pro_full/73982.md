noun|双周刊|A publication issued every two weeks.|例句：I subscribe to a gardening fortnightly. 我订阅了一份园艺双周刊。|近义词：biweekly|反义词:weekly, monthly|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "fourteen nights," reflecting the original meaning of "occurring every fourteen nights." The word "fourteen" itself comes from Old English "fēowertēne,"  a combination of "fēower" (four) and "tēne" (ten).
