cogens:/ˈkoʊdʒənz/ | n. | 联产系统; 热电联产系统 | Systems that produce both heat and power, often abbreviated as CHP (Combined Heat and Power). |  Cogeneration plants are more efficient than separate heat and power plants. 热电联产工厂比单独的热电厂和发电厂效率更高。| synonyms: CHP plants, combined heat and power systems | antonyms: separate heat and power plants | noun
