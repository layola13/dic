hogreeve:/ˈhoʊɡriːv/ | n. | 猪倌，养猪人 |A person who takes care of hogs or pigs. |例句：The hogreeve checked on the piglets every morning.  猪倌每天早上都要检查小猪的情况。

例句：The hogreeve was responsible for feeding and cleaning the pigsty. 猪倌负责喂猪和打扫猪圈。| 近义词：swineherd, pig keeper | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: hog + reeve

* **hog:**  Middle English *hog*, from Old English *hogg*.
* **reeve:** Middle English *reve*, from Old English *ġerēfa* "official, agent, steward," related to Old High German *gravio* "count." Originally, a reeve was a local official.  Over time, the term became associated with specific occupations, such as hogreeve.
