buccaneer:/ˌbʌkəˈnɪər/| n.|海盗，掠夺者|A sailor, often operating outside the law, who attacks and robs ships at sea.  Originally referred to pirates of the Caribbean.|例句：The buccaneers sailed the Caribbean, plundering Spanish galleons.  这些海盗在加勒比海航行，掠夺西班牙大帆船。

例句：Stories of daring buccaneers have captivated readers for centuries.  关于勇敢海盗的故事几个世纪以来一直吸引着读者。|近义词：pirate, corsair, privateer, freebooter|反义词:lawman, sailor|词性:noun
