outclimbs:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪmz/
verb
胜过攀登，爬得比...高
Surpasses in climbing; climbs higher or faster than.
The young mountaineer outclimbs his older companions.  这位年轻的登山者比他的老同伴爬得更高。
The experienced climber easily outclimbs the novice.  经验丰富的登山者轻松地超过了新手。
近义词:surpasses, beats, exceeds
反义词:lags behind, trails

词性:v.
