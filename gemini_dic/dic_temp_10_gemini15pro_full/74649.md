free-form:/friː fɔːrm/| adj.|自由格式的，非正式的|Having no fixed form or structure; not following a conventional pattern.|例句：The artist preferred a free-form style of painting.  这位艺术家偏爱自由形式的绘画风格。

例句：The discussion was free-form, allowing participants to raise any topic. 讨论是自由形式的，允许参与者提出任何话题。|近义词：unstructured, informal, open-ended|反义词: structured, formal, fixed|词性:形容词
free-form:/friː fɔːrm/| n.|自由格式|Something that has no fixed form or structure.|例句：The architect's design was a free-form sculpture.  这位建筑师的设计是一座自由形式的雕塑。

例句：The poem was a free-form expression of the writer's emotions. 这首诗是作者情感的自由形式表达。|近义词：improvisation, ad-lib|反义词: framework, template|词性:名词
