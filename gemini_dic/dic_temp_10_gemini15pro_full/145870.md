overdescribing:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度描述的；过于详尽描述的 | Providing too much detail in a description, often to the point of being tedious or unnecessary. |  She was overdescribing the plot of the movie, spoiling the ending for everyone. 她过度描述了电影的情节，把结局都剧透了。 | 近义词：verbose, prolix, long-winded | 反义词：concise, succinct, brief |词性: 形容词

overdescribe:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskraɪb/ | v. | 过度描述；过于详尽地描述 | To describe something with excessive detail, often to the point of being tedious or unnecessary. | He tends to overdescribe things, making simple stories long and complicated. 他倾向于过度描述事物，使简单的故事变得冗长复杂。 | 近义词：elaborate excessively, belabor | 反义词：summarize, condense |词性: 动词
