ciceronian: /ˌsɪsəˈroʊniən/ | adj. n. |  西塞罗式的；雄辩的；西塞罗的追随者 |  Resembling or characteristic of the Roman orator Cicero, especially in eloquence, elegant style, and elaborate sentence structure.  Of or relating to Cicero. A follower or admirer of Cicero. |  His ciceronian prose impressed everyone. 他的西塞罗式的散文给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。 | 近义词: eloquent, rhetorical, ornate, grandiloquent | 反义词: plain, simple, unadorned, concise | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From the name of Marcus Tullius Cicero (106-43 BC), a Roman statesman, orator, and writer, + -ian.
