impressment:/ɪmˈprɛsmənt/
noun
强制征兵；强征；抓壮丁
The act of forcing someone into service, especially naval service.
The impressment of sailors was a common practice in the 18th and 19th centuries.  18和19世纪，强征水手是一种常见的做法。
conscription, forced enlistment
