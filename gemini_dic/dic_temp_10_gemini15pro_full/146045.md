overdriving:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪvɪŋ/| n. |过度驱动，超速驾驶|The act of pushing something, especially a machine or person, beyond its or their limits, often resulting in damage or exhaustion.  It can also refer to distorting an audio signal by applying excessive gain.|例句：Overdriving the amplifier created a distorted sound.  过度驱动放大器产生了失真的声音。

例句：He was ticketed for overdriving his truck. 他因为超速驾驶卡车而被开了罚单。|近义词：overworking, straining, pushing, exceeding|反义词: underpowering, babying|词性:名词
