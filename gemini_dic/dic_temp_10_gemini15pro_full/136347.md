nonresiliently:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈzɪ.li.ənt.li/ | adv. | 非弹性地，缺乏韧性地 | In a way that lacks resilience; without the ability to recover quickly from difficulties. | The economy nonresiliently reacted to the sudden increase in interest rates. 经济对利率的突然上升反应迟钝，缺乏韧性。| The bridge nonresiliently swayed in the strong winds, causing concern among engineers.  桥梁在强风中摇摆不定，缺乏韧性，引起了工程师们的担忧。 | 近义词：inflexibly, rigidly, stiffly | 反义词：resiliently, elastically, flexibly | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of
resilient (adjective):  able to recoil or spring back into shape after bending, stretching, or being compressed. From Latin *resiliens*, present participle of *resilire* "to leap back, rebound," from re- "back" + salire "to leap."
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
