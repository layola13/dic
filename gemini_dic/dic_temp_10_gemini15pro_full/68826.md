fascinators:/ˈfæsɪneɪtərz/| n. | 头饰，女帽，小礼帽 | A fascinator is a decorative headpiece, typically worn by women at weddings and other formal occasions. It is usually a small, ornate piece attached to a headband or clip. |  She wore a beautiful fascinator to the wedding. 她戴了一顶漂亮的头饰参加婚礼。

The fascinator added a touch of elegance to her outfit.  这顶小礼帽为她的装扮增添了一丝优雅。 | 近义词：headpiece, hair ornament | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "hat" if considering size difference) | 词性:noun
