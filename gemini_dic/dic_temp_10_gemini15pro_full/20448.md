bitterling: /ˈbɪtərliŋ/ | n. | 欧亚苦鱼;鰟鮍 | A small freshwater fish (Rhodeus amarus) of the carp family, native to Europe and Asia. The female deposits its eggs in the gills of a freshwater mussel, where they are fertilized and develop. |  The bitterling relies on mussels for reproduction.  鰟鮍依靠蚌类繁殖。|  The introduction of bitterling has negatively impacted native mussel populations. 引进鰟鮍对本地蚌类种群产生了负面影响。 | synonyms: rose bitterling | antonyms: N/A | noun
