omnivore:/ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/ | n. |杂食动物；什么都读的人 | An animal that eats both plants and animals.  A person who is interested in many different things. |例句：Bears are omnivores, consuming berries and fish. 熊是杂食动物，吃浆果和鱼。

例句：My friend is an intellectual omnivore, reading everything from philosophy to comics. 我的朋友是个知识上的杂食者，从哲学到漫画什么都读。 | 近义词：generalist, all-eater | 反义词：herbivore, carnivore | 词性: noun


omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. | 杂食性的；什么都读的 | Eating both plants and animals.  Having wide-ranging tastes or interests. | 例句：Pigs are omnivorous creatures. 猪是杂食动物。

例句：She has an omnivorous appetite for knowledge. 她对知识有着如饥似渴的渴望。 | 近义词：all-devouring, eclectic | 反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous | 词性: adjective
