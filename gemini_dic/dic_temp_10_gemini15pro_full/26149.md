busway:/ˈbʌsweɪ/
n.
公交专用道；巴士专用道
A road or lane reserved for buses.
The city created a new busway to improve public transportation.  这座城市新建了一条公交专用道以改善公共交通。
bus lane, transit way
road, highway
noun

busway:/ˈbʌsweɪ/
n.
母线槽；汇流排
A metal channel for carrying a large electrical current.
The electrician installed a new busway to handle the increased power demand. 电工安装了一个新的母线槽以应对增加的电力需求。
bus bar, power distribution center
wire, cable
noun
