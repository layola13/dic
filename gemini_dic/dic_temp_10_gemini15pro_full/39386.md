conjunct:/ˈkʌndʒʌŋkt/
noun
连接词
a word that connects clauses or sentences or words.
"And" and "but" are common examples of conjuncts. “和”和“但”是连接词的常见例子。
The use of conjuncts can improve the flow of writing. 使用连接词可以改善写作的流畅性。
近义词:conjunction, connector
反义词:N/A
词性:noun


词源：From Latin conjunctus, past participle of conjungere (“to join together”), from con- (“together”) + jungere (“to join”).
