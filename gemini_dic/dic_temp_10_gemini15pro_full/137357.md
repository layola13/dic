nonteachable:/ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃəbl/
adjective
不可教的; 无法教的
Not able to be taught.
His disruptive behavior made him virtually nonteachable. 他的破坏性行为让他几乎无法被教导。
The concept is so abstract that it's nonteachable to young children. 这个概念太抽象了，幼儿无法理解。
近义词:incapable of being taught, uneducable, unreceptive
反义词:teachable, educable
词性:adjective
