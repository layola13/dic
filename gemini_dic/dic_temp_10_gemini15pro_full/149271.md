ozena:/oʊˈziːnə/| n. |臭鼻症|A chronic disease of the nose, characterized by atrophy of the turbinates and mucous membrane, and the formation of foul-smelling crusts.|He suffered from ozena, which made social interaction difficult.|近义词：atrophic rhinitis|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Ancient Greek οζαίνα (ozaína, “fetid polypus in the nose”), from ὄζω (ózō, “I smell”).
