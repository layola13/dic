bridlers:/ˈbraɪdlərz/
n.
控制者；约束者；给…上嚼口的人
People who bridle or restrain something or someone.  Those who control or check.
The bridlers of the wild horses demonstrated immense skill and patience.  给野马套上嚼口的人展现了极大的技巧和耐心。
Synonyms: restrainers, controllers, checkers, regulators
Antonyms: liberators, enablers, instigators
