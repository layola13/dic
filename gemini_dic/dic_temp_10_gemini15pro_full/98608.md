instructively:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪvli/
adverb
有教育意义地；启发地
in a way that provides useful information or advice
The museum's exhibits are instructively labeled.  博物馆的展品都贴上了有教育意义的标签。
The teacher explained the concept instructively. 老师很有启发性地解释了这个概念。

Synonym:
educatively, informatively, enlighteningly, helpfully

Antonym:
unhelpfully, confusingly, misleadingly

词性: 副词
