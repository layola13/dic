cumulose:/ˈkjuːmjʊləs/| adj.|积云状的，堆积的|Characterized by the accumulation or aggregation of distinct elements; formed by the addition of new material to existing material, often resulting in a rounded or heap-like form.  Relating to or resembling cumulus clouds.|The cumulose limestone formations were impressive. 这些积云状的石灰岩地层令人印象深刻。|Synonyms: amassed, accumulated, aggregated, heaped, piled|Antonyms: dispersed, scattered, dissipated|adjective


cumulus:/ˈkjuːmjʊləs/| n.|积云|A cloud forming rounded masses heaped on each other above a flat base at fairly low altitude.|The sky was dotted with fluffy white cumulus clouds. 天空中点缀着蓬松的白色积云。|Synonyms: cloud, cumulonimbus|Antonyms: N/A|noun
