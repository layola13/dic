embosser:/ɪmˈbɑːsər/ /ɪmˈbɒsə(r)/| n.|浮雕工；压花机；凸纹器|A person or machine that creates raised designs on a surface.|The embosser creates beautiful personalized stationery.  压花机制作出漂亮的个性化文具。|Synonyms:  stamp, die, tool|Antonyms: flattener|noun

emboss:/ɪmˈbɑːs/ /ɪmˈbɒs/| vt.|压印；使凸出；装饰|To create a raised design on a surface.|She embossed the company logo onto the leather. 她把公司标志压印在皮革上。|Synonyms:  stamp, impress, imprint|Antonyms: flatten, smooth|verb
