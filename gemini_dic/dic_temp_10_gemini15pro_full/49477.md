delusionary:/dɪˈluːʒənəri/
adjective
妄想的；错觉的
Based on or having the nature of a delusion.
He was diagnosed with a delusionary disorder. 他被诊断患有妄想症。
Her delusionary beliefs led her to believe she was a queen. 她妄想的信念让她相信自己是个女王。
近义词:delusional, illusory, fanciful, mistaken
反义词:realistic, factual, true
词性:adjective

delusion:/dɪˈluːʒən/
noun
妄想；错觉
A false belief or opinion, especially one held in spite of contradictory evidence.
He suffers from delusions of grandeur. 他患有夸大妄想症。
Many members of the cult shared the same delusion.  这个邪教的许多成员都有着相同的妄想。
近义词:hallucination, illusion, misconception, fallacy
反义词:reality, truth, fact
词性:noun

词源:
From Late Latin *dēlūsiōn-, from Latin dēlūs-, past participle stem of dēlūdere "to mock, deceive,"  from de- "down, from" + lūdere "to play."
