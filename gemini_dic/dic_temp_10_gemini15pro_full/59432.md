earthquake: /ˈɜːrθkweɪk/ (US) /ˈɜːθkweɪk/ (UK)| n.|地震|A sudden violent shaking of the ground, typically causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action.|例句：The earthquake caused widespread damage. 地震造成了广泛的破坏。|例句：The city was devastated by a massive earthquake.  这座城市被一场大地震摧毁了。|近义词：tremor, temblor, quake|反义词:stability, stillness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Middle English *erthequake, equivalent to earth + quake.
