delightedness:/dɪˈlaɪtɪdnəs/| n.|高兴，欣喜|The state of being delighted; great pleasure or happiness.|Her delightedness at seeing her family was evident. 她见到家人的欣喜之情溢于言表。|近义词：delight, joy, elation, happiness, pleasure|反义词：displeasure, unhappiness, sadness|词性:noun

词源: From delighted + -ness.  "Delighted" comes from the verb "delight," which traces back to Old French "deleiter" and ultimately to Latin "delectare," meaning "to allure, charm, delight." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
