noncertified:/ˌnɒnsɜːrtɪfaɪd/ | adj. |未经认证的，未经证明的|Not having received an official document proving that certain standards have been met.|The software is noncertified and may not be compatible with all systems.  这个软件未经认证，可能无法与所有系统兼容。|Example: The electrician was noncertified and therefore not legally allowed to do the work.  这位电工没有获得认证，因此法律上不允许他进行这项工作。|Synonyms: uncertified, unapproved, unlicensed|Antonyms: certified, approved, licensed|adjective
