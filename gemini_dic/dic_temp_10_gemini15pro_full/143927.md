outpunched:/ˌaʊtˈpʌntʃt/
verb
击败，胜过(尤指拳击)
defeated or surpassed someone in punching or similar forceful action.
Ali outpunched his opponent in the final round. 阿里在最后一轮击败了他的对手。
近义词:outfought, outboxed, defeated, conquered
反义词:lost to, defeated by
词性:v.

outpunched:/ˌaʊtˈpʌntʃt/
adjective
被击败的，被胜过的(尤指拳击)
having been defeated or surpassed in punching or similar forceful action.
The outpunched boxer slumped against the ropes. 被击败的拳击手瘫倒在绳索上。
近义词:defeated, beaten, conquered
反义词:victorious, winning
词性:adj.
