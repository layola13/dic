flakiest: /ˈfleɪkiɪst/ | adj. | 最易碎的；最薄片的；最古怪的 |  Superlative form of flaky: most flaky.  It describes something that breaks or crumbles easily into small, thin pieces, or someone who is unreliable, eccentric, or prone to sudden changes in mood or behavior. | The flakiest pastry I've ever eaten crumbled at the slightest touch. 我吃过的最酥的糕点一碰就碎了。| Synonyms: crumbliest, brittle, unreliable, eccentric, capricious | Antonyms: sturdy, robust, reliable, consistent, stable | adjective
