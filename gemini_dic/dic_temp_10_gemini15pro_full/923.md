abutted:/əˈbʌtɪd/
verb
毗邻；邻接；紧靠
To be next to or have a common boundary with.
Their properties abutted onto a golf course.  他们的房产紧邻高尔夫球场。

My land abuts his. 我的土地与他的土地相邻。

近义词:adjoin, border, neighbor, verge, touch
反义词:separate, divide, detach
词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abuten, *abutten, from Old French abouter (“to join end to end, place end to end, join, unite, touch, border on”), from bout (“end”), from Vulgar Latin *buttum, of uncertain origin, probably Gaulish. Compare Occitan abotar, butar (“to join, push, thrust”), Catalan abocar, abutar, butar.
