anarchistically:/ˌænərˈkɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 无政府主义地；无政府状态地 | In a manner characterized by a belief in the abolition of all forms of government and social organization.  |  The group anarchistically rejected all forms of authority.  这个团体以无政府主义的方式拒绝一切形式的权威。 | synonymously: rebelliously, lawlessly, chaotically | antonymously: obediently, lawfully, orderly | adverb

anarchy:/ˈænərki/ | n. | 无政府状态；混乱 | A state of disorder due to absence or nonrecognition of authority. | The country descended into anarchy after the coup.  政变后，这个国家陷入了无政府状态。 | synonymously: chaos, disorder, lawlessness | antonymously: order, law, government | noun
