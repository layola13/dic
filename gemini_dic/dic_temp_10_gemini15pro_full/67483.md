extra-curricular:/ˌekstrəkəˈrɪkjələr/
adjective
课外的，业余的
Relating to activities done in addition to the normal school or college course.  It describes activities that are not part of the regular academic curriculum.
She is involved in many extra-curricular activities, such as playing the violin and volunteering at the local hospital. 她参与许多课外活动，例如拉小提琴和在当地医院做志愿者。

extra-curricular:/ˌekstrəkəˈrɪkjələr/
noun
课外活动
Activities that are not part of the regular academic curriculum.
Her extra-curriculars include debating and photography. 她的课外活动包括辩论和摄影。


近义词:non-academic, outside, supplementary
反义词:curricular, academic


词性: adjective, noun


词源:  "extra-" (Latin, meaning "outside of, beyond") + "curricular" (from "curriculum," ultimately from Latin "currere," meaning "to run," referring to the course of study).
