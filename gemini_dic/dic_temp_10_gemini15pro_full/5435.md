almoner: /ˈɑːlmənər/ | n. |施舍者；施舍管理员；（医院的）社会工作者| A person (especially in a hospital or other institution) who distributes alms or charitable relief. |  The almoner visited the patients to assess their financial needs. 施舍管理员探访了病人，以评估他们的经济需求。|  The hospital almoner helped the family apply for financial aid. 医院的社会工作者帮助这家人申请经济援助。| synonyms: benefactor, philanthropist, charity worker | antonyms: miser, Scrooge | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *almosnier*, from Late Latin *eleemosynarius*, from Latin *eleemosyna* ("alms"), from Greek *ἐλεημοσύνη* (*eleēmosynē*) ("pity, alms").
