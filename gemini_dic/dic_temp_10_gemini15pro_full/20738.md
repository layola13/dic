blanc:/blɑ̃(k)/(French)/ adj. n.|白的，空白的；空白|A blank space on a page. 页面上的空白处。|The canvas was a perfect blanc. 画布一片空白。|近义词：white, empty, void, unfilled|反义词：black, full, filled|词性: 形容词, 名词|词源: From Old French blanc, from Frankish *blank, from Proto-Germanic *blankaz "white, shining, bare".
