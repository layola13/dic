blood-curdling:/ˈblʌdˌkɜːrdlɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人毛骨悚然的，恐怖的 | Causing a feeling of fear and horror, as if making one's blood cold. |  The movie had a blood-curdling climax. 这部电影的高潮部分令人毛骨悚然。| Synonyms: terrifying, horrifying, chilling, hair-raising | Antonyms: comforting, soothing, calming | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "blood" + "curdling" (from "curdle," meaning to thicken or congeal). The imagery evokes the feeling of one's blood turning cold and thick with fear.
