assizer:/əˈsaɪzər/ | n. | 协助者; 估价员; 陪审员 | A person who assists or helps, especially in legal or official matters; someone who assesses or estimates value, especially for taxation; a juror. | The assizer helped the lawyer prepare for the trial. 协助者帮助律师准备审判。 |  The tax assizer evaluated the property's value. 税务估价员评估了房产的价值。| synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, assessor, juror | antonyms: opponent, adversary | noun
