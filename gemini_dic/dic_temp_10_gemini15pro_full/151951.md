pastoralisms: /ˈpɑːstərəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 田园主义，牧歌式生活 |  The social organization and practices of peoples whose lives center on raising livestock. It often encompasses a whole way of life, including economic, social, and cultural aspects associated with herding. |  Many nomadic cultures practice various forms of pastoralisms. 许多游牧文化实行各种形式的田园主义。 |  The documentary explored the pastoralisms of the Mongolian steppe. 这部纪录片探讨了蒙古草原的田园主义。 |  nomadism, herding, transhumance | sedentary lifestyle, industrial agriculture | noun
