equalizes:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzɪz/
v.|使相等，使均衡|To make equal or uniform; to balance.
The government is trying to equalize educational opportunities. 政府正在努力使教育机会均等。
New tax laws will equalize the burden of taxation. 新的税法将均衡税收负担。
近义词: balance, level, even out, match, adjust
反义词: unbalance, differentiate, vary, bias
词性:动词
