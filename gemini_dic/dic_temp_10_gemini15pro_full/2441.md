adjudicature: /əˈdʒuːdɪkətʃər/
noun
司法权;审判权;法官的职位;法院系统
The administration of justice; the system of courts of justice; the position of a judge.
The Supreme Court adjudicature is the highest court of appeal. 最高法院是最高的终审法院。
The adjudicature of the local magistrate was questioned. 当地治安法官的审判权受到质疑。

近义词: jurisdiction, judicatory, judiciary, court system, judgeship
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider concepts like "lawlessness," "anarchy," or "lack of jurisdiction")
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French judicature, from Latin iudicatura ‘judgeship’, from iudicare ‘to judge’.
