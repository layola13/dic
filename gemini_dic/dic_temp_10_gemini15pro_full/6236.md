amnios:/ˈæmniɒs/ | n. |羊膜|The innermost membrane that encloses the developing embryo of a mammal, bird, or reptile. |The amnios surrounds the fetus and is filled with amniotic fluid. 羊膜包围着胎儿，里面充满了羊水。|synonyms: amniotic sac |antonyms: None|noun

amniotic:/ˌæmniˈɒtɪk/ | adj. |羊膜的|Relating to the amnios or the amniotic fluid. |Amniotic fluid cushions the fetus and protects it from injury. 羊水缓冲胎儿并保护其免受伤害。|synonyms: fetal, embryonic|antonyms: None|adjective
