noncongruent:/ˌnɒnkənˈɡruːənt/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈɡruːənt/| adj.|不全等的，不一致的|Not congruent; not having the same size and shape (in geometry) or not matching or agreeing (in other contexts).|The two triangles are noncongruent because their corresponding sides have different lengths.|Near synonyms: dissimilar, different, unequal, inconsistent, incompatible|Near antonyms: congruent, identical, similar, consistent, compatible|adjective

noncongruent:/ˌnɒnkənˈɡruːənt/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈɡruːənt/| n.|不全等|The state or quality of being noncongruent.|The noncongruence of the data sets led the researchers to question the validity of the experiment.|Near synonyms: disparity, difference, discrepancy, inconsistency, incompatibility|Near antonyms: congruence, identity, similarity, consistency, compatibility|noun
