circumscribe:/sərˈkəmˌskraɪb/
|v.|限制，约束；画…的外接圆|To draw a line around; to encircle; to restrict or confine something within limits.|The president's power is circumscribed by the constitution. 总统的权力受宪法限制。|
|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, constrain, delimit|
|反义词：expand, broaden, enlarge, extend, free|
|词性：动词|

circumscribing:/sərˈkəmˌskraɪbɪŋ/
|adj.|限制的，约束的|Acting to restrict or confine something within limits.|The circumscribing walls of the prison made escape impossible. 监狱四周高耸的围墙使得越狱不可能。|
|近义词：restricting, limiting, confining, constraining|
|反义词：expanding, broadening, enlarging, extending, freeing|
|词性：形容词（现在分词）|
