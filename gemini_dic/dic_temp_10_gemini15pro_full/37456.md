commissar:/ˈkɒmɪsɑːr/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪsɑːr/ (US)| n. | 政委；委员；专员 | An official of the Communist Party, especially in the former Soviet Union, responsible for political education and organization.  |  Example: The commissar addressed the troops before the battle.  政委在战斗前向部队讲话。

Example:  He was appointed commissar for foreign affairs. 他被任命为外交事务委员。 | Synonym:  official, commissioner, agent, delegate, representative | Antonym:  civilian (in some contexts) | noun

commissars:/ˈkɒmɪsɑːrz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪsɑːrz/ (US)| n. | 政委们；委员们；专员们 | Plural form of commissar. | Example: The commissars oversaw the distribution of supplies.  政委们监督物资的分配。

Example: Several commissars were present at the meeting.  几位委员出席了会议。 | Synonym: officials, commissioners, agents, delegates, representatives | Antonym: civilians (in some contexts) | noun
