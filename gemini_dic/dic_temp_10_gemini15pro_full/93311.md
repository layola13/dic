ill-fated:/ˌɪlˈfeɪtɪd/
adjective
不幸的；注定要失败的
destined to fail or have bad luck
The ill-fated expedition ended in tragedy.  这支不幸的探险队以悲剧告终。
The ill-fated ship sank on its maiden voyage.  这艘命运多舛的船在其处女航中沉没了。
近义词:doomed, unfortunate, unlucky, cursed
反义词:fortunate, lucky, blessed
词性:adjective

词源:
From ill + fated.  Ill meaning "bad" and fated meaning "destined".  Essentially meaning "destined for bad things".
