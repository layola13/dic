colonize: /ˈkɑːlənaɪz/ (US) /ˈkɒlənaɪz/ (UK)| v. |殖民，拓殖；(动植物) 移生，繁衍| To establish a colony or colonies in (a territory). To settle in and establish political control over (an area). (of animals or plants) To establish itself or themselves in an area.|  European powers colonized much of Africa in the 19th century. 欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了非洲的大部分地区。

The bees colonized the hollow tree. 蜜蜂在这棵空心树里筑巢。|近义词：settle, populate, inhabit|反义词：abandon, evacuate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *colonizare, from Latin colōnus (“farmer, settler, planter”).
