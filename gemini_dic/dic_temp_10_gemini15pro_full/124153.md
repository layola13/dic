mortise: /ˈmɔːrtɪs/ (US) /ˈmɔːtɪs/ (UK)| n. vt.|榫眼，榫槽；开榫眼，凿榫眼|A hole or recess cut into a piece of timber, into which a tenon is inserted to form a joint.|The carpenter carefully cut the mortises for the chair legs. 木匠仔细地为椅腿凿了榫眼。|
近义词: slot, hole, recess, socket|反义词: tenon|词性: noun, verb

mortises: /ˈmɔːrtɪsɪz/ (US) /ˈmɔːtɪsɪz/ (UK)| n.  vt.|榫眼，榫槽（复数）；开榫眼，凿榫眼（复数）|Plural form of mortise, referring to multiple holes or recesses cut into timber for joining with tenons.|He precisely measured and cut the mortises in the frame. 他精确地测量并在框架上凿了榫眼。|近义词: slots, holes, recesses, sockets|反义词: tenons|词性: noun, verb
