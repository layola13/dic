credulousnesses:/krɪˈdʒuːləsnɪsɪz/
|n.|轻信，易受骗|The state of being credulous; willingness to believe or trust too readily, especially without sufficient evidence; gullibility.|His credulousnesses led him to fall for the scam. 他的轻信让他掉进了骗局。|近义词：gullibility, naivety, ingenuousness|反义词：skepticism, cynicism, distrust|词性:名词

credulous:/ˈkrɛdʒələs/
|adj.|轻信的，易受骗的|Having or showing too great a readiness to believe things.|He is a credulous person who believes everything he hears. 他是一个轻信的人，听到什么都信。|近义词：gullible, naive, trusting|反义词：skeptical, cynical, distrustful|词性:形容词
