executor:/ˈɛksɪˌkjuːtər/ (US), /ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|执行者，遗嘱执行人|A person or institution appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will.|The executor of the will was responsible for distributing the assets. 遗嘱执行人负责分配遗产。|近义词:administrator, trustee|反义词:heir, beneficiary|noun

executor:/ˈɛksɪˌkjuːtər/ (US), /ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|执行者，实行者|A person who carries out a plan, order, or course of action.|The CEO is the executor of the company's strategy. 首席执行官是公司战略的执行者。|近义词:implementer, enforcer|反义词:planner, strategist|noun
