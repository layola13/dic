effumability:/ˌɛfjuːməˈbɪləti/
|n.|挥发性，蒸发性|The quality of being able to evaporate or disperse as vapor.|The effumability of certain perfumes makes them ideal for aromatherapy.  某些香水的挥发性使其成为芳香疗法的理想选择。|volatility, evaporability|non-volatility, non-evaporability|noun
Etymology:
From Late Latin *effumabilitas, from Latin effumabilis "capable of being evaporated," from effumare "to evaporate," from ex- "out" + fumare "to smoke," from fumus "smoke."
