muhammadanism:/muːˈhæmədənɪzəm/
|n.|
|伊斯兰教 (过时且冒犯)|
|An outdated and offensive term for Islam, implying a focus on Muhammad rather than God.  It centers on Muhammad as the central figure, similar to how Christianity centers on Christ, which is inaccurate to Islamic belief.|
