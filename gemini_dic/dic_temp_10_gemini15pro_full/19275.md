biannually: /baɪˈænjuəli/
adverb
一年两次地；每半年一次地
Occurring twice a year; half-yearly.
The magazine is published biannually, in June and December. 这本杂志一年出版两次，分别在六月和十二月。
The committee meets biannually to discuss progress. 委员会每半年开一次会讨论进展情况。
近义词: semiannually, twice yearly
反义词: annually, yearly
词性: adverb
