diapery:/ˈdaɪpəri/ (US) /ˈdaɪpəri/ (UK)| adj.|尿布的；像尿布的；幼稚的；琐碎的|Relating to diapers; resembling a diaper; childish; trivial. Diapery originally referred to patterned or ornamented cloth, later specifically to diaper cloth.  It can be used figuratively to describe something trivial or insignificant, like a child's concerns.|The antique doll was dressed in diapery.|近义词:inane, trifling, puerile, diaper-like|反义词:mature, significant, important|词性:adjective
