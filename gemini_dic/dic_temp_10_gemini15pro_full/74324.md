fraggers:/ˈfræɡərz/
noun
碎裂手榴弹兵; 在电子游戏中击杀对手的人
Players who frequently kill opponents, especially with fragmentation grenades, in video games.
He's one of the top fraggers on the team. 他是队里最厉害的击杀者之一。
synonyms: killers, slayers, destroyers
antonyms: pacifists
n.
