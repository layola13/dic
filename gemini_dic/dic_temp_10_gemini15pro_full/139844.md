octopuses:/ˈɒktəpʊsɪz/ (UK), /ˈɑːktəpʊsɪz/ (US)| n.|章鱼|A sea creature with a soft body, eight arms, and large eyes. It can change color and squirt ink.|例句：Octopuses are highly intelligent invertebrates. 章鱼是高智商的无脊椎动物。

例句：The octopuses camouflaged themselves against the coral. 这些章鱼在珊瑚中伪装自己。|近义词：cephalopod|反义词:None|词性:noun

octopodes:/ɒkˈtɒpədiːz/| n.|章鱼|A less common plural form of octopus, derived from Greek.|例句：Some scientists prefer the term octopodes. 一些科学家更喜欢用octopod这个词。
