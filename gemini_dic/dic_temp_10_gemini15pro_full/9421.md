It often emphasizes intuition, emotion, or faith. | The philosopher argued against the antirationalisms of the current political climate, emphasizing the need for logic and evidence-based decision-making.  His book explored the rise of antirationalisms in various cultures throughout history. | 近义词: irrationalism, obscurantism | 反义词: rationalism, empiricism | 词性: 名词
