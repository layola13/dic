chapeless:/ˈtʃæpəllɪs/
adjective
无礼拜堂的；没有小教堂的
Lacking a chapel.  A building or institution without a chapel.
The chapeless school held assemblies in the gymnasium.  这所没有礼拜堂的学校在体育馆举行集会。
The new community center is chapeless, relying on nearby churches for religious services.  新的社区中心没有礼拜堂，依靠附近的教堂进行宗教服务。
Near synonyms:  chapel-less, without a chapel
Antonyms:  with a chapel, having a chapel
词性: adjective
