forecourt: /ˈfɔːrkɔːrt/ (UK), /ˈfɔːrkɔːrt/ (US)
n.
前院，广场
An open area in front of a large building or petrol station.
The hotel has a large forecourt where guests can park their cars.  酒店有一个很大的前院，客人可以把车停在那里。
The petrol station's forecourt was crowded with cars. 加油站的前院挤满了汽车。

近义词: courtyard, plaza, square
反义词: backyard, backcourt

词性: noun

词源:  From 'fore' (meaning 'in front of') + 'court' (meaning an enclosed space).  来源于 'fore'（意为“在...前面”）+ 'court'（意为封闭的空间）。
