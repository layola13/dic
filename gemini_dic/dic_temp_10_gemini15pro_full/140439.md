oklahoma:/ˌoʊkləˈhoʊmə/ | n. |俄克拉荷马州|A state in the south-central United States. It is the 20th largest state by area and the 28th most populous.  Its capital and largest city is Oklahoma City.|Oklahoma is known for its Native American heritage. 俄克拉荷马州以其美洲原住民遗产而闻名。| Oklahoma City is the capital of Oklahoma. 俄克拉荷马城是俄克拉荷马州的首府。 | 近义词: (none - it's a proper noun) | 反义词: (none - it's a proper noun) | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From the Choctaw words *okla* meaning "people" and *humma* meaning "red".
