hollywoodite:/ˈhɑːliˌwʊdaɪt/ |n.|好莱坞石，硅镁石|A rare mineral consisting of nickel silicide (Ni3Si).  It is found in meteorites and has a metallic luster. | This meteorite contained traces of rare minerals like hollywoodite. 这块陨石中含有微量的稀有矿物，例如好莱坞石。 | 近义词:  (in the context of being a nickel silicide mineral)  perryite, awaruite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
