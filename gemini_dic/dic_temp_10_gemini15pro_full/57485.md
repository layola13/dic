drag-anchor:/ˈdræɡ ˈæŋkər/| n.| 拖拽锚点 |A point or handle on a graphical user interface that allows a user to drag and resize or reposition an element by clicking and holding the mouse button.| Example: Click and drag the drag-anchor to resize the window. 点击并拖动拖拽锚点来调整窗口大小。 | Example: The drag-anchor allows you to move the image freely on the canvas.  拖拽锚点允许你在画布上自由移动图像。 | Synonym: handle, resize handle, control point | Antonym: N/A | noun

drag:/dræɡ/| n. v.| 拖, 拉, 阻力|1. (n.) the action of pulling something along the ground.  例句：The drag of the heavy suitcase made her tired.  沉重的行李箱拖着让她很累。 2. (v.) to pull something along the ground. 例句：He dragged the chair across the room. 他把椅子拖过房间。| Synonym: pull, haul, tow | Antonym: push, shove | noun, verb

anchor:/ˈæŋkər/| n. v.| 锚, 锚定|1. (n.) a heavy object attached to a ship by a chain or rope and used to moor the ship to the seabed. 例句：The ship dropped anchor in the harbor.  船在港口抛锚。 2. (v.) to moor a ship with an anchor. 例句：They anchored the boat near the shore. 他们在岸边抛锚停船。 | Synonym: moor, secure, fasten | Antonym: release, unmoor | noun, verb
