overillustratively:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlʌstrətɪvli/
| adv.| 过度图解地，过分举例说明地|  Explaining or depicting something with an excessive amount of illustrations or examples, to the point of being unnecessary or redundant.  It combines "over-" (meaning "excessively") with "illustrative" (meaning "serving to illustrate") and the adverbial suffix "-ly."|  The author overillustratively explained the concept, using far too many diagrams and examples.| 近义词: excessively, redundantly, profusely| 反义词: concisely, succinctly, briefly| 词性: 副词
