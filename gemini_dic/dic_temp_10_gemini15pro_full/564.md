abortions: /əˈbɔːrʃənz/
n.
人工流产，堕胎
The termination of a pregnancy before the fetus is viable.
The clinic provides counseling and abortions.  这家诊所提供咨询和堕胎服务。

The debate about abortions continues to polarize the nation. 关于堕胎的争论继续使国家两极分化。

近义词: terminations
反义词: births, deliveries
词性: 名词(plural)

词源: From the Latin word "abortus," meaning "miscarriage" or "premature birth," ultimately derived from "aboriri," meaning "to miscarry," from "ab-" (away from) + "oriri" (to rise, to be born).
