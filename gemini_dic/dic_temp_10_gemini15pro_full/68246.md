fairytale:/ˈfeəriˌteɪl/ (US) /ˈfɛːriˌteɪl/ (UK)| n. adj.|童话故事；童话般的；不真实的|A story, often intended for children, that involves imaginary creatures and magical events; unreal or magical, like a fairytale. |例句：Cinderella is a classic fairytale. 灰姑娘是一个经典的童话故事。|近义词：story, fable, myth, legend, fantasy|反义词：reality, truth, fact|词性:名词, 形容词

Etymology: From *fairy tale*, a compound of *fairy* and *tale*.  *Fairy* comes from Old French *faerie*, ultimately from Latin *fata* (fate). *Tale* comes from Old English *talu* (speech, story).
