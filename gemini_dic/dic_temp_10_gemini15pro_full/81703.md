greenhorn:/ˈɡriːnhɔːrn/ | n. |新手，生手；容易受骗的人|A greenhorn is a person who is new to a particular activity or situation and is therefore inexperienced.|  The city was full of greenhorns fresh off the bus.  这座城市到处都是刚下车的新手。|  He was a greenhorn in the world of finance. 他是金融界的新手。| 近义词: novice, beginner, rookie, tenderfoot | 反义词: expert, veteran, professional | 词性: 名词
