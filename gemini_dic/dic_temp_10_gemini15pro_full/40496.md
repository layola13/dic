contriturate: /kənˈtrɪtʃəreɪt/ /kənˈtrɪtjʊreɪt/
verb
研磨，磨碎，Triturate
To rub or grind into a fine powder or pulp.
The pharmacist contriturated the tablets into a fine powder for easier ingestion. 药剂师将药片研磨成细粉以便于吞服。

The seeds were contriturated into a paste. 种子被磨成糊状。


近义词: grind, pulverize, crush, comminute
反义词:  coalesce, combine, fuse

词性: verb

词源: From Latin contrītus, past participle of conterere (“to grind, bruise”), from com- + terere (“to rub, grind”).
