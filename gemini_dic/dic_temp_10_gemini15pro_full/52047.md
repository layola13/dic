diallage:/ˈdaɪəlɪdʒ/| n. |异剥辉石，透辉石|A kind of pyroxene mineral that exhibits a shimmering or schiller effect due to its lamellar structure.|The geologist identified the sample as diallage due to its distinctive schiller.  地质学家根据其独特的闪光效应将样品鉴定为透辉石。

Diallage is a common component of gabbro. 透辉石是辉长岩的常见成分。|近义词：pyroxene, augite |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diallage*, from Ancient Greek *διάλλαξις (diállaxis, “interchange, difference”)*, from *διαλλάσσω (diallássō, “I exchange, I differ”)*.  Ultimately from *διά (diá, “through, across”)* + *ἀλλάσσω (allássso, “I change”)*.
