foreadvise:/fɔːrədˈvaɪz/
verb
预先通知，事先告知
To inform or notify someone in advance of something that is going to happen.
The shipping company foreadvised us of the delay.  运输公司事先通知了我们延误的情况。
We were foreadvised of the potential risks. 我们被预先告知了潜在的风险。
近义词: prewarn, notify, inform
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conceal" or "withhold information")
词性: verb

Etymology:
From "fore-" (before) + "advise" (to give counsel or information to)
