murphy:/ˈmɜːrfi/ (US) /ˈmɜːfi/ (UK)| n.|墨菲，墨菲床，意想不到的问题；（Murphy's law 墨菲定律）|A Murphy bed is a bed that can be folded up against the wall.  Murphy's Law states that anything that can go wrong will go wrong.|例句：We pulled the Murphy bed down from the wall. 我们把墨菲床从墙上拉下来。

例句：If anything can go wrong, it will - that's Murphy's Law! 如果有什么可能出错的事情，它就会出错——这就是墨菲定律！|近义词：problem, glitch, snag, hitch |反义词:solution, fix|noun
