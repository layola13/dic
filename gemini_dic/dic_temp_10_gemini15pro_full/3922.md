agriculturists:/ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
农学家；农业工作者
People who work in agriculture, either as farmers or as scientists or specialists in particular areas.
The government is providing subsidies to agriculturists to encourage them to adopt sustainable farming practices. 政府正在向农业工作者提供补贴，以鼓励他们采用可持续的农业实践。
farmers, growers, cultivators
industrialists

词性: 名词(plural)
