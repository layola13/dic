initialled:/ɪˈnɪʃəld/ | adj. | 已签名的，已首字母缩写的 | Bearing or marked with initials. | The initialled document was placed in the safe. 已签名的文件被放在保险箱里。 |  The contract was initialled by both parties.  合同由双方签了姓名首字母。 | signed, autographed | unsigned | adjective

initialled:/ɪˈnɪʃəld/ | v. | 签姓名的首字母 | Mark or sign with initials. |  He initialled the form to show his approval.  他在表格上签了姓名首字母以示同意。 | She initialled each page of the contract. 她在合同的每一页上都签了姓名首字母。 | sign, autograph |  - | verb


Etymology:  From the noun "initial" + "-ed."  The noun "initial" comes from the Late Latin "initialis," meaning "of the beginning," from "initium" "beginning."
