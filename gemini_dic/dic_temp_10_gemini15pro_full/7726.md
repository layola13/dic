anonymization:/əˌnɒnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |əˌnɑːnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|匿名化，匿名处理|The process of removing identifying information from data.  This allows data to be used and shared without compromising individual privacy.|Data anonymization is crucial for protecting user privacy.  数据匿名化对于保护用户隐私至关重要。|近义词：de-identification, pseudonymization|反义词: identification|noun
