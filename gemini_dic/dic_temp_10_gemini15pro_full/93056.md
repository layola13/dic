idolatrous:/aɪˈdɒlətrəs/| adj.|偶像崇拜的；盲目崇拜的|Characterized by excessive devotion to something or someone, treating them as an idol.|例句：Her idolatrous devotion to the pop star was bordering on obsession. 她对这位流行歌星的盲目崇拜近乎痴迷。|例句：The tribe's idolatrous practices involved elaborate rituals and sacrifices. 这个部落的偶像崇拜习俗包括精心设计的仪式和祭祀。|近义词：worshipping, adoring, revering|反义词：irreverent, impious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *idolatrosus*, from Latin *idolatra* (“idolater”), from Greek *εἰδωλολάτρης* (eidōlolátrēs, “idol-worshipper”), from *εἴδωλον* (eídōlon, “idol, image”) + *λάτρις* (látris, “worshipper”).
