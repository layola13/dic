featherlike:/ˈfeðərˌlaɪk/| adj.|轻如羽毛的，羽毛般的|Resembling a feather in lightness, softness, or delicacy.|Her featherlike touch barely registered on his skin. 她轻如羽毛的触摸几乎没有让他感觉到。|The dancer's featherlike movements captivated the audience.  舞者轻盈的舞姿吸引了观众。|近义词：light, airy, delicate, fluffy, downy|反义词：heavy, weighty, coarse|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From feather +‎ -like.  Feather comes from Old English *feþer, from Proto-Germanic *feþrą, from Proto-Indo-European *pet- (“to fly”).  -like is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
