artistry:/ˈɑːrtɪstri/| n. |艺术技巧；艺术才能|Skill and ability in art, especially in the visual arts.| Her artistry in pottery is evident in the intricate designs. 她的陶艺技巧体现在其复杂的图案设计中。|  The painting showed remarkable artistry. 这幅画展现了非凡的艺术技巧。 | synonyms: skill, talent, craft, mastery, technique, creativity, ingenuity, flair, aptitude | antonyms: incompetence, inability, clumsiness | noun
