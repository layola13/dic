defocuses:/diːˈfoʊkəsɪz/| v.|使散焦，使失焦|to cause (something, such as a lens or the eyes) to lose focus; to blur|The photographer deliberately defocused the background to highlight the subject. 摄影师故意虚化了背景以突出主体。|synonyms: blur, soften, unfocus|antonyms: focus, sharpen|verb
