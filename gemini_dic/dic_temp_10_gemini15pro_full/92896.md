例句：She was captivated by the idealness of the mountain scenery, a picture of tranquility and untouched beauty. 她被山间景色的完美所吸引，那是一幅宁静而原始的美丽图画。|近义词：perfection, flawlessness, impeccability|反义词：imperfection, deficiency, faultiness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ideal + -ness.  "ideal" comes from Late Latin ideālis "existing in idea," from Latin idea "idea, mental image" (see idea). The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, meaning "the state or quality of being".
