overtameness:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪmnəs/
n.|过度驯服，过分温顺|The overtameness of the animals made them vulnerable to predators. 这些动物过分温顺，使它们容易受到捕食者的攻击。|English Explanation:  The state of being excessively tame or docile, often to the point of being disadvantageous.|例句：The overtameness of the deer in the park made them easy targets for poachers. 公园里鹿的过度驯服使它们很容易成为偷猎者的目标。

例句：The dog's overtameness resulted in it being bullied by other dogs. 这只狗的过分温顺导致它被其他狗欺负。


近义词：docility, submissiveness, meekness
反义词：wildness, aggressiveness, ferocity
词性: noun
