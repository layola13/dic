hydranth:/ˈhaɪdrænθ/| n.|水螅体|An individual polyp of a hydroid or hydrozoan.  It is the feeding or nutritive zooid of the colony.|The hydranths of the Portuguese man-of-war are specialized for feeding.|Synonym: polyp|Antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially medusa - the free-swimming form of some cnidarians)|Noun
