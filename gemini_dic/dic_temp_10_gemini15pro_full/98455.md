inspiratory:/ɪnˈspaɪrətəri/ /ɪnˈspaɪrətɔːri/
adjective
吸气的
Of or relating to breathing in.
The inspiratory muscles contract to pull air into the lungs.  吸气肌收缩以将空气吸入肺部。
The doctor measured her inspiratory capacity. 医生测量了她的吸气量。
近义词:inhalation, breathing in
反义词:expiratory
词性:adjective
