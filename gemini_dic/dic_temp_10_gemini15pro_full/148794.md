overvaluably:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuəbli/| adv.| 过高地；过分贵重地 |Giving something a value that is too high; excessively.  |He overvaluably assessed the worth of the antique watch. 他过高地估计了那块古董表的价值。|例句：The house was overvaluably priced for its location and condition. 这房子就其位置和状况而言定价过高。|近义词：excessively, exorbitantly, unduly|反义词: undervaluably, cheaply|词性:副词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "too much") + value (noun) + -able (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs)
