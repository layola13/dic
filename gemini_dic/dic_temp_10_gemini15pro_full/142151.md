orion:/əˈraɪən/ /ɔːˈraɪən/ | n. |猎户座；奥赖温|A prominent constellation located on the celestial equator, easily recognizable by its three bright stars in a row, known as Orion's Belt.  It is named after a hunter in Greek mythology.|例句：Orion is one of the most recognizable constellations in the night sky. 猎户座是夜空中最容易辨认的星座之一。

例句：The constellation Orion is visible in both the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.  猎户座在南北半球都可以看到。|近义词：The Hunter (mythological figure)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
