mumbly:/ˈmʌmbli/ | adj. | 含糊不清的，咕哝的 | Speaking indistinctly; muttering.  Characterized by low, indistinct speech. |  He gave a mumbly reply, and I couldn't understand him. 他含糊地回答，我没听懂。 | Synonyms: indistinct, inarticulate, muttered, mumbled | Antonyms: clear, articulate, distinct | adjective

词源：
From Middle English *mombeln, frequentative of mommen (“to mumble”).  Related to Dutch mompelen (“to mumble”), German mummeln (“to mumble”).  Ultimately of imitative origin.
来源于中古英语*mombeln，是mommen（“咕哝”）的重复形式。 与荷兰语 mompelen（“咕哝”），德语 mummeln（“咕哝”）相关。最终是拟声词起源。
