emigrates: /ˈemɪɡreɪts/ | v. | 移民，移居国外 | To leave one's own country in order to settle permanently in another. |  Many people emigrated from Ireland to the United States during the potato famine.  许多人在马铃薯饥荒期间从爱尔兰移民到美国。| synonyms: migrate, relocate, resettle | antonyms: immigrate, return, repatriate | verb
