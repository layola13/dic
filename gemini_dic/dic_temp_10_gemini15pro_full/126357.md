myriametre:/ˌmɪriˈæmɪtər/ |n.| 万米; 一万公尺 |A unit of length equal to 10,000 meters or 10 kilometers. |The marathon is a little over 42 kilometers, which is a bit more than four myriametres. 马拉松略超过42公里，比4万米多一点。| Synonyms: myriameter, 10,000 meters, 10 kilometers | Antonyms: millimeter, centimeter| noun
