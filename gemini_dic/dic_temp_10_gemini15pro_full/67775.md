eyeleted:/ˈaɪlɪtɪd/
adjective
带孔眼的;有金属圈的
Having eyelets; pierced with eyelets.
Her eyeleted boots were perfect for hiking. 她那双带孔眼的靴子非常适合徒步旅行。
Synonyms: perforated, pierced
Antonyms: solid, unperforated
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From eyelet + -ed.  "Eyelet" itself comes from the Old French "oeillet" (little eye), diminutive of "oeil" (eye), from Latin "oculus" (eye).
