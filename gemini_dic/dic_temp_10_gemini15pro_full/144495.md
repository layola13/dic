outthwack:/ˌaʊtˈθwæk/
verb
彻底击败，痛打
To strike or hit harder than someone else.
He outthwacked his opponent in the final round of the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛的最后一轮彻底击败了对手。
She outthwacked the piñata until all the candy fell out. 她用力击打彩罐直到所有的糖果都掉出来。
Synonyms: thrash, trounce, clobber, wallop
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
