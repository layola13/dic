overidentify:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| v. | 过度认同，过度认同感| To identify excessively or too closely, especially with another person or group, so that one's own individuality is lost or obscured. |  She overidentified with the fictional characters in the novel and began to emulate their behavior. 她对小说中的虚构人物过度认同，并开始模仿他们的行为。

| | The therapist suggested that he might be overidentifying with his clients' problems. 治疗师建议他可能过度认同了他客户的问题。

| 近义词: empathize excessively, relate too strongly| 反义词: detach, distance oneself | verb
