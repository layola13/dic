anti-freudians:/ˌæntiˈfrɔɪdiənz/ | n. | 反弗洛伊德主义者；非弗洛伊德学派人士 |  Those who disagree with the theories and practices of Sigmund Freud, the founder of psychoanalysis.  They may offer alternative explanations for human behavior or challenge Freud's emphasis on sexuality and the unconscious. |  The anti-Freudians argued that his theories were too focused on childhood experiences.  反弗洛伊德主义者认为他的理论过于关注童年经历。

Many anti-Freudians emphasize social and cultural factors over innate drives. 许多反弗洛伊德主义者强调社会和文化因素，而不是先天驱动力。 | 近义词: critics of Freud, dissenters from Freudianism | 反义词: Freudians, proponents of Freudianism | 词性: 名词(plural)
