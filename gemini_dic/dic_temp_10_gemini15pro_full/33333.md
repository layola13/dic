chugger: /ˈtʃʌɡər/ | n. | 街头募捐者；慈善劝募员 | A person who approaches passersby in the street and asks them to make regular donations to a charity. |  Example 1: The chugger wouldn't leave me alone until I agreed to donate a small amount each month. 街头募捐员直到我同意每个月捐一小笔钱才肯离开我。

Example 2:  I try to avoid eye contact with chuggers when I'm walking through the city center. 我在市中心走的时候尽量避免与慈善劝募员有眼神接触。 | 近义词: fundraiser, solicitor | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "donor") | 词性: noun

词源:  "Chugger" is a portmanteau word, blending "charity" and "mugger." This reflects the sometimes perceived aggressive tactics of some street fundraisers.  “Chugger”是“charity”和“mugger”的合成词，反映了人们有时认为街头募捐者咄咄逼人的策略。
