non-intrusion:/nɒn-ɪnˈtruːʒən/ | n. | 非侵入，非入侵 |  The act of not intruding or interfering; avoidance of intrusion.  Absence of intrusion, especially with regard to privacy or personal space. |  The software update boasts non-intrusion, meaning it won't interrupt your work. 该软件更新以非侵入性为卖点，意味着它不会中断你的工作。 |  non-interference, discretion, unobtrusiveness | intrusion, interference, intervention | noun

non-intrusive:/nɒn-ɪnˈtruːsɪv/ | adj. | 非侵入式的，非入侵式的 | Not intrusive; not causing disruption or annoyance. Designed to minimize disruption or interference. | The security cameras are non-intrusive, blending seamlessly with the environment. 这些安全摄像头是非侵入式的，与环境无缝融合。 | unobtrusive, discreet, subtle | intrusive, invasive, disruptive | adjective
