misrecital:/ˌmɪsrɪˈsaɪtl/
noun
错误的叙述；误述
an incorrect statement of facts; a misquote
The lawyer pointed out a misrecital of the facts in the witness's testimony. 律师指出了证人证词中对事实的误述。

近义词: misstatement, misquotation, falsification, distortion
反义词: accurate account, correct statement, truthful representation
词性: n.
