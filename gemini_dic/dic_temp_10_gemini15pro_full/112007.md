lonest:/ˈloʊnɪst/| adj.|最孤独的|superlative adjective of lonely; most lonely.  Experiencing the greatest degree of loneliness.|例句：She felt lonest during the holidays when everyone else was with their families.  她假期里感到最孤独，因为其他人都和家人在一起。

例句：He endured the lonest night of his life after hearing the devastating news.  听到这毁灭性的消息后，他熬过了人生中最孤独的夜晚。|近义词：loneliest, most isolated, most secluded|反义词：least lonely, most sociable, most gregarious|词性:形容词


词源: From Middle English *lonest, from Old English lēonest, superlative of lēone (“lonely”). Equivalent to lone +‎ -est.
