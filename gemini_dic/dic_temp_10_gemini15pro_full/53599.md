discalceate: /dɪsˈkælsiˌeɪt/
verb
脱鞋；赤脚

The children discalceated and ran through the grass. 孩子们脱掉鞋子，在草地上奔跑。
近义词: barefoot, unshod, shoeless
反义词: shod, calceate
