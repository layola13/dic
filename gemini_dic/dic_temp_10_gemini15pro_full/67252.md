expunges:/ɪkˈspʌndʒɪz/
|v.|消除，删除，抹去|Third person singular present tense form of expunge, meaning to erase or remove completely (something unwanted or unpleasant).|The judge expunges the conviction from his record. 法官从他的记录中删除了定罪记录。|Synonyms: erase, delete, obliterate, remove, efface|Antonyms:  preserve, retain, record, maintain, keep
|verb
