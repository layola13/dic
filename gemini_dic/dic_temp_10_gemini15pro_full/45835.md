customizer:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzər/| n. |定制者，自定义者|A person or thing that customizes something.  Someone who modifies something to suit particular individual needs.|例句：The website offers a powerful customizer that allows users to personalize their experience.  这个网站提供了一个强大的定制器，允许用户个性化他们的体验。

例句：She works as a shoe customizer, creating unique designs for her clients. 她是一名鞋子定制师，为客户设计独特的款式。|近义词：modifier, personalizer, adapter, tailor|反义词: standardizer |词性: noun
