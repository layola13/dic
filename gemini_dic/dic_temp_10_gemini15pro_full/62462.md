endosarc:/ˈɛndoʊsɑːrk/ | n. | 内胚层；内骨骼 | The inner, soft portion of the cytoplasm of an amoeba or other rhizopod.  The inner germ layer of an embryo, from which are derived the lining of the digestive tract and its associated organs.  A skeletal structure formed within the tissues of organisms, such as the spicules of sponges. | The amoeba extends its pseudopods, which are formed from the endosarc. 变形虫伸出由内质形成的伪足。| 近义词:endoplasm | 反义词:ectosarc | 词性:noun

endoskeleton:/ˌɛndoʊˈskɛlɪtn/ | n. | 内骨骼 | An internal skeleton, such as the bony or cartilaginous skeleton of vertebrates. |  Humans have an endoskeleton made of bone. 人类的内骨骼由骨骼构成。The endoskeleton provides support and protection for internal organs. 内骨骼为内部器官提供支撑和保护。| 近义词:internal skeleton | 反义词:exoskeleton | 词性:noun
