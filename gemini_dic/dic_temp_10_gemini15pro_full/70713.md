financing:/ˈfaɪnænsɪŋ/

financing:/faɪˈnænsɪŋ/
n.|提供资金；贷款|The process of giving money for something.|They offer financing options for car purchases. 他们提供购车融资方案。|synonyms: lending, credit, loaning|antonyms: borrowing, debt collection|noun
