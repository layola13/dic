gamopetalous:/ˌɡæmoʊˈpɛtələs/| adj.|合瓣的|Having petals united or joined, at least at the base.|Example: The morning glory flower is gamopetalous. 牵牛花是合瓣的。|Example: Gamopetalous flowers are characteristic of many plant families. 合瓣花是许多植物科的特征。|Synonym: sympetalous|Antonym: polypetalous|Word type: adjective
