duettino:/ˌdwɛˈtiːnoʊ/ /ˌdjuːɛˈtiːnoʊ/
noun
二重唱，二重奏
A short duet, typically one in an opera or other dramatic work.
The opera features a charming duettino between the two lovers.  这部歌剧以两位恋人之间迷人的二重唱为特色。
synonyms: duet, duo
antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
