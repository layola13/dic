chartomancy:/ˈkɑːrtəmænsi/ | n. | 纸牌占卜 | Divination of the future using playing cards. | She practiced chartomancy to predict the outcome of the election. 她用纸牌占卜来预测选举结果。|  He relied on chartomancy to guide his decisions. 他依靠纸牌占卜来指导他的决定。 | 近义词: cartomancy, fortune-telling | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French "cartomancie", from "carte" (card) + "-mancy" (divination).  Ultimately from Latin "charta" (paper, card) and Greek "manteia" (divination).
