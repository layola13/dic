carelessly:/ˈkeər.ləs.li/ /ˈker.ləs.li/ | adv. |粗心地，漫不经心地 |In a way that shows a lack of attention or thought. | He carelessly left the door unlocked. 他粗心地没锁门。| negligently, thoughtlessly, heedlessly | carefully, cautiously, meticulously | adverb

Etymology:
From *careless* + *-ly*. *Careless* comes from *care* + *-less*.  *Care* has Proto-Germanic roots and relates to anxiety and concern. *-less* is a suffix meaning "without". *-ly* is a suffix forming adverbs.
