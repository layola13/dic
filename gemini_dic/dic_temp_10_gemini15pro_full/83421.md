habilimented:/həˈbɪlɪmɛntɪd/
adjective
穿着衣服的，装扮的
dressed or clothed, especially in a particular or noticeable way.
例句：The king was habilimented in a magnificent purple robe. 国王穿着一件华丽的紫色长袍。

例句：The actors were habilimented in elaborate costumes for the play. 演员们为这出戏穿上了精致的戏服。

近义词：attired, clad, arrayed, garbed
反义词：naked, unclothed, undressed
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *habillement*, from habiller "to dress," from Old French *habiller*, from Frankish *habili*, from Proto-Germanic *habiliþō*, meaning "clothing, dress." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- "to seize, grasp."
