agonic:/əˈɡɒnɪk/ /əˈɡɑːnɪk/ | adj. |无磁偏角的，无方位变化的|Relating to or situated on an agonic line (a line on a map connecting points of zero magnetic declination).  An agonic line is a line on the Earth's surface along which the magnetic declination is zero.  In other words, the magnetic north and true north are aligned. |The agonic line passes through several countries. 这条无磁偏角线穿过好几个国家。|Synonym: non-magnetic, declinationless |Antonym: magnetic, deviating| adjective

agony:/ˈæɡəni/ | n. |极大的痛苦，苦恼|Extreme physical or mental suffering.|She was in agony after breaking her leg.  她摔断腿后非常痛苦。|Synonym: pain, suffering, torment, anguish |Antonym: comfort, relief, pleasure, joy| noun
