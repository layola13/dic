ferforth:/ˈfɜːrfɔːrθ/ |adv.|向前地，进展地|To a significant extent or degree; considerably; greatly.  It also means onward in time or order; further.|He set forth the reasons for his resignation. 他阐述了辞职的理由。|近义词:forward, onward, forth|反义词:backward|词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *ferforth, equivalent to fer (far) + forth.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *furþiz (“fourth”). Cognate with Old Frisian ferth, ferde (“forth, forward”), Old Saxon ferdi, forth, Old High German ferdi, ferti, ford, Old Norse fjǫrði (“fourth”).
