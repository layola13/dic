diverticular:/ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjələr/| adj.|憩室的|Relating to or affected by diverticula (small pouches that can form in the lining of the digestive system).|He suffers from diverticular disease. 他患有憩室病。|Synonym: pouch-like|Antonym: smooth|adjective

diverticulum:/ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjələm/| n.|憩室|A small pouch or sac that forms in the lining of a hollow organ, such as the colon.|The doctor found a diverticulum in his colon. 医生在他的结肠中发现了一个憩室。|Synonym: pouch, sac|Antonym: none|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diverticulum*  "by-road, detour," from *devertere*  "to turn aside,"  from *de-* "away" + *vertere* "to turn."
