overglutted: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌtɪd/ | adj. | 过饱和的，过剩的 | Excessively full; saturated; having a supply that greatly exceeds demand. | The market is overglutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。| The housing market is currently overglutted, leading to falling prices. 目前房地产市场供过于求，导致价格下跌。 | 近义词: saturated, glutted, overflowing, surfeited | 反义词: deficient, lacking, scarce | 词性: 形容词

词源: From over + glutted (past participle of glut).  "Glut" comes from the Old French "glotir" (to swallow) and ultimately from the Latin "glutire" (to swallow).  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning, indicating an excessive amount.
