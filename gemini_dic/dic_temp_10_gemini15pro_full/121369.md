misguidedly:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪdɪdli/
| adv. |错误地；被误导地|In a misguided attempt to help, he actually made the situation worse. 出于好心办坏事，他实际上使情况变得更糟了。|例句：He misguidedly believed that he could solve the problem on his own. 他错误地认为自己可以独自解决这个问题。

例句：She misguidedly invested all her money in a failing company. 她错误地把所有的钱都投资到了一家快要倒闭的公司。|近义词：erroneously, wrongly, foolishly, unwisely|反义词：wisely, prudently, correctly, appropriately|词性:副词
词源: From mis- + guided + -ly.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and French origin meaning "wrongly" or "badly". "Guided" comes from the verb "guide", which ultimately derives from the Old French "guider".  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
