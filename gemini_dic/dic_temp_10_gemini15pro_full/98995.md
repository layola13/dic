interacademic:/ˌɪntərækəˈdɛmɪk/
adjective
校际的；学院间的
Existing or occurring between different academic institutions.
例句：The interacademic sports competition fostered friendly rivalry between the schools.  校际运动比赛促进了学校之间的友好竞争。

例句：Interacademic exchange programs allow students to experience different learning environments. 校际交流项目允许学生体验不同的学习环境。
近义词：interscholastic, intercollegiate
反义词：intra-academy, within-academy
词性：adjective
