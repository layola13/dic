crucified: /ˈkruːsɪfaɪd/ | v. | 钉在十字架上；折磨；严厉批评 |  To put to death by nailing or binding the hands and feet to a cross. To persecute, torment, or criticize severely. | Jesus was crucified. 耶稣被钉死在十字架上。|  The media crucified the politician for his controversial remarks. 媒体严厉批评了这位政客的有争议的言论。 | synonyms: executed, killed, tortured, condemned, castigated | antonyms: praised, lauded, commended | verb
