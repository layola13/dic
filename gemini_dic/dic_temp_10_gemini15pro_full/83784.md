hairiest:/ˈhɛəriɪst/| adj.|毛最多的，毛茸茸的|Covered with the most hair; most hairy.|例句：That's the hairiest dog I've ever seen. 那是我见过的毛最多的狗。|近义词：furriest, shaggiest|反义词: smoothest, baldest|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Old English *hǣrig, from *hǣr (hair). The "-iest" suffix is a superlative ending.
