mispurchasing:/ˌmɪsˈpɜːrtʃəsɪŋ/
n.|错误采购，采购失误|The act of buying something incorrectly, either the wrong item, the wrong quantity, or at the wrong price.| Due to a mispurchasing error, we received 500 blue pens instead of the 50 black pens we ordered. 由于采购失误，我们收到了500支蓝色钢笔，而不是我们订购的50支黑色钢笔。|misbuying, incorrect purchasing|correct purchasing|noun
