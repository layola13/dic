nonpublications:/ˌnɑːnpʌblɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
未出版物；非出版物
Materials that are not formally published, such as manuscripts, reports, or working papers.
The library archives contain many nonpublications, including letters and diaries.  图书馆的档案包含许多未出版物，包括信件和日记。
Near synonyms: unpublished works, unpublished materials, preprints
Antonyms: publications, published works
词性: 名词
