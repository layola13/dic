farad:/ˈfæræd/ | n. | 法拉 | The standard unit of capacitance, equal to the capacitance of a capacitor in which one coulomb of charge causes a potential difference of one volt. | The capacitor has a capacitance of 10 farads.  这个电容器的电容为10法拉。| synonyms:  none (unit of measurement) | antonyms: none (unit of measurement) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of Michael Faraday (1791-1867), English physicist and chemist.
