editorializes:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪzɪz/
| v.|发表社论；（在新闻报道中）发表个人意见|expresses personal opinions, often in a critical way, especially in a news report or broadcast.|The reporter editorialized about the candidate's qualifications. 这位记者就候选人的资格发表了个人意见。|comments, opines, offers opinions|reports objectively, presents facts|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From editorial + -ize.  "Editorial" comes from the Latin "editor," meaning "publisher." The suffix "-ize" means "to make or cause to be."  Therefore, "editorialize" means to act as an editor, specifically by offering opinions.
