hippy:/ˈhɪpi/| n.|嬉皮士|A person, typically young, associated with a subculture involving a rejection of conventional values and the taking of hallucinogenic drugs.|She dressed like a hippy, with long flowing skirts and flowers in her hair. 她穿着像个嬉皮士，穿着飘逸的长裙，头发上插着花。|近义词：bohemian, flower child|反义词:conformist, traditionalist|词性:noun
hippie:/ˈhɪpi/| n.|嬉皮士|Same as hippy.|He considered himself a hippie and embraced the counterculture movement. 他认为自己是一个嬉皮士，并接受了反文化运动。|近义词：bohemian, flower child|反义词:conformist, traditionalist|词性:noun
