couplets:/ˈkʌplɪts/
n.
对句；对联；双韵诗
A pair of successive lines of verse, especially ones that rhyme and have the same meter.
The poem was written in rhyming couplets. 这首诗是用押韵的对句写成的。
verses, pairs, distichs
singletons, monostichs
noun
