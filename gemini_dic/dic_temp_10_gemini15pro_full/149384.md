pacify: /ˈpæsɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈpæsɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v. |平息，抚慰，使平静|To calm someone who is angry or upset; to soothe or appease.| The mother pacified her crying baby with a lullaby. 母亲用摇篮曲安抚哭泣的婴儿。| The government tried to pacify the protesters with promises of reform. 政府试图以改革的承诺来安抚抗议者。| 近义词: soothe, calm, appease, mollify, placate | 反义词: agitate, provoke, incite, inflame | 词性: 动词
词源: From Latin *pacificare*, from pax, pac- "peace" + -ficare "-fy".
