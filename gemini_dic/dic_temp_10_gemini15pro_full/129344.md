noisomely:/ˈnɔɪsəmli/ | adv. | 恶臭地；令人厌恶地；有害地 |  In a noisome or offensive manner; offensively; disgustingly. | The garbage rotted noisomely in the summer heat. 夏天炎热，垃圾腐烂发臭。 | The factory noisomely discharged pollutants into the river. 工厂向河里排放污染物，发出恶臭。| 近义词: offensively, disgustingly, foul, objectionably | 反义词: pleasantly, agreeably, fragrantly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *noisomli, from noisom +‎ -ly. Equivalent to noisome +‎ -ly.
