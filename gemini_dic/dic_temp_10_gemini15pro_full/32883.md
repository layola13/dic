chondritic:/kɒnˈdrɪtɪk/
adjective
球粒状的；由球粒陨石组成的
Relating to or consisting of chondrites, stony meteorites containing small spherical bodies called chondrules.
例句：Chondritic meteorites are the most common type of meteorite found on Earth.  球粒陨石是地球上发现的最常见的陨石类型。

例句：Scientists study chondritic meteorites to learn about the early solar system. 科学家研究球粒陨石以了解早期太阳系。
近义词：meteoritic 陨石的
反义词: achondritic 无球粒的
词性: adjective
