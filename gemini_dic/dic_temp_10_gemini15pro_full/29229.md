cass:/kæs/ | n. | 木薯；木薯粉；木薯块根 | Cassava, also called manioc, yuca, or macaxeira, is a woody shrub native to South America of the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae. Cassava is extensively cultivated as an annual crop in tropical and subtropical regions for its edible starchy tuberous root, a major source of carbohydrates. | Cassava is a staple food in many developing countries. 木薯是许多发展中国家的主食。| Cassava flour is used in baking. 木薯粉用于烘焙。 | synonyms: cassava, manioc, yuca, macaxeira | antonyms: N/A | noun
