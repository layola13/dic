goby:/ˈɡoʊbi/ (US), /ˈɡəʊbi/ (UK)| n. |虾虎鱼|A small, usually brightly coloured fish that lives in shallow coastal waters, often in rock pools or on coral reefs.  It is characterized by its fused pelvic fins, which form a suction cup-like disc. |The goby darted among the rocks.  虾虎鱼在岩石间飞快地游动。|近义词: darter, blenny|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish)|noun
