hanged-over:/ˌhæŋˈoʊvər/ (US), /ˌhʌŋˈəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|宿醉的，感到不适的|Experiencing the unpleasant aftereffects of excessive alcohol consumption, typically a headache, nausea, and fatigue.|例句：I'm too hungover to go to work today. 我宿醉太厉害了，今天不能去上班了。

例句：She felt hungover after last night's party.  昨晚派对后她感到宿醉。

近义词：drunk, intoxicated, under the weather, unwell|反义词:sober, well|词性:形容词
