discounsel:/dɪsˈkaʊnsəl/
词性|v.
中文翻译|劝阻；建议不要
英文解析|To advise against; to try to persuade someone not to do something.
例句|The lawyer discounseled his client from taking the stand.  律师劝他的客户不要出庭作证。

例句|I would discounsel anyone from investing in that company right now. 我会劝阻任何人现在投资那家公司。

近义词|advise against, dissuade, warn against
反义词|encourage, recommend, advocate
词源|From Middle English *disconseilen*, from Old French *desconseillier*, from *des-* (dis-) + *conseiller* (to counsel).
