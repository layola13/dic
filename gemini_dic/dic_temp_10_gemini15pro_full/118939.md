meteoritically:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɪtɪkli/| adv.|  像流星般迅速地；飞速地；突然而迅速地|Relating to or resembling a meteor in speed or sudden brilliance.  Describes something that rises or falls with the dramatic speed of a meteor, often used figuratively to describe rapid success or failure.|Her career rose meteoritically. 她的事业飞速发展。|Synonyms: rapidly, swiftly, suddenly, dramatically, spectacularly|Antonyms: gradually, slowly, steadily|adverb
