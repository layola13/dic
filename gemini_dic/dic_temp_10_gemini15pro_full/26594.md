cacajao:/ˌkækəˈdʒaʊ/| n. |秃猴，秃猴属|A type of New World monkey characterized by a short tail and a bald, brightly colored face.  They are found in the Amazon basin.|例句：The cacajao's bright red face is a striking feature.  秃猴鲜红色的脸是一个显著的特征。

例句：Cacajaos are primarily frugivores, meaning their diet consists mainly of fruit. 秃猴主要是食果动物，这意味着它们的饮食主要由水果组成。|近义词：uakari, saki (related monkey species)|反义词: N/A (No direct opposite for an animal species)|词性:noun
