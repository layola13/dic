|adj.|无效的;失效的;不起作用的|The machine became inoperative after years of use.  这台机器使用多年后失效了。|The contract is now inoperative due to a breach of terms.  由于违反条款，该合同现在无效。|近义词:ineffective, useless, invalid, defunct|反义词:effective, valid, functional, working|词性:形容词
