brushworks: /ˈbrʌʃwɜːrks/
noun
笔法；笔触；（尤指）绘画技巧
The skill or technique of using brushes, especially in painting.
Her brushworks are delicate and precise. 她的笔法细腻而精准。
brushwork, brushstrokes, technique
artistry, craftsmanship
