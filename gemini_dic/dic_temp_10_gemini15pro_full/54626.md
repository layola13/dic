dismissable:/dɪsˈmɪsəbl/ /dɪsˈmɪsəbəl/| adj.|可解雇的；可驳回的；可消除的|Able to be dismissed or rejected.  Something that can be put out of one's mind.|The CEO made it clear that any employee found breaking the rules would be dismissable. 首席执行官明确表示，任何违反规定的员工都将被解雇。|The judge ruled the evidence dismissable due to its questionable origin. 法官裁定该证据因来源不明而无效。|近义词：rejectable, dispensable, fireable|反义词：indispensable, essential|词性:形容词

dismissable:/dɪsˈmɪsəbl/ /dɪsˈmɪsəbəl/| n.|可解雇的人或事|Something or someone that can be dismissed.|His concerns were treated as dismissables by management. 他的担忧被管理层视为无关紧要。|(This usage as a noun is less common)|近义词：none (as a noun)|反义词：none (as a noun)|词性:名词 (rarely used)
