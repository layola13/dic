impressively:/ɪmˈprɛsɪvli/
adverb
令人印象深刻地
in a way that makes a strong, positive impression on someone.
She played the piano impressively. 她弹钢琴弹得令人印象深刻。
近义词:remarkably, strikingly, notably
反义词:unimpressively, poorly, badly
词性:副词

词源:
From impress (verb) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  The verb "impress" ultimately comes from the Latin "impressare," meaning "to press into," from "in-" (in, into) + "premere" (to press).
