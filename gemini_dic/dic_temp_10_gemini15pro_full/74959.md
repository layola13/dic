friation:/fraɪˈeɪʃən/ | n. |摩擦，摩擦声| The act of rubbing one object against another. |The friation of the two stones created a spark. 两块石头的摩擦产生了火花。|近义词：rubbing, friction, abrasion |反义词：smoothing, polishing |词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *friātiō, from Latin friāre ("to rub").
