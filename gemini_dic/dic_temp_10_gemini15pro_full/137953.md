nonwetted:/ˌnɒnˈwɛtɪd/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈwɛtɪd/ (US)| adj.|未润湿的，不沾湿的|Not wetted; not having been made wet.  Describes a surface that repels liquid, or a liquid that does not spread over a surface.|The nonwetted surface of the lotus leaf allows water to bead and roll off.|Synonyms: water-repellent, hydrophobic|Antonyms: wetted, hydrophilic|adjective
