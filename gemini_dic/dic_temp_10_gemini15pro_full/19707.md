|n.|双语现象|Her bilingualism in English and Spanish is a great asset to the company. 她掌握英语和西班牙语的双语能力对公司来说是一笔巨大的财富。|例句：The school promotes bilingualism by offering classes in both English and French. 这所学校通过提供英语和法语课程来推广双语教育。|近义词：multilingualism|反义词：monolingualism|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lingual (relating to language) + -ism (forming nouns denoting doctrines, beliefs, practices, or characteristics).  Ultimately from Latin *bi- (two) and lingua (tongue, language).
