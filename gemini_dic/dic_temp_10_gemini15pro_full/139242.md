obligati's: /ˌɑːblɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 义务，责任 |  A duty or commitment. Something a person is morally or legally bound to do.  Derived from the Latin word "obligare," meaning "to bind." |  例句：He felt a strong obligation to his family.  他感到对家庭有强烈的责任感。

例句：She has many obligations as a parent.  作为一名家长，她有很多责任。

近义词：duty, responsibility, commitment, liability | 反义词：exemption, freedom, prerogative | 词性：名词
