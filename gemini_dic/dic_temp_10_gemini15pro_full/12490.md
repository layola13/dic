aspergill:/ˈæspərˌdʒɪl/| n. |曲霉，曲霉属真菌|A genus of common molds causing food spoilage and some pathogenic infections in humans and animals. |The aspergill was found growing on the bread.  面包上发现了曲霉。|Synonym: mold, fungus|Antonym: N/A|noun

aspergillus:/ˌæspərˈdʒɪləs/| n. |曲霉菌属|A genus of fungi comprising several hundred species, some of which cause aspergillosis. |Aspergillus fumigatus is a common cause of lung infections. 烟曲霉菌是肺部感染的常见原因。|Synonym: mold, fungus|Antonym: N/A|noun
