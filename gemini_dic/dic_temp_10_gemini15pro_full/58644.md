dumbfounder:/dʌmˈfaʊndər/ | v. | 使惊呆，使目瞪口呆 | To greatly astonish or amaze someone to the point of speechlessness. | The magician's sudden disappearance dumbfounded the audience. 魔术师突然消失，使观众惊呆了。

The news of his resignation dumbfounded everyone. 他辞职的消息让所有人都大吃一惊。


| 近义词: astonish, amaze, stun, bewilder, flabbergast | 反义词: enlighten, clarify, explain | 词性: 动词
