emceeing:/ˌɛmˈsiːɪŋ/
n.
主持；司仪；担任司仪
the act of acting as master of ceremonies at a formal occasion.
Example: He enjoys emceeing at weddings and other events. 他喜欢在婚礼和其他活动中担任司仪。
Example:  Her emceeing was professional and engaging, keeping the audience entertained throughout the night.  她的主持专业又吸引人，让观众整晚都很开心。
Synonyms:  hosting, presenting, moderating
Antonyms: attending, participating (as a non-host)
noun
