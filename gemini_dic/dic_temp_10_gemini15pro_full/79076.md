glabrate:/ˈɡleɪbreɪt/| adj.|近乎无毛的，逐渐变无毛的|Becoming glabrous or nearly so; losing hairs with age or maturity.  The plant's leaves start out hairy but become glabrate as they mature.|The stems of this species are initially pubescent but become glabrate with age.  这个物种的茎最初有短柔毛，但随着年龄的增长逐渐变得无毛。|Near glabrous, glabrescent|Pubescent, hairy|adjective
