apetalous:/eɪˈpetələs/
adjective
无瓣的；无花瓣的
Lacking petals; having no petals.
The apetalous flowers are small and inconspicuous. 无瓣的花朵小而不起眼。
The wind-pollinated plants often have apetalous flowers. 由风授粉的植物通常没有花瓣。
近义词:petal-less
反义词:petaliferous, petaled
词性:adjective
