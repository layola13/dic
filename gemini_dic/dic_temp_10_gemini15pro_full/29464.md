catalyzing:/ˈkætəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
催化；促进
Acting as a catalyst; accelerating a process.
The enzyme catalyzes the breakdown of starch into sugar.  这种酶催化淀粉分解成糖。
synonyms: accelerating, furthering, expediting, stimulating, boosting, promoting
antonyms: hindering, inhibiting, obstructing, impeding
词性: 动词(present participle)


catalyze:/ˈkætəˌlaɪz/
verb
催化；促进
To cause or accelerate (a reaction) by acting as a catalyst.
The presence of platinum catalyzes the reaction. 铂的存在催化了反应。
synonyms: accelerate, further, expedite, stimulate, boost, promote
antonyms: hinder, inhibit, obstruct, impede
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *catalyser*, from Greek *kataluein* "to dissolve," from *kata-* "down" + *luein* "to loosen."
