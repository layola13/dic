depressive:/dɪˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. n. | 沮丧的，抑郁的；抑郁症患者 | Causing or tending to cause depression.  A person suffering from or susceptible to depression. | She felt a depressive weight on her chest. 她感到胸口有一股沉重的压抑感。 |  He was diagnosed as a depressive. 他被诊断为抑郁症患者。| 近义词：melancholy, gloomy, dejected, depressed | 反义词：cheerful, happy, elated, manic | 词性: 形容词, 名词 | 词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *depressivus, from Latin depress- ‘pressed down’, from the verb deprimere.

depressing:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人沮丧的，压抑的 | Making one feel sad and without hope. | The news was deeply depressing.  这个消息令人深感沮丧。| It was a depressing thought. 这是一个令人沮丧的想法。 | 近义词：disheartening, discouraging, dispiriting | 反义词：encouraging, uplifting, cheering | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: Late 18th century: from depress + -ing.
