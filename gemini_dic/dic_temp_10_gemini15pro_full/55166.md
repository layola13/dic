dissembling:/dɪˈsemblɪŋ/
| adj. | 掩饰的；虚伪的 | concealing one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs |  Her dissembling smile hid her true anger. 她虚伪的笑容掩盖了她真正的愤怒。 | 近义词: feigning, pretending, masking, cloaking | 反义词: honest, sincere, genuine, candid | 词性: 形容词
dissemble:/dɪˈsembl/
| v. | 掩饰；假装 | to conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs | He dissembled his fear with a laugh. 他用笑掩饰了他的恐惧。 | 近义词: feign, pretend, mask, cloak | 反义词: reveal, expose, be honest | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Old French *dessembler*, from des- (expressing reversal) + sembler (“to seem, appear”).  Ultimately from Latin similis (“like”).
