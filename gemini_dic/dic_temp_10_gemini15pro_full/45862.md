cut-throat:/ˈkʌtˌθroʊt/ (US) /ˈkʌtˌθrəʊt/ (UK)| adj. n.|残酷的，激烈的； 凶手，刺客|Ruthlessly competitive; a murderer.|例句：The music industry is a cut-throat business.  音乐产业是一个残酷的行业。|近义词：ruthless, fierce, merciless, competitive, killer, assassin|反义词:gentle, kind, compassionate, cooperative|词性:adjective, noun
cut-throat:/ˈkʌtˌθroʊt/ (US) /ˈkʌtˌθrəʊt/ (UK)| n.|凶手，刺客|A murderer; a ruthless person.|例句：He was known as a cut-throat in the underworld. 他在地下世界被称为刺客。|近义词：murderer, assassin, killer|反义词:protector, guardian|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the literal act of cutting someone's throat, implying ruthlessness and violence.  The term evolved to describe fiercely competitive situations. 源于字面意义上的割喉行为，暗示着残忍和暴力。该词演变为描述竞争激烈的局面。
