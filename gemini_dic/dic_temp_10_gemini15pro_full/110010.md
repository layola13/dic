libraries:/ˈlaɪbrɛriz/ /ˈlaɪbreəriz/| n.|图书馆（复数）|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|The city has several public libraries.  这个城市有几个公共图书馆。|近义词：archives, collections, depositories|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 bookstore - 书店,  指售卖书籍的地方)|noun
