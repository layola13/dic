mescaline:/ˈmɛskəlɪn/ | n. | 墨斯卡灵；仙人球毒碱 | A hallucinogenic alkaloid drug, C11H17NO3, obtained from the peyote cactus and related species. |  He experienced vivid hallucinations after ingesting mescaline. 他摄入墨斯卡灵后经历了生动的幻觉。|  Synonyms: hallucinogen, psychedelic | Antonyms: None | noun


mescalines:/ˈmɛskəlɪnz/ | n. | 墨斯卡灵（复数形式，较少使用）|  Plural form of mescaline, referring to multiple instances or doses of the drug. |  The researchers were studying the effects of different mescalines on the brain.  研究人员正在研究不同剂量墨斯卡灵对大脑的影响。| Synonyms: hallucinogens, psychedelics | Antonyms: None | noun
