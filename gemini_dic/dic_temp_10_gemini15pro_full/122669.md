modulated:/ˈmɒdʒʊleɪtɪd/ |ˈmɑːdʒəleɪtɪd/| adj.|已调制的，调整的，调控的|Varied or adjusted in some way, such as in pitch, intensity, or frequency.|The singer modulated her voice to express a range of emotions. 歌手调节她的声音来表达各种情绪。|近义词: adjusted, varied, regulated, tempered, adapted|反义词: unmodulated, constant, uniform|词性:形容词

modulate:/ˈmɒdʒʊleɪt/ |ˈmɑːdʒəleɪt/| v.|调制，调整，调控|To vary the strength, tone, or pitch of (one's voice). To change the key of (a piece of music). To alter or adapt (something) to different circumstances or to produce a desired effect.|He modulated his tone of voice to soothe the crying child. 他调整了说话的语气来安慰哭泣的孩子。  The spacecraft's speed was carefully modulated during descent.  宇宙飞船在下降过程中仔细地调整了速度。|近义词: adjust, vary, regulate, temper, adapt|反义词: maintain, fix|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *modulatus*, past participle of *modulare* (“to regulate, measure”), from *modulus* (“measure, standard”).
