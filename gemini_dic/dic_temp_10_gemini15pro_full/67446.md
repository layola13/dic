extirpating:/ˈekstərˌpeɪtɪŋ/
verb
根除，消灭
completely destroying or removing something, especially something unpleasant
The government is committed to extirpating corruption. 政府致力于根除腐败。
Synonyms: eradicating, eliminating, destroying, removing
Antonyms: preserving, conserving, fostering, cultivating
