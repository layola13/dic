cameo:/ˈkæmiˌoʊ/ (US), /ˈkæmiːəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 客串；（尤指演员的）短暂亮相；浮雕宝石|A brief appearance of a well-known person in a film, play, or other work, especially as a minor character. Also, a piece of jewelry, typically oval, consisting of a portrait in relief on a background of a different color.|例句：The film includes a cameo by the director.  这部电影有导演的客串。

例句：She wore a beautiful cameo brooch. 她戴着一个漂亮的浮雕胸针。|近义词：appearance, guest appearance, bit part|反义词:lead role|Noun
