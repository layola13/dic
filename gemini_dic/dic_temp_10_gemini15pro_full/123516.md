monotone:/ˈmɑːnəˌtoʊn/ (US) /ˈmɒnəˌtəʊn/ (UK)| n. |单调，单调的声音|A sound that stays at one pitch without variation.|The speaker's monotone made the lecture incredibly boring. 演讲者单调的声音使讲座极其枯燥。|near synonym: drone, tedium|opposite: variety, diversity|noun
monotone:/ˈmɑːnəˌtoʊn/ (US) /ˈmɒnəˌtəʊn/ (UK)| adj. |单调的，无变化的|Lacking in variation in tone or pitch.|She spoke in a monotone voice, making it difficult to stay awake. 她用单调的声音说话，让人很难保持清醒。|near synonym: dull, tedious, monotonous|opposite: varied, diverse, interesting|adjective
