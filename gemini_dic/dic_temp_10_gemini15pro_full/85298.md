harms:/hɑːrmz/
| n.| 损害，伤害 |Damage, injury, detriment.  Something that causes loss or pain.|The scandal caused irreparable harm to her reputation.  这起丑闻对她的名誉造成了不可挽回的损害。|近义词：damage, injury, detriment, mischief|反义词:benefit, advantage|词性:名词(plural)

harms:/hɑːrmz/
| vt.| 损害，伤害 |To damage, injure, or impair something.|Smoking harms your health. 吸烟有害健康。|近义词：damage, injure, impair, hurt|反义词:help, benefit, aid|词性:动词(third-person singular present)


词源:
From Middle English *harmen*, from Old English *hearmian* (“to harm, hurt”), from Proto-Germanic *harmijaną* (“to harm”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer-*, *ḱor-* (“sick, wounded”). Cognate with West Frisian harmje (“to harm”), Dutch harmen (“to grieve”), German härmen (“to grieve, harm”).
