例句：The scholarship was funded by the munificence of an anonymous donor. 这项奖学金是由一位匿名捐赠者的慷慨资助的。

近义词：generosity, liberality, bounty, philanthropy|反义词:stinginess, parsimony, meanness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *munificentia*, from *munificus* "bountiful, generous," from *munus* "gift, service, duty" + *-ficus*  "making, doing."
