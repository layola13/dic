pannose:/ˈpænoʊs/| adj.|绒毛状的，被毛的，有短柔毛的|Covered with a fine, dense, felted pubescence.|The leaves of the plant are pannose, giving them a soft, velvety texture. 这种植物的叶子有绒毛，使它们具有柔软的天鹅绒般的质感。|近义词：downy, pubescent, tomentose|反义词:glabrous, smooth|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *pannōsus*, from *panna* (“cloth, rag, piece of cloth”).  The sense development is from "covered with cloth" to "covered with a cloth-like pubescence."
