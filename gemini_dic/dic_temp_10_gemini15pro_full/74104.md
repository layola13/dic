fotive:/ˈvoʊtɪv/
adjective
奉献的，还愿的
Offered or performed in fulfillment of a vow or in gratitude or devotion.
The pilgrims left votive offerings at the shrine. 朝圣者在神龛前留下还愿的供品。
近义词: dedicatory, consecrated
反义词: profane, secular
词性: adjective

fotive:/ˈvoʊtɪv/
noun
还愿物
An object offered or dedicated in fulfillment of a vow or in gratitude or devotion.
The museum displayed a collection of votive tablets. 博物馆展出了一批还愿用的牌匾。
近义词: offering, dedication
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
