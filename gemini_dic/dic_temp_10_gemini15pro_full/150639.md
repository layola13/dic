papist:/ˈpeɪpɪst/| n.|天主教徒；教皇拥护者|A derogatory term for a Roman Catholic, implying excessive devotion to the Pope and papal authority.  Historically used by Protestants, often in a hostile or contemptuous way.|例句：He denounced his opponent as a papist. 他谴责他的对手是教皇信徒。

例句：The pamphlet was full of anti-papist rhetoric.  这本小册子充满了反天主教的言论。|近义词：Catholic, Roman Catholic|反义词：Protestant|词性：noun
