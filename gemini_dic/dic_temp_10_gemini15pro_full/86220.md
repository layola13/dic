heathered:/ˈheðərd/| adj.|石南花色的，混色的，杢灰色的|Made of or resembling heather. Also, having a mottled or speckled appearance, as if interwoven with different colored yarns.|例句：She wore a heathered gray sweater. 她穿了一件杢灰色的毛衣。|例句：The hills were covered in heathered moorland. 山丘上覆盖着石南花色的荒野。|近义词：mottled, speckled, variegated, marled|反义词:solid, plain|词性:形容词
词源:From *heather* + *-ed*.
