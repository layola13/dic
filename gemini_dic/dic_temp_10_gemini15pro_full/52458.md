differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪt/ | v. | 区分，区别，辨别；使不同，使差异化 | To recognize or show that two or more things are not the same; to make something different from other similar things. |  It's difficult to differentiate between the two varieties.  很难区分这两个品种。 | Synonyms: distinguish, discriminate, separate | Antonyms: confuse, blend, mix | verb

differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪt/ | v. |  求…的微分；微分 |  (mathematics) to calculate the derivative of a function. | Differentiate the equation with respect to x. 对该方程进行关于x的微分。 | Synonyms: derive (in mathematics) | Antonyms: integrate (in mathematics) | verb
