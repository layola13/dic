alopecias:/ˌæləˈpiːʃəz/ /ˌæləˈpiːsiəz/
noun
秃头症（复数）
Hair loss, often occurring in patches, but can also be total.  It is the plural form of alopecia.
Alopecias can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, autoimmune disorders, and medications. 秃头症可能由多种因素引起，包括遗传、自身免疫性疾病和药物。
The patient presented with multiple alopecias on his scalp. 病人的头皮上出现了多处秃头症。
Synonyms: baldness, hair loss
Antonyms: hair growth
