buzzardly:/ˈbʌzərdli/| adj.|像秃鹫一样地；贪婪地；卑鄙地|Resembling or characteristic of a buzzard, especially in being predatory, greedy, or contemptible.  It implies a sense of scavenging or preying on weakness.|He cast a buzzardly glance at the inheritance. 他贪婪地盯着那份遗产。|近义词：rapacious, avaricious, greedy, grasping, predatory, covetous|反义词：generous, altruistic|词性:形容词

词源:  From buzzard + -ly.  The word "buzzard" itself has a complex etymology, ultimately deriving from the Old French *busard, a variant of *buisart, meaning "buzzard."
