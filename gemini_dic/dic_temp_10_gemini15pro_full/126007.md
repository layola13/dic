musmon:/ˈmʊsmən/ | n. | 欧洲盘羊 | A wild sheep (Ovis musimon) native to Corsica and Sardinia, introduced elsewhere. It is thought to be ancestral to domestic sheep. |  The musmon grazed peacefully on the hillside. 欧洲盘羊在山坡上平静地吃草。| The musmon darted across the rocky terrain. 欧洲盘羊飞快地穿过岩石地带。 | synonyms: mouflon, wild sheep | antonyms: N/A | noun
