hexoctahedron:/ˌhɛksɒkˈteɪdriən/ | n. |六八面体|A polyhedron with 48 faces.  Specifically, in mineralogy, a crystal form of the isometric system composed of 48 equal triangular faces. It is a complex crystal form often seen in diamond and garnet.|The gemologist identified the rare hexoctahedron shape of the diamond.|Synonym: hexakisoctahedron|Antonym: N/A|noun
