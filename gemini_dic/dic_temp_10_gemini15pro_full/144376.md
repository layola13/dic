outstorm:/ˈaʊtstɔːrm/
verb
战胜风暴；比暴风雨更猛烈
To surpass a storm in force or violence.
The ship managed to outstorm the hurricane. 这艘船设法战胜了飓风。
近义词: outweather, withstand
反义词: succumb
词性: verb
