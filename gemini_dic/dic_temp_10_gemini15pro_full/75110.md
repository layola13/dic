fripper:/ˈfrɪpər/| n.|旧衣服；廉价的衣服；旧货|A dealer in frippery, especially old clothes. A person who deals in second-hand clothes.  Something frivolous or tawdry.|She rummaged through the fripper at the flea market, hoping to find a hidden gem. 她仔细翻找跳蚤市场的旧货，希望能找到一件隐藏的珍宝。|近义词：old clothes, secondhand clothes, rags, tatters|反义词:new clothes|词性:noun

fripper:/ˈfrɪpər/| v.|打扮；装饰|To dress up or adorn, especially in a showy or frivolous way.  To decorate with showy or cheap ornaments.|She frippered herself in cheap jewelry and a gaudy dress. 她用廉价的珠宝和艳丽的裙子把自己打扮起来。|近义词：adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament|反义词:strip, undress|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *friper (“to rub, wear out clothes”), from Old French fripier (“dealer in old clothes”).
