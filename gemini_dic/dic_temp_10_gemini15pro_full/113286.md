luteous:/ˈluːtiəs/| adj.|金黄色的，浅黄色的|Of a golden-yellow color; saffron-yellow.|The luteous flowers brightened the meadow. 金黄色的花朵照亮了草地。|近义词：golden, yellow, flaxen, aureate|反义词：dark, dusky|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *luteus*, from *lūtum* "yellow weed, weld," a plant used in dyeing. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*ghel-* "yellow, green, bright."
