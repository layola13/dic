loyalties:/ˈlɔɪəltiz/
noun
忠诚；忠心；忠实
allegiance, fidelity, faithfulness
betrayal, treachery, disloyalty
n.

English Explanation: Loyalties is the plural form of loyalty. It refers to the state of being loyal; faithfulness to commitments or obligations. It often implies a strong feeling of support or allegiance.  Since it's plural, it usually refers to multiple loyalties a person might hold, which can sometimes conflict.


Example Sentences:

Her loyalties were divided between her family and her career.
她的忠诚在家庭和事业之间摇摆不定。

His loyalties to the company never wavered.
他对公司的忠诚从未动摇。


Synonyms: allegiance, fidelity, devotion, faithfulness

Antonyms: betrayal, treachery, disloyalty, infidelity
