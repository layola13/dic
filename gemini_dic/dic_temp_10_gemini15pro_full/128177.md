neufchatel:/ˌnuːʃɑːˈtɛl/ | n. |纽沙泰尔干酪; 一种软质奶油奶酪|A type of soft, creamy cheese, similar to cream cheese but lower in fat. It originates from Neufchâtel-en-Bray, a town in Normandy, France.|  I spread some neufchatel on my bagel. 我在我的贝果上涂了一些纽沙泰尔奶酪。| 近义词: cream cheese, fromage frais | 反义词: hard cheese | 词性: 名词

neufchatel:/ˌnuːʃɑːˈtɛl/ | adj. |纽沙泰尔的; 指的是纽沙泰尔地区或其产品的|Relating to or originating from Neufchâtel-en-Bray, France, especially its cheese.| This is authentic neufchatel cheese from Normandy. 这是来自诺曼底的正宗纽沙泰尔奶酪。| 近义词: Norman | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词
