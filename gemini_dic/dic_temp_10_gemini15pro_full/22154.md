bombarded:/bɒmˈbɑːdɪd/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdɪd/
verb
轰炸，炮击;大量提问;（用电子邮件、信件等）攻击，围攻
to attack a place with continuous heavy fire from large guns or bombs, or to drop a large number of bombs onto a place. Also, to attack someone with a lot of questions, criticisms, or information.
The troops bombarded the city all night.  部队整夜轰炸这座城市。
The children bombarded her with questions. 孩子们向她提了一大堆问题。
bombard, shell, attack
defend, protect
