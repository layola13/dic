intercirculating:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːkjʊleɪtɪŋ/| adj.|相互循环的，互相流通的|Circulating or flowing between each other or among different parts of a system.|The intercirculating fluids help maintain a consistent temperature throughout the engine.  相互循环的液体有助于保持整个发动机温度一致。|例句：The blood intercirculates throughout the body, delivering oxygen and nutrients. 血液在全身循环，输送氧气和营养物质。

例句：The intercirculating currents in the ocean help regulate global temperatures. 海洋中相互循环的洋流有助于调节全球温度。|近义词：circulating, flowing, exchanging|反义词：stagnant, static|词性：形容词
