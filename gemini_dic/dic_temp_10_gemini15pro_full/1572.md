achkans:/ˈɑːtʃkɑːnz/ /ˈɑːkɑːnz/| n.|长袍，长衫，(尤指印度北部男子穿的)一种长外套|A traditional knee-length coat worn especially by men in northern India.|He wore a beautifully embroidered achkan to the wedding. 他穿着一件精美刺绣的长袍参加婚礼。

The groom's achkan was a deep crimson red. 新郎的长袍是深红色的。|近义词：coat, robe, tunic|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern attire")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi and Urdu اکھن/achkan, ultimately from Persian ارخان arkān “sleeves”

USAGE->
Plural form: The achkans were all handmade. 这些长袍都是手工制作的。

Possessive form:  The man's achkan was silk.  这个男人的长袍是丝绸的。

As subject:  The achkan is a traditional garment.  长袍是一种传统服装。

As object: He wore the achkan with pride. 他自豪地穿着长袍。

Prepositional phrase:  He looked regal in his achkan. 他穿着长袍看起来很威严。
