oxybenzoic: /ˌɒksɪbɛnˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. | 羟基苯甲酸的 | relating to or denoting any of the three isomeric crystalline hydroxy derivatives of benzoic acid, especially salicylic acid. |  Oxybenzoic acids are used in the production of certain dyes. 羟基苯甲酸用于生产某些染料。|  Para-oxybenzoic acid is a preservative. 对羟基苯甲酸是一种防腐剂。| synonyms: hydroxybenzoic | antonyms: N/A | adjective
