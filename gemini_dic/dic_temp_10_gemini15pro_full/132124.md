noncrystalline:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | adj. | 非晶体的，无定形的 | Lacking a crystalline structure; amorphous.  Not having the regular structure of a crystal. | Glass is a noncrystalline solid. 玻璃是一种非晶体固体。| The polymer has a noncrystalline structure. 这种聚合物具有非晶态结构。| amorphous, glassy | crystalline | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + crystalline.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Crystalline" comes from the Greek "krystallos," meaning "ice" or "crystal."
