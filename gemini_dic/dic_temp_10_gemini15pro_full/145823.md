词源：
"Over-" (prefix) + "defiantly" (adverb)

"Over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, across, beyond."
"Defiantly" comes from "defy" + "-antly."
"Defy" comes from Old French "defier," meaning "to challenge, renounce."
"-antly" is an adverbial suffix.
