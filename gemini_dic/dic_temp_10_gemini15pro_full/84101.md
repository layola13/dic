half-intelligibly:/ˌhæf ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbli/
|adv.|半明白地，难以理解地|partially understandable; in a way that is difficult to understand.|He mumbled half-intelligibly, and I couldn't grasp his meaning. 他含糊不清地咕哝着，我无法理解他的意思。|She explained the complex theory half-intelligibly to the beginners. 她向初学者半明白地解释了这个复杂的理论。|近义词: unclearly, vaguely, obscurely, incoherently|反义词: clearly, distinctly, intelligibly, coherently|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "intelligibly".  "Half" comes from Old English "healf". "Intelligibly" comes from "intelligible" + "-ly". "Intelligible" comes from Late Latin "intelligibilis", from Latin "intelligere" meaning "to understand".
