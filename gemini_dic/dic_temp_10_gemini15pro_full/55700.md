diurna:/daɪˈɜːrnə/ /diˈɜːrnə/
noun
昼行性动物; 白昼的活动
Animals that are active during the day are called diurnal.
例句：Many desert animals are nocturnal, while others are diurnal to avoid the intense midday heat. 许多沙漠动物是夜行性的，而其他一些则是昼行性的，以避免中午强烈的 Hitze.

例句：The diurnal rhythm of the flowers opening and closing is fascinating to observe.  观察这些花朵昼夜开合的节奏真是令人着迷。

近义词：daily
反义词：nocturnal
词性：n.
