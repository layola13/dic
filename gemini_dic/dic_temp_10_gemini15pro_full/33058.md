chowdered:/ˈtʃaʊdərd/
verb
浓汤炖煮的，弄成糊状的
cooked or prepared as a chowder; thick and creamy.  Often used figuratively to describe something ruined or messed up.
The storm chowdered the coastline, leaving debris everywhere.  暴风雨把海岸线弄得一团糟，到处都是残骸。
近义词: stewed, thickened, ruined, botched
反义词: thin, clear, pristine, perfect
词性: verb
