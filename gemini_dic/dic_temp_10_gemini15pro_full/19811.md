bimastism: /baɪˈmæstɪzəm/
n.
双乳房; 双乳;
The condition of having two breasts.  It refers to the normal anatomical presence of two mammary glands in mammals.
Mammals, including humans, typically exhibit bimastism. 哺乳动物，包括人类，通常表现出双乳房。
Bimastism is a characteristic feature of female mammals. 双乳房是雌性哺乳动物的特征。
近义词:  duality of breasts (more technical/formal)
反义词: amastia (absence of breasts), monomastism (presence of only one breast)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From bi- (two) + mast- (breast) + -ism (condition or state)
