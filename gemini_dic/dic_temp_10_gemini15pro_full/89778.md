horeb:/ˈhɔːrɛb/| n.|何烈山|Mount Horeb, also known as Mount Sinai, is a mountain in the Sinai Peninsula of Egypt that is of significant religious importance to Judaism, Christianity, and Islam.  It is traditionally considered the place where Moses received the Ten Commandments from God.|Moses received the law of God on Mount Horeb. 摩西在何烈山上接受了上帝的律法。|The Israelites camped at the foot of Horeb. 以色列人在何烈山的山脚下扎营。|Sinai, mountain|---|noun
