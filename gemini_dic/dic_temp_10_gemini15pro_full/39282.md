congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡruənt/ (US)| adj.|全等的，一致的，适合的|Agreeing or corresponding; similar in character, form, or function; geometrically coinciding when superimposed.|The two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。|近义词：identical, similar, equivalent, matching, corresponding|反义词：incongruent, dissimilar, different, incompatible|词性:形容词
congruence:/ˈkɒŋɡruənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡruənsi/ (US)| n.|全等，一致，适合|The state of being congruent; agreement, harmony.|There is a congruence between the two sets of data. 这两组数据之间是一致的。|近义词：agreement, harmony, consistency, correspondence|反义词：incongruity, disagreement, discrepancy, incompatibility|词性:名词

词源：From Latin congruentem, present participle of congruere "to come together, agree," from com- "together" (see com-) + -gruere, related to grues "crane" (the birds were thought to fly in congruent flocks).
