diachronicnesses:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɑːnɪknɪsɪz/
noun
历时性，跨时间性
The quality or state of being diachronic; development through time.
Analyzing the diachronicnesses of language evolution helps us understand how words change their meanings over centuries.  分析语言演变的历时性有助于我们理解词语在几个世纪中是如何改变其含义的。
The professor's research explored the diachronicnesses of various musical traditions.  这位教授的研究探索了各种音乐传统的历时性。
synonyms: historical development, temporal evolution, chronological change
antonyms: synchronicity, simultaneity
