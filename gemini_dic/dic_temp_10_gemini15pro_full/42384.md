counterblockaded:/ˌkaʊntərˌblɑːˈkeɪdɪd/ | v. |反封锁，对抗封锁|To blockade a place that has already blockaded oneself.  A retaliatory blockade in response to an enemy's blockade.|The British Navy counterblockaded the French ports.|近义词：blockade, retaliate |反义词: allow passage, permit entry |词性:动词

counterblockade:/ˌkaʊntərˌblɑːˈkeɪd/ | n. |反封锁|A blockade instituted in retaliation for a blockade by the enemy.|The counterblockade crippled the enemy's trade.|近义词：blockade |反义词: open trade, free passage |词性:名词
