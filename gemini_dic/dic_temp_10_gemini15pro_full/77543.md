g'day:/ɡəˈdeɪ/
interjection
你好； 喂； 
An informal greeting, used especially in Australia and New Zealand, equivalent to "good day".
G'day, mate! How are you doing?  你好，伙计！ 你好吗？
G'day, everyone! Welcome to the barbecue.  大家好！欢迎来烧烤。
近义词:hello, hi, howdy
反义词:goodbye, farewell
词性:interjection
