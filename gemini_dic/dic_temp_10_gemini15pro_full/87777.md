hiatus:/haɪˈeɪtəs/| n.|间断，中断，裂缝|A pause or gap in a sequence, series, or process.|After a two-year hiatus, the band released a new album.  乐队在中断两年之后发行了一张新专辑。|例句：There was a brief hiatus in the conversation.  谈话中出现了短暂的停顿。|近义词：pause, break, gap, interruption, interval|反义词：continuation, continuity|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *hiatus*, from *hiare* "to gape, yawn".
