mimes:/maɪmz/| n. |模仿；滑稽剧；哑剧|An entertainment in which performers express themselves through gestures and no words. |The street performer's mimes captivated the crowd.  街头艺人的哑剧表演吸引了人群。|近义词：imitates, mimics, impersonates|反义词: speaks, vocalizes | noun

mimes:/maɪmz/| v. |模仿；做哑剧表演|To imitate or portray through gestures and no words. |He mimed eating a banana. 他模仿吃香蕉的动作。|近义词：imitates, mimics, impersonates|反义词: speaks, vocalizes | verb
