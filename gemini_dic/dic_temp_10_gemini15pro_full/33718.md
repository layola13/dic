circensian:/səˈsɜːrʃ(ə)nziən/
adjective|马戏团的；竞技场的；喧闹的，吵闹的|relating to the circus; characteristic of the Roman circus or its shows; resembling the activities of a circus, especially in being noisy, exciting, or spectacular.|The circensian games were a popular form of entertainment in ancient Rome. 古罗马的竞技场游戏是一种很受欢迎的娱乐形式。|Synonyms:  circus-like, theatrical, spectacular, showy, ostentatious|Antonyms:  calm, peaceful, quiet, subdued, understated
noun|马戏团表演者|a performer in a circus|The circensian wowed the crowd with his daring feats of acrobatics.  这位马戏团表演者以他大胆的杂技表演让观众惊叹。|Synonyms:  performer, acrobat, clown, juggler, equestrian|Antonyms: spectator, audience member
