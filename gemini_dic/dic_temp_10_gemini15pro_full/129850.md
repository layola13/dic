non-immersion:/nɒn-ɪˈmɜːʃən/ | adj. | 非沉浸式 | Not involving immersion; not fully submerging or engaging.  | Example: The non-immersion language learning program focused on grammar and vocabulary rather than practical conversation.  非沉浸式语言学习项目侧重于语法和词汇，而不是实际对话。 | Example:  A non-immersion online course allows students to learn at their own pace. 一个非沉浸式的在线课程允许学生按照自己的节奏学习。 | 近义词: traditional, conventional, non-integrative | 反义词: immersive, integrative | 词性: adjective

non-immersion:/nɒn-ɪˈmɜːʃən/ | n. | 非沉浸 | The state or quality of not being immersed. | Example: The non-immersion of the control group in the study ensured a clear comparison with the experimental group.  对照组的非沉浸状态确保了与实验组的清晰比较。 | Example: The student's non-immersion in the culture hindered their language acquisition. 学生对文化的非沉浸阻碍了他们的语言习得。 | 近义词: detachment, separation | 反义词: immersion, engagement | 词性: noun
