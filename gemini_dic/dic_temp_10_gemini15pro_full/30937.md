chairmanship:/ˈtʃɛəmənʃɪp/ /ˈtʃɛrmənʃɪp/
n.
主席职位；主席身份；主席任期
The position or period of being a chairman.
She held the chairmanship of the committee for five years. 她担任了五年委员会主席。
近义词：presidency, leadership
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps subordination or membership)


chairmanning:/ˈtʃɛəmənɪŋ/  /ˈtʃɛrmənɪŋ/
v.
担任主席；主持
Acting as chairman of a meeting or organization.
He was chairmanning the meeting when the fire alarm went off. 火警响起时，他正在主持会议。
近义词：presiding, moderating
反义词: (No direct antonym)
