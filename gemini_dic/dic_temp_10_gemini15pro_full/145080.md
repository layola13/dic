overannotate:/ˌoʊvərˈænəteɪt/ | v. | 过度注释，过度注解 | To annotate something excessively, providing more notes or comments than necessary. | The professor overannotated the student's essay, making it difficult to read. 教授在学生的文章上做了过多的注释，使得文章难以阅读。 |  The editor overannotated the manuscript with suggestions and corrections. 编辑在手稿上加了过多的建议和修改意见。 | overexplain, overcomment | underannotate | verb
overannotation:/ˌoʊvərˌænəˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 过度注释，过度注解 | The act of overannotating. | The overannotation of the text made it confusing. 文本的过度注释使其令人困惑。 | The student complained about the overannotation on their paper. 学生抱怨他们的论文上的过度注释。 | overexplanation, overcommentary | underannotation | noun
