palatinate: /ˈpælətɪneɪt/ | n.  |腭骨；腭；领地，采邑；（历史）伯爵领地 |A palatinate is a territory ruled by a nobleman with royal privileges, especially the County Palatine of Durham in England. |The Bishop of Durham held extensive powers within his palatinate.  达勒姆主教在其领地内拥有广泛的权力。| synonym: territory, domain, county | antonym: N/A | noun

palatinate: /ˈpælətɪneɪt/ | adj. |腭的；拥有特权的 |Relating to the palate or a palatinate. | The palatine tonsils are located in the back of the throat. 腭扁桃体位于喉咙后部。| synonym: privileged, autonomous | antonym: N/A | adjective
