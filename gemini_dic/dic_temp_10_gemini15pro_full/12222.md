asbestiform:/æsˈbɛstɪfɔːrm/| adj.|石棉状的，像石棉的|Having a fibrous structure similar to that of asbestos.|The mineral has an asbestiform habit. 这种矿物具有石棉状结构。|近义词：fibrous, asbestos-like|反义词: non-fibrous|词性: 形容词

词源:  From asbestos + -iform.  "Asbestos" comes from Greek *ἄσβεστος (ásbestos) meaning "unquenchable", referring to its fire-resistant properties. "-iform" is a suffix meaning "having the form of," derived from Latin "-formis."
