conjure:/ˈkʌndʒər/ (US), /ˈkʌndʒə(r)/ (UK)| v. |变魔术；使…浮现于脑海；恳求|To make something appear unexpectedly or seemingly from nowhere as if by magic; to bring a thought, feeling, or memory to someone's mind; to beg or implore someone earnestly.|The magician conjured a rabbit out of his hat. 魔术师从帽子里变出一只兔子。|Synonyms: evoke, summon, implore, beseech|Antonyms: banish, dispel|verb

conjure:/ˈkʌndʒər/ (US), /ˈkʌndʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. |魔术；戏法|The act of performing magic; a magic trick or illusion.|The conjuring trick amazed the audience.  魔术戏法使观众惊叹。|Synonyms: magic, sorcery, witchcraft|Antonyms: reality|noun
