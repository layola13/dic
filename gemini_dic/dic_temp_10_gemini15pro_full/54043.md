discussionist: /dɪˈskʌʃənɪst/
n.
讨论者，好辩论者
A person who participates in discussions, especially one who enjoys arguing or debating.
He's a known discussionist on online forums, often engaging in heated debates.  他是在线论坛上有名的讨论者，经常参与激烈的辩论。
近义词: debater, arguer, disputant
反义词: listener, observer
