overassists:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɪsts/
|n.|过度助攻|Providing too much assistance, often to the detriment of the recipient's development of skills or independence.  It implies hindering rather than helping.|The coach's overassists stifled the players' creativity on the court. 教练的过度助攻扼杀了球员在球场上的创造力。|Overhelping, excessive support|Under-supporting, neglecting|noun
