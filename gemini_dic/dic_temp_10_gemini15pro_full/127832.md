nematoidean:/ˌnɛməˈtɔɪdiən/ | adj. n. |线虫类的；线虫|Relating to or characteristic of nematodes; a nematode. | Nematoidean worms are abundant in soil.  线虫在土壤中很丰富。| The scientist studied the nematoidean life cycle.  这位科学家研究了线虫的生命周期。|近义词：nematode, roundworm |反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑其他类型的蠕虫，例如flatworm扁形虫) |词性: adjective, noun
