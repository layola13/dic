eventration:/ɪˌvenˈtreɪʃən/ |n.|脏器突出；【医】内脏突出；【解】开腹；取出内脏|The protrusion of the abdominal viscera through an opening in the abdominal wall.  腹部内脏通过腹壁开口突出。|The surgeon performed an eventration of the intestines.  外科医生进行了肠道突出修复手术。|近义词:hernia, protrusion, prolapse|反义词:reduction|词性:名词

e·ven·tra·tion (ĭ-vĕn′trā′shən, ē′-)
Etymology: Latin ēventrātiōn- (stem of ēventrātiō ), from ēventrātus (past participle of ēventrāre ), to disembowel, from ē-, ex- + venter, ventr- belly
