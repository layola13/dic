axunge:/ˈæksʌndʒ/ | n. | 猪油；软膏 |  A soft fat obtained from rendered hog fat. It's traditionally used in ointments and some cooking. |  The old recipe called for axunge, but I substituted it with lard.  这个古老的食谱需要猪油，但我用猪板油代替了它。 | synonyms: lard, grease, fat, ointment, balm | antonyms: leanness (referring to meat lacking fat) | noun


axunge:/ˈæksʌndʒ/ | n. | 软膏 | A fatty, greasy substance used as an ointment or lubricant. | The axunge soothed the chapped skin.  这种油膏舒缓了皲裂的皮肤。 | synonyms: ointment, balm, salve, emollient | antonyms: desiccant (something that dries things out) | noun
