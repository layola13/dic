countersurveillance:/ˌkaʊntər sərˈveɪləns/ | n. |反监视，反侦察|The use of strategies and technologies to detect and prevent surveillance. |例句：The company invested heavily in countersurveillance measures to protect its trade secrets.  这家公司大力投资反监视措施以保护其商业机密。

|例句：Countersurveillance is crucial for journalists working in hostile environments.  对于在敌对环境中工作的记者来说，反监视至关重要。|近义词：anti-surveillance, counterintelligence |反义词: surveillance| 词性: 名词
