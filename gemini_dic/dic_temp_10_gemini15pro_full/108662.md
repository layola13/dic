leafleting:/ˈliːflɪtɪŋ/
noun
散发传单；传单派发
the activity of distributing leaflets to people
例句：Leafleting is a common way to advertise.  散发传单是一种常见的广告方式。

例句：The campaign involved door-to-door leafleting. 这场运动包括挨家挨户散发传单。
近义词：distributing flyers, handing out flyers, pamphlet distribution
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From leaflet + -ing.  "leaflet" itself comes from "leaf" (referring to the small size and shape) + "-let" (a diminutive suffix).
