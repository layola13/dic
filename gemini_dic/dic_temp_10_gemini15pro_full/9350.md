antiprogressive:/ˌæntiprəˈɡrɛsɪv/| adj.|反进步的，反对进步的|Opposing or hindering progress, especially in social or political reform.|The antiprogressive policies of the government led to widespread discontent. 政府的反进步政策导致了普遍的不满。|例句：His antiprogressive stance on environmental issues alienated many voters.  他在环境问题上的反进步立场疏远了许多选民。

例句：The antiprogressive movement seeks to undo many of the social reforms of the last century. 反进步运动试图取消上个世纪的许多社会改革。|近义词：reactionary, conservative, regressive|反义词：progressive, liberal, reformist|词性：adjective
