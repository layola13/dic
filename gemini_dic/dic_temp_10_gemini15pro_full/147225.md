overmaturity:/ˌoʊvər məˈtʃʊrɪti/| n. | 过熟，过度成熟 |The overmaturity of the fruit caused it to spoil quickly. 水果过熟导致它很快腐烂。|近义词：ripeness, overripeness |反义词: immaturity, unripeness | noun

Etymology:  From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "maturity" (from Latin "maturitas," meaning "ripeness").
