bronco:/ˈbrɒŋkəʊ/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːŋkoʊ/ (US)| n. |野马；（尤指）未驯服的马；(非正式)暴烈的人|A small, strong wild horse of the western US.|例句：The bronco bucked and reared, trying to throw the rider. 这匹野马猛然弓背跳跃，试图把骑手摔下来。

例句：He's a real bronco, always getting into fights. 他真是个暴脾气，总是跟人打架。|近义词：mustang, wild horse, outlaw|反义词:tame horse, domesticated horse|词性:noun
