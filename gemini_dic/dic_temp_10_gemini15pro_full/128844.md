nigrescent:/naɪˈɡrɛsənt/
adjective
变黑的，发黑的
Becoming black or dark; turning blackish.
The nigrescent clouds threatened rain.  乌云密布，预示着即将下雨。
The solution turned nigrescent upon exposure to air. 溶液暴露在空气中变黑了。

近义词: darkening, blackening, dusky
反义词: brightening, lightening, whitening
词性: adjective
