codirects:/ˌkoʊdaɪˈrɛkts/| v.| 共同执导，共同指导 | To direct (a film, play, etc.) jointly with another or others. |  Example:  She codirects the program with her colleague. 她和她的同事共同负责这个项目。

Example: They codirected the movie and shared the credit. 他们共同执导了这部电影并分享了荣誉。 | 近义词: co-leads, jointly directs| 反义词: solely directs|词性:动词
