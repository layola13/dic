nonresponsibilities:/nɒnrɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 无责任, 免责事项 | Lack of responsibilities or things for which one is not responsible. |  The contract clearly outlined his nonresponsibilities, including marketing and sales.  合同清楚地列出了他的免责事项，包括市场营销和销售。 | Example:  She enjoyed the nonresponsibilities of being a child. 她享受着童年无忧无虑的生活。 | Synonyms: exemptions, immunities, exclusions | Antonyms: duties, obligations, responsibilities | noun
