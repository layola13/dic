fatter:/ˈfætər/| adj.|更胖的，更肥的|Comparative form of "fat."  Indicates a greater degree of fatness than something else.|例句：My cat is fatter than yours. 我的猫比你的胖。

例句：He's gotten fatter since he stopped exercising.  自从他停止锻炼以来，他变胖了。|近义词：chubbier, plumper, heavier, larger|反义词:thinner, slimmer, leaner|词性:adjective
词源:From Old English *fættra, comparative of fætt (“fat”). Cognate with Dutch vetter, German fetter.
