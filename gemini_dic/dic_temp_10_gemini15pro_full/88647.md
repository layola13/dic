hoar:/hɔːr/ /hɔr/| adj.|灰白的，古老的，白霜覆盖的|Covered with white frost; white or gray with age.|例句：The hoar frost glistened on the trees. 树上的白霜闪闪发光。|近义词：frosty, ancient, venerable, gray, white|反义词:young, new, black|词性:形容词

hoar:/hɔːr/ /hɔr/| n.|白霜|A grayish-white crystalline deposit of frozen water vapor formed in clear still weather on vegetation, fences, etc.|例句：The fields were covered in a thick hoar. 田野上覆盖着一层厚厚的霜。|近义词：frost, rime|反义词: - |词性:名词
