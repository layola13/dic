bullionist:/ˈbʊliənɪst/
n.
金本位主义者
A person who advocates the use of metallic money (especially gold) as a monetary standard.
The bullionists argued that a gold standard would provide a stable and reliable currency. 金本位主义者认为金本位制将提供稳定可靠的货币。
The prominent bullionist believed that tying the currency to gold would prevent inflation.  这位著名的金本位主义者认为，将货币与黄金挂钩可以防止通货膨胀。
Synonyms: goldbug, hard-money advocate
Antonyms: fiat money supporter
