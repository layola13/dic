nonappropriative:/ˌnɒnəˈprəʊpriətɪv/
adjective
非占有的，非挪用的
Not involving or relating to the appropriation of something, especially water rights.
The state recognizes both appropriative and nonappropriative water rights.  该州承认占有性和非占有性水权。
Traditional societies often have nonappropriative systems of resource management.  传统社会通常拥有非占有性的资源管理系统。
Synonyms: non-exclusive, shared, common
Antonyms: appropriative, exclusive, private
词性: 形容词
