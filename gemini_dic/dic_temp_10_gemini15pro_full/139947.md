odontiasis:/ˌoʊdɒnˈtaɪəsɪs/ /ˌoʊdɛnˈteɪsɪs/
n.
出牙，长牙
The process of tooth development and eruption.
The baby is experiencing the discomfort of odontiasis.  婴儿正经历着出牙的不适。
近义词:teething
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps tooth loss or resorption)
词性:noun
