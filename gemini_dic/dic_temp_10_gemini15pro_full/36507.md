coleys:/ˈkoʊliz/| n.|科利犬（通常指边境牧羊犬）|A breed of herding dog, typically a Border Collie, known for its intelligence and agility.|例句：The farmer used his two coleys to round up the sheep. 农夫用他的两只科利犬把羊群赶到一起。

例句：Coleys are highly trainable and excel in dog sports. 科利犬非常容易训练，并且在犬类运动中表现出色。|近义词：Border Collie, sheepdog|反义词: (No direct antonym for a breed of dog)|词性:noun
