festerment:/ˈfestəmənt/| n. |腐烂; 溃烂; 恶化|The festerment of the wound caused him great pain. 伤口的溃烂让他非常痛苦。|例句：The festerment of resentment can poison a relationship.  怨恨的滋长会毒害一段关系。

The festerment of the untreated wound led to a serious infection. 未经处理的伤口的溃烂导致了严重的感染。|近义词：suppuration, putrefaction, decay, rot, deterioration|反义词:healing, recovery|词性:名词

fester:/ˈfestər/| v. |(伤口)化脓, 溃烂; (感情等)恶化,  festering adj. 痛苦的，苦恼的|The wound festered for weeks. 伤口溃烂了几个星期。|例句：His anger festered inside him. 他的愤怒在他心中 festered。

Allowing the problem to fester will only make it worse. 让问题恶化只会使情况更糟。|近义词：rankle, smolder, worsen, aggravate|反义词:heal, improve|词性:动词
