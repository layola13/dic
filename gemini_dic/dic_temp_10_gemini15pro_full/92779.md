icicled:/ˈaɪsɪk(ə)ld/ | adj. | 结冰的， покрытый сосульками| Covered with icicles; decorated with icicles. | The trees were icicled and glistening in the winter sun. 冬日的阳光下，树上结满了冰，闪闪发光。| 近义词：frozen, frosty, glacial | 反义词: thawed, melted | 词性: 形容词

icicle:/ˈaɪsɪk(ə)l/ | n. | 冰柱， сосулька | A hanging, tapering piece of ice formed by the freezing of dripping water. |  A long icicle hung from the roof. 屋檐下挂着一根长长的冰柱。| 近义词：frost, glaciation | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以用描述融化状态的词) liquid water, puddle | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English *īsģicel, from īs "ice" + ģicel "icicle," related to  German Eiszapfen.
