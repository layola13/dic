fountful: /ˈfaʊntfəl/
adjective
丰富的，充满的
abounding in or characterized by springs or fountains; also : copious, plentiful
例句：The land was fountful, with numerous springs providing fresh water.  这片土地水源丰富，有许多泉水提供淡水。

例句：Her mind was fountful of creative ideas. 她的脑海里充满了创意。
近义词：abundant, copious, plentiful, teeming
反义词：scarce, meager, barren
词性: adjective

fountful: /ˈfaʊntfəl/
noun
源泉，源头 (rare usage - primarily used as adjective)
a source or origin, especially of something desirable
例句：The ancient texts are a fountful of wisdom.  这些古代文献是智慧的源泉。(This usage is less common than the adjectival form)

例句：He considered his grandfather a fountful of good advice. 他认为他的祖父是良言的源泉。(Again, this usage is less common)
近义词：source, origin, fountainhead
反义词：end, conclusion
词性: noun (rare)
