emote:/ɪˈmoʊt/ (US) /ɪˈməʊt/ (UK)| v. n.|表达感情；感情流露；表情符号|To express emotion, especially in an excessive or theatrical way.  As a noun, it refers to an action or gesture intended to convey emotion, often in the context of online communication.|

例句：
He emoted excessively during his speech, waving his arms and shouting.
他在演讲时过度表达感情，挥舞着手臂大喊大叫。

例句：
She used a sad emote in her message to show her sympathy.
她在信息中使用了一个悲伤的表情符号来表达她的同情。

近义词：express, display, convey, show, perform, act, gesture|
反义词：suppress, conceal, hide, repress|
词性：verb, noun


词源：
From late 19th century: back-formation from emotion.
