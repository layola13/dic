letterer:/ˈlɛtərər/| n.|字体设计师，写字员，题字员|A person who draws or writes letters, especially for artistic purposes or in a particular style. |The letterer carefully inscribed each name on the trophy.  字体设计师小心翼翼地在奖杯上刻上每个名字。|Synonyms: calligrapher, scribe, inscriber, sign writer|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider "illiterate" in a very broad sense)|Noun
