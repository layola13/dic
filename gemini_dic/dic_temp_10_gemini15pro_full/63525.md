enunciative:/ɪˈnʌnsiətɪv/ /ɪˈnʌnʃiətɪv/
adjective
阐述的；表明的；宣言的
Serving to enunciate or declare.  Expressing something clearly and precisely.
The president delivered an enunciative speech outlining his plans for the next four years. 总统发表了一篇阐述未来四年计划的演讲。
The declaration was an enunciative statement of the country's independence.  这份宣言是国家独立的郑重声明。
declarative, assertive, expository, expressive
