misproposals:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzəlz/
noun
错误的提议；不当的建议
incorrect or inappropriate proposals
The committee rejected the misproposals due to their lack of feasibility. 委员会因为这些提议缺乏可行性而拒绝了它们。
Several misproposals were put forward, but thankfully they were all rejected.  几个不当的建议被提了出来，但谢天谢地，它们都被否决了。
synonyms: bad proposals, flawed proposals, unsuitable suggestions, inappropriate suggestions
antonyms:  good proposals, sound proposals, suitable suggestions, appropriate suggestions
n.
