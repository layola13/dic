maund:/mɔːnd/ | n. | 单位; 一大捆 | A unit of weight varying from place to place in South Asia, originally between 25 and 100 pounds.  A large basket or hamper. |  The merchant sold his goods by the maund. 商人按孟德出售他的货物。| The workers carried heavy maunds of cotton on their backs. 工人们背着沉重的棉花捆。 | synonyms: unit, measure, basket, hamper | antonyms: N/A | noun


maund:/mɔːnd/ | v. | 伤感; 沉思 | To feel or express discontent or low spirits.  | He maunded about his lost love for weeks. 他为他失去的爱伤感了好几个星期。| She sat maunding over her failures. 她坐着沉思自己的失败。| synonyms: mope, brood, sulk, grieve | antonyms: rejoice, celebrate | verb
