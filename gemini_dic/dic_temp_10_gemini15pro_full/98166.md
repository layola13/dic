inscribe:/ɪnˈskraɪb/| vt.|铭刻，题写，雕刻，内切，注册，登记|To write, carve, or mark (words, symbols, etc.) on something, especially as a formal or permanent record. To draw (a figure) within another so that their boundaries touch but do not intersect.|例句：He inscribed his name on the inside cover of the book. 他把他的名字题写在书的内封面上。

例句：The monument is inscribed with the names of the fallen soldiers.  纪念碑上刻着阵亡士兵的名字。


近义词：engrave, carve, write, imprint, etch|反义词:erase, delete|词性:verb
