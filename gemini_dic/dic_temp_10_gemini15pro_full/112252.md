loover:/ˈluːvər/| n.|百叶窗；散热窗；通风窗；天窗|A louver is a window blind or shutter with horizontal slats that are angled to admit light and air but to keep out rain and direct sunshine. |The louvers were closed to keep out the heat. 百叶窗关着以阻挡热气。|近义词: shutter, blind, vent|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid panel")|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *louvre*, from Old French *lovier*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French *lover*, meaning "skylight".
