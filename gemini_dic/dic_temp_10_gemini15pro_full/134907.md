nonmuscularly:/ˌnɒnˈmʌskjʊləli/ | adv. | 非肌肉地；不用肌肉地 | Without the use of muscles; in a way that doesn't involve muscular action. | The jellyfish moved nonmuscularly through the water, propelled by the current. 水母不依靠肌肉运动，而是顺着水流漂浮。|  近义词: passively, inertly | 反义词: muscularly, actively | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "muscular" (adjective meaning "relating to muscles") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Comes from Latin "musculus" (little mouse), as muscles were thought to resemble mice moving under the skin.
