coalescence:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛsəns/| n. |合并，联合，结合|The act or process of coming together to form one thing or system.|The coalescence of the two companies created a powerful new force in the market. 两家公司的合并创造了市场上的一股强大的新力量。|Synonyms: merger, union, combination, fusion|Antonyms: separation, division, split, disintegration|noun
