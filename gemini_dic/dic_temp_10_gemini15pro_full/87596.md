heterosyllabic:/ˌhetərəʊsɪˈlæbɪk/ /ˌhɛtərəsɪˈlæbɪk/| adj.|异音节的|Having syllables with different stress patterns or structures.|The word "photograph" is heterosyllabic, with stress on the first syllable.|近义词:  polysyllabic (in some contexts) |反义词: monosyllabic, isosyllabic|词性: adjective
Etymology:  From hetero- (different) + syllabic (relating to syllables).  Ultimately from Greek *héteros* (other, different) and *sullabé* (taken together).|词源
