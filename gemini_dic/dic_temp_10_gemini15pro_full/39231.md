congou:/ˈkɒŋɡuː/| n. |工夫茶，红茶的一种|A type of black tea, originally from China, that is carefully processed and has a delicate flavor.  It often refers to higher-quality black teas.|例句：She prefers a cup of congou in the afternoon. 她下午喜欢喝一杯工夫茶。|近义词：black tea, pekoe|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially green tea, herbal tea depending on context) |词性:noun
