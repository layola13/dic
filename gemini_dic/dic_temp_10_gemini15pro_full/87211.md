heredity:/həˈredɪti/ |n.|遗传，遗传性|The passing on of physical or mental characteristics genetically from one generation to another. |Eye color is determined by heredity. 眼睛的颜色是由遗传决定的。|Synonyms: inheritance, genetics, descent|Antonyms: environment, nurture|noun

heredity:/həˈredɪti/ |n.|遗传特征|The sum of the qualities and potentialities genetically derived from one's ancestors.|His musical talent comes from his heredity. 他的音乐天赋来自于他的遗传。|Synonyms: heritage, legacy, ancestry|Antonyms: acquired characteristics|noun
