nonadmission:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪʃən/ |/ˌnɑːnədˈmɪʃən/| n. |不承认；不接纳；拒绝入场|The nonadmission of guilt does not imply innocence. 不承认有罪并不意味着无辜。|例句：His nonadmission of the crime frustrated the police. 他不承认罪行让警方感到沮丧。|例句：The nonadmission policy of the club is very strict.  这家俱乐部的会员准入制度非常严格。|近义词：exclusion, rejection, denial, refusal|反义词：admission, acceptance, admittance|词性:名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + admission (from Latin "admissio," meaning "act of admitting")
