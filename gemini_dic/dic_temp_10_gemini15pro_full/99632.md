interlace:/ˌɪntərˈleɪs/ | v. n. | 交织，交错，使…交织 | To weave or twine together; intermingle. | The branches of the trees interlaced, forming a canopy overhead. 树枝交错，在头顶形成一个树冠。 |  Synonyms: intertwine, interweave, braid, plait | Antonyms: separate, disentangle, unravel | verb

interlace:/ˌɪntərˈleɪs/ | n. | 交织；交错 | The act of interlacing or the state of being interlaced. | The interlace of the threads created a beautiful pattern.  线的交织创造了一个美丽的图案。 | Synonyms: intertwining, interweaving, braiding, plaiting | Antonyms: separation, disentanglement, unraveling | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *entrelacer, from entre- (“between”) + lacer (“to lace, tie”).
