infixing:/ˈɪnˌfɪksɪŋ/| n.|中缀法，插入词缀|The process of inserting an affix within a word stem.  This is less common than prefixing or suffixing.|Fan-bloody-tastic is an example of infixing.|近义词:tmesis|反义词:|noun
