inherently:/ɪnˈhɛrəntli/ /ɪnˈhɪrəntli/ | adv. | 内在地；固有地；本质上地 | Existing as a natural or basic part of something. |  The work is inherently dangerous. 这项工作本身就很危险。| synonym: essentially, intrinsically, naturally, fundamentally | antonym: extrinsically, superficially | adverb
