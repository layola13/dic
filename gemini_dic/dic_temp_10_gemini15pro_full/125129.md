multi-lingual:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. | 多语的，使用多种语言的 | Able to speak or understand several languages. | She is a multi-lingual individual, fluent in English, French, and Spanish. 她是一个精通英语、法语和西班牙语的多语人士。

| Example: The hotel employs a multi-lingual staff to cater to its international clientele.  这家酒店雇佣了会说多种语言的员工来服务其国际客户。| Synonym: polyglot, multilingual | Antonym: monolingual | Adjective


Etymology:
From multi- + lingual.  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "many". "Lingual" comes from Latin "lingua" meaning "tongue, language".
