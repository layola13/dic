anacamptically:/ˌænəkæm(p)ˈtɪkli/ | adv. |回响地，反射地|  Relating to or characterized by anacamptics, the part of acoustics dealing with reflected sound, especially echoes.  It describes something that returns, bends back, or is reflected, often referring to sound or light. | The sound echoed anacamptically through the canyon. 声音回响地穿过峡谷。| 近义词: resonantly, reverberantly | 反义词: directly, straight | 词性: 副词
