calipering:/ˈkælɪpərɪŋ/
n.
卡尺测量;卡尺测量法
The act of measuring something with calipers.
The machinist checked the dimensions of the part by calipering it.  机械师用卡尺测量了零件的尺寸。

The doctor is calipering the patient's skin fold thickness. 医生正在用卡尺测量病人的皮肤皱褶厚度。

近义词:measuring, gauging
反义词:(None -  calipering is an action, not a quality with an opposite.)
词性:noun (gerund)
