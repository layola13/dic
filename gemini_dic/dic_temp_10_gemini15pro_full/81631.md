greed: /ɡriːd/ | n. | 贪婪，贪心 | An excessive desire to acquire or possess more than one needs, especially with respect to material wealth. |  He was consumed by greed and wanted all the power for himself. 他被贪婪吞噬，想要所有的权力归自己所有。

| Example:  Her greed for power led her to betray her friends. 她对权力的贪婪导致她背叛了她的朋友。

| Example: The corporate world is often driven by greed and profit. 商界往往受贪婪和利润的驱使。

| Synonym: avarice, covetousness, rapacity | Antonym: generosity, altruism, contentment | noun
