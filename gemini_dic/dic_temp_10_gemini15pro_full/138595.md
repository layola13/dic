nubile:/ˈnjuːbaɪl/ /ˈnuːbəl/ | adj. |  适婚的；性成熟的；青春美丽的 |  (of a young woman) sexually mature and attractive; suitable for marriage.  Refers primarily to young women who are considered physically attractive and ready for marriage, often with connotations of innocence and fertility. The word carries a sense of ripe youthfulness and readiness for procreation. |  The film featured several nubile young actresses. 这部电影里有几位青春靓丽的女演员。|  marriageable, eligible, youthful, attractive, blooming, ripe  |  unattractive, ineligible, old, infertile | adjective
