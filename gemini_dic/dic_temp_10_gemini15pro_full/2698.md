adroitnesses: /əˈdrɔɪtnəsɪz/
noun
熟练, 灵巧, 机敏
Skill and cleverness in handling difficult situations.
Her adroitnesses in handling the crisis averted a disaster. 她熟练地处理了危机，避免了一场灾难。
The diplomat's adroitnesses were key to the successful negotiations.  这位外交官的机智是谈判成功的关键。
近义词: dexterity, skill, proficiency, expertise, cleverness
反义词: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness
词性: noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "adresse" meaning skill, dexterity, from Latin "ad directus" meaning "straight towards," past participle of "dirigere" meaning "to direct, guide." The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "adroit" into the noun "adroitness."  "Adroitnesses" is the plural form.
