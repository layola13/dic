branding: /ˈbrændɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbrændɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|品牌化，品牌塑造|The process involved in creating a unique name and image for a product in the consumers' mind, through advertising campaigns with a consistent theme.  It aims to establish a significant and differentiated presence in the market that attracts and retains loyal customers.|Nike's branding is instantly recognizable worldwide. 耐克的品牌在世界范围内一眼就能认出来。|Synonyms: trademarking, labeling, naming |Antonyms: generic, unbranded|noun
