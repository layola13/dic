malefic:/məˈlɛfɪk/
adjective
有害的；恶毒的；不祥的
causing harm or evil; baleful
例句：The malefic influence of the dark wizard cast a shadow over the land. 黑暗巫师的邪恶影响给这片土地蒙上了一层阴影。

例句：He believed the comet was a malefic omen. 他认为彗星是不祥之兆。
近义词：evil, harmful, baleful, sinister, malevolent
反义词：beneficent, benevolent, auspicious, propitious
词性: adjective
