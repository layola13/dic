nonruminants:/nɒnˈruːmɪnənts/ | n. | 非反刍动物 | Animals that do not have a multi-compartment stomach system for digesting plant material by regurgitating and rechewing it. | Horses and pigs are nonruminants. 马和猪是非反刍动物。| Synonyms: monogastrics | Antonyms: ruminants | Noun

nonruminant:/nɒnˈruːmɪnənt/ | adj. | 非反刍的 | Relating to or characteristic of animals that do not chew the cud. |  A nonruminant digestive system is simpler than a ruminant's. 非反刍动物的消化系统比反刍动物的简单。 | Synonyms: monogastric | Antonyms: ruminant | Adjective
