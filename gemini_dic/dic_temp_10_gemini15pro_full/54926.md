disposited:/dɪˈspɒzɪtɪd/ | adj. | 已处置的，已安排的 |  Placed, arranged, or bestowed, especially by legal or official action.  Primarily used in legal and financial contexts.  While grammatically correct, it is archaic and rarely used in modern English. | The funds were duly disposited according to the will.  资金已根据遗嘱妥善处置。|  | allocated, assigned, distributed | obsolete, unused | adjective
