blowzed:/ˈblaʊzd/| adj.|脸色红润的，粗壮的，(尤指因饮酒或日晒)皮肤粗糙发红的|Having a ruddy complexion; flushed, typically from drinking alcohol or exposure to the sun.|例句：Her face was blowzed from the heat. 她的脸被晒得通红。

例句：He looked blowzed and dishevelled after a night of heavy drinking.  他喝了一夜的酒，看起来脸色红润，衣衫不整。|近义词：ruddy, flushed, florid, red-faced|反义词：pale, pallid|词性：形容词

词源: From Middle English *blowsen, from Old English *blōwan (to blow, bloom)
