neocolonization:/ˌniːoʊˌkɒlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |新殖民主义|The use of economic, political, cultural, or other pressures to control or influence other countries, especially former dependencies.|例句：The country accused the corporation of neocolonization through unfair trade practices.  这个国家指责这家公司通过不公平的贸易行为进行新殖民主义。

例句：Critics argue that international aid can sometimes be a tool of neocolonization. 批评人士认为，国际援助有时可能成为新殖民主义的工具。|近义词：imperialism, hegemony, dominance|反义词：independence, self-determination|词性:noun
