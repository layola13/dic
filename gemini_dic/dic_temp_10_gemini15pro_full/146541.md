overglaze: /ˈoʊvərɡleɪz/ | adj. n.|釉上彩；釉上彩的|A type of ceramic decoration applied over the glaze and fired at a lower temperature.|The artist used vibrant overglaze enamels to decorate the vase. 艺术家用鲜艳的釉上彩瓷釉装饰花瓶。|Synonyms: on-glaze|Antonyms: underglaze|adjective, noun

overglaze: /ˈoʊvərɡleɪz/ | v.|给…上釉上彩|To decorate pottery with colors applied over the glaze.|She overglazed the porcelain with a delicate floral pattern. 她用精致的花卉图案给瓷器上了釉上彩。|Synonyms: enamel|Antonyms: - |verb
