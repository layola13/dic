intermittent:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/| adj.|间断的，断断续续的|Occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous or steady.|The intermittent rain showers made it difficult to plan a picnic. 断断续续的阵雨使得难以计划野餐。|近义词：sporadic, occasional, irregular|反义词：continuous, constant, persistent|词性:形容词
词源：From Late Latin *intermittēns, present participle of intermittō (“I interrupt, intermit”), from inter (“between”) + mittō (“I send, let go”).
