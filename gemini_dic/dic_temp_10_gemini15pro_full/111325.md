live-high-on-the-hog:/lɪv haɪ ɒn ðə hɒɡ/ | idiom | 过奢侈的生活；挥霍；享受高质量的生活 | To live luxuriously and extravagantly, enjoying the best of everything.  |  They've been living high on the hog since they won the lottery.  他们自从中了彩票后就过着奢侈的生活。 |Synonyms: live it up, live in clover,  wallow in luxury | Antonyms: live frugally, live simply, scrimp and save | phrase

Etymology: This idiom originates from the fact that the upper parts of a hog, such as the loin and tenderloin, are considered the choicest cuts of meat. Thus, "living high on the hog" implies enjoying the best and most expensive things in life.
