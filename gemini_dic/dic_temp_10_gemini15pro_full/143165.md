outchiding:/ˌaʊtˈtʃaɪdɪŋ/
noun
斥责，责骂
the act of scolding or rebuking someone severely.
He received an outchiding from his boss for his tardiness. 他因为迟到受到了老板的严厉斥责。
rebuke, reprimand, scolding, reproval
praise, commendation

verb (present participle)
