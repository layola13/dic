machine-guns: /məˈʃiːn ɡʌnz/ | n. | 机枪 | A fully automatic gun that fires bullets rapidly and continuously as long as the trigger is pressed. | The soldiers used machine-guns to suppress the enemy fire. 士兵们用机枪压制敌人的火力。|  The sound of machine-guns echoed through the valley. 机枪的声音在山谷中回荡。 | synonyms: automatic weapons, rapid-fire guns | antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly single-shot rifles, pistols) | noun

machine-gun: /məˈʃiːn ɡʌn/ | v. | 用机枪扫射 | To fire upon with a machine gun. | The aircraft machine-gunned the enemy convoy. 飞机用机枪扫射了敌人的车队。|  They machine-gunned the building, leaving it riddled with bullets. 他们用机枪扫射了大楼，把它打得千疮百孔。 | synonyms: strafe, rake, pepper with bullets | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | verb
