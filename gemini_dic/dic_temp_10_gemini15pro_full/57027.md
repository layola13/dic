doubles:/ˈdʌblz/| n.|双打比赛；两份；加倍|A game between two pairs of players.|例句：They played doubles in the tennis tournament. 他们在网球锦标赛中打了双打。|近义词：pairs|反义词:singles|noun

doubles:/ˈdʌblz/| v.|加倍；是…的两倍；兼任|To become twice as much or as many.|例句：He doubled his investment in a year.  他在一年内将投资翻了一番。|近义词：increase, multiply|反义词:halve|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dobles, *doubles, from Latin duplus (“double”), from duo (“two”).
