octavalent:/ˌɒktəˈveɪlənt/ | adj. | 八价的；化合价为八的 | Having a valence of eight; capable of bonding with eight other atoms. |例句：Osmium is octavalent in osmium tetroxide. 锇在四氧化锇中是八价的。

例句：An octavalent element can form eight bonds with other atoms. 一个八价元素可以与其他原子形成八个键。|近义词：octovalent |反义词：monovalent, divalent, trivalent etc. |词性：形容词

Etymology: From Latin *octo* (eight) + *-valent* (having power). The suffix *-valent* derives from the Latin *valēns*, present participle of *valēre* (to be strong).
