painted:/ˈpeɪntɪd/| adj.|涂漆的，彩绘的|Covered with paint; having been painted. |The walls were painted a vibrant blue. 墙壁被漆成了鲜艳的蓝色。|The artist displayed her painted canvases. 艺术家展示了她画好的油画。|近义词：colored, dyed, tinted|反义词：unpainted, bare|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *peynted, *peynt, past participle of painten, from Old French peint, past participle of peindre (“to paint”), from Latin pingō (“I paint”).
