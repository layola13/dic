outfloat:/ˈaʊtfloʊt/
verb
漂浮在外;浮起;传播;散布
to float on the surface of a liquid or in the air; to spread or circulate
Example: The oil outfloated on the water.  油漂浮在水面上。

Example: Rumors outfloated quickly through the town. 谣言迅速在城里传播开来。
synonym: float, drift, circulate, spread
antonym: sink, submerge
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + float.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "outside" or "beyond." "Float" comes from Old English *flotian, from Proto-Germanic *flotan, from Proto-Indo-European *pleud-, meaning "to flow, float, swim."
