moonfish:/ˈmuːnfɪʃ/| n. |月鱼；翻车鱼|A deep-bodied, compressed, silvery fish (family Molidae) found in temperate and tropical seas. It is the heaviest known bony fish in the world. |例句：The moonfish drifted near the surface, basking in the sun. 月鱼漂浮在水面附近，沐浴在阳光下。

例句：We saw a huge moonfish while scuba diving. 我们在水肺潜水时看到了一条巨大的月鱼。|近义词：ocean sunfish, mola mola|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
etymology: moon + fish, so called from its rounded shape.
