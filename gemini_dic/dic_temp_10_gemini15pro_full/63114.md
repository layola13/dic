ensignship: /ˈɛnsɪnʃɪp/
n.
军旗职位;少尉的职位
The position or rank of an ensign.  The state of being an ensign.
He held the ensignship for two years before being promoted. 他担任少尉两年后晋升了。
近义词: ensigncy,  lieutenancy (for a higher rank)
反义词: (No direct opposite, could consider) admiralship, captaincy (for much higher ranks)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From ensign + -ship.  Ensign ultimately comes from Old French enseigne.
