monopneumona:/ˌmɒnəpnjuːˈməʊnɪə/ /ˌmɑːnəpnʊːˈmoʊnɪə/| n.|单肺感染，单侧肺炎|Inflammation of a single lung. This term is less common than "unilateral pneumonia," which means the same thing.|The patient presented with monopneumona affecting the right lung.  病人右肺出现单肺感染。|Synonym: unilateral pneumonia|Antonym: bilateral pneumonia|noun
