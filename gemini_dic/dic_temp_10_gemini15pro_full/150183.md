pancake:/ˈpænkeɪk/ (US), /ˈpænkeɪk/ (UK)| n.| 薄煎饼；烙饼|A thin, flat cake made from a batter and cooked on a hot griddle or frying pan.|I had pancakes for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了薄煎饼。|近义词：crepe, hotcake, griddlecake|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a thick cake or bread)|noun
