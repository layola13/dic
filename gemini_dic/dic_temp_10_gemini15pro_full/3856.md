agoraphobias:/ˌæɡərəˈfoʊbiəz/| n. |广场恐惧症；旷野恐惧症|An anxiety disorder characterized by a fear of being in situations or places where escape might be difficult or help might not be available in the event of developing panic-like symptoms or other embarrassing or incapacitating symptoms.|She suffers from agoraphobia and rarely leaves her house. 她患有广场恐惧症，很少出门。|近义词：anxiety, panic disorder|反义词:calm, composure|noun
