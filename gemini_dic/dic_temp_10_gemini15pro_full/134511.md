nonliable:/ˌnɒnˈlaɪəbl/| adj.|无责任的，无需承担责任的|Not legally responsible for something.|The company is nonliable for any damages caused by natural disasters.|近义词：irresponsible, unaccountable, exempt|反义词:liable, responsible|词性:形容词
词源:  From non- + liable, from Old French liable, from lier "to bind," from Latin ligare.
