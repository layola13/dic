bespoke:/bɪˈspoʊk/ (US), /bɪˈspəʊk/ (UK)| adj.|定制的，定做的|Made to order; custom-made.  Originally referring to clothes, but now extended to other products and services.|例句：He wore a bespoke suit. 他穿着一套定制西装。|例句：She commissioned a bespoke kitchen. 她定制了一个厨房。|近义词：custom-made, tailor-made, made-to-order, personalized|反义词：off-the-rack, ready-made, mass-produced|词性:形容词
词源: From the past participle of *bespeak*, meaning "to speak for, engage beforehand."  Originally used in the context of ordering clothes, indicating that the cloth had been "spoken for" by a particular customer.
