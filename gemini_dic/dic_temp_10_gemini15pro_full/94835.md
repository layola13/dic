imputableness:/ɪmˈpjuːtəbəlnəs/| n.|可归罪性，可归咎性|The quality or state of being imputable; accountability.  The fact that something can be attributed to a particular cause or source. |The imputableness of the crime to the defendant was the central issue of the trial.  这起罪行是否可归咎于被告是审判的核心问题。|Synonyms: accountability, responsibility, liability, culpability|Antonyms: innocence, blamelessness|noun
