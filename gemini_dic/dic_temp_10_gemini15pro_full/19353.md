bibliophilism:/ˌbɪbliəˈfɪlɪzəm/
n.
藏书癖；爱书癖
A love of books, especially for their physical attributes, such as binding and typeface.
He inherited his father's bibliophilism and now boasts a library of over 5,000 volumes. 他继承了父亲的藏书癖，现在拥有一个超过5000册的图书馆。

近义词:book-collecting, bibliomania
反义词:bibliophobia

词性:noun

Etymology:
From biblio- + -philism.  "Biblio-" comes from the Greek word "biblion" meaning "book." "-philism" comes from the Greek word "philos," meaning "loving."
