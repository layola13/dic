disjoint:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt/ | adj. |不相交的，互斥的 | Having no elements in common; separated. disjoint:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt/ | vt. | 切断，使分离 | To separate or disconnect. | The editor disjointed the narrative by cutting out key scenes.  剪辑师通过剪掉关键场景使叙事脱节。 | The accident disjointed the train cars. 事故使火车车厢脱节。 | near synonyms: separate, disconnect, sever, disunite | near antonyms: connect, join, unite, link | verb
