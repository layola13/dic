nonparental:/ˌnɑːn.pəˈren.təl/ | adj. | 非父母的；非亲本的 | Not relating to or provided by a parent or parents.  Relating to the raising of children by someone other than a biological or adoptive parent. |  Nonparental childcare is becoming increasingly common.  非父母的儿童保育正变得越来越普遍。

The child received nonparental care from her grandmother.  这个孩子接受了来自她祖母的非亲本照顾。 | Synonyms: non-familial, extrafamilial | Antonyms: parental, maternal, paternal | adjective
