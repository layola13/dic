highland:/ˈhaɪlənd/| n. adj.| 高地，高原；苏格兰高地；高地居民|A mountainous region of a country; often used to refer to the Scottish Highlands.  Often used as an adjective to describe things relating to or characteristic of such a region.|例句：The Scottish Highlands are known for their rugged beauty. 苏格兰高地以其崎岖的美丽而闻名。|例句：He wore traditional Highland dress. 他穿着传统的苏格兰高地服饰。|近义词：mountain, upland, plateau|反义词:lowland|词性:名词/形容词
