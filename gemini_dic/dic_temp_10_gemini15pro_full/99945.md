internucleotide:/ˌɪntərˈnjuːkliəˌtaɪd/ | adj. |核苷酸间的| Situated between nucleotides; describing or relating to the bonds or interactions between nucleotides within a nucleic acid chain. | The internucleotide bonds in DNA are phosphodiester bonds.
