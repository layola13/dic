panpsychist: /pænˈsaɪkɪst/ | n. | 泛心论者 | A person who believes in panpsychism, the doctrine that mind is a fundamental and ubiquitous feature of reality and is present in all things. |  David Chalmers, while not strictly a panpsychist, has explored ideas sympathetic to panpsychism. 大卫·查尔默斯虽然并非严格意义上的泛心论者，但他探索了一些与泛心论相似的观点。

| Example: Some panpsychists believe that even inanimate objects possess a rudimentary form of consciousness. 一些泛心论者认为，即使是无生命物体也拥有某种初级的意识。| Near synonyms:  idealist,  mentalist | Antonym: materialist, physicalist | noun
