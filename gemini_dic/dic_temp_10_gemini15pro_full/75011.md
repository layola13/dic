friendlier:/ˈfrɛndliər/ | adj. | 更友好的，更亲切的 | Comparative form of "friendly," meaning showing kindness and a pleasant disposition. | She is friendlier than her sister. 她比她姐姐更友好。| My new neighbor is much friendlier than the previous one. 我的新邻居比之前的邻居友好多了。 | 近义词：more amicable, more affable, more cordial | 反义词：less friendly, more hostile, more aloof | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English *frēondlīc (friendly), derived from frēond (friend). The "-lier" ending is the comparative suffix.
