loral:/ˈlɔːrəl/| adj.|月桂的，桂冠的，胜利的|Relating to laurels or a laurel wreath, especially as an emblem of victory or distinction. |The loral wreath was placed on the victor's head. 桂冠被戴在了胜利者的头上。|近义词：triumphant, victorious, winning|反义词:defeated, losing|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *laurealis*, from laurus (“laurel”).
