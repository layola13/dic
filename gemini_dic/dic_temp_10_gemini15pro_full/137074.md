nonstriking:/nɒnˈstraɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 非罢工的，不引人注目的 | Not striking; not participating in a strike; not attracting attention. |  The nonstriking workers continued to operate the factory. 非罢工的工人继续经营工厂。 |  The company implemented a policy for nonstriking employees. 公司对非罢工员工实施了一项政策。 | 近义词: unnoticeable, inconspicuous, unexceptional | 反义词: striking, noticeable, conspicuous | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + striking

non-:  prefix meaning "not," from Old French non- and directly from Latin non "not."

striking: present participle of strike (v.) "to hit," from Old English *strican (implied in bestrican "to rub over, stroke").
