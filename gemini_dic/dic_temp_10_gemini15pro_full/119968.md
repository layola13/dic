millennium:/mɪˈlɛniəm/ (US), /mɪˈlɛniəm/ (UK)| n.| 千年，千禧年|A period of one thousand years, especially as calculated from the traditional date of the birth of Christ.|例句：The new millennium began on January 1, 2001.  新的千年始于2001年1月1日。

例句：We are living in the third millennium. 我们生活在第三个千年。

近义词：chiliad|反义词:|词性:名词
