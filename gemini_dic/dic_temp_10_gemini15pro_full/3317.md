affluxion:/əˈflʌkʃən/
noun
流入；涌入；充血
a flowing or pouring in of something, especially blood to a part of the body
The doctor diagnosed the redness in his eye as an affluxion of blood. 医生诊断他眼睛里的红肿是充血。
influx, rush, flow
efflux, outflow
