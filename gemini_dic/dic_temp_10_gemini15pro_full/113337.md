luxuriant:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/ | adj. | 茂盛的，繁茂的；丰富的，奢华的 | (of vegetation) growing thickly and strongly;  (of something provided) plentiful;  rich and elaborate or luxurious. |  The garden boasts luxuriant vegetation.  这个花园拥有茂盛的植被。


The hotel offers luxuriant accommodations. 这家酒店提供豪华的住宿。 | 近义词：lush, abundant, opulent, lavish | 反义词：sparse, meager, barren, sterile | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *luxuriant- (stem of luxurians), present participle of luxuriare ‘to be luxuriant, to luxuriate,’ from Latin luxus ‘excess, luxury’ + -iare (suffix forming verbs).
