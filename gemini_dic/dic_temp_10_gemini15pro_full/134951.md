nonnarcotics:/ˌnɑːnɑːrˈkɑːtɪks/ | n. | 非麻醉药; 非麻醉性镇痛药 |  Medications that do not induce narcosis (a state of stupor, unconsciousness, or insensibility).  They relieve pain without causing a loss of consciousness. | The doctor prescribed nonnarcotics for her mild back pain. 医生给她开了非麻醉性镇痛药来治疗轻微的背痛。|  The patient requested nonnarcotics pain relief after surgery. 病人要求术后使用非麻醉性镇痛药。 | synonyms:  analgesics, pain relievers | antonyms: narcotics, opiates | noun
