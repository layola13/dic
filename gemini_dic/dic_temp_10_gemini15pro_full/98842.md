intelligibleness:/ɪnˌtɛlɪdʒəˈbɪlnəs/| n. |可理解性，清晰度|The quality of being intelligible; understandability.  The clarity or lucidity of something, especially speech or writing.|His explanation was remarkable for its intelligibleness. 他的解释以其清晰易懂而著称。|Synonyms: clarity, lucidity, comprehensibility, perspicuity, plainness|Antonyms: unintelligibility, incomprehensibility, obscurity, vagueness|noun
