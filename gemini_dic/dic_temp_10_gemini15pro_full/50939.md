deciduous: /dɪˈsɪdʒʊəs/ | adj. | 落叶的，脱落的 |  Shedding leaves annually, as certain trees and shrubs.  Falling off or shed at a specific season or stage of growth. | Example 1:  The maple tree in my yard is deciduous, so it loses its leaves every fall.  我家院子里的枫树是落叶树，所以每年秋天都会落叶。

Example 2: Deer antlers are deciduous, being shed and regrown annually. 鹿角是脱落的，每年都会脱落并重新生长。 | Synonym:  temporary, transient, ephemeral | Antonym: evergreen, persistent, perennial | word type: adjective
