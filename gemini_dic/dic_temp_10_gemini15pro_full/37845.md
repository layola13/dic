competition:/ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmpəˈtɪʃn/ (US)| n.| 竞争，比赛|A situation in which someone is trying to win something or be more successful than someone else.|The competition for the job was fierce. 这份工作的竞争非常激烈.|synonyms: rivalry, contest, match, tournament|antonyms: cooperation, collaboration|noun

competition:/ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmpəˈtɪʃn/ (US)| n.| 竞争者，参赛者|A person, team, company, etc. that is competing with another.|All the competitors in the race were very fit. 所有参赛者都非常健康.|synonyms: rival, contestant, participant|antonyms: partner, ally|noun
