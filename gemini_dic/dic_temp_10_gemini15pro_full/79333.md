glebous:/ˈɡliːbəs/| adj.|球状的，圆块状的|Resembling a clod or lump; rounded and somewhat massive.  Referring to something that is round and bulky, like a clod of earth.|The glebous moon hung heavy in the sky.  圆块状的月亮沉甸甸地挂在空中。|Near synonyms:  globular, rounded, lumpy, clod-like|Near antonyms:  angular, pointed, flat|adjective
glebous:/ˈɡliːbəs/| adj.|（真菌）有菌盖和菌柄的|Of fungi: Having both a cap and a stipe (stalk).|Many edible mushrooms are glebous. 许多可食用的蘑菇都有菌盖和菌柄。|Near synonyms:  pileate, stipitate|Near antonyms:  sessile (lacking a stalk)|adjective
