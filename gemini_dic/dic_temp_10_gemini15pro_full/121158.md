misdevelops:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvɛləps/
verb
发展不良，发育不全
To develop poorly or incorrectly; to fail to develop properly.
The child misdeveloped due to malnutrition.  由于营养不良，孩子发育不良。
The plant misdeveloped because it didn't get enough sunlight.  由于没有得到足够的阳光，植物发育不良。
近义词:maldevelop, atrophy, degenerate
反义词:develop, flourish, thrive
词性:v.
