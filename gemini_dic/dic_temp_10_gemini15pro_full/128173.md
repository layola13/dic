networkers:/ˈnɛtwɜːrkərz/
n.
人脉拓展者，建立人际关系者
People who actively build and maintain professional relationships for career advancement or business opportunities.
She's a natural networker, always making connections and expanding her circle of contacts. 她天生擅长人脉拓展，总是能建立联系并扩大自己的人脉圈。
近义词：connectors, socializers
反义词：loners, introverts
词性：名词


networkers:/ˈnɛtwɜːrkərz/
n.
网络工作者，网络技术人员
People who work in or with computer networks.
The company is looking for experienced networkers to manage its growing IT infrastructure.  这家公司正在寻找经验丰富的网络工作者来管理其不断增长的IT基础设施。
近义词：network administrators, network engineers, IT professionals
反义词：N/A
词性：名词


词源:
From network + -er (agent suffix).  "network" itself comes from "net" (meaning a woven fabric) and "work" (meaning labor or structure).
