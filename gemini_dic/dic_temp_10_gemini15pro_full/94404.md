PHRASE->
be implicated in = 参与，与…有关
become implicated in = 被卷入，被牵连进
implicate someone in something =  指证某人参与某事
strongly implicate = 强烈暗示
implicate a gene in a disease = 证明某个基因与某种疾病有关

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin implicat- ‘entangled, involved’, past participle of implicare, from in- ‘in, into’ + plicare ‘to fold’.
