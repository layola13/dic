calorimeters:/ˌkæləˈrɪmɪtərz/
n.
热量计；卡计
An apparatus for measuring the amount of heat involved in a chemical reaction or other process.
The scientists used a calorimeter to measure the heat produced by the reaction.  科学家们用热量计测量反应产生的热量。

The heat capacity of the sample was determined using a differential scanning calorimeter.  使用差示扫描量热仪测定了样品的热容。

近义词:  heat meter
反义词:  N/A
词性:  noun (plural)

calorimeter:/ˌkæləˈrɪmɪtər/
n.
热量计；卡计

The calorimeter measured the heat released during the combustion process.  热量计测量了燃烧过程中释放的热量。
