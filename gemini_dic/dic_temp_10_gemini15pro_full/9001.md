antilithic:/ˌæntiˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. | 抗结石的，溶石的 | Preventing or counteracting the formation of calculi (stones) in the body, especially in the kidneys or gallbladder. | This medication has antilithic properties and helps dissolve kidney stones. 这种药物具有抗结石特性，有助于溶解肾结石。 |  Example 2: Drinking plenty of water is believed to have an antilithic effect. 多喝水被认为具有抗结石的作用。 | synonyms: litholytic, calculus-dissolving | antonyms: lithogenic | adjective
