leisurewear: /ˈliːʒəweər/ | n. | 休闲服，运动服 | Clothing designed for leisure activities, typically comfortable and casual. |  Example: She packed her leisurewear for the weekend trip to the mountains. 她打包了休闲服去山上度周末。

Example:  He prefers leisurewear to formal attire.  比起正式服装，他更喜欢休闲服。


近义词: sportswear, casual wear, activewear | 反义词: formal wear, business attire | 词性: noun
