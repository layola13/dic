crinet: /kraɪˈnet/ | n. | 马鬃，马尾毛 | Horsehair, especially from the mane or tail. | The knight's helmet was adorned with a flowing crinet. 骑士的头盔上装饰着飘逸的马鬃。 | 近义词: horsehair, mane, tail | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French "crinet" (little horsehair), diminutive of "crin" (horsehair), from Latin "crinis" (hair).
