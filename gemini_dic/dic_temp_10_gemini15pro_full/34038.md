citrus: /ˈsɪtrəs/ | n. adj.| 柑橘属植物；柑橘类的 | Any of various evergreen trees of the genus Citrus, bearing pulpy fruits with thick rinds, such as the orange, lemon, grapefruit, and lime.  |  Example: I love the scent of citrus blossoms. 我喜欢柑橘花的香味。

Example:  Citrus fruits are rich in Vitamin C. 柑橘类水果富含维生素C。 | 近义词:  Rutaceae (芸香科植物) | 反义词: None (没有明确的反义词) | 词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Latin *citrus*, of unknown origin, perhaps borrowed from an unknown Mediterranean language. Ultimately related to cedar.
