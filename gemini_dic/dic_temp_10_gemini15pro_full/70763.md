finespun:/ˈfaɪnspʌn/| adj.|精细的；细微的；微妙的；(论证)过于精巧的，牵强的|Characterized by extreme delicacy or subtlety of texture, construction, or thought; overrefined or excessively subtle in argument or reasoning.|The lawyer's finespun argument failed to convince the jury. 律师细致入微的论证没能说服陪审团。|近义词：subtle, delicate, refined, intricate, elaborate, nuanced|反义词：coarse, crude, simple, obvious, straightforward|词性:形容词
词源: From fine + spun, past participle of spin.  Meaning literally "finely spun," hence "delicate" or "subtle."
