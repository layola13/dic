nonextinguishable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
adjective
不可熄灭的; 无法扑灭的
Not able to be extinguished.
The fire proved nonextinguishable, despite the firefighters' best efforts.  尽管消防员尽了最大的努力，但火势仍无法扑灭。
The human spirit is nonextinguishable. 人类的精神是不可磨灭的。
Near Synonyms: unquenchable, inextinguishable
Antonyms: extinguishable, quenchable
词性: adjective
