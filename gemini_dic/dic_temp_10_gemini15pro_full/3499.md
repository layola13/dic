afternoons:/ˌɑːftərˈnuːnz/
n.
下午；午后
the part of the day between noon and evening.
We spent our afternoons swimming and sunbathing. 我们下午游泳和晒太阳。

近义词:post meridian (p.m.)
反义词:mornings

词性:名词(plural noun)
