loam:/loʊm/ | n. v. |壤土，肥土；用肥土覆盖|A rich, friable soil containing a relatively equal mixture of sand and silt and a somewhat smaller proportion of clay.  To cover or enrich with loam. |The gardener enriched the soil with loam. 园丁用肥土改良了土壤。|近义词：soil, earth, dirt|反义词: - |n. v.

loaming:/ˈloʊmɪŋ/ | n. |漫游，闲逛|The act of roaming or wandering, especially in a leisurely or aimless way.  Specifically, roaming over or through loam. |He spent the afternoon loaming through the fields. 他整个下午都在田野里闲逛。|近义词：wandering, roaming, strolling|反义词: - |n.
