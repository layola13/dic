enlivened:/ɪnˈlaɪvənd/
verb | 使活跃，使生动，使有生气 |  The music enlivened the party.  音乐使派对活跃起来。| 近义词: animated, invigorated, stimulated | 反义词: subdued, dulled, deadened
adjective | 活跃的，生动的，有生气的 | The lively discussion enlivened the meeting.  热烈的讨论使会议生动起来。 | 近义词: lively, animated, spirited | 反义词: dull, lifeless, boring
词性: 过去分词 (Past Participle) -  用作形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (make, cause to be) + liven (to make lively) + -ed (past participle suffix).  Ultimately from Old English libban "to live."
