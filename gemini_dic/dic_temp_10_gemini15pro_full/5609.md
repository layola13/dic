alternate:/ˈɔːltərneɪt/ (US) /ˈɒltəneɪt/ (UK)| adj. v.| 交替的，轮流的；交替，轮流 | Happening or following one after the other regularly. | The leaves on the stem are alternate. 茎上的叶子是互生的。|  He alternates between hope and despair. 他时而充满希望，时而感到绝望。 | 近义词：alternative, rotating, successive | 反义词: continuous, constant | 词性: 形容词, 动词

alternate:/ˈɔːltərnət/ (US) /ˈɒltərnət/ (UK)| n.| 交替的事物；候补人员 | A person acting in place of another; a substitute. |  She served as an alternate on the committee. 她在委员会中担任候补委员。 | 近义词：substitute, replacement, deputy | 反义词: primary, principal | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alternatus*, past participle of *alternare* "to do by turns," from *alternus* "one after the other," related to *alter* "other."
