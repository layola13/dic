nonseclusion:/ˌnɒn.sɪˈkluːʒən/
n.
非隔离; 不隔离; 非隐居
The state of not being secluded or isolated.
The hospital promotes nonseclusion policies for patients whenever possible.  这家医院尽可能地推行对病人的非隔离政策。
Patients benefit from the nonseclusion policy, as it promotes social interaction. 病人受益于非隔离政策，因为它促进了社会交往。

近义词: integration, inclusion, socialization
反义词: seclusion, isolation, segregation
词性: noun
