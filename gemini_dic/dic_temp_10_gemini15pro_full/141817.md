orbiters:/ˈɔːrbɪtərz/| n.|轨道飞行器；绕轨运行的航天器|A spacecraft designed to orbit a celestial body, such as a planet or moon, without landing on it.  These spacecraft can be used for a variety of purposes, including scientific observation, communication, and navigation.|The orbiters sent images back to Earth. 轨道飞行器将图像发送回地球。|The two orbiters will study the Martian atmosphere. 这两颗轨道飞行器将研究火星大气层。|近义词：satellite, spacecraft|反义词:|noun
