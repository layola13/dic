overpunishes: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃɪz/
verb
过度惩罚
To punish excessively or too harshly.
The school overpunishes students for minor infractions. 学校对轻微违规行为过度惩罚学生。
He felt that the judge overpunished him for a simple mistake. 他觉得法官对一个简单的错误过度惩罚了他。
