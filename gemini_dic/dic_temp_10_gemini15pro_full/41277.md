cordial:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl/ /ˈkɔːrdiəl/| adj. n.|热情友好的；兴奋的；强烈的；甜点酒|Warm and friendly; stimulating; invigorating; a liqueur|例句：She gave us a cordial welcome. 她热情地欢迎我们。|近义词：warm, friendly, affable, genial, hearty, welcoming|反义词：cold, unfriendly, hostile, aloof|词性:形容词

cordial:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl/ /ˈkɔːrdiəl/| n.|甜点酒；兴奋剂|A stimulating medicine or drink; a liqueur|例句：After dinner, they served a delicious cordial.  晚饭后，他们端上了一杯美味的甜酒。|近义词：liqueur, tonic|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑beverage)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cordial*, from Late Latin *cordiālis* "of or for the heart," from Latin *cor* "heart."  The sense of "stimulating" arose from the belief that such drinks revived the heart.
