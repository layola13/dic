legitimizers:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzərz/
n.
合法化者；赋予合法性的人或事物
People or things that make something legitimate or acceptable.
The new law served as a legitimizer for the government's actions.  新的法律使政府的行为合法化。
Synonyms: validators, ratifiers, authorizers
Antonyms: delegitimizers, invalidators
词性: 名词

legitimizer:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzər/
