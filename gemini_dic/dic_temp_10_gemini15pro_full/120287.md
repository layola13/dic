miniaturizes:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəraɪzɪz/
verb
使小型化; 使微型化
To make something much smaller in size.
Engineers miniaturized the device, making it small enough to fit inside a watch. 工程师们将该设备小型化，使其小到足以装入手表内。
miniaturize the components 将组件小型化
synonyms: shrink, reduce, compress, condense
antonyms: enlarge, expand, magnify
词性: 动词
