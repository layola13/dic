diatomous:/ˌdaɪəˈteɪməs/
adjective
硅藻的；由硅藻土构成的
Relating to or consisting of diatoms (microscopic algae with cell walls of silica).
Diatomaceous earth is a common filtering medium. 硅藻土是一种常见的过滤介质。
The diatomous ooze on the seafloor is rich in silica. 海底的硅藻软泥富含二氧化硅。
近义词:siliceous
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: adjective
词源: From diatom + -ous. Diatom from dia- 'through' + tom- 'cut' (because of the markings on the cell walls).
