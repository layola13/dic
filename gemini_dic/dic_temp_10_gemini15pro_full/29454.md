catalysed:/ˈkætəlaɪzd/ | v. |催化的；被催化的 | Caused or accelerated by a catalyst. | The reaction was catalysed by the presence of iron. 该反应由铁的存在催化。|  The decomposition of hydrogen peroxide is catalysed by manganese dioxide. 过氧化氢的分解由二氧化锰催化。 | synonyms: accelerated, facilitated, expedited, promoted | antonyms: inhibited, retarded, hindered | verb (past participle)

catalyze:/ˈkætəlaɪz/ | v. |催化 | To cause or accelerate (a reaction) by acting as a catalyst. |  Platinum catalyses the reaction between hydrogen and oxygen. 铂催化氢和氧之间的反应。 | Enzymes catalyze biochemical reactions. 酶催化生化反应。| synonyms: accelerate, facilitate, expedite, promote | antonyms: inhibit, retard, hinder | verb


词源: From French *catalyser*, from Greek *katalýein* "to dissolve, loosen down," from *kata-* "down" + *lýein* "to loosen."
