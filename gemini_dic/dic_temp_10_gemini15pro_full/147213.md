overmast:/ˌoʊvərˈmæst/ | adj. | 过高的，过度的 | Excessively tall or high; too much. | The ship's overmast spars made it difficult to maneuver in tight spaces. 这艘船过高的桅杆使它难以在狭窄的空间内操纵。| synonyms: excessive, exorbitant, immoderate | antonyms: moderate, reasonable, appropriate | adjective


overmast:/ˌoʊvərˈmæst/ | v. | 装备过高的桅杆，使过度 | To equip with or carry excessively tall masts; to overburden. | They overmasted the small yacht, causing it to capsize in the storm. 他们在小型游艇上装了过高的桅杆，导致它在暴风雨中倾覆。 | synonyms: overload, overburden | antonyms: underload, lighten | verb
