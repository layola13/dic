bobbinet:/ˈbɒbɪnet/ (UK), /ˈbɑːbɪnet/ (US)| n. |薄纱，六角网眼纱|A delicate, machine-made net fabric with a hexagonal mesh, typically used for curtains, veils, and trimming.|例句：The bride wore a veil of fine bobbinet.  新娘戴着精致的薄纱面纱。

例句：The curtains were made of a shimmering bobbinet. 窗帘是用闪闪发光的薄纱制成的。

近义词：tulle, netting, mesh|反义词: None|词性: noun
