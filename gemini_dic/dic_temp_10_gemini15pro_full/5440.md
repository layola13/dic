almery:/ˈɑːlməri/ | n. | 碗橱，食橱 | A cupboard or cabinet, especially one for storing food or dishes. |  The old almery held her grandmother's china.  老碗橱里放着她祖母的瓷器。 | 近义词: cupboard, cabinet, pantry | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *almarie, from Latin armarium "closet, chest, cupboard," neuter of armarius "of or belonging to arms, tools, implements," from arma "arms, tools, implements."
