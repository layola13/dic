frights:/fraɪts/
n.
惊吓；恐惧
Sudden, brief experiences of fear.
The movie was full of frights and jump scares. 这部电影充满了惊吓和突如其来的恐怖场景。
近义词：scares, terrors, alarms
反义词：calm, peace, tranquility
词性: 名词(plural)

frights:/fraɪts/
n.
令人惊恐的事物
Things that cause fear or alarm.
Haunted houses are popular attractions for those who enjoy frights. 鬼屋是那些喜欢惊吓的人们喜爱的景点。
近义词：horrors, nightmares
反义词：comforts, pleasures
词性: 名词(plural)


词源：From Middle English *frightes, plural of *fright, from Old English *fryċto, *fyrhto (“fright, fear, terror”), from Proto-Germanic *furhtō (“fear”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰergʰ- (“high; to rise up”). Cognate with Dutch vrees (“fear”), German Furcht (“fear”), Icelandic ótti.
