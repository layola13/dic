immanation:/ˌɪməˈneɪʃən/
n.
内在化，体现，渗透
The process of becoming inherent or immanent; the state of being inherent or immanent.  A manifestation or embodiment of a divine being or abstract quality.
The artist's work is an immanation of her creative spirit.  艺术家的作品是她创作精神的体现。
Her kindness is an immanation of her generous nature. 她的善良是她慷慨天性的体现。
近义词: embodiment, incarnation, manifestation, personification, representation
反义词: externalization, separation, detachment
