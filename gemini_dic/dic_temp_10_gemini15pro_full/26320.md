buyin's:/ˈbaɪɪnz/| n. |支持，同意，投入，入股|Agreement or acceptance of something, often involving commitment or investment.| Example:  The CEO needed buyin's from the board before proceeding with the acquisition. 首席执行官在进行收购之前需要董事会的支持。| Example:  Getting buyin's from the employees on the new policy was crucial.  让员工接受新政策至关重要。| 近义词: support, agreement, acceptance, commitment, investment | 反义词: opposition, disagreement, rejection | 词性: 名词

词源：buyin's 是 buy-in 的复数形式，buy-in 源于动词短语 buy in，意思是“买入；加入；赞同”。  随着时间的推移，它演变成了名词 buy-in，表示“同意，支持”。 buyin's 则较少使用，通常在非正式场合表示多方或多方面的支持。
