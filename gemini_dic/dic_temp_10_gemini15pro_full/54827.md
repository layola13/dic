dispersion:/dɪˈspɜːrʒən/ | n. | 分散，散布，传播；离差，色散 | The dispersion of seeds is essential for plant reproduction.  种子的散布对植物繁殖至关重要。 | The dispersion of light through a prism creates a rainbow. 光线通过棱镜的色散形成了彩虹。 |  近义词：scattering, dissemination, distribution, spread | 反义词：concentration, gathering, collection | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dispersio, from Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere “to scatter,” from dis- “apart” + spargere “to scatter.”
