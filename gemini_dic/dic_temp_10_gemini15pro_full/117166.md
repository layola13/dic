meanwhile:/ˈmiːnˌwaɪl/| adv.|与此同时；在此期间|A period of time between two events or during which something else is happening.|The doctor will be here soon; meanwhile, try to relax. 医生很快就会来，在此期间尽量放松一下。|Synonyms: in the meantime, at the same time, concurrently|Antonyms: afterward, subsequently|adverb
