nonacculturated: /ˌnɒnəˈkʌltʃəreɪtɪd/ (British) /ˌnɑːnəˈkʌltʃəreɪtɪd/ (American)| adj.|未适应文化的，未融入文化的|Not having assimilated to a different culture; not acculturated.  Referring to a person or group that has not adopted the cultural norms and practices of a dominant or different society.|Many recent immigrants remain nonacculturated to the local customs.|Synonyms: unassimilated, unaccustomed, unadapted|Antonyms: acculturated, assimilated, integrated|Adjective
