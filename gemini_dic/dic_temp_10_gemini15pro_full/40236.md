contiguate:/kənˈtɪɡjʊeɪt/
verb
使…邻接；使…连接
to make contiguous; to bring into contact; to join together
The two properties are contiguated. 这两处房产是相连的。
近义词: abut, adjoin, border, join, connect, link, touch
反义词: separate, divide, disconnect
词性: verb

词源: From Latin contiguatus, past participle of contiguare (“to touch on all sides”), from contiguus (“touching”).
