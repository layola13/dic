by-product:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 | something produced in the making of something else; a secondary result | The by-product of oil refining is gasoline.  石油精炼的副产品是汽油。| synonyms: side effect, spin-off, consequence | antonyms: main product, primary product | noun

by-product:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 |  a secondary or incidental product, activity, or result, especially in an industrial process. |  One of the by-products of unemployment is an increase in crime. 失业的一个副作用是犯罪率的上升。| synonyms:  side effect, spin-off, offshoot, ancillary product | antonyms: main product, primary product | noun
