nonbituminous:/ˌnɑːnbɪˈtuːmɪnəs/
adjective
非烟煤的
Not containing or yielding bitumen.
Nonbituminous coal does not produce volatile hydrocarbons when heated and burns cleanly. 非烟煤加热时不产生挥发性碳氢化合物，燃烧清洁。
These nonbituminous coals are generally low in sulfur. 这些非烟煤的硫含量通常较低。
Synonyms: anthracite, semi-anthracite
Antonyms: bituminous
词性: adjective
