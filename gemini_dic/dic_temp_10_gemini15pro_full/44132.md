crisscrossing:/ˈkrɪsˌkrɒsɪŋ/ /ˈkrɪsˌkrɔːsɪŋ/
verb (present participle) | 交叉；穿梭往来 | moving back and forth or across something repeatedly |  The city is crisscrossed with canals. 这座城市河道纵横交错。| intersecting, traversing, zigzagging | parallel | v.
crisscrossing:/ˈkrɪsˌkrɒsɪŋ/ /ˈkrɪsˌkrɔːsɪŋ/
adjective | 交叉的；纵横交错的 | arranged in a pattern of crossing lines |  A network of crisscrossing footpaths covered the hillside.  纵横交错的小径覆盖了山坡。| intersecting, interwoven, network | parallel | adj.
crisscrossing:/ˈkrɪsˌkrɒsɪŋ/ /ˈkrɪsˌkrɔːsɪŋ/
noun | 交叉；十字形 | the action of crossing something or the state of being crossed | The crisscrossing of the wires caused a short circuit. 电线的交叉导致了短路。| intersection, crossing, network | separation | n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From crisscross (adv. & adj.) + -ing.  Crisscross itself comes from the reduplication of cross.
