before:/bɪˈfɔːr/ /bɪˈfɔːɹ/| prep. adv. conj.|在...之前，以前|Indicates something happened or existed earlier in time or order. |I arrived before him. 我在他之前到达。|近义词：prior to, earlier than, ahead of, previously|反义词:after, subsequently|词性:介词, 副词, 连词

before:/bɪˈfɔːr/ /bɪˈfɔːɹ/| conj.|在...之前|Introduces a clause expressing a time or event preceding another. |Before I go, I need to finish this. 在我走之前，我需要完成这个。|近义词：prior to, until|反义词:after|词性:连词

before:/bɪˈfɔːr/ /bɪˈfɔːɹ/| adv.|以前，从前|At an earlier time. |I've never seen this before. 我以前从未见过这个。|近义词：formerly, previously, earlier|反义词:afterwards, later|词性:副词
