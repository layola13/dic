insulation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 隔热；绝缘；隔离；隔音材料 | The house has good insulation, so it stays warm in winter. 这房子隔热性能好，所以在冬天能保持温暖。| The insulation on the electrical wires is damaged. 电线的绝缘层损坏了。 | 近义词:isolation, lagging, padding | 反义词: conduction, connection | 词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insulatio, from Latin insulare “to make into an island, isolate,” from insula “island.”
