bounce-around:/ˌbaʊns əˈraʊnd/ | phrasal verb | 到处走动；四处游荡；反复思考 | To travel from place to place, often without a fixed plan; to consider different ideas or options. |  I bounced around Europe for a few months after college.  大学毕业后，我在欧洲各地游荡了几个月。

| | | |  We've been bouncing around some ideas for the new marketing campaign.  我们一直在反复思考新营销活动的一些想法。


| 近义词: travel around, wander, roam, kick around (ideas) | 反义词: stay put, settle down | verb phrase


bounce-around (ideas):/ˌbaʊns əˈraʊnd/ | phrasal verb |  反复思考；斟酌；讨论 | To discuss ideas informally with other people, in order to get their opinions. | We bounced around a few ideas for the new project. 我们就新项目的一些想法进行了讨论。

| | | |  Let's bounce this idea around and see what everyone thinks.  让我们把这个想法提出来讨论一下，看看大家怎么想。

| 近义词: discuss, brainstorm, kick around | 反义词: decide, finalize | verb phrase
