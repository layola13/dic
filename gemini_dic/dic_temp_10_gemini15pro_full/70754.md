finely:/ˈfaɪnli/| adv.|精细地，细致地，美好地|In a fine manner; excellently; minutely; into small particles.|The tea leaves were finely ground. 茶叶被磨得很细。|近义词：delicately, exquisitely, precisely|反义词：coarsely, roughly|词性:副词
词源：From Middle English *finely, from Old French finement (“finely”), from fin (“fine”).
