leper:/ˈlɛpər/| n.|麻风病人；受排斥的人；|A person who has leprosy, a chronic infectious disease that affects the skin, nerves, and mucous membranes.  Figuratively, it refers to someone who is shunned or ostracized.|例句：In biblical times, lepers were often ostracized from society. 在圣经时代，麻风病人经常被社会排斥。

例句：He felt like a leper after the scandal broke.  丑闻曝光后，他觉得自己像个被排斥的人。|近义词：outcast, pariah, untouchable|反义词:insider, accepted|词性:noun
