gymnophiona:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈfɪənə/ | n. |蚓螈|A group of limbless, burrowing amphibians resembling worms or snakes. They are found in tropical regions around the world.| Gymnophiona are often mistaken for snakes. 蚓螈常被误认为是蛇。| 近义词:caecilian | 反义词: (No direct antonym, as it is a specific biological classification) | 词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *gymnos* "naked" + *ophis* "serpent"
