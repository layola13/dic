overcomplicated:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmplɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
过于复杂的；过度复杂的
Making something more complex than necessary.
The instructions were overcomplicated and confusing.  说明书过于复杂，令人困惑。
The plot of the movie was overcomplicated and hard to follow.  这部电影的情节过于复杂，难以理解。

近义词:complex, convoluted, elaborate, intricate
反义词:simple, straightforward, easy

词性:adjective
