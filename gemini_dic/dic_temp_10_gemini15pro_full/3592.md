agave:/əˈɡɑːveɪ/ | n. | 龙舌兰 | A succulent plant with rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves and a tall flower stalk, native to Mexico and the southwestern US. It is used to make tequila and other alcoholic drinks. | Tequila is made from the agave plant. 龙舌兰酒是由龙舌兰植物酿造的。|  The agave plant thrives in hot, dry climates. 龙舌兰植物在炎热干燥的气候中茁壮成长。 | synonyms: century plant, maguey | antonyms: N/A | noun
