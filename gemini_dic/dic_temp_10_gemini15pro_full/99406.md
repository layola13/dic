interdistinguish:/ˌɪntər dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
verb
区分；辨别
To distinguish between; to differentiate.
It's difficult to interdistinguish between the two species of birds.  区分这两种鸟类很困难。
The twins are so alike, it's hard to interdistinguish them. 这对双胞胎太像了，很难区分他们。
近义词: differentiate, distinguish, discriminate
反义词: confuse, conflate
词性: verb
