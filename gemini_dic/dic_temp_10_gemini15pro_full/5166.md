allergy: /ˈælərdʒi/ (US), /ˈælədʒi/ (UK)| n. |过敏|A damaging immune response by the body to a substance, especially a particular food, pollen, fur, or dust, to which it has become hypersensitive.|例句：He has an allergy to peanuts. 他对花生过敏。|例句：Many people suffer from allergies in the spring. 许多人在春天都会过敏。|近义词：hypersensitivity, intolerance|反义词: immunity, tolerance|词性: noun

allergic: /əˈlɜːrdʒɪk/ (US), /əˈlɜːdʒɪk/ (UK)| adj. |过敏的|Having an allergy.|例句：I'm allergic to cats. 我对猫过敏。|例句：She is allergic to certain types of pollen. 她对某些类型的花粉过敏。|近义词：hypersensitive, intolerant|反义词: immune, tolerant|词性: adjective
