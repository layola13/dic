machete:/məˈʃɛti/ /məˈtʃeti/| n. |弯刀；大砍刀|A large, heavy knife with a broad blade, used as a weapon or for cutting vegetation.|The workers used machetes to clear the sugarcane field. 工人们用弯刀清理甘蔗田。|近义词:knife, blade, cutlass|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "tool for planting/cultivating")|词性:noun

machetes:/məˈʃɛtiz/ /məˈtʃetiz/| n. |弯刀（machete的复数）|Plural form of machete, referring to more than one machete.|The soldiers carried machetes as they patrolled the jungle. 士兵们在丛林巡逻时带着弯刀。|近义词:knives, blades, cutlasses|反义词:(No direct antonym)|词性:noun
