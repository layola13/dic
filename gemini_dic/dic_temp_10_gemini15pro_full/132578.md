nondifficult:/ˌnɑːnˈdɪfɪkəlt/
adjective
不难的，容易的
Not difficult; easy.
The exam was nondifficult. 考试不难。
Synonyms: easy, simple, straightforward
Antonyms: difficult, hard, challenging
词性: 形容词
