inordinateness:/ˌɪnˈɔːrdɪnɪtnəs/| n. | 过度；无节制；过分 | The state or quality of being excessive or immoderate. |  His inordinateness in eating led to health problems. 他饮食过度导致了健康问题。| excessiveness, immoderateness, extravagance | moderation, restraint | noun

inordinate:/ɪnˈɔːrdɪnɪt/| adj. | 过度的；过分的；无节制的 | Exceeding reasonable limits; excessive. |  She spent an inordinate amount of time on social media.  她在社交媒体上花费了过多的时间。| excessive, immoderate, extravagant | moderate, reasonable, restrained | adjective
