filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/
n.
金银丝细工; 精致的装饰; (尤指金属丝制成的) 花丝，金银丝细工
Ornamental work of fine (typically gold or silver) wire formed into delicate tracery.
The pendant was a delicate filigree of gold and silver.  这个吊坠是用金银制成的精致花丝。

filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/
adj.
花丝状的; 精美的;
Formed of or resembling filigree.
The filigree gate was a beautiful example of craftsmanship.  这扇花丝状的大门是工艺的典范。



近义词: tracery, ornamentation, decoration
反义词: plainness, simplicity
词性: noun, adjective

词源: From Italian *filigrana*, from *filo* “thread” (from Latin *filum*) + *grana* “grain” (probably from Latin *granum*).
