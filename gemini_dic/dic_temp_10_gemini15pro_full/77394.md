gaussian:/ˈɡaʊsiən/| adj. n.|高斯的，高斯函数|Relating to or involving the German mathematician Carl Friedrich Gauss or his work, especially in statistics (normal distribution).|例句：The Gaussian distribution is commonly used in statistics. 高斯分布在统计学中很常用。

例句：Gaussian blur is a common image processing technique. 高斯模糊是一种常见的图像处理技术。|近义词：normal (distribution)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-Gaussian")|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: Named after Carl Friedrich Gauss (1777–1855), German mathematician.
