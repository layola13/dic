inculpableness:/ˌɪnkʌlpəbəlnəs/
n.
无辜，无罪，无可指责
The state of being inculpable; freedom from blame or guilt.
His inculpableness was evident after a thorough investigation. 他的清白在彻底调查后得到了证实。
The jury declared her inculpableness in the crime. 陪审团宣布她在该案中无罪。
innocence, blamelessness, guiltlessness
culpability, guilt, blameworthiness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inculpabilis (blameless) + -ness.  Inculpabilis is formed from in- (not) + culpabilis (blameworthy), from culpa (blame, fault).
