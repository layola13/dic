aped:/eɪpt/| v. |模仿，学…的样子|To imitate someone, often in a playful or mocking way.  It suggests mimicking actions or mannerisms rather than simply copying something.|The child aped his father's shaving routine.  孩子模仿他父亲的刮胡子动作。|mimicked, imitated, copied|originated, innovated|verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From ape (n.) + -ed.  The verb sense derives from the observed behavior of apes, known for their ability to mimic human actions.
