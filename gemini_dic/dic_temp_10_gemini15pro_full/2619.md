adonis: /əˈdɒnɪs/ (UK) /əˈdɑːnɪs/ (US)| n. |美男子； Adonis（希腊神话中一位俊美的男子）|A very handsome young man.| He was a regular Adonis, with his golden hair and sculpted features. 他有着金色的头发和轮廓分明的五官，是个不折不扣的美男子。

She fell in love with an Adonis. 她爱上了一个美男子。

|近义词：beau, hunk, heartthrob|反义词：ugly, unattractive|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Adonis, the name of a handsome youth loved by Aphrodite in Greek mythology.  The name is of Phoenician origin, from adon "lord".

USAGE->
作为名词：
He's considered quite the Adonis around here.  = 他在这里被认为是相当英俊的男子。
The magazine featured several Adonises on the cover. = 这本杂志的封面上有几位美男子。


PHRASE->
an Adonis = 一个美男子
