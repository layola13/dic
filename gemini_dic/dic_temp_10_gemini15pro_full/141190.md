oolong:/ˈuːlɔŋ/ | n. |乌龙茶；乌龙球（指意外的失误或错误）|A type of Chinese tea that is partially oxidized, giving it a flavor between green and black tea. Also, a blunder; an embarrassing mistake. |例句：I ordered an oolong tea. 我点了一杯乌龙茶。

例句：He scored an oolong goal against his own team. 他进了一个乌龙球，对自己的球队不利。|近义词：(tea) semi-oxidized tea, (blunder) mistake, error, gaffe |反义词: (tea) N/A, (blunder) success, triumph |词性:noun
