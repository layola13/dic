ambushers:/ˈæmbʊʃərz/
n.
埋伏者；伏击者
People who lie in wait to attack someone.
The ambushers jumped out from behind the trees. 埋伏者从树后跳了出来。

The soldiers were trained to anticipate and counter ambushers. 士兵们被训练如何预测和反击埋伏者。

Synonyms: attackers, assailants, muggers, waylayers
Antonyms: defenders, protectors, guardians
noun
