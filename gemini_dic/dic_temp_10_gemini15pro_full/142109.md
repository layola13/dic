orienter:/ˈɔːriˌɛnt/ (US), /ɔːriˈɛnt/ (UK)| vt. |使适应；确定方向，使朝向；给…定位|To familiarize someone with new surroundings or circumstances; to align or position something.|The new employee orientation program helps to orienter new hires to the company culture.  The telescope was oriented towards the constellation.|近义词:adapt, adjust, acclimate, familiarize, direct, position|反义词:disorient, confuse|verb
