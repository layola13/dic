delirious:/dɪˈlɪəriəs/| adj.|神志不清的；精神错乱的；极度兴奋的|In a state of delirium; experiencing uncontrolled excitement, emotion, or confusion.|The patient was delirious from the fever.  病人因发烧而神志不清。

He was delirious with joy when he won the lottery. 他中了彩票欣喜若狂。|近义词：frantic, frenzied, crazed, unhinged, raving|反义词：lucid, sane, rational, composed, calm|词性:形容词

delirium:/dɪˈlɪriəm/| n.|谵妄；精神错乱；极度兴奋|A disturbed state of mind resulting from illness or intoxication and characterized by restlessness, illusions, and incoherence of thought and speech.|The high fever brought on delirium. 高烧引起谵妄。

She was in a state of delirium after the accident. 事故发生后，她处于精神错乱状态。|近义词：madness, frenzy, hysteria|反义词：lucidity, sanity, rationality|词性:名词
