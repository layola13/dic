nod-off:/nɒd ˈɒf/ (UK) /nɑːd ˈɔːf/ (US)| vi.| 打瞌睡，睡着 | To briefly fall asleep, typically unintentionally. | Example: I nodded off during the movie. 我在看电影的时候打瞌睡了。

Example:  He was so tired he kept nodding off in meetings. 他太累了，开会时一直打瞌睡。| 近义词: doze off, drift off, fall asleep | 反义词: wake up, stay awake | 词性:  不及物动词 (Intransitive Verb)
