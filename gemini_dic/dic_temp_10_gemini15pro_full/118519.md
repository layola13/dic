mesocoelia:/ˌmɛsəˈsiːliə/| n. |中腔；中脑腔|The cavity within the midbrain, also called the cerebral aqueduct or aqueduct of Sylvius.  It connects the third and fourth ventricles of the brain and allows cerebrospinal fluid to flow between them.|The mesocoelia can become obstructed, leading to hydrocephalus.|近义词:cerebral aqueduct, aqueduct of Sylvius|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Ancient Greek μέσος (mésos, “middle”) + κοῖλος (koîlos, “hollow”).
