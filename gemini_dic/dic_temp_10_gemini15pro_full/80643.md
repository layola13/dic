词源:  "Gopher" originally referred to a type of burrowing rodent. The term was later adopted for the "Gopher" internet protocol, which involved retrieving files from various servers.  The verb "to gopher" then emerged to describe the act of searching for and retrieving information, especially online.  It can also refer to busying oneself with various tasks, often implying a sense of scurrying around like a gopher.
