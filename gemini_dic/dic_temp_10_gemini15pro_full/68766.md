farrier:/ˈfæriər/| n.|蹄铁匠，装钉马掌的铁匠|A farrier is a specialist in equine hoof care, including the trimming and balancing of horses' hooves and the placing of shoes.|The farrier came to shoe the horses. 蹄铁匠来给马钉马掌了。|Synonyms: blacksmith, horseshoer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
