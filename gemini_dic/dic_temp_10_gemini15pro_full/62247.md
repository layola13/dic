encryption:/ɪnˈkrɪpʃən/ | n. | 加密，编码 | The process of converting information or data into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access. |  Example 1: The encryption of the message ensured its confidentiality.  这封邮件的加密确保了其机密性。

Example 2:  Strong encryption is essential for protecting sensitive data online.  强大的加密对于保护在线敏感数据至关重要。 | Synonyms: encoding, ciphering | Antonyms: decryption, deciphering | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin encryptio, encrypt-,  from en- + Greek kryptos "hidden"
