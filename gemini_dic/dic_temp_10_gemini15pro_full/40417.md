contrapuntal:/ˌkɒntrəˈpʌntl/ | adj. | 对位的，复调的 | Relating to or characterized by counterpoint, a musical technique in which two or more independent melodic lines are combined. | The composer's contrapuntal skill is evident in the intricate interplay of voices. 作曲家对位法的技巧在错综复杂的声音交织中显而易见。 |  polyphonic, multi-voiced | homophonic, monophonic | adjective
