condescendence:/ˌkɑːndɪˈsendəns/ | n. | 屈尊；俯就；恩赐态度 | An attitude of patronizing superiority; disdainful graciousness. |  He treated his employees with condescension, as if they were beneath him. 他以屈尊的态度对待他的员工，好像他们不如他。

The king showed condescension in speaking to the commoners. 国王屈尊与平民交谈。 | 近义词：patronizing, superciliousness, disdain, haughtiness | 反义词：humility, respect, egalitarianism | 词性: 名词


词源:

From Late Latin condescentia, from Latin con- ‘together’ + descendere ‘climb down’.  由晚期拉丁语 condescentia 而来，源自拉丁语 con- “一起”+ descendere “向下爬”。
