glue-like:/ˈɡluːlaɪk/
adjective
胶状的; 粘性的
Resembling glue in texture or stickiness.
The substance had a glue-like consistency.  这种物质具有胶状稠度。
The sap from the tree was glue-like and difficult to remove.  树上的汁液很粘，很难去除。
近义词:sticky, adhesive, gummy, viscous
反义词:slippery, non-adhesive
词性:adjective

词源:
From "glue" + "-like."  "Glue" comes from Old French "glu" (birdlime), ultimately from Late Latin "glus," related to "gluten."  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
