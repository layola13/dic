housemasters:/ˈhaʊsmɑːstərz/
n.
舍监；宿舍管理员
A teacher responsible for the welfare of a group of students, especially boarding students, in a particular house or dormitory of a school.
The housemasters held a meeting to discuss the new school regulations.  舍监们开会讨论新的校规。
The housemaster addressed the students about appropriate behavior in the dormitories. 舍监向学生们讲述了宿舍里的适当行为。
synonyms: dorm supervisor, resident advisor, house parent
antonyms: student, pupil

词性: noun (plural)
