mux:/mʌks/| n. v.|多路复用器，多路传输，混合|A device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line.|The engineer used a mux to combine multiple video signals onto one cable. 工程师使用多路复用器将多个视频信号合并到一根电缆上。|近义词：multiplexer, combiner|反义词:demultiplexer, splitter|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology:  Shortened from "multiplexer," which comes from "multiplex"  ultimately from Latin "multus" (many) + "plico" (to fold).
