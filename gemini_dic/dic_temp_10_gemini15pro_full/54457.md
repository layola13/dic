disinhume:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhjuːm/ | vt. | 掘出; 挖出（尤指尸体）| To exhume a body from a grave, especially for reburial or forensic examination. | The police disinhumed the body to investigate the cause of death. 警察掘出尸体以调查死因。| They disinhumed the remains of the king. 他们挖出了国王的遗骸。 | 近义词: exhume, unearth, dig up | 反义词: bury, inter | 词性: 动词
