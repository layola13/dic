hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/ (US) /ˈhezɪtət/ (UK)| v. |犹豫，迟疑|To pause before saying or doing something, especially because of uncertainty or nervousness.|She hesitated before answering the question. 她回答问题前犹豫了一下。

He hesitated to ask for help. 他犹豫要不要寻求帮助。

近义词：pause, waver, falter, demur|反义词:decide, resolve, proceed|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *haesitare*, frequentative of haerere "to stick, adhere, hold fast," from PIE root *ghes- "to stick, adhere." The sense evolution is from "stick fast" to "be at a loss, be uncertain."
