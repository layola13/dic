anachronism: /əˈnækrənɪzəm/ | n. |时代错误，年代误植；不合时代的人或物 | Something that is mistakenly placed in a time where it does not belong in a work of art, or something that belongs to a past time and seems strange and outdated now. |  The movie about ancient Rome contained several anachronisms, like characters wearing wristwatches.  这部关于古罗马的电影中有几个时代错误，比如人物戴着手表。 | Synonyms: misplacement, misdating, solecism, incongruity | Antonyms:  chronological accuracy, contemporaneity | noun
