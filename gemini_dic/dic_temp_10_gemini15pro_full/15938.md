balmlike:/ˈbɑːmlaɪk/| adj.|香膏似的，温和的，抚慰的|Having the soothing or healing qualities of balm; resembling balm.|The lotion had a balmlike effect on my sunburned skin. 这款乳液对我的晒伤皮肤有舒缓作用。|近义词：soothing, calming, comforting, healing, restorative|反义词：irritating, aggravating, exacerbating|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From balm +‎ -like.  "Balm" comes from Old French "basme," ultimately from Latin "balsamum," from Greek "balsamon."  "-like" is a native English suffix.
