lindens:/ˈlɪndənz/| n.|椴树; 菩提树|A deciduous tree with heart-shaped leaves and fragrant yellowish-white flowers.|The lindens lining the street filled the air with a sweet scent. 街道两旁的菩提树使空气中充满了甜蜜的香味。|Synonyms: lime trees, basswood|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lind or *linde, from Proto-Germanic *lendō, from Proto-Indo-European *lent-o- (“flexible”). Cognate with German Linde, Dutch linde.
