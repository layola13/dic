dorbeetle:/ˈdɔːrˌbiːtl/| n.|蜣螂，金龟子|A beetle of the family Scarabaeidae, many species of which feed on dung.|例句：The dorbeetle rolled the dung into a ball. 蜣螂把粪便滚成一个球。|例句：Dorbeetles play an important role in nutrient cycling. 蜣螂在营养循环中起着重要作用。|近义词：dung beetle, tumblebug|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "dor" (meaning drone, a male bee, likely referring to the buzzing sound) + "beetle."  The "dor" part is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse "dǫrr" (meaning spear).
