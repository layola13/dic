oscillatory:/ˈɒsɪlətəri/ (US) /ˈɑːsɪlətɔːri/ (UK)| adj.|振荡的，振动的；摇摆的|Characterized by or producing oscillation; swinging back and forth.|The oscillatory motion of the pendulum was mesmerizing. 钟摆的振荡运动令人着迷。|The stock market has been oscillatory recently. 最近股市一直在震荡。|synonyms: fluctuating, vibrating, wavering, swinging|antonyms: steady, stable, fixed, constant|adjective
