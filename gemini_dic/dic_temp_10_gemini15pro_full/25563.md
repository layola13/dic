bum-around:/ˌbʌm əˈraʊnd/
|v.|闲逛，游荡，无所事事|To spend time idly or without any specific purpose; to loaf or wander.|He bummed around Europe for a year after college.  他大学毕业后在欧洲闲逛了一年。|Synonym: loaf, idle, wander, drift, hang around|Antonym: work, hustle, be productive|verb

bum-around:/ˌbʌm əˈraʊnd/
|n.|游手好闲的人|A person who spends their time idly or without a specific purpose.|He's a bit of a bum-around, never really settling down. 他有点游手好闲，从来不定居下来。|Synonym: loafer, idler, wanderer|Antonym: worker, hustler|noun
