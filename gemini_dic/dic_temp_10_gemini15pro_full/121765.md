misquoted:/ˌmɪsˈkwoʊtɪd/ /ˌmɪsˈkwəʊtɪd/
verb
错误地引用，歪曲
To repeat something someone said in a way that is not accurate, or that misrepresents their meaning.
He claimed he was misquoted by the media. 他声称媒体错误地引用了他的话。
The journalist was accused of deliberately misquoting the politician. 该记者被指控故意歪曲了这位政治家的讲话。
misrepresented, distorted, falsified
accurately quoted, correctly cited
