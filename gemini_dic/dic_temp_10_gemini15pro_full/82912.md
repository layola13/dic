gumma:/ˈɡʌmə/| n. |树胶肿，梅毒瘤|A soft, gummy tumor characteristic of tertiary syphilis. It is a form of granuloma.  |  The patient presented with a gumma on his leg, a sign of late-stage syphilis. 病人的腿上出现了一个树胶肿，这是晚期梅毒的迹象。|Synonym: syphilitic granuloma, gummatous lesion|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a pathological condition)|Noun
Etymology->From Late Latin gumma (“gum, gum-like tumor”), probably from Greek kommi (“gum”).
