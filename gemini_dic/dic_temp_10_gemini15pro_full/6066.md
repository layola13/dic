amentiferous:/əˌmɛnˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
多柔荑花的；产柔荑花序的
Bearing catkins.  Specifically referring to plants that produce catkins, which are a type of slender, cylindrical flower cluster.
The amentiferous willow tree swayed gently in the breeze.  多柔荑花的柳树在微风中轻轻摇曳。
近义词: catkin-bearing
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider) acatkinous (not bearing catkins -  which is not a standard word)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *amentum* (catkin) + *-fer* (bearing)  + *-ous* (adjectival suffix).  The Latin *amentum* refers to a strap or thong,  likely due to the resemblance of the catkin to a hanging strap.
