nonstrike:/ˌnɒnˈstraɪk/
adj.|非罢工的，不罢工的|Not engaging in or involving a strike; not of or pertaining to a strike.|例句：The company implemented a nonstrike policy.  这家公司实施了非罢工政策。|近义词：non-striking|反义词: striking|词性:形容词
nonstrike:/ˌnɒnˈstraɪk/
n.|非罢工者|A person who does not participate in a strike.|例句：The nonstrikes continued to work during the labor dispute. 非罢工者在劳资纠纷期间继续工作。|近义词：non-striker|反义词: striker|词性:名词
