cacoethes:/ˌkækəˈiːθiːz/
n.
an irresistible urge; a mania
an irresistible urge or passion.
He has a cacoethes for collecting stamps. 他有收集邮票的癖好。
Synonyms: mania, obsession, compulsion, urge
Antonyms: indifference, apathy
noun
