ETYMOLOGY->
From "bully" (meaning a ruffian or intimidator) + "rag" (possibly meaning to scold or nag).  The exact origin of the "rag" element is uncertain, but it may be related to the now-obsolete sense of "rag" meaning to tease or torment.
