aliquot:/ˈælɪkwɒt/ (US) /ˈælɪkwɔːt/ (UK)| n. |等分，等份，部分|A portion of a larger whole, especially a sample taken for chemical analysis or other treatment.|The chemist carefully measured out several aliquots of the solution for testing. 化学家仔细量取了几份溶液进行测试。|Synonyms: portion, sample, share, fraction|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

aliquot:/ˈælɪkwɒt/ (US) /ˈælɪkwɔːt/ (UK)| adj. |等分的，成等分的|Divided into equal parts.|The estate was divided into aliquot shares among the heirs.  遗产被等分给继承人。|Synonyms: equal, even, uniform|Antonyms: unequal, uneven, disproportionate|adjective
