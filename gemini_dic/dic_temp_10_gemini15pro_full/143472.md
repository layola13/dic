outgallop:/ˌaʊtˈɡæləp/
verb
超越，跑得比…快
to gallop faster than someone or something else
The young horse outgalloped the older ones. 这匹小马跑得比其他老马都快。
outrun, outstrip, surpass
lag behind, fall behind
