clothlike:/ˈklɔːθlaɪk/
adjective
像布的; 似布的
Resembling cloth in texture, appearance, or feel.
The petals of the flower had a delicate, clothlike texture.  这种花的花瓣质地细腻，像布一样。
The synthetic material had a surprisingly clothlike feel.  这种合成材料摸起来出奇地像布。
近义词:fabric-like, textile-like, material-like
反义词:rigid, stiff, hard
词性:adjective
