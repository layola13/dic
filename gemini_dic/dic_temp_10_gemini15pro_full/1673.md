acnodal: /ˈæknoʊdəl/
adjective
交点的；结点的；【数】二重点的
Relating to or situated at a node; nodal. In mathematics, specifically referring to a curve that has two branches meeting at a point with distinct tangents.
The acnodal points of the curve represent critical junctures in the system.  该曲线的二重点代表了系统中的关键连接点。

The graph displays an acnodal point where the two lines intersect.  图表显示了两条线相交的二重点。

近义词: nodal, intersecting
反义词: non-intersecting, separate
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *aknodos, from akmē "point" + nodos "knot".
