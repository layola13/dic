melotype:/ˈmɛləˌtaɪp/| n.|肖像，蛋白照片，美乐型相|A photographic portrait produced on a plate of blackened glass or other dark material, often hand-colored.|Example 1:  The museum displayed a collection of 19th-century melotypes, offering a glimpse into the past.  博物馆展出了一批19世纪的美乐型照片，让人得以一窥过去。

Example 2:  She found a melotype of her great-grandmother in the attic. 她在阁楼里找到了一张她曾祖母的美乐型照片。|近义词：photograph, portrait, ambrotype, daguerreotype|反义词: None|词性:noun
