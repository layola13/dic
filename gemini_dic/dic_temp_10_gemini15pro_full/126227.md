mycelium:/maɪˈsiːliəm/| n.|菌丝体|The vegetative part of a fungus, consisting of a network of fine white filaments (hyphae).|The mushroom emerged from the mycelium beneath the soil.  蘑菇从土壤下的菌丝体中冒出来。|
|Synonyms:  hyphae (in aggregate), spawn|Antonyms:  fruiting body (loosely), sporocarp (loosely)|noun|

mycelia:/maɪˈsiːliə/| n.|菌丝体（mycelium的复数形式）|Plural form of mycelium.|The mycelia of various fungi were studied under the microscope.  各种真菌的菌丝体在显微镜下被研究。|
|Synonyms:  hyphal networks|Antonyms:  fruiting bodies (loosely)|noun|
