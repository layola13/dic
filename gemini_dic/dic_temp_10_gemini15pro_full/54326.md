disheartenedness:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnədnəs/
n.
沮丧；灰心；气馁
The state of being disheartened; loss of courage or hope.
His disheartenedness was evident in his slumped posture and quiet voice. 他的沮丧从他颓丧的姿势和低沉的声音中显而易见。
近义词: despondency, discouragement, dejection
反义词: encouragement, hopefulness, optimism
词性: 名词

dishearten:/dɪsˈhɑːrtn/
v.
使沮丧；使灰心；使气馁
To cause (someone) to lose courage or hope.
The team was disheartened by their recent loss.  最近的失利让团队很沮丧。
近义词: discourage, dispirit, demoralize
反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire
词性: 动词
