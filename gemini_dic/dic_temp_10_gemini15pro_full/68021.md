factored-in:/ˈfæktərd ɪn/| adj.|已考虑在内的，已纳入的|Included or considered as part of a calculation or plan.|The projected cost of the project has factored-in potential delays and rising material prices.  该项目的预计成本已将潜在的延误和不断上涨的材料价格考虑在内。|例句：We've factored-in the possibility of rain. 我们已经考虑了降雨的可能性。|近义词：included, considered, incorporated|反义词:excluded, omitted|词性:形容词

词源:  From "factor" (v.) + "in" (adv.). "Factor" itself comes from the Latin "factor," meaning "maker" or "doer."  The sense of "including something as a factor" developed in the late 19th century.
