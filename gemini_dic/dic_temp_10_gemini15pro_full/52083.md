dialyzer:/ˈdaɪəˌlaɪzər/| n.|透析器；洗肾机|A dialyzer is a machine used in dialysis to filter a patient's blood when their kidneys are not functioning properly.  It removes waste products and excess fluid.|The dialyzer was essential for keeping the patient alive until a kidney transplant could be performed.  透析器对于维持病人的生命直到可以进行肾移植至关重要。|Synonyms: artificial kidney, hemodialyzer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
