hymn:/hɪm/ | n. |赞美诗，圣歌 |A religious song or poem of praise, typically to God or a god. |例句：The congregation stood and sang a hymn.  会众起立唱了一首赞美诗。

例句：They closed the service with their favorite hymn.  他们以最喜欢的赞美诗结束了礼拜。

近义词：anthem, psalm, carol, song of praise |反义词:blasphemy, curse |词性:noun
