hemispheroidal:/ˌhemiˈsfɪrɔɪdəl/ | adj. | 半球形的，半球状的 | Relating to or resembling a hemispheroid (half of a spheroid). A hemispheroid is a three-dimensional shape that is half of a spheroid, similar to half an ellipsoid. | The Earth is not perfectly spherical, but rather slightly hemispheroidal due to its rotation. 地球并非完美的球形，而是由于自转而略呈半球形。

The dome of the building was hemispheroidal in shape. 这座建筑的穹顶呈半球形。 | Near synonyms:  hemispherical, semiovoid,  | Antonyms: spherical, globular | adjective


hemispheroid:/ˌhemiˈsfɪrɔɪd/ | n. | 半球体 | Half of a spheroid; a solid formed by revolving a semi-ellipse around one of its axes. | The model depicted the northern hemispheroid of the planet.  这个模型描绘了行星的北半球体。

The artist sculpted a perfect hemispheroid out of clay. 艺术家用黏土雕刻了一个完美的半球体。 | Near synonyms: hemisphere, semiellipsoid | Antonyms: sphere, spheroid | noun
