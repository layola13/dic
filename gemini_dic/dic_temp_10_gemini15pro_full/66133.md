exceptionable:/ɪkˈsɛpʃənəbl/ | adj. | 可反对的，可抗议的；异常的，例外的 |  Causing disapproval or protest because of being considered morally wrong or offensive. Also, unusual; not typical. | The judge found the lawyer's conduct exceptionable and reprimanded him. 法官认为律师的行为应受谴责，并对他进行了训斥。 | synonyms: questionable, dubious, debatable, controversial | antonyms:  unobjectionable,  acceptable,  normal,  typical | adjective
