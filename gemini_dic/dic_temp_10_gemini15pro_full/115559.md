marbling:/ˈmɑːrblɪŋ/ |n.| 大理石花纹，大理石纹理；大理石化 |The marbling of the beef makes it more tender. 牛肉的大理石花纹使其更加鲜嫩。| Example: The marbling in the cake batter created a beautiful swirl effect. 蛋糕面糊中的大理石花纹营造出美丽的漩涡效果。| Synonym:  veining, streaks, patterns | Antonym: solid color, plainness | noun

marbling:/ˈmɑːrblɪŋ/ |v.| 使有大理石花纹；使呈大理石状 |He marbled the clay to create a unique design. 他将黏土大理石化以创造独特的设计。| Example: She marbled the paper by swirling different colored inks together. 她通过将不同颜色的墨水一起旋转来使纸张大理石化。| Synonym:  mottling, variegating | Antonym: solidifying, homogenizing | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "marble" (n.) + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs, or indicating the material or style of something).  "Marble" ultimately comes from the Greek "marmaros," meaning "shining stone" or "crystalline rock."
