mccarthyites:/məˈkɑːrθiˌaɪts/
|n.|麦卡锡主义者；麦卡锡的支持者|Supporters or followers of Senator Joseph McCarthy and his anti-communist methods, often characterized by aggressive and unsubstantiated accusations.|During the 1950s, many innocent people were accused of being McCarthyites and communists.|近义词:red-baiters, witch-hunters|反义词:communist sympathizers, liberals|词性:名词

mccarthyism:/məˈkɑːrθiˌɪzəm/
|n.|麦卡锡主义|The practice of making accusations of subversion or treason without proper regard for evidence, named after Senator Joseph McCarthy.|McCarthyism created a climate of fear and suspicion in the United States.|近义词:witch-hunt, red scare|反义词:tolerance, due process|词性:名词
