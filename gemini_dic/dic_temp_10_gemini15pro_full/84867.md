hanging:/ˈhæŋɪŋ/| adj. n.|悬挂的，吊着的；悬而未决的；绞刑；悬挂物|A hanging is something that is suspended or hung.  It can also refer to the act of execution by hanging.  As an adjective, it describes something that hangs.|例句：The hanging plants added a touch of green to the room. 悬挂的植物为房间增添了一抹绿色。|近义词：suspended, dangling, pendent, unresolved (for "hanging" as in unresolved issue)|反义词：standing, upright, resolved (for "hanging" as in unresolved issue)
|词性: adjective, noun


hanging:/ˈhæŋɪŋ/| v.|悬挂，吊着；垂下；（被）绞死|This is the present participle of the verb "to hang".  It describes the action of suspending something.|例句：He was hanging a picture on the wall. 他正在墙上挂一幅画。|近义词：suspending, dangling|反义词：standing, upright
|词性: verb (present participle)


词源: From Old English *hangian, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch hangen and German hängen.
