deregulated:/diːˈreɡjuleɪtɪd/
adjective
撤销管制的；解除管制的
freed from regulation or control.
例句：The deregulated electricity market has led to increased competition.  撤销管制的电力市场导致竞争加剧。

例句：Deregulated financial markets can be volatile.  解除管制的金融市场可能不稳定。

近义词：liberalized, uncontrolled, unrestricted
反义词：regulated, controlled, restricted
词性：adjective

词源:
From de- + regulate + -ed.  "De-" prefix means "remove" or "reverse". "Regulate" means to control or direct according to rule.  "-ed" suffix forms the past participle and often adjectives.
