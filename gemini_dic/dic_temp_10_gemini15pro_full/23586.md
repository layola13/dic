brakeman:/ˈbreɪkmən/ | n. | 铁路制动员；刹车员 | A person who assists the conductor or engineer on a train, often responsible for operating the brakes.  | The brakeman signaled the engineer to stop the train. 铁路制动员示意司机停车。 |  The brakeman checked the couplings between the cars. 制动员检查了车厢之间的连接装置。 | synonyms: switchman, flagman, pointsman | antonym: driver, engineer | noun
