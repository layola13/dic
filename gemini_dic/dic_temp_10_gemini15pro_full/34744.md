clientelage:/ˌkliːənˈtelɑːʒ/
noun
顾客群，客户群
The body of clients of a professional person or business.
The restaurant's clientelage includes many celebrities. 这家餐馆的顾客包括许多名人。
clientele, customers, patrons
non-clients, competitors
n.
