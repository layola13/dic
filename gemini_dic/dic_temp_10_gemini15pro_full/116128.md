marxist:/ˈmɑːksɪst/| adj. n.|马克思主义的；马克思主义者|Relating to the political and economic theories of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels; a follower of these theories. |例句：Marxist ideology emphasizes class struggle. 马克思主义意识形态强调阶级斗争。

例句：He's a self-proclaimed Marxist. 他自称是马克思主义者。|近义词：communist, socialist, leftist|反义词：capitalist, conservative, right-wing|词性：adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Karl Marx (1818-1883), German philosopher, economist, and sociologist, + -ist.
