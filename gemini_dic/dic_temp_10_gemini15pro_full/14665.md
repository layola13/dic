avowry:/əˈvaʊri/
n.|承认，公开宣布；供认|An open declaration or acknowledgment; a confession.|He made an avowry of his guilt. 他供认了他的罪行。
近义词:acknowledgment, admission, confession, declaration, profession
反义词:denial, repudiation
词性:noun

词源:
From Anglo-French *avowerie, from avouer (“to avow”).
