deep-sixed:/ˌdiːpˈsɪkst/
verb
丢弃，抛弃，拒绝，取消
To discard, dispose of, reject, or cancel something completely.
The project was deep-sixed due to budget cuts.  由于预算削减，该项目被取消了。
The CEO deep-sixed the proposal without explanation. 首席执行官未作解释就否决了该提案。

近义词:  discard, scrap, ditch, reject, cancel, kill
反义词:  approve, accept, implement, support
词性: verb
