de-stratifying:/diːˈstrætɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
消除分层，使均匀
The process of breaking down layers, especially in a body of water, to create a more uniform distribution of temperature, oxygen, or other properties.
The engineers are de-stratifying the lake to improve water quality. 工程师们正在消除湖泊的分层，以改善水质。
destratify, mix, homogenize
