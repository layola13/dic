frostbow:/ˈfrɒstbəʊ/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstbəʊ/ (US)| n. |冻疮，冻伤|An injury to body tissue caused by exposure to extreme cold, typically affecting the fingers, toes, ears, and nose.|例句：The explorers suffered from frostbow after their expedition to the Arctic. 探险家们在北极探险后患上了冻疮。

例句：She treated her frostbow with warm water and gentle massage. 她用温水和轻柔的按摩治疗冻疮。|近义词：frostbite, chilblain|反义词:burn|词性:名词
