metalloid:/ˈmɛt(ə)lɔɪd/| n. |类金属|An element (e.g. arsenic, antimony, or tin) whose properties are intermediate between those of metals and solid nonmetals. They are electrical semiconductors.|Silicon is a classic example of a metalloid.  硅是类金属的典型例子。|Germanium, semimetal|metal, nonmetal|noun
