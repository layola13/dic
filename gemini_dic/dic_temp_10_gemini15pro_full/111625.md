localities:/loʊˈkælɪtiz/
noun
地点，地区
places, usually towns, districts, or other small areas within a larger region or country.
Many rural localities have been losing population. 许多农村地区的人口一直在减少。
synonyms: areas, regions, districts, neighborhoods, vicinities, places
antonyms: center, hub
