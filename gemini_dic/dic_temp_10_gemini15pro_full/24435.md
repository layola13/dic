bristliest:/ˈbrɪstliɪst/| adj.|最毛的，最易怒的|Covered with bristles, especially stiff or prickly ones; also, easily annoyed or angered.|The bristliest cactus I've ever seen made me wary of getting too close.|近义词：prickliest, spikiest, touchiest, testiest|反义词:smoothest, softest, calmest, most patient|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From bristle + -y + -est.  "Bristle" comes from Old English "byrst," meaning "bristle."  The "-y" suffix forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by." The "-est" suffix forms the superlative degree of adjectives.
