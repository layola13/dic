blackfin:/ˈblækˌfɪn/| n. |黑鳍金枪鱼；黑鳍笛鲷|A type of tuna (Thunnus atlanticus) found in the western Atlantic Ocean, characterized by its dark fins. Also refers to a type of snapper (Lutjanus buccanella) found in the Caribbean Sea. |例句：The blackfin tuna is a popular game fish. 黑鳍金枪鱼是一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。

例句：Blackfin snapper are often caught by deep-sea fishing. 黑鳍笛鲷通常通过深海捕捞获得。|近义词：tuna, snapper|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "black" + "fin," referring to the dark coloration of the fish's fins.
