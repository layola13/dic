impaint:/ɪmˈpeɪnt/| vt.| 修补（尤指艺术品），（在头脑中）铭记|To restore (a damaged painting, for example) by filling in missing areas. To fix an image or idea firmly in someone's mind.|The artist skillfully impainted the missing section of the fresco.  艺术家巧妙地修复了壁画缺失的部分。|The horrors of war were impainted on his memory. 战争的恐怖深深地印在他的记忆里。|近义词：restore, retouch, fix, ingrain|反义词：damage, erase|verb

impaint:/ɪmˈpeɪnt/| n.| 修补（尤指艺术品）|The act of restoring a damaged painting or other work of art by filling in missing areas.  |The impaint was so well done that it was almost impossible to detect. 修补做得如此出色，几乎难以察觉。|近义词：restoration, retouching|反义词：damage, defacement|noun
