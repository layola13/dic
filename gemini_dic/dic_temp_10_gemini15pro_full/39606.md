consension:/kənˈsɛnʃən/| n.|一致同意，共识|Agreement or accord; harmony.|例句：The consensus among scientists is that climate change is real. 科学家们的共识是气候变化是真实存在的。|例句：After much debate, the committee finally reached a consensus. 经过多次辩论，委员会最终达成了一致。|近义词：agreement, accord, unanimity, harmony, concord|反义词：disagreement, discord, dissent, conflict|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *consēnsiō, from Latin cōnsēnsus (“agreement, accord”), past participle of cōnsentiō (“to agree, accord”), from com- (“together”) + sentiō (“to feel, perceive”).
