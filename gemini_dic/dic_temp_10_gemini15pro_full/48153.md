decolorize:/diːˈkʌləraɪz/| v. |脱色，漂白|To remove the color from something.|例句：The chemist decolorized the solution using activated charcoal. 化学家用活性炭使溶液脱色。

例句：Bleach is commonly used to decolorize fabrics. 漂白剂通常用于使织物脱色。|近义词：bleach, whiten, fade|反义词：color, dye, stain|词性：动词
词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove") + color + -ize (suffix meaning "to make or cause to be")  From French décolorer, from dé- + colorer "to color."
