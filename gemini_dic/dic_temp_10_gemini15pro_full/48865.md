deformer:/dɪˈfɔːrmər/| n.|变形器，使变形的人或物|Something that deforms or distorts something else. |The artist used a digital deformer to create the surreal landscape in the painting. 艺术家使用数字变形器在画中创造了超现实的风景。|The intense heat acted as a deformer, melting the plastic into a strange shape.  高温像一个变形器，把塑料融化成了奇怪的形状。|近义词：distorter, contorter, warper|反义词: reformer, shaper|词性: 名词

deform:/dɪˈfɔːrm/| v.|使变形，使残废|To spoil the shape or appearance of something; to make something misshapen. |The accident deformed his leg. 事故使他的腿变形了。|The intense heat deformed the plastic. 高温使塑料变形了。|近义词：distort, contort, warp|反义词: reform, shape|词性: 动词
