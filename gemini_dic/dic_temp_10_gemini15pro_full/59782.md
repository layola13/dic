ecoterrorism:/ˈiːkoʊtɛrərɪzəm/
n.
生态恐怖主义
The act of using violence or the threat of violence against people or property in order to protect the environment.
Ecoterrorism is a serious crime that can have devastating consequences. 生态恐怖主义是一项可能造成破坏性后果的严重罪行。
Groups resorting to ecoterrorism often believe that conventional methods of environmental activism are ineffective.  诉诸生态恐怖主义的团体通常认为传统的环境行动主义方法无效。

近义词:environmental extremism, green extremism
反义词:environmental conservation, environmental protection
词性:noun
