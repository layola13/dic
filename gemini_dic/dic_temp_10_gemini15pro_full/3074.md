aeromechanic: /ˌɛroʊmɪˈkænɪk/
词性: n.
中文翻译: 航空技师；航空机械师
英文解析: A person skilled in aeromechanics; a specialist in the design, construction, and maintenance of aircraft.
例句: The aeromechanic inspected the plane's engine before takeoff.  航空技师在起飞前检查了飞机的引擎。

例句: Experienced aeromechanics are crucial for ensuring flight safety.  经验丰富的航空技师对于确保飞行安全至关重要。

近义词: aircraft mechanic, aviation mechanic, airplane mechanic
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) pilot, flight engineer (different roles within aviation)
词性: n. (noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air or aircraft) + mechanic (skilled worker who repairs and maintains machinery). Aero- comes from Greek ἀήρ (aēr, "air"). Mechanic comes from Greek μηχανικός (mēkhanikos, "ingenious, inventive").
