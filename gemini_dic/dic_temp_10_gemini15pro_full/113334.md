luxullianite:/lʌkˈsʌliəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 勒克斯里安石 | A rare type of granite containing tourmaline crystals, typically blue, in a quartz and feldspar matrix.  It's named after the village of Luxulyan in Cornwall, England, where it was first discovered. | The museum displayed a polished slab of luxullianite, showcasing its striking blue tourmaline crystals.  博物馆展出了一块抛光的勒克斯里安石板，展示了其醒目的蓝色碧玺晶体。| Synonym: tourmaline granite | Antonym: N/A | noun
