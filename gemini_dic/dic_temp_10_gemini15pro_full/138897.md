nutella: /nʊˈtɛlə/ | n. | 能多益，榛子巧克力酱 | A sweetened hazelnut cocoa spread. | I love Nutella on toast. 我喜欢吐司面包涂能多益。| 近义词: chocolate spread, hazelnut spread | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "peanut butter" as an alternative spread) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  The name "Nutella" is derived from "Nut" (referring to hazelnuts) and "ella," an Italian suffix used to create endearing names.  The product originated in Italy in the mid-20th century.
