actualise: /ˈæktʃʊəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈæktʃuːəlaɪz/ (US)| v. |实现，实行；使现实化|To make something real or happen; to put something into action.|She finally actualized her dream of opening a bakery. 她终于实现了开面包店的梦想。

He struggled to actualize his vision for the company. 他努力实现他对公司的愿景。|近义词：realize, achieve, fulfill, implement|反义词：negate, prevent, hinder|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actualis "active, practical" + -ize.  The Latin word derives from Latin actus "a doing, a driving, an impulse,"  from agere "to do, set in motion, drive, lead, act."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He actualises his potential through hard work. = 他通过努力工作实现自己的潜力。
She actualises her ideas with meticulous planning. = 她通过周密的计划实现自己的想法。

一般过去时:
They actualized their plan despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，他们还是实现了他们的计划。
The company actualized its growth targets last year. = 该公司去年实现了其增长目标。

现在完成时:
She has actualized her lifelong ambition of writing a novel. = 她实现了毕生的夙愿，写了一部小说。
The team has actualized several key milestones in the project. = 该团队已经实现了项目中的几个关键里程碑。

过去完成时:
He had actualized his dream of traveling the world before he turned thirty. = 他在三十岁之前就实现了环游世界的梦想。
They had actualized their initial goals before expanding the business. = 在扩展业务之前，他们已经实现了最初的目标。

将来时:
We will actualize our plans for expansion next year. = 我们明年将实现我们的扩张计划。
The government will actualize new policies to address climate change. = 政府将实施新的政策来应对气候变化。

虚拟语气:
If I could actualize any dream, it would be to end world hunger. = 如果我能实现任何梦想，那就是结束世界饥饿。
I wish I could actualize my potential more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地发挥我的潜力。

被动语态:
The project was actualized ahead of schedule. = 该项目提前完成。
Her vision for the company is being actualized by the new management team. = 她对公司的愿景正在被新的管理团队实现。

疑问句:
How can we actualize our goals with limited resources? = 我们如何在资源有限的情况下实现我们的目标？
When do you plan to actualize this strategy? = 你打算什么时候实施这个策略？

否定句:
They were unable to actualize their plans due to funding cuts. = 由于资金削减，他们无法实现他们的计划。
She did not actualize her full potential in her previous role. = 在她之前的职位上，她没有充分发挥她的潜力。
