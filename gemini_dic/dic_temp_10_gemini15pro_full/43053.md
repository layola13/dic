cowled:/kaʊld/
adjective
戴着风帽的；覆盖着的
covered with or as if with a cowl
The figure was cowled and cloaked, making it difficult to discern their identity.  那个身影戴着风帽，披着斗篷，很难辨认身份。
The monk, cowled and silent, entered the monastery.  戴着风帽、沉默的僧侣走进了修道院。
hooded, cloaked, covered
uncovered, exposed
