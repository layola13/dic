even:/ˈiːvn/| adj. adv.| 平坦的，均匀的；偶数的；平分的；甚至，连；即使；恰好，正好|A flat, smooth surface is even. 平坦的表面是均匀的。|近义词：flat, smooth, level, uniform, regular; also, too, yet; exactly, precisely|反义词:uneven, rough, irregular; odd; before, after|词性:形容词, 副词

even:/ˈiːvn/| v.| 使平坦；使相等|He evened the ground with a rake. 他用耙子把地弄平了。|近义词：level, flatten|反义词:roughen|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *efen "level, equal, just," from Proto-Germanic *ebnaz (source also of Old Frisian even "even, flat," Old Saxon eban, Old High German eban, German eben "flat, level," Old Norse jafn "equal, even," Gothic ibns "even, equal"), from PIE *ep-no-, from the root *ap- "to seize, reach, fit."
