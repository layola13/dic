discoloration:/dɪsˌkʌlərˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 变色，褪色 |The act or process of changing color; the state of being discolored. A change from the original color; a stain or spot.| The old photograph showed signs of discoloration. 这张老照片有褪色的迹象。|近义词：staining, fading, bleaching, tarnish |反义词: brightening, coloration |词性: 名词


discolor:/dɪsˈkʌlər/ | v. | 使变色，使褪色 |To alter the color of; to stain or tarnish.| The sun discolored the curtains. 太阳晒得窗帘褪色了。|近义词：stain, fade, bleach, tarnish |反义词: brighten, color |词性: 动词
