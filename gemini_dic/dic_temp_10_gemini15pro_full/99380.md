interdevouring:/ˌɪntərdiˈvaʊərɪŋ/
adjective
互相吞食的，互相残杀的
consuming one another, mutually destructive
The interdevouring nature of the warring factions prolonged the conflict.  互相残杀的交战派别使得冲突持续不断。
The interdevouring habits of certain species can regulate their populations.  某些物种互相吞食的习性可以调节它们的种群数量。
近义词:cannibalistic, internecine, self-destructive
反义词:symbiotic, cooperative, mutually beneficial
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From inter- + devouring.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," or "reciprocally."  "Devouring" comes from the verb "devour," meaning "to eat up greedily" or "to consume destructively."
