overconsciousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈkɒnʃəsnɪsɪz/
|n.| 过度意识，过度在意|The state of being excessively aware of oneself, one's appearance, or one's actions, often leading to self-consciousness and anxiety.|He suffered from overconsciousness of his stutter. 他对自己的口吃过度在意。|Synonyms: self-consciousness, hyperawareness, excessive awareness|Antonyms: obliviousness, unawareness|noun

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "conscious" (aware) + "-ness" (state of being).  "Conscious" comes from Latin "conscius" (knowing, aware), from "com-" (with) + "scire" (to know).
