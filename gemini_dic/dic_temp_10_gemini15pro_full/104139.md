junctures:/ˈdʒʌŋktʃərz/
n.
连接点；接缝；关头
a particular point in time or in a sequence of events
例句：
At this juncture, I think it's important to remember our original goals.
在这个关头，我认为重要的是记住我们最初的目标。


The novel explores the junctures between history and fiction.
这部小说探讨了历史与虚构之间的连接点。


近义词：
joint, connection, intersection, crossroads, turning point
反义词：
division, separation, disconnection
