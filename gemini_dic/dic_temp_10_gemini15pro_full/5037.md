all-intents-and-purposes:/ɔːl ɪnˈtɛnts ænd ˈpɜːpəsɪz/ |adv.| 实际上，几乎所有方面| This phrase means "in every practical sense" or "virtually." It is used to indicate that something is true in almost every way that matters. | For all intents and purposes, the project is complete.  实际上，这个项目已经完成了。|  The game was, for all intents and purposes, over.  比赛实际上已经结束了。|Synonyms: effectively, practically, virtually | Antonyms: nominally, theoretically | adverb
