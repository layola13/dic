lugging:/ˈlʌɡɪŋ/ | v. | 拖，拉，搬运(重物) | Carrying or pulling something heavy with difficulty. |  I was lugging a heavy suitcase through the airport. 我在机场拖着一个沉重的行李箱。

|  She was lugging groceries home from the supermarket. 她从超市搬运杂货回家。 |  近义词：hauling, dragging, carrying, toting | 反义词: leaving, abandoning | 词性: 动词
