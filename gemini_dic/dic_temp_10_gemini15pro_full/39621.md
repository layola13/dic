consenting:/kənˈsɛntɪŋ/
adjective
同意的，赞成的
willing to give permission or agree to something.
She gave her consenting nod to the proposal. 她点头同意了这个提议。
近义词:agreeing, approving, compliant
反义词:objecting, opposing, dissenting
词性:adjective

consenting:/kənˈsɛntɪŋ/
verb
同意，赞成 (现在分词)
present participle of consent
He is consenting to the surgery. 他同意进行手术。
近义词:agreeing, approving
反义词:objecting, opposing
词性:verb
