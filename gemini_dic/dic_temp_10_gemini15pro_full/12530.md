asphyxial: /æsˈfɪksiəl/ | adj. | 窒息的，导致窒息的 | Relating to or causing asphyxia; characterized by or resulting from asphyxia. |  The coroner determined the death was asphyxial.  验尸官确定死因是窒息。 | Synonyms: suffocating, choking, smothering | Antonyms: resuscitating, oxygenating | adjective

asphyxia: /æsˈfɪksiə/ | n. | 窒息 |  A condition arising when the body is deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocation. | The victim died of asphyxia. 受害者死于窒息。 | Synonyms: suffocation, choking, smothering | Antonyms: respiration, ventilation | noun
