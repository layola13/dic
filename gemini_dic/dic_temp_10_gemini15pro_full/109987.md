libethenite:/laɪˈbiːθənaɪt/
noun
磷铜矿
A naturally occurring hydrous phosphate of copper, Cu2PO4(OH).
Libethenite is a relatively rare mineral. 磷铜矿是一种相对稀有的矿物。
Libethenite forms beautiful green crystals.  磷铜矿形成美丽的绿色晶体。
Synonym:pseudomalachite
Antonym: N/A (Minerals typically don't have direct antonyms)
词性: noun
