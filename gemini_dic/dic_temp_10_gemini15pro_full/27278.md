calumniator:/kəˈləmniˌeɪtər/| n. |诽谤者，造谣者|A person who makes false and malicious statements about someone. |例句：The calumniator spread lies about her rival to ruin her reputation. 这个诽谤者散布关于她对手的谎言以毁坏她的名声。

例句：He was labeled a calumniator after falsely accusing his neighbor of theft. 在诬告邻居偷窃后，他被贴上了诽谤者的标签。|近义词：slanderer, libeler, defamer, traducer |反义词: advocate, defender |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *calumniator*, from Latin calumniārī (“to accuse falsely, slander”), from calumnia (“false accusation, slander”).
