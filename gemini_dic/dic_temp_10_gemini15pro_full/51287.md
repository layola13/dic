detachably:/dɪˈtætʃəbli/
adverb|可拆卸地，可分离地|in a way that something can be detached or separated.|The keyboard connects detachably to the tablet.  键盘可以拆卸地连接到平板电脑上。|removably, separably|permanently, fixedly|词性: 副词

detach:/dɪˈtætʃ/
verb|拆卸，分离，分开|to separate (something) from something else that it was attached to.|He detached the trailer from the car. 他把拖车从汽车上拆了下来。|remove, separate, disconnect|attach, connect, join|词性: 动词
