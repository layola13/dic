avowance:/əˈvaʊəns/
n.
承认，公开宣布; 供认
An open declaration or acknowledgment of something.
He made an avowance of his love for her. 他公开宣称爱她。
近义词：acknowledgment, admission, confession, declaration, profession
反义词：denial, repudiation
词性: 名词

avowance:/əˈvaʊəns/
词源：From Middle English *avowaunce, from Anglo-Norman avouaunce, from avouer (“to avow”), from Latin advocare (“to summon, call to one's aid, claim as one's own”).
词性: 名词
