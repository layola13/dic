flight-lieutenant:/ˈflaɪt lɛfˈtɛnənt/ | n. | 飞行中尉 | An officer in the air force, ranking above a flying officer and below a squadron leader.  | Example: The flight-lieutenant briefed his pilots before the mission. 飞行中尉在执行任务前向他的飞行员作了简报。
Example:  Flight-Lieutenant Smith was awarded a medal for bravery.  飞行中尉史密斯因勇敢而被授予奖章。 | Synonym: air force officer | Antonym: N/A | noun
