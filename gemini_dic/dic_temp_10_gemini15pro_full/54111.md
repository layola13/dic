disembrangle:/dɪsɪnˈtæŋɡl/ | v. | 解开；理清；使摆脱困境 | To free something from a tangled or confused state; to separate things that are intertwined. |  The firefighters worked for hours to disentangle the victims from the wreckage. 消防队员花了几个小时才把受害者从残骸中解救出来。

The detective tried to disentangle the conflicting accounts of the witnesses. 侦探试图理清目击者相互矛盾的叙述。| 近义词：untangle, unravel, extricate, separate | 反义词：tangle, entangle, complicate | 词性: 动词

disembroil:/ˌdɪsɪnˈbrɔɪl/ | v. | 解脱；使脱离纠纷 | To free or extract from a difficult or complicated situation, especially one of confusion or conflict. |  The mediator helped to disembroil the two warring factions. 调解员帮助交战双方脱离了纠纷。

The company finally managed to disembroil itself from the lengthy lawsuit.  这家公司最终设法使自己摆脱了冗长的诉讼。| 近义词：extricate, disengage, disentangle | 反义词：embroil, entangle, involve | 词性: 动词
