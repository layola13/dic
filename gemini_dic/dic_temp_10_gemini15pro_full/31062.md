chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/ (US), /ˈtʃæmfə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|倒角，削边；|A small angled surface formed by removing a sharp edge or corner.|The carpenter chamfered the edges of the table. 木匠把桌子的边缘削成斜角。|近义词：bevel, slope, cant|反义词：sharpen, point|noun, verb
