desensitizer:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzər/ | n. | 脱敏剂；减敏剂 |  A substance that reduces sensitivity or responsiveness to a stimulus, especially an allergen or irritant. |  The doctor prescribed a desensitizer to help with her allergies. 医生给她开了脱敏剂来帮助治疗她的过敏。 | synonyms:  mitigant,  alleviant | antonyms: sensitizer, irritant | noun

desensitize:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/ | vt. | 使脱敏；使减少敏感度 | To make someone less sensitive to something, especially an allergen or irritant.  | Repeated exposure to violence can desensitize people to its effects.  反复接触暴力会使人们对其影响变得麻木。 | synonyms: numb, dull, inure | antonyms: sensitize, stimulate | verb
