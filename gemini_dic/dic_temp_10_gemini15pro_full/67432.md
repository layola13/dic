extinguish:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
verb
熄灭，扑灭
to put out a fire or light; to bring to an end
The firefighters quickly extinguished the blaze. 消防队员迅速扑灭了大火。
synonyms: put out, douse, quench, stifle
antonyms: ignite, kindle, light, start
词性:动词


extinguish:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
verb
消除，使破灭
to destroy or put an end to something
Her hopes were extinguished when she heard the news. 当她听到这个消息时，她的希望破灭了。
synonyms: abolish, destroy, eliminate, eradicate
antonyms: create, establish, foster, promote
词性:动词
