ill-disposed:/ˌɪl dɪˈspəʊzd/ | adj. | 不友善的，恶意的，有敌意的 | Having or showing an unfriendly or hostile attitude. |  He was ill-disposed towards the new management. 他对新管理层怀有敌意。 | Example: The neighbors were ill-disposed to the noisy newcomers. 邻居们对吵闹的新来者很不友好。| Synonyms: hostile, unfriendly, antagonistic, unsympathetic, malevolent | Antonyms: friendly, amicable, well-disposed, benevolent, sympathetic | Attribute
