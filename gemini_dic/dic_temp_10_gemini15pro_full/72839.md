folios:/ˈfoʊlioʊz/ (US), /ˈfəʊliəʊz/ (UK)| n.|对开纸；活页；（尤指成套书籍的）一页，一张；（账户的）页码；（成套书籍的）册数；（尤指成套书籍或账簿的）页码，张数|Large books, manuscripts, and documents are sometimes measured in folios.|例句：The lawyer consulted the folios of the case. 律师查阅了案件的卷宗。

例句：The rare book was bound in vellum and numbered by folios. 这本珍本书用牛皮纸装订，并按页码编号。|近义词：leaves, pages, sheets|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "volume" as a contrasting unit)|词性:noun (plural)

词源:
From Old French *folio* (Modern French *feuillet*), diminutive of Latin *folium* "leaf".
