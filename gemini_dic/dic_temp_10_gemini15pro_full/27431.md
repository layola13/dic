camisole:/ˈkæmɪsɒl/ (US) /ˈkæmɪsɔːl/ (UK)| n. |女士背心；吊带衫|A woman's sleeveless undergarment, typically made of silk or other lightweight fabric, and often worn as lingerie or sleepwear.  It can also be worn as an outer garment, especially in warm weather.|She wore a lacy camisole under her blouse.|近义词：tank top, undershirt, vest|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps outerwear)|noun
