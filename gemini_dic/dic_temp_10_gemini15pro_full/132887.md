nonelectively:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktɪvli/
adverb
非选择性地；非选修地
in a way that is not elective or chosen
Patients were nonelectively admitted to the ICU. 病人们被非选择性地送入重症监护室。
Synonyms: obligatorily, compulsorily, necessarily
Antonyms: electively, optionally, voluntarily
词性: 副词
