paragliding:/ˈpærəɡlaɪdɪŋ/
n.|滑翔伞运动|The act of flying with a paraglider.|He went paragliding in the Alps. 他去阿尔卑斯山滑翔伞了。|近义词：gliding, soaring|反义词：landing, descending|词性：名词
paraglide:/ˈpærəɡlaɪd/
v.|滑翔伞飞行|To fly using a paraglider.|She learned to paraglide in Switzerland. 她在瑞士学会了滑翔伞飞行。|近义词：glide, soar|反义词：land, descend|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From para- (meaning "alongside" or "nearby") + glide.  The term was coined in the 1960s.
