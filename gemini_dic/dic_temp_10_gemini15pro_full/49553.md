近义词：behavior, conduct, bearing, deportment|反义词: |词性:noun

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French demener ‘to conduct’, based on Latin minari ‘to drive (animals)’. The original sense was ‘management, administration’, later ‘conduct, behaviour’.
