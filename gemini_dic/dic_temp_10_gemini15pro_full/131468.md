noncoherently:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərəntli/ /ˌnɑːnkəʊˈhɪrəntli/
| adv. | 非相干地，不连贯地 |  In a way that lacks logical or meaningful connection between parts; in an incoherent manner. | The patient spoke noncoherently, jumping from one topic to another. 病人说话语无伦次，从一个话题跳到另一个话题。| Synonyms: incoherently, disconnectedly, disjointedly, ramblingly | Antonyms: coherently, logically, connectedly | adverb
