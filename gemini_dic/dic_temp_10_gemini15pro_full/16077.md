bandmaster:/ˈbændˌmæstər/ (US), /ˈbændˌmɑːstər/ (UK)| n.|乐队指挥，军乐队指挥|A person who directs a band, especially a military or marching band. |The bandmaster raised his baton and the music began. 乐队指挥举起指挥棒，音乐开始了。|Synonyms: conductor, director, leader|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "band" + "master". "Band" refers to a group of musicians, and "master" denotes a person in charge or with expertise.
