berylline:/ˈbɛrəˌlaɪn, -lɪn/| adj.|绿玉色的，淡绿色的|A pale, light green color, resembling that of beryl.|The berylline waters of the Caribbean were crystal clear. 加勒比海绿玉色的海水清澈见底。|近义词：aquamarine, sea-green, mint-green|反义词:dark green, brown|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *béryllin*, from *béryl* (beryl) + *-in* (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin *beryllus*, from Greek *βήρυλλος* (bḗryllos).
