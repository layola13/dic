overstoutly:/ˌoʊvərˈstaʊtli/
adverb|过于粗壮地，过于结实地|Excessively stoutly; in a manner exhibiting excessive thickness or robustness.|He overstoutly defended his rather weak argument. 他过于强硬地辩护他那相当薄弱的论点。|例句：She overstoutly insisted on carrying all the groceries herself, despite offers of help. 她不顾别人的帮助，执意要自己扛所有的杂货。|近义词：excessively, unduly, too much|反义词：slightly, subtly, delicately|词性:副词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "stoutly" (adverb from "stout," meaning "strong and thickset")
