lythonthriptic: /ˌlɪθɒnˈθrɪptɪk/ | adj. | 碎石的，溶石的 |  Referring to something that dissolves or breaks down stones, particularly in the context of medicine relating to kidney or bladder stones.  Derived from Greek *lithos* (stone) and *thriptikos* (crushing, crumbling). |  The lythonthriptic medication helped dissolve the kidney stones. 这种溶石的药物帮助溶解了肾结石。 | 近义词: lithotriptic, antilithic | 反义词: lithogenic (forming stones) | 词性: adjective
