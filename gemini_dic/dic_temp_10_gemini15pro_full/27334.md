camarasaurus:/ˌkæmərəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |卡玛拉龙|A large herbivorous dinosaur of the Late Jurassic period, with a long neck and tail. |Example: The Camarasaurus was one of the largest sauropods of its time. 卡玛拉龙是当时最大的蜥脚类恐龙之一。
Example:  Paleontologists discovered a Camarasaurus skeleton in Colorado. 古生物学家在科罗拉多州发现了一个卡玛拉龙骨架。|近义词：sauropod, dinosaur|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *kamara* meaning "chambered" or "vaulted" (referring to the hollow spaces in its vertebrae) + *sauros* meaning "lizard".
