contraindication:/ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |禁忌症；禁忌 |A specific situation in which a drug, procedure, or surgery should not be used because it may be harmful to the patient. |  This medication is contraindicated in patients with kidney disease.  这种药物肾病患者禁用。|Synonyms: warning, caution, prohibition | Antonyms: indication, recommendation | noun
