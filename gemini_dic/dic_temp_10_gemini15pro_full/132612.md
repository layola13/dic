nondirigibility:/ˌnɒndɪˌrɪdʒɪˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːndɪˌrɪdʒəˈbɪləti/
noun
不可操纵性；不 dirigible
The state or quality of being nondirigible.  The airship's nondirigibility made it difficult to control in strong winds.  飞艇的不可操纵性使其难以在强风中控制。
例句：The nondirigibility of the balloon made it impossible to steer.  气球的不可操纵性使得它无法被控制。

例句：The early airships suffered from nondirigibility, often being at the mercy of the wind. 早期的飞艇饱受不可操纵性之苦，经常任由风吹。

近义词：uncontrollability, intractability
反义词：controllability, maneuverability
词性：n.
