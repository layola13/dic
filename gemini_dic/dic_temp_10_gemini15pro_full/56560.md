dominical:/dəˈmɪnɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 主日的，属于主的 | Relating to Sunday, the Lord's Day. |  The dominical letter is used in calculating the date of Easter. 主日字母用于计算复活节的日期。 | The preacher gave a dominical sermon. 牧师做了主日布道。 | 近义词：Sabbatical, Sunday | 反义词：weekday | 词性：形容词

词源: From Late Latin dominicalis, from Latin dominicus ‘of a master or lord’, from dominus ‘master, lord’.
