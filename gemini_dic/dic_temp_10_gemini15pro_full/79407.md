glimpsers:/ˈɡlɪmpsərz/ | n. |瞥见者；一瞥而过的人| People who glimpse something; those who have a brief or incomplete view of something. |  The glimpsers caught only a fleeting view of the celebrity. 瞥见者只看到了名人一闪而过的身影。|  The glimpsers at the art exhibition moved quickly from piece to piece. 艺术展上的瞥见者快速地从一件作品移到另一件作品。 | synonyms: observers, viewers, onlookers | antonyms:  participants, examiners | noun
