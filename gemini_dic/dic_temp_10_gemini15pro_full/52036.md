dialectology: /ˌdaɪəˌlɛkˈtɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ˌdaɪəˌlɛkˈtɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n.| 方言学|The scientific study of dialects, their geographical and social distribution, their distinctive features, and their history.|Dialectology helps us understand how language varies across different regions. 方言学帮助我们理解语言是如何在不同地区发生变化的。|Example 1: Researchers in dialectology often travel to rural areas to record local speech patterns. 方言学研究人员经常前往农村地区记录当地的语音模式。

Example 2: The professor's lecture on dialectology focused on the differences between American and British English.  教授关于方言学的讲座重点讲述了美式英语和英式英语之间的差异。|Synonyms: linguistic geography, dialect geography|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
