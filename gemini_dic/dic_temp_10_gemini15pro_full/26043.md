bushiest:/ˈbʊʃiɪst/| adj.|最茂密的，灌木丛生最多的|Having the most bushes; most overgrown with bushes.|The bushiest part of the garden was home to a family of rabbits. 花园里灌木丛生最多的部分是兔子一家居住的地方。|近义词：densest, thickest, most overgrown|反义词:sparsest, thinnest, barest|词性:形容词
词源: From 'bush' (meaning shrub) + '-y' (forming adjectives indicating having the quality of) + '-est' (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *buskaz.
