ashamed:/əˈʃeɪmd/| adj.|羞愧的，惭愧的|Feeling shame or guilt.|例句：I was ashamed of my behavior. 我对我的行为感到羞愧。|例句：She felt ashamed that she had lied. 她为自己撒谎感到羞愧。|近义词：embarrassed, humiliated, guilty|反义词：proud, unashamed|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *āscamod, past participle of *āscamian "to feel shame," from ā- "on, in, at" + scamian "to shame" (from Proto-Germanic *skamōjanan "to put to shame").
