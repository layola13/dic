errata:/ɪˈrɑːtə/ /ɛˈrɑːtə/
noun
勘误表；正误表
A list of errors in a printed work, discovered after printing and shown with their corrections.
例句：The errata sheet listed several misspellings and factual errors.  勘误表列出了几个拼写错误和事实错误。

例句：A separate page of errata was included with the book. 书中附有一页单独的勘误表。
synonym:correction, amendment, revision
antonym:original, unchanged
n.
