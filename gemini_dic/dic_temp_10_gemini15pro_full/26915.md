calamine:/ˈkæləˌmaɪn/ | n. |炉甘石,炉甘石洗剂|A pink powder consisting of zinc oxide with a small proportion of ferric oxide, used in lotions or ointments to treat skin irritations.|She applied calamine lotion to the rash. 她把炉甘石洗剂涂在皮疹上。|近义词:zinc oxide, lotion|反义词:irritant|名词


calamines:/ˈkæləˌmaɪnz/ | n. |炉甘石（复数）|Plural form of calamine, referring to multiple instances or types of calamine.|The pharmacist offered different calamines for various skin conditions. 药剂师提供了不同种类的炉甘石用于各种皮肤状况。|近义词:zinc oxides, lotions|反义词:irritants|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *calemine, from Medieval Latin calamina, probably of uncertain origin, perhaps from Turkish *kalem, meaning "reed," "pen," or "metal stylus," referring to the shape of the zinc ore from which it is derived.
