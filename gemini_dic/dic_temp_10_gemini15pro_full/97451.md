infusing:/ɪnˈfjuːzɪŋ/
verb
灌输；注入；使充满
introducing something gradually or in stages; filling something with a particular quality
例句：The tea is infusing. 茶正在泡着。

例句：They are infusing the drink with herbs. 他们在饮料中加入草药。
近义词：instilling, imbuing, permeating, saturating
反义词：draining, extracting, removing
词性：v.
