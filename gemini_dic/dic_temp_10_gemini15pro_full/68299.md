falcongentil:/ˈfɔːlkən ˈdʒɛntil/ | n. | 雄隼; 猎隼的一种 | A male peregrine falcon, especially one captured and trained for falconry during its first year. |  The falcongentil was prized for its speed and agility.  这只雄隼因其速度和敏捷而备受珍视。 | 近义词: peregrine falcon, hunting hawk | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑prey猎物) | 词性: 名词

falcon:/ˈfɔːlkən/ | n. | 隼 | A bird of prey with long pointed wings and a notched beak, known for its swift flight. | The falcon soared high above the mountains. 隼在群山上空翱翔。 | 近义词: hawk, raptor | 反义词: prey | 词性: 名词

gentil:/ˈdʒɛntil/ | adj. (古语) | 高贵的；温顺的(指猎鹰) | Noble; meek (referring to a hawk). | In medieval times, the gentil falcon was a symbol of nobility. 在中世纪，温顺的猎鹰是贵族的象征。 | 近义词: noble, tame, meek | 反义词: wild, fierce | 词性: 形容词
