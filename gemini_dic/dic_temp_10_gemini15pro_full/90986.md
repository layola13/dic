hurtfulness:/ˈhɜːrtflnəs/| n.|伤害性，恶意，刻薄|The hurtfulness of her words stayed with him for days. 她的话语的恶意让他耿耿于怀了好几天。|近义词：cruelty, unkindness, malice, meanness|反义词:kindness, compassion, gentleness|词性:noun

Etymology: From "hurtful" + "-ness". "Hurtful" comes from "hurt" (Old French "hurter," from a Germanic source related to "thrust") + "-ful" (Old English "-full," meaning "full of").
