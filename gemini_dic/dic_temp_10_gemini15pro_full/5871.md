ambassadorially:/ˌæmˌbæsəˈdɔːriəli/
|adv.|以大使的身份；像大使一样|In a manner befitting an ambassador; diplomatically.  Acting as an ambassador would; representing something. |He spoke ambassadorially, choosing his words carefully and maintaining a dignified demeanor. 他说话很有大使风范，措辞谨慎，举止庄重。

The CEO handled the crisis ambassadorially, calming investors and reassuring the public. 首席执行官以大使般的方式处理了这场危机，安抚了投资者，并让公众放心。|近义词:diplomatically, formally, officially|反义词:informally, unofficially, casually|词性:adverb

Etymology:
From ambassador + -ial + -ly.  "Ambassador" ultimately derives from Late Latin *ambactiāre "to go on a mission," from ambactus "servant, vassal."
