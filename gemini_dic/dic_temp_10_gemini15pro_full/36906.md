colorists:/ˈkʌlərɪsts/| n.|着色师，染发师|A person who colors something, especially hair or photographs/films.|例句：The colorists did a fantastic job on my hair. 染发师把我的头发染得棒极了。

例句：The film's colorists created a vibrant and memorable visual experience.  这部电影的调色师创造了充满活力、令人难忘的视觉体验。|近义词：dyer, tinter, colorer|反义词:|noun
