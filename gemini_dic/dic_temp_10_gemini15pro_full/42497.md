counterinflation:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 反通货膨胀，抑制通货膨胀 |  The act or process of controlling or reducing inflation. | The government introduced new counterinflation measures. 政府推出了新的反通货膨胀措施。 | synonyms: disinflation, deflation | antonyms: inflation, hyperinflation | noun


counterinflationary:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 反通货膨胀的 | Designed to counteract inflation. |  The government adopted counterinflationary policies. 政府采取了反通货膨胀的政策。 | synonyms: deflationary, anti-inflationary | antonyms: inflationary | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
"counter-" (prefix meaning "against, opposite") + "inflation" (from Latin "inflare" - to blow into, inflate)
