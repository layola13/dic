fructed:/ˈfrʌktɪd/
adjective
结果实的; 有果实的; 成熟的
Bearing or producing fruit; ripe.
The fructed branches of the orange tree bowed low. 橘子树结果实的枝条低垂着。
近义词:fruitful, fruiting
反义词:barren, unfruitful
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fructus (fruit) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).
