outfamed:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪmd/
verb
超过…的名声；使…逊色
surpass in fame; outshine in renown.
He outfamed all his rivals in the field of astrophysics. 他在天体物理学领域的名声超过了他所有的竞争对手。
The young artist quickly outfamed his teacher.  这位年轻的艺术家很快就超过了他的老师的名声。
近义词: eclipse, surpass, outshine, transcend
反义词: obscure, diminish
