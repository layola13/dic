fissipedal:/fɪˈsɪpɪdəl/
adjective
裂足的；趾裂的
Having toes or digits separated or cleft.
The fissipedal jerboa uses its long, fissipedal feet for jumping.  这种裂足的跳鼠用它长长的、裂趾的脚来跳跃。
The aye-aye, a nocturnal lemur, has a remarkably fissipedal hand with an elongated middle finger. 指猴，一种夜行狐猴，有着异常裂开的足，中指特别细长。
adjective
近义词:  toed, digitate
反义词:  webbed, palmate
词性: adjective
