nonquantitatively:/nɒnˈkwɒntɪtətɪvli/ | adv. | 非定量地，非数量地 |  In a way that does not involve measurement or quantity.  Describing something without using numbers or measurements. | The results were assessed nonquantitatively. 结果是非定量评估的。|  The survey nonquantitatively explored public opinion on the matter.  这项调查非定量地探讨了公众对此事的看法。 | qualitatively, numerically | non-numerically | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
quantitative (adjective): from Late Latin quantitativus, from Latin quantitas "quantity," from quantus "how much"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
