melastomaceous:/ˌmɛləˌstoʊˈmeɪʃəs/
adjective
野牡丹科的
Belonging to or resembling the Melastomataceae family of plants, characterized by opposite leaves with prominent parallel veins.
The garden boasts a vibrant collection of melastomaceous shrubs.  这个花园里生长着一系列野牡丹科的灌木。
Melastoma candidum is a common melastomaceous plant in Southeast Asia. 野牡丹(毛稔)是东南亚常见的野牡丹科植物。
