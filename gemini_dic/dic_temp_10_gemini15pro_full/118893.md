metate:/mɛˈtɑːteɪ/
n.
磨石，研磨盘
A flat or slightly hollowed stone, usually rectangular, on which materials such as grains, spices, or pigments are ground using a hand-held stone called a mano.
The woman used a metate and mano to grind the corn into flour.  妇女用磨石和磨槌把玉米磨成粉。

Etymology: From Nahuatl *metlatl.
Synonyms: grinding stone, quern
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) mill, grinder (as more modern equivalents)
Noun
