characterise:/ˈkærəktəraɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɛrɪktəˌraɪz/ (US)| v. | 描述，刻画，以…为特征 | To describe the qualities or features of someone or something. | The novel is characterised by its complex plot and vivid characters. 这部小说的特点是情节复杂，人物生动。|  The report characterises the current economic situation as unstable. 该报告将当前的经济形势描述为不稳定。 | synonyms: describe, portray, depict, define | antonyms: misrepresent, distort | verb

characterise:/ˈkærəktəraɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɛrɪktəˌraɪz/ (US)| v. | 以...为典型 | To be a typical feature or quality of something. | Bright colours and bold designs characterise her paintings.  鲜艳的色彩和大胆的设计是她绘画的典型特征。| Frequent power outages characterise life in this remote village.  频繁的停电是这个偏远村庄生活的典型特征。| synonyms: typify, exemplify, distinguish | antonyms:  N/A | verb
