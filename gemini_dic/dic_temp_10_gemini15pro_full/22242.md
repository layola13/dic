bondholder:/ˈbɑːndˌhoʊldər/ (US) /ˈbɒndˌhəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n. |债券持有人|A person or entity that owns a bond issued by a government, corporation, or other entity.|例句：Bondholders are entitled to receive interest payments. 债券持有人有权获得利息支付。|例句：The company defaulted on its payments, angering bondholders. 公司拖欠付款，激怒了债券持有人。|近义词：creditor, investor|反义词: debtor, issuer|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
bond (from Old English “band,” meaning something that binds) + holder (from hold + -er, denoting one who performs an action or holds something)
