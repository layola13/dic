attorney:/əˈtɜːrni/
n.
律师；代理人
A person legally appointed or empowered to act for another.
My attorney filed the paperwork with the court. 我的律师向法院提交了文件。

attorney:/əˈtɜːrni/
n.
(美)检察官，地方检察官
A lawyer, especially one who can act for somebody in court.
The district attorney is prosecuting the case. 地区检察官正在起诉这个案件。


近义词:lawyer, solicitor, barrister, counsel, advocate
反义词:defendant, client
词性:noun
