alexios:/əˈlɛksiɒs/ | n. | 阿莱克修斯（男子名） | A male given name, from the Greek name Ἀλέξιος (Alexios), meaning "defender of mankind." It is a variant of Alexius, which is derived from the Greek word ἀλέξω (alexo), meaning "to defend," "to help," or "to ward off."  | Example: Alexios Comnenus was a Byzantine emperor. 阿莱克修斯·科穆宁是一位拜占庭皇帝。 | Example: My grandfather's name was Alexios. 我祖父的名字叫阿莱克修斯。 | 近义词: Alexius, Alexis | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
