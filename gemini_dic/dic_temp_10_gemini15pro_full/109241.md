leisureness:/ˈliːʒərnəs/ /ˈleʒərnəs/| n.|悠闲，安逸，闲适|The state of being leisurely; freedom from the demands of work or duty.|She enjoyed the leisureness of a Sunday afternoon. 她享受着星期天下午的悠闲时光。|近义词：relaxation, ease, idleness, tranquility|反义词：busyness, activity, industriousness|词性:noun

Etymology: From leisure + -ness.  "Leisure" comes from Old French "leisir" meaning "permission, opportunity," ultimately from Latin "licēre" meaning "to be permitted." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
