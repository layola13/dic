oratorio:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriːoʊ/ (US), /ˌɒrəˈtɔːriːəʊ/ (UK)
n.
清唱剧，神剧
A large-scale musical composition for orchestra, choir, and soloists, usually on a religious or biblical theme, performed without costumes, scenery, or action.
Handel's "Messiah" is a beloved oratorio performed around Christmas.  亨德尔的《弥赛亚》是一部深受喜爱的清唱剧，通常在圣诞节前后演出。
The oratorio told the story of the prodigal son. 这部清唱剧讲述了浪子的故事。
近义词：cantata (康塔塔，通常比清唱剧规模小)
反义词: opera (歌剧，包含服装、布景和表演)
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *oratorio* "prayer hall, oratory," from Late Latin *oratorium* "place for prayer," neuter of *oratorius* "of prayer," from Latin *orare* "to pray, speak, plead."
