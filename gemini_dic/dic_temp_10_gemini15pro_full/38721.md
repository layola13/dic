conditionable:/kənˈdɪʃənəbl/
adjective
可调节的，可训练的
Able to be conditioned; susceptible to conditioning.
Pavlov's dogs proved that salivation is conditionable. 巴甫洛夫的狗证明了唾液分泌是可以调节的。
The child's behavior is highly conditionable.  这孩子的行为很容易被训练。
近义词:trainable, malleable, adaptable
反义词:incorrigible, unyielding, inflexible
词性:adjective
词源:From "condition" + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").  "Condition" comes from Old French "condicion" and ultimately from Latin "condicio" (agreement, condition).
