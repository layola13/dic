boyer:/ˈbɔɪər/ | n. | 船夫，水手 | A person who operates or assists in the navigation of a boat, especially a small boat. |  He worked as a boyer on the river, ferrying passengers and goods. 他在河上当船夫，运送乘客和货物。| Example: The boyer skillfully guided the small boat through the treacherous rapids.  这位船夫熟练地驾驶小船穿过险滩。 | Synonyms: boatman, sailor, waterman | Antonyms: landsman | noun
