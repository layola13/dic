meaninglessness:/ˈmiːnɪŋləsnəs/| n.|无意义；空虚|The meaninglessness of existence is a common theme in existentialist philosophy.  存在的无意义是存在主义哲学中的一个常见主题。|例句：He felt a deep sense of meaninglessness in his life. 他感到人生一片空虚。

例句：The endless paperwork seemed like a task of utter meaninglessness.  无休止的文书工作就像一项毫无意义的任务。|近义词：futility, emptiness, purposelessness, insignificance |反义词:meaning, significance, purpose, value|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From meaning + -less + -ness.  Meaning is from Old English *mænan, from Proto-Germanic *mainijan. -less is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "without," from Old English -lēas. -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, from Old English -nes.
