overskilled:/ˌoʊvərˈskɪld/ | adj. | 过于熟练的，资历过高的 | Having more skills or qualifications than are necessary for a particular job. | She felt overskilled for the administrative assistant position. 她觉得担任行政助理的职位大材小用了。|  He's overskilled for this kind of work; he needs something more challenging.  他对这类工作来说资历过高；他需要更有挑战性的工作。| 近义词：overqualified | 反义词：underskilled, unqualified | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + skilled (adjective meaning "having ability and experience")
