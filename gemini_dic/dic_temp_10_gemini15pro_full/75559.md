fuckiest:/ˈfʌkiɪst/
adjective
最糟糕的，最他妈的，最令人不快的 (very informal, vulgar, and offensive)
This is the fuckiest situation I've ever been in.  这是我遇到过的最糟糕的情况。
The traffic was absolutely fuckiest this morning. 今早的交通真是糟糕透了。
近义词:shittiest, crappiest, worst, awful, terrible
反义词:best, greatest, finest
词性:adjective


词源:
Derived from the word "fuck," a highly offensive and vulgar word, with the superlative suffix "-iest."  "Fuck" itself has uncertain origins, possibly from Proto-Germanic.
