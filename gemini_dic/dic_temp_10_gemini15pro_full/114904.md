man-sized:/ˈmæn saɪzd/| adj.|男子汉般大小的；适合成年男子使用的；巨大的|Of a size suitable for or typical of a man.|例句：He gave the man-sized job to his son. 他把这项艰巨的任务交给了他的儿子。|例句：It was a man-sized portion of steak. 这是一份很大的牛排。|近义词：large, big, huge, enormous|反义词：small, tiny, miniature|词性:形容词
词源:Compound of "man" + "sized".  "Man" from Old English "mann", meaning "human being, person (male or female)"; "sized" from "size" + suffix "-ed", with "size" from Old French "sise", meaning "fixed amount, measure, dimension".
