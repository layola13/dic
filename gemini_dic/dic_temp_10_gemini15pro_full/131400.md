nonclarifications:/ˌnɒnklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 未澄清，未说明 |  The report was full of nonclarifications, making it difficult to understand the true situation.  该报告充满了未澄清之处，难以理解真实情况。 |  Example: The contract was filled with nonclarifications and ambiguities.  合同中充满了未澄清和模棱两可之处。 | Synonyms: ambiguities, obscurities, uncertainties | Antonyms: clarifications, explanations, elucidations | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "clarification" (from "clarify" - to make clear, from Latin "clarus" meaning "clear" + "-fy" a suffix meaning "to make").
