nonsecret:/ˌnɒnˈsiːkrət/ | /ˌnɑːnˈsiːkrət/ | n. | 非秘密的事物；公开的事物 | Something that is not secret; something that is open or public knowledge.  The meeting minutes are a nonsecret. 会议记录不是秘密。 | Synonyms: public knowledge, open information | Antonyms: secret, confidential information | noun
