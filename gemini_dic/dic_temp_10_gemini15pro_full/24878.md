bru:/bruː/| n. |兄弟，哥们（南非非正式用语）| A term of address for a male friend or acquaintance, commonly used in South Africa. | Example: Hey bru, howzit going? 嘿，兄弟，最近怎么样？| Synonym: mate, pal, buddy, dude | Antonym: enemy, foe | noun

bru:/bruː/| interj. |兄弟，哥们（南非非正式用语，用于表达惊讶、兴奋或其他强烈情感）| Used as an interjection to express surprise, excitement, or other strong emotions, commonly used in South Africa. | Example: Bru, that was an awesome game! 兄弟，那场比赛太棒了！ | Synonym: wow, man, dude | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | interjection
