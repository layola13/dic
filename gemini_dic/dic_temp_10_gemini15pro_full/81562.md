graywacke:/ˈɡreɪwækə/ | n. |杂砂岩|A dark gray sedimentary rock containing poorly sorted angular grains of quartz, feldspar, and rock fragments set in a compact, clay-rich matrix.|The geologist identified the sample as graywacke due to its characteristic composition. 地质学家根据其特征成分将样品鉴定为杂砂岩。| The cliffs were composed primarily of graywacke. 这些悬崖主要由杂砂岩构成。 |近义词:greywacke (alternative spelling), impure sandstone|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific rock type.  Could consider contrasting rock types like limestone or marble.)|Noun
