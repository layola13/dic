paridigitate:/ˌpærɪˈdɪdʒɪteɪt/ | adj. | 对趾的；偶蹄的 | Having toes arranged in pairs, typically with the second and fifth digits forming a symmetrical pair on either side of the third and fourth digits, as in artiodactyls.  | Example: The paridigitate foot of a deer allows it to move swiftly through wooded areas.  鹿的对趾足使它能够在树林中快速移动。

Example:  Pigs and hippos are paridigitate mammals. 猪和河马是对趾哺乳动物。 | 近义词: cloven-hoofed | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps perissodactyl - single-hoofed) | 词性:  adjective
