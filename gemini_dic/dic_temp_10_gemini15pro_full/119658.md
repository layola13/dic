middler:/ˈmɪdlər/| n.|中间的人，中等生|A person who is in the middle of a group, scale, or range, especially in terms of age, ability, or status.  A student in the middle years of a school, typically a middle school or junior high school. |He was a middler in the company, not quite a junior but not yet a senior manager. 他是公司的中层人员，不是初级员工，但也还不是高级经理。

The middler struggled to keep up with the advanced students in the class. 这个中等生很难跟上班里优秀的学生。|近义词：average student, intermediate, median|反义词：top student, bottom student, extreme|词性:noun
