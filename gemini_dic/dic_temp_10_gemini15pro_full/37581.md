communally:/kəˈmjuːnəli/
adverb
共同地，集体地
in a way that involves a group of people or animals living together and sharing possessions and responsibilities.
The land was owned communally. 这片土地是集体所有。
Synonyms: collectively, jointly, together, mutually
Antonyms: individually, separately, privately
词性: 副词
