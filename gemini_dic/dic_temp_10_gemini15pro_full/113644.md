machine-gunning:/məˈʃiːnˌɡʌnɪŋ/ | n. | 机枪扫射；猛烈抨击 | The act of firing a machine gun, or metaphorically, a barrage of criticism or questions. |  The soldiers resorted to machine-gunning the enemy position. 士兵们 resorted to machine-gunning the enemy position. | The reporter subjected the politician to a machine-gunning of questions. 记者对政治家进行了连珠炮似的提问。 | 近义词：strafing, bombardment, barrage | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "silence" in the metaphorical sense) | 词性: noun (gerund)


machine-gunning:/məˈʃiːnˌɡʌnɪŋ/ | v. | 用机枪扫射；猛烈抨击 | To fire a machine gun at; to assail with a rapid stream of criticism or questions. | The aircraft machine-gunned the convoy. 飞机用机枪扫射了车队。 | He was machine-gunned with accusations. 他遭到了连珠炮似的指责。 | 近义词：strafe, pepper, bombard | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "support" or "praise" in the metaphorical sense) | 词性: verb (present participle)
