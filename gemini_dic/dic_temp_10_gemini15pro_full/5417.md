almighty:/ɔːlˈmaɪti/ | adj. | 全能的，万能的；有无限权力的 | Having unlimited power; all-powerful. |  God is often referred to as almighty. 上帝常被称为全能的。| synonyms: omnipotent, all-powerful | antonyms: powerless, weak | adjective

almighty:/ɔːlˈmaɪti/ | n. |  全能的神，上帝(尤指基督教) | God (especially the Christian God). | They prayed to the Almighty for guidance.  他们向上帝祈祷指引。| synonyms: God, Lord | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *almeahtig (unattested), from all + miht "might" + -ig "-y." Cognate with German allmächtig.
