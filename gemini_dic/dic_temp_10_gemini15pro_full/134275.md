The noninformativeness of the witness's testimony frustrated the investigators. 证人证词缺乏信息，使调查人员感到沮丧。 | 近义词：uninformativeness, meaninglessness, lack of information | 反义词：informativeness, informativity, meaningfulness | 词性: 名词
