dairymaid:/ˈdeəriˌmeɪd/ | n. |挤奶女工；牛奶场女工|A woman employed in a dairy farm to milk cows and make butter and cheese.| The dairymaid woke up early to milk the cows.  挤奶女工早起去挤牛奶。| 近义词: milkmaid | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From dairy + maid.  "Dairy" comes from Old French *dairie meaning "dairy, dairy farm" from deirie "female servant in charge of a dairy", derived from Old North French *deie "dairymaid," ultimately from a Germanic source. "Maid" comes from Old English mægden meaning "virgin, young unmarried woman, female servant."
