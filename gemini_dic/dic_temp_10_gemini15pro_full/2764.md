adultnesses: /əˈdʌltnɪsɪz/
n.
成年人的状态; 成熟
The state or quality of being adult; maturity.
Due to their increased adultnesses, the teenagers were given more responsibilities. 由于他们日渐成熟，这些青少年被赋予了更多的责任。
The adultnesses of their responses surprised the teachers.  他们成熟的回答让老师们感到惊讶。
近义词: maturity, adulthood, grown-upness
反义词: immaturity, childishness
词性: 名词

词源:  From adult + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  由adult（成年人）加上-ness（表示状态或性质的后缀）构成。
