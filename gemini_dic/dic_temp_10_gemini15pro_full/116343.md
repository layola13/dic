mastersinger:/ˈmɑːstərˌsɪŋər/ /ˈmæstərˌsɪŋər/| n.|大师歌手；名歌手|A highly skilled and trained singer, especially one of a guild in 14th- to 16th-century Germany.|例句：The mastersinger composed a beautiful melody. 大师歌手创作了一首优美的旋律。

例句：He aspired to become a renowned mastersinger. 他渴望成为一名著名的名歌手。|近义词：virtuoso, expert, maestro|反义词：amateur, novice|词性：noun

Etymology:
From German *Meistersinger*, from *Meister* (master) + *Sänger* (singer).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *maiztaz* (more, most) and *sangwaz* (singer).
