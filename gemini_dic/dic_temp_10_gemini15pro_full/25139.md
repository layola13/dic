buckingham:/ˈbʌkɪŋəm/| n. |白金汉|A place name, most commonly associated with Buckingham Palace, the official residence of the British monarch in London.  It can also refer to the town of Buckingham in Buckinghamshire, England. |Buckingham Palace is a popular tourist attraction. 白金汉宫是一个热门的旅游景点。| Buckingham is a historic market town. 白金汉是一个历史悠久的集镇。|近义词:Palace (when referring to Buckingham Palace)|反义词:N/A|名词

Etymology:
From Old English *Boccinga ham*, meaning "homestead of Bucca's people".  "Bucca" was likely a personal name.
