cesarean: /sɪˈzɛəriən/ (US) /siːˈzɛəriən/ (UK)| n. adj.|剖腹产；剖腹产的|A surgical operation for delivering a child by cutting through the wall of the abdomen and the uterus.|例句：She had a cesarean section for her first child. 她第一个孩子是剖腹产的。

|近义词：C-section|反义词: vaginal birth|词性: noun, adjective
