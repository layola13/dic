camo:/ˈkæmoʊ/| n. adj.|伪装，迷彩服|Camouflage, often used for hunting or military purposes.  Material or clothing with a mottled design in dull greens and browns for concealment. It can also function as a verb, meaning to camouflage something.|He wore camo pants and a jacket. 他穿着迷彩裤和夹克。|近义词：camouflage, disguise|反义词: conspicuousness, visibility|词性: noun, adjective

camo:/kæˈmoʊ/| v. |伪装|To camouflage something.|They camoed the tank with branches and leaves. 他们用树枝和树叶伪装了坦克。|近义词：camouflage, disguise, conceal|反义词: expose, reveal|词性: verb
