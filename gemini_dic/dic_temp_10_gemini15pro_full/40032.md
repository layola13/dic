consuming:/kənˈsuːmɪŋ/| adj.|消耗的；耗时的；令人着迷的|engaging all one's attention or interest.|例句：Writing a book is a consuming task. 写书是一项耗时的工作。|近义词：absorbing, engrossing, captivating, time-consuming|反义词：uninteresting, boring, tedious|词性:形容词

consuming:/kənˈsuːmɪŋ/| adj.|消耗的；耗时的；令人着迷的|using up a lot of resources such as fuel, energy, or time.|例句：This car is very consuming on fuel. 这辆车很耗油。|近义词：wasteful, extravagant, exorbitant|反义词：economical, efficient, frugal|词性:形容词
