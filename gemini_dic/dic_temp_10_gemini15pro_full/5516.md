alphabet-soups:/ˈælfəˌbɛt sup(s)/ | n. | 字母汤；杂乱无章的东西 | A jumbled and confusing mixture of things, often letters or words, likened to the random assortment of letters in alphabet soup. |  The contract was full of legal alphabet-soups that I couldn't understand.  合同里充满了我不懂的法律术语。 | synonyms: gibberish, jargon, mumbo jumbo | antonyms: clarity, simplicity | noun

alphabet-soups:/ˈælfəˌbɛt sup(s)/ | adj. (informal, less common) | 混乱的，杂乱无章的 |  Characterized by a confusing mixture of elements, resembling alphabet soup. | The report was an alphabet-soup of statistics and technical terms. 这份报告是一堆统计数据和技术术语的杂烩。 | synonyms: jumbled, confused, chaotic | antonyms: organized, clear, coherent | adjective
