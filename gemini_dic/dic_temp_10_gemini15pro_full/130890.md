nonarrogance:/ˌnɒnˈærəɡəns/
|n.|谦逊，不傲慢|The quality or state of not being arrogant; humility.|Her nonarrogance and willingness to learn made her a beloved colleague. 她谦逊好学的品质使她成为一位受人爱戴的同事。|Synonyms: humility, modesty, unpretentiousness|Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, conceit|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + arrogance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of". "Arrogance" comes from the Latin "arrogant-" (present participle of "arrogare"), meaning "to claim for oneself".
