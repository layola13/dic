ethnological:/ˌɛθnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
人种学的
Relating to the study of the characteristics of different peoples and the differences and relationships between them.
Ethnological research often involves fieldwork and participant observation.  人种学研究通常包括实地考察和参与观察。
The museum houses an impressive collection of ethnological artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的人种学文物。
近义词:anthropological, cultural
反义词:None
词性:adjective
