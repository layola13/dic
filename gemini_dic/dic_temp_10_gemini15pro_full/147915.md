overregulate:/ˌoʊvərˈreɡjəleɪt/ | v. | 过度监管，过度管制 | To regulate excessively or too strictly, often resulting in unintended negative consequences. | The government's attempt to overregulate the banking industry stifled innovation. 政府过度监管银行业的尝试扼杀了创新。 | The city council overregulated food trucks, making it difficult for small businesses to operate. 市议会过度监管食品卡车，使小型企业难以经营。 | overcontrol, overmanage | deregulate, underregulate | verb
