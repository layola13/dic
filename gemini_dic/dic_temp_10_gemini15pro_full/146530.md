overgive:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɪv/ | v. | 过度给予；过度放纵 | To give too much; to indulge excessively. | He overgave his children and they became spoiled. 他过度溺爱他的孩子，结果他们被宠坏了。|  She overgave herself to her work and neglected her health. 她过度投入工作而忽略了健康。 | 近义词: overindulge, spoil, pamper | 反义词: deprive, neglect | 词性: 动词
