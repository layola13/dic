glacious:/ˈɡleɪʃəs/ | adj. | 冰的；冰川的；冰河的 | Relating to, resembling, or consisting of ice or glaciers. Characterized by coldness or iciness, especially in manner. | The glacious peaks of the Himalayas. 喜马拉雅山的冰峰。| 近义词: icy, glacial, frozen, frigid | 反义词: warm, tropical, temperate | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin *glaciōsus, from glaciēs (“ice”).
