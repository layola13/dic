cloudxr:/klaʊd ɛks ɑːr/ | n. | 云XR | A proprietary technology developed by NVIDIA that allows users to stream high-quality virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) experiences from powerful servers to less powerful devices like mobile phones and standalone VR headsets.  | Example: CloudXR makes it possible to experience high-fidelity VR even on a low-powered laptop.  云XR使得即使在低功耗的笔记本电脑上也能体验高保真VR。

Example: The company is using CloudXR to deliver immersive training simulations to employees around the world.  该公司正在使用云XR向世界各地的员工提供沉浸式培训模拟。 | Synonym:  streamed VR/AR, remote VR/AR | Antonym:  local VR/AR | Noun
