noncorporeal:/ˌnɒnkɔːˈpɔːriəl/ /ˌnɑːnkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/
adj.
非物质的，无形的
Not having a physical body or form; intangible.
例句：Ghosts are often described as noncorporeal beings. 鬼魂常被描述为无形的生命。

例句：Love is a noncorporeal concept, but its effects are very real.  爱是一个非物质的概念，但它的影响非常真实。

近义词：immaterial, intangible, incorporeal, spiritual, ethereal
反义词：corporeal, physical, tangible, material
词性：形容词
