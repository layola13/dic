gleaner:/ˈɡliːnər/| n.|拾穗者；收集者； gleaning machine 割晒机|A person who gathers leftover grain or other produce after a harvest.  一个在收割后收集剩余谷物或其他农产品的人。|例句：The gleaners followed the harvesters, picking up the fallen ears of corn. 拾穗者跟在收割者后面，捡拾掉落的玉米穗。

例句：She was a gleaner of information, always seeking knowledge from various sources. 她是一个信息收集者，总是从各种来源寻求知识。|近义词：collector, gatherer, harvester|反义词：sower, planter|词性:noun
