glitz:/ɡlɪts/| v.  |使光彩夺目；使闪耀；|to make something glamorous or showy.|They glitzed up the old car for the parade.  他们把这辆旧车装饰得光彩夺目，以参加游行。|近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish|反义词：tone down, simplify|verb
