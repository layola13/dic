clientless:/ˈklaɪəntləs/
adjective
无客户端的
Not requiring a separate client application to be installed on the user's computer.
Many companies now offer clientless remote access solutions, allowing employees to connect securely from any device with a web browser.  许多公司现在提供无客户端远程访问解决方案，允许员工使用网络浏览器从任何设备安全连接。
Clientless VPNs are becoming increasingly popular due to their ease of use.  由于易于使用，无客户端VPN越来越受欢迎。
近义词: web-based, browser-based, thin-client
反义词: client-based, thick-client
词性: adjective
