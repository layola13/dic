anchored:/ˈæŋkərd/| adj.| 抛锚的，固定的，稳固的| firmly secured or fastened; not likely to move or change | The ship was anchored in the harbor. 这艘船停泊在港口。|  The debate was anchored in reality. 这场辩论基于现实。 | 近义词：fixed, secured, fastened, moored, grounded | 反义词：loose, adrift, floating, untethered | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "anchor," which comes from Old English * ancor, from Latin ancora, from Greek ankura.
