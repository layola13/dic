demarcator:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪtər/ | n. | 分界线，界标，分隔符 | Something that marks a boundary or separates one thing from another. | The river serves as a natural demarcator between the two countries. 这条河流是两国之间的天然分界线。 | synonyms: boundary, divider, separator, delimiter | antonyms: connector, unifier | noun

demarcator:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪtər/ | n. | 界限，界线 | A dividing line; a boundary.  | Property demarcators were clearly marked.  地产界限被清楚地标记出来。| synonyms: boundary, limit, border, frontier | antonyms:  center, interior | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "demarcate," which comes from the French "démarquer" (to mark off, distinguish), derived from the Old French "marque" (mark) and ultimately from the Germanic root "mark-."
