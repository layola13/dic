indemnify:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪ/| vt.|赔偿；保障；使免于受罚|To compensate for loss or damage; to protect against future loss or damage.|The insurance company will indemnify you for any losses you incur as a result of the fire. 保险公司将赔偿你因火灾造成的任何损失。|
The contract indemnifies us against any legal action. 合同保障我们免受任何法律诉讼。|Synonyms: compensate, reimburse, protect, secure|Antonyms: penalize, charge, fine|verb
