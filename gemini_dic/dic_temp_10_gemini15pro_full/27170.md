callers:/ˈkɔːlərz/| n. |呼叫者；来电者；来访者|People who make a call, visit, or request something. |The callers were put on hold while the receptionist checked with the manager. 接待员与经理确认时，打电话的人被要求稍等。|
callers:/ˈkɔːlərz/| n. |打电话的人；访问的人|Those who initiate communication.|The company received numerous callers inquiring about the job opening.  公司接到了许多关于职位空缺的咨询电话。|
callers:/ˈkɔːlərz/| n. |访问者|People who visit a place or person.|The callers left a message for the homeowner. 来访者给房主留了言。|


近义词:visitors, guests, callers, inquirers
反义词:hosts, visited, contacted
词性:noun (plural)

词源：From the verb "call" + "-er" (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse *kalla.
