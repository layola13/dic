microsurgical:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɜːrdʒɪkəl/| adj.|显微外科的|Relating to or involving microsurgery; performed using a microscope and specialized instruments.|Microsurgical techniques are used to reattach severed limbs. 显微外科技术用于重新连接断肢。|近义词:microscopic|反义词:macrosurgical|词性:形容词

Etymology:  "micro-" + "surgical". "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small".  "Surgical" comes from Late Latin "chirurgicalis", from Greek "kheirourgikos", meaning "pertaining to surgery," from "kheirourgos"  "surgeon," from "kheir" meaning "hand" and "ergon" meaning "work".
