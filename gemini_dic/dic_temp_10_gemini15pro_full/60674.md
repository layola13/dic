electress:/ɪˈlɛktrɪs/ | n. | 女选帝侯；王妃；有选举权的妇女 | A woman who has the right to participate in an election, especially one who can vote for an emperor (historical). A wife or widow of a German elector. |  The electress cast her vote for the new emperor. 女选帝侯投了新皇帝的票。|  The electress's influence in the court was considerable. 王妃在宫廷中的影响力相当大。| Synonyms: voter (in a general sense), constituent | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "non-voter," or perhaps "subject" in a historical context where electors held power.) | noun
