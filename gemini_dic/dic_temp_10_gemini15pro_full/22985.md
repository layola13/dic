bouchees:/buˈʃeɪz/
| n. |小点心，一口酥|Small, bite-sized appetizers, often served at parties or receptions.|The bouchees at the wedding were delicious, especially the mini quiches. 婚礼上的小点心很美味，尤其是迷你乳蛋饼。|Synonyms: appetizers, canapés, hors d'oeuvres|Antonyms: main course, entree|Noun

Etymology:
From French *bouchée*, meaning "mouthful," from the verb *boucher*, meaning "to stop up, plug."
