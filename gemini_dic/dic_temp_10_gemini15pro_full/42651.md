counterspell:/ˈkaʊntərˌspel/| n. & v.|反咒，反制咒语|An act of magic that stops another spell from working; to cast such a spell.|The wizard countered the dragon's fire breath with a powerful counterspell.|近义词：neutralize, annul, negate|反义词:cast, spell|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology:
From "counter-" (meaning "against, opposing") + "spell" (meaning "a word or formula believed to have magic power").  The "counter-" prefix is from Old French "contre-" and ultimately from Latin "contra" (against). "Spell" comes from Old English "spell" (story, tale, message).
