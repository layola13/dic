ghostier:/ˈɡoʊstiər/ | adj. | 更像鬼的，更幽灵般的 | More like a ghost; more spectral or ethereal. | The old house felt ghostier at night.  这老房子在晚上感觉更像鬼屋了。 | The abandoned hospital had a ghostier atmosphere than the haunted house. 这家废弃的医院比鬼屋的气氛更诡异。| 近义词: spookier, eerier, more spectral | 反义词: less ghostly, more corporeal | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "ghost" (from Old English "gast") + "-ier" (comparative adjective suffix).
