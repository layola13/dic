minify:/ˈmɪnɪfaɪ/ | v. |  缩小，精简 | To reduce the size of (something, especially computer code or a data file) by eliminating unnecessary characters, such as white space or comments, without changing its functionality.  |  The developer minified the JavaScript file to improve website loading speed. 开发人员缩小了 JavaScript 文件以提高网站加载速度。|  compress, condense, shrink | expand, enlarge | verb
