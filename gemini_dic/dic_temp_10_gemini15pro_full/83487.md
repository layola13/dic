hacked:/hækt/ | v. | 被黑的，被入侵的 | past participle of "hack," meaning to gain unauthorized access to a computer system or account. |  My email account was hacked last week. 我的邮箱账户上周被黑了。|  The website was hacked and all the data was stolen.  网站被黑客入侵，所有数据都被盗了。| synonyms: compromised, breached, cracked | antonyms: secure, protected | verb

hack:/hæk/ | v. |  黑客入侵, 砍, 劈,  | to gain unauthorized access to data in a system or computer; to cut or chop something roughly. |  He hacked into the company's database. 他入侵了公司的数据库。 | She hacked the vegetables into small pieces. 她把蔬菜切成小块。 | synonyms:  infiltrate, breach, chop, gash | antonyms: protect, secure, defend | verb

hack:/hæk/ | n. |  劈, 砍, 干咳,  | a rough cut or blow with a sharp tool; a dry cough. |  He took a hack at the branch with the axe. 他用斧头砍了一下树枝。 |  He had a hacking cough all night. 他整夜都在咳嗽。 | synonyms: chop, gash, cough | antonyms: caress, smooth | noun
