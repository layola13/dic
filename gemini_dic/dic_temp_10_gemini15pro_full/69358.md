federating:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪŋ/
verb
联合，组成联邦
The act of uniting separate states or organizations into a single federal system.
Several small political groups are federating to form a new party.  几个小的政治团体联合起来组建一个新的党。
The individual colleges federated to form the university.  各个学院联合起来组成了这所大学。
Synonyms: uniting, joining, combining, amalgamating, merging, consolidating
Antonyms: separating, dividing, splitting, disintegrating
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin foederatus (past participle of foederare "to make a treaty, league, covenant"), from foedus "league, treaty, covenant".
