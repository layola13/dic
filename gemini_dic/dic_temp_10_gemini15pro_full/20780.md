blankets:/ˈblæŋkɪts/ /ˈblæŋkɪts/| n.|毯子，覆盖物|A piece of soft cloth used for warmth, especially on a bed.|She snuggled under the warm blankets. 她依偎在温暖的毯子下。|近义词：covers, quilts, duvets|反义词：sheets (although often used together)|词性:名词

blanket:/ˈblæŋkɪt/ /ˈblæŋkɪt/| vt. |覆盖，掩盖|To cover something completely with a thick layer.|Snow blanketed the ground. 雪覆盖了地面。|近义词：cover, coat, envelop|反义词：uncover, expose|词性:动词

blanket:/ˈblæŋkɪt/ /ˈblæŋkɪt/| adj. |全面的，总括的|Applying to all cases or instances; comprehensive.|A blanket ban on smoking. 全面禁止吸烟。|近义词：comprehensive, overall, general|反义词：specific, limited, restricted|词性:形容词


词源: From Old North French *blankete "white woolen cloth," diminutive of blanc "white," from Frankish *blank "white, shining," from Proto-Germanic *blankaz.
