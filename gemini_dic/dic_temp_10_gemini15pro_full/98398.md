insoluble:/ɪnˈsɒljʊb(ə)l/ (US) /ɪnˈsɑːljəbəl/
adjective
不能溶解的; 不可解决的
(of a substance) incapable of being dissolved: insoluble matter.  (of a problem or difficulty) admitting of no solution or explanation.
The precipitate is virtually insoluble in water. 这种沉淀物几乎不溶于水。
The problem seemed insoluble. 这个问题似乎无法解决。
近义词: undissolvable, unsolvable, insolvable
反义词: soluble, dissolvable
词性: adjective

insolubles:/ɪnˈsɒljʊb(ə)lz/ (US) /ɪnˈsɑːljəbəlz/
noun
不溶物
insoluble substances.
The insolubles were filtered out of the solution. 不溶物从溶液中过滤出来了。
The experiment required separating the solubles from the insolubles. 实验需要将可溶物与不溶物分离。
近义词: precipitates, residues
反义词: solubles, solutions
词性: noun


词源:
From Late Latin insolubilis, from in- ‘not’ + solubilis ‘soluble’.
