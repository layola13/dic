flotsam and jetsam: /ˈflɒtsəm ən ˈdʒetsəm/ | n. |  漂浮物，废料；(社会)渣滓，流浪者 |  Refers to the debris of a shipwreck. Flotsam is wreckage found floating, and jetsam is debris deliberately thrown overboard. Figuratively, it refers to people or things that are unwanted or unimportant. | The beach was littered with flotsam and jetsam after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上到处都是漂浮物和废料。| The city's flotsam and jetsam gathered in the park. 城市的流浪汉聚集在公园里。 | synonyms: debris, wreckage, refuse, riffraff | antonyms: valuables, treasures | noun
