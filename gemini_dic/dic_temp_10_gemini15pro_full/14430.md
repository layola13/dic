autunite:/ˈɔːtəˌnaɪt/ |n.| 钙铀云母 |A hydrated calcium uranyl phosphate mineral; a secondary uranium ore.|Autunite is a fluorescent mineral and often exhibits a bright yellow-green color under UV light. 钙铀云母是一种荧光矿物，在紫外光下通常呈现亮黄绿色。| Autunite is often found in association with other uranium minerals. 钙铀云母通常与其他铀矿物伴生。| uranyl phosphate, uranium ore| |noun
