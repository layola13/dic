envoi:/ˈɒnvɔɪ/ (UK), /ˈɑːnvɔɪ/ (US)|n.|寄送物；派遣；使者；(尤指)诗歌作品|something that is sent, especially a piece of writing |The poet's latest envoi is a meditation on mortality.  这位诗人的最新作品是对死亡的沉思。|synonyms: submission, offering, message, dispatch, emissary|antonyms: receipt, return|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French envoi, from envoyer "to send," from Vulgar Latin *inviare, from Latin in via "on the way."
