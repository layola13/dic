lints:/lɪnts/| n.| 棉绒，线头；（计算机）程序中的小错误，警告 | bits of thread or fluff; (computing) a small mistake or warning in a computer program |  The dryer was full of lints.  烘干机里全是棉绒。| synonyms: fluff, fuzz, bits, fragments | antonyms: N/A | noun


lints:/lɪnts/| v. (third-person singular simple present lints, present participle linting, simple past and past participle linted) |去除…上的绒毛；对（程序代码）运行静态分析以查找错误和可疑之处| to remove lint from something; to run a static analysis on (program code) to find errors and suspicious constructs | I linted the sweater before wearing it. 我在穿毛衣之前去掉了上面的绒毛。 | synonyms: clean, remove fluff, debug | antonyms: N/A | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *linten, from Old English *lyntan (“to tear off”), from Proto-Germanic *linþaną (“to tear off”), from Proto-Indo-European *lendʰ- (“to tear”).  Cognate with Dutch linten (“to tear to shreds”), German lind (“soft, gentle”).  The computing sense is a metaphorical extension, referring to picking out small errors.
