cleanse:/klɛnz/| v.|清洗，净化，清除|To rid of impurities, foreign matter, or undesirable elements, often in a ritualistic or symbolic way.|例句：The shaman cleansed the area with burning sage.  萨满用燃烧的鼠尾草净化了这个区域。

例句：She felt the need to cleanse herself of the guilt. 她觉得有必要清除自己的罪恶感。|近义词：purify, purge, clear, clean|反义词：pollute, contaminate, defile|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clensen*, from Old French *clenser*, *netoier*, from Vulgar Latin *clēnsāre*, from Latin *clēnsāre*, frequentative of *clēnsis*, past participle of *clēre* (“to clear”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱley- (“to lean; to incline”). Related to English *clean*.
