haematocryal:/ˌhiːmətoʊˈkraɪəl/ | adj. | 冷血的，变温的 | Having cold blood; poikilothermic.  Relating to or characteristic of animals whose body temperature varies with the surrounding environment. |  Haematocryal animals, like reptiles, rely on external sources of heat. 冷血动物，例如爬行动物，依靠外部热源。|  The haematocryal lizard basked in the sun. 这只冷血的蜥蜴在阳光下晒太阳。| synonyms: poikilothermic, cold-blooded | antonyms: homeothermic, warm-blooded | adjective

haemocryal:/ˌhiːmətoʊˈkraɪəl/ | n. | 冷血动物 | A cold-blooded animal; a poikilotherm. | Haematocryals are less active in cold weather. 冷血动物在寒冷的天气里不太活跃。 |  Many haematocryals hibernate during the winter. 许多冷血动物在冬天冬眠。| synonyms: poikilotherm, cold-blooded animal | antonyms: homeotherm, warm-blooded animal | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
