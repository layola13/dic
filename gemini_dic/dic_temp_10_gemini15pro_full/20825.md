blasters:/ˈblæstərz/
noun
爆破器，喷射器
A blaster is a weapon, typically a gun, that fires powerful blasts of energy or projectiles.
The Mandalorian never takes off his helmet or reveals his face, and he is skilled with his blasters and other weaponry.  曼达洛人从不摘下头盔或露出他的脸，而且他精通爆破器和其他武器。
synonyms: guns, weapons, cannons, ray guns
antonyms: shields, armor
