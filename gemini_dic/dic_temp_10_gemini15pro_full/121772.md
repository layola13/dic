misrate:/mɪsˈreɪt/ | v. | 错误地评估；低估；看轻 | To underestimate or undervalue; to assess incorrectly. |  He misrated his opponent's strength and lost the match. 他低估了对手的实力，输掉了比赛。

She misrated the importance of the project and didn't put enough effort into it. 她低估了项目的重要性，没有投入足够的精力。 | 近义词: underestimate, undervalue, misjudge, miscalculate | 反义词: overestimate, overvalue | 词性: 动词
