antimetaphysical:/ˌæntiˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl/
adj.
反形而上学的
Opposed to or rejecting metaphysics; concerned with concrete, observable things rather than abstract concepts.
His antimetaphysical stance led him to focus on empirical evidence. 他反形而上学的立场使他专注于经验性证据。

近义词:empirical, positivist, pragmatic
反义词:metaphysical, abstract, philosophical
词性:adjective
