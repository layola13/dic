antipoetically:/ˌæntipoʊˈetɪkli/
adverb|反诗歌地；缺乏诗意地；索然无味地|In a way that lacks poetic qualities; in a prosaic or uninspired manner.  It describes something expressed in a way that is opposite of poetic, lacking beauty, imagination, or emotional depth.|He described the sunset antipoetically as "a big orange blob in the sky." 他毫无诗意地将日落描述为“天空中的一个大橙色斑点”。|Synonyms: prosaically, dully, unimaginatively, flatly|Antonyms: poetically, lyrically, imaginatively, beautifully|词性: 副词
