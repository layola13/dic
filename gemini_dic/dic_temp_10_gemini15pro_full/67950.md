facie:/ˈfeɪʃiiː/ /ˈfeɪʃiː/| adj.|表面上的，貌似真实的|Appearing to be true or real, but not necessarily so.|例句：He presented a facie case for his innocence. 他提出了表面上证明自己无罪的证据。

例句：The evidence against him is prima facie strong.  针对他的证据表面上看起来很强。|近义词：ostensible, apparent, seeming|反义词：actual, real, genuine|词性:形容词
