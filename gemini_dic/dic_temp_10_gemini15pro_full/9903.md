apb:/eɪ piː biː/ | n. | 全境通告，协查通报 | An "all-points bulletin" is a broadcast message issued by law enforcement agencies to request assistance from other agencies in locating a suspect or person of interest. | The police issued an APB for the escaped convict. 警方对逃犯发出了全境通告。 | synonyms: alert, notice, warning | antonyms: concealment, secrecy | noun
