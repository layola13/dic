methuselah: /məˈθuːzələ/ | n. | 玛土撒拉, 指代高寿的人或物, 特别指酒 | A very old person or thing, especially a large bottle of wine holding six litres. |  Methuselah was said to have lived to 969 years old in the Bible. 圣经中玛土撒拉据说活了969岁。

Methuselah is also a name for a large wine bottle. 玛土撒拉也是一种大酒瓶的名称。| 近义词: patriarch, veteran, ancient | 反义词: youngster, child | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hebrew מְתוּשֶׁלַח (Məṯūšélaḥ), the name of a patriarch in the Book of Genesis said to have lived 969 years.
