medieval: /ˌmɛdiˈiːvəl/ (US), /ˌmɛdˈiːvəl/ (UK) | adj. | 中世纪的，中古时代的 | Relating to the Middle Ages, the period of European history from about the 5th to the 15th century. |  The medieval castle drew many tourists.  中世纪城堡吸引了许多游客。

The knight wore medieval armor.  骑士穿着中世纪的盔甲。

Etymology: From Old French *meien* (middle) and Latin *aevum* (age), via Anglo-Norman *medial*.  Ultimately derived from Latin *medius* (middle).

Synonyms: Middle Age, archaic, ancient, antiquated

Antonyms: modern, contemporary, current

Word type: Adjective
