manuscript:/ˈmænjuskrɪpt/ | n. | 手稿，原稿 |A document written by hand, or a handwritten or typed version of a book, article, etc., submitted for publication. | The author submitted her manuscript to the publisher. 作者将她的手稿提交给了出版商。|  The museum displayed several ancient manuscripts.  博物馆展出了几份古代手稿。 | synonym: document, text, script, draft | antonym: print, published work | noun

manuscript:/ˈmænjuskrɪpt/ | adj. | 手写的 |Written by hand. | This is a manuscript copy of the Bible.  这是一份手抄的圣经。| The library holds a collection of manuscript letters. 图书馆收藏了一批手写信件。 | synonym: handwritten, penned, inscribed | antonym: printed, typed | adjective
