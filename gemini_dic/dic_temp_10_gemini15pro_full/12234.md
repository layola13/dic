asbos: /ˈæzbɒz/ (British English)
| n. (plural) |反社会行为令 (Anti-Social Behaviour Orders)|  A civil order made against a person who has been shown, on the balance of probabilities, to have engaged in anti-social behaviour. |  Many teenagers were given ASBOs for vandalism. 许多青少年因故意破坏公物而被判处反社会行为令。| synonyms:  injunction, order, restriction | antonyms:  permission, allowance | noun
