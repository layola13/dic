argentic:/ɑːrˈdʒɛntɪk/
adjective
銀的；含銀的；銀鹽的
Of or containing silver. Relating to photography using silver salts.
Argentic photography offers a unique aesthetic. 銀鹽攝影具有獨特的審美。
The argentic process is still preferred by some photographers. 一些攝影師仍然偏愛銀鹽工藝。
Synonyms: silver, silvery
Antonyms: non-silver, digital (in the context of photography)
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *argentique*, from Latin *argentum* (silver) + *-icus* (suffix forming adjectives).
