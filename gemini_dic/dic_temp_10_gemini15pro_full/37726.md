comparing:/kəmˈpɛərɪŋ/ | v. | 比较，对照 |  The act of examining two or more things in order to discover similarities and differences. | She is comparing prices at different stores. 她正在比较不同商店的价格。|  He was comparing his new phone with his old one. 他在比较他的新手机和旧手机。 | contrasting, differentiating, juxtaposing |  n/a | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *comparynge*, present participle of *comparien*, from Old French *comparer*, from Latin *comparāre* (“to couple, match, pair, compare”), from *com-* (“together”) + *par* (“equal”).
