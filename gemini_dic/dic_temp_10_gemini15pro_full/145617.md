overconsiderately:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈsɪdərətli/ | adv. | 过分体贴地，过分周到地 |  Showing excessive consideration for the needs and feelings of others; too thoughtfully. | He overconsiderately offered to carry all her bags, even though she only had one small purse. 他过分殷勤地提出要帮她提所有的包，即使她只有一个很小的钱包。| Example: She overconsiderately avoided mentioning his recent job loss. 她过分体贴地避免提及他最近失业的事。 | Synonyms: excessively thoughtfully, too carefully, overly solicitous | Antonyms: thoughtlessly, inconsiderately, carelessly | Part of Speech: Adverb
