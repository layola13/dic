misanthropical:/ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪkl/| adj.|厌世的；遁世的；反人类的|Disliking humankind and avoiding human society.|He became increasingly misanthropical in his later years, preferring the company of his dogs to that of people. 他晚年变得越来越厌世，宁愿与狗为伴，也不愿与人为伍。|例句：His misanthropical views made him unpopular. 他厌世的观点使他不受欢迎。

例句：The hermit lived a misanthropical existence deep in the woods.  这个隐士在森林深处过着遁世的生活。

近义词：antisocial, cynical, pessimistic, reclusive|反义词：philanthropic, sociable, gregarious|词性: adjective
