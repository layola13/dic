forgotten:/fərˈɡɒtn/ (US) /fərˈɡɑːtn/ (UK)| adj.|被遗忘的；忘记的|Past participle of "forget," meaning something that has been lost to memory or no longer remembered.|例句：The forgotten toys lay gathering dust in the attic. 被遗忘的玩具在阁楼里积满了灰尘。|例句：He felt like a forgotten man, lost and alone. 他感觉自己像个被遗忘的人，失落又孤独。|近义词：unremembered, overlooked, neglected|反义词：remembered, cherished, valued|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *forġieten, past participle of forġietan "to forget," from for- "away" + ġietan "to get."  The prefix for- implies loss or removal, similar to its use in "forgo" or "forsake."
