fila:/ˈfiːlə/ (US), /ˈfɪlə/ (UK)| n. |线，细丝，纤维|A slender thread or filament, especially one found in animal or plant structures. |例句：The spider spun a silken fila.  蜘蛛吐出一根丝线。

例句：The fila of the light bulb broke. 灯泡的灯丝断了。|近义词：thread, filament, fiber, strand|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
