fitness:/ˈfɪtnəs/| n.|健康; 健壮; 适宜; 合格|the condition of being physically fit and healthy.  The quality of being suitable for a particular purpose or role.|例句：He works hard to maintain his fitness. 他努力锻炼以保持健康。|近义词：health, strength, suitability, aptness|反义词:unfitness, weakness, inappropriateness, inability|noun
