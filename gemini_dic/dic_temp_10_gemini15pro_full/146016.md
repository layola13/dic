overdraft:/ˈoʊvərdræft/ /ˈoʊvərdrɑːft/| n.|透支；透支额度|An overdraft is an agreement with your bank that allows you to spend more money than you have in your account, up to a certain limit.  透支是指您与银行达成的一项协议，允许您支出的金额超过您账户中的金额，但有一定的限额。| I had to pay overdraft charges this month. 这个月我不得不支付透支费用。|My account is $500 in overdraft. 我的账户透支了500美元。|近义词：deficit, shortfall|反义词:credit, surplus|词性:名词

overdrafts:/ˈoʊvərdræfts/ /ˈoʊvərdrɑːfts/| n.|透支（复数）| The plural form of "overdraft".  透支的复数形式。|  She incurred heavy overdrafts to finance her business. 她为了给自己的生意融资而大量透支。|He's always struggling with overdrafts. 他总是为透支而苦恼。|近义词：deficits, shortfalls|反义词:credits, surpluses|词性:名词
