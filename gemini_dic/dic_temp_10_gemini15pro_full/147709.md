overpreparing:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈpɛrɪŋ/ | n. |过度准备|The act of preparing excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or causing anxiety.|She was so worried about the presentation that she ended up overpreparing and confusing herself. 她非常担心演讲，结果准备过度，把自己都搞糊涂了。|近义词：overdoing it, overpracticing|反义词：underpreparing|词性：名词 (Gerund)


overprepare:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈpɛr/ | v. |过度准备|To prepare excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or causing anxiety.|He overprepared for the exam and ended up burning himself out. 他为考试准备过度，结果把自己累垮了。|近义词： overdo, overpractice|反义词：underprepare|词性：动词
