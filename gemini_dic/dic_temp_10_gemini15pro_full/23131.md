词源：From Middle English *burne, *borne, from Old English burna (“stream, brook, spring, fountain, well”), from Proto-Germanic *brunnō (“spring, well, source”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrew- (“to bubble, boil, brew”). Cognate with Scots burn (“stream, brook”), West Frisian boarne (“spring, source”), Dutch born (“spring, well”), German Brunnen (“well, fountain”), Danish borne (“well”), Swedish brunn (“well”). Related also to brew.
