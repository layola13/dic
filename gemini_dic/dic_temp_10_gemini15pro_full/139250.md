obligatory:/əˈblɪɡətɔːri/ (US), /əˈblɪɡətri/ (UK)| adj.|强制的，义务的，必须的|Required by a legal, moral, or other rule; compulsory.|Attendance at the meeting is obligatory.  出席会议是强制性的。|It is obligatory for all employees to attend the training session. 所有员工都必须参加培训课程。|近义词：compulsory, mandatory, required, necessary|反义词：optional, voluntary|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *obligātōrius, from Latin obligātus (past participle of obligāre ‘to bind, oblige’), from ob- ‘towards’ + ligāre ‘to bind’.
