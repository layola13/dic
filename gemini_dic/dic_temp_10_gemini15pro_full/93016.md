idiothermic:/ˌɪdioˈθɜːrmɪk/| adj.|恒温的，自体温调节的|Maintaining a constant body temperature by generating internal heat; warm-blooded.  Able to regulate its own body temperature, typically used for animals.|Example: Birds and mammals are idiothermic animals, able to maintain a stable internal temperature regardless of the external environment. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物，能够保持稳定的内部温度，而不受外部环境的影响。

Example:  Unlike reptiles, idiothermic creatures can remain active even in cold weather. 与爬行动物不同，恒温动物即使在寒冷的天气里也能保持活跃。|近义词：endothermic, homeothermic, warm-blooded|反义词:ectothermic, poikilothermic, cold-blooded|词性:adjective
