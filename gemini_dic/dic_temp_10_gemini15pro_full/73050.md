footlike:/ˈfʊtlaɪk/
adjective
像足的，足状的
Resembling or shaped like a foot.
The roots are footlike, spreading out to anchor the plant.  这些根像足一样，伸展出去固定植物。
The creature had strange, footlike appendages.  这个生物有奇怪的、像足一样的附属物。
近义词:pedal, pes, podal
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "handlike")
词性:adjective
