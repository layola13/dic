chiselings:/ˈtʃɪzəlɪŋz/
n.
雕刻物，凿屑
pieces of stone, wood, or other material that have been cut away while making a sculpture or carving
The floor was covered in chiselings from the sculptor's work. 地板上满是雕刻家工作时凿下来的碎屑。
