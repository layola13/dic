allomerism:/əˈlɒmərɪzəm/| n. |同质异晶|The existence of different crystalline forms of the same substance, or of different crystalline forms of substances of similar chemical composition.|Allotropes of carbon, such as diamond and graphite, exhibit allomerism. 碳的同素异形体，例如钻石和石墨，表现出同质异晶现象。|Near synonyms: polymorphism, allotropy|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps isomorphism if considering strict structural identity)|Noun
