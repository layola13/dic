burhel:/ˈbɜːrhel/| n. |岩羊|A wild sheep (Pseudois nayaur) of the Himalayas, having large, slightly curved horns.|例句：The burhel nimbly navigated the steep cliffs. 岩羊灵活地穿梭于陡峭的悬崖。

例句：Burhel are adapted to high altitudes and harsh weather. 岩羊适应高海拔和恶劣的天气。|近义词：blue sheep, bharal|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Hindi bharal. Ultimately from Nepali bharal.
