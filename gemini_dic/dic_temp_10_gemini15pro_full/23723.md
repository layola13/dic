brassish:/ˈbræsɪʃ/| adj.|略带黄铜味的；像黄铜的；(指声音)刺耳的，粗糙的|Somewhat resembling brass in color, taste, or sound; harsh and grating.|The air had a brassish, metallic tang. 空气中弥漫着一股黄铜般的金属味。|近义词:brassy, metallic, harsh|反义词:mellow, soft, sweet|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From brass + -ish.  "Brass" itself comes from Old English *bræs. The suffix "-ish" is a common Germanic suffix used to form adjectives, indicating resemblance or approximation.
