crowberry:/ˈkroʊˌbɛri/ | n. |岩高兰，黑果越橘 |A low-growing evergreen shrub (Empetrum nigrum) of northern and alpine regions, having small, heathlike leaves and black, edible berries.| The crowberry is a hardy plant that thrives in harsh conditions. 岩高兰是一种耐寒植物，在恶劣的条件下也能茁壮成长。|近义词:black crowberry, crakeberry |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From crow + berry.  The "crow" part of the name likely refers to the black color of the berries, which resemble crow droppings.
