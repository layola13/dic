niacin:/ˈnaɪəsɪn/ /ˈniːəsɪn/| n.|烟酸，尼克酸，维生素B3|A form of vitamin B3, important for the body's use of carbohydrates, fats, and proteins.|Example:  He started taking niacin supplements to improve his cholesterol levels. 他开始服用烟酸补充剂来改善他的胆固醇水平。

|Example: Niacin deficiency can lead to pellagra.  缺乏烟酸会导致糙皮病。|近义词：vitamin B3, nicotinic acid|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
niacin:  From nicotinic acid + -in (a chemical suffix).  Nicotinic acid itself comes from nicotine (from Jean Nicot, who introduced tobacco to France) + -ic (meaning “of or relating to”) + acid.
