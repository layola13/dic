dressers:/ˈdrɛsərz/| n.|梳妆台；碗柜；穿着讲究的人|A piece of furniture with drawers, often used for storing clothes, especially in a bedroom. Also, a person who dresses well or in a particular way.|例句：She kept her jewelry in one of the dressers. 她把她的珠宝放在一个梳妆台中。

例句：He's always been one of the best dressers in the office. 他一直是办公室里穿着最讲究的人之一。|近义词：bureau, chest of drawers, wardrobe (for furniture); fashionable person, stylish person (for person)|反义词:sloppy dresser (for person)|词性:名词
