gilden:/ˈɡɪldən/| adj.|镀金的，金色的|Covered with gold or a golden color; having a golden appearance. |例句：The gilden frame shimmered in the candlelight.  镀金的画框在烛光下闪闪发光。

例句：The setting sun cast a gilden glow over the mountains. 落日给群山披上了一层金色的光辉。|近义词：golden, gold-plated, gilded|反义词:dull, tarnished|词性:形容词
etymology: From Middle English *gilden, *gylden, from Old English gylden (“golden”), from Proto-Germanic *gulþinaz (“golden”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰl̥tós (“gold, yellow”). Cognate with West Frisian gouden (“golden”), Dutch gouden (“golden”), German golden (“golden”), Danish gylden (“golden”), Swedish gyllene (“golden”).
