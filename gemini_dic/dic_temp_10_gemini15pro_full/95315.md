incautiousness:/ɪnˌkɔːʃəsnəs/
n.
粗心大意，轻率
lack of caution; rashness.
His incautiousness led to a serious accident. 他的粗心大意导致了一场严重的事故。
Synonyms: recklessness, imprudence, carelessness, rashness, heedlessness
Antonyms: caution, prudence, carefulness, circumspection, foresight
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + cautious + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Cautious comes from Late Latin *cautiosus, from Latin cautus (cautious, wary), past participle of cavere (to beware, take heed).
