crackpotism: /ˈkrækˌpɒtɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈkrækˌpɑːtɪzəm/ (US) | n. | 偏执狂；古怪行为；怪诞的言行 |  The practice of dismissing someone's views or actions as eccentric, irrational, or indicative of mental instability, especially when those views or actions challenge established norms or power structures. |  The government dismissed the protestors' concerns as mere crackpotism. 政府将抗议者的担忧斥为纯粹的偏执狂。|  She attributed his unconventional ideas to crackpotism. 她将他不寻常的想法归因于古怪行为。| 近义词：eccentricity, irrationality, paranoia, craziness | 反义词：sanity, rationality, normalcy | 词性: 名词
