gimbal:/ˈɡɪmbəl/| n.|万向节，平衡环|A device that allows an object to rotate freely in multiple directions, typically used to stabilize cameras or other instruments.|The drone's gimbal kept the camera steady despite the wind.  无人机的万向节在风中保持了摄像机的稳定。|Synonyms: stabilizer, mount, pivot|Antonyms: fixed, stationary|noun

gimbal:/ˈɡɪmbəl/| v.|用平衡环支撑|To support or suspend something with a gimbal.|They gimbaled the camera to minimize vibrations during filming.  他们用万向节支撑相机，以最大限度地减少拍摄过程中的振动。|Synonyms: stabilize, balance, suspend|Antonyms: fix, secure|verb
