nonenviously:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəsli/
adv.
没有嫉妒地，不带嫉妒地
without envy; in a way that does not show envy.
She nonenviously admired her friend's new car. 她毫不嫉妒地欣赏着她朋友的新车。
近义词: ungrudgingly, generously
反义词: enviously, jealously
词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "envious" (adjective meaning "feeling or showing envy") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
