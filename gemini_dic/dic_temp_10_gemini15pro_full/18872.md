besetting:/bɪˈsɛtɪŋ/
adjective
困扰的；缠身的；萦绕的
Habitually attacking or troubling; persistently clinging.
His besetting sin is pride. 他最大的毛病是骄傲。
近义词: persistent, chronic, inherent, inveterate, ingrained
反义词: occasional, sporadic, fleeting, transient
词性: adjective

besetting:/bɪˈsɛtɪŋ/
noun
(通常指不好的)习惯，嗜好，弱点
a fault or weakness that you have
One of her besetting sins is a fondness for sweets. 她的一大嗜好就是爱吃甜食。
近义词: weakness, flaw, fault, shortcoming, failing
反义词: strength, virtue
词性: noun


词源:
From the verb "beset"  + "-ing" suffix.  "Beset" comes from Old English "besettan" (to set around, surround, besiege), from "be-" (around) + "settan" (to set).
