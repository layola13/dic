lithotripsy: /ˈlɪθəˌtrɪpsi/ (US) /ˈlɪθəʊˌtrɪpsi/ (UK)| n.| 碎石术，体外震波碎石术 |The medical procedure of breaking up stones in the kidney or bladder using shock waves. |例句：He underwent lithotripsy to remove kidney stones. 他接受了碎石术来去除肾结石。

例句：Lithotripsy is a non-invasive procedure for treating kidney stones. 碎石术是一种治疗肾结石的非侵入性手术。|近义词： extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but open surgery might be considered an opposite approach)|词性: noun
