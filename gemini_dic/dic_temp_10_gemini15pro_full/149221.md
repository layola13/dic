oxymorons: /ˌɒksɪˈmɔːrɒnz/ | n. |矛盾修饰法；逆喻 | A figure of speech in which apparently contradictory terms appear in conjunction. |  The phrase "cruel kindness" is an oxymoron.  残酷的仁慈是一个矛盾修饰法。 | synonyms: paradox, contradiction, antithesis | antonyms:  tautology, pleonasm | noun
