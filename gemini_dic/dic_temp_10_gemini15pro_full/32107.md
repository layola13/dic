chestily:/ˈtʃɛstɪli/
adverb|骄傲地，昂首挺胸地|In a way that suggests self-importance or pride.|He walked chestily into the room, clearly pleased with himself. 他昂首挺胸地走进房间，显然对自己很满意。|例句：She chestily declared her victory. 她骄傲地宣布了她的胜利。|近义词：proudly, haughtily, arrogantly|反义词：humbly, modestly|词性: 副词

chestily:/ˈtʃɛstɪli/
adjective|骄傲的，昂首挺胸的 (较少用作形容词)|Having or showing excessive pride in oneself.|His chestily manner annoyed many people. 他那骄傲自大的态度惹恼了很多人。|例句：He gave a chestily speech about his accomplishments. 他做了一个关于自己成就的骄傲自大的演讲。|近义词：proud, haughty, arrogant|反义词：humble, modest|词性: 形容词
