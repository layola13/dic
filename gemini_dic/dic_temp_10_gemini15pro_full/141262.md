opalescently:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsəntli/ | adv. |乳白色地，彩虹色地|Exhibiting a play of colors like that of an opal; iridescently. | The moonstone glowed opalescently in the dim light. 月光石在昏暗的光线下发出乳白色的光芒。| The surface of the bubble shimmered opalescently. 泡沫的表面闪烁着彩虹般的光芒。| 近义词：iridescently, shimmeringly, pearly | 反义词：dully, plainly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From opalescent + -ly.  Opalescent from opal + -escent. Opal from Latin opalus, from Greek opallios.
