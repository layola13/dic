laxer:/ˈlæksər/ /ˈlæksɚ/| adj.|更松懈的，更宽松的|Less strict or severe; less demanding or rigorous.|The school has a laxer policy on dress code than most. 这所学校的着装规定比大多数学校都宽松。|Synonyms: looser, slacker, more lenient, more relaxed|Antonyms: stricter, tighter, more rigorous, more demanding|adjective

laxer:/ˈlæksər/ /ˈlæksɚ/| n.|放松的人，松懈的人 (较少用作名词)|A person who is lax, especially in morals or discipline. |He was considered a laxer by his colleagues due to his casual approach to deadlines.  他的同事认为他是一个松懈的人，因为他对截止日期的态度很随意。|Synonyms: slacker, idler|Antonyms: disciplinarian, stickler|noun
