austerities:/ɔːˈstɛrɪtiz/
|n.|紧缩；艰苦；严厉；苦行|Difficult economic conditions created by government measures to reduce public expenditure|The government imposed harsh austerities to control inflation. 政府实施了严厉的紧缩措施来控制通货膨胀。|synonyms:  stringency, severity, hardship, asceticism|antonyms: luxuries, extravagances, indulgences
|noun|
