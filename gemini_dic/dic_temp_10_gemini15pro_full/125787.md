muriatiferous:/ˌmjʊəriˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含氯化物的；产生盐酸盐的
Containing or producing muriatic acid or its salts (chlorides).
The muriatiferous springs were known for their therapeutic properties. 这些含氯化物的泉水以其治疗功效而闻名。
The geologist identified the rock as muriatiferous limestone. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为含氯化物的石灰岩。
Synonyms: chloride-bearing, saline
Antonyms: non-saline, fresh
