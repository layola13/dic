computerate: /kəmˈpjuːtəreɪt/
adjective
精通电脑的；计算机知识丰富的
Proficient in using computers; knowledgeable about computers.
He is highly computerate and can solve most software problems. 他精通计算机，可以解决大多数软件问题。
She's computerate enough to handle the company's database. 她有足够的计算机知识来处理公司的数据库。
近义词: computer-literate, tech-savvy
反义词: computer-illiterate, technologically challenged
词性: adjective
词源:  A blend of "computer" and "literate," coined in the late 20th century by analogy with terms like "literate" and "numerate."
