misplaned:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪnd/
verb
计划错误，筹划不周
planned badly or incorrectly
The project was misplanned from the start. 这个项目从一开始就计划不周。
近义词:bungled, botched, messed up
反义词:well-planned, organized
