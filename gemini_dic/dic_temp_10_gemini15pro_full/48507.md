deergrass:/ˈdɪrˌɡræs/| n. |鹿草|A tall, perennial bunchgrass of the genus *Muhlenbergia*, native to western North America, often used for forage and erosion control. It's characterized by its large, open flower clusters.|The deergrass provided good grazing for the elk.|Synonyms:  muhly grass (especially *Muhlenbergia rigens*) |Antonyms: N/A|noun
