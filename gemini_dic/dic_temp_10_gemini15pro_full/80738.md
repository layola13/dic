gosh:/ɡɒʃ/ (UK) /ɡɑːʃ/ (US)| int.|哎呀；天哪|An exclamation of surprise, alarm, or annoyance. |Gosh, that was close! 天哪，那真是千钧一发！|Gosh, I've lost my keys again. 哎呀，我又把钥匙丢了。|近义词：goodness, heavens, gee, crikey|反义词: (As an interjection, gosh doesn't have a direct antonym.  Words expressing disagreement or disapproval might be considered contrasting, like "nonsense" or "rubbish.")|词性:感叹词
