hyperpatriotism:/ˌhaɪpərˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/
n.|极端爱国主义，过度的爱国主义|Excessive patriotism; fanatical patriotism.  It goes beyond simple love of one's country and can manifest as aggressive nationalism or blind support for government policies, even if harmful.|His hyperpatriotism led him to believe that any criticism of the government was an act of treason.|近义词: ultranationalism, jingoism, chauvinism|反义词: disloyalty, treason|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *hyper-* (Greek: 'over, beyond') + *patriotism* (from French *patriote*, 'fellow countryman,' from Greek *patriōtēs*, from *patris*, 'fatherland').
