entomotomy:/ˌɛntəˈmɒtəmi/ | n. |昆虫解剖学|The study of the internal and external structures of insects. | He is specializing in entomotomy for his doctoral studies. 他正在专攻昆虫解剖学以获得博士学位。|  Entomologists often use entomotomy to understand insect physiology. 昆虫学家经常使用昆虫解剖学来了解昆虫生理学。 |  insect anatomy, insect dissection |  -| noun
Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἔντομον (éntomon, “insect”) + -τομία (-tomía, from τέμνειν (témnein, “to cut”)).
