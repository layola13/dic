corrected:/kəˈrɛktɪd/ | adj. | 已改正的，修正的 | past participle of the verb "correct," meaning something has been amended or rectified. |  Example: The corrected version of the essay was much better.  已改正的论文版本好多了。| Example:  He submitted the corrected document to his boss. 他把修改过的文件交给了老板。 | Synonyms: amended, rectified, revised, improved, fixed | Antonyms: flawed, faulty, incorrect, erroneous, mistaken | Verb (past participle)


/kəˈrɛkt/ | v. | 改正，纠正 | to make something right or accurate, to remove errors or faults. | Example:  Please correct any mistakes you find in this report.  请改正你在本报告中发现的任何错误。| Example: The teacher corrected the student's pronunciation. 老师纠正了学生的读音。 | Synonyms: amend, rectify, revise, improve, fix, remedy | Antonyms: worsen, damage, ruin, spoil, break | Verb
