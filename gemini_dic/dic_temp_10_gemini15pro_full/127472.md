necessitarianism:/nəˌsesɪˈtɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
必然论，决定论
The philosophical doctrine that all events are determined by antecedent causes and are therefore inevitable.
例句：Necessitarianism implies that free will is an illusion. 必然论意味着自由意志是一种幻觉。

例句：Some philosophers argue that necessitarianism is incompatible with moral responsibility. 一些哲学家认为必然论与道德责任不相容。

近义词：determinism, fatalism
反义词：indeterminism, libertarianism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *necessitarius (unattested) + -ism.  From Latin necessitas (“necessity”) + -arius.  Surface analysis is necessity + -arian + -ism.
