amulet:/ˈæmjʊlət/ | n. |护身符；驱邪物|An object that is believed to protect its owner from harm or evil. |She wore an amulet to ward off evil spirits. 她戴着护身符来驱邪。|近义词: charm, talisman |反义词: curse |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *amulette*, from Late Latin *amuletum*  of uncertain origin, perhaps from Arabic *ḥimālah* ("something carried") or Latin *amolior* ("to avert, turn away, remove").
