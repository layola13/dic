egomaniacal: /ˌiːɡoʊˌmeɪniˈæk(ə)l/ | adj. | 极度自我的；自恋的；自大狂的 |  Excessively self-centered; obsessed with oneself and one's own importance; characteristic of an egomaniac. | He has an egomaniacal belief in his own importance.  他极度自负，认为自己很重要。 |  narcissistic, self-absorbed, conceited, vain | humble, modest, selfless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ego + maniac + -al.  "Ego" comes from Latin, meaning "I". "Maniac" comes from Greek "mania," meaning madness or frenzy.
