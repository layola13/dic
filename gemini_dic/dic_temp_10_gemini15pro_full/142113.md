orients:/ˈɔːriənts/ | v. | 使适应；确定方向；使朝向 | to adjust to a new situation, environment, etc.; to find one's position in relation to one's surroundings; to direct something towards something else |  She quickly oriented herself to the new city. 她很快适应了这座新城市。| synonyms: adapt, adjust, acclimate, familiarize, align, direct | antonyms: disorient, confuse | verb

orients:/ˈɔːriənts/ | n. | 东方国家；东方文化 | the countries of East and Southeast Asia; the cultures of East and Southeast Asia | The gallery specializes in art from the Orients. 这家画廊专门收藏来自东方国家的艺术品。| synonyms: East, Asia | antonyms: West, Occident | noun
