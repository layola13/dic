colona:/kəˈloʊnə/| n.| 结肠的一部分；居民；殖民者|A part of the colon; an inhabitant; a colonist.| The doctor examined the patient's colona. 医生检查了病人的结肠部分。|Synonyms: inhabitant, settler, resident, colonist|Antonyms: native, indigenous|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *colona, from Latin colonus (“farmer, husbandman, settler, planter, colonist”), from colō (“I cultivate, till, inhabit”).
