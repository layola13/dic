capriform:/ˈkæprɪfɔːrm/
adjective
山羊形的；形似山羊的
Resembling or shaped like a goat, especially having a goat's head.
The gargoyle had a capriform appearance, with its horns and beard.  这个滴水嘴兽有着山羊的形状，有角和胡须。
The demon was depicted with a capriform head and cloven hooves.  这个恶魔被描绘成有山羊的头和偶蹄。
近义词:goat-like, hircine
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "anthropomorphic" if contrasting with human-like)
词性:adjective
