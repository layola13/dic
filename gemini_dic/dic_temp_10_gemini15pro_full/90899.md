hunterlike:/ˈhʌntərˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像猎人的；具有猎人特征的 | Resembling or characteristic of a hunter; predatory. |  The cat stalked the mouse with a hunterlike focus.  这只猫像猎人一样专注地跟踪着老鼠。|  predatory, hawkish, sharp, keen,  vigilant | docile, gentle, timid, passive,  inattentive | adjective

Etymology->
From "hunter" + "-like". "Hunter" comes from Old English "hunta" meaning "hunter," from Proto-Germanic "*huntōn," from Proto-Indo-European "*kéntos," meaning "killer," from the root "*kent-," meaning "to prick, to kill."  "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "similar to, characteristic of."
