fat:/fæt/
adj.|肥胖的，厚的；丰厚的|Containing a lot of fat; thick; plentiful.|例句：The fat cat sat on the mat.  肥猫坐在垫子上。|近义词：plump, chubby, obese, overweight, rich, greasy|反义词：thin, lean, skinny, slender|词性:形容词

fat:/fæt/
n.|脂肪，肥肉|The oily or greasy substance found in animal bodies, or a similar substance found in plants.|例句：The doctor advised him to reduce his intake of fat. 医生建议他减少脂肪摄入。|近义词：grease, blubber, lard|反义词：lean meat|词性:名词

fat:/fæt/
v.|养肥，使肥胖|To make something or someone fat.|例句：They fattened the pigs for market. 他们把猪养肥了以备出售。|近义词：fatten up, plump up|反义词：slim down|词性:动词

词源：Old English *fǣtt, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vet and German Fett.
