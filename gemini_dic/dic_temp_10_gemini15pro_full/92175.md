hyperspeculative: /ˌhaɪpərˈspɛkjʊlətɪv/ | adj. |过度投机的，高度 speculative |Based on or involving speculation that is extreme or exaggerated.  Characterized by conjecture and risk-taking, often to an excessive degree. |例句：The hyperspeculative nature of the cryptocurrency market makes it a risky investment. 加密货币市场的过度投机性使其成为一项高风险投资。

例句：Investing in hyperspeculative ventures can lead to significant losses. 投资于过度投机的企业可能会导致重大损失。|近义词：highly speculative, excessively speculative, over-speculative, very risky, reckless |反义词：conservative, cautious, low-risk, safe |词性：adjective
