auditioner:/ɔːˈdɪʃənər/| n.|试镜者，参加试演的人|A person who auditions, typically for a performing role.|Many auditioners were waiting nervously outside the theater. 许多试镜者在剧院外紧张地等待着。|Synonyms: candidate, applicant, hopeful, aspirant|Antonyms: judge, evaluator, assessor|noun


auditioner:/ɔːˈdɪʃənər/| n.|试听者|A person who listens to a performance, especially a musical one, in order to assess it.|The auditioners were impressed by her vocal range.  评委们对她的音域印象深刻。|Synonyms: listener, observer, critic|Antonyms: performer, player, musician|noun
