natica:/nəˈtaɪkə/
noun
玉螺
A genus of predatory sea snails, typically having a globular shell with a short spire.
The natica drilled a neat hole in the clam shell. 玉螺在蛤蜊壳上钻了一个整齐的洞。
synonyms: moon snail, necklace shell
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a genus of snail)
