atonic:/əˈtɒnɪk/ /eɪˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|无重音的，不重读的，无调的|Lacking tone or accent; unstressed.|The atonic syllable in the word "water" is the second syllable.|近义词：unstressed, unaccented|反义词: tonic, stressed|adjective

atonic:/əˈtɒnɪk/ /eɪˈtɑːnɪk/| n.|无重音音节，无重音字|An unaccented syllable or word.|The word "a" is often an atonic.|近义词：unstressed syllable|反义词: tonic syllable|noun
