electroreduction:/ɪˌlɛktroʊrɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 电还原 | The process of reducing a substance by the addition of electrons, typically achieved by electrolysis.  | Electroreduction is a key process in the production of some metals. 电还原是某些金属生产中的关键过程。| Electroreduction plays an important role in converting carbon dioxide into useful chemicals. 电还原在将二氧化碳转化为有用化学品方面发挥着重要作用。|  synonyms: electrolytic reduction | antonyms: electrooxidation | noun
