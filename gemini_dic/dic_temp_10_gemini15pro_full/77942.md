genomically:/dʒɪˈnoʊmɪkli/
adjective
基因组地，基因组学地
Relating to or involving an organism's genome.  Based on the genome.
The disease was genomically diagnosed.  这种疾病是通过基因组诊断的。
Scientists are genomically mapping the virus. 科学家正在绘制该病毒的基因组图谱。
近义词: genetically
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider context-specific opposites like phenotypically)
词性: adjective
