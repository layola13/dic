millponds:/ˈmɪlpɑːndz/
|n.|
|磨坊池塘（复数）|
|A body of water near a mill, often used to store water to power the mill.  It is often characterized by its stillness and calm surface.|
|The millponds were perfectly still, reflecting the trees like mirrors.|磨坊池塘非常平静，像镜子一样倒映着树木。

|The children loved to skate on the frozen millponds in winter.|孩子们喜欢在冬天结冰的磨坊池塘上滑冰。

|近义词:ponds, lakes|
|反义词:rivers, streams|
|词性:名词|


millpond:/ˈmɪlpɑːnd/
|n.|
|磨坊池塘（单数）|
|A body of water near a mill, often used to store water to power the mill. It is often characterized by its stillness and calm surface.|
|The millpond was so still you could see the fish swimming below the surface.|磨坊池塘非常平静，你可以看到鱼在水面下游动。

|The old millpond had become overgrown with weeds.|旧的磨坊池塘已经杂草丛生。
|近义词:pond, lake|
|反义词:river, stream|
|词性:名词|


词源:
From Middle English *myllepond*, from *mylle* (“mill”) + *pond* (“pond”).  Essentially, it's a compound word combining "mill" and "pond."
