incautiously:/ɪnˈkɔːʃəsli/| adv.|不谨慎地，轻率地|In a manner that lacks caution or prudence; without due care or forethought.|He incautiously revealed the secret to his rival. 他不慎地将秘密泄露给了他的对手。|Synonyms: recklessly, carelessly, heedlessly, rashly, imprudently|Antonyms: cautiously, carefully, prudently, thoughtfully, deliberately|adverb
