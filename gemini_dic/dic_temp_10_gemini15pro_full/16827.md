bash:/bæʃ/ | n. vt. | 猛击，痛击；抨击，严厉批评 | A heavy blow. To hit hard. To criticize harshly. | He bashed his head on the low beam. 他把头撞到了低矮的横梁上。

The critics bashed the new play.  评论家严厉批评了这部新戏。 | 近义词：hit, strike, slam, criticize, attack | 反义词：praise, commend | 词性: 名词, 动词


bash:/bæʃ/ | n. |  盛宴，狂欢 | A large, festive party. |  We had a real bash for New Year's Eve. 我们为除夕举办了一场真正的狂欢。| 近义词：party, celebration, feast | 反义词：-| 词性: 名词

PHRASE->

have a bash (at something) = 试一试，尝试一下

bash something in = 打破，砸碎

bash something out =  快速完成，草草写出

bash into someone/something =  撞到某人/某物

bash someone's head in =  痛打某人
