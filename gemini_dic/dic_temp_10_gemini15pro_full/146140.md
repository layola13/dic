overemulates:/ˌoʊvərˈɛmjuleɪts/
verb
过度模仿
To emulate something excessively, often to the point of parody or ineffectiveness.  Going beyond simply imitating, but doing so in an exaggerated or unnecessary way.
He overemulates his favorite actor, to the point where it's just comical. 他过度模仿他最喜欢的演员，到了滑稽的地步。
synonyms:  overimitates, mimics excessively, parodies, apes
antonyms:  ignores, neglects, disregards
词性: 动词
