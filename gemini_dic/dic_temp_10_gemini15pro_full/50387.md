deportation:/ˌdiːpɔːrˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 驱逐出境，放逐 |The act of deporting a foreigner from a country. |  The government ordered the deportation of all illegal immigrants. 政府下令驱逐所有非法移民。

The deportation of the refugees caused international outrage.  难民的驱逐引起了国际社会的愤怒。 | 近义词：expulsion, banishment, removal | 反义词： repatriation, admission, entry | 词性: noun


deport:/dɪˈpɔːrt/ | v. | 驱逐出境，放逐 |To expel (a foreigner) from a country. | The government plans to deport the illegal immigrants. 政府计划驱逐非法移民。

He was deported for his involvement in criminal activities. 他因参与犯罪活动而被驱逐出境。 | 近义词：expel, banish, exile | 反义词： admit, repatriate, welcome | 词性: verb
