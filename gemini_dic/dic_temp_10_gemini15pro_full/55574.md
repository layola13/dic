disturber:/dɪˈstɜːrbər/| n.|扰乱者，捣乱者|A person or thing that disturbs.  Someone or something that interrupts someone or something else, causing confusion or trouble.|The loud music from the neighbor's party was a real disturber of the peace. 隔壁派对传来的嘈杂音乐真是扰乱了安宁。|Synonyms: troublemaker, agitator, disrupter|Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, pacifier|noun
