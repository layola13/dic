in-the-abstract:/ɪn ði ˈæbstrækt/| adverbial phrase|抽象地，理论上|In a general way, without referring to specific instances or details; theoretically.|例句：It sounds like a good plan in the abstract, but I'm not sure how it would work in practice.  理论上来说，这听起来像个好计划，但我不知道在实践中它会如何运作。

例句：While justice is a noble concept in the abstract, its application can be complex and challenging.  虽然正义在抽象概念上很崇高，但它的应用可能复杂且具有挑战性。|近义词：theoretically, conceptually, generally, ideally|反义词：concretely, specifically, practically|词性：adverbial phrase
