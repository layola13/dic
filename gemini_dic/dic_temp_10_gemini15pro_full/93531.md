|近义词：conceivability, thinkableness|反义词：inconceivability, unthinkableness|词性: 名词


Etymology:
From imaginable + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin imaginari  "to form an image, imagine, picture to oneself,"  from  imago "image, likeness."
