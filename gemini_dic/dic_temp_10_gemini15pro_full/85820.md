hazer:/ˈheɪzər/ (US), /ˈheɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|起雾者；戏弄新生的人；使模糊不清的东西|Someone or something that creates haze, either literally (like a fog machine) or figuratively (like an unclear explanation).|The hazer filled the room with smoke. 起雾者让房间里充满了烟雾。

The fraternity's hazing rituals were cruel and dangerous. 兄弟会的戏弄新生的仪式既残酷又危险。|近义词：fogger, teaser, tormentor|反义词:clarifier|noun
