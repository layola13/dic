gabbled:/ˈɡæbld/| v.|急促而不清楚地说；喋喋不休|He gabbled nervously about the accident. 他紧张地含糊不清地讲述了事故的经过。|例句：She gabbled excitedly about her new job. 她兴奋地絮叨着她的新工作。|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle|反义词:articulate, enunciate|verb

gabbled:/ˈɡæbld/| adj.|急促而不清楚的；喋喋不休的|His gabbled explanation was hard to understand. 他含糊不清的解释很难理解。|例句：The message was a gabbled stream of consciousness. 这条消息是一连串语无伦次的意识流。|近义词：incoherent, rambling|反义词:clear, articulate|adjective
