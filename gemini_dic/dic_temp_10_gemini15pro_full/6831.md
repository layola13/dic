anastigmatic:/ˌænəstɪɡˈmætɪk/
adjective
消像差的; 正光性的
Corrected for astigmatism; free of astigmatism.  An anastigmatic lens is a photographic lens capable of forming an image essentially free of distortion.
This camera has an anastigmatic lens, ensuring sharp images. 这款相机有消像差镜头，确保图像清晰。
The anastigmatic telescope provided clear and undistorted views of the night sky. 这架消像差望远镜提供了清晰无失真的夜空景象。
Synonyms: distortion-free, rectilinear
Antonyms: astigmatic, distorted
词性: adjective
