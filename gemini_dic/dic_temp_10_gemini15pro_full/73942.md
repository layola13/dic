forthcomings:/ˈfɔːθkʌmɪŋz/
n.|即将到来之事; 出版物; 预期会发生的事情|events or publications that are due to happen or appear soon. |The company's forthcomings include a new line of products and an expansion into new markets. 该公司的即将到来之事包括新的产品线和扩展到新市场。|近义词:publications, events, proceedings, works, output|反义词:pasts|词性:名词

forthcoming:/ˈfɔːθkʌmɪŋ/
adj.|即将到来的;可获得的;乐于助人的;准备好的|about to happen or appear; available when required or as promised; willing to give information about something;  ready or willing to help.|The forthcoming elections are expected to be closely contested. 即将举行的选举预计将是一场势均力敌的竞争。|近义词:approaching, imminent, impending, upcoming, future, available, accessible, at hand, ready, willing, helpful, cooperative, obliging, communicative, forthcoming, open, candid, frank, upfront|反义词:past, bygone, unforthcoming, reticent, reserved, unhelpful|词性:形容词
