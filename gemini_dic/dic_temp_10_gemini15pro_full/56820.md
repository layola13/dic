dormancy:/ˈdɔːrmənsi/ /ˈdɔːrmənsi/ | n. | 休眠；蛰伏；静止 |A state of inactivity or reduced metabolic activity in an organism, often during unfavorable environmental conditions. |The seeds remained in dormancy throughout the winter. 这些种子整个冬天都处于休眠状态。

The volcano has been in dormancy for centuries. 这座火山已经休眠了几个世纪。| 近义词: inactivity, quiescence, latency | 反义词: activity, growth, wakefulness | 词性: 名词
