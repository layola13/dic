bopped:/bɑpt/ /bɒpt/| v. |轻敲，轻打，快速移动，(俚语)跳舞|To hit lightly and quickly; to move briskly.  Also, in slang, to dance.|例句：She bopped him on the nose playfully. 她开玩笑地轻敲了一下他的鼻子。

例句：The music was so catchy, everyone was bopping along.  音乐很动听，每个人都跟着节奏跳舞。|近义词：tap, hit, knock, dance, groove|反义词:|词性:动词
