butterless:/ˈbʌtərləs/
adjective
无黄油的;不涂黄油的
Without butter; not containing butter.
The baker offered butterless rolls as a healthier option.  面包师提供无黄油面包卷作为更健康的选择。
She preferred butterless toast with her breakfast. 她早餐更喜欢吃不涂黄油的吐司。
近义词:unbuttered
反义词:buttered
词性:adjective
