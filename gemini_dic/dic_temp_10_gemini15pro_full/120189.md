mindsets:/ˈmaɪndsets/| n.|心态，思维模式|A mindset refers to a set of beliefs, assumptions, and attitudes that shape how a person perceives the world and responds to situations.  It influences their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.|
Example 1:  Growth mindsets allow people to embrace challenges and see failures as opportunities to learn. 成长型思维模式让人们能够拥抱挑战，并将失败视为学习的机会。

Example 2: Fixed mindsets can limit a person's potential, as they believe their abilities are static.  固定型思维模式会限制一个人的潜力，因为他们认为自己的能力是静态的。|近义词：attitudes, beliefs, perspectives, outlook, mentality|反义词: None (Mindset is a relatively recent term, direct antonyms are not well-established, but one could consider concepts like "open-mindedness" as a contrasting idea to a very rigid "fixed mindset").|词性: Noun
