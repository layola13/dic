abing:/ˈɑːbɪŋ/| n.|阿冰，菲律宾烤薄饼|A thin, crisp wafer made from rice flour, cooked on a hot metal surface and typically rolled around a sweet or savory filling.  It is a popular street food in the Philippines.|I love eating abing with cheese and condensed milk. 我喜欢吃加奶酪和炼乳的阿冰。|近义词:crepe, pancake|反义词:|名词
