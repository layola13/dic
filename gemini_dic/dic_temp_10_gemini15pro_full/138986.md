nymphales:/nɪmˈfælɪz/ | n. |蛱蝶科|A family of butterflies, commonly known as brush-footed butterflies.  They are characterized by reduced forelegs and often brightly colored wings.|例句：The monarch butterfly is a member of the Nymphalidae family.  帝王蝶是蛱蝶科的一员。

例句：Many Nymphalidae species exhibit mimicry for protection. 许多蛱蝶科物种展现出拟态以保护自己。|近义词：brush-footed butterflies|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun


Etymology:  From Modern Latin *Nymphalides, from Latin Nympha (nymph) + -ides (plural suffix).  The name refers to the delicate and graceful appearance of these butterflies, reminiscent of mythological nymphs.
