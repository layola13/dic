infidelity:/ˌɪnfɪˈdeləti/| n. |不忠，不贞；背信|The act of being unfaithful to a spouse or other sexual partner; disloyalty or treachery.|例句：His infidelity led to the breakdown of their marriage. 他的不忠导致了他们婚姻的破裂。

例句：The politician's infidelity to his party caused a scandal.  这位政客对他的党不忠，引发了一场丑闻。|近义词：unfaithfulness, disloyalty, treachery, betrayal|反义词: fidelity, loyalty, faithfulness|词性:noun

词源: From Latin infidelitas, from infidelis 'unfaithful', from in- 'not' + fidelis 'faithful'.
