ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *damas*, from Medieval Latin *damascēnus*, from Late Latin *Damascus*, from Ancient Greek *Damaskós* (Δαμασκός), referring to the city of Damascus, Syria, where such fabrics were originally made.
