catalectic:/ˌkætəˈlɛktɪk/
|adj.|
卡塔乐克提克的；诗行结尾缺音节的；不完整的|
A catalectic line of verse is missing one or more unstressed syllables at the end.  卡塔乐克提克的诗句在其结尾缺少一个或多个非重读音节。|
The poem's final line is catalectic, lending a sense of incompleteness. 这首诗的最后一行是不完整的，给人一种不完整的感觉。|
近义词: incomplete, truncated, shortened|
反义词: complete, acatalectic, full|
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *katalēktikós*, from *katalḗgō* "I leave off at the end," from *kata-* "down" + *lēgō* "I speak, I choose, I gather."  源自希腊语 *katalēktikós*，来自 *katalḗgō* “我在结尾处停止”，来自 *kata-* “向下” + *lēgō* “我说，我选择，我收集”。
