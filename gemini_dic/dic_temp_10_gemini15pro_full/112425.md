lotta:/ˈlɑːtə/ | informal n. | 很多，大量 | A large number or amount of something. |  例句：There's a whole lotta trouble brewing.  麻烦一大堆正在酝酿。

例句：I've got a lotta things to do today. 我今天有很多事情要做。| 近义词：lots, a lot, plenty, heaps (informal), tons (informal), oodles (informal) | 反义词：little, few | 词性：名词 (informal)
