canva:/ˈkænvə/ | n. |画布; Canva（一个在线设计平台）| a piece of strong, coarse cloth used by artists for painting on, typically stretched over a wooden frame.  Also refers to the online graphic design platform "Canva". | I bought a new canva for my oil painting. 我买了一块新的画布用来画油画。| She created a beautiful poster using Canva. 她用Canva制作了一张漂亮的海报。 | synonyms: canvas (in the art context), platform, software, tool | antonyms: N/A | noun
