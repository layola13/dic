immunosuppressing:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛsɪŋ/| adj.| 免疫抑制的 | Reducing the activity of the body's immune system. |  The patient was taking immunosuppressing drugs after the transplant.  病人移植后服用免疫抑制药物。| synonyms: immunosuppressive | antonyms: immunostimulatory | adjective

immunosuppression:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊsəˈprɛʃən/| n.| 免疫抑制 | The reduction of the activity of the body's immune system. | Immunosuppression is necessary after organ transplantation to prevent rejection. 器官移植后需要免疫抑制以防止排斥反应。| synonyms: immunodepression | antonyms: immunostimulation | noun


Etymology:

immuno- + suppressing

immuno-: from Latin "immunis" (exempt, free, safe)

suppressing: from Latin "suppressus", past participle of "supprimere" (to press down, suppress)
