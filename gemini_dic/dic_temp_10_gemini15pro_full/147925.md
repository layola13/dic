overreliance:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlaɪəns/| n. |过度依赖，过分依赖|Excessive dependence on something or someone.|The company's overreliance on a single supplier led to production delays when the supplier experienced difficulties.  这家公司过度依赖单一供应商，导致该供应商遇到困难时生产延误。|Example:  Overreliance on technology can hinder the development of practical skills. 过度依赖科技会阻碍实际技能的发展。|Synonyms: dependence, overdependence|Antonyms: independence, self-reliance|noun
