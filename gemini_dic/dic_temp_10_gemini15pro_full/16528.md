barkers:/ˈbɑːrkərz/| n.|叫卖者，吠叫的狗|People selling things in a loud, insistent way, or dogs that bark frequently. |The market was full of barkers advertising their wares.  市场上到处都是叫卖着自己商品的小贩。The neighborhood dogs were incessant barkers, disturbing the peace.  邻居家的狗不停地吠叫，扰乱了安宁。|近义词：hawkers, vendors, yellers (for people); yelpers (for dogs)|反义词:whisperers, murmurers (for people); quiet dogs (for dogs)|词性:名词

barker:/ˈbɑːrkər/| n.|叫卖者，吠叫的狗|A person who barks or shouts to attract customers, or a dog that barks frequently. |The circus barker promised amazing feats inside the tent. 马戏团的叫卖者承诺帐篷里有惊人的表演。My neighbor's dog is a real barker; he barks at everything. 我邻居的狗真是个爱叫的家伙，它对什么都叫。|近义词：shouter, caller, crier (for people);  yelper (for dogs)|反义词:whisperer, murmurer (for people); quiet dog (for dogs)|词性:名词
