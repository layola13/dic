ferociousness:/fəˈrɒʃəsnəs/ /fəˈroʊʃəsnəs/
n.
凶猛；残暴；暴烈
The state or quality of being ferocious; fierceness.
The ferocity of the storm caught everyone by surprise.  这场风暴的凶猛程度让所有人都措手不及。
The lioness protected her cubs with ferocity. 母狮凶猛地保护着她的幼崽。

近义词:fierceness, savagery, brutality, violence, wildness
反义词:gentleness, mildness, meekness, tameness, calmness
词性:noun
