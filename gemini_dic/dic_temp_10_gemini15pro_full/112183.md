lool:/luːl/ | interj. | 大笑 (网络用语) | An internet slang term used to express laughter, similar to "laugh out loud."  It's a variation of "LOL." | Example: "That video of the cat playing the piano was lool!" 那只猫弹钢琴的视频太好笑了！ | Example: "I can't believe he did that, lool." 我不敢相信他竟然那样做了，笑死我了。 | 近义词: LOL, lmao, rofl | 反义词:  (No direct opposite for an expression of laughter) | 词性: interjection
