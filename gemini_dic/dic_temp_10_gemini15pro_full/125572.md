multitones:/ˈmʌltiˌtəʊnz/ /ˈmʌltiˌtoʊnz/
|n.|复音; 多音|A combination of multiple tones, often used in music or telecommunications.|The composer used multitones to create a dissonant effect. 作曲家使用复音来营造不和谐的效果。|synonyms: polytones, overtones|antonyms: monotone, single tone|noun
multitones:/ˈmʌltiˌtəʊnz/ /ˈmʌltiˌtoʊnz/
|adj.|多音的|Having or producing multiple tones.|The multitone signal was difficult to decipher. 多音信号难以 decipher。|synonyms: polyphonic, multi-toned|antonyms: monophonic, single-toned|adjective
