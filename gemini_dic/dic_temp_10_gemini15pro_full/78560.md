ghoul:/ɡuːl/| n. |食尸鬼；盗墓者；残忍的人|An evil spirit or demon believed to rob graves and feed on corpses; a person morbidly interested in death or disaster. |The old stories told of ghouls that haunted the graveyard.  古老的故事讲述了墓地里闹鬼的食尸鬼。

The tabloid press were described as ghouls, preying on the grief of the bereaved.  小报媒体被描述为食尸鬼，以失去亲人的悲痛为食。|近义词：demon, fiend, monster, ogre |反义词:angel, saint|noun
