pardine:/ˈpɑːrdɪn/| adj.|豹纹的，像豹的|Resembling a leopard in markings; spotted or blotched like a leopard. |The pardine coat of the ocelot helped it blend into the dappled sunlight of the forest floor.  豹猫的豹纹皮毛帮助它融入森林地面的斑驳阳光中。|Synonyms: spotted, dappled, mottled, speckled|Antonyms: plain, solid, unspotted|adjective

pardine:/ˈpɑːrdɪn/| n.|豹；豹皮|A leopard; the skin of a leopard. (Archaic/Literary)|The knight wore a pardine cloak, a trophy from his latest hunt. 骑士披着豹皮斗篷，这是他最近一次狩猎的战利品。|Synonyms: leopard, panther|Antonyms: N/A|noun
