armilla:/ɑːrˈmɪlə/| n.|环; 臂钏; (古代)浑天仪; (星盘的)环;|An armilla is a bracelet or an ancient astronomical instrument consisting of rings representing the great circles of the heavens.|The museum displayed a beautiful gold armilla from the Roman era.  博物馆展出了一个来自罗马时代的精美金臂钏。|The astronomer used an armilla to track the movement of celestial bodies. 天文学家使用浑天仪来追踪天体的运动。|近义词：bracelet, bangle, (for astronomical instrument) astrolabe, sphere|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *armilla*, diminutive of *armus* ("shoulder, upper arm").  The astronomical instrument was so named because it was held up by the arm.
