corpuses:/ˈkɔːrpəsɪz/ /ˈkɔːrpəsiːz/ | n. |语料库，文集|A collection of written or spoken material stored electronically and used for linguistic research.  A corpus can contain texts in one language or several languages. |例句：Researchers used a large corpus of English texts to study changes in vocabulary over time. 研究人员使用了一个大型英语文本语料库来研究词汇随时间的变化。

例句：The corpus of Shakespeare's works is vast and rich.  莎士比亚作品的语料库浩瀚而丰富。|近义词：collection, compilation, anthology|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


corpus:/ˈkɔːrpəs/ /ˈkɔːrpəs/ | n. |语料库，文集，全集；主要部分；（解剖学）体；躯体|A collection of written or spoken material stored electronically and used for linguistic research.  A corpus can contain texts in one language or several languages.  Also, the main body or mass of something. In anatomy, the body of a human or animal. |例句：The corpus of evidence suggests that he is guilty.  大量的证据表明他有罪。

例句：The surgeon examined the corpus of the stomach.  外科医生检查了胃体。|近义词：collection, compilation, anthology, body, mass|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
