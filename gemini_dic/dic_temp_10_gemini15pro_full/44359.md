cross-check against:  交叉核对…以防…  例句：Cross-check the invoice against the delivery note. 将发票与送货单进行交叉核对。
cross-check with: 与…交叉核对 例句：Cross-check with your colleagues to ensure everyone is on the same page. 与你的同事交叉核对，以确保每个人都在同一进度上。
perform a cross-check: 执行交叉核对 例句：We performed a cross-check to verify the accuracy of the information. 我们执行了交叉核对以验证信息的准确性。
subject to cross-check: 须经交叉核对 例句：All data is subject to cross-check before publication. 所有数据在发布前都要经过交叉核对。
thorough cross-check: 彻底的交叉核对 例句：A thorough cross-check is essential to ensure the reliability of the results. 彻底的交叉核对对于确保结果的可靠性至关重要。
