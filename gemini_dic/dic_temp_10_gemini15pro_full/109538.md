lessen:/ˈlesən/
verb
减少，减轻，降低
to make or become less; diminish
The rain lessened in the afternoon. 下午雨变小了。
近义词:reduce, decrease, diminish, abate, mitigate
反义词:increase, enhance, aggravate, amplify
词性:动词

lessen:/ˈlesən/
verb
轻视，贬低
to make or cause to appear less important; belittle
He tried to lessen the importance of his mistake. 他试图降低自己错误的重要性。
近义词:minimize, downplay, trivialize
反义词:exaggerate, magnify
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lessenen*, from Old English *læssan* (“to lessen, diminish, make less”), from *læs* (“less”). Cognate with Scots *lessen* (“to lessen”), West Frisian *lesse* (“to lessen”), Dutch *lessen* (“to lessen”), German *lassen* (“to let, leave”), Gothic *𐌻𐌴𐍄𐌰𐌽* (*lētan*).
