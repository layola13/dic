corrosivenesses:/kəˈroʊsɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|腐蚀性；侵蚀性|The quality of being corrosive; the degree to which something is corrosive.  The corrosivenesses of different acids vary.|The corrosivenesses of the various cleaning agents were tested on different metals. 各种清洁剂的腐蚀性都在不同的金属上进行了测试。|Synonyms: causticity, acidity, mordancy|Antonyms: inertness, mildness|noun


corrosiveness:/kəˈroʊsɪvnəs/
|n.|腐蚀性；侵蚀性|The quality of being corrosive; the degree to which something is corrosive.  The corrosiveness of acid can damage metal.|The corrosiveness of the cleaning agent damaged the countertop.  清洁剂的腐蚀性损坏了台面。|Synonyms: causticity, acidity, mordancy|Antonyms: inertness, mildness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From corrosive + -ness. Corrosive comes from Late Latin *corrosivus, from Latin corrodere "to gnaw to pieces," from com- "together" + rodere "to gnaw."
