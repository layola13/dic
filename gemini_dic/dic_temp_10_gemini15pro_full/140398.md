oinking:/ˈɔɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. |猪叫声; (猪)哼哼声 |The sound a pig makes. |The pigs were oinking in the pen. 猪圈里的猪在哼哼叫。| 近义词:grunting, squealing | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑silence 安静) | 词性:名词

Etymology:  Imitative of the sound.  From the verb "to oink."
