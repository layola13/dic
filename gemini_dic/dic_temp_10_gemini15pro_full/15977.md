baltimores:/ˈbɔːltɪmɔːrz/| n.|巴尔的摩（复数）| Baltimore is a major city on the East Coast of the United States. The plural form "Baltimores" is less common and usually refers to multiple instances or aspects related to the city, such as "the two Baltimores" representing different social or economic realities within the city. |Baltimore is known for its harbor. 巴尔的摩以其港口而闻名。

The two Baltimores represent a stark contrast in wealth and opportunity. 两个巴尔的摩代表着财富和机会的鲜明对比。 |近义词:Baltimore City (less common in plural form)|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:  Named after Cecil Calvert, 2nd Baron Baltimore (1605–1675), an English nobleman who was the first Proprietor of the Province of Maryland.
