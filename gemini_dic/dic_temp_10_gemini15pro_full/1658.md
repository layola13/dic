acknowledger:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒər/ | n. | 承认者，确认者 |  A person who acknowledges something; one who confesses or admits. | The acknowledger of the debt signed the document. 债务承认者在文件上签了字。 | Synonyms: recognizer, admitter, confessor | Antonyms: denier, disavower | noun

acknowledger:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒər/ | n. | 承认者，答谢者 | A person who expresses gratitude or thanks. |  The author included a page of acknowledgers in the preface of the book. 作者在书的前言中列出了一页感谢的人。 | Synonyms: thanker | Antonyms: ingrate | noun


USAGE->

He is the acknowledger of the will. = 他是这份遗嘱的承认者。
