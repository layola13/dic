clothed:/kloʊðd/
| adj. | 穿着衣服的，覆盖着的 | Dressed or covered. |  The child was clothed in a blue coat.  孩子穿着一件蓝色外套。| synonyms: dressed, attired, garbed, clad | antonyms: naked, bare, unclothed, nude | adjective


clothe:/kloʊð/
| vt. | 给…穿衣；覆盖 | To dress someone or something; to cover. |  She clothed the children in warm sweaters. 她给孩子们穿上了温暖的毛衣。| synonyms: dress, attire, garb, clad | antonyms: undress, strip, uncover | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "clāthian," from Proto-Germanic "*klæþijan," from "*klæþiz" (cloth).  Related to Old Norse "klæða," Old High German "kleiden," German "kleiden," Dutch "kleden."
