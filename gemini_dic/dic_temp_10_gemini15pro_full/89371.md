homonymy:/hɒˈmɒnɪmi/ /həˈmɑːnɪmi/| n. |同音异义词，同形异义词|A relationship between words that share the same spelling and pronunciation but have different meanings.|The words "bat" (a nocturnal flying mammal) and "bat" (used in baseball) are examples of homonymy.|近义词：homophone, polysemy|反义词:heteronym|noun
