half-fascinatingly:/ˌhæfˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋli/
| adv. | 半令人着迷地；略微引人入胜地 |  In a way that is partially or moderately fascinating. It combines "half" indicating a partial degree and "fascinatingly" meaning in a captivating manner. |  He spoke half-fascinatingly about his travels, capturing my attention only intermittently. 他断断续续地讲述着他的旅行经历，只是偶尔吸引我的注意。 |  mildly interestingly, somewhat captivatingly | dully, boringly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" (Old English healf) + "fascinating" (from fascinate, from Latin fascinare "to bewitch") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
