overinfluences:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnfluənsɪz/
|v.|过度影响|To influence excessively or too much.|Social media overinfluences young people's fashion choices. 社交媒体过度影响了年轻人的时尚选择。|
|近义词：dominates, controls, manipulates|反义词:underinfluences (非正式), neglects|词性:动词

overinfluence:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnfluəns/
|n.|过度影响|The excessive influence of something or someone.|The overinfluence of advertising can lead to unhealthy consumerism. 广告的过度影响会导致不健康的消费主义。|
|近义词：domination, control, manipulation|反义词:underinfluence (非正式), neglect|词性:名词
