dwarfishnesses:/ˈdwɔːrfɪʃnəsɪz/
n.
矮小，侏儒状态，矮小性
The state or quality of being dwarfish; unusually small size.
The dwarfishnesses of the trees in this area is due to the poor soil.  这个地区的树木矮小是由于土壤贫瘠造成的。

近义词: shortness, smallness, diminutiveness
反义词: tallness, height, largeness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From dwarfish + -ness.  Dwarfish comes from dwarf (Old English *dweorg) + -ish (suffix indicating resemblance or belonging to).  -ness is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
