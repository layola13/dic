austerest:/ɔːˈstɪrɪst/ | adj. |  最简朴的，最苦行的 |  Superlative form of austere: most austere.  Characterized by extreme plainness and simplicity; having no adornment or ornamentation.  |  The austerest room in the monastery contained only a bed, a chair, and a crucifix.  修道院里最简朴的房间只有一张床、一把椅子和一个十字架。 | The monks lived an austerest life, devoid of any luxury.  僧侣们过着最简朴的生活，没有任何奢侈品。 |  simple, plain, spartan, ascetic |  luxurious, opulent, extravagant | adjective
