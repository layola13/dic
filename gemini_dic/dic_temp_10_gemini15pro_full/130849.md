nonapproachability:/ˌnɒnəˌprəʊtʃəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可接近性；难以接近；拒人于千里之外 | The nonapproachability of the CEO intimidated many employees. |  The fortress had an air of nonapproachability.  This mathematical proof demonstrates the nonapproachability of this limit. |近义词：inaccessibility, aloofness, remoteness, unfriendliness |反义词：approachability, accessibility, friendliness, warmth | 词性: 名词

nonapproachable:/ˌnɒnəˈprəʊtʃəbl/ | adj. | 难以接近的；令人畏惧的；不可亲近的 | The celebrity seemed nonapproachable surrounded by bodyguards. | He cultivated a nonapproachable demeanor to discourage unwanted attention.  The snowy peak looked nonapproachable due to the blizzard. | 近义词：inaccessible, aloof, remote, unfriendly, forbidding, daunting |反义词：approachable, accessible, friendly, warm, welcoming | 词性: 形容词
