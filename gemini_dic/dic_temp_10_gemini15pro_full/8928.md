antihemorrhagic:/ˌæntiˌheməˈrædʒɪk/ | adj. | 抗出血的，止血的 | Preventing or reducing bleeding. |  The doctor prescribed antihemorrhagic medication. 医生开了止血药。| synonyms: styptic, hemostatic | antonyms: hemorrhagic, bleeding | adjective

antihemorrhagic:/ˌæntiˌheməˈrædʒɪk/ | n. | 止血药，止血剂 | A substance that prevents or reduces bleeding. | The patient was given an antihemorrhagic to control the bleeding.  病人服用了止血药来控制出血。| synonyms: styptic, hemostatic | antonyms:  blood thinner, anticoagulant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
