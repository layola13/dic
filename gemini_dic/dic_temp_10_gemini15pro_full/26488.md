cabal:/kəˈbɑːl/| n.|阴谋集团，秘密结社|a small group of people who plan secretly, especially to gain political power|The cabal was plotting to overthrow the government. 这个阴谋集团正密谋推翻政府。|Synonyms: clique, faction, ring, league|Antonyms:  public, open, transparent|noun

cabal:/kəˈbɑːl/| v. |密谋，策划|to form a cabal; to conspire|They caballed against the chairman. 他们密谋反对主席。|Synonyms: conspire, intrigue, plot, scheme|Antonyms: cooperate, collaborate|verb
