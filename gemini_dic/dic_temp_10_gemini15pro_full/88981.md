holing-up:/ˈhəʊlɪŋ ʌp/| phrasal verb |躲藏，藏匿；闭关|The robbers were holing up in a deserted warehouse. 强盗们藏匿在一个废弃的仓库里。|例句：They were holing up in a cabin in the mountains. 他们躲藏在山里的小屋中。

例句：He's been holing up in his apartment for days, working on his novel.  他窝在公寓里好几天了，一直在写他的小说。


|近义词：hiding, concealing, sheltering, lying low|反义词:emerging, appearing, going out|词性:动词短语
