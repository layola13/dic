millionaires:/ˌmɪljəˈnɛərz/ | n. | 百万富翁（复数） | People who have a net worth of at least one million units of currency (e.g., dollars, euros, etc.). |  Many millionaires invest in real estate. 许多百万富翁投资房地产。|  The neighborhood is known for its high concentration of millionaires. 这个社区以百万富翁高度集中而闻名。 |  tycoons, moguls, wealthy individuals | poor people, paupers | noun
