overjoyfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪfəlnəs/
n.
极度喜悦, 狂喜
The state of being extremely joyful; overwhelming happiness.
Her overjoyfulness was evident in her bright smile and infectious laughter. 她灿烂的笑容和富有感染力的笑声表明她欣喜若狂。
The children jumped up and down with overjoyfulness when they heard they were going to Disneyland. 当孩子们听到他们要去迪士尼乐园时，高兴地跳上跳下。
近义词: ecstasy, rapture, bliss, elation
反义词: despair, misery, sadness, gloom
词性: noun

overjoyed:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪd/
adj.
欣喜若狂的，极度高兴的
Extremely happy; filled with great joy.
She was overjoyed to hear that she had won the lottery. 她欣喜若狂地听到自己中了彩票。
They were overjoyed at the birth of their first child.  他们对第一个孩子的出生感到欣喜若狂。
近义词: thrilled, delighted, ecstatic, elated
反义词: devastated, heartbroken, miserable, sorrowful
词性: adjective


词源:
From over- + joyful + -ness.  "Over-" intensifies the meaning of "joyful." "-ness" transforms the adjective "joyful" into a noun, indicating a state or quality of being joyful.
