dingier:/ˈdɪndʒɪər/| adj.|更脏的，更昏暗的，更破旧的|Comparative form of dingy: more dingy.  Dingier describes something that is more dirty, grimy, or shabby than something else.|The hotel room was dingier than I expected.  这个旅馆房间比我预料的更脏。|The alley became dingier as the sun set.  随着太阳落山，小巷变得更加昏暗。|近义词：shabbier, grimier, dirtier, darker|反义词：cleaner, brighter, newer|词性:形容词

词源: From *dingy* + *-er* (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from the obsolete verb *dingen* "to darken, make dingy".
