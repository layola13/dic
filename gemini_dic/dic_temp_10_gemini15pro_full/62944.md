enneagram:/ˈɛniəɡræm/| n. |九型人格; 九角星|A system of personality typing that describes nine interconnected personality types, each with its own set of core beliefs, motivations, fears, and patterns of behavior. It is often used for self-discovery, personal growth, and improving relationships.|She's studying the Enneagram to better understand herself and her relationships. 她正在学习九型人格，以便更好地了解自己和人际关系。|Synonyms: personality typology, personality system|Antonyms: N/A|noun

enneagram:/ˈɛniəɡræm/| n. |九角星形|A nine-pointed geometric figure.|The symbol of the Enneagram is a nine-pointed star. 九型人格的象征是一个九角星。|Synonyms: nonagon, enneagon|Antonyms: N/A|noun
