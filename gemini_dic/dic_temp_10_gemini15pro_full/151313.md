paring:/ˈpɛərɪŋ/| n. |削皮; 修剪; 配对|The act of cutting away the outer covering of something, especially a fruit or vegetable. Also, the act of reducing something by cutting or trimming, or the act of carefully matching or grouping things.|She was paring an apple. 她正在削苹果。|近义词：peeling, trimming, cutting, reducing, matching, pairing |反义词:augmenting, increasing, expanding|noun


paring:/ˈpɛərɪŋ/| n. |削下的薄片; 配对物|Something which has been pared off, such as a thin strip of peel. Also, one of a pair or set of matched things.|Apple parings lay scattered on the counter. 苹果皮散落在柜台上。|近义词：peelings, shavings, trimmings, match, pair |反义词:whole, entirety|noun
