metempiric:/ˌmɛtəmˈpɪrɪk/ | adj. | 超验的，形而上学的 |  Going beyond empirical observation or experience; transcendental. Relating to metaphysics. |  His arguments were highly metempirical, delving into the nature of reality itself. 他的论点非常超验，深入探讨了现实本身的本质。| 近义词: metaphysical, transcendental, supersensible | 反义词: empirical, experiential, observational | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *meta* "beyond" + *empeiria* "experience"
