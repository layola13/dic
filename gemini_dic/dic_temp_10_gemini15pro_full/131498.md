noncollectivistic:/ˌnɒnkəˈlɛktɪvɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非集体主义的 | Not pertaining to or characteristic of collectivism; emphasizing individual rights, needs, and independence over group goals or cohesion. |  Example:  The United States is often described as having a noncollectivistic culture. 美国通常被描述为具有非集体主义文化。

Example:  She raised her children with a noncollectivistic approach, encouraging them to pursue their own passions. 她以非集体主义的方式抚养孩子，鼓励他们追求自己的热情。| 近义词: individualistic, independent | 反义词: collectivistic, communal | 词性: adjective
