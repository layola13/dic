caesarians:/sɪˈzɛəriənz/ |n.|剖腹产手术；通过剖腹产出生的婴儿|A surgical operation for delivering a child by cutting through the wall of the mother's abdomen.|Many babies are born by Caesarians these days. |如今很多婴儿都是剖腹产出生的。| synonyms: C-section, Cesarean section, Cesarean delivery | antonyms: vaginal birth | noun


caesarian:/sɪˈzɛəriən/ |adj.|剖腹产的|Relating to or involving a Caesarian section.|She had a caesarian birth. |她进行了剖腹产。| synonyms: C-section, Cesarean | antonyms: vaginal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caesarius* "of Caesar," from *Caesar*, the family name of Julius Caesar, who was supposedly born this way.  The story may be apocryphal.
