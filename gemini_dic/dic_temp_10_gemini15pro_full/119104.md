metrology:/mɪˈtrɒlədʒi/
|n.|计量学|The science of measurement, embracing both experimental and theoretical determinations at any level of uncertainty in any field of science and technology.|The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) plays a vital role in metrology in the United States. 美国国家标准与技术研究院 (NIST) 在美国的计量学中扮演着至关重要的角色。|
| | |
metrologies:/mɪˈtrɒlədʒiz/
|n.|计量学（复数）|Plural form of metrology, referring to multiple systems or areas of metrology.|Different countries may have slightly different metrologies for certain measurements. 不同国家对某些测量的计量学可能略有不同。|
