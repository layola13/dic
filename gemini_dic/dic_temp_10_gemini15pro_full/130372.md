non-urgent:/ˌnɒnˈɜːdʒənt/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈɜːrdʒənt/| adj.|非紧急的，不紧急的|Not requiring immediate action or attention; not pressing.|例句：The email was marked as non-urgent. 这封电子邮件被标记为非紧急。|例句：It's a non-urgent matter that can be dealt with later.  这是一件不紧急的事情，可以稍后再处理。|近义词：non-critical, low-priority, routine|反义词:urgent, pressing, critical|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + urgent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "urgent" comes from Latin "urgēns," present participle of "urgēre" meaning "to press, urge, drive."
