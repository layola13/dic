fighters:/ˈfaɪtərz/ (US), /ˈfaɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|战士，战斗机，战斗人员|A person or thing that fights, especially a soldier or aircraft designed for combat. |The brave fighters defended their city. 勇敢的战士们保卫了他们的城市.|synonyms:  warriors, soldiers, combatants, contenders, champions, scrappers|antonyms: pacifists, cowards, quitters|noun

fighters:/ˈfaɪtərz/ (US), /ˈfaɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|斗士，奋斗者|A person who struggles tenaciously for something. |He's a real fighter, never giving up on his dreams. 他是一个真正的斗士，从不放弃他的梦想。|synonyms:  strugglers, strivers, battlers, campaigners|antonyms:  quitters, surrenderers|noun
