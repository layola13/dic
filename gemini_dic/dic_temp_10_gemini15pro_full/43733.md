creeklet: /ˈkriːklɪt/
n.
小溪，细流
A small creek; a rivulet.
The creeklet flowed gently through the meadow. 小溪缓缓流过草地。

近义词: rivulet, brook, stream
反义词: river, ocean
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From creek + -let (diminutive suffix).  The word "creek" itself has a complex etymology, ultimately deriving from Old Norse "kriki" (a bend or corner).  The suffix "-let" is a diminutive, meaning "small," and is of French origin.
