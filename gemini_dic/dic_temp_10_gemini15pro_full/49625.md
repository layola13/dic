demidevil:/ˈdɛmiˌdɛvəl/| n.|半恶魔; 半魔鬼|A being that is half-devil and half-human, often depicted as mischievous or seductive.  It embodies a mixture of good and evil qualities.|She portrayed herself as a demidevil, alluring and dangerous. 她把自己描绘成一个半恶魔，既迷人又危险。|The character in the game was a charming demidevil, torn between his demonic and human sides.  游戏中的角色是一个迷人的半恶魔，在他恶魔和人类的两面之间挣扎。|Near Synonyms: half-devil, half-demon, hybrid|Near Antonyms: angel, saint|noun
