furnishers:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃərz/
n.
家具商，家具店
People who supply furniture; businesses that sell furniture.
The furnishers delivered the new sofa this morning.  家具商今天早上送来了新沙发。
We bought our dining table from a local furnishers. 我们从当地一家家具店买了餐桌。
synonyms: furniture dealers, furniture retailers, furniture stores, furniture suppliers
antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct antonym, one could consider "antique dealers" or "secondhand furniture stores" as contrasting, though not strictly opposite)
