colleaguing: /ˈkɒliːɡɪŋ/ | n. | 同事关系；同事间的合作；同事行为 | The act of working together with colleagues or behaving in a way expected of colleagues. |  Example: Good colleaguing is essential for a positive work environment. 良好的同事关系对于积极的工作环境至关重要。

Example:  Their colleaguing on the project led to innovative solutions. 他们在项目上的合作带来了创新的解决方案。| 近义词: collaboration, teamwork, cooperation | 反义词: antagonism, conflict, rivalry | 词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  Derived from "colleague" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the act or process of being a colleague or engaging in colleague-like behavior.  "Colleague" itself comes from the French "collègue," ultimately from the Latin "collega," meaning "partner in office."
