nondelicately:/nɒndɛlɪkətli/
adv.
不精细地，粗鲁地，不文雅地
in a way that is not delicate; roughly or crudely.
He nondelicately shoved the book into the overflowing backpack. 他粗鲁地把书塞进已经塞满的背包里。
She nondelicately slurped her soup, much to the dismay of her dining companions. 她大声地喝着汤，这让她同桌用餐的同伴们感到很沮丧。
Synonyms:  crudely, roughly, indelicately, clumsily, awkwardly, insensitively
Antonyms: delicately, gently, subtly, gracefully, tactfully, sensitively
