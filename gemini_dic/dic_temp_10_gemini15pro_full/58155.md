droughtiness:/ˈdraʊtinəs/| n. |干旱；干燥|The droughtiness of the land made farming difficult.  这片土地的干旱使得耕作困难。|例句：The prolonged droughtiness caused severe crop failures. 长期的干旱导致了严重的作物歉收。

The droughtiness of the region has led to water restrictions. 该地区的干旱导致了用水限制。|近义词：aridness, dryness, desiccation|反义词：wetness, humidity, moisture|词性：名词

词源：From drought + -iness.  来自 drought（干旱）+ -iness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
