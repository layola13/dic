overdescriptiveness:/ˌoʊvər.dɪˈskrɪp.tɪv.nəs/
|n.| 过度描述; 过于详尽的描述|The overdescriptiveness of the passage made it tedious to read. 这段文字描述过于详尽，读起来很乏味。|
|英文解析: The quality of being excessively descriptive; the use of too many details in a description.|
|例句:  The overdescriptiveness of the witness's account made it difficult for the jury to follow.  证人描述过于详尽，陪审团难以理解。|

|例句:  Her overdescriptiveness in her writing often obscured the main point.  她在写作中过于详尽的描述常常掩盖了要点。|

|近义词:verbosity, prolixity, wordiness|
|反义词:conciseness, brevity, succinctness|
|词性: noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + descriptive + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix indicating "too much" or "excessive." "Descriptive" comes from the Latin "describere," meaning "to write down" or "to depict." "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
