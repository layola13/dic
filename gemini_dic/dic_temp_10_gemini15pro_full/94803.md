impulse:/ˈɪmpʌls/ (US); /ˈɪmpʌls/ (UK)| n. |冲动，脉冲|A sudden, strong and unreflective urge or desire to act.|He had a sudden impulse to buy a sports car. 他突然心血来潮想买一辆跑车。| synonym: urge, whim, instinct | antonym: hesitation, inhibition | noun

impulse:/ˈɪmpʌls/ (US); /ˈɪmpʌls/ (UK)| n. |推动，刺激|Something that incites to action or exertion or quickens activity; impetus.|The impulse for change came from the people. 变革的动力来自人民。| synonym: impetus, stimulus, motivation | antonym: deterrent, discouragement | noun
