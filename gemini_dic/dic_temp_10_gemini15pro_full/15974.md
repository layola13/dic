ETYMOLOGY->
From the Baltic Sea, named after a large island called *Baltia* by the Roman writer Pliny the Elder. The origin of "Baltia" is uncertain, possibly from a Proto-Indo-European word *bhel* meaning "white," referring to the white sand beaches of the region, or perhaps from a Lithuanian word *baltas* also meaning "white".
