meticulous:/məˈtɪkjələs/| adj.|一丝不苟的，细致的|Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.|She planned the party with meticulous care. 她一丝不苟地策划了这场派对。|例句：He kept meticulous records of his expenses. 他一丝不苟地记录自己的开支。|近义词：careful, precise, thorough, scrupulous|反义词：careless, sloppy, negligent|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin meticulosus "fearful, timid," from metus "fear, dread."  The sense evolution is from "fearful of making a mistake" to "taking extreme care to avoid mistakes" to "excessively careful about details."
