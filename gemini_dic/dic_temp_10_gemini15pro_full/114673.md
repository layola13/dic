ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *malignance, from Old French malignance, from Latin malignantia (“malignity, ill-will”), from malignans, present participle of malignare (“to do maliciously”), from male (“badly”) + -gnus (“born, doing”), equivalent to malign + -ance.
