neighborliness:/ˈneɪbərlɪnɪs/| n. |睦邻友好；邻里间的和睦|The sense of community and neighborliness is strong in this town. 这个镇的社区意识和邻里关系很强。|例句：Their neighborliness was heartwarming. 他们的睦邻友好令人感到温暖。

例句：We appreciated the neighborliness of the people in the village. 我们很感激村里人的友善。

|近义词：friendliness, amity, camaraderie, sociability|反义词：hostility, animosity, unfriendliness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From neighbor + -li- + -ness.  "Neighbor" comes from Old English *nēahgebūr, from *nēah "near" + *gebūr "dweller."
