dingoes:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊz/
n.
澳洲野犬
A wild or half-wild dog of Australia, reddish-brown in colour.
Dingoes are known for their cunning and adaptability. 澳洲野犬以其狡猾和适应性而闻名。
近义词:wild dogs, feral dogs
反义词:domestic dogs
词性:名词
