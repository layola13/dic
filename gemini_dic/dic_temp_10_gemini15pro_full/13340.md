atlantal:/ætˈlæntəl/| adj.|寰椎的，与寰椎有关的|Relating to the atlas, the first cervical vertebra (C1) that supports the head.|The atlantal axial joint allows for a wide range of head movement.|近义词:cervical|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps caudal as it refers to the tail end)|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin "atlas" (referring to the mythical figure Atlas who held up the sky) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to"). The atlas vertebra is so named because it supports the head, much like Atlas supported the heavens.
