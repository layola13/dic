overbaited:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度诱饵的；饵料过多的
Having used too much bait, especially when fishing.  This can deter fish rather than attract them.
The hook was overbaited, which might explain why we didn't catch anything.  钩子上饵料太多了，这也许可以解释为什么我们什么也没钓到。
over-baited
overfed, overloaded
underbaited
adjective
