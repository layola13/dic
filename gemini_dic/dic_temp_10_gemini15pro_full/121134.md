misdeliver:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvər/
verb
投递错误，误送
to deliver (something) to the wrong person or address
The package was misdelivered to the wrong address. 包裹被误送到了错误的地址。
misdirect, misaddress
deliver correctly
