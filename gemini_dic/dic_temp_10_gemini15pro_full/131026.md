nonauthoritarian:/ˌnɒnɔːˌθɒrɪˈteəriən/ | adj. | 非专制的，非独裁的 | Not authoritarian; relating to or believing in a political system that is not authoritarian. |  A nonauthoritarian approach to parenting often fosters independence.  非专制主义的教养方式通常会培养孩子的独立性。|  The organization promotes nonauthoritarian leadership styles.  该组织提倡非专制型的领导风格。| 近义词: democratic, liberal, egalitarian | 反义词: authoritarian, dictatorial, totalitarian | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + authoritarian.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Authoritarian" comes from "authority" which ultimately derives from the Latin "auctoritas."
