dangler:/ˈdæŋɡlər/| n.|悬挂物；摇摆不定的人（或物）；悬而未决的事物|Something that hangs loosely or swings to and fro; a person or thing that is in an uncertain or precarious situation.|The broken cable dangled precariously from the bridge.  断裂的电缆摇摇欲坠地悬挂在桥上。|Synonyms: pendant, bob, swing|Antonyms: fixture, support|noun


dangler:/ˈdæŋɡlər/| n.|（语法）悬垂修饰语|In grammar, a dangling modifier is a word or phrase that modifies a word not clearly stated in the sentence. |Walking down the street, the tall buildings impressed me. (Incorrect - the buildings weren't walking)  走在街上，高楼大厦给我留下了深刻的印象。（错误——建筑物不会走路）|Synonyms: misplaced modifier|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, the correct usage is simply a properly placed modifier)|noun
