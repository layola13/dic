disappropriate:/ˌdɪsəˈproʊprieɪt/
verb
中文翻译: 挪用；盗用；侵吞
英文解析: To take (something, especially money) for one's own use, typically without the owner's permission.
例句：The CEO was accused of disappropriating company funds. 首席执行官被指控挪用公司资金。

例句：He disappropriated the charity's donations for his personal expenses. 他盗用了慈善机构的捐款用于个人开支。

近义词：embezzle, misappropriate, pilfer, steal
反义词：donate, contribute, return
词性: verb
