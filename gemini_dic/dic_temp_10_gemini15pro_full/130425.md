nonabidingnesses:/ˌnɒnəˈbaɪdɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
不服从，不遵守
The state of not abiding by rules or laws.  Lack of compliance or adherence.
His consistent nonabidingnesses led to his expulsion from the organization. 他持续的不遵守行为导致他被组织开除。
Near Synonyms: disobedience, noncompliance, recalcitrance, defiance
Antonyms: obedience, compliance, conformity
词性: 名词
