by-lining: /ˈbaɪˌlaɪnɪŋ/
| n. |署名；旁注；加衬里 |The practice of crediting a journalist or writer with an article or story they have written.  Giving someone a byline is a mark of recognition and authorship. |The article appeared with her by-lining. 这篇文章署了她的名字。| synonyms: attribution, crediting, acknowledgment | antonyms: anonymity, uncredited | noun

by-lining: /ˈbaɪˌlaɪnɪŋ/
| v. |署名；旁注；加衬里 |The act of a publication adding a byline to an article.  |The editor by-lined the article to the new journalist. 编辑在这篇文章上署了这位新记者的名字。| synonyms: credit, attribute, acknowledge | antonyms: anonymize, conceal | verb
