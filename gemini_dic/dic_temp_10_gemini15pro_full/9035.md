antimaterialist:/ˌænti-məˈtɪəriəlɪst/
adjective
反物质主义的; 非物质主义的
opposed to materialism.  Focusing on spiritual or ethical values rather than material possessions.
He lived a simple, antimaterialist lifestyle, valuing experiences over possessions. 他过着简单、反物质主义的生活方式，重视经验而非物质财富。
The antimaterialist movement gained traction in the 60s. 反物质主义运动在60年代获得了关注。
近义词:spiritual, ascetic, non-materialistic
反义词:materialistic, consumerist
词性:adjective
