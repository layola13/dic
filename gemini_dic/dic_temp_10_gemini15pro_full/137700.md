nonusurious:/ˌnɒnjuːˈʒʊəriəs/
adj.
非高利贷的
Not charging excessive or exorbitant interest; not usurious.
The loan agreement was carefully worded to ensure it was nonusurious and complied with all applicable regulations.  这份贷款协议措辞谨慎，以确保它是非高利贷性质的，并符合所有适用规定。
He sought a nonusurious loan from a credit union. 他试图从信用合作社获得一笔非高利贷的贷款。
近义词:fair, reasonable, legitimate
反义词:usurious, exorbitant, predatory
词性:adjective
