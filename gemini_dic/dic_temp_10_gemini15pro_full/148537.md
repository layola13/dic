oversusceptibly:/ˌoʊvər səˈsɛptəbli/| adv.|过度敏感地，过分易受影响地|in a way that is too easily affected or influenced, especially by emotions|He reacted oversusceptibly to the criticism, bursting into tears. 他对批评反应过度敏感，突然哭了起来。|She oversusceptibly flinched at the loud noise, even though it wasn't that close. 即使声音不是很近，她还是过度敏感地 flinched 一下。|近义词：overly sensitively, too readily, excessively sensitively|反义词：insensitively, impassively|词性: 副词

词源:  From "oversusceptible" + "-ly".  "Oversusceptible" is formed from "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "susceptible" (from Latin *susceptibilis*, meaning "capable of receiving or undergoing something").
