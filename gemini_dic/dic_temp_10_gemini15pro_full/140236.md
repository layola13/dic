offish:/ˈɒfɪʃ/ (US) /ˈɑːfɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|冷淡的，疏远的，不友好的|Not friendly or forthcoming; aloof and reserved.|She was rather offish with me at the party, so I didn't try to talk to her further. 她聚会上对我很冷淡，所以我没有再跟她说话。|

He gave me a very offish look when I asked him for help. 当我向他寻求帮助时，他给了我一个非常冷淡的表情。|近义词：aloof, distant, reserved, stand-offish, unapproachable|反义词：friendly, warm, approachable, sociable, gregarious|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From off + -ish.  The original sense was "inclined to keep aloof, distant".  First recorded in the late 18th century.  Relates to the adverb "off" implying distance or separation.
