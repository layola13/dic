emplastic:/ɪmˈplæstɪk/
adjective
粘性的; 膏药的; 有粘合力的
adhesive; tending to consolidate or reunite severed parts; suitable for use as a plaster
例句：The emplastic properties of the mud made it difficult to remove from our shoes.  这种泥巴的粘性使得它很难从我们的鞋子上清除掉。

例句：The doctor applied an emplastic bandage to the wound. 医生在伤口上贴了一块膏药绷带。

近义词：adhesive, glutinous, viscous, sticky
反义词：non-adhesive, dry
词性: adjective
