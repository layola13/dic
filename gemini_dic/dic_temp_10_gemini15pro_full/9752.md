antler:/ˈæntlə(r)/ (US) /ˈæntlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |鹿角；茸角|One of the branched bony growths on the head of a deer, elk, moose, or other member of the deer family. |The deer's antlers were magnificent. 这只鹿的鹿角非常壮观。|Synonyms: horn, rack|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
