microcontroller:/ˌmaɪkroʊkənˈtroʊlər/| n. |微控制器|A small computer on a single integrated circuit containing a processor core, memory, and programmable input/output peripherals.  It is designed for embedded applications, controlling the functions of a specific device or system.|The microcontroller in my washing machine regulates the wash cycle.|近义词：microprocessor, embedded controller|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "mainframe computer" as a contrasting concept)|noun
