pan-presbyterian:/pænˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriən/
adjective
泛长老会的
Relating to or involving all or most of the Presbyterian churches or denominations.
The pan-presbyterian council met to discuss theological issues affecting member churches worldwide. 泛长老会理事会开会讨论影响全球成员教会的神学问题。
Synonym: inter-presbyterian
Antonym: denominational (specifically referring to a single denomination)
词性: adjective

pan-presbyterian:/pænˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriən/
noun
泛长老会主义者
A supporter or advocate of pan-presbyterianism.
The pan-presbyterian believed in a unified approach to Presbyterian doctrine and practice.  这位泛长老会主义者相信长老会教义和实践的统一方法。
Synonym: inter-presbyterianist
Antonym: sectarian (specifically referring to a single sect)
词性: noun
