myxocystodea:/ˌmɪksəʊsɪˈstəʊdiə/ /ˌmɪksoʊsɪˈstəʊdiə/
| n. |黏菌纲；黏菌类|A class of slime molds in the division Myxomycota, characterized by the formation of a multinucleate, motile plasmodium that gives rise to fruiting bodies bearing spores.|The *myxocystodea* were once classified as fungi, but are now considered protists.|近义词：slime molds, myxomycetes|反义词: None | noun
