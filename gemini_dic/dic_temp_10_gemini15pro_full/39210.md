conglaciation:/kənˌɡleɪʃiˈeɪʃən/ |n.| 联合；合并|The conglaciation of the two companies created a powerful new entity. 两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新实体。|The conglaciation of several small political parties formed a stronger opposition.  几个小政党的联合形成了更强大的反对派。| 近义词: amalgamation, merger, consolidation | 反义词: division, separation | 词性: 名词
