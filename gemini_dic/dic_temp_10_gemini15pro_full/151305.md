parietal: /pəˈraɪɪtəl/ (US), /pəˈraɪɪtəl/ (UK)| adj. n.| 壁的，顶骨的；（大学）学院的宿舍管理员|Relating to or forming the wall of a body part, cavity, or organ. A bone forming the central side and upper back part of each side of the skull.|The parietal bone is located on each side of the skull, just behind the frontal bone.  顶骨位于颅骨的两侧，就在额骨的后面。| The parietal lobe of the brain is responsible for processing sensory information.  大脑的顶叶负责处理感官信息。|Synonyms: wall, bordering|Antonyms: central, axial|adjective, noun
