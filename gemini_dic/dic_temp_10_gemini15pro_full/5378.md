alluvion:/əˈluːviən/
n.
冲积层；冲积土；淤积地
A deposit of sand, mud, etc., formed by flowing water.
The farmer's land increased through the alluvion deposited by the river.  这片农田因河水带来的冲积层而面积增加。

n.
（法律）淤积地所有权的增加
The increase of land area along a shore by deposited alluvium or by the recession of water.
The alluvion caused by the river changed the property lines. 河流造成的淤积改变了地产界线。


近义词:deposit, sediment, accretion, silt
反义词:erosion
词性:noun
