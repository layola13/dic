benesh:/bəˈnɛʃ/| n.| 贝奈什动作标记法 |A system of movement notation developed by Rudolf and Joan Benesh. It uses abstract symbols to record the position and movement of the body in space, primarily for dance and other forms of human movement.|The Benesh notation allowed the choreographer to preserve the intricate details of the ballet.|近义词:  movement notation, dance notation|反义词: None|词性: Noun
