palmidactyles:/ˌpælmɪˈdæktɪliːz/
| n. |蹼足，蹼状指（趾）|Webbed digits; having the fingers or toes connected by a membrane.|Palmidactyles are a common adaptation in aquatic animals. 蹼足是水生动物的一种常见适应性特征。| The duck's palmidactyles help it swim efficiently. 鸭子的蹼足帮助它有效地游泳。|近义词: webbed feet, webbed toes|反义词: non-webbed, separate digits|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin *palma* (palm of the hand) + Greek *daktylos* (finger, toe).
