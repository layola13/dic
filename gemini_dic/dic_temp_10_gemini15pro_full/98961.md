inter-grapples:/ˌɪntərˈɡræpəlz/ | v. |相互扭打，相互搏斗|engages in a close fight or struggle with another person or thing, often involving gripping or holding|The two wrestlers inter-grappled fiercely for the championship title.  两个摔跤手为了冠军头衔激烈地扭打在一起。|
The octopus inter-grappled with its prey. 章鱼与它的猎物扭打在一起。|近义词：wrestle, grapple, struggle, contend |反义词: yield, surrender |词性: 动词


Etymology:
"inter-" (prefix): from Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally"
"grapple": from Old French "graper" (to seize, grasp), ultimately from Frankish or another Germanic source.
