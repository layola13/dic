megascopic:/ˌmɛɡəˈskɒpɪk/ /ˌmɛɡəˈskɑːpɪk/| adj.|肉眼可见的，宏观的|Visible to the naked eye; large enough to be seen without a microscope.|例句：The megascopic features of the rock indicated it was granite. 这块岩石的宏观特征表明它是花岗岩。|例句：Megascopic examination revealed large crystals embedded in the matrix. 肉眼检查发现基质中嵌有大的晶体。|近义词：macroscopic, visible|反义词：microscopic, invisible|词性:形容词

词源:
From mega- + -scopic, from Greek skopein ‘look at, view’.
