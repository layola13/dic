baillie:/ˈbeɪli/| n.|市政官，区长，执行官|A baillie is a municipal officer in Scotland.  A baillie is similar to a mayor or alderman.  Historically, a baillie was also an officer of the law, similar to a sheriff.|例句：The baillie presided over the town council meeting. 市政官主持了镇议会会议。

例句：The baillie ordered the arrest of the thief. 区长下令逮捕小偷。|近义词：alderman, magistrate, councilor, mayor|反义词:citizen, resident|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *baillif (Modern French bailli), from Late Latin bailivus "administrator, manager," of uncertain origin.
