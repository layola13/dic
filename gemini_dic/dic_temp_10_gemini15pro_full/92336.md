hypo-allergenic:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌælərˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
低过敏性的；抗过敏的
Relatively unlikely to cause an allergic reaction.  Designed to reduce or minimize the possibility of an allergic reaction.
This laundry detergent is hypoallergenic and gentle on sensitive skin.  这种洗衣液是低过敏性的，对敏感肌肤温和。
I switched to a hypoallergenic brand of makeup because my skin was reacting badly to my old one. 我换了低过敏性的化妆品牌，因为我的皮肤对旧的化妆品反应不好。
synonyms: non-allergenic, sensitivity-free
antonyms: allergenic, irritating
词性: adjective
