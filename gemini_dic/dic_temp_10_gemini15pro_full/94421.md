implicitly:/ɪmˈplɪsɪtli/| adv.|含蓄地，暗示地|In a way that is suggested but not communicated directly.|She implicitly criticized the government's policy. 她含蓄地批评了政府的政策。|例句：He implicitly agreed to help. 他默许了帮忙。 他默许了帮忙。|近义词：tacitly, indirectly|反义词：explicitly, directly|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin implicitus, from Latin implicare "to involve, entangle," from in- "in" + plicare "to fold."
