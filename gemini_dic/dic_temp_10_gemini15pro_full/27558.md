canal:/kəˈnæl/ | n. | 运河；水道；管状器官 |  An artificial waterway constructed to allow the passage of boats or ships inland or to convey water for irrigation.  A tubular passage in the body. | The Panama Canal connects the Atlantic and Pacific oceans. 巴拿马运河连接大西洋和太平洋。 | synonym: channel, waterway, duct | antonym: isthmus, ridge | noun

canal:/kəˈnæl/ | vt. |  开凿运河 | To construct a canal through. | They canalized the area to improve transportation.  他们开凿运河以改善交通。| synonym: channel, ditch | antonym: fill, block | verb
