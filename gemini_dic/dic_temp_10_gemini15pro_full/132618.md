nondisadvantageds:/ˌnɒndɪsədˈvæntɪdʒdz/ | n. | 非弱势群体，非处境不利者 |  People who are not disadvantaged; those who do not experience social or economic hardship.  This term is often used in sociological and political contexts to refer to groups who do not face systemic barriers to opportunity. It can sometimes be perceived as awkward or insensitive. |  The study compared the outcomes of nondisadvantageds with those facing significant barriers. 这项研究比较了非弱势群体和面临重大障碍的群体的结果。|  The program aims to create a level playing field for both nondisadvantageds and disadvantaged individuals. 该计划旨在为非弱势群体和弱势群体创造一个公平的竞争环境。 | Synonyms: privileged, affluent, fortunate | Antonyms: disadvantaged, underprivileged, impoverished | Noun
