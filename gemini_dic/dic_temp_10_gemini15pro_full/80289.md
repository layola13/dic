goitered: /ˈɡɔɪtərd/ (US), /ˈɡɔɪtəd/ (UK)| adj.|甲状腺肿大的；患甲状腺肿的|Affected with or exhibiting a goiter, a swelling of the thyroid gland in the neck.|The doctor examined the patient's goitered neck. 医生检查了病人肿大的脖子。|The painting depicted a goitered woman. 这幅画描绘了一个患有甲状腺肿的妇女。|近义词：swollen, enlarged|反义词: normal, healthy|词性:形容词

词源:
From goiter + -ed.  "Goiter" comes from Old French *goitre, from Latin guttur "throat."
