antinationalism:/ˌæntiˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/
n.|反民族主义|Opposition to nationalism; the belief that nationalism is dangerous and harmful.|Many scholars argue that antinationalism is a necessary corrective to the excesses of nationalism. 许多学者认为，反民族主义是对民族主义过度行为的必要纠正。|Example 1:  Her antinationalism stemmed from a belief in global citizenship. 她的反民族主义源于对全球公民身份的信仰。

Example 2:  The conference explored the historical roots of antinationalism. 这次会议探讨了反民族主义的历史根源。|近义词：cosmopolitanism, internationalism, globalism|反义词：nationalism, patriotism, chauvinism|词性:noun
