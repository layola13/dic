flyability:/ˌflaɪəˈbɪləti/| n.|可飞行性，飞行能力|The drone's small size and advanced technology enhance its flyability in tight spaces.  这架无人机的小巧尺寸和先进技术增强了它在狭小空间的飞行能力。|例句：The flyability of the new aircraft was tested in a wind tunnel. 新型飞机的可飞行性在风洞中进行了测试。|例句：The bird's injured wing severely impacted its flyability. 这只鸟受伤的翅膀严重影响了它的飞行能力。|近义词：airworthiness, maneuverability|反义词:unflyability, non-airworthiness|词性:名词

Etymology: fly + ability.  From "fly" (verb) meaning "to move through the air using wings" + "-ability" (suffix) meaning "the quality or state of being able to do something."  最终来源于古英语 *flēogan.
