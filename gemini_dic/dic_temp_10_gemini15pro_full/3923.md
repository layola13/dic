agrief:/əˈɡriːf/
adjective
悲伤的，悲痛的
Deeply sorrowful or distressed, especially by bereavement.
She was agrief after the loss of her beloved pet. 她因为失去心爱的宠物而悲痛欲绝。
近义词: sorrowful, grieving, heartbroken, bereaved
反义词: joyful, happy, elated
