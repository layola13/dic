felinenesses:/fiˈlaɪnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
猫的特性；猫科动物的特性
the characteristic qualities of a cat or feline
Her quiet grace and aloofness were part of her felineness. 她安静的优雅和冷漠是她的猫科动物特性的一部分。

近义词:
cattishness, feline qualities

反义词:
canineness, dog-like qualities

词性:
noun

词源:
From feline + -ness.  Feline comes from the Latin *felīnus, from fēlēs "cat."
