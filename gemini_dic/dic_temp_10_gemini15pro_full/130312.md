non-tarnishing:/ˌnɒnˈtɑːnɪʃɪŋ/ |adj.| 不失去光泽的，不锈的|Not subject to tarnishing; resistant to discoloration or corrosion, especially by exposure to air or moisture. | This non-tarnishing silverware will look beautiful for years to come.  这种不失去光泽的银器在未来几年都会看起来很漂亮。

| Silver-plated items are not truly non-tarnishing, but they tarnish much more slowly than sterling silver. 镀银制品并非真正不失去光泽，但它们失去光泽的速度比纯银慢得多。|Synonyms: stainless, corrosion-resistant, untarnishable | Antonyms: tarnishable, corrosive, rusting | adjective
