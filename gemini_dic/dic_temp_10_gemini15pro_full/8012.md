anthracic: /ænˈθræsɪk/
adjective
炭疽的; 由炭疽杆菌引起的
Relating to or caused by anthrax.
Anthracic infections can be fatal if left untreated. 炭疽感染如果不及时治疗可能会致命。
The vet diagnosed the cow with an anthracic infection. 兽医诊断这头牛感染了炭疽。
Synonyms:  anthrax,  carbuncular
Antonyms:  healthy, noninfectious
词性: adjective
