papyrine:/pəˈpaɪraɪn/
noun
纸莎草纸；莎草纸文件
A material similar to thick paper that was used in ancient times for writing or painting on, made from the pith of the papyrus plant.
The ancient Egyptians used papyrus to make paper. 古埃及人用纸莎草做纸。
synonyms: papyrus, paper
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical material. Could consider digital document as a modern contrast.)
