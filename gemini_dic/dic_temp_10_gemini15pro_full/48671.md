deference:/ˈdefərəns/ /ˈdɛfərəns/| n.|尊重，顺从，敬重|Respectful submission or yielding to the judgment, opinion, will, etc., of another.|I showed deference to his wishes. 我尊重他的意愿。|Synonyms: respect, reverence, regard, esteem, honor|Antonyms: disrespect, disregard, contempt, defiance|noun

deference:/ˈdefərəns/ /ˈdɛfərəns/| n.|尊重，顺从，敬重|Courteous regard for people's feelings.|He treated his elders with deference. 他对长辈毕恭毕敬。|Synonyms: courtesy, politeness, civility|Antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility|noun
