hyacinthine:/ˈhaɪəsɪnθaɪn/| adj.|风信子似的；蓝紫色的；华丽的，辞藻华丽的|Of or like a hyacinth; having a purplish blue color; elaborately or excessively ornamented in language or style. |例句：The hyacinthine sky at dusk was breathtaking.  黄昏时分，风信子般的天空令人叹为观止。

例句：His hyacinthine prose was often criticized for being overly ornate.  他辞藻华丽的散文常因过于华丽而受到批评。|近义词：purple, violet, azure, ornate, flowery|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hyacinthus (source also of Old French jacinte), from Latin hyacinthus, from Greek hyakinthos.
