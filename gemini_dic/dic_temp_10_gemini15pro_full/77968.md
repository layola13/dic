genterie:/ˈdʒɛntri/
n.
绅士阶层，贵族
The landed gentry of the English countryside.  英国乡村的乡绅阶层。
The gentries were known for their refined manners and education. 这些绅士以其优雅的举止和良好的教育而闻名。
synonyms: aristocracy, nobility, elite, upper class
antonyms: commoners, peasantry, lower class
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *genterie, from gentil "noble, of good family," from Latin gentilis "of the same clan or family."
