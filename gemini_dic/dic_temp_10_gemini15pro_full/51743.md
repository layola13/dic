devolutions:/ˌdɛvəˈluːʃənz/
|n.|交还；归还；权力下放|The devolution of power from central government to local authorities. 中央政府向地方当局下放权力。|例句：The devolution of power to Scotland and Wales has led to significant changes. 权力下放给苏格兰和威尔士带来了显著的变化。

例句：The devolution of responsibilities to lower levels of management empowered employees. 将责任下放到较低级别的管理层增强了员工的能力。


|近义词：transfer, delegation, surrender|反义词: centralization, retention|词性:名词
