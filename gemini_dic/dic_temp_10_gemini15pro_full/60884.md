elementary:/ˌelɪˈmentri/ (US), /ˌelɪˈmɛntri/ (UK)| adj.|基本的；初级的；简单的|Relating to the basic or fundamental principles of a subject; of or for beginners; simple or undeveloped.|例句：Elementary school teaches children basic subjects like reading and math. 小学教孩子们基础科目，比如阅读和数学。

例句：This is an elementary question that anyone can answer. 这是一个任何人都能回答的基本问题。|近义词：basic, fundamental, rudimentary, simple, introductory|反义词：advanced, complex, complicated, sophisticated|词性:形容词
