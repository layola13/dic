antsiest: /ˈæŋksiəst/ | adj. | 最焦虑的 |  Superlative form of anxious: most anxious.  Feeling or showing worry, nervousness, or unease about something with an uncertain outcome. | She was the antsiest I'd ever seen her before the exam.  她在考试前是我见过的最焦虑的样子。|  Example: He was the antsiest person in the waiting room. 他是候诊室里最焦虑的人。| Near Synonyms: most worried, most apprehensive, most nervous, most uneasy | Antonyms: calmest, most relaxed, most serene | adjective
