emulousness:/ˈemjələsnəs/
n.
竞争心，好胜心，努力赶超的欲望
The desire to equal or excel; rivalry, competitiveness.
His emulousness drove him to work harder than anyone else. 他的好胜心驱使他比任何人都更加努力工作。
近义词: competitiveness, ambitiousness, rivalry, emulation
反义词: apathy, indifference, contentment
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aemulus "rivaling" + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin aemulari "to rival."
