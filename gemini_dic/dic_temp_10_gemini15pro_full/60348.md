egotist:/ˈiːɡətɪst/ /ˈɛɡətɪst/ | n. | 自我主义者；自负的人 | A person who is excessively conceited and self-absorbed; someone who talks primarily about themselves and their own interests. |  He's such an egotist, he can't talk about anything but himself. 他太自负了，除了他自己什么都谈不了。

| Example 1:  The egotist constantly boasted about his achievements, boring everyone around him.  这个自负的人不断吹嘘自己的成就，让周围的人都感到厌烦。

| Example 2:  She was accused of being an egotist, always putting her own needs before others. 她被指责是一个利己主义者，总是把自己的需求放在别人之前。

| 近义词: narcissist, braggart, blowhard | 反义词: altruist, humble person | 词性: noun
