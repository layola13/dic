dern:/dɜːrn/
adjective
damned (used euphemistically as a mild oath).
A euphemism for "darned," itself a euphemism for "damned."  Expresses mild annoyance or frustration.
"Dern it, I missed the bus again!"  该死的，我又错过公车了！
近义词: darned, blasted, confounded
反义词: blessed
