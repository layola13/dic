fog:/fɒɡ/ (UK) /fɑːɡ/ (US)| n.|雾|A thick cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the atmosphere at or near the earth's surface that obscures or restricts visibility.|The fog was so thick we could barely see the road ahead.  大雾弥漫，我们几乎看不到前方的路。|近义词：mist, haze|反义词: clarity, sunshine|名词

fog:/fɒɡ/ (UK) /fɑːɡ/ (US)| v. |(使)起雾；(使)模糊不清|To become or cause to become obscured by or as if by fog.|Condensation fogged the windows.  冷凝水使窗户模糊不清。|近义词：mist, cloud|反义词: clear|动词
