chummy: /ˈtʃʌmi/ | adj. | 亲密的，友好的 |  Having the quality of a close and friendly relationship.  | They're very chummy with their neighbors. 他们和邻居的关系非常友好。| 近义词: friendly, close, intimate, amicable, matey | 反义词: unfriendly, distant, hostile | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From chum + -y.  "Chum" originally referred to roommates or close companions, especially in schools, likely originating from chamber-fellow.
