cross-selling:/ˈkrɒsˌsɛlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌselɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|交叉销售|The practice of encouraging existing customers to buy additional products or services from the same company.|The bank teller tried cross-selling me a credit card when I opened a checking account. 银行出纳员在我开设支票账户时试图向我交叉销售信用卡。|Related terms: upselling, product bundling|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cross-sell:/ˈkrɒsˌsɛl/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌsel/ (UK)| v.|交叉销售|To sell (an additional product or service) to an existing customer.|The company is trying to cross-sell its new insurance products to existing customers. 该公司正试图向现有客户交叉销售其新的保险产品。|Synonyms: upsell, bundle|Antonyms: N/A|verb
