ataxic:/əˈtæksɪk/ | adj. | 共济失调的，运动失调的 | Characterized by ataxia, or loss of coordination of voluntary muscle movements. |  The patient exhibited ataxic gait and slurred speech. 病人表现出共济失调的步态和言语不清。 | Synonyms: incoordinated, unsteady, clumsy | Antonyms: coordinated, steady, balanced | adjective

ataxia:/əˈtæksiə/ | n. | 共济失调 | Loss of coordination of voluntary muscle movements. | The ataxia made it difficult for her to walk. 共济失调使她行走困难。 | Synonyms: incoordination, unsteadiness | Antonyms: coordination, steadiness | noun

Etymology: From Greek *ataktos* "disordered, irregular," from *a-* "not" + *taktos* "ordered, arranged" (from *tassein* "to arrange").
