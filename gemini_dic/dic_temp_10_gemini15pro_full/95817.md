incorporate: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ (US) /ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/ (UK)
|vt. |包含，吸收；体现；合并；组成公司|To include something as part of something larger.|The new car design incorporates all the latest safety features. 这款新车的设计包含了所有最新的安全特性。|
incorporate: /ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ (US) /ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/ (UK)
|adj. |合并的；一体化的；组成公司的|Formed into a legal corporation.|The company was incorporated in 2002. 该公司于2002年注册成立。|
incorporate:
|词源|From Late Latin incorporatus, past participle of incorporare "to embody," from in- "into" + corporare "to form into a body," from corpus "body."|

|近义词|integrate, include, combine, merge, absorb|

|反义词|separate, divide, exclude, remove|
