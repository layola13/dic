constatives:/ˈkɒnstətɪvz/| n.|述事句，陈述句|A statement that describes or asserts a state of affairs, often contrasted with performatives, which perform an action through the utterance itself.  Constatives can be true or false.| "The cat is on the mat" is a constative.|Synonyms: statement, assertion, declaration, proposition|Antonyms: performative, question, command|noun
