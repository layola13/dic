greenswarded:/ˈɡriːnˌswɔːrdɪd/
adjective
绿草覆盖的；草皮覆盖的
covered with green sward; turfed
The greenswarded hills rolled gently down to the sea.  绿草覆盖的山丘缓缓地向大海倾斜。
The park boasted a beautifully greenswarded bowling green. 公园里有一个绿草如茵的漂亮的保龄球场。
近义词: grassy, verdant, turfed
反义词: barren, arid, desolate
词性: adjective

词源:
From "green" + "sward" + "-ed".  "Green" refers to the color green, "sward" refers to a stretch of grassy turf, and "-ed" is a suffix used to form adjectives.
