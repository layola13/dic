glow-worm:/ˈɡloʊˌwɜːrm/ (US), /ˈɡləʊˌwɜːm/ (UK)| n.|萤火虫|A beetle whose larva and wingless female emit light.|例句：The children were fascinated by the glow-worms in the garden. 孩子们被花园里的萤火虫迷住了。

例句：We saw several glow-worms twinkling in the dark field. 我们看到几只萤火虫在黑暗的田野里闪烁。

近义词：firefly, lightning bug|反义词:|词性:noun
