anthropomorphous:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfəs/| adj.|拟人的，赋予人性的|Attributing human form or personality to a god, animal, or thing.|例句：The children's book featured anthropomorphous animals who went to school and had jobs. 这本童书以拟人化的动物为主角，它们上学和工作。

例句：It's common to anthropomorphize pets, attributing human emotions and thoughts to them. 把宠物拟人化，赋予它们人类的情感和思想是很常见的。|近义词：humanlike, humanoid, personified|反义词: non-human, inanimate|词性: adjective
