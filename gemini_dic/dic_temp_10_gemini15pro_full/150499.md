pantographically:/ˌpæntəˈɡræfɪkəli/
adverb
缩放地，成比例地
in a way that relates to or involves a pantograph; by means of a pantograph; proportionally, on all sides simultaneously.
The design was pantographically enlarged onto the billboard.  这个设计被按比例放大到广告牌上。
The image was pantographically reduced to fit on the screen. 图像按比例缩小以适应屏幕。
近义词: proportionally, scalably
反义词: disproportionately
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From pantograph + -ically. Pantograph comes from Greek παντ- (pant-, “all”) +  γράφειν (graphein, “to write, draw”).
