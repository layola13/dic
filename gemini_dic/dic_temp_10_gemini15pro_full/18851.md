beseeched:/bɪˈsiːtʃt/| v. |恳求，哀求|To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.|She beseeched him to stay. 她恳求他留下。|近义词：implored, begged, pleaded, supplicated|反义词：demanded, commanded|verb

beseeched:/bɪˈsiːtʃt/| v. |恳求，哀求|To ask (someone) urgently and fervently to do something; implore; entreat.|He beseeched the king for mercy. 他恳求国王开恩。|近义词：importuned, entreated, appealed to|反义词：ordered, directed|verb
