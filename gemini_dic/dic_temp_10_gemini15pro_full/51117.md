despised:/dɪˈspaɪzd/| adj.|被鄙视的，令人憎恶的|Feeling or showing contempt or intense dislike.|He was a despised figure in the community. 他是社区里一个被鄙视的人物。|Synonyms: hated, loathed, detested, abhorred|Antonyms: admired, loved, respected, cherished|adjective

despise:/dɪˈspaɪz/| vt.|鄙视，憎恶|Feel contempt or a deep repugnance for.|She despised him for his weakness. 她鄙视他的软弱。|Synonyms: hate, loathe, detest, abhor|Antonyms: admire, love, respect, cherish|verb
