hammeringly:/ˈhæmərɪŋli/
adv.
极其地，强烈地
With overwhelming force or impact; emphatically.
He made his point hammeringly clear. 他把他的观点讲得非常清楚。
近义词:forcefully, emphatically, powerfully, overwhelmingly, decisively
反义词: gently, subtly, mildly
词性:副词

词源: From "hammering" (present participle of the verb "hammer") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hamaraz.
