cheiropterygium:/ˌkaɪrɒpˈtɛrɪdʒɪəm/ | n. | 手翼；鳍状肢；鱼鳍 | A vertebrate limb having digits (fingers or toes); specifically : the limb of a tetrapod vertebrate. |  The cheiropterygium is the skeletal support of the distal part of the paired fins in some fishes, and is believed to be the ancestor of the tetrapod limb.  鱼类的鳍状肢是四足动物肢体的祖先。 | synonym: fin, limb, appendage | antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider structures without digits) | noun
