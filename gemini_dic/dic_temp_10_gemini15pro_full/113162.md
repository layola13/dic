lupulin: /ˈluːpjʊlɪn/ | n. | 蛇麻素；啤酒花苦味素 | A yellow, bitter powder found in the cones of the hop plant, used in brewing and as a sedative.  |  Lupulin gives beer its characteristic bitter flavor. 蛇麻素赋予啤酒特有的苦味。| The lupulin glands contain the resins and essential oils that contribute to beer's aroma and bitterness. 蛇麻腺体包含树脂和精油，这些成分有助于啤酒的香气和苦味。 | synonyms: hop resin, hop powder | antonyms: None | noun
