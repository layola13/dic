circumspectly:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌspektli/ | adv. | 谨慎地，小心地 |  In a careful and thoughtful way, considering all the possible risks or consequences. |  He circumspectly approached the dangerous animal. 他小心翼翼地靠近那只危险的动物。 |  She circumspectly worded her email to avoid any misunderstandings. 她措辞谨慎地写邮件，以避免任何误解。 | cautiously, prudently, carefully | rashly, recklessly, carelessly | adverb
