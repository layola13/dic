parleyers:/ˈpɑːrliərz/
n.
谈判者；交涉者
People who hold a parley; those who discuss terms with an enemy or opponent.
The parleyers met in a neutral zone to discuss the terms of the ceasefire.  交涉者在中立区会面，讨论停火条款。
The parleyers from both sides attempted to reach a peaceful resolution. 双方谈判者试图达成和平解决方案。
negotiators, mediators, emissaries
opponents, adversaries
noun
