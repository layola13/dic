betacismus:/ˈbeɪtəsɪzəm/ | n. |  β音错乱症；将b音发成p音的毛病 | A speech impediment involving the substitution of p for b. |  He suffers from betacismus and often pronounces "ball" as "pall." 他患有β音错乱症，经常把“ball”发成“pall”。 | 近义词: lisp, speech impediment | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek βητακισμός (bētakismós), from βῆτα (bêta, “beta”) + -ισμός (-ismós).
