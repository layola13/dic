cannonballed:/ˈkænənˌbɔːld/ | v. | 炮弹式跳水；猛冲；快速移动 | To jump into water headfirst with your knees bent and your arms wrapped around your knees, like a cannonball. Also, to move quickly and forcefully like a cannonball being fired. |  He cannonballed into the pool, sending a huge wave of water over the side. 他炮弹式地跳入泳池，激起巨大的水花。

She cannonballed through the crowd to catch the train. 她像炮弹一样冲过人群去赶火车。 | 近义词: plunged, dived, hurtled, zoomed | 反义词: crawled, crept, inched | 词性: 动词
