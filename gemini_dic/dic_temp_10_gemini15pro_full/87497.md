heterochlamydeous:/ˌhɛtərəkləˈmɪdiəs/
adjective
异被的
Having a perianth differentiated into calyx and corolla; having dissimilar sepals and petals.  The flower has two distinct whorls: an outer calyx of sepals and an inner corolla of petals.
The rose is a heterochlamydeous flower, showcasing distinct sepals and petals.  玫瑰是一种异被花，展现出不同的萼片和花瓣。
Most flowering plants are heterochlamydeous. 大多数开花植物都是异被的。
近义词:dichlamydeous
反义词:homochlamydeous, monochlamydeous
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hetero- (different) + chlamydeous (having a perianth). Ultimately from Greek heteros (different) and chlamys (cloak, mantle).
