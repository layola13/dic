ferrying:/ˈfɛriɪŋ/
v.
运送，摆渡
transporting something or someone across a body of water.
The boat was ferrying passengers across the river.  这艘船正在运送乘客过河。
The company is ferrying supplies to the island.  这家公司正在向岛上运送物资。
transport, convey, carry, ship

verb
