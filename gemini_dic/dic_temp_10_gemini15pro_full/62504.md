endow:/ɪnˈdaʊ/| vt.|赋予，捐赠|to provide with a quality, ability, or asset, especially as a permanent gift or source of income; to give a large amount of money to a school, hospital, or other institution to provide it with an income|The wealthy businessman endowed the university with a large sum of money for research.  这位富商向大学捐赠了一大笔钱用于研究。

The artist was endowed with great talent.  这位艺术家被赋予了巨大的才能。|近义词：bestow, grant, donate, furnish, equip|反义词：deprive, divest, strip|verb
