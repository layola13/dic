pastiche:/pæˈstiːʃ/ /pɑːˈstiːʃ/| n.|模仿作品，混合作品，大杂烩|An artistic work in a style that imitates that of another work, artist, or period.|例句：The film is a pastiche of several different genres. 这部电影是几种不同类型的模仿作品。|例句：Her novel is a pastiche of 19th-century romantic literature.她的小说是19世纪浪漫主义文学的模仿作品。|近义词：imitation, parody, potpourri, medley|反义词：original|词性:名词

pastiche:/pæˈstiːʃ/ /pɑːˈstiːʃ/| v.|拼凑，模仿|To imitate the style of (an artist or work).|例句：He pastiched the styles of several great painters in his own work. 他在自己的作品中模仿了几位伟大画家的风格。|例句：The architect pastiched several historical styles in the design of the building.这位建筑师在建筑设计中模仿了几种历史风格。|近义词：imitate, copy, emulate|反义词：originate, innovate|词性:动词
