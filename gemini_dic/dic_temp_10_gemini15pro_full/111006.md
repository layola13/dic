liquorish:/ˈlɪkərɪʃ/| adj.|贪吃甜食的；贪婪的；渴望的|Having a strong desire or craving, especially for sweet things; greedy. Also, seductive or enticing.|例句：The child has a liquorish tooth.  这个孩子嗜甜。

例句：She cast a liquorish glance at the handsome stranger. 她向那个英俊的陌生人投去贪婪的一瞥。|近义词：greedy, avaricious, covetous, desirous, craving, sweet-toothed|反义词：abstemious, temperate, indifferent|词性:形容词


liquorice (British English):/ˈlɪkərɪs/| n.|甘草；由甘草根制成的糖果|A black confection made from the root of the liquorice plant.|例句：He bought a stick of liquorice. 他买了一根甘草棒。

例句：Liquorice is used in some medicines.  甘草用于一些药物中。|近义词：licorice (American English)|反义词:|词性:名词
