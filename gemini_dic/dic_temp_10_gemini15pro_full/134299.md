noninjuriousness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʒʊəriəsnəs/
noun
无害；无损
The state or quality of being not injurious; harmlessness.
The doctor assured me of the noninjuriousness of the medication. 医生向我保证了这种药物的无害性。
innocuousness, harmlessness
harmfulness, injuriousness
n.
