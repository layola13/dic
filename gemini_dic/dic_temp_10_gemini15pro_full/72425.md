flugelhorn:/ˈfluːɡəlˌhɔːrn/ (US), /ˈfluːɡəlˌhɔːn/ (UK)| n.| Flügelhorn; 夫吕号|A brass instrument resembling a trumpet but having a wider, conical bore, producing a mellower tone.
