dinoceras:/ˌdaɪnəˈsɪərəs/| n. |恐角兽|An extinct genus of large, herbivorous mammals belonging to the order Dinocerata, characterized by bony protuberances on the head and saber-like upper canine teeth.|Dinoceras was a massive animal, about the size of a rhinoceros. 恐角兽是一种巨大的动物，大约有犀牛那么大。| Dinoceras fossils have been found in North America. 在北美发现了恐角兽化石。|近义词:Uintatherium|反义词:N/A|名词
