imperseverant:/ˌɪmpɜːsɪˈvɪərənt/ | adj. | 不坚忍的，缺乏毅力的 | Lacking perseverance; not persevering.  | He was imperseverant in his studies and gave up easily. 他学习缺乏毅力，容易放弃。|  The imperseverant artist abandoned the project halfway through.  这位缺乏毅力的艺术家在项目进行到一半时放弃了。 | 近义词: fickle, unsteady, erratic, inconstant | 反义词: perseverant, persistent, tenacious, resolute | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "im-" (not) + "persevere" (to continue in a course of action even in the face of difficulty) + "-ant" (forming adjectives).  From Latin "perseverare" (to persist).
