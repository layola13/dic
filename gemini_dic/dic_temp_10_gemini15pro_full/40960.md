cooperativeness:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪvnəs/| n. |合作性，协作性|The quality of being willing to work together with others towards a common goal.|Her cooperativeness made the project a success. 她的合作性使项目取得了成功。|Synonyms: collaboration, teamwork, synergy|Antonyms: uncooperativeness, obstructiveness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cooperative + -ness.  Cooperative itself comes from co- (together) + operate (to work).  Ultimately from Latin *co- (together) + operari (to work).
