caleche:/kəˈlæʃ/ /kæˈleɪʃ/| n. |轻便马车，敞篷马车|A light, open horse-drawn carriage, often with a folding hood.|The caleche clip-clopped along the cobblestone streets.  轻便马车沿着鹅卵石街道发出得得的响声。|Synonyms: carriage, buggy, cabriolet|Antonyms: car, truck|noun

Etymology->
From French *calèche*, from German *Kalesche*, from Czech *kolejka* "small wheel," diminutive of *kolo* "wheel," from Proto-Slavic *kolo*. Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *kʷel-*.
