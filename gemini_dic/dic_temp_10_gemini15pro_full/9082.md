antimodernism:/ˌæntiˈmɒdərnɪzəm/ /ˌæntiˈmɑːdərnɪzəm/| n. |反现代主义|Opposition to modernism, especially in the arts and literature, often characterized by a preference for traditional values and styles.|The resurgence of antimodernism in certain political circles is a cause for concern.  某些政治圈子中反现代主义的复兴令人担忧。|Examples of antimodernism can be seen in the architecture of the early 20th century.  在20世纪早期的建筑中可以看到反现代主义的例子。|近义词：traditionalism, conservatism|反义词: modernism, avant-gardism|词性: noun
