nonnourishment:/ˌnɒnˈnʌrɪʃmənt/ |n.|缺乏营养,营养不足|The state of not receiving adequate nourishment.  Lack of sufficient nutrients to sustain health and growth.|Prolonged nonnourishment can lead to serious health problems. 长期营养不足会导致严重的健康问题.|Synonyms: malnutrition, undernourishment, starvation|Antonyms: nourishment, nutrition|noun
