hyperbolist:/haɪˈpɜːrbəlist/
n.
夸张者，吹牛者
A person who uses hyperbole; a person who exaggerates.
He's such a hyperbolist, you can't believe a word he says. 他真是个吹牛大王，你不能相信他说的任何一句话。
synonyms: exaggerator, embellisher, storyteller, fabricator
antonyms: understater, minimizer
noun

hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/
n.
夸张，夸饰法
The use of exaggeration as a rhetorical device or figure of speech.
"I've told you a million times" is an example of hyperbole.  “我已经告诉你一百万遍了”是夸张的例子。
synonyms: exaggeration, overstatement, magnification, amplification
antonyms: understatement, litotes
noun
