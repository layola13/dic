bettering:/ˈbetərɪŋ/
verb (present participle of 'better')
改善，改进
Improving something; making something better.
She devoted her life to bettering the lives of the poor. 她一生致力于改善穷人的生活。
He's always looking for ways of bettering himself. 他总是在寻找提升自我的方法。
synonyms: improving, enhancing, ameliorating, upgrading
antonyms: worsening, deteriorating, impairing
