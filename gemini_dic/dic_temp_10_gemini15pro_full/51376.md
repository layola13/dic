determinableness:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnəblənəs/
n.
可确定性, 可断定性
The quality or state of being determinable; ascertainableness.
The determinableness of the case depends on the available evidence.  这个案件的可确定性取决于现有的证据。
Near Synonyms: ascertainability, definability, resolvability
Antonyms: indeterminacy, uncertainty
词性: 名词
