mechanismic:/ˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. |机械论的，机械式的|Characterized by or resembling a mechanism; relating to the philosophy of mechanism, which explains phenomena in terms of physical or mechanical causes.| The scientist took a mechanistic view of the human body, seeing it as a complex machine.  科学家将人体视为一台复杂的机器，采取了机械论的观点。|mechanistic approach involves breaking down a complex system into its individual components and analyzing how they interact. 机械论的方法包括将复杂系统分解成各个组成部分，并分析它们是如何相互作用的。|automatic, mechanical|organic, holistic|adjective
