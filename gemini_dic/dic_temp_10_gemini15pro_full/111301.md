lituite:/ˈlɪtʃuˌaɪt/| n.| Lituites |An extinct genus of orthoconic nautiloid cephalopods characterized by a long, slender shell that begins with a tightly coiled portion and then straightens out. |Lituites fossils are found in Ordovician rocks.  奥陶纪岩石中发现了 Lituites 化石。|Near synonyms: orthocone, nautiloid |Antonyms: None (as it's a specific genus)|Noun
