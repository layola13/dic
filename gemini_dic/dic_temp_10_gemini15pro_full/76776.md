gametes:/ˈɡæmiːts/
n.
配子
A mature haploid male or female germ cell that is able to unite with another of the opposite sex in sexual reproduction to form a zygote.
例句：
Gametes are produced by meiosis.
配子是通过减数分裂产生的。

Gametes contain half the number of chromosomes as other cells in the organism.
配子包含的染色体数量是生物体中其他细胞的一半。

近义词：sex cells, germ cells
反义词：somatic cells
词性：noun
