galeated:/ˈɡeɪliˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|盔状的，戴盔的|Having or wearing a helmet or a helmet-like structure, especially a bony or horny crest on the head.|The galeated curassow is a large bird with a prominent casque on its head.|近义词：helmeted, crested|反义词:bareheaded, helmetless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *galeatus*, past participle of *galeare* "to furnish with a helmet," from *galea* "helmet."
