insnarl:/ɪnˈsnɑːrl/ | vt. | 缠住，使陷入混乱 | To catch and entangle in a snare or net; to involve in a complicated or confusing situation. | The fishing net insnarled a large number of fish.  渔网缠住了一大群鱼。

The vines insnarled the hiker's legs. 藤蔓缠住了徒步旅行者的腿。 | 近义词：entangle, trap, snare | 反义词：extricate, free, release | 词性: 动词

词源:  From in- + snarl (v.), from Old English *snearlian "to knot, twist."
