mutabilities:/ˌmjuːtəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可变性；易变性|The quality or state of being mutable; changeability or fickleness.  This refers to the ability of something to be changed or altered. |The mutabilities of the stock market make it a risky investment. 股票市场的易变性使其成为一项风险投资。|Synonym: variability, changeability, inconstancy, fluidity, instability|Antonym: immutability, constancy, stability, permanence, fixedness|noun
