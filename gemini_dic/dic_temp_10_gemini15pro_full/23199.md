bowelless:/ˈbaʊəlɪs/| adj. |无情的，冷酷的，残忍的|Lacking compassion or feeling; merciless; cruel.|He carried out the orders with bowelless efficiency. 他冷酷无情地执行了命令。|近义词：ruthless, merciless, heartless, cruel, callous|反义词：compassionate, merciful, kind|词性:形容词

词源: From bowel + -less.  Originally referred to disemboweling, later extended metaphorically to mean lacking in feeling or compassion, as the bowels were once considered the seat of emotions.
