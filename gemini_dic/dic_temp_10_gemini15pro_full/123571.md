monstrosity:/mɒnˈstrɒsɪti/ (US) /mɑːnˈstrɑːsəti/| n. | 怪物，畸形，可怕的东西 | Something that is extremely large and ugly, or something that is shocking and unacceptable. | The new office building is a real monstrosity. 这座新的办公楼真是个怪物。| synonyms: abomination, eyesore, horror, grotesque | antonyms: beauty, marvel, wonder | noun

monstrosity:/mɒnˈstrɒsɪti/ (US) /mɑːnˈstrɑːsəti/| n. | 恶劣行径，残暴行为 |  An act that is cruel or shocking. | The bombing was a monstrosity.  这次爆炸是残暴的行为。| synonyms: atrocity, outrage, barbarity | antonyms: kindness, compassion, humanity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *monstrositas, from Latin monstrum (“monster, portent, prodigy”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
