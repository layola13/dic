overtimbered:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪmbərd/
adjective
过度林木覆盖的；树木过密的
Having too many trees; excessively wooded.
The valley was dark and overtimbered, making it difficult to navigate.  这个山谷阴暗且树木茂密，难以通行。
The overtimbered plot of land was unsuitable for farming. 这块树木过密的土地不适合耕种。

近义词: densely wooded, overgrown
反义词: sparsely wooded, clear-cut
