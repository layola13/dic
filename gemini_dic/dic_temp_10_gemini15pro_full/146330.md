overfertility: /ˌoʊvər.fərˈtɪl.ə.ti/
noun
过度生育，生育力过剩
The state of being excessively fertile, typically characterized by a high birth rate.
Overfertility can strain resources in developing countries. 过度生育会给发展中国家的资源带来压力。
The country's overfertility has led to social and economic problems.  这个国家的过度生育导致了社会和经济问题。

近义词: hyperfertility, excessive fertility
反义词: infertility, subfertility
词性: noun
