durukuli: /dʊˈruːkʊli/ | n. | 杜鲁库利（一种印度传统坐姿）| Durukuli is a traditional Indian sitting posture with legs folded and tucked under the body.  It is often used during meditation or prayer. |  He sat in durukuli, meditating peacefully. 他以杜鲁库利坐姿平静地冥想。|  She assumed the durukuli posture for her yoga practice.  她摆出杜鲁库利姿势进行瑜伽练习。 | Synonyms: sukhasana, Indian style, cross-legged | Antonyms: standing, reclining | noun
