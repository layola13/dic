flack:/flæk/ | n. | 吹捧；公关；新闻发言人；高射炮；批评；责备 |  A publicist, especially one who engages in aggressive promotion or publicity. Also, strong criticism or disapproval. | The movie star's flack denied the rumors about her upcoming divorce.  这位电影明星的公关否认了有关她即将离婚的传闻。 | Synonyms: publicist, press agent, spokesperson, criticism, flak | Antonyms: criticism (for publicist meaning), praise (for criticism meaning) | noun

flack:/flæk/ | vi. | 做公关工作；吹捧；批评；责备 | To act as a publicist; to promote aggressively. Also, to criticize strongly. | He flacked for the new tech startup, generating a lot of buzz.  他为这家新的科技初创公司做宣传，引起了很多关注。 | Synonyms: publicize, promote, criticize, lambaste | Antonyms: criticize (for publicize meaning), praise (for criticize meaning) | verb
