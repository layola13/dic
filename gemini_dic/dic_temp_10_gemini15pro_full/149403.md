packager:/ˈpækɪdʒər/ | n. |包装商，包装工，包装机|A person or company that packages goods.  A machine that packages goods.|The packager sealed the box carefully. 包装商仔细地封好了箱子。|Synonyms: packer, wrapper |Antonyms: unpacker |noun

packager:/ˈpækɪdʒər/ | n. |打包程序;打包器|A program or tool used to bundle software or other data into a single distributable package. |The packager created an installer for the new application. 打包程序为新的应用程序创建了一个安装程序。|Synonyms: bundler, assembler |Antonyms: unpacker, extractor |noun
