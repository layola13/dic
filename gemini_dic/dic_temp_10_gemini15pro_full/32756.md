What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? = 她做什么谋生？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你这周一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before I arrived? = 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the decade? = 到这十年结束会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
