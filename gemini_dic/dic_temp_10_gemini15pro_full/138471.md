novelize: /ˈnɒvəlaɪz/ (British) /ˈnɑːvəlaɪz/ (American)
verb
小说化；将…改编成小说
To adapt or convert (a story, play, film, etc.) into a novel.
The movie was novelized shortly after its release. 这部电影上映后不久就被改编成了小说。
The studio novelized the screenplay. 电影公司将剧本改编成了小说。
synonyms: adapt, fictionalize
antonyms: N/A
v.
