crosshatch:/ˈkrɒshætʃ/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːshætʃ/ (US)| n. v.|交叉影线，交叉排线；用交叉影线画|A pattern of closely spaced parallel lines crossing each other.  To mark with crosshatching.|The artist used crosshatching to create a sense of depth in the drawing.  艺术家用交叉影线在画中营造了一种纵深感。|The architect crosshatched the area on the blueprint to indicate a change. 建筑师在蓝图上用交叉影线标出修改的地方。|近义词: cross-hatching, shading, hatching|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "blank," "unmarked")|词性: noun, verb


Etymology:
From cross + hatch (from Old English hæcc, meaning "gate, grating")
