chronographer: /krəˈnɒɡrəfər/ /krəˈnɑːɡrəfər/
n.
计时员；计时器；年代史编者
A person who studies or writes about chronology; a timekeeper, especially one who times sporting events; a device for recording time, especially one combined with a watch or clock.
The chronograph recorded the athlete's speed with precision. 计时器精确地记录了运动员的速度。
A chronograph is essential for accurate timekeeping in a race. 精确的计时器在比赛中至关重要。
近义词: timer, timekeeper, chronometer
反义词: None
词性: noun
