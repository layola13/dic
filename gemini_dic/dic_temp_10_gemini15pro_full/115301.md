mannas:/ˈmænəz/| n. |吗哪；天赐之物；意外的礼物|Something that provides nourishment or sustenance, often unexpectedly or miraculously.  It refers to the food miraculously provided for the Israelites in the desert in the Bible.|The government funding was a real manna for the struggling arts program. 政府的资助对于苦苦挣扎的艺术项目来说真是雪中送炭。|Synonyms: windfall, godsend, boon, blessing|Antonyms: curse, misfortune|noun
