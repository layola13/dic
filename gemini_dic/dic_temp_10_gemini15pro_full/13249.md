eat-out: /iːt aʊt/ | v. | 外出就餐；上馆子吃饭 | To have a meal in a restaurant or other establishment, rather than at home. | We ate out last night because I didn't feel like cooking. 昨晚我们出去吃饭了，因为我不想做饭。 |  We decided to eat out for our anniversary.  我们决定出去庆祝我们的周年纪念。| synonyms: dine out, eat at a restaurant | antonyms: eat in, cook at home | verb

eat-out: /iːt aʊt/ | n. | 外出就餐；(尤指经常性的)外出吃饭 | The act of eating a meal at a restaurant or other establishment. |  Eating out is expensive these days.  现在外出就餐很贵。|Let's try that new Thai eat-out. 我们去试试那家新开的泰国餐馆吧。| synonyms: dining out, restaurant meal | antonyms: home-cooked meal | noun
