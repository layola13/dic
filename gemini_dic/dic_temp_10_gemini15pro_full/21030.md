blickey:/ˈblɪki/| interj.|哎呀；我的天；（表示惊讶或恼怒）|An exclamation of surprise or annoyance.| Blickey! I've forgotten my keys! 哎呀！我忘了带钥匙了！|近义词：blimey, crikey, gosh, gee whiz|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection of surprise)|interjection

PHRASE->
(Blickey doesn't typically form part of set phrases.  It stands alone as an interjection. However, here are examples in context):

"Blickey, what's up?" = "哎呀，怎么了？"
"Blickey, what the hell happened?" = "哎呀，到底发生了什么事？"
"Blickey, what if we miss the train?" = "哎呀，如果我们错过了火车怎么办？"
"Blickey, what for?" (in response to being asked to do something) = "哎呀，为什么？" (回应被要求做某事)
"Blickey, no matter what, I'll be there." = "哎呀，无论如何，我都会去的。"


注意事项:

"Blickey" is primarily used in British English, particularly in informal settings. It's considered somewhat dated and not as common as other exclamations like "blimey" or "crikey".
