disobeyer:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪər/ | n. |不服从者，违抗者|A person who disobeys rules, laws, or commands. |例句：The disobeyer was punished for breaking the rules. 违反规定的人受到了惩罚。

例句：The disobeyer refused to follow the instructions.  这个不服从者拒绝遵守指示。|近义词：rebel, nonconformist, insurgent, deviant |反义词: conformist, follower, obedient |词性:noun
