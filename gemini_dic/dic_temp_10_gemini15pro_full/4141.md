air-mails:/ˈɛərˌmeɪlz/
n.
航空邮件
Mail that is transported by airplane.
I sent the documents by air-mail to ensure they arrived quickly. 我通过航空邮件寄送了文件，以确保它们能快速到达。
She received an air-mail letter from her friend overseas. 她收到了海外朋友寄来的航空邮件。
近义词:airmail, air post
反义词:surface mail, sea mail
词性:noun
