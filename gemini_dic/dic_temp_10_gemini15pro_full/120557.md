miraging:/ˈmɪrɑːʒɪŋ/
n. |海市蜃楼；幻景；|The act of creating or experiencing a mirage.  The shimmering effect of heat rising from the asphalt created a miraging effect. 沥青上升的热量产生的闪光效应造成了一种海市蜃楼的效果。|近义词：hallucinating, shimmering, illusion, delusion|反义词:reality, actuality|词性:名词

词源: From mirage + -ing.  Derived from the noun "mirage," which comes from the French verb *mirer* "to look at, to reflect," and the suffix -age.
