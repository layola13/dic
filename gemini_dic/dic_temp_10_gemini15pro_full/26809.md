cage's:/keɪdʒɪz/
noun
笼子的；鸟笼的
Possessive form of cage, indicating ownership or belonging.  It refers to something that belongs to or is associated with a cage.
The bird escaped from its cage's door.  这只鸟从笼子的门逃走了。
The lion paced back and forth in the cage's confines. 狮子在笼子里来回踱步。
Synonyms: cage (in some contexts)
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, depends on the specific context. Perhaps "freedom," "outside," "open air" could be considered antonyms in certain situations)
