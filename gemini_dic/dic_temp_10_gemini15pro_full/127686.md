negationist:/nɪˈɡeɪʃənɪst/| n. |否认者，否定者|A person who denies the truth or existence of something, especially something generally accepted as historically true.  A person who denies the reality of the Holocaust is a Holocaust negationist.|例句：The negationists claim the moon landing was faked.  那些否认者声称登月是伪造的。

例句：He is a known negationist of climate change. 他是一个知名的气候变化否认者。|近义词：denier, revisionist|反义词：believer, supporter|词性: noun
