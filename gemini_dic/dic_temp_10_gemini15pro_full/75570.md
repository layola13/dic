fucusol:/ˈfjuːkəsɒl/
noun
褐藻醇
An oily, colorless liquid aldehyde with a pungent odor, obtained by distilling certain seaweeds with dilute sulfuric acid.
Fucusol is used in the synthesis of various organic compounds.
