antivenereal:/ˌæntivaˈnɪəriəl/ | adj. | 抗性病的，抗梅毒的 |  Used to describe something that prevents or treats venereal diseases (sexually transmitted infections). |  The doctor prescribed antivenereal medication. 医生开了抗性病的药。|Synonyms: antisyphilitic, anti-STD | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: infection-promoting, disease-causing) | adjective

antivenereal:/ˌæntivaˈnɪəriəl/ | n. | 抗性病药 | A medicine used to treat venereal diseases. |  He took an antivenereal for his infection. 他服用了抗性病的药来治疗感染。| Synonyms:  antisyphilitic drug, anti-STD medication | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- (against) + venereal (relating to sexual intercourse or venereal disease).  The word "venereal" comes from Venus, the Roman goddess of love.
