excavating: /ˈekskəveɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 挖掘，开凿 |  The process of digging or removing earth, rock, or other materials from a site. |  They are excavating the site for the new building. 他们正在为新建筑挖掘场地。| synonyms: digging, unearthing, quarrying | antonyms: burying, filling, covering | verb

excavation: /ˌekskəˈveɪʃn/ | n. | 挖掘，开凿，发掘 | The act or process of excavating something. | The archaeological excavation revealed ancient ruins. 考古发掘揭示了古代遗址。| synonyms: digging, unearthing, quarrying | antonyms: burying, filling, covering | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *excavatus*, past participle of excavare "to hollow out," from ex- "out" + cavare "to hollow."
