cinderellas:/ˌsɪndəˈrɛləz/| n. |灰姑娘们；像灰姑娘一样的人|Refers to people, especially women, who are treated unfairly or overlooked, but who experience a positive change in fortune, often through hard work and resilience, like the fairytale character Cinderella.  It can also be used in a more general sense to refer to anyone who achieves unexpected success or recognition after a period of obscurity or hardship.|The movie features several modern-day Cinderellas who overcome adversity to achieve their dreams.  这部电影讲述了几个现代灰姑娘克服逆境实现梦想的故事。

The team were the Cinderellas of the tournament, going from underdogs to champions. 这支队伍是锦标赛中的灰姑娘，从不被看好到成为冠军。| 近义词:underdogs, dark horses|反义词:favorites, privileged|词性:名词
