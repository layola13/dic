dawish: /ˈdeɪwɪʃ/
adj. | 愚蠢的，傻的 | foolish, silly; lacking good sense or judgment. |  He made a dawish mistake and lost all his money. 他犯了一个愚蠢的错误，把所有的钱都赔光了。| synonyms: foolish, silly, stupid, inane, asinine | antonyms: wise, intelligent, sensible, prudent | 词性: 形容词


dawish: /ˈdeɪwɪʃ/
n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋 | a foolish or stupid person. | Don't be such a dawish! 别这么傻！| synonyms: fool, idiot, simpleton, dunce | antonyms: genius, sage, wise person | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant or dialectal form of "daffy," which itself is likely a shortening of "daffadowndilly," ultimately from Dutch "de affodil." The connection between the flower and foolishness is unclear, but perhaps relates to the perceived silliness of the flower's drooping head.
