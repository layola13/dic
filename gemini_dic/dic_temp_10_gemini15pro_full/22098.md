bologna:/bəˈloʊnjə/ /bəˈlɒɡnə/
noun
博洛尼亚香肠; 大腊肠
a large seasoned sausage that is typically eaten cold
例句：I made a sandwich with bologna. 我用博洛尼亚香肠做了个三明治。

例句：He prefers bologna to salami.  比起意大利腊肠，他更喜欢博洛尼亚香肠。

近义词：baloney, sausage
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a food item.  One could argue perhaps "fresh meat" if considering processed vs. unprocessed.)
词性: noun
