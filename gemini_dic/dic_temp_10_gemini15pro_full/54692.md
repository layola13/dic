disordinately: /ˌdɪsˈɔːrdɪnɪtli/ /ˌdɪsˈɔːrdənɪtli/
| adv. | 过度地，无节制地 | Excessively or without order; to an unusual or excessive degree. | He spends a disproportionately large amount of his income on entertainment. 他把收入中不成比例的很大一部分花在了娱乐上。| She worries disproportionately about her children's safety. 她过度担心孩子们的安全。| 近义词: excessively, immoderately, unduly, inordinately | 反义词: moderately, reasonably, appropriately | 词性: 副词
