antheral: /ænˈθɪrəl/
adjective
花的；雄蕊的；雄性生殖的
Relating to anthers or pollen; relating to the male reproductive organs of a flower.
The antheral parts of the flower produce pollen.  花的雄蕊部分产生花粉。
The botanist studied the antheral development of the orchid.  植物学家研究了兰花的雄蕊发育。
近义词: staminate, masculine
反义词: pistillate, feminine
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *antheralis, from Latin anthera "anther," from Greek anthēra "the anther of a flower, blossom," related to anthos "flower."
