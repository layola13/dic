inship:/ˈɪnʃɪp/ |n.| 在船上; 上船; 登船 | The state or fact of being in a ship or on board a ship. |  The cargo is loaded for inship. 货物已装船。| 近义词: embarkation, aboard | 反义词: disembarkation, ashore | 词性: 名词


词源: "in" + "ship".  "In" indicating location and "ship" referring to a vessel.  It's a straightforward combination indicating presence on a ship.
