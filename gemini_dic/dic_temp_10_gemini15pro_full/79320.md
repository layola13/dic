gleanable:/ˈɡliːnəbl/
adjective
可收集的; 可推断的
That which can be gleaned; obtainable from various sources, often with difficulty.
From the scattered remains of the civilization, archaeologists were able to glean valuable information about their culture.  从文明的零星遗迹中，考古学家能够收集到有关其文化的宝贵信息。
The truth was gleanable from the witness’s fragmented statements. 真相可以从证人支离破碎的陈述中推断出来。
synonyms: collectible, gatherable, obtainable, derivable
antonyms: ungatherable, unobtainable, hidden, obscure
词性: adjective
