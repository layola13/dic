countersuit:/ˈkaʊntərˌsuːt/ /ˈkaʊntəˌsjuːt/
noun
反诉; 反请求;
A countersuit is a lawsuit filed in response to an initial lawsuit.  It is a separate claim brought by the defendant against the plaintiff in the original suit.
The company filed a countersuit against its former employee for breach of contract.  这家公司对其前雇员提起反诉，理由是违反合同。
synonyms: counterclaim, countersue
antonyms: lawsuit, claim
n.
