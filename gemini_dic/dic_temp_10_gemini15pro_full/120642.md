mispracticed: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/
mispracticed| adj.|  操作不当的，失职的，玩忽职守的 | Incorrectly or dishonestly practiced, especially in a profession like medicine or law. | The doctor was accused of mispracticed medicine after several patients suffered complications.  这位医生在几名病人出现并发症后被指控医疗失职。 | Synonyms: malpracticed, bungled, mishandled | Antonyms:  competent, skillful, ethical |词性: 形容词

mispractice: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪs/
mispractice| n. | 失职，玩忽职守，不当操作| Improper or negligent professional activity or treatment, especially by a medical practitioner, lawyer, or public official. |  The lawyer was sued for malpractice.  这位律师因玩忽职守而被起诉。 | Synonyms:  malpractice, negligence, dereliction | Antonyms:  diligence, care, competence |词性: 名词

mispractice: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪs/
mispractice| v. |  玩忽职守，失职 | To engage in improper or negligent professional activity. | The surgeon mispracticed when he left a sponge inside the patient.  外科医生在病人体内留下了一块海绵，这是失职行为。 | Synonyms: bungle, mishandle, botch | Antonyms: perform correctly, handle properly |词性: 动词


词源:  "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "practice"
