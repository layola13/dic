minicyclones:/ˈmɪniˌsaɪkloʊnz/| n. |小型气旋，微型气旋|A small-scale atmospheric vortex, resembling a cyclone but smaller in size and often short-lived. These are often associated with severe thunderstorms and can produce damaging winds. |Minicyclones can form within larger thunderstorms. 小型气旋可以在较大的雷暴中形成。

Minicyclones are often responsible for the most intense wind damage in a thunderstorm. 小型气旋通常是雷暴中最强风灾的罪魁祸首。|近义词：whirlwinds, vortices|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps large-scale cyclones)|词性:noun
