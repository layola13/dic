over-garrisoned:/ˌoʊvərˈɡærɪsənd/
adjective
驻军过多的，兵力过剩的
Having more troops stationed in a place than is necessary or practical.
The island was over-garrisoned, with more soldiers than civilians.  这个岛驻军过多，士兵比平民还多。
The castle, though historically important, was over-garrisoned and a drain on resources. 虽然这座城堡具有重要的历史意义，但驻军过多，消耗了大量资源。
Near synonyms: overmanned, overstaffed
Antonyms: understaffed, undermanned
词性: adjective
