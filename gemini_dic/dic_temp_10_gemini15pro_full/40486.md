contributory:/kənˈtrɪbjəˌtɔːri/ /kənˈtrɪbjəˌtəri/
contributory| adj.| 促成的，起作用的；贡献的；分摊的| playing a part in bringing something about. |例句：Smoking is a contributory factor to lung cancer. 吸烟是导致肺癌的一个因素。|近义词：contributing, causative, instrumental|反义词: preventative, hindering|词性:形容词
contributory:/kənˈtrɪbjəˌtɔːri/ /kənˈtrɪbjəˌtəri/
contributory| n.| 贡献者；促成因素| a person or thing that contributes something, especially money, in order to achieve a particular purpose; a contributory factor. |例句：All contributories to the pension fund benefit. 所有为养老基金捐款的人都将受益。|近义词：contributor, donor, factor|反义词: detractor, opponent|词性:名词
