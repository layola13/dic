inspirative:/ɪnˈspaɪrətɪv/ | adj. | 鼓舞人心的；启发灵感的 |  Tending to inspire or elevate.  |  Her speech was truly inspirative, motivating many to pursue their dreams.  她的演讲 truly 鼓舞人心，激励了许多人去追求他们的梦想。 | 近义词: inspiring, motivational, stimulating | 反义词: uninspiring, demotivating, dull | 词性:形容词

词源: From "inspire" + "-ative." "Inspire" comes from Latin "inspirare," meaning "to breathe or blow into," later figuratively "to influence, excite." "-ative" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "tending to; having the nature or quality of."
