hieroglyphical:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkəl/
adjective
象形文字的；如同象形文字的
Relating to hieroglyphs; resembling hieroglyphs.
The ancient Egyptians used a hieroglyphical writing system. 古埃及人使用象形文字书写系统。
The markings on the cave walls were hieroglyphical in nature.  洞穴墙壁上的标记本质上是象形文字。
近义词:hieroglyphic
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "alphabetic" or "modern")
词性:adjective


hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/
noun
象形文字
A picture or symbol representing a word, syllable, or sound, especially as used in ancient Egyptian writing.
The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。
Many hieroglyphics depict animals or everyday objects. 许多象形文字描绘了动物或日常用品。
近义词:symbol, ideogram
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun
