overabsorb:/ˌoʊvərəbˈzɔːrb/ /ˌəʊvərəbˈsɔːb/| v. |过度吸收；过量吸收|To absorb more of something than is needed or usual. |The company overabsorbed manufacturing overhead costs.  这家公司过度吸收了制造费用。|Synonyms: overestimate, soak up excessively|Antonyms: underabsorb, understate|verb

overabsorbed:/ˌoʊvərəbˈzɔːrbd/ /ˌəʊvərəbˈsɔːbd/| adj. |过度吸收的；过量吸收的|Having absorbed more of something than is needed or usual.  |The market was overabsorbed with cheap imports. 市场过度吸收了廉价进口商品。|Synonyms: saturated, glutted|Antonyms: undersupplied, deficient|adjective
