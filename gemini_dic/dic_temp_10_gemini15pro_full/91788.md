hyperexcitement:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtmənt/| n. |过度兴奋，极度兴奋|A state of extreme excitement.  It's characterized by heightened emotional responses, increased energy levels, and often, difficulty focusing.|例句：The child's hyperexcitement on Christmas morning was contagious.  圣诞节早晨，孩子的过度兴奋很有感染力。

例句：Her hyperexcitement about the concert was evident. 她对演唱会的极度兴奋显而易见。|近义词：overexcitement, exhilaration, frenzy|反义词:calmness, tranquility, apathy|词性:noun
Etymology: From hyper- "over, excessive" + excitement.  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" meaning "over, beyond."
