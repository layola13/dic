mised: /maɪzd/
Verb
mixed (past tense of mix) 混合（mix的过去式）
He mised the ingredients together. 他把配料混合在一起。
Synonyms: combined, blended, mingled
Antonyms: separated, divided
词性: 动词

mix: /mɪks/
Verb
混合
Mix the flour and water together. 将面粉和水混合在一起。
Synonyms: combine, blend, mingle, stir
Antonyms: separate, divide
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *miscian (attested only in mischen "dung-heap"), from Proto-Germanic *miskjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)mik- "to mix". Cognate with Old Frisian miska, Middle Dutch misken, German mischen.
