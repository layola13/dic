nonvarieties:/ˌnɒnvəˈraɪətiz/ | n. | 非变体，非品种 |  Things that are not varieties; typically used in a scientific or technical context to refer to entities that don't exhibit significant variation within a group. | The botanist disregarded the nonvarieties in his study, focusing only on the distinct variations within the species.  植物学家在他的研究中忽略了非变体，只关注物种内的明显变异。| synonyms: uniformities, standard forms | antonyms: varieties, variations | noun
