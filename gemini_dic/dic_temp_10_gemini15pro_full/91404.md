hydroxylated:/haɪˈdrɒksɪleɪtɪd/
adjective
羟基化的
Containing a hydroxyl group, formed by the introduction of a hydroxyl group into a compound.
The hydroxylated form of vitamin D is more readily absorbed by the body.  维生素D的羟基化形式更容易被人体吸收。

The researchers studied the effects of the hydroxylated compound on cell growth. 研究人员研究了羟基化合物对细胞生长的影响。
