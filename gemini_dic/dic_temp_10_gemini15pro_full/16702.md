barriers:/ˈbæriərz/ (US), /ˈbæriəz/ (UK)| n.|障碍; 阻碍; 隔阂|Something that prevents or makes it difficult for something to happen or for someone to go somewhere.|High import barriers protect domestic producers.  高进口壁垒保护了国内生产商。|Synonyms: obstacle, impediment, obstruction, hurdle|Antonyms: aid, assistance, help|Noun

barriers:/ˈbæriərz/ (US), /ˈbæriəz/ (UK)| n.|栅栏; 屏障|A structure or object that prevents people or things from passing from one place to another.|The police put up barriers to control the crowd. 警方设置了路障来控制人群。|Synonyms: fence, barricade, wall|Antonyms: opening, passage|Noun
