-ing:/ɪŋ/ | suffix | 进行时态/动名词 |  Used to form the present participle and gerund of verbs. | She is singing. (她正在唱歌。) |  |  | suffix
-ing:/ɪŋ/ | n. | 动名词 |  A verb form that functions as a noun. | Swimming is my favorite exercise. (游泳是我最喜欢的运动。) |  |  | noun

-ing:/ɪŋ/ | adj. | 现在分词 | A verb form that functions as an adjective. | The rising sun was beautiful. (冉冉升起的太阳很美。) |  |  | adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I enjoy reading books. = 我喜欢读书。
He often goes swimming in the summer. = 他夏天经常去游泳。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is playing the piano now. = 她现在正在弹钢琴。
They are watching a movie. = 他们在看电影。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have finished reading that book. = 我已经读完了那本书。
He has been practicing singing for years. = 他已经练习唱歌很多年了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been studying English for three years. = 她已经学习英语三年了。
They have been playing basketball all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在打篮球。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I went swimming yesterday. = 我昨天去游泳了。
She enjoyed reading the book. = 她喜欢读那本书。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was watching TV when she called. = 她打电话时我在看电视。
They were playing games in the park. = 他们在公园里玩游戏。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had finished reading the book before he arrived. = 在他到达之前，我已经读完了这本书。
She had been practicing singing for years before the concert. = 在音乐会之前，她已经练习唱歌很多年了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been working on this project for weeks before the deadline. = 在截止日期前，我已经在这个项目上工作了几个星期。
They had been playing basketball for hours before the rain started. = 在下雨之前，他们已经打了好几个小时的篮球。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will go swimming tomorrow. = 我明天会去游泳。
She will be reading a new book soon. = 她很快就会读一本新书。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be watching TV this time tomorrow. = 明天这个时候我会在看电视。
They will be playing games at the party. = 他们在派对上会玩游戏。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished reading the book by next week. = 到下周我将会读完这本书。
She will have been practicing singing for ten years by then. = 到那时她将已经练习唱歌十年了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been living here for five years by next month. = 到下个月我将已经在这里住了五年了。
She will have been studying French for two years by the end of this semester. = 到这学期末，她将已经学习法语两年了。
