abnegation: /ˌæbnɪˈɡeɪʃən/
noun
放弃；克制；拒绝
the act of renouncing or rejecting something.
She made an abnegation of her right to the throne. 她放弃了她的王位继承权。

Her life was one of quiet abnegation. 她的一生是默默无闻的克己奉献。

近义词: renunciation, rejection, denial, sacrifice, self-denial, forgoing, relinquishment
反义词: acceptance, indulgence, assertion
词性: noun
