nondeleterious:/ˌnɒndɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/
adjective
无害的，非有害的
Not harmful or injurious; harmless.
This genetic mutation is nondeleterious and does not affect the individual's health.  这种基因突变是无害的，不会影响个体的健康。
The environmental impact of this project is considered nondeleterious. 这个项目对环境的影响被认为是无害的。
近义词:harmless, innocuous, benign, safe
反义词:deleterious, harmful, injurious, damaging
词性:adjective
词源:non- (not) + deleterious (harmful)
