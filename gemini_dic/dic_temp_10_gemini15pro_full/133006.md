nonenlightening:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈlaɪtnɪŋ/
adjective
无启发性的，没有教益的
Providing no new information or insight; uninformative.
The lecture was nonenlightening and left the audience confused. 这场讲座毫无启发性，让听众感到困惑。
The meeting was a nonenlightening waste of time.  这次会议毫无意义，纯属浪费时间。
Synonyms: uninformative, unhelpful, useless, pointless, unproductive
Antonyms: informative, insightful, helpful, useful, productive
词性: adjective
