imod:/ˈaɪmɒd/ (UK) /ˈaɪmɑːd/ (US)| n. | 模块; 模组 | A separate unit of software or hardware that can be added to a larger system to provide extra functionality. | Example:  The programmer developed a new imod for the game.  程序员为游戏开发了一个新的模组。

Example: Users can customize their experience by installing different imods. 用户可以通过安装不同的模组来自定义他们的体验。 | Synonyms: module, plugin, add-on, extension | Antonyms: core, base, foundation | Noun
