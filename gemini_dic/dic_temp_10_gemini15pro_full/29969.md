caviar:/ˈkæviɑːr/ (US) /ˈkævɪɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |鱼子酱|Salted roe of sturgeon or other large fish, eaten as a delicacy.|例句：They served caviar and champagne at the reception.  他们在招待会上提供了鱼子酱和香槟。

例句：She loves the taste of caviar. 她喜欢鱼子酱的味道。

近义词：roe, delicacy|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "common food")|词性:noun
