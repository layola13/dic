ob-gyns:/ˌɒbˈɡaɪnz/ /ˌɑːbˈɡaɪnz/
noun
妇产科医生
Short for obstetrician-gynecologists, doctors specializing in women's reproductive health, pregnancy, and childbirth.
My sister sees her ob-gyn every year for a checkup. 我的妹妹每年都去看她的妇产科医生做检查。
Synonyms: OB/GYN, obstetrician-gynecologist
Antonyms: N/A
