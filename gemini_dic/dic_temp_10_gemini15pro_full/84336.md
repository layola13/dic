halfhearted:/ˌhɑːfˈhɑːrtɪd/ | adj. | 不热心的，敷衍的，缺乏热情的 | Showing little enthusiasm or effort; lacking conviction. |  He made a halfhearted attempt to clean his room. 他敷衍地尝试打扫他的房间。

She gave a halfhearted wave goodbye. 她漫不经心地挥了挥手告别。


近义词: lukewarm, apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 反义词: enthusiastic, wholehearted, eager, passionate | 词性: adjective
