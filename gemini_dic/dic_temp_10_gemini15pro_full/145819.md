overdefensively:/ˌoʊvərdiˈfɛnsɪvli/| adv.|过度防御地，过分戒备地|In a manner characterized by excessive defensiveness; reacting too strongly or too quickly to perceived threats or criticism.|He responded overdefensively to the accusations, denying everything vehemently. 他对这些指控反应过度，强烈地否认了一切。|例句：She spoke overdefensively, as if anticipating criticism. 她说话的语气过分戒备，好像预料到会被批评。

例句：He acted overdefensively when questioned about his past. 当被问及他的过去时，他表现得过分防御。|近义词：guardedly, warily, apprehensively|反义词：openly, confidently, trustingly|词性:副词
