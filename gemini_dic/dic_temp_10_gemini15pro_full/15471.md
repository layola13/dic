badger:/ˈbædʒər/ (US), /ˈbædʒə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|纠缠，烦扰；獾|To persistently bother or annoy someone; A stout, burrowing mammal.|
He badgered his parents until they let him go to the concert. 他一直纠缠他的父母，直到他们让他去听音乐会。|
The European badger is a nocturnal animal. 欧洲獾是一种夜行动物。|
近义词：harass, pester, annoy, bother, hound|
反义词：soothe, appease, comfort|
词性: verb, noun


词源:
From Middle English *baggere, probably from badge + -er, referring to the white markings on a badger's face, likened to a beggar's badge.
