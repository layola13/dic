elops:/ˈiːlɒps/ (US) /ˈɛlɒps/ (UK)| n.|鳀科鱼类; 海鲢|A type of fish belonging to the family Elopidae, commonly known as ladyfish, tenpounder, or skipjack. They are silvery, fast-swimming marine fish found in tropical and subtropical waters.|例句：The elops jumped out of the water, flashing its silver scales. 海鲢跃出水面，银色的鳞片闪闪发光。
