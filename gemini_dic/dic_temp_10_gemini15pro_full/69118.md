favicon:/ˈfeɪvɪkɒn/ (US) /ˈfeɪvɪkən/ (UK)| n. |网站图标，收藏夹图标|A small icon associated with a particular website, typically displayed in a user's web browser's address bar or next to the site's name in a list of bookmarks.|Example: The favicon for Google is a colorful "G". 谷歌的网站图标是一个彩色的“G”。|Synonym: website icon, bookmark icon|Antonym: N/A|Noun
