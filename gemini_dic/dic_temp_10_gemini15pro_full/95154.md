inarch:/ˈɪnɑːrtʃ/| n. v.|压条; 靠接;|The process of grafting one plant to another by bending a branch or stem of the first plant and inserting it into the bark of the second plant, where it unites and grows.|例句：The gardener inarched the branches of the apple tree to create a new sapling. 园丁将苹果树的树枝压条，以培育新的树苗。

例句：Inarching is a common technique for propagating fruit trees. 压条是繁殖果树的常用技术。|近义词：graft, layer|反义词:|n. v.
