ludicrousness:/ˈluːdɪkrəsnəs/| n. | 荒谬；滑稽；可笑 |The ludicrousness of the situation was apparent to everyone.  这种情况的荒谬性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。| The sheer ludicrousness of his excuse made me laugh. 他那荒唐的借口让我笑了出来。| 近义词：absurdity, ridiculousness, preposterousness, folly | 反义词：seriousness, sensibleness, rationality | 词性: 名词

ludicrous:/ˈluːdɪkrəs/| adj. | 荒谬的；滑稽的；可笑的 |It's ludicrous to think that anyone would believe such a story.  认为有人会相信这样一个故事是荒谬的。|  Wearing a swimsuit to a funeral is ludicrous. 穿着泳衣参加葬礼是很荒唐的。| 近义词：absurd, ridiculous, preposterous, farcical | 反义词：serious, sensible, rational | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin *lūdicrus (“sportive, playful”), from lūdus (“play, game, sport”).
