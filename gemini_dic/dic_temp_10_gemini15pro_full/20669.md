blackthorn:/ˈblækθɔːrn/ | n. | 黑刺李; 黑刺; 棘手的难题 | A thorny shrub that bears small, dark purple fruits, often used for hedges.  Also metaphorically refers to a difficult or thorny problem. | The path was blocked by a thick blackthorn.  这条路被茂密的黑刺李挡住了。

The border dispute remained a blackthorn between the two countries. 边境争端仍然是两国之间的一个棘手问题。| 近义词：bramble, thornbush | 反义词: (no direct antonym, possibly something smooth or easy) | noun


blackthorns:/ˈblækθɔːrnz/ | n. | 黑刺李（复数）; 棘手的难题（复数） | Plural of blackthorn.  Refers to multiple blackthorn shrubs or multiple difficult problems. | The blackthorns along the fence provided good nesting sites for birds. 篱笆上的黑刺李为鸟类提供了良好的筑巢地点。

Negotiations stalled as they faced numerous blackthorns in the agreement.  谈判陷入僵局，因为他们在协议中面临许多棘手的问题。| 近义词：brambles, thornbushes | 反义词: (no direct antonym) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blæcþorn, from blæc "black" + þorn "thorn".  Literally, "black thorn," referring to the dark color of the bark.
