olympus: /əˈlɪmpəs/ | n. |奥林匹斯山，奥林匹斯，(喻)众神之山，仙境| Mount Olympus is the highest mountain in Greece and in Greek mythology, it was considered the home of the gods. |例句：The climbers finally reached the summit of Olympus. 登山者最终到达了奥林匹斯山的顶峰。

例句：He lives in a photographic Olympus, surrounded by the best equipment. 他生活在一个摄影的仙境中，周围都是最好的设备。 |近义词：heaven, paradise, abode of the gods |反义词: hell, underworld |词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *Olympus*, from Ancient Greek *Ὄλυμπος (Ólympos)*.  The etymology is uncertain, possibly pre-Greek.
