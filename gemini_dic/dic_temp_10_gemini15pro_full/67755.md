eyebrowless:/ˈaɪbraʊləs/ | adj. | 没有眉毛的 | Lacking eyebrows; having no eyebrows. |  The eyebrowless model had a unique look.  没有眉毛的模特看起来很独特。| 近义词: hairless (in the eyebrow area) | 反义词: bushy-browed, thick-browed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "eyebrow" + "-less"  "eyebrow" 源于古英语 "ēagebrū" (eye-brow). "-less" 是一个古英语后缀，表示 "without" or "lacking".
