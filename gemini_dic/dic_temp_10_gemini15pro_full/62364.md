endite:/ɪnˈdaɪt/
verb
创作；写作；赋诗
to write; compose, especially in a literary style
He endited a letter to the editor. 他给编辑写了一封信。
近义词:compose, write, pen, author, draft
反义词:erase, delete
词性:v.

词源:
From Old French *enditer, from en- (“in, into”) + dite (“dictated, said”), ultimately from Latin dictāre (“to say often, prescribe”).
