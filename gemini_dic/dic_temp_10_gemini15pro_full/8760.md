antidomestic:/ˌænti dəˈmɛstɪk/ | adj. | 反家庭的，反对家庭生活的 | Opposed to traditional domestic life or values, often associated with a rejection of traditional gender roles and a preference for life outside the home. |  She had a fiercely antidomestic streak and chafed at the idea of settling down. 她有一种强烈地反感家庭生活的倾向，对安定的想法感到恼火。 |  He led a rather antidomestic existence, preferring to eat in restaurants and avoid household chores. 他过着一种相当反家庭的生活，喜欢在餐馆吃饭，避免做家务。 | 近义词: unconventional, non-traditional, bohemian | 反义词: domestic, traditional, home-loving | 词性: adjective
