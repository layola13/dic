overinclines:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnz/ | v. | (使)过度倾斜，(使)过分倾向 | To incline or cause to incline too much or too far; to lean or cause to lean excessively. | The architect realized he had overinclined the roof and it would need to be adjusted. 建筑师意识到他把屋顶设计得过于倾斜，需要调整。

The heavy load overinclined the truck, causing it to tip over.  沉重的货物使卡车过度倾斜，导致其翻倒。 | Near Synonyms: tilt, tip, lean, list, cant | Near Antonyms: straighten, right, level | Verb
