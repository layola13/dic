out-cold:/aʊtˈkoʊld/ | adj. | 昏迷不醒的，不省人事的 | Unconscious; deeply asleep.  Derived from "out" meaning unconscious and "cold" emphasizing the deep state of unconsciousness, similar to the coldness of a body in a deep sleep or faint.  | He was knocked out-cold in the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛中被打得不省人事。 |  knocked out, unconscious, insensible | conscious, awake | adjective
