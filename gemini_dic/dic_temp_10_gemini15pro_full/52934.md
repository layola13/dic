dinar:/ˈdiːnɑːr/ /daɪˈnɑːr/| n.|第纳尔（货币单位）|A monetary unit of several countries, especially those of Arab origin.  Derived from the Roman denarius.|例句：The dinar is the official currency of Algeria. 第纳尔是阿尔及利亚的官方货币。

例句：Kuwait's currency is the Kuwaiti dinar. 科威特的货币是科威特第纳尔。|近义词：currency, money|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of currency)|词性:noun
