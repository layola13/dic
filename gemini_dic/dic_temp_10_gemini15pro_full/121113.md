misdate:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪt/ | v. n. | 写错日期，日期错误 | To put the wrong date on something; a date that is wrong. |

例句：
I misdated the check and had to write a new one.
我把支票上的日期写错了，不得不重写一张。

The contract was misdated, causing some confusion.
合同日期写错了，引起了一些混乱。


近义词：backdate, postdate | 反义词：date correctly | 词性：verb, noun


misdate:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪt/ | n. | 写错日期，日期错误 | A date that is wrong. |

例句：
The misdate on the invoice led to a billing error.
发票上的日期错误导致了账单错误。

Because of the misdate, the document was invalid.
由于日期错误，该文件无效。


近义词：incorrect date, wrong date | 反义词：correct date | 词性：noun
