overtediously:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːdiəsli/
| adv. |冗长乏味地，沉闷地|In a manner that is excessively tedious and boring; to a degree that causes weariness or impatience.|He explained the process overtediously, losing the attention of his audience. 他解释这个过程冗长乏味，失去了听众的注意力。|The lecturer spoke overtediously for two hours, causing many students to doze off. 演讲者沉闷地讲了两个小时，导致许多学生打瞌睡。|近义词：tediously, boringly, monotonously, dully|反义词：interestingly, engagingly, stimulatingly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "over" (excessively) + "tedious" (boring, tiresome) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Tedious" comes from the Latin "taedium" meaning weariness or disgust.
