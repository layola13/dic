elders:/ˈeldərz/| n.|长者，前辈，老年人|People often seek advice from their elders. 人们经常向长辈寻求建议。|The village elders met to discuss the matter. 村里的长者们开会讨论这件事。|近义词：seniors, older people|反义词:youngsters, juniors|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "eldra" (comparative adjective meaning "older"), from Proto-Germanic "*aldizô", ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*al-" (to grow, nourish). The "-s" ending marks the plural form.
