freezability:/ˌfriːzəˈbɪləti/
n.
可冷冻性，冷冻能力
The freezability of food depends on its water content.
食物的冷冻能力取决于其含水量。
freezability is the ability of something to be frozen.
可冷冻性是指某物被冷冻的能力。
例句：
The freezability of the strawberries was tested.
对草莓的冷冻能力进行了测试。

The freezability of different types of meat varies.
不同类型肉类的冷冻能力各不相同。

近义词：freezing capacity, freezing potential
反义词：unfreezability (非正式)

词源：
From freeze + -ability.  "Freeze" comes from Old English *frēosan, from Proto-Germanic *freusaną. "-ability" comes from Middle English -abilite, from Old French -ableté, from Latin -abilitās, from -abilis.
