demos:/ˈdɛmoʊz/ (US) /ˈdɛməʊz/ (UK)| n.|演示，示范; (尤指新产品或新系统的)演示，示范；样本唱片；示威游行|A demonstration of a new product. 一个新产品的演示。|例句：The software company gave a demo of their new program.  这家软件公司演示了他们的新程序。

例句：Record companies often send out demos to radio stations. 唱片公司经常向广播电台发送样本唱片。|近义词：presentation, exhibition, display, trial, sample, march, protest|反义词:concealment|noun
