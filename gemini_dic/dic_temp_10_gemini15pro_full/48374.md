decury: /ˈdɛkjʊri/ | n. | 十人组，十户联保 | A group of ten, especially as an administrative unit in ancient Rome or medieval England.  A decury was responsible for maintaining order and collecting taxes within its area. |  In medieval England, the decury system was used to enforce law and order. 在中世纪的英格兰，十户联保制被用来维持法律和秩序。| synonym: tithing, group of ten | antonym: N/A | noun
