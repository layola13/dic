ordained:/ɔrˈdeɪnd/ |v. |任命；授予圣职|To officially appoint someone to a religious post, such as a priest or minister; to destine or predestine. |He was ordained as a priest in 1985. 他于1985年被任命为牧师。| The king ordained that the castle be built. 国王下令建造城堡。|Synonyms: appointed, commissioned, consecrated, decreed, destined|Antonyms: dismissed, discharged|verb

ordained:/ɔrˈdeɪnd/ |adj. |被任命的；注定的|Officially appointed to a religious post; destined or predestined. |He is an ordained minister. 他是一位被任命的牧师。|Her ordained path was to become a doctor. 她注定要成为一名医生。|Synonyms: appointed, designated, predestined|Antonyms: unappointed, undesignated|adjective
