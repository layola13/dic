imagine:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ (US) /ɪˈmædʒɪn/ (UK)| v. |想象，设想|To form a mental image or concept of something.|I can't imagine life without you. 我无法想象没有你的生活。|
imagine:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ (US) /ɪˈmædʒɪn/ (UK)| v. |认为，猜想|To believe or suppose something.|I imagine they'll be arriving soon. 我想他们很快就会到了。|
imagine:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ (US) /ɪˈmædʒɪn/ (UK)| v. |料想|To expect or anticipate something.|I didn't imagine it would be so difficult. 我没想到会这么难。|

近义词: visualize, envision, conceive, think, suppose, guess, presume
反义词:  disbelieve, doubt
词性: verb

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
