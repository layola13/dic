overassert:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrt/| v. |过度断言，过分自信地声明|To assert something too strongly or too frequently, often to the point of arrogance or aggression.|He overasserted his authority and alienated his colleagues. 他过度维护自己的权威，疏远了他的同事。|例句：She overasserted her opinions in the meeting, making others feel unheard. 她在会议上过分强调自己的观点，让其他人觉得没有被倾听。|近义词：overstate, exaggerate, assert aggressively|反义词:understate, downplay|词性:动词

overassert:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrt/| v. |过度断言，过分自信地声明|To state something too confidently or forcefully, often inappropriately.|He tended to overassert his knowledge of the subject. 他倾向于过分强调自己对这个主题的了解。|例句：By overasserting his innocence, he actually made himself look more suspicious.  由于过分强调自己的清白，他实际上让自己看起来更可疑。|近义词：overemphasize, overstress|反义词: downplay, be modest|词性:动词
