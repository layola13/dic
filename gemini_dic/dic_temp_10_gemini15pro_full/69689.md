fenestrated:/ˈfɛnɪstreɪtɪd/
adjective
有窗的，穿孔的
Having openings or windows.  Resembling a window or having numerous perforations. Characterized by fenestrations (window-like openings).
The building's fenestrated facade allowed plenty of natural light to enter.  这座建筑有窗的外立面允许充足的自然光进入。

The surgeon performed a fenestrated septoplasty to repair the patient's deviated septum.  外科医生进行了开窗式鼻中隔成形术来修复病人偏曲的鼻中隔。

Synonym: perforated, pierced, windowed
Antonym: solid, closed, unperforated
词性: adjective
