ha'p'orth:/ˈheɪpəθ/ /ˈheɪpwərθ/| n. |少量，一点点，很少的钱|A small amount of something, especially something inexpensive.  Originates from "halfpennyworth," meaning the amount you could buy for half a penny.| I'll just have a ha'p'orth of milk, please. 我只要一点点牛奶。|近义词：bit, little, smidgen, trifle |反义词: lot, much| noun
