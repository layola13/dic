hyperconstitutionally:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/ | adv. | 超宪法地，违宪地 |  In a manner that exceeds or violates the limits of a constitution.  It describes actions or laws that go beyond what is permitted by a constitution. | The court ruled that the law was hyperconstitutionally enacted, exceeding the legislature's authority.  法院裁定该法律的制定超出了宪法规定，超越了立法机关的权限。

The president's actions were deemed hyperconstitutionally intrusive by the opposition. 总统的行为被反对派认为是违宪的侵犯。 | 近义词: unconstitutionally | 反义词: constitutionally | 词性: 副词
