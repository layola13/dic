cubature: /ˈkjuːbətʃər/ | n. | 体积;求积法;立方容积 | The process of determining the volume of a solid figure, especially by dividing it into infinitesimal elements and summing their volumes.  |  The cubature of the irregularly shaped container was a complex mathematical problem.  不规则形状容器的体积计算是一个复杂的数学问题。| synonyms:  volume measurement, mensuration | antonyms: N/A | noun

cubature: /ˈkjuːbətʃər/ | n. | 体积;求积法;立方容积 | The result of determining the volume of a solid figure. | The cubature of the tank was 1000 cubic meters.  油箱的容积是1000立方米。| synonyms: volume, capacity | antonyms: N/A | noun
