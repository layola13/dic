miasmatic:/maɪˈæzmætɪk/ | adj. | 瘴气的；污浊的；令人不快的 | Relating to or resembling miasma, a noxious or poisonous atmosphere formerly thought to rise from swamps and putrid matter and cause disease. | The miasmatic swamp gave off a foul odor. 瘴气弥漫的沼泽散发出一股恶臭。 | The air in the dungeon was thick and miasmatic. 地牢里的空气又浓又浊，令人作呕。 |Synonyms: noxious, pestilential, foul, fetid, putrid, toxic, unhealthy, unwholesome | Antonyms: healthy, wholesome, pure, fresh, clean | Adjective
