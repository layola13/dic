cross-boundary:/ˌkrɒsˈbaʊndri/ /ˌkrɑːsˈbaʊndri/| adj.|跨界的，跨部门的|Involving or relating to different groups, departments, or areas, especially in a way that is new and exciting.|The company is encouraging cross-boundary collaboration between departments to foster innovation.|近义词：interdisciplinary, transboundary, interdepartmental|反义词:intra-boundary, within-boundary|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"cross-" (prefix) from Old English "cros" and Old Norse "kross," meaning "cross, crucifix."  Indicates a crossing or intersection.
"-boundary" from Middle English "bounde," meaning "limit, border," and "-ary," a suffix forming adjectives from nouns.
