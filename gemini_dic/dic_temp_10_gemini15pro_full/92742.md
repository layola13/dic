ichthyocoprolite:/ˌɪkθiəʊˈkɒprəlaɪt/
n.
鱼粪石，鱼类粪便化石
fossilized fish excrement.  It provides valuable insights into the diet and environment of ancient fish.
Example: The ichthyocoprolite revealed the fish had been feeding on small crustaceans. 这块鱼粪石表明这条鱼以小型甲壳类动物为食。
Example:  Researchers discovered an abundance of ichthyocoprolites in the sedimentary layer. 研究人员在沉积层中发现了大量的鱼粪石。
Synonym: coprolite
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "living fish excrement")
noun
