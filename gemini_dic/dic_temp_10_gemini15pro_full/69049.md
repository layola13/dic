faucial:/ˈfɔːʃəl/| adj.|咽门的，喉的|Of or relating to the fauces (the passage from the back of the mouth to the pharynx).|The doctor examined the patient's faucial area. 医生检查了病人的咽喉部位。|Synonyms: pharyngeal, guttural|Antonyms: None|Adjective


fauces:/ˈfɔːsiːz/| n.|咽门，喉|The passage from the back of the mouth to the pharynx, bounded by the soft palate, the base of the tongue, and the palatine arches.|Inflammation of the fauces can cause difficulty swallowing. 咽喉发炎会导致吞咽困难。|Synonyms: throat, pharynx|Antonyms: None|Noun
