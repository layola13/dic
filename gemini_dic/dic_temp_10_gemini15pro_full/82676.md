guesstimator: /ˈɡɛstɪmeɪtər/ | n. | 估算者，粗略估计者 | A person who makes guesstimates. Someone who estimates something based on incomplete information or using guesswork. | The guesstimator reckoned we'd need about $10,000 for the project.  估算者认为我们这个项目大约需要1万美元。

The contractor acted as a guesstimator for the renovation costs. 承包商对翻新成本进行了粗略估算。| 近义词: estimator, approximator | 反义词: calculator, measurer | 词性: 名词
