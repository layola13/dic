multicellular:/ˌmʌltiˈsɛljʊlər/
adjective
多细胞的
Composed of multiple cells, as opposed to unicellular.
例句：Humans are multicellular organisms.  人类是多细胞生物。

例句：Multicellular organisms exhibit greater complexity than unicellular ones. 多细胞生物比单细胞生物表现出更大的复杂性。

近义词：pluricellular
反义词：unicellular
词性：adjective


Etymology:
multi- (prefix):  many, multiple (from Latin multus "much")
-cellular (suffix): relating to cells
