outlengthened:/ˌaʊtˈlɛŋθənd/
verb
超过…的长度
surpassed in length; made longer than.
Her reign outlengthened that of all previous monarchs. 她的统治超过了所有前任君主的统治时间。
Synonyms: surpassed, exceeded, outstripped
Antonyms: shortened, curtailed
词性: 动词
