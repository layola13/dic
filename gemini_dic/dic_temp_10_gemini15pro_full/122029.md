missuit:/ˌmɪsˈsuːt/
verb
不适合，不相配
to be unsuitable for; to clash with
That dress missuits her. 那件衣服不适合她。
近义词: clash, not suit, be inappropriate for
反义词: suit, fit, be appropriate for
词性: verb

missuit:/ˌmɪsˈsuːt/
noun
不合适的衣服，不协调的事物
something that is unsuitable or not in harmony
The bright pink hat was a total missuit with her elegant dress.  那顶亮粉色的帽子和她优雅的连衣裙完全不搭。
近义词: mismatch, incongruity
反义词: match, suitability
词性: noun


词源:  From mis- (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + suit (meaning "to be appropriate for").
