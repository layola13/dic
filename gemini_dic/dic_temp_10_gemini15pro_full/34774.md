climactically:/klaɪˈmæktɪkli/
adverb|高潮地；戏剧性地|At the very peak of intensity or excitement; in a way that resembles a climax.|The movie ended climactically with a dramatic chase scene. 这部电影以一场扣人心弦的追逐戏高潮地结束。|
climactically:/klaɪˈmæktɪkli/
近义词: dramatically, decisively, crucially, significantly, at the height, at the peak
climactically:/klaɪˈmæktɪkli/
反义词: anticlimactically, undramatically, insignificantly, trivially
climactically:/klaɪˈmæktɪkli/
词性:副词
