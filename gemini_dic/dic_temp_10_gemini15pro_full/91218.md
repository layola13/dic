hydrobranchiata:/ˌhaɪdrəbræŋkiˈeɪtə/
| n. |水鳃类动物|Aquatic animals that breathe through gills.|Hydrobranchiata, a group including crustaceans and other gill-breathing aquatic animals, was once considered a taxonomic class. 水鳃类动物，包括甲壳类动物和其他用鳃呼吸的水生动物，曾被认为是一个分类学类别。|No longer a valid taxonomic group, the term "Hydrobranchiata" is rarely used in modern scientific literature.  “水鳃类动物”这个术语已不再是一个有效的分类群，在现代科学文献中很少使用。|近义词:crustaceans, aquatic animals|反义词:terrestrial animals|词性:noun

词源:  From Greek "hydro" (water) + "branchia" (gills) + Latin suffix "-ata" (forming a plural noun).
