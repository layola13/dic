antifoam:/ˈæntiˌfoʊm/ | n. | 消泡剂，抑泡剂 | A substance that reduces or prevents the formation of foam. |  The paint manufacturer added antifoam to the mixture to prevent bubbling.  油漆制造商在混合物中添加了消泡剂以防止起泡。

Antifoam agents are crucial in many industrial processes. 消泡剂在许多工业过程中至关重要。| 近义词: defoamer, foam inhibitor | 反义词: foaming agent | 词性: noun

Etymology: anti- (prefix meaning "against") + foam (noun)
