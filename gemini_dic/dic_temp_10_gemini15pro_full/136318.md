nonrequisite:/ˌnɑːnˈrɛkwɪzɪt/
adjective
非必要的，非强制的
Not required; optional.
Students can choose from a range of nonrequisite courses to broaden their education. 学生可以选择一系列非必要的课程来拓宽他们的教育。
This information is nonrequisite for completing the application.  这项信息对于完成申请是非必要的。
近义词:optional, elective, non-essential, unnecessary
反义词:required, mandatory, compulsory, essential
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + requisite.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "requisite" comes from the Latin word "requisitus," the past participle of "requirere," meaning "to require" or "to demand."
