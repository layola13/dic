miseased:/mɪsˈiːzd/
adjective
患病的；不健康的
troubled, as with a disease or illness; exhibiting symptoms of disease.
The miseased dog was taken to the vet.  这只患病的狗被送到了兽医那里。
近义词:sick, ill, diseased, ailing
反义词:healthy, well, robust
词性:adjective

词源:
From misease (n.) + -ed. Misease itself comes from Middle English misese, from Old French mesaise "discomfort, uneasiness," from mes- "wrongly" + aise "ease."
