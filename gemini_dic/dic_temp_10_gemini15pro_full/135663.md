nonpreferentialism:/ˌnɒnprɛfərˈɛnʃəlɪzəm/
|n.|非优惠主义|The policy or practice of not granting preferential treatment to any particular country or group of countries in trade or other matters.  It emphasizes equal treatment for all parties involved.|
|Example 1: The country's shift towards nonpreferentialism in its trade policies has raised concerns among its traditional allies. 这个国家贸易政策转向非优惠主义的做法引起了其传统盟友的担忧。

Example 2:  Nonpreferentialism can be seen as a way to promote fair competition on the global stage. 非优惠主义可以被视为促进全球舞台上公平竞争的一种方式。|
|Synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, egalitarianism|
|Antonyms: preferentialism, favoritism, discrimination|
|noun|
