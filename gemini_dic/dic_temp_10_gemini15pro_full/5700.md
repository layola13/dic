aluminous:/əˈluːmɪnəs/
adjective
含铝的；发光的
Containing aluminum or alumina; shining brightly.
The aluminous soil is good for growing tea. 这种含铝的土壤适合种茶。
The moon cast an aluminous glow on the water. 月光在水面上投下明亮的光芒。

近义词:bright, shining, radiant, gleaming
反义词:dark, dim, dull
词性:adjective
