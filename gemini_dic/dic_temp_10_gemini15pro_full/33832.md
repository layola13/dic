circumlocution:/ˌsɜːrkəmloʊˈkjuːʃən/| n. |迂回说法，冗长绕圈子的说法|The use of many words where fewer would do, especially in a deliberate attempt to be vague or evasive.|He used a circumlocution to avoid answering the question directly. 他拐弯抹角地避免直接回答问题。|Synonyms: periphrasis, indirectness, verbosity, wordiness|Antonyms: directness, conciseness, brevity|noun
