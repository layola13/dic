etherealizes:/ɪˈθɪrɪəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使空灵化；使超凡脱俗；使精神化
To make something ethereal; to give something a delicate and unearthly quality.
The music etherealized the atmosphere, transporting us to another realm.  音乐使气氛变得空灵，将我们带到了另一个境界。
The artist's brushstrokes etherealized the landscape, making it seem like a dream.  艺术家的笔触使风景空灵化，使其看起来像梦境一般。
近义词: spiritualize, refine, sublimate, rarify
反义词: materialize, coarsen, solidify
词性: verb
