bombazine:/ˌbɒmbəˈziːn/ /ˌbɑːmbəˈziːn/
noun
薄哔叽，洋绉
A twilled fabric, typically black, of silk and worsted, or of cotton and worsted, used for mourning clothes.

She wore a severe bombazine dress to the funeral. 她穿着严肃的洋绉连衣裙参加了葬礼。
