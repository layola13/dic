eyeball:/ˈaɪbɔːl/| n. vt.|眼球，注视，粗略估计|The doctor examined her eyeball. 医生检查了她的眼球。|He eyeballed the distance across the room. 他目测了一下房间的宽度。|近义词：eye, gaze, stare, estimate, assess|反义词：(没有直接的反义词)|noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From eye + ball.  The word "eye" comes from Old English ēage, from Proto-Germanic *augō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ōkʷ-. "Ball" comes from Old Norse bollr, meaning "round object," from Proto-Germanic *balluz.
