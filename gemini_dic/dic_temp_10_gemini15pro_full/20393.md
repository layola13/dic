bitchiest: /ˈbɪtʃiɪst/ | adj. | 最恶毒的，最刻薄的 | Superlative form of bitchy:  most unpleasant and unkind, especially in a spiteful way.  |  例句：She made the bitchiest comments about her coworker's outfit. 她对同事的穿着做了最刻薄的评论。

例句：He was in the bitchiest mood I'd ever seen him in. 他处于我见过的他最恶毒的情绪中。


近义词：nastiest, meanest, cruelest, spiteful, catty | 反义词: kindest, nicest, sweetest | 词性: 形容词
