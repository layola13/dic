lucuma:/luːˈkuːmə/| n. |卢库马果，蛋黄果|A subtropical fruit native to the Andes mountains, with a sweet, maple-like flavor. It is often used in desserts and ice cream.|例句：Lucuma ice cream is a popular treat in Peru.  秘鲁的卢库马冰淇淋很受欢迎。

例句：She added lucuma powder to her smoothie for extra sweetness and nutrients. 她往冰沙里加了卢库马粉，以增加甜味和营养。|近义词：eggfruit|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
