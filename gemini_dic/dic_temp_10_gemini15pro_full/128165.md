nettliest: /ˈnet(ə)liɪst/
adjective
最易怒的；最刺人的
Covered in stinging hairs, causing a sharp or irritating sensation.  Most easily provoked to anger or irritation.
例句：He asked the nettliest question at the press conference. 他在新闻发布会上问了最尖锐的问题。

例句：She is by far the nettliest person I have ever met. 她是我迄今为止遇到的最易怒的人。

近义词：prickliest, thorniest, touchiest, most irritable, most sensitive
反义词：calmest, most patient, most even-tempered
词性：adjective

词源：From Middle English *netle + -iest, from Old English *netele + -ist.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *natilō.  Related to nettle.
