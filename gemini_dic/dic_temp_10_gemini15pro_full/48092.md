declawed:/diːˈklɔːd/| adj.|去爪的，被切除爪子的|Having had the claws removed (usually referring to cats).  The cat's claws were surgically removed to prevent scratching.|例句：The declawed cat could no longer climb trees.  这只去爪的猫再也不能爬树了。

例句：Declawing is a controversial procedure. 去爪是一项有争议的程序。|近义词：clawless|反义词：clawed|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + clawed, past participle of claw (“to scratch or tear with claws; to seize or pull with claws or hands”).
