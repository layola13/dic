impeachability:/ɪmˌpiːtʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
可弹劾性；可指责性
The quality or state of being impeachable.  The possibility of being accused of misconduct in office.
The president's lawyers argued against his impeachability.  总统的律师辩称他不应被弹劾。
The committee is investigating the impeachability of the judge. 委员会正在调查该法官是否可以被弹劾。
synonyms: accussability, liability, culpability
antonyms: unimpeachability, innocence, blamelessness
noun
