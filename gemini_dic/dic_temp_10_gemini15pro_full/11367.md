areometric:/ˌɛəriəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
比重计的; 液体比重测量的
relating to or determined by an areometer; relating to the measurement of the specific gravity of liquids.
The scientist conducted areometric tests to determine the density of the liquid.  科学家进行了比重计测试以确定液体的密度。
The areometric properties of the solution were carefully analyzed.  仔细分析了溶液的比重特性。
近义词: hydrometer, densitometer
反义词: None
词性: adjective

词源:  From French *aréométrique*, from *aréomètre* ("areometer, hydrometer") + *-ique* (*-ic*), ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀραιός (*araiós*, “thin, sparse”) + μέτρον (*métron*, “measure”).
