cross-post:/ˌkrɒs ˈpəʊst/ (British), /ˌkrɔːs ˈpoʊst/ (American)| v. n.|交叉发布，跨平台发布|To publish the same message, piece of content, or question in multiple online forums, groups, or platforms.|I cross-posted my question on several related subreddits to reach a wider audience. 我在几个相关的subreddit上交叉发布了我的问题，以接触更广泛的受众。|Synonyms: re-post, share, syndicate, distribute|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps limit, restrict)|verb, noun


cross-post:/ˌkrɒs ˈpəʊst/ (British), /ˌkrɔːs ˈpoʊst/ (American)| n.|交叉发布的帖子|A message or piece of content that has been published in multiple online locations.|Her cross-post about the fundraiser generated a lot of interest. 她关于筹款活动的交叉帖子引起了很多人的兴趣。|Synonyms: duplicated post, shared post, syndicated content|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps original post)|noun
