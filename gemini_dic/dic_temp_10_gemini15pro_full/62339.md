endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj.  n. | 地方性的，特有的；地方病 | Native or confined to a particular region or people; characteristic of or prevalent in a field. |  Many tropical diseases are endemic to the region. 许多热带疾病是该地区特有的。 |  The kangaroo is endemic to Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚特有的动物。 | 近义词：native, indigenous, aboriginal | 反义词：introduced, exotic, foreign | 词性: 形容词，名词

endemics:/ɛnˈdɛmɪks/ | n. | 地方性事物；地方病 | Things that are endemic to a particular region or field, especially endemic diseases. |  The island's endemics include several species of birds. 该岛特有的物种包括几种鸟类。|  The conference discussed the endemics affecting the local population. 会议讨论了影响当地居民的地方病。| 近义词：natives, indigenous species | 反义词：introduced species | 词性: 名词


词源: From Greek *éndēmos* "native," from *en* "in" + *dēmos* "people."
