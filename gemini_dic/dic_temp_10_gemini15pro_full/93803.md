immersive:/ɪˈmɜːrsɪv/ | adj. | 身临其境的，沉浸式的 |  fully involving the senses and creating a feeling of being present in another reality |  The new video game offers an immersive experience.  这款新电子游戏提供了一种身临其境的体验。 | 近义词: engaging, absorbing, captivating | 反义词:  superficial, shallow, detached | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *immersus*, past participle of *immergere* "to plunge in, dip, sink, immerse", from *in-* "in" + *mergere* "to dip, plunge".
