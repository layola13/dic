long-suffering:/ˌlɔːŋˈsʌfərɪŋ/ | adj. | 长期受苦的，忍耐的 | Patient and tolerant, especially in enduring hardship or provocation for a long time. | She has been long-suffering with her demanding boss. 她一直容忍着她那苛刻的老板。|  He was long-suffering in his marriage. 他在婚姻中一直很忍耐。 | 近义词: patient, tolerant, forbearing, enduring | 反义词: impatient, intolerant, irritable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "long" (meaning extended in time) + "suffering" (experiencing pain or hardship).  This compound word emphasizes the extended duration of the suffering.
