incorpse:/ɪnˈkɔːrps/ | v. | 包含，纳入，合并 | To incorporate something means to include or combine it as part of something larger. |  The new car design incorporates several new safety features.  新车设计包含了几项新的安全特性。

The company incorporated the feedback from customers into their new product. 公司将客户的反馈纳入到他们的新产品中。 | 近义词: integrate, include, embody, assimilate | 反义词: exclude, separate, remove |  词性:动词
