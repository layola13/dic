hatched:/hætʃt/| v. adj.|孵出，孵化的；策划的| emerge from an egg; to devise (a plot or plan) secretly |例句：The chicks hatched last week. 小鸡们上周孵出来了。|近义词：emerged, born, produced, concocted, plotted |反义词:unhatched, undeveloped|verb
hatched:/hætʃt/| adj.|孵出的|having emerged from an egg |例句：The newly hatched chicks were very small. 新孵出的小鸡非常小。|近义词：newborn, fledgling |反义词:unhatched|adjective
