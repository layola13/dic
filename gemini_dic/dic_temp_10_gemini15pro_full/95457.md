incisorial:/ɪnˈsaɪzəriəl/
adjective
切牙的；门牙的
Relating to or having the characteristics of incisors; adapted for cutting.
The incisorial teeth of a beaver are very sharp. 海狸的门齿非常锋利。
近义词:cutting, biting, gnawing
反义词:molar, grinding
词性:adjective
