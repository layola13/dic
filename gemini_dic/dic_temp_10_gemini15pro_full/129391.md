nomenclaturally:/ˌnoʊmənˈkleɪtʃərəli/ | adv. | 命名法上地，术语上地 |  In terms of naming, according to the rules of nomenclature.  |  The two species are nomenclaturally distinct, although genetically very similar. 这两个物种在命名上是不同的，尽管基因上非常相似。 |  taxonomically, formally |  informally | adverb
