immure:/ɪˈmjʊər/| v. |禁闭，囚禁；封闭，封存|To enclose or confine within walls; imprison.|The king was immured in the castle after the coup. 国王在政变后被囚禁在城堡里。|例句：The documents were immured in a vault. 这些文件被封存在一个保险库里。|例句：He immured himself in his study for days. 他把自己关在书房里好几天。|近义词：confine, imprison, incarcerate|反义词：liberate, free, release|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin immurare "to wall in," from Latin in- "in" + murus "wall."
