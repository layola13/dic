absentness: 心不在焉；缺席；漫不经心
absentness: The state of being absent or not present, either physically or mentally.  It can also refer to a lack of a particular quality or feature.
absentness: Her absentness during the meeting was noticeable. 她开会时心不在焉，很明显。

absentness: His frequent absentness from work led to his dismissal. 他经常旷工导致被解雇。

absentness: 近义词：inattention, distraction, preoccupation, absence
absentness: 反义词：presence, attentiveness, mindfulness
