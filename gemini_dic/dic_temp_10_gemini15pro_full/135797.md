nonprolific:/ˌnɒnprəˈlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 非多产的，不结果实的 | Not producing much fruit, offspring, or work; unproductive. | The apple tree was surprisingly nonprolific this year, bearing only a few small apples. 今年的苹果树出奇地 unproductive，只结了几个小苹果。| The writer's nonprolific period lasted for several years.  这位作家的非多产时期持续了好几年。 | 近义词: unproductive, barren, infertile, sterile | 反义词: prolific, productive, fertile, fruitful | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + prolific (from Latin proles "offspring" + ficus "making")
