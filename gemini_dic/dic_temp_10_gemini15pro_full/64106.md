epotation:/ˌiːpoʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 豪饮，痛饮 |  A (usually copious) draught, especially of alcoholic liquor.  | He celebrated his victory with a hearty epotation of champagne. 他豪饮香槟庆祝胜利。 | 近义词: carousal, bacchanal, binge, drinking bout | 反义词: abstinence, sobriety, temperance | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *ēpōtātiōn- (stem of ēpōtātiō ), from Latin ēpōtāre  "to drink up," from ē- (variant of ex- ) "out" + pōtāre "to drink."
