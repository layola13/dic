anger:/ˈæŋɡər/ (US), /ˈæŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|愤怒，生气|A strong feeling of displeasure and hostility.|She was filled with anger at the injustice. 她对这种不公正充满了愤怒。|近义词：fury, rage, wrath, resentment, indignation|反义词：calmness, peacefulness, tranquility, contentment|词性:名词,动词

anger:/ˈæŋɡər/ (US), /ˈæŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| v.|使生气，激怒|To provoke feelings of displeasure and hostility in (someone).|His constant lateness angered her. 他总是迟到让她很生气。|近义词：enrage, infuriate, provoke, irritate, annoy|反义词：calm, soothe, placate, appease|词性:动词
