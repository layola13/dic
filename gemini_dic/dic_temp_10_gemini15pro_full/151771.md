pasque:/ˈpæsk/ (US), /pɑːsk/ (UK)| n. |复活节|A European anemone (Pulsatilla vulgaris) that has purplish flowers in the spring, around Easter.  It's also called the pasqueflower.|The pasque flowers bloomed early this year.  今年的复活节花开得很早。|Synonyms: pasqueflower, windflower, Easter flower|Antonyms: N/A|noun
