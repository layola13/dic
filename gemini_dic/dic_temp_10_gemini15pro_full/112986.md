lulls:/lʌlz/
lull:/lʌl/| n. |平静；间歇；暂停|A temporary period of quiet or inactivity.|The lull in the fighting allowed the villagers to escape.  战斗的间歇使得村民们得以逃脱.|近义词：pause, respite, break, calm|反义词: activity, bustle, commotion, excitement|名词
lull:/lʌl/| vt. |使平静；使安静；哄骗|To calm or soothe someone, typically with gentle sounds or movements.|The gentle rocking of the boat lulled the baby to sleep. 船的轻轻摇晃使婴儿入睡。|近义词：soothe, calm, pacify, quiet|反义词:agitate, excite, stimulate, rouse|动词
