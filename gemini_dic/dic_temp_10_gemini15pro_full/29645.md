caterwauler:/ˈkætərˌwɔːlər/| n.|夜猫子，嚎叫者；发出刺耳声音的人或动物|A person or animal that makes a loud, harsh, wailing cry, especially at night. |例句：The caterwauler next door kept me awake all night. 隔壁的夜猫子让我整夜没睡。

例句：The alley cats were caterwauling outside our window. 巷子里的野猫在我们窗外嚎叫。|近义词：shrieker, screamer, howler|反义词:singer, melodist|词性:noun
