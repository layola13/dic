overdevoted:/ˌoʊvərdiˈvoʊtɪd/ | adj. | 过分投入的，过分 dévoué | excessively devoted; too dedicated or committed to something or someone | She was so overdevoted to her work that she neglected her family. 她如此投入于工作，以至于忽略了家人。|  He's overdevoted to his favorite sports team. 他对最喜欢的运动队过分投入了。 |近义词：fanatical, obsessive, zealous | 反义词：indifferent, apathetic | 词性: 形容词

词源： "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "devoted" (past participle of "devote," from Latin "devovere" - to vow, dedicate)
