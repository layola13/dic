monogenistic:/ˌmɒnədʒɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 单元发生的，单一起源的 | Relating to or characterized by monogenism; believing in a single origin of humanity. |  Monogenistic theories posit that all humans descended from a single ancestral population. 单元发生理论认为所有人类都起源于一个单一的祖先群体。

Monogenistic views contrast sharply with polygenistic ones. 单元起源的观点与多元起源的观点截然不同。 |  Near Antonyms: polygenistic | Near Synonyms: monogenic | adjective
