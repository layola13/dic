freegan:/ˈfriːɡən/| n.|觅食者；不消费主义者；免费素食主义者|A person who rejects consumerism and seeks to help the environment by reducing waste, especially by retrieving and using discarded food and other goods.|Example 1: The freegan retrieved discarded vegetables from the market's dumpster.  这个觅食者从市场的垃圾箱里捡回了被丢弃的蔬菜。

Example 2:  He identifies as a freegan and tries to minimize his impact on the environment. 他认为自己是一个不消费主义者，并试图尽量减少他对环境的影响。|近义词：anti-consumerist, dumpster diver|反义词: consumerist|词性:noun
