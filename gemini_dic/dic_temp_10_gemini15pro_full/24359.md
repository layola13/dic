briners:/ˈbraɪnərz/| n.|盐水商，制盐工人，盐水井工人|Workers who produce or deal with brine (highly concentrated salt water) are called briners. |The briners worked long hours in the harsh conditions of the salt flats. 制盐工人在盐滩恶劣的环境下长时间工作。|The briners carefully monitored the evaporation process. 制盐工人们仔细地监控着蒸发过程。|近义词：salt makers, salt workers|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
