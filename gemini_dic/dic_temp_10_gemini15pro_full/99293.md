intercorrelate:/ˌɪntərkɔːrɪˈleɪt/ /ˌɪntərˈkɔːrəˌleɪt/
verb
相互关联，互相影响
To have a mutual relationship or connection; to be related to each other.
The study found that stress levels and sleep quality intercorrelate. 研究发现，压力水平和睡眠质量相互关联。
The various factors intercorrelate in complex ways. 各种因素以复杂的方式相互关联。
近义词: correlate, interrelate, connect, link
反义词: independent, unrelated
词性: verb

intercorrelation:/ˌɪntərkɔːrɪˈleɪʃn/ /ˌɪntərˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃn/
noun
相互关联，相互影响
A mutual relationship or connection.
There is a strong intercorrelation between poverty and crime. 贫困与犯罪之间存在着很强的相互关联。
The study examined the intercorrelation of various economic indicators. 这项研究考察了各种经济指标之间的相互关联。
近义词: correlation, interrelation, connection, link
反义词: independence
词性: noun

词源:  From inter- + correlate, from Latin com- "together" + relate, from relatus, past participle of referre "to carry back, bring back."
