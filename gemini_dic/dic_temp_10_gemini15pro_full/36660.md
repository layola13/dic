collenchyma: /kəˈlɛŋkɪmə/ | n. | 厚角组织 | A plant tissue consisting of elongated cells with unevenly thickened walls, providing flexible support to young stems and petioles. |  The collenchyma cells provide support for the growing plant. 厚角组织细胞为正在生长的植物提供支撑。|  The strings of celery are composed primarily of collenchyma. 芹菜的茎主要由厚角组织组成。 |  parenchyma, sclerenchyma |  None | noun
