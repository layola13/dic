misericordia:/ˌmɪzərɪˈkɔːrdiə/| n. |怜悯，慈悲，宽恕|An act of mercy or compassion, especially the sparing of punishment or the granting of forgiveness.  It derives from the Latin "misericordia," meaning "mercy" or "pity."|例句：The judge showed misericordia and granted the defendant a reduced sentence. 法官表现出仁慈，给予被告减刑。

例句：He begged for misericordia, hoping to avoid execution. 他乞求怜悯，希望免于处决。|近义词：mercy, compassion, clemency, pity, forgiveness|反义词:cruelty, harshness, ruthlessness|词性:noun
