houndlike:/ˈhaʊndˌlaɪk/| adj.|像猎犬的；猎犬般的|Resembling or characteristic of a hound, especially in being keen in pursuit or having a keen sense of smell.|The detective, with his houndlike determination, finally tracked down the suspect.|近义词：dogged, tenacious, relentless, persistent|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, lackadaisical|词性:形容词

词源: From hound + -like.  "Hound" comes from Old English *hund, of Germanic origin, related to Dutch hond and German Hund. "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English -lic.
