passionist: /ˈpæʃənɪst/
n.
热情的人；激情主义者；[宗]苦难会会员
A person who shows or feels great enthusiasm for something; a member of a Roman Catholic religious order devoted to meditation on the Passion of Christ.
She's a real passionist about her work. 她对她的工作充满热情。
The Passionists are known for their devotion to the suffering of Christ. 苦难会会员以他们对基督受难的虔诚而闻名。
近义词: enthusiast, zealot, fanatic
反义词: apathete, phlegmatic
词性: 名词
