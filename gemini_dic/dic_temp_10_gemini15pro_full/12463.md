ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æsp, from Proto-Germanic *aspo, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ep- (“tree, plant”). Cognate with Dutch esp, German Espe, Danish asp.  The sense of "venomous snake" comes from Latin aspis, from Ancient Greek ἀσπίς (aspís, “asp, shield”), likely referring to the cobra's hood.
