bevil:/ˈbɛvəl/|v.|使成斜角，使倾斜；斜切；给…加斜边|To cut at a slant; to slope the edge of (something).|He beveled the edges of the mirror to give it a more antique look. 他把镜子的边缘斜切，使其更具古董感。|近义词：slant, slope, angle, chamfer|反义词：straighten, flatten|词性：动词

beveled:/ˈbɛvəld/|adj.|斜角的，倾斜的|Having a sloping edge or surface.|The beveled glass in the window caught the light beautifully. 窗户上的斜面玻璃美丽地捕捉着光线。|近义词：slanted, sloping, angled|反义词：straight, flat|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bivel*, diminutive of *bive* "oblique," of uncertain origin.
