diplogenic:/ˌdɪploʊˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |双基因的，由两个不同基因产生的 |  Relating to or produced by the interaction of two different genes.  | Example: The researchers discovered a diplogenic inheritance pattern for the disease. 研究人员发现了这种疾病的双基因遗传模式。 | Example:  Diplogenic mutations can contribute to complex diseases. 双基因突变会导致复杂的疾病。|  Synonym:  biallelic (in some contexts) | Antonym:  monogenic | 词性: 形容词
