membered:/ˈmɛmbərd/| adj.|有…成员的；由…构成的|Having the specified number or kind of members.  Composed of a specified number of individuals.|例句：A three-membered ring. 一个三元环。|近义词：constituted, composed, comprising|反义词: N/A |词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "member" + "-ed" suffix.  "Member" comes from Old French "membre" and ultimately from Latin "membrum" meaning "limb, member of the body." The "-ed" suffix forms adjectives from nouns, indicating possession or characterization by the noun.
