nominatival:/ˌnɒmɪˈneɪtɪvəl/
adjective
主格的；名词性的
relating to or belonging to the case of a noun or pronoun that is typically used as the subject of a verb
The nominatival case is used for the subject of a sentence. 主格用于句子的主语。
The nominatival form of the pronoun is "he."  这个代词的主格形式是“he”。
Synonyms: subjective, nominative
Antonyms: objective, accusative
word type: adjective
