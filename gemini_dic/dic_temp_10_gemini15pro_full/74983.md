frictions:/ˈfrɪkʃənz/
n.
摩擦；冲突；不和
The force resisting the relative motion of solid surfaces, fluid layers, and material elements sliding against each other. Disagreement or conflict between people or groups.
例句：
There are frictions between the two countries. 两国之间存在摩擦。

The frictions between management and staff are affecting productivity.  管理层和员工之间的摩擦正在影响生产力。


近义词：
conflict, disagreement, discord, strife, resistance, abrasion

反义词：
harmony, agreement, accord, concord, cooperation, smoothness

词性
noun (plural)

词源
From Latin *friction-, from frictiō (“a rubbing, friction”), from frictus, past participle of fricāre (“to rub”).
