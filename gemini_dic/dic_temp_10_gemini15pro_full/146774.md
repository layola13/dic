overidealistic: /ˌoʊvərˌaɪdiːəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过于理想化的 | Excessively idealistic; having ideals that are unrealistic or impractical. |  He was young and overidealistic, believing he could single-handedly change the world. 他年轻又过于理想化，认为自己可以单枪匹马地改变世界。

She had an overidealistic view of marriage, expecting a fairytale romance. 她对婚姻抱有过于理想化的看法，期待着童话般的浪漫。 | synonyms: utopian, impractical, quixotic, starry-eyed | antonyms: realistic, pragmatic, cynical, down-to-earth | adjective
