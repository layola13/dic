anti-patriotic:/ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
反爱国的，不爱国的
Opposed to patriotism; lacking or expressing opposition to patriotic feeling.
His anti-patriotic speeches caused outrage. 他的反爱国言论引起了众怒。

His anti-patriotic stance led to his exile. 他的不爱国立场导致他被流放。

Synonym: unpatriotic, disloyal, treasonous, seditious
Antonym: patriotic, loyal, nationalistic
词性: adjective
