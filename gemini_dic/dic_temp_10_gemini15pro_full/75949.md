funicle:/ˈfjuːnɪkl/ | n. | 珠柄，珠心索 | A small stalk that attaches an ovule (seed) to the placenta in a plant's ovary. | The funicle of the orchid is short and thick.  兰花的珠柄短而粗。| Synonyms: funiculus | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


funicle:/ˈfjuːnɪkl/ | n. | (解剖学) 脐带 | A cord-like structure, especially the umbilical cord. | The doctor examined the funicle for any abnormalities. 医生检查了脐带是否有异常。| Synonyms: umbilical cord, cord | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fūniculus, diminutive of fūnis (“rope, cord”).
