hand-me-downs: /ˌhænd miː ˈdaʊnz/ | n. |旧衣服，别人穿过的衣服 | Secondhand clothes, typically those passed down from an older sibling or relative to a younger one. |  My younger brother always wore hand-me-downs. 我的弟弟总是穿旧衣服。

My closet is full of hand-me-downs from my older sister. 我的衣橱里装满了姐姐穿过的旧衣服。


| Synonym: secondhand clothes, used clothes | Antonym: new clothes | Noun
