fetidnesses:/ˈfɛtɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
恶臭，臭气熏天
The state or quality of being fetid; a foul, offensive smell.
The fetidnesses emanating from the garbage dump were unbearable.  垃圾堆散发出的恶臭令人难以忍受。
近义词:stenches, foulnesses, ranknesses
反义词:fragrances, sweetnesses
词性:n.

Etymology: From fetid + -ness.  Fetid comes from Late Latin *foetidus, from Latin foeteō (“to stink”).
