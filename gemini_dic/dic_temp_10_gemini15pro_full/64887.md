essayist:/ˈɛseɪɪst/| n.|散文家，随笔作家|A writer of essays, especially one who does so professionally or habitually.|Michel de Montaigne is considered the father of the essayist.  米歇尔·德·蒙田被认为是散文之父。

Virginia Woolf was a prolific essayist as well as a novelist. 弗吉尼亚·伍尔夫是一位多产的散文家，同时也是一位小说家。|近义词：writer, author, columnist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-writer)|词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *essayiste*, from *essai* ("attempt, trial, essay") + *-iste* (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Late Latin *exagium* ("weighing").
