metromaniac: /ˌmɛtroʊˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 写诗狂；写坏诗癖者 | A person with an obsession for writing verse, especially bad verse. | He fancies himself a poet, but he's really just a metromaniac. 他自以为是诗人，但实际上只是一个写诗狂。 |  Example:  The aspiring writer, blinded by metromania, churned out reams of doggerel.  这位胸怀抱负的作家，被写诗癖蒙蔽了双眼，写出了大量的打油诗。

Example: Despite the criticism, the metromaniac continued to fill notebooks with his rhyming couplets. 尽管受到批评，这位写诗狂仍然继续在他的笔记本上写满押韵的对句。 | synonyms: versifier, rhymester, poetaster | antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for an obsession with writing verse) | noun
