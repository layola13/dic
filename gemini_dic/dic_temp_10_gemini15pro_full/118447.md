mescaline:/ˈmɛskəlɪn/ | n. | 麦司卡林 | A hallucinogenic alkaloid drug, C₁₁H₁₇NO₃, obtained from the peyote cactus and other cacti.  It produces hallucinations and other psychoactive effects. |  He experienced vivid hallucinations after ingesting mescaline. 他摄入麦司卡林后经历了生动的幻觉。 | Synonyms:  peyote, hallucinogen | Antonyms:  None | noun
