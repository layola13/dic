depersonalizing:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使人失去个性化的；使人感觉不像个人的
Tending to cause a person to feel less like an individual person, and more like an object or part of a system.
The experience was profoundly depersonalizing. 这次经历令人深感丧失个性。
近义词:alienating, dehumanizing, objectifying
反义词:personalizing, individualizing
词性:adjective

depersonalize:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪz/
verb
使失去个性；使感觉不像个人
To make someone feel less like an individual person, and more like an object or part of a system.
Bureaucracy can depersonalize the relationship between doctor and patient.  官僚作风会使医生和病人之间的关系变得没有人情味。
近义词:dehumanize, objectify
反义词:personalize, individualize
词性:verb

Etymology:
From de- + personalize, from person + -al + -ize.  The prefix "de-" denotes removal or reversal of an action.
