ambulate:/ˈæmbjəleɪt/| v.|行走，移动|to walk; move about.|The patient was able to ambulate with assistance after surgery.  手术后，病人能够在辅助下行走。|The physical therapist helped the patient ambulate down the hall.  物理治疗师帮助病人沿着走廊行走。|近义词：walk, move, perambulate|反义词:sit, lie down|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ambulare, frequentative of Latin ambulare "to walk," from ambi- "around" + -ulare (frequentative suffix).
