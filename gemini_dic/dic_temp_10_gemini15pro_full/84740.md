handleable:/ˈhændləbl/| adj.|可处理的，易操作的|Something that is handleable is manageable or easy to deal with.  It can be physically handled or dealt with effectively. |The boxes were small and handleable. 这些箱子很小，容易搬运。| The situation, though complex, seemed handleable.  虽然情况复杂，但似乎可以处理。|近义词：manageable, controllable, tractable|反义词: unmanageable, unwieldy, cumbersome|词性:形容词
