Hofbräuhaus:/ˈhoʊfbrɔɪhaʊs/| n.|皇家啤酒馆|A large beer hall, especially the famous one in Munich, Germany. It literally translates to "court brewery house."|例句：We visited the Hofbräuhaus in Munich and had a great time. 我们参观了慕尼黑的皇家啤酒馆，玩得很开心。

例句：The Hofbräuhaus is known for its lively atmosphere and traditional Bavarian food. 皇家啤酒馆以其热闹的氛围和传统的巴伐利亚美食而闻名。|近义词：beer hall, brewery, pub|反义词: None|词性: Noun
