extorts:/ɪkˈstɔːrts/
verb
勒索，敲诈
obtain (something) by force, threats, or other unfair means.
The gang extorted money from local businesses.  这伙歹徒向当地企业勒索钱财。
He was convicted of extorting money from several of his clients. 他被判犯有向几位客户勒索钱财的罪行。
近义词:coerces, forces, compels, extracts, wrings
反义词:gives, offers, donates, grants
词性:v.
