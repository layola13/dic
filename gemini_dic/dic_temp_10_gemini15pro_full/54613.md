dismayingly:/dɪsˈmeɪɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地
in a way that causes dismay; alarmingly
The results of the survey were dismayingly low.  这项调查的结果低得令人沮丧。
Synonyms: alarmingly, disappointingly, distressingly, worryingly
Antonyms: encouragingly, hearteningly, reassuringly
词性: 副词
