meum:/ˈmiːəm/| n.|我的东西；我的财产（尤指土地或牲畜）|Something that belongs to me, especially land or livestock.  It's a legal term often used in older texts. |The lawyer argued that the land was rightfully the farmer's meum. 律师辩称这块土地理应是农民的财产。|The ancient texts referred to the meum and tuum of the villagers. 古代文献提到了村民们的财产所属。|Synonyms: property, possessions, belongings|Antonyms: tuum (another's property)|noun
