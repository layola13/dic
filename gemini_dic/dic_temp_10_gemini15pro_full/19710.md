bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. n.|双语的；能说两种语言的人|Speaking two languages fluently; a person who speaks two languages fluently.|She is bilingual in English and Spanish. 她精通英语和西班牙语。|Near Synonyms: multilingual, polyglot|Antonym: monolingual|adjective, noun

bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | n. |能说两种语言的人|A person who speaks two languages fluently.|The company is looking for bilinguals to work as interpreters. 这家公司正在寻找能说两种语言的人担任翻译。|Near Synonyms: multilingual, polyglot|Antonym: monolingual|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bilinguis* (two-tongued), from *bi-* (two) + *lingua* (tongue, language).
