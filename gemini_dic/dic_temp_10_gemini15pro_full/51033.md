desmine:/ˈdɛzmɪn/| n.|桥粒蛋白|A type of intermediate filament protein found in desmosomes, which are intercellular junctions that provide strong adhesion between cells.|Desmine plays a crucial role in maintaining the structural integrity of tissues such as muscle and epithelial tissues.|近义词:intermediate filament protein|反义词:None|词性:noun
词源:From Ancient Greek δεσμός (desmós, “bond, ligament”).
