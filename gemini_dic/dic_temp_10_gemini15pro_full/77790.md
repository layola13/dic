generalists:/ˈdʒɛnərəlɪsts/ | n. | 通才，多面手 | A person competent in several different fields or activities. |  She's a generalist, able to contribute to many different aspects of the project. 她是一位通才，能够为项目的许多不同方面做出贡献。|  He's a generalist rather than a specialist. 他是一位通才，而不是一位专家。 | 近义词: versatile person, all-rounder, polymath | 反义词: specialist, expert | 词性: noun

generalist:/ˈdʒɛnərəlɪst/ | n. |  博学者（尤指文艺复兴时期）| A person with wide-ranging knowledge or learning.  | Leonardo da Vinci was a true generalist, excelling in art, science, and engineering.  达芬奇是一位真正的博学者，在艺术、科学和工程方面都表现出色。 |  Many Renaissance thinkers were generalists, embracing a wide variety of disciplines. 许多文艺复兴时期的思想家都是博学者，他们涉猎各种各样的学科。 | 近义词: polymath, Renaissance man, scholar | 反义词: specialist | 词性: noun
