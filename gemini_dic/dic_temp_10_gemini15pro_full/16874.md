basilical:/bəˈsɪlɪkəl/
adjective|教堂式的，雄伟的|Resembling or suitable for a basilica, especially in being spacious and having a high central nave. |The new concert hall had a basilical design.  这个新的音乐厅采用了教堂式的设计。|近义词:grand, majestic, imposing|反义词:small, modest, unassuming|词性:adjective
basilical:/bəˈsɪlɪkəl/
noun|长方形教堂|A large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, especially one used as a law court or for public assemblies in ancient Rome. Also, an important church. |St. Peter's Basilica in Rome is a renowned example. 罗马的圣彼得大教堂是一个著名的例子。|近义词:cathedral, church, temple|反义词:chapel, shrine|词性:noun
