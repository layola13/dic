child-abuse: /ˈtʃaɪld əˈbjuːs/ | n. | 虐待儿童 | The act of physically, sexually, or emotionally mistreating a child. |  The child showed signs of child-abuse.  这个孩子表现出被虐待的迹象。| synonyms: child maltreatment, child neglect, child endangerment | antonyms: child protection, child welfare | noun
