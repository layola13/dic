luxuriantly:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriəntli/| adv.| 茂盛地，丰富地，奢华地 |Growing or producing abundantly; lush; characterized by luxury or richness. | The vines grew luxuriantly over the old stone wall. 藤蔓在古老的石墙上茂盛地生长。|  The hotel was luxuriantly decorated with fresh flowers.  酒店用鲜花装饰得富丽堂皇。 |近义词：abundantly, profusely, richly, lavishly |反义词：sparsely, meagerly, poorly |词性:副词

词源: From Latin *luxuriant- (stem of luxurians), present participle of luxuriare "to be luxuriant," from luxus "luxury, extravagance."
