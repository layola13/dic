comprehensibilities: /ˌkɑːmprɪˌhɛnsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 可理解性；可理解的事物 | The comprehensibilities of the instructions were questioned by several users.  一些用户质疑说明书的可理解性。 |  The new software boasts improved comprehensibilities for beginners. 新软件吹嘘提高了对初学者的可理解性。 | 近义词: intelligibility, clarity, understandability | 反义词: incomprehensibility, unintelligibility, obscurity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From comprehensible + -ity.  来自 comprehensible（可理解的） + -ity（名词后缀）。 Comprehensible 本身来自 Late Middle English: from late Latin comprehensibilis, from comprehendere ‘grasp mentally’ (see comprehend).  来自晚期中古英语：源自晚期拉丁语 comprehensibilis，来自 comprehendere “在精神上掌握”（参见 comprehend）。
