opaquely:/oʊˈpeɪkli/ | adj. | 不透明地；晦涩地；含糊地 | Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Also, obscure in meaning; unclear. | The frosted glass window allowed light in, but opaquely, so shapes were indistinct. 磨砂玻璃窗透光，但不透明，所以形状模糊不清。 | The artist's statement about the meaning of the work was opaquely worded, leaving the audience confused. 艺术家关于作品含义的陈述措辞含糊，让观众感到困惑。 | 近义词：obscurely, vaguely, dimly, darkly | 反义词：clearly, transparently, lucidly | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *opacus* "shaded, dark," of uncertain origin.
