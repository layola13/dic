inauthentically:/ˌɪnɔːˈθɛntɪkli/
adverb
不真实地，虚伪地
in a way that is not genuine or true; in a fake or artificial manner.
She inauthentically portrayed herself as an expert. 她虚伪地把自己描绘成一个专家。
The scene was inauthentically staged.  这个场景的布置很不真实。

近义词:falsely, artificially, deceptively, spuriously, disingenuously
反义词:genuinely, authentically, truly, sincerely

词性:adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + authentic + -ally (adverbial suffix).  "Authentic" comes from the Greek "authentikos" meaning "genuine, original."
