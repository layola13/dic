changlong: /ˈtʃɑːŋlɒŋ/ (Approximation -  no standard pronunciation exists as this is a transliteration from Chinese) | n. | 长龙, 长队 |  A long queue or line, often used to describe a long procession of people or vehicles. Derived from the Chinese words "chang" (long) and "long" (dragon), implying a long, winding line resembling a dragon. | Example: There was a changlong outside the popular new restaurant.  这家热门的新餐厅外排起了长龙。| Example: The changlong of cars stretched for miles.  长长的车龙绵延数英里。 | Synonym: queue, line, procession | Antonym: short line, few people | noun
