creatures:/ˈkriːtʃərz/ (US), /ˈkriːtʃəz/ (UK)| n. |生物，动物，人|Animals, especially those that are not human, or imaginary beings that look like animals.  Sometimes used to refer to people in a specific situation.|例句：The forest is full of strange creatures. 森林里充满了奇异的生物。|近义词：animals, beings, organisms|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps 'inanimate objects')|Noun
