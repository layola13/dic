avuncularly: /əˈvʌŋkjələrli/ | adv. | 像伯父（叔父、舅父、姑父、姨父）般地；慈祥地 | In a way that is like an uncle, especially a kind and helpful one. | He smiled avuncularly and offered me some advice. 他慈祥地笑了笑，并给我提供了一些建议。 |  He patted her head avuncularly. 他像长辈一样慈爱地拍了拍她的头。 | 近义词：kindly, paternally, benevolently | 反义词：unsympathetically, coldly | 词性: 副词

词源：From Latin *avunculus* "maternal uncle" + -ar + -ly.
