dissheathe:/dɪsˈʃiːð/
verb
拔出（尤指剑）
to draw or pull out (a weapon, especially a sword) from its sheath or covering.
The knight dissheathed his sword and prepared for battle. 骑士拔出剑，准备战斗。
synonyms: unsheathe, draw, unveil, uncover
antonyms: sheathe, conceal, hide
