noyade:/nɔɪˈjɑːd/ | n. |集体溺毙; 蓄意溺毙(作为一种执行死刑的方式) |  The deliberate drowning of a person or group of people, especially as a form of execution. | During the Reign of Terror, noyades were a common method of execution. 在恐怖统治时期，集体溺毙是一种常见的处决方式。| The French revolutionary government used noyades to execute large numbers of people. 法国大革命政府用集体溺毙处决了大量的人。 | synonyms: drowning, submersion, execution | antonyms: rescue, resuscitation | noun
