mounter:/ˈmaʊntər/| n.|安装工，装配工，裱画员|A person or thing that mounts something, especially a person who mounts pictures, photographs, or other artwork for display, or a machine that mounts electronic components onto circuit boards.|The mounter carefully affixed the photograph to the mat board.  裱画员小心翼翼地把照片贴在卡纸上。|Synonyms: installer, assembler, fitter, erector|Antonyms: dismantler, remover|noun

mounter:/ˈmaʊntər/| n.|安装程序，挂载器|A program or device that prepares a storage device or a file system for access.|The operating system uses a mounter to access the files on the USB drive. 操作系统使用挂载器访问U盘上的文件。|Synonyms: installer, loader|Antonyms: unmounter|noun
