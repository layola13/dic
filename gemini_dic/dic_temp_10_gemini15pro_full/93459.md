illusionist:/ɪˈluːʒənɪst/| n.|魔术师；幻术师|A person who performs illusions to entertain an audience.  A magician skilled in creating deceptive appearances.|The illusionist amazed the crowd with his disappearing act. 魔术师的消失表演使观众惊叹。|Synonyms: magician, conjurer, prestidigitator|Antonyms: realist|noun
