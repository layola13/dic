gigabits:/ˈɡɪɡəˌbɪts/| n. |千兆位|A unit of data transfer rate equal to one billion bits per second.  It's often used to measure network speeds or data transfer rates.|The internet connection boasts speeds of up to 100 gigabits per second.  该网络连接拥有高达每秒 100 千兆位的速度。

The hard drive can transfer data at 5 gigabits per second.  该硬盘可以以每秒 5 千兆位的速度传输数据。 |近义词:Gbps|反义词:kilobits|词性:noun
