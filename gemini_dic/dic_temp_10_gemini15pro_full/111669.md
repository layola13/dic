lock-in:/ˈlɒkɪn/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkɪn/ (US)| n.|锁定；套牢；(消费者对某一特定产品或服务的)依赖|The company's strategy is to create a lock-in effect with its customers. 这家公司的策略是使其客户产生依赖效应。|例句：

Customers were locked in to long contracts.  客户被长期合同套牢。

The software company uses a proprietary format to lock in its users.  这家软件公司使用专有格式来锁定用户。


近义词：dependency, tie-in, commitment,  |反义词:freedom, flexibility|词性:名词


lock-in:/ˈlɒkɪn/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkɪn/ (US)| adj.|锁定的；套牢的|The lock-in period for this investment is five years. 这项投资的锁定期限为五年。|例句：

They offer a lock-in interest rate for the first year. 他们提供第一年的锁定利率。

A lock-in contract prevents customers from switching providers. 锁定合同阻止客户更换供应商。

近义词：fixed, tied, committed|反义词:flexible, open|词性:形容词
