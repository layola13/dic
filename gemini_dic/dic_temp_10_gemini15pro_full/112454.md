loudliest:/ˈlaʊdɪst/ | adj. | 最响亮的 | Superlative form of loud: most loud.  The loudest sound was the explosion. | 例句：He shouted the loudest of all the children. 他在所有孩子中喊得最大声。

例句：The loudest cheer came from his own fans. 最响亮的欢呼声来自他自己的粉丝。

近义词：noisiest, clamorous, deafening | 反义词：quietest, softest | 词性：形容词

词源：From Middle English *loudest, superlative of *loud (“loud”), from Old English hlūd (“loud, noisy, clamorous, turbulent”), from Proto-Germanic *hlūdaz (“loud”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear”). Cognate with Dutch luid (“loud”), German laut (“loud”), Swedish ljud (“sound”).  The -est suffix is a common superlative ending in English.
