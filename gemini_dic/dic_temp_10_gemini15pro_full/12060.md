artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/ | n. |人工制品，史前古器物，人为现象|An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest.|The museum displays many artifacts from ancient Egypt.  博物馆展出了许多来自古埃及的文物。| synonym: relic, object, antique, item | antonym: natural object | noun

artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/ | n. |（尤指科技产品测试中的）假象，伪影| something observed in a scientific investigation or experiment that is not naturally present but occurs as a result of the preparative or investigative procedure.|The image was distorted by artifacts. 这张图片被假象扭曲了。| synonym: anomaly, glitch, error | antonym: genuine feature | noun
