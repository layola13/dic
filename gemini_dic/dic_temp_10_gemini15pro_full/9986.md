aphoristically: /ˌæfərɪˈstɪkli/ | adv. | 警句式地，格言式地 | In a concise and forcefully expressive manner, like an aphorism. | He spoke aphoristically, offering nuggets of wisdom in short, memorable sentences. 他说话像格言一样，用简短而难忘的句子表达智慧的精髓。| Example:  She aphoristically summed up the situation with the phrase, "Less is more." 她用“少即是多”这句话精辟地概括了情况。| Synonyms: concisely, pithily, succinctly, sententiously | Antonyms: verbosely, prolixly | word type: adverb
