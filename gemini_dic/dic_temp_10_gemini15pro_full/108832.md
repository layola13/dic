leavers:/ˈliːvəz/
n.
离开者；毕业生；离校生
People who are leaving a place, especially school or a job.
The school leavers are excited about their future.  学校的毕业生对他们的未来感到兴奋。
graduates, alumni, departing students
stayers, newcomers
