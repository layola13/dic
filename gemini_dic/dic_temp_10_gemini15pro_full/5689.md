aluminas:/əˈluːmɪnəz/| n. |氧化铝|A white crystalline powder, the most common form of aluminum oxide (Al₂O₃), occurring naturally as corundum and used as an abrasive, refractory, and catalyst.|Aluminum oxide is used for producing aluminas with different properties to suit different applications. 氧化铝用于生产具有不同性能的氧化铝，以适应不同的应用。|Synonyms: aluminum oxide, corundum|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


aluminas:/əˈluːmɪnəz/| n. |矾土|A naturally occurring hydrated aluminum oxide, containing varying amounts of iron oxides and other impurities, and used as a source of aluminum.|Bauxite is the principal ore of aluminas. 铝土矿是氧化铝的主要矿石。|Synonyms: bauxite, aluminum ore|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
