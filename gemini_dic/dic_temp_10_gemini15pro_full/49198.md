delayer:/dɪˈleɪər/| n.|延误者，延迟者|Someone or something that causes a delay. |The delayer in the project was a lack of funding.  项目中的延误因素是缺乏资金。|Synonym: procrastinator, obstructer, impediment|Antonym: expediter, facilitator|noun


delayer:/dɪˈleɪər/| n.|延迟器|A device or substance that delays something. |The chemical acts as a delayer in the setting process of the concrete.  这种化学物质在混凝土的凝固过程中起到了延迟剂的作用。|Synonym: retardant|Antonym: accelerator|noun
