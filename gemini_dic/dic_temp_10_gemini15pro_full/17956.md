beflower:/bɪˈflaʊər/
verb
用花装饰；使布满鲜花
to decorate or cover with flowers
The wedding arch was beflowered with white roses and lilies. 婚礼拱门上装饰着白色的玫瑰和百合花。
decorate, adorn, embellish, garland
bare, plain
v.
