gate:/ɡeɪt/| n.|大门，门口，闸门|A hinged barrier used to close an opening in a wall, fence, or hedge.|She opened the garden gate and walked through. 她打开了花园门，走了进去。|近义词：entrance, doorway, portal|反义词：exit, opening|noun

gate:/ɡeɪt/| vt.|控制，限制|To control the passage of (something, such as a flow of liquid or gas) by means of a gate.|The flow of water was gated to control the irrigation. 水流被闸门控制以调节灌溉。|近义词：control, regulate, restrict|反义词：release, open|verb
