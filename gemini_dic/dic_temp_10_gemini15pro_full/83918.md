half-boiling:/ˈhæfˈbɔɪlɪŋ/
adj.
半沸腾的，半开的
Partially boiling; not at a full, rolling boil.
The chef instructed to keep the sauce half-boiling to prevent it from thickening too much.  厨师指示要保持酱汁半开，以防止它变得太稠。
The water for the pasta is half-boiling; it needs a few more minutes.  煮意大利面的水半开了，还需要几分钟。
近义词: simmering
反义词: fully boiling, rapidly boiling
词性: adjective
