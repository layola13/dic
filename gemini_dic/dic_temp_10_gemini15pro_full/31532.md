charterage:/ˈtʃɑːrtərɪdʒ/
n.|租船费，包租费|The cost of hiring or chartering a vessel.  It covers the use of the ship for a specified period or voyage.|The charterage for the oil tanker was exorbitant.  油轮的租船费用过高。|freight, hire|N/A|noun
Etymology: From "charter" (to hire or lease) + "-age" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a charge or fee).  Ultimately from Latin "charta" (paper, document).
