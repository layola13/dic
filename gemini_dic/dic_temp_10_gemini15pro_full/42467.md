counterfeiter:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtər/| n.|伪造者；假冒者|Someone who makes fake money, products, or documents.|The police arrested the counterfeiter who was producing fake $100 bills. 警方逮捕了制造伪造100美元钞票的伪造者。|synonyms: forger, faker, imitator, fraudster|antonyms: authenticator, verifier|noun

counterfeit:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪt/| n. adj. vt.|伪造品；假冒的；伪造|a fraudulent imitation of something else; not genuine;  to make a fraudulent copy of something|The handbag was a counterfeit of a designer brand.  这个手提包是冒牌货。  He was arrested for counterfeiting money. 他因伪造货币而被捕。|synonyms: fake, forged, spurious, sham|antonyms: genuine, authentic, real|noun, adjective, verb
