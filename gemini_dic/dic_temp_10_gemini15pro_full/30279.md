cementers:/sɪˈmɛntərz/
n.
水泥匠；固井工人；粘合剂
Workers who cement things, especially in oil wells.  Those who use cement to bind or solidify materials.
The cementers worked tirelessly to seal the well.  固井工人们不知疲倦地工作以封闭油井。
Experienced cementers are crucial for a successful well completion.  经验丰富的固井工人对于成功完成一口井至关重要。
synonyms: concreters, bonders
antonyms: dismantlers, separators
noun
