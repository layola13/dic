navaratri:/nəˈvɑːrəːtriː/ | n. |九夜节|A Hindu festival lasting nine nights, celebrated in the autumn, particularly in honor of the goddess Durga. |  Example 1:  We celebrated Navaratri with fasting and prayers.  我们通过禁食和祈祷来庆祝九夜节。

Example 2: The vibrant dances during Navaratri are a sight to behold. 九夜节期间充满活力的舞蹈是一道美丽的风景。|Synonym:  Durga Puja (specifically for the Durga aspect),  Sharad Navaratri (for the autumnal Navaratri)| Antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym for a festival)|noun
