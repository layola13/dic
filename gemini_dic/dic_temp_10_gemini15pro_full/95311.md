incatenation:/ɪnˌkætəˈneɪʃn/ | n. |连接，串联，连锁|The act of linking things together in a series or chain.  The concatenation of events led to the disaster.  事件的连锁导致了这场灾难。|例句：The poem is a concatenation of beautiful images. 这首诗是由一连串美丽的意象串联而成。

例句：The concatenation of unfortunate events led to his downfall. 一系列不幸事件的连锁导致了他的垮台。

近义词：linking, joining, chaining, connection, series |反义词: separation, division |词性:noun
