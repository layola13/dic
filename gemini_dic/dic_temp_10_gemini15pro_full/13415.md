atomology:/ætəˈmɒlədʒi/ |n.| 原子论；原子学 |The study of atoms, their structure, and their behavior.  The study of the nature of atoms and the way in which they combine. |  He's studying atomology in his physics class. 他在物理课上学习原子论。|  Atomic theory, atomic physics |  Molecular theory | noun
Etymology: From the Greek ἄτομος (átomos, “indivisible”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
