consortia's:/kənˈsɔːrʃəz/
|n.|财团的，协会的|The possessive form of "consortia," indicating ownership or association with a group of companies or organizations working together. | The consortia's proposal was accepted by the government. 财团的提案被政府接受了。|近义词:syndicate's, alliance's, coalition's|反义词:individual's, company's|词性:名词所有格

consortium:/kənˈsɔːrtiəm/
|n.|财团，协会|A group of companies or organizations working together. | The consortium was formed to build the new bridge.  该财团成立的目的是建造新的桥梁。|近义词:syndicate, alliance, coalition|反义词:individual, company|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *consortium*, from *consors* ("sharing, partner"), from *con-* ("together") + *sors* ("lot, fate").
