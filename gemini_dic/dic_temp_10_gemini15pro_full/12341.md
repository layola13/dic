ash's:/ˈæʃɪz/| n.|灰烬，骨灰|Ash's is the possessive form of ash, referring to something belonging to or associated with ash.  It can refer to the remains of something burnt, or metaphorically to the remnants of something destroyed or lost.|These are the ash's of my dreams. 这些是我梦想的灰烬。|近义词：remains, cinders, embers|反义词: none|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *æsc, from Proto-Germanic *askō, from Proto-Indo-European *os- (“to burn”).
