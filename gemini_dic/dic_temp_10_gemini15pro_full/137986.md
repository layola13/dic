nonzoologically:/ˌnɒnˌzuːəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
非动物学地
not in a zoological manner; without relation to zoology.
His research approached the subject nonzoologically, focusing on the cultural significance of animals rather than their biological classification.  他从非动物学的角度研究这个主题，重点关注动物的文化意义，而不是它们的生物学分类。
He categorized the animals nonzoologically. 他以非动物学的方式对动物进行了分类。

Synonyms: non-biologically, non-scientifically
Antonyms: zoologically, biologically

Etymology:
non- (prefix) : not
zoological (adjective): relating to the study of animals
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
