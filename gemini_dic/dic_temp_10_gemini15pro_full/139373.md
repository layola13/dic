obsequent:/ˈɒbsɪkwənt/ /ɑːbˈsiːkwənt/| adj.| 顺从的，服从的； 随之发生的|Following or resulting from something that happened or was said before; compliant or obedient.|例句：The king's obsequious courtiers quickly agreed with his every whim. 国王那些卑躬屈膝的朝臣们对他的每一个奇想都表示赞同。

例句：The market crash was obsequent to the housing bubble. 股市崩盘是房地产泡沫破灭的结果。|近义词：compliant, obedient, subservient, ductile, malleable|反义词：rebellious, defiant, recalcitrant, disobedient|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin obsequentem (nominative obsequens), present participle of obsequi (“to comply with, obey, yield to”), from ob- (“towards”) + sequi (“to follow”).
