dogtooth:/ˈdɒɡtuːθ/ (UK), /ˈdɑːɡtuːθ/ (US)| n. adj.|狗牙；犬齿；（建筑）锯齿饰；（植物）一种百合科植物|A pointed canine tooth, especially of a dog. Also, a type of architectural ornament resembling such a tooth, and a kind of wildflower.|The dog bared its dogteeth in a menacing snarl. 那条狗露出了犬齿，发出威胁的咆哮。|Synonyms:canine tooth, eyetooth,  cuspid (tooth),  dog's-tooth violet (plant)|Antonyms: none (for tooth), cultivated flower (for plant)|noun, adjective


dogtooth:/ˈdɒɡtuːθ/ (UK), /ˈdɑːɡtuːθ/ (US)| adj.|锯齿状的|Having a sharp, pointed edge like a dog's tooth.|The roofline had a distinctive dogtooth design. 屋顶线条有独特的锯齿状设计。|Synonyms: serrated, jagged, toothed|Antonyms: smooth, rounded|adjective
