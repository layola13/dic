nondependent:/ˌnɑːndɪˈpɛndənt/ | adj. | 非依赖的，自主的 | Not dependent; not relying on others for support or survival.  |  She is a strong, nondependent woman who built her business from the ground up. 她是一位坚强独立的女性，白手起家创办了自己的企业。 |  independent, self-reliant, autonomous | dependent, reliant | adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
dependent (adjective): from Late Latin *dependens, present participle of dependere "to hang down, hang from, depend on," from de- "down, off" + pendere "to hang, weigh"
