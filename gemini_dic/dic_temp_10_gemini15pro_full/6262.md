amole: /əˈmoʊli/ (US), /əˈməʊli/ (UK)| n. | 阿莫勒肥皂；龙舌兰属植物| A soap made from the roots of certain agave plants, especially Agave americana, or the plant itself.  The plant is also used for various other purposes, including making fiber and alcoholic beverages.|  Example:  The women used amole to wash their clothes in the river.  妇女们用阿莫勒肥皂在河里洗衣服。

Example: The amole plant is native to Mexico and the southwestern United States.  阿莫勒植物原产于墨西哥和美国西南部。 |Synonym: agave, soap plant | Antonym: N/A | noun
