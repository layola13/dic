healthinesses:/ˈhɛlθinɪsɪz/
|n.|健康状态（复数）|The state of being healthy, often used in the plural to refer to multiple instances or aspects of health.  It can refer to the general state of someone's physical and mental well-being, or to specific aspects of health, such as the healthiness of a diet or lifestyle.|The healthinesses of their diets varied considerably. 他们的饮食健康状况差异很大。|Synonyms: health, well-being, fitness, soundness|Antonyms: unhealthiness, illness, sickness, infirmity|Noun
