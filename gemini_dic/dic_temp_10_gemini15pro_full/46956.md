dars: /dɑːrz/ | n. | lessons; lectures; courses (plural of darś)|  Lessons or lectures, particularly in a traditional or religious context. Primarily used in South Asian languages like Hindi, Urdu, and Persian, often transliterated into English in this form.| He attended all his dars diligently. 他认真地上了所有的课。| synonyms: classes, lessons, lectures, tutorials | antonyms: breaks, recesses | noun
