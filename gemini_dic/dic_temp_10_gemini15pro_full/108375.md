law:/lɔː/ (British) /lɑː/ (American)| n.|法律，法规；定律，规律；法则；诉讼；司法界|A system of rules created and enforced through social or governmental institutions to regulate behavior, with its precise definition a matter of ongoing debate. It is also used to refer to a statement of fact deduced from observation, to the field of study, and to the profession.|The law requires everyone to wear a seatbelt.  法律要求每个人都系安全带。|Synonyms: rule, regulation, statute, ordinance, principle, decree|Antonyms: anarchy, chaos, lawlessness, disorder|noun
