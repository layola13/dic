goodnight:/ɡʊdˈnaɪt/ /ɡʊdˈnaɪt/| n. int.|晚安；告别语|A polite expression used when parting from someone at night or before going to bed. |Goodnight, sleep tight. 晚安，睡个好觉。|Synonyms: sleep well, sweet dreams|Antonyms: good morning|interjection, noun
