misdeserve:/ˌmɪsdɪˈzɜːrv/
verb
应受惩罚；不配得到
To receive something undeserved, usually something bad.
He misdeserved the punishment he received. 他受到的惩罚是他不应得的。
近义词:unmerit, be unworthy of
反义词:deserve, merit
词性:v.


Etymology:
From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + deserve.
