boars:/bɔːrz/
| n. |野猪（boar 的复数形式）|Plural form of boar, referring to multiple male wild pigs.|The boars roamed the forest in search of food. 野猪在森林里四处觅食。|
|例句：The hunters tracked the boars through the dense undergrowth. 猎人追踪野猪穿过茂密的灌木丛。|近义词：hogs, swine|反义词：(none specific, possibly domesticated pigs)|词性:noun
boars:/bɔːrz/
| n. |公猪（boar 的复数形式，尤指未阉割的）|Plural form of boar, particularly referring to multiple uncastrated male domestic pigs.|The farmer kept several boars for breeding. 农场主养了几头公猪用来繁殖。|
