medal:/ˈmedl/ (US), /ˈmɛdl/ (UK)| n.|奖牌，勋章|A piece of metal, typically disc-shaped, bearing an inscription or design, awarded as a prize or commemoration.|She won a gold medal at the Olympics. 她赢得了奥运会金牌。|Synonyms: award, prize, decoration, trophy|Antonyms: forfeit, penalty|noun

medal:/ˈmedl/ (US), /ˈmɛdl/ (UK)| v.|授予奖牌|To award a medal to.|He was medalled for his bravery. 他因勇敢而被授予奖牌。|Synonyms: honor, decorate, reward|Antonyms: strip, dishonor|verb
