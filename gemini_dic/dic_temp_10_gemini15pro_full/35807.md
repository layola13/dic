cockeyed:/ˈkɒkiˌaɪd/ /ˈkɑːkiˌaɪd/| adj.|歪斜的，不正的，荒谬的|He gave me a cockeyed look. 他给了我一个古怪的眼神。|例句：She wore her hat at a cockeyed angle. 她把帽子戴歪了。|近义词：askew, crooked, lopsided, awry|反义词:straight, even|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From cock (歪斜的) + eyed (眼睛的) + -ness (名词后缀).  "Cock" in this sense likely originated from the idea of a gun's hammer being half-cocked or improperly positioned.
