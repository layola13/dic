finickier:/ˈfɪnɪkiər/ /ˈfɪnɪkɪər/ | adj. | 过分讲究的，挑剔的 | Excessively particular, difficult to please; fussy. |  He's become so finickier about his food lately. 他最近对食物变得非常挑剔。|  She's a finickier eater than her brother. 她比她哥哥更挑食。| 近义词: fussy, picky, choosy, fastidious | 反义词: undemanding, easygoing, tolerant | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From *finick*, from *finicking*, present participle of *finick* "to do something in a dainty or fastidious manner," perhaps from *fine* (adj.) + *-ick*, a frequentative suffix.
