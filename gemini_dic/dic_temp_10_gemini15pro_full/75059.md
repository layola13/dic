frightens:/ˈfraɪtnz/| v. |使惊吓，使害怕| To cause fear or alarm in (someone).| The loud noise frightens the baby. 巨大的噪音吓到了婴儿。| The scary movie frightens me.  这部恐怖电影吓到我了。|近义词：scares, alarms, terrifies|反义词：comforts, reassures, calms|词性:动词

frighten:/ˈfraɪtn/| v. |使惊吓，使害怕| To cause fear or alarm in (someone).|  Don't frighten the birds away. 不要把鸟吓跑。| The thunder frightened the dog. 雷声吓到了狗。|近义词：scare, alarm, terrify|反义词：comfort, reassure, calm|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *frightenen, from Old English *fryhtnian (“to frighten”), from Proto-Germanic *frihtjaną (“to frighten”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)preigʰ- (“to jump, start”). Cognate with Dutch *frijten (“to frighten”), German *friten (“to frighten”).
