obturate:/ˈɒbtjʊreɪt/ (US) /ˈɒbtʃəreɪt/ (UK)| v. | 阻塞，封闭 | To close or block up (an opening, orifice, or passage). | The dentist obturated the cavity with a filling. 牙医用填充物封闭了蛀牙洞。 | The surgeon obturated the wound to prevent infection.  外科医生封闭伤口以防止感染。| 近义词: close, block, occlude, seal | 反义词: open, unblock |词性: 动词

obturator:/ˈɒbtjʊreɪtər/ (US) /ˈɒbtʃəreɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 阻塞器，闭塞物 | A device or substance used to close or block an opening. | The dentist used an obturator to fill the cavity. 牙医用闭塞器填补了蛀牙。|  A heart valve obturator is used during surgery. 心脏瓣膜闭塞器在手术中使用。| 近义词: plug, stopper, seal | 反义词: opener |词性: 名词
