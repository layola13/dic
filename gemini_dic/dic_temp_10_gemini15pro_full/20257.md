birthday: /ˈbɜːrθdeɪ/ (UK) /ˈbɜːrθdeɪ/ (US)| n.|生日|The anniversary of the day on which a person was born.|It's my birthday today! 今天是我的生日！|近义词：anniversary|反义词: deathday (不常用，更正式的说法是 date of death)
词性: 名词
