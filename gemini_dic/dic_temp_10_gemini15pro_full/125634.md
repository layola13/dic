mumblingly:/ˈmʌmblɪŋli/| adv.|含糊地，咕哝地|In a low, indistinct, and often unclear manner, as if muttering to oneself.|She answered mumblingly, too embarrassed to speak clearly. 她含糊地回答，羞愧得说不出话来。|例句：He mumblingly agreed to the terms. 他咕哝着同意了这些条款。|例句：She mumblingly apologized for being late. 她咕哝着道歉迟到了。|近义词：inarticulately, indistinctly, mutteredly|反义词：clearly, distinctly, articulately|词性:副词
