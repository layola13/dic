arnee: /ˈɑːrniː/
n.
野水牛，印度野牛

The arnee is a powerful and dangerous animal.  印度野牛是一种强壮而危险的动物。
The photographer captured a stunning image of an arnee in its natural habitat. 摄影师捕捉到了印度野牛在其自然栖息地的惊人图像。

近义词:wild buffalo, wild ox, gaur
