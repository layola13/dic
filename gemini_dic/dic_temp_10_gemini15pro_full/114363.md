majorette:/ˌmeɪdʒəˈret/ | n. | 女鼓乐队指挥，女乐队队长 | A female member of a marching band, especially a high school or college band, who twirls a baton and leads the band. |  The majorette twirled her baton with impressive skill.  这位女鼓乐队指挥以令人印象深刻的技巧旋转着她的指挥棒。

The majorette led the band onto the field. 女乐队队长带领乐队进入场地。 | Synonyms: drum major, baton twirler | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
