ectomorph:/ˈɛktəmɔːrf/| n. |瘦型体质者|A person with a lean and delicate body build characterized by long limbs and a fast metabolism, often having difficulty gaining weight.|He's a classic ectomorph – tall, thin, and struggles to put on muscle. 他是典型的瘦型体质——个子高，瘦，很难长肌肉。|Synonym: skinny, slender, lean, lanky|Antonym: endomorph, mesomorph|noun
