ferally:/ˈfɪrəli/ | adv. | 野蛮地，凶猛地 | In a wild or untamed manner; like a wild animal. | The cat, abandoned in the woods, began to behave ferally.  那只被遗弃在树林里的猫开始表现得像野兽一样。 | Examples:


The dog, usually so gentle, snarled ferally at the intruder.  这只通常很温顺的狗，对着入侵者凶猛地咆哮。


He looked at me ferally, his eyes full of rage. 他凶狠地瞪着我，眼里充满了愤怒。


| 近义词：wildly, savagely, fiercely | 反义词：tamely, domestically, gently | 词性：副词
