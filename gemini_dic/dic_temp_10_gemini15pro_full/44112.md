crispiest: /ˈkrɪspɪɪst/ | adj. |最脆的|Superlative form of crispy; having a pleasingly firm, dry, and brittle texture.  It describes something that is extremely crisp. | The crispiest fries are my favorite.  我最喜欢最脆的薯条。| The lettuce was wonderfully crisp and cold. 生菜爽脆冰凉，非常美味。 | synonyms: crunchiest, firmest, brittlemost | antonyms: soggiest, softest, chewiest | adjective
