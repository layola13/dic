oologist:/ɔːˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 鸟类学家 | A scientist who studies birds, their behavior, physiology, classification, and distribution. |  He is a renowned oologist, specializing in the study of raptors. 他是一位著名的鸟类学家，专门研究猛禽。

| Example 1: The oologist spent years studying the migration patterns of the Arctic tern.  这位鸟类学家花了数年时间研究北极燕鸥的迁徙模式。

| Example 2:  The museum hired an oologist to catalog their extensive collection of bird eggs.  博物馆聘请了一位鸟类学家来整理他们大量的鸟蛋收藏。
| Synonym: ornithologist | Antonym: N/A | Noun
