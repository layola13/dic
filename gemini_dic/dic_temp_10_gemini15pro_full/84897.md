hankerers:/ˈhæŋkərərz/
n.
渴望者，追求者
People who have a strong desire for something.
Many hankerers for a simpler life are moving to the countryside. 许多渴望过上更简单生活的人都搬到了乡下。
synonyms: yearners, desirers, longers, cravers
antonyms: contented, satisfied

词源:
From the Middle Dutch hankeren, frequentative of hangen "to hang," hence "to hang about," then "to linger expectantly."
来自中古荷兰语 hankeren，hangen 的重复形式，意思是“悬挂”，因此是“闲逛”，然后是“充满期待地徘徊”。
