antihierarchy:/ˌæntiˈhaɪərɑːrkiː/
|n.|反等级制度|A system or organization that opposes or rejects hierarchy, typically advocating for equality and shared power. |The antihierarchy movement gained momentum in the 1960s. 反等级制度运动在20世纪60年代获得了发展势头。|
The company embraced antihierarchy, empowering employees at all levels.  这家公司信奉反等级制度，赋予各级员工权力。|
近义词: egalitarianism|
反义词: hierarchy|
词性: noun

Etymology:
"anti-" (Greek) meaning "against, opposite of" + "hierarchy" (from Greek "hierarchia," meaning "rule of a high priest")
