azurite:/ˈæʒəˌraɪt/ | adj. | 天蓝色的，蔚蓝色的 (Rarely used) | Having the color of azurite; deep blue. | The azurite sky shimmered above the desert.  蔚蓝的天空在沙漠上闪耀。（此用法较罕见）| Synonym: azure, cerulean | Antonym: N/A | adjective
