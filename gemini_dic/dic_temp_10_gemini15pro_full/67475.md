extort:/ɪkˈstɔːrt/ | vt. | 勒索，敲诈 | to obtain something by force or threats | He extorted money from the old lady by threatening to reveal her secrets. 他以揭露她的秘密为要挟，向老太太勒索钱财。|近义词：wrest, extract, blackmail |反义词:donate, give |动词
