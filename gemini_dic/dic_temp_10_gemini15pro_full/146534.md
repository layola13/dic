overglamorizes: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməraɪzɪz/
verb
过分美化，过分渲染
Makes something seem more glamorous or exciting than it actually is.
The movie overglamorizes the life of a rock star, showing only the parties and fame, not the hard work and struggles. 这部电影过分美化了摇滚明星的生活，只展现了派对和名声，而没有展现他们的辛勤工作和奋斗。
The advertisement overglamorizes the product, making it seem like a miracle cure.  这则广告过分美化了该产品，使其看起来像是一种神奇的疗法。
Synonyms: romanticizes, idealizes, exaggerates, glorifies
Antonyms: downplays, understates, de-emphasizes, diminishes
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + glamorize + -s (third person singular present tense)
"Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much."
"Glamorize" comes from "glamour" (meaning "magic, enchantment") + "-ize" (a suffix that forms verbs meaning "to make or become").
