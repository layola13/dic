melanated: /ˈmɛləˌneɪtɪd/
adjective
有黑色素的；肤色黝黑的
Having melanin; dark-skinned.
例句：She praised the beauty of melanated skin. 她赞美了黝黑肤色的美丽。

例句：Melanated people have a natural protection against the sun. 肤色黝黑的人对阳光有天然的保护。

近义词：dark-skinned, Black, African American (in some contexts), brown-skinned
反义词：pale, light-skinned

词性: adjective
