nomarch:/ˈnəʊmɑːrk/ | n. | 省长，方伯 | A governor of a nome (province) in ancient Egypt. |  The nomarch controlled the province's resources and acted as a local representative of the pharaoh.  | 例句：The nomarch collected taxes and oversaw irrigation projects. 省长征税并监督灌溉工程。

| 例句：The powerful nomarch ruled his nome like a king.  这位强大的省长像国王一样统治着他的省份。 | 近义词：governor, provincial governor, prefect | 反义词：subject, citizen | 词性：noun

Etymology:  From Ancient Greek νόμαρχος (nómarkhos), from νόμος (nómos, “district, province”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule”).
