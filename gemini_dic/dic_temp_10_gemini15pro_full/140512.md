oleaginously:/ˌoʊliˈædʒɪnəsli/ | adj. | 油腻地；油滑地；谄媚地 | Excessively smooth, suave, or ingratiating; unctuous. | He spoke in an oleaginous tone, trying to flatter his boss. 他说话油腔滑调的，试图奉承他的老板。| The salesman's oleaginous manner made me distrust him.  这个销售员油滑的态度让我不信任他。 | 近义词: unctuous, greasy, smooth, suave, ingratiating | 反义词: sincere, genuine, honest, straightforward | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *oleaginosus, from Latin olea (“olive”) + -ginosus (akin to English -genous).
