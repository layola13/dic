obstacle:/ˈɒbstəkəl/ (UK) /ˈɑːbstəkəl/ (US)| n.|障碍，阻碍，妨碍|Something that blocks or hinders progress or movement.|例句：The biggest obstacle to their plan was lack of money.  他们计划的最大障碍是缺钱。

例句：Her disability proved no obstacle to her success. 她的残疾并没有妨碍她的成功。


近义词：barrier, hindrance, impediment, obstruction|反义词:aid, assistance, help|词性:noun
