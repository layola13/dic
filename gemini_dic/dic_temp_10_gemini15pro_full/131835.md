nonconsonance:/nɑːnˈkɑːnsənəns/
|n.|不和谐，不一致|Lack of harmony or agreement; dissonance.|The nonconsonance between her words and actions made her seem untrustworthy. 她的言行不一让她显得不可信。|
|近义词：discord, dissonance, disagreement, incongruity, inconsistency|
|反义词：consonance, harmony, agreement, concord|
|词性：名词|
|词源：non- (not) + consonance (agreement)|
