parafollicular:/ˌpærəfɒˈlɪkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌpærəfɑːˈlɪkjələr/ | adj.|滤泡旁的；滤泡周围的|Located beside or around a follicle, especially a thyroid follicle.|The parafollicular cells of the thyroid gland produce calcitonin.  甲状腺的滤泡旁细胞产生降钙素。|
近义词:juxtafollicular|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective

parafollicular:/ˌpærəfɒˈlɪkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌpærəfɑːˈlɪkjələr/ | n.|滤泡旁细胞|A cell located beside or around a follicle, especially a C cell of the thyroid gland.|Parafollicular cells secrete calcitonin, a hormone that lowers blood calcium levels. 滤泡旁细胞分泌降钙素，一种降低血液中钙水平的激素。|
近义词:C cell|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From para- (beside, near) + follicle + -ar (relating to).  "Para-" comes from Greek, "follicle" comes from Latin folliculus (small bag), and "-ar" is a Latin suffix. 来自para-（旁边，附近）+ follicle（滤泡）+ -ar（与...有关）。 “Para-”来自希腊语，“follicle”来自拉丁语folliculus（小袋），而“-ar”是拉丁语后缀。
