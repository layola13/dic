felini:/fɛˈliːni/ | adj. | 费里尼式的; 费里尼风格的 | Characteristic of or resembling the films of Federico Fellini, especially in their dreamlike or fantastical quality. |  The film had a distinctly Feliniesque atmosphere, full of bizarre characters and surreal imagery.  这部电影有一种明显的费里尼式氛围，充满了奇异的角色和超现实的意象。 | Example: The movie's dreamlike sequences and grotesque characters felt very Felini. 这部电影如梦似幻的场景和怪诞的角色让人感觉很有费里尼风格。 | Synonym: surreal, dreamlike, fantastical | Antonym: realistic, mundane | word type:adjective
