misgivingly:/mɪsˈɡɪvɪŋli/
adverb
心存疑虑地，不放心地
With doubt or apprehension; uneasily.
She accepted the offer misgivingly, unsure if it was the right decision. 她心存疑虑地接受了这份工作，不确定这是否是正确的决定。
He glanced misgivingly at the dark clouds gathering overhead. 他忧心忡忡地瞥了一眼头顶上聚集的乌云。
近义词:apprehensively, doubtfully, hesitantly, uneasily
反义词:confidently, assuredly, certainly
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From misgiving + -ly.  Misgiving itself comes from the verb 'misgive', meaning to fill with doubt or apprehension.  'Mis-' is a prefix indicating something wrong or amiss, while 'give' in this context relates to the feeling or impression something produces.
