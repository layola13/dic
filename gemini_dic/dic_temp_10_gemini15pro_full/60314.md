ego-surfing:/ˈiːɡoʊ sɜːrfɪŋ/
n.
自我搜索；网络自恋
The act of searching for oneself on the Internet, typically using a search engine to find mentions of one's own name.
He spends hours ego-surfing, looking for articles and blog posts that mention him. 他花几个小时在网上搜索自己，寻找提到他的文章和博客帖子。
vanity searching, self-googling
