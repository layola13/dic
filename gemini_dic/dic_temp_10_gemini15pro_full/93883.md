immoral:/ɪˈmɔːrəl/ | adj. |不道德的，邪恶的 | Not conforming to accepted standards of morality. |  His actions were considered immoral by many. 他的行为被许多人认为是不道德的。

It's immoral to steal. 偷窃是不道德的。


| 近义词：unethical, wicked, sinful, wrong | 反义词：moral, ethical, righteous, virtuous | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immoralis, from in- "not" + moralis "moral."
