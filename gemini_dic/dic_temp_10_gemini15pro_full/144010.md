outrebounding:/ˌaʊtrɪˈbaʊndɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
抢篮板球超过（对手）
Outrebounding means to grab more rebounds than the opposing team in a basketball game.
The Lakers were outrebounding the Celtics by a large margin. 湖人队的篮板球数量远远超过了凯尔特人队。
Golden State Warriors outrebounded Cleveland Cavaliers in game 3. 金州勇士队在第三场比赛中篮板球超过了克利夫兰骑士队。
近义词: out-rebounding, dominating the boards
反义词: being outrebounded
词性: verb
