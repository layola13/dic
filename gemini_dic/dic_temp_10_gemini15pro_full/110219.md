lifeboats:/ˈlaɪfbəʊts/ (UK) /ˈlaɪfboʊts/ (US)| n.|救生艇|A small boat kept on a ship for use in emergencies, especially if the ship is sinking.|The crew launched the lifeboats as the ship began to list. 船开始倾斜时，船员们放下了救生艇。|synonyms: life raft, rescue boat, dinghy|antonyms: ship, vessel|noun
