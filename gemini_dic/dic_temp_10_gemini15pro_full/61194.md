elutriate:/ɪˈluːtriˌeɪt/
verb
洗净；漂洗；淘洗
to remove (less dense material) from a mixture by washing or decantation.  To purify or separate by washing.
The chemist elutriated the precipitate to remove impurities. 化学家洗涤沉淀物以去除杂质。
The archaeologist carefully elutriated the soil samples to recover tiny artifacts. 考古学家仔细淘洗土壤样本，以回收微小的文物。
synonyms: wash, rinse, cleanse, purify, separate, decant
antonyms: contaminate, pollute, combine, mix
verb

elutriate:/ɪˈluːtriˌeɪt/
noun
洗净；漂洗；淘洗
The act of elutriating.
The elutriation of the soil revealed several ancient coins. 对土壤的淘洗发现了几枚古钱币。
synonyms: washing, rinsing, cleansing, purification, separation, decantation
antonyms: contamination, pollution, combination, mixture
noun
