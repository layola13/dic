lopes:/ləʊps/| v.|大步慢跑，轻快地大步跑|The wolf lopes across the snowy field. 狼在雪地上轻快地大步跑。|例句：The horse lopes gracefully across the meadow.  马优雅地在草地上慢跑。

例句：She loped along the beach, enjoying the fresh air. 她沿着海滩慢跑，享受着新鲜空气。|近义词：runs, trots, canters, gallops|反义词:crawls, creeps|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lopen, from Old Norse *hlaupa (“to leap, run”), from Proto-Germanic *hlaupaną (“to leap, run”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lew- (“to slip, flow”). Cognate with Dutch lopen (“to walk, run”), German laufen (“to run”), Gothic  hlaupan (“to leap, run”).
