nonabstemious:/ˌnɒnəbˈstiːmiəs/
adjective
不节制的，放纵的
Not abstemious; indulging freely in pleasures, especially food and drink.
He led a nonabstemious life, indulging in rich food and fine wine. 他过着放纵的生活，沉迷于丰富的食物和美酒。
近义词: indulgent, intemperate, immoderate
反义词: abstemious, temperate, moderate
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
abstemious (from Latin abstemius): sparing or moderate in eating and drinking
