chab:/tʃæb/| n. |小子，小伙子（主要用于苏格兰英语）|A young man; a lad; a boy. |例句：The chab was eager to prove himself.  这个小伙子急于证明自己。

例句：Did you see that chab trying to impress the lassies? 你看到那个小伙子试图给姑娘们留下深刻印象吗？|近义词：lad, boy, fellow, chap|反义词:man, adult|词性:名词

词源:
Likely a shortened form of the word "chappie," which itself is a diminutive of "chap," meaning fellow or man.  Ultimately derived from the Old French "chap," meaning jaw or cheek.
