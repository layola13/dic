broadscale:/ˈbrɔːdskeɪl/
adjective
大规模的; 广泛的
Relating to or involving a large area or a wide range of things.
The broadscale destruction of the rainforest is alarming.  亚马逊雨林的大规模破坏令人担忧。
Broadscale changes are needed to address climate change.  需要大范围的变革来应对气候变化。
synonym: extensive, widespread, large-scale, wide-ranging
antonym: limited, restricted, small-scale, localized
词性: adjective
