carlings:/ˈkɑːrlɪŋz/ | n. |炒豌豆，通常在四旬斋前食用|Dried peas, typically eaten before Lent.|Carlings were traditionally eaten on Carling Sunday.|近义词: peas, dried peas |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Old Norse *karlingr, meaning "old woman," possibly referring to the shriveled appearance of the dried peas.  Related to "carl," meaning "man," but with a feminine suffix.  The connection between "old woman" and peas is unclear, but may relate to a folk belief or ritual.
