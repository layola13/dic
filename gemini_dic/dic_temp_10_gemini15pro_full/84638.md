hands-on:/ˌhændzˈɒn/ /ˌhændzˈɑːn/ | adj. |亲身实践的，亲自动手的| involving or requiring personal experience or participation; practical rather than theoretical |  I learned a lot from the hands-on training. 我从实践训练中学到了很多。

|例句：
The museum offers hands-on exhibits for children.  这家博物馆为孩子们提供可以亲自动手的展品。

|例句：
She has a hands-on approach to management. 她采用亲力亲为的管理方式。


|近义词：practical, active, involved, experiential |反义词:theoretical, passive, uninvolved |词性:形容词
