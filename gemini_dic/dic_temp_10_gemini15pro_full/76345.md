gadabout:/ˈɡæd.əˌbaʊt/ | n. | 闲逛者；游荡者 | A person who goes to many different places for pleasure and social activity.  A restless seeker of social activity and pleasure. | She's a real gadabout – always off to parties and events. 她是一个真正的社交蝴蝶——总是参加各种派对和活动。|  He was known as a gadabout, flitting from one social gathering to the next. 他以游荡者而闻名，从一个社交聚会飞到另一个社交聚会。 | synonyms: social butterfly, party animal, jet-setter, globe-trotter | antonyms: homebody, stay-at-home, recluse | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *gad* (to wander, roam) + *about*.  The verb "to gad" is now archaic, but the noun "gadabout" remains in use.
