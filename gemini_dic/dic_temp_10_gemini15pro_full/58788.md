dunsmere:/ˈdʌnzmɪər/
n.
昏暗的沼泽地；阴暗的湖泊
a dark, gloomy mere or lake.
The mists swirling over the dunsmere gave it an eerie atmosphere.  昏暗的沼泽地上弥漫着薄雾，给人一种阴森的气氛。
The old boat slowly drifted across the dunsmere.  那艘旧船慢慢地漂过阴暗的湖泊。
近义词:bog, marsh, swamp, fen
反义词:bright meadow, sunny glade
词性:名词


词源:
'dun' (dark, gloomy) + 'mere' (lake, pool)
