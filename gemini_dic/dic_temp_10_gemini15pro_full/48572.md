defaults:/dɪˈfɔːlts/ | n. | 违约；默认值；预设值 |  Failure to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in a court of law.  A preselected option adopted by a computer program or other mechanism when no alternative is specified by the user or programmer. | The company defaulted on its loan payments.  这家公司拖欠了贷款。| synonyms: nonpayment, failure to pay, bad debt,  inaction, neglect, omission, dereliction | antonyms: payment, fulfillment, compliance | noun

defaults:/dɪˈfɔːlts/ | v. |  违约；默认；预设；不履行义务 | Fail to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in a court of law. (computing) (Of a computer program or other mechanism) automatically select (a preselected option) if no alternative is specified by the user or programmer. | He defaulted on his mortgage. 他拖欠了抵押贷款。| synonyms: fail to pay, renege on, go back on, back out of | antonyms: honor, fulfill | verb
