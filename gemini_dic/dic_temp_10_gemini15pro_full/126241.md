mycologists:/ˌmaɪˈkɒlədʒɪsts/ /ˌmaɪˈkɑːlədʒɪsts/
n.
真菌学家
A scientist who studies fungi.
Mycologists are studying the potential of fungi in medicine. 真菌学家正在研究真菌在医学中的潜力。
The mycologists identified a new species of mushroom. 真菌学家鉴定了一种新的蘑菇种类。
synonyms:fungi expert, fungi researcher
antonyms: N/A
