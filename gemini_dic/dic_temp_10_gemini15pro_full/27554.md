canadians:/kəˈneɪdiənz/
n.
加拿大人
People who live in or originate from Canada.
Canadians are known for their politeness.  加拿大人以其礼貌而闻名。
Many Canadians enjoy hockey. 许多加拿大人喜欢曲棍球。
近义词:Canucks (informal, sometimes derogatory)
反义词:(None - requires a specific nationality for antonym)
词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Canada + -ian (suffix forming adjectives and nouns denoting origin, affiliation, or adherence).  Canada itself comes from the St. Lawrence Iroquoian word *kanata meaning "village" or "settlement."
