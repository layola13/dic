nondisciplinary:/ˌnɒndɪsəˈplɪnəri/
adjective
非学科的；跨学科的
Not limited to a single academic discipline or field of study; interdisciplinary.
This course takes a nondisciplinary approach, drawing on insights from sociology, history, and psychology. 这门课程采用跨学科方法，借鉴了社会学、历史学和心理学的见解。
The university offers a nondisciplinary program in cultural studies. 这所大学提供文化研究方面的跨学科课程。

synonyms: interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary, multidisciplinary, transdisciplinary
antonyms: disciplinary, specialized, subject-specific
词性: adjective
