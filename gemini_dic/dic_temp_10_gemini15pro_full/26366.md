by-halves:/baɪ hɑːvz/
| adv.|不完全地；敷衍地；半途而废地|Doing something by halves means doing it incompletely or poorly, without putting in full effort.|He studied for the exam by halves and, unsurprisingly, failed. 他考试准备得不充分，不出所料地挂了科。|half-heartedly, perfunctorily, superficially|thoroughly, completely, wholeheartedly|adverb
Etymology:
From "by" (meaning "by means of") + "halves" (plural of "half"). The sense is of doing something using only half of one's effort or attention.
