dither:/ˈdɪðər/ (US), /ˈdɪðə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 犹豫，踌躇，颤动|A state of indecision or nervous excitement.|例句：She was in a dither about what to wear. 她对穿什么衣服感到很紧张。|近义词：hesitation, indecision, wavering|反义词：decision, resolution|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *didderen, frequentative of an unknown verb.  Possibly related to Old Norse *titra "to tremble."
