bipectinate:/baɪˈpɛktɪˌneɪt/
adjective
双栉状的；两侧有梳齿状的
Having two sets of comb-like teeth or projections, one on each side of an axis.  Resembling a feather with barbs arranged on both sides of a central shaft.
The bipectinate antennae of the moth are quite noticeable.  这只蛾子的双栉状触角非常引人注目。
Near synonyms: pectinate, comb-like, feathery
Antonyms: smooth, unbranched
词性: adjective
