grinspoon:/ˈɡrɪnspuːn/
n.
鬼脸蘑菇（一种迷幻蘑菇）
A type of mushroom (Psilocybe semilanceata), also known as liberty cap, containing the psychoactive compound psilocybin and known for its hallucinogenic effects.
He found some grinspoons growing in the field. 他在田野里发现了一些鬼脸蘑菇。
Synonyms: liberty cap, magic mushroom, psilocybe semilanceata
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "non-psychoactive mushroom")
