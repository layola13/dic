anaglyptograph:/ˈænəˌɡlɪptəˌɡræf/ | n. | 浮雕; 立体照片; |A picture or design carved in low relief.  A process of producing stereoscopic effects by superimposing two slightly different images of the same subject, typically printed in contrasting colors (such as red and green or red and blue) and viewed through spectacles with correspondingly colored lenses. | The museum displayed an anaglyptograph of the ancient city.  博物馆展出了一幅古城的浮雕。| synonym:  bas-relief, cameo, intaglio | antonym:  excision, engraving | noun

anaglyptographic:/ˌænəˌɡlɪptəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 浮雕的; 立体照片的; |Relating to or produced by anaglyptography. |  He used anaglyptographic techniques to create a 3D image. 他使用立体照片技术创建了一个3D图像。 | synonym:  stereoscopic, 3D | antonym:  2D, flat | adjective
