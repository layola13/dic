ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alcyōn*, from Ancient Greek ἀλκυών (alkuṓn, “kingfisher”).  The ancient Greeks believed that the halcyon (kingfisher) nested at sea during the winter solstice, and that the sea remained calm during this period, hence the association with peace and tranquility.
