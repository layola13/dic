nihility:/naɪˈhɪləti/ /nɪˈhɪləti/| n.|虚无；空虚；不存在；无效|The concept of nihility is central to existentialist philosophy. 虚无的概念是存在主义哲学的核心。|例句：

The vastness of space conveys a sense of nihility.
浩瀚的宇宙给人一种虚无的感觉。

He felt a sense of nihility after his life's work was destroyed.
他毕生的工作被毁后感到一阵虚无。


|近义词：nothingness, void, emptiness, nonexistence|反义词:existence, being, reality|词性:noun
