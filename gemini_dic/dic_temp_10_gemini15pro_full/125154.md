multiarticulate: /ˌmʌltiɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/ | adj. | 多关节的；多节的 | Having many joints or articulations. |  The multiarticulate robot arm can perform complex tasks.  多关节机器人手臂可以执行复杂的任务。 | multijointed, polyarticular | simple, single-jointed | adjective

Etymology:
From "multi-" (many) + "articulate" (jointed). "Articulate" comes from Latin "articulatus," past participle of "articulare" (to joint), from "artus" (joint).
