charitableness:/ˈtʃærɪtəblnəs/
n.
仁慈，慈善，宽容
The quality of being charitable; kindness and tolerance in judging others.
He treated his enemies with charitableness.  他以仁慈的态度对待他的敌人。
近义词:benevolence, generosity, kindness, compassion, tolerance, magnanimity
反义词:malice, cruelty, intolerance, meanness
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *chariteablenesse*, equivalent to charitable +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Late Latin *caritativus* (“loving”) + *-ness*.
