mahouts:/məˈhaʊts/| n. |驯象人|A keeper and driver of elephants, especially in South and Southeast Asia.|例句：The mahouts led the elephants in a procession through the streets. 驯象人带领大象穿过街道组成一个队伍。

例句：The mahout skillfully guided his elephant through the dense jungle.  驯象人熟练地引导他的大象穿过茂密的丛林。

|近义词：elephant driver, elephant keeper, elephant handler|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi महाउत (mahāut), from Sanskrit महामात्र (mahāmātra, “high official”).
