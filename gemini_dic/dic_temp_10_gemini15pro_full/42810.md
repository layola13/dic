courage: /ˈkʌrɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ (US)| n. |勇气，胆量|The quality of mind or spirit that enables a person to face difficulty, danger, pain, etc., without fear; bravery.|He showed great courage in the face of danger. 他在危险面前表现出了极大的勇气。|近义词: bravery, valor, boldness, daring, fortitude, nerve, pluck|反义词: fear, cowardice, timidity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *corage "heart, innermost feelings; temper, anger," from Vulgar Latin *coraticum "heart," from Latin cor "heart," from PIE root *kerd- "heart."
