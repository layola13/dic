exhibits:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪts/
n.| 展览品，展品；证据| The museum exhibits a fascinating collection of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆展出了一系列迷人的恐龙化石。| synonyms: displays, shows, presentations | antonyms: concealments, hidings | noun
exhibits:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪts/
v.| 展览，展出；表现出，显出|  He exhibits a remarkable talent for music. 他展现出非凡的音乐天赋。| synonyms: displays, demonstrates, reveals | antonyms: hides, conceals | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exhibitus*, past participle of *exhibere*  "to hold out, show, display," from *ex-* "out" + *habere* "to hold."
