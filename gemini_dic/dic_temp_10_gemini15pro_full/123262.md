monodical:/məˈnɒdɪkəl/
adjective
单调的，单音调的，无变化的
Characterized by monotony or a single, unchanging tone or style; lacking variation.
The monodical chanting of the monks lulled me to sleep.  僧侣们单调的吟唱让我昏昏欲睡。
The speaker's monodical delivery made the lecture incredibly boring. 演讲者单调的演讲让讲座变得无比乏味。
近义词: monotonous, tedious, dull, repetitive, unvaried
反义词: varied, diverse, interesting, stimulating, dynamic
词性: adjective
