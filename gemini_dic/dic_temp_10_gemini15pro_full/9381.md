antiquated:/ˈæn.tɪ.kweɪ.tɪd/ | adj. | 过时的，陈旧的 | Old-fashioned and no longer suitable for modern conditions or needs; obsolete. |  The museum displayed many antiquated tools used by farmers in the 1800s. 博物馆展示了许多农民在 19 世纪使用的过时工具。 |  The company's antiquated computer system needs a major upgrade.  这家公司的过时计算机系统需要进行重大升级。| 近义词: obsolete, outdated, archaic, old-fashioned, outmoded | 反义词: modern, contemporary, current, up-to-date | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *antiquatus*, past participle of *antiquare* (“to make old-fashioned”), from *antiquus* (“old”).
