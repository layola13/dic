emblemed: /ɪmˈblɛmd/ | adj. | 以...为标志的；饰以...纹章的 | Decorated or marked with an emblem; symbolized by an emblem. |  The knight's shield was emblemed with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金狮纹章。| Synonyms: symbolized, represented, marked, decorated | Antonyms: plain, undecorated, unmarked | adjective

emblemed: /ɪmˈblɛmd/ | v. | 以...为标志；饰以...纹章 (通常用被动语态) | To mark or decorate with an emblem; to symbolize with an emblem. | The flag was emblemed with the national bird.  旗帜上饰有国鸟纹章。| Synonyms: symbolized, represented, marked, decorated | Antonyms: plain, undecorated, unmarked | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *emblemmen*, from Old French *emblemmer*, from Late Latin *emblemare*, from Latin *emblema* ("emblem, inlaid work"), from Greek *ἔμβλημα* (*émblēma*) ("something inserted, inlaid ornament"), from *ἐμβάλλειν* (*embállein*) ("to throw in, insert").
