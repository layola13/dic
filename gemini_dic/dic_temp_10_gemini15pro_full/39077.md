confoundable:/kənˈfaʊndəbl/ | adj. | 可混淆的，易混淆的 |  Able to be confused with something else; capable of being confounded.  Specifically used in statistics to describe variables that can distort the apparent relationship between other variables. |  The study's results were confoundable because the researchers didn't control for socioeconomic status.  这项研究的结果是可混淆的，因为研究人员没有控制社会经济地位。| Synonyms:  confusable, mistakable, ambiguous | Antonyms:  distinct, clear, unambiguous | Adjective
