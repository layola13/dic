anticonscription:/ˌæntiˌkənˈskrɪpʃən/
| adj. n.|反征兵的，反征兵运动|Opposed to conscription, the compulsory enlistment of people into military service.  This word describes both a stance and the movement advocating it.|The anticonscription movement gained momentum during the Vietnam War.  反征兵运动在越南战争期间声势浩大。|Synonyms: antidraft, pacifist (adj.), conscientious objector (n.)|Antonyms: pro-conscription, militarist|adjective, noun
anticonscription:/ˌæntiˌkənˈskrɪpʃən/
| n.|反征兵运动|The organized movement opposing conscription. |He joined the anticonscription league. 他加入了反征兵联盟。|Synonyms: antidraft movement, peace movement|Antonyms: pro-conscription movement, recruitment drive|noun
