floaters:/ˈfloʊtərz/ (American) /ˈfləʊtəz/ (British)| n.|飞蚊症，漂浮物|Small spots or specks that drift across the field of vision, often caused by debris in the vitreous humor of the eye.|  I've been seeing floaters in my vision lately. 我最近看到视野里有飞蚊症。

He noticed some strange floaters after looking at the bright sunlight. 他在看了强烈的阳光后注意到一些奇怪的漂浮物。|近义词：specks, spots, cobwebs|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific phenomenon)|词性: Noun
