panivorous:/pəˈnɪvərəs/
adjective
杂食的；无所不吃的
Feeding on all kinds of food; omnivorous.
Panivorous animals thrive in diverse environments. 杂食动物在多样化的环境中茁壮成长。
The pig is a panivorous animal. 猪是一种杂食动物。
omnivorous
herbivorous, carnivorous
adjective
