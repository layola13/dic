neoplastic:/ˌniːəˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 新生组织的，肿瘤的 | Relating to or constituting a neoplasm; characterized by new, abnormal tissue growth. |  The neoplastic cells were rapidly dividing. 肿瘤细胞正在快速分裂。| Synonyms: cancerous, tumorous, malignant | Antonyms: benign, healthy | adjective

neoplasm:/ˈniːəˌplæzəm/ | n. | 瘤，赘生物 |  An abnormal mass of tissue that forms when cells grow and divide more than they should or do not die when they should. Neoplasms may be benign (not cancer) or malignant (cancer). | The doctor discovered a neoplasm on her lung. 医生在她的肺部发现了一个瘤。 | Synonyms: tumor, growth, lesion | Antonyms: healthy tissue | noun
