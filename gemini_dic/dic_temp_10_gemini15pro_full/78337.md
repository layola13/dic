gerundial:/dʒɪˈrʌndiəl/ | adj. |动名词的| Relating to or like a gerund; verbal nouns ending in -ing that function as nouns. |  The gerundial phrase "swimming in the ocean" acts as the subject of the sentence.  动名词短语“在海里游泳”在句中作主语。| Synonyms: verbal, nominal | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider non-verbal, non-nominal) |词性: adjective


Etymology->
From Late Latin *gerundialis, from gerundium (“gerund”) + -alis.  Ultimately from Latin gerundus (“to be carried on”), gerere (“to carry, to bear, to manage, to conduct, to perform”).
