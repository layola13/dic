infinitely:/ˈɪnfɪnɪtli/| adv.|无限地，无穷地|Without limit; endlessly.|The universe is infinitely vast. 宇宙浩瀚无垠。|近义词：endlessly, boundlessly, limitlessly|反义词: finitely, limitedly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *infinitē, from Latin infinitus (“unbounded, unlimited, countless, infinite”), from in- (“not”) + finitus (“limited, bounded”).
