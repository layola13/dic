avoirdupois:/ˌævərdəˈpɔɪz/
| n. |常衡制|A system of weights based on a pound having 16 ounces.  |例句：Most goods are sold by avoirdupois weight. 大多数商品按常衡制出售。|近义词：weight, system of weights|反义词: troy (when referring to precious metals)|noun


avoirdupois:/ˌævərdəˈpɔɪz/
| adj. |常衡的|Relating to the avoirdupois system of weights.  |例句：An avoirdupois pound is equal to 7,000 grains. 常衡制一磅等于7000格令。|近义词：weight-based|反义词: troy (when referring to precious metals)|adjective
