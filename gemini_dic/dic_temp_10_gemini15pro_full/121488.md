mislays:/ˌmɪsˈleɪz/ | v. |放错地方，乱放|To put something in a place and then forget where it is. | I always mislay my keys. 我总是乱放我的钥匙。| I mislaid my glasses and couldn't find them anywhere. 我把眼镜放错了地方，怎么也找不到。 | lose, misplace | find, locate | verb
词源: From mis- + lay (v.) "to put, place."  The prefix "mis-" indicates doing something incorrectly or badly.
