avaunt:/əˈvɔːnt/
| interj.|走开！；滚开！|An archaic exclamation used to drive away or repel.|Avaunt, vile creature! 走开，你这卑鄙的家伙！|近义词：begone, away, depart, get out|反义词：approach, welcome, stay|词性:感叹词
avaunt:/əˈvɔːnt/
| v. (archaic, transitive)|驱逐；赶走|To order or drive away; to repel.|He avaunts the troublesome thoughts from his mind. 他把那些恼人的思绪从脑海中驱逐出去。|近义词：expel, banish, dismiss, repel|反义词：invite, welcome, attract|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
