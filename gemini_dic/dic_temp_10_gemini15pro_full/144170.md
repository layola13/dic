outshowing:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊɪŋ/
|v.|胜过，优于，超过|Surpassing or exceeding in performance or appearance. |Her garden outshowed all the others in the neighborhood. 她的花园胜过附近所有其他的花园。|The new model outshowed the older one in fuel efficiency. 新型号在燃油效率方面超过了旧型号。|近义词：outperforming, surpassing, excelling, exceeding, eclipsing|反义词：underperforming, lagging behind|词性:动词

outshowing:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊɪŋ/
|n.|胜过，优于，超过(的行为或事例)|The act of surpassing or exceeding in performance or appearance.|The outshowing of the new athlete surprised everyone. 新运动员的出色表现让所有人感到惊讶。|Her outshowing of her rivals was a clear victory. 她对竞争对手的超越是一场明显的胜利。|近义词：outperformance, surpassing, excellence|反义词：underperformance|词性:名词
