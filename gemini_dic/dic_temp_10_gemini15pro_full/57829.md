dressage:/drɛˈsɑːʒ/ /drəˈsɑːʒ/
n.
盛装舞步；马术训练
The art of training a horse in obedience and precision of movement.
例句：The rider and horse performed a difficult dressage test. 骑手和马完成了一项高难度的盛装舞步测试。

例句：She has been competing in dressage for many years. 她多年来一直参加盛装舞步比赛。

近义词：equestrianism, horsemanship
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "untrained riding")
词性：noun
