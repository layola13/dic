decongregate:/diːkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/
verb
分散，疏散，解除隔离
To break up a gathering or assembly of people, causing them to scatter.
The police decongregated the protesters. 警察驱散了抗议者。
The school decongregated the students after the fire alarm. 火警响起后，学校疏散了学生。

synonym: disperse, scatter, break up, disband
antonym: congregate, assemble, gather
