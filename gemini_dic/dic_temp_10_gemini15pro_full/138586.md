nubbin:/ˈnʌbɪn/| n. |小块，小瘤，残株|A small lump or protuberance, especially one on a plant or animal; a small or undeveloped part.|The corn had several nubbins that were too small to eat. 玉米上有几个太小而不能吃的小玉米棒。|近义词：lump, knob, stub, projection|反义词:|名词
