evocativenesses:/ɪˈvɑːkətɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
唤起性；引起回忆的力量；感染力
The quality of evoking strong images, memories, or feelings.
The evocativenesses of the old photographs brought tears to her eyes. 老照片的感染力让她热泪盈眶。
synonyms: suggestiveness, resonance, poignancy, vividness
antonyms: blandness, dullness, vapidity
n.
