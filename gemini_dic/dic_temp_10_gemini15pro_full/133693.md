nonfrequence: /nɒnˈfriːkwəns/ | n. | 非频繁；稀少；不常发生 | Absence or infrequency of occurrence.  It refers to something that doesn't happen often. | The nonfrequence of rain in the desert makes it a harsh environment. 沙漠中降雨的稀少使它成为一个恶劣的环境。|  The doctor noted the nonfrequence of the patient's symptoms. 医生注意到病人症状的出现频率很低。 | 近义词：infrequency, rarity, rareness | 反义词：frequency, commonness, regularity | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "frequence" (from Latin "frequentia" meaning "assembly, crowd, multitude," later evolving to mean "frequency").
