nonmelodic:/ˌnɒn məˈlɒdɪk/ | /ˌnɑːn məˈlɑːdɪk/ | adj. | 非旋律的，不悦耳的 | Lacking melody; not melodic.  |  The nonmelodic sound of the machinery was jarring.  机器不悦耳的声音令人烦躁。 | The piece was atonal and nonmelodic, challenging traditional notions of musical beauty. 这首曲子是非调性的，不悦耳的，挑战了传统的音乐美感。| 近义词：atonal, dissonant, unmusical | 反义词：melodic, tuneful, harmonious | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
non- + melodic

non-:  a prefix meaning “not,” freely used as an English formative, usually with elements of Latin origin (nonalcoholic; nonbeliever; nonessential).

melodic:  from Greek melos “song” + -ikos -ic.
