lingered:/ˈlɪŋɡərd/ | adj. (rare) | 持续的，挥之不去的 | lasting for a long time or slow to disappear |  a lingering illness  久治不愈的疾病 | lasting, persistent, enduring | fleeting, ephemeral | adjective
