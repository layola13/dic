overperemptory:/ˌoʊvərˌpɛrˈɛmptəri/
adjective
专横的，盛气凌人的，过于武断的
Excessively peremptory; dictatorial.  Asserting authority in an imperious or arrogant manner.
例句：His overperemptory tone alienated his colleagues. 他专横的语气疏远了他的同事。

例句：The manager's overperemptory instructions made the staff feel resentful. 经理武断的指示让员工感到不满。

近义词：dictatorial, imperious, arrogant, domineering, high-handed
反义词：humble, modest, meek, unassuming, deferential
词性: adjective
