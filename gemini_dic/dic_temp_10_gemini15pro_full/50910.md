deserver:/dɪˈzɜːrvər/| n.|应得者，值得拥有的人|Someone who deserves something. |She is a deserver of praise. 她是一个值得称赞的人。|近义词:recipient, beneficiary|反义词:undeserving person|词性:名词

词源:  "Deserver" comes from the verb "deserve" which originates from the Old French word "deservir," meaning "to serve well, to earn, to merit."  The suffix "-er" is added to form a noun indicating a person who performs the action of the verb.
