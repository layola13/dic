cantilever:/ˈkæntilɪvər/| n. |悬臂，悬梁|A projecting beam or structure supported at only one end.|The balcony is a cantilever extending out from the building.  阳台是从建筑物延伸出来的悬臂。|近义词：projection, overhang, bracket|反义词:support, pier|noun


cantilever:/ˈkæntilɪvər/| v. |使成悬臂，悬出|To support or project (something) as a cantilever.|The roof is cantilevered over the entrance. 屋顶在入口上方悬挑出来。|近义词：project, extend, overhang|反义词:support, underpin|verb
