mistressless:/ˈmɪstrɪsləs/
adjective
无女主人的；无情妇的；无女教师的
Without a mistress; having no mistress.  This can refer to a household without a female head, a man without a female lover (in an older sense), or (less commonly) lacking a female teacher or instructor.
The mistressless house fell into disrepair. 这座没有女主人的房子年久失修。
He wandered, a mistressless soul, through the streets of the city. 他像一个没有情妇的灵魂，在城市的街道上游荡。
近义词: unowned, independent (in certain contexts)
反义词: owned, kept (in certain contexts)
词性: adjective

词源:
From mistress + -less.  "Mistress" comes from Old French "maistresse," ultimately from Latin "magistra" (female teacher, mistress). "-less" is a Germanic suffix indicating lack or deprivation.
