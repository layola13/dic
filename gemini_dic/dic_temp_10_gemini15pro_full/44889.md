cryogenically:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪkli/ | adv. |低温地；冷冻地 |In a way that involves very low temperatures. |The samples were cryogenically frozen for preservation. 样本被低温冷冻保存。| The food was cryogenically stored.  食物被低温保存。 | 近义词: at low temperatures, at very cold temperatures | 反义词: at high temperatures, at room temperature | 词性: 副词

cryogenic:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |低温的；冷冻的|Relating to or involving very low temperatures. |Cryogenic techniques are used in various scientific fields. 低温技术用于各种科学领域。|  The cryogenic chamber maintained a temperature of -196°C. 低温室保持-196°C的温度。 | 近义词: low-temperature, freezing | 反义词: high-temperature, warm | 词性: 形容词

词源: cryo- (cold) + -genic (producing)  来自希腊语 "kryos" (冰冷) 和 "genes" (产生).
