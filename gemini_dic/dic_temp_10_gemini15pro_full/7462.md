anisosthenic:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈsθɛnɪk/ | adj. | 不等肌力的，力量不对称的 |  Characterized by unequal strength in corresponding muscles or limbs on the two sides of the body. | Example 1: An anisosthenic condition can make it difficult to maintain balance. 不等肌力的情况会使人难以保持平衡。

Example 2:  His anisosthenic legs made running awkward. 他力量不对称的双腿使跑步变得笨拙。 | Near synonyms: asymmetrical, unbalanced, uneven | Antonyms: symmetrical, balanced, even | Word type: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *an-* (not, without) + *iso-* (equal) + *sthenos* (strength).
