gauchest:/ˈɡɔːtʃɪst/| adj.|最笨拙的，最不灵巧的|Most awkward or clumsy; left-handed (especially if considered clumsy).|例句：He was the gauchest boy in the class, always tripping over his own feet. 他是班上最笨拙的男孩，总是被自己的脚绊倒。

例句：Her gauchest attempt at a compliment ended up insulting the guest of honor. 她最笨拙的恭维尝试最终侮辱了贵宾。|近义词：clumsiest, awkwardest, most inept|反义词：dexterous, graceful, adept|词性:形容词

词源: From gauche (awkward) + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Frankish *waik "weak."
