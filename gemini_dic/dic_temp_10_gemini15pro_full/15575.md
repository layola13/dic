bahadur:/bəˈhɑːdʊr/| adj. n.|勇敢的，勇士|A Hindi and Urdu word meaning "brave" or "heroic." It can be used as an adjective to describe someone courageous or as a noun to refer to a brave person, often a warrior.|He was a true bahadur, facing the enemy without fear. 他是一位真正的勇士，无所畏惧地面对敌人。|近义词:brave, courageous, valiant, heroic|反义词:cowardly, timid|词性:adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi बहादुर (bahādur), from Persian بهادر (bahādor). Ultimately from Mongolian *baghatur (“hero, warrior”).
