emmantle:/ɪnˈmæntl/ |v.| 披上；覆盖；赋予（权力、尊严等）|to clothe or invest with a mantle; to cover or envelop|The queen was emmantled in a velvet robe. 女王披着天鹅绒长袍。|近义词：cloak, drape, cover, invest, endow|反义词：uncover, expose, divest|词性: 动词

Etymology:
From en- + mantle, from Old French mantel, from Latin mantellum "cloak, covering," perhaps related to manus "hand."  The prefix en- acts as an intensifier, emphasizing the act of covering or enveloping.
