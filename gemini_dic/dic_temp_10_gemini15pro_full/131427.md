nonclogging:/ˌnɑːnˈklɑːɡɪŋ/
adjective
防堵塞的，不易堵塞的
Designed or formulated to resist clogging; not easily blocked.
This paint is specially formulated to be nonclogging, ensuring a smooth and even application.  这种油漆经过特殊配方，不易堵塞，确保涂抹顺滑均匀。
The nonclogging shower head provides consistent water pressure.  防堵塞的淋浴喷头提供持续的水压。
non-blocking, free-flowing
clogging, blocking
