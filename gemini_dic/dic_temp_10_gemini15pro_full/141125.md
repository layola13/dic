onu:/ˈoʊ ɛn juː/ /ˈɒn juː/ | n. | 光网络单元 (Optical Network Unit) | A device located at a customer's premises that connects their local network to a fiber-optic access network. It converts optical signals transmitted over the fiber to electrical signals that can be used by the customer's equipment. | Example: The ONU provides high-speed internet access to the home.  光网络单元为家庭提供高速互联网接入。

Example:  The technician installed a new ONU to improve the network connection.  技术人员安装了一个新的光网络单元来改善网络连接。 | Synonym: ONT (Optical Network Terminal), subscriber unit | Antonym: OLT (Optical Line Terminal) | noun
