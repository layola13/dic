monarchally:/məˈnɑːrkɪkli/ | adv. | 以君主的方式；专制地 | In a manner befitting or characteristic of a monarch; autocratically. | He ruled monarchally, demanding absolute obedience. 他以君主的方式统治，要求绝对服从。 | The king dismissed the parliament monarchally, ignoring their pleas. 国王专制地解散了议会，无视他们的请求。 | 近义词：autocratically, despotically, tyrannically | 反义词：democratically, constitutionally | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From monarch + -ally.  "Monarch" comes from Old French "monarche," ultimately from Greek "monarkhos," meaning "sole ruler," from "monos" (alone) + "arkhein" (to rule).  "-ally" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a ... manner."
