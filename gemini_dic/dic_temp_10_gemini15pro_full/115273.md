manitrunk: /ˈmænɪˌtrʌŋk/
n.|马蹄干|The central, trunk-like part of a manatee's flipper, resembling a hand with rudimentary fingernails.|Manatees use their manitrunks to grasp and manipulate objects in their environment. 海牛用它们的马蹄干来抓取和操纵周围环境中的物体。|flipper, forelimb|hind limb|noun
