dioptase:/ˈdaɪəpteɪs/| n. |透辉石|A vibrant emerald-green copper cyclosilicate mineral, often found in oxidized zones of copper deposits. |例句：The museum displayed a stunning specimen of dioptase. 博物馆展出了一块令人惊叹的透辉石标本。|例句：Dioptase is prized by collectors for its intense green color. 透辉石因其浓郁的绿色而受到收藏家的珍视。|近义词：emerald, chrysocolla (in terms of color)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From the Greek words "dia" (through) and "optos" (visible), referring to the visibility of cleavage planes within the crystal.
