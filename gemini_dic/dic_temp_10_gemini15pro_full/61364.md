embezzlers:/ɪmˈbɛzlərz/
n.
挪用公款者；贪污者
People who illegally take money that they have been entrusted with, often from their employer.
The embezzlers were caught and sentenced to prison.  那些挪用公款者被抓获并判处监禁。
Synonyms: thieves, defrauders, swindlers
Antonyms: philanthropists, donors
词性: 名词(plural)


embezzle:/ɪmˈbɛzl/
v.
挪用；盗用；侵吞
To steal money that you have been entrusted with, often from your employer.
He embezzled millions of dollars from the company. 他挪用了公司数百万美元。
Synonyms: misappropriate, pilfer, defraud
Antonyms: donate, contribute
词性: 动词
