overadjusted:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌstɪd/
adjective
过度调整的
Having been adjusted excessively, often with negative consequences.  The act of adjusting something too much, resulting in an imbalance or instability.
The overadjusted thermostat caused the house to be too cold.  过调的恒温器导致房子太冷。
The economy became overadjusted after the government's intervention. 政府干预后，经济变得过度调整。
近义词: overcompensated, overcorrected
反义词: underadjusted
词性: adjective
