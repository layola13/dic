misplanting:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːntɪŋ/
noun
错误种植，误植
the act of planting something in the wrong place or at the wrong time
例句：The misplanting of the seedlings resulted in a poor harvest. 幼苗的误植导致收成不好。
例句：Misplanting can lead to stunted growth and reduced yields. 误植会导致生长发育迟缓和产量降低。
近义词：incorrect planting, wrong planting
反义词：correct planting, proper planting
词性:n.

Etymology:
mis- + planting
mis-: prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly"
planting: from plant + -ing, the act of putting something into the ground to grow
