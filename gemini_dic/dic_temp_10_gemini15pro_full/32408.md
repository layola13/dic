chillness:/ˈtʃɪlnəs/| n.|寒冷，冷淡，寒意|A state of being cold, or a cold feeling; also, a lack of warmth or friendliness in manner.|例句：The chillness of the air made her shiver. 空气的寒冷让她颤抖。|近义词：coldness, coolness, frigidity, aloofness|反义词:warmth, heat, friendliness|词性:noun
Etymology: From "chill" (n. & adj.) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  The word "chill" comes from Old English "ciele" and is related to the Old High German "kalt" (cold).
