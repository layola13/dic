murder:/ˈmɜːrdər/ (US) /ˈmɜːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|谋杀，凶杀|The unlawful premeditated killing of one human being by another.|例句：The murder of the businessman remains unsolved.  这位商人的谋杀案仍未侦破。

例句：The detective investigated the brutal murder.  侦探调查了这起残忍的谋杀案。|近义词：homicide, killing, slaughter, assassination|反义词: resuscitation, saving|词性:名词,动词


murder:/ˈmɜːrdər/ (US) /ˈmɜːdə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|谋杀，凶杀|To kill (someone) unlawfully and with premeditation.|例句：He was murdered in cold blood. 他被残忍地谋杀了。

例句：She was accused of murdering her husband. 她被指控谋杀了她的丈夫。|近义词：kill, slay, execute, assassinate|反义词: revive, save|词性:动词
