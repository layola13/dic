eaglewood:/ˈiːɡəlˌwʊd/ | n. |沉香木，沉香|A fragrant resinous wood, especially from southeast Asia, used in incense and perfumes.  It is formed in the heartwood of aquilaria trees when they become infected with certain types of mold. |The eaglewood beads were worth a king's ransom. 这些沉香木珠子价值连城。

Eaglewood is highly prized in many cultures for its unique fragrance. 在许多文化中，沉香因其独特的香味而备受珍视。|近义词：agarwood, aloeswood|反义词:|名词
