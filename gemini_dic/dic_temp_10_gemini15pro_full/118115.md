menobranch:/ˈmɛnəˌbræŋk/ | n. | 美西螈，泥螈 | A large, permanently aquatic salamander (Necturus maculosus) of the eastern North America, having persistent external gills.  It is also called a mud puppy. | 例句：The menobranch, with its feathery gills, is a fascinating creature to observe.  美西螈有着羽毛状的鳃，是一种引人入胜的生物。
