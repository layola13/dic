inspirers:/ɪnˈspaɪrərz/
n.
鼓舞人心的人; 激励者
People who inspire others; motivators.
The Olympic athletes are inspirers to many young people. 奥运健儿是很多年轻人的激励者。
Nelson Mandela was an inspirer to millions.  纳尔逊·曼德拉激励了数百万人。
Synonyms: motivators, role models, heroes, influencers
Antonyms: discouragers, deterrents, pessimists
noun
