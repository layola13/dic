moshers:/ˈmɒʃəz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːʃərz/ (US)| n. |冲撞乐迷，疯狂跳舞的人|People who take part in moshing, a type of vigorous dancing in a crowd, often at rock concerts.|The moshers went wild when the band started playing. 冲撞乐迷在乐队开始演奏时变得疯狂。|近义词：headbangers, slam dancers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) audience members who don't mosh|词性:Noun
