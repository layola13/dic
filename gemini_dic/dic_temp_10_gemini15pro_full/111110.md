literality:/ˌlɪtəˈrælɪti/
n.|字面性；拘泥字面; 字面意义|The adherence to the exact letter or the literal meaning of a word or text.|She interpreted the poem with a strict literality, missing its deeper meaning. 她对这首诗进行了严格的字面解读，忽略了它更深层的含义。|literalness, exactness|figurativeness, symbolism|noun
