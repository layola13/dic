easy-peasy:/ˌiːziˈpiːzi/ | adj. | 非常容易，轻而易举 |Very easy; extremely simple.  A child's term.  | Example:  "The test was easy-peasy."  考试非常容易。 | Example: "Putting the furniture together was easy-peasy, thanks to the clear instructions."  多亏了清晰的说明书，组装家具变得轻而易举。 | 近义词: simple, effortless, child's play, a piece of cake | 反义词: difficult, hard, challenging | 词性: adjective

easy-peasy:  Originated as reduplication of "easy" to emphasize simplicity, commonly used in informal contexts. | 词源：由“easy”的重复构成，用于强调简单性，通常用于非正式场合。
