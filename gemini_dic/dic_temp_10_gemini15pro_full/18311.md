belleek:/bəˈliːk/ |n.|贝里克瓷器；贝里克镇|A type of fine Parian china, originally made at Belleek, County Fermanagh, Northern Ireland, characterized by its thinness, slightly iridescent glaze, and often elaborate pierced or molded decoration.  Also refers to the town itself.|I bought a beautiful belleek vase in Ireland. 我在爱尔兰买了一个漂亮的贝里克花瓶。|Synonyms: Parian china, porcelain|Antonyms: earthenware|noun
