launderers:/ˈlɔːndərərz/
noun
洗钱者; 洗衣工
People who launder money or clothes.  Those involved in money laundering are criminals. Those who launder clothes may work in a laundromat or for a dry cleaning service.
The launderers were caught trying to move millions of dollars through offshore accounts. 洗钱者试图通过离岸账户转移数百万美元时被抓获。
The launderers worked tirelessly to get the grass stains out of the baseball uniforms. 洗衣工不知疲倦地工作，以去除棒球制服上的草渍。
Synonyms: money launderers (for those involved in illegal activity), cleaners, laundry workers
Antonyms:  N/A (no direct opposite for the act of laundering)
词性: 名词(n.)
