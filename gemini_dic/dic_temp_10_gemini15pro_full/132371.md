nondeliriously:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪəriəsli/ | adv. |  清醒地，不谵妄地 |  In a way that is not characterized by delirium; lucidly, rationally. | He spoke nondeliriously, clearly outlining his plans. 他清醒地发言，清晰地概述了他的计划。|  She answered the questions nondeliriously, demonstrating her mental clarity. 她清醒地回答了问题，展现了她的头脑清晰。| Synonyms: lucidly, rationally, clearly, coherently, sanely | Antonyms: deliriously, incoherently, irrationally, madly | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  不，相反的
delirious (adjective): in a state of delirium; raving  谵妄的；胡说的
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs  构成副词
