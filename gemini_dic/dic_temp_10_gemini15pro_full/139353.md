obscuration:/ɒbˌskjʊˈreɪʃn/ /ˌɑːbskjʊˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 昏暗，遮蔽，模糊 |The act of obscuring or the state of being obscured; something that obscures.  The darkening of the sky caused by clouds or smoke.
