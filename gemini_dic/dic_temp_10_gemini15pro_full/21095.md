blinkered:/ˈblɪŋkərd/| adj.|狭隘的，心胸狭窄的，目光短浅的|Having or showing a narrow or limited outlook, unable to understand other people's beliefs or ways of life.|He has a rather blinkered view of the world. 他对世界的看法相当狭隘。|近义词：narrow-minded, prejudiced, limited, parochial, myopic|反义词：open-minded, tolerant, broad-minded, receptive|词性:形容词
词源: From "blinkers," leather flaps on a horse's bridle to restrict its vision to the path ahead.
