dredger:/ˈdrɛdʒər/ | n. | 疏浚机，挖泥船 | A machine used to remove mud from the bottom of rivers, harbors, etc. | The dredger was working day and night to deepen the channel. 挖泥船日夜不停地工作以加深河道。 | Synonyms: excavator, dredger boat, digging machine | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


dredge:/drɛdʒ/ | v. | 疏浚，挖掘 | To remove mud, sand, etc. from the bottom of a river, harbor, etc. | They dredged the river to make it deeper.  他们疏浚了河流以使其更深。| Synonyms: excavate, scoop, dig | Antonyms: fill, bury | Verb
