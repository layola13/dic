non-linearity:/ˌnɒnˌlɪnɪˈærɪti/ | n. | 非线性 |The quality or state of being non-linear.  A relationship between two quantities that is not a straight line when graphed. | The non-linearity of the system made it difficult to predict its behavior.  系统的非线性使其难以预测其行为。| nonlinear behavior, nonlinear response | linearity | noun


non-linear:/ˌnɒnˈlɪniər/ | adj. | 非线性的 | Not linear; not denoting, involving, or arranged in a straight line.  Not proportional. | The graph shows a non-linear relationship between the two variables.  该图显示了两个变量之间的非线性关系。 | nonlinear behavior, nonlinear response | linear | adjective
