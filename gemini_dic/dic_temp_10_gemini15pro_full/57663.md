drawdown:/ˈdrɔːdaʊn/| n.| (投资组合的) 最大回撤 |The peak-to-trough decline during a specific period for an investment portfolio. |The hedge fund experienced a 20% drawdown during the financial crisis.  该对冲基金在金融危机期间经历了 20% 的最大回撤。|synonyms: peak-to-trough decline, maximum loss |antonyms: peak, maximum gain| noun
