myronic:/mɪˈrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 芥子油苷的; 含有芥子油苷的 | Relating to or containing myronate, a glycoside found in certain plants, especially mustard seeds, which yields a pungent oil when hydrolyzed. |  Black mustard seeds contain myronic acid. 黑芥菜籽含有芥子油苷酸。| synonym: glucosinolate | antonym: None | adjective
