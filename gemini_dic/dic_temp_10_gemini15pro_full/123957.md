morin:/ˈmɔːrɪn/| n.|桑黄素, 黄木素|A yellow crystalline flavonol glycoside, C21H20O11, present in the wood of the Osage orange (Maclura pomifera) and Old Fustic (Maclura tinctoria), and used as a yellow dye.|The morin in the wood reacted with the iron in the water, creating a dark stain. 木材中的桑黄素与水中的铁发生反应，形成了一个深色的污渍。|近义词：fustic, osage orange dye|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
