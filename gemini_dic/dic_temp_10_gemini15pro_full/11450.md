argues:/ˈɑːrɡjuːz/ (US), /ˈɑːrɡjuːz/ (UK)| v. |争论，辩论| to disagree or fight by using angry words |  They argued about money all the time. 他们总是为了钱争吵。| quarrels, disputes, fights | agrees, compromises | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *arguen*, from Old French *arguer*, from Latin *arguere* ("to make clear, prove, accuse, convict").
