magdalene:/ˈmæɡdəlɪn/ /ˈmæɡdəliːn/| n.|抹大拉的马利亚; 改造了的妓女; (M-)抹大拉的马利亚 (耶稣的信徒);|A follower of Jesus Christ named Mary Magdalene, from whom he cast out seven demons. She was present at his crucifixion and resurrection. In art, she is often depicted with long, flowing hair.| Mary Magdalene was a devoted follower of Jesus. 抹大拉的马利亚是耶稣的忠实信徒。|Mary Magdalene witnessed the resurrection. 抹大拉的马利亚见证了耶稣的复活。|近义词: penitent, reformed prostitute|反义词: sinner|词性:noun
