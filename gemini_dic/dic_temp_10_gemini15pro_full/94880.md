in-character:/ɪn ˈkærɪktər/| adj.|符合角色的，性格真实的|Acting in character means portraying a role in a way that is consistent with the established personality and traits of the fictional character.  扮演角色意味着以与虚构角色的既定个性和特征一致的方式来描绘角色。|例句：The actor's performance was truly in character, capturing the essence of the historical figure.  这位演员的表演非常符合角色，抓住了这位历史人物的精髓。

例句：She stayed in character throughout the entire play, never breaking the fourth wall. 她整部戏都保持着角色状态，从未打破第四面墙。|近义词：true to life, believable, convincing, realistic|反义词：out of character, unbelievable, unconvincing|词性:形容词
