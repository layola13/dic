nagana:/nəˈɡɑːnə/ | n. |非洲锥虫病,昏睡病|A disease of cattle, horses, and other livestock in Africa, caused by trypanosomes and transmitted by tsetse flies. It is characterized by fever, lethargy, and often death.|例句：The cattle were diagnosed with nagana. 牛被诊断出患有非洲锥虫病。|例句：Nagana is a major problem for livestock farmers in Africa. 非洲锥虫病是非洲牲畜养殖户面临的一个主要问题。|近义词：trypanosomiasis, sleeping sickness (in animals)|反义词:|noun
