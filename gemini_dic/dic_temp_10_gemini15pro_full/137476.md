nontolerated: /nɒnˈtɒləreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不能容忍的，不被接受的 | Not tolerated; unacceptable.  This word emphasizes that something is actively rejected or considered impermissible. |  Smoking is nontolerated in this restaurant.  这家餐厅禁止吸烟。|  Discrimination is nontolerated in our company. 我们公司不容忍歧视。|Synonyms: unacceptable, impermissible, prohibited, forbidden | Antonyms: tolerated, acceptable, permissible, allowed | adjective
