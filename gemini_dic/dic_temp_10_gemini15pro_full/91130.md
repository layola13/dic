hybridism:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪzəm/ | n. |杂交, 杂交性, 混合性 |The hybridism of cultures creates something new and unique. 文化的融合创造了新的和独特的东西。| The artist's work reflects a hybridism of traditional and modern techniques. 这位艺术家的作品反映了传统和现代技术的融合。 | 近义词: mixture, blend, combination, crossbreed | 反义词: purity, homogeneity | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French *hybridisme*, from hybride + -isme.  Ultimately from Greek *hybris* (ὕβρις) "insolence, outrage, wanton violence".
