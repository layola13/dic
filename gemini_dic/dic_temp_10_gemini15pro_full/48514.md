deerstalker:/ˈdɪərstɔːkər/
n.
猎鹿帽；侦探帽
A close-fitting cap with visors front and back, typically worn by Sherlock Holmes.
Sherlock Holmes is often depicted wearing a deerstalker.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯经常被描绘成戴着猎鹿帽。
He pulled his deerstalker down over his eyes. 他把猎鹿帽拉低遮住眼睛。
近义词:cap, hat
反义词:(无)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
deer + stalker.  From its association with hunting deer.
