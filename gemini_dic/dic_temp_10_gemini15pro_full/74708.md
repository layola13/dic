freely:/ˈfriːli/| adv.|自由地，随意地|In a free manner; without restriction or constraint.  It refers to the ability to act or express oneself without limitations.|Birds fly freely in the sky. 鸟儿自由地在天空飞翔。|Synonyms: willingly, readily, easily, loosely|Antonyms: restrictedly, tightly, reluctantly, unwillingly|adverb
freely:/ˈfriːli/| adv.|免费地，无偿地|Without cost or payment.|The software is available freely online.  这款软件可以在网上免费获取。|Synonyms: gratuitously, for free, without charge|Antonyms: expensively, for a fee, at a cost|adverb
