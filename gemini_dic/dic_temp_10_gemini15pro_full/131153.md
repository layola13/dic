nonbookishness:/ˌnɒnˈbʊkɪʃnəs/
noun
不爱读书；不学究气
lack of interest in books and studying; not scholarly or pedantic
He surprised everyone with his nonbookishness, preferring outdoor activities to reading. 他对读书缺乏兴趣，更喜欢户外活动，这让每个人都感到惊讶。
non-academic, unscholarly, unintellectual
bookishness, scholarly, studious
n.
