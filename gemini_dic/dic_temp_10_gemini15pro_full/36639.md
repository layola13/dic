collectivizing: /kəˈlɛktɪvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
集体化
The process of bringing privately owned land, businesses, or other properties under the control of the state or a collective group.
The government was collectivizing farms in the region. 政府正在将该地区的农场集体化。
The Soviet Union began collectivizing agriculture in the late 1920s.  苏联在20世纪20年代后期开始集体化农业。
