bemourn:/bɪˈmɔrn/ /biˈmɔːrn/| v. |哀悼，悲痛，对…表示哀悼|To express deep sorrow or regret for something lost or gone.|She bemoaned the loss of her beloved pet. 她哀悼她心爱的宠物的离去。

He bemoaned the passing of the simpler times. 他哀叹过去简单时光的流逝。

|近义词：lament, grieve, mourn, bewail|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + mourn.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly," intensifying the verb "mourn."  "Mourn" comes from Old French "morner," ultimately from Latin "lugere" (to mourn).
