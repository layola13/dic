deodorizer:/diˈoʊdəˌraɪzər/
noun
除臭剂，防臭剂
A substance that destroys or masks unpleasant odours.
I bought a new deodorizer for the bathroom. 我买了个新的浴室除臭剂。
The refrigerator needed a deodorizer. 冰箱需要一个除臭剂。
synonyms: air freshener, fragrance, scent, perfume
antonyms: odorant, stink, stench
