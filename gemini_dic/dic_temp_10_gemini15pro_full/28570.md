careenage:/ˌkærɪˈniːdʒ/
n.
码头，船坞
A place where ships are careened (hauled out of the water for cleaning or repair).
The careenage was bustling with activity as the fishermen prepared their boats for the upcoming season. 码头随着渔民们为即将到来的捕鱼季节准备船只而熙熙攘攘。
The old careenage is now a popular tourist spot.  旧码头现在是一个受欢迎的旅游景点。
Synonyms: dock, wharf, shipyard, harbor
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps open sea)
noun
