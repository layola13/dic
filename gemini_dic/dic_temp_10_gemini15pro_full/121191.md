misdoer:/ˌmɪsˈduːər/| n. |作恶者，坏人，违法者|A person who does something wrong or illegal. |The misdoers were caught and punished. 坏人被抓获并受到了惩罚。|
The police are searching for the misdoers who vandalized the park. 警察正在搜捕破坏公园的坏人。|Synonyms: culprit, offender, criminal, wrongdoer, delinquent|Antonyms: law-abider, good Samaritan, do-gooder|noun
