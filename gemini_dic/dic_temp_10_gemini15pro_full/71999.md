flint:/flɪnt/| n.|燧石；打火石；坚硬的东西|A very hard grey or black stone that produces a spark when it is hit against steel.|He struck the flint against the steel to make a fire. 他用燧石撞击钢材生火。|近义词：stone, rock, quartz|反义词：tinder|名词
