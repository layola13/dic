ditrochee:/daɪˈtrəʊkiː/
|n.|双音步|A metrical foot in poetry consisting of two trochees (a trochee being a metrical foot consisting of a stressed syllable followed by an unstressed syllable).  It's a rhythmic pattern.|Example: The word "broken" is a ditrochee (BRO-ken).|近义词: (loosely) dimeter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
