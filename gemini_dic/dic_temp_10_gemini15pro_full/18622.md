benthos:/ˈbenθɒs/ (UK) /ˈbenθɑːs/ (US)| n. |底栖生物，海底生物|The organisms that live on, in, or near the seabed are collectively called benthos.  生活在海底、海底或海底附近的生物统称为底栖生物。|Benthos plays a vital role in the marine ecosystem. 底栖生物在海洋生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：bottom fauna, bottom dwellers|反义词:plankton (浮游生物)|noun
