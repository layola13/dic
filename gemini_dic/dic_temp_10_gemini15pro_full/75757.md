fulminating:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪtɪŋ/ /ˈfʌlmɪˌneɪtɪŋ/| adj.|大声斥责的；猛烈的；爆发性的|Characterized by sudden and violent denunciation or condemnation; exploding with a loud noise.|例句：The senator delivered a fulminating speech against the proposed law.  参议员发表了猛烈抨击拟议法律的演讲。

例句：The patient experienced fulminating pain in his chest. 病人感到胸部一阵剧烈的疼痛。|近义词：explosive, vehement, thunderous, raging, furious|反义词：calm, gentle, mild, quiet, subdued|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin fulminare (“to flash with lightning”), from fulmen (“lightning bolt”).
