obsequiousness:/ˌɒbˈsiːkwiəsnəs/ /ˌɑːbˈsiːkwiəsnəs/| n. |谄媚，奉承，奴颜婢膝|Excessive eagerness to please or obey; fawning servility.|His obsequiousness to the boss was embarrassing to witness. 他对老板的谄媚让人尴尬.|近义词：servility, subservience, fawning, sycophancy|反义词：independence, assertiveness, defiance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin obsequiosus (“compliant, obedient”), from Latin obsequium (“compliance”), from ob- (“towards”) + sequi (“to follow”).
