agriculturalist:/ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | n. | 农业学家；农学家 | A person who studies or practices agriculture. |  例句：The agriculturalist advised the farmers on the best crops to plant.  这位农业学家向农民们建议了最佳的种植作物。

例句：She's a respected agriculturalist in the community. 她是社区里受人尊敬的农业学家。

近义词：farmer, agronomist, cultivator | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From agriculture + -ist.  Agriculture comes from Latin *agricultūra*  (cultivation of the fields) from *ager* (field) and *cultūra* (cultivation).
