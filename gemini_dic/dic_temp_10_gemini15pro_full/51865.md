dextrorsal:/ˈdɛkstrɔːrsl/
adjective
右旋的，向右旋转的
Turning or spiraling to the right, as a shell or plant stem.
例句：The snail shell had a dextrorsal spiral.  这个蜗牛壳有一个右旋的螺旋。

例句：Most garden snails have dextrorsal shells. 大多数花园蜗牛的壳是右旋的。

近义词：clockwise, right-handed
反义词：sinistral, left-handed
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *dexter* (right) + *-orsus* (turning), from *orior* (to rise, originate).
