defoliated:/ˌdiːˈfoʊliˌeɪtɪd/
adjective
落叶的；被除去树叶的
Stripped of leaves, either naturally or artificially.
The defoliated trees looked stark against the winter sky. 光秃秃的树木在冬日的天空下显得格外荒凉。
近义词:bare, leafless, denuded
反义词:leafy, verdant


defoliate:/ˌdiːˈfoʊliˌeɪt/
verb
使落叶；除去…的叶子
To cause the leaves of (a plant, tree, or forest) to fall off.
The chemical defoliated the trees in the area. 这种化学物质使该地区的树木落叶。
近义词:strip, denude
反义词:leaf


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *defoliatus*, past participle of *defoliare* "to strip of leaves," from de- "off, down from" + folium "leaf."
