carnallite:/kɑːrˈnəˌlaɪt/
n.
光卤石，钾镁盐
A mineral consisting of hydrated potassium magnesium chloride, used as a source of potassium and magnesium.
Carnallite is an important source of potassium for fertilizers. 光卤石是肥料中钾的重要来源。
Deposits of carnallite are found in various parts of the world. 世界各地都有光卤石矿床。
