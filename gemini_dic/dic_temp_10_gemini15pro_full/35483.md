coact:/koʊˈækt/| v. |共同行动，协作，合作|To act together or in concert; to cooperate.|The two organizations coacted to achieve their shared goals.  这两个组织共同努力以实现他们的共同目标。|The team members coacted effectively to complete the project. 团队成员有效合作完成了项目。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, work together|反义词：act alone, work independently|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin co- (together) + actus (act, action).
