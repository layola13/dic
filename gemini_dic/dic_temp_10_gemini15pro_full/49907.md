demystifies:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪz/
verb
揭开…的神秘面纱；使不再神秘
to make something easier to understand
The book demystifies the complex world of quantum physics. 这本书揭开了量子物理复杂世界的神秘面纱。
clarifies, explains, simplifies
obscures, confuses, complicates
verb
