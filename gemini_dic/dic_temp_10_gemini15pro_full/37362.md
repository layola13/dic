commensals:/kəˈmɛnslz/| n.|共生生物，共食者|Organisms that benefit from another species without harming or helping it.|Barnacles are commensals of whales. 藤壶是鲸鱼的共生生物。|synonyms: symbionts, associates|antonyms: parasites, pathogens|noun

commensality:/kəˌmɛnsˈælɪti/| n.|共食，共生|The relationship between two species where one benefits and the other is neither harmed nor helped.|Commensality is a common phenomenon in nature. 共生是大自然中常见的现象。|synonyms: symbiosis, mutualism (sometimes)|antonyms: parasitism, predation|noun

commensal:/kəˈmɛnsl/| adj.|共生的，共食的|Relating to commensalism, a relationship where one organism benefits and the other is unaffected.|The commensal relationship between the two species is well documented. 这两个物种之间的共生关系已有充分的文献记载。|synonyms: symbiotic, mutualistic (sometimes)|antonyms: parasitic, predatory|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commensalis*, from Latin com- 'together' + mensa 'table'.  Literally 'eating at the same table'.
