bicyclic:/baɪˈsaɪklɪk/
adjective
双环的；二环的
Having two rings in its molecular structure.  A bicyclic compound contains two rings which share two or more atoms.
This compound has a bicyclic structure.  这个化合物具有双环结构。

bicyclic:/baɪˈsaɪklɪk/
adjective
自行车(式)的
Relating to or resembling a bicycle.  Having two wheels.
The town promotes bicyclic tourism.  这个城镇推广自行车旅游。



Etymology:
From bi- (two) + cyclic (circular, ring-shaped).  "Cyclic" ultimately derives from the Greek "kyklos" meaning "circle" or "wheel".

Synonyms:
(for chemical meaning):  di-cyclic
(for bicycle meaning): two-wheeled

Antonyms:
(for chemical meaning): monocyclic, acyclic
(for bicycle meaning):  There isn't a direct antonym, perhaps "pedestrian" in some contexts.

词性: adjective
