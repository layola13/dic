nicker:/ˈnɪkər/| n. vi. |轻声笑；嘶鸣；(尤指英国)一英镑|A short, quiet laugh. The characteristic sound made by a horse.|例句：She let out a little nicker of amusement. 她轻轻地笑了一声，表示觉得有趣。

The horse gave a soft nicker. 那匹马轻轻地嘶鸣了一声。|近义词：chuckle, giggle, whinny (for horses)|反义词: roar, cry (for sounds)|词性:名词，动词

nicker:/ˈnɪkər/| n. |(尤指英国)一英镑|One pound sterling (British slang).|例句：He bet a nicker on the horse race. 他在赛马上赌了一英镑。

It cost him a few nicker. 这花了他几英镑。|近义词：quid, pound|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of currency)|词性:名词
