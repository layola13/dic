hyperdemocratic:/ˌhaɪpərˌdeməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 过度民主的，极端民主的 |  Characterized by an excessive or exaggerated emphasis on democratic principles, often to the point of inefficiency or instability.  It suggests a system where democratic processes are taken to an extreme, potentially undermining effective governance. |  The hyperdemocratic nature of the online forum led to endless debates and no clear decisions.  这个在线论坛过度民主的性质导致了无休止的辩论，没有明确的决定。 | The hyperdemocratic structure of the organization made it difficult to implement any real change.  该组织极端民主的结构使其难以实施任何真正的变革。 |近义词: excessively democratic, ultrademocratic |反义词: authoritarian, autocratic, oligarchical | 词性: adjective
