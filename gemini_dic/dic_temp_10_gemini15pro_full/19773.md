billions:/ˈbɪljənz/| n.|数十亿，大量|An extremely large, indefinite number (used with a plural verb).|例句：Billions of stars are visible on a clear night.  晴朗的夜晚可以看到数十亿颗星星。

|例句：The company is worth billions of dollars. 这家公司价值数十亿美元。

|近义词：zillions, countless, myriad|反义词:few, little|词性:名词
