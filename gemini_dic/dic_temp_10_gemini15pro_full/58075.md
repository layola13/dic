drony:/ˈdroʊni/| adj.| 单调的，沉闷的；懒惰的，迟钝的| lacking in variety and interest; tediously repetitious; sluggish| The professor's drony voice put half the class to sleep.  教授单调的声音让一半的学生都睡着了。| The afternoon heat made everyone feel drony and listless. 下午的酷热让每个人都感到昏昏欲睡，无精打采。|近义词：monotonous, tedious, dull, sluggish, lethargic|反义词：exciting, stimulating, energetic, lively|词性:形容词

drony:/ˈdroʊni/| n.|雄蜂；寄生虫；懒汉|male honeybee; a person who lives on the labors of others; a drone|  The drony in the hive does no work but fertilizes the queen. 蜂巢里的雄蜂除了让蜂王受精外什么也不做。| He's a drony who never lifts a finger to help. 他是个懒汉，从来不帮忙。|近义词：idler, loafer, parasite|反义词：worker|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English drane, drone (“male bee”), from Old English drān (“male bee, drone”), from Proto-Germanic *dranō (“drone”), from Proto-Indo-European *dhre- (“to roar, buzz”).
