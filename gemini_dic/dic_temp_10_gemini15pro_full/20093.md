biosis:/baɪˈoʊsɪs/| n.|共生, 生物共同生活;|A close relationship between two or more different species, which may or may not be beneficial to each.  两种或多种不同物种之间的密切关系，可能对彼此有益，也可能无益。|例句：The biosis between the clownfish and the sea anemone is a classic example of mutualism. 小丑鱼和海葵之间的共生关系是互利共生的典型例子。|例句：Researchers are studying the biosis of different bacteria in the human gut. 研究人员正在研究人类肠道中不同细菌的共生关系。|近义词：symbiosis, commensalism|反义词: antibiosis|Noun
