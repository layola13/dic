gravitases:/ˈɡrævɪteɪsiz/ /ˈɡrævɪˌteɪsiːz/
n.
向地性；重力性
The downward growth of roots in response to gravity.  植物的根向下生长以响应重力。
Plant roots exhibit positive gravitases, growing downwards towards the pull of gravity. 植物的根表现出正向地性，朝着重力方向向下生长。
geotropism, geotaxis
negative gravitases
noun
