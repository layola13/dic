constructibility:/kənˌstrʌktɪˈbɪləti/
n.
可构造性，可建性
The quality or state of being constructible; capability of being constructed.
The constructibility of the bridge was questioned due to the challenging terrain.  这座桥的可建性由于具有挑战性的地形而受到质疑。
constructability:/kənˌstrʌktɪˈbɪləti/
n.
可造性，可构成性
The capability of being formed or constituted.
The constructibility of the theory relies on a set of basic axioms.  该理论的可构成性依赖于一组基本公理。
synonym: feasibility, buildability, realizability
antonym: impossibility, impracticability
noun
