malest:/ˈmeɪlɪst/ | adj. | 最雄性的 |  Superlative form of male; most characteristic of the male sex.  | The malest deer in the herd had the largest antlers.  鹿群中最雄壮的鹿拥有最大的鹿角。 | 近义词: most masculine, virile | 反义词:  most feminine, female | 词性: 形容词

词源：From Middle English *malest, a superlative formed from male, ultimately from Latin masculus (“male, masculine”).
