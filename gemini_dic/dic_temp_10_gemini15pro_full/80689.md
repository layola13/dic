gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/| n.|峡谷；暴食；咽喉；狼吞虎咽|A deep, narrow valley with steep sides, often with a river flowing through it.  Also, the act of eating greedily.|We hiked through the magnificent gorge. 我们徒步穿越了壮丽的峡谷。|近义词：canyon, ravine, chasm, abyss|反义词：plain, plateau|词性:名词

gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/| v.|狼吞虎咽；塞满；充斥|To eat a large amount greedily; to fill something completely.|He gorged himself on pizza. 他狼吞虎咽地吃了披萨。|近义词：stuff, cram, devour, gobble|反义词：nibble, pick at|词性:动词
