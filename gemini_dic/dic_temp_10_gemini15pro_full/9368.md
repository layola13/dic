antipyresis:/ˌæntipaɪˈriːsɪs/
noun
退热，解热
The reduction of fever; the cooling of the body.
Aspirin is commonly used for antipyresis. 阿司匹林通常用于退烧。
近义词:fever reduction, defervescence
反义词:pyrexia, fever
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀντιπύρεσις (antipúresis, “cooling down, abatement of fever”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + πύρεξις (púrexis, “fever”).
