conscientize:/ˈkɒnʃɪənˌtaɪz/ /ˈkɑːnʃənˌtaɪz/| vt.|使…意识到；唤醒…的良知；启发|To make (someone) aware of social, political, or economic conditions, especially so as to inspire action.|The workshops were designed to conscientize the workers about their rights.|近义词：awaken, enlighten, educate, raise awareness|反义词：suppress, deceive, mislead|词性:动词

Etymology: From Portuguese *conscientizar*, from consciência (“conscience”) + -izar (a suffix forming verbs).
