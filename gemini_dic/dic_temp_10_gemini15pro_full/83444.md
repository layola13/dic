habitations:/ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃənz/
n.
住处，住所
places where people live; dwellings
例句：The government is providing temporary habitations for the earthquake victims. 政府正在为地震灾民提供临时住所。

例句：The cliff face was dotted with the habitations of birds.  悬崖壁上点缀着鸟类的巢穴。

近义词：dwellings, residences, homes, abodes
反义词：none (lack of habitation could be homelessness)
词性：noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Late Middle English habitacioun, from Old French habitacion, from Latin habitātiō (“dwelling, habitation”), from habitō (“I dwell, inhabit”), frequentative of habeō (“I have, hold, possess, inhabit”).
