granitic: /ɡrəˈnɪtɪk/
adjective
花岗岩的; 似花岗岩的; 坚硬的
Resembling or characteristic of granite; hard and unyielding.
The geologists found granitic rocks in the mountain range. 地质学家在山脉中发现了花岗岩岩石。
The CEO's granitic expression showed no sign of compromise. 首席执行官坚定的表情表明他不会妥协。

近义词: granitelike, igneous, plutonic, hard, inflexible, unyielding
反义词: soft, yielding, flexible

词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *graniticus, from Latin granum (“grain”) + -iticus.
