flophouses:/ˈflɑːphaʊzɪz/
noun
廉价旅馆；低级客栈
A cheap, run-down hotel or rooming house, often frequented by homeless or transient people.
The documentary followed the lives of several people living in flophouses.  这部纪录片讲述了几个住在廉价旅馆的人的生活。
The area was full of flophouses and bars. 这个地区到处都是廉价旅馆和酒吧。

近义词:dosshouses, hostels, shelters, lodging houses
反义词:luxury hotels, five-star hotels, resorts
词性:noun
