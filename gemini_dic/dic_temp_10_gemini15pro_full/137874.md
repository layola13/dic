nonviscid:/nɒnˈvɪskɪd/ | adj. | 非粘性的，无粘性的 | Not viscous; having little or no viscosity.  A nonviscid fluid flows easily. |  The lubricant was nonviscid, allowing for smooth movement of the parts.  润滑剂是非粘性的，可以使零件平稳移动。| Synonyms: inviscid, fluid, watery | Antonyms: viscous, sticky, thick | adjective
