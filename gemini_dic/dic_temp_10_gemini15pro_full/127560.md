necrosed:/nəˈkroʊst/ /ˈnekrəʊzd/| adj.|坏死的，坏疽的|Affected with necrosis; mortified.  Tissue or cells that have died.|The surgeon removed the necrosed tissue.  外科医生切除了坏死的组织。|Synonyms: gangrenous, mortified, dead, decayed|Antonyms: living, healthy, vital|adjective

necrose:/nəˈkroʊs/ /ˈnekrəʊz/| v.|坏死，(使)发生坏疽|To undergo necrosis; to cause necrosis.|The untreated wound began to necrose.  未经处理的伤口开始坏死。|Synonyms: decay, rot, decompose|Antonyms: heal, regenerate, grow|verb
