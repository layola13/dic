acescency: /ˌæsɪˈsɛnsi/
n.
酸败；发酸
the process of becoming acescent or sour, or the state of being slightly sour
The acescency of the milk made it undrinkable. 牛奶变酸了，不能喝了。

近义词: sourness, acidity, tartness
反义词: sweetness, alkalinity
词性: 名词


USAGE->
There is a noticeable acescency in the wine. = 这酒明显有酸败的味道。

The acescency of the dough indicated that it had gone bad. = 面团的酸味表明它已经变质了。


PHRASE->
the acescency of... = ...的酸败
detect the acescency = 检测酸败
prevent acescency = 防止酸败
due to acescency = 由于酸败
acescency development = 酸败发展


词源:
From Latin *acescens, present participle of acescere (“to turn sour”), from acēre (“to be sour”).
来自拉丁语 *acescens，acescere（“变酸”）的现在分词，来自 acēre（“变酸”）。
