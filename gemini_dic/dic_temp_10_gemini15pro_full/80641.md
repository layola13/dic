gopher:/ˈɡoʊfər (US) / ˈɡəʊfə(r) (UK)| n.|地鼠，囊鼠；(计算机)一种信息检索系统|A burrowing rodent with fur-lined pouches on the outside of its cheeks, found in North and Central America.  Also, a distributed document search and retrieval network protocol used in the early days of the internet. |例句：The gopher quickly disappeared into its burrow. 地鼠迅速钻进了洞里。

例句：He used a gopher client to access files on the university server. 他使用gopher客户端访问大学服务器上的文件。|近义词：ground squirrel, (for software) Veronica|反义词: N/A|noun
