cliffs:/klɪfs/| n.|悬崖，峭壁|A steep, high rock face, especially at the edge of the sea.|The cliffs overlooking the ocean were breathtaking. 俯瞰大海的悬崖令人叹为观止。|近义词：bluffs, precipices, crags|反义词:plains, valleys|noun

Etymology: From Old English *clif, from Proto-Germanic *klifą (“cliff, rock, shore”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)klei- (“to lean, incline”). Cognate with Dutch klif (“cliff”), German Kliff (“cliff”).
