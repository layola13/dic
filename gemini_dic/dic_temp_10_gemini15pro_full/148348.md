overspecification:/ˌoʊvərˌspɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度规范，过度指定，过分规定 | The act of specifying something in too much detail, often unnecessarily or redundantly.  This can lead to increased complexity, cost, and limitations in design or implementation. |  The contract's overspecification made it difficult to adapt to changing market conditions.  合同的过度规范使得难以适应变化的市场环境。

Overspecification of the product requirements led to delays and cost overruns. 产品需求的过度规范导致了延误和成本超支。 |近义词：over-engineering, overdesign, redundancy |反义词: underspecification |词性: noun
