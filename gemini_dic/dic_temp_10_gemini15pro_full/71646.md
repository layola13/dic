flatteners:/ˈflætənərz/ | n. |扁平化因素；夷平者；使变平的事物 | Things or forces that level or reduce differences, often in a global context. | The book "The World Is Flat" discusses various "flatteners" like the internet and outsourcing that have leveled the global playing field. 这本书《世界是平的》讨论了各种“扁平化因素”，例如互联网和外包，这些因素使全球竞争环境趋于平等。 |  近义词: levelers, equalizers | 反义词:  intensifiers, amplifiers | 词性: 名词(plural)

flatten:/ˈflætn/ | v. |使变平；夷平；压扁；摧毁；彻底击败 | To make something flat or flatter; to knock down or destroy something; to defeat someone decisively. |  He flattened the cardboard box for recycling. 他把纸板箱压扁以便回收。 | 近义词: level, smooth, crush, demolish | 反义词: raise, elevate, build | 词性: 动词

flat:/flæt/ | adj. |平的；扁平的；单调的；不景气的 | Having a level surface; lacking depth or dimension; dull; unprofitable. | The painting was criticized for its flat, lifeless colors. 这幅画因其呆板、毫无生气的色彩而受到批评。 | 近义词: level, even, smooth, dull | 反义词: uneven, bumpy, exciting | 词性: 形容词
