mutteringly:/ˈmʌtərɪŋli/ | adv. | 嘀咕地，咕哝地 | In a low, indistinct voice; with a mutter.  Expressing something in a quiet and unclear way, often because of annoyance or embarrassment. | He answered mutteringly, clearly unhappy with the situation. 他咕哝着回答，显然对情况很不满。| She read the instructions mutteringly to herself. 她小声嘀咕着读着说明书。 | 近义词：mumblingly, under one's breath, indistinctly | 反义词：clearly, distinctly, aloud | 词性：副词
