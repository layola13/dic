baggala:/bəˈɡɑːlə/ | n. | 巴加拉帆船 (一种阿拉伯帆船) | A type of Arab sailing vessel, typically with one or two masts and a lateen sail. |  The baggala sailed gracefully across the Arabian Sea. 巴加拉帆船优雅地航行在阿拉伯海上。

The old baggala was no match for the modern steamship. 老旧的巴加拉帆船无法与现代的汽船匹敌。 | 近义词: dhow, felucca | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "steamship" as a contrasting vessel type) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic بَغَلَة (baġala), meaning "mule."  The reason for this name is uncertain, but it might refer to the vessel's ability to carry cargo like a mule.
