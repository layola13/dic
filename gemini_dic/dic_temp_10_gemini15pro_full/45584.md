curmudgeonlinesses:/kərˈmʌdʒənlinəsəz/
|n.|脾气暴躁，乖戾|The state of being a curmudgeon; a curmudgeonly disposition or character.  A collection or aggregation of curmudgeonly traits or behaviors.|His curmudgeonlinesses were legendary, alienating even his closest friends. 他脾气暴躁是出了名的，甚至疏远了他最亲密的朋友。|近义词：grumpiness, cantankerousness, irascibility, churlishness|反义词：amiability, affability, geniality, good humor|词性: 名词

词源:  From curmudgeon + -liness + -es.  "Curmudgeon" is of uncertain origin, possibly from the Anglo-French *cornmudgin, "corn hoarder," or from the obsolete word *mudge, "to stir up mud."
