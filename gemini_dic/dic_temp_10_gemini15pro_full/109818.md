lexeme's:/ˈlɛksiːmz/| n.| 词位(的)|A lexeme is a unit of lexical meaning that exists regardless of any inflectional endings it may have or the number of words it may contain.  词位是一个词汇意义的单位，独立于它可能拥有的任何屈折词尾或它可能包含的单词数量。|例句：The lexeme "run" has many forms, such as "runs," "ran," and "running." 词位“run”有很多形式，例如“runs”、“ran”和“running”。|近义词：word, term, vocabulary item|反义词: (No direct antonym for a linguistic term like lexeme)|词性: noun
