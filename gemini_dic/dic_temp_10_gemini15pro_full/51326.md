detasselling:/diːˈtæsəlɪŋ/
n.|雄穗去除；去雄|The process of removing the tassel from a corn plant.  去除玉米植株雄穗的过程。|Detasselling is a common practice in hybrid seed corn production. 去雄是杂交玉米种子生产中的常见做法。|Synonyms: tassel removal, emasculation|Antonyms: pollination|Noun
