geomancies: /ˈdʒiːoʊmænsiz/
n.
占卜术；风水术；地理占卜
The art of divination by means of figures or lines or geographic features.  Interpreting signs from the earth to predict the future or determine the best location for buildings, etc.
He studied ancient geomancies to understand the placement of the temple. 他研究古代风水术以了解寺庙的位置。
Synonyms: divination, augury, soothsaying, forecasting
Antonyms: science, reason, logic
noun

geomancy: /ˈdʒiːoʊmænsi/
n.
占卜术；风水术；地理占卜
The art of divination by means of figures or lines or geographic features. Interpreting signs from the earth to predict the future or determine the best location for buildings, etc.
She consulted a geomancer before building her house. 她在建房子之前咨询了一位风水师。
Synonyms: divination, augury, soothsaying, forecasting
Antonyms: science, reason, logic
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *geomancie*, from Medieval Latin *geomantia*, from Late Greek *geōmanteía*, from *gē* (earth) + *manteía* (divination).  Ultimately from Greek *gē* (earth) and *mantis* (prophet, seer).
