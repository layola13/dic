hexavalent:/ˌhɛksəˈveɪlənt/| adj.|六价的|Containing six valence electrons or having a valence of six.  Relating to or containing a chromium atom with a valence of six, especially in the toxic and carcinogenic form chromium(VI).|Chromium(VI) is a hexavalent form of chromium.|近义词: sexivalent|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider specifying a different valence, like "trivalent")|词性: adjective
