countercaster:/ˈkaʊntərˌkæstər/
noun
反咒术者，反制施法者
Someone who casts a counter-spell; a spellcaster who counters another's magic.
The countercaster interrupted the wizard's spell, saving the village from destruction. 反咒术者打断了巫师的咒语，拯救了村庄免于毁灭。
Synonyms: spellbreaker, magic disruptor
Antonyms: caster, spellcaster
