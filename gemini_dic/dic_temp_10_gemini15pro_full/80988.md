gradations:/ɡrəˈdeɪʃənz/
n.
渐变；等级；刻度
a series of gradual changes, stages, or degrees
例句：The artist captured the subtle gradations of light in the painting. 艺术家在画作中捕捉到了微妙的光线渐变。

例句：There are gradations of social status within the company. 公司内部存在社会地位的等级差异。


近义词：stages, degrees, steps, nuances, levels
反义词：sudden change, abrupt change, discontinuity

词性: 名词 (n.)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gradationem, accusative of *gradatio ("a step, climbing"), from Latin gradus ("a step, degree").
