overassisting:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɪstɪŋ/
| adj. |过度协助的；过度帮助的|Providing too much assistance, often to the point of hindering independence or development.|The overassisting parent hovered around their child, constantly correcting and preventing them from making mistakes.|近义词：overhelping, overprotective, smothering|反义词:neglectful, inattentive|词性:形容词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "assisting" (present participle of "assist," from Latin "assistere" meaning "to stand by, support")
