globed:/ɡloʊbd/| adj.|球形的，球状的|Shaped like a globe; spherical. |例句：The full, globed moon hung in the night sky.  满月，球状的月亮挂在夜空中。

例句：The astronomer studied the globed surface of Mars. 天文学家研究了火星的球形表面。|近义词：spherical, round, orbicular|反义词：flat, planar|词性:形容词
词源:From globe + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin globus (“sphere, ball”).
