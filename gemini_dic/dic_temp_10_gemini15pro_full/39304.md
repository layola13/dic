conidium:/kəˈnɪdiəm/ (plural conidia: /kəˈnɪdiə/) | n. | 分生孢子 | An asexual spore formed on a conidiophore of certain fungi. |  The conidia are dispersed by the wind. 分生孢子随风传播。|  Synonym: asexual spore, mitospore | Antonym: sexual spore | noun


conidiophore:/kəˈnɪdiəˌfɔːr/ | n. | 分生孢子梗 | A specialized hypha on which one or more conidia are produced. | The conidiophores of this fungus are branched. 这种真菌的分生孢子梗是分枝的。| Synonym: spore-bearing hypha | Antonym: N/A | noun
