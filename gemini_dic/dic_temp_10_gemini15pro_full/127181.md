naturalizing:/ˈnætʃrəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使归化；使入籍；移植；使适应
the process of becoming a citizen of a country that you were not born in
例句：He is naturalizing as a British citizen. 他正在归化为英国公民。

例句：The process of naturalizing can take several years. 归化过程可能需要几年时间。

近义词：acclimatizing, adapting, assimilating
反义词：denaturalizing, expatriating
词性：verb
