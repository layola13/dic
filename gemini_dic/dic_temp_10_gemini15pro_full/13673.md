attractively:/əˈtræktɪvli/
adverb
动词
迷人地，有吸引力地
in a way that attracts interest or pleasure
She was attractively dressed in a red dress. 她穿着一件红色的连衣裙，非常迷人。
The food was attractively presented. 食物摆放得很诱人。
近义词:appealingly, charmingly, enchantingly, pleasantly
反义词:unattractively, repellently, displeasingly
