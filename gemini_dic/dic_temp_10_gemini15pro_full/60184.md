efficaciously:/ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəsli/
adverb
有效地；奏效地
in a way that produces the desired result or effect.
The problem was dealt with efficaciously.  这个问题得到了有效的处理。
The medicine worked efficaciously to reduce the fever.  这种药有效地降低了发烧。
近义词: effectively, successfully, potently
反义词: ineffectively, unsuccessfully, inefficiently
词性: adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *efficaciōsus, from Latin efficax (“effective”) + -ōsus.
