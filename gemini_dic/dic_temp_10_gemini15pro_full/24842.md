brownfields:/ˈbraʊnˌfiːldz/
n.
棕地，污染场地
Abandoned or underused industrial or commercial sites that may be contaminated by hazardous waste or pollution.
The city council is looking at ways to redevelop the old factory site, a large brownfield in the heart of the city.  市议会正在研究如何重新开发旧工厂遗址，这是市中心的一大片棕地。
The government offers tax incentives to encourage businesses to invest in brownfields redevelopment. 政府提供税收优惠，鼓励企业投资棕地重建。
synonyms: contaminated land, derelict land, industrial wasteland
antonyms: greenfield, undeveloped land
