egoistical:/ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪkl/ /ˌɛɡəʊˈɪstɪkl/
adjective
自私的；利己主义的
Thinking only of oneself; self-centered.
He was so egoistical that he never considered anyone else's feelings. 他太自私了，从不考虑别人的感受。

She made an egoistical decision to put her career ahead of her family. 她做了一个自私的决定，把事业放在家庭之上。

近义词:selfish, self-centered, narcissistic, conceited
反义词:altruistic, selfless, considerate, compassionate
词性:adjective
