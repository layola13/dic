ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bracelet, diminutive of bracel "armlet," from Vulgar Latin *brachiale, from Latin bracchium "arm," from Greek brakhion "arm."  Meaning narrowed to "ornament for the wrist" by the 18th century.
