outsmarts:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrts/
verb
以智取胜, 智胜
to defeat or gain an advantage over someone by being more clever
She outsmarted her opponents in the negotiation. 她以智取胜，在谈判中击败了对手。
outwit, outmaneuver, outfox
be outsmarted by, be fooled by
