overfluentness:/ˌoʊvərˈfluːəntnəs/
|n.| 过度流利，口若悬河 |The overfluentness of his speech made it difficult to follow his train of thought. 他说话过于流利，让人难以跟上他的思路。|
|英文解析: The quality or state of being excessively fluent, often to the point of being verbose or rambling.|例句：His overfluentness sometimes obscured the meaning of what he was trying to say. 他过于流利，有时反而掩盖了他想要表达的意思。|近义词：volubility, garrulity, loquacity|反义词: taciturnity, reticence|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + fluent + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Fluent" comes from the Latin "fluentem," meaning "flowing." "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
