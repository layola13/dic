cragged:/ˈkræɡɪd/ | adj. |崎岖的，陡峭的|Having many crags; rugged and rocky.|The climbers scaled the cragged peaks of the Himalayas. 登山者攀登了喜马拉雅山脉崎岖的山峰。|近义词：rugged, jagged, rough, uneven|反义词：smooth, even, flat|词性:形容词
词源:From crag + -ed.  Crag comes from a Celtic origin, related to Welsh *craig and Breton krag.
