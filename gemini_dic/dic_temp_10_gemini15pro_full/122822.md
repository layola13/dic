moled:/moʊld/ | v. | 发霉的；被虫蛀的 | Affected by mold or decay; eaten into by moths or other insects. | The old books in the attic were moled and smelled musty.  阁楼里的旧书都发霉了，闻起来有股霉味。 |  The bread has moled. 面包发霉了。 | 近义词: moldy, mildewed, decayed, rotten | 反义词: fresh, new, preserved | 词性: 动词 (past participle of *mole*)


词源: From Middle English *moled*, past participle of *molen*, from Old English *molian* "to grow moldy," from Proto-Germanic *muljaną* "to be musty, moldy," from Proto-Indo-European *mel-/*mōl- "soft, bruised."  The word is related to the noun "mold" (fungus).
