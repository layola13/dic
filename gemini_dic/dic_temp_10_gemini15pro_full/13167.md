at all events:/æt ɔːl ɪˈvɛnts/| adv.|无论如何，至少，在任何情况下|In any case; at least; in any event.  This phrase emphasizes certainty despite potential obstacles or uncertainties.|例句：At all events, we'll meet tomorrow. 无论如何，我们明天都会见面。

例句：I may be late, but at all events I will be there. 我可能会迟到，但无论如何我都会到那里。|近义词：anyway, in any case, at any rate|反义词:specifically, particularly|词性:副词
