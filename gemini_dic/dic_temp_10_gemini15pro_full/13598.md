attenuator:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪtər/ | n. | 衰减器，减弱器 | A device that reduces the strength of a signal, power, or current. |  The engineer adjusted the attenuator to lower the signal strength. 工程师调整了衰减器以降低信号强度。 |  Synonyms: damper, reducer, suppressor | Antonyms: amplifier, booster | Noun

attenuator:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪtər/ | n. | 衰减器，减弱器 | A substance or material that reduces the virulence of a disease. | The scientist researched the use of an attenuator to weaken the virus. 科学家研究了使用衰减剂来减弱病毒毒性的方法。 | Synonyms: mitigator, moderator | Antonyms: intensifier, aggravator | Noun
