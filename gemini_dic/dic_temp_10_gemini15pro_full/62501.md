endothermic:/ˌɛndoʊˈθɜːrmɪk/| adj.|吸热的|Absorbing heat; characterized by or formed with absorption of heat.  A reaction or process accompanied by or requiring the absorption of heat.|(Chemistry) (of a reaction or process) accompanied by or requiring the absorption of heat.|Example 1:  Melting ice is an endothermic process.  融化冰是一个吸热过程。

Example 2: Photosynthesis is an endothermic reaction because it requires energy from sunlight. 光合作用是一个吸热反应，因为它需要来自阳光的能量。|近义词:heat-absorbing|反义词:exothermic|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From endo- (within) + -therm (heat) + -ic (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *endon* (within) and *thermē* (heat).
