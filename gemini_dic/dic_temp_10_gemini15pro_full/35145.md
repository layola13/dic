clouting:/ˈklaʊtɪŋ/| n. |影响力；声望；（尤指在社交媒体上）获得关注；打击；击打|The act of gaining influence or attention, especially on social media, or the act of hitting or striking something.|He's been clout chasing for years, trying to build his online presence.  他多年来一直在追求影响力，试图建立自己的网络形象。|The boxer delivered a powerful clouting blow to his opponent's jaw. 拳击手重重一击打在了对手的下巴上。|近义词：influence, prestige, hitting, striking|反义词：obscurity, anonymity|词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "clout," meaning "to hit hard," from Old English *clūt, meaning "lump, piece of cloth, patch."  The meaning evolved to include "influence" and "prestige" in the 20th century, particularly in American slang.  The more recent sense of "gaining attention online" emerged in the 2010s.
