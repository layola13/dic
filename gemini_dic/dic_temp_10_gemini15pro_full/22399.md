Often used as an imperative to encourage dancing. | Let's boogie-on-down tonight! 今晚让我们尽情跳舞吧！ |  The DJ told everyone to boogie-on-down. DJ告诉大家一起狂欢。 | 近义词: party, dance, celebrate, get down | 反义词: relax, rest | 词性: 动词短语
