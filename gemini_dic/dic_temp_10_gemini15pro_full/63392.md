entotic:/enˈtɒtɪk/| adj.|内源性的，眼内的|Relating to images that originate within the eye itself, as opposed to from light entering the eye.|Floaters are an example of entotic phenomena.  飞蚊症是内源性现象的一个例子。|Entoptic images can be caused by debris in the vitreous humor. 内源性图像可能是由玻璃体中的碎片引起的。|Synonyms: internal, intrinsic|Antonyms: extrinsic, external|Adjective
