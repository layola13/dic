gelatinous:/dʒɪˈlætɪnəs/ | adj. | 胶状的；凝胶状的 | Having a jelly-like consistency; viscous. | The jellyfish had a gelatinous body. 水母有一个胶状的身体。 | The soup had cooled into a gelatinous mass. 汤冷却成了胶状物。| 近义词: jelly-like, viscous, gummy, slimy | 反义词: firm, solid, watery | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from French gélatineux, from gélatine (see gelatine).
