forward-looking:/ˈfɔːrwərdˌlʊkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɔːwədˌlʊkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|前瞻性的；有远见的|Considering or planning for the future; progressive.|例句：The company has a forward-looking approach to research and development.  这家公司对研发工作采取前瞻性的方法。|近义词：progressive, visionary, far-sighted, anticipatory|反义词:short-sighted, myopic, backward-looking|词性:形容词

词源:  "forward" (向前) + "looking" (看的)  复合词，由表示方向的副词 "forward" 和表示动作的动词现在分词 "looking" 组成。
