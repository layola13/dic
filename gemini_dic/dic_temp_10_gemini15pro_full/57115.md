douse:/daʊs/ | vt. | 浇灭，熄灭；使浸透 | to extinguish (a fire, flames, etc.) by pouring water or other liquid on it; to plunge into water; drench | Firefighters quickly doused the flames. 消防队员迅速扑灭了火焰。|  近义词: extinguish, quench, soak, drench | 反义词: ignite, kindle | 词性: 动词

douse:/daʊs/ | n. | 浸泡，浇灭 | an act of dousing; a drenching | The boat received a douse from a large wave. 这艘船被一个大浪头浇了个透。|  近义词: soaking, drenching | 反义词: drying | 词性: 名词
