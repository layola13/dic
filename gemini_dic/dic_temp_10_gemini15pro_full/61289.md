embalms:/ɪmˈbɑːmz/
verb
(对尸体)进行防腐处理，(对尸体)涂以香料
treats (a corpse) with preservatives in order to prevent decay.
The Egyptians embalmed their pharaohs.  埃及人对他们的法老进行防腐处理。
embalm the body  对尸体进行防腐处理
synonyms: preserve, mummify
antonyms: decay, decompose
verb
