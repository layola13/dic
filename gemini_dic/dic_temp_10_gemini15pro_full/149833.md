paleornithology:/ˌpeɪliːɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒi/ /ˌpeɪlioʊɔːrnɪˈθɑːlədʒi/| n. |古鸟类学|The study of fossil birds.  It involves the identification, classification, and evolutionary history of extinct avian species. |例句：Paleornithology has revealed much about the evolution of flight in birds. 古鸟类学揭示了很多关于鸟类飞行进化的信息。

例句：New discoveries in paleornithology are constantly changing our understanding of prehistoric ecosystems.  古鸟类学的新发现不断改变着我们对史前生态系统的理解。|近义词：avian paleontology|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
