counterconventions:/ˌkaʊntər.kənˈven.ʃənz/| n. |反传统，反常规，打破常规|Actions or behaviors that go against established norms or conventions.|例句：Her art is full of counterconventions, challenging traditional aesthetics. 她的艺术充满了反传统，挑战了传统的审美观。

例句：The punk rock movement was built on counterconventions, rejecting mainstream values. 朋克摇滚运动建立在反传统的基础上，拒绝主流价值观。|近义词：unconventionality, heterodoxy, nonconformity|反义词: conventions, traditions, norms|词性:noun
