ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *barbarie*, from Late Latin *barbaria*, from Latin *barbarus* "foreign, barbarous," from Greek *barbaros* "foreign, non-Greek," probably imitative of unintelligible speech.  The plant sense is from the use of barberry bark as a source of a yellow dye.
