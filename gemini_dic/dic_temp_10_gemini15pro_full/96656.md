industrialization:/ɪnˌdʌstriələɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|工业化|The process of transforming an agrarian society into an industrial one, through the development of large-scale manufacturing and other industries.|The Industrial Revolution marked a period of rapid industrialization in Europe. 欧洲的工业革命标志着一个快速工业化的时期。|近义词：industrialisation, development, modernization|反义词:ruralization, deindustrialization|词性:noun

Etymology: From *industrialize* + *-ation*. *Industrialize* comes from *industrial* + *-ize*. *Industrial* derives from *industry* + *-al*. *Industry* comes from French *industrie* or directly from Latin *industria* "diligence, activity," from *industrius* "diligent, active."
