brouded:/ˈbruːdɪd/| adj.|沉思的，忧郁的，阴沉的|Deeply thoughtful, often in a worried or melancholy way.  Reflecting or expressing gloom.|例句：He sat with a brouded expression, contemplating his next move. 他带着沉思的表情坐着，思考着下一步行动。|近义词：pensive, thoughtful, contemplative, gloomy, morose, sullen|反义词：cheerful, lighthearted, jovial|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From brood (v.) + -ed.  The verb "brood" comes from Old English "brōd" meaning "to sit on eggs," later extending to the metaphorical sense of dwelling on something persistently.
