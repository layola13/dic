autopneumatic:/ˌɔːtəʊnjuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. |自动充气的；气动的| Operating by compressed air, especially automatically; self-inflating. | The life raft was autopneumatic, inflating within seconds of hitting the water. 救生筏是自动充气的，落水后几秒钟内就会膨胀。| The tyres are autopneumatic and adjust pressure according to the terrain. 轮胎是气动的，可根据地形调节压力。| 近义词: self-inflating, automatic, pneumatic | 反义词: manual |词性: 形容词
