inquartation:/ɪnˌkwɔːrˈteɪʃən/ |n.|四分法，加银分离金|The process of separating gold from silver by alloying the mixture with silver, then dissolving the silver in nitric acid, leaving the gold behind.|The inquartation method was crucial for accurately determining the gold content in alloys.|近义词:assaying, refining|反义词:alloying|词性:名词
