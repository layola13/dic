lardlike: /ˈlɑːrdlaɪk/
adjective
像猪油的；肥胖的
Resembling lard; fat.
His face was lardlike and pale. 他的脸像猪油一样肥胖苍白。
The man had a lardlike belly.  这个男人有一个像猪油一样的大肚子。

近义词: fatty, obese, flabby, porcine
反义词: lean, thin, slender, slim
词性: adjective
