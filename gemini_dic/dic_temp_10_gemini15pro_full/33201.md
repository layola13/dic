chromospheric:/ˌkroʊməˈsfɪrɪk/ | adj. | 色球的，色球层的 | Relating to the chromosphere, a layer of the Sun's atmosphere above the photosphere. | The chromospheric network is a web-like pattern visible in the chromosphere. 色球网络是在色球层中可见的网状图案。 |  The chromospheric temperature rises dramatically with altitude. 色球层的温度随着高度急剧上升。 | 近义词: solar (relating to the sun) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective

chromosphere:/ˈkroʊməˌsfɪr/ | n. | 色球，色球层 | A gaseous layer of the Sun's atmosphere between the photosphere and the corona. |  The chromosphere is visible during a total solar eclipse. 在日全食期间可以看到色球层。 | Scientists study the chromosphere to learn more about the Sun's activity. 科学家研究色球层以了解更多关于太阳活动的知识。 | 近义词: N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
