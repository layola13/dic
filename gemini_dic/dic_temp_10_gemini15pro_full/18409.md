below-the-fold:/bɪˈloʊ ðə foʊld/ | adj. | 下半部分，不被立即看到的部分 |  Refers to the content of a webpage that is not visible without scrolling down.  Originates from the physical fold of a newspaper, where the less important stories were placed. | The most important information should be above-the-fold, while less crucial details can be placed below-the-fold.  网站最重要的信息应该放在首屏，而不太重要的细节可以放在下半部分。 |  Synonyms: lower section, bottom half | Antonyms: above-the-fold, top half | adjective
