choosiness:/ˈtʃuːzinəs/| n. |挑剔，讲究|The state of being choosy; fastidiousness or discrimination in making a selection.|Her choosiness about food made it difficult to find a restaurant everyone liked. 她对食物的挑剔使得很难找到一家大家都喜欢的餐馆。|近义词：selectivity, fastidiousness, discrimination, pickiness|反义词：indiscriminateness, acceptance|词性: noun
词源: From *choose* (v.) + *-iness* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or state).  The verb *choose* comes from Old English *cēosan*, from Proto-Germanic *keusaną*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵews-*.
