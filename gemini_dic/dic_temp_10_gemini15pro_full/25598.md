bump-against:/bʌmp əˈɡɛnst/| phrasal verb | 撞上，碰上，遇到(困难等) | To physically collide with something or to encounter a problem or obstacle. | I bumped against the table in the dark. 我在黑暗中撞到了桌子。

The project bumped against several unforeseen difficulties.  这个项目遇到了几个意想不到的困难。 | 近义词：encounter, run into, come up against | 反义词：avoid, bypass | 词性：动词短语

bump:/bʌmp/ | n. v. | 碰撞，肿块；撞击 | A protuberance, swelling, or lump; to strike or collide with. |  The car had a bump on its fender. 汽车的挡泥板上有一个凹痕。

He bumped his head on the low ceiling. 他的头撞到了低矮的天花板上。 | 近义词：knock, hit, collide | 反义词：miss, avoid | 词性：名词，动词

against:/əˈɡɛnst/ | prep. | 反对，倚靠，逆着 | In opposition to; in contact with; contrary to. |  She leaned against the wall. 她倚着墙。

He swam against the current. 他逆流而游。 | 近义词：opposite, versus, counter | 反义词：with, for, pro | 词性：介词
