baleen:/bəˈliːn/| n. |鲸须，鲸 baleen plates|A horny substance growing in plates from the upper jaws of certain whales, forming a fringe-like sieve used to filter plankton from the water.|The whale uses its baleen to filter krill from the ocean.  鲸鱼用它的鲸须从海洋中过滤磷虾。|例句：Baleen whales are filter feeders. 须鲸是滤食动物。|近义词：whalebone|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
