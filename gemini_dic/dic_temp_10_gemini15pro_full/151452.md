paronymy:/ˈpærənɪmi/| n.|同音异义词, 语音相近的词|The words "affect" and "effect" are a common example of paronymy. “affect”和“effect”是同音异义词的常见例子。|例句：The professor lectured on paronymy, explaining the subtle differences between similar-sounding words. 教授讲授了同音异义词，解释了发音相似的词语之间的细微差别。

例句：Understanding paronymy is crucial for avoiding misunderstandings in both written and spoken communication. 理解同音异义词对于避免书面和口头交流中的误解至关重要。|近义词：homophony, near homonym|反义词: antonym, heteronym|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *paronymie*, from Ancient Greek *παρωνυμία (parōnumía)*, from *παρά (pará, “beside, near”) + ὄνομα (ónoma, “name”)*.
