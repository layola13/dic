amomum:/əˈmoʊməm/| n. |豆蔻|A genus of plants in the ginger family, Zingiberaceae, whose members are native to China, the Indian Subcontinent, Southeast Asia, New Guinea, and Queensland. Several species are grown for their seed, which is used as a spice (cardamom).|The seeds of the amomum are used in many Asian cuisines. 豆蔻的种子在许多亚洲菜肴中使用。| The amomum plant thrives in tropical climates. 豆蔻植物在热带气候中茁壮成长。|Synonym: cardamom|Antonym: N/A|Noun
