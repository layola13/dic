what /wɒt; hwɒt (UK), wʌt; hwʌt (US)/ |pron. det. adv. int.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something or someone.|
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What time is it? 现在几点了？


近义词：which|反义词: (no single antonym for "what")|pronoun
近义词：how (when asking about manner or degree)|反义词: (no single antonym for "what")|determiner
近义词：how (when asking about manner or degree)|反义词: (no single antonym for "what")|adverb
近义词：huh, pardon|反义词: (no single antonym for "what")|interjection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What does she do for a living? = 她做什么工作？

一般过去时:
What did he say? = 他说了什么？
What happened yesterday? = 昨天发生了什么事？

现在完成时:
What have you done? = 你做了什么？
What has she eaten today? = 她今天吃了什么？

过去完成时:
What had happened before I arrived? = 在我到达之前发生了什么事？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what was going on. = 我希望我知道发生了什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to solve the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What are you thinking about? = 你在想什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
She didn't tell me what she wanted. = 她没有告诉我她想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样？/ ...如何？
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？/ 为什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
