eliminators:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪtərz/
n.
消除器; 清除器; 排除器;
A person or thing that eliminates something.
The team were the eliminators in the last round of the competition.  这个队伍在上一轮比赛中是淘汰者。
The new cleaning product is marketed as a stain eliminator.  这种新的清洁产品作为一种去污剂销售。

synonyms:remover, eradicator, exterminator
antonyms:creator, producer, originator
noun
