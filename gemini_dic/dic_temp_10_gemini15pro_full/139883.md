od'ing:/ˈoʊdiɪŋ/
|v.| 过量服用（药物）；吸毒过量 (通常指致死)|Taking an overdose of a drug, usually resulting in death. |He od'ed on sleeping pills. 他因服用过量安眠药而死亡。| 近义词: overdosing| 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 abstaining from drugs)|词性: 动词 (源于动名词)


词源:  "OD'ing" is a shortened form of "overdosing," which comes from "overdose."  "Over" indicates an excess, and "dose" refers to a specific quantity of medicine or drug.
