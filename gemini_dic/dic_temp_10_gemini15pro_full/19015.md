bestud:/bɪˈstʌd/| vt.|仔细研读，埋头研究|To study something intensely and thoroughly.|He bestudded the ancient texts for years. 他多年来仔细研读古代文献。|近义词：pore over, scrutinize, examine, study|反义词: neglect, ignore|词性:动词|词源：From be- + stud (meaning "to cover with studs"). Originally meant to adorn or cover with studs, later evolved to mean covering a subject with intense study.|
