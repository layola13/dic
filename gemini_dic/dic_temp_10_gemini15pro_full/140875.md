on-the-square:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈskwɛr/ | adv. | 正直地，诚实地 | Honestly and fairly. | He always deals on-the-square with his clients. 他总是公平地对待他的客户。 | They conducted the negotiations on-the-square. 他们光明正大地进行了谈判。| 近义词: honestly, fairly, ethically | 反义词: dishonestly, unfairly, unethically | 词性: 副词


词源:  This idiom originated in the 18th century and likely refers to the carpenter's square, a tool used to ensure right angles and straight lines, symbolizing honesty and correctness.
