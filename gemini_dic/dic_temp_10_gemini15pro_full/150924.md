parallelogrammatic:/ˌpærəˌlɛləˈɡræmætɪk/
adj.
平行四边形的, 由平行四边形组成的
having the shape of a parallelogram; relating to or resembling a parallelogram.
The parallelogrammatic shape of the crystal was immediately apparent.  这颗水晶的平行四边形形状立刻显现出来。
The architect incorporated parallelogrammatic designs into the building's facade.  建筑师将平行四边形设计融入建筑的立面。
近义词:rhomboid, diamond-shaped
反义词:trapezoidal
