hit-off:/hɪt ˈɒf/ (US) /hɪt ˈɒf/ (UK)| phrasal verb |模仿；相处融洽；立即成为朋友；成功地开始|To imitate someone, often humorously; to get along well with someone immediately; to have a successful start.|例句：He hit off the professor's mannerisms perfectly. 他完美地模仿了教授的举止。

例句：They hit it off immediately and became good friends. 他们一见如故，很快就成了好朋友。

例句：The project hit off well, exceeding all expectations.  这个项目一开始就很成功，超出了所有预期。

近义词：imitate, mimic, get along, click, succeed|反义词：clash, disagree, fail|词性:动词短语

hit-off:/hɪt ˈɒf/ (US) /hɪt ˈɒf/ (UK)| n. |  (尤指体育比赛中的)良好开端|A good start, especially in a sports match.|例句：The team got off to a great hit-off, scoring two goals in the first ten minutes. 该队开局良好，前十分钟就进了两球。

例句：Her hit-off in the golf tournament put her in a strong position. 她在高尔夫锦标赛中的良好开端使她处于有利地位。


近义词：good start, strong beginning|反义词：bad start, poor beginning|词性:名词

词源:  "Hit" 和 "off" 的组合，"hit" 有“击中、达到”的意思，"off" 在这里表示“离开、开始”的意思。结合起来，"hit off" 就有了“成功开始”或“模仿得恰到好处”的意思。
