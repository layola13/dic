boilings:/ˈbɔɪlɪŋz/| n.| 沸腾，煮沸（通常用复数形式）|The act of something boiling; bubbling up under the action of heat.  The rapid vaporization of a liquid.|The chef checked the boilings of the pot on the stove. 厨师检查了炉子上锅里的沸腾情况。|近义词：ebullition, effervescence|反义词:freezing, cooling|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *boyling, *boylyng, *byling, *bylyng, present participle of boilen (“to boil”), from Old French boillir (“to boil”), from Latin bullire (“to bubble, boil”).
