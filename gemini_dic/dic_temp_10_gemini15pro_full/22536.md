boon:/buːn/
n.
恩惠，好处，福利
something that is helpful or beneficial.
例句：The rain was a boon to the farmers. 这场雨对农民来说是件好事。

例句：The new software proved a boon to researchers. 新软件对研究人员来说非常有用。

近义词：blessing, advantage, benefit, gift
反义词：curse, disadvantage, misfortune, bane
词性: 名词

boons:/buːnz/
n.
恩惠，好处，福利（boon的复数形式）
plural form of boon.
例句：The recent economic upturn has been a boons to many businesses. 最近的经济好转对许多企业来说都是好事。

例句：Modern technology offers many boons to society. 现代科技为社会提供了许多好处。

近义词：blessings, advantages, benefits, gifts
反义词：curses, disadvantages, misfortunes, banes
词性: 名词

词源:
From Old Norse *bón "a petition, prayer," from Proto-Germanic *bōniz (source also of Old English bēn "prayer, petition, request; favor, boon," Old High German bēna "supplication," Gothic bōn "prayer").
