orthodoxes:/ˈɔːrθədɑːksiːz/
n.
正统观念，正统做法
Accepted or traditional ways of doing things.
例句：The orthodoxes of the scientific community were challenged by the new findings.  科学界的正统观念受到了新发现的挑战。

例句：He challenged the orthodoxes of the art world with his unconventional style. 他以其非传统的风格挑战了艺术界的正统观念。

近义词：conventions, traditions, norms
反义词：heterodoxies, unorthodoxies
词性：名词 (plural noun)


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *orthodoxie*, from Old French *orthodoxie*, from Late Latin *orthodoxia*, from Greek *orthodoxía*, from *orthódoxos* ‘right-believing’, from *orthós* ‘straight, right’ + *dóxa* ‘opinion, belief’.
