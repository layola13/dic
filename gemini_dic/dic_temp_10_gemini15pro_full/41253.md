corbelling:/ˈkɔːrbəlɪŋ/ | n. | 悬臂结构；托臂；梯形叠涩 |  A construction technique where masonry units (like bricks or stones) are each placed slightly further out than the one below, creating a projecting structure without external supports. | The castle walls featured intricate corbelling. 城堡的墙壁上有复杂的托臂结构。| synonym: cantilever, bracket, overhang | antonym: support, underpinning | noun

corbelling:/ˈkɔːrbəlɪŋ/ | v. | 建造悬臂结构；用托臂支撑 | To construct using the corbelling technique. | They corbelled the arch to create a wider entrance. 他们用托臂支撑拱门以创造更宽的入口。 | synonym: cantilever, project, overhang | antonym: support, underpinning | verb
