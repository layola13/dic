blanket:/ˈblæŋkɪt/ /ˈblæŋkət/| n. adj. v. |毯子; 覆盖物; 毯状物; (隔音、绝缘或保护性的)覆盖层;  |A large piece of soft material, typically wool, used for warmth as a bed covering.  As a verb, it means to cover something completely.|例句：She wrapped herself in a warm blanket. 她用一条温暖的毯子把自己裹了起来。|近义词：cover, quilt, duvet, comforter, spread |反义词:sheet |词性:名词, 形容词, 动词


blanket:/ˈblæŋkɪt/ /ˈblæŋkət/| adj. v. |全面的，总括的，通用的; 覆盖; 掩盖; |Applying to all conditions or instances; comprehensive. As a verb, it can mean to cover completely with something.|例句：The government imposed a blanket ban on smoking in public places. 政府对在公共场所吸烟实行全面禁令。|近义词：overall, general, comprehensive, universal, widespread |反义词:specific, limited, partial |词性:形容词, 动词
