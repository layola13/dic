horsewhipped:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪpt/| v. |用马鞭抽打；严厉斥责| To flog or beat with a horsewhip; to punish severely or criticize harshly. |例句：The slave was horsewhipped for disobeying his master.  这个奴隶因为不服从主人而被鞭打。

例句：The politician was horsewhipped in the press for his controversial comments.  这位政治家因其有争议的言论而遭到媒体的严厉批评。|近义词：flogged, lashed, whipped, castigated, rebuked|反义词：praised, commended|词性：动词
