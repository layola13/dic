palmiferous: /ˌpɑːlmɪˈfɛrəs/
adjective
多棕榈的；产棕榈的
Bearing palms; abounding in palms.
例句：The palmiferous islands of the Pacific are a popular tourist destination. 太平洋上多棕榈的岛屿是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。

例句：The explorers discovered a lush, palmiferous valley hidden deep within the jungle. 探险家们在丛林深处发现了一个郁郁葱葱、生长着许多棕榈树的山谷。

近义词：palm-bearing, palm-rich, palm-covered
反义词:  barren, treeless
