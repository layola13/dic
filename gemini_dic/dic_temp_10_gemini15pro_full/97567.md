/ˈɪnɡruːp/ | n. | 内群体；自己所属的群体 | A group to which a person belongs, or thinks he or she belongs, and with which he or she identifies, especially in contrast to an outgroup. |  Example:  He felt a strong sense of belonging to his ingroup. 他对自己的内群体有强烈的归属感。

Example:  The ingroup often shows prejudice against the outgroup. 内群体通常会对外部群体表现出偏见。| Synonym: clique, circle, inner circle | Antonym: outgroup | noun


/ˈɪnɡruːp/ | adj. | 内群体的 | Belonging to or relating to an ingroup. | Example: Ingroup bias can lead to unfair treatment of others. 内群体偏见会导致不公平地对待他人。

Example:  Ingroup solidarity is an important factor in team success. 内群体团结是团队成功的关键因素。 | Synonym: inner | Antonym: outgroup | adjective

From in- + group.  "In" indicating belonging or inclusion, and "group" referring to a collection of individuals.
