dullard:/ˈdʌlərd/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or slow-witted person. |He called his competitor a dullard during the debate.  他在辩论中称他的对手是个笨蛋。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, simpleton|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant|noun

dullard:/ˈdʌlərd/| n.|迟钝的人|A person who is not quick to understand or learn. |The dullard struggled to keep up with the fast-paced lecture. 这个迟钝的人很难跟上快节奏的讲座。|Synonyms: slowpoke, laggard|Antonyms: whiz, prodigy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dullare, from dull + -ard. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dulą (“dull”).
