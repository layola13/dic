multicolor:/ˈmʌltiˌkʌlər/ (US) /ˈmʌltiˌkʌlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.| 多色的，五彩的|Having many colors.|The dress was multicolor, with stripes of red, blue, and green. 这条裙子是多色的，有红色、蓝色和绿色的条纹。|近义词：polychromatic, multicolored, varicolored|反义词：monochromatic, single-colored|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From multi- + color.  "Multi-" is a combining form meaning "many," derived from Latin "multus." "Color" comes from Old French "color" (modern French "couleur"), ultimately from Latin "color."
