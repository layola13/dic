overcomplicating: /ˌoʊvərˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ (US) | v. (present participle) | 过于复杂化；把…搞得过于复杂 | Making something more complicated than it needs to be. |  Example:  He was overcomplicating the issue by bringing up irrelevant details. 他提及无关紧要的细节，把问题复杂化了。

Example: Stop overcomplicating things! It's a simple problem with a simple solution. 别把事情搞得太复杂！这是一个简单的问题，有一个简单的解决方法。

| 近义词:  complicating, confusing, muddling | 反义词: simplifying, streamlining | 词性: Verb (present participle)
