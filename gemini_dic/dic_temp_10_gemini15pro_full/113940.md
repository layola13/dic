magazine-show: /ˈmæɡəˌzin ʃoʊ/ (US), /ˈmæɡəziːn ʃəʊ/ (UK) | n. | 杂志型节目；杂志式节目 | A television or radio programme that presents a variety of short features on different subjects.  It often includes interviews, news stories, and entertainment segments, similar to the diverse content found in a magazine. | Example:  "60 Minutes" is a popular magazine-show that has been on television for decades. 六十分钟是一个流行的杂志型节目，已经在电视上播出了几十年。

Example:  Many local television stations produce their own magazine-shows covering local events and personalities. 许多地方电视台制作他们自己的杂志型节目，涵盖当地事件和人物。| 近义词: newsmagazine, variety show, anthology series | 反义词: documentary, serial drama | 词性: noun
