overweeningness:/ˌoʊvərˈwiːnɪŋnəs/| n. |傲慢；自负；狂妄|The state of being excessively arrogant, presumptuous, or conceited.  An excessive belief in one's own importance or ability.|His overweeningness alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了他的同事们。|Synonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, presumption, conceit, superciliousness|Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence|noun
