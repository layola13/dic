misnarrate:/ˌmɪsnəˈreɪt/ | v. | 错误地叙述，歪曲叙述 | To narrate or tell a story incorrectly, often by omitting crucial details or adding false information. | He misnarrated the events of the evening, making himself seem like the hero. 他歪曲了那天晚上的事情，把自己说成了英雄。 | The journalist misnarrated the facts, leading to a public outcry. 记者歪曲了事实，引起了公众的强烈抗议。 | synonyms: misrepresent, distort, falsify, garble | antonyms: report accurately, represent faithfully, portray truthfully | verb
