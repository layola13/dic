corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/| adj.|腐蚀性的；侵蚀性的|Having the quality of corroding or eating away; tending to cause corrosion.|Acid rain is highly corrosive to limestone buildings. 酸雨对石灰岩建筑物有很强的腐蚀性。|Synonyms: caustic, erosive, abrasive, mordant|Antonyms: protective, preservative|adjective

corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/| n.|腐蚀剂|A substance that corrodes.|Industrial corrosives must be handled with extreme care. 工业腐蚀剂必须非常小心地处理。|Synonyms: acid, alkali, oxidant|Antonyms: inhibitor, neutralizer|noun
