cronyism:/ˈkroʊnɪɪzəm/ /ˈkrəʊnɪɪzəm/| n. |裙带关系；任人唯亲；朋党主义|The practice of favoring one's friends, especially in political appointments, regardless of their qualifications.|His years in office were marked by cronyism and corruption. 他的任期充斥着任人唯亲和腐败。|
近义词:nepotism, favoritism, patronage|
反义词:impartiality, meritocracy|
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From crony + -ism. Crony (17th century) is of uncertain origin, possibly from Greek chronios "long-lasting," referring to a long-standing friendship.
