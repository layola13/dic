mishear:/ˌmɪsˈhɪr/ | v. | 误听；听错 | To hear (something) incorrectly. | I misheard her name and called her Mary instead of Marie. 我听错了她的名字，把她叫做玛丽而不是玛丽埃。| Example: He misheard the instructions and went the wrong way. 他听错了指示，走错了路. | Synonyms: misinterpret, misunderstand | Antonyms: hear correctly, understand | verb
