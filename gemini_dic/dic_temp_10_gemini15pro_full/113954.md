magdalena:/ˌmæɡdəˈleɪnə/ | n. | 玛德琳蛋糕；忏悔的妓女 | A small, rich, shell-shaped sponge cake.  Often associated with Mary Magdalene, a figure of repentance in the Bible. |  例句：She baked a batch of fresh magdalenas for breakfast. 她烤了一批新鲜的玛德琳蛋糕作为早餐。

例句：The novel explores the theme of redemption through the character of a modern-day magdalena. 这部小说通过一个现代忏悔的妓女的角色探索了救赎的主题。 | 近义词：madeleine, sponge cake | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
