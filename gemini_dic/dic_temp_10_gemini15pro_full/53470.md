disarming:/dɪsˈɑːrmɪŋ/
adjective
解除武装的；使人消除敌意的；迷人的
reducing hostility and suspicion; making someone like you
Her disarming smile put me at ease immediately. 她那令人放松的微笑立刻让我感到自在。
近义词: charming, winning, engaging, captivating
反义词: alarming, threatening, intimidating
词性: adjective

disarming:/dɪsˈɑːrmɪŋ/
verb (disarms, disarming, disarmed)
解除（某人）的武装；使（某人）消除敌意；
to take weapons away from someone; to make someone feel less hostile or suspicious
The police disarmed the robber. 警察解除了抢匪的武装。
His friendly manner disarmed her. 他友好的态度消除了她的敌意。
近义词: charm, win over, captivate
反义词: alarm, threaten, intimidate
词性: verb

词源:  From dis- (expressing reversal) + arm (to furnish with weapons).  Essentially meaning to reverse the process of arming.
