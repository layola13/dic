elver:/ˈɛlvər/| n. |幼鳗，鳗线|A young eel, especially one migrating up rivers from the sea.|例句：The elvers were swimming upstream. 幼鳗正逆流而上。|例句：Fishermen catch elvers to sell to eel farms. 渔民捕捞鳗线卖给养鳗场。|近义词：young eel, glass eel|反义词:adult eel|noun
