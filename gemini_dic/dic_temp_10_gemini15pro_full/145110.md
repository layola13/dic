overapprehensiveness:/ˌoʊvərˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪvnəs/| n. |过度忧虑，过分担心|Excessive worry or anxiety, often about potential problems or dangers.|Her overapprehensiveness about the upcoming exam made it difficult for her to focus on studying. 她对即将到来的考试过度忧虑，导致她难以集中精力学习。|例句：His overapprehensiveness about his health led him to see the doctor frequently.  他对自己的健康过分担心，导致他经常去看医生。|近义词：anxiety, worry, solicitude, apprehension, nervousness|反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility, serenity|词性: noun

词源:  From over- + apprehensiveness, from apprehensive + -ness. Apprehensive ultimately from Latin apprehendere "to seize, grasp," from ad- "to" + prehendere "to grasp, seize."
