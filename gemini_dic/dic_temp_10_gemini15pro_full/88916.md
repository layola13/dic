hold-the-bag:/ˌhoʊld ðə ˈbæɡ/ | idiom | 被迫承担责任；背黑锅 | To be left with responsibility for something unpleasant or disadvantageous, especially something that others have abandoned. |  Example 1: When the company went bankrupt, the small investors were left holding the bag.  公司破产时，小投资者成了背锅侠。

Example 2:  He promised to help with the project, but at the last minute he backed out, leaving me holding the bag. 他答应帮忙做这个项目，但在最后一刻他退出了，让我承担了所有的责任。 | Synonyms:  left in the lurch,  take the rap, carry the can, bear the brunt | Antonyms:  pass the buck, shirk responsibility | idiom
