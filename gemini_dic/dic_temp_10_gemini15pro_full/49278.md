deliberative:/dɪˈlɪbərətɪv/ /dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪv/| adj.| 慎重的；深思熟虑的；审议的|Characterized by or involving careful consideration or discussion.|The council held a deliberative meeting to discuss the budget proposal.  市议会召开了一次审议会议，讨论预算提案。|近义词：thoughtful, careful, reasoned, cautious|反义词：rash, impulsive, hasty|词性:形容词

deliberative:/dɪˈlɪbərətɪv/ /dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪv/| n.| 审议会；审议团体|A group or assembly that deliberates.|The deliberative voted to approve the new policy.  审议会投票通过了新政策。|近义词：council, assembly, committee|反义词: (No clear single antonym, perhaps "mob" or "dictatorship" depending on context) |词性:名词
