meroistic:/ˌmɛrəˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 部分的，局部发育的 | Relating to or exhibiting merogony, a type of development in which the egg cytoplasm is not equally divided among the blastomeres, resulting in some cells containing only part of the necessary materials for development. |  The meroistic ovariole produces nurse cells that contribute to the development of the oocytes.  局部发育的卵巢管会产生滋养细胞，有助于卵母细胞的发育。|  Meroistic cleavage is characteristic of insect eggs. 局部卵裂是昆虫卵的特征。|  holomeristic | partial | adjective
