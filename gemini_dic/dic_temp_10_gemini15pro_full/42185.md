coulisse:/kuːˈliːs/ | n. |幕后；舞台侧景；侧幕；（尤指窗帘的）滑槽|A grooved piece of wood, metal, or plastic in which a sliding part of something such as a window or door moves.  A piece of scenery at the side of a stage. The area of a theater offstage, especially the wings.| The curtains were drawn along the coulisses. 窗帘沿着滑槽拉上。| The actor waited in the coulisses for his cue.  演员在侧幕等待他的提示。|Synonyms: backdrop, wing, side scene, curtain track|Antonyms:  forestage, center stage|noun
