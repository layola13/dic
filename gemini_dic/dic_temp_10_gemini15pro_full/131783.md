nonconnivences:/ˌnɑːnkəˈnɪvənsɪz/
n.
lack of connivance; absence of collusion or secret cooperation.
中文翻译：不串通；不共谋；没有默契
英文解析：The state of not conniving; absence of secret or tacit understanding or cooperation.
例句：The investigators found clear evidence of nonconnivences between the two parties. 调查人员发现了双方没有串通的明确证据。

例句：The nonconnivences of the witnesses strengthened the prosecution's case.  证人之间没有串通加强了控方的论点。

近义词：noncollusion, independence
反义词：connivance, collusion, conspiracy
词性：名词 (plural)
