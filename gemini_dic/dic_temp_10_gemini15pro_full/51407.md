deterministic:/dɪˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 决定性的; 确定性的 |  Relating to determinism; determined or decided beforehand by events that are fixed or inevitable. |  A deterministic system is one in which future states are entirely determined by past states.  一个确定性系统是指未来状态完全由过去状态决定的系统。 | predictable, foreseeable, fixed, preordained | random, stochastic, probabilistic, indeterminate | adjective
