instil:/ɪnˈstɪl/ (US) /ɪnˈstɪl/ (UK)| vt. | 灌输，逐渐 instill |To gradually establish (an idea or attitude, especially a desirable one) in a person's mind.| The coach instilled a sense of discipline in his players. 教练给他的队员灌输了纪律意识。

|Example:  Her parents instilled in her a love of reading. 她父母在她心中灌输了对阅读的热爱。

|Example: The teacher tried to instil a sense of responsibility in her students. 老师试图向学生灌输责任感。


|Synonym: implant, infuse, inculcate, introduce, inspire|Antonym:  eradicate, remove, uproot|verb
