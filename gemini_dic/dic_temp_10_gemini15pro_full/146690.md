overheartily:/ˌōvərˈhärdəlē/
adv.
过分热情地，过分真诚地
with excessive enthusiasm or sincerity.
He thanked us overheartily for our help. 他过分热情地感谢我们的帮助。

近义词: excessively, effusively, too enthusiastically
反义词: coldly, apathetically, halfheartedly
