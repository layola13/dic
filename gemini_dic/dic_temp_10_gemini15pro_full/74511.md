frapping:/ˈfræpɪŋ/| v.| 包扎，缠绕；(尤指用绳索)捆绑；(用冷敷)处理|The doctor is frapping the wound. 医生正在包扎伤口。|例句：He frapped the rope tightly around the package. 他用绳子把包裹紧紧地捆了起来。|例句：She frapped her sprained ankle with ice. 她用冰敷扭伤的脚踝。|近义词：binding, wrapping, lashing, securing|反义词：unwrapping, loosening, untying|词性:动词

词源:  From the French word "frapper," meaning "to strike" or "to hit."  The meaning evolved to include wrapping or binding, likely related to the action of securing something by striking or fastening it.
