bid-in:/ˈbɪdɪn/ | n. | 底价买回，内部投标 | The act of a seller buying back their own item at an auction, often because the bidding did not reach the reserve price. |  The painting was bid-in at $5,000. 这幅画以5000美元的底价被收回。 | Synonym: buy-back, repurchase | Antonym: sell, auction | noun
