normalized:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzd/ | adj. |标准化的，规范化的|Made conform to a standard; brought into a common form or arrangement.|例句：The data has been normalized for easier comparison. 数据已被标准化以便于比较。|近义词：standardized, regularized|反义词：irregular, abnormal|adjective

normalized:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪzd/ | v. |标准化，规范化|(of a database) reorganize (data) into tables that satisfy the rules for a relational database.  Also means to make (something) conform to a standard.|例句：The database needs to be normalized to improve efficiency.  数据库需要标准化以提高效率。|近义词：standardize, regularize|反义词：distort, corrupt|verb
