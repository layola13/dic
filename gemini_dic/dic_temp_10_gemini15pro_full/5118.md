alleghenian: /ˌæləˈɡeɪniən/ | adj. | 阿勒格尼的；阿巴拉契亚的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Allegheny Mountains or the Allegheny Plateau. |  The alleghenian forests are known for their diverse flora and fauna. 阿勒格尼森林以其多样化的动植物群而闻名。 |  The alleghenian region experiences distinct seasonal changes. 阿勒格尼地区经历明显的季节变化。 | 近义词: Appalachian | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Allegheny + -ian.  Allegheny itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from a Lenape word meaning "fine river".
