defract:/dɪˈfrækt/ | v. | 使(光、声等)衍射；使偏斜 | To cause (something, such as light or sound waves) to spread or bend when passing through a narrow opening or across an edge. |  The prism defracted the light into a rainbow of colors. 棱镜将光衍射成彩虹般的颜色。

The sound waves were defracted as they passed through the doorway. 声波穿过门口时发生了衍射。 | 近义词: deviate, deflect | 反义词: focus, concentrate | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēfrāctus, from dē- + frangere "to break."  Literally, "to break away from."
