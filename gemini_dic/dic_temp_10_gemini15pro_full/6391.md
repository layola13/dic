amphigonous:/æmˈfɪɡənəs/
adjective
两性繁殖的
Reproducing sexually, involving the union of male and female gametes.
例句：Most flowering plants are amphigonous, relying on pollination for reproduction. 大多数开花植物是两性繁殖的，依靠授粉进行繁殖。

例句：Amphigonous reproduction ensures genetic diversity within a species. 两性繁殖确保了物种内的遗传多样性。

近义词：sexual, bisexual
反义词：asexual, parthenogenetic
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek amphi- (both) + gonos (offspring, seed)
