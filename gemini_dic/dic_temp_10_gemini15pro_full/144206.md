outsings:/ˌaʊtˈsɪŋz/
verb
胜过…的歌声；比…唱得好
To sing better or more loudly than someone else.
She completely outsings everyone else in the choir. 她比唱诗班里的其他人都唱得好。
近义词: outperforms, surpasses
反义词: is outsung by
词性: verb
