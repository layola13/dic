/ˈmɑːrkʌp/ |/ˈmɑːrkʌp/| n. | 标记；加价；涨价；成本加成 |A markup is an amount added to the cost price of goods to determine the selling price.|The shop owner added a 30% markup to the wholesale price. 店主在批发价的基础上加价30%。|近义词：increase, surcharge, premium|反义词：discount, markdown| noun

/ˈmɑːrk ʌp/ |/ˈmɑːrk ʌp/| vt. | 标高价格；提高成本；加价 | To markup is to increase the price of something.| The retailer marked up the price of the imported goods. 零售商提高了进口商品的价格。|近义词：increase, raise, inflate|反义词：reduce, lower, discount| verb
