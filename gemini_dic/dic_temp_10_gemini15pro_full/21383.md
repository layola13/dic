blot:/blɒt/ (UK), /blɑːt/ (US)| n. v.|污点，墨渍；缺点，瑕疵；吸墨纸；涂污，弄脏|A mark or stain, typically one made by ink, paint, or dirt. Also, an act of absorbing excess ink or liquid.  As a verb, it means to dry or absorb excess ink or liquid.|例句：The ink blot ruined the document. 墨水渍毁了文件。|近义词：stain, smudge, blemish, mark, spot, flaw, defect|反义词：cleanliness, purity|词性: noun, verb

blot:/blɒt/ (UK), /blɑːt/ (US)| v.|涂污，弄脏；吸干|To dry or absorb excess ink or liquid, or to make a mark or stain.|例句：She blotted the excess ink with a tissue. 她用纸巾吸干了多余的墨水。|近义词：absorb, soak up, dry, smear, smudge|反义词：wet, soak|词性: verb
