circuitous:/sərˈkjuːɪtəs/
adjective
迂回的，绕行的
(of a route or journey) longer than the most direct way.
The bus took a circuitous route to avoid the town centre. 公共汽车绕道而行，避开了市中心。

The journey was circuitous, taking us through several small towns before we finally reached our destination. 旅程迂回曲折，我们经过了好几个小镇才最终到达目的地。

synonym:indirect, roundabout, winding
antonym:direct, straight
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *circuitus*, past participle of *circuire* "to go around," from *circum* "around" + *ire* "to go."
