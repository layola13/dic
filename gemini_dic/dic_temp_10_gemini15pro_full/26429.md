BYOD:/biː waɪ əʊ diː/
noun
自带设备；自带移动设备办公
Bring Your Own Device:  A policy that allows employees to bring personally owned mobile devices (laptops, tablets, and smartphones) to their workplace, and to use those devices to access privileged company information and applications.
Many companies are now adopting a BYOD policy. 许多公司现在都采用自带设备的政策。
Our company's BYOD policy allows employees to use their own smartphones for work. 我们公司的自带设备政策允许员工使用自己的智能手机进行工作。

synonyms:  personal device use, employee-owned device use
antonyms:  company-issued device policy
