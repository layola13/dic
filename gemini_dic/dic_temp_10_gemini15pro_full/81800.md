grenadillo:/ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪloʊ/ /ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪləʊ/| n. |百香果；西番莲果；格拉纳迪拉木|A tropical American passion flower with edible fruit; the fruit of this plant, which has a hard rind and sweet, juicy pulp with numerous seeds. Also, the wood of certain tropical American trees of the genus Dalbergia, used for making musical instruments.|The grenadillo makes a delicious juice.  百香果可以做成美味的果汁。|Synonyms: passion fruit, granadilla|Antonyms: N/A|noun

grenadillo:/ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪloʊ/ /ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪləʊ/| n. |格拉纳迪拉木|The wood of certain tropical American trees of the genus Dalbergia, used for making musical instruments, especially clarinets and oboes.|Clarinets are often made from grenadillo wood.  单簧管通常由格拉纳迪拉木制成。|Synonyms: African Blackwood, Dalbergia melanoxylon|Antonyms: N/A|noun
