compline:/ˈkɒmplɪn/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmplɪn/ (US)| n.| 晚祷，睡前祈祷 |A service of evening prayer, traditionally the last of the canonical hours. |例句：The monks gathered in the chapel for compline.  僧侣们聚集在小教堂里做晚祷。

例句：Compline is a time for quiet reflection before sleep. 晚祷是睡前安静反思的时刻。|近义词：night prayer, vespers|反义词:matins, lauds|词性:noun
