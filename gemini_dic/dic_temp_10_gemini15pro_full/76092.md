furrow-like:/ˈfʌroʊˌlaɪk/
adjective
沟状的；像犁沟的
Resembling a furrow; having the appearance of a long, narrow trench or groove.
The old man's face was covered with furrow-like wrinkles. 老人的脸上布满了沟壑般的皱纹。
The tractor left furrow-like tracks in the mud. 拖拉机在泥泞中留下了犁沟状的痕迹。
近义词: grooved, furrowed, channeled
反义词: smooth, flat
