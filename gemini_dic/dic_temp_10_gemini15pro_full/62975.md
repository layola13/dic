enode:/ˈiːnoʊd/ | n. | 以太坊节点 | A computer that connects to the Ethereum network and maintains a copy of the blockchain. | Example: Running an enode allows you to participate in the Ethereum network and validate transactions. 运行一个以太坊节点可以让你参与以太坊网络并验证交易。 | Example:  The enode stores the entire history of the blockchain.  该节点存储了区块链的完整历史记录。 | Synonym: Ethereum node, node | Antonym: N/A | Noun
