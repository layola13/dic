fatal:/ˈfeɪtl/| adj.|致命的，毁灭性的，灾难性的|Causing death or disaster; leading to failure or ruin.|The accident proved fatal. 事故证明是致命的。|近义词：lethal, deadly, mortal, catastrophic|反义词：harmless, benign, beneficial|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Middle English *fatal*, from Middle French *fatal*, from Latin *fātālis* (“ordained by fate”), from *fātum* (“fate, destiny”).
