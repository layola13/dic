middle-of-the-roaders:/ˌmɪdl əv ðə ˈroʊdərz/ |n.| 中间派，温和派|People who hold moderate political views, avoiding extreme positions.| The middle-of-the-roaders in the party are trying to appeal to a wider range of voters.  该党中的温和派正试图吸引更广泛的选民。|Synonyms: moderates, centrists|Antonyms: extremists, radicals|Noun

middle-of-the-road:/ˌmɪdl əv ðə ˈroʊd/ |adj.| 中庸的，温和的|Not extreme, avoiding strong opinions or actions.|  He adopted a middle-of-the-road approach to the issue. 他对这个问题采取了中庸之道。|Synonyms: moderate, average, mainstream|Antonyms: extreme, radical, unconventional|Adjective
