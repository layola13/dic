nonperceptively:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɛptɪvli/
adverb
不易察觉地；未被察觉地
in a way that is not perceptible; without being noticed or perceived.
The changes happened nonperceptively over time. 这些变化随着时间的推移不易察觉地发生了。
The artist nonperceptively incorporated the symbols into the painting. 艺术家巧妙地将这些符号融入到画作中，不易察觉。
近义词: imperceptibly, subtly, invisibly
反义词: perceptibly, noticeably, obviously
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- (not) + perceptively (in a way that can be perceived).  Ultimately from Latin *percept-, past participle stem of percipere "to seize entirely, feel, understand," from per- "thoroughly" + capere "to take."
