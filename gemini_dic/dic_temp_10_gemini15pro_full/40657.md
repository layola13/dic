conver:/kənˈvɜːr/ (BrE) /kənˈvɝː/ (AmE)| v. | 聚集，汇合；集中；谈话|To come together from different directions; to meet at a particular point; To have a conversation.|The two rivers converge at this point. 这两条河流在此交汇。|近义词: meet, assemble, gather, congregate, talk, chat, discuss|反义词: diverge, separate, disperse|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *convergere*, from *com-* ("together") + *vergere* ("to incline, to turn").
