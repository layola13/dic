babiest:/ˈbeɪbiɪst/| adj.|最婴儿化的；幼稚的|Most infantile; childish.  A superlative form of "babyish," suggesting extreme immaturity or an excessive focus on childish things.|例句：He has the babiest voice I've ever heard. 他说话的声音是我听过的最幼稚的。

例句：She throws the babiest tantrums when she doesn't get her way.  当她得不到自己想要的东西时，她就会像个婴儿一样大发脾气。|近义词：childish, infantile, immature, puerile|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
