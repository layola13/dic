dignifies:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪz/| v. |使...显得尊贵；使...有尊严|Makes something seem worthy and impressive.|Her presence dignified the occasion. 她的出席使这个场合显得隆重。|近义词：ennobles, elevates, exalts|反义词：degrades, demeans, humiliates|词性:动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *dignificare*, from Latin *dignus* (worthy) + *-ficare* (make).
