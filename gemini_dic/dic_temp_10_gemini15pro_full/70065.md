feudal:/ˈfjuːdəl/| adj.|封建的，封建制度的|Relating to a social system that existed in Europe during the Middle Ages, in which people worked and fought for a lord in exchange for protection and the use of land.|The king rewarded his loyal knights with feudal estates.|Synonyms: medieval, aristocratic, hierarchical|Antonyms: democratic, egalitarian|adjective

feudalism:/ˈfjuːdəlɪzəm/| n.|封建主义|The social system of feudalism.|Feudalism was dominant in Europe for centuries.|Synonyms: feudal system, manorialism|Antonyms: democracy, republic|noun
