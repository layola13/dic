aretaics: /ˌærəˈteɪɪks/
n.
美德伦理学; 德性伦理学
The study of virtue and moral character.  It emphasizes the role of one's character and the virtues that one's character embodies for determining or evaluating ethical behavior.
例句：Aretaics focuses on the virtues of the actor rather than the consequences of actions. 德性伦理学关注的是行为者的美德，而不是行为的后果。

例句：Modern aretaics is often contrasted with consequentialism and deontology. 现代德性伦理学经常与结果主义和义务论进行对比。

近义词：virtue ethics, moral philosophy
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but contrasting ethical theories include) consequentialism, deontology
词性: noun
