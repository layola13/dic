deaconesses:/ˈdiːkənɪsɪz/
noun
女执事，女助手
a woman who has been ordained to serve a Christian church, typically one with a rank below that of a deacon.
The deaconesses helped the priest prepare for the service. 女执事们帮助牧师准备仪式。
近义词:church worker, female minister, assistant
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider layman/laywoman)
