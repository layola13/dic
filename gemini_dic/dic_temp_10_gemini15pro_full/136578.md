nonsalutarinesses:/ˌnɒnsæljuːˈtɛrɪnɪsɪz/ |n.|  无益；不健康；不卫生 |The nonsalutarinesses of their living conditions contributed to their poor health. | Their unhealthy habits are a testament to the nonsalutarinesses of their lifestyle. | 近义词: unhealthiness, insalubrity, noxiousness | 反义词: healthiness, salubrity, wholesomeness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- + salutary + -ness + -es (plural).  "Salutary" comes from Latin "salūtāris" meaning "healthful."  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning, resulting in "nonsalutary" meaning "not healthful."  "-ness" forms the noun, and "-es" marks the plural.
