cased:/keɪst/ | adj. | 有外壳的；装在盒子里的；(语法)格的 | enclosed in a case or covering; (grammar)  relating to or denoting a grammatical case | The lawyer presented a well-cased argument. 律师提出了一个论证充分的论点。 | synonyms: covered, enclosed, boxed | antonyms: open, exposed, uncovered | adjective


/keɪst/ | n. | 箱子；盒子；案例；实例 | an instance of something; a container or box | The detective studied the case carefully.  侦探仔细研究了这个案件。 | synonyms: instance, example, box, container | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *casse* (modern French *caisse*), from Latin *capsa* "box, case."  The grammatical sense derives from the idea of words being "put into cases" (inflections) to indicate their function in a sentence.
