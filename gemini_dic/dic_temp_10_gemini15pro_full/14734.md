awe-inspiring:/ˈɔːɪnspaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
令人惊叹的；令人敬畏的
inspiring a feeling of awe
The Grand Canyon is an awe-inspiring sight.  大峡谷是一个令人叹为观止的景象。
The sheer size of the universe is awe-inspiring. 宇宙的浩瀚令人敬畏。
synonyms: breathtaking, magnificent, stunning, impressive, wondrous, remarkable
antonyms: unimpressive, ordinary, mundane
词性: adjective
