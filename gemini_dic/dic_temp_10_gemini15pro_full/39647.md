conservation:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 保护，保存，守恒 |The act of preserving something from loss, waste, damage, or decay.  The careful use of natural resources to prevent them from being lost or wasted. |例句：The conservation of wildlife is crucial for maintaining biodiversity. 保护野生动物对于维护生物多样性至关重要。

|例句：Energy conservation is important for reducing our carbon footprint.  节约能源对于减少我们的碳足迹很重要。 |近义词：preservation, protection, maintenance |反义词：destruction, waste, depletion |词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin conservatio(n-), from conservare ‘preserve’.
