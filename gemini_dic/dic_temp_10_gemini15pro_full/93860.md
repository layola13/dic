immobile:/ɪˈmoʊbəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|不动的，静止的，固定的|Not able to move or be moved; motionless.|例句：The patient lay immobile in the bed. 病人一动不动地躺在床上。

例句：The immobile car blocked the entire street.  这辆无法移动的车挡住了整条街道。

近义词：motionless, fixed, static, stationary|反义词：mobile, movable, active|词性:形容词


immobile:/ɪˈmoʊbəl/ (US), /ɪˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK)| n.|不动产|Property, especially land or buildings.|例句：The family's immobiles include a large house and several acres of land.  这个家庭的不动产包括一栋大房子和几英亩土地。

例句：Investing in immobiles can be a good long-term strategy.  投资不动产可以是一个很好的长期策略。

近义词：real estate, property|反义词: (No direct antonym for the noun form)|词性:名词

词源: From French *immobile*, from Latin *immobilis*, from *in-* (not) + *mobilis* (movable).
