gigglier:/ˈɡɪɡliər/ | adj. |更容易笑的，更爱傻笑的 |More giggly; given to or characterized by more giggling. | She was much gigglier after a glass of wine. 她喝了一杯酒后更容易笑了。|  The children were gigglier than usual today. 今天孩子们比平时更爱傻笑。| 近义词：more lighthearted, merrier, more joyful | 反义词：more serious, more somber, more solemn | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From giggle + -ier (comparative adjective suffix). Ultimately from Middle English *giglen, of imitative origin.
