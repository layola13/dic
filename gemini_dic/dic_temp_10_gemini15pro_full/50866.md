desensationalizes:/diːˌsɛnsəˈʃeɪʃənəˌlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |  使不耸人听闻；使不引起轰动；降低…的轰动效应| To make something seem less exciting or shocking, especially by reporting it or presenting it in a calm or ordinary way. | The media desensationalized the story by focusing on the facts and avoiding emotional language. 媒体通过关注事实和避免使用煽动性语言，降低了这条新闻的轰动效应。| Synonyms: downplay, minimize, de-emphasize, understate | Antonyms: sensationalize, exaggerate, dramatize, hype | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + sensationalize.  Sensationalize itself comes from sensation (from Latin sensationem) + -al + -ize.  The prefix de- indicates removal or reversal.
