datsu:/dɑːtsu/ | n. | 脱出，脱离 |  Escape, withdrawal, secession.  Primarily used in Japanese compounds borrowed into English, often related to political or social separation. |例句：The datsu-A movement aimed to escape from Asia's perceived backwardness. 脱亚入欧运动旨在摆脱亚洲被认为的落后状态。

例句：Datsu-sara refers to escaping the salaryman lifestyle.  脱サラ指的是逃离上班族的生活方式。 | 近义词：escape, secession, withdrawal | 反义词：integration, joining, inclusion | 词性：名词
