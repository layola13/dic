double-breasted:/ˌdʌbəlˈbrɛstɪd/
adj.
双排扣的
Having two parallel columns of buttons and overlapping front flaps.
He wore a double-breasted navy blue suit. 他穿着一套双排扣的深蓝色西装。

double-breasted:/ˌdʌbəlˈbrɛstɪd/
adj.
双排扣的
A garment, especially a coat or jacket, fastened with two parallel rows of buttons and having one half of the front overlapping the other.
She looked smart in her double-breasted jacket. 她穿着双排扣的夹克衫，看起来很时髦。


ETYMOLOGY->
From "double" + "breasted". "Double" refers to the two rows of buttons, and "breasted" refers to the front of a garment covering the chest.

SYNONYMS->
(No direct synonyms, but describes a specific style)

ANTONYMS->
single-breasted
