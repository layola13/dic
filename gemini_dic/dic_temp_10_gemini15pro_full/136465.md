nonrevisions:/ˌnɒnriˈvɪʒənz/ | n. | 未修订版本；无修改版本 | Absence of revisions; documents or texts that have not been altered or updated. | The archive contained numerous nonrevisions of the author's manuscripts.  该档案馆包含了大量作者手稿的未修订版本。| Example:  The lawyer requested the nonrevisions of the contract to compare with the latest version. 律师要求查看合同的未修订版本，以便与最新版本进行比较。 | Synonyms: original versions, first drafts, unaltered copies | Antonyms: revisions, amended versions, updated copies | noun
