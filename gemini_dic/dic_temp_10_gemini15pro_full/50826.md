descriptively:/dɪˈskrɪptɪvli/
adverb|描述地，叙述地|In a way that describes something or someone; providing a description.  |The witness descriptively recounted the events of the robbery. 目击者详细地叙述了抢劫案的经过。|近义词：graphically, vividly, pictorially|反义词：briefly, concisely, succinctly|词性: adverb

词源: From the adjective "descriptive" + "-ly." "Descriptive" comes from the Latin "describere" (to write down, copy) + "-ive."
