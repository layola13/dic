linemen:/ˈlaɪnmən/| n. |线路工人；（尤指）架线工人；（美式橄榄球的）锋线球员|A lineman is a person who installs or repairs electrical or telephone lines.  Especially in American football, a lineman is a player who is positioned in the line of scrimmage.|The linemen worked tirelessly to restore power after the storm.  线路工人们在暴风雨后不知疲倦地工作以恢复电力。

The team's linemen are known for their strength and agility.  这支球队的锋线球员以他们的力量和敏捷而闻名。|近义词：wireman, cableman (for electrical work);  offensive lineman, defensive lineman (for football)|反义词:  None (highly context-specific)|noun
