babyishnesses:/ˈbeɪbiɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
幼稚的行为；孩子气的举动
The plural form of babyishness, referring to multiple instances of childish behavior or characteristics.
Her constant whining and pouting were just a few of her babyishnesses. 她不断的抱怨和撅嘴只是她幼稚行为中的一部分。
He needs to grow up and stop with all the babyishnesses. 他需要长大，别再做那些幼稚的事情了。
近义词：childishness, immaturity, puerility
反义词：maturity, sophistication
