materialising:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzɪŋ/
v.
实现；出现；形成
becoming real or actual; appearing
The opportunity she had been waiting for finally materialized. 她一直等待的机会终于实现了。
A figure materialized out of the fog.一个人影从雾中浮现出来。
Synonyms: appearing, emerging, manifesting, taking shape
Antonyms: disappearing, vanishing, dematerializing
verb
