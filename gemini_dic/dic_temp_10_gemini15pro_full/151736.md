parvenus:/ˈpɑːrvəˌnuːz/ (plural), /ˈpɑːrvəˌnuː/ (singular)| n.|暴发户；新贵|People who have recently become rich or powerful and are considered to be uncultured or lacking in social graces.  Derived from the past participle of the French verb parvenir "to arrive at, reach," from Latin pervenire "to arrive," from per- "through" + venire "to come."|Many of the city's parvenus flaunted their wealth with ostentatious displays.  这个城市里的许多暴发户都用炫耀的方式来展示他们的财富。|Synonyms: upstart, nouveau riche, social climber|Antonyms: aristocrat, old money, established family|Noun
