bess:/bes/| n.|女子, 姑娘, 女仆|A woman or girl; especially a girl or woman regarded as lively and attractive.  It can also refer to a female servant. |Example 1:  "Young Bess was known for her cheerful disposition." 年轻的贝丝以她开朗的性格而闻名。

Example 2: "The farmer's bess helped with the chores." 农夫的女仆帮忙做家务。|近义词：girl, lass, maid, servant|反义词:boy, man|词性:noun
