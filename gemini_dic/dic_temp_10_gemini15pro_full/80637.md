goost: /ɡuːst/ | n. | 精神，灵魂；气息；一阵强风 | A sudden strong rush of wind or air.  | A sudden goost of wind blew the door shut. 一阵强风把门吹关上了。 |  近义词：blast, gust, rush | 反义词: calm, stillness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *goste, *goost, from Old Norse gustr (“gust of wind”). Cognate with Swedish gust (“gust of wind”), Danish gust (“gust of wind”), Icelandic gustur (“gust of wind”). More at gust.
