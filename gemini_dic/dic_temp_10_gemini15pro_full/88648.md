hoard:/hɔːrd/ (US) /hɔːd/ (UK)| n. v. |贮藏，囤积；贮藏物|A hoard is a large amount of something valuable that is kept hidden. As a verb, it means to accumulate and keep something valuable, often in secret. |She kept a hoard of chocolate hidden in her closet. 她把大量巧克力藏在壁橱里。|Synonyms: stockpile, cache, stash, accumulation|Antonyms: distribute, share, disperse|noun
