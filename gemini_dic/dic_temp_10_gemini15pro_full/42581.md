counterplead:/ˌkaʊntərˈpliːd/
v.|反诉，提出反请求|To plead against (something).  To make a counterclaim or counter-allegation in legal proceedings.|The defendant counterpleaded that the plaintiff owed him money.|近义词：countersue, counterclaim|反义词: admit, concede|词性: verb

counterplead:/ˌkaʊntərˈpliːd/
n.|反诉，反请求|A counterclaim or counter-allegation in legal proceedings.|The defendant's counterplead alleged fraud on the part of the plaintiff.|近义词：counterclaim, countersuit|反义词: admission, concession|词性: noun
