carbonising:/ˈkɑːbənaɪzɪŋ/
v.
碳化，炭化
The process of heating a substance in the absence of air to decompose it into carbon and other volatile products.
Wood is carbonised to produce charcoal.  木材被碳化以生产木炭。
The intense heat carbonised the organic material.  高温使有机物质碳化。
Synonyms: charring, coking
Antonyms:  oxidizing, burning
verb
