fatty:/ˈfæti/ | adj. n. | 肥胖的，脂肪的；胖子 | Containing a lot of fat; a fat person. |  The doctor told him to avoid fatty foods. 医生告诉他要避免吃油腻的食物。| 近义词：oily, greasy, plump, obese (for a person) | 反义词：lean, thin, skinny (for a person) | 词性: 形容词, 名词

fatty:/ˈfæti/ | n. | 胖子 | An overweight or obese person (informal and often derogatory). |  He called him a fatty, which was very rude.  他叫他胖子，这很粗鲁。 | 近义词：chubster, roly-poly (less derogatory) | 反义词：thin person, skinny person | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From fat + -y.  The word "fat" comes from Old English "fætt", of Germanic origin.
