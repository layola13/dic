odontophorous:/ˌoʊdɑːnˈtɒfərəs/
adjective
有齿舌的；具齿舌的
bearing a toothed ribbon or tongue, specifically the radula, a rasping organ found in mollusks.
The snail is an odontophorous mollusk, using its radula to scrape algae off rocks.  蜗牛是一种有齿舌的软体动物，用它的齿舌从岩石上刮下藻类。
The odontophorous radula is key to the mollusk's feeding habits. 有齿舌的齿舌是软体动物摄食习惯的关键。
Near synonyms: radula-bearing
Antonyms:  N/A (lack of a direct antonym; one might describe a creature lacking a radula as "aradulate")
word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *odonto-* (ὀδόντ-), stem of *odous* (ὀδούς) "tooth" + *-phoros* (-φορος) "bearing," from *pherein* (φέρειν) "to bear, carry."
