arillus:/əˈrɪləs/| n. |假种皮|A fleshy outgrowth from the seed coat that partly or wholly surrounds the seed.  It is often brightly colored and attractive to animals, which aid in seed dispersal.|The yew tree has a bright red aril surrounding its poisonous seed.| 近义词:seed covering|反义词:|名词
