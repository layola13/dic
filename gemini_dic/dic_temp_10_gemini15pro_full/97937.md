innovating:/ˈɪnəveɪtɪŋ/
v.|进行创新|introducing new methods, ideas, or products.|The company is constantly innovating to stay ahead of the competition. 这家公司不断创新以保持竞争优势。|近义词:pioneering, developing, creating, inventing|反义词:copying, imitating, stagnating|词性:动词现在分词/动名词
Etymology: From Latin "innovare" (to renew, alter, change), from "in-" (in, into) + "novus" (new).
