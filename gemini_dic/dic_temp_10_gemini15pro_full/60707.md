electro-analysis:/ɪˌlɛktroʊəˈnælɪsɪs/| n.|电分析|The process of using electrochemical methods to analyze a substance, typically to determine its composition or concentration.|Electro-analysis is commonly used in environmental monitoring. 电分析常用于环境监测。|近义词:electrochemical analysis|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "classical chemical analysis")|noun

electro-analysis:/ɪˌlɛktroʊəˈnælɪsɪs/| adj.|电分析的|Relating to or involving electro-analysis.|Electro-analysis techniques are becoming increasingly sophisticated. 电分析技术正变得越来越复杂。|近义词:electrochemical|反义词: (No direct antonym)|adjective
