infernality:/ˌɪnfərˈnælɪti/
n.
地狱般的状态; 邪恶; 恶魔性
the quality of being infernal; hellishness.  A state of extreme wickedness or immorality.
The painting depicted the infernality of the underworld. 这幅画描绘了地狱般的景象。
The infernality of the dictator's regime was evident in the widespread torture and killings. 独裁者政权的恶魔性在普遍的酷刑和杀戮中显露无遗。
Synonyms: hellishness, fiendishness, devilishness, wickedness, depravity
Antonyms: heavenliness, divinity, goodness, virtue
