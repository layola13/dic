normans:/ˈnɔːrmənz/
noun
诺曼人
People of Normandy, a region in northern France, who notably conquered England in 1066.
The Normans introduced French language and culture to England.
诺曼人将法语和法国文化引入了英国。
近义词:Vikings, conquerors
反义词:Anglo-Saxons, English (in the context of the Norman Conquest)
词性:n.
