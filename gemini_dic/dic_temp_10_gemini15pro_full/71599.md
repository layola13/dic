flat-mate:/ˈflætmeɪt/ (US), /ˈflætmeɪt/ (UK)| n.|室友，合租公寓的人|A person who shares a flat with one or more other people.|例句：My flat-mate is a student from China. 我的室友是一位来自中国的学生。

例句：I get along really well with my flat-mates. 我和我的室友们相处得很好。

近义词：roommate, housemate|反义词:landlord, landlady|词性:noun
