anodyne:/ˈænədaɪn/| adj. n.|止痛的，安慰的；镇痛剂|Not likely to provoke dissent or offense; inoffensive, often deliberately so.  Also, a painkilling drug or medicine.|The politician gave an anodyne speech, hoping not to offend anyone.|近义词：bland, inoffensive, harmless, painkilling, soothing|反义词：provoking, stimulating, irritating, exacerbating|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *anodynus, from Ancient Greek ἀνώδυνος (anṓdynos, “painless”), from ἀν- (an-, “not, without”) + ὀδύνη (odúnē, “pain”).
