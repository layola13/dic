laws:/lɔːz/
n.
法律；法规；定律
A system of rules that a particular country or community recognizes as regulating the actions of its members and may enforce by the imposition of penalties.
The laws of physics.  物理定律。
The new law comes into force next month. 这项新法律下个月生效。
Synonyms: regulations, rules, statutes, ordinances
Antonyms: lawlessness, anarchy
noun
