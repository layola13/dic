cookie:/ˈkʊki/ (US) /ˈkʊki/ (UK)| n. |小甜饼，饼干|A small, flat, sweet baked good, typically made with flour, sugar, butter or shortening, and often including chocolate chips, nuts, or other ingredients.|例句：I baked a batch of chocolate chip cookies. 我烤了一批巧克力饼干。

例句：The website uses cookies to track user activity.  这个网站使用cookie来跟踪用户活动。|近义词：biscuit, cracker|反义词: None|词性:noun


cooky:/ˈkʊki/ (US) /ˈkʊki/ (UK)| n. |小甜饼，饼干|(Informal) A cookie.|例句：Grandma always has a jar of cookies on the counter. 奶奶的柜台上总有一罐饼干。

例句：He offered me a cooky with my tea. 他给了我一块饼干配我的茶。|近义词：cookie, biscuit|反义词: None|词性:noun


词源：Both "cookie" and "cooky" ultimately derive from the Dutch word "koekje," meaning "little cake." "Cookie" is the more common spelling in American English, while "cooky" is more common in British English, although both spellings are understood in both regions.
