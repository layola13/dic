overcontribution:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃən/
n.
过度捐款，超额捐款
An amount contributed in excess of the allowable limit.
Example:  He had to pay a penalty for his overcontribution to his retirement account.  他因为向退休账户过度捐款而不得不支付罚金。

Example: The overcontribution to the charity resulted in a refund.  对慈善机构的超额捐款导致了退款。

近义词:excess contribution, overpayment
反义词:undercontribution, underpayment
词性:名词
