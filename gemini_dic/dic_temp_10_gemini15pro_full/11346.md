arendator:/ˌærənˈdeɪtər/ | n. | 承租人，租赁人 | A person or organization that leases or rents something, especially land or property.  A tenant. | Example 1: The arendator agreed to pay a monthly rent for the farmland. 承租人同意按月支付农田租金。

Example 2: The arendator was responsible for maintaining the property during the lease period.  承租人负责在租赁期间维护房产。 | Near Synonyms: tenant, lessee, renter | Antonyms: lessor, landlord, owner | Noun
