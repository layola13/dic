nonduality:/ˌnɒnˈdjuːˈælɪti/ | n. |非二元性，不二|The state of being without duality; not separated into two parts or categories; oneness.|  Spiritual teachers often speak of the nonduality of mind and body.  精神导师经常谈到身心不二。| synonyms: oneness, unity, wholeness | antonyms: duality, separation, division | noun

non-duality:/ˌnɒnˈdjuːˈælɪti/ | n. |非二元性，不二|Another spelling variant, functionally equivalent to "nonduality."| Some texts prefer the hyphenated form, non-duality. 一些文本更喜欢用带连字符的形式，non-duality。| synonyms: oneness, unity, wholeness | antonyms: duality, separation, division | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + duality.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "duality" refers to the state of being two or having two parts.
