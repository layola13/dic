monomial:/ˌmɒˈnəʊmiəl/ /ˌmɑːˈnoʊmiəl/| n. |单项式|A single term in algebraic expression; a product of a constant and variables raised to non-negative integer powers.|Example: 3x²y is a monomial.  3x²y是一个单项式。|Example:  The expression 5x is a monomial. 表达式 5x 是一个单项式。|近义词:term|反义词:polynomial|词性:noun
