outgrowing:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊɪŋ/
verb
长得太大而不适用; 长大成熟而不再
growing too large for; growing up and beyond

She's already outgrown her school uniform.
她已经长得连校服都穿不下了。

He's outgrowing his childish fears.
他正逐渐克服他儿时的恐惧。

近义词: outstrip, surpass, exceed
反义词: shrink, diminish, regress
词性: verb (present participle)
