churchyards: /ˈtʃɜːrtʃjɑːrdz/ | n. | 教堂墓地；墓园 | A churchyard is an area of land surrounding a church, traditionally used for burials. |  The old churchyards were filled with weathered headstones.  古老的教堂墓地里满是风化的墓碑。 |  The poet was buried in the churchyard of his local church.  这位诗人被安葬在他当地教堂的墓地里. | Synonyms: cemetery, graveyard, burial ground | Antonyms: N/A | noun
