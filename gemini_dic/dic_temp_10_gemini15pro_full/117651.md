megazostrodon:/ˌmɛɡəˈzɒstrədɒn/ | n. | 巨嘴兽 | An extinct, shrew-like mammal considered to be one of the earliest mammals. It lived during the late Triassic and early Jurassic periods. | Megazostrodon is a key transitional fossil in the evolution of mammals. 巨嘴兽是哺乳动物进化过程中一个关键的过渡化石。|  Megazostrodon fossils have been found in Southern Africa. 巨嘴兽化石已在南部非洲被发现。 | synonym:  early mammal, mammal-like reptile (loosely) | antonym: None (as it's a specific extinct genus) | noun
