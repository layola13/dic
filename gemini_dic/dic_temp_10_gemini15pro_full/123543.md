monoxenous:/ˌmɒnɒkˈsiːnəs/ /ˌmɑːnɑːkˈsiːnəs/| adj.|单宿主的|Having only one host throughout its life cycle (of a parasite).|例句：The parasite is monoxenous, completing its entire life cycle within a single host.  这种寄生虫是单宿主的，在单个宿主内完成其整个生命周期。

例句：Monoxenous parasites are easier to study than those with complex life cycles involving multiple hosts. 单宿主寄生虫比那些生命周期复杂、涉及多个宿主的寄生虫更容易研究。|近义词：monogenic|反义词：heteroxenous, polyxenous|词性:adjective
