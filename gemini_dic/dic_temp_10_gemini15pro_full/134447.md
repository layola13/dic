nonirritant:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪtənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈɪrɪtənt/
adjective
无刺激性的；不刺激的
Not causing irritation to the skin or other tissues.
This cream is nonirritant and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款面霜无刺激性，适合敏感肌肤。
The baby wipes are nonirritant and hypoallergenic.  这些婴儿湿巾无刺激性且低过敏性。
近义词:soothing, gentle, mild
反义词:irritant, irritating
词性:adjective
