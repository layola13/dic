graphites: /ˈɡræfaɪts/| n. |石墨|A crystalline form of carbon, soft, black, and greasy, used as a lubricant, in pencils, and as a moderator in nuclear reactors.|The battery uses graphites as its anode material.  这种电池使用石墨作为其阳极材料。|Synonyms: plumbago, black lead|Antonyms: diamond (in terms of crystalline structure)|noun

graphites: /ˈɡræfaɪts/| n. |石墨 (复数)|Multiple pieces or types of graphite.|Different graphites exhibit varying properties. 不同种类的石墨表现出不同的特性。|Synonyms: plumbago, black lead (both usually singular)|Antonyms: diamonds (in terms of crystalline structure, usually plural)|noun
