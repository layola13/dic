expiators:/ˈekspiˌeɪtərz/
n.
赎罪者；补偿者
People who make amends or atone for a wrong or sin.
The expiators sought forgiveness for their past transgressions. 赎罪者为他们过去的罪过寻求宽恕。
The expiators offered sacrifices to appease the gods.  赎罪者们献祭来安抚神灵。
近义词:atoners, redeemers
反义词:sinners, offenders
词性:noun

expiate:/ˈekspiˌeɪt/
v.
赎罪；补偿
To make amends or atone for (guilt or sin).
He tried to expiate his sins by giving to charity. 他试图通过慈善捐赠来赎罪。
She sought to expiate her guilt through good deeds. 她试图通过做好事来消除她的罪恶感。
近义词:atone, redeem
反义词:offend, sin
词性:verb
