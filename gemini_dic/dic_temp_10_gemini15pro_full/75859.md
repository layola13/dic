functionalism:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlɪzəm/ /ˈfʌŋkʃənəˌlɪzəm/
n.
功能主义
A theory that emphasizes the interdependence of the parts of a system and how each part contributes to the overall functioning of the system.  It often focuses on the purpose or role that something serves within a larger context.
Functionalism played a major role in the development of sociology.  功能主义在社会学的发展中扮演了重要的角色。
The design of the building reflects the architect's belief in functionalism. 这座建筑的设计反映了建筑师对功能主义的信仰。
functionalist, pragmatism
structuralism, formalism
noun
