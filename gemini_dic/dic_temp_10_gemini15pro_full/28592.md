carefulnesses:/ˈkɛərfəlnɪsɪz/
n.
谨慎；小心；细心
The state of being careful; attention to detail and avoidance of errors.
She handled the antique vase with utmost carefulnesses. 她小心翼翼地拿着古董花瓶。
近义词:caution, prudence, diligence, meticulousness
反义词:carelessness, negligence, recklessness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From carefulness + -es (plural suffix).
Careful from care (Old English caru, cearu "sorrow, anxiety, grief") + -ful (suffix indicating "full of").
