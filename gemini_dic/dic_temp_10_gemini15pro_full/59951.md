edibleness:/ˈɛdɪbəlnəs/| n.|可食性；适口性|The edibleness of the mushrooms was questioned. 这些蘑菇的可食性受到了质疑。|例句：The edibleness of wild berries should always be confirmed before consumption. 野生浆果的可食性应该在食用前得到确认。

例句：The chef tested the edibleness of the new dish. 厨师测试了新菜的可食性。|近义词：comestibility, palatability|反义词：inedibility, unpalatability|词性：名词

词源: From edible + -ness.  "Edible" comes from the Late Middle English, from the Late Latin *edibilis, from the Latin edere "to eat."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
