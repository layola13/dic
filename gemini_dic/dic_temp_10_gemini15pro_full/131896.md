noncontemptibly:/ˌnɒnkənˈtemptɪbli/ | adv. | 不屑一顾地，不轻蔑地 |  In a manner that does not express or deserve contempt; respectably.  Not in a way that is scornful or disdainful. | He spoke of his former rivals noncontemptibly, acknowledging their contributions. 他谈到他以前的竞争对手时并没有轻蔑之意，反而肯定了他们的贡献。| 近义词: respectfully, appreciatively, honorably | 反义词: contemptuously, disdainfully, scornfully | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Latin origin, meaning "not."
contempt:  From Latin "contemptus," past participle of "contemnere" (to despise, scorn).
-ible (suffix):  Forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
