amboyna:/ˌæmˈbɔɪnə/ | n. adj. |安汶木，安汶木做的|A hard, lustrous wood from the amboyna tree (Pterocarpus indicus), prized for its decorative uses in furniture and veneers; also, anything made of this wood.  It is named after Ambon Island in Indonesia. |  The antique table was inlaid with beautiful amboyna. 这张古董桌子镶嵌着美丽的安汶木。 | 近义词: narra, padauk | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of wood) | 词性: noun, adjective
