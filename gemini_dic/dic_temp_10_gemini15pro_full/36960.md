colourlessness:/ˈkʌlərləsnəs/| n.|无色; 苍白; 平淡无奇|The colourlessness of the design made it seem dull.  这个设计的无色使其显得沉闷。|近义词: paleness, blandness, drabness, neutrality|反义词: vibrancy, colourfulness, brightness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From colour + -less + -ness.  "Colour" comes from Old French "color," ultimately from Latin "color." "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
