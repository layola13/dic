demarkation:/ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 界限，分界线 |  The act of marking or establishing boundaries or limits; a boundary or limit itself. |  The river serves as a natural demarcation between the two countries. 这条河是两国之间的天然分界线。 | synonyms: boundary, limit, border, division, separation | antonyms:  union, connection, juncture | noun

demarkation:/ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 划分，区分 | The act of distinguishing or differentiating something from something else. | There’s a clear demarcation between his professional and personal life. 他的职业生活和个人生活之间有明确的区分。| synonyms: distinction, differentiation, separation | antonyms:  integration, combination | noun
