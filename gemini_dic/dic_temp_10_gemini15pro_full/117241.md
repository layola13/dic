mecca:/ˈmɛkə/| n.|麦加；圣地；向往的地方|A place that attracts people of a particular group or with a particular interest.|例句：For art lovers, Paris is a mecca. 对于艺术爱好者来说，巴黎是圣地。

例句：Silicon Valley became a mecca for tech entrepreneurs. 硅谷成为了科技创业者的圣地。|近义词：center, hub, magnet|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
