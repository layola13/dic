nonphilosophies:/ˌnɒnˌfɪˈlɒsəfiːz/
|n.|非哲学，非哲学思想，非哲学体系|A system of thought or belief that is not considered philosophical.  It often refers to practical or pragmatic approaches to life, rather than abstract or theoretical ones.|His nonphilosophies about life guided his decisions more than any grand theory.|近义词: pragmatism, practicality, realism|反义词: philosophy, idealism, theory|词性: 名词

Etymology:
Prefix "non-" (Latin origin) meaning "not" + "philosophy" (from Greek "philosophia" - love of wisdom)
