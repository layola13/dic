conventionality:/kənˌvɛnʃəˈnælɪti/
n.|常规性，因循守旧，俗套|The conventionality of the plot made the movie predictable.  这部电影的情节俗套，使得它可以预见。|例句：She challenged the conventionality of the wedding ceremony. 她对婚礼仪式的传统提出了挑战。|近义词：conformity, orthodoxy, tradition, custom|反义词：originality, unconventionality, innovation, novelty|词性:noun
Etymology: Late 18th century. From conventional + -ity. Ultimately from Latin *conventiōnālis (unattested) from conventiō 'agreement, coming together' from convenīre 'come together, assemble, agree'  from con- 'together' + venīre 'come'.
