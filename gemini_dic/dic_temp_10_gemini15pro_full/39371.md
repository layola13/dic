conjugial:/kənˈdʒuːdʒiəl/ | adj. | 婚姻的；夫妻的；配偶的 | Of or relating to marriage or the relationship between husband and wife. |  例句：Conjugial love is the cornerstone of a strong marriage.  夫妻之爱是稳固婚姻的基石。

例句：They enjoyed a long and happy conjugial life together. 他们一起享受着漫长而幸福的婚姻生活。 | 近义词：marital, matrimonial, spousal | 反义词：non-marital, extramarital | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *conjugialis*, from conjux (“spouse”), from com- (“together”) + jungō (“join”).
