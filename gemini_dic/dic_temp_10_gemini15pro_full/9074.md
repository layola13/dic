antimilitary:/ˌæntiˈmɪləteri/ | adj. | 反军事的，反对军事主义的 | Opposed to the military or military ideals; pacifistic. |  He has strong antimilitary views and believes in peaceful conflict resolution. 他持有强烈的反军事观点，相信和平解决冲突。| synonyms: pacifist, dovish, peace-loving | antonyms: pro-military, militarist, hawkish | adjective

antimilitary:/ˌæntiˈmɪləteri/ | n. | 反军事主义者 | A person who opposes the military or military ideals.  (Less common usage) |  The antimilitary protested the new defense spending bill.  反军事主义者抗议新的国防开支法案。| synonyms: pacifist, peace activist | antonyms: warmonger, militarist | noun
