bouquets:/boʊˈkeɪz/ | n. |花束；酒香；赞美 |A bunch of flowers, typically given as a gift or for decoration. Also refers to the characteristic aroma of wine or a collection of compliments. |She received a beautiful bouquet of roses for her birthday. 她生日那天收到了一束漂亮的玫瑰花。|Synonyms: bunch, posy, nosegay, arrangement, tribute | Antonyms: single flower, individual bloom | Noun
