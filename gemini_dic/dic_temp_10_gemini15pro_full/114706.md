malleable:/ˈmæliəbl/ | adj. | 可塑的，有延展性的，易受影响的 | Able to be hammered or pressed permanently out of shape without breaking or cracking; easily influenced; pliable. |例句：

Gold is a malleable metal, easily shaped into intricate jewelry.
金是一种可塑性金属，很容易塑造成复杂的珠宝。


The young student's opinions were still malleable and open to influence.
这位年轻学生的观点仍然很容易受影响和改变。

| 近义词：pliable, ductile, adaptable, flexible, susceptible | 反义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding, stubborn, intractable | 词性：形容词
