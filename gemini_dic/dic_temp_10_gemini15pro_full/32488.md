chinesery:/ˌtʃaɪˈniːzəri/
noun
中式装饰；中国风味；中国货
Ornamental objects in a Chinese style, especially those of inferior quality. Also, things regarded as typically Chinese.
例句：The room was filled with cheap chinesery.  房间里摆满了廉价的中国风装饰品。

例句：He dismissed their customs as mere chinesery. 他认为他们的习俗不过是些中国玩意儿。

近义词：chinoiserie, orientalism, exotica
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "Western artifacts/decor")
词性: noun
