namespace:/ˈneɪmˌspeɪs/| n.|命名空间|A designated space or area within which names are unique and have meaning, especially in computing or programming.  A namespace prevents naming conflicts.|The XML namespace helps to avoid element name conflicts.|近义词：scope, domain, area|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "global scope" as a conceptual opposite) |noun
