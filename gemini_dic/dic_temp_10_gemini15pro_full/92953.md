ideological:/ˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌaɪdiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
意识形态的
Based on or relating to a particular ideology; relating to the study of ideas.
例句：The two parties have fundamental ideological differences. 这两个政党有着根本的意识形态差异。

例句：His writings are highly ideological. 他的作品具有高度的意识形态色彩。

近义词：doctrinal, philosophical, theoretical
反义词：pragmatic, empirical, practical
词性：adjective
