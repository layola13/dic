fugally:/ˈfjuːɡəli/ | adv. | 节俭地，简朴地 | In a frugal manner; economically; sparingly. |  She lived fugally, rarely indulging in luxuries. 她生活简朴，很少沉迷于奢侈品。|  He ate a frugal meal of bread and cheese. 他吃了一顿简单的面包和奶酪。| 近义词: economically, thriftily, sparingly, prudently | 反义词: extravagantly, lavishly, wastefully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Latin *frugalis*, from *frug-, frux* "fruit, produce, profit."  The original sense was "fruitful, profitable," later shifting to "making profitable use of things," hence "thrifty, economical."
