hexastichon:/hɛkˈsæstɪkɒn/ | n. | 六行诗 | A poem or stanza of six lines. |  Example: The poet concluded his thoughts with a poignant hexastichon. 诗人以一首 poignant 的六行诗总结了他的想法。 | Example: She chose to express her grief through a hexastichon rather than a longer elegy. 她选择用六行诗而不是更长的挽歌来表达她的悲伤。 | Synonym: sestet, sixain | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps couplet or quatrain for poems with fewer lines) | noun
