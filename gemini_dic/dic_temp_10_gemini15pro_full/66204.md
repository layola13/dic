excitable:/ɪkˈsaɪtəbl/| adj.|易激动的，兴奋的|Easily roused to strong emotion; easily excited.|例句：The excitable puppy barked at every sound.  这只容易兴奋的小狗一听到声音就叫。|例句：She's a very excitable child and gets overstimulated easily. 她是一个很容易兴奋的孩子，很容易受到过度刺激。|近义词：nervous, edgy, restless, fidgety, easily aroused|反义词：calm, composed, serene, tranquil|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English excitable, from Late Latin excitābilis, from Latin excitāre (to excite), frequentative of exciēre (to call forth, rouse), from ex- (out) + ciēre (to move, stir).
