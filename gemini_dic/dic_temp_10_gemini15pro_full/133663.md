nonfortuitously:/ˌnɒnfɔːrˈtjuːɪtəsli/
adverb
非偶然地，非巧合地
Not happening by chance; intentionally or deliberately.
The meeting occurred nonfortuitously, having been planned weeks in advance.  这次会议的召开并非偶然，而是提前几周计划好的。
The evidence suggests the fire was started nonfortuitously.  证据表明，这场火灾并非偶然发生。
Synonym: deliberately, intentionally, purposefully
Antonym: accidentally, coincidentally, fortuitously
词性: 副词
