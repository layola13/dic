co-owns:/koʊˈoʊnz/
|v.|共同拥有|To possess something jointly with another person or entity.|Example: She co-owns the business with her brother. 她和她兄弟共同拥有这家公司。|
Example: They co-own a vacation home in the mountains. 他们在山里共同拥有一栋度假屋。|
近义词:shares, jointly owns|反义词:solely owns|词性:动词
