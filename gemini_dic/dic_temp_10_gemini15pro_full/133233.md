nonexclusive:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈskluːsɪv/
adjective
非独家的，非排斥的
Not limited to one person or group; shared.  Allowing more than one party to participate or benefit.
This is a nonexclusive agreement, meaning other distributors can also sell the product. 这是一份非独家协议，意味着其他经销商也可以销售该产品。
The restaurant offers a nonexclusive dining experience, welcoming guests of all backgrounds. 这家餐厅提供非独家的用餐体验，欢迎来自各个背景的客人。
synonyms: nonspecific, general, open, unrestricted, shared
antonyms: exclusive, restricted, limited, sole, private
词性: 形容词
