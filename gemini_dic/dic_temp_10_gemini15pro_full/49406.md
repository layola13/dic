deliverers:/dɪˈlɪvərərz/
noun
递送员；送货人；交付者
People or companies who bring things to a place.
The deliverers dropped off the packages at the front door. 送货员把包裹放在了前门。
synonyms: couriers, messengers, carriers
antonyms: recipients, receivers
n.

deliverer:/dɪˈlɪvərər/
noun
拯救者；解救者
A person who saves someone from danger or difficulty.
She was hailed as a deliverer of the people. 她被誉为人民的拯救者。
synonyms: savior, rescuer, liberator
antonyms: captor, oppressor
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French delivrer (to free, deliver), from Latin dēliberāre (to free from), from dē- (from) + līberāre (to free), from līber (free).
