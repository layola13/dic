colstaff:/ˈkɒlstɑːf/ | n. | 竿子，旗杆，长柄 | A long pole, staff, or shaft, especially one used as a support for a banner or flag. |  The knight's colstaff bore his family's banner. 骑士的旗杆上挂着他家族的旗帜。| Synonyms: pole, staff, shaft, pike, rod | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps base, foundation) | noun
