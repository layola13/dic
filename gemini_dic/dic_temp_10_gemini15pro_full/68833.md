fascist:/ˈfæʃɪst/ /ˈfæʃɪst/| n. adj.|法西斯主义者；法西斯主义的|A believer in and supporter of fascism; relating to or characteristic of fascism, especially in involving totalitarian aggression or racism. |Example: He was accused of being a fascist. 他被指控为法西斯主义者。|Example: The fascist regime ruthlessly suppressed dissent. 法西斯政权残酷地镇压异议。|近义词：dictatorial, totalitarian, autocratic|反义词：democratic, liberal, egalitarian|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Italian *fascista*, from *fascio* (“bundle, fasces”), from Latin *fascis* (“bundle”).  The fasces were a bundle of rods tied around an axe, an ancient Roman symbol of authority.  Mussolini adopted the term for his political movement in 1919.
