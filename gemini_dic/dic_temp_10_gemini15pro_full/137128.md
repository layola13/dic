nonsubsidies:/ˌnɒnˈsʌbsɪdiz/
|n.|无补贴；非补贴|Absence or lack of subsidies; things or activities that are not subsidized.  The government's focus on nonsubsidies has led to increased competition in the market.|The report highlighted the importance of nonsubsidies in promoting free market principles.  这份报告强调了非补贴在促进自由市场原则方面的重要性。|Near synonyms:  non-support, lack of funding, unsubsidized goods/services|Antonyms: subsidies, grants, financial aid|noun
