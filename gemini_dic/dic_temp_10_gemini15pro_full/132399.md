nondemonstrativeness:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrətɪvnəs/
|n.| 不外露，含蓄；不善表达 |The state or quality of being nondemonstrative; reserved or restrained in expressing emotion. |Her nondemonstrativeness made it difficult to know how she truly felt. 她不轻易表露情感，让人很难知道她内心的真实想法。|Near synonyms: reserve, reticence, restraint, unemotionalism, undemonstrativeness |Antonyms: demonstrativeness, expressiveness, effusiveness|noun
