arbitrable:/ˈɑːrbɪtrəbəl/
adjective
可仲裁的
Capable of being settled by arbitration.
例句：The contract contained an arbitrable clause.  合同中包含一个可仲裁条款。

例句：Whether the dispute is arbitrable will be determined by the court. 法院将决定该争议是否可仲裁。

近义词：resolvable, adjudicable, mediatable
反义词：non-arbitrable, unresolvable

词性:adjective
