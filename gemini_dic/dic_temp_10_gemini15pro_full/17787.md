bedplate:/ˈbedpleɪt/ | n. |床板，底座，底板|A heavy metal base upon which an engine or machine rests. |The bedplate of the engine was cracked.  发动机底座破裂了。|
The large turbine was secured to the bedplate. 这台大型涡轮机被固定在底板上。|synonyms: base, foundation, chassis, support | antonyms: superstructure| noun


Etymology:
From 'bed' (meaning a base or foundation) + 'plate' (a flat piece of metal).  The term originated in the late 18th century, referring to the base of a printing press.
