nonmalignancies:/ˌnɒnˌməˈlɪɡnənsiz/ | n. | 非恶性肿瘤 |Medical term referring to abnormal growths that are not cancerous and do not spread to other parts of the body.  They are generally considered benign. |  The doctor assured her that the lumps were nonmalignancies. 医生向她保证肿块是良性的。|  The biopsy results revealed the presence of several nonmalignancies. 活检结果显示存在多个良性肿瘤。 | synonyms: benign tumors, benign growths | antonyms: malignancies, cancers | noun
