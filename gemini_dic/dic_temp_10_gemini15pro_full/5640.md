altimeters:/ˈæltɪˌmiːtərz/
n.
高度计，测高仪
Instruments that measure altitude, especially above sea level.
The pilot checked the altimeters to ensure they were functioning correctly. 飞行员检查了高度计，以确保它们工作正常。
Synonyms:  height gauges, altitude meters
Antonyms: depth gauges
noun
