belittle:/bɪˈlɪt(ə)l/| vt.|贬低，轻视|To make something or someone seem unimportant. |He belittled her efforts to get a promotion. 他贬低了她为晋升所做的努力。|synonyms: disparage, denigrate, minimize, underestimate, deprecate, diminish, trivialize |antonyms: praise, extol, magnify, aggrandize, commend, applaud, compliment|verb


belittle:/bɪˈlɪt(ə)l/| vt.|小看，瞧不起|To cause (someone or something) to seem less important : to speak slightingly of : DISPARAGE|She belittled his contribution to the project. 她小看了他对这个项目的贡献。|synonyms: disparage, denigrate, minimize, underestimate, deprecate, diminish, trivialize |antonyms: praise, extol, magnify, aggrandize, commend, applaud, compliment|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + little.  The prefix "be-" is used to form verbs, often with a causative or intensive meaning.  "Little" refers to small size or importance.  Therefore, "belittle" literally means "to make little."
