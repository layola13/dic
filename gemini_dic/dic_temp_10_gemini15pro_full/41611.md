corrode:/kəˈroʊd/| v. |腐蚀，侵蚀|To destroy or damage slowly by chemical action.|Acid rain corrodes stone buildings. 酸雨腐蚀石制建筑。|The metal was corroded by the salt water. 金属被盐水腐蚀了。|近义词：erode, wear away, eat away|反义词:protect, preserve|动词

corrasion:/kəˈreɪʒən/| n. |腐蚀，磨损|The process of being corroded.|The corrasion of the metal was caused by exposure to the elements.  金属的腐蚀是由暴露于自然环境造成的。|The bridge suffered severe corrasion from the wind and rain. 桥梁遭受了风雨的严重侵蚀。|近义词：erosion, abrasion, attrition|反义词:construction, building|名词

corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/| adj. |腐蚀性的，侵蚀性的|Having the power to corrode.|Sulfuric acid is a highly corrosive substance. 硫酸是一种高度腐蚀性的物质。|His corrosive comments damaged her reputation. 他尖刻的评论损害了她的名誉。|近义词：caustic, erosive, abrasive|反义词:gentle, mild|形容词
