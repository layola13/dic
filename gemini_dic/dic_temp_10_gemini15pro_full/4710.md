aleuronic:/ˌæljuˈrɒnɪk/| adj.|糊粉的，关于糊粉的|Relating to or containing aleurone, a protein found in the outer layer of the endosperm of grass seeds.|Aleurone grains are packed with protein and are found in the aleuronic layer of cereal grains.|近义词:protein-rich, endosperm-related|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:形容词
