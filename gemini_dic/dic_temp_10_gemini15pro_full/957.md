academicize: /ˌækəˈdɛmɪsaɪz/
verb
使学术化；使学究化
To make something academic; to give something an academic character, often in a way that is seen as overly theoretical or removed from practical concerns.
Some critics argue that the novel has been academicized and lost its popular appeal.  一些评论家认为这部小说已经被学术化了，失去了它的通俗吸引力。

The teaching of creative writing has become increasingly academicized in recent years. 近年来，创意写作的教学越来越学术化了。

近义词: intellectualize, theorize, formalize
反义词: popularize, simplify, dumb down
词性: verb
