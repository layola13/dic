audienceplatz: /ˈɔːdiənsˌplats/ (German pronunciation may vary)| n.|观众席；观众广场|A designated area, often a square or plaza, adjacent to a public building or venue, where audiences gather before or after performances or events.  Often used in the context of German-speaking countries.|The audienceplatz was filled with people waiting for the concert to begin. 观众广场上挤满了等待音乐会开始的人们。|synonyms: plaza, square, forecourt, courtyard|antonyms: backstage, behind the scenes|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Audienz* (audience) + *Platz* (place, square)
