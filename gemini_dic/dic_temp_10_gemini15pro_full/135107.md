nonobsessiveness:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɛsɪvnəs/ | n. | 非执着；不痴迷 | The state of not being obsessed with something or someone.  A lack of obsession. | Her nonobsessiveness with material possessions was refreshing. 她对物质财富的淡泊令人耳目一新。| synonyms: detachment, indifference, nonchalance | antonyms: obsession, fixation, preoccupation | noun

nonobsessive:/ˌnɒnəbˈsɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非执着的；不痴迷的 | Not obsessed with something or someone. | He has a nonobsessive approach to fitness. 他对健身的态度并不痴迷。| synonyms: casual, relaxed, moderate | antonyms: obsessive, compulsive, fixated | adjective
