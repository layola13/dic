exorhizal:/ˌɛksoʊˈraɪzəl/
adjective
外生的
(of mycorrhizae) characterized by the fungal hyphae forming a sheath around the root tip but not penetrating the cells of the root cortex.
Pine trees often form exorhizal associations with fungi. 松树经常与真菌形成外生菌根关联。
ectotrophic, ectomycorrhizal
endomycorrhizal, endotrophic
