bardlike:/ˈbɑːrdlaɪk/| adj.|像吟游诗人的；诗意的|Resembling or characteristic of a bard; poetic. |His bardlike pronouncements captivated the audience. 他那吟游诗人般的发言吸引了观众。|Near synonyms: poetic, lyrical, rhapsodic, eloquent, expressive|Antonyms: prosaic, mundane, unimaginative|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bard + -like.  "Bard" comes from the Old Celtic and Gaelic word for a poet, traditionally one reciting epics and associated with a particular chieftain or noble.  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
