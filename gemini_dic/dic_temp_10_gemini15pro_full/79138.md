gladlier: /ˈɡlædliər/ (This form is rarely used and considered archaic or dialectal) | adj. | 更高兴地，更乐意地 |  A comparative form of "gladly," meaning "in a more glad or willing manner."  It's much less common than "more gladly." | Example:  He *more gladly* accepted the invitation than I did. 他比我更乐意接受邀请。(Using the more common form) | Synonym: more gladly, more willingly, more cheerfully | Antonym: more reluctantly, less willingly, less enthusiastically | word property: adjective
