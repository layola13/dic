lycotropous:/laɪˈkɒtrəpəs/
adjective
倒生的，胚珠倒生的
(of an ovule) curved so that the micropyle is near the hilum and the chalaza is at the opposite end.
The lycotropous ovule is the most common type found in flowering plants. 倒生胚珠是被子植物中最常见的类型。
The tomato plant has lycotropous ovules. 西红柿植株具有倒生胚珠。
synonym:anatropous (partially), inverted
antonym:orthotropous, straight
词性: adjective
