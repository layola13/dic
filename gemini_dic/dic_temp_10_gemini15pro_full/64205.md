equidimensional:/ˌiːkwɪdɪˈmɛnʃənl/
adjective
等维的; 等次的
Having all dimensions equal or uniform.  Relating to or being a system of homogeneous equations or forms each of whose terms is of the same degree.
例句：The crystals are equidimensional, ranging from 0.1 to 0.5 mm in size. 这些晶体是等维的，大小在0.1到0.5毫米之间。

例句：The artist created an equidimensional sculpture, where height, width, and depth were all the same.  艺术家创作了一个等维的雕塑，高、宽、深都相同。

近义词：uniform, isometric, equal-dimensional
反义词：anisometric, unequal, varying

词性: adjective
