hurdle:/ˈhɜːrdl/ (US) /ˈhɜːdl/ (UK)| n. v.|障碍；栏；跨栏；克服；跳过|A hurdle is a difficulty or problem that you have to deal with before you can make progress. As a verb, it means to overcome such difficulties.|
She overcame many hurdles to achieve success. 她克服了许多障碍才取得成功。

He hurdled the fence and escaped. 他跳过栅栏逃跑了。

近义词:obstacle, barrier, difficulty, challenge, impediment|
反义词:advantage, aid, assistance, help|
词性:noun, verb|


hurdle:/ˈhɜːrdl/ (US) /ˈhɜːdl/ (UK)| v.|克服；跳过|To hurdle means to overcome an obstacle or jump over something.|
The company hurdled its financial difficulties. 公司克服了财务困难。

The horse hurdled the fence effortlessly.  这匹马毫不费力地跳过了栅栏。

近义词:overcome, conquer, clear, jump, vault|
反义词:fail, surrender, succumb|
词性:verb|
