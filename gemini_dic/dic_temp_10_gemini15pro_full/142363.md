orthopercussion:/ˌɔːrθoʊpərˈkʌʃən/ | n. |叩诊法; 直角叩诊法 |A method of percussion in which the pleximeter finger is held at a right angle to the plexor finger, used especially for mapping out the superficial cardiac dullness. |The doctor used orthopercussion to assess the patient's lung condition.| 近义词: percussion | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but auscultation is a different examination technique) | noun
