digitized:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzd/| adj.|数字化的，数码化的|Converted from analog to digital form.  Representing data as a series of digits.|例句：The library has digitized its entire collection of rare books. 图书馆已将其所有珍藏书籍数字化。|例句：Digitized images are easier to store and transmit than analog photographs.  数码图像比模拟照片更容易存储和传输。|近义词：digital, electronic, computerized|反义词：analog|词性:形容词

digitize:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪz/| vt.|数字化，将…转换成数字格式|To convert (analog data or information) into digital form.|例句：They are digitizing old recordings to preserve them. 他们正在将旧录音数字化以保存它们。|例句：The company is digitizing its entire archive of documents. 公司正在将其所有文件档案数字化。|近义词：convert, encode, scan|反义词：analogize|词性:动词
