floccose:/ˈflɒkəʊs/ (UK) /ˈflɑːkəʊs/ (US)| adj.|絮状的，绒毛状的|Covered with tufts of soft woolly hairs.  Characterized by a woolly or cottony appearance.|The fungus had a floccose appearance.  这种真菌外观呈绒毛状。|The plant's leaves were covered in floccose hairs.  这种植物的叶子覆盖着绒毛。|近义词: woolly, downy, fluffy, tomentose|反义词: glabrous, smooth|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *floccōsus, from floccus (“flock of wool, tuft of wool”)
