craned:/kreɪnd/
| v.|伸长脖子看，(使)伸出|From crane + -ed, past participle suffix.|She craned her neck to see over the fence. 她伸长脖子越过栅栏看。|近义词：stretched, peered, strained|反义词：ducked, lowered|词性：动词

craned:/kreɪnd/
| adj.|伸长的，伸出的 (较少用)|From crane + -ed, past participle suffix.|He had a craned neck, trying to see the stage. 他伸着脖子想看舞台。|近义词：extended, outstretched|反义词：retracted, withdrawn|词性：形容词
