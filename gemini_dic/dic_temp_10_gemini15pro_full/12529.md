asphyxia: /əsˈfɪksiə/ /æsˈfɪksiə/
n.
窒息，缺氧
a condition arising when the body is deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocation.
The victim died of asphyxia. 受害者死于窒息。

近义词: suffocation, choking, smothering
反义词: respiration, breathing

词性: noun


词源: From French asphyxie, from Late Latin asphyxia, from Greek asphyxia "stopping of the pulse," from a- "without" + sphyxis "pulse."
