furbelow:/ˈfɜːrbəloʊ/ (US), /ˈfɜːbɪləʊ/ (UK)| n.|饰边，褶边，皱褶；虚饰，浮华|A furbelow is a piece of pleated or gathered fabric used as a trimming or decoration on clothing, especially women's clothing. It can also refer to showy or superfluous ornamentation or to something frivolous or ostentatious.|The dress was adorned with delicate furbelows at the hem and sleeves. 这件衣服的袖口和下摆饰有精致的褶边。|Synonyms: frill, ruffle, flounce, ornament, embellishment|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun
