disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/| n. v.|厌恶，反感；使作呕|A feeling of revulsion or strong disapproval aroused by something unpleasant or offensive. |The smell of rotten eggs filled me with disgust. 腐烂鸡蛋的气味让我感到恶心。|近义词：revulsion, aversion, loathing, nausea|反义词：admiration, pleasure, liking|noun
disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/| v.|使厌恶，使反感|Cause (someone) to feel revulsion or strong disapproval.|His behavior disgusted everyone present. 他的行为让在场的所有人都感到厌恶。|近义词：revolt, sicken, nauseate, repel|反义词：please, delight, attract|verb
