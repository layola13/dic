indulgent:/ɪnˈdʌldʒənt/| adj.|放纵的；纵容的；溺爱的|Having or indicating a tendency to be overly generous to or lenient with someone.|She gave her children indulgent smiles. 她对孩子们露出了溺爱的笑容。|Synonyms: lenient, permissive, tolerant, forgiving, accommodating|Antonyms: strict, austere, severe, harsh, disciplined|adjective

indulgent:/ɪnˈdʌldʒənt/| adj.|沉溺于享乐的；喜好…的|Characterized by or given to indulgence; enjoying something without restraint.|He took an indulgent sip of fine wine. 他呷了一口上好的葡萄酒，尽情享受。|Synonyms: luxurious, decadent, self-indulgent, pampering, lavish|Antonyms: austere, ascetic, abstinent, self-denying, spartan|adjective
