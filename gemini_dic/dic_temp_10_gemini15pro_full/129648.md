non-culture:/ˈnɒn kʌltʃə(r)/ (US) /ˈnɑːn kʌltʃər/| n.|非文化;无培养;不培养|A situation where cells or microorganisms are not deliberately grown in a laboratory setting.  It can also refer to a lack of appreciation or understanding of culture.|The bacteria thrived in a non-culture environment.|近义词: sterility, barrenness |反义词: culture, cultivation|noun
