battle-fatigued: /ˈbætəl fəˈtiːɡd/ | adj. | 战争疲劳的，身心俱疲的 | Exhausted and demoralized from prolonged exposure to combat or other highly stressful situations.  It can manifest as emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion. | The battle-fatigued soldiers longed for peace.  战争疲劳的士兵们渴望和平。|  The battle-fatigued doctor finally took a break. 身心俱疲的医生终于休息了一下。 |  exhausted, weary, shell-shocked, traumatized | refreshed, invigorated | adjective
