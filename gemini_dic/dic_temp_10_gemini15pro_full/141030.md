onion:/ˈʌnjən/| n.|洋葱|A bulbous plant with a pungent taste and smell, composed of fleshy, modified leaves, used as a vegetable.|I added some chopped onion to the stew. 我在炖菜里加了一些切碎的洋葱。|近义词：scallion, shallot|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源: From Old French * oignon * , from Latin * ūniō *  meaning "large pearl," also "a kind of onion," from * ūnus * meaning "one."  The semantic shift occurred because of the onion's layered, bulbous structure.
