cheapen:/ˈtʃiːpən/| v. |使便宜；降低…的价值；贬低|To make something cheaper or to make something seem less valuable or respectable. |Mass production has cheapened the cost of cars. 大规模生产降低了汽车的成本。|Synonyms: devalue, depreciate, downgrade, lower|Antonyms: enhance, increase, raise, upgrade|verb
