florideae: /flɔːˈɪdiːiː/
| n. |红藻纲|A class of red algae, characterized by having no flagella at any stage in their life cycle and possessing complex pit connections between adjacent cells.|Red algae of the class Florideae are abundant in warm marine waters. 红藻纲的红藻在温暖的海水中很丰富。|近义词: Rhodophyceae|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification) |词性: noun
