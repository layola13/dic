contributer:/kənˈtrɪbjətər/ | n. |贡献者, 投稿者| someone who gives something, especially money, in order to provide or achieve something together with other people; someone who writes articles for a newspaper, magazine, or book |例句：She is a regular contributer to the magazine. 她是一位定期为该杂志投稿的人。

例句：He was a major contributer to the charity. 他是该慈善机构的主要捐助者。

近义词：donor, subscriber, contributor (Note: "contributor" is the preferred spelling in most style guides) | 反义词：detractor, opponent | 词性：noun
