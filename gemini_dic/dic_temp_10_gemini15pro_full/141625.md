opticity:/ɒpˈtɪsɪti/| n.|光学性，光学特性|The opticity of the material affects its transparency.  这种材料的光学性影响其透明度。|例句：The opticity of the lens determines how light refracts.  镜片的光学特性决定了光线的折射方式。

例句：Researchers are exploring new materials with unique opticity.  研究人员正在探索具有独特光学特性的新材料。|近义词：optical properties, optical behavior|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *opticité*, from *optique* ("optical") + *-ité* (suffix forming abstract nouns). *Optique* is from Latin *opticus*, from Ancient Greek *ὀπτικός* (*optikós*), from *ὄψις* (*ópsis*) meaning "sight, vision."
