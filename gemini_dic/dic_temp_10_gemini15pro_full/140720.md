omnilateral:/ˌɒmnɪˈlætərəl/
adjective
全面的，多方面的
involving all sides or parties; all-embracing
例句：The government took an omnilateral approach to the economic crisis, addressing issues of trade, employment, and investment. 政府对经济危机采取了全面的方法，解决了贸易、就业和投资问题。

例句：An omnilateral peace agreement, involving all warring factions, was finally reached.  一项涉及所有交战派别的全面和平协议最终达成。

近义词：comprehensive, all-encompassing, multilateral, universal
反义词：unilateral, limited, restricted
词性: adjective
词源: omni- (all) + lateral (sided)
