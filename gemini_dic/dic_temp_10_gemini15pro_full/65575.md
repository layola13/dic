evacuating: /ɪˈvækjueɪtɪŋ/
verb
撤离，疏散
the process of moving people from a dangerous place to somewhere safe
例句：
They are evacuating the building because of a fire.
他们正在因为火灾疏散大楼。

The city is evacuating residents due to the approaching hurricane.
由于飓风临近，该市正在疏散居民。

近义词: leaving, withdrawing, removing, clearing
反义词: entering, occupying, populating
词性: verb (present participle)

evacuate: /ɪˈvækjueɪt/
verb
撤离，疏散
to move people from a dangerous place to somewhere safe
例句：
The police evacuated the area after the bomb threat.
警方在炸弹威胁后疏散了该地区。

We had to evacuate the building due to a gas leak.
由于煤气泄漏，我们不得不疏散大楼。

近义词: clear, vacate, abandon, desert
反义词: occupy, inhabit, populate
词性: verb

Etymology: From Latin *evacuatus*, past participle of *evacuare* "to empty out," from *ex-* "out" + *vacuare* "to empty," from *vacuus* "empty."
