air-dropped:/ˈeərˌdrɑːpt/ /ˈeərˌdrɒpt/| v. |空投；空降；通过空投发送|To deliver or send (something) by parachute from an aircraft.  Also, to send (files) wirelessly, especially between mobile devices.|The humanitarian aid was air-dropped to the refugees. 人道主义援助被空投给了难民。|

The files were air-dropped to my phone. 文件被空投到我的手机上。|近义词：parachuted, delivered, transmitted, sent wirelessly|反义词:picked up, retrieved, downloaded (for files)|词性:动词

air-dropped:/ˈeərˌdrɑːpt/ /ˈeərˌdrɒpt/| adj. |空投的；空降的|Delivered or sent by airdrop.|The air-dropped supplies provided much-needed relief. 空投的物资提供了急需的救济。|近义词:parachuted, airlifted|反义词:ground-delivered|词性:形容词
