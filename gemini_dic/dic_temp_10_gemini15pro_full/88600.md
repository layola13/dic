hitting-out:/ˈhɪtɪŋ aʊt/ | n. |猛击，猛烈抨击| A forceful attack, especially in words or argument. | The politician launched a hitting-out against his rivals.  这位政治家对他的对手发起了猛烈抨击。|
hitting-out:/ˈhɪtɪŋ aʊt/ | n. |击球练习，击球得分|Practice session in cricket or baseball where batsmen aim to score as many runs as possible. | The team had a hitting-out at the nets before the match.  比赛前，球队在球网进行了击球练习。|
hitting-out:/ˈhɪtɪŋ aʊt/ | n. |猛烈反击|A forceful counterattack, either physical or verbal. | He responded to the accusations with a hitting-out of his own. 他以自己的猛烈反击回应了这些指控。|
