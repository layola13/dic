doggerman:/ˈdɑːɡərmən/ | n. | 码头工人，搬运工 | A dock worker who loads and unloads cargo. |  He got a job as a doggerman at the port. 他在港口找到了一份码头工人的工作。 |  Longshoreman, stevedore, docker |  Clerk, office worker | noun

Etymology:
Likely from "dog" (possibly referring to the heavy lifting or menial nature of the work) + "-er" (suffix indicating a person who performs an action) + "man".  The exact origin is uncertain and not well-documented.
