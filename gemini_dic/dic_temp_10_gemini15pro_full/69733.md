feracity:/fəˈræsɪti/
n.
凶猛；野性；肥沃；多产
The feracity of the lion is well known.  狮子的凶猛是众所周知的。
The lawyer argued with unusual feracity.  这位律师辩护得异常激烈。
例句：The feracity of the storm surprised everyone.  这场风暴的猛烈程度让所有人感到惊讶。
The defense attorney displayed impressive feracity in cross-examining the witness. 辩护律师在盘问证人时表现出惊人的气势。

近义词：fierceness, savagery, intensity, vehemence, aggressiveness
反义词：gentleness, mildness, timidity, meekness
词性: 名词
