morro:/ˈmɔːroʊ/ (US), /ˈmɒrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|小山，圆丘; (尤指)突出于海面的岩石或岬角|A rounded hill or promontory, especially one projecting into the sea.|We hiked to the top of the morro for a breathtaking view of the coastline. 我们徒步登上小山顶，欣赏壮丽的海岸线景色。|The lighthouse stood atop a steep morro. 灯塔矗立在一个陡峭的海岬上。|近义词：hill, knoll, promontory, bluff|反义词:valley, depression|Noun
