drought:/draʊt/| n.|干旱，旱灾|A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.|The severe drought has caused widespread crop failures. 严重的干旱造成了 widespread 的农作物歉收。|近义词：aridness, dryness|反义词: flood, deluge|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *druchte, from Old Dutch *druhti, from Proto-Germanic *druhti (“dryness, drought”), from Proto-Indo-European *dhers- (“to be turbid, muddy; to dry up”). Cognate with Middle Low German dröchte (“drought”), Dutch droogte (“drought”), German Dürre (“dry spell”), Old English drugoþ (“dryness”).
