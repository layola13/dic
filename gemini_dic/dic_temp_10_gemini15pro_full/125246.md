multidimensionalities:/ˌmʌltiˌdaɪˌmɛnʃəˈnælɪtiz/ | n. |多维度，多维性|The state or quality of having multiple dimensions or aspects.  It refers to something that exists in or involves several dimensions or perspectives.|例句：The multidimensionalities of the problem require a multifaceted solution.  这个问题的多维性需要一个多方面的解决方案。

例句：Understanding the multidimensionalities of human experience is crucial for social scientists. 理解人类经验的多维性对于社会科学家至关重要。| 近义词：complexity, multifacetedness, intricacy | 反义词：simplicity, unidimensionality | 词性: noun
