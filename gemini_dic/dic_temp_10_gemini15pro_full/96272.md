indiction:/ɪnˈdɪkʃən/| n.|指示;指定;宣布;征税|(1) An official proclamation or order. (2) A period of fifteen years used for dating documents, especially in the Middle Ages. (3) A tax or levy.|The emperor issued an indiction calling for a new tax. 皇帝发布公告，要求征收新税。

The indiction cycle was used for centuries to date official documents. 指示周期被使用了几个世纪来确定官方文件的日期。

The indiction was a heavy burden on the peasants.  这项税收对农民来说是一个沉重的负担。|近义词：proclamation, decree, announcement, levy, tax|反义词: revocation, repeal|词性:noun
