octagonal:/ɑːkˈtæɡənəl/| adj.|八角形的，八边的|Having eight angles and eight sides.  |例句：The octagonal table was a unique piece in the dining room.  八角形的桌子是餐厅里一件独特的家具。

例句：They built an octagonal gazebo in the garden.  他们在花园里建了一个八角形的凉亭。|近义词：eight-sided|反义词:circular, square|词性:adjective
词源: From Late Latin *octagonus*, from Greek *oktōgonos*  ("eight-angled"),  from *oktō* ("eight") + *gōnia* ("angle").
