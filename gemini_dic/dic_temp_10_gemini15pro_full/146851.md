overindulge: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ/ /ˌəʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 纵容；过度沉溺；过分放纵 | To allow oneself or another person to have too much of something enjoyable, especially food or drink. | I overindulged at the buffet and now I feel sick. 我在自助餐吃太多了，现在感觉不舒服了。|  He overindulged his children, giving them everything they wanted. 他溺爱他的孩子们，给了他们想要的一切。| 近义词: pamper, spoil, coddle | 反义词: abstain, moderate | 词性: 动词
