monopterous:/məˈnɒptərəs/| adj.|单列柱式的，单翼的|Having a single row of columns supporting a roof, typically circular, and without walls; having only one wing.|例句：The monopterous temple stood gracefully in the garden.  单列柱式的庙宇优雅地伫立在花园里。

例句：Some insects are monopterous, possessing only one pair of wings. 一些昆虫是单翼的，只有一对翅膀。|近义词：single-winged, columnar|反义词：dipterous (双翼的), peripteral (四周环列柱廊的)|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mónopteros* (having a single wing or row of columns), from *mónos* (single) + *pterón* (wing, feather).
