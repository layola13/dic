numericalness:/njuːˈmɛrɪkəlnɪs/| n.|数量性，数值性|The numericalness of the data allows for statistical analysis.  这些数据的数量性允许进行统计分析。|例句：The numericalness of the stars in the sky is overwhelming. 夜空中星星的数量之多令人震撼。|近义词：quantity, numerousness|反义词: scarcity, paucity|词性:noun

Etymology:  From "numerical" + "-ness". "Numerical" comes from Late Latin "numeralis",  from Latin "numerus" (number).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
