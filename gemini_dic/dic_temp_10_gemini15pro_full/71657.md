flatteringly: /ˈflætərɪŋli/ | adv. | 奉承地，讨好地，使人显得更漂亮地 | in a way that praises someone, often insincerely and with the intention of gaining an advantage; in a way that makes someone look or seem more attractive | The dress flattered her figure flatteringly. 这条裙子把她衬托得格外漂亮。| Synonyms: complimentarily, fawningly, ingratiatingly | Antonyms: unflatteringly, critically, disparagingly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the adjective "flattering," which comes from the verb "flatter," ultimately from Old French "flater" (to stroke, caress, flatter), from Germanic origin.
