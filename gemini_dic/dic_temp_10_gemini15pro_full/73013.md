footboard:/ˈfʊtbɔːrd/ | n. | 脚踏板，床尾板 |A board forming the foot of a bed or a low platform for the feet in a vehicle.|  The antique bed had a beautifully carved footboard. 这张古董床有一个雕刻精美的床尾板。

The driver rested his feet on the footboard. 司机把脚放在踏板上。| synonym: footrest, pedal (in some contexts) | antonym: headboard | noun
