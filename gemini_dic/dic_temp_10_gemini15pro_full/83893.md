half-armed:/ˌhɑːfˈɑːrmd/
adjective
半武装的；装备不足的
lacking some usual or necessary equipment or skills; inadequately prepared or equipped.
The soldiers were sent into battle half-armed and poorly trained. 这些士兵被派上前线，装备不足，训练也很差。
Near synonyms: ill-equipped, under-equipped, underequipped, underprepared, unprepared
Antonyms: well-equipped, well-prepared
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" + "armed"
half: From Middle English *half, from Old English healf, from Proto-Germanic *halbaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“whole, healthy”).
armed: From Middle English armed, from Old French armé, past participle of armer (“to arm”), from Late Latin armāre (“to arm”), from Latin arma (“arms, weapons”).
