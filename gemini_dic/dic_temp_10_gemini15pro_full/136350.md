nonresistibilities:/ˌnɒnrɪˌzɪstɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可抗拒性；无法抗拒的事物 | The quality or state of being nonresistible; something that cannot be resisted. |  The nonresistibilities of her charm captivated everyone in the room. 她的魅力无法抗拒，迷住了房间里的每个人。 | Synonyms: allure, appeal, attraction, magnetism, temptation | Antonyms: repulsion, aversion, distaste | Noun

Etymology:
From nonresistible + -ities.  Nonresistible is formed from non- + resistible. Resistible comes from the Latin resistere "to resist," from re- "back" + sistere "to stand."
