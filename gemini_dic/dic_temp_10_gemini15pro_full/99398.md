interdigital:/ˌɪntəˈdɪdʒɪtəl/
adjective
指间的，趾间的
Situated between the fingers or toes.
The frog has interdigital webbing. 青蛙有趾间蹼。
interdigital
adjective
近义词：interdigital, digital
反义词：None

Etymology:
From Latin *interdigitalis*, from inter- ‘between’ + digitalis ‘of a finger or toe’.  The Latin word digitalis is derived from digitus ‘finger, toe’.
