Medicaid: /ˈmedɪkeɪd/ (US) /ˈmedɪkeɪd/ (UK)| n. |医疗补助; 贫困医疗援助| A US government program financed by federal, state, and local funds, that provides health care for low-income people.|  Medicaid helps pay for the medical costs of many low-income families. 医疗补助帮助许多低收入家庭支付医疗费用。|Synonyms: welfare, public assistance|Antonyms: private insurance|Noun
