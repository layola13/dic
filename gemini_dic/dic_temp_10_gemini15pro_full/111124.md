literary-editorship: /ˈlɪtərəri ˈedɪtərʃɪp/
n.
文学编辑职位；文学编辑工作
The position and duties of a literary editor.  The role involves overseeing the editorial content of a publication, particularly concerning literary works.
She held the literary-editorship of the prestigious journal for over a decade. 她担任这家著名期刊的文学编辑已有十多年。
The literary-editorship required a deep understanding of both contemporary and classic literature.  这份文学编辑工作需要对当代和古典文学有深刻的理解。

近义词: editorship, literary editorship
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a position)
