otosteal:/ˌoʊtəˈstiːl/| adj.|耳蜗镫骨的|Relating to the otosteal bone, a small bone in the middle ear of amphibians, reptiles, and birds, homologous to the stapes in mammals.|The otosteal bone plays a crucial role in transmitting sound vibrations.  耳蜗镫骨在传递声音振动中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonym: stapedial (in mammals)|Antonym: N/A|adjective
