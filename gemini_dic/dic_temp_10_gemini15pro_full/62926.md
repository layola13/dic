enmesh:/ɪnˈmɛʃ/| v. |使纠缠，使陷入困境|To involve someone or something in a complicated or unpleasant situation that is difficult to escape from.|例句：She became enmeshed in a web of lies. 她陷入了一张谎言之网。|例句：The company was enmeshed in legal battles. 这家公司陷入了法律纠纷。|近义词：entangle, involve, trap|反义词：extricate, free, liberate|词性：动词

enmeshed:/ɪnˈmɛʃt/| adj. |被缠住的，陷入困境的|Involved in a complicated or unpleasant situation that is difficult to escape from.|例句：He was enmeshed in a difficult family situation. 他陷入了困境的家庭状况。|例句：The country is enmeshed in a political crisis.  这个国家陷入了政治危机。|近义词：entangled, involved, trapped|反义词：extricated, free, liberated|词性：形容词
