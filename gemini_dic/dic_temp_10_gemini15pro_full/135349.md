nonperceiving:/ˌnɑːnpərˈsiːvɪŋ/
adjective
非感知的；无知觉的
Not perceiving; lacking perception or awareness.
The nonperceiving patient was unresponsive to stimuli.  非感知的病人对刺激没有反应。
He seemed nonperceiving of the danger around him. 他似乎没有察觉到周围的危险。

近义词:unaware, insensible, uncognizant, insensitive
反义词:perceiving, aware, conscious, sensitive
词性:adjective
