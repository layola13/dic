laxes:/ˈlæksiːz/| v. (third-person singular simple present laxes, present participle laxing, simple past and past participle laxed)| 放松，松懈，缓和；通便，导泻 |The rules have laxed in recent years. 近年来规则有所松懈。|Synonyms: loosen, relax, slacken, ease|Antonyms: tighten, strengthen, intensify|verb

laxed:/lækst/| adj.| 松弛的，不严格的；松懈的；腹泻的|Security has become increasingly laxed.  安全措施越来越松懈。|Synonyms: loose, relaxed, slack, lenient|Antonyms: tight, strict, rigorous|adjective

laxness:/ˈlæksnəs/| n.| 松弛；懈怠；不严格|The laxness of the rules led to chaos. 规则的松懈导致了混乱。|Synonyms: looseness, slackness, leniency|Antonyms: tightness, strictness, rigor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *laxus* meaning "loose, wide, spacious".
