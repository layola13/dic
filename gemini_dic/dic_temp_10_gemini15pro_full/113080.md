lunar-days:/ˈluːnər deɪz/ | n. |阴历日，农历日| Lunar days are based on the cycles of the moon, unlike solar days which are based on the sun. |  Many traditional festivals are celebrated according to the lunar days.  许多传统节日都是根据阴历日来庆祝的。| synonyms: lunar dates | antonyms: solar days | noun

lunar-days:/ˈluːnər deɪz/ | n. |月球日，月球上的日| A lunar day is the time it takes for the Moon to rotate once relative to the Sun.  | One lunar day is approximately 29.5 Earth days. 一个月球日大约相当于29.5个地球日。 | synonyms: lunar periods | antonyms: Earth days| noun
