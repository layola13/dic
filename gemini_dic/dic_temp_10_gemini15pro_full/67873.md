fabrication:/ˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
制造; 制造过程; 捏造; 伪造物
The process of making something from raw materials or components.  A lie or a story that is made up.
The bridge is a marvelous fabrication of steel and concrete. 这座桥是钢铁和混凝土的奇妙组合。
His story about being a millionaire was a complete fabrication. 他关于自己是百万富翁的故事完全是捏造的。
construction, manufacture, production, invention, falsification, forgery
truth, reality
noun
