commandlet:/kəˈmændlɪt/| n. |小型命令，cmdlet，(PowerShell中的)轻量级命令 |A commandlet is a lightweight command that is used in the Windows PowerShell environment. |Example: Get-ChildItem is a useful commandlet for listing files and directories.  获取子项目是一个用于列出文件和目录的有用命令。|Example:  The commandlet `Set-Location` changes the current working directory. 命令`Set-Location`更改当前工作目录。|Synonym: command, script, applet |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "function" or "program" as conceptually broader terms.) |noun
