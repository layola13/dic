bombproofs:/ˈbɒmpruːfs/ (UK) /ˈbɑːmpruːfs/ (US)| v.|防弹; 防爆; 使...不受炸弹破坏|Makes something resistant to bombs or explosions.|The engineers bombproofed the building's foundation. 工程师们对建筑物的地基进行了防爆处理。|The shelter was bombproofed against air raids.  这个掩体可以抵御空袭。|近义词: fortify, reinforce, protect|反义词: weaken, expose|词性: 动词

bombproof:/ˈbɒmpruːf/ (UK) /ˈbɑːmpruːf/ (US)| adj.|防弹的; 防爆的; 不受炸弹破坏的|Resistant to bombs or explosions; unshakeable, imperturbable.|The bunker had a bombproof door. 地堡有一扇防弹门。|His confidence was bombproof. 他的信心坚不可摧。|近义词: impenetrable, invulnerable, unshakeable|反义词: vulnerable, fragile|词性: 形容词

词源: bomb (炸弹) + proof (证明; 防...的)  => bombproof (防弹的) => bombproofs (使...防弹)
