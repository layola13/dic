chikara:/tʃiːˈkɑːrɑː/
n.
power, strength, force, energy
Japanese word meaning "power," "strength," "force," or "energy."  It is often used in the context of martial arts and spiritual practices.
The martial artist demonstrated impressive chikara in his strikes.  这位武术家在他的攻击中展现了惊人的力量。
He channeled his inner chikara to overcome the obstacle. 他引导内在的力量来克服障碍。
synonyms: power, strength, might, force, energy, vigor
antonyms: weakness, impotence, feebleness
