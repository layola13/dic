overrichly:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪtʃli/| adv.|过于丰富地，过度华丽地| excessively richly; in a way that is too rich or ornate. |The dessert was overrichly decorated with chocolate and cream. 这道甜点装饰得过于华丽，巧克力和奶油太多了。| 近义词：excessively, elaborately, lavishly, ornately | 反义词：simply, plainly, modestly, sparsely |词性:副词

overrichly:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪtʃli/| adv.|过于肥沃地| excessively fertile; in a way that has too many nutrients. |The soil was overrichly fertilized, leading to stunted growth. 土壤施肥过多，导致生长发育不良。| 近义词：overfertilized, excessively rich | 反义词：infertile, barren, poor |词性:副词
