oxgoad:/ˈɑːksɡoʊd/ | n. | 牛虻; 刺激物 | Something that urges or stimulates. | The deadline acted as an oxgoad, pushing him to finish the project. 截止日期就像一根刺，促使他完成项目。 | Synonyms: incentive, spur, stimulus | Antonyms: deterrent, discouragement | noun
