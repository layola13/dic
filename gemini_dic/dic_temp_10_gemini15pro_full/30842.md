It is related to the guans and curassows. |  We heard the chachalacas calling in the early morning mist. 我们在清晨的薄雾中听到了恰恰拉卡鸟的叫声。 | Example: The chachalaca's loud, raucous calls echoed through the forest. 恰恰拉卡鸟响亮刺耳的叫声在森林中回荡。 |Synonyms: guan, curassow | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific bird species) | noun
