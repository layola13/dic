bilayer:/ˈbaɪˌleɪər/| n. |双分子层|A thin membrane made of two layers of lipid molecules.|The cell membrane is a phospholipid bilayer.|Synonym: double layer|Antonym: monolayer|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *bi-* (prefix meaning 'two') + *layer*.  "Bi-" comes from Latin, while "layer" comes from Old English *læger* (a lying place).
