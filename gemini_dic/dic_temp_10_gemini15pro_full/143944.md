ETYMOLOGY->
From "output" + "-ing". "Output" originates from the combination of "out" and "put".  "Out" indicates direction away from a source, while "put" refers to placing or setting something.  The suffix "-ing" transforms the noun "output" into a present participle, indicating an ongoing action.
