fossoria:/fɒˈsɔːriə/
noun
掘土动物；适于挖掘的器官
animals adapted for digging; organs adapted for digging
Fossorial adaptations, such as strong claws and forelimbs, are common in moles. 鼹鼠通常具有掘土适应性，例如强壮的爪子和前肢。
The fossoria of the mole are remarkably efficient. 鼹鼠的挖掘器官非常高效。
近义词: digging, burrowing
反义词: surface, aboveground
词性: noun
