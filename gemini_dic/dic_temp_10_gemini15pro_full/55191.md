dissenting:/dɪˈsɛntɪŋ/
adjective
持不同意见的；反对的
Holding or expressing opinions that are different from those that are officially accepted.
The judge wrote a dissenting opinion. 法官写了一份反对意见。
近义词: disagreeing, opposing, objecting
反义词: agreeing, concurring, assenting
词性: adjective

dissenting:/dɪˈsɛntɪŋ/
noun
持异议者；反对者
A person who holds or expresses dissenting opinions.
There were two dissenters on the committee. 委员会中有两名持异议者。
近义词: objector, dissident, nonconformist
反义词: conformist, supporter
词性: noun
