flawless:/ˈflɔːləs/ | adj. |完美的，无瑕的 | Without any imperfections or defects; perfect. | Her performance was flawless, not a single note out of place. 她的表演完美无瑕，没有一个音符出错。

Her skin was flawless, smooth and unblemished. 她的皮肤完美无瑕，光滑无斑点。

| 近义词: perfect, impeccable, immaculate, faultless | 反义词: flawed, imperfect, defective, faulty | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From flaw + -less.  "Flaw" comes from Old Norse *flaga "slab of stone, layer of rock," related to Old Norse flagna "to flake off."  The suffix "-less" means "without."
