freethinkers:/ˈfriːˌθɪŋkərz/| n. |自由思想家（复数）|People who form their own opinions and beliefs, especially on religious matters, independently of tradition or authority.  They are not afraid to question conventional wisdom.|Freethinkers often challenge societal norms. 自由思想家经常挑战社会规范。|Many scientists of the Enlightenment were freethinkers. 许多启蒙运动时期的科学家都是自由思想家。|synonyms: nonconformists, skeptics, rationalists, iconoclasts|antonyms: conformists, traditionalists, dogmatists|noun
