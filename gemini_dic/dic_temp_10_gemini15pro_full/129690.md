non-disjunction:/ˌnɒn.dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ | n. | 非分离 | The failure of homologous chromosomes or sister chromatids to separate properly during cell division (meiosis or mitosis). |  Non-disjunction can lead to genetic disorders like Down syndrome. 非分离会导致像唐氏综合症这样的遗传疾病。| Nondisjunction during meiosis can result in gametes with an abnormal number of chromosomes.  减数分裂过程中的非分离会导致配子染色体数目异常。 |  aneuploidy, malsegregation |  disjunction | noun
