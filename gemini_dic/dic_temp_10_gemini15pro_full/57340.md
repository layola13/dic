downweigh:/ˌdaʊnˈweɪ/| vt.| 使…显得不重要；降低…的权重；使…处于劣势；|To cause something to have less influence or importance than something else.|The negative publicity downweighed the positive aspects of the project.  负面宣传降低了该项目积极方面的重要性。|近义词：devalue, depreciate, minimize, underemphasize, detract from|反义词:  overemphasize, exaggerate, inflate, upgrade, enhance|verb

Etymology->
"down-" + "weigh".  "Down-" acts as a prefix indicating reduction or lowering, while "weigh" refers to assigning importance or significance.  The combined meaning is to reduce the importance or weight of something.
