falcon:/ˈfɔːlkən/ (US) /ˈfɔːlkən/ (UK)| n.|猎鹰；隼|A bird of prey with long pointed wings and a notched beak, known for its swift flight and keen eyesight.  It is often trained for hunting.|The falcon soared high above the mountains. 猎鹰在群山上空翱翔。|近义词：hawk, raptor|反义词:prey|noun
