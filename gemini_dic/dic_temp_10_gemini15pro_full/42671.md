counterstroke:/ˈkaʊntərˌstroʊk/| n.|反击，回击|A counterstroke is a stroke made in return for another, especially in fencing or boxing; a retaliatory action or measure.|The boxer delivered a swift counterstroke, knocking his opponent off balance.|近义词: counterattack, retaliation, riposte|反义词: attack, offense|noun
