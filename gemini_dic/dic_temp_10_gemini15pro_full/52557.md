diffractogram:/dɪˈfræktəɡræm/ | n. | 衍射图；衍射花样 | A graphical representation of the intensity of scattered radiation (X-rays, neutrons, or electrons) as a function of scattering angle, used to determine the structure of a material.  | The diffractogram showed sharp peaks, indicating a crystalline structure. 衍射图显示出尖锐的峰，表明其具有晶体结构。|Synonyms: diffraction pattern, diffraction record | Antonyms: N/A | noun
