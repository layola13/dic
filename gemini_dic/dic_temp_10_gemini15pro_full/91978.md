hypernationalistic:/ˌhaɪpərˈnæʃənəlɪstɪk/
adjective
极端民族主义的
Excessively or fanatically nationalistic.  Characterized by extreme nationalism.
The government's hypernationalistic rhetoric fueled tensions with neighboring countries. 政府极端民族主义的言论加剧了与邻国的紧张关系。
His hypernationalistic views alienated many of his former friends. 他的极端民族主义观点疏远了他的许多老朋友。

近义词: ultranationalistic, jingoistic, chauvinistic
反义词: internationalist, cosmopolitan, globalist
词性: adjective


词源:
From hyper- + nationalistic.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond" derived from Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ). "Nationalistic" comes from "nation" with the suffix "-istic."
