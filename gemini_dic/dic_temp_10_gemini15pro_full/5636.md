although:/ɔːlˈðoʊ/ (US) /ɔːlˈðəʊ/ (UK)| conj.| 尽管，虽然 | Introduces a subordinate clause that makes a concession.  It expresses a contrast but doesn't prevent the main clause from being true. | Although it was raining, we went for a walk. 虽然下雨了，我们还是去散步了。| Near Synonyms: though, even though, while, albeit | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for conjunctions, but contrasting ideas could be expressed with "but," "however," "yet," etc.) | Conjunction
