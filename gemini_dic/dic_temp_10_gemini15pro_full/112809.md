lucernal:/luːˈsɜːrnəl/| adj.| 灯笼的；照明的；发光的 | Relating to a lucerne, a lamp or lantern, or providing light. | The lucernal glow of the fireflies lit up the night. 萤火虫的灯光照亮了夜晚。 | 近义词: luminous, bright, shining | 反义词: dark, dim, unlit | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *lucernālis*, from lucerna (“lamp, lantern”).
