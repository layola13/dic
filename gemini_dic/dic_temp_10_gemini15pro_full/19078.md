betrayed:/bɪˈtreɪd/| v. |背叛，出卖；辜负；（非故意地）暴露，显露|To be disloyal to someone or something that trusts you. To reveal something unintentionally.|He betrayed his country by selling secrets to the enemy. 他向敌人出卖机密，背叛了他的国家。

Her trembling hands betrayed her nervousness. 她颤抖的双手暴露了她的紧张。|近义词：deceived, double-crossed, misled, ratted out, stabbed in the back|反义词：supported, helped, defended|词性: 动词
