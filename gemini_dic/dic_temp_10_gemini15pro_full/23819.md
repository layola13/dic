brazilin: /ˈbræzɪlɪn/ | n. |巴西红素| A red crystalline compound extracted from brazilwood and sappanwood, used as a dye and indicator. | Brazilin is a natural dye that gives a vibrant red color. 巴西红素是一种天然染料，可以产生鲜艳的红色。| The brazilin dye was used extensively in the 17th century. 17世纪，巴西红素染料被广泛使用。 | Synonyms: brazilwood extract, sappanwood extract | Antonyms: None | Noun
