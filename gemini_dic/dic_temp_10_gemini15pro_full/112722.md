loyally:/ˈlɔɪəli/ | adv. | 忠诚地，忠实地 | In a loyal manner; faithfully. |  She served her country loyally for many years. 她忠诚地为国家服务了很多年。| Synonyms: faithfully, devotedly, steadfastly | Antonyms: treacherously, disloyally, faithlessly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *lealliche*, from Old French *lealment*, from *leal* (“loyal”) + *-ment* (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *lēgālis* (“legal, lawful”).
