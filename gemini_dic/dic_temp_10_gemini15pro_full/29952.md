cavemen:/ˈkeɪvˌmɛn/ (US), /ˈkeɪvˌmɛn/ (UK)| n. |穴人；（史前）洞穴人|A caveman is a man who lived in a cave, especially during prehistoric times.|The museum exhibit featured life-sized models of cavemen and their tools.  博物馆的展览展出了真人大小的穴人和他们的工具模型。|Cavemen hunted woolly mammoths for food. 穴人猎杀猛犸象作为食物。|近义词：troglodyte, prehistoric man|反义词: modern man| noun
