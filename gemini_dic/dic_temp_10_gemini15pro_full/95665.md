incompressible:/ˌɪnkəmˈprɛsəb(ə)l/
adjective
不可压缩的
Not able to be compressed.  Unable to be reduced in volume or size by pressure.
Air is compressible, but water is virtually incompressible.  空气是可压缩的，但水实际上是不可压缩的。
Liquids are generally considered incompressible. 液体通常被认为是不可压缩的。

近义词:noncompressible, unyielding, inflexible
反义词:compressible, yielding, flexible
词性:adjective
