nonagrarian:/ˌnɒnəˈɡrɛəriən/ | adj. | 非农业的，非耕地的 | Relating to or characteristic of people who are not farmers or their way of life; not connected with agriculture. |  例句：The growth of nonagrarian industries has transformed the economy. 非农业产业的增长改变了经济。| 近义词：industrial, urban | 反义词：agrarian, rural | 词性: 形容词

nonagrarian:/ˌnɒnəˈɡrɛəriən/ | n. | 非农人口；非农业劳动者 | A person who is not a farmer.  | 例句：Many nonagrarians moved to the city for work. 许多非农人口进城务工。 | 近义词：city dweller, urbanite | 反义词：farmer, peasant | 词性: 名词
