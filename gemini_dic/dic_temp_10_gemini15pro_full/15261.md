backheel:/ˈbækˌhiːl/| n. v.|后脚跟，后跟踢；用后脚跟踢|A backheel is the part of the human foot where the heel is located.  As a verb, it refers to kicking a ball with the heel.  It originates from the words "back" and "heel."|He scored a brilliant goal with a backheel. 他用后脚跟踢进了一个精彩的进球。|近义词：heel kick|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps toe kick in a football context)|n. v.
