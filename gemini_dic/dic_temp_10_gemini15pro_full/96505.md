indo-pak:/ˌɪndoʊˈpɑːk/
adjective
印巴的；与印度和巴基斯坦有关的
Relating to both India and Pakistan.
India and Pakistan have a complex indo-pak relationship. 印度和巴基斯坦有着复杂的印巴关系。
The indo-pak conflict has been ongoing for decades. 印巴冲突已经持续了几十年了。
近义词:Indian-Pakistani
反义词:None
词性:adjective

Etymology:
A blend of "Indo-" (short for India) and "-Pak" (short for Pakistan).
