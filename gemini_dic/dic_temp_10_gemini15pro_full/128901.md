nimbus:/ˈnɪmbəs/ (US), /ˈnɪmbəs/ (UK)| n. |光轮，祥云，雨云|A luminous cloud or a halo surrounding a supernatural being or a saint in art; a large gray rain cloud.|例句：The saint was depicted with a golden nimbus around his head. 圣人被描绘成头部周围环绕着金色的光轮。

例句：A dark nimbus of smoke hung over the city. 城市上空笼罩着一团乌黑的烟云。|近义词：halo, aura, cloud|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可根据语境选择例如：clear sky 晴空)|词性: 名词
