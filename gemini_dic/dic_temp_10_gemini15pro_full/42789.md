coupleable:/ˈkʌpləbl/
adjective
可耦合的, 可联接的
Capable of being coupled or joined together.
The two systems are coupleable, allowing for data transfer between them. 这两个系统可以耦合，允许它们之间进行数据传输。
The new software is coupleable with existing hardware.  这个新软件可以与现有的硬件连接。
近义词:connectable, joinable, linkable
反义词:uncoupleable, inseparable (depending on context)
词性:adjective

couple:/ˈkʌpl/
noun, verb
一对;几个;连接;结合
(n.) Two things of the same kind; a few; a small indefinite number. (v.) To join or combine.
(n.) We saw a couple of birds in the tree. 我们在树上看到几只鸟。
(n.)  I'll be there in a couple of minutes. 我几分钟后就到。
(v.)  They coupled the train cars together. 他们把火车车厢连接在一起。
(v.) The wedding coupled the two families. 婚礼使两个家庭结合在一起。
近义词: (n.) pair, duo, few; (v.) join, link, connect
反义词: (n.) many; (v.) separate, disconnect
词性:noun, verb

-able:/əbl/
suffix
可...的，能...的
Used to form adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for being," or "tending to."
washable 可洗的
readable 可读的
近义词: -ible
反义词: None (it's a suffix)
词性:suffix
