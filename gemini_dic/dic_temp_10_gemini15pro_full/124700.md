PHRASE->
mowed down:  (figuratively) defeated easily.  轻松击败。
mowed the lawn: cut the grass of a lawn. 修剪草坪。
get mowed down: (informal) to be killed or injured by gunfire or by being hit by a vehicle. 被枪击或被车辆撞击而死伤。
mowed over: knocked down. 撞倒。
mowed through:  cut through something quickly and efficiently, often implying a large amount. 快速有效地切割，通常意味着大量。
