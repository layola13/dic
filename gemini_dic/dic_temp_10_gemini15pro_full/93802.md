immersions:/ɪˈmɜːrʒənz/
n.
沉浸, 浸入, 专心
the act of putting something completely under the surface of a liquid
例句：Language immersions are crucial for fluency. 语言沉浸对于流利程度至关重要。

例句：The VR headset provided complete immersion in the game. VR 头显提供了完全沉浸在游戏中的体验。

近义词：submersions, plunges, absorption, engrossment
反义词：emergence, withdrawals

词性：名词 (plural)
