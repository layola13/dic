non-proliferation:/ˌnɒn prəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 不扩散，防止扩散 |  The action of preventing the spread of something, especially the spread of nuclear weapons. |  The treaty aims at the non-proliferation of nuclear weapons. 该条约旨在防止核武器扩散。 | synonyms: containment, restriction, limitation | antonyms: proliferation, expansion, spread | noun
