encroaches:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃɪz/
| v. |侵入，侵占；蚕食|intrude on (a person's territory or a thing considered to be a right).|例句：The town encroaches further into the rural areas each year.  这个城镇每年都进一步侵占农村地区。

例句：The sea is encroaching on the land. 海洋正在侵蚀陆地。|近义词：intrude, infringe, trespass, impinge|反义词：retreat, withdraw|词性：动词
