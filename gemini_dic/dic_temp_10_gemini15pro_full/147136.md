overlogicalnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈlɒdʒɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
过度逻辑性；过分理性
The state of being excessively logical or relying too heavily on logic.
His overlogicalnesses sometimes made him seem cold and unfeeling. 他过分的逻辑性有时让他显得冷漠无情。
近义词：hyper-rationality, excessive logic
反义词：irrationality, illogicality
词性：n.


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "logical" (from Late Latin logicalis, from Latin logica "logic," from Greek logikē (tekhnē) "(the art of) reason," from logos "word, reason") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Essentially, the state of being "overly logical."
