leucophane:/ˈluːkəfeɪn/ | n. |白硅钙石|A mineral, a silicate of calcium, sodium, and beryllium, occurring in transparent or translucent whitish crystals. It exhibits fluorescence. |Leucophane is a rare mineral found in alkaline igneous rocks. 白硅钙石是一种稀有的矿物，发现于碱性火成岩中。|Synonyms: none (highly specific mineral name) |Antonyms: none |Noun
