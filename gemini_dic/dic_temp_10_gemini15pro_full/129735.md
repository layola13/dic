non-episcopalian:/ˌnɒn ɪˌpɪskəˈpeɪliən/ | adj. n. |非圣公会的；非圣公会教徒| Not belonging to or associated with the Episcopal Church, a denomination of Christianity.|  Many non-episcopalian churches also hold confirmation services. 许多非圣公会教堂也举行坚信礼。 |  She's a non-episcopalian, but she still attends Christmas services at the Episcopal church. 她不是圣公会教徒，但她仍然参加圣公会教堂的圣诞节礼拜。 | 近义词: non-Anglican, Protestant (in some contexts) | 反义词: Episcopalian, Anglican | 词性: 形容词，名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix): Latin, meaning "not"
episcopalian (adjective/noun): From Late Latin episcopālis, from Latin episcopus "bishop," from Greek episkopos "overseer," from epi- "over" + skopos "watcher."
