manicheans:/ˌmænɪˈkiːənz/
|n.|摩尼教徒;二元论者|Followers of Manichaeism, a religion that viewed the world as a struggle between good and evil; also, more broadly, those who see things in terms of absolute good and evil.|The Manicheans believed in a cosmic struggle between light and darkness. 摩尼教徒相信光明与黑暗之间的宇宙斗争。|
|近义词:dualists|
|反义词:|
|词性:名词|

manichean:/ˌmænɪˈkiːən/
|adj.|摩尼教的;二元论的|Of or relating to Manichaeism; characterized by a dualistic worldview.|His Manichean philosophy saw the world in black and white. 他的二元论哲学将世界视为非黑即白。|
|近义词:dualistic|
