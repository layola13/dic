cantrap:/ˈkæntræp/| n.|魔法陷阱，圈套|A magical trap, often illusory, designed to catch the unwary.  It is typically a minor spell. |The wizard set a cantrap in the doorway to alert him of intruders.  巫师在门口设置了一个魔法陷阱，以便提醒他入侵者的到来。

The dungeon was filled with cantraps and other dangers.  地牢里充满了魔法陷阱和其他危险。|Synonyms: snare, trap, gin, pitfall|Antonyms: safeguard, protection|Noun
