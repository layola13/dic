churchmanly:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃmənli/
adjective
像神职人员的；有教会人士风范的
Resembling or characteristic of a churchman; befitting a churchman.
He spoke in a grave and churchmanly tone. 他以严肃而有教会人士风范的语气说话。
近义词:clerical, priestly, ecclesiastical
反义词:secular, worldly, lay
词性:adjective
