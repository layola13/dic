deffest:/ˈdɛfɪst/
adjective
最聋的; 最棒的 (俚语)
Superlative form of "def," which is slang for "deaf" or "awesome."  It means extremely deaf or exceptionally good.  Its usage depends heavily on context and is most common in informal settings.
例句：That concert was deffest!  那场音乐会真是太棒了！

例句：He's deffest after years of working in a loud factory.  在嘈杂的工厂工作多年后，他耳朵非常不好使了。

近义词：(for awesome) amazing, incredible, fantastic, wicked (slang), brilliant (UK slang)
近义词：(for deaf) profoundly deaf, hearing impaired
反义词：(for awesome) terrible, awful, dreadful
反义词：(for deaf) hearing
词性: 形容词
