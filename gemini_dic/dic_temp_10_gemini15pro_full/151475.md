parqueting:/ˈpɑːrkɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 镶木地板；拼花地板 | The act of laying a parquet floor, or a parquet floor itself.  A floor covering of parquetry. |  The parqueting in the hall was beautifully intricate.  大厅的拼花地板精美复杂。| synonyms: parquetry, wood flooring, inlaid floor | antonyms: carpet, rug | noun

parqueting:/pɑːrˈkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 镶木地板的；拼花地板的 | Made of or resembling parquetry. | The parqueting floor gleamed in the sunlight. 拼花地板在阳光下闪闪发光。 | synonyms: parquet, inlaid | antonyms: plain, unadorned | adjective
