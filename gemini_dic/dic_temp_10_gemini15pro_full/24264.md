briered:/ˈbraɪərd/
adjective
荆棘丛生的；多刺的
Covered with or full of briers; thorny.
The path was briered and difficult to traverse. 这条小路荆棘丛生，难以通行。
The briered fence kept the animals in. 多刺的篱笆把动物围在里面。
Near Synonyms: thorny, prickly, barbed, spiny
Antonyms: smooth, soft, even
