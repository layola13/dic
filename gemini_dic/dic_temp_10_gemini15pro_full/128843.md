nigraniline:/ˌnaɪɡrəˈænɪlɪn/| n.| 苯胺黑|A dark green crystalline compound, C6H5N, the simplest aromatic amine. It is a base, and is used in the manufacture of dyes, drugs, resins, etc.|Nigraniline is a conducting polymer. 苯胺黑是一种导电聚合物。|The sensor was fabricated using nigraniline. 该传感器是用苯胺黑制造的。|Synonyms: polyaniline, emeraldine|Antonyms: leucoemeraldine, pernigraniline|noun
