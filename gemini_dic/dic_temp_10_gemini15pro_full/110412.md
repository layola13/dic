like-a-shot:/ˌlaɪk ə ˈʃɒt/ /ˌlaɪk ə ˈʃɑːt/| adv.|立即，马上|Very quickly; immediately.  It implies a sudden and swift action, often in response to a request or command.|例句：When I heard the doorbell, I went to answer it like a shot. 当我听到门铃声时，我立刻去开门。

例句：He offered me the job and I accepted like a shot. 他给我提供了这份工作，我马上就接受了。|近义词：immediately, instantly, promptly, swiftly, at once|反义词：slowly, gradually, hesitantly|词性:副词

词源: This phrase likely originates from the speed and directness of a fired shot.  It evokes the image of something moving as quickly and decisively as a projectile.
