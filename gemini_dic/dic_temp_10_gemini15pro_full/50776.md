descales:/diːˈskeɪlz/
verb
除垢，去除鳞片
Removes scales (like from a fish or boiler).
The dentist descaled my teeth.  牙医给我洗牙除垢了。
The kettle needs descaling.  水壶需要除垢。
近义词: clean, remove
反义词: scale, encrust
词性:v.

descales:/diːˈskeɪlz/
noun
除垢剂
A substance used for removing scales. (Less common as a noun)
Use a commercial descaler to remove the limescale. 使用商用除垢剂去除水垢。
The descaler worked effectively on the shower head.  除垢剂有效地清洁了淋浴喷头。
近义词: cleaning agent, scale remover
反义词: N/A
词性:n.
