overornaments: /ˌōvərˈɔːrnəmənts/ | n. | 过度装饰；过多的装饰品 | Excessive ornamentation; too many ornaments. | The Christmas tree was laden with overornaments, making it look cluttered.  圣诞树上装饰品过多，看起来很杂乱。| The room suffered from overornaments, making it feel stuffy. 这个房间装饰过度，让人感觉很闷。 | 近义词: excessive decoration, superfluity of ornaments | 反义词: simplicity, minimalism | 词性: 名词


词源:

"over-" (prefix): from Old English "ofer," meaning "above," "across," "beyond." 表示“过度”、“超过”。
"ornament" (noun): from Old French "ornement," ultimately from Latin "ornare" ("to adorn," "to equip"). 表示“装饰品”、“装饰”。
