detachers:/dɪˈtætʃərz/ | n. |分离器，脱离器，拆卸器 | People who detach themselves emotionally or physically from something or someone.  Those who disengage or unfasten something. | The detachers worked quickly to separate the rocket boosters from the main spacecraft. 分离器迅速地将火箭助推器与主航天器分离。 | The museum hired expert detachers to carefully remove the ancient artifact from its display case. 博物馆聘请了专业的拆卸器小心地将古代文物从展柜中取出。| synonyms: separators, disconnectors, removers, unfasteners | antonyms: attachers, connectors, fasteners | noun
