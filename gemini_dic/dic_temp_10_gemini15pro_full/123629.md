monticulous:/mɒnˈtɪkjʊləs/| adj.|一丝不苟的，过分注意细节的|Excessively particular or meticulous about details; fastidious.|She is monticulous about her appearance, always perfectly dressed and groomed. 她对自己的外表一丝不苟，总是穿着打扮得完美无瑕。|近义词：meticulous, fastidious, scrupulous, punctilious|反义词：careless, sloppy, negligent|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *monticulus* ("small hill, mound"), diminutive of *mons* ("mountain"). The semantic shift is likely due to the image of carefully constructing a small hill or mound, requiring attention to detail.
