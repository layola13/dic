indusiate: /ɪnˈduːzieɪt/ | v. | 覆盖，包被;  (尤指用膜) | To cover or enclose, especially with a membrane. |  The developing fern fronds are indusiate.  发育中的蕨类植物叶片被膜覆盖。| cover, enclose, sheathe | uncover, expose | verb

词源: From Latin *indusium* "tunic, undergarment," from *induere* "to put on," from *in* "on" + *duere* "to put, place".  The botanical sense refers to the membrane covering the sori of ferns, likened to a garment.
