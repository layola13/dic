huron:/ˈhjʊrən/| n.|休伦人；休伦语|A member of a North American Indian people formerly inhabiting the region around Lake Huron.  A language of the Iroquoian family formerly spoken by the Huron.|例句：The Huron were skilled agriculturalists. 休伦人是熟练的农业家。

例句：The Huron language is now extinct. 休伦语现在已经灭绝了。|近义词：Wyandot (sometimes considered the same group), Iroquoian|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
