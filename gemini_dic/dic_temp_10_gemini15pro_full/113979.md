magicality:/ˌmædʒɪˈkælɪti/
noun
魔力; 神奇性
The quality of being magical; enchantment.
The magicality of the performance captivated the audience. 演出中的魔力深深吸引了观众。
synonyms: magic, enchantment, wonder, mystique, supernaturalness
antonyms: mundanity, ordinariness, banality
