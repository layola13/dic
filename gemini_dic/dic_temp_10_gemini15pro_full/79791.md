gluttony: /ˈɡlʌtəni/ | n. | 暴食；贪吃；贪婪 | Excessive eating and drinking; greediness. | He was known for his gluttony and extravagant lifestyle. 他以暴饮暴食和奢侈的生活方式而闻名。|  The sheer gluttony of the buffet was overwhelming. 自助餐的丰盛程度令人不知所措。 | 近义词: voracity, greed, rapacity | 反义词: moderation, abstinence, temperance | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *gloutonnie, from glouton "glutton", from Latin gluttire "to swallow, gulp down".
