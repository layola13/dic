misrefers:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrz/
verb
错误地引用；曲解
To cite or refer to incorrectly; to misquote or misapply.
He misrefers to the author's main point in his essay. 他在他的文章中错误地引用了作者的论点。
misquote, miscite, misapply
