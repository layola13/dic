arrogates:/ˈærəɡeɪts/| v. | 擅取；霸占；僭越 | To claim or take something without right. | He arrogates to himself the right to decide what is best for everyone. 他擅自决定什么对大家最好。|  The government arrogated the power to censor the press. 政府僭取了审查新闻界的权力。 |近义词：usurp, seize, appropriate, assume |反义词：relinquish, abdicate, surrender |词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *arrogātus*, past participle of *arrogāre* (“to claim for oneself, appropriate”), from *ad* (“to”) + *rogāre* (“to ask”).
