expeditate: /ˈɛkspɪdaɪt/ (US), /ˈɛkspɪdaɪt/ (UK)| v.| 加快；促进；迅速执行|To make a process happen more quickly.|例句：The company hired more staff to expedite the shipping process. 公司雇佣了更多员工来加快运输流程。|例句：We need to expedite the production of this new model. 我们需要加快这款新模型的生产。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, speed up, facilitate|反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct|词性:动词
词源: From Latin *expeditātus*, past participle of *expedītare* (“to disentangle, disengage, extricate”), from *ex* (“out, out of, from”) + *pedis* (“of a foot, footed, foot-entangled”).
