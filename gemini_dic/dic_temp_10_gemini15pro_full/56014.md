do-it-yourselves: /ˌduː ɪt jərˈsɛlvz/ | adj. | 自己动手的 | Relating to or intended for amateur craftspeople who make, build, or repair things themselves. |  Many people enjoy do-it-yourself projects around the house. 许多人喜欢在家做DIY项目。| Synonym: DIY, homemade | Antonym: professional, prefabricated | Adjective

do-it-yourself: /ˌduː ɪt jərˈsɛlf/ | n. | 自己动手 | The activity of making, building, or repairing things oneself, rather than hiring a professional. | The popularity of do-it-yourself has grown in recent years. 近年来，DIY的受欢迎程度有所提高。 | Synonym: DIY, self-service | Antonym: professional service | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"Do it yourself" originated as a phrase in the early 20th century, gaining popularity in the post-World War II era.  It reflects the growing trend of homeowners undertaking home improvement and other projects themselves, driven by factors like economic necessity and the availability of affordable tools and materials.  The acronym "DIY" became widespread later, further simplifying the term.
