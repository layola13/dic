domineeringly:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋli/ /ˌdɑːmɪˈnɪrɪŋli/| adv.|专横地，跋扈地|In a manner that shows a tendency to control others; overbearingly. |She spoke domineeringly to the staff, making them feel uncomfortable. 她专横地对员工说话，让他们感到不舒服。|Synonyms: overbearingly, imperiously, tyrannically, dictatorially, autocratically|Antonyms: submissively, meekly, humbly|Adverb

domineer:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪər/ /ˌdɑːmɪˈnɪr/| v.|支配，控制|To assert one's will over another in an arrogant way. |He tends to domineer in conversations, never letting anyone else get a word in edgewise. 他总是在谈话中盛气凌人，从不让别人插嘴。|Synonyms: control, dominate, overpower, subjugate, tyrannize|Antonyms: submit, yield, obey|Verb

domineering:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ /ˌdɑːmɪˈnɪrɪŋ/| adj.|专横的，跋扈的|Asserting one's will over another in an arrogant way. |She has a domineering personality that can be quite intimidating. 她有着专横的个性，让人感到害怕。|Synonyms: overbearing, imperious, tyrannical, dictatorial, autocratic|Antonyms: submissive, meekly, humble|Adjective
