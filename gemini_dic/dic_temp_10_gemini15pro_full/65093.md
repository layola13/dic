etheral:/ɪˈθɪəriəl/ | adj. | 天上的，飘渺的，超凡脱俗的 | Extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world. | Her ethereal beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她的空灵之美迷住了房间里的每一个人。| The music had an ethereal quality that transported me to another world. 这音乐有一种空灵的特质，把我带到了另一个世界。 | 近义词：airy, celestial, heavenly, delicate, otherworldly | 反义词：earthly, mundane, substantial | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin aethereus, from Latin aether + -eus. Ultimately from Greek aithēr (upper air, bright sky).
