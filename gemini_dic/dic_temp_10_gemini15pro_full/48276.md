decouplers:/ˌdiːˈkʌplərz/| n.|去耦器，解耦器|A device or system that separates or disconnects two or more things, allowing them to operate independently.|The decouplers allowed the two systems to function without interference. 这些去耦器使两个系统能够互不干扰地运行。|近义词：isolators, separators, disconnectors|反义词: couplers, connectors|noun

decoupler:/ˌdiːˈkʌplər/| n.|去耦器，解耦器|A device or system that separates or disconnects two or more things, allowing them to operate independently.|The decoupler isolated the sensitive equipment from the main power grid.  这个去耦器将敏感设备与主电网隔离。|近义词：isolator, separator, disconnector|反义词: coupler, connector|noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + coupler (from couple, meaning "to join or connect").  Ultimately from Latin copula ("link, tie, band").
