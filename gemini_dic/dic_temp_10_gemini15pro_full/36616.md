collecting:/kəˈlɛktɪŋ/ | adj.  n. |收集的，收藏的；募捐；收集，收藏品|The act of gathering or accumulating something. Also, an adjective describing something related to this act. | He enjoys collecting stamps. 他喜欢集邮。 | synonyms: gathering, accumulating, assembling, amassing | antonyms: dispersing, distributing, scattering | verb (present participle)


collecting:/kəˈlɛktɪŋ/ | n. |收藏品；募捐|A group of objects or items gathered or accumulated. Also, the act of soliciting contributions of money. | His collecting of antique books is impressive.  他收藏的古董书籍令人印象深刻。 | synonyms: collection, accumulation, hoard, assemblage | antonyms: distribution, dispersal | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "colligere," meaning "to gather together," from "com-" (together) + "legere" (to gather).
