cottontail:/ˈkɒtnˌteɪl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtnˌteɪl/ (US)| n.|棉尾兔；棉尾兔的毛皮|A small rabbit with a white fluffy tail.  A type of rabbit known for its fluffy white tail.| We saw a cottontail hopping through the garden. 我们看到一只棉尾兔在花园里跳来跳去。|Synonyms: rabbit, bunny, hare|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an animal name)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the descriptive phrase "cotton tail," referring to the fluffy white underside of the tail.  First recorded use in the late 18th century.
