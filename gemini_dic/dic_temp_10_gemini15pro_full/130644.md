nonadopters:/ˌnɑːnəˈdɑːptərz/
|n.|非采用者；未采用者|People who haven't adopted a particular technology, product, or idea.|Many nonadopters of smartphones cite cost as a major factor. 许多不使用智能手机的人都把成本作为主要因素。|Near synonyms: laggards, resisters|Antonyms: adopters, users|Noun
