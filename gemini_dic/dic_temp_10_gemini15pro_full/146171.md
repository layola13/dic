overevaluations:/ˌoʊvərɪˌvæljuˈeɪʃənz/| n.|高估，过高评价|The overevaluations of the company's assets led to significant losses. 该公司资产的过高估值导致了重大损失。|例句：The real estate market is experiencing a period of overevaluations. 房地产市场正经历一个估值过高的时期。

例句：The overevaluations of many tech stocks contributed to the market crash. 许多科技股的过高估值导致了市场崩盘。


|近义词：overestimates, inflations|反义词:undervaluations, underestimates|词性:名词(plural)
