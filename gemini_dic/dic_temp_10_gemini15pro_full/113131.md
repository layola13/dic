lungworm:/ˈlʌŋwɜːrm/| n. | 肺虫 | A parasitic worm that infects the lungs of animals, especially mammals. |例句：The dog was diagnosed with lungworm. 这只狗被诊断出患有肺虫病。

例句：Lungworm can be a serious health problem for livestock. 肺虫可能是牲畜的一个严重的健康问题。|近义词：parasite, nematode|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology->
From lung + worm.
