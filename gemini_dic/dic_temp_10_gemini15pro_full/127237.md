nauseative: /ˈnɔːzieɪtɪv/ /ˈnɔːʃiˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
令人作呕的; 恶心想吐的
Causing nausea; nauseating.
The smell of the hospital was nauseative.  医院的气味令人作呕。
The rocking of the boat made me nauseative. 船的摇晃让我感到恶心。
近义词: nauseating, sickening, disgusting, repulsive
反义词: pleasant, agreeable
词性: adjective

nauseous: /ˈnɔːʃəs/ /ˈnɔːziəs/
adjective
感到恶心的; 欲吐的
Affected with nausea; inclined to vomit.
I felt nauseous after eating the spoiled food. 我吃了变质的食物后感到恶心。
The pregnant woman was often nauseous in the morning.  这位孕妇早上经常感到恶心。
近义词: sick, queasy, bilious
反义词: well, healthy
词性: adjective
