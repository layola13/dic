asceticism:/əˈsɛtɪsɪzəm/| n. |禁欲主义，苦行|The practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.|He lived a life of asceticism and devoted himself to prayer. 他过着禁欲的生活，致力于祈祷。|Near synonyms: austerity, self-denial, abstinence, renunciation, mortification|Near antonyms: indulgence, hedonism, luxury, self-indulgence|noun
