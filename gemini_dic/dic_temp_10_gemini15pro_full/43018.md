cowboy-hats: /ˈkaʊbɔɪ hæts/ | n. | 牛仔帽 | A wide-brimmed hat, typically made of felt or straw, traditionally worn by cowboys. |  He wore a weathered cowboy hat. 他戴着一顶风化的牛仔帽。

The cowboy hats protected them from the sun. 这些牛仔帽保护他们免受阳光照射。

| 近义词: stetson, western hat | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps baseball cap as a contrasting modern headwear) | 词性: noun
