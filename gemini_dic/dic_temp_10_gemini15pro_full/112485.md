lounge: /laʊndʒ/ | n. vi. |休息室，休息厅；闲逛，懒散地躺卧 |A public room (as in a hotel or airport) with comfortable furniture where people can sit and relax.  To spend time relaxing or idly.|  例句：The hotel has a comfortable lounge for guests.  这家酒店有一个舒适的休息厅供客人使用。| 近义词：lobby, waiting room, relax, loll | 反义词：work, rush | 词性: 名词, 动词

lounges: /laʊndʒɪz/ | n. |休息室，休息厅（复数）|Plural form of "lounge," referring to multiple places where people can relax. |例句：The airport has several lounges for different airlines.  这个机场有几个不同航空公司的休息室。| 近义词：lobbies, waiting rooms | 反义词：workplaces | 词性: 名词


词源: 18th century, from French “languir” meaning "to languish."
