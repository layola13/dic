muffishness:/ˈmʌfɪʃnɪs/
n.
闷闷不乐；不友好；脾气坏
A state of being sullen, ill-humored, or unfriendly.
He exhibited a certain muffishness after his team lost the game.  他在球队输掉比赛后表现出一种闷闷不乐的情绪。
sullenness, grumpiness, surliness
amiability, affability, geniality
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From muffy + -ness.  Muffy meaning "sullen, ill-humored" likely comes from muff, referring to something muffled or suppressed, as in muffled emotions.
