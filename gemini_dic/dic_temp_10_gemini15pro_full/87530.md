heterogeneousness:/ˌhɛtərəʊdʒɪˈniːɪtnəs/
|n.|异质性，多样性|The heterogeneousness of the group made it difficult to reach a consensus. 这个群体的异质性使得很难达成共识。|
|Etymology: From heterogeneous + -ness.  "heterogeneous" comes from the Greek heterogenēs, from heteros "other" + genos "kind."|
|Synonyms: diversity, variety, dissimilarity, difference, heterogeneity|
|Antonyms: homogeneity, uniformity, similarity, sameness|
|Word Type: Noun|
