granules:/ˈɡrænjuːlz/| n. |颗粒，微粒|Small, compact particles of a substance.|The granules of sugar dissolved quickly in the hot water. 糖的颗粒很快溶解在热水中。|近义词：particles, grains, specks|反义词:bulk, mass|Noun

词源: From Late Latin *granulum, diminutive of Latin granum (“grain”).
