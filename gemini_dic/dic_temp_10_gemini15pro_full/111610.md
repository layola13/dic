loca's: /ˈloʊkəz/| n.| 地方，场所（通常指餐馆或酒吧）;  洛卡的（人名或地名）| A loca is a Spanish slang term for a crazy or wild person, often used in a playful or affectionate way.  It can also refer to a place, particularly a restaurant or bar, often implying a local hangout.  In some contexts, it can simply be a possessive form of the name "Loca." | Let's go grab some tacos at the loca's.  我们去洛卡的餐馆吃点墨西哥卷饼吧。|  I saw Loca's car parked outside. 我看到洛卡的车停在外面。|Synonyms:  joint, spot, place, hangout, dive (informal), establishment|Antonyms: (For place) nowhere,  (For person) sane, rational|noun
