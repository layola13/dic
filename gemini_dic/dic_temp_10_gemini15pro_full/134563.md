nonlitigiousness:/ˌnɑnˌlɪˈtɪdʒəsnəs/ | n. |无诉讼倾向，非诉讼性|The state of being unwilling or unlikely to engage in lawsuits or legal disputes.  A preference for resolving disagreements through negotiation or other non-legal means. | The company's nonlitigiousness is a reflection of its commitment to amicable business relationships.  该公司不诉讼的倾向反映了其对友好商业关系的承诺。| Synonyms: amicability, conciliatoriness, peacefulness | Antonyms: litigiousness, quarrelsomeness, contentiousness | noun
