federacy:/ˈfɛdərəsi/ | n. | 联邦；同盟；联盟 | A union of states, groups, or people under a federal government or a system similar to it. | The Southern states formed a short-lived federacy during the American Civil War.  南方各州在美国内战期间组成了一个短暂的联邦。 | Example: The Iroquois Confederacy was a powerful force in North America for centuries.  几个世纪以来，易洛魁联盟一直是北美一股强大的力量。 | Synonyms: confederation, league, alliance, union | Antonyms: division, separation, disunity | noun
