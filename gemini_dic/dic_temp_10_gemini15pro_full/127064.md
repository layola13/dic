nataloin:/nəˈteɪloɪn/| n. |芦荟素，芦荟苷|A bitter crystalline compound, C₁₄H₁₂O₇, present in aloes. It is a cathartic.|Nataloin is extracted from certain aloe species. 芦荟素是从某些芦荟品种中提取的。|Synonyms: aloin|Antonyms: None|noun
