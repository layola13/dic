bossily:/ˈbɒsɪli/ (UK) /ˈbɑːsɪli/ (US)| adv.|专横地，跋扈地|In a domineering or overbearing manner; like a boss.|She bossily ordered everyone around. 她专横地指挥着每个人。|例句：He bossily told her what to do.  他跋扈地告诉她该怎么做。

例句：She bossily directed the team, ignoring their suggestions. 她专横地指挥团队，无视他们的建议。


近义词：domineeringly, dictatorially, imperiously, overbearingly|反义词:submissively, meekly, humbly|词性:副词
