exoculate:/ˈɛksɒkjʊleɪt/ | vt. | 嫁接；移植；挖出（眼睛）| To remove the eyes of, especially of a crustacean;  to bud, as in grafting; to transplant. |  Scientists exoculated the crayfish's eyes to study its hormonal responses. 科学家挖出小龙虾的眼睛来研究它的荷尔蒙反应。| The gardener carefully exoculated the rosebud onto the rootstock. 园丁小心地将玫瑰芽嫁接到砧木上。 | 近义词：transplant, graft, remove | 反义词：implant, insert | 词性：动词
