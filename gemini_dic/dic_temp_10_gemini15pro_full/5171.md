alleviates:/əˈliːvieɪts/
verb
减轻，缓解
makes (suffering, deficiency, or a problem) less severe.
The medication alleviates the pain.  这药减轻了疼痛。
The government is trying to alleviate the suffering of the refugees. 政府正在努力减轻难民的痛苦。
Synonyms:ease, lessen, relieve, mitigate, reduce, diminish, soothe, allay
Antonyms:aggravate, worsen, exacerbate, intensify
词性:动词
