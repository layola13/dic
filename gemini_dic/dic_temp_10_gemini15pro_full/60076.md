eejit:/ˈiːdʒɪt/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜
A foolish or inept person.
例句：He's such an eejit, he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be an eejit, put your coat on, it's freezing! 别傻了，穿上你的外套，外面冷死了！

近义词：idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, nitwit, dope, twit
反义词：genius, clever person
词性：noun
