matcha:/ˈmætʃə/ | n. |抹茶|A finely ground powder of specially grown and processed green tea leaves, traditionally consumed in East Asia.  It has a vibrant green color and a slightly bitter, umami flavor.|I love a matcha latte in the morning. 我早上喜欢喝抹茶拿铁。|  Matcha is used in traditional Japanese tea ceremonies. 抹茶用于传统的日本茶道。|Synonym: green tea powder| Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps coffee?) | noun
