climbers:/ˈklaɪmərz/
n.
攀登者；攀缘植物；向上爬的人或物
People or plants that climb.
The climbers reached the summit just before sunset.  登山者们在日落前到达了峰顶。

The climbers slowly made their way up the steep rock face. 攀登者们缓慢地爬上陡峭的岩壁。


近义词: mountaineers,  rock climbers, vines, creepers
反义词: descenders

词性: noun (plural)


climber:/ˈklaɪmər/
n.
攀登者；攀缘植物；向上爬的人或物
A person or plant that climbs.
He is an experienced climber. 他是一位经验丰富的登山者。

That rose is a climber, so you'll need a trellis. 那朵玫瑰是攀缘植物，所以你需要一个棚架。

近义词: mountaineer, rock climber, vine, creeper
反义词: descender

词性: noun (singular)
