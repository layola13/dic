duces:/ˈdjuːsiːz/
n.
leaders, guides, commanders
Latin word meaning "leaders," plural of "dux."  It's often used in historical or formal contexts.
The duces of the warring factions met to negotiate a truce. 交战各派的领导人会面谈判休战。
近义词:leaders, chiefs, commanders
反义词:followers, subordinates
词性:noun
