nonexpedient:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspiːdiənt/
adjective
不利的，不合适的，不明智的
Not practical or advisable; not suitable or appropriate for achieving a particular end.
It was deemed nonexpedient to pursue the matter further.  继续追究此事被认为是不明智的。
The committee decided that it was nonexpedient to hold a meeting at that time. 委员会认为在那时举行会议是不合适的。
近义词:inadvisable, impractical, unwise, impolitic
反义词:advisable, expedient, practical, wise
