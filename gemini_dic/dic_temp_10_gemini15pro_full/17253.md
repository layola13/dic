baybolt:/ˈbeɪbəʊlt/| n.|门闩，插销|A bar that slides into a socket to fasten a door or window.|The old baybolt was rusty and difficult to move.  旧的门闩生锈了，很难移动。|近义词：bolt, latch, bar|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 open)|词性: 名词

baybolt:/ˈbeɪbəʊlt/| vt.|用门闩闩上|To fasten with a baybolt.|He baybolted the door securely before leaving. 他离开前用门闩把门牢牢地闩上了。|近义词：bolt, latch, secure, fasten|反义词: unbolt, unlatch, open|词性: 动词
