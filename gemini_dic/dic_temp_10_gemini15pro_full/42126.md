cottoned:/ˈkɒtənd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtənd/ (US)| v. |喜欢；理解；明白；亲近|To take a liking to; to become fond of; to understand; to get along with.|例句：She cottoned to the new puppy immediately. 她立刻就喜欢上了那只新小狗。

例句：He didn't cotton to the idea at first. 起初他不喜欢这个主意。

近义词：like, take to, fancy, get along with, understand, grasp|反义词:dislike, hate, misunderstand|词性:动词

cottoned:/ˈkɒtənd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːtənd/ (US)| adj. |（美式，非正式）棉织的|Made of cotton.|例句：He wore a cottoned shirt. 他穿了一件棉布衬衫。

例句：She prefers cottoned sheets. 她更喜欢棉质床单。


近义词：cotton|反义词:synthetic, nylon, polyester|词性:形容词
