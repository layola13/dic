hastate:/ˈheɪsteɪt/| adj.|戟形的，矛尖形的|Shaped like a spearhead; triangular, with the basal lobes pointing outward.|The leaves of some plants are hastate.|近义词：spear-shaped, lanceolate|反义词:rounded, ovate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *hastatus*, from hasta (“spear”).
