goonda:/ˈɡuːndə/ (UK), /ˈɡuːndɑː/ (US)| n.|暴徒，恶棍，打手|A violent criminal, especially one acting on behalf of a politician or other powerful person.  A thug or hooligan.|The goondas threatened the shopkeeper into paying protection money.|近义词：thug, hoodlum, gangster, ruffian, villain|反义词: law-abiding citizen|词性:noun
