archaeological:/ˌɑːrkiəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ (US) /ˌɑːrkiəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/| adj.|考古学的；考古学上的|Relating to the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：Archaeological excavations have revealed a wealth of information about the ancient civilization. 考古发掘揭示了关于古代文明的大量信息。

例句：The museum houses an impressive collection of archaeological finds.  博物馆收藏了令人印象深刻的考古发现。

近义词：historical, prehistoric|反义词: modern, contemporary|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin archaeologicus, from Greek arkhaiologikos, from arkhaiologia "antiquities, study of antiquities," from arkhaios "ancient" + -logia "-logy."
