anti-dissident:/ˈæntiˈdɪsɪdənt/ | adj. | 反异议的，反对持不同政见者的 | Opposed to dissent; against those who express political disagreement. | The government implemented anti-dissident policies to suppress opposition. 政府实施了反异议政策以压制反对派。| The anti-dissident laws were criticized by human rights organizations.  反异议人士法受到人权组织的批评。 | 近义词: repressive, authoritarian | 反义词:  tolerant, liberal | 词性: 形容词

anti-dissident:/ˈæntiˈdɪsɪdənt/ | n. | 反异议人士，镇压异议者 |  A person who opposes dissent or dissidents. | He was known as a fierce anti-dissident. 他被称为激烈的反异议人士。 |  The anti-dissidents used violence to silence their critics. 反异议人士使用暴力来压制批评他们的人。| 近义词: oppressor, authoritarian | 反义词:  activist, reformer | 词性: 名词


词源:  "anti-" (against) + "dissident" (person who disagrees). "Dissident" comes from Latin "dissidēre" (to disagree).
