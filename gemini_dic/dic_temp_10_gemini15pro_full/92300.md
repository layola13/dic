hypnagogic:/ˌhɪpnəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ /ˌhɪpnəˈɡɑːdʒɪk/| adj.|入睡前的，睡前幻觉的|Relating to the state immediately before falling asleep, especially the drowsy state characterized by vivid mental imagery.|She experienced hypnagogic hallucinations before drifting off to sleep. 她入睡前经历了睡前幻觉。|Near synonyms: presleep, pre-dormant, ante-somnial|Antonyms:  post-sleep, waking|adjective
