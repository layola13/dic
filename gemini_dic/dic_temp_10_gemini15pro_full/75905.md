fundraisers:/ˈfʌndˌreɪzərz/
noun
筹款人；募捐者
A person or group who organizes activities to raise money for a cause or organization.
The school's fundraisers worked tirelessly to reach their target. 学校的筹款人不知疲倦地工作以达到他们的目标。
The charity relies on volunteer fundraisers to generate income.  这个慈善机构依靠志愿筹款人来创造收入。

近义词: solicitor, collector, canvasser
反义词: donor, contributor
词性: noun (plural)


fundraiser:/ˈfʌndˌreɪzər/
noun
筹款活动
An event or campaign organized to raise money for a cause or organization.
The annual fundraiser was a huge success.  年度筹款活动取得了巨大的成功。
They organized a fundraiser to help victims of the earthquake.  他们组织了一场筹款活动来帮助地震灾民。

近义词: benefit, drive, campaign
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (singular)


词源：fund (n.) + -raise (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents)

"fund" 来自拉丁语 "fundus"，意思是 "bottom, foundation."
"-raise" 是一个动词后缀，表示“使...升高”或“收集”。
"-er" 是一个名词后缀，表示“执行动作的人或物”。
