outhumoring:/ˌaʊtˈhjuːməriŋ/
verb
胜过幽默，以幽默胜过
surpassing in humor; outdoing in jocularity.  To be more humorous than someone else; to defeat or best someone by being funnier.
He outhumored his opponent in the debate, winning over the audience with his wit. 他在辩论中以幽默胜过了对手，用他的机智赢得了观众。
近义词: outwit, outsmart
反义词:  be outdone, lose to
