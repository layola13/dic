hyperromantics:/ˌhaɪpərroʊˈmæntɪks/ | n. |超级浪漫主义者；过度浪漫的人| People who are excessively or unrealistically romantic.  They often have idealized views of love and relationships. |  She's such a hyperromantic, always dreaming of fairytale endings. 她真是个超级浪漫主义者，总是梦想着童话般的结局。

Hyperromantics tend to see the world through rose-tinted glasses. 超级浪漫主义者往往戴着玫瑰色的眼镜看世界。 | 近义词: idealists, dreamers, sentimentalists | 反义词: realists, cynics, pragmatists | 词性: noun (plural)


Etymology:  "hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér) meaning "over, beyond" + "romantic" (from French "romantique").
