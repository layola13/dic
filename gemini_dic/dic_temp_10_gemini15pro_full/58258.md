drummer:/ˈdrʌmər/ (US), /ˈdrʌmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|鼓手|A person who plays a drum or drums, especially as a profession or as part of a band. |The drummer kept a steady beat throughout the song. 乐队鼓手在整首歌中保持着稳定的节奏。|The band's drummer is incredibly talented.  这个乐队的鼓手非常有才华。|近义词：percussionist|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *drumere, from drum + -er.
