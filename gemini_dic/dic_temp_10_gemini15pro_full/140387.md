oilmen:/ˈɔɪlmən/| n. |石油工人|A man who works in the oil industry, especially one who drills for or refines oil.|例句：The oilmen were working hard to extract the crude oil. 石油工人正在努力开采原油。

例句：Many oilmen became wealthy during the oil boom. 许多石油工人在石油繁荣时期变得富有。|近义词：oil worker, petroleum worker, roustabout (especially for a less skilled worker)|反义词: None (No direct opposite exists. One might consider environmental activist as a contrasting profession, but not a direct antonym.)|词性:Noun
