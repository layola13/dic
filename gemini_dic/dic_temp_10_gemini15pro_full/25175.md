bucolically: /bjuːˈkɒlɪkli/ /buːˈkɑːlɪkli/ | adv. | 田园诗般地，乡村风味地 | In a way that relates to the pleasant aspects of the countryside. |  The sheep grazed bucolically in the meadow. 羊群在草地上田园诗般地吃草。|  We spent the afternoon strolling bucolically through the vineyards. 我们下午在葡萄园里悠闲地散步，享受田园风光。 | 近义词: rustically, pastorally, idyllically | 反义词: urbanely, sophisticatedly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From the Late Latin *bucolicus*, from Greek *boukolikos* "pastoral, relating to herdsmen," from *boukolos* "cowherd, herdsman," from *bous* "cow, ox."
