clogginess:/ˈklɒɡɪnəs/
n.
阻塞; 堵塞; 迟缓
The state of being clogged or blocked; thickness or stickiness that impedes flow.
The clogginess of the drain caused the sink to overflow. 下水道的堵塞导致水槽溢水。
近义词：blockage, obstruction, congestion, thickness, stickiness
反义词：clearness, fluidity, openness


clogged:/klɒɡd/
adj.
堵塞的；阻塞的
Blocked; obstructed.
The pipes were clogged with hair. 管道被头发堵塞了。
近义词：blocked, obstructed, jammed, plugged
反义词：clear, open, unobstructed
