foreslow:/fɔːrˈsloʊ/| v. |减速，放慢|To reduce the speed of; slow down.|The captain foreslowed the ship as it approached the harbor. 船长在接近港口时放慢了船速。| The cyclist foreslowed to avoid a pothole.  骑自行车的人为了避开坑洼而放慢了速度。|近义词：decelerate, slow, retard|反义词:accelerate, hasten, speed up|词性:动词
Etymology: fore- + slow.  "Fore-" acts as a prefix meaning "before" or "front," implying an action taken in advance, in this case, slowing down beforehand.
