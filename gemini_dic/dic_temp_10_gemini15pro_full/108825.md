leavened:/ˈlevənd/| adj.|发酵的，加了酵母的|Caused to rise and become light by the action of yeast or other fermenting agent.|例句：Leavened bread is softer and fluffier than unleavened bread.  发酵面包比未发酵面包更柔软更蓬松。|例句：She used leavened dough to make the pizza crust. 她用发酵面团做披萨饼皮。|近义词：fermented, raised|反义词：unleavened|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *levener (from lever "to raise"), ultimately from Latin levare "to raise, lighten."
