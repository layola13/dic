corage:/ˈkʌrɪdʒ/ /ˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ |n.|勇气，胆量|The quality of mind or spirit that enables a person to face difficulty, danger, pain, etc., without fear; bravery.|He showed great corage in the face of danger. 他在危险面前表现出了极大的勇气。|近义词: bravery, valor, boldness, daring, fearlessness|反义词: cowardice, fear, timidity|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French *corage, from Vulgar Latin *coraticum, from Latin cor heart.
