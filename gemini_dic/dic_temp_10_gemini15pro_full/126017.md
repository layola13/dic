mosquito:/məˈskiːtoʊ/ (US) /məˈskiːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood. Some types of mosquito spread serious diseases like malaria.|例句：The mosquito buzzed around my ear. 蚊子在我耳边嗡嗡叫。|例句：Mosquitoes are more active at night. 蚊子在夜间更加活跃。|近义词：gnat, midge|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|noun
