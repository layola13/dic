overtechnically:/ˌoʊvərˈtɛknɪkli/| adv.| 过于技术性地，过于专业地|  Explaining something using excessive technical language, often to the point of making it difficult for a non-expert to understand. | He explained the problem overtechnically, and I couldn't understand a word he said. 他把问题解释得过于技术化，我一句也听不懂。|  The manual was written overtechnically, making it inaccessible to the average user.  这本手册写得过于技术性，一般用户难以理解。| 近义词: excessively technically, too technically| 反义词: simply, plainly| 词性: 副词
