decagon:/ˈdekəɡɒn/ (US) /ˈdekəɡɑːn/ (UK)| n. |十边形，十角形|A plane figure with ten straight sides and angles.|例句：A regular decagon has ten equal sides and angles. 一个正十边形有十条相等的边和角。

例句：The architect incorporated a decagon into the building's design.  建筑师将一个十边形融入到建筑设计中。|近义词：ten-sided figure|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
