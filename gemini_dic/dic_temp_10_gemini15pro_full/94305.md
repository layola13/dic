imperviable:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəbl/
adjective
不能渗透的；不透水的；不受影响的
not allowing fluid to pass through; not penetrable or permeable.  Also, not susceptible to or affected by.
The raincoat is made of an impervious material, keeping me completely dry in the downpour. 这件雨衣是用不透水材料制成的，让我在大雨中保持完全干燥。

The politician seemed impervious to criticism, continuing his campaign despite the scandal.  这位政治家似乎无视批评，尽管丑闻缠身，仍继续他的竞选活动。
近义词:impenetrable, impermeable, resistant, immune
反义词:permeable, porous, vulnerable, susceptible
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *imperviabilis, from Latin in- (“not”) + pervius (“passable”) + -abilis.
