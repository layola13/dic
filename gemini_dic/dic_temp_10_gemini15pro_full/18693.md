berate:/bɪˈreɪt/ | v. | 斥责，严厉责备 | To scold or criticize someone angrily. | The coach berated the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉斥责了球队糟糕的表现。|  The teacher berated the student for being late. 老师责骂学生迟到。 |  reprimand, scold, rebuke, chastise | praise, commend | verb
Etymology: From Latin *beratus*, past participle of *berare* "to scratch, scrape".  The sense development likely went through a stage of "to claw at" (figuratively) before settling on the modern meaning of "to scold sharply".
