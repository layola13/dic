degrader's:/dɪˈɡreɪdərz/| n.|降解者的（所有格）|Something that belongs to a degrader. A degrader is something or someone that degrades or breaks down something else, often a substance like plastic or a chemical compound.  It could also refer to someone who lowers the quality or character of something.|The degrader's enzymes were highly effective.|近义词: decomposer's, breaker-down's|反义词: synthesizer's, builder's|词性:noun (possessive)

degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/| v.|降解，降低，退化|To lower the quality or character of something; to break down or decompose a substance.|Pollution degrades air quality. 污染降低了空气质量。|近义词: deteriorate, worsen, decompose|反义词: improve, enhance, synthesize|词性:verb
