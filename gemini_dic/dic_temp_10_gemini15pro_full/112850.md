luckily:/ˈlʌkɪli/| adv.|幸运地，幸亏|Fortunately; by good luck. |例句：Luckily, I had a spare tire. 幸好，我有一个备用轮胎。|例句：Luckily, the rain stopped before we left. 幸好，我们离开前雨停了。|近义词：fortunately, thankfully|反义词:unfortunately, unluckily|词性:副词
词源: From luck + -y + -ly. Ultimately from Middle Dutch *luc.
