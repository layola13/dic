anaemically:/əˈniːmɪkli/| adv.|贫血地；无力地；缺乏活力地|In a manner characterized by or suggesting anaemia; weakly, feebly, without vigor.|The economy grew anaemically last year, only increasing by 0.5%. 去年经济增长乏力，仅增长了0.5%。|例句：The project was anaemically funded and ultimately failed.  这个项目资金不足，最终失败了。

例句：He delivered the speech anaemically, lacking any real passion.  他演讲得很无力，缺乏真正的激情。|近义词：weakly, feebly, listlessly, sluggishly|反义词：robustly, vigorously, energetically|词性:副词
Etymology: From anaemia + -ically.  Anaemia comes from Greek *anaimia, from *an- 'without' + *haima 'blood'.
