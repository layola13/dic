overcarking:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːrkɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度操劳，负担过重
Overburdening with work or care; overloading.
He was overcarking himself with too many responsibilities. 他承担了太多的责任，把自己累坏了。
The manager was overcarking the staff with unrealistic deadlines. 经理给员工设定了不切实际的最后期限，让他们过度操劳。
近义词: overburdening, overloading, overworking
反义词: relaxing, resting, underworking
词性: verb (present participle)


overcark:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːrk/
verb
过度操劳，负担过重
To overburden with work or care; to overload.
She overcarked herself trying to please everyone. 她为了取悦所有人而过度操劳。
Don't overcark yourself with unnecessary worries. 不要为不必要的担忧而过度操劳。
近义词: overburden, overload, overwork
反义词: relax, rest, underwork
词性: verb


词源: From Middle English *overcarken*, from *over-* + *carken* (“to load, burden”), from Old Norse *karkr* (“burden, load”).
