obstinacy: /ˈɒbstɪnəsi/ (UK) /ˈɑːbstɪnəsi/ (US) | n. | 固执，顽固 | The quality of being unreasonably determined to act in a particular way and refuse to change one's mind, despite good arguments or persuasion to the contrary. |  He showed a dogged obstinacy in pursuing his goals. 他在追求目标时表现出顽强的毅力。|  stubbornness, pertinacity, tenacity,  doggedness | compliance, acquiescence, submissiveness | noun
