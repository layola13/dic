companioned:/kəmˈpænjənd/
v.|陪伴的，有同伴的|Past participle of "companion," meaning to accompany or be a companion to someone.  Essentially, it describes someone or something that *has been* accompanied.|She felt companioned by her dog during the long walk. 她觉得在长途跋涉中，她的狗一直陪伴着她。|Synonyms: accompanied, escorted, attended|Antonyms: alone, solitary, unaccompanied|Verb (past participle)
