amalgamation: /əˌmælɡəˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 合并，混合，融合 | The act or process of combining or uniting multiple entities into a single, unified whole. It can refer to the merging of companies, ideas, substances, or other elements. |  The amalgamation of the two companies created a powerful new corporation. 两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新公司。

The chef's recipe was an amalgamation of French and Italian cuisine.  这位厨师的食谱是法国和意大利菜的融合。 | synonyms: merger, union, blend, fusion, synthesis, combination | antonyms: separation, division, segregation, fission | noun
