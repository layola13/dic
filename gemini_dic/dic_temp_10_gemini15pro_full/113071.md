lumpy:/ˈlʌmpi/ | adj. | 块状的；疙疙瘩瘩的；凹凸不平的 | Covered with or containing lumps; not smooth. | The porridge was lumpy and unpleasant. 粥块状的，很难吃。| The mattress was old and lumpy.  床垫又旧又凹凸不平。| 近义词： bumpy, uneven, knobbly | 反义词： smooth, even, flat | 词性: 形容词

词源: From lump + -y.  来自名词lump（块，肿块）加上形容词后缀-y。
