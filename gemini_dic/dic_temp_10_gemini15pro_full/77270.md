gastrosplenic: /ˌɡæstrəˈsplɛnɪk/ /ˌɡæstroʊˈsplɛnɪk/
adjective
胃脾的
Relating to or connecting the stomach and spleen.
The gastrosplenic ligament connects the stomach to the spleen. 胃脾韧带连接胃和脾。
The surgeon carefully dissected the gastrosplenic vessels.  外科医生仔细解剖了胃脾血管。
Synonyms:  gastrolienal
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider terms referring to other organ connections, e.g., hepatorenal - relating to the liver and kidney)
词性: adjective
