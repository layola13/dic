clevernesses:/ˈklɛvərnɪsɪz/
noun
机灵；聪明；巧妙；（尤指）小聪明
The quality of being clever, especially in a tricky or cunning way.
He admired her clevernesses in handling the situation. 他钦佩她处理这种情况的机智。
Synonyms: ingenuity, resourcefulness, shrewdness, cunning, craftiness
Antonyms: stupidity, foolishness, naivety, dullness
