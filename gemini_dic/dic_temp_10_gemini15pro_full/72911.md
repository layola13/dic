foment:/foʊˈmɛnt/ | v. | 煽动，激起 | to instigate or stir up (an undesirable or violent sentiment or course of action). |  The rebels fomented a rebellion against the government. 叛乱分子煽动了一场反政府叛乱。

The politician's speech fomented unrest in the crowd.  这位政治家的演讲激起了人群中的骚动。| 近义词：incite, instigate, provoke, agitate | 反义词：quell, suppress, allay, calm | 词性: 动词
