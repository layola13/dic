halftime:/ˈhɑːftaɪm/ /ˈhæftaɪm/| n. adj.| 中场休息，半场 |A period of rest between the two halves of a sports game, especially football, basketball, or rugby. | The coach gave the team a pep talk during halftime. 教练在中场休息时给球队打气。 | The halftime score was 2-1. 半场比分是2比1。 |近义词: intermission, break |反义词: full time, game time |词性: 名词，形容词

halftime:/ˈhɑːftaɪm/ /ˈhæftaɪm/| adj.| 半场的，中场的 |Relating to or occurring during halftime. |  The halftime entertainment was a performance by the marching band. 中场娱乐是游行乐队的表演。 |  He grabbed a hotdog during the halftime break.  他在中场休息时抓起一个热狗。 |近义词: mid-game |反义词: full-time, game-long |词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "time".  "Half" meaning one of two equal parts, and "time" referring to a period or duration.  The compound word signifies the period of time halfway through a game.
