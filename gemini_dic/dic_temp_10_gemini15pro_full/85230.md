harlequinisms:/ˈhɑːrlɪkwɪnɪzəmz/
n.
丑角症，丑角样症状
A pattern of transient, localized redness of one side of the face, neck, and upper chest, sometimes accompanied by sweating and tearing on the same side.  It is a rare autonomic disorder.
Harlequinisms can be triggered by exercise or emotional stress.  丑角症可以由运动或情绪压力引发。
The patient exhibited harlequinisms after a strenuous workout.  这位病人在剧烈运动后出现了丑角症。
