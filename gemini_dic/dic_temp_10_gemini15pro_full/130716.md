nonalkaloids:/ˌnɒnˈælkəlɔɪdz/| n.|非生物碱|Substances found in plants that are not alkaloids.  These can include things like carbohydrates, fats, and terpenes.|Example: Researchers are studying the nonalkaloids in this herb to understand its medicinal properties. 研究人员正在研究这种草药中的非生物碱，以了解其药用价值。|Example:  While alkaloids are often the focus of research, nonalkaloids can also play important roles in a plant's biological activity.  虽然生物碱通常是研究的重点，但非生物碱也可以在植物的生物活性中发挥重要作用。|Synonyms: non-alkaloid constituents, non-alkaloid compounds|Antonyms: alkaloids|noun
