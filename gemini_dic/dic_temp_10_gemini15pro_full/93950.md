immunosuppression:/ˌɪmjʊnoʊsəˈprɛʃən/| n. |免疫抑制|The reduction or prevention of the body's immune response.|例句：Immunosuppression is necessary after organ transplantation to prevent rejection.  器官移植后需要进行免疫抑制以防止排斥反应。

例句：Long-term immunosuppression can increase the risk of infections. 长期免疫抑制会增加感染的风险。|近义词：immunocompromise, immunodeficiency|反义词：immunocompetence, immunity|noun
