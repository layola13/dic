nonmanual:/ˌnɒnˈmænjuəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈmænjuəl/| adj.|非手动的；非体力的；（尤指聋哑人）不用手的|Relating to communication that does not involve the use of hands, especially by deaf or hard-of-hearing people who use facial expressions and body movements.|The teacher used nonmanual signals like raised eyebrows and head nods to convey meaning to her deaf students. 老师使用非手动信号，如扬眉和点头，向她的聋哑学生传达意思。|近义词：nonverbal, gestural|反义词：manual, physical|词性:形容词

nonmanual:/ˌnɒnˈmænjuəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈmænjuəl/| n.|非体力劳动者|A person who does nonmanual work.|Many nonmanuals work in offices. 许多非体力劳动者在办公室工作。|近义词：white-collar worker|反义词：manual worker, blue-collar worker|词性:名词
