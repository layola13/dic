nonracialism:/nɒnˈreɪʃəlɪzəm/ | n. |非种族主义 |The principle or practice of treating all people equally regardless of their race.  A belief that race should not be a factor in social, political, or economic interactions.| South Africa's transition to nonracialism in the 1990s was a landmark achievement. 南非在20世纪90年代向非种族主义的过渡是一项具有里程碑意义的成就。| The government is committed to promoting nonracialism in all aspects of society. 政府致力于在社会各方面促进非种族主义。| synonyms: equality, integration, multiracialism | antonyms: racism, segregation, apartheid | noun
