legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/ | adj. |合法的；正当的；合理的；正统的| allowed by law; reasonable and acceptable|例句：The lawyer questioned the legitimate grounds for the arrest. 律师质疑逮捕的合法依据。|例句：Is his business legitimate? 他的生意合法吗？|近义词：legal, lawful, valid, justifiable|反义词：illegal, unlawful, invalid, illegitimate|词性:形容词
