palindrome:/ˈpælɪndrʊm/ (US) /ˈpælɪndroʊm/ (UK)| n. |回文，回文词，回文结构|A word, phrase, or sequence that reads the same backward as forward.|例句："Racecar" is a classic example of a palindrome. “赛车”是一个典型的回文例子。

例句：Madam, I'm Adam is a well-known palindrome. “女士，我是亚当”是一个著名的回文。|近义词：anagram (字谜)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
