muti:/ˈmuːtiː/ | n. |  传统药物；护身符；巫术 | Traditional medicine or charms, especially those used in Southern Africa, believed to have magical powers. |  He consulted a traditional healer for muti to protect him from evil spirits. 他咨询了一位传统的治疗师，寻求可以保护他免受邪灵侵害的护身符。 | synonyms: charm, amulet, talisman, fetish | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "remedy" in a modern medical context) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Zulu *umuthi*, meaning "medicine," "tree," or "plant."  The word reflects the traditional use of plants and natural substances in African medicine.
