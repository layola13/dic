mulligatawny: /ˌmʌlɪɡəˈtɔːni/ | n. | 马里加塔尼汤（一种用肉、蔬菜和咖喱粉做的浓汤） | A curry-flavored soup of Anglo-Indian origin, often containing chicken or other meat and thickened with rice or lentils. |  I had a delicious bowl of mulligatawny soup for lunch. 我午餐喝了一碗美味的马里加塔尼汤。|  Example: The mulligatawny was rich and flavorful, perfect for a cold evening.  这马里加塔尼汤味道浓郁，非常适合在寒冷的夜晚享用。 | Synonyms: curry soup, rasam | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific type of soup) | noun

Etymology: From Tamil  *miḷagāy*  "pepper water" +  *taṇṇīr*  "water".
