consanguineal:/ˌkɒnsæŋˈɡwɪniəl/ /ˌkɑːnsæŋˈɡwɪniəl/| adj.|同血缘的，血亲的|Related by blood; descended from the same ancestor.|例句：They were consanguineal relatives, sharing a common great-grandfather. 他们是血亲，有一个共同的曾祖父。

例句：Consanguineal marriages are more common in some cultures.  在某些文化中，近亲结婚更为普遍。

近义词： kindred, related |反义词: unrelated, non-consanguineal|词性: adjective
