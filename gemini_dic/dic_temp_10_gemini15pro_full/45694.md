curtsying:/ˈkɜːtsiɪŋ/ /ˈkɜːrtsiɪŋ/
|n.|行屈膝礼|The act of performing a curtsy.  A gesture of respect or greeting made by bending the knees and lowering the body.|She greeted the queen with a deep curtsy.|近义词：bowing, genuflecting|反义词:standing


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "curtsy," which is a shortened form of "courtesy."  Ultimately derived from Old French "curteis" (courteous).
