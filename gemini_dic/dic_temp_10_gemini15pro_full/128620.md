nickel-and-dime:/ˈnɪkəl ænd daɪm/| v. |斤斤计较; 为小钱操心; 在小事上削减开支;克扣|To treat someone unfairly or meanly, especially by charging them small amounts of money for many things or by reducing costs in many small ways.|The airline nickel-and-dimes its customers with fees for baggage and other services. 这家航空公司对乘客收取行李和其他服务的费用，斤斤计较。|近义词：scrimp, pinch pennies, economize |反义词:splurge, be generous|动词

nickel-and-diming:/ˈnɪkəl ænd ˈdaɪmɪŋ/| n. |斤斤计较；在小事上削减开支|The practice of nickel-and-diming customers can lead to dissatisfaction. 对顾客斤斤计较的做法会导致不满。|近义词：penny-pinching, economizing|反义词: extravagance, generosity|名词
