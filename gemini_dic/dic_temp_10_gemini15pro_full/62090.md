encephalotomy:/ɛnˌsɛfəˈlɒtəmi/
n.
脑切开术
Surgical incision into the brain.
The patient underwent an encephalotomy to remove the tumor.  病人接受了脑切开术以切除肿瘤。
An encephalotomy was performed to alleviate the patient's seizures.  进行了脑切开术以减轻患者的癫痫发作。
synonyms: craniotomy, brain surgery
antonyms: None (There's no direct opposite for a surgical procedure.)
