beggar:/ˈbeɡər/ (US), /ˈbeɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|乞丐；使贫穷；难以描述|A person who lives by asking for money or food.|The beggar sat on the corner asking for spare change. 乞丐坐在角落里乞讨零钱。|近义词：pauper, mendicant, supplicant|反义词：philanthropist, benefactor|noun

beggar:/ˈbeɡər/ (US), /ˈbeɡə(r)/ (UK)| v.|使贫穷；难以描述；胜过|To make a person poor; to exhaust the resources of.|The war beggared the nation. 战争使国家变得贫穷。|近义词：impoverish, deplete, exhaust|反义词：enrich, enhance|verb
