doggies:/ˈdɒɡiz/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡiz/ (US)| n.|小狗（复数，非正式）|An informal and affectionate term for dogs, often used when speaking to children or expressing fondness.  It's the plural of "doggy," which is itself a diminutive of "dog."|例句：Look at all the cute doggies! 看看所有可爱的小狗！

例句：The doggies are playing in the park. 小狗们正在公园里玩耍。

近义词：pups, puppies, dogs|反义词:cats (a contrasting animal, not a true opposite) |词性:Noun
