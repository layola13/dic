backlunds:/ˈbæklʌndz/ | n. | 巴克兰变换 | A type of transformation used in the study of integrable systems, particularly in soliton theory. It relates two different solutions of a partial differential equation.  | The Backlunds transformation helps to generate new solutions from known ones. 巴克兰变换有助于从已知解生成新解。|  The Korteweg–de Vries equation can be solved using Backlunds transformations.  KdV方程可以用巴克兰变换求解。 | 近义词: transformation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists as it's a specific mathematical operation) | 词性: noun
