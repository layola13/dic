grittily:/ˈɡrɪtɪli/
adverb
坚决地；毅然地；不屈不挠地
In a gritty manner; with courage and resolve, especially in the face of hardship or pain.
He grittily fought back tears as he spoke of his lost love. 他说起失去的爱人时，强忍着不让眼泪掉下来。
近义词: resolutely, determinedly, courageously, persistently
反义词: timidly, fearfully, weakly, hesitantly
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From gritty + -ly.  Gritty itself comes from grit (n.), from Old English *grytt, of uncertain origin.
