half-defiant:/ˌhæf dɪˈfaɪənt/
| adj. | 半挑衅的；略带反抗的 | Showing a mixture of defiance and some other emotion, often hesitation or fear.  It suggests a subdued or incomplete act of resistance. |  He gave a half-defiant, half-pleading look. 他带着半挑衅半恳求的眼神。| Near synonyms:  rebellious, resistant, challenging, insubordinate,  | Near antonyms: submissive, obedient, compliant, yielding | adjective
