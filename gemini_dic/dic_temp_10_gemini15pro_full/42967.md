coverside: /ˈkʌvərsaɪd/
noun
封面
the outside surface of a book, magazine, etc., that protects the pages
The coverside of the book was damaged. 这本书的封面损坏了。

近义词: cover, front cover
反义词: inside, back cover
词性: n.
