crapefish: /ˈkreɪpfɪʃ/ | n. | 胭脂鱼 | A freshwater fish (Myxocyprinus asiaticus) native to China, also known as the Chinese sucker, characterized by its bright red coloration and rubbery lips. It is considered a delicacy and is also popular in the aquarium trade. |  The crapefish is a popular aquarium fish due to its vibrant color. 胭脂鱼因其鲜艳的颜色而成为一种受欢迎的观赏鱼。

The crapefish population in the Yangtze River has declined significantly. 长江中的胭脂鱼数量已经显著下降。
