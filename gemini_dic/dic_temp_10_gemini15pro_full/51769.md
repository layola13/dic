devotion:/dɪˈvoʊʃn/ |n.| 奉献，献身；热爱；虔诚 |love, loyalty, or enthusiasm for a person, activity, or cause.|例句：She has shown unwavering devotion to her children. 她对她的孩子们表现出了坚定不移的奉献精神。

例句：His devotion to his work is admirable.  他对工作的奉献精神令人钦佩。


近义词：dedication, commitment, loyalty, piety, faithfulness|反义词：disloyalty, indifference, apathy|词性：名词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French devocion, from late Latin devotio(n-), from the verb devovere ‘dedicate by vow’.
