cranioclast: /ˈkreɪniəˌklæst/ | n. | 碎颅钳，颅骨钳 | An instrument used to crush the fetal skull to facilitate delivery in cases of obstructed labor. |  The cranioclast was a last resort in difficult childbirths. 碎颅钳是难产时的最后手段。 | Synonyms: cephalotribe | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps forceps which aid in *preserving* the skull) | noun
