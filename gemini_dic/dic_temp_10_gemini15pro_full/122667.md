modulars:/ˈmɒdʒʊlərz/ (US) /ˈmɒdjʊləz/ (UK)
|n.|模块;组件;模块化单元|Pre-fabricated modulars are becoming increasingly popular for housing. 预制模块化房屋正变得越来越流行。|
|例句：The company specializes in producing high-quality modulars for the construction industry.  这家公司专门为建筑行业生产高质量的模块。|
|例句：Modulars offer a flexible and cost-effective solution for expanding office space. 模块化单元为扩展办公空间提供了一种灵活且经济高效的解决方案。|
|近义词：units, components, modules, sections|
|反义词：integrated system, whole|
|词性|noun (plural)|


modular:/ˈmɒdʒʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈmɒdjʊlə(r)/ (UK)
|adj.|模块化的;组件式的|The modular design allows for easy customization. 模块化设计方便定制。|
|例句：Modular furniture is ideal for small spaces. 模块化家具是小空间的理想选择。|
|例句：The course is based on a modular system, allowing students to choose the units they want to study.  这门课程是基于模块化系统，允许学生选择他们想要学习的单元。|
|近义词：sectional, component-based, prefabricated|
|反义词：integrated, monolithic, unified|
|词性|adjective|


词源: From "module" + "-ar," ultimately from Latin "modulus" (measure, standard).
