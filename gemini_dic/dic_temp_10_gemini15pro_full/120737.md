misanalyses:/ˌmɪsəˈnæləˌsiːz/ | n. | 错误分析；误诊 |  Incorrect or faulty analyses.  The process of analyzing something incorrectly, leading to flawed conclusions. |  His misanalyses of the data led to incorrect conclusions. 他的数据错误分析导致了错误的结论。

The report was full of misanalyses and misinterpretations.  这份报告充满了错误的分析和曲解。 | 近义词: misinterpretations, miscalculations, errors | 反义词: accurate analyses, correct interpretations | 词性: 名词(plural)
