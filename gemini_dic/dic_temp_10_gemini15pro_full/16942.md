basset:/ˈbæsɪt/| n.|短腿猎犬（单数）|A breed of hound with short legs, long ears, and a keen sense of smell, often used for hunting small game.|The basset followed the scent with its nose to the ground.|近义词：hound, dog|反义词:|名词


词源: From Old French *basset, meaning "rather low," from bas, "low."  Ultimately from a Vulgar Latin word *bassus.
