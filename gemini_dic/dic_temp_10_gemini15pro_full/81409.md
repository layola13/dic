grasslands:/ˈɡrɑːslændz/ (US), /ˈɡrɑːsləndz/ (UK)| n. | 草原，草地 | A large open area covered with grass. |  The vast grasslands of the American West teemed with bison.  美国西部广阔的草原上充满了野牛。

The African savanna is a type of grassland. 非洲大草原是一种草原。| synonyms: prairie, savanna, steppe, pasture, meadow | antonyms: forest, woodland, desert | noun
