cordwainer:/ˈkɔːrdweɪnər/| n.|皮匠，鞋匠|A shoemaker, especially one who makes high-quality shoes.|例句：The cordwainer crafted a beautiful pair of leather boots.  这位鞋匠制作了一双漂亮的皮靴。

例句：Cordwainers were highly respected craftsmen in medieval times. 在中世纪，鞋匠是非常受尊敬的工匠。|近义词：shoemaker, cobbler|反义词:(None -  No direct opposite for a profession)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *cordewaner, from Old French cordoanier, from cordouan “of Cordoba,” from Cordoue “Cordoba,” a city in Spain famous for its leather.  Ultimately from Latin Corduba.
