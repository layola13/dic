nonportentous:/ˌnɒnpɔːˈtɛntəs/ | adj. | 平凡的，不预兆的，不凶兆的 | Not ominous; not portending evil.  | The meeting was nonportentous, simply a routine update.  会议很平常，只是例行更新。|  The dark clouds seemed nonportentous, and it didn't rain after all.  乌云似乎并不预示着什么，结果根本没下雨。| Synonyms: ordinary, commonplace, unremarkable, insignificant | Antonyms: ominous, portentous, foreboding, significant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + portentous.  "Portentous" comes from the Latin "portentosus," meaning "full of portents," from "portentum" ("portent, omen").  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
