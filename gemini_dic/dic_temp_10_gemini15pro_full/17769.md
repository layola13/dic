bedkey:/ˈbedkiː/
noun
床钥匙
A key to a bedroom, especially in a hotel or boarding house.
She handed me the bedkey and pointed me towards my room. 她把房间钥匙递给我，并指向我的房间。
近义词:room key
反义词:N/A
词性:n.


Etymology:  A compound word formed from "bed" and "key".  "Bed" comes from Old English "bedd", and "key" from Old English "cæg".
