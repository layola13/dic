attiring:/əˈtaɪərɪŋ/
verb
穿着，装扮
The act of dressing or clothing someone.
She was attiring herself in a beautiful gown for the ball. 她正为自己穿上美丽的礼服去参加舞会。
dress, clothe, garb, array, adorn
undress, disrobe

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English atiren, from Old French atirier, from a- (to) + tire (order, rank).
