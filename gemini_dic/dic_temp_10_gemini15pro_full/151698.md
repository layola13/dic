partner:/ˈpɑːrtnər/ (US) /ˈpɑːtnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|伙伴，合伙人，搭档|A person who shares in an activity or business with another or others.|My business partner and I started this company together. 我和我的商业伙伴一起创办了这家公司.|synonyms: associate, colleague, companion, comrade, mate|antonyms: opponent, adversary, enemy, foe|noun

partner:/ˈpɑːrtnər/ (US) /ˈpɑːtnə(r)/ (UK)| v.|与…合伙，成为…的伙伴|To join with another person or group in order to achieve something.|They partnered with a local charity to raise money. 他们与当地一家慈善机构合作募款.|synonyms: collaborate, cooperate, team up, join forces|antonyms: separate, divide, split|verb
