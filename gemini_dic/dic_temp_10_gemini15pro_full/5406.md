almoravids: /ˌælməˈrævɪdz/ | n. | 阿尔摩拉维德王朝 |  A Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled in Morocco and al-Andalus (Spain) in the 11th and 12th centuries. | The Almoravids conquered much of North Africa and Spain. 阿尔摩拉维德王朝征服了北非和西班牙的大部分地区。| The Almoravid empire stretched from Senegal to Spain. 阿尔摩拉维德帝国从塞内加尔延伸到西班牙。 |  synonyms: dynasty, empire | antonyms: N/A | noun
