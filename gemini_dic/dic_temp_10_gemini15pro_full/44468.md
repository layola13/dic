crossbars:/ˈkrɒsbɑːz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔsbɑːrz/ (US)| n.|横杆，球门的横木|Horizontal bars, especially those connecting the upright posts of a goal.  The ball hit the crossbars and bounced out. 球击中了横梁弹了出来。|Synonyms: rails, bars, beams|Antonyms: uprights, posts|noun


crossbar:/ˈkrɒsbɑː/ (UK) /ˈkrɔsbɑːr/ (US)| n.|横杆，球门的横木|A horizontal bar, especially the one connecting the upright posts of a goal.  He scored with a shot that grazed the crossbar. 他用一个擦着横梁的射门得分了。|Synonyms: rail, bar, beam|Antonyms: upright, post|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cross" (meaning transverse) + "bar."  "Cross" ultimately comes from the Latin "crux," meaning "cross." "Bar" comes from Old French "barre."
