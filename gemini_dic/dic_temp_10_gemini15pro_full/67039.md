explanatorily:/ɪkˌsplænəˈtɔːrɪli/ /ɪkˌsplænəˈtɔrəli/ | adv. | 解释地，说明性地 | in a way that explains something or makes it clear | He spoke explanatorily about the complex process. 他解释性地讲述了这个复杂的过程。|  She described the situation explanatorily, ensuring everyone understood. 她解释性地描述了情况，确保每个人都理解。 | synonymously, clearly, explicitly | implicitly, vaguely | adverb
