hot-button:/ˈhɑːt ˈbʌtn/ (US) /ˈhɒt ˈbʌtn/ (UK)| adj. n.|热点问题，敏感话题|A hot-button issue is one that is likely to cause strong emotions and disagreement.|例句：Abortion is a hot-button issue in many countries.  堕胎在许多国家都是一个热点问题。

|例句：The candidate tried to avoid the hot-button topic of immigration. 这位候选人试图避开移民这个敏感话题。|近义词：controversial, sensitive, touchy|反义词:uncontroversial, unimportant|词性:形容词，名词


hot-button (as a noun):/ˈhɑːt ˈbʌtn/ (US) /ˈhɒt ˈbʌtn/ (UK)| n.|热点问题，敏感话题|A hot-button is a topic that is likely to cause strong emotions and disagreement.|例句：Immigration is a hot-button for many voters. 对许多选民来说，移民是一个热点问题。

|例句：The politician pushed all the right hot-buttons to excite his base.  这位政治家触及了所有恰当的热点问题来激发他的支持者。|近义词：controversial issue, sensitive topic|反义词:uncontroversial issue, unimportant topic|词性:名词

词源:  This term originates from the idea of a button that, when pushed, triggers an immediate and strong reaction, like a panic button.  这个词来源于按下按钮会触发立即强烈反应的概念，就像紧急按钮一样。
