overwarily:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛrɪli/| adv.|过度谨慎地，过分小心地|In a way that is too cautious or careful.|He moved overwarily across the creaking floorboards. 他小心翼翼地走过嘎吱作响的地板。|近义词：cautiously, carefully, gingerly|反义词：recklessly, carelessly|词性:副词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "warily" (adverb from "wary," meaning "marked by keen caution and watchful prudence," from Old English "wær," meaning "aware").
