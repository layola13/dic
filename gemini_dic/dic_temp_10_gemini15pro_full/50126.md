dentolingual:/ˌdɛntoʊˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj.|舌侧的，齿舌的|Pertaining to the teeth and the tongue; specifically, designating the surface of a tooth next to the tongue.|The dentist placed the dentolingual brackets on the back of the patient's teeth. 牙医将齿舌托槽放置在病人牙齿的背面。|Near Synonyms: lingual, dental|Antonyms: labial, buccal|adjective

dentolingual:/ˌdɛntoʊˈlɪŋɡwəl/| n.|齿舌音|A sound produced with the tongue against the teeth.|The "th" sound in "thin" is a dentolingual sound.  “thin”中的“th”音是一个齿舌音。|Near Synonyms: dental consonant, interdental consonant|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, depends on specific sound being contrasted)|noun
