nominates:/ˈnɒmɪneɪts/ (UK) /ˈnɑːmɪneɪts/ (US)| v.|指定；指派|choose (someone) to hold a particular position or perform a particular role.|He was nominated to represent the group. 他被指派代表这个团体。|appoints, designates, selects|dismisses, removes|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin nominatus, past participle of nominare (“to name”), from nomen (“name”).
