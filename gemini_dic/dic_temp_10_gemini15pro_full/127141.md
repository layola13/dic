natterer:/ˈnætərər/ | n. | 喋喋不休的人; 碎嘴子 | A person who talks incessantly; a chatterbox. | He's a terrible natterer; I could hardly get a word in edgewise. 他太碎嘴了，我几乎插不上话。 | 近义词: chatterbox, gossiper, windbag | 反义词: reticent person, quiet person | 词性: noun

词源:  From the verb "natter" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Natter" is of imitative origin, echoing the sound of persistent, inconsequential talk.
