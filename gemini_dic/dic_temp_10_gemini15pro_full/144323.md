outspurned:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːrnd/
adjective
向外伸展的；向外弯曲的
extending or curving outward
例句：The eagle landed with outspurned wings.  老鹰张开翅膀着陆了。

例句：The branches of the old oak tree were outspurned, reaching for the sunlight.  老橡树的树枝向外伸展，努力争取阳光。

近义词：outstretched, extended, spread, splayed
反义词：incurved, inward, retracted
词性: adjective

词源:  "out-" (meaning "outward") + "spurn" (meaning "to kick or thrust away").  The original meaning implied a kicking or thrusting outward motion.  Over time, the meaning evolved to simply describe something extending outward.
