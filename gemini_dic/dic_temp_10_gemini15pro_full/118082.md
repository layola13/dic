mends:/mɛndz/
| n. | 修理,修补;改进,改善 | the act of repairing something that is broken or damaged | The mends in the old sweater were barely visible.  这件旧毛衣上的补丁几乎看不出来。|近义词:repairs, fixes, improvements|反义词:damage, breaks, harms|词性:名词

mends:/mɛndz/
| v. | 修理,修补;改进,改善 | to repair something that is broken or damaged | He mends shoes for a living. 他以修鞋为生。 |近义词:repairs, fixes, improves|反义词:breaks, damages, harms|词性:动词


词源: From Middle English *menden, from Old English *mendan, from Proto-Germanic *mandijaną (“to improve, correct, repair”), from Proto-Indo-European *mendʰ- (“to improve, free from faults”). Cognate with Old Frisian menda (“to improve, correct”), Old Saxon mendian (“to improve, correct”), Middle Dutch meneden, menden (“to improve, amend, correct”), Old High German bementen (“to improve, correct, repair”), Gothic (gamanþjan) (“to improve, amend”).
