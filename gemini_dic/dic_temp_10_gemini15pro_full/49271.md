deliberately:/dɪˈlɪbərɪtli/
adverb
故意地，蓄意地
consciously and intentionally; on purpose
She deliberately misled us about the true cost. 她故意误导我们真实的成本。
He deliberately avoided answering the question. 他故意避而不答这个问题。
近义词:intentionally, purposely, knowingly, consciously
反义词:accidentally, unintentionally, unknowingly, unconsciously
词性:副词

词源:
From Latin  *dēlīberātus*, past participle of *dēlīberāre*  "to weigh in mind, consider," from *dē-* "thoroughly" + *lībra* "balance, scales."
