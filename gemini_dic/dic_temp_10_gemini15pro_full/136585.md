nonsanctification:/ˌnɒnˌsæŋktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|未圣化，不圣洁|The state of not being sanctified; lack of holiness or purification.|His nonsanctification was evident in his behavior.|近义词：profaneness, ungodliness, impiety|反义词：sanctification, holiness, piety|词性:名词

词源:
From non- + sanctification.  "Non-" is a Latin prefix meaning "not," and "sanctification" comes from the Late Latin "sanctificatio,"  meaning "a making holy," from the verb "sanctificare," meaning "to make holy."
