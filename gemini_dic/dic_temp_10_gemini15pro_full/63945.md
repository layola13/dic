episcopalian: /ɪˌpɪskəˈpeɪliən/ | adj. n. | 圣公会教徒的；圣公会的；圣公会教徒 | Of or relating to the Episcopal Church, a Protestant denomination based on the Church of England. |  She is an Episcopalian. 她是一名圣公会教徒。 | The church follows Episcopalian traditions. 这座教堂遵循圣公会的传统。 | 近义词: Anglican | 反义词: (No single direct antonym, could be other Christian denominations like Catholic, Baptist, Methodist etc.) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *episcopālis, from Latin episcopus (“bishop”) + -ālis (suffix forming adjectives).
