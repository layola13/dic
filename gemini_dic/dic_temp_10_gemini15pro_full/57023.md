doubleness:/ˈdʌbəlnəs/
n.
双重性；两面性；口是心非
the state of having two parts or aspects, often contradictory ones; duplicity
His doubleness manifested itself in a superficial charm concealing a ruthless ambition. 他的两面性表现在他表面上的魅力掩盖了残酷的野心。

近义词:duplicity, hypocrisy, two-facedness, ambiguity
反义词:sincerity, honesty, straightforwardness
词性:noun

词源: From double + -ness
