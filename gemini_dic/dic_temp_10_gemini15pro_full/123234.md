monocrotic:/ˌmɒnəˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. |单搏脉的| Relating to or characterized by a single beat or pulsation, especially of the pulse.  It describes a pulse with a single, distinct expansion in each cardiac cycle. |The patient's monocrotic pulse was a concerning sign. 病人的单搏脉是一个令人担忧的迹象。|The physician noted the monocrotic pulse in the patient's chart.  医生在病人的图表中记录了单搏脉。| 近义词: single-beat,  unifrequent (less common) | 反义词: dicrotic, polycrotic|词性:形容词
