e-learning:/ˈiːˌlɜːrnɪŋ/ (US), /ˈiːˌlɜːnɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|电子学习|Learning conducted via electronic media, typically on the Internet.|例句：Many universities now offer e-learning courses. 很多大学现在都提供在线学习课程。

例句：E-learning has become increasingly popular in recent years. 近年来，在线学习变得越来越受欢迎。

近义词：online learning, distance learning, virtual learning|反义词:traditional learning, classroom learning|词性:noun
