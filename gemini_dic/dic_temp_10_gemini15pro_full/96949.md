infallibilist: /ɪnˈfælɪbɪlɪst/
n.|绝对正确论者；认为教皇不会犯错的人|A person who believes in infallibility, especially papal infallibility.| The infallibilist refused to accept any criticism of the Pope's pronouncements.  绝对正确论者拒绝接受任何对教皇声明的批评。|Synonym: dogmatist|Antonym: skeptic|Noun

infallibility: /ɪnˌfælɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.|绝对正确；不会犯错|The quality of being infallible; inability to be wrong.|He believed in the infallibility of his own judgment. 他相信自己的判断是绝对正确的。|Synonym: impeccability, inerrancy|Antonym: fallibility|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *infallibilitas, from infallibilis ("infallible").  From in- ("not") + fallere ("to deceive, err") + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "able to be").
