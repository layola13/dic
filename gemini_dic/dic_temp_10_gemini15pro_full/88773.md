hodg:/hɒdʒ/ | n. | 灰浆桶; 装灰泥的木制容器 | A hodg is a wooden box with a long handle, used for carrying mortar or bricks. | The builder carried the hodg full of mortar to the bricklayer. 建筑工人把装满砂浆的灰浆桶搬给了砌砖工。|  近义词: bucket, container | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *hod, from Old French hotte (“basket, pannier”), of Germanic origin. Related to Dutch hot (“coal scuttle”).
