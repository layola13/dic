alimentally:/ˌælɪˈmentəli/
adjective
营养地；通过消化道
Involving nutrition; by way of the alimentary canal (the digestive tract).
The patient was alimentally challenged due to their inability to swallow solid food.  病人由于无法吞咽固体食物，在营养方面受到了挑战。
Nutrients are supplied alimentally.  营养物质通过消化道供给。
Synonym: nutritionally, digestively
Antonym: parenterally
词性: adjective
