medicalization:/ˌmɛdɪkələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 医学化 | The process by which non-medical problems become defined and treated as medical issues, usually in terms of illnesses or disorders. | Example 1: The medicalization of childbirth has led to an increase in hospital births.  生育的医学化导致了医院分娩的增加。

Example 2:  Some sociologists argue that the medicalization of grief is a negative trend. 一些社会学家认为，悲伤的医学化是一个负面趋势。 | Synonyms: pathologization, | Antonyms: demedicalization | noun
