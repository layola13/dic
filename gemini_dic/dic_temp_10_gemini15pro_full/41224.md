coralliferous:/ˌkɒrəˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
产珊瑚的；含珊瑚的
Containing or producing coral.
The expedition discovered a coralliferous stratum in the seabed.  探险队在海底发现了含珊瑚的地层。
The warm, shallow waters provided an ideal environment for a coralliferous ecosystem.  温暖的浅水区为珊瑚生态系统提供了理想的环境。
近义词:coral-producing, coral-bearing
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "barren" in the context of marine life)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "corallium" (coral) + "-fer" (bearing) + "-ous" (adjective suffix).
