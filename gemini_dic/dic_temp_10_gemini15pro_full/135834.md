nonproportionally: /ˌnɒnˌprəˈpɔːʃənəli/ /ˌnɑːnˌprəˈpɔːrʃənəli/ | adv. | 非比例地，不成比例地 | Not in a proportional manner; not increasing or decreasing at the same rate. |  The costs increased nonproportionally to the benefits.  成本的增加与收益不成比例。|  The size of the animal's body parts grew nonproportionally.  这种动物的身体部位大小增长不成比例。| 近义词: disproportionately, unevenly | 反义词: proportionally, evenly | 词性: 副词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + proportionally (adverb derived from proportional, ultimately from Latin *proportionālis*, from *pro* "for" + *portio* "portion, share")
