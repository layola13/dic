overprove:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːv/ | v. |过度醒发|To allow dough to rise for too long, resulting in a loss of flavor and a collapsed or dense texture.| The baker overproved the dough, and the bread came out flat. 面包师把面团醒发过度了，导致面包烤出来是扁的。|  He overproved the dough, resulting in a dense loaf. 他把面团醒发过度了，导致面包很硬。 |overrise, overferment | underprove, underferment |verb
