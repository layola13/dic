fronts:/frʌnts/
n.|前线，方面，前部|The army advanced on all fronts. 军队在各条战线上推进。|synonyms: foreparts, facades, van, vanguard|antonyms: rears, backs|noun
fronts:/frʌnts/
v.|面向，面对，朝向|The house fronts the street. 这座房子面向街道。|synonyms: faces, overlooks|antonyms: backs onto|verb

Etymology:
From Old French *front, *fruns (“forehead, front”), from Vulgar Latin *frons, *frontis, from Latin frōns (“forehead, brow, front”).  The verb sense developed later, from the noun.
