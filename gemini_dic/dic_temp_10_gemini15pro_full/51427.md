detestable:/dɪˈtɛstəbl/| adj. |令人厌恶的；可憎的|His behavior was utterly detestable. 他的行为令人十分厌恶。|近义词：abominable, hateful, loathsome|反义词: admirable, lovable|adjective


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *detestable*, from Old French *detestable*, from Latin *dētestābilis*, from *dētestārī* (“to curse, to abominate”), from *dē* (“from, down from”) + *testārī* (“to bear witness, to testify”).
