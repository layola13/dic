lounge:/laʊndʒ/ | n. vi.|休息室，休息厅；闲逛，懒洋洋地躺着|A room in a house or hotel where people can relax. / To stand, sit, or lie in a lazy or relaxed way.|例句：The hotel has a comfortable lounge.  这家酒店有一个舒适的休息室。|近义词：lobby, relax, loiter|反义词: rush, hustle|词性:名词/动词

lounge:/laʊndʒ/ | vi.|闲逛，懒洋洋地躺着|To stand, sit, or lie in a lazy or relaxed way.|例句：He lounged on the sofa all afternoon. 他整个下午都懒洋洋地躺在沙发上。|近义词：relax, loiter, loaf|反义词: work, hurry|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "logier" meaning "to lodge," ultimately from Frankish "laubja" meaning "shelter."
