entreating:/ɪnˈtriːtɪŋ/
adjective
恳求的，哀求的
Asking earnestly; pleading.
She gave him an entreating look, hoping he would change his mind. 她恳求地看着他，希望他能改变主意。
The lost child cast entreating eyes at the policeman.  迷路的孩子恳求地看着警察。
近义词: pleading, imploring, beseeching, supplicating
反义词: demanding, commanding, ordering
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *entreten, from Old French entraitier, from Vulgar Latin *intrāctāre, from Latin in- (“in, into”) + tractāre (“to handle, manage, treat”).
