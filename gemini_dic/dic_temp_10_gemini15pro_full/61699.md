emolumental:/ɪˈmɒljʊmənt(ə)l/ | adj. | 有报酬的；带来收益的 | Relating to or providing emoluments; profitable. |  The emolumental aspects of the job were less important to her than the opportunity to travel.  她觉得这份工作带来的收益不如旅行的机会重要。 | Synonyms: lucrative, remunerative, profitable, gainful | Antonyms: unprofitable, unrewarding | adjective

emolument:/ɪˈmɒljʊmənt/ | n. | 酬金，薪水；报酬 | A salary, fee, or profit from employment or office. | The emoluments of the CEO include a generous salary, stock options, and a company car.  首席执行官的酬金包括优厚的薪水、股票期权和公司配车。 | Synonyms: compensation, remuneration, salary, wages, earnings | Antonyms: loss, expense | noun
