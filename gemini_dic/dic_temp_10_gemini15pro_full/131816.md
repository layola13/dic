nonconsequentiality:/ˌnɒnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | n. | 无关紧要，不重要 |The state or quality of being of little or no importance; insignificance. |The nonconsequentiality of the matter was apparent to everyone. 这件事无关紧要，每个人都显而易见。|  The nonconsequentiality of his remark was clear from the lack of response.  他的话无关紧要，从无人回应就可以看出。 | insignificance, triviality, unimportance | significance, importance, relevance | noun
