ichthyosaurian:/ˌɪkθiəˈsɔːriən/| n. adj.|鱼龙；鱼龙类的|An extinct marine reptile of the Mesozoic era resembling a dolphin, with a long pointed head, four flippers, and a vertical tail fin.  A member of the order Ichthyosauria.|例句：The ichthyosaurian fossil was remarkably well-preserved. 这块鱼龙化石保存得非常好。

例句：The museum's ichthyosaurian exhibit is very popular.  博物馆的鱼龙展览非常受欢迎。|近义词：fish-lizard, shastasaurus, dolphin (in terms of shape)|反义词:None (extinct group)|词性:noun, adjective
