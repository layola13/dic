declinature:/ˌdɛklɪˈneɪtʃər/
n.
拒绝；谢绝；婉拒
The act of declining or refusing something; a refusal.
He sent a letter of declinature in response to the job offer. 他寄了一封婉拒信，回复了工作邀请。
近义词：refusal, rejection, denial
反义词：acceptance, agreement, approval
词性: noun

declinature:/ˌdɛklɪˈneɪtʃər/
n.
下降; 衰退
A downward slope; a decline.  (Less common usage)
The declinature of the land made farming difficult.  这块土地的倾斜使耕作变得困难。
近义词：decline, descent, slope
反义词：ascent, rise, incline
词性: noun
