in-box:/ˈɪnˌbɑːks/ | vt. | 将…放入收件箱；发送电子邮件至 | To send or deliver something to someone's inbox. | Please inbox me the details of the meeting. 请将会议细节发送到我的收件箱。| synonyms: send, email, forward | antonyms: delete, archive | verb
