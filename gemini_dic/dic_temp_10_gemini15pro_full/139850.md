octostyle: /ˈɒktəʊstaɪl/
adjective
八柱式的
Having eight columns across the front.
The Parthenon is an octostyle temple.  帕台农神庙是一座八柱式神庙。
Octostyle temples were common in ancient Greece.  八柱式神庙在古希腊很常见。
近义词: eight-columned
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like tetrastyle (four columns), hexastyle (six columns), depending on context)
