aggravator:/ˈæɡrəveɪtər/| n.|加剧者，恶化因素|Something that makes a problem or bad situation worse. |The noise was a real aggravator when I was trying to study. 噪音是我学习时的一个真正的干扰因素。|Synonyms: irritant, exacerbator, intensifier|Antonyms: mitigator, alleviator|noun

aggravators:/ˈæɡrəveɪtərz/| n.|加剧者们，恶化因素们|Plural form of aggravator.  |Stress and lack of sleep are common aggravators of skin conditions. 压力和睡眠不足是皮肤病的常见加剧因素。|Synonyms: irritants, exacerbators, intensifiers|Antonyms: mitigators, alleviators|noun
