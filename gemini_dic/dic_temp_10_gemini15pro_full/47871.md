deceivingly:/dɪˈsiːvɪŋli/
adverb
欺骗地；迷惑地
in a way that is deceptive; so as to give a false impression
The climb looked deceptively easy. 这段攀登看起来很容易，其实不然。
近义词:misleadingly, falsely, deceptively
反义词:truthfully, honestly, genuinely
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From deceive + -ing + -ly.  Deceive ultimately comes from Latin *dēcipere, from dē- (“away, from”) + capere (“to take”).
