bearishness:/ˈbɛrɪʃnəs/
noun
看跌; 熊市情绪
A general decline in the stock market over a period of time. It reflects negative sentiment and the expectation of further price declines.
The current bearishness in the market has led many investors to sell their stocks.  当前市场上的熊市情绪导致许多投资者抛售股票。

The bearishness of investors was palpable. 投资者的悲观情绪显而易见。
近义词:pessimism, negativity, gloom
反义词:bullishness, optimism
词性:n.
