nonporousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈpɔːrəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|无孔性；不透气性|The nonporousness of the material makes it suitable for underwater applications.  这种材料的不透气性使其适用于水下应用。|The nonporousness of the rock prevented water from seeping through.  岩石的无孔性阻止了水渗透。|impenetrability, impermeability|porosity, permeability|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- + porous + -ness

non-:  prefix meaning "not"
porous: from Latin *porosus "full of pores," from porus "pore"
-ness: suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
