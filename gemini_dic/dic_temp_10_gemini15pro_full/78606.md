gibbeting:/ˈdʒɪbɪtɪŋ/
n.|绞刑示众;  示众的绞刑架|The practice of gibbeting criminals was common in the 18th century.  18世纪，绞刑示众的做法很普遍。|Synonyms: hanging, execution, public display|Antonyms: burial, concealment|noun
gibbeting:/ˈdʒɪbɪtɪŋ/
v.|把…吊在绞刑架上示众|The body was gibbeted as a warning to others.  这具尸体被吊在绞刑架上示众，以儆效尤。|Synonyms: hang, display, expose|Antonyms: bury, hide|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From gibbet (n.) "gallows," from Old French gibet "gallows, pillory," perhaps from a Germanic source (compare Old High German wib "beam," Middle High German wibel "gallows")
