daisies:/ˈdeɪziz/ | n. |雏菊|A small European plant with white or pink flowers and a yellow centre, or the flower itself.  It is a common wild flower, often seen in grassy areas. |She picked a bunch of daisies from the field. 她从田野里摘了一束雏菊。| synonyms: marguerite, bellis | antonyms: N/A | noun
