notarially:/noʊˈtɛriəli/
adverb
公证地；经公证
in a notarial manner; by a notary public.  Performed or certified by a notary.
The document was notarially certified.  这份文件经过了公证。
The signature was notarially witnessed.  签名由公证人见证。
Synonyms: officially, legally, formally
Antonyms: informally, unofficially
词性: 副词
