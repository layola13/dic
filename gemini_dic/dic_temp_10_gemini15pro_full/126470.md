mythologian:/ˌmɪθəˈloʊdʒiən/
n.
神话学家，神话研究者
A person who studies mythology or myths.
The mythologian spent years researching the origins of ancient Greek myths.  这位神话学家花了数年时间研究古希腊神话的起源。
