ogive:/ˈoʊdʒaɪv/ | n. | 累积频率曲线; 尖拱; 葱形饰; | A curve that represents the cumulative frequency distribution for the values of a random variable.  It's a graph that shows how many data points fall below a certain value. In architecture, it refers to a pointed arch. | The statistician plotted the data on an ogive to visualize the distribution. 统计学家将数据绘制在累积频率曲线上以可视化分布。 The cathedral's ceiling featured elegant ogives.  大教堂的天花板上有优雅的尖拱。 | 近义词: cumulative frequency curve, pointed arch | 反义词: N/A | noun
