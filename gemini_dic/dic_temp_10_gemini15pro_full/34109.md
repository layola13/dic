ck:/k/ | suffix | 无中文翻译，表示k的音 | A digraph (two letters representing one sound) used at the end of words, especially after a short vowel, to represent the sound /k/.  It evolved from Old English and is related to similar spellings in other Germanic languages.  |  The words "quick" and "duck" use "ck" to spell the /k/ sound. | 近义词: k (sometimes), c (sometimes) | 反义词: 无 | 词缀
