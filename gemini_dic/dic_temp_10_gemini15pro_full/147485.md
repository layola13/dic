overorganized:/ˌōvərˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzd/
adjective
过度组织的，组织过度的
Excessively organized; organized to an unnecessary or excessive degree.
例句：
Her overorganized pantry made it difficult to find anything.
她过度组织的食品储藏室使得很难找到任何东西。

例句：
He was so overorganized that he scheduled every minute of his day.
他组织过度，以至于他把每天的每一分钟都安排好了。

近义词：excessively organized, overly systematic
反义词：disorganized, messy, chaotic
词性: adjective
