heedy:/ˈhiːdi/| adj.|兴冲冲的，轻率的，头脑发热的|Characterized by heedlessness; rash; impulsive. |He made a heedy decision to invest all his money in the volatile stock market. 他轻率地决定将所有资金投入动荡的股市。|近义词：rash, impulsive, reckless, impetuous|反义词:cautious, prudent, deliberate|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *hedi, from Old English *hēdig.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *haidiz. Related to heed.
