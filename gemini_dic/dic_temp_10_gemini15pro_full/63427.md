entrappingly:/ɪnˈtræpɪŋli/
adv.
以诱捕的方式；使人落入圈套地
in a way that traps someone or something
例句：The spider entrappingly spun its web. 蜘蛛巧妙地编织了它的网。
例句：He smiled entrappingly, hiding his true intentions. 他露出了一个诱人的微笑，掩盖了他真正的意图。
近义词：deceitfully, insidiously, treacherously
反义词：honestly, openly, fairly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From entrap + -ing + -ly.  Entrap comes from the Old French word "entraper," meaning "to catch in a trap."
