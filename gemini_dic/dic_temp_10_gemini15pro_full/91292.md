hydrology:/haɪˈdrɒlədʒi/ (UK) /haɪˈdrɑːlədʒi/ (US)|n.|水文学|The scientific study of the properties, distribution, and effects of water on the earth's surface, in the soil and underlying rocks, and in the atmosphere.|例句：The hydrology of the region is complex due to the varied terrain. 这个地区的水文情况很复杂，因为地形多样。

hydrology:|n.|水文学|The branch of science concerned with the properties of the earth's water, especially its movement in relation to land.|例句：He specialized in hydrology and water resource management. 他专门研究水文学和水资源管理。
