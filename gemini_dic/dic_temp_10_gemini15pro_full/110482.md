lily-livered:/ˈlɪliˌlɪvərd/| adj.|胆小的，怯懦的|Lacking courage and resolution; cowardly.  Originating from the ancient belief that cowardice was associated with a pale or white liver.|He was too lily-livered to stand up to the bully. 他太胆小了，不敢反抗那个恶霸。|近义词：cowardly, timorous, faint-hearted, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, bold, valiant|词性:形容词
