azobenzene:/ˈæzoʊˌbɛnˌziːn/ /ˈæzoʊˌbɛnˌziːn/ | n. |偶氮苯|An orange-red crystalline compound used in the manufacture of dyes and some drugs. It is the simplest example of an aryl azo compound.|Azobenzene is commonly used as a dye in textiles.|近义词:dye, colorant |反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
