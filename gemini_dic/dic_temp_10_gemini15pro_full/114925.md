management's:/ˈmænɪdʒmənts/| n.|管理层的，经营者的|The possessive form of "management," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with management.  It signifies ownership or association.|Example: Management's decision to downsize was met with resistance. 管理层裁员的决定遭到了抵制。|Synonym: administration's, executive's|Antonym: employee's, worker's|Possessive Noun

management:/ˈmænɪdʒmənt/| n.|管理，经营，管理层|The process of dealing with or controlling people or things; the people who run and control a business or similar organization.|Example: The management of the company is excellent. 公司的管理非常出色。|Synonym: administration, leadership|Antonym:  mismanagement, neglect|Noun
