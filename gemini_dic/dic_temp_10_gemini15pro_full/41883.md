coshering:/ˈkoʊʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 犹太教洁食认证；符合犹太教规的食品 | The process of preparing food according to Jewish dietary laws. | Example: The restaurant's coshering process is overseen by a rabbi. 这家餐厅的犹太洁食认证过程由一位拉比监督。 | Example:  The coshering of the meat took several hours.  这块肉的犹太洁食处理花了几个小时。 | 近义词: kashering, shechita (specifically for slaughtering) | 反义词: treif (non-kosher), non-kosher | 词性: noun

词源: From Yiddish כּשר (kosher), from Hebrew כָּשֵׁר (kasher), meaning "fit" or "proper."
