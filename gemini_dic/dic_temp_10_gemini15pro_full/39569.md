consciousness-raising: /ˈkɒnʃəsnəs ˈreɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 意识提升，觉醒 | The process of increasing awareness or understanding of a particular social or political issue, often with the aim of inspiring action or change.  |  The feminist movement of the 1970s involved a lot of consciousness-raising about women's rights.  20世纪70年代的女权运动中有很多关于妇女权利的意识提升活动。| synonyms: awareness-raising, education, enlightenment | antonyms: suppression, ignorance | noun

consciousness-raising: /ˈkɒnʃəsnəs ˈreɪzɪŋ/ | adj. (less common) |  用于提升意识的 |  They organized consciousness-raising workshops for the community. 他们为社区组织了提高认识的研讨会。 | synonyms: educational, enlightening, informative | antonyms: suppressive, misleading | adjective
