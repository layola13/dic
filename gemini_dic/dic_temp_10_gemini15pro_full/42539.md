countermeasures:/ˈkaʊntərˌmeʒərz/
noun
对策，反措施
actions taken to counteract something undesirable or detrimental.
The government implemented countermeasures to combat inflation. 政府实施了应对通货膨胀的对策。
The company developed countermeasures to protect its data from hackers.  公司制定了保护其数据免受黑客攻击的对策。
synonyms: remedies, steps, actions, precautions, safeguards
antonyms: inaction, negligence
