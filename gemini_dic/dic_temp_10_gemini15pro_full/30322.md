censor:/ˈsensər/ | n. |审查员，检查员| An official who examines books, films, news, etc. to remove anything considered offensive or harmful. | The censor cut several scenes from the movie. 审查员从电影中剪掉了几个场景。|  synonym: examiner, inspector, reviewer | antonym: promoter, advocate | noun
censor:/ˈsensər/ | vt. |审查，检查| To examine books, films, news, etc. to remove anything considered offensive or harmful. | The government censored all news reports about the protest. 政府审查了所有关于抗议的新闻报道。 | synonym: examine, inspect, review, expurgate | antonym:  publish, promote, disseminate | verb
