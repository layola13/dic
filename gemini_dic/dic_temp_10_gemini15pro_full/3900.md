agrestic: /əˈɡrɛstɪk/ | adj. | 乡村的；田园的；粗野的 | Relating to the countryside; rural; rustic.  Sometimes also implying unsophisticated or unpolished. | The painting depicted an agrestic scene of farmers harvesting wheat. 这幅画描绘了农民收割小麦的田园景象。|  The family lived an agrestic life, far from the city's hustle and bustle.  这家人过着远离城市喧嚣的乡村生活。 |Synonyms: rural, rustic, pastoral, bucolic | Antonyms: urban, sophisticated, polished, refined | 词性: 形容词
