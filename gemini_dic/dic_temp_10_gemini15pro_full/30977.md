chalcographist:/kælˈkɒɡrəfɪst/
n.
铜版雕刻师，铜版印制师
An engraver, especially of copper plates.
Rembrandt was a master chalcographist, renowned for the depth and detail of his etchings. 렘브란트는 숙련된 동판화가였으며, 그의 에칭의 깊이와 디테일로 유명했습니다.
The chalcographist carefully etched the design onto the copper plate. 동판화가는 구리판에 디자인을 조심스럽게 새겼습니다.
近义词:engraver, etcher
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "art destroyer" in a very specific context)
