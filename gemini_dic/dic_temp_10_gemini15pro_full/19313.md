bibirine:/bɪˈbɪriːn/| n.|比比林|An alkaloid with antiprotozoal properties, extracted from the bark and roots of several species of *Nectandra* trees native to South America.  It has been used historically to treat malaria and other parasitic infections.|Bibirine has shown promise in the treatment of leishmaniasis. 比比林在治疗利什曼病方面显示出希望。|Synonyms: bebeerine, nectandrine|Antonyms: None|noun

bibirine:/bɪˈbɪriːn/| adj.|比比林的|Relating to or containing bibirine.|Bibirine sulfate is a common salt form used in pharmaceutical preparations.  硫酸比比林是药物制剂中常用的盐形式。|Synonyms: bebeerine, nectandrine|Antonyms: None|adjective
