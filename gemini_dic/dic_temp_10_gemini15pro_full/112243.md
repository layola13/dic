lootable:/ˈluːtəbl/| adj.|可掠夺的，可抢劫的|Able to be looted; available for looting.  Something that can be taken by force, especially during war or a riot.|The abandoned city was lootable after the earthquake. 地震后，废弃的城市可以被掠夺。|Synonyms: plunderable, pillable, ransackable|Antonyms: protected, secure|Adjective
