hyperconstitutional:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/
adjective
极端宪政的，超级宪政的
Excessively or extremely constitutional; adhering rigidly or excessively to the principles of a constitution.  Focusing on or emphasizing constitutional principles to an excessive degree.
The senator's hyperconstitutional stance prevented any practical legislative progress.  这位参议员极端宪政的立场阻碍了任何实际的立法进展。
His hyperconstitutional arguments often seemed to ignore the realities of the situation. 他极端宪政的论点似乎常常忽略现实情况。
近义词:strict constitutionalist,  ultra-constitutional
反义词: unconstitutional, anti-constitutional
词性: adjective
