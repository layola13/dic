lubber:/ˈlʌbər/ | n. | 笨拙的人；水手；大块头 | a clumsy, awkward person; a seaman; a large, heavy person |  He's such a lubber, he tripped over his own feet. 他真是个笨蛋，居然被自己的脚绊倒了。| synonyms: clumsy oaf, awkward person, landlubber | antonyms: agile person, graceful person | noun

lubber:/ˈlʌbər/ | n. | 新手水手；不熟练的水手 | an inexperienced or unskilled sailor | The lubber kept getting the ropes tangled.  那个新手水手总是把绳子弄乱。 | synonyms: greenhorn, landlubber, novice | antonyms: seasoned sailor, old salt | noun
