congressional:/kənˈɡrɛʃənəl/| adj.|国会的，议会的|Relating to a congress, especially the US Congress.|例句：Congressional approval is needed for the new bill.  新法案需要国会批准。

例句：The congressional hearing will be televised. 国会听证会将进行电视转播。|近义词：legislative, parliamentary|反义词:executive, judicial|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congressus*, past participle of *congredi* "to come together"  + "-ional" (suffix forming adjectives).
