errants:/ˈɛrənts/| adj. n.|错误的，漫游的；漂泊者|Traveling in search of adventure; wandering or straying from a proper course.|The knight-errants of medieval romances traveled the land righting wrongs.|近义词：wandering, roving, nomadic, straying|反义词:settled, stationary, resident|词性:形容词, 名词

词源: From Old French errant, present participle of errer "to wander, stray, err," from Latin errare "to wander, go astray, roam, drift, deviate, stray, err."
