astatically:/ˌeɪˈstætɪkli/
| adv. | 无定向地；不稳定地 | Without a fixed direction or position; unstably. | The compass needle spun astatically. 指南针的指针不稳定地旋转。 |  近义词：erratically, unsteadily, randomly | 反义词：fixedly, stably, steadily | 词性: 副词


Etymology:  From Greek *astatos* "unstable" (from *a-* "not" + *statos* "standing," from *histēmi* "to make stand") + *-ikos* + *-ly*.
