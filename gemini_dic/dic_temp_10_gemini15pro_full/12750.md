assiduousness:/ˌæsɪˈdjuːəsnəs/
|n.|勤奋；刻苦；孜孜不倦
|The quality of being assiduous; persistent application or diligence.|
|Her assiduousness in studying helped her achieve top marks. 她学习的勤奋帮助她取得了最高分。|
|diligence, perseverance, industry, commitment, dedication|
|laziness, negligence, apathy, indifference, idleness|
|noun|


assiduous:/əˈsɪdʒuəs/
|adj.|勤勉的；刻苦的；孜孜不倦的
|Showing great care and perseverance.|
|He is an assiduous student, always studying late into the night. 他是一个勤奋的学生，总是学习到深夜。|
|diligent, hardworking, meticulous, painstaking, dedicated|
|lazy, negligent, careless, indifferent, indolent|
|adjective|
