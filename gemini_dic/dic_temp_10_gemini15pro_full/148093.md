overscrupulosity:/ˌoʊvərˌskruːpjəˈlɒsəti/
n.
过度谨慎；过分小心；过于拘泥
Excessive concern with fine points of conscience or propriety; extreme meticulousness or concern with detail.
He suffered from overscrupulosity about his work, checking every detail multiple times. 他对工作过于谨慎，每个细节都要反复检查。
近义词：conscientiousness, meticulousness, punctiliousness, fastidiousness
反义词：carelessness, negligence, laxity
词性: noun

overscrupulous:/ˌoʊvərˈskruːpjələs/
adj.
过度谨慎的；过分小心的；过于拘泥的
Excessively concerned with fine points of conscience or propriety; extremely meticulous or concerned with detail.
She's so overscrupulous that she spends hours agonizing over minor decisions. 她过于谨慎，以至于在一些小的决定上都要纠结好几个小时。
近义词：conscientious, meticulous, punctilious, fastidious
反义词：careless, negligent, lax
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *overscrupulosus, from Latin over- "over" + scrupulosus "full of sharp stones," figuratively "uneasy," from scrupulus "small sharp stone," diminutive of scrupus "uneasiness, anxiety, scruple."
