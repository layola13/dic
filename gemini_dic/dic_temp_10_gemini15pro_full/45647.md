cursory:/ˈkɜːrsəri/ (US) /ˈkɜːsəri/ (UK)| adj.|粗略的，草率的，仓促的|Performed with haste and superficiality, without attention to detail.  Characterized by a lack of thoroughness.|He gave the report a cursory glance before filing it away. 他草草地看了一眼报告，然后就把它归档了。|The doctor gave the patient a cursory examination. 医生对病人进行了粗略的检查。|Synonyms: superficial, hasty, perfunctory, quick, brief, rapid|Antonyms: thorough, detailed, meticulous, comprehensive, exhaustive|adjective
