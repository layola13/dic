disquietedly:/dɪsˈkwaɪətɪdli/
adverb
不安地，焦躁地
In a manner that shows worry or unease.
She shifted disquietedly in her chair.  她不安地在椅子上挪动身子。
He paced disquietedly around the room. 他焦躁地在房间里踱步。
近义词: nervously, anxiously, restlessly, uneasily
反义词: calmly, peacefully, serenely
词性: adverb

词源:
From disquiet + -ed + -ly.  Disquiet comes from the Old French *desquetier, from des- "un-" + quiet "quiet".
