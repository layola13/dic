cheapening:/ˈtʃiːpənɪŋ/ | v. | 降低价值；贬低；使变廉价 | Making something seem less valuable or important; reducing the value or price of something. |  The mass production of the artwork cheapened its value. 大量复制这件艺术品降低了它的价值。

The overuse of the word "love" has cheapened its meaning.  “爱”这个词的过度使用降低了它的意义。 | 近义词: devaluing, degrading, diminishing, lowering, reducing | 反义词: enhancing, increasing, raising, valuing | 词性: 动词

cheapening:/ˈtʃiːpənɪŋ/ | n. | 降低价值；贬低 | The act of making something less valuable or important. | The cheapening of human life in war is a tragedy. 战争中对生命的贬低是一场悲剧。

The constant cheapening of the currency made it difficult to save money.  货币的不断贬值使得存钱变得困难。 | 近义词: devaluation, degradation, diminution | 反义词: enhancement, increase, appreciation | 词性: 名词
