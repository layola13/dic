bitterroot:/ˈbɪtəˌruːt/ /ˈbɪtərˌruːt/| n. |苦根,一种植物|A perennial herb (Lewisia rediviva) of western North America, having a thick bitter root used as food by Native Americans. It is also the state flower of Montana.|The Bitterroot River flows through Montana. 苦根河流经蒙大拿州。|Synonyms:  Lewisia rediviva, state flower of Montana|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bitterroot:/ˈbɪtəˌruːt/ /ˈbɪtərˌruːt/| adj. |苦的, 苦涩的 (rare usage as adjective)|Having a sharp, pungent taste; not sweet.  Relating to the bitterroot plant.|The bitterroot's root has a bitter taste when raw. 生苦根的根部味道苦涩。|Synonyms: acrid, pungent, sharp|Antonyms: sweet, sugary|adjective
