dost:/dʌst/ | v. (archaic) | do的第二人称单数现在时 |  An archaic form of the second person singular present tense of the verb "do."  It means "you do." | Thou dost protest too much. 你抗议得太多了。|  doeth (archaic), doest (archaic) |  | verb

Etymology: From Middle English *dost*, *dest*, from Old English *dēst*, second-person singular present indicative of *dōn* (“to do”). Cognate with West Frisian *doest*, Dutch *doet*, German *tust*, and Gothic *taus*.
