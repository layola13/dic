flashers:/ˈflæʃərz/ (US), /ˈflæʃəz/ (UK)| n.| 闪光灯；暴露狂；（汽车）闪光转向灯|People who expose themselves in public are sometimes referred to as flashers. 在公共场合暴露自己的人有时被称为暴露狂。|The car's flashers were on, indicating an emergency. 汽车的闪光灯亮着，表示紧急情况。|Synonyms: exhibitionists (for people), blinkers (for car lights), indicators (for car lights)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the person meaning. For lights) steady lights|noun
