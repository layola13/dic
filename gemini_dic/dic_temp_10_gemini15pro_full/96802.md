inerringly:/ɪˈnɜːrɪŋli/
adverb
无误地，准确无误地
Without error; infallibly.
The compass needle pointed inerringly north. 指南针准确无误地指向北方。
Synonyms: infallibly, accurately, precisely, unerringly, faultlessly
Antonyms: erringly, mistakenly, inaccurately, faultily
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + err (to wander, stray) + -ing (forming present participle) + -ly (forming adverbs).  The root "err" comes from Latin *errare, meaning "to wander, stray, err."
