crit:/krɪt/ | n. | 评论；批评；标准 | A critical analysis or evaluation of something, typically a work of art, literature, or a performance. | The professor gave me some helpful crits on my essay. 教授就我的论文给我提出了一些有用的批评。| synonym: criticism, review, feedback | antonym: praise, commendation | noun

crit:/krɪt/ | v. | 批评；评论 | To offer critical observations or judgments about something. | She critted my painting, pointing out areas that could be improved. 她评论了我的画作，指出了可以改进的地方。| synonym: critique, review, assess | antonym: praise, commend | verb
