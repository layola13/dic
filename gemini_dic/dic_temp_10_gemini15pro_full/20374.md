bisulcate:/baɪˈsʌlkeɪt/
adjective
双沟的，具两沟的
Having two grooves or furrows.
The bisulcate leaf of the plant was a key identifying feature.  这种植物的双沟叶片是一个关键的识别特征。
The archaeologist identified the bone as belonging to a bisulcate mammal. 考古学家鉴定这块骨头属于一种有双沟的哺乳动物。
近义词: grooved, furrowed, channeled
反义词: smooth, unfurrowed
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin "bi-" (two) + "sulcus" (groove, furrow)
