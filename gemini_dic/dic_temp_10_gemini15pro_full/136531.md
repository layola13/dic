nonruminating:/nɒnˈruːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非反刍的；不沉思的 | Not chewing the cud; not engaging in deep or considered thought. |  Cows are ruminating animals, while pigs are nonruminating. 牛是反刍动物，而猪是非反刍动物。

The nonruminating child quickly moved on to the next toy.  这个不沉思的孩子很快就去玩下一个玩具了。| 近义词：unreflective, unthinking | 反义词：ruminating, contemplative | 词性：形容词

词源:
From non- + ruminating, present participle of ruminate (“to chew the cud; to meditate”).  来自non-（非） + ruminating（ruminate的现在分词，“反刍；沉思”）。
