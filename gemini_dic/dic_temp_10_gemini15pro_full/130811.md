nonapostolic:/ˌnɒnˌæpəˈstɒlɪk/
adj.
非使徒的；不符合使徒传统的
Not of or relating to the apostles or their teachings.
The group held nonapostolic beliefs, rejecting the authority of the original apostles.  这个团体持有非使徒的信仰，拒绝承认最初使徒的权威。
Their practices were deemed nonapostolic by the church council. 教会会议认为他们的做法不符合使徒传统。
Near Synonyms: unorthodox, heterodox, dissenting
Antonyms: apostolic, orthodox
word type: adjective
