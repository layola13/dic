assessee:/əˌseˈsiː/| n.|被评估者；纳税人|A person who is assessed, especially for tax purposes.  被评估的人，尤指为了税收目的。|The assessee was required to provide documentation of their income.  被评估者被要求提供其收入的证明文件。|The assessee challenged the tax assessment in court.  被评估者在法庭上对税务评估提出质疑。|synonyms: taxpayer, subject (of assessment), ratepayer|antonyms: assessor|noun
