adherable:/ədˈhɪərəbl/
adjective
可粘附的; 可附着的
Capable of adhering; sticky.  Able to stick fast to a surface.
The label is adherable to most surfaces.  这个标签可以粘附到大多数表面上。

The adhesive is adherable to various materials.  这种粘合剂可以粘附到各种材料上。

sticky, adhesive, adherent
non-adherent, non-adhesive
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From adhere + -able.  Adhere comes from Latin adhaerere "to stick to," from ad- "to" + haerere "to stick."

USAGE->
This material is adherable to metal. = 这种材料可以粘附到金属上。
The new bandage is highly adherable. =  这种新的绷带非常粘。


PHRASE->
highly adherable = 高度粘附的
readily adherable = 易于粘附的
poorly adherable = 粘附性差的
not adherable = 不可粘附的
surface adherable = 表面可粘附的
