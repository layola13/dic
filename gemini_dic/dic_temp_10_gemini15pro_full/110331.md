lightest:/ˈlaɪtɪst/| adj.|最轻的|The superlative form of "light," meaning having the least weight or mass. |This feather is the lightest of all. 这根羽毛是最轻的。

The helium balloon was the lightest thing in the room.  这个氦气球是房间里最轻的东西。

|近义词：least heavy, slightest, airiest|反义词: heaviest, weightiest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *lightest, *lyghtest, from Old English lēohtest, līhtest (“lightest”), superlative form of lēoht (“light”). Cognate with West Frisian lichtste, Dutch lichtst, German leichtest.
