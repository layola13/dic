aund:/ɑːnd/ | n. | aunt | A sister of one's father or mother; or the wife of one's uncle. | My aund is coming to visit next week. 我的阿姨下周要来看我。|  Synonym: auntie | Antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *aunte*, from Old French *ante*, from Latin *amita* ("father's sister").  The modern spelling reflects a pronunciation change influenced by Anglo-Norman.
