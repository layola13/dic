indehiscent:/ˌɪndiˈhɪsənt/ | adj. | 不裂开的，不开裂的 | Not splitting open spontaneously when ripe to release seeds, as a fruit or seedpod. | The indehiscent pods of the plant hold the seeds until they decompose. 这种植物的不裂荚果会一直保存种子，直到它们腐烂。|  The peanut is an example of an indehiscent fruit. 花生是不裂果实的一个例子。 | 近义词: closed, sealed | 反义词: dehiscent, splitting | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *indehiscens, present participle of *indehiscere "not to gape," from in- "not" + dehiscere "to gape."
