cobweblike:/ˈkɒbwɛblaɪk/ (UK) /ˈkɑːbwɛblaɪk/ (US)| adj.|蜘蛛网状的；蛛网似的|Resembling a cobweb; filmy, delicate, and intricate.|The old house was filled with cobweblike draperies.|近义词：gossamer, filmy, diaphanous|反义词:thick, dense|词性:形容词
Etymology: cobweb + like.  "Cobweb" comes from Middle English *coppeweb*, from *coppe* (spider) + *web*.
