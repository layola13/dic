grownups:/ˈɡroʊnˌʌps/ (US), /ˈɡrəʊnˌʌps/ (UK)| n. |成年人|Adults, mature people.  Refers to people who are no longer children.|Example: The grownups are talking in the living room. 成年人在客厅里谈话。|Example:  Only grownups are allowed to drive. 只有成年人才可以开车。|近义词：adults, elders, mature people|反义词:children, kids, youngsters|Noun
