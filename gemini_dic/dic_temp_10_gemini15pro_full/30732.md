cervixes: /ˈsɜːrvɪsiːz/
noun
子宫颈（cervix 的复数）
The plural form of cervix, referring to the lower, narrow end of the uterus.
During childbirth, the cervixes of the women dilated to allow the babies to pass through.  分娩期间，这些妇女的子宫颈扩张以允许婴儿通过。
The doctor examined the patients' cervixes for any abnormalities. 医生检查了病人的子宫颈，看看是否有异常。
