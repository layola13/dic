如果您是想了解 "what" 这个单词，我可以提供它的解析：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. |

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？

近义词：which, how

反义词:  没有直接的反义词


词性: pronoun

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she do for a living? = 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing here? = 他在这里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 3 PM yesterday? = 昨天下午3点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 中断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们已经讨论了几个小时什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this weekend? = 这个周末你会做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么？
What will she have been working on by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将一直在做什么？


PHRASE->

What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样？
So what? = 那又怎样？
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？


注意事项:  "what" 没有直接的反义词.  它更多的是一个疑问词，用于提问而不是表达相反的概念。
