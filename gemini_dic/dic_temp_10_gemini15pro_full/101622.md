irrecoverably: /ˌɪrɪˈkʌvərəbli/
adverb
不能挽回地，不可逆地
in a way that is impossible to recover from
His reputation was irrecoverably damaged. 他的名誉受到了无法挽回的损害。
irreparably, permanently, beyond repair
