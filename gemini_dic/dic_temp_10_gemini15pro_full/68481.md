familistical:/ˌfæmɪˈlɪstɪkəl/
adjective
家庭本位的；家族主义的
Characterized by strong family ties and loyalty, often to the exclusion of outside interests or influences.  Prioritizing family relationships and obligations above all else.
例句：
Her familistical values led her to choose a career close to home.
她家庭本位的价值观使她选择了一份离家近的工作。

Their familistical culture emphasizes the importance of extended family.
他们的家族主义文化强调大家庭的重要性。

近义词：family-oriented, clannish, tribal
反义词：individualistic, independent


词性: adjective

词源:  From "family" + "-istical," a suffix forming adjectives meaning "pertaining to" or "characterized by."
