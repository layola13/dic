hemispheroid:/ˌhemɪˈsfɪrɔɪd/ | n. | 半球体，半球状物 | A solid formed by revolving a semi-ellipse about one of its axes. It resembles a sphere flattened at the poles or at the equator. | The Earth is not a perfect sphere, but rather an oblate hemispheroid. 地球不是一个完美的球体，而是一个扁球体。

The orange was shaped like a hemispheroid. 这个橙子形状像一个半球体。| 近义词: ellipsoid, spheroid | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "sphere" for contrast) | noun
