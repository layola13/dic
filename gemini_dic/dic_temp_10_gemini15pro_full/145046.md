overagitate: /ˌoʊvərˈædʒɪteɪt/
verb
过度搅拌，过度搅动
to agitate excessively, typically a liquid or mixture.
The instructions warned not to overagitate the mixture, or it would become too frothy.  过度搅拌混合物，否则会变得太泡沫状。
She overagitated the paint, causing it to separate. 她过度搅拌油漆，导致它分层。
近义词: overmix, overstir, overwork
反义词: undermix, understir
词性: verb


overagitate: /ˌoʊvərˈædʒɪteɪt/
adjective
过度激动的，过度焦虑的
(of a person) excessively agitated or nervous.
He became overagitated when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后变得过度激动。
The overagitated patient paced the room restlessly.  过度焦虑的病人焦躁地在房间里踱步。
近义词: overwrought, frantic, distraught
反义词: calm, composed
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (prefix meaning "excessive") + 'agitate' (from Latin 'agitare', frequentative of 'agere' meaning "to drive, do, act").
