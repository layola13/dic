atrophy:/ˈætrəfi/| v. |萎缩，衰退|(of body tissue or an organ) waste away, especially as a result of the degeneration of cells, or become vestigial during evolution.|His muscles atrophied from lack of use. 他的肌肉因缺乏使用而萎缩。|degenerate, decline, deteriorate, wither|grow, develop, flourish|verb
