leaseholder:/ˈliːshəʊldər/ (UK) /ˈliːshˌhoʊldər/ (US)| n.|租赁人|A person who holds a lease.|The leaseholder has the right to occupy the property for the duration of the lease.|Synonym: lessee, renter|Antonym: lessor, landlord|noun
