crenellated: /ˈkrɛnəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 城垛状的，有雉堞的 | Having battlements or notches like those on a castle wall. | The crenellated walls of the old fort stood stark against the sky. 古堡城垛状的墙壁在天空中显得格外醒目。|  The crenellated tower offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside.  这座有雉堞的塔楼可以俯瞰周围的乡村。| 近义词: battlemented, notched, indented | 反义词: smooth, flat | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Old French *creneler, from crenel 'notch', from Vulgar Latin *crenellus, diminutive of Latin crena 'notch'.
