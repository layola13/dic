leftpad:/lɛftˈpæd/| v. n.|左填充，左补齐|To add characters to the beginning of a string, typically spaces, to achieve a desired length.  Origin: Emerged in the JavaScript community, initially as a small, seemingly trivial package, but gained notoriety due to a 2016 incident where its removal from the npm registry broke numerous other projects that depended on it.  This highlighted the interconnectedness of software dependencies and the importance of even seemingly small modules.|Example: The function `leftpad("hello", 10)` would return "     hello".  该函数 `leftpad("hello", 10)` 将返回 "     hello".|近义词：pad, prepend|反义词: rightpad, append|词性:verb, noun
