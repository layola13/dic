learn:/lɜːrn/ (British) /lɝːn/ (American)| v. |学习，学会；得知，获悉|To gain knowledge or skill by study, instruction, or experience.  To become aware of something by information or from observation.|She learned to play the piano at a young age. 她从小就学会了弹钢琴。  He learned of his father's death through a telegram. 他通过电报得知了父亲去世的消息。|近义词：study, discover, acquire, grasp|反义词:forget, unlearn|词性:动词
