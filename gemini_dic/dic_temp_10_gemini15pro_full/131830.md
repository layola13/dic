nonconsignments:/ˌnɒnkənˈsaɪnmənts/ | n. |未寄售货物，非寄售商品 | Goods that are not sent on consignment, meaning they are not delivered to a third party to sell on the owner's behalf.  The ownership remains with the original seller until the goods are sold.|  The warehouse was full of nonconsignments, waiting to be shipped directly to customers. 仓库里堆满了未寄售的货物，等待直接运送给客户。| The company prefers to sell nonconsignments to maintain control over pricing and distribution.  该公司倾向于销售非寄售商品，以保持对定价和分销的控制。 | synonyms:  direct sales, outright sales | antonyms: consignments | noun
