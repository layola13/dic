flatties:/ˈflætiz/| n.|平底鞋；平底船；奉承话|Flat shoes, especially ballet pumps. Also, a type of shallow-draft boat, or insincere flattery.|She prefers wearing flatties to heels. 她更喜欢穿平底鞋而不是高跟鞋。|The fishermen used flatties to navigate the shallow waters. 渔民们用平底船在浅水区航行。|He wasn't fooled by their flatties. 他没有被他们的奉承话所愚弄。|近义词：flats, ballet pumps (for shoes), shallow-draft boats (for boats), compliments, flattery (for insincere praise)|反义词：heels (for shoes), deep-draft boats (for boats), criticism (for praise)|词性:名词
