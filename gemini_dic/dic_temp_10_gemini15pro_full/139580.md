obviate:/ˈɒbviˌeɪt/ (US) /ˈɒbvieɪt/ (UK)| v. |排除；避免；使不必要|To remove a difficulty, especially so that action to deal with it is unnecessary.|The new medical treatment obviates the need for surgery. 新的治疗方法避免了手术的需要。|Synonyms: preclude, prevent, avert, forestall|Antonyms: necessitate, require, cause, create|verb
