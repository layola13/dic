depraving:/dɪˈpreɪvɪŋ/
adjective
堕落的；腐化的；使人堕落的
making someone morally bad
例句：
The depraving influence of violence in the media.
媒体中暴力的腐化影响。

Pornography is seen as depraving and corrupting.
色情被视为堕落和腐败的。


近义词：corrupting, debasing, degrading, perverting, vitiating
反义词：ennobling, uplifting, improving
词性：adjective (形容词)
