overcourtesy:/ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtəsi/| n.|过分客气，过度的礼貌|Excessive politeness; more courtesy than is necessary or appropriate.|His overcourtesy made her uncomfortable. 他的过分客气让她感到不舒服。|近义词：excessive politeness, overpoliteness|反义词:rudeness, impoliteness|词性:名词
Etymology: From over- + courtesy.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above," "across," "beyond," or "excessive." "Courtesy" comes from Old French "curteisie," meaning "courtly behavior."
