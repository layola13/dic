engraven:/ɪnˈɡreɪvən/
adjective
雕刻的，铭刻的
Deeply engraved in my memory. 深深地铭刻在我的记忆中.
The inscription was engraved on the tombstone. 这段铭文被刻在了墓碑上.
近义词: carved, etched, inscribed
反义词: erased, obliterated
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *engraven, from Anglo-Norman engraver, from en- + graver (“to carve, engrave”).  Ultimately from Latin *gravare, from gravis (“heavy”). Related to grave.
