acuminose:/əˈkjuːmɪˌnoʊs/| adj.|尖的，针状的|Covered with sharp points or prickles; needle-shaped.  Having a tapering point; acuminate.|The acuminose leaves of the holly bush are a deterrent to grazing animals. 冬青树尖锐的叶子可以阻止食草动物啃食。

The cactus has acuminose spines for protection. 仙人掌有尖锐的刺以保护自己。|近义词：acuminate, sharp, pointed, prickly, spiny|反义词：blunt, obtuse, rounded|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acuminosus, from acuminare (“to sharpen”), from acumen (“point, sharp edge”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ḱ- (“sharp”).

USAGE->
Attributive:
The acuminose leaves provided excellent camouflage. 这些尖锐的叶子提供了极好的伪装。
Acuminose thorns covered the stem of the rose. 尖锐的刺覆盖了玫瑰的茎。

Predicative (less common):
The tips of the crystals are acuminose. 晶体的尖端是尖的。
The plant's defenses are acuminose.  这种植物的防御是尖锐的。

PHRASE->
