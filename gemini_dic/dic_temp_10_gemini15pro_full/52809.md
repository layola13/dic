dillydally: /ˈdɪlɪˌdæli/ | vi. | 磨蹭，闲荡，拖延 | to waste time or delay doing something |  Example: Stop dillydallying and get ready for school! 别磨蹭了，快准备上学！

Example:  They dillydallied over breakfast and missed the bus. 他们在早餐上磨蹭，错过了公交车。

近义词: dawdle, loiter, procrastinate, linger | 反义词: hurry, hasten, expedite | 词性: verb (不及物动词)
