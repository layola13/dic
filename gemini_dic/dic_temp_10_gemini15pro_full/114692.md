malingerer:/məˈlɪŋɡərər/ | n. | 装病者，逃避责任者 |  Someone who pretends to be ill or injured in order to avoid work or duty. | He was accused of being a malingerer because he was always calling in sick on Mondays.  他被指责为装病者，因为他总是在星期一打电话请病假。| synonyms: shirker, slacker, idler, dodger | antonyms: hard worker, diligent worker | noun

malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/ | v. | 装病，逃避责任 | To pretend to be ill or injured in order to avoid work or duty. | He malingered for weeks to avoid taking the exam. 他装病数周以逃避考试。 | synonyms: feign illness, fake sickness, shirk, dodge | antonyms: work diligently, fulfill obligations | verb
