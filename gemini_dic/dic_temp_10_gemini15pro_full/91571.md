hyperaccurately:/ˌhaɪpərˈækjərətli/
|adv.|极其精确地，超级精确地|With advanced technology, we can now measure distances hyperaccurately. 借助先进技术，我们现在可以超级精确地测量距离。| This new telescope allows astronomers to observe celestial objects hyperaccurately. 这台新的望远镜使天文学家能够极其精确地观察天体。 |近义词：extremely accurately, very precisely, with extreme precision|反义词:inaccurately, imprecisely|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, beyond") + accurately.  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ), meaning "over, above, beyond."  "Accurately" comes from the Latin "accuratus," past participle of "accurare" (to take care of), from "ad-" (to) + "cura" (care).
