ego-tripped:/ˈiːɡoʊtrɪpt/ | adj. |自我陶醉的，自恋的 |  Experiencing or exhibiting an inflated sense of self-importance or an excessive preoccupation with oneself.  Derived from "ego trip," a noun referring to an experience or activity that boosts one's self-esteem, often excessively. | He went on a real ego-tripped rant about how great he was. 他开始了一场关于自己有多伟大的、非常自恋的咆哮。 | 近义词: narcissistic, self-absorbed, conceited, vain | 反义词: humble, modest, self-effacing | 词性: 形容词
