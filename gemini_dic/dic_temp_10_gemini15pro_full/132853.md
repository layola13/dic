nonefficacy:/ˌnɒnˈɛfɪkəsi/
n.
无效；无用；缺乏疗效
The study demonstrated the nonefficacy of the drug in treating the disease. 这项研究证明了该药物治疗这种疾病无效。
The nonefficacy of the new policy was evident in the rising unemployment rate. 新政策的无效性在不断上升的失业率中显而易见。
ineffectiveness, inefficacy, failure
efficacy, effectiveness, potency
noun
