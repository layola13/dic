cerebrose:/səˈriːbroʊs/| n. |脑糖；半乳糖|A monosaccharide sugar (C6H12O6) occurring in the brain.|Cerebrose is a component of certain glycolipids.|近义词:galactose|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From cerebr(o)-  + -ose (suffix forming names of sugars).  Cerebr(o)- comes from Latin cerebrum "brain."
