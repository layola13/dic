hands-on:/ˈhændzˈɒn/ (US), /ˈhændzˈɒn/ (UK)| adj.|亲自动手的，实际操作的| involving or requiring personal participation, especially in a practical way; characterized by active personal involvement|  This workshop is very hands-on, giving students practical experience.  这个研讨会非常注重实践，让学生获得实际经验。

|例句：She's a hands-on manager who likes to be involved in every aspect of the business. 她是一位事必躬亲的经理，喜欢参与业务的方方面面。
|例句：Hands-on experience is the best way to learn.  亲身实践是最好的学习方法。
|近义词：practical, applied, active, involved|反义词：theoretical, passive, uninvolved|词性:形容词
