balloting:/ˈbælətɪŋ/
n.
投票；选举
the process of voting, typically in writing and secretly.
The balloting for the new chairman took place yesterday. 昨天进行了新主席的投票选举。

The union members are balloting on whether to strike. 工会成员正在投票决定是否罢工。

近义词: voting, poll, election
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider concepts like "appointment," "dictatorship")
词性: noun
