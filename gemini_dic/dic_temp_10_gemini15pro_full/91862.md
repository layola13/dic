hyperinflate:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪt/| v. |过度膨胀；恶性通货膨胀|To inflate (a currency or an economy) excessively, causing a steep and continuing rise in prices.|The government's reckless spending policies hyperinflated the economy. 政府不计后果的支出政策导致经济恶性通货膨胀。|Example: The Weimar Republic experienced hyperinflation after World War I.  魏玛共和国在第一次世界大战后经历了恶性通货膨胀。

|Synonyms: inflate rapidly, inflate excessively, overinflate|Antonyms: deflate, stabilize, control inflation|verb
