amenable:/əˈmiːnəbl/ /əˈmenəbl/| adj. |顺从的，服从的；经得起检验的；有义务的| (of a person) open and responsive to suggestion; easily persuaded or controlled. (of a thing) capable of being tested by; answerable or accountable to. |例句：The children are generally amenable to discipline. 孩子们通常都服从纪律。

例句：The problem is amenable to solution. 这个问题是可以解决的。|近义词：responsive, receptive, compliant, agreeable, acquiescent, persuadable, susceptible, tractable |反义词：unresponsive, resistant, intractable, unwilling, uncooperative |词性：形容词

词源: late Middle English: from Old French, from amener ‘bring to’, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + mener ‘lead’ (from Latin minare ‘drive animals’, from minari ‘threaten’). The original sense was ‘liable to be brought to account, answerable’, later ‘ready to submit or yield’.
