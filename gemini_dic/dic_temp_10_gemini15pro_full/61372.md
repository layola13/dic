embitterers:/ɪmˈbɪtərərz/ | n. | 使痛苦的人；使怨恨的人 |  People or things that cause someone to feel bitter or resentful. | The constant criticism from his colleagues were the embitterers that ultimately led to his resignation. 他同事不断的批评是使他最终辞职的痛苦之源。 | The war and its aftermath were powerful embitterers for an entire generation. 战争及其后果使整整一代人痛苦不堪。 |  近义词: aggravators, vexers, annoyers | 反义词: soothers, pacifiers, comforters | Noun
