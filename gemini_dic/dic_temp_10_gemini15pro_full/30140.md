ceiling:/ˈsiːlɪŋ/| n.|天花板，上限|The ceiling fan is spinning. 吊扇正在旋转。|The government imposed a price ceiling on gasoline. 政府对汽油设定了价格上限。|近义词：roof, upper limit|反义词:floor, lower limit|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *celier "to panel, wainscot," from Latin caelum "sky, heaven."  The sense shifted from "lining of a room" to the overhead surface.
