bookkeeper:/ˈbʊkkiːpər/| n.|簿记员，记账员|A person whose job is to keep records of the financial affairs of a business.|例句：The bookkeeper meticulously recorded every transaction. 簿记员一丝不苟地记录了每一笔交易。

例句：She worked as a bookkeeper for a small firm. 她在一家小公司担任簿记员。|近义词：accountant, clerk|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "manager" in some contexts, as they oversee but don't typically perform bookkeeping themselves)|词性:noun
