charbroil: /ˈtʃɑːrbrɔɪl/ | vt. n. | 烤焦，炭烤，(炭烤的)食物 | To cook food by broiling it over hot coals or a gas or electric burner designed to simulate the heat of coals.  The food is cooked by direct heat from above or below. |

例句：
I charbroiled the steaks for dinner.
我炭烤了牛排作为晚餐。

He ordered a charbroiled chicken sandwich.
他点了一个炭烤鸡肉三明治。

近义词：grill, barbecue, broil | 反义词：boil, stew, poach | 词性：动词，名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From char + broil. Char meaning "to burn or scorch the surface of something" and broil meaning "to cook by exposure to radiant heat."
