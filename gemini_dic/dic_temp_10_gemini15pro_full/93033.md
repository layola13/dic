idlehood:/ˈaɪdlˌhʊd/ | n. | 懒散状态；无所事事；闲散时光 |A state of inactivity or leisure. | She enjoyed a brief idlehood after finishing her exams. 她考试结束后享受了一段短暂的闲暇时光。 | He spent his idlehood reading and relaxing. 他把空闲时间都用来读书和放松。 | 近义词: idleness, inactivity, leisure | 反义词: activity, busyness, work | 词性: 名词


词源:
From idle + -hood.  "Idle" comes from Old English "idel," meaning "empty, vain, useless," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*ithalaz," meaning "empty." "-hood" is a suffix denoting a state or condition.
