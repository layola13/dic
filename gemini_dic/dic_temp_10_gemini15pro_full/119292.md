micro-environment:/ˌmaɪkroʊ ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/| n.|微环境; 微观环境|The micro-environment refers to the factors close to a company that affect its ability to serve its customers. 微环境是指靠近公司并影响其服务客户能力的因素。|例句：The company's marketing team analyzed the micro-environment to understand consumer behavior. 该公司的营销团队分析了微环境以了解消费者行为。

micro-environment:/ˌmaɪkroʊ ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/| n.|微环境; 微观环境|Changes in the micro-environment, such as new competitors, can significantly impact a business. 微环境的变化，例如新的竞争对手，会对企业产生重大影响。|近义词：immediate environment, operating environment, business environment|反义词:macro-environment|词性:名词
