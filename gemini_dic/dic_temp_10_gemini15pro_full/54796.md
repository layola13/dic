dispensatory:/dɪˈspɛnsətɔːri/ | /dɪˈspɛnsəˌtɔri/ | n. | 药典；配方书；（尤指医院的）配药处 | A book that lists medicinal drugs and how to prepare and use them, or a place where medicines are prepared and given out, especially in a hospital. |  例句：The pharmacist consulted the dispensatory to check the correct dosage. 药剂师查阅了药典以核对正确的剂量。


例句：The hospital's dispensatory was busy filling prescriptions. 医院的配药处正忙着配药。


近义词：pharmacopeia, formulary | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
