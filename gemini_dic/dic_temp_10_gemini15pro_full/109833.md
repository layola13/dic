lexicography:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 词典编纂学 | The practice or profession of compiling dictionaries. |  例句：Lexicography requires a deep understanding of language. 词典编纂学需要对语言的深刻理解。

例句：She dedicated her life to lexicography, producing several acclaimed dictionaries. 她毕生致力于词典编纂，出版了几部广受赞誉的词典。 | 近义词：dictionary-making | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lexicographie*, from Greek *lexikographikos* “pertaining to dictionary-making,” from *lexikon* "dictionary" + *graphein* “to write.”
