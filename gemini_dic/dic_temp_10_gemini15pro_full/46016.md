cyberpunk:/ˈsaɪbərˌpʌŋk/| n. adj.|网络朋克|A subgenre of science fiction that features a dystopian future characterized by advanced technology and a breakdown of social order.|The movie "Blade Runner" is a classic example of cyberpunk. 电影《银翼杀手》是网络朋克的经典例子。| Cyberpunk aesthetics often include neon lights and gritty urban landscapes. 网络朋克美学通常包括霓虹灯和粗犷的城市景观。|近义词: dystopian, futuristic|反义词: utopian|词性:名词,形容词

Etymology:
cybernetics + punk  (1980s)
From *cybernetics*, the science of communication and control systems in both machines and living things, and *punk*, a rebellious or nonconformist person, especially a young person, typically characterized by brightly colored hair and clothing and an aggressive or anarchic attitude.
