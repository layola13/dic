dyeing:/ˈdaɪɪŋ/| n. |染色，染料|The process of adding color to fabric or other materials. |例句：The dyeing process took several hours. 染色过程花了几个小时。

例句：Natural dyeing uses plant-based pigments.  天然染色使用植物色素。|近义词：coloring, tinting |反义词:bleaching|noun

dyeing:/ˈdaɪɪŋ/| adj. |垂死的，临终的|Being in the process of dying.  This meaning is less common and usually refers to a fading action, not a person's death.|例句：The dyeing embers glowed faintly in the fireplace.  将熄的余烬在壁炉里发出微弱的光芒。

例句： We watched the dyeing light of the setting sun. 我们看着落日余晖逐渐消失。|近义词：fading, waning|反义词:brightening, strengthening|adjective
