capitulate: /kəˈpɪtʃʊleɪt/ | vi. | 投降，屈服；停止抵抗 | To cease resisting : surrender often after negotiation of terms  |  The rebels were forced to capitulate after a long siege.  叛军在长期围困后被迫投降。| synonyms: surrender, yield, submit, give in | antonyms: resist, fight, oppose | verb

capitulate: /kəˈpɪtʃʊleɪt/ | vi. | (对论点或要求)让步，妥协 | To acquiesce or give in  | After a long argument, he finally capitulated and agreed to her demands.  经过长时间的争论，他最终妥协并同意了她的要求。 | synonyms: concede, acquiesce, comply | antonyms: refuse, reject, deny | verb
