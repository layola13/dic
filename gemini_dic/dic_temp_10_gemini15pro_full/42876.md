courtlier:/ˈkɔːrtliər/ /ˈkɔːrtiər/ | adj. |更有礼貌的，更殷勤的 |More polite and gracious than someone or something else; comparative form of 'courtly'.| He gave a courtlier bow than his brother. 他比他兄弟鞠躬更有礼貌。| The hostess offered a courtlier welcome this time.  这次女主人提供了更殷勤的欢迎。| 近义词: more polite, more gracious, more courteous, more civil | 反义词: ruder, less polite, more impolite, less courteous | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *cortelyer*, comparative of *cortely* (“courtly”), from Old French *corteis* (“courteous”), from *cort* (“court”). Ultimately from Latin *cohors* (“courtyard, enclosure”).
