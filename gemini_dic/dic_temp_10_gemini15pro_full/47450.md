deafnesses:/ˈdɛfnəsəz/ | n. | 聋，失聪（复数） | The state of being unable to hear, in varying degrees, in one or both ears.  Multiple instances of the condition of deafness. |  Many older adults experience varying degrees of deafnesses. 许多老年人经历不同程度的失聪。| The clinic specializes in treating childhood deafnesses. 这家诊所专门治疗儿童失聪。 | synonyms: hearing losses, hearing impairments | antonyms: hearing, auditory acuity | noun
