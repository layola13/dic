fantasizers:/ˈfæntəˌsaɪzərz/
n.
幻想者，空想家
People who indulge in fantasies; daydreamers.
She's one of those fantasizers who spends all day imagining she's a princess. 她就是那种整天幻想自己是公主的空想家。
The fantasizers were lost in their own worlds. 这些幻想家沉浸在他们自己的世界里。
dreamers, visionaries, idealists
realists, pragmatists
noun
