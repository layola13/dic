anaesthetist:/ənˈiːsθətɪst/
n.
麻醉师
a medical specialist who administers anesthetics.
The anaesthetist monitored the patient's vital signs during the surgery. 麻醉师在手术期间监测病人的生命体征。
The anaesthetist explained the procedure and the risks involved. 麻醉师解释了手术过程和相关风险。

反义词: (No direct antonym)
