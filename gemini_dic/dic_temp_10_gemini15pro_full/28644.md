caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ (US), /ˈkærɪbuː/ (UK)| n. |驯鹿|A large deer of the northern regions of North America, Europe, and Asia, with large antlers in both sexes.  It is closely related to the reindeer of Eurasia.|例句：The caribou migrated south for the winter. 驯鹿在冬天向南迁徙。

例句：We saw a herd of caribou crossing the frozen river. 我们看到一群驯鹿穿过结冰的河。|近义词：reindeer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
