decomposing:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ | adj. | 分解的，腐烂的 |  breaking down or rotting; decaying.  |  The decomposing leaves enriched the soil.  分解的树叶使土壤肥沃。 | synonyms: decaying, rotting, putrefying, disintegrating | antonyms:  growing, developing, flourishing | adjective

decompose:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊz/ | v. | 分解，腐烂 | to break down or rot; to decay. |  The body had started to decompose. 尸体已经开始腐烂。 | synonyms: decay, rot, putrefy, disintegrate | antonyms:  grow, develop, flourish | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *decomposĭtus, past participle of decomposĭtus, from Latin de- "down, away" + componere "to put together".  Meaning "to separate into constituent elements" is from 1640s.
