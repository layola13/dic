dustinesses:/ˈdʌstɪnəsəz/
n.
灰尘,尘土飞扬的状态
The state of being dusty; the presence of much dust.
The dustinesses of the attic made me sneeze.  阁楼上的灰尘让我打喷嚏。
The library suffered from dustinesses due to lack of cleaning.  由于缺乏清洁，图书馆里满是灰尘。
Synonyms: dust, dirtiness, grittiness
Antonyms: cleanliness, tidiness
