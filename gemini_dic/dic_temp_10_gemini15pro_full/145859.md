override: /ˌōvərˈrīd/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈraɪd/ (UK)| vt. n.| 覆盖，推翻，否决，超驰 | To use one's authority to reject or cancel (a decision, view, etc.).  As a noun, it refers to the act of overriding. |  The CEO overrode the board's decision. 首席执行官否决了董事会的决定。 | synonyms: overrule, overturn, supersede, cancel, nullify | antonyms: uphold, sustain, confirm, approve, endorse | Verb
