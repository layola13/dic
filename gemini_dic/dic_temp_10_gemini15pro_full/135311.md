nonpathological:/ˌnɒnpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnpæθəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非病理性的；正常的
Not caused by disease; not indicative of disease.
Her forgetfulness is nonpathological, simply a result of getting older. 她的健忘不是病理性的，只是年龄增长的结果。
Synonyms: normal, healthy, typical, natural, physiological
Antonyms: pathological, diseased, abnormal, morbid
词性: adjective
