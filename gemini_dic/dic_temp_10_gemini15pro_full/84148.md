half-offended:/ˌhæf əˈfɛndɪd/
| adj. |
| 半生气；略有不悦；有点被冒犯 |
| Feeling slightly offended or insulted, but not deeply so. It implies a mild reaction to something perceived as a slight or disrespect. |
| He gave a half-offended, half-amused snort. 他哼了一声，一半生气，一半觉得好笑。 |
| slightly offended, mildly insulted, a little hurt, miffed, piqued |
| delighted, pleased, flattered, unoffended |
| adjective |
