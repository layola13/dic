endoscopic:/ˌɛndəˈskɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 内窥镜的；内窥镜检查的 | Relating to or performed by means of an endoscope. |  The doctor performed an endoscopic procedure to examine the patient's stomach. 医生进行了内窥镜检查以检查病人的胃。| Near synonyms: minimally invasive, laparoscopic | Antonym: open (surgery) | Adjective

endoscopy:/ɛnˈdɒskəpi/ | n. | 内窥镜检查 | Visual examination of the interior of a body cavity or hollow organ using an endoscope. | The patient underwent an endoscopy to investigate the cause of his abdominal pain. 病人接受了内窥镜检查以调查腹痛的原因。| Near synonyms: laparoscopy,  minimally invasive surgery | Antonym: open surgery | Noun
