nonlubricants:/ˌnɒnˈluːbrɪkənts/ | n. | 非润滑剂 | Substances that are not designed to reduce friction between moving parts.  They might be used for other purposes like sealing, cleaning, or cooling. |  The kit contains cleaning solutions and other nonlubricants.  该套件包含清洁溶液和其他非润滑剂。| synonyms: non-lubricating materials, dry lubricants (in some contexts) | antonyms: lubricants, oils, greases | noun
