diabetes insipidus: /ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ɪnˈsɪpɪdəs/ | n. | 尿崩症 | A disorder characterized by the inability of the kidneys to conserve water, resulting in excessive thirst and urination. | The patient's diabetes insipidus was managed with medication. 病人的尿崩症通过药物治疗得到控制。| 近义词: None | 反义词: None | 词性: noun



Etymology:  "diabetes" from Late Latin, from Greek *diabētēs "a siphon," from diabainen "to pass through," from dia- "through" + bainein "to go." "insipidus" from Latin insipidus "tasteless," from in- "not" + sapidus "tasty," from sapere "to taste."
