nonsaccharins:/ˌnɑːnˈsækərɪnz/ |n.| 非糖类甜味剂 | Artificial sweeteners that are not saccharins. These are substances that taste sweet but do not contain sugar or saccharin. |  Many people use nonsaccharins in their coffee or tea to reduce sugar intake. 许多人用非糖类甜味剂代替咖啡或茶里的糖，以减少糖的摄入。| synonyms: artificial sweeteners, sugar substitutes | antonyms: sugar, saccharin | noun
