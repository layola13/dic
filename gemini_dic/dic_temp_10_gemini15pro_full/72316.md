flourless:/ˈflaʊərlɪs/| adj.|无面粉的；不含面粉的|Made without flour; containing no flour.|She baked a delicious flourless chocolate cake. 她烤了一个美味的无面粉巧克力蛋糕。|Example: This recipe is for a flourless cake, perfect for those with gluten intolerance.  这个食谱是做无面粉蛋糕的，非常适合那些麸质不耐受的人。|Synonyms: gluten-free, wheat-free|Antonyms: flour-based|word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From flour + -less.  "Flour" comes from Old French "flor," meaning "flower, blossom, best part," which itself comes from Latin "flos."  The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix indicating "without" or "lacking."
