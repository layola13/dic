flashboard:/ˈflæʃbɔːrd/| n. |闪光板; 防汛板; 活动溢洪道|A board or series of boards placed on the top of a dam or spillway to increase the depth of water.  Used to regulate the flow of water. |The flashboards on the dam were raised in anticipation of the heavy rain.  防汛板在大雨来临前被升高了。|

The dam operators lowered the flashboards to release some of the excess water. 水坝操作员降低了防汛板以释放一些多余的水。|Synonyms: spillway crest gate, stop logs|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "open channel," "unobstructed flow")|noun
