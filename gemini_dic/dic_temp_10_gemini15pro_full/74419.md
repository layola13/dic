franchisements:/ˈfræn.tʃaɪz.mənts/ | n. |特许经营权（复数）; 特许经营协议（复数）| The act of franchising or the state of being franchised.  The rights granted to a franchisee. |  The company expanded rapidly through franchisements.  该公司通过特许经营迅速扩张。| 近义词：franchises, concessions, licenses | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源:  From Old French "franchisement,"  from "franchir" (to free), from Frankish "*frankōn" (free, not a slave).
