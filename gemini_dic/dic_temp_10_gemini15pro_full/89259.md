hominoid:/ˈhɒmɪnɔɪd/ | n. adj. |类人猿，类人猿的|A primate of the superfamily Hominoidea, including humans, great apes, and gibbons.  |例句：Chimpanzees and gorillas are both hominoids. 黑猩猩和大猩猩都是类人猿。|近义词：ape, primate|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-primate) | noun, adjective
