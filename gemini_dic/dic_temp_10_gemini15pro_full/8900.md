antigenically:/ˌæn.tɪˈdʒen.ɪ.kəl.i/
| adv. |抗原性地|In a way that relates to antigens or their properties.  Relating to the properties of antigens, especially their ability to stimulate antibody production.|The virus has evolved antigenically, making previous vaccines less effective. 病毒在抗原性方面发生了进化，使得以前的疫苗效果降低。|
Synonyms: immunologically
| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using phrases like "non-immunologically")
|词性:副词
