agnostic: /æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | n. adj. |不可知论者，不可知论的 | A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God.  Relating to agnosticism. |  He described himself as an agnostic. 他自称是一个不可知论者。|  She has an agnostic attitude towards the existence of God. 她对上帝的存在持不可知论的态度。 | Synonyms: doubter, skeptic, unbeliever | Antonyms: believer, devotee, faithful | noun, adjective
