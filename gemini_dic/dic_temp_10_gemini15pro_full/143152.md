outchatter:/ˌaʊtˈtʃætər/
verb
胜过…的讲话；比…说得更多
To talk longer or more loudly than someone else.
He outchattered her easily, dominating the conversation. 他很容易就比她讲得更多，主导了谈话。
outtalk, outspeak
be outchattered by

verb
通过聊天胜过
To defeat or surpass someone by chattering.
She outchattered her opponent in the debate, leaving them speechless.  她在辩论中滔滔不绝地胜过了对手，让他们哑口无言。
outwit, outmaneuver
be outchattered by


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + chatter.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding.
