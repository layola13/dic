oscitantly:/ˈɒsɪtəntli/ | adv. | 打哈欠地，懒洋洋地，无精打采地 |  In a manner characterized by yawning, drowsiness, or lack of interest; listlessly. | He listened oscitantly to the lecture, barely able to keep his eyes open. 他懒洋洋地听着讲座，几乎睁不开眼睛。|  drowsily, sleepily, lethargically, listlessly, apathetically | attentively, alertly, eagerly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oscitant- (stem of oscitans), present participle of oscitare “to yawn,” frequentative of os “mouth” (from PIE root *os- “mouth”).  The -ly suffix transforms the adjective "oscitant" into the adverb "oscitantly."
