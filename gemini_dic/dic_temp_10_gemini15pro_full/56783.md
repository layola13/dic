doppelganger:/ˈdɒpəlˌɡæŋə(r)/ (UK), /ˈdɑːpəlˌɡæŋər/ (US)| n. |分身，幽灵，二重身|An apparition or double of a living person.|I swear I saw my doppelganger on the street yesterday. 我发誓我昨天在街上看到了我的分身。|近义词:double, look-alike, twin, counterpart|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

doppelgangers:/ˈdɒpəlˌɡæŋəz/ (UK), /ˈdɑːpəlˌɡæŋərz/ (US) | n. |分身们，幽灵们，二重身们|The plural form of doppelganger, referring to multiple apparitions or doubles of living persons.|Many people claim to have seen doppelgangers of celebrities. 很多人声称见过名人的分身。|近义词:doubles, look-alikes, twins, counterparts|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From German *Doppelgänger*, literally "double-goer," from *doppel* "double" + *Gänger* "goer," from *gehen* "to go."
