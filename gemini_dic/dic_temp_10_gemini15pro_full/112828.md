luciferous:/luːˈsɪfərəs/ | adj. | 光亮的，带来光的；恶魔的，邪恶的 | Bringing light; pertaining to Lucifer, the fallen angel, often associated with evil. | The luciferous glow of the moon illuminated the path. 月亮的光芒照亮了道路。 | The luciferous cult leader attracted many followers with his charismatic speeches.  邪恶的邪教头目用他富有魅力的演讲吸引了许多追随者。 | 近义词: luminous, radiant, bright; satanic, diabolic | 反义词: dark, dim, obscure; angelic, holy | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *lucifer "light-bringing," from lux "light" + -fer "bearing."  Lucifer is also the name of the planet Venus when appearing as the morning star, and was later associated with the fallen angel Satan.
