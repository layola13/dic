colligate:/ˈkɒlɪɡeɪt/ /ˈkɑːlɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 整理，收集，汇集 | To gather, assemble, or arrange into a systematic whole; to bind or unite. |  The researcher colligated data from various sources for the study. 研究人员从各种来源收集数据用于研究。

The editor colligated the different chapters into a coherent book. 编辑将不同的章节整理成一本连贯的书籍。 | 近义词: collect, gather, assemble, compile, amass | 反义词: disperse, scatter, separate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin "colligatus," past participle of "colligare," from "com-" (together) + "ligare" (to bind).
