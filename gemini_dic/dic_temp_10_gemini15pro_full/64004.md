epistolographic:/ɪˌpɪstəˈlɒɡræfɪk/ /ɪˌpɪstəˈlɑːɡræfɪk/
adjective
书信体的；书信形式的
Relating to the writing of letters; contained in or suitable to letters.
Example: His epistolographic style was reminiscent of 18th-century letter writers. 他的书信体风格让人想起18世纪的书信作家。

Example: The novel is written in an epistolographic format, composed entirely of letters exchanged between the characters. 这部小说是以书信体写成的，完全由人物之间交换的信件组成。

Synonym:  epistolary
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider "narrative," "conversational," or "dramatic" as contrasting styles)
词性: adjective
