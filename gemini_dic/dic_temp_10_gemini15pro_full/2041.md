aculeous: /əˈkjuːliəs/
adjective
多刺的；针状的
Prickly; pointed; needle-like.  Having a sharp point; stinging.
The aculeous thorns of the rose bush snagged my clothes. 玫瑰丛中尖锐的刺钩住了我的衣服。
The cactus is an aculeous plant. 仙人掌是一种多刺的植物。
synonyms: prickly, thorny, spiny, barbed, pointed, sharp
antonyms: smooth, blunt, soft
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aculeolus, diminutive of aculeus (“sting, prick, thorn”), from acus (“needle”).

USAGE->
The aculeous leaves of the holly tree can be quite painful. 冬青树多刺的叶子会很痛。

The porcupine's aculeous quills protect it from predators. 豪猪锋利的刺保护它免受捕食者的侵害。
