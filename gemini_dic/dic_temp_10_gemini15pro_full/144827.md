over-lauded:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːdɪd/| adj.| 过誉的，吹捧过度的| Excessively praised; praised more than is deserved.  The movie was over-lauded by critics, but audiences found it rather dull. 这部电影被评论家过度吹捧，但观众觉得它相当沉闷。|Synonyms: overpraised, overrated, overhyped|Antonyms: underrated, undervalued, underappreciated|Adjective
