calculatingly:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪŋli/
adverb
精明地；有算计地；工于心计地
In a way that is carefully planned in order to get an advantage.
She smiled calculatingly. 她不怀好意地笑了笑。
近义词:deliberately, shrewdly, craftily, artfully
反义词:naively, innocently, artlessly
词性:adverb

词源: From calculate + -ing + -ly.  "Calculate" comes from Latin "calculus" meaning "pebble" (used in counting).
