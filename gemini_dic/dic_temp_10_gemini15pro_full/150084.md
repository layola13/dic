paltry:/ˈpɔːltri/ (US), /ˈpɔːltri/ (UK)| adj.|微不足道的，无价值的，少量的|Small or meager; insignificant; worthless.|例句：He received a paltry sum of money for his work.  他工作得到的报酬少得可怜。

例句：She offered a paltry excuse for her behavior. 她为自己的行为找了个蹩脚的借口。

近义词：meager, insignificant, trifling, negligible, petty, measly|反义词：substantial, significant, considerable, ample, generous|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *palteri (“rags, tattered clothes”), from pel (“skin, hide”) (akin to Latin pellis (“skin”)). Originally referred to ragged or worthless clothing, hence the modern meaning.
