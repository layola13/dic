cadencing:/ˈkeɪdənsɪŋ/
n. |节奏安排，节奏变化|The cadencing of the music was complex and intriguing.  这首音乐的节奏安排复杂而引人入胜。|
|cadencing is the act of creating or imposing a rhythmic pattern, especially in music or poetry.  节奏安排是指创造或施加一种节奏模式的行为，尤其是在音乐或诗歌中。|
The poet's careful cadencing of the lines created a hypnotic effect. 诗人对诗句节奏的精心安排创造了一种催眠效果。
|rhythm, tempo, pacing|monotony, uniformity|noun
