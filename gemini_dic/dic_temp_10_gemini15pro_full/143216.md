outcull: /ˈaʊtkʌl/ | v. |剔除，淘汰| To remove or select something, often of inferior quality, from a larger group. | The farmer outculled the weakest lambs from the flock.  农民从羊群中剔除了最弱的小羊。 | Example: They outculled the damaged fruit before sending the shipment. 他们剔除了坏掉的水果才发货。 | Synonyms: cull, weed out, eliminate, reject | Antonyms: select, choose, keep, retain | verb

outcull: /ˈaʊtkʌl/ | n. |剔除物，淘汰物|  The items or individuals that have been removed or selected out from a larger group. | The outcull from the apple harvest was used to make cider. 苹果收获后的剔除物被用来制作苹果酒。| Example: The outcull of the herd consisted of the older, less productive cows. 牛群的剔除物包括了较老的、产奶量较低的奶牛。| Synonyms: rejects, culls | Antonyms: selections, choices | noun
