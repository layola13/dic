bullcomber:/ˈbʊlkəʊmər/ | n. | 海滩拾荒者；海滩流浪汉 | A person who scavenges along beaches, especially for useful or salvageable items. | The bullcomber found a beautiful piece of driftwood on the beach.  海滩拾荒者在海滩上发现了一块美丽的浮木。 | Example: He lived as a bullcomber, surviving off what he could find washed ashore. 他像个海滩流浪汉一样生活，靠在岸边找到的东西生存。 | Synonyms: beachcomber, scavenger, forager | Antonyms:  merchant, vendor | Noun
