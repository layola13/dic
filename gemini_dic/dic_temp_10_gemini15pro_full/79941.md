gnoscopine:/nɒˈskɒpiːn/ | n. | 诺司卡品 |A non-narcotic antitussive drug derived from opium. It suppresses coughs without causing the addictive effects of other opiates. | Example: The doctor prescribed gnoscopine for her persistent cough. 医生给她开了诺司卡品来治疗她持续的咳嗽。| Example: Gnoscopine is considered a safer alternative to codeine for cough suppression.  诺司卡品被认为是可待因的一种更安全的镇咳替代品。| Synonym: dextromethorphan, levopropoxyphene | Antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym as it's a specific drug) | noun
