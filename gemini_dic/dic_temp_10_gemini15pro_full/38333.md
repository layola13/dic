conceit:/kənˈsiːt/| n.|自负；巧思；奇想|An excessive pride in oneself or one's abilities; an ingenious or fanciful notion or expression.|He was puffed up with conceit. 他自负得不得了。|Synonyms: vanity, arrogance, pride, egotism|Antonyms: humility, modesty|noun

conceit:/kənˈsiːt/| n.|比喻；巧妙的比方|A fanciful, particularly clever extended metaphor.|The poem is full of conceits. 这首诗充满了巧妙的比喻。|Synonyms: metaphor, simile, analogy|Antonyms: literalism|noun
