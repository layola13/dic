nominals:/ˈnɒmɪnəlz/ | n. | 名词性词; 名词词组 |Words or groups of words that function as nouns.  These can include nouns, pronouns, noun phrases, and nominal clauses. | The nominals in the sentence "The big red ball bounced high" are "The big red ball" and "high".  这个句子“The big red ball bounced high”中的名词性词是“The big red ball”和“high”。| synonyms: noun phrases, substantives | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider verbs, adjectives, adverbs) | noun
