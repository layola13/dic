fillip:/ˈfɪlɪp/| n. vt.|刺激，激励；弹指；少量烈酒|Something that acts as a stimulus or boost to an activity or interest.  A snap of the finger and thumb. A small amount of liquor.|The news provided a much-needed fillip to the flagging economy. 这个消息为疲软的经济注入了急需的活力。|Synonyms: boost, stimulus, impetus, incentive, encouragement|Antonyms: discouragement, deterrent, hindrance|noun

fillip:/ˈfɪlɪp/| vt.|轻弹；激励|To strike with a fillip; to stimulate.|He filliped the top of the bottle. 他轻弹了一下瓶盖。|Synonyms: flick, snap, stimulate, encourage|Antonyms: discourage, suppress|verb
