heterogeny:/ˌhɛtərˈɒdʒɪni/ | n. |异质性，异源性 |The condition or state of being heterogenous; dissimilarity or diversity of structure or composition.| The heterogeny of the population makes it a vibrant and interesting place to live.  人口的异质性使其成为一个充满活力和有趣的生活场所。| Near synonyms: heterogeneity, diversity, variety, difference, dissimilarity | Near antonyms: homogeneity, uniformity, similarity, sameness | Noun

heterogeneous:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |异质的，异种的，由不同成分组成的 |Diverse in character or content.| The city is a heterogeneous mix of cultures and backgrounds. 这座城市是文化和背景的异质混合体。|Near synonyms: diverse, varied, assorted, mixed, miscellaneous | Near antonyms: homogeneous, uniform, similar, alike | Adjective
