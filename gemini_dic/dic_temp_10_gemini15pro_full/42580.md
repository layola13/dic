counterplays:/ˈkaʊntərˌpleɪz/
noun
对策，反制措施
actions taken to counteract or offset another action or policy.
The coach devised several counterplays to neutralize the opponent's aggressive offense. 教练设计了几种对策来抵消对手的猛烈进攻。
synonyms: countermeasures, counterstrategies, countertactics
antonyms: strategies, tactics
