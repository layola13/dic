commonhold:/ˈkɒmənˌhəʊld/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənˌhoʊld/ (US)| n.|共有产权|A system of property ownership in which each flat owner owns their individual unit outright and also jointly owns the freehold of the building and common areas with other flat owners.|例句：The new development offers apartments under a commonhold scheme.  这个新开发项目提供共有产权方案下的公寓。

例句：Commonhold allows flat owners more control over the management of their building. 共有产权让公寓业主对建筑物的管理有更多控制权。|近义词：leasehold (in some aspects), strata title (Australia), condominium (US)|反义词:freehold|词性:名词
