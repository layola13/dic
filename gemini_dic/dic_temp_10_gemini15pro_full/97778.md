injoin:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪn/ | v. | 命令；禁止；吩咐 | To direct or order with authority; to prohibit or forbid. | The court enjoined the company from selling the product. 法院禁止该公司销售该产品。 |  The king enjoined his knights to be brave. 国王命令他的骑士要勇敢。 |  Near synonyms: command, order, direct, bid, prohibit, forbid | Near antonyms: allow, permit | verb
