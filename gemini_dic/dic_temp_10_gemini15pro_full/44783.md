crunchable:/ˈkrʌntʃəbl/| adj.|易碎的，松脆的|Something that is crunchable is firm and makes a loud noise when you bite it.  It suggests a pleasant texture and sound associated with eating.|The chips were perfectly crunchable.  薯片非常酥脆。|近义词：crispy, crunchy|反义词:chewy, soggy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From crunch + -able.  Crunch itself is likely imitative of the sound.  “-able” is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."
