catechin:/ˈkætɪtʃɪn/| n.|儿茶素|A type of plant compound with antioxidant properties found in foods like green tea, dark chocolate, and berries.  It is a flavan-3-ol, a subclass of flavonoids.|Green tea is a rich source of catechins. 绿茶富含儿茶素。|Catechins are believed to have health benefits. 儿茶素被认为对健康有益。|Synonyms: flavanol, polyphenol|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps pro-oxidant)|noun
