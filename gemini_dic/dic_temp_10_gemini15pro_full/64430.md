erg:/ɜːrɡ/ | n. | 尔格（功的单位）| A unit of work or energy equal to one dyne acting through a distance of one centimeter. | The physicist calculated the energy in ergs. 物理学家以尔格计算能量。| Example: The energy required to lift a feather a few centimeters is only a few ergs. 将一根羽毛举起几厘米只需要几尔格的能量。 | Synonym: joule (though not a direct equivalent, joule is the more common SI unit of energy) | Antonym: N/A | noun
