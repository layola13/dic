neurotically:/nʊəˈrɒtɪkli/ /nʊˈrɑːtɪkli/| adv.|神经质地，神经兮兮地|In a neurotic way, characterized by excessive anxiety, obsessive thoughts, or compulsive behaviors.  Exhibiting or characteristic of neurosis.|She washes her hands neurotically. 她神经质地洗手。|Synonyms: obsessively, compulsively, anxiously, nervously|Antonyms: calmly, rationally, reasonably|Word Type: Adverb


Etymology:

From neurotic + -ally.  Neurotic comes from Greek *neuron* (nerve) + -otic (suffix indicating a diseased condition).
