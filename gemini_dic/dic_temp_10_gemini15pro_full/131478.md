noncoincidences:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɪnsɪdənsiz/
n.
不一致，不重合，不符合
lack of agreement or correspondence
The noncoincidence of the two lines caused a problem in the design.  这两条线的不重合导致了设计上的问题。

The noncoincidence of their schedules made it difficult to meet.  他们的时间安排不一致，很难见面。

Synonyms:  discrepancies, disagreements, disparities, variances, divergences
Antonyms:  coincidences, agreements, correspondences, consistencies, harmonies
noun
