executively:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪvli/
| adv. | 执行地；作为行政人员；以管理身份 |  In a manner characteristic of or suitable for an executive.  | She dressed executively for the board meeting. 她穿着行政人员的服装出席董事会。| 近义词: administratively, managerially | 反义词: passively, subordinately | 词性: 副词
词源:  From executive + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin exsequī (“to follow out, carry out”).
