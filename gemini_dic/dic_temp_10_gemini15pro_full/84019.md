half-drowning:/ˌhɑːf ˈdraʊnɪŋ/ | n. | 半溺水；濒死体验 | The state of having nearly drowned, but surviving.  A person who has experienced half-drowning may suffer from lasting physical effects. |  He survived a half-drowning incident as a child, which left him with a fear of water. 他小时候经历了一次半溺水事件，这让他对水产生了恐惧。 | near-drowning, almost drowning | survival, resuscitation | noun
