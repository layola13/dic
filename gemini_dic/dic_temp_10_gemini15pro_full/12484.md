aspen's:/ˈæspənz/
noun
白杨树的；白杨木的
belonging to or associated with an aspen tree.
The aspen's leaves trembled in the breeze. 白杨树的叶子在微风中颤抖。
近义词:poplar's
反义词:N/A
词性:noun (possessive form)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aspe, from Old English æspe, from Proto-Germanic *aspo, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂es- (“tree”). Cognate with Scots aspe (“aspen”), West Frisian espe (“aspen”), Dutch esp (“aspen”), German Espe (“aspen”), Danish and Swedish asp (“aspen”).
