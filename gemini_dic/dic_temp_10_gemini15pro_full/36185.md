cofinances:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænsɪz/
verb
共同融资；共同出资
To provide funding for something jointly with another or others.
The World Bank cofinanced the project with several European governments.  世界银行与几个欧洲政府共同资助了这个项目。

The museum's expansion was cofinanced by private donations and government grants. 博物馆的扩建由私人捐款和政府拨款共同资助。

Synonyms: co-fund, joint-finance, share the cost of
Antonyms: solely fund, independently finance
词性: verb (动词)
