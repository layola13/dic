hilt:/hɪlt/| n.|剑柄，刀柄；(事情的)紧要关头|The handle of a sword or dagger, especially the part that protects the hand.  Figuratively, it can also mean a crucial or critical point.|He held the sword by the hilt. 他握住剑柄。|近义词：handle, grip, haft|反义词:blade, tip|noun


hilt:/hɪlt/| vt.|配上柄|To furnish with a hilt.|The swordsmith hilted the blade with polished wood.  刀匠用抛光木料给刀刃装上柄。|近义词：fit, equip, furnish|反义词: dismantle, disassemble |verb
