nonponderosity:/ˌnɒnpɒndəˈrɒsɪti/
n.|轻飘；无重状态|The nonponderosity of the helium balloon allowed it to float effortlessly.|氦气球的轻飘使它毫不费力地漂浮起来。|
例句：
The nonponderosity of the feathers allowed them to drift on the breeze.
羽毛的轻盈使它们能随风飘荡。

The magician created an illusion of nonponderosity, making the assistant appear to float in mid-air.
魔术师制造了一种失重的幻觉，让助手看起来像漂浮在半空中。

近义词：lightness, weightlessness, buoyancy, imponderability
反义词：heaviness, weight, ponderosity
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- + ponderosity.  Ponderosity comes from Late Latin *ponderositas, from Latin ponderosus (“weighty”), from pondus (“weight”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)pend- (“to pull, stretch, spin”).
