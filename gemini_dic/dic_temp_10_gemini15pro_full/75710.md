full-colour:/ˌfʊlˈkʌlə(r)/ |adj.|全彩色的，彩色的|Printed in full colour.  印刷成全彩色的。|例句：The magazine is printed in full-colour. 这本杂志是全彩印刷的。

例句：She wore a full-colour dress to the party. 她穿了一件彩色的连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词：multicoloured, polychromatic, colorful, colored (US)|反义词：monochrome, black and white|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
"full" + "colour"
"full" (adj.) Old English *full (West Saxon, Kentish ful, Mercian full), from Proto-Germanic *fullaz "full," from PIE root *pel- (2) "to fill."
"colour" (n.) Middle English colour, from Old French colour, from Latin color "color of the skin, complexion; hue, tint, dye, paint; appearance, character," of uncertain origin.
