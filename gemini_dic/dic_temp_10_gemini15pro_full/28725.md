ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *carnage, from Late Latin carnaticum "tax on meat," from Latin carnaticum "flesh," from caro (genitive carnis) "flesh."  The sense shifted from "tax on meat" to "slaughter of animals (for meat)" and then to "slaughter of people."
