cammer:/ˈkæmər/| n.|摄像头使用者，拍摄者|Someone who uses a camera, especially a webcam or a dashboard camera, to record and often share video footage.|例句：The cammer captured the entire incident on his dashcam. 这位摄像头使用者用他的行车记录仪拍下了整个事件。

例句：Many cammers upload their videos to social media platforms. 许多摄像头使用者将他们的视频上传到社交媒体平台。|近义词：videographer, recorder, filmmaker|反义词:viewer, audience|词性:noun
