letheed: /ləˈθiːd/ | adj. | 遗忘的，被遗忘的；冷漠的，无动于衷的 | Forgotten; oblivious; indifferent.  Characterized by a state of oblivion, as if under the influence of Lethe, the river of forgetfulness in Greek mythology.  Can also imply an apathetic or indifferent attitude. | The letheed masses seemed unaware of the impending crisis.  被遗忘的民众似乎没有意识到即将到来的危机。|  He gave a letheed shrug, showing no concern for the outcome. 他冷漠地耸了耸肩，对结果漠不关心。 | 近义词: forgotten, oblivious, unmindful, apathetic, indifferent | 反义词: mindful, aware, concerned, interested | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Lethe (Greek mythology) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).  Lethe was one of the five rivers of the underworld, causing forgetfulness in those who drank from it.
