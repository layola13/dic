overcleanly:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːnli/
| adv. | 过分干净地，洁癖地 | excessively clean; too concerned with cleanliness |  She washes her hands overcleanly, sometimes scrubbing them raw. 她洗手过分干净，有时会搓到皮肤破损。|  He keeps his apartment overcleanly, not a speck of dust is allowed. 他把公寓打扫得过分干净，一点灰尘都不允许存在。| 近义词: excessively clean, too cleanly | 反义词: uncleanly, messily | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "cleanly" (adverb form of "clean").  From Old English "clæne" (clean).
