cirrocumulus:/ˌsɪrəʊˈkjuːmjələs/ /ˌsɪroʊˈkjuːmjələs/| n.|卷积云|A type of cloud forming a thin white layer of small ripples.|例句：The cirrocumulus clouds looked like fish scales in the sky. 卷积云看起来像天空中的鱼鳞。

例句：The appearance of cirrocumulus clouds sometimes indicates an approaching weather front. 卷积云的出现有时预示着天气锋面的来临。|近义词：mackerel sky (非正式)|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑其他云的种类，例如 stratus 层云)|词性:noun
