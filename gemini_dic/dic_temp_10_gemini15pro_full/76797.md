gammadion:/ˈɡæmədiən/ | n. |万字符，卍字 | An ancient symbol in the form of a Greek cross with the arms bent at right angles, often used as a religious symbol in Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism, but also later adopted by the Nazi Party.  The term "gammadion" is generally preferred in academic contexts to avoid the negative connotations associated with the swastika. | The archaeologist discovered a gammadion carved into the ancient stone. 考古学家发现一块古老的石头上刻着一个万字符。| The museum displayed several artifacts featuring the gammadion, explaining its various historical and cultural meanings.  博物馆展出了几件带有万字符的文物，并解释了其不同的历史和文化意义。 | synonyms: swastika, fylfot | antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as it's a symbol. One might consider symbols of peace as conceptual opposites, e.g., the peace sign) | noun
