infectious:/ɪnˈfekʃəs/| adj.|传染的；有感染力的；易传染的|Likely to spread or influence others.|The flu is highly infectious. 流感传染性很强。|近义词：contagious, transmissible, catching|反义词：non-infectious, non-contagious|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin infectiōsus (“apt to infect”), from infectus, past participle of inficere (“to stain, dye, tinge, taint, infect”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon”) + facere (“to do, make”).
