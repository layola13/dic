memoirist:/ˈmɛmwɑːrɪst/ |n.|回忆录作者，传记作家|A person who writes memoirs, especially one who is known for doing so.|Example: The memoirist vividly captured the atmosphere of her childhood.  这位回忆录作者生动地描绘了她的童年氛围。

Example:  He became a celebrated memoirist after publishing his war experiences.  在发表了他的战争经历后，他成为了一位著名的回忆录作家。|Synonym: autobiographer, biographer, diarist, writer|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fiction writer")|Noun
