bobbed:/bɒbd/ (UK) /bɑːbd/ (US)| v. adj.|剪短，上下跳动|past tense and past participle of bob. To move up and down quickly and repeatedly. To cut (hair) short.|
She bobbed her hair short for the summer. 她为了夏天把头发剪短了。
The cork bobbed on the water.  软木塞在水面上上下跳动。
|近义词：cropped, trimmed (for hair), bounced, dipped (for movement)|反义词: grew (for hair), sank (for movement)|词性: verb (primarily), adjective (describing hair)|

bobbed:/bɒbd/ (UK) /bɑːbd/ (US)| adj.|剪短的|Having been cut short.|
She has a bobbed hairstyle. 她留着波波头。|近义词：short, cropped|反义词: long|词性: adjective|
