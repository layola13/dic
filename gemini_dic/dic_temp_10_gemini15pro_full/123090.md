mongers:/ˈmʌŋɡərz/
n.
贩子，商贩
a person who promotes a specified activity, situation, or feeling, especially one that is undesirable or discreditable.
Warmongers are those who advocate war.  战争贩子是那些鼓吹战争的人。
Synonyms:  traders, dealers, merchants, traffickers, vendors
Antonyms:  customers, buyers, consumers
词性: 名词
