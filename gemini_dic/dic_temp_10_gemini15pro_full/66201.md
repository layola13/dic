excisions:/ɪkˈsɪʒənz/
noun
切除；删除；删节
the act or process of removing something, especially by cutting
The surgery involved the excision of a small tumor.  这个手术包括切除一个小肿瘤。
近义词: removal, deletion, resection
反义词: addition, insertion, inclusion
词性: noun (plural)

excision:/ɪkˈsɪʒən/
noun
切除；删除；删节
the act or process of removing something, especially by cutting
The doctor recommended the excision of the mole. 医生建议切除这颗痣。
近义词: removal, deletion, resection
反义词: addition, insertion, inclusion
词性: noun (singular)
