manias:/ˈmeɪniəz/ | n. | 狂热，癖好 | An excessive enthusiasm or obsession for something.  A period of abnormally high spirits and activity. |  She has a mania for collecting shoes. 她有收集鞋子的癖好。|  The economic mania of the 1990s ended badly. 20世纪90年代的经济狂热以糟糕的结局告终。 | synonyms: obsessions, crazes, fixations | antonyms: apathy, indifference | noun
