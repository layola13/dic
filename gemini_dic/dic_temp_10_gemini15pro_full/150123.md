pampero:/pɑmˈpɛroʊ/ /pæmˈpɪəroʊ/| n. |南美草原的暴风，寒风|A cold, often violent wind, usually accompanied by rain or snow, that blows across the pampas of South America.|The pampero swept across the plains, bringing a sudden drop in temperature. 南美草原的寒风横扫平原，气温骤降。|
The ship was battered by the fierce pampero. 这艘船被猛烈的寒风袭击。|近义词：gale, squall, windstorm|反义词:calm, breeze|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *pampero*, from *pampa* ("plain"), from Quechua *pampa* ("plain").
