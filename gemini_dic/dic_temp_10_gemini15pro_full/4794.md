algific:/ælˈdʒɪfɪk/ | adj. |  产生寒冷的; 致冷的 | Producing or causing coldness; cooling. | The algific slopes of the mountain were covered in frost. 山上寒冷的斜坡上覆盖着霜冻。|  The algific cave maintained a constant cool temperature. 致冷的洞穴保持着恒定的低温。 | 近义词:  frigid, chilling, refrigerating | 反义词:  heating, warming | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *algificus, from algēre (“to be cold”) + -ficus (“-making”).
