nonrequisition:/ˌnɒnˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən/
n.
未经申请的物品; 非申请物品
An item not formally requested or requisitioned.
The warehouse was full of nonrequisitioned supplies. 仓库里堆满了未经申请的物资。
non-requested, unsolicited, unasked for
requisitioned, requested
noun
