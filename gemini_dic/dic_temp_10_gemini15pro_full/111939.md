lollies:/ˈlɒliz/ (UK), /ˈlɑːliz/ (US)| n.|棒棒糖；甜食|A lollipop, a piece of hard candy on a stick.  Also used more generally to refer to sweets or candy.|The children were given lollies after their dinner. 孩子们晚饭后得到了棒棒糖。|Synonyms: lollipops, sweets, candy, treats|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "healthy food" in a contextual sense)|Noun
