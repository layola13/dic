overplentiful:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪfʊl/
adjective
过剩的，过多的
Excessively plentiful; more than sufficient or required.
The harvest was overplentiful this year, leaving much of the produce to rot. 今年的收成过多，导致许多农产品腐烂。
The library has an overplentiful supply of books on the subject. 图书馆里关于这个主题的书籍过多。
synonym: abundant, excessive, superfluous, copious
antonym: scarce, insufficient, meager, deficient
词性: 形容词
