bratling: /ˈbræt.lɪŋ/ | n. | 小家伙，顽童，乳臭未干的小子 | A child, especially a mischievous or annoying one.  Often used affectionately or humorously. |  例句：The bratlings were running amok in the garden. 那些小家伙在花园里乱跑。

例句：Don't be such a bratling! 别这么淘气！| 近义词：child, kid, urchin, rascal, imp | 反义词: adult, grown-up | 词性: noun

bratling: 词源来自 brat + -ling (指小或者不重要的后缀). Brat 源自古英语 *bratt，含义不明，可能与“rag” (碎布) 有关，暗示衣衫褴褛的孩子。
