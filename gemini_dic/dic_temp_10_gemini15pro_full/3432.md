africanity:/ˌæfrɪˈkænɪti/
n.
非洲性，非洲特性
The quality or state of being African or of having African origins; African character or nature.
Her art celebrates Africanity in all its forms. 她的艺术歌颂了各种形式的非洲性。
近义词:Africanness, African character
反义词: (No clear antonym, possibly Westernization or Eurocentrism depending on context)
