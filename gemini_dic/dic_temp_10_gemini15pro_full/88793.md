hoedown:/ˈhoʊˌdaʊn/| n. |乡村舞会，欢快的聚会|A social gathering featuring traditional American folk music and dancing, especially square dancing.|例句：The town held a hoedown to celebrate the harvest.  小镇举行了一场乡村舞会来庆祝丰收。

例句：We're going to a hoedown this weekend.  我们这个周末要去参加一个乡村舞会。|近义词：party, celebration, shindig, get-together|反义词:|n.
