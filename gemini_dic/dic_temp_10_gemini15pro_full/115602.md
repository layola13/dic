marblings:/ˈmɑːrblɪŋz/
n.
大理石花纹，大理石纹理
the patterns or streaks of different colors like those in marble
The marblings in the cake were a beautiful swirl of chocolate and vanilla.  蛋糕上的大理石花纹是巧克力和香草的美丽漩涡。

近义词:  veining, streaks, patterns
反义词:  solid, plain


词性: noun


词源: From the verb "to marble" + "-ings" (suffix indicating a product or result of an action).  "Marble" itself comes from Old French "marbre," ultimately from Latin "marmor."
