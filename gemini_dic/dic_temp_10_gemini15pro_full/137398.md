non-tentative: /ˌnɒnˈtɛntətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛntətɪv/
adjective
非试验性的；非暂定的；确定的
Not tentative; definite and certain.
The CEO made a non-tentative decision to expand the company into new markets. 首席执行官做出了一个确定的决定，将公司扩展到新的市场。
The schedule is non-tentative and will not be changed.  时间表已确定，不会更改。
近义词: definite, certain, firm, conclusive, decided
反义词: tentative, provisional, uncertain, hesitant
