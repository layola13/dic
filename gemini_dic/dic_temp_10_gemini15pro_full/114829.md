mammal-like:/ˈmæməlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像哺乳动物的；类似哺乳动物的|Resembling or characteristic of a mammal.  This term often describes extinct animals that share characteristics with mammals but aren't classified as true mammals.|The Cynognathus was a mammal-like reptile that lived during the Triassic period. 犬颌兽是一种生活在三叠纪的类似哺乳动物的爬行动物。|Near synonyms: mammalian, therapsid-like|Antonyms: reptile-like, avian-like|adjective
