magnetization:/ˌmæɡnɪtɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|磁化；磁化强度；磁化作用|The process of making a substance magnetic or the state of being magnetic.  The magnetization of the iron bar created a strong magnetic field. |例句：The magnetization of the iron core increases the strength of the electromagnet. 铁芯的磁化增加了电磁铁的强度。

例句：The strength of the magnetization depends on the material and the applied magnetic field.  磁化强度取决于材料和外加磁场。|近义词：magnetic induction, magnetic polarization|反义词:demagnetization|词性:noun
