page-turner:/ˈpeɪdʒˌtɜːrnər/| n. |引人入胜的书（通常指小说）|A book that is so exciting or interesting that you want to keep reading it quickly.|The new thriller is a real page-turner; I couldn't put it down.|近义词：gripping, compelling, absorbing, riveting|反义词:boring, tedious, dull|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Compound of "page" and "turner".  Relatively modern, likely originating in the mid-20th century.  "Turner" in this context refers to the act of turning pages quickly due to the engaging nature of the book.
