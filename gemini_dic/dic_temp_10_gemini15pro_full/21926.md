bogan:/ˈboʊɡən/ (AU) /ˈbɒɡən/ (UK)| n. |粗俗的人，没教养的人|A person who is considered unrefined or unsophisticated, often associated with lower socioeconomic status, characterized by a particular style of clothing, music, and behavior.  Often used derogatorily.|He's such a bogan, always wearing flannies and listening to AC/DC.|近义词：redneck, chav, yob|反义词:sophisticate, cultured person|noun
