nonprejudicially:/ˌnɒnˌprɛdʒʊˈdɪʃəli/
adverb
无偏见地，不损害地
without causing harm or injury to any rights or claims; without bias
The court ruled nonprejudicially, allowing the case to be reopened later. 法院做出了不损害任何一方权利的裁决，允许此案日后重新审理。
The issue was resolved nonprejudicially to either party.  这个问题得到了解决，没有对任何一方造成损害。
近义词: impartially, fairly, neutrally, objectively
反义词: prejudicially, biasedly, unfairly

词性: adverb
词源:  "non-" (not) + "prejudicial" (harmful to someone's rights or claims) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Prejudicial" comes from Latin "praejudicium" (prejudgment).
