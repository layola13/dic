eudialyte:/juːˈdaɪəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 硅锆钛矿，霓石 |A reddish-brown mineral, a cyclosilicate of sodium, calcium, cerium, iron, manganese, zirconium, and other elements. | Eudialyte is a relatively rare mineral.  硅锆钛矿是一种相对稀有的矿物。| Eudialyte can be found in alkaline igneous rocks. 硅锆钛矿可以在碱性火成岩中找到。 | Synonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral) | Antonyms: None | noun
