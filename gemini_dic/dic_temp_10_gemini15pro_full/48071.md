declarator:/ˈdɛkləˌreɪtər/| n.|声明者，声明符|A declarator is a part of a C++ declaration that introduces the name being declared and may modify the base type to specify the type of the entity being named.  It is the identifier or name being declared and any additional modifiers that specify the type. |`int x;` - Here, 'x' is the declarator. It declares an integer variable named x. | synonym: identifier, name| antonym: N/A| noun
