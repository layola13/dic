crumbliest:/ˈkrʌmbliɪst/
adjective
最易碎的；最酥的
Most likely to crumble; easily broken into small fragments.
The crumbliest cake I've ever eaten. 我吃过的最酥的蛋糕。
The walls of the ancient city were crumbliest to the touch.  这座古城的城墙一碰就碎。
近义词: brittle, friable, powdery
反义词: firm, solid, tough
词性: adjective


crumbly:/ˈkrʌmbli/
adjective
易碎的；酥的
Easy to break into small fragments; brittle.
This cheese is wonderfully crumbly.  这种奶酪非常酥脆。
The soil was dry and crumbly. 土壤干燥易碎。
近义词: brittle, friable
反义词: firm, solid
词性: adjective


crumble:/ˈkrʌmbəl/
verb
弄碎；崩溃；瓦解
Break or fall apart into small fragments, especially over time.
The empire began to crumble.  帝国开始崩溃。
Crumble the biscuits into fine crumbs. 把饼干碾碎成细屑。
近义词: disintegrate, collapse
反义词: solidify, strengthen
词性: verb

crumble:/ˈkrʌmbəl/
noun
碎屑；糕点
Small fragments of something that has crumbled; a baked dish of fruit covered with a crumbly mixture of flour, butter, and sugar.
A crumble of cheese. 一些奶酪碎屑。
Apple crumble is a classic dessert. 苹果碎是经典的甜食。
近义词: fragments, debris
反义词: whole
词性: noun


词源: From Middle English *cromble, from crumb + -le. Related to crum.
