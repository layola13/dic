microgametophyte:/ˌmaɪkroʊɡəˈmiːtəfaɪt/| n. |雄配子体|The microscopic male gametophyte of a plant, which produces male gametes (sperm).  It is the pollen grain in seed plants.| The microgametophyte develops within the pollen grain. 雄配子体在花粉粒内发育。|  The microgametophyte of the flowering plant consists of two or three cells. 开花植物的雄配子体由两到三个细胞组成。| pollen grain, male gametophyte | - | noun
