consulting:/kənˈsʌltɪŋ/ | n. | 咨询；顾问工作；商议；[医]会诊 | the act of giving expert advice to people working in a specific field |  Example: She works in management consulting. 她从事管理咨询工作。

Example:  He offers consulting services to small businesses. 他向小型企业提供咨询服务。 | Synonym:  advisory, guidance, counsel | Antonym:  ignorance, neglect | noun

consult:/kənˈsʌlt/ | v. | 咨询；商议；查阅；考虑 | to seek information or advice from (someone with expertise in a particular area) | Example: I consulted my doctor about the pain in my back. 我向我的医生咨询了背痛的问题。

Example:  We need to consult with our legal team before making a decision. 在做决定之前，我们需要咨询我们的法律团队。 | Synonym: advise, confer, discuss | Antonym: disregard, ignore | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *consulten*, from Latin *cōnsultāre* (“to consult, deliberate, consider”), frequentative of *cōnsulere* (“to consult”), from *con-* (“together”) + *salīre* (“to leap, spring”).
