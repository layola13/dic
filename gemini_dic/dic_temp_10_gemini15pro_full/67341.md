extendedly:/ɪkˈstɛndɪdli/
adv.
延伸地，扩展地
in a way that extends something or makes it last longer
The discussion was extendedly debated.  这个讨论被广泛地辩论了。
近义词: lengthily, protractedly, extensively
反义词: briefly, concisely
词性: 副词

词源: From "extended" + "-ly". "Extended" comes from the Latin "extendere", meaning "to stretch out".
