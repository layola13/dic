besides:/bɪˈsaɪdz/| prep. adv.|除...之外（还有）; 此外;|Besides its beautiful beaches, Hawaii is known for its volcanoes. 除了美丽的沙滩，夏威夷还以火山而闻名。|近义词：in addition to, as well as, moreover, furthermore|反义词:except, excluding|词性:介词, 副词
besides:/bɪˈsaɪdz/| adv.|而且，此外|I don't want to go out; besides, I'm tired. 我不想出去；此外，我累了。|近义词：moreover, furthermore, in addition, also|反义词:nevertheless, however|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bisides, from Old English *bī sīdan (“by the side of”), equivalent to by + side. Cognate with Dutch behalve.
