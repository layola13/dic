containerless:/kənˈteɪnərlɪs/
adj.
无容器的
Relating to or denoting a process in which a material is melted and solidified without coming into contact with a container.
The containerless processing method allows materials to be processed without contamination from the crucible.  无容器处理方法允许材料在不受坩埚污染的情况下进行处理。
Containerless solidification allows the formation of unique materials with novel properties. 无容器凝固可以形成具有新特性的独特材料。
近义词: crucible-free, free-floating
反义词: contained
词性: 形容词
