group:/ɡruːp/| n. v.|组，团体，群；分组，聚集|A number of people or things that are located close together or considered as a unit. |例句：The children were divided into small groups for the activity. 孩子们被分成小组进行活动。|近义词：team, band, cluster, assembly|反义词:individual, single|noun

group:/ɡruːp/| v.|分组，聚集|To arrange (people or things) into groups. |例句：The teacher grouped the students by age. 老师按年龄给学生分组。|近义词：assemble, gather, cluster|反义词:separate, disperse|verb
