odontopteryx:/ˌoʊdɒnˈtɒptərɪks/| n.|齿翼鸟|An extinct, large, flightless bird from the Eocene epoch, known from fossil remains found in Egypt. It had a powerful beak with tooth-like projections. |例句：Odontopteryx was a formidable predator of its time. 齿翼鸟是那个时代的可怕掠食者。

例句：The fossil record of Odontopteryx is still incomplete. 齿翼鸟的化石记录仍然不完整。|近义词：Pelagornis, Dasornis (similar extinct seabirds)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
