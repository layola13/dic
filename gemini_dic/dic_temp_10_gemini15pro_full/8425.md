anti-zionist: /ˌæntiˈzaɪənɪst/ | n. adj. | 反犹太复国主义者；反犹太复国主义的 | A person who opposes Zionism; opposing Zionism.  Zionism is the national movement for the return of the Jewish people to their homeland and the resumption of Jewish sovereignty in the Land of Israel.  Anti-Zionism often manifests as opposition to the State of Israel and its policies. |  The speaker identified as an anti-zionist.  这位发言者自称是反犹太复国主义者。| The anti-zionist group protested outside the embassy.  这个反犹太复国主义团体在大使馆外抗议。 | Synonyms:  non-Zionist, anti-Israel (often used but not always synonymous) | Antonyms: Zionist, pro-Israel | noun, adjective

注意事项:
请注意，"anti-Zionism" 经常与 "anti-Semitism"（反犹太主义）混淆，但两者并不相同。反犹太复国主义是反对犹太复国主义的政治意识形态，而反犹太主义是对犹太人作为民族或宗教群体的仇恨。虽然两者有时可能重叠，但重要的是要区分它们。使用该词时需要谨慎，并确保其在正确的语境中使用。
