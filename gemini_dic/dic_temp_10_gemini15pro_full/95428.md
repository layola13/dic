incinerating:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
焚烧，烧成灰烬
The process of burning something completely to ashes.
The city is incinerating its garbage.  这座城市正在焚烧垃圾。
The evidence was incinerated.  证据被烧毁了。
synonyms: burning, combusting, cremating
antonyms: extinguishing, preserving
