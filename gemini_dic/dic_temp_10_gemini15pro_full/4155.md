airbnb:/ˈɛərbɪɛnbiː/
n.|爱彼迎；民宿短租平台|An online marketplace and hospitality service, enabling people to lease or rent short-term lodging including vacation rentals, apartment rentals, homestays, hostel beds, or hotel rooms.|We stayed in an Airbnb in Paris. 我们在巴黎的爱彼迎住了一晚。|近义词:VRBO, HomeAway, Booking.com|反义词:hotel, motel|noun


Etymology:
A portmanteau of "air bed and breakfast".  Originally, the founders rented out air mattresses in their living room, offering bed and breakfast.  The name evolved to Airbnb as the platform expanded beyond air mattresses.
