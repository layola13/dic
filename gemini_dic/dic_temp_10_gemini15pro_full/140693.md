omitted: /əˈmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 省略的，遗漏的 | Left out or excluded.  Derived from the Latin "omittere" meaning "to let go, send away". | Example: The omitted details were crucial to understanding the story.  省略的细节对于理解故事至关重要。| Synonym: excluded, left out, deleted, neglected | Antonym: included, added, inserted | word type: adjective
