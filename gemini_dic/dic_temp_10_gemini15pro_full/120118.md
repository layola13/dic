mimicries:/ˈmɪmɪkriz/ | n. | 模仿；拟态；（尤指）滑稽模仿 | the action or skill of imitating someone or something, especially in order to entertain or ridicule | The comedian's mimicries of famous politicians were hilarious.  这位喜剧演员对著名政治家的滑稽模仿令人捧腹。 | synonyms: imitations, impersonations, parodies | antonyms: originals, authenticity | noun

mimicry:/ˈmɪmɪkri/ | n. | 模仿；拟态 | the action or skill of imitating someone or something, especially in order to entertain or ridicule; the close external resemblance of an animal or plant (or part of one) to another animal, plant, or inanimate object | The butterfly's mimicry of a dead leaf protects it from predators. 蝴蝶对枯叶的模仿保护它免受捕食者的侵害。 | synonyms: imitation, impersonation, camouflage | antonyms: originality, distinction | noun
