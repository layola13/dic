clover:/ˈkləʊvər/ (UK) /ˈkloʊvər/ (US)| n.|三叶草，苜蓿|A small plant with three round leaves on each stem, often considered to be lucky.|I found a four-leaf clover! 我找到了一株四叶草！|Synonyms: trefoil, shamrock|Antonyms: N/A|noun

clover:/ˈkləʊvər/ (UK) /ˈkloʊvər/ (US)| adj.|幸运的 (比喻)|Relating to or resembling clover, often used figuratively to describe something lucky or fortunate. |He's living the clover life. 他过着幸福的生活。|Synonyms: lucky, fortunate|Antonyms: unlucky, unfortunate|adjective
