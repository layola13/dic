muffing: /ˈmʌfɪŋ/ | n. | 失误，笨拙处理 | An act of mishandling or bungling something, especially a catch in sports. | He cost his team the game by muffing an easy catch. 他因为失误了一个简单的接球而葬送了球队的比赛。 |  Near synonyms: blundering, botching, flubbing | Near antonyms: succeeding, achieving, accomplishing | noun

muffing: /ˈmʌfɪŋ/ | v. |  弄糟，搞砸 | To handle clumsily or badly; to bungle, especially in failing to catch a ball. |  She muffed her lines in the play. 她在戏剧中搞砸了她的台词。| Near synonyms: bungle, fumble, mishandle | Near antonyms: manage, handle, succeed | verb
