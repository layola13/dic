glandulation:/ˌɡlændʒʊˈleɪʃən/
n.
腺状隆起;腺的形成;腺的结构
The formation or development of glands.  The small rounded protuberances or gland-like structures, especially in plants.
The botanist noted the glandulation on the underside of the leaf.  植物学家注意到叶子底部的腺状隆起。
The glandulation of the tissue was a key indicator of the disease.  组织的腺状结构是该疾病的关键指标。
近义词: glandularization,  glandular development
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms related to absence of glands, e.g., aglandular)
