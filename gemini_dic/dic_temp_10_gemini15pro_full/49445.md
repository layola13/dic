delphinoid:/dɛlˈfɪnɔɪd/| adj. n.|海豚类的，海豚科动物|Relating to or resembling dolphins; a member of the Delphinidae family.|例句：The delphinoid's sleek body allows it to move swiftly through the water. 海豚光滑的身体使它能够在水中快速移动。

例句：The study focused on the communication patterns of delphinoid species. 这项研究的重点是海豚科动物的交流模式。|近义词：cetacean, dolphin|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology: From Modern Latin *Delphinoides*, from Delphinus (dolphin) + -oides (resembling).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek δελφίς (delphís, “dolphin”).
