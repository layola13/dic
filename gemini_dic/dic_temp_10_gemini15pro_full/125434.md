multiplane:/ˈmʌltiˌpleɪn/| adj. n.|多平面的，多翼飞机|Relating to or denoting an aircraft with more than one set of wings, one above the other.  Also, relating to or denoting a process of animation in which several transparent sheets of artwork are overlaid and moved relative to each other to create an illusion of depth.|The Disney classic "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs" was one of the first films to utilize the multiplane camera technique. 迪士尼经典动画《白雪公主和七个小矮人》是最早使用多平面相机技术的电影之一。|The Wright brothers' first successful flyer was a biplane, a type of multiplane aircraft.  莱特兄弟的第一架成功飞行的飞机是双翼飞机，一种多翼飞机。|multi-winged, multi-deck|single-plane, monoplane|adjective, noun
