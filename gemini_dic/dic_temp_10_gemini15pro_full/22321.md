bonnaz:/ˈbɒnəz/| n.|奖金，红利|A sum of money added to a person's wages as a reward for good performance.|She received a Christmas bonnaz of $500. 她收到了500美元的圣诞奖金。|Synonyms: bonus, premium, reward|Antonyms: penalty, deduction|noun

bonnaz:/ˈbɒnəz/| adj.|好的，优良的（古语，现已很少使用）|Good, excellent (archaic and rarely used). |He gave her a bonnaz apple. 他给了她一个好苹果。(古语用法，现已少见)|Synonyms: good, excellent, fine|Antonyms: bad, poor, inferior|adjective
