assignation: /ˌæsɪɡˈneɪʃən/ (US), /əˌsaɪɡˈneɪʃən/ (UK)
n.
约会;指定;分配;指派
an appointment to meet someone in secret, typically one made by lovers; the allocation or attribution of someone or something as belonging to a particular group, cause, or source.
例句：The lovers met at their usual assignation. 恋人们在他们通常幽会的地方见面。

例句：The assignation of blame for the accident is still under investigation. 事故责任的认定仍在调查中。

近义词：appointment, rendezvous, allocation, attribution
反义词：cancellation, withdrawal
词性: 名词
