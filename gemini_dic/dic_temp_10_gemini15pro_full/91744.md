hyperdicrotous:/ˌhaɪpərdaɪˈkrɒtəs/| adj.|过度分叉的；过度二歧的|Having a branching pattern in which each branch divides into more than two branches; excessively dichotomous.|The fossilized plant displayed a hyperdicrotous venation pattern.  这块植物化石展现出过度二歧的脉络模式。|Near synonyms:  multifid, polychotomous|Antonym:  dichotomous|Adjective
