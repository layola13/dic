infrequency:/ɪnˈfriːkwənsi/| n.|不常发生；罕见；稀少|The infrequency of his visits made us wonder if he was still interested.  他的 infrequent visits made us wonder if he was still interested. 他很少来访，这让我们怀疑他是否还感兴趣。|例句：The infrequency of rain in the desert makes it a harsh environment. 沙漠中降雨稀少，使其成为一个恶劣的环境。|近义词：rarity, uncommonness, rareness|反义词: frequency, commonness, prevalence|词性:noun

Etymology:  From in- "not" + frequency.  The word "frequency" comes from Late Latin frequentia "a crowd, throng, multitude," from frequentem (nominative frequens) "crowded, frequent, common, numerous," present participle of frequentare "to visit often, resort to, haunt," related to farcire "to stuff, cram."
