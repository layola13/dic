backbone:/ˈbækˌboʊn/| n.|支柱，骨干，脊梁|The backbone of the company's success is its innovative products.  这家公司成功的支柱是其创新产品。|例句：A strong backbone is essential for good posture.  强壮的脊柱对良好的姿势至关重要。

例句：The teachers form the backbone of the education system. 教师是教育体系的骨干。|近义词：foundation, core, support, spine|反义词: weakness, vulnerability|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *bakbon, from back + bone.  来自中古英语 *bakbon，由 back（背部）+ bone（骨头）组成。
