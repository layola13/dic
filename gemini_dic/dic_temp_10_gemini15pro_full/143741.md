outlying:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪɪŋ/| adj.|偏远的，边远的|Situated far from the center or main body; remote.|例句：The outlying villages were cut off by the floods. 洪水切断了边远村庄的联系。|例句：They live in an outlying district of the city. 他们住在城市的偏远地区。|近义词：remote, distant, peripheral|反义词：central, inner, nearby|词性:形容词
词源: From out + lie + -ing.  "Out-" signifies "outside" or "beyond." "Lie" in this context refers to location.  "-ing" transforms the verb into a present participle used as an adjective.
