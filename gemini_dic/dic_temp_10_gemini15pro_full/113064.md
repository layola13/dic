lumping:/ˈlʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 归并，集中；总括；（一起）处理 | The act of putting things together in a group or mass, often without careful distinction. |  They resorted to lumping all the expenses together. 他们将所有费用都合在一起计算了。| grouping, combining, aggregating | separating, dividing, distinguishing | noun

lumping:/ˈlʌmpɪŋ/ | adj. |  笨重的，庞大的 |  (Informal) Large and heavy; clumsy.  |  He carried a lumping great suitcase. 他提着一个笨重的大手提箱。| bulky, heavy, massive | small, light, dainty | adjective
