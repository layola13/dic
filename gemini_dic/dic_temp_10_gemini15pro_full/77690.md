gemer:/ˈdʒɛmər/ | n. | 宝石; 宝石工; 宝石切割师 | A precious stone, especially when cut and polished. Also, a person who cuts and polishes gems. |  Example: The gemer carefully examined the raw diamond. 宝石工仔细检查了未加工的钻石。

Example:  She inherited a collection of gemers from her grandmother. 她继承了祖母的一套宝石。 | Synonym: jeweler, lapidary, gem-cutter | Antonym: N/A | Noun
