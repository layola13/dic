nostalgically: /nɒˈstældʒɪkli/ /nɑːˈstældʒɪkli/
| adv. |怀旧地，念旧地 |In a way that involves thinking fondly about the past. |She looked back nostalgically on her childhood. 她怀旧地回顾她的童年。|He spoke nostalgically about his college days. 他怀念地谈起他的大学时光。|sentimentally, longingly |unsentimentally, practically |adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nostalgique* + *-ly*. *Nostalgique* from *nostalgie* "homesickness," coined by Johannes Hofer (1669) from Greek *nóstos* "return home" + *álgos* "pain, grief, distress."
