chippangu:/ˌtʃɪˈpæŋɡuː/
n.
日本（古称）
An archaic name for Japan, derived from Marco Polo's rendering of the Chinese name.
The tales of gold in Chippangu lured many European explorers eastward.  古老的关于黄金之国“契丹”的故事吸引了很多欧洲探险家向东探索。
Chippangu, as Japan was then known, was described as an island rich in gold.  当时被称为“契丹”的日本被描述成一个黄金遍地的岛屿。
近义词:Japan, Cipango
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
