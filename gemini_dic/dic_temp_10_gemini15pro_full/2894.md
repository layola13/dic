advertising's:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪŋz/
noun
广告的；广告业的
Possessive form of advertising, referring to something that belongs to or is related to advertising.
Advertising's impact on children is a serious concern.  广告对儿童的影响是一个严重的问题。
The advertising's message was unclear. 广告的信息不明确。

近义词: promotional, marketing
反义词: None (Possessive forms don't typically have antonyms)
词性: Possessive noun

词源:
From "advertising," which is a present participle of the verb "advertise."  "Advertise" comes from Old French "advertir" (to warn, inform), ultimately from Latin "advertere" (to turn toward).  The possessive suffix "-'s" indicates belonging or association.
