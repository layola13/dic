geodiferous:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɪfərəs/
adjective
含晶洞的；多晶洞的
Containing geodes or many geodes.
The cave explorers were thrilled to discover a geodiferous chamber filled with sparkling crystals.  洞穴探险家们兴奋地发现了一个充满闪亮晶体的含晶洞的房间。
Geologists were excited to find a geodiferous vein in the newly discovered cave. 地质学家很兴奋在新发现的洞穴中找到一条含晶洞的矿脉。
Synonyms: geode-bearing, geode-rich
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could use descriptions like "barren", "devoid of geodes")
词性: adjective
