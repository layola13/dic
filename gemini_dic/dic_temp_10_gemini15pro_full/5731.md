alveoli:/ælˈviːəlaɪ/
n.|肺泡；小窝，牙槽|A small cavity, pit, or cell, especially in lung tissue or bone. |The alveoli are tiny air sacs in the lungs where the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide takes place.|Synonyms: air sac, cavity, socket|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
alveolar:/ælˈviːələr/
adj.|肺泡的，牙槽的|Relating to or resembling an alveolus. |The alveolar process is the part of the jawbone that holds the teeth.|Synonyms: pulmonary, respiratory (in the context of lungs)|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
