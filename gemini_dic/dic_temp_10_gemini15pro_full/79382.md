glidder:/ˈɡlɪdər/ | n. | 滑翔机; 滑翔者; 滑翔伞; (尤指鸟类)滑翔 | A glider is a very light aircraft with no engine, which flies by gliding. | The glider soared gracefully through the air.  滑翔机优雅地在空中翱翔。 | 近义词：sailplane | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑powered aircraft (动力飞机)) | 词性: 名词

glidder:/ˈɡlɪdər/ | v. | 滑翔; 滑行; (目光)扫过; (思绪)掠过 | To glidder is to move smoothly and quietly, especially through the air. | The hawk gliddered effortlessly above the valley.  老鹰毫不费力地在山谷上空滑翔。 | 近义词：soar, glide | 反义词: plummet, fall | 词性: 动词
