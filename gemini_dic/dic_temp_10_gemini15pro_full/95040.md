inactivated:/ɪnˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
灭活的，失活的
Made inactive; deactivated.  Having been made inactive, made unable to function, or made ineffective.
例句：The inactivated virus is used in the vaccine.  这种灭活病毒用于疫苗。

例句：The enzyme was inactivated by heat.  这种酶被高温灭活了。
近义词：deactivated, disabled, neutralized
反义词：activated, active, live
词性：adjective
