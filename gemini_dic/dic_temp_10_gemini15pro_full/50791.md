descendant:/dɪˈsendənt/ | n. | 后裔；子孙；派生物 | a person who is related to someone and who lives after them, especially someone who is a lot younger than them |  My descendants will inherit this land. 我的后代将继承这片土地。| synonyms: offspring, heir, successor | antonyms: ancestor, forebear | noun

descendant:/dɪˈsendənt/ | adj. |  下降的；衍生的 | proceeding from an ancestor or source |  The descendant branch of the family moved to America. 这个家族的後裔分支移居到了美国。 | synonyms: derived, inherited | antonyms: ancestral, original | adjective
