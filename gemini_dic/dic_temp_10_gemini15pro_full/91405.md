hydrozoa:/haɪdrəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 水螅纲 | A class of Cnidaria, mostly marine and colonial animals, that includes hydras, jellyfish, and Portuguese men-of-war.  They have a simple body plan with a single opening serving as both mouth and anus. |  Hydrozoa are found in both freshwater and marine environments. 水螅纲动物在淡水和海洋环境中都有发现。|  The Portuguese man-of-war is a colony of hydrozoa. 僧帽水母是水螅纲动物的一个群体。| synonyms:  Hydrozoans | antonyms: N/A | noun
