hyperaspist:/ˈhaɪpərˈæspɪst/| n.|超级盾兵，超级护卫者|A person or thing that provides exceptional protection or defense.|The new anti-missile system acted as a hyperaspist against aerial attacks. 新的反导弹系统充当了对抗空袭的超级盾牌。|近义词：protector, defender, guardian, champion|反义词: assailant, attacker|词性:名词

词源:  "Hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, huper) meaning "over, above, beyond" + "Aspist" (Greek: ἀσπιστής, aspistēs) meaning "shield-bearer."  The word is a modern coinage, not found in classical Greek.
