dive-bombers:/ˈdaɪv bɑːmərz/
n.
俯冲轰炸机
Military aircraft designed to attack ground targets by making a steep dive.
The dive-bombers screamed down towards their target. 俯冲轰炸机呼啸着向目标俯冲下去。
近义词:attack aircraft, bombers
反义词:fighter aircraft
词性:名词

dive-bomber:/ˈdaɪv bɑːmər/
n.
俯冲轰炸机
A military aircraft designed for dive-bombing.
The squadron of dive-bombers was ready for action.  俯冲轰炸机中队准备行动。
近义词:attack plane, bomber
反义词:fighter plane
词性:名词


词源:
From dive + bomber. "Dive" as a verb meaning to descend steeply dates back to the late 14th century. "Bomber" shortened from "bomb thrower," emerged in the early 20th century with the advent of aircraft capable of carrying and dropping bombs.
