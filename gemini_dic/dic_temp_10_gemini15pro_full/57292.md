downside:/ˈdaʊnsaɪd/| n.|缺点，不利方面|A disadvantage of a situation.|例句：The downside of living in the city is the high cost of rent.  住在城市的缺点是房租高。

例句：One of the downsides of the new job is the long commute. 这份新工作的缺点之一是通勤时间长。|近义词：disadvantage, drawback, negative, con|反义词:advantage, upside, benefit, pro|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "down" + "side," literally referring to the lower or less favorable aspect.
