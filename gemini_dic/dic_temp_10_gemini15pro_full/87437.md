hesitantly:/ˈhezɪtəntli/| adv.|犹豫地，迟疑地|In a hesitant manner; showing or feeling hesitation.|She hesitantly agreed to go with him. 她犹豫地同意和他一起去。|近义词：reluctantly, uncertainly, tentatively|反义词：confidently, decisively, resolutely|词性:副词
词源：From the adjective hesitant + -ly.  Hesitant comes from the Latin word haerere, meaning "to stick," implying being stuck or unable to proceed easily.
