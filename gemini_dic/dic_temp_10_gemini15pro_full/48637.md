defenderism: /dɪˈfɛndərɪzəm/
n.
保卫主义；防御主义
The ideology or practice of defending one's country, culture, or values against perceived threats, often with an emphasis on military preparedness and a suspicion of outsiders.
Defenderism can manifest in various forms, from advocating for increased military spending to promoting nationalistic sentiments.  保卫主义可以以各种形式表现出来，从倡导增加军费开支到宣扬民族主义情绪。
Some argue that defenderism is a necessary response to a dangerous world.  有些人认为，保卫主义是对危险世界的必要回应。
近义词:  protectionism, nationalism, isolationism
反义词:  internationalism, globalism, cosmopolitanism
词性: noun

Etymology: From "defend" (from Old French "defendre," ultimately from Latin "defendere" - to ward off, protect) + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-ism" (doctrine, ideology suffix).
