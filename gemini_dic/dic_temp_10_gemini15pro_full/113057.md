lumpiest:/ˈlʌmpiɪst/ | adj. | 最多块的；最粗糙的 |  Superlative form of "lumpy," meaning having or full of lumps; uneven in texture. | The lumpiest mashed potatoes I've ever seen. 我见过最粗糙的土豆泥。| The porridge was cold and lumpy. 粥又冷又结块。|  Synonyms:  most uneven,  most knobbly,  most bumpy | Antonyms: smoothest, flattest | adjective
