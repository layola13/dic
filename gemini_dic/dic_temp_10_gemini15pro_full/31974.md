chemists:/ˈkemɪsts/| n.|化学家；药剂师|Experts say that chemists play a vital role in drug discovery.  专家说，化学家在药物发现中起着至关重要的作用。|Chemists prepare and dispense medications. 药剂师配制和分发药物。|Synonyms: scientist, researcher, pharmacist, druggist|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: chimist, via Old French from alchimiste (see alchemist). The -ist suffix was influenced by analogous learned formations in -ist, such as artist.
