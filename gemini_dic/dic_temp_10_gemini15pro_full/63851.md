epigrammatically:/ˌepɪɡrəˈmætɪkli/
adv.
警句式地，简洁地
In a concise, witty, and pointed manner, often using antithesis or paradox, characteristic of an epigram.
He summarized the situation epigrammatically. 他用警句概括了情况。
近义词: concisely, pithily, succinctly, wittily
反义词: verbosely, tediously, lengthily
词性：副词

epigrammatic:/ˌepɪɡrəˈmætɪk/
adj.
警句的，简洁的
Of or relating to an epigram; concise and pointed.
His epigrammatic style is reminiscent of Oscar Wilde. 他简洁的风格让人想起奥斯卡·王尔德。
近义词: concise, pithy, succinct, witty
反义词: verbose, tedious, lengthy
词性：形容词

epigram:/ˈepɪɡræm/
n.
警句，箴言
A short, witty saying, often ending with a surprising or satirical twist.
Oscar Wilde was a master of the epigram. 奥斯卡·王尔德是警句大师。
近义词: saying, maxim, adage, aphorism
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "long speech" or "diatribe")
词性：名词
