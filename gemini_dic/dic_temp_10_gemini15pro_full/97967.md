innutritive:/ˌɪnjuˈtrɪtɪv/
adjective
无营养的;不提供营养的
Lacking nourishment; not providing nutrients.
例句：
Foods like potato chips are innutritive and should be avoided.
薯片之类的食物没有营养，应该避免食用。

Many processed foods are innutritive and full of empty calories.
许多加工食品没有营养，充满了空热量。

近义词：non-nutritive, non-nourishing, unwholesome
反义词：nutritive, nourishing, wholesome
词性：adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + nutritive.
