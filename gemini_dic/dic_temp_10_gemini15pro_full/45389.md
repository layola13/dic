cupbearer:/ˈkʌpˌberər/| n.|侍酒者，斟酒官|A person who served drinks, especially wine, at banquets or feasts, typically of high rank in a royal or noble household.|The king's cupbearer was always at his side during the feast. 国王的侍酒者在宴会期间一直在他身边。|近义词：butler, steward, server|反义词:guest|noun
