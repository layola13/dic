A small compartment, often one of several, for storing personal belongings.|  I keep my winter scarves in a cubbyhole under the stairs. 我把冬天的围巾放在楼梯下的一个小隔间里。|  She found the old photo album tucked away in a cubbyhole in the attic.  她在阁楼的一个小隔间里找到了那本旧相册。|synonyms: compartment, locker, niche, nook, pigeonhole | antonyms:  hall, auditorium| noun
