facsimile:/fækˈsɪməli/ /fækˈsɪmɪli/
n.|摹本，复制品，传真|An exact copy or reproduction of something, such as a document, signature, or work of art. | This book is a facsimile of a 16th-century manuscript. 这本书是16世纪手稿的摹本。

facsimile:/fækˈsɪməli/ /fækˈsɪmɪli/
n.|传真（文件）|A method of transmitting documents electronically over telephone lines. |I sent a facsimile of the contract to the head office. 我把合同的传真件发到了总部。

facsimile:/fækˈsɪməli/ /fækˈsɪmɪli/
adj.|复制的，摹仿的|Made in exact imitation of something else. |The museum displayed a facsimile edition of the Declaration of Independence.  博物馆展出了《独立宣言》的复制版。


Etymology:
From Latin *fac simile "make similar," imperative of facere "to make" + simile, neuter of similis "like."

Synonyms:
copy, replica, reproduction, duplicate, imitation, fax

Antonyms:
original
