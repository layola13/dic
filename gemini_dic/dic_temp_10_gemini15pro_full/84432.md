hallucinatorily:/həˈluːsɪnəˈtɔːrɪli/
adv.
幻觉地，引起幻觉地
in a way that relates to or causes hallucinations; as if under the influence of hallucinations.
The desert heat made him see mirages hallucinatorily. 沙漠的酷热让他产生了幻觉，看到了海市蜃楼。
The fever made her speak hallucinatorily. 高烧使她说起了胡话，如同产生了幻觉。
Synonym: delusionally, visionarily, dreamily
Antonym: realistically, soberly, clearly
词性: 副词
