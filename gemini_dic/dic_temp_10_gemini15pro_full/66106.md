excel:/ɪkˈsɛl/ (v.) /ˈɛksəl/ (n.)| v. 擅长；胜过  n. 优秀；成绩优异| To be exceptionally good at something; to surpass others.  A spreadsheet program.|
例句：She excels at playing the piano. 她擅长弹钢琴。

例句：He excelled in all his exams. 他所有考试都名列前茅。

例句：Microsoft Excel is a widely used spreadsheet program. 微软Excel是一个广泛使用的电子表格程序。

例句：Her performance in the competition was excel. 她在比赛中的表现非常出色。

近义词：(v.) surpass, outdo, shine, triumph,  (n.) brilliance, excellence, merit, superiority
反义词：(v.) fail, lag, underperform,  (n.) inferiority, mediocrity
词性：verb, noun
词源：From Latin excellere "to rise, surpass," from ex- "out" + -cellere "to rise high, project."
