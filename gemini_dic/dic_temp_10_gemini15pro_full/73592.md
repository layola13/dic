foretellers:/fɔːrˈtelərz/
noun
预言者，预卜者
People who predict the future.
The ancient foretellers predicted the fall of the empire. 古代的预言者预言了帝国的衰落。

The foretellers warned of a coming disaster. 预言者警告即将发生的灾难。

Synonym: prophets, soothsayers, diviners, oracles
Antonym: n/a
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "foretell" + "-er" (agent suffix).
"Foretell" comes from Old English "foretellan," from "fore-" (before) + "tellan" (to tell).
