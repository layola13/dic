alevin:/ˈælɪvɪn/| n. | 鱼苗，仔鱼 | A young fish that has recently hatched from its egg and still has its yolk sac attached. |  The alevin stage is a critical period for the survival of salmon. 鱼苗阶段是鲑鱼生存的关键时期。|  The tiny alevin hid amongst the riverbed gravel.  小鱼苗藏在河床的砾石中。 | Synonyms: fry, fingerling | Antonyms: adult (fish), mature (fish) | Noun
