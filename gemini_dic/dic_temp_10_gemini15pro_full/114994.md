mandarin:/ˈmændərɪn/ (US), /ˈmændərɪn/ (UK)| n. adj.| 普通话，柑橘，中国官话，满清官吏 |A citrus tree (Citrus reticulata) and its small, sweet to tart, easy-to-peel fruit.  A person who speaks Mandarin Chinese. A high public official in imperial China. |例句：She speaks Mandarin fluently. 她普通话说得很流利。


mandarin:/ˈmændərɪn/ (US), /ˈmændərɪn/ (UK)| adj.| 官僚的，  mandarins' |Relating to or denoting Mandarin Chinese or its speakers.  Of or relating to a mandarin or mandarins. |例句：Mandarin dialects are spoken across a vast area of China.  中国的广大地区都使用官话方言。


近义词：tangerine (n. - fruit), official (n. - person), bureaucratic (adj.)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun, adjective
