cannibalization:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 食人，同类相食；(产品或服务的)自相蚕食 | The reduction of the sales of a company's product caused by the introduction of another similar product by the same company.  A situation where a new product or service eats into the sales/share of an existing product or service offered by the same company. | Example 1: The introduction of the new sports car led to the cannibalization of sales of the company's existing sedan.  推出新款跑车导致公司现有轿车的销量下降。

Example 2:  The company was worried about cannibalization of their existing customer base by their new online service. 公司担心他们的新在线服务会蚕食他们现有的客户群。| 近义词：erosion, undermining, encroachment | 反义词：synergy, reinforcement | 词性: 名词
