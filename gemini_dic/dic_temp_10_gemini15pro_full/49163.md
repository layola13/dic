dekabrist:/dɪˈkæbrɪst/| n. |十二月党人|A member of a group of Russian revolutionaries who led an unsuccessful uprising in December 1825.|The Decembrists were idealistic young noblemen who sought to reform Russia's autocratic system. 十二月党人是一群理想主义的年轻贵族，他们试图改革俄罗斯的专制制度。|The Decembrist revolt was a pivotal moment in Russian history. 十二月党人起义是俄罗斯历史上的一个关键时刻。|Synonyms: revolutionary, insurgent, rebel|Antonyms: loyalist, reactionary, conservative|Noun
