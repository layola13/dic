lichenographical:/ˌlaɪkənəˈɡræfɪkəl/

Lichens are fascinating organisms, and lichenographical research helps us understand their role in ecosystems.  地衣是迷人的生物体，地衣学研究帮助我们了解它们在生态系统中的作用。
The expedition brought back numerous lichenographical samples for analysis.  探险队带回了许多地衣学样本进行分析。
Synonyms:  None readily available.
Antonyms:  None readily available.
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From lichen + -graphical.  Lichen comes from Greek *leikhēn* meaning "lichen, tetter, ringworm."  -graphical comes from Greek *graphikos* meaning "of or for writing," from *graphein* meaning "to write."
