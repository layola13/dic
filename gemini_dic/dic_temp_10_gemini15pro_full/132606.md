nondiplomacy:/ˌnɒndɪˈpləʊməsi/ | n. | 非外交，非外交手段 | Absence or lack of diplomacy; the opposite of diplomatic methods.  It often implies aggressive or confrontational tactics. |  The country's nondiplomacy in the region led to increased tensions. 这个国家在该地区的非外交手段导致紧张局势加剧。 |  The general's nondiplomacy was evident in his blunt ultimatum. 将军的非外交手段体现在他直截了当的最后通牒中。 | Synonyms: aggression, belligerence, confrontation, hostility | Antonyms: diplomacy, tact, negotiation, conciliation | noun
