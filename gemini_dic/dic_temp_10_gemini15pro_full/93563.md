imagings:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪŋz/
n.
成像，影像，图像
The process of creating visual representations of the interior of a body, especially using techniques such as X-ray, ultrasound, or magnetic resonance imaging.
Doctors used various imagings to diagnose the patient's condition. 医生使用各种成像技术来诊断病人的病情。
synonyms: imaging, imagery, visualization, representation, depiction
antonyms: None (Abstract concepts don't have direct antonyms)
