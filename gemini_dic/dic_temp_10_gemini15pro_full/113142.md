lunt:/lʌnt/
n.
一阵微风; 一股轻烟; 火绒; 点火装置
A gentle breeze; a puff of smoke; tinder; a slow match.
The lunt from his pipe curled upwards. 他烟斗里冒出的一缕轻烟向上飘卷。
近义词: breeze, puff, tinder, slow match
反义词: gale, gust
