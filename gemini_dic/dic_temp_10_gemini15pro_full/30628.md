ceremonialists:/ˌserɪˈmoʊniəlɪsts/
n.
仪式专家；司仪
Experts in ceremonies; those who officiate or participate in formal rituals.
The ceremonialists prepared the temple for the annual festival.  仪式专家们为一年一度的节日准备了寺庙。
The ceremonialists led the procession into the cathedral.  司仪们带领队伍进入大教堂。
Synonyms:officiants, masters of ceremonies, ritualists
Antonyms:participants, attendees, guests
noun
