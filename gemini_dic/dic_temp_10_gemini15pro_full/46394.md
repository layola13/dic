daemon:/ˈdiːmən/ (US) /ˈdeɪmən/ (UK)| n.|守护进程，后台程序|A computer program that runs as a background process, rather than being under the direct control of an interactive user.|The email server runs as a daemon, constantly checking for incoming messages.|Synonyms: background process, service, agent|Antonyms: foreground process|Noun
