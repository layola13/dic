compositional:/ˌkɑːmpəˈzɪʃənl/
adjective
组成的，成分的
Relating to the way in which something is made up of different parts or elements.
The compositional elements of the painting are masterfully balanced. 这幅画的构成元素巧妙地平衡。
The compositional structure of the music is complex and layered. 这首音乐的构成结构复杂而多层次。
synonyms: constituent, structural, component
antonyms: holistic, complete, whole
词性: adjective
