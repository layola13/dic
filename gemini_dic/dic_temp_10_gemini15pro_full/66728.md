exorbitate: /ɪɡˈzɔːrbɪteɪt/
verb
使过高；使过分；索价过高
To charge an exorbitant price for something means to charge a price that is much too high.
例句：The hotel exorbitated their prices during the holiday season.  酒店在节日期间价格过高。

例句：Don't exorbitate the price of goods, or no one will buy them. 不要对商品漫天要价，否则没人会买。

近义词：inflate, overcharge, overprice
反义词：undercharge, discount
词性：v.
