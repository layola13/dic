melatonin: /ˌmeləˈtəʊnɪn/ (UK) /ˌmeləˈtoʊnɪn/ (US) | n. | 褪黑素，褪黑激素 | A hormone secreted by the pineal gland in the brain that helps regulate sleep-wake cycles.  It is involved in synchronizing circadian rhythms, including sleep-wake timing, blood pressure regulation, seasonal reproduction, and many other functions. |  I started taking melatonin supplements to help me sleep. 我开始服用褪黑激素补充剂来帮助我睡眠。 |  Melatonin production decreases with age. 随着年龄的增长，褪黑激素的产量会减少。 | Synonyms: sleep hormone | Antonyms: None | Noun
