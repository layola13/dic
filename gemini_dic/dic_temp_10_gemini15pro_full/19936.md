biodefence:/ˈbaɪəʊdɪˈfɛns/ (UK) /ˈbaɪoʊdɪˈfɛns/ (US)| n.|生物防御|Protective measures taken against the threat or use of biological weapons.  The development and stockpiling of vaccines, antidotes, and other therapeutic or prophylactic measures.|The government invested heavily in biodefence research after the anthrax attacks.|Near synonyms: biological defense, biosecurity|Near antonyms: biological warfare, bioterrorism|noun
