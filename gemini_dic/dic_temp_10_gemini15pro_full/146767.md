overhydration:/ˌoʊvərhaɪˈdreɪʃən/ | n. |过度水合，水中毒 |The condition of having too much water in the body, which can cause serious health problems. | He suffered from overhydration after drinking excessive amounts of water during the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中喝了过多的水，导致水中毒。|  Overhydration can lead to a dangerous imbalance of electrolytes. 过度水合会导致危险的电解质失衡。 |  hyperhydration, water intoxication | dehydration | noun
