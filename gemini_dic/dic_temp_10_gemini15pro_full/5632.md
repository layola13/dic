altheine:/ˈælθiɪn/
n.
咖啡因;茶碱
A purine alkaloid, C₈H₁₀N₄O₂, that is a stimulant of the central nervous system, found in coffee, tea, and cola nuts, and used medicinally as a cardiac and respiratory stimulant and as a diuretic. It is identical to caffeine.
Tea contains altheine, which is a stimulant. 茶含有茶碱，这是一种兴奋剂。
近义词：caffeine, theine
反义词: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound.  One could consider depressants as functional antonyms: e.g., alcohol, barbiturates)
词性:noun
