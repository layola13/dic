attractions:/əˈtrækʃənz/| n.| 吸引人的事物；景点；吸引力|Something that attracts or is intended to attract people by appealing to their desires and tastes. |例句：The city's attractions include a beautiful cathedral and a fascinating museum. 这座城市的景点包括一座美丽的教堂和一座引人入胜的博物馆。

例句：The main attraction of the festival is the fireworks display.  这个节日最吸引人的地方是烟花表演。

近义词：allurements, appeals, draws, enticements, temptations|反义词:repulsions, deterrents|词性:名词
