albuminiferous:/ˌælbjʊˈmɪnɪfərəs/
adjective
含蛋白的；产生蛋白的
Containing or producing albumin.
Albuminiferous glands secrete albumin. 含蛋白腺分泌蛋白。
近义词: protein-producing, protein-rich
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider albumin-deficient)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *albuminifer*, from *albumin* (albumen) + *-fer* (bearing, producing).  The *-fer* suffix is related to the verb *ferre* (to bear, carry).
