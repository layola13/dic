galvanometric:/ˌɡælvənəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 电流计的；测电流的 | Relating to or measured by a galvanometer, an instrument for detecting and measuring small electric currents. | The experiment used a galvanometric method to detect the subtle changes in current.  该实验使用电流计方法来检测电流的细微变化。 | 近义词:  electrometric | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

galvanometer:/ˌɡælvəˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. | 电流计 | An instrument for detecting and measuring small electric currents. | The galvanometer needle deflected, indicating a change in the magnetic field. 电流计指针偏转，表明磁场发生了变化。| 近义词: ammeter (for larger currents) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
