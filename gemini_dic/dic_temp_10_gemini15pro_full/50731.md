derogatory:/dɪˈrɑːɡəˌtɔːri/ /dɛˈrɑɡətɔri/ | adj. | 贬低的，贬损的 |  Expressive of low opinion; disparaging.  | His derogatory remarks about her appearance offended everyone. 他对她外貌的贬损言论冒犯了所有人。 | synonyms: disparaging, belittling, demeaning, insulting, pejorative, slighting, uncomplimentary | antonyms: complimentary, flattering, laudatory, praising | 词性: 形容词

derogator:/dɪˈrɑːɡəˌteɪtər/  /dɛˈrɑɡətɛɪtər/ | n. | 贬低者，诽谤者 | One who disparages or belittles someone or something. |  He was known as a derogator of anyone who challenged his authority.  他因贬低任何挑战他权威的人而闻名。 | synonyms: critic, detractor, slanderer | antonyms: admirer, supporter, advocate | 词性: 名词
