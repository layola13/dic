intergossip:/ˈɪntərˌɡɒsɪp/| n.|闲聊，私下八卦|The intergossip among the employees created a toxic work environment. 员工之间的闲聊制造了一个有害的工作环境。|例句：The intergossip about the new CEO spread like wildfire. 关于新任首席执行官的闲聊像野火一样蔓延开来。|例句：Their intergossip focused on the latest office romance. 他们的闲聊集中在最新的办公室恋情上。|近义词：gossip, chatter, rumor, whispers|反义词:silence, discretion|词性:名词

Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among (from Latin)
gossip (noun): casual or uncritical conversation or reports about other people, typically involving details that are not confirmed as being true.
