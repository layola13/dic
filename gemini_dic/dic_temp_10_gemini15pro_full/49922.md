denationalize: /diːˌnæʃənəlaɪz/ | v. | 国有化；取消国有化；使非国有化 | To transfer (an industry or enterprise) from government to private ownership and control.  | The government decided to denationalize the railway system. 政府决定将铁路系统私有化。 |  The government is planning to denationalize several key industries. 政府正计划将几个关键产业私有化。 | 近义词: privatize, deregulate | 反义词: nationalize | 词性: 动词
