orabassu:/ˌɔːrəˈbɑːsuː/ | n. | 巴巴苏油 | A yellowish-white waxy oil obtained from the seeds of the babassu palm (Attalea speciosa), native to South America, used in cosmetics, soaps, and as a cooking oil. |  Orabassu oil is a sustainable alternative to palm oil. 巴巴苏油是棕榈油的可持续替代品。 | synonyms: babassu oil, babassu butter | antonyms: N/A | noun
