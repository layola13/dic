miss-cue: /ˌmɪsˈkjuː/
noun
失误，错误
An action or remark that is inappropriate or ill-timed, especially in a social situation.
The politician's miss-cue about taxes cost him the election.  这位政治家在税收问题上的失误让他输掉了选举。
近义词: blunder, gaffe, error, mistake
反义词: success, triumph
