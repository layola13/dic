abstinent: /ˈæbstɪnənt/
adjective
节制的；禁欲的；戒绝的
Not indulging in something, especially food or drink.  Choosing not to do something.
He has been abstinent from alcohol for five years.  他戒酒五年了。

She remained abstinent from drugs after completing rehab. 完成康复治疗后，她继续戒毒。

synonym: temperate, sober, moderate, ascetic
antonym: indulgent, intemperate, immoderate
词性: 形容词


USAGE->

He is abstinent from alcohol. = 他戒酒。
She is abstinent from all forms of gambling. = 她戒绝所有形式的赌博。

过去完成时:
