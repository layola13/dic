ambassadrice: /æmˈbæsədrɪs/ /ɒmˈbæsədrɪs/
noun
女大使；大使夫人
A female ambassador; the wife of an ambassador.
The ambassadrice hosted a reception at the embassy. 女大使在使馆举行招待会。
The French ambassadrice gave a speech at the United Nations. 法国女大使在联合国发表了讲话。
synonyms: ambassador (more common and gender-neutral), envoy, diplomat, minister
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a job title, but perhaps) citizen, civilian
n.
