ecdysis:/ˈekdəsɪs/
noun
蜕皮;脱皮
The process of shedding the old skin (in reptiles, insects, etc.) or casting off an outer layer (as in some worms or crustaceans).
The snake's ecdysis left its skin draped over the branch.  这条蛇蜕皮后，它的皮挂在树枝上。
molting, shedding, sloughing
n/a
noun
