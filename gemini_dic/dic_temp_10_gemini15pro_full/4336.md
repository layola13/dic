akees:/əˈkiːz/| n. |西非荔枝果；阿开木果|The fruit of the ackee tree (Blighia sapida), native to West Africa, which is poisonous until it opens naturally on the tree. Its flesh is then edible when cooked. |例句：Akees are a popular breakfast food in Jamaica.  西非荔枝果在牙买加是一种受欢迎的早餐食物。

例句：Be careful when preparing akees, as the unripe fruit is toxic.  准备西非荔枝果时要小心，因为未成熟的果实有毒。|近义词：Ackee fruit, Blighia sapida|反义词: None|词性:Noun
