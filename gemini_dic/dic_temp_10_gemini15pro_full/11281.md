arctoidea:/ɑːrkˈtɔɪdiə/ | n. | 犬形总科 | A superfamily of mammals in the order Carnivora, including bears, dogs, seals, and others. |  The red panda was once classified under Arctoidea but recent studies suggest a separate lineage. 小熊猫曾经被归类于犬形总科，但最近的研究表明它属于一个独立的谱系。| 近义词: Canoidea (sometimes used as a contrasting group) | 反义词: N/A | noun

arctoid:/ˈɑːrkˌtɔɪd/ | adj. |  犬形总科的；类似熊的 | Relating to or resembling the Arctoidea superfamily. | The arctoid characteristics of the animal were clearly visible in its skull structure.  这种动物的犬形总科特征在其头骨结构中清晰可见。| 近义词: ursine (bear-like) | 反义词: N/A | adjective
