osteogenesis: /ˌɒstiəʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌɑːstiəʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
成骨；骨生成
The formation and development of bone tissue.  This process involves the differentiation of osteoblasts, which synthesize and deposit the bone matrix.
Osteogenesis imperfecta is a genetic disorder that affects bone formation.  成骨不全症是一种影响骨骼形成的遗传性疾病。
synonyms: bone formation, ossification
antonyms: bone resorption, osteolysis
词性: noun
