notion:/ˈnoʊʃən/ (US) /ˈnəʊʃən/ (UK)| n.|概念；想法；见解；意图|a conception or belief about something|例句：I have a notion that he's not telling the truth. 我认为他没有说实话。

例句：She had a vague notion of what she wanted to do. 她对自己想做什么只有一个模糊的概念。


近义词：idea, concept, belief, opinion, view, perception, impression|反义词：misconception, ignorance|词性：noun
