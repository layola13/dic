magnetizers:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtaɪzərz/
n.|磁化器，磁化者|A person or device that magnetizes something.  A magnetizer can be an electromagnet used in industry or a person who claims to imbue objects with magnetic properties.|The factory uses a powerful magnetizer to align the magnetic domains in the steel rods.|近义词:magnetizing equipment, magnetizing apparatus|反义词:demagnetizer|词性:名词

magnetizer:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtaɪzər/
n.|磁化器，磁化者|A device used to create a magnetic field, typically an electromagnet. Also, someone who performs the action of magnetizing.|The magnetizer created a strong magnetic field that aligned the iron filings.|近义词:magnetizing device, magnetizing apparatus|反义词:demagnetizer|词性:名词
