hunks:/hʌŋks/| n.|大块；猛男；帅哥|A large piece of something, especially food; An attractive, well-built man.|She gave me a huge hunk of cheese. 她给了我一大块奶酪。|近义词:chunk, piece, slab, hunk, stud, beefcake|反义词:slivers, fragment, shrimp, weakling|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *hunke "lump, hunch," probably from Proto-Germanic *hunko "hook, bend," from PIE *kenk- "to bend, curve." The sense of "attractive, well-built man" arose in the mid-20th century.
