furrowers:/ˈfɜːroʊərz/
n.
犁地的人，起皱纹的东西
People or things that make furrows (long narrow trenches or wrinkles).
The furrowers worked diligently in the field, preparing the soil for planting.  犁地的人在田里辛勤劳作，为播种准备土壤。
Age is one of the furrowers of the brow. 年龄是额头起皱纹的原因之一。
近义词:plowmen, cultivators
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smoothers")
词性:noun
