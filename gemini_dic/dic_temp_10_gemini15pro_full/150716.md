parabronchium:/ˌpærəˈbrɒŋkiəm/ | n. | 副支气管 | A small air passage in the lungs of birds, branching off from the secondary bronchi.  It is a site of gas exchange. |  The parabronchia are crucial for the efficient respiratory system of birds. 鸟类的副支气管对其高效的呼吸系统至关重要。|  The intricate network of parabronchia allows for unidirectional airflow in bird lungs. 副支气管的复杂网络使鸟类肺部的气流单向流动。 | synonyms: air capillary | antonyms: N/A | noun
