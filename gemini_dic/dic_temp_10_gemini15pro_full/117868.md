melodiograph:/məˈloʊdiəɡræf/| n. |旋律记录器，旋律描绘器|A device or system for recording or representing melodies.|The inventor demonstrated his new melodiograph, which could transcribe whistled tunes into musical notation. 发明家展示了他的新旋律记录器，它可以将口哨曲转录成乐谱。|近义词: melody writer, music notation software |反义词: N/A|名词
词源:  melodio- (from Greek "melos," meaning "song" or "melody") + -graph (from Greek "graphein," meaning "to write")
