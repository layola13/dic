exclusionism:/ɪkˈskluːʒənɪzəm/
n.
排外主义；排斥主义
The practice of excluding certain groups from a society, organization, or community.
The country's exclusionism towards immigrants sparked protests.  这个国家对移民的排外主义引发了抗议。
近义词：discrimination, prejudice, intolerance, bigotry, segregation
反义词：inclusion, integration, tolerance, acceptance
词性: 名词

词源:  From exclude + -ism.  Exclude comes from the Latin excludere "to shut out," from ex- "out" + claudere "to shut."
