overvaries:/ˌoʊvərˈvɛəriz/
verb
过度变化，变化过多
To vary or change excessively or more than is usual or necessary.
The thermostat overvaries, causing the temperature to fluctuate wildly.  恒温器变化过度，导致温度剧烈波动。
近义词: fluctuate, oscillate, waver
反义词: stabilize, steady, remain constant
词性:v.


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "vary" (verb meaning "to change or differ")
