crowing:/ˈkroʊɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkrəʊɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v. adj.| 欢呼，庆祝胜利| A triumphant cry or sound.| The team's supporters were crowing after their victory. 球队的支持者在他们胜利后欢呼雀跃。| Synonyms: cheering, celebrating, rejoicing | Antonyms: mourning, grieving | verb

crowing:/ˈkroʊɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkrəʊɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v. adj.| 最高的，顶端的| Situated at the top or highest point.| The crowing achievement of his career was winning the Nobel Prize. 他职业生涯的最高成就是获得诺贝尔奖。| Synonyms: topmost, highest, culminating | Antonyms: lowest, bottom | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crowinge*, verbal noun from crowen (“to crow”), from Old English crāwan (“to crow”), from Proto-Germanic *krāaną (“to crow”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“to cry, shout”).
