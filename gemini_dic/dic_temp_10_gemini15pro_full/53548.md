disband:/dɪsˈbænd/| v. |解散，遣散|To break up or dissolve (an organization, group, or assembly).|The club was disbanded due to lack of funding. 俱乐部由于缺乏资金而解散。|The army was disbanded after the war. 战后军队被解散了。|近义词：dismiss, dissolve, break up, scatter|反义词：assemble, gather, unite|词性:动词

disbanded:/dɪsˈbændɪd/| adj. |解散的，遣散的|Having been broken up or dissolved.|The disbanded soldiers returned to their homes. 解散的士兵们回到了他们的家园。|The disbanded orchestra members went their separate ways. 解散的管弦乐队成员各奔东西。|近义词：dissolved, broken up, scattered|反义词：assembled, gathered, united|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + band (a group of people).  Ultimately from Old French desbander and Germanic band.
