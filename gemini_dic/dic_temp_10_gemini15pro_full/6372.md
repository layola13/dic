amphicarpic: /ˌæmfɪˈkɑːrpɪk/
adjective
两型果实的; 异果的
Producing fruit of two kinds, especially one above and one below ground.
例句：Amphicarpic plants are fascinating because they have evolved this dual fruiting strategy.  两型果实植物很吸引人，因为它们进化出了这种双重结果策略。

例句：The peanut is a well-known example of an amphicarpic plant. 花生是两型果实植物的一个著名例子。
近义词: heterocarpic
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "homocarpic" if referring to plants producing only one type of fruit)
词性: adjective
