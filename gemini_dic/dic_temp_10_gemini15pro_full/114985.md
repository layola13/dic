mancus:/ˈmæŋkəs/| adj.|有缺陷的，残缺的|Defective or imperfect, especially lacking a part; mutilated.  Referring especially to coins, clipped or otherwise damaged, and hence reduced in value.|The museum acquired a mancus denarius from the Roman era.  博物馆获得了一枚罗马时代的残缺便士。|近义词：deficient, flawed, imperfect, incomplete|反义词：perfect, intact, whole|形容词
