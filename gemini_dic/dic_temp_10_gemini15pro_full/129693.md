non-dissipative:/ˌnɒn dɪˈsɪpətɪv/ | adj. |非耗散的，无损耗的 |  Not dissipative; conserving energy within a system. It refers to systems where energy is not lost through processes like friction or heat.  |  A non-dissipative system, such as a perfect pendulum in a vacuum, will oscillate indefinitely.  一个非耗散系统，例如真空中的理想摆，将无限期地振荡。 | conservative, lossless | dissipative | adjective
