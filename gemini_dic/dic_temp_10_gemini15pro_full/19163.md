词源：Old English bewarian "guard against," from be- "thoroughly" + warian "be cautious."


注意事项： "Bewared" is an archaic form, rarely used in modern English. "Beware" is the more common and current form.  The verb "beware" is most often used in the imperative mood (e.g., "Beware of the dog") or followed by "of" + a noun or gerund.  It is not commonly used in continuous tenses.
