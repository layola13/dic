bye:/baɪ/
interjection
再见，告别语
Used to express farewell.
Bye, see you later! 再见，回头见！
Bye, have a good day! 再见，祝你今天愉快！
近义词:goodbye, farewell, so long, see you, adieu
反义词:hello, hi, greetings
词性:interjection
