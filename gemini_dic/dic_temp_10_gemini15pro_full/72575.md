fluxes:/ˈflʌksɪz/
n.|流量; 变迁; 不断的变动; 熔剂|a continuous change or movement; a substance added to a metal to help it melt more easily|The magnetic fluxes were measured. 磁通量被测量了。|
近义词:flows, changes, variations, oscillations, instabilities, shifts, transitions, transformations, alterations, mutations, developments, evolutions, tides, currents, streams, surges, fluctuations
反义词:constants, stabilities, stillnesses
词性:名词 (plural of flux)

flux:/flʌks/
n.|流量; 变迁; 不断的变动; 熔剂|a continuous change or movement; a substance added to a metal to help it melt more easily|The market is in constant flux. 市场处于不断变化中。|
近义词:flow, change, variation, oscillation, instability, shift, transition, transformation, alteration, mutation, development, evolution, tide, current, stream, surge, fluctuation
反义词:constant, stability, stillness
词性:名词

flux:/flʌks/
v.|熔化; 变成液体; 用焊剂处理|to melt or become liquid; to treat with a flux|The metal fluxed easily. 金属很容易熔化。|
近义词:melt, liquefy, fuse
反义词:solidify, freeze
词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *fluxus*, from Latin fluxus (“a flowing”), past participle of fluere (“to flow”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlewh₁- (“to swell, overflow”).
