chlorous:/ˈklɔːrəs/| adj.|亚氯的；含三价氯的|Containing or derived from chlorine, especially in its +3 oxidation state.  It refers to compounds where chlorine has an oxidation state of +3.|Sodium chlorite is a chlorous compound. 亚氯酸钠是一种亚氯化合物。|近义词:chlorite (as in chlorite ion)|反义词:chloric, perchloric|词性:adjective
