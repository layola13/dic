coursework:/ˈkɔːrswɜːrk/ (US) /ˈkɔːswɜːk/ (UK)| n.|课程作业；功课|Written or practical work done by students during a course of study. |Example:  The coursework for this semester includes three essays and a presentation.  这学期的课程作业包括三篇论文和一次演讲。

Example:  I'm struggling to keep up with all the coursework. 我很难跟上所有的课程作业。|Synonym:  assignment, homework, schoolwork, studies|Antonym:  exam, test|noun
