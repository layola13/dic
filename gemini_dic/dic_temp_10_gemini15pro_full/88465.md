histologies:/hɪˈstɑːlədʒiz/
n.
组织学（复数）
the study of the microscopic structure of tissues
例句：The histologies of the tumor samples revealed cancerous cells. 肿瘤样本的组织学检查显示癌细胞。
例句：Comparative histologies of different species can provide insights into evolutionary relationships. 不同物种的比较组织学可以提供对进化关系的洞察。
近义词：microscopic anatomy, tissue biology
反义词：(No direct antonym, but perhaps macroscopic anatomy)
词性：noun
