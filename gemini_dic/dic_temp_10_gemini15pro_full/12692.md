assentors:/əˈsɛntərz/
n.
同意者，赞同者
Those who agree to or approve of something.
The assentors to the agreement signed their names at the bottom. 协议的同意者在底部签了名。
近义词：approvers, supporters, concurring parties
反义词： dissenters, objectors, opponents
词性: 名词(plural)

assentor:/əˈsɛntər/
n.
同意者，赞同者
Someone who agrees to or approves of something.
He was a reluctant assentor to the plan. 他很不情愿地同意了这个计划。
近义词：approver, supporter, concurring party
反义词： dissenter, objector, opponent
词性: 名词(singular)

词源: From the verb "assent," which comes from the Old French "assentir," meaning "to agree to" or "to feel," ultimately from the Latin "assentire," meaning "to think the same" (ad- "to" + sentire "to feel").
