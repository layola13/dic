discoverably:/dɪˈskʌvərəbli/
adv.
可发现地，明显地
in a way that can be discovered or noticed
The flaw in the system was discoverably present. 系统中的缺陷显而易见。
近义词：noticeably, perceptibly, detectably
反义词：inconspicuously, subtly, imperceptibly
词性：副词

词源：
From discoverable + -ly. Discoverable is from discover + -able. Discover comes from Old French descovrir, from des- "un-" + covrir "to cover".
