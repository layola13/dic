erector:/ɪˈrɛktər/| n.|安装工，勃起肌，竖立装置|A person or thing that erects something, especially a structure or building. erector:/ɪˈrɛktər/| n.|竖立装置|A device or mechanism that erects something.|The crane's erector arm lifted the heavy beam into place. 起重机的竖立臂将沉重的梁吊到位。|Synonyms: hoist, lift, crane|Antonyms: lowering device|noun
