banks:/bæŋks/ | n. | 银行；堤岸；岸；河畔 |A financial institution that accepts deposits and channels the money into lending activities.  The land alongside a river or lake.|  例句：I went to the bank to deposit a check. 我去银行存了一张支票。

banks:/bæŋks/ | n. | 银行；堤岸；岸；河畔 |The rising banks of the river.  河岸高耸。| 例句：The house is on the banks of the River Thames. 这房子位于泰晤士河畔。| 近义词：shore, riverside, financial institution | 反义词：None | 词性: Noun


banks:/bæŋks/ | v. | (飞机)倾斜飞行；堆积；(火)烧旺 |To tilt an aircraft in flight, especially to make a turn. To heap something up. To add fuel to a fire to make it burn more strongly.| 例句：The plane banked sharply to the left. 飞机猛地向左倾斜。|例句: She banked the fire for the night. 她把火堆积起来过夜。| 近义词：tilt, lean, pile, heap | 反义词：None | 词性: Verb


Etymology: From Old Norse *bakki (“ridge, bank”), from Proto-Germanic *bankiz (“slope, mound”).
