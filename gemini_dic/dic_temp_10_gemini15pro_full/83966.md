half-contentednesses:/ˌhæf kənˈtɛntɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
一半的满足感；不太满足的状态
A state of being only partially satisfied or content.
She felt a sense of half-contentedness, happy with some aspects of her life but yearning for more. 她感到一种半满足的状态，对生活的一些方面感到高兴，但也渴望更多。
The half-contentedness in his smile suggested he was settling for less than he desired.  他笑容中流露出的些许满足表明，他正在接受低于他期望的结果。
Near synonyms: partial contentment, mild satisfaction, qualified happiness
Near antonyms: complete contentment, utter satisfaction, total happiness
noun
