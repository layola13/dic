limitingly:/ˈlɪmɪtɪŋli/
adv.
限制地，限定地
in a way that limits something; to a limited extent.
His diet was limitingly small. 他的饮食受到很大的限制。
近义词: restrictively, narrowly, confiningly
反义词: expansively, broadly, limitlessly
词性: 副词

Etymology:  From "limiting" + "-ly." "Limiting" comes from the verb "limit," which derives from the Old French "limite" and ultimately from the Latin "limes," meaning "boundary" or "limit."  The suffix "-ly" is commonly used to form adverbs.
