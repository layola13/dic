despumation:/ˌdiːspjʊˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |去沫，除沫；澄清|The process of removing scum, froth, or foam from a liquid.|Despumation is a crucial step in the winemaking process to remove unwanted substances.  去沫是酿酒过程中去除不需要物质的关键步骤。|Synonyms: clarification, skimming, purification|Antonyms: foaming, frothing|noun
