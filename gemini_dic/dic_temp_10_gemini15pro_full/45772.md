cusp:/kʌsp/| n.|尖端，顶点，分界线|a pointed end where two curves meet.  The point of transition between two different states.|例句：He stood on the cusp of a new career. 他站在了新事业的起点。|例句：The country is on the cusp of a major political change.  这个国家正处于重大政治变革的边缘。|近义词：point, apex, verge, brink, edge|反义词:nadir, bottom, trough|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cuspis* "point, spear, pointed end."
