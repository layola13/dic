hobbyhorse:/ˈhɒbihɔːs/ (UK), /ˈhɑbihɔːrs/ (US)| n. |爱好；癖好；反复谈论的话题；竹马|A favorite pastime or topic of conversation that someone frequently returns to. |例句：He's always riding his hobbyhorse about the importance of organic food. 他总是反复谈论有机食品的重要性。

例句：Photography is her hobbyhorse. 摄影是她的爱好。|近义词：pastime, interest, obsession, preoccupation|反义词：disinterest, aversion|词性：名词
