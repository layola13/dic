cense:/sens/
n.
香, 香气, 焚香, 奉献
An offering of incense.
例句：The cense filled the temple with a sweet aroma.  庙里充满了香的甜美气息。
近义词：incense, aroma, fragrance
反义词: stench, odor

cense:/sɛns/
v.
焚香, 用香薰
To perfume with incense.
例句：The priest censed the altar.  神父在祭坛上焚香。
近义词：perfume, fumigate
