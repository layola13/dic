idealize:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪz/
verb
理想化，将…理想化
To regard or represent as perfect or better than in reality.
She tends to idealize her childhood. 她倾向于将她的童年理想化。
He idealized the rural life, not knowing how hard it really was. 他把乡村生活理想化了，并不知道真实的乡村生活有多么艰辛。
近义词: romanticize, glorify, exalt
反义词: vilify, denigrate, disparage
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *idéalisé*, past participle of *idéaliser*, from *idéal* (“ideal”) + *-iser*.  Ultimately from idea.
