credit-worthy:/ˈkrɛdɪtˌwɜːrði/
adj.
值得信贷的; 信用可靠的
able to be trusted to repay a loan
The bank considers him a credit-worthy customer.  银行认为他是一位信用可靠的客户。

John's excellent credit score made him a credit-worthy borrower in the eyes of the lender.  约翰优秀的信用评分使他成为贷款人眼中值得信贷的借款人。


近义词:creditable, reliable, trustworthy, dependable
反义词:unworthy, untrustworthy, unreliable, risky


词性:adjective (形容词)
