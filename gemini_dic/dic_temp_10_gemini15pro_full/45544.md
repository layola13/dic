curiouser:/ˈkjʊəriəsər/| adj.|更奇怪的，更好奇的|A nonstandard or humorous form of "more curious," used to emphasize the degree of curiosity.  It is considered grammatically incorrect in formal writing but acceptable in informal contexts, especially in creative writing.|Example: "Curiouser and curiouser!" cried Alice (in Wonderland).  爱丽丝（在仙境中）喊道：“越来越奇怪了！”|Example:  He gave me a curiouser look than usual. 他给了我一个比平常更奇怪的眼神。|近义词：more curious, stranger, odder|反义词:less curious, more normal, more ordinary|词性:形容词
