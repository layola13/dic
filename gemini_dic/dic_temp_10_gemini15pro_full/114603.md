maledicency:/ˌmælɪˈdɪksənsi/
n.
诽谤；咒骂；恶言
The act of speaking evil or uttering curses.
His maledicency towards his rivals was well known. 他对竞争对手的恶毒咒骂是众所周知的。
Synonyms: slander, defamation, calumny, vilification, vituperation
Antonyms: praise, commendation, eulogy, compliment
noun
