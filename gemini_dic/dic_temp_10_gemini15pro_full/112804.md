luce:/lus/ /ljuːs/ | n. | 光; 鱼; 梭子鱼; |A type of freshwater fish, the pike.  Also, a literary term for light, often used poetically or archaically.|The luce flashed silver in the clear water.  梭子鱼在清澈的水中闪着银光。|Synonyms: pike, jack (for the fish); light, radiance, gleam (for light) |Antonyms: darkness, shadow (for light)| noun
