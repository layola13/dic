imaginativenesses:/ɪˌmædʒɪnətɪvnəsɪz/ | n. |想象力，创造力 (复数形式，罕用) | The quality of being imaginative; the ability to create new ideas or images. |  His imaginativenesses know no bounds, constantly generating new worlds and characters. 他的种种想象力无边无际，不断创造新的世界和人物。

 It's rare to see such imaginativenesses in someone so young.  很少见到这么年轻的人有如此丰富的想象力。(注意：这个词的复数形式非常罕见，通常使用单数形式 imaginativeness) | 近义词：imagination, creativity, inventiveness, ingenuity, originality | 反义词：lack of imagination, unimaginativeness, dullness, banality | 词性: 名词


词源:  From imaginative + -ness.  "imaginative" comes from  "imagine" (from Latin imaginari "to form an image, represent, conceive, imagine," from imago "image, likeness").
