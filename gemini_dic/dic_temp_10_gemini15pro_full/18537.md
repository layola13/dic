beneficently:/ˌbɛnɪˈfɪsntlɪ/
adverb
仁慈地，慈善地
in a way that is kind and helpful
He smiled beneficently at the children. 他慈祥地对孩子们微笑。
Synonyms: kindly, charitably, generously, philanthropically
Antonyms: cruelly, harshly, unkindly, maliciously
词性: 副词
