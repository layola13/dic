marchlike:/ˈmɑːrtʃlaɪk/ | adj. | 行军般的，像行军一样 | Resembling a march; marked by a steady, rhythmic, and often forceful or purposeful quality. |  The protesters moved with a marchlike precision. 抗议者以行军般的精确度前进。 |  The music had a marchlike rhythm.  这音乐有进行曲般的节奏。 |  近义词：military, martial, rhythmic, measured | 反义词：erratic, irregular, unsteady | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "march" (n. & v.) + "-like" (suffix meaning "resembling").  Ultimately from Old French "marcher" (to walk, tread).
