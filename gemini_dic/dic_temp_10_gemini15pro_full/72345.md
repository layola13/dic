flowerers:/ˈflaʊərərz/
n.
开花植物；养花人
Plants that produce flowers; People who cultivate flowers.
The flowerers in the garden are blooming beautifully.  花园里的开花植物正在盛开。
Experienced flowerers know the best time to prune roses. 有经验的养花人知道修剪玫瑰的最佳时间。
近义词: bloomers,  florists (for people)
反义词: None (for plants), non-gardeners (for people)
词性: Noun
