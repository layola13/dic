lesserness:/ˈlɛsɚnɪs/
n.
次要；劣势；较差
The state or quality of being lesser in importance, size, or degree.
His sense of lesserness made him avoid social gatherings. 他自卑感让他避免社交聚会。
近义词: inferiority, insignificance, secondary status
反义词: superiority, greatness, importance
词性: 名词

lesser:/ˈlɛsər/
adj.
较小的；次要的；更少的
Smaller in size, amount, or importance.
He chose the lesser of two evils. 他选择了两个坏选项中较轻的那个。
近义词: smaller, minor, inferior
反义词: greater, major, superior
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Middle English *lesserness, equivalent to lesser +‎ -ness.  Lesser itself comes from Old English læssa (“less”), comparative of *læs (“little”).
