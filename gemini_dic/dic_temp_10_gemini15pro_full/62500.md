endotheloid:/ˌɛndoʊˈθiːlɔɪd/
adjective
内皮样的，类似内皮的
Resembling or having the appearance of endothelial cells.
The tumor was composed of endotheloid cells.  肿瘤由内皮样细胞组成。
The biopsy revealed endotheloid differentiation. 活检显示内皮样分化。
近义词: endothelial-like
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From endo- (within) + -thel- (nipple, layer of cells) + -oid (resembling).  The term "endothelial" refers to the thin layer of cells that lines the interior surface of blood vessels and lymphatic vessels.
