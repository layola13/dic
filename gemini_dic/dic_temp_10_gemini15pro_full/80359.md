goldsmiths: /ˈɡoʊldˌsmɪθs/ /ˈɡəʊldˌsmɪθs/ | n. |金匠；金饰店；金匠行业| A goldsmith is a person who makes or repairs gold jewelry and other objects made of gold.  Goldsmiths can also refer to a shop where such items are sold or the craft itself. |  The goldsmith crafted a beautiful necklace. 金匠制作了一条漂亮的项链。

The goldsmiths in this town are known for their intricate designs.  这个镇上的金匠以其复杂的设计而闻名。| 近义词: jeweler, silversmith | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider something like "customer," "buyer," or a profession unrelated to metalworking) | 词性: noun

词源:
From Old English *goldsmiþ, from gold (gold) + smiþ (smith).  The word "smith" originally referred to someone who works with metal.
