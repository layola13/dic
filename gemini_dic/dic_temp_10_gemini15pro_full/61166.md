elucidate:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/| v. |阐明，解释|to make something clear; explain|例句：The professor elucidated the complex theory with clear examples. 教授用清晰的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。

例句：Could you please elucidate your point further? 你能进一步阐明你的观点吗？


elucidated:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪd/| v. |已阐明，已被解释|past participle of elucidate|例句：The mystery was finally elucidated after years of investigation.  经过多年的调查，这个谜团终于被解开了。

例句：The origins of the universe are still not fully elucidated. 宇宙的起源仍未完全阐明。


elucidating:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |阐明，解释|present participle of elucidate|例句：He is elucidating the principles of quantum physics. 他正在解释量子物理学的原理。


例句：The speaker was elucidating the main points of the argument. 演讲者正在阐明论证的要点。



近义词：explain, clarify, expound, illuminate|
反义词：obfuscate, confuse, complicate|
词性：verb (past participle form: elucidated, present participle form: elucidating)


词源：From Latin *elucidatus, past participle of elucidare "to make clear, brighten," from e- "out" + lucidus "clear, bright," from lucere "to shine."
