diathermic:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmɪk/
adjective
透热的，热的能穿过的
Allowing heat to pass through; relating to the passage of heat through a substance.
Diathermic walls allow heat to flow freely between systems.  透热壁允许热量在系统之间自由流动。
The diathermic container allowed the contents to quickly reach room temperature. 透热的容器使内容物迅速达到室温。
近义词:thermally conductive, heat-transmitting
反义词:adiabatic, insulating
词性:adjective

diathermanous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmənəs/
adjective
(主要指对辐射热)透热的
Allowing radiant heat to pass through.  (主要指对辐射热)透热的
Diathermanous materials are used in infrared saunas. 透热材料用于红外线桑拿房。
The diathermanous window allowed sunlight to warm the room. 透热的窗户让阳光温暖了房间。
近义词:transparent to radiant heat
反义词:opaque to radiant heat
词性:adjective


词源:
From Greek dia- "through" + therme "heat".  来自希腊语 dia- “穿过” + therme “热”。
