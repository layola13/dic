alunogen:/əˈluːnədʒən/
n.
毛矾石, 羽毛矾
A hydrous aluminum sulfate mineral, Al2(SO4)3·17H2O, occurring as a white, fibrous, or granular efflorescence in shales and other rocks containing pyrite.
Alunogen is a soft, white mineral often found in volcanic areas. 毛矾石是一种柔软的白色矿物，常见于火山地区。
Alunogen can form as an alteration product of pyrite. 毛矾石可以由黄铁矿蚀变形成。
Synonym: hair salt, feather alum
