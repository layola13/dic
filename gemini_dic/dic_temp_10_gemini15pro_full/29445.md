catalogue:/ˈkætəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈkætəlɔːɡ/ (US)| n. v.|目录；系列；种类|A complete list of items, typically one in alphabetical or other systematic order, in a printed form or available as an electronic document.|The library has a catalogue of all its books. 图书馆有所有书籍的目录。|synonyms: list, directory, register, index, inventory|antonyms: disorder, chaos|noun
