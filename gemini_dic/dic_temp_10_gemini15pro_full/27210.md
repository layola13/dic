callousness:/ˈkæləsnəs/ | n. |  冷酷无情；无情；麻木不仁 |  The state of being insensitive and unsympathetic to the feelings of others.  A thickened and hardened part of the skin or soft tissue, especially in an area that has been subjected to friction.| He showed a callous disregard for her feelings. 他对她的感情表现出无情的漠视。 | insensitivity, apathy, indifference, heartlessness | sensitivity, compassion, empathy, tenderness | noun
