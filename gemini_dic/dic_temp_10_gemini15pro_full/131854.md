nonconstructive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
无建设性的，没有帮助的
Not helpful or likely to produce a good result.
His criticism was completely nonconstructive. 他的批评完全没有建设性。
Her nonconstructive comments only made the situation worse. 她没有建设性的评论只会让情况变得更糟。
近义词:unhelpful, unproductive, negative, counterproductive
反义词:constructive, helpful, productive, positive
词性:adjective
