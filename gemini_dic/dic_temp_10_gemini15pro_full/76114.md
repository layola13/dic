furthermore:/ˌfɜːrðərˈmɔːr/| adv.|此外，而且|Used to introduce a new point in support of an argument or discussion.  It indicates addition to what has already been said.|例句：The house is too small; furthermore, it's too far from the city. 这房子太小了；而且，离城市也太远了。

例句：He's intelligent, hardworking, and, furthermore, he's very reliable. 他聪明、勤奋，而且非常可靠。

近义词：moreover, in addition, besides, also|反义词:however, nevertheless, nonetheless|词性:副词
