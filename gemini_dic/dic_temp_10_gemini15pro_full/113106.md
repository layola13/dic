lunchroom: /ˈlʌntʃruːm/, /ˈlʌntʃrʊm/ | n. | 餐厅，午餐室 | A room, especially in a school or workplace, where people eat lunch. |  The students gathered in the lunchroom for their midday meal. 学生们聚集在餐厅吃午饭。
