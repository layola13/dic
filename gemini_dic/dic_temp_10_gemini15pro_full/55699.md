diuretics:/ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪks/ | n. | 利尿剂 | Medications that increase the rate of urination.  They are used to treat conditions like high blood pressure, heart failure, and edema by removing excess fluid from the body. | Example 1:  The doctor prescribed diuretics to help control his blood pressure. 医生开了利尿剂来帮助控制他的血压。

Example 2:  Diuretics can be helpful in reducing swelling in the legs and ankles. 利尿剂可以帮助减轻腿部和脚踝的肿胀。 | Synonyms: water pills | Antonyms: antidiuretics | Noun
