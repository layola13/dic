designs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/
n.|设计；图案；计划；意图|A drawing or outline showing how something is to be made and how it will work or look. |The architect showed us the designs for the new building. 建筑师向我们展示了新建筑的设计图。|plans, blueprints, drawings, sketches, patterns|n/a|noun
designs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/
v.|设计；构思；计划；预定|To make or draw plans for something, for example clothes or buildings. |She designs clothes for children. 她设计童装。|create, plan, conceive, develop, engineer|destroy, ruin|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "deisignier" (to designate, appoint, describe), from Latin "designare" (to mark out, designate), from "de-" (out, from) + "signare" (to mark).
