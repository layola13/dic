noninformatively:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɔːmətɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈfɔːrmətɪvli/ | adv. | 无信息地，没有提供任何信息地 | In a way that does not provide any useful or relevant information.  | The witness testified noninformatively, offering little of substance to the case.  证人提供的证词没有信息量，对案件几乎没有实质性帮助。 |  The spokesperson answered the questions noninformatively, dodging the real issues. 发言人对问题的回答没有提供任何信息，回避了真正的问题。 | synonym: meaninglessly, unhelpfully, vaguely | antonym: informatively, helpfully, clearly | adverb
