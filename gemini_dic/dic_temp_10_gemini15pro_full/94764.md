improvisation:/ˌɪmprəvɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 即兴创作，即兴表演 | the act of improvising; something that is improvised |  The musician's improvisation on the piano was breathtaking.  这位音乐家在钢琴上的即兴演奏令人叹为观止。 | synonyms: ad-libbing, extemporization, off-the-cuff | antonyms: preparation, rehearsal | noun


improvise:/ˈɪmprəvaɪz/ | v. | 即兴创作，即兴表演 | to create and perform (music, drama, or verse) spontaneously or without preparation |  He had to improvise a speech when the scheduled speaker failed to show up.  当预定的演讲者没有出现时，他不得不即兴演讲。 | synonyms: ad-lib, extemporize, wing it | antonyms: plan, rehearse, prepare | verb
