centrifuging: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒɪŋ/ | n. | 离心 | the process of separating substances of different densities by whirling them in a machine called a centrifuge | The blood sample was separated into its components by centrifuging.  血液样本通过离心分离成不同的成分。| synonyms: spinning, whirling, rotating | antonyms: sedimentation, precipitation | noun

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ | n. vt. | 离心机；用离心机分离 | a machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.  |  The lab uses a centrifuge to separate blood cells from plasma.  实验室使用离心机将血细胞从血浆中分离出来。|  We centrifuged the mixture to isolate the desired compound. 我们用离心机分离混合物以提取所需的化合物。 | synonyms: separator, extractor | antonyms: integrator, combiner | noun, verb
