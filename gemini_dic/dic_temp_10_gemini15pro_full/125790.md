muricoid:/mjʊˈrɪkɔɪd/| adj. n.|类骨螺的，骨螺超科动物|Relating to or resembling the Muricidae, a family of predatory sea snails; a member of the Muricidae family.|The muricoid shells are often elaborately sculptured. 这些骨螺科贝壳通常雕刻精美。|The fisherman collected a variety of muricoid snails. 渔夫收集了各种各样的骨螺科蜗牛。|近义词：murex, rock shell|反义词:|adj. n.
詞源-> From Latin *murex* (purple dye, shellfish from which purple dye was obtained) + *-oid* (resembling).
