caftan:/ˈkæftæn/| n. |长袍，罩袍|A long, loose robe or gown, typically with long sleeves, worn by men and women in some Eastern countries.|She wore a brightly colored caftan to the beach party. 她穿着一件颜色鲜艳的罩袍去参加沙滩派对。|例句：The sultan appeared in a magnificent silk caftan.  苏丹身穿华丽的丝绸长袍出现。|近义词：robe, gown, tunic|反义词:|n.
