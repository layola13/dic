例句：The orchestra's concertion for the upcoming symphony was intense and productive.  管弦乐队为即将到来的交响乐演出进行了紧张而富有成果的合练。| 近义词：cooperation, collaboration, joint effort, teamwork | 反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict | 词性: 名词
