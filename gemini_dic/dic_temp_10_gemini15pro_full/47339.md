de-shells:/diːˈʃɛlz/
verb
去壳，脱壳
Removes the shells from something, typically nuts or shellfish.
The machine de-shells peanuts efficiently.  这台机器能有效地去花生壳。
The chef de-shelled the prawns before grilling them.  厨师在烤虾之前先去掉了虾壳。
Synonyms: shell, husk, peel, shuck
Antonyms: shell, encase
词性:v.
