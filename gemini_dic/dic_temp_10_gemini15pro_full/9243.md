antipathetical:/ˌænti.pəˈθɛt.ɪ.kəl/
adjective
相反的，对立的，格格不入的
Showing or expressing strong opposition or dislike.  Directly opposed.
His views are antipathetical to mine. 他的观点与我的截然相反。
synonyms: opposed, hostile, averse, antagonistic, contrary, conflicting, incompatible, irreconcilable
antonyms: sympathetic, similar, alike, compatible, harmonious
词性: adjective
