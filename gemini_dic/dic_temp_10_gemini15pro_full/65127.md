ethicalities:/ˈeθɪkælɪtiz/
noun
伦理问题；道德方面；伦理体系
The ethicalities of using animals in scientific research are complex and often debated.  在科学研究中使用动物的伦理问题很复杂，经常引起争论。
The ethicalities of the situation were discussed at length.  该情况的伦理问题得到了详细讨论。
近义词:ethics, morals, morality
反义词: immorality, unethicalness
词性:n.
