overspeculations: /ˌoʊvərˌspɛkjʊˈleɪʃənz/
noun
过度推测，过分臆测
Excessive speculation, often based on insufficient or unreliable information.
The market crash was caused by overspeculations in the housing market. 房市崩盘是由于对房地产市场的过度投机引起的。
Overspeculations about the company's future led to a volatile stock price. 对公司未来的过度猜测导致股价波动。
synonyms: conjecture, surmise, guesswork, supposition
antonyms: facts, data, evidence, certainty
n.
