overproduction:/ˌoʊvərprəˈdʌkʃən/| n. |过度生产，生产过剩|The overproduction of goods led to a drop in prices. 商品的过度生产导致价格下跌。|例句：The overproduction of agricultural products resulted in lower prices for farmers.  农产品生产过剩导致农民的价格降低。

例句：The factory's overproduction of widgets led to a surplus in the warehouse.  这家工厂过度生产小部件导致仓库积压。|近义词：surplus, glut, excess|反义词: shortage, scarcity, underproduction|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + production.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Production" comes from the Latin word "producere," meaning "to lead forth" or "to bring forth."
