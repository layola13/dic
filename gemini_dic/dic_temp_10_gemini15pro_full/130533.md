nonaccumulative:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːmjʊlətɪv/
adjective
非累积的，非累加的
Not accumulating or tending to accumulate; not increasing in quantity or intensity over time.
The effects of the drug are nonaccumulative, meaning they wear off quickly.  这种药物的作用是非累积的，意味着它们会很快消失。
This type of paint has a nonaccumulative drying process, allowing for easy layering. 这种油漆具有非累积干燥过程，易于分层。
