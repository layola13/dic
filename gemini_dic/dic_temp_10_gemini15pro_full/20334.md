bisie:/ˈbɪzi/| adj.|忙碌的，繁忙的|Occupied with or involved in activity; working hard.|例句：I'm very bisie today with work. 我今天工作很忙。

例句：She has a bisie schedule this week. 她这周行程很满。|近义词：occupied, engaged, swamped, bustling|反义词：idle, free, unoccupied|词性：形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *bisi*, from Old English *bysg* (“busy, active, diligent”), from Proto-Germanic *busgiz* (“careful, anxious”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewg- (“to bend, turn, flex”).  Related to Old Frisian *bisi* (“busy”), Dutch *bezig* (“busy”), German *beschäftigt* (“busy, occupied”).
