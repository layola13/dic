glares:/ɡlɛərz/| n.| 怒视，瞪眼；刺眼的光 |A glare is a long, angry stare.  It can also refer to a strong, unpleasant light.|He gave me a fierce glare. 他狠狠地瞪了我一眼。|The sun's glare reflected off the water. 阳光的刺眼光芒反射在水面上。|近义词：stare, scowl, glower, blaze, dazzle|反义词：glance, glimpse|词性:名词

glares:/ɡlɛərz/| v.| 怒视，瞪眼；发出刺眼的光 |To glare is to stare angrily and intensely. It can also mean to shine with a strong, harsh light.|She glared at him across the table. 她隔着桌子怒视着他。|The sun glared down on the desert. 太阳炙烤着沙漠。|近义词：stare, scowl, glower, blaze, dazzle|反义词：glance, glimpse|词性:动词
