abscision: /æbˈsɪʒən/
n.
脱落，切除
The natural abscission of leaves occurs in autumn. 秋天树叶自然脱落。
The abscission of the diseased limb is necessary. 切除患病的肢体是必要的。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscissio (“a cutting off”), from abscindere (“to cut off”), from ab- (“away from”) + scindere (“to cut, split”).

USAGE->
The process of abscission is crucial for plant survival. 脱落过程对植物的生存至关重要。
The surgeon performed an abscission of the tumor. 外科医生进行了肿瘤切除手术。

The premature abscission of fruit can reduce crop yields.  果实过早脱落会降低作物产量。
Researchers are studying the hormonal control of leaf abscission. 研究人员正在研究激素对落叶的控制。

The abscission of the lower leaves allows more sunlight to reach the developing fruit. 下部叶片的脱落可以让更多的阳光照射到正在发育的果实。
The gardener noticed the abscission of several branches after the storm. 园丁注意到暴风雨后有几个树枝脱落了。


SYNONYM->
separation, detachment, shedding, cutting off
ANTONYM->
attachment, adherence, connection
词性: noun
