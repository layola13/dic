carabao:/ˌkærəˈbaʊ/| n.|水牛|A domesticated water buffalo (Bubalus bubalis) found in the Philippines and other parts of Southeast Asia, used as a beast of burden and for its milk and meat.|The farmer plowed his field with a carabao.  农民用水牛耕地。|Synonyms: water buffalo, Asian buffalo|Antonyms: N/A|noun
