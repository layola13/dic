inadvertent:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtənt/| adj.|不注意的，疏忽的，无意的|Not resulting from or achieved through deliberate planning.|She made an inadvertent remark that hurt her friend's feelings. 她无意中说了一句伤害她朋友感情的话。|Synonyms: unintentional, accidental, unplanned, unintended|Antonyms: deliberate, intentional, purposeful, conscious|adjective

inadvertently:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtəntli/| adv.|不注意地，疏忽地，无意地|Without intention; accidentally.|He inadvertently deleted the file. 他不小心删除了文件。|Synonyms: unintentionally, accidentally, unconsciously|Antonyms: deliberately, intentionally, purposely|adverb
