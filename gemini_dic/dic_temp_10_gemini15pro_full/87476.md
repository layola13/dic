hetaera:/hɪˈtaɪrə/ /hiːˈtɪərə/
n.
高级妓女；艺妓
A highly educated and accomplished courtesan in ancient Greece, often skilled in music, poetry, and conversation.
Aspasia, the partner of Pericles, was a renowned hetaera.  阿斯帕西娅，伯里克利的伴侣，是一位著名的艺妓。
The hetaera entertained the men with her wit and knowledge of philosophy.  这位艺妓以她的智慧和哲学知识娱乐了男人们。
近义词：courtesan, concubine
反义词: wife, matron
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἑταίρα (hetaíra, “female companion”), from ἑταῖρος (hetaîros, “companion, comrade”).
