nonartistically:/ˌnɒnɑːˈtɪstɪkli/
adverb
非艺术地，缺乏艺术性地
Not in an artistic manner; without art or skill.
She arranged the flowers nonartistically, just stuffing them into the vase. 她把花插在花瓶里，一点也不讲究艺术，只是随便塞进去。
The cake was decorated nonartistically, but it tasted delicious.  蛋糕装饰得很粗糙，但味道很好吃。
Near Synonyms: clumsily, crudely, inartistically, artlessly, unskillfully
Antonyms: artistically, skillfully, artfully

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + artistically (adverb)
"non-" comes from Latin meaning "not"
"artistic" comes from Latin "ars" meaning "art"
