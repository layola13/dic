nobbler:/ˈnɒblə(r)/ (UK) /ˈnɑːblər/ (US)| n.|少量烈酒;  一口酒;  偷马贼;  阉割公马的人|A small glass of spirits, especially whisky. A person who steals horses. A person who castrates stallions.|He ordered a nobbler of whisky. 他点了一小杯威士忌。|Synonyms: tot, dram, nip, shot (for a small drink of spirits); horse thief, rustler (for someone who steals horses)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a small drink. For horse thief, perhaps "lawman")|noun

nobbler:/ˈnɒblə(r)/ (UK) /ˈnɑːblər/ (US)| n.|阉割过的公马|A castrated stallion.|The farmer used the nobbler for farm work. 农民用这匹阉割过的公马干农活。|Synonyms: gelding|Antonyms: stallion|noun
