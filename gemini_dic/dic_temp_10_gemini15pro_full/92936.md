identify:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| v. |识别，认出，确定|To recognize somebody/something and be able to say who or what they are.|The police officer identified the suspect from a photograph. 警察通过照片认出了嫌疑人。|recognize, pinpoint, distinguish|confuse, mistake|verb
identify:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| n. |身份，认同|The condition of having the same characteristics or qualities as something else.|She struggled to reconcile her public identify with her private self. 她努力使自己的公众形象与真实的自我保持一致。|identification, recognition|difference, distinction|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *identificāre, from Latin idem (“same”) + Late Latin facere (“to make”).
