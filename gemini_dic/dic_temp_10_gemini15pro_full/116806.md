maws:/mɔːz/| n. |嘴，咽喉；动物的胃；贪婪的欲望|The lion opened its maws wide and roared. 狮子张开大嘴咆哮。| 英文解析: Jaws, especially of a voracious animal; the throat, gullet, or jaws regarded as the entrance to the stomach and hence to the inner being; a gaping chasm or void.|例句：The monster's maws dripped with venom.  怪物的嘴里滴着毒液。

The earth opened its maws and swallowed the village. 大地张开大口，吞噬了村庄。|近义词：jaws, gullet|反义词:|词性:noun
