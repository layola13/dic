noncitation:/ˌnɑːnsaɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不引证，未引用 |  The act of not citing or referencing a source.  Lack of citation. |  His essay was criticized for its noncitation of primary sources. 他的论文因未引用主要来源而受到批评。| synonyms: omission of citation, lack of reference, failure to cite | antonyms: citation, referencing, attribution | noun
