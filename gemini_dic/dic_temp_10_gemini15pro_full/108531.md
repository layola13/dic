lazarlike:/ˈlɑːzərlaɪk/
adjective
像拉撒路一样的；复活的；死而复生的；奇迹般复苏的
Resembling Lazarus, the biblical figure raised from the dead by Jesus; relating to a miraculous recovery or resurrection, especially after a period of decline or inactivity.
The lazarlike recovery of the economy surprised everyone.  这经济奇迹般的复苏让每个人都感到惊讶。
The team's lazarlike performance in the second half secured their victory.  该队在下半场的起死回生般的表现确保了他们的胜利。
近义词:resurrected, revived, reborn
反义词:dead, deceased, defunct
词性:adjective

词源: From Lazarus, the name of the man raised from the dead by Jesus in the Gospel of John, + -like.
