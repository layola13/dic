monmouth:/ˈmɒnməθ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnməθ/ (US)| n. |蒙茅斯|A town in Wales, historically important as the birthplace of Henry V.  It also refers to the Battle of Monmouth, a major battle of the American Revolutionary War.|King Henry V was born in Monmouth.  亨利五世出生在蒙茅斯。|The Battle of Monmouth was fought in intense heat. 蒙茅斯战役是在酷热中进行的。|synonyms: town, city (general); birthplace (related to Henry V); battlefield (related to the battle)|antonyms: N/A (proper noun)|noun
