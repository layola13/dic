nonvirulent:/ˌnɒnˈvɪrʊlənt/ | /ˌnɑːnˈvɪrələnt/ | adj. | 无毒的；非毒性的 | Not virulent; not able to produce disease. |  The researchers used a nonvirulent strain of the virus for the experiment. 研究人员在实验中使用了一种无毒的病毒株。

|  This strain of bacteria is nonvirulent and poses no threat to human health.  这种细菌菌株是无毒的，不会对人类健康构成威胁。|  近义词：avirulent, harmless, innocuous | 反义词：virulent, toxic, pathogenic | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  非，相反的
virulent (adjective): extremely harmful or poisonous 剧毒的；有害的
