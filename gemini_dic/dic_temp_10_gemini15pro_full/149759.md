palatial:/pəˈleɪʃl/| adj.|富丽堂皇的；宏伟的；宫殿般的|Resembling a palace in splendor or magnificence; stately.|例句：The celebrity lives in a palatial mansion overlooking the ocean.  这位名流住在一座俯瞰大海的富丽堂皇的豪宅里。

例句：The hotel lobby was palatial, with soaring ceilings and glittering chandeliers. 酒店的大堂富丽堂皇，有着高耸的天花板和闪闪发光的枝形吊灯。|近义词：grand, magnificent, luxurious, opulent, stately|反义词：humble, modest, simple, small|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin palatialis, from Latin palatium ‘palace’.
