editorialize:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪz/
verb
发表社论；在报道中发表个人意见
to express opinions rather than just report facts, especially in a newspaper or broadcast
The news anchor was criticized for editorializing during the report.  这位新闻主播因在报道中发表个人意见而受到批评。
The reporter tried not to editorialize, but his personal biases were evident. 这位记者尽量不发表个人意见，但他的个人偏见很明显。
synonyms: comment, opine, pontificate
antonyms: report, narrate, describe
词性: 动词
