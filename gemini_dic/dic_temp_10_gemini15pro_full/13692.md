attributes: /ˈætrɪbjuːts/ | n. | 属性，特性，特征 | qualities, characteristics, or features that belong to a person or thing |  The car has many desirable attributes, including excellent fuel economy. 这辆车有许多令人满意的特性，包括极佳的燃油经济性。 | synonyms: qualities, characteristics, traits, features | antonyms: defects, flaws | noun

/əˈtrɪbjuːt/ | vt. | 归因于，归咎于 | regard something as being caused by someone or something |  He attributed his success to hard work and dedication. 他将他的成功归因于努力工作和奉献精神。 | synonyms: ascribe, assign, credit, impute | antonyms: disavow, deny | verb
