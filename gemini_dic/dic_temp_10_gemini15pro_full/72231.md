floricomous: /ˌflɔːrɪˈkəʊməs/ /ˌflɔːrɪˈkɑːməs/
adjective
多花的；花盛开的；绚丽的
Covered with flowers; flowery; florid.
例句：The floricomous meadows in springtime were a sight to behold. 春天里百花盛开的草地是一道美丽的风景。

例句：Her floricomous prose was often criticized for being overly ornate. 她华丽的散文常因过于华丽而受到批评。

近义词：flowery, blooming, blossoming, ornate
反义词：barren, desolate, plain

词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *floricomus, from flōs (“flower”) + coma (“hair”).  Literally "having flowery hair."
