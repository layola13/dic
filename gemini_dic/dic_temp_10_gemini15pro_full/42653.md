counterstate:/ˈkaʊntərˌsteɪt/
n.|对立国，对抗国|A state or entity in opposition to another. |The small counterstate posed a significant threat to the larger empire.| 近义词:rival state, enemy state, adversary|反义词:allied state, friendly state|词性:名词

Etymology:
From counter- + state.  "Counter-" acts as a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite." "State" refers to a sovereign political entity.
