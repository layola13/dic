dismantlement:/dɪsˈmæntl̩mənt/
n.|拆除，拆卸，解散|The dismantlement of the nuclear weapons was a crucial step towards world peace. 核武器的拆除是迈向世界和平的关键一步。|The dismantlement of the company took several months.  公司的解散花了几个月的时间。|
近义词:disassembly, demolition, breaking up|
反义词:assembly, construction, establishment|
词性:noun


Etymology:
From dis- + mantle (verb), from Old French mantler "to cloak," from Latin mantum "cloak."  The original meaning was "to divest of a mantle or cloak."  Later, it extended to mean "to strip of equipment or defenses" and then to "take apart."
