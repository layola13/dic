hansard:/ˈhænzɑːrd/ | n. |议会记录，议会辩论记录 | An official verbatim record of debates in a legislature, especially that of the British, Canadian, Australian, New Zealand, or other Commonwealth parliaments. | The minister's comments can be found in Hansard. 部长的评论可以在议会记录中找到。 |  The opposition's arguments are detailed in Hansard. 反对派的论点在议会记录中有详细说明。| synonyms: transcript, record, minutes | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fiction", "fabrication") | noun
