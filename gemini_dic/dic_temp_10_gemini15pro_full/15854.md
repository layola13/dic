baller's:/ˈbɔːlərz/ | n. | 球员的；行家的；酷炫的 |  Possessive form of "baller," which itself is slang derived from "basketball player."  It signifies someone skilled, stylish, and successful, particularly in a flashy way.  It can refer to possessions or attributes of such a person. | That's a baller's car! = 那是一辆很酷的车！ |  stylish, cool, awesome, fly | dull, boring | noun
