cysticercus:/sɪsˈtɪsɜːrkəs/ | n. | 囊尾蚴，囊虫 | The larval stage of a tapeworm, consisting of a fluid-filled sac containing an inverted scolex. | Example 1: The pig was infected with cysticerci. 这头猪感染了囊尾蚴。

Example 2: The doctor found cysticerci in the patient's brain. 医生在病人的大脑中发现了囊尾蚴。 | 近义词: bladder worm | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

cysticercus:/sɪsˈtɪsɜːrkaɪ/ (plural) | n. | 囊尾蚴，囊虫（复数） | The larval stage of a tapeworm, consisting of a fluid-filled sac containing an inverted scolex. (plural form) | Example 1: Multiple cysticerci were found in the muscle tissue. 在肌肉组织中发现了多个囊尾蚴。

Example 2: The presence of cysticerci can cause serious health problems. 囊尾蚴的存在会导致严重的健康问题。 | 近义词: bladder worms | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin, from Ancient Greek *kystikerkos*  (kustikerkos) "bladder worm," from *kustis* (kustis) "bladder, pouch" + *kerkos* (kerkos)  "tail."
