deceptious:/dɪˈsɛpʃəs/
adjective
欺骗性的，虚假的
Giving a misleading impression; deceptive.
例句：The advertisement was deceptious, promising results that were impossible to achieve.  这广告是骗人的，承诺的结果根本不可能实现。

例句：The calm surface of the water was deceptious, hiding dangerous currents beneath.  平静的水面是具有欺骗性的，下面隐藏着危险的暗流。

近义词：misleading, deceitful, deceptive, fallacious, spurious
反义词：honest, truthful, genuine, authentic, real
词性: adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French decevable, from decever ‘deceive’.
