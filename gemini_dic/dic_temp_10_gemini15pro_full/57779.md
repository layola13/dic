drearily:/ˈdrɪərɪli/ | adv. | 沉闷地，阴郁地 | In a dreary manner; gloomily; dismally.  Characterized by or expressive of dreariness. | The rain fell drearily all day. 雨沉闷地下了一整天。 | 近义词: gloomily, sadly, dismally, dejectedly | 反义词: cheerfully, brightly, happily | 词性: 副词

dreary:/ˈdrɪəri/ | adj. | 沉闷的，阴郁的 | Causing sadness or gloom.  Dull, bleak, and lifeless; depressing. | It was a dreary day. 这是阴沉的一天。| 近义词: gloomy, dull, bleak, depressing | 反义词: cheerful, bright, happy | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *dreri, from Old English *drēorig (“sad, sorrowful, mournful, gory, bloody”), from Proto-Germanic *drauzijaz (“sad, troubled”). Cognate with Old Saxon drōrag (“sad, mournful”), Old Frisian drērich (“sad”), Dutch droevig (“sad, sorrowful”), German traurig (“sad, sorrowful”), Old Norse dreyrigr (“bloody”).
