loamy:/ˈloʊmi/ | adj. |肥沃的，壤土的 | Consisting of or similar to loam; rich in organic matter.  A soil composed of sand, silt, and clay in relatively even concentration. | The garden boasts rich, loamy soil, perfect for growing vegetables.  这个花园拥有肥沃的壤土，非常适合种植蔬菜。 | fertile, rich, productive | barren, infertile, sterile | adjective
