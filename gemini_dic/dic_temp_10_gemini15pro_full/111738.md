locus:/ˈloʊkəs/ (US), /ˈləʊkəs/ (UK)| n. |地点；场所；轨迹|A particular position, point, or place.  A place where something is situated or has been put.  The specific site of a particular gene on its chromosome.|The locus of decision-making is in the head office. (决策地点在总部。)  The earthquake's locus was 25 miles offshore. (地震的震中位于离岸25英里处。) The locus of the gene for eye color is on chromosome 15. (眼睛颜色基因的位点在15号染色体上。)|近义词：location, position, site, spot, place|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
