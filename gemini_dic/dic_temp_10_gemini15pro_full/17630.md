bebung:/ˈbeːbʊŋ/| n.|颤音 (一种键盘乐器演奏技巧)|A slight fluctuation of pitch, typically produced on a clavichord or other keyboard instrument by varying the finger pressure on a key.|The pianist expertly employed bebung in the piece, creating a shimmering effect.|Synonyms: vibrato, tremolo|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'steady tone')|noun
