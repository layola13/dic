branching:/ˈbrɑːntʃɪŋ/| n. adj.|分支，分叉；枝状的|The act of splitting into two or more parts, resembling the branches of a tree.  It can refer to physical branching, like the branches of a tree, or abstract branching, like the branching of possibilities or storylines.|The company is undergoing a period of rapid branching, expanding into new markets.  这家公司正处于快速扩张时期，正在向新的市场扩张。|synonyms: diverging, forking, ramifying|antonyms: converging, uniting, merging|n.

branching:/ˈbrɑːntʃɪŋ/| adj.|分支的，分叉的|Having branches; resembling the branches of a tree.|The branching structure of the organization made communication difficult.  该组织的分支结构使得沟通变得困难。|synonyms: branched, forked, dendritic|antonyms: linear, straight, unbranched|adj.
