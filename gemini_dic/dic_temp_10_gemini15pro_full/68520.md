fancied:/ˈfænsiːd/| v.| 想要；倾慕；想象；认为|Past tense and past participle of fancy.  Derived from the Old French "fancier," meaning "to imagine, invent, create."  It evolved from the Latin "phantasia," ultimately from Greek "phantasia," meaning "appearance, image, imagination."|例句：She fancied a cup of tea. 她想喝杯茶。

例句：He fancied himself as a bit of a singer.  他自认为有点像个歌手。|近义词：desired, liked, imagined, believed|反义词：disliked, loathed|词性:动词
