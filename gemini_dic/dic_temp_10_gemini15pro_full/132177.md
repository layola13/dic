noncyclical:/ˌnɑːnˈsaɪklɪkəl/
adjective
非周期性的，非循环的
Not recurring in cycles; not subject to periodic fluctuation.
The demand for basic necessities like food and water is generally considered noncyclical.  对食物和水等基本必需品的需求通常被认为是非周期性的。
Noncyclical industries tend to be less affected by economic downturns. 非周期性行业往往受经济衰退的影响较小。
Synonyms: nonperiodic, countercyclical, stable
Antonyms: cyclical, periodic, volatile
词性: adjective
