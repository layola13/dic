caesarism:/ˈsiːzərɪzəm/
n.
凯撒主义; 帝王主义; 独裁主义
A form of government characterized by a single ruler with absolute power, often achieved and maintained through force.  The term derives from Julius Caesar, the Roman general and statesman.
The country slowly descended into caesarism after the coup.  这个国家在政变后慢慢陷入了独裁统治。
autocracy, dictatorship, tyranny, despotism
democracy, republic
noun
