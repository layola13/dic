asterism:/ˈæstərɪzəm/| n.|星群; 星宿; 星座|A visually obvious pattern of stars, often a smaller part of a larger constellation.  A group of stars that form a pattern but are not officially recognized as a constellation.|例句：The Big Dipper is an asterism within the constellation Ursa Major. 北斗七星是大熊座中的一个星群。

例句：The Summer Triangle is a prominent asterism composed of three bright stars. 夏季大三角是一个由三颗亮星组成的显著星群。|近义词：constellation, star pattern|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
