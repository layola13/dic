hello's:/həˈloʊz/ | n. | 你好的（属于某人/某物） |  It's a contraction of "hello" and the possessive 's, indicating ownership or association.  Similar to saying "belonging to hello" although not grammatically precise. Primarily used informally. | Hello's blessings be upon you.  愿你得到祝福。| 近义词: greetings, regards | 反义词: N/A |  contraction (of hello + 's)
