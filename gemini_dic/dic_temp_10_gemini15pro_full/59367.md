earnest:/ˈɜːrnɪst/| n. | 定金; 保证金; 认真;| something given or done as a pledge or security; seriousness |  He gave me $100 as an earnest of his intention to buy the car. 他给了我100美元作为购买这辆车的定金。| synonym: deposit, down payment, pledge, seriousness | antonym: frivolity, levity | noun
earnest:/ˈɜːrnɪst/| adj. | 认真的; 诚挚的; 热心的| resulting from or showing sincere and intense conviction | He was an earnest student, always attending classes and completing assignments. 他是一个认真的学生，总是上课并完成作业。| synonym: sincere, serious, fervent, zealous | antonym: frivolous, insincere, apathetic | adjective
