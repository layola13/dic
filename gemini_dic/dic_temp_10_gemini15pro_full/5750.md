am:/æm/ | v. | 是 |  The first person singular present tense form of the verb "be". Used to indicate existence, identity, or state of being. | I am a student. 我是一名学生。 |  is, are, exist | wasn't, aren't | verb

Etymology: From Old English *eom, from Proto-Germanic *immi, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ésmi. Cognate with German bin, Latin sum, Greek εἰμί (eimí), Sanskrit ásmi.


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I am happy. 我很高兴。
I am a doctor. 我是一名医生。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am studying English. 我正在学习英语。
I am cooking dinner. 我正在做晚饭。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
This tense doesn't use "am".  现在完成时不使用am。
(Example with "have": I have finished my work. 我已经完成了我的工作。)


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This tense doesn't use "am". 现在完成进行时不使用am。
(Example with "have": I have been studying English for two years. 我学习英语已经两年了。)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I was happy. 我很高兴。
I was a student. 我是一名学生。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was studying English. 我正在学习英语。
I was cooking dinner. 我正在做晚饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had been happy. 我曾经很快乐。
I had been a student. 我曾经是一名学生。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been studying English for two years. 我学习英语已经两年了。
I had been living in London before I moved to New York. 在搬到纽约之前，我一直住在伦敦。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be happy. 我将会很高兴。
I will be a teacher. 我将会成为一名老师。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be studying English. 我将会学习英语。
I will be traveling next year. 我明年将会去旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have been happy. 我将会一直很高兴。
I will have been a teacher for ten years. 我将会成为一名教师十年了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been studying English for five years. 我将会学习英语五年了。
I will have been living in Paris for a year by then. 到那时，我将会在巴黎住一年了。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go. 如果我是你，我会去。
I wish I were taller. 我希望我更高。


被动语态:
This tense doesn't use "am" in the passive voice. 被动语态不使用am。
(Example with "is":  The cake is being eaten. 蛋糕正在被吃。)

疑问句:
Am I late? 我迟到了吗？
Am I doing this right? 我这样做对吗？


否定句:
I am not happy. 我不高兴。
I am not a student. 我不是一名学生。
