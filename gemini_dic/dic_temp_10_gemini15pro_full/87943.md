higgledy-piggledy:/ˌhɪɡəldiˈpɪɡəldi/| adj. adv.|乱七八糟的，杂乱无章的|in a confused and disordered way|The toys were scattered higgledy-piggledy across the floor. 玩具乱七八糟地散落在地板上。|The books were piled higgledy-piggledy on the shelf. 书架上的书堆放得乱七八糟。|近义词：disorderly, chaotic, messy, jumbled|反义词：orderly, neat, tidy|词性:形容词, 副词
词源:Likely a playful reduplication based on "pig," suggesting the disorderly manner of pigs.  Possibly influenced by similar-sounding words like "higgle" (to haggle or bargain), adding to the sense of confusion.
