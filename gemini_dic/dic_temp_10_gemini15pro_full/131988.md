|n.| 未转让，未让渡（通常指财产） |The nonconveyance of the property led to a legal dispute.  该房产的未转让导致了法律纠纷。 | non-transfer, non-assignment | conveyance, transfer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + conveyance (the act of conveying or transferring property).  Conveyance itself comes from Old French conveiance, from conveier "to convey," ultimately from Latin *conviare "to accompany on the way," from com- "with, together" + via "way, road."
