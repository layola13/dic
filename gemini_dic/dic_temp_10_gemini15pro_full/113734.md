macroeconomicses: /ˌmækroʊiːkəˈnɑːmɪksɪz/ | n. | 宏观经济学 | The study of the behavior of the economy as a whole, including such factors as inflation, unemployment, and economic growth.  It analyzes aggregate measures like GDP, national income, and price levels. |  Macroeconomicses, while not standard, implies multiple systems or instances of macroeconomics being considered, perhaps in different countries or theoretical models.  *Standard usage is macroeconomics (singular).* | 近义词: macroeconomics (singular) | 反义词: microeconomics | 词性: noun

词源:  From Greek *makros* (large) + economics.
