campaigners:/kæmˈpeɪnərz/
noun
活动家；运动倡导者
People who actively participate in a campaign to achieve a particular social, political, or commercial goal.
The campaigners worked tirelessly to raise awareness about climate change.  活动家们不知疲倦地工作，以提高人们对气候变化的认识。
Animal rights campaigners protested outside the laboratory.  动物权利活动家在实验室外抗议。
advocates, activists, promoters
opponents, adversaries
n.
