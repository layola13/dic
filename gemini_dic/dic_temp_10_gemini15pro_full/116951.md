mazing: /ˈmeɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 惊人的，令人惊异的 |  Causing great surprise or wonder; astonishing.  Derived from the verb "amaze" meaning to overwhelm with wonder.  | The acrobat performed some amazing feats.  杂技演员表演了一些惊人的绝技。| synonyms: astonishing, astounding, surprising, wonderful, incredible, remarkable, phenomenal | antonyms:  ordinary, mundane, unimpressive, boring, dull | adjective
