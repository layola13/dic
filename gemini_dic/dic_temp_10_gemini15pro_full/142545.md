ostensibility:/ˌɑːstənˈsɪbɪləti/ /ˌɒstənˈsɪbɪləti/| n.|表面上; 表面性;|the state or quality of appearing or claiming to be one thing when it is really something else.  A way of seeming or pretending to be true or real when it is not.|He joined the club for reasons of ostensibility, but his real aim was to make business contacts.|近义词：appearance, semblance, pretense|反义词：reality, genuineness, authenticity|词性:noun

ostensible:/ɑːˈstɛnsəbl/ /ɒˈstɛnsəbl/| adj.|表面上的；貌似真实的；宣称的|stated or appearing to be true, but not necessarily so.|The ostensible reason for his absence was illness, but I think he just didn't want to come.|近义词：apparent, seeming, outward|反义词：real, actual, genuine|词性:adjective
