overhumanizes:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪzɪz/
|v.|过度人性化|Attributes human qualities or emotions to something that is not human, often to an excessive degree.|She tends to overhumanize her pets, treating them like children. 她倾向于过度人性化她的宠物，把它们当作孩子对待。|The author overhumanizes the AI in the novel, giving it complex emotions and motivations. 作者在小说中过度人性化了人工智能，赋予了它复杂的情感和动机。|anthropomorphize, personify|dehumanize|verb
