dentition:/dɛnˈtɪʃən/
n.
牙列；齿系；出牙
The arrangement, type, and number of teeth in a person or animal.
The child’s dentition was severely affected by the medication.  这个孩子的牙列受到了药物的严重影响。

The horse's dentition indicated it was around five years old. 这匹马的齿系表明它大约五岁。

teeth, set of teeth
edentulousness (lack of teeth)
noun
