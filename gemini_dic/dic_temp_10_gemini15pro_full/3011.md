aerator:/ˈeəˌreɪtər/ /ˈɛrˌeɪtər/ | n. |通气器，曝气器|A device used to introduce air into a substance, such as soil or water.|The fish tank aerator keeps the water oxygenated. 鱼缸里的曝气器保持水中的氧气含量。|synonyms:  oxygenator, ventilator, diffuser | antonyms: sealant, blocker | noun

aerator:/ˈeəˌreɪtər/ /ˈɛrˌeɪtər/ | n. |加气器|A device used to introduce air into a beverage, such as wine.|He used an aerator to enhance the flavor of the wine. 他用加气器来提升葡萄酒的风味。|synonyms: pourer, decanter | antonyms: N/A | noun
