overburdening: /ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdnɪŋ/ | v. | 过负荷；使负担过重；| placing excessive burdens on someone or something; overwhelming. |  The company is overburdening its employees with excessive workload. 这家公司给员工的工作负担过重。|  Overburdening the electrical system can cause a fire.  使电力系统负荷过重会导致火灾。| 近义词: overloading, overwhelming, taxing | 反义词: easing, lightening | verb (present participle)

overburden: /ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdn/ | n. | 过重的负担；覆盖层 |  a heavy load or burden; the earth and rock that cover a mineral deposit. | The overburden of paperwork is stifling productivity. 大量的文书工作扼杀了生产力。| The miners had to remove a thick overburden to reach the coal seam. 矿工们不得不清除厚厚的覆盖层才能到达煤层。|近义词: load, burden, weight | 反义词: relief, ease | noun

词源: From over- + burden.  "Burden" comes from Old English *byrðen "load, weight," related to "bear."
