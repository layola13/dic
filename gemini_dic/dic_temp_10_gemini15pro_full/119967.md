millennist:/mɪˈlɛnɪɪst/ | n. | 千禧一代；千禧年主义者 | A person who belongs to the generation who became adults around the year 2000; a person who believes that the millennium (a period of 1,000 years) is important, especially in religious contexts. | Many millennials are burdened with student loan debt. 许多千禧一代背负着学生贷款债务。| The millennist preacher predicted the end of the world in 2000.  那位千禧年主义的传教士预言世界末日将在2000年来临。 | synonyms: Generation Y, millennial generation (for the generation); chiliast (for the religious belief) | antonyms: (No direct antonym for the generation); premillennialist, postmillennialist (for differing religious beliefs)| noun
