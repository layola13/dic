discounter:/dɪˈskaʊntər/| n.|折扣店; 贴现者|A retailer who sells goods at reduced prices.|Example:  The online discounter offers a wide range of electronics. 这家在线折扣店提供各种电子产品。
Example: We bought our new TV from a discounter. 我们从一家折扣店买了新电视。|近义词：cheap store, budget store, value retailer|反义词:luxury store, upscale retailer|词性:noun
discount:/ˈdɪskaʊnt/| n. vt.|折扣; 贴现|A deduction from the usual cost of something.|Example: They offer a 10% discount for students. 他们为学生提供九折优惠。
Example:  The store discounted the damaged goods. 这家商店对受损商品打折出售。|近义词：reduction, rebate, allowance|反义词:premium, surcharge|词性:noun, verb
