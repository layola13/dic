daylit:/ˈdeɪlɪt/| adj.|日光照亮的，白天明亮的|Lit by daylight; illuminated by the natural light of day.|The daylit room was cheerful and inviting. 日光照亮的房间令人感到愉快和温馨。|The market was bustling and daylit. 市场熙熙攘攘，光线充足。|近义词：sunlit, light, bright|反义词：dark, dim, night-time|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'day' + 'lit', the past participle of 'light'.  'Day' comes from Old English 'dæg', and 'light' comes from Old English 'lēoht'.
