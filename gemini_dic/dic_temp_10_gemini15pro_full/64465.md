/-ɪˈzeɪʃən/ |-ization|名词|化，…化作用|The suffix "-ization" is used to form nouns from verbs, often indicating the process or result of making something or someone into a specified state or condition.|The modernization of the factory increased efficiency. 工厂的现代化提高了效率.|近义词:transformation, conversion|反义词: (Often context-dependent, no single direct antonym) |n.
