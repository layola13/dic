chromolithographer:/ˌkrəʊməʊlɪˈθɒɡrəfər/| n. |彩色石印技师|A person who produces chromolithographs (pictures printed in color from a series of stone or metal plates).|The skilled chromolithographer meticulously layered the inks to create a vibrant image.  这位熟练的彩色石印技师一丝不苟地将油墨分层，以创造出一幅充满活力的图像。|The chromolithographer's work was highly sought after for its rich detail and color. 这位彩色石印技师的作品因其丰富的细节和色彩而备受追捧。|近义词:lithographer, printmaker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
chromo- (Greek) meaning "color" + litho- (Greek) meaning "stone" + -grapher (Greek) meaning "writer" or "one who draws"
