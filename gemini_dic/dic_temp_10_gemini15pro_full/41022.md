copatriot:/ˌkoʊˈpeɪtriət/ /ˌkəʊˈpætriət/| n.|同胞，同国人|A person from the same country as another.|She met a fellow copatriot while traveling abroad. 她在国外旅行时遇到了一位同胞。|Synonyms: countryman, fellow citizen|Antonyms: foreigner, alien|noun

copatriot:/ˌkoʊˈpeɪtriət/ /ˌkəʊˈpætriət/| adj.|同国的，同胞的|Sharing the same country of origin.|He felt a sense of camaraderie with his copatriot soldiers. 他对他的同胞士兵感到一种同志情谊。|Synonyms: national, compatriotal|Antonyms: foreign, alien|adjective
