metaleptical:/ˌmɛtəˈlɛptɪkəl/
adjective
修辞转移的；超越常规的；打破叙事层级的
A metaleptical narrative device is a figure of speech in which one frame of narrative is blended with another.  It disrupts the conventional boundaries between different levels of narrative.
The author’s use of metalepsis, where the narrator interrupts the story to address the reader directly, is a striking example of a metaleptical technique. 作者运用了一种修辞转移的技巧，即叙述者打断故事直接与读者对话，这是一个显著的例子。
The film's metaleptical ending, where a character from a "movie within the movie" steps into the "real world," left the audience bewildered.  这部电影超越常规的结局，即“戏中戏”中的一个角色走进了“现实世界”，让观众感到困惑。
synonyms:  self-reflexive, metafictional, unconventional, transgressive
antonyms: conventional, traditional, orthodox
词性: 形容词
