metabranchial:/ˌmetəˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective
后鳃的，后鳃骨的
Relating to or denoting the posterior gill or gill region in vertebrates, specifically the last gill arch in fish and amphibians, which can be modified into various structures in different species.
The metabranchial bone plays a crucial role in supporting the gill structure of some fish. 后鳃骨在支撑某些鱼类的鳃结构中起着至关重要的作用。
The metabranchial glands in cephalopods contribute to the production of mucus.  头足类动物的后鳃腺有助于产生粘液。
Synonym: postbranchial
Antonym: prebranchial
词性: adjective
