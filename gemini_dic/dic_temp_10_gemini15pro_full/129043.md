nitratine:/ˈnaɪtreɪtiːn/ |n.|硝酸钠; 智利硝石|A naturally occurring mineral form of sodium nitrate, NaNO₃.  It is a soft, colorless or white, sometimes reddish or brownish mineral, typically found in arid regions. |Nitratine is mined as a source of nitrates for use in fertilizers and explosives. 智利硝石被开采用作肥料和炸药中硝酸盐的来源。|Synonyms: soda niter, Chile saltpeter|Antonyms: N/A|noun
