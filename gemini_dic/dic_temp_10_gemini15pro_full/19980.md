biogenesis:/ˌbaɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
生命起源; 生源说
the production of living organisms from other living organisms.  The principle that living organisms can only originate from other living organisms, and not from non-living matter.
Biogenesis is the theory that living things can only come from other living things.  生源说是生命只能来自其他生命的理论。
The theory of biogenesis was a major breakthrough in scientific understanding. 生源说理论是科学理解上的一个重大突破。

近义词:abiogenesis (in older usage)
反义词:abiogenesis
词性:noun
