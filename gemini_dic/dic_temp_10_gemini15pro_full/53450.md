disappropriation:/dɪsəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/| n.|非法挪用，侵吞，盗用|The disappropriation of company funds is a serious offense. 公司资金的非法挪用是严重的违法行为。|The investigation revealed a disappropriation of public resources for private gain. 调查揭露了公共资源被挪作私用的情况。|近义词：misappropriation, embezzlement, theft, defalcation|反义词: allocation, appropriation|词性: noun

词源: dis- (not, opposite of) + appropriate (to take for one's own use) + -ion (forming nouns of action or condition)  The prefix "dis-" signifies negation or reversal, suggesting the opposite of proper appropriation. "Appropriate" comes from the Latin "appropriare," meaning "to make one's own."  The suffix "-ion" creates a noun denoting the act or process of disappropriating.
