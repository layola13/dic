aspiringly:/əˈspaɪərɪŋli/
adv.
有抱负地；渴望地
With ambition; in a manner that shows a strong desire to achieve something.
He looked aspiringly at the mountains, dreaming of climbing them. 他渴望地望着群山，梦想着攀登它们。
近义词:ambitiously, hopefully, yearningly
反义词:apathetically, indifferently, hopelessly
词性:副词

aspire:/əˈspaɪər/
v.
渴望；追求；有志于
Direct one's hopes or ambitions towards achieving something.
She aspired to a career in medicine. 她渴望从事医学事业。
近义词:aim, strive, yearn
反义词:resign, give up, abandon
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aspirare, frequentative of Latin aspirare "to breathe on, breathe at, blow gently," figuratively "to strive for, long for," from ad- "to" + spirare "to breathe."
