paraquats:/ˈpærəkwɒts/ | n. | 百草枯 | A highly toxic herbicide and contact desiccant.  It is rapidly inactivated on contact with soil. | Paraquats are banned in many countries due to their toxicity.  百草枯因其毒性在许多国家被禁用。 | herbicides, weedkillers |  (no direct antonym, perhaps "soil amendments" or "fertilizers" as they promote growth rather than kill it) | noun
