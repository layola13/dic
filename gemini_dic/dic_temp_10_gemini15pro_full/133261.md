nonexhaustiveness:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɔːstɪvnəs/ | n. | 非穷尽性，非完备性 | The nonexhaustiveness of the list means that there may be other valid options.  |  例句：The nonexhaustiveness of this study's data limits its conclusions.  这项研究数据的非穷尽性限制了它的结论。

例句：The report acknowledged the nonexhaustiveness of its findings due to time constraints.  该报告承认由于时间限制，其调查结果并非详尽无遗。 | 近义词：incompleteness, partialness | 反义词：completeness, exhaustiveness | 词性: noun
