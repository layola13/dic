mesorrhine:/ˈmɛzəˌraɪn/| adj.|中鼻型的|Having a nose of medium width; between leptorrhine (narrow-nosed) and platyrrhine (broad-nosed).  It is a term used in physical anthropology to classify nasal shapes.|The ancient Egyptians were typically depicted as mesorrhine.|近义词:mesonasal|反义词:leptorrhine, platyrrhine|词性:adjective
