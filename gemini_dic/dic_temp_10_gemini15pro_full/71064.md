firster:/ˈfɜːrstər/ (This word is not standard English and may be considered informal or dialectal)| n.| 第一名；最先的人；新手 | Someone who comes first or does something first, often in a competition or informal setting. It can also refer to a beginner or novice.|  He claimed to be a firster in the local marathon, but nobody had ever heard of him. 他声称是本地马拉松的第一名，但从来没有人听说过他。|Near synonyms: winner, champion, beginner, novice| Antonyms: loser, last, expert| Noun
