mestizo:/mɛˈstiːzoʊ/ (US), /mɛˈstiːzəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 混血儿（尤指西班牙裔和美洲原住民混血）| A person of mixed racial ancestry, especially one having a Spanish and Indigenous American parent.|  Many mestizos in Mexico identify with both their European and Indigenous heritage.  墨西哥的许多混血儿认同他们的欧洲和土著血统。| synonyms: mixed-race, hybrid | antonyms: purebred, full-blooded | noun

mestizos:/mɛˈstiːzoʊz/ (US), /mɛˈstiːzəʊz/ (UK)| n. | 混血儿们（尤指西班牙裔和美洲原住民混血）| Plural form of mestizo, referring to a group of people of mixed racial ancestry, especially those having Spanish and Indigenous American parentage.| The mestizos formed a distinct social class in colonial Latin America. 在殖民时期的拉丁美洲，混血儿形成了一个独特的社会阶层。| synonyms: mixed-race people, hybrids | antonyms: purebreds, full-blooded individuals | noun
