over-optimism:/ˌōvərˈɒptɪmɪzəm/ | n. | 过度乐观；盲目乐观 | Excessive optimism; unrealistic or unfounded hopefulness. |  His over-optimism about the project led to disappointment when it failed. 他对这个项目的过度乐观导致了项目失败时的失望。|  Her over-optimism blinded her to the potential risks. 她过度的乐观使她对潜在的风险视而不见。 | 近义词：excessive optimism, Pollyannaism | 反义词: pessimism, realism | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From 'over-' (prefix meaning "excessive") + 'optimism' (from French optimisme, from Latin optimus "best").
