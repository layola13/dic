constipated: /ˈkɒnstɪpeɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstɪpeɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|便秘的；不通畅的|Affected with constipation; unable to easily pass feces.  The condition of having infrequent or difficult bowel movements.|例句：I've been feeling constipated lately. 我最近感觉便秘。|例句：The medication can make you constipated. 这种药可能会让你便秘。|近义词：blocked, bound up, plugged up|反义词: loose|词性:形容词

constipate: /ˈkɒnstɪpeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstɪpeɪt/ (US)| v.|使便秘；使不通畅|To cause constipation in; to make the bowels unable to evacuate readily.|例句：A diet low in fiber can constipate you. 缺乏纤维的饮食会导致便秘。|例句：Certain medications can constipate patients.  某些药物会使病人便秘。|近义词：obstruct, clog|反义词: loosen|词性:动词

constipation: /ˌkɒnstɪˈpeɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnstɪˈpeɪʃn/ (US)| n.|便秘|Infrequent or difficult evacuation of the bowels.|例句：She suffers from chronic constipation. 她患有慢性便秘。|例句：Constipation can be caused by a lack of fiber in the diet. 饮食中缺乏纤维会导致便秘。|近义词：costiveness|反义词: diarrhea|词性:名词

词源：From Latin *constipatus*, past participle of *constipare* (“to press closely together, crowd, condense”), from *con-* (“together”) + *stipare* (“to press, pack”).
