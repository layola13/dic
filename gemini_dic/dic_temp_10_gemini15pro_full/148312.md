oversolidifies:/ˌoʊvər səˈlɪdɪˌfaɪz/
verb
过度凝固，凝固过头
to solidify to an excessive degree; to become too solid
The molten metal oversolidified due to the rapid cooling. 熔融金属由于快速冷却而过度凝固。
The alloy oversolidifies at low temperatures, making it brittle.  这种合金在低温下会过度凝固，使其变脆。
近义词: harden excessively, solidify too much
反义词: melt, liquefy
词性: verb
