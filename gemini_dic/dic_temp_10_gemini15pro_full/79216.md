glandule:/ˈɡlændjuːl/| n. |小腺体|A small gland.  A gland is an organ in the body that produces and releases substances like hormones or enzymes.|The biologist identified a previously unknown glandule in the insect's abdomen. 生物学家在昆虫的腹部发现了一个以前不为人知的小腺体。|Synonym: glandlet,  acinus|Antonym: N/A (There isn't a direct opposite of a small gland.)|noun
