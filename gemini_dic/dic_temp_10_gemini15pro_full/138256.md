not-for-profit:/ˌnɒt fəˈprɒfɪt/ /ˌnɑːt fərˈprɑːfɪt/| adj. n.|非营利的，非营利组织|Not intended to generate profit; operating for charitable, educational, or other purposes rather than for financial gain.  The organization's primary goal is to provide services, not to make a profit. |The Red Cross is a not-for-profit organization. 红十字会是一个非营利组织。|Synonyms: non-profit, charitable, philanthropic|Antonyms: for-profit, commercial|Adjective/Noun

not-for-profit:/ˌnɒt fəˈprɒfɪt/ /ˌnɑːt fərˈprɑːfɪt/| n.|非营利组织|An organization that does not operate for profit. |Many not-for-profits rely on donations. 许多非营利组织依靠捐款。|Synonyms: non-profit organization, charitable organization, philanthropic organization|Antonyms: for-profit business, commercial enterprise|Noun
