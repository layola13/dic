armil:/ˈɑːrmɪl/ | n. |  环圈;  (尤指天球仪、浑天仪上的)赤道环，地平环，子午环 | An armil is a graduated ring, especially one of a series forming the framework of an astrolabe, celestial globe, or armillary sphere. | The astronomer adjusted the armil on his celestial globe. 天文学家调整了他天球仪上的环圈。 | near synonyms: ring, circle, hoop | antonym: (no direct antonym, possibly line) | noun
