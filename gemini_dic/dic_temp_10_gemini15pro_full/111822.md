loggat:/ˈlɒɡət/ (British English) | n. | 木材计量单位，圆木 | A log gat was a historical unit of measurement for timber, specifically roundwood, used primarily in England.  It was equivalent to 16 cubic feet. |  The timber merchant sold several loggats of oak.  木材商卖了几洛加特橡木。 | 近义词: load, cord (though not exact equivalents) | 反义词: N/A | Noun
