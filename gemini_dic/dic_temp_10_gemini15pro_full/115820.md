markers:/ˈmɑːrkərz/ | n. |标记，标志物，记号笔|A marker is an object used to indicate a position, place, or route.  It can also refer to a pen with a broad felt tip, used for writing on whiteboards or other surfaces. |The hikers followed the trail markers.  徒步旅行者沿着路标走。  She used a brightly coloured marker to write on the whiteboard. 她用一支颜色鲜艳的记号笔在白板上写字。| synonyms: signs, indicators, signals, symbols, highlighters | antonyms: erasers, concealments | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *marker*, from marken (“to mark”), from Old English *mearcian* (“to mark, designate”), from Proto-Germanic *markōną* (“to mark, border”), from Proto-Indo-European *merk- (“boundary, border”).
