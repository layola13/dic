lin:/lɪn/ | n. |  蔺, 霖 | A surname; light rain.  Derived from the Chinese characters 蔺 (a type of reed; a surname) and 霖 (continuous rain). | Example: Mr. Lin is a renowned scientist. 林先生是一位著名的科学家。 | Synonym: (For 霖) drizzle, shower | Antonym: (For 霖) downpour, torrent | noun


lin:/lɪn/ | n. | 磷 |  Phosphorus, a chemical element.  Derived from the Greek word "phosphoros" meaning "light-bearing".  This is a different word from the Chinese "lin" but shares the same pronunciation in English. |  Example:  Plants need phosphorus to grow. 植物需要磷才能生长。 | Synonym: P (chemical symbol) | Antonym:  N/A | noun
