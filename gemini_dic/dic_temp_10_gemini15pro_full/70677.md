final hurrah: /ˈfʌɪn(ə)l hʊˈrɑː/ | n. |最后的欢呼；最后的努力；最后的告别|A last, typically especially vigorous, effort, display, or success.|The aging rock star gave his fans one final hurrah with a farewell tour.  这位老摇滚明星用告别巡演给了歌迷们最后的欢呼。|近义词：last attempt, swan song, farewell performance|反义词：beginning, start, commencement|词性:名词

词源：
"Final" comes from the Latin "finalis," meaning "of the end." "Hurrah" is an exclamation of joy or triumph, possibly originating from the Slavic "huray" or the German "hurra."  The combined phrase emphasizes a last, celebratory effort or performance.
