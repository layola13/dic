off-the-hook:/ˌɔːf ðə ˈhʊk/ | adj. | 脱离困境；摆脱麻烦；免受责罚 | Free from difficulty, responsibility, or blame.  | I was supposed to work this weekend, but my boss let me off the hook. 我本应该这周末工作的，但我的老板让我不用去了。|  I finally got off the hook for that speeding ticket. 我终于摆脱了那张超速罚单。| 近义词: free, clear, released, exempt | 反义词: responsible, liable, trapped | 词性: 形容词

词源:  This idiom originated in the early 20th century from the idea of a fish being caught on a hook and then released.  如果鱼被钓上钩，然后被放生，它就“off the hook”了。这个习语来源于20世纪初。
