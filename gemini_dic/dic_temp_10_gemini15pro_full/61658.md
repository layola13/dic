emissory:/ɪˈmɪsəri/ |adj.| 发射的；放射性的 | Relating to emission, especially of light, heat, or radiation. | The emissive properties of the material were carefully studied.  这种材料的发射特性得到了仔细研究。| Synonyms: radiant, emitting, radiating | Antonyms: absorbent, non-radiating | adjective

emissory:/ɪˈmɪsəri/ |n.| 发射体；发射源 | A substance or object that emits something, such as light, heat, or radiation. | The sun is a powerful emissive body. 太阳是一个强大的发射体。| Synonyms: emitter, radiator, source | Antonyms: absorber, sink | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēmissārius (unattested), from Latin ēmissus, past participle of ēmittere (“to send forth”), from ē- (“out, from”) + mittere (“to send”).  Equivalent to e- +‎ missory.
