congregates:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪts/ (US)| v.|聚集，集合|comes together in a group; assembles|例句：The crowd congregates in the square for the concert. 人群聚集在广场上观看音乐会。

例句：Birds of a feather congregates together. 物以类聚，人以群分。

近义词：assemble, gather, cluster|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:verb

congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (US)| adj. (obsolete, rare)|聚集的，集合的|formed into a group|例句：The congregate body of worshippers filled the church.  聚集在一起的礼拜者挤满了教堂。(例句较为古老，现代英语很少这样使用)

例句：The congregate flowers formed a beautiful bouquet.  聚集在一起的花朵形成了美丽的花束。(例句较为古老，现代英语很少这样使用)


近义词：assembled, gathered|反义词:dispersed, scattered|词性:adjective


词源: From Latin *congregātus, past participle of congregāre (“to collect into a flock, assemble”), from con- (“together”) + greg- (“flock”), from grex (“flock”).
