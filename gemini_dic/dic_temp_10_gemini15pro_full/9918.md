aperea:/ˌæpəˈriːə/| n.|岩豚鼠；岩豚鼠的毛皮|A South American rodent (genus *Cavia*) resembling the guinea pig, but larger and more brightly coloured. |The aperea is a social animal, living in small groups. 岩豚鼠是一种群居动物，以小群体生活。| The aperea's fur is used to make clothes. 岩豚鼠的毛皮用来做衣服。 |近义词：guinea pig, cavy|反义词:|名词

词源:
From Spanish *aperea*, from Guarani *apere'a*.
