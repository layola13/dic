non-receivable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsiːvəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn rɪˈsiːvəbl/| adj. n.|无法收回的，坏账|An amount (as a debt) that is unlikely to be paid and therefore written off as a loss.|The company wrote off $50,000 in non-receivable debts.|近义词：uncollectable, bad debt, irrecoverable|反义词:receivable, collectable|词性:形容词, 名词

non-receivable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsiːvəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn rɪˈsiːvəbl/| n.|坏账，应收账款|An amount (as a debt) that is unlikely to be paid and therefore written off as a loss.|The company had a large amount of non-receivables this quarter.|近义词：bad debts, uncollectibles|反义词:receivables, assets|词性:名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "receivable" (from "receive," ultimately from Latin "recipere").
