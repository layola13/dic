neocolonial:/ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəl/
|adj.|新殖民主义的|Relating to or characteristic of neocolonialism; the use of economic, political, cultural, or other pressures to control or influence other countries, especially former dependencies.|Many developing nations are struggling with the legacy of neocolonialism.|Synonyms: imperialist, exploitative|Antonyms: independent, autonomous|adjective


neocolonials:/ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəlz/
|n.|新殖民主义者|People who support or practice neocolonialism.|The neocolonials continue to exploit the resources of the developing world.|Synonyms: imperialists, exploiters|Antonyms: liberators, nationalists|noun


Etymology:
neo- + colonial
neo-:  a new or revived form of
colonial:  relating to or characteristic of a colony or colonies.
