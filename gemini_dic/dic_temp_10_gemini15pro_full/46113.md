cylindricality:/ˌsɪlɪnˈdrɪkəlɪti/
n.
圆柱度，圆柱形
The state or quality of being cylindrical.
The cylindricality of the pipe was measured using a laser scanner.  用激光扫描仪测量了管子的圆柱度。
Cylindricality is an important factor in the design of engine components. 圆柱度是发动机部件设计中的一个重要因素。
近义词: cylindricalness, roundness
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like asymmetry, irregularity)
