debilitation:/dɪˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən/
n.|衰弱，虚弱|The process of weakening or making someone very weak and infirm.|Long-term illness had resulted in a severe debilitation of his body. 长期疾病导致他的身体严重衰弱。|
近义词: weakening, enfeeblement, impairment, incapacitation
反义词: strengthening, invigoration, empowerment
