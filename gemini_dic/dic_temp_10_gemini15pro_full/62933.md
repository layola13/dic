enmist:/ˈɛnmɪst/ | n. | 敌人；仇敌 | An enemy; a foe. | He saw her as his enmist after the betrayal. 他在背叛后视她为敌人。| 近义词: enemy, foe, adversary, opponent | 反义词: friend, ally, supporter | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *enmist, borrowed from Old French enemist, enemi, ultimately from Latin inimīcus (“unfriendly, hostile; a personal enemy”). Equivalent to en- +‎ enemy. More at enemy.
