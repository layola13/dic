overtrading:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 过度交易，超额交易 | Engaging in more business or trading activity than is prudent or justified by one's capital or resources.  Operating a business with insufficient working capital. | The company's overtrading led to severe financial difficulties. 该公司的过度交易导致了严重的财务困难。 |  The rapid expansion of the business into new markets suggests they might be overtrading. 该公司迅速扩张到新市场，表明他们可能在进行过度交易。 | 近义词：overexpansion, speculation | 反义词：undertrading, caution | noun
