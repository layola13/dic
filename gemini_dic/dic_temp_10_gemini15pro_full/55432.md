distinctest:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪst/
adjective
最清晰的，最明显的
Most clearly different or separate; most easily noticeable.
The distinctest sound I heard was a baby crying. 我听到的最清晰的声音是婴儿的哭声。
Synonyms: clearest, sharpest, plainest, most obvious
Antonyms: vaguest, faintest, blurred, indistinct, obscure
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *distinctest, superlative of distinct, from Latin distinctus (“separated, divided, distinct”), past participle of distinguō (“distinguish, separate, divide, mark”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + stinguō (“prick, extinguish”).
