endodermal:/ˌɛndəˈdɜːrməl/ | adj. | 内胚层的 | Of or relating to the endoderm, the innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo. | The endodermal cells differentiate into the lining of the digestive tract and associated organs. 内胚层细胞分化成消化道和相关器官的内层。| The endodermal origin of the lungs is well established. 肺的内胚层起源已得到证实。 |  近义词: entodermal | 反义词: ectodermal | 词性: 形容词

词源: endo- (within) + derm (skin) + -al (adjectival suffix)
