interactables:/ˌɪntərˈæktəbəlz/ | n. | 可交互对象，可互动元素 | Objects in a virtual environment that a user can interact with, often by clicking, touching, or manipulating them.  These could include buttons, levers, characters, or items within a game or simulation. |  Example: The game designers carefully placed interactables throughout the level to enhance player engagement.  游戏设计师在整个关卡中精心放置了可交互对象，以增强玩家的参与度。

Example:  Many modern museums use interactables to make exhibits more engaging for visitors. 许多现代博物馆使用互动元素，使展览对游客更具吸引力。 | 近义词: interactive elements, interactive objects | 反义词: static elements, non-interactive objects | 词性: Noun (plural)
