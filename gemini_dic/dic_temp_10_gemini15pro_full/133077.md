nonequilibria:/ˌnɒnˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriə/ | n. | 非平衡 (状态) |  A state where a system is not in equilibrium, meaning it's dynamic and changing.  It often refers to systems in physics, chemistry, or economics that are not stable and are subject to fluxes or flows. | The researcher studied the nonequilibria of the chemical reaction.  这位研究人员研究了化学反应的非平衡状态。

The economist analyzed the market's nonequilibria caused by the sudden policy change.  这位经济学家分析了由突发的政策变化引起的市场非均衡状态。 | 近义词: disequilibrium, instability | 反义词: equilibrium, stability | 词性: 名词
