dovetailing:/ˈdʌvˌteɪlɪŋ/
n.|榫卯连接; 紧密配合; 吻合|The process of joining two pieces of wood together by means of dovetails.  Also used figuratively to describe things that fit together neatly or harmoniously.|例句：The carpenter skillfully employed dovetailing to join the corners of the drawer. 木匠熟练地运用榫卯连接抽屉的各个角。|近义词：interlocking, meshing, integrating, coordinating|反义词：clashing, conflicting|词性:名词

dovetail:/ˈdʌvˌteɪl/
v.|吻合; 衔接; 紧密配合|To fit together neatly or harmoniously.|例句：Our plans dovetailed perfectly with theirs. 我们的计划与他们的计划完全吻合。|近义词：interlock, mesh, integrate, coordinate|反义词：clash, conflict|词性:动词

dovetailed:/ˈdʌvˌteɪld/
adj.|榫卯连接的; 紧密配合的; 吻合的|Joined by means of dovetails; fitting together neatly or harmoniously.|例句：The dovetailed joints were a testament to the craftsman's skill. 榫卯接头证明了工匠的技艺。|近义词：interlocked, meshed, integrated, coordinated|反义词：clashing, conflicting|词性:形容词
