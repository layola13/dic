pajama:/pəˈdʒɑːməz/ (US), /pəˈdʒɑːməz/ (UK)| n. | 睡衣 | Loose-fitting nightclothes consisting of trousers and a jacket, typically made of cotton or silk. |  I always change into my pajamas before bed. 我总是在睡觉前换上睡衣。| synonyms: pyjamas, nightclothes, sleepwear | antonyms: daywear, street clothes | noun

pajamas:/pəˈdʒɑːməz/ (US), /pəˈdʒɑːməz/ (UK)| n. | 睡衣裤 |  (treated as plural) |  These pajamas are so soft and comfortable. 这条睡衣裤很柔软舒适。 | synonyms: pyjamas, nightclothes, sleepwear | antonyms: daywear, street clothes | noun
