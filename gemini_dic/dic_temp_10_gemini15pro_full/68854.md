fashionista:/ˌfæʃəˈnɪstə/
n.
时尚达人; 穿着入时的人
A person who is highly interested in fashion, especially in the latest styles and trends.
She's a real fashionista, always dressed in the latest designer clothes. 她是个真正的时尚达人，总是穿着最新的名牌服装。
近义词: trendsetter, style icon
反义词: None


fashionisto:/ˌfæʃəˈnɪstoʊ/
n.
男性时尚达人
A male version of a fashionista.
He's quite the fashionisto, always sporting the latest trends. 他是个十足的男性时尚达人，总是炫耀着最新的潮流。
近义词: dandy, trendsetter
反义词: None


Etymology:
From Italian *fashionista*, ultimately from *moda* "fashion," from Latin *modus* "manner, measure." The -ista suffix denotes a person who practices or is interested in a particular thing.
