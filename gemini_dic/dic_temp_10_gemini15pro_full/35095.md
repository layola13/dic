clothier:/ˈkloʊðiər/ (US), /ˈkləʊðiə(r)/ (UK)| n. |服装商，成衣商|A person who makes or sells clothes.  A clothier can refer to a single person who makes clothes, a shop owner who sells clothes, or a company that manufactures and/or sells clothing.|The local clothier offered a wide selection of suits.  这家当地的服装店提供各种各样的西装。|Synonyms: garment maker, apparel maker, outfitter, haberdasher (for men's clothing)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "customer," "consumer," or "buyer")|Noun
