ligate:/ˈlaɪɡeɪt/ /ˈlɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 结扎，捆绑 | To tie or bind something, especially with a ligature. | The surgeon ligated the artery to stop the bleeding.  外科医生结扎了动脉以止血。|  bind, tie, fasten | untie, loosen | verb

ligate:/ˈlaɪɡeɪt/ /ˈlɪɡeɪt/ | n. | 连接物 | Something that binds or ties. (Less common usage) |  The ligates used in the surgery were sterile.  手术中使用的连接物是无菌的。 | bond, tie, fastener |  release | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ligātus*, past participle of ligāre (“to bind, tie”), from Proto-Italic *ligā-, from Proto-Indo-European *leyǵ- (“to bind”).
