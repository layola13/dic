overwhelmingnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛlmɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
压倒性，势不可挡，极度
The state of being overwhelming; an excessive amount or degree.
The overwhelmingness of the evidence led to a quick conviction.  证据的压倒性导致了迅速定罪。
近义词：intensity, immensity, vastness
反义词：insignificance, weakness, mildness
词性：名词

词源:  From overwhelming + -ness.  Overwhelming from over- + whelm (to cover completely) + -ing.
