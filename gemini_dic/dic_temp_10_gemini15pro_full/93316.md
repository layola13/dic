ill-judged:/ˌɪlˈdʒʌdʒd/
adjective
判断错误的；不明智的
Showing or resulting from poor judgment; unwise.
His ill-judged remarks caused a lot of offense. 他不明智的言论引起了很多人的反感。
ill-advised, unwise, foolish, misguided, inappropriate, injudicious
wise, sensible, prudent, judicious
adjective
