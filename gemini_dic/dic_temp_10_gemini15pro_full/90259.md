housecoats:/ˈhaʊskoʊts/
n.|家居服，晨衣（尤指妇女穿的）|A loose, informal robe-like garment worn at home, typically by women.|She slipped into her housecoat after taking a shower. 她洗完澡后穿上了她的晨衣。|Synonyms: dressing gown, robe, house gown, negligee, duster|Antonyms: outdoor clothes, formal wear
