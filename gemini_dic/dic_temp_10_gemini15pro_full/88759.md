hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| n.|戏法，把戏，花招；胡言乱语|A form of words used by conjurors when performing tricks.  It's often used to describe deceptive or misleading language or actions.|The magician wowed the audience with his impressive hocus-pocus. 魔术师以其令人印象深刻的戏法让观众惊叹不已。|Synonyms: trickery, sleight of hand, magic, deception, mumbo jumbo|Antonyms: reality, truth, honesty|noun

hocus:/ˈhoʊkəs/| vt.|欺骗，施魔法|To trick or deceive someone.|He hocused the investors into believing his scheme was legitimate. 他欺骗投资者相信他的计划是合法的。|Synonyms: trick, deceive, cheat, swindle|Antonyms: be honest, be truthful|verb
