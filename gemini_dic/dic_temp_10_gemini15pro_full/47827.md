decategorized:/diːˈkætəɡəraɪzd/| adj.|去分类的，非分类的|Removed from a category or classification.  No longer belonging to a specific category.  Often implies a deliberate act of removing something from a previously assigned category.|The decategorized data was more difficult to analyze.  这些去分类的数据更难分析。|The library decategorized its fiction section, arranging books alphabetically by author instead of genre.  图书馆取消了小说类的分类，将书籍按作者而不是类型按字母顺序排列。|unclassified, ungrouped, uncategorized|categorized, classified|adjective
