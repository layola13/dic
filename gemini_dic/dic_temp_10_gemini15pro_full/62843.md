enjail: /ɪnˈdʒeɪl/
verb
监禁，关押
To put someone in jail; imprison.
The king enjailed his enemies. 国王监禁了他的敌人。

The journalist was enjailed for libel.  这名记者因诽谤罪被监禁。

近义词: imprison, incarcerate, confine, immure
反义词: release, liberate, free
词性: verb

词源:  From en- + jail.  The prefix "en-" means "to put into" or "to cause to be in".  "Jail" comes from Old French "jaiole," ultimately from Latin "cavea" meaning "cage."
