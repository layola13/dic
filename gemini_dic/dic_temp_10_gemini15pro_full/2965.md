adzes:/ˈædzɪz/ | n. | 手斧，扁斧 | A tool with a curved blade set at right angles to the handle, used for shaping wood. | The carpenter used adzes to shape the beams for the roof. 木匠用扁斧来 shaping 屋顶的梁。

The museum displayed ancient adzes used by early humans.  博物馆展出了早期人类使用的古代手斧。 | 近义词: axe, hatchet | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *adesa (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *adiziō, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂edʰes- (“adze”). Cognate with Old High German dehsala (German Dechsel), Old Norse adsa.


USAGE->
复数用法:
The adzes were sharp and ready for use. = 这些手斧很锋利，可以使用了。
He collected antique adzes from different cultures. = 他收集了来自不同文化的古董手斧。


所有格用法 (不常见，但理论上可行):
The adzes' handles were made of wood. = 这些手斧的把手是木制的。 (更常见的表达方式是: The handles of the adzes were made of wood.)
