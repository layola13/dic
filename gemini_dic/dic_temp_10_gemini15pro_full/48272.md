decorums:/dɪˈkɔːrəmz/
n.
礼仪，礼节
propriety and good taste in conduct, manners, speech, dress, etc.
She was careful to observe the social decorums. 她小心翼翼地遵守社交礼仪。

decorum:/dɪˈkɔːrəm/
n.
礼仪，礼节
behavior in keeping with good taste and propriety.
He acted with decorum. 他举止得体。
The club members are expected to maintain decorum at all times.  俱乐部成员被要求时刻保持礼貌。

近义词: propriety, etiquette, decency, civility
反义词: impropriety, indecency, rudeness
