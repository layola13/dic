homatropine:/hɒˈmæt.rə.piːn/ | n. | 后马托品 | An anticholinergic medication used to dilate the pupil of the eye. |  Homatropine is used to dilate pupils before eye examinations. 后马托品用于眼科检查前散瞳。

Homatropine eye drops are prescribed to treat uveitis.  后马托品眼药水被用来治疗葡萄膜炎。 | 近义词: atropine, cyclopentolate | 反义词: pilocarpine | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From homo- + atropine, referring to its chemical similarity to atropine.  由 homo- + atropine 组成，指其与阿托品的化学相似性。
