offshoots:/ˈɒfʃuːts/ /ˈɔːfʃuːts/
n.|分支；支流；衍生物；旁系子孙|A thing that branches off from something larger.|The company has several offshoots in other countries. 这家公司在其他国家有几个分支机构。|branches, subsidiaries, spin-offs|mainstream, core|noun
offshoots:/ˈɒfʃuːts/ /ˈɔːfʃuːts/
n.|结果；副产品|Something that develops from something else, often unexpectedly.|This line of research is an offshoot of our main project. 这项研究是我们主要项目的一个副产品。|results, byproducts, consequences|origin, source|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From off + shoot.  "Off" indicating separation or deviation, and "shoot" referring to a new growth from a plant.
