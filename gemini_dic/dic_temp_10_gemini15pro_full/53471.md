disarmingly:/dɪsˈɑːmɪŋli/
disarmingly| adv.|  解除警戒地，消弭敌意地，使人放下戒备地|  In a way that removes suspicion or hostility, making someone feel less guarded or defensive. | He smiled disarmingly, and I immediately felt at ease. 他露出了令人放松的笑容，我立刻感到轻松自在。|  Synonym: charmingly, winningly, persuasively | Antonym: alarmingly, threateningly |词性: 副词

disarming:/dɪsˈɑːmɪŋ/
disarming| adj.|  使人消除敌意的；令人放松的| Having the effect of removing suspicion or hostility, making someone feel less guarded or defensive. |  He had a disarming smile that put everyone at ease.  他有一个令人放松的微笑，让每个人都感到自在。| Synonym: charming, winning, persuasive | Antonym: alarming, threatening |词性: 形容词

disarm:/dɪsˈɑːrm/
disarm| vt.| 解除武装；消除敌意；使息怒| 1.  To take weapons away from someone. 2. To remove suspicion or hostility, making someone feel less guarded or defensive. | The police disarmed the robber. 警察解除了抢匪的武装。/ Her friendly approach disarmed his anger. 她友好的态度消除了他的愤怒。| Synonym:  neutralize, conciliate, appease (for sense 2) | Antonym: arm, antagonize, provoke (for sense 2) |词性: 动词

词源:  From dis- (meaning "reverse" or "remove") + arm (meaning "to equip with weapons").  The literal meaning is "to remove weapons," which extends figuratively to removing hostility or suspicion.
