detains:/dɪˈteɪnz/
verb
拘留，扣留
keeps someone in official custody, typically for questioning about a crime or in politically sensitive situations.
The police detained him for questioning. 警察把他拘留审问。
hold back or delay.
Heavy traffic detained us for an hour.  交通拥堵耽误了我们一个小时。

synonym:delay, hold, keep, confine, imprison, arrest
antonym:release, free, liberate
