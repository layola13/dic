founding:/ˈfaʊndɪŋ/ | adj. | 创始的，成立的 |  Relating to the establishment or creation of something. |  He was a founding member of the club. 他是俱乐部的创始会员。| The founding fathers of the United States played a crucial role in shaping the nation. 美国的开国元勋们在塑造这个国家方面发挥了至关重要的作用。|  inaugural, original, initial | later, subsequent | adjective
