incondensability:/ˌɪnkɒndɛnsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌɪnkɑːndɛnsəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可凝结性，不可冷凝性|The incondensability of certain gases makes them difficult to liquefy.  某些气体的不可凝结性使其难以液化。|例句：The incondensability of helium at normal temperatures poses a challenge for storage. 氦在常温下的不可凝结性给储存带来了挑战。|近义词：noncondensability, non-condensability|反义词：condensability|词性:名词
