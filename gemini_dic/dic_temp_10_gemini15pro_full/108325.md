lausanne:/loʊˈzæn/ | n. | 洛桑 |A city in western Switzerland, on Lake Geneva; population 139,000 (est. 2009). It is the headquarters of the International Olympic Committee.| The Olympic Museum is located in Lausanne. 奥林匹克博物馆位于洛桑。| 近义词: Geneva (as a Swiss city) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a city name) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *Lausanne*, ultimately from Latin *Lausonium* (name of a Roman settlement).
