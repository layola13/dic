disaffectedly:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛktɪdli/| adv.|不满意地，不满地|In a manner showing discontent and lack of trust; resentfully.|He folded his arms disaffectedly and glared at the speaker. 他不满地抱起胳膊，怒视着演讲者。|Synonyms: resentfully, sullenly, grudgingly, rebelliously|Antonyms: enthusiastically, contentedly, approvingly|Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From disaffect + -ed + -ly. Disaffect comes from dis- (expressing negation) + affect (to act on; to influence).
