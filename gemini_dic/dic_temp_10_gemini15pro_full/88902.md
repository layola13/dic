holcomania: /ˌhɒlkəˈmeɪniə/ /ˌhoʊlkəˈmeɪniə/
| n. | 痴迷；狂热；着魔|An excessive enthusiasm or passion for something.|He had a holcomania for collecting stamps. 他对集邮着了迷。|近义词：obsession, mania, craze, infatuation|反义词：apathy, indifference|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Greek ὅλος (hólos, “whole”) +‎ μανία (manía, “madness, frenzy, enthusiasm”).  Essentially, a "whole" or complete madness/enthusiasm for something.
