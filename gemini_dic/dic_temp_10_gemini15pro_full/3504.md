aftersensation:/ˌæftərˈsɛnˈseɪʃən/| n. |后觉；余觉|A sensation that persists after the stimulus causing it has ceased.|The aftersensation of the bright light made it difficult to see in the dimly lit room.  明亮的光线过后，在昏暗的房间里很难看清东西。|Synonyms: afterimage, aftereffect, residual sensation|Antonyms: presensation|noun
