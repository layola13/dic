antitarnishing:/ˌæntiˈtɑːrnɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
防锈的，防腐蚀的
Resistant to tarnishing; preventing discoloration or loss of luster, especially in metals.
This antitarnishing silver polish will keep your jewelry looking new. 这种防锈银抛光剂可以让你的珠宝看起来像新的一样。
Silver requires special antitarnishing cloths for storage.  银器存放需要特殊的防锈布。
近义词:corrosion-resistant, rustproof, non-tarnishing
反义词:tarnishing, corrosive
词性:adjective
