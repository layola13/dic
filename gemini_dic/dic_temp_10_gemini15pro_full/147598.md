overplanting: /ˌōvərˈplæntiNG/
n.|过度种植|The act of planting too densely or too much of a single crop, leading to negative consequences.|Overplanting of corn can deplete soil nutrients. 过度种植玉米会耗尽土壤养分。|overcropping, excessive planting|underplanting|noun
Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "planting" (from "plant" (v.), from Old French planter, from Latin plantare "to fix in place, set, plant")
