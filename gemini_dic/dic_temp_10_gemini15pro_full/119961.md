millennium:/mɪˈlɛniəm/| n. |千年，千禧年|a period of one thousand years, especially as calculated from the traditional date of the birth of Christ|例句：The new millennium brought with it a sense of hope and renewal. 新千年的到来带来了一种希望和复兴的感觉。

例句：People celebrated the turn of the millennium with great fanfare. 人们以盛大的庆祝活动来庆祝千禧年的到来。|近义词：thousand years, chiliad |反义词: (No direct antonym for a period of time) |词性:Noun


Etymology: From Latin *millennium*, from *mille* "thousand" + *annum* "year".
