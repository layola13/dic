derth:/dɜːrθ/
n.
scarcity, lack
a scarcity or lack of something.
The derth of affordable housing is a serious problem in many cities. 城市里经济适用房的缺乏是一个严重的问题。
近义词：scarcity, lack, shortage, paucity
反义词：abundance, surplus, plethora, excess
词性：名词

词源: From Middle English *derthe, from Old Norse *dyrð (“dearth, famine, high price”), from Proto-Germanic *durþiz (“dearness”), from Proto-Indo-European *der- (“to harm, injure”). Cognate with Icelandic dýrð (“glory, fame, honour”). More at dear.
