expansionist:/ɪkˈspænʃənɪst/| adj. n.|扩张主义的；扩张主义者|Relating to or characteristic of expansionism; a person who favors expansionism, especially territorial expansion.|例句：The expansionist policies of the empire led to many wars.  帝国的扩张主义政策导致了许多战争。|近义词：aggressive, imperialist, acquisitive|反义词：pacifist, isolationist|词性：形容词/名词
expansionist:/ɪkˈspænʃənɪst/| n.|扩张主义者|A person who favors expansionism, especially territorial expansion.|例句：The expansionist was eager to conquer new lands. 扩张主义者渴望征服新的土地。|近义词：imperialist, aggressor|反义词：pacifist, isolationist|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From expansion + -ist. Expansion from Latin expansio, from expandere "to spread out," from ex- "out" + pandere "to spread."
