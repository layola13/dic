abiogenesis: /ˌeɪbaɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
自然发生; 无生源说
The development of life from non-living matter.
Some scientists believe abiogenesis occurred in hydrothermal vents deep in the ocean.  一些科学家认为自然发生起源于深海热液喷口。

Abiogenesis is a complex process that is still not fully understood. 自然发生是一个复杂的过程，至今仍未完全了解。

近义词: spontaneous generation
反义词: biogenesis


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + bio- (life) + genesis (origin)
