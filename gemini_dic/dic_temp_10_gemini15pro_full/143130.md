outcarolling:/ˌaʊtkəˈrɒlɪŋ/
verb
胜过歌声; 比…唱得更好听
surpassing in carolling; singing more beautifully than.
The nightingale was outcarolling all the other birds in the forest. 夜莺的歌声胜过森林里所有其他的鸟。
She outcarolled the renowned soprano. 她的歌声胜过了那位著名的女高音。
synonyms: outsing, surpass in singing
antonyms: be outsung, be surpassed in singing
词性:v.
