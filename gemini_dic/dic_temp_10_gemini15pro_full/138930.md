nutritiousnesses:/nʊˈtrɪʃəsnəsɪz/| n. |营养价值；营养成分|The state or quality of being nutritious.  The plural form of nutritiousness, though rarely used, emphasizes multiple aspects or instances of nutritional value.|  This diet emphasizes the nutritiousnesses of fruits and vegetables. 这种饮食强调水果和蔬菜的营养价值。 |Near Synonyms:  nutritional value, nutritive value, nourishment| Antonym: unhealthiness|noun
