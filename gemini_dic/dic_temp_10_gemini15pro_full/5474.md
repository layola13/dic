alon:/əˈloʊn/ | adj. adv.|独自的，单独的；独自地，单独地| Being alone means being without the company of other people.|She lives alone. 她独自一人生活。| 近义词: solitary, lonely, solo| 反义词: together, accompanied, with someone| 词性:形容词/副词

词源:  From Middle English *alon, from Old English *allāna, *ānlāna, from *all + *āna (“one”). Cognate with Scots alane (“alone”), West Frisian allinne (“alone”), Dutch alleen (“only, alone”), German allein (“alone”), Swedish allena (“alone”).
