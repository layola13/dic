digital-signature:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈsɪɡnətʃər/| n. |数字签名|A digital code (generated and authenticated by public key encryption) which is attached to an electronically transmitted document to verify its contents and the sender's identity.|Example: The email was secured with a digital signature to prove it came from the CEO.  这封电子邮件附有数字签名，以证明它来自首席执行官。|Synonym: electronic signature, e-signature|Antonym: N/A|noun

digital-signatures:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈsɪɡnətʃərz/| n. |数字签名（复数）|Plural form of digital signature, referring to multiple digital codes used to authenticate electronic documents.|Example:  All contracts require digital signatures from both parties. 所有合同都需要双方签署数字签名。|Synonym: electronic signatures, e-signatures|Antonym: N/A|noun
