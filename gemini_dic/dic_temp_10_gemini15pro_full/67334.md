extend:/ɪkˈstend/ (UK), /ɪkˈstɛnd/ (US)| v. |延伸，延长，扩大，扩展; 提供，给予;  | To make something longer or larger in space or time; to offer or give something such as help, thanks, or an invitation | The company plans to extend its operations into Asia.  该公司计划将其业务扩展到亚洲。| 近义词: lengthen, expand, prolong, stretch, offer, proffer | 反义词: shorten, contract, reduce, curtail, withdraw | verb

extend:/ɪkˈstend/ (UK), /ɪkˈstɛnd/ (US)| n. | 延伸，扩展 | An act of extending something. (This noun form is less common) |  The extend of the damage was surprising.  损害的范围令人惊讶。| 近义词: expansion, lengthening, prolongation | 反义词: reduction, shortening, contraction | noun
