desuetude:/ˈdɛswɪˌtjuːd/ /ˈdɛswituːd/| n. |废止，不用；过时|A state of disuse or inactivity. The practice had fallen into desuetude.  这种做法已经废弃不用了。|例句：The old building fell into desuetude after the factory closed.  工厂关闭后，这座旧建筑就废弃不用了。

例句：Many old customs have fallen into desuetude. 许多古老的习俗已经废弃不用了。|近义词：disuse, obsolescence, inactivity|反义词：use, currency, activity|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *desuetudo (“disuse”), from Latin desuetus (“disused, unaccustomed”), past participle of desuescere (“to disaccustom, discontinue”), from de- (“away from”) + suescere (“to become accustomed, to be wont”), from Latin suus (“one's own”).
