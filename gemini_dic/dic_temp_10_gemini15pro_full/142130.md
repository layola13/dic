originalities:/əˌrɪdʒəˈnælɪtiz/
n.
独创性；新颖；新奇的想法
the quality of being new and interesting in a way that is different from anything that has existed before
Her designs have great originality. 她的设计非常有创意。

近义词:novelties, innovations,creativities
反义词:banalities, staleness, clichés
词性:名词复数
