obscurantists:/əbˌskjʊˈræntɪsts/ | n. | 蒙昧主义者；故弄玄虚者 |  A person who deliberately makes matters unclear, obscure, or difficult to understand.  | The academic obscurantists refused to accept the new theory. 那些学术界的蒙昧主义者拒绝接受新理论。 |  obscurantist (adjective form), dogmatist, pedant |  enlightener, elucidator | noun

obscurantist:/əbˌskjʊˈræntɪst/ | adj. | 蒙昧主义的；故弄玄虚的 |  Relating to or characteristic of obscurantism; deliberately unclear or abstruse.  | The professor's obscurantist lectures left the students confused. 教授故弄玄虚的讲课让学生们感到困惑。|  dogmatic, pedantic, esoteric |  lucid, clear, transparent | adjective
