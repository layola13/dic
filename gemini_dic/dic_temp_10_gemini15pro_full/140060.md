oestrogenic:/ˌiːstrəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 雌激素的；产生雌激素的 | Relating to or producing estrogen, a female sex hormone. |  Soybeans contain oestrogenic compounds. 大豆含有雌激素化合物。

The drug has an oestrogenic effect. 这种药有雌激素样的作用。


estrogen:/ˈiːstrədʒən/ | n. | 雌激素 | Any of a group of steroid hormones that promote the development and maintenance of female characteristics of the body. |  Estrogen levels fluctuate throughout a woman's menstrual cycle.  雌激素水平在女性的月经周期中波动。

Doctors may prescribe estrogen to relieve menopausal symptoms. 医生可能会开雌激素来缓解更年期症状。


-genic:/ˈdʒɛnɪk/ | suffix | 产生...的；由…引起的 | Producing; produced by. | carcinogenic  致癌的


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek οἶστρος (oîstros, “gadfly; frenzy; lust”) + -genic.

SYNONYMS->
estrogen-producing, feminizing

ANTONYMS->
