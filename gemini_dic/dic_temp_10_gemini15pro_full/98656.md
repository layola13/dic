insufficiently:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃəntli/
adverb
不足地；不够地
not enough; inadequately
The building was insufficiently heated.  这栋楼供暖不足。
The project was insufficiently funded, leading to its eventual failure.  这个项目资金不足，最终导致失败。
近义词: inadequately, deficiently, poorly, improperly
反义词: sufficiently, adequately, amply, fully
词性: adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + sufficient (enough) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin sufficere (to be sufficient).
