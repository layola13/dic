conferment's:/kənˈfɜːrmənts/
n.

The act of conferring something, such as an honor, degree, or title.
The conferment's ceremony was held in the grand hall. 授予仪式在大会堂举行。
Near Synonyms: bestowal, awarding, presentation, granting
Antonyms: revocation, withdrawal, denial
