awl's:/ɔːlz/| n.| 锥子; |A pointed tool for making small holes, especially in leather.|She used an awl to make holes in the leather for the stitching. 她用锥子在皮革上扎孔以便缝合。|synonyms: bradawl, piercer, punch|antonyms: none|noun

Etymology:
From Old English *æle, from Proto-Germanic *aliz (whence also Old Norse alr), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂él- (“awl”).
