marvellingly:/ˈmɑːrvəlɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人惊叹地，不可思议地 | In a way that causes wonder; extraordinarily. |  She played marvellously well. 她演奏得非常好。 |  The cake was marvellously decorated. 蛋糕装饰得非常漂亮。 | 近义词: wonderfully, amazingly, astonishingly, remarkably, superbly | 反义词: poorly, badly, terribly | 词性: 副词

词源: From marvel + -ing + -ly.  "Marvel" comes from Old French "merveille" meaning "wonder, miracle," ultimately from Latin "mirabilia," plural of "mirabilis" "wonderful," from "mirari" "to wonder at."
