aemail: /ˈeɪmeɪl/ | n. | 电子邮件 |  A shortened form of "electronic mail," referring to messages sent and received electronically over a network. | I sent him an aemail yesterday. 我昨天给他发了一封电子邮件。 | synonym: email, e-mail | antonym: snail mail, letter | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "electronic mail," with the "electronic" portion shortened to "e-."  This shortening became common with the rise of the internet and the widespread use of email.
