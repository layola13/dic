overbounteously: /ˌoʊvərˈbaʊntiəsli/
adverb
过度慷慨地；过分丰富地
in an excessively generous or abundant manner.
He overbounteously showered her with gifts. 他过分慷慨地送给她很多礼物。
近义词: lavishly, profusely, excessively, exorbitantly
反义词: sparingly, meagerly, stingily
词性: 副词

词源:
From over + bounteous + -ly.  "Bounteous" comes from the Old French "bounte" meaning goodness, virtue, kindness.
