elongated:/ɪˈlɒŋɡeɪtɪd/ /iˈlɔːŋɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 延长了的，拉长的 |Made longer than usual; stretched out. |例句：The artist drew an elongated figure.  艺术家画了一个拉长的人物。

例句：The giraffe has an elongated neck. 长颈鹿的脖子很长。

近义词： lengthened, extended, stretched, prolonged | 反义词： shortened, compressed, contracted | 词性：形容词

elongate:/ɪˈlɒŋɡeɪt/ /iˈlɔːŋɡeɪt/ | v. |  (使)延长，(使)伸长 | To make something longer, or to grow longer. | 例句：The cells elongate as they grow. 细胞在生长过程中会变长。

例句：He elongated his stride to catch up with her. 他加大了步伐赶上她。

近义词： lengthen, extend, stretch, prolong | 反义词： shorten, compress, contract |词性：动词

elongation:/ˌiːlɒŋˈɡeɪʃn/ /ˌiːlɔːŋˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 延长，伸长 | The act of making something longer, or the state of being made longer. |例句：The elongation of the metal rod was caused by heat. 金属棒的伸长是由热引起的。

例句：The painting featured the elongation of human forms. 这幅画的特点是拉长了人物的形态。

近义词： lengthening, extension, stretching | 反义词： shortening, compression, contraction | 词性：名词


词源: From Late Latin elongatus, past participle of elongare "to lengthen," from ex- "out" + longus "long."
