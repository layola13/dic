banland:/ˈbænˌlænd/| n.| 禁地，禁止入内的土地|Land where entry or settlement is prohibited. |Example: The military base was declared banland to civilians.  军事基地被宣布为平民禁地。|Example:  The contaminated area after the chemical spill became banland. 化学品泄漏后的污染区域变成了禁地。|近义词：forbidden land, restricted area, no-go zone|反义词:public land, accessible area|词性:noun
