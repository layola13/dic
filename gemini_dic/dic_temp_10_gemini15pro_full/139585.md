obviator:/ˈɒbvieɪtər/ (US) /ˈɒbviˌeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |消除者，排除者|Someone or something that obviates something, removes a difficulty or the need for something.|The new software obviates the need for manual data entry. 新软件消除了手动数据录入的需要。|Synonyms: eliminator, remover, preventer|Antonyms: creator, instigator|noun
