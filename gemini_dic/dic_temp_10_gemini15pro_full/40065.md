contactless:/ˈkɒntæktləs/ /ˈkɑːntæktləs/
adjective
非接触式的，无接触的
Relating to or involving technologies that allow a device to be used without being touched.  A contactless payment system allows customers to pay for goods by waving their bank card over a reader.
Example: I paid for my groceries using the contactless payment option on my phone. 我用手机上的非接触式支付选项支付了我的杂货。
Example: Contactless technology is becoming increasingly popular. 非接触式技术正变得越来越流行。
Synonyms: touch-free, wireless, remote
Antonyms: contact, physical
词性: 形容词
