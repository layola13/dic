imprisonable:/ɪmˈprɪzənəbl/
adjective
可监禁的
Capable of being imprisoned; deserving of imprisonment.
His actions were deemed imprisonable offenses. 他的行为被认为是可监禁的罪行。
The judge ruled that the crime was imprisonable. 法官裁定该罪行可判处监禁。
Synonyms: jailable, incarcerable
Antonyms: non-imprisonable, pardonable
词性: adjective
