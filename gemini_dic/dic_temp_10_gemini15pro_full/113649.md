machine-tooled:/məˈʃiːn tuːld/
adjective
机加工的
Made or shaped by machine tools.
The watch has a beautifully machine-tooled finish. 这块手表有精美的机加工表面。
近义词:machine-made, precision-engineered
反义词:hand-crafted, hand-made
词性:adjective

machine:/məˈʃiːn/ | n. | 机器 | A device that performs a task, typically powered by electricity or an engine.  | The sewing machine is old but still works. 这台缝纫机很旧但还能用。 | 近义词:device, apparatus, appliance | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "manual tool")
tool:/tuːl/ | n. | 工具 | An implement, especially one held in the hand, used to carry out a particular purpose. | A hammer is a useful tool. 锤子是一个有用的工具。 | 近义词:implement, utensil, instrument | 反义词:(No direct antonym)

词源: "machine" comes from Old French "machine," ultimately from Latin "machina" (contrivance, engine, military machine). "Tool" comes from Old English "tōl" (instrument, implement).
