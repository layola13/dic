epitheca:/ˈɛpɪˌθikə/ | n. | 上壳，盖壳 | The epitheca is the larger, older portion of a diatom's cell wall, fitting over the hypotheca like a lid. |  Diatoms are encased in a silica shell composed of two parts, the epitheca and the hypotheca. 硅藻包裹在一个由两部分组成的硅质壳中，即上壳和下壳。| The intricate patterns on the epitheca of diatoms are used for identification. 硅藻上壳的复杂图案用于识别。 |  valve, theca | frustule | noun
