gurge:/ɡɜːrdʒ/| n. v. | 涌流，倾泻；（使）大量涌出 | A large amount of something that flows or gushes out. | The dam burst, sending a gurge of water down the valley.  大坝决堤，山谷里涌出一股洪流。|  近义词：gush, surge, flood, outpouring | 反义词: trickle, drip | 词性:名词,动词

gurge:/ɡɜːrdʒ/| n. v. |  吞没；吞噬；狼吞虎咽 | To swallow something greedily or in large amounts. | He gurged down the entire bottle of water in one go. 他一口气吞下了整瓶水。 | 近义词：gulp, devour, gobble | 反义词: nibble | 词性:名词,动词
