nonassignment:/ˌnɒnəˈsaɪnmənt/ | n. | 不分配；不指派；不转让 | The nonassignment clause in the contract prevents the transfer of rights without prior consent.  合同中的不转让条款阻止未经事先同意转让权利。|  The nonassignment of the patent led to its eventual expiration.  该专利的未转让导致其最终失效。 |  non-allocation, non-distribution | assignment | noun
Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "assignment" (from Old French "assigner" and ultimately Latin "assignare" - to mark out, designate)
