antielitist:/ˌæntiɪˈliːtɪst/
adjective
反精英主义的; 反对精英主义的
Opposed to or hostile towards elitism.
The candidate ran on an antielitist platform, appealing to working-class voters.  这位候选人以反精英主义的纲领进行竞选，吸引了工人阶级的选民。
近义词:populist, egalitarian
反义词:elitist, snobbish
词性:adjective

antielitist:/ˌæntiɪˈliːtɪst/
noun
反精英主义者
A person who opposes elitism.
The antielitists protested the exclusive club's opening.  反精英主义者抗议这家专属俱乐部的开业。
近义词:populist, egalitarian
反义词:elitist, snob
词性:noun
