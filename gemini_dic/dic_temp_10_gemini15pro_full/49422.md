Deloitte:/dəˈlɔɪt/| n.|德勤|A multinational professional services network providing audit, consulting, financial advisory, risk management, tax, and legal services.  It is one of the "Big Four" accounting organizations and the largest professional services network in the world by revenue and number of professionals.  The name "Deloitte" refers to the global brand of the network of independent member firms.| Deloitte audited our company's financial statements. 德勤审计了我们公司的财务报表。|Synonyms: PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC), Ernst & Young (EY), KPMG|Antonyms: N/A|noun
