misappropriate: /ˌmɪsəˈproʊprieɪt/ | v. | 盗用，挪用，滥用 | To dishonestly or unfairly take (something, especially money or property belonging to another) for one's own use. | The CEO was accused of misappropriating company funds. 首席执行官被指控挪用公司资金。|  He misappropriated the charity's funds for his own personal use. 他盗用了慈善基金作私用。 | synonyms: embezzle, pilfer, purloin, steal, appropriate | antonyms: donate, contribute, return, reimburse | verb

misappropriated: /ˌmɪsəˈproʊprieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被盗用的，被挪用的，被滥用的 |  Having been dishonestly or unfairly taken (especially money or property) for another's own use. | The misappropriated funds were never recovered. 被挪用的资金从未追回。 |  The investigation revealed a large amount of misappropriated resources. 调查揭露了大量的被滥用资源。 | synonyms: embezzled, pilfered, purloined, stolen | antonyms: donated, contributed, returned, reimbursed | adjective
