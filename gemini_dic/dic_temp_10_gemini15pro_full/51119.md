despisement:/dɪˈspaɪzmənt/| n.|轻蔑，鄙视|The feeling of despising someone or something; contempt.|He looked at her with despisement in his eyes. 他带着鄙视的眼神看着她。|近义词：contempt, scorn, disdain, loathing|反义词:admiration, respect|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *despisement, from Old French despitement, from despiter (“to despise”), from des- + piter (“to esteem, value”), from Latin pitāre (“to appease, propitiate”).
