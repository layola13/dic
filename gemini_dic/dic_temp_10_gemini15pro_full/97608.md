inhalant:/ɪnˈheɪlənt/ | n. | 吸入剂；可吸入的物质 | A substance that is inhaled, often for medicinal or recreational purposes. |  The fumes from the inhalant made him dizzy.  吸入剂的烟雾使他头晕。| synonyms:  aerosol, vapor, propellant | antonyms: exhalant | noun


inhalant: /ɪnˈheɪlənt/ | adj. | 可吸入的 |  Relating to or designed for inhalation.  | The inhalant medication provided quick relief. 可吸入的药物提供了快速缓解。 | synonyms: breathable, respirable | antonyms: non-inhalable | adjective
