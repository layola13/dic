iffiest:/ˈɪfɪɪst/
adjective
最不确定的，最犹豫的
most uncertain; most hesitant.
例句：It was the iffiest proposal I'd ever heard.  这是我听过的最不靠谱的提议。

例句：He had the iffiest smile I've ever seen. 他脸上露出了我见过的最犹豫不决的笑容。

近义词：uncertain, hesitant, doubtful, dubious, questionable
反义词：certain, sure, confident, resolute, decisive
词性：adjective
