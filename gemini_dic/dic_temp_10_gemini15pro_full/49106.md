deil:/diːl/
noun
devil; mischievous person; rogue
a mischievous person (often used playfully or affectionately)
The wee deil hid my keys again!  这个小淘气鬼又把我的钥匙藏起来了！
近义词: devil, imp, rascal, scamp, rogue
反义词: angel
词性:n.


deil:/diːl/
verb (Scotland)
to deal, share out
to distribute or share something
The auld man deiled oot the sweeties tae the bairns.  老人把糖果分给了孩子们。
近义词: distribute, share, dole out
反义词: hoard, keep
词性:v.
