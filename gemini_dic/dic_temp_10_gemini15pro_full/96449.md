indiums: /ˈɪndiəmz/
noun
铟（复数）
plural form of indium, a chemical element with the symbol In and atomic number 49.  It is a soft, silvery-white metal.
Several indiums isotopes are used in nuclear medicine.  多个铟同位素用于核医学。
Synonyms: (none for the element itself, but for its uses perhaps: metals, elements)
Antonyms: (none for elements)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From indigo, referring to the indigo blue line in its spectrum, from Latin indicum, from Greek indikon, meaning "Indian dye".
