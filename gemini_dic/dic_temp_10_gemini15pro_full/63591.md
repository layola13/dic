envisioning:/ɪnˈvɪʒəniŋ/ | v. | 构想，设想 | The act of forming a mental image or concept of something, especially something that is not yet real or has not yet happened.  |  She was envisioning a future where she lived in a small cottage by the sea. 她正在设想一个未来，她住在海边的一个小别墅里。 | imagining, visualizing, conceiving, picturing |  - | Verb
