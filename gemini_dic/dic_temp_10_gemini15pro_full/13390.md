atom-bombs:/ˈætəm bɒmz/ (US) /ˈætəm bɒmz/ (UK)| n.|原子弹|A weapon of mass destruction that uses nuclear fission to release a tremendous amount of energy.|The atom-bombs dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki brought World War II to an end. 原子弹轰炸广岛和长崎，结束了第二次世界大战。|synonyms: nuclear bombs, A-bombs, nukes|antonyms: conventional weapons|noun
