abdominals: /æbˈdɒmɪnəlz/ | n. | 腹肌 | The abdominal muscles are the muscles in the front of the abdomen. |  She's been doing crunches to strengthen her abdominals. 她一直在做仰卧起坐来加强腹肌。 |  abs, stomach muscles |  None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *abdominalles*, from Latin *abdominalis* (“of or belonging to the abdomen”), from *abdōmen* (“belly, paunch”), perhaps from *abdere* (“to hide”) + an unknown suffix.
