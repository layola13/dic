nonentreatingly:/nɒnɪnˈtriːtɪŋli/| adv.|不以对待的方式，不加处理地|Referring to something not subjected to a particular treatment or process.  It suggests a lack of intervention or manipulation.|The data was analyzed nonentreatingly, simply observing the trends.  数据未经处理就被分析了，只是观察了趋势。|The raw ingredients were presented nonentreatingly, allowing the judges to experience their natural flavors.  原料未经处理就呈现在评委面前，让他们体验其天然风味。|neutrally, objectively, passively|actively, manipulatively|adverb
