multilobar:/ˌmʌltiˈloʊbər/ | adj. | 多叶的；多瓣的 | Having multiple lobes.  A lobe is a rounded projection or division, often used in anatomy to describe parts of organs like the lungs, brain, or liver. | The radiologist identified a multilobar pneumonia in the patient's right lung. 放射科医生在病人的右肺中发现了一个多叶性肺炎。| multilobulated, multipartite | unilobar | adjective
