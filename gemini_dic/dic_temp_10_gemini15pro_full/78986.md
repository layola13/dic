girthed:/ɡɜːrθt/
verb| 测量周长；环绕；围绕 |To measure the circumference of something, especially a person's waist. Also, to encircle or surround.|The tailor carefully girthed the customer's waist.  裁缝仔细地测量了顾客的腰围。|
The ancient oak was girthed by a thick vine.  这棵古老的橡树被粗壮的藤蔓环绕着。|Synonyms: encircle, encompass, surround, band|Antonyms: unbind, loosen, free|v.


girthed:/ɡɜːrθt/
adjective|有特定周长的；被环绕的|Having a specified girth; encircled or surrounded.| a tree girthed with ivy  一棵被常春藤环绕的树|A man girthed about the waist with a rope 一个腰上缠着绳子的男人|Synonyms: encircled, encompassed, surrounded, banded|Antonyms: unbound, loosened, freed|adj.
