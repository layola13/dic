esurine:/ˈɛsjʊraɪn/ | adj. | 与尿液相关的；尿液的 | Relating to or of the nature of urine; urinary. | The esurine odor permeated the air. 空气中弥漫着尿液的气味。 |  近义词: urinary | 反义词: None | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *esurīnus*, from *esuries* "hunger".  While seemingly unrelated to urine, the connection lies in the ancient belief that urine could be used as a hunger suppressant.  Over time, the word's meaning shifted to solely refer to urine itself.
