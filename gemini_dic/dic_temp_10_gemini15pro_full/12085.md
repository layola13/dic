arting-up:/ˈɑːtɪŋ ʌp/
phrasal verb
打扮；装饰；美化
The process of making something more visually appealing, often in a superficial or exaggerated way.
They were arting up the float for the parade. 他们正在为游行装饰彩车。
dress up, decorate, embellish
dress down, simplify
