noncommendableness:/ˌnɒnkəmɛndəblnəs/
n.
不可称道, 不值得推荐
lack of commendableness; the state or quality of not being worthy of praise or recommendation.
His noncommendableness made him unpopular. 他的不值得称道使他不受欢迎。
近义词:undesirability, unworthiness, reprehensibility
反义词: commendableness, praiseworthiness, merit
词性:名词
