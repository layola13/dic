overdramatized: /ˌoʊvərˈdræməˌtaɪzd/ | adj. | 过分戏剧化的，夸张的 | Exaggerated or represented in a melodramatic way, making something seem more important or serious than it really is. | She overdramatized the story of her trip to the grocery store, making it sound like a life-or-death adventure. 她把去杂货店的故事说得太夸张了，听起来像是一场生死攸关的冒险。 |  The media often overdramatizes minor incidents. 媒体经常夸大轻微的事件。 | 近义词: exaggerated, melodramatic, theatrical, histrionic | 反义词: understated, downplayed, realistic | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + dramatize (verb meaning "to present in a dramatic or theatrical manner") + -ed (suffix forming the past participle)
