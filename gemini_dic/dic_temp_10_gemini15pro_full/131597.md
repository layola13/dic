noncompetitors:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtərz/ | n. | 非竞争者，非竞争对手 |  Individuals or businesses that do not compete with one another in a particular market or industry.  They may operate in different markets, offer different products or services, or target different customer segments. |  The small bookstore thrived because its noncompetitors were large online retailers selling different types of books.  这家小型书店生意兴隆，因为它的非竞争对手是销售不同类型书籍的大型在线零售商。

The software company partnered with several noncompetitors to expand its market reach. 该软件公司与几家非竞争对手合作，以扩大其市场范围。 |  Near synonyms: non-rivals, non-contenders | Antonyms: competitors, rivals | Noun
