cover-ups:/ˈkʌvərˌʌps/ (US) /ˈkʌvərˌʌps/ (UK)
noun
掩盖；隐瞒；遮掩物
actions or statements that are intended to hide the truth about something illegal or embarrassing
The company was accused of a cover-up of the safety risks.  这家公司被指控掩盖安全风险。
The president denied any involvement in the cover-up. 总统否认参与了掩盖事件。
concealment, disguise, whitewash
revelation, exposure
n.
