hearken:/ˈhɑːrkən/ (US), /ˈhɑːkən/ (UK)| vi.|倾听，留心，关注|To listen attentively; to pay attention.|例句：Hearken to my words and you will understand.  留心我的话，你就会明白。

例句：He refused to hearken to their pleas for mercy.  他不理会他们恳求怜悯的请求。


近义词：listen, attend, heed, pay attention|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hearcian, hyrcnian "to listen, hear," from Proto-Germanic *harkōną, from the root *hark- "to hear." Related to "hark."
