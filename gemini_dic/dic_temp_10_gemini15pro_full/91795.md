hyperfastidiously:/ˌhaɪpərˌfæˈstɪdiəsli/
| adv. |过度讲究地，过分挑剔地 | In an excessively fastidious manner; with extreme attention to detail and a tendency to be overly critical or demanding. | He hyperfastidiously arranged the flowers in the vase, ensuring each petal was perfectly positioned. 他过度讲究地把花插在花瓶里，确保每一片花瓣都摆放得完美无瑕。|
|  She hyperfastidiously cleaned her apartment, not a speck of dust could be found. 她过分挑剔地打扫她的公寓，一点灰尘都找不到。 | 近义词: meticulously, scrupulously, punctiliously | 反义词: carelessly, negligently, sloppily | 词性: 副词
