butterflylike:/ˈbʌtərˌflaɪˌlaɪk/| adj.|像蝴蝶的，蝶形的|Resembling or characteristic of a butterfly, especially in lightness, grace, or fragility.|Her dress, light and airy, moved in a butterflylike manner. 她的裙子轻盈飘逸，像蝴蝶一样舞动。|近义词：fluttery, delicate, gossamer|反义词:clumsy, heavy, awkward|词性:形容词

词源:
butterfly + like.  "Butterfly" comes from Old English "buttorfleoge," possibly because butterflies were thought to be attracted to butter.  "Like" comes from Old English "lic" and "gelic," meaning "similar" or "having the same form."
