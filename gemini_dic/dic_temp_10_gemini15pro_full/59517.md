easy-as-pie:/ˌiːzi əz ˈpaɪ/ | adj. | 非常容易，轻而易举 | Something that is easy as pie is very easy to do. |  Making a sandwich is easy as pie. 做三明治非常容易。 | Synonyms: simple, effortless, child's play | Antonyms: difficult, hard, challenging | adjective

easy-as-pie:/ˌiːzi əz ˈpaɪ/ | idiom | 非常容易，轻而易举 |  This idiom compares the ease of a task to the simplicity of eating a pie. | Passing the exam was easy as pie. 通过考试非常容易。| Synonyms: piece of cake, as easy as ABC | Antonyms: hard as nails, tough as leather | idiom
