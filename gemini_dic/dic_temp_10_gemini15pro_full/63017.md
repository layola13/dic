enquiring:/ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ/ /ɛnˈkwaɪrɪŋ/| adj.|好问的，探究的|Inclined to ask questions; inquisitive.  Showing or expressing curiosity.|She had an enquiring mind and was always eager to learn new things. 她有着一颗求知若渴的心，总是渴望学习新事物。|近义词：inquisitive, curious, probing|反义词：apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词

enquiring:/ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ/ /ɛnˈkwaɪrɪŋ/| n.|询问，打听|The act of asking for information.|Upon further enquiring, I discovered the train was delayed.  经过进一步打听，我发现火车晚点了。|近义词：inquiry, investigation, query|反义词：declaration, statement|词性:名词
