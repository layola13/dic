betaken:/biˈteɪkən/
verb
被带走；被迷住；被吸引
past participle of betake
例句：He was quite betaken by her beauty. 他完全被她的美貌迷住了。

例句：The castle was betaken by the enemy forces. 城堡被敌军占领了。

近义词：captivated, charmed, enchanted, entranced
反义词：repulsed, disgusted
词性：动词(past participle)
