hellespontine:/ˌhelɪˈspɒntɪn/ /ˌhelɪˈspɑːntaɪn/
adjective
达达尼尔海峡的；与达达尼尔海峡有关的
Relating to the Hellespont (now called the Dardanelles), a narrow strait separating Europe from Asia Minor.
The hellespontine currents are notoriously strong. 达达尼尔海峡的水流非常强劲。
Near synonyms: Dardanelles, Hellespont
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use geographical opposites like "Mediterranean" or "Aegean" depending on context)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hellespont + -ine.  Hellespont comes from Greek *Ἕλλης πόντος (Héllēs póntos) meaning "sea of Helle." Helle was a mythological figure who drowned in the strait.
