guideways:/ˈɡaɪdweɪz/
n.
导轨，导槽
Tracks or rails that guide the movement of a vehicle or other moving object.
The train runs on guideways. 火车在导轨上行驶。
The automated guided vehicle followed the guideways through the factory. 自动导引车沿着导轨穿过工厂。
