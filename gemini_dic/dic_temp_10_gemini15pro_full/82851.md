gull:/ɡʌl/| n.|海鸥；易受骗的人|A large white-and-grey seabird.|例句：The gulls wheeled overhead. 海鸥在头顶盘旋。|近义词：dupe, fool, sucker|反义词:skeptic|noun
gull:/ɡʌl/| vt.|欺骗；愚弄|To fool or trick someone.|例句：He was gulled into giving them his credit card details. 他被骗给了他们他的信用卡信息。|近义词：deceive, trick, hoodwink|反义词:disabuse|verb
