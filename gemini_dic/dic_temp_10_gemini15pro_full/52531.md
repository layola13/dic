difficulties: /ˈdɪfɪkəltiz/
n.
困难，困境
things that are hard to do or understand; problems
例句：
She overcame many difficulties to achieve her goals.
她克服了许多困难才实现了目标。

例句：
They are experiencing financial difficulties.
他们正在经历财务困难。

近义词：problems, challenges, obstacles, hardships, troubles
反义词：easiness, simplicity, advantages, benefits
词性：名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French difficulté (“difficulty”), from Latin difficultās (“difficulty, trouble”), from difficilis (“difficult”), from dis- (“not”) + facilis (“easy”).
