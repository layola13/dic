denutrition:/ˌdiːnjuːˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |营养不良|A lack of sufficient nutrients in the body, leading to various health problems. |Malnutrition is a serious problem, especially denutrition in young children. 营养不良是一个严重的问题，尤其是在幼儿中。| The doctor diagnosed him with denutrition due to his poor diet. 医生诊断他由于饮食不良而营养不良。| 近义词:malnutrition, undernutrition | 反义词: overnutrition, nourishment | 词性: noun

Etymology: From de- (prefix meaning "removal" or "lack of") + nutrition.
