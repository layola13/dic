circumventors:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈventərz/
noun
规避者；绕行者；设法回避者
Those who find ways to avoid rules or obstacles.
The tax circumventors found a loophole in the law.  那些逃税者在法律中找到了一个漏洞。
The circumventors of the blockade managed to get supplies through. 封锁线的规避者设法通过了补给。
synonyms: evaders, dodgers, avoiders
antonyms: conformists, followers, adherents
n.
