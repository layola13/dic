cam:/kæm/| n.|凸轮，摄像头|A rotating or sliding piece, or projection, in a mechanical linkage, designed to produce reciprocating or other variable motion. Also, short for camera. |The camshaft in the engine controls the opening and closing of the valves.|近义词：lobe, cog, webcam (for camera)|反义词:|noun
