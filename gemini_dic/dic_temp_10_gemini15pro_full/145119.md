overargued:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjud/
adjective
过度争论的
having been argued or debated excessively
The case was overargued, leading the jury to become confused and ultimately acquit the defendant.  这个案子被过度争论，导致陪审团感到困惑，最终宣判被告无罪。
The point about funding was overargued and ultimately became a distraction.  关于资金的观点被过度争论，最终成为了一个干扰。
synonyms: overdebated, overdiscussed, belabored
antonyms: underdiscussed, briefly argued
词性: adjective
