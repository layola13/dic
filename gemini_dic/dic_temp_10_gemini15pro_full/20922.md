bleariest:/ˈblɪəriɪst/| adj.|最模糊的；最朦胧的；最迷糊的|Most blurry, hazy, or dim; most unable to see clearly.|(Example 1) After working all night, I had the bleariest vision imaginable.  工作了一整夜后，我的视力模糊到了极点。(Example 2)  The bleariest, rain-streaked window made it hard to see the road ahead.  模糊不清、雨水淋漓的窗户使我们很难看清前面的路。|近义词：blurriest, haziest, dimmest, foggiest, most unclear|反义词：clearest, sharpest, most distinct|词性:形容词

词源:
From *blear* (verb) + *-y* (adjective suffix) + *-est* (superlative suffix). *Blear* comes from Middle English *bleren*, from Old English *blēarian* "to make dim," of uncertain origin.
