harmel:/ˈhɑːrməl/ | n. | 叙利亚芸香, 野芸香 | A plant (Peganum harmala) of the Mediterranean region and central Asia, the seeds of which are used as a spice and a source of dyes.  It is also known as Syrian rue or wild rue. | The harmel plant is known for its psychoactive properties. 叙利亚芸香以其精神活性而闻名。 | The seeds of the harmel plant are used to make a red dye. 叙利亚芸香的种子被用来制作红色染料。 | synonyms: Syrian rue, wild rue, African rue, esfand | antonyms: N/A | noun
