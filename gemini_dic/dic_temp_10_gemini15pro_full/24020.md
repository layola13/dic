breeching:/ˈbriːtʃɪŋ/ /ˈbritʃɪŋ/| n.|后膛；马裤；惩罚（尤指打屁股）；违反|The act of putting breeches on; the part of a harness that passes round the hinder part of a horse; the part of a cannon behind the vent.|The sailor was given six strokes of the breeching for disobedience.|近义词：punishment, castigation, spanking, trousers, pants|反义词:obedience, reward|词性:noun

breeching:/ˈbriːtʃɪŋ/ /ˈbritʃɪŋ/| v.|给…穿马裤；用后膛炮射击；惩罚（尤指打屁股）|To put breeches on; to punish, especially by spanking.|The captain breeched the young sailor for his insolence.|近义词：punish, castigate, spank, dress|反义词:reward, praise|词性:verb
