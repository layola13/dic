multi-ethnic:/ˌmʌltiˈɛθnɪk/
adjective
多种族的；多民族的
Consisting of or relating to people of many different ethnic groups.
The United States is a multi-ethnic society. 美国是一个多民族社会。
Canada has a vibrant multi-ethnic population. 加拿大拥有充满活力的多民族人口。
Synonyms: multicultural, cosmopolitan, diverse, pluralistic
Antonyms: homogeneous, uniform, monolithic
词性: 形容词
