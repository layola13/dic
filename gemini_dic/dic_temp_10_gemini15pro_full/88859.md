hoiden:/ˈhɔɪdn/| n.|野丫头，假小子|A boisterous, tomboyish girl; a romp.|例句：She was a real hoiden as a child, always climbing trees and playing with the boys. 她小时候是个十足的野丫头，总是爬树和男孩子们玩耍。
