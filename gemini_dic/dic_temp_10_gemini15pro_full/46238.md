cystotomy: /sɪˈstɒtəmi/ /sɪˈstɑːtəmi/
n.
膀胱切开术
Surgical incision into the urinary bladder.
A cystotomy was performed to remove the bladder stones.  为移除膀胱结石进行了膀胱切开术。
The surgeon performed a cystotomy to repair the bladder wall.  外科医生进行了膀胱切开术来修复膀胱壁。
近义词: vesicotomy
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology:
From  cysto-  +‎ -tomy.  cysto- comes from Greek kystis "bladder". -tomy comes from Greek -tomia  "cutting, incision".
