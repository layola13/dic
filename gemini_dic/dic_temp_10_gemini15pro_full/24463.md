britisher:/ˈbrɪtɪʃər/| n.|英国人|A person from Great Britain, often considered informal or even slightly derogatory in modern usage.  It can carry a connotation of outdated or stereotypical views of British people.|例句：The britisher ordered fish and chips.  那个英国人点了炸鱼薯条。

例句：Some older books still refer to people from the UK as britishers. 一些老书仍然把来自英国的人称为英国人。|近义词：Briton, British person|反义词:None (It's a nationality, not a characteristic).|词性:noun
