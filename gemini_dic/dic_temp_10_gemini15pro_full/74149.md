foundership:/ˈfaʊndərʃɪp/
n.|创始人身份；创办者地位|The state or quality of being a founder.  The role or position of a founder.|She took on the foundership of the company at a young age. 她年纪轻轻就担任了公司的创始人。|近义词：foundation, establishment, origination|反义词： dissolution, termination|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "founder" + "-ship"

"Founder" comes from the Old French "fondour" (founder, creator), which in turn comes from the Latin "fundāre" (to found, establish, lay the foundation of).

"-ship" is a suffix used to form abstract nouns denoting state, quality, or condition.
