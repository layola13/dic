nonprosperities:/ˌnɒnprɒˈspɛrɪtiz/ | n. | 不繁荣，不景气 |  Lack of prosperity; periods or situations characterized by a lack of success, growth, or economic well-being.  It refers to times of hardship, stagnation, or decline. | The war led to a long period of nonprosperities. 战争导致了长期的不景气。 |  The region experienced a series of nonprosperities after the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，该地区经历了一系列的不景气。 | Synonyms: adversity, hardship, misfortune, poverty, decline | Antonyms: prosperity, success, affluence, boom | noun
