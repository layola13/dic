acroter: /ˈækrəˌtɪər/
noun
acroterion (plural acroteria)
（建筑，尤指古典建筑）顶饰，acroterion（尤指山墙或屋檐角上的雕像或装饰）
An acroter is an architectural ornament placed on the apex or corner of a pediment or roof.  Acroteria are typically statues or decorative elements.
The temple's pediment was adorned with elaborate acroteria. 这座寺庙的山墙装饰着精致的顶饰。

The acroteria on the corners of the roof were damaged by the storm. 屋顶角落的顶饰被暴风雨损坏了。

近义词: ornament, decoration, finial
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 plain, undecorated)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acroterium*, from Ancient Greek *ἀκρωτήριον* (akrōtḗrion, “summit, extremity, pediment”), from *ἄκρον* (ákron, “tip, summit, extremity”) + *-τήριον* (-tḗrion, instrumental suffix).


USAGE->
The acroteria on the ancient Greek temple were remarkably well-preserved.  这座古希腊神庙上的顶饰保存得非常完好。

Acroteria were often used in classical architecture to add a sense of grandeur and elegance.  顶饰经常用于古典建筑中，以增添宏伟和优雅的感觉。
