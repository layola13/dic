half-made:/ˌhɑːfˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 半成品的，未完成的 |  Something that is half-made is only partly completed or constructed. | The dress was half-made, with pins still holding the seams together. 这件衣服只做了一半，接缝处还用别针固定着。| The builder left the house half-made when he ran out of money.  当建筑商的钱用完时，他把房子留下一半没有完工。 | Near synonyms: unfinished, incomplete, partly made | Near antonyms: finished, complete, whole | adjective

half-made:/ˌhɑːfˈmeɪd/ | n. | 半成品 |  A product that is only partially completed. | The factory was full of half-mades, waiting for the final stages of production. 工厂里堆满了半成品，等待着最后阶段的生产。|  They sold the half-mades at a discounted price. 他们以折扣价出售了这些半成品。| Near synonyms:  work in progress, unfinished product | Near antonyms: finished product, completed item | noun
