misapprehensiveness:/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhɛnsɪvnəs/
|n.|误解，误会的倾向|The misapprehensiveness of his remarks led to a heated argument. 他的言论的误导性导致了激烈的争论。|Etymology: From misapprehensive + -ness.  "mis-" (wrongly) + "apprehend" (to understand) + "-ness" (state of being).|Example: His misapprehensiveness about the contract terms caused significant delays. 他对合同条款的误解导致了严重的延误。|Synonyms: misunderstanding, misconception, misinterpretation|Antonyms: understanding, comprehension, clarity|Noun
