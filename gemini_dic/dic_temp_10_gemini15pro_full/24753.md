brookable:/ˈbrʊkəbl/
adjective
可容忍的，可忍受的
able to be tolerated or allowed; bearable.
His behavior was barely brookable. 他的行为几乎无法容忍。
近义词:tolerable, bearable, acceptable, endurable
反义词:intolerable, unbearable, unacceptable, unendurable
词性:adjective
词源:From brook (v.) + -able.  Brook (v.) comes from Old English brūcan "to use, enjoy, possess," from Proto-Germanic *brukanan, ultimately from PIE *bhrug- "to enjoy."
