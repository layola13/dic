gandhiesque:/ˌɡɑːnˈdiːɛsk/ | adj. | 甘地式的；具有甘地精神的；主张非暴力不合作的 | Characteristic of or resembling Mahatma Gandhi, especially in advocating nonviolent resistance. |  Her gandhiesque approach to conflict resolution proved effective. 她甘地式的解决冲突的方法证明是有效的。|  His simple lifestyle and commitment to peace were quite gandhiesque.  他简朴的生活方式和对和平的承诺非常具有甘地精神。| 近义词: nonviolent, pacifistic, peaceful | 反义词: violent, aggressive, militant | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  Derived from the name of Mahatma Gandhi + -esque (a suffix meaning "in the style of; resembling").
