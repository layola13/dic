arresters:/əˈrɛstərz/
|n.|避雷器；制动器；阻止器；逮捕者|A device used to protect electrical equipment from overvoltage, such as lightning strikes.  A device or mechanism used to stop or slow down motion.|
|例句：The arresters protected the power grid from the lightning storm. 避雷器保护电网免受雷暴的袭击。

|例句：The train's emergency arresters were deployed to prevent a collision. 火车的紧急制动器被启动以防止碰撞。|
|近义词：protector, suppressor, brake, stopper|
|反义词：accelerator|

词源: From the verb "arrest" (meaning to stop or check), which comes from Old French "arester" and ultimately from Latin "ad-" (to) + "restare" (to stay, remain).
