amphiuma:/æmˈfaɪ.uːmə/ | n. | 两栖鲵，鳗螈 | A type of aquatic salamander found in the southeastern United States, with an elongated body and tiny limbs.  |  Amphiumas are sometimes mistaken for eels. 两栖鲵有时被误认为是鳗鱼。|  Amphiuma tridactylum is the three-toed amphiuma. 三趾两栖鲵是三趾的两栖鲵。 |  synonym:  congo eel, ditch eel | antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | noun
