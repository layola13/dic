hour: /aʊər/ (US) /ˈaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|小时，时间，时刻|A period of time equal to 60 minutes; a particular time or point in time.|例句：The meeting lasted for two hours. 会议持续了两个小时。|例句：He arrived at the appointed hour. 他在约定的时间到达了。|近义词：time, moment, period|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "instant" for a very short time)
词性: 名词
