actioning:/ˈækʃənɪŋ/
verb
实施，执行，处理
The team is actioning the recommendations from the report.  团队正在执行报告中的建议。
We're actioning a new strategy to improve customer service. 我们正在实施一项新的战略来改进客户服务。
synonyms: implementing, executing, carrying out, performing, enacting
antonyms: delaying, postponing, deferring
词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From action + -ing.  来自 action + -ing.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company actioning the plan now. = 公司现在正在执行该计划。
He actioning the request immediately. = 他立即执行了请求。

一般过去时:
They actioned the decision last week. = 他们上周执行了这项决定。
We actioned the changes as soon as possible. = 我们尽快实施了这些改变。

现在完成时:
We have actioned all the critical tasks. = 我们已经完成了所有关键任务。
She has actioned the manager's instructions. = 她已经执行了经理的指示。

过去完成时:
They had actioned the plan before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已执行了该计划。
He had actioned the request before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，他已经执行了请求。

将来时:
We will actioning the new policy next month. = 我们下个月将实施新政策。
They will actioning the recommendations soon. = 他们很快就会执行这些建议。


被动语态:
The recommendations are being actioned. = 这些建议正在被执行。
The plan was actioned immediately. = 该计划立即被执行。


PHRASE->
actioning a plan = 执行计划
actioning a request = 执行请求
actioning a strategy = 实施战略
actioning a decision = 执行决定
actioning changes = 实施变更


注意事项:

"Actioning" is considered business jargon and may not be appropriate in all contexts.  "Actioning" 被认为是商业术语，可能并非在所有情况下都适用。  Using more standard verbs like "implementing" or "carrying out" is often preferred in formal writing.  在正式写作中，通常更倾向于使用更标准的动词，如 "implementing" 或 "carrying out"。
