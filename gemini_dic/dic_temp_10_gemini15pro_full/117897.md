meloplastic:/ˈmɛləˌplæstɪk/| adj.|旋律优美的，悦耳的|Relating to or having a pleasant melody; melodious.  Formed from *melo-*(Greek, meaning "song, melody") + *plastic* (Greek *plastikos*, meaning "moldable, fit for molding"). This implies a melody that is well-formed and pleasing to the ear.|The composer's meloplastic style captivated the audience.  作曲家优美的旋律风格吸引了观众。|Synonyms: melodious, tuneful, euphonious, harmonious|Antonyms: dissonant, cacophonous, discordant|adjective
