demeanor:/dɪˈmiːnər/| n. |举止，行为，风度|Outward behavior; the way a person behaves toward others.  It refers to one's conduct, bearing, and facial expression.|She maintained a calm demeanor throughout the crisis.  她在整个危机期间保持着镇定的举止。|Synonym: bearing, conduct, deportment, mien, manner |Antonym:  None (demeanor describes a specific outward behavior; a true antonym would require a specific opposite behavior which is impossible to define.)|noun
