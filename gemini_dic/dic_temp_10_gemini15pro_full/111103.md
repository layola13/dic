liter:/ˈliːtər/ (US), /ˈliːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|升；公升|A metric unit of capacity, formerly defined as the volume of one kilogram of pure water at 4°C and now equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters (about 1.75 pints).|例句：This bottle contains one liter of milk.  这瓶装有一升牛奶。

例句：I drank two liters of water today. 我今天喝了两升水。|近义词：litre (British spelling)|反义词: milliliter, cubic centimeter|词性:noun
