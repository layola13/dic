From Latin *frigorificus*, from *frigus* "cold" + *-ficus* "making, doing"|The chef stored the ingredients in the frigorifical chamber.  厨师将食材存放在冷藏室里。|The frigorifical equipment kept the vaccines at the correct temperature. 冷藏设备将疫苗保持在正确的温度。|近义词：refrigerating, cooling, chilling, freezing|反义词:heating, warming|词性:形容词
