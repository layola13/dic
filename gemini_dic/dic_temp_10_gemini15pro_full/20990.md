blenk:/blæŋk/ | adj. n. v. | 空白的，空虚的；空白表格；使…无效 |A blank space, page, or line has nothing written or printed on it.|  He handed me a blank sheet of paper. 他递给我一张白纸。 | 近义词:empty, vacant, void, bare | 反义词:full, complete, filled | 词性:形容词、名词、动词

词源: From Old French *blanc "white, pale; empty,"  from Frankish *blank, from Proto-Germanic *blanga- "shining, white." The semantic development from "white" to "empty" is likely due to the association of unwritten surfaces being white.
