inextinct:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. | 未灭绝的，现存的 | Not extinct; still in existence. |  The coelacanth, once thought extinct, is actually inextinct and living in deep ocean waters.  腔棘鱼，曾经被认为已经灭绝，实际上仍然存在，生活在深海中。 | Examples:
The Tasmanian devil, despite facing threats, is thankfully still inextinct.  尽管面临威胁，但值得庆幸的是，袋獾仍然没有灭绝。

Many species of plants and animals thought to be extinct are actually inextinct. 许多被认为已经灭绝的动植物实际上仍然存在。 | Synonyms: extant, surviving, living, existent | Antonyms: extinct, vanished, defunct, exterminated | word type: adjective
