HIPAA:/ˈhɪpə/ (US) /ˈhɪpɑː/ (UK)| n. |健康保险流通与责任法案 (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)|A US law designed to provide privacy standards to protect patients' medical records and other health information provided to health plans, doctors, hospitals and other health care providers.|例句：The hospital is HIPAA compliant. 这家医院符合HIPAA的规定。|例句：HIPAA regulations protect the privacy of patient health information. HIPAA法规保护患者健康信息的隐私。|近义词：patient confidentiality, medical privacy|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) public disclosure, data breach|词性:noun (缩写)
