frontierism:/ˌfrʌnˈtɪərɪzəm/
n.
开拓精神；边疆主义
The spirit, way of life, or values of the frontier.  A cultural movement promoting this spirit.
The westward expansion of the United States exemplified frontierism.  美国的西进完美地体现了开拓精神。
近义词: pioneering, expansionism
反义词: conservatism, stagnation
词性: noun


frontierism:/ˌfrʌnˈtɪərɪzəm/
n.
前沿性，尖端性
The quality of being at the forefront of a field or area of endeavor.  Pushing the boundaries of what is known or possible.
The frontierism of Silicon Valley drives technological innovation. 硅谷的前沿性推动了技术创新。
近义词: innovation, cutting-edge
反义词: traditionalism, obsolescence
词性: noun
