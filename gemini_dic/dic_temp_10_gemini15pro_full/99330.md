intercurrent:/ˌɪntərˈkʌrənt/| adj.|并发发生的，间发的，穿插的|Occurring between or interrupting the usual course of a disease, symptom, or the like.|例句：The patient's pneumonia was complicated by an intercurrent infection. 病人的肺炎因并发感染而变得复杂。|例句：Intercurrent illness delayed his recovery.  并发疾病延缓了他的康复。|近义词：concurrent, concomitant, incidental, intervening|反义词：primary, principal|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "intercurrēns", present participle of "intercurrere" (to run between), from "inter-" (between) + "currere" (to run).
