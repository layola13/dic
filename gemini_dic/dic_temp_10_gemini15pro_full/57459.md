draftback:/ˈdræftˌbæk/| n.|回流，返流；通风不良；退税，退款|A reverse flow of air, gases, or smoke, especially in a chimney or flue; a refund of duties or taxes on imported goods that are later exported.|The fireplace wasn't drawing properly, creating a draftback that filled the room with smoke. 壁炉的通风不好，产生的回流使房间里充满了烟。|近义词：backdraft, reflux, rebate, drawback|反义词:|noun
