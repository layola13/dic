crowd-pulling:/ˈkraʊdˌpʊlɪŋ/
| adj.|吸引人群的，叫座的|Attracting large numbers of people; popular.|例句：The circus is a crowd-pulling event. 马戏团是一个吸引人群的活动。

例句：The band's crowd-pulling performance sold out the stadium.  这支乐队吸引人的表演让体育场座无虚席。
|近义词：popular, attractive, successful, box-office, blockbuster|反义词：unpopular, unsuccessful, flop|词性:形容词
crowd:/kraʊd/ |n. 人群；群众；v. 挤满；聚集| A large number of people gathered together; to fill a space with a large number of people |词源: From Old English *crūd "crowd, throng," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse krūta "to crouch, stoop." The verb sense of "to press, cram" developed from the noun.|
