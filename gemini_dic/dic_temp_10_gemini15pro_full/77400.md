gauzes:/ˈɡɔːzɪz/
n.
纱布；薄纱；薄雾
Thin, transparent fabrics used for dressings or curtains.
The nurse carefully applied the gauzes to the wound.  护士小心翼翼地把纱布敷在伤口上。
The stage was draped in shimmering gauzes. 舞台上挂着闪闪发光的薄纱。
A thin gauze of mist hung over the valley. 山谷中笼罩着一层薄雾。

近义词:mesh, netting, bandage, scrim
反义词:solid

词性:名词(plural of gauze)

词源:From Old French gaze, from Gaza, a city in Palestine where this type of fabric was made.
