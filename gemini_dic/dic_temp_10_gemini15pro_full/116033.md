marsupium:/mɑːrˈsuːpiəm/ | n. | 育儿袋 | A pouch on the abdomen of female marsupials, such as kangaroos and opossums, in which the young are carried and suckled. |  The kangaroo joey peeked out of its mother's marsupium. 袋鼠宝宝从它妈妈的育儿袋里探出头来。| synonyms: pouch, pocket | antonyms: N/A | noun

marsupium:/mɑːrˈsuːpiəm/ | n. | (植物)囊状体 |  A pouch-like structure in certain plants or fungi. | The orchid's marsupium contains the pollen.  这株兰花的囊状体包含花粉。| synonyms: sac, pouch | antonyms: N/A | noun
