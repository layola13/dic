balance:/ˈbæləns/| n. v.|平衡，均衡；余额；结余|A state of equilibrium or equipoise; stability.|She lost her balance and fell. 她失去了平衡，摔倒了。|近义词：equilibrium, stability, symmetry|反义词：imbalance, instability, asymmetry|noun
balance:/ˈbæləns/| v.|使平衡；权衡；结算|To bring into or maintain in a state of equilibrium.|He balanced the book on his head. 他把书顶在头上保持平衡。|近义词：equalize, stabilize, counterbalance|反义词：destabilize, unbalance|verb
