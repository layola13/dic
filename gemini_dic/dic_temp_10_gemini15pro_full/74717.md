freemen:/ˈfriːmən/| n.|自由民；公民；享有特许权的人|A person who is not a slave or serf. A person who enjoys certain specified privileges or rights, especially that of citizenship in a borough or city.|The freemen of the city were granted special trading rights. 城市的自由民被授予特殊的贸易权。|synonyms: citizen, burgess, commoner|antonyms: slave, serf|noun
