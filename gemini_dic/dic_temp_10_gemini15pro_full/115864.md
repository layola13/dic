marl:/mɑːrl/ (US) /mɑːl/ (UK)| n. |泥灰岩；泥灰土；|A crumbly mixture of clay and calcium carbonate, formed from deposits at the bottom of lakes or seas. |The farmer enriched the soil with marl. 农民用泥灰土改良土壤。|The lake bed was composed primarily of marl. 湖床主要由泥灰岩构成。|近义词：clay, loam, silt|反义词:sand, gravel|词性:名词

marl:/mɑːrl/ (US) /mɑːl/ (UK)| vt. |用泥灰土施肥；|To fertilize or enrich (land) with marl. |He marled the fields to improve drainage. 他用泥灰土给田地施肥以改善排水。|They marled the vineyard every few years. 他们每隔几年就给葡萄园施肥。|近义词：fertilize, enrich|反义词: deplete|词性:动词
