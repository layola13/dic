optimising: /ˈɒptɪmaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzɪŋ/ (US)
verb
优化;最佳化
The process of making something as good as possible.
例句：They are optimising the website for mobile devices. 他们正在优化网站以适应移动设备。

例句：The company is optimising its production processes to increase efficiency.  公司正在优化其生产流程以提高效率。

近义词：improving, enhancing, perfecting, refining
反义词：worsening, degrading

词性：v.
词源：From optimize + -ing. Optimize comes from optimum (Latin optimus 'best') + -ize.
