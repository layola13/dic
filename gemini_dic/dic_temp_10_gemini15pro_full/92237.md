hypertrichosis:/ˌhaɪpərtraɪˈkoʊsɪs/ | n. | 多毛症；毛发过多 |  An abnormal amount of hair growth over the body.  | Example 1:  The patient presented with hypertrichosis, exhibiting excessive hair growth on her face and back. 病人表现出多毛症，面部和背部毛发生长过多。

Example 2:  Hypertrichosis can be a side effect of certain medications. 多毛症可能是某些药物的副作用。 | Synonym: hirsutism (especially in women), excessive hair growth | Antonym: hypotrichosis, alopecia | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hyper* (over, excessive) + *trikhos* (hair) + *-osis* (condition, abnormal state).
