bred:/brɛd/
adjective
培育的；饲养的；教养的
produced by breeding; raised or reared in a specified environment; brought up in a specified way
例句：These horses are bred for racing. 这些马是为赛马而培育的。
例句：He was born and bred in London. 他生于伦敦，长于伦敦。
近义词：raised, reared, cultivated, nurtured
反义词：wild, untamed
词性：adjective
