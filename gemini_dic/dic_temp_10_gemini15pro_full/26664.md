cacoxenite:/kəˈkɒksɪnaɪt/
n.
针铁矿，纤铁矿
A mineral, a hydrated iron phosphate.
Cacoxenite is a relatively rare phosphate mineral. 针铁矿是一种比较稀有的磷酸盐矿物。
Cacoxenite often occurs as radiating tufts of yellow to brownish needles. 针铁矿常以黄色至棕色的针状放射状簇状出现。
近义词:rockbridgeite, beraunite (These are related phosphate minerals)
反义词:None (There's no direct opposite for a specific mineral)
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "kakos" (bad) + "xenos" (guest, stranger), alluding to the difficulty early chemists had in analyzing it.
