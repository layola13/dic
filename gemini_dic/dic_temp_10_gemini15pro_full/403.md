abjectly:/ˈæbdʒɛktli/| adv.|卑贱地；可怜地；凄惨地|In the face of defeat, he surrendered abjectly.  面对失败，他卑躬屈膝地投降了。|例句：He apologized abjectly for his mistake. 他为自己的错误低声下气地道歉。

He abjectly begged for forgiveness. 他低声下气地乞求原谅。|近义词：miserably, wretchedly, basely, servilely|反义词：proudly, nobly, bravely|词性:副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *abjecte, from Latin abjectus (“cast down, downtrodden”), past participle of abicere (“to throw or cast away”), from ab- (“away from”) + jacere (“to throw”).
