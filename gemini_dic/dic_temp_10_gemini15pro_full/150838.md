paragnath:/ˈpærəɡnæθ/| n. |下颚附属物，侧颚|A small, hard, jaw-like appendage found in some invertebrates, particularly polychaete worms and mollusks, often used in feeding.|Polychaete worms use their paragnaths to grasp and manipulate food particles. 多毛类蠕虫用它们的侧颚来抓取和操纵食物颗粒。|Synonyms: mandible, jaw, appendage|Antonyms: N/A|noun
