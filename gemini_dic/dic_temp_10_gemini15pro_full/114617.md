maleficiate:/məˈlɛfɪʃɪeɪt/
verb
施加恶咒；使遭殃
To cast an evil spell upon; to bewitch; to injure by witchcraft or other supernatural means.
The witch maleficiated the prince, turning him into a frog.  女巫对王子施了恶咒，把他变成了青蛙。
Synonyms: curse, hex, jinx, bewitch
Antonyms: bless, protect
词性: 动词
