mullerian: /mʌˈlɪəriən/ | adj. | 米勒管的，缪勒氏管的 | Relating to the Müllerian ducts, embryonic structures that develop into the female reproductive tract in mammals. | The mullerian ducts differentiate into the fallopian tubes, uterus, and cervix.  米勒管分化成输卵管、子宫和子宫颈。|  Mullerian inhibiting substance prevents the development of the mullerian ducts in males.  米勒管抑制物质阻止雄性米勒管的发育。 | synonyms:  paramesonephric | antonyms: Wolffian | adjective
