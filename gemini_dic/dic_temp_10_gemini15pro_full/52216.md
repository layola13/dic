diathermanous:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmənəs/| adj.|透热的，允许热辐射透过的|Allowing radiant heat to pass through readily. |Rock salt is diathermanous to radiant heat. 岩盐能透过辐射热。|Synonyms:  transmissive, transparent (to heat), permeable (to heat)|Antonyms:  adiathermanous, opaque (to heat)|adjective

diathermancy:/ˌdaɪəˈθɜːrmənsi/| n.|透热性|The property of transmitting radiant heat.|The diathermancy of the material allowed the heat to pass through.  这种材料的透热性允许热量通过。|Synonyms:  transmissivity, permeability (to heat)|Antonyms:  adiathermancy, opacity (to heat)|noun
