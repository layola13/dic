drillstock: /ˈdrɪlstɒk/ (US) /ˈdrɪlstɒk/ (UK)| n. |钻台，钻架|A frame or stand for holding a drill, especially a brace and bit, while drilling. A drillstock can be handheld, clamped to a workbench, or floor-standing. |He secured the wood in the drillstock before beginning to bore the hole. 他把木头固定在钻台上，然后开始钻孔。| The carpenter used a drillstock to create a precise hole for the dowel. 木匠用钻架钻了一个精确的孔来插销子。 |Synonyms: drill stand, boring table| Antonyms: N/A| noun
