ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *graspen, *grapsen, from Old Norse *grapsa (“to grasp, grope”), from Proto-Germanic *grapōną (“to grab, grasp”). Cognate with Swedish grabba (“to grab”). Related also to grapple.
