afar-off:/əˈfɑːr ˈɒf/ /əˈfɑːr ˈɔːf/ | adj.|遥远的，远方的|situated at a great distance|例句：We saw the afar-off mountains shrouded in mist. 我们看到远处的群山笼罩在薄雾中。

例句：The afar-off sound of drums could be heard. 可以听到远处传来的鼓声。|近义词：distant, remote, faraway|反义词：near, nearby, close|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *feorr of, from feorr (far) + of (off).
