figurative:/ˈfɪɡjərətɪv/ /ˈfɪɡjʊrətɪv/| adj.|比喻的，象征的|Departing from literal use of words; metaphorical.  Representing by a figure or resemblance; typical.|例句：The expression "raining cats and dogs" is figurative. “倾盆大雨”这个表达是比喻性的。|近义词：metaphorical, symbolic, emblematic|反义词：literal, factual|词性:形容词

figurative:/ˈfɪɡjərətɪv/ /ˈfɪɡjʊrətɪv/| n.|象征|A figurative representation; an image, especially in sculpture or painting.|例句：The artist used figurative language to express his emotions. 艺术家用比喻的语言来表达他的情感。|近义词：symbol, emblem, image|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
