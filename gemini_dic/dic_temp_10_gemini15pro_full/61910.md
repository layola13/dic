empyreal: /ɛmˈpɪriəl/, /ɛmˈpaɪriəl/ | adj. | 天上的，天空的；崇高的，高尚的 | Of or relating to the heavens or the sky; sublime. |  The poet described the empyreal beauty of the sunset. 诗人描绘了日落时天空的壮丽美景。

The eagle soared into the empyreal heights.  鹰飞向了天空的高度。 | 近义词：celestial, heavenly, ethereal, sublime | 反义词：earthly, mundane, terrestrial | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle French *empyrée*, from Late Latin *empyreus*, from Ancient Greek *ἐμπύριος (empúrios)* (fiery, in fire), from *ἐν (en)* (in) + *πῦρ (pûr)* (fire).
