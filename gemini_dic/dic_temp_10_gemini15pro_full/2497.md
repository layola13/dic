adminicle: /ædˈmɪnɪkəl/
noun
辅助物，佐证，支持
something that provides help or support, especially as a secondary or accessory item.
The lawyer introduced several adminicles as evidence to support his client's alibi. 律师提出了一些辅助证据来支持其委托人的不在场证明。
The museum curator carefully catalogued the various adminicles associated with the ancient artifact.  博物馆馆长仔细地将与古代文物相关的各种辅助物品编入目录。

近义词: aid, support, accessory, adjunct
反义词: hindrance, obstacle, impediment
词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *adminiculum, from Latin adminiculari "to prop up, support," from ad- "to" + *miniculum "support," diminutive of *mina "prop, support."
