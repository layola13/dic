deprovincialize: /diːprəˈvɪnʃəlaɪz/
verb
使不狭隘；使开阔眼界；去除地方偏见
to free from provincial prejudices or manners; to broaden the outlook of
例句：
Travel can deprovincialize a person. 旅行可以使人开阔眼界。

The university aims to deprovincialize its students by exposing them to diverse cultures. 这所大学旨在通过让学生接触多元文化来开阔他们的眼界。

近义词：broaden, liberalize, enlighten, open up, cosmopolitanize
反义词：narrow, restrict, limit, confine, provincialize
词性：v.
