corrects:/kəˈrɛkts/ | v. |  纠正，改正；批改 | puts right; makes true; amends |  He corrects the students' papers every week.  他每周批改学生的作业。 | synonyms: amends, rectifies, remedies, revises | antonyms:  worsens, exacerbates,  misses | verb


corrects:/kəˈrɛkts/ | adj. | 正确的；恰当的 | right; true; accurate |  His answer was perfectly correct. 他的答案完全正确。| synonyms: accurate, right, proper, true | antonyms: incorrect, wrong, improper, false | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *correcten*, from Latin *correctus*, past participle of *corrigō* (“I make straight, put right, amend”), from *com-* (intensive prefix) + *regō* (“I rule, guide, keep straight”).
