feature:/ˈfiːtʃər/ (US), /ˈfiːtʃə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 以...为特色；特写；主演 |To give prominence to; to include as a special attraction.|例句：The film features a young actress.  这部电影由一位年轻女演员主演。|近义词：highlight, showcase, spotlight, star |反义词: exclude, omit |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *faiture "fashion, shape, form," from Latin factura "a making, a forming, a structure," from factus, past participle of facere "to make, to do."
