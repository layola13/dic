bookworm: /ˈbʊkwɜːrm/ (US) /ˈbʊkwɜːm/ (UK)| n. |书虫；酷爱读书的人|A person who spends a great deal of time reading.|例句：My daughter is a real bookworm; she always has her nose in a book. 我的女儿是个十足的书虫，她总是埋头读书。

例句：He was a shy bookworm growing up. 他从小就是一个害羞的书虫。

近义词：bibliophile, avid reader|反义词：illiterate, non-reader|词性：noun
