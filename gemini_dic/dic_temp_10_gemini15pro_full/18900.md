besmirch: /bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
verb
玷污，败坏（名声）
To damage the reputation of (someone or something) in the opinion of others by saying or suggesting that they are bad or dishonest.
His reputation was besmirched by the scandal. 他的名誉被丑闻玷污了。
tarnish, sully, stain, smear
clean, purify, honor, exalt
词性: 动词
