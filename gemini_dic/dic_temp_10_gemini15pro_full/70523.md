filial:/ˈfɪliəl/| adj.|子女的；孝顺的|Of or relating to a son or daughter.  Showing or due from a son or daughter; respectful.|例句：She has a strong filial duty towards her parents. 她对父母有很强的孝道。

例句：His filial piety was praised by everyone. 他的孝顺受到大家的称赞。|近义词：dutiful, respectful, obedient|反义词：disrespectful, disobedient|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *filial*, from Old French *filial*, from Latin *fīliālis* (“of or belonging to a son or daughter”), from *fīlius* (“son”) + *-ālis*.
