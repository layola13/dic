USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast?  你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you cooking? 你正在做什么菜？
What is he working on right now? 他现在正在做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了些什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon? 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has he been studying lately? 他最近一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I walked in? 我走进来的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been working on before the interruption?  被打扰之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? 他们明天会在会议上讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by the time we arrive? 等我们到达的时候他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she have done if she had known the truth? 如果她知道真相，她会怎么做？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you're saying. 她不明白你在说什么。
