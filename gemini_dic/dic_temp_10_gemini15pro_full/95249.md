incandescent:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/| adj.|白炽的；炽热的；容光焕发的|emitting light as a result of being heated.  Also, passionate or brilliant.|The incandescent bulb burned brightly. 白炽灯泡发出明亮的光。|近义词：glowing, luminous, radiant, brilliant|反义词：dull, dim, dark|词性:形容词
incandescence:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsns/| n.|白炽；炽热；光辉|the state of being incandescent; emission of light as a result of being heated.|The incandescence of the fire lit up the whole room. 火焰的白炽照亮了整个房间。|近义词：glow, radiance, luminosity|反义词：darkness, dimness|词性:名词
