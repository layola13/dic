aortitis:/eɪɔːrˈtaɪtɪs/| n. |主动脉炎|Inflammation of the aorta, the main artery of the body.|例句：Aortitis can cause chest pain and shortness of breath. 主动脉炎会导致胸痛和呼吸急促。

例句：Early diagnosis of aortitis is crucial for effective treatment.  早期诊断主动脉炎对于有效治疗至关重要。


|近义词：vasculitis (血管炎)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，健康状态可以视为相对反义)|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Modern Latin *aortitis, from aorta + -itis.  Aorta comes from Greek aortē, meaning "the great artery."  The suffix -itis denotes inflammation.
