gouache:/ɡuˈɑːʃ/ /ɡwɑːʃ/| n. |水粉画，水粉颜料|An opaque watercolor paint, similar to poster paint but finer.|例句：She prefers to paint with gouache because of its vibrant colors. 她喜欢用水粉画画，因为它颜色鲜艳。

例句：The artist used gouache to create a textured effect in the painting.  这位艺术家用水粉颜料在画中创作出一种纹理效果。|近义词：opaque watercolor, poster paint|反义词:watercolor|词性:noun
