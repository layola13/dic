cantoral:/kænˈtɔːrəl/
adjective
歌唱的；圣歌的；歌曲集的
Relating to singing or song; of or pertaining to a cantor or precentor.
The cantoral music filled the cathedral with a sense of awe.  教堂的圣歌音乐充满了一种敬畏感。
The choir performed a beautiful cantoral piece.  唱诗班演唱了一首优美的圣歌。
近义词:choral, musical
反义词:instrumental
