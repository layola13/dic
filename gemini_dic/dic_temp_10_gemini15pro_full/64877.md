esquire:/ɪˈskwaɪər/ /ˈɛskwaɪər/| n.|先生；律师|A title of courtesy, formerly given to men of higher social standing, especially in the US, placed after the surname. In the UK, it is used mainly for lawyers.|Example 1: John Smith, Esquire. 约翰·史密斯先生。

Example 2:  He addressed the letter to "David Jones, Esquire." 他在信上写了“大卫·琼斯先生”。|近义词：Mr., gentleman|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
