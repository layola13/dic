auletic:/ɔːˈlɛtɪk/
adjective
与笛子相关的，笛子吹奏的
Relating to or played on the aulos (an ancient Greek wind instrument, a double reed instrument like an oboe).
The auletic competition was a highlight of the ancient Greek games.  古希腊运动会的笛子比赛是一大亮点。
The museum displayed several auletic instruments from ancient Greece.  博物馆展出了几件来自古希腊的笛子乐器。
近义词:wind, pipe, reed
反义词:string, percussion
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *auletikos*, from *aulos* "reed pipe, flute."
