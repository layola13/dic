basilican:/bəˈsɪlɪkən/
adjective
巴西利卡式的；长方形的
Relating to or resembling a basilica (a large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, used in ancient Rome as a court of law or for public assemblies).
The church has a basilican plan. 这座教堂有巴西利卡式的平面图。
近义词:basilica-like, oblong
反义词:circular, round
词性:adjective

basilican:/bəˈsɪlɪkən/
noun
巴西利卡式教堂
A large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, especially one used as a Christian church.
St. Peter's Basilica is a famous example. 圣彼得大教堂就是一个著名的例子。
近义词:basilica, cathedral
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
