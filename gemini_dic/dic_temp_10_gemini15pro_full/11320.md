area codes:/ˈɛəriə koʊdz/ (US) /ˈeəriə kəʊdz/ (UK)| n. | 区号 | A three-digit number that precedes a seven-digit phone number, indicating a specific geographic area within a country or region. |  Example: The area code for Los Angeles is 213. 洛杉矶的区号是213。

Example:  I need to know the area code for London. 我需要知道伦敦的区号。| 近义词: dialing code, telephone code | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
