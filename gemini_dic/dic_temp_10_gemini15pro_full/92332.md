hypnotizes:/ˈhɪpnəˌtaɪzɪz/| v. |催眠，使着迷|to produce a state of hypnosis in (someone); to fascinate or enthrall|The magician hypnotizes his audience with his dazzling performance. 魔术师以他令人眼花缭乱的表演催眠了他的观众。

The therapist hypnotizes her patient to help them overcome their trauma.  治疗师催眠她的病人以帮助他们克服创伤。|Synonyms: mesmerizes, entrances, captivates, spellbinds|Antonyms: awakens, stimulates, enlivens|Verb


Etymology: From Late Latin *hypnotizāre, from Ancient Greek ὑπνόω (hupnóō, “I put to sleep”) + -ize.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek  ὕπνος (húpnos, “sleep”).
