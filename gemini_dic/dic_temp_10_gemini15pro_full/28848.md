carper:/ˈkɑːrpər/ | n. | 吹毛求疵的人；爱挑剔的人 | A person who finds fault, especially in a petty or unnecessary way. |  He's a real carper, always finding something to complain about. 他真是个爱挑剔的人，总是能找到抱怨的事情。 | Example: The carper complained about the slightest imperfections in the paint job.  这个吹毛求疵的人抱怨油漆工作中最细微的瑕疵。 | Synonym: critic, nitpicker, faultfinder | Antonym: admirer, praiser, flatterer | noun
