crinoid:/ˈkraɪnɔɪd/ | n. |海百合|A marine invertebrate animal of the class Crinoidea, typically having a cup-shaped body with five or more feathery arms. |  Crinoids are sometimes called sea lilies. 海百合有时被称为海百合。

Crinoids are ancient animals that have existed for millions of years. 海百合是已经存在了数百万年的古老动物。 | 近义词:sea lily | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crinoïde*, from Ancient Greek κρινοειδής (krinoeidḗs, “lily-like”), from κρίνον (krínon, “lily”) + -ειδής (-eidḗs, “-like”).
