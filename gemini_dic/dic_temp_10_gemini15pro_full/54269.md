disgregation:/dɪsˌɡrɛˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
分解，瓦解，崩溃
the process of breaking into many small parts or pieces; the condition of being broken into many small parts or pieces
The disgregation of the rock formation was caused by years of erosion.  岩石构造的瓦解是由多年的侵蚀造成的。
近义词：disintegration, breakdown, fragmentation, decomposition
反义词：integration, unification, consolidation
词性：名词

disgregation:/dɪsˌɡrɛˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
(群体、组织等的)分裂，离散
the separation of a group or organization into smaller units
The disgregation of the empire led to years of civil war.  帝国的分裂导致了多年的内战。
近义词：division, separation, dispersal, scattering
反义词：union, unity, cohesion
词性：名词
