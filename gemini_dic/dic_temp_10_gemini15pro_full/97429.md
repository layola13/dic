infuneral: /ɪnˈfjuːnərəl/
adjective
丧葬的，适合葬礼的
Relating to or suitable for a funeral; mournful.
Her infuneral expression betrayed her grief. 她悲伤的表情流露出了她的悲痛。
近义词: funereal, mournful, somber, gloomy
反义词: joyful, celebratory, festive
词性: adjective

词源:  "In-" (prefix meaning "in, into, on, or not") + "funeral" (from Latin "funus," meaning "funeral procession, burial").  The prefix "in-" in this case likely intensifies the meaning of "funeral" to suggest something deeply associated with or characteristic of a funeral.
