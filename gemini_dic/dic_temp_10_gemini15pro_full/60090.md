eeliest:/ˈiːliɪst/
adjective
最令人毛骨悚然的
most eerie; strangest
The eeliest thing was the silence. 最令人毛骨悚然的事情就是寂静。
近义词:creepiest, spookiest, weirdest
反义词:normal, comforting, reassuring
词性:形容词

词源:
From eerie + -est (superlative suffix).  Eerie itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Scots or northern English dialect words meaning "fearful" or "timid."
