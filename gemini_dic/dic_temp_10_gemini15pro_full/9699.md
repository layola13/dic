antitonic's:/ˌæntaɪˈtɒnɪks/ | n. | 解毒剂；中和物 |  A substance that neutralizes or counteracts the effects of a poison or toxin.  It refers specifically to something that opposes or reduces muscle tone.  The added 's indicates the possessive form, meaning "belonging to or associated with an antitonic."|  The antitonic's effect was immediate, relaxing the tense muscles. 解毒剂的作用立竿见影，放松了紧张的肌肉。 | synonyms: antidote, neutralizer, counteragent | antonyms: toxin, poison, stimulant | noun
