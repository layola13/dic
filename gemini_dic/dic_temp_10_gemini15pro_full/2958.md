adynamia: /ˌeɪdaɪˈneɪmiə/ /ˌædɪˈnaɪmiə/ | n. |无力，虚弱|A lack of strength or vigor; weakness.  The adynamia caused by her illness kept her bedridden. 她的病导致的无力让她卧床不起。|Synonyms: weakness, debility, asthenia, lassitude, lethargy|Antonyms: strength, vigor, energy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀδυναμία (adynamía, “powerlessness”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + δύναμις (dýnamis, “power, strength”).


USAGE->
The patient experienced prolonged adynamia after the surgery.
病人术后经历了长时间的无力。

Adynamia is a common symptom of various medical conditions.
无力是各种疾病的常见症状。


PHRASE->
muscle adynamia = 肌肉无力
generalized adynamia = 全身无力
postoperative adynamia = 术后无力
profound adynamia = 极度无力
adynamia and fatigue = 无力和疲劳
