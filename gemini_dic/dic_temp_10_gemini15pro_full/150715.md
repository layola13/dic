ETYMOLOGY->
From parabola + -oidal.  Parabola comes from Late Latin parabola, from Greek parabolē "application, comparison, parabola," literally "a throwing beside," from para- "beside" + ballein "to throw."  -oidal is a suffix meaning "resembling," from -oid + -al.
