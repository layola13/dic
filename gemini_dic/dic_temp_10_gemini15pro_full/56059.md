docibility:/ˌdoʊsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌdɒsɪˈbɪləti/| n. |可教性，温顺，驯良|The quality of being easily taught or trained; willingness to learn.|The dog's docility made him easy to train.  这只狗的温顺使它很容易被训练。|Synonyms:  teachability, malleability, compliance, obedience, tractability|Antonyms:  stubbornness, obstinacy, intractability, recalcitrance, unruliness|noun
