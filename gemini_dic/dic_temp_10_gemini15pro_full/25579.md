bumbles:/ˈbʌmbəlz/
verb (third person singular simple present bumbles, present participle bumbling, simple past and past participle bumbled)
笨手笨脚地走；踉跄；语无伦次地说；弄糟
move or speak in a clumsy, awkward way; mishandle something
The actor bumbled his lines.  这个演员把台词念得结结巴巴。
近义词:stumble, blunder, fumble
反义词:glide, sail, succeed
词性:verb

bumble:/ˈbʌmbəl/
noun
笨拙的行为；失误；低沉的嗡嗡声
a clumsy or awkward act or mistake; a low humming sound
He made a bumble of the presentation. 他把演讲搞砸了。
近义词:blunder, mistake, error
反义词:success, triumph
词性:noun
