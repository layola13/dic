laughingstock:/ˈlæfɪŋ stɒk/ (UK) /ˈlæfɪŋ stɑːk/ (US)| n. | 笑柄；嘲笑的对象 | someone or something that is considered a joke or a source of amusement by others | After his disastrous speech, he became the laughingstock of the whole company.  他那场糟糕的演讲之后，成了全公司的笑柄。| synonyms: butt of jokes, object of ridicule, mockery | antonyms: role model, paragon, exemplar | noun
