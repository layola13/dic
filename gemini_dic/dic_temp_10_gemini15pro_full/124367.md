motivational:/ˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 激励的，鼓舞人心的 | Providing motivation; intended to encourage or inspire. |  A motivational speaker can make a real difference in someone's life. 一位激励人心的演说家可以真正改变一个人的生活。

The coach gave a motivational speech to the team before the big game. 教练在大型比赛前给团队做了一次激励人心的演讲。| synonyms: inspiring, encouraging, stimulating, uplifting | antonyms: demotivating, discouraging, dispiriting | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From *motive* (n.) + *-ational* (suffix forming adjectives). *Motive* comes from Old French *motif*, ultimately from Latin *motus*, past participle of *movēre* "to move."
