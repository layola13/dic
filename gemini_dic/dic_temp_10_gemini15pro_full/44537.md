crossways:/ˈkrɒsweɪz/ (UK), /ˈkrɔːsweɪz/ (US)| n.|十字路口，交叉路|a place where two or more roads meet and cross each other (less common usage)|We met at the crossways. 我们在十字路口相遇。|近义词：crossroads, intersection, junction|反义词: - |词性:名词

From Middle English *crosswaies, equivalent to cross +‎ ways. Cognate with Scots crossways (“crosswise, across”).
