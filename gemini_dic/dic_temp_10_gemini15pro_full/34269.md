claque:/klæk/
noun
喝彩；捧场；雇佣的捧场者；拍手喝彩者
A group of people hired to applaud a performer or performance; an organized body of clappers.
The comedian's claque erupted in laughter after every joke, even the bad ones.  那个喜剧演员的捧场者在他讲完每个笑话后都爆发出笑声，即使是那些不好笑的笑话。
The opera singer had a claque to ensure a positive reception.  这位歌剧演唱家雇佣了一群捧场者以确保获得积极的反响。
synonyms:  fan club, cheering section,  applause, ovation
antonyms:  critics, detractors, opposition, booing
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "claque" (a slap, clap), from claquer (to clap, slap). Ultimately from imitative origin.
