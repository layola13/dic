nakedest:/ˈneɪkɪdɪst/
adjective
最赤裸的，最无遮蔽的
superlative adjective of naked: most naked
She felt nakedest standing before the large crowd. 她站在一大群人面前感到非常 exposed。
近义词: barest, most exposed, most uncovered, most undressed
反义词: most covered, most clothed, most dressed
词性: adjective

词源:
From Old English nacod.
