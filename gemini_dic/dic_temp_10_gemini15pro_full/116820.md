maxfps:/mæks ɛf piː ɛs/| n. |最大帧率|Maximum Frames Per Second, the highest number of frames that can be displayed per second.  It determines the smoothness of motion in videos or games.|The game's maxfps is capped at 60.  这款游戏的最大帧率限制在60。|近义词：frame rate limit, maximum frame rate|反义词:minfps (minimum frames per second)|abbreviation
