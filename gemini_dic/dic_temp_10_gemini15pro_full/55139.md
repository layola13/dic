disseat:/dɪsˈsiːt/
verb
使（某人）离座；免除（某人）的席位；剥夺（某人）的席位
to remove someone from a position of power or authority, especially in parliament
The scandal led to his being disseated from Parliament.  这起丑闻导致他被剥夺了议会席位。
近义词: oust, remove, unseat, depose
反义词: install, enthrone, appoint
词性: verb

disseat:/dɪsˈsiːt/
verb
使（主教）离职；撤销（主教）的职位
To deprive (a bishop) of office or position.
The bishop was disseated for heresy.  这位主教因异端邪说被撤职。
近义词: dismiss, remove, depose
反义词: appoint, install
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + seat.  来自dis-（表示否定或相反）+ seat（座位）。
