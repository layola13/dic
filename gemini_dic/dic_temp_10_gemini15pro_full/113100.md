luncheons:/ˈlʌn(t)ʃənz/| n.|午餐，午宴 (复数)|A formal lunch, often for a group of people, especially for a business or social purpose.  It is the plural form of "luncheon".|We attended several business luncheons last week. 我们上周参加了几场商务午餐会。|The hotel caters for wedding receptions and other special luncheons. 这家酒店承办婚礼招待会和其他特别的午餐会。|Synonyms: lunches, midday meals|Antonyms: dinners, suppers|noun
