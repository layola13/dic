nonimporting:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɔːtɪŋ/
adjective
非进口的
Not importing or related to importing; referring to a country, economy, or entity that does not engage in importing goods or services from other countries.
The government is encouraging nonimporting industries to develop domestic markets. 政府正在鼓励非进口产业发展国内市场。
Countries with strong nonimporting sectors are less vulnerable to global trade fluctuations. 拥有强大的非进口产业的国家不易受到全球贸易波动的影响。

近义词:domestic, local
反义词:importing, foreign

词性
adjective
