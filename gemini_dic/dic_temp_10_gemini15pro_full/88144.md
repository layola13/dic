hilbert:/ˈhɪlbərt/| n. |希尔伯特; 希尔伯特空间|A German mathematician who reduced geometry to a series of axioms and contributed substantially to the foundations of mathematics.  一位德国数学家，他将几何简化为一系列公理，并对数学基础做出了重大贡献。|David Hilbert was a very influential mathematician. 大卫·希尔伯特是一位非常有影响力的数学家。|The Hilbert space is an important concept in quantum mechanics. 希尔伯特空间是量子力学中的一个重要概念。|synonyms: mathematician, scholar|antonyms: N/A|noun
