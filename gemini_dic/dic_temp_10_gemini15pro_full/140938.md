one-liner:/ˈwʌnˌlaɪnər/ | n. |  单行笑话; 简洁而妙趣横生的语句 | A short joke or witty remark, typically only one sentence long. | He's known for his clever one-liners. 他以妙语连珠而闻名。| synonym:quip, joke, gag | antonym:speech, essay, monologue | noun

one-liner:/ˈwʌnˌlaɪnər/ | adj. | 单行的; 一句的 | Consisting of a single line or sentence. |  He gave a one-liner answer to the complex question. 他用一句话回答了这个复杂的问题。 | synonym:concise, brief, short | antonym:lengthy, verbose, prolix | adjective
