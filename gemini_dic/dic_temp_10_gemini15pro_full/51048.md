desolateness:/ˈdɛsəlɪtnəs/| n.|荒凉，凄凉，孤寂|A state of being empty, lonely, and without hope or comfort. |The desolateness of the abandoned town was chilling.  被遗弃的城镇的荒凉令人不寒而栗。|近义词：barrenness, bleakness, desolation, loneliness, emptiness|反义词: cheerfulness, liveliness, comfort, hope|词性:noun

desolate:/ˈdɛsəlɪt/ | adj. |荒凉的，凄凉的，孤寂的|Empty and without people, making you feel sad or frightened. |The landscape was desolate and windswept. 这片风景荒凉而多风。|近义词：barren, bleak, deserted, lonely, empty|反义词: cheerful, lively, populated, comforting|词性:adjective
