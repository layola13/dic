overargumentative: /ˌoʊvərˌɑːrɡjʊˈmentətɪv/
adjective
好争辩的，过于喜欢争论的
Given to excessive arguing; disputatious.  Characterized by an excessive tendency to argue or dispute.
He became overargumentative after a few drinks. 他几杯酒下肚后就变得爱争论了。
She's known to be overargumentative in meetings.  大家都知道她在会议上好争辩。
近义词: disputatious, contentious, quarrelsome, argumentative
反义词: agreeable, amenable, compliant, conciliatory
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "argumentative" (from "argument" + "-ative")  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or too much of the quality described by the base word "argumentative."  "Argument" comes from the Latin "argumentum," meaning "proof, evidence, reason."
