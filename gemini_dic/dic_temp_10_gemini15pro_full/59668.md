echinoderms:/ɪˈkaɪnəˌdɜːrmz/ | n. | 棘皮动物 |Marine animals distinguished by radial symmetry, typically having a five-point body plan.  They include starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers. | Starfish and sea urchins are common examples of echinoderms.  海星和海胆是棘皮动物的常见例子。| synonyms: none (it's a specific biological classification) | antonyms: none | noun
