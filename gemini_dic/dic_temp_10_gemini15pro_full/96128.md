indemnifier:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪər/| n. |赔偿者，保障者|A person or entity who promises to protect another from financial loss or damage.|The contract named the company as the indemnifier in case of any accidents.|Synonyms: guarantor, surety, sponsor|Antonyms: claimant, plaintiff|noun
