god-man:/ˈɡɒdˌmæn/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdˌmæn/ (US)| n. |神人; 人神; 神化的人|A being believed to be both divine and human, or a human with divine qualities.  Incarnations of gods are sometimes referred to as god-men.| Jesus Christ is considered by Christians to be a god-man. 基督徒认为耶稣基督是神人。|

The pharaohs of ancient Egypt were seen as god-men. 古埃及的法老被视为神人。| synonyms:  deity, divine being, incarnation, avatar | antonyms: mortal, human | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *godmann (unattested), from god "god" + mann "man".  Cognate with German Gottmensch.  The concept of a god-man appears in various mythologies and religions.
