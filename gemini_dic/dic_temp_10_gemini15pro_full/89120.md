home-and-dry:/ˌhoʊm ən ˈdraɪ/| adj.|安全无虞的，成功的，大功告成的|Safe and successful; having completed something successfully.|例句：After months of hard work, we were finally home and dry on the project.  几个月辛苦工作后，我们终于完成了这个项目。|例句：With the last question answered correctly, she was home and dry in the quiz.  最后一个问题答对了，她在测验中稳操胜券。|近义词：safe, secure, successful, out of the woods|反义词:in trouble, at risk, in danger|词性:形容词

词源:
This idiom originates from sailing terminology. A ship is "home" when it reaches its port and "dry" when it is safely on land, away from the dangers of the sea.
