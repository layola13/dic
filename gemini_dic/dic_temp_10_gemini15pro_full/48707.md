defibrination:/diːˌfaɪbrɪˈneɪʃən/
noun
去纤维蛋白作用
The removal of fibrin from the blood.
Disseminated intravascular coagulation (DIC) can lead to defibrination.  弥散性血管内凝血 (DIC) 可导致去纤维蛋白作用。
The patient underwent defibrination therapy.  该病人接受了去纤维蛋白治疗。
近义词: fibrinolysis
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider  coagulation or clotting)
