dasty:/ˈdeɪsti/ | adj. | 过时的，陈腐的；污秽的，肮脏的 | Old-fashioned, out of date; dirty, unclean. | The dasty old furniture was covered in dust and cobwebs.  过时的旧家具上满是灰尘和蜘蛛网。| 近义词: musty, fusty, stale, dingy, grimy | 反义词: modern, contemporary, clean, pristine | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  Likely a variant of "dust" + "-y" suffix indicating a quality.  The association with dust and dirt likely led to the meaning of "unclean."  The connection to "old-fashioned" may stem from the idea of things becoming dusty and neglected over time.
