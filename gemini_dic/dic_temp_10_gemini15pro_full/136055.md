nonrecalcitrant:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˈkælsɪtrənt/
adjective
不倔强的，不反抗的，顺从的
Not recalcitrant; yielding readily to authority or guidance.
The nonrecalcitrant students followed the teacher's instructions. 顺从的学生们听从了老师的指示。
The usually nonrecalcitrant dog suddenly refused to go for a walk. 通常很温顺的狗突然拒绝去散步。
近义词: compliant, amenable, docile, obedient, submissive, tractable
反义词: recalcitrant, rebellious, defiant, resistant, obstinate, uncooperative
词性: adjective


词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "recalcitrant" (stubbornly resistant to authority or guidance).  "Recalcitrant" comes from the Latin "recalcitrare," meaning "to kick back," from "re-" (back) + "calcitrare" (to kick), from "calx" (heel).
