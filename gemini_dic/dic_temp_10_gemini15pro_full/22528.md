boondoggle:/ˈbuːndɑːɡəl/| n. v. | 虚度光阴，浪费时间（金钱）的活动；粗制滥造的工艺品 | A project or activity that is wasteful or pointless; a piece of shoddy or useless work. |  例句：The new airport terminal was a complete boondoggle, costing millions and barely functioning.  新机场航站楼完全是浪费金钱，耗资数百万美元却几乎无法运作。

例句：They boondoggled away the entire afternoon playing video games. 他们整个下午都在玩电子游戏，虚度光阴。 | 近义词：waste, dawdle, trifle,  frivolity | 反义词：work, labor, toil, productivity | 词性：名词、动词

boondoggle:/ˈbuːndɑːɡəl/| n. v. | 粗制滥造的工艺品 | A piece of shoddy or useless handicraft. |  例句：He made a boondoggle out of some old rope and beads. 他用一些旧绳子和珠子做了一个粗制滥造的小玩意儿。

例句：She tried to sell her boondoggles at the craft fair, but no one was interested. 她试图在工艺品展览会上出售她的粗制滥造的工艺品，但没有人感兴趣。 | 近义词： trinket, knickknack (sometimes) | 反义词：masterpiece, work of art | 词性：名词
