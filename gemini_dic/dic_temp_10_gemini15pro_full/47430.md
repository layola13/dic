deadpan:/ˈdɛdˌpæn/| adj.  n.  v. |面无表情的，不动声色的；无表情的脸；面无表情地说|delivering something in a deadpan way:  in a way that shows no emotion, especially when this is surprising or funny|He deadpanned, "I thought you'd never ask." 他面无表情地说：“我以为你永远不会问。”|近义词：expressionless, impassive, blank|反义词：expressive, animated|词性:形容词, 名词, 动词
deadpans:/ˈdɛdˌpænz/| v. |面无表情地说|Third-person singular simple present tense form of deadpan.|He deadpans his jokes, which makes them even funnier. 他面无表情地讲笑话，这使得它们更加有趣。|近义词：says expressionlessly|反义词：expresses emotionally|词性:动词
