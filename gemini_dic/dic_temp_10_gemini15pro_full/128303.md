neuroskeleton: /ˈnjʊərəʊˈskɛlɪtən/ | n. | 神经骨架 | A network of microtubules, neurofilaments, and microfilaments that provides structural support for neurons and helps to maintain their shape.  It also plays a role in axonal transport. | The neuroskeleton is essential for neuronal function. 神经骨架对神经元的功能至关重要。|  The integrity of the neuroskeleton is crucial for axonal transport. 神经骨架的完整性对于轴突运输至关重要。 | cytoskeleton, neuronal scaffold |  N/A | noun
