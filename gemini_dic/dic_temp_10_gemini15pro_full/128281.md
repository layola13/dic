neuropore:/ˈnjʊərəˌpɔːr/ (US), /ˈnjʊərəˌpɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |神经孔|An opening in the neural tube during embryonic development.  It closes as the neural tube develops into the brain and spinal cord.|The anterior neuropore closes around day 25 of gestation. 前神经孔在妊娠第25天左右闭合。|The failure of the posterior neuropore to close can result in spina bifida. 后神经孔闭合失败会导致脊柱裂。|Synonym: neural opening|Antonym: (No direct antonym, but closure of the neuropore is the opposite process)|Noun
