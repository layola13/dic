dodecagynia:/ˌdoʊdekəˈɡɪniə/
noun
十二雌蕊，十二枚雌蕊
Having twelve pistils (the female reproductive organs of a flower).
The botanist identified a rare dodecagynia variant of the flower.  植物学家发现了一种罕见的十二雌蕊变异花。
Near Synonyms:  polygynia (many pistils)
Antonyms: monogynia (one pistil)
词性: noun
