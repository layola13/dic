flatulence: /ˈflætʃʊləns/ (US) /ˈflætjʊləns/ (UK)| n.|胀气；肠胃气胀|The act of expelling intestinal gas from the anus.  It is a natural bodily function, but excessive flatulence can be embarrassing or a sign of a digestive issue.|Example 1:  The beans gave him terrible flatulence. 豆子让他胀气得厉害。

Example 2:  She excused herself discreetly because of a sudden bout of flatulence. 她因为突然胀气而谨慎地离开了。|Synonym: gas, wind, bloating|Antonym:  None (there isn't a direct opposite for a bodily function)|Noun
