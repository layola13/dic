ninefold:/ˈnaɪnfoʊld/| adj. adv.|九倍的；九重的|Multiplied by nine; having nine parts. Also used as an adverb, meaning "by nine times".|例句：The company's profits increased ninefold this year.  公司今年的利润增长了九倍。

例句：The recipe calls for a ninefold increase in the amount of flour.  这个食谱要求面粉的量增加九倍。|近义词：nonuple|反义词:|词性:adjective, adverb
