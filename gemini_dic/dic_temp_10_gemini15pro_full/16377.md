barbette: /bɑːrˈbɛt/ | n. |炮座，露天炮台|A platform or mound, typically circular, on which a heavy gun is mounted, especially on a warship, allowing it to rotate and fire over a parapet. | The battleship's main guns were mounted on barbettes, offering a wide firing arc.| Synonyms: gun platform, emplacement | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "gun port" which is a covered opening) | Noun

barbette: /bɑːrˈbɛt/ | n. | (in fortifications) a mound of earth behind a parapet, on which guns were mounted |  The fort's barbettes offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside. | Synonyms: rampart, earthwork | Antonyms: ditch, moat | Noun
