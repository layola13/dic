doves:/dʌvz/| n.|鸽子（复数）|The plural form of "dove," referring to multiple birds of this species.  It symbolizes peace, love, or innocence.|例句：The doves cooed softly in the trees. 鸽子在树上轻轻地咕咕叫。

例句：White doves were released at the wedding ceremony. 婚礼上放飞了白鸽。|近义词：pigeons (more general), peace symbols|反义词:hawks (symbolically)|词性:noun
