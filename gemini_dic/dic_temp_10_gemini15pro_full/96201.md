indevotion: /ˌɪndɪˈvoʊʃən/
n.
lack of devotion; neglect
缺乏奉献精神；疏忽；不虔诚

Indevotion to one's work can lead to failure.
对工作缺乏奉献精神会导致失败。

His indevotion to his family caused them much pain.
他对家庭缺乏奉献精神，给他们带来了很多痛苦。

近义词: neglect, negligence, disregard, apathy, indifference
反义词: devotion, dedication, commitment, piety

词性: 名词
