mayday:/ˈmeɪdeɪ/| n. int.|求救信号；五朔节|A distress signal used internationally, especially by ships and aircraft. Also refers to May 1st, a traditional spring festival. |例句：The pilot issued a mayday call before the plane crashed. 飞行员在飞机坠毁前发出了求救信号。

例句：May Day is celebrated as a public holiday in many countries. 五朔节在许多国家被作为公共假日庆祝。|近义词：SOS, distress call, help|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "all clear") |词性: noun, interjection
