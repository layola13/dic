barghest:/ˈbɑːrɡɛst/ | n. | 巴吉斯特，一种传说中的鬼怪 | A barghest is a mythical monstrous black dog, found primarily in the folklore of northern England, especially Yorkshire. It is said to be a death omen, appearing as a large, black dog with large paws and fiery eyes. |  The villagers whispered tales of the barghest haunting the moors. 村民们低声讲述着巴吉斯特鬼怪在荒野出没的故事。 The appearance of the barghest was taken as an omen of impending doom. 巴吉斯特的出现被认为是即将到来的厄运的预兆。 | synonym: black dog, hellhound, phantom | antonym: N/A (mythical creatures don't typically have antonyms) | noun
