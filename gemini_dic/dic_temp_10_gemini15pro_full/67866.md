fabling:/ˈfeɪblɪŋ/
n.|虚构; 创作寓言|The act of creating or telling fables.  The process of inventing stories, often with a moral or didactic purpose.|He spent his evenings fabling, creating intricate tales of mythical creatures.|近义词：mythmaking, storytelling, inventing|反义词: truth-telling, reporting|词性:名词 (derived from the verb "to fable")

fabling:/ˈfeɪblɪŋ/
adj.|寓言般的; 虚构的|Resembling or characteristic of a fable; fictitious.|Her fabling narratives captivated the children.|近义词：mythical, legendary, fictional|反义词: factual, real, true|词性:形容词 (derived from the verb "to fable")
