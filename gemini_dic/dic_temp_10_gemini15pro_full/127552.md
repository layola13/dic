necropolitan:/ˌnɛkrəˈpɒlɪtən/| adj. n.|与大城市相关的，都市人的；（尤指）与太平间、殡仪馆相关的；大都市居民|Relating to a large city, its inhabitants, or (especially) its cemeteries and mortuaries; an inhabitant of a large metropolis|例句：The necropolitan skyline was dominated by skyscrapers.  都市的天际线被摩天大楼占据。

例句：He worked in the necropolitan funeral home. 他在都市的殡仪馆工作。|近义词：urban, metropolitan, cosmopolitan|反义词：rural, rustic|词性：形容词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek νεκρός (nekrós, “dead body”) + πόλις (pólis, “city”).  来自古希腊语 νεκρός (nekrós, “尸体”) + πόλις (pólis, “城市”)。
