discarders:/dɪˈskɑːrdərz/
n.
抛弃者，丢弃者
People who discard something. Those who throw something away or reject it.
The city council criticized the discarders of litter in public parks. 市议会批评了在公园里乱扔垃圾的人。
discarders of old traditions  抛弃旧传统的人
近义词:rejectors, abandoners, dumpers
反义词:keepers, savers, hoarders
词性:名词
