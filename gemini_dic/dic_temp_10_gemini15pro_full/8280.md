anti-knock:/ˌæntiˈnɒk/ (UK), /ˌæntiˈnɑːk/ (US)| adj. |抗爆的；防震的|Resisting or preventing knocking in an internal combustion engine.|High-octane gasoline has better anti-knock properties. 高辛烷值汽油具有更好的抗爆性能。|Synonyms: knock-resistant, detonation-resistant|Antonyms: knock-prone, detonation-prone|adjective

anti-knock:/ˌæntiˈnɒk/ (UK), /ˌæntiˈnɑːk/ (US)| n. |抗爆剂|A substance added to fuel to prevent knocking in an internal combustion engine.|Tetraethyl lead was a common anti-knock additive. 四乙基铅是一种常见的抗爆添加剂。|Synonyms: knock inhibitor, octane booster|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the noun form)|noun
