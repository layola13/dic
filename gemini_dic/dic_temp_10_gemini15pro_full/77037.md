garlanding:/ˈɡɑːrləndɪŋ/
n.
花环装饰，戴花环
The act of decorating with garlands or the garlands themselves.
The bride and groom exchanged garlands during the ceremony.  新娘和新郎在仪式上交换了花环。
The garlanding of the war heroes was a moving sight. 为战争英雄献上花环是一个感人的景象。
Synonyms: festooning, adorning, decorating
Antonyms: stripping, denuding
noun


garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/
n. vt.
花环；花冠；荣誉；奖赏；用花环装饰；给…戴花环
A wreath of flowers and leaves, worn on the head or hung as a decoration.  Also, can be used as a verb meaning to decorate with garlands.
The queen wore a garland of white roses. 女王戴着白色的玫瑰花环。
They garlanded the winning horse with flowers. 他们用鲜花给获胜的马匹戴上花环。
Synonyms: wreath, chaplet, crown, festoon, adorn
Antonyms: strip, denude
noun, verb
