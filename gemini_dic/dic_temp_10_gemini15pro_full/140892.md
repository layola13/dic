on-your-uppers:/ˌɒn jɔːr ˈʌpəz/
adjective
穷困潦倒的，落魄的
being very poor; having no money or very little money
Example:  They were on their uppers after both losing their jobs.  他们俩都失业后，穷困潦倒。

Example:  With the factory closing down, many families are on their uppers. 工厂倒闭后，许多家庭都陷入了困境。

Synonym:destitute, impoverished, penniless, broke
Antonym: wealthy, affluent, prosperous
词性:adjective
