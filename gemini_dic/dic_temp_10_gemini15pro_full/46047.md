cyclically: /ˈsaɪklɪkli/ | adv. | 循环地，周期性地 | In a cyclical manner; recurring in cycles. | The seasons change cyclically. 季节循环变化。| The economy cyclically goes through periods of growth and recession. 经济周期性地经历增长和衰退时期。 |近义词: periodically, regularly, recurrently | 反义词: erratically, irregularly, randomly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From cycle + -ical + -ly. "Cycle" comes from the Latin "cyclus" and Greek "kyklos" meaning "circle" or "wheel."
