alienable:/ˈeɪliənəbl/ |adj.|可让渡的，可转让的|Capable of being transferred to another.|Mineral rights are often alienable separate from surface rights. 矿产权通常可以与地表权分开转让。|transferable, assignable|inalienable|adjective
