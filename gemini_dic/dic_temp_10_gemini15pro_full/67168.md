expostulate:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪt/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəˌleɪt/
verb
抗议；劝诫；告诫；责备
express strong disapproval or disagreement.
例句：
She expostulated with him about his rudeness. 她责备他的粗鲁无礼。
He expostulated with the referee about the decision. 他向裁判抗议这个判决。

近义词：remonstrate, protest, object, argue, complain
反义词：agree, accept, condone
词性：v.
