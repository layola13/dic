feather:/ˈfeðər/ (US), /ˈfeðə(r)/ (UK)| n. |羽毛|A covering of the skin of birds, consisting of a central shaft with numerous slender barbs on each side.|The bird preened its feathers.  这只鸟梳理它的羽毛。|Synonyms: plume, plumage|Antonyms: scale, fur|noun
