miswrites:/ˌmɪsˈraɪts/| v.|写错，误写|He often miswrites common words due to his dyslexia. 他因为阅读障碍经常拼错常见的单词。|例句：She miswrites her name sometimes when she's tired. 她累的时候有时会写错自己的名字。

例句：He miswrites the address on the envelope, causing the letter to be returned. 他把信封上的地址写错了，导致信件被退回。|近义词：misspells, misspells, scribbles, scrawls|反义词:writes correctly, spells correctly|词性:动词
