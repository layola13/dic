hypermarket:/ˈhaɪpəmɑːrkɪt/ (US), /ˈhaɪpəmɑːkɪt/ (UK)| n. |大型超级市场|A very large self-service store selling a wide range of food and household goods.|Example: We bought all our groceries at the hypermarket. 我们在大卖场买了所有的食品杂货。|Example: The hypermarket offers a wide variety of products at competitive prices. 这家大型超市以具有竞争力的价格提供各种各样的产品。|Synonyms: superstore, megastore|Antonyms: convenience store, corner shop|noun
