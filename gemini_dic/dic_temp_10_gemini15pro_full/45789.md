例句：My grandmother is a terrible cusser when she's playing cards.  我祖母打牌的时候脏话连篇。|近义词：swearer, curser|反义词: polite person, respectful person|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "curse" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Curse" comes from Old English "cursian," meaning to utter imprecations or pronounce evil upon. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*karzjan," related to "care" in the sense of anxiety or sorrow.
