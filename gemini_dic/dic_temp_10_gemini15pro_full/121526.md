mismake:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪk/
verb
做错；弄错；错误地制造
to make incorrectly or badly
The carpenter had to remake the chair because he had mismade it the first time. 木匠不得不重做椅子，因为他第一次做错了。
近义词: bungle, botch, spoil, ruin, mar
反义词: perfect, create, construct
词性: verb

mismake:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪk/
noun
错误之作；失败之作
something that is made incorrectly or badly
The mismake was evident in the uneven legs of the table. 桌子腿的不平整显露出制作上的错误。
近义词: blunder, error, mistake, fault
反义词: masterpiece, success
词性: noun
