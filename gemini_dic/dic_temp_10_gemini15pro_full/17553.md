ETYMOLOGY->
The band name "Beatles" is a pun on the word "beetles," referring to insects, combined with "beat" for the musical beat.  It was originally spelled "Beetles" before being changed to "Beatles."  The name was chosen to reflect the band's admiration for Buddy Holly and The Crickets.  The "beat" element also alluded to the beatnik movement of the era.
