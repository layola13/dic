fruitless:/ˈfruːtləs/| adj.|无结果的，徒劳的|Producing no valuable or desired results; unproductive.|例句：After many fruitless attempts, he finally gave up. 经过多次徒劳的尝试，他最终放弃了。|例句：The meeting proved fruitless, as no agreement was reached.  会议结果毫无成果，因为没有达成任何协议。|近义词：unsuccessful, unproductive, futile, vain, ineffective|反义词：fruitful, productive, successful, effective|词性:形容词

fruitless:/ˈfruːtləs/| adj.|无益的，无效的|Having no positive effect or no success in achieving something.|例句：Their efforts to save the company were fruitless. 他们拯救公司的努力是徒劳的。|例句：It's fruitless arguing with him; he won't change his mind.  和他争论是没有用的；他不会改变主意。|近义词：useless, pointless, futile|反义词：useful, effective|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fruit + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking."  So, "fruitless" literally means "without fruit." The original meaning referred to literal fruit, but it evolved to encompass a broader meaning of lacking any desired outcome.
