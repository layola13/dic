electro-acupuncture:/ɪˌlektroʊˈækjʊpʌŋktʃər/ | n. |电针疗法|A form of acupuncture where a small electric current is passed between pairs of acupuncture needles. |Electro-acupuncture can be used to treat chronic pain. 电针疗法可以用来治疗慢性疼痛。| Electro-acupuncture was shown to be effective in relieving the patient's neck pain. 电针疗法 terbukti efektif dalam meredakan nyeri leher pasien.|Synonym: electroacupuncture, EA |Antonym: None| noun

electro-acupuncture:/ɪˌlektroʊˈækjʊpʌŋktʃər/| n. |电针|The insertion of fine needles into the skin at specific points along what are considered to be lines of energy (meridians), combined with the application of a pulsating electrical current.|The doctor recommended electro-acupuncture for my back pain. 医生建议我用电针治疗背痛。|She found electro-acupuncture to be more effective than traditional acupuncture. 她发现电针比传统针灸更有效。|Synonym: electropuncture|Antonym: None| noun
