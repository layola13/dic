albinism: /ˈælbɪˌnɪzəm/ | n. | 白化病 | A genetic condition characterized by a deficiency or the absence of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes. |  Albinism affects people of all races. 白化病影响所有种族的人。

Albinism can also affect animals. 白化病也会影响动物。

| 近义词: achromasia, achromatosis | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
