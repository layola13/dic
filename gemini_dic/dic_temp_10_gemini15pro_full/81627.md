grecque:/ɡrɛk/| adj. n.|希腊式的，希腊风格的；希腊图案|A decorative motif, especially in embroidery, resembling a Greek fret.  Also, a style or fashion reminiscent of ancient Greece. |The dress featured a delicate grecque pattern along the hemline. 这件连衣裙的裙摆上有精致的希腊图案。|Synonyms: Hellenic, Greek, Grecian|Antonyms: Roman, Byzantine|adjective, noun
