fumigators:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪtərz/| n. |熏蒸员，熏蒸器|A person or device that fumigates (exposes to fumes, especially to disinfect or kill vermin).|例句：The fumigators arrived to treat the house for termites.  熏蒸员来处理房子里的白蚁。

例句：The fumigators used a powerful insecticide to eradicate the pests. 熏蒸员使用强力杀虫剂来消灭害虫。|近义词：exterminators, pest controllers|反义词:|noun
