foliage: /ˈfoʊliɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈfəʊliɪdʒ/ (UK)
n.
叶子; 植物; (尤指树的)叶子
The plant’s foliage is a vibrant green. 这种植物的叶子是鲜艳的绿色。
The dense foliage blocked out the sunlight. 茂密的树叶遮住了阳光。
leaves, greenery, verdure
bareness, nakedness
noun
