exalbuminous:/ˌɛksælˈbjuːmɪnəs/
adjective
无胚乳的
(of a seed) having no endosperm or perisperm; containing all its nutrient matter within the embryo.
The orchid seed is minute and exalbuminous, depending on a symbiotic fungus for germination. 兰花的种子很小，没有胚乳，依靠共生真菌萌发。
近义词: endosperm-less
反义词: albuminous
词性: adjective
