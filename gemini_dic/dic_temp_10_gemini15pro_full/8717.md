anticyclone:/ˈæntiˌsaɪkloʊn/ (US) /ˌæntiˈsaɪkləʊn/ (UK)| n.|反气旋|A weather system with high atmospheric pressure at its center, around which air slowly circulates in a clockwise direction in the Northern Hemisphere and counterclockwise in the Southern Hemisphere. Anticyclones are generally associated with fair weather.|Example 1: The anticyclone brought clear skies and sunshine. 反气旋带来了晴朗的天空和阳光。

Example 2:  The weather forecast predicts an anticyclone will settle over the region next week. 天气预报预测下周该地区将出现反气旋。|近义词:high-pressure system, high|反义词:cyclone, low-pressure system, low|词性:名词
