belittlers:/bɪˈlɪtlərz/| n. |轻视者；贬低者；蔑视者|People who belittle others often do so to feel superior.  轻视别人的人经常这样做是为了让自己感觉优越。|The constant belittlers in her life made her feel insecure. 她生活中那些不断贬低她的人让她感到没有安全感。|Synonyms: deprecators, disparagers, minimizers|Antonyms: admirers, praisers, supporters|noun

belittle:/bɪˈlɪtl/| vt. |轻视；贬低；小看|He belittled her achievements, making her feel insignificant. 他贬低了她的成就，让她觉得自己微不足道。|Don't belittle the importance of hard work. 不要轻视努力工作的重要性。|Synonyms: demean, disparage, underestimate|Antonyms: praise, extol, overestimate|verb
