ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *molle*, from Old English *molde*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mulþaz*  meaning "dust, loose earth". Related to Dutch *mol*, German *Maulwurf* ("mole" - the animal).  The chemical sense derives from German *Mol*.
