alienly:/ˈeɪliənli/
adverb
异样地；陌生似地；格格不入地
in a way that is alien; strangely; unnaturally.
He looked at her alienly, as if he'd never met her before. 他异样地看着她，仿佛以前从未见过她似的。
近义词：strangely, bizarrely, uncannily, oddly
反义词：familiarly, normally, naturally
词性：副词

alienly:/ˈeɪliənli/
adverb
像外星人一样地；与外星人有关地
in a way that relates to or resembles aliens.
The creature moved alienly, its limbs twitching in unnatural ways.  这个生物的动作很怪异，四肢以不自然的方式抽搐着。
近义词：extraterrestrially
反义词：humanly, earthly
词性：副词
