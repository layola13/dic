nohs:/noʊz/| n.| Noh|A major form of classical Japanese musical drama that has been performed since the 14th century. It combines music, dance, and acting, often using masks, and typically deals with themes of dreams, the supernatural, and ghosts.|例句：I attended a captivating Noh performance last night. 昨晚我看了一场引人入胜的能剧表演。
