debauchedly:/dɪˈbɔːtʃɪdli/
adverb
放荡地，堕落地
In a debauched manner; indulging in excessive pleasure or sensuality.
He lived debauchedly for years, indulging in every excess. 他多年来过着放荡的生活，沉溺于各种各样的过度享乐。
The rock star stumbled debauchedly out of the limousine.  这位摇滚明星跌跌撞撞地从豪华轿车里走了出来。
Synonyms: dissolutely, immorally, licentiously, wantonly, riotously
Antonyms: virtuously, morally, chastely, modestly, temperately
词性: 副词
