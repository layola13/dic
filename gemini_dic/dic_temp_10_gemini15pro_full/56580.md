don't:/doʊnt/ |aux. v. |不要，不做| Contraction of "do not". Used to form negative sentences in the simple present tense, except for the third person singular.| I don't like spinach. 我不喜欢菠菜。| 近义词: do not | 反义词: do | 词性: auxiliary verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I don't eat meat.  我不吃肉。
They don't speak French. 他们不说法语。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm not working, I'm studying. 我没在工作，我在学习。(don't 用于否定 simple present, am not 用于否定 present continuous)
He isn't sleeping, he's reading. 他没在睡觉，他在看书。 (don't 用于否定 simple present, isn't 用于否定 present continuous)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I haven't finished my homework yet. 我还没完成我的作业。(don't 用于否定 simple present, haven't 用于否定 present perfect)
She hasn't seen that movie. 她还没看过那部电影。(don't 用于否定 simple present, hasn't 用于否定 present perfect)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I haven't been feeling well lately. 我最近感觉不太好。(don't 用于否定 simple present, haven't been 用于否定 present perfect continuous)
They haven't been studying very hard. 他们最近学习不太努力。(don't 用于否定 simple present, haven't been 用于否定 present perfect continuous)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I didn't go to the party. 我没去参加派对。
He didn't finish his dinner. 他没有吃完他的晚餐。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I wasn't working when you called. 你打电话的时候我并没有在工作。(don't 用于否定 simple present, wasn't 用于否定 past continuous)
She wasn't sleeping, she was reading. 她没在睡觉，她正在读书。(don't 用于否定 simple present, wasn't 用于否定 past continuous)


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I hadn't finished my work before you arrived. 你来之前我还没有完成我的工作。(don't 用于否定 simple present, hadn't 用于否定 past perfect)
They hadn't seen the movie before. 他们之前没有看过那部电影。(don't 用于否定 simple present, hadn't 用于否定 past perfect)

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I hadn't been feeling well before the trip. 旅行前我感觉不太好。(don't 用于否定 simple present, hadn't been 用于否定 past perfect continuous)
They hadn't been studying hard before the exam. 考试前他们学习不太努力。(don't 用于否定 simple present, hadn't been 用于否定 past perfect continuous)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I won't go to the party tomorrow. 我明天不会去参加派对。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't 用于否定 simple future)
He won't finish his work on time. 他不会按时完成他的工作。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't 用于否定 simple future)

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I won't be working tomorrow afternoon. 明天下午我不会在工作。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't be 用于否定 future continuous)
She won't be studying at this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候她不会在学习。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't be 用于否定 future continuous)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I won't have finished my project by next week.  下周前我不会完成我的项目。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't have 用于否定 future perfect)
They won't have arrived by then. 到那时他们不会到达。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't have 用于否定 future perfect)

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I won't have been working here for ten years by next June. 到明年六月，我在这里工作还没有满十年。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't have been 用于否定 future perfect continuous)
She won't have been studying English for five years by then. 到那时，她学习英语还不到五年。(don't 用于否定 simple present, won't have been 用于否定 future perfect continuous)


PHRASE->
Don't worry.  别担心。
Don't mention it.  不客气。/不用谢。
Don't you dare! 你敢!
Don't be silly! 别傻了!
Don't tell me!  不会吧！/ 你别说！


注意事项: don't 用于 simple present tense 的否定形式，不适用于其他时态。其他时态的否定形式需要使用相应的助动词，例如 doesn't, didn't, won't, haven't, hasn't, am not, isn't, aren't, wasn't, weren't 等。
