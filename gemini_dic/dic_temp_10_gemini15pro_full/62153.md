enclosers:/ɪnˈkloʊzərz/
n.
围场; 附件; 围栏;  (信封内的) 附件
People who enclose something, or things that enclose something.  Specifically, it can refer to people or things that surround or confine something else.
The enclosers of the park kept the deer safe from predators. 公园的围栏保护鹿免受捕食者的侵害。
The enclosers with the letter contained important documents. 信中的附件包含重要文件。
synonyms: containers, wrappers, holders, fences, barriers
antonyms: openers, liberators, releasers
noun
