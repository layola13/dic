ETYMOLOGY->
From "grandstand" (n.), referring to a large, prominent viewing platform, implying a desire to be seen.  "Grandstand" is a compound of "grand" (large, impressive) and "stand" (a raised platform). The verb sense developed from the noun, referring to the act of performing on such a platform, often with the intention of showing off.
