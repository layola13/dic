immortalize: /ɪˈmɔːrtəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使不朽，使永垂不朽 | To confer enduring fame upon; to make immortal. |  Shakespeare immortalized the name of Hamlet. 莎士比亚使哈姆雷特的名字永垂不朽。

The photograph immortalized the moment of victory. 这张照片使胜利的时刻永垂不朽。

| 近义词: commemorate, eternalize, memorialize | 反义词: forget, obliterate | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immortalizare, from Latin immortalis ("immortal") + -izare (suffix forming verbs).  Latin immortalis is from in- (not) + mortalis (mortal).
