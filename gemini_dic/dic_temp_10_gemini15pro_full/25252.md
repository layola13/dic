buffer:/ˈbʌfər/ (US) /ˈbʌfə(r)/ (UK)| v. |缓冲，减轻；保护|To buffer something means to lessen the negative impact of something on it. |The government introduced measures to buffer the economy against recession. 政府采取措施以缓冲经济衰退的影响。| synonym: protect, shield, cushion | antonym: expose, worsen | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *buffeter "to strike repeatedly, puff up," from buffe "blow, puff of air." Originally referred to a blow or a padded shield used to soften blows. The meaning extended to anything that absorbs shock or protects from harm.
