gristly: /ˈɡrɪsli/ | adj. |  多软骨的；坚韧的；令人毛骨悚然的 | Consisting of or full of gristle; tough and chewy; causing a feeling of disgust or horror. | The steak was cheap but incredibly gristly. 这块牛排很便宜，但有很多软骨。 | The movie featured a gristly murder scene. 这部电影里有一个令人毛骨悚然的谋杀场景。 | synonyms: cartilaginous, tough, chewy, grisly, gruesome | antonyms: tender, soft, smooth | adjective

gristle: /ˈɡrɪsəl/ | n. | 软骨 |  Tough, flexible tissue that is a precursor to bone. | I couldn't eat the meat because it was full of gristle. 我吃不了这块肉，因为它全是软骨。 |  synonyms: cartilage | antonyms: bone | noun
