parliamentarily:/ˌpɑːrləˈmentərɪli/ | adv. |议会地；按照议会程序地|In a manner consistent with the rules and procedures of a parliament.  Refers to actions or discussions conducted according to the established customs and regulations of a legislative body.|The matter was handled parliamentarily, ensuring a fair debate. 这件事按照议会程序处理，确保了公平的辩论。|Synonyms:  legislatively, officially, formally|Antonyms:  illegally, improperly, irregularly|Word type: Adverb

Etymology:
From parliament + -ary + -ly.  Ultimately from Old French parlement (from parler "to speak").
