outstudy: /ˌaʊtˈstʌdi/
verb
超过…学习; 比…用功
To study harder or for longer than someone else.

例句：
He outstudied his classmates and got the highest grade.
他比他的同学们学习更努力，获得了最高分。

例句：
She outstudied everyone in her class to win the scholarship.
她比班上所有的人都学习更努力，赢得了奖学金。


近义词：surpass, outwork, outdo
反义词：underperform, lag behind
词性：verb
