overplan:/ˌoʊvərˈplæn/ | v. | 过度计划；计划过多 | To plan in excessive detail or to make too many plans. |  We overplanned our trip and ended up feeling rushed. 我们过度计划了我们的旅行，最后感觉很匆忙。| synonyms: overorganize, overschedule | antonyms: underplan, improvise | verb

overplan:/ˌoʊvərˈplæn/ | n. |  过度计划；过多的计划 | An excessively detailed plan or an instance of overplanning.  | The overplan led to unnecessary stress.  这个过度计划导致了不必要的压力。| synonyms: overorganization, overkill | antonyms: underplanning, improvisation | noun
