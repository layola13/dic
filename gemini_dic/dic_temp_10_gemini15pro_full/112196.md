ETYMOLOGY->
From "loony" (adjective and noun) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition) + "-'s" (possessive suffix).  "Loony" itself comes from "lunatic", ultimately derived from the Latin word "lunaticus", meaning "moonstruck".  The belief was that the moon influenced mental states.
