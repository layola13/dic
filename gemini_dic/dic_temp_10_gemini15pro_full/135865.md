nonprotestation:/ˌnɒnˌprɒtɪˈsteɪʃən/ |n.| 不抗议，不反对，默认 |Absence or lack of protest; the act of not protesting or objecting. |His nonprotestation implied agreement to the terms. 他的不抗议意味着他同意这些条款。| The government interpreted the people's nonprotestation as consent for the new policy. 政府将人民的不抗议解释为对新政策的同意。 |Near synonyms: acquiescence, acceptance, compliance, resignation. |Antonyms: protest, objection, dissent, resistance.| noun
