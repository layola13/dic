anjelica:/ænˈdʒɛlɪkə/ | n. |当归；欧白芷|A tall aromatic plant of the parsley family, the stalks of which are candied and eaten.|例句：Angelica is used in traditional medicine. 当归在传统医学中被使用。

例句：Candied angelica is a popular sweet treat. 蜜饯当归是一种受欢迎的甜食。|近义词：herb, plant|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *angelica, feminine of angelicus (“angelic”), from angelus (“angel”). So called because it was believed to bloom on the feast day of the Archangel Michael (May 8) and to offer protection against evil spirits and disease.
