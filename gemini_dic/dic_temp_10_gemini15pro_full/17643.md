becharm: /bɪˈtʃɑːrm/ /biˈtʃɑːrm/
verb
使…着迷；迷住；吸引
To attract and please someone greatly.

例句：
He was becharmed by her beauty and wit.
他被她的美貌和智慧迷住了。

Her smile becharmed everyone in the room.
她的笑容迷住了房间里的每一个人。


近义词：enchant, captivate, fascinate, charm, allure
反义词：repel, disgust, offend
词性：verb
