nonruinous:/nɒnˈruːɪnəs/
adjective
非毁灭性的；未造成破坏的
Not causing ruin or destruction; not disastrous.
The storm was severe, but thankfully nonruinous.  暴风雨很猛烈，但谢天谢地没有造成破坏。
The policy had a nonruinous effect on the economy. 这项政策对经济没有造成破坏性的影响。
Synonyms: harmless, benign, innocuous, safe
Antonyms: ruinous, destructive, devastating, catastrophic
词性: 形容词
