insurant:/ɪnˈʃʊərənt/ | n. | 投保人，被保险人 | A person who contracts for insurance; a person whose life or property is insured. | Example 1: The insurant paid the premium on time.  投保人按时支付了保费。

Example 2: The insurant's car was damaged in the accident. 被保险人的汽车在事故中受损了。 | Synonym: insured, policyholder | Antonym: insurer, underwriter | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French, present participle of ensurir ‘to insure’, from en- ‘make’ + sur ‘sure’.
