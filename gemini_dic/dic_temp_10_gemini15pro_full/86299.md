hebdomadary:/ˌhɛbˈdɒmədəri/ | adj. n.| 每周的；一周一次的；周报 |Occurring or done every week; weekly.  Relating to a week; lasting seven days. A publication issued weekly, especially a periodical.| The hebdomadary meeting was held every Monday morning. 每周例会于每周一早上举行。|  She subscribed to a hebdomadary magazine. 她订阅了一份周刊。| 近义词: weekly, septenary | 反义词: daily, monthly | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源：From Late Latin *hebdomadarius, from Latin hebdomas ‘period of seven days’, from Greek hebdomas, from hepta ‘seven’.
