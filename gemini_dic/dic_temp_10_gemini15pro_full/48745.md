defining:/dɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 定义的；决定性的；典型的 |  serving to identify or characterize; giving the meaning of; decisive;  | The defining moment of her career was winning the Olympic gold medal. 她职业生涯的决定性时刻是赢得奥运金牌。 |  characteristic, crucial, decisive, essential, important  |  unimportant, insignificant, minor, vague | adjective

defining:/dɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 定义；界定 | the act of stating the meaning of something; | The defining of the terms was crucial to the success of the negotiation.  对条款的定义对于谈判的成功至关重要。 |  definition, clarification, explanation, specification | ambiguity, vagueness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *definen, from Old French definer, from Latin dēfīnīre (“to limit, bound, define”), from dē (“from, down from, away from”) + fīnīre (“to end, limit, finish”), from fīnis (“boundary, limit, end”).
