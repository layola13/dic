half-contentedly:/ˌhæf kənˈtɛntɪdli/
| adv. | 半心半意地；有点满意地 |  Feeling or showing a moderate degree of contentment; not fully satisfied. | She smiled half-contentedly, still unsure about her decision. 她半心半意地笑了笑，仍然对自己的决定不确定。 |  almost contentedly, partly contentedly, somewhat contentedly | discontentedly, unhappily | adverb
