dogtie: /ˈdɒɡtɑɪ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡtaɪ/ (US) | n. | 狗绳；拴狗的皮带 | A short leash used to tie up a dog, typically to a fixed object. |  The dogtie was too short, and the dog couldn't reach its water bowl.  狗绳太短了，狗够不到它的水碗。| Near synonyms: leash, lead, tether | Antonyms: unleash, untie | Noun
