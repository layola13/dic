embered:/ˈɛmbərd/
adjective
燃烧着的；闷烧的；闪耀的
glowing like embers; still burning or smoldering.
The embered logs in the fireplace cast a warm glow. 壁炉里闷烧的木头发出温暖的光芒。
glowing, smoldering, burning
extinguished, cold, dead
词性: 形容词

词源:  From the noun "ember" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Ember" comes from Old English *ǣmyrian "to be embers," from Proto-Germanic *aimuz- "ashes," related to Old Norse eimyrja "embers."
