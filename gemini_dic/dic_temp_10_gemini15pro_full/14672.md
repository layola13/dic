avuncular: /əˈvʌŋkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 叔伯般的；像伯父（或叔父、舅父、姑父、姨父）的 | Kind and friendly towards a younger or less experienced person, like an uncle. | He gave me some avuncular advice. 他给了我一些像长辈一样的建议。 | My professor has an avuncular manner. 我的教授有一种慈父般的态度。 | 近义词: fatherly, paternal, benevolent, kind | 反义词: unkind, hostile, austere | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *avunculus* "maternal uncle," diminutive of *avus* "grandfather."
