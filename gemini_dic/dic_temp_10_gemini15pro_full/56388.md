doldrums:/ˈdoʊldrəmz/ /ˈdɒldrəmz/ | n. | 沉闷；忧郁；赤道无风带 | A state of inactivity or stagnation, often caused by depression or boredom; a period of calm, light winds, or squalls, especially in the tropics. |  The economy has been in the doldrums for months. 经济数月来一直不景气。 | synonyms: slump, depression, stagnation, blues, inertia, apathy | antonyms:  boom, excitement, activity, energy, enthusiasm | noun

doldrums:/ˈdoʊldrəmz/ /ˈdɒldrəmz/ | n. | 赤道无风带 | A region of the ocean near the equator characterized by calms, light winds, or squalls. |  The ship was becalmed in the doldrums. 这艘船在赤道无风带停滞不前。| synonyms:  intertropical convergence zone, ITCZ | antonyms: trade winds | noun
