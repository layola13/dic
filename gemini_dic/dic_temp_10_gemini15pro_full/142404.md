orval: /ɔːrˈvæl/ | n. | 奥尔瓦尔（一种 Trappist 啤酒）| A Trappist ale brewed at the Notre-Dame d'Orval monastery in Belgium. |  I enjoyed a bottle of Orval last night. 我昨晚喝了一瓶奥尔瓦尔。|  Example: This Orval has a complex, hoppy flavor.  这瓶奥尔瓦尔啤酒味道复杂，带有啤酒花的香味。| 近义词:Trappist ale, Belgian beer | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: From the French "Val d'Or" meaning "golden valley."
