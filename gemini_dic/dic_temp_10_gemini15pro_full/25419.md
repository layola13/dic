bulk:/bʌlk/| n. |体积，容量；大部分，主体；散装货物|the large size or mass of something|例句：The bulk of the population lives in cities.  大部分人口居住在城市。|近义词：mass, majority, main part|反义词:minority, small part|noun
bulk:/bʌlk/| v. |显得重要；显得庞大|to appear large or important|例句：The building bulked large against the sky. 这座建筑物在天空中显得格外庞大。|近义词：loom, dominate|反义词:shrink, diminish|verb
bulk:/bʌlk/| adj. |散装的|bought or sold in large quantities|例句：We buy our flour in bulk. 我们批量购买面粉。|近义词：wholesale|反义词:retail|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bulke*, from Old Norse *bulki* (“cargo, heap”), from Proto-Germanic *bulkijǭ* (“lump, swelling”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰelǵʰ- (“to swell”). Cognate with Icelandic *bulki* (“cargo”), Swedish *bulk* (“trunk of the body”), Danish *bulk* (“protruding part”).
