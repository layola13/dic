diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US) /ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/ (UK)| n.|腹泻|An abnormally frequent discharge of semisolid or fluid fecal matter from the bowel.|She had a bad case of diarrhea after eating contaminated food. 她吃了被污染的食物后腹泻得很厉害。|近义词：the runs, loose stools, the shits (informal)|反义词: constipation|noun


diarrhoea (chiefly British):/ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/| n.|腹泻|Spelling variant of diarrhea, primarily used in British English.|The doctor diagnosed her with diarrhoea. 医生诊断她患有腹泻。|近义词：the runs, loose stools|反义词: constipation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diarrhoia, from Greek diarrhoia "a flowing through," from diarrhein "to flow through," from dia- "through" + rhein "to flow."
