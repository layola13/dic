idiolectal:/ˌɪdiəˈlɛktəl/
adjective
个人语言特色的；属于个人语言习惯的
Relating to an idiolect, the speech habits peculiar to a particular person.
Her idiolectal expressions often confused her colleagues. 她的个人语言习惯常常让同事们感到困惑。
Synonyms: personal, individual, characteristic
Antonyms: common, shared, general
词性: 形容词
