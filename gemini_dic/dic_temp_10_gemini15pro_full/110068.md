lichenicolous:/laɪˈkɛnɪkələs/| adj.|地衣寄生性的，生长在地衣上的|Growing on or inhabiting lichens.  This fungus is lichenicolous, meaning it lives on lichens.|This lichenicolous fungus is only found growing on specific species of lichens.|近义词: lichen-dwelling|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly  'saxicolous' (rock-dwelling) or 'corticolous' (bark-dwelling) depending on the context)|词性: adjective
