agentic:/əˈdʒɛntɪk/
adjective
能动的；具有能动性的
Acting as an agent; having the power to act.
She felt very agentic in her new role as project manager. 她作为项目经理，感到非常有自主能动性。
近义词: active, proactive, self-determining, autonomous
反义词: passive, submissive, dependent
词性: adjective

agentic:/əˈdʒɛntɪk/
adjective
代理的
Relating to or characteristic of an agent.  (This meaning is less common than the psychological sense.)
The agentic properties of the chemical allowed it to bond with other substances.  这种化学物质的代理特性使其能够与其他物质结合。
近义词: representative, acting, deputy
反义词: principal
词性: adjective
