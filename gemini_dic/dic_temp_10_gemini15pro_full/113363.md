lycanthropous:/ˌlaɪkənˈθrɒpəs/| adj.|患狼狂的，变狼狂的|Affected with, or subject to, lycanthropy; of or relating to lycanthropy.  Exhibiting characteristics of a werewolf, especially the supposed ability to transform into a wolf.|The villagers feared the lycanthropous creature lurking in the woods. 村民们害怕潜伏在树林里的狼人。|Synonyms: werewolfish, lupine|Antonyms: human|Adjective


lycanthropy:/ˌlaɪkənˈθrɒpi/| n.|变狼狂|The supernatural transformation of a person into a wolf, or a condition in which a person believes they are a wolf. Also, a literary theme concerning such transformations.|Ancient legends are filled with tales of lycanthropy. 古老的传说中充满了关于变狼狂的故事。|Synonyms: therianthropy (more general), shape-shifting|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
