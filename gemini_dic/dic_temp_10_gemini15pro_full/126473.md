mythologically:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adv.
神话地，神话学上地
In terms of or relating to mythology; from the standpoint of mythology.
The hero was mythologically connected to the sun god.  这位英雄在神话上与太阳神有关联。
The story is mythologically significant.  这个故事在神话学上意义重大。
近义词:allegorically, symbolically, fabulously
反义词:historically, factually, literally
词性:副词


词源:
From mythology + -ically.  来自 mythology（神话） + -ically（副词后缀）。
