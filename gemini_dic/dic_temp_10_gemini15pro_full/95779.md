incontinence:/ɪnkɒntɪnəns/ (British) /ˌɪnkɑːnˈtɪnəns/ (American)| n. |失禁，不能自制|Inability to control the discharge of urine or feces.  The loss of self-restraint.|She suffers from incontinence.| 近义词: inability to control urination/defecation, loss of bladder/bowel control| 反义词: continence |词性: 名词
