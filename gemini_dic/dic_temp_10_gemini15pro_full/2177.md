addicted: /əˈdɪktɪd/
adjective
上瘾的，沉迷的
Physically and mentally dependent on a particular substance, and unable to stop taking it without incurring adverse effects.
He is addicted to nicotine. 他对尼古丁上瘾。
She became addicted to gambling. 她赌博成瘾了。
近义词: dependent, hooked, obsessed
反义词: indifferent, averse
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin addictus, past participle of addicere "to assign, devote, give over," from ad- "to" + dicere "to say." Originally meaning "bound over, delivered over, assigned," especially of debtors assigned to a creditor as slaves. The sense of "given over to a habit" emerged in the 19th century.
