overstiffened: /ˌoʊvərˈstɪfənd/
adjective
过度硬化的，僵硬的
Made too stiff or rigid.  The fabric had been overstiffened with starch, making it uncomfortable to wear.
The car's suspension felt overstiffened, resulting in a bumpy ride.
近义词: rigid, inflexible, unyielding
反义词: flexible, pliable, supple
词性: adjective

词源:
From "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "stiffen" (verb meaning to make or become stiff) + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Stiffen" comes from the Old English "stiefan."
