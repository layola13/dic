crosstalk:/ˈkrɔːstɔːk/ | n. | 串扰，串音，串话 | Unwanted transfer of signals between communication channels. | The crosstalk from the other line made it difficult to hear.  另一条线路的串音使得很难听清。 | interference, noise, leakage | isolation, shielding | noun

crosstalk:/ˈkrɔːstɔːk/ | vi. | 串扰，串音，互相干扰 | To interfere with another signal or conversation. | The wires were so close together they started to crosstalk.  电线靠得太近，开始互相干扰了。 | interfere, overlap | separate, isolate | verb
