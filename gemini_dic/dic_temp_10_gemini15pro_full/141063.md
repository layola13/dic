onomantical:/ˌɒnəˈmæntɪkəl/| adj.|与名字有关的；命名上的；占卜的|Pertaining to names or onomastics; of or relating to divination by names. |例句：Onomantical studies reveal the cultural significance of names. 命名学研究揭示了名字的文化意义。

例句：Some cultures have onomantical traditions where names are believed to influence destiny. 一些文化有命名传统，认为名字会影响命运。|近义词：nominal, nomenclatural|反义词:anonymous|词性:adjective
