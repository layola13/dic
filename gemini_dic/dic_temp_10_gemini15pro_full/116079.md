martlemas: /ˈmɑːrtlɪməs/ | n. | 马丁节 | A Christian feast day celebrated on 11 November in honour of St Martin of Tours. |  例句：Martlemas was traditionally a time for hiring fairs and settling accounts. 马丁节传统上是招聘会和结算账目的时间。

例句：The farmers hoped for good weather until Martlemas so they could harvest their crops. 农民们希望马丁节之前天气都好，这样他们就能收割庄稼了。

近义词: Martinmas | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
