outmanoeuvre:/ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/ /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/
verb
智胜，以策略取胜
to defeat someone by being more clever or skillful in your plans and methods
例句：The company outmanoeuvred its rivals by launching a new product first.  这家公司通过率先推出新产品，智胜了竞争对手。

例句：The politician skillfully outmanoeuvred his opponents in the debate.  这位政治家在辩论中巧妙地战胜了对手。

近义词：outwit, outsmart, surpass, defeat, circumvent
反义词：be outmanoeuvred, be defeated, lose to

词性：v.
