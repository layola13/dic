explode:/ɪkˈsploʊd/| v. |爆炸，爆发，激增|To burst or shatter violently and noisily, as from internal pressure.|The bomb exploded near the market. 炸弹在市场附近爆炸了。|近义词：burst, detonate, erupt, blow up|反义词:implode, collapse|verb

explode:/ɪkˈsploʊd/| v. |爆发(感情)，激增|To suddenly express a strong emotion.|He exploded with rage when he heard the news. 当他听到这个消息时，他勃然大怒。|近义词：erupt, burst out, vent|反义词:suppress, contain|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin explodere, "to drive off the stage by clapping," from ex- "out" + plaudere "to clap."  The sense shifted from driving someone off stage to a sudden bursting forth.
