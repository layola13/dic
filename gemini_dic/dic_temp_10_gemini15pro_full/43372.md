craniology: /kreɪniˈɒlədʒi/ /kreɪniˈɑːlədʒi/
noun
颅骨学
The scientific study of the shape and size of skulls, especially human skulls.
例句：Craniology was once used to support racist theories. 颅骨学曾被用来支持种族主义理论。

例句：Modern craniology focuses on variations in skull morphology within and between populations. 现代颅骨学关注人群内部和人群之间颅骨形态的差异。

近义词：phrenology
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider fields like psychology or neurology as focusing on different aspects of the brain and behavior.)
词性: Noun
