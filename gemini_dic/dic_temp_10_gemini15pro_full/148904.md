overwidely:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪdli/ | adv. | 过于广泛地，过分广泛地 |  In an excessively wide manner; to an excessive degree of wideness or breadth. | The topic was overwidely discussed, leading to confusion.  这个话题讨论得过于广泛，导致了混乱。| The net was cast overwidely, catching unintended species. 这张网撒得过宽，捕获了非目标物种。| 近义词: excessively, too broadly | 反义词: narrowly, specifically | 词性: 副词
