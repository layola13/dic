hoarded:/ˈhɔːrdɪd/
|v.|囤积，贮藏|past tense and past participle of hoard|The squirrels hoarded nuts for the winter. 松鼠为冬天囤积坚果。|
近义词:accumulated, amassed, stockpiled, cached|反义词:squandered, wasted, dissipated|词性:Verb (past tense)

hoard:/hɔːrd/
|n.|贮藏物，积蓄；v. 囤积，贮藏|a large amount of something valuable that is kept hidden; to collect and keep large amounts of food, money, or valuable objects, especially secretly|He had a hoard of old coins in his attic. 他在阁楼里藏了一堆旧硬币。|She hoards old newspapers and magazines. 她囤积旧报纸和杂志。|近义词:cache, stockpile, store, reserve|反义词:spend, squander, waste, distribute|词性:Noun, Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hord (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *huzdam, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱeus- (“to hide, conceal”). Cognate with Old Norse huzða (“to hoard, treasure”), Old Frisian horda (“treasure”), Middle Dutch horten (“to hoard”), Old High German hurt (“treasure”), German Hort (“treasure, hoard”).
