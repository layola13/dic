misopinion: /ˌmɪsəˈpɪnjən/ | n. | 错误的观点，误解 | An incorrect or mistaken belief or judgment. | He held a strong misopinion about the efficacy of the new drug. 他对这种新药的疗效抱有强烈的误解。| Synonyms: misconception, fallacy, delusion, misapprehension | Antonyms:  correct opinion, understanding, truth | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrong, bad) + opinion (belief, judgment).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old French "mes-" (wrongly), ultimately from a Proto-Germanic source meaning "wrongly, badly." "Opinion" comes from Old French "opinion," ultimately from Latin "opinio" (opinion, belief, conjecture).
