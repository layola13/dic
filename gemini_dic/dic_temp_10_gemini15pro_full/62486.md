endosteal: /ɛnˈdɒstiəl/ (British) /ɛnˈdɑːstiəl/ (American)| adj.|骨内膜的|Relating to the endosteum, the membrane lining the inner surface of bones.|The endosteal surface of the bone is where bone resorption occurs.|近义词: intraosseous|反义词: periosteal|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From endo- (within) + -osteal (relating to bone).  Ultimately from Greek *éndon* (within) and *ostéon* (bone).
