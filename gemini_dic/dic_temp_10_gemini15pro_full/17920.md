beeswaxed:/ˈbiːzwækst/
adjective
涂了蜂蜡的；擦亮的
polished with beeswax; shiny.
The antique table was beeswaxed to a gleaming shine.  古董桌子被涂上蜂蜡，闪闪发光。
Near Synonyms: polished, buffed, glossed, shiny, gleaming
Antonyms: dull, matte, unpolished
词性: adjective

beeswaxed:/ˈbiːzwækst/
verb (past participle)
用蜂蜡涂抹；用蜂蜡擦亮
to polish or treat with beeswax.
The floorboards had been beeswaxed years ago and still retained a soft sheen.  地板几年前就涂过蜂蜡，现在仍然保持着柔和的光泽。
Near Synonyms: waxed, treated, polished
Antonyms: stripped, untreated
词性: verb (past participle)
