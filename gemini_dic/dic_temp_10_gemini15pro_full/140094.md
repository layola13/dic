off-grid:/ˌɒfˈɡrɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈɡrɪd/| adj.|离网的，不依赖公共设施的|Not connected to public utilities such as the electricity grid, water supply, or sewage system.|They live an off-grid life in a remote cabin. 他们在一个偏远的小木屋里过着离网生活。|Synonyms: independent, self-sufficient, autonomous|Antonyms: on-grid, connected, dependent|adjective

off-grid:/ˌɒfˈɡrɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈɡrɪd/| n.|离网生活，离网系统|A lifestyle or system that is not connected to public utilities.|Off-grid living is becoming increasingly popular. 离网生活正变得越来越流行。|Synonyms: independent living, self-sufficient living|Antonyms: on-grid living, connected living|noun
