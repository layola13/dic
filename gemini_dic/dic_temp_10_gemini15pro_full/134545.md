nonliquidation:/ˌnɑːnˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃən/
n.|非清算，不清理|The nonliquidation of assets allowed the company to continue operating.  公司的资产未清算，使其能够继续运营。|The agreement ensured the nonliquidation of the family estate. 该协议确保了家族财产不被清算。|continuation, preservation|liquidation, dissolution|noun
nonliquidating:/ˌnɑːnˈlɪkwɪˌdeɪtɪŋ/
adj.|非清算的|A nonliquidating dividend is paid out of current earnings, not by selling assets. 非清算股息是从当前收益中支付的，而不是通过出售资产。|The company issued a nonliquidating distribution to its shareholders. 公司向其股东发放了非清算分配。|continuing, ongoing|liquidating, dissolving|adjective
