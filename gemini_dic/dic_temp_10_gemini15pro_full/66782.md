exoticism:/ɪɡˈzɒtɪsɪzəm/ (US) /ɛɡˈzɑːtɪsɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|异国情调；外来风格|The exoticism of the Far East fascinated him.  他对远东的异国情调着迷。|Example: The restaurant's decor was full of exoticism, with brightly colored fabrics and unusual plants. 这家餐厅的装饰充满了异国情调，有着色彩鲜艳的织物和奇特的植物。|Synonyms: foreignness, strangeness, otherness, unusualness, outlandishness|Antonyms: familiarity, ordinariness, commonness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *exotisme*, from *exotique* (“exotic”), from Late Latin *exoticus*, from Ancient Greek *ἐξωτικός* (*exōtikós*, “foreign”), from *ἔξω* (*exō*, “outside”).
