limbo:/ˈlɪmboʊ/ (US) /ˈlɪmbəʊ/ (UK)| n.| 困境；不确定的状态；边缘；地狱边境|An uncertain period of awaiting a decision or resolution; a state of neglect or oblivion.  The supposed place of the souls of unbaptized infants and of the righteous who died before Christ's coming.|例句：The project has been in limbo for months, waiting for funding.  这个项目因为等待资金，已经搁置好几个月了。

例句：He felt his career was in limbo. 他觉得自己的事业正处于停滞状态。|近义词：uncertainty, oblivion, purgatory, abeyance|反义词：certainty, resolution, decision|词性: noun


Etymology->
From Latin Limbus, meaning "border," "edge," referring to the edge of hell.
