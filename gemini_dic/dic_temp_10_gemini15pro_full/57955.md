dripper:/ˈdrɪpər/ (US), /ˈdrɪpə/ (UK)| n.|滴水器；滴滤式咖啡壶；点滴器|A device that dispenses liquid drop by drop.  A coffee maker that brews coffee by dripping hot water over ground coffee beans contained in a filter.|The coffee dripper made a soothing sound as it brewed my morning coffee. 咖啡滴滤壶在冲泡我早晨的咖啡时发出舒缓的声音。|The nurse adjusted the IV dripper to control the flow of medicine.  护士调整了静脉点滴器来控制药物的流动。|synonyms: percolator, filter, infuser|antonyms: N/A|noun

dripping:/ˈdrɪpɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdrɪpɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|滴落；滴下的液体；湿淋淋的|The process of falling in drops; liquid that falls in drops; very wet.|The dripping faucet kept me awake all night.  滴水的水龙头让我整夜没睡。|The dog was dripping wet after its swim in the lake.  那条狗在湖里游完泳后浑身湿透了。|synonyms: leaking, trickling, sopping|antonyms: dry, arid|noun, adjective
