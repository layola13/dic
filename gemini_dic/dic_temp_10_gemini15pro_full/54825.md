dispersible:/dɪˈspɜːsəbl/ /dɪˈspɜːrsəbl/
adjective
可分散的，可弥散的
Capable of being dispersed or spread out, especially in a liquid.
The powder is dispersible in water.  这种粉末可分散在水中。
The seeds are dispersible by wind. 这些种子可随风散播。
synonyms: spreadable, diffusible, distributable, scatterable
antonyms: insoluble, non-dispersible, indivisible
词性: adjective
