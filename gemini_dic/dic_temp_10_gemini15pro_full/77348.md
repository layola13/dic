gaudiest:/ˈɡɔːdiɪst/
adjective
最花哨的；最俗丽的
superlative adjective of gaudy: extravagantly bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless.
例句：She wore the gaudiest dress at the party. 她在派对上穿了最花哨的裙子。

例句：The gaudiest float in the parade featured a giant inflatable dinosaur. 游行中最花哨的花车以一只巨大的充气恐龙为特色。

近义词：flashiest, showiest, loudest, most flamboyant
反义词：plainest, simplest, subtlest, most understated
词性：adjective

词源：From gaudy + -est (superlative suffix). Gaudy itself comes from the late 16th century, probably from the obsolete gaud ("an ornament; a trinket"), from Anglo-Norman gaude, ultimately of uncertain origin.
