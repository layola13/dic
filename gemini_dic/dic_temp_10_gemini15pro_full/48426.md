deduce:/dɪˈd(j)uːs/ /dɪˈduːs/| v. |推断，演绎|To reach a conclusion from something known or assumed.|例句：We can deduce from his behavior that he is guilty. 我们可以从他的行为推断出他有罪。|例句：Scientists deduced that the Earth revolves around the sun. 科学家们推断出地球绕着太阳转。|近义词：infer, conclude, reason, gather|反义词：guess, speculate, assume|词性:动词

deducing:/dɪˈd(j)uːsɪŋ/ /dɪˈduːsɪŋ/| v. |推断，演绎(现在分词)|The act of reasoning from something known or assumed to a conclusion.|例句：He is deducing the answer from the given information. 他正在根据给定的信息推断答案。|例句：She is deducing the killer's motive from the clues. 她正在根据线索推断凶手的动机。|近义词：inferring, concluding, reasoning, gathering|反义词：guessing, speculating, assuming|词性:动词(现在分词)
