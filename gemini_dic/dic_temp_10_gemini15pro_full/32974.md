choreatic:/ˌkɔːriˈætɪk/ | adj. | 舞蹈症的；舞蹈样的；不规则的 | Relating to or resembling chorea; characterized by involuntary jerky movements. |  Her choreatic movements made it difficult for her to perform everyday tasks. 她的舞蹈症动作使她难以完成日常工作。|  The patient exhibited choreatic twitching in his face and limbs. 病人的脸和四肢出现了舞蹈症样的抽搐。 | synonyms: jerky, spasmodic, involuntary, twitchy, uncontrolled | antonyms: controlled, smooth, steady, voluntary | adjective

choreic:/kɔːˈriːɪk/ | adj. | 舞蹈症的 | Affected with chorea. |  The choreic movements were a side effect of the medication. 舞蹈症的动作是药物的副作用。 | The doctor diagnosed the patient as choreic. 医生诊断病人患有舞蹈症。 | synonyms:  jerky, spasmodic, involuntary, twitchy, uncontrolled | antonyms: controlled, smooth, steady, voluntary | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *khoreía* (χορεία, “dance”) + *-ikos* (-ικος, adjectival suffix). Related to *khóros* (χόρος, “chorus, dance”).
