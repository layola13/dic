gerontes: /ɡɛˈrɔnˌtɛz/
n.
council of elders
A council of elders, especially in ancient Sparta.
The gerontes held significant power in Spartan society.  斯巴达长老会在社会中拥有重要的权力。
The decisions of the gerontes were highly respected.  长老会的决定受到高度尊重。
elders, old men
A group of elders or old men.
The gerontes gathered to discuss the future of the city.  长老们聚集在一起讨论这座城市的未来。
The gerontes offered their wisdom and guidance.  长老们提供了他们的智慧和指导。
近义词: elders, council, senate
反义词: youth, youngsters
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology: From Ancient Greek γέροντες (gérontes), plural of γέρων (gérōn, “old man”).
