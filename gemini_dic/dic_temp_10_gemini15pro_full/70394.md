fiftieth:/ˈfɪftiəθ/ (US), /ˈfɪftiɪθ/ (UK)| adj. n.|第五十, 第五十个|Representing the number 50 in a series.  It can function as both an adjective and a noun.|例句：This is her fiftieth birthday. 这是她的五十岁生日。|近义词：quinquagesimal|反义词: first|词性: adjective, noun

例句：She came in fiftieth place in the marathon. 她在马拉松比赛中获得了第五十名。
