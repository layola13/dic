anti-busing:/ˌæntiˈbʌsɪŋ/
adjective
反校车接送的
Opposed to the practice of busing students to schools outside their neighborhoods, especially as a means of achieving racial integration.
The anti-busing protests turned violent. 反校车接送的抗议演变成了暴力事件。
The anti-busing movement gained momentum in the 1970s.  反校车运动在20世纪70年代势头强劲。
synonyms: segregationist, discriminatory
antonyms: integrationist, pro-busing
词性: adjective
