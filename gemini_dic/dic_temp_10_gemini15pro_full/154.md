abashedly:/əˈbæʃɪdli/| adv. |害羞地，尴尬地|In a way that shows shame or embarrassment.|She abashedly admitted she had forgotten his birthday. 她不好意思地承认她忘记了他的生日。

He abashedly apologized for his clumsy remark.  他窘迫地为他笨拙的言辞道歉。|近义词：shamefacedly, sheepishly, shyly, embarrassedly|反义词：boldly, confidently, proudly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "abash," which comes from the Old French "esbahir" meaning "to astonish, amaze, bewilder."  The prefix "a-" intensifies the meaning, and "-ed" forms the past participle, which is then combined with "-ly" to create the adverb.

USAGE->
She abashedly accepted the award. = 她不好意思地接受了奖项。
He abashedly looked down at his feet. = 他难为情地低头看着自己的脚。
