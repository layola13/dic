heavenlier:/ˈhɛvənliər/ | adj. |更天堂般的，更神圣的|More heavenly; resembling or characteristic of heaven to a greater degree.|The music was even heavenlier than I remembered.  这音乐比我记忆中的更加天籁之音。|The view from the mountaintop was heavenlier than any picture could capture. 从山顶望去的景色比任何照片都能捕捉到的更加天堂般。|近义词：more divine, more celestial, more blissful|反义词：earthlier, more mundane, more worldly|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *hevenliker, comparative of hevenli (“heavenly”), equivalent to heavenly +‎ -er (comparative suffix).
