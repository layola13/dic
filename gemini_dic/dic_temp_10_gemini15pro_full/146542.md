overglide:/ˈoʊvərɡlaɪd/| n.|滑音;过渡音|A sound produced when the tongue moves from one vowel sound to another, creating a slight glide between the two sounds. It is often considered a type of diphthong.|例句：The overglide in the word "boy" is from /ɔɪ/.  单词"boy"中的滑音是从/ɔɪ/发出的。

例句：The singer's smooth overglides connected the notes beautifully. 歌手的平滑滑音优美地连接了音符。|近义词：glide, diphthong|反义词:monophthong|词性:noun
