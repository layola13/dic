barnlike:/ˈbɑːrnlaɪk/| adj.|像谷仓的；简陋的|Resembling a barn, especially in being large and plain.  Characterized by simplicity or plainness.|The living room was barnlike, with high ceilings and exposed beams.  客厅像谷仓一样，有很高的天花板和裸露的横梁。|近义词：spacious, cavernous, rustic, simple|反义词：small, cramped, ornate, elaborate|词性:形容词
