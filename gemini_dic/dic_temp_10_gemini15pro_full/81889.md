grievousnesses:/ˈɡriːvəsnəsɪz/
noun
严重性；悲痛；沉重
The state or quality of being grievous;  extreme seriousness or severity.
The grievousnesses of war are felt most acutely by civilians. 战争的沉重性对平民的影响最为严重。

The speaker enumerated the grievousnesses of the current economic situation. 演讲者列举了当前经济形势的严重性。

近义词:severity, gravity, seriousness, atrocity, hardship, affliction
反义词:mildness, leniency, triviality


词性:n. (noun)


词源: From grievous + -ness.  Grievous itself comes from Old French grevos, from grever "to burden, aggrieve," from Latin gravare "to burden," from gravis "heavy."
