overwet:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛt/ | adj. | 过湿的，过度潮湿的 | Excessively wet; saturated beyond a desirable or normal degree. | The soil was overwet, making it difficult to plant the seeds. 土壤过湿，难以播种。| The towel was overwet and dripped on the floor. 毛巾太湿了，滴到地板上了。 | 近义词：soaked, drenched, soggy, waterlogged | 反义词：dry, arid, parched | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "wet" (Old English wæt, from Proto-Germanic *wataz "wet")
