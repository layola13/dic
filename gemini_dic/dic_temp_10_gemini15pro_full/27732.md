caniest:/ˈkeɪniɪst/
adjective
最狡猾的,最精明的
most like a canine; most crafty or shrewd.
He's the caniest negotiator I've ever met. 他是我见过的最精明的谈判者。
近义词:  craftiest, shrewdest, wiliest, slickest
反义词: naive, gullible, ingenuous
词性: adjective

词源: From "canine" (dog-like), implying the shrewdness or cleverness often associated with canids.  The "-est" suffix indicates the superlative form.
