innumerably:/ɪˈnjuːmərəbli/
adv.
无数地，数不清地
Too numerous to be counted.  Existing in numbers too great to be counted.
The stars in the sky are innumerably many. 天空中的星星不计其数。
Near synonyms: countless, myriad, numerous, uncountable, untold, infinite
Antonyms: countable, few, limited, measurable, numbered, numerable, scanty, scarce
词性: 副词
