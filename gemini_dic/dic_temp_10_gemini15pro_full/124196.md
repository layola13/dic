moselle:/moʊˈzɛl/| n.|摩泽尔葡萄酒；摩泽尔河|A light, dry white wine produced in the Moselle valley, Germany.  Also refers to the Moselle River.|We enjoyed a bottle of crisp Moselle with our dinner. 我们晚餐喝了一瓶清爽的摩泽尔葡萄酒。|Synonyms: Riesling (often used for Moselle wines), white wine|Antonyms: red wine|noun


moselle:/moʊˈzɛl/| n.|摩泽尔河|A river that flows through France, Luxembourg, and Germany.|The Moselle River is known for its scenic beauty. 摩泽尔河以其优美的景色而闻名。|Synonyms: river, waterway|Antonyms: N/A|noun
