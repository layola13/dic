gateless:/ˈɡeɪtləs/| adj.|无门的，无栅栏的，开放的|Without a gate; open and unrestricted.|The gateless community fostered a sense of freedom and belonging.  这个没有大门的社区营造了一种自由和归属感。|The meditation retreat was held in a gateless monastery.  这个冥想静修会在一个没有大门的寺院举行。|近义词：open, unrestricted, accessible, free|反义词：gated, restricted, closed, inaccessible|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"gate" (n.) + "-less" (suffix)

"gate" comes from Old English "geat" meaning "opening, passage."

"-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English "-lēas."
