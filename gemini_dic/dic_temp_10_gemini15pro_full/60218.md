effluviate:/ˈɛfluːvieɪt/| v. |散发，发出（尤指气味或光）|To give off or emit a subtle or invisible emanation, as of light, warmth, or fragrance. |The flowers effluviated a sweet perfume. 这些花散发出一股甜美的香味。|emit, exude, radiate|absorb, contain|verb

effluvium:/ɪˈfluːviəm/| n. |散发物，（尤指难闻的）气味|An invisible or barely perceptible emanation, especially an offensive exhalation or smell. |The effluvium from the garbage dump was overpowering. 垃圾场的臭气令人难以忍受。|emanation, exhalation, odor|fragrance, perfume|noun
