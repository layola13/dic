elope: /ɪˈloʊp/ (American), /ɪˈləʊp/ (British)
verb
私奔；逃走
To run away secretly, especially to get married without the consent of one's parents.
例句：They eloped to Las Vegas and got married. 他们私奔到拉斯维加斯结婚了。

例句：The young couple eloped because their families disapproved of their relationship. 这对年轻情侣因为家人反对他们的关系而私奔了。

近义词：run away, abscond, flee
反义词：stay, remain

词性: verb
