grimacer:/ˈɡrɪməsər/ | n. | 做鬼脸的人；扮鬼脸的人 | A person who grimaces; someone who makes a facial expression of disgust, pain, or disapproval. |  He's such a grimacer when he eats broccoli.  他吃西兰花时总是做鬼脸。 |  scowler, frowner | smiler | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "grimacier," from "grimace" (grimace), from Old Provençal "grimaisa," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Frankish "*wrimp" (wrinkle).
