outreign:/ˌaʊtˈreɪn/
verb
统治比…更久; 比…活得长
to reign longer than; to live longer than
King Louis XIV outreigned all previous French monarchs. 路易十四在位时间超过了所有以前的法国君主。
She outreigned her husband by twenty years. 她比她丈夫多活了二十年。
近义词: outlive, survive
反义词: predecease
词性: verb
