hopeless:/ˈhəʊplɪs/ (UK) /ˈhoʊplɪs/ (US)| adj.|绝望的，无望的，无可救药的|Feeling or causing despair about the possibility of improvement or success.|例句：The situation seemed hopeless.  情况似乎毫无希望。|例句：She felt hopeless about her future. 她对自己的未来感到绝望。|近义词：desperate, despairing, incurable|反义词：hopeful, promising, optimistic|词性:形容词

hopelessness:/ˈhəʊplɪsnəs/ (UK) /ˈhoʊplɪsnəs/ (US)| n.|绝望，无望|The state of being without hope.|例句：A sense of hopelessness pervaded the community.  一种绝望的情绪弥漫着整个社区。|例句：He was consumed by hopelessness after losing his job.  失业后，他被绝望吞噬了。|近义词：despair, despondency|反义词：hope, optimism|词性:名词
