beaucoup:/boːkuː/| adv.|很多，大量|A French word adopted into English, meaning "a lot" or "much." It is used adverbially, not adjectivally.|We had beaucoup snow this winter. 今年冬天我们下了很多雪。|近义词：many, much, a lot of, plenty of, lots of|反义词:few, little|副词
