outglow:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊ/
verb
发出比...更强的光；比...更亮
to shine more brightly than something else
The setting sun outglowed the city lights. 落日比城市灯光更亮。
近义词: outshine, eclipse
反义词: be outshone by, be eclipsed by
词性: verb

词源:  out- + glow.  "Out-" prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing."  "Glow" meaning to give out steady light without flame.
