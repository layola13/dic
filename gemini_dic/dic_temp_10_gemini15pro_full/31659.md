chattering:/ˈtʃætərɪŋ/ | adj. n.|喋喋不休的，唠叨的；啁啾声，鸣叫声| characterized by rapid, repetitive sounds, like birds or people talking incessantly. Also, the act of making such sounds.| The chattering of the monkeys filled the jungle. 猴子们的啁啾声充满了整个丛林。|近义词:jabbering, babbling, twittering, chirping |反义词:silent, quiet |词性:adjective, noun

chatter:/ˈtʃætər/ | v. n.| 喋喋不休，唠叨；啁啾，鸣叫| to talk rapidly and incessantly about unimportant matters; to make rapid, repetitive sounds, like birds.|  The children chattered excitedly about their presents. 孩子们兴奋地谈论着他们的礼物。 | 近义词:jabber, babble, prattle, twitter, chirp | 反义词:be quiet, be silent |词性:verb, noun
