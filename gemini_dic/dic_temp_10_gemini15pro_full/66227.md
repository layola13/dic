excitinger: /ɪkˈsaɪtɪŋɡər/ | adj. (comparative) | 更令人兴奋的 | More exciting than something else.  |例句：This rollercoaster is excitinger than the last one we rode. 这个过山车比我们上次坐的更刺激。

|例句：The new movie is excitinger than the book. 这部新电影比书更精彩。|近义词：more thrilling, more stimulating, more captivating |反义词：less exciting, more boring, less stimulating | 词性: 形容词 (Comparative Adjective)

词源:  From "exciting" + "-er" (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "excitare" (to rouse, stir up).
