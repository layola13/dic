pandemoniacal: /ˌpændɪməˈnaɪək(ə)l/ | adj. |  喧闹的，混乱的；狂暴的；恶魔似的 |  Resembling or suggesting pandemonium; wildly chaotic or unruly.  Characterized by noise, disorder, and confusion, as if caused by demons. |  The pandemoniacal screams from the haunted house sent shivers down my spine.  从鬼屋里传来的恶魔般的尖叫声让我脊背发凉。

The party descended into a pandemoniacal free-for-all as the night wore on. 随着夜幕降临，派对沦为一场混乱不堪的混战。 | 近义词：chaotic, frenzied, tumultuous, wild, infernal, demonic | 反义词：peaceful, calm, orderly, serene, tranquil | 词性：adjective

Etymology: From Pandemonium (the capital of Hell in Milton's Paradise Lost) + -iacal (suffix forming adjectives meaning "of, relating to, or resembling").  Ultimately from Greek "pan-" (all) + "daimōn" (demon).
