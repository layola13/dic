legend: /ˈledʒənd/ (US) /ˈledʒənd/ (UK) | n. | 传说；传奇人物；图例 | A traditional story sometimes popularly regarded as historical but unauthenticated.  A famous or important person who is known for a particular skill or achievement. A caption or key accompanying an illustration or map. |  King Arthur is a legend in British history.  亚瑟王是英国历史上的一个传奇人物。 The map has a legend that explains the symbols. 地图上有一个图例解释各种符号。 | synonym: myth, fable, saga, icon, star | antonym: reality, truth | noun

legend: /ˈledʒənd/ (US) /ˈledʒənd/ (UK) | adj. (informal) |  非常有名的；极好的 |  Remarkably good or famous.  | That guitar solo was legendary! 那段吉他独奏真是太棒了！ He's a legend in his own lunchtime. 他在自己的圈子里可是个传奇人物。 | synonym: famous, celebrated, renowned, iconic | antonym: unknown, obscure | adjective
