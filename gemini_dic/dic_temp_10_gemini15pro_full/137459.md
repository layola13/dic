nonthreateningly:/nɒnˈθrɛtənɪŋli/ | adv. | 无威胁地；不构成威胁地 | In a way that does not cause alarm or fear. | He smiled nonthreateningly. 他露出了没有威胁的微笑。|  The dog approached nonthreateningly, tail wagging.  这只狗摇着尾巴，没有威胁地走近。 | 近义词: harmlessly, peacefully, safely, gently | 反义词: menacingly, aggressively, threateningly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "threatening" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Threatening" comes from the Old English word "þreatnian" meaning "to compel by threats."
