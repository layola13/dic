intercessory:/ˌɪntərˈsesəri/
adjective
代祷的，求情的
Of or relating to intercession; characterized by pleading on behalf of another.
The priest offered intercessory prayers for the sick.  神父为病人做了代祷。

The group held an intercessory service for peace.  这个团体举行了祈求和平的代祷仪式。
近义词: mediatory, mediating, pleading, supplicatory, interventional
反义词: accusatory
词性: adjective
