co-operated:/koʊˈɑːpəreɪtɪd/
verb
合作，协作
Worked together towards a common goal or purpose.
The two companies co-operated on the project.  两家公司在这个项目上合作。
We co-operated with the police in their investigation. 我们与警方合作进行调查。
近义词: collaborated, worked together, joined forces
反义词: competed, opposed, resisted
词性: verb
