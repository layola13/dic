antispiritual:/ˌæntiˈspɪrɪtʃuəl/
adj.
反精神的，反对精神上的，非精神的
Opposed to or hostile towards spiritual values or principles; lacking spirituality.
His antispiritual attitude made it difficult for him to connect with others on a deeper level. 他反精神的态度使他很难与别人进行更深层次的交流。
近义词: materialistic, worldly, secular, profane
反义词: spiritual, religious, devout, pious
词性: 形容词
