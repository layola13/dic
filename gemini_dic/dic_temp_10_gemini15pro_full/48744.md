defines:/dɪˈfaɪnz/ | v. | 定义，规定，确定 | Explains or identifies the essential qualities of something. | The dictionary defines "love" as an intense feeling of deep affection.  字典将“爱”定义为一种深厚的感情。 |  Synonyms: specifies, describes, determines, establishes | Antonyms: confuses, obscures | verb

defines:/dɪˈfaɪnz/ | v. |  限定，界定 |  Marks the limits or boundaries of something.  | The river defines the eastern border of the property.  这条河界定了这块地产的东部边界。 | Synonyms: delimits, outlines, circumscribes | Antonyms: expands, broadens | verb

詞源: From Old French *definer, from Latin dēfīnīre ("to limit, bound, determine, explain"), from dē ("from, down from, away from") + fīnīre ("to finish, limit, bound").

注意事项:  "Defines" is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "define."  It is crucial to use the correct tense and form of the verb depending on the context of the sentence.
