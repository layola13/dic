free-range:/ˌfriː ˈreɪndʒ/
adjective
自由放养的
(of livestock, especially poultry) kept in natural conditions, with freedom of movement.
例句：We buy free-range eggs. 我们买散养鸡蛋。

例句：Free-range chickens are generally healthier. 散养鸡通常更健康。

近义词：free-roaming, organic, natural
反义词：battery-farmed, caged, confined
词性: adjective
