adjust:/əˈdʒʌst/| vt. vi.|调整，调节；适应|To change something slightly, especially to make it more correct, effective, or suitable.  To become more familiar with a new situation.|She adjusted her glasses and smiled. 她调整了一下眼镜，笑了。

He adjusted quickly to the new environment. 他很快适应了新环境。|近义词：adapt, modify, regulate, alter, change|反义词：misalign, disarrange, upset|verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I adjust the thermostat every morning. = 我每天早上都要调整恒温器。
She adjusts her schedule to fit her client's needs. = 她调整自己的日程安排以适应客户的需求。


一般过去时:
He adjusted the volume on the radio. = 他调整了收音机的音量。
They adjusted their plans due to the bad weather. = 由于天气不好，他们调整了计划。


现在完成时:
We have adjusted the recipe to make it healthier. = 我们调整了食谱，使其更健康。
The company has adjusted its marketing strategy. = 公司调整了营销策略。


过去完成时:
After she had adjusted the settings, the picture became clearer. = 她调整设置后，画面变得更清晰了。
They had adjusted their budget before the project began. = 他们在项目开始前调整了预算。


将来时:
I will adjust the height of the chair later. = 我稍后会调整椅子的高度。
We will adjust our strategy as needed. = 我们将根据需要调整我们的策略。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would adjust my attitude. = 如果我是你，我会调整我的态度。
I wish I could adjust to the time difference more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地适应时差。


被动语态:
The settings were adjusted by the technician. = 设置由技术人员调整。
The temperature is adjusted automatically. = 温度自动调节。


疑问句:
Can you adjust the brightness of the screen? = 你能调整屏幕的亮度吗？
How do you adjust to living in a new country? = 你是如何适应在新国家生活的？


否定句:
I couldn't adjust the focus of the camera. = 我无法调整相机的焦距。
They didn't adjust their plans despite the warnings. = 尽管有警告，他们也没有调整计划。



PHRASE->
adjust to = 适应
adjust for = 调整…以适应…
adjust oneself to = 使自己适应于…
make adjustments = 做出调整
adjust the settings = 调整设置
