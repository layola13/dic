flashback:/ˈflæʃbæk/| n.|闪回；倒叙|A sudden and vivid memory of a past event, typically an emotional one.|例句：The film uses flashbacks to tell the story of the protagonist's childhood.  这部电影用倒叙的手法讲述了主人公的童年故事。

例句：She experienced a disturbing flashback to the night of the accident. 她突然清晰地回想起事故发生的那个夜晚，感到非常不安。|近义词：recollection, memory, reminiscence|反义词: - |词性:noun
