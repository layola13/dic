caducous:/kəˈduːkəs/
| adj. |脱落的，早落的|Falling off or shed prematurely, especially of leaves, petals, or fruit.|The oak tree's caducous leaves formed a colorful carpet beneath it. 橡树早落的叶子在树下形成了一块五彩的地毯。|Synonyms: deciduous, ephemeral, fugacious |Antonyms: persistent, evergreen|adjective
