natatory:/ˈneɪtətɔːri/
adjective
游泳的；适于游泳的
Relating to swimming or adapted for swimming.
The duck's natatory abilities are impressive.  鸭子的游泳能力令人印象深刻。
The frog has natatory webs between its toes. 青蛙的脚趾间有蹼，适于游泳。
近义词:swimming, aquatic
反义词:terrestrial
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *natatorius*, from natator (“swimmer”), from nato (“I swim”).
