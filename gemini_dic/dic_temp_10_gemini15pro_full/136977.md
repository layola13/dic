nonsphericalities:/ˌnɒnˌsfɪrɪˈkælɪtiz/ | n. | 非球形，非球面性 |  The deviations of a shape from perfect sphericity.  This term refers to the bumps, irregularities, or asymmetry of an object that prevent it from being perfectly round. | The nonsphericalities of the Earth affect its gravitational field. 地球的非球形影响其引力场。 |  asymmetries, irregularities, deviations from sphericity | sphericity, roundness | noun
