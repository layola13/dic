badian:/ˈbeɪdiən/ | n. | 八角，大茴香 | Star anise, a spice made from the fruit of the Illicium verum plant, native to northeast Vietnam and southwest China. It is used in Asian cuisine and has a strong, licorice-like flavor. |  The recipe calls for a whole badian.  这道菜需要一整颗八角。 | Synonyms: star anise, Chinese anise | Antonyms: N/A | noun
