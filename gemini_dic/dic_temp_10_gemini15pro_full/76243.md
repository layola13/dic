futurition:/ˌfjuːtəˈrɪʃən/ | n. | 未来化，未来主义，未来展望 | The act or process of imagining or planning for the future; a futuristic vision or depiction.  The development or depiction of something in a futuristic style or manner. |  His paintings offer a bleak futurition of urban decay. 他的画作描绘了一幅城市衰败的惨淡未来景象。 | 近义词：futurism, future, foresight, prediction | 反义词：past, history, nostalgia | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From Latin *futuritio*, from futurus (future participle of esse "to be") + -tio (suffix forming nouns of action).
