dumpier:/ˈdʌmpiər/ | adj. | 更矮胖的，更粗短的 |Comparative form of "dumpy": shorter and thicker than average; stocky. | The snowman this year is dumpier than last year's. 今年的雪人比去年的更矮胖。| The new building is dumpier and less elegant than the old one. 新建筑比旧建筑更矮胖，不那么优雅。 | 近义词: stockier, squatter, chubbier | 反义词: taller, thinner, slenderer | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "dumpy" + "-er" (comparative suffix).  "Dumpy" likely originates from a combination of "dump" (referring to a short, rounded heap) and a suffix like "-y" or "-ie."
