hectorly:/ˈhɛktərli/| adv.|专横地；欺凌地；威吓地|In a bullying or domineering way, like the Trojan warrior Hector.|He spoke to his employees hectorly, demanding immediate obedience. 他专横地对他的员工说话，要求立即服从。|例句：The landlord hectorly demanded the overdue rent. 房东蛮横地索要逾期未付的租金。

例句：She hectorly dismissed his concerns, refusing to listen to his explanation. 她蛮横地驳回了他的担忧，拒绝听他的解释。|近义词：bullyingly, domineeringly, overbearingly, imperiously|反义词: meekly, humbly, gently|词性:副词
