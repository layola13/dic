busybodies:/ˈbɪziˌbɒdiz/ (US) /ˈbɪziˌbɒdɪz/ (UK)| n.|爱管闲事的人；好事者|A person who is too interested in the private lives of other people. |例句：The neighborhood busybodies were always gossiping about everyone's business. 邻居里的那些爱管闲事的人总是对每个人的私事说三道四。

例句：He was tired of the busybodies interfering in his life. 他厌倦了那些爱管闲事的人干涉他的生活。|近义词：gossips, meddlers, interferers|反义词:mind their own business person|词性:Noun
