nonunitarians:/ˌnɒnjuːnɪˈtɛəriənz/
|n.|非一神论者；不相信三位一体的人|People who do not believe in the doctrine of the Trinity, which states that God is one being in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.|The nonunitarians formed a separate congregation. 非一神论者们组成了一个独立的教会。|
| | |Synonyms: antitrinitarians, dissenters|Antonyms: trinitarians|noun|

nonunitarian:/ˌnɒnjuːnɪˈtɛəriən/
|adj.|非一神论的；不相信三位一体的|Relating to or characteristic of nonunitarians.|They held a nonunitarian view of God.  他们持有非一神论的上帝观。|
| | |Synonyms: antitrinitarian|Antonyms: trinitarian|adjective|
