digestional:/dɪˈdʒɛstʃənl/ /daɪˈdʒɛstʃənl/
adjective|消化性的；助消化的|Relating to digestion; promoting digestion.|The digestive system plays a crucial role in breaking down food. 消化系统在分解食物方面起着至关重要的作用。|The doctor recommended digestive enzymes to aid with his condition. 医生建议服用消化酶来帮助他的病情。|近义词: digestive, gastric|反义词: indigestible|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *digestional, *digestiounal, from Anglo-Norman digestionel, digestival, and Old French digestable, from Late Latin dīgestīvus (“serving for digestion”), from Latin dīgestus, past participle of dīgerō (“digest, distribute, dissolve”). Surface analysis is digest +‎ -ional.
