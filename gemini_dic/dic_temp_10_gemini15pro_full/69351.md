federalness:/ˈfɛdərəlnɪs/
n.
联邦性；联邦制原则
The quality or state of being federal.  The principle or system of federal government.
The federalness of the United States allows for both national unity and state autonomy. 美国的联邦性允许国家统一和州自治。
The debate focused on the federalness of the proposed healthcare system.  辩论的焦点是拟议的医疗保健系统的联邦性。
近义词: federalism, federation
反义词: centralism, unitary system
词性: noun

Etymology:
From federal + -ness.  "Federal" ultimately derives from the Latin "foedus" (league, treaty, covenant).
