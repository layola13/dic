downgrading:/ˌdaʊnˈɡreɪdɪŋ/
noun|降级，降低等级|The downgrading of the company's credit rating caused concern among investors. 该公司信用评级的下调引起了投资者的担忧。|The software is undergoing a downgrading to an earlier version.  该软件正在被降级到早期版本。|reduction, devaluation|upgrade, promotion|n.

downgrade:/ˌdaʊnˈɡreɪd/
verb|降低…的等级，使降级|They downgraded the security risk from high to medium. 他们将安全风险从高降至中等。|The airline downgraded me from business class to economy. 航空公司将我的机票从商务舱降级为经济舱。|reduce, demote|upgrade, promote|v.

downgraded:/ˌdaʊnˈɡreɪdɪd/
adjective|降级的|The downgraded version of the software lacks some features. 降级版软件缺少某些功能。|He felt downgraded after being transferred to a smaller office.  被调到较小的办公室后，他感到被降级了。|reduced, demoted|upgraded, promoted|adj.
