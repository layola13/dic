adjunctive:/əˈdʒʌŋktɪv/
adjective
附加的，辅助的
Serving as a supplement or addition; auxiliary.
The doctor prescribed an adjunctive therapy to help alleviate the patient's pain. 医生开了辅助疗法来帮助减轻病人的疼痛。

She was taking adjunctive medication to help with her anxiety. 她正在服用辅助药物来帮助治疗她的焦虑。

近义词: supplementary, auxiliary, additional, complementary
反义词: primary, main, principal
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adjunctivus, from Latin adjunctus (past participle of adjungere ‘to join to’), from ad- ‘to’ + jungere ‘to join’.

USAGE->
Adjunctive therapy is often used in conjunction with the primary treatment. 辅助治疗通常与主要治疗一起使用。
Adjunctive medications can help to reduce the side effects of the main drug. 辅助药物可以帮助减少主要药物的副作用。

The adjunctive staff provided valuable support to the main team.  辅助人员为主要团队提供了宝贵的支持。
The report provided adjunctive information that helped to clarify the situation. 报告提供了有助于澄清情况的补充信息。


PHRASE->
