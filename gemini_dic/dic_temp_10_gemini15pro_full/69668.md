fencings:/ˈfɛnsɪŋz/
n.
击剑，剑术；围栏，栅栏
The sport or activity of fighting with swords, especially foils, épées, or sabres, according to a set of rules.  Also, fences or a fence, especially one consisting of a barrier of posts and wire or boards.
He competed in several fencings tournaments.  他参加了几次击剑比赛。
The property was surrounded by high fencings.  这处房产周围环绕着高高的围栏。
近义词：swordsmanship, fencing (singular), barriers, enclosures
反义词：openness (for barriers)
