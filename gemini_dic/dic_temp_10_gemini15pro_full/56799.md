doree:/dɔːˈreɪ/ | adj. n. |金色的，镀金的；金鱼 |A golden color; a goldfish, especially a golden orfe. |The doree fish shimmered in the pond.  金鱼在池塘里闪闪发光。|近义词：golden, gilded, gold, goldfish, orfe |反义词:silver, dull |词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: From French *doré*, past participle of *dorer* "to gild," from Old French *dorer*, from Late Latin *deaurare* "to gild," from Latin *de-* "from" + *aurum* "gold."
