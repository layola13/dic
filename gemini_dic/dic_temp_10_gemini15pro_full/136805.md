nonshatter:/ˌnɒnˈʃætər/ | adj. | 防碎的，不易碎的 | Designed to resist shattering; made of material that will not break into fragments. |  The car's nonshatter windshield prevented serious injury in the accident. 汽车的防碎挡风玻璃在事故中防止了严重的伤害。| Synonyms: shatterproof, shatter-resistant | Antonyms: shatterable, fragile | adjective

Etymology:  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "shatter" refers to breaking into pieces.  Therefore, "nonshatter" describes something that does not shatter.  The word "shatter" comes from the Old English word "scateran" meaning "to scatter."
