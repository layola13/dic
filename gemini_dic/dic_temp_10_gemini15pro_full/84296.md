half-vellum:/ˌhɑːfˈvɛləm/ | n. | 半羊皮纸（一种装订材料）| A bookbinding material consisting of a strong paper backing covered with a thin layer of vellum. | The limited edition was bound in half-vellum.  限量版用半羊皮纸装订。| synonyms: half-calf (sometimes), quarter-bound | antonyms: full-vellum, full-leather | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"half" + "vellum".  "Half" indicates that only a portion of the binding is vellum, typically the spine and corners, while the rest is usually a more durable and less expensive material like cloth or paper. "Vellum" comes from Old French "velin," meaning "calfskin."
