choky:/ˈtʃəʊki/ (UK) /ˈtʃoʊki/ (US)| adj.|令人窒息的，哽咽的，堵塞的|Causing or likely to cause choking; feeling as if you are choking.  Having a tight feeling in your throat because of strong emotion.|例句：The smoke was thick and choky. 烟雾很浓，令人窒息。

例句：I felt a choky feeling in my throat when I said goodbye.  当我道别时，我的喉咙感到一阵哽咽。|近义词：suffocating, stifling, constricting, tight|反义词：clear, open, airy|词性:形容词
