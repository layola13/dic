agamous:/ˈæɡəməs/| adj.|无性的，无配偶的|Reproducing asexually; having no sex organs.  Not involving fusion of gametes.|The agamous reproduction of some plants allows them to spread quickly.  某些植物的无性繁殖使它们能够迅速蔓延。|Synonyms: asexual, nonsexual, vegetative|Antonyms: sexual, gamous|adjective

agamous:/ˈæɡəməs/| adj.|无融合生殖的|Relating to reproduction without the fusion of gametes.|Agamous reproduction is common in lower organisms. 低等生物中无融合生殖很常见。|Synonyms: apomictic, parthenogenetic|Antonyms: sexual, gamous|adjective
