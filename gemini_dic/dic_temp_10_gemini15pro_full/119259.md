michelin: /ˈmɪʃəlɪn/ (US), /ˈmɪʃəlɪn/ (UK)| n. adj.| 米其林 | A French tire company famous for its tires and its restaurant rating system (stars). Also used as an adjective to describe things related to the Michelin company, particularly the restaurant guide.|  The restaurant has been awarded a Michelin star. 这家餐厅被授予了一颗米其林星。| synonyms: (for tire) tyre, radial, (for guide) guidebook, directory | antonyms: (no direct antonym, could be something like "low-quality" in context of the restaurant ratings) | noun, adjective
