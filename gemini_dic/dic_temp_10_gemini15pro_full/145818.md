overdefensive:/ˌoʊvərdiˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 过于防御的，过分自卫的 | Excessively defensive; too quick to defend oneself, often in a way that seems disproportionate to the threat or criticism received.  Characterized by an excessive or exaggerated tendency to protect oneself from perceived attack, criticism, or threat. |  He became overdefensive when asked about his spending habits. 他在被问及消费习惯时变得过分 defensive。|  She was so overdefensive about her work that she couldn't take any constructive criticism. 她对自己的工作过于 defensive，以至于无法接受任何建设性的批评。 | synonyms: touchy, sensitive, thin-skinned, insecure, guarded | antonyms: open, receptive, confident, secure | adjective
