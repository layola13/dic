globalists:/ˈɡloʊbəlɪsts/ | n. | 全球主义者；世界主义者 | People who advocate for globalism, the idea of increased interconnectedness and interdependence among nations through free trade, open borders, and international cooperation. |  Many globalists believe that international cooperation is essential to address global challenges. 许多全球主义者认为，国际合作对于应对全球挑战至关重要。

| 近义词: cosmopolitans, internationalists, one-worlders (often used pejoratively) | 反义词: nationalists, isolationists | noun
