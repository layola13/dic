orgy:/ˈɔːrdʒi/ (US) /ˈɔːɡi/ (UK)| n. |狂欢；纵欲；放纵|An unrestrained indulgence in a particular activity, especially one involving excessive eating, drinking, or sexual activity. |例句：The Roman emperor was known for his lavish orgies.  这位罗马皇帝以其奢华的狂欢而闻名。

例句：The weekend was an orgy of shopping and eating out.  这个周末尽是购物和外出就餐的放纵。

近义词：spree, binge, debauchery, bacchanalia|反义词: abstinence, moderation|词性:noun
