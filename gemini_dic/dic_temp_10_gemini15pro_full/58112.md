dropouts:/ˈdrɑːpaʊts/ (US) /ˈdrɒpaʊts/ (UK)| n.|辍学者；退学者；中途退学的人|People who withdraw from a course of study, a school, etc. before completion.|Many high school dropouts later regret their decision. 许多高中辍学者后来后悔他们的决定。|The program aims to help dropouts get their GED. 这个项目旨在帮助辍学者获得他们的GED(普通教育发展证书)。|近义词：non-graduates, withdrawals|反义词: graduates|词性:名词
