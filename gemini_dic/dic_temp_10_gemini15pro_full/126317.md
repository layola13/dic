myomorpha:/maɪˈɔːmɔːrfə/ | n. | 鼠形亚目 | A suborder of rodents that includes mice, rats, hamsters, gerbils, and voles.  This group is characterized by their infraorbital foramen structure. | The house mouse belongs to the Myomorpha suborder.  家鼠属于鼠形亚目。| Synonyms: Muroidea (sometimes used interchangeably, though technically a superfamily) | Antonyms: None (taxonomic groups don't usually have antonyms) | noun
