commences:/kəˈmɛnsɪz/
verb
开始，着手
to begin, to start
The ceremony commences at noon.  典礼将于中午开始。
The academic year commences in September. 学年从九月开始。
近义词:begin, start, initiate, inaugurate
反义词:end, finish, conclude, terminate
词性:v.
