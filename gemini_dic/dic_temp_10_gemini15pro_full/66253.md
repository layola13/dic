excluding:/ɪksˈkluːdɪŋ/ | prep.|不包括，排除|excluding tax 不包括税|例句：All employees, excluding senior management, will receive a bonus. 所有员工，不包括高级管理人员，都将获得奖金。

例句：The price is $10 excluding shipping. 价格是10美元，不包括运费。

近义词：except, except for, barring, but, without|反义词：including, inclusive of|词性：介词

词源：Late Middle English: present participle of exclude, from Latin excludere ‘shut out’, from ex- ‘out’ + claudere ‘to shut’.
