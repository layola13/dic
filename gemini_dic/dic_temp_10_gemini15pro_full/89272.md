homocercal:/ˌhɒməˈsɜːkəl/| adj.|同尾的，正尾的|Having a tail with the upper and lower lobes approximately equal, as in most bony fishes.|The trout is a homocercal fish. 鳟鱼是一种正尾鱼。|symmetrical, externally symmetrical|heterocercal|adjective
