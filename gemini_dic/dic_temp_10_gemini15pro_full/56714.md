doomster:/ˈduːmstər/| n. |厄运预言者，悲观主义者|A person who predicts doom or disaster; a pessimist.|The doomster warned of impending economic collapse. 厄运预言者警告即将发生的经济崩溃。|近义词：pessimist, prophet of doom, Cassandra|反义词：optimist, Pollyanna|词性:名词

Etymology:
From doom + -ster (a suffix forming agent nouns).  Doom comes from Old English *dōm meaning "judgment, law, statute; condition, state; destiny, fate."
