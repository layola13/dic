anthropopathical:/ˌænθrəpəˈpæθɪkəl/
adjective
拟人化的；赋予人性的
Attributing human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.
例句：The cartoon gives the animals anthropopathical qualities, like the ability to speak and wear clothes.  这部动画片赋予动物们拟人化的特质，比如说话和穿衣服的能力。

例句：It's anthropopathical to think that a dog feels guilt the way a human does.  认为狗会像人类一样感到内疚是一种拟人化的想法。

近义词：anthropomorphic, personified
反义词： (No direct antonym, perhaps 'objective,' 'scientific')
词性：adjective
