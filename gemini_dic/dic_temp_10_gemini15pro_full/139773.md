octamerous:/ɒkˈtæmərəs/
adjective
八基数的；八部的
Having eight parts, especially petals or sepals.
The octamerous flower has eight petals. 这朵八基数的花有八片花瓣。
The plant has octamerous flowers.  这种植物开八基数的花。
Synonyms: eight-part, eight-membered
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, would depend on specific number of parts, e.g., pentamerous, hexamerous)
词性: adjective
