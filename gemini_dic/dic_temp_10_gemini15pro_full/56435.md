dollop:/ˈdɒləp/ (British) /ˈdɑːləp/ (American)| n.|一小团，一小块，一小堆|A small amount of a soft or sticky substance. |She added a dollop of whipped cream to her hot chocolate. 她往热巧克力里加了一小团鲜奶油。

dollop:| n.|（尤指食物的）一小团，一小块|A small shapeless mass of something, especially soft food. |A dollop of sour cream. 一小块酸奶油。


近义词:blob, lump, spoonful, dab, bit, drop
反义词:heap, pile, mound
词性:noun
