alethiology:/əˌliːθiˈɒlədʒi/
n.
真理学; 真理的理论
The study of truth.  It encompasses the philosophical study of the nature of truth, how it can be known, and its relationship to knowledge, language, and belief.
Alethiology explores questions like "What is truth?" and "How can we know if something is true?"
近义词:epistemology, philosophy of truth
反义词: (No direct antonym.  One might consider "the study of falsehood" but there's no established term.)
词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *ἀλήθεια* (alétheia, “truth”) + *-λογία* (-logía, “study of”).
