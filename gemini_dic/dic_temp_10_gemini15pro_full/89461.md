honesty:/ˈɑːnɪsti/ | n. |诚实，正直|The quality of being honest.  Being truthful and having strong moral principles.| She valued honesty above all else. 她把诚实看得比什么都重要。|integrity, truthfulness, sincerity, probity | dishonesty, deceit, deception | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English honeste, honestee, from Old French honeste, honesté (“honesty, honor, decency, courtesy”), from Latin honestās (“honor, respectability”), from honestus (“honorable, respected”), from honos (“honor, reputation, office”).
