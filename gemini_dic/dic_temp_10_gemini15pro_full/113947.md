magazinish:/ˌmæɡəˈziːnɪʃ/
adjective
类似杂志的；杂志风格的
Resembling or characteristic of a magazine, especially in being superficial or stylish.
Her apartment had a very magazinish look, all sleek furniture and carefully arranged accessories. 她的公寓看起来非常像杂志上的，全是时尚的家具和精心布置的装饰品。
近义词:glossy, stylish, trendy
反义词:unstylish, dowdy, drab
词性:adjective

magazinish:/ˌmæɡəˈziːnɪʃ/
adjective
杂志化的
presented or adapted in the manner or style of a magazine.
The report was given a magazinish makeover to appeal to a wider audience.  这份报告被改造成杂志风格以吸引更广泛的读者。
近义词:popularized, commercialized
反义词:academic, scholarly
词性:adjective


词源: magazine + -ish.  "magazine" 源自法语 magasin，意为仓库，后引申为期刊。"-ish"是一个后缀，表示“有点像”，“带有...性质的”。
