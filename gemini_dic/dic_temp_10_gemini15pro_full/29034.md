cartoonishness:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪʃnəs/
noun
卡通化；卡通式；漫画式
The quality of resembling or suggesting a cartoon, often in an exaggerated or simplified way.  The state of being cartoonish.
Her cartoonishness makes her stand out in a crowd. 她的卡通式风格让她在人群中脱颖而出。
The cartoonishness of the movie's villains made them seem less threatening.  电影中反派的卡通化使得他们看起来不那么具有威胁性。
synonyms: exaggeration, caricature, simplification, absurdity
antonyms: realism, naturalism, verisimilitude
