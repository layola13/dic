available:/əˈveɪləbəl/| adj.|可获得的；可用的；有空的|Ready for use, at hand.  Able to be used or obtained; not busy.|例句：Are there any tickets available?  还有票吗？|近义词：accessible, obtainable, free, usable|反义词:unavailable, inaccessible, busy, occupied|词性:adjective
Etymology: From late Middle English *avaylable*, from Old French *available* "advantageous, profitable," from *availer* "to be of use, profit, help," ultimately from Latin *valēre* "to be strong, be well."
