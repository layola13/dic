logrollings:/ˈlɔːɡˌroʊlɪŋz/
noun
互相吹捧；相互支持；交换投票
The practice of exchanging favors, especially in politics, by reciprocal voting for each other's proposed legislation.
The politicians engaged in logrollings to ensure their bills were passed. 这些政客们互相吹捧以确保他们的法案获得通过。
logrolling, back-scratching, quid pro quo
opposition, antagonism
n.
