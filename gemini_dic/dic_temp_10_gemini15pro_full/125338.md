multilaterally:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəli/ | adv. | 多边地，多方地 | Involving or participated in by more than two nations or parties. | The issue was discussed multilaterally by the member states.  这个问题由成员国多边讨论。|Synonym: jointly, collectively, together | Antonym: unilaterally, bilaterally | adverb

multilateral:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəl/ | adj. | 多边的，多方的 | Having or involving more than two sides, nations, or parties. | A multilateral trade agreement was signed.  一项多边贸易协定签署了。| Synonym: multinational, polycentric | Antonym: unilateral, bilateral | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + lateral (sided).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "Lateral" comes from Latin "lateralis" meaning "of or belonging to the side".
