adopting: /əˈdɒptɪŋ/ (UK) /əˈdɑːptɪŋ/ (US)| v.|采用，采纳；收养|The process of beginning to use or follow something new, or of formally accepting someone into a family.|例句：They are adopting a new approach to teaching. 他们正在采用一种新的教学方法。

Many couples are adopting children from overseas. 许多夫妇正在从海外收养孩子。

|近义词：embracing, accepting, taking on, fostering (for children)|反义词:rejecting, discarding, abandoning|词性: verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *adopter*, from Latin *adoptare* "to choose for oneself, take by choice as one's own child," from *ad- "to" + *optare* "to choose, wish, select."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is adopting a new marketing strategy. = 公司正在采用新的营销策略。
He is adopting a healthier lifestyle. = 他正在采用更健康的生活方式。


一般过去时:
They adopted the proposal without any changes. = 他们未作任何修改就采纳了这项提议。
She adopted a stray dog last year. = 她去年收养了一只流浪狗。


现在完成时:
The school has adopted a new uniform policy. = 学校已经采用了新的校服政策。
They have adopted two children from China. = 他们已经从中国收养了两个孩子。


过去完成时:
By the time he arrived, the committee had adopted the resolution. = 到他到达时，委员会已经通过了这项决议。
She had adopted a vegetarian diet before she met him. = 在遇到他之前，她就已经开始吃素了。


将来时:
We will be adopting new software next month. = 我们下个月将采用新的软件。
They will be adopting a baby girl in the spring. = 他们将在春天收养一个女婴。


虚拟语气:
If I were adopting a child, I would adopt a baby. = 如果我要收养一个孩子，我会收养一个婴儿。
I wish they would consider adopting a more flexible approach. = 我希望他们考虑采用更灵活的方法。


被动语态:
A new policy is being adopted by the company. = 公司正在采用一项新政策。
The abandoned kitten was adopted by a loving family. = 被遗弃的小猫被一个充满爱的家庭收养了。


疑问句:
Are they adopting a new dog? = 他们要收养一只新狗吗？
Why are they adopting this strategy? = 他们为什么要采用这种策略？


否定句:
They are not adopting any new policies this year. = 他们今年不打算采用任何新政策。
She did not adopt the child because she wasn't ready. = 她没有收养那个孩子，因为她还没有准备好。
