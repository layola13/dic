codependent:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənt/| adj. n.|相互依赖的；共依存的；共依赖者|Characterized by excessive emotional or psychological reliance on a partner, typically one who requires support due to an illness or addiction.  A relationship where one person enables another person's addiction or unhealthy behavior.|例句：She was in a codependent relationship with her alcoholic husband. 她和她酗酒的丈夫处于一种相互依赖的关系。

例句：His codependent behavior stemmed from his childhood experiences. 他的共依赖行为源于他的童年经历。|近义词：interdependent, mutually dependent, enabling|反义词：independent, autonomous|词性：adjective, noun

codependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|相互依赖；共依存|The state of being codependent.|例句：Codependency can be a destructive force in relationships. 共依存在人际关系中可能是一种破坏性的力量。

例句：Therapy helped her overcome her codependency. 治疗帮助她克服了共依存。|近义词：interdependence, mutual dependence|反义词：independence, autonomy|词性：noun
