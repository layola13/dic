antimodernness:/ˌæntiˈmɑːdərnəs/ | n. |反现代性，反现代主义 |The state of being opposed to or rejecting modern trends, ideas, or values.  It encompasses a preference for traditional or pre-modern ways of life, thinking, and aesthetics. |His antimodernness was evident in his rejection of technology and his embrace of traditional craftsmanship. 他的反现代性体现在他对科技的排斥和对传统工艺的拥护。|Synonyms: traditionalism, conservatism, primitivism |Antonyms: modernism, contemporaneity |noun
