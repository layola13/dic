fictions:/ˈfɪkʃənz/
n.
小说，虚构的事物
literary works based on the imagination and not necessarily on fact
Example:  She writes children's fictions. 她写儿童小说。

Example: His account of the journey is a complete fiction. 他对这次旅行的描述纯属虚构。
近义词:fabrications, inventions, lies, stories, untruths
反义词:facts, realities, truths
词性:noun


fiction:/ˈfɪkʃən/
n.
小说，虚构的事物
something feigned, invented, or imagined
Example: Science fiction often explores the possibilities of future technology. 科幻小说经常探索未来技术的可能性。

Example:  It's a legal fiction that a corporation is a person. 将公司视为一个人是一个法律上的虚构。
近义词:fabrication, invention, lie, story, untruth
反义词:fact, reality, truth
词性:noun
