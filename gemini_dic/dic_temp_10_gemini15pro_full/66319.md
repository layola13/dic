excrete:/ɪkˈskriːt/ /ɛkˈskriːt/| v. |排泄，分泌|To separate and eliminate from an organic body; to discharge (waste matter, especially urine and feces) from the body. |The kidneys excrete waste products from the blood. 肾脏排泄血液中的废物。

The glands in the skin excrete sweat. 皮肤上的腺体分泌汗液。


|近义词：discharge, eliminate, expel, void|反义词: absorb, ingest|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin excretus, past participle of excernere (“to sift out, separate”), from ex- (“out”) + cernere (“to distinguish, separate, sift”).
