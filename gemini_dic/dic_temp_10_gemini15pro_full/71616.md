flatcar:/ˈflætˌkɑːr/ (US), /ˈflætˌkɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |平板车，平车|A railroad freight car with a flat deck and no sides or roof, used for transporting large or bulky items.|The heavy machinery was loaded onto a flatcar for transport. 重型机械被装上平板车运输。|Synonyms:  railcar, gondola (when referring to a low-sided flatcar)|Antonyms: boxcar, hopper car|noun
