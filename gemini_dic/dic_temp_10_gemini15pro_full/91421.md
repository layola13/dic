hyoid:/ˈhaɪɔɪd/| adj. n.|舌骨的，舌骨|Relating to or associated with the hyoid bone.  A U-shaped bone at the base of the tongue that supports the tongue and its muscles.|The hyoid bone is crucial for swallowing. 舌骨对吞咽至关重要。|The doctor examined the patient's hyoid area. 医生检查了病人的舌骨区域。|近义词：lingual (related to the tongue), glossal (related to the tongue)|反义词: None (There's no direct opposite of "hyoid")|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hyoeidēs*  meaning "shaped like the letter upsilon (υ)," from *hyoeid*-,  *hyoeides* "shaped like the letter upsilon" + *-oeidēs* "-oid."  The Greek letter upsilon (υ) resembled the shape of the hyoid bone.
