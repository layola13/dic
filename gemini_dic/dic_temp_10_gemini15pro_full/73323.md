foreday:/ˈfɔːrdeɪ/ | n. |黎明，破晓|The time just before dawn; daybreak. |We woke before foreday to start our journey. 我们在黎明前醒来开始我们的旅程。|近义词：dawn, daybreak, sunrise |反义词:sunset, nightfall |词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *foreday*, from Old English *foredæg* ("preceding day, eve, vigil"), from *fore* ("before") + *dæg* ("day"). Cognate with Dutch *voor-dag* ("dawn"), German *Vortag* ("previous day").
