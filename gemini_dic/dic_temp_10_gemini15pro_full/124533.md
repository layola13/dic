ETYMOLOGY->
From mountainous + -ness.  "Mountainous" comes from Late Middle English *mountaynous*, from Old French *montagneus*, ultimately from Latin *montanus* "of a mountain," from *mons* (genitive *montis*) "mountain."  The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
