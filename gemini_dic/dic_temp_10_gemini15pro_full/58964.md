duststorms:/ˈdʌstˌstɔːrmz/ (US), /ˈdʌstˌstɔːmz/ (UK)
n.
沙尘暴
A severe windstorm that raises clouds of dust, especially in a desert.
Duststorms are common in arid and semi-arid regions. 沙尘暴在干旱和半干旱地区很常见。
The duststorms made it difficult to see. 沙尘暴使得很难看清东西。
sandstorms, haboobs
(无明显的反义词，可以考虑 clear skies 晴朗的天空)
