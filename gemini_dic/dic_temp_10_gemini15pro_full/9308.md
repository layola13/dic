antipleasures:/ˈæntiˌpleʒərz/
noun
不快乐，不愉快；令人厌恶的事物
Things that do not give pleasure, but rather displeasure or discomfort.
The antipleasures of a long commute include traffic jams and crowded trains.  长时间通勤的不愉快包括交通堵塞和拥挤的火车。
Anticipating the antipleasures of root canal work made me anxious. 预想根管治疗的痛苦让我很焦虑。
Synonyms: displeasures, discomforts, annoyances, irritations
Antonyms: pleasures, joys, delights, comforts
