intermise:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪz/
verb
插入；介入；干预
To insert or introduce between; to interject.
The lawyer intermised a question during the witness's testimony. 律师在证人作证时插话提问。

verb
调停；斡旋
To mediate or intervene between parties.
The ambassador intermised in the conflict between the two nations. 大使在两国冲突之间进行斡旋。


noun
插入；介入；干预
Interposition; intervention; interjection.
The king's intermise prevented further bloodshed. 国王的介入阻止了进一步的流血事件。

noun
插入语；插入物
Something interjected or interposed; an interposed remark or action.
His humorous intermise lightened the tense atmosphere. 他幽默的插话缓和了紧张的气氛。

近义词:intervene, mediate, interject, insert, interpose
反义词:ignore, neglect, withdraw, abstain

词性: verb, noun
