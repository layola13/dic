erubescency:/ˌɛrʊˈbɛsənsɪ/ /ˌɪrʊˈbɛsənsɪ/
n.
脸红；羞愧
The act of blushing; a blush.
例句：Her erubescency betrayed her embarrassment. 她的脸红暴露了她的尴尬。

例句：He felt a wave of erubescency wash over him as he realized his mistake.  当他意识到自己的错误时，他感到一阵脸红涌上心头。

近义词：blush, reddening, flushing
反义词：pallor, paleness

词性: noun

词源: From Latin *erubescere, from e- + rubescere "to grow red," from ruber "red."
