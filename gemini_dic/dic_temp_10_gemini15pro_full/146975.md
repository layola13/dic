overjoyfully: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪfəli/ | adv. | 非常高兴地，欣喜若狂地 |  In a manner expressing extreme joy; with overwhelming happiness. | She laughed overjoyfully when she heard the good news. 她听到这个好消息后欣喜若狂地大笑起来。 |  happily, joyfully, elatedly, blissfully | sadly, sorrowfully | adverb

Etymology:
From "over-" (meaning excessively) + "joyful" (full of joy) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Joyful" ultimately comes from Old French "joie" (joy), which is derived from Latin "gaudia" (joys), plural of "gaudium" (joy).
