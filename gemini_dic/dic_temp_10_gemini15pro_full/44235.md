crocodiling:/ˈkrɒkəˌdaɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 鳄鱼式游泳; 鳄鱼式晒太阳 |  The act of swimming or sunbathing like a crocodile, with the body partially submerged and the limbs spread out. | He was crocodiling by the pool, soaking up the sun. 他像鳄鱼一样趴在泳池边晒太阳。 |  basking, lounging, sunbathing | antonyms:  swimming actively, exercising | noun
