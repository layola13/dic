gamba:/ˈɡæmbə/ | n. |  腿 (尤指动物的腿);  赌博;  冒险; |  From Italian *gamba* "leg," ultimately from Late Latin *gamba*, of uncertain origin, possibly from Greek *kampē* "bend, curve." It refers to the leg, especially of an animal, and can also be used figuratively to mean a gamble or risk.  | The dog hurt its gamba. 狗的腿受伤了。|  leg, limb | torso, body | noun

gamba:/ˈɡæmbə/ | n. |  (音乐) 古提琴; |  From Italian *gamba*, "leg," because of the way the instrument is held between the legs.  | He plays the viola da gamba. 他演奏古提琴。|  viol |  (no clear opposite) | noun
