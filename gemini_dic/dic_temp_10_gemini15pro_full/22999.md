bouge:/buʒ/
Verb
动词
to move or budge (especially in French)
移动或挪动（尤指在法语中）
The table wouldn't bouge.  桌子一动也不动。
近义词: move, shift, stir, budge
反义词: stay, remain, be still
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Old French bouger, from Vulgar Latin *bullire, frequentative of Latin bullīre “to bubble, boil.”
