arbitrage:/ˈɑːrbɪtrɑːʒ/| n.|套利，套汇|The simultaneous buying and selling of securities, currency, or commodities in different markets or in derivative forms in order to take advantage of differing prices for the same asset.|Example 1: He made a fortune through arbitrage in the currency markets. 他通过在货币市场套利发了财。

Example 2:  Arbitrage opportunities exist when the same asset is priced differently in different markets.  当同一资产在不同市场上的价格不同时，就存在套利机会。|Synonyms: speculation, trading, profiteering|Antonyms: investment, lending|noun
