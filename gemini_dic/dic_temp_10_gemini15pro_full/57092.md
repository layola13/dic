doughtily:/ˈdaʊtɪli/ | adv. |勇敢地，坚决地 | In a brave and resolute manner.  |  The knight doughtily defended the castle against the invaders. 骑士勇敢地保卫城堡抵御侵略者。 |  bravely, courageously, valiantly, resolutely | timidly, cowardly, fearfully | adverb

doughtily:/ˈdaʊtɪli/ | adv. |顽强地，不屈不挠地 |  In a persistent and unwavering manner. | The team fought doughtily despite being outnumbered.  尽管寡不敌众，队伍还是顽强地战斗。 |  persistently, tenaciously, stubbornly | weakly, feebly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doughtili, from Old English *dōhtiglīce (“valiantly, bravely”), from dōhtig (“valiant, brave, doughty”) + -līce (adverbial suffix). The word "doughty" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *dugō (“useful, fit, valid”).
