aforethought:/əˈfɔːrθɔːt/
adjective
预谋的，事先考虑的
Considered or planned beforehand; premeditated.
例句：The murder was clearly a crime of aforethought. 这起谋杀显然是预谋犯罪。

例句：With malice aforethought, he planned the perfect alibi. 他怀着恶意预谋，策划了完美的不在场证明。

近义词：premeditated, deliberate, planned, intentional, calculated
反义词：unintentional, unplanned, impulsive, spontaneous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *aforethoght*, equivalent to afore + thought.  Afore is from Old English *ærbēforan*  (before).  Thought is from Old English *þōht* (thought).
