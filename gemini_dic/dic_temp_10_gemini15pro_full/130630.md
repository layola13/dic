nonadmirer:/ˌnɒnədˈmaɪərər/ (US) /ˌnɒnədˈmaɪrər/ (UK)
n.
非崇拜者；不喜欢…的人
a person who does not admire someone or something
例句：He has many admirers, but also his share of nonadmirers. 他有很多崇拜者，但也有一些不喜欢他的人。

例句：The book received mixed reviews, with both admirers and nonadmirers voicing their opinions. 这本书收到的评价褒贬不一，崇拜者和非崇拜者都表达了他们的观点。

近义词：critic, detractor, opponent, antagonist
反义词：admirer, fan, supporter, devotee
词性：名词
