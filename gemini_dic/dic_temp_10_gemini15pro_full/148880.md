overwell:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛl/| v.| 淹没，压倒，充满| To cover completely with water; submerge. To overcome completely in mind or feeling.| The river overwelled its banks and flooded the town. 河水淹没了河岸，淹没了城镇。| 近义词: overwhelm, inundate, flood, engulf | 反义词: drain, dry |词性: 动词


overwell:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛl/| v.| (情感) 充满，使…不堪承受| To overcome with emotion; overwhelm.|She was overwelled with grief when she heard the news.  当她听到这个消息时，她悲痛欲绝。| 近义词: overwhelm, overcome | 反义词: underwhelm |词性: 动词



词源:  From Old English *oferwellan, from ofer- (over) + wellan (to well up, boil).  源自古英语 *oferwellan，由 ofer-（越过）+ wellan（涌出，沸腾）组成。
