flakeless:/ˈfleɪklɪs/
adjective
无鳞的，不剥落的
Not flaking or peeling; smooth and unbroken.
The flakeless paint provided a perfect finish.  无鳞油漆提供了完美的表面。
The advertisement promised a flakeless foundation that would last all day.  广告承诺了一种不脱落的粉底，可以持续一整天。
Near synonyms: smooth, unbroken, seamless, unblemished
Antonyms: flaky, peeling, scaly, chipped
word class: adjective
