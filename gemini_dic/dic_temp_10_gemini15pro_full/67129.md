exportability:/ˌɛkspɔːrtəˈbɪləti/
n.|可出口性；可输出性|The exportability of a product depends on various factors, including its cost, quality, and demand in the target market.  一种产品的可出口性取决于多种因素，包括其成本、质量和目标市场的需求。|
例句：The software's exportability was a key factor in its success. 该软件的可出口性是其成功的关键因素。

例句：The government is trying to improve the exportability of agricultural products. 政府正在努力提高农产品的可出口性。

近义词：transferability, portability, tradability
反义词：non-exportability, non-tradability
词性：noun

export:/ɪkˈspɔːrt/
v. |出口；输出|To send goods or services to another country for sale. 将商品或服务送到另一个国家销售。
n.|出口; 出口商品|The product is an important export for the country. 该产品是该国重要的出口商品。
词性: verb, noun
词源: From Latin exportare "to carry out," from ex- "out" + portare "to carry."

ability:/əˈbɪləti/
n.|能力；才能|The quality of being able to do something. 做某事的能力。
词性: noun
词源: From Old French habilité, from Latin habilitatem "aptitude, fitness, skill," from habilis "apt."
