intercombine:/ˌɪntərˈkəmˌbaɪn/
|v.|互相结合，相互合并|To combine with each other; to combine mutually.|The two companies intercombined to form a larger corporation. 这两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。|The different chemicals intercombined to create a new substance. 不同的化学物质相互结合，创造出一种新物质。|近义词：merge, combine, unite, amalgamate|反义词：separate, divide, split|词性:动词

词源:
From inter- + combine.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "combine" means to join or mix together.
