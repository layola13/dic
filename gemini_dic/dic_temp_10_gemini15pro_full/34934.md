cloaks:/kloʊks/
n.
斗篷；披风；掩盖物；幌子
A loose outer garment, typically sleeveless and fastening at the neck, worn over other clothes as protection from the weather. Something that covers or conceals.
The spy used darkness as a cloak.  间谍用黑暗作为掩护。
近义词:capes, mantles, covers, disguises
反义词:reveals, exposes


cloak:/kloʊk/
v.
遮掩；掩盖；隐藏
To cover or conceal something.
He cloaked his real intentions with a smile. 他用微笑掩盖了他真实的意图。
近义词:conceal, hide, mask, veil
反义词:reveal, expose, uncover
