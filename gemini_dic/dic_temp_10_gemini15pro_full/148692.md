overtipped:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪpt/ |v. |给…太多小费；过分倾斜|To give someone a larger tip than is usual or expected, or to tip excessively.  To cause something to tilt or lean too far. |I think I overtipped the waiter – I gave him 25%. 我想我给服务员的小费太多了——我给了他25%。 The boat overtipped and we all fell into the water. 船过度倾斜，我们都掉进了水里。| 近义词: overcompensate, overpay | 反义词: undertip |词性: 动词

overtipped:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪpt/ |adj. |被给太多小费的；过度倾斜的|Having received a larger tip than is usual or expected. Tilted or leaning too far. |The waiter seemed surprised to be so overtipped. 服务员似乎对收到这么多小费感到惊讶。 The overtipped lamp fell off the table.  过度倾斜的灯从桌子上掉了下来。| 近义词: overloaded, top-heavy | 反义词: stable, upright |词性: 形容词
