outflourish:/ˌaʊtˈflɝɪʃ/ | v. | 超过…的繁荣; 胜过 | To surpass in flourishing; to grow or prosper more than. | The new company outflourished its competitors in the first year. 这家新公司在第一年就超过了竞争对手的繁荣。 | The sunflowers outflourished the other plants in the garden.  花园里的向日葵比其他植物长得更茂盛。 | 近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed | 反义词: wither, decline, fail | 词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding") + "flourish" (from Old French "floriss-" meaning "to blossom, prosper")
