一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Noi mâncăm pizza în fiecare vineri. = We eat pizza every Friday.
Noi învățăm engleza la școală. = We learn English at school.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Noi gătim cina acum. = We are cooking dinner now.
Noi ascultăm muzică în acest moment. = We are listening to music at the moment.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Noi am terminat proiectul. = We have finished the project.
Noi am vizitat multe țări. = We have visited many countries.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Noi am lucrat la acest proiect toată ziua. = We have been working on this project all day.
Noi am studiat engleza de mulți ani. = We have been studying English for many years.

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Noi am mers la cinema ieri. = We went to the cinema yesterday.
Noi am mâncat la restaurant aseară. = We ate at the restaurant last night.

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Noi citeam o carte când ai sunat. = We were reading a book when you called.
Noi ne uitam la televizor când a început ploaia. = We were watching TV when it started to rain.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Noi terminasem proiectul înainte să pleci. = We had finished the project before you left.
Noi mâncasem deja când ai ajuns. = We had already eaten when you arrived.

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Noi lucraserăm la acest proiect toată ziua înainte să vii. = We had been working on this project all day before you came.
Noi studiaserăm engleza de mulți ani înainte să ne mutăm în Anglia. = We had been studying English for many years before we moved to England.

注意事项:
"noi" in Romanian is primarily used in formal settings or in certain dialects. In informal conversation, "we" is often expressed using other pronominal forms depending on the context and the people involved.  There is no direct antonym for "we".


The provided examples use Romanian since "noi" is a Romanian word.  The English translations are provided for clarity.  The word itself doesn't have different tenses, the verb conjugated with it reflects the tense.
