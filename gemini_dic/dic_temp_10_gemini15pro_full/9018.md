antimachinery:/ˌæntiˈməˈʃiːnəri/
adjective
反对机械化的
Opposed to the use of machinery, especially in industry or agriculture.
The antimachinery movement gained traction during the Industrial Revolution.  反对机械化运动在工业革命期间获得了关注。
Some farmers held antimachinery beliefs, fearing job displacement. 一些农民持有反对机械化的信念，担心工作被取代。
近义词: Luddite, technophobic
反义词: pro-technology, automation-friendly
词性: adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) + machinery (noun)
"anti-" comes from Greek, meaning "against" or "opposite."
"machinery" comes from Old French "machinerie."
