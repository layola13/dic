compositeness:/kəmˈpɒzɪtnəs/ /kəmˈpɑːzɪtnɪs/| n. |复合性，合成性|The state or quality of being composite; the result of being made up of distinct parts or elements.|The compositeness of the material made it strong and flexible. 这种材料的复合性使它既坚固又灵活。|近义词：complexity, compoundness|反义词：simplicity, homogeneity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From composite + -ness.
From Late Latin compositus, past participle of componere (“to put together”), from com- (“together”) + ponere (“to put, place”).
