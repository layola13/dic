inmeats: /ɪnˈmiːts/ | n. | 内脏; (供食用的)动物内脏 | The inmeats of the pig were used to make sausages.  猪的内脏被用来做香肠。 |  Synonym: offal, viscera | Antonym: None (No direct opposite for edible animal organs) | noun


Etymology:  "inmeats" comes from Middle English *inmete*, from *in* (inside) + *mete* (meat, food).  Originally referring to any kind of food, it narrowed in meaning to specifically refer to the internal organs of an animal used as food.
