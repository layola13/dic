nonscholars:/ˌnɒnˈskɒləz/
noun
非学者；没有学问的人
people who are not scholars; people who lack scholarship
The event was attended by scholars and nonscholars alike.  这次活动学者和非学者都参加了。
Nonscholars often find academic texts difficult to understand. 非学者通常觉得学术文本难以理解。
Synonyms: laymen, non-academics, laypeople
Antonyms: scholars, academics, intellectuals
词性: 名词
