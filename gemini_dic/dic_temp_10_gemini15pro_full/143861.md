outplodding:/ˌaʊtˈplɒdɪŋ/| v. | 辛苦地胜过，比...更努力工作 |  working harder and longer than someone else, especially in order to achieve something | He outplodded his rivals and won the race. 他比对手更努力，赢得了比赛。 | synonyms: outwork, outperform, outstrip | antonyms:  underperform, lag behind | verb

outplodding:/ˌaʊtˈplɒdɪŋ/| adj. | 勤勉的，坚持不懈的 | Characterized by steady, persistent effort, especially over a long period. |  His outplodding efforts eventually paid off. 他坚持不懈的努力最终得到了回报。 | synonyms:  diligent, persevering, tenacious | antonyms:  lazy, indolent, lackadaisical | adjective
