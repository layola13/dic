词源：
"obstacle" comes from Old French "obstacle" (14c.) and directly from Latin "obstaculum" "hindrance, obstacle," from "obstare" "stand opposite to, stand in the way of, oppose," from "ob" "against" + "stare" "to stand."
"race" comes from Old Norse "rás" "running, race, current."
