entrochite:/ˈɛntrəˌkaɪt/| n.|海百合茎化石|A fossilized segment of the stem of a crinoid (sea lily).|例句：The museum displayed a collection of entrochites from the Jurassic period.  博物馆展出了一批侏罗纪时期的海百合茎化石。

例句：He found an entrochite embedded in the limestone cliff. 他在石灰岩悬崖中发现了一块海百合茎化石。|近义词：crinoid stem, fossil|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "living crinoid")|词性:noun
