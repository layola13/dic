abrade:/əˈbreɪd/| vt.|磨损，擦伤|To wear away by rubbing; to scrape or wear off.|The rocks abraded the skin from her knees.  岩石擦破了她膝盖的皮。

The constant wind and sand abraded the paint off the car.  持续的风沙把汽车的油漆磨掉了。|近义词：scrape, scratch, erode, wear down, chafe|反义词：build up, augment, enhance|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abradere (to scrape off), from ab- (away) + radere (to scrape).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Sandpaper abrades the surface of wood. = 砂纸磨损木头的表面。
The harsh weather abrades the exposed rocks. = 恶劣的天气侵蚀着裸露的岩石。

一般过去时:
The rope abraded against the rock, eventually breaking. = 绳子在岩石上摩擦，最终断了。
Her skin was abraded by the rough fabric. = 她的皮肤被粗糙的布料擦伤了。

现在完成时:
The constant rubbing has abraded the finish on the furniture. = 不断的摩擦已经磨损了家具的表面。
The wind and rain have abraded the inscription on the tombstone. = 风雨磨损了墓碑上的铭文。

过去完成时:
The glacier had abraded the valley floor over thousands of years. = 冰川在数千年的时间里磨损了谷底。
The rocks had abraded the bottom of the boat, causing a leak. = 岩石磨损了船底，导致漏水。

将来时:
The new shoes will abrade your heels if you don't wear socks. = 如果你不穿袜子，新鞋会磨损你的脚后跟。
Continued use will abrade the sharp edges of the knife. = 持续使用会磨损刀的锋利边缘。

被动语态:
The surface was abraded by the constant friction. = 表面被不断的摩擦磨损了。
The paint was abraded from the car by the sandstorm. = 汽车上的油漆被沙尘暴磨掉了。

PHRASE->
abraded by = 被...磨损
abraded surface = 磨损的表面
abraded skin = 擦伤的皮肤
be abraded away = 被磨损掉
abrasion resistant = 耐磨的
