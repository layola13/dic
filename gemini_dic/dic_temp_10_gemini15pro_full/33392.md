chuprassy:/tʃʌpˈræsi/ | n. |  杂役，办公室勤杂人员 | An office attendant or messenger, especially in India.  Derived from Hindi.  Historically, a chuprassy performed various tasks like serving tea, delivering messages, and running errands.  While the term is less common now, it still sometimes refers to a low-ranking office worker. |  The chuprassy brought in the tea. 杂役把茶端了进来。|Synonyms: peon, orderly, messenger | Antonyms: manager, executive | Noun
