finalizes:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
最终确定；完成；定稿
puts the final touches on something; completes all the necessary arrangements for something.
The committee finalizes the budget every year. 委员会每年都会最终确定预算。
The lawyers are finalizing the contract. 律师们正在最终确定合同。
近义词:concludes, completes, settles, confirms
反义词:begins, starts, initiates, commences
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From final + -ize.  "Final" comes from the Latin "finalis" meaning "of or pertaining to the end".  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or render" something.
