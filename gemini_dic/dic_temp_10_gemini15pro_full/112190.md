looney:/ˈluːni/| adj. n.|疯狂的，古怪的；疯子|A slang term for someone considered insane or eccentric. It can also refer to a Canadian one-dollar coin.|He's a bit looney, always talking to himself. 他有点疯疯癫癫的，总是自言自语。|近义词：crazy, insane, mad, eccentric|反义词:sane, rational|词性:adjective, noun

loonie:/ˈluːni/| n.|加元（加拿大货币单位）|Canadian one-dollar coin.  It's called a loonie because it has a picture of a loon (a type of bird) on it.|Can you lend me a loonie?  你能借我一个加元吗？|近义词：dollar (in the context of Canadian currency)|反义词:None (specific to currency)|词性:noun

词源:
looney (adj.) is derived from lunatic, influenced by the image of the moon (luna) and its supposed connection to madness.
loonie (n.) is short for loon dollar, referring to the image of the common loon on the Canadian one-dollar coin.
