hematoid:/ˈhiːmətɔɪd/| adj.|类血的；血状的；含铁的|Resembling blood; bloody. Containing iron.|The hematoid stain on the carpet was alarming. 地毯上的血迹令人担忧。|Synonyms: bloodlike, sanguineous, ferruginous|Antonyms: bloodless, anemic|adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek αἷμα (haîma, “blood”) + -oid.
