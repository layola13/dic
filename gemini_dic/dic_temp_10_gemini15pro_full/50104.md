dentile:/ˈdɛnˌtaɪl/
n.
小齿状装饰, 齿状线脚
A small, tooth-like projection used as an architectural ornament, typically forming a series along a cornice or molding.
The dentils along the roofline added a touch of elegance to the building.  屋檐上的齿状装饰为建筑增添了一丝优雅。
synonyms: tooth-molding, dentil molding
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, consider plain molding or undecorated)
