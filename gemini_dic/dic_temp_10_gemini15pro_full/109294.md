lemurlike:/ˈliːməlaɪk/| adj.|像狐猴的，狐猴似的|Resembling a lemur, especially in appearance or behavior.|The child's large eyes and clinging movements were almost lemurlike.  这孩子的 大眼睛和紧紧抓住的动作几乎像狐猴一样。|近义词：monkeylike, simian, primate-like|反义词:humanlike, anthropoid|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From lemur + -like.  Lemur (from Latin lemures, meaning "ghosts" or "spirits of the dead," referring to the nocturnal habits and reflective eyes of lemurs) + -like (Old English -lic, meaning "having the form or characteristics of").
