conceptualization:/kənˌsɛptʃuələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 概念化；概念形成 | The process of forming a concept or idea.  |  His conceptualization of the project was brilliant.  他的项目构思非常出色。 | synonyms:  conception, ideation, formulation, abstraction | antonyms: concretization, materialization | noun


conceptualize:/kənˈsɛptʃuəˌlaɪz/ | v. |  概念化；构思 | To form a concept or idea of something. | I can't conceptualize how anyone could be so cruel. 我无法理解怎么会有人如此残忍。 | synonyms: conceive, envision, imagine, formulate | antonyms: realize, actualize | verb
