decipiency:/dɪˈsɪpiənsi/
n.
欺骗; 欺骗性; 蒙蔽性
the quality of being deceptive or misleading.
The decipiency of the advertisement led many customers to believe the product was much better than it actually was.  该广告的欺骗性使许多顾客相信该产品比实际情况要好得多。

近义词: deception, deceit, fraud, trickery, guile
反义词: honesty, truthfulness, candor, frankness
词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *decipientia, from Latin decipere ‘ensnare’ (see deceive).
