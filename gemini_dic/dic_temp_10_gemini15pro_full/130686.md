nonaffiliation:/ˌnɑːnəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 无 affiliation; 不隶属（任何组织或群体）|  The state of not being affiliated with any particular group, organization, or cause. |  He declared his nonaffiliation with any political party. 他声明不隶属于任何政党。|synonyms: independence, neutrality, detachment | antonyms: affiliation, membership, association | noun

nonaffiliated:/ˌnɑːnəˈfɪliˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不隶属于...的；无 affiliation 的 | Not officially attached to or connected with a particular group, organization, or cause.  |  Many nonaffiliated voters are undecided about who they will support. 许多无党派选民还没有决定支持谁。 | synonyms: independent, unattached, freelance | antonyms: affiliated, connected, associated | adjective
