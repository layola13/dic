brouhaha:/ˌbruːˈhɑːhɑː/ /ˌbruːˈhɑːhɑː/
noun
喧闹，骚动；吵闹；纷扰
an uproar or commotion that is usually over a trivial matter
例句：There was a big brouhaha about the new dress code at school.  学校里关于新的着装规定有很多的喧闹。

例句：The brouhaha over the celebrity's divorce filled the gossip columns for weeks.  这位名人的离婚事件在八卦专栏里喧闹了好几个星期。

近义词：commotion, uproar, fuss, hubbub, hullabaloo
反义词：calm, quiet, peace, tranquility
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brouhaha*, of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative.
