neotropical:/ˌniːəˈtrɒpɪkəl/| adj.|新热带区的|Relating to or denoting the biogeographic region comprising South America, Central America, the Antilles, and tropical southern Mexico.|Many neotropical birds migrate north for the summer. 许多新热带鸟类夏季向北迁徙。|The Amazon rainforest is a neotropical ecosystem. 亚马逊雨林是一个新热带生态系统。|近义词:tropical, South American|反义词:Nearctic, Palaearctic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Neo- + tropical.  "Neo-" meaning "new" and "tropical" referring to the tropics.
