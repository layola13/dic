bodies: /ˈbɒdiz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdiz/ (US) | n. | 身体；尸体；主要部分；团体；大量 |  The physical structure of a person or an animal, or a corpse; the main part of something; a group of people with a common purpose or identity; a large amount of something. |

例句：
The dancers moved their bodies gracefully.  舞者们优雅地移动着他们的身体。

After the battle, bodies lay scattered across the field.  战斗结束后，尸体散落在田野里。

近义词：
corpse, carcass, remains (for dead bodies); physique, frame, build (for living bodies); group, organization, association (for groups of people); mass, bulk, quantity (for large amounts)

反义词:
spirit, soul, mind (opposite of physical body)


词性: noun
