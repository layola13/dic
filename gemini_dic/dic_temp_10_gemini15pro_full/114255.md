maim:/meɪm/| vt.  adj.|使残废，残废的|To injure someone so severely that a part of their body is permanently damaged, usually losing its use or function; permanently damaged in this way.|The soldier was maimed in battle and lost his leg.  这个士兵在战斗中致残，失去了一条腿。|
The accident left him maimed for life.  这起事故使他终身残疾。|Synonyms: cripple, disable, mutilate, injure, wound|Antonyms: heal, cure, mend|verb, adjective

maimed:/meɪmd/| adj.|残废的|Permanently damaged and having lost the use or function of a body part.|The maimed veteran relied on a wheelchair.  这位残疾的退伍军人依靠轮椅。|Her maimed hand made everyday tasks difficult.  她残废的手使日常工作变得困难。|Synonyms: crippled, disabled, mutilated, injured, wounded|Antonyms: healthy, sound, whole, able-bodied|adjective
