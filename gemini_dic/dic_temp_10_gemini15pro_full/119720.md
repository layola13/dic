midst:/mɪdst/ | n. |中间，当中；围绕 |in the middle of something; surrounded by something |例句：The house stood in the midst of a large garden. 这座房子坐落在一个大花园的中央。|例句：She found herself alone in the midst of a bustling crowd. 她发现自己孤身一人置身于熙攘的人群中。 |近义词：center, middle, heart, midst |反义词：edge, periphery, outside |词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *middes, from Old English middes (“midst, middle”), dative plural of middel (“middle”). Cognate with Old Frisian middes, Old Saxon middes, Middle Dutch middes, Old High German mittes (German Mittes), Old Norse miðjum (dative plural). Related also to amid.
