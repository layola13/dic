bouffant:/buːˈfɑːnt/| adj. n.|蓬松的，鼓起的；蓬松的发型|A bouffant hairstyle is one in which the hair is styled high on the head and usually puffed out at the sides.  蓬松的发型是指头发在头顶高高梳起，通常在两侧鼓起。|She wore her hair in a bouffant style. 她梳了一个蓬松的发型。|近义词：puffy, full, voluminous|反义词:flat, sleek|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: French, present participle of bouffer "to puff," from Old French boufer "to swell, puff up."
