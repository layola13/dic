epichordal:/ˈɛpɪˌkɔːrdl/
adjective
脊索上的，脊索背面的
Located above or dorsal to the notochord.
The epichordal cartilage is a small, paired cartilage located above the notochord in some vertebrates.  脊索上软骨是在一些脊椎动物中位于脊索上方的小的成对软骨。
The development of the epichordal structures in the larval stage is crucial for the organism's later mobility.  幼虫阶段脊索上结构的发育对生物体后期的活动能力至关重要。
Synonym: dorsal to notochord
Antonym: ventral to notochord
词性: adjective
