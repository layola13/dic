melado:/meɪˈlɑːdoʊ/ | n. | 蜜糖；糖蜜; 甘蔗渣 | A dark, viscous syrup produced during the refining of sugar.  It is also a term for the solid residue left after sugarcane processing, used as fuel or fertilizer. | The melados was thick and dark.  这种蜜糖又浓又黑。| synonyms: molasses, treacle, syrup | antonyms: none | noun

melado:/meɪˈlɑːdoʊ/ | adj. |  蜜糖色的; 甜蜜的 | Resembling or characteristic of molasses; sweet. (Less common usage) |  She had melados-colored eyes. 她有一双蜜糖色的眼睛. | synonyms: honey-colored, sweet, sugary | antonyms: bitter, sour, unsweetened | adjective
