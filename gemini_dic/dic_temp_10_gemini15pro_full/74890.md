freshest:/ˈfreʃɪst/| adj.|最新鲜的|The superlative form of 'fresh'.  It means the most recently made, harvested, or obtained, and therefore in the best condition.|These are the freshest strawberries I've ever tasted. 这是我吃过最新鲜的草莓。|近义词：newest, latest, recent, |反义词:stalest, oldest, rotten|adjective
