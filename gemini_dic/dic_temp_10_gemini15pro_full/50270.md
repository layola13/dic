dephase:/diːˈfeɪz/| n. |失相；不同步|The state of being out of phase or synchronization.|The dephase between the two signals caused interference. 两个信号之间的失相造成了干扰。|The slight dephase in their movements made the dance look less polished.  他们动作上的轻微不同步使得舞蹈看起来不够完美。|近义词：asynchrony, discordance |反义词: synchrony, harmony|词性:名词
