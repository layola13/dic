disulphide:/daɪˈsʌlfaɪd/ | n. | 二硫化物 | A compound containing a disulphide group, formed from two sulphur atoms bonded together. |  Disulphide bonds are important in protein structure. 二硫键在蛋白质结构中很重要。| synonym: disulfane | antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially reducing agents which break disulphide bonds) | noun

disulfide:/daɪˈsʌlfaɪd/ | n. | 二硫化物 | A compound containing a disulfide group, formed from two sulphur atoms bonded together. | Disulfide bridges contribute to the stability of a protein's tertiary structure. 二硫桥有助于蛋白质三级结构的稳定性。 | synonym: disulfane | antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially reducing agents which break disulfide bonds) | noun
