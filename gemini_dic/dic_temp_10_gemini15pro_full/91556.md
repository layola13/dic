hyper-natural:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəl/| adj.|超自然的，极度自然的|Existing in a state beyond what is considered natural; exceeding the natural or normal.  Something that appears incredibly natural, perhaps to an exaggerated degree, or paradoxically, something artificial that mimics natural qualities perfectly.|The hyper-natural acting in the film made the characters incredibly believable.  电影中超自然的表演使角色令人难以置信地可信。


The valley had a hyper-natural beauty, almost as if it were from a dream.  山谷有一种超自然的美丽，仿佛来自梦境。|近义词：supernatural, preternatural, uncanny, extraordinary|反义词：natural, ordinary, common|词性:形容词

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek):  meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
"natural" (Latin "naturalis"): meaning "of or pertaining to nature"
