monovalent:/ˌmɒnəˈveɪlənt/ /ˌmɑːnəˈveɪlənt/ | adj. | 单价的；一价的；单价 | Having a valency of one; combining with only one atom of another element. |  Example: Sodium is a monovalent element. 钠是一种单价元素。

Example: A monovalent antibody binds to only one type of antigen. 单价抗体只与一种类型的抗原结合。 | 近义词: univalent | 反义词: multivalent, polyvalent | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From mono- (one) + -valent (having power).  "Mono-" comes from Greek "monos" meaning "alone, single, solitary." "-valent" comes from Latin "valentem" (nominative valens), present participle of "valere" meaning "to be strong, be worth."
