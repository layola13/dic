boomest:/ˈbuːməst/ | adj. | 最为繁荣的，最兴旺的 (non-standard, humorous) |  A humorous superlative form of "booming," implying the highest degree of prosperity or growth.  Used informally and often ironically. |  "That new tech company is the boomest business in town right now." 那家新科技公司是现在城里最兴旺的企业。| 近义词: most prosperous, thriving, flourishing | 反义词: declining, failing, stagnant | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  Derived from "boom" (meaning a period of rapid economic growth) with the superlative suffix "-est."  The word "boom" itself has origins in imitative words for loud noises, possibly related to the sound of cannons or explosions, later coming to represent a sudden increase or expansion.
