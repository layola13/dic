octopath:/ˈɒktəʊpɑːθ/ /ˈɑːktəpæθ/ | n. | 八方旅人；八条路径 |A portmanteau of "octo" (eight) and "path," referring to the eight protagonists in the video game "Octopath Traveler," each with their own distinct storyline.  The term can also more generally refer to having eight paths or options.|The game *Octopath Traveler* allows you to choose from eight different characters, each with their own octopath. |近义词: eightfold path, multiple paths|反义词: single path|词性:名词
