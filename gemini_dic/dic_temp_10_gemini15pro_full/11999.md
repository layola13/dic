arthrodesis:/ˌɑːrθrəˈdiːsɪs/
noun
关节固定术
Surgical immobilization of a joint by fusion of the adjacent bones.
The patient underwent arthrodesis of the wrist due to severe arthritis.  这位病人由于严重的关节炎而接受了腕关节固定术。
Synonyms: fusion, ankylosis
Antonyms: arthroplasty, joint mobilization
