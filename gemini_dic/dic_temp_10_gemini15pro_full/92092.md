hyperrealizing:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 超现实化；过度现实化 | The process of making something seem more real than real, often to an exaggerated or heightened degree.  This can involve intensifying sensory details, emotional responses, or the perceived significance of an event. |  He was hyperrealizing the danger, imagining every worst-case scenario. 他在过度现实化危险，想象着每一个最坏的情况。

He found himself hyperrealizing the texture of the carpet beneath his feet. 他发现自己过度关注于脚下的地毯纹理。 | 近义词: exaggerating, amplifying, intensifying | 反义词: downplaying, minimizing, understating | verb
