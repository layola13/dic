epopoeia:/ˌɛpəˈpiːə/ | n. | 史诗; 史诗体裁;  | A long poem, typically one derived from ancient oral tradition, narrating the deeds and adventures of heroic or legendary figures or the history of a nation. | Example 1: Homer's *Odyssey* is a classic example of epopoeia. 荷马的《奥德赛》是史诗的经典范例。

Example 2:  The *Aeneid* is an epopoeia celebrating the founding of Rome.  《埃涅阿斯纪》是一部歌颂罗马建国的史诗。| 近义词: epic, saga | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly lyric or sonnet for contrasting poetic forms) | 词性: noun
