compositae:/kəmˈpɒzɪtiː/
noun
菊科
A large family of flowering plants, including daisies, sunflowers, and asters.  Characterized by flower heads composed of many small florets.
The sunflower belongs to the compositae family.  向日葵属于菊科。
synonyms: Asteraceae
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic family)
