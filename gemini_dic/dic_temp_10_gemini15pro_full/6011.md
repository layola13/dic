amebic:/əˈmiːbɪk/ | adj. | 变形虫的；阿米巴样的 | Relating to or caused by amebas; resembling an ameba. |  The doctor diagnosed an amebic infection. 医生诊断出一种阿米巴感染。|  Amoebic dysentery is a serious health concern in some regions. 阿米巴痢疾在某些地区是一个严重的健康问题。 | Synonyms: amoebic, protozoan | Antonyms: None | adjective

ameba:/əˈmiːbə/ | n. | 变形虫；阿米巴 | A single-celled animal that changes shape as it moves. |  Amebas are found in freshwater and soil. 变形虫存在于淡水和土壤中。|  The ameba engulfs its food by extending pseudopods. 变形虫通过伸出伪足来吞噬食物。| Synonyms: amoeba, protozoan | Antonyms: None | noun
