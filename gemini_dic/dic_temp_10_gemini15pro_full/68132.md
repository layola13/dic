fahlerz:/ˈfɑːlɛərts/ | n. | 辉锑铜矿，灰铜矿 | A gray copper ore, consisting of a complex sulfide of antimony, arsenic, copper, iron, and zinc. | Fahlerz is a complex mineral and can be challenging to identify. 辉锑铜矿是一种复杂的矿物，难以识别。 | The miners discovered a rich vein of fahlerz. 矿工们发现了一条丰富的辉锑铜矿矿脉。 | 近义词: tetrahedrite, tennantite | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From German *Fahlerz*, literally "pale ore," from *fahl* "pale" + *Erz* "ore."
