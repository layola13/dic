over-contraction:/ˌoʊvər kənˈtrækʃən/ | n. |过度收缩| Excessive shortening or contraction, often referring to muscles.  This can lead to stiffness, pain, and reduced range of motion. |The over-contraction of his bicep caused a sharp pain. 他的肱二肌过度收缩引起了一阵剧痛。|  The patient experienced over-contraction of the muscles in his hand. 病人的手部肌肉过度收缩。|  hypercontraction, spasm | relaxation, extension | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
