mesobranchial:/ˌmezəˈbræŋkiəl/
adjective
中鳃的，中鳃骨的
Relating to or denoting the middle section of the gill arches or the filaments attached to them in fish.
The mesobranchial arch plays a crucial role in fish respiration. 中鳃弓在鱼类呼吸中起着至关重要的作用。
The researchers studied the development of the mesobranchial filaments. 研究人员研究了中鳃丝的发育。
近义词: mid-gill (less formal)
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider probranchial - related to the first gill arch, or hypobranchial - related to the lower part of the gill arch)
词性: adjective
