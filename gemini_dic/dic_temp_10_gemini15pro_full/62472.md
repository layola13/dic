endoskeleton:/ˈɛndoʊˌskɛlɪtn/ | n. | 内骨骼 | An internal skeleton, such as the bony or cartilaginous skeleton of vertebrates. |  Humans have an endoskeleton made of bone. 人类的内骨骼是由骨骼构成的。

The endoskeleton provides support and protection for internal organs.  内骨骼为内脏器官提供支撑和保护。 |  Synonym: internal skeleton | Antonym: exoskeleton | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  endo- (within) + -skeleton (skeleton).  "Endo-" comes from Greek "endon" (within). "-skeleton" comes from Greek "skeletos" (dried body).
