ectomorph: /ˈɛktəmɔːrf/ | n. | 瘦型体质者 | A person with a lean and delicate body build, characterized by long limbs and a fast metabolism.  Ectomorphs are often said to have difficulty gaining weight. |  He's a typical ectomorph – tall and skinny. 他是典型的瘦型体质——又高又瘦。|  She's always been an ectomorph and struggles to put on muscle. 她一直都是瘦型体质，很难增肌。 | synonyms:  lean, slender, thin, wiry | antonyms: endomorph, mesomorph | noun
