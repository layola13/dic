biotically:/baɪˈɒtɪkli/
adverb
生物地；由生物引起的
Involving living organisms; by biological means.
The pollutants were biotically degraded in the soil. 污染物在土壤中被生物降解。
Biotically diverse ecosystems are more resilient. 生物多样性丰富的生态系统更有弹性。

近义词: biologically, organically
反义词: abiotically, chemically
词性: adverb

词源:
From biotic + -ally.  Biotic comes from Greek *biōtikos*, from *biōtē* "life," from *bios* "life."
