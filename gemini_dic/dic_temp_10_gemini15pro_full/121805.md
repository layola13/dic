misreflect:/ˌmɪsrɪˈflekt/
verb
错误地反映;歪曲
to reflect wrongly or give a false impression of
The curved mirror misreflected her image, making her look much taller and thinner than she actually was. 扭曲的镜子错误地反映了她的形象，使她看起来比实际高得多也瘦得多。
misrepresent, distort, falsify
reflect accurately, portray truthfully
