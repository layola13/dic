overadvertise:/ˌoʊvərˈædvərtaɪz/ | v. | 过度宣传，广告做得过火 | To advertise excessively or to an unnecessary degree. | The company overadvertised the product, leading to consumer fatigue.  这家公司对该产品的广告宣传过度，导致消费者产生了厌倦感。| The movie was so overadvertised that it couldn't possibly live up to the hype. 这部电影被过度宣传，以至于它不可能达到宣传的效果。|  synonyms: overpromote, overhype, oversaturate | antonyms: underpromote, underadvertise | verb
