cross-sectoral:/ˌkrɒsˈsɛktərəl/ | adj. | 跨部门的，跨行业的 | Involving or relating to different sectors of an economy, industry, or sphere of activity. |  The government is implementing a cross-sectoral approach to address climate change. 政府正在实施一项跨部门的方法来应对气候变化。|  The conference brought together cross-sectoral experts to discuss the issue.  这次会议汇集了跨行业的专家来讨论这个问题。 |interdisciplinary, multi-sectoral, collaborative |  sector-specific, compartmentalized | adjective
