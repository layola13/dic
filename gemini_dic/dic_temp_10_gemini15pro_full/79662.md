glottal:/ˈɡlɒtəl/ (UK) /ˈɡlɑːtəl/ (US)| adj.|声门的，喉音的|Relating to the glottis (the opening between the vocal cords).|The singer produced a series of glottal stops. 歌手发出了一系列的喉塞音。|近义词:laryngeal|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:adjective

glottal stop:/ˈɡlɒtəl stɒp/ (UK) /ˈɡlɑːtəl stɑːp/ (US)| n.|喉塞音|A consonant sound produced by closing the glottis (the opening between the vocal cords).|The word "uh-oh" often includes a glottal stop. “呃哦”这个词通常包含一个喉塞音。|近义词:(No direct synonym)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
