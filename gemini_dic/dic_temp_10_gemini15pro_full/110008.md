librarianship:/laɪˈbrɛəriənʃɪp/
n.
图书馆学；图书馆管理工作
The study or profession of organizing and managing libraries.
She has a master's degree in librarianship. 她拥有图书馆学硕士学位。
He dedicated his life to librarianship. 他一生致力于图书馆事业。
近义词:library science, library management
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From librarian + -ship.  Librarian comes from Latin *librarius "copyist, scribe," from liber "book."  The suffix -ship denotes "state, condition, office, skill."
