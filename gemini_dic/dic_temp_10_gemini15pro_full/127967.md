neotechnic:/ˌniːoʊˈtɛknɪk/ | adj. | 新技术的，新工艺的 | Relating to or characteristic of the most modern techniques and technology, especially in industry. |  The neotechnic phase of industrialization is characterized by the use of electricity and alloys. 新技术产业化阶段的特点是使用电力和合金。|  Neotechnic societies rely heavily on advanced communication networks. 新技术社会严重依赖先进的通信网络。|  Synonyms: modern, advanced, high-tech, cutting-edge | Antonyms: primitive, outdated, obsolete | adjective
