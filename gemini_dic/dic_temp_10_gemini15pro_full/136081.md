nonrecitals:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsaɪtəlz/
noun
非叙述部分；不重复叙述；不提及；不列举
The part of a legal document that does not recite the facts of the case or the relevant law.  It often includes clauses related to boilerplate language, definitions, or choice of law.
The contract included several nonrecitals regarding jurisdiction and governing law.  该合同包括几项关于管辖权和适用法律的非叙述部分。
non-recital clauses, exclusory provisions, boilerplate language
