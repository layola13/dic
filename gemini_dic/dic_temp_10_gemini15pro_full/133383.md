nonexultant: /ˌnɒnɪɡˈzʌltənt/
adjective
非欣喜的，不狂喜的
Not exultant; not feeling or expressing triumphant joy or jubilation.
Her nonexultant tone surprised everyone, given her team's victory.  她团队获胜了，但她并没有欣喜的语气让每个人都感到惊讶。
The team's nonexultant response to their win suggested they had expected to do better.  该队对他们胜利的非欣喜反应表明他们原本期望做得更好。
近义词: unemotional, subdued, reserved, impassive, calm
反义词: exultant, jubilant, triumphant, elated, overjoyed
词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "exultant" (from Latin "exultare," meaning "to leap for joy").
