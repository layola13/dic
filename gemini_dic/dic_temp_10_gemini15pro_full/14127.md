authorially:/ɔːˈθɔːriəli/
| adv.|以作者身份地；像个作家似地|In a manner that befits or suggests an author; with the air of an author.  Expressing opinions or providing information as if one is the creator or authority on a subject.|He spoke authorially about the history of the town, as if he had written the book himself. 他以作者的口吻讲述了小镇的历史，就好像他自己写过这本书一样。|Synonym: definitively, magisterially, knowingly, dogmatically|Antonym: humbly, tentatively, uncertainly|word property:adverb
