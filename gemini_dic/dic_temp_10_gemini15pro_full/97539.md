ingrate:/ˈɪnˌɡreɪt/| adj.|忘恩负义的|Not grateful; unthankful.|She felt he was being ingrate after all she had done for him.  她觉得在她为他做了那么多之后，他真是忘恩负义。|Synonyms: thankless, unappreciative|Antonyms: grateful, appreciative|Adjective
