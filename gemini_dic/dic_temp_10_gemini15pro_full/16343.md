barbarossa: /ˌbɑːrbəˈrɒsə/ | n. | 巴巴罗萨  |  Code name for the Axis invasion of the Soviet Union during World War II.  |  Operation Barbarossa was launched in June 1941. 巴巴罗萨行动于1941年6月发动。 | 近义词: invasion, assault, attack | 反义词: defense, retreat | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Italian *Barbarossa* "Redbeard",  referring to Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor (1122-1190). Hitler chose this name for the invasion of the Soviet Union, perhaps hoping to evoke a similar success.
