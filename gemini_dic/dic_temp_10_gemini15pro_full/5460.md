alnohad: /ælnoʊˈhæd/
n.
Alnohad (Arabic: النوهد)
Alnohad is a term used primarily in Saudi Arabia and some other Gulf countries, referring to a type of traditional, elaborately embroidered women's cloak or overgarment. It is often made of luxurious fabrics like silk or velvet and adorned with intricate gold or silver thread embroidery.
She wore a beautiful black alnohad embroidered with gold thread. 她穿着一件绣着金线的漂亮黑色阿勒诺哈德。
Synonyms: Abaya, thobe, bisht (depending on region and specific style)
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific garment)
