out-trade:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪd/
verb
胜过；在贸易上超过
to surpass in trade; to be more successful in trade than another entity.
China has out-traded many developed countries in recent years. 中国近年来在贸易上超过了许多发达国家。
Their innovative products allowed them to out-trade their competitors. 他们的创新产品使他们在贸易上胜过竞争对手。
近义词: outsell, outperform, surpass
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb
