incontestability:/ˌɪnkɒnˌtɛstəˈbɪləti/ /ˌɪnkɑːnˌtɛstəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可争辩性，无可争议性|The state or quality of being incontestable; inability to be challenged or disputed.|The incontestability clause in the life insurance policy protects the beneficiary from challenges after a certain period. 人寿保险单中的不可抗辩条款在一定期限后保护受益人免受质疑。|Synonyms:  indisputability, irrefutability, unquestionability|Antonyms: disputability, contestability|noun

incontestable:/ˌɪnkɒnˈtɛstəbl/ /ˌɪnkɑːnˈtɛstəbl/| adj. |不可争辩的，无可争议的|Not able to be contested or disputed; undeniable.|His victory in the election was incontestable. 他在选举中的胜利是无可争议的。|Synonyms:  indisputable, irrefutable, unquestionable, undeniable|Antonyms: disputable, contestable, questionable, deniable|adjective
