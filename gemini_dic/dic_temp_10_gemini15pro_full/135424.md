nonpersecutory:/ˌnɒnpɜːrˈsɛkjʊtəri/ | adj. | 非迫害性的 | Not involving or characterized by persecution; not persecuting.  Relating to or denoting a delusion that is not characterized by feelings of being persecuted. | The psychiatrist determined that the patient's delusions were nonpersecutory, involving themes of grandiosity rather than fear. 这位精神科医生确定病人的妄想是非迫害性的，涉及的是夸大妄想而不是恐惧。|  The patient exhibited nonpersecutory delusions, believing he was a famous inventor.  病人表现出非迫害性妄想，认为自己是一位著名的发明家。 |  non-threatening, benign | persecutory, threatening | adjective
