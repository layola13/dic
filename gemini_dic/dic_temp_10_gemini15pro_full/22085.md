bollocks's:/ˈbɒl.əks.ɪz/ |n.|胡说八道；废话；睾丸（粗俗）| This is a load of bollocks's. 这纯属胡说八道。  The horse kicked him in the bollocks's. 那匹马踢了他的睾丸。 | 近义词: nonsense, rubbish, crap, testicles (formal) | 反义词: truth, sense | 词性: 名词
