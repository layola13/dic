benight:/bɪˈnaɪt/
verb
使陷入黑暗;  使蒙昧;  使迷失;  (由于夜幕降临)滞留;
To envelop in darkness, intellectual or moral darkness, or ignorance. To be overtaken by night.
例句：
The travelers were benighted in the forest.
旅行者们在森林里迷了路，被夜幕笼罩。

例句：
His narrow upbringing had benighted him to the realities of the world.
他狭隘的成长环境使他对世界的现实一无所知。
近义词：obscure, darken, bewilder, confuse
反义词：enlighten, illuminate
词性：v.
