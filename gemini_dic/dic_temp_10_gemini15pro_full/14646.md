avouchment:/əˈvaʊtʃmənt/
|n.|保证；担保；断言|An act of avouching; the act of affirming or asserting positively; earnest assertion or affirmation; positive declaration.|He made an avouchment of his friend's innocence. 他担保他的朋友是无辜的。|Synonyms: affirmation, assertion, declaration, guarantee, assurance|Antonyms: denial, refutation, contradiction|noun


avouch:/əˈvaʊtʃ/
|v.|保证；担保；断言|To affirm or assert positively; to vouch for; to guarantee.|I can avouch for the quality of this product. 我可以保证这款产品的质量。|Synonyms: affirm, assert, declare, guarantee, assure|Antonyms: deny, refute, contradict|verb
