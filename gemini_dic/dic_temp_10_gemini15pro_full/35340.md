co-determinant:/ˌkəʊdɪˈtɜːmɪnənt/ | n. | 共同决定因素；共因 | A factor that jointly determines an outcome with other factors. |  Co-determination plays a significant role in German corporate governance.  共同决定在德国公司治理中扮演着重要的角色。| synonyms: co-factor, contributing factor, joint determinant | antonyms: independent factor, sole determinant | noun

co-determinant:/ˌkəʊdɪˈtɜːmɪnənt/ | adj. | 共同决定的 | Jointly determining an outcome. | The success of the project was co-determinant on several factors. 该项目的成功取决于几个因素。| synonyms: jointly decisive, mutually influential | antonyms: independent, solely responsible | adjective
