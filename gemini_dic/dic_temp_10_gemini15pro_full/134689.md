nonmartially:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːrʃəli/
adverb. |非军事地，非战争地| In a non-military or non-warlike manner; peacefully.  |The dispute was resolved nonmartially through diplomacy. 这场争端通过外交途径和平解决了。|
Synonyms: peacefully, pacifically, amicably, nonviolently, non-aggressively
Antonyms: martially, militarily, belligerently, aggressively, violently
词性: 副词
