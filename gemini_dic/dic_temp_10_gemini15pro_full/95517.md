inclosure:/ɪnˈkloʊʒər/ /ɛnˈkloʊʒər/| n.|围场，圈地；附件；封入|An inclosure is an area that is surrounded by a fence or wall.  围场是被栅栏或墙壁包围的区域。|The sheep were grazing in the inclosure. 羊群在围场里吃草。|近义词：enclosure, compound, paddock|反义词: open field|noun


inclosure:/ɪnˈkloʊʒər/ /ɛnˈkloʊʒər/| n.|附件，随函附上|An inclosure is something that is included with a letter or package. 附件是与信件或包裹一起包含的东西。|Please find the inclosure attached to this email. 请查找附加在此电子邮件中的附件。|近义词：attachment, supplement, appendix|反义词: omission|noun


inclosure:/ɪnˈkloʊʒər/ /ɛnˈkloʊʒər/| n.|封闭，圈地行为|Inclosure refers to the act of enclosing land. 圈地是指封闭土地的行为。|The inclosure of common land led to social unrest. 公共土地的圈地导致了社会动荡。|近义词：enclosing, fencing, hedging|反义词: opening|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *enclosour*,  from Anglo-Norman *enclosur*, from Old French *enclos*, past participle of *enclore*, from Latin *includere* ("to shut in"), from *in-* + *claudere* ("to shut").
