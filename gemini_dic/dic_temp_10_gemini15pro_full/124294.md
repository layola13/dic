mother-of-pearl:/ˌmʌðər əv ˈpɜːrl/ (US), /ˌmʌðər əv ˈpɜːl/ (UK)| adj. |珍珠母制的，有珍珠母光泽的|She wore a mother-of-pearl necklace. 她戴着一条珍珠母项链。|例句：The inlay was made of mother-of-pearl.  这镶嵌物是用珍珠母制成的。


|近义词：nacre, pearl shell|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性: 名词, 形容词


词源:  From Middle English *moder of perle, borrowed from Anglo-Norman mereperle, from Old French mere perle (“mother of pearl”), from perle (“pearl”) + mere (“mother”).
