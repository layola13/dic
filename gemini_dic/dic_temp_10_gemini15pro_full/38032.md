componentize:/kəmˈpoʊnənˌtaɪz/
|v.|组件化，模块化|To design or convert (something) into components; to divide (something) into self-contained components.|We need to componentize our software to make it more flexible and maintainable. 我们需要将我们的软件组件化，使其更灵活，更易于维护。|Example: The company decided to componentize its product offerings to better serve diverse customer needs.  该公司决定将其产品供应组件化，以便更好地服务于不同的客户需求。|Synonyms: modularize, decompose, break down|Antonyms: integrate, unify, combine|verb

componentization:/kəmˌpoʊnənˌtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|组件化，模块化|The act or process of componentizing something.|Componentization of the system allowed for easier upgrades and customization. 系统的组件化使得升级和定制更容易。|Example: The componentization of the design made it easier for different teams to work on individual parts simultaneously. 设计的组件化使得不同的团队更容易同时处理各个部分。|Synonyms: modularization, decomposition|Antonyms: integration, unification|noun
