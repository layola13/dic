emberings: /ˈɛmbərɪŋz/ | n. | 余烬，闷烧的木块 | Smoldering pieces of wood or coal.  The remains of a fire. | The campfire's emberings glowed softly in the twilight.  篝火的余烬在暮色中发出柔和的光芒。 | 近义词: embers, cinders | 反义词: flame, blaze | 词性: 名词


词源: From Middle English *embring, from Old Norse *eimbreiða ("glowing ashes").  Related to ember.
