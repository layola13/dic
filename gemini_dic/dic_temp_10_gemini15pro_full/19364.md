bibliophile:/ˈbɪbliəfaɪl/
n.
藏书家；爱书的人
A person who collects or has a great love of books.
He was a renowned bibliophile, with a library containing thousands of rare first editions. 他是一位著名的藏书家，他的图书馆藏有数千本珍贵的初版书籍。
Synonyms: bookworm, book lover, bibliomaniac
Antonyms:  non-reader, illiterate


bibliomania:/ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniə/
n.
藏书癖；书痴
An excessive fondness for acquiring and possessing books.
His bibliomania led him to spend his entire fortune on collecting books. 他的藏书癖导致他倾家荡产地收集书籍。
Synonyms:  book madness, bibliophilism
Antonyms: bibliophobia (fear of books)



bibliotaph:/ˈbɪbliətæf/
n.
藏书者；（尤指）把书藏起来不读的人;  a person who hides or hoards books.
Someone who hoards books, often without reading them.
The old bibliophile turned bibliotaph when he started locking his library and refusing to let anyone borrow his books.  那位老藏书家变成了一个藏书者，因为他开始锁上他的图书馆，拒绝任何人借阅他的书籍。
Synonyms: book hoarder
Antonyms:  bibliophile, book lover, book sharer
