It refers to multiple instances of incorrect judgment or flawed reasoning leading to wrong actions or beliefs.|His misguidednesses led him down a path of destruction. 他的错误引导使他走上了一条毁灭之路。|Near synonyms: errors, fallacies, misconceptions, misjudgments|Antonyms: wisdom, prudence, foresight|noun
