hands:/hændz/ | n. | 手；掌握；控制；帮助 | The plural form of hand.  The part of the body at the end of the arm that includes the fingers and thumb.  Power, control, or possession. Assistance or aid. |
He held her hands gently. 他轻轻地握着她的手。

She has the company in her hands. 她掌握着这家公司。

Can you give me a hand with these boxes? 你能帮我搬一下这些箱子吗？

| 近义词：control, possession, grip, assistance, aid | 反义词：incompetence, weakness, helplessness | 词性：名词
