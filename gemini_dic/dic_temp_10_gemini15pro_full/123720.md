moonlike:/ˈmuːnlaɪk/| adj.|像月亮的，月亮般的|Resembling the moon, especially in being pale, luminous, or round. |The landscape had a moonlike, desolate quality. 这片景色有一种像月球表面一样荒凉凄清的特点。|The child's face, pale and round, was moonlike.  孩子那张苍白圆润的脸像月亮一样。|近义词：lunar, silvery, pale, ethereal|反义词：sunlike, solar, bright, vibrant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From moon + -like.  "Moon" comes from Old English "mōna," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*mēnōn," related to Latin "mēnsis" (month) and Greek "mēn" (month, moon). "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," from Old English "-līc."
