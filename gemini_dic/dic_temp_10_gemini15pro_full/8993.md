antilibertarians:/ˌæntiˌlɪbərˈtɛəriənz/
noun
反自由意志主义者
Those who oppose libertarianism, a political philosophy that emphasizes individual liberty and minimal government intervention.
Antilibertarians argue that unregulated markets lead to inequality and exploitation. 反自由意志主义者认为不受监管的市场会导致不平等和剥削。
The antilibertarians proposed stricter regulations on businesses. 反自由意志主义者提议对企业进行更严格的监管。
near synonyms:  statists, authoritarians, collectivists
antonyms: libertarians, classical liberals
n.
