europhobe:/ˈjʊərəˌfoʊb/ | n. | 恐欧者；厌恶欧洲的人 | A person who dislikes or fears Europe, its people, or its culture. |  He's a confirmed Europhobe and voted against joining the EU. 他是一个坚定的恐欧者，投票反对加入欧盟。

Europhobe:/ˈjʊərəˌfoʊb/ | adj. | 仇视欧洲的；恐欧的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a Europhobe. | His Europhobe sentiments are well known. 他仇视欧洲的情绪是众所周知的。

Etymology: euro- (from Europe) + -phobe (from Greek phobos, meaning "fear")

Synonyms: anti-European

Antonyms: Europhile
