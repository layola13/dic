noctambulism: /nɒkˈtæmbjʊlɪzəm/ /nɑːkˈtæmbjʊlɪzəm/
n.
梦游症
sleepwalking, walking or performing other actions during sleep
Example: The child's noctambulism worried his parents.  这孩子的梦游症使他的父母很担心。
Example:  She had an episode of noctambulism and woke up in the kitchen. 她梦游了一次，醒来时发现自己在厨房里。
近义词: sleepwalking, somnambulism
反义词: wakefulness
词性: noun


noctambulant: /nɒkˈtæmbjʊlənt/ /nɑːkˈtæmbjʊlənt/
adj.
梦游的
relating to or characterized by sleepwalking
Example: Noctambulant behavior can be dangerous. 梦游行为可能很危险。
Example: He has a history of noctambulant episodes. 他有梦游的病史。
近义词: sleepwalking, somnambulistic
反义词: awake
词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin *noctambulus* (sleepwalking), from *nox* (night) + *-ambulus* (walking).
