flakeyer:/ˈfleɪkiər/ | n. |片状剥落者，剥落器| A person or thing that causes something to flake or peel off. | The paint flakeyer helped remove the old paint from the wall. 油漆剥落器帮助去除了墙上的旧油漆。| synonyms: scraper, remover | antonyms: sealant, adhesive | noun

flakey:/ˈfleɪki/ | adj. |薄片的，易剥落的，古怪的，不靠谱的|Tending to flake or peel off; eccentric or unreliable. |例句1：The pie crust was too flakey and crumbled everywhere.  馅饼皮太酥了，碎得到处都是。
