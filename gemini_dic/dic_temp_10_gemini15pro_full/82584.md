guarder:/ˈɡɑːrdər/ | n. | 守卫者，保护者，保管者 | Someone who guards or protects something or someone. | The guarder of the museum made sure no one touched the exhibits.  博物馆的守卫确保没有人触摸展品。 | 近义词：guardian, protector, custodian, keeper | 反义词：attacker, assailant | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "guard" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns).  "Guard" comes from Old North French "garder" (to keep, watch, guard), ultimately from Frankish *wardōn.
