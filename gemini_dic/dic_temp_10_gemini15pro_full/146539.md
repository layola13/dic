overglamourizing: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
过度美化；过分渲染
Presenting or portraying something as more glamorous or attractive than it actually is.
The advertisement was accused of overglamourizing the effects of the diet pills.  这则广告被指责过分夸大了减肥药的效果。

The movie overglamourizes the life of a rock star. 这部电影过度美化了摇滚明星的生活。

近义词: exaggerating, romanticizing, idealizing
反义词: understating, downplaying

词性: verb (present participle)
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "glamour" (noun meaning "attractive or exciting quality") + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs from nouns or adjectives) + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
