disagreeableness:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəbəlnəs/
n.
不愉快, 不合意, 难以相处
the trait of being bad-tempered and uncooperative.
Her disagreeableness made it difficult for her to make friends. 她的不友善让她很难交朋友。
unpleasantness, hostility, bad temper
agreeableness, pleasantness
noun
