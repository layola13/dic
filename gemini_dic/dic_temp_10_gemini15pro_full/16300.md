bara:/ˈbɑːrə/ | n. |  rose (especially in poetry or other contexts with literary or symbolic connotations)| A rose, often used in a more formal or poetic context than the common word "rose." |例句：The poet compared his love's cheeks to a bara. 诗人把爱人的脸颊比作玫瑰。

例句：The bara bloomed in the deserted garden. 玫瑰在荒芜的花园里盛开。 | 近义词：rose, blossom, flower | 反义词:weed, decay | 词性:noun


词源:
From Persian بَرگ (barg, “leaf, petal”) or Arabic وَرْدَة (warda, “rose”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wṛdʰ- (“to grow”). Related to English wort.
