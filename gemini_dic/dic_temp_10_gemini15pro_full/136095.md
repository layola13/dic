nonrecoupable:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈkuː.pə.bəl/
adjective
不可收回的，不可补偿的
Not able to be recouped or recovered (especially of an expense).
例句：The nonrecoupable advance from the publisher helped the author finish the book.  这笔不可收回的预付款帮助作者完成了这本书。

例句：Nonrecoupable expenses for the project included travel and marketing.  该项目的不可收回费用包括差旅费和营销费。
近义词：nonrefundable, unrecoverable, irretrievable
反义词：recoupable, recoverable, refundable
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + recoupable, from recoup (from French recouper, "to cut back, to recover") + -able.
