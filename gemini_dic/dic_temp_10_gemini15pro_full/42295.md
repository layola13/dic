counter-interrogation:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˌtɛrəˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 反讯问，反审讯 | The process of interrogating someone who has already interrogated you or someone on your side. It often involves turning the tables and questioning the interrogator's motives, methods, or evidence. | The lawyer subjected the witness to a rigorous counter-interrogation, exposing inconsistencies in his testimony. 律师对证人进行了严格的反讯问，揭露了他证词中的矛盾之处。|  The detective's counter-interrogation revealed the suspect's attempt to manipulate the investigation.  侦探的反讯问揭露了嫌疑犯试图操纵调查的企图。 | Synonyms: cross-examination, counter-questioning | Antonyms: interrogation, questioning | Noun
