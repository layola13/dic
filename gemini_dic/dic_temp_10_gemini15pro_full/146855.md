overindulgent: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒənt/ | adj. | 过分放纵的，溺爱的 | Excessively indulgent; allowing oneself or another person to have or do too much of something enjoyable, especially food or drink. | He gave the children an overindulgent look, then handed them each a large scoop of ice cream. 他宠溺地看了孩子们一眼，然后给他们每人一大勺冰淇淋。|  She admitted to being overindulgent with her grandchildren, showering them with gifts. 她承认自己太溺爱孙辈了，总是给他们买很多礼物。 | 近义词: pampering, indulgent, permissive | 反义词: strict, austere, disciplined | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From over- + indulgent.  "Indulgent" comes from Late Latin *indulgentem (nominative indulgens), present participle of indulgere "to be kind or tender to, yield to, gratify," from in- "in" + dulgere "be sweet or pleasant."
