impersuasible:/ˌɪmpərˈsweɪzəbl/ | adj. | 不可说服的，固执的 | Not easily persuaded; unyielding to argument or entreaties. |  He was absolutely impersuasible and refused to change his mind. 他绝对是不可说服的，拒绝改变主意。| synonyms: adamant, inflexible, obstinate, unmovable | antonyms: persuadable, flexible, compliant, susceptible | adjective

impersuasible:/ˌɪmpərˈsweɪzəbl/ | adj. | 顽固的 | Stubbornly unwilling to change one's opinion or decision. |  She remained impersuasible despite all our efforts to convince her. 尽管我们尽力说服她，但她仍然固执己见。| synonyms: headstrong, willful, intractable, unyielding | antonyms: amenable, open-minded, receptive, persuadable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impersuasibilis, from in- "not" + persuasibilis "persuadable," from persuadere "to persuade."
