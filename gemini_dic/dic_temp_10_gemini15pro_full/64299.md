equivalved:/ˌikwɪˈvælvd/
adjective
等壳的；两壳相等的
Having two valves or halves that are equal in size and shape.
The mussel is an equivalved bivalve mollusc.  这只贻贝是一种等壳的双壳软体动物。
The equivalved shells of the clam snapped shut. 蛤蜊的两片等大的壳猛地合上了。

反义词: inequivalved
词性: adjective
