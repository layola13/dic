NEPA:/ˈniːpə/| n.|美国国家环境政策法 (National Environmental Policy Act)|A United States environmental law that promotes the enhancement of the environment and established the President's Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ).|The NEPA review process can be lengthy and complex. 国家环境政策法的审查过程可能漫长而复杂。|Synonym: environmental law, environmental act|Antonym: deregulation|noun
