desman:/ˈdɛzmən/ | n. | 麝鼠 | A small semiaquatic, insectivorous mammal of the family Talpidae, found in parts of Europe and Asia.  It has a long, sensitive snout and webbed feet. | The desman is a shy creature, rarely seen by humans. 麝鼠是一种害羞的生物，很少被人看到。 | The Pyrenean desman is endangered due to habitat loss. 比利牛斯麝鼠由于栖息地丧失而濒临灭绝。 | 近义词:mole shrew (loosely) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

desman:/ˈdɛzmən/ | n. | 鼹鼠 | (Archaic) A mole. |  In older texts, "desman" may refer to any mole-like creature. 在较古老的文献中，“desman”可能指任何类似鼹鼠的生物。| They trapped a desman in the garden. 他们在花园里捕获了一只鼹鼠。 | 近义词:mole | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
