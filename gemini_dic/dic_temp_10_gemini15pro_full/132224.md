nondecayed:/ˌnɒndɪˈkeɪd/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈkeɪd/
adjective
未腐烂的；未衰变的
Not having decayed or rotted; still intact.
The archaeologists discovered a nondecayed wooden artifact from the ancient tomb.  考古学家在古墓中发现了一件未腐烂的木制工艺品。
The nondecayed fruit was still fresh and edible. 未腐烂的水果仍然新鲜可食用。
近义词:undecayed, intact, preserved, fresh
反义词:decayed, rotten, decomposed, putrid
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix) + decayed (adjective)

"non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not".
"decayed" comes from the Old French word "decair" meaning "to fall, decline".
