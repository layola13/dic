myriapods:/ˌmɪriəˌpɒdz/ /ˌmɪriəˌpɑːdz/| n. |多足类动物|A class of arthropods having the body composed of numerous similar segments, most of which bear one or two pairs of legs. This includes centipedes and millipedes.|Centipedes and millipedes are myriapods. 蜈蚣和千足虫是多足类动物。|Synonyms: myriapodans|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps other arthropod classes like insects or arachnids)|Noun
