maskinonge:/ˈmæskɪnɑːndʒ/ (US), /ˈmæskɪnɒndʒ/ (UK)| n. | 麝香鱼 |A large freshwater game fish (Esox masquinongy) of the pike family, native to North America and noted for its size and voracity. |例句：The angler landed a massive maskinonge after a long fight. 垂钓者经过一番搏斗，钓到了一条巨大的麝香鱼。

例句：Maskinonge are apex predators in their freshwater habitats. 麝香鱼在其淡水栖息地中是顶级掠食者。|近义词：muskie, musky (informal abbreviations)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Ojibwe *maashkinoozhe*  meaning "ugly pike" or "great fish."
