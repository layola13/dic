nihilist: /ˈnaɪəˌlɪst/ (US), /ˈnɪɪlɪst/ (UK)| n.|虚无主义者； нигилист|A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles.|He's such a nihilist, he doesn't believe in anything. 他真是个虚无主义者，什么都不信。|
例句：
The nihilist denied the existence of any objective moral values.  这个虚无主义者否认任何客观道德价值观的存在。

The novel explores the dark and cynical worldview of a nihilist.  这部小说探讨了一个虚无主义者阴暗和愤世嫉俗的世界观。

|近义词：pessimist, cynic, skeptic|反义词:optimist, believer|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French nihiliste, from Latin nihil ("nothing") + -ist.
