panchayat:/ˌpʌnˈtʃɑːjət/ | n. | 村务委员会；地方议会 | A village council in India and other South Asian countries. |  The panchayat met to discuss the water shortage. 村务委员会开会讨论缺水问题。|  council, committee, assembly |  (No direct antonym) | noun

panchayat:/ˌpʌnˈtʃɑːjət/ | n. |  村务委员会成员 |  The members of a village council. | The panchayat decided to build a new school. 村务委员会成员决定建一所新学校。| council members, committee members | (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi पंचायत (pañcāyat), from Sanskrit पञ्चायत (pañcāyat), from पञ्च (pañca, “five”) + आयतन (āyatana, “assembly”).  Literally, an assembly of five.
