bibulousnesses:/ˈbɪbjʊləsnəsɪz/
n.
嗜酒; 酗酒
Given to or marked by the consumption of alcoholic beverages.  Excessive fondness for alcoholic drink.
He lamented his friend's bibulousnesses, which had led to health problems. 他对朋友的酗酒感到惋惜，因为这导致了健康问题。
The old sailor's bibulousnesses were legendary among the dockworkers. 老水手的嗜酒在码头工人中是传奇故事。
近义词: alcoholism, drunkenness, inebriety
反义词: sobriety, abstinence, temperance
词性: 名词

bibulous:/ˈbɪbjʊləs/
adj.
嗜酒的; 吸收的
1. Fond of or addicted to drink.
2. Highly absorbent.
The bibulous man frequented the local pub.  这个嗜酒的男人经常光顾当地的酒吧。
The bibulous paper towels quickly soaked up the spill.  吸水的纸巾很快就把洒出来的东西吸干了。
近义词: absorbent, porous, spongy (for absorbent sense); alcoholic, drunken, sottish (for fondness of drink)
反义词:  teetotal, abstemious, sober (for fondness of drink);  nonabsorbent, waterproof, impermeable (for absorbent sense)
词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin bibulus "drinking, fond of drink," from bibere "to drink."
