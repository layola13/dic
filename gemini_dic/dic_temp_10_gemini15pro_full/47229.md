dazy:/ˈdeɪzi/| adj.|朦胧的，眩晕的，迷糊的|Feeling confused and unable to think clearly, often because of tiredness, heat, or illness.|例句：The heat made me feel dizzy and hazy.  高温让我感到头晕目眩。

例句：I felt dazed and confused after the long flight. 长途飞行后，我感到昏昏沉沉，思绪混乱。|近义词：lightheaded, foggy, bewildered, confused, disoriented|反义词：alert, clearheaded, focused|词性:形容词
