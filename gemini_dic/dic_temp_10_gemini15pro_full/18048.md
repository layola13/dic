begrimed:/bɪˈɡraɪmd/
adjective
污秽的，沾满污垢的
covered with grime; very dirty
例句：The begrimed windows barely let in any light. 沾满污垢的窗户几乎透不进光。

例句：His begrimed face showed the day's hard labor. 他满是污垢的脸显示出一天的辛勤劳动。

近义词：dirty, soiled, filthy, grimy
反义词：clean, spotless, immaculate
词性：adjective


词源：From be- + grime + -ed.  "Grime" comes from Middle English *grime*, from Old Norse *grima*, "mask, visor."  The sense shifted from something that covers the face to dirt that covers something.
