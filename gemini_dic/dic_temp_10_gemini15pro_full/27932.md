cantilevered:/ˌkæn.tɪˈliː.vəd/ | adj. | 悬臂式的，伸臂式的 | Supported at only one end or on short piers, projecting beyond its support. |  The cantilevered balcony offered a stunning view of the valley. 悬臂式阳台提供了山谷的壮丽景色。| projecting, overhanging | supported, anchored | adjective


cantilever:/ˈkæn.tɪˌliː.vər/ | n. | 悬臂，悬桁 | A long projecting beam or girder fixed at only one end, used chiefly in bridge construction. | The bridge was supported by massive cantilevers. 这座桥由巨大的悬臂支撑。| beam, girder, bracket | support, pier | noun
