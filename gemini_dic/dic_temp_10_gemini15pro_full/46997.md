dashboards:/ˈdæʃbɔːrdz/| n.|仪表盘；控制面板；信息显示板|A visual display of the most important information needed to achieve one or more objectives; consolidated and arranged on a single screen so the information can be monitored at a glance. |The CEO checks the company's key performance indicators on the dashboards every morning. 首席执行官每天早上都会在仪表板上查看公司的关键绩效指标。|synonyms: control panel, instrument panel, console, display|antonyms: None|noun
