comfortableness:/ˈkʌmfərtəblnəs/| n. |舒适；安逸；惬意|The state of being comfortable; ease and freedom from pain or constraint.|She sank into the comfortableness of the sofa. 她舒服地陷进沙发里。|The comfortableness of the old armchair was inviting.  旧扶手椅的舒适感很诱人。|近义词：comfort, ease, coziness, well-being|反义词：discomfort, unease, distress|词性:名词

Etymology:
From comfortable + -ness.  "Comfortable" comes from Middle French *confortable*, from Late Latin *confortabilis* "strengthening," from Latin *confortare* "to strengthen much," from *com-* "with, together" (see com-) + *fortis* "strong" (see fort).
