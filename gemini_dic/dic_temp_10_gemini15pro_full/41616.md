corraled:/kəˈrɔːld/
verb
被聚集的，被围拢的
gathered together and confined, as in a corral; rounded up.
The cowboys corralled the cattle for branding. 牛仔们把牛群赶到一起烙印。
Synonyms: rounded up, gathered, assembled, herded
Antonyms: dispersed, scattered, released, freed
词性: Verb (Past Participle)


corral:/kəˈrɔːl/
noun
畜栏
a pen for livestock, especially horses or cattle.
The horses were kept in the corral overnight. 马被关在畜栏里过夜。
Synonyms: pen, enclosure, paddock
Antonyms: open field, range


corral:/kəˈrɔːl/
verb
聚集，围拢
gather together and confine (a group of people or things).
The police corralled the protesters in the square. 警察把抗议者围在广场上。
Synonyms: gather, round up, collect, assemble
Antonyms: disperse, scatter, release, free
词性: Verb
