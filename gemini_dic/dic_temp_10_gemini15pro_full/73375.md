foregrounds:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊndz/ | n. |前景，最显著的位置|The artist placed the figure in the foreground of the painting. 艺术家将人物置于画面的前景。|例句：The report foregrounds the need for improved safety measures. 这份报告强调了加强安全措施的必要性。

The company foregrounded its commitment to environmental sustainability. 该公司突出强调了其对环境可持续性的承诺。|近义词：emphasize, highlight, feature, spotlight |反义词:background |词性:名词

foreground:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊnd/ | v. |使突出，使重要|The article foregrounds the plight of refugees. 这篇文章突出了难民的困境。|例句：The director foregrounded the emotional aspects of the story. 导演突出了故事的情感方面。

The campaign foregrounded the candidate's experience in business. 这场竞选突出了候选人在商业方面的经验。|近义词：emphasize, highlight, feature, spotlight |反义词:background |词性:动词

词源:  From fore- + ground.  "Fore-" comes from Old English "fore," meaning "before," "in front of," or "former."  "Ground" comes from Old English "grund,"  meaning "ground," "bottom," "foundation," or "surface."
