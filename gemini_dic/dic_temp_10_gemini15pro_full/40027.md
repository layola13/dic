consumerization:/kənˌsuːmərɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 消费化 | The process by which products and services initially designed for businesses or specific industries are adopted and adapted for use by individual consumers. | Example 1: The consumerization of IT has led to employees using their own smartphones and laptops for work. 消费化IT导致员工使用自己的智能手机和笔记本电脑办公。

Example 2:  The consumerization of healthcare allows patients to have more control over their medical information and treatment options. 医疗保健的消费化使患者能够更好地控制自己的医疗信息和治疗方案。 | 近义词: popularization, democratization | 反义词: specialization, industrialization | 词性: noun
