orielled:/ˈɔːriəld/ | adj. | 装有凸窗的；有凸窗的 | Having an oriel or oriels.  An oriel is a bay window, especially one cantilevered or corbeled out from a wall. | The orielled room offered a stunning view of the garden.  那个装有凸窗的房间可以欣赏到花园的迷人景色。| Example 2: The orielled facade of the building gave it a distinctive character.  建筑物装有凸窗的立面赋予了它独特的个性。 | Synonym: bay-windowed | Antonym: flat-fronted | adjective
