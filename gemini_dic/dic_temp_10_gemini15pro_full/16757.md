barway:/ˈbɑːrweɪ/| n.|栅栏路，围栏路; barrier way的缩写|A barway is a gap in a fence or wall, typically closed by bars or a gate, that allows passage while preventing livestock from escaping. |We climbed over the barway and entered the field. 我们爬过栅栏路，进入了田野。|近义词：gate, entrance, opening, passage|反义词:wall, fence, barrier|词性:名词

barway:/ˈbɑːrweɪ/| n.|障碍通道; barrier way的缩写|A barway can also refer to a path or passage obstructed by obstacles or barriers. |The barway was narrow and difficult to navigate. 这条通道狭窄，难以通行。|近义词：obstacle course, hurdle, impediment|反义词:clear path, open road|词性:名词

注意事项:

词源：bar + way.  "bar" 指的是栅栏或障碍物，"way" 指的是通道或道路。


“barway”主要用于美式英语，尤其在新英格兰地区。


虽然 “barway” 的反义词可以是 wall, fence, barrier，但这取决于语境。如果 “barway” 指的是栅栏中的开口，那么反义词就是完整的栅栏或墙壁。如果 “barway” 指的是有障碍的通道，那么反义词就是畅通无阻的道路。
