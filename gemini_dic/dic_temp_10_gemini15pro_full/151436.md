parol:/pəˈroʊl/ | n. | 口头协议；假释；誓言；（尤指中世纪的）口令 | A promise or undertaking, especially one given as security.  A conditional release of a prisoner before the expiry of a sentence, on the promise of good behavior. |  The prisoner was released on parol.  这名囚犯获得假释。| synonyms: word, promise, pledge, oath, parole | antonyms:  breach, violation | noun

parol:/pəˈroʊl/ | adj. | 口头的 | Expressed or given orally. |  They had a parol agreement. 他们有一个口头协议。| synonyms: oral, spoken, verbal | antonyms: written, documented | adjective
