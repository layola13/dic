balaniferous:/ˌbælənˈɪfərəs/
adjective
产橡子的；多橡子的
bearing acorns; producing acorns
The balaniferous oak tree provided ample food for the squirrels.  这棵产橡子的橡树为松鼠提供了充足的食物。
The forest was balaniferous, with acorns covering the ground. 这片森林盛产橡子，地面上覆盖着橡子。
近义词: acorn-bearing, nut-bearing
反义词: barren, sterile
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *balanifer, from balan(us) "acorn" + -fer "bearing."
