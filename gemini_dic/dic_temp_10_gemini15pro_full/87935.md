hierarchy: /ˈhaɪərɑːrki/ (US), /ˈhaɪərɑːki/ (UK)| n.|等级制度；统治集团；等级体系|A system or organization in which people or groups are ranked one above the other according to status or authority.|The company has a rigid hierarchy.|公司有严格的等级制度。|synonyms: pecking order, ranking, ladder, chain of command|antonyms: equality, anarchy|noun

hierarchy: /ˈhaɪərɑːrki/ (US), /ˈhaɪərɑːki/ (UK)| n.|层次结构；层级|An arrangement or classification of things according to relative importance or inclusiveness.|The hierarchy of needs is a theory in psychology.|需求层次理论是心理学中的一个理论。|synonyms:  structure, organization, system, classification|antonyms: disorder, chaos|noun
