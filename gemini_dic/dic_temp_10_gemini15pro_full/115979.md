marsala:/mɑːrˈsɑːlə/ | n. | 马沙拉葡萄酒；马沙拉色 | A fortified wine from Sicily, similar to sherry or port. Also, a brownish-red color resembling this wine. | I ordered chicken marsala for dinner. 我晚餐点了鸡肉马沙拉。|  The walls were painted a deep marsala. 墙壁被漆成了深马沙拉色。 | 近义词:wine, color (depending on usage) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym. Could consider opposite colors if referring to the color, e.g., cyan, teal) | 词性: noun
