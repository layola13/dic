coagulability:/koʊˌæɡjəˈlɪbɪli/| n. |凝血性，凝固性|The ability of a liquid, especially blood, to change into a solid or semi-solid state.|The patient's coagulability was impaired due to the medication.  病人的凝血性由于药物作用而受损。|Near synonyms: clotting ability, coagulating power|Near antonyms: incoagulability, fluidity|noun
