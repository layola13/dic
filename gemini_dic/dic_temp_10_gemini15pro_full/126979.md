narwhal:/ˈnɑːrwəl/ (US), /ˈnɑːwəl/ (UK)| n. |独角鲸|A medium-sized toothed whale (Monodon monoceros) that possesses a long, straight, spirally twisted tusk extending forward from the upper jaw. This tusk, usually found only in males, can grow up to 10 feet long and is believed to be a sensory organ.|例句：The narwhal is known for its distinctive tusk. 独角鲸以其独特的长牙而闻名。

例句：Researchers are studying the function of the narwhal's tusk. 研究人员正在研究独角鲸长牙的功能。|近义词：unicorn whale, toothed whale|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
