edenized:/ˈiːdənˌaɪzd/
adjective
伊甸园化的，天堂般的
transformed into a paradise; made idyllic like the Garden of Eden.
The valley was edenized by the careful cultivation of generations of farmers.  山谷被几代农民精耕细作，变成了伊甸园。
The couple sought to edenize their suburban backyard. 这对夫妇试图把他们郊区的后院变成伊甸园。
synonyms: paradisiacal, idyllic, utopian, blissful
antonyms: dystopian, hellish, infernal, blighted
word type: adjective
