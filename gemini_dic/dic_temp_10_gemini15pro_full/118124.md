menorahs:/məˈnɔːrəz/
n.
烛台（犹太教节日光明节用的九枝烛台）; 七枝烛台（犹太教圣器）
A candelabrum used in Jewish worship, especially one with nine branches used during Hanukkah.
The family lit the menorahs on the first night of Hanukkah.  家人在光明节的第一夜点燃了烛台。
The museum displayed an ancient seven-branched menorah. 博物馆展出了一座古老的七枝烛台。
Synonyms: candelabrum, Hanukkah lamp
Antonyms: None
