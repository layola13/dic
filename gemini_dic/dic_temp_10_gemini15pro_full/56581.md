don't-know:/ˌdoʊnt ˈnoʊ/ |pronoun/phrase|不知道，不清楚|A phrase indicating lack of knowledge or information. |"I don't know where she went." 我不知道她去哪儿了。|"I don't know what time it is." 我不知道现在几点。|Synonyms:  unaware, uncertain, ignorant |Antonyms:  know, aware, certain |phrase

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I don't know what he wants. = 我不知道他想要什么。
She doesn't know where the library is. = 她不知道图书馆在哪儿。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm not knowing what to do. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)
He isn't knowing the answer right now. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I haven't known her for very long. = 我认识她不久。
They haven't known the truth until now. = 他们直到现在才知道真相。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I haven't been knowing him long. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)
She hasn't been knowing the answer all along. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I didn't know where to go. = 我不知道该去哪里。
He didn't know the answer to the question. = 他不知道问题的答案。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I wasn't knowing what to expect. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)
They weren't knowing the truth at the time. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I hadn't known him before the party. = 在聚会之前我不认识他。
She hadn't known the truth until her friend told her. = 直到她的朋友告诉她，她才知道真相。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I hadn't been knowing her for very long before we became friends. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)
He hadn't been knowing the answer until he studied. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I won't know the results until tomorrow. = 我要到明天才知道结果。
They won't know what to do if he leaves. = 如果他离开，他们将不知道该怎么办。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I won't be knowing the answer by then. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)
She won't be knowing what to do next. (Incorrect - generally not used in continuous tenses)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have known him for a year. = 到下周，我就认识他一年了。
They will have known the truth by the time the police arrive. = 在警察到达之前，他们就会知道真相了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been knowing him for a decade. (While grammatically correct, this sounds unnatural and is rarely used).

虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. = 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what to do. = 我希望我知道该怎么办。

被动语态 (Not applicable - "don't know" cannot be used in the passive voice)

否定句:
