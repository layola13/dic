hobbist:/ˈhɒbɪɪst/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbiɪst/ (US)| n.|业余爱好者；沉溺于某种癖好的人|A person who engages in a particular activity, especially one that is pursued for pleasure or relaxation rather than as a profession.|She's a keen hobbiest photographer. 她是一位热衷摄影的业余爱好者。|Synonyms: amateur, enthusiast, devotee, buff|Antonyms: professional|noun

hobbies:/ˈhɒbiz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbiz/ (US)| n.|业余爱好（hobby的复数）；嗜好；癖好|Plural form of hobby, referring to activities done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure.|His hobbies include hiking and playing the guitar. 他的爱好包括徒步旅行和弹吉他。|Synonyms: pastimes, interests, pursuits|Antonyms: work, profession|noun
