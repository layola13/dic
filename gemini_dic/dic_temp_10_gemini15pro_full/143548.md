outhastened:/ˌaʊtˈheɪsnd/
verb
超过；赶在…之前
To hasten beyond or ahead of someone or something.
He outhastened his rivals to the finish line. 他比他的对手更快到达终点线。
She outhastened the storm and arrived home safely. 她赶在暴风雨前安全到家。
近义词: outpaced, outstripped, outran
反义词: lagged behind, trailed

词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + "hasten" (verb meaning "to hurry")
