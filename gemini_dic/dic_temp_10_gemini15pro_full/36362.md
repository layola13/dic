coincident:/koʊˈɪnsɪdənt/| adj.|一致的，符合的，同时发生的|Happening at the same time or occupying the same space or position. |The two events were coincident. 这两件事同时发生。|近义词：simultaneous, concurrent, synchronous|反义词:non-concurrent, asynchronous|adjective

coincidence:/koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/| n.|巧合，一致，符合|The occurrence of events that happen at the same time by accident but seem to have some connection. |It was pure coincidence that we met in Paris. 我们在巴黎相遇纯属巧合。|近义词：chance, accident, fluke|反义词:intention, plan|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *coincidens*, present participle of *coincidere* ("to coincide"), from *co-* ("together") + *incidere* ("to fall into, happen, befall"), from *in-* ("in, on, upon") + *cadere* ("to fall").
