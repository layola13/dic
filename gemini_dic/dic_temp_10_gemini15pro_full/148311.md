oversolidified: /ˌoʊvər səˈlɪdɪfaɪd/
adjective
过固化的，过度凝固的
Solidified beyond the normal or desired degree.  The metal was oversolidified, making it brittle and prone to cracking.
The lava flow oversolidified, forming unusually hard rock.
近义词：overhardened, excessively solidified
反义词：undercooled, molten, liquid
词性: adjective

词源：
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "solidify" (verb meaning "to make or become solid") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
