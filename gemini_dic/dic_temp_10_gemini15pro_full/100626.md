intinctivity: /ɪntɪŋkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 内在性; 本质;  | The inherent nature or essence of something. The quality of being intinctive.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered non-standard or even incorrect.  It likely stems from "instinctive" and "inactivity" but does not carry a clear, established meaning. |  *It's difficult to define the intinctivity of this artwork.*  (很难定义这件艺术品的内在性。) *The intinctivity of her response surprised everyone.* (她反应的本质让每个人都感到惊讶。) | Near synonyms:  essence, nature, inherent quality, instinctiveness. | Antonyms:  externality, acquired characteristic. | Noun
