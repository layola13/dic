balloons:/bəˈluːnz/| n.|气球|A small bag made of thin rubber or other light material, inflated with air or gas, typically used as a toy or decoration. |The child loved playing with the colorful balloons.  孩子喜欢玩彩色的气球。|synonyms: inflatables, blimps|antonyms: weights, anchors|noun

balloons:/bəˈluːnz/| v. (third-person singular simple present balloons, present participle ballooning, simple past and past participle ballooned)|膨胀，激增|To swell or puff out; to increase rapidly.|The population of the city ballooned in the last decade.  这个城市的人口在过去十年里激增。|synonyms: expand, inflate, swell|antonyms: shrink, contract, deflate|verb
