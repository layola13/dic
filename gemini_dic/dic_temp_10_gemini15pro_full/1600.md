acid:/ˈæsɪd/| n. adj.|酸，酸的|A chemical substance that neutralizes alkalis, dissolves some metals, and turns litmus red; typically, a corrosive or sour-tasting liquid of this kind.|例句：Sulfuric acid is a very strong acid. 硫酸是一种非常强的酸。

The acid rain is damaging the ancient monuments. 酸雨正在破坏古迹。|近义词：sour, tart, sharp, acrid|反义词:alkaline, base, basic|noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acidus*, from acer "sharp, pungent, keen".
