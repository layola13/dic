hubba:/ˈhʌbə/| int.|哇，哇哦（表示惊讶或赞叹）|An exclamation of surprise or admiration, often related to attractiveness.  It's often used in response to seeing someone attractive.|例句：Hubba hubba! Look at her! 哇哦！看看她！

例句：Hubba hubba! He's looking good today. 哇哦！他今天看起来真不错。|近义词：wow, whoa, hey there|反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation of this type) |词性:interjection
