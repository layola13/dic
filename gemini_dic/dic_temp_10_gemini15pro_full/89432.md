honchoes:/ˈhɑːntʃoʊz/| n. |领导，主管，负责人|A person in charge; a leader or manager.  Origin: From Japanese 班長 (hanchō, “squad leader”). Borrowed into American English during the Korean War.|The honchoes of the company decided to implement a new marketing strategy. 公司的领导们决定实施新的营销策略。|Synonyms: boss, chief, leader, manager, supervisor|Antonyms: subordinate, employee, follower|Noun
