overcapacities: /ˌoʊvər kəˈpæsɪtiz/ | n. | 过剩产能，生产能力过剩 |  The state of having more production capacity than is needed to meet demand.  This often leads to lower prices and reduced profitability. |  The steel industry is struggling with chronic overcapacities. 钢铁行业正与长期产能过剩作斗争。

The airline industry suffered from significant overcapacities after the pandemic. 疫情过后，航空业产能严重过剩。 | 近义词：excess capacity, surplus capacity | 反义词: undercapacity, shortage of capacity | noun
