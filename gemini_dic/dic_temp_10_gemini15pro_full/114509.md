maladministration:/ˌmælədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
n.
管理不善，治理不当，滥权
Bad management or governance; dishonest or incompetent administration.
The report revealed widespread maladministration within the government. 这份报告揭露了政府内部普遍存在的管理不善。
The committee was set up to investigate allegations of maladministration.  该委员会的成立是为了调查关于管理不善的指控。
近义词:mismanagement, misconduct, corruption, abuse of power
反义词:good governance, sound management
词性:名词
