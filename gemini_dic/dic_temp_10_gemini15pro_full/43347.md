cramps:/kræmps/ | n. | 痉挛，抽筋 |  A sudden, painful, involuntary contraction of a muscle or muscles. | I get terrible cramps in my legs when I run. 我跑步时腿会抽筋得很厉害。 |  spasms, contractions | relaxation, looseness | noun

cramp:/kræmp/ | n. |  限制，束缚；狭窄的空间 | Something that restricts or confines. |  Lack of funding is cramping our research efforts. 资金短缺限制了我们的研究工作。 | restriction, constraint | freedom, liberty | noun

cramp:/kræmp/ | v. |  限制，束缚；使…抽筋 | To restrict or confine; to cause a muscle to contract painfully and involuntarily.  | The small apartment cramped his style. 这间小公寓限制了他的生活方式。 | restrict, confine | free, liberate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cramp or *crump, akin to Old High German krampf, from a Germanic base meaning "to draw together, contract."
