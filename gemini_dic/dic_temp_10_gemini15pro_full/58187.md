drowsy:/ˈdraʊzi/ | adj. | 昏昏欲睡的，困倦的 | Sleepy and lethargic. | The warm room made me feel drowsy. 温暖的房间让我感到昏昏欲睡。 | synonyms: sleepy, lethargic, heavy-eyed, dozy | antonyms: alert, awake, wakeful, energetic | adjective
