carnifex: /ˈkɑːrnɪfɛks/ | n. |刽子手，行刑者 | An executioner; a person whose job was to kill criminals or prisoners, especially by beheading them. |  In ancient Rome, the carnifex was a despised figure. 在古罗马，刽子手是一个被人鄙视的人物。

The carnifex prepared for the execution. 刽子手准备行刑。 | 近义词: executioner, headsman | 反义词: savior, rescuer | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *carnifex*, from *caro* ("flesh") + *facere* ("to make, do").  Literally "flesh-maker," likely a euphemism.
