interclusion:/ˌɪntərˈkluːʒən/
n.|相互闭塞，咬合过紧|The interclusion of the teeth made it difficult for him to chew properly. 牙齿咬合过紧使他难以正常咀嚼。|
Etymology: From Latin "inter" (between) + "claudere" (to close).|
例句：The dentist diagnosed the patient with interclusion. 牙医诊断病人患有咬合过紧。

例句：The interclusion of the front teeth caused speech problems.  前牙咬合过紧导致了说话问题。

近义词：malocclusion, overbite, crossbite|
反义词：open bite|
词性：noun
