maneuverability:/məˌnuːvərəˈbɪləti/ /məˌnuːvərəˈbɪlɪti/| n. |机动性，可操作性|The quality of being easy to maneuver; ease of steering or directing.  The ability of something, especially a vehicle or aircraft, to be moved easily and accurately.|The fighter jet's maneuverability gave it an edge in dogfights.  这架战斗机的机动性使它在空战中占据优势。|Synonyms: agility, flexibility, mobility, nimbleness|Antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, inflexibility|noun
