inconvertible:/ˌɪnkənˈvɜːrtəbl/ /ˌɪnkənˈvɜːrtɪbl/
adjective
不可兑换的；不能变换的；固执的
not able to be converted.
例句：The currency of many countries is now inconvertible. 许多国家的货币现在是不可兑换的。

例句：He has an inconvertible belief in his own righteousness. 他对自己正直的信念坚定不移。
近义词：unchangeable, fixed, rigid, inflexible, unalterable
反义词：convertible, changeable, flexible, adaptable
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + convertible (capable of being converted), from Late Latin convertibilis, from convertere (to turn around, change).
