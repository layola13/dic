applique:/ˌæplɪˈkeɪ/ /ˌæplɪˈkeɪ/ | n. v.|贴花，装饰；贴花工艺；|A decorative design made of one material sewn or glued over another.|She wore a dress with delicate lace appliqués. 她穿着一件有精致蕾丝贴花的连衣裙。|近义词：patch, inlay, ornamentation|反义词: N/A|noun
applique:/ˌæplɪˈkeɪ/ /ˌæplɪˈkeɪ/ | v. |缝贴，粘贴；|To sew or glue one material over another as decoration.|She appliqued flowers onto the quilt. 她把花贴在被子上。|近义词：attach, affix, sew on|反义词: detach, remove|verb
