candied:/ˈkændid/ | adj. | 蜜饯的；糖渍的；甜言蜜语的 | Preserved in or encrusted with sugar.  Figuratively, it can also mean excessively sweet or sentimental. | The candied fruit was a delicious addition to the cake.  蜜饯水果是蛋糕的美味添加。 |  The politician delivered a candied speech, full of empty promises. 这位政客发表了一通甜言蜜语，充满了空洞的承诺。 | synonyms: sugared, glazed, crystallized, saccharine | antonyms: unsweetened, plain, savory, tart | adjective
