nonirrigating:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪˌɡeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非灌溉的
Not requiring or involving irrigation; not irrigated.
The farmer planted nonirrigating crops like sorghum and millet due to the lack of water. 由于缺水，农民种植了像高粱和小米这样的非灌溉作物。
The desert region is suitable for nonirrigating plants.  这个沙漠地区适合种植非灌溉植物。
近义词:dryland, rainfed
反义词:irrigated
词性:adjective
