oolitic:/ˌoʊəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |鲕状的，鱼卵状的 | Composed of oolites or resembling oolites.  Oolites are small, rounded grains of calcium carbonate formed in shallow marine environments. | The geologist examined the oolitic limestone. 地质学家检查了鲕状石灰岩。| The beach was covered in oolitic sand. 海滩上覆盖着鲕状沙子。|  近义词: granular, pisolitic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-granular or amorphous) | 词性: adjective
