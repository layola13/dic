outpolling:/ˌaʊtˈpoʊlɪŋ/
verb
在民意调查中领先
Surpassing another candidate in opinion polls.
The challenger is outpolling the incumbent by a significant margin. 挑战者在民意调查中大幅领先现任者。
The recent polls show the Democratic candidate outpolling his Republican rival. 最近的民意调查显示，民主党候选人的支持率超过了他的共和党竞争对手。
Synonyms:  surpassing, exceeding, beating, leading
Antonyms: trailing, lagging behind
词性: 动词 (v.)
