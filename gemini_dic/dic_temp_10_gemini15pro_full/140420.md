okapi:/oʊˈkɑːpi/ | n. | 霍加狓 |A giraffe-like African mammal with zebra-like stripes on its legs and hindquarters. It is closely related to the giraffe. |  The okapi is a shy and elusive creature. 霍加狓是一种害羞且难以捉摸的生物。 |  Okapis are native to the dense rainforests of Central Africa. 霍加狓原产于中非茂密的雨林。 | synonyms: none (unique animal) | antonyms: none (unique animal) | noun
