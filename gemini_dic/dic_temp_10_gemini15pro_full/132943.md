nonemendations:/ˌnɒnɪmɛnˈdeɪʃənz/| n.|不修改，不修正|Absence of emendations or corrections; a state of being unamended or uncorrected.  The manuscript remained in its original state, with nonemendations despite its numerous errors.|The editor's approach was marked by nonemendations, preserving the author's original text.|近义词:non-revision, non-correction|反义词: emendations, corrections|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "emendation" (from Latin "emendatio," meaning "correction" or "improvement").
