gymnosperms:/ˈdʒɪmnəˌspɜːrmz/
n.
裸子植物
Plants that produce seeds that are not enclosed within an ovary or fruit.  They are characterized by exposed seeds, often borne on cones.
Conifers, such as pines and spruces, are gymnosperms.  松树和云杉等针叶树是裸子植物。
Conifers, cycads, ginkgoes
Angiosperms (flowering plants)
noun
Etymology:  From Greek *gymnos* (naked) + *sperma* (seed)
