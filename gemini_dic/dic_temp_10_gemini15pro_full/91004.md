husbanded:/ˈhʌzbəndɪd/

husbanded:/ˈhʌzbəndɪd/
adjective|已婚的(指女性), 谨慎管理的|Managed or used economically; conserved.|Her husbanded resources allowed her to survive the economic downturn. 她谨慎管理的资源使她能够在经济衰退中生存下来。|thrifty, frugal, provident|wasteful, extravagant|adj.
