earshot:/ˈɪrʃɒt/ (British), /ˈɪrʃɑːt/ (American)| n. |听力范围；能听到的距离|The sound of their laughter carried within earshot. 他们的笑声在听得见的范围内传播。|She whispered, making sure no one else was within earshot. 她低声说着，确保没有其他人在听力范围内。|近义词：hearing distance, range of hearing|反义词: out of earshot|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'ear' + 'shot'. 'Shot' in this context refers to the distance a sound can travel, similar to the range of a projectile.
