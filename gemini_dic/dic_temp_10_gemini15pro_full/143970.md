outra:/ˈoʊtrə/ | adj. | other (feminine form in Portuguese and Galician) |  Used in Portuguese and Galician to refer to another (feminine) person or thing. | Ela tem outra casa. (She has another house.) | Synonyms: (In English) another, different, (In Portuguese/Galician) mais uma, diferente | Antonyms: (In English) same, identical, (In Portuguese/Galician) mesma, igual | Adjective
