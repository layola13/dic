interdivisionally:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˈvɪʒənəli/
| adv. |部门间地，跨部门地 | Between or involving different divisions of a company or organization. |  The company encourages interdivisional communication to foster collaboration.  公司鼓励部门间沟通以促进合作。 |
| 例句：The new software will be implemented interdivisionally.  这个新软件将在各部门之间实施。 |
| 例句：Interdivisional rivalries can sometimes hinder progress. 部门间的竞争有时会阻碍进步。|
| 近义词：between divisions, across divisions, company-wide |
| 反义词：intradivisionally (within a division) |
| 词性 | 副词 |
