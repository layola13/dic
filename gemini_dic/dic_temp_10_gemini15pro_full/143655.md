verb (present participle)
胜过亲吻；比…更会亲吻
Surpassing someone in kissing; kissing more or better than someone else.

He was outkissing his competition in the kissing contest. 在接吻比赛中，他胜过了他的对手。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, excelling
反义词: being outkissed
词性: verb (present participle)
