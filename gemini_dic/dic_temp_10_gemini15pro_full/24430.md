bristleless:/ˈbrɪsəllɪs/| adj.|无刚毛的，光滑的|Lacking bristles; smooth.|The bristleless brush was surprisingly effective at cleaning the delicate fabric. 这把无刚毛的刷子在清洁精致的布料方面出奇地有效。|近义词：smooth, hairless, glabrous|反义词：bristly, hairy, hirsute|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From bristle + -less. Bristle comes from Old English *byrst, related to German Borste. The suffix -less is a common Old English suffix meaning "without."
