overexuberance:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzuːbərəns/| n. |过度热情; 过度繁荣|Excessive enthusiasm or exuberance; unrestrained excitement or optimism, often leading to irrational decisions.|The dot-com bubble was fueled by overexuberance in the market.  互联网泡沫是由市场过度繁荣推动的。|The team's overexuberance after their first win led to some reckless behavior. 球队在第一次获胜后的过度兴奋导致了一些鲁莽的行为。| 近义词: ebullience, effervescence, exhilaration, frenzy| 反义词: apathy, indifference, restraint, moderation| noun
