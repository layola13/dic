impunibly:/ɪmˈpjuːnɪbli/
adv.
免于惩罚地，不受惩罚地
without fear of punishment; without bad effects
He continued to break the law impunibly. 他继续逍遥法外地违法。
Synonyms: with impunity, scot-free, safely
Antonyms: punishably, accountably
词性: 副词
