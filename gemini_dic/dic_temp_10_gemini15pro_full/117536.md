meetinghouse:/ˈmiːtɪŋˌhaʊs/| n.|礼拜堂；聚会场所|A building used for religious worship, especially by Quakers or other Protestant groups.  It can also refer to a building used for community meetings.|The Quakers held their weekly meeting at the meetinghouse.|Synonyms: church, chapel, house of worship, assembly hall|Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct opposite)|Noun
