gravewards:/ˈɡreɪvwədz/ | adv. | 朝向坟墓，走向死亡 | Towards the grave; in the direction of death. |  He walked slowly gravewards, his steps heavy with grief. 他悲伤地缓缓走向坟墓，步履沉重。| Synonyms: deathward, tombward | Antonyms: life-affirming, life-ward | adverb

Etymology:
From "grave" (Old English græf, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grabą, meaning "ditch, grave") + "-wards" (Old English -weardes, a suffix indicating direction).
