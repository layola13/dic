misbecoming:/ˌmɪsbɪˈkʌmɪŋ/
adjective
不合适的，不得体的
Not appropriate or proper; unbecoming.
Her short skirt was misbecoming for a funeral.  她穿短裙参加葬礼很不合适。
His misbecoming behavior embarrassed his family. 他不得体的行为让他的家人很尴尬。
近义词: inappropriate, unsuitable, unseemly, improper, indecorous
反义词: appropriate, suitable, fitting, proper, decorous
词性: adjective

misbecoming:/ˌmɪsbɪˈkʌmɪŋ/
adjective
不雅观的，难看的
Not attractive; unsightly.
The bright yellow paint was misbecoming to the old house.  亮黄色的油漆用在那栋老房子上很难看。
He wore a misbecoming hat that clashed with his suit. 他戴了一顶难看的帽子，和他的西装很不搭。
近义词: unflattering, unattractive, unsightly
反义词: flattering, attractive, becoming
词性: adjective


词源: From mis- + becoming.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating wrong or bad. "Becoming" originally meant "coming to be" or "suitable."
