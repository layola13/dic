hestern:/ˈhestərn/| adj.|昨天的，属于昨天的|Of or relating to yesterday; pertaining to the day past.|The hestern sun set in a blaze of glory. 昨日的太阳在一片辉煌中落下。|近义词：yesterday's, yester, bygone|反义词：tomorrow's, future|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *hestern, from Old English *ġiestran “yesterday's," from ġiestran dæg "yesterday."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gesteraną "yesterday." Related to German gestern "yesterday."
