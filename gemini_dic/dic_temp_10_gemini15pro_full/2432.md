adjudicate-upon: /əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt əˈpɒn/ | v. | 裁决，判定 | To formally judge and settle a dispute or contest, especially in a court of law.  | The panel will adjudicate upon the entries and announce the winner.  评审团将对参赛作品进行评判并宣布获胜者。

The committee was asked to adjudicate upon the merits of the different proposals.  委员会被要求对不同提案的优劣进行评判。 | 近义词: judge, decide, rule, arbitrate, determine | 反义词: defer, postpone, waive | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *adjudycaten*, from Latin *adiudicatus*, past participle of *adiudicare* (“to award to”), from *ad-* (“to”) + *iudicare* (“to judge”), from *iudex* (“judge”).


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court adjudicates upon such matters. = 法院对这类事情进行裁决。
The committee adjudicates upon all scholarship applications. = 委员会对所有的奖学金申请进行裁决。


一般过去时:
The judge adjudicated upon the case last week. = 法官上周对这个案件作出了裁决。
They adjudicated upon the dispute and awarded damages to the plaintiff. = 他们对争议进行了裁决，并判给原告赔偿金。


现在完成时:
The panel has adjudicated upon all the submissions. = 评审团已经对所有提交的材料进行了评判。
The board has adjudicated upon the matter and reached a decision. = 董事会已就此事件作出裁决并达成决定。


过去完成时:

The judge had already adjudicated upon the case before the new evidence emerged.  = 在新的证据出现之前，法官已经对案件作出了裁决。

虚拟语气:

I wish they would adjudicate upon this matter quickly. = 我希望他们能尽快解决这件事。


被动语态:

否定句:
