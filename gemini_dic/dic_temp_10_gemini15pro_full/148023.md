overroasted:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊstɪd/| adj.|烤过头的，烤焦了的|Roasted for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.|The overroasted chicken was dry and tough. 烤过头的鸡肉又干又硬。|The vegetables were slightly overroasted. 蔬菜有点烤过头了。|近义词：burnt, scorched, overcooked|反义词: undercooked, rare|词性:形容词
Etymology: over- + roasted. "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "excessively," and "roasted" comes from the past participle of "roast," which has roots in Old French "rostir."
