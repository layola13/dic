overthinks:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪŋks/ |v. |想太多；过度思考；|To think about something too much or for too long, especially to the point of causing stress or anxiety.|She tends to overthink things and gets anxious easily. 她容易想太多，很容易焦虑。| He was overthinking the interview and made himself nervous. 他过度思考面试，让自己变得紧张。 |近义词：analyze excessively, dwell on, ruminate, obsess over|反义词：underthink, simplify, let go, be decisive|词性:动词

overthink:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪŋks/ |n.|过度思考|The act of thinking about something too much.|Her overthinking often leads to sleepless nights. 她过度思考经常导致失眠。|Overthinking can be paralyzing. 过度思考会让人束手无策。| 近义词：rumination, obsession|反义词：decisiveness, acceptance|词性:名词
