leftwardly: /ˈleftwərdli/| adv.|向左地，朝左地|Moving or directed toward the left.|The crab scuttled leftwardly across the sand.  螃蟹向左横穿过沙滩。|近义词：leftwards, to the left|反义词: rightwardly, rightwards|词性:副词
Etymology: From "leftward" + "-ly."  "Leftward" is from "left" + "-ward."  "Left" comes from Old English "lyft," meaning "weak" (the left hand traditionally being considered weaker), and "-ward" comes from Old English "-weard," denoting direction.
