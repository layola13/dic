nonimmunized: /ˌnɒnˈɪmjʊnaɪzd/ /ˌnɑːnˈɪmjʊnaɪzd/
adjective
未免疫的
Not immunized; not having been made resistant to a particular infection or toxin.
The nonimmunized children were at greater risk of contracting measles. 未接种疫苗的儿童感染麻疹的风险更大。
Nonimmunized individuals are susceptible to various diseases. 未免疫的个体容易感染各种疾病。
近义词: unvaccinated, unprotected
反义词: immunized, vaccinated
词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix) meaning "not" + immunized (from immunize, meaning "to make immune")
