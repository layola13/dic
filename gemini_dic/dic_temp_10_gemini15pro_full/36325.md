cohosting:/ˌkoʊˈhoʊstɪŋ/| n. |共同主持|The act of hosting something together with another person or group.|例句：They are cohosting a charity gala this weekend.  他们这周末共同主持一场慈善晚会。

例句：Cohosting the Olympics can be a huge undertaking. 共同举办奥运会是一项巨大的事业。|近义词：joint hosting, shared hosting|反义词:solo hosting|词性:noun (名词)
