ferny:/ˈfɜːrni/ | adj. |蕨类的；多蕨的；似蕨的|Covered with ferns; resembling a fern.|例句：The path led through a cool, ferny glen. 小路穿过一个凉爽的、长满蕨类植物的峡谷。|例句：The garden had a lush, ferny feel. 花园里有一种郁郁葱葱的蕨类植物的感觉。|近义词：brackeny, fern-covered |反义词: barren, bare |词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From fern + -y.  Fern (n.) comes from Old English fearn.  The suffix -y forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."
