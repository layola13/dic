compacts:/kəmˈpækts/ /ˈkɑːmpækts/| n. v.|合约，协议；化妆粉盒；使紧凑，使简洁 (compact的第三人称单数形式或复数形式)|The machine compacts the trash into smaller cubes.  这台机器将垃圾压缩成更小的立方体。|近义词：agreements, treaties, powder cases|反义词： (作为名词) 无明确的反义词|词性: noun, verb


词源：From late Middle English (as a noun denoting a formal agreement): from Latin compactum ‘agreement’, neuter past participle of compangere ‘fasten together’, from com- ‘together’ + pangere ‘fix’.
