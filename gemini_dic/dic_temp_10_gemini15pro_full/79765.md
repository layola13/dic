gluten:/ˈɡluːtn/| n.|谷蛋白；麸质|A protein composite found in wheat and related grains, especially barley and rye.|例句：Gluten is found in many common foods. 谷蛋白存在于许多常见食物中。

例句：People with celiac disease must avoid gluten. 乳糜泻患者必须避免麸质。|近义词：protein 蛋白质|反义词: None (Gluten is a specific type of protein, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性:noun
