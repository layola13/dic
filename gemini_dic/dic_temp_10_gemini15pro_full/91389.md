hydrothermal:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. |热液的；热水作用的 | Relating to or denoting hot water, especially of geothermal origin. |  Hydrothermal vents are fissures on the seafloor from which geothermally heated water issues. 热液喷口是海底的裂缝，地热加热的水从中喷出。|  Hydrothermal alteration of rocks can produce valuable mineral deposits. 岩石的热液蚀变可以产生有价值的矿床。| 近义词: geothermal | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From hydro- + thermal.  "Hydro-" comes from the Greek "hydōr" (ὕδωρ), meaning "water." "Thermal" comes from the Greek "thermē" (θέρμη), meaning "heat."
