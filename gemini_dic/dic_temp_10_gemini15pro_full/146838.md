overinclining:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈklaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
过度倾斜的，过分倾向的
Having a tendency to incline too much; excessively inclined.
The tower was overinclining, making it dangerous.  这座塔过度倾斜，很危险。
The ship was overinclining to port in the storm.  在暴风雨中，船过度向左舷倾斜。
近义词:leaning, tilting, sloping
反义词:upright, vertical
词性:adjective
词源:From "over-" (excessively) + "inclining" (present participle of "incline," from Latin "inclinare," to bend, lean).
