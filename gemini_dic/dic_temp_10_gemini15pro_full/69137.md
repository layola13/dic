favosite:/ˈfeɪvəsaɪt/| n. |蜂巢珊瑚；蜂窝珊瑚石|A fossil coral of the genus Favosites, characterized by polygonal closely packed corallites.  It is a common fossil in Silurian and Devonian rocks.|The museum displayed a large favosite specimen from the Devonian period.  博物馆展出了一块来自泥盆纪的大型蜂巢珊瑚标本。|Synonyms: honeycomb coral, alveolar coral|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
