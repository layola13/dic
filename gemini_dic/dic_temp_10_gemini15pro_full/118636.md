messily:/ˈmesɪli/ | adv. | 凌乱地，杂乱地 | in a messy way; creating or covered with a mess |  He ate messily, dropping crumbs everywhere. 他吃得很乱，到处都是面包屑。|  The papers were scattered messily on the desk. 文件凌乱地散落在桌子上。| 近义词:sloppily, untidily, carelessly | 反义词: neatly, tidily, orderly | 词性:副词

词源: From messy + -ly.  "messy" comes from the earlier "mess," likely originating from Old French "mes," meaning a portion of food.
