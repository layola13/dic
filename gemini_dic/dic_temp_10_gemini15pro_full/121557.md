mismeter:/mɪsˈmiːtər/
noun
错误的韵律，韵律不和谐
A line of verse having an incorrect number of metrical feet or syllables.
The poem was marred by a mismeter in the third stanza. 这首诗因第三节中出现韵律错误而大为逊色。
近义词: metrical flaw, rhythmic error
反义词: perfect meter, correct rhythm
词性: n.

Etymology:
From mis- (wrong, bad) + meter (poetic rhythm)
