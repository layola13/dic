holostean:/ˈhɒləstiən/ (US) /ˈhɒləstiːən/ (UK)| n. | 全骨鱼类 |A primitive bony fish of the subclass Holostei, such as a gar or bowfin, having ganoid scales and a cartilaginous skeleton partly replaced by bone.  They are considered an intermediate group between chondrosteans (cartilaginous fish like sturgeon) and teleosts (modern bony fish).|Gar is a type of holostean fish found in North America.  雀鳝是北美发现的一种全骨鱼类。| The bowfin is another example of a holostean. 弓鳍鱼是全骨鱼类的另一个例子。|近义词:ganoid fish|反义词:teleost|词性:noun


Etymology: From Greek *hólos* (whole) + *ostéon* (bone).
