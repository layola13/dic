authorisation: /ˌɔːθərəˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌɔːθərɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US)| n. |授权，批准|The act of giving official permission for something to happen.|例句：You'll need authorisation from the manager to access these files. 你需要经理的授权才能访问这些文件。

例句：He received authorisation to proceed with the project. 他获得了继续进行该项目的授权。

近义词：permission, approval, sanction, consent|反义词：prohibition, denial, refusal|词性: noun
