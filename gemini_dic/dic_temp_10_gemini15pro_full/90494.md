huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/| n. v.|叫卖小贩，推销员；兜售，宣传|A person who sells goods, especially small items, in a loud, aggressive way, or who promotes something in a dishonest or exaggerated way. | The huckster at the fair was selling miracle cures.  这个集市上的小贩在兜售神奇的疗法。|Synonyms: peddler, hawker, salesman, promoter|Antonyms: customer, buyer|noun, verb

huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/| v.|兜售，宣传|To promote or sell something, often using aggressive or dubious methods.|He was huckstering his latest invention to anyone who would listen. 他向任何愿意听的人兜售他的最新发明。|Synonyms: peddle, hawk, promote, tout|Antonyms: buy, purchase|verb
