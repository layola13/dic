obconic:/ɒbˈkɒnɪk/
adjective
倒圆锥形的
Shaped like an inverted cone; cone-shaped with the point downward.
The stalactite had an obconic form. 钟乳石呈倒圆锥形。
近义词: inversely conic, cone-shaped
反义词: conic, conical
词性: adjective
