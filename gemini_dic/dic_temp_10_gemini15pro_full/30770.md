cesspit:/ˈsespɪt/ (US) /ˈsespɪt/ (UK)| n.|污水池，粪坑；肮脏或腐败的地方|A cesspit is a covered underground tank or pit used for the temporary storage of sewage, especially one that is emptied by pumping rather than by drainage. Figuratively, it refers to a place of moral filth or corruption.|The old farmhouse still relied on a cesspit for sewage disposal. 那座旧农舍仍然依靠污水池来处理污水。

The internet can be a cesspit of hate speech and misinformation. 互联网可能成为仇恨言论和错误信息的污水池。|近义词：cesspool, sump, latrine, mire, morass|反义词: paradise, utopia|词性: noun
