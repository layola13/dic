humbuggeries:/ˌhʌmbʌˈɡəriːz/
noun
欺骗；骗局；胡说八道
Deceptive or nonsensical language or behavior, often intended to trick or mislead someone.  It's typically used in the plural form.
The salesman's claims about the product's effectiveness were pure humbuggeries.  这个销售员关于产品功效的说法纯属胡说八道。
The politician's promises turned out to be a series of humbuggeries designed to gain votes. 这位政客的承诺结果是一系列旨在赢得选票的骗局。
synonyms:  deceptions, trickery, frauds, shenanigans, chicanery
antonyms:  truths, honesty, realities, facts
