conformably:/kənˈfɔːrməbli/
adv.
一致地，顺应地
in a way that follows the established rules, customs, or standards.
The rocks lie conformably on top of older strata. 这些岩石与较老的地层一致地叠加在一起。
近义词: accordingly, agreeably, compatibly, correspondingly
反义词: unconformably, discordantly, incompatibly
词性: 副词

conformably:/kənˈfɔːrməbli/
adverb
顺应地；一致地
in a way that is similar to or consistent with something else
New members are expected to behave conformably. 新成员应该表现得与其他人一致。
近义词: appropriately, suitably, fittingly
反义词: inappropriately, unsuitably, discordantly
词性: 副词
