albumin:/ælˈbjuːmɪn/
n.
白蛋白
A simple form of protein that is soluble in water and coagulable by heat, such as that found in egg white, milk, and (in particular) blood serum.
Albumin is essential for maintaining the correct amount of fluid in the blood. 白蛋白对于维持血液中正确的液体量至关重要。

Albumin levels can be checked with a blood test.  可以通过血液检查来检测白蛋白水平。
