mugweed:/ˈmʌɡwiːd/
noun
傻瓜，笨蛋
A foolish or stupid person.
He's such a mugweed, he fell for that obvious scam. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信了那个明显的骗局。
Synonyms: idiot, fool, moron, dimwit, simpleton
Antonyms: genius, intellectual, brainiac
