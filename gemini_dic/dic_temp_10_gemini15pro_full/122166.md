mistruth:/ˌmɪsˈtruːθ/
n.
不实之词；谎言
a statement that is not true
He spread mistruths about his political rivals. 他散布关于他政敌的不实之词。

近义词:lie, falsehood, fabrication, untruth
反义词:truth, fact, verity

词性:noun

词源:
From mis- + truth.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrongly," "badly," "unsuitably."
