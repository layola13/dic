anvil:/ˈænvɪl/| n.|铁砧|A heavy iron block with a smooth, flat top on which metal is shaped by hammering.|The blacksmith pounded the hot metal on the anvil. 铁匠在铁砧上敲打热金属。|Synonyms: forge, block|Antonyms: None|noun
