overattachment:/ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃmənt/ | n. |过度依恋，过度依赖|An excessive or unhealthy emotional dependence on someone or something.| She showed signs of overattachment to her mother. 她表现出对她母亲过度依赖的迹象。|近义词：dependence, clinginess |反义词: independence, detachment |词性:名词

overattach:/ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃ/ | v. |过度依恋|To become excessively or unhealthily emotionally dependent on someone or something.| The child overattached to his blanket.  这个孩子过度依赖他的毯子。|近义词：cling, depend |反义词: detach |词性:动词
