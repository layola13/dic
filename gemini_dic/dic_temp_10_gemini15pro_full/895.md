abuna:/ˈɑːbuːnə/| n.|主教;神父(尤指埃塞俄比亚东正教的)|A title of respect for a bishop or priest, especially in the Ethiopian Orthodox Church.|The faithful gathered around the Abuna to receive his blessing. 信徒们聚集在主教周围接受他的祝福。|近义词：bishop, priest, father|反义词: None|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ge'ez (Classical Ethiopic) አቡነ (ʾabunä), meaning "our father", from አቡ (ʾabu, "father") + ነ (nä, "our").  Ultimately from Aramaic ܐܒܘܢ (ʾabbūnā).
