go-through-with:/ɡoʊ θruː wɪð/
|phrasal verb|完成；实行；坚持做(尤指困难或不愉快的事)|to do something unpleasant or difficult that you have planned or agreed to do|例句：He went through with the wedding ceremony even though he was having second thoughts. 即使他再三考虑，他还是完成了婚礼。

例句：Despite the risks, she decided to go through with the operation. 尽管有风险，她还是决定进行手术。|近义词：carry out, complete, fulfill, execute|反义词：abandon, give up, back out|词性：动词短语
