go-around:/ˈɡoʊ əˌraʊnd/ | n. | 复飞; 绕行; 争吵; 分配 | An act of circling an airport prior to landing, typically because of another aircraft on the runway or bad weather. Also, a circular route or course. Can also refer to an argument or a distribution. | The pilot had to perform a go-around because of a deer on the runway. 飞行员不得不复飞，因为跑道上有一只鹿。

The traffic was so bad we had to take a go-around to get home.  交通太糟糕了，我们不得不绕行回家。

| 近义词: detour, argument, distribution, circle,  | 反义词: landing, agreement, straight path | 词性: 名词

go-around:/ˈɡoʊ əˌraʊnd/ | n. | 绕道; 迂回 | A detour or circuitous route. | We had to take a go-around because the main road was closed. 我们不得不绕道而行，因为主要道路封闭了。

There was an accident on the highway, so we took a go-around through the back roads. 高速公路上发生了一起事故，所以我们绕道从小路走。

| 近义词: detour, bypass, indirect route | 反义词: direct route | 词性: 名词


go-around:/ˈɡoʊ əˌraʊnd/ | n. | 争吵; 争论 | An argument or quarrel. |  They had a big go-around about who was going to pay the bill.  他们就谁来付账的问题大吵了一架。

We had a go-around about the best way to handle the project.  我们就处理这个项目的最佳方式进行了一番争论。

| 近义词: argument, quarrel, dispute, disagreement | 反义词: agreement, harmony | 词性: 名词



go-around:/ˈɡoʊ əˌraʊnd/ | n. | 分配; 分发 | A distribution or sharing of something. | There's enough cake for everyone, so there should be no need for a go-around. 蛋糕够大家吃了，所以应该不需要再分了。

There was a go-around of flyers advertising the new restaurant.  有人分发了传单，宣传这家新餐馆。

| 近义词: distribution, allocation, sharing | 反义词: hoarding | 词性: 名词
