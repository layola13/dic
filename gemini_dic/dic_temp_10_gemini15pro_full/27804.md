cannoned:/ˈkænənd/| v.| 猛烈地移动，快速移动|He cannoned down the stairs. 他冲下楼梯。|近义词：rushed, darted, sped, bolted, careered|反义词：strolled, ambled|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cannon + -ed. Ultimately from Latin canna "reed, cane."  The verb sense developed from the idea of a projectile being fired from a cannon.
