embolism:/ˈɛmbəlɪzəm/| n. |栓塞; 栓子|An obstruction of a blood vessel, typically by a blood clot or an air bubble.|The patient died from a pulmonary embolism.  病人死于肺栓塞。|Synonyms: clot, blockage, obstruction|Antonyms: flow, circulation|noun


embolisms:/ˈɛmbəlɪzəmz/| n. |栓塞（复数）|Obstructions of blood vessels, typically by blood clots or air bubbles.|Multiple embolisms caused the stroke. 多发性栓塞导致了中风。|Synonyms: clots, blockages, obstructions|Antonyms: flows, circulations|noun
