maelstroms:/ˈmeɪlstrəmz/ | n. | 大漩涡；混乱；动乱 | A powerful whirlpool in the sea or a river. Also used metaphorically to describe a situation of great confusion or turmoil. | The ship was caught in the maelstroms and destroyed. 这艘船被卷入大漩涡并被摧毁了。


The political maelstroms surrounding the scandal threatened to bring down the government. 这场丑闻引起的政治动乱威胁到政府的垮台。| 近义词: whirlpool, vortex, chaos, turmoil, turbulence | 反义词: calm, peace, tranquility, order | 词性: 名词

词源: From Dutch maalstroom, from malen "to grind" + stroom "stream."
