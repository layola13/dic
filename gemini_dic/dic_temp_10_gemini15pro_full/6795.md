anaplastic:/ˌænəˈplæstɪk/| adj.|退行发育的，间变的|Relating to or characterized by anaplasia, the loss of differentiation of cells and their reversion to a more primitive form.  This often occurs in malignant tumors.|The biopsy revealed anaplastic carcinoma.  活检显示间变性癌。|Synonyms: undifferentiated, dedifferentiated, primitive|Antonyms: differentiated, mature|adjective

anaplasia:/ˌænəˈpleɪʒə/| n.|退行发育，间变|A reversion of cells to a more primitive or embryonic form, often associated with malignancy.|Anaplasia is a hallmark of aggressive cancers.  退行发育是侵袭性癌症的一个标志。|Synonyms: dedifferentiation, reversion|Antonyms: differentiation, maturation|noun
