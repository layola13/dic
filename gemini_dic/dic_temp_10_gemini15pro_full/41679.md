correspondent:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndənt/ (US) /ˌkɔːrɪˈspɑːndənt/| n. |通讯员，记者，通信者|A person who writes letters to another person, especially on a regular basis.  A person employed by a newspaper, a television station, etc. to report news from a particular place.|例句：The war correspondent sent back harrowing reports from the front line. 战地记者从前线发回了令人痛心的报道。|近义词：reporter, journalist, writer, communicator|反义词:recipient|词性:noun

correspondent:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndənt/ (US) /ˌkɔːrɪˈspɑːndənt/| adj. |相应的，一致的，符合的|Similar or analogou.|例句：All rights and privileges correspondent to those of natural-born citizens. 所有与自然出生公民相对应的权利和特权。|近义词：corresponding, analogous, similar, equivalent|反义词:dissimilar, different|词性:adjective
