glaciations:/ˌɡleɪsiˈeɪʃənz/
n.
冰川作用；冰河时期
a process or period of time characterized by the presence or advance of glaciers
The last ice age involved several glaciations.  上次冰期包括几次冰川作用。

The Earth has experienced numerous glaciations throughout its history. 地球在其历史上经历了多次冰川作用。

近义词:glacial periods, ice ages
反义词:interglacials (间冰期)
词性:noun
