interdistinguished:/ˌɪntərˌdɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃt/
adjective
相互区分的；区别开来的
Distinguished from one another; differentiated.
The twins are easily interdistinguished by their different hairstyles. 这对双胞胎很容易通过不同的发型来区分。
近义词: differentiated, distinguished, separate, distinct
反义词: similar, alike, identical
词性: adjective

词源:
From inter- + distinguished.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "distinguished" means marked as different or separate.
