aeonial: /iˈəʊniəl/
adjective
永久的，永恒的
lasting for an immeasurably or indefinitely long period of time
例句：
The aeonial truths of mathematics. = 数学的永恒真理。

The universe is thought to have existed for aeons. = 宇宙被认为已经存在了亿万年。

近义词：eternal, everlasting, perpetual, timeless
反义词：temporary, transient, ephemeral
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek αἰώνιος (aiṓnios, “eternal”) + -al.
