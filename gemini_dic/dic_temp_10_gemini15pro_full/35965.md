codefendant:/ˌkoʊdɪˈfɛndənt/| n.|共同被告，共同被告诉讼人|A person who is charged with a crime along with another person or other people.|例句：The codefendant pleaded guilty to lesser charges. 共同被告对较轻的指控认罪。|例句：She and her codefendant were both sentenced to prison. 她和她的共同被告都被判处监禁。|近义词：fellow defendant|反义词:plaintiff, prosecutor|noun
