logoff:/lɒɡˈɒf/ (UK) /lɔːɡˈɔːf/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|注销，退出|The act of disconnecting from a computer system or network.|Please logoff when you're finished using the computer.  请在使用完电脑后注销。|She logged off the network and shut down the computer. 她从网络注销并关闭了电脑。|近义词：logout, sign out, disconnect|反义词：login, logon, sign in, connect|词性:名词，动词

词源：log (航海日志) + off (离开) - 原指从航海日志上签字离开，后引申为从计算机系统退出。
