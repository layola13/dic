decisive:/dɪˈsaɪsɪv/
adjective
决定性的；果断的；毋庸置疑的
Having the power or quality of deciding; putting an end to controversy; crucial or most important.
例句：The Battle of Gettysburg was a decisive victory for the Union army.  葛底斯堡战役是联邦军队决定性的胜利。

例句：She played a decisive role in the negotiations.  她在谈判中起了决定性的作用。

近义词：conclusive, definitive, crucial, pivotal, resolute
反义词：indecisive, hesitant, ambiguous, equivocal
词性: adjective
