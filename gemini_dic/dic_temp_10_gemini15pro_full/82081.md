grizzly:/ˈɡrɪzli/| adj. n.|灰色的，灰熊|A large, powerful brownish-yellow bear of North America.|例句：The grizzly bear is a formidable predator. 灰熊是一种可怕的捕食者。|例句：Her grizzly hair made her look older than she was. 她灰白的头发使她看起来比实际年龄大。|近义词：gray, greyish (for the adjective); brown bear, Kodiak bear (for the noun)|反义词：black (for the adjective);  (No direct opposite for the noun - perhaps "cub" for a young bear?)|词性:adjective, noun
词源：From the earlier *grizzled*, meaning "grayish," from the verb *grizzle* "to become gray."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grisaz*.
