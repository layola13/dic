hostal:/ˈhɒstəl/ (British English), /hɑːˈstɛl/ (American English)| n. |旅馆，招待所 (尤指西班牙语国家廉价的旅馆)|A hostal is a type of inexpensive lodging, especially in Spanish-speaking countries, similar to a hostel or inn. |We stayed in a charming hostal in the mountains of Spain. 我们住在西班牙山区一个迷人的旅馆里。|Synonym: hostel, inn, guesthouse, lodging|Antonym: hotel, resort|noun
