organophosphates:/ɔːrˌɡænəˈfɒsfeɪts/ | n. |有机磷酸酯；有机磷酸盐|A class of phosphorus-containing organic chemicals. Many are used as pesticides, herbicides, and nerve agents. |例句：Organophosphates are widely used in agriculture. 有机磷酸酯广泛用于农业。

例句：Exposure to organophosphates can be harmful to human health. 接触有机磷酸酯可能对人体健康有害。|近义词：OP compounds,  phosphoric acid esters|反义词: None (no direct chemical antonym)|Noun

Etymology->
From *organo-*  + *phosphate*. *Organo-*  indicates an organic compound, and *phosphate* refers to a salt or ester of phosphoric acid.
