erythrina:/ˌerɪˈθraɪnə/ | n. | 刺桐属；刺桐| A genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae, subfamily Faboideae, native to tropical and subtropical regions worldwide.  These trees are known for their bright red or orange flowers. | Erythrina crista-galli is the national flower of Argentina and Uruguay.  鸡冠刺桐是阿根廷和乌拉圭的国花。 | 近义词: coral tree (common name) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a genus of plants) | 词性: noun
