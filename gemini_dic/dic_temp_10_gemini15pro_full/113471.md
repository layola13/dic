lyre:/ˈlaɪər/| n.|里拉琴；七弦琴|A stringed instrument of the harp family having a yoke-shaped frame consisting of two curved arms connected by a crossbar, used especially in ancient Greece.|Orpheus played a golden lyre. 奥菲斯弹奏了一把金里拉琴。|近义词：harp, cithara|反义词:|noun
