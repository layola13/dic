gula:/ˈɡuːlə/ | n. | 糖; （印度等地）粗糖; 糖浆; | A sweet crystalline or powdered substance, especially sucrose, obtained from various plants, used as a sweetener in food and drink.  In some regions, especially India, it can also refer to jaggery or palm sugar. | Example 1:  I need two spoons of gula for my coffee.  我需要两勺糖加到我的咖啡里。

Example 2:  Gula melaka is a type of palm sugar popular in Southeast Asia.  椰糖是东南亚流行的一种棕榈糖。| Synonym: sugar, sucrose, jaggery (in some contexts), sweetener | Antonym:  No direct antonym, but consider "artificial sweetener" as a contrasting term. | Noun
