giglet: /ˈɡɪɡlət/ | n. | 轻佻女子；傻笑的女孩 | A frivolous, silly, or giddy girl, especially a young one who laughs a lot. | She was known as a giglet in her youth, always giggling and flitting about. 她年轻时被称为轻佻女子，总是咯咯地笑着，四处乱窜。| Synonym: flirt, minx, flibbertigibbet | Antonym: prude, matron | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gigelot, possibly from Old Norse *gikla "to tickle."  The word suggests a lighthearted and perhaps somewhat irresponsible young woman.
