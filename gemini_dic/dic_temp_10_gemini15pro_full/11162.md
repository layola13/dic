archetype:/ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/| n.|原型；典型|An original model or pattern from which other later copies are made, especially in literature, art, or mythology.  A perfect example of something. |例句：The hero is a common archetype in literature. 英雄是文学中的一个常见原型。

例句：She is the archetype of a successful businesswoman. 她是一位成功女商人的典型。|近义词：prototype, model, example, paradigm, epitome|反义词：antithesis, atypical example|词性: noun


archetypes:/ˈɑːrkɪtaɪps/| n. |原型；典型（复数）|Plural form of archetype.|例句：Jungian psychology identifies several archetypes in the collective unconscious.  荣格心理学在集体无意识中识别出几种原型。

例句：Many fairy tales feature similar archetypes, such as the wicked stepmother and the brave knight. 许多童话故事都有类似的原型，例如邪恶的继母和勇敢的骑士。|近义词：prototypes, models, examples, paradigms, epitomes|反义词：N/A (plural of archetype, so no direct opposite)|词性: noun
