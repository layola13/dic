exuviate:/ɛkˈsjuːvieɪt/ /ɪɡˈzuːvieɪt/
verb
蜕皮; 脱落
to shed (periodically) as an outer layer, such as skin, feathers, or a shell
The snake exuviated its skin.  蛇蜕掉了它的皮。
近义词: shed, molt, slough
反义词: grow, develop
词性: verb


exuviae:/ɛkˈsjuːviiː/ /ɪɡˈzuːviiː/
noun
(动物)蜕下的皮; (植物)脱落物
cast-off skin, covering, or shell of an animal (especially of an insect or crustacean); something shed or cast off
The exuviae of the cicada clung to the tree bark. 蝉蜕 clinging 在树皮上。
近义词:  skin, shell, covering
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "new growth")
词性: noun
