boyau:/ˈbɔɪoʊ/ (US), /ˈbɔɪəʊ/ (UK)| n.|地道；坑道；肠道|A narrow passage or trench, especially one used for communication between trenches in warfare; also, a natural or artificial tunnel-like passage, such as a narrow channel in a glacier or a section of the intestines.|例句：The soldiers used the boyau to move between trenches undetected. 士兵们利用地道在战壕之间不被发现地移动。

例句：The explorers discovered a boyau in the glacier, providing access to a hidden cavern. 探险家们在冰川中发现了一条地道，可以通往一个隐藏的洞穴。|近义词：tunnel, passage, trench, channel|反义词:barrier, obstacle|词性:noun
