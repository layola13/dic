deregulate:/diːˈreɡjuleɪt/
verb
解除管制，撤销管制
to remove government regulatory controls from (a business, industry, etc.)
The government plans to deregulate the airline industry. 政府计划解除对航空业的管制。
deregularize, liberalize
regulate, control
词性: 动词

deregulated:/diːˈreɡjuleɪtɪd/
adjective
解除管制的
freed from government regulation
The deregulated market led to increased competition. 解除管制的市场导致竞争加剧。
liberalized, uncontrolled
regulated, controlled
词性: 形容词

deregulation:/diːˌreɡjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
撤销管制，解除管制
the act of removing government regulations
The deregulation of the financial markets has been a controversial topic. 金融市场的放松管制一直是一个有争议的话题。
liberalization
regulation, control
词性: 名词

词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + regulate (from Latin regulare, meaning "to control by rule")
