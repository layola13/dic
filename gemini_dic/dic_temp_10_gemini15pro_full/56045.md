dobchick:/ˈdɒbtʃɪk/ (UK) /ˈdɑːbtʃɪk/ (US)| n. |小䴙䴘|A small diving bird with a short thick neck and a small tail, found on lakes and ponds. It is also called a little grebe.|例句：The dobchick dived quickly beneath the surface of the water. 小䴙䴘迅速潜入水面之下。

例句：We saw a family of dobchicks swimming on the lake. 我们看到一家小䴙䴘在湖上游泳。

近义词：grebe, dabchick|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dobbe-chik, from dobben (“to bob”) + chik (“chicken”).  The "dob" refers to the bird's habit of bobbing up and down on the water.
