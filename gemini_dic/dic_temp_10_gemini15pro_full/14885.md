ayme:/eɪm/ | n. | Aim, goal, objective, intention | An ayme is a desired outcome or goal.  It represents what someone is trying to achieve. | He set his ayme on becoming a doctor. 他立志成为一名医生。| Synonyms: goal, objective, target, purpose, intention | Antonyms:  lack of direction, aimlessness| noun

Etymology:  From Old French *aesmer, *aimer "to estimate, appraise, esteem," from Late Latin *aesimare,  from Latin aestimare "to value, appraise."  The meaning shifted to "aim, point at" in Middle English.
