mournfulness:/ˈmɔːrnfəlnəs/| n.|悲哀，忧愁|The mournfulness of the occasion was palpable.  这个场合的悲伤气氛很明显。|例句：The music's mournfulness reflected her inner sorrow.  这音乐的悲伤反映了她内心的悲痛。

例句：A sense of mournfulness pervaded the funeral service. 葬礼上弥漫着一种悲伤的气氛。|近义词：sadness, grief, sorrow, melancholy|反义词：joy, happiness, cheerfulness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mournful + -ness.  Mournful comes from Middle English *mornful, from mornen (“to mourn”) + -ful.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *murnan (“to care, remember, mourn”).
