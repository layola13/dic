biseptate:/baɪˈsɛpteɪt/
adjective
二分隔的；具有两个隔膜的
Divided into two parts or compartments by a septum.
The biseptate uterus is a common uterine anomaly.  子宫二分隔是一种常见的子宫畸形。
The pollen grains are biseptate. 这些花粉粒是二分隔的。
近义词: bilobed, divided
反义词: entire, undivided
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From bi- (two) + septate (having septa).  "Septate" ultimately derives from Latin *saeptum, meaning "fence" or "enclosure."
