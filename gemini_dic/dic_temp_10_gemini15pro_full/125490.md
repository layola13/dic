multiracial:/ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəl/ | adj. |多种族的，多民族的 |  Of or relating to people of several or many races. |  The school has a multiracial student body. 学校的学生来自多个种族。| synonyms: mixed-race, interracial, cosmopolitan | antonyms: uniracial, homogeneous | adjective

multiracial:/ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəl/ | n. |多种族的人 |  A person of more than one race. |  Many multiracials identify with more than one culture. 许多多种族的人认同多种文化。| synonyms: mixed-race person | antonyms: N/A | noun
