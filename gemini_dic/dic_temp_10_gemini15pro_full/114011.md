magmata:/ˈmæɡmətə/ | n. | 岩浆（复数）| Molten rock found beneath the Earth's surface, which can erupt from volcanoes as lava.  |  The magmata rose and filled the chamber. 岩浆上升并充满了岩浆房。 |  magma, molten rock, lava (when erupted) | solidified rock | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magmata, plural of magma, from Greek mágma "kneaded mixture, salve, plaster," from mássein "to knead."
