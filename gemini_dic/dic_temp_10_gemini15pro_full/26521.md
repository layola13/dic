cabbalas:/kəˈbɑːləz/ | n. | 卡巴拉，犹太神秘哲学；神秘教义 | A system of Jewish mystical interpretation of the Bible, first taught in the 12th century, based on a complex system of numbers, letters, and symbols. | He studied the ancient cabbalas for years, hoping to unlock hidden truths.  他研究古代卡巴拉多年，希望能解开隐藏的真理。 | 近义词: mysticism, esotericism | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps: rationalism, empiricism) | 词性: noun
