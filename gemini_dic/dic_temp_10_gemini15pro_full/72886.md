follow-up:/ˈfɑːloʊ ʌp/ (US) /ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ (UK)| n. adj.|后续行动，跟进；补充；续篇；后续报道|A further action or thing done after something else.|例句：I scheduled a follow-up appointment with the doctor. 我安排了与医生的后续预约。|近义词：continuation, sequel, review|反义词:precursor, introduction|词性:名词

follow-up:/ˈfɑːloʊ ʌp/ (US) /ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ (UK)| vt.|跟进；追查；继续|To continue or investigate something further.|例句：The police are following up on several leads in the case. 警方正在跟进此案的几条线索。|近义词：pursue, investigate, continue|反义词:initiate, begin|词性:动词

follow-up:/ˈfɑːloʊ ʌp/ (US) /ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ (UK)| adj.|后续的；补充的|Done, added, or given after something else.|例句：This is a follow-up study to the one conducted last year. 这是对去年进行的研究的后续研究。|近义词：subsequent, additional, supplementary|反义词:initial, primary|词性:形容词


USAGE->

词源:  "follow" + "up"，字面意思是“向上跟随”，引申为“跟进、后续”。

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I always follow up on my job applications. 我总是跟进我的工作申请。
She follows up with her clients regularly. 她定期与客户跟进。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am following up on that lead right now. 我现在正在跟进那条线索。
They are following up with the customer about their complaint. 他们正在就客户的投诉进行跟进。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
We have followed up on all the customer complaints. 我们已经跟进了所有客户的投诉。
He has followed up with the supplier about the delayed shipment. 他已经与供应商跟进了延迟发货的问题。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been following up on this issue for weeks. 我已经跟进这个问题好几个星期了。
They have been following up with the authorities about the permit. 他们一直在与当局跟进许可证的事情。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
