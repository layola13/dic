over-graded:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdɪd/ | adj. | 过度分级的，等级过高的 | Assigned a grade higher than deserved or warranted. | The students felt the exam was over-graded, making the curve unfair. 学生们觉得考试评分过高，导致曲线不公平。| The mineral was over-graded, leading to an inflated estimate of its value. 这种矿物被过度分级，导致对其价值的估计过高。| 近义词: overestimated, overrated | 反义词: under-graded, underestimated | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "graded" (past participle of "grade," meaning "to assign a grade or rank").  "Over-" 源于古英语 "ofer,"  "grade" 源于拉丁语 "gradus" (step, degree).
