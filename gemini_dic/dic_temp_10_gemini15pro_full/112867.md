lucrativenesses:/ˌluːkrətɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
获利丰厚；有利可图
The state of being lucrative; profitability.
The lucrativenesses of the various business ventures were compared.  比较了各种商业企业的盈利能力。
近义词: profitability, gainfulness, richness
反义词: unprofitability, loss


ETYMOLOGY->
From lucrative + -ness. Lucrative comes from Late Latin lucrativus "gainful," from lucratus, past participle of lucrari "to gain," from lucrum "gain, profit."
