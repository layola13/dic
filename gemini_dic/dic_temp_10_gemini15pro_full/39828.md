conspirator:/kənˈspɪrətər/| n. |共谋者，阴谋者|A person who takes part in a conspiracy; someone who plots with others, usually to do something illegal or harmful.|The conspirators were arrested before they could carry out their plan. 阴谋者在实施计划之前就被逮捕了。|Synonyms: plotter, schemer, collaborator,  intriguer|Antonyms: loyalist, dissident, opponent|noun

conspirator:/kənˈspɪrətər/| n. |同谋者，合谋者|A person who joins with others in a secret agreement to do something unlawful or harmful.|The conspirators met in secret to discuss their plan. 同谋者秘密会面，讨论他们的计划。|Synonyms: accomplice, confederate,  abettor|Antonyms: informant, whistleblower|noun
