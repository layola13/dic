egal:/ˈiːɡəl/ | adj. | 平等的，相等的 | Relating to or believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  Example 1:  The company strives to create an egalitarian workplace where everyone is treated fairly.  这家公司致力于创造一个平等的工作场所，每个人都受到公平对待。

Example 2:  They believe in an egalitarian distribution of wealth.  他们相信财富的平等分配。 | Synonyms: equal, equitable, impartial, fair, just, unbiased, nondiscriminatory | Antonyms: unequal, inequitable, biased, discriminatory, prejudiced, unfair, unjust | adjective
