hydrochlorate:/ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːreɪt/
noun
盐酸盐
A salt of hydrochloric acid.
Morphine hydrochlorate is used as a pain reliever. 吗啡盐酸盐用作镇痛剂。
synonyms: chloride, muriate
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a chemical compound)
