crones:/kroʊnz/| n. |老妇人，老太婆 (尤指丑陋或令人讨厌的)|An old woman who is thin and ugly.  A withered old woman.|The three crones cackled over their cauldron. 三个老太婆对着她们的大锅咯咯地笑着。|近义词：hag, witch, beldam|反义词:maiden, young woman|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crone*, from Old Northern French *carogne* (“carrion, carcass”), from Late Latin *carōnia* (“corpse, carrion”), from Latin *caro*, *carnis* (“flesh”). Doublet of *carrion*.
