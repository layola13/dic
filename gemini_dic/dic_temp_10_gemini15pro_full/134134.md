nonimperiousness:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəsnəs/ | n. | 不专横；不傲慢；谦逊 |  The state or quality of not being imperious; lack of arrogance or domineering attitude.  |  Her nonimperiousness made her a beloved leader. 她不专横的性格使她成为一位受人爱戴的领导者。 |  humility, modesty, unassumingness | imperiousness, arrogance, haughtiness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + imperiousness (the state of being imperious).  Imperious comes from Latin *imperiōsus*, from imperium (command, power).
