hexagynian:/ˌhɛksəˈdʒɪniən/
adj.
六雌蕊的
Having six pistils or styles.
The hexagynian flower has six distinct styles emanating from the ovary.  这种六雌蕊的花有六个从子房伸出的明显的花柱。
Hexagynian plants are less common than those with fewer pistils. 六雌蕊的植物比雌蕊较少的植物少见。
Near Synonyms:  six-styled, six-pistilled
Antonyms: monogynous, digynous
词性: adjective
