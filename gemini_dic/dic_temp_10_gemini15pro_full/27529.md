campuses: /ˈkæmpəsɪz/ | n. | 校园（复数） | The grounds and buildings of a university, college, or school. |  Many students live on campus during the school year. 许多学生在学年期间住在校园里。

Students gathered on the campuses to protest. 学生们聚集在校园里抗议。 | 近义词：grounds, sites | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: From the Latin "campus," meaning "field" or "plain."
