metalliform:/mɪˈtælɪfɔːrm/
adjective
金属状的；似金属的
Resembling metal; having the form or appearance of metal.
The mineral has a metalliform luster. 这种矿物具有金属般的光泽。
Near synonyms: metallic, metalline, metal-like
Antonyms: nonmetallic, non-metalline
词性: 形容词
