ambushment:/ˈæmbʊʃmənt/ |n.|埋伏，伏击|The act of lying in wait to attack by surprise; an ambush.|The soldiers prepared an ambushment for the enemy troops in the forest. 士兵们在森林里为敌军准备了一次伏击。|例句：The ambushment was successful, and the enemy was caught off guard.  伏击很成功，敌人被打了个措手不及。

The ambushment was planned with great care.  伏击计划非常周密。|近义词：ambush, trap, snare|反义词: - |词性: 名词
