libellee:/ˌlɪbəˈliː/
n.
被告; 被诽谤者
The person against whom a libel or defamation suit is brought.
The libellee denied all the accusations made against him in the newspaper article.  被告否认了报纸文章中对他提出的所有指控。
The libellee presented strong evidence to support his case. 被告提供了强有力的证据来支持他的案件。
近义词：defendant, accused
反义词：libellant, plaintiff
词性：noun

词源：From Anglo-French *libelle, from Latin libellus (“little book, pamphlet”), diminutive of liber (“book”).  The legal sense emerged in the 16th century.
