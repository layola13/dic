clattering:/ˈklætərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈklætərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |发出咔哒声的|Making a rattling or clattering sound.|The clattering train passed by the house. 咔哒咔哒响的火车从房子旁边驶过。|Synonyms: rattling, banging, noisy|Antonyms: quiet, silent|adjective
