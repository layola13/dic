heartbroken:/ˈhɑːrtˌbroʊkən/ (US) /ˈhɑːtˌbrəʊkən/ (UK)| adj.| 心碎的，伤心的| Overwhelmed by grief or disappointment, especially due to a failed romantic relationship.  Experiencing extreme emotional pain.| She was heartbroken when her fiancé broke off the engagement. 她未婚夫取消了婚约，她心都碎了。| heartbroken by the news. 被这个消息伤透了心。|synonyms: devastated, inconsolable, grief-stricken, wretched, miserable| antonyms: joyful, elated, happy, overjoyed| adjective
