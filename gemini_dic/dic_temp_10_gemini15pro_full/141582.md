oppression:/əˈprɛʃən/| n.| 压迫，压制，压抑|Prolonged cruel or unjust treatment or control.|The people suffered years of oppression under the dictator. 人民在独裁者的统治下遭受了多年的压迫。|Synonyms: tyranny, repression, subjugation, persecution, suppression|Antonyms: freedom, liberty, liberation, emancipation|noun

oppression:/əˈprɛʃən/| n.|沉闷，郁闷|A feeling of being heavily burdened, mentally or physically.|A sense of oppression hung over the city. 城市笼罩在一片沉闷的气氛中。|Synonyms: depression, gloom, dejection, melancholy|Antonyms: elation, joy, happiness, cheerfulness|noun
