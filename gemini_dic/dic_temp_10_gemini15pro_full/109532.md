注意事项:

"Less's" is a contraction best avoided in formal writing. Use "less is" or "less has" instead.  While grammatically understandable, its use can appear colloquial and less polished.  The examples provided above illustrating various tenses are often awkward and forced because the contraction simply isn't commonly used in those contexts.  Using "less" with standard verb conjugations is always preferred.
