helispheric:/ˌhiːliˈsfɪərɪk/ /ˌhelɪˈsfɪrɪk/
adjective
日球层的；太阳圈的
Relating to the heliosphere, the region of space dominated by the Sun's magnetic field and solar wind.
The Voyager 1 spacecraft crossed the heliospheric boundary in 2012.  旅行者1号宇宙飞船于2012年越过了日球层边界。

The heliospheric magnetic field protects the solar system from cosmic rays. 日球层磁场保护太阳系免受宇宙射线的侵害。


近义词: solar
反义词: (No commonly used antonym)
