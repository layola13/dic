hyperbolical:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪkəl/ (US) /ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːlɪkl/| adj.|夸张的，双曲线的|Exaggerated; of or relating to hyperbole.  Relating to a hyperbola.|例句：His hyperbolical claims about his wealth were clearly untrue. 他对自己财富的夸张说法显然是不真实的。|近义词：exaggerated, inflated, overstated|反义词：understated, moderate, realistic|词性:形容词
hyperbola: /haɪˈpɜːrbələ/ (US) /haɪˈpɜːrbələ/| n.|双曲线|A type of curve.|例句：The graph of this equation is a hyperbola. 这个方程的图像是双曲线。|近义词：curve|反义词: (No direct antonym for a mathematical concept)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hyperbolicus, from Ancient Greek ὑπερβολικός (huperbolikós, “exceeding, exaggerated”), from ὑπερβολή (huperbolḗ, “a throwing beyond, overshooting, exaggeration”), from ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, beyond”) + βάλλω (bállō, “I throw”).
