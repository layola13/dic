liableness:/ˈlaɪəblnəs/
n.
倾向；易于…的可能性；责任；义务
the state of being liable to something, or something that someone is liable for.
The liableness of the bridge to collapse caused concern.  这座桥容易倒塌的可能性引起了人们的担忧。
The liableness for the accident fell on the driver. 事故的责任落在了司机身上。
liability, tendency, susceptibility, proneness
immunity, resistance
noun
