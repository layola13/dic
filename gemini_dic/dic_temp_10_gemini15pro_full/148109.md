oversecularizations:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛkjələrɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|过度世俗化|The process of becoming excessively secular, often to the point of neglecting or rejecting religious or spiritual values.  The oversecularization of society has led to a decline in moral values. 社会的过度世俗化导致了道德价值的下降.|Example 1: Some sociologists argue that the oversecularization of Western societies has contributed to a sense of meaninglessness among individuals.  一些社会学家认为，西方社会的过度世俗化导致了个人意义感的缺失。

Example 2: The oversecularization of education has been a topic of debate in recent years. 近年来，教育的过度世俗化一直是一个争论的话题。| 近义词: secularism, worldliness|反义词: religiosity, spiritualism|词性:名词
