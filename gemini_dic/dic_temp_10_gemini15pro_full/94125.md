impedance:/ɪmˈpiːdəns/| n.|阻抗|The opposition to the flow of alternating current in an electric circuit, analogous to resistance in a direct current circuit.  It is a complex quantity consisting of resistance and reactance.|The impedance of the circuit is too high. 电路的阻抗太高了。|近义词：resistance, reactance|反义词: admittance, conductance |noun
