disaggregate:/dɪsˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 分解，分散，分类 | To separate something into its component parts; to break down a total into its constituent elements. | The data was disaggregated to show the results for each region. 数据被分解以显示每个地区的结果。

The company disaggregated its financial results by product line. 公司按产品线分解其财务业绩。

| 近义词: separate, break down, analyze, decompose, disperse, scatter | 反义词: aggregate, combine, unite, consolidate | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing reversal) + aggregate (to gather into a mass).  Ultimately from Latin *disgregare*, from dis- + gregare "to herd, flock," from grex "flock."
