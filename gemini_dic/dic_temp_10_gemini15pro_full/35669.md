cobble:/ˈkɒbl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːbl/ (US)| n. vt.|鹅卵石，用鹅卵石铺|A small, rounded stone used for paving streets.  To pave or mend with cobblestones.|The street was cobbled with smooth, grey stones. 这条街道是用光滑的灰色石头铺成的。|He cobbled the driveway with stones from the river. 他用河里的石头铺设了车道。|近义词：stone, pebble, rock|反义词: - |noun, verb
