grayscale:/ˈɡreɪskeɪl/| n. adj.|灰度，灰阶|A range of gray shades from white to black, used in photography and computing.|The image was converted to grayscale. 这张图像被转换成灰度。|近义词：monochrome, achromatic|反义词:color, chromatic|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology: gray + scale | From "gray" (referring to the shades of gray) + "scale" (referring to a range of values).
