discodactylia:/dɪsˌkoʊdækˈtɪliə/
n.
趾骨融合;指(趾)并指(趾)畸形
Fusion of the fingers or toes, a congenital anomaly.
Example: The infant was born with discodactylia, affecting two of his fingers.  这个婴儿出生时患有趾骨融合，影响了他的两根手指。
Example: Discodactylia can range in severity from a slight webbing to complete fusion of digits. 趾骨融合的严重程度从轻微的蹼状到指(趾)完全融合不等。
Synonym: syndactyly, brachydactyly
Antonym:  N/A (Antonyms for physical anomalies are not typically used)
词性: noun
