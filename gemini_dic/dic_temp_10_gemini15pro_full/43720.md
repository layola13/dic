credulous: /ˈkrɛdʒʊləs/ | adj. | 轻信的，易受骗的 | Having or showing too great a readiness to believe things. |  She was so credulous that she believed his every word. 她太轻信了，竟然相信了他说的每一句话。|  He is a credulous person, easily taken in by scams. 他是一个轻信的人，很容易被骗局所蒙蔽。 | synonyms: gullible, naive, trusting, unsuspecting | antonyms: skeptical, cynical, suspicious, distrustful | adjective
