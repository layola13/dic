leadsman:/ˈliːdzmən/ | n. |领港员，测深员 | A person who uses a lead line to measure the depth of water, especially in shallow water or near the shore. | The leadsman called out the depth of the water as the ship approached the harbor. 领港员在船只接近港口时报出水深。| synonyms: sounder, depthsman | antonyms: N/A | noun

leadsman:/ˈliːdzmən/ | n. |领路人，领导者 | A person who leads or guides others. (Less common usage) | He acted as the leadsman for the group of hikers. 他担任了这群徒步旅行者的领路人。| synonyms: leader, guide, director | antonyms: follower | noun
