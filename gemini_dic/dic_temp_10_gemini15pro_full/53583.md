disbud:/dɪˈsbʌd/ | v. | 除芽，摘除花蕾 | To remove buds, especially from a plant, to control its growth or shape. |  The gardener disbudded the roses to encourage larger blooms. 园丁摘除了玫瑰的花蕾以促进开出更大的花朵。

The grower disbudded the chrysanthemums to produce a single, large flower. 种植者摘除了菊花的花蕾以培育出一朵单一的大花。| 近义词: prune, pinch, nip | 反义词: graft | 词性: 动词
