catacrotic: /ˌkætəˈkrɒtɪk/ /ˌkætəˈkrɑːtɪk/
adjective
回降的；降支的
Relating to the descending portion of a pulse wave, especially the notch in the descending limb of a sphygmogram.  Characterized by a slow decline in pressure following a sudden peak.
The catacrotic limb of the pulse wave shows a small notch.  脉搏波的降支显示一个小切迹。

近义词:  descending, falling
反义词:  anacrotic, rising
词性: adjective
