glitterless:/ˈɡlɪtərləs/| adj.|无光泽的，黯淡的|Lacking glitter or sparkle; dull.|The old, glitterless Christmas ornaments were packed away in the attic.  那些旧的、没有光泽的圣诞装饰品被收在阁楼里。|例句：The dusty, glitterless mirror reflected a dim image.  满是灰尘、毫无光泽的镜子映出一个模糊的影像。|近义词：dull, lackluster, drab, matte|反义词：glittery, sparkling, shiny, shimmering|词性:形容词

词源: glitter + -less.  Glitter comes from Old Norse *glitra "glitter, sparkle."  The suffix -less is a native English suffix meaning "without."
