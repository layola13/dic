classes:/ˈklæsɪz/ (US) /ˈklɑːsɪz/ (UK)| n.|课程；班级；种类|A series of meetings in which students are taught a particular subject or activity; a group of students who are taught together; a set or category of things having some property or attribute in common.|

My son is in the top class at school.
我儿子在学校的尖子班。
