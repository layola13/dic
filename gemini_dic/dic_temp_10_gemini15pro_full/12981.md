astipulate:/əˈstɪpjʊleɪt/
verb
规定；约定；明确要求
to specify or agree to something formally
The contract stipulates that the work must be completed within six months. 合同规定，工作必须在六个月内完成。
specify, agree on, contract, require
dispute, disagree, contest
v.
