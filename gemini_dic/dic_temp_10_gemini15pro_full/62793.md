engrossing:/ɪnˈɡroʊsɪŋ/ /ɛnˈɡroʊsɪŋ/
adjective
引人入胜的；非常有趣的
absorbing all one's attention or interest.
例句：The book was so engrossing that I lost track of time. 这本书太吸引人了，我都忘了时间。

例句：She found the documentary engrossing and informative. 她发现这部纪录片引人入胜，而且信息丰富。

近义词：captivating, absorbing, fascinating, gripping, riveting
反义词：boring, tedious, dull, uninteresting, monotonous
词性：adjective
