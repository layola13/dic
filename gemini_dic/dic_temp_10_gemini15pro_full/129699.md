non-dom:/ˌnɒnˈdɒm/ (UK), /ˌnɑːnˈdɑːm/ (US)| n.| 非 domiciled 居民；非英国税务居民|A person who lives in the UK but is not legally domiciled there and who may be able to claim tax advantages as a result. |例句：Many non-doms are wealthy individuals who have chosen the UK as their primary residence. 许多非英国税务居民是选择英国作为主要住所的富人。

例句：The government is considering changing the tax laws for non-doms. 政府正在考虑修改针对非英国税务居民的税法。|近义词：expatriate, overseas resident|反义词：resident, domiciled individual|词性: noun
