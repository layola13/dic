anagrammatist:/ˌænəˈɡræməˌtɪst/
n.
字谜家，字谜爱好者
A person who constructs or solves anagrams.
He was a skilled anagrammatist, able to rearrange letters into new words with ease. 他是一位技艺高超的字谜家，能够轻松地将字母重新排列成新单词。
近义词:wordsmith, logophile
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes wordplay)


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anagrammateur*, from Late Latin *anagrammatisator*, from *anagrammatisare* "to form an anagram," from *anagramma* (see anagram).
