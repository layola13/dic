creekfish: /ˈkriːkfɪʃ/ | n. |溪鱼，小河鱼 |A fish that lives in a creek.  A freshwater fish inhabiting small streams. |例句：The creekfish darted among the rocks.  溪鱼在岩石间飞快地游动。

例句：We caught several small creekfish while wading in the creek. 我们在小溪里涉水时捉到了几条小溪鱼。|近义词：minnow, darter, small fish |反义词: ocean fish, saltwater fish |词性: noun
