christianlike:/ˈkrɪstʃənlaɪk/
adjective
像基督徒的；基督徒似的
Resembling or characteristic of a Christian; Christlike.
She responded with christianlike forgiveness. 她以基督徒般的宽恕回应。
He lived a simple, christianlike life. 他过着简朴的、像基督徒一样的生活。

近义词:Christlike, charitable, virtuous, pious
反义词:uncharitable, ungodly, impious
词性:adjective
