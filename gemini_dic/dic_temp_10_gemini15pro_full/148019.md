overripens:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpənz/ | v. | 过熟，熟过头 | to become or make too ripe | The bananas overripened on the counter. 香蕉在柜台上熟过头了。|The hot weather overripened the strawberries. 炎热的天气使草莓熟过头了。 | 近义词: overmature | 反义词: underripe | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "ripen" (verb meaning "to become ripe")
