asides:/əˈsaɪdz/| adv. n.|旁白，在旁边；撇开；|Meaning "to or on one side"  from a prepositional phrase meaning "on the side of," from Old English on sīdan.|例句：Asides from a few scratches, the car was undamaged. 除了几处划痕外，汽车没有损坏。|近义词：apart from, except for, besides|反义词: including|词性:副词，名词

asides:/əˈsaɪdz/| n.|（尤指演员对观众说的）旁白|From aside (adv.) + -s, by analogy with words such as besides. Meaning "remark made to the audience" from 1711.|例句：The actor made several asides to the audience. 演员对观众说了几句旁白。|近义词：whispers, comments|反义词: dialogue|词性:名词
