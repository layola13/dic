hearsal:/rɪˈhɜːrsl/ | n. | 排练，预演 | A practice or trial performance of a play or other work before it is presented to the public. |  The orchestra held a final rehearsal before the concert.  管弦乐队在音乐会前举行了最后一次彩排。| synonyms: practice, run-through, preparation | antonyms: performance, show | noun
