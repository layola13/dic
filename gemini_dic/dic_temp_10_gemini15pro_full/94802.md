impulsed:/ɪmˈpʌlst/ | adj. | 受冲动的；受刺激的；| Driven by a sudden, involuntary urge to do something. |  She felt impulsed to buy the dress, even though she couldn't afford it. 她一时冲动买了那条裙子，即使她买不起。

He impulsedly quit his job without another lined up. 他一时冲动辞掉了工作，而没有找到新的工作。 | 近义词：impulsive, spontaneous, rash, hasty | 反义词：considered, deliberate, planned, cautious | 词性：形容词

词源: From the past participle of the verb "impulse," derived from the Latin "impulsus," meaning "pushed against" or "driven."
