chiefships:/ˈtʃiːfʃɪps/
n.
首领地位；酋长职位；首领的职权
The position or office of a chief.
Several chiefships were contested in the recent tribal elections. 最近的部落选举中，几个首领职位都受到了竞争。
leadership, headship, chieftaincy, authority, command
subordination, subservience

词性: 名词 (Noun)
