overgrateful: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪtfəl/
adjective
过分感激的，感激过头的
excessively grateful; more grateful than is necessary or appropriate.
例句：I felt overgrateful for his help, as it was a minor task. 我对他的帮助感到过分感激，因为那只是一件小事。

例句：She was overgrateful for the small gift, making me feel a bit embarrassed. 她对这件小礼物表现得过分感激，让我觉得有点尴尬。

近义词：excessively grateful, too thankful
反义词：ungrateful, unappreciative
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "grateful" (from Latin "gratus" meaning "pleasing, thankful")
