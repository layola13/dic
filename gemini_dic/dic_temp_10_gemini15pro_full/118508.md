mesobiology:/ˌmiːzoʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/| n.|中生物学|The study of organisms of intermediate size, especially those of a size between microscopic and macroscopic.  It often focuses on the community level of organization.|Mesobiology plays a crucial role in understanding ecosystem dynamics. 中生物学在理解生态系统动力学方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:community ecology|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
meso- (Greek μέσος mesos, "middle") + bio- (Greek βίος bios, "life") + -logy (Greek -λογία -logia, "study of")
