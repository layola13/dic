maccabean: /ˌmækəˈbiːən/| n. adj.| 马加比家族成员；马加比家族的支持者；犹太起义的参与者|A member or supporter of the Maccabees, a Jewish rebel group that fought against the Seleucid Empire in the 2nd century BC; also, pertaining to this rebellion.|The Maccabean revolt is a significant event in Jewish history. 马加比起义是犹太历史上的一件重大事件。|The Maccabean spirit inspired generations of Jewish resistance. 马加比精神激励了几代犹太人的抵抗。|Synonyms: rebel, insurgent, freedom fighter|Antonyms: loyalist, collaborator, oppressor|noun, adjective
