dendrochronology:/ˌdɛndroʊkrəˈnɒlədʒi/
n.
树木年代学, 年轮年代学
The science or technique of dating events, environmental change, and archaeological artifacts by using the characteristic patterns of annual growth rings in timber and tree trunks.
Dendrochronology was used to determine the age of the ancient ruins.  树木年代学被用来确定古代遗迹的年代。
The archaeologists used dendrochronology to date the wooden beams. 考古学家使用树木年代学来确定木梁的年代。
近义词: tree-ring dating
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps radiocarbon dating as an alternative method)
词性: noun
