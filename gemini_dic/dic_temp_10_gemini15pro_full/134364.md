noninterferingly:/ˌnɒnɪntəˈfɪərɪŋli/
adverb
非干扰地，无干扰地
Without interfering; in a way that does not interfere.
The two programs run noninterferingly. 这两个程序互不干扰地运行。
Synonyms: independently, separately, autonomously
Antonyms: interfering, intrusively
