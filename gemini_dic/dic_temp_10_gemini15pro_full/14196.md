autobiographically: /ˌɔːtəˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkli/
|adv.| 自传地，自传式地 |In her memoir, she wrote autobiographically about her childhood experiences. 在她的回忆录中，她以自传的方式写下了她童年的经历。|例句：She spoke autobiographically about her struggles with depression. 她以自传的方式讲述了她与抑郁症的斗争。|近义词：personally, subjectively|反义词：objectively|词性：副词

Etymology:
From "autobiography" + "-ically."  "Autobiography" comes from the Greek "autos" (self) + "bios" (life) + "graphein" (to write).
