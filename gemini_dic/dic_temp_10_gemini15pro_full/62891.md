词源：
From enlighten + -ed¹ + -ness.  Enlighten comes from en- prefix meaning "to cause to be" + light (v.) meaning "to illuminate."  Thus, enlightenedness refers to the state of being caused to be illuminated, metaphorically speaking, in a spiritual or intellectual sense.
