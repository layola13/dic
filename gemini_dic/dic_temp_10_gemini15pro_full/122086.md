mistico: /ˈmɪstɪkoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɪstɪkəʊ/ (UK) | adj. n. | 神秘的；神秘主义者 | Relating to mystics or mysticism; spiritual and mysterious. |  The old woman had a mistico air about her, as if she knew secrets of the universe.  这位老妇人身上有一种神秘的气质，仿佛她知道宇宙的秘密。|  近义词: mystical, esoteric, occult, arcane | 反义词: mundane, ordinary, common | 词性: adjective, noun

mistico: /ˈmɪstɪkoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɪstɪkəʊ/ (UK) | n. | 神秘主义者 | A person who practices mysticism. | He considered himself a mistico, seeking enlightenment through meditation and prayer. 他认为自己是一个神秘主义者，通过冥想和祈祷寻求启迪。 | 近义词: mystic, spiritualist | 反义词: materialist, rationalist | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Spanish *místico*, from Late Latin *mysticus*, from Greek *mystikós*, from *mystēs* "initiate," from *myein* "to close the eyes or mouth."  The noun form in English is borrowed directly from Spanish.
