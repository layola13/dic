chips: /tʃɪps/ | n. | 薯条，炸薯片，芯片，筹码 | Thinly sliced pieces of potato fried in oil. chips: /tʃɪps/ | n. | 木屑，碎片 | Small pieces of wood, stone, or other material broken off from a larger piece. | The carpenter swept up the wood chips. 木匠扫掉了木屑。 | Synonyms: fragments, shards, splinters | Antonyms: whole, complete | Noun
