orneriest:/ˈɔːrnəriɪst/| adj.|最乖戾的，最暴躁的|Superlative form of ornery: most ornery.  Meaning bad-tempered and combative.|He was the orneriest mule I'd ever encountered, refusing to budge an inch.|Synonyms: cantankerous, bad-tempered, grumpy, irascible|Antonyms: good-natured, agreeable, amiable|adjective
