lignification:/ˌlɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.|木质化，木质化作用|The process by which plant cell walls become hardened by the deposition of lignin.|The lignification of the cell walls provides structural support to the plant.  植物细胞壁通过木质素的沉积而硬化的过程。|近义词：woodying, hardening|反义词: softening
词性: 名词
