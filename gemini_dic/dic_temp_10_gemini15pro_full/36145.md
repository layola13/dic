coexisted:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstɪd/
verb
共存，同时存在
existed together at the same time or in the same place.
Dinosaurs coexisted with many other species. 恐龙与许多其他物种共存。
Humans and animals coexisted peacefully in the valley. 人类和动物在这个山谷里和平共处。
近义词: cohabitated, lived together, existed simultaneously
反义词: clashed, conflicted, fought
