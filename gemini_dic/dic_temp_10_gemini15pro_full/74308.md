fractiousness:/ˈfrækʃəsnəsnɪs/
n.
易怒,暴躁,倔强
The state of being fractious; peevishness; unruliness.
The fractiousness of the parliament prevented any meaningful legislation from being passed.  议会的暴躁使得任何有意义的立法都无法通过。

The fractiousness of the child made it difficult to take him anywhere.  孩子的暴躁让他很难被带到任何地方。

Synonym: irritability, peevishness, querulousness, cantankerousness, crossness, grumpiness, petulance, testiness, irascibility
Antonym: docility, agreeableness, pleasantness, affability, amiability

Etymology:  From fractious + -ness.  Fractious comes from the Latin *fractiō, from frangere "to break."
