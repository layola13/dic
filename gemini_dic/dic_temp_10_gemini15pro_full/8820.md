antiestrogens:/ˌæntiˈɛstrədʒənz/ | n. | 抗雌激素 | Substances that block or reduce the effects of estrogen in the body. |  Tamoxifen is a commonly used antiestrogen in the treatment of breast cancer. 它莫西芬是治疗乳腺癌的常用抗雌激素。

Antiestrogens can be used to treat certain types of infertility. 抗雌激素可用于治疗某些类型的生育障碍。 | Synonyms: estrogen antagonist, estrogen blocker | Antonyms: estrogen, estrogen agonist | noun
