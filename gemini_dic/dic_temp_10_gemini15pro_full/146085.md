overeducates:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪts/
verb
过度教育
To educate someone beyond what is necessary or useful for their intended career or life path.
Many graduates feel they are overeducated for the jobs available. 许多毕业生觉得他们的教育程度超过了现有工作的要求。
The system overeducates people for the jobs that actually exist.  这个体系为实际存在的工作提供了过多的教育。
近义词: overqualify
反义词: undereducate
词性: verb
