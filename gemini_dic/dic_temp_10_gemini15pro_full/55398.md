distensibility:/dɪˌstɛnsəˈbɪlɪti/| n. |延展性；扩张性|The ability of something to be stretched or expanded.  The distensibility of the lungs allows them to fill with air.|The balloon's high distensibility made it easy to inflate.  气球的高延展性使其易于充气。|extensibility, flexibility, elasticity|rigidity, stiffness|noun

distensible:/dɪˈstɛnsəbl/| adj. |可扩张的；可膨胀的|Capable of being stretched or expanded.  The arteries are distensible blood vessels. |A distensible bladder can hold a large volume of urine. 可扩张的膀胱可以容纳大量的尿液。|expandable, stretchable, elastic|rigid, stiff, inflexible|adjective
