fireball:/ˈfaɪərbɔːl/| n.|火球；流星；烈酒|A bright meteor or a ball of fire.  A ball of fire can also refer to a person who is full of energy and enthusiasm. Also, a type of strong alcoholic beverage.|例句：Did you see that fireball streak across the sky? 你看到那个火球划过天空了吗？

例句：She's a real fireball on the basketball court. 她在篮球场上真是个活力四射的人。|近义词：meteor, bolide, comet, rocket, projectile, bombshell (figurative), dynamo (figurative)|反义词:dud (figurative)|词性:noun
