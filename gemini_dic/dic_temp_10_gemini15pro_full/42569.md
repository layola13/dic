counterpetition:/ˌkaʊntər pəˈtɪʃən/ | n. | 反诉，抗诉 | A petition that opposes another petition, especially one filed in response to an initial petition.  | Example: The residents filed a counterpetition against the proposed development project. 居民们提交了一份反对拟议开发项目的反诉讼。

Example:  A counterpetition was submitted challenging the validity of the original claim.  一份反诉讼书被提交，质疑最初索赔的有效性。 | Synonym: counterclaim, countersuit | Antonym: petition, claim | noun
