defroster:/diːˈfrɔːstər/ |n.|除霜器，除霜装置|A device that removes frost or ice, especially from a car windshield.|I turned on the defroster to clear the ice from the windshield. 我打开了除霜器来清除挡风玻璃上的冰。|Synonym: de-icer|Antonym: freezer|noun

defrosters:/diːˈfrɔːstərz/ |n.|除霜器（复数）|Plural form of "defroster," referring to multiple devices that remove frost or ice.|Make sure both defrosters are working properly before you drive in icy conditions. 确保两个除霜器在冰雪路面驾驶前都能正常工作。|Synonym: de-icers|Antonym: freezers|noun
