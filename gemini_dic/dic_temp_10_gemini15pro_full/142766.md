ottomanlike:/ˈɒtəmənlaɪk/ | adj. | 像奥斯曼式的，像土耳其式软垫沙发一样的 | Resembling or characteristic of an ottoman, a type of upholstered seat or footrest. | The low, cushioned chairs had an ottomanlike appearance. 这些矮矮的软垫椅子看起来像奥斯曼式的。| The room was furnished with ottomanlike cushions scattered on the floor. 房间里摆放着像奥斯曼式坐垫一样的垫子散落在地上。| synonyms: cushioned, upholstered, plush | antonyms: hard, rigid | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *Ottoman* + *-like*.  *Ottoman* refers to the Ottoman Empire, and thus things associated with Turkish culture. *-like* is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
