chaek:/tʃɛk/ | n. | 书，册 | Korean word for "book".  It's borrowed directly from the Chinese word 冊 (cè). | I bought a new chaek about Korean history. 我买了一本关于韩国历史的新书。 | book, volume, publication |  (No direct antonym, consider "nothing/emptiness" in a metaphorical sense if referring to knowledge) | noun
