hypermnesic:/ˌhaɪpərˈmnesɪk/ | adj. | 记忆力超群的，记忆力异常好的 | Having an unusually sharp memory; relating to or characterized by hypermnesia. |  She has a hypermnesic ability and can recall details from her childhood with stunning clarity. 她拥有超凡的记忆力，能清晰地回忆起童年的细节。

| 近义词:  exceptional memory, prodigious memory,  eidetic memory | 反义词: amnesiac, forgetful | 词性: adjective
