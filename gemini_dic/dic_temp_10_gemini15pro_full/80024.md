go-to:/ˈɡoʊ tuː/ (American), /ˈɡəʊ tuː/ (British)| adj.|首选的；最喜欢的；必不可少的|A go-to resource, person, or thing is the one that people automatically turn to in a particular situation.  It's reliable and often the first choice.|例句：He's my go-to guy for computer problems. 他是我解决电脑问题的首选之人。|近义词：favorite, reliable, essential, first-choice|反义词:avoid, unused, unimportant|词性:形容词

go-to:/ˈɡoʊ tuː/ (American), /ˈɡəʊ tuː/ (British)| n.|首选之物；必备之物|Something that is a go-to is a reliable and preferred option.|例句：This website is my go-to for recipes. 这个网站是我查找食谱的首选。|近义词：staple, favorite, essential|反义词:alternative, substitute|词性:名词
