outissuing:/ˌaʊtˈɪʃuːɪŋ/
adjective
超发的，过度发行的
Exceeding what is authorized or necessary; issuing in excess.
The outissuing of currency can lead to inflation. 过度发行货币会导致通货膨胀。
近义词: overissuing, excessive issuing
反义词: underissuing
