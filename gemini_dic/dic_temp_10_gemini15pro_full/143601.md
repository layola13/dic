outinfluencing:/ˌaʊtˈɪnflʊənsɪŋ/
verb
超越影响；在影响力上胜过
To exert greater influence than someone or something else.
The new marketing campaign is outinfluencing all of our competitors. 新的营销活动在影响力上胜过了我们所有的竞争对手。
The company is outinfluencing its rivals in the social media space.  该公司在社交媒体领域的影响力超过了其竞争对手。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, outperforming
Antonyms: being outdone, being surpassed, losing influence
词性: 动词
