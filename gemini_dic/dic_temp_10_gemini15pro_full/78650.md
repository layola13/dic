giddy:/ˈɡɪdi/| adj.|头晕目眩的，轻浮的，眼花缭乱的|Feeling dizzy and unsteady, often caused by excitement, happiness, or height; frivolous and lighthearted.|例句：The children were giddy with excitement on Christmas morning.  圣诞节早晨，孩子们兴奋得晕头转向。|近义词：dizzy, lightheaded, frivolous, flighty|反义词:serious, solemn, steady|词性:形容词
giddies:/ˈɡɪdiz/| n.|头晕，眼花|A feeling of dizziness or lightheadedness.|例句：The roller coaster gave me the giddies. 过山车让我感到头晕。|近义词：dizziness, lightheadedness|反义词:stability, steadiness|词性:名词
