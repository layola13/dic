bicoastalisms:/ˌbaɪˈkoʊstəlɪzəmz/
n.
双岸现象；两岸特色；往返两岸的生活方式
The cultural trends and practices associated with living on or frequently traveling between the two coasts of the United States (typically the East Coast and the West Coast).
Many bicoastalisms, such as a preference for organic food and a focus on wellness, have become mainstream. 许多两岸特色，例如偏好有机食品和注重健康，已经成为主流。
近义词: bicoastal lifestyle, transcontinentalism
反义词: regionalism, localism
词性: noun
