occasioning:/əˈkeɪʒənɪŋ/
verb
导致，引起
causing something to happen
The heavy rain occasioning the flood. 这场大雨导致了洪水。
synonyms: causing, producing, generating, bringing about, leading to
antonyms: preventing, hindering, stopping, obstructing
