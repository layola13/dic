freefall:/ˈfriːfɔːl/| vi.|自由落体；暴跌|The stock market is in freefall. 股票市场正在暴跌。|例句：The skydiver began to freefall towards the earth. 跳伞员开始向地面自由落体。|近义词：plunge, drop, plummet|反义词:rise, ascent|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From free + fall.  The literal sense dates to the mid-19th century. The figurative sense of a rapid and uncontrolled decline dates to the mid-20th century.
