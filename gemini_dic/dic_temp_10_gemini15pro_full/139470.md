obstetrical: /əbˈstɛtrɪkəl/  /ɑːbˈstɛtrɪkəl/ | adj. | 产科的; 助产的 | Relating to childbirth and the care of women giving birth. |  She had some obstetrical complications during her pregnancy. 她在怀孕期间有一些产科并发症。| synonyms:  parturient, prenatal | antonyms: none | adjective
