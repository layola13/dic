aerophobia:/ˌeərəˈfəʊbiə/ (UK) /ˌɛrəˈfoʊbiə/ (US)| n.|恐高症，飞行恐惧症|An extreme or irrational fear of flying.|例句：His aerophobia prevented him from travelling abroad. 他的恐高症使他无法出国旅行。

例句：She had to overcome her aerophobia to visit her family overseas. 她不得不克服飞行恐惧症才能出国探望家人。


近义词：aviophobia, fear of flying|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (Greek ἀήρ (aḗr) "air") + -phobia (Greek φόβος (phóbos) "fear").
