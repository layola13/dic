monologist: /məˈnɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /məˈnɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)
n.
独白者；进行独白的人
A person who performs a monologue.
The comedian's act consisted of a single, long monologue about his childhood.  这位喜剧演员的表演是由一段关于他童年的长篇独白组成的。
She delivered a powerful monologue about the struggles of single mothers. 她发表了一段关于单身母亲挣扎的强有力的独白。
speaker, orator, performer
dialogist
noun


monologue: /ˈmɒnəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəlɔːɡ/ (US)
n.
独白；独角戏
A long speech by one actor in a play or film, or as part of a theatrical or broadcast programme.
Hamlet's famous "To be or not to be" is a classic monologue. 哈姆雷特的著名独白“生存还是毁灭”是一段经典的独白。
He interrupted the conversation with a long, rambling monologue about his political views. 他用一段关于他政治观点的冗长、漫无边际的独白打断了谈话。
soliloquy, speech, address
dialogue
noun
