allocative:/ˈæləkeɪtɪv/
adjective
分配的，配置的
Relating to the distribution of resources or funds among different uses.
The government's allocative decisions have a significant impact on the economy. 政府的分配决策对经济有重大影响。
Allocative efficiency is crucial for maximizing social welfare. 分配效率对于最大化社会福利至关重要。
近义词: distributive, apportioning
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From allocate + -ive. Allocate comes from Late Latin allocatus, past participle of allocare "to place, assign," from ad- "to" + locare "to place," from locus "a place."
