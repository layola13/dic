interflashing: /ˌɪntərˈflæʃɪŋ/
adjective
间歇闪光的，交替闪光的
flashing intermittently or alternately; characterized by intermittent or alternating flashes.
The interflashing lights of the police car caught my attention. 警车的间歇闪光引起了我的注意。
Near synonyms:  blinking, flickering, flashing, winking
Antonyms: steady, continuous
词性: adjective
