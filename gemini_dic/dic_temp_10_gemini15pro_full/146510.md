overgeneralizes:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度概括，以偏概全
Draws a conclusion about a group or category based on too few examples or insufficient evidence.
The study overgeneralizes the findings based on a small sample size.  这项研究基于小样本量过度概括了研究结果。

He overgeneralizes about women based on his limited experience. 他基于有限的经验对女性做出了过度概括。

Synonym: stereotypes, simplifies, extrapolates
Antonym: specifies, qualifies, particularizes
词性: 动词
