cohesiveness:/koʊˈhiːsɪvnəs/| n. |凝聚力；粘结性；内聚性|The quality of being cohesive; the act or state of sticking together.|The cohesiveness of the group was remarkable, they always supported each other.  这个团队的凝聚力非凡，他们总是互相支持。|Synonyms: unity, solidarity, togetherness, cohesion|Antonyms: division, disunity, fragmentation|noun


cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/| n. |凝聚；结合；团结|The action or fact of forming a united whole.|The team showed great cohesion in the face of adversity.  面对逆境，团队表现出了强大的凝聚力。|Synonyms: unity, solidarity, togetherness, cohesiveness|Antonyms: division, disunity, fragmentation|noun
