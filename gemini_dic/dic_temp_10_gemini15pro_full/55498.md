distractors:/dɪˈstræktərz/| n.|干扰物，分心的事物|Things that distract; things that draw attention away from something else. |The teacher removed all distractors from the classroom before the test. 老师在考试前把教室里所有会分散学生注意力东西都移走了。| The loud music was a major distractor while I was trying to study.  喧闹的音乐是我学习时的一大干扰。| synonyms: diversions, interruptions, distractions | antonyms: aids,  focus enhancers| noun

distractor:/dɪˈstræktər/| n.|干扰物，分心的事物|Something that prevents someone from giving full attention to something else.|I left my phone in another room to avoid distractors while working. 我把手机留在另一个房间，以免在工作时分心。|  Social media can be a huge distractor when you're trying to meet a deadline. 社交媒体在你赶截止日期时会让你分心。| synonyms:  interferences, distractions, diversions| antonyms:  focus, concentration | noun
