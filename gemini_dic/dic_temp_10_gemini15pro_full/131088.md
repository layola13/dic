nonbeneficial:/ˌnɑːnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəl/
adjective
无益的，不利的
not helpful or useful; not producing any benefits.
This policy is nonbeneficial to the environment.  这项政策对环境无益。
The side effects of the drug are nonbeneficial and can be detrimental to one's health. 这种药物的副作用是不利的，可能对健康有害。
近义词: detrimental, harmful, unfavorable, disadvantageous, adverse
反义词: beneficial, advantageous, helpful, useful, profitable
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + beneficial.  "Beneficial" comes from Late Latin "beneficialis," from Latin "beneficium" (benefit, kindness, favor), from "bene" (well) + "facere" (to do).
