nonintellectually:/ˌnɒnɪntəˈlɛktʃuəli/
adverb
非智力地，不靠智力地
Not in an intellectual way; without using or involving intelligence.
He approached the problem nonintellectually, relying on his gut feeling. 他非智力地处理这个问题，依靠他的直觉。
She solved the puzzle nonintellectually, using trial and error. 她非智力地解决了这个难题，使用了反复试验的方法。
Synonym: instinctively, intuitively, emotionally
Antonym: intellectually, rationally, logically
词性: 副词
