camphene:/ˈkæmfiːn/| n. |莰烯|A white crystalline volatile hydrocarbon with a camphor-like smell, used in the manufacture of camphor and as a solvent.|Camphene is a bicyclic monoterpene. 莰烯是一种双环单萜。

Camphene is used in the production of fragrances. 莰烯用于生产香料。|近义词:isoborneol, pinene|反义词: None|词性:noun
