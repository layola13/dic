divorcement:/dɪˈvɔːrsmənt/
n.|离婚，脱离|The divorcement of church and state is a fundamental principle of modern democracies. 政教分离是现代民主的基本原则。|The novel explored the painful process of divorcement. 这部小说探讨了痛苦的离婚过程。|近义词：divorce, separation, disunion|反义词：marriage, union|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *divorcement (Modern French divorcement), from divorcer (“to divorce”), from Latin divortium (“divorce”), from divertere (“to turn aside, separate”), from dis- (“apart”) + vertere (“to turn”).
