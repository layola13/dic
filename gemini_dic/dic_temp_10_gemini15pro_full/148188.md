overshadower:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædəwər/| n.|使黯然失色的人或物|Something or someone that overshadows another thing or person makes it seem less important or impressive.|Her new book was overshadowed by her previous bestseller. 她的新书被她以前的畅销书比了下去。|近义词:eclipse, dwarf, outshine|反义词:highlight, emphasize|词性:名词

overshadow:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊ/| vt.|使黯然失色；遮蔽；使蒙上阴影|1. To make something seem less important or impressive. 2. To cast a shadow over. 3. To make someone feel sad or worried.|1. The success of the movie was overshadowed by the scandal surrounding the lead actor. 这部电影的成功被围绕男主角的丑闻所掩盖。 2. The tall trees overshadowed the garden. 高大的树木遮蔽了花园。 3. The threat of war overshadowed the peace talks. 战争的威胁给和平谈判蒙上了阴影。|近义词:eclipse, obscure, dim|反义词:highlight, illuminate|词性:动词
