colonel: /ˈkɜːrnl/ (US) /ˈkɜːnəl/ (UK)| n. |上校；团长|A senior officer in the army, air force, or marine corps. |例句：The colonel addressed his troops before the battle. 上校在战斗前向他的部队讲话。

例句：She was promoted to the rank of colonel. 她被提升为上校军衔。

近义词：commander, officer|反义词: private, recruit|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *coronnel* (modern French *colonel*), from Italian *colonello*, ultimately from Latin *columna* ("column"). Originally referred to the commander of a column of soldiers.
