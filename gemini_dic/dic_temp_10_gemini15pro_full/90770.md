humorists:/ˈhjuːmərɪsts/| n.|幽默作家；滑稽演员|A humorist is a writer or performer who uses humor to entertain or amuse. |Mark Twain was a famous American humorist. 马克·吐温是一位著名的美国幽默作家。|  The humorists entertained the crowd with their witty jokes. 这些幽默作家用他们机智的笑话娱乐了人群。 | synonyms: comedian, comic, wit, wag | antonyms: bore, dullard | noun
