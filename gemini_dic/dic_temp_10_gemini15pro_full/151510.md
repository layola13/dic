parrotry: /ˈpærətri/ | n. |  学舌；鹦鹉学舌般重复；机械模仿 |  The mechanical repetition of words or phrases without understanding their meaning, like a parrot.  |  His speech was mere parrotry, lacking any original thought. 他的演讲只是鹦鹉学舌，没有任何原创思想。 |  The student's essay was full of parrotry from the textbook.  这个学生的文章充满了来自教科书的鹦鹉学舌般的重复。 |  mimicry, imitation, repetition | originality, creativity | noun
