mortifiedness:/ˈmɔːrtɪfaɪdnəs/ /ˈmɔːr təˌfīd nəs/
|n.|羞愧，尴尬，屈辱|The state of being extremely embarrassed or ashamed.|She couldn't hide her mortifiedness after tripping in front of everyone.  她当着所有人的面绊倒后，无法掩饰自己的尴尬。|Synonyms: humiliation, shame, embarrassment, chagrin|Antonyms: pride, confidence, self-assurance|noun

mortify:/ˈmɔːrtɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɔːr təˌfī/
|v.|使感羞辱，使尴尬，克制|To cause (someone) to feel extremely embarrassed or ashamed.|It mortified her to have to ask her parents for money.  不得不向父母要钱让她感到很羞愧。|Synonyms: humiliate, embarrass, shame, chagrin|Antonyms:  exalt, glorify, honor|verb
