bairn:/bɛrn/| n.|孩子，小孩 (尤指苏格兰和英格兰北部方言)|A child, especially in Scotland and northern England.|例句：The farmer's bairn was playing in the field.  农夫的孩子在地里玩耍。

例句：She's a bonny bairn. 她是个漂亮的孩子。|近义词：child, kid, youngster, tot, infant|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:noun
