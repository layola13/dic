divestible:/daɪˈvɛstɪbl/ /dɪˈvɛstɪbl/
adjective
可剥夺的，可出售的
Capable of being divested; that can be sold off or disposed of.
The company is selling off its non-core, divestible assets.  该公司正在出售其非核心、可剥离的资产。
The government is considering making some of its holdings in state-owned enterprises divestible. 政府正在考虑将其持有的部分国有企业股权可出售。
近义词:salable, disposable, alienable
反义词:non-divestible, inalienable
词性:adjective
词源:From divest + -ible.  来自divest（剥夺）+ -ible（可…的）。
