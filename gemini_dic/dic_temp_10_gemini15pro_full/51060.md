desolatory:/ˈdesələtɔːri/ (US), /ˈdɛsələtri/ (UK)| adj.| 荒凉的，凄凉的，使人沮丧的|Causing or marked by desolation; depressing.  Characterized by or causing a state of bleak and dismal emptiness.|The desolatory landscape stretched for miles, offering no sign of life.  荒凉的景色绵延数英里，没有任何生命的迹象。|Synonyms: bleak, desolate, dreary, forlorn, barren|Antonyms: cheerful, comforting, lively, vibrant|adjective
