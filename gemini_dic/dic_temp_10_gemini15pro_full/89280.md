homodermic:/ˌhɒməˈdɜːrmɪk/ |adj.|同种皮肤移植的；同种皮肤的|Relating to or characterized by a graft of skin from the same species.  A homodermic graft is a skin graft taken from a donor of the same species as the recipient.|A homodermic skin graft was performed to treat the burn victim. 同种皮肤移植手术被用来治疗烧伤患者。|The surgeon opted for a homodermic graft rather than an autograft.  外科医生选择了同种皮肤移植而不是自体移植。|近义词:allograft, allogeneic|反义词:autograft, xenograft|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From homo- (Greek 'same') + derma (Greek 'skin') + -ic (suffix forming adjectives).
