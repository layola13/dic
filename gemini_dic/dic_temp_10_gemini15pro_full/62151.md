enclosed:/ɪnˈkloʊzd/ | adj. | 被附上的；封闭的；围住的 | Surrounded or closed off on all sides.  | Example: Please find enclosed a copy of my resume.  请查收随信附上的我的简历。 | Example: The enclosed garden was a peaceful retreat.  封闭的花园是一个宁静的休憩之所。 | Synonyms: attached, included, confined, enclosed | Antonyms: open, exposed, unenclosed | adjective

enclosed:/ɪnˈkloʊzd/ | v. |  附上；围住；圈起（enclose 的过去式和过去分词）| To include something with a letter or package; to surround or close off on all sides. | Example: I enclosed a check with my application. 我在申请表中附上了一张支票。 | Example:  They enclosed the garden with a high fence.  他们用高高的栅栏围住了花园。 | Synonyms: included, attached, surrounded, fenced | Antonyms: opened, released, freed, excluded | verb
