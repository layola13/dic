misdescribing:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/
verb
错误地描述；歪曲；描述不当
Giving an inaccurate or misleading account of something.
He was misdescribing the situation to make himself look better. 他为了让自己看起来更好而歪曲了情况。
misrepresenting, distorting, falsifying
correctly describing, accurately portraying
verb
