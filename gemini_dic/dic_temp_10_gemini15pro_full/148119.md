oversecurities:/ˌoʊvər sɪˈkjʊrɪtiz/ | n. (plural) | 过度担保，超额担保 |  The act of providing more collateral than is necessary to secure a loan or other obligation. This can be done intentionally or unintentionally. | The company's oversecurities led to a complex legal battle when they defaulted on the loan. 该公司的过度担保导致他们在贷款违约时陷入了复杂的法律纠纷。| 近义词: excess collateral, surplus security | 反义词: undercollateralization, insufficient security | 词性: noun

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "securities" (noun referring to assets pledged as collateral).  The term combines the idea of an excess of security provided for a financial obligation.
