hyostylic:/ˈhaɪoʊˌstaɪlɪk/
adjective
舌颌型
(of the jaw suspension of most fishes) in which the upper jaw is attached to the skull by the hyomandibular bone.
The shark exhibits a hyostylic jaw suspension.  这条鲨鱼展现了舌颌型颚悬式。

近义词: amphistylic
反义词: autostylic
词性: adjective
