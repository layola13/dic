disenable:/dɪsɪˈneɪbl/
verb
使丧失能力，使残疾
to make someone unable to use a part of their body properly
The accident disenabled him from walking.  这起事故使他无法行走。
近义词: disable, incapacitate, cripple
反义词: enable, empower
词性: verb
