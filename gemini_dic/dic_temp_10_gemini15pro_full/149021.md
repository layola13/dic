owes:/oʊz/
verb
欠(债)；欠(情)；感激；归功于
is obligated to pay or return something received from another.  Is under an obligation because of something received.
He owes me fifty dollars. 他欠我五十美元。
She owes her success to hard work and dedication. 她的成功归功于努力和奉献。
近义词: is indebted to, is obliged to
反义词: is owed, is due

PHRASE->
owing to = 由于；因为
He cancelled the trip owing to the bad weather. 由于天气不好，他取消了行程。

what he owes = 他欠的债
He needs to figure out what he owes. 他需要弄清楚他欠了什么。

owe someone an apology = 应该向某人道歉
You owe her an apology for your rude behavior. 你应该为你的粗鲁行为向她道歉。

owe someone a favor = 欠某人一个人情
I owe you a favor. 我欠你一个人情。

owe it to oneself = 应该为自己做某事
You owe it to yourself to take a break. 你应该让自己休息一下。
