mamluk:/ˈmæmlʊk/| n.|马穆鲁克，奴隶兵|A soldier of slave origin, especially a member of one of the elite forces of the Turkish and Egyptian armies in the Middle Ages.|The Mamluks ruled Egypt for centuries. 马穆鲁克统治埃及数百年。|Synonyms: slave soldier, mercenary|Antonyms: freeman, citizen|noun
