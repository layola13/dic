mestizoes:/mɛˈstiːzoʊz/
n.
混血儿（尤指西班牙人和美洲印第安人的后裔）
A person of mixed racial ancestry, especially of Spanish and Indigenous American descent.
Many mestizoes in Mexico identify with both their European and Indigenous heritage.  墨西哥的许多混血儿认同他们的欧洲和土著血统。
The cultural traditions of the mestizoes are a blend of Spanish and indigenous practices. 这些混血儿的文化传统融合了西班牙和土著习俗。

近义词:hybrids, mixed-race individuals
反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider "purebred" in specific contexts, but can be offensive)
词性:noun
