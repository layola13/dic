decorativenesses:/ˈdɛkərətɪvnəsɪz/| n. |装饰性（复数）|The quality of being decorative; ornateness.  The plural form of "decorativeness." |The decorativenesses of the various rooms in the palace were breathtaking.  宫殿里各个房间的装饰性都令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: ornateness, embellishment, ornamentation, adornment|Antonyms: plainness, simplicity, austerity|noun

Etymology: From "decorative" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) ultimately from Latin "decorare" (to decorate).
