god-fearing:/ˈɡɒdˌfɪərɪŋ/ /ˈɡɑːdˌfɪrɪŋ/| adj.|虔诚的；敬畏上帝的|Having or showing reverence for God; devout.|例句：She comes from a god-fearing family. 她来自一个虔诚的家庭。|例句：The god-fearing villagers attended church every Sunday. 敬畏上帝的村民每个星期天都去教堂。|近义词：devout, pious, religious|反义词：irreverent, impious, ungodly|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From god + fearing (present participle of fear).  The word "fear" in this context carries the older sense of profound reverence and awe, rather than simply being afraid.
