freewiller:/ˈfriːˈwɪlər/| n.|自由意志者；相信自由意志的人|A person who believes in free will, the ability to make choices that are not determined by prior causes.|例句：The freewiller argued that we are responsible for our own actions.  自由意志者认为我们应该为自己的行为负责。

例句：As a freewiller, she believed in the power of individual choice.  作为一名自由意志论者，她相信个人选择的力量。|近义词：libertarian, voluntarist|反义词：determinist, fatalist|词性:noun
