deleteriously:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəsli/
adverb
有害地
in a way that is harmful or damaging
例句：Stress can deleteriously affect your health. 压力会对你的健康造成有害影响。

例句：The chemical spill deleteriously impacted the local ecosystem. 化学品泄漏对当地生态系统造成了有害影响。

近义词：harmfully, detrimentally, injuriously, adversely
反义词：beneficially, advantageously, positively
词性: adverb

词源: From Latin *dēlēteriālis, from dēlēre (“to destroy, blot out”) + -ius.
