germinate:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/| v. |发芽，萌芽；开始发展|To begin to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy, or to cause a seed to do this.  To begin to develop or form.|例句：The seeds will germinate in a few days. 这些种子几天后就会发芽。

例句：The idea germinated in his mind for years before he acted on it. 这个想法在他心中酝酿多年后他才付诸行动。|近义词：sprout, bud, develop, grow|反义词: wither, die, stagnate|词性:动词

germinates:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪts/| v. |发芽，萌芽；开始发展|(Third-person singular simple present form of germinate) |例句：The seed germinates quickly in warm, moist soil. 种子在温暖潮湿的土壤中迅速发芽。

例句：His latest business idea germinates from his passion for sustainable living. 他最新的商业理念源于他对可持续生活的热情。|近义词：sprouts, buds, develops, grows|反义词: withers, dies, stagnates|词性:动词
