folly:/ˈfɒli/ (UK) /ˈfɑːli/ (US)| n. |愚蠢；愚蠢的行为；愚蠢的事；轻率；荒唐事|Lack of good sense; a foolish action, idea, or practice. |例句：His folly cost him dearly. 他的愚蠢让他付出了高昂的代价。

例句：It would be folly to ignore their warnings.  忽视他们的警告是愚蠢的。|近义词：stupidity, foolishness, absurdity, recklessness, imprudence|反义词：wisdom, prudence, sense, sagacity|词性:名词
