brasserie:/bræsəri/ |n.|啤酒屋，小餐馆|A restaurant serving beer, wine, and simple meals. It is often modeled on a French or Belgian establishment.|例句：We had dinner at a charming brasserie in the city center. 我们在市中心一家迷人的小餐馆吃了晚饭。

例句：The brasserie offers a wide selection of Belgian beers. 这家啤酒屋提供各种各样的比利时啤酒。|近义词：restaurant, cafe, bistro, pub|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brasserie*, from *brasser* "to brew," from Old French *bracier*, from Vulgar Latin *braciare* "to brew," from Latin *bracium* "malt." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bruwō*
