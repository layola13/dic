hurrygraph: /ˈhɜːriɡræf/ | n. | 速记图; 快速图表 | A graph made quickly, often conveying information in a simplified or summarized way.  A visual representation of data created quickly, often for the purpose of quick understanding or communication. |  He jotted down a hurrygraph of the sales figures on a napkin. 他在餐巾纸上草草画了一个销售数字的速记图。| synonyms: quick chart, sketch graph, rapid visualization | antonyms: detailed graph, comprehensive chart | noun
