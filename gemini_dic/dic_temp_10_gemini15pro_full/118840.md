metaphoricalness:/ˌmetəˈfɔːrɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 比喻性，隐喻性 | The quality of being metaphorical; the extent to which something is metaphorical.  | The poem is notable for its metaphoricalness, using images of nature to represent human emotions. 这首诗以其比喻性而著称，它用自然意象来代表人类的情感。 | synonyms: figurativeness, symbolism, allegory | antonyms: literalness, directness | noun
