outknave:/ˌaʊtˈneɪv/
verb
胜过，以流氓手段超过
to surpass in knavery; to outdo in roguery.
He outknaved his rivals and secured the lucrative contract through bribery. 他用贿赂的手段胜过了竞争对手，获得了利润丰厚的合同。
近义词: outwit, outsmart, cheat, deceive
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
