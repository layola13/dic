biographist:/baɪˈɒɡrəfɪst/ | n. |传记作家；传记作者|A person who writes biographies, often professionally. | Boswell is best known as the biographist of Samuel Johnson. | 近义词: biographer, writer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From bio- + -graphy + -ist.  Bio- comes from Greek bios "life." -graphy comes from Greek -graphia "writing." -ist is a suffix forming agent nouns.
