chuncha:/ˈtʃʌntʃə/| n.|蠢货，笨蛋|A derogatory term used to describe a foolish or inept person.  This word is considered vulgar slang and should be avoided in formal settings.|Example: "Don't be such a chuncha and spill your drink."  别像个蠢货一样把你的饮料洒了。|Synonym: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, dolt|Antonym: genius, intelligent person|noun
