feuilltonist:/ˌfɜɪˈtɒnɪst/ |n.|专栏作家；連載小說作家|A writer of feuilletons, especially for newspapers or magazines.  A writer who produces serialised fiction or non-fiction for a newspaper or magazine.|The feuilltonist captivated readers with his ongoing tale of intrigue and romance.|Synonyms: serial writer, columnist, novelist, author|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) editor, critic|noun
