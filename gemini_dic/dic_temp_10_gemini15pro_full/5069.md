all-there:/ɔːlˈðɛər/
adjective
神志清醒的，机智的，正常的
mentally alert and sound; sane; intelligent.
He's not quite all there.  他有点不太正常。
近义词:sane, lucid, compos mentis
反义词:insane, crazy, out of it
词性:adjective


all-there:/ɔːlˈðɛər/
adjective
头脑灵活的，精明的
Having or showing shrewdness and good judgment.
She's all there when it comes to business. 她做生意很精明。
近义词:sharp, astute, clever
反义词:foolish, naive, unintelligent
词性:adjective


词源: This idiom is of relatively recent origin, first appearing in the late 19th century.  It likely arose from the idea that someone who is "all there" has all their mental faculties present and functioning.
