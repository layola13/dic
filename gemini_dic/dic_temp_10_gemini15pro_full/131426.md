nonclinically:/ˌnɑːnˈklɪnɪkli/
adverb
非临床地
Not in a clinical setting; outside of a hospital or doctor's office; relating to research or testing not performed on humans or animals.
The drug was tested nonclinically in a laboratory setting.  这种药物在实验室环境下进行了非临床测试。
The data were collected nonclinically.  这些数据是非临床收集的。
近义词: in vitro, preclinical, laboratory-based
反义词: clinically, in vivo
词性: adverb
