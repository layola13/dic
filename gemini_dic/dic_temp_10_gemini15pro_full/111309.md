liturgiology:/ˌlɪtərˈdʒɪɑlədʒi/ | n. | 礼拜学 | The study of liturgical forms and practices, including their origins, development, and significance. |  Liturgiology helps us understand the historical context of different worship traditions. 礼拜学帮助我们理解不同崇拜传统的歷史背景。 |  The professor of liturgiology explained the evolution of the Eucharist. 礼拜学教授解释了圣餐的演变。 |  Near synonyms:  liturgical studies, ritual studies | Antonyms: None | Noun
