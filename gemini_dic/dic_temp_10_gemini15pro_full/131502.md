noncollusion:/ˌnɑːnkəˈluːʒən/ | n. | 不串通，无勾结 | Absence of secret or illegal cooperation or conspiracy, especially in bidding or pricing. | The government investigation found evidence of noncollusion between the companies. 政府调查发现公司之间没有串通的证据。 |  The contract stipulated noncollusion in the bidding process.  合同规定了招标过程中不得串通。 | 近义词: independence, noncooperation | 反义词: collusion, conspiracy | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + collusion (from Latin "colludere" - to conspire)
