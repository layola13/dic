cattiest:/ˈkætiɪst/
adjective
最刻薄的；最恶毒的
Superlative adjective of "catty":  Meaning the most malicious, spiteful, and mean-spirited, especially in a sly or indirect way. Often used to describe remarks or behavior.
例句：She made the cattiest remark about her rival's dress. 她对竞争对手的着装做了最刻薄的评价。

例句：He's known for his cattiest gossip. 他以最恶毒的八卦闻名。

近义词：bitchiest, meanest, cruelest, spitefulest, nastiest
反义词：kindest, nicest, most generous, most compassionate
词性: adjective
