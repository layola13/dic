counterpane:/ˈkaʊntərpeɪn/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəpeɪn/ (UK)| n. |床罩，被单|A bedspread, typically less decorative than a quilt.|例句：She smoothed the counterpane before getting into bed. 她上床前把床罩铺平。

例句：The antique counterpane was hand-stitched with intricate floral designs. 这条古董床罩是用复杂的图案手工缝制的。|近义词：bedspread, coverlet, quilt|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly sheet as a less substantial covering) |词性:noun
