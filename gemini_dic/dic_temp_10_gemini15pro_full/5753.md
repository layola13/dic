amadas:/ɑːˈmɑːðɑːs/ | adj. | Beloved (feminine plural) |  This word is the feminine plural form of the Spanish adjective "amado," which means "loved" or "beloved."  It's used to describe a group of feminine nouns or people. |  Mis amadas hijas, estoy muy orgullosa de ustedes. (My beloved daughters, I am very proud of you.) | Synonyms: queridas (beloved), adoradas (adored) | Antonyms: odiadas (hated), detestadas (detested) | Adjective

Etymology: From Latin "amātus," the past participle of "amāre" (to love).
