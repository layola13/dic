nonremediable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈmiːdiəbl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈmiːdiəbl/
adjective
不可弥补的；无法补救的
Not capable of being remedied, cured, or improved.
The doctor informed the family that the patient's condition was nonremediable. 医生告知家人，病人的病情已无法治愈。

The damage to the ecosystem is extensive and nonremediable.  对生态系统的破坏是广泛且不可弥补的。

Synonym: incurable, irreparable, irrecoverable, terminal
Antonym: remediable, curable, fixable, reparable
词性: 形容词
