outslide:/ˈaʊtslaɪd/
verb
向外滑动；滑出
to slide out or cause something to slide out
The drawer outslid smoothly. 抽屉平稳地滑了出来。
近义词: slide out, extend
反义词: slide in, retract
词性: verb

outslide:/ˈaʊtslaɪd/
noun
向外滑动；滑出
the act of sliding out
The outslide of the drawer revealed a hidden compartment. 抽屉滑出，露出了一个隐藏的隔间。
近义词: extension, protrusion
反义词: retraction, withdrawal
词性: noun

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward") + "slide" (verb meaning "to move smoothly along a surface")
