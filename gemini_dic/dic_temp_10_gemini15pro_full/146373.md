overflood:/ˈoʊvərˌflʌd/ | n. | 泛滥，洪水 | An excessive flood; an inundation. | The overflood caused widespread damage.  洪水造成了广泛的破坏。| 近义词: deluge, inundation, flood | 反义词: drought, dryness | 词性: 名词
