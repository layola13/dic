distortable:/dɪˈstɔːrtəbl/ /dɪˈstɔːrtəbəl/| adj.|可扭曲的，可变形的|Capable of being distorted or altered from its original shape, meaning, or condition. |The image on the screen was easily distortable. 屏幕上的图像很容易变形。| The politician's words were highly distortable and easily taken out of context.  这位政客的话很容易被歪曲，很容易断章取义。|近义词：malleable, pliable, flexible, alterable, modifiable|反义词：inflexible, rigid, fixed, unchangeable, immutable|词性:adjective
词源: From distort (verb) + -able (suffix). Distort comes from Latin distortus, past participle of distorquere "to twist different ways," from dis- "apart" + torquere "to twist."
