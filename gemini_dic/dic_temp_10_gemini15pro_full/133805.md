nongerundial:/nɒnˌdʒɪˈrʌndiəl/ | adj. | 非动名词的 | Not pertaining to or resembling a gerund.  A word or phrase that cannot function as a gerund. |  The word "quickly" is nongerundial; you can't say "quicklying".  快速这个词是非动名词的；你不能说“quicklying”。| Near synonyms: non-gerundival, non-verbal (sometimes). | Antonyms: gerundial, verbal (sometimes). | adjective
