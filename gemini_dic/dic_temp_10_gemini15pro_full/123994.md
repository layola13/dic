morning-glory:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ˌɡlɔːri/| n. |牵牛花；昙花一现的人（或物）|A climbing plant with trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the morning and close later in the day. Also refers to something beautiful but short-lived.|例句：The morning-glories climbed up the trellis. 牵牛花爬上了棚架。

|近义词：bindweed, convolvulus|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
