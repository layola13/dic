animistic:/ˌænɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. |万物有灵的，泛灵论的 | Relating to or characterized by animism, the belief that natural objects, natural phenomena, and the universe itself possess souls or consciousness. |  Many indigenous cultures have animistic beliefs, seeing spirits in trees, rivers, and mountains. 许多土著文化都有万物有灵的信仰，认为树木、河流和山脉中都有神灵。 |  The artist's work explores animistic themes, depicting rocks and clouds with human-like qualities.  这位艺术家的作品探索了万物有灵的主题，描绘了具有类人品质的岩石和云朵。 | 近义词: spiritual, pantheistic, primal | 反义词: materialistic, scientific, secular | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From French *animiste*, from Latin *anima* ("breath, soul") + *-istic*.
