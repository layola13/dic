abuelito:/ˌaβweˈlito/| n. |爷爷; 老爷爷 (affectionate term)|A Spanish term of endearment for "grandfather," expressing affection and closeness. It's the diminutive of "abuelo."|My abuelito told me stories of his childhood. 我的老爷爷给我讲了他童年时代的故事。

Abuelito always gives me candy when I visit. 我去看望爷爷的时候，他总是给我糖果。|近义词：grandpa, grandfather|反义词:grandson|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *abuelito*, diminutive of *abuelo* "grandfather," from Old Spanish *avuelo*, from Latin *avolus* "grandfather."  The diminutive suffix *-ito* adds a sense of affection and smallness.
