demurring:/dɪˈmɜːrɪŋ/
adjective
表示反对的，犹豫的，迟疑的
raising doubts or objections or showing reluctance.
She accepted his offer without demurring. 她毫不犹豫地接受了他的提议。
近义词: objecting, hesitant, reluctant, wavering
反义词: agreeing, accepting, consenting, compliant
词性: adjective

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/
noun
反对，异议
an objection
They expressed some demur at the proposal. 他们对这个提议表示了一些异议。
近义词: objection, protest, dissent
反义词: agreement, acceptance, consent
词性: noun

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/
verb
表示反对，提出异议
raise doubts or objections or show reluctance.
The lawyer demurred at the judge's ruling. 律师对法官的裁决表示异议。
近义词: object, protest, disagree
反义词: agree, accept, consent
词性: verb


词源: From Old French *demorer "to linger, delay," from Latin demorari "to linger, delay," from de- "down" + morari "to delay."
