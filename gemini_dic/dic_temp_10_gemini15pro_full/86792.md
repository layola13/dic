hemadynamics:/ˌhiːmədaɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 血流动力学 | The study of the physical principles governing blood flow.  It encompasses the interrelationships between blood pressure, blood flow, and vascular resistance.|  The patient's hemadynamics were unstable after surgery. 病人术后血流动力学不稳定。 | synonyms: hemodynamics, circulatory dynamics | antonyms: N/A | noun
