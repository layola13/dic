overaccelerate:/ˌoʊvərəkˈsɛləˌreɪt/
verb
过度加速；加速过快
To accelerate beyond a safe or desirable rate.
The driver overaccelerated and lost control of the car. 司机过度加速，失去了对汽车的控制。
The company overaccelerated its expansion plans and went bankrupt.  公司过度加速扩张计划，最终破产了。
近义词: speed up excessively, accelerate too quickly
反义词: decelerate, slow down
词性: verb
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + accelerate (verb meaning "to increase speed")
