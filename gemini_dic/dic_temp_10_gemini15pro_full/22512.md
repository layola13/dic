boomeranging:/ˈbuːməræŋɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
返回，回飞，产生事与愿违的结果
returning to the point of origin, often unexpectedly or with negative consequences
The criticism he aimed at his opponent boomeranged on him. 他针对对手的批评反而落到了自己头上。
synonyms: rebounding, recoiling, backfiring
antonyms: progressing, advancing
词性: 动词


boomerang:/ˈbuːməræŋ/
noun
回旋镖，自食其果的行为
a curved wooden missile that can be thrown so as to return to the thrower; something that returns to its originator, often with harmful consequences
His attempt to discredit her ended up being a political boomerang. 他试图败坏她名声的举动最终变成了搬起石头砸自己的脚。
synonyms: backlash, comeuppance, recoil
antonyms: success, advantage
词性: 名词
