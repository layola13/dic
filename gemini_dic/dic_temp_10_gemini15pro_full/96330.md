indignations:/ˌɪndɪɡˈneɪʃənz/| n. |愤慨，义愤，愤怒|A feeling of anger or annoyance provoked by what is perceived as unfair treatment.|The public expressed their indignation at the government's decision. 公众对政府的决定表示愤慨。|近义词：anger, resentment, fury, outrage|反义词:pleasure, satisfaction, happiness|noun
