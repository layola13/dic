baldric:/ˈbɔːldrɪk/| n.|佩带, 腰带; 肩带; 剑带|A wide belt, often richly ornamented, worn over one shoulder to support a sword, horn, or other object.|The knight wore a baldric across his chest, from which his sword hung.|Synonyms: sash, strap, belt, girdle|Antonyms: None|Noun
