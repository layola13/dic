boom:/buːm/ | n. | 繁荣；兴旺；激增；隆隆声 | a period of great economic prosperity | The tech industry experienced a boom in the 1990s. | 近义词: surge, growth, expansion, roar | 反义词: slump, decline, recession | 词性: 名词

boom:/buːm/ | v. | 发出隆隆声；迅速发展；兴旺 | to make a deep resonant sound; to experience rapid growth |  The economy is booming. | 近义词: thrive, flourish, prosper, resound | 反义词: fail, decline, weaken | 词性: 动词
