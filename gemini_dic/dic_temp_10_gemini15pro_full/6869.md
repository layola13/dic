anatropous:/əˈnætrəpəs/
adj.
倒生的
(of an ovule) inverted such that the micropyle is near the funiculus and the chalaza is at the opposite end.
The anatropous ovule is the most common type of ovule in flowering plants.  倒生胚珠是被子植物中最常见的胚珠类型。
The lily has anatropous ovules. 百合有倒生胚珠。
近义词: inverted, resupinate
反义词: orthotropous, atropous
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀνάτροπος (anátropos, “inverted”)
