misemphasizing:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪŋ/
|v.| 不够重视；轻描淡写；淡化|Representing something as less important, significant, or complex than it really is. | The report misemphasized the severity of the economic crisis. 这份报告淡化了经济危机的严重性。|Synonyms: downplaying, understating, de-emphasizing, soft-pedaling|Antonyms: emphasizing, highlighting, stressing, overstating|Verb

misemphasis:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəsɪs/
|n.| 不够重视；轻描淡写；淡化|The act or instance of misemphasizing; an instance of placing the wrong emphasis.| There was a misemphasis on the role of technology in the report. 报告中对技术的作用不够重视。|Synonyms: understatement, downplaying, de-emphasis|Antonyms: overemphasis, overstatement, highlighting|Noun
