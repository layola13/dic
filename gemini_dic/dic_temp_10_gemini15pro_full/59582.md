ebracteolate: /iːbrækˈtiːəleɪt/ | adj. | 无苞片的 | Lacking bracteoles; (of a flower) without bracteoles.  Bracteoles are small leaf-like structures located at the base of a flower stalk. | The ebracteolate flowers of this species are a distinguishing feature.  这种植物的无苞片花是一个显著特征。| Synonym:  ebracteate | Antonym: bracteolate, bracteate | adjective

ebracteolate: /iːbrækˈtiːəleɪt/ | n. | 无苞片植物 | A plant lacking bracteoles. |  Several ebracteolate species were observed during the field study. 在野外研究中观察到几种无苞片植物。| Synonym:  ebracteate plant | Antonym: bracteolate plant, bracteate plant | noun
