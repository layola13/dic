ox's:/ˈɑːksɪz/
noun
公牛的; (尤指)阉牛的
belonging to an ox, especially a castrated one
The ox's hooves were worn down from years of plowing. 这头公牛的蹄子由于多年的耕作而磨损了。
近义词:bull's, steer's
反义词:cow's
词性:n. (noun, 名词)
