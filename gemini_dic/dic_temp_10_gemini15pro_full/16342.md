barbarossa: /ˌbɑːrbəˈrɒsə/ | n. |巴巴罗萨|Barbarossa, from Italian barba rossa ("red beard"), usually refers to Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor (1122–1190).  It can also refer to the German invasion of the Soviet Union in World War II (Operation Barbarossa).|Frederick Barbarossa was a powerful medieval emperor.| 近义词: (For Frederick I) the Redbeard; (For the operation) invasion, attack |反义词: (For the operation) defense, retreat | 词性: noun

barbarossa: /ˌbɑːrbəˈrɒsə/ | adj. |巴巴罗萨的；与巴巴罗萨有关的|Relating to or characteristic of Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor, or the German invasion of the Soviet Union in 1941.|The Barbarossa campaign was a turning point in World War II.|近义词:  (For the operation) German, Nazi |反义词: (For the operation) Soviet, Russian |词性: adjective
