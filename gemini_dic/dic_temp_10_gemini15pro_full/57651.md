draw-the-line:/drɔː ðə laɪn/ | idiom | 划定界限；拒绝做某事 | To set a limit on what one is willing to do or tolerate. |  I draw the line at working on weekends. 我拒绝在周末工作。

draw-the-line:/drɔː ðə laɪn/ | idiom | 划清界限 | To make a clear distinction between two things. | He draws the line between his professional and personal life. 他把他的职业生活和个人生活区分得很清楚。

draw-the-line:/drɔː ðə laɪn/ | idiom | 设定界限 | To establish a boundary or limit. |  We need to draw the line somewhere when it comes to spending.  在花钱方面，我们需要设定一个限度。
