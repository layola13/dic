macer:/ˈmeɪsər/ | n. |浸渍器; 浸软器|A macer is a device used to soften or break down substances by soaking them in liquid.|The botanist used a macer to prepare the delicate plant specimens. 植物学家使用浸渍器来处理娇嫩的植物标本。|Synonyms:  soaker, steeper|Antonyms: dryer, dehydrator|noun
