liberates:/ˈlɪbəreɪts/
verb
解放，释放
sets free; releases from confinement or constraint.
The new law liberates small businesses from excessive paperwork.  新法律将小型企业从繁琐的文书工作中解放出来。
frees, releases, emancipates
confines, restricts, imprisons
