monetized:/ˈmɒnɪtaɪzd/ |adj.|货币化的；已盈利|Converted into money; made profitable.|The company monetized its user data by selling it to advertisers.  这家公司通过将用户数据出售给广告商来使其货币化。|Synonyms: commercialized, profitable|Antonyms: unprofitable, non-commercial|adjective

monetize:/ˈmɒnɪtaɪz/ |v.|货币化；使…盈利|To convert (something, such as an asset) into or express in the form of money.|The website monetizes its content through advertising.  该网站通过广告使其内容盈利。|Synonyms: commercialize, capitalize on|Antonyms: demonetize|verb
