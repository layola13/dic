herdlike:/ˈhɜːrdlaɪk/| adj.|像羊群一样的；缺乏独立思考的；盲从的|Resembling or characteristic of a herd; behaving in a way that shows a lack of individual thought or initiative, tending to follow the crowd.|例句：The herdlike behavior of the investors led to a market crash. 投资者们像羊群一样的行为导致了市场崩盘。|例句：The tourists moved in a herdlike fashion, following the guide without question.  游客们像羊群一样地移动，毫无疑问地跟着导游。|近义词：conformist, unoriginal, sheepish, imitative|反义词：independent, original, individualistic|词性:形容词
词源:herd (n.) + -like (suffix). From Old English *heord, from Proto-Germanic *herdo (source also of Old Saxon herda "herd," Old Norse hirð "house troop, bodyguard," Old High German hirti "shepherd," German Herde "herd, flock," Gothic hairda "herd").  The suffix -like is from Old English -lic, meaning "having the form or appearance of."
