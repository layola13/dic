nondiscoveries:/ˌnɒndɪˈskʌvəriz/
noun
未发现的事物; 未被发现的东西
lack of discoveries; things not yet discovered or found
Many scientific nondiscoveries remain, mysteries yet to be unveiled. 许多科学上的未发现事物依然存在，有待揭开神秘面纱。
The archaeologist lamented the nondiscoveries of the expedition. 考古学家对这次探险没有新发现感到惋惜。
Synonyms: unexplored areas, unknown facts, unsolved mysteries
Antonyms: discoveries, findings, breakthroughs
词性: 名词
