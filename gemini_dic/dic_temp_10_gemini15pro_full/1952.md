actionary: /ˈækʃənəri/ | n. | 行动指南；行动计划；行动纲领 | A plan or set of guidelines for a course of action, especially in response to a problem or crisis. |  The government has yet to release its actionary on climate change. 政府尚未公布其应对气候变化的行动纲领。

The company developed an actionary to address the declining sales. 公司制定了一个行动纲领来应对销售额下降的问题。

| 近义词: plan, strategy, program, agenda, scheme | 反义词: inaction, passivity | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From action + -ary (suffix forming nouns denoting something connected with or characterized by the thing specified).  Ultimately from Latin *actionem (nominative actio) "a doing, act," from past-participle stem of agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
