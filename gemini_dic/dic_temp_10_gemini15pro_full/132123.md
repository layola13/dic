noncryptically:/ˌnɑːnkrɪpˈtɪkli/ | adv. | 非密码地；明白地；直截了当地 | In a way that is easy to understand and not hidden or secret.  | She explained the situation noncryptically, ensuring everyone understood. 她直截了当地解释了情况，确保每个人都理解。 | He stated his intentions noncryptically, leaving no room for misinterpretation.  他明确地表达了他的意图，没有留下任何误解的余地。 | 近义词: clearly, plainly, openly, explicitly, transparently | 反义词: cryptically, obscurely, vaguely, ambiguously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From "non-" (not) + "cryptic" (hidden, secret) + "-ally" (adverbial suffix). "Cryptic" ultimately derives from the Greek word "kryptikos" meaning "fit for concealing."
