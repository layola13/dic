muskmelon:/ˈmʌskˌmɛlən/ | n. |甜瓜，香瓜 | A type of melon with sweet, juicy, usually orange flesh.  It is characterized by its musky aroma. |  We enjoyed a slice of cold muskmelon on a hot day. 我们在大热天享用了一片冰凉的香瓜。

|  The farmer grew a variety of muskmelons in his field.  这位农民在他的田里种植了各种各样的香瓜。 |  近义词: cantaloupe, honeydew | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
