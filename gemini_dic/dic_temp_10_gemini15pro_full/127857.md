neo-classical:/ˌniːoʊˈklæsɪkəl/| adj.|新古典主义的|Relating to a revival or adaptation of classical styles, principles, or values, especially in art, literature, music, or architecture.|The architect designed a neo-classical building with grand columns and a symmetrical facade.  这位建筑师设计了一座带有宏伟柱子和对称外观的新古典主义建筑。|Synonyms: neoclassic, classical revival|Antonyms: modern, contemporary|adjective

neo-classical:/ˌniːoʊˈklæsɪkəl/| n.|新古典主义|A revival or adaptation of classical styles, principles, or values, especially in art, literature, music, or architecture. (Less common as a noun)|Neo-classical is a dominant style in 18th-century European architecture. 新古典主义是18世纪欧洲建筑的主导风格。|Synonyms: neoclassicism, classical revival|Antonyms: modernism, contemporary style|noun
