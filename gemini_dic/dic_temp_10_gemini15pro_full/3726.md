aggrievednesses:/əˈɡriːvdnəsəz/
|n.|委屈，冤屈；愤懑|The state of being aggrieved; a feeling of resentment or injustice.|Her aggrievednesses over the years had hardened her heart. 多年来的委屈使她的心肠变硬了。|
|近义词：grievances, resentments, injustices, complaints|
|反义词：contentment, satisfaction, happiness|
|词性：名词|

aggrieved:/əˈɡriːvd/
|adj.|感到委屈的；愤懑的|Feeling resentment at having been unfairly treated.|He felt aggrieved at not being chosen for the team.  他因没有被选中入队而感到委屈。|
|近义词：resentful, offended, wronged, indignant|
|反义词：content, satisfied, happy|
|词性：形容词|
