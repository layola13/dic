burthen:/ˈbɜːrðən/ /ˈbɝːðn/ | n. v. |负担，重负；装载，加重担子|A load, especially a heavy one; something difficult to bear.  To load heavily; weigh down.|The old donkey struggled under its heavy burthen. 老驴在其重负下挣扎。  He burthened the horse with too much baggage. 他给马匹装载了太多的行李。 |近义词：load, weight, burden, encumbrance |反义词: relief, ease |词性:名词，动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *burthen*, from Old English *byrþen* (“burden, load, duty, charge, weight”), from Proto-Germanic *burþinō (“burden”). Cognate with West Frisian *burden* (“burden”), Dutch *borden* (“burden”), German *Bürde* (“burden”), Danish *byrde* (“burden”), Swedish *börda* (“burden”).  The spelling *burthen* is now archaic, having been replaced by *burden*.
