calamistrum:/ˌkæləˈmɪstrəm/| n.|梳毛器；栉状器|A row of stiff bristles on the metatarsus of the fourth leg of certain spiders, used to comb out the silk secreted by the spinnerets.|The spider used its calamistrum to create a fluffy web.|Near synonyms:  cribellum (often used in conjunction), comb|Antonym: None (lack of a calamistrum is the opposing state)|noun
