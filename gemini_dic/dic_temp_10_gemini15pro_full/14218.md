autochthonousness:/ˌɔːtɒkˈθɒnəsnəs/ | n. | 土著性；原住民性；本土性 | The state of being autochthonous; indigenousness.  The quality of being native or original to a place. |  The archaeologist presented compelling evidence for the autochthonousness of the ancient civilization. 考古学家提出了令人信服的证据，证明了古代文明的本土性。| synonyms:indigenousness, nativeness, aboriginality | antonyms: foreignness, alienness | noun

autochthonous:/ɔːˈtɒkθənəs/ | adj. | 土著的；原住民的；本土的 |  (of an inhabitant of a place) indigenous rather than descended from migrants or colonists.  Originating where it is found.  Formed in the place where found (of rocks, minerals, etc.) | The autochthonous population of the island had a unique culture.  该岛的土著居民拥有独特的文化。| synonyms: indigenous, native, aboriginal | antonyms: foreign, alien, introduced | adjective
