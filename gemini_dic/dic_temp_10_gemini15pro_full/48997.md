deglutinate: /diːˈɡluːtɪneɪt/
verb
使分离，使解除粘着；分开，脱胶
to separate or make something separate from something else that it is stuck to
The enzyme deglutinates the platelets. 这种酶能使血小板分离。
Synonyms: separate, detach, unglue, disjoin
Antonyms: adhere, stick, bind, glue
词性: 动词
