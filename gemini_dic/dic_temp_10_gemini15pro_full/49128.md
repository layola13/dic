deisms:/ˈdiːɪzəmz/
n.
自然神论
The belief in a creator god who does not intervene in the universe after its creation.
Many Enlightenment thinkers embraced deisms. 许多启蒙思想家信奉自然神论。
近义词:theism, natural religion
反义词:atheism, agnosticism
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French déisme, from Latin deus (“god”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deywós (“god, sky”).
