energised:/ˈenədʒaɪzd/ | adj. | 充满活力的，精力充沛的 | Full of energy; invigorated. |  She felt energised after a good night's sleep. 她睡了一夜好觉后感到精力充沛。| energized, invigorated, stimulated, revitalized |  lethargic, tired, exhausted, drained | adjective


energised:/ˈenədʒaɪzd/ | v. |  使充满活力，使精力充沛 | To fill with energy; to invigorate. | The coach's speech energised the team. 教练的讲话使队员们充满了活力。 |  invigorate, stimulate, revitalize, empower |  drain, exhaust, tire, weaken | verb
