bequeathals:/bɪˈkwiːðəlz/
|n.| 遗产；遗赠物；遗赠
|The act of bequeathing something, or something that is bequeathed.  The distribution of property, especially personal property, in accordance with the terms of a will.|
|His will made several charitable bequeathals.| 他的遗嘱里有好几项慈善遗赠。
|His bequeathals included a valuable collection of books to the library.| 他的遗赠包括捐赠给图书馆的珍贵藏书。

|Synonym:|legacy, inheritance, endowment, bequest
|Antonym:|acquisition, forfeiture

词性: noun
