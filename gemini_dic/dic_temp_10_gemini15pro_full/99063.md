interassuring:/ˌɪntərəˈʃʊrɪŋ/
adjective
互相安慰的；使人感到安心的
Providing or intended to provide mutual reassurance.
例句：They exchanged interassuring glances. 他们交换了令人安心的眼神。

例句：The team members found the coach's words interassuring before the big game.  队员们在大赛前觉得教练的话很安心。

近义词：comforting, reassuring, supportive, encouraging
反义词：alarming, distressing, worrying, unsettling
词性: adjective
