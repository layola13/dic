design:/dɪˈzaɪn/ | n. vt.| 设计，图案；构思，计划 |A plan or drawing produced to show the look and function or workings of a building, garment, or other object before it is built or made.  |The design of the new car is very modern. 这款新车的设计非常现代。|  She designs clothes for a living. 她以设计服装为生。 | synonyms: plan, blueprint, drawing, sketch, pattern, model, scheme, conception, idea | antonyms: accident, chance, unplanned | noun, verb
