antitobacco:/ˌænti təˈbækoʊ/
adjective
反烟草的，反对吸烟的
Opposed to the use of tobacco.
The antitobacco campaign has been successful in reducing the number of smokers. 反烟草运动在减少吸烟人数方面取得了成功。
The government implemented new antitobacco laws. 政府实施了新的反烟草法律。

近义词:antismoking, nonsmoking
反义词:pro-tobacco

词性:adjective
