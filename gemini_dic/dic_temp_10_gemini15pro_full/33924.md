cirrhiferous:/sɪˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
卷须的，有卷须的
Bearing cirri or tendrils.  Equipped with cirri, especially thin, flexible appendages in aquatic animals.
Example: The cirrhiferous anemone waved its delicate tendrils in the current.  卷须海葵在水流中挥舞着纤细的卷须。
Example:  Scientists studied the cirrhiferous feeding appendages of the barnacle. 科学家们研究了藤壶的卷须状摄食器官。
Synonym: tendrilled, cirrose
Antonym: smooth, bare
word type: adjective
