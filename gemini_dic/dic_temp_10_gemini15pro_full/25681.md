bungle:/ˈbʌŋɡəl/| n. v.| 笨拙的错误，拙劣的工作；弄糟，搞砸|A bungle is a clumsy mistake or a piece of work that is done badly. |He completely bungled the interview. 他把面试彻底搞砸了。|synonyms: blunder, mess, screw up, botch|antonyms: succeed, achieve, triumph|noun, verb
