monasticize:/məˈnæstɪsaɪz/
verb
使成为修道院；使过禁欲生活；使遁入空门
To cause to become monastic or like a monastery; to seclude or confine, as in a monastery.
He decided to monasticize his life after the tragedy.  他决定在悲剧发生后过禁欲的生活。
The old manor house was monasticized in the 18th century. 这座古老的庄园在18世纪被改成了修道院。
近义词: cloister, seclude, isolate
反义词: secularize, socialize
