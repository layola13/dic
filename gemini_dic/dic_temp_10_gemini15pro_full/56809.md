dorics:/ˈdɔːrɪks/
n.
多利安式; 多立克柱式; 多利安人; 多利安方言
Doric is one of the three orders of classical Greek architecture, characterized by heavy fluted columns with plain, circular capitals.  It also refers to a dialect of Ancient Greek.
The temple was built in the Doric order. 这座寺庙是以多利克式建造的。
Synonyms: Doric order, Doric style
Antonyms: Ionic, Corinthian
noun
