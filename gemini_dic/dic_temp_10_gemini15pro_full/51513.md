detrimentally:/ˌdɛtrɪˈmɛntəli/
adverb
有害地，不利地
in a way that causes harm or damage
Smoking affects health detrimentally. 吸烟对健康有害。
Synonyms: harmfully, damagingly, adversely, negatively
Antonyms: beneficially, advantageously, positively
词性: 副词


detrimental:/ˌdɛtrɪˈmɛntəl/
adjective
有害的，不利的
causing harm or damage
Smoking is detrimental to your health. 吸烟对你的健康有害。
Synonyms: harmful, damaging, adverse, negative
Antonyms: beneficial, advantageous, positive
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *dētrimentālis, from Latin dētrīmentum (“loss, damage”), from dēterere (“to rub away, wear off”), from dē- (“away, down”) + terere (“to rub”).
