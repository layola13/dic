ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grater, from Anglo-Norman gratere, Old Northern French *grateoir, from grater “to scrape, scratch,” from Frankish *kratton, from Proto-Germanic *krattaną (“to scratch”). Cognate with Old High German kratōn (“to scratch”), Old English crattian (“to scratch”).
