organdie:/ˈɔːrɡəndi/| n. |薄纱，透明硬纱|A thin, stiff, translucent fabric, typically made of cotton, used for dresses, curtains, etc. |She wore a beautiful organdie dress to the summer party. 她穿了一件漂亮的薄纱连衣裙去参加夏日派对。|The curtains were made of delicate white organdie. 窗帘是用精致的白色薄纱制成的。| 近义词: voile, muslin, gauze | 反义词: None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *organdi*, ultimately from Urdu *अरगंडी* (*argaṛī*).
