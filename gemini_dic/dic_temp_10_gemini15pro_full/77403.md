gauzing:/ˈɡɔːzɪŋ/ | v. | 包扎，覆盖，(用薄纱)装饰 | covering or wrapping with gauze; examining or considering something superficially |  The nurse was gauzing the wound. 护士正在包扎伤口。|  He was gauzing the situation before making a decision. 他在做决定前先大致了解了一下情况。 | synonyms: bandaging, dressing, covering, veiling | antonyms: uncovering, exposing | verb

gauze:/ɡɔːz/ | n. | 薄纱，纱布 | a thin, transparent fabric with a loose open weave | The wound was covered with gauze. 伤口上覆盖着纱布。 | synonyms: mesh, netting, scrim | antonyms: solid, opaque | noun
