nonlucidly:/nɒnˈluːsɪdli/ | adv. | 不清醒地，不明朗地 | Not clearly or intelligibly; in a way that is not lucid. | He spoke nonlucidly, his words slurred and disjointed. 他说话含糊不清，断断续续。|  The report was written nonlucidly, making it difficult to understand. 这份报告写得不明确，很难理解。 | 近义词: unclearly, vaguely, incoherently, confusedly | 反义词: lucidly, clearly, distinctly, coherently | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "lucid" (from Latin "lucidus," meaning "clear, bright, shining") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
