eternity:/ɪˈtɜːrnəti/ /ɪˈtɜːrnɪti/
n.
永恒; 永世; 无穷无尽
Infinite or unending time.

例句：
He promised to love her for all eternity.
他承诺永远爱她。

The universe seems to stretch into eternity.
宇宙似乎延伸到永恒。

近义词：
infinity, perpetuity, forever, timelessness

反义词：
brevity, transience, temporality
