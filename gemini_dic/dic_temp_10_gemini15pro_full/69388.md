feedback:/ˈfiːdbæk/| n. |反馈，意见|Information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc. which is used as a basis for improvement.|The teacher gave me some feedback on my essay. 老师就我的论文给我了一些反馈。|synonyms: response, reaction, input, comment, review|antonyms: silence, disregard|noun

feedback:/ˈfiːdbæk/| v. |反馈，提供意见|(transitive) To give (someone) feedback.|I'll feedback my thoughts to the team. 我会把我的想法反馈给团队。|synonyms: report, respond, reply|antonyms: withhold, suppress|verb
