overfullness:/ˌōvərˈfʊlnəs/| n. |过饱；过度充盈|The state of being overfull; excessive fullness.  An uncomfortable feeling of fullness after eating too much. |I experienced a feeling of overfullness after the Thanksgiving feast. 我在感恩节盛宴后感到非常饱胀。|Synonyms: satiety, repletion, surfeit|Antonyms: emptiness, hunger|noun

overfullness:/ˌōvərˈfʊlnəs/| n. |过量；过度|An excessive amount or abundance of something.  A state of being filled beyond capacity. |There was an overfullness of information in the report, making it difficult to understand. 报告中信息过多，难以理解。|Synonyms: plethora, excess, superabundance|Antonyms: lack, scarcity, deficiency|noun
