attempter:/əˈtɛmptər/| n.| 尝试者; 试图做某事的人 |A person who attempts something, especially something difficult. | The daring attempter scaled the sheer cliff face.  这位大胆的尝试者攀登了陡峭的悬崖。|近义词：trier, aspirant, candidate|反义词: quitter, non-starter|词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "attempt" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs an action). The verb "attempt" comes from Old French "attempter" and ultimately from Latin "attentare" (to try, test, attack).
