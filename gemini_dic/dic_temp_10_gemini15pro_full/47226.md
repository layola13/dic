dazednesses:/ˈdeɪzɪdnəsɪz/| n. | 迷惑，茫然，神志不清的状态|A state of being dazed; a feeling of being stunned or confused.|He emerged from the wreckage in a state of dazedness. 他从残骸中出来时，处于一种神志不清的状态。|近义词：confusion, bewilderment, disorientation, stupor|反义词：clarity, alertness, awareness|词性:名词

词源: From daze + -ed + -ness.  The noun "daze" comes from the Old Norse *dasa, meaning "to be sluggish."
