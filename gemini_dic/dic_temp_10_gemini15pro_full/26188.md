butlers:/ˈbʌtlərz/
n.
男管家（复数）
Male domestic workers who are typically in charge of the household, especially serving meals and drinks, and often supervising other staff.
The butlers served the guests champagne. 男管家给客人们上了香槟。
近义词：servants, stewards, staff
反义词：maids, housekeepers
词性：名词

butlers:/ˈbʌtlərz/
n.
管家（复数）
A person, especially a man, employed in a large household to manage the staff and oversee the serving of meals.
The butlers ensured everything ran smoothly at the dinner party. 管家们确保晚宴一切顺利进行。
近义词：domestic workers, house managers
反义词：guests, residents
词性：名词

词源: From Old French *butelier, from butel "bottle," of Germanic origin.
