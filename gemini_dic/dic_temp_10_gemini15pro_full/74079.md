fossilizing:/ˈfɑːsəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈfɒsəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb|使...变成化石；使...过时；使...僵化|The remains of the dinosaur were fossilized over millions of years. 这只恐龙的遗骸在数百万年间变成了化石。|The company's thinking has fossilized, making it resistant to change.  这家公司的思维僵化了，使其难以改变。|近义词:petrifying, hardening, ossifying|反义词:modernizing, evolving, developing
词性:v.

fossilize:/ˈfɑːsəlaɪz/ /ˈfɒsəlaɪz/
verb|变成化石；变得过时；变得僵化|The bones of the animal fossilized over time.  动物的骨头随着时间的推移变成了化石。|Their attitudes towards women have fossilized. 他们对女性的态度已经僵化。|近义词: petrify, harden, ossify|反义词: modernize, evolve, develop
词性:v.

fossil:/ˈfɑːsl/ /ˈfɒsl/
noun|化石；老顽固|The museum has a large collection of fossils.  博物馆收藏了大量的化石。|He's a bit of a fossil and doesn't approve of new ideas. 他有点老顽固，不赞成新想法。|近义词: relic, remnant, dinosaur (figurative)|反义词:modernist, innovator
词性:n.
