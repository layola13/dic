backless:/ˈbækləs/| adj.|无背的，露背的|Having no back or having a low back.|She wore a backless dress to the party. 她穿了一件露背的连衣裙去参加聚会。|Synonyms: open-backed, low-backed|Antonyms: high-backed, full-backed|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From back + -less (suffix meaning "without").  The suffix "-less" comes from Old English "-lēas," meaning "deprived of, free from."  "Back" comes from Old English "bæc."
