haemorrhage:/ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|出血；（大量）流失|A haemorrhage is a medical condition where there is heavy bleeding. It can also refer to a large loss of something, like money or staff.|The patient suffered a severe haemorrhage after the accident.  病人事故后大出血。|近义词：bleeding, loss, outflow|反义词:influx, gain|noun, verb

haemorrhage:/ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/ (US)| v. |出血；（大量）流失|To haemorrhage means to bleed heavily or to lose a large amount of something rapidly.|The company was haemorrhaging money due to poor management. 公司由于管理不善而大量亏损。|近义词：bleed, lose, deplete|反义词:accumulate, gain|verb
