conformity:/kənˈfɔːrməti/ /kənˈfɔːrmɪti/| n.|一致性，符合，遵从|behavior in accordance with socially accepted conventions or standards.|例句：Conformity to social norms is expected in most cultures. 大多数文化都期望人们遵守社会规范。

例句：The company's products are in conformity with international safety standards. 该公司的产品符合国际安全标准。

近义词：compliance, obedience, agreement, accordance|反义词：nonconformity, individuality, rebellion, dissent|词性:noun
