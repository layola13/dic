graphology: /ɡræˈfɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ɡræˈfɑːlədʒi/ (US)
n.
笔迹学，笔相学
The study of handwriting, especially when considered as a means of analyzing character and personality.
例句：Graphology is used by some employers as a tool in personnel selection.  一些雇主使用笔迹学作为人事选择的工具。

例句：He studied graphology in hopes of understanding people better.  他学习笔迹学是为了更好地理解人。


近义词：handwriting analysis
反义词:  None (No direct antonym exists)
词性: Noun
