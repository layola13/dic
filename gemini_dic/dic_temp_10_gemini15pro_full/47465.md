dealerships:/ˈdiːlərʃɪps/
n.
经销商；代理店；经销权
Businesses that sell products, typically cars, on behalf of a manufacturer.  A dealership usually has an agreement with the manufacturer to sell only their products.
I bought my new car from the local Ford dealership. 我从当地的福特经销商那里买了我的新车。
synonyms: agencies, distributors, retailers, outlets
antonyms: manufacturers, suppliers
