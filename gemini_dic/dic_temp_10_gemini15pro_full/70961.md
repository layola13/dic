firelight:/ˈfaɪə(r)laɪt/
n.
火光，炉火的光
The soft firelight flickered on the walls. 柔和的火光在墙上闪烁。
The room was lit only by firelight. 房间里只有火光照明。
The firelight cast dancing shadows on the ceiling. 火光在天花板上投下舞动的影子。
glow, gleam, flicker
darkness, shadow
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fire + light.  Fire (from Old English fȳr) ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fōr, from Proto-Indo-European *pūr- (fire). Light (from Old English lēoht) from Proto-Germanic *leuhtą, from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (light, brightness).
