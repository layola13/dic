antitraditional:/ˌæntitrəˈdɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 反传统的，非传统的 | Opposing or rejecting tradition. | Example:  Her antitraditional wedding ceremony was held on a mountaintop. 她的反传统婚礼是在山顶举行的。

Example:  The antitraditional art exhibit shocked many of the gallery's patrons.  这个反传统的艺术展震惊了许多画廊的顾客。

| 近义词: unconventional, nonconformist, unorthodox, iconoclastic, modern, innovative | 反义词: traditional, conventional, orthodox, conformist | 词性: adjective
