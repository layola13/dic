lecherousness:/ˈlɛtʃərəsnəs/| n. |好色，淫荡|Excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustfulness. ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lecherousnes*, from Old French *lechereus*, from *lecher* (“to lick”), of Germanic origin. Related to lick.
