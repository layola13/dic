hyped:/haɪpt/| adj.|兴奋的，激动的，被大肆宣传的|stimulated to a high degree of enthusiasm or excitement; publicized extensively, especially in an exaggerated way|例句：The new video game is so hyped up.  这款新电子游戏被大肆宣传。|近义词：excited, enthusiastic, thrilled, pumped up|反义词：bored, apathetic, unenthusiastic|词性:形容词
词源:  From hype (n.), shortened from hyperbole.
