erythrolitmin:/ɪˌrɪθrəˈlɪtmɪn/ | n. |石蕊精，红靛|A natural coloring matter obtained from certain lichens, used as an acid-base indicator. It turns red in acidic solutions and blue in alkaline solutions.|Litmus paper is coated with erythrolitmin. 石蕊试纸涂有石蕊精。|The scientist carefully added drops of the solution to the erythrolitmin-treated paper. 科学家小心地将溶液滴到经过石蕊精处理的纸上。|Synonyms: litmus, indicator|Antonyms: None|Noun
