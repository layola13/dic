grobian: /ˈɡroʊbiən/ | n. | 粗鲁的人，粗野的人 | A coarse, boorish person; a glutton. | He behaved like a complete grobian, shoving his way to the front of the line and belching loudly. 他表现得像个十足的粗野之人，挤到队伍的最前面，大声打嗝。| 近义词: boor, lout, churl, yahoo | 反义词: gentleman, lady | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From German *Grobian*, from *grob* ("coarse, rough").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *graubaz* ("gray, grim").
