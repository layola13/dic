criticisable:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzəbl/ /ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəbəl/
adjective
可批评的；可非难的
Open to or deserving criticism.  Subject to justifiable criticism.
His performance was highly criticisable. 他的表现非常值得批评。
The government's decision is criticisable on several grounds. 政府的决定在几个方面都值得批评。
近义词:blameable, censurable, reprehensible, faultfinding
反义词:praiseworthy, commendable, laudable
词性:adjective
