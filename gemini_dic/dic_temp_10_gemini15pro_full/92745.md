ichthyoid:/ˈɪkθiɔɪd/ | adj. |鱼形的，鱼类的|Resembling or characteristic of a fish; fish-like.|例句：The ichthyoid creature swam swiftly through the murky water.  鱼形生物在浑浊的水中快速游动。

例句：The fossil revealed an ichthyoid form from the Devonian period. 这块化石展现了泥盆纪时期的一种鱼形生物。|近义词：piscine, fishlike|反义词:mammalian, avian|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ikhthuoeidēs* (ἰχθυοειδής)  "fish-shaped", from *ikhthus* (ἰχθύς) "fish" + *-oeidēs* (-οειδής)  "resembling," a suffix used to form adjectives.|
