idiomatically:/ˌɪdiəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 地道地；惯用地 | In a way that is natural to native speakers of a language. | She speaks English idiomatically. 她英语说得地道。 | 近义词: naturally, fluently | 反义词: unnaturally, awkwardly | 词性: 副词

USAGE->

What do you usually eat for breakfast? = 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you cooking right now? = 你现在正在做什么饭？
What is he doing at the moment? = 他现在正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 今年她取得了什么成就？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

What are your plans for the future? = 你对未来的计划是什么？

否定句:

词源: From Old English *hwæt, neuter singular nominative and accusative of hwā "who," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch wat and German was.
