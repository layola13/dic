immovables:/ɪˈmuːvəblz/
| n. |不动产|Property that cannot be moved, such as land or buildings.|The immovables included the house and the surrounding land. 不动产包括房屋和周围的土地。|Synonyms: real estate, property, land|Antonyms: movables, chattels|noun


immovable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/
| adj. |不可移动的，固定的|Not able to be moved.|The statue was massive and immovable. 这座雕像是巨大且不可移动的。|Synonyms: fixed, stationary, immobile|Antonyms: movable, mobile, portable|adjective


Etymology:
From Old French *immoble (Modern French immobile), from Latin immobilis (“immovable”), from in- (“not”) + mobilis (“movable”).
