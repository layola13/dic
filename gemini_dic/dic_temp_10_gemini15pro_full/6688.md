analectic:/ˌænəˈlɛktɪk/
adjective
选集的；片段的；残缺不全的
Relating to or consisting of a collection of short literary extracts.  A collection of choice extracts from various authors.
This book is an analectic compilation of poems from different eras. 这本书是从不同时代诗歌中选录的诗集。
She presented an analectic reading of her favorite authors. 她朗读了她最喜欢的几位作家的选集。
近义词:anthological, collected, compiled
反义词:complete, whole, entire
词性:adjective

analecta:/ˌænəˈlɛktə/
noun
选集；文选；摘录
A collection of short literary extracts.
The professor assigned us analecta from ancient Greek philosophers. 教授给我们布置了古希腊哲学家的文选。
This analecta includes excerpts from some of the greatest writers in history. 这本文选包含了历史上一些最伟大作家的节选。
近义词:anthology, collection, compilation, chrestomathy
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

词源: From Greek *analectos*, from *analégein*  "to gather up," from *ana-* "up" + *légein* "to gather."
