barrenly:/ˈbærənli/ | adv. | 不毛地，贫瘠地，荒芜地 | In a barren or unproductive way; bleakly and lifelessly. | The landscape stretched barrenly before them, devoid of any vegetation.  这片土地荒芜地展现在他们面前，没有任何植被。| Examples of barren landscapes include deserts, dry salt flats, and rocky mountains.  荒芜地貌的例子包括沙漠、干盐滩和岩石山脉。 | synonym: unproductively, infertilely, bleakly, desolately | antonym: fertilely, productively, lushly | word type: adverb

barrenness:/ˈbærənnəs/ | n. | 不毛，贫瘠，荒芜 | The state or condition of being barren or unproductive. | The barrenness of the soil made it impossible to grow crops. 土壤的贫瘠使得种植作物成为不可能。 | The barrenness of the Arctic tundra limits the diversity of plant and animal life. 北极苔原的荒凉限制了动植物的多样性。| synonym: infertility, sterility, unproductiveness, desolation | antonym: fertility, fecundity, richness, lushness | word type: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bareinli, from Old French baraigne (barren) + -ly. Ultimately from Late Latin *barrenus.
