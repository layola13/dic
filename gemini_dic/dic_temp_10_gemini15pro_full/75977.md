funnel-like:/ˈfʌn(ə)lˌlaɪk/| adj.|漏斗状的；像漏斗的|Resembling a funnel in shape, tapering inward from a wide opening to a narrow end.|The tornado formed a funnel-like cloud in the sky. 龙卷风在空中形成了一个漏斗状的云。|The flower had a funnel-like center.  这朵花有一个漏斗状的花心。|近义词：conical, tapered, cone-shaped|反义词: cylindrical, wide, expanding|词性:形容词
词源: From "funnel" + "-like." "Funnel" comes from the Old French "funel," diminutive of "fune," meaning "funnel," ultimately from Latin "infundibulum." "-like" is a common suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
