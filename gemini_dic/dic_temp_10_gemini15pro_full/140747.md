omniscient:/ɒmˈnɪʃɪənt/ | adj. | 无所不知的；全知的 | Knowing everything; having unlimited awareness or understanding. |  The narrator in the story is omniscient, revealing the thoughts and feelings of all the characters. 故事中的叙述者是无所不知的，揭示了所有人物的思想和感受。 |  all-knowing, all-seeing | limited, restricted | adjective
omniscience:/ɒmˈnɪʃ(ə)ns/ | n. | 全知；无所不知 | The state of knowing everything. | The detective's omniscience allowed him to solve the mystery quickly.  侦探的无所不知让他很快解开了谜团。 | all-knowingness, prescience | ignorance, unawareness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin omnis ("all") + scientia ("knowledge").
