collagists:/ˈkɒlɑːʒɪsts/
n.
拼贴艺术家
Artists who create collages.
The collagists used a variety of materials in their artwork, including newspaper clippings, photographs, and fabric scraps.  拼贴艺术家在他们的作品中使用了各种材料，包括剪报、照片和碎布。
The exhibition showcased the works of several renowned collagists.  展览展示了几位著名拼贴艺术家的作品。
