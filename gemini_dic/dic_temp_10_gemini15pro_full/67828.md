eying:/ˈaɪɪŋ/| v. | 注视，打量 | looking at something or someone with interest, often because you want something or you think they are attractive |  She was eyeing the cake with longing. 她渴望地注视着蛋糕。

He was eyeing up the competition. 他在打量着竞争对手。| 近义词：watching, observing, gazing, scrutinizing, considering | 反义词：ignoring, overlooking | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the noun "eye" + "-ing" suffix forming present participles and gerunds.  Essentially, it's the act of "eyeing" something.
