paramorphous:/ˌpærəˈmɔːrfəs/ | adj. | 同质异形的，同质多象的 | Having the same chemical composition but a different crystal structure. | Example 1: Diamond and graphite are paramorphous forms of carbon. 钻石和石墨是碳的同质异形体。

Example 2:  The scientist discovered a paramorphous mineral with unique properties.  科学家发现了一种具有独特特性的同质异形矿物。 | 近义词: allotropic, polymorphic | 反义词:  N/A (lack of a direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology: para- (beside, alongside) + -morph (form) + -ous (adjective ending)  From Greek "para" (beside) + "morphē" (form).
