ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gros (“big, thick, fat, coarse”), from Late Latin grossus (“thick, coarse”), probably from a Germanic source akin to Old High German grōz (“great”), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grautaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵrewh₂- (“to grow, become big”).
