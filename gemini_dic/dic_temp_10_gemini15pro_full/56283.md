doggerelize:/ˈdɒɡərəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡərəlaɪz/ (US)| v. | 格律化；以蹩脚的诗表达；歪改 | To write something in the style of doggerel, or to make something appear trivial or ridiculous by rendering it in doggerel. | He doggerelzed the famous poem, making it sound silly and childish. 他把这首著名的诗歌格律化了，使它听起来愚蠢幼稚。 |  Synonyms: trivialize, vulgarize, debase, parody | Antonyms: elevate, ennoble, refine | verb

doggerelizer:/ˈdɒɡərəlaɪzər/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡərəlaɪzər/ (US)| n. | 格律诗作者；歪改者 | A person who writes doggerel or makes something appear trivial by rendering it in doggerel. | The doggerelzer was known for his humorous, albeit sometimes crude, verses.  这位格律诗作者以其幽默、尽管有时粗俗的诗句而闻名。 | Synonyms: parodist, satirist | Antonyms:  poet laureate (perhaps, in a loose sense) | noun

doggerelization:/ˌdɒɡərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌdɑːɡərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US)| n. | 格律化；以蹩脚的诗表达的行为 | The act of writing something in the style of doggerel, or the process of making something appear trivial or ridiculous by rendering it in doggerel. | The doggerelzation of the classic text sparked outrage among literary scholars.  这部经典文本的格律化激起了文学学者们的愤怒。 | Synonyms: trivialization, vulgarization, debasement | Antonyms:  elevation, refinement | noun
