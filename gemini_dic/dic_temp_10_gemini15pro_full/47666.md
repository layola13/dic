debriefing:/diːˈbriːfɪŋ/
noun
汇报，任务报告; 询问，盘问
a meeting that takes place after a job or project has been completed, at which people discuss what happened, what was successful and what was not
例句：
The team held a debriefing after the project was completed.
项目完成后，团队举行了一次汇报会。

The pilot underwent a debriefing after the emergency landing.
飞行员在紧急着陆后接受了盘问。

近义词：
review, discussion, analysis, assessment, post-mortem
反义词:
briefing
