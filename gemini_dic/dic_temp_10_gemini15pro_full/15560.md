bagpipes:/ˈbæɡˌpaɪps/| n.|风笛|A wind instrument consisting of a leather bag and pipes, one of which is a chanter with finger holes, and the others are drones that produce a continuous note.|The sound of the bagpipes filled the air. 风笛声响彻空中。|Synonyms: pipes|Antonyms: None|Noun
