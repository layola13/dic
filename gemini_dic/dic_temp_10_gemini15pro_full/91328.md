hydrophobias:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbiəz/
n.
狂犬病；恐水症；惧水症
An abnormal or unnatural dread of water.  It is a symptom of rabies in humans and some animals. It can also refer to a persistent, irrational fear of water.
Rabies, once contracted, almost invariably leads to hydrophobias and death. 一旦感染狂犬病，几乎总是会导致恐水症和死亡。
He developed hydrophobias after being bitten by a stray dog. 他被流浪狗咬伤后患上了恐水症。
Synonyms: aquaphobia, rabies
Antonyms:hydrophilia
