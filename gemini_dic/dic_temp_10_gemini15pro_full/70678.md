finale:/fɪˈnɑːli/ | n. | 结局，终曲，最后一场 |The finale of the symphony was breathtaking. 交响曲的终曲令人叹为观止。| Example: The fireworks display was a spectacular finale to the festival.  烟花表演是节日的高潮，也是一个壮观的结局。 | Synonyms: conclusion, culmination, end, finish | Antonyms: beginning, start, commencement | Noun

Etymology: From French *finale*, from Italian *finale*, from Late Latin *finalis* "of the end," from Latin *finis* "end, boundary, limit."
