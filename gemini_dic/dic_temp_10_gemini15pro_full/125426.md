multiphotography:/ˌmʌlti fəˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 多重摄影，多次曝光摄影 | The process or technique of producing multiple images on a single photographic plate or film, often by superimposing exposures.  It can be used to create composite images, study motion, or achieve special effects. |  He experimented with multiphotography to capture the movement of a hummingbird's wings. 他用多次曝光摄影来捕捉蜂鸟翅膀的运动。| Synonyms: multiple exposure, superposition photography | Antonyms: single exposure | noun
