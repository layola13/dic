conceptualistic: /kənˌsɛptʃuəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
概念主义的；观念化的
Relating to or characteristic of conceptualism, a philosophical theory that emphasizes the importance of universal concepts or ideas.
His art is highly conceptualistic, focusing on ideas rather than representation. 他的艺术高度概念化，注重思想而非具象表现。

Her conceptualistic approach to design prioritizes functionality over aesthetics. 她概念化的设计方法优先考虑功能性而非美观性。

Synonyms: abstract, theoretical, notional, ideational
Antonyms: concrete, tangible, realistic, pragmatic
词性: adjective
