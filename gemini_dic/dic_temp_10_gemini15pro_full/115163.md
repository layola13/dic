mania:/ˈmeɪniə/| n. | 狂热，躁狂 | An excessive enthusiasm or desire; an obsession.  A mental illness marked by periods of great excitement, euphoria, delusions, and overactivity. |  He had a mania for collecting stamps. 他有收集邮票的狂热。| synonyms:  obsession, craze, passion, infatuation, frenzy | antonyms: apathy, indifference, calmness, composure | noun
