chimera: /kaɪˈmɪərə/ /kɪˈmɪərə/ | n. |  嵌合体；虚构的怪物；幻想；妄想 | A fire-breathing female monster with a lion's head, a goat's body, and a serpent's tail in Greek mythology.  A thing that is hoped or wished for but in fact is illusory or impossible to achieve. An organism containing a mixture of genetically different tissues, formed by processes such as fusion of early embryos, grafting, or mutation. | The economic sovereignty you claim is a chimera. 你所声称的经济主权是一个幻想。| synonyms: illusion, delusion, fantasy, dream, fancy, figment of the imagination, phantom, will-o'-the-wisp | antonyms: reality, actuality, fact | noun

chimera: /kaɪˈmɪərə/ /kɪˈmɪərə/ | adj. |  嵌合的 | Composed of material from more than one genome. |  The chimera mouse model is useful for studying immune responses. 嵌合小鼠模型对研究免疫反应很有用。| synonyms: hybrid, mixed, composite, mosaic | antonyms: pure, homogeneous | adjective
