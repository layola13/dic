delawyering:/diːˈlɔːjərɪŋ/
n.
减少律师参与；去律师化
The reduction or elimination of the involvement of lawyers in a particular activity or process.
Companies are increasingly resorting to delayering to cut costs.  公司越来越多地 resorting to delayering to cut costs.
近义词: streamlining, simplifying, downsizing
反义词: lawyer up (informal), increase legal involvement
