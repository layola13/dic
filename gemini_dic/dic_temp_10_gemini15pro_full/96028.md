indamaged: /ɪnˈdæmɪdʒd/
| adj. | 未受损的，完好的 | Not damaged; unharmed. | The package arrived undamaged. 包裹完好无损地到达了。| 近义词：unharmed, intact, pristine, perfect | 反义词：damaged, broken, harmed, impaired | 词性：形容词

词源:
"in-" (prefix): not, opposite of.  From Latin "in-".
"damaged" (past participle of "damage"): harmed, impaired, or injured. From Old French "damager".
