inter-clouded:/ˌɪntərˈklaʊdɪd/
adjective
间有云的；部分多云的
Partly cloudy; with some clouds obscuring the sky.
The sky was inter-clouded, with patches of blue peeking through. 天空间有云，点点蓝色从中透出。
The forecast predicted an inter-clouded afternoon. 天气预报预测下午部分多云。
近义词: partly cloudy, partially cloudy
反义词: clear, cloudless
词性: adjective

词源:
"Inter-" (Latin) meaning "between" + "clouded" (from "cloud").  The prefix "inter-" suggests the presence of clouds between clearer areas of the sky.
