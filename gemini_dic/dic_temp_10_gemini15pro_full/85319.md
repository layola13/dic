harper:/ˈhɑːrpər/ (US) /ˈhɑːpə/ (UK)| n.|竖琴演奏者，吟游诗人|A person who plays the harp.  A poet, especially one reciting epics and associated with a particular lord or court.|例句：The harper played a beautiful melody. 竖琴演奏者演奏了一曲优美的旋律。

例句：The harper entertained the king with tales of bravery.  吟游诗人用勇敢的故事娱乐国王。|近义词：minstrel, musician, bard|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider non-musician or listener)|词性:noun
