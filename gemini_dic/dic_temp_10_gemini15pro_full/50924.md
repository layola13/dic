desiccant:/ˈdɛsɪkənt/| n. adj.|干燥剂，使干燥的|A desiccant is a hygroscopic substance that induces or sustains a state of dryness (desiccation) in its vicinity.  干燥剂是一种吸湿性物质，可在其附近引起或维持干燥状态（干燥）。|例句：Silica gel is a common desiccant. 硅胶是一种常见的干燥剂。

|近义词：drying agent, dehydrator|反义词: humectant, moisturizer|词性: 名词, 形容词


desiccant:/ˈdɛsɪkənt/| adj.|使干燥的|Having the property of absorbing moisture; drying. 具有吸收水分的特性；干燥。|例句：Desiccant clays are used in many industrial processes. 干燥粘土用于许多工业过程。|近义词：drying, dehydrating|反义词: moisturizing, humidifying|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *desiccans, present participle of desiccare "to dry up," from de- "thoroughly" + siccare "to dry," from siccus "dry."  源自拉丁语 *desiccans，desiccare 的现在分词，意为“使干燥”，来自 de- “彻底地” + siccare “使干燥”，来自 siccus “干燥的”。
