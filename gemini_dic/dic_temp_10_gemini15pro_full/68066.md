fadaise:/fəˈdeɪz/ | n. | 无聊的言行；胡说八道；废话 | A silly or affected way of behaving or speaking; nonsense; twaddle. |  She dismissed his grand pronouncements as mere fadaise. 她认为他的豪言壮语不过是胡说八道。 | synonyms: nonsense, twaddle, balderdash, claptrap, gibberish | antonyms: sense, wisdom, truth | noun

fadaise:/fəˈdeɪz/ | v. | 胡扯；瞎说 | To talk nonsense; to chatter idly. | He fadaises about inconsequential matters for hours. 他就无关紧要的事情胡扯了几个小时。 | synonyms: chatter, babble, prattle, blather | antonyms: articulate, enunciate, expound | verb
