inducting:/ɪnˈdʌktɪŋ/
verb
正式就职；使正式入伍；入门；引导
introducing someone into a position or organization, or introducing someone to a subject or skill.
The club is inducting three new members this year. 俱乐部今年将接纳三名新成员。
He was inducted into the army last month. 他上个月正式入伍了。
Synonyms: install, inaugurate, initiate, introduce
Antonyms: discharge, dismiss, expel

induction:/ɪnˈdʌkʃn/
noun
正式就职；入伍；入门；归纳
The act or process of inducting someone into a position or organization, or of introducing someone to a subject or skill.
The induction ceremony for new members will be held next week. 新成员的就职典礼将于下周举行。
The book provides a good induction to the study of poetry. 这本书是对诗歌学习的一个很好的入门。
Synonyms: initiation, installation, inauguration, introduction
Antonyms: discharge, dismissal, expulsion
