anti-pestilential:/ˌæntiˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl/
adjective
防瘟疫的，抗瘟疫的
Preventing or protecting against pestilence (a deadly epidemic disease).
The doctor prescribed anti-pestilential herbs to ward off the plague.  医生开了防瘟疫的草药来抵御瘟疫。
The village relied on anti-pestilential rituals during the outbreak.  在疫情爆发期间，村庄依靠防瘟疫的仪式。
近义词:anti-epidemic, prophylactic
反义词:pestilential, infectious


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + pestilential (relating to pestilence).  Pestilential comes from the Latin word "pestilentia" meaning "plague, pestilence."
