outstaggering:/ˌaʊtˈstæɡərɪŋ/
adjective
令人震惊的，惊人的
So great, shocking, or surprising as to stagger one.
The sheer number of applicants was outstaggering.  申请者的数量之多令人震惊。
The company’s outstaggering success is due to its innovative products. 该公司的惊人成功归功于其创新产品。

近义词: astounding, astonishing, amazing, staggering, overwhelming, shocking, surprising
反义词: unimpressive, insignificant, ordinary, common

词性: adjective

词源:  "out-" (exceeding) + "staggering" (causing to reel or totter)
