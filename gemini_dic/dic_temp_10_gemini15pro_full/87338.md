herodian:/hɪˈroʊdiən/ | adj. n. |希律党人的，希律党人|Relating to Herod the Great or his dynasty, or to a supporter of his dynasty.| Herod Antipas, a Herodian ruler, feared John the Baptist. 希律·安提帕，一位希律王朝的统治者，害怕施洗约翰。| The Herodian dynasty faced frequent challenges to its power. 希律王朝经常面临权力挑战。|近义词: Herod's, supporter of Herod |反义词: anti-Herodian|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From the name Herod (ultimately from Hebrew הוֹרְדוֹס, Hōrdos) + -ian (suffix forming adjectives and nouns).
