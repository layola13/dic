enlace: /enˈlase/ (US), /ɪnˈlɑːseɪ/ (UK) | n. | 连接，联系；纽带 | A link, connection, or bond. |  The president stressed the importance of the enlace between the two countries. 总统强调了两国之间联系的重要性。

The family reunion served as an enlace between generations. 家庭聚会成为了连接几代人的纽带。

近义词: link, connection, bond, tie, union

反义词: separation, division, disconnection

词性: noun


词源: From Spanish *enlace*, from *enlazar* "to link, connect," from *en- + lazo* "noose, bond." Ultimately from Latin *laqueus* "noose, snare."
