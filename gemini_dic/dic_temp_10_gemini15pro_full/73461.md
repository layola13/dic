foreoath:/fɔːrˈoʊθ/
noun
预先宣誓；预誓
A preliminary or anticipatory oath.
The witness took a foreoath before giving testimony.  证人在作证前进行了预先宣誓。
Synonyms: pre-oath, preliminary oath
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, possibly post-oath or affirmation if contrasting with swearing an oath *after* an event or choosing not to swear at all)
