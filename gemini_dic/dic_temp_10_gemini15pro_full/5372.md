alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的；淤积的 | Relating to or composed of alluvium; deposited by flowing water. | The alluvial soil in the river valley is very fertile. 河谷里的冲积土非常肥沃。 | The farmer planted his crops in the alluvial plain. 农民在冲积平原上种植庄稼。 | 近义词：sedimentary, deposited | 反义词：igneous, metamorphic | 词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *alluvialis, from alluvius (“wash, inundation”), from alluere (“to wash against”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + -luere, from lavere (“to wash”).
