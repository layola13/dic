mastiffs:/ˈmæstɪfs/ | n. |獒犬（复数）| Large, powerful breed of dog, known for their strength and protective instincts.  Origin: Old French *mastin (unattested), probably from Vulgar Latin *mansuetīnus “tame, gentle, domestic (animal),” from Latin mansuētus.|
例句：The mastiffs guarded the estate. 獒犬守护着庄园。

例句：Two mastiffs barked at the intruder. 两只獒犬对着入侵者吠叫。


近义词：dog, hound, canine|
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑小型犬种，例如：chihuahua 吉娃娃)|
词性：名词
