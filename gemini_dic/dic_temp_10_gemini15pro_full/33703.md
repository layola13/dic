cipherer:/ˈsaɪfərər/| n.|密码员，加密员，使用密码的人|A person who enciphers or uses ciphers; an encoder.|The cipherer encoded the message before sending it. 密码员在发送消息之前对其进行了编码。|近义词：cryptographer, encoder, coder|反义词：decoder, decipherer|词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "cipher" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Cipher" comes from Old French "cifre" meaning "zero," ultimately from Arabic "sifr."
