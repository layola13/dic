fibulare:/fɪbjʊˈlɛəriː/
adjective|腓骨的,  مربوط به استخوان نازک نی|Pertaining to the fibula.
The fibulare artery supplies blood to the lateral aspect of the leg. |腓动脉为腿的外侧供血。
The fibulare muscles are located on the lateral side of the leg. |腓骨肌位于腿的外侧。
近义词:peroneal|
反义词:tibial|
词性:adjective

fibula:/ˈfɪbjʊlə/
noun|腓骨, استخوان نازک نی|The outer and usually smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle.
A fracture of the fibula can be a painful injury. |腓骨骨折可能是一种痛苦的损伤。
The fibula and tibia are the two bones of the lower leg. |腓骨和胫骨是小腿的两块骨头。
近义词:calf bone|
反义词:tibia|
词性:noun
