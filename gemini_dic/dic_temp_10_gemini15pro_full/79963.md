go-ape:/ɡəʊˈeɪp/| idiom |勃然大怒，发狂|To become extremely angry or excited.|例句：He went ape when he found out his car had been stolen. 他发现他的车被偷了，气得暴跳如雷。

例句：She went ape over the new shoes. 她对这双新鞋爱不释手，欣喜若狂。|近义词：go bananas, go ballistic, lose it, flip out, hit the roof|反义词：calm down, stay calm|词性：idiom
