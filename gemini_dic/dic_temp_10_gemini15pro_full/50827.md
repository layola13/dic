descriptiveness:/dɪˈskrɪptɪvnəs/| n.|描述性，描写性|The descriptiveness of the passage brought the scene to life. 这段文字的描述性使场景栩栩如生。|例句：The descriptiveness of his writing style captivated the readers. 他的写作风格的描述性吸引了读者。|近义词：vividness, detail, richness|反义词：vagueness, obscurity|词性:noun
Etymology: From descriptive + -ness.  Descriptive comes from the Latin *describere*, meaning "to write down" or "copy."
