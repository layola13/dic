aventurine:/əˈvɛntʃərɪn/ | n. |砂金石，东陵石 |A translucent quartz spangled with scales of mica or other minerals. A form of quartz containing small inclusions of a flaky mineral that cause a shimmering or glistening effect.| She wore an aventurine pendant. 她戴着一个砂金石吊坠。|Synonyms: sunstone, goldstone|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

aventurine:/əˈvɛntʃərɪn/ | adj. |砂金石制的，东陵石制的 |Made of or resembling aventurine. | The vase was made of aventurine glass.  这个花瓶是用砂金石玻璃制成的。|Synonyms: shimmering, glistening|Antonyms: dull, matte|Adjective
