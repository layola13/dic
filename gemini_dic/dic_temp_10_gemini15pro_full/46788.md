dangling:/ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ/
adjective
悬垂的，摇摆的
hanging loosely or swinging freely.
The dangling wires posed a safety hazard.  悬垂的电线构成了安全隐患。
The monkey was dangling from a tree branch. 猴子悬挂在树枝上。
近义词: hanging, swinging, suspended
反义词: fixed, secured, attached
词性: adjective

dangling:/ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ/
verb
悬挂, 悬荡
to hang loosely and swing freely
He dangled his feet in the water. 他把脚悬在水中晃荡。
The chandelier dangled precariously from the ceiling. 枝形吊灯摇摇欲坠地悬挂在天花板上。
近义词: hang, swing, droop
反义词: fasten, secure
词性: verb (present participle)


Etymology:
From Middle English *danglen, frequentative of dangen (“to hang”), from Old Norse dingla (“to swing, dangle”).  Related to Old English denġan (“to strike, knock”).
