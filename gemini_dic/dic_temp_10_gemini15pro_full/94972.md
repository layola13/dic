in-the-bag:/ɪn ðə bæɡ/
adjective
稳操胜券的；十拿九稳的
Certain to be won or achieved.
The election is in the bag for the incumbent mayor.  现任市长稳操胜券。
The deal is pretty much in the bag. 这笔交易差不多是十拿九稳了。
近义词:assured, certain, clinched
反义词:uncertain, doubtful, precarious
词性:adjective

in-the-bag:/ɪn ðə bæɡ/
idiom
稳操胜券；囊中之物
Something that is certain to be obtained or achieved.
With her talent and hard work, the scholarship is in the bag.  凭借她的才华和努力，奖学金已是囊中之物。
He knew the job was in the bag after the final interview.  最后一次面试后，他知道这份工作稳操胜券了。
近义词:assured, certain, clinched
反义词:uncertain, doubtful, precarious
词性:idiom


词源: This idiom likely originates from the imagery of having something securely placed "in the bag," implying possession and certainty.  这个习语可能源于把某物安全地放在“袋子里”的意象，暗示着拥有和确定性。
