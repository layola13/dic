epistoma:/ˈɛpɪˌstoʊmə/| n. |上唇基，口上片|A plate or shield-like structure covering the mouthparts in some invertebrates, especially arthropods.  It is situated above the mouth and below the frons (forehead).|The epistoma of the beetle was distinctly notched.  甲虫的上唇基有明显的缺口。|Synonym: clypeus (though not always interchangeable, they are often used synonymously)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists for such a specific anatomical term)|noun
