half-acquainted:/ˌhæf əˈkweɪntɪd/
| adj. | 半生不熟的；略有交情的 | Partly acquainted; slightly familiar. |  I was half-acquainted with the author, having met him briefly once. 我和这位作者半生不熟，只见过一面。|
|  We exchanged a few awkward words, as we were only half-acquainted. 我们只是半生不熟，所以只交换了几句尴尬的话。|
| 近义词: slightly acquainted, partially acquainted, vaguely familiar | 反义词: well-acquainted, close, intimate | 词性: 形容词
