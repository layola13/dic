clumped:/klʌmpt/
adjective
成群的，簇生的，凝聚的
gathered together in a clump or mass.
The trees were clumped together at the edge of the forest. 树木成群地聚集在森林边缘。
The bacteria were clumped together under the microscope. 在显微镜下，细菌聚集在一起。
close together, clustered, grouped
scattered, dispersed, spread out
词性: 形容词


clump:/klʌmp/
noun
团，块，簇
a compact mass of something.
A clump of earth fell from the ceiling. 一块土从天花板上掉了下来。
There was a clump of bushes in the middle of the field. 田野中央有一丛灌木。
mass, cluster, bunch
individual, single, separate
词性: 名词

clump:/klʌmp/
verb
聚集，形成团块
to form a clump or mass.
The snow clumped together on the roof. 雪在屋顶上结成块。
The children clumped together for warmth. 孩子们 huddled together for warmth.
gather, cluster, group
separate, disperse, scatter
词性: 名词，动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From perhaps a Scandinavian source, akin to Norwegian *klump* "lump, mass," Swedish *klumpa* "lump, clod."
