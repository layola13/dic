ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *notemuge, from Old French nois muguete (“nutmeg”), a compound of nois (“nut”) (from Latin nux) + muguete (“musk,” referring to the scent), from Latin muscus.  Ultimately from Sanskrit मुष्क (muṣka).
