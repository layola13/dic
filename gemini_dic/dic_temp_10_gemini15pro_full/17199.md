balky:/ˈbɔːlki/ | adj. | 倔强的；不愿合作的；难以处理的 | Reluctant to comply or cooperate; unmanageable.  | The balky mule refused to move.  这头倔强的骡子拒绝移动。| Synonyms: obstinate, stubborn, recalcitrant, uncooperative, difficult | Antonyms: compliant, cooperative, docile, obedient, willing | adjective

balky:/ˈbɔːlki/ | adj. | （机器）运转不灵的；故障的 | (of a machine) not working smoothly or regularly; prone to malfunction. | The engine was balky and difficult to start. 发动机运转不灵，难以启动。| Synonyms: unreliable, temperamental, erratic, faulty | Antonyms: reliable, dependable, smooth-running | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From balk (v.) + -y.  The verb "balk" originates from Middle English balken "to leave unplowed," likely from Old Norse balkr "ridge of land."
