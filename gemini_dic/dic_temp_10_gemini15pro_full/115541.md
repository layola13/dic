mapmakers:/ˈmæpˌmeɪkərz/
noun
地图制作者
People who create maps.
Mapmakers use a variety of tools and techniques to create accurate representations of the world.  地图制作者使用各种工具和技术来创建准确的世界地图。
Early mapmakers relied on celestial navigation and surveying. 早期地图制作者依靠天文导航和测量。
Synonym: cartographers
Antonym: N/A
