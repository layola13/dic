orthodromics:/ˌɔːrθəˈdrɒmɪks/ |n.| 大圆航法；球面航行学 |The study of orthodromics is essential for long-distance navigation.  研究大圆航法对于长途航行至关重要。| Example: Orthodromics calculates the shortest distance between two points on a sphere. 大圆航法计算球面上两点之间的最短距离。 |Synonym: great-circle navigation| Antonym: rhumb-line navigation| noun

Etymology: From Greek *orthos* (straight) + *dromos* (running, course).
