bales:/beɪlz/| n.|大包，捆；（尤指）大捆的货物|Large bundles of goods, especially merchandise packaged for shipping or storage. |The ship carried bales of cotton. 这艘船装载着大包的棉花.|近义词：bundles, packages, packs|反义词: individual items, loose items|名词

bales: /beɪlz/ |v. |打包，捆扎|(usually of farmers) To make something into bales. |They baled the hay for winter storage. 他们把干草打包以便过冬储存. |近义词：bundle, package, pack |反义词: unpack, unbundle |动词


词源: From Old French *bale, from a Germanic source akin to Old High German balla "ball."  Meaning shifted from "ball" to "bundle" over time.
