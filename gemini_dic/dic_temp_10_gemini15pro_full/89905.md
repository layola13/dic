horrifyingly:/ˈhɒrɪfaɪɪŋli/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrɪfaɪɪŋli/ (US)| adv.|可怕地，令人恐惧地|In a way that causes horror; terribly.  It describes something in a manner that inspires intense fear or disgust.|The movie depicted the accident horrifyingly. 这部电影可怕地描绘了那场事故。|Synonyms:  terrifyingly, frightfully, dreadfully, appallingly|Antonyms:  pleasantly, delightfully, beautifully|Adverb
