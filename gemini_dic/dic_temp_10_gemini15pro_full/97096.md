infested:/ɪnˈfɛstɪd/| adj.| 遍布的；泛滥的；大批出没的|Overrun and occupied by large numbers of troublesome insects or other animals.|例句：The apartment was infested with cockroaches. 这间公寓蟑螂成灾。

例句：The garden is infested with weeds. 花园里杂草丛生。|近义词：overrun, plagued, swarming, teeming|反义词：clean, clear, pure|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin infestus "hostile, troublesome," related to infesto "attack, molest, annoy, trouble."
