nonsemantic:/ˌnɑːnsɪˈmæn.tɪk/| adj.|非语义的，无意义的|Not having or relating to meaning in language; lacking semantic content.  It refers to elements of language that do not convey specific meaning but contribute to the structure or form of an utterance.|The nonsemantic aspects of the poem, such as its rhyme and rhythm, are just as important as its meaning.|Synonyms: meaningless, nonsensical, insignificant|Antonyms: semantic, meaningful|adjective
