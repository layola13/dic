例句：He's been hunkering down in his study all week, working on his dissertation. 他整个星期都待在他的书房里，潜心撰写他的论文。|近义词：crouching, squatting, huddled, hunched,  persisting, persevering|反义词:standing, straightening, abandoning|词性：动词

hunker:/ˈhʌŋkər/ |v.|蹲下，蜷缩|He hunkered down beside the fire to warm himself. 他蹲在火旁取暖。|例句：She hunkered down to avoid being seen. 她蹲下以避免被看到。

例句：We hunkered down in the basement during the tornado.  我们在龙卷风期间躲在地下室里。|近义词：squat, crouch, hunch|反义词:stand, straighten|词性：动词

词源:
Likely from the Dutch *hunk, to squat.  Probably related to Low German hunken, to sit on one's heels.
