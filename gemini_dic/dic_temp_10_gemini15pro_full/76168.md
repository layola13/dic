fusing:/ˈfjuːzɪŋ/ | v. | 熔合；融合；结合 | The two companies are fusing to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成一家更大的公司。|
例句：
The wires are fusing together. 电线正在熔合在一起。
The artist is fusing different styles of music. 艺术家正在融合不同风格的音乐。

近义词：melting, blending, merging, combining, uniting
反义词：separating, dividing, splitting
词性：动词 (verb)

词源：From Latin "fusus" (past participle of "fundere" - to pour, melt).
