cleave (1):/kliːv/| v.| 劈开，粘住|To split or divide by force, especially with a sharp instrument; to adhere firmly.|The butcher cleaved the meat in two. 屠夫把肉劈成两半。|Synonyms: split, divide, sever, adhere, cling|Antonyms: join, unite, separate|verb

cleave (2):/kliːv/| v.| 忠于，坚持|To remain faithful to; to adhere strongly to.|He cleaved to his principles despite the pressure. 尽管压力很大，他仍然坚持自己的原则。|Synonyms: adhere, stick, cling, hold fast|Antonyms: abandon, desert, forsake|verb
