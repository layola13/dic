immoderatenesses:/ɪˌmɒdərətˈnɛsɪz/
noun
过度；无节制；放肆
The state or quality of being immoderate; excess.  Lack of restraint or moderation.
His immoderatenesses in eating and drinking ultimately ruined his health. 他饮食上的无节制最终毁了他的健康。
Synonyms: excesses, extravagances, intemperances
Antonyms: moderations, restraints, temperances
词性: 名词
