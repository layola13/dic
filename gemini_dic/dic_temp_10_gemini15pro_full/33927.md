cirrhoses: /sɪˈroʊsiːz/
noun
肝硬化
A late stage of scarring (fibrosis) of the liver caused by many forms of liver diseases and conditions, such as hepatitis and chronic alcoholism.
The patient's cirrhoses was caused by years of heavy drinking.  这位病人的肝硬化是由多年的酗酒引起的。

cirrhoses: /sɪˈroʊsiːz/
noun
肝硬变
The end stage of chronic liver disease characterized by fibrosis, disruption of the normal liver architecture, and widespread nodules in the liver.
Cirrhoses can lead to liver failure and death. 肝硬变会导致肝衰竭和死亡。


近义词:  liver damage, hepatic fibrosis
反义词:  healthy liver


词源:  From Greek *kirrhos* "tawny, orange-colored," referring to the appearance of the cirrhotic liver.
