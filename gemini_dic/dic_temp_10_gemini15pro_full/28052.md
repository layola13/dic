capella:/kəˈpɛlə/| n.| 五车二（御夫座α星）; 清唱; 小教堂|A bright star, the sixth brightest in the sky, and the brightest in the constellation Auriga. It is actually a quadruple star system. Also refers to vocal music performed without instrumental accompaniment, or a small chapel.|例句：Capella is a beautiful star to observe through a telescope.  通过望远镜观察五车二是一件很美好的事情。

例句：The choir sang a capella, their voices blending beautifully.  唱诗班清唱，他们的声音融合得很美妙。|近义词：chapel (for the small chapel meaning), a cappella (for the musical meaning), star (for the astronomical meaning)|反义词: N/A (no direct opposite for a star or a type of music)|词性: noun
