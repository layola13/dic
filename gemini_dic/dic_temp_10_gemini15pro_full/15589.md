n.
巴林（国）
Bahrain, officially the Kingdom of Bahrain, is an island country in Western Asia. It is situated on the Persian Gulf, and comprises a small archipelago made up of 50 natural islands and an additional 33 artificial islands, centered on Bahrain Island which comprises around 83 percent of the kingdom's landmass.
Bahrain is a small island nation situated in the Persian Gulf. 巴林是一个位于波斯湾的小岛国。
The Kingdom of Bahrain has a rich history and culture. 巴林王国有着丰富的历史和文化。
