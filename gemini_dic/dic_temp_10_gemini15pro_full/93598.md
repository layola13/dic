imbarn:/ɪmˈbɑːrn/ | vt. | 储存(尤指谷物)；监禁；禁闭 | To store (especially grain) in a barn; to confine; to enclose. | The farmer imbarned the wheat for the winter.  农民把小麦储存在谷仓里过冬。

The king imbarned the traitor in the dungeon. 国王把叛徒关进了地牢。| 近义词：store, house, confine, imprison | 反义词: release, liberate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From im- (in, into) + barn (a building for storing grain).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *barniz.
