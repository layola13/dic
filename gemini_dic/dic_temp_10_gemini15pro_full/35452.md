co-work:/koʊˈwɜːrk/| v. n.|共同工作，协同工作|To work together with others, especially in a shared workspace.|例句：We co-worked on a project to develop a new marketing strategy. 我们合作开发了一个新的营销策略项目。

例句：Many freelancers choose to co-work in shared office spaces. 许多自由职业者选择在共享办公空间协同工作。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, team up|反义词：work alone, work independently|词性：动词, 名词

co-working:/koʊˈwɜːrkɪŋ/| n. adj.|共同工作，联合办公的|The activity of working together with others, especially in a shared workspace, or relating to this activity.|例句：Co-working spaces offer a flexible and collaborative environment. 联合办公空间提供灵活的协作环境。

例句：The co-working trend is growing rapidly in major cities. 联合办公的趋势在大城市中迅速发展。|近义词：shared workspace, collaborative workspace|反义词：private office, individual workspace|词性：名词, 形容词
