affeerer:/əˈfɪərər/| n.|估价人，确定罚款金额的人|A person, usually appointed by a court leet, who formerly assessed the monetary penalty for offenses.|The affeerers determined the fines for those found guilty of minor offenses.|Synonym: assessor|Antonym: N/A|noun
Etymology-> From Anglo-French *afeurer, from afeurer  "to fix, assess,"  from a- "to" (from Latin ad-) + feur "price, fixed amount" (from Latin forum "market").
