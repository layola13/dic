despotist:/ˈdɛspətɪst/| n.|暴君，专制君主|A ruler with absolute power, especially one who exercises it in a cruel or oppressive way.  A despot is a ruler who has total power and often uses that power in a cruel or unjust way.|例句：The despotist ruled the country with an iron fist.  这个暴君用铁腕统治着国家。

例句：The people suffered greatly under the despotist's reign. 人民在暴君的统治下遭受了巨大的苦难。|近义词：tyrant, dictator, autocrat|反义词：democrat|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *despote*, from Late Latin *despota*, from Greek *despótēs* "master, lord," originally "master of a house, head of a family," later "a Byzantine emperor, a lord, a master."
