exaggeratory:/ɪɡˈzædʒəˌrɔːri/ | adj. | 夸张的，夸大的 | characterized by exaggeration; overstating or magnifying the truth. |  She has an exaggeratory way of telling stories, making even mundane events sound like epic adventures. 她讲故事的方式很夸张，即使是平凡的事情听起来也像史诗般的冒险。

|  His claims about the product's effectiveness were exaggeratory and misleading. 他关于产品功效的说法夸大其词，具有误导性。| 近义词: hyperbolic, overstated, inflated | 反义词: understated, modest, realistic | 词性: adjective
