clannishly:/ˈklænɪʃli/| adv.|排外地；小集团地；抱团地|In a way that shows loyalty and friendship only to members of one's own group, excluding others.|They stuck together clannishly, rarely mixing with people outside their tight-knit community. 他们抱团在一起，很少与他们紧密联系的社区以外的人交往。|Example 2: The team members behaved clannishly, refusing to share information with other departments. 团队成员表现得非常排外，拒绝与其他部门共享信息。|近义词：exclusively, cliquishly, insularly|反义词：inclusively, openly, sociably|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From clan + -ish + -ly.  "Clan" originates from the Gaelic word "clann," meaning offspring, family, or descendants. The suffix "-ish" forms adjectives meaning "of or belonging to" or "having the character of," while "-ly" forms adverbs.
