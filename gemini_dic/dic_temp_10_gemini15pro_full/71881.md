fletching:/ˈflɛtʃɪŋ/| n.|箭羽，羽毛，飞镖尾翼|The fletching on the arrow stabilizes its flight.  箭上的羽毛稳定了它的飞行。|例句：The archer carefully attached the fletching to the arrow shaft.  弓箭手小心翼翼地把羽毛粘在箭杆上。

例句：Damaged fletching can affect the accuracy of the arrow.  损坏的羽毛会影响箭的准确性。|近义词：vane, feather, plumage|反义词：(无直接反义词)|词性:noun


fletch:/flɛtʃ/| vt.|装箭羽，给…装上羽毛|He fletched the arrows with goose feathers. 他用鹅毛给箭装上羽毛。|例句：She learned to fletch her own arrows. 她学会了给自己装箭羽。

例句：He carefully fletched each arrow with three brightly colored feathers. 他仔细地用三根颜色鲜艳的羽毛给每支箭装上箭羽。|近义词：feather, plume|反义词：(无直接反义词)|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flyċċe ("arrow feather"), from Proto-Germanic *flihtjō ("feather, fin"). Related to Old Norse flytja ("feather"), Old High German fliunga ("fin").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pleuk- ("to pluck, pull").
