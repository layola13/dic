epistolize: /ɪˈpɪstəlaɪz/ | v. | 写信；用书信往来 | to write letters; to correspond by letters |  She epistollized her friends regularly while traveling abroad.  她在国外旅行时定期给朋友们写信。| synonyms: correspond, write, communicate | antonyms:  call, meet (in person) | verb

epistolize: /ɪˈpɪstəlaɪz/ | v. |写信；书信体写 | to write in an epistolary style | The author epistollized the entire novel, using only letters and diary entries. 作者以书信体写了整部小说，只用了书信和日记。| synonyms: write, compose, pen | antonyms: speak, converse | verb
