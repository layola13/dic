foregift:/ˈfɔːrˌɡɪft/ | n. | 预付款，定金；彩礼 | A payment made in advance, especially as a deposit or a bride-price. | He offered a foregift of $10,000 to secure the property.  他预付了1万美元来确保这处房产。| Synonyms: deposit, down payment, advance payment, earnest money, handsel, bride-price | Antonyms: arrears, debt | noun

foregift:/ˈfɔːrˌɡɪft/ | n. | 礼物，赠品 (古语) | A gift, present (archaic). |  The king bestowed a foregift upon his loyal knight. 国王赐给忠诚的骑士一份礼物。 | Synonyms: gift, present, offering, bounty | Antonyms:  N/A (archaic usage makes antonyms less relevant) | noun
