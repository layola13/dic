heralding:/ˈhɛrəldɪŋ/ | v. | 预示，预告，宣告 | being a sign that (something) is about to happen | The robin's song is heralding the arrival of spring. 知更鸟的歌声预示着春天的到来。 | The warm weather heralded the start of summer.  温暖的天气预示着夏天的开始。 | synonyms: announcing, proclaiming, signaling, foreshadowing | antonyms: concealing, hiding, suppressing | verb
