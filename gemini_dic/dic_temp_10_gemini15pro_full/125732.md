munjeet:/mʌnˈdʒiːt/ | n. |印度茜草，茜草根，茜草染料 |A red dye obtained from the root of the munjeet plant (Rubia cordifolia), native to India and used for dyeing fabrics. |  The vibrant red color of the carpet was achieved using munjeet dye. 这块地毯鲜艳的红色是用印度茜草染料染成的。|  The traditional method of dyeing silk with munjeet has been passed down through generations. 用印度茜草染丝绸的传统方法已经代代相传。 | synonyms: Indian madder, Rubia cordifolia| antonyms: N/A | noun
