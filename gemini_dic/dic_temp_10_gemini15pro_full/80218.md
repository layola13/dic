gods:/ɡɒdz/ (UK) /ɡɑːdz/ (US)| n.|神; 神像; 上帝|A supernatural being considered divine or sacred.|The ancient Greeks worshipped many gods. 古希腊人崇拜许多神。

The gods smiled on their endeavors. 诸神保佑他们的努力。|近义词：deities, divinities|反义词：mortals, humans|noun
