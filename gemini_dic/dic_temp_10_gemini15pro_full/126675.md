namaste:/nɑːˈmɑːsteɪ/ /nəmˈɑːsteɪ/| n. int.|合十礼，你好，再见 (印度问候语)|A respectful greeting or farewell, originating in India, typically accompanied by a slight bow and the palms pressed together. |Namaste, welcome to our home. 合十礼，欢迎来到我们家。|Namaste, it was a pleasure meeting you. 合十礼，很高兴见到你。|近义词：hello, greetings|反义词: (No direct antonym as it's a culturally specific greeting)|词性:名词，感叹词
