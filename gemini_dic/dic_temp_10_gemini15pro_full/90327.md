houseworker:/ˈhaʊswɜːkər/ (US), /ˈhaʊswɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|家务劳动者，家庭佣工|A person who is employed to do housework in a private home.|She worked as a live-in houseworker for a wealthy family. 她在一户富裕人家里做住家佣工。| My grandmother was a houseworker for many years before she retired.  我祖母退休前做了多年的家政工人。 |近义词：maid, domestic, servant, housekeeper, cleaner|反义词: employer, boss|noun
