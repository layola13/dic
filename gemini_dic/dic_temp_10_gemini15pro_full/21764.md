boatnecked:/ˈbəʊtnɛkt/ (UK) /ˈboʊtnɛkt/ (US)| adj.|船领的，船型领口的|Having a wide, low neckline resembling the shape of a boat's hull.|She wore a boatnecked sweater. 她穿着一件船领毛衣。|近义词：scoop-necked, low-necked|反义词：high-necked, turtleneck|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
boat + neck + -ed.  From the resemblance of the neckline to the shape of a boat.
