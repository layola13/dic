aganza:/əˈɡænzə/ | n. | 盛会，庆典 | A large, extravagant, and often ostentatious celebration or event. | The company held a week-long sales aganza to clear out old inventory.  公司举行了一周的销售盛会来清理旧库存。|Example: The town's annual summer aganza is always a big draw for tourists.  这个小镇一年一度的夏季盛典总是吸引很多游客。| synonym: celebration, festival, fiesta, jamboree | antonym: wake, funeral | noun
