commutator:/ˈkɒmjʊˌteɪtə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmjʊˌteɪtər/ (US)| n. |换向器；整流子|A device used in electric motors and generators to reverse the direction of the electric current in the rotor or armature.|The commutator allows the motor to spin continuously by switching the direction of the current. 换向器通过改变电流方向使电机连续旋转。|Synonyms: rectifier, inverter|Antonyms: N/A|noun

commutator:/ˈkɒmjʊˌteɪtə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmjʊˌteɪtər/ (US)| n. |(数学)交换子|In mathematics, an operator that measures the extent to which two operations fail to commute.  |The commutator of two operators A and B is defined as [A, B] = AB - BA. 两个算符A和B的交换子定义为[A,B] = AB - BA。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|noun
