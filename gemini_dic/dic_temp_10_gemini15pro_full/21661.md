blushless:/ˈblʌʃləs/| adj.|无耻的，厚颜无耻的|Without shame or embarrassment; brazen.|He told a blushless lie. 他厚颜无耻地说谎了。|近义词：shameless, impudent, brazen, audacious|反义词：ashamed, embarrassed, modest, shy|词性:形容词
词源: From blush (v.) + -less (suffix forming adjectives meaning "without"). Blush itself comes from Middle English blischen, probably from Old English blyscan "to blush, shine red," related to Old Norse blys "a torch."
