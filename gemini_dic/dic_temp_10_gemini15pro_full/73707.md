forgone:/fɔːrˈɡɒn/ | /fɔːrˈɡɔːn/ | adj. | 放弃的，过去的 | Something that is forgone is no longer possible or available, especially because you have chosen something else. |  She had to accept the forgone conclusion that her career was over. 她不得不接受她的职业生涯已经结束这个无法改变的结论。

The opportunity for further study was a forgone conclusion. 继续深造的机会是必然的。


| 近义词：past, lost, bygone, relinquished, sacrificed | 反义词： attainable, achievable, possible | 词性: 形容词
