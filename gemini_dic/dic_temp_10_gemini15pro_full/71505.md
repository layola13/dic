flanker:/ˈflæŋkər/| n.|侧翼队员，包抄队员，侧卫|A player positioned on the flank, especially in football or rugby.  A flanker is responsible for supporting the forwards and backs, often tackling and disrupting the opposition's play.|The flanker made a crucial tackle, preventing a try.  侧卫队员完成了一次关键的擒抱，阻止了对方达阵。|Synonyms: wing forward, wingman|Antonyms: center, inside player|noun

flanker:/ˈflæŋkər/| v.|包抄，攻击侧翼|To attack the flank of; to outflank.|The enemy troops flanked the retreating army. 敌军包抄了撤退中的部队。|Synonyms: outflank, surround, encompass|Antonyms: confront, face directly|verb
