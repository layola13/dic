awesomeness:/ˈɔːsəmnəs/| n.|令人敬畏，惊叹，雄伟壮观|The awesomeness of the Grand Canyon is truly breathtaking.  大峡谷的壮丽景色真是令人叹为观止。|近义词：wonder, magnificence, grandeur, splendor|反义词: ordinariness, mediocrity, insignificance|noun
Etymology: From awesome + -ness.  "Awesome" originates from "awe," which comes from Old Norse "agi" meaning "fear, terror." Over time, the meaning shifted to a feeling of reverential respect and wonder.
