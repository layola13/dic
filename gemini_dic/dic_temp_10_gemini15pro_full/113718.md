macrochires:/ˈmækrəˌkaɪriːz/ | n. |夜鹰目；夜鹰类|A taxonomic order of nocturnal birds characterized by their long wings, short legs, and wide mouths, commonly known as nightjars, nighthawks, and their relatives.  They are adapted for catching insects in flight.|例句：Macrochires are known for their aerial insectivory. 夜鹰目以其空中食虫习性而闻名。

例句：The macrochires include several families of birds. 夜鹰目包括几个科的鸟类。|近义词：Caprimulgiformes|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
