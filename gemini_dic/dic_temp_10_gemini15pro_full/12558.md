aspirator:/ˈæspɪreɪtər/ (US), /əˈspaɪreɪtər/ (UK)| n.|抽吸器，吸气器|A device that creates suction, used for removing fluids or gases. It can refer to various types of suction devices used in medical, laboratory, or domestic settings.|The dentist used an aspirator to remove saliva from the patient's mouth. 牙医用抽吸器清除病人嘴里的唾液。

The lab technician used an aspirator to remove excess liquid from the beaker. 实验室技术员用吸气器去除烧杯中多余的液体。|近义词：suction device, vacuum pump|反义词:blower, inflator|noun


aspirators:/ˈæspɪreɪtərz/ (US), /əˈspaɪreɪtərz/ (UK)| n.|抽吸器（复数）|Plural form of "aspirator," referring to multiple suction devices.|The hospital ordered new aspirators for the operating rooms. 医院为手术室订购了新的抽吸器。

The science lab had several aspirators of different sizes. 科学实验室有几个不同尺寸的抽吸器。|近义词：suction devices, vacuum pumps|反义词:blowers, inflators|noun
