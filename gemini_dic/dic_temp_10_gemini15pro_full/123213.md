monocles:/ˈmɑːnəkəlz/ (US), /ˈmɒnɪklz/ (UK)| n. |单片眼镜|A single eyeglass, kept in position by the muscles around the eye socket.|The old gentleman adjusted his monocles before peering at the painting. 老绅士调整了一下他的单片眼镜，然后仔细地看着这幅画。|Near synonyms: eyeglasses, spectacles, eyeglass|Antonym: None (lack of a specific antonym, perhaps contact lenses could be considered a modern alternative)|Noun
