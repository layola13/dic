multicast:/ˈmʌltɪkɑːst/| n. v. adj.|多播，组播|Sending data packets to multiple recipients simultaneously on a network.|The network administrator configured the router for multicast. 网络管理员将路由器配置为多播。|近义词：multicasting|反义词：unicast|词性：主要作名词，也可作动词和形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + cast (to throw, send).  The term emerged in the early 1980s with the development of computer networking.
