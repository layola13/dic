hoity-toity: /ˌhɔɪtiˈtɔɪti/ | adj. | 傲慢的，自以为是的 |  Behaving in a way that shows you think you are very important and better than other people. |  She gave me a hoity-toity look. 她傲慢地看了我一眼。|  Snobbish, haughty, supercilious, pretentious | Down-to-earth, modest, humble | adjective


hoity-toity: /ˌhɔɪtiˈtɔɪti/ | n. | 傲慢，自命不凡 | The behavior or attitude of someone who is hoity-toity.  |  She couldn't stand the hoity-toity of the upper classes. 她无法忍受上流社会的傲慢。 | Snobbishness, haughtiness, arrogance | Modesty, humility | noun
