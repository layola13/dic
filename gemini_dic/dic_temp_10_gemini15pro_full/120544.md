miracle: /ˈmɪrəkəl/ (US) /ˈmɪrəkl/ (UK)| n. |奇迹，神奇的事|An event that appears inexplicable by the laws of nature and so is attributed to a divine or supernatural cause. |例句：The recovery of the patient was a miracle. 病人的康复是一个奇迹。

例句：It's a miracle that no one was hurt in the accident.  真是奇迹，事故中竟然没有人受伤。


近义词：wonder, marvel, phenomenon |反义词:disaster, curse|词性:noun
