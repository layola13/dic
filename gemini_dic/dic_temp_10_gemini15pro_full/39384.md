conjunctivitis:/kənˌdʒʌŋktɪˈvaɪtɪs/
n.
结膜炎
Inflammation of the conjunctiva, the membrane that lines the eyelids and covers the exposed surface of the eyeball.
The doctor diagnosed her with conjunctivitis. 医生诊断她得了结膜炎。
Conjunctivitis can be caused by allergies or infections. 结膜炎可能是由过敏或感染引起的。

近义词: pinkeye, red eye
反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease)
