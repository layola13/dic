clambering:/ˈklæmbərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈklæmbərɪŋ/ (UK)| v. |攀登，攀爬|Climbing with difficulty, using both hands and feet.|例句：The children were clambering over the rocks. 孩子们正在攀爬岩石。

例句：They clambered up the steep hill. 他们攀登上了陡峭的山坡。

近义词：climbing, scaling, scrambling, ascending|反义词：descending, falling|词性：动词现在分词
