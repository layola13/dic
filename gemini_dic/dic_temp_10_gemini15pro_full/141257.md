opaled:/ˈoʊpəld/ | adj. | 乳白色的，蛋白石色的；(像蛋白石般)闪光的 | Having the iridescent sheen of an opal; made of or resembling opal. | The sky was opaled with the colours of the setting sun.  天空被落日的颜色染成了乳白色。 | 近义词：iridescent, pearly, shimmering | 反义词：dull, matte | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From opal + -ed.  Opal ultimately comes from the Sanskrit *upala- (“precious stone, gem”).
