pasteurization:/ˌpæstərəˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 巴氏灭菌法；巴氏消毒法 |The process of heating a liquid, such as milk or beer, to a specific temperature for a specific period in order to kill harmful bacteria without destroying the flavor or nutritional value. |例句：Pasteurization is a common method for preserving milk. 巴氏灭菌法是一种常见的牛奶保鲜方法。

例句：The pasteurization of milk has greatly reduced the incidence of milk-borne illnesses. 牛奶的巴氏灭菌大大减少了牛奶传播疾病的发生率。


|近义词：sterilization, disinfection|反义词: contamination|词性: noun
