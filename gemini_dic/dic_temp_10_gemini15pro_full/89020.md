holly:/ˈhɑːli/ (US), /ˈhɒli/ (UK)| n. |冬青; 冬青树; 冬青叶|An evergreen shrub or tree with prickly dark green leaves, small white flowers, and red berries, used especially at Christmas.|We decorated the house with boughs of holly for Christmas. 我们用冬青树枝装饰房子过圣诞节。|Synonyms: evergreen, holly tree|Antonyms: deciduous tree|noun
