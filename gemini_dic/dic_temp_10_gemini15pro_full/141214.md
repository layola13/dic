oophyte:/ˈoʊ.əˌfaɪt/ | n. | 卵生植物 | A plant whose seeds are fertilized and develop outside the body of the mother plant. |  Example 1: Ferns are oophytes.  蕨类植物是卵生植物。

Example 2:  Mosses and liverworts are also examples of oophytes. 苔藓和地钱也是卵生植物的例子。 | Near synonyms:  embryophyte,  land plant | Antonym: None (There's no direct opposite for a plant with external fertilization and development.) | Noun
