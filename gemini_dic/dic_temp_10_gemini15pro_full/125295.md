multifunction:/ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃən/| adj.|多功能的|Having or performing several different functions.|This multifunction printer can copy, scan, and fax. 这台多功能打印机可以复印、扫描和传真。|近义词：versatile, all-purpose|反义词：single-function, specialized|adjective

multifunction:/ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃən/| n.|多功能设备|A device or appliance that performs several different functions.|This multifunction is a real space saver in my small apartment.  这个多功能设备在我的小公寓里非常节省空间。|近义词：all-in-one, multi-purpose device|反义词：single-purpose device|noun
