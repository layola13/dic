inseminates:/ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪts/| vt.| 使受精，授精；灌输| introduces semen into a female reproductive tract or an ovum; impregnates; also, instills (ideas, etc.) into someone's mind. |例句：The farmer inseminates the cows artificially. 农民给母牛人工授精。

例句：The propaganda campaign inseminated the public with fear.  宣传活动向公众灌输了恐惧。|近义词：impregnate, fertilize, infuse, instill|反义词: N/A |verb
