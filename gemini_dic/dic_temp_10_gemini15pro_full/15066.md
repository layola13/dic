babyism:/ˈbeɪbiɪzəm/| n.|婴儿语言；幼稚的言行；|A word, phrase, or action characteristic of a baby.  A childish way of speaking or behaving. |She still uses babyisms like "din-din" for dinner. 她仍然使用像“din-din”这样的婴儿语言来表示晚餐。|He reverted to babyisms when he was sick. 他生病时又恢复了婴儿般的言行。|synonyms: baby talk, childishness, infantilism|antonyms: maturity, sophistication|noun
