anapestic:/ˌænəˈpɛstɪk/ | adj. |抑抑扬格的，抑抑扬格的诗句的 | Of or relating to anapest, a metrical foot consisting of two short or unstressed syllables followed by one long or stressed syllable. | The poem is written in anapestic tetrameter. 这首诗是用抑抑扬格四音步写的。 |  近义词:  dactylic | 反义词: iambic | 词性: adjective

anapestic:/ˌænəˈpɛstɪk/ | n. |抑抑扬格 | A metrical foot consisting of two short or unstressed syllables followed by one long or stressed syllable. | The poet's skillful use of anapest created a sense of urgency. 诗人巧妙地运用抑抑扬格，营造了一种紧迫感。 | 近义词:  dactyl | 反义词: iamb | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀνάπαιστος (anápaistos, “struck back, rebounding”), from ἀναπαίω (anapaiō, “I strike back”), from ἀνά (aná, “back, up”) + παίω (paiō, “I strike”). The term refers to the reversal of the more common dactyl (– ⏑ ⏑).
