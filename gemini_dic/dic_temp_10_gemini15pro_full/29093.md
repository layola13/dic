caryophyllaceous:/ˌkæriəfɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
石竹科的；似石竹科植物的
Relating to or characteristic of plants of the pink family (Caryophyllaceae), typically having opposite leaves, swollen nodes, and flowers with five petals.
Carnations and pinks are caryophyllaceous plants.  康乃馨和石竹都是石竹科植物。
Caryophyllaceous flowers are often found in alpine meadows. 石竹科植物的花通常生长在高山草甸上。
synonyms: pink-like, caryophyllic
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as it describes a botanical family)
词性: adjective
