overscepticism:/ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/| n. |过度怀疑；过分怀疑|Excessive scepticism; an extreme or exaggerated tendency to doubt or disbelieve.|His overscepticism prevented him from seeing the genuine opportunity.|近义词：cynicism, distrust, incredulity|反义词：credulity, gullibility, naiveté|词性:名词

Etymology:
From over- + scepticism.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much."  "Scepticism" comes from the Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful."
