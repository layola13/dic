outfloated:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊtɪd/
verb
超过（在漂浮方面）,胜过,浮力超过
surpassed in floating; having greater buoyancy.
The larger boat outfloated the smaller one in the storm.  这艘大船在暴风雨中比小船更能漂浮。
The new life raft outfloated the old, damaged one. 新的救生筏比旧的、损坏的救生筏漂浮性能更好。
近义词:surpassed, exceeded, outmatched
反义词:submerged, sunk
词性:v.
