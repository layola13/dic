maturities:/məˈtʃʊrətiz/| n.|到期；成熟；到期日|The maturities of these bonds are staggered over the next ten years. 这些债券的到期日错开了未来十年。|例句：The company has several maturities coming up in the next quarter.  这家公司下个季度有几笔款项到期。

例句：Investors are concerned about the maturities of their investments. 投资者担心他们的投资到期日。|近义词：expirations, due dates, deadlines|反义词:inceptions, beginnings|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *maturen*, from Old French *maturer*, from Latin *maturare* "to ripen," from *maturus* "ripe."  The plural form "maturities" refers to multiple instances of reaching maturity or due dates.
