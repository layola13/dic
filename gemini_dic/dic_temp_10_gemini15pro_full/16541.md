barley-break: /ˈbɑːrli breɪk/ | n. | 打谷休息；一种古老的儿童游戏 | A children's game played on a square divided into nine smaller squares, in which one square is left blank and the others are occupied by eight numbered pieces that are moved around to try to rearrange them in numerical order.  Also, a short break or pause, particularly during harvest. | Example 1: The children were playing barley-break in the field. 孩子们正在田野里玩打谷休息游戏。

Example 2:  Let's take a barley-break before we start harvesting again.  我们再开始收割之前休息一下吧。| Synonym:  break, pause, respite, game | Antonym: work, continuation | noun
