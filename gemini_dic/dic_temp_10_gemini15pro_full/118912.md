metazoan: /ˌmɛtəˈzoʊən/ | n. | 后生动物 | An animal of the Metazoa division; a multicellular animal having cells differentiated into tissues and organs and usually a digestive cavity and nervous system. |  Metazoans are a diverse group, ranging from simple sponges to complex mammals. 后生动物是一个多样化的群体，从简单的海绵到复杂的哺乳动物。| synonyms: animal, multicellular animal | antonyms: protozoan, single-celled organism | noun
