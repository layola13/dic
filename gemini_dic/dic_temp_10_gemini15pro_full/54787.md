dispensation:/ˌdɪspenˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 分配；免除；豁免；特许；（宗教）天命；神意 | An act of distributing or giving out something.  An exemption from a rule or usual requirement. | The teacher gave the student a dispensation from taking the final exam due to a family emergency. 老师因家庭紧急情况免除了该学生参加期末考试。 | synonyms: exemption, immunity, exception, release, remission | antonyms:  obligation, requirement, restriction, prohibition | noun

dispensation:/ˌdɪspenˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 分配；施予 | The act of giving or dealing out something. | The dispensation of justice is the responsibility of the courts.  司法的执行是法院的责任。 | synonyms: distribution, allocation, allotment, apportionment | antonyms: withholding, retention, collection | noun
