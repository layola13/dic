hinderers:/ˈhɪndərərz/
noun
阻碍者，妨碍者
Those who hinder or obstruct progress or movement.
The hinderers of progress must be overcome.  进步的阻碍者必须被克服。
synonyms: obstructers, impediments, obstacles, barriers
antonyms: facilitators, helpers, supporters, enablers
n.
