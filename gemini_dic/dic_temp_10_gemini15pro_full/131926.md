noncontinuations:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非延续，中断，终止 |  The noncontinuations of several key programs have hampered progress.  Several programs not continuing have hampered progress. | 例句：The noncontinuations of the peace talks led to renewed hostilities. 和谈的中断导致敌对行动重新开始。

例句：Budget cuts resulted in the noncontinuations of several important research projects. 预算削减导致几个重要研究项目终止。 | 近义词：discontinuations, cessations, interruptions | 反义词：continuations, renewals, extensions | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "continuation" (from continue, from Latin *continuare, from continuus "uninterrupted," from tenere "to hold").  Essentially, it means the state of not continuing.
