fermata:/fərˈmɑːtə/ | n. | 延音，延长记号 | A symbol placed over a note or rest indicating that it is to be held longer than its normal time value.  | The fermata over the final chord added a sense of drama.  延长记号加在最后的和弦上，增添了一种戏剧感。 | synonyms: hold, pause | antonyms:  rush,  accelerando | noun

fermatas:/fərˈmɑːtəz/ | n. | 延音（复数） | Plural form of fermata.  |  The composer used several fermatas throughout the piece. 作曲家在整首曲子中使用了几个延音。 | synonyms: holds, pauses | antonyms: rushes, accelerandos | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *fermata*, meaning "stopped," from the verb *fermare*, "to stop," derived from Late Latin *firmare*, "to strengthen, make firm," ultimately from Latin *firmus*, "firm, strong, steadfast."
