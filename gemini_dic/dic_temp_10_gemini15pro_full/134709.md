nonmatriculated:/ˌnɑːn.məˈtrɪkjə.leɪ.tɪd/
adjective
未注册的；未正式录取的
Not officially enrolled in a regular course of study at a school or college.
Example: He took some nonmatriculated courses at the local university. 他在当地大学修读了一些非学位课程。
Example:  She audited the class as a nonmatriculated student. 她以非学位学生的身份旁听了这门课。
Synonym: non-enrolled, unregistered, unmatriculated
Antonym: matriculated, enrolled, registered
词性: adjective
