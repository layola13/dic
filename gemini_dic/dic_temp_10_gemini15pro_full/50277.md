dephlogisticate:/diːfləˈdʒɪstɪkeɪt/| vt.|去除燃素；使氧化|To remove phlogiston from; to oxidize.  It's an archaic term related to the outdated phlogiston theory of combustion.|Chemists of the 18th century would attempt to dephlogisticate substances. 18世纪的化学家会试图去除物质中的燃素。|近义词:oxidize, burn|反义词:reduce|词性:动词

dephlogistication:/diːfləˌdʒɪstɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|去除燃素；氧化|The action or process of removing phlogiston; oxidation.  Again, this is an outdated term.|The dephlogistication of mercury was a key experiment in the development of modern chemistry.  汞的氧化是现代化学发展中的一个关键实验。|近义词:oxidation, combustion|反义词:reduction|词性:名词
