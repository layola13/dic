counterclaim:/ˈkaʊntərkleɪm/| n.|反诉; 反驳; 抗辩|An opposing claim, especially one made in response to the claims of another.|The defendant filed a counterclaim, alleging breach of contract by the plaintiff.  被告提出了反诉，声称原告违反了合同。|The company issued a counterclaim denying all accusations of wrongdoing. 该公司发表声明驳斥所有不当行为的指控。|近义词:rebuttal, rejoinder, defense|反义词:claim, allegation, assertion|词性:noun
