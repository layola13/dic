foresightedness:/fɔːrˈsaɪtɪdnəs/| n. |远见，先见之明|The ability to anticipate future needs or events and make plans accordingly.  The quality of being able to foresee and prepare wisely for the future.|She demonstrated remarkable foresightedness in investing her money wisely. 她投资理财方面展现出非凡的远见。|近义词：foresight, prudence, vision, wisdom|反义词：shortsightedness, myopia, imprudence|noun

foresighted:/fɔːrˈsaɪtɪd/| adj. |有远见的，有先见之明的|Having or showing foresight; able to anticipate future needs or events.|His foresighted planning ensured the company's success. 他有远见的计划确保了公司的成功。|近义词：prescient, visionary, provident|反义词：shortsighted, myopic, improvident|adjective
