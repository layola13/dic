noncontributiveness:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjʊtɪvnəs/ | n. | 无贡献；不贡献；无益 |  The state or quality of not contributing or being unproductive.  Lack of contribution, especially to a shared effort or goal.  |  His noncontributiveness to the project ultimately led to his dismissal. 他对这个项目的毫无贡献最终导致他被解雇。 |  The team's noncontributiveness resulted in the project's failure.  团队的无所作为导致了项目的失败。 |  inactivity, uselessness, idleness |  contribution, helpfulness, productiveness | noun
