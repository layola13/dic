hedonistic:/ˌhiːdəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 享乐主义的；快乐主义的 |  Engaged in the pursuit of pleasure; sensually self-indulgent. |  He lived a hedonistic lifestyle, indulging in every pleasure imaginable.  他过着享乐主义的生活，沉迷于各种可以想象的快乐。| synonyms: pleasure-seeking, self-indulgent, sybaritic, epicurean | antonyms: ascetic, self-denying, abstemious | adjective

hedonist:/ˈhiːdənɪst/ | n. | 享乐主义者 | A person who believes that the pursuit of pleasure is the most important thing in life. | The hedonist spent his days lounging on the beach, sipping cocktails.  这个享乐主义者整天躺在沙滩上，啜饮着鸡尾酒。| synonyms: pleasure-seeker, voluptuary, epicure | antonyms: ascetic, puritan | noun
