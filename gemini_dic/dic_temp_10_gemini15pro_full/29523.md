catatonia: /ˌkætəˈtoʊniə/ | n. |紧张症，僵住症|A condition characterized by reduced responsiveness to external stimuli, often involving muscle rigidity, stupor, and posturing.|The patient exhibited signs of catatonia, remaining motionless for hours. 病人表现出紧张症的迹象，一动不动地保持了几个小时。|近义词：stupor, rigidity, immobility|反义词:mobility, responsiveness|词性:noun

catatonias: /ˌkætəˈtoʊniəz/ | n. |紧张症（复数）|Plural form of catatonia, referring to multiple instances or types of the condition.|The study examined various catatonias and their underlying causes.  这项研究调查了各种紧张症及其根本原因。|近义词：stupors, rigidities|反义词:mobilities, responses|词性:noun
