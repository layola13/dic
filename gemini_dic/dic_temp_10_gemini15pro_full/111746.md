locusts:/ˈloʊkəsts/ (US) /ˈləʊkəsts/ (UK)| n. |蝗虫，蚱蜢|A large, mainly tropical grasshopper with strong powers of flight, which often travels in vast swarms and strips large areas of vegetation.|  Locusts devoured the entire crop. 蝗虫吞噬了整个庄稼。|synonyms: grasshoppers, cicadas| antonyms: None (Locusts don't have a direct opposite.  Perhaps beneficial insects could be considered a very broad antonym in terms of impact on agriculture.)| noun
