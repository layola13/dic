eternalized: /ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪzd/ /ɪˈtɝːnəlaɪzd/
verb
使永恒化；使不朽
to make eternal; to immortalize
His name was eternalized in the annals of history. 他的名字被载入史册，永垂不朽。
近义词: immortalized, perpetuated, enshrined
反义词: forgotten, obliterated
词性: verb

eternalize: /ɪˈtɜːrnəlaɪzd/ /ɪˈtɝːnəlaɪzd/
adjective
永恒的，不朽的 (少用，通常使用动词的过去分词形式作形容词)
made eternal; immortalized
His eternalized fame will live on forever. 他不朽的声名将永远流传下去。
近义词: eternal, immortal, everlasting
反义词: temporary, transient
词性: adjective
