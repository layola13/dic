antilopine:/ˈæntɪləʊpaɪn/| adj.|羚羊的；像羚羊的|Resembling or characteristic of an antelope, especially in grace and speed.|The antilopine grace of the gazelle was captivating.  瞪羚的羚羊般的优雅令人着迷。|Example: The dancer moved with antilopine grace.  这位舞者以羚羊般的优雅舞动。|Synonyms: gazelle-like, deer-like, graceful, agile|Antonyms: clumsy, awkward|Adjective


antilopine:/ˈæntɪləʊpaɪn/| n.|羚羊类动物|An animal of the antelope family.|Several different antilopines grazed on the savanna.  几种不同的羚羊在草原上吃草。|Example: The zoo had a diverse collection of antilopines.  这家动物园收集了各种各样的羚羊。|Synonyms: antelope, gazelle, springbok|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps predator)|Noun
