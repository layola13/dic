avenue:/ˈævənjuː/ (US), /ˈævɪnjuː/ (UK)| n.|大道，途径，方法|A broad road in a town or city, typically having trees at regular intervals along its sides. Also, a way of approaching a problem or making progress toward something.|例句：We strolled down the avenue, admiring the elegant buildings. 我们沿着大道漫步，欣赏着典雅的建筑。

例句：Diplomacy is the best avenue to peace.  外交是通往和平的最佳途径。|近义词：street, road, boulevard, path, approach, method|反义词: dead end, impasse |词性:noun
