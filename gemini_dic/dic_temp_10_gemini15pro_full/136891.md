nonsocialists:/ˌnɒnˈsoʊʃəlɪsts/
n.|非社会主义者|People who are not socialists; those who do not subscribe to socialist principles.|Many nonsocialists believe in free market capitalism.|近义词:anti-socialists, capitalists|反义词:socialists|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non*, meaning "not"
socialist (noun): from French *socialiste*, ultimately from Latin *socius*, meaning "companion" or "ally"
