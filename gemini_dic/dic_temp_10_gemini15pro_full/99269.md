interconfounding: /ˌɪntərˌkənˈfaʊndɪŋ/ | adj. |相互混杂，交互混淆 | The situation is complex due to interconfounding factors, making it difficult to isolate the true cause.  | 例句1：The interconfounding effects of poverty and lack of education make it difficult to address either issue in isolation. 贫困和缺乏教育的相互混杂效应使得单独解决任何一个问题都很困难。

例句2：Interconfounding variables made it challenging to determine the precise impact of the new policy.  相互混杂的变量使得难以确定新政策的确切影响。| 近义词：intertwined, interacting, correlated | 反义词：independent, separate | 词性:形容词
