myology:/maɪˈɒlədʒi/ /maɪˈɑːlədʒi/
n.
肌肉学
The study of muscles.
Myology is the branch of anatomy that deals with the muscular system.  肌肉学是解剖学中研究肌肉系统的一个分支。
The professor's myology lectures were fascinating. 教授的肌肉学讲座引人入胜。
近义词:  musculoskeletal anatomy
反义词:  None
词性: Noun
