convex:/ˈkɒnvɛks/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvɛks/ (US)| adj.|凸的，凸面的|Curving outwards; bulging.|The lens is convex on one side and concave on the other.  这个透镜一面是凸的，另一面是凹的。|近义词：bulging, rounded, protuberant|反义词: concave|形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin convexus "vaulted, arched, curved," past participle of convehere "to bring together," from com- "together" (see com-) + vehere "to carry" (from PIE root *wegh- "to move, transport in a vehicle").
