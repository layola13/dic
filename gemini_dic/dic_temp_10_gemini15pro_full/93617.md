imbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/| v. |吸收，吸取（液体），接受（信息、想法等）|To absorb or assimilate something, especially information or ideas, or to drink (alcohol). |She imbibed the culture of the country during her year abroad.  她在国外的那一年吸收了这个国家的文化。|Synonyms: absorb, assimilate, drink, soak up, take in|Antonyms: excrete, expel, discharge|verb

imbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/| v. |饮，喝|To drink (alcohol). |He imbibed a large quantity of beer at the party. 他在聚会上喝了很多啤酒。|Synonyms: drink, quaff, swallow|Antonyms: abstain|verb
