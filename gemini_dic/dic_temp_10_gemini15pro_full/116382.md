mastitises:/mæˈstaɪtiːsiːz/| n. |乳腺炎（复数）|Inflammation of the mammary gland, typically due to bacterial infection.  It's characterized by pain, swelling, redness, and warmth in the affected breast, and can also cause fever and chills.|The farmer was concerned about mastitises in his dairy cows.  这位农民担心他的奶牛患上乳腺炎。| The veterinarian treated the sow for mastitises to prevent complications for her piglets.  兽医治疗了母猪的乳腺炎，以防止对她的仔猪造成并发症。|近义词：mammary gland infection|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"mammary health"乳腺健康)|词性:noun
