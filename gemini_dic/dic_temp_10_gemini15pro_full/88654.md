hoards:/hɔːrdz/ | n. | 贮藏物，囤积物 | A large amount of something valuable that is kept hidden. | The dragon guarded its hoard of gold.  这条龙守护着它的金子宝藏。 | synonyms: stockpile, cache, store, reserve | antonyms:  expenditure, spending, waste | noun

hoard:/hɔːrd/ | v. | 囤积，贮藏 | To accumulate and keep (something valuable) in a secret place. | He hoarded old newspapers in the attic. 他在阁楼里囤积旧报纸。 | synonyms: accumulate, amass, stockpile, gather | antonyms: spend, squander, distribute, share | verb
