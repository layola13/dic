familism:/ˈfæmɪlɪzəm/
n.
家庭主义；家族主义
A social structure where the needs of the family are more important than the needs of any individual family members.
Strong familism is a core value in many cultures. 在许多文化中，浓厚的家庭主义是核心价值观。
His sense of familism made him reluctant to move away.  他的家庭观念使他不愿搬走。
近义词：familial piety, clannishness
反义词：individualism
词性：名词
