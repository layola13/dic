biorhythmically:/ˌbaɪoʊˈrɪðmɪkli/

生物节律地; 按照生物节律
in a way that is related to or influenced by biorhythms.  Biorhythms are cyclical variations in physiological processes thought to affect mood, alertness, and physical performance.
Her moods seemed to shift biorhythmically, with periods of high energy followed by slumps. 她的情绪似乎随着生物节律而变化，精力充沛的时期之后是低潮期。

反义词: erratically, irregularly
词性: adverb
