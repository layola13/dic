landfill:/ˈlændfɪl/| vt.|将…填埋；用垃圾填|To dispose of (garbage, etc.) in a landfill.|They landfilled the waste instead of recycling it. 他们把垃圾填埋而不是回收利用。|Synonyms: dump, bury|Antonyms: recycle, compost|verb
