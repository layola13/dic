nonattachment:/ˌnɒnəˈtætʃmənt/ /ˌnɑːnəˈtætʃmənt/| n. |不执着，无执念，超脱|The state of being free from emotional or material attachments.|She practiced nonattachment to material possessions. 她练习对物质财富的超脱。|近义词：detachment, indifference, neutrality|反义词：attachment, dependence, clinging|noun
