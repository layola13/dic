fettled:/ˈfɛt(ə)ld/
adjective
准备好的，整理好的
Made ready, put in order, or repaired.
例句：The mechanic fettled the engine and it runs smoothly now. 技工把引擎修好了，现在运转很顺畅。

例句：Once I've fettled the garden, I'll be ready to plant the vegetables. 一旦我整理好花园，我就可以种菜了。
近义词：fixed, repaired, prepared, ready
反义词：broken, unprepared, disorganized
词性：adjective


fettled:/ˈfɛt(ə)ld/
verb
修理，整理，使准备好
To make ready, put in order, or repair.
例句：He fettled the bicycle before his long ride. 他在长途骑行前整理好了自行车。

例句：She fettled her affairs before going on vacation. 她去度假前把自己的事情安排妥当。
近义词：fix, repair, prepare, mend
反义词：break, damage, neglect
词性：verb


词源: From the dialectal verb *fettle*, meaning "to make ready," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old English *fetel* "girdle, belt," suggesting the idea of preparing or girding oneself for action.
