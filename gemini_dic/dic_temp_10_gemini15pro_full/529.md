abominate:/əˈbɒmɪneɪt/ /əˈbɑːmɪneɪt/| vt.|憎恶，厌恶，痛恨|To feel intense hatred or loathing for something.|She abominates cruelty to animals. 她痛恨虐待动物。|近义词：loathe, detest, despise, abhor|反义词:adore, love, cherish|verb

abomination:/əˌbɒmɪˈneɪʃn/ /əˌbɑːmɪˈneɪʃn/| n.|令人憎恶的事物，可憎之物|Something that is regarded with intense hatred or loathing.|War is an abomination. 战争是令人憎恶的事情。|近义词：atrocity, evil, outrage|反义词:delight, pleasure|noun
