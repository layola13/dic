obstipation:/ˌɒbstɪˈpeɪʃn/ | n. | 严重便秘 |Intractable constipation; severe difficulty in emptying the bowels.  Extreme or complete constipation. |The patient presented with obstipation and abdominal distension.| 近义词: constipation, costiveness | 反义词: diarrhea | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin obstīpātiōn (“a stopping up”), from obstīpō (“I stop up”), from ob- (“against, in front of”) + stīpō (“I pack, press, cram”).
