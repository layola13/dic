lutescent:/luːˈtɛsənt/
adjective
浅黄色的；略带黄色的
Becoming yellowish.  A pale yellow color, especially of fungi.
The gills of the mushroom were lutescent.  这种蘑菇的菌褶呈浅黄色。
近义词: yellowish, buff, straw-colored
反义词:  dark, deep-colored
词性: adjective
