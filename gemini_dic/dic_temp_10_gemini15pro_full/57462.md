draftees:/ˌdræfˈtiːz/
n.
被征募者；应征入伍者
People who have been drafted, especially into the military.
Many young draftees were sent to Vietnam during the war.  许多年轻的应征入伍者在战争期间被派往越南。
The draftees were undergoing basic training. 应征入伍者正在接受基础训练。

Synonyms: conscripts, recruits
Antonyms: volunteers, professionals
词性: 名词 (Noun)
