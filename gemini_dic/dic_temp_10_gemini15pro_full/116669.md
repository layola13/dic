mattock:/ˈmætək/ | n. | 鹤嘴锄; 短柄锄 |A tool with a heavy metal head on a long handle, used for breaking up soil, digging, or cutting roots.| The gardener used a mattock to break up the hard soil. 园丁用鹤嘴锄翻硬土。|  近义词: pickaxe, pick, adze | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其用途的反面，例如 trowel 小铲子，用于精细工作) | 词性: 名词

mattocks:/ˈmætəks/ | n. | 鹤嘴锄; 短柄锄 (复数) | Plural form of mattock.|  The mattocks were leaned against the shed. 鹤嘴锄靠在棚子上。| 近义词: pickaxes, picks, adzes | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源：
From Old English *mattuc, from Proto-Germanic *mattuką,  possibly related to *mat- “meat, food” (source of English meat), with the original sense perhaps being "tool for digging up edible roots".
