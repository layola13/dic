mixable:/ˈmɪksəbl/| adj.|可混合的，可搅拌的|Able to be mixed.  Something that can be combined with something else, typically a liquid, without separating or curdling.|The paint is mixable with water.  这种颜料可以和水混合。|近义词：blendable, combinable|反义词：immiscible, non-mixable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mix + -able.  "mix" comes from Old French "mixter" from Latin "mixtus," past participle of "miscere" meaning "to mix." "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of, fit for, tending to."
