gloomier:/ˈɡluːmiər/ | adj. | 更阴郁的，更忧郁的 | comparative adjective of gloomy; more dark or depressing | The weather is gloomier today than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更阴郁。| The economic forecast is gloomier than expected. 经济预测比预期的更悲观。 | 近义词：darker, drearier, sadder, more depressing | 反义词：brighter, cheerier, happier | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glome*, akin to Old English *glom*, meaning "twilight, dusk."  The sense of "darkness" and later "depression" developed from this original meaning.
