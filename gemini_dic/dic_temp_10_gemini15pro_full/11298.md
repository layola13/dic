ardent:/ˈɑːrdənt/| adj.|热情的，热烈的；炽热的|Characterized by warmth of feeling typically expressed in eager zealous support or activity.|She is an ardent supporter of animal rights. 她是一位热心的动物权益支持者。|Synonyms: passionate, fervent, enthusiastic, zealous|Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|adjective

ardent:/ˈɑːrdənt/| adj.|燃烧的，炽热的 (literary, poetic) |Burning; glowing.|He gazed at her with ardent eyes. 他用炽热的目光注视着她。|Synonyms: burning, fiery, blazing|Antonyms: cold, frigid|adjective
