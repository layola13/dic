antecedency:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dən.si/| n. |先前，优先，居先|The antecedency of the cause to the effect is evident.  原因先于结果是显而易见的。|例句：His antecedency in the company gave him an advantage. 他在公司的资历使他具有优势。|近义词：priority, precedence, anteriority|反义词: posteriority, subsequence|词性:名词

antecedent:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt/| n. adj. |先行词，祖先，先例；在前的，先行的|The antecedent of "it" in the sentence is unclear.  句子中“it”的先行词不明确。|例句：His antecedent had established the family business. 他的祖先创立了家族企业。|近义词：predecessor, forerunner, precursor|反义词: successor, descendant|词性:名词/形容词


词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French antecedence, from late Latin antecedentia, from antecedentem (see antecedent).
