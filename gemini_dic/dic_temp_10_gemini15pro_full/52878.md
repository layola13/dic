diminuendo:/dɪˌmɪnjuˈendoʊ/ (US), /dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ (UK)| n. |渐弱， diminuendos (plural)|A gradual decrease in the volume of a piece of music.|The music ended with a long, slow diminuendo.|Synonyms: decrescendo, fade-out|Antonyms: crescendo, increase|Noun

diminuendo:/dɪˌmɪnjuˈendoʊ/ (US), /dɪˌmɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ (UK)| adv. |渐弱地|Gradually decreasing in volume.|The orchestra played the final bars diminuendo.|Synonyms: decrescendo, fading|Antonyms: crescendo, increasing|Adverb
