agley:/əˈɡleɪ/
adv.
错误地，歪斜地
wrong, awry; off the desired course or direction
The best laid plans of mice and men often go agley.  老鼠和人的最佳计划常常出错。
近义词:awry, amiss, wrong, askew
反义词:right, straight, correctly
词性:副词

agley:/əˈɡleɪ/
adj. (Scottish, obsolete)
歪斜的，错误的
askew, awry
His hat was agley. 他的帽子歪了。
近义词:awry, amiss, wrong, askew
反义词:right, straight, correctly
词性:形容词
