miscibilities:/ˌmɪsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |混溶性，互溶性|The ability of two or more substances to mix together to form a homogeneous solution.  This is often used in the plural to refer to the miscibility properties of multiple substances or mixtures.|

例句：The miscibilities of different oils with water are important in environmental science. 不同油类与水的混溶性在环境科学中很重要。

例句：The chemist studied the miscibilities of various solvents. 化学家研究了各种溶剂的混溶性。

近义词：compatibility, solubility|反义词：immiscibility|词性:noun
