outwiggle: /ˌaʊtˈwɪɡəl/
verb
扭动胜过；比…更会扭动
To wiggle more or better than someone or something else.
The dancer outwiggled her competitors with her energetic and fluid movements.  这位舞者以充满活力和流畅的动作比她的竞争对手扭动得更好。
The baby outwiggled his older brother, escaping from his grasp.  婴儿比他哥哥更会扭动，从他手中逃脱了。
近义词: outmaneuver, surpass, outperform
反义词: be outdone, lose to

词性: verb
