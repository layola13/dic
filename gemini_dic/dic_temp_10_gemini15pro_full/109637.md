lettings: /ˈletɪŋz/
noun
出租; 租赁
the act of renting a house, flat, etc. to someone
例句：The agency specializes in lettings and property management.  这家代理公司专门从事房屋租赁和物业管理。

例句：They have a large portfolio of student lettings. 他们有大量的学生出租房源。

近义词：rentals, leases
反义词：purchases

词性: noun
