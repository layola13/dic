overdecadently:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkədɛntli/
adv. | 过于颓废地，过分放纵地 | Excessively, immoderately, or luxuriously indulgent, often to the point of moral decay or decline. |  He lived overdecadently, indulging in every imaginable pleasure. 他生活过于颓废，沉溺于各种可以想象的享乐。 |  She furnished her apartment overdecadently, with velvet drapes and gold fixtures. 她把她的公寓装饰得过于奢华，配有天鹅绒窗帘和金色装置。 | 近义词：extravagantly, luxuriously, immoderately, excessively | 反义词：moderately, abstemiously, simply, plainly | 词性: 副词 (adverb)

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "decadent" (adjective from French "décadent," ultimately from Latin "decadere" - "to fall down, decay") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
