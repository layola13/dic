monocarpellary:/ˌmɒnəʊkɑːˈpɛləri/ | adj. | 单心皮的 | Having a single carpel.  A carpel is the female reproductive organ of a flower, and monocarpellary means the flower has only one. | Example: The pea flower is monocarpellary. 豌豆花是单心皮的。

Example:  Monocarpellary fruits develop from a single ovary formed from a single carpel. 单心皮果实由单一心皮形成的单一子房发育而来。| 近义词: unicarpellate | 反义词: polycarpellary, multicarpellary | 词性: adjective
