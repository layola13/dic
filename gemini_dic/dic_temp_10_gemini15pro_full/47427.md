deadpanner:/ˈdɛdˌpænər/ (US), /ˈdɛdˌpænə/ (UK)| n.|面无表情的人；不露声色的人|A deadpanner is someone who delivers jokes or funny stories with a serious expression, making the humor unexpected and often more effective.|He's a real deadpanner; you never know if he's joking. 他真是个面瘫，你永远不知道他是不是在开玩笑。|Synonyms: poker face, straight face|Antonyms: expressive person, animated person|noun

deadpan:/ˈdɛdˌpæn/ (US), /ˈdɛdˌpæn/ (UK)| adj.  adv.|面无表情的；不露声色的|Delivered or performed without any change of facial expression, even when expressing something humorous or emotional.|He delivered the joke in a completely deadpan way. 他面无表情地讲了这个笑话。  She deadpanned her response, leaving the audience wondering if she was serious. 她面无表情地回答，让观众怀疑她是不是认真的。|Synonyms: expressionless, impassive, blank|Antonyms: expressive, animated|adjective, adverb
