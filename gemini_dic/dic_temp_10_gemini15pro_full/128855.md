nihilism:/ˈnaɪəˌlɪzəm/ /ˈniːəˌlɪzəm/
n.|虚无主义|The rejection of all religious and moral principles, often in the belief that life is meaningless.|Many young people experiencing an existential crisis turn to nihilism.|pessimism, cynicism, skepticism|optimism, faith, belief|noun
