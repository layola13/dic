helmet-like:/ˈhɛlmɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像头盔的；头盔状的
Resembling a helmet in shape or function.
The turtle's shell is helmet-like, offering protection from predators.  这只乌龟的壳像头盔一样，可以保护它免受捕食者的伤害。
The rock formation had a helmet-like appearance.  这块岩层看起来像头盔一样。
近义词:helmet-shaped, dome-shaped, domed
反义词:open, unprotected, exposed
词性:adjective
