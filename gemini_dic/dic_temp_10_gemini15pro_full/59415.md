earthiest:/ˈɜːrθiɪst/
adjective
最朴实的，最粗俗的，最世俗的
Resembling or suggestive of earth; most earthly or unspiritual; coarse; gross.
例句：He told the earthiest jokes I've ever heard. 他讲了我听过的最粗俗的笑话。

例句：Her earthiest desires were for a big house and a fancy car. 她最世俗的愿望就是拥有一栋大房子和一辆豪华轿车。

近义词：earthy, crude, vulgar, coarse, unrefined, unsophisticated, simple, natural, rustic
反义词：refined, sophisticated, spiritual, heavenly, ethereal


词性：adjective

词源：From earth + -y + -est.  "Earth" comes from Old English "eorþe," meaning "ground, soil, dry land."  The suffix "-y" forms adjectives, meaning "characterized by" or "full of."  The suffix "-est" is used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
