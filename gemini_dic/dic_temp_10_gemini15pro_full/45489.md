curded:/ˈkɜːrdɪd/| adj.|凝固的，变成凝乳状的|Having turned into curds; coagulated.  Thickened; congealed.|The curded milk was perfect for making cheese. 凝固的牛奶非常适合制作奶酪。|近义词：coagulated, thickened, clabbered|反义词:liquid, runny|词性:形容词
词源:From curd + -ed.  Ultimately from the Latin word "coagulāre," meaning "to curdle."
