oracle's: /ˈɔːrəkəlz/ (UK) /ˈɔːrəkəlz/ (US)| n.| 神谕的；预言的；神使的；圣贤的 | Belonging to or associated with an oracle; resembling or characteristic of an oracle; uttered or delivered as if by an oracle. | The oracle's pronouncements were always cryptic.  神谕的宣告总是很神秘。| synonyms: prophetic, divinatory, sibylline, apocalyptic | antonyms: mundane, ordinary, common | possessive noun

Etymology: From Middle English *orakel's*, from *orakel* (from Old French *oracle*, from Latin *oraculum*, from *orari* "to speak, pray") + *-s* (possessive suffix).
