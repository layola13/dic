onerous:/ˈəʊnərəs/ (UK) /ˈɑːnərəs/ (US)| adj.|繁重的，麻烦的，艰巨的|involving a great amount of effort, trouble, or difficulty; burdensome|例句：The onerous task of cleaning the entire house fell to me.  打扫整个房子的繁重任务落到了我身上。|近义词：burdensome, arduous, taxing, difficult, demanding|反义词：easy, simple, light, effortless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *onerosus*, from onus (“burden”) + -osus (“full of”).
