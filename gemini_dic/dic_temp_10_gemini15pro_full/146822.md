overimposing:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ |v. |叠印，重叠；把…强加于|The artist overimposed one image on another to create a layered effect. 艺术家将一个图像叠加在另一个图像上，以创造一种分层效果。 |例句：He overimposed his own ideas onto the project, ignoring the team's input. 他把自己的想法强加到项目上，忽略了团队的意见。|近义词：superimpose, overlay |反义词: separate, detach |词性: 动词

overimposing:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊzɪŋ/ |adj. |专横的，强加的|His overimposing personality made it difficult for others to contribute. 他专横的个性使其他人难以做出贡献。|例句：Her overimposing manner made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她专横的态度让她不受同事欢迎。|近义词：domineering, overbearing |反义词: accommodating, yielding |词性: 形容词
