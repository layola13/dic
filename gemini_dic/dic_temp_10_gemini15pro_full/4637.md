aldol:/ˈældɒl/ (UK), /ˈɔːldɔːl/ (US)| n. |醛醇|An organic compound containing both an aldehyde group (-CHO) and an alcohol group (-OH), usually formed by the aldol reaction.|The aldol reaction is a key step in the synthesis of many organic compounds.|Example 1: The chemist synthesized an aldol by reacting acetaldehyde with a base.  化学家通过使乙醛与碱反应合成了醛醇。

Example 2: Aldols can undergo dehydration to form α,β-unsaturated aldehydes or ketones. 醛醇可以脱水形成α,β-不饱和醛或酮。|近义词: β-hydroxyaldehyde, β-hydroxyketone|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
