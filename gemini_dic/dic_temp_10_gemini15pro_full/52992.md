dinmont:/ˈdɪnmɒnt/| n. adj.|丁蒙(梗犬的一种), 粗壮结实的|A type of short-legged, strong-bodied terrier originating in the Scottish Borders.  Also used to describe something strong and stocky. |例句：The farmer had a loyal Dinmont terrier. 这位农民有一只忠诚的丁蒙梗犬。

|例句：He had a dinmont build, sturdy and compact. 他身材粗壮结实，矮壮紧凑。|近义词：stocky, sturdy, stout, thickset|反义词: slight, slender, thin|词性:名词，形容词
