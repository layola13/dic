cytomegalovirus: /ˌsaɪtoʊˈmɛɡəloʊˌvaɪrəs/ | n. | 巨细胞病毒 | A herpesvirus that can cause serious complications in people with weakened immune systems, especially newborns and transplant recipients.  It is a member of the herpes family of viruses. |  Cytomegalovirus can be dangerous for pregnant women.  巨细胞病毒对孕妇来说可能很危险。

Cytomegalovirus infection is common in infants. 巨细胞病毒感染在婴儿中很常见。 | 近义词: CMV (abbreviation), human herpesvirus 5 (HHV-5) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
