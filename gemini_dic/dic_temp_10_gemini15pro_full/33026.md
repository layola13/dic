chorus: /ˈkɔːrəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːrəs/ (UK)| n. |合唱，副歌，齐声|A group of singers performing together, especially in opera or other musical works. Also, the part of a song that is repeated after each verse.|The chorus sang a beautiful rendition of "Hallelujah."  合唱团演唱了一曲优美的《哈利路亚》。|synonyms: choir, ensemble, refrain|antonyms: solo, individual|noun

chorus: /ˈkɔːrəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːrəs/ (UK)| v. |合唱，齐声说|To sing or speak simultaneously.|The audience chorused their approval. 观众齐声表示赞同。|synonyms: chime in, join in, echo|antonyms: solo, whisper|verb
