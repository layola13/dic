intergalactic:/ˌɪntərɡəˈlæktɪk/| adj.|星系间的，银河间的|Situated or occurring between galaxies; relating to travel between galaxies.|例句：Intergalactic travel is still a dream for humanity. 人类星际旅行仍然是一个梦想。|例句：Scientists are searching for intergalactic signals. 科学家们正在寻找星系间的信号。|近义词：interstellar|反义词：intragalactic (within a galaxy)|词性:adjective

Etymology:
inter- (Latin, "between") + galactic (from galaxy, ultimately from Greek galaxias kyklos "milky circle")
