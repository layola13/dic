maenad:/ˈmiːnæd/ | n. | 女祭司；狂女；酒神的女祭司 | A female follower of Bacchus, known for their wild, ecstatic, and often frenzied behavior. |  The maenads danced wildly in the forest. 女祭司们在森林里疯狂地跳舞。

 The maenads, fueled by wine and religious ecstasy, tore Orpheus limb from limb.  在葡萄酒和宗教狂喜的刺激下，女祭司们将奥菲斯撕成了碎片。| 近义词：bacchante, devotee, reveler | 反义词: prude, ascetic | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Maenās*, from Ancient Greek Μαϊνάς (Mainás), from μαίνομαι (maínomai, “I rage, I rave”).
