emissive:/ɪˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
发光的，发射的
Emitting light; luminous.
The emissive material in the screen makes it bright and clear. 屏幕上的发光材料使它明亮清晰。
近义词: radiant, luminous, bright
反义词: non-emissive, dark, dull
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin ēmissus, past participle of ēmittere (“to send forth, let go, discharge”), from ē- (“ex-”) + mittere (“to send”).
