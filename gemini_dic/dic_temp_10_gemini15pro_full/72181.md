flophouse:/ˈflɑːphaʊs/| n. |廉价旅馆；flop house (美式) /ˈflɒphaʊs/ (英式)|A cheap hotel or lodging house, often providing temporary accommodation for the homeless or down-and-out.|He ended up staying in a flophouse after losing his job and apartment. 他失去工作和公寓后，最终住进了一家廉价旅馆。|Synonyms: dosshouse, hostel, shelter|Antonyms: luxury hotel, penthouse, suite|Noun
