decatoic: /dɪˈkeɪtɔɪk/
| adj. |癸酸的；十烷的|Relating to or derived from decanoic acid (also called capric acid), a saturated fatty acid with ten carbon atoms.  It can also refer to compounds containing ten carbon atoms.|Decatoic acid is found in coconut oil. 癸酸存在于椰子油中。|Synonyms: capric, decyl|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it describes a specific chemical structure.  One might consider shorter or longer chain fatty acids as indirect antonyms, e.g., octanoic, dodecanoic)|adjective
