cross-training: /ˈkrɒs ˌtreɪnɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkrɒs ˌtreɪnɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|交叉训练|The action or practice of engaging in two or more sports or types of training in order to improve fitness or performance, especially in a main sport.|Many athletes use cross-training to improve their overall fitness and prevent injuries.|Synonyms: multi-sport training, varied training|Antonyms: specialization|noun

cross-training: /ˈkrɒs ˌtreɪnɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkrɒs ˌtreɪnɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|交叉训练的|Relating to or involving cross-training.|He adopted a cross-training approach to prepare for the marathon.|Synonyms: multidisciplinary, diversified|Antonyms: specialized, focused|adjective
