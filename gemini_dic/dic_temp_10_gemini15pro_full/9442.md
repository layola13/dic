antireducing:/ˌæntiɹɪˈduːsɪŋ/
adjective
抗还原的，防止还原的
Preventing or inhibiting reduction (a chemical reaction that involves the gain of electrons).  A substance that prevents reduction is an antireducing agent.
The solution contained an antireducing agent to stabilize the compound.  该溶液含有一种抗还原剂以稳定化合物。
Antireducing properties are crucial in maintaining the stability of certain chemicals. 抗还原特性对于维持某些化学物质的稳定性至关重要。
近义词: antioxidant, reducing inhibitor
反义词: reducing, reductant
词性: adjective

词源:
"anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposite of") + "reducing" (present participle of "reduce," from Latin "reducere" - to lead back, bring back, restore).
