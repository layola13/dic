disallows:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊz/
verb
禁止，不允许
to refuse to allow something; to forbid
The school disallows students from bringing cell phones to class. 学校禁止学生带手机进教室。
近义词: forbids, prohibits, bans
反义词: allows, permits, authorizes
词性: verb

Etymology:  From dis- "not, opposite of" + allow.  The prefix "dis-" comes from Latin, meaning "apart, asunder, away, in different directions." "Allow" comes from Old French "alouer," meaning "to praise, approve, grant, allot," from Latin "allocare" "to set aside, assign."
