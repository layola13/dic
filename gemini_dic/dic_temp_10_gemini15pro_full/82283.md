groundswell:/ˈɡraʊndˌswɛl/ | n. |  激增；浪潮；民情汹涌；舆论压力 | A groundswell of opinion/support etc is a sudden increase in a particular feeling among a large group of people. |  A groundswell of support for the proposal emerged from the community. 社区民众对这项提议突然涌现出大量的支持。| synonyms: surge, upsurge, wave, rise | antonyms: decline, decrease, ebb | noun

groundswell:/ˈɡraʊndˌswɛl/ | n. |  涌浪；底涌 | A large, rolling wave that does not break.  | The surfers waited patiently for the perfect groundswell. 冲浪者耐心地等待着完美的涌浪。 | synonyms: roller, swell | antonyms: ripple | noun
