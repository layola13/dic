expansionism:/ɪkˈspænʃənɪzəm/
n.
扩张主义；膨胀主义
The policy or practice of territorial or economic expansion.
例句：The 19th century was a period of rapid expansionism for many European powers. 19世纪是许多欧洲列强快速扩张的时期。

例句：The company's expansionism led to its takeover of several smaller businesses.  公司的扩张主义导致其接管了几家较小的企业。

近义词：imperialism, colonialism, growth
反义词：isolationism, contraction
词性：名词
