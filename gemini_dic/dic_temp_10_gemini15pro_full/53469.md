disarmer:/dɪsˈɑːrmər/ | n. |解除武装者；裁军者；消除敌意的事物；使人消除戒备心的事物| A person or thing that disarms, either literally (taking away weapons) or figuratively (reducing hostility or suspicion). | His gentle approach was a disarmer in the tense negotiations.  他在紧张的谈判中采取了温和的方式，这让人消除了戒备。| 近义词: conciliator, peacemaker, mediator | 反义词: agitator, provocateur | 词性: 名词


词源:  From the verb "disarm" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Disarm" comes from Old French "desarmer," from "des-" (opposite of) + "armer" (to arm), ultimately from Latin "arma" (weapons).
