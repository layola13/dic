jibbing:/ˈdʒɪbɪŋ/
verb
拒绝；不愿做；反感
refusing to do something; objecting to something
He's jibbing at the idea of working on Saturdays. 他不愿在星期六工作。
近义词：balking, resisting, shirking
反义词：complying, accepting
词性：v.
