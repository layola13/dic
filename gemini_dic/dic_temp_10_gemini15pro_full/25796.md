burg:/bɜːrɡ/ | n. | 城镇，市镇；城区 | A fortified town; borough.  Originally referred to a fortified settlement, often on a hill. Now often used as a suffix in place names. |  Pittsburgh is a major burg in Pennsylvania. 匹兹堡是宾夕法尼亚州的一个主要城市。| The burg was known for its annual festival.  这个小镇以其年度节日而闻名。| synonyms: town, city, borough | antonyms: countryside, rural area | noun
