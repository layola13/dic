criminality:/ˌkrɪmɪˈnælɪti/ | n. | 犯罪；犯罪行为；犯罪性 | The state or quality of being criminal; criminal acts or practices. |  The criminality of his actions shocked everyone. 他的行为的犯罪性质震惊了所有人。

The rising criminality in the city is a serious concern. 城市中不断上升的犯罪率是一个严重的问题。 | 近义词：crime, lawlessness, delinquency, wrongdoing | 反义词：lawfulness, legality, obedience | 词性: noun


词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin criminalitas from Latin criminalis (see criminal).
