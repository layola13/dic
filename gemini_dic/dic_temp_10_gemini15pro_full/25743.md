buppie: /ˈbʌpi/ (US), /ˈbʌpi/ (UK)| n. | 雅皮士；黑人雅皮士 |A blend of "black" and "yuppie," referring to a young upwardly mobile black professional. |  例句：He's a buppie who works in a high-powered law firm. 他是一位在一家实力雄厚的律师事务所工作的雅皮士。

例句：Many buppies live in affluent suburbs. 许多雅皮士住在富裕的郊区。| 近义词：yuppie, professional | 反义词: working class, blue collar| noun
