nebula:/ˈnɛbjʊlə/ (US) /ˈnɛbjələ/ (UK)| n.|星云|A cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter.|例句：The Orion Nebula is a popular target for amateur astronomers.  猎户座星云是业余天文学家们的热门观测目标。

例句：The Horsehead Nebula is a dark nebula silhouetted against a bright emission nebula. 马头星云是一个在明亮发射星云背景下的暗星云。|近义词：galaxy, cloud|反义词: void|词性: noun
