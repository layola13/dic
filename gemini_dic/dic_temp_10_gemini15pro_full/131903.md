noncontention:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɛnʃən/ | n. | 无争议；不争辩；默许 |  Absence of contention or dispute; agreement or acquiescence. | The issue of funding was settled by noncontention, with both parties agreeing on the proposed budget. 双方都同意拟议的预算，资金问题通过无争议的方式解决了。

The election became a noncontention when the other candidates withdrew. 当其他候选人退出时，选举变得毫无争议。| 近义词: agreement, acquiescence, harmony, concord, unanimity | 反义词: contention, dispute, disagreement, conflict, opposition | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + contention (from Latin "contendere," meaning "to strive, struggle, contend")
