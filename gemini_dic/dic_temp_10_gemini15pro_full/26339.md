buzzers:/ˈbʌzərz/| n.|蜂鸣器；按钮；(复数)嗡嗡作响的东西|An electrical device that makes a buzzing sound, often used as a signal or alarm.  Also refers to the contestants who press the buzzers in a quiz show.|The buzzers sounded, signaling the end of the round. 蜂鸣器响起，标志着这一轮的结束。

The quiz show host asked a question, and the buzzers went off immediately.  智力竞赛节目主持人问了一个问题，蜂鸣器立即响了。|近义词：alarms, bells, signals|反义词:silence|noun
