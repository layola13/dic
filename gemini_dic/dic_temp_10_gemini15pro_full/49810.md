demonstrate:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪt/ (US) /ˈdɛmənstreɪt/ (UK)| v.|演示，证明，示威|to show or make something clear; to show how something works or how to do something; to take part in a public meeting or march, usually to show support for or opposition to something or someone.|He demonstrated the new product to the customers. 他向顾客演示了新产品。|synonyms: show, prove, exhibit, display|antonyms: conceal, hide, refute|verb

demonstration:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən/ (US) /ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|演示，证明，示威|an act of showing or explaining how something works or is done; an act of showing support for or opposition to something or someone.|The teacher gave a demonstration of the experiment. 老师演示了这个实验。|synonyms: presentation, display, exhibition, protest|antonyms: concealment, suppression|noun
