liposuction:/ˈlɪpoʊˌsʌkʃən/ (US) /ˈlɪpəʊˌsʌkʃən/ (UK)| n.|抽脂术|The surgical removal of fat beneath the skin by means of suction.|She had liposuction to remove fat from her thighs. 她做了抽脂手术去除大腿上的脂肪。|Synonyms: liposculpture, body contouring|Antonyms: fat grafting, lipofilling|noun
