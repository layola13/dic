alligatored:/ˈælɪˌɡeɪtərd/
adj.
像鳄鱼皮的；（尤指皮革）仿鳄鱼皮的; 费劲的，筋疲力尽的
Resembling alligator skin, especially (of leather) imitation alligator skin; exhausted, worn out.
Her handbag was made of alligatored leather. 她的手提包是用仿鳄鱼皮革制成的。

After a long day of hiking, I felt completely alligatored.  经过一整天的徒步旅行，我感到筋疲力尽。

近义词:  crocodile-skin (for leather), exhausted, drained, worn out, spent
反义词:  energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性:  adjective


词源:  From "alligator" + "-ed."  The meaning related to exhaustion likely comes from the tough, textured appearance of alligator skin, suggesting something that has been through a lot of wear and tear.
