incompassion:/ˌɪnkəmˈpæʃən/ | n. |无情，缺乏同情心 | Lack of compassion; absence of pity or concern for others' suffering. | He showed incompassion for the victims of the disaster. 他对灾难的受害者表现出无情。| 近义词: ruthlessness, heartlessness, cruelty, indifference | 反义词: compassion, pity, empathy, sympathy | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + compassion (from Old French compassion, from Latin compassio, from compati “to suffer with,” from com- (with) + pati “to suffer”).
