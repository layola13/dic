hyperimmunities:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnɪtiz/| n. |超强免疫力；超免疫性|The state of having an extremely high level of immunity, typically induced by repeated exposure to an antigen or by the administration of hyperimmune serum.|  The patient exhibited hyperimmunities to several common viruses after the treatment.  治疗后，病人对几种常见病毒表现出超强免疫力。|  hyperimmunity (singular), superimmunity |immunodeficiency, immunosuppression| noun
