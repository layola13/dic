lince:/ˈlinseɪ/ /ˈlɪnsiː/| n.|猞猁|A medium-sized wild cat with tufted ears, short tail, and long legs, found in forests of North America, Europe, and Asia. |The lince stalked its prey through the undergrowth. 猞猁在灌木丛中跟踪它的猎物。|近义词：bobcat, lynx|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal. Could use "domestic cat" for contrast) |noun
