interfrictions:/ˌɪntərˈfrɪkʃənz/
n.
摩擦，冲突，矛盾
The act or process of rubbing one thing against another; friction between different groups or individuals.
例句：The interfrictions between the departments slowed down the project. 部门间的摩擦减缓了项目进度。

例句：Cultural interfrictions can lead to misunderstandings. 文化冲突会导致误解。

近义词：friction, conflict, discord, tension, strife, clash, disagreement, disharmony
反义词：harmony, agreement, accord, concord, cooperation, collaboration


词性: 名词 (plural noun)
词源:  "inter-" (between) + "friction" (rubbing)
