overpriced:/ˌoʊvərˈpraɪst/ | adj. | 价格过高的；要价过高的 | Charging too high a price. |  That handbag is overpriced; you can get it much cheaper online.  那个手提包定价过高；你可以在网上买到便宜得多的。| Synonyms: exorbitant, inflated, steep, excessive | Antonyms: underpriced, reasonable, affordable, cheap | adjective

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "priced" (past participle of "price," from Old French pris "price, value, praise," from Latin pretium "price, reward").
