harbor:/ˈhɑːrbər/ (US) /ˈhɑːbə(r)/ (UK)| v.|  窝藏，怀有(想法或感受)|To give shelter or refuge to.|He harbored a secret ambition to become a writer.  他怀有一个成为作家的秘密抱负。|近义词：shelter, conceal, nurture|反义词：expose, betray|词性:动词
