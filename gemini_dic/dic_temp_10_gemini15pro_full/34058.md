civets:/ˈsɪvɪts/ | n. |灵猫；麝猫|A slender nocturnal carnivorous mammal of tropical Asia and Africa, having a long body, short legs, and a spotted or striped coat.  It produces a musklike secretion used in perfumery. |Civets are nocturnal animals. 灵猫是夜行动物。|
Civet coffee is made from beans that have been eaten and excreted by civets.  猫屎咖啡是由被灵猫吃掉并排泄出来的咖啡豆制成的。| 近义词: genet, mongoose | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun
