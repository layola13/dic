mallow:/ˈmæloʊ/ (US), /ˈmæləʊ/ (UK)| n.|锦葵; 蜀葵;|A plant with hairy stems, broad leaves, and pink, purple, or white flowers.  The leaves and flowers of some types of mallow can be used in salads, and other types are used to make marshmallow.|The field was covered with wild mallows.|Synonyms: hollyhock, rose mallow|Antonyms: N/A|noun


mallow:/ˈmæloʊ/ (US), /ˈmæləʊ/ (UK)| adj.|锦葵色的; 蜀葵色的;|Of a pale purplish-pink color.|She wore a dress of a delicate mallow hue.|Synonyms: lavender, lilac, mauve|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
