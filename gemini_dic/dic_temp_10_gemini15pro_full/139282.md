obliterate: /əˈblɪtəreɪt/
verb
消灭，抹去，摧毁
to remove or destroy all traces of; do away with completely.
The bombing raid obliterated the small town. 轰炸袭击摧毁了小镇。

The heavy snowfall obliterated all signs of the path. 大雪掩盖了所有小路的痕迹。

近义词: destroy, erase, annihilate, eradicate, expunge
反义词: create, build, establish, preserve
