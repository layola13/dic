morningly:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋli/| adv.|早晨地，每天早晨地|This word is not commonly used in modern English.  It means "in the manner of morning," or "every morning."  While grammatically correct, it sounds archaic and unnatural.  Use "every morning" or "each morning" instead. |*He morningly greets the sunrise.* (Archaic/Uncommon -  Better: He greets the sunrise every morning.)|*She morningly practices yoga.* (Archaic/Uncommon - Better: She practices yoga every morning.)|近义词：every morning, each morning, daily|反义词:nightly, every night|词性:adverb
