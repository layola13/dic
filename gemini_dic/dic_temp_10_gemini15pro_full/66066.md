excalceate: /ˌɛksˈkælsieɪt/
verb
脱鞋；赤脚
to remove one's shoes or go barefoot
例句：The monk excalceated before entering the temple.  僧侣在进入寺庙前脱掉了鞋子。

例句：She excalceated and walked across the cool grass. 她脱掉鞋子，走过凉爽的草地。

近义词：discalceate, unshoe, take off one's shoes
反义词：shod, put on shoes
