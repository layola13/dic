hubbing:/ˈhʌbɪŋ/
n.|轮毂加工，轮毂连接|The process of machining a hub or connecting parts through a hub.  This can refer to machining a wheel hub for a car or connecting network components through a central hub.|例句：The hubbing process ensures a precise fit for the wheel bearings. 轮毂加工过程确保了轴承的精确配合。|例句：Network hubbing allows multiple devices to communicate with each other. 网络集线器连接允许多个设备相互通信。|近义词：connecting, joining, centering|反义词：disconnecting, separating|词性:noun
