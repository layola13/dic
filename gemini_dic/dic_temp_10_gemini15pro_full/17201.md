baume:/boʊˈmeɪ/ | n. | 波美度，波美比重计 | A scale for measuring the specific gravity of liquids, especially solutions of sugar, salt, or acids.  It is named after Antoine Baumé, a French chemist. | The baume of the syrup was measured to be 30 degrees. 糖浆的波美度测得为30度。 |  hydrometer, specific gravity | density | noun
