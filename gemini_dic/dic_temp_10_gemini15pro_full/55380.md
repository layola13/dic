distemperately:/dɪsˈtɛmpərɪtli/
adv.
放肆地，无节制地，过度地
in an uncontrolled or excessive way, lacking in moderation
He distemperately lashed out at his critics. 他放肆地抨击了他的批评者。

The child distemperately demanded more candy.  这孩子无节制地要求更多的糖果。

immoderately, excessively, intemperately, unrestrainedly
moderately, temperately, restrainedly
adverb
