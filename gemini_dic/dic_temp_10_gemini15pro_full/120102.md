mimet:/maɪˈmet/ | n. | 模仿者; 小丑 | A performer who acts out stories or scenes without speaking, using only gestures and facial expressions. | The mimet entertained the crowd with his silent performance.  模仿者以他的默剧表演娱乐了观众。 | synonyms: mimic, impersonator, pantomimist | antonyms: speaker, orator | noun

mimet:/maɪˈmet/ | v. | 模仿; 演哑剧 | To act out a story or scene without speaking, using only gestures and facial expressions. | He mimeted eating a banana. 他模仿吃香蕉的动作。 | synonyms: mimic, impersonate, pantomime | antonyms: speak, articulate | verb
