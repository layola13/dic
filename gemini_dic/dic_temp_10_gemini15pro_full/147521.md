overpartiality: /ˌoʊvərˌpɑːrʃiˈælɪti/
noun
过度偏袒，过分偏爱
Excessive favoritism or bias towards someone or something.
The judge was accused of overpartiality towards the defendant.  法官被指控对被告过度偏袒。
His overpartiality towards his youngest child caused resentment among his other children. 他对最小的孩子的过分偏爱引起了其他孩子的怨恨。
近义词: bias, favoritism, prejudice
反义词: impartiality, fairness, objectivity
词性: noun

Etymology:
From over- + partiality.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Partiality" comes from the Old French word "partialité" and ultimately from the Latin "pars," meaning "part."
