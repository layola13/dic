manorialism:/məˈnɔːriəlɪzəm/
n.
庄园制度；领主制
The economic and social system by which the peasants of medieval Europe were rendered dependent on their land and on their lord.
The manor was the basic economic unit of medieval rural life, and manorialism was the system that governed it. 庄园是中世纪农村生活的基本经济单位，而庄园制度是管理它的制度。

Manorialism declined with the rise of towns and a money-based economy. 随着城镇的兴起和货币经济的发展，庄园制度衰落了。

近义词:feudalism, seigniorialism
反义词:(No direct antonym, but consider "capitalism," "market economy")
词性:noun
