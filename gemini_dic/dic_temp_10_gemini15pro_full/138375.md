notist: /ˈnəʊtɪst/ | n. |记录者; 抄写员; 公证人| A person who takes notes, especially one employed to make a formal record of proceedings. |例句：The notist recorded every word of the meeting. 记录员记录了会议的每一个字。|例句：The court notist transcribed the witness's testimony. 法庭记录员抄写了证人的证词。|近义词：recorder, scribe, clerk |反义词: None |词性: noun
词源: From the verb "note" + "-ist" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something).  Ultimately from Latin "nota" (mark, sign).
