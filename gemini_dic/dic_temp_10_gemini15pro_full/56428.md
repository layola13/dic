dollied:/ˈdɒliːd/ (UK) /ˈdɑːliːd/ (US)| v.|搬运（尤指用台车）, (尤指摄影机)被安装在台车上移动|To move (something, especially a camera) on a dolly. |The cameraman dollied in for a close-up. 摄影师用台车把摄影机推近来拍摄特写。|近义词：wheeled, moved, transported|反义词: carried, stationary|词性:动词

词源: From "dolly" (n.) + "-ed".  "Dolly" originally referred to a low, wheeled frame or platform used for moving heavy objects.  Its origin is uncertain, but it may be related to the female name "Dolly," possibly because such platforms were seen as helpful or supportive, like a helpful person.
