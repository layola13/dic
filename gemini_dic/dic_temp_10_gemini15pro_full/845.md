abstractest: /æbˈstræktɪst/
adjective
最抽象的
Most abstract; most theoretical.  Existing in thought or as an idea but not having a physical or concrete existence.
His paintings became increasingly abstractest over time. = 他的画作随着时间的推移变得越来越抽象。
The philosopher's abstractest theories were difficult for the students to grasp. = 这位哲学家最抽象的理论让学生们难以理解。
近义词: most theoretical, most conceptual, most intangible
反义词: most concrete, most tangible, most specific
词性: adjective

词源:
From abstract + -est (superlative suffix).  "Abstract" comes from Late Latin abstractus, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from Latin abs- "away" + trahere "to draw."
