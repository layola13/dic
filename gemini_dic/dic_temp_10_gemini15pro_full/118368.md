meritoriously:/ˌmerɪˈtɔːriəsli/
adv.
值得赞扬地，有功地
in a way that deserves praise or reward
He served the company meritoriously for over thirty years. 他为公司服务了三十多年，功勋卓著。
Synonyms: laudably, commendably, praiseworthily
Antonyms: discreditably, shamefully, dishonorably
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *meritorius, from Latin meritum (“merit, reward, due”), from merēre, mereri (“to deserve, earn”).
