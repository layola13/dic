herbicidal:/ˌhɜːrbɪˈsaɪdl/ /ˌɜːrbɪˈsaɪdl/| adj.|除草的，杀草的|Relating to or acting as an herbicide; weed-killing.|Farmers use herbicidal chemicals to control weeds. 农民使用除草剂来控制杂草。|The herbicidal action of the spray was evident within days.  这种喷雾的除草作用在几天内就显现出来了。|synonyms: weed-killing, phytocidal|antonyms:  growth-promoting|adjective
