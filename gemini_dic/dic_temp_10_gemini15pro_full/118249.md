merchandisable:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzəbl/ /ˈmɜːr-/
| adj. |适于销售的，可销售的|Suitable to be merchandised; able to be marketed or sold. |This old furniture is perfectly merchandisable with a little restoration.  这些旧家具稍加修复就可以出售了。| The band's new album is very merchandisable.  这支乐队的新专辑很适合销售。|marketable, salable, vendable | unmarketable, unsalable | adjective
