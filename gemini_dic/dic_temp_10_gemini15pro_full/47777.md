ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *decantatus, past participle of decantare (“to sing or chant solemnly”), from Latin de- (“from, down from”) + cantare (“to sing”).  The chemical sense arose from the idea of pouring off a liquid as if carefully and ceremoniously.
