impatiens:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənz/| n.|凤仙花;急性子的人|A flowering plant, genus *Impatiens*, of the family Balsaminaceae, native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa, cultivated for its showy flowers. Also, a person who is impatient.|The impatiens in her garden were a riot of color.|近义词:touch-me-not, jewelweed, busy lizzie (for the plant); restless, fidgety (for the person)|反义词:patient (for the person)|词性:noun

impatient:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənt/| adj.|不耐烦的，急躁的|Not patient; easily annoyed by someone's mistakes or because you have to wait.|He became impatient with the slow progress.|近义词:restless, fidgety, restive, antsy|反义词:patient, tolerant, forbearing|词性:adjective
