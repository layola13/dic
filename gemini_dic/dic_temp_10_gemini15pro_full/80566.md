goody-goody: /ˈɡʊdi ˌɡʊdi/ | adj. n. |伪善的，假正经的；伪君子|A person who behaves extremely well in an affected way, often to please people in authority.  A goody-goody is someone who always follows the rules and tries to please authority figures, often appearing insincere. |She was such a goody-goody in school, always telling the teacher on other kids.  她在学校里真是个伪君子，总是向老师打小报告。| near synonyms: sanctimonious, self-righteous, priggish | near antonyms: rebellious, mischievous, unruly | adjective
