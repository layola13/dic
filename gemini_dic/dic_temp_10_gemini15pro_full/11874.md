arriving:/əˈraɪvɪŋ/
verb, adjective|到达，抵达|The act of coming to a place.  The arriving train was delayed. 到达的火车晚点了。|例句：The guests are arriving soon. 客人们很快就要到了。|近义词：coming, approaching, reaching|反义词:departing, leaving|词性:动词的现在分词/形容词

arriving:/əˈraɪvɪŋ/
adjective|到达的，新到的|Used to describe someone or something that is coming to a place.  The arriving passengers were greeted by the hotel staff. 刚到的旅客受到了酒店员工的迎接。|例句：The arriving flight is from London.  即将到达的航班来自伦敦。|近义词：incoming, inbound|反义词:outgoing, departing|词性:形容词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The train arrives at 8 am every day. 火车每天早上8点到达。
She arrives early for work every morning. 她每天早上上班都很早。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The plane is arriving now. 飞机现在正在降落。
They are arriving later today. 他们今天晚些时候会到。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The guests have just arrived. 客人们刚到。
The package has arrived safely. 包裹已安全送达。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been arriving early all week. 我们整个星期都到得很早。
They have been arriving late to class recently.  他们最近上课一直迟到。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The bus arrived late yesterday. 公共汽车昨天晚点到达。
He arrived at the party at 9 pm. 他晚上九点到达了派对。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was arriving home when I saw him. 我正要到家的时候看到了他。
She was arriving at the station when the train left.  她到达车站时火车开走了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The train had already arrived when I got to the station. 我到车站时，火车已经到了。
They had arrived at the hotel before the storm started. 他们在暴风雨开始前到达了酒店。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been arriving late for weeks before they got a warning.  他们在收到警告之前已经连续几周迟到了。
We had been arriving early to practice all season.  我们整个赛季都提前到达进行练习。



一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The flight will arrive at 6 pm tomorrow.  航班将于明天下午6点到达。
She will arrive next week. 她下周会到。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be arriving home around 6 pm.  我将在下午6点左右到家。
They will be arriving at the airport at noon.  他们将在中午到达机场。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By this time tomorrow, I will have arrived in London.  到明天这个时候，我就已经到达伦敦了。
By the end of the week, they will have arrived at their destination. 到本周结束时，他们将到达目的地。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, we will have been arriving early for work for two years.  到明年，我们将已经提前两年上班了。
By the time the party starts, we will have been arriving guests for an hour.  到派对开始时，我们将已经接待客人一个小时了。


虚拟语气:
If she were arriving earlier, we could go to the cinema.  如果她早点到，我们就可以去看电影了。
I wish they were arriving tomorrow instead of next week.  我希望他们是明天而不是下周到达。

被动语态:
The decision was arrived at after much debate.  经过一番激烈的辩论后，才做出了这个决定。（注意：arrive at通常用于表示达成协议或决定，而不是到达某个地点）
No consensus was arrived at during the meeting.  会议期间没有达成共识。


疑问句:
When are they arriving? 他们什么时候到？
Is he arriving tomorrow? 他明天到吗？

否定句:
They are not arriving until next week. 他们要到下周才到。
He was not arriving when I left. 我离开时他还没到。
