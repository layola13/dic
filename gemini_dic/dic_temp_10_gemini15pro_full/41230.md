corallin:/ˈkɒrəlɪn/ /ˈkɔːrəlɪn/
adjective
珊瑚的；珊瑚色的；由珊瑚组成的
Made of coral; resembling coral.
The divers discovered a corallin reef teeming with life. 潜水员发现了一个充满生机的珊瑚礁。
synonyms: coral, coralloid
antonyms: non-coralline
词性: adjective


coralline:/ˈkɒrəlɪn/ /ˈkɔːrəlɪn/
noun
珊瑚藻
A type of red algae that secretes calcium carbonate, contributing to the formation of coral reefs.
Coralline algae play a crucial role in cementing coral reefs together.  珊瑚藻在将珊瑚礁粘合在一起方面起着至关重要的作用。
synonyms: n/a
antonyms: n/a
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corallinus, from corallium (“coral”).
