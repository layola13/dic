insurrectionist:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪst/
n.
暴动者，叛乱者，起义者
A person who takes part in an insurrection; a rebel.
The insurrectionists stormed the capitol building. 暴动者冲进了国会大厦。
synonyms: rebel, revolutionary, insurgent, mutineer, rioter
antonyms: loyalist, conformist
noun
