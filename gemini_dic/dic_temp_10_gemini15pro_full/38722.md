conditional:/kənˈdɪʃənəl/| adj. n.|有条件的，条件句；条件语|Depending on a condition; expressing a condition.  A grammatical mood or form used to express a proposition whose validity is dependent on some condition.|例句：Attendance at the meeting is conditional upon registering beforehand. 参加会议的条件是事先登记。|近义词：dependent, contingent, provisional, subject to|反义词:unconditional, absolute, categorical|词性:形容词

conditional:/kənˈdɪʃənəl/| n.|条件句；条件语|A sentence or clause expressing a condition. |例句：The “if” clause in a sentence is a conditional. 句子中的“如果”从句是一个条件句。|近义词：provision, stipulation, prerequisite|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
