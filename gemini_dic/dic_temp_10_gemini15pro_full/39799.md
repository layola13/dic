consortia:/kənˈsɔːrʃə/ /kənˈsɔːrtiə/| n. |财团，联合体，共同体|A consortium is an association of two or more individuals, companies, organizations or governments (or any combination of these entities) with the objective of participating in a joint venture or pooling their resources for achieving a common goal.|Several universities formed a consortium to conduct research on climate change. 几所大学组成了一个联盟来进行气候变化研究。|synonyms: alliance, coalition, federation, union|antonyms:  division, separation|noun
