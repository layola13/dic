colossus:/kəˈlɒsəs/ (UK), /kəˈlɑːsəs/ (US)| n. | 巨像；巨人；庞然大物 | A statue of gigantic size; a person or thing of immense size or power. |  The company is a colossus in the tech industry.  这家公司是科技行业的巨头。| synonyms: giant, behemoth, mammoth, titan | antonyms: dwarf, pygmy | noun

colossal:/kəˈlɒsəl/ (UK), /kəˈlɑːsəl/ (US)| adj. | 巨大的；庞大的 | Extremely large or great. | They suffered a colossal defeat in the election. 他们在选举中惨败。| synonyms: enormous, gigantic, immense, huge | antonyms: tiny, minuscule | adjective
