condones:/kənˈdoʊnz/| v.|宽恕，原谅，纵容|to accept or allow (behavior that is considered morally wrong or offensive) to continue|例句：The college cannot condone any behavior that involves illicit drugs.  这所大学不能容忍任何涉及非法毒品的行径。

例句：She couldn't condone his dishonesty. 她不能容忍他的不诚实。|近义词：forgive, pardon, excuse, overlook, tolerate|反义词：condemn, denounce, criticize|词性：动词
