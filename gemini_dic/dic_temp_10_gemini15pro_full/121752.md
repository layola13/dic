mispublished:/ˌmɪsˈpʌblɪʃt/
verb
错误地出版；刊登错误
published incorrectly, containing errors.
The article was mispublished with several factual inaccuracies. 这篇文章出版时出现了几个事实错误。
近义词: misprinted, published incorrectly
反义词: published correctly, accurately printed
词性: verb

词源:
mis- (prefix): wrongly, badly.  From Old English, akin to Old High German missi "wrongly."
publish (verb): to prepare and issue (a book, journal, piece of music, etc.) for public sale or distribution. From Latin publicare "make public," from publicus "public."
