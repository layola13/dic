culminate:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪt/| v. |达到顶点，最终达到，告终|reach a climax or point of highest development.|例句：The celebration culminated in a spectacular fireworks display.  庆祝活动以一场壮观的烟火表演达到高潮。

例句：Her career culminated in her appointment as CEO. 她的职业生涯最终以她被任命为首席执行官而达到顶峰。|近义词：climax, peak, conclude, end|反义词:begin, start, commence|词性:动词


culmination:/ˌkʌlmɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |顶点，高潮，最终结果|the highest or climactic point of something, especially something attained after a long time.|例句：The summit meeting was the culmination of several months of negotiation. 峰会是数月谈判的最终结果。

例句：Winning the championship was the culmination of years of hard work. 赢得冠军是多年努力的顶峰。|近义词：climax, peak, apex, conclusion|反义词:beginning, start, commencement|词性:名词
