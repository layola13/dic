loest:/ˈloʊɪst/ | adj. | 最低的，最小的 |  Superlative form of "loe" (obsolete form of "low"). Meaning the lowest in position, degree, or value.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |  He bowed his loest to the king. 他向国王深深鞠躬。(This example uses "loest" metaphorically to mean the deepest bow, indicating utmost respect.) |  Lowest, smallest, least | Highest, greatest, most | adjective
