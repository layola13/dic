linings:/ˈlaɪnɪŋz/
n.
衬里，内衬；里子
A layer of material covering the inside surface of something.
The coat had a silk lining.  这件外套有丝绸衬里。

近义词:facing, inner layer, interior, covering
反义词:exterior, outside
