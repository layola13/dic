misboden:/mɪsˈboʊdən/
adjective
offered incorrectly; made a faulty offer
This word is archaic and rarely used.  It comes from the Old English "misbēodan," meaning "to offer improperly."  The prefix "mis-" indicates something done wrongly or incorrectly.
He misboden the price of the horse, offering far too little. 他对马的出价失误了，出价太低了。
The merchant misboden the goods, leading to a dispute. 商人对商品出价错误，导致了纠纷。
Near Synonyms: misoffered, wrongly offered
Antonyms: correctly offered, properly offered
词性: adjective
