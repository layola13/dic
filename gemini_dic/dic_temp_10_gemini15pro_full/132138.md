noncultivable:/ˌnɒnkʌlˈtɪvəbl/ | adj. | 不可培养的 | Not able to be grown in laboratory culture.  This term is often used in microbiology to describe microorganisms that cannot be grown using standard laboratory methods. | Many microorganisms found in environmental samples are noncultivable, making their study challenging.  许多在环境样本中发现的微生物是不可培养的，这使得研究它们具有挑战性。

The noncultivable bacteria posed a significant hurdle for the researchers. 这些不可培养的细菌给研究人员带来了巨大的障碍。| Near synonyms: uncultivable, unculturable | Near antonyms: cultivable, culturable | word type: adjective
