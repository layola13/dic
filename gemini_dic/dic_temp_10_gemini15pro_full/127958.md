neoprene: /ˈniːəpriːn/ (US) /ˈniːəpriːn/ (UK)| n.|氯丁橡胶，聚氯丁二烯|A synthetic rubber resistant to oils, heat, and weathering.|例句：Wetsuits are often made of neoprene.  潜水服通常由氯丁橡胶制成。

例句：Neoprene gloves provide good protection from chemicals. 氯丁橡胶手套可以很好地防止化学物质腐蚀。|近义词：polychloroprene|反义词:|noun
