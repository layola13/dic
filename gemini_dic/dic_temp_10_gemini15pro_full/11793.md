arraigning: /əˈreɪnɪŋ/
verb
传讯；责难；控告
calling someone before a court to answer a criminal charge; accusing someone of wrongdoing.
The prosecutor is arraigning the defendant on charges of theft.  检察官正在就盗窃罪对被告进行传讯。

He was arraigned for murder. 他因谋杀罪被传讯。

Synonym: accusing, indicting, prosecuting, charging
Antonym: acquitting, exonerating, defending, absolving
词性: 动词 (verb)
