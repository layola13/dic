manchet:/ˈmæntʃɪt/
n.
小圆白面包，上等白面包
A small loaf of fine white bread.
The baker prepared a fresh manchet for the king's table.  面包师为国王的餐桌准备了一个新鲜的小圆白面包。

近义词: roll, loaf, bun
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "coarse bread")

词性: noun
