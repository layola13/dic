innocent:/ˈɪnəs(ə)nt/ | adj. n.|无辜的，清白的；无罪的人；天真的人|Free from guilt or wrongdoing; naive or unsuspecting.|例句：The jury found the defendant innocent. 陪审团认定被告无罪。|例句：She has an innocent face. 她有一张天真的脸。|近义词：guileless, harmless, pure, blameless|反义词：guilty, culpable, wicked|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology:
From Old French *innocent* (Modern French *innocent*), from Latin *innocentem* (nominative *innocens*), present participle of *innocere* "to be harmless," from *in-* "not" + *nocēre* "to harm."
