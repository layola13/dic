antecursor:/ˌæntiˈkɜːrsər/ | n. |先驱，前兆，预兆|Something that precedes and foreshadows something else.  A forerunner; a precursor. |例句：The dark clouds were an antecursor to the storm.  乌云是暴风雨的前兆。

例句：The invention of the telegraph was an antecursor to the development of the internet. 电报的发明是互联网发展的前兆。|近义词：precursor, forerunner, harbinger, herald |反义词:aftermath, consequence, result |词性:noun
