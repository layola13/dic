changeovers:/ˈtʃeɪndʒˌoʊvərz/
n.|转换，切换，交接|The changeovers between shifts were often chaotic. 轮班交接常常很混乱。|例句：The factory implemented automated changeovers to reduce downtime. 工厂实施了自动化转换以减少停机时间。

例句：Smooth changeovers are essential for maintaining production efficiency. 平稳的交接对于维持生产效率至关重要。

近义词：transitions, switches, handovers
反义词：continuations, stagnations
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "change" + "over". "Change" comes from Old French "changier" (to change, alter). "Over" comes from Old English "ofer" (above, across, beyond).
