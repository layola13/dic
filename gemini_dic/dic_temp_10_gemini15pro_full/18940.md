adjective
洒满的，点缀的
Covered or sprinkled with something.
The meadow was besprent with wildflowers.  草地上点缀着野花。
The ship's deck was besprent with sea spray. 船的甲板上洒满了海浪。

反义词: bare, clean, empty
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *besprengen, from Old English besprengan (“to sprinkle, besprinkle”), from be- + sprengan (“to sprinkle”).  Cognate with Dutch besprengen (“to sprinkle”), German besprengen (“to sprinkle”), Swedish bespringa (“to leap, jump”).
