didst:/dɪdst/| v.|你做(do的第二人称单数过去式)|A second person singular past tense form of the verb "do".|Thou didst speak wisely. 你说得很有智慧。|You did well. 你做得很好。|近义词：did, performed, executed|反义词：forbore, refrained|verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
You do your homework every day.  你每天都做你的家庭作业。
She does her best in every situation. 她在任何情况下都尽力而为。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
You are doing a great job. 你做得很好。
He is doing the dishes right now. 他现在正在洗碗。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
You have done well in your exams. 你考试考得很好。
She has done everything she could. 她已经尽力了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
You have been doing this for hours. 你已经做了好几个小时了。
He has been doing research on this topic for months. 他几个月来一直在研究这个课题。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
You did a fantastic job yesterday. 你昨天做得非常出色。
She did her homework last night. 她昨晚做了她的家庭作业。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
You were doing the dishes when I called. 我打电话时你正在洗碗。
He was doing his homework when the power went out. 停电时他正在做作业。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
You had done your homework before you went out. 你出去玩之前已经做完了作业。
She had done everything she could before asking for help. 在寻求帮助之前，她已经尽力了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
You had been doing research for months before you published your findings. 你发表你的研究结果之前已经做了几个月的研究。
He had been working on this project for years before it was completed.  他在这个项目完成之前已经工作了好几年。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
You will do great things in the future. 你将来会成就一番大事业。
She will do her best to help you. 她会尽力帮助你。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
You will be doing your homework this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候你将会在做作业。
He will be doing research next semester. 他下学期将会做研究。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
You will have done your homework by the time I get home.  在我到家之前，你将会完成你的作业。
She will have done everything she can by then. 到那时，她将已经尽力了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
You will have been doing this for ten years by next year. 到明年你将已经做这件事十年了。
He will have been studying medicine for six years by the time he graduates. 到他毕业时，他将已经学了六年医。


虚拟语气:
If you did your homework, you would get better grades.  如果你做了作业，你的成绩会更好。
I wish I did more to help. 我希望我做了更多来帮助。


被动语态:
The work was done quickly and efficiently. 这项工作被快速高效地完成了。
The dishes were being done when I arrived. 我到达时，碗碟正在被洗。


疑问句:
Did you do your homework? 你做作业了吗？
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？


否定句:
You didn't do your homework. 你没做作业。
She didn't do anything wrong. 她什么也没做错。



PHRASE->
How do you do? 你好 (正式问候)
What do you do? 你是做什么工作的？
Why did you do that? 你为什么要那样做？
When did you do it? 你什么时候做的？
Where did you do it? 你在哪里做的？

注意事项:  didst 是古英语用法，现代英语中已不再使用。  在现代英语中，用 did 代替 didst。
