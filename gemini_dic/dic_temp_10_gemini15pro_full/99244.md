intercommunicable:/ˌɪntər.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.kə.bəl/
adjective
可互相传递的；可互相交流的
Capable of being communicated between each other.
Measles is an intercommunicable disease. 麻疹是一种可互相传染的疾病。
communicable, contagious, transmittable
noncommunicable, noncontagious
adjective
