consentingly:/kənˈsɛntɪŋli/
adverb
同意地，默认地
in a consenting manner; willingly.
She consentingly agreed to help with the project. 她欣然同意帮助完成这个项目。
He consentingly nodded his head, accepting the terms of the agreement. 他点头表示同意，接受了协议的条款。
近义词:willingly, readily, voluntarily
反义词:unwillingly, reluctantly, forcibly
词性:adverb
