disdainously:/dɪsˈdeɪnəsli/
adverb
轻蔑地，鄙夷地
Showing contempt or lack of respect.
She disdainously rejected his offer of help. 她轻蔑地拒绝了他的帮助。
近义词: contemptuously, scornfully, haughtily
反义词: respectfully, admiringly
词性: 副词
词源: From disdain (n.) + -ous (adj.) + -ly (adv.). Disdain comes from Old French desdeigner, from des- (expressing negation) + deigner "to deign," from Latin dignari "to deem worthy."
