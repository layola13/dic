gleamless:/ˈɡliːmlɪs/| adj.|无光泽的，黯淡的|Lacking gleam; dull or not shiny.|The old car had gleamless paint, covered in dust and scratches. 这辆旧车的油漆毫无光泽，满是灰尘和划痕。|Synonyms: dull, matte, lackluster, lusterless|Antonyms: gleaming, shiny, glossy, radiant|adjective
