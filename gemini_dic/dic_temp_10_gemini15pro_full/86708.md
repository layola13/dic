helmet:/ˈhɛlmɪt/ | n. |头盔；安全帽|A hard or padded protective hat, typically worn by motorcyclists, construction workers, or soldiers. |例句：
He always wears a helmet when he rides his motorcycle.
他骑摩托车时总是戴着头盔。

The construction worker's helmet protected him from falling debris.
建筑工人的头盔保护他免受坠落碎片的伤害。|近义词：hard hat, headgear |反义词: None (No direct antonym for a protective device)|noun
