nondeliverances:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪvərənsɪz/ (US: /ˌnɑːndɪˈlɪvərənsɪz/)
noun
未交付;未履行;未实现
The state of not being delivered, performed, or realized.
The company faced numerous customer complaints due to nondeliverances of promised goods.  这家公司因未能交付承诺的货物而面临众多客户投诉。

The frequent nondeliverances of the postal service are becoming a serious problem. 邮政服务的频繁未交付正成为一个严重问题。

Synonyms:  nonperformance, failure, default, breach, omission
Antonyms: delivery, fulfillment, performance, achievement, execution
词性: 名词
