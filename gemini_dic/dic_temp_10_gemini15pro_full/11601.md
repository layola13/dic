arkose:/ˈɑːrkoʊs/| n. |长石砂岩|A sedimentary rock composed primarily of feldspar grains, usually derived from the erosion of granitic rocks, along with quartz and other minerals. |The arkose formations in this region suggest a nearby granitic source rock.  这个地区的砂岩地层表明附近有花岗岩源岩。|
The geologist identified the sample as arkose due to its high feldspar content. 地质学家根据其高长石含量将样品鉴定为长石砂岩。|近义词:feldspathic sandstone|反义词: (No direct antonym, but rocks like quartz sandstone or shale contrast in composition)|词性:noun
