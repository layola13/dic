out-shrieking:/ˌaʊtˈʃriːkɪŋ/
verb
发出比...更大的尖叫声；盖过...的尖叫声
Screaming louder than something or someone else.
The wind out-shrieked the cries of the gulls. 风的呼啸声盖过了海鸥的叫声。
Synonyms: out-yell, out-scream, drown out
Antonyms: be drowned out, be quieter than
词性: 动词
