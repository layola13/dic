deadlocked:/ˈdedlɒkt/ (UK), /ˈdedˌlɑːkt/ (US)| adj.|僵持的，陷入僵局的|A situation in which no progress can be made or no agreement can be reached because of a disagreement between two or more parties.|The negotiations remained deadlocked after several hours. 谈判几个小时后仍处于僵局。|近义词：stalemated, gridlocked, at a standstill|反义词:fluid, progressing, resolved|词性:形容词

词源:  From "dead" (completely) + "lock" (a mechanism for fastening something).  Implies a situation as firmly fixed and unyielding as a locked mechanism.
