What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

What are you cooking right now? 你现在正在做什么饭？
What is she studying at the moment? 她现在正在学习什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):

What has she achieved in her career? 她在事业上取得了什么成就？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
