overdestructive:/ˌoʊvərdiˈstrʌktiv/ | adj. | 过度破坏性的；破坏性过大的 | Excessively destructive; causing too much destruction. | The overdestructive force of the hurricane flattened the entire town. 飓风的过度破坏力夷平了整个城镇。 |  The overdestructive logging practices left the forest barren. 过度破坏性的伐木行为使森林变得荒芜。 | 近义词：ruinous, devastating, cataclysmic | 反义词： constructive, beneficial, restorative | 词性：形容词

词源：
"over-" (prefix) + "destructive" (adjective)
"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively."
"destructive" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French "destructif," from Latin "destruct-" (stem of "destruere") meaning "to tear down, destroy," + "-ive" (suffix forming adjectives).
