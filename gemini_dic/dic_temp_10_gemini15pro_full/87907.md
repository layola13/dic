hieroglyph:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪf/ | n. | 象形文字 |A character used in a system of pictorial writing, particularly that of the ancient Egyptians, in which many of the symbols represent recognizable objects, but others are conventionalized and represent sounds or words.| The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphs.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。| synonym: ideogram, pictogram | antonym: letter, alphabet | noun

hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 象形文字的 | Relating to or expressed by hieroglyphs; obscure or enigmatic.|  The hieroglyphic inscriptions on the tomb told the story of the pharaoh. 墓穴上的象形文字铭文讲述了法老的故事。 | synonym: symbolic, cryptic | antonym: clear, explicit | adjective
