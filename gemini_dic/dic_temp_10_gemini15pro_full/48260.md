decore:/dəˈkɔːr/ | n. |装饰;装潢;舞台美术|The decore of the restaurant was elegant and inviting.  这家餐厅的装潢优雅而诱人。|The stage decore was minimal, focusing on the actors. 舞台的装饰极简，突出演员。|近义词:decoration, ornamentation, adornment|反义词:plainness, simplicity|词性:名词

词源: From French décor, from décorer "to decorate," from Old French decorer. Ultimately from Latin decorare "to adorn, embellish, beautify."
