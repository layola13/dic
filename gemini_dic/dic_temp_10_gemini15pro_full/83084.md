guruships:/ˈɡʊruːʃɪps/
n.
大师地位；专家地位；导师地位
The state or position of being a guru; expertise or authority in a particular field.
Many people seek guruships in the world of technology. 很多人在科技领域寻求大师地位。
He achieved guruship in the field of artificial intelligence. 他在人工智能领域取得了大师地位。
近义词: mastery, expertise, authority, leadership
反义词: inexperience, ignorance, incompetence
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From guru + -ship.  "Guru" comes from Sanskrit गुरु (gurú) meaning "heavy, weighty; venerable, respectable; a spiritual parent or preceptor." "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
