open-neck:/ˈəʊpən nek/ (US) /ˈoʊpən nek/| adj.|开领的，敞领的|A shirt or garment with a collar that is open at the neck, not fastened with a button or tie.|He wore an open-neck shirt and jeans. 他穿着一件开领衬衫和牛仔裤。|Synonyms: unbuttoned, unfastened, open-collared|Antonyms: buttoned-up, fastened, closed-neck|adjective

open-neck:/ˈəʊpən nek/ (US) /ˈoʊpən nek/| n.|开领衬衫|A shirt with an open neck.|He chose a stylish open-neck for the evening. 他为晚上选择了一件时尚的开领衬衫。|Synonyms: open-necked shirt, open-collared shirt|Antonyms: button-down shirt, formal shirt|noun
