overscrupulousness:/ˌoʊvərˈskruːpjələsnəs/| n. |过度谨慎；过分小心；过于拘泥|The state of being excessively scrupulous, characterized by extreme caution and concern for details, often to the point of being overly cautious or hesitant.|His overscrupulousness prevented him from taking any risks. 他的过分小心使他不敢冒险。|近义词：meticulousness, fastidiousness, punctiliousness|反义词：carelessness, negligence, recklessness|词性: noun

词源:
From over- + scrupulous + -ness.  "Over-" indicates excess, "scrupulous" means having moral integrity and being very careful about doing what is right, and "-ness" forms a noun indicating a state or quality.
