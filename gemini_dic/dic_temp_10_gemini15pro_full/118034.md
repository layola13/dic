menaceful:/ˈmɛnɪsfʊl/| adj.|威胁的，险恶的|Suggesting the presence of danger; threatening. |The dog growled menacingly, a menaceful glint in its eyes. 那条狗发出威胁的低吼，眼中闪烁着危险的光芒。

The dark, menaceful clouds gathered overhead. 乌云密布，险恶地聚集在头顶。|近义词：threatening, ominous, sinister, dangerous, intimidating|反义词：safe, harmless, benign, innocuous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From menace (n.) + -ful.  Menace comes from Old French manace "threat," from Late Latin minacia "a threat," from Latin minari "to threaten," related to minax "threatening."
