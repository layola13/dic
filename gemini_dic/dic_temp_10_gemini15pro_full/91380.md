hydrotellurate:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈtɛljʊˌreɪt/ | n. |氢碲酸盐；碲氢化物|A salt of hydrotelluric acid, containing the TeH− ion.  A compound of hydrogen and tellurium.|Sodium hydrotellurate is a powerful reducing agent. 氢碲酸钠是一种强还原剂。|Near synonyms: telluride |Antonyms: None (lack of common chemically relevant antonym)|noun
