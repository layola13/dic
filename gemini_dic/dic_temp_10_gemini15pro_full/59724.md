eclipses:/ɪˈklɪpsiz/
n.
日食，月食；黯失；衰退
an obscuring of the light from one celestial body by the passage of another between it and the observer or between it and its source of illumination.
The lunar eclipse will be visible across North America.  这次月食在北美各地都能看到。

The eclipse of the king's power was gradual. 国王的权力逐渐衰落。

occultation, darkening
emergence, brightening
noun
