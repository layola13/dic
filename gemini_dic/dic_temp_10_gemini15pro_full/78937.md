girasole:/ˌdʒɪrəˈsoʊleɪ/ (US), /ˌdʒɪərəˈsɒleɪ/ (UK)| n.|向日葵，葵花(籽)，|A tall plant with large yellow flowers that turn towards the sun. It is grown for its seeds and oil.|The farmer planted a field of girasoli. 农民种了一块向日葵地。|Synonyms: sunflower, helianthus|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian girasole, literally "turns sun," from girare "to turn" + sole "sun." Ultimately from Latin gyrare "to turn round" and sol "sun."
