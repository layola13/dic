mensio:/ˈmɛnʃioʊ/ /ˈmɛnsiːoʊ/
noun
测量，尺寸
the act or process of measuring something; a measurement
The mensio of the room is 10 feet by 12 feet.  房间的尺寸是10英尺乘12英尺。
Synonyms: measurement, dimension, size
Antonyms: estimation, guess
