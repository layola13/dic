dampen:/ˈdæmpən/ (US), /ˈdæmpən/ (UK)| v. |使潮湿；抑制；减弱；使沮丧|To make something slightly wet or to reduce the intensity of something, like enthusiasm or a sound.|The rain dampened the ground. 雨水使地面潮湿了。|synonyms: moisten, dampen, stifle, mute, soften|antonyms: dry, intensify, encourage, excite|verb

dampen:/ˈdæmpən/ (US), /ˈdæmpən/ (UK)| adj. (rare, archaic) |潮湿的|Slightly wet.|The dampened cloth felt cold. 潮湿的布摸起来很冷。|synonyms: damp, moist|antonyms: dry|adjective
