enclitical: /ɪnˈklɪtɪkəl/ /ɛnˈklɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 附加的，后接的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an enclitic.  An enclitic is a word that is unstressed and pronounced as if it were part of the preceding word.  It is grammatically independent but phonetically dependent. | The word "-n't" in "didn't" is an enclitic.  "Didn't" is a contraction of "did not," where "not" has become enclitic. | 近义词: unstressed, dependent | 反义词: stressed, independent | 词性: 形容词

enclitic: /ɪnˈklɪtɪk/ /ɛnˈklɪtɪk/ | n. | 附加词，后接词 | A word that is unstressed and pronounced as if it were part of the preceding word.  It is grammatically independent but phonetically dependent. |  The word "'s" in "He's" is an enclitic. "He's" is a contraction of "He is" or "He has."  | 近义词: clitic | 反义词: proclitic | 词性: 名词
