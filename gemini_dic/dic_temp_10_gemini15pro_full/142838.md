out-despatches:/ˌaʊt dɪˈspætʃɪz/ |v.| 派遣，发送；胜过，超越| To send out quickly, especially on an official mission or to surpass or outdo in dispatching or sending. | The general out-despatched a messenger with the urgent news. 将军派出了一名信使送紧急消息。| The company out-despatched its rivals in getting the product to market. 这家公司在将产品推向市场方面胜过了竞争对手。| synonyms: outstrip, surpass, exceed, better, outdo | antonyms: lag behind, fall short of | verb

out-despatches:/ˌaʊt dɪˈspætʃɪz/ |n.| (通常用复数)发送，派遣；发送的物品| The act of sending something out quickly, or the things that are sent. | The out-despatches from headquarters arrived late. 总部的发送物来得晚了。| The out-despatches contained vital information for the troops. 这些发送物包含了部队的重要信息。| synonyms: shipments, deliveries, transmissions | antonyms: receipts, arrivals | noun
