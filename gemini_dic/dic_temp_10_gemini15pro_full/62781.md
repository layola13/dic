engraves:/ɪnˈɡreɪvz/
verb
雕刻，铭刻
cuts or carves a design or words onto a hard surface
The jeweler engraves the ring with the couple's initials. 珠宝商在戒指上刻上这对夫妇的名字缩写。

近义词: carve, etch, inscribe
反义词: erase, efface
词性: verb
