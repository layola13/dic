melt:/mɛlt/ | n. | 熔化；熔化的东西 | The process of changing from a solid to a liquid state. The molten material resulting from this process. | The melt from the volcano flowed down the mountainside. 火山熔岩顺着山坡流了下来。| Synonyms: liquefaction, thawing | Antonyms: freezing, solidification | Noun
