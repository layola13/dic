ferrocyanic:/ˌfɛroʊsaɪˈænɪk/| adj.|亚铁氰的，六氰合铁(II)酸的|Containing the ferrocyanide ion, [Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻.|Potassium ferrocyanide is used in blueprint paper. 亚铁氰化钾用于蓝图纸。|近义词: hexacyanoferrate(II) (more formally)|反义词: ferricyanic|词性: adjective

ferrocyanide:/ˌfɛroʊˈsaɪənaɪd/| n.|亚铁氰化物，六氰合铁(II)酸盐|A salt containing the ferrocyanide anion, [Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻, typically pale yellow in color.|Sodium ferrocyanide is an anti-caking agent. 亚铁氰化钠是一种抗结块剂。|近义词: hexacyanoferrate(II) (more formally)|反义词: ferricyanide|词性: noun

Etymology:

From *ferro-* (iron) + *cyanic* (containing cyanide).  Ultimately from Latin *ferrum* (iron) and Greek *kyanos* (dark blue).
