diadelphous:/ˌdaɪəˈdɛlfəs/| adj.|二体雄蕊的|Having stamens united into two groups or bundles by their filaments, with one stamen often free, as in many members of the pea family.|例句：Pea plants have diadelphous stamens. 豌豆植物有二体雄蕊。

例句：The diadelphous arrangement of stamens is characteristic of the Fabaceae family. 二体雄蕊的排列是豆科植物的特征。|近义词：monadelphous (single bundle)|反义词：polyandrous (many stamens, free and distinct)|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *di-* (two) + *adelphos* (brother) -> diadelphos (having brothers) ->  Modern Latin *diadelphus* -> diadelphous
