loxodromism:/ˌlɒksəˈdrɒmɪzəm/ | n. |斜航术，恒向线航行|Navigation along a rhumb line, a course of constant bearing.  A loxodrome is a curve on the surface of a sphere that crosses all meridians of longitude at the same angle.|The ship's captain employed loxodromism to navigate across the Atlantic. 船长使用斜航术横渡大西洋。|近义词:rhumb line navigation|反义词:great circle navigation|词性:noun

loxodromic:/ˌlɒksəˈdrɒmɪk/ | adj. |斜航的，恒向线的|Relating to or involving a loxodrome; following a rhumb line.|The loxodromic curve on the map represented the ship's course. 地图上的斜航曲线代表了船的航线。|近义词:rhumb|反义词:orthodromic|词性:adjective
