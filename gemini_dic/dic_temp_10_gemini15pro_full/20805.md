blasphemer:/ˈblæsfɪmər/ | n. |亵渎神明者；渎神者|Someone who speaks or acts in a way that shows disrespect or contempt for God or sacred things.|The blasphemer was banished from the community.  亵渎神明者被逐出了社区。| The emperor was declared a blasphemer and overthrown.  皇帝被宣布为亵渎神明者并被推翻。 |Synonyms: heretic, infidel, unbeliever|Antonyms: believer, devotee, worshipper|noun
