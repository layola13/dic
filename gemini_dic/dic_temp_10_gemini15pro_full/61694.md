emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmɑːliənt/
adjective
润肤的；缓和的
Having the quality of softening or soothing the skin.
例句：This emollient cream is good for dry skin.  这款润肤霜对干性皮肤很好。

例句：The negotiator's emollient tone helped to calm the angry crowd.  谈判者缓和的语气帮助平息了愤怒的人群。

近义词：soothing, softening, moisturizing, demulcent
反义词：irritating, astringent
词性: adjective


emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ /ɪˈmɑːliənt/
noun
润肤剂；缓和剂
A substance that softens or soothes the skin.
例句：She used an emollient to protect her skin from the harsh winter weather. 她使用润肤剂来保护她的皮肤免受严酷的冬季天气影响。

例句：Aloe vera is a natural emollient.  芦荟是一种天然的润肤剂。

近义词：moisturizer, lotion, cream, balm
反义词：irritant
词性: noun
