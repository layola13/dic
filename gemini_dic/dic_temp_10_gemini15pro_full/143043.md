outbluffing: /ˌaʊtˈblʌfɪŋ/
verb
以虚张声势胜过; 比…更会虚张声势
The act of bluffing more successfully than another.
He outbluffed his opponent in the poker game. 他在扑克游戏中以虚张声势战胜了对手。
outplay, outsmart, outmaneuver
-
v.
