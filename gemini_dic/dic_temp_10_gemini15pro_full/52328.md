dicotyledon:/daɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (US) /daɪˌkɑːtɪˈliːdən/| n. |双子叶植物|A flowering plant with two embryonic seed leaves or cotyledons. Dicotyledons typically have broad leaves with netlike veins, and flowers with parts in multiples of four or five.|例句：The rose is a dicotyledon. 玫瑰是一种双子叶植物。

|例句：Dicotyledons constitute the larger of the two great divisions of flowering plants. 双子叶植物构成开花植物两大类中较大的一类。


|近义词：dicot|反义词:monocot (单子叶植物)|词性:noun

dicot:/ˈdaɪkɒt/ (US) /ˈdaɪkɑːt/| n. |双子叶植物|Short for dicotyledon.|例句：Dicots are known for their net-veined leaves. 双子叶植物以其网状叶脉而闻名。

|例句：Most trees and shrubs are dicots. 大多数树木和灌木都是双子叶植物。


|近义词：dicotyledon|反义词:monocot|词性:noun
