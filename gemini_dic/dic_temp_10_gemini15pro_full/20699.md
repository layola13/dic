ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blæd, from Proto-Germanic *bladam (source also of Old Saxon blad, Old Frisian bled, Old High German blat “leaf, blade,” German Blatt, Dutch blad), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel-  “to swell, bloom, leaf.”  The sense development is from "sprouting thing" to "leaf" to "blade of grass, sword blade."
