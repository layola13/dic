evaporative:/ɪˈvæpərətɪv/| adj.|蒸发的，挥发的|Relating to or causing evaporation.  The process of a liquid changing to a gas.|The evaporative cooler worked well in the dry heat.  蒸发式冷却器在干燥的热天中效果很好。|近义词：vaporizing, volatilizing|反义词：condensing|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin evaporativus, from evaporare "to evaporate," from ex- "out" + vapor "steam, vapor."
