lumpectomy:/ˌlʌmˈpɛktəmi/| n.|乳房肿块切除术|Surgical removal of a lump from the breast, typically for biopsy or removal of a benign tumor or a localized area of breast cancer. |She had a lumpectomy to remove the suspicious lump. 她做了乳房肿块切除术以切除可疑的肿块。|Synonyms:  breast lump removal, partial mastectomy, excisional biopsy|Antonyms: mastectomy|noun
