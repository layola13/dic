bagman:/ˈbæɡmən/| n. |提包人；（尤指）携款人，（尤指为非法目的或秘密交易）运送现金者；（非正式）销售代表|A person, especially one carrying a bag, who collects or distributes money, especially for illicit or secret purposes; (informal) a sales representative.|The bagman delivered the payoff to the corrupt official. 提包人把报酬送给了腐败的官员。|Synonyms: courier, messenger, go-between, agent, representative|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider "police," "investigator" in specific contexts)|noun
