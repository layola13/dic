gyreful:/ˈdʒaɪərfl/
adjective
旋转的; 涡旋状的
Moving in a gyre or spiral; whirling.  Characterized by gyres or spirals.
The gyreful winds whipped the leaves into a frenzy. 旋转的风把树叶吹得狂乱飞舞。
The artist captured the gyreful motion of the dancers in her painting.  这位艺术家在她的画中捕捉到了舞者旋转的动作。
近义词: swirling, whirling, spiral, rotating, vortexing
反义词: linear, straight
词性: adjective
