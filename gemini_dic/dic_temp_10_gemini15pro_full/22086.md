bollockses:/ˈbɒləksɪz/ | n. | 废话，胡说八道，睾丸（粗俗）|  A vulgar slang term primarily used in British English.  It can refer to nonsense, lies, or something of poor quality. It can also literally refer to testicles. Due to its vulgarity, it's best avoided in formal settings.  |  He was talking absolute bollockses about winning the lottery. 他胡说八道说他中了彩票。|  Synonyms: rubbish, nonsense, crap, testicles (literal) |  Antonyms: truth, sense  | noun
