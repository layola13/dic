mock-at:/mɑːkˈæt/ | phrasal verb | 嘲笑，讥讽 | To tease or make fun of someone, often by mimicking them in a disrespectful way. |  He mocked at her attempts to speak French. 他嘲笑她说法语的尝试。

|  The children mocked at the old man's limp. 孩子们嘲笑老人的跛脚。

| 近义词: ridicule, deride, scoff at, sneer at | 反义词: praise, admire, respect | 词性: 动词短语
