over-flowable:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊəbəl/
adjective
可溢出的，可满溢的
able to overflow or be overflowed; excessively full.
The cup was over-flowable with water.  杯子里的水溢出来了。
The river was over-flowable after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，河水泛滥。
近义词: overflowing, brimming, inundating
反义词: contained, limited, empty
词性: adjective
