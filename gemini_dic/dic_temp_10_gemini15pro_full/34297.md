clarifying:/ˈklærɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 阐明的，解释的 | Making something clearer or easier to understand. | The teacher provided a clarifying example to help the students understand the concept. 老师提供了一个阐明的例子来帮助学生理解这个概念。|  synonyms: explanatory, elucidating, illuminating | antonyms: confusing, obscuring, mystifying | adjective

clarify:/ˈklærɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 阐明，澄清 | To make something clearer or easier to understand. | Could you please clarify your earlier statement? 你能澄清一下你之前的陈述吗？ | synonyms: explain, elucidate, explicate | antonyms: confuse, obscure, complicate | verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *clarificare, from Latin clarus (“clear”) + -ficare, a variant of facere (“to make”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“bright”).
