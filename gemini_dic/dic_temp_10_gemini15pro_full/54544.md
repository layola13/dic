dislikes:/dɪsˈlaɪks/| v. |不喜欢，厌恶|To regard with aversion or disapproval.|He dislikes being told what to do. 他不喜欢别人告诉他该怎么做。|近义词：hates, detests, loathes|反义词:likes, enjoys|verb
