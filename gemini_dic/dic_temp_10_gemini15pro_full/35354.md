co-guardian:/kəʊˈɡɑːdiən/ | n. | 共同监护人 | A person who shares the legal responsibility for the care and upbringing of a child with another person. |  The child's parents appointed co-guardians in their will.  孩子的父母在遗嘱中指定了共同监护人。


The divorced parents acted as co-guardians for their children.  离婚的父母共同担任他们孩子的监护人。


| 近义词：joint guardian, shared guardian | 反义词： N/A | 词性：名词
