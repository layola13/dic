abhorrible: /əˈbɔːrəbl/
adjective
令人憎恶的；可恶的
Inspiring disgust and loathing; repugnant.
He committed an abhorrible crime. 他犯下了令人发指的罪行。

I find the idea of eating insects abhorrible. 我觉得吃昆虫的想法很恶心。

近义词: detestable, loathsome, hateful, repugnant, repulsive, disgusting, offensive, obnoxious, abominable, execrable
反义词: admirable, delightful, pleasant, lovely, appealing
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abhorribilis*, from abhorrere ‘shrink from, shudder at’ (see abhor).

USAGE->
She described the treatment of the prisoners as abhorrible. 她将囚犯的待遇描述为令人憎恶的。

The smell coming from the dumpster was absolutely abhorrible. 垃圾箱里散发出的气味绝对令人作呕。

The living conditions in the refugee camp were abhorrible.  难民营的生活条件非常恶劣。

The food in the cafeteria was abhorrible; I couldn't eat it.  自助餐厅的食物糟透了，我吃不下。

He told an abhorrible joke that offended everyone in the room. 他讲了一个令人厌恶的笑话，冒犯了房间里的每个人。

They witnessed the abhorrible aftermath of the hurricane. 他们目睹了飓风过后可怕的后果。

The movie depicted the abhorrible acts of violence committed during the war. 这部电影描绘了战争期间令人发指的暴力行为。

The dictator's abhorrible regime was finally overthrown. 独裁者的可恶政权终于被推翻了。
