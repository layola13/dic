eruptions: /ɪˈrʌpʃənz/
noun
喷发；爆发
a sudden outbreak of something, typically something violent or unpleasant.
The volcano's eruptions caused widespread devastation. 火山的喷发造成了大范围的破坏。

Volcanic eruptions can be incredibly destructive. 火山爆发具有极强的破坏力。


synonym: outbursts, explosions, emissions
antonym: suppressions, containments
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:
From Latin *ēruptiō* (“a breaking out”), from the verb *ērumpere*, from *ē-* (“out”) + *rumpere* (“to break”).
