eont: /ˈiːɒnt/ | n. | 亿万年; 极长时期;  | An immeasurably or indefinitely long period of time; an age.  It's often used hyperbolically or poetically. | It felt like eons before he finally replied to my message.  感觉过了亿万年他才回复我的信息。| Example:  The dinosaurs roamed the earth eons ago. 恐龙在亿万年前漫游地球。 | Synonyms: age, era, epoch, eternity | Antonyms: instant, moment, second | noun
