allusion:/əˈluːʒən/| n.|暗示；典故；提及|an indirect or passing reference to something or someone, typically one that is well-known; the act of alluding to something or someone|The poem is full of allusions to classical mythology. 这首诗充满了对古典神话的典故。|synonyms: reference, mention, implication, suggestion, hint, intimation|antonyms: explicit statement, direct reference|noun
