encyclic:/ɪnˈsaɪklɪk/ /ɛnˈsaɪklɪk/ | n. adj.|通谕；通函；百科的|A papal letter sent to all bishops of the Roman Catholic Church.|The Pope issued an encyclical on the environment. 教宗发布了一封关于环境的通谕。|近义词：circular, letter, bull, decree|反义词: None|词性: noun, adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *encyclicus*, from Greek *enkyklios* "circular," from *en* "in" + *kyklos* "circle."
