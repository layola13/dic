nightertale: /ˈnaɪtərˌteɪl/
n.
夜间故事; 睡前故事; 夜间奇遇
A story told or read at night, especially to children before they go to sleep, or an adventure or experience that happens during the night.
例句：My grandma used to tell us nightertales about magical creatures. 我奶奶过去常常给我们讲关于神奇生物的睡前故事。

例句：Last night's nightertale involved a flat tire and a long walk home. 昨晚的夜间奇遇包括一个爆胎和一段长途步行回家。
近义词：bedtime story, night story, night adventure
反义词: daytime activity
