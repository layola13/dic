degreases:/diːˈɡriːsɪz/
verb
减少油脂; 去油; 脱脂
reduces the amount of grease on something
The detergent degreases dirty dishes effectively.  这种洗涤剂能有效去除脏盘子上的油脂。

degreases:/diːˈɡriːsɪz/
verb
减少，降低（数量、数量等）
reduces or lessens something, such as a quantity or amount
The company has degreased its workforce by 10%.  该公司已将其员工人数减少了 10%。

近义词:clean, scour, remove grease, lessen, reduce, decrease
反义词:grease, increase

词性:v.
