heptachord: /ˈhɛptəkɔːrd/ | n. |七声音阶; 七弦琴 |A diatonic series of seven notes; a scale of seven notes.  A seven-stringed musical instrument. |The ancient Greek lyre was a heptachord. 古希腊的里拉琴是一种七弦琴。| The composer experimented with different heptachords.  这位作曲家尝试了不同的七声音阶。 |Synonym: scale, gamut | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider scales with different numbers of notes like pentachord or hexachord) | noun
