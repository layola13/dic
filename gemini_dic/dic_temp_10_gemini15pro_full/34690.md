clerkly:/ˈklɜːrkli/| adj.|  像职员的；文书的；书法工整的|  Resembling or characteristic of a clerk; relating to a clerk's duties; neatly and legibly written, as by a clerk.|  He had a clerkly manner, precise and a little fussy.  他有一种职员式的举止，一丝不苟，有点小题大做。|Synonyms:  scribal, clerical, neat, methodical|Antonyms:  sloppy, disorganized, untidy|词性: 形容词

clerkly:/ˈklɜːrkli/| adv.|  像职员一样地；文书地；书法工整地|  In a manner resembling or characteristic of a clerk; in a neat and legible manner, as befitting a clerk.|  The minutes were clerkly recorded in a bound volume. 会议记录工整地记录在一个装订好的册子里。|Synonyms: neatly, methodically, precisely|Antonyms: sloppily, carelessly, haphazardly|词性: 副词


词源:  From Middle English *clerkly, equivalent to clerk +‎ -ly.
