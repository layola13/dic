drum:/drʌm/| n. v. |鼓; 鼓声; 鼓状物; (鼓状的)圆桶; 击鼓; 不断重复;  |A musical instrument, typically cylindrical, with a taut membrane stretched over one or both ends, which is beaten with sticks or the hands to produce a sound. Also, the sound produced by beating a drum.  |He plays the drums in a band. 他在一个乐队里打鼓。|近义词：beat, percussion, barrel, cylinder |反义词: silence, quiet |词性:名词, 动词
