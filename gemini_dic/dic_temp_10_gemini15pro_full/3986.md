aguish:/ˈeɪɡwɪʃ/| adj.|忽冷忽热的，患疟疾的，颤抖的|Feeling or relating to the alternating chills and fever characteristic of malaria.|He felt aguish and weak after returning from the tropics. 他从热带回来后感到忽冷忽热，身体虚弱。|近义词：feverish, shivering, chilly, cold|反义词：healthy, well, warm|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *aguese, *aguesh, from ague (“fever”) + -ish.  Ague itself comes from Old French ague, from feminine of agu, acute, from Latin acūtus, acute.
