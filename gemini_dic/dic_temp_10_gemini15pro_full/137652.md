nonunderstandingly:/ˌnɒnʌndəˈstændɪŋli/
adverb
不理解地，缺乏理解地
without understanding; in a way that shows a lack of understanding.
She stared at him nonunderstandingly. 她茫然地盯着他。
Synonyms: blankly, vacantly, uncomprehendingly, obtusely
Antonyms: knowingly, intelligently, perceptively, comprehensively
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + understanding (noun) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "understand" comes from Old English "understandan," meaning "to comprehend."
