albication:/ˌælbɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
变白，漂白；白化病
The process of making something white or the state of being white.  Specifically refers to the whitening of plant tissues.
The albication of the coral reefs is a serious environmental problem. 珊瑚礁的白化是一个严重的环境问题。

近义词:whitening, bleaching, blanching
反义词:darkening, blackening
词性:noun

词源: From Latin *albicatio*, from albicare "to whiten," from albus "white."
