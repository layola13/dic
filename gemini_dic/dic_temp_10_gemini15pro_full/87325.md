hero: /ˈhɪəroʊ/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊ/ (UK)| n.|英雄; 男主角; 男主人公|A person who is admired or idealized for courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities.  A chief male character in a book, play, or movie, who is typically identified with good qualities, and with whom the reader is expected to sympathize.|例句：He was a hero in the war. 他是战争中的英雄。

例句：The main hero of the story is a young boy named Leo. 故事的男主人公是一个名叫利奥的小男孩。

|近义词：champion, idol, legend, protagonist, star|反义词：villain, coward|词性: 名词
