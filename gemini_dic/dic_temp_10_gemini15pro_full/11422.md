argillite:/ˈɑːrdʒɪlaɪt/ | n. |泥质板岩，硬质泥岩 |A compact, fine-grained sedimentary rock consisting of consolidated clay or mud. It is harder and more brittle than shale and shows no fissility. |The arrowheads were crafted from argillite. 箭头是用泥质板岩做的。|Synonyms: claystone, mudstone, slate |Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but contrasting rocks include sandstone, limestone) | noun
