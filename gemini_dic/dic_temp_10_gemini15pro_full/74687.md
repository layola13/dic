free-enterprising: /ˌfriːˈɛntərˌpraɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 自由创业的；富于创业精神的；有进取心的 | Characterized by or engaging in free enterprise; having or showing initiative and willingness to undertake new projects. |  She's a free-enterprising woman who started her own business. 她是一位富有创业精神的女性，自己创办了企业。| He has a free-enterprising spirit and is always looking for new opportunities. 他有进取心，总是寻找新的机会。| 近义词: entrepreneurial, enterprising, ambitious, go-getting | 反义词: unambitious, lazy, passive | 词性: adjective

词源:  "free" + "enterprising". "Free" meaning unrestricted and "enterprising" meaning marked by initiative and willingness to undertake new projects.
