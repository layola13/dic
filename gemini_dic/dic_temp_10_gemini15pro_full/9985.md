aphoristical:/ˌæfəˈrɪstɪkəl/
adj.
格言式的，警句式的
terse and concise, like an aphorism; expressing a general truth or astute observation in a concise and pithy manner.
His writing style is aphoristical and memorable. 他的写作风格简洁精辟，令人难忘。
His aphoristical style made his speeches both memorable and impactful. 他格言式的风格使他的演讲既令人难忘又具有影响力。
近义词: pithy, concise, succinct, epigrammatic, terse
反义词: verbose, prolix, rambling, diffuse

词性: adjective
