looted:/ˈluːtɪd/| v. adj.|掠夺，被掠夺的|past participle of loot; stolen goods, especially after a war or riot.  Having been robbed and ransacked.|例句：The city was looted after the earthquake.  地震过后，这座城市被洗劫一空。|近义词：ransacked, pillaged, plundered|反义词:protected, preserved|verb, adjective
