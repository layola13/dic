inveracity: /ˌɪnvəˈræsɪti/
n.
不真实，虚假
lack of truth or accuracy
His inveracity makes him an unreliable witness. 他的不真实使他成为一个不可靠的证人。
dishonesty, falsehood, mendacity
truthfulness, veracity, honesty
