outmanned: /ˌaʊtˈmænd/
Verb| 以人数/人力胜过；在人数/人力上超过| to have more people than someone else, so that you have an advantage|  The enemy army outmanned ours by two to one. 敌军在人数上以二比一超过我军。| outnumbered, surpassed in numbers | outnumbered, surpassed in numbers |v.

outmanned: /ˌaʊtˈmænd/
Adjective | 人数/人力不足的；寡不敌众的 | having fewer people than someone else, so that you are at a disadvantage | They were outmanned and outgunned. 他们在人数和武器装备上都处于劣势。 | outnumbered, understaffed | superior in numbers, stronger |adj.
