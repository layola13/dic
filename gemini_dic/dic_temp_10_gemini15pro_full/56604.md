donatory:/ˈdoʊnətɔːri/ /ˈdɒnətəri/| n. |捐赠者；赠与者|A person who donates something, especially money, to a charity or other cause.|例句：The donatory wished to remain anonymous.  这位捐赠者希望保持匿名。

例句：The hospital received a large donation from a generous donatory.  这家医院收到了一位慷慨捐赠者的大笔捐款。|近义词：donor, contributor, benefactor|反义词:recipient, beneficiary|词性:noun

donatory:/ˈdoʊnətɔːri/ /ˈdɒnətəri/| adj. |捐赠的；赠与的|Relating to or characteristic of a donation or donator.|例句：The donatory funds were used to build a new library.  捐赠资金用于建造一个新的图书馆。

例句：The plaque commemorated the donatory act of the family. 这块牌匾是为了纪念这个家庭的捐赠行为。|近义词：donated, gifted, contributed|反义词:received|词性:adjective
