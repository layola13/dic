observantine:/əbˈzɜːrvənt/ /əbˈzɝːvənt/| adj.|善于观察的；机警的；敏锐的|Quick to notice things; perceptive.|例句：She is an observant child and notices everything around her. 她是一个善于观察的孩子，注意到周围的一切。

例句：He was observant enough to notice the subtle changes in her mood. 他很细心，注意到了她情绪的微妙变化。

近义词：perceptive, astute, sharp, vigilant, attentive, aware, watchful|反义词：inattentive, unobservant, oblivious, unaware|词性:形容词
