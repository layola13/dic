futurology:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɒlədʒi/ /ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɑːlədʒi/
n.
未来学
The study of future possibilities or of developing future scenarios.
例句：Futurology is a fascinating field, but predictions are often inaccurate. 未来学是一个引人入胜的领域，但预测往往不准确。

例句：He's a professor of futurology at the University of Hawaii. 他是夏威夷大学的未来学教授。
近义词：future studies, forecasting, prediction
反义词:  past, history
词性: noun
