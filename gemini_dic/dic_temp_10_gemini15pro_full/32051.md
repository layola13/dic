cherry:/ˈtʃeri/ (US), /ˈtʃɛri/ (UK)| n.|樱桃；樱桃树；樱桃色；处女膜|A small, round, soft red or black fruit with a single hard stone in the middle.  A tree that bears cherries. A bright red colour.|例句：The cherry pie was delicious. 樱桃派很美味。

例句：She wore a cherry-red dress. 她穿着一件樱桃红色的连衣裙。


近义词：fruit (水果)


反义词: N/A (没有确切的反义词)


词性: noun (名词)


词源: From Old North French *cherise, from Vulgar Latin *ceresia, from Latin cerasus “cherry tree,” from Ancient Greek kerasos “cherry, cherry tree”.
