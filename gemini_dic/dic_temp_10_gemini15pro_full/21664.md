blustered:/ˈblʌstərd/| v.|咆哮，怒吼；狂吹，刮大风|talked loudly and aggressively, often with little effect; (of wind) blew strongly and noisily|The wind blustered through the trees. 风咆哮着穿过树林。|Synonyms: roared, raged, stormed, thundered|Antonyms: whispered, murmured, calmed, subsided|verb

bluster:/ˈblʌstər/| n. v.|咆哮，怒吼；狂风，大风|loud, aggressive talk with little effect; strong, noisy wind|He blustered about how he would fix everything, but I didn't believe him. 他咆哮着说他会把一切都修好，但我不相信他。|Synonyms: ranting, raving, storm, gale|Antonyms: whisper, murmur, calm, stillness|noun, verb
