cacology:/kæˈkɒlədʒi/
n.
恶言恶语，粗俗的语言
bad choice of words, poor style or expression, or bad pronunciation
例句：His cacology offended many at the formal dinner. 他的粗俗语言在正式晚宴上冒犯了许多人。

例句：The teacher was appalled by the student's cacology. 老师对学生的恶言恶语感到震惊。

近义词：malapropism, solecism, dysphemism
反义词：eloquence, euphony
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Greek "kakos" (bad) + "-logia" (speaking).
