continuing:/kənˈtɪnjuɪŋ/ | adj. | 继续的，持续的 | Ongoing, still happening, not finished | The continuing saga of the royal family captivated the nation.  皇室家族的持续传奇故事吸引了全国人民。|  The continuing rain ruined our picnic plans.  持续的降雨毁了我们的野餐计划。 | synonyms: ongoing, persistent, enduring, ceaseless, incessant | antonyms: ceasing, ending, stopping, halting, intermittent | adjective


Etymology: From Middle English *continuinge*, present participle of *continuen*, from Old French *continuer*, from Latin *continuare* "to make all one, join together, unite; to make continuous," from *continuus* "continuous," from *con-* "together" (see con-) + *tenere* "to hold" (see tenet).
