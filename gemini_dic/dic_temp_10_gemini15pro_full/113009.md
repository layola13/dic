lumberjack:/ˈlʌmbərˌdʒæk/| n.|伐木工人|A person who fells trees, especially as an occupation.|例句：The lumberjack swung his axe and felled the giant redwood. 伐木工人挥动斧头，砍倒了巨大的红杉树。

例句：Lumberjacks work long hours in challenging conditions. 伐木工人在艰苦的条件下长时间工作。

近义词：logger, woodsman|反义词:|词性:noun
