avowednesses:/əˈvaʊɪdnəsɪz/
noun
公开承认；公开声明（的状态）
The state of being avowed; open acknowledgment or declaration.
His avowednesses of loyalty were met with skepticism. 他公开声明的忠诚受到了怀疑。
Near Synonyms: declarations, pronouncements, affirmations, assertions
Antonyms: denials, disavowals, concealments, reticence
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
