pacer:/ˈpeɪsər/| n.| 步速设定者，领跑者，起搏器 |A person or device that sets the pace, especially in a race or other activity; a device that regulates the heartbeat.|The marathon runner used a pacer to help him maintain a steady speed.  马拉松选手用领跑员来帮助他保持稳定的速度。|Synonyms: pacesetter, leader, regulator|Antonyms: follower, straggler|noun


pacer:/ˈpeɪsər/| n.| 定速装置 |A device that helps maintain a steady speed.|The car's pacer helped it achieve optimal fuel efficiency. 这辆车的定速装置帮助它实现了最佳的燃油效率。|Synonyms: cruise control, speed regulator|Antonyms: accelerator, brake|noun
