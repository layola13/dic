alphabetise:/ˈælfəbətaɪz/ | vt. | 按字母顺序排列 | To arrange in alphabetical order. |  Example 1:  Please alphabetise these files by the clients' last names.  请按客户的姓氏按字母顺序排列这些文件。

Example 2:  The librarian alphabetised the books on the shelf. 图书管理员把书架上的书按字母顺序排列好。 | Synonyms: alphabetize, order, arrange, sort | Antonyms: disorder, disarrange, jumble | verb
