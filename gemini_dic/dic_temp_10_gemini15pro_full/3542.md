against the grain: /əˈɡɛnst ðə ɡreɪn/ | idiom | 违背本性；格格不入 | going against one's natural inclination or feeling; contrary to one's principles or preferences |  Asking for help goes against the grain for him.  寻求帮助违背了他的本性。 | unnaturally, awkwardly, reluctantly | naturally, willingly, smoothly | idiom

against the grain: /əˈɡɛnst ðə ɡreɪn/ | idiom |  逆着纹理；与…格格不入 | literally, against the direction of the fibers in wood or other materials; figuratively, against the prevailing tendency or opinion | Sanding against the grain can damage the wood. 逆着纹理打磨会损坏木材。 | counter to, opposing | with, along | idiom
