emblazoning:/ɪmˈbleɪzənɪŋ/
|v.|（纹章）用纹章装饰；修饰；盛赞；宣扬|The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金色的狮子。|
|v.|The story of their heroism was emblazoned across the newspapers.  他们的英雄事迹被各大报纸宣扬。|
|近义词：decorating, adorning, celebrating, proclaiming|
|反义词：obscuring, concealing, downplaying|
|词性: 动词|


emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/
|v.|（纹章）用纹章装饰；修饰；盛赞；宣扬|He had the family crest emblazoned on his stationery. 他在文具上印有家族的盾形纹章。|
|v.|Her name was emblazoned across the headlines. 她的名字赫然印在报纸头条上。|
|近义词：decorate, adorn, celebrate, proclaim|
|反义词：obscure, conceal, downplay|
|词性: 动词|


词源:
From Old French *emblazoner, from emblaser "to set on fire, blaze," from en- "in, on" + blason "coat of arms."  源自古法语 *emblazoner，来自 emblaser “点燃，燃烧”，来自 en- “在...里面，在...上面” + blason “盾形纹章”。
