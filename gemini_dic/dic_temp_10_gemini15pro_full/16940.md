basseted:/ˈbæsɪtɪd/| adj.|垂耳的；矮腿的；悲伤的|Having long, drooping ears; short-legged; exhibiting sadness or dejection.|The basseted hound looked mournful with its drooping ears.  垂耳猎犬耳朵低垂，看起来很悲伤。|近义词：drooping, sad, dejected, low-slung|反义词:upright, cheerful, happy|词性:形容词
词源:From "basset," referring to a type of short-legged dog, from Old French "basset" (low), ultimately from a Vulgar Latin diminutive of "bassus" (low).
