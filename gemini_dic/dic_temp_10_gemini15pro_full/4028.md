ahungered:/əˈhʌŋɡərd/
adjective
饥饿的
Extremely hungry; ravenous.  Feeling a powerful desire or need for something.
The ahungered children looked longingly at the bakery window.  那些饥肠辘辘的孩子们渴望地看着面包店的橱窗。
近义词: starving, famished, ravenous, eager, yearning
反义词: satiated, full, satisfied, apathetic
词性: adjective

词源: From a- + hungered, past participle of hunger.  The prefix "a-" is an archaic intensifier, meaning "very" or "thoroughly."
