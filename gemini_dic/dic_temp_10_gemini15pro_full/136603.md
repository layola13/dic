nonsanguinely:/ˌnɒnˈsæŋɡwɪnli/
| adv. |悲观地，不乐观地|Without optimism or cheerfulness; pessimistically. |He nonsanguinely predicted the company's failure. 他悲观地预测了公司的失败。|例句：She nonsanguinely assessed her chances of winning the lottery. 她悲观地评估了自己中彩票的机会。|近义词：pessimistically, gloomily, despondently|反义词：optimistically, hopefully, cheerfully|词性:副词
Etymology: From non- + sanguine + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Sanguine" comes from Latin "sanguineus" meaning "of blood," later associated with a cheerful temperament believed to be caused by an abundance of blood. "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
