limpitude:/lɪmˈpɪtjuːd/
n.
lack of firmness, strength, or resolve; weakness of character or will.
lack of firmness or strength; feebleness.
She displayed a surprising limpitude in the face of adversity. 她面对逆境时表现出惊人的意志薄弱。
limpitude of character  性格懦弱
The limpitude of the government's response to the crisis was shocking. 政府对危机的反应软弱无力，令人震惊。
Near Synonyms: weakness, feebleness, flaccidity, lassitude, lethargy
Antonyms: strength, firmness, resolve, determination, vigor
noun
