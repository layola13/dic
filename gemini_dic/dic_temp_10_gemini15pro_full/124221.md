mosque:/mɒsk/ (UK), /mɑːsk/ (US)| n. |清真寺|A building used for Muslim prayer.|We visited the grand mosque in Istanbul. 我们参观了伊斯坦布尔的大清真寺。|The mosque is a place for worship and community gatherings. 清真寺是做礼拜和社区聚会的场所。|Synonyms: Islamic temple, masjid|Antonyms: church, synagogue, temple|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mosquée*, from Italian *moschea*, from Spanish *mezquita*, from Arabic *masjid* (مسجد), meaning "place of prostration (in prayer)".
