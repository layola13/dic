mauling:/ˈmɔːlɪŋ/| n.| 伤害；袭击；严厉批评 |A violent attack, especially by a wild animal; a harsh criticism or defeat.|The hiker suffered a mauling from a bear.  这名徒步旅行者被熊袭击了。|Synonyms: attack, assault, savaging, beating, thrashing|Antonyms: protecting, defending|noun

maul:/mɔːl/| v.| 伤害；袭击；严厉批评|To attack someone or something violently, especially with claws or teeth; to criticize someone or something harshly.|The lion mauled the zebra. 狮子袭击了斑马。|Synonyms: attack, assault, savage, beat, thrash|Antonyms: protect, defend|verb
