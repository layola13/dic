embroil:/ɪmˈbrɔɪl/| vt.|使卷入；使纠缠|To involve someone deeply in an argument, conflict, or difficult situation.|The two countries were embroiled in a bitter trade dispute. 这两个国家陷入了激烈的贸易争端。|近义词：entangle, involve, implicate|反义词:extricate, disentangle|verb
词源: From Old French *embroillier, from en- 'in' + broillier 'to mix,' from a Germanic base meaning 'to brew.'
