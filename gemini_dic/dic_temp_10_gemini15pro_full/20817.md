blast-it:/ˈblæst ɪt/ | int. | 该死！；见鬼！；讨厌！| An exclamation of annoyance or frustration.  Expresses mild anger or impatience.  It's a milder form of swearing. | Blast it! I've lost my keys again!  该死！我又把钥匙弄丢了！| Blast it! The train is late.  见鬼！火车晚点了。|  Synonyms: darn it, shoot, dang it | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for exclamations of frustration) | interjection
