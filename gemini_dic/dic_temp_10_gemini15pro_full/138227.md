nosography: /nɒˈsɒɡrəfi/ /noʊˈsɑːɡrəfi/
n.
疾病分类学
The branch of medical science that deals with the classification and description of diseases.
Nosography provides a framework for understanding the relationships between different illnesses. 疾病分类学提供了一个理解不同疾病之间关系的框架。
The nosography of mental disorders is constantly evolving. 精神疾病的分类学在不断发展。
近义词: taxonomy, classification
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "disorder," "chaos" in a loose sense)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *nosographie*, from Ancient Greek *νόσος* (*nosos*, “disease”) + *-γραφία* (*-graphía*, “description, writing”).
