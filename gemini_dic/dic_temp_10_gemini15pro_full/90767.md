humorist:/ˈhjuːmərɪst/ (US), /ˈhjuːmərɪst/ (UK)| n.|幽默作家；滑稽演员|A humorist is someone who writes or performs humorous material, often with the intent to entertain or provoke thought.|Mark Twain was a famous American humorist. 马克·吐温是一位著名的美国幽默作家。|He's a stand-up comedian and humorist. 他是一位单口相声演员和幽默作家。|Synonyms: comedian, wit, satirist, wag|Antonyms: bore, dullard|noun
