maxis:/ˈmæksiːz/
noun
轴线;  最大值;  极点
A long, central line around which something turns, develops, or is organized.  The greatest possible amount, degree, or intensity. The furthest or highest point of something; a culminating point.
The Earth rotates on its maxis. 地球绕其轴线自转。
The temperature reached its maxis at noon. 温度在中午达到了最高点。
The maxis of his career was when he won the Nobel Prize. 他事业的巅峰是获得诺贝尔奖的时候。
近义词: axis, peak, apex, culmination, zenith
反义词: minimum, nadir
词性:n.
