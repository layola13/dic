amoebian:/əˈmiːbiən/ | adj. n. | 变形虫的；像变形虫的；变形虫 | relating to or resembling an amoeba; an amoeboid animal, especially a protozoan of the class Rhizopoda |  The amoebian's shape constantly shifted. 变形虫的形状不断变化。|  amoeboid, protozoan |  static, fixed | adjective, noun

amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/ | n. | 变形虫 | a single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm. Amoebas are either free-living in damp environments or parasitic. | The pond water teemed with amoebas.  池塘水里充满了变形虫。| protozoan, rhizopod |  | noun
