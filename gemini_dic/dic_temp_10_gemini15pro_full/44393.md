cross-fertilizes:/ˌkrɔːsˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪz/
|v.|互相施肥，交叉受精，（思想、方法等）相互影响，相互促进|
|The company cross-fertilizes ideas between its different departments.|该公司在其不同部门之间相互交流想法。|
|This approach cross-fertilizes the strengths of both traditional and modern methods.|这种方法结合了传统和现代方法的优势。|
|fertilize, pollinate, impregnate, interbreed|
||
|verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross- + fertilize.  Fertilize comes from Late Middle English: from Old French fertiliser, based on Latin fertilis ‘fertile’.
