nontelescopic:/ˌnɒnˌtɛlɪˈskɒpɪk/
adjective
非伸缩式的
Not capable of being extended or collapsed like a telescope; fixed in length.
The ladder was nontelescopic, making it difficult to store.  这个梯子是非伸缩式的，难以存放。
The scaffolding utilized nontelescopic poles for added stability.  脚手架使用了非伸缩杆来增加稳定性。
近义词:fixed, rigid, non-extendable
反义词:telescopic, extendable, retractable
词性:adjective
