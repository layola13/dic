carburizes:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
渗碳（使硬化）
To introduce carbon into (iron or steel) in order to harden it.
The blacksmith carburizes the steel to make it stronger.  铁匠将钢渗碳以使其更坚固。
The process carburizes the metal surface. 这个过程使金属表面渗碳。
Synonyms: harden, strengthen, temper
Antonyms: soften, weaken
词性: 动词
