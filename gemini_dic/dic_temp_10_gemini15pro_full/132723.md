nondiversified:/ˌnɒndaɪˈvɜːsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 非多元化的，未分散的 | Not diversified; lacking variety or having all resources concentrated in one area or type of investment. |  Many small businesses are nondiversified and thus vulnerable to market fluctuations. 许多小企业业务单一，因此容易受到市场波动的影响。|  The farmer had a nondiversified portfolio, relying solely on his apple orchard. 这位农民的投资组合单一，完全依赖于他的苹果园。 | Synonyms: specialized, concentrated, undiversified | Antonyms: diversified, varied, multifarious | adjective
