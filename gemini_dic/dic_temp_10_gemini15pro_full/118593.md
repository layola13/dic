mesozoic:/ˌmɛzəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n. | 中生代的；中生代 |  Relating to or denoting the era between the Paleozoic and Cenozoic eras, comprising the Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous periods. | The Mesozoic Era is best known for the dinosaurs that roamed the earth. 中生代最著名的就是漫游在地球上的恐龙。| The museum has a large collection of Mesozoic fossils.  博物馆收藏了大量的来自中生代的化石。 | synonyms: secondary | antonyms:  Paleozoic, Cenozoic | adjective, noun
