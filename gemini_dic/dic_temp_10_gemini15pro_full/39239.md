congratulation: /kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃn/ (US) /kənˌɡrætʃʊˈleɪʃn/ (UK) | n. | 祝贺，恭喜 | An expression of pleasure and best wishes, especially on a special occasion. | Example:  Congratulations on your graduation! 恭喜你毕业！

Example: Please accept my warmest congratulations. 请接受我最热烈的祝贺。

近义词:  felicitation, commendation, praise

反义词:  commiseration, condolence


词性: noun


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin congratulatio(n-), from the verb congratulari ‘wish joy to’, from con- ‘together with’ + gratulari ‘give thanks, congratulate’.
