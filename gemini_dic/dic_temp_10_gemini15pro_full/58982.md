dutiful:/ˈdjuːtɪfəl/ | adj. | 尽职的；顺从的 | Performing the duties expected or required of one; obedient and respectful. | She is a dutiful daughter who always takes care of her parents. 她是一位孝顺的女儿，总是照顾她的父母。|  He is a dutiful employee who always completes his tasks on time.  他是一位尽职的员工，总是按时完成任务。 | 近义词: obedient, respectful, responsible, conscientious | 反义词: disobedient, disrespectful, irresponsible, negligent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From duty + -ful.  "Duty" comes from Old French "deu" meaning "due, owed," ultimately from Latin "debitum" meaning "something owed."  "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
