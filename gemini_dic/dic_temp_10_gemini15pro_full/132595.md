nondilapidated:/ˌnɒndɪˈlæpɪdeɪtɪd/
adjective
未破旧的；未荒废的
not dilapidated; in good repair.
The house, though old, was nondilapidated and well-maintained. 这座房子虽然老，但没有破损，维护得很好。
近义词:well-maintained, sound, intact, in good repair, undamaged
反义词:dilapidated, run-down, ramshackle, derelict
