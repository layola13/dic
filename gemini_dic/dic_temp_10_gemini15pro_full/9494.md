antiroyalist:/ˌæntiˈrɔɪəlɪst/ | n. adj. | 反君主主义者；反君主主义的 |  A person who opposes monarchy or royal rule. Someone who believes that a monarchy is not the right form of government.  |  Example: The antiroyalist sentiment was strong during the French Revolution.  法国大革命时期反君主主义情绪高涨。

Example:  He was a known antiroyalist and advocated for a republic. 他是一位著名的反君主主义者，倡导共和制。 | 近义词: republican, democrat, revolutionary | 反义词: royalist, monarchist | 词性: 名词, 形容词
