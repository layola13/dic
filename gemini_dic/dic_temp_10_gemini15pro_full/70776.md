finfoot:/ˈfɪnfʊt/| n.|非洲水雉|Any of several tropical African birds of the family Heliornithidae, resembling rails or coots and having lobed toes.|例句：The finfoot is a shy bird that is difficult to spot. 非洲水雉是一种害羞的鸟，很难被发现。

例句：The finfoot dives underwater to catch fish and other aquatic creatures. 非洲水雉潜入水中捕捉鱼类和其他水生生物。|近义词：sungrebe, African jacana (similar appearance/habitat but different family)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *fin* (referring to the lobed toes) + *foot*
