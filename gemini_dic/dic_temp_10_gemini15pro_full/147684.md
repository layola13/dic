overpoweringness:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋnɪs/
n.
势不可挡；压倒性；无比强大
The quality of being overpowering; overwhelming force or intensity.
The overpoweringness of her grief left her unable to speak. 她无比巨大的悲痛让她无法言语。
Synonyms: intensity, strength, force, dominance, potency
Antonyms: weakness, feebleness, powerlessness
