anytime:/ˈɛniˌtaɪm/| adv.|随时，任何时候|At any time; whenever.  It can be used as an informal way to say "you're welcome" after someone thanks you.|例句：Call me anytime. 随时给我打电话。|近义词：whenever, at any time|反义词:never|词性:副词
Etymology: From *any* + *time*.
