estimator:/ˈestɪmeɪtər/ (US), /ˈestɪˌmeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 估计者，估算者；评估员|A person who estimates, especially the cost of work to be done.|The builder's estimator calculated the cost of repairing the roof. 建筑商的估价员计算了修理屋顶的费用。|近义词：appraiser, assessor, valuer|反义词: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "estimate," which comes from Late Latin "aestimatus," past participle of "aestimare" (to value, appraise), from "aes" (copper, bronze, money) + "-timare" (frequentative suffix of "timere" - to fear, respect).  The "-or" suffix denotes an agent or person who performs the action.
