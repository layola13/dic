conversational:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənl/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənl/ (US)| adj.|对话的，健谈的|Relating to or characteristic of conversation; informal.|例句：She has a very conversational style of writing. 她的写作风格非常口语化。|近义词：informal, chatty, communicative|反义词：formal, reserved, taciturn|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conversationalis, from Latin conversatio (“conversation”), from conversari (“to associate with, keep company with”).
