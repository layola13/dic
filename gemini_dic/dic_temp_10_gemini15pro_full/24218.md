bridgeheads:/ˈbrɪdʒˌhɛdz/
n.
桥头堡
A fortified position established on the enemy's side of a river or other obstacle.
The troops established a bridgehead on the opposite bank. 部队在对岸建立了桥头堡。

bridgeheads:/ˈbrɪdʒˌhɛdz/
n.
据点；立足点(比喻义)
A position or foothold giving access to further progress or development.
The company sees this new product as a bridgehead into the European market. 该公司将这款新产品视为进军欧洲市场的立足点。

bridgeheads:/ˈbrɪdʒˌhɛdz/
n.
(化学)桥头原子
An atom in a molecule that is bonded to three or more other atoms. (Less common usage)
The carbon atom acts as a bridgehead in this complex structure. 在这种复杂的结构中，碳原子充当了桥头原子。


近义词:foothold, base, stronghold, outpost
反义词:rear, retreat
