annotating:/ˈænəteɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 注释；注解；标记 | Adding notes or comments to a text or diagram, explaining or criticizing it. |  She is annotating the historical document with her research findings. 她正在用她的研究结果注释这份历史文献。

The teacher asked students to annotate the poem for deeper understanding. 老师要求学生注释这首诗以加深理解。| synonyms: commenting, glossing, explaining, interpreting | antonyms:  ignoring, neglecting, overlooking | verb (present participle)

Etymology: From Late Latin *annotatus*, past participle of *annotare* "to mark, note," from Latin *ad-* "to" + *notare* "to mark."
