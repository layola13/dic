marquis:/ˈmɑːrkwɪs/ | n. |侯爵；侯爵夫人（尤指法国的）| A nobleman ranking next below a duke and above an earl or count.  A marquis is a title of nobility in some European countries, ranking below a duke and above a count. | The Marquis de Lafayette played a key role in the American Revolution. 拉斐特侯爵在美国独立战争中发挥了关键作用。| synonym: nobleman, peer, lord | antonym: commoner | noun

marquise:/mɑrˈkiːz/ | n. |(尤指过去法国贵妇的)遮阳帽；天篷；（尤指剧院等建筑物入口处的）雨篷 | A woman's wide-brimmed hat, typically worn tilted forward to shade the face. Also, a projecting structure over an entrance, such as a canopy or awning. | The marquise wore a stunning diamond necklace. 这位侯爵夫人戴着一条令人惊艳的钻石项链。 | synonym: canopy, awning, sunshade | antonym: - | noun
