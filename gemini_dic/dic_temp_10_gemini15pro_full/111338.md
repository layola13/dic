lived-in:/ˌlɪvdˈɪn/| adj.|住人的；有生活气息的；（尤指衣服、鞋子）穿旧了的| Appearing comfortable and inviting as a result of being used and occupied. | The house had a warm, lived-in feel.  这房子有一种温暖的家的感觉。|  The shoes had a comfortable, lived-in look.  这双鞋看起来很舒服，很有生活气息。|  homey, cozy, comfortable, familiar, worn | pristine, immaculate, new, unused | adjective

lived-in:/ˌlɪvdˈɪn/| adj.|（尤指房屋）有人住的| Occupied by residents. | The house was clearly lived-in, with toys scattered around.  这房子显然有人住，玩具散落一地。 | The apartment building looked lived-in and welcoming. 这栋公寓楼看起来有人居住，很温馨。| occupied, inhabited, tenanted | vacant, empty, unoccupied | adjective
