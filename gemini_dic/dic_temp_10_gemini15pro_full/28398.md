carbonade:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪd/ | n. |红烧牛肉；炭火烤肉|A stew of beef, typically braised in beer. Also, meat cooked over coals.|例句：We enjoyed a delicious carbonade flamande in Bruges. 我们在布鲁日享用了一份美味的佛兰德红烧牛肉。

例句：The chef prepared a savory carbonade over a charcoal fire.  厨师在炭火上准备了一份美味的烤肉。|近义词：stew, braise, pot roast|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "grill" if referring to the cooking method and contrasting with braising) |词性：noun
