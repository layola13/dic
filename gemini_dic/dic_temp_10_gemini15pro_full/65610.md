evangel:/ɪˈvændʒəl/ | n. | 福音，佳音，福音书 |  Something regarded as bringing good news or salvation.  A book of the Gospels. | The preacher delivered the evangel of hope and redemption. 传教士传递了希望和救赎的福音。|  The four Evangelists are Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. 四福音传道者是马太、马可、路加和约翰。 | synonyms: gospel, good news, glad tidings, message | antonyms: bad news, ill tidings | noun
