keck:/kek/
verb
呕吐; 作呕
to feel as if you are going to vomit
例句：The smell made me keck. 这气味让我作呕。

例句：I almost kecked when I saw the blood. 我看到血差点吐了。

近义词：retch, gag, heave
反义词: None

词性: verb
