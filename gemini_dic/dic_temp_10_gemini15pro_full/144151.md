outshapes:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪps/
verb
胜过，优于，超过(在形状或形式上)
surpasses in shape or form; excels in physical attributes.  This implies being superior in a way that relates to physical form, either literally or metaphorically.
The new sports car outshapes all its competitors. 这款新的跑车在外形上胜过所有竞争对手。

The modern skyscraper outshapes the older buildings in the city.  这座现代摩天大楼比城市里 older 的建筑物更具造型感。

Synonym: surpasses, excels, outclasses, outstrips, transcends
Antonym:  lags behind, falls short of, is inferior to
词性: 动词
