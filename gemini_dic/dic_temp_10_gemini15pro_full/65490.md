europeanized:/jʊərəˈpiːənaɪzd/
adjective
欧洲化的
Having adopted or been influenced by European customs, styles, or ideas.
The city has become increasingly europeanized in recent years.  这座城市近年来变得越来越欧洲化了。
europeanized:/jʊərəˈpiːənaɪzd/
verb
使欧洲化
To cause to adopt or be influenced by European customs, styles, or ideas.
Many developing countries have been europeanized through colonialism. 许多发展中国家由于殖民主义而被欧洲化了。


Synonyms: westernized, occidentalized
Antonyms: indigenized, localized
Etymology: From Europe + -ize (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become like") + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives)
