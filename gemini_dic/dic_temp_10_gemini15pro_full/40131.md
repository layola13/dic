contemplativeness:/kənˌtɛmplətɪvnəs/
|n.|沉思；冥想；深思熟虑|The state of being contemplative; given to or characterized by contemplation.|She sat in quiet contemplativeness, pondering the meaning of life. 她静静地坐着沉思，思考着生命的意义。|近义词：thoughtfulness, pensiveness, reflection, musing|反义词：frivolity, levity, thoughtlessness|词性: 名词

contemplate:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪt/ /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪt/
|v.|沉思；冥想；深思熟虑；打算|To think profoundly and at length; meditate.|He contemplated the meaning of the poem for hours. 他沉思了这首诗的含义好几个小时。|近义词：consider, ponder, meditate, muse|反义词：ignore, neglect, disregard|词性: 动词

contemplative:/kənˈtɛmplətɪv/ /kənˈtɛmplətɪv/
|adj.|沉思的；冥想的|Given to or characterized by contemplation.|She had a contemplative look on her face. 她脸上带着沉思的表情。|近义词：thoughtful, pensive, reflective, meditative|反义词：frivolous, thoughtless, impulsive|词性: 形容词
