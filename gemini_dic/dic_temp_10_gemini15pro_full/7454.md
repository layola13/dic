anisometric:/ˌænɪsɵˈmetrɪk/ |adj.| 不等轴的；大小不等的|Having unequal dimensions or measurements, especially of the eyes.  Referring to a condition where the two eyes have different refractive powers.|Example 1: Anisometric amblyopia is a type of lazy eye caused by significant differences in the prescription between the two eyes. 不等轴性弱视是一种由两只眼睛的处方存在显著差异引起的弱视。

Example 2: The architect designed an anisometric building with varying heights and widths.  这位建筑师设计了一座高低宽窄不等的建筑。|Synonym: unequal, asymmetrical, disproportionate|Antonym: isometric, symmetrical, proportionate|adjective
