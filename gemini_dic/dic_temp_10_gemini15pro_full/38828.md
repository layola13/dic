cones:/kəʊnz/ (British) /koʊnz/ (American)| n.|锥形物，圆锥体；(尤指松树的)球果；视锥细胞|A solid or hollow object with a round flat base and sides that slope up to a point, or something of this shape. Also refers to the seed-bearing structure of a pine tree, or the cone-shaped cells in the eye that detect light and color.|
The pine tree was covered in cones.|松树上满是球果。

The ice cream was served in cones.|冰淇淋是用蛋筒装的。

Traffic cones were placed around the accident.|交通锥被放置在事故周围。

The doctor checked the cones in her eyes.|医生检查了她眼睛里的视锥细胞。

近义词:pyramids,  spikes, strobili (for pine cones)|
反义词:|
词性:noun
