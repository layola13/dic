collation:/kəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |整理，校勘，核对；（尤指正式场合的）便餐，小吃 |The collation of the ancient manuscripts took many years.  古代手稿的校勘工作耗费了多年。|例句：The museum is undertaking the collation of its collection of medieval pottery.  博物馆正在着手整理其中世纪陶器藏品。

例句：A light collation was served after the lecture.  讲座结束后供应了简单的便餐。|近义词：comparison, assembly, gathering, arrangement, snack, light meal |反义词: dispersal, disorganization| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *collationem, accusative singular of collatio (“a bringing together, collecting, comparing”), from collatus, past participle of conferre (“to bring together, compare”), from com- (“together”) + ferre (“to bring, bear”).
