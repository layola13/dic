noncoherences:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərənsɪz/
n.
不连贯性；无条理性；不一致
The lack of logical connection or consistency.  A state where things do not fit together sensibly.
The student's essay was marked down for its noncoherences and lack of clear arguments.  这个学生的论文因为不连贯且缺乏清晰的论证而被扣分。
There were several noncoherences in his alibi, making the police suspicious.  他的不在场证明中存在几处不一致，这使得警方产生了怀疑。
incoherence, inconsistency, disunity, illogicality, disjointedness
coherence, consistency, unity, logicality
