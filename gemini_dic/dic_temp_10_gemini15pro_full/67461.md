extolling:/ɪkˈstəʊlɪŋ/ /ɪkˈstoʊlɪŋ/
verb
赞美；颂扬；高度赞扬
Praising enthusiastically.
The article extolled the virtues of a vegetarian diet. 这篇文章高度赞扬了素食的好处。

The speaker extolled the benefits of regular exercise. 演讲者赞扬了经常锻炼的好处。

近义词: praising, lauding, celebrating, commending, acclaiming
反义词: criticizing, condemning, denouncing, disparaging, belittling
词性: verb (present participle of extol)
词源: From Latin extollere "to raise up, lift up, elevate," from ex- "up" + tollere "to lift, raise."
