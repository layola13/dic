cheekiest:/ˈtʃiːkiɪst/
adj.
最厚脸皮的，最无耻的
Superlative adjective of cheeky: most cheeky.  Implies a playful or mildly disrespectful boldness, often in a charming or amusing way.
例句：That was the cheekiest thing I've ever heard! 那是我听过的最厚颜无耻的话！

例句：He gave me the cheekiest grin. 他给了我一个最淘气的笑容。
近义词：impudent, brazen, bold, audacious, forward
反义词：respectful, polite, timid, shy
词性：adjective
