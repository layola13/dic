mesocephalic:/ˌmesəˈsɛfəlɪk/ | adj. | 中头型的 | Having a head of medium proportions; specifically, having a cephalic index between 75 and 80. |  The skull was determined to be mesocephalic.  这个头骨被认定为中头型。 | Synonyms: normocranial, mesocephalic | Antonyms: brachycephalic, dolichocephalic | adjective

mesocephalic:/ˌmesəˈsɛfəlɪk/ | n. | 中头型的人 | A person with a mesocephalic head. |  Mesocephalics are common in many populations. 中头型的人在许多人群中很常见。 | Synonyms: normocranial | Antonyms: brachycephal, dolichocephal | noun
