例句："Crumbs!" she exclaimed, realizing she'd forgotten her keys. “哎呀！”她叫道，意识到自己忘了带钥匙。|近义词：fragments, bits, scraps, leavings (for food crumbs); gosh, darn, oh dear (for the exclamation)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the noun. For the exclamation, antonyms would be expressions of satisfaction or approval.)|词性:noun (primarily), interjection
