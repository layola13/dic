bannable:/ˈbænəbl/| adj.|可禁止的，可取缔的|Something that is bannable is something that is considered worthy of being banned or prohibited.  It often refers to actions or content in online communities or games.|His behavior in the online forum was bannable. 他在网上论坛的行为是应该被禁止的。|近义词：prohibitable, forbidden, illegal|反义词: permissible, allowed, legal|词性:形容词
词源: ban (v.) + -able (suffix)  The word "ban" comes from Old French "banir" meaning "to summon, proclaim, banish." The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for," or "worthy of."
