beauteously:/ˈbjuːtɪəsli/| adv.|美丽地，优美地|In a way that is beautiful; gracefully or attractively.|She danced beauteously across the stage. 她优美地舞动着穿过舞台。|The sunset painted the sky beauteously. 夕阳把天空染得非常美丽。|近义词：beautifully, gracefully, elegantly, attractively, prettily|反义词：ugly, hideously, unattractively, displeasingly|词性:副词
词源: From beauty + -ous + -ly. Ultimately from Latin bellus “beautiful”.
