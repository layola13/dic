misspend:/ˌmɪsˈspend/ | vt. | 误用，浪费，虚度 | to spend (time or money) unwisely or wastefully | He misspent his youth in idleness. 他把青春浪费在懒散上。 | Synonyms: squander, waste, fritter away | Antonyms: save, conserve, invest | verb

misspend:/ˌmɪsˈspend/ | vt. | 滥用，挥霍 | to use (something, especially money) in a way that is wrong or illegal |  The investigation revealed that he had misspent public funds. 调查显示他滥用了公共资金。 | Synonyms: embezzle, misuse, misappropriate | Antonyms: allocate, appropriate, distribute | verb
