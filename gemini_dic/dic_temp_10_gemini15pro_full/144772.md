over-effort:/ˌoʊvərˈɛfərt/| n.|过度努力，过度用力|Excessive effort, exertion beyond what is necessary or reasonable.  This can lead to burnout or injury.|He suffered a muscle strain from over-effort during the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛中因过度用力而肌肉拉伤。|近义词：overexertion, strain, overwork|反义词:under-exertion, relaxation|noun
