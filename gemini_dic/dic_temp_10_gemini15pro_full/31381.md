chardonnay:/ʃɑːr.dəˈneɪ/ (US), /ˌʃɑː.dəˈneɪ/ (UK)| n.|霞多丽（一种白葡萄酒）|A dry white wine made from green grapes originally grown in the Burgundy region of France.|例句：We enjoyed a bottle of chilled Chardonnay with our dinner. 我们晚餐喝了一瓶冰镇霞多丽。|例句：This Chardonnay has a crisp, fruity flavor.  这款霞多丽口感清爽，果香浓郁。|近义词：white wine, Chablis (similar style of wine)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific wine type, could consider red wine as a general contrast)|词性:noun
