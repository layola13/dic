nonreflective:/ˌnɑːnriˈflektɪv/
adjective
非反射性的；不反射的
Not reflecting light or other radiation.
The car's nonreflective paint made it difficult to see at night.  这辆车的不反光油漆使它在夜间难以被看见。
Stealth aircraft have nonreflective surfaces. 隐形飞机有不反光的表面。
Synonyms:  non-reflecting, matt, matte, dull, unpolished
Antonyms: reflective, shiny, glossy, polished
词性: 形容词
