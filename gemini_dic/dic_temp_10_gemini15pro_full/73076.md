footslog:/ˈfʊtslɒɡ/ (British English) /ˈfʊtslɔːɡ/ (American English)| n. vi.|苦差事，艰苦跋涉，步测|A long or tiring walk.|例句：After a long footslog through the mountains, we finally reached our destination.  经过在山区漫长而疲惫的跋涉，我们终于到达了目的地。

例句：The campaign involved a lot of footslogging around the country. 这场竞选活动需要在全国各地进行大量的艰苦跋涉。|近义词：trek, hike, slog, tramp|反义词:ride, drive, flight|词性:名词, 动词
