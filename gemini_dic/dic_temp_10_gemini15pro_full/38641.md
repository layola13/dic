concupiscible:/kənˈkjuːpɪsəbl/ /kɑːnˈkjuːpɪsəbl/
adjective
好色的；贪图肉欲的；易受诱惑的
Characterized by strong sexual desire; lustful.  Relating to desires, especially sexual desires, that are considered morally wrong.
The priest warned against concupiscible thoughts.  神父告诫人们要抵制淫念。
Synonyms: lustful, lecherous, lascivious, carnal, sensual
Antonyms: chaste, pure, virtuous, ascetic
词性: adjective

concupiscence:/kənˈkjuːpɪsns/ /kɑːnˈkjuːpɪsns/
noun
性欲；贪欲；肉欲
Strong sexual desire; lust. A strong desire for something, especially something considered morally wrong.
He struggled to overcome his concupiscence. 他努力克服自己的欲望。
Synonyms: lust, lechery, lasciviousness, carnality
Antonyms: chastity, purity, virtue, asceticism
词性: noun
