denumbering:/diːˈnʌmbərɪŋ/
n.|去数;减少数量|The denumbering of the troops after the war was a complex process. 战后部队的裁减是一个复杂的过程。|English explanation:  The act of reducing a number, often referring to a decrease in population or workforce.|例句：The denumbering of the workforce due to automation is a growing concern. 由于自动化，劳动力的减少是一个日益受到关注的问题。

|近义词：reduction, decrease, downsizing|反义词: increase, growth, expansion|词性:名词

denumbering:/diːˈnʌmbərɪŋ/
v.|减少…的数目|The company is denumbering its staff. 公司正在裁员。|English explanation: Reducing the number of something, often referring to reducing the size of a population or workforce.|例句：They are denumbering the herd to prevent overgrazing. 他们正在减少牧群数量以防止过度放牧。
