hyperdulia:/ˌhaɪpərˈduːliə/ | n. | 超敬礼; 对圣母玛利亚的特殊敬礼 |  Special veneration given to Mary, the mother of Jesus, recognizing her unique role in salvation history. It is a higher veneration than dulia (veneration of saints), but lower than latria (adoration due to God alone). | Catholics practice hyperdulia towards Mary. 天主教徒对玛利亚行超敬礼。| 近义词: veneration, reverence | 反义词: disrespect, disdain | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *hyperdulia, from Ancient Greek ὑπερδουλεία (huperdouleía, “excessive servitude”), from ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, above”) + δουλεία (douleía, “slavery, servitude”).
