beseech:/bɪˈsiːtʃ/
verb
恳求，哀求
To ask (someone) urgently and sometimes emotionally to do something; implore; beg.
The villagers beseeched the king for help. 村民恳求国王的帮助。
Synonyms: implore, entreat, beg, plead
Antonyms: demand, command, order
词性: 动词
