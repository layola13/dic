filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/| n. adj.| 细丝工艺；金银丝细工；(尤指文学或音乐)精雕细琢，精美的装饰| Ornamental work of fine (typically gold or silver) wire formed into delicate tracery.| The pendant was a delicate filigree of gold and silver.  这枚吊坠是用金银细丝精雕细琢而成的。|  The story is told with a filigree of detail.  这个故事讲述得非常细致。 | 近义词：tracery, ornamentation, decoration, embellishment | 反义词: plainness, simplicity | 词性: 名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *filigrana*, from Latin *fīlum* (thread) + *grānum* (grain).
