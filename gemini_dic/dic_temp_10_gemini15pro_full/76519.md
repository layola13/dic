galeopithecus:/ˌɡeɪliˈoʊpɪθəkəs/ | n. | 菲律宾鼯猴 | A Southeast Asian arboreal mammal (also called a colugo, flying lemur, or dermopteran) with large eyes, a pointed snout, and skin folds that form gliding membranes stretching between its neck, limbs, and tail. |  Galeopithecus, also known as colugos, are fascinating creatures that glide through the Southeast Asian rainforest. 菲律宾鼯猴，也被称为猫猴，是在东南亚雨林中滑翔的迷人动物。 |  colugo, flying lemur, dermopteran | None | Noun
