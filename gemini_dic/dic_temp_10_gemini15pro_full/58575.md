dulciana:/duːlˈsiːənə/ /dʌlˈsiːənə/
n.
一种柔和音调的古钢琴|A keyboard instrument of the harpsichord family, having a soft, sweet tone.
The dulciana produced a delicate and ethereal sound, perfect for intimate chamber music. | 这架德西玛钢琴发出一种精致空灵的声音，非常适合私密的室内乐。
synonyms: harpsichord, virginal, spinet
antonyms: fortepiano, pianoforte
noun
