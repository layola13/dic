flask:/flɑːsk/ /flæsk/ | n. |烧瓶；细颈瓶；酒瓶|A container, often with a narrow neck, used for holding liquids, especially chemicals or for serving drinks.|例句：The chemist poured the solution into a flask.  化学家将溶液倒入烧瓶中。
