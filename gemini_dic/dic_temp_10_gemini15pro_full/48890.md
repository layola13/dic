defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/
|vt.|欺诈，诈骗|To illegally obtain money from (someone) by deception.|例句：He was found guilty of defrauding the government of millions of dollars. 他被判犯有诈骗政府数百万美元的罪行。

例句：She defrauded her elderly parents out of their life savings. 她骗取了她年迈父母的毕生积蓄。
|近义词：cheat, swindle, deceive, trick|反义词：repay, compensate|词性: 动词 (Verb)


词源:
From Late Middle English *defrauden, from Anglo-Norman defrauder, from Old French defrauder, from Latin dēfraudāre, from dē- (“de-, from”) + fraudāre (“to cheat, deceive”).
