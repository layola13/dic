citys: /ˈsɪtiz/
|n.| 城市（复数，非标准用法，标准用法是cities）| "Citys" is a non-standard plural form of "city." The standard plural is "cities."  It refers to multiple urban centers. While occasionally seen, it's grammatically incorrect.| Example: He visited many citys (incorrect usage) on his trip.  他在旅途中参观了许多城市。|Synonym: cities|Antonym: countryside, rural areas|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cite*, from Old French *cité*, from Latin *cīvitās* ("citizenship, condition of a citizen, community of citizens, state, city"), from *cīvis* ("citizen").
