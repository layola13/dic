dubiosity:/ˌdjuːbiˈɒsɪti/
n.
可疑性，不确定性
The dubiosity of the evidence led the judge to dismiss the case.
证词的可疑性导致法官驳回了案件。
Dubiosity surrounded the company's claims of record profits.
该公司创纪录利润的说法令人怀疑。
近义词:doubt, uncertainty, suspicion, questionableness
反义词:certainty, assurance, conviction
词性:noun

dubious:/ˈdjuːbiəs/
adj.
可疑的，不确定的
I was dubious about his explanation.
我对他的解释表示怀疑。
The company's claims of record profits seemed dubious.
该公司创纪录利润的说法似乎令人怀疑。
近义词:doubtful, uncertain, suspicious, questionable
反义词:certain, sure, definite, reliable
词性:adjective
