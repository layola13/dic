caper:/ˈkeɪpər/| v. n.|雀跃，嬉戏;  恶作剧，胡闹; 刺山柑花蕾;|From Middle French *capre, from Italian capriccio "whim," from capra "goat," perhaps from the goat's frisky behavior.  A playful leap or skip; a prank or mischievous act; the flower bud of a Mediterranean shrub, pickled and used as a seasoning.|The children capered about in the garden. 孩子们在花园里嬉戏玩耍。|近义词：frolic, gambol, prance, frisk, skip, romp, cavort, lark, prank, antic|反义词:mope, sulk|词性:动词, 名词
