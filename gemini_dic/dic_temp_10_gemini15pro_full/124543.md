mountebank:/ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋk/
n.|江湖骗子；吹牛大王|A person who deceives others by claiming to have special knowledge or skills, especially in medicine. |He was a mountebank, selling fake cures to desperate people. 他是个江湖骗子，向绝望的人兜售假药。|Synonyms: charlatan, quack, fraud, imposter, swindler|Antonyms: expert, professional, authority|noun

mountebank:/ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋk/
v.|招摇撞骗；吹嘘|To deceive or cheat people by pretending to have special knowledge or skills. |He mountebanked his way into a position of power. 他靠招摇撞骗获得了权力地位。|Synonyms: deceive, cheat, defraud, swindle, trick|Antonyms: be honest, be truthful|verb
