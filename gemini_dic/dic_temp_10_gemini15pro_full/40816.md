convolvulin:/kənˈvɒlvjʊlɪn/ | n. |旋花苷，泻根素|A purgative glycoside, C₅₅H₉₆O₂₇, obtained from the root of jalap.| Convolvulin is a powerful purgative and should be used with caution. 旋花苷是一种强泻药，应谨慎使用。|Synonyms: jalapin, scammonin|Antonyms: None|Noun

convolvulin:/kənˈvɒlvjʊlɪn/ | n. |旋花树脂|A resin obtained from the root of jalap, containing convolvulin.|The convolvulin resin is used in some traditional medicines. 旋花树脂用于一些传统药物中。|Synonyms: jalap resin|Antonyms: None|Noun
