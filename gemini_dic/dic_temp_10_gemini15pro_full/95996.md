incuriously:/ˌɪnˈkjʊəriəsli/
adverb
漠不关心地，不好奇地
Showing a lack of interest or curiosity.
She looked at the painting incuriously, then walked away. 她漠不关心地看了一眼那幅画，然后走开了。
He incuriously flipped through the pages of the book.  他漫不经心地翻阅着书页。
Synonyms: apathetically, indifferently, listlessly, uninterestedly
Antonyms: curiously, inquisitively, interestedly
词性: 副词
