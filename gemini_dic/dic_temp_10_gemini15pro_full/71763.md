flea:/fliː/
n.
跳蚤
A small, wingless, bloodsucking insect that lives as a parasite on mammals and birds.
The dog was scratching constantly because it had fleas.  这只狗不停地抓挠，因为它身上有跳蚤。
近义词:louse, parasite
反义词:None
词性:noun


fleagh (Irish): /flæ/ or /fleɪ/ (depending on dialect)
n.
盛宴；节日；集市 (尤指爱尔兰或苏格兰的)
A festival, feast, or fair (especially in Ireland or Scotland).
The annual fleagh draws musicians and dancers from all over the country.  一年一度的盛宴吸引了来自全国各地的音乐家和舞蹈家。
近义词:festival, celebration, feast, fair
反义词:None
词性:noun


词源：

flea: From Old English *flēah, from Proto-Germanic *flauhō, from Proto-Indo-European *pléh₁us (“flea”). Cognate with Dutch vloo, German Floh, Danish loppe.

fleagh (Irish): From Old Irish fledach, from Proto-Celtic *wledag-os.  Related to Welsh gwledd (“feast, banquet”).
