edgily:/ˈɛdʒɪli/
adjective
紧张不安地；焦躁地
In a nervous or irritable way.
She laughed edgily. 她紧张地笑了笑。
Synonyms: nervously, anxiously, tensely, jumpily, apprehensively
Antonyms: calmly, serenely, peacefully
词性: 形容词
