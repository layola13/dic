extractabilities:/ɪkˌstræktəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可提取性；可萃取性|The extractabilities of different compounds vary depending on the solvent used. 不同化合物的可提取性取决于所使用的溶剂。|例句：The study investigated the extractabilities of various metals from contaminated soil.  这项研究调查了从污染土壤中提取各种金属的可提取性。

例句：The extractabilities of the active ingredients from the herbal medicine were analyzed. 对草药中活性成分的可提取性进行了分析。|近义词：solubility, dissolvability|反义词: insolubility|词性:noun
