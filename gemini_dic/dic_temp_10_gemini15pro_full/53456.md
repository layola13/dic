disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/| v. |不赞成，反对|To have or express an unfavorable opinion of.|例句：The teacher disapproved of the student's behavior. 老师不赞成这个学生的举动。|例句：My parents disapprove of my new boyfriend. 我的父母不赞成我的新男友。|近义词：object to, oppose, condemn, criticize|反义词：approve, endorse, support|词性:动词
disapproved:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvd/| adj. |不被批准的，不被认可的|Not approved of; viewed unfavorably.|例句：The disapproved project was eventually abandoned. 不被批准的项目最终被放弃了。|例句：His disapproved methods led to his dismissal. 他不被认可的方法导致了他被解雇。|近义词：rejected, condemned, censured|反义词：approved, accepted, endorsed|词性:形容词
