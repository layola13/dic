colcothar: /kɒlˈkəθɑːr/ /kɑːlˈkoʊθɑːr/
colcothar: n.
colcothar:  红粉，铁丹，氧化铁红
colcothar: A reddish-brown powder of ferric oxide, obtained by heating ferrous sulfate. It is used as a pigment and a polishing agent.
colcothar: Colcothar is commonly used as a pigment in paints and primers.
colcothar: synonym: rouge, iron oxide red, red oxide, ferric oxide
colcothar: antonym:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound.  One could consider opposing colors, like green or blue pigments, as contextual antonyms in specific applications.)
