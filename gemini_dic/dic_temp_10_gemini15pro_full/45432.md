curtailer:/kərˈteɪlər/
|n.|  削减者; 缩减者; 限制者 | someone or something that curtails something | The budget curtailer reduced spending on non-essential items.  预算削减者减少了不必要的开支。 | synonyms: reducer, restricter, cutter | antonyms: expander, increaser | noun

curtail:/kərˈteɪl/
|vt.| 缩减；削减；限制 | reduce in extent or quantity; impose a restriction on |  The government curtailed public spending. 政府削减了公共开支。 | synonyms: reduce, decrease, lessen, diminish, cut back, cut down | antonyms: increase, expand, extend, lengthen, prolong | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *curtailler*, from Old French *curtal*, from Late Latin *curtus* "shortened," from Latin *cors*, *cordis* "heart."  The original sense was "to dock the tail of an animal," later generalized to "shorten" or "reduce."
