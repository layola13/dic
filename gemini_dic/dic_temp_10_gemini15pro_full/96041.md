indecencies:/ˌɪnˈdiːsənsiz/| n. |不雅行为，猥亵行为；下流举动|acts or remarks that offend against accepted standards of decency or morality|The comedian was criticized for the indecencies in his routine.  这个喜剧演员因其表演中的不雅行为而受到批评。

The book was banned for its sexual indecencies. 这本书因其性方面的不雅内容而被禁。|近义词：obscenities, improprieties, immoralities|反义词: decencies, proprieties|词性:名词(plural)


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French indecence, from indecent (see indecent).
