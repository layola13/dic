desideratum:/dɪˌzɪdəˈreɪtəm/ /dɪˌsɪdəˈreɪtəm/
noun
必需品；迫切需要的东西
something that is needed or wanted
例句：A reliable source of information is a desideratum for any historian. 对任何历史学家来说，可靠的信息来源都是必需的。

例句：Peace and quiet are the main desiderata of city dwellers. 和平与安宁是都市居民的主要愿望。

近义词：requirement, necessity, essential, prerequisite, requisite
反义词：nonessential, luxury

词性:n.

词源: Latin dēsīderātum "something desired," neuter past participle of dēsīderāre "to long for," from de- "from" (see de-) + sīderāre "to long for the stars," from sīdus (genitive sīderis) "star, group of stars, constellation."
