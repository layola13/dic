heartedness:/ˈhɑːrtɪdnəs/| n.|热心肠，善良，亲切|The nurse showed great heartedness in caring for her patients.  这位护士在照顾病人方面表现出了极大的热心肠。|Example:  Her heartedness and generosity made her beloved by all. 她的善良和慷慨让她受到所有人的喜爱。|Synonyms: kindness, compassion, warmth, generosity, benevolence|Antonyms: coldness, cruelty, indifference, selfishness, malevolence|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From heart + -ed + -ness.  "Heart" refers to the emotional center, and "-ed" forms an adjective indicating a quality possessed. "-ness" creates a noun from the adjective, signifying the state or quality of being hearted.
