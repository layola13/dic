cist:/sɪst/ | n. | 囊肿; 膀胱;  |A closed sac having a distinct membrane and developing abnormally in a cavity or structure of the body. |  The doctor found a cyst on her ovary. 医生在她的卵巢上发现了一个囊肿。| synonym: sac, vesicle | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a physical structure like a cyst) | noun


Etymology:
From Latin *cista* "box, chest," from Greek *kistē* "box, chest, basket."
