eat crow:/iːt kroʊ/
verb phrase
承认错误;被迫做不愿做的事;收回前言;吞下苦果
To admit one's mistake and apologize, especially when one has been wrong in a humiliating way.
He boasted that his team would win, but he had to eat crow when they lost badly.  他吹嘘他的球队会赢，但当他们惨败时，他不得不承认错误。
I might have to eat crow if I'm wrong about this. 如果我在这件事上错了，我可能不得不收回前言。
synonyms: admit defeat, back down, climb down, swallow one's pride, retract a statement
antonyms:  gloat, boast, brag
