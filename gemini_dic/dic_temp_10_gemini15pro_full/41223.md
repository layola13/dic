corallian:/kɒˈræliən/ (UK) /ˌkɔːrˈeɪliən/ (US)| adj. n.|珊瑚的，珊瑚礁的；科拉里阶的|Relating to or denoting the Corallian, the European stage of the Upper Jurassic, characterized by coral and oolitic limestone formations.  Also, a noun referring to the rocks of this stage.|The museum displayed fossils from the Corallian stage.  博物馆展出了科拉里阶的化石。|The Corallian rocks are rich in fossils.  科拉里阶的岩石富含化石。|近义词：Jurassic, Oxfordian, Kimmeridgian|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From "coral" + "-ian" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin "corallium".
