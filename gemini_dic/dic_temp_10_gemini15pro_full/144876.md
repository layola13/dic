over-report:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈpɔːrt/ | v. | 过度汇报，夸大汇报 | To report something as greater in number, amount, or extent than it actually is.  | The company was accused of over-reporting its profits.  该公司被指控夸大其利润。 | The witness tended to over-report the speed of the car. 目击者倾向于夸大汽车的速度。 | exaggerate, inflate, overstate | under-report, downplay | verb

over-report:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈpɔːrt/ | n. | 过度汇报，夸大汇报(的情况) | An instance of reporting something as greater than it actually is. |  There was evidence of significant over-report in the survey data. 调查数据中有明显的夸大汇报的证据。| The over-report of minor incidents created unnecessary panic. 对小事件的夸大报道造成了不必要的恐慌。 | exaggeration, inflation, overstatement | under-report, understatement | noun
