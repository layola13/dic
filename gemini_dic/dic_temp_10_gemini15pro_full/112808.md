lucerne:/luːˈsɜːrn/ /luːˈsɝːn/| n.|紫花苜蓿；苜蓿草|A leguminous plant (Medicago sativa) with bluish-purple flowers and trifoliate leaves, grown as fodder.  It is also called alfalfa.|The farmer planted several acres of lucerne for his cattle.|近义词：alfalfa|反义词:|noun
