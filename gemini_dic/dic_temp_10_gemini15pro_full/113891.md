madreporaria:/ˌmædrɪpəˈrɛəriə/ | n. | 珊瑚虫纲，石珊瑚目 |A large order of coelenterates, the stony corals, that form reefs in warm seas. They secrete a calcareous skeleton and have radial partitions within the corallite.  | 例句：The Great Barrier Reef is primarily composed of madreporaria. 大堡礁主要由珊瑚虫纲构成。

例句：Madreporaria are vital for maintaining the biodiversity of coral reefs. 珊瑚虫纲对于维护珊瑚礁的生物多样性至关重要。 | 近义词：stony corals, scleractinians | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially soft corals as a contrasting group) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *madrepora, from Ancient Greek μᾰδρεπώρης (madreporēs, “mother of pores”), from μήτηρ (mḗtēr, “mother”) + πόρος (póros, “pore”).
