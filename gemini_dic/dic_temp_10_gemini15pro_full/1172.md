acclimate:/ˈæklaɪmeɪt/ (US) /əˈklaɪmət/ (UK)| vt. vi.| 使适应；使服水土；适应|To adapt to a new climate, environment, or situation.|It took me a while to acclimate to the high altitude. 我花了一段时间才适应高海拔。

The plants acclimated quickly to the greenhouse. 这些植物很快就适应了温室环境。|近义词：adapt, adjust, accustom|反义词: unacclimate|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She acclimates easily to new surroundings. = 她很容易适应新环境。
He acclimates well to different cultures. = 他能很好地适应不同的文化。


一般过去时:
The team acclimated quickly to the heat. = 团队很快适应了炎热的天气。
They acclimated themselves to the new time zone. = 他们适应了新的时区。


现在完成时:
The climbers have acclimated to the thin air. = 登山者已经适应了稀薄的空气。
We have acclimated to the colder weather. = 我们已经适应了较冷的天气。


过去完成时:
By the time the expedition began, they had acclimated to the altitude. = 到探险开始时，他们已经适应了海拔高度。
She had already acclimated to the city life before she started college. = 在她开始上大学之前，她已经适应了城市生活。


将来时:
The astronauts will need to acclimate to zero gravity. = 宇航员需要适应零重力。
They will acclimate to their new roles quickly. = 他们会很快适应他们的新角色。


虚拟语气:
If I were to move to Alaska, I would need to acclimate to the cold. = 如果我要搬到阿拉斯加，我需要适应寒冷的天气。
I wish I could acclimate to this humidity more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地适应这种湿度。


被动语态:
The animals are being acclimated to their new enclosure. = 动物们正在适应它们的新围栏。
The seedlings were acclimated to the outdoor conditions gradually. = 幼苗逐渐适应了室外条件。


疑问句:
How long does it take to acclimate to a new time zone? = 适应新的时区需要多长时间？
Do you acclimate easily to new environments? = 你容易适应新环境吗？


否定句:
He did not acclimate well to the pressure of the job. = 他不太适应这份工作的压力。
I haven't acclimated to the spicy food yet. = 我还没有适应辛辣的食物。
