annuities:/əˈnjuːɪtiz/
n.
年金；养老金
a fixed sum of money paid to someone each year, typically for the rest of their life.
Example: She receives an annuity of $10,000 per year. 她每年收到一万美元的年金。

Example:  He invested in an annuity to provide a steady income stream during retirement. 他投资年金是为了在退休期间获得稳定的收入来源。

近义词:pension, allowance, payment
反义词:lump sum
词性:noun
