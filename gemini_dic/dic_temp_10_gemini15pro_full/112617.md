low-cost:/ˌloʊˈkɔːst/ /ˌləʊˈkɒst/| adj.|低成本的，廉价的|Relatively inexpensive; charging low prices.|例句：They offer low-cost flights to Europe. 他们提供飞往欧洲的廉价航班。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, budget, affordable|反义词：expensive, costly, high-end, premium|词性:形容词

low-cost:/ˌloʊˈkɔːst/ /ˌləʊˈkɒst/| n.|低成本|The state of being inexpensive.|例句：The low-cost of living attracted many people to the area.  该地区低廉的生活成本吸引了许多人。|近义词：cheapness, affordability|反义词：expense, costliness|词性:名词
