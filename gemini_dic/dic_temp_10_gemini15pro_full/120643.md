mispracticing: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪsɪŋ/
verb
渎职；失职；不当操作；滥用
engaging in malpractice; practicing incorrectly or unethically.
The doctor was accused of mispracticing medicine.  这个医生被指控医疗失当。
He lost his license for mispracticing law. 他因不当执业而失去了律师执照。
Synonyms: malpractice,  misconduct,  abuse,  negligence
Antonyms:  proper practice, ethical conduct, due diligence
词性：动词
