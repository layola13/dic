cliqueless:/ˈkliːklɪs/| adj.|无小集团的；不结党营私的|Not divided into cliques; not favoring cliques.  Lacking cliques or the tendency to form cliques. Characterized by the absence of cliques.|The teacher fostered a cliqueless classroom environment where everyone felt included. 老师营造了一个没有小集团的课堂环境，让每个人都感到被包容。|Synonyms: inclusive, unbiased, impartial, egalitarian|Antonyms: cliquish, exclusive, partisan|adjective
