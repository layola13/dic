hematinic:/ˌhiːməˈtɪnɪk/ | adj. n. |补血的，增血的；补血剂| An agent that increases the number of red blood cells or the hemoglobin concentration in the blood. |
Example 1: The doctor prescribed a hematinic to treat her anemia. 医生给她开了补血药来治疗她的贫血。

Example 2: Iron supplements are a common type of hematinic. 铁补充剂是一种常见的补血药。
| 近义词: antianemic,  erythropoietic | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示"减少红细胞"或"导致贫血"的词，例如：hemolytic（溶血的）) | 词性: adjective, noun


hematinic:/ˌhiːməˈtɪnɪk/ | n. |补血剂| A medicine or substance that helps to increase the number of red blood cells or the hemoglobin concentration in the blood. |
Example 1:  She was given a hematinic to help boost her red blood cell count. 她被给予了一种补血剂来帮助提高她的红细胞计数。

Example 2:  He took hematinics regularly after his surgery.  他在手术后定期服用补血剂。

词源: From Greek haima (blood) + -ic.
