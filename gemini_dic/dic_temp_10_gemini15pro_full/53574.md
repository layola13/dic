disbend:/dɪsˈbɛnd/
verb
放松；卸下（武器）；解散
to relax from a strained or stiff position; to take apart (a weapon); to dismiss from service

The soldiers were allowed to disbend and rest after the long march.
士兵们在长途行军后被允许放松休息。

The hunter disbended his bow after a successful hunt.
猎人成功狩猎后卸下了弓。

The king disbended his army after the war was over.
战争结束后，国王解散了他的军队。


近义词: relax, unbend, dismiss, discharge
反义词: tighten, assemble, convene

词性: v.
