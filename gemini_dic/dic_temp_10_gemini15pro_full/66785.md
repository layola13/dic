ETYMOLOGY->
From French *exotique*, from Late Latin *exoticus*, from Greek *exōtikós* “foreign,” from *exō* “outside.”  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is interested in something. Therefore, "exoticist" is someone interested in things that are "exotic" or foreign.
