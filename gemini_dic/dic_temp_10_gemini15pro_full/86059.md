heapiest:/ˈhiːpiɪst/| adj.|最多的，堆积最高的|Superlative form of "heaping," meaning piled up to the greatest extent.  It describes something that is filled to overflowing or contains the largest amount of something piled up. |The heapiest bowl of ice cream I've ever seen. 我见过堆得最高的冰淇淋。|近义词：fullest, most abundant, overflowing|反义词: emptiest, smallest, least|词性:形容词
