communique:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪ/ /kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪ/
noun
公报；官方声明
An official announcement or statement, especially one made to the public or media.
The president issued a communique after the meeting. 总统在会后发表了一份公报。
The government issued a communique concerning the recent political unrest. 政府就最近的政治动荡发表了一份公报。
synonyms: statement, announcement, bulletin, report, dispatch, release
antonyms: secret, concealment, cover-up
n.

Etymology:
Borrowed from French communiqué, past participle of communiquer (“to communicate”), from Latin communicare.
