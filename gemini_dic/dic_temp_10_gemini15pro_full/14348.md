autonomism:/ɔːˈtɒnəmɪzəm/
n.
自治主义; 自治; 自主性
The belief in or advocacy of a policy of autonomy.  A political theory that holds that the primary political unit should be autonomous, or self-governing.
The region's growing autonomism led to calls for independence. 该地区日益增长的自治主义导致了独立的呼声。
Autonomism is a key concept in many libertarian philosophies. 自治是许多自由主义哲学中的一个关键概念。
synonyms: autonomy, independence, self-government, self-determination, home rule
antonyms: dependence, subordination, heteronomy
noun
