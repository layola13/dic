nonutilities:/ˌnɑːnjuːˈtɪlətiz/
noun
非公用事业公司；非公共事业
Companies that are not involved in providing essential services like water, electricity, or gas.
Many nonutilities are investing in renewable energy sources. 许多非公用事业公司正在投资可再生能源。
Synonyms: non-utility companies, private sector businesses
Antonyms: utilities, public utility companies
