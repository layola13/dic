fallacious:/fəˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
谬误的，虚假的
containing or based on a fallacy; misleading
例句：His argument was based on fallacious reasoning. 他的论点是基于谬误的推理。

例句：The advertisement made fallacious claims about the product's effectiveness.  这则广告对产品的效果做出了虚假的声明。

近义词：false, misleading, deceptive, erroneous, spurious, invalid, unsound
反义词：true, valid, sound, correct, accurate, legitimate
词性: adjective
