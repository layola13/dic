deployability:/dɪˌplɔɪəˈbɪləti/| n. |可部署性，可配置性|The ease and speed with which a system, application, or product can be deployed into a production environment.  It encompasses factors like automation, configuration management, and testing.|The team prioritized deployability to ensure rapid releases.  该团队优先考虑可部署性以确保快速发布.|近义词：installability, usability, readiness|反义词：undeployability, inflexibility|noun
