gendarme:/ʒɑ̃ˈdɑːrm/ | n. |宪兵，警察|A member of a body of armed police, especially in France and some other European countries.|例句：The gendarmes patrolled the streets on horseback. 宪兵骑着马在街上巡逻。

例句：The gendarme investigated the scene of the accident. 宪兵调查了事故现场。

近义词：police officer, policeman, law enforcement officer|反义词:criminal, lawbreaker |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gent d'armes (“men-at-arms”), from gent (“people”) + de (“of”) + armes (“arms, weapons”).
