ambrosian:/æmˈbroʊʒən/ |adj.|神仙的; 美味的; 特别美味的| Exceptionally pleasing to taste or smell; extremely delicious; divine.| The ambrosian aroma of the freshly baked bread filled the kitchen. 新烤面包的美味香气充满了整个厨房。|Synonyms: delicious, heavenly, divine, delectable, exquisite|Antonyms: unpleasant, distasteful, unappetizing|adjective

ambrosia:/æmˈbroʊʒɪə/ |n.| 神仙的食物; 美味佳肴|The food of the Greek and Roman gods; something extremely pleasing to taste or smell.|  The chef's signature dish was truly an ambrosia.  这位厨师的招牌菜真是一道美味佳肴。|Synonyms: delicacy, nectar, treat|Antonyms: swill, garbage|noun
