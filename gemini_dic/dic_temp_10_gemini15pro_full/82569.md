guaranty:/ˈɡærənti/ | n. vt.|保证，担保；保证书，担保物|a formal promise that something will be done, or a document containing this promise|The store gave a guaranty that the television would work for at least two years. 这家商店保证这台电视至少能用两年。|近义词：warranty, assurance, pledge|反义词：breach, denial|noun
guaranty:/ˈɡærənti/ | vt.|保证，担保|to promise that something will be done|The manufacturer guarantees this product for one year. 制造商保证该产品一年。|近义词：warrant, assure, pledge|反义词：deny, refuse|verb
