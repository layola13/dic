pacifism:/ˈpæsɪfɪzəm/| n.|和平主义|The belief that war and violence are unjustifiable and that all disputes should be settled by peaceful means.|Her pacifism prevented her from supporting the war effort. 她的和平主义使她无法支持战争。|近义词：peacefulness, nonviolence, anti-militarism|反义词：militarism, belligerence|词性:名词
词源：Late 19th century: from French pacifisme, from pacifique ‘pacific’.
