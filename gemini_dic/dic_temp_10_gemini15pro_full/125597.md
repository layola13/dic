multivagant: /ˌmʌltɪˈveɪɡənt/
adjective
多游荡的；漂泊不定的；游历多国的
Wandering or straying much; roving.  Having traveled to many different places.
He led a multivagant life, rarely staying in one place for long. 他过着漂泊不定的生活，很少在一个地方待很长时间。
She boasts a multivagant past, having visited over fifty countries. 她夸耀自己游历多国的过去，已经访问过五十多个国家。
近义词: nomadic, itinerant, peripatetic, wandering, roving
反义词: settled, sedentary, static
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *multivagans, from multi- ("many") + vagans ("wandering").
