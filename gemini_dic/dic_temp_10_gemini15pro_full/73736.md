forklift:/ˈfɔːrklɪft/ (US) /ˈfɔːklɪft/ (UK)| n.|叉车|A powered industrial truck used to lift and move materials over short distances.|The forklift driver carefully maneuvered the heavy load. 叉车司机小心地操纵着沉重的货物。|Synonyms: lift truck, forklift truck, stacker|Antonyms: N/A|noun

forklift:/ˈfɔːrklɪft/ (US) /ˈfɔːklɪft/ (UK)| v.|用叉车搬运|To move (something) with a forklift.|They forklift the pallets onto the truck. 他们用叉车把货盘搬到卡车上。|Synonyms: lift, hoist, raise|Antonyms: lower, drop|verb
