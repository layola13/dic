misanalysis:/ˌmɪsənˈælɪsɪs/ | n. | 错误分析，误析 | An incorrect or faulty analysis of something. |  The misanalysis of the data led to flawed conclusions. 数据的错误分析导致了有缺陷的结论。

The doctor's misanalysis of her symptoms resulted in a delayed diagnosis. 医生对她的症状的误诊导致了延误诊断。 | 近义词: misinterpretation, misconstruction, error | 反义词: correct analysis, accurate interpretation | 词性: 名词
