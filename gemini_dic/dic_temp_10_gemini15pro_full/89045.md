holocryptic:/ˌhɒləˈkrɪptɪk/| adj.|全隐晶质的；完全隐晶的|Composed entirely of crystals too small to be seen with the naked eye.  A rock texture in which the individual crystals are too small to be seen unaided. |The rock sample was determined to be holocryptic after microscopic analysis.  这块岩石样本经过显微镜分析后被确定为全隐晶质的。

The volcanic rock exhibited a holocryptic texture. 这块火山岩展现出全隐晶质的结构。|近义词: aphanitic, microcrystalline|反义词: phaneritic|词性: adjective
