immensurability:/ɪnˌmenʃərəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可估量性；不能比较性；不相称
The state or quality of being immeasurable or impossible to compare, especially because of different qualities or values.
The immensity of the universe demonstrates its immensurability. 宇宙的浩瀚展现了它的不可估量性。
Near synonyms: incommensurability, incomparability, disparity, disproportion
Antonyms: commensurability, comparability, similarity, equivalence
noun

incommensurable:/ɪnˌkəˈmenʃərəbl/
adj.
不可比较的；不相称的；不能通约的
Not able to be judged by the same standard as something else; disproportionate.
The two cultures were so different as to be incommensurable. 这两种文化截然不同，无法比较。
Near synonyms: incompatible, disparate, disproportionate, unequal
Antonyms: commensurable, comparable, similar, equivalent
adjective
