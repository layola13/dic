outsporting:/ˌaʊtˈspɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
以运动胜过,在体育运动上胜过
Surpassing someone in athletic ability or skill.
He was outsporting his opponent in every aspect of the game. 他在比赛的各个方面都胜过他的对手。
outsport:v.在体育运动上胜过
outplay, outmaneuver, outperform
be outsorted
