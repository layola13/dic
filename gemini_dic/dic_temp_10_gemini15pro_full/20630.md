blacklister: /ˈblækˌlɪstər/
noun| 列入黑名单者; 将…列入黑名单者 | A person who blacklists someone or something. | The blacklister refused to comment on his actions.  将…列入黑名单者拒绝评论他的行为。|Synonym: censurer | Antonym: supporter
verb| 将…列入黑名单 | To put someone or something on a blacklist. |  The company blacklisted several suppliers for unethical practices. 这家公司将几家供应商列入黑名单，原因是它们有不道德的行为。|Synonym: ban, bar | Antonym: approve, endorse
