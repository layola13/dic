assetless:/ˈæsɛtlɪs/
adjective
无资产的；没有财产的
Having no assets; without property or resources.
He found himself assetless after the economic downturn.  他在经济衰退后发现自己一无所有。
The assetless company was forced to declare bankruptcy. 这家没有资产的公司被迫宣布破产。
synonyms: penniless, impoverished, destitute, broke
antonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
word type: adjective
