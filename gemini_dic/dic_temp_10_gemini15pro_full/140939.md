one-liner:/ˈwʌnˌlaɪnər/ | n. | 单行代码 | A one-liner is a single line of computer code that performs a specific task. |  He wrote a clever one-liner to solve the problem. 他写了一行简洁的代码就解决了这个问题。| synonyms:  short code, concise code | antonyms:  verbose code, multi-line code | noun
