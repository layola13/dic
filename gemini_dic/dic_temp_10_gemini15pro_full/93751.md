immaturity:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊrəti/ |n.|不成熟，幼稚|The immaturity of their political system is a major obstacle to development. 他们的政治体系不成熟是发展的主要障碍。|例句：His immaturity makes it difficult for him to hold down a job. 他的不成熟使他难以保住一份工作。

例句：The immaturity of the technology is the main reason for the high cost.  这项技术的不成熟是成本高的主要原因。


|近义词：childishness, puerility, naivete|反义词：maturity, sophistication, adulthood|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *immaturite, from Latin *immaturitas (“unripeness”), from immaturus (“unripe”), from in- (“not”) + maturus (“ripe”).
