houp: /hup/ | interj. | (古语，罕用) 加油；催促 | An archaic exclamation used to urge someone on, similar to "gee up" or "hup". | Example: "Houp! Houp! Pull harder, lads!" 加油！加油！用力拉，小伙子们！ | 近义词: gee up, mush, come on | 反义词: whoa, stop | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Likely imitative of a sound used to encourage horses or other animals. Its origins are obscure, and it is considered archaic and rarely used in modern English.
