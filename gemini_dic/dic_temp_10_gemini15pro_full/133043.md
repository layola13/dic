nonenvironmentally:/ˌnɑːnɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəli/
adjective
非环境的；对环境不利的
Not environmentally friendly; harmful or damaging to the environment.
The company was criticized for its nonenvironmentally sound practices.  这家公司因其不环保的做法而受到批评。
Disposing of waste nonenvironmentally can lead to pollution.  以非环保的方式处理废物会导致污染。
近义词:ecologically damaging, environmentally harmful, unsustainable
反义词:environmentally friendly, eco-friendly, sustainable
词性:adjective
词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "environmentally" (adverb derived from "environment")
