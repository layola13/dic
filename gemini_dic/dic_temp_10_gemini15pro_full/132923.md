noneloquently:/ˌnɑːnˈɛləkwəntli/
adv.
不雄辩地，缺乏口才地
lacking eloquence; in a way that is not fluent, graceful, or persuasive
He stammered noneloquently, struggling to express his feelings. 他结结巴巴地说得不太流畅，努力表达自己的感受。
近义词:inarticulately, haltingly, clumsily
反义词:eloquently, fluently, articulately
词性:副词
