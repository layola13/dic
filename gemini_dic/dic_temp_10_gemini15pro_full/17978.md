befringe:/bɪˈfrɪndʒ/
verb
饰以流苏；给…加边缘
to decorate with or as if with a fringe
The curtains were befringed with gold tassels. 窗帘上饰有金色的流苏。
近义词: fringe, edge, border, trim
反义词: strip, denude
词性: verb
