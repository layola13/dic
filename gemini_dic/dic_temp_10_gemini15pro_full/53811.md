disconformably:/ˌdɪskənˈfɔːrməbli/ | adv. |不整合地|In a manner that represents a discontinuity in the deposition of sedimentary rock, where strata are separated by an erosional surface representing a period of non-deposition.|The younger sandstone rests disconformably upon the older limestone.  较新的砂岩不整合地覆盖在较老的石灰岩上。|Synonym: unconformably|Antonym: conformably|Part of Speech: adverb
