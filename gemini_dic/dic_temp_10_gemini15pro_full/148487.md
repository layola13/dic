overstridency: /ˌōvərˈstrīdənsi/
| n. | 过分尖锐；过分刺耳；过分强硬 | The state of being excessively strident or harsh in sound or manner.  It refers to an unnecessarily forceful or aggressive way of speaking or behaving, often characterized by a loud, insistent tone. | Her overstridency in the meeting alienated several potential allies.  她在会议上过分强硬的态度疏远了一些潜在的盟友。| synonyms: shrillness, harshness, stridency, aggressiveness, vehemence | antonyms: gentleness, softness, mildness, calmness, quietness | noun
