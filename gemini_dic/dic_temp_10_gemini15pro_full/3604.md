age-set:/ˈeɪdʒ sɛt/ | n. | 年龄组，年龄集 | A group of people of similar age who share a common identity, undergo initiation rites together, and often have specific social roles and responsibilities within their community. |  The age-set system is an important part of Maasai social structure.  马赛人的年龄组制度是其社会结构的重要组成部分。


 The warriors' age-set was responsible for protecting the cattle.  武士年龄组负责保护牛群。| 近义词: age group, cohort, peer group | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "individuals" or "all ages") | 词性: noun
