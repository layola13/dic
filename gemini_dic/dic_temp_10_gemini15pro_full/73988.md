fortress:/ˈfɔːrtrəs/ (US) /ˈfɔːtrɪs/ (UK)| n. |堡垒，要塞|A fortified place, especially a large castle or other fortified building, designed to be defended from attack.|The old city walls formed a fortress against invaders. 古城墙构成了抵御侵略者的堡垒。|Synonyms: citadel, stronghold, bastion, fortification|Antonyms: vulnerability, weakness|noun

fortress:/ˈfɔːrtrəs/ (US) /ˈfɔːtrɪs/ (UK)| n. |精神堡垒，避难所 (比喻义)|Something that provides protection or support.|Her home was her fortress against the outside world. 她的家是她抵御外界的堡垒。|Synonyms: refuge, sanctuary, haven|Antonyms: exposure, vulnerability|noun
