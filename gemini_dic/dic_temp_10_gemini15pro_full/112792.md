lubricous: /ˈluːbrɪkəs/ | adj. | 光滑的；油滑的；滑溜的；不稳定的；含糊其辞的；不诚实的 | (of something) smooth and slippery; (of a person or their behaviour) appearing to be friendly and helpful but in a way that is not sincere or honest | The floor was freshly polished and rather lubricous. 地板刚擦过，很滑。 |  His lubricous charm made him popular with women. 他那油嘴滑舌的魅力让他很受女人欢迎。 | 近义词: slippery, oily, greasy, evasive, shifty | 反义词: rough, coarse, honest, straightforward | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *lubricus* "slippery," from *lubricāre* "to make smooth or slippery," from *lubricus* "smooth, slippery."
