one-piece:/ˈwʌn piːs/ (US), /wʌn piːs/ (UK)| n.|连体衣，连衣裙|A single garment combining top and bottom, such as a swimsuit or jumpsuit.|She bought a new one-piece for her vacation. 她为假期买了一件新的连体衣。|近义词：all-in-one, single-piece|反义词：two-piece|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "one" + "piece".  "One" from Old English "ān," from Proto-Germanic "*ainaz." "Piece" from Old French "piece," from Vulgar Latin "*pecia," of uncertain origin.
