bulbel:/ˈbʌlbəl/| n.| 小鳞茎，球茎|A small bulb; a bulblet.  A small rounded swelling on a plant stem, often producing a new plant. |The bulbels on the tiger lily can be used for propagation. 虎百合上的小鳞茎可以用来繁殖。|近义词：bulblet, offset|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用root根)|词性:noun
