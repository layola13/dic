deprecators:/ˈdeprɪkeɪtərz/
|n.|反对者，弃用者|People who express disapproval or deprecate something, especially a software feature or practice that is considered outdated or obsolete.|The deprecators of the old system were vocal about its flaws. 旧系统的反对者直言不讳地指出了它的缺陷。|Critics, objectors, naysayers|Supporters, advocates|noun

deprecator:/ˈdeprɪkeɪtər/
|n.|反对者，弃用者|A person who expresses disapproval of something.|The deprecator of the new policy argued it would be ineffective.  新政策的反对者认为它将无效。|Critic, objector|Supporter, proponent|noun
