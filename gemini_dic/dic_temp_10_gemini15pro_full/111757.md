lodestar: /ˈloʊdstɑːr/ (US) /ˈləʊdstɑːr/ (UK)| n.| 指导原则；北极星 |Something that serves as a guide or inspiration.|例句：The principles of democracy have always been our lodestar. 民主原则一直是我们的指导原则。

例句：Her lodestar throughout her career has been her unwavering commitment to social justice. 在她的整个职业生涯中，她对社会正义的坚定承诺一直是她的指导原则。

近义词：guide, beacon, compass, north star, polestar|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
