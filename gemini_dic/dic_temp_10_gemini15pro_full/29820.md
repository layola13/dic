caulicle: /ˈkɔːlɪkəl/
n.
胚轴；下胚轴；幼茎
The part of a plant embryo or seedling below the cotyledons, forming the first section of stem.
The caulicle emerges from the seed first, anchoring the plant and allowing the plumule to emerge. 胚轴首先从种子中出现，固定植物并允许胚芽出现。
The seedling's caulicle was a vibrant green. 幼苗的胚轴是充满活力的绿色。
近义词: hypocotyl, radicle
反义词: plumule
词性: 名词
