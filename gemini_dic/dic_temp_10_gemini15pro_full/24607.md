broiler:/ˈbrɔɪlər/| n. |肉用仔鸡；烤架|A young chicken raised specifically for meat. A broiler is typically slaughtered at a young age and is tender and flavorful.  A device used for broiling food.|例句：The farmer raises broilers for the local market.  这位农民为当地市场饲养肉鸡。

例句：He put the steaks in the broiler to cook them quickly. 他把牛排放进烤架里快速烹饪。|近义词：roaster, fryer|反义词: (无明确反义词，可考虑layer 蛋鸡) |词性:noun
