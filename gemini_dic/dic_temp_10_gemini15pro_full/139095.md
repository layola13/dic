obdiplostemonous: /ˌɒbdɪpləˈstɛmənəs/
adjective
双雄蕊的；外轮对萼雄蕊的
Having the stamens in two whorls, the outer whorl opposite the sepals.
The flower of this plant is obdiplostemonous.  这种植物的花是外轮对萼雄蕊的。
Synonym: antipetalous
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but could use terms like haplostemonous or diplostemonous depending on the specific contrasting stamen arrangement)
词性: adjective
