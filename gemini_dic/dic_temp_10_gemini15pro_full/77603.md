geeky:/ˈɡiːki/| adj.|书呆子气的，古怪的，对技术狂热的|Having or showing an excessive interest in technical things, especially computers.|He's a bit geeky, always talking about computers.|近义词：nerdy, dorky|反义词:cool, trendy|词性:形容词
词源: From geek + -y.  "Geek" originally referred to a carnival performer who performed grotesque acts. Over time, it evolved to mean a socially awkward or intellectually obsessive person, particularly in the realm of technology.
