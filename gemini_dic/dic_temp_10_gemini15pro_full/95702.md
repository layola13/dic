confused: /kənˈfjuːzd/
adjective
困惑的，混乱的
Being unable to think clearly or understand something.
Example: I was confused by the complicated instructions. 我被复杂的指令弄糊涂了。
Example: She felt confused and disoriented after the accident. 事故发生后，她感到困惑和迷失方向。
Synonym: bewildered, perplexed, puzzled
Antonym: clear, certain, understanding
