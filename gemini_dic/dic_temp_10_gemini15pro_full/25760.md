burbly:/ˈbɜːrbli/ | adj. |汩汩的，冒泡的，多泡的|Making a bubbling sound; full of bubbles. | The brook flowed burbly over the rocks. 小溪汩汩地流过岩石。| 近义词：bubbly, gurgling, effervescent | 反义词: still, quiet | 词性:形容词
Etymology: From "burble" + "-y".  "Burble" is imitative of the sound.  Its first recorded use is from the late 16th century.
