nontalker:/ˈnɑːnˌtɔːkər/ | n. |不说话的人；沉默寡言的人|A person who is habitually silent or uncommunicative. | He's a real nontalker; we barely exchanged a word all evening. 他真是个不说话的人；我们整个晚上几乎没说一句话。|Synonyms: reticent person, introvert, quiet person |Antonyms: talker, chatterbox, extrovert | noun
