The erectness of her spine gave her an air of confidence. 她挺直的脊背让她显得充满自信。|近义词：uprightness, straightness, righteousness|反义词：slouch, droop, dishonesty|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *erectnesse, derived from erect + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin ērigō (I raise up).
