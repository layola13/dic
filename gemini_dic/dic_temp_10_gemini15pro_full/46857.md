Dardanelles:/ˌdɑːrdəˈnɛlz/
proper noun
达达尼尔海峡
A narrow strait connecting the Aegean Sea with the Sea of Marmara.
The Dardanelles is a strategically important waterway. 达达尼尔海峡是一条具有战略意义的水道。
近义词:Hellespont
反义词:N/A
词性:proper noun

Dardanelles:/ˌdɑːrdəˈnɛlz/
noun
达达尼尔战役（尤指第一次世界大战中发生在此地的战役）
The Gallipoli Campaign, a costly and ultimately unsuccessful Allied campaign in World War I to capture the Ottoman capital of Constantinople.
The Dardanelles Campaign was a disaster for the Allies. 达达尼尔战役对盟军来说是一场灾难。
近义词:Gallipoli Campaign
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
