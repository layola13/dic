exfoliating:/ɛksˈfoʊliˌeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
去角质的；脱落的
removing dead skin cells from the surface of the skin
例句：She uses an exfoliating scrub twice a week. 她每周使用两次去角质磨砂膏。

例句：Exfoliating gloves can help to remove dead skin. 去角质手套可以帮助去除死皮。

近义词：abrasive, sloughing
反义词：nurturing, moisturizing
词性：adjective


exfoliate:/ɛksˈfoʊliˌeɪt/
verb
去角质；剥落
to remove dead skin cells from the surface of the skin
例句：You should exfoliate your skin regularly to keep it healthy. 你应该定期去角质，以保持皮肤健康。

例句：The bark of the tree exfoliates in thin layers.  这棵树的树皮薄薄地剥落。

近义词：shed, peel, slough
反义词：accumulate, build up
词性：verb
