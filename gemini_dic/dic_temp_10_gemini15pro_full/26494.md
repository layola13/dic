cabalistical:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪkəl/
adjective
神秘的，隐秘的，玄奥的
Relating to or characteristic of the Kabbalah (Jewish mysticism), or resembling it in being esoteric, occult, or mysterious.
例句：The old book was filled with cabalistical signs and symbols.  这本古书充满了神秘的符号和象征。

例句：He spoke in a cabalistical manner, making it difficult to understand his true intentions. 他说话的方式很神秘，让人很难理解他的真实意图。

近义词：mysterious, occult, esoteric, cryptic, arcane
反义词：clear, obvious, straightforward, transparent, exoteric
词性: adjective
