ethicists:/ˈɛθɪsɪsts/
n.
伦理学家；道德学家
A person who specializes in ethics; a moral philosopher.
Ethicists are debating the implications of this new technology. 伦理学家们正在辩论这项新技术的含义。

Ethicists from around the world gathered for the conference.  来自世界各地的伦理学家聚集参加了这次会议。

近义词:moralists, moral philosophers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) nihilists, immoralists
词性: Noun
