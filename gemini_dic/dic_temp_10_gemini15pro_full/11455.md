argument:/ˈɑːrɡjʊmənt/ | n. | 争论，辩论；论点，论据 | A reason or set of reasons given with the aim of persuading others that an action or idea is right or wrong. |  She presented strong arguments in favor of the proposal. 她提出了支持这项提案的有力论据。

argument:/ˈɑːrɡjʊmənt/ | n. | 争吵，口角 | An angry disagreement. |  They had a heated argument about money. 他们就钱的问题发生了激烈的争吵。

argument:/ˈɑːrɡjʊmənt/ | n. | (数学、计算机) 参数 | A value that is passed to a function or subroutine. | The function takes two arguments: a string and an integer.  这个函数接受两个参数：一个字符串和一个整数。
