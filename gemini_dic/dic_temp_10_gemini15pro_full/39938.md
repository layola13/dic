constructed:/kənˈstrʌktɪd/ /kənˈstrʌktɪd/ | adj. | 建造的，构成的，人为的 |  built or created, often with careful planning or arrangement | The bridge was constructed of steel and concrete.  这座桥由钢筋混凝土建造而成。 | built, fabricated, erected | natural, organic | adjective

constructed:/kənˈstrʌktɪd/ /kənˈstrʌktɪd/ | v. |建造，构成，创立（construct的过去分词） | past participle of construct, meaning to build or create something | The theory was constructed from a series of experiments.  该理论是从一系列实验中构建出来的。| built, created, established | destroyed, demolished | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *constructus*, past participle of *construere*  "to heap up, pile together, build, construct," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *struere* "to pile up" (see structure).
