chasmiest:/ˈtʃæzmiɪst/ | adj. | 最深不可测的，最深奥的 |  Superlative form of "chasmy," meaning resembling a chasm or abyss; deeply mysterious or profound. |  His chasmiest thoughts were kept hidden in a journal. 他最深奥的思想都藏在一本日记里。 |  The chasmiest parts of the ocean remain unexplored. 海洋中最深不可测的部分仍未被探索。 | 近义词: profoundest, deepest, most abysmal, most unfathomable | 反义词: shallowest, most superficial | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "chasm" (from Greek "khasma" meaning "gaping hollow, gulf") + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix).
