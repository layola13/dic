deterring:/dɪˈtɜːrɪŋ/
adjective|威慑的，制止的，阻挠的|Discouraging someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.|The high security cameras are deterring shoplifters. 高安全摄像头正在威慑商店扒窃者。|近义词: discouraging, dissuading, inhibiting, preventing|反义词: encouraging, inciting, promoting|词性: adjective
词源: From the verb "deter," which comes from Late Latin "deterrere"  meaning "to frighten from."  Ultimately from Latin "de-" (from, away) + "terrere" (to frighten).
