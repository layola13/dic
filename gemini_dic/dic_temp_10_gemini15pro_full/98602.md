instructing:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪŋ/
verb
指示; 教导; 命令
present participle of instruct
例句：He is instructing the students in chemistry. 他正在指导学生学习化学。
例句：She was instructing them on how to use the new software.  她正在指导他们如何使用新软件。

近义词：teaching, training, directing, guiding, ordering, commanding
反义词：learning, following, obeying
词性：v.
