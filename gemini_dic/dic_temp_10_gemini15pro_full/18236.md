belgravian: /bɛlˈɡreɪviən/ | adj. n.|贝尔格莱维亚的；贝尔格莱维亚居民|Relating to or characteristic of Belgravia, a fashionable residential district in central London.|She lives in a belgravian mansion. 她住在一座贝尔格莱维亚的豪宅里。|近义词：posh, upscale, affluent|反义词：poor, working-class|词性：形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From Belgrave Square, the central feature of the district, named after Viscount Belgrave, later the 1st Marquess of Westminster, who developed the area. The area acquired its name in the early 19th century.
