capable-of:/ˈkeɪpəbl ɒv/ | adj. | 有能力的，能够的 |  Indicates that someone or something has the ability, skill, or potential to do something. It's always followed by a gerund (verb ending in -ing) or a noun phrase. | This software is capable of processing large amounts of data.  这个软件能够处理大量的数据。|近义词：able to, competent, qualified |反义词：incapable of, unable to| 词性: 形容词
