blacktopping:/ˈblækˌtɑːpɪŋ/| n.|铺设柏油路面；柏油路面|The blacktopping of the road was completed last week. 这条路的柏油路面铺设上周完成了。|English Explanation: The act or process of covering a surface with asphalt or a similar material.  It can also refer to the asphalt surface itself.|Example 1: The crew was blacktopping the driveway.  施工队正在铺设车道的柏油路面。

Example 2: The new blacktopping made the road much smoother. 新的柏油路面使道路更加平坦。|Synonym: asphalt paving, surfacing|Antonym: unpaved, dirt road|noun
