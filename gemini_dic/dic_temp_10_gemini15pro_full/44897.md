cryolite:/ˈkraɪəˌlaɪt/ | n. |冰晶石|A white or colorless mineral consisting of a fluoride of sodium and aluminum, used as a flux in the electrolytic production of aluminum.|Cryolite is essential for the production of aluminum. 冰晶石是生产铝的必需品。|Cryolite is mined in Greenland. 冰晶石在格陵兰开采。|Synonyms: Greenland spar, sodium aluminum fluoride|Antonyms: N/A|noun
