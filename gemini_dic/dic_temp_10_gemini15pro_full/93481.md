illustrations:/ˌɪləˈstreɪʃənz/ | n. | 插图；例证；说明 | Visual examples used to explain or decorate something. | The book contains many beautiful illustrations. 这本书包含许多美丽的插图。|  The illustrations in the children's book brought the story to life. 儿童读物中的插图使故事栩栩如生。 | synonyms: pictures, images, diagrams, examples | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of "obscurations") | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *illustracioun*, from Late Latin *illūstrātiō*, from Latin *illūstrāre* "to light up, brighten, make clear," from *in-* "in, on" + *lūstrāre* "to purify, make bright," from *lūster* "light, brightness."
