davidic:/dəˈvɪdɪk/| adj.|大卫的；大卫王朝的；像大卫的|Of or relating to David, the second king of Israel, or his dynasty.|例句：The davidic line continued through many generations. 大卫的 lineage 延续了许多代。

例句：The Psalms are often referred to as davidic, though not all were written by him.  诗篇常被称为大卫的，尽管并非所有都是他写的。|近义词：kingly, royal|反义词:|adjective
