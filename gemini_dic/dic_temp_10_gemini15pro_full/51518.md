detrital:/dɪˈtraɪtəl/| adj.|碎屑的，由碎屑组成的|Formed from or consisting of detritus; fragmental.|The geologist examined the detrital rocks for signs of ancient life. 地质学家检查了碎屑岩，寻找古代生命的迹象。|Synonyms: fragmental, clastic|Antonyms: non-clastic, crystalline|adjective

detritus:/dɪˈtraɪtəs/| n.|碎屑，残渣|Waste or debris of any kind; organic matter produced by the decomposition of organisms.|The forest floor was covered in detritus. 森林地面覆盖着碎屑。|Synonyms: debris, waste, remains|Antonyms:  n/a (no direct antonym)|noun
