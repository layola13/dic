ancillary:/ˈænsɪləri/ /ænˈsɪləri/| adj. n.|辅助的，附属的；辅助人员，附属设备|Providing necessary support to the primary activities or operation of an organization, institution, industry, or system.  |The company's ancillary businesses include catering and transportation.  这家公司的辅助业务包括餐饮和交通运输。|Synonyms: auxiliary, supplementary, subordinate|Antonyms: primary, principal, main|adjective

ancillary:/ˈænsɪləri/ /ænˈsɪləri/| n.|辅助人员，附属设备|A person or thing providing necessary support to the primary activities or operation of an organization, institution, industry, or system. |The hospital has a range of ancillaries, including a pharmacy and a cafe.  这家医院有一系列附属设施，包括药房和咖啡馆。|Synonyms: auxiliary, supplement, adjunct|Antonyms: primary, principal, main|noun
