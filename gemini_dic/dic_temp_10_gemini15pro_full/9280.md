antiphilosophically:/ˌæntiˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkli/
adverb
反哲学地；非哲学地
In a manner opposed to or rejecting philosophy.  Rejecting or opposing the methods or principles of philosophy.
He antiphilosophically dismissed the question as irrelevant. 他反哲学地认为这个问题无关紧要。
She approached the issue antiphilosophically, preferring practical solutions over abstract theories.  她以非哲学的方式处理这个问题，更喜欢实际的解决方案而不是抽象的理论。
近义词: unphilosophically, non-philosophically
反义词: philosophically
