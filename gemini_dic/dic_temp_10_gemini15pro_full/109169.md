legitimatist:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmətɪst/
n.|正统主义者；合法主义者|A person who supports a legitimate ruler, especially a monarch, or believes in the principle of hereditary right.|The aging legitimatist refused to acknowledge the new republic.  这位年迈的正统主义者拒绝承认新共和国。|
legitimatist:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmətɪst/
adj.|正统的；合法主义的|Supporting a legitimate ruler, especially a monarch, or believing in the principle of hereditary right.|He held a legitimatist view of the monarchy. 他持有君主制正统的观点。|
近义词:royalist, monarchist|
反义词:republican, revolutionary|
词性:noun, adjective|
词源:From French *légitimiste*, from *légitime* (“legitimate”), from Latin *legitimus*.|
