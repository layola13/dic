acetimetry: /ˌæsɪˈtɪmətri/
noun
乙酰测定法
The measurement of the concentration of acetate in a solution.
Acetimetry is used in clinical chemistry to determine the level of acetate in blood or urine samples. = 乙酰测定法用于临床化学中测定血液或尿液样本中乙酸盐的水平。
Acetimetry can also be used in environmental monitoring to measure acetate levels in water or soil samples. = 乙酰测定法也可用于环境监测中测量水或土壤样本中的乙酸盐水平。
