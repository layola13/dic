burdener's:/ˈbɜːrdənərz/
n.
施加负担者;加重负担者
One who burdens another; someone who imposes a hardship or load.
The burdener's actions left the family in financial ruin.  施加负担者的行为使这个家庭陷入了经济困境。
The burdener's constant complaints made everyone uncomfortable.  不断抱怨的施加负担者让每个人都不舒服。
近义词: oppressor, imposer
反义词: helper, supporter
词性: 名词 (possessive form)

词源:
From "burden" (n.) + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns) + "-'s" (possessive suffix).  "Burden" comes from Old French "burdein", ultimately from Germanic origin.
