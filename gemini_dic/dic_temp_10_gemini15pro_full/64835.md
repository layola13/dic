esperance:/ˈɛspərəns/| n.|希望，期望|A feeling of hope or expectation.|例句：They looked to the future with esperance. 他们满怀希望地展望未来。

例句：The small town held onto the esperance of finding the missing child.  小镇紧紧抱着找到失踪孩子的希望。|近义词：hope, expectation, optimism, anticipation|反义词：despair, hopelessness, pessimism|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *esperance, from Latin spērāns, present participle of spērāre (“to hope”).
