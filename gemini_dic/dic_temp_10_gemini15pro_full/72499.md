fluoride:/ˈflʊəraɪd/ /ˈflɔːraɪd/| n.|氟化物|A binary compound of fluorine with another element or radical.  A salt of hydrofluoric acid.|Fluoride is added to toothpaste to help prevent cavities.  在牙膏中添加氟化物有助于防止蛀牙。|Synonyms:  fluorinide|Antonyms: None|noun
