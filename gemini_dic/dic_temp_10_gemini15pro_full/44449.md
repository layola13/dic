cross-springer:/ˈkrɒsˌsprɪŋər/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌsprɪŋə(r)/ (UK)| n.|交叉弹簧，交叉跳跃者|A type of leaf spring used in vehicle suspensions, characterized by having the leaves arranged in a crisscross pattern.  It can also refer to a person or animal that jumps or leaps in a crosswise or diagonal direction. |The carriage used cross-springers for a smoother ride. 这辆马车使用交叉弹簧以获得更平稳的行驶。|The impala is a graceful cross-springer, leaping across the savanna. 黑斑羚是一种优雅的交叉跳跃者，在热带草原上跳跃。|Synonym: (for spring) transverse spring, elliptic spring|Antonym: (for spring) coil spring|noun
