malingered:/məˈlɪŋɡərd/
| v. | 装病；逃避责任 | to pretend to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty | He malingered for weeks, hoping to avoid military service. 他装病数周，希望逃避兵役。| Synonyms: feign illness, shirk, dodge, fake | Antonyms: work diligently, fulfill obligations | verb

malingering:/məˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/
| n. | 装病；逃避责任（名词形式） | the act of pretending to be ill or incapacitated in order to avoid work or duty |  The doctor suspected malingering when the patient's symptoms conveniently disappeared on weekends. 当病人的症状在周末神奇地消失时，医生怀疑他在装病。| Synonyms: feigning illness, shirking, dodging, faking | Antonyms: diligence, dedication | noun
