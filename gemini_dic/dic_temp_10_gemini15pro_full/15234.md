backfire:/ˈbækˌfaɪər/ (US) /ˈbækˌfaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|适得其反；逆火| (of a plan or action) have an unintended and unwelcome result that is the opposite of what was intended; (of an internal combustion engine) undergo a mistimed explosion of fuel in the cylinder or exhaust.|

例句：
His plan to get her attention backfired when she started dating his best friend.
他想要引起她注意的计划适得其反，她开始和他最好的朋友约会。

Her attempt to help actually backfired and made the situation worse.
她试图帮忙，结果却适得其反，使情况变得更糟。

The car backfired loudly as it went down the street.
汽车在街上行驶时发出巨大的逆火声。

The engine backfired several times before it finally stalled.
发动机逆火了几次，最后熄火了。
