martyred:/ˈmɑːrtərd/ | adj. | 殉难的，殉道的；（为事业等）牺牲的；受折磨的 | Having been killed because of one's religious or other beliefs; having suffered greatly for a cause. |  He was seen as a martyred hero. 他被视为一位殉道的英雄。 | synonyms: sacrificed, immolated, slain, victimized | antonyms: surviving, living, thriving | adjective

martyred:/ˈmɑːrtərd/ | n. | 烈士；殉道者 | A person who is killed because of their religious or other beliefs. |  The church remembers its martyrs. 教会纪念它的殉道者。| synonyms: martyr, victim | antonyms: survivor | noun
