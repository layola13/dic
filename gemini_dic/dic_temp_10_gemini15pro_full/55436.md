distinctive:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪv/| adj.|独特的，有特色的|Having a quality or characteristic that makes something easy to recognize and different from other things.|Her distinctive voice is easily recognizable. 她独特的声音很容易辨认。|近义词：characteristic, unique, individual, peculiar|反义词：common, ordinary, typical, usual|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *distinctīvus, from Latin distinct- (past participle stem of distinguere ‘distinguish’) + -īvus.
