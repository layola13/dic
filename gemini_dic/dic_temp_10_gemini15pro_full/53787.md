discomposedly:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzɪdli/
adv.
不安地，慌张地
In a manner that shows one is upset, disturbed, or agitated.
She rose discomposedly and left the room. 她慌张地站起来离开了房间。
Near Antonyms: calmly, serenely, peacefully, tranquilly
Antonyms: composedly

词源:
From the verb "discompose," meaning to disturb the composure of; ruffle; perturb.  "Discompose" is formed from "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") and "compose" (meaning to form by putting together). The "-ly" suffix turns the verb into an adverb.
