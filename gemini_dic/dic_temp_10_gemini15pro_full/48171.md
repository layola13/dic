decompile:/ˌdiːkəmˈpaɪl/
verb
反编译
To convert (a program) from machine language to a higher-level language, such as assembly language.
The program was decompiled to analyze its functionality.  这个程序被反编译以分析其功能。
The hacker decompiled the software to understand its vulnerabilities.  黑客反编译了软件以了解其漏洞。
synonyms: disassemble, reverse engineer
antonyms: compile
词性: 动词
