outranked:/ˌaʊtˈræŋkt/| v. | 超越，级别高于 | To have a higher rank or position than someone or something. |  My team outranked all the others in the competition. 我的团队在比赛中超过了所有其他团队。

|  The new manager outranked everyone else in the department. 新经理的级别比部门里的其他任何人都高。 | 近义词：surpassed, exceeded, outstripped, eclipsed | 反义词：subordinate to, inferior to | verb (past tense)
