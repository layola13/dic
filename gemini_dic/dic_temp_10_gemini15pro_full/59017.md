dvt:/ˌdiː viː ˈtiː/
n.
deep vein thrombosis (深静脉血栓形成)
A blood clot that forms in a deep vein, usually in the leg.
例句：The long flight increased her risk of DVT. 长途飞行增加了她患深静脉血栓形成的风险。
例句：He was diagnosed with DVT after experiencing pain and swelling in his leg. 他腿部疼痛肿胀后被诊断为深静脉血栓形成。
近义词：thrombosis, blood clot
