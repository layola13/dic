maffioso:/ˌmæfiˈoʊsoʊ/ (US), /ˌmæfiˈəʊzəʊ/ (UK)| n.|黑手党成员|A member of the Mafia, especially one of high rank. |The police were convinced the restaurant owner was a maffioso, though they couldn't prove it. 警方确信这家餐馆老板是黑手党成员，尽管他们无法证明。|Synonyms: gangster, mobster, racketeer, Mafioso|Antonyms: lawman, police officer|noun

mafiosi:/ˌmæfiˈoʊsi/ (US), /ˌmæfiˈəʊzi/ (UK)| n.|黑手党成员（复数）|The plural form of "maffioso", referring to multiple members of the Mafia. |Several mafiosi were arrested in the early morning raid.  几次清晨的突袭逮捕了数名黑手党成员。|Synonyms: gangsters, mobsters, racketeers, Mafiosi|Antonyms: lawmen, police officers|noun
