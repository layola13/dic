coils:/kɔɪlz/| n. |线圈; 盘绕; (头发的)卷儿; |A coil is a length of something such as rope or wire arranged into a series of circles, one above or inside the other.  |The snake coiled tightly around its prey. 蛇紧紧地盘绕在猎物身上。|近义词：loops, spirals, rings, curls|反义词:straighten|名词

coil:/kɔɪl/| v. |盘绕; 卷成圈状; |If something coils or if you coil it, it forms a series of circles, one above or inside the other.  |The rope coiled on the deck. 绳子盘绕在甲板上。|近义词：wind, twist, twine, spiral|反义词:uncoil, unwind|动词
