downturns:/ˈdaʊntɜːrnz/
noun
衰退；低迷时期
a decline in economic, business, or other activity
Example: The company experienced several downturns in the last decade.  这家公司在过去十年经历了几次衰退。

Example:  The recent economic downturn has affected many businesses. 最近的经济衰退影响了许多企业。

synonym: decline, slump, recession, downturn
antonym: upturn, boom, surge, recovery
n.
