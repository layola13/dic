dispersion:/dɪˈspɜːrʒən/ | n. | 分散; 散布; 弥散; |the action or process of distributing things or people over a wide area.|The dispersion of seeds is crucial for plant reproduction.  种子的散布对植物繁殖至关重要。| synonyms: distribution, scattering, dissemination | antonyms: concentration, gathering, accumulation | noun

dispersion:/dɪˈspɜːrʒən/ | n. | (统计)离散; 差量; 色散 |the extent to which values in a distribution vary from the average or mean.|The dispersion of test scores was quite wide. 考试分数的离散度相当大。| synonyms: variation, deviation, spread | antonyms: uniformity, consistency | noun
