clinicians:/klɪˈnɪʃənz/
|n.|临床医生；临床工作者|Medical professionals who work directly with patients, providing diagnosis, treatment, and care.|The clinicians at the hospital worked tirelessly to treat the patients during the pandemic.  医院的临床医生们在大流行期间不知疲倦地治疗病人。|Synonyms: doctors, physicians, practitioners, therapists|Antonyms: researchers, administrators|noun
