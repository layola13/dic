axial:/ˈæksiəl/| adj.|轴的，轴向的|Of, relating to, or situated around an axis. |The Earth's axial tilt is responsible for the seasons. 地球的自转轴倾斜是造成四季的原因。|The axial skeleton includes the skull, vertebral column, and rib cage.  中轴骨骼包括头骨、脊柱和肋骨。|近义词：longitudinal, central|反义词:peripheral, radial|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *axialis, from Latin axis (“axle, axis”) + -alis.
