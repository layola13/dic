covey:/ˈkʌvi/| n.|一群（尤指鸟类，也指人）；小海湾；隐蔽处|A small flock of birds, especially partridges or grouse. Also, a small group of people.|例句：A covey of quail burst from the undergrowth. 一群鹌鹑从灌木丛中飞出。

例句：The CEO addressed the covey of executives. 首席执行官向这群高管发表了讲话。|近义词：group, flock, bevy, cluster|反义词: individual|词性: 名词
