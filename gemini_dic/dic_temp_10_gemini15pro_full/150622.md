papilla:/pəˈpɪlə/ (US), /pəˈpɪlə/ (UK)| n. |乳头，乳突；味蕾|A small, nipple-like projection or elevation on a surface, such as the tongue or skin. It can refer to taste buds, optic discs, or other small, rounded protuberances.|The papillae on my tongue are swollen. 我的舌头上的味蕾肿了。|Synonyms: protuberance, projection, nipple, mound|Antonyms: depression, cavity|Noun

papilla:/pəˈpɪlə/ (US), /pəˈpɪlə/ (UK)| n. |(植物)毛状体|A small, nipple-shaped projection on a plant, often involved in secretion or absorption. |The papillae on the flower petals give them a velvety texture. 花瓣上的毛状体使它们具有天鹅绒般的质感。|Synonyms: trichome, hair|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
