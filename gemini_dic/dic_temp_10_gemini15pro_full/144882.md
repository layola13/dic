over-riding:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 最重要的；首要的；高于一切的 |  Having ultimate power or importance; taking precedence over all others. | The over-riding factor in her decision was the safety of her children. 她做决定时最重要的因素是她孩子的安全。|  The over-riding concern was the health of the patients. 最重要的担忧是病人的健康。| paramount, dominant, principal, chief, primary | secondary, subordinate | adjective

over-ride:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪd/ | vt. | 推翻；否决；凌驾 | To use one's authority to reject or cancel (a decision, view, etc.); to prevail over. | The president overrode the committee's decision. 总统否决了委员会的决定。|  Safety concerns overrode all other considerations.  安全问题凌驾于所有其他考虑之上。| overturn, cancel, reverse, veto, nullify | uphold, support, confirm | verb
