dulciloquy: /ˈdʌlsɪˌloʊkwi/ /ˈdʌlsɪləkwi/
n.
甜言蜜语；温柔的谈话
sweet speech or song
例句：The lovers engaged in dulciloquy under the starry night. 恋人们在星空下说着甜言蜜语。

例句：Her dulciloquy soothed the crying child. 她温柔的话语安抚了哭闹的孩子。

近义词：sweet talk, soft words, endearments, caress, blandishment
反义词：harsh words, reprimand, criticism, diatribe
词性：名词
