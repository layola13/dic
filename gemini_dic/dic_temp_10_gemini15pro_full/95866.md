incorruptible:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptəbl/
adjective
不腐败的；廉洁的；不能收买的
not susceptible to corruption, especially bribery; not subject to decay or decomposition.
The judge was known for his incorruptible nature, refusing all bribes.  这位法官以其廉洁的本性而闻名，拒绝一切贿赂。
The saint's incorruptible body was a source of wonder for believers.  这位圣徒不朽的遗体是信徒们惊叹的源泉。
incorrupt, honest, upright, principled
corrupt, corruptible, dishonest, venal
