madeleine:/ˌmædˈleɪn/ | n. |玛德琳蛋糕; 贝壳状的小蛋糕 | A small rich sponge cake baked in a shell-shaped mold. |

例句：
The aroma of freshly baked madeleines filled the kitchen.
厨房里充满了新鲜出炉的玛德琳蛋糕的香味。

She dipped the madeleine in her tea and took a bite.
她把玛德琳蛋糕浸在茶里，咬了一口。


近义词：sponge cake, petit four |
反义词: None (It's a specific type of cake, not a concept with an opposite.) |
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *madeleine*, named after Madeleine Paulmier, a 19th-century French cook who is said to have popularized the cake.
