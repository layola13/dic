indoxyl: /ɪnˈdɒksɪl/ (UK) /ɪnˈdɑːksɪl/ (US) | n. | 吲哚酚，羟吲哚 | A nitrogenous substance, C₈H₇NO, isomeric with oxindol, from which indigo is derived by oxidation.  It is found in the urine of herbivores, especially horses, as indoxyl sulfuric acid, or indican, and is readily converted into indigo blue. | Indoxyl is an important precursor in the synthesis of indigo dye. 吲哚酚是合成靛蓝染料的重要前体。|  Indoxyl can be detected in urine samples.  吲哚酚可以在尿液样本中检测到。| Synonym: 3-hydroxyindole | Antonym: N/A | noun
