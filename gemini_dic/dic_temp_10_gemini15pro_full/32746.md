chloroplastid: /ˈklɔːrəˌplæstɪd/ | n. | 叶绿体 |A plastid that contains chlorophyll and in which photosynthesis takes place.  It is found in the cells of green plants and algae. | Example 1: Chloroplastids are essential for plant growth because they convert light energy into chemical energy. 叶绿体对植物生长至关重要，因为它们将光能转化为化学能。

Example 2: The chloroplastids in the leaves of the plant absorb sunlight for photosynthesis. 植物叶子中的叶绿体吸收阳光进行光合作用。 | Synonym: chloroplast | Antonym: N/A | noun
