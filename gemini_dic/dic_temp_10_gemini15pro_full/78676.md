giftedness:/ˈɡɪftɪdnəs/
n.|天赋；禀赋|The state of being gifted; exceptional talent or natural ability.|She showed remarkable giftedness in music from a young age. 她从小就展现出非凡的音乐天赋。|talent, aptitude, genius, faculty,  brilliance|lack of talent, mediocrity, inability
etymology: From *gifted* + *-ness*. *Gifted* comes from the past participle of *gift*, "to endow with a gift," from *gift* (n.), from Old Norse *gift* "gift, dowry."  *-ness* is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
