batfowler:/ˈbætˌfaʊlər/| n.|捕蝙蝠的人；捕鸟的人|A person who hunts or catches bats.  This term can also refer more generally to someone who hunts birds.|例句：The old batfowler knew the caves like the back of his hand.  这位老捕蝙蝠人对这些洞穴非常熟悉。

例句：In the past, batfowlers would sometimes use nets to catch bats.  过去，捕蝙蝠的人有时会用网来捕捉蝙蝠。|近义词：birdcatcher, fowler|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps conservationist or protector)|词性:noun
