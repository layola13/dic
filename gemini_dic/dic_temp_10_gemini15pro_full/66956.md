experientialism:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəlɪzəm/
n.
经验主义;经验论
A philosophy that emphasizes the role of experience in acquiring knowledge.  It asserts that knowledge is primarily or solely derived from sensory experience.
例句：Experientialism is central to many educational theories. 经验主义是许多教育理论的核心。

例句：Her teaching methods reflect a strong belief in experientialism. 她的教学方法反映了她对经验主义的坚定信念。

近义词：empiricism, pragmatism
反义词：rationalism, idealism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From experience + -ial + -ism.  "Experience" comes from the Latin "experientia" meaning "trial, proof, experiment," from "experiri" meaning "to try, test."
