nonreceptivity:/ˌnɒnrɪˌsepˈtɪvɪti/ (US) /ˌnɒnrɪˌsepˈtɪvəti/ (UK)| n. |无接受性；不接受；不接纳|The state of being nonreceptive; an unwillingness or inability to receive or accept something.|His nonreceptivity to new ideas frustrated his colleagues. 他对新想法的不接受让他的同事们很沮丧。|近义词：resistance, unwillingness, closed-mindedness, hostility, indifference|反义词：receptivity, openness, willingness, acceptance|词性:名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + receptive + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)  From Latin *receptīvus*, from recipiō (“I receive, accept, admit, take back, recover”).
