buckram:/ˈbʌkrəm/ | n. |粗硬棉布，粗硬亚麻布|A stiff-finished cotton or linen fabric, typically heavily sized with glue, used for bookbindings, interlinings, and stiffening garments.|The lawyer wore a suit of dark buckram.  这位律师穿着一套深色的粗硬棉布西装。| The curtains were made of heavy buckram. 窗帘是用厚重的粗硬棉布做的。|近义词: stiff cloth, canvas, linen |反义词: silk, satin |词性: noun


buckrams:/ˈbʌkrəmz/ | n. |粗硬棉布（复数）|Plural form of buckram, referring to multiple pieces or types of this stiff fabric. |  He purchased several buckrams for his bookbinding projects. 他为他的装订项目购买了几种粗硬棉布。| The buckrams used in these hats provide excellent shape retention.  这些帽子中使用的粗硬棉布能很好地保持形状。 |近义词: stiff cloths, canvases, linens |反义词: silks, satins |词性: noun
