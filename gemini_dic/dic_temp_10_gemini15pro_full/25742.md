buoys:/bɔɪz/ | n. | 浮标，航标 | An anchored float serving as a navigation mark, to show reefs or other hazards, or for mooring. | The buoys marked the channel into the harbor.  浮标标出了进入港口的航道。| synonyms: float, marker, beacon | antonyms: anchor, sinker | noun

buoy:/bɔɪ/ | v. | 使浮起，支持，鼓励 | To keep afloat or aloft; support:  | The life jacket buoyed him up in the water. 救生衣使他浮在水面上。|The good news buoyed their spirits.  好消息鼓舞了他们的精神。| synonyms: uplift, encourage, sustain, float | antonyms: depress, discourage, sink | verb
