contemper:/kənˈtɛmpər/ | v. | 调和；使缓和；使温和 | To moderate or mitigate; to temper. |  The king contempered his anger with mercy. 国王以仁慈缓和了他的愤怒。

The judge contempered justice with mercy. 法官以慈悲缓和了正义。| 近义词: moderate, mitigate, temper, allay, alleviate, assuage | 反义词: aggravate, exacerbate, intensify | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *contemperare*, from *com-* (together) + *temperare* (to mix, temper).
