glucometer:/ˌɡluːˈkɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌɡluːˈkɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |血糖仪|A device used for measuring the concentration of glucose in the blood, typically by using a small drop of blood obtained by pricking a finger.|Example:  I use a glucometer to check my blood sugar levels regularly. 我使用血糖仪定期检查我的血糖水平。

Example:  The glucometer gave a reading of 120 mg/dL. 血糖仪显示读数为120 毫克/分升。|Synonym: blood glucose meter, glucose monitor|Antonym: N/A|noun
