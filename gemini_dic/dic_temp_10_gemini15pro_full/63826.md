epidurals: /ˌɛpɪˈdʊrəlz/ | n. | 硬膜外麻醉；硬膜外注射 | An injection of anesthetic into the space around the spinal cord to numb the lower part of the body, often used during childbirth. |  She had an epidural during labor. 她分娩时进行了硬膜外麻醉。|  spinal anesthesia, spinal block | general anesthesia | noun

epidural: /ˌɛpɪˈdʊrəl/ | adj. | 硬膜外的 | Relating to the outer covering of the spinal cord. | The epidural space is located outside the dura mater. 硬膜外腔位于硬脑膜外。 | spinal |  | adjective
