elided:/ɪˈlaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 省略的，删节的 | Omitted or suppressed.  Derived from the verb "elide," meaning to omit (a sound or syllable when speaking) or to suppress or pass over (something, such as a fact or event). | The elided passage in the book made the story confusing.  这本书中省略的段落使故事令人困惑。 | 近义词: omitted, deleted, suppressed, expunged | 反义词: included, added, inserted | 词性: adjective
