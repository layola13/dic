grumbled:/ˈɡrʌmbəld/ (US) /ˈɡrʌmbld/ (UK)| v. |抱怨，嘟囔，发牢骚|To complain or protest in a bad-tempered but quiet way.| He grumbled about the cold weather. 他抱怨天气冷。|近义词：complained, muttered, grumbled, moaned, whined|反义词：praised, complimented, approved, rejoiced|verb

grumble:/ˈɡrʌmbəl/ (US) /ˈɡrʌmbl/ (UK)| n. |抱怨，牢骚|A complaint uttered in a low, indistinct tone.| There were grumbles about the new working hours.  对于新的工作时间，大家怨声载道。|近义词：complaint, murmur, protest|反义词：praise, compliment, approval|noun
