habitational:/ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
居住的；适于居住的
Suitable for living in; used for living in.
The building was deemed habitational by the city inspector. 这栋建筑被市政检查员认为适于居住。
residential, inhabitable, livable
uninhabitable, non-residential
adjective
