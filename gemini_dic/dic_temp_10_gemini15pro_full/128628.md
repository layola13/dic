nickeling:/ˈnɪkəlɪŋ/| n. |少量收费；斤斤计较；积少成多|The act of charging or paying small amounts of money, often perceived as excessive or petty.  It can also refer to the process of accumulating a significant sum through small, repeated transactions.|He made his fortune by nickeling and diming his customers. 他靠对顾客斤斤计较积攒了财富。|Synonyms: penny-pinching, chiseling, haggling|Antonyms: generosity, extravagance|noun


nickeling:/ˈnɪkəlɪŋ/| v. |少量收费；斤斤计较|To charge or pay small amounts of money, often perceived as excessive or petty.|The airline started nickeling and diming passengers for checked bags. 这家航空公司开始对乘客托运行李收取小额费用。|Synonyms:  penny-pinch, chisel, haggle|Antonyms: splurge, be generous|verb
