contender:/kənˈtendər/| n.|竞争者，角逐者|Someone who competes with others for something, especially a championship or high position.|例句：He is a strong contender for the championship. 他是冠军的有力竞争者。

例句：She emerged as a leading contender in the presidential race.  她在总统竞选中脱颖而出，成为主要竞争者。|近义词：rival, competitor, candidate, aspirant|反义词:incumbent, nonparticipant|词性:noun

contend:/kənˈtend/| v.|竞争，争夺；声称，主张|1. To compete with others for something. 2. To assert something as a position in an argument.|例句：Several teams are contending for the championship.  几支队伍正在争夺冠军。

例句：He contends that the theory is flawed. 他声称这个理论是有缺陷的。|近义词：compete, vie, argue, assert|反义词:yield, surrender|词性:verb
