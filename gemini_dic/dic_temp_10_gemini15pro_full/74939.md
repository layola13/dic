freuder:/ˈfrɔɪdər/| n.| 佛洛伊德主义者; 精神分析学家|A follower of Sigmund Freud's theories; a psychoanalyst.|例句：He considered himself a freuder, deeply interested in the unconscious mind. 他自认为是弗洛伊德主义者，对潜意识非常感兴趣。

例句：The freuder interpreted her dreams as manifestations of repressed desires.  这位精神分析学家将她梦境解读为被压抑欲望的体现。

|近义词：psychoanalyst, Freudian|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider someone with opposing psychological theories) |词性: noun
