diagenesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒenɪsɪs/
n.
成岩作用
The physical and chemical changes occurring during the conversion of sediment to sedimentary rock.
Diagenesis transforms loose sediments into hard rocks. 成岩作用将松散的沉积物转化为坚硬的岩石。
The fossils were well preserved despite diagenesis. 尽管经历了成岩作用，这些化石仍然保存完好。
