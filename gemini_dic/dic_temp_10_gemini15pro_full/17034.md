batfowling:/ˈbætˌfaʊlɪŋ/
noun
蝙蝠捕鸟; 夜间捕鸟
The act of catching birds at night using nets or other devices.
例句：Batfowling was a popular pastime in some rural areas.  夜间捕鸟在一些农村地区曾是一种流行的消遣。

例句：He went batfowling with his friends last night. 他昨晚和朋友们一起去夜间捕鸟了。

近义词：birding, bird-catching
反义词: bird-releasing
