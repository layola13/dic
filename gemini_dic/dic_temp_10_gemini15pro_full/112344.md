lorica:/ləˈraɪkə/
noun
胸甲；护胸甲；（某些原生动物的）壳
A protective case or shell, as of a rotifer or an insect, or a cuirass of leather or metal.
The Roman soldier wore a lorica to protect his chest in battle.  罗马士兵穿胸甲在战斗中保护胸部。

lorica:/ləˈraɪkə/
noun
(某些原生动物的)壳
A protective outer covering of certain microorganisms.
The lorica of the paramecium provides structural support. 草履虫的壳提供结构支撑。


Synonym: armor, cuirass, shell, carapace
Antonym: vulnerability

Etymology:  From Latin *lōrīca* (“corselet, cuirass”), from *lōrum* (“leather strap”).
