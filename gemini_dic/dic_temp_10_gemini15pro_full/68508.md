fanatically:/fəˈnætɪkli/
adverb|狂热地，极端地|in a way that shows extreme and often unreasonable enthusiasm|He works fanatically, often pulling all-nighters. 他工作狂热，经常熬通宵。|
例句：
She fanatically collects antique dolls. 她狂热地收集古董娃娃。

例句：
He followed the football team fanatically. 他狂热地追随那支足球队。

近义词：obsessively, zealously, fervently, passionately
反义词：moderately, indifferently, apathetically
词性: 副词

词源:  From fanatic + -ally.  Fanatic comes from the Latin fanaticus "inspired by a divinity, frenzied, enthusiastic," from fanum "temple."
