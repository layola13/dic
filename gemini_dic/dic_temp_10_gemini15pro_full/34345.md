class-conscious:/ˈklæs ˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK) /ˈklæs ˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| adj.|阶级意识强的，具有阶级意识的|Aware of one's social class and the differences between social classes, often in a way that influences their behavior and attitudes.|例句：The party attracted many class-conscious voters.  这个党吸引了许多具有阶级意识的选民。

例句：Her choice of clothing reflected her class-conscious upbringing. 她的服装选择反映了她具有阶级意识的成长环境。|近义词：status-conscious, rank-conscious|反义词：class-blind, unaware of social class|词性:形容词
