defrayable:/dɪˈfreɪəbl/
adjective
可支付的，可报销的
Capable of being paid or provided for; reimbursable.
The company will defray all defrayable expenses. 公司将支付所有可报销的费用。
The travel expenses are defrayable by the company.  差旅费可由公司报销。
synonyms: reimbursable, payable, compensable
antonyms: non-reimbursable, non-payable
