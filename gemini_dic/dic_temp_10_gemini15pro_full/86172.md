heartstrings:/ˈhɑːrtstrɪŋz/
noun
心弦
The deepest feelings or affections.  It refers to the things that affect us emotionally at a very deep level.
The film really tugged at the heartstrings. 这部电影真的触动了我的心弦。
synonyms: emotions, feelings, affections
antonyms: apathy, indifference
