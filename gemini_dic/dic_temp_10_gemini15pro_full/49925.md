denationalizing: /diːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
取消国有化; 私有化
The act of transferring ownership or control of an industry or asset from the state to private hands.
The government is denationalizing the railway system. 政府正在将铁路系统私有化。
The denationalizing of the coal mines led to job losses. 煤矿的私有化导致了失业。
synonyms: privatizing, deregulating
antonyms: nationalizing, state-owned
