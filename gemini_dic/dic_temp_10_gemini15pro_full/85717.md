hawai'i:/həˈvaɪʔiː/ | n. |夏威夷|A state of the United States, located in the central Pacific Ocean, comprising a chain of volcanic islands.|例句：I'm going to Hawai'i for vacation. 我要去夏威夷度假。

例句：Hawai'i is known for its beautiful beaches. 夏威夷以其美丽的海滩而闻名。|近义词：Big Island (referring to the island of Hawaiʻi), Aloha State|反义词: N/A |词性: 名词


Etymology: From the Hawaiian language *Hawaiʻi*, the origin of which is uncertain, possibly from Proto-Polynesian *Sawaiki*, meaning "homeland."
