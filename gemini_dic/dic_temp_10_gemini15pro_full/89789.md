horizontalization:/ˌhɔːrɪˌzɒntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌhɔːrɪˌzɑːntələˈzeɪʃən/
n.|水平化，横向化|The process of making something horizontal or more horizontal; the act of spreading something out over a wider area or to more people or groups.  |The horizontalization of management structures has led to more collaborative teamwork.|synonyms: flattening, broadening, decentralization, diffusion, spreading|antonyms: verticalization, centralization, consolidation|noun
