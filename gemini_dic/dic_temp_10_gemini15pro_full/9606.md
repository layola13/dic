antispeculativeness:/ˌæntiˌspɛkjʊˈleɪtɪvnəs/
| n. | 反投机性，非投机性 |  The quality or state of being antispeculative; opposition to speculation.  It emphasizes caution and a preference for avoiding risky ventures. |  His antispeculativeness made him hesitant to invest in the volatile stock market. 他的反投机性使他犹豫是否要投资动荡的股票市场。 |  prudence, caution, conservatism |  speculativeness, risk-taking, adventurousness | noun
