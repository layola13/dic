hormonal:/hɔːrˈmoʊnl/ (US), /hɔːˈməʊnl/ (UK)| adj.|荷尔蒙的；激素的|Relating to or controlled by hormones.|Her hormonal imbalances caused mood swings. 她的荷尔蒙失调导致情绪波动。|The doctor prescribed hormonal therapy to alleviate her symptoms. 医生开了激素疗法来缓解她的症状。|近义词：endocrine, glandular|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hormone + -al. Hormone comes from the Greek word "horman" meaning "to set in motion."
