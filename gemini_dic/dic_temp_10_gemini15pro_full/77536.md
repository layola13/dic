gce:/dʒiː siː iː/ | n. | General Certificate of Education | A British examination taken by students at the age of 16 (GCSE) or 18 (GCE A Level).  It is a qualification that shows a certain level of achievement in secondary education. |  She passed her GCEs with flying colors. 她以优异的成绩通过了普通教育证书考试。 |  synonym: examination, qualification | antonym: N/A | noun
