elicits:/ɪˈlɪsɪts/| vt.| 引出，探出，诱出| To evoke or draw out a response, answer, or fact from someone, often with some difficulty. | The teacher elicits responses from her students by asking thought-provoking questions. 老师通过提出发人深省的问题来引出学生的回答。

The comedian's jokes elicited laughter from the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话引来了观众的笑声。

|近义词： evoke, draw out, extract, obtain |反义词: suppress, repress, conceal| verb
