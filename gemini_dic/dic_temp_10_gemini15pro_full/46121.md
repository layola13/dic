cyma:/ˈsaɪmə/| n. | 希玛,  (建筑)波状线饰,  (植物)聚伞花序 |A molding or other architectural feature having a double curve, concave at one end and convex at the other; resembling an S.  In botany, a cyme. |The cyma recta is a common decorative element in classical architecture.  这希玛线饰是古典建筑中常见的装饰元素。| The cyma reversa is also known as an ogee.  希玛反曲线也被称为葱形饰。| synonym: wave, ogee, curve | antonym: straight line | noun

cyma:/ˈsaɪmə/| n. | 聚伞花序（植物学）| A type of inflorescence in which the main axis terminates in a flower, and subsequent flowers develop from lateral buds. |Elderflower is an example of a cyma. 接骨木花就是一个聚伞花序的例子。| This plant produces its flowers in a cyma. 这种植物的花以聚伞花序的形式生长。| synonym: inflorescence, flower cluster | antonym: single flower | noun
