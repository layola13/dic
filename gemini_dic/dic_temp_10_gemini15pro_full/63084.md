ensconce:/ɪnˈskɒns/ /ɛnˈskɑːns/| vt.|安置；使…舒适地安顿下来；隐藏|To settle securely or comfortably; to hide or conceal.|He ensconced himself in a comfortable armchair by the fire. 他在壁炉旁的舒适扶手椅里安顿下来。|近义词：settle, nestle, install, shelter|反义词：dislodge, remove, expose|词性：动词

词源: From Old French <em>ensconcer</em> "to hide, conceal," from <em>en-</em> "in" + a Germanic base represented by Old High German <em>skunta</em> "protection."
