mark:/mɑːrk/ (US) /mɑːk/ (UK)| v. n.|标记，记号；分数；标志；庆祝；留意|To make a visible impression or sign on something; to indicate or represent something; to give a grade or score to; to celebrate or commemorate; to pay attention to.|
例句：He marked the spot on the map. 他在地图上标出了这个地点。
例句：She marked her birthday with a party. 她举办了一个派对来庆祝她的生日。

近义词：label, indicate, note, score, celebrate, observe
反义词：erase, ignore, disregard
词性：verb, noun
