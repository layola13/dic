mainstream:/ˈmeɪnstriːm/| n. adj.|主流，主流的|The prevailing current of thought, influence, or activity.|  Her music has never really been part of the mainstream. 她的音乐从未真正成为主流的一部分。|近义词：conventional, orthodox, prevalent|反义词：unconventional, unorthodox, fringe|词性:名词，形容词
Etymology:  From main (principal) + stream (current).  Originally referred to the principal current of a river.
