bruschettas:/bruːˈskettəz/ /bruːˈʃettəz/
n.
烤面包片（复数）
Grilled slices of bread, typically rubbed with garlic and topped with tomatoes, olive oil, and basil.
We ordered a plate of bruschettas as an appetizer.  我们点了一盘烤面包片作为开胃菜。
The bruschettas were topped with fresh mozzarella and balsamic glaze.  这些烤面包片上面放了新鲜的马苏里拉奶酪和香醋。
近义词:crostini, appetizers, canapés
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Italian bruschetta, from bruscare "to roast over coals," from Vulgar Latin *bruscus "butcher's broom" (a shrub used for making brooms), of uncertain origin.
