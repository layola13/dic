incloser:/ɪnˈkloʊzər/ | n. | 附件; 围场; 随函附上之物 |  Something that is enclosed, such as a document or photograph, with a letter.  A piece of land surrounded by a fence or wall. | Example: Please find the incloser for your reference.  请参考附件。The horses grazed peacefully in the incloser. 马匹在围场里平静地吃草。 | 近义词: enclosure, attachment, insert, paddock | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the verb "inclose" (now usually "enclose"), from Old French "enclore," from Latin "includere" "to shut in," from "in-" "in" + "claudere" "to shut."
