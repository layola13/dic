co-ordinations: /kəʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 协调，配合 | The act or process of coordinating or being coordinated; harmonious combination or interaction, especially of multiple bodily movements or muscle groups. | The dancer's co-ordinations were impeccable, each movement flowing seamlessly into the next. 舞蹈家的动作协调无懈可击，每个动作都流畅地衔接到下一个动作。| The team's success depended on the co-ordinations of all its members. 团队的成功取决于所有成员的协调配合。 |  synonyms: collaboration, cooperation, synchronization, integration, harmony | antonyms: discord, conflict, disharmony, disorganization | noun
