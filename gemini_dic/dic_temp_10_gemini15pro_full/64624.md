erysipelatous: /ˌɛrɪsɪˈpɛlətəs/ /ˌɛrɪsɪˈpiːlətəs/
adjective
丹毒的；患丹毒的；似丹毒的
Relating to, resembling, or affected with erysipelas (an acute streptococcal infection of the skin and subcutaneous tissue).

Erysipelatous inflammation can spread rapidly.
丹毒性炎症会迅速蔓延。


The patient presented with an erysipelatous rash on her leg.
病人的腿上出现了丹毒性皮疹。

Synonym:  erysipeloid, inflamed, reddened
Antonym: healthy, uninflamed

词性: adjective
