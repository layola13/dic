befogged:/bɪˈfɒɡd/ /bɪˈfɑːɡd/
adjective
困惑的，迷茫的；被雾笼罩的
mentally confused; obscured by fog
例句：
I was completely befogged by the complex instructions. 我完全被复杂的指令弄糊涂了。

The morning mist befogged the valley. 早晨的薄雾笼罩着山谷。

近义词：confused, bewildered, perplexed, hazy, misty, foggy
反义词：clear, lucid, distinct
词性：adjective
