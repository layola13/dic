draw:/drɔː/ (British) /drɑː/ (American)| v. n.| 画，绘制；拉，拖；吸引；取出；提取；(比赛)打成平局 |The artist drew a portrait.  这位艺术家画了一幅肖像画。| synonyms: sketch, pull, attract, haul, extract, withdraw | antonyms: push, repel, insert, deposit | verb

draw:/drɔː/ (British) /drɑː/ (American)| n.| 平局；抽签；吸引人的事物 |The match ended in a draw.  比赛以平局结束。| synonyms: tie, stalemate | antonyms: victory, defeat | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dragan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *draganą (“to draw, pull, drag”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰregʰ- (“to draw, pull”). Cognate with Old Frisian draga (“to draw”), Old Saxon dragan (“to carry, bear, draw”), Old High German tragan (“to carry, bear”), Old Norse draga (“to draw, pull, drag”), Gothic 𝘥𝘳𝘢𝘨𝘢𝘯 (dragan, “to draw”).
