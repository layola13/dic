disband:/dɪsˈbænd/
verb
解散，遣散
to break up or dissolve (a group, organization, or assembly)
The club disbanded due to lack of funding. 俱乐部由于缺乏资金而解散。
The army was disbanded after the war. 战后军队被解散了。
synonyms: break up, dissolve, disperse, scatter, dismiss
antonyms: assemble, gather, unite, form
词性: 动词


disband:/dɪsˈbænd/
noun
解散，遣散（名词形式较少用）
the act of disbanding
The disband of the group was inevitable. 该组织的解散是不可避免的。（用法略显生硬）
synonyms: dissolution, breakup, dispersal
antonyms: formation, assembly, union
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French desbander, from des- (dis-) + bander "to form into a band," from bande "band, troop."
