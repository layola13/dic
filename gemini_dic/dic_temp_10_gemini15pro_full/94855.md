in-a-fix:/ɪn ə fɪks/| idiom.|处于困境；陷入困境|Being in a difficult situation; having a problem that is difficult to solve.|例句：I'm in a fix because I lost my wallet. 我很麻烦，因为我的钱包丢了。|近义词：in trouble, in a jam, in a predicament, in a bind|反义词:safe, secure|词性:idiom
