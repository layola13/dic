myriologist:/ˌmɪriˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 万物学家，博学家 | A person who studies a vast range of subjects or has a wide range of knowledge. |  He was a true myriologist, conversant in everything from ancient history to quantum physics. 他是一位真正的博学家，从古代历史到量子物理无所不知。| polymath, savant, erudite | specialist | noun

Etymology:
From Greek *myrios* "ten thousand, countless" + *-logos* "one who speaks or treats (of), student of"
