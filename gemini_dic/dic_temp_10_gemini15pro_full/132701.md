nondistinguishableness:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəlnəs/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəlnəs/
n.
不可区分性，不可辨别性
The nondistinguishableness of the twins made it difficult to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎难以区分，很难把他们分开。
The nondistinguishableness of the counterfeit bills from the real ones is alarming.  假钞与真钞的不可区分性令人担忧。
近义词：indistinguishability, sameness, uniformity
反义词：distinguishability, difference, distinctness
词性：名词

词源：
From non- (not) + distinguishable + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Distinguishable itself comes from distinguish (to recognize or treat (someone or something) as different), from Old French distinguer, from Latin distinguere (to separate by pricking, to distinguish), from dis- (apart) + -stinguere (to prick, to extinguish).
