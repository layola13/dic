arrowhead: /ˈæroʊhɛd/ (US) /ˈærəʊhɛd/ (UK)| n. |箭头；箭头状物|The arrowhead was made of flint. 箭头是用燧石做的。| An arrowhead is the pointed tip of an arrow, typically made of metal, stone, bone, or other hard material. It is designed to penetrate the target when the arrow is shot.  |例句：


The Native Americans made arrowheads from obsidian.  美国原住民用黑曜石制作箭头。


Archaeologists found several arrowheads at the ancient burial site.  考古学家在古代墓地发现了几枚箭头。


|近义词：tip, point, head |反义词: base, fletching (箭羽)|noun
