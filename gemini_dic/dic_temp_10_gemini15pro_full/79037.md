give-the-air:/ɡɪv ði ɛər/
|phrasal verb|
|冷落；怠慢；抛弃|
|To snub or ignore someone; to reject or dismiss someone or something.|
|She gave him the air after he lied to her.|她在他对她撒谎后就把他甩了。
|He gave the air to his old friends when he became famous.|他成名后就冷落了他的老朋友们。
|近义词：snub, ignore, reject, dismiss, jilt, cold-shoulder|
|反义词：welcome, embrace, accept|
|词性: 动词短语|


give-the-air:/ɡɪv ði ɛər/
|idiom|
|播出；播放|
|To broadcast something, particularly over radio or television.|
|The interview was given the air last night.|采访昨晚播出了。
|The new song will be given the air next week.|新歌将在下周播出。
|近义词：broadcast, air, transmit|
|反义词：suppress, censor|
|词性: 惯用语|
