bake:/beɪk/
v.
烘烤；烧制；烘焙
to cook food by dry heat in an oven
例句：
I baked a cake for his birthday.
我为他生日烤了一个蛋糕。

She baked cookies for the children.
她为孩子们烤了饼干。

近义词：roast, cook, grill
反义词：freeze

词性：动词
