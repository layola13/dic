overmined:/ˌoʊvərˈmaɪnd/ | adj. | 过度开采的 |  Excessively mined; mined to the point of depletion or exhaustion.  Describes a resource, such as a mineral or ore, that has been extracted to an unsustainable degree. | The overmined hillsides were prone to landslides. 过度开采的山坡容易发生山体滑坡。| The overmined coal seam eventually collapsed. 过度开采的煤层最终坍塌了。 | overexploited, depleted | underutilized, preserved | adjective
