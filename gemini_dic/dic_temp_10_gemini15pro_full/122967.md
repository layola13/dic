monad:/ˈmɒnæd/ (UK), /ˈmoʊnæd/ (US)| n.|单子， монада (monada - Russian)|A single unit; an indivisible entity. In computer science, a monad is a design pattern that represents computations defined as sequences of steps.  In philosophy, it refers to an ultimate unit of being. |例句：
In Leibniz's philosophy, the monad is the fundamental building block of reality. 在莱布尼茨的哲学中，单子是现实世界的基本构成要素。

The concept of a monad is used in functional programming to manage side effects. 单子的概念在函数式编程中用于管理副作用。

|近义词：unit, entity, atom|反义词:dyad, plurality|noun
