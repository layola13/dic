clownishly: /ˈklaʊnɪʃli/ | adv. | 小丑般地，滑稽地，笨拙地 | In a manner resembling a clown; comically or foolishly. | He clownishly tripped over his own feet. 他滑稽地被自己的脚绊倒了。|  He danced clownishly around the room.  他滑稽地在房间里跳舞。 | 近义词：comically, foolishly, ridiculously, buffoonishly | 反义词：seriously, gracefully, solemnly | 词性: 副词
