palpicorn:/ˌpælpɪˈkɔːrn/| adj.  n.|触角末端膨大的，棒角的；棒角甲虫|Having palps, or organs of touch, resembling horns, or antennae. Specifically applied to a family of pentamerous beetles (Palpicornia), having short clavate antennae.|The palpicorn beetle floated on the surface of the pond. 棒角甲虫漂浮在池塘的水面上。|近义词：clavicorn|反义词:|adjective, noun
