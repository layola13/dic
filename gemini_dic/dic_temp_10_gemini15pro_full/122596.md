modernistic:/ˌmɒdəˈnɪstɪk/ (US) /ˌmɑːdərˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|现代主义的，现代风格的|Of or relating to modernism, especially in art, architecture, or design; having a consciously modern style.  Characterized by a deliberate break with traditional styles and techniques, often embracing new materials and technologies.|Her apartment was decorated in a starkly modernistic style, with chrome furniture and abstract art. 她的公寓以极简的现代主义风格装饰，配有镀铬家具和抽象艺术。|The architect's modernistic design incorporated sleek lines and large windows.  这位建筑师的现代主义设计融合了流畅的线条和大窗户。|近义词：modern, contemporary, avant-garde|反义词：traditional, conventional, classical|词性: adjective
