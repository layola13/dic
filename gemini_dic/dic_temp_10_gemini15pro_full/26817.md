cagey:/ˈkeɪdʒi/| adj.|谨慎的，守口如瓶的，讳莫如深的|Reluctant to give information owing to caution or suspicion.|He was very cagey about his plans.|近义词：secretive, guarded, wary, reticent, evasive|反义词：open, frank, candid|词性:形容词
词源: From "cage" + "-y," suggesting being enclosed or restrained like a caged animal, thus reluctant to reveal information.
