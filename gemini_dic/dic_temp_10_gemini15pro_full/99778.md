intermedium:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəm/ | n. | 中间物，媒介，媒介物 |Something that acts as a means or agency for another; a medium. | The enzyme acts as an intermedium in the chemical reaction. 这种酶在化学反应中充当媒介。 | 近义词: medium, agent, intermediary | 反义词: extreme, direct | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Latin *intermedium*, neuter of *intermedius* ("intermediate"), from *inter-* ("between") + *medius* ("middle").
