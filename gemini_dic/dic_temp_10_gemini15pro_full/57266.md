downplay: /ˌdaʊnˈpleɪ/ | v. | 淡化；贬低；轻描淡写 | To make something appear less important or significant than it really is. | The government tried to downplay the seriousness of the economic crisis. 政府试图淡化经济危机的严重性。|  The coach downplayed the team's loss, focusing instead on the players' improvement. 教练淡化了球队的失利，反而强调了球员们的进步。 | synonyms: minimize, understate, de-emphasize | antonyms: exaggerate, overstate, emphasize, play up | verb
