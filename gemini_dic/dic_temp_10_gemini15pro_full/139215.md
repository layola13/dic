objector:/əbˈdʒektər/ | n. | 反对者; 异议者 | A person who expresses or holds an objection. |  例句：Several objectors voiced their concerns at the public meeting.  几个反对者在公开会议上表达了他们的担忧。

例句：The objector to the proposal presented a compelling argument.  该提案的反对者提出了令人信服的论点。| 近义词：dissenter, protestor, opponent | 反义词：supporter, proponent | 词性: noun
