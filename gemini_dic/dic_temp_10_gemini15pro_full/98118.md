insalubrious:/ˌɪnsəˈluːbriəs/| adj.|有害健康的，不卫生的|Not conducive to good health; unhealthy.|(Of a place) not healthy to live in; tending to cause illness.|例句：The air in the factory was thick and insalubrious. 工厂里的空气又浓又对健康有害。

例句：They lived in an insalubrious, swampy region. 他们住在一个不卫生的沼泽地区。|近义词：unhealthy, noxious, detrimental, injurious|反义词:healthy, salubrious, wholesome|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *insalubris* (unhealthy), from Latin *in-* (not) + *salubris* (healthy). *Salubris* is from *salus* (health, safety, well-being).
