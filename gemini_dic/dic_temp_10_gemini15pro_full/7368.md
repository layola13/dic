animadversive:/ˌænɪmædˈvɜːsɪv/
adjective
批评的，非难的
characterized by or expressing censure; containing or constituting critical remark
His animadversive tone made it clear he disapproved of her actions. 他批评的语气表明他不赞成她的行为。

近义词:critical, censorious, reproving, rebuking, chiding
反义词:laudatory, complimentary, praising, approving

词性:adjective

词源: From Latin animadversus, past participle of animadvertere (“to turn one's mind to, attend to, consider, notice, perceive, remark upon, criticize”), from animus (“mind, spirit, courage, passion, wrath”) + advertere (“to turn to”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + vertere (“to turn”).
