filmstrips:/ˈfɪlmˌstrɪps/
noun
电影胶片，幻灯片
A length of film containing a sequence of images for projection as a slide show.
We used to watch filmstrips in school. 我们过去在学校看电影胶片。
synonyms: slides, film roll, transparencies
antonyms: movie, video
