gallivat:/ˈɡælɪvət/| n.|小型帆船，单桅帆船|A small sailboat, especially a fast one.|例句：The gallivat skimmed across the waves. 这艘单桅帆船掠过海浪。

例句：He sailed his gallivat around the Caribbean islands. 他驾驶他的小型帆船环游加勒比群岛。|近义词：sloop, cutter, sailboat|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *galiote*, from Italian *galeotta*, ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly from late Latin *galea*, “galley”.
