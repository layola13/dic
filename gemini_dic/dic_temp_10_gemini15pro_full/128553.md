Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before the movie? = 看电影前你吃了什么？
What had she done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前她做了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 中断前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习了五年什么？
What will she have been working on by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将一直在做什么？


Interrogative Sentences:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to think. = 我不知道该怎么想。
She doesn't understand what happened. = 她不明白发生了什么。
