lcd:/ˌɛl siː ˈdiː/| n. |液晶显示器|Liquid Crystal Display, a flat-panel display technology commonly used in televisions, computer monitors, and other electronic devices. |例句：My laptop has a bright LCD screen. 我的笔记本电脑有一个明亮的液晶显示屏。

例句：The LCD projector displayed a crisp, clear image. 液晶投影仪显示出清晰的图像。

|近义词：display, screen|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑CRT - Cathode Ray Tube)|词性:noun
