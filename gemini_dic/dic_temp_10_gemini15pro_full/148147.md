oversensitizes:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪz/
verb
使…過敏；使…過度敏感
makes someone or something too sensitive or easily upset.
The media coverage has oversensitized the public to the issue. 媒体的报道使公众对这个问题过于敏感。
近义词:exaggerates, inflames
反义词:desensitizes, numbs
词性:v.


oversensitive:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/
adjective
過度敏感的；神經過敏的
easily upset or offended.
He's oversensitive to criticism. 他对批评过于敏感。
近义词:touchy, thin-skinned
反义词:insensitive, thick-skinned
词性:adj.


oversensitivity:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/
noun
過度敏感；神經過敏
the state of being easily upset or offended.
Her oversensitivity makes it difficult for her to handle criticism. 她的过度敏感使她难以处理批评。
近义词:touchiness, vulnerability
反义词:insensitivity, indifference
词性:n.


词源:
From over- + sensitive + -ize.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" and "-ize" transforms the adjective "sensitive" into a verb meaning "to make sensitive."  The noun form, "oversensitivity," is derived by adding the suffix "-ity."
