me-time: /ˈmiː taɪm/ | n. | 我时间；个人时间 | Time spent relaxing on one's own as opposed to working or socializing. | I need some me-time to recharge after a busy week. 我在忙碌的一周后需要一些自己的时间来充电。| Example: After a long day at work, she enjoys some me-time with a good book. 下班后，她喜欢用一本好书来享受自己的时间。| 近义词: personal time, downtime, alone time | 反义词: social time, work time | 词性: noun

词源:  A compound noun formed from the pronoun "me" and the noun "time."  It gained popularity in the late 20th century.
