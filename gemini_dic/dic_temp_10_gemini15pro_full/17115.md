batten:/ˈbætən/ (US), /ˈbætən/ (UK)| n. v.|板条，压条；养肥，长得膘肥|A long, flat strip of wood or metal used especially to fasten something in place or as a support.|The shutters were secured with battens.|近义词:strip, plank, lath, support, brace, strengthen, reinforce|反义词:weaken, undermine|n.

batten:/ˈbætən/ (US), /ˈbætən/ (UK)| v.|养肥，长得膘肥；准备，采取措施（尤指防范不测）；在…上钉板条|To thrive; prosper; to prepare; to fasten with battens.|The cattle battened on the rich pasture.|近义词:thrive, flourish, prosper, fatten, prepare, fortify, secure|反义词:starve, wither, weaken, neglect|v.
