overhair: /ˈoʊvərˌhɛr/ (US), /ˈəʊvəˌheə(r)/ (UK)| n. |覆盖毛; 护毛; 长毛|The longer, coarser hairs of an animal's coat that form a protective layer over the softer underhair.|The cat's overhair was sleek and shiny. 这只猫的护毛光滑而有光泽。|近义词: guard hair, topcoat, outer coat|反义词: underhair, undercoat|词性:名词

词源:  'over' (OE ofer) meaning "above" + 'hair' (OE hær)
