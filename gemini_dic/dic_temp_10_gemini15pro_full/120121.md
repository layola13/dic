miming:/ˈmaɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 模仿，哑剧 | The act of expressing or conveying something through gestures and facial expressions without speaking. |  He entertained the children with his miming. 他用他的哑剧表演逗乐了孩子们。|  imitation, impersonation, acting | speech, talking | noun
