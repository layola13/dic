cackler:/ˈkæklər/| n.|咯咯叫的人/动物，爱饶舌的人|A cackler is someone or something that cackles, often referring to a hen or a goose, but can also be used figuratively for a person who laughs loudly or talks incessantly.|The cackler in the henhouse announced the arrival of a new egg.  母鸡舍里咯咯叫的母鸡宣告了一枚新蛋的到来。|The old woman was known as a cackler, always gossiping about her neighbors.  那个老妇人被称为饶舌妇，总是说邻居的闲话。|近义词：gossiper, chatterbox, hen|反义词:quiet person, listener|词性:noun
