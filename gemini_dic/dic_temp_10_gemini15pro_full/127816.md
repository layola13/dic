nemalite:/ˈnɛməˌlaɪt/| n. |纤滑石，纤蛇纹石|A fibrous variety of brucite, Mg(OH)2, usually found as a soft, white, silky material associated with serpentine and other magnesium minerals. It is used as a minor source of magnesium and in some fireproofing applications.|The geologist identified the white, silky mineral as nemalite. 地质学家将这种白色丝状矿物鉴定为纤滑石。|Synonyms: brucite fiber, chrysotile (when similar in appearance)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
