incandescents:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənts/| n. |白炽灯；白炽物|Emitting light as a result of being heated.|The old incandescents were replaced with energy-saving LEDs. 旧的白炽灯被节能的LED灯取代了。|Synonyms: glowing, luminous, radiant|Antonyms: dark, dim, unlit|Noun

incandescent:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/| adj. |白炽的；发光的；灿烂的|Emitting light as a result of being heated; passionate and brilliant.|The metal was heated until it became incandescent. 金属被加热到白炽状态。|Synonyms: glowing, luminous, radiant, brilliant, dazzling|Antonyms: dark, dim, unlit, dull|Adjective
