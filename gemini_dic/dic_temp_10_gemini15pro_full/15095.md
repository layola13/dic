bacchanalianism:/ˌbækəˈneɪliənɪzəm/
n.
狂欢; 放荡; 酒神节式的狂欢作乐
Unrestrained revelry and merrymaking, characteristic of the festivals of Bacchus.
The fraternity party devolved into pure bacchanalianism, with loud music, copious amounts of alcohol, and questionable behavior.  兄弟会的聚会变成了纯粹的狂欢作乐，充斥着喧闹的音乐、大量的酒精和令人质疑的行为。
Synonyms: debauchery, revelry, carousal, orgy, intemperance
Antonyms: abstinence, sobriety, moderation, temperance
noun

bacchanalian:/ˌbækəˈneɪliən/
adj.
酒神节式的; 狂欢的; 放荡的
Characterized by or resembling the festivals of Bacchus; riotously drunken and merry.
The bacchanalian atmosphere of the concert was infectious, and everyone was dancing and singing along.  音乐会的狂欢气氛很有感染力，每个人都跟着跳舞唱歌。
Synonyms: riotous, orgiastic, drunken, dissolute, intemperate
Antonyms: sober, restrained, moderate, temperate
adjective

bacchanal:/ˈbækənəl/
n.
狂欢作乐; (尤指)纵酒狂欢
A wild, drunken revelry.
The wedding reception turned into a bacchanal, with guests dancing on tables and singing at the top of their lungs.  婚宴变成了狂欢作乐，客人们在桌子上跳舞，放声高歌。
Synonyms: orgy, carousal, revel, spree, debauch
Antonyms: restraint, sobriety, moderation
noun
