astray:/əˈstreɪ/
adjective|误入歧途的，迷路的|Away from the correct path or direction. |The hikers went astray in the dense fog.  徒步旅行者在浓雾中迷路了。|
近义词: lost, off course, off track, amiss, misguided
反义词: on track, on course, right, correct
词性: adjective


astray:/əˈstreɪ/
adverb|误入歧途地，迷路地|Away from the correct path or direction. |His plans went astray. 他的计划偏离了方向。|
近义词: wrongly, amiss, off course
反义词: correctly, right, properly
词性: adverb

词源: From a- (meaning "on" or "in") + stray (verb).  "Stray" itself comes from Old French "estraier" meaning "to wander, roam."
