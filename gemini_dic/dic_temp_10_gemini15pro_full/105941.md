clicks:/klɪks/
n.|点击量，点击次数|The website received thousands of clicks in the first hour.  这个网站在第一个小时就收到了数千次点击。|例句：The advertisement received a high number of clicks. 这条广告获得了很高的点击量。|近义词：hits, visits|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 low engagement 低参与度)|词性:名词
clicks:/klɪks/
v. (third-person singular simple present clicks, present participle clicking, simple past and past participle clicked)|点击，发出咔哒声|I clicked on the link to open the website. 我点击了链接打开了网站。|例句：The mouse clicks when I press the button. 当我按下按钮时，鼠标会发出咔哒声。|近义词：press, select (for computer usage); snap, tick (for the sound)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:动词
