masterly:/ˈmɑːstərli/ | adj. | 熟练的，精湛的，大师般的 | Showing great skill or technique, like a master. | He gave a masterly performance on the piano. 他在钢琴上进行了大师级的演奏。|  She delivered a masterly presentation. 她做了一个精彩绝伦的演讲。 | 近义词: skillful, expert, adept, proficient, accomplished | 反义词: clumsy, inept, unskilled, amateurish | 词性: 形容词

词源: From master + -ly, meaning "in the manner of a master."  Ultimately from Latin *magister* ("master, chief, director").
