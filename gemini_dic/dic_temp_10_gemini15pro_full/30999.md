chalkest:/ˈtʃɔːkɪst/
verb |用粉笔写或画；使…变白|You chalkest the lines on the court. 你用粉笔在球场上画线。| 近义词: draw, write, sketch | 反义词: erase, rub out |词性: 动词

Etymology: From Middle English *chalken, from Old English *cealcian (“to chalk, mark with chalk”), from cealc (“chalk”). Ultimately from Latin calx (“lime, limestone”).
