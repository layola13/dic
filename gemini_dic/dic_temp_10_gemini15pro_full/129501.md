non-artistic:/ˌnɒn.ɑːˈtɪs.tɪk/
adjective
非艺术的；缺乏艺术性的
Not related to art; lacking artistic skill or talent.
Her non-artistic pursuits include hiking and birdwatching. 她不具艺术性的爱好包括远足和观鸟。
Synonyms: unartistic,inartistic, noncreative
Antonyms: artistic, creative, imaginative
词性: 形容词
