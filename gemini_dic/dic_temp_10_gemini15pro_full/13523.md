attains:/əˈteɪnz/| v. |达到，获得，实现|To reach or succeed in getting something.|She finally attained her goal of becoming a doctor. 她终于实现了成为一名医生的目标。|例句：After years of hard work, he attained a high level of proficiency in playing the piano. 经过多年的努力，他达到了很高的钢琴演奏水平。

例句：The cheetah attains speeds of up to 70 miles per hour. 猎豹的速度可以达到每小时70英里。|近义词：achieve, accomplish, reach, obtain, gain|反义词:fail, lose, forfeit|词性:动词

PHRASE->
attain to something:  达到某种程度或状态  He attained to a position of great power. 他达到了一个非常有权势的地位。
attain enlightenment:  达到觉悟/开悟  Many Buddhists seek to attain enlightenment. 许多佛教徒寻求觉悟。
attain one's potential:  发挥某人的潜能  She worked hard to attain her full potential. 她努力工作以充分发挥自己的潜力。
attain a goal/objective:  达到目标  The company attained its sales goals for the quarter. 公司实现了该季度的销售目标。
attain success:  获得成功  He attained great success in his chosen field. 他在他选择的领域取得了巨大的成功。


词源: From Old French ataindre, from Latin attingere "to touch upon, reach, attain," from ad- "to" + tangere "to touch."
