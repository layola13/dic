corymbosely:/kɒrɪmˈbəʊsli/ | adv. |伞房状地，聚伞状地 | In a corymbose manner; arranged in corymbs.  A corymb is a flat-topped or convex inflorescence in which the outer flowers open first. | The flowers were arranged corymbosely on the stem.  花朵在茎上呈伞房状排列。 |  Near synonyms: cymosely,  | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use terms describing other inflorescence types like "racemosely" or "paniculately") | Word type: Adverb


Etymology->
From corymb + -ose + -ly. Corymb comes from the Greek word *korymbos*, meaning "cluster of flowers, top of a tree".
