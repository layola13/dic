figureless:/ˈfɪɡjərlɪs/
adjective
无形状的，无花纹的，朴素的
Lacking a distinct or attractive shape; plain, unadorned.
Her figureless dress was simple but elegant. 她那件朴素的连衣裙简洁而优雅。
The building's figureless facade was imposing in its starkness.  这栋建筑毫无装饰的正面在荒凉中显得气势磅礴。
近义词:plain, simple, unadorned, shapeless, formless
反义词:shapely, figured, ornate, decorated, embellished
词性:adjective
