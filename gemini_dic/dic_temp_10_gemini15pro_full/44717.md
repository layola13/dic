cruelties:/ˈkruːəltiz/
n.
残酷行为；暴行
acts of extreme cruelty or violence
The history books are filled with accounts of the cruelties inflicted upon innocent people. 历史书中充满了对无辜人民施加暴行的描述。

The refugees told harrowing stories of the cruelties they had suffered. 难民们讲述了他们遭受的令人痛苦的暴行。

近义词:atrocities, barbarities, brutalities, savagery
反义词:kindness, compassion, mercy
词性:名词(plural noun)

词源:
From Middle English *crualte, from Old French crualte, from Latin crūdēlitās (“crudeness, cruelty”), from crūdus (“raw, unripe, harsh, cruel”) + -itās (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Doublet of crudity.
