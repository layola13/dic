corneas:/ˈkɔːrniəz/ (US), /ˈkɔːniəz/ (UK)| n.|角膜|The transparent front part of the eye that covers the iris, pupil, and anterior chamber.  It refracts light and contributes to the eye's focusing power.|Example 1:  The surgeon transplanted the corneas.  外科医生移植了角膜。

Example 2:  Damage to the corneas can cause blindness.  角膜损伤会导致失明。|近义词:  (none - cornea is a specific anatomical term)|反义词: (none)|词性:noun
