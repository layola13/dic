otorrhea:/ˌoʊtəˈriːə/| n. |耳溢；耳漏|Discharge from the ear.  It can be serous, mucous, purulent, or bloody.|例句：The patient presented with otorrhea and pain in his right ear.  病人右耳疼痛并伴有耳漏。

例句：Chronic otorrhea can be a sign of infection.  慢性耳漏可能是感染的迹象。|近义词：ear discharge, ear drainage|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
