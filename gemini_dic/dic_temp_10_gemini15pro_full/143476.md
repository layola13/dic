outgamble: /ˌaʊtˈɡæmbəl/
verb
以赌博胜过; 赌赢
To defeat someone by gambling more skillfully or by taking greater risks.
He outgambled his opponents at the poker table. 他在扑克桌上赌赢了对手。
近义词: outsmart, outmaneuver, defeat
反义词: lose to
词性: verb

outgamble: /ˌaʊtˈɡæmbəl/
verb
比…承担更大的赌博风险
To take greater risks than someone else in gambling.
She outgambled him by betting everything on a single hand. 她孤注一掷，比他承担了更大的风险。
近义词: outbid, overbid
反义词: play it safe
词性: verb
