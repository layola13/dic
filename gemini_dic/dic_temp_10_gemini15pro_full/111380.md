livestock:/ˈlaɪvstɒk/ (US) /ˈlaɪvstɔːk/ (UK)| n. |牲畜，家畜|farm animals, such as cows, sheep, and pigs, that are kept for agricultural purposes|例句：The farmer raised livestock for meat and dairy products. 农民饲养牲畜以获取肉类和奶制品。

例句：The drought severely impacted the livestock population. 干旱严重影响了牲畜的数量。|近义词：cattle, farm animals|反义词: wild animals|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *liuestok*, from *lif* (“life, living”) + *stok* (“stock, store”).  Essentially meaning "living stock" or animals kept alive as an asset.
