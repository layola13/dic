monopolizers:/məˈnɑːpəlaɪzərz/
n.
垄断者；独占者
People or companies that monopolize something, gaining exclusive control over it and preventing others from accessing or using it.
Microsoft was accused of being a monopolizers in the software industry. 微软被指控在软件行业是垄断者。
The monopolizers kept prices artificially high. 垄断者人为地抬高价格。
Synonyms: cornerer, hogger, controller
Antonyms: sharer, distributor
