alcalimeter:/ˌælkəlˈɪmɪtər/ | n. |碱量计；碱定量器 |An instrument used to determine the amount of alkali present in a solution, typically by titration with a standard acid solution. | The chemist used an alcalimeter to measure the strength of the base. 化学家用碱量计测量碱的强度。| The lab technician carefully filled the alcalimeter with the titrant. 实验室技术员小心地将滴定剂装入碱量计。 | synonyms: alkalimeter, burette | antonyms: None | noun
