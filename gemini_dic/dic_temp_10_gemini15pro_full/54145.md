disengagements:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/
n.
脱离，解脱，撤离
The act of disengaging; separation; release from something that engages or involves.  The military announced a phased disengagement of its troops from the conflict zone.  军队宣布分阶段从冲突地区撤军。
The process of withdrawing from a situation or relationship.  The therapist helped the couple navigate their disengagement.  治疗师帮助这对夫妇处理他们的脱离关系。
Instances of disengaging, especially in a military context.  There have been several disengagements between the opposing forces in recent weeks.  最近几周，敌对势力之间发生了几次脱离接触。

近义词:separations, withdrawals, detachments, releases
反义词:engagements, involvements, connections

词性:名词(plural)
词源: From disengage + -ment. Disengage comes from Old French desengager, from des- (expressing reversal) + engager (to pledge, engage).
