interconnector:/ˌɪntəkəˈnɛktə(r)/ | n. | 互连器，连接器 |A device or system that connects two or more things, especially for the transfer of electricity or data. |  The new interconnector will allow electricity to be shared between the two countries. 新的互连器将允许两国共享电力。

The interconnector failed, causing a power outage. 互连器故障，导致停电。| synonym: link, connector, coupler,  | antonym:  disconnector, separator | noun
