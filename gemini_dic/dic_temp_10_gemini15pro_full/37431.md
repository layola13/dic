comminglement:/kəˈmɪŋɡlmənt/
n.|混合，混合物|The comminglement of different cultures in the city creates a vibrant atmosphere. 城市中不同文化的混合创造了充满活力的氛围。|The report detailed the comminglement of company and personal funds. 报告详细说明了公司和个人资金的混合。|blend, mixture, amalgamation, fusion, intermingling|separation, division, segregation|noun
commingled:/kəˈmɪŋɡld/
v. (past tense)|混合，混杂|The company commingled customer funds with its own. 这家公司将客户资金与其自有资金混在一起。|The chef commingled various spices to create a unique flavor. 厨师将各种香料混合在一起，创造出独特的风味。|blend, mix, merge, combine, intermingle|separate, divide, segregate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb commingle, from com- (together) + mingle (to mix).  The word "mingle" comes from Old English "mengen" meaning "to mix".
