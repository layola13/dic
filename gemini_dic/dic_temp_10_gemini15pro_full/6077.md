amercement:/əˈmɜːrsmənt/
|n.|罚金，罚款|A pecuniary penalty; a fine imposed at the discretion of a court or other authority, not fixed by statute.|The amercement for speeding was considerable.  超速的罚款相当高。|Synonyms: fine, penalty, forfeiture|Antonyms: reward, prize|noun
