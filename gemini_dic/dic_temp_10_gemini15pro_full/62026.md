enarthrosis:/ˌɛnɑːrˈθroʊsɪs/
n.
球窩關節
A freely moving joint in which a rounded head of one bone fits into a cuplike cavity of another, allowing movement in all directions.
The shoulder joint is an example of an enarthrosis. 肩关节是球窝关节的一个例子。
近义词: ball-and-socket joint
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider less mobile joints like hinge joints or fibrous joints)
词性: noun
