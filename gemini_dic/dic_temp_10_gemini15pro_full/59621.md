ecchymotic:/ˌɛkiˈmɒtɪk/ | adj. | 瘀斑的，呈现瘀斑的 | Relating to, or resembling, an ecchymosis; marked by ecchymoses. |  The patient presented with ecchymotic areas on their arms and legs. 病人的胳膊和腿上出现了瘀斑。| Synonyms: bruised, contused | Antonyms: unblemished, clear | adjective

ecchymosis:/ˌɛkɪˈmoʊsɪs/ | n. | 瘀斑 |  A discoloration of the skin resulting from bleeding underneath, typically caused by bruising. | The ecchymosis on his knee indicated a recent fall. 他膝盖上的瘀斑表明他最近摔倒了。| Synonyms: bruise, contusion | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "unblemished skin") | noun
