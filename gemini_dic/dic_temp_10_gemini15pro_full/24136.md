briared:/ˈbraɪərd/
adjective
多荆棘的；荆棘丛生的
covered with briars or thorns.
The briared path was difficult to navigate. 多荆棘的小路很难走。
近义词:thorny, prickly, brambly
反义词:smooth, clear
词性:adjective
