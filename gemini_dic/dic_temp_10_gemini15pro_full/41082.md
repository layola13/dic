copperworm:/ˈkɒpəwɜːm/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpərwɜːrm/ (US)| n. |铜腹绿蝇幼虫; 螺旋蛆|The larvae of the blowfly *Lucilia cuprina*, parasitic on sheep. It's also known as the Australian sheep blowfly. |The farmer was concerned about a copperworm infestation in his flock.  农场主担心他的羊群感染了铜腹绿蝇幼虫。|Synonyms: screw-worm,  blowfly larva|Antonyms: None|Noun
