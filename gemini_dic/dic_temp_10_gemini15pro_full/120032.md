millionaires:/ˌmɪljəˈnɛərz/ | n. | 百万富翁（复数） |  A person whose assets are worth one million or more units of currency (e.g., dollars, pounds, euros). |  Many millionaires live in this exclusive neighborhood. 许多百万富翁住在这个高档社区。|  The lottery created several new millionaires overnight.  一夜之间，彩票造就了几个新的百万富翁。 |  tycoons, magnates, wealthy individuals | paupers, the poor, impoverished people | noun
