garrulousnesses:/ˈɡærələsnəsɪz/
noun
饶舌；多话；喋喋不休
The quality or state of being garrulous; excessive talkativeness.
Her garrulousnesses made it difficult to have a serious conversation. 她喋喋不休，使得难以进行严肃的对话。
Synonyms: loquacity, talkativeness, volubility, verboseness
Antonyms: reticence, taciturnity
词性: 名词


garrulous:/ˈɡærələs/
adjective
饶舌的；多话的；喋喋不休的
Excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters.
He was known for his garrulous nature and could talk for hours. 他以多话的性格而闻名，可以连续讲几个小时。
Synonyms: talkative, loquacious, voluble, verbose
Antonyms: reticent, taciturn
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Latin *garrulus*, from *garrire* "to chatter."
