intercentrum:/ˌɪntərˈsɛntrəm/
n.
intercentrum (plural intercentra)
椎间体
A small, wedge-shaped bone or cartilage located ventrally between the centra of adjacent vertebrae in some vertebrates, especially fishes and amphibians.
The intercentrum plays a significant role in the flexibility of the spine. 椎间体对脊柱的灵活性起着重要作用。
The fossil showed a well-preserved intercentrum. 这块化石显示出一个保存完好的椎间体。
Synonyms:  hypocentrum (in some contexts)
Antonyms:  centrum (in some contexts), pleurocentrum (in some contexts)
noun
