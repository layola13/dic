carbamide:/ˈkɑːrbəmaɪd/ (US), /kɑːˈbæmaɪd/ (UK)| n.|脲，尿素|A white crystalline organic compound that is the chief nitrogenous end product of protein metabolism in mammals and is the chief nitrogenous constituent of the urine. It is used as a fertilizer and in the manufacture of resins and plastics.|Carbamide is an important nitrogenous fertilizer. 尿素是一种重要的氮肥。|Synonym: urea|Antonym: N/A|noun
