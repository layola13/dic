muddly:/ˈmʌdli/ (US), /ˈmʌdi/ (UK)| adj.|混乱的，糊涂的，泥泞的|Confused and unclear; not coherent or logical. Also, covered in or full of mud.|The instructions were so muddly that no one knew what to do.  说明书太混乱了，没人知道该怎么办。|近义词：confused, unclear, vague, messy, murky, muddy|反义词：clear, lucid, coherent, tidy|词性:形容词

muddly:/ˈmʌdli/ (US), /ˈmʌdi/ (UK)| v.|使…混乱，使…糊涂，弄脏|To make something confused or unclear; to make something dirty with mud.|Her constant interruptions muddled my train of thought. 她不断的打断扰乱了我的思路。|近义词：confuse, obscure, complicate, befuddle|反义词：clarify, elucidate, simplify|词性:动词
