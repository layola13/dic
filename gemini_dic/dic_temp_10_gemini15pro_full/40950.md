cooperant:/koʊˈɑːpərənt/
adjective
合作的
Working or acting together willingly for a common purpose.
例句：The cooperant team members successfully completed the project. 合作的团队成员成功地完成了项目。

例句：The cooperant spirit among the volunteers was inspiring. 志愿者们之间的合作精神令人鼓舞。
近义词：cooperative, collaborative, joint
反义词：uncooperative, antagonistic, opposing
词性：adjective
