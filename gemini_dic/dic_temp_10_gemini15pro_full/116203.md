masking: /ˈmɑːskɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɑːskɪŋ/ (UK) | n. | 掩蔽，遮蔽 | The act of concealing something, often with a mask or other covering. |  The magician's skillful masking of the trick amazed the audience. 魔术师巧妙地掩盖了戏法，使观众惊叹不已。

The masking of the data protected the identities of the participants. 数据的掩蔽保护了参与者的身份。
