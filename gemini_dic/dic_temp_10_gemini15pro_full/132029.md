noncorroborative:/ˌnɒnkəˈrɒbəreɪtɪv/
adjective
不确证的，未经证实的
Not providing corroboration; not serving to confirm.
The alibi was noncorroborative, weakening the defendant's case.  这个不在场证明未经证实，削弱了被告的案子。
The witness provided noncorroborative testimony.  证人提供的证词未经证实。
近义词:unsubstantiated, unconfirmed, unsupported
反义词:corroborative, confirmatory, validating
词性:adjective
词源:non- (not) + corroborative (tending to confirm)
