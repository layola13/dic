drinkable:/ˈdrɪŋkəbl/| adj.|可饮用的，适于饮用的|Suitable for drinking; potable.|The tap water here is perfectly drinkable.  这里的水龙头里的水完全可以饮用。|近义词：potable, safe to drink|反义词：undrinkable, non-potable|词性:形容词
词源: From "drink" + "-able"  来自动词“drink”（喝）加上形容词后缀“-able”（可...的）
