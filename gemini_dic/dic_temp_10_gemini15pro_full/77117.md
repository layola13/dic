garvie:/ˈɡɑːrvi/| n.|小鲱鱼，鲱鱼幼鱼|A young herring.  Specifically, a herring in its second year. |例句：The fishermen brought in a net full of garvie. 渔民们带回了一网小鲱鱼。

例句：Garvie are an important food source for many seabirds. 小鲱鱼是许多海鸟的重要食物来源。|近义词：sprat, herring, whitebait|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific type of fish)|词性:noun
