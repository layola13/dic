airbags:/ˈɛərˌbæɡz/ (US), /ˈeəˌbæɡz/ (UK)| n.|安全气囊|A safety device in a vehicle consisting of a bag that inflates rapidly on collision to protect occupants.|例句：The airbags deployed on impact.  安全气囊在撞击时展开了。

例句：The airbags saved her life in the crash. 安全气囊在车祸中救了她的命。|近义词：safety cushion, air cushion|反义词: None|词性:noun
