grabbed:/ɡræbd/ | v. | 抓住，攫取，赶紧做（某事） |  past tense and past participle of "grab" meaning to take hold of something suddenly and roughly.  It also means to take or obtain something quickly. | He grabbed his coat and ran out the door. 他抓起外套，冲出门外。|  seized, snatched, clutched, grasped | released, let go | verb
