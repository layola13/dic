friskers:/ˈfrɪskərz/| n. |搜身者；进行搜身的人|People who frisk others, typically police officers searching for weapons or contraband.  Those conducting the search.|The friskers found a small knife concealed in the suspect's boot.  搜身者在嫌疑人的靴子里发现了一把小刀。|synonyms: searchers, inspectors|antonyms:  None (lack of a direct antonym; one could consider concepts like "ignorers" or "those who avoid contact" as loosely antonymous.)|noun

frisker:/ˈfrɪskər/| n. |搜身器|A device used to frisk or scan someone, often a metal detector.|The airport security used a frisker to check passengers for metallic objects. 机场安检使用搜身器检查乘客是否携带金属物品。|synonyms: metal detector, scanner|antonyms: None|noun
