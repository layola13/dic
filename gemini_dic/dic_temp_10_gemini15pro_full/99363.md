interdependency:/ˌɪntərˈdɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|相互依存，互相依赖|The interdependency of nations in a globalized world is undeniable. 在全球化的世界中，各国之间的相互依存是不可否认的。|例句：The interdependency of the ecosystem is delicate and easily disrupted. 生态系统的相互依存关系是微妙的，很容易被破坏。

例句：The project's success relies on the interdependency of various teams.  这个项目的成功依赖于各个团队的相互依存。

近义词：interrelation, interconnectedness, interdependence|反义词:independence, autonomy|词性:名词

interdependent:/ˌɪntərˈdɪˈpɛndənt/| adj.|相互依存的，互相依赖的|The two companies are interdependent on each other for resources.  这两家公司在资源方面相互依赖。|例句：The global economy has made many countries interdependent. 全球经济使许多国家相互依存。

例句：Different departments within a company are often interdependent.  公司内部的不同部门通常是相互依赖的。

近义词：interrelated, interconnected|反义词:independent, autonomous|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + dependent + -cy.  "Inter-" means "between" or "among," "dependent" means relying on something else, and "-cy" forms a noun indicating a state or quality.
