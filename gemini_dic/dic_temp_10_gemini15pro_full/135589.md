nonporous:/nɑːnˈpɔːrəs/ | adj. | 无孔的，不透气的 | Not allowing liquids or gases to pass through. |  This type of container is nonporous and will keep liquids from leaking. 这种容器是无孔的，可以防止液体泄漏。| The nonporous material is ideal for protecting the surface from water damage.  这种无孔材料是保护表面免受水损害的理想选择。 |  impermeable, impervious, airtight | porous, permeable, pervious | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + porous (having pores).  The word "porous" comes from the Latin "porus" meaning "pore."
