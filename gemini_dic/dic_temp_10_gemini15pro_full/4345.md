akinesic: /ˌeɪkaɪˈniːsɪk/ /ˌeɪkɪˈniːzɪk/
adjective
运动不能的; 无运动的
Relating to or characterized by akinesia (loss or impairment of the power of voluntary movement).  Pertaining to the study of body language and nonverbal communication, especially facial expressions and gestures.
His face was akinesic, showing no emotion. 他的脸毫无表情，没有流露出任何情绪。
The researchers studied the akinesic behavior of patients with Parkinson's disease. 研究人员研究了帕金森病患者的运动不能行为。
Near synonyms: motionless, immobile, static, unmoving
Antonyms: mobile, active, kinetic, dynamic
词性: adjective
