antigrowth:/ˌæntiˈɡroʊθ/
adjective
反增长的，抑制增长的
Opposing or hindering economic or population growth.
The antigrowth policies have stifled innovation. 这些反增长政策扼杀了创新。
近义词: anti-development, no-growth
反义词: pro-growth, growth-oriented
词性: adjective
