giver:/ˈɡɪvər/| n.|给予者，赠予者|A person who gives something, especially habitually. |She is a generous giver to charities. 她对慈善机构慷慨解囊。|近义词：donor, contributor, benefactor|反义词：receiver, taker|名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *yevere*, from Old English *giefan* (to give). Related to Dutch *gever*, German *Geber*.
