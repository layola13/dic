misreliances:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪənsɪz/ | n. | 过度依赖；错误依赖；不当信赖 | The act of relying on something or someone incorrectly or to an excessive degree.  It implies a misplaced trust or dependence that can lead to negative consequences. |  His misreliance on the faulty GPS led him astray. 他对故障GPS的过度依赖使他迷路了。


The company's misreliance on a single supplier proved disastrous when the supplier went bankrupt.  这家公司过度依赖单一供应商，结果在供应商破产时 proved disastrous. | 近义词: overdependence, overreliance, undue reliance | 反义词: independence, self-reliance | 词性: 名词 (plural)

词源: "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "reliance" (noun meaning "dependence" or "trust")
