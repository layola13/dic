drug: /drʌɡ/ | n. | 药物; 毒品 | A medicine or other substance which has a physiological effect when ingested or otherwise introduced into the body. |  She's been prescribed some strong drugs for her condition. 她被开了些强效药来治疗她的病情。

drug: /drʌɡ/ | v. |  给…下药; 掺麻醉药/毒品于 | To administer a drug to (a person or animal), especially in order to affect behavior.  |  He was drugged and robbed. 他被下了药然后遭到了抢劫。


Etymology: From Old French drogue, of uncertain origin, possibly from Middle Dutch droog "dry (goods)," from droog "dry." Originally referred to dried herbs or plants used in medicine.


Synonyms: medication, medicine, pharmaceutical, narcotic (for illegal drugs), dope (informal, often for illegal drugs), remedy, cure

Antonyms:  placebo (for medication), antidote (for poison/overdose)
