misidentify:/ˌmɪsɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ/| vt.|错误识别，误认|To incorrectly identify someone or something.|例句：The witness misidentified the suspect in the police lineup. 目击者在警方列队辨认中认错了嫌疑人。

例句：The software misidentified the object in the image as a bird.  这个软件错误地将图像中的物体识别为一只鸟。|近义词：mislabel, mistake, misrecognize|反义词：identify, recognize|词性：动词
