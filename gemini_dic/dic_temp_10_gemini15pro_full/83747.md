例句：The vineyard was completely hailstoned, ruining the harvest.  葡萄园完全被冰雹袭击，毁了收成。|近义词：battered, pelted, damaged|反义词: undamaged, protected|词性:形容词

词源: From 'hail' (Old English *hagol, from Proto-Germanic *haglaz) + '-stone' (Old English stān) + '-ed' (past participle suffix).
