consign:/kənˈsaɪn/ | vt. |把...打发到 | to put someone or something in an unpleasant place or situation |She consigned the letter to the flames. 她把信投入火中烧掉了。|Synonyms: banish, exile, dismiss |Antonyms: welcome, embrace| verb
