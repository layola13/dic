passively:/ˈpæsɪvli/| adv.|被动地；消极地；顺从地|In a passive manner; without acting or resisting.  Not actively participating.|She passively accepted his criticism.|近义词：submissively, inactively, inertly|反义词：actively, aggressively, assertively|词性:副词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French passif, -ive, from Latin passivus, from pati ‘suffer’.
