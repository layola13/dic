nondeafened:/ˌnɒnˈdɛfənd/ /ˌnɑːnˈdɛfənd/
adjective
未聋的
Not having lost the ability to hear; hearing.
例句：The study focused on the responses of nondeafened participants to various sounds.  这项研究的重点是未聋的参与者对各种声音的反应。

例句：The nondeafened control group performed significantly better on the auditory processing tasks.  未聋的对照组在听觉处理任务中表现明显更好。

近义词：hearing,  auditory
反义词：deaf, deafened
词性：adjective
