fuel-injected:/ˈfjuːəl ɪnˈdʒɛktɪd/
adjective
燃油喷射的
Supplied with fuel by a fuel injection system.
例句：This car has a fuel-injected engine. 这辆车有一个燃油喷射引擎。

例句：Fuel-injected engines are more efficient than carbureted engines. 燃油喷射式发动机比化油器式发动机更高效。

近义词：injected
反义词：carbureted (化油器的)
词性：adjective
