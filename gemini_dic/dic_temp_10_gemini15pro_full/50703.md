dermohaemal: /ˌdɜːrmoʊˈhiːməl/ | adj. | 皮肤血液的；皮血的 | Relating to the circulatory system of the skin and its connection to the main blood system.  Specifically, it refers to sinuses in the integument of invertebrates that are filled with blood and function in respiration and excretion. | The dermohaemal system is crucial for some invertebrates. 皮血系统对一些无脊椎动物至关重要。|  Synonym: cutaneous-vascular | Antonym: N/A | adjective
