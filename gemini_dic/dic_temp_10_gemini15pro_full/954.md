academician: /əˌkædɪˈmɪʃən/
n.
院士；学会会员
A member of an academy, especially a society of scholars, artists, or scientists.
He is a respected academician in the field of physics. 他是物理学界受人尊敬的院士。

The Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences selects the Nobel laureates in physics and chemistry. 瑞典皇家科学院负责遴选诺贝尔物理学奖和化学奖的获得者。

近义词: scholar, intellectual, expert, authority
反义词: amateur, layman
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *académicien*, from *académie* ("academy"), from Latin *academia*, from Ancient Greek *Ἀκαδήμεια* (*Akadḗmeia*), referring to the gymnasium where Plato taught.  Ultimately from *Ἀκάδημος* (*Akádēmos*), a mythical hero.
