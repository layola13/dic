dynamitism:/ˈdaɪnəmɪzəm/ | n. |活力，精力，动态主义|The dynamism of the city is palpable.  这座城市的活力显而易见。| dynamism is the quality of being characterized by vigorous activity and progress. It refers to a state of constant change, activity, or progress. It can describe a person, a place, a system, or even an idea. |The dynamism of the tech industry is constantly changing the world. 科技行业的活力正在不断地改变世界。| 近义词: energy, vigor, vitality, drive,  | 反义词: lethargy, inactivity, stagnation | noun
