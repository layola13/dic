augustus:/ɔːˈɡʌstəs/| n. |奥古斯都；八月|A male given name, derived from the Roman title Augustus, meaning "venerable" or "magnificent". It is also the name of the eighth month of the year.|Augustus was the first Roman emperor. 奥古斯都是第一位罗马皇帝。|近义词：Caesar, emperor|反义词:N/A|名词

augustus:/ɔːˈɡʌstəs/| adj. |威严的；雄伟的|Marked by majestic dignity or grandeur; venerable.|He had an augustus presence that commanded respect. 他有一种威严的气质，令人肃然起敬。|近义词：majestic, grand, stately, dignified|反义词:humble, modest|形容词
