cast-out: /ˈkæst aʊt/ | adj. n. | 被抛弃的，被驱逐的；流浪者 |  Driven out or rejected; a person who has been rejected or ostracized. | The cast-out prince wandered the wilderness. 被驱逐的王子在荒野中游荡。| 近义词: outcast, exile, pariah | 反义词: accepted, included | 词性: 形容词，名词

cast out: /ˈkæst aʊt/ | v. | 驱逐，抛弃 | To expel or reject someone or something. |  They cast out the traitor from their midst. 他们把叛徒从他们中间驱逐出去。 | 近义词: expel, banish, ostracize | 反义词: welcome, embrace | 词性: 动词

词源: From the phrasal verb "cast out," which combines the verb "cast" (meaning to throw or project) with the adverb "out" (meaning away or forth). The verb "cast" has Old Norse origins.
