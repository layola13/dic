buggier:/ˈbʌɡiər/ | adj. | 更多的bug，更容易出故障的 | More buggy; having more problems or defects, especially in software or computer systems. |  This new version of the software is buggier than the last one.  这个新版本的软件比上一个版本bug更多。| synonyms:  flakier, glitchier, less stable, more problematic | antonyms: more stable, more reliable, less problematic | adjective

buggier:/ˈbʌɡiər/ | adj. |  (informal) More difficult; more complicated; more troublesome.  | The terrain was buggier than we expected, making the hike much harder. 地形比我们预想的要更难走，使得徒步旅行更加艰难。 | synonyms: tougher, trickier, harder, more challenging | antonyms: easier, simpler, less challenging | adjective
