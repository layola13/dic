encomium:/ɛnˈkəʊmiəm/ /ɛnˈkoʊmiəm/| n. |赞美; 颂词; 赞扬|A formal expression of high praise; eulogy.  A speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly.|The retiring CEO was showered with encomiums for his contributions to the company.  退休的首席执行官因其对公司的贡献而获得了大量的赞誉。|Synonyms: tribute, praise, accolade, panegyric, laudation|Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure|noun
