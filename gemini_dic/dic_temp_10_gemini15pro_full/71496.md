flange:/flændʒ/
noun|法兰；凸缘；边缘|A projecting rim, edge, or rib, especially one for strengthening or attachment or for forming a flange joint.|The pipe is connected to the tank by a flange.|近义词:rim, edge, lip, brim|反义词:center, core|词性:n.

flange:/flændʒ/
verb|给…装上凸缘；用凸缘连接|To furnish with a flange; to connect by means of flanges.|The pipes were flanged together.|近义词:join, connect, attach|反义词:separate, detach, disconnect|词性:v.
