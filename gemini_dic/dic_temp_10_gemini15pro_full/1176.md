acclimating: /əˈklaɪmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
适应环境；驯化
The process of becoming accustomed to a new climate or environment.
The climbers spent a few days acclimating to the high altitude before attempting the summit.  登山者在尝试登顶之前花了几天空闲时间来适应高海拔。

It took him a while to acclimate to the hot and humid weather. 他花了一段时间才适应湿热的天气。

近义词: adapting, adjusting, habituating
反义词: unacclimating


词性: verb (present participle)


词源: From French acclimater, from à (to) + climat (climate). Ultimately from Greek klima (region, slope, zone).
