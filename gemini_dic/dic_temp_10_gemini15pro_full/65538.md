euthanasia:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ | n. | 安乐死| The act of putting to death painlessly or allowing to die, as by withholding extreme medical measures, a person or animal suffering from an incurable, especially a painful, disease or condition. |  He supported euthanasia for patients in intractable pain. 他支持对难以忍受疼痛的病人实施安乐死。| synonyms: assisted dying, physician-assisted suicide | antonyms:  preservation of life | noun
