ascribable:/əˈskraɪbəbl/
adjective
可归因的；可归咎的
able to be ascribed to a particular cause or source.
The success of the project is ascribable to the hard work of the team. 这个项目的成功归功于团队的辛勤工作。
Synonyms: attributable, imputable, creditable, assignable
Antonyms: unascribable, non-attributable
词性: adjective
