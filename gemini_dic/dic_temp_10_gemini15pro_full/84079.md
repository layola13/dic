half-importantly:/ˌhæf ɪmˈpɔːrtəntli/
adv.
一半重要地；不太重要地
Modifying an adverb or adjective to indicate a lesser degree of importance.  It suggests that something is important, but perhaps not as crucial as other factors.
He mentioned, half-importantly, that he had once met the Queen.  他不太重要地提了一句他曾经见过女王。
Synonyms: slightly importantly, moderately importantly, not very importantly
Antonyms: very importantly, crucially, essentially
词性: 副词
