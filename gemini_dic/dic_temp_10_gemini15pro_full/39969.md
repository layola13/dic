consubstantial:/ˌkɑːnsəbˈstænʃəl/ | adj. | 同体的，同质的，同本质的 | Having the same substance or essence; of the same nature. | The doctrine of the Trinity holds that the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit are consubstantial.  三位一体的教义认为圣父、圣子和圣灵是同体的。| The Nicene Creed states that Jesus is consubstantial with the Father. 尼西亚信经声明耶稣与圣父同体。 | synonyms: coessential, homoousian | antonyms: heterogeneous, dissimilar | adjective
