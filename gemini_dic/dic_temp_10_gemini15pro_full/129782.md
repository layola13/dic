non-fiction:/ˌnɒnˈfɪkʃn/ | n. | 非虚构作品；纪实文学 | Prose writing that is based on facts, real events, and real people, such as biography or history. |  I prefer reading non-fiction to fiction. 我更喜欢读非虚构作品而不是小说。|  Non-fiction books can be very informative. 非虚构类书籍可以提供很多信息。 | synonyms: factual writing, documentary, factual prose | antonyms: fiction, fantasy, fabrication | noun
