outdistance:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstəns/| vt.|超越，把…远远抛在后面|To leave someone or something far behind in a race or competition, or to be much more successful than someone or something.|The Kenyan runner easily outdistanced his rivals in the marathon. 肯尼亚选手在马拉松比赛中轻松超越了他的对手们。|The company has outdistanced all its competitors in terms of sales.  这家公司在销售方面已经远远超过了所有竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, outrun, exceed, outstrip|反义词：lag behind, trail|词性：动词

Etymology:
From out- + distance.  The prefix "out-" means exceeding or surpassing.  "Distance" refers to the space between two points.  Therefore, "outdistance" literally means to exceed the distance covered by someone else.
