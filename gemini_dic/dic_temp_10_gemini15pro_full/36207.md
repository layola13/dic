cogitabund:/ˈkɒdʒɪtəbʌnd/ | adj. | 深思熟虑的；沉思的 | Given to thought; thoughtful, meditative. | He sat in his chair, cogitabund, pondering the complexities of the situation. 他坐在椅子上，深思熟虑，思考着局势的复杂性。|  She had a cogitabund expression as she listened to the lecture.  她听讲座时表情若有所思。 |近义词: pensive, thoughtful, meditative, contemplative, reflective | 反义词: unthinking, impulsive, careless | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *cogitabundus*, present participle of *cogitabundare* "to ponder," from *cogitare* "to think," frequentative of *cogere* "to compel, collect."
