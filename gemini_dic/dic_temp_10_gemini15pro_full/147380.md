overnervous:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrvəs/| adj.|过度紧张的，过分焦虑的|Excessively nervous; extremely anxious.  Characterized by excessive or undue nervousness.|She was overnervous about her job interview. 她对她的工作面试过度紧张。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, jittery, agitated, stressed, tense|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed, tranquil|词性:形容词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + nervous (adjective meaning "easily agitated or alarmed")
