bruisingly:/ˈbruːzɪŋli/
bruisingly| adv.|极其残酷地，非常伤人地|In a way that inflicts or suggests emotional or physical injury; harshly.|The election was bruisingly close. 选举结果异常胶着。|近义词：harshly, cruelly, severely, ruthlessly|反义词：gently, kindly, mildly|词性:副词
词源: From bruise (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Bruise" comes from Old French "bruisier," meaning "to crush," ultimately from a Vulgar Latin word *brusiare.
