adjustage: /əˈdʒʌstɪdʒ/
noun
调整；调解；理算(尤指保险)
The act of adjusting or the state of being adjusted; settlement, especially of an insurance claim.
The adjustage of the insurance claim took several weeks.  理赔的调整花了几个星期。
The adjustage of the brakes is crucial for safety.  刹车的调整对安全至关重要。
synonyms: adjustment, regulation, settlement, reconciliation
antonyms:  disagreement, discord, imbalance
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From adjust + -age.  Adjust comes from Old French ajuster "to arrange, order, prepare," from à "to" (from Latin ad) + juste "just, proper" (from Latin iustus).  The suffix -age is from French, used to form nouns of action or result.
