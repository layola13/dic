deerlike:/ˈdɪrˌlaɪk/
adjective
像鹿的;似鹿的;
Resembling a deer, especially in grace or gentleness.
The deerlike creature bounded across the field.  这像鹿一样的生物跳跃着穿过田野。
The child, with her large, gentle eyes, had a deerlike quality.  这个孩子有着一双温柔的大眼睛，气质像鹿一样。
近义词:graceful, gentle, timid, shy
反义词:clumsy, awkward, aggressive, bold
词性:adjective

词源:
deer + like.  'deer' comes from Old English 'deor', meaning 'animal'. 'like' comes from Old English 'lic', meaning 'having the appearance or form of'.
