conciliative: /kənˈsɪliətɪv/ | adj. | 调解的，安抚的，愿意和解的 | Intended or likely to placate or pacify. |  The company adopted a conciliative approach to the disgruntled employees. 公司对不满的员工采取了安抚的态度。| synonyms: appeasing, pacifying, placatory, mollifying, soothing, calming | antonyms: antagonistic, aggressive, hostile, belligerent, confrontational | adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *conciliativus, from conciliare "to bring together, win over," from concilium "council, assembly," from com- "together" (see com-) + calare "to call, summon" (see calendar).
