hyperemetic:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmɛtɪk/
adjective
过度呕吐的
Characterized by or causing excessive vomiting.
She experienced hyperemetic episodes during her pregnancy.  她在怀孕期间经历了过度呕吐的症状。
The chemotherapy caused hyperemetic side effects. 化疗引起了过度呕吐的副作用。
近义词: excessively emetic
反义词: none
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek 'over, excessive') + emetic (from Greek emetikos 'causing vomiting').
