aldermanship:/ˈɔːldərmənʃɪp/
n.
市議員的職位；市政委員的職位
The office or dignity of an alderman.
His ambition was to achieve aldermanship. 他的抱负是成为市议员。
近义词: councilorship, membership (on a council)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ealdormann-scipe, from ealdorman (alderman) + -scipe (ship, state, condition).  Ultimately from eald (old) + man.
