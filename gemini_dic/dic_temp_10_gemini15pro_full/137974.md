nonylenic:/ˌnɒnɪˈliːnɪk/ | adj. |壬烯的；壬烯酸的| Relating to or derived from nonylene, a colorless liquid alkene with nine carbon atoms. | Nonylenic acid is a fatty acid found in some plants. 壬烯酸是在某些植物中发现的一种脂肪酸。| Nonylenic derivatives are used in the production of certain fragrances. 壬烯衍生物用于生产某些香水。|近义词: unsaturated (in the chemical sense) |反义词: saturated (in the chemical sense) |词性: adjective
