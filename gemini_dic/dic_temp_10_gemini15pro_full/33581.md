ciliiform:/ˈsɪliəˌfɔːrm/ /ˈsɪliəˌfɔːrm/
adjective
纤毛状的，有纤毛的
Resembling cilia; having the form or appearance of cilia.  Cilia are tiny hair-like structures on the surface of some cells, used for movement or sensing.
The paramecium is a ciliiform protozoan. 草履虫是一种纤毛状原生动物。
近义词:ciliated, fimbriate
反义词:None
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ciliformis, from cilium ("eyelash, eyelid, small hair") + -formis ("-shaped").
