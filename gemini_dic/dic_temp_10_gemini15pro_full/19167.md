beweep:/bɪˈwiːp/
verb
中文翻译：恸哭，哀悼
英文解析：To weep over someone or something; to mourn or lament.
例句：She beweeped the loss of her beloved pet. 她为失去心爱的宠物而恸哭。

例句：The entire nation beweeped the death of their leader. 整个国家都在哀悼他们领袖的去世。

近义词：mourn, lament, grieve, cry over, sob over
反义词：rejoice, celebrate
词性：v.
