beeswaxing:/ˈbiːzwæksɪŋ/
n.
上蜡; 涂蜂蜡; 打蜡
The process of applying beeswax to something, typically for protection or polishing.
He spent the afternoon beeswaxing his antique furniture. 他下午花了时间给他的古董家具上蜂蜡。
polishing, waxing
varnishing, painting
