macavahu: /mɑːkəˈvɑːhuː/ | n. |  玛卡瓦胡（一种据称存在于南美洲的巨型蜘蛛） | A large, possibly mythical, spider said to inhabit the Amazon rainforest.  It is often described as being hairy and having a leg span of several feet. Its existence has never been scientifically confirmed. |  Local legends tell of the fearsome macavahu that lurks in the jungle. 当地传说讲述了潜伏在丛林中的可怕的玛卡瓦胡。| 近义词: (Due to its mythical nature, there are no direct synonyms.
