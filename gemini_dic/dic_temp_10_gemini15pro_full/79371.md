gliadin:/ˈɡlaɪədɪn/ | n. |麦醇溶蛋白|A protein present in wheat and other cereals that is insoluble in water and soluble in dilute alcohol. It contributes to the elastic properties of dough and can trigger celiac disease in susceptible individuals. |例句：Gliadin is a component of gluten. 麦醇溶蛋白是谷蛋白的一个组成部分。

例句：People with celiac disease must avoid foods containing gliadin. 乳糜泻患者必须避免食用含有麦醇溶蛋白的食物。 |近义词：glutenin(麦谷蛋白,两者共同构成麸质gluten)|反义词: None|词性:noun
