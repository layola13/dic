niellist: /ˈniːlɪst/ | n. | 虚无主义者 | A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles. |  He was a nihilist who believed in nothing. 他是一个什么都不信的虚无主义者。

| Example: The nihilist argued that all values are baseless and that nothing can be known or communicated.  这个虚无主义者认为所有的价值观都是毫无根据的，没有什么可以被认知或传达。| Synonym: cynic, pessimist, skeptic | Antonym: optimist, believer | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Nihilist*, from Latin *nihil* (“nothing”) + *-ist*.
