anticivic:/ˌæntiˈsɪvɪk/
adjective
反公民的；危害公民的；反社会(公德)的
Opposing or harmful to the interests of a community or the duties of citizenship.
His anticivic behavior led to his arrest. 他的反公民行为导致他被捕。
Near Synonyms: antisocial, uncivic, undemocratic, subversive, lawless, rebellious
Antonyms: civic, patriotic, law-abiding, prosocial
词性: 形容词
