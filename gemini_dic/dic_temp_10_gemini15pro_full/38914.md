confessionalism: /kənˈfɛʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
忏悔主义；教派主义
A system of government in which different religious groups are given political power and representation.  A principle or system of religious belief emphasizing the importance of confession of sins.
The country's political system was based on confessionalism, with different religious communities having their own representatives in parliament.  这个国家的政治体制建立在教派主义之上，不同的宗教团体在议会中有自己的代表。
His confessionalism led him to believe that open admission of his failings was the only path to redemption. 他的忏悔主义使他相信，公开承认自己的缺点是救赎的唯一途径。

近义词: sectarianism, denominationalism
反义词: secularism, nonsectarianism
词性: noun
