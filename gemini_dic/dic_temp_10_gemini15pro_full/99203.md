interclerical:/ˌɪntərˈklɛrɪkəl/
adjective
神职人员之间的; 教士间的
relating to matters between members of the clergy
例句：Interclerical rivalry sometimes surfaces in church politics. 教士间的竞争有时会在教会政治中显露出来。

例句：The interclerical discussions focused on the new liturgical practices. 教士间的讨论集中在新礼拜仪式上。

近义词：priestly, clerical, ecclesiastical
反义词：lay, secular
词性：adjective
