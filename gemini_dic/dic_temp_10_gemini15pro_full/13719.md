atwixt:/əˈtwɪkst/| prep. adv.|在...之间；在两者之间|Between; in the midst of.  It describes something situated between two things, often implying a close or narrow space, or a state of being in between two conditions.|The house sits atwixt two hills. 房子坐落在两座小山之间。|近义词：between, betwixt, amidst|反义词:outside, beyond|词性:介词, 副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *atwixen, from Old English *betwuxn, from Proto-Germanic *bitwix, from Proto-Indo-European *du̯ih₁- (“two”). Cognate with between.
