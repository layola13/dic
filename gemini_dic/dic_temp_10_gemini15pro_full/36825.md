colonial:/kəˈloʊniəl/| adj.|殖民的，殖民地的|Relating to or characteristic of a colony or colonies.|The colonial period in America lasted for several centuries. 美国的殖民时期持续了几个世纪。|Example:  Many colonial buildings are still standing in the historic district. 许多殖民时期的建筑物仍然屹立在历史街区。|Synonyms: imperial, dominion, dependent|Antonyms: independent, sovereign|adjective
