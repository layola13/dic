outfool:/aʊtˈfuːl/
verb
智胜；愚弄；胜过
to fool or trick someone more effectively than they fool or trick you
He tried to outfool me, but I was too clever for him. 他想骗我，但我比他更聪明。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, deceive, trick
反义词: be fooled, be tricked
