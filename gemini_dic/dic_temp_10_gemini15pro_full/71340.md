flabelliform:/fləˈbɛlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
扇形的；扇状的
Shaped like a fan.
The peacock displayed its flabelliform tail. 孔雀展示了它扇形的尾巴。

The organism had flabelliform appendages.  这个生物体有扇形的附属物。

近义词:fan-shaped, flabellate
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes unlike a fan, e.g., linear, circular)
词性: adjective
