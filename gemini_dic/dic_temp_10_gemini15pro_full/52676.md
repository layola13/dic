diglossic:/daɪˈɡlɒsɪk/| adj.|双语的，双言的|Relating to a situation where two distinct varieties of a language are used in a community, one for high (formal) contexts and one for low (informal) contexts.|The diglossic situation in this country is fascinating, with the formal language used in official settings and the informal language used in everyday life. 这个国家的双语现象很吸引人，正式语言用于官方场合，非正式语言用于日常生活。|近义词：bilingual, multilingual|反义词：monolingual|词性:形容词

diglossia:/daɪˈɡlɒsiə/| n.|双语现象|A situation in which two distinct varieties of a language are used in a community, one for high (formal) contexts and one for low (informal) contexts.|Diglossia is common in many parts of the world. 双语现象在世界很多地方都很常见。|近义词：bilingualism, multilingualism|反义词：monolingualism|词性:名词
