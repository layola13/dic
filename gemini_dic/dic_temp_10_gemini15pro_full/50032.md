denotable:/dɪˈnoʊtəbl/| adj.|可表示的，可指示的|Something that is denotable can be referred to or designated by a name or symbol.  It means capable of being denoted or signified.|The specific features of the artifact are denotable by symbols.  这些文物的具体特征可以用符号表示。|近义词：referable, designable, nameable, expressible|反义词：unnameable, ineffable|词性:形容词
词源: From denote + -able.  Denote comes from the Latin dēnotāre, meaning "to mark out, indicate."
