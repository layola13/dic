interlanguages:/ˌɪntərˈlæŋɡwɪdʒɪz/
| n. | 中介语；过渡语 | A language system created by a second-language learner who is still in the process of learning the target language. It reflects the learner's current understanding of the target language rules and may contain features of both their native language and the target language. |  Learners' interlanguages are constantly evolving as they acquire more knowledge of the target language. 学习者的中介语随着他们获得更多目标语言的知识而不断发展。 | synonyms: learner language, transitional competence | antonyms: native language, target language | noun
