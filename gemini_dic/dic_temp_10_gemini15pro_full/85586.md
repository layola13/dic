hatrack:/ˈhætˌræk/| n. |帽架；衣帽架|A framework or stand for hanging hats and often coats on.|I hung my coat on the hatrack. 我把我的外套挂在衣帽架上。|近义词：coat stand, coat rack, clothes stand|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps floor if hats are thrown there instead)|noun
