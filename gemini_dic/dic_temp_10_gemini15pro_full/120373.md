minion:/ˈmɪnjən/| n.| 奴才，仆从，小黄人 |A follower or underling, especially a servile or unimportant one.  Also, a mischievous subordinate, often used playfully.  More recently, refers to the small, yellow creatures from the "Despicable Me" franchise.|The evil villain had an army of minions to do his bidding.  这个邪恶的反派有一群奴才听他差遣。

The mischievous minion tripped his boss accidentally.  这个调皮的部下不小心绊倒了他的老板。

He refers to his eager assistants as his minions.  他把他那些热心的助手称为他的小黄人。 |Synonyms: henchman, follower, servant, underling, subordinate |Antonyms: leader, boss, master |noun
