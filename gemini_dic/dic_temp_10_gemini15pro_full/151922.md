pasteurize:/ˈpæstʃəraɪz/ /ˈpɑːstəraɪz/| v. | 巴氏消毒，加热杀菌 | to heat a liquid, especially milk, to a specific temperature for a specific period in order to kill harmful bacteria but without changing the flavour or nutritional value. | 例句：The milk is pasteurized to kill harmful bacteria.  牛奶经过巴氏消毒以杀死有害细菌。| 近义词：sterilize, disinfect | 反义词: contaminate | 词性: 动词
