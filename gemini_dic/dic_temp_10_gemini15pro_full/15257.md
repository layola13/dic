backhander:/ˈbækˌhændər/ (UK) /ˈbækˌhændər/ (US)| n.|暗中行贿，回扣，不正当的钱|A sum of money or other payment given, typically secretly, in return for a favor or service.|He received a backhander for making sure the contract went to the right company. 他收受了贿赂，确保合同落到了合适的公司。|Synonyms: bribe, kickback, payoff, sweetener|Antonyms: legitimate payment, honest dealing|Noun

backhander:/ˈbækˌhændər/ (UK) /ˈbækˌhændər/ (US)| n.|反手击球|A blow or stroke made with the back of the hand or with a racket held with the back of the hand facing the direction of movement.|He hit a powerful backhander across the court. 他在球场上打了一个强有力的反手球。|Synonyms: backhand stroke, backhand shot|Antonyms: forehand|Noun
