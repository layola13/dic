accomplice: /əˈkɑːmplɪs/ /əˈkʌmplɪs/
noun
同谋，共犯
A person who helps someone else to commit a crime or do something wrong.
He was convicted as an accomplice to murder. 他被判为谋杀案的同谋。

The getaway driver was arrested and charged as an accomplice. 逃跑的司机被逮捕并被指控为同谋。

近义词: accessory, abettor, collaborator, partner in crime
反义词: opponent, adversary, enemy
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acomplicier, based on Latin ad + complere "to fulfill."  Originally meant "to complete," later evolving to mean "to make complete by adding something," hence "to be an accomplice."
