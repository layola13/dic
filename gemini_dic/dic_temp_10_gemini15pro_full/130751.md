nonamendment:/ˌnɒnəˈmɛndmənt/
noun
非修正案；未修正的内容
Something that is not an amendment; the state of not being amended.
The nonamendment of the constitution led to further disputes.  宪法的未修正导致了进一步的争议。
The committee's decision resulted in the nonamendment of the proposal. 委员会的决定导致该提案未被修正。
Near Antonyms: amendment, alteration, revision
Antonyms: modification, change
