hurriedly:/ˈhʌrɪdli/ | adv. | 匆忙地，急忙地 | In a hurried manner; quickly and without taking much care. | She hurriedly packed her bags and left the house. 她匆忙地收拾行李离开了房子。|  He hurriedly finished his lunch and rushed to the meeting. 他匆匆吃完午饭，赶去开会。 | 近义词: hastily, rapidly, swiftly, quickly | 反义词: slowly, leisurely, deliberately | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "hurried" + the adverbial suffix "-ly."  "Hurried" comes from the verb "hurry," which has its roots in Middle English *hurrien, likely of imitative origin.
