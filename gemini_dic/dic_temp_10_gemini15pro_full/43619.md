create:/kriˈeɪt/ | vt. | 创建，创造，创作；引起，产生 | To bring something into existence; to cause something new to exist; to produce or give rise to something. | God created the world. 上帝创造了世界。

The artist created a beautiful sculpture. 艺术家创作了一座美丽的雕塑。 | synonyms: make, produce, generate, originate, invent, devise, form, fashion, build, construct | antonyms: destroy, demolish, ruin, eradicate | verb
