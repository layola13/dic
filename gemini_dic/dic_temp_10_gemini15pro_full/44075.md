crinoidal:/kraɪˈnɔɪdəl/
adj.
海百合的；似海百合的
Relating to or resembling a crinoid (sea lily).
The fossil record is rich with crinoidal remains.  化石记录中富含海百合遗骸。
The intricate, branching structure was clearly crinoidal in origin.  这种复杂的、分支的结构显然起源于海百合。
近义词:  crinoid,  lily-like
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "non-crinoidal" or words describing dissimilar organisms like "coral-like")
词性: adjective


crinoidal:/kraɪˈnɔɪdəl/
n.
海百合类动物
An animal belonging to the class Crinoidea. (Less common usage)
The divers observed several crinoidals swaying gently in the current. 潜水员观察到几只海百合在水流中轻轻摇摆。(Less common)
The museum's collection included a variety of crinoidals from different geological periods.  博物馆的收藏包括来自不同地质时期的各种海百合。(Less common)
近义词: crinoid
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun (Less common usage)



词源: From Modern Latin *Crinoidea*, from Greek *krinon* "lily" + *-eides* "having the form of."
