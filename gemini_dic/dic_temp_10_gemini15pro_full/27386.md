chameleon:/kəˈmiːliən/ | n. |变色龙；善变的人|A lizard that changes skin color, especially to camouflage itself.  A person who changes their opinions or behavior according to the situation.|The chameleon blended perfectly with the leaves. 变色龙完美地融入了树叶中。|Synonyms: lizard, adapter, conformist |Antonyms: nonconformist, individualist|noun

chameleon:/kəˈmiːliən/ | adj. |善变的；反复无常的|Adaptable or readily changing.|He has a chameleon-like ability to fit in with any group. 他有一种变色龙般的适应能力，可以融入任何群体。|Synonyms: adaptable, versatile, changeable |Antonyms: inflexible, rigid, unadaptable|adjective
