nonmalignancies: /ˌnɒnˌməˈlɪɡnənsiz/ | n. | 非恶性肿瘤 |  The plural form of nonmalignancy, referring to medical conditions that are not cancerous and do not spread aggressively. |  The doctor assured her that all the growths were nonmalignancies. 医生向她保证所有的增生物都是非恶性的。 |  benign tumors, noncancerous growths | malignant tumors, cancers | noun


Etymology:

* non-:  Latin prefix meaning "not"
* malign-: from Latin malignus "ill-disposed, bad," from male "badly" + -gnus "born"
* -ancy: suffix forming nouns of state or quality
* -s: plural suffix
