distributable:/dɪˈstrɪbjʊtəbl/
adj.
可分配的；可散发的；可分发的
Capable of being distributed or apportioned.
The profits are distributable among the shareholders. 这些利润可在股东之间分配。
近义词: allocable, apportionable, divisible
反义词: indivisible, non-distributable
词性: adjective
词源: From distribute + -able. Distribute comes from Latin distribuere, from dis- "apart" + tribuere "to allot, assign."
