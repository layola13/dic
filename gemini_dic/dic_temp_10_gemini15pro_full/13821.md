auditioners:/ɔːˈdɪʃənərz/
n.
试镜者，参加试演的人
People who try out for a part in a play, movie, or other performance.
The auditioners waited nervously for their turn to perform.  试镜者紧张地等待着他们的表演机会。
The director was impressed by the talent of the auditioners.  导演对试镜者的才能印象深刻。
synonyms:  candidates, applicants, aspirants, hopefuls
antonyms:  selectors, judges, assessors
noun
