contributive:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/
adjective
贡献的，起作用的
tending to contribute to something; contributory.  Relating to or resulting from contribution.
Her contributive efforts to the project were greatly appreciated. 她对这个项目的贡献得到了高度赞赏。
synonyms: contributory, conducive, helpful, instrumental
antonyms: obstructive, hindering, detrimental
词性: adjective
