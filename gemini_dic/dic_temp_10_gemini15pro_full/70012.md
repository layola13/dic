fetidly:/ˈfetɪdli/ | adv. | 发臭地，恶臭地 |  In a fetid manner; offensively smelling.  Describes something that emits a strong, unpleasant smell. | The garbage fetidly filled the alleyway. 垃圾恶臭地弥漫着整个小巷。| The stagnant water fetidly permeated the air.  stagnant water fetidly permeated the air. 停滞不动的水散发着恶臭，弥漫在空气中。 | synonym: offensively, putridly, rankly, foully | antonym: fragrantly, sweetly, aromatically
词性: 副词

词源: From Latin *fetidus*, from *fetēre* "to stink."
