nonfatal:/ˌnɑːnˈfeɪtl/ /ˌnɒnˈfeɪtl/| adj.|非致命的|Not causing or resulting in death; not fatal.|The accident was nonfatal, thankfully.  谢天谢地，这起事故没有造成人员死亡。| The injury was nonfatal but serious.  伤势不致命，但很严重。 | 近义词:nonlethal, harmless, innocuous|反义词:fatal, lethal, deadly|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + fatal.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "fatal" comes from the Latin word "fatalis," meaning "destined" or "fated," ultimately from "fatum," meaning "fate."
