dissemblance:/dɪˈsɛmbləns/ | n. |不像，差异，伪装|lack of resemblance; difference; disguise|  The dissemblance between the twins was remarkable. 这对双胞胎之间的差异很明显。|  He maintained a dissemblance of calm, despite his inner turmoil. 尽管内心很乱，但他仍然保持着一种平静的伪装。|近义词：difference, disparity, distinction, dissimilitude, disguise, pretense |反义词：resemblance, similarity, likeness, similitude |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *dissemblaunce, from Anglo-Norman dissemblaunce, from dissembler (“to dissemble”), from dis- (“not”) + sembler (“to seem, resemble”), from Latin similis (“like”).
