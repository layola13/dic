consonantism:/ˈkɒnsənəntɪzəm/ | n. |辅音音系；辅音体系|The system of consonants used in a particular language or by a particular speaker.  The study of consonants and their patterns.|The consonantism of Arabic is very different from that of English.|近义词: consonant system, consonant structure|反义词: vocalism|词性: noun
