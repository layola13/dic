mumbles:/ˈmʌmbəlz/ (British), /ˈmʌmblz/ (American)| v.| 喃喃自语；咕哝；含糊地说| To speak in a low, indistinct manner, making it difficult for others to understand.| He mumbles when he's nervous. 他紧张的时候会喃喃自语。|
|近义词：mutters, murmurs|反义词：articulates, enunciates|词性：动词|

mumble:/ˈmʌmbəl/ (British), /ˈmʌmbəl/ (American)| n.| 喃喃自语；咕哝；含糊不清的话| A low, indistinct utterance.| I couldn't hear her mumble. 我听不清她的咕哝。|
|近义词：mutter, murmur|反义词：articulation, enunciation|词性：名词|
