hyoliths:/ˈhaɪəˌlɪθs/
n.
hyoliths(复数)；笔石纲动物；海笔石
An extinct group of marine invertebrates, abundant in the Paleozoic era, characterized by a conical shell.
Hyoliths are an extinct group of marine animals.  海笔石是一组已灭绝的海洋动物。
Hyoliths fossils are found in rocks from the Paleozoic era.  在古生代的岩石中发现了海笔石化石。
近义词: Paleozoic fossils, Cambrian fossils
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "extant species")
词性: noun
