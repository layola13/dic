beemaster:/ˈbiːˌmæstər/
noun
养蜂人；蜜蜂专家
A person who keeps bees, especially for commercial purposes or as a skilled hobbyist.
The beemaster carefully inspected each hive for signs of disease. 养蜂人仔细检查每个蜂箱，寻找疾病的迹象。
The beemaster extracted the honey with a centrifugal machine.  养蜂人用离心机提取蜂蜜。
Synonyms: apiarist, beekeeper
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bee exterminator")
