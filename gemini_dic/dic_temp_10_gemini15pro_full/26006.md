Burundians: /bʊˈrʊndiənz/ | n. | 布隆迪人 | People from Burundi, a country in East Africa. |  Many Burundians speak Kirundi. 许多布隆迪人说基隆迪语。| Synonyms:  Burundi citizens, people of Burundi | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

Etymology:  From "Burundi" + "-an" (suffix denoting nationality or origin). "Burundi" itself likely derives from the Bantu language Kirundi, meaning "land of those who speak Rundi."
