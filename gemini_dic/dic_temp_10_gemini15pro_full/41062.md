copo:/ˈkoʊpoʊ/ (US), /ˈkɒpəʊ/ (UK)| n. |杯子; (尤指盛放冰淇淋、甜点等的) 高脚杯|A stemmed glass, especially one used for ice cream, desserts, or other treats. |She ate her dessert from a tall, elegant copo. 她用一个高雅的高脚杯吃了她的甜点。|Near synonyms: goblet, glass, cup, chalice|Antonyms: bowl, plate|noun
