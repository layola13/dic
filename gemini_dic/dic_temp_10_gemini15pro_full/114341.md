majestically:/məˈdʒɛstɪkli/| adv.|雄伟地，庄严地，壮丽地|In a manner befitting royalty or great dignity; with imposing grandeur.  Having the qualities of majesty; grand; stately; dignified.|The eagle soared majestically through the sky.  这只鹰雄伟地在空中翱翔。|近义词：grandly, stately, magnificently, impressively, nobly|反义词：humbly, simply, plainly|词性:副词
词源: From majestic + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin majestatem.
