litho:/ˈlɪθoʊ/| adj. n.|石版印刷的，石版画|Relating to or produced by lithography; a lithograph.|例句：The museum has a large collection of 19th-century lithos.  博物馆收藏了大量的19世纪石版画。

例句：The litho process is still used by some artists today.  石版印刷工艺至今仍被一些艺术家使用。|近义词：print, engraving|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑digital print 数字印刷)|词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *lithos* meaning "stone".
