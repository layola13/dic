life-size:/ˈlaɪf saɪz/
adjective
与真人一样大小的，实物大小的
Of the same size as the person or thing represented.
The museum has a life-size replica of a blue whale.  博物馆里有一个与真实蓝鲸一样大小的复制品。
The children were excited to see the life-size dinosaur skeleton. 孩子们兴奋地看到了与实物一样大小的恐龙骨架。

近义词:full-scale, real-size
反义词:miniature, small-scale
词性:adjective
