mispriced:/ˌmɪsˈpraɪst/
adjective
定价错误的；估价不当的
incorrectly priced; either too high or too low
The stock market is volatile, and some stocks are inevitably mispriced. 股票市场波动不定，一些股票不可避免地被错误定价。
The house was clearly mispriced, and we got a great deal. 这房子显然定价错误，我们捡了个大便宜。
Synonyms: overvalued, undervalued, wrongly priced
Antonyms: correctly priced, fairly valued
词性: 形容词
