nonhypnotics:/ˌnɒnˌhɪpˈnɒtɪks/ /ˌnɑːnˌhɪpˈnɑːtɪks/| n.| 非安眠药，非催眠药 |Substances that do not induce sleep or hypnosis.  These can include medications for other purposes that don't have sleep-inducing side effects. |Patients with sleep problems may benefit from nonhypnotics that address underlying anxiety or pain. 患者睡眠问题可能受益于解决潜在焦虑或疼痛的非安眠药。 |Synonyms: non-sedatives, non-soporifics |Antonyms: hypnotics, sedatives, soporifics| noun
