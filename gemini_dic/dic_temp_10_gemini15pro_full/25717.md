bunted: /ˈbʌntɪd/ (US), /ˈbʌntɪd/ (UK) | v. | 触击，轻击（棒球）；用头撞 | To deliberately hit a baseball lightly so that it rolls slowly in the infield.  To push or hit something with your head. |  He bunted the ball down the first base line. 他触击球到一垒线。 The goat bunted me in the leg. 山羊用头撞了我的腿。 | 近义词： tapped, nudged (for baseball), butted (for hitting with head)| 反义词: slugged, smashed (for baseball) | 词性: 动词
