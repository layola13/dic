bicallous: /baɪˈkæləs/
adjective
硬皮的；长茧的
having two hard, thickened areas (calluses)
Example: The bicallous hands of the blacksmith showed years of hard work.  铁匠那双长满老茧的手显示出他多年辛勤劳作。

Example:  The guitarist's bicallous fingertips allowed him to play for hours without pain.  吉他手的老茧指尖让他可以连续弹几个小时而不会感到疼痛。
synonym: calloused, hardened, leathery
antonym: soft, smooth, tender
word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "bi-" (two) + "callus" (hardened skin).
