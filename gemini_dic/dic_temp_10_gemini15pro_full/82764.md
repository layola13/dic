guillemot:/ˈɡɪləmɒt/ (UK) /ˈɡɪləˌmɑːt/ (US)| n. |海鸠|A small, short-winged, black-and-white seabird of northern waters, related to the auks.|例句：We saw a flock of guillemots diving for fish near the cliffs. 我们看到一群海鸠在悬崖附近潜水捕鱼。

例句：The guillemot colony nested on the narrow ledges of the island. 海鸠群在岛上狭窄的岩架上筑巢。|近义词：auk, murre|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *guillemot*, possibly from Guillaume, a common French personal name, perhaps because the bird's call resembles this name.
