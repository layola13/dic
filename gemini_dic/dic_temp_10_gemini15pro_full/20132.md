bipartitions:/ˌbaɪpɑːrˈtɪʃənz/ | n. |二分法，二分，划分|The act of dividing something into two parts, or the resulting division. |The bipartition of Cyprus has caused lasting political tension. 塞浦路斯的二分法造成了持久的政治紧张局势。|  The algorithm creates multiple bipartitions of the graph.  该算法创建了图的多个二分法。| synonyms: division, partition, dichotomy | antonyms: union, merging, integration | noun
