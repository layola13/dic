disveil:/dɪsˈveɪl/
verb
揭露，揭开，使显露
to uncover or reveal something that was hidden or secret
The company finally disveiled its new electric car.  该公司终于公布了其新款电动汽车。
The documentary disveiled the truth about the corruption scandal. 这部纪录片揭露了腐败丑闻的真相。
近义词: reveal, uncover, expose, unveil
反义词: conceal, hide, cover, mask
词性: verb

词源:
From dis- + veil, from Old French desveler, from des- (expressing reversal) + veler "to veil," from Latin velare "to cover, veil," from velum "sail, curtain, veil."
