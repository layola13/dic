overdoubts: /ˌoʊvərˈdaʊts/
verb
过度怀疑；怀疑过度
To doubt to an excessive degree; to be overly skeptical or suspicious.
He overdoubts everyone's motives, even his closest friends. 他过度怀疑每个人的动机，即使是他最亲密的朋友。
She overdoubted her ability to succeed, leading to her downfall. 她过度怀疑自己成功的能力，导致了她的失败。
Synonym: distrust, suspect, question, be skeptical of
Antonym: trust, believe, have faith in
词性: 动词
