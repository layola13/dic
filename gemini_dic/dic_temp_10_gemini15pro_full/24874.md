browsing:/ˈbraʊzɪŋ/ /ˈbraʊzɪŋ/| n. v.|浏览，吃草|The act of looking through various items or information, often casually or without a specific purpose. Also, the act of an animal grazing.|例句：I was browsing through the bookshop when I found a rare first edition. 我在书店里浏览时发现了一本罕见的初版书。

例句：The deer were browsing in the meadow. 鹿在草地上吃草。|近义词：scanning, skimming, grazing, perusing|反义词：studying, scrutinizing|词性:Noun, Verb (present participle of browse)
