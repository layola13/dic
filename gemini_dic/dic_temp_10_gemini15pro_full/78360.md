gestation:/dʒɛˈsteɪʃən/
n.
怀孕，孕育; 酝酿
the process of carrying or being carried in the womb between conception and birth.
The gestation period of a horse is about 11 months.
马的妊娠期大约是11个月。


The project is still in its gestation period.
这个项目仍在酝酿阶段。

近义词:pregnancy, incubation, development
反义词:birth, parturition, delivery
词性:noun
