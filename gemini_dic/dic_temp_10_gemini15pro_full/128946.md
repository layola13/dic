ninga:/ˈnɪŋɡə/| n.|忍者|A person trained in Japanese martial arts, typically characterized by stealth and secrecy.|例句：The ninja moved silently through the shadows. 忍者在阴影中悄无声息地移动。|例句：He trained to be a ninja for many years. 他训练成为忍者很多年。|近义词：shinobi, assassin, spy|反义词:samurai (sometimes), knight (broadly)|词性:noun

词源:
From Japanese 忍者 (ninja), from the two kanji characters 忍 (nin) meaning "to endure, persevere, conceal" and 者 (ja) meaning "person".
