hypomorphic:/ˌhaɪpəˈmɔːrfɪk/
adjective
亚形的，基因表达低于正常的
Having a reduced level of gene expression or activity compared to the wild-type allele, resulting in a less severe phenotype.
The hypomorphic mutation resulted in a milder form of the disease.  亚形突变导致了该疾病的较轻形式。
The researchers identified a hypomorphic allele of the gene.  研究人员发现了该基因的一个亚形等位基因。
近义词: leaky, weak
反义词: hypermorphic, neomorphic
词性: adjective
