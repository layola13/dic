meta-talk:/ˈmɛtəˌtɔːk/| n.|元语言，元交谈|Discussion or analysis of the conversation itself, rather than the topic at hand.  It involves talking about the process of communication.| "During the debate, the candidates engaged in a lot of meta-talk, arguing about the rules of engagement rather than the actual issues."|近义词：self-reflexive discourse, commentary on communication|反义词: object-level talk|词性:noun
