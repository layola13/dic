butteriest:/ˈbʌtəriɪst/| adj.|最像黄油的，最油腻的，最奉承的|Containing or covered with the most butter; most resembling butter in smooth richness, or most unctuous. Also, most flattering in a servile way.|The butteriest croissants are my favorite. 我最喜欢最油腻的牛角面包。|近义词：richest, oiliest, greasiest, most unctuous, most flattering|反义词：driest, leanest, least unctuous|词性:形容词

词源:
From butter + -y + -est.  "Butter" comes from Old English "butere," from Latin "butyrum," and ultimately from Greek "boutyron."  The "-y" suffix forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "having the quality of." The "-est" suffix indicates the superlative form.
