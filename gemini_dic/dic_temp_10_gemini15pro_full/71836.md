fleetness:/ˈfliːtnəs/| n. |速度；敏捷；快速|The quality of being fast or quick.  It refers to the ability to move with speed and agility. |The deer's fleetness allowed it to escape the predator.  鹿的速度使它能够逃脱捕食者。

The fleetness of her fingers on the piano was remarkable. 她弹钢琴时手指的灵活敏捷令人惊叹。|近义词：speed, rapidity, swiftness, quickness, velocity, celerity|反义词：slowness, sluggishness|词性:noun
