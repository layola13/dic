consequency:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənsi/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwənsi/| n.|结果，后果，重要性|a result or effect of something|The consequency of her actions was a loss of trust. 她行为的后果是失去了信任。|Example:  The economic consequency of the war was devastating. 战争带来的经济后果是毁灭性的。

Example:  He didn't consider the consequencies of his hasty decision. 他没有考虑他草率决定的后果。|Synonym: result, outcome, effect, aftermath, implication|Antonym: cause, origin, source|noun
