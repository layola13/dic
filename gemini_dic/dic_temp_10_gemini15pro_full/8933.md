antihierarchies:/ˌæntiˈhaɪərɑːrkiːz/ | n. | 反等级制度；反等级结构 |  A system or organization that opposes or rejects traditional hierarchies, emphasizing equality and shared power. |  Many startups embrace antihierarchies to foster collaboration and innovation. 许多初创公司拥护反等级制度以促进合作和创新。 | synonyms: egalitarianism, non-hierarchical structures | antonyms: hierarchies, stratified systems | noun
