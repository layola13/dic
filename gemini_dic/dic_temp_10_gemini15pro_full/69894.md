fertilising:/ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzɪŋ/
verb, present participle
施肥，使肥沃
The act of adding nutrients to soil or plants to promote growth.
The farmer is fertilising his fields.  农民正在给田地施肥。
They are fertilising the lawn with organic compost.  他们正在用有机堆肥给草坪施肥。
近义词: enriching, nurturing, manuring
反义词: depleting, impoverishing
词性: verb (present participle)

fertilise:/ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/
verb
施肥，使肥沃
To add nutrients to soil or plants to promote growth.
I fertilise my garden every spring. 我每年春天都给花园施肥。
The flowers need to be fertilised regularly. 这些花需要定期施肥。
近义词: enrich, nurture, manure
反义词: deplete, impoverish
词性: verb

fertiliser:/ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzər/
noun
肥料
A substance added to soil or plants to supply nutrients and promote growth.
This fertiliser is ideal for roses.  这种肥料非常适合玫瑰。
Organic fertilisers are better for the environment. 有机肥料对环境更好。
近义词: manure, compost, plant food
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 pollutant if fertiliser is misused)
词性: noun

词源:  From Late Middle English *fertilisen*, from Old French *fertiliser*, from Latin *fertilis* ("bearing in abundance, fruitful, fertile"), from *ferō* ("I bear, carry").
