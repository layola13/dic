employability:/ɪmˌplɔɪəˈbɪləti/
n.|就业能力; 受雇就业能力|The quality of being suitable for paid work.  The skills, knowledge, and personal attributes that make someone more likely to secure and retain satisfying employment.|Her strong communication skills and work ethic enhance her employability. 她强大的沟通能力和职业道德提高了她的就业能力。|近义词：suitability, eligibility, capability|反义词：unemployability|词性:名词
