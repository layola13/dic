evagination:/iːˌvædʒɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |外翻，翻出|The process of turning something inside out or the state of being turned inside out.  Specifically in biology, the protrusion of some part or organ. |例句：The evagination of the larva's body allows it to move. 幼虫身体的外翻使其能够移动。

例句：The surgeon corrected the evagination of the intestine.  外科医生纠正了肠子的外翻。|近义词：protrusion, outpouching, extroversion|反义词：invagination, intussusception|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *evaginatio, from Latin evaginare (“to unsheath”), from e- (“ex-”) + vagina (“sheath”).
