convoluted:/ˈkɒnvəluːtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvəluːtɪd/ (US)| adj.|复杂的，费解的，盘绕的|Extremely complex and difficult to follow.|例句：The plot of the movie was so convoluted that I couldn't keep track of the characters.  这部电影的情节太复杂了，我根本记不住人物。

例句：His explanation was convoluted and confusing. 他的解释很复杂，令人困惑。

|近义词：complex, intricate, complicated, tangled|反义词：simple, straightforward, clear|词性:形容词
