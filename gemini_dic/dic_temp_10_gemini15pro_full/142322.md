orthodoxnesses:/ˈɔːrθədɑːksnɪsɪz/
n.
正统性，正统观念
The state of being orthodox; adherence to accepted or traditional beliefs or practices.
The various orthodoxnesses within the Christian faith.  基督教信仰中的各种正统性。
synonyms: conformity, conventionality, traditionalism
antonyms: heterodoxy, unorthodoxy, heresy
noun
