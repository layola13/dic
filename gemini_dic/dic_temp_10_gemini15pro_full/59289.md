eagre:/ˈiːɡər/ (US), /ˈeɪɡər/ (UK)| n.|大潮，涌潮|A tide that rushes rapidly up an estuary or river against the current.  A sudden, powerful surge or influx of something.|例句：The eagre roared up the river, a wall of white water.  涌潮咆哮着冲上河流，形成一道白色的水墙。

例句：An eagre of tourists flooded the small town.  一波游客涌入小镇。|近义词：bore, tidal wave, surge, influx|反义词:ebb, outflow|Noun
