actress: /ˈæktrəs/ (US) /ˈæktrɪs/ (UK)| n. |女演员|A woman who performs in plays, movies, or television shows.|Meryl Streep is a highly acclaimed actress. 梅丽尔·斯特里普是一位备受赞誉的女演员。

The young actress delivered a powerful performance.  这位年轻的女演员的表演很有感染力。

近义词: performer, player, artist, star
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider "non-actor" or a specific profession outside of acting)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *actrice (unattested), feminine of acteur (“actor”), from Latin āctor (“doer, performer, speaker, pleader”), from agō (“I do, drive, act”).  The English word actress was first adopted around the late 16th century.
