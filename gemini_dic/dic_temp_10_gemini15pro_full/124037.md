morphed:/mɔːrft/| v. |变形，变化|To change smoothly from one image to another by small gradual steps, using computer animation techniques.  To be transformed.|The caterpillar morphed into a butterfly. 毛毛虫变成了蝴蝶。|近义词：transformed, changed, mutated, evolved|反义词: stagnated, fixed, unchanged|词性:动词
