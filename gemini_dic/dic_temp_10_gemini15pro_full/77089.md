garrisoning: /ˈɡærɪsənɪŋ/ | v. |驻守，驻防|The act of providing soldiers to defend a place. | The army is garrisoning the town.  军队正在驻守这个城镇。|近义词：manning, occupying, defending, fortifying |反义词: abandoning, deserting | Verb

词源: From the Old French word "garison," meaning "protection," "defense," ultimately derived from the Frankish "*warjan" meaning "to defend, protect."
