nonmigratory:/ˌnɑːnˈmaɪɡrətɔːri/ /ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrətri/
adjective
非迁徙的，不迁徙的
Not migratory; not migrating.  Residing permanently in one place rather than migrating.
Sparrows are nonmigratory birds, meaning they stay in the same region year-round. 麻雀是非迁徙的鸟类，这意味着它们一年四季都待在同一个地区。
Some butterflies are nonmigratory and live their entire lives in a relatively small area.  有些蝴蝶不迁徙，它们的一生都生活在一个相对较小的区域。
Synonyms: resident, settled, sedentary
Antonyms: migratory, nomadic, wandering
词性: adjective
