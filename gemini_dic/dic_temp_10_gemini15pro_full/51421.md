deters:/dɪˈtɜːrz/
verb
阻止，威慑，使不敢
discourages someone from doing something, by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.
The heavy fines deter many drivers from speeding.  高额罚款使许多司机不敢超速行驶。
deter, discourage, dissuade, prevent, inhibit
encourage, promote, incite
v.
