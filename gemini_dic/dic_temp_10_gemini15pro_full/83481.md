hackamore:/ˈhækəmɔːr (US); ˈhækəmɔː(r) (UK)| n. |无勒衔的马笼头|A type of bridle that does not have a bit, and instead uses a noseband of various materials and constructions to control the horse.  It exerts pressure on the nose and poll (top of the head).|例句：The young horse was trained in a hackamore before a bit was introduced. 这匹幼马在使用衔铁之前是用无勒衔的马笼头训练的。

例句：He prefers to ride his sensitive horse in a hackamore. 他更喜欢用无勒衔的马笼头骑他敏感的马。|近义词：bosal, jaquima (specific types of hackamores)|反义词：bridle (although not a perfect opposite, as a hackamore is a *type* of bridle, it's the most common contrasting equipment)|词性:noun
