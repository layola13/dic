drifts:/drɪfts/| v.| 漂流，漂移；堆积；迷失方向；缓慢移动 | To be carried along by currents of air or water. To accumulate in heaps or piles. |  Snow drifted against the houses. 雪堆积在房屋旁。| synonyms: wanders, meanders, strays, floats | antonyms:  stays, remains, fixes | verb
