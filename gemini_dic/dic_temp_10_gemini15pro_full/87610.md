heterozygotes:/ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡoʊts/| n.|杂合子|An organism that has two different alleles at a particular locus on homologous chromosomes.|The study focused on the prevalence of a specific gene in heterozygotes.|近义词:hybrid|反义词:homozygote|词性:noun

词源:
From hetero- (different) + zygote (fertilized egg).  Ultimately from Greek *heteros* (different) + *zygon* (yoke).
