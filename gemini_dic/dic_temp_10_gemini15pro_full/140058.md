oestrian:/ˈɛstrɪən/ | adj. |  雌性的，与雌激素有关的 | Relating to oestrus (the period of sexual receptivity in female mammals) or to estrogen. |  The study focused on the oestrian cycle of the mice. 这项研究集中在老鼠的动情周期。| synonyms:  female, feminine | antonyms: male, masculine | adjective

oestrian:/ˈɛstrɪən/ | n. |  雌性动物 | A female animal, especially a horse or other large quadruped. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.) |  The oestrian was easily spooked by the loud noise.  这匹母马很容易被巨大的噪音吓到。| synonyms: mare, female | antonyms: stallion, male | noun
