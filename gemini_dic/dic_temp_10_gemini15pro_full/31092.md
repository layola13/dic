champiest:/ˈtʃæmpiɪst/
adjective
最具冠军相的，最有冠军气质的
Superlative form of champy: most champy.  Meaning having the qualities or characteristics of a champion; especially: being ostentatiously successful or impressive.  Informal.
He had the champiest swagger I'd ever seen. 他有着我见过的最具冠军气质的昂首阔步。
近义词:  most triumphant, winningest
反义词:  losing, defeated
词性: adjective


champy:/ˈtʃæmpi/
adjective
像冠军的，有冠军风范的
Informal. Having the qualities or characteristics of a champion; especially: being ostentatiously successful or impressive.
She struck a champy pose after winning the race.  她在赢得比赛后摆出了一个冠军的姿势。
近义词:  triumphant, winning
反义词:  losing, defeated
词性: adjective


词源:  "Champy" and "champiest" are informal derivations from "champion."  The suffix "-y" is often added to nouns to form adjectives indicating resemblance or characteristic. The "-iest" is the superlative suffix.
