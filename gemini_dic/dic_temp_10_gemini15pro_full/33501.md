ciborium:/saɪˈbɔːriəm/ | n. |华盖，天篷 | A canopy-like structure, often supported by columns, built over a tomb, altar, throne, etc. |  The ciborium over the tomb was intricately carved. 墓穴上方的华盖雕刻精美。| synonyms: canopy, baldachin | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ciborium*, from Greek *κιβώριον (kibōrion)*, a kind of cup made from the leaves of the Egyptian bean, later used to denote a canopied structure, possibly due to resemblance.
