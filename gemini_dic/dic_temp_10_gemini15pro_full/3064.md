aerolite: /ˈɛə.rəˌlaɪt/
noun
陨石；陨星
A stony meteorite.  A meteorite consisting of stony material, as distinguished from a metallic body like a siderite.
The museum has a large collection of aerolites. =  博物馆收藏了大量的陨石。
Scientists studied the aerolite to learn more about the solar system. = 科学家们研究了这块陨石，以了解更多关于太阳系的信息。
Synonyms: meteorite, meteoroid
Antonyms: N/A
n.

From French aérolite, from Ancient Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”).
