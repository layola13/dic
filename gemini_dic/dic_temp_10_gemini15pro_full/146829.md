overimpressionable:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛʃənəbəl/
adjective
易受影响的，过于敏感的
Easily influenced by others; overly sensitive or susceptible to impressions.
He's still young and overimpressionable, so be careful what you say around him. 他还年轻，容易受影响，所以在他身边说话要小心。
Near Synonyms: impressionable, susceptible, sensitive, naive, gullible
Antonyms: insensitive, impervious, resistant, skeptical, cynical
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + impressionable. "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Impressionable" comes from "impression" (from Latin "impressio") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
