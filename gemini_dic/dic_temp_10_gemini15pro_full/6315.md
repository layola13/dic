The word "amorwe" is an archaic word, not commonly used in modern English.

amorwe: /ˈɑːmɔːrweɪ/ | adv.| 明天，翌日 |  An archaic word meaning "on the morrow" or "tomorrow". From Old English 'on morȝne'.  It is no longer used in modern English. |  例句：He sayde he wolde come amorwe. 他说他明天会来。 | 近义词：tomorrow | 反义词: yesterday | 词性: 副词
