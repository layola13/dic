hierarchy: /ˈhaɪərɑːrki/ (US), /ˈhaɪərɑːki/ (UK)| n.| 等级制度；层级；统治集团|A system or organization in which people or groups are ranked one above the other according to status or authority.|The company has a rigid hierarchy.  这家公司等级森严。|synonyms: ranking, pecking order, chain of command|antonyms: equality, anarchy|noun
