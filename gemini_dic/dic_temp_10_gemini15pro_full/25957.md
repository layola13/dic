burring:/ˈbɜːrɪŋ/ | n. v. | 毛刺，去毛刺，嗡嗡声 | The act of removing burrs from a material, or a whirring sound. | The machinist was burring the edges of the metal plate. 机械师正在去除金属板边缘的毛刺。| The burring of the machinery was deafening.  机器的嗡嗡声震耳欲聋。 | synonyms: deburring, whirring, buzzing | antonyms: smoothing, silencing | noun/verb

burring:/ˈbɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 嗡嗡作响的 | Making a whirring sound. | The burring noise of the fan kept me awake. 风扇嗡嗡作响的声音让我睡不着。 | synonyms: whirring, buzzing, droning | antonyms: quiet, silent | adjective
