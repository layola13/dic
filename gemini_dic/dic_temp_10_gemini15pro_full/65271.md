eucharistically:/ˌjuːkərɪˈstɪkli/
adverb
圣体圣事地，以圣餐仪式的方式
in a way that relates to the Eucharist (Holy Communion)
The bread and wine are eucharistically consumed.  面包和酒以圣餐仪式的方式被领受。
The priest eucharistically blessed the congregation.  神父以圣餐仪式的方式祝福会众。
Synonym: sacramentally
Antonym: profanely
词性: adverb (副词)
