cybernaut:/ˈsaɪbərˌnɔːt/ | n. | 网络行者；网民 | A person who spends a lot of time using the internet.  A habitual user of the internet. |  Example 1: He's a true cybernaut, spending hours every day exploring online forums. 他是一位真正的网络行者，每天花费数小时浏览在线论坛。

Example 2: Many cybernauts contribute to online communities and share their knowledge. 许多网民为在线社区做出贡献并分享他们的知识。 | 近义词: netizen, internet user, online user | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "luddite") | 词性: noun
