defacers:/dɪˈfeɪsərz/
n.
毁坏者，涂鸦者
People who deliberately damage or vandalize property, often by painting graffiti or other markings.
The defacers sprayed graffiti all over the newly painted wall.  毁坏者在新建的墙上喷满了涂鸦。
synonyms: vandals, graffitists, hooligans
antonyms: restorers, conservators
词性: 名词
