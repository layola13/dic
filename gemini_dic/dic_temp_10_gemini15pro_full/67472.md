extorter:/ɪkˈstɔːrtər/| n.|勒索者，敲诈者|A person who obtains something, especially money, through coercion or intimidation.|The extorter threatened to release damaging information unless he was paid.  勒索者威胁说，如果不付钱，他就会公布破坏性的信息。|Example: The company president was accused of being an extorter, demanding kickbacks from contractors. 公司总裁被指控为勒索者，要求承包商回扣。|Synonyms: blackmailer, racketeer, con artist|Antonyms: benefactor, philanthropist|Noun
