blandish:/ˈblændɪʃ/| v. | 奉承；哄骗；劝诱 | To use flattery or coaxing to persuade someone to do something. | He blandished her with promises of a promotion. 他用升职的承诺来劝诱她。|  She blandished the guard into letting her pass. 她用甜言蜜语哄骗警卫让她通过。 | 近义词：coax, flatter, cajole, wheedle | 反义词：threaten, coerce, bully |词性：动词
