fuff:/fʌf/
n.
绒毛，软物，无价值的东西
Something unimportant or worthless; nonsense.
He dismissed their complaints as mere fuff.  他把他们的抱怨斥为无稽之谈。
近义词:fluff, nonsense, trifle
反义词:sense, value
