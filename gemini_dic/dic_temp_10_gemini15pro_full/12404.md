aside:/əˈsaɪd/ | adv. n.| 旁边，撇开，留出 |  To one side; out of the way.  |  He pulled me aside to whisper something. 他把我拉到一边低声说了些什么。 | synonyms: apart, away, separately | antonyms: together, included | adverb

aside:/əˈsaɪd/ | n.|  A remark or passage by a character in a play that is intended to be heard by the audience but unheard by the other characters in the play. | Hamlet's asides let the audience know his true feelings. 哈姆雷特的旁白让观众了解他的真实感受。| synonyms: whisper, murmur, undertone | antonyms: statement, declaration | noun
