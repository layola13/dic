opportune:/ˌɑːpərˈtuːn/ /ˌɒpəˈtjuːn/| adj.|合适的，恰当的，适宜的|Suitable or convenient for a particular purpose; occurring at an appropriate time.|This seemed an opportune moment to raise the subject. 这似乎是提出这个问题的适当时机。|近义词：favorable, auspicious, timely, convenient, appropriate, fitting, suitable, apt, felicitous|反义词：inopportune, unfortunate, inconvenient, inappropriate|adjective

opportune:/ˌɑːpərˈtuːn/ /ˌɒpəˈtjuːn/| adj.|有利的，有益的|Presenting a good opportunity; advantageous.|The present political climate is opportune for reform. 当前的政治气候有利于改革。|近义词：advantageous, beneficial, favorable, propitious|反义词：disadvantageous, unfavorable, adverse|adjective
