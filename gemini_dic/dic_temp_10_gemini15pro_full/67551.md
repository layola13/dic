extraneously:/ɛkˈstreɪniəsli/
adverb
外在地；无关地；外部地
Coming from outside; not belonging to something; irrelevant.
His personal problems are extraneous to his job performance. 他的个人问题与他的工作表现无关。
extraneous to
extraneous to the subject 与主题无关
synonym: externally, outwardly, superficially, irrelevantly, incidentally
antonym: intrinsically, inherently, relevantly, internally
词性: 副词
