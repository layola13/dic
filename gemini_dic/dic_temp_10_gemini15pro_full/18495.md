bendable:/ˈbendəbl/| adj.|可弯曲的，柔韧的|Able to be bent easily without breaking. |The wire is thin and bendable. 这根金属丝又细又容易弯曲。|The plant's stems are bendable. 这种植物的茎是可以弯曲的。|近义词：flexible, pliable, supple|反义词：rigid, stiff, inflexible|词性:形容词
词源:From bend (v.) + -able.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bindaną ("to bind").
