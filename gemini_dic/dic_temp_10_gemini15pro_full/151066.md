paraplegia:/ˌpærəˈpliːdʒə/ /ˌpærəˈpliːdʒiə/| n. | 截瘫，下身麻痹 | Paralysis of the legs and lower body, typically caused by spinal injury or disease. |  He suffered paraplegia after a motorcycle accident.  他遭遇摩托车事故后下半身瘫痪了。| 近义词：paralysis, palsy | 反义词: mobility, able-bodied | 词性: noun

paraplegias:/ˌpærəˈpliːdʒəz/ /ˌpærəˈpliːdʒiəz/| n. | 截瘫（复数）| Plural form of paraplegia, referring to multiple instances of the condition. | The hospital ward specialized in treating paraplegias and quadriplegias.  这个病房专门治疗截瘫和四肢瘫痪。 | 近义词：paralyses, palsies | 反义词: - | 词性: noun
