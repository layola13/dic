coverers:/ˈkʌvərərz/
n.
覆盖物；覆盖者；遮盖物
People or things that cover something.
The coverers quickly spread the tarp over the baseball field before the rain started.  覆盖者在雨开始前迅速地把防水布铺在棒球场上。
synonyms: protectors, shields, blankets
antonyms: exposers, revealers
noun

cover:/ˈkʌvər/
v.
覆盖；遮盖；掩盖
To place something over or on top of something else.
She covered the sleeping child with a blanket. 她给睡着的孩子盖上了毯子。
synonyms: conceal, hide, protect
antonyms: expose, reveal, uncover
verb

封面；覆盖物；掩护
Something that is placed over or on top of something else.
