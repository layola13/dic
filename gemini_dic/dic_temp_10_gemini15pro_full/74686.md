freedstool:/ˈfriːdstuːl/
|n.| 免税地; 庇护所; 特权场所|A place of sanctuary or privilege; an area exempt from certain laws or restrictions.  Historically, it referred to a church or its grounds where criminals could seek refuge. |The fugitive sought freedstool in the cathedral.  逃犯在大教堂寻求庇护。|近义词: sanctuary, refuge, asylum |反义词: prison, jail |词性: noun
词源: From Old English *frēo-stōl "free seat," from frēo "free" + stōl "seat, stool."  This referred to a seat or chair within a church which offered sanctuary.
