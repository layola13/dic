nonsecretory:/ˌnɒnˈsiːkrɪtəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈsiːkrɪˌtɔːri/
adjective
非分泌性的
Not secreting or producing a secretion.  Relating to or denoting a cell, gland, or other structure that does not secrete a particular substance.
The nonsecretory cells of the stomach lining protect it from acid. 胃壁的非分泌性细胞保护它免受酸的侵蚀。
Synonyms: nonproducing, inactive
Antonyms: secretory, producing
词性: adjective
