ornithopoda:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒpədə/ | n. | 鸟脚亚目 |  A suborder of ornithischian dinosaurs characterized by their bipedal or quadrupedal gait, beaked mouths, and complex chewing mechanisms.  They were primarily herbivorous and thrived during the Mesozoic Era. |  Ornithopoda includes well-known dinosaurs like Iguanodon and Hadrosaurus. 鸟脚亚目包括著名的恐龙，如禽龙和鸭嘴龙。 | synonyms: None (as it is a specific taxonomic classification) | antonyms: None (as it is a specific taxonomic classification) | noun
