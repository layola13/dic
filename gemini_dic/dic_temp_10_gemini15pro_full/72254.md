floristries: /ˈflɔːrɪstriːz/
n.
花店；花卉栽培；花卉装饰
The business of a florist; the art of arranging flowers.  Also, an establishment where flowers are sold and arrangements are made.
The floristries in this town are known for their beautiful window displays.  这个镇上的花店以漂亮的橱窗陈列而闻名。
近义词: flower shops, floral businesses
反义词:  None (no direct opposite for a place of business)
词性: Noun


floristry: /ˈflɔːrɪstri/
n.
花卉栽培；花卉装饰；花艺
The art of cultivating, arranging, and selling flowers.
She took a course in floristry and now creates stunning bouquets. 她参加了花艺课程，现在可以创作出令人惊叹的花束。
近义词: flower arranging, floral design
反义词: None (no direct opposite for an artistic skill)
词性: Noun
