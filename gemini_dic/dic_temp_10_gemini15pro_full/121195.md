misdrawing:/ˌmɪsˈdrɔːɪŋ/
n.
错误的绘图; 绘图错误; 曲解
an incorrect or inaccurate drawing; a drawing that represents something incorrectly or inaccurately; a misinterpretation

The architect noticed a misdrawing in the blueprint that could have led to a structural issue.
建筑师注意到蓝图中有一个绘图错误，可能会导致结构问题。

The child's misdrawing of the horse was charming, with its five legs and rainbow mane.
孩子画的马，有五条腿和彩虹鬃毛，虽然画错了，但却很可爱。

His misdrawing of the situation led to unnecessary conflict.
他对情况的曲解导致了不必要的冲突。


近义词:misinterpretation, distortion, misrepresentation
反义词:accurate drawing, correct representation

词性:n.
