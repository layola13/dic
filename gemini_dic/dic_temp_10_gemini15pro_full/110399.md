lii:/liː/ | n. |  里 (长度单位，中国市制，等于一英里的千分之五) | A traditional Chinese unit of length, equal to one thousandth of a Chinese mile. | Example: The village is about two lii from here. 这个村子离这里大约两里地。 | 近义词: Chinese mile | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
