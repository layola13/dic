demobilize: /diːˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ (US), /diːˈməʊbəlaɪz/ (UK)
|v.|复员，遣散|To release (someone) from military service.  To disband (troops).  To change (a factory or piece of equipment) from producing goods for a war to producing goods for normal use.|The army was demobilized after the war. 战后军队复员了。|Synonyms: discharge, dismiss, muster out|Antonyms: mobilize, enlist, recruit|verb
demobilization: /diːˌmoʊbələˈzeɪʃən/ (US), /diːˌməʊbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK)
|n.|复员，遣散|The act of demobilizing.|The demobilization of the troops took several months. 部队的复员花了几个月的时间。|Synonyms: discharge, dismissal|Antonyms: mobilization, enlistment, recruitment|noun
