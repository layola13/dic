cheerled:/ˈtʃɪəld/ | v. | 欢呼，喝彩 |  Past tense and past participle of "cheerlead".  Meaning to lead cheers or support enthusiastically, often for a sports team. | The crowd cheerled the winning team.  人群为获胜的队伍欢呼。|  They cheerled their team to victory. 他们为自己的队伍加油助威直到胜利。| Synonyms:  applauded, rooted for, supported | Antonyms: booed, jeered | verb
