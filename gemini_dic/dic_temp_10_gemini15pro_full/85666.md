haut-pas:/ˌoʊˈpɑː/ | n. | 踏脚石；垫脚石；过河石 | A raised paved area by a doorway, used for mounting or dismounting from a horse or carriage. |  She stepped onto the haut-pas and into the waiting carriage. 她踏上踏脚石，进了等候的马车。

|  The hotel entrance featured a grand haut-pas.  酒店入口处有一个宏伟的踏脚石。 | 近义词: stepping stone, mounting block, horseblock | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *haut pas*, literally "high step."
