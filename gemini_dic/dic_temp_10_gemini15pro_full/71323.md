fjord:/ˈfjɔːrd/ (US) /ˈfɪɔːd/ (UK)| n. |峡湾|A long, narrow, deep inlet of the sea between steep mountains or cliffs, typically formed by glacial erosion.|The Geirangerfjord in Norway is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 挪威的盖朗厄尔峡湾是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。|近义词：inlet, sound|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'plain' or 'open sea')|词性: noun
