gravimeter:/ɡræˈvɪmɪtər/
n.
重力仪，测重器
An instrument for measuring variations in the earth's gravitational field.
Geophysicists use gravimeters to measure subtle variations in the Earth's gravity. 地球物理学家使用重力仪来测量地球重力的细微变化。
The gravimeter detected a slight increase in gravity as they approached the mountain. 当他们接近山的时候，重力仪检测到重力略有增加。

synonym: gravity meter
antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a measuring instrument)
noun
