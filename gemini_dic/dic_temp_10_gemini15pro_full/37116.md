combustibleness:/kəmˈbʌstɪbəlnəs/
n.
可燃性, 易燃性
The quality of being combustible; readiness to burn.
The combustibleness of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard.  干树叶的可燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。
flammability, ignitability
incombustibility, non-flammability
