gammoned:/ˈɡæmənd/ | adj. | 输光了的，彻底失败的，被击败的 | Defeated, especially in backgammon; also used figuratively to mean completely defeated or tricked. |  例句：He was completely gammoned in the last game of backgammon. 他在最后一局西洋双陆棋中输得精光。

例句：I felt utterly gammoned when I realized I'd been tricked. 当我意识到自己被骗了时，我感到彻底失败了。| 近义词：defeated, beaten, tricked, routed, thrashed | 反义词：victorious, winning, triumphant | 词性：形容词

gammoned:/ˈɡæmənd/ | v. | （在西洋双陆棋中）输光，彻底击败 | To defeat (an opponent) in backgammon, especially so as to prevent them from scoring; also used figuratively to mean to completely defeat or trick. | 例句：She gammoned her opponent in a swift and decisive match.  她在一场快速而果断的比赛中彻底击败了对手。

例句：He gammoned me into believing his lies. 他骗我相信了他的谎言。 | 近义词：defeat, beat, trick, rout, thrash | 反义词：lose to | 词性：动词

词源: From the Old French word *gamen*, meaning "game".
