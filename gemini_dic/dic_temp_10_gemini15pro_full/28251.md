captainless: /ˈkæptɪnləs/
adjective
无船长的；群龙无首的
Lacking a captain; without a leader.
The captainless ship drifted aimlessly.  这艘无船长的船漫无目的地漂流。
The team was captainless after their leader resigned.  队长辞职后，球队群龙无首。
leaderless, rudderless
headed, led
