nudibrachiate:/ˌnjuːdɪˈbrækiət/ /ˌnuːdɪˈbreɪkiət/
adjective
裸鳃的，裸鳃亚目的
Having gills not enclosed in a cavity but arranged in tufts, plumes, or other exposed structures, as in certain marine gastropods.
Nudibrachiate sea slugs are known for their vibrant colors. 裸鳃海蛞蝓以其鲜艳的颜色而闻名。
Synonyms:  exposed-gilled, gymnobranchiate
Antonyms: tectibranch (having gills enclosed in a mantle cavity)
word type: adjective
