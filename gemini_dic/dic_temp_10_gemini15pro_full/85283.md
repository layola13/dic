harmonium:/hɑːrˈmoʊniəm/ | n. | 风琴，簧风琴 | A keyboard instrument resembling a small organ, with metal reeds blown by foot-operated bellows. |  The harmonium filled the church with a rich, resonant sound.  教堂里回荡着风琴浑厚而洪亮的声音。| synonyms: reed organ, melodeon | antonyms: N/A | noun

harmonium:/hɑːˈmoʊniəm/ | n. | 和声，和谐 | A combination of musical notes played simultaneously to produce a pleasing effect; concord. | The harmonium created a beautiful harmonium with the choir. 风琴与唱诗班的歌声配合得非常和谐。 | synonyms: harmony, concord, consonance | antonyms: dissonance, discord | noun
