dearths:/dɜːrθs/
n.
缺乏，不足
a scarcity or lack of something
例句：There is a dearth of affordable housing in the city. 城市里缺乏经济适用房。

例句：The dearth of qualified teachers is a serious problem. 合格教师的缺乏是一个严重的问题。
近义词：scarcity, lack, shortage, paucity, deficiency
反义词：abundance, surplus, excess, plethora
词性：名词

词源：From Middle English *derthe, from Old English *dierþu (“scarcity, famine, high price”), from Proto-Germanic *diurþiz (“preciousness, dearth”), from Proto-Indo-European *dur- (“hard, firm”). Cognate with Dutch duurte (“dearness, high price”), German Teuerung (“price increase”), Icelandic dýrð (“glory, majesty”).  The semantic development from "costly, expensive" to "scarcity" is paralleled in Latin cāritās.
