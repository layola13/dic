micrococcus:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈkɒkəs/| n. |微球菌|A genus of spherical bacteria belonging to the family Micrococcaceae, typically occurring in irregular clusters, tetrads, or pairs. They are gram-positive and aerobic, and are commonly found in soil, water, and dust, as well as on human skin.|Example 1: Micrococcus luteus is a common species found on human skin. 微球菌属黄杆菌是人类皮肤上常见的菌种。

Example 2:  The researchers isolated several strains of micrococcus from the soil sample. 研究人员从土壤样本中分离出几种微球菌菌株。|近义词：coccus, bacteria|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a genus of bacteria)|noun
