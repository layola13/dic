insulation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 隔热，绝缘，隔离 | The house has good insulation, so it stays warm in winter.  这房子隔热性好，所以冬天很暖和。|  Material used to prevent the passage of heat, electricity, or sound. 用于防止热量、电力或声音通过的材料。| The attic needs more insulation to keep the house warm.  阁楼需要更多的隔热材料来保持房子温暖。| synonyms: padding, lagging, isolating | antonyms: conduction, connection | noun

insulate:/ˈɪnsjʊleɪt/ | v. | 隔离，使绝缘，使隔热 |  They insulated the attic to reduce heat loss. 他们把阁楼隔热以减少热量损失。| To protect something from outside influences.  保护某物免受外界影响。|  Double-glazed windows help to insulate the house against noise. 双层玻璃窗有助于隔离房屋噪音。| synonyms: protect, shield, cushion | antonyms: expose | verb
