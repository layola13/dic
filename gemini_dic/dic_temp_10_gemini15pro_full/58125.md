dropsied:/ˈdrɒpsiːd/ | adj. | 水肿的，患水肿病的 | Affected with, or characteristic of, dropsy (edema). |  He looked pale and dropsied. 他看起来脸色苍白，而且水肿。| 近义词：edematous, swollen, bloated | 反义词：slim, thin | 词性: 形容词

词源: From *dropsical*, from *dropsy* + *-ical*. *Dropsy* comes from obsolete French *hydropisie*, from Latin *hydropisis*, from Greek *ὑδρωπικός* (hudrōpikos), from *ὕδωρ* (hudōr, “water”) + *ὤψ* (ōps, “appearance”).
