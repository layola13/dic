heftinesses:/ˈheftɪnɪsɪz/
n.
沉重，份量，重要性
The heftinesses of the responsibilities overwhelmed her.  这些责任的沉重压垮了她。
The heftinesses of the books made them difficult to carry.  这些书的沉重使它们难以携带。
Synonyms: weights, massiveness, bulkiness
Antonyms: lightness, slightness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hefty + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Hefty itself comes from heave + -y.  Heave meaning "to lift or raise with effort."  Therefore, heftiness refers to the quality of being heavy and requiring effort to lift.
