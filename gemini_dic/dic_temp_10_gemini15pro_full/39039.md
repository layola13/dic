conflicts:/ˈkɒnflɪkts/ (British), /ˈkɑːnflɪkts/ (American)| n. v.|冲突，矛盾；抵触；争执|A serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one.|The two countries have been in conflict for decades. 这两个国家已经冲突了几十年了。|Synonyms:  disagreement, dispute, clash, friction, strife, contention, discord, variance, antagonism|Antonyms: agreement, harmony, peace, concord, accord|noun


conflicts:/kənˈflɪkts/ (British), /kənˈflɪkts/ (American)| v.|冲突，抵触；与…相矛盾|Be incompatible or at variance; clash.|His views conflict with those of his colleagues. 他的观点与他同事的观点相冲突。|Synonyms: clash, disagree, be incompatible, be at variance, be inconsistent, collide, interfere, jar, be at odds|Antonyms: agree, harmonize, coincide, conform, correspond|verb
