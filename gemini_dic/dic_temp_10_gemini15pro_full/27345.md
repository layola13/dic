cambist:/ˈkæmbɪst/| n.|货币兑换商，外汇交易员|A person who deals in foreign currencies or bills of exchange; an expert in foreign exchange.|例句：The cambist quickly calculated the exchange rate between the euro and the dollar.  外汇交易员快速计算了欧元和美元之间的汇率。

例句：Experienced cambists are highly sought after in the international finance market. 经验丰富的外汇交易员在国际金融市场上非常抢手。|近义词：currency trader, foreign exchange dealer, forex trader|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cambiste*, from Italian *cambista*, from *cambiare* "to exchange," from Late Latin *cambiare* "to exchange, barter," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Arabic *kambiyal* "bill of exchange".
