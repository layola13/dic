carville:/ˈkɑːrvɪl/| n.|卡维尔（地名）|A place name, typically a small town or village.  It often refers to Carville, Louisiana, a town known for its historical association with leprosy treatment.|He grew up in Carville, Louisiana. 他在路易斯安那州的卡维尔长大。|近义词:town, village|反义词:city, metropolis|词性:noun

Etymology: Likely derived from a personal name, such as "Carr" or "Carver," combined with "ville," meaning "town" or "settlement," from the French.
