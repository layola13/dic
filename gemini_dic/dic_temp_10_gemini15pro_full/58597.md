dull-wittedness:/ˌdʌlˈwɪtɪdnəs/| n.|迟钝；愚笨|Lack of intelligence; stupidity.|His dull-wittedness prevented him from understanding the complex instructions.|近义词：stupidity, obtuseness, denseness|反义词：intelligence, cleverness, sharpness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dull (lacking sharpness or brightness) + wit (intelligence, understanding) + -ed (forming an adjective) + -ness (forming a noun denoting a state or quality).
