genomes:/ˈdʒiːnoʊmz/
n.
基因组，基因体
The complete set of genes or genetic material present in a cell or organism.
The Human Genome Project has mapped all the genes in human DNA. 人类基因组计划已经绘制了人类DNA中的所有基因。
Scientists are studying the genomes of various species to understand evolution. 科学家正在研究各种物种的基因组以了解进化。
Synonyms: genetic material, DNA, chromosome set
Antonyms:  (Genomes doesn't have a direct antonym.  It's a concrete noun representing a totality.  You could consider concepts like "gene fragment" or "partial sequence" as conceptually opposite, but not true antonyms.)
