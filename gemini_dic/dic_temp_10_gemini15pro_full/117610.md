megaphonist:/ˈmɛɡəˌfoʊnɪst/| n. |扩音器操作员；喊话员|A person who uses a megaphone to address a crowd.  A person who speaks through a megaphone.|The megaphoneist announced the arrival of the train. 扩音员宣布火车到站了。|The megaphoneist rallied the crowd to support the team. 喊话员召集人群支持队伍。|近义词：announcer, caller, crier|反义词:listener, audience|noun
