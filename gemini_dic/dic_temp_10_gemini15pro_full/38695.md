condensible:/kənˈdensəbl/ | adj. | 可冷凝的; 可凝结的 | Able to be condensed; capable of being made more dense or compact. | The steam is condensible into water.  蒸汽可以凝结成水。| The condensible gases were removed from the mixture. 可凝结气体从混合物中去除。 | 近义词: condensable | 反义词: non-condensible | 词性: adjective
