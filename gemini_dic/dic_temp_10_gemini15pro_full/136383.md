nonrestorative:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈstɔː.rə.tɪv/
adjective
非恢复性的，非修复性的
Not restoring health, strength, or well-being.
The patient underwent nonrestorative surgery to remove the tumor.  病人接受了非恢复性手术以切除肿瘤。
His sleep was nonrestorative, leaving him tired even after eight hours. 他的睡眠不能恢复精力，即使睡了八个小时后仍然很累。
Near synonyms: non-rehabilitative, non-therapeutic, palliative
Near antonyms: restorative, curative, therapeutic, rehabilitative
word type: adjective
