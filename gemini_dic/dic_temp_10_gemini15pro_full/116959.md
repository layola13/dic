mazurka:/məˈzɜːrkə/ /məˈzʊərkə/| n. |玛祖卡舞曲；玛祖卡舞|A lively Polish folk dance in triple time, characterized by strong accents on the second or third beat. Also, music written for this dance.|She danced a spirited mazurka. 她跳了一支充满活力的玛祖卡舞曲。|The orchestra played a beautiful mazurka. 管弦乐队演奏了一首优美的玛祖卡舞曲。|近义词：polonaise, waltz|反义词: None|noun
