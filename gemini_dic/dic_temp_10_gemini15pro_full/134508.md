nonlexical:/nɑːnˈlɛksɪkəl/ | adj. | 非词汇的；非语义的 | Relating to sounds or features of speech that do not contribute directly to the meaning of words, such as intonation, stress, and pauses. |  She's studying the nonlexical aspects of communication, like body language. 她正在研究沟通的非词汇方面，例如肢体语言。 | Nonlexical cues, such as tone of voice, can convey a lot of information. 非词汇线索，例如语气，可以传达很多信息。 | 近义词: paralinguistic, extralinguistic | 反义词: lexical | 词性: adjective
