limitable:/ˈlɪmɪtəbl/
adjective
可限制的
able to be limited
例句：The government's power is limitable by law. 政府的权力受法律限制。

例句：The damage caused by the storm was thankfully limitable.  幸好暴风雨造成的损害是可控的。

近义词：restrictable, controllable, confinable
反义词：unlimited, boundless, infinite
词性: adjective
