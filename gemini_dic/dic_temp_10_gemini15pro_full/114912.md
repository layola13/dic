manacling:/ˈmænəklɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
束缚；限制
The act of restraining or restricting someone or something, often literally with manacles (shackles), but also figuratively.
His hands were manacled behind his back. 他的双手被铐在背后。
Bureaucracy is manacling innovation. 官僚主义正在束缚创新。
近义词: restraining, shackling, fettering, binding, confining, restricting, limiting
反义词: freeing, liberating, releasing, unshackling
词性: verb (present participle)


manacle:/ˈmænəkl/
noun
手铐；镣铐
A metal band, chain, or shackle for fastening someone's hands or ankles.
The prisoner was in manacles. 囚犯戴着手铐。
近义词: handcuff, shackle, fetter, chain
反义词: release
词性: noun


manacle:/ˈmænəkl/
verb
给…戴上手铐；束缚
To restrain with manacles; to shackle.
They manacled the prisoner and took him away. 他们给囚犯戴上手铐，然后把他带走了。
Fear of failure manacled her creativity. 对失败的恐惧束缚了她的创造力。
近义词: handcuff, shackle, fetter, bind, restrain, restrict
反义词: free, liberate, release, unchain
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Old French "manicle," diminutive of "manche" (handle, sleeve), from Latin "manica" (sleeve, long sleeve).
