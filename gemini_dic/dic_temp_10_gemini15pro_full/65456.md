euripize:/jʊˈrɪpɪzaɪz/
verb
欧里庇得斯化，使具有欧里庇得斯风格
To imbue with the characteristics of Euripides's tragedies, especially with an emphasis on realism, psychological complexity, and skeptical questioning of traditional values.
The playwright attempted to euripize his historical drama by introducing a psychologically complex female protagonist.  剧作家试图通过引入一个心理复杂的女性主角来使他的历史剧具有欧里庇得斯的风格。
The modern adaptation euripized the ancient myth by giving the gods human flaws.  现代改编通过赋予神人性化的缺陷，使古代神话具有欧里庇得斯的风格。
Near Synonyms: modernize, reinterpret, revise
Antonyms: traditionalize, archaize
词性: 动词
