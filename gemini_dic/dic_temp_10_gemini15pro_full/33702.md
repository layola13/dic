ciphered:/ˈsaɪfərd/ | adj. | 加密的，用密码写的 | converted into cipher; coded; encrypted | The message was ciphered to protect its contents. 这条消息经过加密以保护其内容。 | 近义词: encoded, encrypted, cryptic | 反义词: deciphered, decoded, plain | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From the noun "cipher" + "-ed." "Cipher" comes from Old French "cifre" meaning "zero," ultimately from Arabic "sifr."
