annul:/əˈnʌl/| vt.|废除，取消，宣告无效|To declare officially that something is no longer legally valid.|The marriage was annulled after two months. 这段婚姻两个月后被宣告无效。|Synonyms: invalidate, nullify, abolish, repeal, revoke, cancel|Antonyms: validate, ratify, confirm, uphold|Verb

annul:/əˈnʌl/| vt.|使失效，取消|To make something invalid or of no effect.|The contract was annulled due to a breach of terms.  由于违反条款，合同被取消了。|Synonyms: invalidate, nullify, abolish, repeal, revoke, cancel|Antonyms: validate, ratify, confirm, uphold|Verb
