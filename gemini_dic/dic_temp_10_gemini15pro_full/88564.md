hitchhiker:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪkər/| n.|搭便车的人；免费搭车旅行的人|A person who travels by getting free rides in passing vehicles.|He picked up a hitchhiker on the way to Los Angeles. 他在去洛杉矶的路上载了一名搭便车的人。|Synonym: free rider, thumb traveler|Antonym: driver, passenger|noun
