passman:/ˈpɑːsmən/| n. |及格生；勉强及格的人|A student who passes a course or exam, often barely.  A student who does just enough work to pass.|He was a passman, never striving for excellence. 他是一个及格生，从不追求卓越。|Synonyms: mediocre student, low achiever|Antonyms: high achiever, honors student|noun
