antecedent:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt/| n. adj.| 先行词，先例；祖先|Something that existed before or logically precedes another.| The antecedent of "it" in the sentence is unclear.  这句话中 "it" 的先行词不明确。|synonyms: precursor, predecessor, forerunner| antonyms: subsequent, successor| noun

antecedent:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt/| adj.| 先前的|Existing, happening, or coming before something else.|  He referred to the antecedent events that led to the war. 他提到了导致战争的先前事件。| synonyms: previous, prior, earlier| antonyms: subsequent, later| adjective
