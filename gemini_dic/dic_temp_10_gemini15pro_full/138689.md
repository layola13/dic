nuke's:/nuːks/| n.|核武器（的）|This refers to something belonging to or associated with a nuclear weapon.  It's a contraction of "nuke" and the possessive suffix "'s".|"The nuke's destructive power was immense." 核武器的破坏力是巨大的。|近义词：atomic bomb's, nuclear weapon's|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "peace treaty")|词性:noun


nuke:/nuːk/| n. vt.|核武器；用核武器攻击| A colloquial term for a nuclear weapon; to attack with nuclear weapons. From "nuclear."|"The country threatened to nuke its enemies."  这个国家威胁要用核武器攻击其敌人。"They tested a new nuke in the desert." 他们在沙漠里测试了一枚新核武器。|近义词：atomic bomb, nuclear weapon, A-bomb, H-bomb|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun, verb
