oracularity:/ˌɔːrækjuˈlærəti/ | n. |神谕性，预言能力|The quality of being oracular; resembling or suggesting an oracle, especially in solemnity, obscurity, or ambiguity.  The ability to predict the future or give wise counsel. |Her pronouncements had an air of oracularity that impressed her followers. 她的声明带有一种神谕性，给她的追随者留下了深刻的印象。|Synonyms: prophecy, divination, foresight, prescience |Antonyms: ignorance, blindness, shortsightedness | noun
