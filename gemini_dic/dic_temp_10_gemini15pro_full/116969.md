mbes:/ɛmˈbiːz/ | n. | Multibeam echosounders (多波束回声探测仪) | A type of sonar that uses multiple beams of sound waves to map the seabed. |例句：The mbes revealed a detailed image of the shipwreck. 多波束回声探测仪展现了沉船的详细图像。

例句：The research vessel is equipped with a state-of-the-art mbes. 这艘科考船配备了最先进的多波束回声探测仪。|近义词：sonar, multibeam sonar |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "single beam echosounder" could be considered a contrasting term) |词性: noun
