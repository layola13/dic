overmortgage:/ˌōvərˈmɔːrgɪdʒ/| v.|过度抵押|To mortgage (property) for more than its value.|He overmortgaged his house and now owes more than it's worth. 他过度抵押了他的房子，现在欠的钱比房子的价值还多。|Example: The struggling business owner overmortgaged their property in a desperate attempt to stay afloat.  苦苦挣扎的企业主过度抵押了他们的房产，孤注一掷地想要维持下去。|Synonyms: overborrow, overlend|Antonyms: undermortgage (rarely used)|Verb


overmortgage:/ˌōvərˈmɔːrgɪdʒ/| n.|过度抵押（贷款）|A mortgage that exceeds the value of the property.|The overmortgage made it difficult to sell the house. 过度抵押使得房子难以出售。|Example:  The bank wouldn't approve the sale because the house had a significant overmortgage. 由于房子有巨额的过度抵押贷款，银行不批准出售。|Synonyms: excessive mortgage, top-heavy mortgage|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "conservative mortgage")|Noun
