macrotone:/ˈmækrəˌtoʊn/ | n. |宏音; 长音节;  |A syllable pronounced with relatively greater stress and length. It typically carries a higher pitch or a longer duration compared to other syllables in a word or phrase. |The macrotone in the word "banana" falls on the second syllable.  香蕉这个词的宏音落在第二个音节上。|近义词:long syllable, stressed syllable |反义词:microtone, short syllable |词性:noun

Etymology:  From Ancient Greek  *μακρότονος (makrótonos)  meaning  "having a long accent," from  μακρός (makrós,  "long") +  τόνος (tónos, "tone," "accent," "pitch").
