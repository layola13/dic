overstressing:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度强调；过分紧张；给…过大的压力 | Putting too much emphasis on something; subjecting something to excessive stress or strain. |  The coach warned against overstressing the young athletes. 教练警告不要给年轻运动员过大的压力。

The constant deadlines were overstressing the team.  不断的截止日期让团队压力过大。

| 近义词：overemphasizing, overworking, straining | 反义词: understating, relaxing | 词性: 动词 (present participle of overstress)
