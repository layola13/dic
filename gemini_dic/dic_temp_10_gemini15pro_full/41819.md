coruler:/ˈkoʊˌrulər/| n.|共同统治者; 共治者|A person who rules jointly with another or others.|The two brothers served as corulers of the empire after their father's death.  两兄弟在父亲去世后共同统治着帝国。|Synonyms: co-regent, joint ruler|Antonyms: subject, subordinate|noun
