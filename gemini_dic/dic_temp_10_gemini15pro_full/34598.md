cleavable:/ˈkliːvəbl/| adj.|可裂解的，可劈开的|Able to be cleaved or split.|The diamond is cleavable along certain planes. 这颗钻石可以沿着某些平面裂开。|Synonyms: splittable, divisible, separable|Antonyms: indivisible, inseparable|adjective

cleavable:/ˈkliːvəbl/| adj.|可分裂的|Capable of being divided; fissile.|Certain chemical bonds are cleavable by enzymes. 某些化学键可以被酶裂解。|Synonyms:  fissionable, scissile |Antonyms: infusible, unbreakable|adjective
