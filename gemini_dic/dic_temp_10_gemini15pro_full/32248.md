chickory:/ˈtʃɪkəri/ (US), /ˈtʃɪkəri/ (UK)| n.| 菊苣; 菊苣根(可磨成粉加在咖啡里); |A blue-flowered plant whose root is roasted and ground for use as a coffee substitute or additive. |例句：He added chickory to his coffee.  他把菊苣加到咖啡里。

例句：The cafe served a blend of coffee and chickory.  这家咖啡馆供应咖啡和菊苣的混合物。|近义词：endive(尤其指作为沙拉蔬菜的菊苣)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
