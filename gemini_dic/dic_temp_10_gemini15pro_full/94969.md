in-that:/ɪn ðæt/| conj.|因为，由于；既然|in that introduces a reason or explanation for something. It's a more formal way of saying "because".|He was fortunate in that he had friends to help him. 他很幸运，因为他有朋友帮助他。

She succeeded in that she was able to adapt to the new environment. 她成功了，因为她能够适应新环境。|近义词: because, since, as, seeing that|反义词: although, though, even though|词性:连词
