guardship:/ˈɡɑːrdʃɪp/ | n. | 警备舰；警卫船；护卫舰 | A warship stationed in a particular area to protect it.  A ship, aircraft, or unit of troops assigned to guard a place or person. | The guardship patrolled the coast. 警备舰在海岸巡逻。| The admiral ordered a guardship to protect the harbor.  海军上将命令一艘警备舰保护港口。 | synonyms: patrol boat, picket ship, escort vessel, watchdog | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unprotected vessel/area") | noun
