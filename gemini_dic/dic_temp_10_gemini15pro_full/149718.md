palaeotype:/ˈpeɪliəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 古字体；古字印刷 | A system of phonetic transcription for representing the sounds of ancient languages, or a printed text using such a system. | The palaeotype used in this edition of Beowulf helps modern readers understand the pronunciation.  这个版本的贝奥武夫史诗使用的古字体帮助现代读者理解发音。 | 近义词: paleography | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
