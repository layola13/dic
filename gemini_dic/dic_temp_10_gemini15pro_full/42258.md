counter-arch:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrtʃ/| n.|反拱，逆拱，对拱|An arch built against the underside of another arch to strengthen it, often used in bridges and vaults.|The bridge's stability was reinforced by a series of counter-arches.|近义词:supporting arch, relieving arch|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps main arch, primary arch if contrasting)|noun

counter-arch:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrtʃ/| n.|反作用力，对抗力量 (Figurative, less common)|A force or influence that opposes another.|The government's new policy faced a strong counter-arch of public opinion.|近义词:opposition, resistance|反义词:support, reinforcement|noun
