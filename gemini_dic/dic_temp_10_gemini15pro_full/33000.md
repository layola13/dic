chorically:/kɔːˈrɪkli/ | adv. | 合唱地，齐声地 | In a manner resembling a chorus; performed by a chorus. | The audience chorically gasped at the shocking twist. 观众齐声对这令人震惊的转折倒吸了一口气。 | The crowd chorically chanted the team's name.  人群齐声高呼球队的名字。| 近义词:unison, together, collectively | 反义词:individually, separately | 词性:副词

词源:
From Greek "khorikós" (relating to a chorus), from "khorós" (chorus, dance).
