noncoverages:/ˌnɑːnˈkʌvərɪdʒɪz/
|n.| 未承保范围，未覆盖范围 |Lack of insurance coverage for specific risks or situations. The things that an insurance policy does not protect against.|The policy has several noncoverages, including flood and earthquake damage. 该保单有几项未承保范围，包括洪水和地震损害。|exclusions, exceptions|inclusions, coverages|noun
