nonamputation:/ˌnɒnˌæmpjuˈteɪʃn/
n.| 非截肢，保肢| The preservation of a limb or body part that might otherwise be amputated. | The surgeon performed a nonamputation procedure, saving the patient's leg.  外科医生进行了保肢手术，挽救了病人的腿。| synonyms: limb salvage, limb preservation | antonyms: amputation, disarticulation | noun

nonamputation:/ˌnɒnˌæmpjuˈteɪʃn/
adj.| 非截肢的，保肢的| Relating to a medical procedure that avoids amputation. | The nonamputation surgery was a success. 保肢手术很成功。| synonyms: limb-sparing, limb-saving | antonyms: amputating | adjective
