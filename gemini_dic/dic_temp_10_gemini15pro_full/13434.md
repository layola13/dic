atoning:/əˈtoʊnɪŋ/
adjective
赎罪的，补偿的
making amends or reparation for a wrong or injury.
He was atoning for his past sins. 他正在为他过去的罪孽赎罪。
近义词:expiating, redeeming, propitiatory
反义词:sinning, offending
