paraclinical: /ˌpærəˈklɪnɪkəl/
adjective
辅助临床的；临床辅助科的
Relating to or denoting medical procedures or tests performed in a laboratory in order to aid diagnosis and treatment, rather than being performed directly on a patient.
Paraclinical tests, such as blood tests and X-rays, can provide valuable information.  辅助临床的检查，例如验血和X光，可以提供有价值的信息。

Paraclinical services are essential for accurate diagnosis. 辅助临床服务对于准确诊断至关重要。

Synonym: auxiliary, ancillary
Antonym: clinical

Etymology: para- (beside, near) + clinical
