ideologue:/ˈaɪdiəˌlɔːɡ/ /ˈɪdiəˌlɒɡ/| n. |理论家；空想家；意识形态的拥护者|A person who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas, especially political ones, and tries to persuade others to accept them.|例句：He is a staunch ideologue who believes capitalism is the root of all evil. 他是一个坚定的理论家，认为资本主义是一切罪恶的根源。

例句：She's a political ideologue who refuses to compromise. 她是一个政治理论家，拒绝妥协。

近义词：theorist, doctrinaire, partisan|反义词: pragmatist, moderate |词性:noun
