ingots:/ˈɪŋɡəts/| n. |锭; 金属块|A mass of metal, such as a bar of gold or silver, cast in a convenient shape for storage or transportation.|The gold was stored in ingots in the vault. 黄金以金锭的形式存放在金库里。|Synonyms: bars, blocks, lumps|Antonyms: fragments, pieces|noun
