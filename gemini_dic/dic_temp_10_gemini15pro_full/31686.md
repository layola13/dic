chaunt:/tʃɑːnt/ /tʃɔːnt/| n. v. |吟诵，反复唱；单调的歌；反复说|A monotonous, repetitive song or chant.  A repeated rhythmic phrase, typically one shouted or sung in unison by a crowd.|The monks chaunted their prayers. 僧侣们吟诵着他们的祈祷。|近义词：chant, intone, sing, recite|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:名词,动词
Etymology: From Old French *chanter, from Latin cantare "to sing."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kan- "to sing."
