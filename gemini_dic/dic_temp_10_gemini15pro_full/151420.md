parochial:/pəˈroʊkiəl/ | adj. | 教区的；狭隘的；地方性的 | of or relating to a church parish; having a limited or narrow outlook or scope. |  The church collects donations for parochial schools. 教会为教区学校募捐。 |  The politician's parochial views prevented him from seeing the bigger picture. 这位政客狭隘的观点使他无法看到全局。 | 近义词: narrow-minded, provincial, insular | 反义词: broad-minded, cosmopolitan, universal | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *parochialis*, from Latin parochia "parish," from Greek paroikia "sojourning in a foreign land," from paroikos "dwelling beside, neighbor," from para- "beside" + oikos "house."
