overprogrammed:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræmd/
adjective
过度编程的，程序过多的
having too many activities or tasks scheduled; excessively planned or structured
例句：The children were overprogrammed with extracurricular activities and had no time to just play. 孩子们的课外活动安排得太满，没有时间玩耍。

例句：Feeling overprogrammed and stressed, she decided to cancel some of her commitments.  感到行程安排过满，压力很大，她决定取消一些承诺。

近义词：overscheduled, overloaded, overbooked
反义词：under-scheduled, free

词性: adjective
