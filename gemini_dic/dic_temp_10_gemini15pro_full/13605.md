attestable:/əˈtɛstəbl/
adjective
可证实的，可作证的
able to be attested; verifiable.
His exemplary conduct is attestable by all his colleagues. 他的模范行为可以由他所有的同事证实。
synonyms: verifiable, confirmable, provable, demonstrable, supportable, corroborable
antonyms: unprovable, unverifiable
词性: adjective
