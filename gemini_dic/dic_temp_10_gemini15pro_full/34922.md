clo:/kloʊ/ | n. | 克罗(织物重量单位) | A unit of measurement for the thermal insulation of clothing, equal to the insulation provided by a standard suit of clothes. | Example:  This sleeping bag has a CLO rating of 3.  这个睡袋的克罗值是3。| Example:  A CLO value of 1 is roughly equivalent to the insulation provided by a typical business suit. 克罗值1大致相当于一套典型商务西装提供的保暖性。| Synonym:  tog (primarily British) | Antonym:  N/A | noun
