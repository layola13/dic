overcares:/ˌoʊvərˈkɛrz/ | v. | 过度关心，过分操心 | To care for someone or something to an excessive degree. |  She overcares for her children, hovering over them constantly. 她过度关心她的孩子们，总是围着他们转。|  He overcares about his work, often staying late and neglecting his family. 他过分操心他的工作，经常加班到很晚，忽略了他的家人。 | 近义词: fusses, worries excessively, overprotects | 反义词: neglects, ignores | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "cares" (verb form of "care").  "Over-" 源于古英语 "ofer,"  "care" 源于古英语 "carian."
