buildings:/ˈbɪldɪŋz/| n. |建筑物；房屋；楼房|Structures constructed for shelter, accommodation, or other purposes.  A plural form of "building".|例句：The city has many tall buildings.  这个城市有很多高楼大厦。

|近义词：structures, edifices, constructions, houses (when referring to residences)|反义词: ruins, rubble|词性:noun


buildings:/ˈbɪldɪŋz/| n. |建设；建筑；建造|The act or process of constructing something, usually a structure. A gerund form of "build".  Less common as a plural noun.|例句：The buildings of new homes are underway. 新房子的建造正在进行中。(This usage is less common, and often sounds more natural with a possessive: "The building of new homes...")|近义词：construction, erection|反义词: demolition, destruction|词性:noun (gerund)
