bollocks:/ˈbɒləks/ (UK) /ˈbɑːləks/ (US)| n. interj.| 睾丸；胡说；废话|Testicles; nonsense; rubbish; an exclamation of annoyance, frustration, or disbelief.|例句：That's a load of bollocks!  那是一派胡言！

例句：He kicked him in the bollocks. 他踢了他的睾丸。

近义词：nonsense, rubbish, crap, balls, testicles|反义词:sense, truth|词性:名词,感叹词

bollocks:/ˈbɒləks/ (UK) /ˈbɑːləks/ (US)| vt.| 把…搞砸|To ruin or mess something up|例句：I completely bollocksed up the exam. 我彻底搞砸了考试。

例句：Don't bollocks it up this time! 这次别搞砸了！

近义词：ruin, mess up, screw up|反义词:succeed, fix|词性:动词
