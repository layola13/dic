bomar:/ˈboʊmɑːr/ /ˈbɒmɑː/ | n. | 轰炸机，轰炸员 |A bomber aircraft, or a crew member of a bomber aircraft. |The bomar released its payload over the target. 轰炸机在目标上空投放了有效载荷。|The young bomar was nervous on his first mission.  这位年轻的轰炸员在第一次执行任务时很紧张。|近义词:bomber|反义词:None|词性:noun


词源:
bomar is a shortened, informal variant of bomber.  "Bomber" itself comes from the verb "to bomb," which ultimately derives from the Greek word "bombos" meaning a deep, hollow sound.
