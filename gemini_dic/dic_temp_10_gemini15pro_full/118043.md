menagerie:/məˈnædʒəri/
n.|动物园；动物展览馆；一批奇特的或异样的东西|A collection of wild or foreign animals kept especially for exhibition or a strange or diverse collection of people or things.|The duke kept a menagerie of exotic animals on his estate. 公爵在他的庄园里养了一群奇特的动物。|Synonyms: collection, assortment, array, assemblage|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "emptiness," "lack," or specific antonyms based on the context of the collection)|noun
