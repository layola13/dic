corolla:/kəˈrɒlə/ (UK), /kəˈroʊlə/ (US)| n. |花冠|The petals of a flower, forming a ring inside the sepals. |The bright yellow corolla of the daffodil attracted the bees. 水仙花鲜黄色的花冠吸引了蜜蜂。|近义词：petals|反义词: calyx (花萼)|noun
