numberers:/ˈnʌmbərərz/
n.
编号员，计数员
People who assign numbers to things or count them.
The numberers worked diligently, ensuring each item received a unique identifier.  编号员们勤奋地工作，确保每件物品都收到一个唯一的标识符。
The library employed numberers to catalog the vast collection of books. 图书馆雇佣了编号员来为大量的藏书编目。
近义词: counters, enumerators
反义词: None
词性: noun
