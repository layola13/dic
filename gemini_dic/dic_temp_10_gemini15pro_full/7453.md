anisomerous: /ˌænɪˈsɒmərəs/ /ˌænɪˈsɑːmərəs/
adjective
不同数的；不等数的；各部分数量不等的
Having an unequal number of parts in the whorls of a flower.  A flower is anisomerous if the number of sepals, petals, stamens, or carpels do not match up.
The wild rose is anisomerous, having five sepals and petals but numerous stamens. 野玫瑰是不等数的，有五个萼片和花瓣，但有许多雄蕊。
Some primitive dicots have anisomerous flowers. 一些原始的双子叶植物有不等数的花。
近义词: asymmetrical, irregular
反义词: isomerous, symmetrical
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *anisos* (unequal) + *meros* (part).
