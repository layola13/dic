nonpenitent:/ˌnɑːnˈpɛnɪtənt/ | adj. | 不悔悟的，不忏悔的 | Not feeling or showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong; unrepentant. |  The nonpenitent criminal showed no remorse for his actions.  不悔悟的罪犯对自己的行为毫无悔意。 |  Example:  Even after being confronted with the evidence, the nonpenitent thief maintained his innocence. 即使面对证据，这个不忏悔的小偷仍然坚称自己无辜。 | Synonyms: impenitent, unrepentant, remorseless, unapologetic | Antonyms: penitent, repentant, contrite, remorseful | Adjective

Etymology:
From non- (not) + penitent (feeling or showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong). The word "penitent" comes from the Old French word "penitent" and ultimately from the Latin word "paenitēns," meaning "repenting."
