abbreviature:/əˈbriːviˌeɪtʃər/| n.|缩写，缩写词|A shortened form of a word or phrase.  It allows for quicker and more efficient communication, especially in writing.|例句：The abbreviation for "mister" is "Mr."  “mister”的缩写是“Mr.”

例句：She used many abbreviations in her notes to save time.  她在笔记中使用了许多缩写来节省时间。|近义词：abbreviation, acronym, contraction, short form|反义词:expansion, elaboration|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abbreviatura*, from Latin *abbreviare* "to shorten," from *ab-* "from" + *brevis* "short."  The word entered English in the late 15th century.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
An abbreviation represents a longer word or phrase. = 缩写代表一个更长的单词或短语。
He often uses abbreviations when texting. = 他发短信时经常使用缩写。


一般过去时:
The doctor abbreviated the medical term for the patient. = 医生为病人缩写了医学术语。
They abbreviated the company name for the logo. = 他们为徽标缩写了公司名称。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
