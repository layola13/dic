indefeasible:/ˌɪndɪˈfiːzəbl/| adj.|不可废除的，不可剥夺的|Not capable of being annulled, voided, or forfeited.|例句：He has an indefeasible right to the property. 他对这处房产拥有不可剥夺的权利。|例句：The constitution guarantees citizens indefeasible rights. 宪法保障公民不可废除的权利。|近义词：inalienable, unassailable, absolute|反义词：defeasible, revocable, contingent|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *indefeasibilis, from in- "not" + defeasibilis "that can be annulled," from Latin defeasibilis, from de- "away from" + facere "to make, do."
