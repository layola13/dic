neuralinker:/ˈnʊrəlɪŋkər/| n.|神经连接器；神经链路器|A device or system that creates a direct connection between the brain and a computer or other external device.|Elon Musk's company, Neuralink, is developing a neuralinker to help people with paralysis. 伊隆·马斯克的公司Neuralink正在开发一种神经连接器来帮助瘫痪的人。|Synonyms: brain-computer interface, brain-machine interface, neural interface|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "natural connection")|noun
