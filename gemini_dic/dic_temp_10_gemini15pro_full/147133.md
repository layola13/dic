overlogicality:/ˌoʊvərˈlɑːdʒɪˈkælɪti/
|n.| 过度逻辑性；过分理性|The state of being excessively logical, often to the detriment of practical considerations or emotional intelligence.|His overlogicality sometimes made him seem cold and unfeeling. 他过分的逻辑性有时让他显得冷漠无情。|近义词：hyperrationality, overthinking|反义词：irrationality, emotionality|词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "logicality" (noun form of "logical," derived from Late Latin "logicalis," from Greek "logikos" meaning "of or pertaining to reason").  由表示“过度”的前缀 "over-" 加上 "logicality"（"logical" 的名词形式，源自晚期拉丁语 "logicalis"，来自希腊语 "logikos"，意为“与理性有关的”）组成。
