indices:/ˈɪndɪsiːz/| n.|指数，指标，索引|A numerical scale used to compare values of a variable over time or across different geographic areas.  It is also used as the plural of index.|The consumer price index rose sharply last month. 消费者物价指数上个月急剧上升。|synonyms: indexes, indicators, signs|antonyms: N/A|noun

index:/ˈɪndɛks/| n. |索引，指数，指标|An alphabetical list of names, subjects, etc., with references to the places where they occur, typically found at the end of a book.|The index at the back of the book lists all the important terms. 书后面的索引列出了所有重要的术语。|synonyms: directory, guide, register|antonyms: N/A|noun
