banu: /ˈbɑːnuː/
n.
lady; mistress; wife
A respectful term for a woman, particularly a wife or mistress.  Derived from Persian.
She addressed the queen as "banu." 她尊称女王为“夫人”。
近义词: lady, madam, mistress, wife
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "servant" or "slave" depending on context)
词性: noun
