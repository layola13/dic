asphaltums: /æsˈfɔːltəmz/ /æsˈfɑːltəmz/
n.
沥青, 地沥青
A dark, sticky form of bitumen found in natural deposits or obtained as a residue from petroleum distillation. It is used for paving and roofing.
The asphaltums used in road construction are carefully selected for their durability.  用于道路建设的沥青是经过精心挑选的，以保证其耐用性。
synonyms: bitumen, tar, pitch
antonyms: None (lack of a direct opposite for a substance)
noun
