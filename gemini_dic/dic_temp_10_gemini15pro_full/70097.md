feverish:/ˈfiːvərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈfiːvərɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|发烧的；狂热的；焦躁的|Showing the symptoms of a fever; marked by intense emotion or activity.|例句：He felt feverish and weak. 他感到浑身发热，而且很虚弱。|近义词：febrile, burning, heated, frantic|反义词：calm, cool, composed|词性:形容词

feverish:/ˈfiːvərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈfiːvərɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|发烧的；狂热的；焦躁的|Resembling or suggesting fever; marked by unusual restlessness or excitement.|例句：The stock market has been feverish all week.  整个星期股市都狂热波动。|近义词：excited, restless, frenzied|反义词：relaxed, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French feveros, based on Latin febris ‘fever’.
