ihlang:/iːˈlɑːŋ/ | n. | 伊兰伊兰 (一种热带树及其芬芳的花) | A tropical tree (Cananga odorata) native to Southeast Asia, cultivated for its fragrant greenish-yellow flowers. The flowers yield a volatile oil (ylang-ylang oil) used in perfumery. |  The ihlang tree is prized for its beautiful, fragrant flowers. 伊兰伊兰树因其美丽芬芳的花朵而备受珍视。

The perfume had a strong scent of ihlang. 这款香水有浓郁的伊兰伊兰香味。| 近义词: cananga, ylang-ylang | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑与其香味相反的花，例如：marigold - 万寿菊(气味较浓烈)) | 词性: 名词
