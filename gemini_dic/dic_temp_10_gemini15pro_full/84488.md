haltingness:/ˈhɔːltɪŋnɪs/| n.|犹豫，迟疑，吞吞吐吐|The haltingness of his speech suggested he was nervous. 他的讲话吞吞吐吐，表明他很紧张。|例句：The haltingness in her voice betrayed her uncertainty. 她声音中的犹豫暴露了她的不确定。

例句：His haltingness of speech made it difficult to understand him. 他说话吞吞吐吐，让人难以理解。|近义词：hesitancy, indecision, uncertainty|反义词：fluency, decisiveness, certainty|词性:名词

词源: From "halting" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). "Halting" comes from the verb "halt," meaning "to stop," ultimately from the Old English "healdan," meaning "to hold, keep, guard."
