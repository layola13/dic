outbleats:/ˌaʊtˈbliːts/| v. | 比...叫得更响亮;  胜过; 超过 (尤指羊叫)| To cry louder than; to surpass or exceed, especially in bleating (like sheep). | The alpha male outbleated the other rams in the flock.  领头的公羊的叫声盖过了羊群中其他公羊的声音。| The young lamb outbleats its mother in its eagerness for milk.  小羊羔为了喝奶，叫声比它妈妈还响。| 近义词：outcries, outshouts, surpasses, exceeds | 反义词：is outdone by, is surpassed by|词性:动词
