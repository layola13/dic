instop:/ˈɪnstɒp/ | n. | 内侧鞋帮; 鞋内帮 | The instop of the shoe was rubbing against my ankle. 鞋子的内侧鞋帮摩擦着我的脚踝。|  The instop is the part of a shoe that covers the instep.  鞋内帮是覆盖足弓的鞋子部分。 | 近义词: lining, inner | 反义词: outstep (although not a direct opposite, it refers to the outer part of the foot) | 词性: noun


词源: "In" + "stop," where "stop" likely refers to a piece or section of material.  "In" indicates its location inside the shoe.  Precise etymological details are scarce.
