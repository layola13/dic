foes:/fəʊz/| n. |敌人，仇敌|An enemy or opponent.|例句：He fought bravely against his foes. 他勇敢地与他的敌人作战。

例句：Her political foes tried to discredit her. 她的政敌试图败坏她的名声。|近义词：enemies, adversaries, opponents, rivals|反义词：friends, allies, supporters|词性:名词

词源: Middle English: from Old French fo, plural of fel, based on Latin inimicus ‘enemy’.
