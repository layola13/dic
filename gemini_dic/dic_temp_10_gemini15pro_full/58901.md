during:/ˈdjʊərɪŋ/| prep.|在…期间，在…过程中|A preposition that indicates the time frame within which something happens.|例句：The shop is open during the day.  这家商店白天营业。|例句：He worked hard during the summer. 他在夏天努力工作。|近义词：throughout, in, within, for, over|反义词：before, after|词性:介词

词源: From Middle English *duringe, *durynge, from Old French durant, present participle of durer (“to last, endure”), from Latin dūrō (“to harden, last, endure”).
