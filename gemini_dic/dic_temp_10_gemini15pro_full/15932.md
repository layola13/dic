balmiest:/ˈbɑːmiɪst/ | adj. | 最温和的，最宜人的 | Mildest and most pleasant; soothing. | It was the balmiest day of the year so far. 这是今年迄今为止最宜人的一天。| The balmiest evening breezes caressed our faces as we sat on the porch. 当我们坐在门廊上时，最柔和的晚风轻抚着我们的脸。 |  近义词：mildest, gentlest, serenest | 反义词：harshest, coldest, chilliest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From balm + -y + -est.  "Balm" ultimately comes from the Latin "balsamum," referring to a fragrant, resinous substance used for healing. The "-y" suffix creates an adjective meaning "having the qualities of balm," and "-est" is the superlative suffix, indicating the highest degree.
