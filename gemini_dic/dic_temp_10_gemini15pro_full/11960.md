artefact:/ˈɑːtɪfækt/ (US) /ˈɑːtəfækt/ (UK)| n.|人工制品；史前古器物；人为现象|An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest. |例句：The museum displays many fascinating artefacts from ancient Egypt.  博物馆展出了许多来自古埃及的迷人文物。
