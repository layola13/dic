cairn:/kɛːn/
n.
石冢，堆石路标
A mound of stones piled up as a memorial or landmark.
The hikers followed the cairns marking the trail through the fog.  徒步旅行者沿着雾中标记小路的石堆前进。

Cairns are often used to mark trails in mountainous areas. 石堆经常被用来标记山区的路径。

近义词: mound, heap, pile
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑valley, depression表示与隆起相对的地形)
词性: noun
