ommatidium:/ˌɒməˈtɪdiəm/
n.
小眼，单眼
One of the small optical units of the compound eye of an arthropod.
Each ommatidium contains a cluster of photoreceptor cells surrounded by support cells and pigment cells. 每个小眼包含一簇感光细胞，周围环绕着支持细胞和色素细胞。
The compound eyes of insects are made up of many ommatidia. 昆虫的复眼由许多小眼组成。
