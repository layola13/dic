achylous:/əˈkaɪləs/| adj.|无乳糜的，缺乏乳糜的|Lacking chyle; deficient in chyle.|The patient presented with an achylous ascitic fluid, suggesting a blockage in the lymphatic system.  病人出现无乳糜腹水，提示淋巴系统阻塞。

The biopsy revealed achylous drainage, ruling out a chyle leak. 活检显示无乳糜引流，排除了乳糜泄漏的可能性。|近义词:chyle-free, nonchylous|反义词:chylous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (without) + Greek chylos (juice, chyle).  来自a-（无）+希腊语chylos（汁液，乳糜）。


USAGE->
The achylous nature of the fluid was confirmed by laboratory analysis. = 通过实验室分析证实了该液体的无乳糜性质。

The surgeon noted the achylous appearance of the peritoneal fluid. = 外科医生注意到腹膜液的无乳糜外观。


PHRASE->
achylous ascites = 无乳糜性腹水
achylous effusion = 无乳糜性渗出
achylous drainage = 无乳糜性引流
achylous fluid = 无乳糜性液体
achylous appearance = 无乳糜性外观
