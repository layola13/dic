nonabstemiousness:/ˌnɒnˌæbstˈiːmiəsnəs/
noun
不节制, 放纵
Lack of moderation or restraint, especially in eating or drinking.
His nonabstemiousness led to health problems. 他的不节制导致了健康问题。
Synonyms: intemperance, indulgence, excess, immoderation
Antonyms: abstinence, moderation, temperance, sobriety
