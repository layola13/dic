enthusiasm: /ɪnˈθuːziæzəm/ | n. |热情，热忱，热衷 | Intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval. It refers to a strong feeling of excitement and interest in something. |  She approached the task with enthusiasm. 她热情地对待这项任务。 | His enthusiasm for the project was contagious. 他对这个项目的热情很有感染力。 | 近义词：passion, interest, zeal, fervor | 反义词：apathy, indifference, disinterest | 词性: 名词


词源:
From French enthousiasme, from Late Latin enthusiasmus, from Greek enthousiasmos, from enthous "inspired, possessed by a god," from en- "in" + theos "god."
