honored:/ˈɑːnərd/ (US) /ˈɒnəd/ (UK)| adj.|感到荣幸的；受尊敬的|Feeling or showing respect and admiration; given special recognition.| I am honored to be invited to speak at this conference. 我很荣幸被邀请在此次会议上发言。|  She was honored for her contributions to science. 她因对科学的贡献而受到表彰。|近义词：respected, esteemed, privileged, distinguished|反义词：dishonored, disgraced, shamed|词性:形容词

honored:/ˈɑːnərd/ (US) /ˈɒnəd/ (UK)| v.|尊敬；给予…荣誉（honor的过去式和过去分词）|To regard with great respect.|  The university honored her with an honorary degree. 大学授予她荣誉学位。| The soldiers were honored for their bravery. 士兵们因其英勇而受到表彰。|近义词：respected, recognized, celebrated|反义词：dishonored, disgraced, shamed|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *honouren, from Anglo-Norman onurer, honurer, from Old French onorer, honorer, from Latin honorāre (“to honor, distinguish, adorn”), from honor (“honor”).
