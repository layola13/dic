duskier:/ˈdʌskiər/ | adj. | 更昏暗的，更朦胧的 | comparative form of dusky; more dusky; darker and more shadowy | The room became duskier as the sun began to set. 房间随着太阳开始落山而变得更加昏暗。| The path ahead grew duskier and more difficult to follow. 前面的路变得更加昏暗，更难走。 |  Near synonyms: darker, dimmer, shadier | Near antonyms: brighter, lighter, clearer | adjective

词性: adjective

词源: From dusky + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from dusk.
