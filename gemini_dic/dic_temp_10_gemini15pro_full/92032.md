hyperpatriotically:/ˌhaɪpərˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪkli/ | adv. | 极端爱国地 |  In an excessively or extremely patriotic manner. It combines "hyper-" (meaning excessive or above normal) with "patriotically" (showing love and devotion to one's country). | He spoke hyperpatriotically about the nation's military might. 他极端爱国地谈论了国家的军事实力。 |  The politician hyperpatriotically declared that anyone who questioned the war was a traitor.  这位政客极端爱国地宣称，任何质疑战争的人都是叛徒。 |  excessively patriotically, ultra-patriotically, jingoistically |  unpatriotically, disloyally | adverb
