idocrase:/ˈaɪdəkreɪs/ | n. | 绿柱石 |A green, brown, or yellow mineral of the sorosilicate group. It is a complex calcium aluminum silicate and is sometimes used as a gemstone. | I found a beautiful piece of idocrase while rockhounding. 我在寻找岩石时发现了一块美丽的绿柱石。|  Synonyms: vesuvianite, californite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
