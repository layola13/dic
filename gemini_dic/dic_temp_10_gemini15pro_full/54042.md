discussional:/dɪˈskʌʃənl/
adjective
讨论的; 谈论的; 协商的
Relating to or suitable for discussion.  Pertaining to discussion; characterized by or used for discussion.
例句：The meeting was highly discussional, with everyone contributing their ideas.  会议充满了讨论，每个人都贡献了自己的想法。

例句：The discussional nature of the workshop allowed for a productive exchange of views.  研讨会的讨论性质促成了富有成效的意见交流。

近义词：conversational, deliberative, consultative
反义词：undiscussable, uncommunicative
词性: adjective
