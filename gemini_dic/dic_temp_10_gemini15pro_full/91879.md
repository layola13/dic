hyperintelligences:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtelɪdʒənsɪz/ | n. | 超级智能（复数） |  Hypothetical intellects that greatly surpass the cognitive performance of humans in virtually all domains of interest. It is a concept often explored in science fiction, futurism, and discussions about artificial intelligence. | The advent of hyperintelligences could reshape civilization in unimaginable ways. 超级智能的出现可能会以难以想象的方式重塑文明。|  The development of hyperintelligences poses both immense opportunities and existential risks. 超级智能的发展既带来了巨大的机遇，也带来了生存风险。 | Synonyms: superintelligence, ultra-intelligence | Antonyms: unintelligence, stupidity | noun
