dao:/daʊ/ | n. | 刀，剑，道 | A Chinese broadsword, often double-edged, but sometimes single-edged. Also, a principle or way of life, as in Taoism. | He skillfully wielded his dao. 他熟练地挥舞着他的刀。 | synonyms: sword, blade, path, way | antonyms: peace, inaction | noun

dao:/daʊ/ | n. | 道 (哲学概念) | The Way; the absolute principle underlying the universe; the way of nature or life.  A key concept in Taoism. |  Living in accordance with the dao brings harmony.  遵循道而生活带来和谐。| synonyms: way, path, principle, doctrine | antonyms: disorder, chaos | noun
