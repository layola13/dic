forkiness:/ˈfɔːrkɪnɪs/
n.
分叉; 叉状; 分歧
The quality or state of being forked or divided into branches.
The forkiness of the tree's branches made it a perfect climbing spot.  这棵树枝杈的分叉使其成为一个完美的攀爬地点。
branchiness, dividedness
solidity, wholeness
noun
