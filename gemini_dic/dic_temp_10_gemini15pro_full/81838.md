greyly: /ˈɡreɪli/ | adv. | 灰暗地，阴沉地 | In a grey or dull manner; gloomily. | The sky lowered greyly over the city. 天空阴沉沉地笼罩着城市。| The old man looked greyly at the ruins of his home. 老人灰暗地看着他家的废墟。 | 近义词：drably, gloomily, somberly | 反义词：brightly, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From grey + -ly.  "Grey" comes from Old English græg.
