after-the-fact:/ˈæftər ðə ˈfækt/ | adj.| 事后的，马后炮的 |  Happening or done after something, especially a crime or accident, has occurred; retroactive.  It refers to actions taken or decisions made after an event has already happened, often implying a lack of foresight or preparedness. | The investigation was an after-the-fact attempt to understand what went wrong. 事后调查是为了了解哪里出了问题。

The after-the-fact justification for the decision was unconvincing. 事后对该决定的辩解并不令人信服。 |  retrospective, ex post facto, subsequent, post hoc |  prior, beforehand, anticipatory | adjective
