barken:/ˈbɑːrkən/ | v. |吠叫；咆哮；厉声说话|To make a loud, harsh sound, typically of a dog.  The sound itself can also be referred to as a bark.|The dog barked at the mailman. 狗对着邮递员吠叫。|近义词：yap, howl, bay, growl|反义词:whimper, whine|词性:动词
barken:/ˈbɑːrkən/ | v. |剥树皮|To remove the bark from a tree.|They barked the logs before building the cabin. 他们在建造小屋之前剥去了原木的树皮。|近义词：peel, strip|反义词:cover|词性:动词
