outissue:/ˌaʊtˈɪʃuː/
|v.|胜过，比…重要； 比…更突出|to surpass or overshadow someone or something; to be more important or prominent than someone or something|The new features of the phone outissue its competitors. 这款手机的新功能胜过它的竞争对手。|surpass, eclipse, outdo, outstrip, transcend|lag behind, fall short of|verb
outissue:/ˌaʊtˈɪʃuː/
|n.|重要议题；突出问题|an important or prominent issue or topic|The economy is the outissue in this election. 经济是这次选举中的重要议题。|key issue, main point, central theme, major concern, important matter|minor issue, trivial matter|noun
