fantasticalnesses:/fænˈtæstɪklnəsɪz/
noun
奇异；奇妙；幻想性
The quality of being fantastical; a collection of fantastical things or qualities.
The fantasticalnesses of her stories captivated the children. 她故事的奇幻之处深深吸引了孩子们。
Near Synonyms:  fantasticality, whimsicality, imaginativeness, unreality, dreaminess
Antonyms:  reality, mundanity, ordinariness
