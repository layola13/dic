covers:/ˈkʌvərz/ (US), /ˈkʌvəz/ (UK)| v. (third-person singular simple present covers, present participle covering, simple past and past participle covered)|覆盖，遮盖，包括，涉及，报道，支付，行走（一段路程）|This blanket covers the entire bed. 这条毯子盖住了整张床。|近义词：conceals, shields, protects, includes, encompasses, reports, traverses|反义词：exposes, reveals, excludes, omits|词性:动词

covers:/ˈkʌvərz/ (US), /ˈkʌvəz/ (UK)| n. (plural)|封面，盖子，掩护物|The book has attractive covers. 这本书的封面很吸引人。|近义词：lids, tops, jackets, exteriors|反义词：insides, interiors, contents|词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *coveren, *keveren, from Old French covrir (French couvrir), from Vulgar Latin *coōperīre, from Latin cooperīre (“to cover completely”), from com- (“together, completely”) + operīre (“to cover, conceal”).

PHRASE->
cover up: 掩盖  He tried to cover up his mistake. 他试图掩盖他的错误。
cover for: 顶替，掩护 I'll cover for you if the boss asks where you are. 如果老板问你在哪里，我会替你掩护。
cover story: 封面故事 The magazine's cover story is about the royal wedding. 这本杂志的封面故事是关于皇室婚礼的。
be covered in/with: 被…覆盖 The car was covered in snow. 汽车被雪覆盖了。
cover the cost/expenses: 支付费用  The company will cover the cost of the trip. 公司将支付旅行费用。
