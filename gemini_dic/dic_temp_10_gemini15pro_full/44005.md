criminally:/ˈkrɪmɪnəli/| adv.|犯罪地，罪恶地；在刑事上|In a criminal manner; involving or relating to a crime. |He was criminally negligent in his duties. 他玩忽职守，罪责难逃。|近义词：illegally, unlawfully, wrongly|反义词:legally, lawfully|词性:副词
词源: From crime + -al + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin word "crimen" meaning "accusation, crime, fault."
