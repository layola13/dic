non-fatal:/ˌnɒnˈfeɪtl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfeɪtl/| adj.|非致命的，非致命性的|Not causing death; not resulting in death.  It describes something, usually an injury or accident, that does not kill anyone.|The victim suffered non-fatal injuries in the car crash.  受害者在车祸中受了非致命伤。|The police reported a non-fatal shooting incident. 警方报告了一起非致命枪击事件。|近义词：harmless, innocuous, nonlethal|反义词:fatal, lethal, deadly|词性:adjective
