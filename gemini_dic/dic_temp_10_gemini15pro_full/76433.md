gainable:/ˈɡeɪnəbl/
adjective
可获得的；可赢得的
That which can be gained or attained.
The summit was gainable despite the difficult terrain. 尽管地形艰难，山顶还是可以到达的。
近义词: attainable, achievable, obtainable, acquirable
反义词: unattainable, unachievable, unavailable
