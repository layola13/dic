affranchisement:/əˈfræntʃɪzmənt/
|n.|解放；释放；赋予公民权|The act of setting free from slavery, bondage, or restraint; emancipation. Also, the act of granting certain privileges or rights, especially the right to vote.|The affranchisement of the serfs marked a turning point in Russian history.  农奴的解放标志着俄罗斯历史的转折点。|近义词：liberation, emancipation, freedom, release|反义词：enslavement, subjugation, oppression|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *affranchiss-, stem of affranchir, from a- (ad-) + franc "free". Ultimately from Frankish *franko "free, not in bondage, belonging to the Franks".
