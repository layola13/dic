brambled:/ˈbræmbəld/| adj.| 荆棘丛生的，灌木丛生的；混乱的，棘手的|Overgrown with brambles; tangled and thorny.  Also, figuratively, difficult or complicated.|例句：The path was brambled and difficult to follow. 小路荆棘丛生，难以通行。

例句：They faced a brambled legal situation. 他们面临着棘手的法律状况。|近义词：thorny, overgrown, tangled, prickly, intricate, convoluted|反义词：clear, straightforward, simple, uncluttered|词性:形容词

bramble:/ˈbræmbəl/| n.| 荆棘；黑莓|A thorny shrub, especially a blackberry bush.|例句：The bramble scratched my leg. 荆棘划伤了我的腿。

例句：We picked blackberries from the bramble. 我们从荆棘丛中摘了黑莓。|近义词：bush, shrub, thicket, briar|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) open ground, clearing|词性:名词
