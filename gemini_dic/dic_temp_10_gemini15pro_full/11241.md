archonship: /ˈɑːrkɒnʃɪp/ (US) /ˈɑːkɒnʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|执政官的职位；执政官任期|The position or term of office of an archon.  An archon was a high-ranking magistrate in ancient Athens.|His archonship was marked by significant political reforms. 他的执政官任期以重大的政治改革为标志。|Synonyms: magistracy, office, reign|Antonyms: subordination, subjection|noun
