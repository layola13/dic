officialdom: /əˈfɪʃəldəm/
n.
官僚机构；官僚作风；官员；官场
The body of officials in an organization or government; the ways of behaving that are typical of officials.
The project has been held up by officialdom.  这个项目被官僚作风耽搁了。
Synonyms: bureaucracy, administration, the establishment, authority
Antonyms:  citizenry, the public, the people
词性: 名词
