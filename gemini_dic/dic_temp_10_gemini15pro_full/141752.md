oranges:/ˈɔːrɪndʒɪz/
n.
橙子;橘子
A round juicy citrus fruit with a reddish-yellow rind.
I bought a bag of oranges at the grocery store. 我在杂货店买了一袋橙子。

oranges:/ˈɔːrɪndʒɪz/
n.
橙色
The colour between red and yellow; the colour of a ripe orange.
The sunset painted the sky in vibrant shades of oranges. 日落将天空染成了鲜艳的橙色。

oranges:/ˈɔːrɪndʒɪz/
adj.(罕见)
橙色的
Of the colour orange.
She wore an oranges dress to the party. 她穿了一件橙色的连衣裙去参加聚会。


Etymology: From Old French *orenge (modern French orange), from Arabic nāranj, from Persian nārang, from Sanskrit nāranga ("orange tree").

Synonyms: (for fruit) citrus, tangerine, clementine (sometimes, depending on variety)
Synonyms: (for color)  amber,  carrot,  coral,  gold,  mandarin,  peach,  salmon,  tangerine
Antonyms: (for color) blue, green, purple
