antipopularization:/ˌæntiˌpɒpjələraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 反普及化，反通俗化 | The process of making something less popular or accessible to the general public.  It can involve deliberately making something more complex, expensive, or exclusive. | The antipopularization of scientific knowledge makes it harder for the average person to understand. 反普及化科学知识使得普通人更难理解。|近义词: obfuscation, mystification |反义词: popularization, simplification |词性: noun
