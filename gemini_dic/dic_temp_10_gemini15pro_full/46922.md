darling:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdɑːlɪŋ/ (UK)| n. adj.|亲爱的，宠儿，心爱的人/物，可爱的|A term of endearment, or referring to someone or something much loved. Can also be used as an adjective to mean cute or sweet.|例句：She called him "darling" affectionately. 她亲切地叫他“亲爱的”。|近义词：dear, sweetheart, beloved, honey, cute, sweet|反义词：detested, hated|词性:名词, 形容词
darling:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdɑːlɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|可爱的，讨人喜欢的|Used to describe something or someone charming or appealing.|例句：Look at that darling little puppy! 看那只可爱的小狗！|近义词：adorable, charming, lovely|反义词：unpleasant, unattractive|词性:形容词
