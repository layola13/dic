fulled:/fʊld/
v.|使饱满，变浓，把（布）弄厚|to make something full or thicker, especially cloth, by shrinking it|The fabric was fulled and shrunk to create a denser texture.|近义词: thicken, condense, shrink|反义词: thin, stretch|词性:动词


fulled:/fʊld/
adj.|饱满的，浓厚的（通常指布）|full and thick, usually referring to cloth|The fulled wool felt warm and substantial.|近义词: thick, dense, substantial|反义词: thin, flimsy|词性:形容词


词源:  From the Old English *fullian, "to cleanse, whiten, make full," related to "full."  The sense of thickening cloth developed from the process of shrinking and felting it by cleansing.
