careen:/kəˈriːn/
verb
倾斜，(尤指车辆)歪歪斜斜地行驶；猛地倾斜
To lean, sway, or tip to one side while in motion. (especially of a vehicle) to swerve or lurch from side to side while moving at speed.
The car careened around the corner. 汽车歪歪斜斜地驶过拐角。
The bus careened down the hill, narrowly missing a tree. 公共汽车歪歪斜斜地冲下山坡，差点撞到一棵树。

近义词:lurch, sway, tilt, tip, veer, swerve
反义词:stabilize, steady

词性:v.

词源:From the French *cariner*, meaning "to lay a ship on its side for cleaning or repair", from *carene* "keel, bottom of a ship."
