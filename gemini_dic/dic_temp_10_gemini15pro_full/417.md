abjurer: /əbˈdʒʊərər/
n.|放弃者，发誓放弃者|Someone who abjures a belief or way of life formally rejects or renounces it. |He was an abjurer of his former faith.  他放弃了他以前的信仰。

The king demanded the abjurer swear an oath of loyalty to the new religion. 国王要求放弃者宣誓效忠新宗教。|近义词：renouncer, repudiator, retractor|反义词：advocate, supporter, upholder|词性：名词

abjure: /əbˈdʒʊr/
vt.|正式放弃，发誓放弃，公开放弃|To abjure something means to formally renounce or reject it.  |She abjured her previous lifestyle. 她放弃了她以前的生活方式。

He abjured his allegiance to the king. 他宣布放弃对国王的效忠。|近义词：renounce, forswear, repudiate|反义词：affirm, uphold, embrace|词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abjures violence. = 他放弃暴力。
She abjures all forms of discrimination. = 她公开反对一切形式的歧视。

一般过去时:
He abjured his old beliefs. = 他放弃了他以前的信仰。
She abjured her claim to the throne. = 她放弃了对王位的要求。

现在完成时:
He has abjured his former life of crime. = 他已经放弃了他以前的犯罪生活。
She has abjured her allegiance to the old regime. = 她已经宣布放弃效忠旧政权。

过去完成时:
He had abjured his faith before he joined the army. = 他在参军前就已经放弃了他的信仰。
She had abjured her right to an inheritance before she left the country. = 她在离开这个国家之前就已经放弃了她的继承权。

将来时:
He will abjure his membership in the organization. = 他将放弃在该组织的成员资格。
She will abjure her past actions. = 她将宣布放弃她过去的行为。

虚拟语气:
If he were to abjure his beliefs, he would be ostracized. = 如果他放弃他的信仰，他将被排斥。
I wish she would abjure her reckless behavior. = 我希望她能放弃她鲁莽的行为。

被动语态:
His former lifestyle was abjured. = 他的以前的生活方式被放弃了。
All forms of violence are abjured by this community. = 这个社区放弃了所有形式的暴力。


PHRASE->
abjure all rights to... = 放弃所有...的权利
abjure one's former beliefs = 放弃以前的信仰
abjure the use of force = 放弃使用武力
abjure one's allegiance to... = 放弃对...的效忠
abjure a claim to... = 放弃对...的索赔


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abjuren*, from Old French *abjurer*, from Latin *abjūrāre* (“to deny on oath, forswear”), from *ab-* (“away from”) + *jūrāre* (“to swear”).  The word literally means "to swear away from."
