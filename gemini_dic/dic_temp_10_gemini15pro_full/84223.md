half-scottish:/ˌhɑːf ˈskɒtɪʃ/| adj.|半苏格兰血统的；|Of mixed Scottish and other ancestry.  A person who has one parent of Scottish descent and one of another. |Her father was English and her mother Scottish, making her half-Scottish. 她的父亲是英国人，母亲是苏格兰人，这使得她是半个苏格兰血统。|Mixed race, dual heritage|Pure-blooded|adjective
