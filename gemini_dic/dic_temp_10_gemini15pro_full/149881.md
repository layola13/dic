palindromist:/ˈpælɪnˌdroʊmɪst/| n.|回文爱好者，回文创作者|A person who creates or is interested in palindromes (words, phrases, or numbers that read the same backward as forward).|例句：The palindromist spent hours crafting a new poem that could be read both forwards and backwards.  这位回文爱好者花了几个小时创作一首可以正反读的诗。

例句：He was a known palindromist, constantly challenging himself to create longer and more complex palindromes. 他是一位著名的回文爱好者，不断挑战自己，创作更长、更复杂的回文。|近义词：wordsmith, logophile|反义词: None|词性: Noun
