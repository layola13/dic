interosculant:/ˌɪntərˈɒskjʊlənt/
adjective
相互吻合的；中间过渡的
Blending or merging characters of two groups so as to be difficult to classify; intermediate.  Having characteristics common to two or more groups and difficult to classify.
例句：The interosculant species made it difficult to distinguish between the two plant families.  这些相互吻合的物种使得很难区分这两个植物科。

例句：The fossil record reveals several interosculant forms that bridge the gap between reptiles and birds. 化石记录揭示了几种连接爬行动物和鸟类之间差距的过渡形态。
近义词：intermediate, transitional, connecting, bridging
反义词：distinct, separate, discrete
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *inter* ("between") + *osculant* (present participle of *osculari* "to kiss").  Thus, literally "kissing between".
