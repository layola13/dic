grays:/ɡreɪz/| n.| 灰色；灰马；灰色颜料；灰衣服；格雷斯（女子名）|A mixture of black and white; achromatic; intermediate in tone or shade between white and black.  Also, horses with gray coats, or things of a gray color.|The artist mixed several grays for the background.  艺术家为背景混合了几种灰色。|近义词：grey, ash, silver, slate|反义词:black, white|词性:名词

grays:/ɡreɪz/| v. (gray 的第三人称单数形式)| 变灰；使变灰|To become or cause to become gray.|His hair began to gray at the temples. 他的头发开始在太阳穴变灰。|近义词：age, whiten|反义词:blacken, darken|词性:动词
