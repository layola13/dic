bootless:/ˈbuːtlɪs/| adj.|无用的，徒劳的，无益的|Without advantage or benefit; unprofitable; useless.|His bootless attempts to find a job left him discouraged. 他徒劳的求职尝试让他感到灰心丧气。|例句：All our efforts proved bootless. 我们所有的努力都证明是徒劳的。|例句：It was a bootless errand, as the shop was closed. 这趟差事白跑了，因为商店关门了。|近义词：futile, useless, vain, ineffective|反义词：useful, effective, fruitful, productive|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *boteløs, from Old English *bōtlēas (“remedyless, incurable, irremediable, bootless”), equivalent to boot +‎ -less. Cognate with Scots butless (“useless, unprofitable”).
