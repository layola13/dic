noob:/nuːb/ | n. |菜鸟，新手|A person who is inexperienced in a particular sphere or activity, especially computing or the Internet.|例句：He's such a noob at this game. 他玩这个游戏真是个菜鸟。|例句：Don't be such a noob!  别这么菜鸟！|近义词：newbie, beginner, rookie, amateur|反义词：expert, veteran, pro, professional|词性:名词

词源:  Shortening of newbie, which itself is a slang respelling of *new boy*.
