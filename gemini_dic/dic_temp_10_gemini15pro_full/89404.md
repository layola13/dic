homosystemic:/ˌhoʊmoʊsɪˈstɛmɪk/
adjective
同性系统内的; 与同性恋群体相关的
Relating to or operating within the systems and structures of same-sex relationships or the LGBTQ+ community.  Specifically, it acknowledges the unique social, cultural, and political dynamics within this community, as distinct from heteronormative systems.
Example:  Homosystemic power dynamics can be complex and influenced by factors like age, race, and class.  同性系统内的权力动态可能很复杂，并受年龄、种族和阶级等因素的影响。

Example:  Researchers are studying homosystemic support networks and their impact on mental health.  研究人员正在研究同性系统内的支持网络及其对心理健康的影响。
Synonym:  intra-LGBTQ+, within the LGBTQ+ community
Antonym:  heterosystemic, heteronormative
词性: adjective
