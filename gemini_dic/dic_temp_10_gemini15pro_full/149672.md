paisley-print:/ˈpeɪzli prɪnt/| adj. n.|佩斯利印花，佩斯利图案|A teardrop-shaped pattern of Persian origin. It became very popular in the West in the 19th century, especially in Scotland.|She wore a paisley-print dress.|Synonyms: paisley pattern, teardrop pattern|Antonyms: solid color, plain|adjective, noun

paisley:/ˈpeɪzli/| n. adj.|佩斯利，佩斯利花纹的|A town in west central Scotland, near Glasgow; population 76,800 (est. 2009). It was formerly a major centre of textile manufacture, especially of paisley, a distinctive intricate pattern of curved feather-shaped figures.|The shawl had a beautiful paisley design.|Synonyms: paisley pattern, teardrop motif|Antonyms: solid color, plain|noun, adjective
