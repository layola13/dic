maritime:/ˈmærɪtaɪm/ (US), /ˈmærɪtɪm/ (UK)| adj.|海事的；海运的；近海的|Connected with the sea, especially in relation to seafaring commerce or naval affairs.  Relating to or bordering on the sea.|例句：The maritime industry is vital to global trade. 海事行业对全球贸易至关重要。

例句：The country has a long and rich maritime history.  这个国家拥有悠久而丰富的航海历史。|近义词：naval, marine, oceanic, nautical|反义词：inland, terrestrial|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle French *maritime* and directly from Latin *maritimus* "of or belonging to the sea," from *mare* "sea."
