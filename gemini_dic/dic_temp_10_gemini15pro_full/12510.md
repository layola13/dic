aspersoir: /ˌæspɜːrˈswɑːr/ | n. |洒水器; 洒水壶 |A watering device, often a perforated vessel or a watering can with a rose, used for sprinkling water, especially in religious ceremonies or gardening. |The priest used an aspersoir to sprinkle holy water on the congregation.  神父用洒水器向会众洒圣水。

The gardener filled the aspersoir and watered the delicate seedlings. 园丁装满了洒水器，给娇嫩的幼苗浇水。| 近义词: sprinkler, watering can | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *aspersoir*, from *asperser* "to sprinkle," from Latin *aspergere* "to sprinkle upon," from *ad-* "to" + *spargere* "to scatter."
