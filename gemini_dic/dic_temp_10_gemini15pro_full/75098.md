fringed:/frɪndʒd/
adjective
有边的，有流苏的
bordered or edged with fringe.
The fringed tablecloth added a touch of elegance to the dining room.  有流苏的桌布给餐厅增添了一丝优雅。
The plant has fringed leaves. 这种植物的叶子有边。

近义词: bordered, edged, tasselled, fringed
反义词: plain, unadorned

词性: adjective

词源: From fringe + -ed.  From the noun fringe, meaning "decorative border of threads," + the suffix -ed, which forms adjectives from nouns.  The noun "fringe" itself comes from Old French "frenge," ultimately from Latin "fimbria," meaning "fiber, thread."
