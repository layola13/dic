hyperperistalses:/ˌhaɪpərˌpɛrɪˈstɔːlsiːz/
|n.|亢进的蠕动|Excessively rapid waves of contractions passing along tubular organs, such as the intestines.  It's an abnormally increased frequency and strength of peristalsis.|The patient experienced hyperperistalses after eating contaminated food.|近义词：hypermotility, spasm|反义词:hypoperistalsis, atony|noun
