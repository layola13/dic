gothicism: /ˈɡɒθɪˌsɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːθɪˌsɪzəm/ (US)| n. |哥特主义，哥特式风格|A style in architecture, literature, and art originating in medieval Europe, characterized by pointed arches, ribbed vaults, flying buttresses, intricate tracery, and often grotesque or macabre ornamentation. In literature, gothicism emphasizes the mysterious, the supernatural, the grotesque, and the macabre.|例句：The gothicism of the old mansion sent shivers down her spine.  这座古老宅邸的哥特式风格让她毛骨悚然。

例句：Edgar Allan Poe's stories are prime examples of American gothicism.  埃德加·爱伦·坡的故事是美国哥特主义的典型例子。|近义词：Gothic, Gothic architecture, Gothic literature|反义词:Classicism, Romanticism|词性:noun
