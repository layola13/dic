chitter:/ˈtʃɪtər/| v. n.|快速地低声说话，唧唧喳喳地叫；叽叽喳喳声|To make a series of short, high-pitched sounds, like a bird or insect.  As a noun, it refers to the sound itself.|The birds were chittering in the trees.  鸟儿在树上唧唧喳喳地叫。|近义词：twitter, chatter, chirp|反义词：(no clear antonym for the sound itself; for the verb, perhaps "be silent")|词性：verb, noun
