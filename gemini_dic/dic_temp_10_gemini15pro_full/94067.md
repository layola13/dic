impassionable:/ɪmˈpæʃənəbəl/
adjective
冷漠的，无动于衷的
Not easily roused to strong feeling; apathetic.
例句：His impassionable face gave nothing away.  他那张冷漠的脸什么也没流露出来。

例句：She remained impassionable throughout the trial, never shedding a tear. 在整个审判过程中，她始终无动于衷，从未流过一滴眼泪。

近义词：unemotional, apathetic, indifferent, stoic, phlegmatic, impassive
反义词：passionate, emotional, ardent, fervent, zealous
词性: adjective

词源：Late 16th century: from in- (expressing negation) + passion + -able.
