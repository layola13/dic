miniaturized: /ˈmɪnɪətʃəraɪzd/ | adj. |小型化的，微型化的 | Made smaller than usual; reduced in size. |  例句：The miniaturized device is easier to carry.  这个小型化的设备更容易携带。

例句：Scientists have developed a miniaturized camera that can be implanted in the eye. 科学家们开发了一种可以植入眼睛的微型相机。

近义词：small, tiny, compact, miniature, reduced | 反义词：enlarged, magnified, expanded | 词性：形容词

词源: From miniature + -ize + -ed.  "Miniature" comes from the Italian word "miniatura," originally referring to the small pictures used to illustrate medieval manuscripts.
