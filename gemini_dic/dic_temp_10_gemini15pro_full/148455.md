overstraighten: /ˌoʊvərˈstreɪtn/
verb
矫枉过正；过度拉直
To straighten something too much, beyond what is natural or desirable.
The hairdresser overstraightened my hair, and now it looks flat and lifeless.  理发师把我的头发拉得太直了，现在看起来又扁又没生气。
He overstraightened his tie, making it too tight. 他把领带拉得太紧了。

近义词: overcorrect, overdo
反义词: undercorrect, underdo
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "straighten" (verb meaning "to make or become straight")
