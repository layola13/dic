leftism: /ˈlɛftɪzəm/ | n. | 左翼主义；左派思想 |  A political philosophy or ideology that emphasizes social equality, often favoring government intervention to achieve this.  It encompasses a range of views from moderate social democracy to revolutionary communism. |  The rise of leftism in the 20th century led to significant social and political changes. 20世纪左翼主义的兴起导致了重大的社会和政治变革。 | synonyms:  progressivism, liberalism, radicalism, socialism | antonyms: rightism, conservatism, reactionism | noun
