coercively:/koʊˈɜːrsɪvli/
adverb
强制地；胁迫地
in a way that uses force or threats to make someone do something
The government coercively implemented the new policy. 政府强制执行了新政策。
The confession was obtained coercively.  这份供词是胁迫下获得的。
Synonym: forcibly, compulsorily, by force
Antonym: voluntarily, willingly, freely
词性: 副词
