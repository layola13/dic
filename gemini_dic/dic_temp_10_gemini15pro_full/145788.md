overdebate:/ˌoʊvərdiˈbeɪt/ | v. | 过度辩论，过度讨论 | To debate or discuss something excessively, often to the point of being unproductive. | The issue of school uniforms has been overdebated, and no consensus has been reached. 校服问题已经被过度讨论，没有达成共识。 |  They overdebated the merits of the different proposals and missed the deadline. 他们过度讨论了不同提案的优点，错过了截止日期。 |  overdiscuss, overanalyze | underdiscuss | verb
