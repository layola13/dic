blinkering:/ˈblɪŋkərɪŋ/| n. |狭隘，蒙蔽，限制|The act of limiting someone's perspective or understanding, like putting blinders on a horse.|His blinkering prevented him from seeing the bigger picture. 他的狭隘阻止了他看到更大的局面。|近义词：limiting, restricting, narrowing, blinding|反义词: expanding, broadening, enlightening|noun
blinkering:/ˈblɪŋkərɪŋ/| adj. |狭隘的，蒙蔽的，限制的|Having a limited perspective or understanding.|Her blinkering worldview made it difficult for her to accept new ideas. 她狭隘的世界观使她难以接受新思想。|近义词：narrow-minded, parochial, insular, myopic|反义词: open-minded, broad-minded, cosmopolitan|adjective
