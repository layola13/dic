chirp:/tʃɜːrp/ (US); /tʃɜːp/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|啁啾声，唧唧声；(使)发出啁啾声|A short, sharp, high-pitched sound, often made by birds or insects.  To make such a sound.|The birds chirped merrily in the trees. 鸟儿在树上欢快地啁啾。|近义词：twitter, peep, cheep|反义词:croak, growl|词性:名词，动词

chirp:/tʃɜːrp/ (US); /tʃɜːp/ (UK)| vi. vt.|愉快地说；轻松地说；(使)愉快地说；(使)轻松地说|(informal) To speak in a lively, cheerful, and often high-pitched tone. To cause someone to speak in such a tone.|She chirped a greeting to her friends. 她愉快地跟朋友们打招呼。|近义词：chatter, babble|反义词:mumble, mutter|词性:动词
