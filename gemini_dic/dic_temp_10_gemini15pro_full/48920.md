defrosts:/diːˈfrɒsts/ (UK), /diːˈfrɔːsts/ (US)| v. |除霜，解冻| To remove frost or ice from something, or to become free of frost or ice. | I defrosted the chicken before cooking it. 我在烹饪鸡肉之前先解冻了它。

The refrigerator automatically defrosts.  这台冰箱会自动除霜。|近义词：thaws, melts|反义词：freezes|词性：动词
