monolingual:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəl/ /ˌmɑːnəˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj. n.|单语的；使用单一语言的人|Speaking only one language; a person who speaks only one language.|She is monolingual in English. 她只说英语。|近义词：unilingual|反义词：bilingual, multilingual|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology:  From mono- (one) + lingual (related to language). Ultimately from Greek monos (single, alone) and Latin lingua (tongue, language).
