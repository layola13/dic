on-side:/ˈɒnˈsaɪd/ (UK), /ˈɑːnˈsaɪd/ (US)| adj.|处于合法位置的；赞同的；支持的|Being in a legal or permitted position, especially in sports; in agreement; supportive|The referee ruled that the player was on-side and the goal was valid. 裁判判定球员没有越位，进球有效。|Synonyms: legal, legitimate, valid, supportive, agreeing|Antonyms: off-side, illegal, illegitimate, opposed, dissenting|adjective

on-side:/ˈɒnˈsaɪd/ (UK), /ˈɑːnˈsaɪd/ (US)| adv.|在合法的位置上|In a legal or permitted position|The player moved on-side just before receiving the pass.  这名球员在接到传球前正好回到了合法位置。|Synonyms: legally, legitimately|Antonyms: off-side, illegally|adverb
