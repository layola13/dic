deteriorate:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/| v. |恶化，变坏|to become worse in quality, character, or condition|例句：The patient's condition began to deteriorate rapidly. 病人的病情开始迅速恶化。|例句：Relations between the two countries have deteriorated sharply. 两国关系急剧恶化。|近义词：worsen, decline, degenerate|反义词:improve, enhance, ameliorate|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Latin *dēteriorātus, past participle of dēteriorāre (“to make worse”), from Latin dēterior (“worse”).
