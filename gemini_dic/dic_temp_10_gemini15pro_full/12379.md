ashrams:/ˈɑːʃrɑːmz/
n.
修行处；静修院；道场
A place of religious retreat or community living, typically associated with Hinduism or other Indian religions.
He spent several years living in an ashram in India. 他在印度的修行院里住了几年。
retreat, monastery, convent, sanctuary
