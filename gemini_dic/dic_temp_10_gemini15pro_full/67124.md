exponentiation:/ˌɛkspoʊˌnɛnʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 指数运算，乘方 | The process of raising a number to a power. |  The exponentiation of 2 to the power of 3 is 8 (2³ = 8).  2的3次方是8。 | synonyms: powering, raising to a power | antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym for a mathematical operation) | noun


exponentiations:/ˌɛkspoʊˌnɛnʃiˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 指数运算（复数）| Multiple instances of raising numbers to powers. | The calculations involved several exponentiations.  这些计算涉及多次指数运算。 | synonyms:  powerings, raisings to powers | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From exponent + -iation.  Exponent ultimately comes from Latin *exponentem, present participle of exponere "to put forth, set forth, explain," from ex- "out" + ponere "to put, place."
