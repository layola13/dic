nonsibilant:/ˌnɑːnˈsɪbɪlənt/ | adj. | 非咝音的 |  Not producing or characterized by a hissing sound.  A nonsibilant consonant is one that is not produced by forcing air through a narrow channel in the mouth, creating a hissing sound. Sibilant consonants include /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /tʃ/, and /dʒ/. | The "th" sound in "think" is a nonsibilant. “think”中的“th”音是非咝音。| 近义词: non-hissing | 反义词: sibilant | 词性: adjective
