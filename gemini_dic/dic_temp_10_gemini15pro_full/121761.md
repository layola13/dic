misqualifying:/ˌmɪsˈkwɑːlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
使失去资格的；取消资格的
Causing someone or something to no longer have the right to do or have something.
His third warning for unsportsmanlike conduct resulted in a misqualifying penalty.  他因违反体育道德行为的第三次警告而被取消比赛资格。

Her lack of required certifications was a misqualifying factor for the job. 她缺少必要的证书，这是她不符合该职位要求的一个因素。

近义词: disqualifying, invalidating
反义词: qualifying, validating
词性: adjective
