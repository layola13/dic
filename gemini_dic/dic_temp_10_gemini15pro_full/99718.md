interlude:/ˈɪntərluːd/| n.|间奏; 插曲; 幕间休息|A period of time between two events or activities, typically providing a contrast to what comes before and after.|The band played a short interlude while the stage was being set up. 乐队在舞台布置期间演奏了一段简短的间奏曲。|After a brief interlude, the speaker continued his presentation.  短暂的休息之后，演讲者继续他的演讲。|synonyms: break, interval, pause, respite|antonyms: continuation,  persistence|noun
