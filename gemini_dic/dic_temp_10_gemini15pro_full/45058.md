cubemap:/ˈkjuːbmæp/ | n. | 立方体贴图 | A cubemap is a set of six square textures that represent the reflections in an environment.  It's like wrapping a box around an object and capturing the environment from each face of the box. | The game uses a cubemap to create realistic reflections on the car's surface.  游戏使用立方体贴图在汽车表面创建逼真的反射。 | synonyms: environment map, reflection map | antonyms: N/A | noun

cubemap:/ˈkjuːbmæp/ | n. | 立方体贴图 |  A method of environment mapping that uses the six faces of a cube to store images representing reflections. This allows for a wider range of viewing angles compared to simpler methods. |  The cubemap provided realistic reflections of the skybox. 立方体贴图提供了逼真的天空盒反射。 | synonyms: environment map, reflection map | antonyms: N/A | noun
