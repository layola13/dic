indisposed:/ˌɪndɪˈspoʊzd/ | adj. | 不舒服的，不愿意的 | slightly unwell, or unwilling to do something |  I'm feeling a bit indisposed today and won't be coming into work.  我今天感觉有点不舒服，不会来上班了。|  She was indisposed to accept his invitation. 她不愿意接受他的邀请。 | 近义词: unwell, ill, reluctant, averse | 反义词: well, healthy, willing, eager | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + disposed (inclined).  The word "disposed" comes from Old French "disposer" (to arrange, order), from Latin "disponere" (to put in different places, arrange, distribute).
