decussate:/ˌdiːkʌˈseɪt/
adjective|交叉的, 交叉成X形的|Arranged in the form of an X; intersecting.|The plant has decussate leaves. 这种植物的叶子是交叉排列的。|crossed, intersecting, overlapping|parallel, separate|adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *decussatus*, past participle of *decusso*, from *decussis* "a crossing, an X", from *decem* "ten" + *as* (genitive *assis*) "unit of weight, money".  Originally referred to the Roman numeral X (ten).
