lovelorn:/ˈlʌvlɔːrn/| adj.|失恋的，因爱情而悲伤的|Pining or grieving because of unrequited love or the absence of a loved one.  Deserted by one's lover; heartbroken.|She wrote a lovelorn poem about her lost love. 她写了一首关于她失去的爱情的悲伤的诗。|Synonyms: heartbroken, lovesick, forlorn, desolate, bereft|Antonyms: happy, joyful, content|Adjective
