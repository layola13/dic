musketeers:/ˌmʌskəˈtɪərz/| n. |火枪手；火枪兵|Soldiers armed with muskets.  A body of soldiers armed with muskets, especially as a royal guard in France. |The Three Musketeers is a famous novel by Alexandre Dumas.  《三个火枪手》是亚历山大·仲马的著名小说。|Synonyms: guardsmen, soldiers, riflemen |Antonyms: civilians |noun
