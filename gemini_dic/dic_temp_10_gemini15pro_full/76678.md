galvanograph:/ɡælˈvænəɡrɑːf/
noun
电流记录仪，电镀电流记录器
An instrument that records variations in current during electrodeposition.
The galvanograph recorded a steady increase in current during the experiment. 电流记录仪记录了实验过程中电流的稳定增长。
galvanometer, ammeter
--
noun
电流图
A record produced by a galvanograph.
The galvanograph showed a series of peaks and troughs. 电流图显示出一系列的峰值和谷值。
graph, chart
