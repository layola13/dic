flea-marketeers:/ˈfliː ˌmɑːr.kɪˈtɪərz/
n.
跳蚤市场摊贩；经常逛跳蚤市场的人
People who sell goods at flea markets or frequently attend them.
The flea-marketeers were setting up their stalls early in the morning.  跳蚤市场摊贩们一大早就开始摆摊了。
Experienced flea-marketeers know how to haggle for a good price.  经验丰富的跳蚤市场顾客知道如何讨价还价。
近义词: vendors, sellers, bargain hunters
反义词: buyers, customers (in a broader sense)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
flea market + -er + -s
"flea market"  (跳蚤市场) 的起源有几种说法，最流行的是指二手商品市场可能滋生跳蚤。 "-er" 是一个后缀，表示从事某种活动的人。 "-s" 表示复数。
