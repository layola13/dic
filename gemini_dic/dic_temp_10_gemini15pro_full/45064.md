cubic:/ˈkjuːbɪk/| adj.|立方的，三次的|shaped like a cube; relating to the third power of a number|例句：The box has a cubic capacity of one meter. 这个盒子的容积为一立方米。|近义词：three-dimensional, cube-shaped|反义词:linear, two-dimensional|词性:adjective
cubic:/ˈkjuːbɪk/| n.|立方体，三次方|a cube; the third power of a number|例句：Eight is the cubic of two. 八是二的立方。|近义词：cube|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin cubicus, from Latin cubus (“cube”), from Ancient Greek κύβος (kúbos, “cube, die”).
