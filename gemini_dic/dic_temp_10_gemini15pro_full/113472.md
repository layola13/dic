lyrebird: /ˈlaɪəbɜːrd/ (US), /ˈlaɪəbɜːd/ (UK)| n. |琴鸟|A bird found in Australia, known for its remarkable ability to mimic sounds, including other bird calls, human speech, and even machinery noises.|The lyrebird is a master of mimicry. 琴鸟是模仿大师。|The lyrebird's song is a complex mix of its own calls and imitations. 琴鸟的歌声是它自己叫声和模仿的复杂组合。|近义词:mimic, imitator|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "original" in terms of sound production)|词性:noun
