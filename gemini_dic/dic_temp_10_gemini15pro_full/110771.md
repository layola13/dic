linguist:/ˈlɪŋɡwɪst/| n. |语言学家|A person skilled in foreign languages. A specialist in linguistics.|She's a brilliant linguist, fluent in five languages. 她是一位杰出的语言学家，精通五种语言。|Synonyms: polyglot, philologist, translator, interpreter|Antonyms: monoglot|noun
