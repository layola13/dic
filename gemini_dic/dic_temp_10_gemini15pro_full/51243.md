destructions:/dɪˈstrʌkʃənz/| n.|毁灭，破坏，摧毁|The earthquake caused widespread destructions. 地震造成了大范围的破坏。|例句：The destructions caused by the war were devastating. 战争造成的破坏是毁灭性的。

The destructions of the rainforest have a negative impact on the environment.  雨林的破坏对环境有负面影响。

|近义词：damage, ruin, devastation, havoc|反义词:construction, creation, repair, restoration|词性:名词(plural)

Etymology: From Middle English *destruccion, from Anglo-Norman destrucciun,  Old French destruction, and directly from Latin dēstructiō (“a pulling down, demolition, destruction”), from dēstruō (“I pull down, demolish, destroy”) + -tiō.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *strews- (“to flatten, stretch, spread”).
