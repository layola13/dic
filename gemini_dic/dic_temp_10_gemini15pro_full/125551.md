multistranded:/ˌmʌltiˈstrændɪd/| adj.|多股的，多链的|Composed of multiple strands; having many different aspects or elements.  Many strands twisted or braided together, as of a rope or wire; complex; multifaceted.|The virus has a multistranded genome. 这种病毒拥有多链基因组。|Synonyms: multifaceted, complex, intricate, multilayered|Antonyms: simple, single-stranded, unidimensional|Adjective
