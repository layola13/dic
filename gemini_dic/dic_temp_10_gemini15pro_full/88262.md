hinglish:/ˈhɪŋɡlɪʃ/
n.
混杂语;印地语-英语
A blend of Hindi and English.
She often speaks Hinglish with her family. 她经常和家人说混杂语。
近义词:Hinglish, Indlish
反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider "pure Hindi" or "pure English")
词性: noun
