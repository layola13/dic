castigates:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪts/
verb
严厉批评；责骂
reprimand (someone) severely.
The teacher castigates the student for being late. 老师严厉批评了迟到的学生。
The judge castigated the defendant for his dishonesty. 法官严厉斥责了被告的不诚实行为。
rebuke, reprimand, censure, criticize, condemn
praise, commend, laud, approve
v.
