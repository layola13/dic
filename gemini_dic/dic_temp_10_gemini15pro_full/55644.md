ditheistical:/ˌdaɪθiːˈɪstɪkəl/
adjective
二神论的
Believing in two gods, especially two equal and opposing gods.
The ancient Manichean religion was ditheistical, believing in a god of good and a god of evil.  古代摩尼教是二神论的，相信善神和恶神。
