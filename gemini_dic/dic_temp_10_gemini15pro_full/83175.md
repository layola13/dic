gutturality:/ˌɡʌtəˈrælɪti/ | n. |喉音，喉音化|The gutturality of his voice made him sound menacing. 他的嗓音低沉沙哑，听起来很吓人。|The gutturality of the singer's voice added a unique depth to the performance.  这位歌手沙哑的嗓音为表演增添了独特的深度。|近义词：hoarseness, raspiness, gruffness|反义词: smoothness, melodiousness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *gutturalitas, from Latin gutturālis (“of the throat”) + -ity.  Ultimately from Latin guttur (“throat”).
