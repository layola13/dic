avigato:/əˌviːˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /əˌviːˈɡɑːtəʊ/ | interj. |谢谢 (非正式，幽默用法) | A humorous and informal way of saying "thank you", often used in online communities and derived from a mispronunciation of "arrivederci". It's often associated with the character Super Mario.  | Example: "Avigato, friend, for the gold!" 谢谢你，朋友，给了我金币！ | Example: "Avigato! I appreciate the help." 谢谢！我很感激你的帮助。| Synonym: thanks, thank you, cheers (informal) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an expression of gratitude) | Word Type: Interjection
