fantasticism:/fænˈtæstɪˌsɪzəm/
n.
幻想主义；狂想风格；奇想
A tendency to fantasize; the creation or development of fantasies.  A literary or artistic style of the fantastic.
例句：The author's fantasticism is evident in his depiction of talking animals and mythical creatures.  作者对会说话的动物和神话生物的描写，充分展现了他的幻想主义风格。

例句：Her art is characterized by a playful fantasticism that blurs the lines between reality and dreams. 她的艺术特点是充满趣味的幻想主义，模糊了现实与梦境之间的界限。

近义词：fantasy, imagination, reverie, daydreaming
反义词：realism, practicality

词性: noun
