aseptics: /eɪˈsɛptɪks/ | n. |无菌法；无菌技术；防腐法|The practice of preventing contamination by harmful microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi.  Maintaining a sterile environment is crucial in aseptics.|例句：Aseptics are crucial in surgery. 无菌法在外科手术中至关重要。

例句：The nurse followed strict aseptics when changing the dressing.  护士在换药时严格遵守无菌操作。|近义词：sterilization, disinfection|反义词：sepsis, contamination|词性: 名词


Etymology: From a- (not) + septic (relating to sepsis or putrefaction). Ultimately from Greek *sēpein* (to rot).
