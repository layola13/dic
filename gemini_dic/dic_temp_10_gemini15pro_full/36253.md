cognoscente:/ˌkɒnjəˈʃɛnteɪ/ /ˌkɑːnjəˈʃɛnteɪ/ | n. | 鉴赏家；行家；内行 | A person who has expert knowledge in a particular subject, especially in the fine arts or food and drink. |  She is a cognoscente of Italian opera. 她是一位意大利歌剧鉴赏家。

The restaurant is popular with cognoscenti who appreciate the chef's innovative cuisine.  这家餐厅很受那些欣赏厨师创新菜肴的美食家的欢迎。


| 近义词：connoisseur, expert, specialist, authority | 反义词: amateur, novice, beginner | 词性: noun
