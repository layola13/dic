nonincreases:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkriːsɪz/ | n. | 不增加；非增长 |  A state where there is no increase in quantity, size, or number.  It implies stability or even a slight decrease but not a significant drop. | The nonincrease in sales this quarter is concerning. 这个季度销售额的不增长令人担忧。|  The graph showed a period of nonincreases followed by a sharp decline. 图表显示了一段时间的非增长，随后急剧下降。| 近义词: stagnation, plateau, stability | 反义词: increase, growth, rise | 词性: 名词


词源: non- + increase.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "increase" refers to becoming greater in size, amount, or degree.
