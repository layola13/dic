high-handed:/haɪˈhæːndɪd/ | adj. | 高傲的，专横的，霸道的 | using power or authority without considering the feelings or wishes of other people |  The government's high-handed approach to the protests drew international criticism. 政府对抗议活动的高压态势引起了国际社会的批评。

The manager's high-handed treatment of his staff led to low morale. 经理对员工专横跋扈的态度导致了士气低落。


| 近义词: arrogant, dictatorial, domineering, imperious, overbearing, tyrannical | 反义词: considerate, democratic, humble, modest, respectful | 词性: 形容词

high-handed manner: 专横的态度  He dismissed her concerns in a high-handed manner. 他以一种高傲的态度驳回了她的担忧。

high-handed tactics:  高压手段  The government used high-handed tactics to suppress dissent. 政府使用高压手段压制异议。

act in a high-handed way:  以专横的方式行事  The company acted in a high-handed way by firing the whistleblowers.  公司以解雇举报人的方式行事专横。

high-handed decision:  专横的决定  The high-handed decision to close the factory angered the workers. 关闭工厂的专横决定激怒了工人。

high-handed approach: 高压手段  A high-handed approach will not solve this problem. 高压手段无法解决这个问题。
