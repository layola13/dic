loungiest:/ˈlaʊndʒiɪst/| adj.|最悠闲的，最懒散的|superlative form of loungy: most loungy. Characterized by relaxing or lounging; exceptionally comfortable and inviting for relaxation.|例句：This is the loungiest chair I've ever sat in. 这是我坐过的最舒服的椅子。|近义词：relaxed, comfortable, laid-back, leisurely|反义词：energetic, active, busy, industrious|词性:形容词
词源: From lounge + -y + -est.  "Lounge" originates from the Old French word "logier" meaning "to lodge."
