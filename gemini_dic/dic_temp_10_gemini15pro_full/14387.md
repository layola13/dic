autoschediastic:/ˌɔːtəʊskɪˈdæstɪk/ | adj. | 自调度 |  (Statistics) Having unequal variances; relating to or characterized by heteroscedasticity. In statistics, a sequence of random variables is autoschediastic if the variance of the  error term in a regression model is a function of the lagged values of the squared error terms.  | The researchers found evidence of autoschediastic errors in the financial time series data. 研究人员在金融时间序列数据中发现了自调度误差的证据。|  The model assumes that the errors are not autoschediastic.  该模型假设误差不具有自调度性。 |Near Synonyms: heteroscedastic | Antonyms: homoscedastic | Adjective
