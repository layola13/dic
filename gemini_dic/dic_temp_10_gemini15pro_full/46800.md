ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *Danis, *Denishe, from Old English Denisc (“Danish”), from Proto-Germanic *daniskaz (“Danish”), derived from *Danō (“Danes”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰen- (“to stretch, flow”). Cognate with German Dänisch, Dutch Deens.
