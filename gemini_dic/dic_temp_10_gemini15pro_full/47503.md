dearie:/ˈdɪəri/ /ˈdɪri/| n.|亲爱的，宝贝|A term of endearment, similar to "dear" but often more informal or childish.|例句：Sleep tight, dearie.  睡个好觉，亲爱的。

例句：Don't worry, dearie, everything will be alright.  别担心，亲爱的，一切都会好起来的。|近义词：dear, darling, sweetheart, honey|反义词: None (terms of endearment don't typically have direct opposites)|词性: Noun
