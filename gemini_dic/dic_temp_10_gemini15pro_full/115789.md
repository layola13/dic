mariposa:/ˌmɑːrɪˈpoʊsə/ /ˌmærɪˈpoʊzə/| n. |蝴蝶|A flying insect with large, often brightly colored wings.  It is the Spanish word for butterfly.|例句：I saw a beautiful mariposa fluttering in the garden. 我看到一只美丽的蝴蝶在花园里飞舞。

例句：The children chased the mariposas through the meadow. 孩子们在草地上追逐蝴蝶。|近义词：butterfly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
