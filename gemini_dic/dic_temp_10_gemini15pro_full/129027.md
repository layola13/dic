nitaac:/ˈnaɪtæk/ | n. | National Institutes of Health Information Technology Acquisition and Assessment Center (美国国立卫生研究院信息技术采购和评估中心) |  A center within the Department of Health and Human Services that provides information technology (IT) contracting vehicles for government agencies. |  Example: Agencies can utilize NITAAC's CIO-SP3 contract for IT services.  各机构可以利用NITAAC的CIO-SP3合同获得IT服务。| 近义词: (No direct synonym, but similar organizations include GSA) | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
