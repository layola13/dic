hypoderma:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmə/ (US), /ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːmə/ (UK)| n. |皮下组织；真皮|The layer of skin beneath the epidermis, containing connective tissue, fat cells, blood vessels, and nerve endings.|The injection was administered into the hypoderma.|Synonyms: subcutaneous tissue, subcutis|Antonyms: epidermis|noun
