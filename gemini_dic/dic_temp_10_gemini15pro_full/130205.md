non-segmentation:/ˌnɒnˌsɛɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非分割，非分段 |  The absence of segmentation or division into separate parts.  It refers to a state where something is whole or continuous, without being broken down into smaller units. | The non-segmentation of the market makes it difficult to target specific customer groups.  市场未分段，难以针对特定的客户群。 |  wholeness, continuity, integration | segmentation, division, fragmentation | noun
