montage:/mɒnˈtɑːʒ/ (UK), /mɑːnˈtɑːʒ/ (US)| n.|蒙太奇；剪辑；组合|A montage is a piece of work produced by combining smaller parts, often in a rapid or dynamic way, especially in film or other visual media.  A montage can also refer to any combination of disparate elements that form a unified whole.|The film used a montage of newsreel footage to show the passage of time.|近义词：collage, compilation, medley|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
