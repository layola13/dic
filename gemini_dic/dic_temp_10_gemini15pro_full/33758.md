circulars: /ˈsɜːrkjələrz/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjələz/ (UK)| n.| 通知；印刷广告；传单|A printed notice or advertisement that is distributed to a large number of people.|The company sent out circulars advertising their new product. 公司发出了宣传他们新产品的传单。|Synonyms: leaflet, pamphlet, flyer, handbill, notice, bulletin, advertisement|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct opposite concept)|Noun
