arthritically:/ɑːrˈθrɪtɪkli/
adjective
关节炎地；患关节炎地
in a manner of someone suffering from arthritis.  Affected by or relating to arthritis.
Her fingers moved arthritically as she tried to button her coat. 她试图扣上外套时，手指因关节炎而活动不便。
She walked arthritically, her knees stiff and painful. 她步履蹒跚，膝盖僵硬疼痛。
近义词: stiffly, painfully
反义词: fluidly, smoothly
词性: adjective

词源:
From arthritis + -ically.  "Arthritis" comes from Greek *árthron* (joint) and *-itis* (inflammation).
