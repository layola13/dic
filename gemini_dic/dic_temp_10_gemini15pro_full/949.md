academic: /ˌækəˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. n.| 学术的；学院的；学者的；理论的；空谈的；学究式的 | Relating to education and scholarship.|  She has a strong academic background. 她有很强的学术背景。 | scholarly, educational, theoretical | practical, applied, vocational | adjective

academic: /ˌækəˈdɛmɪk/ | n. | 学者；大学教师 | A teacher or scholar in a college or institute of higher education.| He is a distinguished academic. 他是一位杰出的学者。 | scholar, professor, lecturer | - | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *academique*, from Latin *academicus*, from Greek *akadēmikos* ("of the Academy"), from *Akadēmeia* (the name of a gymnasium near Athens where Plato taught). Ultimately from *Akadēmos*, the name of a mythical hero.
