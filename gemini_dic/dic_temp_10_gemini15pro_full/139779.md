octane:/ˈɑːkteɪn/ (US), /ˈɒkteɪn/ (UK)| n.|辛烷|A saturated hydrocarbon, C8H18, found in petroleum and used as a fuel and solvent.  It is also used as a standard in measuring the antiknock properties of gasoline. |High-octane gasoline is more resistant to knocking. 高辛烷值汽油更抗爆。|Synonyms: petrol, gasoline |Antonyms: N/A|noun
