freeman:/ˈfriːmən/| n.|自由民；拥有公民权的人；|A person who is not a slave or serf. |例句：He was born a slave but died a freeman. 他生为奴隶，死为自由人。

例句：The freemen of the city had the right to vote. 该市的自由民有投票权。|近义词：citizen, civilian|反义词：slave, serf|词性:名词

词源：Old English *frēoman "free man," from frēo "free" + mann "man."
