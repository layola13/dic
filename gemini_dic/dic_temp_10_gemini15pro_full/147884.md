overreadily:/ˌoʊvərˈredɪli/| adv.|太容易地，太快地|Too readily; too quickly or easily.  It describes an action done with excessive haste or without sufficient thought.|He overreadily agreed to her request, later regretting his impulsiveness. 他太轻易地答应了她的请求，后来后悔自己的冲动。|Synonyms: too easily, hastily, rashly, impulsively|Antonyms: cautiously, carefully, deliberately|adverb
