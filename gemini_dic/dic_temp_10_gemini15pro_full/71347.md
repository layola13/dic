flaccidity:/flækˈsɪdəti/
n.
松弛；软弱无力
The flaccidity of his muscles was a sign of the disease. 他的肌肉松弛是疾病的征兆。
The flaccidity of the economy is worrying. 经济的疲软令人担忧。
近义词：limpness, looseness, softness, weakness
反义词：firmness, tightness, hardness, strength
词性: 名词

flaccid:/ˈflæsɪd/
adj.
松弛的；软弱无力的
His handshake was flaccid. 他的握手软弱无力。
The lettuce leaves were flaccid and unappetizing.  生菜叶软塌塌的，没有食欲。
近义词：limp, loose, soft, weak
反义词：firm, tight, hard, strong
词性: 形容词


词源：From Latin *flaccidus, from flaccus (“flabby, flaccid”).
