demiwolf:/ˈdɛmiˌwʊlf/| n.|半狼人；半狼|A being that is half human and half wolf, often found in fantasy literature and games.|The demiwolf shifted under the moonlight, its human eyes gleaming with an unnatural light.  半狼人在月光下变换着身形，它的人类眼睛闪烁着诡异的光芒。|Example: In the game, the player could choose to be a demiwolf, gaining enhanced strength and speed. 在游戏中，玩家可以选择成为半狼人，获得更强的力量和速度。|Synonym: werewolf, lycanthrope|Antonym: human|Noun
