greasiest:/ˈɡriːsiɪst/ /ˈɡriːziɪst/| adj.|最油腻的|Covered with or full of grease; very oily.|The greasiest burger I've ever eaten. 我吃过的最油腻的汉堡。|Synonyms: oily, fatty, greasy, unctuous|Antonyms: clean, dry, nonfat|adjective

grease:/ɡriːs/ /ɡriːs/| n. v.|油脂,润滑|A thick oily substance, or to lubricate with grease.|The mechanic greased the gears. 机修工给齿轮上了润滑油。|Synonyms: oil, fat, lubricant|Antonyms: cleaner, degreaser|noun, verb
