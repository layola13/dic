changemakers:/ˈtʃeɪndʒˌmeɪkərz/| n. |变革者，变革推动者|People who are actively working to bring about significant positive change in society.  Those who initiate and implement solutions to social problems.|例句：The conference brought together changemakers from around the world.  这次会议汇集了来自世界各地的变革者。

例句：These young changemakers are developing innovative solutions to address climate change.  这些年轻的变革推动者正在开发创新的解决方案来应对气候变化。|近义词：reformers, innovators, pioneers, activists, leaders|反义词:conservatives, traditionalists|词性:noun
