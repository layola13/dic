cartography:/kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfi/ (US) /kɑːrˈtɑːɡrəfi/ (UK)| n.|制图学；地图绘制|The science or art of making maps.  The study and practice of making maps.|例句：Modern cartography relies heavily on computer technology. 现代制图学非常依赖计算机技术。|近义词：mapmaking, charting|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  From French *cartographie*, from Greek *khartēs* (paper, papyrus, map) + *-graphia* (writing, description).
