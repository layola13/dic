organoids:/ˈɔːrɡənɔɪdz/| n. |类器官|A miniaturized and simplified version of an organ produced in vitro in three dimensions that shows realistic micro-anatomy. |Scientists are growing brain organoids to study neurological diseases.|Synonyms: mini-organs, 3D tissue cultures|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "in vivo models")|Noun
