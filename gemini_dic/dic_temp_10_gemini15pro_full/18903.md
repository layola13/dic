besmirches:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃɪz/
verb
玷污，败坏，损害(名誉)
To damage the reputation of (someone or something) in the opinion of others by saying or doing something that is unpleasant or bad.
The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire company.  这桩丑闻玷污了整个公司的名声。
His accusations besmirched her good name. 他的指控损害了她的好名声。
synonyms: tarnish, sully, smear, stain, discredit, disgrace
antonyms: honor, praise, extol, laud, elevate
词性: 动词
