nonarrogancies:/nɒnˈærəɡənsiz/
noun
非傲慢；谦逊
Lack of arrogance; qualities of humility and modesty.
His nonarrogance and willingness to learn impressed everyone. 他的谦逊和好学的态度给大家留下了深刻的印象。
Near Synonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness, unpretentiousness
Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, pride, conceit
