bifarious:/baɪˈfɛəriəs/
adjective
两方面的，双重的
Of two kinds; twofold; double.
The bifarious nature of the problem required a multifaceted solution.  这个问题的两面性需要一个多方面的解决方案。
The artist explored the bifarious nature of good and evil in his paintings. 这位艺术家在他的绘画中探索了善与恶的两面性。
近义词: twofold, double, dual
反义词: single, singular
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin bifarius ("two-rowed, in two ranks"), from bi- ("two") + farius ("of a row, rank").
