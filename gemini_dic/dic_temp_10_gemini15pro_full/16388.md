barbiton:/ˈbɑːrbɪtɒn/ | n. | 巴比顿琴(一种古代里拉琴) | An ancient stringed musical instrument, a type of lyre, associated with Sappho and other lyric poets of ancient Greece. It had a longer soundbox than the typical lyre and possibly more strings. |  Sappho's poetry often mentions the barbiton. 萨福的诗歌里经常提到巴比顿琴。|  The musician skillfully played a mournful melody on his barbiton.  这位音乐家熟练地在巴比顿琴上演奏了一曲悲伤的旋律。| Synonyms: lyre, cithara | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern instrument") | Noun
