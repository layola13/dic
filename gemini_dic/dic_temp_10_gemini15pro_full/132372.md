nondeliriousness: /ˌnɒndɪˈlɪəriəsnəs/ | n. |清醒；神志清楚|The state of being not delirious; clarity of mind.|After the fever broke, the patient experienced a period of nondeliriousness. 病人退烧后，经历了一段清醒期。|The medication helped restore her to a state of nondeliriousness. 药物帮助她恢复了清醒。| 近义词: lucidity, alertness, coherence, sanity |反义词: delirium, confusion, incoherence, insanity |词性: noun

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + delirious (adjective meaning "in a state of delirium") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
