incitative:/ɪnˈsaɪtətɪv/| adj.|煽动性的，刺激的|Tending to incite; stimulating.  Something that encourages or stirs someone to action. |The incitative speech roused the crowd to protest.  煽动性的演讲激起了群众的抗议。|近义词：provocative, stimulating, inspiring, instigating|反义词：soothing, calming, deterrent, dissuasive|词性:形容词
词源：From French incitatif, from inciter “to incite,” from Latin incitāre "to urge on, rouse, excite," frequentative of inciēre "to set in motion," from in- "in, on" + ciēre "to set in motion."
