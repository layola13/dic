jeepers:/ˈdʒiːpərz/
interjection
哎呀；我的天哪
An exclamation of surprise, astonishment, or alarm.
Jeepers, that was close! 哎呀，那真是千钧一发！
Jeepers creepers, where'd you get those peepers? 我的天哪，你从哪里弄来的这些眼睛？
近义词: gosh, gee, crikey, heavens
反义词:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation)
词性: interjection
