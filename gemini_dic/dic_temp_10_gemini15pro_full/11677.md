armorial:/ɑːrˈmɔːriəl/
adjective
纹章的; 徽章的
Of or relating to coats of arms.
The duke’s armorial bearings were prominently displayed. 公爵的纹章显著地展示出来。
The family's armorial crest featured a griffin.  这个家族的徽章上有一只狮鹫。
近义词:heraldic
反义词:None
词性:adjective
