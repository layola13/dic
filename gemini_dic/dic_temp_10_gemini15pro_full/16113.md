noun
有害；恶毒；毁灭性
the quality or state of being baneful; destructiveness.
The banefulnesses of the prolonged drought devastated the region's agriculture. 长期干旱的危害摧毁了该地区的农业。
Near synonyms: perniciousness, noxiousness, destructiveness, harmfulness
Near antonyms: beneficence, advantageousness, helpfulness


baneful:/ˈbeɪnfʊl/
adjective
有害的；恶毒的；毁灭性的
causing ruin; harmful or destructive.
The baneful effects of the pollution were evident in the declining fish population. 污染的有害影响在鱼类数量下降中显而易见。
