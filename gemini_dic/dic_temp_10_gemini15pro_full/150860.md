paragrapher:/ˈpærəˌɡræfər/| n.|分段写作者，撰写短文者|A paragrapher is someone who writes short paragraphs, often for newspapers or magazines.  |The newspaper employed a skilled paragrapher to write short, impactful pieces. 这家报纸雇佣了一位熟练的短文作者来撰写简短而有影响力的文章。|
|近义词：writer, columnist, journalist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-writer)|n.
