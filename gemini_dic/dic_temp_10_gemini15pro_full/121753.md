mispunctuate: /ˌmɪsˈpʌŋktʃueɪt/
verb
错误地使用标点符号
to use punctuation marks incorrectly.
He mispunctuated the sentence, making it difficult to understand. 他错误地使用了标点符号，导致句子难以理解。
The student mispunctuated their essay, losing marks for grammar.  这个学生在论文中标点符号使用错误，语法方面丢了分。
misuse, punctuate incorrectly
punctuate correctly
v.
