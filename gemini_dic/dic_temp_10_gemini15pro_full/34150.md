clairvoyant:/ˌklɛərˈvɔɪənt/ | adj. n. | 透视的，有洞察力的；透视者，千里眼 | Able to see beyond the range of ordinary perception.  |  She is said to be clairvoyant and able to predict the future. 据说她有洞察力，能够预测未来。 | synonyms: psychic, prophetic, visionary | antonyms: oblivious, unperceptive | adjective

clairvoyant:/ˌklɛərˈvɔɪənt/ | n. | 透视者，千里眼 | A person who claims to have a supernatural ability to perceive events in the future or beyond normal sensory contact. | The clairvoyant accurately described the missing necklace.  这位透视者准确地描述了丢失的项链。| synonyms: psychic, seer, fortune teller | antonyms: skeptic | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French clairvoyant, from clair (“clear”) + voyant (“seeing”), present participle of voir (“to see”). Ultimately from Latin clarus (“clear”) and vidēre (“to see”).
