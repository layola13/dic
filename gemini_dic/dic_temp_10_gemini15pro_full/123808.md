mopped:/mɒpt/ (UK), /mɑːpt/ (US)| v. |擦，拖，抹，清除|cleaned or wiped something up with a mop or cloth; also used figuratively to mean to wipe out or defeat completely|例句：I mopped the floor after I spilled the water. 我把水洒了之后拖了地。

例句：He mopped his brow with a handkerchief. 他用手帕擦了擦额头。|近义词：cleaned, wiped, swabbed, scrubbed|反义词: soiled, dirtied|词性:verb

mopped:/mɒpt/ (UK), /mɑːpt/ (US)| adj. |垂头丧气的，沮丧的|(informal) dejected, dispirited|例句：She looked mopped after failing the exam. 她考试不及格后看起来很沮丧。

例句：He felt utterly mopped after the team lost the championship. 输掉冠军后，他感到十分沮丧。 |近义词：dejected, downcast, disheartened|反义词: cheerful, elated|词性:adjective
