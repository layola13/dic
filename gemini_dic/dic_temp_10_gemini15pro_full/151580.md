parterre: /pɑːrˈtɛər/ /pɑːrˈtɛr/ | n. |花坛; parterres (复数)|A level space in a garden or park, typically one that is ornamented with flower beds.  A section of the ground floor of a theater, especially the part beneath the balconies.|The formal parterre was filled with colorful blooms.  正式的花坛里开满了五颜六色的花朵。|Synonyms: flower bed, border, plot|Antonyms: wilderness, wasteland|noun

parterre: /pɑːrˈtɛər/ /pɑːrˈtɛr/ | n. |(剧院)正厅后座区|The part of the ground floor of a theater beneath the balconies.|We had seats in the parterre for the opera. 我们在歌剧院的正厅后座区有座位。|Synonyms: orchestra stalls, pit|Antonyms: balcony, gallery|noun
