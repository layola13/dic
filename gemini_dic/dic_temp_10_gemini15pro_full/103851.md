一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? = 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they watching on TV? = 他们在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this award? = 他做了什么值得这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading all afternoon? = 她整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't take action? = 如果我们不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get there? = 等我们到那里的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
