full-bodied:/ˌfʊlˈbɒdid/ (UK) /ˌfʊlˈbɑːdid/ (US)| adj.|浓郁的，醇厚的，丰满的|Having a strong, rich flavor and a thick texture, often used to describe wines, coffee, or other beverages. Also, can describe a sound or voice that is resonant and powerful.|This wine is wonderfully full-bodied and complex. 这款酒口感浓郁，层次丰富。|Synonyms: rich, robust, strong, powerful, intense|Antonyms: thin, weak, light, watery|adjective

full-bodied:/ˌfʊlˈbɒdid/ (UK) /ˌfʊlˈbɑːdid/ (US)| adj.|（声音）洪亮的，浑厚的|Describing a sound, especially a voice, that is resonant and deep.|She has a surprisingly full-bodied voice for someone so petite. 对于如此娇小的人来说，她的声音出奇地洪亮。|Synonyms: resonant, deep, rich, powerful|Antonyms: thin, reedy, weak|adjective
