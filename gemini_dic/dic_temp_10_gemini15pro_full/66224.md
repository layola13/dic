exciter:/ɪkˈsaɪtər/| n.| 激励者；刺激物；兴奋剂；激励器|Something that excites or stimulates.|The band hired a professional exciter to warm up the crowd before the concert. 乐队在演唱会开始前聘请了一位专业的暖场主持人来调动观众的情绪。|近义词：stimulant, catalyst, motivator|反义词：depressant, inhibitor|noun

exciter:/ɪkˈsaɪtər/| n.| 激励者；刺激物；兴奋剂；激励器|A device or substance used to produce excitation, especially in electronics or physics.|The exciter in the radio transmitter amplifies the signal. 无线电发射机中的激励器放大信号。|近义词：amplifier, booster|反义词：suppressor|noun
