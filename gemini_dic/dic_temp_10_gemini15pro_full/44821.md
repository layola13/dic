crushed:/krʌʃt/
adjective
压碎的，被压坏的
broken into small pieces or powder by being pressed hard.
The car was crushed in the accident. 这辆车在事故中被压坏了。
My dreams were crushed when I failed the exam. 当我考试不及格时，我的梦想破灭了。
近义词: smashed, pulverized, mangled
反义词: intact, whole
词性: adjective

crushed:/krʌʃt/
adjective
(informal)  伤心欲绝的，极度失望的
extremely upset and disappointed.
She was crushed when her boyfriend broke up with her. 当她的男朋友和她分手时，她伤心欲绝。
He was crushed by the news of his father's death. 他被他父亲去世的消息压垮了。
近义词: devastated, heartbroken, despondent
反义词: elated, overjoyed
词性: adjective

crushed:/krʌʃt/
verb
压碎，压坏
to press something very hard so that it breaks into small pieces or powder.
The machine crushed the rocks into gravel. 机器把岩石压碎成砾石。
He crushed the can in his hand. 他用手把罐头捏扁了。
近义词: smash, pulverize, grind
反义词: repair, mend
词性: verb

词源: From Middle English *crushen, *crusshen, from Old Norse *krussa (“to bruise, crush”), from Proto-Germanic *krusjaną (“to crush, crackle”), from Proto-Indo-European *greus- (“to gnaw, crush, grind”). Cognate with Danish krusse (“to crisp, curl, crimp”).
