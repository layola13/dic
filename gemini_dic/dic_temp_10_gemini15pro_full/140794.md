on-air:/ˌɒn ˈeər/ (UK) /ˌɑːn ˈer/ (US)| adj. adv.|正在播出的，现场直播的|Being broadcast on radio or television; actively broadcasting.|The red light is on, indicating that the program is on-air. 红灯亮着，表示节目正在播出。|Synonyms: broadcasting, live, on the air|Antonyms: off-air, pre-recorded|adjective, adverb

on-air:/ˌɒn ˈeər/ (UK) /ˌɑːn ˈer/ (US)| n.|广播时间|A period of time when a program is being broadcast.|The president's on-air was scheduled for 8 pm. 总统的广播时间安排在晚上8点。|Synonyms: broadcast, airtime|Antonyms: downtime|noun
