anesthetises:/ˌænɪsˈθɛtaɪzɪz/ | v. |麻醉；使麻木| To administer an anesthetic to (a person or animal), especially so as to induce a loss of consciousness. | The doctor anesthetises the patient before the surgery. 医生在手术前给病人麻醉。| The dentist anesthetised my gums before pulling the tooth. 牙医在拔牙之前麻醉了我的牙龈。 | synonyms: numb, desensitize | antonyms: stimulate, sensitize | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "anaisthesia,"  meaning "lack of sensation," from "an-" (without) + "aisthesis" (sensation).
