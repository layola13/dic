hardiness:/ˈhɑːrdinəs/| n.|强壮；耐劳性；大胆；鲁莽|The hardiness of the plant surprised the botanist. 这种植物的强壮令植物学家感到惊讶。|近义词：robustness, resilience, toughness, fortitude, audacity|反义词: weakness, fragility, vulnerability|词性:名词
Etymology: From hardy + -ness.  "Hardy" comes from the Old North French "hardi," meaning bold, brave, or daring, ultimately from the Germanic root "harduz" meaning hard.
