degelled:/diːˈdʒɛld/
adjective
去胶的，去壳的
removed the gelatinous coating from; specifically : having the silk glands removed (as by immersion in hot water)
例句：Degelled silkworm pupae are a good source of protein. 去胶蚕蛹是良好的蛋白质来源。

例句：The degelled seeds are then ready for planting. 去壳后的种子就可以播种了。
近义词：husked, shelled, decorticated
反义词：coated, covered
词性：adjective
