bundy:/ˈbʌndi/| n.|邦迪（澳大利亚俚语，指夹克）|A short jacket made of thick woollen material, typically with a belt and buttons.  It's a common item of clothing in Australia, especially in rural areas.|He wore his trusty bundy to keep warm in the chilly evening air. 他穿着他那件可靠的邦迪夹克在寒冷的夜风中保暖。|近义词：jacket, coat|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps shorts/t-shirt for contrasting clothing)|词性:noun
