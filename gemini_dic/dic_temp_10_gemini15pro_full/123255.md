monodelphia:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfiə/ | n. |单孔目动物|A subclass of mammals characterized by having a single urogenital opening, as in monotremes.|Monodelphia are a fascinating group of mammals. 单孔目动物是一组迷人的哺乳动物。| The platypus is a member of Monodelphia. 鸭嘴兽是单孔目动物的成员。|近义词:Prototheria (although not entirely synonymous as it includes Metatheria)|反义词: (No direct opposite, but contrasting groups include) Metatheria (marsupials) and Eutheria (placental mammals)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *monos* "single" + *delphys* "womb"
