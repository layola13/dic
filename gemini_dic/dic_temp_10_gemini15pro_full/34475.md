claws:/klɔːz/| n.|爪子，钳子，螯|A sharp, curved nail on the foot of an animal, especially a mammal or bird.  Also, a similar part in some invertebrates. |The cat sharpened its claws on the tree. 猫在树上磨爪子。|Synonyms: talons, nails, pincers|Antonyms: paws, pads|noun
