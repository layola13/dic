fundholder:/ˈfʌndˌhoʊldər/ (US) /ˈfʌndˌhəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| n. |基金持有人；资金持有人|A person or entity that owns shares or units in a mutual fund or other investment fund.|Example 1:  The fundholders voted to merge with another fund. 基金持有人投票决定与另一个基金合并。

Example 2: The fundholder received a quarterly statement showing the performance of their investment. 基金持有人收到了一份季度报表，显示了他们投资的业绩。|Synonym: investor, shareholder, unit holder|Antonym:  fund manager|noun
