madam:/ˈmædəm/ (US), /ˈmædəm/ (UK)| n.|女士，夫人，太太|A polite term of address to a woman, originally used for a woman of high social standing, now used more generally in formal situations.|例句：May I help you, madam? 女士，我可以帮您吗？

例句：Madam, your table is ready. 夫人，您的桌子准备好了。

近义词：ma'am, Mrs., lady, miss|反义词:sir, mister|词性:名词
