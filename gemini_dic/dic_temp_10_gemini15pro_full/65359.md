eulogized:/ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzd/
verb
赞扬；颂扬；称赞
Praised highly in speech or writing, especially in a formal way, typically after someone has died.
The fallen soldiers were eulogized for their bravery and sacrifice.  阵亡的士兵因其英勇和牺牲而受到赞扬。

He was eulogized as a visionary leader. 他被誉为富有远见的领导者。

近义词:praised, lauded, extolled, commended, celebrated
反义词:criticized, condemned, denigrated, vilified
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek eu- "well" + logos "word, speech, discourse" + -ize "to cause to be"
