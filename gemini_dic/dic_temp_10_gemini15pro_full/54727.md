disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ /dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ | vt. | 贬低，轻视，诋毁 | To speak of or treat slightingly; belittle.  |  He disparaged his competitor's products. 他贬低了他的竞争对手的产品。

He never missed an opportunity to disparage her. 他从不放过任何一个诋毁她的机会。

近义词: belittle, denigrate, deprecate, detract, minimize, underestimate, underrate | 反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol, compliment | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desparagier "to marry below one's class," from des- "expressing removal" + parage "lineage, rank," from Latin par "equal." The meaning shifted from "marry unequally" to "degrade" in the 16th century.
