obtrusionist: /əbˈtruːʒənɪst/
n.
闯入者，打扰者，不速之客
Someone who obtrudes, or forces their presence or opinions on others.
例句：The obtrusionist interrupted the meeting with his irrelevant comments.  这个不速之客用他不相关的评论打断了会议。

例句：He acted like an obtrusionist, barging into conversations he wasn't invited to. 他像个闯入者一样，闯入他没有被邀请的谈话中。

近义词：intruder, interloper, meddler, busybody
反义词：recluse, hermit

词性：noun

词源：From the verb "obtrude" + "-ist" (suffix forming agent nouns). "Obtrude" comes from Latin "obtrudere," from "ob-" (against, toward) + "trudere" (to thrust, push).
