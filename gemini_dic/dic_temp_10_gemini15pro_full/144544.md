outvoted:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊtɪd/
verb
被投票否决；投票落败
defeated in a vote.
例句：The chairman was outvoted by the board. 董事长被董事会投票否决了。

例句：The proposal was outvoted by a narrow margin.  这项提议以微弱劣势被否决了。

近义词：defeated, overruled, overborne
反义词：approved, carried, passed
词性：Verb (Past Participle)


outvote:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊt/
verb
以票数胜过；投票击败
defeat (someone) by gaining more votes.
例句：They outvoted the opposition by a large majority. 他们以压倒性多数票击败了反对派。

例句：The government was outvoted on the new tax bill. 政府在新税法案上投票失败了。

近义词：defeat, overrule, conquer
反义词：lose, be defeated
词性：Verb
