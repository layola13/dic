agriologist:/ˌæɡriˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.
农学家;农业学家
A specialist in agriology; an agriculturalist.
The agriologist studied the effects of the drought on the corn crop. 这位农学家研究了干旱对玉米作物的影响。

近义词:agriculturalist, farmer, agronomist
反义词:None

词性:名词
