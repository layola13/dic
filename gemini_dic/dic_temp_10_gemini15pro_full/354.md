abided-by:/əˈbaɪdɪd baɪ/ | v. | 遵守，遵循 | To adhere to, comply with, or follow (rules, laws, agreements, etc.). | The company abided by the terms of the contract.  公司遵守了合同条款。

They abided by the judge's ruling. 他们遵守了法官的裁决。 | 近义词: comply with, adhere to, follow, observe, respect | 反义词: disobey, violate, breach, ignore, defy | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abiden bi*, from Old English *ābīdan bī* (“to wait by, remain by”), from *ābīdan* (“abide”) + *bī* (“by”). The meaning shifted from "waiting by something" to "staying with something" and eventually to "following or obeying something."


USAGE->
