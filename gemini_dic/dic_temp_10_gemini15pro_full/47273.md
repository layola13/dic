de-automated:/diːˈɔːtəmeɪtɪd/
|adj.|去自动化的，非自动化的|Removed from a state of automation; having automation reversed or removed.  The process was de-automated to allow for more human control.|The factory de-automated some production lines to improve product quality. 工厂去自动化了一些生产线以提高产品质量。|The company de-automated its customer service to provide a more personal touch.  公司去自动化了客户服务，以提供更个性化的服务。|manual, human-operated|automated, automatic|adjective
