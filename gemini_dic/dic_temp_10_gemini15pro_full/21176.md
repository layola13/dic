blobby:/ˈblɒbi/ (UK) /ˈblɑːbi/ (US)| adj.|一团团的，粘糊糊的，不成形的|Having a rounded, indistinct shape; amorphous and somewhat sticky or viscous.|The monster in the movie was a large, blobby creature.  电影里的怪物是一个巨大的、粘糊糊的生物。|The pudding had a rather blobby consistency.  布丁的稠度相当粘稠。|近义词：amorphous, shapeless, formless, globular, lumpy|反义词：sharp, defined, distinct|词性:形容词
词源:  From blob + -y.  源自 blob（一团，一滴）+ -y（形容词后缀）。
