hemihedron:/ˌhɛmiˈhiːdrən/| n. |半面体；半面晶体|A crystal form which has only half the number of faces that the full or normal form would have.  It is a crystal with half the usual number of planes.|Crystals of pyrite often occur as hemihedra.|Synonym: half-crystal|Antonym: holohedron|noun
