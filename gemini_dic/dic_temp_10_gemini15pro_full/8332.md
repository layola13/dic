anti-philosophies:/ˌænti fɪˈlɒsəfiz/ | n. | 反哲学，非哲学 | Systems of belief or thought that oppose or reject the fundamental principles of traditional philosophy, often by emphasizing practicality, skepticism, or emotional experience over abstract reasoning. |  Many modern art movements embrace anti-philosophies, prioritizing feeling and expression over intellectual analysis. 许多现代艺术运动都拥护反哲学，将感觉和表达置于智力分析之上。

Anti-philosophies sometimes manifest as a rejection of intellectualism altogether. 反哲学有时表现为对知识分子主义的全面排斥。 | Near synonyms: counter-philosophies, non-philosophies, unphilosophical doctrines | Antonyms: philosophies, philosophical systems | noun
