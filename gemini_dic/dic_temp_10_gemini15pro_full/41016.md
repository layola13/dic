copartners:/koʊˈpɑːrtnərz/
n.
合伙人，共同合伙人
Joint partners in a business venture.
The copartners shared the profits equally.  合伙人平分了利润。
The copartners decided to dissolve their business partnership. 合伙人决定解散他们的商业合作关系。
synonyms: partners, associates, collaborators
antonyms: competitors, rivals
noun
