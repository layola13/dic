hippasus:/hɪˈpeɪsəs/| n. |希帕索斯|A Greek Pythagorean philosopher who discovered irrational numbers, leading to a crisis in Pythagorean mathematics. He is sometimes said to have been drowned at sea, possibly as punishment for his discovery. |Hippasus is credited with the discovery of irrational numbers. 希帕索斯被认为发现了无理数。|近义词:mathematician, philosopher|反义词:none|名词
