dunderpate:/ˈdʌndərpeɪt/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A foolish or stupid person.|He's such a dunderpate, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙锁在车里了。|Synonyms: simpleton, numskull, blockhead|Antonyms: genius, intellectual|Noun

Etymology->
From 17th century, possibly from *dunder* "thunder" + pate "head", meaning a head struck by thunder.  This suggests someone dazed or stupid.
