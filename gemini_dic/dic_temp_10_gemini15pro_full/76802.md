gammon:/ˈɡæmən/| n. v.|熏猪后腿；胡说；骗局；彻底击败 |A cured ham, especially from the hindquarters of a pig; nonsense; a hoax; to defeat soundly. |例句：We had gammon for Christmas dinner. 我们圣诞晚餐吃了熏猪后腿。
