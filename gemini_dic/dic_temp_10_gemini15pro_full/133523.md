nonfiction:/ˌnɒnˈfɪkʃən/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪkʃn/| n. |非小说，纪实文学| Prose writing that is based on facts, real events, and real people, such as biography or history. |  Example: I prefer reading nonfiction to fiction. 我更喜欢读非小说类作品而不是小说。| Example:  She's a well-known author of historical nonfiction. 她是一位著名的历史纪实作家。 | 近义词: factual writing, documentary | 反义词: fiction | 词性:noun
