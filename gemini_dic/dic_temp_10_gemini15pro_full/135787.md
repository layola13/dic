nonprohibition:/ˌnɒnˌprəʊɪˈbɪʃən/
noun
非禁止；不禁
The absence of prohibition; permission or allowance.
The nonprohibition of alcohol sales on Sundays has increased business for local bars.  周日不禁止销售酒类增加了当地酒吧的生意。
Synonyms: allowance, permission, authorization, sanction
Antonyms: prohibition, ban, forbiddance, interdiction
词性: 名词
