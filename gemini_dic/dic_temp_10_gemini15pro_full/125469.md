multiplier:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪər/| n.|乘数；倍增器；乘法器|A quantity by which a given number (the multiplicand) is multiplied.|例句：The multiplier effect of government spending can boost the economy. 政府支出的乘数效应可以刺激经济。

例句：A multiplier of 2.5 was used to calculate the final score.  使用2.5的乘数来计算最终得分。|近义词：factor, coefficient|反义词:divisor, divider|词性:noun
