feltry:/ˈfɛltri/| adj.|毡状的；像毡的；覆盖着毡的|Made of, resembling, or covered with felt. |The artist used feltry material to create the sculpture.  这位艺术家用毡状材料创作了雕塑。|The room was decorated with feltry wall hangings.  房间里装饰着毛毡壁挂。|近义词：woolen, fuzzy, nappy|反义词:smooth, sleek|词性:adjective
Etymology->
From felt + -ry.  Felt itself comes from Old English *felt, from Proto-Germanic *feltą, related to Latin pilus "hair." The suffix -ry denotes a collection or place of something, or a quality or characteristic.
