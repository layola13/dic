orthophotomap:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈfoʊtoʊˌmæp/ | n. |正射影像图|An aerial photograph or image geometrically corrected such that the scale is uniform and the photo has the same metric properties as a map.  It combines the image characteristics of a photograph with the geometric qualities of a map.|例句：The orthophotomap clearly showed the extent of the flooding. 正射影像图清晰地显示了洪水的范围。

例句：We used orthophotomaps to plan the new hiking trail. 我们使用正射影像图来规划新的徒步路线。|近义词：orthoimage, rectified photograph|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps uncorrected aerial photograph) |词性:noun
