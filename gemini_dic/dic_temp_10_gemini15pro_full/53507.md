disassimilate:/dɪsəˈsɪməˌleɪt/
verb
同化
to cause (something) to become less similar
Example:  The immigrants disassimilated from their native cultures over time.  随着时间的推移，移民们逐渐脱离了他们的原生文化。

Example:  The company tried to disassimilate its product from the competition.  该公司试图使其产品与竞争对手的产品区分开来。
近义词:differentiate, diverge, separate
反义词:assimilate, integrate, homogenize
词性:v.


disassimilation:/dɪˌsɪməˈleɪʃən/
noun
异化;异化作用;异化同化作用;异化同化
the process by which a person or group becomes less similar to another person or group
Example:  Disassimilation of cultural values can lead to social conflict. 文化价值观的异化会导致社会冲突。

Example:  The disassimilation of the dialects resulted in the development of distinct languages. 方言的异化导致了不同语言的发展。
近义词:differentiation, divergence, separation
反义词:assimilation, integration, homogenization
词性:n.
