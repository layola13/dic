expendability:/ɪkˌspɛndəˈbɪləti/
n.|可牺牲性，可消耗性|The expendability of low-skilled workers in that industry is a serious social problem.  那个行业低技能工人的可牺牲性是一个严重的社会问题。|Expendability refers to the degree to which something or someone can be sacrificed or used up without significant consequence. 可牺牲性指的是某物或某人在没有重大后果的情况下被牺牲或用完的程度。|The general saw the soldiers as mere expendable resources. 将军把士兵看作仅仅是可牺牲的资源。|synonyms: disposability, replaceability, sacrificeability|antonyms: indispensability, irreplaceability|noun
