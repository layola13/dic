disguisedly:/dɪsˈɡaɪzɪdli/ | adv. | 伪装地，假装地 | in a disguised manner; so as to be unrecognizable | He coughed disguisedly to cover his laughter. 他假装咳嗽来掩饰笑声。| Synonym: covertly, secretly, furtively, stealthily | Antonym: openly, plainly, obviously | 词性: 副词

disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/ | n. vt. | 伪装，掩饰；假扮 | something that is worn to hide one's true appearance or identity; to give a different appearance to, especially in order to hide its true form | He put on a disguise and slipped out of the house. 他乔装打扮后溜出了房子。| Synonym: camouflage, mask, cloak | Antonym: reveal, expose | 词性: 名词，动词
