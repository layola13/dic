circularise:/ˈsɜːrkjələraɪz/ (UK) /ˈsɜːrkjələˌraɪz/ (US)| vt.|征求…的意见|To ask a number of people for their opinions or information.|The committee circularised its members for their views on the proposal. 委员会向其成员征求对该提案的意见。|poll, canvass, survey| - |verb|
