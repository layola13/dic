PHRASE->
epistemic access (认识论途径):  How we gain knowledge.
epistemic beliefs (知识信念): Beliefs about knowledge and justification.
epistemic certainty (认识论确定性):  The idea of absolute, undeniable knowledge.
epistemic closure (认识论封闭): A state where a belief system rejects evidence that contradicts its core beliefs.
epistemic humility (认识论谦逊):  Acknowledging the limits of one's own knowledge.
