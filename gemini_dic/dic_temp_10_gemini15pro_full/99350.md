interdenominationalisms:/ˌɪntərdiːˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.|跨教派的主义；不同教派间的联合主义|The rise of interdenominationalisms reflects a growing desire for unity among Christians. 跨教派主义的兴起反映了基督徒之间日益增长的团结愿望。|例句：The conference promoted interdenominationalisms by bringing together leaders from various Christian traditions.  这次会议通过汇聚来自不同基督教传统的领袖来促进跨教派主义。|近义词：ecumenism, interfaith cooperation|反义词: sectarianism, denominationalism|词性:名词

词源:
inter- (between) + denominational (relating to a religious denomination) + -ism (a doctrine, belief, or practice)
