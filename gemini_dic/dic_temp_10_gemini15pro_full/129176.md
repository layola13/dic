nob:/nɒb/ | n. | 富人，要人，有钱有势的人；球形把手，旋钮 | A person of wealth or high social position; a rounded handle, especially one for controlling a device. | He acts like a nob, always ordering people around.  他表现得像个大人物，总是对别人颐指气使。 The nob on the radio is broken. 收音机上的旋钮坏了。 | 近义词：aristocrat, toff, bigwig, handle, knob | 反义词：commoner, peasant | 词性: 名词

词源:
Possibly from a shortened form of *nobility*.  可能源自 *nobility* 的缩写形式.
