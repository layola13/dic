alphabetism: /ˈæl.fə.bə.ˌtɪ.zəm/ | n. | 字母主义；字母崇拜 |  The belief in the inherent power or magical significance of letters of the alphabet.  The practice of using letters of the alphabet for divination or in spells and charms. | He practiced alphabetism, believing certain letter combinations held mystical powers.  |  letter worship, graphemysticism |  - | noun
Etymology: alphabetic + -ism.
