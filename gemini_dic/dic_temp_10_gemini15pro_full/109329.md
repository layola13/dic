lengthiness:/ˈlɛŋθinəs/| n.|冗长，啰嗦|The lengthiness of the speech bored the audience. 演讲的冗长让听众感到厌烦。|近义词：prolixity, verbosity, tediousness|反义词: brevity, conciseness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From lengthy + -ness. Lengthy from length + -y. Length from Old English *lenþu (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *langþō, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)leng-.
