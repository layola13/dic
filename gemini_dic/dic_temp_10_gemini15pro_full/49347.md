delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/| n.|描绘者，描述者；限定物，界定物|something that serves to define or mark off a boundary or limit.  It can refer to a person who describes or outlines something clearly, or to a physical object that marks a boundary. |The traffic cones served as delineators, separating the bike lane from the car lane. 交通锥筒作为分界线，将自行车道和车道隔开。|Synonyms: marker, indicator, definer, describer|Antonyms: obscurer, confuser|noun

delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/| n.|分隔线，分界线|a line or other marking that separates one area or thing from another.|The white lines acted as delineators between the parking spaces. 白线充当了停车位之间的分隔线。|Synonyms: divider, separator, boundary|Antonyms: connector, unifier|noun
