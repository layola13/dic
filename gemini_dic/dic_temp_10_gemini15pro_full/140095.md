off-ground:/ˌɒfˈɡraʊnd/ | adj. | 离地的，不接地的 | Not touching the ground; raised or elevated. | The off-ground pool is easier to maintain than an in-ground one.  离地泳池比地下泳池更容易维护。| elevated, raised, airborne | grounded, on-ground | adjective


off-ground:/ˌɒfˈɡraʊnd/ | adj. | 非现实的，不切实际的 | Not practical or realistic; impractical, unrealistic. | His off-ground ideas for the project were quickly dismissed. 他对这个项目不切实际的想法很快就被否决了。| impractical, unrealistic, fanciful | practical, realistic, grounded | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"off" (adv., prep.): From Old English "of," meaning "away, from."
"ground" (n.): From Old English "grund," meaning "ground, bottom, foundation."
