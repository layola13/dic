carking:/ˈkɑːrkɪŋ/
adjective
令人焦虑的; 烦人的
causing worry or annoyance.
例句：The carking responsibilities of her job kept her awake at night. 她工作中令人焦虑的责任让她夜不能寐。

例句：He found the constant noise of the city carking. 他觉得城市的持续噪音令人烦躁。

近义词：worrying, annoying, troublesome, burdensome
反义词：soothing, calming, relaxing, comforting
词性：adjective

cark:/kɑːrk/
verb
(为某事) 担忧; 烦恼
to be worried or annoyed (about something)
例句：She carked about her son's future. 她为儿子的未来担忧。

例句：He carked over every little detail. 他为每个小细节而烦恼。

近义词：worry, fret, agonize
反义词：relax, unwind
词性：verb

cark:/kɑːrk/
noun
担忧; 烦恼
worry; annoyance
例句：The cark of finding a new job was overwhelming. 找新工作的烦恼让她难以承受。

例句：He suffered from constant cark about his health. 他一直为自己的健康担忧。

近义词：worry, anxiety, concern
反义词：peace, tranquility
词性：noun
