over-talkativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌtɔːkətɪvnəs/
|n.|健谈，喋喋不休|The state or quality of talking too much.|Her over-talkativeness made it difficult to get a word in edgewise. 她太健谈了，让我很难插上一句话。|Synonyms: loquacity, garrulity, volubility|Antonyms: reticence, taciturnity|noun


over-talkative:/ˌoʊvərˈtɔːkətɪv/
|adj.|健谈的，喋喋不休的|Inclined to talk too much.|The over-talkative child was constantly interrupting the teacher.  这个话多的孩子不断打断老师讲话。|Synonyms: loquacious, garrulous, voluble|Antonyms: reticent, taciturn|adjective
