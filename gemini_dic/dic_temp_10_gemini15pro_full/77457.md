gayal:/ˈɡaɪəl/ | n. |  野牛，印度野牛 | A large, semi-domesticated ox ( *Bos gaurus frontalis* ) of eastern India. |  Gayals are often kept for their meat and milk. 印度野牛通常被饲养以获取肉和奶。| synonym: mithan, wild ox | antonym: none | noun

gayal:/ˈɡaɪəl/ | n. |  野牛肉 | The meat of the gayal. |  Gayal meat is considered a delicacy in some regions. 在一些地区，野牛肉被认为是一种美味。| synonym: mithan beef, wild ox meat | antonym: none | noun
