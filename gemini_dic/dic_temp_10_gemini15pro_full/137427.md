nontheistic:/ˌnɒnθiˈɪstɪk/
adjective
非神论的，非有神论的
Not believing in a god or gods.
Buddhism is a nontheistic religion. 佛教是一种非神论宗教。
His worldview is nontheistic. 他的世界观是非神论的。
近义词:atheistic, nonreligious
反义词:theistic, religious
词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + theistic (from Greek theos "god" + -istic)
