fasten:/ˈfæsn/ (US), /ˈfɑːsn/ (UK)| v.|系紧，固定，扣住|To close or join securely; to make firm or tight.|Please fasten your seatbelts. 请系好安全带。|Synonyms: secure, attach, tie, bind|Antonyms: unfasten, loosen, detach, undo|verb

fasten:/ˈfæsn/ (US), /ˈfɑːsn/ (UK)| v.|集中(注意力等)|To direct one's attention or gaze intently.|He fastened his eyes on the road ahead. 他目不转睛地盯着前方的路。|Synonyms: focus, concentrate, fix|Antonyms: distract, divert|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fæstnian, from fæst ("firm, fixed")
