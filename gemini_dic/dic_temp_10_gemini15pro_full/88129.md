hiker:/ˈhaɪkər/| n.|徒步旅行者；远足者|A person who goes for long walks in the country, especially for pleasure or exercise. |例句：The hikers enjoyed the scenic views along the trail. 徒步旅行者欣赏着沿途的风景。

例句：Experienced hikers always carry a map and compass.  经验丰富的徒步旅行者总是随身携带地图和指南针。|近义词：walker, rambler, trekker|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hike + -er.  "Hike" likely originated in the early 19th century, possibly as a variant of "hich," meaning to hoist or jerk, suggesting the movement of walking with effort.
