morsel:/ˈmɔːrsəl/ (US), /ˈmɔːsəl/ (UK)| n. |小块;一口;少量|A small piece of food; a small amount of something, especially something pleasant.|She enjoyed every morsel of the delicious cake. 她很享受这块美味蛋糕的每一小口。|synonyms: bit, piece, mouthful, fragment, snippet|antonyms: abundance, plethora|noun

morsel:/ˈmɔːrsəl/ (US), /ˈmɔːsəl/ (UK)| n. |(尤指悦耳的)一小段(音乐、信息等)|A small piece or amount of something, especially something intangible like information or music.|He gave us a few tantalizing morsels of information about his new project. 他透露了一些关于他新项目的诱人信息。 |synonyms: snippet, bit, piece, fragment, taste|antonyms: whole, entirety|noun
