cartooning:/kɑːrˈtuːnɪŋ/
n.
漫画创作；漫画艺术
The art or process of creating cartoons.
He studied cartooning at the art school. 他在艺术学校学习漫画创作。
illustration, animation, sketching
painting, sculpture
noun
