fugato:/fuˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /fjuˈɡɑːtəʊ/
noun
赋格段；赋格曲
A short passage in a fugue style, or a movement based on such a passage.
The composer included a brief fugato in the second movement of the sonata.  作曲家在奏鸣曲的第二乐章中加入了一段简短的赋格段。
The final movement is a brilliant fugato.  最后一乐章是一段精彩的赋格曲。
synonyms: fugue-like passage, fugal section
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider non-fugal sections, homophonic passages)
n.
