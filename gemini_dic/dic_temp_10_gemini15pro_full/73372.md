foreground:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊnd/ | n.  |前景；最显著的位置|The artist placed the figure in the foreground of the painting. 艺术家将人物放在画面的前景中。|Example: The company's focus on customer service is in the foreground of their business strategy.  该公司对客户服务的关注是其业务战略的重点。|Synonyms: forefront, prominence|Antonyms: background, backdrop|noun
foreground:/ˈfɔːrɡraʊnd/ | v. |使突出；强调|The report foregrounds the need for improved safety measures. 该报告强调了改进安全措施的必要性。|Example:  The director foregrounded the emotional aspects of the story. 导演突出了故事的情感方面。|Synonyms: emphasize, highlight, feature|Antonyms: downplay, minimize, obscure|verb
