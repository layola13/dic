citers:/ˈsaɪtərz/| n.|引用者，引证者|People who quote or cite a source, especially in academic writing.  Those who mention or refer to something as proof or example.|The citers of this study failed to acknowledge the original source.  这项研究的引用者未能注明原始来源。|近义词：quoters, referencers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps originators or authors)|noun
