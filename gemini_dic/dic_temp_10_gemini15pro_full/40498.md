contrivance:/kənˈtraɪvəns/
n.
发明；装置；计谋；人为的东西
a thing that is created skillfully and inventively to perform a particular function.  Something artificial or unnatural.  A plan or scheme.
The author used a clever contrivance to bring the two main characters together.  作者巧妙地设计了一个情节，让两个主要人物相遇。

近义词:invention, device, gadget, scheme, plot, plan
反义词:reality, naturalness, simplicity

词性:noun
