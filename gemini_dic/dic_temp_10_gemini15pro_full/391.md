abirritate: /æˈbɪrɪˌteɪt/
verb
激怒，使烦躁
To irritate or annoy mildly.  To make someone slightly angry or impatient.
His constant complaining abirritated her. 他不停的抱怨让她很烦躁。
The fly buzzing around his head abirritated him. 苍蝇在他头上嗡嗡作响，使他烦躁不安。
近义词: annoy, irritate, bother, vex, irk
反义词: soothe, calm, please, delight
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ab- (away, from) + irritare (to irritate).
