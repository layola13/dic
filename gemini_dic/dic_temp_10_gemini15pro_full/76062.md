furnish:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃ/ (US) /ˈfɜːnɪʃ/ (UK)| v. |布置；供应；提供|To provide with furniture or other necessary equipment; to supply or provide.|例句：They furnished the apartment with modern furniture. 他们用现代家具布置了公寓。|近义词：equip, supply, provide, outfit|反义词：strip, deprive|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French furniss-, lengthened stem of furnir "to furnish, provide, equip," from Frankish *frumjan "to perform, accomplish, equip," from Proto-Germanic *frumjanan "to perform, do," from PIE *per- "to try, risk."
