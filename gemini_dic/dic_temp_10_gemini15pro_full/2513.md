administrations:/ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃənz/
n.
管理；行政；实施；行政部门；政府
the process or activity of running a business, organization, etc.
The Bush administration faced many challenges. 布什政府面临许多挑战。

The administration of the new policy has been difficult. 新政策的实施很困难。

近义词:management, governance, execution
反义词:mismanagement
词性:名词复数

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English administracioun, from Old French administration, from Latin administratiō (“management”), from administrātus, past participle of administrāre (“to manage, direct”), from ad- (“to”) + ministrāre (“to serve, manage”), from minister (“servant, assistant”).
