homicidally:/ˌhɒmɪˈsaɪdəli/ | adv. |杀人地；蓄意谋杀地| In a manner suggesting the intent to commit homicide. | He glared homicidally at his opponent. 他凶狠地瞪着对手，眼神中充满了杀意。|  He spoke homicidally about his boss. 他用充满杀意地语气谈论他的老板。 |Synonyms: murderously, violently, destructively | Antonyms: peacefully, calmly, gently | word property: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From homicide + -al + -ly.  Homicide comes from Late Latin homicidium, from homo "man" + -cidium "-cide".
