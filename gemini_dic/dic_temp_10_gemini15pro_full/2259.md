adeling: /ˈeɪdlɪŋ/
noun
王子；年轻的贵族；小王子
A young noble; a prince.
The young adeling was known for his bravery.  这位年轻的王子以其勇敢而闻名。
He was raised as an adeling in the royal court. 他在宫廷里被抚养长大，像个王子。
Synonyms: prince, nobleman, lordling
Antonyms: commoner, peasant
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æþeling (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *aþalingaz, meaning "son of a noble family," from *aþal- "noble."  Cognate with Old Saxon athiling, Old Norse aþlingr, Old High German adalinc, Gothic aþalwoareis.
