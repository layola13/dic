clearwater:/ˈklɪərˌwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈklɪəˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|清水，清澈的水|Clear water is essential for healthy aquatic life.  清澈的水对健康的水生生物至关重要。|例句：We swam in the cool, clearwater of the lake. 我们在凉爽清澈的湖水中游泳。|近义词：clean water, pure water, fresh water|反义词:murky water, polluted water|noun

clearwater:/ˈklɪərˌwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈklɪəˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|清澈的|The clearwater stream flowed gently through the meadow. 清澈的小溪缓缓流过草地。|例句：The hotel boasts a clearwater swimming pool. 这家酒店拥有一个清澈的游泳池。|近义词：limpid, crystalline, transparent|反义词:cloudy, murky, opaque|adjective
