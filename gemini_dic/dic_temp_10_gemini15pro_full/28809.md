carotene:/ˈkærətɪn/ /ˈkærəˌtin/ | n. |胡萝卜素|Any of several orange or red isomeric hydrocarbons, C40H56, found in carrots and other yellow and dark-green leafy vegetables, and a precursor of vitamin A.| Carrots are a good source of beta-carotene. 胡萝卜是β-胡萝卜素的良好来源。|Synonyms: carotenoid|Antonyms: None|Noun

carotene:/ˈkærətɪn/ /ˈkærəˌtin/ | n. |胡萝卜素|A yellow or orange plant pigment that can be converted to vitamin A in the body.| Dark leafy greens also contain carotene. 深色绿叶蔬菜也含有胡萝卜素。|Synonyms: provitamin A|Antonyms: None|Noun
