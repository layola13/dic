/ˈbɪɡˌneɪm/ | n. | 大人物，名人，要人 | A person who is very famous and important, especially in a particular profession or industry. |  Beyoncé is a big name in the music industry.  碧昂丝是音乐界的大人物。

He's trying to become a big name in Hollywood. 他正努力成为好莱坞的知名人物。 | synonyms: celebrity, star, VIP, luminary, notable, personage | antonyms: nobody, unknown | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Combination of "big" and "name".  "Big" meaning important or significant, and "name" referring to reputation or recognition. The term gained prominence in the early 20th century, particularly in the context of entertainment and business.
