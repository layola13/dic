opiate:/ˈoʊpiət/ (US) /ˈəʊpiət/ (UK)| n. |鸦片制剂；麻醉剂|A drug derived from opium, having a narcotic effect.|The doctor prescribed an opiate for the patient's severe pain. 医生给病人开了鸦片类药物来缓解剧烈的疼痛。|Synonyms: narcotic, opioid, painkiller|Antonyms: stimulant|noun


opiate:/ˈoʊpiət/ (US) /ˈəʊpiət/ (UK)| adj. | 似鸦片的；麻醉的 | Relating to or resembling opium in its narcotic effects.  | The music had an almost opiate effect on the listeners.  这音乐对听众几乎有麻醉的效果。|Synonyms: soporific, narcotic|Antonyms: stimulating|adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From French *opiat*, from Latin *opiātum* (“poppy juice, opium”), neuter of *opiātus* (“containing opium”), from *opium* (“poppy juice, opium”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ὄπιον (ópion)* (“poppy juice”).
