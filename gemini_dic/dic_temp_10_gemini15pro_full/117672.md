meio:/ˈmeɪoʊ/ (US), /ˈmaɪoʊ/ (UK)| adj. |减数分裂的; 由减数分裂产生的|Relating to or produced by meiosis, a type of cell division that results in four daughter cells each with half the number of chromosomes of the parent cell. |例句：Meiotic division is essential for sexual reproduction. 减数分裂对于有性生殖至关重要。

例句：The meiotic process generates genetic diversity. 减数分裂过程产生遗传多样性。

|近义词：reductional (referring to chromosome number)|反义词：mitotic|词性：形容词
