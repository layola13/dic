noncoercively:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɜːsɪvli/
adverb
非强制地
without coercion; in a way that does not involve force or threats.
The agreement was reached noncoercively, with both parties willingly participating.  双方自愿参与，非强制地达成了协议。
He persuaded her noncoercively to join the team. 他非强制地说服她加入了团队。
Synonyms: voluntarily, willingly, freely, of one's own accord
Antonyms: coercively, forcibly, compulsorily
词性: 副词
