godly:/ˈɡɒdli/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdli/ (US)| adj.|虔诚的；敬神的；神圣的|Resembling or having the qualities of God; devout, pious, righteous.|She lived a godly life, devoted to prayer and charity. 她过着虔诚的生活，致力于祈祷和慈善。|He spoke in a godly tone, full of wisdom and compassion. 他用虔诚的语气说话，充满了智慧和同情心。|近义词：pious, devout, religious, holy, righteous|反义词：ungodly, impious, wicked, sinful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *godlīc (“divine, godly”), equivalent to god + -ly. Cognate with Dutch goddelijk (“godly”), German göttlich (“divine”), Swedish gudlig (“godly”).
