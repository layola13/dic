handshakings:/ˈhændʃeɪkɪŋz/
n.
握手, 握手次数
The act of shaking hands, often as a greeting or to seal an agreement; also, instances of this act.
There were many handshakings after the conference.  会议结束后有很多握手。
synonyms: handshakes, greetings, salutations
antonyms: snubs, rebuffs
noun
