overchosen:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃoʊzən/
adjective

having too many choices, leading to difficulty making a decision or dissatisfaction with the choice made
The consumer is now overchosen, with hundreds of brands of cereal available. 消费者现在面临过度选择，有数百种品牌的麦片可供选择。
overwhelmed, spoiled for choice
limited, restricted
