contrive:/kənˈtraɪv/| v.|设计；发明；图谋；设法做到|to invent or plan cleverly; to manage to do something difficult|例句：She contrived to get a seat on the crowded bus.  她在拥挤的公交车上设法找到了一个座位。|近义词：devise, invent, engineer, plan, scheme, plot|反义词:fail, neglect|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French controver (Modern French controuver), from Late Latin contropare "to compare," from Latin com- "with, together" + tropus "a figure of speech, trope." The original sense was "to compare, collate," hence "to invent, devise."
