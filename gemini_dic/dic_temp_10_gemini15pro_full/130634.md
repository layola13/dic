nonadmissible:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪsəbl/ /ˌnɑːnədˈmɪsəbl/| adj.|不可接受的； inadmissible; not allowed; not acceptable|Not admissible, especially as evidence in a court of law.|The evidence was deemed nonadmissible due to its questionable origin.  由于来源可疑，该证据被认为不可接受。|synonyms: unacceptable, inadmissible, invalid, ineligible, improper|antonyms: admissible, acceptable, valid, eligible, proper|adjective

nonadmissible:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪsəbl/ /ˌnɑːnədˈmɪsəbl/| adj.|不可许可的|Not permissible or allowable.|His behavior was considered nonadmissible by the school's code of conduct.  他的行为被学校的行为准则认为是不可接受的。|synonyms: impermissible, prohibited, forbidden|antonyms: permissible, allowed, permitted|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + admissible.  Admissible from Latin admittere "to admit," from ad- "to" + mittere "to send."
