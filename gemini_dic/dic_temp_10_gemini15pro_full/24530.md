broadens:/ˈbrɔːdnz/
verb
(使)变宽，(使)变阔，扩大，扩展
Makes something wider or larger in scope.
Travel broadens the mind. 旅行使人开阔眼界。
近义词: expands, widens, increases, extends
反义词: narrows, restricts, limits, shrinks
词性: 动词


Etymology:
From Old English *brādian, from brād (“broad”).
