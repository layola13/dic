fivefold:/ˈfaɪvfəʊld/| adj. adv.|五倍的；五重的|Multiplied by five; having five parts or elements.  Being five times as great or as much.|例句：The company saw a fivefold increase in profits this year. 今年，这家公司的利润增长了五倍。|近义词：quintuple, five times|反义词: one-fifth, a fifth|词性:形容词/副词
fivefold:/ˈfaɪvfəʊld/| n.|五倍|A quantity five times as great as another.|例句：The investment yielded a fivefold return. 这项投资获得了五倍的回报。|近义词：quintuple|反义词: one-fifth, a fifth|词性:名词
