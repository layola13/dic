dabble:/ˈdæbəl/| v.|涉猎，浅尝，戏水|To casually engage in an activity without serious commitment or deep involvement.|He dabbled in painting but never pursued it seriously. 他涉猎过绘画，但从未认真钻研。|近义词：tinker, experiment, dip into|反义词：specialize, master|词性:动词
dabble:/ˈdæbəl/| n.|少量，少许（尤指液体）|A small amount, especially of a liquid.|She added just a dabble of milk to her tea.  她在茶里加了一点儿牛奶。|近义词：splash, drop, bit|反义词：lot, abundance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *dabbelen, frequentative of dabben (“to strike, hit lightly”), probably of imitative origin.
