The artist was known for her contemplative nature, spending hours observing the world around her.  这位艺术家以其沉思的性格而闻名，她会花几个小时观察周围的世界。 | 近义词: thinkers, meditators,  reflectors | 反义词: activists, doers, extroverts | 词性: Noun (Plural)

contemplative: /kənˈtɛmplətɪv/ | adj. | 沉思的；冥想的| Expressing or involving prolonged thought. |  She had a contemplative look on her face. 她脸上带着沉思的表情。

He spent a contemplative afternoon by the river. 他在河边度过了一个沉思的下午。 | 近义词: thoughtful, reflective, meditative, pensive | 反义词: unthinking, impulsive, rash | 词性: Adjective
