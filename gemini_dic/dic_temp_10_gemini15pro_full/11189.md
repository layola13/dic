archipelago:/ˌɑːrkɪˈpeləɡoʊ/ (US), /ˌɑːkɪˈpeləɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. |群岛；列岛|A group of islands closely scattered in a sea or ocean.|The Indonesian archipelago is known for its biodiversity.  印度尼西亚群岛以其生物多样性而闻名。|例句：The Philippines is an archipelago of over 7,000 islands. 菲律宾是一个由7000多个岛屿组成的群岛。

|例句：The boat sailed through the archipelago, stopping at several islands. 这艘船航行穿过群岛，在几个岛屿停靠。|近义词：island chain, island group|反义词:continent, mainland|词性:noun
