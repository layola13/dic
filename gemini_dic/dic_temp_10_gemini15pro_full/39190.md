congenialize:/kənˈdʒiːniəlaɪz/
verb
使合人心意；使意气相投；使 congenial
To make congenial; to adapt to one's tastes or sensibilities.
She tried to congenialize with her new colleagues by bringing in cookies. 她试图通过带饼干来和新同事们搞好关系。
The host congenialized the atmosphere by playing lively music. 主人播放了欢快的音乐，使气氛融洽。
近义词:harmonize, accommodate, adapt
反义词:alienate, estrange
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From congenial + -ize.  Congenial itself comes from Late Latin congeniālis "of the same kind," from Latin com- "together" + geniālis "of birth."
