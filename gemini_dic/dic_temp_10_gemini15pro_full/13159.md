asystolism: /eɪˈsɪstəlɪzəm/ /əˈsɪstəlɪzəm/
n.
心脏停搏；心搏停止
Cardiac arrest; cessation of heartbeat.
The patient went into asystole and all attempts at resuscitation failed. 病人出现了心脏停搏，所有复苏的尝试都失败了。
The paramedics worked frantically to revive the victim of asystolism.  护理人员拼命工作以使心脏停搏的受害者苏醒。
近义词: cardiac arrest, heart stoppage
反义词: normal sinus rhythm, heartbeat
词性: noun
