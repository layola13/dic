misreprint:/ˌmɪsˈriːprɪnt/
noun
印刷错误；错印；印错的字
an error in printing
The book had to be reprinted because of a misreprint on page 32.  这本书因为第32页有一个印刷错误而不得不重印。
synonyms: typo, misprint, erratum
antonyms: correct print
