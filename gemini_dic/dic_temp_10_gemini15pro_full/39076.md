confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ /ˈkɑːnfaʊnd/ | vt.|使困惑；使惊讶；证明…是错的；挫败；混淆；诅咒|To cause surprise or confusion in (someone), especially by acting against their expectations. |The sudden rise in prices confounded economists.  价格的突然上涨使经济学家感到困惑。

The magician confounded the audience with his tricks. 魔术师的戏法使观众感到困惑。|近义词：bewilder, perplex, puzzle, confuse, frustrate, baffle|反义词：clarify, enlighten, distinguish|词性：动词
