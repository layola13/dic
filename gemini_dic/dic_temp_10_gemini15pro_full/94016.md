impanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/| vt.|组成陪审团；选任（陪审团）|To enroll a list of persons to serve on a jury.|The judge impaneled a jury of twelve people. 法官选任了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。|Synonyms: enlist, enroll, swear in|Antonyms: discharge, dismiss|Verb

impanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/| n.|陪审团名单；陪审团|A list of jurors enrolled for jury duty; a jury.|The impanel was made up of people from all walks of life. 陪审团由各行各业的人组成。|Synonyms: jury, panel|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
