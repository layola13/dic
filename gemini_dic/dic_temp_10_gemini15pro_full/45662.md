curtain: /ˈkɜːrtən/ (UK) /ˈkɜːrtn/ (US)| v. | 用窗帘遮盖；掩蔽 | To cover or conceal with a curtain. |  The stage was curtained off during the intermission. 幕间休息时，舞台被幕布遮住了。 | synonyms: screen, veil, mask | antonyms: uncover, unveil | verb
