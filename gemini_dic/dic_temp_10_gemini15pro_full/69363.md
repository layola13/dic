federator:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪtər/| n.| 联合者；联邦成员；同盟者|A person or organization that unites separate states or groups into a federation or alliance. |The United States acted as a federator in bringing together several countries for the trade agreement.|Synonyms: unifier, joiner, integrator, consolidator, ally|Antonyms: divider, separator, disintegrator|noun
