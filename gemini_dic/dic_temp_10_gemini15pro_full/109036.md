legacy's:/ˈlɛɡəsiːz/
noun
遗产(的)
Something (such as property or money) that is received from someone who has died; something that happened in the past or that comes from someone in the past.  It is the possessive form of "legacy," indicating ownership or association.
This house is part of my family's legacy. 这栋房子是我们家族遗产的一部分。
The legacy's value has increased significantly over the years. 这份遗产的价值近年来大幅增长。
synonyms: inheritance, bequest, heritage
antonyms: debt, loss
