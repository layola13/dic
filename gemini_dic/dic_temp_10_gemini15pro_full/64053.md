epitomical:/ˌɛpɪˈtɒmɪkəl/ | adj. | 典型的，摘要的 | Representing something perfectly; being a typical example of something. | This painting is epitomical of the Impressionist movement. 这幅画是印象派运动的典型代表。

The concise summary is epitomical of the entire report.  这份简明的摘要是整个报告的缩影。| 近义词: representative, typical, exemplary, quintessential | 反义词: atypical, unusual, anomalous | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *epitomicus, from Greek epitomikos, from epitemnein "to cut short," from epi- "upon" + temnein "to cut."
