checkin:/ˈtʃɛkɪn/| n. |登记，报到|The process of registering one's arrival, typically at a hotel, airport, or other facility.|Example:  The hotel checkin was quick and efficient.  酒店登记很快捷高效。

Example: Online checkin saves time at the airport. 在线登记可以节省在机场的时间。|近义词：registration, sign-in|反义词:checkout|词性:名词
