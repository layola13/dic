hexandrous:/hɛkˈsændrəs/
adjective
六雄蕊的；具有六个雄蕊的
Having six stamens in each flower.
The hexandrous flower attracted many bees.  六雄蕊的花朵吸引了许多蜜蜂。
Near synonyms: six-stamened
No common antonyms
词性: adjective
