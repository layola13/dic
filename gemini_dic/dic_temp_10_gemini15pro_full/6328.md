amovable:/əˈmuːvəbəl/
adjective
可移动的；活动的
capable of being moved; not fixed
The furniture is all amovable, so we can rearrange the room easily.  这些家具都是可移动的，所以我们可以很容易地重新布置房间。
The museum exhibit featured several amovable parts that visitors could interact with.  博物馆的展览中有几个可移动的部分，游客可以与之互动。
近义词: mobile, portable, transportable
反义词: immovable, fixed, stationary
词性: adjective

词源:
From a- + movable.  "a-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without," although in this case, it functions as an intensifier. "Movable" comes from the verb "move," which has roots in Old French "mover" and ultimately Latin "movēre."
