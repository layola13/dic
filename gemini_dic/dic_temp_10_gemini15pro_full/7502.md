annalists:/ˈænəlɪsts/
n.
记录者，编年史家
A person who writes annals or chronicles; a historian, especially one who records events year by year.
The annalists recorded the king's reign in meticulous detail.  记录者一丝不苟地记录了国王的统治。
The annalists documented the major events of that era.  编年史家记录了那个时代的主要事件。

近义词:chroniclers, historians, recorders
反义词:None
词性:noun
