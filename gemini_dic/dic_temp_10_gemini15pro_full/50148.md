denunciate:/dɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪt/ /dɪˈnaʊnsiˌeɪt/
verb
公开谴责，痛斥
to criticize something or someone strongly and publicly
The government's economic policies were denounced by the opposition. 政府的经济政策受到了反对派的谴责。
The human rights group denounced the use of torture. 人权组织谴责使用酷刑。
近义词: condemn, criticize, censure, denigrate, lambaste
反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol
词性: verb

denunciation:/dɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/ /dɪˌnaʊnsiˈeɪʃən/
noun
谴责，痛斥
public criticism of something or someone
The minister's denunciation of the violence was welcomed by all parties. 部长对暴力的谴责受到了各方的欢迎。
His speech contained a fierce denunciation of the government's policies. 他的演讲中包含了对政府政策的猛烈抨击。
近义词: condemnation, criticism, censure
反义词: praise, commendation, laudation
词性: noun
