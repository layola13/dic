owlishness:/ˈaʊlɪʃnəs/
n.
猫头鹰般的品质;严肃而学究气的样子
The quality of resembling or suggesting an owl, especially in being solemn and studious.
He peered owlishly at the contract through his spectacles, his owlishness adding to the gravity of the situation. 他戴着眼镜，像猫头鹰一样凝视着合同，他严肃而学究气的样子使情况显得更加严重。
近义词：solemnity, seriousness, gravity, studiousness
反义词：frivolity, levity, lightheartedness
