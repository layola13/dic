caterwaul: /ˈkætərˌwɔːl/ (US) /ˈkætəwɔːl/ (UK)| vi.| 发出刺耳的尖叫声；嚎叫；|To make a harsh cry or howl.|  The singer caterwauled her way through the song. 那位歌手尖声怪气地唱完了这首歌。 |近义词：shriek, howl, wail, yowl |反义词: purr, murmur |词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *caterwawen*, perhaps from *catir-* (cat) + *-wawen* (to wail).
