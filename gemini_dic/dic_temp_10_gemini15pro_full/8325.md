anti-patriotically:/ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪkli/ | adv. | 非爱国地，不爱国地 | In a manner that opposes or shows a lack of love for one's country. | He acted anti-patriotically by burning the flag. 他通过焚烧国旗表现得非常不爱国。|  She spoke anti-patriotically about the government's policies. 她对政府的政策发表了不爱国的言论。 | 近义词: disloyally, treasonously, seditiously | 反义词: patriotically, loyally | 词性: 副词
