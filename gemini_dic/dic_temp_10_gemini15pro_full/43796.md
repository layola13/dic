cremosin:/ˈkrɛməsɪn/ | n. |凝乳酶|An enzyme, also known as rennin, found in the stomach of calves and used in cheese making to coagulate milk.|Example 1:  Cremosin is essential for producing many types of cheese. 凝乳酶对于生产许多类型的奶酪至关重要。

Example 2:  Traditional cheesemaking relies on cremosin derived from calf stomachs.  传统的奶酪制作依赖于从小牛胃中提取的凝乳酶。|近义词：rennin, chymosin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps an inhibitor of the enzyme)|词性:noun
