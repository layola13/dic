akarunya:/ˌəkəˈruːnjə/ | n. |同情，怜悯，慈悲 | Compassion, pity, mercy; kindness, especially to those suffering.  It derives from Sanskrit "karuṇā" meaning "compassion."| He showed akarunya to the beggar. 他对乞丐表现出了怜悯。|Synonyms: compassion, pity, mercy, kindness, empathy| Antonyms: cruelty, harshness, indifference, apathy| Noun
