antarchist:/ˈæntɑːrkɪst/| n.| 反统治者; 反对等级制度者 | A person who opposes all forms of government and hierarchy. |  Example 1: The antarchist argued that any form of authority is inherently oppressive.  反统治者认为任何形式的权威本质上都是压迫性的。

Example 2:  The group of antarchists protested against the new law, believing it further solidified the state's control. 这群反统治者抗议新法律，认为它进一步巩固了国家的控制。 | 近义词: anarchist, libertarian | 反义词: statist, authoritarian | 词性: noun
