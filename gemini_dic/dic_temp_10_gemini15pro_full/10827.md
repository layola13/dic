aquitaid:/ˌækwɪˈteɪd/| n.| 阿奎泰德（一种用于治疗轻度脱水的口服补液）|A commercial name for an oral rehydration solution used to prevent and treat dehydration, especially in infants and children suffering from diarrhea. It contains a specific balance of salts and sugars.|例句：The doctor recommended Aquitaid for the child's diarrhea. 医生建议给孩子服用阿奎泰德来治疗腹泻。

例句：Always have some Aquitaid on hand, especially during hot weather.  尤其是在炎热的天气里，手边要常备一些阿奎泰德。

|近义词：Pedialyte, Rehydralyte, electrolyte solution|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps dehydration itself)|词性:noun
