mour:/mʊr/, /mɔːr/ | v. | 哀悼，忧伤 | To feel or express sorrow or grief. | She mourned the loss of her beloved pet. 她哀悼她心爱宠物的离去。

He mourned for his lost youth. 他为他逝去的青春感到悲伤。

近义词: grieve, lament, sorrow | 反义词: rejoice, celebrate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *mouren*, from Old French *murer*, of uncertain origin, probably from a Vulgar Latin *mūgire* (“to moan, bellow”), from Latin *mūgīre* (“to bellow, low”).  Ultimately related to *mock*.
