Etymology:
From anti- + plague.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against," "opposite of," "instead of," or "equivalent to." "Plague" comes from the Old French "plage" and ultimately from the Latin "plaga," meaning "a blow," "stroke," "wound," or "calamity."
