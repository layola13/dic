challan:/tʃəˈlɑːn/ | n. |罚单，传票，挑战|An official document, especially in India, Pakistan, and other South Asian countries, used as a receipt for payment, a summons, or a written challenge.|例句：He received a challan for speeding. 他收到了一张超速罚单。

例句：The police issued a challan to the driver. 警察给司机开了一张罚单。|近义词：ticket, summons, citation|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

challan:/tʃəˈlɑːn/ | v. |挑战，传唤，开罚单|To issue a challan to someone.|例句：The traffic police challaned him for driving without a license. 交警因为他无证驾驶给他开了罚单。

例句：They were challaned for illegal parking. 他们因非法停车而被开了罚单。|近义词：summon, cite, ticket|反义词:N/A|词性:verb


词源: From Hindi चालाना (cālānā), from Sanskrit चालयति (cālayati, “to move, drive”).
