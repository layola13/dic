amateurism:/ˈæmətərɪzəm/ | n. | 业余性；业余爱好；业余精神 | The state or quality of being an amateur; the practice of pursuing an activity, especially a sport, on an unpaid basis, as opposed to a professional. |  He strongly defended amateurism in sport. 他极力维护体育运动的业余性。 | 近义词: dilettantism, nonprofessionalism | 反义词: professionalism | 词性: noun

词源: From French *amateurisme*, from amateur + -isme.
