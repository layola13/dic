convincer:/kənˈvɪnsər/| n.|说服者，令人信服的人或事物|A person or thing that convinces. |The lawyer was a skilled convincer.  这位律师是一位很有技巧的说服者。|近义词：persuader, advocate|反义词：dissuader, opponent|noun

convince:/kənˈvɪns/| vt.|使确信，使信服|To persuade someone to believe something or to do something.|I managed to convince him to come with us. 我设法说服了他和我们一起来。|近义词：persuade, assure|反义词：dissuade, deter|verb
