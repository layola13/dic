nomadic: /noʊˈmædɪk/ (US) /nəʊˈmædɪk/ (UK)| adj.|游牧的；流浪的|Relating to nomads; wandering.|The nomadic tribes moved their herds across the vast plains. 游牧部落将他们的畜群迁移到广阔的平原上。|The family lived a nomadic lifestyle, traveling from town to town. 这家人过着游牧的生活方式，从一个城镇到另一个城镇旅行。|近义词：wandering, roving, migratory, itinerant|反义词：settled, stationary, sedentary|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *nomadicus, from Greek νομαδικός (nomadĭkos), from νομάς (nomás, “roaming, wandering”), from νέμειν (némein, “to pasture, to distribute”).
