flowk: /fləʊk/
n.|人群，羊群;大量，许多|A large number of people or things, especially birds or animals, moving together.  A large number of similar things or people appearing at the same time.|例句：A flowk of tourists descended on the small town. 一大群游客涌入小镇。|例句：There was a flowk of applications for the job.  这份工作收到了大量的申请。|近义词：crowd, flock, swarm, multitude, host|反义词：few, scattering, handful|词性:名词
