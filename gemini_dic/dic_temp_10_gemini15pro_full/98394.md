insolubility:/ɪnˌsɒljʊˈbɪləti/ | n. |不溶性，无法解决 |The insolubility of the problem frustrated the team.  这个问题的无法解决让团队感到沮丧。| insolubleness,  indissolubility| solubility, resolvability | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insolubilitatem, from insolubilis ("insoluble") + -itatem (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). Insolubilis is from in- (prefix meaning "not") + solubilis ("that can be loosened, dissolved"), from solvere ("to loosen, dissolve").
