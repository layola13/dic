angel-cake:/ˈeɪndʒəl keɪk/| n.|天使蛋糕|A type of sponge cake made with egg whites, sugar, and flour, but no butter. It is light, airy, and white in color.|We had angel-cake for dessert. 我们甜点吃了天使蛋糕。|近义词：sponge cake, chiffon cake|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps a dense, rich cake like a devil's food cake could be considered an antonym in terms of texture and richness)|noun
