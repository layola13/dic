crewmen:/ˈkruːmən/ | n. |船员，水手|A member of a ship's crew. |The crewmen were busy preparing the ship for departure. 船员们正忙着准备船只出发。|Synonym: sailor, seaman, mariner |Antonym: passenger, landsman | Noun

Etymology: From crew + -man.  "Crew" comes from Old French "creue" meaning "increase, growth," ultimately from the Latin "crescere," "to grow."  "-man" is a common suffix denoting a male person.
