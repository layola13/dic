antioxidizing:/ˌæntiˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ /ˌæntiˈɑːksɪdaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
抗氧化的
Inhibiting oxidation; preventing or slowing the deterioration of a substance by oxidation.
Antioxidizing agents help protect the body from free radical damage. 抗氧化剂有助于保护身体免受自由基的损害。
Antioxidizing properties of vitamin C are well known. 维生素C的抗氧化特性是众所周知的。

近义词: antioxidant, anti-oxidative
反义词: oxidizing, pro-oxidant
词性: adjective
