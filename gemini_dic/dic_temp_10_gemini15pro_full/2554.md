ETYMOLOGY->
From admissible + -ness.  Admissible traces back to the Latin "admittere," meaning "to admit" or "to allow to enter." The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "admissible" into the noun "admissibleness," indicating the state or quality of being admissible.
