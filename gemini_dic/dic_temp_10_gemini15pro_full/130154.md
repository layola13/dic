non-repatriable:/ˌnɒn riːˈpeɪtriəbl/ /ˌnɑːn riːˈpeɪtriəbl/
adjective
不可汇回的，不能遣返的
Not allowed or able to be sent back to one's own country.
Example: Non-repatriable earnings are profits earned in a foreign country that cannot be legally transferred back to the company's home country.  在国外赚取的不可汇回的收益是指在外国赚取的利润，这些利润不能合法地转移回公司所在的国家。

Example: The refugees were considered non-repatriable due to the ongoing conflict in their homeland. 由于家乡持续的冲突，这些难民被认为是不可遣返的。
Synonym: non-returnable, unrepatriable
Antonym: repatriable, returnable
词性: adjective
