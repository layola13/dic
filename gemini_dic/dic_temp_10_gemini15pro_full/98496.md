instancing:/ˈɪnstənsɪŋ/| n.|实例化，例示|The process of creating multiple copies of an object in computer graphics, often with slight variations, without individually modeling each one.  This technique is used to efficiently render large numbers of similar objects, such as trees in a forest or crowds of people.|The game uses instancing to render the thousands of blades of grass in the field.|近义词：replication, duplication, instantiation|反义词: N/A|noun
