mediant:/ˈmiːdiənt/| n.|中音，中项，调解人|A term used in music theory, mathematics, and negotiation. In music, it's the third degree of a diatonic scale. In mathematics, it refers to a fraction formed by adding the numerators and denominators of two other fractions. In negotiation, it can refer to a person who helps to resolve a dispute.|The mediant chord adds a sense of yearning to the melody. 中音和弦给旋律增添了一种渴望的感觉。|近义词：mediator, intermediary|反义词:extremist|词性:noun

mediant:/ˈmiːdiənt/| adj.|调解的，居间的|Acting as a mediator; relating to or situated in the middle.|His mediant role helped the two parties reach an agreement. 他的调解作用帮助双方达成了协议。|近义词：mediating, intermediary|反义词:extreme|词性:adjective
