chantingly:/ˈtʃæntɪŋli/ | adv. |吟诵地，反复唱地 |In a chanting manner; rhythmically and repetitively, often in a monotonous tone.  |She spoke chantingly, as if reciting a prayer. 她吟诵着说话，就像在背诵祈祷文。|Example: The monks chantingly recited their prayers. 僧侣们吟诵着他们的祈祷文。|Synonyms: rhythmically, monotonously, repetitively, singingly |Antonyms: disjointedly, erratically, irregularly |词性:副词
