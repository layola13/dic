despicableness:/dɪˈspɪkəbəlnəs/
n.
卑鄙；卑劣；可鄙
The state or quality of being despicable; extreme vileness or meanness.
His despicableness was evident in his willingness to betray his friends for personal gain. 他的卑鄙体现在他为了个人利益而背叛朋友。

The despicableness of the crime shocked the entire community. 这起罪行的卑劣程度震惊了整个社区。

近义词: vileness, baseness, meanness, contemptibility, wickedness
反义词: nobleness, goodness, virtue, honorableness

词性: noun

词源: From despicable + -ness.  Despicable comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin *despicabilis, from despicari ‘look down on’, from de- ‘down’ + specere ‘to look’.
