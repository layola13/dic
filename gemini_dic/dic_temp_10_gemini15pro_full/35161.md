clown: /klaʊn/| n. v.|小丑；笨蛋；粗鲁的人；扮小丑|A clown is a comic entertainer, often wearing a colourful costume, makeup, and oversized shoes, who performs slapstick or similar types of comedy.|例句：The children loved the clown at the circus. 孩子们喜欢马戏团的小丑。

例句：He acted like a clown throughout the meeting. 他在整个会议中都像个小丑一样。|近义词：jester, buffoon, fool|反义词：serious person|词性: noun, verb

clown: /klaʊn/| v.|扮小丑；胡闹|To act like a clown; to behave in a silly or foolish way.|例句：Stop clowning around and get to work! 别再胡闹了，快去工作！

例句：He's always clowning about, trying to make people laugh. 他总是扮小丑，试图逗人笑。|近义词：fool around, joke|反义词：behave, be serious|词性: verb
