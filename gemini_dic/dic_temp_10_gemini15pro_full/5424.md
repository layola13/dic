almighty:/ɔːlˈmaɪti/
adjective
万能的；全能的；至高无上的
having unlimited power; able to do anything
例句：The almighty dollar is the driving force behind many decisions. 金钱万能是许多决策背后的驱动力。

例句：They prayed to the Almighty for guidance. 他们向上帝祈祷寻求指引。

近义词：omnipotent, all-powerful, supreme
反义词：powerless, weak, impotent
词性：adjective


almighty:/ɔːlˈmaɪti/
noun
上帝；全能的神
God
例句：They believe in the Almighty. 他们信仰上帝。

例句：May the Almighty protect us. 愿上帝保佑我们。

近义词：God, Lord, Creator
反义词：N/A (No antonym for a deity)
词性：noun
