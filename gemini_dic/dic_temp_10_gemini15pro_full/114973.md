manchukuo:/mænˈtʃuːkwəʊ/ |n.|伪满洲国|A puppet state established in Manchuria by the Empire of Japan in 1932.|Japan invaded Manchuria in 1931 and established Manchukuo the following year. 日本于1931年入侵满洲，并在次年建立了伪满洲国。|Synonym: puppet state, Manzhouguo|Antonym: independent state|noun
