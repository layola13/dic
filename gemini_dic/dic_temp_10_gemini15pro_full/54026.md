discursiveness:/dɪsˈkɜːsɪvnəs/
n.
离题；散漫；冗长；东拉西扯
The quality of being discursive; digressing from subject to subject.
His writing suffers from discursiveness; he can't seem to stick to the point. 他的写作显得散漫冗长；他似乎无法抓住重点。
近义词: rambling, digression, prolixity, verbosity, diffuseness
反义词: conciseness, brevity, succinctness, terseness
词性: noun

discursive:/dɪsˈkɜːsɪv/
adj.
离题的；散漫的；冗长的；东拉西扯的
Passing aimlessly from one subject to another; rambling.
The professor's lecture was discursive, covering a wide range of topics but lacking focus.  教授的讲座很散漫，涵盖了广泛的主题，但缺乏重点。
近义词: rambling, digressive, prolix, verbose, diffuse
反义词: concise, brief, succinct, terse
词性: adjective
