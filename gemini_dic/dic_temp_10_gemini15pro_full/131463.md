noncoherence:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərəns/ (British) /ˌnɑːnkəʊˈhɪrəns/ (American)| n.|非连贯性，不一致|The lack of logical or consistent connection between ideas or parts of something; incoherence.|The noncoherence of his argument made it difficult to follow.|Synonyms: incoherence, disunity, disjointedness, inconsistency|Antonyms: coherence, consistency, unity|noun

noncoherent:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərənt/ (British) /ˌnɑːnkəʊˈhɪrənt/ (American)| adj.|不连贯的，不一致的|Lacking logical or consistent connection between ideas or parts; incoherent.|His speech was rambling and noncoherent.|Synonyms: incoherent, disjointed, rambling, illogical|Antonyms: coherent, consistent, logical, connected|adjective
