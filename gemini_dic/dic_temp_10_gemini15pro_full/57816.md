drenched:/drentʃt/
adjective
湿透的
Thoroughly soaked or wet.
The hikers were drenched after the downpour.  徒步旅行者在大雨之后浑身湿透。
soaked, saturated
dry, arid
词性:形容词

drenched:/drentʃt/
verb
使湿透
To make someone or something completely wet.
The sudden storm drenched us to the bone. 突如其来的暴风雨把我们淋得湿透了。
soaked, saturated
dry
词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *drenchen*, from Old English *drencan* (“to make drink, give to drink, drench, drown”), from Proto-Germanic *drinkaną* (“to drink, give to drink”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrénǵʰeti* (“to drink”). Cognate with Dutch *drenken* (“to water, soak”), German *tränken* (“to water, soak, irrigate”). Related to drink.
