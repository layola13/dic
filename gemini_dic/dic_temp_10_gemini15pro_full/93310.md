ill-equipped:/ˌɪlɪˈkwɪpt/
adjective
装备不良的，准备不足的
lacking the necessary tools, skills, or knowledge to do something effectively
The expedition was ill-equipped for such a challenging climb.  这支探险队装备不足，无法应对如此具有挑战性的攀登。
The school is ill-equipped to deal with students with special needs. 这所学校没有充分的准备来应对有特殊需求的学生。
近义词: unprepared, unequipped, unfitted, inadequate
反义词: well-equipped, prepared, ready, competent
词性: adjective
