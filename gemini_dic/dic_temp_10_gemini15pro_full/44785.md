crunches:/ˈkrʌntʃɪz/| n. |仰卧起坐|An exercise in which you lie on your back with your knees bent and raise your upper body.|She does 50 crunches every morning. 她每天早上做50个仰卧起坐。|Synonyms: situps, abdominal exercises|Antonyms: rests, relaxation|noun

crunch:/krʌntʃ/| n. |嘎吱声，紧要关头|A sound of something being crushed or squeezed, or a difficult situation.|The snow crunched under our feet. 雪在我们脚下嘎吱作响。|Synonyms: crush, grind, crisis|Antonyms: silence, calm|noun
crunch:/krʌntʃ/| v. |嘎吱作响，处理数据|To crush or squeeze something so that it makes a noise, or to process data.|The car crunched into the wall. 汽车嘎吱一声撞上了墙。|Synonyms: crush, grind, process|Antonyms: smooth, quiet|verb
