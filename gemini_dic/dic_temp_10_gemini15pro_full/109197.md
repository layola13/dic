legume:/ˈleɡjuːm/ /ˈlɛdʒuːm/| n. |豆科植物；豆类；豆荚|A plant of the pea family (such as peas or beans) having pods that split open when ripe, or the pods or seeds of such plants. |例句：Lentils, peas, and beans are all legumes.  扁豆、豌豆和豆类都是豆科植物。

例句：Legumes are a good source of protein. 豆类是良好的蛋白质来源。

近义词：pulse, bean, pea|反义词:|词性:noun

etymology->
From French *légume*, from Latin *legūmen* (“pulse, bean”), related to *legō* (“to gather, collect”).
