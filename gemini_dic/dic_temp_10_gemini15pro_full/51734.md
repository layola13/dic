devoid:/dɪˈvɔɪd/
adjective
缺乏的，空的
Entirely lacking or free from.
The house was devoid of furniture. 这房子里没有任何家具。
devoid of human warmth 缺乏人情味

近义词:lacking, empty, barren, bereft, destitute
反义词:full, replete, abundant, rich

词性:adjective

词源: From Old French *desvoidier, from des- "out, from" + voidier "to empty".
