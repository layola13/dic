over-genialities: /ˌoʊvərˌdʒiːniˈælɪtiz/ | n. | 过分的热情，过度的友好 | Excessive geniality; an instance of this.  This word refers to instances of friendliness that are perceived as excessive, insincere, or even unsettling. | His over-genialities made her uncomfortable. 他的过分热情让她感到不舒服。| The salesman's over-genialities felt forced and insincere.  这个推销员过度的热情让人感觉很假。 | 近义词: effusiveness, exuberance, unctuousness | 反义词: coldness, aloofness, reserve | 词性: 名词

词源:
From 'over-' (prefix indicating excess) + 'geniality' (from Latin 'genialis' meaning 'festive, cheerful, of or belonging to marriage').  The plural form indicates multiple instances of this excessive geniality.
