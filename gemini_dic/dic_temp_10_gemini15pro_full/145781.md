overdazzle: /ˌoʊvərˈdæzəl/ | vt. | 使目眩，使眼花缭乱 | To dazzle excessively; to overwhelm with brilliance or display. | The fireworks overdazzled the children.  烟花使孩子们眼花缭乱。| 近义词: blind, bewilder, overwhelm | 反义词: subdue, underwhelm | 词性: 动词

overdazzle: /ˌoʊvərˈdæzəl/ |  | 使目眩，使眼花缭乱 | To dazzle excessively; to overwhelm with brilliance or display. | The sheer opulence of the palace overdazzled the visitors. 宫殿的富丽堂皇使参观者眼花缭乱。| 近义词: bedazzle, stun | 反义词:  dim, darken | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "dazzle" (from Old English *dæsel, "to become foolish").
