mentorial:/mɛnˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
导师的;指导的
Relating to or characteristic of a mentor.  Serving as a mentor.
She offered me some mentorial advice on my career path. 她就我的职业道路给我提供了一些导师般的建议。
The professor played a mentorial role in his student's life.  这位教授在他的学生生活中扮演了导师的角色。
近义词:  guiding, advisory, supportive, nurturing
反义词:  unsupportive, neglectful
词性: adjective
