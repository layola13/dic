officiating: /əˈfɪʃieɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
主持；担任裁判；执行公务
acting as an official in charge of something, especially a sporting event.
The referee was officiating at the game.  裁判正在比赛中担任裁判。
He officiated at their wedding. 他主持了他们的婚礼。
近义词: refereeing, judging, umpiring, presiding, administering
反义词:  None (The action of officiating doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: verb (present participle)


officiate: /əˈfɪʃieɪt/
verb
主持；担任裁判；执行公务
act as an official in charge of something, especially a sporting event.
Who will officiate at the wedding? 谁将主持婚礼？
A local priest officiated. 一位当地牧师主持了仪式。
近义词: referee, judge, umpire, preside, administer
反义词: None (The action of officiating doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: verb

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin officiatus, past participle of officiare ‘perform a duty’, from officium ‘duty’ (see office).
