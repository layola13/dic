fainthearted:/ˈfeɪntˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|胆小的，怯懦的|Lacking courage; timid.|He was too fainthearted to ask her out. 他太胆小了，不敢约她出去。|近义词:timid, cowardly, fearful, pusillanimous|反义词:brave, courageous, bold, daring|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "faint" (weak, lacking strength) + "hearted" (having a specified kind of heart or disposition).  The word literally means having a weak heart, in the sense of lacking courage.
