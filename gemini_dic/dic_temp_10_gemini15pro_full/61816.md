empir:/ˈɛmpaɪr/ |n.| 经验，经验主义 | based on, concerned with, or verifiable by observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic |  例句：Empiric evidence suggests that regular exercise is beneficial for health. 经验表明，经常锻炼对健康有益。|例句：The scientist conducted an empiric study to test his hypothesis.  这位科学家进行了一项以检验其假设的经验性研究。| 近义词：experimental, practical, observed | 反义词：theoretical, hypothetical | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *empiricus, from Ancient Greek ἐμπειρικός (empeirikós, “experienced”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + πεῖρα (peîra, “trial, experiment”).
