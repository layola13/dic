crackpot:/ˈkrækˌpɒt/ (UK) /ˈkrækˌpɑːt/ (US)| n. |怪人，疯子；不切实际的人|A person who is considered to be eccentric, unrealistic, or mentally unstable.|He dismissed them as a bunch of crackpots. 他把他们斥为一群疯子。|Synonyms: eccentric, lunatic, madman, oddball, kook, nutcase, nutjob|Antonyms: sane person, rational person|Noun

crackpot:/ˈkrækˌpɒt/ (UK) /ˈkrækˌpɑːt/ (US)| adj. |古怪的，疯狂的|Relating to or characteristic of a crackpot.|He had some crackpot idea about turning lead into gold. 他有一些关于把铅变成金的疯狂想法。|Synonyms: eccentric, bizarre, crazy, foolish, unrealistic|Antonyms: sane, rational, sensible|Adjective
