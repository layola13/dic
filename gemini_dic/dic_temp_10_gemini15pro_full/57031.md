doublespeak:/ˈdʌbəlˌspiːk/| n.|双重语言，模棱两可的话，故意含糊其辞|Language deliberately used to disguise, distort, or reverse the meaning of words, often used by governments or other institutions.|The government engaged in doublespeak when they called the invasion a "liberation." 政府将入侵称为“解放”，这是在使用双重语言。|近义词：equivocation, euphemism, jargon, gobbledygook|反义词：plain speaking, straightforwardness|词性:名词

doublespeaks:/ˈdʌbəlˌspiːks/| n.|双重语言（复数）|The plural form of "doublespeak," referring to multiple instances of this kind of language. |The politician's doublespeaks made it difficult to understand their true intentions.  这位政客的双重语言使人们难以理解他们的真实意图。|近义词：equivocations, euphemisms, jargons|反义词：plain speaking, straightforwardness|词性:名词

词源：double + speak.  Double指的是“双重的”，speak指的是“说话”。 合在一起表示具有双重含义的语言，即模棱两可、含糊其辞的语言。  这个词在20世纪中期开始流行，用来批评政治和企业中使用的欺骗性语言。
