blazer:/ˈbleɪzər/| n. |休闲西装，运动夹克|A type of jacket, often worn as part of a uniform, or as part of semi-formal attire.  It resembles a suit jacket but is usually less formal. |He wore a navy blue blazer and khaki pants. 他穿着一件海军蓝休闲西装和卡其裤。|
|近义词：jacket, sports coat|反义词: N/A|noun|

blazer:/ˈbleɪzər/| n. |(尤指开拓性的)领路人，带头人|A person who is a pioneer or trailblazer in a particular field.  Less common than the clothing definition.|She was a blazer in the field of women's rights. 她是女权领域的先驱。|
|近义词：pioneer, trailblazer, innovator|反义词: follower|noun|
