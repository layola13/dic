gabbing:/ˈɡæbɪŋ/
n.|闲聊，喋喋不休|The constant gabbing of the girls annoyed him. 女孩子们不停的闲聊让他很烦。|近义词：chatting, gossiping, prattling|反义词：silence, taciturnity|词性:名词
gab:/ɡæb/
v.|闲聊，喋喋不休|They gabbed on the phone for hours. 他们在电话里聊了几个小时。|近义词：chat, gossip, prattle|反义词：be silent, be quiet|词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *gabben, of uncertain origin; perhaps related to Old Norse gabba (“to mock, scoff”), or from a拟声词.
