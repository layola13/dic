ambusher's:/ˈæmbʊʃərz/
noun
埋伏者的人或物的
belonging to or associated with someone who ambushes.
The ambusher's rifle was found abandoned near the scene. 埋伏者的步枪在现场附近被发现遗弃。
synonyms: attacker's, assailant's, waylayer's
antonyms: defender's, protector's
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From ambush + -er + -'s.  Ambush comes from Old French *embuschier, from en- "in" + busche "wood."
