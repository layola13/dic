hatcheler:/ˈhætʃələr/ | n. |梳麻工；梳理者；分裂者|A person who hatchels flax or hemp; one who separates or splits. | The hatcheler skillfully separated the flax fibers. 梳麻工熟练地分离亚麻纤维。|  The machine acts as a hatcheler, separating the different materials. 这台机器充当梳理机，分离不同的材料。| Synonyms: comber, carder | Antonyms: combiner, joiner | noun
