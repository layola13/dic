narrows:/ˈnæroʊz/ | n. | 狭窄的地方，窄路；海峡 | A narrow passage or channel; a strait. | The boat navigated the treacherous narrows. 这艘船航行过危险的海峡。 | 近义词: straits, channels, passages | 反义词: wides, expanses | 词性: 名词

narrowses:  This is not a standard English word.  "Narrows" is already plural.  You would not typically add an additional "es".  If you absolutely had to pluralize it further to indicate multiple sets of narrows, you could say "sets of narrows" or "groups of narrows".

词源: From the Old English "nearu," meaning "narrow," related to the German "eng."
