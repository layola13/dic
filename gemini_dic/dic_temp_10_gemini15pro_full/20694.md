bladeless:/ˈbleɪdləs/| adj.|无叶片的|Without a blade or blades; having no blades.  Relating to or denoting a device, such as a fan or turbine, that operates without blades.|This bladeless fan is very quiet. 这台无叶片风扇很安静。|The bladeless turbine is a new technology.  这种无叶涡轮机是一项新技术。|blade-free, bladeless turbine design|bladed|adjective
词源:  blade + -less.  "Blade" comes from Old English *blæd, from Proto-Germanic *bladam. "-less" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "without," from Old English -lēas.
