insconce:/ɪnˈskɑːns/ /ɪnˈskɒns/| vt.|安置；使…舒适地坐下；牢固地放置|To settle securely or snugly; to place or fix firmly.|He insconced himself in the armchair by the fire. 他在壁炉旁的扶手椅上舒服地坐了下来。|近义词：settle, nestle, establish, ensconce|反义词：dislodge, remove, displace|Verb

insconce:/ɪnˈskɑːns/ /ɪnˈskɒns/| n.|壁灯；壁式烛台|A bracket on a wall for holding candles or other lights.|A decorative insconce flanked the doorway.  一盏装饰性的壁灯位于门口的两侧。|近义词：sconce, wall bracket, wall light|反义词：-|Noun
