closest:/ˈkloʊsɪst/ | adj. | 最近的；最亲密的 |  nearest in space, time, or relationship. | Example: This is the closest hospital to our house. 这是离我们家最近的医院。| Example: She is my closest friend. 她是我最亲密的朋友。 |Synonyms: nearest, next, intimate | Antonyms: furthest, farthest, distant | adjective
