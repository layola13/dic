lidar:/ˈlaɪdɑːr/ (US), /ˈlaɪdɑː/ (UK)| n.|激光雷达|A surveying method that measures distance to a target by illuminating the target with pulsed laser light and measuring the reflected pulses with a sensor.  It's an acronym of "Light Detection and Ranging".|例句：The researchers used lidar to create a 3D map of the forest. 研究人员使用激光雷达创建了森林的3D地图。

例句：Lidar technology is becoming increasingly important in autonomous vehicles. 激光雷达技术在自动驾驶汽车中变得越来越重要。|近义词：laser scanning,  3D scanning|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
