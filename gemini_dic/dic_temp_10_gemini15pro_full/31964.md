chemiloon: /ˌkɛmɪˈluːn/
noun
化学气球（一种用于进行化学实验的气球）
A balloon specifically designed for chemical experiments, often made of a material resistant to certain chemicals.
Chemiloons are often used in demonstrations to show the effects of different gases. 化学气球通常用于演示不同气体的作用。
The students inflated the chemiloon with hydrogen gas. 学生们用氢气给化学气球充气。
近义词: balloon, laboratory balloon
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider something that contains chemicals but isn't a balloon, like a beaker or flask)
词性: n.
