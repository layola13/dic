paradigmatic:/ˌpærədɪɡˈmætɪk/| adj.|范例的；典范的；词范式的|Relating to or exemplifying a paradigm; serving as a model or example.|The discovery of penicillin was a paradigmatic moment in medical history.  青霉素的发现是医学史上的一个范例时刻。| The "cat sat on the mat" sentence is paradigmatic in linguistics. “猫坐在垫子上”这句话在语言学中是典范的。|exemplary, typical, model, archetypal|anomalous, atypical, irregular|adjective
