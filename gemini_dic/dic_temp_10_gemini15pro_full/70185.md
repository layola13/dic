fibrillous:/ˈfɪbrɪləs/ /faɪˈbrɪləs/ | adj. | 纤维状的；由细丝组成的 | Composed of or containing small fibers or fibrils. | The plant has fibrillous roots.  这种植物有纤维状的根。|  The doctor examined the fibrillous tissue under the microscope. 医生在显微镜下检查了纤维状组织。 | 近义词: fibrous, filamentous, threadlike | 反义词: compact, dense | 词性: 形容词

fibril:/ˈfaɪbrɪl/ /ˈfɪbrəl/ | n. | 细纤维；原纤维 | A small or slender fiber. |  Muscle fibers are made up of smaller fibrils. 肌肉纤维由更小的细纤维组成。| Collagen fibrils provide structural support to tissues. 胶原纤维为组织提供结构支撑。| 近义词: fiber, filament, thread | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "mass" or "bulk" in certain contexts) | 词性: 名词
