carbonators:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtərz/
noun
碳酸化器; 碳酸饱和器
A device that adds carbon dioxide to a liquid, typically water, to make it carbonated.
The bartender used a soda carbonator to make fizzy drinks.  调酒师用苏打碳酸化器制作碳酸饮料。
The home carbonator allows you to make sparkling water on demand. 家用碳酸化器可让您随时制作苏打水。
synonyms: carbonation machines, soda makers
antonyms: decarbonators
