angelolatry: /ˌeɪndʒəˈlɒlətri/ | n. | 天使崇拜；天使崇敬 | The worship or veneration of angels. |  Some people see angelolatry as a deviation from true worship of God.  有些人认为天使崇拜是对真正敬拜上帝的一种偏离。 | 近义词: angel worship | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *angelolatria, from Greek aggelos (angel) + -latria (worship).
