hydrochloric:/ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːrɪk/ |adj.| 盐酸的；氯化的| relating to or containing hydrochloric acid |Hydrochloric acid is a strong acid. 盐酸是一种强酸。|近义词: muriatic (尤指工业用盐酸)|反义词: alkaline, basic|词性: adjective

Etymology->
From hydro- + chloric (from chlorine). "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water), and "chloric" ultimately derives from Greek "khlōros" (greenish-yellow), referring to the color of chlorine gas.
