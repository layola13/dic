atheistical:/ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪk(ə)l/
adj.
无神论的
Disbelieving or denying the existence of God or gods.
His atheistical views were not popular in the small town. 他在小镇上的无神论观点并不受欢迎。
近义词：godless, unbelieving, irreligious, agnostic
反义词：religious, devout, pious, believing
词性: adjective

词源:
From French *athéistique*, from Greek *atheos* "without god" + -istic.
