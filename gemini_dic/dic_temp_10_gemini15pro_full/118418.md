merry-go-round:/ˈmeri ɡoʊ ˌraʊnd/| n.|旋转木马|A revolving circular platform with seats, often in the form of animals, on which people, especially children, ride for amusement.  It is also sometimes referred to as a carousel.|The children were delighted with the merry-go-round at the fair. 孩子们对游乐场里的旋转木马感到很高兴。

The brightly painted merry-go-round played cheerful music.  色彩鲜艳的旋转木马播放着欢快的音乐。
