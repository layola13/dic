dusters:/ˈdʌstərz/
n.
掸子；除尘器；防尘罩；防尘服
Tools or clothing used for removing dust.
She used dusters to clean the shelves. 她用掸子清洁架子。
The car was covered in dusters to protect it. 汽车上覆盖着防尘罩以保护它。
synonyms: dust cloths, dustpans and brushes, cleaning cloths, feather dusters, dust mops
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a cleaning tool, but conceptually) dirt, grime, filth
noun
