hymenium:/haɪˈmiːniəm/
n.
子实层
The fertile, spore-bearing layer of a fungal fruiting body.
The hymenium of the mushroom was covered in spores.  蘑菇的子实层上覆盖着孢子。
近义词: sporophore
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider sterile tissue of the fungus)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin, from Greek *hymēnion, diminutive of hymēn (“membrane”)
