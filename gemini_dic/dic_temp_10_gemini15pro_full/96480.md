individuative:/ɪnˌdɪvɪˈdʒuːətɪv/
adj.
赋予个性的，个体化的
Making or tending to make individual; distinguishing individual things from others.
His individuative style of painting set him apart from other artists. 他独特的绘画风格使他与其他艺术家区别开来。

近义词: distinctive, characteristic, peculiar, individualizing
反义词: collective, general, common

词性: adjective
