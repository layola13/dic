gruff:/ɡrʌf/ | adj. | 粗暴的，粗声的 |  (of a voice) rough and low in pitch. |  "He answered in a gruff voice." 他用粗哑的声音回答。 | 近义词: hoarse, husky, rough | 反义词: gentle, smooth, soft |词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From Middle English *gruffen*, probably imitative.  Related to Dutch *grof* (“coarse”).
