crave:/kreɪv/| v.|渴望，热望|To have a strong desire for something.|She craves chocolate. 她渴望巧克力。| I crave a quiet evening at home. 我渴望在家度过一个安静的夜晚。|近义词：desire, yearn for, long for|反义词:abhor, detest|动词

Etymology->
From Old English *crafiġan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *krabōną (“to crave, demand”), from Proto-Indo-European *gerebh- (“to scratch, scrape, dig”). Cognate with Old High German kerben (“to notch, carve”), Old Norse krefa (“to demand”), Gothic ⴽְר𐌰𐌱𐌾𐌰𐌽 (krabjan, “to demand”).
