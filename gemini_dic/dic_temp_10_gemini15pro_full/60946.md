eleven-plus:/ɪˈlevən ˈplʌs/| n.|十一年级考试; 小升初考试|An examination taken by some pupils in England and Northern Ireland at the age of 10 or 11, used to select those who will go to grammar schools.|Many children prepare for the eleven-plus exam for years. 很多孩子多年来一直在准备十一年级考试。|The eleven-plus exam determines which secondary school a child will attend. 十一年级考试决定了孩子将就读哪所中学。|synonyms: 11+, transfer test|antonyms: N/A|noun
