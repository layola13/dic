fleecier:/ˈfliːsiər/| adj.|更羊毛似的，更柔软的|Comparative form of fleecy: more fleecy.  Covered with, or resembling, fleece; woolly; soft and fluffy.|The blanket was so fleecier than the one I have at home.|近义词：softer, fluffier, woollier|反义词:coarser, rougher|词性:形容词
Etymology: From fleece + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *flys, from Proto-Germanic *fleusą.
