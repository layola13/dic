bushy:/ˈbʊʃi/| adj.|浓密的，茂密的|Covered with bushes; resembling a bush; thick and spreading.|例句：The squirrel hid in the bushy tail of the fox. 松鼠藏在狐狸浓密的尾巴里。|近义词：thick, dense, lush, full|反义词:sparse, thin, bare|adjective

bushy:/ˈbʊʃi/| adj.|多毛的，毛茸茸的|Having a lot of hair or fur.|例句：He had a bushy beard that covered most of his face. 他留着浓密的胡须，遮住了大半张脸。|近义词：hairy, furry, shaggy|反义词:bald, smooth|adjective
