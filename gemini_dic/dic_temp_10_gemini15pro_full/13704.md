attritional:/əˈtrɪʃənəl/
adjective
消耗性的, 损耗性的
Characterized by or causing attrition; gradual wearing down or weakening.
The attritional warfare of the trenches took a heavy toll on both sides.  堑壕战的消耗战给双方都造成了巨大的伤亡。
The company faced attritional losses in its workforce due to low salaries.  由于工资低，该公司面临着员工的逐渐流失。

近义词:  grinding, wearing, depleting, exhausting
反义词:  strengthening, invigorating, bolstering
词性: adjective
