improve:/ɪmˈpruːv/ (US) /ɪmˈpruːv/ (UK)| v. |改善，改进，提高|to make or become better|例句：Her English has improved greatly. 她的英语进步很大。|例句：The company's performance has improved significantly this year. 公司今年的业绩显著提高。|近义词：enhance, better, upgrade, develop, refine|反义词：worsen, deteriorate, decline|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *emprover, based on Late Latin *probare "to improve, test," itself from Latin probus "good."
