didynamous: /ˌdaɪdɪˈneɪməs/ /ˌdɪdɪˈneɪməs/
| adj. | 二强雄蕊的 | Having four stamens, two of which are long and two short. |  The flowers of the mint family are characteristically didynamous.  薄荷科植物的花的典型特征是二强雄蕊。| Near synonyms: tetradynamous (specifically six stamens, four long and two short) | Antonyms:  isodynamous (stamens of equal length)| adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *di-* (two) + *dynamis* (power), referring to the two different lengths of stamens.
