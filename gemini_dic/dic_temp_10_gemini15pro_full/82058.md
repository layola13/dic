gritless:/ˈɡrɪtləs/
adjective
没有毅力的，缺乏勇气的
lacking grit; without courage or resolve.
He gave up easily, proving himself to be a gritless individual. 他轻易放弃了，证明自己是个缺乏毅力的人。
spineless, cowardly, weak
gritty, determined, resolute
词性: 形容词
