blurting:/ˈblɜːrtɪŋ/
n.
脱口而出；突然说出
the act of saying something suddenly and without thinking
He regretted his blurting out of the secret. 他后悔自己脱口说出了秘密。
近义词：uttering, exclaiming, divulging
反义词：concealing, suppressing


blurt:/blɜːrt/
v.
脱口而出；突然说出
to say something suddenly and without thinking
She blurted out the truth before she could stop herself. 她还没来得及阻止自己就脱口说出了真相。
近义词：utter, exclaim, divulge
反义词：conceal, suppress
