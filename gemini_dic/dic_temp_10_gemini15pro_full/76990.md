gardeners:/ˈɡɑːrdnərz/ (US), /ˈɡɑːdənəz/ (UK)
n.
园丁，花匠
People who cultivate or tend gardens.
The gardeners planted roses in the garden. 园丁们在花园里种了玫瑰。

The head gardener is responsible for the entire estate's landscaping.  总园丁负责整个庄园的景观美化。

近义词:horticulturists, cultivators
反义词: N/A
词性:Noun
