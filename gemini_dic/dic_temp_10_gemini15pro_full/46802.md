danishry:/ˈdeɪnɪʃri/ | n. | 丹麦糕点（一种用大量黄油和鸡蛋制成的糕点）| A type of rich pastry made with lots of butter and eggs, often associated with Danish cuisine.  |  I love to have a danishry with my coffee in the morning. 我喜欢早上喝咖啡配丹麦糕点。 | synonyms: Danish pastry, Viennoiserie | antonyms: N/A | noun


danishry:/ˈdeɪnɪʃri/ | adj. | 丹麦糕点风格的；像丹麦糕点的 | Resembling or characteristic of Danish pastries; rich, buttery, and flaky. | The bakery offered a danishry croissant, a delightful hybrid. 这家面包店提供一种丹麦糕点风格的可颂，美味的混搭。 | synonyms: flaky, buttery, rich | antonyms: dry, plain, bland | adjective
