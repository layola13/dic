lusk:/lʌsk/
| vi. n.| 潜伏；躲藏；闲荡；懒散；(尤指动物) 隐蔽处；潜伏期 |
| To lie hidden or concealed, especially in ambush; to shirk work or duty; to loiter. |
| The leopard lusked in the tall grass, waiting for its prey. 豹子潜伏在高高的草丛中，等待着猎物。|
|近义词：lurk, skulk, loiter, idle, malinger |
|反义词：work, toil, hustle |
|词性：verb, intransitive; noun |
|词源：From Middle English *lusken, from Old Norse *loska (“to lie in wait, lurk”), from Proto-Germanic *luskōną (“to lie hidden, lurk”), from Proto-Indo-European *leuk- (“to bend, turn, twist”).|
