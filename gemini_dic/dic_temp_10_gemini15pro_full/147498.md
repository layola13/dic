overoxidizes:/ˌoʊvərˈɒksɪdaɪzɪz/
verb
过度氧化
To oxidize excessively; to combine or become combined chemically with oxygen beyond a desired or normal degree.
The iron overoxidized in the humid environment, creating a thick layer of rust.  铁在潮湿的环境中过度氧化，形成了一层厚厚的锈。
The chemist carefully monitored the reaction to prevent the compound from overoxidizing.  化学家仔细监测反应，以防止化合物过度氧化。
Synonyms: burn, corrode
Antonyms: reduce, deoxidize
词性: 动词
