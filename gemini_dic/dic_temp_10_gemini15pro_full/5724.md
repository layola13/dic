alveolar:/ælˈviːələr/ | adj. n.| 肺泡的；齿槽的 |Relating to or resembling alveoli (small air sacs in the lungs) or the alveolus (the bony socket of a tooth).| The alveolar process holds the roots of the teeth. 齿槽突固定着牙根。|  近义词:pulmonary (for lung alveoli), dental (for tooth sockets) | 反义词: None |词性: adjective, noun

alveolus:/ælˈviːələs/ | n. | 肺泡；齿槽 |A small air sac in the lungs where the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide takes place;  the bony socket of a tooth.|  Oxygen diffuses into the blood from the alveoli. 氧气从肺泡扩散到血液中。| 近义词: air sac (for lung alveoli), tooth socket | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
