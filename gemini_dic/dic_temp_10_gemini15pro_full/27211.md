callow:/ˈkæloʊ/| adj.|稚嫩的，不成熟的|Lacking adult sophistication or experience; immature.|例句：The callow youth made a series of foolish mistakes.  这个幼稚的年轻人犯了一系列愚蠢的错误。|例句：He was just a callow boy when he joined the army. 他参军时还是个乳臭未干的小伙子。|近义词：immature, inexperienced, naive, green|反义词：mature, experienced, sophisticated|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *calu "bald, bare," from Old English calu "bald."  The sense transferred from "bald" (like a young bird) to "immature."
