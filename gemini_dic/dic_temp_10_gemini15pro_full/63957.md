episcopize:/ɪˈpɪskəˌpaɪz/| v. |监督；控制；以主教身份统治|To act as a bishop; to oversee or control in a manner resembling a bishop.  It can also mean to introduce or impose episcopal church government.|例句：He attempted to episcopize the newly formed church. 他试图监督新成立的教会。

例句：The new leader episcopized the organization with an iron fist.  这位新领导者以铁腕控制着这个组织。|近义词：supervise, oversee, control, dominate, govern|反义词：follow, obey, submit|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *episcopizare*, from Latin episcopus ("bishop") + -izare, equivalent to English episcopal + -ize.  The Latin "episcopus" itself comes from Greek ἐπίσκοπος (epískopos), meaning "overseer", from ἐπί (epí, "upon, over") + σκοπός (skopós, "watcher, guard").
