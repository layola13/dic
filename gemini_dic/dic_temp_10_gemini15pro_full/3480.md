aftereffect:/ˈæftərɪˌfekt/| n.|后遗症，副作用，余波|An aftereffect is a change that continues to happen to a person or thing after the event or action that caused it.|The aftereffects of the storm were devastating.  风暴的后遗症是毁灭性的。|The medication had some unpleasant aftereffects.  这种药物有一些令人不快的副作用。|Synonyms: consequence, result, repercussion, aftermath|Antonyms: cause, origin, source|noun
