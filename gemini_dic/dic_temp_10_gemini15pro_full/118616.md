messengers:/ˈmesɪndʒərz/| n. |信使；使者；报信者|People who carry messages or information from one person or place to another. |The king sent messengers to all parts of the kingdom. 国王派遣信使到王国各地。

The company uses bicycle messengers for quick deliveries in the city. 这家公司使用自行车信使在市内快速送货。|近义词：couriers, envoys, heralds, emissaries|反义词: recipient, receiver|noun
