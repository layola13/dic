hyperacutely:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkjutli/| adv.|极度地，极其剧烈地|Characterized by extreme acuteness or severity, especially in relation to a medical condition.  It describes something happening very suddenly and intensely.|The patient reacted hyperacutely to the medication. 病人对药物的反应极其剧烈。|Synonyms: extremely, intensely, severely, acutely|Antonyms: mildly, slightly, moderately|Adverb
