cross-questions:/ˈkrɒsˌkwɛstʃənz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsˌkwɛstʃənz/ (US)| n.|交叉提问，盘问|The lawyer subjected the witness to rigorous cross-questions. 律师对证人进行了严格的交叉盘问。|Example: The detective used cross-questions to expose the inconsistencies in the suspect's story.  侦探用交叉提问揭露了嫌疑人故事中的前后矛盾之处。

Example:  During the debate, the candidates engaged in a series of pointed cross-questions.  在辩论中，候选人进行了一系列尖锐的交叉提问。|近义词：cross-examination, interrogation, questioning|反义词: statement, testimony |词性:名词
