oversceptical:/ˈəʊvəˈskeptɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于怀疑的，过度怀疑的 | Excessively skeptical; inclined to doubt or disbelieve beyond what is reasonable or warranted.  | He was oversceptical about the new policy, refusing to believe it could work. 他对新政策过度怀疑，拒绝相信它能奏效。|  She's oversceptical about everything he says, even when he's telling the truth. 她对他所说的一切都过度怀疑，即使他说的是实话。|  synonym: cynical, distrustful, doubting, incredulous | antonym: credulous, gullible, trusting | adjective
