datas:/ˈdeɪtəz/ /ˈdætəz/ | n. | 数据，资料 | Facts and statistics collected together for reference or analysis. |

例句：The scientist analyzed the datas from the experiment.  科学家分析了实验数据。

例句：The datas show a clear upward trend in sales. 数据显示销售额明显呈上升趋势。


近义词：information, statistics, figures, facts | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


词源：From Latin *datum*, the neuter past participle of *dare* "to give". Originally a singular noun in Latin, but commonly used as a plural in English.
