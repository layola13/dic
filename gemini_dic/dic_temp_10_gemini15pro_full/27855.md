canonizer:/ˈkænəˌnaɪzər/| n.|册封圣徒者；圣经正典的编纂者|A person who declares someone a saint or compiles a canon of scriptures.|The Pope is the canonizer of saints in the Catholic Church.  教皇是天主教教会册封圣徒的人。|近义词:saint-maker|反义词:|(None -  The concept of "un-canonizing" doesn't neatly translate to a single antonym.)

canonizer:/ˈkænəˌnaɪzər/| n.|权威；经典作家；经典作品|A person who establishes or promotes something as authoritative or canonical.|Shakespeare is considered a canonizer of the English language.  莎士比亚被认为是英语的权威。|近义词:authority, expert|反义词:critic, detractor
