beleaguered:/bɪˈliːɡərd/
adjective
被围困的；饱受批评的；处于困境的
Surrounded and under attack; beset with difficulties.
The beleaguered city finally fell to the enemy. 被围困的城市最终落入了敌人手中。

The beleaguered CEO faced mounting pressure from investors.  饱受批评的首席执行官面临着来自投资者越来越大的压力。
besieged, troubled, harassed, under pressure, under siege
safe, secure, protected, unmolested
词性: 形容词
