non-intervention:/ˌnɒnɪntəˈvenʃən/ | n. |不干涉，不介入| The policy of non-intervention is often debated in international relations.  不干涉政策在国际关系中经常被讨论。|Example: The UN adopted a policy of non-intervention in the country's civil war. 联合国对该国内战采取了不干涉政策。
Example: Their non-intervention allowed the situation to escalate. 他们的不干涉导致局势升级。|Synonym: noninterference, neutrality |Antonym: intervention, interference | noun
