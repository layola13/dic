dodecane:/ˈdoʊdeɪkeɪn/ | n. | 十二烷 | A saturated aliphatic hydrocarbon with twelve carbon atoms, C₁₂H₂₆.  It's a colorless liquid, a component of kerosene and diesel fuel. | Dodecane is commonly used as a solvent. 十二烷通常用作溶剂。 | Dodecane is a major component of jet fuel. 十二烷是喷气燃料的主要成分。 | alkane, paraffin |  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound) | noun
