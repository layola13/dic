monographist:/məˈnɑːɡrəfɪst/ | n. | 专题论文作者；专著作家 | A person who writes a monograph (a detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it). |  She is a renowned monographist in the field of 18th-century English literature. 她是一位18世纪英国文学领域的著名专著作家。|  He is a respected monographist, known for his in-depth studies of specific historical events. 他是一位受人尊敬的专著作家，以其对特定历史事件的深入研究而闻名。|  Synonym: specialist, scholar, writer, author | Antonym: generalist | noun
