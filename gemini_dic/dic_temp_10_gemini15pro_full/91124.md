hybodont:/ˈhaɪbəˌdɑːnt/| adj. n.|弓鲛齿型的；弓鲛目成员|Relating to or denoting hybodonts, an extinct order of shark-like fishes with distinctive teeth.  Also, a member of the Hybodontiformes.|The hybodont teeth were well adapted for crushing shellfish.|近义词: (as a noun) elasmobranch, chondrichthyan|反义词: (no direct antonym, could consider modern shark groups)|词性: adjective, noun
