nemesis:/ˈneməsɪs/ /ˈnɛməsɪs/| n. |报应；天敌；劲敌；复仇女神| a source of harm or ruin; an opponent that cannot be beaten; (Greek mythology) the goddess of divine retribution and vengeance |例句：He finally met his nemesis in the championship match. 他终于在冠军赛中遇到了他的劲敌。

例句：The rabbit is a natural nemesis of the fox. 兔子是狐狸的天敌。

近义词：rival, opponent, adversary, foe, enemy, bane, scourge|反义词: ally, friend, supporter|词性: noun


nemeses:/ˈneməsiːz/ |n.| 报应；天敌；劲敌；复仇女神（nemesis的复数形式）| the plural form of nemesis|例句：Throughout history, many great leaders have been brought down by their nemeses. 纵观历史，许多伟大的领袖都被他们的劲敌打倒了。

例句：The company's nemeses are its main competitors in the market.  这家公司的天敌是它在市场上的主要竞争对手。
