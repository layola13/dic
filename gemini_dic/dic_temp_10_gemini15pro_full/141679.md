optometer:/ɒpˈtɒmɪtə(r)/ | n. | 视力计，验光仪 | An instrument for measuring the refractive power of the eye.  It helps determine whether corrective lenses are needed. | The optometrist used an optometer to assess my vision.  验光师用视力计评估我的视力。 |Synonyms: refractor, phoropter | Antonyms: None | Noun
