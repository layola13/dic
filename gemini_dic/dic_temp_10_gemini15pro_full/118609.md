messengers:/ˈmesɪndʒərz/| n.|信使；送信人；报信者|People used messengers to send important information before the invention of the telephone.  在电话发明之前，人们使用信使传递重要信息。| Messengers carried news between villages. 信使在村庄之间传递消息。|近义词：couriers, envoys, heralds, emissaries|反义词:recipient, receiver|noun
