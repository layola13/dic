incertitude:/ɪnˈsɜːrtɪtjuːd/ /ɪnˈsɜːrtətuːd/
n.
不确定性，犹豫，疑惑
a state of uncertainty or hesitation
例句：The incertitude of the situation made it difficult to plan.  情况的不确定性使规划变得困难。

例句：She expressed incertitude about her future career path. 她对未来的职业道路表达了不确定性。

近义词：uncertainty, doubt, hesitancy, ambiguity, vagueness
反义词：certainty, confidence, assurance, conviction
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incertitudo, from Latin incertus (“uncertain”) + -itudo (“-itude”).  Equivalent to in- + certitude.
