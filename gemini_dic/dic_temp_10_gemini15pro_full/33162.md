chromatoscope: /krəˈmætəˌskoʊp/ /krəˈmætəˌskəʊp/
noun
色度计，色谱仪
An instrument used for analyzing or displaying colors.  It can separate colors into their component wavelengths or analyze the color composition of a substance.
Chromatoscopes were popular in the Victorian era for creating colorful displays.  色度计在维多利亚时代很流行，用于制造彩色展示。
The scientist used a chromatoscope to analyze the pigment in the paint sample. 科学家用色度计分析了油漆样品中的色素。
Synonyms: colorimeter, spectrophotometer
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
