dastardly:/ˈdɑːstərdli/ (US) /ˈdɑːstədli/ (UK)| adj.| 卑鄙的，懦弱的|wicked and cowardly|例句：The dastardly villain tied the damsel to the railroad tracks.  卑鄙的恶棍把少女绑在铁轨上。

例句：He was accused of a dastardly attack on a defenseless old woman. 他被指控卑鄙地袭击了一位手无寸铁的老妇人。|近义词：cowardly, despicable, vile, shameful, villainous|反义词：noble, courageous, honorable, valiant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dastard*, from *dastardly* "cowardly," from Old French *dastard*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *dæstr*, meaning "exhausted, worn out."  The "-ly" suffix transforms the noun into an adjective.
