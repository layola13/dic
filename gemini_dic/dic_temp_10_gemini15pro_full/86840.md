hemelytrum:/ˈhɛmɪlaɪtrəm/| n. |半鞘翅; 前翅|The hemelytrum is the modified forewing of certain insects, especially of the order Hemiptera (true bugs).  半鞘翅是某些昆虫，特别是半翅目（真蝽）昆虫的前翅的变形。|The stink bug's hemelytrum is partially hardened. 椿象的半鞘翅部分硬化。|The scientist examined the hemelytrum under a microscope.  科学家在显微镜下检查了半鞘翅。|近义词:forewing, tegmen|反义词:hindwing|词性:noun
