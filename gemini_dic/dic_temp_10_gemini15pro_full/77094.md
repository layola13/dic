garroter:/ɡəˈrɒtər/ /ɡəˈrɑːtər/| n.|绞杀者；勒索者|A person who garrotes someone.  A person who extorts money from someone.|The garroter was apprehended by the police. 绞杀者被警察逮捕了。|Synonyms: strangler, choker, extortionist|Antonyms: rescuer, savior|noun

garrote:/ɡəˈrɒt/ /ɡəˈrɑːt/| n. vt.|绞索；勒杀；勒索|A method of execution or assassination by strangulation, typically with an iron collar or a length of wire or cord; to strangle with a garrote; to extort money from.|The victim was garroted with a piece of wire. 受害者被一根铁丝勒死了。|Synonyms: strangle, choke, extort|Antonyms: release, free|noun, verb
