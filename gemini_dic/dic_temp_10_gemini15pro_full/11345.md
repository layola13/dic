arenation: /ˌɑːrəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 角斗场化; 竞技场化 | The process of turning something into a competitive arena, often with a focus on conflict or spectacle.  | The arenation of politics has led to more focus on personal attacks than policy debates. 政治的竞技场化导致人们更加关注人身攻击而不是政策辩论。| 近义词: gladiatorialization, combatization | 反义词: cooperation, collaboration | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "arena" (a place of contest) + "-ation" (forming nouns of action or process).  "Arena" ultimately derives from Latin "harena" (sand), referring to the sand-covered floor of Roman amphitheaters.
