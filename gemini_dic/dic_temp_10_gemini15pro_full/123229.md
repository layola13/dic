monocotyledons:/ˌmɒnəʊˌkɒtɪˈliːdənz/ | n. | 单子叶植物 |  A class of flowering plants (angiosperms) characterized by having only one embryonic seed leaf, or cotyledon.  |  Examples of monocotyledons include grasses, lilies, and orchids. 单子叶植物的例子包括草、百合和兰花。 | synonyms: monocots | antonyms: dicotyledons, dicots | noun
