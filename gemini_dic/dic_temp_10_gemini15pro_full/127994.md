nephelite:/ˈnɛfəlaɪt/ | n. | 霞石 |A feldspathoid mineral, NaAlSiO4, occurring typically in igneous rocks deficient in silica. | Nephelite is a common mineral in some alkaline igneous rocks. 霞石在一些碱性火成岩中很常见。|Nephelite syenite is a common source of this mineral.霞石正长岩是这种矿物常见的来源。 |近义词:eleolite (a massive variety)|反义词:None|词性:noun

nepheline:/ˈnɛfəˌlin, ˈnɛfəlaɪn/ | n. | 霞石 |A synonym for nephelite.  | Nepheline syenite is used in ceramics. 霞石正长岩用于陶瓷。|Nepheline is a major constituent of some igneous rocks.霞石是一些火成岩的主要成分。 |近义词:nephelite, eleolite|反义词:None|词性:noun
