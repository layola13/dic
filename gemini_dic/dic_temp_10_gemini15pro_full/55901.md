divorces:/dɪˈvɔːrsɪz/
noun
离婚（复数）
The legal dissolution of a marriage.
Many divorces are caused by financial problems. 许多离婚是由经济问题引起的。
The rising divorce rate is a concern for sociologists.  不断上升的离婚率是社会学家关注的问题。

近义词: separations, dissolutions
反义词: marriages, unions
词性: noun


divorce:/dɪˈvɔːrs/
noun
离婚
The legal ending of a marriage.
They got a divorce after five years of marriage. 他们结婚五年后离婚了。
The divorce was finalized last month. 离婚手续上个月办完了。

近义词: separation, dissolution
反义词: marriage, union
词性: noun


divorce:/dɪˈvɔːrs/
verb
离婚；分离
To legally end a marriage.
They divorced after many years of unhappiness.  他们多年不幸福后离婚了。
She divorced him for adultery. 她因他通奸而与他离婚。

近义词: separate, split up
反义词: marry, wed
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *divorcer, from Latin divortium "divorce, separation," from divertere "to turn aside, separate," from dis- "apart" + vertere "to turn."
