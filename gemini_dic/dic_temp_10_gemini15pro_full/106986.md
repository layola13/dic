laconically:/ləˈkɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 简洁地，简而言之 |  In a concise or terse manner; using few words. | He answered laconically, "No." 他簡潔地回答：「不。」 | concisely, briefly, tersely, succinctly | verbosely, wordily, lengthily | adverb
