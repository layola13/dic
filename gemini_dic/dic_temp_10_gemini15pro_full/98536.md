instigation:/ˌɪnstɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n. |煽动，怂恿，教唆|The instigation of violence is a serious crime. 煽动暴力是一项严重的罪行。|例句：The riot was believed to be an instigation by a rival gang. 人们认为这场暴乱是由敌对帮派煽动的。

例句：His instigation led to a heated argument between the two parties. 他的煽动导致双方发生了激烈的争论。

近义词：incitement, encouragement, goading, prodding|反义词:dissuasion, deterrence, prevention|词性:名词
