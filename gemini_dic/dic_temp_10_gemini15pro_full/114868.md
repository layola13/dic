mammonish:/ˈmæmənɪʃ/
adjective
拜金的；贪婪的；唯利是图的
Characterized by a devotion to material wealth and possessions, especially in a way that is considered excessive or sinful.
He lived a mammonish lifestyle, obsessed with acquiring more and more wealth. 他过着拜金的生活方式，痴迷于积累越来越多的财富。
The mammonish banker cared little for the plight of his struggling customers.  唯利是图的银行家对苦苦挣扎的客户的困境漠不关心。
近义词: avaricious, greedy, materialistic, mercenary
反义词: generous, altruistic, charitable
词性: adjective
词源: From "Mammon," the Aramaic word for riches, personified in the New Testament as a false god.
