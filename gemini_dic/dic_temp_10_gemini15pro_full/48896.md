defraudments:/dɪˈfrɔːdmənts/
n.
欺诈行为（复数）
The act of illegally obtaining money from someone by deception.
Several defraudments have been reported in the last few months. 过去几个月里报道了几起欺诈案件。
synonyms: scams, swindles, cheats, deceptions, frauds
antonyms: honesty, integrity, probity
noun


defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/
vt.
欺骗，诈骗
To illegally obtain money from someone by deception.
He defrauded the company of thousands of dollars. 他诈骗了公司数千美元。
synonyms: cheat, swindle, deceive, trick
antonyms: be honest, compensate, reimburse
verb
