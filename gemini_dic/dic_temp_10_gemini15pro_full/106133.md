knittable:/ˈnɪtəbl/
adjective

Suitable for knitting.
This yarn is soft and knittable, perfect for a baby blanket.  这种纱线柔软且可编织，非常适合做婴儿毯。
The wool is very fine and knittable.  这羊毛很细，可以用来编织。
近义词:suitable for knitting
反义词:unknittable
