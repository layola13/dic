nonelucidation:/ˌnɒnɪˌluːsɪˈdeɪʃn/
n.
缺乏阐明;未解释;未澄清
lack of clarification or explanation; failure to elucidate.
The nonelucidation of the complex technical jargon left the audience confused.  复杂的专业术语没有得到解释，让观众感到困惑。
The witness's nonelucidation of the crucial details frustrated the investigators.  证人没有解释关键细节，这让调查人员感到沮丧。
近义词:obscurity, ambiguity, vagueness
反义词:clarification, explanation, elucidation
词性:名词


词源:
non- (prefix):  not, absence of  (来自拉丁语 non)
elucidation (noun):  explanation; clarification  (来自拉丁语 elucidare, meaning "to make clear")
