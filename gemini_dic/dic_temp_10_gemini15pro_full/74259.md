foxlike:/ˈfɒkslaɪk/ (US) /ˈfɒkslaɪk/ (UK)| adj.|像狐狸的；狡猾的|Resembling a fox in appearance or behavior; crafty or cunning.|例句：He had a foxlike face with a pointed chin and sly eyes. 他有一张像狐狸一样的脸，下巴尖尖的，眼睛狡猾。|近义词：cunning, sly, crafty, vulpine|反义词：honest, ingenuous, naive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *fox* + *-like*.  *Fox* comes from Old English *fox*, from Proto-Germanic *fuhsaz*. *-like* is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English *-līc*.
