crossers:/ˈkrɒsərz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsərz/ (US)| n.|穿越者，交叉者|People or things that cross something, such as a road, border, or river.|例句：The crossers braved the swift currents of the river.  穿越者勇敢地面对着湍急的河水。

例句：The border patrol apprehended several illegal crossers. 边境巡逻队逮捕了几名非法越境者。|近义词：travelers, commuters, migrants, pedestrians|反义词:residents, locals|词性:noun
