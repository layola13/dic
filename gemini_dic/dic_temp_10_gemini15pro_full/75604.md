fufu:/ˈfuːfuː/| n.| 芙芙（西非和中非的一种主食，由煮熟的淀粉类蔬菜（如山药、木薯或大蕉）捣碎制成）|A staple food in West and Central Africa, fufu is a dough-like dish made from boiled starchy vegetables such as yam, cassava, or plantain, which are pounded into a smooth consistency.|Fufu is often served with soup or stew. 芙芙通常与汤或炖菜一起食用。|Fufu is a popular dish in Ghana. 芙芙在加纳是一道很受欢迎的菜。|近义词：sadza (Zimbabwe), ugali (East Africa), posho (Kenya), pap (South Africa)|反义词: None|词性:名词
