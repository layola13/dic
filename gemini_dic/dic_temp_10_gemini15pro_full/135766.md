nonproficiency:/ˌnɒnprəˈfɪʃənsi/ /ˌnɑːnprəˈfɪʃənsi/| n.|不熟练，不精通|lack of proficiency or skill in something|His nonproficiency in French made it difficult for him to communicate in Paris. 他法语不熟练，这让他在巴黎很难沟通。|Example: Her nonproficiency in math was a barrier to her pursuing a career in engineering. 她数学不精通，这阻碍了她从事工程方面的工作。|Synonyms: incompetence, inability, inadequacy, lack of skill|Antonyms: proficiency, skill, expertise, mastery|noun
