homier:/ˈhoʊmiər/| adj.|更像家一样的，更舒适的，更亲切的|Feeling more comfortable and familiar, like being at home.  It suggests a sense of warmth, comfort, and belonging.|This new apartment feels much homier than the last one.  这套新公寓比上一套感觉更像家一样。|The atmosphere at the small cafe was homier than the larger, more impersonal chain restaurant. 这家小咖啡馆的氛围比大型、缺乏个性连锁餐厅更温馨。|近义词:cozier, more comfortable, more welcoming, more familiar|反义词:less welcoming, less comfortable, colder, more impersonal|词性:形容词
词源:From 'home' + '-y' + comparative '-er'.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *haimaz.
