noncontemptibility:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
不可轻视，不值得鄙视
The noncontemptibility of her work led to her promotion.  她工作的重要性导致她升职。
Her dedication and perseverance made her achievements noncontemptible. 她的奉献精神和毅力使她的成就不可轻视。
respectfulness, worthiness, value
contemptibility, insignificance, worthlessness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + contemptible (deserving contempt) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Contemptible comes from Old French contemptible, from Latin contemptibilis, from contemnere (to despise).
