buy-the-farm:/ˌbaɪ ðə ˈfɑːrm/ | v. | 死亡，阵亡 | To die, especially in battle or in an accident.  This is a euphemism. | Example 1: He bought the farm in a plane crash. 他在一次飞机失事中丧生了。

Example 2:  A lot of soldiers bought the farm in that last battle. 许多士兵在最后一场战斗中阵亡了。 | 近义词: die, perish, kick the bucket, pass away | 反义词: survive, live | 词性: 动词
