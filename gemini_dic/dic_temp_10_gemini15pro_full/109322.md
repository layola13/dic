lengtheners:/ˈlɛŋθənərz/
noun
延长器；加长物；增长器
Devices or methods used to increase the length of something.
Shoe lengtheners can add up to two inches to your height.  鞋增高器可以增加你高达两英寸的身高。
These hair lengtheners are very popular these days.  这些接发很流行。
synonyms:extenders, elongators
antonyms:shorteners, reducers
