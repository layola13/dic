convinces:/kənˈvɪnsɪz/| v.|说服，使确信|persuades someone to do something or believe something by giving them good reasons.|The salesman convinced me to buy the new phone.  销售员说服我买了新手机。|近义词：persuades, assures, satisfies|反义词：dissuades, fails to persuade|词性:动词
词源: From Old French convaincre, from Latin convincere "to overcome, convict, prove wrong," from com- "together" (see com-) + vincere "to conquer" (see victor).
