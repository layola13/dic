downtrends:/ˈdaʊnˌtrɛndz/
noun
下降趋势
A downward trend in business activity, prices, etc.
The stock market has been in a downtrend for the past six months. 股市在过去六个月里一直呈下降趋势。
declines, falls, decreases
rises, increases, uptrends
