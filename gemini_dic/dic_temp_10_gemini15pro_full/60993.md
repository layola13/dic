eligible:/ˈɛlɪdʒəbl/| adj.|合格的，有资格的|Meeting the requirements or qualifications for something; entitled to something.|She is eligible for a scholarship. 她有资格获得奖学金.|近义词：qualified, suitable, appropriate|反义词：ineligible, unqualified|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *eligible, from Latin eligibilis (“that may be chosen, desirable”), from eligere (“to choose out, elect”), from ex (“out”) + legere (“to choose, gather, read”).
