overhostility:/ˌoʊvərhɑːˈstɪləti/| n.|过度敌意，过度的敌对|Excessive hostility; an extreme level of animosity or antagonism.|His overhostility towards his colleagues made it difficult for him to work effectively. 他对同事的过度敌意使他难以有效地工作。|The lawyer's overhostility alienated the jury. 律师的过度敌意疏远了陪审团。|近义词：aggression, belligerence, animosity, antagonism|反义词：friendliness, amity, goodwill|词性:名词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "hostility" (noun from Latin "hostilitas," meaning "unfriendliness").
