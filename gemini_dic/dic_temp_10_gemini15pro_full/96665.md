industrials:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlz/ | adj. | 工业的 | Relating to industry or industries. | Example: The city has a strong industrial base.  这座城市拥有强大的工业基础。

Example:  Industrial pollution is a serious problem. 工业污染是一个严重的问题。| 近义词: manufacturing, factory | 反义词: agricultural, service | 词性: adjective


词源: From industry + -al (suffix forming adjectives) + -s (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin industria "diligence, activity."
