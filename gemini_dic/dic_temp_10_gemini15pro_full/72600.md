fly-out:/ˈflaɪ aʊt/ | n. | 飞行表演; (尤指政客的)短途出行; (从屏幕边缘)滑出的菜单 | A short trip, especially one taken by a politician or government official. Also, a menu or submenu that slides out from the edge of a computer screen. | The governor took a fly-out to meet with business leaders. 州长短途出行与商业领袖会面。| synonyms: trip, excursion, jaunt | antonyms: stay, arrival | noun

fly-out:/ˈflaɪ aʊt/ | adj. |  飞出去的; 滑出的 |  Relating to or characteristic of something that flies out or slides out, like a fly-out menu. |  The fly-out panel displayed additional options. 滑出面板显示了额外的选项。 |  synonyms: slide-out, pop-out | antonyms: slide-in, pop-in | adjective
