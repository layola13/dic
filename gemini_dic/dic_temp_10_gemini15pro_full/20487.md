bituminizes:/bɪˈtʃuːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
使沥青化; 用沥青处理
treats or coats with bitumen.  The process of applying or impregnating with bitumen.
The city council bituminizes the roads every year. 市议会每年都把道路沥青化。
They bituminized the driveway to make it smoother. 他们给车道铺上了沥青，使它更平整。
近义词: asphalt, tar
反义词: None
