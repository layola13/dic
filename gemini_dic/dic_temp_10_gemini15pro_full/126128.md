muticous: /ˈmjuːtɪkəs/
adjective
多粘液的; 含粘液的
Covered with or secreting mucus; slimy.
例句：The frog's skin was muticous and slippery.  青蛙的皮肤黏糊糊的，很滑。

例句：The muticous lining of the stomach protects it from digestive acids. 胃的粘膜层保护它免受消化酸的侵蚀。

近义词：slimy, viscous, mucousy
反义词：dry
