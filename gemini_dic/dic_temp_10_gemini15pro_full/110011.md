library: /ˈlaɪbrəri/ (US) /ˈlaɪbri/ (UK)| n.|图书馆；藏书；书房|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|例句：I often go to the library to study. 我经常去图书馆学习。

例句：He has a large library in his home. 他家里有一个很大的书房。|近义词：archive, collection|反义词: None|词性: noun
