nattering:/ˈnætərɪŋ/| adj.  vi. |喋喋不休的，唠叨的；闲聊|Chattering or gossiping constantly.|She was nattering on about her neighbours. 她一直在喋喋不休地谈论她的邻居们。|近义词：chatting, gossiping, prattling, babbling|反义词:silent, quiet|adjective, verb(intransitive)
natter:/ˈnætər/| vi. n.|闲聊，唠叨；饶舌|To talk casually, especially about unimportant matters.|They nattered away for hours. 他们闲聊了几个小时。|近义词：chat, gossip, prattle, babble|反义词:be quiet, be silent|verb(intransitive), noun

词源: From Middle English *knateren*, frequentative of *knaten* (“to chatter, talk idly”), of imitative origin.
