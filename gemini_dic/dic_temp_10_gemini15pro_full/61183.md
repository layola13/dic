eludes:/ɪˈluːdz/
verb
逃避，躲避
to escape the understanding, perception, or grasp of; to avoid or evade skillfully
The cause of the disease still eludes researchers.  这种疾病的病因仍然让研究人员难以捉摸。
近义词：evades, avoids, escapes
反义词：faces, confronts, meets
词性：v.

Etymology:
From Middle English *eluden, from Old French eluder, from Latin ēlūdere (“to elude, mock, deceive”), from ē- (“ex-”) + lūdere (“to play”).
