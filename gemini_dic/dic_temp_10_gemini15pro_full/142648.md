ostracism:/ˈɒstrəsɪzəm/ (US) /ˈɑːstrəsɪzəm/ (UK)|n.|排斥，放逐，孤立|Exclusion from a society or group.|He suffered years of ostracism after the scandal. 他在丑闻之后遭受了多年的排斥。|Synonyms: exclusion, banishment, isolation, shunning, blacklisting|Antonyms: inclusion, acceptance, integration|noun
