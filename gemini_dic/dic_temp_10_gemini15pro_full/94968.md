in terms of:/ɪn ˈtɜːrmz əv/ /ɪn ˈtɝːmz əv/| prep.|就…而言；在…方面；根据；用…的语言|Regarding; concerning; with respect to; expressed or measured by|例句：We need to think about this problem in terms of long-term solutions. 我们需要从长远解决方案的角度来思考这个问题。|近义词：regarding, concerning, with respect to, in relation to, as to, with regard to|反义词: N/A|词性:介词(preposition)

词源:  From the Latin "terminus" meaning "limit" or "boundary."  Originally referred to boundaries of land, then evolved to mean the limits or scope of a topic or discussion, eventually leading to the modern usage of "in terms of" to indicate a specific perspective or framework.
