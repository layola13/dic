anticonstitutionalism:/ˌæntɪˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪzəm/ |n.| 反宪政主义；违宪主义|  Opposition to or defiance of the principles of a constitution, especially a belief that a particular law, practice, or institution is in violation of the constitution. |  His anticonstitutionalism led him to challenge the legitimacy of the Supreme Court. 他的反宪政主义导致他质疑最高法院的合法性。

The group's anticonstitutionalism is a threat to democracy.  该组织的反宪政主义是对民主的威胁。

|近义词： unconstitutionality, illegality, lawlessness |反义词: constitutionalism, legality | 词性: noun
