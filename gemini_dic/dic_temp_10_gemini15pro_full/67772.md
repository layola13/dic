eyelash:/ˈaɪˌlæʃ/ /ˈaɪˌlæʃ/| n. |睫毛(单数)|A single hair that grows on the edge of the eyelid.|An eyelash fell into my eye. 一根睫毛掉进了我的眼睛里。|Near synonyms: lash|Antonym: None|Noun
