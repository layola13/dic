multichannel:/ˈmʌltiˌtʃænəl/ | adj. | 多通道的，多频道的 |  Having or using many channels, especially for sending or receiving electronic signals. |  This new television offers multichannel sound. 这款新电视提供多声道音效。 | The company launched a multichannel marketing campaign. 该公司开展了多渠道营销活动。 |  multifaceted, multimodal | single-channel | adjective
Etymology->
"multi-" + "channel". "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "Channel" comes from Old French "chanel" meaning "canal, channel".
