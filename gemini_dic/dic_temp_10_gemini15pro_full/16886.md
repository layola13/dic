basine:/ˈbeɪsɪn/| n.|盆；脸盆；流域|A wide, shallow container used for holding liquids, especially water for washing.|She filled the basine with warm water. 她把盆里装满了温水。|Example: The river basin covers a large area. 这条河流域覆盖了很大一片区域.|Synonyms: basin, bowl, washbowl|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object. Could consider something like "peak" or "summit" if referring to a geographical basin.)|noun
