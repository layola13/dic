asphyxiating: /əsˈfɪksiˌeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 窒息的，令人窒息的 | Causing or likely to cause asphyxia; suffocating.  Depriving of oxygen and thus causing unconsciousness or death. Also used figuratively to describe something overwhelmingly oppressive or stifling. | The fumes from the fire were asphyxiating. 大火产生的烟雾令人窒息。| 近义词: suffocating, choking, stifling, smothering | 反义词: invigorating, refreshing | 词性: 形容词


asphyxiate: /əsˈfɪksiˌeɪt/ | v. |  使窒息，闷死 | To kill or make unconscious by cutting off the supply of air. | The fire asphyxiated several people.  火灾使几个人窒息而死。| 近义词: suffocate, choke, stifle, smother | 反义词: resuscitate, revive | 词性: 动词

asphyxia: /æsˈfɪksiə/ | n. | 窒息 | A condition arising when the body is deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocation. | The victim died of asphyxia. 受害者死于窒息。| 近义词: suffocation | 反义词: respiration | 词性: 名词


词源: From Late Latin *asphyxia, from Greek asphyxia "stopping of the pulse," from a- "without" + sphyxis "pulse."
