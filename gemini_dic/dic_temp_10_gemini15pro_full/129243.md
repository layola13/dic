nocturn:/ˈnɒktɜːn/ (UK) /ˈnɑːktɜːrn/ (US)|n.|夜曲; 夜间活动; 晚祷|A piece of music, typically for piano, that is expressive and evocative of night.|Chopin's nocturnes are some of his most beautiful compositions. 肖邦的夜曲是他最优美的作品之一。|Synonyms: serenade, lullaby|Antonyms: diurnal|noun

nocturnal:/nɒkˈtɜːnəl/ (UK) /nɑːkˈtɜːrnl/ (US)|adj.|夜间的; 夜间活动的|Done, occurring, or active at night.|Bats are nocturnal creatures.  蝙蝠是夜行动物。|Synonyms: nightly, nighttime|Antonyms: diurnal, daytime|adjective
