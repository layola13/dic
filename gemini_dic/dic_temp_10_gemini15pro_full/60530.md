elaidin:/ɪˈleɪɪdɪn/
noun
反式异油酸; 反油酸甘油酯
A solid fat produced by the hydrogenation of liquid unsaturated fats.
Elaidin is used in the manufacture of margarine and other food products. 反式异油酸用于人造黄油和其他食品的生产。
Elaidin is formed by the isomerization of oleic acid. 反式异油酸是由油酸异构化形成的。
Synonyms: trans-fat, hydrogenated fat
Antonyms: unsaturated fat
