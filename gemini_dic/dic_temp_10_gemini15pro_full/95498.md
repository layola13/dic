inclinable:/ɪnˈklaɪnəbl/| adj.|可倾斜的，倾向于…的|Having a tendency or disposition to do something; readily disposed; willing. Also, capable of being inclined or tilted.|The seat is inclinable to several different positions.  这个座位可以倾斜到几个不同的位置。|The young man is inclinable to poetry.  这个年轻人倾向于诗歌。|近义词：disposed, prone, liable, susceptible|反义词：disinclined, averse, resistant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *inclinable*, from Old French *inclinable*, from Latin *inclinābilis*, from *inclināre* (“to incline, bend, lean”), from *in-* + *clināre* (“to bend, lean”).
