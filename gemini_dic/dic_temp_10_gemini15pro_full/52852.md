dimensity: /dɪˈmɛnsɪti/ | n. | 维度; 量级; 规模;  |  A measurable extent of some kind, such as length, width, depth, or height. It can also refer to the magnitude or scope of something.  While not a standard English word, it seems to be a blend of "dimension" and "immensity," suggesting a vast or significant dimension. | The dimensity of the problem became clear only after extensive research.  这个问题的维度只有经过广泛的研究后才变得清晰。|  The sheer dimensity of the universe is awe-inspiring. 宇宙的浩瀚令人敬畏。| Near synonyms: dimension, magnitude, scope, scale, vastness | Antonyms: smallness, insignificance | noun
