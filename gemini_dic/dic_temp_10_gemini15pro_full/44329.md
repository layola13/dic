croquante:/kroʊˈkɑːnt/ | adj. n.| 酥脆的，脆皮的；脆皮甜点 |A crispy or crunchy texture, often used to describe pastries or candies.  Can also refer to a specific type of confection.| The croquante topping on the cake was delicious. 蛋糕上的脆皮太好吃了。| 近义词：crispy, crunchy, brittle | 反义词：soft, chewy, soggy | 词性: adjective, noun

croquante:/kroʊˈkɑːnt/ | n. | 脆皮甜点 | A sweet, typically almond-based confection with a brittle, crunchy texture.| She served a delicious croquante with coffee. 她端上了一份美味的脆皮甜点和咖啡。| 近义词：brittle, confection | 反义词：None | 词性: noun

词源: From French, present participle of *croquer* "to crunch."
