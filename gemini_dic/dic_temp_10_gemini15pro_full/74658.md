free-versifiers:/friːˈvɜːsɪfaɪərz/| n. | 自由诗体诗人 | A person who writes free verse, poetry that does not rhyme or have a regular rhythm. |  Walt Whitman and Allen Ginsberg are considered influential free-versifiers. 沃尔特·惠特曼和艾伦·金斯伯格被认为是有影响力的自由诗体诗人。 | synonyms: free verse poets, vers libre writers | antonyms: formalists, traditional poets | noun
