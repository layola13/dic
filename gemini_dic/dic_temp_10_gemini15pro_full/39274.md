congressman:/ˈkɒŋɡrəsmən/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrəsmən/ (US)| n. |国会议员（尤指美国众议院议员）|A male member of a congress, especially the US House of Representatives.  A woman who is a member is usually called a congresswoman. |例句：The congressman voted against the bill.  这位国会议员投票反对该法案。|近义词：representative, legislator, delegate |反义词: N/A |名词

congressman:/ˈkɒŋɡrəsmən/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡrəsmən/ (US)| n. |女国会议员（尤指美国众议院议员）|Although less common, can sometimes refer to a female member. Now usually congresswoman is preferred. |例句：The congressman voiced her concerns about the new policy.  这位女国会议员表达了她对新政策的担忧。|近义词：representative, legislator, congresswoman, delegate |反义词: N/A |名词
