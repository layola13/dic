dogg:/dɒɡ/ | n. |朋友, 哥们 (俚语) |A slang term used to refer to a friend, often a close male friend.  Originally associated with West Coast hip hop culture. | Example: "What's up, dogg?" 你好，哥们？| Example:  "Me and my doggs are going to the game." 我和我的朋友们要去看比赛。 | Synonym: friend, buddy, pal, homeboy (slang) | Antonym: enemy, foe | noun
