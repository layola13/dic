elderwort:/ˈɛldərˌwɜːrt/| n. |接骨木; 接骨木花|A herbaceous plant (Sambucus ebulus) of the honeysuckle family, closely related to the elder and having medicinal properties.|The elderwort was used in traditional medicine to treat inflammation.|近义词：walewort, dwarf elder, danewort|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
