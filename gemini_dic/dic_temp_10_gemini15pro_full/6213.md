ammonium:/əˈmoʊniəm/| n.|铵|A positively charged ion, NH4+, formed by the reaction of ammonia with a proton.|例句：Ammonium chloride is a common fertilizer. 氯化铵是一种常见的肥料。

例句：Ammonium nitrate is used in explosives. 硝酸铵用于炸药。|近义词: N/A|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ammonia + -ium.  Ultimately from the name of the ancient Egyptian god Amun (whose temple was near a deposit of ammonium chloride).
