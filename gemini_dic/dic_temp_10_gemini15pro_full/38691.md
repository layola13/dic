condensedly:/kənˈdɛnsɪdli/| adv.| 简洁地，简要地|In a condensed or concentrated manner; briefly and compactly.|He explained the complex theory condensedly, making it easy to understand. 他简洁地解释了这个复杂的理论，使之易于理解。|Synonyms: succinctly, briefly, compactly, concisely|Antonyms: verbosely, lengthily, protractedly|adverb

condense:/kənˈdɛns/| v.| 冷凝，凝结；浓缩，压缩|To reduce to a denser form, as by cooling or removing water; to make more concise, to shorten.|The steam condensed into water droplets. 蒸汽凝结成水滴。|Synonyms: compress, concentrate, abridge, shorten|Antonyms: expand, dilute, lengthen|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin condensātus, past participle of condensāre (“to make dense, thicken”), from Latin con- (“together”) + dēnsus (“dense”).
