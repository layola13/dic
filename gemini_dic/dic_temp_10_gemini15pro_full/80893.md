governmentalizes: /ˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使政府化;使官僚化
to make something governmental; to cause something to be controlled or operated by a government.
The new regulations governmentalize many aspects of the healthcare industry.  新的法规使医疗保健行业的许多方面政府化。
The increasing governmentalization of education has led to standardized testing. 日益增长的教育政府化导致了标准化考试。
Synonyms: nationalize, bureaucratize, regulate
Antonyms: privatize, deregulate
词性: 动词
