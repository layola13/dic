fumigates:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪts/
verb
熏蒸，以烟熏消毒
exposes to smoke or fumes, especially to disinfect or kill pests.
The warehouse fumigates the grain regularly to prevent insect infestation.  仓库定期熏蒸谷物以防止虫害。
sterilizes, disinfects, treats
cleans, airs

Etymology:
From Late Latin fumigatus, past participle of fumigāre "to fumigate," from Latin fūmus "smoke."
