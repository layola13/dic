hypochondry:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriə/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriə/ (UK)| n. |疑病症，臆想病|A psychological disorder characterized by excessive worry about having a serious illness, despite medical reassurance to the contrary.|She suffers from hypochondry and is always convinced she has a deadly disease.|近义词:health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder|反义词:robust health|词性:noun


hypochondriac:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/ (UK)| n. adj. |疑病症患者，疑病的|A person who suffers from hypochondry; exhibiting or characterized by hypochondria.|He's a real hypochondriac and rushes to the doctor for every little ache or pain. /  Her hypochondriac tendencies made her difficult to live with.|近义词: valetudinarian |反义词: stoic |词性:noun, adjective
