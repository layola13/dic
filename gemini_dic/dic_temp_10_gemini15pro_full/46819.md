dantean:/ˈdæntiən/
adjective
但丁式的;地狱般的
Relating to or characteristic of the Italian poet Dante or his writings, especially with reference to the Divine Comedy and its depiction of Hell.  Resembling the Inferno; hellish.
The movie presented a dantean vision of a post-apocalyptic world. 这部电影呈现了世界末日后地狱般的景象。
The prisoners endured dantean conditions in the overcrowded jail. 囚犯们在拥挤的监狱里忍受着地狱般的条件。
Synonyms: hellish, infernal, satanic, diabolical
Antonyms: heavenly, paradisiacal, celestial
词性: 形容词
