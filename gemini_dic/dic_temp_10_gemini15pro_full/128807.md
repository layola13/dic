nightish:/ˈnaɪtɪʃ/| adj.|将近夜晚的，夜间的|Relatively late in the evening; approaching nighttime.  It suggests a time that is not quite night but getting close to it.|We arrived at the cabin nightish, just as the sun was setting. 我们快到晚上的时候到达了小屋，正好太阳落山。|近义词：late, evening, nearly night|反义词:early, morning|词性:形容词

词源: From "night" + "-ish" (a suffix indicating approximation or resemblance).
