crank-in:/ˈkræŋkɪn/
n.
开机；开拍
The commencement of filming or photography for a motion picture or television program.
The movie had its crank-in ceremony last week.  这部电影上周举行了开机仪式。
commencement, start, beginning
closure, wrap-up
noun

crank-in:/ˈkræŋkɪn/
v.
开拍；开始拍摄
To begin filming or photography for a motion picture or television program.
They cranked in the new season of the show yesterday. 他们昨天开拍了新一季的节目。
commence, start, begin
finish, stop
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
