impenitent:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtənt/ | adj. | 不悔悟的，顽固的 | Not feeling or showing any regret for having done something wrong. | He remained impenitent about his crime. 他对自己的罪行仍然毫无悔意。 |  The impenitent thief showed no remorse for his actions.  那个顽固不化的窃贼对自己的行为没有表现出一丝悔恨。 |  near synonyms: unrepentant, remorseless, unapologetic, obdurate | near antonyms: repentant, contrite, remorseful | adjective
