compatible: /kəmˈpætəbəlz/ | n. | 兼容物，兼容设备 | Things that are compatible with each other. | These two devices are compatibles. 这两个设备是兼容的。| synonyms: counterparts, equivalents | antonyms: incompatibles | noun
