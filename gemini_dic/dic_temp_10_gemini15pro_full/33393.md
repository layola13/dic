church: /tʃɜːrtʃ/ (British) /tʃɜːrtʃ/ (American)| n.|教堂；教会；礼拜；教派|A building used for public Christian worship.|例句：The church bells rang out on Sunday morning.  星期天早上教堂的钟声响了。

例句：She goes to church every Sunday. 她每个星期天都去教堂。

近义词：chapel, cathedral, temple, house of worship|反义词: None (No single antonym encompasses all meanings)|词性: noun

church: /tʃɜːrtʃ/ (British) /tʃɜːrtʃ/ (American)| adj. |教会的|Relating to or used in a church.|例句：Church music can be very moving. 教堂音乐可以很感人。

例句：They held a church wedding. 他们举行了教堂婚礼。

近义词：ecclesiastical, religious|反义词: secular|词性: adjective (less common)
