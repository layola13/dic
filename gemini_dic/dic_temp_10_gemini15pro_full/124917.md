mudless:/ˈmʌdləs/
adjective
无泥的；干净的
Free from mud; not muddy.
The new path was paved and mudless, even after the rain. 雨后，新铺的小路仍然没有泥泞，很干净。
近义词:clean, spotless, immaculate
反义词:muddy, dirty, soiled
词性:adjective

词源:
From mud + -less.  "Mud" comes from Proto-Germanic *mut-, meaning "soft, wet earth."  The suffix "-less" is also of Germanic origin and indicates the absence of something.
