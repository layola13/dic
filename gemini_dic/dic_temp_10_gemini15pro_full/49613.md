demi-puppet:/ˈdɛmiˌpʌpɪt/ | n. | 半傀儡，受他人影响的人 |  A person who appears to have some degree of autonomy but whose actions are largely controlled or influenced by another.  This term implies a greater degree of freedom than a "puppet," suggesting the individual retains some agency but is still significantly manipulated. | He acted like a demi-puppet, outwardly independent but secretly following his mentor's every instruction. 他表现得像个半傀儡，表面上独立自主，但暗地里却听从导师的每一个指示。|  Near synonyms: tool, pawn, cat's-paw  | Antonym:  independent, free agent | noun

demi-puppet:/ˈdɛmiˌpʌpɪt/ | n. | 半自动玩偶 | A puppet or toy that combines elements of automation and manual control. | The demi-puppet moved its arms automatically but the puppeteer controlled its head and facial expressions. 这个半自动玩偶的手臂会自动移动，但木偶师控制着它的头部和面部表情。 | Near synonyms:  partially automated toy, semi-automatic puppet | Antonym: fully automated toy, manually operated puppet | noun
