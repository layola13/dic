nonaerobiotic:/ˌnɒnˌɛəˈrəʊbɪɒtɪk/ | adj. | 非需氧的；厌氧的 |  Not requiring oxygen for growth or survival; anaerobic. Relating to or denoting an organism that does not require free oxygen for life. |  Certain nonaerobiotic bacteria thrive in environments devoid of oxygen.  某些厌氧细菌在无氧环境中茁壮成长。 | synonyms: anaerobic, anoxic | antonyms: aerobic, oxygen-requiring | adjective
