dysfunctional:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəl/ | adj. |功能失调的；不正常的|Not operating normally or properly.|例句：The dysfunctional family was constantly arguing.  这个不正常的家庭总是争吵不断。

例句：The company's dysfunctional management led to its downfall.  公司功能失调的管理层导致了它的垮台。|近义词：malfunctioning, impaired, broken, disturbed|反义词：functional, healthy, normal|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dys- (prefix meaning "bad," "ill," or "abnormal") + functional.  The prefix "dys-" comes from Greek "dus-".
