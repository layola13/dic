homochromy:/ˈhɒməʊkrəʊmi/ /ˈhoʊməkroʊmi/| n. |保护色，拟态|The homochromy of the chameleon allows it to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙的保护色使它能够与周围环境融为一体。|The insect's homochromy made it almost invisible against the bark of the tree.  这种昆虫的保护色使它在树皮上几乎看不见。|近义词：camouflage, mimicry|反义词：aposematism (警戒色)|词性: noun
Etymology:  From Greek *homos* (same) + *chroma* (color)
