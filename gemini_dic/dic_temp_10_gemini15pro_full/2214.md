address:/əˈdrɛs/ (US) /əˈdres/ (UK)| n. vt.|地址，演讲，称呼，处理|A location of a building or residence.  To speak to someone. To deal with an issue.|例句：Please write your address clearly on the form. 请在表格上清晰地写下你的地址。

My professor addressed the class about the importance of the upcoming exam. 我的教授向全班同学讲述了即将到来的考试的重要性。|近义词：location, speech, handle, tackle, greet|反义词:ignore, neglect|词性:noun, verb


address:/əˈdrɛs/ (US) /əˈdres/ (UK)| vt.|称呼，致辞|To speak to or write to someone, especially formally or respectfully.|例句：She addressed the Queen as "Your Majesty". 她称呼女王为“陛下”。

He addressed the letter to the manager. 他把信寄给了经理。|近义词：speak to, write to, greet, hail|反义词:|词性:verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The letter is addressed to Mr. Smith. = 这封信是寄给史密斯先生的。
She always addresses her boss formally. = 她总是正式地称呼她的老板。


一般过去时:
The president addressed the nation last night. = 总统昨晚向全国发表了讲话。
I addressed the letter to the wrong person. = 我把信寄错了人。

现在完成时:
I have addressed all the envelopes. = 我已经给所有信封写上地址了。
The committee has addressed the issue of funding. = 委员会已经解决了资金问题。

过去完成时:
He had addressed the crowd before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经向人群发表了讲话。
She had addressed the letter before she realized her mistake. = 在她意识到自己的错误之前，她已经写好了信的地址。

将来时:
The CEO will address the shareholders at the annual meeting. = 首席执行官将在年度会议上向股东们发表讲话。
I will address that problem later. = 我稍后会处理那个问题。

虚拟语气:
If I were to address the Queen, I would be very nervous. = 如果我要对女王讲话，我会非常紧张。
I wish I had addressed the issue sooner. = 我希望我早点解决这个问题。

被动语态:
The issue was addressed at the meeting. = 这个问题在会议上得到了解决。
The letter was addressed to the wrong department. = 这封信被寄到了错误的部门。

疑问句:
How should I address the judge? = 我应该如何称呼法官？
What issues did the speaker address? = 演讲者讨论了哪些问题？

否定句:
The letter wasn't addressed correctly. = 信上的地址写错了。
I haven't addressed the problem yet. = 我还没有解决这个问题。
