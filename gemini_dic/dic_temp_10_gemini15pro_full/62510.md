endowments:/ɪnˈdaʊmənts/| n. |捐赠，天赋，才能|Something that is bestowed; a gift, talent, or quality. It often refers to a large sum of money given to an institution. |The university relies heavily on endowments to fund its research programs. 这所大学的研究项目很大程度上依赖于捐赠基金。|
The singer's natural endowments include a beautiful voice and a charismatic stage presence.  这位歌手的天赋包括优美的嗓音和富有魅力的舞台表现力。|近义词：gifts, talents, abilities, bequests, donations|反义词：deficiencies, shortcomings, weaknesses|词性:noun
