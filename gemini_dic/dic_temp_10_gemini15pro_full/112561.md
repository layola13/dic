lovelily:/ˈlʌvlɪli/| adv.|可爱地；秀丽地；妩媚地|In a lovely manner; beautifully; charmingly.|She sang lovelily, her voice filling the hall. 她唱得优美动听，歌声充满了整个大厅。|The garden was lovelily arranged with colorful flowers. 花园布置得赏心悦目，五彩缤纷的花朵竞相开放。|近义词：beautifully, charmingly, attractively, prettily|反义词：unattractively, uglily|词性:副词
Etymology: From "lovely" + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs). "Lovely" comes from Middle English "lofly," from Old English "luflic," meaning "loving, amiable."
