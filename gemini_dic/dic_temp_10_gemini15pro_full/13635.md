attitudinal:/ˌætɪˈtjuːdɪnl/
adjective
态度上的，态度的
Relating to attitudes.
Her attitudinal problems were affecting her performance at work. 她的态度问题正在影响她的工作表现。
synonyms:  mental, psychological, emotional, dispositional
antonyms:  behavioral, factual, objective
词性: adjective
