Hodgkin's:/ˈhɒdʒkɪnz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːdʒkɪnz/ (US)| adj.|霍奇金(氏)的；患霍奇金病的|Relating to Hodgkin's disease, a type of lymphoma.|He was diagnosed with Hodgkin's lymphoma at the age of 25. 他25岁时被诊断出患有霍奇金淋巴瘤。|Synonym: Hodgkin's lymphoma, Hodgkin's disease|Antonym: Non-Hodgkin's lymphoma|adjective

Hodgkin's disease:/ˈhɒdʒkɪnz dɪˈziːz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːdʒkɪnz dɪˈziːz/ (US)| n.|霍奇金病|A type of lymphoma, a cancer of the lymphatic system.|Hodgkin's disease is often curable with chemotherapy and radiation. 霍奇金病通常可以通过化疗和放疗治愈。|Synonym: Hodgkin's lymphoma|Antonym: Non-Hodgkin's lymphoma|noun

Hodgkin's lymphoma:/ˈhɒdʒkɪnz lɪmˈfəʊmə/ (UK) /ˈhɑːdʒkɪnz lɪmˈfoʊmə/ (US)| n.|霍奇金淋巴瘤|A malignant cancer of the lymphatic system characterized by the presence of Reed-Sternberg cells.|Advances in treatment have significantly improved the prognosis for Hodgkin's lymphoma. 治疗的进步显著改善了霍奇金淋巴瘤的预后。|Synonym: Hodgkin's disease|Antonym: Non-Hodgkin's lymphoma|noun
