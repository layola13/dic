convalescence:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesəns/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesəns/ (US)| n.|康复期；恢复期|The period of gradual recovery of health and strength after illness or injury.  The state of convalescing.|例句：She is now in convalescence after her operation. 她现在术后正在康复中。

例句：He spent a month in convalescence at a health resort. 他在一个疗养胜地度过了一个月的康复期。|近义词：recovery, recuperation, rehabilitation|反义词: illness, sickness|词性:noun


convalesce:/ˌkɒnvəˈles/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnvəˈles/ (US)| v. |恢复健康；康复|To recover health and strength gradually after illness or weakness.|例句：He is convalescing from a serious illness. 他正在从一场重病中康复。

例句：She convalesced at home for several weeks after leaving the hospital. 她出院后在家休养了几周。|近义词：recover, recuperate, rehabilitate|反义词: deteriorate, weaken|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convalescere*, from Latin *con-* (together, with) + *valescere* (to grow strong). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weg-* (to be strong, lively).
