modernities:/məˈdɜːrnɪtiz/| n.|现代性（复数）|The multiple and complex aspects of modern life, society, and culture, often considered in relation to their historical and social contexts.  It acknowledges that "modernity" is not a singular, monolithic entity, but rather takes various forms across different times and places.|  The rapid advancements in technology have shaped our modernities in profound ways. 技术的快速进步深刻地塑造了我们的现代性。|  The book explores the conflicting modernities of East and West. 这本书探讨了东西方相互冲突的现代性。| synonyms: contemporary trends, modern developments, current times | antonyms: traditions, antiquities, past eras | noun
