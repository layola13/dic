Celts: /kɛlts/ /sɛlts/ | n. |凯尔特人| A group of people who shared a similar language and culture and who lived in parts of Europe more than 2,000 years ago.  |The Celts were known for their intricate metalwork. 凯尔特人以其复杂的金属制品而闻名。|  The Celts inhabited much of Europe during the Iron Age. 铁器时代凯尔特人居住在欧洲的大部分地区。 |  Gauls, Britons | Romans | noun
