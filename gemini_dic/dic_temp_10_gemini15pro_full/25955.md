burr:/bɜːr/
n.|刺果；带刺的植物；毛刺|A rough or prickly seed case or flower head that clings to clothing or animal fur. Also, a whirring sound, a rough edge on metal, or a dialect.|The burrs stuck to my socks.|近义词:burrer, prickle, sticker, barb|反义词:smoothness|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *burre, from Old Norse burr (“prickle”), from Proto-Germanic *burzō (“thorn, thistle”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰers- (“to bristle; to project; point”). Cognate with Dutch bur (“burdock”), German Bürste (“brush”), Low German Borste (“bristle”).
