misspender:/mɪsˈspɛndər/
n.|挥霍者，浪费者|Someone who misspends, especially money. |He was a misspender of public funds. 他挥霍公款。|近义词：spendthrift, prodigal, waster|反义词：saver, economizer|词性:名词

Etymology:
From mis- + spender, from spend + -er.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrongly" or "badly". "Spend" comes from Old English "spendan," meaning "to expend, pay out." "-er" is a suffix used to form nouns denoting a person who performs the action of the verb.
