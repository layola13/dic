megastar:/ˈmɛɡəstɑːr/ (US) /ˈmɛɡəstɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |超级巨星；国际巨星|A very famous and successful performer, especially a singer, actor, or sports player.  A person who has achieved extraordinary fame and success in their field.|例句：She is a true megastar, adored by millions worldwide. 她是一位真正的超级巨星，受到全世界数百万人的喜爱。

例句：The football megastar announced his retirement after a long and illustrious career.  这位足球巨星在漫长而辉煌的职业生涯后宣布退役。|近义词：superstar, celebrity, icon, legend|反义词: unknown, nobody|词性: noun
