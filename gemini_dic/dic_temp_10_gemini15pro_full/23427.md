bracky: /ˈbræki/
adjective
微咸的，略带咸味的; Brackish
Slightly salty; somewhat saline.
The water in the estuary was bracky.  河口的水微咸。
近义词:briny, saline, salty
反义词:fresh
词性:adjective

brackish: /ˈbrækɪʃ/
adjective
微咸的，略带咸味的
Slightly salty, as is the mixture of river water and seawater in estuaries.
The brackish water of the marsh was home to a variety of unusual creatures. 沼泽的微咸水域是各种奇异生物的家园。
The boat sailed up the brackish river. 船沿着略带咸味的河流向上航行。
近义词: saline, briny
反义词: fresh
词性:adjective
