malacozoic:/ˌmæləˈzoʊɪk/| adj.| 软体动物的；属于软体动物的|Relating to or characteristic of the Mollusca phylum, a large group of invertebrate animals including snails, slugs, mussels, octopuses, and squid.  This term describes anything pertaining to this group, their characteristics, or their study.| Snails and clams are malacozoic animals.  蜗牛和蛤蜊是软体动物。|近义词：molluscan, molluscoid|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-molluscan)|词性: adjective
