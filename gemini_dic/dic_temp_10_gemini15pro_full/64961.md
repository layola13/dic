estheticizes:/ɛsˈθɛtɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
美化；赋予美感
To make something aesthetically pleasing; to represent something in a way that emphasizes its beauty or artistic qualities.
The photographer estheticizes the ordinary lives of farmers.  摄影师美化了农民的日常生活。
The film estheticizes violence, making it seem glamorous and exciting.  这部电影美化了暴力，使其看起来充满魅力和刺激。
synonyms: beautifies, glorifies, romanticizes, idealizes
antonyms: uglifies, deforms, distorts, vilifies
