deinternationalize:/diːɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪz/
verb
去国际化
To reduce the international character or involvement of something; to make something less international.
The new trade policies threaten to deinternationalize the economy. 新的贸易政策有可能使经济去国际化。
The government is trying to deinternationalize the education system. 政府正在努力降低教育体系的国际化程度。
localize, nationalize
globalize, internationalize
