carpel: /ˈkɑːrpl/ (US), /ˈkɑːpəl/ (UK) | n. | 心皮 | The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of the stigma, style, and ovary. |  The carpel develops into the fruit after fertilization. 心皮受精后发育成水果。| synonym: pistil | antonym: stamen | noun
