laurentia:/lɔːˈrɛnʃɪə/ /lɔːˈrɛnʃə/
n.
劳伦大陆，劳伦古陆
An ancient continent that formed the core of North America. It is named after the Laurentian Shield, a large area of exposed Precambrian rock in eastern Canada.
The geological history of Laurentia spans billions of years. 劳伦大陆的地质历史跨越数十亿年。
The breakup of Laurentia led to the formation of several smaller continents. 劳伦大陆的分裂导致了几个较小大陆的形成。
近义词:North American craton, Canadian Shield
