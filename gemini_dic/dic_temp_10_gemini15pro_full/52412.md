didelphid:/daɪˈdɛlfɪd/| n. adj.|负鼠；负鼠类的|A marsupial mammal of the order Didelphimorphia, found in the Americas and characterized by a prehensile tail, some species of which play dead when threatened.  (美洲有袋目动物，特征是有能缠绕的尾巴，有些种类在受到威胁时会装死)|The Virginia opossum is the most common didelphid in North America.  弗吉尼亚负鼠是北美最常见的负鼠。|The didelphid used its prehensile tail to hang from the branch. 这只负鼠用它能缠绕的尾巴挂在树枝上。|Synonym: opossum|Antonym:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑其他哺乳动物类别，例如 placental mammal 胎盘哺乳动物)|noun, adjective
