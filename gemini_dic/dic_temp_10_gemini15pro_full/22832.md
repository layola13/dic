bossies:/ˈbɒsiz/ (UK), /ˈbɔːsiz/ (US)| n.|老板们，女上司们 (Informal, often disapproving)|A term used to refer to people in charge, especially women, often carrying a slightly negative or disrespectful connotation.  It suggests that the speaker finds them domineering or officious.|例句：The bossies told us we had to work overtime. 那些女上司们告诉我们必须加班。|例句：Don't let the bossies see you slacking off. 别让那些老板娘们看到你偷懒。|近义词：bosses, managers, supervisors|反义词: subordinates, employees|词性:noun
