barouche:/bəˈruːʃ/| n.|四轮马车（一种轻便的四轮马车，车顶可折叠）|A four-wheeled carriage with a folding top, fashionable in the 19th century. |例句：The elegant barouche carried the wealthy family through the park.  这辆优雅的四轮马车载着富裕的家庭穿过公园。

例句：She arrived at the ball in a stylish barouche drawn by two white horses. 她乘坐一辆由两匹白马拉着的时髦四轮马车来到舞会。|近义词：carriage, coach|反义词:|noun
