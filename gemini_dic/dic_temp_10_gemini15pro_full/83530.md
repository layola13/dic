hadith:/hæˈdiːθ/ | n. | 圣训，传述 | A collection of traditions containing sayings of the prophet Muhammad that, with the Koran, constitute the major source of guidance for Muslims. |  Example 1: The hadith emphasizes the importance of charity. 这段圣训强调了慈善的重要性。

Example 2:  Scholars study the hadith to understand Islamic law and practice. 学者们研究圣训以理解伊斯兰法律和实践。 | synonym: tradition, narration, record | antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "fabrication" in the context of a falsely attributed hadith) | noun
