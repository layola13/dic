incurs:/ɪnˈkɜːrz/ (ɪnˈkɝːz)| v. |招致，遭受，引起|To become subject to something unwelcome or unpleasant as a result of one's own behavior or actions. |例句：The company incurred heavy losses due to the economic downturn.  公司由于经济衰退而遭受了严重的损失。

|例句：He incurred the wrath of his parents when he dropped out of school. 他辍学激怒了他的父母。|近义词：acquire, sustain, obtain, suffer, experience|反义词:avoid, prevent, avert|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *encurren*, from Anglo-Norman *encurrer*, from Old French *encorir*, from *en- + courir* (“to run”), from Latin *incurrō* (“I run into, run against, incur”), from *in- + currō* (“I run”).
