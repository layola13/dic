epipedometry:/ˌɛpɪˌpiːdɪˈɒmɪtri/ | n. | 表面测量学 | The measurement of surface areas, especially of the skin. |  Epidpedometry is crucial for assessing burn wounds accurately. 表面测量学对于准确评估烧伤创面至关重要。

The researchers used epipedometry to determine the exact size of the skin graft needed. 研究人员使用表面测量学来确定所需皮肤移植物的确切大小。 | 近义词: planimetry, surface area measurement | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *epi- "upon" + *pedon* "ground, surface" + *-metria* "measurement."
