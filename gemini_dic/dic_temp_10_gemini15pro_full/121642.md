misperceiving:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːvɪŋ/
verb
误解，错误地感知
incorrectly interpreting or understanding something.
She was misperceiving his silence as anger, when in fact he was just deep in thought. 她误以为他的沉默是生气，实际上他只是在沉思。
He misperceived her friendliness as romantic interest. 他误解了她的友好，以为是浪漫的兴趣。
misinterpreting, misunderstanding, misconstruing, misreading
correctly perceiving, accurately understanding, rightly interpreting
v.
