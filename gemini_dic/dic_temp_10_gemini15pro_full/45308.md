cumberbunds:/ˈkʌmbərbʌndz/
n.
腰带，腰封
A broad sash worn around the waist, typically over a man's dinner jacket or evening suit.
He wore a cummerbund with his tuxedo. 他穿着燕尾服，系着腰封。

近义词:sash, waistband, girdle
反义词:(No direct antonym, as it's a specific item of clothing)
词性:Noun
