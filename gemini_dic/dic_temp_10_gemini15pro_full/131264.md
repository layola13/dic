noncategoricalness:/ˌnɑːnkætəˈɡɔːrɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 非绝对性，非范畴性 | The state or quality of not being absolute or categorical; allowing for degrees or exceptions. |  The noncategoricalness of the diagnosis made it difficult to determine the best course of treatment.  诊断的非绝对性使得难以确定最佳治疗方案。 |  ambiguity, vagueness, indeterminacy |  categoricalness, definiteness, precision | noun
