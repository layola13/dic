cheeriest:/ˈtʃɪriɪst/| adj.|最快乐的，最兴高采烈的|Superlative form of cheery: most cheery.  It describes someone or something that exhibits the greatest degree of cheerfulness, happiness, and optimism.|Her cheeriest smile brightened the room. 她最灿烂的笑容照亮了整个房间。|Synonyms: happiest, most joyful, most optimistic, most upbeat|Antonyms: gloomiest, saddest, most depressing|adjective

Etymology:
From cheer + -y + -est.  "Cheer" comes from Old French "chiere" meaning "face, countenance," later meaning "pleasant mood." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives, and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
