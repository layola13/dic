gimmies:/ˈɡɪmiz/ | n. |  免费的东西; 施舍物; 容易得到的东西 (Informal, often childish) | Something given freely or easily obtained; a handout.  "Give me some of that candy!" can become "Gimmies!" in child speak. |  The kid kept yelling "Gimmies!" at his mom in the store.  这个孩子在商店里一直对他妈妈喊“给我！”| Synonyms: freebies, handouts | Antonyms: earnings,  payment | noun
