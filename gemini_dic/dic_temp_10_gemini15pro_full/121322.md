misframed:/mɪsˈfreɪmd/| adj.|装裱错误的，歪曲的，措辞不当的|Incorrectly framed, either literally (as with a picture) or figuratively (as with an argument or statement).|The photograph was misframed, cutting off the top of the subject's head. 这张照片装裱错误，把拍摄对象的头部顶部切掉了。

The lawyer argued that the case was misframed from the start. 律师辩称，这个案子从一开始就措辞不当。|近义词：distorted, misrepresented, misconstrued|反义词：accurate, correctly framed, properly represented|词性:形容词
