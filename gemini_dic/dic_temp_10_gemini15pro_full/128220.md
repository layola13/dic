neurochordal:/ˈnʊəroʊkɔːrdəl/| adj.|神经脊索的|Relating to or denoting the neurochord, a transient embryonic structure that defines the midline of the developing central nervous system.|The neurochordal cells give rise to the notochord. 神经脊索细胞会发育形成脊索。|The formation of the neurochordal plate is a crucial step in embryonic development. 神经脊索板的形成是胚胎发育的关键步骤。|近义词: notochordal|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-neurochordal")|词性: adjective
