annelida:/əˈnɛlɪdə/
n.
环节动物门
A phylum of invertebrate animals, characterized by segmented bodies.
Earthworms and leeches belong to the annelida phylum. 蚯蚓和水蛭属于环节动物门。
近义词:segmented worms
反义词:None
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin Annelida, from Latin annellus (“little ring”) + -ida (suffix forming names of animal classes and phyla).
