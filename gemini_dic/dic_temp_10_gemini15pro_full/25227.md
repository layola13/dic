budging:/ˈbʌdʒɪŋ/
verb
移动; 让步
yielding or changing one's opinion or course of action, especially reluctantly.
The protestors refused to budge from the square. 抗议者拒绝离开广场。
He wouldn't budge on the price. 他不愿在价格上让步。
近义词: moving, shifting, yielding, compromising
反义词: resisting, staying, refusing, holding firm
词性: verb (present participle)

budge:/bʌdʒ/
verb
(使)轻微移动; (使)改变主意
make or cause to make the slightest movement.
I couldn't budge the door. 我无法推动那扇门。
He wouldn't budge on the price. 他不愿在价格上让步。
近义词: move, shift, stir, give way
反义词: resist, stay put, hold firm
词性: verb

词源:
From Middle English *buggen*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old French *bouger* (“to move”), from Late Latin *bullire* (“to boil, bubble”).
