mirths:/mɜːrθs/| n.|欢笑，欢乐，高兴|instances of mirth; joyful laughter.|The room was filled with mirths. 房间里充满了欢笑。|近义词：laughter, joy, gaiety, merriment, amusement|反义词:sadness, sorrow, grief|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mirthes, plural of mirthe, from Old English *myrgþu (“mirth, joy, pleasure, delight, amusement”), from Proto-Germanic *murgiþō (“joy”), from Proto-Indo-European *merǵ- (“to shine, flash; short, brief, quick”). Cognate with Old Frisian merth (“joy”), Middle Low German merthe (“joy”), Old High German murgi (“joy”). Related to merry.
