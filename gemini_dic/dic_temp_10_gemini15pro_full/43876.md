crest:/krɛst/ | v. | 到达顶峰，达到最高点;  饰以顶饰 | To reach the top of something; to furnish with a crest. |  The wave crested and then crashed on the shore.  海浪达到顶峰，然后拍打在岸边。 | synonyms: top, crown, peak, culminate | antonyms: descend, decline, fall | verb
