dystrophias:/dɪˈstrɒfiəz/
|n.|肌营养不良症|A group of progressive muscle disorders characterized by the weakening and wasting away of muscle tissue.|Muscular dystrophy is a group of dystrophias that cause progressive weakness and loss of muscle mass.|近义词: atrophies, degenerations|反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider terms like "muscle growth" or "development")|词性:Noun, plural


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek *dys-* (bad, difficult) +  *-trophē* (nourishment)
