cliffiest:/ˈklɪfiɪst/
adjective
最陡峭的，最多悬崖的
having the most or steepest cliffs.
例句：The cliffiest part of the coast was inaccessible by foot.  海岸最陡峭的部分无法步行到达。

例句：They chose the cliffiest route for their hiking adventure. 他们选择了最陡峭的路线进行徒步探险。

近义词：steepest, craggy, precipitous
反义词：flattest, smoothest, gentlest
词性：adjective
