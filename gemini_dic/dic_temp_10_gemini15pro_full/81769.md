gregariousness:/ˌɡrɛˈɡɛəriəsnəs/| n.|合群；群居；社交性|The quality of being fond of company; sociability.|Her gregariousness makes her a natural hostess. 她的合群使她成为一个天生的女主人。|近义词：sociability, conviviality, affability|反义词：solitude, introversion, aloofness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gregarius* "belonging to a flock or herd," from *grex* "flock, herd."
