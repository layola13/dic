desoldered:/diːˈsɒldəd/ /diːˈsɑːdərd/ | v. |  拆焊的，去掉焊料的 | Removed solder from (an electronic component or connection). | The component was desoldered from the circuit board.  该组件已从电路板上拆焊下来。| Near Synonyms: unsoldered, detached | Antonyms: soldered, attached | Verb (past participle)


desoldered:/diːˈsɒldəd/ /diːˈsɑːdərd/ | adj. | 拆焊的 | Having had solder removed. | The desoldered connection was cleaned with alcohol.  拆焊下来的连接处用酒精清洗干净了。| Near Synonyms: unsoldered, detached | Antonyms: soldered, attached | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): From Latin, meaning "down, away from, off, reversal".
solder (verb): From Old French "soudure," ultimately from Latin "solidare" (to make solid).
-ed (suffix): Past participle marker.
