half-asian:/ˌhæfˈeɪʒən/ | adj. | 半亚裔的 |  Of or relating to a person whose parentage is half Asian and half of another ethnicity.  One parent is of Asian descent and the other parent is not. |  My friend is half-Asian; his mother is Korean, and his father is American. 我的朋友是半亚裔；他母亲是韩国人，他父亲是美国人。

| Example 2:  Many half-Asian individuals navigate complex cultural identities. 许多半亚裔人士在复杂的文化认同中摸索。 |  biracial, multiracial |  mono-racial, single-race | adjective
