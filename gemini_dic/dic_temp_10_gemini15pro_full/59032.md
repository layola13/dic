dwarfishness:/ˈdwɔːrfɪʃnɪs/
noun
矮小；侏儒；矮小性
The state or quality of being a dwarf; smallness of stature.
His dwarfishness made him stand out in the crowd. 他的矮小使他在人群中很显眼。
synonyms: shortness, smallness, diminutiveness
antonyms: tallness, height, gigantism
n.
