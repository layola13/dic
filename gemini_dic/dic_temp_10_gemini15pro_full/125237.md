multicuspidate:/ˌmʌltɪˈkʌspɪdeɪt/
| adj. |多尖的，多齿的|Having many cusps or points, especially referring to teeth.|The dentist identified the tooth as a multicuspidate molar. 牙医将这颗牙齿确定为多尖磨牙。|
|Synonyms: multipointed, polycuspid|Antonyms: unicuspid, bicuspid|adjective|


multicuspid:/ˌmʌltɪˈkʌspɪd/
| n. |多尖齿|A tooth with multiple cusps.|The multicuspid was extracted without complications. 这颗多尖齿被顺利拔掉了。|Synonyms: molar (often)|Antonyms: incisor, canine (generally contrasting tooth types)|noun|



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "multus" (many) + "cuspis" (point) + "-ate" (suffix forming adjectives).
