outwork:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrk/
verb
胜过；做得比…好
to work harder than someone else
例句：She consistently outworked her colleagues, achieving impressive results. 她一直比同事更努力，取得了令人瞩目的成果。

例句：He outworked everyone on the team and earned the promotion. 他比团队中的任何人都更努力，赢得了晋升。

近义词：outperform, surpass, excel, beat
反义词：underperform
词性：v.


outwork:/ˈaʊtwɜːrk/
noun
（尤指防御工事的）外围工事
a defensive structure outside a main fortification (especially in plural)
例句：The outworks of the castle were heavily damaged during the siege. 城堡的外围工事在围攻期间遭到严重破坏。

例句：The enemy breached the outworks and stormed the main fortress. 敌人突破了外围工事，攻占了主堡垒。

近义词：fortification, rampart, bulwark, defense
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "interior" in a specific context)
词性：n.


词源：
"out-" (prefix meaning "outside, beyond") + "work" (verb or noun)
