centralization:/ˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 集中化，中央集权 | The process of bringing something under the control of a central authority. | The government implemented a policy of centralization, bringing all decision-making power to the capital. 政府实行集中化政策，将所有决策权集中到首都。|  The centralization of resources led to greater efficiency. 资源的集中化带来了更高的效率。 | synonyms: consolidation, unification, integration | antonyms: decentralization, distribution, dispersion | noun

centralize:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 集中，中央集权 | To bring something under the control of a central authority. | The company decided to centralize its operations to reduce costs.  公司决定集中运营以降低成本。| The new CEO centralized all marketing decisions. 新任首席执行官将所有营销决策集中起来。| synonyms: consolidate, unify, integrate | antonyms: decentralize, distribute, disperse | verb
