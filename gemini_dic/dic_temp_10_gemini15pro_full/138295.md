notch:/nɒtʃ/ (British), /nɑːtʃ/ (American)| n. vt.|等级；档次；刻痕；峡谷；v.赢得；获得；刻凹痕|A small cut in a surface, typically one of a series, or a V-shaped indentation.  A level or degree of something.|

例句：He made a notch on the stick for every day he spent on the island.  他在棍子上每过一天就刻一个凹痕。
例句：She's already reached a high notch in her career. 她在事业上已经达到了很高的层次。

近义词：degree, level, mark, score, groove, indentation, nick
反义词:  smoothness (for the noun, referring to surface)

词性: noun, verb


notch:/nɒtʃ/ (British), /nɑːtʃ/ (American)| vt.|赢得；获得；刻凹痕|To achieve or obtain something, typically a victory or a goal. To make a notch in something.|

例句：The team notched up another victory. 该队又赢得了一场胜利。
例句：He notched the wood to keep track of the days. 他在木头上刻凹痕来记录天数。

近义词：win, gain, achieve, secure, score, mark
反义词: lose, fail

词性: verb
