disprepare:/dɪsprɪˈpɛər/
verb
使...没有准备好；使...失去准备
to make unprepared; deprive of preparation
The sudden change of plan disprepared us for the meeting.  计划的突然改变使我们对会议没有做好准备。
The army was caught disprepared for the enemy's attack.  军队对敌人的进攻毫无防备。
近义词:unprepare, disarm
反义词:prepare, equip
词性:v.
