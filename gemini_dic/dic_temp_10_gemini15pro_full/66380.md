execrable:/ˈɛksɪkrəbəl/| adj.|极坏的；糟糕的；恶劣的|Extremely bad or unpleasant.|The food at the restaurant was execrable. 这家餐馆的食物糟透了.|近义词：appalling, atrocious, awful, terrible, dreadful|反义词：excellent, superb, wonderful|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *exsecrabilis* "deserving to be cursed," from *exsecrari* "to curse greatly," from *ex-* "out" + *sacrare* "to devote to a deity, to curse."
