faves:/feɪvz/ | n. | 最喜欢的东西（人）; 最爱 |  A shortened, informal form of "favorites."  It refers to things or people that someone likes the most. | My faves are pizza and ice cream. 我的最爱是披萨和冰淇淋。 | favorites, preferred | dislikes | noun
