fleas:/fliːz/
n.
跳蚤
small, wingless, jumping insects that feed on the blood of mammals and birds
The dog was scratching constantly because of fleas. 这只狗因为跳蚤而不断地抓挠。

近义词:parasites
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēah, from Proto-Germanic *flauhō (“flea”), from Proto-Indo-European *pleu- (“to flow, to swim, to fly”). Cognates include German Floh, Dutch vlo, and Latin pulex.  The connection to "flow" likely refers to the flea's jumping ability.
