midwiferies: /ˌmɪdˈwɪfəriz/ | n. | 助产术；助产士业务；接生 |  The practice of assisting women in childbirth.  It refers to the profession and services provided by midwives. | The hospital is known for its excellent midwiferies. 这家医院以其出色的助产服务而闻名。|  midwifery services, obstetrics, birthing services | gynecology (not a direct antonym, but a related and contrasting field) | noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *midwyfrye*, from *midwif* + *-rye*.  *Midwif* comes from Old English *mīd* (with) + *wīf* (woman, wife).  *-rye* denotes an occupation, practice, or place of business.
