denies:/dɪˈnaɪz/
verb
否认，拒绝
declares something to be untrue; refuses to admit or accept something.
He denies all responsibility for the accident. 他否认对这起事故负有任何责任。
refuses to fulfill a request or desire
She denied him access to her files. 她拒绝让他访问她的文件。
synonyms: contradicts, refutes, rejects, repudiates, disclaims, gainsays
antonyms: admits, accepts, acknowledges, confirms, confesses
v.
