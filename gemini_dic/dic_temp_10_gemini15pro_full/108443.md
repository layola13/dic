laxatives: /ˈlæksətɪvz/ | n. |泻药；通便剂|Substances that loosen the bowels and relieve constipation.  They stimulate bowel movements.|She took some laxatives to relieve her constipation. 她服用了一些泻药来缓解便秘。|Synonyms: purgatives, aperients, cathartics|Antonyms: antidiarrheals, constipating agents|Noun

laxative: /ˈlæksətɪv/ | n. adj. |泻药；通便的|A medicine or agent that causes bowel movements.  Can also be used adjectivally to describe something that has a laxative effect.|The doctor prescribed a mild laxative. 医生开了一种温和的泻药。 |  Prunes have a laxative effect. 西梅有通便的作用。|Synonyms: purgative, aperient, cathartic|Antonyms: antidiarrheal, constipating agent|Noun, Adjective
