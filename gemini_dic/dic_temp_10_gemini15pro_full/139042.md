oarfish:/ˈɔːrfɪʃ/ | n. | 皇带鱼 | A large, greatly elongated, pelagic lampriform fish (family Regalecidae) of warm or temperate seas, having a ribbonlike body, a red dorsal fin extending the length of the back, and pelvic fins represented by long filaments. |  The oarfish is the longest bony fish alive. 皇带鱼是现存最长的硬骨鱼。| synonyms: king of herrings, ribbonfish | antonyms: None (It's a specific type of fish, not a concept with an opposite.) | noun
