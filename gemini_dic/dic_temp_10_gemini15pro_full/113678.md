mack-on:/ˈmæk ɒn/ |n.|亲吻;爱抚;调情;（尤指）性挑逗|An act of kissing, caressing, or making romantic advances, especially with sexual intent. |He tried a mack on her, but she wasn't interested. 他试图和她调情，但她不感兴趣。|近义词:flirtation, pass, come-on|反义词:rejection, brush-off|词性:名词

词源:
From rhyming slang "mackintosh art," where "mackintosh" referred to a raincoat and "art" was shortened from "heart." The raincoat was seen as a means of protection or concealment, perhaps for amorous activities.
