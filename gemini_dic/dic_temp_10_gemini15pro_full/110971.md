liquidambar:/ˈlɪkwɪdˌæmbər/| n. |枫香树；枫香树脂；苏合香|A type of tree known for its fragrant resin and star-shaped leaves, also known as sweetgum.  |例句：The liquidambar trees in the park turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall. 公园里的枫香树在秋天变成了鲜艳的红色和橙色。

例句：Liquidambar resin has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.  枫香树脂几个世纪以来一直被用于传统医学。|近义词：sweetgum, redgum, stargum|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *liquidus ambar* "liquid amber," from liquidus "liquid, fluid" + Arabic *ʿanbar* "ambergris".  The resin of the liquidambar tree was thought to be similar to ambergris.
