distraction:/dɪˈstrækʃən/
n.|分心，注意力分散；使人分心的事物|Something that prevents someone from giving their full attention to something else. |The children were a constant distraction. 孩子们总是让人分心。|synonyms: interruption, disturbance, diversion|antonyms: concentration, focus|noun

distraction:/dɪˈstrækʃən/
n.|娱乐,消遣|An activity that amuses or entertains. |Playing video games is his favorite distraction. 玩电子游戏是他最喜欢的消遣。|synonyms: entertainment, amusement, pastime|antonyms: work, boredom|noun
