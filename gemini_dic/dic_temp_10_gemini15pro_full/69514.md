fell-through:/fɛl θruː/
verb phrase
失败；落空；泡汤
If a plan or agreement falls through, it fails to happen.
Our plans for a picnic fell through because of the rain.  我们的野餐计划因为下雨而泡汤了。
The deal fell through at the last minute.  这笔交易在最后一刻告吹了。
Synonyms: collapse, fail, founder, miscarry
Antonyms: succeed, triumph, prevail
