dyspnoea:/dɪsˈpniːə/ /dɪsˈpniːɑː/ | n. | 呼吸困难 | Difficult or labored breathing. | Example 1:  The patient complained of dyspnoea after climbing the stairs. 病人抱怨爬楼梯后呼吸困难。

Example 2: Dyspnoea is a common symptom of heart failure. 呼吸困难是心力衰竭的常见症状。| Synonym: shortness of breath, breathlessness,  labored breathing | Antonym: eupnea (normal breathing) | noun
