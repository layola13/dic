disserviceable:/dɪsˈsɜːvɪsəbəl/
adjective
有害的，帮倒忙的
Harmful; causing harm or disadvantage; unhelpful.
His disserviceable comments alienated potential supporters. 他帮倒忙的评论疏远了潜在的支持者。

近义词: detrimental, harmful, injurious, prejudicial, counterproductive, unhelpful
反义词: helpful, beneficial, advantageous, useful, serviceable

词性: adjective

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + serviceable.  The prefix "dis-" negates the meaning of "serviceable," which means helpful or useful.
