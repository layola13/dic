dressings:/ˈdrɛsɪŋz/
n.
调味料，敷料，包扎材料
A sauce added to salads or other foods. Materials used to cover and protect a wound.
The salad was delicious with a light vinaigrette dressing.  沙拉配上清淡的香醋调味汁非常美味。
The nurse changed the dressings on the patient's wound. 护士更换了病人伤口上的敷料。
近义词:sauces, condiments, bandages, coverings
反义词:(For food dressings) plain, undressed. (For medical dressings) bare, uncovered
词性:名词(plural)

dressings:/ˈdrɛsɪŋz/
n.
(衣服等的)装饰，修饰
Something added to improve the appearance of something.
The colorful dressings on the stage made the performance more vibrant.  舞台上五彩缤纷的装饰使表演更加生动。
近义词:decorations, ornaments, adornments
反义词:plainness, simplicity
词性:名词(plural)

词源: From the verb "dress," which comes from Old French "dresser" meaning "to arrange, prepare."
