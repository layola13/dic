horseworm:/ˈhɔːrswɜːrm/| n. |马蛔虫|A parasitic worm that infects horses, specifically *Parascaris equorum*.|例句：The veterinarian found horseworms in the foal's stool sample.  兽医在小马驹的粪便样本中发现了马蛔虫。

例句：Horseworms can cause serious health problems if left untreated. 如果不治疗，马蛔虫会导致严重的健康问题。|近义词：equine ascarid, intestinal parasite|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a parasite)|词性:noun
