non-flammable:/ˌnɒnˈflæməbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈflæməbəl/ (UK)| adj.|不易燃的，非易燃的|Not easily set on fire; not flammable.|例句：Non-flammable materials are used in building construction to reduce fire hazards.  建筑施工中使用不易燃材料以减少火灾隐患。|例句：This fabric is treated with a special coating to make it non-flammable.  这种织物经过特殊涂层处理使其不易燃。|近义词：fire-resistant, fireproof|反义词:flammable, combustible|词性:形容词

non-flam:/ˌnɒnˈflæm/ (Shortened form, less common)| adj.|不易燃的|Shortened form of "non-flammable," less formally used.|例句：Use a non-flam cleaning solution. 使用不易燃的清洁溶液。|例句：Check the label to ensure the product is non-flam.  检查标签以确保产品不易燃。|近义词：fire-resistant, fireproof|反义词:flammable, combustible|词性:形容词
