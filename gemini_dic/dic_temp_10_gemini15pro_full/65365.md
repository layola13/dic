eulytite:/ˈjuːləˌtaɪt/ | n. | 硅铍石 |A rare mineral, a silicate of bismuth.  A tetragonal mineral consisting of bismuth silicate, typically occurring as small brown tetrahedra. | Eulytite is a rare mineral found in Germany.  硅铍石是一种罕见的矿物，发现于德国。| Eulytite crystals are usually small and brown. 硅铍石晶体通常很小，呈棕色。 |  bismuth silicate,  silicate mineral |  None | noun
