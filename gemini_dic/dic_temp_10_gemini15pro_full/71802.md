flee:/fliː/
verb
逃跑，逃走
To run away from danger or from something unpleasant.
The family fled the country during the war.  这家人在战争期间逃离了该国。
He fled the scene of the accident. 他逃离了事故现场。
近义词: escape, run away, abscond
反义词: remain, stay, face, confront
词性: verb

flee:/fliː/
noun (obsolete)
逃跑，逃离 (古语，现已很少使用)
The act of running away from danger.
Their flee from the burning building was miraculous.  他们从燃烧的建筑物中逃脱是一个奇迹。
近义词: escape, flight
反义词: arrival, approach
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēon (West Saxon flēon, Anglian flēon) "to flee, escape, vanish," from Proto-Germanic *fleuhaną (source also of Old Saxon fliohan, Old Frisian fliā, Old Norse flýja, Dutch vlieden, Old High German fliohan, German fliehen, Gothic þliuhan), from Proto-Indo-European *pleuk-, extended form of *plew- "to flow, swim, float, fly."  Related to fly (v.). The noun is from the verb.
