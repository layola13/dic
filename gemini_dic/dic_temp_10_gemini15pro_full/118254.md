merchandisers:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzərz/ | n. | 商人；推销员；采购员 | People or companies involved in the buying and selling of goods, especially on a large scale.  They often focus on presentation, promotion, and pricing strategies to maximize sales.| The merchandisers arranged the clothing displays to attract customers.  推销员们布置服装陈列以吸引顾客。 | synonyms:  salespeople, vendors, retailers, distributors, marketers | antonyms: customers, consumers | noun
