infallibility:/ɪnˌfælɪˈbɪlɪti/| n. |无谬性，绝对可靠|The quality of being incapable of making mistakes or being wrong. |The Pope’s infallibility is a doctrine of the Catholic Church.  教皇的无谬性是天主教会的一项教义。

The system boasts infallibility, but users have reported errors. 该系统号称绝对可靠，但用户报告出现了错误。|近义词：certainty, reliability, impeccability|反义词：fallibility, errability, imperfection|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: infallibilite, from Old French infallibilité, from Medieval Latin infallibilitas, from infallibilis ‘incapable of error’ (see infallible).
