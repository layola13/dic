overpresumptuousnesses:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptʃuəsnɪsɪz/
| n. | 过分自以为是，过分放肆 |  The state of being excessively presumptuous; characterized by an unwarranted assumption of importance, privilege, or familiarity. | His overpresumptuousnesses alienated his colleagues, who found his constant boasting unbearable. 他的过分自以为是疏远了他的同事，他们觉得他不断的吹嘘令人难以忍受。| 近义词: arrogance, audacity, conceit, presumption | 反义词: humility, modesty, diffidence | 词性: noun

词源:
From over- + presumptuous + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix indicating excess. "Presumptuous" comes from the Late Latin praesumptuosus, from praesumere "to anticipate, presume," from prae- "before" + sumere "to take." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
