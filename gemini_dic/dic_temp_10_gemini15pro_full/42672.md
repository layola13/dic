counterstroke:/ˈkaʊntərˌstroʊk/| n.|反击，回击|An action taken in opposition to another action, especially one taken to counteract an attack or disadvantage.|The boxer delivered a swift counterstroke, knocking his opponent off balance.  拳击手迅速回击，打得对手失去平衡。|Synonyms: counterattack, retaliation, riposte|Antonyms: attack, assault|noun

counterstroke:/ˈkaʊntərˌstroʊk/| v.|反击，回击|To make a counterstroke.|He counterstroked effectively against his opponent's arguments. 他有效地反驳了对手的论点。|Synonyms: counterattack, retaliate, riposte|Antonyms: attack, assault|verb
