mozzie:/ˈmɒzi/ (UK), /ˈmɑːzi/ (US)| n. |蚊子|Informal word for mosquito, primarily used in Australia and New Zealand.|例句：The mozzies were terrible last night. 昨晚蚊子太多了。|例句：She got bitten by a mozzie. 她被蚊子咬了。|近义词：mosquito, gnat|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortening of mosquito, influenced by Aussie slang's tendency towards adding the "-ie" or "-y" suffix.
