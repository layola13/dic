amoebic:/əˈmiːbɪk/| adj.|变形虫的；阿米巴样的；无定形的|Relating to or resembling an amoeba, especially in being shapeless or in having the ability to constantly change shape.  Also, used metaphorically to describe something that is formless or constantly changing.|The company's amoebic structure allows it to adapt quickly to market changes.  这家公司的变形虫式结构使其能够快速适应市场变化。|Synonyms: amorphous, shapeless, protean, fluid, adaptable|Antonyms: rigid, fixed, inflexible|adjective

amoeboid:/əˈmiːbɔɪd/| adj.|变形虫的；阿米巴样的|Relating to or resembling an amoeba, especially in having the ability to constantly change shape.|The amoeboid robot could squeeze through tight spaces.  这个变形虫状的机器人可以挤过狭窄的空间。|Synonyms: amoebic, amorphous, shapeless, protean, fluid, adaptable|Antonyms: rigid, fixed, inflexible|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *amoebicus*, from Latin amoeba + -icus.  Ultimately from Greek amoibē, meaning "change, alteration."
