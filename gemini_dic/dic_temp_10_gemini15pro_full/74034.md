forworn:/fɔːrˈwɔːrn/| adj.|疲惫不堪的；憔悴的；破旧的|Exhausted, worn out, or showing the effects of hardship or long use.  Often used to describe a person's appearance or clothing.|例句：The refugees arrived at the camp looking forworn and weary. 难民们到达营地时，看起来疲惫不堪，十分憔悴。

例句：Her forworn coat offered little protection against the biting wind. 她那件破旧的外套几乎抵挡不住刺骨的寒风。|近义词：exhausted, weary, haggard, worn-out, ragged, tattered|反义词：energetic, refreshed, vibrant, pristine, new|词性：形容词
