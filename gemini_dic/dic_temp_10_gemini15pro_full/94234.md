imperil:/ɪmˈperəl/
adjective
处于危险中的，受到威胁的
put (someone or something) at risk of being harmed, injured, or destroyed.
The dam imperiled the fish populations downstream.  大坝危及了下游的鱼类种群。
endanger, jeopardize, threaten
protect, safeguard
adjective
