disproportionateness:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənɪtnəs/| n. |不相称，不成比例，不均衡|The disproportionateness of the punishment to the crime caused public outcry.  这种惩罚与罪行的不相称引起了公众的强烈抗议。| disproportion, imbalance, inequality | proportionality, balance, equilibrium | noun

Etymology:
From dis- (not) + proportionate (having a correct relationship in size, amount, importance, etc.) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin pro- (for) + portio (share, portion).
