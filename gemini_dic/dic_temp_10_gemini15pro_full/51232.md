destroying:/dɪˈstrɔɪɪŋ/
v.|破坏，毁灭，摧毁|The fire is destroying the building. 火灾正在摧毁这栋建筑。|近义词：ruining, damaging, demolishing|反义词：creating, building, repairing|词性:动词-现在分词
Etymology: From Middle English *destroyen*, from Old French *destruire*, from Latin *dēstruere* (“to pull down, demolish, destroy”), from *dē* (“down, away from”) + *struere* (“to pile, build”).
