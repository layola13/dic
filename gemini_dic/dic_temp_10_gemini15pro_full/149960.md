palm-off:/ˈpɑːm ɒf/ (US) /ˈpɑːm ɔːf/ (UK)| phrasal verb | 蒙骗; 以欺骗手段出售; 搪塞; 把...硬塞给|to persuade someone to accept something that is not worth much, or not what they think it is|He tried to palm off a fake watch on me. 他试图把一块假表塞给我。|近义词：foist, pass off, fob off|反义词: -(难以找到完全相反的词)|phrasal verb

palm-off:/ˈpɑːm ɒf/ (US) /ˈpɑːm ɔːf/ (UK)| n. | 蒙骗; 以欺骗手段出售; 搪塞; 把...硬塞给 (名词化用法较少见)|The act of deceiving someone into accepting something worthless.|His attempt to palm off the damaged goods was unsuccessful. 他试图把受损的货物塞给别人的尝试失败了。|近义词：deception, fraud|反义词: -(难以找到完全相反的词)|noun
