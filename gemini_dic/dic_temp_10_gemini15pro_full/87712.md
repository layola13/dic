hexastichic:/ˌhɛksəˈstɪkɪk/
n.
六行诗; 六行诗节
A stanza or poem of six lines.
The poet experimented with the hexastichic form.  这位诗人尝试了六行诗体。

hexastichic:/ˌhɛksəˈstɪkɪk/
adjective
六行的; 有六行的
Having six lines.
He composed a hexastichic poem for the occasion. 他为此场合创作了一首六行诗。
