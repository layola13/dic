inaccessiblenesses:/ɪnəkˌsɛsəˈbɪlɪtɪz/
n.
难以接近; 无法到达; 达不到
the state of being impossible or very difficult to reach or get to.

The inaccessibleness of the remote mountain village made it a haven for endangered species.
这个偏远山村的难以接近使它成为濒危物种的避难所。


The researchers noted the inaccessibleness of the necessary data for their study.
研究人员注意到他们研究所需数据的难以获取。


Synonym:  remoteness, isolation, unavailability, impenetrability
Antonym: accessibility, availability, nearness, closeness
词性: noun (plural)
