gemmaceous:/dʒɛˈmeɪʃəs/| adj.|芽状的，多芽的|Multiplying by gemmae; reproducing by budding, as in certain mosses and liverworts.|例句：The gemmaceous liverwort reproduces asexually. 这种芽状地钱以无性方式繁殖。|例句：Gemmaceous propagation is common in bryophytes.  芽殖在苔藓植物中很常见。|近义词：budding, gemmiferous, gemmiform|反义词: seed-bearing, flowering|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *gemmaceus (“budding”), from gemma (“bud”).
