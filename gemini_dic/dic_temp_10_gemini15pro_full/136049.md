ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + rebelliousness (the quality of being rebellious).  Rebelliousness comes from rebellious + -ness. Rebellious comes from rebel + -ious. Rebel ultimately derives from the Latin *rebellis*, meaning "making war again."
