discolorate:/dɪsˈkʌlərət/ /dɪsˈkʌl.ə.reɪt/
verb
使变色；褪色
to change the color of something, especially by making it paler or darker
Exposure to sunlight can discolorate fabrics. 暴露在阳光下会使织物褪色。
近义词:stain, fade, bleach, tarnish
反义词:color, brighten
词性:v.


discoloration:/dɪsˌkʌl.əˈreɪ.ʃən/ /dɪsˌkʌl.əˈreɪ.ʃən/
noun
变色；褪色
a change in the color of something, especially to a less attractive color
The discoloration of the painting was due to age. 这幅画的褪色是由于年代久远。
近义词:stain, blemish, tarnish
反义词:brightness, clarity
词性:n.
