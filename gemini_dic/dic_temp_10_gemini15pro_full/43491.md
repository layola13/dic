craterless:/ˈkreɪtərlɪs/
adj.
无陨石坑的，没有坑的
Having no craters.
The surface of Europa appears surprisingly craterless.  木卫二的表面看起来惊人地没有陨石坑。
The landscape was eerily smooth and craterless.  这片地貌光滑得可怕，而且没有陨石坑。
近义词:smooth, unblemished, unmarked
反义词:cratered, pitted, pocked
词性:形容词
