anatron:/ˈænətrɒn/| n.|电子动画人偶|A computerized method of animating figures, typically for use in theme parks and other attractions.  It uses pre-recorded movements and dialogue synchronized with video playback.|The Haunted Mansion at Disneyland uses animatronics to create spooky ghosts.|Synonyms: automaton, robot, android|Antonyms: None|noun
