dramatizer:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzər/| n. |剧作家，戏剧化的人|A person who adapts material for dramatic presentation or who exaggerates the seriousness of situations. |例句：The playwright was a skilled dramatizer, capable of turning any story into a gripping play.  这位剧作家是一位技艺娴熟的戏剧化者，能够把任何故事变成引人入胜的戏剧。

例句：He's such a dramatizer; a small cut becomes a life-threatening injury in his telling. 他太爱小题大做了；一个小伤口在他嘴里就成了危及生命的伤势。|近义词：playwright, dramatist, exaggerator |反义词:understater, minimizer| noun
