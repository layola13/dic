antiplatelet:/ˌæntiˈpleɪtlɪt/ | adj. n. | 抗血小板的；抗血小板药 |  Relating to or denoting a substance that reduces the tendency of platelets to adhere to each other and form blood clots. Can be used as an adjective to describe such substances or as a noun to refer to the substance itself.  |  The doctor prescribed an antiplatelet medication to prevent further clotting. 医生开了抗血小板药物以防止进一步凝血。 |  The antiplatelet effects of aspirin are well-documented. 阿司匹林的抗血小板作用已得到充分证明。 | Synonyms: antithrombotic, anticoagulant (partially) | Antonyms: procoagulant, thrombogenic | adjective/noun
