conservatrix:/kənˈsɜːvətrɪks/ |n.|女保护人；女管理人；女院长|(especially in older use) a woman who protects or maintains something, especially an institution or tradition.  A female equivalent of "conservator".|例句：The conservatrix of the museum ensured the artifacts were well preserved.  博物馆的女管理员确保文物得到了很好的保存。

例句：She was appointed conservatrix of the royal gardens. 她被任命为皇家花园的保护人。|近义词：custodian, guardian, protector, curator |反义词：destroyer, vandal|词性: noun
