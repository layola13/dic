absorb:/əbˈzɔːrb/ /əbˈsɔːrb/| v.|吸收，理解，使全神贯注|The sponge absorbed the spilled water. 海绵吸收了洒出的水。|近义词：soak up, take in, assimilate, incorporate|反义词:exude, emit, release|verb

absorb:/əbˈzɔːrpt/ /əbˈsɔːrpt/| adj.|被…吸引住的，专注的|He was completely absorbed in his work. 他完全沉浸在他的工作中。|近义词：engrossed, preoccupied, immersed|反义词:distracted, uninterested|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin absorbere, from Latin ab- + sorbere "to suck in."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
A sponge absorbs water. = 海绵吸水。
The company absorbs the cost of training. = 公司承担培训费用。

一般过去时:
The towel absorbed the spilled milk. = 毛巾吸收了洒出的牛奶。
He absorbed all the information quickly. = 他很快吸收了所有的信息。

现在完成时:
The market has absorbed all the new products. = 市场已经消化了所有新产品。
She has absorbed a lot of knowledge from the training. = 她从培训中学到了很多知识。

过去完成时:
The company had absorbed several smaller firms before it went public. = 公司在上市之前已经并购了几家小公司。
He had absorbed the main points of the lesson before the quiz. = 在测验之前，他已经掌握了课程的要点。

将来时:
The new material will absorb impacts better. = 新材料将更好地吸收冲击。
I will absorb as much information as I can. = 我会尽可能多地吸收信息。

虚拟语气:
If the material could absorb more light, it would be more efficient. = 如果这种材料能吸收更多光线，它的效率会更高。
I wish I could absorb information more quickly. = 我希望我能更快地吸收信息。

被动语态:
The spilled water was absorbed by the sponge. = 洒出的水被海绵吸收了。
The cost of the repairs will be absorbed by the insurance company. = 维修费用将由保险公司承担。

疑问句:
Does this material absorb sound? = 这种材料吸音吗？
How much water can the sponge absorb? = 这块海绵能吸收多少水？

否定句:
The fabric doesn't absorb water well. = 这种织物不吸水。
He didn't absorb the information properly. = 他没有正确理解信息。


PHRASE->
be absorbed in = 全神贯注于
absorb into = 融入，吸收到
absorb knowledge/information = 吸收知识/信息
fully absorb = 完全吸收
absorb the cost/impact = 承担费用/冲击
