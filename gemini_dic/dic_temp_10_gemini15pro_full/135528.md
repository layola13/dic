nonpliability:/ˌnɒnplaɪəˈbɪləti/
n.|不顺从，固执，僵硬|The nonpliability of the material made it difficult to work with.  这种材料的僵硬使得它难以加工。|例句：His nonpliability made negotiations difficult. 他的固执使得谈判变得困难。  The nonpliability of the rules frustrated the employees. 规则的僵化使员工感到沮丧。|近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, obstinacy|反义词：flexibility, pliability, compliance, docility|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + pliable + -ity.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Pliable" comes from Old French "pliable," from "plier" meaning "to bend," ultimately from Latin "plicare" meaning "to fold."  "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
