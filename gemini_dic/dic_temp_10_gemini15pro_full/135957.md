nonqualification:/ˌnɒnˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
不合格，没有资格
lack of qualification; failure to meet the requirements or standards for something.
His nonqualification for the job was due to his lack of experience. 他由于缺乏经验而不符合该工作的资格。
ineligibility, disqualification
qualification, eligibility
noun
