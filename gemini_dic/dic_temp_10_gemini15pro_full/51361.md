detergent:/dɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/ /dɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/ | n. | 洗涤剂，清洁剂 | A substance used for cleaning, especially washing clothes or dishes, that removes dirt by emulsifying grease and suspending dirt particles. |  I use a biological detergent for my laundry. 我用生物洗涤剂洗衣服。

| Example: This detergent is tough on stains but gentle on fabrics.  这种洗涤剂去污力强，但不伤衣物。 | Synonym: cleanser, soap, washing powder, washing-up liquid | Antonym: pollutant | noun
