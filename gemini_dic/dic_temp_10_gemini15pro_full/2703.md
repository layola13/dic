adscript:/ædˈskrɪpt/ | adj. | 附加的，附属的 | Added or attached to something else; supplementary. | The adscript note provided further explanation. 附加的注释提供了进一步的解释。  The adscript building was later demolished. 附属的建筑后来被拆除了。 | 近义词: attached, supplementary, appended, annexed | 反义词: detached, separate, independent | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY ->
From Latin *adscriptus*, past participle of *adscribere* ("to enroll, assign, add in writing"), from *ad* ("to") + *scribere* ("to write").

USAGE ->
Attributive usage:
The adscript document provided further details. = 附加文件提供了更多细节。
He referred to the adscript information in the footnote. = 他提到了脚注中附加的信息。

Predicative usage (less common):
The information was adscript to the main report. = 该信息是附加在主要报告中的。
The building is adscript to the main hospital. = 该建筑物附属主医院。


PHRASE ->
