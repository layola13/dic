melonist:/ˈmɛlənɪst/
n.
西瓜爱好者; 对西瓜有强烈爱好的人
A person who loves melons; someone with a strong liking for melons.
Melons are a summertime staple, and this melonist can't wait for the season to begin. 西瓜是夏季的主食，这位西瓜爱好者迫不及待地想开始这个季节。
The melonist carefully selected a ripe watermelon at the market.  这位西瓜爱好者在市场上精心挑选了一个成熟的西瓜。

近义词: melon lover, watermelon enthusiast
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes melons)
