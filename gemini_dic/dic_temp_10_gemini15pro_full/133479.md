nonfelicitously: /ˌnɒnfəˈlɪsɪtəsli/ /ˌnɑːnfəˈlɪsɪtəsli/
| adv. | 不恰当地，不幸地 | In a way that is not appropriate or suitable for the occasion; unfortunately. |  He nonfelicitously chose to wear a bright yellow suit to the funeral. 他很不恰当地穿了一套亮黄色的西装去参加葬礼。| Synonyms: inappropriately, unsuitably, awkwardly, unfortunately, inaptly | Antonyms: appropriately, suitably, aptly, felicitously | Adverb

Etymology:
From non- + felicitous + -ly.  "Felicitous" comes from the Latin "felix" (meaning "lucky, fruitful, blessed") + "-ous" (forming adjectives). "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not".
