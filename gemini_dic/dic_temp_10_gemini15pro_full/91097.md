hyacinth:/ˈhaɪəsɪnθ/ | n. | 风信子；红锆石；紫蓝色 |A bulbous plant of the lily family with a fragrant bell-shaped flower, native to the eastern Mediterranean. Also, a purplish-blue color resembling that of the hyacinth flower.  A reddish-orange gemstone, a variety of zircon. |例句：The hyacinths in the garden are blooming beautifully. 花园里的风信子开得很漂亮。

例句：She wore a dress of a beautiful hyacinth hue. 她穿着一件漂亮的紫蓝色连衣裙。|近义词：bluebell, grape hyacinth|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

hyacinth:/ˈhaɪəsɪnθ/ | adj. | 风信子色的；紫蓝色的 |Of a purplish-blue color resembling that of the hyacinth flower. |例句：The hyacinth sky was breathtaking. 紫蓝色的天空令人叹为观止。

例句：Her hyacinth eyes sparkled with mischief. 她紫蓝色的眼睛闪着调皮的光芒。|近义词：purplish-blue, lavender, violet |反义词: N/A |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *jacincte*, from Latin *hyacinthus*, from Ancient Greek *ὑάκινθος (huákinthos)*, perhaps of Pre-Greek origin.
