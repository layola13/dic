nonaffiliates:/ˌnɑːnəˈfɪliɪts/
n.
非附属机构，非会员，无关联公司/个人
People or organizations not officially attached to or connected with a particular group, company, or institution.
The university offers discounted rates to students and nonaffiliates.  大学为学生和非附属人员提供折扣价。
This parking lot is for employees only; nonaffiliates will be towed.  此停车场仅供员工使用；非附属车辆将被拖走。
synonyms: nonmembers, outsiders, independents
antonyms: members, affiliates, associates
noun
