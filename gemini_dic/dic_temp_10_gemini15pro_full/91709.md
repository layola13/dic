hyperconservativeness:/ˌhaɪpərˈkənˈsɜːrvətɪvnəs/ | n. | 极端保守主义；极端保守性 |  The state of being extremely averse to change and holding onto traditional values or practices very rigidly. |  Her hyperconservativeness made it difficult for her to adapt to the rapidly changing world. 她极端保守，难以适应快速变化的世界。| synonyms:  ultraconservatism, reactionism, right-wing extremism | antonyms: progressivism, liberalism, radicalism | noun


hyperconservative:/ˌhaɪpərˈkənˈsɜːrvətɪv/ | adj. | 极端保守的 | Extremely averse to change and holding onto traditional values or practices very rigidly. |  His hyperconservative views on social issues alienated many of his younger colleagues. 他在社会问题上的极端保守观点疏远了他的许多年轻同事。| synonyms: ultraconservative, reactionary, right-wing | antonyms: progressive, liberal, radical | adjective


Etymology:  "Hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér, "over, beyond") + "conservative" (from Latin: conservare, "to preserve").
