Abdullah: /æbˈdʌlə/ | n. | 阿卜杜拉 | A male given name of Arabic origin, meaning "servant of God". It is a common Muslim name, often given in honor of Abdullah ibn Abd al-Muttalib, the father of the Islamic prophet Muhammad. |  Abdullah is a popular name in many Muslim countries. 阿卜杜拉在许多穆斯林国家都是一个很受欢迎的名字。| My friend Abdullah is a doctor. 我的朋友阿卜杜拉是一位医生。 |  Near Synonyms: Abd Allah, Abdul | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic عبد الله (`Abd Allāh), meaning "servant of God".  `Abd (عبد) means "servant" and Allāh (الله) means "God".
