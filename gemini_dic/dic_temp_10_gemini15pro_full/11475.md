argy-bargy:/ˈɑːɡi ˈbɑːɡi/
noun
争吵，争论
A noisy quarrel or disagreement.
Example: The kids were having a bit of an argy-bargy over who got to play with the new toy. 孩子们正在为谁可以玩新玩具而争吵。
Example:  I could hear the neighbours having a right argy-bargy next door. 我能听到邻居家正在大声争吵。
Synonyms: quarrel, argument, squabble, dispute, row, tiff
Antonyms: agreement, harmony, peace
