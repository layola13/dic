hypnotic:/hɪpˈnɑːtɪk/ | adj. |催眠的，有催眠作用的|inducing or tending to induce sleep or a trancelike state; mesmerizing|The rhythmic sound of the waves had a hypnotic effect. 海浪有节奏的声音有催眠的效果。|Synonyms: mesmerizing, soporific, trance-inducing|Antonyms: stimulating, invigorating|adjective

hypnotic:/hɪpˈnɑːtɪk/ | n. |安眠药，催眠药|a drug that induces sleep|The doctor prescribed a hypnotic to help her sleep. 医生给她开了安眠药来帮助她睡眠。|Synonyms: sleeping pill, sedative|Antonyms: stimulant|noun

hypnotics:/hɪpˈnɑːtɪks/ | n. |催眠药（复数）|drugs that induce sleep|Hypnotics should only be taken under medical supervision.  安眠药只能在医生的监督下服用。|Synonyms: sleeping pills, sedatives|Antonyms: stimulants|noun
