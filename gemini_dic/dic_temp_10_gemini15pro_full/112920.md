luffa:/ˈlʌfə/ (US) /ˈlʊfə/ (UK)| n.|丝瓜；丝瓜络；丝瓜瓤|A tropical climbing plant bearing large, ribbed fruits with fibrous flesh. The dried fibrous interior of the fruit is used as a sponge.|例句：I bought a natural luffa to use in the shower. 我买了一个天然丝瓜络用来洗澡。

例句：Luffa is a popular vegetable in Asian cuisine. 丝瓜是亚洲菜肴中一种很受欢迎的蔬菜。|近义词：loofah, vegetable sponge, dishcloth gourd|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a plant/object)|词性: Noun
