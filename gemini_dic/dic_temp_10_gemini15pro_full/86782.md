hem:/hɛm/ | n. |边，边缘，摺边 |The hem of her dress was torn. 她的连衣裙的边缘撕破了。|  
hem:/hɛm/ | vt. |缝边，包围，限制 |She hemmed the skirt. 她给裙子缝边。|
hem:/hɛm/ | vi. |咳嗽一声(以引起注意) |He hemmed nervously before speaking. 他在说话前紧张地咳嗽了一声。|


Etymology->
From Old English *hemm, from Proto-Germanic *hemmaz. Cognate with Old High German hem (German Hemd "shirt").

Synonyms->
(n.) edge, border, rim, fringe
(vt.) sew, stitch, border, enclose, confine
(vi.) clear one's throat

Antonyms->
(n.) center, middle
(vt.)  free, liberate, release
