antipatriarchally:/ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkəli/
adverb
反父权制地，反对父权制地
In a manner that opposes or challenges patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power.
She spoke antipatriarchally about the need for equal representation in government. 她以反父权制的方式谈到了政府中平等代表的必要性。
The organization works antipatriarchally to dismantle systems of male dominance.  该组织以反父权制的方式致力于瓦解男性主导的体系。
近义词:feministically, anti-sexist
反义词:patriarchally
词性:adverb
