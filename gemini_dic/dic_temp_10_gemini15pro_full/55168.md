disseminate:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/
verb
传播，散布，宣传
To spread or scatter widely, as if sowing seed; to distribute, circulate, or publicize widely.
The organization disseminated information about the disease through pamphlets and public service announcements.  该组织通过小册子和公益广告宣传有关这种疾病的信息。

The internet allows us to disseminate information quickly and efficiently. 互联网使我们能够快速有效地传播信息。

近义词:spread, distribute, circulate, propagate, broadcast
反义词:suppress, withhold, conceal, restrict
词性:verb
