hastier:/ˈheɪstiər/ | adj. | 更匆忙的，更草率的 | Comparative form of hasty: more hasty. Done or acting with excessive speed, often without due consideration. | He made a hastier decision than usual due to the approaching deadline. 由于截止日期临近，他做出了比平时更草率的决定。| synonyms: quicker, faster, rapid, hurried, rushed, speedy, swift, precipitate, impetuous, impulsive | antonyms: slower, more deliberate, cautious, careful | adjective

hastily:/ˈheɪstɪli/ | adv. | 匆忙地，草率地 | In a hasty manner; quickly and without much thought. | She hastily packed her bags and left for the airport. 她匆忙地收拾行李，前往机场。| synonyms: quickly, rapidly, speedily, swiftly, hurriedly, in a hurry, in a rush, posthaste, at speed, at full tilt | antonyms: slowly, deliberately, carefully | adverb
