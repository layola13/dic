echometry:/ɛˈkɒmɪtri/ | n. |回声测距，回波测距|The measurement of distance by timing the return of reflected sound waves or other forms of energy.  It is used in various fields like medicine, sonar, and seismology.|例句：Echometry is used to determine the depth of the ocean.  回声测距用于确定海洋的深度。

例句：Doctors use echometry, also known as ultrasound, to examine internal organs. 医生使用回声测距（也称为超声波）来检查内脏器官。|近义词：sonometry, acoustic ranging|反义词: None|词性:noun
