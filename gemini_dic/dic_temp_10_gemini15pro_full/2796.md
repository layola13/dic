advancements:/ədˈvɑːnsmənts/
n.
进步，进展
instances of progress in development
There have been significant advancements in medical technology.  医学技术取得了重大进展。

Recent advancements in artificial intelligence have been remarkable. 人工智能的最新进展引人注目。


近义词:progress, development, improvement, breakthrough
反义词:regression, decline, setback, stagnation
词性:名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French avancement, from avancer "to advance," from Late Latin abante "from before."

USAGE->
There have been significant advancements in renewable energy technology.
可再生能源技术取得了重大进展。

The company has made notable advancements in the field of robotics.
该公司在机器人领域取得了显著进展。



PHRASE->
scientific advancements 科学进步
technological advancements 技术进步
medical advancements 医学进步
recent advancements 最新进展
major advancements 重大进展
