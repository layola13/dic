electrostatic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk/| adj.|静电的；静电学的|Relating to stationary electric charges or fields as opposed to electric currents.|例句：Electrostatic discharge can damage electronic components. 静电放电会损坏电子元件。

例句：Electrostatic forces hold the atoms together in a molecule. 静电作用力将分子中的原子结合在一起。|近义词：static|反义词：electrodynamic|词性：形容词
