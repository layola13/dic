macrame:/ˈmækrəmeɪ/ (US), /ˈmækrəmiː/ (UK)| n.|花边结, 壁毯; 用绳结编织的工艺品|The art of knotting cord or string in patterns to make decorative articles.  A coarse fringe or trimming made of knotted thread or cord.|She made a beautiful macrame wall hanging.|Synonyms: knotting, lacework|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
