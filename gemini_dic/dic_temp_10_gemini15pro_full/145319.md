overbroadening:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdnɪŋ/
|adj.|过度泛化|The process of extending the application of a rule or concept to instances that it does not properly govern or describe. In language acquisition, it refers to children applying a word to a wider range of objects or actions than is appropriate in adult usage.|The child overbroadened the word "dog" to include all four-legged animals.  这个孩子过度泛化了“狗”这个词，把它用在了所有四足动物身上。|overgeneralization, overextension|undergeneralization|adjective
overbroadening:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdnɪŋ/
|n.|过度泛化|The act or result of overbroadening.|The overbroadening of the term "fake news" has made it difficult to have productive discussions about media bias.  “假新闻”一词的过度泛化使得难以就媒体偏见进行富有成效的讨论。|overgeneralization, overextension|undergeneralization|noun
