ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *almeshows*, *almeshous*, from Old English *ælmesshūs* (“almshouse”), equivalent to *ælmesse* (“alms”) + *hūs* (“house”).  The word *ælmesse* comes from Late Latin *eleemosyna*, from Ancient Greek *ἐλεημοσύνη* (*eleēmosúnē*, “pity, alms”), from *ἐλεήμων* (*eleḗmōn*, “compassionate”).
