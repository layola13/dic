incanting:/ɪnˈkæntɪŋ/ | v. | 吟唱，念咒 | The act of speaking or chanting a ritual formula or spell aloud, often believed to have magical power. | The wizard was incanting a spell to summon a dragon.  巫师正在念咒召唤一条龙。| 近义词：chanting, conjuring, invoking | 反义词: silencing, dispelling | verb

incantation:/ˌɪnkænˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 咒语，吟唱 | A series of words said as a magic spell or charm. |  The witch muttered an incantation over the bubbling cauldron. 女巫对着沸腾的大锅低声念着咒语。 | 近义词：spell, charm, conjuration | 反义词: prayer, benediction | noun
