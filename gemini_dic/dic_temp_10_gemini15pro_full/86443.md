heep:/hiːp/
noun
堆；大量；群
a large, untidy pile of something
例句：He left his clothes in a heap on the floor. 他把衣服乱七八糟地堆在地板上。

例句：We have heaps of work to do. 我们有很多工作要做。
pile, mound, stack, mass
few, little
