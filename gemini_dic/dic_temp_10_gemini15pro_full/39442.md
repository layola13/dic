connection:/kəˈnekʃn/ (US) /kəˈnɛkʃn/ (UK)| n.|连接，联系，关系|A relationship in which a person, thing, or idea is linked or associated with something else. |例句：There is a strong connection between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间有很强的联系。|近义词：link, relationship, association, tie, bond|反义词:disconnection, separation|noun

connection:/kəˈnekʃn/ (US) /kəˈnɛkʃn/ (UK)| n.|联运，转车|The act of connecting or the state of being connected. |例句：I missed my connection in Chicago. 我在芝加哥错过了转机。|近义词：transfer, interchange|反义词: N/A|noun

connection:/kəˈnekʃn/ (US) /kəˈnɛkʃn/ (UK)| n.|熟人，人脉|Someone you know, especially somebody who can be helpful to you in your work. |例句：He used his connections to get the job. 他利用他的人脉关系得到了这份工作。|近义词：contact, acquaintance|反义词: N/A|noun
