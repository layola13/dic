disinvests:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛsts/
|v.|撤资，减少投资|To reduce or withdraw investment in something.|The company disinvested from fossil fuels.  该公司从化石燃料领域撤资。|Synonyms: divest, withdraw, pull out|Antonyms: invest, fund, finance|verb


disinvest:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛst/
|v.|撤资，减少投资|To reduce or withdraw investment in something.|They disinvested in the company due to ethical concerns. 由于道德方面的原因，他们撤回了对该公司的投资。|Synonyms: divest, withdraw, pull out|Antonyms: invest, fund, finance|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing reversal) + invest.  The prefix "dis-" negates the action of "invest," which means to put money into something.
