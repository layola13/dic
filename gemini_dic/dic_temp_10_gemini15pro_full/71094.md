fish-out:/ˈfɪʃ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 捞出；找出；掏出 | to remove something from a bag, pocket, etc. or from inside or under something | He fished out a crumpled five-dollar bill from his pocket. 他从口袋里掏出一张皱巴巴的五美元钞票。| synonyms: retrieve, extract, pull out | antonyms: insert, put in | verb phrase

fish-out:/ˈfɪʃ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 捞出；找出；掏出 | to find something or someone after searching for them, often with difficulty |  I finally managed to fish out the information I needed from the internet. 我终于从网上找到了我需要的信息。| synonyms: locate, discover, unearth | antonyms: conceal, hide, lose | verb phrase
