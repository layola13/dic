exegeses: /ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːsiːz/ | n. | 注释；解释；(尤指对圣经的)详细解释 | A critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of scripture. | The professor's exegeses of the ancient texts were insightful and thought-provoking. 教授对古代文献的注释富有洞察力和启发性。

| The rabbi offered a learned exegesis on the Torah portion. 拉比对律法部分作了学术性的解释。 | 近义词: interpretations, explanations, commentaries, analyses | 反义词: misinterpretations, distortions | 词性: 名词 (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exegesis, from Greek exēgēsis "explanation," from exēgeisthai "to interpret, explain," from ex- "out" + hēgeisthai "to lead, guide, tell."
