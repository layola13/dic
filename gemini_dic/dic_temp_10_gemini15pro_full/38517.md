conciliators:/kənˈsɪliˌeɪtərz/
n.|调解人，和事佬|A person who tries to end disagreements between people or groups. |例句：The conciliators worked tirelessly to find a peaceful resolution. 调解员不知疲倦地工作，以寻求和平解决办法。

例句：The UN sent conciliators to the region to help negotiate a ceasefire. 联合国向该地区派遣了调解员，以帮助谈判停火。
|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, peacemaker|反义词：agitator, instigator|词性:noun
