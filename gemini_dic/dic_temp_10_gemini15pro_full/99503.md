interfolding:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊldɪŋ/ | n. | 交叠，褶合，交织 | The process of folding one thing within another; the state of being interfolded. | The interfolding of the layers of rock created a complex geological structure. 岩层的交叠形成了复杂的地质结构。| The intricate interfolding of the protein chains is essential to its function. 蛋白质链的复杂交织对其功能至关重要。 | 近义词：folding, layering, intertwining, interweaving | 反义词：unfolding, separating, disentangling | noun


interfold:/ˌɪntərˈfoʊld/ | v. | 交叠，褶合，交织 | To fold something within another thing. | The artist interfolded the paper to create a three-dimensional form.  艺术家将纸张折叠以创建三维形状。|  Carefully interfold the sheets of tissue paper to prevent tearing. 小心地将薄纸折叠起来，以免撕裂。| 近义词：fold, layer, intertwine, interweave | 反义词：unfold, separate, disentangle | verb
