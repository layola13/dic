buildingless:/ˈbɪldɪŋlɪs/
adjective
无建筑的；没有建筑物的
lacking buildings; without buildings
The vast, buildingless desert stretched as far as the eye could see.  广阔无垠、没有建筑的沙漠一直延伸到视野尽头。
The photograph captured the buildingless expanse of the moon's surface.  这张照片捕捉到了月球表面空旷无垠的景象。
synonym: undeveloped, uninhabited, barren, desolate
antonym: developed, built-up, urban, populated
词性: adjective
