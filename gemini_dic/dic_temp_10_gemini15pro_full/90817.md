humus:/ˈhjuːməs/ | n. |腐殖质；腐殖土|The dark, organic material that forms in soil when plant and animal matter decays.  It is rich in nutrients and improves soil structure.| The gardener mixed humus into the soil to enrich it. 园丁将腐殖质混入土壤以使其肥沃。

The forest floor was covered in a thick layer of humus. 森林地面覆盖着一层厚厚的腐殖质。 | synonyms: compost, topsoil, organic matter | antonyms: inorganic matter, subsoil | noun
