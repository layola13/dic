overappareled:/ˌoʊvərəˈpærəld/
adjective
穿着过度的，打扮过分的
dressed more formally or elaborately than is appropriate or necessary for the occasion.
例句：She felt overappareled in her evening gown at the casual backyard barbecue.  她在休闲的后院烧烤会上穿着晚礼服，感觉穿着过度了。

例句：He was definitely overappareled for a hike in the woods, wearing a suit and tie. 他穿着西装打领带去树林里徒步旅行，显然是穿着过度了。

近义词：overdressed, too formally dressed
反义词：underdressed, casually dressed
词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "appareled" (past participle of "apparel," meaning "to clothe")
