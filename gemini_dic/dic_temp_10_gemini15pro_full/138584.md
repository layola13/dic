nuancing:/ˈnjuːɑːnsɪŋ/| n.|细微差别；微妙之处|The article lacked nuance and failed to capture the complexities of the situation. 这篇文章缺乏细微的差别，未能捕捉到情况的复杂性。|近义词：refinement, subtlety, gradation|反义词：simplification, generalization|noun

nuanced:/ˈnjuːɑːnst/| adj.|细致入微的；具有细微差别的|Her nuanced performance captured the subtle emotions of the character. 她细致入微的表演捕捉到了角色微妙的情感。|近义词：subtle, refined, sophisticated|反义词：crude, simplistic, unsubtle|adjective

nuance:/ˈnjuːɑːns/| n. vt.|细微差别；微妙之处；使细致入微|He nuanced his voice to express a wide range of emotions. 他使自己的声音细致入微，以表达各种各样的情感。|近义词：shade, subtlety, distinction|反义词：obviousness, bluntness|noun, verb
