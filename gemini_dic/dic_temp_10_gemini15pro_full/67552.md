extraneousness:/ˌɛkˈstreɪniəsnəs/| n. | 外来性；无关性；不相干 |The state or quality of being extraneous; irrelevance.| The extraneousness of the information made it difficult to understand the core message.  这些信息的无关性使得理解核心信息变得困难。|Synonyms: irrelevance, immateriality,  inapplicability,  unimportance,  peripherality |Antonyms: relevance,  pertinence,  importance,  essentialness | noun

extraneous:/ɪkˈstreɪniəs/| adj. | 外来的；无关的；不相干的 |Not essential or inherent; coming from outside.|  Extraneous details can bog down a story.  无关紧要的细节会拖累故事。 |Synonyms: irrelevant, immaterial,  inapplicable,  unimportant,  peripheral |Antonyms: relevant,  pertinent,  important,  essential | adjective
