paniculated: /ˈpænɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 圆锥花序的；散房状的 | Arranged in a panicle, a branched, indeterminate inflorescence with the youngest flowers at the apex. | The plant has paniculated white flowers.  这种植物有圆锥花序的白色花朵。| synonym: branched, diffuse | antonym: compact, unbranched | adjective

paniculated: /ˈpænɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 恐慌的，惊慌失措的 (less common usage, usually refers to the inflorescence) | Experiencing sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety. |  His breathing became rapid and shallow, his eyes wide and paniculated. 他的呼吸变得急促而浅薄，眼睛睁得大大的，充满了恐慌。(This usage is less common and often poetic.)  | synonym: panicked, terrified | antonym: calm, composed | adjective
