architected:/ˈɑːrkɪtɛktɪd/
verb
设计；构建；策划
designed and constructed, especially in a skillful or ingenious manner.
The system was architected to be scalable and reliable.  该系统的设计具有可扩展性和可靠性。
The software was poorly architected, leading to numerous bugs.  该软件架构设计糟糕，导致了许多错误。
Synonyms: designed, engineered, constructed, built, planned
Antonyms: unplanned, disorganized, chaotic
词性: v.
