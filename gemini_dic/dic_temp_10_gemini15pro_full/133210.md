nonexaggerating:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非夸张的，不夸大的 | Not exaggerating; representing accurately or without distortion. | She gave a nonexaggerating account of the accident. 她对事故作了不夸大的描述。 | He gave a nonexaggerating report of the situation. 他对情况作了不夸大的报告。 | 近义词: accurate, factual, truthful, realistic, understatement | 反义词: exaggerating, overstated, hyperbolic, embellished | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "exaggerating" (present participle of "exaggerate"). "Exaggerate" comes from Latin "exaggeratus," past participle of "exaggerare" ("to heap up, increase"), from "ex-" ("out, beyond") + "agger" ("heap, mound").
