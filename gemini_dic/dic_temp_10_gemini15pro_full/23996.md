breathiness:/ˈbrɛθɪnəs/| n.| 气息感；呼吸声；沙哑|A quality of voice characterized by audible exhalation.|Her voice had a breathiness that made her sound intimate.|近义词：huskiness, whisper, murmur|反义词：clarity, resonance|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From breathy + -ness.  Breathy comes from breath (Old English *brǣþ, from Proto-Germanic *brǣþuz) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).
