cinnamic:/sɪˈnæmɪk/| adj.|肉桂的，桂皮酸的|Relating to or derived from cinnamon; containing cinnamic acid.|The cinnamic fragrance filled the air. 肉桂的香味弥漫在空气中。|近义词:aromatic, spicy|反义词:bland|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cinnamique*, from Latin *cinnamum* "cinnamon," from Greek *kinnamōmon*, of Semitic origin (compare Hebrew *qinnamon*, Aramaic *qinnamona*).
