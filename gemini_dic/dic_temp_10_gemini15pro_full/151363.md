parks:/pɑːrks/
| n. |公园；停车场|A large public garden or area in a town, used for recreation. A place where vehicles are parked.|We went for a walk in the park. 我们去公园散步了。  The car is parked in the parking lot. 车停在停车场了。|近义词：gardens, green spaces, recreation grounds, parking lots, parking garages|反义词：streets, buildings|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Old French *parcs (plural of parc), from Frankish *parruk, meaning "enclosure, paddock," of uncertain origin.
