aras:/ˈɑːrɑːs/| n.| 阿拉斯河 (土耳其东部河流) |A river in eastern Turkey, flowing from the Armenian Highlands into the Kura River. |The Aras River forms part of the border between Turkey and Armenia. 阿拉斯河构成了土耳其和亚美尼亚之间部分边界。| The Aras River valley is known for its fertile land. 阿拉斯河谷以其肥沃的土地而闻名。 |  Kura River, Euphrates River | Tigris River |noun
