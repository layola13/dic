palmarium:/pɔːlˈmɛəriəm/
n.|温室，玻璃房，尤其指用来种植棕榈树的|A building, usually made of glass, for growing and displaying plants, especially palms.
The Victorian palmarium housed a variety of exotic flora.|维多利亚时代的温室里种植着各种各样的奇花异草。
近义词: greenhouse, conservatory, glasshouse
反义词: None
词性: Noun

palmarium:/pɔːlˈmɛəriəm/
n.|手掌；掌状物|Something resembling a palm of a hand. (Rare usage)
The rock formation looked like a giant palmarium reaching towards the sky.|这块岩石看起来像一只巨大的手掌伸向天空。(罕见用法)
近义词: palm (of hand), hand
反义词: None
词性: Noun


词源: From Latin "palma" (palm tree) + "-arium" (a place for).
