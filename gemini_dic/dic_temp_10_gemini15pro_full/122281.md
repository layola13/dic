mitochondrial:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriəl/ /ˌmaɪtoʊˈkɑːndriəl/| adj.|线粒体的|Relating to or characteristic of mitochondria, the organelles in cells responsible for energy production.|Mitochondrial DNA is inherited from the mother. 线粒体DNA遗传自母亲。|近义词:  cellular, cytoplasmic|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps extracellular)|词性: adjective

mitochondrion:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriən/ /ˌmaɪtoʊˈkɑːndriən/| n.|线粒体|An organelle found in large numbers in most cells, in which the biochemical processes of respiration and energy production occur. It has a double membrane, the inner layer being folded inward to form layers (cristae).|The mitochondrion is the powerhouse of the cell. 线粒体是细胞的能量工厂。|近义词: organelle|反义词: (No direct antonym exists)|词性: noun


词源: From Greek mitos “thread” + chondrion “granule”
