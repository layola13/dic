colorising:/ˈkʌləˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 着色，上色 | The process of adding color to a black and white image or film. | Example 1: They are colorising an old black and white movie. 他们正在给一部黑白老电影上色。
Example 2:  Colorising photos can bring them to life. 给照片上色可以让它们更生动。 | 近义词: coloring, colorizing | 反义词: desaturating, bleaching | 词性: 动词

colorising:/ˈkʌləˌraɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 着色 | The act or process of adding color. | Example 1: The colorising of the old photos took several hours. 老照片的着色工作花了几个小时。
Example 2:  Digital colorising has become increasingly sophisticated.  数字着色技术已经变得越来越复杂。| 近义词: coloring, colorization | 反义词: desaturation, bleaching | 词性: 名词
