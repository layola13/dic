panic:/ˈpænɪk/| n. v.| 恐慌，惊慌；使恐慌 |A sudden overwhelming fear, often causing wildly unthinking behavior.  |  The crowd panicked when the fire broke out. 人群在火灾爆发时惊慌失措。 | synonyms: fear, terror, alarm, fright | antonyms: calm, composure, tranquility | noun
panic:/ˈpænɪk/| v. |（使）恐慌，惊慌 |To be affected by or to inspire with panic. | The sound of the explosion panicked the horses. 爆炸声使马匹惊慌失措。 | synonyms: terrify, frighten, alarm, scare | antonyms: calm, soothe, reassure | verb
