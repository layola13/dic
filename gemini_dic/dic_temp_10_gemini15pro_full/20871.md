blaxploitation:/ˌblæksˌplɔɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |剥削黑人电影；黑人剥削电影|A genre of film, typically from the early 1970s, featuring black actors in stereotyped roles and often exploiting themes of violence, crime, and sexuality.|Example 1:  "Shaft" is considered a classic blaxploitation film.  《夏福特》被认为是一部经典的黑人剥削电影。

Example 2:  Many critics argue that blaxploitation films perpetuated harmful stereotypes. 许多评论家认为，剥削黑人电影延续了有害的刻板印象。|近义词:ghetto film|反义词:|词性:noun
