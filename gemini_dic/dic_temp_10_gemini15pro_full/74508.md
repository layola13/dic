frappeed:/fræˈpid/ | adj. | 冰镇的，加冰沙的 |  Covered with or blended with ice, typically a sweet flavored beverage. Origin: From the French "frappé," past participle of "frapper" (to strike, chill). It gained popularity in the US referring to blended iced coffee drinks. |  例句：I ordered a frappeed mocha with extra whipped cream. 我点了一杯加了额外奶油的冰沙摩卡咖啡。 | 近义词：iced, blended, chilled | 反义词：hot, warm | 词性：形容词
