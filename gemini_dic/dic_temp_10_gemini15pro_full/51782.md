devoto:/dəˈvoʊtoʊ/ (US), /dɪˈvəʊtəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|虔诚的，忠诚的；信徒|Deeply religious or devoted; a follower of a religion or cause. |She is a devoto of the opera. 她是一位歌剧的忠实爱好者。|近义词：pious, devout, dedicated, fervent, ardent, faithful, loyal|反义词：irreligious, faithless, disloyal, impious|adjective, noun

devoto:/dəˈvoʊtoʊ/ (US), /dɪˈvəʊtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|信徒，热心支持者|A follower or enthusiast of someone or something.|He is a devoto of the local football team. 他是当地足球队的忠实球迷。|近义词：follower, adherent, enthusiast, fan, admirer|反义词：critic, opponent, detractor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *devoto*, ultimately from Latin *dēvōtus* "dedicated, devoted," past participle of *dēvovēre* "to dedicate, vow," from *dē-* "down, completely" + *vovēre* "to vow."
