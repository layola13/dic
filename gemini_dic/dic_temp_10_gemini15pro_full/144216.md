outskating:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪtɪŋ/
verb
超越滑行，滑得比…快
The act of skating faster or more skillfully than someone else.
He easily outskated the other players on the ice. 他在冰上轻松地超越了其他选手。
Synonyms: outmaneuvering, surpassing
Antonyms: lagging behind
verb

outskating:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪtɪŋ/
gerund
超越滑行，滑得比…快
The act of skating faster or more skillfully than someone else.
Outskating the competition is his key to victory. 超越对手是他的胜利关键。
Synonyms: outmaneuvering, surpassing
Antonyms: lagging behind
