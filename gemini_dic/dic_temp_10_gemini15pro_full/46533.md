dalali: /dɑːˈlɑːli/ | n. | 中间人，经纪人，代理人 | A broker or agent, especially in real estate or other transactions.  Often used in East Africa. |  He worked as a dalali, connecting buyers and sellers of land. 他作为一名中间人，为土地买卖双方牵线搭桥。| Synonyms: broker, agent, intermediary, middleman | Antonyms: principal, buyer, seller | noun
