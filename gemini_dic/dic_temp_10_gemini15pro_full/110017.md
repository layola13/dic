libratory:/ˈlaɪbrətɔːri/ (US), /ˈlɪbrətəri/ (UK)| adj.|摆动的，振动的，摇摆的|Relating to oscillation or vibration, swinging back and forth.|The libratory motion of the pendulum was mesmerizing. 钟摆的摆动令人着迷。|近义词：oscillatory, vibrating, swinging, rocking|反义词：stationary, fixed, still|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "lībrātōrius," from "lībrāre" (to balance, swing) + "-tōrius" (forming adjectives).
