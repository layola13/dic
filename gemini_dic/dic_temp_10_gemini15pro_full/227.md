abdal:/ˈæbdɑːl/| n.|阿卜达尔（伊斯兰教苏菲派神秘主义者）|A Sufi mystic, especially one considered to be a saint.|He was revered as an abdal, a holy man with miraculous powers. 他被尊为阿卜达尔，一位拥有神奇力量的圣人。|近义词：saint, mystic, dervish|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic ʻabdāl (“substitutes”), plural of badal (“substitute”).  The Abdal are believed to be hidden saints who protect the world.

USAGE->
The abdals are often mentioned in Sufi literature. 阿卜达尔经常在苏菲派文学中被提及。

The concept of the abdal varies across different Sufi orders.  阿卜达尔的概念在不同的苏菲派教团中有所不同。
