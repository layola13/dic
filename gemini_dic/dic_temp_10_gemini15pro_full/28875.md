carpogenic:/kɑːrpəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
产果实的；子实体形成的
Relating to or involved in the production of fruit or fruiting bodies (especially in fungi).
Carpogenic structures are essential for fungal reproduction.  子实体结构对真菌繁殖至关重要。
The carpogenic germination of the spores allows for the development of new fruiting bodies. 孢子的产果萌发允许新子实体的发育。
近义词:fruiting, sporogenous
反义词:vegetative
词性:adjective
