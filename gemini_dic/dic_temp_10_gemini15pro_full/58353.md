dts:/diː tiː ɛs/| abbreviation |数字影院系统（Digital Theater Systems）|Digital Theater Systems, a multi-channel audio format commonly used in movie theaters and home theater systems.|The movie was shown in DTS for a more immersive sound experience. 这部电影以 DTS 格式放映，以获得更身临其境的音效体验。|近义词：surround sound, 5.1, 7.1|反义词:mono, stereo|n.
