complotment:/kəmˈplɒtmənt/ /kɑːmˈplɑːtmənt/
n.
阴谋，共谋，密谋
a secret plan by a group to do something unlawful or harmful.
They uncovered a complotment to overthrow the government. 他们揭露了一个推翻政府的阴谋。
conspiracy, plot, scheme, intrigue
machination
noun

complotment:/kəmˈplɒtmənt/ /kɑːmˈplɑːtmənt/
n.

a piece of land, especially a small one.
They have a small complotment of land where they grow vegetables. 他们有一小块地，用来种植蔬菜。
plot, allotment, patch, parcel
tract
noun
