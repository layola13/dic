abstain:/əbˈsteɪn/| v. |戒绝，弃权|To restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something.|He abstained from voting in the election. 他在选举中弃权了。

She abstains from alcohol. 她戒酒。|近义词：refrain, forbear, desist|反义词：indulge, partake|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abstinen, from Old French abstenir, from Latin abstinēre (“to hold back from, withhold oneself, abstain, refrain”), from abs- (“from, away from”) + tenēre (“to hold”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor advised him to abstain from smoking. = 医生建议他戒烟。
She abstains from eating meat. = 她不吃肉。

一般过去时:
He abstained from alcohol during his treatment. = 在治疗期间他戒酒了。
They abstained from voting on the new policy. = 他们没有对新政策进行投票。

现在完成时:
She has abstained from sugar for a month. = 她已经戒糖一个月了。
He has abstained from making any public statements. = 他一直没有发表任何公开声明。

过去完成时:
He had abstained from caffeine for several weeks before the race. = 在比赛前的几个星期里，他一直没有摄入咖啡因。
She had abstained from voting in the past. = 她过去弃权过。

将来时:
I will abstain from dessert tonight. = 我今晚不吃甜点。
They will abstain from commenting until the investigation is complete. = 他们将 abstain from commenting until the investigation is complete. = 调查结束前他们不会发表评论。

虚拟语气:
If I were to abstain from coffee, I would be very tired. = 如果我不喝咖啡，我会很累。
I wish I could abstain from social media. = 我希望我可以戒掉社交媒体。

被动语态:
Voting was abstained from by several members. = 一些成员弃权了。 (被动语态较不自然)
Further comment will be abstained from until more information is available. = 在获得更多信息之前，将不作进一步评论。(被动语态较不自然)

疑问句:
Why did you abstain from voting? = 你为什么弃权？
Will you abstain from alcohol during pregnancy? = 你在怀孕期间会戒酒吗？

否定句:
He did not abstain from expressing his opinion. = 他没有不表达他的意见。
She couldn't abstain from laughing. = 她忍不住笑了。
