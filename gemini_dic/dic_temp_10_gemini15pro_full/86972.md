hendiadys:/hɛnˈdaɪədɪs/| n.|  偏义复词，重言修饰|A figure of speech in which two words connected by a conjunction (usually *and*) express a single complex idea.  The two words could be synonyms, or related terms that emphasize different aspects of the same idea. |例句：He came with might and main (meaning with all his strength). 他使尽全力来了。

例句：They lived in peace and harmony. 他们生活在和谐之中。|近义词：tautology, redundancy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
