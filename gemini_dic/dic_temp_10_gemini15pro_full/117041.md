mcmanifest:/ˌɛm siː ˈmænɪfɛst/ | n. | Minecraft清单文件 | A file used in Minecraft mods that provides metadata about the mod, such as its name, version, dependencies, and the files it contains.  It's essential for mod loaders to understand and load mods correctly. | Example: The mod developer updated the mcmanifest to include the new textures.  这个模组开发者更新了mcmanifest以包含新的纹理。

Example: Without a valid mcmanifest, the mod loader won't be able to recognize the mod.  如果没有有效的mcmanifest，模组加载器将无法识别该模组。 | 近义词: mod metadata, manifest file | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
