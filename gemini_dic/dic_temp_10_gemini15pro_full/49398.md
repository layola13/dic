deliverabilities:/dɪˌlɪvərəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可交付成果，可交付物
Things that can be delivered, often referring to tangible or intangible products or services resulting from a project.
The project manager outlined the key deliverables for the next quarter. 项目经理概述了下一季度的主要可交付成果。
The deliverables for this project include a website, a mobile app, and a marketing plan.  这个项目交付成果包含一个网站，一个手机应用和一份营销计划。
Synonyms: outputs, results, products, achievements
Antonyms: inputs, resources
