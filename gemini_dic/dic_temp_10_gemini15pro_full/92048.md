hyperphosphorylated:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɒsfərɪleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度磷酸化的 |  Containing an excessive number of phosphate groups.  Hyper- means excessive, and phosphorylated refers to the addition of phosphate groups to a molecule, often a protein. |  The hyperphosphorylated tau protein is a hallmark of Alzheimer's disease. 过度磷酸化的tau蛋白是阿尔茨海默病的一个标志。| Hyperphosphorylation of certain proteins can lead to their dysfunction. 某些蛋白质的过度磷酸化会导致其功能障碍。 | overphosphorylated | hypophosphorylated | adjective
