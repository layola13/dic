deframes:/diːˈfreɪmz/| v. |去除框架，打破框架|To remove or break out of a restrictive framework, often referring to ideas or perspectives.|The artist deframed their work, challenging traditional notions of presentation.  艺术家将他们的作品去框架化，挑战了传统的展示观念。|例句：The new CEO is deframing the company's culture, encouraging more innovative thinking.  新任首席执行官正在打破公司文化的框架，鼓励更多创新思维。|近义词：deconstruct, dismantle, challenge, rethink|反义词: reinforce, uphold, maintain, preserve|词性:动词

deframes:/diːˈfreɪmz/| n. |去除框架的行为|The act of removing or breaking out of a restrictive framework.|The deframing of traditional gender roles is a complex and ongoing process.  打破传统性别角色的框架是一个复杂而持续的过程。|例句：The deframing of the curriculum allowed for more student choice and agency.  课程框架的打破允许学生有更多的选择和自主性。|近义词：deconstruction, dismantling, challenging|反义词: reinforcement, upholding, maintenance, preservation|词性:名词
