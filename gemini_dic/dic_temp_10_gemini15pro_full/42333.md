counter-title:/ˈkaʊntərˌtaɪtl/ | n. | 副标题；对立标题；反题 | A secondary or subordinate title; a title that contrasts with or opposes the main title. | The book had the counter-title "A Story of Resilience." 这本书的副标题是“一个关于韧性的故事”。 | synonym: subtitle, alternative title | antonym: main title, primary title | noun

counter-title:/ˈkaʊntərˌtaɪtl/ | adj. | 对立的；反的 | Opposing or contrasting with the main title.  This usage is less common but can be found in specific contexts. |  The counter-title argument presented a different perspective.  对立标题的论点提出了不同的视角。 | synonym: opposing, contrasting | antonym: supporting, conforming | adjective
