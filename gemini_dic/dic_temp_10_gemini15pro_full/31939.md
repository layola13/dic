chelerythrine: /ˌkɛlɪˈrɪθriːn/ | n. |  血根碱 | An alkaloid, C21H17NO4, obtained from the bloodroot plant (Sanguinaria canadensis) and other Papaveraceae species. It is used in biochemical research and has shown some activity against cancer cells. | Chelerythrine inhibits protein kinase C activity. 血根碱抑制蛋白激酶C活性。|  Chelerythrine is being investigated as a potential anticancer drug. 血根碱正在被研究作为一种潜在的抗癌药物。| Synonyms: sanguinarine (closely related but not identical), benzophenanthridine alkaloid | Antonyms: N/A | noun
