directorial:/ˌdɪrɛkˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
导演的；指导的
Relating to the work of a director.
His directorial debut was a critical success. 他的导演处女作获得了评论界的好评。
directorial style 导演风格
synonyms: directing, leading, managerial, supervisory
antonyms: subordinate, following
