competitioner:/kəmˈpɛtɪtər/| n. |竞争者，参赛者|A person who takes part in a sporting contest or other competition.|例句：All the competitors lined up at the starting line. 所有的参赛者都排在了起跑线上。|例句：She's a fierce competitor and always strives to win. 她是一个强劲的竞争者，总是力求胜利。|近义词：rival, contestant, opponent, participant|反义词:ally, teammate, partner|词性:noun
Etymology: From compete (v.) + -or (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or doer).  Ultimately from Latin competere "to strive together."
