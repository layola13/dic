mare:/mɛər/ | n. | 母马; 噩梦 | A female horse; An unpleasant dream. |  例句：The mare gave birth to a healthy foal. 这匹母马生了一匹健康的小马驹。

例句：He woke up in a cold sweat after a terrible mare. 他做了一个可怕的噩梦，醒来时一身冷汗。| 近义词：(for female horse) broodmare, dam; (for nightmare) incubus, bad dream | 反义词：(for female horse) stallion, colt; (for nightmare) pleasant dream | 词性: noun

词源: From Old English *mere, from Proto-Germanic *marhijǭ, from Proto-Indo-European *márḱos (“horse”). Cognate with Old Norse merr (“mare”), Old High German meri (“mare”), Latin marcus (“stallion”).
