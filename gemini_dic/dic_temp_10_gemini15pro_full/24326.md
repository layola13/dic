brilliantness's:/ˈbrɪljəntnɪsɪz/
n.|才华，才智，光辉|The brilliance of the diamond dazzled her. 钻石的光辉使她眼花缭乱。|近义词：genius, talent, radiance, splendor|反义词：dullness, mediocrity, dimness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *brilliantnes, from Anglo-Norman brilliantnes, from Old French brilliant, from briller (“to shine”), from Latin beryllus (“beryl”).
