antioptimism:/ˌæntiˈɒptɪmɪzəm/ | n. | 反乐观主义；悲观主义 | The philosophy or belief that the world is fundamentally bad or getting worse.  A tendency to expect the worst. |  His antioptimism made it difficult for him to celebrate any victories. 他的反乐观主义使他很难庆祝任何胜利。| pessimism, cynicism, defeatism | optimism, hopefulness | noun
