import:/ɪmˈpɔːrt/ | n. v. |进口, 输入; 重要性|To bring (goods or services) into a country from abroad for sale.|

例句：The US imports a lot of oil from other countries. 美国从其他国家进口大量石油。

例句：We import coffee beans from Colombia. 我们从哥伦比亚进口咖啡豆。

近义词：bring in, introduce, receive | 反义词: export, ship out | 词性: noun, verb

import:/ɪmˈpɔːrt/ | n. |重要性, 意义|The meaning or significance of something, especially with regard to the influence it may have.|

例句：The import of his words was not immediately clear. 他话里的含义没有立即明了。

例句：The meeting was of great import to the future of the company.  这次会议对公司的未来至关重要。

近义词：significance, importance, meaning | 反义词: insignificance, unimportance | 词性: noun
