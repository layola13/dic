nocturnal:/nɑːkˈtɜːrnl/ | adj. |夜间的，夜行的|Active at night.  Relating to or occurring in the night.| Bats are nocturnal creatures.  蝙蝠是夜行动物。| 近义词: nightly, nighttime | 反义词: diurnal, daytime | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin nocturnus, from nox (night).
