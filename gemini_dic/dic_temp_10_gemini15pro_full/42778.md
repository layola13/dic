countywide:/ˈkaʊntiwaɪd/
adjective
全县的，遍及全县的
Extending throughout a county.
The countywide vote resulted in a new tax levy.  全县的投票导致了一项新的税收征收。
The sheriff's department implemented a countywide search for the missing child. 警长部门在全县范围内展开了对失踪儿童的搜寻。
Synonyms: county-wide,  across the county
Antonyms: local, localized
词性: adjective
