confessors:/kənˈfɛsərz/ | n. | 忏悔者；告解者；听忏悔的神父 |  A person who confesses their sins, especially to a priest. Also, a priest who hears confessions and gives absolution. | The confessors listened patiently to the penitents' stories. 忏悔者耐心地听着忏悔者的故事。 |  The king sought guidance from his confessors. 国王向他的告解神父寻求指导。 | synonyms: penitent, confesser, priest | antonyms: unbeliever, sinner | noun
