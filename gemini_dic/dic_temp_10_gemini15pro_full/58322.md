drying:/ˈdraɪɪŋ/| adj.  v.|干燥的，变干的；使干燥，晒干|The process of removing moisture.  The act of becoming dry.|例句：The drying clothes flapped in the wind.  晾晒的衣服在风中飘动。|近义词：dehydrating, airing, parching|反义词:wetting, moistening, soaking|词性:adjective, present participle of dry (verb)

Etymology: From Old English *drugian, from Proto-Germanic *druzjaną (“to dry”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰreus- (“to fall, to flow”). Related to dry.
