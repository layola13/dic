amability:/ˌeɪməˈbɪləti/ | n. |和蔼；友善；亲切|The quality of being friendly, pleasant, and easy to get along with. |She was known for her amability and generosity. 她以和蔼可亲和慷慨大方而闻名。|Synonyms: affability, amiability, cordiality, friendliness, geniality |Antonyms: coldness, hostility, unfriendliness |noun
