evidentiality:/ˌɛvɪˌdɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | n. |  证据性; 表明来源 |  The grammatical marking of the source of information, indicating how the speaker knows what they are saying.  | The verb forms in the language express evidentiality, showing whether the speaker saw the event, heard about it, or inferred it.  说话者通过语言的动词形式表达证据性，表明他们是亲眼目睹了事件、听说了事件还是推断出来的。| Synonym: source-marking | Antonym: N/A | noun


evidential:/ˌɛvɪˈdɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 证据的; 表明来源的 | Relating to or providing evidence. | The evidential markers in the sentence indicate that the speaker learned the information from someone else. 句子中的证据标记表明说话者是从别人那里得知信息的。| Synonym: probative, evidentiary | Antonym: N/A | adjective
