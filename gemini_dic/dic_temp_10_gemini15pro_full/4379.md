aladinist:/əˈlædɪnɪst/ | n. | 阿拉丁主义者；主张自由贸易和放任政策的人 | A supporter of free trade and laissez-faire policies, often with an emphasis on deregulation and globalization, sometimes with negative connotations suggesting exploitation or disregard for social consequences. |  Example 1:  The aladinists argued that removing trade barriers would benefit everyone. 阿拉丁主义者认为，消除贸易壁垒将使所有人受益。

Example 2: Critics labeled the new economic policy as a form of aladinism, predicting it would lead to job losses and social inequality. 批评家将新的经济政策贴上阿拉丁主义的标签，预测这将导致失业和社会不平等。 | 近义词: free trader,  deregulationist, globalist | 反义词: protectionist, interventionist | 词性: 名词
