fittedness:/ˈfɪtɪdnəs/| n. |合适性，适当性，贴切性|The state or quality of being fitted or suitable; appropriateness.|The fittedness of the punishment to the crime was questioned. 对判决是否与罪行相符提出了质疑。|近义词：suitability, appropriateness, aptness, fitness|反义词：unsuitability, inappropriateness, inaptness, unfitness|词性:名词

Etymology: From "fitted" + "-ness". "Fitted" comes from the verb "fit," which has Old Norse roots.  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
