grogs:/ɡrɒɡz/| n.|烈酒，掺水朗姆酒（通常指过去海军配给的）|A beverage traditionally made of rum diluted with water, often served hot and spiced, especially formerly in the British navy.|The sailors lined up for their daily ration of grogs. 水手们排队领取每日的朗姆酒配给。|Synonyms: rum, liquor, spirits|Antonyms: soft drink, juice|noun
