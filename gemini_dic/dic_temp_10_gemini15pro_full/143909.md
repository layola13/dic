outpraying:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过祈祷,祈祷更久
Praying longer or more fervently than someone else.
She believed she could outpray her rivals and secure the blessing. 她相信她可以比她的对手祈祷更久，从而获得祝福。
近义词: outdo in prayer
反义词: being outprayed
词性: verb (present participle)
