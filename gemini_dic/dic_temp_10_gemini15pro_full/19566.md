bigamist:/ˈbɪɡəmɪst/| n. |重婚者|A person who commits the crime of bigamy (marrying someone while already legally married to another person).|例句：The bigamist was eventually caught when his two wives met at a parent-teacher conference.  这个重婚者最终在他的两个妻子在家长会上相遇时被抓住了。

例句：She discovered she was married to a bigamist after finding photos of another wedding on his phone. 她在他手机上发现了另一场婚礼的照片后，发现她嫁给了一个重婚者。|近义词：polygamist|反义词: monogamist|词性: noun
