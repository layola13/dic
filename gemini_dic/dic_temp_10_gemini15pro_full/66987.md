expertised:/ˌɛkspərˈtiːzd/ | v. | 鉴定了, 成为专家 |  Having been assessed by an expert.  The painting was expertised by Sotheby's. 这幅画由苏富比拍卖行进行了鉴定。| 近义词: appraised, evaluated, assessed | 反义词: unverified, unassessed | 词性: 动词

词源:  Derived from the noun "expertise" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Expertise" comes from French "expertise," from "expert."  "Expert" ultimately comes from Latin "expertus," past participle of "experiri" (to try, test).
