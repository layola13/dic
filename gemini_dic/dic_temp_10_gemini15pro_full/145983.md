overdoers:/ˌoʊvərˈduːərz/
| n. | 过度者；做得过分的人 | People who overdo something; those who go to extremes or exaggerate. |  The overdoers at the gym often end up injured.  健身房里那些过度锻炼的人经常最终受伤。| synonyms: extremists, fanatics, zealots | antonyms: moderates, underachievers | noun


overdo:/ˌoʊvərˈduː/
| vt. | 做得过分；过度；夸张 | To do something to excess; to exaggerate. | She overdid the makeup and looked like a clown. 她化了过分的妆，看起来像个小丑。| synonyms: exaggerate, overact, go overboard | antonyms: understate, minimize, downplay | verb
