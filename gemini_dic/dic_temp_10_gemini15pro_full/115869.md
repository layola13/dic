marler:/ˈmɑːrlər/ | n. | 马勒；泥瓦匠；砌砖工 | A person who works with marl (a type of soil).  A person who builds with brick or stone. | The marler skillfully laid the bricks for the new wall.  泥瓦匠熟练地为新墙砌砖。| 近义词: bricklayer, mason, builder | 反义词: demolisher | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *marlere, from marl + -er.  Ultimately from Old French marle.
