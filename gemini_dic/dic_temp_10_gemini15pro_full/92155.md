hypersentimental:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 过于感伤的，过度多愁善感的 | Excessively sentimental; overly emotional or nostalgic. |  She wrote a hypersentimental poem about her childhood pet. 她写了一首关于她童年宠物的过于感伤的诗。 |  The movie was so hypersentimental that I couldn't help but roll my eyes. 这部电影太感伤了，我不禁翻白眼。 |  sentimental, mawkish, maudlin | unsentimental, pragmatic, stoic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (prefix meaning 'excessive, over') + sentimental (from French sentiment, ultimately from Latin sentire 'to feel').
