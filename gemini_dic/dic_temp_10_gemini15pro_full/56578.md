dompas:/ˈdɒmpɑs/| n.|通行证，身份证（尤指南非种族隔离时期黑人必须携带的身份证）|A document formerly required for black South Africans, similar to an internal passport, controlling their movement.|例句：During apartheid, black South Africans were required to carry a dompas at all times.种族隔离时期，南非黑人必须随时携带通行证。

例句：The dompas was a symbol of oppression and control.通行证是压迫和控制的象征。|近义词：pass, permit, identification document|反义词:None|词性:noun
