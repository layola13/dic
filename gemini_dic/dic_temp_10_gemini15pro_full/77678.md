gelsemine:/ˈdʒɛlsɪmiːn/
n.
钩吻碱
A highly toxic alkaloid derived from the gelsemium plant.
Gelsemine is a potent neurotoxin. 钩吻碱是一种强效神经毒素。
Gelsemine poisoning can cause respiratory failure. 钩吻碱中毒会导致呼吸衰竭。
近义词: neurotoxin, poison
反义词: antidote
