mikado:/mɪˈkɑːdoʊ/
n.|天皇；(尤指旧时日本的)皇帝；一种儿童游戏|A term, now largely obsolete, for the emperor of Japan.  It derives from the Japanese word "mikado" (御門), meaning "honorable gate," a metonym for the imperial palace and thus the emperor himself. The game Mikado involves picking up sticks without disturbing others.|The Mikado was a powerful figure in Japanese history.  天皇在日本历史上是一个强大的角色。|synonyms: emperor, sovereign, ruler|antonyms: subject, commoner|noun
