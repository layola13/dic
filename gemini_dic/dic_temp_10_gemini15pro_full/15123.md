bachelor:/ˈbætʃələr/ (US) /ˈbætʃələ/ (UK)| n.|单身汉; 学士; 未婚男子|A man who is not married.  A person who holds a bachelor's degree from a college or university.|例句：He remained a bachelor all his life. 他终身未娶。

例句：He earned his bachelor's degree in physics. 他获得了物理学学士学位。|近义词：single man, unmarried man, graduate|反义词: husband, married man|词性:noun

bachelors: /ˈbætʃəlɚz/ (US) /ˈbætʃələz/ (UK) | n. |单身汉们; 学士学位们|Plural form of "bachelor". Refers to multiple unmarried men or multiple bachelor's degrees.|例句：The bachelors lived together in a shared apartment. 这些单身汉们合住在同一间公寓里。

例句：They all received their bachelors in engineering. 他们都获得了工程学学士学位。|近义词：single men, unmarried men, graduates|反义词: husbands, married men|词性:noun
