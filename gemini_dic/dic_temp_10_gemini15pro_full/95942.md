incubator: /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪtər/ (US) /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪtə/ (UK)| n.|孵化器；保育箱；培养皿；创业孵化器|An apparatus used to hatch eggs or grow microorganisms under controlled conditions; also used figuratively to describe a place or situation that fosters development or innovation.|The hospital has several incubators for premature babies.  医院有几个早产儿保育箱。|The university's business incubator helps students develop their startup ideas. 这所大学的创业孵化器帮助学生发展他们的创业想法。|Synonyms: hatchery, brooder, nurturer|Antonyms:  N/A|noun
