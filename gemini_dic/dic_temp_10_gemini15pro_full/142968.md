outbabbled:/ˌaʊtˈbæbəld/
verb
胜过…的饶舌；说得比…更多；比…更健谈
to talk more, longer, or more persuasively than someone else
He outbabbled her in the debate.  他在辩论中说得比她多。
Synonyms: outtalk, outspeak
Antonyms: be quiet, listen
词性:动词
