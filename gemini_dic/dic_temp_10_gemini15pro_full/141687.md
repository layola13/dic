opulency:/ˈɒpjʊlənsɪ/ /ˈɑːpjələnsɪ/| n. | 富裕，奢华，丰富 | Great wealth or luxuriousness. |  The opulency of the ancient Roman emperors is legendary. 古罗马皇帝的奢华生活是传奇。

The hotel was characterized by its sheer opulency. 这家酒店的特点是极致奢华。 | 近义词：luxury, affluence, richness, lavishness, extravagance | 反义词：poverty, austerity, simplicity, modesty | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *opulentia, from Latin opulentus (“wealthy”), from ops (“power, resources, wealth”).
