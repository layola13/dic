drove:/droʊv/| v. | 驱赶，驾驶，迫使 |Past tense of drive.  Meaning to operate and control the movement of a vehicle or animal. Also, to force or urge someone into a particular state or course of action. |He drove the cattle to the market. 他把牛赶到市场。|近义词：herded, steered, compelled, propelled |反义词：followed, trailed


drove:/droʊv/| n. |畜群，人群 |A large number of people or animals moving together. |A drove of tourists descended upon the small town. 一大群游客涌入小镇。|近义词：herd, flock, crowd, swarm |反义词：individual, scattering


词性:
drove can be both a verb (past tense of drive) and a noun.
