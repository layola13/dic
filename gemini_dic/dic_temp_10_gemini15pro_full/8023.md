anthracometric:/ˌænθrəkəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
煤尘测量的；炭肺测量的
Relating to the measurement of coal dust or anthracosis (black lung disease).
Anthracometric measurements are crucial for assessing the risk of black lung disease in coal miners. 煤尘测量对于评估煤矿工人患黑肺病的风险至关重要。
The study used anthracometric methods to analyze lung tissue samples. 这项研究使用煤尘测量方法来分析肺组织样本。
近义词:  pulmonary, respiratory (related to lungs and breathing, not specifically to coal dust measurement)
反义词: None (There isn't a direct antonym for this specific term.)
词性: adjective
词源:  From Greek *anthrax* "coal" + *-metric* "measuring."
