outbartered: /ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtərd/
verb
以物易物胜过，用 barter 比别人换得更多
exceeded in bartering; traded more successfully than another.
He outbartered all the other traders at the market, securing the finest silks and spices. 他在市场上比所有其他商人交易得都更好，获得了最好的丝绸和香料。
Synonyms: outtraded, outsmarted in trading
Antonyms: outbid, overpaid in trading
词性: 动词
