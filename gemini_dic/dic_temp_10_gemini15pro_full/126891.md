narcissist:/ˈnɑːrsɪsɪst/ (US) /ˈnɑːsɪsɪst/ (UK)| n.|自恋者，自负的人|A person who has an excessive admiration of themselves. |例句：He's such a narcissist, he only talks about himself. 他真是个自恋狂，只谈论自己。

例句：She's a classic narcissist – always seeking attention and admiration. 她是个典型的自恋者——总是寻求关注和赞美。

近义词：egotist, self-absorbed person, conceited person|反义词:  humble person, modest person|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Narcissus, a figure in Greek mythology who fell in love with his own reflection.
