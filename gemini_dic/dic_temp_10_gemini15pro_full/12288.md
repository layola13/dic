ascidiarium:/æsɪˈdɪəriəm/
n.|海鞘养殖场；海鞘缸|A structure or tank for raising or keeping ascidians (sea squirts).|The marine biologist kept a meticulously clean ascidiarium to study the reproductive cycles of the sea squirts.  海洋生物学家维护着一个一丝不苟的干净的海鞘缸，以研究海鞘的繁殖周期。|近义词:aquarium, tank|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *ascidium* (sea squirt) + *-arium* (place for). The Latin *ascidium* ultimately derives from Greek *askidion*, diminutive of *askos* (wineskin, bag), referring to the shape of the sea squirt.
