atomicity:/ˌætəˈmɪsəti/
noun
原子性
The property of a transaction to be treated as a single, indivisible unit of work.  It ensures that all changes within a transaction are either committed or rolled back, preventing partial updates to the database.
Database transactions must have atomicity to maintain data integrity. 数据库事务必须具有原子性以维护数据完整性。
Near Synonyms: indivisibility, unity, all-or-nothing
Antonyms: divisibility, separability
词性: 名词


atomicities:/ˌætəˈmɪsətiz/
noun
原子性（复数）
The plural form of atomicity, referring to multiple instances of the property of transactions being treated as single, indivisible units of work.
The atomicities of the various transactions were carefully analyzed.  各种事务的原子性都经过了仔细的分析。
Near Synonyms: indivisibilities, unities
Antonyms: divisibilities, separabilities
词性: 名词
