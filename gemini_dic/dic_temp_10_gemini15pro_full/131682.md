nonconclusive:/ˌnɒnkənˈkluːsɪv/ | /ˌnɑːnkənˈkluːsɪv/ | adj. | 非决定性的；非结论性的；无定论的 | Not leading to a firm conclusion; not decisive. | The evidence was nonconclusive, so the jury could not reach a verdict.  证据不确凿，所以陪审团无法作出裁决。| The results of the study were nonconclusive.  这项研究的结果尚无定论。 | inconclusive, indecisive, ambiguous | conclusive, decisive, definitive | adjective
