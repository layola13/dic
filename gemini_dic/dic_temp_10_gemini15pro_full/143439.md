outflowed:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊd/
verb
流出, 外流
past participle of outflow (meaning to flow out)
Large amounts of capital outflowed from the country during the economic crisis. 经济危机期间，大量资金从该国流出。
Synonyms:  discharged, emanated, issued, streamed
Antonyms:  inflowed,  inundated
词性: 动词 (past participle)


outflow:/ˈaʊtfloʊ/
noun
流出, 外流
The outflow of funds from the stock market has been significant.  资金从股市流出非常显著。
Synonyms: efflux, discharge, exodus
Antonyms: inflow, influx
词性: 名词
