feasted:/ˈfiːstɪd/| v.| 宴请；尽情享用；饱餐 |  past tense and past participle of feast | They feasted on roast turkey and all the trimmings. 他们享用了烤火鸡和各种配菜。| 近义词：celebrated, indulged, reveled | 反义词：starved, fasted | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Middle English *festen*, from Old French *fester* "to feast, celebrate," from Vulgar Latin *festare*, from Latin *festum* "festival, feast."
