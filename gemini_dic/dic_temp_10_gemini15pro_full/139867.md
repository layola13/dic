inoculate:/ɪˈnɑːkjəleɪt/ /ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪt/
verb
接种，灌输
to introduce something into the mind of (someone)
例句：The doctor inoculated the children against measles. 医生给孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。
例句：He tried to inoculate his children with the right attitudes. 他试图给他的孩子们灌输正确的态度。
近义词：vaccinate, immunize, inject, introduce, implant
反义词：infect, contaminate
词性：verb

inoculated:/ɪˈnɑːkjəleɪtɪd/ /ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
接种过的，被灌输的
having been inoculated
例句：The inoculated plants showed resistance to the disease. 接种后的植物对这种疾病表现出抵抗力。
例句：He seemed inoculated against all criticism. 他似乎对所有批评都免疫了。
近义词：vaccinated, immunized, protected
反义词：unvaccinated, unprotected, susceptible
词性：adjective
