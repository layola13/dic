dolloping:/ˈdɑːləpɪŋ/ | n. | 一大团，一大块 | A large, shapeless amount of something, typically soft food. |  She added a dolloping of whipped cream to the pie.  她往派上加了一大块生奶油。|  glob, blob, lump, spoonful |  smidgen, dash | noun


dollop:/ˈdɑːləp/ | vt. |  舀，盛，加 | To serve or add (a soft or semi-liquid substance) in a large shapeless mass. | He dolloped some mashed potatoes onto his plate. 他往盘子里盛了一大勺土豆泥。|  spoon, ladle, serve, plop |  skimp, sprinkle | verb
