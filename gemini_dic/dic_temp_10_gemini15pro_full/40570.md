contusion:/kənˈtjuːʒən/ /kənˈtuːʒn/| n. | 挫伤 | An injury in which the skin is not broken; a bruise. | The doctor diagnosed the injury as a severe contusion. 医生诊断该损伤为严重挫伤。 | Synonyms: bruise, injury | Antonyms: healing | noun
