overcommends:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmendz/
verb
过分推荐；过度赞扬
praises excessively; recommends too highly
The manager overcommends his employees, making it difficult to distinguish truly exceptional performance. 经理过度赞扬他的员工，使得很难区分真正出色的表现。
He overcommends the restaurant, setting unrealistic expectations for his friends. 他过分推荐这家餐厅，给他朋友们设定了不切实际的期望。
近义词: overpraise, exaggerate, oversell
反义词: understate, downplay, criticize
词性: verb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "commend" (verb meaning "praise")
