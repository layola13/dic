crawling:/ˈkrɔːlɪŋ/| adj. n.|爬行的，缓慢的；爬行|The baby was crawling on the floor. 婴儿在地板上爬行。|例句：The traffic was crawling along the highway.  交通在高速公路上缓慢行驶。|近义词：creeping, inching|反义词: sprinting, running|adjective

crawling:/ˈkrɔːlɪŋ/| n.|爬行|The baby's crawling was getting faster. 婴儿爬得越来越快了。|例句：The crawling of the insects made her skin crawl. 昆虫的爬行让她起鸡皮疙瘩。|近义词：creeping, inching|反义词: walking, running|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crawlen*, frequentative of *creulen* "to crawl," from Old Norse *krylja* "to crawl," from Proto-Germanic *krupjaną* "to creep, crawl."  Related to creep.
