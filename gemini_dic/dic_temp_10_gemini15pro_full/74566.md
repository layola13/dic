fraught:/frɔːt/| adj.|令人担忧的|Causing or affected by anxiety or stress.|She looked fraught. 她看起来很焦虑。|Synonyms: anxious, worried, stressed, distressed|Antonyms: relaxed, calm, composed|adjective
