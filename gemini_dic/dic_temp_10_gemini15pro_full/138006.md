nooklet: /ˈnʊklɪt/
n.
小角落；小隐蔽处
A small nook; a cozy little corner.
She curled up with a book in a quiet nooklet of the library. 她蜷缩在图书馆一个安静的小角落里看书。
corner, alcove, niche, recess
