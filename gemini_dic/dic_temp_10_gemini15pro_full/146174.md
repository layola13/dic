overexaggerated: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪd/
| adj. | 过分夸大的，夸张过度的 | Exaggerated to an excessive degree. | Her story was so overexaggerated that no one believed her. 她的故事太夸张了，以至于没有人相信她。 | The pain was overexaggerated for dramatic effect. 为了戏剧效果，疼痛被夸大了。 | overstated, overblown, inflated, amplified | understated, downplayed | adjective
