increaseful: /ɪnˈkriːsfʊl/ | adj. |  增加的，增长的 |  Tending to increase; promoting increase.  This word is considered archaic or obsolete and is rarely used in modern English. |  "The increaseful bounty of the harvest brought prosperity to the village." (This example is contrived as the word is not common.) | 近义词: increasing, growing, expanding, augmenting | 反义词: decreasing, diminishing, declining, shrinking | 词性: adjective


词源:  From "increase" + "-ful" (suffix indicating abundance or causing the specified action).
