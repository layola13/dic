colophonic: /ˌkɒləˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌkɑːləˈfɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 出版商标记的；书籍末尾的 | Relating to a colophon (a publisher's emblem or imprint, often placed at the end of a book).  |  This rare edition features a beautiful colophonic device. 这本珍稀版本有一个漂亮的出版商标记。 | Synonyms: concluding, final, terminal | Antonyms: introductory, beginning, initial | adjective

colophon: /ˈkɒləfən/ /ˈkɑːləfɑːn/ | n. | (书籍末尾的)出版商标记，版本记录 | A statement at the end of a book giving information about its publication, such as the printer and date. | The colophon revealed that the book was printed in Venice in 1522. 版本记录显示这本书是1522年在威尼斯印刷的。 | Synonyms: imprint, emblem, device, logo | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "preface" or "introduction" as opposing locations in a book) | noun
