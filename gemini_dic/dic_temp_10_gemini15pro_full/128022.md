neptune:/ˈnɛptjuːn/ (US) /ˈnɛptʃuːn/ (UK)| n.|海王星|The eighth planet from the Sun, named after the Roman god of the sea.|例句：Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun in our solar system. 海王星是太阳系中距离太阳最远的行星。

例句：Voyager 2 was the only spacecraft to fly by Neptune. 旅行者2号是唯一飞越海王星的航天器。

近义词: (As a planet, it has no direct synonyms, but in mythology) Poseidon (Greek equivalent)|反义词: None|词性:Noun
