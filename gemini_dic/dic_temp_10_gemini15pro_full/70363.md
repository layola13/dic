fieri:/ˈfɪəriː/
adjective
fiery
火热的；激烈的；暴躁的
Characterized by or as if by fire; intensely emotional; easily angered.
Her fiery personality made her a formidable opponent in debates. 她火热的性格使她成为辩论中一个强大的对手。
近义词: passionate, ardent, fervent, impetuous, spirited
反义词: calm, placid, apathetic, impassive
词性: adjective

fieri:/ˈfɪəriː/
noun (plural fieri)
market day; fair
集市日；庙会
A day on which a market or fair is regularly held.
The annual fieri attracted merchants and entertainers from all over the kingdom.  一年一度的庙会吸引了来自全国各地的商人和艺人。
近义词: market, fair, bazaar
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "weekday" in some contexts)
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *feriæ* "holidays, festivals," plural of *feria* "holiday, festival," related to *festive* and *feast*.  The word evolved in Italian to mean specifically a market day or fair held on a holiday.
