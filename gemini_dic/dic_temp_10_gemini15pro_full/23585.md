brakeless:/ˈbreɪklɪs/| adj.|无闸的，无制动的|Without brakes; lacking a braking mechanism.  This typically refers to vehicles, particularly bicycles or other wheeled conveyances, that do not have brakes installed or functional.|He rode a brakeless bike down the hill. 他骑着一辆没有刹车的自行车下山。|例句：Riding a brakeless bicycle can be very dangerous. 骑没有刹车的自行车很危险。|近义词：unbraked|反义词: braked|词性:形容词
