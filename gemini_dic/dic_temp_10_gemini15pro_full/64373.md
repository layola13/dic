erectilities: /ɪˌrɛkˈtɪlɪtiz/
noun
勃起功能；勃起状态
The ability to achieve and maintain an erection.  This term usually refers to the capability for penile erection, but can also refer to clitoral erection.
He experienced difficulties with his erectilities. 他勃起功能出现了问题.
近义词: erections, potency
反义词: impotence, erectile dysfunction
词性: noun
