non-septic:/nɒnˈsɛptɪk/ | adj. | 非败血症的，无菌的 | Not relating to or caused by sepsis; free from bacteria that cause disease. | The wound was cleaned thoroughly to ensure it remained non-septic.  伤口被彻底清洁以确保它保持无菌状态。| Example 2:  Non-septic arthritis can be caused by various factors, including wear and tear on the joints. 非败血性关节炎可以由多种因素引起，包括关节的磨损。 | Synonyms: aseptic, sterile | Antonyms: septic, infected | adjective
