dissolutions:/ˌdɪsəˈluːʃənz/
n.
解散，溶解，解体
the act of formally ending a parliament, marriage, business arrangement, etc.
The dissolution of the parliament was announced by the president. 总统宣布解散议会。

The dissolution of the company resulted in job losses. 公司的解散导致了失业。

近义词：termination, disbandment, breakup, disintegration
反义词：establishment, formation, union, solidification
词性: 名词 (plural)
