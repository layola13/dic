exaggerating:/ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
夸大;夸张
representing something as being larger, better, or worse than it really is.
He was exaggerating when he said he was the best player on the team. 他说他是队里最好的球员，这是在夸大其词。
overstating, embellishing, amplifying
understating, downplaying
v.
