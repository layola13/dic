admired: /ədˈmaɪərd/
adjective
敬佩的，受人赞赏的
Regarded with respect or warm approval.
She was much admired for her courage. = 她因勇气而备受赞赏。
His work was much admired by critics. = 他的作品受到了评论家的高度赞扬。

近义词: respected, esteemed, revered, lauded, praised
反义词: despised, scorned, loathed, disliked

词性: adjective
词源: From the verb admire, which comes from the Latin admirari "to wonder at, marvel at," from ad- "to" + mirari "to wonder."
