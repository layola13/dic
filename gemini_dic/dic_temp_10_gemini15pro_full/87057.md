heptagons:/ˈheptəɡɑːnz/
|n.|七边形|A plane figure with seven straight sides and seven angles. |A beehive’s cells are hexagons, not heptagons.  蜂巢的单元是六边形，不是七边形。|
| |The sum of the interior angles of a heptagon is 900 degrees.  七边形的内角和是900度。|
| |polygon, septagon| |noun|
