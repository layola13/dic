cymbalists: /ˈsɪmbəlɪsts/
n.
铙钹演奏者
A person who plays the cymbals.
The cymbalists added a dramatic flourish to the orchestral piece.  铙钹演奏者为管弦乐曲增添了戏剧性的 flourish。
The band's cymbalists were incredibly talented.  乐队的铙钹演奏者才华横溢。
Synonym: percussionist
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "silent musician")
