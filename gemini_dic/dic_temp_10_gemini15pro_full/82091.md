groan:/ɡroʊn/ (US) /ɡrəʊn/ (UK)| n. v. | 呻吟声；抱怨声；发出呻吟声；抱怨 | A deep inarticulate sound conveying pain, despair, or disapproval.  | The patient groaned in pain. 病人痛苦地呻吟。 | 近义词:moan, sigh, complain, gripe | 反义词: cheer, applaud | 词性: 名词; 动词

词源: From Middle English *grone, from Old English grānian (“to groan, lament”), from Proto-Germanic *granōną (“to groan”).
