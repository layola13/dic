cunette:/kjuːˈnɛt/ | n. |水沟，边沟，路边排水沟|A ditch, especially one running along the side of a road, for drainage.|The cunette alongside the highway was filled with debris after the storm. 公路旁的水沟在暴风雨后充满了碎屑。|The workers cleared the cunette to improve drainage. 工人们清理了边沟以改善排水。|近义词：ditch, gutter, channel, drain|反义词:ridge, mound|名词
