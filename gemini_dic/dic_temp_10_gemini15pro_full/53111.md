diphyllous:/daɪˈfɪləs/
adjective
二叶的；双叶的
Having two leaves, petals, or sepals.
The diphyllous calyx is a distinguishing feature of this plant species.  这种植物的二裂花萼是其区别特征。
This rare orchid has a diphyllous perianth.  这种稀有的兰花有一个双叶的花被。
近义词: two-leaved, bilobed
反义词: polyphyllous, many-leaved
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *di- "two" + *phyllon "leaf"
