intercrust:/ˈɪntərkrʌst/ | adj. | 地壳间的，地壳内部的 | Existing or occurring between layers of the Earth's crust. |  The intercrustal earthquake surprised geologists. 地壳间的这次地震让地质学家们感到惊讶。|  The intercrustal magma flow contributed to the volcanic eruption. 地壳内部的岩浆流动导致了火山爆发。 | 近义词: intracrustal, subcrustal | 反义词: surface, supercrustal | 词性: adjective

词源: inter- (between) + crust (Earth's crust)
