felspathic:/fɛlˈspæθɪk/
adjective
长石的；含长石的
Containing or relating to feldspar, a common group of crystalline minerals typically made of aluminum silicates of potassium, sodium, or calcium.
The geologist examined the felspathic rock.  地质学家检查了这块长石质的岩石。
The soil is felspathic and drains well.  这种土壤富含长石，排水良好。
Near synonyms: feldspathic
Antonyms: None readily available.
