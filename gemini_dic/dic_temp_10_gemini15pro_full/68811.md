fasciculate:/ˈfæsɪkjʊleɪt/ /ˈfæsɪkjəleɪt/| adj. v.|簇生的，成束的；使成束|Growing in a bundle or cluster; to form into bundles or clusters.|The nerve fibers fasciculate to form a nerve.|神经纤维成束形成神经。|近义词：clustered, bundled, grouped|反义词:separate, dispersed|词性:形容词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fasciculus (small bundle, diminutive of fascis, bundle), from Proto-Italic *faski-, perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *bhasko- (band, tie).
