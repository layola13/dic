dowset:/ˈdaʊsɪt/ | n. |红脸灰雀|A small, gregarious, pale brown finch, *Pyrrhula pyrrhula*, with a black cap and (in the male) a bright pink breast, breeding in Europe and Asia and wintering south to the Mediterranean and the Himalayas.|The dowset is a beautiful bird with distinctive markings. 红脸灰雀是一种美丽的鸟，有独特的斑纹。|近义词：bullfinch|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

dowset:/ˈdaʊsɪt/ | n. |(指葡萄酒的)沉淀物|Sediment in wine, especially port.|The dowset in the old port was quite thick.  这瓶老波特酒里的沉淀物很厚。|近义词：lees, sediment|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
