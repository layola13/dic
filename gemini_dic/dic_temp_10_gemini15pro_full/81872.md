griefs:/ɡriːfs/
n.
悲伤，悲痛；伤心事，不幸
Deep feelings of sadness, typically caused by the death of someone loved.
She has suffered many griefs in her life. 她一生经历了许多悲伤。
sorrow, sadness, anguish
joy, happiness
