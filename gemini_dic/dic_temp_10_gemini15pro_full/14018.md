auroral:/əˈrɔːrəl/
adjective
极光的；曙光的；玫瑰色的
Relating to or resembling the aurora borealis or aurora australis; characteristic of the dawn.
The auroral display was breathtaking.  极光展示令人叹为观止。
The sky shimmered with auroral light. 天空闪烁着极光。
近义词:polar, northern lights (specifically for aurora borealis)
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "aurōra" (dawn) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).
