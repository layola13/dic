confusably:/kənˈfjuːzəbli/| adv.|容易混淆地，令人困惑地|In a way that is likely to cause confusion.  This word describes the manner in which something is presented or expressed, leading to potential misunderstanding.|The instructions were written so confusably that nobody knew what to do.  说明书写得让人摸不着头脑，以至于没有人知道该怎么做。|similarly: unclearly, vaguely, ambiguously|clearly, distinctly, precisely|adverb
