childbed: /ˈtʃaɪldˌbed/ | n. | 产床；分娩期；产褥 |The state of a woman immediately after childbirth; confinement.  A bed used by a woman during childbirth or during the period immediately after childbirth.|  She remained in childbed for a week after the delivery. 她分娩后在产床上待了一个星期。| synonyms: confinement, lying-in, postpartum period | antonyms:  None (This word represents a specific physiological state, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.) | noun
