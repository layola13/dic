parlor: /ˈpɑːrlər/ (US) /ˈpɑːlə/ (UK)| n. | 客厅；接待室；营业室 | A room in a private house or establishment used for conversation or the reception of guests, or a business establishment offering specified services. |  We gathered in the parlor for tea and conversation. 我们聚集在客厅喝茶聊天。

The beauty parlor offers a range of services. 这家美容院提供各种服务。 |  Synonyms: sitting room, living room, lounge, salon, drawing room | Antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym for a physical space) | Noun
