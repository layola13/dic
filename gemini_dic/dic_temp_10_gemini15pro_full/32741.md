chlorophyll: /ˈklɔːrəfɪl/ (US), /ˈklɒrəfɪl/ (UK) | n. | 叶绿素 | A green pigment, present in all green plants and in cyanobacteria, responsible for the absorption of light to provide energy for photosynthesis. | Example: Chlorophyll makes plants look green. 叶绿素使植物看起来是绿色的。| Example:  The chlorophyll in the leaves absorbs sunlight. 叶子中的叶绿素吸收阳光。 | Synonym: pigment,  chloroplast (indirect) | Antonym: N/A | Noun

chlorophyll: /ˈklɔːrəfɪl/ (US), /ˈklɒrəfɪl/ (UK) | adj. |  叶绿素的 | Relating to or containing chlorophyll. | Example: Chlorophyll molecules absorb light energy. 叶绿素分子吸收光能。 | Example: The chlorophyll content of the leaves is high. 叶子中的叶绿素含量很高。| Synonym:  pigmented (general) | Antonym: N/A | Adjective
