formalising:/ˈfɔːməlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使正式化；正式确定 | Making something official or giving it a definite structure or form. |  The company is formalising its dress code policy. 公司正在将其着装规范政策正式化。

The two countries are formalising their trade agreement. 这两个国家正在将其贸易协定正式化。 | 近义词: systematizing, standardizing, codifying, regularizing | 反义词: informalizing | 词性: 动词 -ing 形式 (Present Participle)


formalise:/ˈfɔːməlaɪz/ | v. | 使正式化；正式确定 | To make something official or give it a definite structure or form. | They formalized the agreement with a signed contract. 他们签署合同，使协议正式化。

We need to formalise the procedures for submitting expense reports. 我们需要正式确定提交费用报告的程序。| 近义词: systematize, standardize, codify, regularize | 反义词: informalize | 词性: 动词


词源:  From the French formaliser, from formel (formal) + -iser (suffix forming verbs). Ultimately from Latin formalis.
