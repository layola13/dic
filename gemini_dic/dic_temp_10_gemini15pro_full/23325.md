boyhoods:/ˈbɔɪhʊdz/| n. |童年，少年时代|The period of time when someone is a boy.  It refers to the state or time of being a boy, often encompassing the experiences, memories, and characteristics associated with that stage of life. |He remembered his boyhoods with fondness. 他深情地回忆起他的童年时光。|Synonym: childhood, youth, adolescence |Antonym: adulthood, old age|noun

boyhoods:/ˈbɔɪhʊdz/| n. |童年，少年时代（复数形式，较少用，可能指多个男孩的童年或不同阶段的童年）|The plural form of boyhood, referring to the period of time when multiple individuals are boys, or perhaps different periods of boyhood within one person's life.  While grammatically correct, it's less common than the singular form.|He often recounted anecdotes from his various boyhoods. 他经常讲述他不同童年时期的轶事。|Synonym: childhoods, youths |Antonym: adulthoods |noun
