nonprosecutable:/ˌnɒnˌprɒsɪˈkjuːtəbl/
adj.
不可起诉的
Not liable to or subject to criminal prosecution.
The case was deemed nonprosecutable due to lack of evidence.  由于缺乏证据，该案件被认为不可起诉。
The statute of limitations had expired, rendering the crime nonprosecutable.  诉讼时效已过，使得该罪行无法起诉。
近义词:unprosecutable, indictable
反义词:prosecutable
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  非，相反的
prosecute (verb): to institute legal proceedings against (a person or organization)  起诉
-able (suffix): capable of, susceptible of, fit for, tending to, given to  可以...的，易于...的，适合...的
