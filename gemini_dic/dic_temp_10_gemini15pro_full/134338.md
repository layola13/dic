nonintegrated:/ˌnɒnˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/
adjective
未整合的，未集成的
Not integrated; not combined into a whole.
Example: The company's nonintegrated systems caused numerous problems.  这家公司未整合的系统导致了许多问题。
Example:  The report criticized the nonintegrated approach to healthcare. 报告批评了未整合的医疗保健方法。
Synonym: separate, disconnected, disjointed, independent
Antonym: integrated, combined, unified, consolidated
词性: 形容词
