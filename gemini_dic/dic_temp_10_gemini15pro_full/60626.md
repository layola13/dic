elding:/ˈɛldɪŋ/ | n. | 焊接，熔接 | The process of joining metal pieces by heating them to their melting point and fusing them together. |  The elding of the two pipes created a strong, leak-proof joint.  两个管子的焊接创造了一个牢固、防漏的连接。 | synonyms: welding, fusing, joining | antonyms: separating, detaching | noun
