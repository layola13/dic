cometography: /ˌkʌmɪˈtɒɡrəfi/
n.|彗星摄影术|The art or practice of photographing comets.|He was a pioneer in cometography, capturing stunning images of Halley's Comet.|近义词: astrophotography|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:  "cometo-" (from Greek *komētēs* meaning "comet") + "-graphy" (from Greek *-graphia* meaning "writing" or "representation by means of lines").
