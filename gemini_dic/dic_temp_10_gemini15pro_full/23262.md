noun
弓弦的制作或安装; 用弓弦勒死
The act of making or fitting a bowstring; strangulation with a bowstring.
The museum exhibit showcased the intricate art of bowstringing.  博物馆的展览展示了复杂的弓弦制作艺术。
The ancient warrior was executed by bowstringing.  古代武士被弓弦勒死。

bowstring:/ˈboʊstrɪŋ/
noun
弓弦
A string made of various materials used to propel arrows from a bow.
The archer carefully waxed his bowstring.  弓箭手仔细地给他的弓弦上蜡。
A broken bowstring can ruin a hunt.  断掉的弓弦会毁掉一次狩猎。
近义词: cord, string
反义词: (No direct antonym)
