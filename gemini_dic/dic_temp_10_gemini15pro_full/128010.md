nephrolithic:/ˌnɛfrəˈlɪθɪk/
adjective
肾结石的；与肾结石有关的
Relating to or afflicted with kidney stones.
The patient experienced nephrolithic pain.  病人感到肾结石的疼痛。
The doctor diagnosed a nephrolithic condition. 医生诊断出肾结石病症。

近义词:renal calculus, kidney stone
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑healthy kidneys 健康的肾脏)
词性:adjective
