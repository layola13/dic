encapsulation:/ɪnˌkæpsjʊˈleɪʃn/| n.|封装，包裹|The act of enclosing something in a capsule or other container; in computing, the bundling of data with the methods that operate on that data.|Object-oriented programming uses encapsulation to protect data. 面向对象编程使用封装来保护数据。|近义词：packaging, enclosure, bundling|反义词: exposure, revelation|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French encapsuler, from en- (in) + capsule (capsule). Ultimately from Latin capsula (small box).
