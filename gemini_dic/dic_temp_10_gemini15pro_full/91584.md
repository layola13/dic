hyperacute:/ˌhaɪpərəˈkjuːt/| adj.|超急性的|Characterized by a very sudden onset and rapid course.|例句：The patient presented with hyperacute rejection of the transplanted organ. 病人出现了移植器官的超急性排斥反应。

例句：Hyperacute encephalomyelitis is a rare but severe condition. 超急性脑脊髓炎是一种罕见但严重的疾病。|近义词：fulminant, very acute, sudden|反义词:chronic, long-term|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From hyper- "excessive, above" + acute "sharp, severe."  "Hyper-" comes from Greek, while "acute" comes from Latin.
