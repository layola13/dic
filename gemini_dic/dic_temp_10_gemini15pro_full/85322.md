harping:/ˈhɑːrpɪŋ/ | v. | 喋喋不休；反复抱怨；弹奏竖琴 | To talk or write persistently and tediously on a particular topic; to play a harp. |  He's always harping on about his health problems. 他总是喋喋不休地谈论他的健康问题。

She was harping on her harp, filling the room with beautiful music. 她正在弹奏竖琴，房间里充满了优美的音乐。 | 近义词：dwelling, nagging, carping, reiterating | 反义词：forgetting, ignoring | 词性: 动词(present participle)


harping:/ˈhɑːrpɪŋ/ | n. |  弹奏竖琴(较少用) | The act of playing a harp. | The harping was soothing and melodic. 竖琴演奏舒缓而悠扬。 | 近义词：harp playing | 反义词： N/A | 词性: 名词(gerund)
