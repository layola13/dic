literalism:/ˈlɪtərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 字面主义；拘泥字句；字面解释 | Adherence to the exact letter or meaning, as of a text or dogma, without regard to the underlying spirit or intent. |  He interpreted the Bible with a strict literalism. 他对圣经的解释非常拘泥于字面。 | literalness, exactness |  figurativeness, symbolism | noun
