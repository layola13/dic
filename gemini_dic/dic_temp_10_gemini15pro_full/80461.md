gonidium:/gəˈnɪdɪəm/ | n. | 生殖细胞，无性孢子 |A specialized, asexual reproductive cell in certain algae, fungi, and lichens; an algal cell in a lichen. | Example 1: The gonidia of the alga are released into the water.  该藻类的生殖细胞被释放到水中。

Example 2:  Lichens are composed of fungi and gonidia of algae. 地衣是由真菌和藻类的生殖细胞组成的。 | Synonym: propagule, spore | Antonym: N/A | noun
