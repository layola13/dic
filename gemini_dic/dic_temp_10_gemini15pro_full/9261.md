antipatriotically: /ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪkli/ /ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪkli/
adverb
反爱国地;不爱国地
In a manner that opposes or shows a lack of love for one's country.
He spoke antipatriotically about the war, criticizing his own country's involvement.  他以反爱国的方式谈论战争，批评自己国家的参与。
The protesters acted antipatriotically by burning the national flag. 抗议者焚烧国旗，表现得反爱国。
近义词: treasonously, seditiously, disloyally
反义词: patriotically, loyally
词性: adverb
