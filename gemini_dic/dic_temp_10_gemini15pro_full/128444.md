news's:/njuːzɪz/ | n. |新闻的（所有格）|  Possessive form of "news," indicating ownership or association.  Used less commonly than "news'," particularly in formal writing. | The news's impact was significant.  新闻的影响很大。| The news's coverage of the event was extensive.  新闻对该事件的报道很广泛。 |  Synonym: news' (often preferred) | Antonym: N/A | noun
