erotesis: /ɛrəˈtiːsɪs/ /ɛrəˈtoʊsɪs/ | n. | 反问;诘问 | A figure of speech in which a question is asked and then answered by the speaker; a rhetorical question. |  "Is this any way to treat a friend? Of course not!" This is an erotesis. | 近义词: rhetorical question, interrogation | 反义词: hypophora, statement | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐρώτησις (erṓtēsis, “questioning”), from ἐρωτάω (erōtáō, “I ask”).

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Do you know what time it is? = 你知道现在几点了吗？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you thinking about right now? = 你现在在想什么？
What is she doing in the garden? = 她在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you been up to lately? = 你最近都在忙些什么？
What has she learned from this experience? = 她从这次经历中学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你这整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你打算做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next year? = 明年这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
