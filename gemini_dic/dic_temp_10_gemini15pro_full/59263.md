词源:
From Middle English techere, from Old English tēocere, from Proto-Germanic *taikijaną (“to show, point out, teach”), from Proto-Indo-European *deḱ- (“to show, teach”). Cognate with Dutch teekenaar (“draftsman”), German zeigen (“to show”).
