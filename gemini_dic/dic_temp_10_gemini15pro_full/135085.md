nonobjectifications:/ˌnɒnəbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非对象化，非物化 | The act of not treating someone as an object, especially a sexual object. Recognizing someone's full humanity and agency. |  Feminist theory emphasizes the importance of nonobjectifications of women in media. 女权主义理论强调了媒体中女性非物化的重要性。 |  non-reification, humanization, personification | objectification, commodification | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "objectification" (from "object" + "-ification"). "Object" comes from the Latin "objectum" (something presented to the mind).  "-ification" is a suffix denoting the process of making or becoming something.
