discretion:/dɪˈskreʃ(ə)n/
n.
谨慎，慎重；自行决定的自由；判断力；考虑周到
The ability to behave without causing embarrassment or attracting too much attention, especially by keeping information secret.
She handled the awkward situation with great discretion. 她非常谨慎地处理了这个尴尬的局面。
prudence, circumspection, carefulness
indiscretion, carelessness, recklessness
noun

discretion:/dɪˈskreʃ(ə)n/
n.
自由裁量权
The right or ability to decide something.
I leave the decision to your discretion. 我把决定权留给你。
choice, option, judgment
obligation, compulsion
noun
