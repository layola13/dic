indemnified:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪd/
verb (past participle)
已赔偿的，已保障的
Secured against or compensated for loss or damage.  Having been protected against potential loss, damage, or liability.
The contract indemnified the company against any legal action.  合同保障公司免受任何法律诉讼。
The landlord indemnified the tenant against any damage caused by the faulty wiring.  房东保障租户免受由于线路故障造成的任何损失。
compensated, protected, insured, secured
liable, responsible, accountable, unprotected
verb
