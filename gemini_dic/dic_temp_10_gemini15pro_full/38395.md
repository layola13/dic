conceptualizable:/kənˌsɛptʃuəˈlaɪzəbl/
adj.
可以概念化的，可以形成概念的
able to be formed or understood as a concept
The feeling of happiness is easily conceptualizable.  幸福的感觉很容易被概念化。
This complex theory is not easily conceptualizable.  这个复杂的理论不容易被概念化。
近义词: conceivable, imaginable, thinkable
反义词: inconceivable, unimaginable, unthinkable
词性: adjective
