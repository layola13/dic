non-violence:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪələns/| n.|非暴力|The policy of non-violence is effective in some situations. 非暴力政策在某些情况下是有效的。|近义词：pacifism, peaceful resistance|反义词：violence, aggression|noun

non-violent:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪələnt/| adj.|非暴力的|They chose a non-violent approach to protest. 他们选择了一种非暴力的抗议方式。|近义词：peaceful, pacifist|反义词：violent, aggressive|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + violence.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "lack of." "Violence" comes from the Latin "violentia," meaning "vehemence, impetuosity; violence, fury."
