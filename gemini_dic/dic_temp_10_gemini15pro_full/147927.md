overreliant:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlaɪənt/| adj.|过度依赖的，过分信赖的|Excessively reliant on someone or something; depending too much on a particular person or thing for support or help.|例句：He's become overreliant on his parents for financial support. 他变得过度依赖父母的经济支持。|近义词：dependent, reliant |反义词:independent, self-reliant |词性:adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "reliant" (adjective meaning "dependent")
