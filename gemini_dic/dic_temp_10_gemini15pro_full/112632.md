low-pitched:/ˌloʊˈpɪtʃt/| adj.|低沉的；音调低的|Having a low pitch (frequency).  A low-pitched sound is deep and resonant.|The cello has a low-pitched sound. 大提琴的声音低沉。|Synonyms: deep, resonant, bass, grave|Antonyms: high-pitched, shrill, treble|adjective
