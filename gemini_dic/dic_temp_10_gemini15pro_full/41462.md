cornification:/ˌkɔːrnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |角化，角质化|The process of forming a horny substance, especially the formation of keratin in the epidermis of the skin.|Example 1: Excessive cornification can lead to dry, flaky skin. 过度角化会导致皮肤干燥和脱屑。

Example 2:  The cornification of the cells provides a protective barrier. 细胞的角化提供了一层保护屏障。|Synonym: keratinization,  keratinisation |Antonym: softening, maceration|noun
