arminian:/ɑːrˈmɪniən/
adjective|亚米尼亚的；亚米尼亚人的；亚米念派的|Relating to Armenia, its people, or their language.  Also, relating to Arminianism, a theological doctrine emphasizing free will.|例句：He studied Arminian theology. 他研究了亚米念派神学。|近义词：Armenian (for relating to Armenia), free will (for the theological doctrine)|反义词：Calvinist (for the theological doctrine)|词性：adjective
arminian:/ɑːrˈmɪniən/
noun|亚米尼亚人；亚米念派信徒|A native or inhabitant of Armenia, or a follower of Arminianism.|例句：She is an Arminian. 她是一位亚米念派信徒。|近义词：Armenian (for a person from Armenia)|反义词：Calvinist (for the theological doctrine)|词性：noun
