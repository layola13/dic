diptych:/ˈdɪptɪk/| n. |双联画；双折画板|A diptych is a work of art (usually a panel painting) with two hinged panels which may be closed like a book. |The museum displayed a beautiful diptych from the 15th century.  博物馆展出了一幅15世纪的精美双联画。| The altarpiece was a diptych depicting scenes from the life of Christ.  祭坛画是一幅描绘基督生平的双联画。|synonyms: panel painting, two-paneled artwork |antonyms: N/A|noun
