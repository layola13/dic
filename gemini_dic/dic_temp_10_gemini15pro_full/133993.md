nonhit:/ˌnɑːnˈhɪt/ | n. | 未击中；未命中；失败；不成功 | A baseball pitch that is not swung at by the batter and is not called a strike by the umpire, or any similar occurrence in other sports.  A failure or something that is not successful. |  Example 1: The pitcher threw a nonhit, and the batter walked to first base. 投手投了一个未击中的球，击球手走上一垒。 Example 2: The marketing campaign was a nonhit, and sales remained stagnant. 这次营销活动没有成功，销售额仍然停滞不前。 | 近义词: miss, failure, flop | 反义词: hit, success | 词性: 名词
