militiate:/ˈmɪlɪteɪt/| v. |产生不利影响；妨碍；对…不利|To have weight or effect against; to operate to disadvantage, disfavor, or oppose.|His poor health militated against his chances of promotion. 他的健康状况不佳，妨碍了他晋升的机会。|近义词：count against, work against, operate against, weigh against, tell against, prejudice, hinder, obstruct, impede, hamper, detract from, oppose|反义词：favor, support, help, promote, aid, assist, facilitate|词性:动词
