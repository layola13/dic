nonsegmentation:/ˌnɑːnˌsɛɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非分段，不分段 | The absence of segmentation or division into separate parts. This can refer to physical structures, data, or processes. | The nonsegmentation of the worm's body makes it difficult to determine its length.  这种蠕虫的身体不分段，使得很难确定它的长度。|  Nonsegmentation of the market means treating all customers as one group.  市场的非分段意味着将所有客户视为一个群体。 |  wholeness, integration, unity | segmentation, division, separation | noun
