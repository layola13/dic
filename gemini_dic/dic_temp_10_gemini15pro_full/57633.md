draughtsmanship: /ˈdrɑːftsmənʃɪp/ /ˈdræftsmənʃɪp/
n. | 制图术；绘图技巧；草拟能力 | The skill or art of making drawings, especially technical drawings. |  His draughtsmanship is impeccable, capturing every detail with precision.  他的绘图技巧无可挑剔，精确地捕捉到了每一个细节。| synonyms: drawing, sketching, rendering, illustration | antonyms: ineptitude, clumsiness | noun

draughtsmanship: /ˈdrɑːftsmənʃɪp/ /ˈdræftsmənʃɪp/
n. | 草拟（文件）能力；起草（文件）技巧 | The ability to write or draft documents clearly and effectively. |  Her draughtsmanship of legal documents is highly regarded. 她起草法律文件的能力备受推崇。 | synonyms: drafting, writing, composition, formulation | antonyms: inarticulateness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From draught +‎ -manship.  Draught meaning "a drawing, sketch, or design" comes from the Old English dragan "to draw, drag, pull". -manship is a suffix meaning "skill or art of".
