non-conscious:/nɒnˈkɒnʃəs/ (UK), /nɑːnˈkɑːnʃəs/ (US)| adj.|无意识的，非意识的|Not consciously perceived or controlled; occurring without awareness.|例句：Many bodily functions are non-conscious. 许多身体机能是无意识的。|例句：Non-conscious biases can influence our decisions.  无意识的偏见会影响我们的决定。|近义词：unconscious, involuntary, automatic, subliminal|反义词：conscious, aware, deliberate, intentional|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix) from Latin *non "not"
conscious (adjective) from Latin *conscius "knowing with," from com- "with," and scire "to know."
