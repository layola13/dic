nonexploitative:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔɪtətɪv/
adjective
非剥削性的
Not exploiting others, especially not making a profit from their labor without giving them a fair return.
Fairtrade organizations ensure that producers receive a nonexploitative price for their goods.  公平贸易组织确保生产者获得其商品的非剥削性价格。
The company prides itself on its nonexploitative labor practices.  该公司以其非剥削性的劳动实践为荣。
Synonyms: fair, just, equitable, ethical
Antonyms: exploitative, unfair, unjust, unethical
词性: 形容词
