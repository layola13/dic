legislations: /ˌlɛdʒɪsˈleɪʃənz/
| n. | 法律；法规（legislation的复数形式，通常用单数）| The body of laws enacted by a legislature.  The act or process of legislating; lawmaking. | New legislations were passed to protect the environment.  通过了新的立法来保护环境。| 近义词：laws, statutes, acts, regulations | 反义词：deregulation, lawlessness | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French legislacion, from Latin legislatio(n-), from the verb legere ‘choose, read’.
