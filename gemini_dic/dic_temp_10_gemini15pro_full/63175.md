entailer:/ɪnˈteɪlər/| n.|必然结果；需要；附带条件；蕴涵|Something that is entailed is a necessary part or consequence of something else.  Entailers are propositions that logically follow from another proposition.|Building a new factory entails hiring new workers. 建设新工厂需要雇用新工人。|近义词：necessitate, require, involve, imply|反义词: preclude, prevent|noun

entailer:/ɪnˈteɪlər/| n.|限定继承人|A person who inherits property under an entail.|The entailer specified that the estate could only be passed down to male heirs.  限定继承人规定，遗产只能传给男性继承人。|近义词：heir, inheritor|反义词: N/A|noun
