hide-out:/ˈhaɪdaʊt/| n.| 藏身处，躲藏处|A place where someone hides, especially from the police or from other people who are looking for them.|The robbers used an abandoned cabin as their hide-out. 强盗们用一个废弃的小屋作为藏身处。|synonyms: hideaway, refuge, shelter, sanctuary|antonyms: exposure, open, public|noun

hide-out:/ˈhaɪdaʊt/| n.| 隐蔽处，秘密地点|A place that is secret and hidden.|The children had a secret hide-out in the woods. 孩子们在树林里有一个秘密藏身处。|synonyms: hideaway, retreat, den|antonyms: exposure, open, public|noun
