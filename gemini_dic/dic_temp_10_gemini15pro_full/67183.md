expounds:/ɪkˈspaʊndz/
verb
阐述，解释，详细说明
To explain or discuss something in detail.
The professor expounds on the theory of relativity in his lectures. 教授在他的讲座中详细阐述了相对论。
explain, elaborate, clarify
obscure, confuse
词性:动词
