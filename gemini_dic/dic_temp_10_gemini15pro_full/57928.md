drink-driver:/ˈdrɪŋk ˈdraɪvər/| n.|酒后驾驶者，醉驾司机|A person who drives a vehicle after consuming an amount of alcohol that impairs their ability to drive safely.|The drink-driver was arrested and charged with DUI. 酒后驾驶者被逮捕并被控酒驾。|Example: The drink-driver caused a serious accident.  这个酒驾司机造成了严重事故。|Synonyms: drunk driver, impaired driver|Antonyms: sober driver|Noun
