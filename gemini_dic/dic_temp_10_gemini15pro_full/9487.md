antiromantic:/ˌæntiroʊˈmæntɪk/ | adj. | 反浪漫的，非浪漫的 | Opposed to or lacking in romance; unromantic. |  She has a rather antiromantic view of marriage, seeing it more as a practical partnership. 她对婚姻持有一种相当反浪漫的观点，更多地将其视为一种务实的伙伴关系。

antiromantic:/ˌæntiroʊˈmæntɪk/ | adj. |  不切实际的 | Not impractical or idealistic. | His antiromantic approach to business proved successful. 他不切实际的商业方法证明是成功的。


antiromantic:/ˌæntiroʊˈmæntɪk/ | n. | 反浪漫主义者 | A person who is opposed to or lacks romance. | He's such an antiromantic, he never buys her flowers. 他真是个反浪漫主义者，从不给她买花。
