artifice:/ˈɑːrtɪfɪs/| n.|技巧，诡计，策略；人为的技巧，不自然的东西|A clever trick or device, especially one designed to deceive; skill in employing such tricks or devices.|She employed every artifice to gain his trust. 她用尽各种诡计来取得他的信任。|synonyms: trick, ruse, stratagem, deception, maneuver, contrivance, wile, subterfuge|antonyms: honesty, sincerity, forthrightness, candor, artlessness, naturalness|noun

artifice:/ˈɑːrtɪfɪs/| n.|精巧的工艺品|A skillfully constructed object or work of art.|The elaborate ice sculptures were true artifices. 这些精美的冰雕是真正的工艺品。|synonyms: creation, construction, handiwork|antonyms: nature, simplicity|noun
