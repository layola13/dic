gise:/dʒaɪz/ | v. | 装备，提供…穿戴 | To dress or clothe, especially in fine or special attire.  Often used in a reflexive sense. | He gised himself in the finest silks. 他给自己穿上最好的丝绸。| 近义词: array, attire, clothe, garb, deck | 反义词: undress, disrobe | Verb

gise oneself =  给自己穿衣服

词源： From Middle English *gisen, *gysen, from Old French giser, from Frankish *gīsan, from Proto-Germanic *gīsaną (“to make ready, prepare”). Cognate with Old High German gīsan (“to terrify, frighten, make ready”). Related to aghast.


注意事项:

gise 这个词语现在已经很少使用，主要出现在古英语或诗歌中。在现代英语中，更常用的词语是 dress, clothe, attire 等。
