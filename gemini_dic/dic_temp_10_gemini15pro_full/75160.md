fritting:/ˈfrɪtɪŋ/
n.
烧结，熔结；熔块
the action or process of fusing a substance at a high temperature, especially to make glass or enamel.
The fritting process is essential for creating vibrant colors in ceramics. 烧结过程对于在陶瓷中创造鲜艳的色彩至关重要。
The artist used fritting to create a unique texture on the sculpture.  这位艺术家用熔结技术在雕塑上创造了独特的纹理。
sintering, fusing, melting
solidifying, hardening
noun
