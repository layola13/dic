for that matter:/fɔːr ðæt ˈmætər/| idiom |就此而言；更何况；而且|Used to add a comment which emphasizes or supports what has just been said, often introducing a further and more surprising point.|She doesn't like him much. For that matter, I don't either.| 近义词: besides, moreover, furthermore, incidentally, in addition|反义词: however, nevertheless, on the other hand|词性:idiom
