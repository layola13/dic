coleopter:/ˌkoʊliˈɑːptər/| n. |鞘翅目昆虫；甲虫|An insect of the order Coleoptera, characterized by hard, shell-like front wings that cover and protect the membranous hind wings used for flight.  |例句：The ladybug is a common coleopter. 瓢虫是一种常见的鞘翅目昆虫。

例句：Coleopters are the largest order of insects. 鞘翅目是昆虫中最大的目。|近义词：beetle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κολεόπτερος (koleópteros, “sheath-winged”), from κολεός (koleós, “sheath”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
