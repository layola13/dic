broke-back:/ˈbroʊkˌbæk/| adj.|驼背的，脊背受伤的|Having a broken or deformed back, especially of an animal; (of an animal) having a sloping back or spine.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something weakened or damaged. |The rancher noticed a broke-back mare among his herd. 牧场主注意到他的马群中有一匹驼背的母马。

The scandal left the company broke-back and struggling to recover.  这桩丑闻使公司元气大伤，难以恢复。|近义词：humpbacked, swaybacked, crippled|反义词:straight-backed, sound|词性:形容词
