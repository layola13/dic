illiterately:/ɪˈlɪtərətli/
adverb
文盲地；不识字地
in an illiterate manner; without the ability to read or write.
He scrawled his name illiterately on the document. 他在文件上笨拙地签上了自己的名字。
近义词:uneducatedly, ignorantly
反义词:literately, knowledgeably
词性:adverb

illiterate:/ɪˈlɪtərət/
adjective
文盲的；不识字的
unable to read or write.
A surprisingly large percentage of the population is illiterate.  人口中文盲的比例高得惊人。
近义词:unlettered, uneducated
反义词:literate, educated
词性:adjective

illiteracy:/ɪˈlɪtərəsi/
noun
文盲；不识字
the inability to read or write.
Illiteracy is a major problem in many developing countries. 文盲是许多发展中国家的主要问题。
近义词:unletteredness, ignorance
反义词:literacy, education
词性:noun
