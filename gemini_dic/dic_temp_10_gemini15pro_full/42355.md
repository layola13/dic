counterargue:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuː/
verb
反驳，提出相反的论点
To argue against something that someone else has said.
He counterargued that the evidence was flawed. 他反驳说证据有缺陷。
The defense lawyer skillfully counterargued the prosecutor's claims. 辩护律师巧妙地反驳了检察官的主张。

近义词: rebut, refute, dispute, challenge
反义词: concede, agree, accept
词性: verb
