meister:/ˈmaɪstər/| n.|大师，师傅，专家|A skilled craftsman or artisan, especially in Germany; a master of a particular skill or craft.|例句：He is a meister of his craft. 他是技艺精湛的大师。

例句：The meister carefully inspected the apprentice's work.  这位师傅仔细检查了学徒的工作。|近义词：expert, master, specialist, professional, virtuoso, adept, proficient|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner, apprentice|词性:noun
