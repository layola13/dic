e-pal:/ˈiːpæl/ | n. |网友；笔友| An e-pal is a friend you communicate with regularly on the internet, especially by email.| I met my e-pal from Japan online last year. 我去年在网上认识了我的日本网友。|  近义词: online friend, internet friend, pen pal | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered "offline friend" in a loose sense)| noun
