brimless:/ˈbrɪmlɪs/| adj.|无边的，没有帽檐的|Lacking a brim, as a hat.|She wore a brimless hat to the beach. 她戴着一顶无檐帽去了海滩。|近义词：unbrimmed|反义词: brimmed|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From brim + -less.  "Brim" comes from Old English "brymm," meaning edge or rim.
