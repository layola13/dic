grebe:/ɡriːb/| n.|鸊鷉|Any of various diving birds of the family Podicipedidae, having lobed toes, short wings, and a rudimentary tail. They are awkward on land but agile in the water.|We saw a grebe diving for fish. 我们看到一只鸊鷉潜入水中捕鱼。|近义词：diver, water bird|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑陆地鸟类) land bird|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *grèbe*, probably of Germanic origin, related to Dutch *griet*.
