overrefine:/ˌoʊvərɪˈfaɪn/| v. |过度精炼；过分细化|To refine something to an excessive degree, often resulting in a loss of essential qualities or character.|The artist overrefined his technique, losing the spontaneity of his earlier work.  这位艺术家过度精炼了他的技巧，失去了早期作品的自发性。|The winemaker overrefined the grapes, stripping the wine of its distinctive flavor.  酿酒师过度精炼了葡萄，剥夺了葡萄酒独特的风味。| 近义词: overelaborate, overpolish | 反义词: simplify, streamline |词性:动词
