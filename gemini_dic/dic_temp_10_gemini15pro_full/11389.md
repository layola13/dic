argan:/ˈɑːrɡən/| n.|摩洛哥坚果树，阿甘树，坚果油|A thorny evergreen tree ( *Argania spinosa* ) native to southwestern Morocco, bearing a fruit with a nut from which argan oil is extracted.|The argan tree is vital to the ecosystem of southwestern Morocco. 摩洛哥坚果树对摩洛哥西南部的生态系统至关重要。|近义词:ironwood tree (类似耐旱树种)|反义词:无|n.

argan:/ˈɑːrɡən/| n.|摩洛哥坚果油，阿甘油|An oil extracted from the kernels of the argan tree, used in cosmetics and cooking.|Argan oil is known for its moisturizing properties.  阿甘油以其保湿特性而闻名。|近义词:Moroccan oil|反义词:无|n.
