encodable:/ɪnˈkoʊdəbl/| adj.|可编码的，可以编码的|Something that can be encoded (converted into a coded form).|The data is encodable into a binary format.  这些数据可以编码成二进制格式。|Example 2:  All the characters are encodable using this software. 所有字符都可以使用此软件进行编码。|近义词:codable, cipherable|反义词:uncodable, indecipherable|词性:adjective
encode:/ɪnˈkoʊd/| v.|编码|To convert information into a coded form.|近义词:code, cipher, encrypt|反义词:decode, decipher, decrypt|词性:verb
