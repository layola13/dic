orthogneiss: /ˈɔːrθəˌnaɪs/ | n. | 正片麻岩 | A metamorphic rock formed by high temperature and pressure alteration of igneous rock (granite, diorite, or gabbro). | The geologist identified the sample as orthogneiss due to its distinct banding. 地质学家根据其明显的条带状结构将样品鉴定为正片麻岩。| Example: The cliff face was composed primarily of orthogneiss.  悬崖面主要由正片麻岩组成。| Synonym: gneiss | Antonym: N/A | noun
