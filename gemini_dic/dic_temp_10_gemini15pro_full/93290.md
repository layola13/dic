注意事项:

Although "like" can technically be used in the continuous tenses, it is generally considered informal and less common than the simple tenses. Native speakers often prefer to use the simple tenses when expressing fondness or preference.  The continuous tenses are more acceptable when describing a developing feeling of liking something.
