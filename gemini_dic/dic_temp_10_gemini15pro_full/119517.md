microprocessing:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈprɑːsesɪŋ/ | n. |微处理|The execution of instructions by a microprocessor.  It involves fetching instructions from memory, decoding them, and executing them to perform various operations. |Microprocessing has revolutionized the computer industry. 微处理彻底改变了计算机行业。|Microprocessing is central to the function of modern computers. 微处理是现代计算机功能的核心。| synonym: information processing, data processing | antonym: manual processing | noun

microprocessors:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈprɑːsesərz/ | n. |微处理器|A central processing unit (CPU) contained on a single integrated circuit (IC) chip. It performs arithmetic, logic, and control operations based on instructions received from software. |Modern microprocessors contain millions of transistors. 现代微处理器包含数百万个晶体管。|The speed of a computer is largely determined by its microprocessor. 计算机的速度很大程度上取决于它的微处理器。| synonym: CPU, chip, processor | antonym:  (no direct antonym, perhaps "discrete logic") | noun
