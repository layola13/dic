ferreous:/ˈfɛriəs/ | adj. | 铁的；含铁的；像铁的 | Containing or consisting of iron; resembling iron. |  The soil is rich in ferrous oxides. 土壤中富含氧化铁。|  Synonyms: iron, irony, chalybeate | Antonyms: nonferrous | word type: adjective

ferric:/ˈfɛrɪk/ | adj. | 铁的；含铁的；像铁的 | Relating to or containing iron, especially with valence 3. | Ferric chloride is used as a flocculant in sewage treatment. 氯化铁在污水处理中用作絮凝剂。|  Synonyms: iron, ferrous, chalybeate | Antonyms: ferrous (in a comparative sense) | word type: adjective
