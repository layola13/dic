instalment:/ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (UK)| n. |分期付款；部分；一期|a sum of money due as one of several equal payments for something; a portion of something issued or published in stages|例句：She paid for the car in instalments. 她分期付款买了这辆车。|例句：The first instalment of the trilogy was released last year.  三部曲的第一部去年上映了。|近义词：payment, part, portion, installment (US spelling)|反义词: lump sum|词性:名词
