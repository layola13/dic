humblingly:/ˈhʌmbliŋli/| adv.|谦逊地，谦卑地|In a way that shows you are not proud or arrogant.  It derives from the adjective "humble" with the addition of the suffix "-ly" to form an adverb.  The word "humble" has its roots in the Latin word "humilis," meaning "low" or "on the ground."|She humblingly accepted the award, acknowledging the contributions of her team. 她谦逊地接受了奖项，并肯定了团队的贡献。|Synonyms: modestly, meekly, unassumingly|Antonyms: arrogantly, proudly, haughtily|词性:副词
