misthrown:/ˌmɪsˈθroʊn/
Verb
投掷错误,投掷失误
Thrown incorrectly or inaccurately.
The quarterback misthrew the ball, costing them the game.  四分卫投球失误，让他们输掉了比赛。
Near Synonyms: mishandled, misdirected, flubbed, botched
Antonyms:  well-aimed, accurate, perfect, on-target
词性: 动词
