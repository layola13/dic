如果您是想了解疑问词 what 的用法，我可以提供相关信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. | 什么 |  used to ask for information about something |

例句：
What is your name? 你叫什么名字？
What do you want? 你想要什么？


近义词: which, whatever
反义词:  (What 没有直接的反义词，它的意义在于提问而不是表达一个具体的概念。)
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually do on weekends? 你周末通常做什么？
What does she like to eat for breakfast? 她早餐喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing right now? 你现在正在做什么？
What is he working on at the moment? 他此刻正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了些什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon? 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? 做出决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been working on before you changed projects? 你换项目之前一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么改变？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by the time you graduate?  到你毕业的时候，你将已经学习五年什么专业了？
What will she have been working on by the end of the project? 到项目结束时，她将一直在做什么？
