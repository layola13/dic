egoists:/ˈiːɡoʊɪsts/| n.|利己主义者；自私自利的人|A person who is excessively self-centered and concerned primarily with their own interests, needs, and well-being, often disregarding the feelings and well-being of others.|例句：Egoists rarely consider the needs of others. 利己主义者很少考虑别人的需要。

|例句：The egoists only cared about their own advancement. 这些利己主义者只关心自己的进步。|近义词：narcissists, self-seekers, selfish people|反义词：altruists, philanthropists|词性:noun
