blackbirding:/ˈblækˌbɜːrdɪŋ/
n.
诱拐;强迫劳动;绑架劳工;拐骗;非法招募劳工
The act of kidnapping people and forcing them to work, especially in the 19th century when Pacific Islanders were taken to work on plantations in Australia and elsewhere.
The practice of blackbirding was widespread in the 19th century.  19世纪，诱拐劳工的做法很普遍。
Blackbirding was a cruel and inhumane practice.  拐骗劳工是一种残酷和不人道的行为。
近义词:kidnapping, enslavement, forced labor, trafficking
反义词:liberation, freedom
