frustulent: /ˈfrʌstrələnt/
adjective
令人沮丧的；使人泄气的；徒劳无功的
causing feelings of frustration; tending to frustrate
例句：The frustulent search for the missing documents continued for weeks.  对丢失文件的徒劳搜索持续了数周。

例句：It's frustulent to work so hard and see no results.  努力工作却看不到结果，令人沮丧。
近义词：frustrating, disappointing, discouraging, fruitless, futile
反义词：successful, rewarding, satisfying
词性: adjective


词源:  From Late Latin *frustulentus, from Latin frustrā (“in vain, to no purpose”) + -lentus (suffix forming adjectives).
