interdepartmental:/ˌɪntərdɪpɑːrtˈmɛntl/ | adj. | 部门间的，跨部门的 | Involving or existing between two or more departments within an organization. |  The company formed an interdepartmental committee to address the issue of declining sales. 公司成立了一个跨部门委员会来解决销售额下降的问题。 | The interdepartmental rivalry was hindering the project's progress. 部门间的竞争阻碍了项目的进展。 |  inter-organizational, cross-departmental | intradepartmental | adjective
