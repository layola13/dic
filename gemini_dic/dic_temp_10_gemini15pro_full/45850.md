cut-and-run:/ˌkʌt ən ˈrʌn/| idiom|逃走；溜之大吉；仓皇撤退|To make a hasty retreat or departure, often to avoid consequences or responsibilities.|The company decided to cut and run from the failing project rather than invest more money. 这家公司决定从失败的项目中抽身而退，而不是投入更多资金。|近义词：abandon, desert, flee, escape|反义词：stay, remain, face, confront|词性: idiom

cut-and-run:/ˌkʌt ən ˈrʌn/| n.|逃走；溜之大吉；仓皇撤退|The act of making a hasty retreat or departure.|The general's cut and run left the troops demoralized. 将军的仓皇撤退使部队士气低落。|近义词：flight, desertion, escape|反义词：stand, resistance, confrontation|词性: noun
