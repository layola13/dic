aureola:/ɔːˈriːələ/ /ɔˈriːələ/ | n. | 光环，晕，（圣像上的）金环 | A circle of light or brightness surrounding something, especially as depicted in art around the head or body of a holy person. |  The painting depicts the Virgin Mary with a golden aureola around her head. 这幅画描绘了圣母玛利亚，她的头上有一个金色的光环。

 The sun appeared with a hazy aureola around it. 太阳出现了，周围环绕着一圈朦胧的光环。 | 近义词：halo, nimbus, corona, aura | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps darkness or shadow if contrasting the light aspect) | 词性: noun
