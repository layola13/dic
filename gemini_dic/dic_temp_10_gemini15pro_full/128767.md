nighed:/naɪd/ | v. | 临近，靠近 (尤指夜幕降临) |  The day nighed, and the shadows lengthened. 白天将尽，影子越来越长。| 近义词: approached, drew near, neared | 反义词: receded, withdrew | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *nīgian, from Proto-Germanic *nīhwijaną (“to approach, draw near”), from *nēhwaz (“near”). Cognate with Dutch nijgen (“to curtsy, bow, incline”), German neigen (“to incline, tilt, tend”), Swedish nigh (“to approach”).  More at nigh.
