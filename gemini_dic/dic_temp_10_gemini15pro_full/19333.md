bibliographically:/ˌbɪbliəˈɡræfɪkli/
adverb
以书目方式
in a way that relates to bibliography (the study of books as physical objects, and the identification, description, and classification of books)
The sources are listed bibliographically at the end of the article.  文章末尾以书目方式列出了参考文献。
The book is bibliographically significant due to its rare printing.  这本书因其罕见的印刷而具有重要的文献价值。
Synonym: documentarily
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "casually" or "informally" in some contexts)
词性: 副词
