exaction:/ɪɡˈzækʃən/
n.
勒索;苛求;强征;强取的款项
The act of demanding and obtaining something, often money, from someone, especially in an unfair or oppressive way.
The government's exactions on the people caused widespread resentment. 政府对人民的苛捐杂税引起了普遍的怨恨。

The landlord's exaction of high rents forced many tenants to leave. 房东苛刻的高租金迫使许多房客搬走。

extortion, levy, imposition
donation, gift
