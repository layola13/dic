habilis:/ˈhæbɪlɪs/| adj.| 手巧的，灵巧的；有技能的| Skilled, dexterous, able.  Relating to or denoting Homo habilis. |  Homo habilis was known for its ability to make and use tools.  能人以其制造和使用工具的能力而闻名。 | 近义词: skillful, adept, dexterous, proficient | 反义词: clumsy, inept, unskilled | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *habilis*, from habēre "to have, hold".  The term *Homo habilis* ("handy man") was coined by Louis Leakey. 源自拉丁语 *habilis*，来自 habēre “拥有，持有”。 *Homo habilis*（“能人”）一词由路易斯·利基创造。
