bdelloidea:/dɛˈlɔɪdiə/
|n.|蛭形轮虫类|A class of rotifers (microscopic aquatic animals) characterized by their ability to survive desiccation and reproduce by parthenogenesis.|Bdelloidea are microscopic animals found in freshwater habitats. 蛭形轮虫类是生活在淡水栖息地中的微型动物。|Synonyms:  rotifers (more general),  Philodinidae, Adinetidae, Habrotrochidae (specific families within Bdelloidea)|Antonyms: N/A (There's no direct opposite for a biological classification)|noun
