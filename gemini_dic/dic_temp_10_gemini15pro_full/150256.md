pandit:/ˈpʌndɪt/ (US), /ˈpændɪt/ (UK)| n.|专家，学者，权威；（尤指印度教的）学者，梵学家|A pandit is a Hindu scholar learned in Sanskrit and Hindu philosophy and religion, often acting as an advisor.  A pandit can also refer to an expert or authority in a particular field.|例句：The pandit explained the meaning of the ancient scriptures.  这位梵学家解释了古代经文的意思。

例句：He is considered a pandit in the field of computer science. 他被认为是计算机科学领域的专家。|近义词：scholar, expert, authority, guru, sage|反义词:amateur, novice|词性:noun
