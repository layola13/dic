bridegroom:/ˈbraɪdɡruːm/| n.|新郎|A man who is about to be married or has just been married.|例句：The bridegroom looked very handsome in his tuxedo. 新郎穿着燕尾服看起来很英俊。

例句：The bride and bridegroom exchanged vows. 新娘和新郎交换了誓言。|近义词：groom, husband (after marriage)|反义词：bride|词性：名词
