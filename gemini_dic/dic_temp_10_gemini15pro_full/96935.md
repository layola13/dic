inexterminable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɜːmɪnəbəl/
adj.
不能根除的，消灭不掉的
Not capable of being exterminated; indestructible.
The weeds in my garden seem to be inexterminable.  我花园里的杂草似乎无法根除。
The human spirit is inexterminable.  人类的精神是不可磨灭的。
近义词: indestructible, ineradicable, unquenchable
反义词: eradicable, destructible, extinguishable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *inexterminabilis, from in- "not" + exterminabilis "capable of being exterminated," from Latin exterminare "to drive beyond the boundaries, banish, destroy utterly," from ex- "out of, from" + terminus "boundary, limit."
