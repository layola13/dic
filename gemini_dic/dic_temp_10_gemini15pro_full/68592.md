fantasia:/fænˈteɪʒə/ /fænˈteɪziə/| n. |幻想曲，幻想作品，奇想|A musical composition of a free form, often whimsical and imaginative.  A creation of the imagination; a fanciful notion. | The composer's fantasia was a vibrant and exciting piece.  作曲家的幻想曲是一部充满活力和令人兴奋的作品。

She had fantasias of living in a grand castle. 她幻想住在宏伟的城堡里。|近义词：fantasy, daydream, reverie, vision|反义词：reality, actuality|词性:noun
