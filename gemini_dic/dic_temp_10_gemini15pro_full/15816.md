balk:/bɔːk/ | v. n. | 犹豫，不愿做；障碍，阻碍 | To refuse to do something or to stop and refuse to continue; an obstacle or hindrance. | The horse balked at the jump.  这匹马在跳跃前犹豫了。| 近义词：hesitate, resist, recoil, obstacle, impediment | 反义词：comply, cooperate, proceed, aid, assist | 词性：动词，名词

balk:/bɔːlk/ | v. n. | 阻止，阻挠；障碍物 | To stop or prevent someone from doing something; something that prevents progress. | The high cost of the project balked their plans.  该项目的高成本阻碍了他们的计划。| 近义词：thwart, hinder, obstruct, barrier, hurdle | 反义词：facilitate, promote, encourage | 词性：动词，名词

Etymology:  Late Middle English: perhaps of Scandinavian origin; related to Icelandic *balkr ‘ridge, partition’.  词源：中古英语晚期：可能源自斯堪的纳维亚语；与冰岛语 *balkr “山脊，隔板”相关。
