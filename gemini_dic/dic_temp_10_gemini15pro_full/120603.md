mirthfulness:/ˈmɜːrθfəlnəs/| n.|欢乐，高兴，愉快|The atmosphere was filled with mirthfulness and laughter.  气氛中充满了欢乐和笑声。|Mirthfulness is a state of being merry and cheerful. 欢乐是一种快乐和高兴的状态。|例句：The party was full of mirthfulness. 派对上充满了欢乐。  Her mirthfulness was infectious. 她的快乐很有感染力。|近义词：cheerfulness, gaiety, jollity, merriment|反义词：gloom, sadness, melancholy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mirthfulnes, equivalent to mirthful +‎ -ness.  Mirthful from Middle English mirtheful, equivalent to mirth +‎ -ful.  Mirth from Middle English mirthe, from Old English myrgþ, from Proto-Germanic *murgiþiz.
