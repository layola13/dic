chambering:/ˈtʃeɪmbərɪŋ/ | n. |膛室加工，装填子弹，（乐器）室内乐演奏 |The process of cutting a spiral groove inside a gun barrel to impart spin to a projectile, or the act of loading a cartridge into the chamber of a firearm. Also refers to the performance of chamber music. |The gunsmith meticulously performed the chambering of the rifle barrel.  枪匠一丝不苟地对步枪枪管进行膛室加工。|Synonyms: rifling, loading,  (for music) playing |Antonyms: unloading, discharging |noun

chambering:/ˈtʃeɪmbərɪŋ/ | v. |进行膛室加工，装填子弹，演奏室内乐 |Performing the action of chambering a firearm or playing chamber music. |He was chambering a round when the gun accidentally discharged. 他正在装填子弹时，枪意外走火了。 |Synonyms: loading, inserting, playing |Antonyms: unloading, removing |verb
