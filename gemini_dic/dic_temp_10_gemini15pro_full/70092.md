fevered:/ˈfiːvərd/| adj.|发烧的；狂热的；激动的|Having or showing the symptoms of a fever; intensely excited or enthusiastic.|She had a fevered dream last night. 她昨晚做了一个狂热的梦。|近义词： feverish, delirious, frantic, hectic, heated|反义词：calm, composed, cool, peaceful, relaxed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From fever + -ed, from Old French fevere, from Latin febris "fever."
