bicho:/ˈbiʃu/ (Brazilian Portuguese)| n.|小动物，虫子，家伙，(俚语) 屌，阴茎|A general term for a small creature, often used informally or colloquially.  Can also be used as a slang term for "penis" or as a general term of address like "guy" or "dude".|Example 1: Olha o bicho! (Look at that creature!) 看那只小动物！

Example 2: E aí, bicho, tudo bem? (Hey, dude, everything ok?) 嘿，伙计，一切都好吗？|Synonyms: criatura (creature), animal (animal), inseto (insect) (when referring to insects), cara (guy, dude) |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning being used) |noun
