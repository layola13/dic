matriculants:/məˈtrɪkjʊlənts/
n.
新生，入学的学生
Students entering a college or university.
This year's matriculants are particularly bright. 今年的新生特别聪明。
The university welcomed its new matriculants with a week of orientation.  大学用一周的迎新活动来欢迎新生。
synonyms: freshmen, newcomers, entrants, incomers
antonyms: graduates, alumni, leavers
