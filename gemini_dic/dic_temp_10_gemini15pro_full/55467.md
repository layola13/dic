distorter:/dɪˈstɔːrtər/| n.|失真器，扭曲者，歪曲者|A person or device that distorts something, especially sound or images.  Something that misrepresents or twists facts or information.|The guitarist used a distortion pedal, a type of distorter, to create a fuzzy sound. 吉他手使用失真踏板（一种失真器）来制造模糊的声音。 The media's biased reporting acted as a distorter of the truth.  媒体的偏见报道歪曲了事实。|近义词：deformer, perverter, falsifier|反义词:clarifier, purifier|noun

distort:/dɪˈstɔːrt/| v.|扭曲，变形，歪曲|To pull or twist out of shape; to give a false or misleading account of.|The heat distorted the plastic.  高温使塑料变形。 The media distorted the facts to fit their narrative. 媒体歪曲事实以符合他们的叙述。|近义词：deform, twist, misrepresent|反义词: clarify, represent accurately|verb
