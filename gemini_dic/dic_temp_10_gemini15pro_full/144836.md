over-loved:/ˌoʊvərˈlʌvd/
adjective
过分宠爱的，溺爱的
Loved excessively, often to the point of spoiling.
The over-loved child threw a tantrum when he didn't get his way.  这个被溺爱的孩子在没有得到自己想要的东西时大发脾气。
Synonyms: spoiled, pampered, coddled
Antonyms: neglected, ignored
词性: 形容词
