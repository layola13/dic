directionlessness:/dɪˈrɛkʃənləsnəs/ /daɪˈrɛkʃənləsnəs/
|n.| 无方向感；漫无目的；缺乏方向|A state of lacking clear goals or a sense of purpose.|He felt a deep sense of directionlessness after losing his job.  他失业后感到深深的茫然。|Synonyms: aimlessness, purposelessness, drift, indecision, lack of purpose|Antonyms: purposefulness, decisiveness, determination, focus, resolve|noun
