nonaggressive:/ˌnɑːnəˈɡrɛsɪv/ | adj. | 非侵略性的；不具攻击性的；温顺的 | Not aggressive; not given to hostile or violent behavior. |  He took a nonaggressive stance during the debate.  他在辩论中采取了非侵略性的姿态。

The dog has a nonaggressive temperament. 这只狗性情温顺。| 近义词: peaceful, passive, docile, unaggressive | 反义词: aggressive, hostile, belligerent | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "aggressive" (from Latin "aggress-" past participle stem of "aggredi"  "to attack" + "-ive").
