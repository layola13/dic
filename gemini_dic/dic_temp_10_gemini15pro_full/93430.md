illuminary: /ɪˈluːmɪnəri/ | n. | 杰出人物， luminary, 知识分子，专家 | A person who inspires or influences others, especially one prominent in a particular sphere. |例句：He was a leading illuminary in the world of physics. 他是物理学界的一位杰出人物。

例句：The conference attracted several leading illuminaries in the field of artificial intelligence.  这次会议吸引了人工智能领域几位顶尖的专家。

近义词：luminary, leading light, expert, authority, pundit | 反义词：nonentity, nobody, unknown | 词性：noun
