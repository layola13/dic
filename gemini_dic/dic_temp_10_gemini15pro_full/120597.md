mirrors:/ˈmɪrərz/ (US), /ˈmɪrəz/ (UK)| n. |镜子; 反映; 写照|A surface, typically of glass coated with a metal amalgam, that reflects an image.|The mirrors in the hall made the room look bigger.  大厅里的镜子使房间看起来更大。|synonyms: reflectors, looking glasses|antonyms: absorbers|noun

mirrors:/ˈmɪrərz/ (US), /ˈmɪrəz/ (UK)| v. |反映; 映照; 模仿|To show an image of; to reflect; to imitate.|Her novel mirrors the lives of ordinary people. 她的小说反映了普通人的生活。|synonyms: reflects, echoes, imitates|antonyms: distorts, misrepresents|verb
