drillmaster:/ˈdrɪlˌmæstər/ | n. | 教練, 訓練員, 操練官 | A person who trains others, especially in a strict and disciplined manner, often in a military context. | The drillmaster barked orders at the new recruits. 教練對新兵大聲吼叫著命令。| The drillmaster put the soldiers through their paces. 教練讓士兵們練習步伐。 |Synonyms: instructor, trainer, coach, teacher | Antonyms: student, pupil, trainee | Noun
