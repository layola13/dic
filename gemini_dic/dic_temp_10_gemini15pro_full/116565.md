matiness:/ˈmeɪtinəs/| n.|成熟度，早熟|The matiness of the ten-year-old surprised everyone.  这个十岁孩子的早熟让每个人都感到惊讶。|Maturity, precociousness, sophistication|Immaturity, childishness|noun
Etymology:  From "mate" (meaning mature, ripe) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Ultimately from Old English "gemaca" (suitable, fitting).
