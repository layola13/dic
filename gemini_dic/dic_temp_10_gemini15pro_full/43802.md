crenature: /ˈkrɛnətʃər/ | n. | 城垛，雉堞;  锯齿状缺口 | An open space between the merlons of a battlement. A notched or indented edge, as of a leaf. | The castle walls were topped with crenatures. 城墙顶部有雉堞。 The leaf had a crenature edge. 叶子边缘呈锯齿状。 | 近义词: battlement, indentation, notch, serration | 反义词: smoothness | 词性: 名词

crenate: /ˈkriːneɪt/ | adj. | 城垛状的，锯齿状的 | Having a notched or indented edge, like a battlement. | The crenate leaves of the plant are its most distinctive feature. 这种植物锯齿状的叶子是它最显著的特征。 The old city walls were crenate. 古城墙呈锯齿状。| 近义词: notched, indented, serrated | 反义词: smooth, entire | 词性: 形容词

crenation: /krɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |  钝齿状, 锯齿状 | The state of being crenate; a notched or indented edge. | The crenation of the red blood cells was a sign of dehydration. 红细胞的锯齿状是脱水的迹象。 The artist used crenation to create a textured effect on the sculpture. 艺术家利用锯齿状的边缘在雕塑上创造出一种纹理效果。 | 近义词: notching, indentation, serration | 反义词: smoothness | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Late Latin *crenatura (“indentation, notch”), from Latin crena (“notch, indentation”).
