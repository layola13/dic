harambee:/ˌhɑːrɑːmˈbeɪ/ (US), /ˌhɑːrəmˈbeɪ/ (UK)
n.
集体劳动，团结互助，共同努力
A Swahili word meaning "pulling together" or "all together," referring to a tradition of community self-help events, where people come together to work on a project or raise funds for a common cause.
Example: The village organized a harambee to build a new school.  这个村庄组织了一次集体劳动来建造一所新学校。

Example:  They held a harambee to raise money for the hospital. 他们举行了一次募捐活动为医院筹款。
近义词: cooperation, collaboration, joint effort, teamwork, collective action
反义词: individualism, selfishness, discord, disunity
词性: 名词

词源:
From Swahili *haramu bee* "all pull together", from *haramu* (from Arabic *ḥarām* "forbidden, sacred") + *bee* (an imperative form of *kuwa* "to be").  The original meaning referred to a communal work song and evolved to describe community self-help events.
