clerestoried:/ˈklɪərstɔːrid/ | adj. | 有天窗照明的；高窗照明的 | Having a clerestory or clerestories; lighted by a clerestory. | The clerestoried hall was bright and airy.  高窗照明的厅堂明亮通风。 | The clerestoried nave of the cathedral was impressive.  教堂带天窗的中殿令人印象深刻。| synonyms: high-windowed, skylighted | antonyms: windowless, dark | adjective
