diarthrodial:/ˌdaɪɑːrˈθroʊdiəl/| adj.|滑膜关节的|Relating to or exhibiting diarthrosis; freely movable.|The knee is a diarthrodial joint, allowing for flexion and extension.|近义词：synovial|反义词: synarthrotic|词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *diá* (through, across) + *árthron* (joint) + -al (suffix forming adjectives).  Essentially meaning "moving through a joint."
