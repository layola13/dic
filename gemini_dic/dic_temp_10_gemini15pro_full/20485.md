bituminize: /bɪˈtʃuːmɪnaɪz/ /bɪˈtuːmɪnaɪz/
verb
沥青化; 用沥青处理
To treat or coat with bitumen.
The road was bituminized last year. 去年这条路铺上了沥青。
The driveway needs to be bituminized to protect it from the weather.  车道需要铺上沥青以防风雨侵蚀。

近义词: asphalt, pave, surface
反义词: excavate, dig up
词性: verb
