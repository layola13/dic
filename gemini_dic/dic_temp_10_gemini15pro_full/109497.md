leptorrhine:/ˌlɛptəˈraɪn/
adjective
狭鼻的
Having a nose that is narrow in relation to its length, characteristic of Caucasoids.
Leptorrhine noses are common in populations of European descent.  狭鼻在欧洲血统的人群中很常见。
People of Northern European descent often have leptorrhine noses. 北欧血统的人通常有狭鼻。
synonym:narrow-nosed
antonym:platyrrhine
adjective
