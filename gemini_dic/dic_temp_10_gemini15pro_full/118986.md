methanes:/ˈmɛθeɪnz/| n. |甲烷|A colorless, odorless flammable gas that is the main constituent of natural gas.  It is the simplest alkane and is used as a fuel and is a greenhouse gas.|Methane is a potent greenhouse gas. 甲烷是一种强效的温室气体。|Methane leaks can be dangerous. 甲烷泄漏可能很危险。|synonyms:  natural gas, marsh gas, CH₄|antonyms: N/A|noun
