carmack: /ˈkɑːrmæk/ | n. | 卡马克（通常指约翰·卡马克）|  Primarily refers to John Carmack, a renowned computer programmer and game developer, known for his work on groundbreaking games like Doom and Quake. It can also, less commonly, refer to his programming style or philosophy. | John Carmack is a legend in the gaming industry. 约翰·卡马克是游戏界的传奇人物。 | Carmack revolutionized 3D graphics programming. 卡马克彻底改变了3D图形编程。|  Synonyms: (For the person) genius, pioneer, innovator; (For the style) efficient, optimized. | Antonyms: (For the person) amateur, novice; (For the style) clunky, inefficient. | noun
