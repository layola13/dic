naivety:/naɪˈiːvɪti/ /nɑːˈiːvɪti/| n. |天真，幼稚，轻信|The state or quality of being naive; lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment.|Her naivety led her to trust the stranger. 她的天真使她相信了那个陌生人。|Synonyms: innocence, credulity, ingenuousness|Antonyms: sophistication, worldliness, cynicism|noun
