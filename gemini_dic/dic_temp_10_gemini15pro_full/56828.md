dormitory: /ˈdɔːrmɪtɔːri/ (US) /ˈdɔːmɪtri/ (UK)| n. |宿舍；寝室|A large bedroom for a number of people in a school or institution.|Example:  The university provides dormitory housing for first-year students. 大学为一年级学生提供宿舍住宿。

Example:  He lived in a dormitory during his first year of college. 他大学一年级时住在宿舍里。|近义词: dorm, hostel, residence hall|反义词: apartment, house|词性: noun
