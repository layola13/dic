linothorax:/ˈlaɪnəθɔːræks/ | n. |亚麻胸甲|A type of ancient Greek armor, probably made of layers of linen glued together, sometimes also incorporating metal scales. |Alexander the Great's army was equipped with linothorax armor. 亚历山大大帝的军队配备了亚麻胸甲。| The linothorax offered surprisingly good protection against arrows and swords. 亚麻胸甲对弓箭和刀剑提供了令人惊讶的良好保护。 |近义词:cuirass, armor, breastplate |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps vulnerability) | noun
