believably:/bɪˈliːvəbli/
adverb
可信地；令人信服地
in a way that makes someone believe that something is true or likely
He believably described the events of that night. 他令人信服地描述了那天晚上的事件。

The actor believably portrayed a historical figure.  这位演员令人信服地刻画了一位历史人物。

近义词:convincingly, plausibly, credibly
反义词:unbelievably, implausibly, incredibly
词性:adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From believe (from Old English belīefan "to believe, trust; hold dear, value; think, suppose," from be- "thoroughly" + līefan "to allow, permit, believe") + -ably (suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a specified manner").
