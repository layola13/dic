incommunicative:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv/
adjective
不爱交际的，沉默寡言的
Not disposed to communicate; reserved.
He became incommunicative after the accident. 他在事故后变得沉默寡言。

近义词:
reserved, taciturn, unforthcoming, withdrawn, silent, quiet, reticent

反义词:
communicative, talkative, outgoing, sociable, loquacious
