materialistic:/məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. |物质主义的；拜金的 | Excessively concerned with material possessions; believing that material possessions are the most important things in life. | She's so materialistic; all she cares about is designer clothes and expensive cars. 她太物质了，她只在乎名牌服装和豪车。 |  近义词: acquisitive, greedy, mercenary | 反义词: spiritual, altruistic, ascetic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "material" + "-istic". "Material" comes from Late Latin "materialis" meaning "relating to matter".  "-istic" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of".
