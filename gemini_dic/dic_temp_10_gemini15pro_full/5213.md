alignment: /əˈlaɪnmənt/ | n. | 校准，队列，对齐，准线; 结盟，联合 |  The process of adjusting parts so that they are in proper relative position.  An arrangement in a straight line or in correct relative positions. A position of agreement or alliance. | The wheels need alignment.  车轮需要校准。| The two countries are in close alignment on this issue. 这两个国家在这个问题上紧密结盟。 | synonyms: arrangement, positioning, adjustment, alliance, affiliation | antonyms: misalignment, disunity, discord | noun
