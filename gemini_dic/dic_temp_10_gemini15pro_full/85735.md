hawker:/ˈhɔːkər/ (UK) /ˈhɔːkɚ/ (US)| n.|小贩；叫卖者|A person who travels about selling goods, typically advertising them by shouting or ringing a bell.|The hawker sold fresh fruit and vegetables from his cart. 小贩从他的手推车上出售新鲜的水果和蔬菜。|近义词：vendor, peddler, salesman, merchant|反义词:buyer, customer|noun

hawker:/ˈhɔːkər/ (UK) /ˈhɔːkɚ/ (US)| v.|兜售；叫卖|To sell goods by going from place to place, usually advertising them by shouting.|He hawked his wares on the street corner. 他在街角叫卖他的商品。|近义词：peddle, vend, sell|反义词:buy, purchase|verb
