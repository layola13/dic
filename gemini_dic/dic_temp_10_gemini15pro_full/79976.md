go-bush:/ɡəʊ bʊʃ/ | vi. | 逃亡；躲藏；到灌木丛中去；过隐居生活 | To escape into the bush; to live in a remote or sparsely populated area, often to avoid civilization or the law. | He went bush after the robbery to avoid the police. 他在抢劫后逃到了丛林里以躲避警察。| 近义词：abscond, hide, flee, escape | 反义词: remain, stay, face | verb

go-bush:/ɡəʊ bʊʃ/ | adj. |  粗野的；未开化的；与丛林相关的 | Relating to the bush; unrefined; uncultivated. |  His go-bush manners embarrassed his city friends. 他粗野的举止让他的城里朋友感到尴尬。 | 近义词：wild, untamed, rustic, uncivilized | 反义词: civilized, refined, cultivated | adjective
