outflows:/ˈaʊtˌfloʊz/
noun
流出；流出物；外流
The movement of money or assets out of a fund, account, or country.
Large outflows of capital from the country.  大量的资金从该国流出。
synonyms: outflow, efflux, discharge, issue, egress
antonyms: inflow, influx, intake
n.
