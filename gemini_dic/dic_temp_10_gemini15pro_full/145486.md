overcleverness:/ˌoʊvərˈklɛvərnəs/
n.| 过分聪明，自作聪明 | An excessive or misplaced cleverness that often backfires.  Being too clever for one's own good, often resulting in a negative outcome.| His overcleverness led him to a convoluted solution that no one could understand. 他的自作聪明导致他提出了一个没人能理解的复杂方案。 |  over-ingenuity, over-subtlety,  cunning | wisdom, prudence | noun
