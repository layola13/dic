exest:/ɪɡˈzɪst/| v.|存在，生存|To have actual being; to live.|例句：Dinosaurs no longer exist. 恐龙已经不复存在了。

例句：Does life exist on other planets? 其他行星上存在生命吗？|近义词：live, survive, be, occur|反义词：perish, vanish, disappear|词性：动词

Etymology:
From Latin *exsistere*, from ex- 'out' + sistere 'to stand, cause to stand'.
