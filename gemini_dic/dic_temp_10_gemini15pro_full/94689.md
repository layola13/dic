improbability:/ɪmˌprɒbəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可能性，未必会发生的事 |The improbability of the event made it all the more remarkable.  这个事件的不可能性使它更加引人注目。|例句：The improbability of winning the lottery is very high.  赢得彩票的可能性非常低。|近义词：unlikelihood, implausibility |反义词：probability, likelihood |词性:名词
Etymology: From im- (not) + probable (likely) + -ity (noun suffix).  来源于im-（不）+ probable（可能的）+ -ity（名词后缀）。
