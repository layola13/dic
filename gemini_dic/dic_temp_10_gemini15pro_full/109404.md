lents:/lɛnts/ | n. | 四旬斋;  (Lent) | The period of forty days, not including Sundays, leading up to Easter, observed in many Christian churches as a time of fasting, abstinence, and penitence.  |  Lent is a time for reflection and prayer. 四旬斋是反思和祈祷的时期。| synonym: fasting period, penitential season | antonym: feast, celebration | noun
