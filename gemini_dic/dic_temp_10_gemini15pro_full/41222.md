coralled:/kəˈrɔːld/ /kəˈræld/| v. |把…赶入畜栏；聚集；控制|To gather a group of people or animals and confine them in a restricted area, often for control or protection.  Derived from "corral," a noun meaning an enclosure for livestock.|The police corralled the protesters in the square. 警察把抗议者聚集在广场上。|The cowboys corralled the cattle for branding. 牛仔们把牛赶进畜栏进行烙印。|近义词：gather, round up, herd, confine, enclose|反义词:release, disperse, free|词性:动词
