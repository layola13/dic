falcula:/fælˈkjuːlə/ | n. |小镰刀，小刀，钩状物|A small, curved, sharp-pointed blade, often used in surgery or for dissecting.  A diminutive of falx, meaning sickle. |The surgeon used a falcula to carefully dissect the tissue. 外科医生用小镰刀仔细地解剖组织。| The botanist used a falcula to remove a small section of the plant for examination. 植物学家用小刀切下一小块植物进行检查。 |近义词:scalpel, lancet, knife|反义词: N/A| noun
