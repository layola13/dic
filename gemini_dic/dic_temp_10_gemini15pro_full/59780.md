ecosystems:/ˈiːkoʊˌsɪstəmz/ | n. | 生态系统 | A biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment. |
例句：Rainforests are complex ecosystems with a vast array of species.  热带雨林是拥有大量物种的复杂生态系统。

例句：Protecting our planet's ecosystems is crucial for our survival. 保护我们星球的生态系统对我们的生存至关重要。

近义词：biomes, environments, habitats | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From eco- (from Greek *oîkos 'house, dwelling place') + system
