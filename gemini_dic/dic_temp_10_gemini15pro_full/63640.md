eophytic:/ˌiːoʊˈfɪtɪk/
adj.
始生植物的; 早期植物的
Of or relating to the earliest land plants.  Specifically, relating to or denoting plants of the Eophytic era, a hypothetical geological era preceding the Paleozoic, during which the first land plants supposedly evolved.
例句：Eophytic flora colonized the barren land.  始生植物群落覆盖了贫瘠的土地。

例句：Scientists debate the characteristics of eophytic ecosystems. 科学家们争论始生植物生态系统的特征。

近义词：primitive, early, primordial
反义词：modern, recent, advanced
词性:  adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From eo- (dawn, early) + -phytic (relating to plants).  The prefix "eo-" comes from Greek "ēōs" meaning "dawn". The suffix "-phytic" relates to plants and is derived from Greek "phuton" meaning "plant".
