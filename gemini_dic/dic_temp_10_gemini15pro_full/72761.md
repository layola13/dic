fogging:/ˈfɒɡɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈfɔːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.|喷雾，起雾；(医学)记忆模糊|The act of applying a pesticide or disinfectant as a fine mist, or the condensation of moisture on a surface. Also can refer to a state of mental confusion.|例句：They are fogging the area for mosquitoes. 他们正在该地区喷洒灭蚊剂。

例句：The windows were fogging up due to the humidity. 由于潮湿，窗户起雾了。|近义词：spraying, misting (for liquid application); clouding, steaming (for condensation); confusion, bewilderment (for mental state)|反义词:clearing, ventilating (for condensation); clarity, focus (for mental state)|词性:noun, verb
