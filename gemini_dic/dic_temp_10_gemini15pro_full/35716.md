cocaptains:/ˌkoʊˈkæptənz/| n.|共同队长，联席队长|Two individuals sharing the duties and responsibilities of a captain.  A team, group, or organization may have cocaptains to distribute leadership or in cases where two people are equally qualified.|例句：The team elected two cocaptains this year. 今年这个队伍选出了两名共同队长。|例句：The cocaptains led the team onto the field. 两位共同队长带领队伍进入场地。|近义词：joint captains, dual captains|反义词:|noun
