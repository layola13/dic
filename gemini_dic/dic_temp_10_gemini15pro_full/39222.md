conglomerators:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪtəz/ (US) /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪtərz/ (UK)| n.|企业集团, 联合大公司|A company that owns a controlling stake in a number of smaller companies all operating in generally diverse business areas.| Berkshire Hathaway is a famous example of a conglomerate. 伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司是企业集团的著名例子。|Synonyms: combine, corporation, multinational |Antonyms: subsidiary, small business| noun

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (US) /kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (UK)| n.|聚集体, 砾岩,企业集团|A thing formed by conglomerating; a mass or collection.|The rock is a conglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。|Synonyms: mixture, combination, aggregate |Antonyms: element, component| noun

conglomerate:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (US) /kənˈɡlɑːməreɪt/ (UK)| v.|聚集, 集结, 合并|Gather or form into a mass or group.|Several small businesses conglomerated to form a larger corporation.  几家小企业合并成了一家大公司。|Synonyms: amass, accumulate, combine |Antonyms: separate, divide, disperse| verb
