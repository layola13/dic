what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|pron. det. adv. int.|什么，多么，|Used to ask for information about something. 用于询问有关某事的信息。|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What color is her hair?  她头发是什么颜色？


近义词:which|反义词:(无)|pron.

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|det.|什么，哪一个，哪一些|Used with a noun to ask for information about people or things. 与名词连用，用于询问有关人或事的信息。|What books did you buy? 你买了哪些书？

What size shoes do you wear? 你穿多大号的鞋？

What a shame!  真可惜！

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|int.|什么？|Used to express surprise or disbelief, or to ask for something to be repeated. 用于表达惊讶或怀疑，或要求重复某事。|What? You're leaving already? 什么？ 你已经要走了？

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, neuter singular nominative and accusative of hwā (“who”). Cognate with Scots what (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Gothic 𐍈𐍀 ("what").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What time did you arrive? = 你几点到的？


现在完成时:
What have you been up to lately? = 你最近在忙些什么？
What has he learned from this experience? = 他从这次经历中学到了什么？

过去完成时:
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

将来时:
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后会做什么？
What time will the meeting start? = 会议几点开始？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to say. = 我希望我知道该说什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you thinking about? = 你在想什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want here. = 这里没有我想要的东西。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样？/ ...怎么办？
What if...? = 如果...怎么办？/要是...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？/为什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
