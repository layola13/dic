diurnally: /daɪˈɜːnəli/ /daɪˈɜːrnəli/
adjective
每日地；白天活动的
Happening or active during the daytime.
Bats are not diurnally active, they prefer to fly at night.  蝙蝠不是白天活动的，它们更喜欢在夜间飞行。
The diurnally blooming flowers closed as darkness fell.  随着夜幕降临，白天开花的花朵闭合了。
synonyms: daily, daytime, during the day
antonyms: nocturnally, nightly
词性: adjective
