bathhouses:/ˈbæθˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
公共浴室，澡堂
A building containing many bathrooms or showers, often with facilities such as steam rooms and saunas.
The Romans were famous for their elaborate bathhouses. 罗马人以其精心设计的澡堂而闻名。

The town's old bathhouses were recently renovated.  小镇的旧澡堂最近翻新了。
