overstriven:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɪvən/ |adj.|过度努力的；过度奋斗的；精疲力竭的|Exhausted and worn out from excessive striving or effort.  This word is often used to describe someone who has pushed themselves too hard, physically or mentally, and is now suffering the consequences.|He looked overstriven and weary after working non-stop for a week.  他连续工作一周后看起来过度劳累，疲惫不堪。|The overstriven athlete collapsed at the finish line.  这位过度拼命的运动员在终点线倒下了。|近义词:overworked, exhausted, burnt out, stressed, strained|反义词:relaxed, rested, refreshed|词性:形容词

词源: From "over-" (excessively) + "striven," the past participle of "strive" (to exert oneself vigorously).  The word "strive" comes from Old French "estriver" and ultimately from a Germanic root meaning "to contend."
