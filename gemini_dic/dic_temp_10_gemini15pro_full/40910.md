cooldowns:/ˈkuːldaʊnz/| n.|冷却时间，冷却期|A period of time required for something to cool down, often referring to a period of inactivity between strenuous activities or the time needed for a system or device to return to its normal operating temperature after overheating.  It can also refer to a period of time for emotions to calm down after an argument or disagreement.|The athlete took several cooldowns during the intense workout.|近义词：rest periods, breaks, recovery time, waiting periods|反义词:warm-ups, heat-ups
