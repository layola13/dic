cupuliferous:/ˌkjuːpjuˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
壳斗科植物的; 产生壳斗的
Bearing cupules (cup-shaped structures containing fruits or nuts, characteristic of the oak family (Fagaceae)).
The tree is cupuliferous, indicating its membership in the Fagaceae family. 这棵树是壳斗科植物，表明它属于山毛榉科。
Near Synonyms: acorn-bearing, nut-bearing
Antonyms: acarpous (not producing fruit)
词性: adjective
