assecuration:/əˌʃʊəˈreɪʃn/ |n.|保证；担保；确信；断言|A positive declaration intended to give confidence; an assurance.  It implies a stronger degree of certainty and formality than "assurance."|He gave me his assecuration that the work would be completed on time. 他向我保证工作会按时完成。|Synonyms: assurance, guarantee, pledge, avowal|Antonyms: denial, refutation|Noun
