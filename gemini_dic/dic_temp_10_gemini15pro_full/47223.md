dazed:/deɪzd/| adj.| 茫然的，恍惚的，震惊的 | In a state of stunned confusion; bewildered. |  He wandered around in a dazed state after being hit on the head. 他被击中头部后茫然地走来走去。| synonyms: stunned, bewildered, confused, disoriented | antonyms: alert, aware, focused, oriented | adjective

dazed:/deɪzd/| v.| 使茫然，使恍惚 | To stun or stupefy with a blow, shock, or surprise. | The bright lights dazed her.  强光使她眼花缭乱。 | synonyms: stun, stupefy, bewilder | antonyms: enlighten, clarify, focus | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *deisaz "to become insensible," related to deyja "to die."  The original sense was "to lie as if dead," then "to be stunned."
