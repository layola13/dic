ETYMOLOGY->
From fashionable + -ness.  "fashionable" comes from the Old French "façon" (manner, way, fashion) ultimately from Latin "factio" (a making, doing), from "facere" (to make, do). "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
