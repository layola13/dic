feebleminded:/ˈfiːblˌmaɪndɪd/| adj.|低能的；愚笨的； слабоумный (Russian)|Having significantly below average intelligence.  Characterized by a lack of mental acuteness.  Once a common medical term, now considered offensive.|例句：In the past, the term "feebleminded" was used to describe individuals with intellectual disabilities. 过去，“feebleminded”一词用于描述智力残疾的个人。

例句：Using "feebleminded" is now considered highly offensive and inappropriate.  现在使用“feebleminded”被认为是极其冒犯和不合适的。|近义词：stupid, foolish, simpleminded, unintelligent, witless, slow-witted, dim-witted, half-witted, mentally deficient, intellectually disabled|反义词：intelligent, bright, clever, smart, astute, sharp, brilliant, gifted|词性:adjective
