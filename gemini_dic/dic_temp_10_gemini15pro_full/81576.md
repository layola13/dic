grease:/ɡriːs/ /ɡris/| n. vt.|油脂；润滑油；贿赂|A thick oily substance, especially as used as a lubricant.  To lubricate with grease.|例句：The mechanic greased the gears. 技工给齿轮上了油。
例句：He greased the palm of the official to get the permit. 他贿赂官员以获得许可证。|近义词：lubricant, oil, fat, bribe|反义词:cleanliness|词性:名词,动词

greasy:/ˈɡriːsi/ /ˈɡriːzi/| adj.|油腻的；滑溜溜的；谄媚的|Covered with or containing grease; slippery; unpleasantly or insincerely polite.|例句：The food was too greasy for me. 这食物对我来说太油腻了。
例句：He gave me a greasy smile. 他给了我一个谄媚的微笑。|近义词：oily, slippery, slimy, unctuous|反义词:clean, dry|词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *graisse, from Late Latin *crassus, from Latin crassus (“thick, fat”).
