capitulator:/ˈkəˈpɪtʃʊˌleɪtər/| n. |投降者，屈服者|A person who surrenders or gives in to an opponent or demand.|例句：The capitulator signed the treaty, ending the war.  投降者签署了条约，结束了战争。

例句：He was branded a capitulator for agreeing to their terms. 他因为同意他们的条件而被称为投降者。|近义词：surrenderer, defeatist, quitter|反义词：resister, fighter, holdout|词性:noun
