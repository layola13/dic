arthropathy:/ɑːrˈθrɒpəθi/ /ɑːrˈθrɑːpəθi/
n.
关节病
Any disease of a joint.
例句：The doctor diagnosed her with arthropathy after examining her swollen knee.  医生检查了她肿胀的膝盖后，诊断她患有关节病。

例句：Osteoarthritis is a common form of arthropathy. 骨关节炎是一种常见的关节病。
近义词：joint disease, joint pain, arthritis
反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease state)
词性: noun
