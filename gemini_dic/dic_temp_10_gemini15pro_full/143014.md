outbidders:/ˌaʊtˈbɪdərz/
noun
出价更高者；超过出价者
Those who offer a higher price than others, especially at an auction.
The outbidders grumbled as the gavel fell, signaling the end of the auction. 出价失败者在拍卖槌落下、拍卖结束时抱怨着。
synonyms: higher bidders, overbidders, successful bidders (sometimes), rivals
antonyms: underbidders, losing bidders
n.
