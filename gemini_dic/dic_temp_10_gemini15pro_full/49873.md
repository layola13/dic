demotivator:/ˌdiːˈməʊtɪveɪtə(r)/ | n. |   desmotivador;  desmotivational poster; 丧图; 负能量图 | Something that reduces motivation; especially : a demotivational poster.  A parody of a motivational poster, typically featuring a photograph or image accompanied by a sardonic, cynical, or humorous caption, and intended to dishearten rather than inspire. | The demotivator on his wall featuring a picture of a sinking ship with the caption "Giving Up: It's easier than trying" perfectly reflected his current mood. 这张贴在他墙上的丧图，画着一艘正在下沉的船，配文是“放弃吧：这比尝试更容易”，完美地反映了他现在的心情。 |  The demotivator with a picture of a messy desk and the caption "Procrastination: Hard work pays off later. Laziness pays off now." made me chuckle. 这张丧图上画着一张凌乱的桌子，配文是“拖延症：努力工作以后会有回报。懒惰现在就有回报。” 让我笑了。 | synonyms: discourage, dishearten, dispirit, demoralize | antonyms: motivator, inspiration, encouragement | noun


demotivate:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪt/ | v. | 使失去动力; 使灰心 |  To reduce or destroy someone's motivation. |  Constant criticism can demotivate even the most enthusiastic employees.  不断的批评会使即使是最热情的员工失去动力。 | The team's recent losses have demotivated them. 球队最近的失利让他们灰心丧气。| synonyms: discourage, dishearten, dispirit, demoralize | antonyms: motivate, inspire, encourage | verb


demotivating:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人沮丧的; 令人泄气的 | Causing someone to lose motivation. | Working long hours for little pay is demotivating.  长时间工作却报酬很少，这很令人沮丧。 |  The lack of recognition for her hard work was demotivating.  对她辛勤工作的缺乏认可令人沮丧。 | synonyms: discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting, demoralizing | antonyms: motivating, inspiring, encouraging | adjective
