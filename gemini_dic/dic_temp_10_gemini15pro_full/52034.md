dialectologically:/ˌdaɪəˌlɛktəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˌdaɪəˌlɛktəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adv.
方言学地
in a way that relates to the study of dialects.
This variation is explained dialectologically. 这种变异可以用方言学来解释。
The pronunciation of this word varies dialectologically.  这个词的发音因方言而异。
近义词: regionally
反义词: universally
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dialect + -o- + -logy + -ical + -ly.  "Dialect" comes from the Greek "dialektos" meaning "conversation, discourse, language of a district."  "-logy" signifies "study of." "-ical" forms an adjective meaning "relating to," and "-ly" forms an adverb.
