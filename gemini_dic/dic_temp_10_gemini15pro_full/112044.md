longbeard:/ˈlɒŋbɪəd/ (US) /ˈlɒŋbɪəd/ (UK)| n.|长胡子的人；（尤指）德高望重的人；（某种）长须鱼|A man, especially an old or wise man, with a long beard. Also, a type of fish with long barbels.|The village elder, a venerable longbeard, offered sage advice.|近义词：patriarch, elder, sage|反义词:youth, youngster|noun

longbeard:/ˈlɒŋbɪəd/ (US) /ˈlɒŋbɪəd/ (UK)| adj.|长胡子的|Having a long beard.|The longbeard wizard cast a powerful spell.|近义词：bearded|反义词:clean-shaven|adjective
