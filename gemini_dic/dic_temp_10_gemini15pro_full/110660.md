lincture:/ˈlɪŋktʃər/| n.|药水；涂剂；(尤指)甜药水|A liquid medicine, especially a sweet one.|She gave the child a lincture for his cough. 她给孩子喝了一种治咳嗽的药水。|近义词:potion, elixir, syrup|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *linctūra, from Latin linctus (“licked”), past participle of lingō (“to lick”).
