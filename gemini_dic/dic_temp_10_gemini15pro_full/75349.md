frosty:/ˈfrɔːsti/ (US) /ˈfrɒsti/ (UK)| adj.|寒冷的；结霜的；冷淡的|Covered with frost; very cold; unfriendly and lacking warmth.|例句：The frosty air nipped at our cheeks. 刺骨的寒风吹在我们的脸上。|近义词：icy, freezing, frigid, cold, chilly, wintry, glacial, hostile, unfriendly|反义词:warm, friendly, cordial|词性:adjective
frosty:/ˈfrɔːsti/ (US) /ˈfrɒsti/ (UK)| adj.|寒冷的；结霜的；冷淡的|Giving the impression of coldness or unfriendliness.|例句：She gave me a frosty stare. 她冷冷地瞪了我一眼。|近义词：icy, freezing, frigid, cold, chilly, wintry, glacial, hostile, unfriendly|反义词:warm, friendly, cordial|词性:adjective

词源: From Old English *forstiġ "frosty", from frost + -iġ, adjectival suffix.
