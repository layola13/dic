inconversable:/ˌɪnkɒnvɜːsəbl/ /ˌɪnkɑːnvɜːrsəbl/
adjective
不能交谈的，不善言谈的
Unable to engage in conversation; not inclined to converse.
He was so shy as to be almost inconversable. 他太害羞了，几乎不能交谈。
近义词:uncommunicative, taciturn, reticent
反义词:talkative, loquacious, communicative
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + conversable (able to converse).  "Conversable" comes from the Late Latin conversabilis, from conversari "to associate with," frequentative of convertere "to turn around, be together," from com- "together" + vertere "to turn."
