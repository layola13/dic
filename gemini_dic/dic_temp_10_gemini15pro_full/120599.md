mirrory:/ˈmɪrəri/| adj.|像镜子的；光亮的；反射的|Reflecting light; resembling a mirror.|The lake's surface was mirrory, reflecting the surrounding trees. 湖面像镜子一样，倒映着周围的树木。|The polished floor had a mirrory finish. 磨光的地板像镜子一样光亮。|近义词：reflective, specular, glassy|反义词：dull, matte|词性:形容词


词源: From mirror + -y, meaning "like a mirror."  Derived ultimately from Old French *mirëoir, from Latin mirari "to wonder at."
