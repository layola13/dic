inherits:/ɪnˈherɪts/ | v. |继承，遗传|receives (money, property, or a title) as an heir at the death of the previous holder.|She inherited her mother's house. 她继承了她母亲的房子。|Synonyms: acquire, receive, obtain, derive, gain|Antonyms: forfeit, lose, relinquish|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enheriter (to inherit), from Latin inhereditare, from in- (in) + heres (heir).
