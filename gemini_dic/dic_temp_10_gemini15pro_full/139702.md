oceanographers:/ˌoʊʃəˈnɑːɡrəfərz/
n.
海洋学家
Scientists who study the ocean.
Oceanographers study the physical and biological aspects of the ocean. 海洋学家研究海洋的物理和生物方面。
Many oceanographers are concerned about the effects of pollution on marine life. 很多海洋学家都担心污染对海洋生物的影响。
近义词：marine scientists, marine biologists
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps land surveyors or geologists in a contrasting sense)
词性: noun
