glens:/ɡlɛnz/| n. |峡谷，狭窄的山谷|Narrow, secluded valleys.|We hiked through the glens of Scotland. 我们徒步穿越了苏格兰的峡谷。|The glens were filled with wildflowers. 峡谷里开满了野花。|近义词：valleys, canyons, gorges|反义词:mountains, peaks|词性:名词

Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic *gleann*, from Old Irish *glenn*, ultimately from Proto-Celtic *glenā*. Cognate with Welsh *glyn*.
