clear-sighted:/ˌklɪərˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. |头脑清晰的，有远见的| Having a clear understanding of a situation and good judgment about what to do next; perceptive.| She demonstrated clear-sighted leadership during the crisis. 她在这场危机中展现了富有远见的领导才能。|  Example: He made a clear-sighted decision to invest in the new technology. 他做出了一个有远见的决定，投资了这项新技术。|Synonyms: discerning, perceptive, astute, insightful, wise, judicious, shrewd | Antonyms: short-sighted, unperceptive, myopic, obtuse, foolish | word property: adjective

clear-sighted:/ˌklɪərˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. |明智的，精明的| Able to make good judgments based on a clear understanding of a situation.|  His clear-sighted analysis of the market helped the company avoid a major loss. 他对市场的精辟分析帮助公司避免了一次重大损失。| Example:  She has a clear-sighted view of the future of the industry. 她对该行业的未来有着清晰的认识。| Synonyms: wise, judicious, prudent, sagacious | Antonyms: unwise, imprudent, foolish, injudicious | word property: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "clear" + "sighted". "Clear" meaning free from obscurity or ambiguity and "sighted" meaning having sight or vision.  Therefore, the word literally means having clear vision, which extends metaphorically to having clear mental vision or judgment.
