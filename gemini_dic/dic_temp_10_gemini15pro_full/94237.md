imperilling: /ɪmˈpɛrəlɪŋ/ /ɪmˈpɛrɪlɪŋ/
verb
使处于危险，危及
putting someone or something in danger
The rising floodwaters are imperilling the lives of the villagers.  上涨的洪水正危及村民的生命。

The war is imperilling the region’s fragile stability. 这场战争正在危及该地区脆弱的稳定。
endangering, jeopardizing, risking
securing, safeguarding
v.
