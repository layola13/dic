enclasp:/ɪnˈklɑːsp/ | v. | 拥抱；紧握；扣住；环绕 | To hold firmly within arms or hands; embrace.  To surround or enclose. | She enclasped the child in a warm hug. 她紧紧地拥抱了孩子。 The ivy enclasped the old oak tree. 常春藤缠绕着古老的橡树。 | 近义词：hug, embrace, grasp, clasp, enclose, surround | 反义词：release, free | 词性: 动词

词源:  From en- + clasp.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "into". "Clasp" comes from Middle English claspe, from Old Norse klǫsp.
