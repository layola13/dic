despoilers:/dɪˈspɔɪlər z/
n.
掠夺者，破坏者
Those who pillage or plunder; those who rob, especially by force.  Those who strip something of its value, beauty, or effectiveness.
The despoilers ransacked the village, leaving nothing but ashes.  掠夺者洗劫了村庄，只留下一片灰烬。

The despoilers of the environment must be held accountable. 破坏环境的人必须被追究责任。

Synonyms: plunderers, pillagers, looters, raiders, ravagers, vandals, wreckers
Antonyms: protectors, guardians, conservators, preservers

词性: noun (名词)
