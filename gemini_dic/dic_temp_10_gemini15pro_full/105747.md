kipping:/ˈkɪpɪŋ/
verb
摆动; 快速摇摆; (体操)摆动身体; 摇摆(身体)
swinging the body, especially in gymnastics, in order to gain momentum
She's been practicing her kipping pull-ups. 她一直在练习摆动引体向上。
近义词: swinging, rocking, swaying
反义词: still, motionless
词性: verb
