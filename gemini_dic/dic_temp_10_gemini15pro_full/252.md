abdominalia: /ˌæbdəˈmɪniə/
noun
腹部；腹甲
The abdominalia of the insect were segmented. = 昆虫的腹部是分节的。
The crab's abdominalia were tucked under its carapace. = 蟹的腹部缩在它的甲壳下。
English Explanation:  The posterior segments of the body of an arthropod, especially an insect or crustacean, located behind the thorax or cephalothorax.  Often referring to the ventral (underside) portion of the abdomen, particularly in crustaceans.  Can also refer to the abdominal appendages.
Synonyms: abdomen, belly, underbelly
Antonyms: thorax, cephalothorax
词性: noun
