debellate:/dɪˈbɛleɪt/
verb
征服，击败，彻底战胜
To vanquish or subdue completely, especially by war; to crush.
The Roman legions debellated their enemies and expanded the empire. 罗马军团征服了他们的敌人，并扩张了帝国。
After a long and bloody campaign, the rebels were finally debellated. 经过漫长而血腥的战役，叛军最终被击败。
conquer, vanquish, subdue, defeat, overcome, crush
- (No clear single antonym; consider "lose to," "be defeated by," "surrender to")
