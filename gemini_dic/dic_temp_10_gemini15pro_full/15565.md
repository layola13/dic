baguette:/bæˈɡɛt/ | n. | 法式长棍面包 | A long, thin loaf of French bread. |例句：I bought a fresh baguette from the bakery. 我从面包店买了一条新鲜的法式长棍面包。

例句：The baguette was crusty on the outside and soft on the inside.  这条法棍面包外皮酥脆，内里柔软。| 近义词：French bread, French stick | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *baguette*, diminutive of *bague* "ring, hoop".  The name refers to the shape of the bread.
