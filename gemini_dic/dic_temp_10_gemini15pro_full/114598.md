maladministration:/ˌmælədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/| n. |管理不善，治理不当|Bad or dishonest management of something, typically by a government or organization.| The committee investigated claims of maladministration in the department. 委员会调查了该部门管理不善的指控。| Example: The maladministration of the fund led to significant financial losses.  资金管理不善导致了重大经济损失。|Synonyms: mismanagement, misconduct, corruption, incompetence|Antonyms: good governance, proper management|noun
