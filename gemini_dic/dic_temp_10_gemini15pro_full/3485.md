afterglows:/ˈæftərɡloʊz/
n.
余晖，晚霞；美好的回忆，回味
a light or radiance remaining in the sky after sunset. The pleasant memory of a past experience.
The afterglow of the sunset was breathtaking. 日落后的余晖令人叹为观止。

We basked in the afterglow of our victory. 我们沉浸在胜利的喜悦之中。

近义词：aftermath, remnants, consequences, echoes
反义词：prelude, beginning, onset

词性: 名词 (plural)
词源:  From after + glow.  "After" meaning following and "glow" referring to a soft light.
