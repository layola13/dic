atoll:/ˈætɒl/, /ˈætɔːl/| n.|环礁，环状珊瑚岛|A ring-shaped coral reef, island, or chain of islands formed of coral. |例句：The Maldives are a chain of atolls in the Indian Ocean. 马尔代夫是印度洋上的一串环礁。

例句：We explored the beautiful atoll during our diving trip. 在我们的潜水之旅中，我们探索了美丽的环礁。|近义词：reef, island, cay|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "continent")|词性:noun
