forthrightly:/fɔːrθˈraɪtli/ | adv. | 直率地，坦率地 | In a direct and honest manner, without hesitation. | She forthrightly expressed her disagreement with the proposal. 她坦率地表达了她对这项提议的反对。| He forthrightly admitted his mistake. 他坦率地承认了自己的错误。 | 近义词: candidly, frankly, directly, openly, bluntly | 反义词: evasively, indirectly, subtly, deceptively | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Middle English forthright, from forth (forward) + right (straight).  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "forthright" into the adverb "forthrightly".
