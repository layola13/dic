adduction:/əˈdʌkʃən/
noun
内收
The movement of a limb or other part toward the midline of the body or toward another part.
The doctor tested the adduction of her hip. 医生检查了她髋关节的内收情况。

Muscle adduction is an important part of many exercises.  肌肉内收是许多运动的重要组成部分。

近义词:  approximation
反义词: abduction
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin adductio, from Latin adducere "to lead to," from ad- "to" + ducere "to lead."

USAGE->
The adduction of the arm was painful. =  手臂的内收很痛苦。

The therapist helped the patient with hip adduction exercises. =  治疗师帮助病人进行髋关节内收练习。

PHRASE->
Hip adduction = 髋内收
Adduction strength = 内收力量
Adduction movement = 内收运动
Adduction exercise = 内收练习
Adduction of the thumb = 拇指内收
