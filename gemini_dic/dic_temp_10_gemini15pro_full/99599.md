interjacency:/ˌɪntərˈdʒeɪsənsi/| n.|相互邻接，介于中间|The interjacency of the two properties caused disputes. 这两处房产的相互邻接引发了纠纷。|例句：The interjacency of the two countries led to increased trade. 这两个国家的毗邻促进了贸易往来。

例句：The interjacency of the two ecosystems allows for species migration.  这两个生态系统的邻接使得物种得以迁徙。

近义词：proximity, adjacency, contiguity|反义词: distance, separation|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *interiacēns*, present participle of *interiacēre* (“to lie between”), from *inter-* (“between”) + *iacēre* (“to lie”).
