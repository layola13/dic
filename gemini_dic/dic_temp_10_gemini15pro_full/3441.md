afrikaner:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnər/ | n. |南非白人（尤指布尔人）|A descendant of Dutch and Huguenot settlers in southern Africa, especially one whose native language is Afrikaans.  A person of European, especially Dutch, descent born in South Africa. |例句：The Afrikaners have a rich cultural heritage. 南非白人拥有丰富的文化遗产。

例句：Many Afrikaners speak Afrikaans as their first language. 许多南非白人以南非荷兰语为母语。|近义词：Boer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Black South African in certain contexts, but use with caution due to potential oversimplification and historical implications.)|词性:noun
