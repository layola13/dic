derail:/dɪˈreɪl/
v. |脱轨；阻挠；扰乱|To cause a train or other vehicle to leave its tracks. To obstruct the progress of something.|The train was derailed by a landslide. 火车因山体滑坡而脱轨。|synonyms: obstruct, disrupt, thwart, hinder, foil |antonyms: assist, aid, support, promote, advance|verb
derail:/dɪˈreɪl/
n. |脱轨|The act of a train or other vehicle leaving its tracks.|The derailment caused significant delays. 脱轨造成了严重的延误。|synonyms:divergence, deviation|antonyms: convergence, adherence|noun
