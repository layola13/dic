multicastings:/ˈmʌltɪˌkɑːstɪŋz/ |n.| 多播；组播 |The process of transmitting data packets to multiple recipients simultaneously.  It's like sending a single email to a group of people instead of individual emails.|  The company uses multicastings to distribute software updates to its employees' computers. 公司使用多播向其员工的计算机分发软件更新。 | synonyms: multicasting, groupcasting | antonyms: unicasting | noun
