backpacks: /ˈbækˌpæks/ | n. | 背包 | A bag carried on the back, typically made of cloth or other flexible material and supported by straps over the shoulders, used to carry items. |  I carry my books and laptop in my backpack. 我把我的书和笔记本电脑放在我的背包里。 |  rucksacks, knapsacks | suitcases, briefcases | noun

Etymology:
From "back" + "pack".  "Back" traces back to Old English "bæc," and "pack" comes from Middle Dutch "pac" or "pak," meaning "bundle."
