monopathy: /məˈnɒpəθi/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpəθi/ (US) | n. | 单一病症; 局部病变 | A disease affecting only one part of the body or one organ. |  Example: The doctor diagnosed his condition as a monopathy of the knee. 医生诊断他的病情为膝盖局部病变。

Example:  Monopathy, unlike systemic diseases, is localized to a specific area.  不像全身性疾病，局部病变只发生在特定区域。| 近义词: localized disease, focal disease | 反义词: systemic disease, generalized disease | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *monos* (single, alone) + *pathos* (suffering, disease)
