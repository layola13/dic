arteriography: /ɑːrˌtɪəriˈɒɡrəfi/ /ɑːrˌtɪriˈɑːɡrəfi/
noun
动脉造影术
The radiographic visualization of an artery or arteries after injection of a radiopaque substance.
The doctor recommended an arteriography to examine the blood flow in the patient's legs. 医生建议进行动脉造影术以检查病人腿部的血流。
