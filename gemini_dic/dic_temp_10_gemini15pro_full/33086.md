chrissake:/fərˈkraɪˈseɪk/ | interj. | 天哪；老天爷；哎呀（表示恼怒、不耐烦、惊讶等）| A euphemism for "for Christ's sake," used to express annoyance, impatience, or surprise.  It's considered mild profanity.| For chrissake, hurry up!  天哪，快点吧！ | Near Synonyms:  for goodness sake, for heaven's sake, geez, jeez | Opposite: (No direct opposite for an exclamation) | interjection
