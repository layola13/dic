emollition: /ˌiːməˈlɪʃən/ | n. | 柔化；缓和；软化 | the act of making something softer or less intense | The emollition of her anger was evident in her calmer voice. 她的怒气缓和下来，从她平静的声音中可以明显听出来。 | 近义词: softening, alleviation, mitigation | 反义词: hardening, intensification, aggravation | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *ēmollitiōn-, from Latin ēmollire "to soften," from ē- "out" + mollire "to soften," from mollis "soft."
