hypercautiousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəsnəs/| n. |过度谨慎，过分小心|The state of being excessively cautious or wary.|Her hypercautiousness prevented her from taking any risks in the business. 她过度谨慎，不敢在生意上冒险。|近义词：wariness, cautiousness, prudence, circumspection|反义词：recklessness, imprudence, carelessness|noun


Etymology:
From hyper- + cautiousness.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over," "beyond," or "excessive," derived from Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ). "Cautiousness" is derived from the adjective "cautious," which comes from the Latin word "cautus" meaning "careful" or "wary."
