overmasterfully:/ˌoʊvərˈmæstərˌfʊli/ | adv. | 专横地；不可抗拒地；压倒性地 | In a way that completely overcomes or overwhelms; in a domineering or imperious manner. | He overmasterfully silenced the opposition with his powerful arguments. 他用强有力的论据专横地压制了反对派。| 近义词: overwhelmingly, decisively, imperiously, dominantly, powerfully | 反义词: submissively, meekly, weakly | 词性: 副词

overmasterfully:/ˌoʊvərˈmæstərˌfʊli/ | adv. | 以压倒性的优势 | With overwhelming superiority or force; decisively. |  She overmasterfully won the chess match in just a few moves. 她以压倒性的优势在几步棋之内就赢得了比赛。| 近义词: decisively, conclusively, triumphantly | 反义词: marginally, narrowly, weakly | 词性: 副词


词源:
From "overmaster" + "-fully". "Overmaster" comes from "over-" (meaning "above, beyond") + "master" (meaning "to control, to have power over").  "-fully" is a suffix indicating "in a ... manner."
