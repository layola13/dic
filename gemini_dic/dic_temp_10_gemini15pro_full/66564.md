exhibitions:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənz/
n.
展览会，展览品
a public display of works of art or other items of interest
例句：The museum's exhibitions change regularly.  博物馆的展览定期更换。

例句：We visited several art exhibitions during our trip to Italy.  我们在意大利之旅期间参观了几个艺术展览。

近义词：displays, shows, expositions
反义词：concealments

词性: 名词
