longbeak:/ˈlɔːŋbiːk/| n. |长喙鸟；长喙动物|A bird or animal with a long beak. |例句：The longbeak probed the flower for nectar. 长喙鸟探入花中吸取花蜜。

例句：Several longbeaks were seen wading in the shallows.  几只长喙鸟在浅滩涉水。|近义词：wader, shorebird|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider short-beaked bird) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'long' + 'beak'.  'Long' from Old English 'lang', ultimately from Proto-Germanic *langaz. 'Beak' from Middle English 'bek', from Old North French 'bec', ultimately from Vulgar Latin *beccus.
