carack:/ˈkærək/| n. |大型帆船，卡拉克帆船|A large merchant ship of the 14th-17th centuries, used by the Portuguese and Spanish especially.  A carrack typically had three or four masts and a high, rounded stern.|The carrack was instrumental in opening up trade routes to the East. 这艘卡拉克帆船在开辟通往东方的贸易路线方面发挥了重要作用。|近义词：galleon, ship|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small boat")|noun
