immobilized:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/| adj.|固定的，不能动的|Made unable to move or function.  Rendered inactive or motionless.|The broken leg was immobilized in a cast.  腿断了，用石膏固定住了。|近义词：fixed, stationary, stuck, incapacitated|反义词:mobile, free, active|词性:形容词
Etymology: From im- (not) + mobilize (to make mobile), from Latin mobilis (movable).
