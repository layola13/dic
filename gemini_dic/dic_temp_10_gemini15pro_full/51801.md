例句：The monk's devoutness was reflected in his peaceful demeanor.  这位僧侣的虔诚反映在他平静的举止中。

近义词：piety, religiousness, godliness|反义词：impiety, irreverence, ungodliness|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *devotnes*, from Anglo-Norman *devotnese*, from Old French *devot*, from Latin *dēvōtus* (“devoted”), past participle of *dēvovēre* (“to vow, dedicate, devote”), from *dē-* (“down, away”) + *vovēre* (“to vow”).
