accompanier: /əˈkʌmpəniər/
n.
伴奏者；陪同者
A person who accompanies someone, especially a musician who plays along with another instrument or singer.
The pianist is a skilled accompanier. =  这位钢琴家是一位技艺娴熟的伴奏者。

Her mother acted as her accompanier on the trip. = 在旅途中她母亲担任她的陪同人员。

近义词: companion, escort, attendant
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用soloist如果指音乐上的伴奏)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French acompignier, from Late Latin acompanio (“I share bread with”), from Latin ad (“to”) + companio (“companion”), ultimately from Latin com (“with”) + panis (“bread”).
