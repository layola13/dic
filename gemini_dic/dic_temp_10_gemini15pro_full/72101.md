flocculence:/ˈflɒkjʊləns/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjələns/ (UK)| n. |絮状；绒毛状；蓬松 |The state or quality of being flocculent, or containing loose, fluffy clumps resembling wool or cotton.  |The flocculence of the clouds made them look like cotton candy.  云的蓬松使它们看起来像棉花糖。|Near synonyms: fluffiness, downiness, wooliness |Near antonyms: smoothness, compactness, solidity|noun


flocculent:/ˈflɒkjʊlənt/ (US) /ˈflɑːkjələnt/ (UK)| adj. |絮状的；绒毛状的；蓬松的|Having a fluffy or woolly appearance; resembling a loose mass of wool or cotton. |The scientist observed a flocculent precipitate form in the test tube. 科学家观察到试管中形成了絮状沉淀物。|Near synonyms: fluffy, downy, woolly |Near antonyms: smooth, compact, solid|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flocculentus*, from *floccus* ("tuft of wool").  Related to "flock."
