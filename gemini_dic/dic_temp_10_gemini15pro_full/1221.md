accommodate: /əˈkɒmədeɪt/ (UK) /əˈkɑːmədeɪt/ (US)| v.| 容纳；提供住宿；适应；顾及| To provide lodging or sufficient space for; to adapt to; to oblige or help someone by meeting their needs. | The hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests. 这家酒店可以容纳多达500位客人。

The company accommodated the employee's request for a flexible work schedule. 公司满足了员工灵活工作时间的要求。| 近义词: house, lodge, adapt, adjust, fit, suit, oblige | 反义词: refuse, reject, disoblige | 词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel accommodates many tourists every summer. = 这家酒店每年夏天都接待许多游客。
She accommodates her husband's wishes. = 她迁就她丈夫的愿望。


一般过去时:
The inn accommodated us for the night. = 这家小旅馆为我们提供了一夜的住宿。
They accommodated themselves to the new environment. = 他们适应了新环境。


现在完成时:
We have accommodated all the guests. = 我们已经安置好了所有客人。
The company has accommodated the changing market. = 公司已经适应了变化的市场。


过去完成时:
They had accommodated the refugees before the storm hit. = 他们在暴风雨来临之前安置好了难民。
She had accommodated her sister’s needs for years. = 她多年来一直满足她妹妹的需求。


将来时:
We will accommodate your request if possible. = 如果可能，我们将满足您的要求。
The new stadium will accommodate thousands of spectators. = 新体育场将容纳数千名观众。


虚拟语气:
If we could accommodate more people, we would. = 如果我们能容纳更多的人，我们会的。
I wish I could accommodate your request, but I can't. = 我希望我能满足你的要求，但我不能。


被动语态:
All the guests were accommodated in the hotel. = 所有客人都被安排在了酒店里。
His request for a later deadline was accommodated. = 他要求延期的请求得到了满足。


疑问句:
Can you accommodate us for the night? = 你能为我们提供住宿吗？
How many people can this hall accommodate? = 这个大厅可以容纳多少人？


否定句:
We cannot accommodate any more guests. = 我们不能再接待更多客人了。
The small car couldn't accommodate all of our luggage. = 这辆小汽车容纳不下我们所有的行李。




PHRASE->
accommodate to = 适应
accommodate with = 向…提供；供应
accommodate oneself to = 使自己适应于
accommodate sb with sth = 为某人提供某物
accommodate the needs of = 满足…的需求
