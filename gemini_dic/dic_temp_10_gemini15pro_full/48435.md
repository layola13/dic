deducting:/dɪˈdʌktɪŋ/
verb
扣除，减去
present participle of deduct: taking away an amount from a total
例句：They are deducting tax from my salary. 他们正在从我的工资中扣除税款。
例句：Deducting expenses from income, the company made a small profit. 扣除费用后的收入，公司获得了少量利润。
近义词：subtracting, withholding, removing
反义词：adding, increasing
