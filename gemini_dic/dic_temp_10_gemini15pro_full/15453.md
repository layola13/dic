badassed:/ˈbædæs/ /ˈbædˌæst/ | adj. n.|强悍的；厉害的；令人敬畏的；硬汉；狠角色 |  (adj.) Showing impressive strength, skill, or confidence, often in a rebellious or unconventional way. (n.) A person who embodies these qualities. |  例句：She's a badass lawyer who doesn't take crap from anyone. 她是一位强悍的律师，不会容忍任何人的废话。

例句：He pulled off a badass stunt on his motorcycle. 他骑着摩托车完成了一个惊险的特技。| 近义词：tough, cool, awesome, formidable, intimidating | 反义词: timid, weak, cowardly | 词性: adjective, noun

badassed:/ˈbædæs/ /ˈbædˌæst/ | n.|硬汉；狠角色 | A tough, uncompromising, or intimidating person. | 例句：He's a real badass, always ready for a fight. 他是个真正的硬汉，随时准备战斗。

例句：  The movie features a group of badasses who take on the corrupt government. 这部电影讲述了一群硬汉对抗腐败政府的故事。| 近义词：tough guy, hard-boiled, renegade | 反义词: wimp, softie | 词性: noun


词源:  A blend of "bad" and "ass," originating in African American Vernacular English (AAVE) in the mid-20th century.  Initially used to describe someone tough and aggressive, it has evolved to encompass a broader range of meanings, including competence and coolness.
