misdeems:/ˌmɪsˈdiːmz/
verb
错误地认为；误判
To have a wrong opinion of; misjudge.
He misdeems their silence as agreement. 他误以为他们的沉默就是同意。
近义词: misjudges, misinterprets, mistakes
反义词: understands, comprehends, judges correctly
词性: verb

词源:
From Middle English *misdemen, from mis- + demen (“to deem”). Equivalent to mis- + deem.
