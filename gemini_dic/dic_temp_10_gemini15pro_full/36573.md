collared:/ˈkɒləd/ (British) /ˈkɑːlərd/ (American)| adj.|有领的；戴着项圈的；被捕的|Having a collar, wearing a collar, or apprehended by authorities.|例句：He wore a collared shirt to the interview. 他面试时穿了一件有领衬衫。

例句：The dog was collared and led away. 狗被戴上项圈带走了。


近义词：collared (for apprehended): arrested, captured, caught

近义词：collared (for clothing): button-down, dressy

反义词：collared (for apprehended): free, released

反义词：collared (for clothing): collarless

词性: adjective
