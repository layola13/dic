communicates:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪts/ | v. | 沟通，交流，传达 | Makes information known; conveys. | She communicates effectively with her team. 她和她的团队有效地沟通。 | conveys, imparts, transmits, shares | withholds, suppresses | verb

communicates:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪts/ | v. | 传播，感染 | Transmit (a disease or feeling). | Mosquitoes can communicate diseases like malaria. 蚊子可以传播像疟疾这样的疾病。 | spreads, transmits, passes on | prevents, resists | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Latin communicat- ‘shared, imparted’, from the verb communicare, from communis ‘common’.
