adverting:/ədˈvɜːtɪŋ/ | v. | 注意，提及；评论；谈到 |  To refer to something in speech or writing, especially briefly or incidentally. |  He adverted briefly to the problem of unemployment.  他简要地提到了失业问题。

The speaker adverted to the recent tragedy. 演讲者提到了最近发生的悲剧。 | 近义词:referring, mentioning, alluding, touching upon | 反义词: ignoring, avoiding, overlooking | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin advertēns, present participle of advertō (“I turn to, pay attention to”), from ad (“to”) + vertō (“I turn”).

USAGE->

He often adverts to his time in the army. = 他经常提到他在部队的时光。
She adverts to the issue in her report. = 她在报告中提到了这个问题。
