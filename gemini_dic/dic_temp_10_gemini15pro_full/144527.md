outvanish:/ˌaʊtˈvænɪʃ/
verb
超越消失；消失得更彻底
To disappear more completely or thoroughly than something else.
The magician outvanished his rival by making the entire building disappear.  魔术师通过让整栋建筑消失，使他的对手黯然失色。
The morning mist outvanished with the rising sun. 晨雾随着太阳的升起而彻底消散。
近义词:eclipse, surpass, outdo
反义词:appear, materialize
词性:verb
