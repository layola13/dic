equisetiform:/ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 木贼形的，像木贼的 | Resembling or characteristic of plants of the genus Equisetum (horsetails).  The plant had an equisetiform stem, jointed and hollow. | 例句1：The fossil showed an equisetiform structure, suggesting it was related to ancient horsetails.  这块化石呈现出木贼形的结构，表明它与古代的木贼有关。

例句2：The alien landscape was dotted with tall, equisetiform plants.  外星地貌上点缀着高大、木贼形的植物。 | 近义词：jointed, segmented, reed-like | 反义词: smooth, unsegmented | 词性: adjective
