mobilizers:/ˈməʊbɪlaɪzəz/ (UK) /ˈmoʊbɪlaɪzərz/ (US)| n.|动员者；动员器|People or things that mobilize something or someone.  Those who organize and encourage a group of people to take action for a particular purpose.|The union mobilizers rallied the workers for the strike. 工会动员者召集工人举行罢工。|Synonyms: activists, campaigners, organizers, promoters|Antonyms: demobilizers, pacifiers|noun

mobilizer:/ˈməʊbɪlaɪzə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmoʊbɪlaɪzər/ (US)| n.|动员者；动员器|A person or thing that mobilizes something or someone. Someone who organizes and encourages a group of people to take action for a particular purpose.|The community mobilizer worked tirelessly to get people to vote. 社区动员员不懈地努力让人们投票。|Synonyms: activist, campaigner, organizer, promoter|Antonyms: demobilizer, pacifier|noun
