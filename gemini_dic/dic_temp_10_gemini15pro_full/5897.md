ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/
adjective
双撇子；灵巧的；左右手都善用的
(of a person) able to use the right and left hands equally well.
She's an ambidextrous tennis player, equally comfortable hitting forehands and backhands. 她是一位双撇子网球运动员，正反手都能轻松应对。
The ambidextrous surgeon could operate with either hand.  这位灵巧的外科医生可以用任何一只手做手术。
近义词: versatile, adaptable, skillful
反义词: clumsy, awkward, one-handed
词性: adjective
