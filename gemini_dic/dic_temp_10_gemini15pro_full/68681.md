farcicality:/fɑːrˈsɪkəlɪti/ | n. |滑稽；荒谬；闹剧 |The farcicality of the situation was undeniable.  这种情况的荒谬性是不可否认的。|The play was filled with farcicality, bordering on the absurd. 这部戏剧充满了滑稽，近乎荒诞。| absurdity, ridiculousness, ludicrousness | seriousness, solemnity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From farce (n.) + -ical (adj. suffix) + -ity (n. suffix).  Farce comes from Old French farsir "to stuff," referring to comic interludes "stuffed" into religious plays.
