overgrace: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪs/
adjective
过分地施恩惠；过分地装饰
to endow with excessive grace; to decorate excessively
The king overgraced his favorite with titles and lands. 国王过度地赏赐他的宠臣以头衔和土地。
近义词: overindulge, overdecorate, lavish
反义词: neglect, deprive
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "grace" (noun meaning "elegance, favor").  The prefix "over-" modifies "grace" to indicate an excessive or immoderate amount.
