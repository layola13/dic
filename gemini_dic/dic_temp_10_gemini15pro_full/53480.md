disarranges:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒɪz/
verb
扰乱，弄乱
to disturb the arrangement or order of; throw into disorder
The wind disarranged her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。
近义词: disorder, disorganize, disrupt, unsettle, confuse
反义词: arrange, organize, order, tidy
词性:v.


disarrangement:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒmənt/
noun
混乱，紊乱
the state of being disarranged or disordered
The disarrangement of the books annoyed the librarian.  书籍的凌乱惹恼了图书管理员。
近义词: disorder, disorganization, chaos, mess, confusion
反义词: arrangement, organization, order, tidiness
词性:n.
