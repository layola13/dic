ho-hum:/ˈhoʊ hʌm/| adj. int. n.|无聊的；乏味的；无精打采的；(表示无聊或不感兴趣)|An expression of boredom or lack of interest.|例句：He gave a ho-hum response to the news. 他对这个消息反应冷淡。|近义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting, monotonous, vapid, insipid|反义词：exciting, interesting, stimulating, engaging|词性：形容词,感叹词,名词
Etymology:  Imitative of a sigh expressing boredom or indifference.  First recorded in the early 20th century.
