antirecessionary:/ˌænti rɪˈsɛʃəˌnɛri/
adjective
反衰退的，对抗经济衰退的
Designed or intended to prevent or reduce a recession.
The government introduced antirecessionary measures to stimulate the economy. 政府推出了反衰退措施以刺激经济。
The central bank lowered interest rates as an antirecessionary move.  中央银行降低利率作为一项反衰退举措。
近义词: countercyclical, expansionary
反义词: recessionary, deflationary
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (prefix meaning "against") + recession + -ary (suffix forming adjectives).  "Recession" comes from the Latin "recessus," meaning "a going back, retreat."
