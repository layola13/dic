officiate:/əˈfɪʃieɪt/
verb
主持；执行；担任裁判
act as an official in charge of something, especially a sporting event or religious service.
The bishop officiated at the wedding. 主教主持了婚礼。
officiate as referee 担任裁判
preside, referee, administer, conduct, direct
boycott
