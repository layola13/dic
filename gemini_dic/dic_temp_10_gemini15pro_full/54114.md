disemployment:/ˌdɪsɪmˈplɔɪmənt/
n.
失业; 失业状态
The state of being involuntarily unemployed.  The displacement of workers by machines or other factors.
The factory closure led to widespread disemployment in the town. 工厂关闭导致该镇普遍失业。
Unemployment caused by technological advances can lead to significant social problems. 技术进步造成的失业会导致严重的社会问题。
近义词: unemployment, joblessness, redundancy
反义词: employment, full employment
