disshiver:/dɪˈʃɪvər/ | v. |  瓦解，碎裂；使不安，使颤抖 | To break apart or disintegrate; to cause to tremble or be disturbed. | The empire began to disshiver after the death of the emperor. 帝国在皇帝死后开始瓦解。 | The cold wind disshivered me to the bone. 寒风刺骨，让我瑟瑟发抖。 | 近义词: crumble, disintegrate, shatter,  tremble, shiver | 反义词: solidify, unite, strengthen, steady, compose | 词性: 动词

词源:  From dis- + shiver,  intensifying the sense of "shiver."  "Dis-"  is a prefix indicating separation or reversal. "Shiver" comes from Old English "scierfan," meaning "to break into splinters."
