lodestone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n. |天然磁石；吸引人的事物或人|A piece of magnetite or other naturally magnetized mineral, able to attract iron.  Something that strongly attracts people or things. |例句：The lodestone drew the iron filings towards it. 天然磁石把铁屑吸了过去。

例句：For him, books were a lodestone. 书籍对他有着强大的吸引力。|近义词：magnet, attraction, draw, lure|反义词：repellent|词性:noun

lodestones:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊnz/| n. |天然磁石；吸引人的事物或人（复数）|Plural form of lodestone. Pieces of magnetite or other naturally magnetized minerals, able to attract iron. Things that strongly attract people or things. |例句：The lodestones were used for navigation in ancient times. 古代人用天然磁石导航。
