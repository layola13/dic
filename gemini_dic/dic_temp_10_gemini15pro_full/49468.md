deluge's:/ˈdɛljuːdʒɪz/ | n. | 洪水（deluge的名词所有格） |  A deluge's impact can be devastating.  洪水的影响可能是毁灭性的。 | 例句：The deluge's force swept away the bridge. 洪水的力量冲走了桥梁。 | 近义词：flood's, inundation's, torrent's | 反义词: trickle's, drip's | 词性: 名词所有格

词源:  From Old French *deluge, from Latin diluvium (“flood, inundation”), from diluere (“to wash away”), from di- (“apart, away”) + luere (“to wash”).
