mutilation:/ˌmjuːtɪˈleɪʃn/| n.| 毁坏，残害 | the act of inflicting serious damage on something |  The museum curator was horrified by the mutilation of the ancient statue.  博物馆馆长对古代雕像的毁坏感到震惊。| damage, injury, maiming, disfigurement | repair, healing | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mutilatus*, past participle of *mutilare* "to maim, cut off," from *mutilus* "maimed, broken, docked," of unknown origin.
