discontinue:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/
verb
停止，中断
to stop doing or providing something
The company discontinued the production of that model.  这家公司停止了该型号的生产。
The service will be discontinued next month.  这项服务将于下个月停止。
cease, stop, terminate, abandon
continue, maintain, persist
词性: 动词
