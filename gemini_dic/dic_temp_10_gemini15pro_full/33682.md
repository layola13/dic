cinnamon: /ˈsɪnəmən/ (US), /ˈsɪnəmən/ (UK)| n. |肉桂，肉桂色|A spice made from the inner bark of a tropical tree, used in cooking and baking. Also, a light yellowish-brown color resembling this spice.|例句：I added a pinch of cinnamon to the apple pie. 我在苹果派里加了一撮肉桂。|例句：She wore a dress the color of cinnamon. 她穿着一件肉桂色的连衣裙。|近义词：cassia|反义词: (No direct antonym for the spice itself. For the color, perhaps) vibrant colors |词性: noun
