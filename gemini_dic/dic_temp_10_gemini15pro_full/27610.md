cancelled: /ˈkænsəld/ (US) /ˈkænsld/ (UK)| v. adj.|取消的，撤销的|To decide that an arranged event will not take place; to order that something already arranged should not happen.  To declare invalid.|The flight was cancelled due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班被取消了。|Synonyms: aborted, abandoned, withdrawn|Antonyms: approved, confirmed, continued|verb

cancelled: /ˈkænsəld/ (US) /ˈkænsld/ (UK)| adj.|取消的，撤销的|No longer valid or in effect.|The cancelled stamp was useless.  取消的邮票没用了。|Synonyms: invalid, void, annulled|Antonyms: valid, current, effective|adjective
