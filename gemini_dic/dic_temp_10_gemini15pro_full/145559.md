overcompetitively: /ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/
| adv. | 过度竞争地 |  In a way that involves competing too much or in a way that is too forceful. | He approached the game overcompetitively, alienating his teammates. 他过度竞争地对待比赛，疏远了他的队友。| Example: She overcompetitively pursued every opportunity, leaving little time for personal life. 她过度竞争地追求每一个机会，几乎没有时间留给个人生活。| Synonyms: excessively competitive, hypercompetitive, ruthlessly competitive | Antonyms: cooperatively, collaboratively, unambitiously | 词性: 副词
