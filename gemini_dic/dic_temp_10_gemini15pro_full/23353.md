braai:/brʌɪ/
n.
烧烤;户外烤肉聚会
A social gathering at which food is cooked over an open fire outdoors.
We had a braai in the park last weekend.  我们上周末在公园里烧烤。
barbecue, cookout
picnic


braai:/brʌɪ/
v.
烧烤;户外烤肉
To cook food over an open fire outdoors.
We braaied some sausages and steaks. 我们烤了一些香肠和牛排。
barbecue, grill
microwave


Etymology:
Afrikaans, from braden, to roast (from Middle Dutch braden).
