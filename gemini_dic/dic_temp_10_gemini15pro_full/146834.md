overinclinations:/ˌoʊvərɪnklɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |过度倾向，过分爱好|An excessive inclination or preference for something.| He has overinclinations towards sugary foods, which is affecting his health. 他过度偏爱含糖食物，这正在影响他的健康。|Example: Her overinclinations for luxury goods led her into debt. 她对奢侈品的过度偏好让她负债累累。|Synonyms:  predilections, propensities, biases|Antonyms: aversions, dislikes|Noun
