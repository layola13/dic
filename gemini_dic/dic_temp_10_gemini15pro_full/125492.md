multiracialist:/ˌmʌltiˈreɪʃəlɪst/| n. adj.|多种族主义者，多种族主义的|A person who believes in or advocates for multiracialism; relating to or characterized by multiracialism.  A multiracialist society embraces diversity and promotes equality among people of different racial backgrounds.|例句：The multiracialist argued for policies that would benefit all racial groups.  这位多种族主义者主张制定有利于所有种族群体的政策。

例句：The politician advocated for a more multiracialist approach to immigration. 这位政治家主张对移民采取更加多种族主义的方式。|近义词：integrationist, pluralist|反义词：racist, segregationist|词性:名词, 形容词
