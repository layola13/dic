half-ovals:/ˌhæf ˈoʊvəlz/| n.|半椭圆形(复数)|A shape resembling half of an oval. |The designer incorporated half-ovals into the pattern.  设计师将半椭圆形融入到图案中。

The table had half-ovals carved into its legs. 桌子的腿上雕刻着半椭圆形。|近义词：semicircles, hemicycles|反义词:circles, ovals|noun
Etymology->
"half-" + "oval"
"half" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "one of two equal parts."
"oval" comes from Late Latin "ovalis,"  from Latin "ovum," meaning "egg."
