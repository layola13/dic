insoluble:/ɪnˈsɒljʊb(ə)l/ (US) /ɪnˈsɑːljəbəl/| adj.|不溶的；无法解决的|Not able to be dissolved in a liquid; impossible to solve.|例句：Sugar is soluble in water, but sand is insoluble. 糖溶于水，但沙子不溶于水。|例句：The problem proved insoluble. 这个问题证明无法解决。|近义词：indissoluble, unsolvable|反义词：soluble, solvable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insolubilis, from in- ‘not’ + solubilis ‘soluble’ (from solvere ‘to loosen, dissolve’).
