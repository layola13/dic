engagingness:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋnɪs/
n.
迷人；吸引力；魅力
The quality of being engaging; attractiveness or charm.
Her engagingness made her a popular teacher. 她的魅力使她成为一位受欢迎的老师。
Synonyms: attractiveness, charm, appeal, magnetism
Antonyms: unattractiveness, dullness, tediousness, boredom
词性:名词


engagingnesses:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
(复数) 迷人之处；吸引力
Plural form of engagingness.
The engagingnesses of the various activities kept the children entertained for hours.  各种活动的吸引力让孩子们玩了好几个小时。
Synonyms:  attractions, charms, appeals
Antonyms:  unattractivenesses, dullnesses, tediousnesses
