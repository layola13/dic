frugiferous:/fruːˈdʒɪfərəs/
adjective
结果实的；多果的
Bearing or producing fruit in abundance.
The frugiferous tree was laden with ripe apples.  这棵结果实累累的树上挂满了成熟的苹果。
The orchard was incredibly frugiferous this year.  今年的果园产量异常丰富。
Synonyms: fruitful, prolific, productive
Antonyms: barren, sterile, infertile
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frūgifer*  (fruit-bearing), from *frūx* (fruit) + *-fer* (bearing).
