aweigh:/əˈweɪ/
adjective
已起锚的，离底的
Just clear of the bottom; said of an anchor.
The anchor is aweigh.  锚已起。
近义词: off the ground, lifted
反义词: aground, grounded
词性: adjective

Etymology: From a- + weigh.  The original sense was "in the process of being weighed," hence "lifted up" (as if to be weighed).
