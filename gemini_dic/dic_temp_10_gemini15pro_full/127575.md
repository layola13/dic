nectaried:/ˈnɛktərid/ | adj. | 花蜜的，富含花蜜的 | Containing or producing nectar; sweet like nectar. | The nectaried blossoms attracted a multitude of bees.  富含花蜜的花朵吸引了大量的蜜蜂。 |  近义词: honeyed, sweet, sugary | 反义词: dry, bitter | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From nectar + -ed.  "Nectar" comes from Latin "nectar,"  the drink of the gods, from Greek "νέκταρ (néktar)." The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives from nouns, meaning "having, characterized by."
