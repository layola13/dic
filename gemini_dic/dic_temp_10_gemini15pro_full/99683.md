interlobar:/ˌɪntərˈloʊbɑːr/
adjective
叶间的，小叶间的
Situated between lobes.
The interlobar arteries supply blood to the renal columns.  叶间动脉为肾柱供血。
The interlobar fissures separate the lobes of the lung. 叶间裂将肺叶分开。
Synonyms: interlobular
Antonyms: intralobar
词性: adjective
