calligraphies: /kəˈlɪɡrəfiz/ | n. | 书法作品 |  The artistic practice of handwriting, often using specialized pens and inks to create decorative lettering. It refers to the finished products of calligraphy, meaning the actual handwritten pieces. |  The museum exhibited a collection of Islamic calligraphies.  博物馆展出了一系列伊斯兰书法作品。| synonyms: handwritings, scripts, letterings | antonyms: printings, typings | noun
