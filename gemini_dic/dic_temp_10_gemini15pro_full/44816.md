crush:/krʌʃ/| n. vt. vi.|迷恋；压碎；拥挤；果汁饮料|A brief but intense infatuation for someone, especially someone unattainable.  To compress with force so as to break, pulverize, or distort in shape. A crowd of people pressed closely together.|例句：She had a crush on her history teacher. 她暗恋她的历史老师。|近义词：infatuation, grind, squash, squeeze|反义词:dislike, aversion|词性:名词,动词

crush:/krʌʃ/| v.|压碎；镇压；挤压；使…变形|To press something very hard so that it breaks or changes shape.|例句：The car was crushed under the weight of the truck. 汽车在卡车的重压下被压扁了。|近义词：grind, pulverize, squash|反义词:repair, mend|词性:动词
