dreamery: /ˈdriːməri/ (US), /ˈdriːməri/ (UK)| n.|梦工厂，幻想之地，白日梦|A place where dreams are made or where one is lost in dreams; a place of fantasy or reverie.|例句：She retreated into her own little dreamery, oblivious to the world around her. 她躲进自己的小幻想世界里，对外界的一切浑然不觉。

例句：The old abandoned theatre was a dreamery for the children, filled with imagined adventures.  这座废弃的老剧院是孩子们幻想的乐园，充满了想象中的冒险。|近义词：fantasyland, wonderland, never-never land|反义词：reality|词性：名词
