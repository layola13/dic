impartibility:/ɪmpɑːrʃiˈælɪti/
n.
公正性，不偏不倚
The quality of being impartial; fairness.
The judge demonstrated impartiality in his handling of the case.  法官在处理此案时表现出了公正性。
Synonyms: fairness, neutrality, objectivity, justice, evenhandedness
Antonyms: bias, prejudice, partiality, unfairness
noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *impartialitatem, from impartial + -ity.  Impartial from in- + partial.
