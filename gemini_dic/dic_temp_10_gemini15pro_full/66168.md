excessed:/ɪkˈsɛst/| v. | 过量；超过；超额 | to be or become greater than a particular number or amount | The baggage was excessed by 20 pounds. 行李超重20磅。 | 近义词：surpassed, exceeded | 反义词:  lacked, deficient | Verb

excessed:/ɪkˈsɛst/| adj. | 超额的；过量的 | exceeding a prescribed or desirable amount |  Excessed inventory can be a significant cost for businesses. 过多的库存对企业来说可能是一笔巨大的成本。 | 近义词：surplus, extra | 反义词: insufficient, lacking | Adjective
