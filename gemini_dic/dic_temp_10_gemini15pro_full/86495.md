heinousness:/ˈheɪnəsnəs/| n.|邪恶; 滔天罪行; 恶劣; 极凶残|The heinousness of the crime shocked the nation.  这起罪行的滔天罪行震惊了全国。|The judge commented on the sheer heinousness of the act. 法官评论了该行为的极端恶劣性。|近义词：atrocity, wickedness, evil, depravity|反义词：goodness, virtue, righteousness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From heinous + -ness.  Heinous itself comes from Old French *haineus, from haine "hatred," ultimately from Frankish *hatjan "to hate."
