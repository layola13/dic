nagging:/ˈnæɡɪŋ/ (US), /ˈnæɡɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|唠叨的，烦人的；持续的，挥之不去的|persistently annoying or troubling; bothering or irritating constantly|The nagging pain in her shoulder wouldn't go away. 她肩膀上持续的疼痛挥之不去。|Synonyms: persistent, bothersome, irritating, pestering|Antonyms: soothing, calming, pleasant|adjective

nagging:/ˈnæɡɪŋ/ (US), /ˈnæɡɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|唠叨，抱怨|the act of nagging someone|Her constant nagging about his messy room was driving him crazy. 她不断地唠叨他房间的脏乱，这快把他逼疯了。|Synonyms: complaining, carping, faultfinding|Antonyms: praising, complimenting|noun
