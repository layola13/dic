heft:/hɛft/ | n. vt.|重量，份量；举起，掂量|The box had an unexpected heft. 这个盒子出乎意料地重。|近义词：weight, bulk, lift, raise|反义词：lightness|词性:名词,动词

heft:/hɛft/ | vt.|举起，掂量|He hefted the suitcase to judge its weight. 他掂了掂手提箱，估量它的重量。|近义词：lift, raise, weigh|反义词：lower, drop|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *heften*, from Old Norse *hepta* (“to seize, grasp”), from Proto-Germanic *hafjaną* (“to lift, raise”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱep- (“to seize, take”).
