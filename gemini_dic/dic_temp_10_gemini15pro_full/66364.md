excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːs/ (US), /ɪkˈskjuːz/ (UK)| n. vt.|借口，理由；原谅，宽恕|An excuse is a reason, often false, given to justify a fault or failing.  To excuse is to forgive or overlook a fault or failing. |例句：He made an excuse for being late. 他为迟到找了个借口。|近义词：apology, justification, pardon, forgive|反义词: accusation, blame|noun
