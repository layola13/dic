disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/
n.
弟子，信徒，追随者
A follower or student of a teacher, leader, or philosophy.
Jesus had twelve disciples. 耶稣有十二个门徒。

The disciples of Freud. 弗洛伊德的信徒们。

近义词: follower, adherent, supporter, advocate
反义词: opponent, critic, detractor
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French disciple, from Latin discipulus (“pupil, student”), from discere (“to learn”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dek- (“to take, accept”).
