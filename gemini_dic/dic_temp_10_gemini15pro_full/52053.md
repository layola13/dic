dialog:/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡ/ /ˈdaɪəˌlɑːɡ/| n.|对话，交谈|A conversation between two or more people.|例句：The dialog between the two characters was witty and engaging.  两个角色之间的对话机智而引人入胜。

例句：They engaged in a meaningful dialog about the future of the company. 他们就公司的未来进行了有意义的对话。|近义词：conversation, discussion, exchange, talk|反义词：monologue, soliloquy|noun


dialog:/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡ/ /ˈdaɪəˌlɑːɡ/| vi.|进行对话|To engage in a conversation.|例句：The two sides finally agreed to dialog. 双方最终同意进行对话。

例句：We need to dialog with our customers to understand their needs. 我们需要与客户对话以了解他们的需求。|近义词：converse, discuss, communicate|反义词：ignore, refuse to communicate|verb
