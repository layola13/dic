ceaselessness:/ˈsisləsnəs/
noun
不停，连续，无休止
The state of being ceaseless; continuity.
The ceaselessness of the waves crashing on the shore was mesmerizing. 海浪不停地拍打着海岸，令人着迷。
continuity, persistence, incessancy
intermittence, cessation, pause
