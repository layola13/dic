adjective
无刺的，无防御器官的
lacking spines or other defensive structures.
例句：The inerm cactus relies on camouflage for protection.  这种无刺的仙人掌依靠伪装来保护自己。

例句：Many inerm varieties of plants have been cultivated for ornamental purposes. 许多无刺的植物品种被培育用于观赏。

近义词：unarmed, defenseless, harmless
反义词：spiny, thorny, armed
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *inermis (unarmed, defenseless), from in- (not) + arma (arms, weapons).
