outsteals: /ˌaʊtˈstiːlz/
verb
超过…的抢断数
To steal the ball from an opponent more times than they steal it from you.
The point guard outstole everyone on the opposing team.  这名控球后卫的抢断次数超过了对方球队的所有人。
Synonyms: surpasses in steals, excels in steals
Antonyms: is outstolen, has fewer steals
词性: 动词
