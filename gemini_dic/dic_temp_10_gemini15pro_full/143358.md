outfacing:/ˈaʊtˌfeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
外向的，面向外部的
directed or facing outwards.
例句：The outfacing windows offered a stunning view of the city.  面向外面的窗户提供了令人惊叹的城市景观。

例句：The company adopted an outfacing strategy to engage with its customers. 公司采取了面向外部的策略来与客户互动。

近义词：external, outward-facing, outward
反义词：internal, inward-facing, inward
词性：adjective
