beat-ups:/ˈbiːtˌʌps/| n.|破旧的东西；破烂的汽车；殴打；(非正式)旧衣服|A beat-up is something that is old and damaged, especially a car.  It can also refer to a physical beating or, informally, old clothes.|例句：He drives a beat-up old truck. 他开着一辆破旧的旧卡车。

|近义词：ramshackle, dilapidated, battered, worn-out, thrashing (for beating)|反义词:new, pristine, mint-condition|词性:noun


beat-ups:/ˈbiːtˌʌps/| n.|殴打|A beating; a fight.|例句：He got into a beat-up with some guys at the bar. 他在酒吧里和几个人打了一架。|近义词：fight, brawl, scuffle|反义词:peace, truce|词性:noun
