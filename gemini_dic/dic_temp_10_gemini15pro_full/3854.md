agoraphobiac: /ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbiæk/
| n. | 恐旷症患者；患恐旷症的人 | A person who suffers from agoraphobia, a fear of open spaces or crowded places. |  She became agoraphobiac after the traumatic incident and rarely left her house.  她在那次创伤事件后患上了恐旷症，很少出门。|  近义词: agoraphobe | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "social butterfly" or "extrovert" to describe opposite behavior, but not a direct antonym of the condition itself) | 词性: noun
