eremitish:/ˌɛrɪˈmɪtɪʃ/
adjective
隐居的; 遁世的; 孤僻的
Like a hermit; solitary and secluded.  Characterized by a preference for solitude and seclusion.
He led an eremitish existence in a remote cabin. 他在一个偏远的 cabin 里过着隐居的生活。
The old professor had become increasingly eremitish over the years.  这些年来，这位老教授变得越来越孤僻。
近义词:reclusive, solitary, secluded, withdrawn, introverted
反义词:gregarious, sociable, outgoing, extroverted
词性:adjective

词源:
From hermit + -ish.
