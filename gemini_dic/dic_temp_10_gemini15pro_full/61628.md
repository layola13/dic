emigrationist: /ˌɛmɪˈɡreɪʃənɪst/
noun
移民主义者；支持移民的人
A person who advocates or supports emigration.
The emigrationist argued that moving to a new country offered better economic opportunities.  这位移民主义者认为，移居到一个新的国家可以提供更好的经济机会。
The government's policies were criticized by emigrationists who felt they discouraged people from seeking better lives abroad. 政府的政策受到了移民主义者的批评，他们认为这些政策阻碍了人们寻求更好的海外生活。
synonyms: advocate of emigration, supporter of emigration
antonyms: immigrationist, nativist
