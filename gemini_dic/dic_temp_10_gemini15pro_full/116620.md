matronize:/ˈmeɪtrənaɪz/ | v. | 以恩人自居地对待;摆出高人一等的姿态对待 | to treat (someone) in a way that suggests that you are superior to them and that you think they need your help or advice |  She hated being matronized by her older sister. 她讨厌被姐姐以恩人自居地对待。| synonyms: patronize, condescend,  | antonyms: respect, admire | verb

matronize:/ˈmeɪtrənaɪz/ | v. | 像对待老妇人一样对待;像对待已婚妇女一样对待 | to treat (someone) as if they were a matron, especially in a way that is overprotective or condescending. | He matronized the young waitress, telling her to be careful carrying the heavy tray.  他像对待老妇人一样对待年轻的女服务员，告诉她搬运沉重的托盘时要小心。| synonyms: coddle, mother | antonyms: neglect, ignore | verb
