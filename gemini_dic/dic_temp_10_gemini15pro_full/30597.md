cerebellum: /ˌserəˈbeləm/ (US) /ˌsɛrɪˈbɛləm/ (UK)| n. |小脑|The part of the brain at the back of the skull in vertebrates. Its function is to coordinate and regulate muscular activity.|The cerebellum is essential for maintaining balance and coordination.|近义词: hindbrain (more general)|反义词:  N/A (no direct anatomical opposite)|noun
