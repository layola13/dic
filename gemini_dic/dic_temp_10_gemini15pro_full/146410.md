overfocusses:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəsɪz/ |v. |过度集中，过度关注|To concentrate on something to an excessive degree, often to the detriment of other things.|He overfocusses on details and misses the bigger picture. 他过度关注细节，反而忽略了大局。|Example: She overfocusses on her work and neglects her family. 她过度专注于工作而忽略了家人。|Synonyms: fixate, obsess, dwell|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|Verb

overfocussed:/ˌoʊvərˈfoʊkəst/ |adj. |过度集中的，过度关注的|Excessively concentrated on one thing.|He has an overfocussed attention span. 他的注意力过度集中。|Example: She became overfocussed on the negative aspects of her performance. 她变得过度关注自己表现的负面方面。|Synonyms: fixated, obsessed|Antonyms: distracted, inattentive|Adjective
