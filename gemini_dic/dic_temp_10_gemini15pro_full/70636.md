filterers:/ˈfɪltərərz/ | n. | 过滤器; 过滤者 | People or things that filter something.  Those who use a filter to remove impurities or unwanted elements from a substance. | The filterers removed the sediment from the water. 过滤者从水中去除了沉淀物。 | The coffee filterers separated the grounds from the brewed coffee. 咖啡过滤器将咖啡渣与煮好的咖啡分离。 |  近义词: strainers, sieves | 反义词: contaminants, pollutants | 词性: 名词(plural)

Etymology:
From the verb "filter" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Filter" comes from Old French "filtre" from Medieval Latin "filtrum," likely from Arabic "filtrum."
