committeeisms:/kəˈmɪtiːɪzəmz/ | n. | 委员会式的作风；委员会的习气；冗长而无意义的委员会语言 | The tendency of committees to produce verbose, vague, and generally ineffective pronouncements.  It refers to the characteristic style of language often used in committee reports and documents, which can be wordy, evasive, and lacking in clarity. |  The report was filled with committeeisms, making it difficult to understand the actual recommendations.  这份报告充满了委员会式的语言，很难理解实际的建议。 | synonyms: jargon, officialese, gobbledygook | antonyms: clarity, conciseness, directness | noun
