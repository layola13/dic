hyperlethargy:/ˌhaɪpərˈlɛθərdʒi/ | n. |极度嗜睡，过度困倦 |A state of extreme lethargy, excessive drowsiness or sluggishness. |  The patient exhibited hyperlethargy after the surgery.  病人术后表现出极度嗜睡。|  somnolence, stupor, lassitude | energy, alertness, vigor | noun


Etymology->
From hyper- + lethargy.  Hyper- comes from the Greek "hyper" meaning "over, excessive," and lethargy comes from the Greek "lēthargia," meaning "forgetfulness, drowsiness," from "lēthargos"  "forgetful," from "lēthē" "forgetfulness" + "-argos" "idle."
