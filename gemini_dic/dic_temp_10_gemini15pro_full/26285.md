buttressless:/ˈbʌtrəslɪs/
adjective
无扶壁的，无支撑的
lacking buttresses; unsupported
The buttressless wall eventually crumbled under the pressure. 这面没有扶壁的墙最终在压力下倒塌了。
The old church's buttressless walls showed signs of cracking. 这座古老教堂没有扶壁的墙壁出现了裂缝。
近义词:unsupported, unbraced, weak
反义词:buttressed, supported, reinforced
词性:adjective

词源:
From buttress + -less.  "Buttress" comes from Old French "bouterez," meaning "flying buttress," from "bouter," "to thrust, push."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
