fogger:/ˈfɑːɡər/ /ˈfɒɡə(r)/| n.|喷雾器，烟雾器|A device that creates a fog or mist, often used for pest control, humidification, or special effects.|例句：The farmer used a fogger to apply pesticide to his crops. 农民用喷雾器给他的作物喷洒农药。

例句：A fogger is often used in theatrical productions to create a spooky atmosphere.  烟雾器常用于戏剧作品中以营造诡异的氛围。

|近义词：atomizer, nebulizer, sprayer, vaporizer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示清除雾气的设备，例如：dehumidifier)|词性: noun


fogger:/ˈfɑːɡər/ /ˈfɒɡə(r)/| v.|喷雾，用烟雾器喷洒|To apply a fog or mist, often using a fogger.|例句：He fogged the room with insecticide. 他用杀虫剂喷洒了房间。

例句：They fogged the area for mosquitoes. 他们在该地区喷洒了灭蚊剂。

|近义词：spray, mist, atomize, vaporize|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示清除雾气的动作，例如：clear the fog)|词性: verb
