dedesignating:/ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪŋ/
verb
取消指定；撤销命名
removing a designation or name; no longer assigning a particular purpose or title to something.
The committee is dedesignating the area as a protected wetland. 委员会正在取消该地区作为受保护湿地的指定。
synonyms: revoking, rescinding, withdrawing, canceling, annulling
antonyms: designating, appointing, assigning, naming, labeling
词性: 动词
