indicible:/ɪnˈdɪsɪb(ə)l/ | adj. | 不可言喻的，难以形容的 | Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words. | The beauty of the sunset was indicible. 夕阳的美丽难以言喻。|  The pain he felt was indicible. 他感受到的痛苦难以名状。 |近义词：unspeakable, ineffable, inexpressible | 反义词：describable, expressible | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin indicibilis, from in- ‘not’ + dicibilis (from dicere ‘to say’).
