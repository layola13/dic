heulandite:/ˈhjuːlənˌdaɪt/ | n. | 片沸石, 斜沸石 | A mineral, hydrous calcium and sodium aluminosilicate, occurring in pearly, white, red, or brown monoclinic crystals. It is a zeolite. | Heulandite is often found in volcanic rocks. 片沸石常发现于火山岩中。| 近义词: stilbite, clinoptilolite | 反义词: None | noun
