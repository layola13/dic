padrone:/pəˈdroʊneɪ/ /pæˈdroʊn/| n.|老板，雇主，监工，保护人|A master; an employer, overseer, or protector, especially in Italy.|The padrone exploited the immigrant workers, paying them low wages and providing poor living conditions.  这个包工头剥削移民工人，付给他们低工资，提供恶劣的生活条件。|Synonyms: boss, employer, foreman, overseer, master|Antonyms: employee, worker, servant|noun

padrone:/pəˈdroʊneɪ/ /pæˈdroʊn/| n.|餐馆老板|The owner or manager of a restaurant, especially in Italy.|We enjoyed a delicious meal at the padrone's trattoria. 我们在老板的小餐馆享用了一顿美味佳肴。|Synonyms: restaurateur, owner, manager|Antonyms: customer, patron|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
