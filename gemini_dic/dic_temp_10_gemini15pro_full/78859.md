gingerness:/ˈdʒɪndʒərnəs/
n.
谨慎；小心；胆怯
The quality or state of being cautious or wary.
He approached the task with gingerness, aware of its delicate nature.  他小心翼翼地处理这项任务，因为他知道它的性质很微妙。

Etymology: From ginger (cautious, wary) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  源自 ginger（谨慎的，小心的） + -ness （构成名词的后缀，表示状态或性质）。

Synonyms: caution, wariness, carefulness, circumspection, prudence, hesitancy, timidity
Antonyms: boldness, recklessness, audacity, rashness, impulsiveness, carelessness
