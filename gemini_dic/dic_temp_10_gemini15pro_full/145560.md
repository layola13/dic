overcompetitiveness:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnəs/
n.
过度竞争，竞争过度
The state of being excessively competitive, often to the detriment of oneself or others.
His overcompetitiveness made him unpopular with his teammates.  他的过度竞争让他在队友中不受欢迎。
Overcompetitiveness can lead to burnout. 过度竞争会导致倦怠。
近义词:hypercompetitiveness, excessive competition
反义词:cooperativeness, collaboration
词性:noun
