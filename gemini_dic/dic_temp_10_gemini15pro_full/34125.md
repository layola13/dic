claddings:/ˈklædɪŋz/
n.
覆层，包层
A covering or coating on a structure or material.
The building's claddings were made of aluminum panels. 这栋建筑的覆层是由铝板制成的。

The wooden claddings of the house need to be repainted.  这栋房子的木质覆层需要重新粉刷。

近义词:covering, facing, veneer, coating
反义词:core, interior


词性:noun (plural)
词源:From the verb "clad" (past participle of "clothe"), meaning to cover or dress.  Ultimately from Old English "clǣthian".
