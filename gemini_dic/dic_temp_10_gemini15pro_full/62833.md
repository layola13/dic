enigmatical:/ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 神秘的；难以理解的；谜一样的 | Difficult to interpret or understand; mysterious. |  Her enigmatical smile made me curious. 她神秘的微笑让我很好奇。| The plot of the movie was enigmatical and kept us guessing until the very end. 这部电影的情节扑朔迷离，让我们一直猜到最后。 | 近义词: mysterious, puzzling, perplexing, cryptic, obscure | 反义词: clear, obvious, straightforward, transparent | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from French énigmatique or late Latin enigmaticus, from Greek ainigmatikos, from ainigma ‘riddle’.
