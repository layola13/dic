disperses:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪz/
verb
分散，驱散
to spread across or move away over a large area, or to make something do this
The crowd dispersed after the concert. 音乐会结束后人群散开了。
近义词: scatter, dissipate, distribute
反义词: gather, collect, assemble
词性: verb

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/
verb
分散，驱散
to spread across or move away over a large area, or to make something do this
The seeds are dispersed by the wind. 种子被风吹散了。
近义词: scatter, dissipate, distribute
反义词: gather, collect, assemble
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere (“to scatter”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + spargere (“to scatter, strew”).
