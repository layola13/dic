ill-prepared:/ˌɪlprɪˈpeərd/ | adj. |准备不足的，毫无准备的 | Not ready or adequately prepared for something. |  The team was ill-prepared for the competition and lost badly.  这个队伍对比赛准备不足，输得很惨。 | The hikers were ill-prepared for the blizzard and had to be rescued.  徒步旅行者对暴风雪毫无准备，不得不获救。 | synonyms: unprepared, unready, unequipped | antonyms: prepared, ready, equipped | adjective
