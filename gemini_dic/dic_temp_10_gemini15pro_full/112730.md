lozenger:/ˈlɒzɪndʒə(r)/ /ˈlɑːzɪndʒər/| n.|菱形, 斜方形, 含片, 药片|A lozenge is a shape with four equal sides, but which is not a square. It is like a square that has been pushed over to one side.  A lozenge is also a small flavored tablet that you suck on, especially to soothe a sore throat.|The window was made up of many small lozenges of colored glass.  窗户由许多彩色玻璃小菱形组成。

She took a cough lozenge to soothe her throat. 她吃了一颗止咳片来舒缓喉咙。|近义词：diamond, rhombus, tablet, pastille|反义词:|noun
