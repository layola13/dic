gullibility:/ˌɡʌləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 易受骗，轻信 | The quality of being easily tricked or cheated. |  His gullibility made him an easy target for con artists.  他的轻信使他很容易成为骗子的目标。| credulity, naiveté | skepticism, cynicism | noun

gullible:/ˈɡʌləbl/ | adj. | 易受骗的，轻信的 | Easily persuaded to believe something; credulous. | He was gullible enough to believe the stranger's story. 他很容易相信陌生人的故事。| credulous, naive | skeptical, cynical | adjective
