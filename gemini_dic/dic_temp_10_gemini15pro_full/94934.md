USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden?  = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this weekend? = 你这周末要做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will they have built by next year? = 到明年他们将建成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习了五年什么？
What will she have been doing for a living for ten years by 2030? = 到2030年，她将从事什么工作十年了？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

Interrogative Sentence:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。

what if = 如果...会怎么样？
what for = 为了什么？
