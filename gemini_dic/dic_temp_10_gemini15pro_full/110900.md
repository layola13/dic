liparite:/ˈlɪpəˌraɪt/ | n. | 流纹岩 | An extrusive igneous rock, the volcanic equivalent of granite, typically light-colored and fine-grained. | Example 1: The cliffs were composed primarily of liparite. 这些悬崖主要由流纹岩构成。

Example 2:  The geologist identified the sample as liparite due to its high silica content. 地质学家根据其高二氧化硅含量将样品鉴定为流纹岩。 | 近义词: rhyolite | 反义词: basalt(玄武岩,成分和颜色差异较大,可作反义词) | 词性: noun
