earth-bound:/ˈɜːrθbaʊnd/
adjective
受地球束缚的；尘世的；渴望尘世的
Stuck on Earth; unable to leave the Earth; preoccupied with earthly matters.
He felt earth-bound and longed to travel the world. 他感觉被束缚在地球上，渴望环游世界。
earthbound:/ˈɜːrθbaʊnd/
adjective
受地球束缚的；尘世的；渴望尘世的
Stuck on Earth; unable to leave the Earth; preoccupied with earthly matters.
Her earth-bound spirit yearned for the heavens. 她那渴望尘世的灵魂向往着天堂。

近义词:mundane, terrestrial, earthly
反义词:heavenly, celestial, spiritual
词性:adjective
词源:From earth + bound (past participle of bind).  Meaning "confined to the earth" is from the mid-15c. The figurative sense of "preoccupied with earthly matters" is from the late 16c.
