dipyrenous:/daɪˈpaɪrənəs/ | adj. | 双核的，有两个核的 | Having two pyrenes or nuclei.  Relating to or characterized by the presence of two pyrenes (fruit stones with two seeds).  It's most often used in a botanical context to describe fruits. |  This plant produces dipyrenous fruits, each containing two seeds.  这种植物结出双核果实，每个果实包含两颗种子。 | dipyrenous drupe | monopyrenous | adjective
