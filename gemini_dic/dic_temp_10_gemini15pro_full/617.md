abram:/ˈeɪbrəm/| n. |亚伯兰（男子名）|A masculine given name, the original name of the biblical patriarch Abraham.|Abram was the father of many nations. 亚伯兰是许多民族的父亲。|近义词: Abraham|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hebrew אַבְרָם ('Aḇrām), meaning "exalted father".  Later changed to Abraham (אַבְרָהָם, 'Aḇrāhām), meaning "father of a multitude".
