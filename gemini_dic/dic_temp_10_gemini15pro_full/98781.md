integral:/ˈɪntɪɡrəl/ /ɪnˈtɛɡrəl/ | adj. n.| 完整的，整体的；积分；组成整体不可缺少的部分 | essential, necessary, complete, whole |

The engine is an integral part of the car. | 发动机是汽车不可缺少的一部分。

Honesty is an integral part of her character. |  诚实是她性格中不可或缺的一部分。

integral:/ˈɪntɪɡrəl/ /ɪnˈtɛɡrəl/ | n. | 积分; 完整 | integration, entirety | derivative, differential | noun |

Calculate the integral of the function. | 计算该函数的积分。

The integral of the curve represents the area under it. | 曲线的积分表示它下面的面积。


Etymology:  From Late Latin *integralis*, from Latin integer "whole, complete" (see integer). The mathematical sense developed in the late 17th century.
