fusillis:/ˌfuːˈsiːliːz/ /ˌfjuːˈsiːliːz/
noun
螺旋粉，fusilli（一种短的螺旋状意大利面）
A type of pasta that is shaped like a short, tight spiral.
I made a salad with fusilli, tomatoes, and mozzarella.  我做了一个沙拉，里面有螺旋粉、西红柿和马苏里拉奶酪。
pasta, rotini, spirals
(No commonly used antonym for a type of pasta)
