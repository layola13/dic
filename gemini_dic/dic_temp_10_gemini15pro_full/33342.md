chuko:/ˈtʃuːkoʊ/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A somewhat derogatory term for a person considered foolish or naive. This word is primarily used in internet slang and gaming communities.|He called me a chuko after I fell for his obvious prank. 他在我上当了他的恶作剧后叫我傻瓜。|近义词：fool, idiot, dummy, dolt, simpleton|反义词: genius, smart person|名词
