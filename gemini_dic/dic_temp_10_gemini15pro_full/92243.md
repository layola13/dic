hypertrophied: /haɪˈpɜːrtrəfid/ | adj. | 肥大的，过度发达的 | Enlarged or overgrown, especially in relation to a tissue or organ due to increased size of its cells. |  The bodybuilder's hypertrophied muscles were impressive. 健美运动员肥大的肌肉令人印象深刻。 |  The patient's heart was hypertrophied due to a long-standing condition.  由于长期患病，病人的心脏肥大。 | synonyms: enlarged, overgrown, expanded, dilated | antonyms: atrophied, shrunken, diminished | adjective
