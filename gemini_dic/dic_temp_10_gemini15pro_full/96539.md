indomitable: /ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbl/ (UK) /ɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbl/ (US)| adj. |不屈不挠的，顽强的 |  Unconquerable, incapable of being subdued or overcome;  unyielding. |  She showed indomitable spirit in the face of adversity.  她在逆境中表现出不屈不挠的精神。| Synonyms:  resolute, unyielding, tenacious, persistent, persevering | Antonyms:  submissive, yielding, weak, cowardly | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indomitabilis, from in- "not" + domitabilis "tamable," from Latin domitare "to tame," frequentative of domare "to tame, subdue."
