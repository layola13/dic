crane:/kreɪn/| n. v.|起重机，鹤，伸长(脖子)|A tall metal structure with a long arm, used for lifting and moving heavy objects. A large, long-legged, long-necked bird, typically with white or grey plumage.|例句：The crane lifted the heavy container onto the ship. 起重机将沉重的集装箱吊到船上。|例句：We saw a flock of cranes flying overhead. 我们看到一群鹤从头顶飞过。|近义词：(n.) hoist, derrick, (v.) stretch, extend|反义词: (v.) contract, compress|词性: 名词, 动词

crane:/kreɪn/| v.|伸长(脖子)|To stretch out one's neck in order to see something better.|例句：She craned her neck to see over the crowd. 她伸长脖子想越过人群看。|例句：He craned forward to hear the conversation. 他探身向前想听清对话。|近义词：stretch, extend|反义词: contract, compress|词性: 动词
