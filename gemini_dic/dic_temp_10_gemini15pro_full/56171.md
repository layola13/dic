dodecahedron:/ˌdoʊdekəˈhiːdrən/ /ˌdɒdəkəˈhiːdrən/| n.|十二面体|A solid figure with twelve plane faces, especially one having regular pentagons as its faces.|The ancient Greeks studied the dodecahedron as one of the five Platonic solids.|Synonym: polyhedron|Antonym: (No direct antonym, possibly consider two-dimensional shapes like square, circle)|noun
