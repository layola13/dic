ergative:/ˈɜːrɡətɪv/| adj. n.| 作格的，作格|Relating to or denoting a case of nouns (the ergative) that identifies the agent or patient of a verb when the object is also present and the verb is intransitive.  A noun in the ergative case. |The Basque language uses ergative case markings. 巴斯克语使用作格标记。|The sword, wielded by the hero, vanquished the dragon. (Here, "hero" could be considered an ergative, as the sword is both the instrument and, in a sense, the "agent" of the action.) 英雄挥舞着剑，战胜了恶龙。（这里，“英雄”可以被认为是作格，因为剑既是工具，又在某种意义上是动作的“施动者”。）|transitive, accusative|absolutive|adjective, noun
