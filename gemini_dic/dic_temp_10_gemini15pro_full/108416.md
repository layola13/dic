lawniest:/ˈlɔːniɪst/
adjective
最草坪茂盛的; 草地最多的
Covered with the most lawns; having the most extensive area of lawn.
Their house had the lawniest front yard in the whole neighborhood.  他们家的前院是整个街区草坪最茂盛的。
The park boasts the lawniest expanse in the city.  这个公园拥有全市最广阔的草坪。
近义词: greenest, grassiest
反义词: barrenest, baldest
词性: adjective
词源: lawn (草坪) + -y (形容词后缀，表示“充满...的”) + -est (形容词最高级后缀)
