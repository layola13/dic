hollower:/ˈhɒləʊər (UK), ˈhɑːloʊər (US)| n. |挖空者，掏空者|Someone or something that makes something hollow. |The woodpeckers were the hollowers of the dead tree, creating homes for other creatures.  啄木鸟是枯树的掏空者，为其他生物创造了家园。| 近义词：excavator, borer | 反义词: filler, packer | noun
hollower:/ˈhɒləʊər (UK), ˈhɑːloʊər (US)| adj. (比较级) |更空的，更凹陷的|More hollow than something else. |This bowl is hollower than that one, so it can hold more soup. 这个碗比那个碗更凹，所以可以盛更多的汤。| 近义词：emptier, more concave | 反义词: fuller, more convex | adjective
