disadvantageousness:/ˌdɪsədvænˈteɪdʒəsnəs/
n.|不利，劣势|The disadvantageousness of the location hampered the business's growth.  这个位置的不利条件阻碍了业务的增长。|例句：The disadvantageousness of his background made it difficult for him to get ahead. 他背景的不利条件使他很难取得进展。|近义词：unfavourableness, detriment, drawback, handicap|反义词:advantageousness, favourableness, benefit|词性:名词

disadvantageousness:/ˌdɪsədvænˈteɪdʒəsnəs/
词源:  From disadvantage + -ous + -ness.  Ultimately from the Old French desavantage.
