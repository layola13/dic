daddle:/ˈdæd(ə)l/
verb
闲逛，游荡
to walk slowly or casually; to dawdle
例句：They daddled along the riverbank, enjoying the sunshine. 他们沿着河岸漫步，享受着阳光。

例句：Don't daddle! We're going to be late! 别磨蹭了！我们要迟到了！
近义词：dawdle, stroll, amble, wander
反义词：hurry, rush, hasten
词性:v.

daddle:/ˈdæd(ə)l/
noun
闲逛，游荡
a leisurely walk or stroll
例句：We went for a daddle in the park. 我们去公园里闲逛了一下。

例句：After lunch, we took a daddle around the lake. 午饭后，我们绕着湖边散步。
近义词：stroll, amble, wander
反义词：rush, sprint
词性:n.
