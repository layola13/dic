argy-bargy:/ˈɑːrɡi ˈbɑːrɡi/
|n.| 争吵，争论|A noisy quarrel or argument.|例句：They had a bit of an argy-bargy about the washing-up. 他们就洗碗的事吵了一架。

例句：The kids were having an argy-bargy over who got to sit in the front seat. 孩子们正在为谁坐在前排座位而争吵。
|近义词：quarrel, argument, squabble, dispute, row|反义词：agreement, harmony, peace|词性:名词

Etymology:
Possibly from Scots *argie-bargie,* perhaps related to *argue.*
