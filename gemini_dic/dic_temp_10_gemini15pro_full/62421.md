endomorph:/ˈɛndəˌmɔːrf/| n.|内胚层体型者；肥胖体型者|A person with a soft, rounded body build and a tendency to gain weight easily.  Characterized by a preponderance of body fat, large trunk and thighs, and relatively slender distal segments.|Example: He has the typical endomorph body type, with a stocky build and a predisposition to weight gain. 他是典型的内胚层体型，体格粗壮，易于增重。

Example: Endomorphs often excel at strength-based activities. 内胚层体型的人通常擅长力量型运动。|近义词:mesomorph(中等体型的人)|反义词:ectomorph(外胚层体型者，瘦长体型者)|词性:noun
