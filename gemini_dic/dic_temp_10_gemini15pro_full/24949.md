brush-up:/ˈbrʌʃ ʌp/ /ˈbrʌʃ ˌʌp/ | n. | 复习；温习；提高；改进 | An act of improving one's existing knowledge or skill in a particular subject. |  I took a brush-up course in French before my trip to Paris. 我去巴黎之前参加了一个法语复习课程。

brush-up:/ˈbrʌʃ ʌp/ /ˈbrʌʃ ˌʌp/ | v. | 复习；温习；提高；改进 | To improve one's existing knowledge or skill in a particular subject. | I need to brush up on my Spanish before my vacation. 我需要在假期前温习一下我的西班牙语。


Etymology->
From the phrase "brush up", meaning to improve or refresh something, as if by brushing away dust or cobwebs.  The earliest recorded usage is from the early 19th century.
