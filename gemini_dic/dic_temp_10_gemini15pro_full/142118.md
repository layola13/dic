oriflamme: /ˈɔːrɪˌflæm/ | n. | 金色火焰旗；(喻)象征；战旗 | A banner of red silk split into flames at the end and attached to a gilded spear, formerly the royal standard of France.  A symbol or ideal that serves as a rallying point, especially one associated with a cause or movement. | The oriflamme of freedom flew proudly above the barricades. 自由的金色火焰旗在路障上方骄傲地飘扬。| synonyms: banner, standard, pennant, ensign, emblem, symbol | antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider) surrender, defeat | noun
