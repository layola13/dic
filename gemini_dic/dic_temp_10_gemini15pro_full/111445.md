llm:/ɛl ɛl ɛm/ | n. | 大型语言模型 (Large Language Model) | A large language model is a computer program that can understand and generate human language. |  Example:  The LLM generated a poem that was surprisingly good.  大型语言模型生成了一首令人惊讶的好诗。

Example:  Researchers are working on improving the accuracy of LLMs.  研究人员正在努力提高大型语言模型的准确性。 | 近义词: language model, AI model | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "rule-based system") | 词性: noun
