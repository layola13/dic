discerners:/dɪˈsɜːrnərz/ | n. |鉴赏家；识别者；洞察者|  A discerner is someone who has or shows the ability to recognize or distinguish things, especially things of good quality or fine detail. |  The true wine discerners could tell the difference between the two vintages. 真正的葡萄酒鉴赏家可以分辨出这两个年份的葡萄酒。|  connoisseurs, experts, judges, specialists | undiscerning, naive, uncritical | noun
