nucleiform:/ˈnjuːkliɪfɔːrm/
adjective
核形的；似核的
Resembling a nucleus in form.  Having the shape of a nucleus.
The nucleiform structure was observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到了核形结构。
The amoeba exhibited a nucleiform body within its cytoplasm.  变形虫在其细胞质内呈现出一个核形体。
近义词: nuclear, nucleated
反义词: anuclear
