docudramas:/ˈdɑːkjuːˌdrɑːməz/ /ˈdɒkjuːˌdrɑːməz/
noun
纪实剧；文献片
A television programme or film which shows real events in the form of a story.
Example: The docudrama about the Titanic disaster was both moving and informative.  关于泰坦尼克号灾难的纪实剧既感人又富于信息量。

Example:  Many people find docudramas more engaging than traditional documentaries. 很多人觉得纪实剧比传统的纪录片更吸引人。

synonyms:  docufiction, fact-based drama
antonyms:  fiction, fantasy
词性:n.
