hymenopter:/ˌhaɪməˈnɒptər(ə)/, /ˌhaɪməˈnɑːptər/ | n. | 膜翅目昆虫 | An insect of the order Hymenoptera, including bees, wasps, ants, and sawflies, characterized by two pairs of membranous wings. | Example: Bees are hymenopterans known for their honey production. 蜜蜂是膜翅目昆虫，以其蜂蜜生产而闻名。

Example:  The hymenopteran stung the unsuspecting hiker.  这只膜翅目昆虫蛰了毫无防备的徒步旅行者。

| 近义词:  bee, wasp, ant, sawfly (depending on the specific hymenopteran) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Modern Latin *Hymenoptera,  from Ancient Greek ὑμήν (humḗn, “membrane”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
