inanition:/ˌɪnəˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 衰弱；营养不良；空虚 |A state of exhaustion caused by lack of nourishment.  A general lack of interest or vitality.|  The prolonged famine led to widespread inanition. 长期的饥荒导致普遍的营养不良。|  The inanition of the government led to widespread discontent. 政府的无所作为导致了普遍的不满。 | synonyms: exhaustion, weakness, lethargy, emptiness, apathy | antonyms: energy, vigor, strength, enthusiasm | noun
