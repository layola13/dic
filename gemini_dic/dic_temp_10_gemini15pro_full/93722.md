immanent:/ˈɪmənənt/| adj.|内在的，固有的|Existing or operating within; inherent.|例句：God is immanent in the universe. 上帝存在于宇宙万物之中。|例句：The immanent power of nature is awe-inspiring.  大自然固有的力量令人敬畏。|近义词：inherent, intrinsic, ingrained|反义词：external, extrinsic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin immanens, present participle of immanēre “to remain in, dwell in,” from in- “in” + manēre “to stay.”
