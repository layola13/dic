fortieth:/ˈfɔːrtiəθ/
adjective
第四十
The ordinal number of forty, being the number following thirty-ninth.
This is her fortieth birthday. 这是她的四十岁生日。
He finished in fortieth place. 他最终获得了第四十名。
近义词:quadragesimal
反义词:(no specific antonym for ordinal numbers)
词性:adjective

注意事项:
