hyper-immunity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. | 超级免疫，过度免疫 |  A heightened state of immunity, often achieved through repeated exposure to an antigen or through passive immunization. It refers to an abnormally increased immune response. |  The patient exhibited hyper-immunity after receiving multiple doses of the vaccine.  病人接种多剂疫苗后表现出超级免疫。| near synonyms: heightened immunity, increased immunity, excessive immunity | antonyms: immunodeficiency, immunosuppression | noun

hyper-immunity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmjuːnɪti/ | n. | 超级免疫力 | The condition of having an exceptionally strong immune response. |  Her hyper-immunity made her resistant to most common illnesses. 她的超级免疫力使她对大多数常见疾病具有抵抗力。| near synonyms:  super immunity, enhanced immunity | antonyms: immunodeficiency, immune deficiency | noun
