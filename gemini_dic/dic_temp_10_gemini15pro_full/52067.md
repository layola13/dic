dialogue:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəˌlɔːɡ/ (US)| n.|对话，交谈|A conversation between two or more people, especially in a book, play, or film.|The dialogue between the two characters was witty and engaging. 这两个角色之间的对话机智而引人入胜。|近义词：conversation, discussion, exchange, talk|反义词：monologue, soliloquy|noun

dialogue:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəˌlɔːɡ/ (US)| v.|进行对话|To engage in a conversation.|The two sides have agreed to dialogue to resolve the conflict. 双方同意通过对话解决冲突。|近义词：converse, discuss, talk, communicate|反义词：ignore, refuse to communicate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dialoge*, from Latin dialogus, from Ancient Greek διάλογος (diálogos, “conversation”), from διά (diá, “through, across”) + λέγω (légō, “I speak”).
