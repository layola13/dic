endostosis:/ɛnˌdɒˈstəʊsɪs/ | n. | 内生骨疣；骨内膜骨化 | The formation of bone within the medullary cavity of a bone.  It is the development of bony tissue within the substance of bone. |  The X-ray revealed areas of endostosis in the femur.  X光显示股骨内有内生骨疣区域。| synonym:  osseous metaplasia,  intraosseous ossification | antonym:  osteolysis | noun
