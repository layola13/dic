carrow:/ˈkærəʊ/ (UK), /ˈkæroʊ/ (US)| n.|小船，狭长的小船|A carrow is a type of small, narrow boat. |We took a carrow down the river. 我们乘着小船顺流而下。|The carrow glided silently through the water. 小船静静地滑过水面。|近义词：boat, skiff, punt|反义词:ship|词性:名词


词源：From Old English *cearr, from Proto-Germanic *karjon-, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷerh₂- (“to turn, bend”). Cognate with Old Norse kjǫrr (“ship”). The word is related to the verb "to careen," which means to tilt a boat to one side for cleaning or repair.
