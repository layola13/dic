boomingly:/ˈbuːmɪŋli/| adv.|蓬勃地，兴旺地，繁荣地|In a booming manner; with a loud, resonant sound, suggesting rapid growth or success.  Describes something increasing or progressing rapidly and successfully.|The business was boomingly successful, expanding into new markets at an incredible rate.  这家公司蓬勃发展，以惊人的速度向新市场扩张。|例句：The economy is growing boomingly. 经济正在蓬勃发展。|近义词：resoundingly, thrivingly, prosperously, flourishingly|反义词：decliningly, failingly, sluggishly|词性:副词
