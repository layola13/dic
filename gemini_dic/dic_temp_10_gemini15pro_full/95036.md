inacquiescent:/ˌɪnˌækwiˈɛsənt/
adjective
不默许的，不顺从的，不情愿的
Not acquiescing; not accepting something quietly or without protest.
His inacquiescent nature made him challenge every rule. 他不默许的性格使他对每条规则都提出质疑。
The crowd was inacquiescent to the new regulations. 群众不情愿接受新规定。

近义词:dissenting, objecting, resistant, rebellious, defiant, noncompliant
反义词:acquiescent, compliant, agreeable, consenting, yielding, submissive
词性:adjective
