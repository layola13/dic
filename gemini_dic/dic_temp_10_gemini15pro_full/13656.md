attornment:/əˈtɜːrnmənt/
n.
承认新业主; 转让租赁契约; 租赁关系的转移;
The act of a tenant acknowledging a new landlord.  The transfer of a tenant's lease to a new landlord. The transfer of a tenancy.
The attornment of the tenants to the new owner was completed swiftly.  房客们迅速地承认了新业主。
The lease required attornment to the new landlord upon sale of the property.  租赁协议要求在房产出售后承认新业主。
近义词: acknowledgment, recognition, transfer
反义词: denial, repudiation
