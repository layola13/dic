bestow:/bɪˈstoʊ/ | vt. |授予，给予，放置|to give something as a gift or honor|The queen bestowed the knighthood upon him. 女王授予他爵位。|Synonyms: confer, grant, award, present |Antonyms: withhold, deprive, revoke|verb


bestow:/bɪˈstoʊ/ | vt. |放置|to put something somewhere carefully|She bestowed her handbag on the chair. 她小心翼翼地把她的手提包放在椅子上。|Synonyms: place, put, position |Antonyms: remove, take|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bestēowan (unattested), from be- + stēowan "to place," from Proto-Germanic *stawjaną "to stand, place" (source also of Old Saxon stōian, Old Norse stœja, Danish støde "to push," Dutch stuwen "to stow").
