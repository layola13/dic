indented:/ɪnˈdɛntɪd/ | adj. | 缩进的，锯齿状的 |  Having an outline or surface that is uneven by having parts that go inwards or outwards. |  The coastline is deeply indented.  海岸线是 deeply indented（深深锯齿状的）. |  notched, jagged, serrated | smooth, even | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *endenten*, from Old French *endenté* "toothed, notched," past participle of *endenter*, from *dent* "tooth."  Ultimately from Latin *dens, dent-* "tooth."
