carry-the-ball:/ˈkæri ðə bɔːl/| idiom|承担责任，负起主要责任|To take responsibility for something and see it through to completion, often a difficult or important task.|例句：He carried the ball on that project and made sure it was a success. 他在那个项目中承担了主要责任，并确保它取得了成功。

例句：She's always willing to carry the ball when there's a tough job to do. 当有困难的工作要做时，她总是愿意承担责任。|近义词：take charge, take responsibility, take the lead, spearhead|反义词：shirk responsibility, pass the buck|词性: idiom
