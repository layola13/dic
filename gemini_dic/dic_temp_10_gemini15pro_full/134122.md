nonimpeachability:/ˌnɒnɪmˌpiːtʃəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可弹劾性，不可指责性 | The state or quality of being exempt from impeachment or accusation.  | The president's nonimpeachability shielded him from legal consequences. 总统的不可弹劾性使其免于承担法律后果。 |  Synonyms: immunity, inviolability | Antonyms:  vulnerability, liability | noun
