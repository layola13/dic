overcautiousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəsnəs/| n. |过度谨慎，过分小心|The state of being excessively cautious; extreme or undue caution.|His overcautiousness prevented him from taking the necessary risks to succeed. 他过分的谨慎阻碍了他去承担获得成功所需的风险。|近义词：cautiousness, prudence, circumspection|反义词：recklessness, impulsiveness, rashness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From over- + cautiousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Cautiousness" is the noun form of "cautious", ultimately derived from the Latin word "cautus" meaning "careful" or "wary".
