nonenviousness:/nɒnˈɛnviəsnɪs/
n.
无嫉妒; 不羡慕;
lack of envy; the state of not being envious.
She expressed nonenviousness at her friend's success. 她对朋友的成功表示并不羡慕。
近义词: lack of envy, generosity, magnanimity
反义词: envy, jealousy, covetousness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + envious + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "envious" comes from the Old French "envieus," ultimately from the Latin "invidia" (envy), and "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality.
