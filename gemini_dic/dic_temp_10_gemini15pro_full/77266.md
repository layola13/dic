gastroraphy:/ˈɡæstrəˌræfi/| n.|胃缝合术|The surgical suturing of a wound or tear in the stomach.  It is a procedure to close a perforation or repair damage to the stomach.|The surgeon performed a gastroraphy after discovering a perforation in the patient's stomach.  外科医生在发现病人胃穿孔后进行了胃缝合术。

The gastroraphy was successful and the patient recovered quickly. 胃缝合手术很成功，病人恢复得很快。|近义词:stomach repair, stomach suture|反义词:|n.
