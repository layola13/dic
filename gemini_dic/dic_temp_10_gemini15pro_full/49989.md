denizations:/ˌdɛnɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|入籍；给予公民权；(尤指)给予外国人公民权|The denizations of immigrants have increased significantly this year. 今年移民入籍的人数显著增加。|
|n.|居住权；居住许可|The government granted denizations to refugees fleeing the war. 政府给予逃离战争的难民居住权。|
|Etymology:|From denizen + -ation.  Denizen itself comes from Old French *denezein, from denz "within, from within," from Latin de- "from" + intus "within."|
|Synonyms:|naturalizations, citizenships, enfranchisements|
|Antonyms:|expulsions, deportations, exiles|
|Word Type:|Noun|
