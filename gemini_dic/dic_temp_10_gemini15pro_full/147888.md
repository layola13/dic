overrealism:/ˌōvərˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. | 超现实主义; 过度现实主义 | A style in art or literature characterized by the representation of the real world with heightened or exaggerated detail, often to a disturbing or surreal effect.  It focuses on the mundane aspects of everyday life, portraying them with intense precision and clarity, sometimes to the point of absurdity or unease. | The artist's overrealism captured the starkness of urban decay. 这位艺术家的超现实主义捕捉到了城市衰败的严酷景象。 | hyperrealism, photorealism | realism, naturalism | noun
