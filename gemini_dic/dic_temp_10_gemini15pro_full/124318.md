motherlessness:/ˈmʌðərləsnəs/| n. |无母的状态，失去母亲的悲痛|The state of being without a mother, either through death or absence.  It can also refer to the emotional impact of this absence. |Her motherlessness deeply affected her childhood. 她无母的状态深深地影响了她的童年。| The profound motherlessness he felt after her death was overwhelming.  她去世后，他感受到的深深的无母之痛让他难以承受。|近义词：orphanhood, maternal deprivation|反义词: motherhood|词性:名词
