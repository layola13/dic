honeycombs:/ˈhʌnikəʊmz/ (UK) /ˈhʌnikomz/ (US)| n.|蜂窝，蜂巢，蜂窝状结构|A structure of rows of hexagonal compartments, resembling a honeycomb made by bees. |The bees were busy building honeycombs in the hive. 蜜蜂们正忙着在蜂箱里筑蜂巢。|The chocolate bar had a honeycomb structure. 这块巧克力棒有着蜂窝状的结构。| synonyms: beehive, cells, network, lattice| antonyms: solid, mass| noun
