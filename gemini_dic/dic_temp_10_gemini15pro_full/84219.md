half-ruined:/ˌhæf ˈruːɪnd/
adj.
半毁的，部分损毁的
partially ruined or destroyed; damaged or decayed but not completely destroyed
The half-ruined castle stood on the hill overlooking the town. 半毁的城堡矗立在可以俯瞰小镇的山丘上。
The earthquake left many buildings half-ruined. 地震使许多建筑物半毁。
Synonyms: dilapidated, damaged, decayed, partially destroyed
Antonyms: intact, pristine, undamaged, perfect
词性: 形容词
