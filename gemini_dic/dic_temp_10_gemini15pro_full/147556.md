overpensive:/ˌoʊvərˈpɛnsɪv/| adj.|过度沉思的，忧郁的|Deeply or excessively thoughtful, often in a melancholy way; preoccupied with serious or sad thoughts.|She sat by the window, overpensive and staring into the distance. 她坐在窗边，忧心忡忡地凝视着远方。|例句：He had an overpensive look on his face. 他脸上带着忧郁的表情。|近义词：pensive, thoughtful, contemplative, brooding, melancholic|反义词：carefree, lighthearted, cheerful|词性:形容词
词源: From over- + pensive, from Old French pensif, from penser "to think," from Latin pensare "to weigh, consider."
