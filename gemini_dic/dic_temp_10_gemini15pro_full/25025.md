bruv:/brʌv/| n.|兄弟，哥们儿 (Informal, chiefly British)|A shortened form of "brother," used as a term of address for a male friend or acquaintance.  It carries a casual and friendly tone, similar to "mate" or "dude."|例句：Alright, bruv, what's the plan? 好，兄弟，有什么计划？

例句：I saw my bruv at the pub last night. 我昨晚在酒吧看到我兄弟了。|近义词：mate, pal, dude, bro, brother|反义词:enemy, foe|Noun
