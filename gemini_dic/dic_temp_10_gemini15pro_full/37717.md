comparativeness:/kəmˈpærətɪvnəs/| n.|比较性，可比性|The comparativeness of the two products is striking. 这两种产品的可比性很强。|例句：The study explored the comparativeness of different teaching methods.  这项研究探讨了不同教学方法的比较性。

例句：The comparativeness of their situations made it easier to understand their choices.  他们处境的相似性使得更容易理解他们的选择。|近义词：similarity, comparability, resemblance|反义词：dissimilarity, difference, incomparability|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From comparative + -ness.  Comparative stems from Late Latin comparativus, from Latin comparare "to compare."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
