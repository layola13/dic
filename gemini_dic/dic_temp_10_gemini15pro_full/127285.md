navigable:/ˈnævɪɡəbl/| adj.|可航行的，可通航的|Suitable for sailing or traveling on water.  Able to be navigated or traversed, especially of waterways.|The river is navigable for small boats. 这条河可供小船航行。|近义词：passable, traversable|反义词：impassable, unnavigable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *navigabilis, from Latin navigare "to sail, sail over, navigate," from navis "ship" + agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
