anthropic:/ænˈθrɒpɪk/ | adj. |人类的；与人类有关的；由人类引起的| Relating to human beings or their influence on nature, especially the negative impact of human activities on the environment. |例句：The anthropic principle suggests that the universe is the way it is because if it were different, we wouldn't be here to observe it. 人择原理表明，宇宙之所以是现在这个样子，是因为如果它不同，我们就不会在这里观察它。

例句： Some scientists believe that climate change is an anthropic phenomenon, caused by human activities. 一些科学家认为，气候变化是一种人为现象，是由人类活动造成的。|近义词：human, humanistic |反义词: natural, non-human |词性: adjective
