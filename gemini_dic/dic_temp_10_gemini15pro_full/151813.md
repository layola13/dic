passed away:/ˈpɑːst əˈweɪ/
verb phrase
去世，逝世
Euphemism for "died." It implies a peaceful or gentle death.
My grandfather passed away peacefully in his sleep last night. 我祖父昨晚在睡梦中安详地去世了。
The beloved actress passed away after a long battle with cancer.  这位深受爱戴的女演员在与癌症长期抗争后去世了。
近义词: died, expired, deceased
反义词: born, living
词性: verb phrase
