headlinese: /ˈhɛdˌlaɪniz/ | n. |  标题体; 标题语言 | A style of writing characteristic of headlines, especially in newspapers, using short words, abbreviations, clichés, and unusual word order. | The article was full of headlinese, making it difficult to understand the actual details. 这篇文章充满了标题体，很难理解实际的细节。| synonyms: journalese, tabloidese | antonyms:  standard English, formal writing | noun
