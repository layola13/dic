cessionary:/ˈsɛʃənɛri/
n.
受让人，接受权利或财产的人
a person who receives a right or property by cession.
The bank is the cessionary of the mortgage. 该银行是抵押权的受让人。
近义词: assignee, transferee, recipient
反义词: cedent, assignor, grantor
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cessionarius, from Latin cessio (“cession, surrender”), from cedere (“to yield, withdraw”).
