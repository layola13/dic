allotropic:/ˌæləˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 同素异形的 | Relating to or exhibiting allotropy; existing in different forms, each with distinct physical properties. |  Diamond and graphite are allotropic forms of carbon. 钻石和石墨是碳的同素异形体。 | synonyms: polymorphic, isomeric | antonyms: monomorphic | adjective


allotrope:/ˈæləˌtrəʊp/ | n. | 同素异形体 | Any of two or more different physical forms in which an element can exist. |  Graphite is an allotrope of carbon. 石墨是碳的一种同素异形体。 | synonyms: polymorphic form, isomer | antonyms: N/A | noun
