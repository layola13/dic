archipelagoes: /ˌɑːrkɪˈpɛləˌɡoʊz/ (US) /ˌɑːkɪˈpɛləˌɡəʊz/ (UK)| n. |群岛|A group of islands closely scattered in a sea or ocean.|The Indonesian archipelagoes are renowned for their biodiversity. 印度尼西亚群岛以其生物多样性而闻名。|近义词：islands, island chain, island group|反义词:continent, mainland|词性:noun

archipelagoes: /ˌɑːrkɪˈpɛləˌɡoʊz/ (US) /ˌɑːkɪˈpɛləˌɡəʊz/ (UK)| n. |群岛|The Philippines is an archipelago of over 7,000 islands. 菲律宾是一个由7000多个岛屿组成的群岛。|近义词：islands, island chain, island group|反义词:continent, mainland|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *arcipelago*, from Greek *arkhipelagos* (ἄρχιπέλαγος), meaning "chief sea,"  composed of *arkhi-* (ἄρχι-) meaning "chief" and *pelagos* (πέλαγος) meaning "sea."  Originally referred specifically to the Aegean Sea, later generalized to any sea studded with islands, and eventually to the islands themselves.
