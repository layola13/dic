just-in-time:/ˌdʒʌst ɪn ˈtaɪm/ | adj. | 及时的; 准时的; 零库存的 | Done or occurring at the most opportune or appropriate moment. | The company operates a just-in-time inventory system.  这家公司实行准时制库存系统。 | Synonym: timely, prompt, opportune | Antonym: late, delayed, untimely | adjective

just-in-times:/ˌdʒʌst ɪn ˈtaɪmz/ | n. | 及时生产; 零库存 |  A system of manufacturing based on the principle of producing goods only as they are needed, thus minimizing the time they are held in stock. | Many manufacturers have adopted just-in-times production methods. 许多制造商已经采用了准时制生产方法。| Synonym:  JIT, lean manufacturing | Antonym: mass production, stockpiling | noun
