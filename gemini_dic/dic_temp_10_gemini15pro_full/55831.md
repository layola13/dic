dividendless:/ˈdɪvɪdendˌlɛs/
adjective
无股息的，不分红的
Not paying dividends; without dividends.
This year has been dividendless for investors in that company. 今年对于那家公司的投资者来说是颗粒无收的一年。
The company issued dividendless shares. 该公司发行了无股息股票。
synonym: non-dividend-paying, unproductive, barren
antonym: dividend-paying, profitable, fruitful
词性: adjective
