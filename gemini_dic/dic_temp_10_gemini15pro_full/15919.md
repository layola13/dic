ballsy:/ˈbɔːlzi/| adj.|有胆量的，大胆的，有魄力的|Showing courage and determination, often in a way that is considered rude or shocking.  Taking risks; gutsy.|例句：It was a ballsy move to start his own business.  开创自己的事业是一个大胆的举动。

例句：She made a ballsy decision to quit her job and travel the world. 她做了一个大胆的决定，辞掉工作去环游世界。|近义词：gutsy, courageous, daring, audacious, bold, brave|反义词：timid, cowardly, fearful, cautious|词性:形容词

词源:  Derived from "balls" (testicles), referring to stereotypical masculine bravery.  源自“balls”（睾丸），指的是刻板印象中的男性勇敢。
