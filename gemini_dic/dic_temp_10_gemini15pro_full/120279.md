miniaturising:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
微型化；小型化
making something much smaller than it usually is.
例句：They are miniaturising electronic circuits. 他们正在使电子电路小型化。
例句：Miniaturising technology has revolutionized the electronics industry. 小型化技术彻底改变了电子工业。
近义词：shrinking, reducing, scaling down
反义词：enlarging, expanding, maximizing
词性：v.

miniaturization:/ˌmɪnɪətʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
微型化；小型化
the process of making something very small using modern technology.
例句：The miniaturisation of electronics has made portable devices possible. 电子产品的小型化使便携式设备成为可能。
例句：Further miniaturisation of components is essential for progress in this field.  组件的进一步小型化对于该领域的进步至关重要。
近义词：reduction, shrinking, scaling down
反义词：enlargement, expansion, maximizing
词性：n.
