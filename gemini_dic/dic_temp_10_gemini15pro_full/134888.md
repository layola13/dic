nonmountainous:/ˌnɒnˈmaʊntɪnəs/ | adj. | 非山区的；非多山的 | Not having mountains; relatively flat or rolling. |  The region is characterized by its nonmountainous terrain, making it suitable for agriculture. 这个地区以其非山区的地形为特征，使其适合农业发展。 | The nonmountainous landscape offered stunning views of the vast plains.  非山区的景观提供了广阔平原的壮丽景色。 | 近义词: flat, level, plain | 反义词: mountainous, hilly, rugged | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "mountainous" (adjective meaning "having many mountains").
