denudement:/ˌdiːˈnjuːdmɒnt/ /ˌdiːˈnuːdmənt/| n.|结局，收场；（森林等的）裸露，剥蚀|The denouement of the play was surprising and unexpected. 这部戏的结局令人惊讶和出乎意料。|The denudation of the hills by overgrazing is a serious ecological problem. 过度放牧导致山丘裸露是一个严重的生态问题。|synonyms: conclusion, resolution, outcome, climax, culmination, unveiling, exposure, stripping|antonyms: beginning, start, commencement, covering, concealment|noun

denude:/dɪˈnjuːd/ /dɪˈnuːd/| vt.|使裸露；剥夺，夺去|The fire denuded the hillside of trees. 大火烧光了山坡上的树木。|The new law denudes citizens of their basic rights.  这项新法律剥夺了公民的基本权利。|synonyms: strip, bare, divest, deprive, expose|antonyms: cover, clothe, protect, enrich|verb
