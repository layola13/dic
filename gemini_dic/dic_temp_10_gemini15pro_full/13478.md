atrophying:/ˈætrəfiɪŋ/ | v. | 萎缩，衰退 |  Gradually declining in effectiveness or vigor due to underuse or neglect. |  His muscles were atrophying from lack of exercise. 他的肌肉因为缺乏锻炼而萎缩。 |  deteriorating, weakening, withering | developing, growing, strengthening | verb

atrophy: /ˈætrəfi/ | n. | 萎缩，衰退 | The process of gradually declining in effectiveness or vigor due to underuse or neglect. |  Muscle atrophy is a common side effect of prolonged bed rest.  长时间卧床休息的常见副作用是肌肉萎缩。| decline, deterioration, degeneration | growth, development, strengthening | noun

词源: From French *atrophie*, from Latin *atrophia*, from Greek *ἀτροφία* (atrophia), from *ἄτροφος* (atrophos, "ill-fed, unnourished"), from *ἀ-* (a-, "not") + *τροφή* (trophē, "nourishment").
