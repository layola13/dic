controlled:/kənˈtrəʊld/ (UK) /kənˈtroʊld/ (US)| adj.|受控制的；克制的；管制的|Governed or regulated, restrained or managed.|例句：The experiment was conducted under controlled conditions. 实验是在受控条件下进行的。|近义词：managed, regulated, restrained, governed, supervised|反义词:uncontrolled, unrestrained, wild, free|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "control" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Control" comes from Old French "contreroller" (to check, verify), from "contre-" (against) + "rolle" (roll, list). Ultimately from Latin "contra" (against) and "rotulus" (small wheel, roll).
