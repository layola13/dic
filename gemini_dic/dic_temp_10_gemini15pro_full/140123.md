off-street:/ˌɒfˈstriːt/ /ˌɔːfˈstriːt/
adjective
非街道的；路外的；不在街上的
Located or taking place away from public streets.
Example: Off-street parking is available behind the building.  大楼后面有路外停车位。

Example: The hotel offers convenient off-street parking for guests.  酒店为客人提供方便的非街停车位。

近义词: private, non-street
反义词: on-street
词性: adjective
