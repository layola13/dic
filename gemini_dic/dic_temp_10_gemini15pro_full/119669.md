middling: /ˈmɪdlɪŋ/ | adj. | 中等的，一般的 | Moderate or average in size, amount, or quality. | He was a middling student, neither brilliant nor failing. 他是一个中等水平的学生，既不出色也不失败。 | The restaurant received middling reviews. 这家餐馆收到的评价一般。| 近义词: average, moderate, mediocre | 反义词: excellent, superior, inferior | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *middelinges, from middling + -es (plural suffix). Ultimately from Old English middel.
