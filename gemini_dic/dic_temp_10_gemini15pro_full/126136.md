mutilators:/ˈmjuːtɪleɪtərz/| n. |残害者，毁坏者|People who mutilate something, causing damage or injury, especially by cutting or tearing.  Those who disfigure or make imperfect.|The mutilators defaced the statue with graffiti. 这些残害者用涂鸦污损了雕像。|synonyms: vandals, destroyers, defacers,  damager|antonyms: protectors, preservers, restorers, conservators|noun
