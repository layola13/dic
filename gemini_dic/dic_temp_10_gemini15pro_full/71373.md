flagellum:/fləˈdʒɛləm/ | n. |鞭毛 | A slender threadlike structure, especially a microscopic whiplike appendage that enables many protozoa, bacteria, spermatozoa, etc., to swim. |  The bacterium uses its flagellum to move towards the food source.  细菌利用鞭毛游向食物来源。 | synonyms: cilium, appendage | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "immobile part") | noun

flagella:/fləˈdʒɛl.ə/ | n. |鞭毛（复数） | Plural form of flagellum. | Many bacteria have multiple flagella. 许多细菌有多条鞭毛。 | synonyms: cilia, appendages | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
