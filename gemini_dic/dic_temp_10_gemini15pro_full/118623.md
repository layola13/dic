messiad:/məˈsaɪəd/ | n. |  弥赛亚诗; 史诗;  | An epic poem celebrating a hero, especially one concerned with a deliverer of a people.  A long narrative poem celebrating the achievements of a hero, usually one who has a divinely ordained mission to save his people or nation. |  Milton's "Paradise Lost" is considered a messiad.  弥尔顿的《失乐园》被认为是一部弥赛亚诗。

The Aeneid is a Roman messiad celebrating the founding of Rome.  《埃涅德》是一部罗马史诗，歌颂了罗马的建立。 | 近义词: epic, saga | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
