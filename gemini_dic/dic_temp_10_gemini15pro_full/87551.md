heteromorphous:/ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfəs/
adjective
异形的，不同形态的
Differing in form or structure, especially from the usual or expected form.
Example: The butterfly undergoes a heteromorphous transformation from caterpillar to its winged adult stage. 蝴蝶经历了从毛毛虫到有翅成虫的异形转变。

Example: The heteromorphous crystals were difficult to classify. 这些不同形态的晶体很难分类。

synonym: polymorphic, allomorphic, dissimilar, variant, diverse
antonym: homomorphic, isomorphic, similar, uniform, identical
词性: adjective
