nonpenetrations:/ˌnɒnˌpɛnɪˈtreɪʃənz/| n.| 非穿透，不渗透|The system relies on nonpenetrations of the protective barrier to maintain sterility.|该系统依靠保护屏障的非穿透性来保持无菌状态。|近义词：impenetrations, impermeability|反义词: penetrations, permeability|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + penetration.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "penetration" comes from the Latin "penetrat-" (past participle stem of "penetrare," meaning "to enter, pierce").
