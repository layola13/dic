commandlet:/kəˈmændlɪt/| n. |小型命令程序，cmdlet|A lightweight command used in PowerShell.  It is a specialized .NET class that implements a specific operation. |Example: The `Get-ChildItem` commandlet lists all files and directories in the specified location.  获取子项目cmdlet列出了指定位置中的所有文件和目录。

Example:  He used a commandlet to restart the service. 他使用了一个小型命令程序来重启服务。|近义词: command, script, applet |反义词: N/A | Noun
