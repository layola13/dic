cut a figure:/kʌt ə ˈfɪɡjər/
|idiomatic phrase|
|炫耀；引人注目；给人留下深刻印象|
|To make a striking or noticeable impression, often through appearance or behavior.|
|He cut a dashing figure in his new suit. 他穿着新西装，显得十分潇洒。|
|make an impression, stand out, attract attention, be noticeable|
|blend in, go unnoticed|
|phrase|


cut a fine figure:/kʌt ə faɪn ˈfɪɡjər/
|idiomatic phrase|
|展现优雅姿态；给人留下好印象|
|To present a striking and elegant appearance.|
|She cut a fine figure on the dance floor. 她在舞池中展现了优美的姿态。|
|make a good impression, look good, appear elegant|
|look awkward, be clumsy|
|phrase|
