amphilogism: /æmˈfɪləˌdʒɪzəm/
n.
模棱两可的话，双关语
A statement or phrase that is worded in such a way that it can be interpreted in two or more different ways.
Her remark about the "interesting" dress was an amphilogism; it could be taken as either a compliment or an insult.
她的关于那件“有趣的”连衣裙的评论是一句模棱两可的话；它可以被理解为赞美，也可以被理解为侮辱。

近义词: ambiguity, double entendre, equivocation
反义词: clarity, precision
词性: noun
