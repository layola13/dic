bolometer:/boʊˈlɒmɪtər/ | n. | 辐射热测量计，测辐射热计 | An instrument that measures the power of incident electromagnetic radiation via the heating of a material with a temperature-dependent electrical resistance. |  A bolometer is used to detect faint heat radiation. 辐射热测量计用于探测微弱的热辐射。

The astronomer used a bolometer to measure the infrared radiation from the star. 天文学家用辐射热测量计来测量来自恒星的红外辐射。 | 近义词: radiometer, infrared detector | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific instrument) | 词性: noun
