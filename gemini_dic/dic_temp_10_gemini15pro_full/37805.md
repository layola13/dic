compendiate:/kəmˈpɛndiˌeɪt/ | v. | 概括；缩写；汇编 | To shorten or abridge; to summarize; to condense. |  He compendiated the lengthy report into a concise summary. 他把冗长的报告概括成一个简洁的摘要。

compendiate:/kəmˈpɛndiˌeɪt/ | adj. | 简洁的；概括的 |  Containing the substance of a larger work in a brief form. | The book offers a compendiate overview of the history of philosophy. 这本书简要概述了哲学史。

compendious:/kəmˈpɛndiəs/ | adj. | 简洁的；概括的 | Containing much in a small space; concise. |  A compendious dictionary. 一本简明的词典。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *compendiāre*, present active infinitive of *compendiō* (“I weigh together, abridge, shorten”), from *com-* (“together”) + *pendō* (“I weigh, pay”).
