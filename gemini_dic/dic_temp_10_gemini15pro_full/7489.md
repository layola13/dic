ankylosis:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊsɪs/ /ˌæŋkaɪˈləʊsɪs/| n. |关节强直，僵硬|Stiffness or fixation of a joint.|The patient experienced ankylosis of the knee after the injury.  病人受伤后膝盖僵硬了。|Synonyms: stiffness, rigidity, immobility|Antonyms: flexibility, mobility|noun

ankylose:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊz/ /ˌæŋkaɪˈləʊz/| v. |使关节强直，使僵硬|(of a joint) become stiff or fixed.|The disease can ankylose the joints of the spine. 这种疾病会导致脊柱关节僵硬。|Synonyms:  stiffen, rigidify, immobilize|Antonyms: loosen, free|verb
