criminaloid:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlɔɪd/
n.|犯罪类人|A person who displays criminal tendencies but is not considered a true criminal due to their social standing or adherence to outward respectability.  A person who commits crimes that are technically illegal but not viewed as morally reprehensible by society or themselves.|The wealthy businessman, though engaged in shady dealings, was considered a criminaloid rather than a hardened criminal.|near synonyms:  delinquent, miscreant, wrongdoer|antonym: law-abiding citizen|noun
