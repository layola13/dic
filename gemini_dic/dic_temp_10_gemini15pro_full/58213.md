drug-up:/drʌɡ ˈʌp/ | v. | 给…用药；使…兴奋 | To administer drugs to someone, often secretly or illicitly, to sedate or stimulate them. | The vet drugged up the agitated horse to calm it down. 兽医给焦躁不安的马用了药，让它平静下来。

The kidnappers drugged up their victim to keep him quiet. 绑匪给受害者用药，让他保持安静。| 近义词: sedate, dope, narcotize | 反义词: sober up | 词性: 动词
