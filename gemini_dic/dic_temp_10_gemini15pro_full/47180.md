day-release:/ˈdeɪ rɪˈliːs/
noun
日间释放；半工半读
A system or program where someone, typically a prisoner or student, is allowed to leave their place of confinement or study during the day, often for work or education.
Many prisoners are eligible for day-release towards the end of their sentence. 许多囚犯在刑期即将结束时有资格获得日间释放。
The college offers day-release courses for working professionals.  这所大学为在职专业人士提供日间释放课程。
近义词: part-time study, work-study program
反义词: full-time imprisonment, full-time study
