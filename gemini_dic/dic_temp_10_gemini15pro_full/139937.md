odometer:/oʊˈdɑːmɪtər/ | n. |里程表；里程计； |An instrument for measuring the distance travelled by a vehicle. |The odometer showed that the car had traveled over 100,000 miles. 里程表显示这辆车已经行驶了超过10万英里。|Synonyms: milometer, trip meter |Antonyms: None | noun
