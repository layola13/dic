anoplura:/ænəˈplʊərə/
n.
虱目；吸虱类
Lice; an order of parasitic insects.
Anoplura are wingless insects that live as ectoparasites on mammals. 虱目是寄生在哺乳动物体外的无翅昆虫。
The Anoplura, commonly known as sucking lice, infest a variety of mammals. 虱目，通常被称为吸虱，寄生在各种哺乳动物身上。
近义词:lice
词性:noun
