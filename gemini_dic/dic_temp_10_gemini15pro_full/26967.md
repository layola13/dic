calcifying:/ˈkælsɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
钙化的；硬化的
Becoming hardened by the deposit of calcium salts.
例句：The X-ray showed calcifying arteries.  X光显示动脉钙化。

例句：Calcifying tendinitis is a painful condition. 钙化性肌腱炎是一种痛苦的疾病。

近义词：hardening, ossifying
反义词：softening
词性: adjective
