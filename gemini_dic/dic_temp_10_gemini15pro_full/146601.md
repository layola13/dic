overgrieving:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度悲伤
Grieving for too long or too intensely.  The prefix "over-" indicates excess.
She was overgrieving the loss of her pet hamster, much to her family's concern. 她对失去宠物仓鼠过度悲伤，令她的家人非常担心。

He realized he was overgrieving and sought professional help. 他意识到自己过度悲伤，并寻求了专业帮助。

近义词: excessive grieving, wallowing in grief
反义词:  (No direct opposite, but consider) acceptance, moving on, healing
词性: verb (present participle)
