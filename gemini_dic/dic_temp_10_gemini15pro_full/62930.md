enmeshment:/ɪnˈmɛʃmənt/| n. |纠缠，卷入，牵连|A situation in which people are too involved with each other in a way that is difficult for them to separate themselves.|Her enmeshment with her family made it hard for her to develop her own identity. 她与家人的过度牵连让她难以发展自己的身份认同。|envelopment, entanglement, involvement| detachment, separation|noun

enmeshment:/ɪnˈmɛʃmənt/| n. |陷入困境，深陷其中|A situation of being trapped or caught in something difficult.|The company's enmeshment in legal battles drained its resources. 该公司深陷法律纠纷，耗尽了资源。|entrapment, involvement| freedom, extrication|noun
