dancingly:/ˈdɑːnsɪŋli/ | adv. | 舞蹈般地，轻盈地 | In a dancing or light manner; gracefully. | She moved dancingly across the floor. 她轻盈地穿过地板。| The leaves floated dancingly on the breeze. 叶子在微风中轻盈地飘动。 | 近义词：gracefully, lightly, nimbly, elegantly | 反义词：clumsily, awkwardly | 词性: 副词

词源: From dance + -ing + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" transforms the present participle "dancing" into an adverb.  Ultimately from Old French "dancier."
