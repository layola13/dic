fruitful:/ˈfruːtfəl/| adj.|富有成效的；多产的；丰硕的|Producing good results; producing a lot of fruit or crops.|例句：The meeting was very fruitful, and we made a lot of progress. 会议非常富有成效，我们取得了很大进展。|例句：The apple tree was fruitful this year, yielding a bountiful harvest. 今年苹果树硕果累累，收获丰盛。|近义词：productive, profitable, fertile, abundant|反义词：unproductive, barren, sterile|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fruteful, *fritful, from Old English *frēohtfull (“fruit-bearing”), equivalent to fruit +‎ -ful. Cognate with Dutch vruchtbaar (“fruitful”), German fruchtbar (“fruitful”).
