mateys:/ˈmeɪtiz/ | n. |伙计们，朋友们（主要用于海盗或航海语境）| An informal, often humorous term of address for a group of friends or companions, typically used in a nautical context.  Derived from "mate". | Ahoy, mateys! Let's set sail!  喂，伙计们！我们起航吧！|Synonyms: friends, buddies, pals, comrades | Antonyms: enemies, foes | Noun
