guising: /ˈɡaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 伪装；乔装打扮；(尤指儿童在万圣节)讨要糖果 | The practice of dressing up in disguise, typically for fun or to solicit treats, especially at Halloween. |  Children went guising around the neighborhood on Halloween. 孩子们在万圣节那天乔装打扮到附近讨要糖果。 | 近义词: masquerading, costuming, dressing up | 反义词: revealing, exposing | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the Scots word "guise" meaning "manner, fashion, appearance," ultimately from Old French "guise."
