cutaneously:/kjuːˈteɪniəsli/
adjective
皮肤地；经皮地
Relating to, affecting, or applied to the skin.
The drug is administered cutaneously.  这种药物是通过皮肤给药的。
The infection manifested cutaneously with a rash.  感染以皮疹的形式出现在皮肤上。
synonym:dermally, percutaneously
antonym:systemically, orally
词性:adjective
