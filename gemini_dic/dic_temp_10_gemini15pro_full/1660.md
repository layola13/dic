acknowledges:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒɪz/
verb
承认；确认；答谢
To accept or admit the truth or existence of something.  To express gratitude or appreciation for something.
The company acknowledges the receipt of the complaint. = 公司确认收到投诉。

She acknowledged his presence with a nod. = 她点点头，表示注意到他的存在。

近义词:admit, concede, confirm, recognize, accept
反义词:deny, dispute, reject
词性:v.
