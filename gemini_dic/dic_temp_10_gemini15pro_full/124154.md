mortising:/ˈmɔːrtɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 榫眼加工，开榫 | The process of creating a mortise (a hole or recess) in a piece of wood or other material, typically to receive a tenon (a projecting member) and form a joint. | He spent the afternoon mortising the legs for the table. 他一下午都在为桌子腿开榫眼。 |  近义词: grooving, slotting | 反义词:  None (no direct antonym) | 词性: noun

mortise: /ˈmɔːrtɪs/ | n. vt. | 榫眼；开榫眼 |  A hole or recess cut into a piece of timber, into which a tenon is inserted to form a joint.  |  He cut a precise mortise in the wood. 他在木头上切了一个精确的榫眼。 |  近义词: slot, groove, recess | 反义词: tenon | 词性: noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mortaise, from mort "dead" (from Latin mortuus "dead") + -aise, a suffix of unknown origin. The connection with "dead" is unclear, possibly referring to the firm grip of the joint.
