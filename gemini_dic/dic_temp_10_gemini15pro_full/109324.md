lengthens:/ˈleŋθənz/
verb
加长，延长，变长
Makes or becomes longer.
The days lengthen in spring. 春天白天变长。
近义词:extends, prolongs, stretches
反义词:shortens, contracts, reduces
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lengthnen*, from Old English *len(c)þnian*, from *lang* (“long”). Cognate with Dutch *lengen*, German *längern*.
