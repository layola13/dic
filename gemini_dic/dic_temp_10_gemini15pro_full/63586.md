envisagements:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒmənts/ | n. | 设想，展望，预想 |  The act of envisaging; contemplation or conception of something as a possibility or a desirable future event.  | The government's envisagements for the future of the economy are optimistic. 政府对未来经济的展望是乐观的。|  The architect presented several envisagements for the new building. 建筑师提出了新建筑的几种设想。 | synonyms: visions, conceptions, projections, expectations, forecasts | antonyms:  misconceptions, misunderstandings | noun
