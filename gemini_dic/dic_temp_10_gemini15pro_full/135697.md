nonpreservation:/ˌnɑːnprɛzərˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 未保存，未保护，不保存 | The nonpreservation of these historical documents is a great loss. 这些历史文献没有被保存下来是一个巨大的损失。|  The nonpreservation of the rainforest is leading to the extinction of many species.  对雨林的未保护正导致许多物种的灭绝。 | 近义词: neglect, lack of preservation, failure to preserve | 反义词: preservation, conservation, protection | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + preservation (from preserve, from Latin praeservare "to guard beforehand, keep safe")
