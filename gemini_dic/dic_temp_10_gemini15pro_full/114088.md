magnificent:/mæɡˈnɪfɪsnt/
adjective
壮丽的，宏伟的，极好的
Extraordinarily beautiful, impressive, or admirable.
The palace is a magnificent building. 这座宫殿是一栋宏伟的建筑。
The view from the top of the mountain was magnificent. 从山顶望去的景色壮丽无比。
近义词：grand, splendid, glorious, superb, majestic, impressive, awe-inspiring
反义词：unimpressive, ordinary, mediocre, paltry
词性: adjective
