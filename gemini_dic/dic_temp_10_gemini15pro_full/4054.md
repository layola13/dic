aiding:/ˈeɪdɪŋ/
verb
援助; 帮助
present participle of aid
例句：The organization is aiding refugees.  这个组织正在援助难民。

例句：They were aiding in the search for the missing child. 他们正在协助寻找失踪的孩子。

近义词：assisting, supporting, helping
反义词：hindering, obstructing, impeding
词性: verb (present participle)


aid:/eɪd/
verb
援助; 帮助
to help someone or something
例句：The government aided the flood victims. 政府援助了洪灾灾民。

例句：The new software aids in the design process.  这个新软件有助于设计过程。
近义词：assist, support, help
反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede
词性: verb
