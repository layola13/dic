exertions:/ɪɡˈzɜːrʃənz/| n. |努力，尽力， exertion的复数形式|Vigorous physical exertions.  剧烈的体力劳动。|The team's exertions were rewarded with a victory. 球队的努力获得了胜利。|近义词：efforts, endeavors, struggles, attempts|反义词:inaction, idleness, passivity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *exercion, from Old French exertion, from Late Latin exertio (“exercise, effort”), from Latin exertus (“thrust forth, driven out”), past participle of exserō (“I thrust out, I put forth”).  Ultimately from ex (“out”) + serō (“I join, I connect”).
