name-dropped:/ˈneɪmˌdrɑːpt/
verb
提及（名人）以提高身价
To mention (a famous person) casually in conversation to impress others.
He name-dropped several celebrities during the interview, trying to make himself seem important.  他在面试中提到了几个名人的名字，试图让自己显得重要。
近义词:boasted, bragged
反义词:humble, modest
词性:v.
