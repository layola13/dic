half-dazed:/ˌhæfˈdeɪzd/
adjective
半昏迷的；神志不清的
in a state of drowsiness or confusion; partially stunned
He stumbled out of the wreckage, half-dazed and bleeding. 他跌跌撞撞地从残骸中走出来，半昏迷着，身上流着血。
Synonyms: bewildered, confused, dazed, disoriented, foggy, groggy, muzzy
Antonyms: alert, awake, clearheaded, conscious, focused, lucid, oriented
词性: 形容词
