low-rent:/ˌloʊˈrɛnt/
adjective
低租金的；廉价的；低端的
of, relating to, or inhabiting housing with low rents; also : of low quality or status.  Characterized by low cost and often poor quality.
例句：
They live in a low-rent district.
他们住在低租金区。

He prefers low-rent motels.
他喜欢住廉价的汽车旅馆。

近义词：cheap, inexpensive, downscale, shabby, seedy
反义词：expensive, upscale, posh, luxurious
词性: adjective


low-rent:/ˌloʊˈrɛnt/
noun
低租金的房子或地区
housing with low rents or a district with such housing.
例句：
The area is known for its low-rent apartments.
该地区以其低租金公寓而闻名。

Many low-income families are forced to live in low-rent.
许多低收入家庭被迫住在低租金的房子里。

近义词：cheap housing, affordable housing
反义词：luxury housing, expensive housing
词性: noun
