anti-sovietisms: /ˌæntiˈsoʊviətɪzəmz/ | n. | 反苏言论；反苏情绪 |  Expressions of opposition or hostility towards the Soviet Union, its government, or its policies. |  His speech was full of anti-sovietisms, criticizing their human rights record and economic policies. 他的演讲充满了反苏言论，批评他们的人权记录和经济政策。|  Anti-Soviet propaganda fueled anti-sovietisms among the population. 反苏宣传加剧了民众的反苏情绪。 | Synonyms: anti-communism (broader), anti-Stalinism (more specific) | Antonyms: pro-Sovietism, Sovietophilia | noun
