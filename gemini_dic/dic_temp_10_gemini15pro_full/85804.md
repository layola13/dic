hazardless:/ˈhæzərdləs/| adj.|无危险的，安全的|Free from hazard; safe.|例句：The path was hazardless and easy to follow.  这条小路很安全，很容易走。|例句：They ensured a hazardless working environment for all employees. 他们确保所有员工都有一个安全的工作环境。|近义词：safe, secure, risk-free|反义词:dangerous, hazardous, risky|词性:形容词
词源: hazard + -less. Hazard comes from Old French hasard, meaning "game of dice," ultimately of uncertain origin.  -less is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "without" or "free from."
