gosling:/ˈɡɒzlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːzlɪŋ/ (US)| n.|小鹅，幼鹅|A young goose, especially one that has not yet developed its adult plumage.|例句：The goslings followed their mother to the pond. 小鹅们跟着它们的妈妈去了池塘。

例句：The farmer protected the goslings from predators.  农夫保护小鹅免受捕食者的伤害。

近义词：chick, fledgling, young bird|反义词:adult goose, mature goose|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gōsling, diminutive of gōs (“goose”). Cognate with West Frisian goesling, Dutch gosling, German Gänslein, Swedish gåsling.
