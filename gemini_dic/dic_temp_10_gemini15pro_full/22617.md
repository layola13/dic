boozes:/ˈbuːziz/| n.|酒；豪饮| Alcoholic drinks; an act of drinking alcohol to excess. |He spent the weekend boozing with his friends. 他周末和朋友们一起豪饮。|近义词：drinks, alcohol, liquor, intoxicants|反义词: sobriety, abstinence|名词

booze:/buːz/| v.|豪饮；痛饮|To drink alcohol, especially in large quantities.|He boozed all night. 他整晚都在喝酒。|近义词：drink, imbibe, guzzle, carouse|反义词: abstain|动词

词源：booze的词源可以追溯到中古荷兰语的būsen，意思是“喝大量”。
