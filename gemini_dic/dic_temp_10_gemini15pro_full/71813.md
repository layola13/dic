fleeciness:/ˈfliːsinəs/| n.|柔软，蓬松|The fleeciness of the lamb's wool was delightful to touch. 羊羔毛的柔软令人愉悦。|例句：The blanket's fleeciness made it perfect for a cold night. 这条毯子的蓬松度使其成为寒冷夜晚的完美选择。

The fleeciness of the clouds made them look like cotton candy. 云的蓬松使它们看起来像棉花糖。


|近义词：softness, fluffiness, downiness|反义词:coarseness, roughness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From fleece + -iness.  "Fleece" comes from Old English *flys, from Proto-Germanic *fleusą (“fleece”).
