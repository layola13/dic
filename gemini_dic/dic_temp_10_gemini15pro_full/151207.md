pardners:/ˈpɑːrdnərz/ | n. |伙伴，合伙人| An informal, often humorous or affectionate, term for "partners."  It evokes a sense of camaraderie and shared experience, often in a Western or rural context. |  "Well, pardners, let's ride!"  好吧，伙计们，我们上马吧！|  "The two pardners had been prospecting for gold for years." 这两个伙伴已经一起探矿多年了。 | synonyms: partners, companions, friends, associates, colleagues | antonyms: enemies, adversaries, opponents | noun
