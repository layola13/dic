acclimatizes:/əˈklaɪməˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
适应环境，适应气候
To become accustomed to a new climate or environment.
It took him a while to acclimatize to the altitude. = 他花了一段时间才适应了高海拔。

The climbers spent a few days acclimatizing themselves to the thin air. = 登山者花了几天时间来适应稀薄的空气。

近义词:adapt, adjust, accustom
反义词:unaccustomed

词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French acclimater, from à (to) + climat (climate). Ultimately from Greek klima (slope, zone).


USAGE->

She acclimatizes quickly to new surroundings. = 她很快就能适应新环境。
He acclimatizes easily to different cultures. = 他很容易适应不同的文化。
