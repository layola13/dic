nevermind:/ˌnɛvərˈmaɪnd/| interj. |没关系；不要紧；别放在心上|Used to tell someone not to worry about something because it is not important or you will do it yourself. |"I was going to ask you to help me move this weekend, but nevermind, I'll ask my brother." 我本来想让你这周末帮我搬家，不过算了，我还是让我弟弟帮忙吧。| 近义词:it's okay, it doesn't matter, don't worry about it | 反义词:it matters, it's important |词性: interjection
