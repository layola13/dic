ETYMOLOGY->
This phrase originates from the literal idea of traveling in the middle of the road, which is generally considered the safest place to avoid collisions.  This concept was then figuratively applied to political and social views, implying a position that avoids extremes.  It became common in American English in the mid-20th century.
