flaker:/ˈfleɪkər/ | n. | 薄片，小碎片；不靠谱的人，爱放鸽子的人 |A person who is unreliable, especially one who habitually cancels plans at the last minute. Someone or something that flakes off small pieces. | The paint was old and beginning to flaker.  油漆老化了，开始剥落。 He's such a flaker; he always cancels plans at the last minute. 他真是个不靠谱的人，总是最后一刻取消计划。 |Synonyms: flake, dropout, no-show | Antonyms: reliable person, stand-up guy | Noun
