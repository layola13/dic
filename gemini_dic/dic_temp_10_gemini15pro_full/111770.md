lodgings:/ˈlɒdʒɪŋz/
n.
住所，寄宿处
A place to live temporarily, especially a rented room or rooms in someone's house.
We found cheap lodgings in a nearby town. 我们在附近的一个镇上找到了便宜的住处。
accommodations, quarters, rooms
residence, home
noun
