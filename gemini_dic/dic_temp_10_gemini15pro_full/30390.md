centiliter:/ˈsɛntɪˌliːtər/ (US), /ˈsɛntɪˌliːtə/ (UK)| n. | 厘升；百分之一升 | A unit of volume equal to one hundredth of a liter. |  The recipe calls for 10 centiliters of milk.  食谱需要10厘升牛奶。

The doctor prescribed a cough syrup with a dosage of 5 centiliters. 医生开了5厘升的止咳糖浆。 | 近义词: cc (cubic centimeter), ml (milliliter) | 反义词: liter, kiloliter | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French centilitre, from centi- (hundredth) + litre (liter).  Ultimately from Latin centum (hundred) and Greek litra (a unit of weight).
