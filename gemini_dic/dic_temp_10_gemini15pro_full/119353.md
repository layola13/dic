microclastic:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈklæstɪk/
adjective
微碎屑的；由微小碎屑组成的
Composed of minute fragments of rock, derived especially from the mechanical weathering of older rocks.
The geologist examined the microclastic sediments under the microscope.  地质学家在显微镜下检查了微碎屑沉积物。
The microclastic limestone contained numerous fossils. 微碎屑灰岩包含大量的化石。
近义词:fine-grained, detrital
反义词:crystalline
词性:adjective
