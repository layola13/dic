airways:/ˈɛə(r)ˌweɪz/ /ˈɛrˌweɪz/| n. |航线；呼吸道|The system of routes that airlines use for flights, or the passages in the lungs through which air passes during breathing. |例句：The airways are congested during the holiday season. 假期期间航线拥堵。

例句：His airways were constricted due to an allergic reaction. 由于过敏反应，他的呼吸道收缩了。|近义词：routes, passages, respiratory tract|反义词: None| noun
