disbecome:/dɪsbɪˈkʌm/
v.
变得不相称；不再适合；使失态
To cease to befit; to be unbecoming to; to misbecome.
Her anger disbecomes her. 她的愤怒使她失态。
近义词:misbecome, unsuit, unbefit
反义词:become, suit, befit
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + become.  "Dis-" is a prefix indicating negation or reversal. "Become" comes from Old English "becuman," meaning "to arrive, happen, come to be."
