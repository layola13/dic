interminate:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnət/ /ɪnˈtɝːmɪnət/| adj.|无休止的，无限的|Having or seeming to have no end; endless.|The lecture seemed interminable. 这讲座似乎没完没了。|近义词：endless, ceaseless, incessant, perpetual|反义词：finite, limited, temporary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interminatus, past participle of interminare (“to be boundless, have no limits”), from in- (“not”) + terminatus, past participle of terminare (“to limit, bound”), from terminus (“boundary, limit”).
