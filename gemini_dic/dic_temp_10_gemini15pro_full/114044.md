magnetical:/mæɡˈnɛtɪkəl/
adjective
磁的；有磁性的
Relating to or exhibiting magnetism; having the properties of a magnet.
The Earth has a magnetical field. 地球有一个磁场。
The magnetical force pulled the iron filings towards the magnet. 磁力将铁屑吸向磁铁。
近义词:magnetic, attracting
反义词:nonmagnetic, repelling
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin magneticalis, from Latin magneticus (see magnetic).
