duriel:/ˈdjʊəriəl/ | adj. | 硬脑膜的; | Relating to the dura mater, the tough outermost membrane enveloping the brain and spinal cord. |  Example: A subdural hematoma is a collection of blood below the dura mater. 硬膜下血肿是硬脑膜下方积血。
