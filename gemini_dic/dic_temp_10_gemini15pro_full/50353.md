depolishing:/diːˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/
verb
抛光; 去光; 使失去光泽
The act of removing the polish or shine from a surface.
The depolishing of the silver left it looking dull.  把银器上的光泽去掉后，它看起来很暗淡。
dulling, tarnishing
polishing, shining, burnishing
verb
