marlitic: /mɑːrˈlɪtɪk/
marlitic| adj.|泥灰质的，泥灰岩的|Relating to or resembling marl; consisting of or containing marl.  Formed from, or containing, clay and calcium carbonate.|The marlitic soil was ideal for growing grapes.|近义词：calcareous, clayey|反义词：sandy, siliceous|词性: 形容词
