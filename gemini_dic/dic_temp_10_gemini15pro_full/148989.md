ovolo:/ˈoʊvəloʊ/ (US), /əʊˈvəʊləʊ/ (UK)| n.| 卵饰，蛋形线脚，（尤指古典建筑中的）凸圆饰 |A convex molding, especially in classical architecture, having a profile in the form of a quarter of a circle or of an ellipse.| The architect incorporated ovolo moldings into the window frames. 建筑师在窗框中加入了卵饰。|近义词: quarter-round, echinus, torus |反义词: cavetto, scotia |noun
