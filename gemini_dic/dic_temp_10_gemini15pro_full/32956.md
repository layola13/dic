choral:/ˈkɔːrəl/ (US), /ˈkɒrəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|合唱的，合唱队|Relating to or sung by a choir or chorus; a choral composition.| The choral music filled the cathedral.  合唱音乐充满了大教堂。| The school has a renowned choral group.  这所学校有一个著名的合唱团。 |近义词：choric|反义词:solo, individual|词性:形容词，名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *choralis*, from Latin chorus (choir), from Greek khoros (chorus, dance, dancing-place).
