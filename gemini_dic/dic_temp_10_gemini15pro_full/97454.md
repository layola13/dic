infusionism:/ɪnˈfjuːʒənɪzəm/| n. |文化传播论，文化渗透论|The theory that cultural traits can spread from one society to another through contact and borrowing, rather than independent invention.  It posits that significant cultural features were invented in one or a few centers and then diffused to other cultures.|Anthropologists debated the merits of infusionism versus independent invention.  人类学家们就文化传播论与独立发明论的优劣进行了辩论。|Synonyms: diffusionism|Antonyms: independent invention|Noun
