fireballs:/ˈfaɪərbɔːlz/| n. |火球；流星|A bright meteor or a ball of fire. A ball of fire propelled by an explosion or other means.|例句：The wizard conjured a fireball and hurled it at the dragon.  巫师变出一个火球，朝龙扔去。

例句：We saw a fireball streak across the night sky. 我们看到一个火球划过夜空。|近义词：meteor, bolide,  conflagration|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "ember" as a weak opposite)|词性:noun
