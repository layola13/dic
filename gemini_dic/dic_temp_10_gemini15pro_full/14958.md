b-movie:/ˈbiː ˈmuːvi/| n.|B级片，低成本电影|A low-budget, often genre film, originally shown as the second feature in a double bill.|例句：We watched a b-movie about giant spiders. 我们看了一部关于巨型蜘蛛的B级片。|例句：B-movies are often characterized by their cheesy special effects. B级片的特点通常是廉价的特效。|近义词：exploitation film, low-budget film, genre film|反义词: A-movie, blockbuster|词性: noun
