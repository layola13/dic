foodless:/ˈfuːdləs/
adjective
无食物的；缺乏食物的
Without food; lacking food.
The refugees were facing a foodless winter.  难民们面临着一个没有食物的冬天。
The shipwrecked sailors endured foodless days on a raft.  失事的船员们在木筏上忍受了几天没有食物的日子。
近义词:hungry, starving, famished
反义词:well-fed, nourished
