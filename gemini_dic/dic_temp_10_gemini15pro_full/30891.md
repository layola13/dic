chaffless:/ˈtʃæflɪs/
adj.
无谷壳的，无糠的
Free from chaff; without chaff.
The combine harvester efficiently separates the chaffless grains from the stalks.联合收割机有效地将无壳谷物与茎秆分离。
近义词:seedless, huskless
反义词:chaffy
词性:形容词
