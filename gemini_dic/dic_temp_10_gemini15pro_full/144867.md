over-rate:/ˌəʊvəˈreɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd/ (US)| adj. |被高估的|Rated or valued too highly.|The movie was critically over-rated. 这部电影被评论家高估了。|overvalued, overrated|underrated, undervalued|adjective
