non-fluency:/ˌnɒnˈfluːənsi/ /ˌnɑːnˈfluːənsi/
noun
不流利，不流畅
Lack of fluency or smoothness, especially in speech.  The inability to produce speech easily and smoothly.
His non-fluency in English made it difficult for him to communicate. 他英语不流利，难以沟通。
halting speech, disfluency, stammering
fluency, eloquence
n.
