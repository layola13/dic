circumscribers:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪbərz/ | n. |限定者，限制者 |  Those who draw a line around something, either literally or figuratively, thus defining its limits. | The circumscribers of the new law carefully defined its scope to avoid loopholes.  新的法律的制定者仔细界定了其范围，以避免漏洞。|近义词： delimiters, restrictors | 反义词： expanders, enlargers | noun

circumscribe:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪb/ | v. |限定，限制 | To draw a line around; to encircle; to restrict something within limits. |  His authority is circumscribed by the company's rules. 他的权力受到公司规章的限制。 | 近义词： restrict, confine, limit | 反义词： expand, broaden, enlarge | verb
