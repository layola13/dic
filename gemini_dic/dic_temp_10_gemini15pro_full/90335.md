houss:/haʊs/ | n. | house | A building for human habitation, especially one that consists of a ground floor and one or more upper storeys. |  I grew up in a two-story house. 我在一个两层楼的房子里长大。| synonyms: home, residence, dwelling, abode | antonyms: homelessness, outdoors | noun

Etymology: From Middle English hous, from Old English hūs, from Proto-Germanic *hūsan, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱews- (“to cover, conceal”). Cognate with West Frisian hûs (“house”), Dutch huis (“house”), German Haus (“house”).
