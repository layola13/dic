ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not; opposite of.  From Latin *non*.
feeble (adjective): lacking physical strength, especially as a result of age or illness. From Old French *feble*, ultimately from Latin *flebilis* "lamentable."
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
