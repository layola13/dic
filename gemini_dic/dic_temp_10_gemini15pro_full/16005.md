ban:/bæn/
noun|禁令，禁止|An official or legal prohibition.|The government placed a ban on smoking in public places. 政府禁止在公共场所吸烟。|prohibition, embargo, restriction|allowance, permission|n.
ban:/bæn/
verb|禁止，取缔|To prohibit or forbid, especially officially.|The city banned smoking in restaurants. 该市禁止在餐馆吸烟。|prohibit, forbid, outlaw|permit, allow|v.
