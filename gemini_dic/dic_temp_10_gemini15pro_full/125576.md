multitrack: /ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ | adj. n. |多轨的，多声道的；多轨录音|Recording on separate tracks allows engineers to adjust levels and add effects to each instrument or vocal individually. This is called multitrack recording. 在单独的轨道上录音可以让工程师分别调整每个乐器或人声的音量并添加效果。这被称为多轨录音。|例句：The song was recorded using multitrack technology. 这首歌是用多轨技术录制的。

例句：He used a multitrack recorder to create the demo. 他用多轨录音机制作了样本唱片。 |近义词：multichannel |反义词: monotrack, single-track |词性：形容词，名词

multitrack: /ˈmʌltiˌtræk/ | v. |多轨录音|To record (music, sound, etc.) on separate tracks of a recording medium. 在录音介质的不同轨道上录制（音乐、声音等）。|例句：They multitracked the album in a small studio. 他们在一个小工作室里多轨录制了这张专辑。


例句：The band multitracked the vocals and instruments separately. 乐队分别对人声和乐器进行了多轨录音。|近义词：record multichannel |反义词: record single-track |词性：动词


词源: multi- (many) + track (a path or course)  “multi-”（多的） + “track”（轨道）
