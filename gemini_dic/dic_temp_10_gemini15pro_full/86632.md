hell:/hɛl/| n.|地狱；阴间；苦境；极大的痛苦|A place regarded in various religions as a spiritual realm of evil and suffering, often traditionally depicted as a place of perpetual fire beneath the earth where the wicked are punished after death. Also used to express extreme annoyance or displeasure or to emphasize something.|例句：The traffic was hell this morning. 今早交通真是糟透了。|近义词：inferno, underworld, abyss|反义词:heaven, paradise|名词
