dystrophies:/ˈdɪstrəfiz/| n. |营养不良；肌营养不良|A group of hereditary diseases characterized by progressive weakening and wasting of the muscles.|Muscular dystrophies are a group of genetic diseases. 肌营养不良是一组遗传性疾病。|Many forms of muscular dystrophy exist. 存在多种形式的肌营养不良。|synonyms: degeneration, atrophy, wasting|antonyms: growth, development|noun
