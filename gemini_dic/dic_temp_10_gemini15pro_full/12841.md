associationistic:/əˌsoʊʃiˈeɪʃənɪstɪk/
| adj. |联想主义的；联想派的| Relating to associationism, a theory in psychology or philosophy that regards the simple association or co-occurrence of ideas or sensations as the primary basis of meaning, thought, or learning. |  His theory is firmly rooted in associationistic principles. 他的理论牢固地植根于联想主义原则。|  The philosopher proposed an associationistic model of memory. 这位哲学家提出了一个联想主义的记忆模型。|  connective, related, associative |  dissociative, unrelated | adjective
