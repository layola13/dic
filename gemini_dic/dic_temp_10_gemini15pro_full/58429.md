duchies: /ˈdʌtʃiz/
n.
公国；公爵领地
The territory ruled by a duke or duchess.
The king divided the land into several duchies. 国王将土地划分为几个公国。
近义词:principalities, fiefdoms
反义词:kingdom (意义上相对)
词性:名词


Etymology:
From Old French *duchie, from duc (“duke”) + -ie, from Latin ducatus (“dukedom, leadership”).
