overconsumption:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 过度消费；过量消耗 | The act of consuming something to excess. |  Overconsumption of processed foods can lead to health problems. 过度食用加工食品会导致健康问题。

Overconsumption of resources is a major environmental concern. 资源的过度消耗是一个主要的环境问题。

| 近义词: overspending, overuse, excess consumption | 反义词: underconsumption, moderation, conservation | 词性: noun
