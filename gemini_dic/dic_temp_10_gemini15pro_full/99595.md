interiority:/ɪnˌtɪəriˈɒrəti/ /ɪnˌtɪriˈɔːrəti/ | n. | 内在性；内心世界；主观性 | The quality of being focused on one's inner life and thoughts.  It refers to the subjective experience of one's own thoughts, feelings, and sensations. |  She explored the themes of isolation and interiority in her latest novel. 她在她最新的小说中探讨了孤独和内在性的主题。

|  The artist's work is known for its intense interiority.  这位艺术家的作品以其强烈的内在性而闻名。| 近义词：subjectivity, introspection, inner life, inwardness | 反义词：exteriority, externality | 词性：名词
