hullo:/həˈloʊ/ | int. | 你好，喂 | An informal greeting, similar to "hello".  It's a more old-fashioned or regional variation. | Hullo there, how are you doing? 你好，你怎么样？ | Hi, hello, hey | Goodbye, farewell | Interjection
