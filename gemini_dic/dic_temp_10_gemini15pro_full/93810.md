immethodically:/ˌɪmɪˈθɒdɪkli/ /ˌɪmɪˈθɑːdɪkli/
adjective
无条理地；无系统地
without any order or system; unsystematically
He searched the room immethodically, tossing things aside as he went. 他毫无章法地搜查了房间，一边走一边把东西扔到一边。
The books were stacked immethodically on the shelves. 书架上的书堆放得杂乱无章。
near synonyms: haphazardly, randomly, chaotically, unsystematically
antonyms: methodically, systematically, orderly
词性: 形容词
