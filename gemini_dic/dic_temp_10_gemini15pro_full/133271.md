nonexistentialism:/ˌnɒnɪɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 非存在主义 | The denial or rejection of existentialist philosophy.  It posits that existence does not precede essence, rejecting the idea that individuals create their own meaning and purpose in life. |  He argued against nonexistentialism, claiming that individuals are responsible for shaping their own destinies. 他反对非存在主义，声称个人要对塑造自己的命运负责。|  Near Antonyms: existentialism | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it's a philosophical stance) | Noun
