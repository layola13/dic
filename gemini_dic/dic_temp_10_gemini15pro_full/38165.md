compromised: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪzd/ (US)
|adj.|妥协的；受损的；遭到泄露的|weakened or damaged; exposed to risk or danger|例句：The security of the building was compromised.  这栋楼的安全受到了损害。|近义词：jeopardized, endangered, vulnerable, exposed|反义词：safe, secure, protected|词性:形容词
compromised: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/ (US)
|v.|妥协；让步；危及；损害|to make a deal between different parties where each side gives up something they want; to expose to danger, suspicion, or disrepute|例句：We refused to compromise our principles. 我们拒绝在原则问题上妥协。|近义词：negotiate, concede, endanger, jeopardize|反义词：resist, refuse, protect, defend|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin compromissum, past participle of compromittere “to promise mutually, to submit to arbitration,” from com- “together” + promittere “to promise”
