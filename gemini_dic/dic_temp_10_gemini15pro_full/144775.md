over-emoting: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度表达情感
Expressing emotions in an excessive or exaggerated way.
The actor was criticized for over-emoting in the death scene. 演员在死亡场景中因过度表达情感而受到批评。
He tends to over-emote when he's telling a story. 他讲故事的时候容易过度表达情感。

近义词: overacting, exaggerating, hamming it up
反义词: underplaying, downplaying
词性: verb (present participle)
