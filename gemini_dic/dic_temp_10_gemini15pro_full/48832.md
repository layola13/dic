defoliate:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ /diːˈfəʊliˌeɪt/| vt. |落叶；使落叶；去除叶子|To cause the leaves of (a plant, tree, or forest) to fall off, typically as a result of a chemical agent or disease.|The government decided to defoliate the area to expose enemy hiding places. 政府决定对该地区进行落叶处理，以暴露敌人的藏身之处。|Synonyms: strip, denude, bare|Antonyms:  leaf, grow foliage|Verb

defoliate:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪt/ /diːˈfəʊliˌeɪt/| adj. |落叶的；使落叶的|Causing defoliation.|The defoliate effects of the herbicide were visible within days. 除草剂的落叶效果在几天内就显现出来了。|Synonyms: leafless, bare, denuded|Antonyms: leafy, verdant|Adjective

defoliation:/diːˌfoʊliˈeɪʃən/ /diːˌfəʊliˈeɪʃən/| n. |落叶；脱叶|The shedding of leaves.|Defoliation can be a natural process or caused by external factors. 落叶可以是一个自然过程，也可以是由外部因素引起的。|Synonyms: leaf fall, leaf drop|Antonyms: foliation, leafing|Noun
