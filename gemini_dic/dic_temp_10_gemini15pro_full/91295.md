hydrolytic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|水解的|Relating to, characterized by, or causing hydrolysis (a chemical reaction in which water is used to break down a compound).|The hydrolytic enzyme breaks down complex carbohydrates.|近义词:degradative, catabolic|反义词:synthetic, anabolic|词性:形容词

词源: From "hydro-" (water) + "-lytic" (relating to breakdown), ultimately from Greek "hydōr" (water) and "lysis" (loosening).
