nitrogenous:/naɪˈtrɒdʒɪnəs/
adj.
含氮的；氮的
Containing nitrogen.
例句：Plants absorb nitrogenous compounds from the soil. 植物从土壤中吸收含氮化合物。

例句：Amino acids are nitrogenous organic compounds. 氨基酸是含氮有机化合物。

近义词：nitrogen-containing, nitrogen-bearing
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "non-nitrogenous")
词性: adjective
