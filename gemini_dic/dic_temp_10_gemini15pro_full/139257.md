obligee:/ˌɒblɪˈdʒiː/ /ˌɑːblɪˈdʒiː/
noun
债权人；受惠者
a person to whom someone else is obligated, especially under a legal contract.
The obligee is entitled to payment under the terms of the bond.  债权人有权根据债券条款获得付款。
The bank is the obligee in this loan agreement.  在这份贷款协议中，银行是债权人。
creditor, beneficiary
debtor, obligor
n.
