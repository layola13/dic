cordwain:/ˈkɔːrdweɪn/| n.|科尔多瓦皮革;山羊皮;细软皮革|A fine, soft leather originally made in Cordoba, Spain, from goatskin tanned with alum and other substances. It was historically used for bookbindings and shoes of high quality.|The nobleman's shoes were crafted from supple cordwain.|近义词：leather, goatskin|反义词:|n.
