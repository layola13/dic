daintiest:/ˈdeɪntiɪst/| adj.|最精致的，最美味的，最优雅的|The daintiest cakes were served at the wedding. 婚礼上供应了最精致的蛋糕。|例句：She wore the daintiest little shoes I'd ever seen. 她穿着我见过的最精致的小鞋子。

例句：The restaurant served the daintiest appetizers, each a miniature work of art.  这家餐厅供应最精致的开胃菜，每一道都是一件微型艺术品。|近义词：delicate, exquisite, refined, elegant|反义词：coarse, crude, rough, clumsy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *deyntee, from Old French daintie (“pleasant, delightful, delicate”), from Latin dignitas (“worth, dignity, honor, rank”).  The superlative form, daintiest, developed later.
