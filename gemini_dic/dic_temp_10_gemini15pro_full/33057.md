chowder:/ˈtʃaʊdər/| n. |浓汤，杂烩，周打|A thick soup or stew made with milk or cream, often containing seafood or vegetables.|例句：We had a delicious clam chowder for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了一顿美味的蛤蜊浓汤。

例句：She made a hearty corn chowder for the chilly evening.  她为寒冷的夜晚做了一份丰盛的玉米浓汤。|近义词：soup, stew, bisque|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thin broth")|词性:noun

词源: The word "chowder" likely comes from the French Canadian "chaudière," meaning "cauldron" or "large pot," referring to the pot used to make the stew.  It may also be related to the Latin "caldaria," also meaning a vessel for heating liquids.
