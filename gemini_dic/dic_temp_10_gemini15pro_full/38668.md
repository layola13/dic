condemnable:/kənˈdɛmnəbəl/| adj.|应受谴责的；该罚的；可非难的|Deserving of condemnation; blameworthy.|His actions were utterly condemnable. 他的行为完全应该受到谴责。|近义词：reprehensible, culpable, blameworthy, deplorable|反义词：commendable, laudable, praiseworthy|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French condamnable, from condamner ‘to condemn’.
