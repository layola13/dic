ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hyll, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hulną (“covering, husk”), from Proto-Indo-European *kel- (“to hide, conceal”). Cognate with Dutch hul (“hull”), German Hülle (“cover, covering, sheath”), Icelandic hjallr (“shed, barn”).
