nonreservations:/ˌnɒnˌrɛzərˈveɪʃənz/
noun
无保留; 非保留; 不预订
absence of reservations; lack of restrictions or limitations
They proceeded with nonreservations. 他们毫无保留地继续进行。
近义词:openness, candor, frankness
反义词:reservations, hesitations, doubts
词性:n.
