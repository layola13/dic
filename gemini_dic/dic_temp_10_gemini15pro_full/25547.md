bullyrock:/ˈbʊliˌrɑːk/ |n.|霸陵石，圆砾岩，一种坚硬的砂岩|A type of hard sandstone, also known as conglomerate. It's composed of various sized pebbles and stones cemented together. |The old house was built from bullyrock quarried nearby. 这座老房子是用附近采石场的霸陵石建造的。

The cliffs are made of bullyrock, resistant to erosion. 这些悬崖是由耐侵蚀的霸陵石构成的。|近义词:conglomerate, puddingstone|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


Etymology:
From "bully" (large, excellent) + "rock."  "Bully" in this sense is an archaic adjective meaning "fine" or "excellent," and refers to the stone's durability.
