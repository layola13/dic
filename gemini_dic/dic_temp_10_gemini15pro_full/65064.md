eternalness:/ɪˈtɜːrnlnəs/ |n.|永恒;永久;无穷|The eternalness of love is a common theme in poetry.  爱的永恒是诗歌中的常见主题。|例句：The eternalness of the universe is a concept difficult to grasp. 宇宙的永恒是一个难以理解的概念。

The artist tried to capture the eternalness of the moment in his painting.  这位艺术家试图在他的画中捕捉这一刻的永恒。|近义词：eternity, perpetuity, immortality|反义词：transience, temporality|词性:名词

词源:  From "eternal" + "-ness".  "Eternal" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French *eternel, from Latin aeternus, from aevum "age, eternity".  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being".
