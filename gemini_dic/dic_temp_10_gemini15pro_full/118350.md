merino:/məˈriːnoʊ/| n. |美利奴羊毛，美利奴绵羊|A breed of sheep known for producing very fine wool, or the wool itself.  A soft, fine wool from the Merino sheep.|例句：This sweater is made of 100% merino. 这件毛衣是100%美利奴羊毛制成的。

例句：Merino wool is prized for its softness. 美利奴羊毛因其柔软性而备受珍视。

近义词：wool, fleece|反义词:|词性:noun
