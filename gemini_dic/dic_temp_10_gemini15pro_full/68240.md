fairy-tale:/ˈfeəri teɪl/ (US) /ˈfɛːri teɪl/ (UK)| n. adj.|童话故事；童话般的；不真实的|A children's story about magical and imaginary beings and lands.  Can also be used figuratively to describe something unrealistically perfect.|例句：She used to tell me fairy-tales before bed. 她以前会在睡前给我讲童话故事。|近义词：fantasy, fable, legend, myth|反义词：reality, truth|词性:名词, 形容词

fairy-tale:/ˈfeəri teɪl/ (US) /ˈfɛːri teɪl/ (UK)| adj.|童话般的；不真实的|Resembling a fairy tale in being magical, unrealistic, or wonderful.|例句：Their wedding was a fairy-tale romance. 他们的婚礼就像童话般浪漫。|近义词：magical, enchanting, unreal, fantastic|反义词：realistic, mundane, ordinary|词性:形容词
