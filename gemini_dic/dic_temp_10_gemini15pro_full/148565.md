overtakable:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪkəbəl/| adj.|可以超车的，容易被超越的|Able to be overtaken (especially of another vehicle).|The car in front was driving so slowly that it was easily overtakable. 前面的车开得很慢，很容易超车。|The cyclist was overtakable on the long straight road.  在这条笔直的路上，骑自行车的人很容易被超车。|近义词：passable|反义词：impassable|词性:形容词
词源: overtake (超车) + -able (可...的)
