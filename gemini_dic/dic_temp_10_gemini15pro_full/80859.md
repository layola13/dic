gourmandizing:/ˈɡɔːrməndaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 暴饮暴食；贪吃 | The act of eating greedily or to excess. | He spent the weekend gourmandizing at the all-you-can-eat buffet. 他整个周末都在自助餐馆暴饮暴食。|  overeating, indulging, feasting | dieting, abstaining | noun

gourmand:/ˈɡɔːrmənd/ | n. | 美食家；贪吃的人 | A person who enjoys eating good food and often eats too much. | He's a gourmand who appreciates fine wines and cheeses. 他是一位美食家，对美酒和奶酪很欣赏。| epicure, connoisseur, glutton | ascetic | noun

gourmand:/ˈɡɔːrmɑːnd/ | adj. | 贪吃的；好吃的 |  Fond of good eating; greedy. |  He had a gourmand appetite for rich desserts. 他对甜点有着贪婪的胃口。| gluttonous, voracious | abstemious | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gourmand*, from Old French *gourmant*, ultimately from Frankish *gurmant* ("glutton").
