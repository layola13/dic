outthrobs:/ˌaʊtˈθrɒbz/
verb
强烈跳动，剧烈搏动
throbs outwardly; pulsates intensely.
Her heart outthrobbed with joy when she saw her newborn. 当她看到新生儿时，她的心脏因喜悦而剧烈跳动。
近义词：pulsates, palpitates, throbs
反义词：subsides, weakens
