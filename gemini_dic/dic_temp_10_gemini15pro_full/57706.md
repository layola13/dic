drayage:/ˈdreɪɪdʒ/| n.|短途运输；搬运费|The cost of drayage from the port to the warehouse was significant. 从港口到仓库的短途运输费用很高。|The company offers drayage services within a 50-mile radius. 这家公司在50英里范围内提供短途运输服务。|近义词：cartage, haulage, transportation|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *drayage*, from *drai*, from Old French *trai*, from Late Latin *traha*, from Latin *trahere* "to draw, pull".
