mobilizes:/ˈmoʊbɪˌlaɪzɪz/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪlaɪzɪz/ (UK)| v.|动员，调动|To assemble and make ready for war or other emergencies; to bring resources into use.|The government mobilized the army for the upcoming war. 政府为即将到来的战争动员了军队。|The charity mobilized volunteers to help with the relief effort.  慈善机构动员志愿者来帮助救灾工作。|synonyms: rally, assemble, marshal, activate, deploy|antonyms: demobilize, disband, stand down|verb
