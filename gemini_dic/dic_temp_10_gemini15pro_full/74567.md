fraughtage: /ˈfrɔːtɪdʒ/ | n. | 货运; 运费; 货物 | The cost of transporting goods; freight. | The fraughtage of the goods was more than the value of the goods themselves. 货物的运费比货物本身的价值还高。| The ship's fraughtage arrived safely at the port. 船上的货物安全抵达了港口。 | 近义词: freight, cargo, shipment | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *fraght, from Middle Dutch/Middle Low German *vracht, from Proto-Germanic *fraxtiz (“fruit, reward, profit”). Cognate with Scots fraucht, Old Frisian frecht (“fruit, freight”), Middle High German vrehte (“freight, cargo, produce”), Old English fréht (“fruit, gain, produce”).
