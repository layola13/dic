nongenetic:/ˌnɒn.dʒəˈnɛt.ɪk/ | adj. |非遗传的，非基因的|Not relating to genes or heredity; acquired rather than inherited. |例句：The nongenetic factors, such as environment and lifestyle, also play a crucial role in determining one's health.  非遗传因素，例如环境和生活方式，也在决定一个人的健康方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Nongenetic changes in cells can sometimes lead to cancer. 细胞的非基因改变有时会导致癌症。|近义词：acquired, environmental, external|反义词：genetic, hereditary, inherited|词性：形容词
