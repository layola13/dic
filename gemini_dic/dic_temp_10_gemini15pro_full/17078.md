batista:/bɑːˈtiːstə/ /bəˈtiːstə/| n.|咖啡师，咖啡调配师|A person who makes and serves coffee.|例句：The batista crafted a beautiful latte with intricate foam art. 咖啡师制作了一杯带有精美拉花艺术的漂亮拿铁。

例句：He trained as a batista for several months before getting the job at the popular cafe.  他在那家热门咖啡馆找到工作之前，接受了几个月的咖啡师培训。|近义词：barista (Note: This is often considered the correct spelling), coffee maker|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *barista*, meaning "bartender," ultimately from Latin *barra*, meaning "bar."  The term shifted in meaning to refer specifically to a person who makes coffee.
