nonprofessorially:/ˌnɑːnˌprɑːfəˈsɔːriəli/
adverb
非专业地；不像教授那样地
Not in a professional or professorial manner; lacking the characteristics or expertise of a professor.
He spoke about the topic nonprofessorially, making it easy for everyone to understand. 他以非专业的方式谈论这个话题，使每个人都能轻松理解。
Synonym: amateurishly, unprofessionally, informally
Antonym: professionally, expertly, academically
词性: 副词
