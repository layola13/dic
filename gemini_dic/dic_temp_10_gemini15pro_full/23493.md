brahman:/ˈbrɑːmən/
noun
瘤牛
A breed of cattle originating in India, often used for crossbreeding.
The brahman is known for its heat tolerance. 瘤牛以耐热性而闻名。
近义词:zebu, bull
反义词:cow (female)
词性:n.
