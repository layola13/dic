arrestments:/əˈrɛstmənts/
n.
扣押，扣留（苏格兰法律术语）
The legal seizure of someone's property or money, especially to pay a debt (Scottish Law).
The court ordered arrestments on the debtor's bank account. 法院下令扣押债务人的银行账户。
近义词：seizure, attachment, garnishment
反义词：release
词性：名词


arrestment:/əˈrɛstmənt/
n.
停止，阻止
The act of stopping or delaying something. (Less common usage)
The sudden arrestment of his breathing alarmed everyone. 他突然停止呼吸让所有人都惊慌失措。
近义词：cessation, halt, stopping
反义词：continuation, commencement
词性：名词
