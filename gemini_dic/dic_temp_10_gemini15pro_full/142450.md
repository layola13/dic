oscillograph:/əˈsɪləɡræf/ /ˈɑːsələɡræf/
noun|示波器；示波图|An instrument that displays electrical signals as waveforms on a screen.|The oscillograph showed a sudden spike in voltage. 示波器显示电压突然飙升。|synonyms: oscilloscope, cathode-ray oscilloscope|antonyms: N/A
noun|示波图；波形图|A visual record of the oscillations of an electrical current or other varying quantity.|The oscillograph revealed the complex nature of the sound wave. 示波图揭示了声波的复杂性质。|synonyms: waveform, graph, trace|antonyms: N/A
