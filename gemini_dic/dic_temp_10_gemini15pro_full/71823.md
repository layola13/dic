fleet:/fliːt/
n.|舰队，车队，机群|A group of ships, vehicles, or aircraft operating together or under the same ownership.|The company has a large fleet of delivery trucks.  这家公司拥有一支庞大的送货卡车车队。|synonyms: squadron, armada, flotilla|antonyms: single, individual|noun
fleet:/fliːt/
adj.|快速的，敏捷的；转瞬即逝的|Moving swiftly; rapid.|A fleet-footed runner.  一位脚步飞快的跑步者。|synonyms: swift, rapid, quick, nimble|antonyms: slow, sluggish|adjective
