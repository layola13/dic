ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *parochialis, from Latin parochia ("parish"), from Ancient Greek παροικία (paroikía, “sojourning abroad, dwelling beside, a district”).  最终源自古希腊语πάροικος (pároikos, “dwelling near”), from παρά (pará, “beside”) + οἶκος (oîkos, “house”).
