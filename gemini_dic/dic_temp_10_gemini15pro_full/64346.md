erasement:/ɪˈreɪsmənt/ | n. |擦除，消除，抹去|The act of erasing or the state of being erased. The erasure of the data was accidental. |例句：The erasing of the hard drive was necessary for security reasons.  为了安全起见，必须擦除硬盘。

例句：The erasurement of her name from the list was a deliberate act. 把她的名字从名单上抹去是一个故意的行为。|近义词：deletion, obliteration, removal, effacement|反义词：preservation, restoration, creation|词性: 名词
