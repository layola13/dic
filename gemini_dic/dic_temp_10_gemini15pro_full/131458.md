noncognitive:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːɡnɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非认知的 | Relating to abilities that are not directly related to intellectual capacity, such as personality traits, social skills, and emotional intelligence. |  Research suggests that noncognitive skills are important for success in life. 研究表明，非认知技能对人生成功很重要。|  Noncognitive factors like motivation and perseverance can influence academic performance.  像动力和毅力这样的非认知因素会影响学业成绩。 | 近义词: non-intellectual, affective, interpersonal | 反义词: cognitive, intellectual | 词性: adjective
