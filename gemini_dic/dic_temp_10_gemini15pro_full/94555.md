imposture:/ɪmˈpɒstʃə(r)/ /ɪmˈpɑːstʃər/ | n. | 欺骗；冒名顶替；骗局 | The act of deceiving someone by pretending to be someone else. | He maintained his imposture for a remarkable length of time. 他竟然长期维持着他的骗局，真是出奇。 | Synonyms: deception, fraud, sham, pretense, charade | Antonyms: honesty, truthfulness, genuineness | noun

impostured:/ɪmˈpɒstʃəd/ /ɪmˈpɑːstʃərd/ | adj. |  假冒的；欺骗的 |  Deceived by an imposter; having one's identity stolen. |  The impostured heiress was eventually exposed.  那位假冒的女继承人最终被揭穿了。| Synonyms: fake, counterfeit, fraudulent, sham, bogus | Antonyms: genuine, authentic, real, true | adjective
