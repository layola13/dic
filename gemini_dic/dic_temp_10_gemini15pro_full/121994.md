misspell:/ˌmɪsˈspɛl/
verb
拼写错误
to spell a word incorrectly
例句：I often misspell "necessary". 我经常把“necessary”拼错。
例句：He misspelled my name on the invitation. 他在邀请函上拼错了我的名字。
近义词：spell incorrectly, miswrite
反义词：spell correctly
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + spell.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrongly" or "badly." "Spell" comes from Old English "spell," meaning "story, tale, speech, charm." The meaning shifted to "to name the letters of a word" in Middle English.
