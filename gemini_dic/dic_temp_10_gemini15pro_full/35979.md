codependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/ | n. | 共同依赖，相互依赖 | A relationship where one person supports or enables another person's addiction, poor mental health, or dysfunction. |  She was in a codependent relationship with her alcoholic husband. 她和她酗酒的丈夫处于一种相互依赖的关系。

| Example: His codependency on his mother prevented him from developing healthy adult relationships. 他对母亲的依赖阻碍了他发展健康的成年人关系。 |  Near Synonyms: interdependence, enabling, attachment, reliance | Antonyms: independence, autonomy, self-reliance | Noun
