acceptable:/əkˈseptəbl/
adj.|可接受的，合格的|Something that is acceptable is satisfactory or good enough to be agreed upon or approved of.|Her work is acceptable, but not outstanding. 她的工作可以接受，但不出色。|近义词：tolerable, satisfactory, adequate|反义词：unacceptable, intolerable, inadequate|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin acceptabilis, from acceptare ‘accept’.
