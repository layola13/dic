overformalize:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrməlaɪz/ | v. | 过分拘泥形式，过分程式化 | To make something excessively formal, often to the point of hindering effectiveness or naturalness. | The company's overformalized procedures stifled creativity.  这家公司过分拘泥形式的程序扼杀了创造力。

The welcome ceremony was overformalized and felt stiff. 欢迎仪式过于正式，让人感觉拘谨。 | 近义词: ritualize, overregulate | 反义词: simplify, streamline | 词性: 动词
