deplorer:/dɪˈplɔːrər/| n. |悲叹者；哀悼者；痛惜者|One who deplores; one who laments or regrets something deeply. |例句：He was a deplorer of the current state of affairs.  他痛惜目前的局势。

例句：She was a deplorer of lost opportunities. 她对错失的机会感到惋惜。|近义词：mourner, lamenter, griever|反义词: celebrant, rejoicer | noun

deploring:/dɪˈplɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. |令人痛惜的； deplorable | Expressing or showing deep regret or disapproval. | 例句：The deploring conditions in the refugee camp were a cause for international concern.  难民营令人痛惜的状况引起了国际社会的关注。

例句：She gave a deploring sigh at the news.  听到这个消息，她深深地叹了口气。| 近义词： lamentable, regrettable, deplorable | 反义词： commendable, admirable | adjective
