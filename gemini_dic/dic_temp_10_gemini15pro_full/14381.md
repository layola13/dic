autor:/ˈɔːθər/ (UK) /ˈɑːθər/ (US)| n.|作者，作家|A person who writes books or articles, especially as their job.|例句：The author of the book is unknown. 这本书的作者不为人知。|例句：She is a successful author of several children's books. 她是一位成功的几本儿童读物作家。|近义词：writer, novelist, composer|反义词：reader, audience|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *autor*, *auctor* (“author, originator, creator, instigator; guarantor, surety”), from Latin auctor (“originator, producer, father, ancestor; founder, leader; authority, adviser; guarantor, surety”), from auctus, past participle of augēre (“to increase, enlarge, augment, enrich; to advance, promote, honor”).
