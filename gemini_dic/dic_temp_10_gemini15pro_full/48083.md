declassifiable:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪəbl/
|adj.|可解密的|able to be declassified (officially stated to no longer be secret)|The documents are now declassifiable. 这些文件现在可以解密了。|近义词:releasable, disclosable|反义词:classified, confidential|词性:形容词
词源:de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + classify + -able (suffix meaning "capable of being")  Ultimately from Latin *classis* (class, division)
