aggravating:/ˈæɡrəˌveɪtɪŋ/| adj.|令人恼火的，加重的|Making a problem or situation worse; annoying.|例句：The aggravating traffic made me late for work. 恼人的交通让我上班迟到了。|例句：His constant complaining was very aggravating. 他不停的抱怨很烦人。|近义词：irritating, annoying, exasperating, infuriating|反义词：soothing, calming, relieving|词性:形容词
aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/| vt.|加重，恶化，使恼火|To make a bad situation worse; to annoy or irritate someone.|例句：The humid weather aggravated my asthma. 潮湿的天气加重了我的哮喘。|例句：His insensitive remarks aggravated the situation. 他不敏感的言论使情况恶化了。|近义词：worsen, exacerbate, irritate, annoy|反义词：improve, alleviate, soothe|词性:动词
