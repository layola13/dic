cookers:/ˈkʊkərz/| n. | 炉灶，炊具 |A cooker is an appliance used for cooking food, typically using gas or electricity.  |例句：She bought a new set of cookers for her kitchen. 她为她的厨房买了一套新的炉灶。

例句：The cookers in this restaurant are all top-of-the-line. 这家餐厅的炉灶都是顶级配置的。|近义词：stoves, ovens, ranges|反义词: refrigerator (in a broad sense, as an appliance that deals with food but in the opposite way)|词性:noun
