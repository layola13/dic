acidiferous:/ˌæsɪˈdɪfərəs/
adjective
产酸的；含酸的
Containing acid; producing acid.
The acidiferous glands of ants produce formic acid. 蚂蚁的产酸腺体产生甲酸。
The chemist analyzed the acidiferous properties of the solution.  化学家分析了溶液的酸性。
近义词:acidic, acid-forming, acid-producing
反义词:alkaline, basic
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin acidus ("sour") + -ferous (from ferre "to bear, carry").

The scientist studied the acidiferous properties of the newly discovered mineral. 科学家研究了新发现矿物的产酸特性。

The acidiferous environment of the stomach helps to break down food. 胃的酸性环境有助于分解食物。
