eviscerator:/ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪtər/| n.|取出内脏的人/器械；剖腹者|A person or instrument that eviscerates; a disemboweler. |The surgeon used a specialized eviscerator to remove the organ.  外科医生用专门的取出内脏的器械取出器官。|Synonyms: disemboweler, gut remover|Antonyms: N/A|noun

eviscerate:/ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪt/| vt.|取出内脏；除去主要部分|1. To remove the entrails of; disembowel. 2. To deprive of vital content or force. |1. The hunter eviscerated the deer before taking it back to camp. 猎人把鹿的内脏取出后才把它带回营地。 2. The editor eviscerated the article, leaving only a few bland paragraphs. 编辑删掉了文章的主要部分，只剩下几段平淡无奇的文字。|Synonyms: disembowel, gut, exenterate, emasculate|Antonyms: fill, stuff, augment|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *evisceratus*, past participle of *eviscerare* "to disembowel," from *ex-* "out" + *viscera* "internal organs."
