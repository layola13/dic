mutinying:/ˈmjuːtɪnaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 叛变，哗变 | Engaging in mutiny; refusing to obey orders and attempting to seize control from authority. | The sailors were mutinying against the cruel captain.  水手们正在反抗残暴的船长。| 近义词: rebelling, revolting, uprising | 反义词: obeying, complying | Verb (present participle)


mutiny:/ˈmjuːtɪni/ | n. | 叛乱，兵变 | An open rebellion against the proper authorities, especially by soldiers or sailors against their officers. | The mutiny was quickly suppressed by the loyal troops.  叛乱很快就被忠诚的部队镇压了。| 近义词: rebellion, revolt, uprising | 反义词: obedience, compliance | Noun
