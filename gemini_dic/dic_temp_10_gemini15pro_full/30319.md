censoriously:/ˌsɛnsɔːriəsli/
adverb
挑剔地，吹毛求疵地
In a manner that expresses disapproval or criticism, often excessively or unfairly.
He looked at her censoriously, clearly disapproving of her outfit. 他挑剔地看着她，显然不赞成她的穿着。
The teacher censoriously marked every single error in the essay. 老师吹毛求疵地在文章中标记了每一个错误。
近义词: critically, judgmentally, disapprovingly, faultfindingly
反义词: approvingly, supportively, uncritically
词性: adverb

censorious:/ˌsɛnsɔːriəs/
adjective
爱挑剔的，吹毛求疵的
Having or showing a tendency to criticize or judge harshly.
She cast a censorious eye over the messy room. 她用挑剔的目光扫视着凌乱的房间。
He had a censorious attitude towards everyone and everything. 他对每个人每件事都持挑剔的态度。
近义词: critical, judgmental, disapproving, faultfinding
反义词: approving, supportive, uncritical
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *cēnsōrius* (“of a censor”), from *cēnsor* (“censor”), from *cēnsēre* (“to assess, estimate, judge”).
