advertising:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪŋ/
n.
广告；广告业；做广告
the activity or profession of producing advertisements for commercial products or services.
The company invested heavily in online advertising.  这家公司在网络广告上投入了巨资。
They launched a new advertising campaign for their latest product.  他们为最新产品发起了一项新的广告宣传活动。
近义词:promotion, publicity, marketing
反义词:n/a
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin advertere.

USAGE->
Noun (as subject):
Advertising is a powerful tool for businesses. = 广告是企业的有力工具。
Advertising can influence consumer behavior. = 广告可以影响消费者行为。

Noun (as object):
The company is increasing its spending on advertising. = 这家公司正在增加广告支出。
They are planning a new advertising campaign. = 他们正在计划一项新的广告活动。

Noun (as part of a prepositional phrase):
She works in advertising. = 她在广告行业工作。
He has a lot of experience in advertising. = 他在广告方面有很多经验。

PHRASE->
advertising agency = 广告公司
advertising campaign = 广告活动
advertising budget = 广告预算
advertising revenue = 广告收入
advertising standards = 广告标准
