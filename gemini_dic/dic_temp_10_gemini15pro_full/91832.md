hypergeometric:/ˌhaɪpərˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 超几何的 | Relating to or designating a hypergeometric series or distribution, which involves combinations and is used in statistics to calculate probabilities in situations where samples are drawn without replacement. | Example: The hypergeometric distribution is used to model the probability of drawing a certain number of red balls from a bag containing both red and blue balls without replacement.  超几何分布用于模拟从一个装有红色和蓝色球的袋子中取出一定数量红色球的概率（无放回抽取）。

Example:  He used a hypergeometric function to solve the problem. 他用超几何函数解决了这个问题。 | Near Synonyms: combinatorial, statistical | Antonyms: N/A | Word Type: Adjective
