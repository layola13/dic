overlard:/ˌoʊvərˈlɑːrd/| vt.|使肥胖|To make excessively fat.|A diet rich in fats and sugars will overlard the body.  富含脂肪和糖的饮食会使身体肥胖。|近义词：fatten|反义词：slim, thin|verb
