glassily:/ˈɡlɑːsɪli/ | adv. | 光滑地；呆滞地；冷漠地 | Smoothly and shinily like glass; in a dull, lifeless, or unfeeling manner. | She stared glassily ahead, lost in thought. 她目光呆滞地望着前方，陷入了沉思。| Near synonyms: blankly, vacantly, dully, lifelessly, impassively | Near antonyms: expressively, animatedly, warmly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From the adjective "glassy" + the adverbial suffix "-ly."  "Glassy" comes from "glass" (Old English "glæs"), referring to the smooth, shiny surface of glass.
