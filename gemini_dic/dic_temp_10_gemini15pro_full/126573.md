naff-off: /næf ˈɒf/ (British English) | phrasal verb | 走开；滚蛋；别烦我 |  A rude way of telling someone to go away.  Used primarily in British English. | Example 1: "Just naff off and leave me alone!"  (滚开，别烦我！)

Example 2: He told the annoying salesman to naff off. (他叫那个烦人的推销员滚蛋。) | 近义词: piss off, bugger off, get lost, sod off (all vulgar), go away | 反义词: come here, stay | 词性: 动词短语
