instructive:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪv/| adj.|有益的，教育性的|Providing or conveying information that is useful or educational.|例句：This documentary is highly instructive about the history of the Roman Empire. 这部纪录片对罗马帝国的历史很有教育意义。|近义词：informative, educational, enlightening, edifying|反义词：uninformative, useless, pointless|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English instructif, from Old French instructif, from Latin instructīvus, from instructus (past participle of instruere ‘build in, furnish, equip, teach’).
