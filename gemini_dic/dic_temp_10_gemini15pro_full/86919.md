hemoglobin:/ˈhiːməˌɡloʊbɪn/ (US), /ˈhiːməˌɡləʊbɪn/ (UK)| n. |血红蛋白|A red protein responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood of vertebrates. |Example 1:  A low hemoglobin level can indicate anemia. 血红蛋白水平低可能表明贫血。

Example 2: Hemoglobin carries oxygen from the lungs to the rest of the body. 血红蛋白将氧气从肺部输送到身体其他部位。|近义词:  (None -  Hemoglobin is a specific protein) |反义词: (None - No direct opposite for a specific protein)|词性:noun
