megachile: /ˈmɛɡəkaɪliː/
noun
切叶蜂
A large, robust bee of the genus *Megachile*, the females of which cut circular pieces from leaves to line their nests.
Megachile bees are important pollinators of many crops. 切叶蜂是许多作物的重要传粉者。
Megachile bees use their sharp mandibles to cut perfect circles from leaves. 切叶蜂用它们锋利的上颚从叶子上切出完美的圆圈。
Synonyms: leafcutter bee, leaf-cutting bee
Antonyms: None (No direct antonym for a specific genus of bee)
