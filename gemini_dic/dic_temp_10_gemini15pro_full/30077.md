cdr:/ˌsiː diː ˈɑːr/ | n. | 光盘刻录文件；CorelDRAW文件；CDR文件 | a file format for vector graphics, used by CorelDRAW software |  I saved the logo design as a CDR file. 我把logo设计保存为CDR文件。 | 近义词:  SVG, AI, EPS | 反义词: None | noun

cdr:/ˌsiː diː ˈɑːr/ |abbr.| compact disc-recordable (可录光盘) | An abbreviation for "compact disc-recordable", referring to a type of optical disc that can be written to once. | I bought a pack of CDRs to back up my data. 我买了一包可录光盘来备份我的数据。 | 近义词: CD-R, recordable disc | 反义词: CD-ROM | abbreviation
