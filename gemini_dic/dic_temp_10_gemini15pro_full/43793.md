cremocarp: /ˈkrɛməˌkɑːrp/ | n. |双悬果，分果爿|A dry fruit that splits into two one-seeded segments at maturity, characteristic of the parsley family (Apiaceae/Umbelliferae).|例句：The fennel plant produces a cremocarp.茴香植物会结出双悬果。

例句：The cremocarp of the carrot plant is small and dry. 胡萝卜植物的双悬果小而干燥。|近义词:schizocarp (裂果), fruit (果实)|反义词: None (没有明确的反义词，因为是指一种特定类型的果实)|词性:noun


Etymology: From Greek *kréma* (something hanging) + *karpós* (fruit).
