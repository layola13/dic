megacity:/ˈmɛɡəˌsɪti/ | n. | 大城市；特大城市 | A very large city, typically one with a population of over ten million people. |  Tokyo is one of the world's largest megacities. 东京是世界上最大的特大城市之一。

Megacities face unique challenges in terms of infrastructure and resource management.  特大城市在基础设施和资源管理方面面临着独特的挑战。

| 近义词: metropolis, megalopolis | 反义词: village, hamlet | 词性: noun
