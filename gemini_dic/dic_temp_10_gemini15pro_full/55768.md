diversifies:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪz/ | v. | 使多样化；多元化 | to make or become more diverse; to expand into different areas or activities  |  The company diversifies its product range to appeal to a wider market.  这家公司将其产品范围多样化，以吸引更广阔的市场。 | synonyms: vary, expand, broaden, branch out | antonyms: concentrate, specialize, focus, narrow | verb
