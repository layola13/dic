dependencies /dɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. | 依赖性，依赖关系 | The project has a lot of dependencies, which makes it difficult to manage.  这个项目有很多依赖项，这使得它难以管理。 | Example: The success of the project has dependencies on several key factors.  这个项目的成功依赖于几个关键因素。 | Synonyms: reliance, dependence, requirement | Antonyms: independence, autonomy | noun

dependency /dɪˈpɛndənsi/ | n. | 依赖，附属国 | The island nation was formerly a dependency of the British Empire.  这个岛国以前是大英帝国的附属国。 | Example:  The small country is a dependency of its larger neighbor. 这个小国家是其较大邻国的附属国。 | Synonyms:  territory, protectorate, colony, satellite | Antonyms:  independent nation, sovereign state | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dependentia, from Latin dependēns (present participle of dependēre “to hang down, hang from, depend on”), from de- “down, from” + pendēre “to hang, be suspended”.
