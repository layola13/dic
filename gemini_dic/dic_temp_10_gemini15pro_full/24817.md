browbeaten:/ˈbraʊˌbiːtn/ | adj. | 胆怯的，畏缩的，被吓倒的 | intimidated by overbearing or domineering behavior; subdued. |  He was a browbeaten husband, afraid to contradict his wife. 他是一个畏缩的丈夫，不敢反驳他的妻子。 | Example: The browbeaten child finally spoke up against his bully.  这个被吓倒的孩子终于勇敢地对抗了他的欺凌者。 | Synonyms: intimidated, cowed, subdued, bullied, henpecked | Antonyms: assertive, confident, bold, defiant | Word type: adjective
