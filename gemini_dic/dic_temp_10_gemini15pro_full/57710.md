dray:/dreɪ/
n.
运货马车；敞篷货车
a low, strong cart or wagon without sides, used for carrying heavy loads
The farmer used a dray to haul hay. 农民用敞篷货车运干草。
近义词：cart, wagon
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly truck for a modern context)
词性：名词


drays:/dreɪz/
n.
运货马车（复数）
plural of dray
The drays were lined up ready to transport the goods.  这些敞篷货车排成一列，准备运送货物。
近义词：carts, wagons
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：名词


词源:
From Old English *dræge, from Proto-Germanic *dragijaną "to draw, pull," from Proto-Indo-European *dheregh- "to draw, pull."
