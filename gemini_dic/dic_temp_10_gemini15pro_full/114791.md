maltreatment:/ˌmælˈtriːtmənt/| n.|虐待，粗暴对待|The child suffered terrible maltreatment at the hands of his caregivers.  这个孩子遭受了其照顾者的可怕虐待。|例句：The investigation revealed years of maltreatment of prisoners. 调查揭露了多年来对囚犯的虐待。

The animal shelter rescued dogs from situations of maltreatment and neglect.  动物收容所从虐待和忽视的环境中救出了狗。|近义词：abuse, mistreatment, ill-treatment, cruelty|反义词:care, kindness, protection|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (bad, ill) + treatment.  来自 mal-（坏的，不好的）+ treatment（对待）。
