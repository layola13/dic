overpump:/ˌoʊvərˈpʌmp/| v.|过度抽取，过量泵送|To pump (something, especially a liquid) excessively or beyond a safe or recommended limit.|They overpumped the well, causing it to dry up. 他们过度抽取井水，导致井干涸了。|近义词：overdraw, exhaust, deplete|反义词: underpump, conserve|词性:动词

词源:  From over- + pump.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Pump" refers to a device used for moving fluids.
