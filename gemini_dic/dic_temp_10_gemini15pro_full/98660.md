insuitable:/ʌnˈsuːtəbl/
adj.
不合适的，不适宜的
not fitting or appropriate; unsuitable accommodation.
例句：The dress was unsuitable for the occasion. 这件衣服不适合这个场合。

例句：The climate here is unsuitable for growing bananas. 这里的气候不适合种植香蕉。

近义词：inappropriate, unfit, unsuited, improper
反义词：suitable, appropriate, fitting, proper
词性：adjective


词源：in- (not) + suitable (fitting or appropriate)
