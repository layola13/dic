meltable:/ˈmɛltəbəl/| adj.|可熔化的，可融化的|Capable of being melted; able to be changed from a solid to a liquid state by heat.|The chocolate is meltable at a low temperature.  这种巧克力在低温下就能融化。|The candle wax is meltable. 蜡烛的蜡是可熔化的。|近义词：fusible, liquefiable|反义词：infusible, non-meltable|词性:形容词
