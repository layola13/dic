geomembrane:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈmɛmbreɪn/ | n. |土工膜|A very low permeability synthetic membrane liner or barrier used with any geotechnical engineering related material so as to control fluid (or gas) migration in a human-made project, structure, or system.|  The geomembrane prevented the landfill leachate from contaminating the groundwater. 土工膜防止垃圾填埋场渗滤液污染地下水。

The geomembrane is a crucial component in the construction of modern landfills.  土工膜是现代垃圾填埋场建设中的关键组成部分。


|近义词: liner, barrier, membrane |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "permeable material") |词性: noun
