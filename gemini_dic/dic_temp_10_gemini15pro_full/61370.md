embittered: /ɪmˈbɪtərd/ (US), /ɪmˈbɪtəd/ (UK)
adjective
痛苦的，怨恨的
Feeling or showing bitterness; resentful.
She was embittered by years of disappointment. 她因多年的失望而变得痛苦。
The experience left him embittered and cynical.  这段经历使他变得愤世嫉俗，怨天尤人。
近义词: resentful, bitter, acrimonious, disillusioned, disaffected
反义词: content, happy, satisfied, cheerful, optimistic
词性: adjective

embitter: /ɪmˈbɪtər/ (US), /ɪmˈbɪtər/ (UK)
verb
使怨恨，使痛苦
To cause someone to feel bitter or resentful.
The betrayal embittered their relationship.  背叛使他们的关系恶化。
Failure to get the promotion embittered him.  未能获得晋升使他感到怨恨。
近义词: sour, poison, alienate, estrange
反义词: sweeten, improve, reconcile, appease
词性: verb


词源:
From em- (expressing intensification) + bitter.  来自em-（表示加强）+ bitter（痛苦的）。
