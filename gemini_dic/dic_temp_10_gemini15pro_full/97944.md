innovativeness:/ˈɪnəˌveɪtɪvnəs/
n.|创新性，革新性|The quality of being innovative; the tendency to introduce new ideas, methods, or products.|例句：The company's innovativeness has led to several groundbreaking products.  这家公司的创新性催生了几款突破性的产品。| 近义词：creativity, ingenuity, originality|反义词：stagnation, conservatism, traditionalism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From innovative + -ness.  Innovative comes from innovate, which stems from the Latin word "innovare," meaning "to renew" or "to alter."
