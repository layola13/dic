hexandria:/hɛkˈsændriə/
noun
六雄蕊纲
A class in the Linnaean system of plant classification, characterized by flowers with six stamens.
Hexandria includes plants like lilies and tulips. 六雄蕊纲包括百合和郁金香之类的植物。
