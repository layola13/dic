blowtorch:/ˈbləʊtɔːtʃ/ (UK), /ˈbloʊtɔːrtʃ/ (US)| n.|喷灯，吹管|A portable device producing a very hot flame by burning a fuel gas and air under pressure, used especially for soldering or cutting metal.|He used a blowtorch to melt the ice on the driveway. 他用喷灯融化了车道上的冰。|近义词：welding torch, gas torch|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ice pack" in a very specific contrasting context)|noun

blowtorches:/ˈbləʊtɔːtʃɪz/ (UK), /ˈbloʊtɔːrtʃɪz/ (US)| n.|喷灯（复数）|Plural form of blowtorch, referring to more than one blowtorch.|They used blowtorches to cut through the thick metal bars. 他们用喷灯切割厚厚的金属棒。|近义词：welding torches, gas torches|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
