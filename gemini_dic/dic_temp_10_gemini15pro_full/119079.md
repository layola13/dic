metreless:/ˈmiːtələs/ | adj. | 无韵律的，无格律的，散文体的 | Lacking poetic meter or rhythm; not written in verse. |  Her metreless prose was a refreshing change from the usual flowery poetry. 她那无韵律的散文，与通常华丽的诗句相比，令人耳目一新。| Synonyms: prosaic, unmetrical, unrhythmical | Antonyms: metrical, rhythmical, poetic | adjective
