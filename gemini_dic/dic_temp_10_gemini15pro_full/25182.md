budded:/ˈbʌdɪd/
| v. adj.|发芽的，萌芽的|Having produced buds; being in bud.  Also used figuratively to describe something in its early stages of development.|The trees have budded early this year. 今年树木发芽很早。|近义词：sprouted, germinated, burgeoning|反义词:wilted, withered|词性:动词(过去式/过去分词), 形容词


budded:/ˈbʌdɪd/
| v. adj.|萌发，开始发展|To begin to grow or develop.|Their friendship budded during their time at university.  他们的友谊在大学时期开始萌芽。|近义词：developed, emerged, commenced|反义词:declined, ceased|词性:动词(过去式/过去分词), 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *budden, from Old English *buddian, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Dutch bot, meaning "bud."
