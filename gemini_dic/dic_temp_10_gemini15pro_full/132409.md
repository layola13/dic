nondensities:/ˌnɑːnˈdɛnsɪtiz/| n. |非密度，非浓缩物|Substances or areas that are not characterized by high density or concentration.  Refers to things that are spread out or sparsely populated.|The researchers focused their study on the nondensities in the galaxy's outer arms.|近义词: dispersions, rarities |反义词: densities, concentrations| noun
