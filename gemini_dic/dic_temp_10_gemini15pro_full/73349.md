foreflow:/ˈfɔːrfloʊ/
noun
预流，先行流
The precursory flow, often of information or resources, before the main flow.  This can be seen as a preparatory phase or a leading indicator.
The foreflow of data helped analysts predict the market trend.  预流数据帮助分析师预测了市场趋势。

The foreflow of refugees alerted the aid agencies to the impending crisis.  难民的预流使援助机构意识到即将发生的危机。
precursor, vanguard, leading edge
aftermath, consequence
