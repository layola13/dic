cutaneous:/kjuːˈteɪniəs/
adjective
皮肤的
Relating to or affecting the skin.
Example: Cutaneous leishmaniasis is a parasitic skin infection. 皮肤利什曼病是一种寄生性皮肤感染。
Example: The doctor prescribed a cutaneous ointment for her rash. 医生给她开了治疗皮疹的皮肤软膏。
Synonyms: dermal, epidermal, skin
Antonyms: internal, visceral
