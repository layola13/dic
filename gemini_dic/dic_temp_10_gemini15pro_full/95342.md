incensurable:/ˌɪnsɛnˈʃʊrəbl/
adj.
无可指责的，无懈可击的
Beyond reproach; blameless.
Her conduct throughout the ordeal was incensurable. 她在整个考验中的行为无可指责。
近义词：blameless, impeccable, irreproachable, faultless, unblemished
反义词：censurable, blameworthy, reprehensible, culpable
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin incensurābilis, from in- (“not”) + censurābilis (“censurable”).
