mesothoracic:/ˌmiːzəʊθəˈræsɪk/
adjective
中胸的
Relating to the middle segment of the thorax, the part of an insect's body between the head and the abdomen.
The mesothoracic legs of a grasshopper are adapted for jumping.  蝗虫的中胸足适于跳跃。
The butterfly's colorful wings are attached to its mesothoracic segment. 蝴蝶色彩鲜艳的翅膀连接到它的中胸部。
Synonym: midthoracic
