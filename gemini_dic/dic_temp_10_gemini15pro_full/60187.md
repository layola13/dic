efficacity:/ˌɛfɪˈkæsɪti/
n.
效力；功效
The power to produce an intended effect; effectiveness.
The medicine's efficacity was proven in clinical trials. 这种药的疗效在临床试验中得到了证实。
近义词: effectiveness, potency, efficacy
反义词: inefficacy, ineffectiveness
词性: noun

词源:
From Late Latin efficacitas, from Latin efficax (“effective, effectual”), from efficere (“to work out, accomplish”), from ex- (“out”) + facere (“to do, to make”).
