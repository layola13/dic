blubber:/ˈblʌbər/| n. vi.|鲸脂；哭泣|The whale's blubber keeps it warm in the icy water. 鲸鱼的鲸脂使其在冰冷的水中保持温暖。|She blubbered uncontrollably when she heard the bad news. 当她听到这个坏消息时，她忍不住哭了。|近义词：(n.) fat, lard; (v.) weep, sob|反义词: (n.) leanness; (v.) laugh|词性:名词，动词
blubber:/ˈblʌbər/| v.|哭泣|He blubbered like a baby when he lost his toy.  他丢了玩具后哭得像个婴儿。|近义词：cry, weep, sob|反义词: laugh, rejoice|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *blubberen, frequentative of blobben (“to swell, bubble”), of imitative origin.  Related to English blob.
