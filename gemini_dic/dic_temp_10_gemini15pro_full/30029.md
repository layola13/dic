cayugas:/keɪˈjuːɡəz/ | n. |卡尤加人；卡尤加语|Cayugas are a Native American tribe of the Iroquois Confederacy.  They traditionally lived in the Cayuga Lake region of what is now New York State.  The word can also refer to their language. |The Cayugas were skilled farmers.  卡尤加人是熟练的农民。|  Cayuga pottery is highly prized by collectors.  卡尤加陶器受到收藏家的高度评价。|  Iroquois, Haudenosaunee |  None | Noun
