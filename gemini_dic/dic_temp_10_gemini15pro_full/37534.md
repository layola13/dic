commonholder: /ˈkɒmənˌhəʊldə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənˌhoʊldər/ (US) | n. | 公地持有人，共有土地持有人 | A person who has rights to use common land, typically for grazing animals. |  Example: The commonholders met to discuss the upkeep of the grazing land. 公地持有人开会讨论牧场的维护。

Example:  The commonholders were concerned about the proposed development near their land. 公地持有人担心他们土地附近拟议的开发项目。


| 近义词: commoner, | 反义词: landowner (in some contexts) | 词性: noun
