non-carrier:/ˌnɑːnˈkæriər/ | n. |非携带者，非运营商|A person or organism that does not carry a specific gene or disease, especially a recessive one.|例句：She is a non-carrier for the cystic fibrosis gene. 她不是囊性纤维化基因的携带者.|近义词：unaffected individual|反义词:carrier|词性:名词

non-carrier:/ˌnɑːnˈkæriər/ | n. |非运营商|A company that does not operate its own transportation network, but relies on other carriers for delivery services.|例句：The company acts as a non-carrier, using established shipping lines for transport. 这家公司是非运营商，使用已建立的航运线路进行运输。|近义词：freight forwarder, logistics provider|反义词:carrier, operator|词性:名词
