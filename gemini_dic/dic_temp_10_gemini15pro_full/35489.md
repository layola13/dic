coactively:/koʊˈæktɪvli/
adv.|共同地，强制地|Acting together or by compulsion.|The new policy was coactively implemented by the government and the private sector.|近义词: jointly, concurrently, compulsorily|反义词: independently, separately|词性:副词
Etymology: From "coactive" + "-ly". "Coactive" comes from "co-" (together) + "active".
