electro-hydraulically:/ɪˌlektroʊhaɪˈdrɔːlɪkli/
adjective
电液的，由电和液压联合驱动的
Operated or controlled by a combination of electric and hydraulic power.
The landing gear was retracted electro-hydraulically.  起落架是用电液压装置收回的。
The electro-hydraulically controlled steering provides precise maneuverability. 电液控制的转向系统提供了精确的操控性。
Near Synonyms: electro-hydraulic
Antonyms: manually, mechanically
词性: adjective
