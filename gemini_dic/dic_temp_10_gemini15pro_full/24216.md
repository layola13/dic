bridged:/brɪdʒd/
|v. |桥接，连接；弥合；渡过|To connect two things or places, literally or metaphorically.  To span or cross (a gap or obstacle).|They bridged the gap between the two buildings with a walkway. 他们在两栋楼之间建了一条走道作为桥梁。|近义词：connected, linked, spanned, crossed, joined|反义词:separated, divided
|verb|

bridged:/brɪdʒd/
|adj. |有桥的，桥梁状的；桥接的|Having a bridge or bridges.  Connected by a bridge. Spanning like a bridge.|The river was bridged at several points. 这条河上有好几座桥。|近义词：connected, linked|反义词:unconnected, separated
|adjective|
