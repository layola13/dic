Example 1: The beeriness of the bread was unexpected.  面包的啤酒味出乎意料。
Example 2:  He noted the distinct beeriness on his breath. 他注意到自己呼吸中明显的啤酒味。
Synonyms: beer-like flavor, beery taste
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could use descriptions like "wine-like", "fruity", etc. depending on the context)
Part of Speech: noun
