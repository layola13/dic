nightcap:/ˈnaɪtkæp/| n.|睡前饮料，睡帽|An alcoholic drink taken just before going to bed.|I had a nightcap of whisky before bed.  我睡前喝了一杯威士忌。|Synonyms: night drink, one for the road|Antonyms: eye-opener, morning coffee|noun

nightcap:/ˈnaɪtkæp/| n.|睡帽|A cap worn in bed.|My grandmother always wore a nightcap. 我的祖母总是戴着睡帽。|Synonyms: sleeping cap, bonnet|Antonyms: (no direct antonym)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "night" + "cap" (originally referring to a physical cap worn in bed, later extended to a drink taken before bed).  由“night”（夜晚）和“cap”（帽子）组成（最初指睡时戴的帽子，后来引申为睡前喝的饮料）。
