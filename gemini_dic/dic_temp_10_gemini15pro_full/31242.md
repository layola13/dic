chantor:/ˈtʃæntər/ (US), /ˈtʃɑːntə(r)/ (UK)| n.|圣咏歌手；领唱者；(犹太教) 吟诵者|A person who chants, especially in religious services.  A cantor leads the singing in a synagogue. |例句：The chantor's voice resonated through the cathedral. 圣咏歌手的声音在教堂里回荡。

例句：The synagogue hired a new chantor for the High Holidays.  犹太教堂为高级假日聘请了一位新的吟诵者。

|近义词：singer, vocalist, chorister, precentor|反义词: listener, audience|词性: noun
