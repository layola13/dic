hybridizing:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 杂交，混合 | The process of crossbreeding two different species or varieties of plants or animals to create a hybrid. | Scientists are hybridizing different types of corn to create a more drought-resistant strain. 科学家们正在将不同类型的玉米杂交，以创造出更耐旱的品种。|  synonyms: crossbreeding, interbreeding, cross-fertilizing | antonyms: purebreeding, inbreeding | verb

hybridization:/ˌhaɪbrɪdəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 杂交，混合 | The act or process of hybridizing. |  Hybridization of different plant species can lead to new and improved varieties.  不同植物物种的杂交可以产生新的和改良的品种。 | synonyms: crossbreeding, interbreeding, cross-fertilization | antonyms: purebreeding, inbreeding | noun
