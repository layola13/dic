osteomanty: /ˈɒstiəʊˌmænti/ | n. | 骨卜 | Divination by examination of bones, especially by scapulomancy (divination using shoulder blades).  It is a form of divination based on the belief that the bones, particularly those of animals, can reveal messages from the spiritual world or predict the future. |  Osteomanty was practiced in ancient cultures to predict the outcome of battles.  骨卜在古代文化中被用来预测战争的结果。|  Shamans sometimes used osteomanty to diagnose illnesses.  萨满有时会使用骨卜来诊断疾病。 |  Synonyms: bone divination, scapulomancy | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
