altimeter:/ˈæltɪˌmiːtər/ | n. |高度计|An instrument that measures altitude, especially of an aircraft, above sea level or above ground level.|The pilot checked the altimeter to ensure they were at the correct cruising altitude. 飞行员查看了高度计，以确保他们在正确的巡航高度。|近义词：altitude gauge, height gauge, hypsometer|反义词: depth gauge|noun
