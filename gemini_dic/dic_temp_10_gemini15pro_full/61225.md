emaciation:/ɪˌmeɪʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |消瘦；憔悴；衰弱|The state of being abnormally thin or weak.|The emaciation of the famine victims was horrifying. 饥荒受害者的消瘦令人震惊。|Synonyms: thinness, leanness, gauntness, boniness, skinniness, underweight, starvation|Antonyms: obesity, plumpness, stoutness, chubbiness, robustness|noun

emaciated:/ɪˈmeɪʃiˌeɪtɪd/| adj. |消瘦的；憔悴的；衰弱的|Abnormally thin or weak, especially because of illness or a lack of food.|The refugees arrived at the camp looking emaciated and exhausted. 难民到达营地时，看起来消瘦而疲惫。|Synonyms: thin, lean, gaunt, bony, skinny, underweight, starved|Antonyms: obese, plump, stout, chubby, robust|adjective
