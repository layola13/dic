liberated:/ˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/| adj.|解放的，无拘无束的|freed from social or sexual restrictions or constraints; uninhibited.|例句：She felt liberated after leaving her stressful job.  她离开压力大的工作后感到解放了。|近义词：freed, emancipated, unrestricted, unrestrained|反义词:oppressed, restricted, confined|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *liberatus*, past participle of *liberare* "to free," from *liber* "free."
