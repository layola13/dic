bonsai:/ˈbɒnsaɪ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnsaɪ/ (US)| n.|盆景|A miniature tree or shrub grown in a pot, typically by dwarfing techniques.|She has a beautiful collection of bonsai trees. 她收藏了很多漂亮的盆景。|synonyms: miniature tree, dwarf tree||noun
bonsai:/ˈbɒnsaɪ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnsaɪ/ (US)| adj.|盆景的|Relating to the art of growing bonsai.|He attended a bonsai workshop to learn the techniques. 他参加了一个盆景研讨会来学习技巧。|synonyms: miniaturized||adjective
