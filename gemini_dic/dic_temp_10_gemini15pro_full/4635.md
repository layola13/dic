aldis:/ˈɔːldiz/ | n. | 奥乐齐（一家德国廉价超市连锁店） | A global discount supermarket chain originating from Germany. | Example: I bought some groceries at Aldi's yesterday. 我昨天在奥乐齐买了一些食品杂货。
