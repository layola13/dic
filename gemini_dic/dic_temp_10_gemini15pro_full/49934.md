dendrite:/ˈdɛndraɪt/| n. |树突; 枝状晶体|A short branched extension of a nerve cell, along which impulses received from other cells at synapses are transmitted to the cell body.|The dendrites receive signals from other neurons.|近义词:arborization, ramification|反义词:axon|noun
