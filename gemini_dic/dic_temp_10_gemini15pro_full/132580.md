nondiffidently:/ˌnɑːndɪˈfɪdəntli/
| adv. |不害羞地，自信地| In a manner that is not lacking in self-confidence; boldly. |She nondiffidently approached the stage and began her speech. 她自信地走上舞台，开始了她的演讲。|近义词：confidently, assertively, boldly|反义词：diffidently, timidly, shyly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From non- + diffident + -ly.  "Diffident" comes from the Latin "diffīdens," present participle of "diffīdere" meaning "to distrust, lack confidence," from "dis-" (apart, away) + "fīdere" (to trust).
