nonexaggeratedly:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪdli/
adverb
不夸张地，实事求是地
in a way that does not exaggerate; truthfully.
She described the events nonexaggeratedly, sticking to the facts. 她实事求是地描述了事件，坚持事实。
The witness nonexaggeratedly recounted the details of the accident. 目击者如实地讲述了事故的细节。

近义词:truthfully, accurately, faithfully, factually, literally, plainly, simply, soberly, understatedly
反义词:exaggeratedly, hyperbolically, dramatically, theatrically, sensationally, extravagantly, overstatedly
词性:adverb


词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
exaggerate (verb): From Latin exaggeratus, past participle of exaggerare "to heap up, increase, amplify," from ex- "out" + aggerare "to heap up," from agger "heap, mound."
-ed (suffix): Forms the past participle of verbs.
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
