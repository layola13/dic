debating:/dɪˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb.
辩论；讨论；斟酌
engaging in a formal discussion on a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.
The students are debating the merits of school uniforms. 学生们正在辩论穿校服的利弊。
They were debating whether to go to the cinema or stay home. 他们在讨论是去看电影还是待在家里。

近义词:discussing, arguing, considering
反义词:agreeing, concurring
词性:verb (present participle)

debating:/dɪˈbeɪtɪŋ/
noun.
辩论；讨论
the activity of formally discussing a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.
The debating society meets every Tuesday evening. 辩论社每周二晚上聚会。
There was much debating about the best course of action. 关于最佳行动方案有很多争论。
近义词:discussion, argument, deliberation
反义词:agreement, concord
词性:noun (gerund)


词源: From the Old French *debatre* "to fight, contend," derived from the Latin *battuere* "to beat, fight."
