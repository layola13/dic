词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
collusion (noun): From Late Latin collusio "a playing together, secret agreement," from Latin colludere "to play together, conspire," from com- "together" (see com-) + ludere "to play" (see ludicrous).

用法说明:
Noncollusion is the noun form of noncollusive, which is the opposite of collusive. It refers to the absence of a secret agreement or cooperation, especially for illegal or deceitful purposes.  It's often used in legal and business contexts.
