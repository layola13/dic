correlatives:/kəˈrɛlətɪvz/ | n. |  关联词; 相对词;  | words or phrases that express a mutual relationship or correspondence. Correlatives are often used in pairs, such as "both...and," "either...or," "neither...nor," "not only...but also." | Both the students and the teacher enjoyed the field trip. 学生和老师都很喜欢这次实地考察旅行。 | synonyms: related terms, corresponding words | antonyms: unrelated terms, disparate words | noun

correlative:/kəˈrɛlətɪv/ | adj. | 相关的; 相互关联的;  | mutually related; corresponding. | The correlative increase in both population and pollution is a serious concern. 人口和污染的相关增长是一个严重的问题。 | synonyms: related, interconnected, corresponding | antonyms: unrelated, independent, disparate | adjective
