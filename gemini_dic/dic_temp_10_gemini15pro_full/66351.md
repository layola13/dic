excursionize:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒənaɪz/
verb
去远足，旅行
To go on an excursion; to make a trip or journey, especially for pleasure.
例句：They decided to excursionize to the mountains for the weekend. 他们决定周末去山上远足。

例句：We excursionized through the countryside, enjoying the scenic views. 我们在乡间旅行，欣赏美丽的景色。


近义词：travel, journey, tour, explore, trek
反义词：stay, remain, settle
词性：verb
