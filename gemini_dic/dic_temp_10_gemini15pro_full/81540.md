gravy:/ˈɡreɪvi/| n.| 肉汁；意外之财；轻松的钱|A rich sauce made from the juices that come out of meat when it is cooked, often mixed with flour and water or stock.|The roast beef was served with rich gravy.| 近义词:sauce, dressing| 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 plain sauce 清淡的酱汁) |词性:名词
