outclimb:/ˌaʊtˈklaɪm/ | v. | 胜过；在攀登中超过 | To climb higher or faster than someone or something else. | The experienced climber easily outclimbed the beginner. 经验丰富的登山者轻松地超过了初学者。 |  He outclimbed his rivals to reach the summit first. 他比他的对手爬得更快，第一个到达山顶。 | surpass, exceed, outstrip | lag behind, trail | verb
