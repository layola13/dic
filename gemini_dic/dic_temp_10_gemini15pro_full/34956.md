clockwork:/ˈklɑːkwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈklɒkwɜːk/ (UK)| n. adj.| 发条装置; 钟表机构; 精密复杂的系统;  (像) 钟表机构一样运行的; 规律的; 呆板的|A mechanism, especially of a clock or other device, that operates by means of a spring and a series of gears.
