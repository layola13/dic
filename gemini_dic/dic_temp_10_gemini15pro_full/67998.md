factions:/ˈfækʃənz/ | n. |派系，派别，阵营|A dissenting group within a larger one, especially in politics.|例句：The party split into warring factions. 该党分裂成互相争斗的派系。|例句：Several factions within the company opposed the merger. 公司内部几个派别反对合并。|近义词：cliques, groups, parties, sects, sides|反义词：whole, unity|词性:名词

Etymology->
From Middle French *faccion* (modern French *faction*), from Latin *factio* ("a making, a doing, a party"), from *facere* ("to do, to make").
