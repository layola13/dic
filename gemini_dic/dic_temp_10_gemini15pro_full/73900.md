forsaking:/fərˈseɪkɪŋ/
verb
放弃，抛弃
The act of abandoning or leaving something or someone behind.
He is forsaking his family for a new life abroad.  他为了去国外开始新生活而抛弃了他的家庭。
She was forsaking her old habits. 她正在改掉她的旧习惯。

近义词：abandoning, deserting, leaving, renouncing
反义词：keeping, cherishing, embracing, upholding
词性：Present Participle of forsake
