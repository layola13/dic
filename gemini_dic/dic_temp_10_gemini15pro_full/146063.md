overeagerness:/ˌoʊvərˈiːɡərnəs/| n. |过度热情，过分渴望|The salesman's overeagerness put the customer off.  这个销售员的过分热情让顾客反感。|English Explanation: Excessive eagerness; too much enthusiasm or desire.  |例句：Her overeagerness to please her boss was obvious. 她渴望取悦老板的心思显而易见。

Her overeagerness made her seem desperate. 她过分的热情让她看起来很 desperate(不顾一切)。

|近义词：zeal, enthusiasm, impatience, fervor|反义词: apathy, indifference, reluctance|词性:noun
