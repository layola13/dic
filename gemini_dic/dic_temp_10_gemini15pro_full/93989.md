impairer:/ɪmˈpeərər/| n.|损害者，削弱者|Something or someone that impairs or weakens something else. |The loud music was an impairer of concentration.  喧闹的音乐妨碍了注意力集中。|Synonyms: detriment, hindrance, obstacle|Antonyms: enhancer, improver|noun

impair:/ɪmˈper/| v.|损害，削弱|To weaken or damage something, especially a faculty or function.|His vision was impaired by the bright light.  他的视力被强光损害了。|Synonyms: damage, harm, weaken|Antonyms: improve, enhance, strengthen|verb
