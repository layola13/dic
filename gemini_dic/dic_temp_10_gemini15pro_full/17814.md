bedsit: /ˈbɛdsɪt/ (UK), /ˈbɛdsɪt/ (US)| n. |单间公寓式出租屋|A room that is both a bedroom and a living room, usually rented out.|He couldn't afford a proper apartment, so he rented a bedsit. 他负担不起一套合适的公寓，所以租了一间单间公寓式出租屋。|Synonym: studio flat, single room accommodation|Antonym: house, apartment (multi-room)|noun
