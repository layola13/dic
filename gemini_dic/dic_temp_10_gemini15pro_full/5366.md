allusive:/əˈluːsɪv/ | adj. | 暗指的，影射的 | Suggesting something indirectly; hinting at. |  The poem is allusive and requires a deep understanding of mythology to fully appreciate it. 这首诗充满了典故，需要对神话有深入的了解才能完全领会。

Her speech was allusive, filled with references to historical events. 她的演讲充满了暗示，提及了很多历史事件。 | 近义词：suggestive, indirect, implied, referential, connotative | 反义词：explicit, direct, literal, unambiguous | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *allusīvus, from Latin allūsus, past participle of allūdere (“to play with, allude to”).  Alludere is composed of ad- (“to, towards”) + lūdere (“to play”).
