/wɒt/, /hwɒt/ |det. |什么，哪一个，哪一些| Used to ask for information specifying something. | What books do you like? 你喜欢哪些书？| Which, Any | N/A | determiner

Etymology:
From Middle English *what, *hwat, from Old English hwæt (“what”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat (“what”), from Proto-Indo-European *kʷod (“what”). Cognate with Scots what (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Latin quod (“what; which, that”), Sanskrit कत् (kát, “who, which”).

USAGE->

Simple Present:
What do you think about this? 你觉得这个怎么样？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you working on? 你正在做什么？
What is he doing these days? 他这些天在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? 她最近在忙什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been reading lately? 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been doing all afternoon? 他整个下午都在做什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? 她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? 会议前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before you started this job? 你开始这份工作之前一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she got sick? 她生病前一直在做什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 下学期她会学习什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will they have done by then? 到那时他们会做什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2025? 到2025年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
