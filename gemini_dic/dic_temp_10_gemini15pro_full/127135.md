natrium:/ˈneɪtriəm/| n.|钠|A soft, silver-white, highly reactive metallic element, the most abundant alkali metal.  It is found in nature combined in many minerals, especially common salt (sodium chloride).|Sodium is an essential element for animal life.|Synonym: Na (chemical symbol)|Antonym: None|noun
