humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n.  interj.| 欺骗；骗局；谎话；胡说；废话|something that is intended to deceive people; deceptive or false talk or behaviour| "The promise of tax cuts was just a humbug."  税收减免的承诺不过是个骗局。|synonyms: hoax, fraud, sham, deception, trickery, nonsense, rubbish|antonyms: truth, honesty, reality|noun

humbug:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| interj.|  呸！胡说！|used to express disbelief or contempt| "Humbug! I don't believe a word of it." 呸！我一个字都不信。|synonyms: nonsense, rubbish, balderdash|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an interjection expressing disbelief)|interjection
