overrichnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪtʃnɪsɪz/
noun
过度丰富；过分华丽
The overrichnesses of the decor made the room feel stuffy.  房间装饰的过度华丽使它感觉很闷。
The overrichnesses of the prose style detracted from the clarity of the message.  散文风格的过分华丽削弱了信息的清晰度。
synonym:  opulence, extravagance, luxuriance
antonym:  simplicity, austerity, plainness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + richness + -es (plural suffix). "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Richness" comes from the Old English "rīce" meaning "powerful, wealthy, splendid".  The suffix "-es" creates the plural form.
