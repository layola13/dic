caginess:/ˈkeɪdʒinɪs/
noun
谨慎, 笼统, 含糊
The quality of being cautious, secretive, and unwilling to give information.
He answered questions with a certain caginess. 他回答问题时有所保留。
Synonyms: reticence, guardedness, secrecy, wariness
Antonyms: openness, frankness, candor
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cage + -iness.  Relates to the idea of being trapped or restrained, hence unwilling to reveal information.
