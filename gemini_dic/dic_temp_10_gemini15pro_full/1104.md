accessibility:/əkˌsesəˈbɪləti/| n.|可及性；易接近性；可理解性|The accessibility of the internet has made information readily available to everyone. 互联网的可及性使每个人都能轻松获得信息。  The museum has improved accessibility for disabled visitors.  博物馆改善了残疾游客的可及性。|近义词：availability, reachability, usability|反义词：inaccessibility, unavailability|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From accessible + -ity.  Accessible comes from Late Latin accessibilis, from accedere "to approach," from ad- "to" + cedere "to go."

The accessibility of affordable housing is a growing concern in many cities. = 在许多城市，经济适用房的可及性日益受到关注。

The report highlighted the importance of accessibility in education for all students. = 该报告强调了教育可及性对所有学生的重要性。
