parosteal:/ˌpærɒˈstiːəl/ (US) /ˌpærɑːˈstiːəl/| adj.|骨膜旁的，近骨的|Located beside or adjacent to the periosteum (the membrane covering the outer surface of bones).|

Example 1: The parosteal osteosarcoma is a rare type of bone cancer. 骨膜旁骨肉瘤是一种罕见的骨癌。

Example 2: Parosteal lipoma is a benign tumor that grows on the outer surface of a bone. 骨膜旁脂肪瘤是一种生长在骨骼外表面的良性肿瘤。
