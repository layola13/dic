outpromising: /ˌaʊtˈprɒmɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 过度承诺；承诺过多 | Making promises that exceed what one can or will deliver. |  The company outpromised and underdelivered, leaving customers frustrated.  这家公司承诺过多而交付不足，让客户感到沮丧。| Example: The politician outpromised during his campaign and now faces criticism for not fulfilling his pledges.  这位政治家在竞选期间过度承诺，现在因未能履行承诺而面临批评。 | Synonyms: overpromising, overcommitting | Antonyms: underpromising, undercommitting | verb
