memorialising:/məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
纪念，缅怀
The act of creating a memorial to someone or something.
The museum is memorialising the victims of the war.  博物馆正在纪念战争的受害者。
They are memorialising the event with a plaque. 他们正在用一块匾来纪念这一事件。
commemorating, remembering, celebrating
forgetting, ignoring
v.
