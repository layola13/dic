mistrust:/mɪsˈtrʌst/ | n. vt. |不信任，怀疑；猜疑|Lack of trust or confidence; suspicion.|She had a deep mistrust of strangers. 她对陌生人深感不信任。|近义词：suspicion, distrust, doubt|反义词：trust, confidence, faith|词性:名词,动词

mistrust:/mɪsˈtrʌst/ | vt. |不信任，怀疑；猜疑|To regard with distrust or suspicion; doubt.|He mistrusted the government's motives. 他怀疑政府的动机。|近义词：doubt, suspect, question|反义词：trust, believe, accept|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mistristen*, from mis- + trusten (“to trust”). Equivalent to mis- +‎ trust.
