misorganized:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/| adj.|杂乱无章的，组织混乱的|badly or wrongly organized|Her notes were disorganized and difficult to follow. 她的笔记杂乱无章，难以理解。|The project was misorganized from the start. 这个项目从一开始就组织混乱。|近义词：disordered, chaotic, messy, cluttered|反义词：organized, orderly, neat, tidy|词性:形容词
词源:  mis- (prefix) + organized.  "mis-"  means "badly" or "wrongly". "Organized" comes from the Old French "organiser," ultimately from the Latin "organum" meaning "instrument" or "tool."
