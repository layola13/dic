confinedness:/kənˈfaɪndnɪs/
n.
限制；禁闭；狭窄
The state of being confined; restriction within limits.
The confinedness of the small apartment made her feel claustrophobic.  这间小公寓的狭窄让她感到幽闭恐惧。
confinement, restriction, imprisonment
freedom, openness, liberty
