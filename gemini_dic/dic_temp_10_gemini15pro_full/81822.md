grewsome:/ˈɡruːsəm/ (US), /ˈɡruːs(ə)m/ (UK)| adj.|可怕的，令人毛骨悚然的，阴森的|Causing repulsion or horror; grisly.|The scene of the accident was gruesome. 事故现场惨不忍睹。|近义词：grisly, ghastly, horrible, macabre, frightful|反义词：pleasant, delightful, agreeable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grewsome, *gresome, from Old English *grēsom "horrible," from Proto-Germanic *grisan "to shudder," of uncertain origin.
