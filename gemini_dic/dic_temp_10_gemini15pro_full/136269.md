nonrepetitious:/ˌnɑːnˌrepɪˈtɪʃəs/ | adj. | 不重复的，无重复的 | Not repetitive; having no repetition. | The composer created a nonrepetitious melody, each note unique and flowing seamlessly into the next. 作曲家创作了一段不重复的旋律，每个音符都是独特的，并且无缝地衔接在一起。 | The teacher emphasized the importance of nonrepetitious practice problems. 老师强调了不重复练习题的重要性。 | 近义词: unique, original, varied, diverse | 反义词: repetitive, monotonous, redundant | 词性: adjective
词源: non- (not) + repetitious (characterized by repetition)  Ultimately from Latin repetere "to repeat".
