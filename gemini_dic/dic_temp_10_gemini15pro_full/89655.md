hooky:/ˈhʊki/| n.|逃学，旷课|The act of staying away from school or work without permission.|He played hooky and went to the beach. 他逃学去了海滩。|Synonyms: truancy, absenteeism|Antonyms: attendance, presence|noun

hooky:/ˈhʊki/| adj.|偷偷摸摸的，钩状的 (较少用)|Relating to or resembling a hook; clandestine or deceptive (less common).|The hooky path led through the woods. 这条弯弯曲曲的小路穿过树林。 (钩状的)|  His hooky dealings finally caught up with him. 他偷偷摸摸的交易最终让他自食恶果。(偷偷摸摸的) |Synonyms: crooked, devious (for deceptive sense)|Antonyms: straight, honest (for deceptive sense)|adjective
