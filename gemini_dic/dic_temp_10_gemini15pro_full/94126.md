impedances:/ɪmˈpiːdənsiz/| n.|阻抗（impedance的复数形式）|The impedances of the circuit components must be carefully matched. 电路元件的阻抗必须仔细匹配。|例句：The engineer measured the impedances of the various cables. 工程师测量了各种电缆的阻抗。

例句：Matching the impedances is crucial for optimal signal transfer. 匹配阻抗对于最佳信号传输至关重要。

近义词：resistance, reactance|反义词: admittance|词性:名词
