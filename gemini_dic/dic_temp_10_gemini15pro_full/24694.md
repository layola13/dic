bronchophony:/brɒŋˈkɒfəni/ /brɑːŋˈkɑːfəni/
noun
支气管语音，支气管传音
An increase in the intensity and clarity of vocal sounds heard during auscultation of the lungs, indicating increased density of lung tissue, as in consolidation.
During the examination, the doctor detected bronchophony in the patient's right lung, suggesting pneumonia. 在检查过程中，医生在病人的右肺检测到支气管语音，提示可能患有肺炎。
Synonyms: increased vocal resonance
Antonyms: decreased vocal resonance
