haystalk:/ˈheɪstɔːk/| n.|干草堆，草垛|A large pile of dried grass, used as animal fodder.|The children were playing hide-and-seek in the haystacks. 孩子们在干草堆里玩捉迷藏。|Synonyms: hayrick, haystack|Antonyms: N/A|noun
