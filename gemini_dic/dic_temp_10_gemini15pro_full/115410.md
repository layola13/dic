manteau:/ˈmæntoʊ/ (US), /ˈmæntəʊ/ (UK)| n.|外套; 披风; 掩饰; 幌子|A loose cloak or wrap worn chiefly by women.|She wore a stylish manteau over her evening gown.|近义词:cloak, cape, cover, disguise|反义词:revelation, exposure|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French manteau, from Old French mantel, from Latin mantellum "cloak, mantle," diminutive of mantum "cloak."
