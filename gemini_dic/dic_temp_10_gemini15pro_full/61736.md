impair:/ɪmˈper/| v. |损害，削弱|To weaken or damage something (especially a human faculty or function).|The illness impaired his ability to think clearly. 这种疾病损害了他清晰思考的能力。|Synonyms: damage, harm, weaken, injure, undermine, compromise|Antonyms: improve, enhance, strengthen, boost, benefit|verb

impair:/ɪmˈper/| v. |损害，削弱|Alcohol can severely impair your judgment.  酒精会严重损害你的判断力。|Synonyms: diminish, reduce, deteriorate|Antonyms: repair, restore, ameliorate|verb
