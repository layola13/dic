lists:/lɪsts/ | n. | 列表，清单 | A series of names, words, or other items written or printed one below another. |  I made a list of things to buy at the grocery store. 我列了一个在杂货店要买的东西的清单。

| Synonym:  roster, register, inventory, catalog | Antonym: disorder, chaos | noun


lists:/lɪsts/ | v. | 列出，列举 | To make a list of; to itemize. | She listed all the attendees for the conference. 她列出了所有与会者名单。

| Synonym: enumerate, itemize, catalog | Antonym: omit, exclude | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *liste (“strip, band, selvedge”), from Frankish *lista, from Proto-Germanic *līstō (“hem, border, list”), from Proto-Indo-European *leys- (“to loosen, to release”).
