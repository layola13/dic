multigranular:/ˌmʌltiˈɡrænjʊlər/
adjective
多粒度的，多层次的
Characterized by or involving multiple levels of granularity or detail.
Multigranular computing allows for flexible and efficient data processing.  多粒度计算允许灵活高效的数据处理。
The multigranular approach to security considers threats at different levels. 多粒度安全方法考虑不同级别的威胁。
近义词: hierarchical, layered, multilevel
反义词: uniform, monolithic
