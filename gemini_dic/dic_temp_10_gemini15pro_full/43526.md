crawfished:/ˈkrɔːfɪʃt/| v.| 退出，食言，反悔|To back out of a commitment or agreement; to retreat from a position or undertaking, often in a timid or awkward way.  Originally referring to the backward movement of a crawfish. | He crawfished out of the deal at the last minute. 他在最后一刻退出了这笔交易。|Synonyms: back out, renege, retreat, withdraw|Antonyms:  commit,  proceed, follow through|verb

crawfishing:/ˈkrɔːfɪʃɪŋ/| n.|退出，食言，反悔的行为|The act of backing out of a commitment or agreement. | His crawfishing angered his business partners. 他的反悔激怒了他的商业伙伴。|Synonyms: reneging,  retreating, withdrawing|Antonyms: commitment,  perseverance|noun
