epistemological:/ɪˌpɪstɪməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
认识论的
Relating to the theory of knowledge, especially with regard to its methods, validity, and scope.
Epistemological debates often center on the nature of truth and justification. 认识论的辩论通常围绕真理和辩护的本质展开。
The professor raised an epistemological question about the nature of belief.  教授提出了一个关于信仰本质的认识论问题。
近义词:cognitive, intellectual
反义词:None
词性:adjective
