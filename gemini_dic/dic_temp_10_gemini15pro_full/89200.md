homesick: /ˈhoʊmsɪk/ (US) /ˈhəʊmsɪk/ (UK) | adj. | 想家的；思乡的 | Experiencing a longing for one's home and family. | I felt homesick when I was away at college. 我在大学的时候很想家。|  She was terribly homesick during her first semester abroad.  她在国外第一个学期非常想家。| 近义词: nostalgic, lonesome | 反义词: content, happy | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From home + sick.  Originally meant "sick from home," implying illness caused by missing one's home. The meaning shifted over time to simply "longing for home."
