cross-sectioning:/ˌkrɔːsˈsɛkʃənɪŋ/
n.|横截面，横截面分析|The process of cutting something at a right angle to its length or axis to examine its internal structure.|例句：Cross-sectioning the tree revealed its growth rings.  对树进行横截面分析揭示了它的年轮。|近义词：sectioning, transection|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun

cross-section:/ˈkrɔːsˌsɛkʃən/
n.|横截面，横断面；代表性样本|A surface or shape that is or would be exposed by making a straight cut through something, especially at right angles to its longest axis; a group of people or things that are typical or representative of a larger group.|例句：The cross-section of the cable revealed its complex wiring. 电缆的横截面显示了其复杂的线路。|例句：A cross-section of society was interviewed for the survey.  该调查采访了社会各阶层的人士。|近义词：section, slice, sample|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
