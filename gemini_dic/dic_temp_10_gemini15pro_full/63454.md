entrepots:/ˈɑːntrəpoʊz/ (US), /ˈɒntrəpəʊz/ (UK)| n. | 仓库；货栈；贸易中心|A place where goods are imported or stored, often without paying import duties, before being re-exported.|The goods were stored in entrepots before being shipped to their final destination. 这些货物在运往最终目的地之前存放在货栈中。|
Synonyms: warehouse, depot, storehouse, depository|
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "retail outlet" as an opposite concept)|
noun
