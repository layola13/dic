average: /ˈævərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈævərɪdʒ/ (UK)| adj. n. v. |平均的；普通的；平均数；平均水平|A number expressing the central or typical value in a set of data, calculated by dividing the sum of the values in the set by their number; constituting or relating to the usual or ordinary amount or kind| The average age of the students is 20. 学生的平均年龄是20岁。 | 近义词: mean, median, normal, typical, usual, ordinary | 反义词: unusual, extraordinary, atypical | 词性: adjective, noun, verb

average: /ˈævərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈævərɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. |平均数；平均水平|The result obtained by adding several quantities together and then dividing this total by the number of quantities.|The average rainfall in July is high. 七月份的平均降雨量很高。|近义词: mean, median |反义词: extreme |词性: noun

average: /ˈævərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈævərɪdʒ/ (UK)| v. |平均为；计算平均值|To calculate the average of (a set of numbers or quantities)|My grades averaged out to a B. 我的平均成绩是B。|近义词: calculate the mean of, compute the average of |反义词: (no direct antonym) |词性: verb
