croaky:/ˈkroʊki/| adj.|嘶哑的；呱呱叫的|Having a rough or hoarse voice, typically as a result of a cold or sore throat; (of a frog) making a croaking sound.|例句：He woke up with a croaky voice. 他醒来时嗓子嘶哑。|例句：The frogs in the pond were croaking loudly. 池塘里的青蛙大声呱呱叫。|近义词：hoarse, raspy, gravelly, husky|反义词：clear, smooth, melodious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the word "croak," which imitates the sound a frog makes.  The suffix "-y" turns it into an adjective.
