bingos:/ˈbɪŋɡoʊz/
n.|宾果游戏（的多次进行）; 宾果游戏获胜（多次）|A game of chance played with cards on which numbers are printed. Players cover the numbers on their cards as they are called out. The first player to cover a row of numbers shouts "Bingo!"|We played three bingos last night and I won two of them. 我们昨晚玩了三局宾果游戏，我赢了两局。|近义词:lotto, raffle|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑用 games of skill)|词性:名词
