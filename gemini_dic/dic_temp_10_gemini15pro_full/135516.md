nonplausible:/ˌnɒnˈplɔːzɪbl/ | adj. | 不合理的，难以置信的 | Not believable; not having the appearance of truth or reason. |  His explanation for the missing money was completely nonplausible. 他对丢失的钱的解释完全不可信。 |  The plot of the movie was so nonplausible that it was laughable.  这部电影的情节太难以置信了，简直让人想笑。 |  improbable, unlikely, unbelievable, unconvincing |  plausible, credible, believable, likely | adjective
