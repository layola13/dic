convolutedness:/ˌkɒnvəˈluːtɪdnəs/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈluːtɪdnəs/| n. | 迂回曲折；复杂性；卷绕 |The convolutedness of the plot made it difficult to follow.  情节的复杂性使它难以理解。| convolutedness describes the state of being convoluted, meaning extremely complex and difficult to follow. convoluted:/ˈkɒnvəluːtɪd/ /ˈkɑːnvəˈluːtɪd/| adj. | 迂回的；费解的；旋绕的 | The movie's plot was so convoluted that I couldn't follow it.  这部电影的情节太复杂了，我看不懂。| convoluted describes something that is extremely complex and difficult to follow. ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convoluta, past participle of convolvere (“to roll together, roll up”), from con- (“together”) + volvere (“to roll”).
