fraudulently:/ˈfrɔːdjələntli/
adverb
欺诈地
in a way that involves deception or misrepresentation for personal gain.
He fraudulently obtained a visa. 他以欺骗手段获得了签证。
She was accused of fraudulently claiming benefits. 她被指控诈骗福利。
Synonyms: deceitfully, dishonestly, falsely, illegally
Antonyms: honestly, legitimately, truthfully
词性: 副词
