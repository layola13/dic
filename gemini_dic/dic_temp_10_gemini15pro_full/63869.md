epilepsy:/ˈɛpɪˌlɛpsi/ (US) /ˈɛpɪˌlɛpsi/ (UK)| n.|癫痫，癫痫症|A neurological disorder marked by sudden recurrent episodes of sensory disturbance, loss of consciousness, or convulsions, associated with abnormal electrical activity in the brain.|She has suffered from epilepsy since childhood. 她从小就患有癫痫。|近义词：seizure disorder, convulsive disorder|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease)|noun
