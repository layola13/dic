buccinal:/bʌˈsɪnəl/
adjective
喇叭的；喇叭声的；似喇叭的
Relating to a trumpet, or resembling the sound of a trumpet.
The buccinal blast announced the arrival of the king. 国王的到来由喇叭声宣告。
Synonyms:trumpet-like, horn-like, clarion
Antonyms:soft, quiet
词性: adjective
