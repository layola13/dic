diterebene: /ˌdiːtɛrɛˈbɛneɪ/ (Italian pronunciation may vary) | adj. |  过得很好，一切安好 |  Italian phrase meaning "to say good things," often used as a farewell or closing remark.  It expresses good wishes and a hope for continued well-being. | Example: "Ciao, diterebene!" (Bye, all the best!)  | Synonym: farewell, goodbye, all the best, take care | Antonym:  (No direct antonym; perhaps "curse" or "ill-wish") | Phrase
