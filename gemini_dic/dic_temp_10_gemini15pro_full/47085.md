daturine:/ˈdeɪtʃərɪn/ | n. |曼陀罗碱；东莨菪碱| A poisonous alkaloid occurring in some plants of the nightshade family, such as deadly nightshade and jimsonweed. It is a powerful anticholinergic and is used medicinally to dilate pupils and relieve spasms. |  Daturine is extracted from the leaves of certain plants. 曼陀罗碱是从某些植物的叶子中提取的。

Daturine can cause hallucinations if ingested. 如果摄入曼陀罗碱会导致幻觉。 | 近义词: hyoscyamine, scopolamine | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *dātūra, from Hindi धतूरा (dhatūra).
