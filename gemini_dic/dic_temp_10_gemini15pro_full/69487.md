felicify: /fəˈlɪsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使幸福，使快乐 | To make happy; to gratify. |  He was felicitated on his promotion. 他因晋升而感到高兴。 |  She was felicitated by her colleagues on her award.  她的同事们为她获奖而祝贺她。| 近义词: gladden, delight, please | 反义词: sadden, depress | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Late Latin felicitare, from Latin felix "happy, lucky, fruitful"
