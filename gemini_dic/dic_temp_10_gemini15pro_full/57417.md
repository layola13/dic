drab:/dræb/ | adj. | 单调的，乏味的，灰暗的 |  Dull and uninteresting; lacking brightness or color. | The landscape was drab and depressing.  景色单调而令人沮丧。| She wore a drab brown coat. 她穿着一件单调的棕色外套。| 近义词：dull, uninteresting, monotonous, colorless | 反义词：bright, vibrant, colorful, interesting | 词性: 形容词


词源:
From drab (adjective) + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from the Old Norse *drabbi, meaning "dregs, sediment."
