lemuriform:/ˌlɛmjʊˈrɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. |狐猴形的；狐猴类动物 |Relating to or resembling a lemur; a primate of the suborder Strepsirrhini, which includes lemurs, lorises, and galagos.  |例句：The lemuriform primates are found only in Madagascar, Comoros, and some parts of Africa. 狐猴类灵长类动物只在马达加斯加、科摩罗群岛和非洲部分地区被发现。

例句：The aye-aye, with its unusual elongated middle finger, is a fascinating example of a lemuriform. 指猴，凭借其异常细长的中指，是狐猴类动物中一个引人入胜的例子。|近义词：prosimian |反义词：simian (anthropoid) | 词性:形容词, 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "lemures" (ghosts, spirits of the dead) + "-formis" (shaped like).  Lemurs were so named due to their nocturnal habits and large reflective eyes.
