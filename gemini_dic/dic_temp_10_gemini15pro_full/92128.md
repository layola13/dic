hyperscrupulous:/ˌhaɪpərˈskruːpjələs/
adjective
极其谨慎的；过分拘泥的
Excessively scrupulous or meticulous; extremely conscientious about details of conscience or morality.
He was so hyperscrupulous about following the rules that he missed the point entirely. 他对遵守规则过于谨慎，以至于完全忽略了重点。
She's hyperscrupulous about cleanliness, washing her hands dozens of times a day. 她对清洁极其谨慎，每天洗手几十次。

近义词: meticulous, conscientious, painstaking, fastidious
反义词: careless, negligent, remiss, unscrupulous

词源:
From hyper- + scrupulous.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive."  "Scrupulous" comes from the Latin "scrupulus," meaning "a small sharp stone," hence "a cause of uneasiness or anxiety," and later "conscientious, principled."
