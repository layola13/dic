nubigenous:/njuːˈbɪdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 云生的，由云产生的 | Formed or produced in clouds. |  Example: The nubigenous precipitation created a beautiful rainbow. 云致降水形成了一道美丽的彩虹。| Example:  Scientists studied the nubigenous formation of ice crystals. 科学家研究了云中冰晶的形成过程。 | Synonym: cloud-born, cloud-formed | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider "terrestrial" or "surface-formed") | Word Type: Adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *nubigenus ("cloud-born"), from nūbēs ("cloud") + -genus ("born," related to genus).
