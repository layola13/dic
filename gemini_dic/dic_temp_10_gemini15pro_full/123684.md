mooed:/muːd/| v. |哞哞叫|The sound a cow makes.  It's the past tense of "moo".|例句：The cow mooed softly to her calf. 母牛对着她的小牛犊轻轻地哞哞叫。

例句：The contented cows mooed in the pasture.  心满意足的奶牛在牧场上哞哞叫。

近义词：lowed, bellowed|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "was silent", "whimpered" for contrasting sounds) |词性:动词
