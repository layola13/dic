frying:/ˈfraɪɪŋ/| adj.|油炸的；油煎的|Used to describe food that is cooked in hot oil or fat.|例句：Frying chicken is a popular dish. 炸鸡是一道受欢迎的菜。|近义词：fried|反义词:boiled, steamed|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fryen*, from Old French *frire*, from Latin *frīgere* (“to roast, fry, parch”).
