mealinesses:/ˈmiːlinəsɪz/
|n.|粉状；粗糙；干巴巴|The mealinesses of the overcooked potatoes ruined the dish.  煮过头的土豆的粉状破坏了这道菜。|
|英文解析: The plural form of "mealiness," referring to the state or quality of being mealy, often used to describe a dry, powdery, or crumbly texture in food, especially fruits or vegetables.|
|例句: The mealinesses of the apples made them unpleasant to eat. 这些苹果的粉状口感使它们不好吃。|
|例句:  He noticed the mealinesses in the different varieties of potatoes. 他注意到不同品种的土豆的粉状程度。|
|近义词: dryness, powderiness, crumbliness|
|反义词: moistness, smoothness, firmness|
|词性: noun (plural)|
