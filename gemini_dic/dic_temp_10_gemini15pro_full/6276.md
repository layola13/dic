amoralities:/ˌeɪmɔːˈrælɪtiz/
n.
不道德行为；非道德性；无道德原则
lack of moral principles; absence of morality.
The amoralities of the war troubled many people.  战争的非道德性困扰着许多人。

The politician's amoralities eventually led to his downfall.  这位政客的不道德行为最终导致了他的垮台。
近义词:immoralities, wrongdoing, unethicalness, improprieties
反义词:moralities, ethics, principles, virtues
词性:名词(plural noun)
