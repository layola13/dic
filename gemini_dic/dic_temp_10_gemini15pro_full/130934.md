nonaspirin:/ˌnɑːnˈæspərɪn/| adj.| 非阿司匹林的|A drug or medication that is not aspirin.  Specifically, it refers to pain relievers that don't contain acetylsalicylic acid (the active ingredient in aspirin).|Patients with stomach ulcers are often advised to take nonaspirin pain relievers.|近义词：non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) (some overlap), analgesic|反义词:aspirin|词性:形容词
