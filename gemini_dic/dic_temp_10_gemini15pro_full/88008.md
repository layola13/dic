high-spirited:/ˌhaɪˈspɪrɪtɪd/
adjective
精力充沛的；兴致勃勃的
Full of energy and enthusiasm.
She was a high-spirited girl who loved to dance and sing. 她是一个精力充沛、热爱唱歌跳舞的女孩。
The team was high-spirited after their victory. 球队在获胜后兴致勃勃。
近义词: lively, energetic, exuberant, enthusiastic
反义词: downcast, dejected, depressed, listless
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From high + spirited.  "Spirited" itself comes from "spirit" (meaning animating force, life, vigor) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin "spiritus" (breath, courage).
