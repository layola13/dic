counter-passant:/ˌkʊntərˈpɑːsɒ̃/ /ˌkaʊntərˈpæsənt/| n.|过路兵|A special move in chess where a pawn can capture an opponent's pawn that has just moved two squares forward from its starting position, as if the captured pawn had only moved one square forward.|Example: Black's pawn moved two squares forward, and I captured it en passant. 黑方的兵前进了两格，我用过路兵吃掉了它。

Example: En passant is a rarely used but important rule in chess. 过路兵是一种很少使用但在国际象棋中很重要的规则。|近义词: (No direct synonym - it's a specific chess term)|反义词: (No antonym)|词性: Noun
