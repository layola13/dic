grubhood: /ˈɡrʌbhʊd/
noun
幼虫期，幼虫状态
the state or period of being a grub (a larva of an insect)
The caterpillar spent several weeks in its grubhood before transforming into a butterfly. 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前，在幼虫期度过了几个星期。
近义词:  larval stage, larvahood
反义词: adulthood, maturity
词性: noun


grubhood: /ˈɡrʌbhʊd/
noun
(比喻) 卑微的境况，默默无闻的状态
(figurative) a state of being insignificant or obscure
He emerged from his grubhood as a writer to become a celebrated novelist. 他从一个默默无闻的作家成长为一位著名的作家。
近义词: obscurity, insignificance
反义词: fame, prominence
词性: noun
