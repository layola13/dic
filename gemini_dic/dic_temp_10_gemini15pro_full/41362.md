corked:/kɔːrkt/ | adj. |  软木塞味的，（因软木塞污染而）变味的，无味的，迟钝的 | Having the taste or smell of cork, especially of a wine spoiled by a tainted cork. Also, figuratively, dull or lifeless. |  The wine was corked, so we sent it back. 这酒有软木塞味，所以我们把它退回去了。 | 近义词：tainted, spoiled, dull, flat | 反义词：fresh, vibrant, lively | 词性: 形容词

corked:/kɔːrkt/ | v. | 用软木塞塞住 | To seal or stop with a cork. | He corked the bottle of wine after pouring a glass. 他倒了一杯酒后，用软木塞塞住了酒瓶。 | 近义词：seal, stopper, plug | 反义词：uncork, open | 词性: 动词
