consideration:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/| n.|考虑；体贴；报酬；尊敬|Careful thought, typically over a period of time.  A factor taken into account in forming a judgment or plan.|After careful consideration, we decided to move. 经过仔细考虑后，我们决定搬家。|Synonyms: thought, deliberation, contemplation, reflection, attention, regard, notice|Antonyms: disregard, neglect, inattention|noun

consideration:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/| n.|体贴；关心|The quality of being sensitive towards others and their feelings.|She always shows great consideration for others. 她总是对别人表现出极大的体谅。|Synonyms: thoughtfulness, concern, solicitude, care, compassion|Antonyms: disregard, selfishness, inconsiderateness|noun

consideration:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/| n.|报酬；酬金|Something of value given in return for a service or favor.|They offered him a large sum of money as consideration for his services. 他们给了他一大笔钱作为对他服务的报酬。|Synonyms: payment, fee, recompense, remuneration|Antonyms:  penalty, forfeiture|noun
