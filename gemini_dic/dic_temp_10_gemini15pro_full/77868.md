genetic:/dʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |遗传的；基因的；起源的| Relating to genes or heredity. |例句：Genetic mutations can cause diseases. 基因突变会导致疾病。|例句：Scientists are studying the genetic basis of intelligence. 科学家正在研究智力的遗传基础。|近义词：hereditary, inherited, congenital |反义词：acquired |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *genētikos* "genitive, relating to origin," from *genesis* "origin, creation, generation," from *gignesthai* "to be born, become, happen."
