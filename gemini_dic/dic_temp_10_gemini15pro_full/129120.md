niyak:/ˈniːæk/| n.|煮熟的鱼; (尤指)鲑鱼|A Chinook jargon word referring to cooked or baked fish, especially salmon.|We had niyak for dinner last night. 我们昨晚吃了煮熟的鲑鱼。|近义词：salmon, cooked fish|反义词:raw fish|词性:名词

词源: From Chinook Jargon, ultimately from Lower Chinook *nə́yqa*
