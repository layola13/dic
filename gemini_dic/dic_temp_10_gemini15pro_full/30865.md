chaetiferous:/kiːˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
具刚毛的，有鬃毛的
Bearing bristles or setae.
例句：The chaetiferous caterpillar crawled across the leaf.  具刚毛的毛毛虫爬过叶子。

例句：Chaetiferous mammals, like pangolins, have specialized scales. 像穿山甲这样的具刚毛的哺乳动物有专门的鳞片。

近义词：bristly, setaceous, hispid
反义词：smooth, glabrous
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *chaetifer* (bearing bristles), from *chaeta* (bristle) + *-fer* (bearing).  Ultimately from Greek *khaitē* (long hair, mane).
