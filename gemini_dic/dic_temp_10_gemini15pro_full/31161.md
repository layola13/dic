chanfrin:/ˈtʃænfrin/| n.|鼻梁；马的额带|A chanfrin is the piece of armor that protects a horse's forehead.  It can also refer to the forehead of a horse itself.|The knight polished the chanfrin of his warhorse. 骑士擦拭了他的战马的额甲。|近义词：forehead (for the horse itself), headpiece, face guard|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
