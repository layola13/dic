easily:/ˈiːzɪli/| adv.|容易地；轻易地；顺利地|In a manner that is easy; without difficulty.|She easily passed the exam. 她轻松地通过了考试.|Synonyms: effortlessly, simply, readily|Antonyms: difficultly, arduously, laboriously|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *esili, *esilich, from Old English *ēaðlīċe, from ēaðe (“easily”) + -līċe (suffix forming adverbs).  The first element is ultimately from Proto-Germanic *auþaz (“easy”).
