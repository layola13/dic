overwhelm:/ˌoʊvərˈwelm/ | vt. | 淹没；压倒；使不知所措 | to affect someone very strongly, especially with a negative feeling, so that they cannot think clearly | The sheer volume of work overwhelmed her.  她完全被大量的工作压垮了。

The beauty of the sunset overwhelmed me. 夕阳的美丽深深震撼了我。

| 近义词：overpower, subdue, conquer, crush | 反义词：calm, soothe, comfort | 词性：动词

overwhelmed:/ˌoʊvərˈwelmd/ | adj. | 不知所措的；被压倒的；淹没的 | feeling like you have so much to deal with that you cannot cope |  I was overwhelmed by the generosity of strangers. 我被陌生人的慷慨深深感动，不知所措。

She felt completely overwhelmed by the news. 她被这个消息完全惊呆了。


| 近义词：overpowered, inundated, swamped | 反义词：calm, composed, in control | 词性：形容词


Etymology:
From Middle English *overwhelmen, from Old English *oferþelmen, *oferþwylman (“to overwhelm, cover over”), from ofer (“over”) + þelma, þylma (“flood, overwhelming mass of water”). Cognate with Dutch overwelmen (“to overwhelm”), German überwältigen (“to overwhelm, overpower”).
