numismatists:/ˌnjuːmɪˈzmætɪsts/
n.
钱币学家；钱币收藏家
A person who studies or collects coins, medals, paper money, etc.
My grandfather was a keen numismatist and had a vast collection of old coins. 我的祖父是一位热衷的钱币学家，收藏了大量古钱币。
The numismatists gathered at the convention to share their latest finds. 钱币学家们聚集在大会上分享他们的最新发现。
Synonyms: coin collector, philatelist (for stamps)
Antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite)

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *numismatique*, from Latin *numisma*, *numismatis* "coin, money," from Greek *nomisma* "current coin, usage, custom," from *nomizein* "to use customarily, to hold as customary," from *nomos* "law, custom."  Meaning "study of coins" first attested 1829.
