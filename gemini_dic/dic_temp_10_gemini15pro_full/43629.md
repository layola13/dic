creatinine: /kriˈætɪnɪn/ /kriˈætɪniːn/ | n. |肌酐| A waste product produced by muscles and filtered from the blood by the kidneys. High levels of creatinine in the blood can indicate kidney problems.| Your doctor may order a creatinine test to check your kidney function. 你的医生可能会要求进行肌酐测试来检查你的肾功能。|Synonyms: None (as it's a specific chemical compound)|Antonyms: None|Noun

creatinine: /kriˈætɪnɪn/ /kriˈætɪniːn/ | n. |肌酸酐|A nitrogenous compound formed as the end product of creatine metabolism.  Elevated levels in the blood indicate impaired kidney function.|The creatinine levels in her blood were abnormally high. 她血液中的肌酸酐水平异常高。|Synonyms: None (specific chemical compound)|Antonyms: None|Noun
