noun
遗忘, 赦免
an act of washing away; cleansing; the state of being washed away; a state of oblivion or forgetfulness, especially with regard to past sins or offenses.
The abluvion offered by the priest brought him a sense of peace.  神父的赦免让他感到安心。
The warm bath provided a welcome abluvion from the stresses of the day.  温暖的沐浴让他从一天的压力中解脱出来。

近义词: forgiveness, pardon, remission, cleansing, purification
反义词: condemnation, guilt, retention
