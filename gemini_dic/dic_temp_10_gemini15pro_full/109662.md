leucocythemia:/ˌluːkəsaɪˈθiːmiə/| n.|白血病|A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leucocytes.  This condition is also known as leukemia.|He was diagnosed with leucocythemia.|Near synonyms: leukemia, cancer of the blood|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun
