foxhounds:/ˈfɒksˌhaʊndz/
n.
猎狐犬（复数）
A type of hound bred and trained to hunt foxes.  Foxhounds are known for their speed, stamina, and excellent sense of smell.
The foxhounds chased the fox across the fields.  猎狐犬追逐着狐狸穿过田野。
近义词：hounds, hunting dogs
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "pet dogs" in a contrasting context)
词性:noun


foxhound:/ˈfɒksˌhaʊnd/
n.
猎狐犬（单数）
A dog of a breed trained to hunt foxes, typically having a short, dense coat, long ears, and a deep voice.
The foxhound bayed loudly as it picked up the scent.  猎狐犬嗅到气味后大声吠叫。
近义词：hound, hunting dog
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "pet dog" in a contrasting context)
词性:noun
