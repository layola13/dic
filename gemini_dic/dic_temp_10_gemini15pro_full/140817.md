on-its-last-legs:/ˌɑːn ɪts ˌlæst ˈleɡz/
adjective
奄奄一息的；快要坏掉的；临近结束的
Near the end of its usefulness or life.
My old car is on its last legs. 我的旧车快要报废了。
Synonyms:  worn out, decrepit, failing, dying, expiring
Antonyms:  new, brand-new, pristine, robust, healthy
词性: 形容词
