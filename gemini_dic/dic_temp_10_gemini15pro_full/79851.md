/ˈɡræm/ | n. | 克 | A unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a kilogram. | Example:  A paperclip weighs about a gram. 一个回形针重约一克。 | Example: Please give me 500 grams of flour. 请给我500克面粉。| Synonym:  (unit of mass) milligram, kilogram | Antonym:  N/A (units of mass don't have direct antonyms) | noun
