callosum:/kəˈloʊsəm/| n. |胼胝体|A thick band of nerve fibers that divides the cerebral hemispheres in mammals and connects them, allowing for communication between the two sides of the brain.|The corpus callosum plays a vital role in coordinating information between the two hemispheres of the brain. 胼胝体在大脑两个半球之间的信息协调中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: cerebral commissure|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
