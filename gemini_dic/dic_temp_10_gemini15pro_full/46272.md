czarinas:/zɑːˈriːnəz/
noun
女沙皇；皇后
The wives or widows of czars.  A female ruler or emperor (especially of Russia prior to 1917).
Catherine the Great was one of the most famous czarinas of Russia.  凯瑟琳大帝是俄罗斯最著名的女沙皇之一。
