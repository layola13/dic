half-flattering:/ˌhæf ˈflætərɪŋ/
|adj.|半奉承的；略带奉承的；不太真诚的恭维的|Partially flattering; conveying a compliment that is not entirely sincere or genuine.  The prefix "half-" diminishes the intensity of "flattering," suggesting the compliment is insincere or qualified.|She gave me a half-flattering remark about my new haircut, implying it wasn't that much of an improvement. 她对我的新发型发表了半奉承的评论，暗示这并没有多大改进。

|He offered a half-flattering apology, which didn't really sound sincere. 他提出了一个半奉承的道歉，听起来并不真诚。
|近义词：backhanded, lukewarm, double-edged, faint praise|反义词: genuinely flattering, sincere, complimentary|词性: adjective
