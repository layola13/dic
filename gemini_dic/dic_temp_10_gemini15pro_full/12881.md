assumabilities:/əˌsjuːməˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可假定性；假设的可能性|The assumabilities of the project were carefully considered.  该项目的可假定性经过了仔细考虑。|例句：The report questioned the assumabilities underpinning the financial projections.  该报告质疑了支撑财务预测的假设的可能性。

例句：We need to examine the assumabilities of the market before launching the product. 我们需要在推出产品之前检查市场的假设可能性。|近义词：assumptions, hypotheses, premises|反义词:certainties, facts|词性:noun

词源: From "assumable" + "-ity" + "-ies" (plural). "Assumable" comes from the verb "assume" (from Old French *assumer, from Latin assumere "to take up, adopt"), with the suffix "-able" indicating capability.  "-ity" turns it into a noun representing the state or quality. "-ies" makes it plural.
