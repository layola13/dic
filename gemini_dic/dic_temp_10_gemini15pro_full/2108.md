adani:/əˈdɑːni/| n.|阿达尼|An Indian multinational conglomerate company, headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. It is primarily involved in port development and operations, but also has interests in logistics, resources, power generation and transmission, renewable energy, mining, airport operations, gas, food processing and defence.|The Adani Group's rapid expansion has raised concerns among some investors. 阿达尼集团的快速扩张引起了一些投资者的担忧。
The Adani family holds a majority stake in the Adani Group. 阿达尼家族持有阿达尼集团的多数股权。|近义词:conglomerate, corporation|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
