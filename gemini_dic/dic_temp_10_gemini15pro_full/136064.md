nonreceptively:/ˌnɒnrɪˈseptɪvli/
adverb
非接受地；不接受地
in a way that is not receptive or responsive
The audience listened nonreceptively to the speaker's message.  观众对演讲者的信息反应冷淡。
He nonreceptively stared at the wall, ignoring my attempts to engage him in conversation. 他毫无反应地盯着墙，无视我试图与他交谈。
近义词:unresponsively, indifferently, passively
反义词:receptively, responsively, actively
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From non- + receptive + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Receptive" comes from the Latin "receptus," past participle of "recipere," meaning "to receive."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
