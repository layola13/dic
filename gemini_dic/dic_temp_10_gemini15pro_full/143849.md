outplanning:/ˌaʊtˈplænɪŋ/
n.
超越规划；过度规划；比...计划得更好
The act of planning more effectively or comprehensively than someone or something else.  This can involve anticipating future needs or challenges more accurately, developing more robust strategies, or simply being more organized and prepared.
Our outplanning of the competition allowed us to secure the contract. 我们比竞争对手计划得更好，从而赢得了合同。
superior planning, advanced planning, strategic foresight
lack of planning, poor planning
noun
