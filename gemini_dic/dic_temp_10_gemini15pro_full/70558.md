filipina:/ˌfɪlɪˈpinə/ | n. |菲律宾女子；菲律宾女佣|A woman or girl who is a native or inhabitant of the Philippines.|例句：She is a Filipina working as a nurse in the United States. 她是一位在美国做护士的菲律宾女子。

例句：My grandmother was a Filipina who immigrated to Canada in the 1950s. 我的祖母是一位在20世纪50年代移民到加拿大的菲律宾女子。|近义词：Filipino (when referring to a woman) |反义词: (No direct antonym, could be a woman of a different nationality, e.g., American, Chinese etc.) |词性:noun
