colliquate:/ˈkɒlɪkweɪt/ /ˈkɑːlɪkweɪt/| v. |熔化，液化；瓦解，崩溃|To melt or dissolve; to break down or disintegrate.|The intense heat caused the metal to colliquate.  高温使金属熔化。|The empire began to colliquate under the weight of its own corruption. 这个帝国在自身腐败的重压下开始瓦解。|近义词：melt, dissolve, liquefy, disintegrate, crumble|反义词: solidify, harden, strengthen|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *colliquāre*, from *com-* (together) + *liquāre* (to liquefy), from *liquidus* (liquid).
