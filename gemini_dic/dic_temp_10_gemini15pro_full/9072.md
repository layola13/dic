antimilitaristic:/ˌænˌtiˌmɪlɪtəˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
反军国主义的; 反战的
opposed to the principles of militarism.
例句：
The antimilitaristic movement gained traction after the devastating war.
反战运动在毁灭性的战争之后获得了支持。

Her antimilitaristic stance made her unpopular with the more hawkish members of the government.
她反军国主义的立场使她不受政府中更强硬的成员的欢迎。

近义词：pacifistic, dovish, antiwar
反义词：militaristic, hawkish, pro-war
词性: adjective
