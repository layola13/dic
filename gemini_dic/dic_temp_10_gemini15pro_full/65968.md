exaggeratingly:/ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪŋli/
adverb
夸张地，夸大地
in a way that makes something seem larger, better, or worse than it really is
She described the incident exaggeratingly, making it sound much worse than it actually was. 她夸张地描述了这件事，使它听起来比实际情况糟糕得多。
excessively, overmuch, unduly
moderately, reasonably
adverb
