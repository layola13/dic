fumifugist: /ˌfjuːmɪˈfjuːdʒɪst/ | n. |熏蒸消毒专家；烟熏消毒师| A person who carries out fumigation. | Example: The fumifugist carefully sealed the building before releasing the fumigant. 熏蒸消毒专家在释放熏蒸剂之前仔细地密封了建筑物。

Example:  The fumifugist inspected the premises for signs of pests. 熏蒸消毒专家检查了房屋是否有害虫的迹象。| 近义词: exterminator, pest control specialist | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider "pest breeder") | 词性: noun
