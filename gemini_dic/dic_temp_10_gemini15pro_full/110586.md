limiteds: /ˈlɪmɪtɪdz/ | n. | 有限公司（复数，非标准用法） |  "Limiteds" is not a standard English word.  The plural of "limited" (when referring to a limited company) is generally treated as uncountable and therefore takes no plural marker. The correct term would be "limited companies." |  Incorrect:  The limiteds are merging. | Correct: The limited companies are merging. | 近义词: (singular) limited company, corporation, firm | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: Noun (non-standard plural)
