connotation:/ˌkɑːnəˈteɪʃən/
|n.|含义;言外之意;内涵|An idea or feeling that a word invokes in addition to its literal or primary meaning.|Example 1: The word "cheap" has negative connotations, implying poor quality as well as low price. “便宜”这个词带有负面含义，暗示质量差和价格低。

Example 2:  The connotation of the word "rose" is often love and romance. “玫瑰”一词的含义通常是爱和浪漫。|Synonym: implication, nuance, undertone, overtone, suggestion|Antonym: denotation, explicit meaning|Noun
