ductility:/dʌkˈtɪləti/
n.|延展性，韧性|The ductility of the metal allows it to be drawn into wires.  这种金属的延展性使它可以被拉成金属丝。|例句：The ductility of copper makes it ideal for wiring. 铜的延展性使它非常适合用于电线。|近义词：malleability, plasticity, flexibility|反义词：brittleness, fragility|词性:名词

ductile:/ˈdʌktaɪl/
adj.|可延展的，韧性的|Copper is a ductile metal. 铜是一种延展性金属。|例句：Gold is a highly ductile metal, which is why it can be made into such intricate jewelry. 金是一种延展性很高的金属，这就是为什么它可以被制成如此复杂的珠宝。|近义词：malleable, pliable, flexible|反义词：brittle, fragile, rigid|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ductilis, from Latin ductus (“leading, conducting”), past participle of dūcere (“to lead, draw”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dewk- (“to lead”).
