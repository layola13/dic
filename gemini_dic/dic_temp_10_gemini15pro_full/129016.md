Nishapur: /nɪʃəˈpʊr/ | n. | 内沙布尔 | A city in northeastern Iran, known for its historical and cultural significance. |  Nishapur was once a major city on the Silk Road. 内沙布尔曾经是丝绸之路上的一个主要城市。| Synonyms: city, town | Antonyms: countryside, village | noun

Etymology: From Persian نیشابور (Nīšābūr),  believed to be founded by Shapur I of the Sasanian Empire.
