krupp: /krʊp/ | n. | 克虏伯 |  A German industrial family that built a major steel and armaments firm.  Refers to the company founded by and named after the family, known for its production of steel, heavy machinery, and especially weapons. | The Krupp family played a significant role in German industrialization and military history. 克虏伯家族在德国工业化和军事史上扮演了重要角色。 | Krupp steel was renowned for its quality. 克虏伯钢以其质量而闻名。 | synonyms: conglomerate, industrial empire | antonyms: small business, cottage industry | noun
