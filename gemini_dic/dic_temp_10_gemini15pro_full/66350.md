excursionist:/ɪkˈskɜːrʃənɪst/
n.
短途旅行者，远足者
A person who goes on an excursion, especially one who travels for pleasure.
The excursionists boarded the bus early in the morning.  游客们一大早就上了巴士。
The boat was filled with excursionists heading to the island.  船上坐满了前往岛屿的游客。
synonyms:  tourist, traveler, sightseer, tripper, day-tripper
antonyms: resident, local, native
noun
