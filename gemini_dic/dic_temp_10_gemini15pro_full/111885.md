logomachist: /loʊˈɡɒməkɪst/ (US) /ˌlɒɡəˈmækɪst/ (UK)| n. |好争论的人；文字游戏爱好者|A person who engages in logomachy (a dispute about words).|He's such a logomachist, always arguing about the precise meaning of words. 他真是个好争论的人，总是争论词语的确切含义。|Synonyms: arguer, disputant, quibbler, hair-splitter|Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator|Noun
