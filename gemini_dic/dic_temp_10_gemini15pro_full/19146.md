bevelled:/ˈbevəld/ /ˈbevl̩d/| adj.|斜角的，切成斜面的|A bevelled edge or surface slopes at an angle. |The mirror had a bevelled edge.  镜子有一个斜边。|近义词：sloped, angled, slanted, chamfered|反义词:straight, square|adjective

beveled:/ˈbevəld/ /ˈbevl̩d/| adj.|斜角的，切成斜面的|A beveled edge or surface slopes at an angle. |The table top was beveled to prevent spills. 桌子的顶部被做成斜面以防止溢出。|近义词：sloped, angled, slanted, chamfered|反义词:straight, square|adjective

bevel:/ˈbevəl/ /ˈbevl̩/| n. v.|斜角，斜面；使成斜角，使成斜面|n. a sloping edge or surface. v. to cut or shape to a bevel. |The carpenter used a plane to create a bevel on the wood. 木匠用刨子在木头上做了一个斜角。|近义词：slope, angle, slant, chamfer|反义词:squareness|noun, verb
