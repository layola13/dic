dithecous:/daɪˈθiːkəs/
adjective
双药的，有两个药囊的
Having two thecae or sacs, typically referring to anthers in botany.  (Relating to an anther that is divided into two lobes, each containing two pollen sacs.)
The dithecous anther is a common characteristic of many flowering plants.  双药花药是许多开花植物的共同特征。
The botanist examined the dithecous anthers under the microscope.  植物学家在显微镜下检查了双药花药。
近义词:bilocular (referring to ovaries), two-celled
反义词:monothecous (having a single theca)
词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek *di- "two" + theke "case, box" + -ous (adjective forming suffix).
