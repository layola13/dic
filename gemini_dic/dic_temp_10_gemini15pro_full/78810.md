gimlet:/ˈɡɪmlɪt/| n.|手钻；锥子；鸡尾酒|A small T-shaped tool with a cross handle and a sharp point, used for boring small holes.|He used a gimlet to make a hole for the screw. 他用锥子钻了个孔来上螺丝。|Synonyms: awl, borer, drill|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gimlet:/ˈɡɪmlɪt/| adj.|锐利的；敏锐的|Sharp and piercing, especially of eyes or glances.|He gave me a gimlet stare. 他冷冷地盯着我。|Synonyms: piercing, sharp, keen|Antonyms: dull, unfocused|adjective
