endophragma:/ˌɛndoʊˈfræɡmə/ | n. | 内韧皮部; 内韧皮; 内筛部 | The endophragma is a layer of tissue found inside the phloem of some plants.  内韧皮部是在某些植物的韧皮部内部发现的一层组织。 |  The researchers studied the structure of the endophragma in the redwood tree. 研究人员研究了红杉树内韧皮部的结构。| The endophragma plays a crucial role in nutrient transport. 内韧皮部在营养物质运输中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: inner phloem (though not a perfect synonym, as it refers to the location rather than the specific tissue) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
