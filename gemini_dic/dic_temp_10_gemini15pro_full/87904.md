hierocracy:/haɪˈɪərəkrəsi/| n.|神权政治，僧侣统治|A system of government ruled by religious leaders or priests.  A system where the priesthood constitutes the ruling class.| The ancient Egyptian civilization was a hierocracy, with the pharaoh considered a divine ruler. 古埃及文明是一个神权政治，法老被认为是神圣的统治者。

The country's hierocracy exerted significant influence over daily life.  这个国家的神权政治对日常生活施加了重大影响。|近义词：theocracy, priesthood|反义词: secularism, democracy|noun
