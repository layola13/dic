exult:/ɪɡˈzʌlt/ /ɪɡˈzʌlt/| v. |感到欢欣鼓舞，欣喜若狂|To show or feel great joy or triumph, especially due to success.|She exulted at/over her victory. 她为自己的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。|近义词：rejoice, triumph, revel|反义词:mourn, grieve|动词

exult:/ɪɡˈzʌlt/ /ɪɡˈzʌlt/| n. |欢欣，狂喜 (较少用作名词)|A feeling of great joy or triumph.|His exult at winning the championship was evident. 他赢得冠军的喜悦显露无疑。|近义词：jubilation, elation|反义词:grief, sorrow|名词
