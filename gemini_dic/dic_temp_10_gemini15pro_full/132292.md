nondefensiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪvnəs/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛnsɪvnəs/| n.|无防备状态；不设防；不辩解|The state of being open and receptive to feedback, without feeling the need to justify or protect oneself.|Her nondefensiveness made the conversation much more productive. 她的不设防姿态让谈话变得更有成效。|近义词：openness, receptiveness, acceptance|反义词: defensiveness, guardedness|词性:名词

nondefensive:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪv/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|不设防的；不辩解的|Not defensive; open and receptive to criticism or feedback.|She took the feedback in a nondefensive way. 她以不辩解的方式接受了反馈。|近义词：open, receptive, accepting|反义词: defensive, guarded|词性:形容词
