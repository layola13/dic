hardwire:/ˈhɑːrdwaɪər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. adj. n.|硬连线，固化，本能的|To connect something directly to a system using wires; to be built-in or inherent, not learned.|例句：The instinct to survive is hardwired into our DNA. 生存的本能深深植根于我们的DNA中。|近义词：inbuilt, innate, ingrained, inherent|反义词：learned, acquired, flexible|verb

hardwire:/ˈhɑːrdwaɪər/ (US) /ˈhɑːdwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. adj. n.|硬连线，固化，本能的|A system or component that is directly connected using wires; something that is inherent or deeply ingrained.|例句：The new building has hardwiring for high-speed internet access. 新大楼预先安装了高速互联网接入线路。|近义词：wiring, circuitry, hardware|反义词：software|noun
