esemplastic:/ˌɛzəmˈplæstɪk/ | adj. |  塑造的；有造型力的；想象力丰富的 | Having the power to shape or mold; formative.  Relating to or characterized by the imagination's ability to shape disparate elements into a unified whole. | Coleridge described the imagination as "esemplastic."  柯勒律治将想象力描述为“塑造性的”。 |  formative, creative, imaginative, synthesizing, unifying |  rigid, inflexible, uncreative | adjective
