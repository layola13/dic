diving:/ˈdaɪvɪŋ/| n. |跳水，潜水|The act of plunging into water headfirst.  The sport or activity of swimming underwater, especially using special breathing equipment.|例句：She enjoys diving into the pool. 她喜欢跳进泳池。

例句：He went diving in the Great Barrier Reef. 他去大堡礁潜水了。|近义词：plunging, submerging, underwater swimming|反义词：surfacing, ascending|词性:noun


diving:/ˈdaɪvɪŋ/| adj. |潜水的，跳水的|Relating to or used for diving.|例句：He bought a new diving suit. 他买了一套新的潜水服。

例句：She made a perfect diving entry. 她做了一个完美的跳水动作。|近义词：submersible, underwater|反义词：surface|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dūfan (unattested) meaning "to dive, duck, stoop," from Proto-Germanic *dūbjaną, related to Old High German tūfan "to dip, sink."  The present participle "diving" came to be used as a noun and adjective.
