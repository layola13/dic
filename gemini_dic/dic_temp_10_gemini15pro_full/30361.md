centennials:/sɛnˈtɛniəlz/
noun
百年纪念; 百岁老人; 百年诞辰;  (尤指 1997 年至 2012 年间出生的人)千禧一代
People born between 1997 and 2012 are referred to as centennials.
1997 年至 2012 年间出生的人被称为千禧一代。
Centennials are transforming the consumer landscape.
千禧一代正在改变消费格局。
synonyms: Generation Z, Post-Millennials, iGeneration
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider previous generations like Millennials, Generation X, Baby Boomers)
n.
