de-Americanization: /diːəˈmɛrɪkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
| n. |去美国化；非美化|The process of reducing or eliminating American influence or characteristics, particularly in culture, politics, or economics.|The historian argued that the de-Americanization of European cinema was a complex process. 这位历史学家认为欧洲电影的去美国化是一个复杂的过程。|近义词：localization, indigenization|反义词：Americanization|词性:名词


词源:  Formed from "Americanization" with the prefix "de-" indicating removal or reversal.  "Americanization" itself comes from "American" + "-ization" (suffix forming nouns of process or result).
