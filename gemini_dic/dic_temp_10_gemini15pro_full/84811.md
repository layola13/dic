handwritings:/ˈhændˌraɪtɪŋz/| n. |笔迹（复数）|The plural form of "handwriting," referring to multiple instances of handwriting.  It refers to the distinctive style of writing by hand, encompassing the forms of letters, the overall slant and spacing, and other individual characteristics.|例句：The expert compared the handwritings on the two documents. 专家比较了这两份文件上的笔迹。|近义词：scripts, calligraphy (for artistic handwriting)|反义词: typing, print|noun
