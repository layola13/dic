overcuriosities:/ˌoʊvərˌkjʊəriˈɑːsətiz/
|n.| 过度好奇，过分好奇 | Excessive curiosity, an undue interest in the affairs of others. | Her overcuriosities led her to snoop through her neighbor's mail. 她的过度好奇让她偷看了邻居的邮件。 | 近义词: inquisitiveness, nosiness, prying | 反义词: indifference, apathy | 词性: 名词


词源:  From "over" (meaning excessive) + "curiosity" (from Late Latin "curiositas," meaning inquisitiveness).
