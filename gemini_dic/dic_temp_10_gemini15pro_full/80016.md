go-slow:/ˈɡəʊ sləʊ/ (UK) /ˈɡoʊ sloʊ/ (US)| n. |怠工；磨洋工|A deliberate reduction in the rate of production as a form of protest.|The workers staged a go-slow to protest against the low wages.|近义词：work-to-rule, slowdown, industrial action|反义词:speedup, Stakhanovite movement|noun

go-slow:/ˈɡəʊ sləʊ/ (UK) /ˈɡoʊ sloʊ/ (US)| adj. |怠工的；磨洋工的|Relating to or characterized by a go-slow.|The go-slow tactics of the employees were starting to affect production.|近义词：slow, deliberate|反义词:fast, rapid|adjective
