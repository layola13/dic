camber:/ˈkæmbər/ (US), /ˈkæmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|倾斜，拱起；使…倾斜，使…拱起|A slightly arched or convex shape, especially of a road or other surface.  To cause to have a camber. |The road has a slight camber to allow rainwater to run off. 这条路略微拱起以便雨水流走。|Synonyms: arch, curve, bend, slope |Antonyms: dip, hollow, depression|noun
