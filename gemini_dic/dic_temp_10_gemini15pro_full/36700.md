colliquable:/kəˈlɪkwəbl/ /kəˈlɪkwɪəbl/
adjective
可熔化的；可液化的
capable of being melted or liquefied.
The colliquable nature of the metal made it ideal for casting.  这种金属的可熔性使其成为铸造的理想选择。
The wax, once colliquable, can be molded into various shapes.  蜡一旦熔化，就可以塑造成各种形状。
近义词: fusible, meltable, liquefiable
反义词: infusible, non-meltable
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin colliquabilis, from colliquāre (“to melt, dissolve”), from com- (“together”) + liquare (“to liquefy”).
