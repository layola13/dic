deadlight:/ˈdɛdlaɪt/| n.|（舞台灯光）不透光挡板，遮光板|A shutter or screen used in a theatre to prevent light from spilling onto unwanted areas of the stage.|The stagehands adjusted the deadlights to focus the spotlight on the lead actor.  舞台工作人员调整遮光板，将聚光灯对准主角。|Synonyms:  screen,  blinder,  mask|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "floodlight")|Noun
