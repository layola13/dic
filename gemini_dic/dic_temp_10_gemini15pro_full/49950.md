dendrology: /dɛnˈdrɒlədʒi/ (UK) /dɛnˈdrɑːlədʒi/ (US)
n.|树木学|The scientific study of trees, including their taxonomy, identification, characteristics, distribution, and ecology.| He's studying dendrology at the university. 他在大学学习树木学。|Synonyms:  arboriculture, tree science|Antonyms: N/A|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δένδρον (déndron, “tree”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
