miltwaste:/ˈmɪltˌweɪst/ | n. | 鱼白; 精液;  |Semen of a male fish, especially when it contains sperm.  Can also refer to semen in general, but this usage is archaic and now considered vulgar. | The miltwaste from the salmon was used to fertilize the eggs.  鲑鱼的鱼白被用来给鱼卵受精。 | synonyms:  (for fish) sperm, roe, milt; (archaic/vulgar) semen, cum | antonyms: (none for fish usage; for archaic/vulgar usage)  None | noun
