calfskins:/ˈkɑːfˌskɪnz/ /ˈkæfˌskɪnz/
noun
小牛皮
the skin of a calf, typically used to make leather
The finest calfskins are used to create luxurious leather goods.  最优质的小牛皮被用来制作奢华的皮革制品。
Synonyms: calf leather, calf hide
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider) cowhide, mature hide
