midair:/ˈmɪdeər/ (US), /ˈmɪdeə(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.|半空中，空中|in the middle of the air; while in the air|The acrobat performed a daring flip in midair.  杂技演员在半空中表演了一个大胆的空翻。|
例句：
The bird caught the insect in midair.  鸟在半空中抓住了昆虫。

The two planes nearly collided in midair. 两架飞机几乎在半空中相撞。

|近义词：airborne, aloft|反义词:grounded, landed|词性:形容词，名词
