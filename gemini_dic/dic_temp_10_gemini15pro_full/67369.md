extensivities:/ɪkˌstɛnsɪˈvɪtiz/ | n. |广延性，广泛性|The quality of being extensive; extensiveness.  The properties of a system that depend on the size or amount of matter in the system, such as volume, mass, and entropy. | The extensivities of the system need to be considered when scaling up the process. 系统的广延性在扩大工艺规模时需要考虑。 | 近义词: extensiveness, scope, range, breadth | 反义词: intensiveness, limitation, restriction | 词性: 名词

词源: From extensive + -ity.  Ultimately from Latin extendere (to stretch out).
