febricitate: /ˈfɛbrɪˌkeɪt/ (also /ˈfiːbrɪˌkeɪt/)
verb
发烧; (尤指)装病
to have or cause to have a fever (especially to pretend to be ill)
He febricitated for three days after contracting the virus. 感染病毒后，他发烧了三天。
She was suspected of febricitating to avoid taking the exam.  她被怀疑装病以逃避考试。

近义词:  have a fever, be feverish, malinger (for pretending to be ill)
反义词: be healthy, be well, recover

词性: verb

词源: From Latin *febricitatus, past participle of febricitare ‘to have a fever’, from febris ‘fever’.
