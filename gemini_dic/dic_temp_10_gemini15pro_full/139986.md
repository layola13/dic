odour:/ˈəʊdə(r)/ (British) /ˈoʊdər/ (American)| n.|气味，香味，臭味|A distinctive smell, especially an unpleasant one. |例句：The odour of stale cigarette smoke hung in the air. 空气中弥漫着陈旧的香烟味。

例句：The flowers emitted a sweet odour. 这些花散发出甜美的香味。|近义词：smell, scent, aroma, fragrance (for pleasant smells), stink, stench (for unpleasant smells)|反义词:None (Odors can be pleasant or unpleasant, there isn't a direct opposite)|noun
