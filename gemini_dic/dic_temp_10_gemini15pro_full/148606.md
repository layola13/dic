overtenacious: /ˌoʊvərˈtɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
过于顽强的，过于执着的
holding on too firmly; too persistent
He was overtenacious in his pursuit of the job, calling the company repeatedly even after being rejected. 他在追求这份工作时过于执着，即使被拒绝后仍反复打电话给公司。
The lawyer's overtenacious questioning of the witness alienated the jury. 律师对证人过于执着的盘问疏远了陪审团。
近义词: persistent, pertinacious, dogged, tenacious
反义词: yielding, flexible, compliant, irresolute
词性: adjective
词源: From over- + tenacious, from Latin tenax, from tenere "to hold."
