electro-osmosis:/ɪˌlɛktroʊ ɒzˈmoʊsɪs/ /ɪˌlɛktroʊ ɒsˈmoʊsɪs/
noun
电渗
The movement of liquid through a porous material (such as a membrane or capillary) due to an applied electric field.
Electro-osmosis is used to remove water from walls and foundations. 电渗用于去除墙壁和地基中的水分。
Electro-osmosis is a key process in microfluidics. 电渗是微流体学中的一个关键过程。
synonyms: electroendosmosis
antonyms: N/A
