arrish:/ˈærɪʃ/| adj.| 粗野的，像流氓的|Resembling or characteristic of a ruffian; like a thug. |He had an arrish look about him, with his unshaven face and torn clothes. 他蓬头垢面，衣衫褴褛，一副流氓模样。|近义词：thuggish, hooliganish, ruffianly|反义词：gentlemanly, refined, courteous|词性:形容词

Etymology:  Likely a derivation of "arrant," meaning "downright; thoroughgoing," often used in a negative context (e.g., "arrant knave").  "Arrish" takes this sense of "thoroughgoing" and applies it to the qualities of a ruffian.  The suffix "-ish" indicates resemblance or characteristic of.
