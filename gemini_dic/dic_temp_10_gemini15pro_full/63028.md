enrages:/ɪnˈreɪdʒɪz/
verb
激怒，使暴怒
to fill with rage; to make furious
The unfair treatment enraged him. 不公平的待遇激怒了他。
近义词:infuriates, angers, maddens
反义词:calms, soothes, placates
词性:verb

enraged:/ɪnˈreɪdʒd/
adjective
被激怒的，暴怒的
filled with rage; furious
She was enraged by his lies. 她被他的谎言激怒了。
近义词:furious, angered, infuriated
反义词:calm, peaceful, tranquil
词性:adjective


词源: From Old French *enragier, from en- (“in, into”) + rager (“to rage”), from Latin rabies (“rage, madness”).
