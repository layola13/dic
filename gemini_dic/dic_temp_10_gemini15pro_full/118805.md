metamict:/ˌmɛtəˈmɪkt/ | adj. |  变晶质的；非晶质化的 |  Describing a mineral that has lost its crystalline structure due to radioactive decay, becoming amorphous.  The mineral appears outwardly crystalline but its internal structure is disordered. | The zircon was metamict due to prolonged exposure to radiation. 这块锆石由于长时间暴露于辐射而变成了变晶质。 |  amorphous, disordered, glassy | crystalline, ordered | adjective
