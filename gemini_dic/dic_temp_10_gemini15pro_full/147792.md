overprotecting:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtektɪŋ/
adjective
过度保护的
Protecting someone or something too much, often to the point of hindering their development or independence.
She's so overprotecting of her children that they never learn to take care of themselves. 她对孩子们过度保护，以至于他们从未学会照顾自己。
overprotective, cosseting, shielding, sheltering
neglectful, careless, indifferent
adjective
