lordkin:/ˈlɔːrdkɪn/
n.
封建领主的亲属;贵族;同族
A person of noble descent or of the same kinship as a lord.
The lordkin gathered for the annual feast at the castle.  封建领主的亲属聚集在城堡里参加年度盛宴。
He boasted of being a lordkin, descended from ancient royalty. 他吹嘘自己是贵族后裔，是古代皇室的后代。
近义词:noble, kinsman, relative
反义词:commoner, peasant
词性:名词

词源：
"lordkin" is a combination of "lord" and the diminutive suffix "-kin."  "Lord" comes from Old English "hlāford," meaning "bread keeper" or "loaf guardian."  "-kin" is a suffix indicating a smaller version or a familial relationship.
