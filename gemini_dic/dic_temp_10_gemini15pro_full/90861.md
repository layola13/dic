hungred:/ˈhʌŋɡərd/| adj.|饥饿的|Feeling or showing great desire or craving, especially for something non-material (like knowledge or experience).  Often used in a figurative sense, though archaic in common speech for literal hunger.|He was hungred for knowledge and spent hours in the library. 他渴望知识，在图书馆里待了几个小时。|近义词：eager, avid, yearning, craving|反义词：sated, satisfied, apathetic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *hungred, past participle of hungrian "to be hungry," from Proto-Germanic *hungrijanan, from *hungraz "hungry."
