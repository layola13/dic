hut-like:/ˈhʌtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像小屋的；简陋的
Resembling a hut; small and simple.
The hermit lived in a hut-like dwelling deep in the woods.  隐士住在森林深处的一个简陋的住所里。
The children built a hut-like structure out of branches and leaves. 孩子们用树枝和树叶搭建了一个像小屋一样的建筑。
近义词:  hut-shaped, primitive, rustic, basic
反义词:  grand, luxurious, elaborate, sophisticated
词性: adjective
