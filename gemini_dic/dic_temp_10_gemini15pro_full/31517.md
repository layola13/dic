charrs:/tʃɑːrz/| n. |红点鲑，炭火烤鱼|A group of freshwater fish in the salmon family (Salmonidae), typically found in cold, deep lakes.  They are closely related to trout and salmon, and are known for their bright red spots and often vibrant coloration during spawning season.|例句：The charrs in this lake are a unique subspecies. 这个湖里的红点鲑是一个独特的亚种。

例句：We caught several charrs while ice fishing. 我们在冰钓时钓到了几条红点鲑。|近义词：trout, salmon|反义词: (no direct antonym, could consider warm-water fish)|词性:noun
