indiscriminate:/ɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət/| adj.|不加选择的，不加区别的，肆意的，滥杀滥伤的|Done at random or without careful judgment.|The indiscriminate use of pesticides can harm the environment. 滥用杀虫剂会破坏环境。|Synonyms: random, haphazard, arbitrary, unselective, aimless|Antonyms: selective, discriminating, careful, discerning|adjective

indiscriminate:/ɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət/| adj.|不加选择的，不加区别的，肆意的，滥杀滥伤的|Not making or based on careful distinctions.|The indiscriminate bombing of the city caused many civilian casualties. 对该城市不加区分的轰炸造成了许多平民伤亡。|Synonyms: wholesale, sweeping, general, universal|Antonyms: selective, discriminating, targeted|adjective
