calculus: /ˈkælkjʊləs/ (US) /ˈkælkjələs/ (UK)| n. |微积分；结石|A branch of mathematics that deals with the finding and properties of derivatives and integrals of functions, by methods originally based on the summation of infinitesimal differences.  It has two main branches, differential calculus and integral calculus.|He's studying calculus in college. 他在大学里学习微积分。|Synonyms: computation, calculation |Antonyms: N/A |noun
