bisilicate:/baɪˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
n.|二硅酸盐|A salt containing two silicon atoms per molecule or unit cell.|Calcium bisilicate is a common component of cement. 硅酸二钙是水泥的常见成分。
近义词:disilicate|反义词:|词性:noun

bisilicate:/baɪˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
adj.|二硅酸的|Containing two silicon atoms per molecule or unit cell.|Bisilicate minerals are often found in igneous rocks. 二硅酸盐矿物经常在火成岩中发现。
