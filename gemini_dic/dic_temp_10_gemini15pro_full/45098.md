cuckolded:/ˈkʌkəldɪd/| adj.|被戴绿帽子的，妻子与人通奸的|A man is cuckolded when his wife has sex with another man. |例句：He felt cuckolded and humiliated after discovering his wife's affair.  他发现妻子有外遇后感到被戴绿帽子和羞辱。

例句：The play explores the themes of jealousy and betrayal in a cuckolded husband.  这出戏探讨了一个被戴绿帽子的丈夫的嫉妒和背叛主题。|近义词：cheated on, betrayed|反义词:faithful, loyal|词性:形容词

词源:
From cuckold + -ed.  Cuckold itself comes from the Old French cucuault, referring to the cuckoo bird, which lays its eggs in other birds' nests.  This analogy was used to describe a man whose wife has been unfaithful, as he is raising another man's child.
