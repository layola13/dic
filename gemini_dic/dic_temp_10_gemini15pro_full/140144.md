offbeatness:/ˌɔːfˈbiːtnəs/ | n. | 不落俗套，另类，反传统 |The offbeatness of her style made her stand out from the crowd.  她的风格不落俗套，使她从人群中脱颖而出。 |  The film's offbeatness is what attracted me to it in the first place.  这部电影的另类之处正是最初吸引我的地方。 |  unconventionality, eccentricity, quirkiness | conformity, conventionality | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From offbeat + -ness.  "Offbeat" itself comes from "off" + "beat," referring to being off the main beat or rhythm, hence unconventional.
