epigene:/ˈɛpɪˌdʒiːn/ | adj. | 表观遗传的 | Relating to or arising from non-genetic influences on gene expression. |  Epigenetic changes can be influenced by environmental factors. 表观遗传变化会受到环境因素的影响。|  Epigenetic modifications can be passed down through generations. 表观遗传修饰可以代代相传。 | 近义词: non-genetic, acquired | 反义词: genetic, inherited | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *epi- "upon, in addition to" + -genetic.  Coined by C. H. Waddington in 1942.
