oratory:/ˈɔːrətɔːri/ /ˈɔːrəˌtɔːri/
n.
雄辩术；演讲才能；演说；祈祷室
the art of public speaking, especially in a formal and eloquent manner; a small chapel, especially for private worship.
He displayed his oratory skills at the debate. 他在辩论中展现了他的演讲才能。
The king prayed in the small oratory. 国王在小祈祷室里祈祷。
eloquence, rhetoric, public speaking
inarticulacy, silence
noun
