aphanite:/ˈæfəˌnaɪt/| n. |隐晶岩，细晶岩|A fine-grained igneous rock whose individual mineral crystals are too small to be seen without the aid of a microscope.|The geologist identified the sample as an aphanite due to its fine texture.|Synonym: fine-grained rock|Antonym: phaneritic rock|noun
aphanitic:/ˌæfəˈnɪtɪk/| adj. |隐晶质的|Having a texture so fine-grained that the constituent minerals cannot be distinguished macroscopically.|The volcanic rock displayed an aphanitic texture.|Synonym: fine-grained|Antonym: phaneritic|adjective
