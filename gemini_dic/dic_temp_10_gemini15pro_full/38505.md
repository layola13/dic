conciliate: /kənˈsɪlieɪt/ | v. | 安抚，调解，平息 | To stop someone from being angry or discontented; placate; pacify. |  The government tried to conciliate the protesting workers by offering a small pay raise. 政府试图通过提供少量加薪来安抚抗议的工人。

The company offered a discount to conciliate customers affected by the shipping delays.  公司向受运输延误影响的顾客提供折扣以示安抚。

近义词: appease, placate, pacify, mollify, soothe | 反义词: antagonize, provoke, aggravate, incite | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conciliatus, past participle of conciliare (“to bring together, unite, win over”), from concilium (“council”).
