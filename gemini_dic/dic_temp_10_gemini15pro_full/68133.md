fahlunite:/ˈfɑːlunˌaɪt/ | n. |透辉石|A variety of iolite, a mineral of the cordierite group, containing magnesium, iron, and aluminum. It is typically found in metamorphic rocks. | Fahlunite is often found in association with garnet and staurolite. 透辉石常与石榴石和十字石伴生。| Synonyms: iolite, cordierite | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Falun, Sweden, where it was first discovered + -ite (suffix forming names of minerals).
