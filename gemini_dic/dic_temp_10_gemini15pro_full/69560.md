fellowshipping:/ˈfɛləʊʃɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 交誼，團契，友誼 | The act of sharing fellowship; companionship and close association with others, especially on a spiritual or religious basis. |  We enjoyed the fellowship of our church group. 我们享受着教会团体的友谊。 | synonyms: communion, companionship, camaraderie, brotherhood, sisterhood | antonyms: isolation, loneliness, alienation | noun

fellowshipping:/ˈfɛləʊʃɪpɪŋ/ | v. |  建立友誼，參與團契 | Engaging in fellowship; spending time with others in a friendly and supportive way. |  They were fellowshipping with other believers after the service.  他们在礼拜后与其他信徒联谊。 | synonyms: associating, socializing, fraternizing, bonding | antonyms: isolating, withdrawing, shunning | verb
