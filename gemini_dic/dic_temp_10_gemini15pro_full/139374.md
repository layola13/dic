obsequience:/ɒbˈsiːkwiəns/ /ɑːbˈsiːkwiəns/| n.|顺从，恭顺，殷勤|The waiter's obsequience was almost embarrassing.  服务员的殷勤几乎令人尴尬。|例句：He bowed his head in obsequience to the king. 他低下头，向国王表示恭顺。

例句：Her obsequience to her boss was sickening. 她对她老板的顺从令人作呕。

|近义词：submission, compliance, deference, servility|反义词：rebellion, defiance, disobedience|词性：名词

obsequious:/əbˈsiːkwiəs/ /ɑːbˈsiːkwiəs/| adj.|顺从的，恭顺的，殷勤的|He is obsequious to anyone in authority. 他对任何当权者都毕恭毕敬。|例句：The obsequious waiter hovered around their table, anticipating their every need.  殷勤的服务员在他们的桌子旁转来转去，预料着他们的每一个需求。

例句：His obsequious manner irritated her. 他那阿谀奉承的态度激怒了她。

|近义词：submissive, compliant, deferential, servile|反义词：rebellious, defiant, disobedient|词性：形容词
