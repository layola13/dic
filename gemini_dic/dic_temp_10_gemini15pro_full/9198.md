antioptimistic:/ˌæntiˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/
adjective
反乐观主义的; 悲观的
Expecting the worst; pessimistic.  Characterized by or displaying a lack of optimism.
She had an antioptimistic view of the future, always expecting disaster. 她对未来持反乐观态度，总是期待着灾难的发生。
He delivered an antioptimistic speech, warning of the challenges ahead. 他发表了一场反乐观主义的演讲，警告了未来的挑战。
近义词: pessimistic, cynical, gloomy, negative
反义词: optimistic, hopeful, positive
词性: adjective

词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposite of  + optimistic (adjective): hopeful and confident about the future.
