demilitarized:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/
adjective
非军事化的，解除武装的
An area from which military forces have been removed.
例句：The demilitarized zone between North and South Korea.  朝韩之间的非军事区。

例句：They agreed to demilitarize the region. 他们同意将该区域非军事化。

近义词：neutral, unarmed, disarmed
反义词：militarized, armed

词性：adjective
