paper-pushers:/ˈpeɪpərˌpʊʃərz/
noun
纸上谈兵者；文牍主义者；办公室人员（通常指不从事实际工作的人）
People who do a lot of paperwork in an office, but little practical work.  This term often carries a negative connotation, implying that their work is unimportant or bureaucratic.
The office was full of paper-pushers, not people who actually got things done.  办公室里都是些纸上谈兵的人，没有真正做事的人。
synonym: bureaucrat, office worker, administrator (neutral), functionary
antonym:  doer, go-getter, action-oriented person
n.
