antagonistical:/ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkəl/| adj.|敌对的，对抗性的|Showing or feeling active opposition or hostility towards someone or something.|The two countries have an antagonistic relationship. 这两个国家关系敌对。|近义词：hostile, opposed, inimical, adversarial|反义词：friendly, amicable, cooperative, supportive|词性:形容词
antagonism:/ænˈtæɡənɪzm/| n.|敌对，对抗|Active hostility or opposition.|There is a deep antagonism between the two groups.  这两个团体之间存在着深厚的敌意。|近义词：hostility, animosity, friction, conflict|反义词：friendship, amity, cooperation, harmony|词性:名词
antagonist:/ænˈtæɡənɪst/| n.|对手，敌手|A person who actively opposes or is hostile to someone or something.|He played the antagonist in the movie. 他在电影里扮演反派角色。|近义词：opponent, adversary, rival, foe|反义词：protagonist, ally, supporter, friend|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *antagonista, from Ancient Greek ἀνταγωνιστής (antagonistēs, “competitor, opponent, rival”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + ἀγωνίζομαι (agōnízomai, “I contend for a prize”).
