copiosities:/ˌkɒpiˈɒsɪtiz/
n.
丰富，大量；众多；丰裕
The state or quality of being copious; abundance.  A large quantity or number.
The writer displayed a copiousity of knowledge on the subject.  这位作家在该主题上展现了丰富的知识。
abundance, profusion, wealth, plentifulness
scarcity, dearth, lack, paucity
noun
