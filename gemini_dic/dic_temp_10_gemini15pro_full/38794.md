conductivity:/ˌkɑːndʌkˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌkɒndʌkˈtɪvɪti/| n. |电导率，导电性，传导率|The ability of a substance to transmit heat, electricity, or sound.| Copper has high electrical conductivity. 铜具有高导电性。|Synonyms: conductance, transmittance|Antonyms: resistance, insulation|noun

conductivity:/ˌkɑːndʌkˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌkɒndʌkˈtɪvɪti/| n. |导热性|The ability of a substance to transmit heat.|Diamond has very high thermal conductivity. 钻石具有很高的导热性。|Synonyms: thermal conductance|Antonyms: thermal resistance|noun
