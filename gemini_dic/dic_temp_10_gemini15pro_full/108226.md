laugh-out-loud:/ˌlæf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ /ˌlɑːf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ | adj. interj. initialism | 大声地笑；非常有趣 | Used to emphasize that something is very funny. Often used in online communication. |  例句：The comedian's jokes were laugh-out-loud funny.  这个喜剧演员的笑话非常有趣，让人捧腹大笑。 | 近义词：hilarious, funny, amusing | 反义词：serious, sad, boring | 词性: adjective, interjection, initialism
laugh-out-loud:/ˌlæf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ /ˌlɑːf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ | n. | 大笑；捧腹大笑 | The act of laughing out loud.  | 例句：The movie had several laugh-out-louds. 这部电影有几个让人捧腹大笑的地方。 | 近义词：guffaw, roar of laughter | 反义词：silence, whimper | 词性: noun
