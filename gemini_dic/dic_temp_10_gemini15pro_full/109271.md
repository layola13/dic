lemniscate: /lɛmˈnɪskət/ | n. | 双纽线，连珠线 | A plane curve shaped like a figure eight, generated by the locus of a point that moves so that the product of its distances from two fixed points is constant. | The lemniscate is a fascinating mathematical curve. 这条双纽线是一条引人入胜的数学曲线。| The orbit of a binary star system can sometimes resemble a lemniscate. 双星系统的轨道有时类似于双纽线。 | synonyms:  figure eight curve, analemma | antonyms:  straight line | noun
