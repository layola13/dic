inquisitorial:/ɪnˌkwɪzɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
审讯式的，爱打听的
Resembling or characteristic of an inquisitor; given to rigorous or harsh questioning.
The lawyer adopted an inquisitorial tone with the witness.  律师对证人采取了审讯式的语气。

The committee's questioning was quite inquisitorial. 委员会的提问非常具有审讯性质。

近义词:probing, inquiring, questioning, scrutinizing, interrogative, examining, investigative
反义词:forthright, reticent
词性:adjective

词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin inquisitorius, from inquisitor ‘inquirer’ (see inquisitor).
