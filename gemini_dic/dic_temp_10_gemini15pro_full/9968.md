aphidivorous: /ˌeɪfɪˈdɪvərəs/
adjective
食蚜的; 以蚜虫为食的
Feeding on aphids.
例句：Ladybugs are aphidivorous insects, which is why they are beneficial in gardens. 瓢虫是食蚜昆虫，这就是为什么它们在花园里是有益的。

例句：The aphidivorous larvae help control the aphid population. 食蚜的幼虫有助于控制蚜虫的数量。


近义词：aphid-eating
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective

词源：From  "aphid" + "-vorous" (eating).  "Aphid" comes from the Modern Latin "aphis," and "-vorous" comes from the Latin "vorare," meaning "to devour."
