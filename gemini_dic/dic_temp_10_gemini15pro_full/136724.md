nonsensiblenesses: /ˌnɒnsɛnsɪblənəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnsɛnsɪblənəsɪz/
n.
胡说八道；荒谬的想法；愚蠢的行为
The state or quality of being nonsensical; absurd or foolish words or actions.
His speech was full of nonsensiblenesses. 他的演讲充满了胡说八道。
近义词: absurdities, follies, inanities
反义词: sense, wisdom, rationality
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:  From nonsensical + -ness.  "Nonsensical" itself comes from "nonsense" + "-ical". "Nonsense" originates from "non-" (not) + "sense" (meaning, reason).
