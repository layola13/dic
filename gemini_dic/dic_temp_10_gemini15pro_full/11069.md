ETYMOLOGY->
From French *archéologue*, from Ancient Greek ἀρχαιολογία (arkhaiología, “antiquarian lore”), from ἀρχαῖος (arkhaîos, “ancient”) + -λογία (-logía, from λέγω (légō, “I speak”)).
