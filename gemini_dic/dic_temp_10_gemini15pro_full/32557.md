chippiest:/ˈtʃɪpiɪst/
adjective
最 impertinent 的；最 cheeky 的；最 saucy 的
Most impudent, impertinent, or cheeky.  Demonstrating a lively, and perhaps pert, self-confidence.
例句：He gave the chippiest response I've ever heard. 他给出了我听过的最 impertinent 的回复。

例句：She had a chippiest grin on her face as she delivered the sarcastic remark.  她说完讽刺的话后，脸上露出了最 impertinent 的笑容。

近义词：impudent, impertinent, cheeky, saucy, bold, brazen, sassy
反义词：respectful, polite, courteous, deferential, meek, timid
