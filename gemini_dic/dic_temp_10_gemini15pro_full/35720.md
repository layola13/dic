cocciferous:/ˌkɒkˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
含浆果的; 结球果的
Bearing cocci (berry-like fruits) or cones.
Coccifera oaks produce small acorns. 圣栎结出小橡子。
Synonyms: berry-bearing, cone-bearing, fruit-bearing
Antonyms: barren, sterile, infertile
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Latin *coccifer, from coccum (“berry”) + -fer (“bearing”).
