dichogamous:/daɪˈkɒɡəməs/| adj.|雌雄异熟的|Having the male and female reproductive organs maturing at different times, thus preventing self-fertilization.|Many plants are dichogamous, ensuring cross-pollination.|近义词：heterogamous|反义词：homogamous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dícha* (apart, asunder) + *gámos* (marriage)
