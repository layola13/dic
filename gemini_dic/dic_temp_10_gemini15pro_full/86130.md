hearteningly:/ˈhɑːrtnɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人鼓舞地，振奋人心| In a way that gives encouragement and hope. | The economy is hearteningly showing signs of recovery. 经济正令人鼓舞地显示出复苏的迹象。|  The team played hearteningly well despite the difficult circumstances. 尽管情况艰难，但球队的表现令人振奋。| 近义词: encouragingly, inspiringly | 反义词: discouragingly, depressingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From hearten + -ing + -ly.  "Hearten" comes from the Old English "hearty," meaning courageous or spirited.
