parolees:/pəˌroʊˈliːz/
|n.|假释犯（复数）|A person who has been released from prison on parole, meaning they are allowed to live in the community under certain conditions and supervision.|The parolees are required to meet with their parole officers regularly.|近义词：ex-convicts, probationers|反义词：inmates, prisoners|noun
