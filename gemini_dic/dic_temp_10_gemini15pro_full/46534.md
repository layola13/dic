dalan: /ˈdɑːlɑːn/
n.
covered walkway; veranda; porch; balcony
A covered walkway, often open on one side, typically found in traditional Indonesian or Malay architecture.
Example:  We sat on the dalan, enjoying the cool evening breeze. 我们坐在走廊上，享受着凉爽的晚风。
Example: The dalan was decorated with intricate carvings. 走廊装饰着复杂的雕刻。
Synonym: veranda, porch, balcony, gallery, loggia
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps) interior, inside


Etymology:
From Malay *dalan  "road, way, path”.
