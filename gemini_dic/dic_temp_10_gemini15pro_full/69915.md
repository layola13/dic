ferule:/ˈfɛruːl/ (US), /ˈfɛrʊl/ (UK)| n. |戒尺；惩罚；严厉的管教|A flat piece of wood or other rigid material used for hitting people as punishment, especially children in schools.|The teacher used a ferule to discipline unruly students. 老师用戒尺管教不守规矩的学生。|Synonyms: rod, cane, switch|Antonyms: reward, praise|noun


ferule:/ˈfɛruːl/ (US), /ˈfɛrʊl/ (UK)| v. |用戒尺打；严厉地管教|To punish with a ferule.|The headmaster feruled the boy for his disobedience. 校长用戒尺惩罚了这个男孩的违抗行为。|Synonyms: chastise, discipline, punish|Antonyms: reward, praise|verb
