comradeliness:/ˌkɒmˈreɪdlinəs/ /ˌkɑːmˈreɪdlinəs/| n. |同志情谊；友情；伙伴关系|The spirit of comradeliness filled the air as the volunteers worked together. |例句：The team demonstrated a strong sense of comradeliness throughout the project.  团队在整个项目中展现出强烈的同志情谊。

例句：There was a spirit of comradeliness among the soldiers. 士兵们之间有一种同志情谊。|近义词：friendship, camaraderie, fellowship, companionship|反义词:hostility, animosity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From comrade + -liness. Comrade comes from the Spanish "camarada," meaning roommate or cabin mate, ultimately from "cámara" meaning chamber or room.
