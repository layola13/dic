liberatory:/ˈlɪbərəˌtɔːri/
adjective
解放的，释放的
Bringing about liberation; freeing.
The liberatory power of education can transform lives.  教育的解放力量可以改变生活。
The fall of the Berlin Wall was a liberatory moment for many. 柏林墙的倒塌对许多人来说是一个解放的时刻。
Synonyms: emancipatory, freeing, liberating, redemptive, releasing
Antonyms: oppressive, restrictive, enslaving, confining
