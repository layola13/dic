aloof:/əˈluːf/ | adj. | 冷淡的，疏远的， aloof from |  Not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant. |  He stood aloof from the other guests at the party.  他在聚会上与其他客人保持距离。| synonyms: distant, remote, detached, standoffish, reserved, withdrawn | antonyms: involved, sociable, friendly, approachable, warm | adjective

aloof:/əˈluːf/ | adv. | 远离地， aloof from |  At a distance, especially in feeling or interest. | The cat sat aloof from the noisy children. 猫远离吵闹的孩子们坐着。 | synonyms: apart, aside, away | antonyms: near, close, together | adverb
