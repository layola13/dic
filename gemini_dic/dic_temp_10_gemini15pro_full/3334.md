afforestation:/əˌfɔːrɪˈsteɪʃən/ /əˌfɔːrəˈsteɪʃən/
noun
造林；植树造林；绿化
The establishment of a forest or stand of trees in an area where there was no previous tree cover.
例句：Afforestation projects are crucial for combating climate change.  造林项目对于应对气候变化至关重要。

例句：The government has initiated a large-scale afforestation program. 政府启动了一项大规模的造林计划。

近义词：forest planting, reforestation, tree planting
反义词：deforestation
词性：n.
