outfeeds:/ˈaʊtfiːdz/
noun
输出;输出物;输出量;输出设备
The process or equipment that moves finished products away from a machine or production line.
The outfeed of the sawmill was piled high with lumber.  锯木厂的输出物堆满了木材。
synonyms: output, product, production, delivery, discharge
antonyms: input, intake, feed
n.
