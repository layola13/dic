aviarists:/ˈeɪviərɪsts/
n.
养鸟者，鸟类饲养员
A person who keeps or breeds birds, especially as a hobby or profession.
The aviarists at the zoo specialize in breeding endangered parrots.  动物园的养鸟者专门饲养濒临灭绝的鹦鹉。
bird keepers, bird breeders, ornithologists
--- (no antonym applicable)
