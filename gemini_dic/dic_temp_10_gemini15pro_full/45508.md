cure-all:/ˈkjʊrˌɔːl/ (US), /ˈkjʊərˌɔːl/ (UK)
n.
万能药; 万灵丹
A remedy for all ills; a panacea.
例句：
He thought the new law would be a cure-all for the country's problems.
他认为这项新法律将是解决国家问题的万能药。

Many people consider money to be a cure-all, but it can't buy happiness.
许多人认为金钱是万能药，但它买不到幸福。

近义词：panacea, remedy, elixir, nostrum
反义词：
(No single antonym, but concepts like "specific remedy," "targeted treatment," or "ineffective solution" represent opposing ideas.)

词性: 名词
