neuropodous:/ˌnjʊərəˈpoʊdəs/ /ˌnʊərəˈpɒdəs/| adj.|神经足的，具有神经足的|Having or relating to neuropods, which are fleshy, unjointed leg-like appendages.  These are found in certain larval insects. |Example: The neuropodous larvae moved slowly across the leaf. 神经足的幼虫在叶子上缓慢移动。

Example:  Researchers studied the movement of the neuropodous insect. 研究人员研究了这种神经足昆虫的运动。|近义词:apodous (in the context of lacking true legs) |反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a highly specific term.  Perhaps "legged" in a very general sense.)|词性: adjective
