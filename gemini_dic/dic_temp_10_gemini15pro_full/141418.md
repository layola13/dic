ophiura:/ˌɒfiˈjʊərə/ | n. | 蛇尾纲动物; 蛇尾 | Brittle star. A marine invertebrate animal with a body like a flattened star, having five slender flexible arms. |  例句：The ophiura crawled across the seabed. 蛇尾在海底爬行。| 近义词：brittle star, serpent star | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Latin *ophiura, from Ancient Greek ὀφιοῦρος (ophioûros, “having a tail like a snake”), from ὄφις (óphis, “snake”) + οὐρά (ourá, “tail”).
