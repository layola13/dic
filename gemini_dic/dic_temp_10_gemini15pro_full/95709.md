incongruity:/ˌɪnkɒŋˈɡruːɪti/ /ˌɪnkɑːŋˈɡruːɪti/| n.|不协调，不一致，不相称|lack of harmony or agreement; inappropriateness|Her bright pink dress was an incongruity at the funeral. 她那件亮粉色的连衣裙在葬礼上显得格格不入。|Synonyms: disparity, inconsistency, incompatibility, mismatch, clash, discordance|Antonyms: harmony, consistency, agreement, compatibility, conformity|noun

incongruous:/ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəs/ /ɪnˈkɑːŋɡruəs/| adj.|不协调的，不一致的，不相称的|not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something.|His casual jeans and t-shirt looked incongruous with the formal setting of the awards ceremony.  他穿着休闲牛仔裤和T恤，与颁奖典礼的正式场合显得格格不入。|Synonyms: inappropriate, unsuitable, out of place, incompatible, inconsistent, discordant|Antonyms: appropriate, suitable, fitting, harmonious, consistent, compatible|adjective
