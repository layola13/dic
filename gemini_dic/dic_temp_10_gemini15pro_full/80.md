aardvark:/ˈɑːrdvɑːrk/ (US), /ˈɑːdvɑːk/ (UK)| n.|土豚|A medium-sized, burrowing, nocturnal mammal native to Africa. It has a long pig-like snout, large ears, and a powerful tail, and feeds on ants and termites.|We saw an aardvark on our safari. 我们在旅行中看到了一只土豚。

The aardvark uses its powerful claws to dig for insects. 土豚用它强壮的爪子挖昆虫。|近义词：antbear, anteater|反义词:None|词性:noun
