infamousness:/ˈɪnfəməsnəs/| n. |恶名昭彰，臭名昭著|The state of being infamous; notoriety, especially of a negative kind.  It refers to the quality of being well known for some bad quality or deed.|His infamousness spread throughout the land after the scandal.  丑闻之后，他的恶名传遍了全国。|近义词：notoriety, disrepute, ill fame|反义词：fame, renown, celebrity|词性:noun

Etymology: From infamous + -ness.  Infamous comes from Late Middle English, from medieval Latin *infamosus, from in- ‘not’ + Latin famosus ‘famous.’
