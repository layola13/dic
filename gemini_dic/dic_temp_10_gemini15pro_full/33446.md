churnable:/ˈtʃɜːrnəbəl/
adj.
可流失的，易变动的
Likely to churn; susceptible to churning.  Specifically referring to customers who are likely to stop using a service or switch providers.
Example:  The company identified a segment of churnable customers and offered them special incentives to stay. 这家公司识别出一部分容易流失的客户，并向他们提供特别奖励以挽留他们。

Example:  The telecommunications industry is known for its high churnable rate. 电信业以其高客户流失率而闻名。
近义词:volatile, fickle, unstable, fluid
反义词:loyal, stable, committed, steadfast
词性:adjective
