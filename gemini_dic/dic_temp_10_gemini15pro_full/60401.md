eidolon: /ˈaɪdəlɒn/ (UK) /ˈaɪdlən/ (US) | n. | 幻象，幽灵，理想化的形象 | An image, apparition, or ideal; a phantom.  |  例句：The poet's lost love became an eidolon that haunted his later works. 这位诗人失去的爱成为了萦绕在他后期作品中的幻象。

例句：He chased the eidolon of his perfect career, never finding true satisfaction. 他追逐着理想职业的幻象，却从未找到真正的满足感。

近义词：phantom, apparition, ghost, specter, vision, ideal | 反义词：reality | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek εἴδωλον (eídōlon, “image, likeness, phantom”), diminutive of εἶδος (eîdos, “form, shape, appearance”).
