bitmap:/ˈbɪtmæp/| n. |位图|A representation in which each item corresponds to one or more bits of data, especially such a representation within a computer's memory of the pixels that make up an image displayed on a screen.|The image was stored as a bitmap.|近义词：raster image, pixel map|反义词:vector graphic|名词
