helveti:/hɛlˈvɛːtɪ/
n.|赫维提卡（字体）|A typeface with a clean, sans-serif design. |The designer chose Helvetica for the logo because of its modern look.| 近义词:Arial, Univers|反义词:Times New Roman|词性:名词
词源:From the Latin name for Switzerland, "Helvetia".
