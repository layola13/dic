howdahs: /ˈhaʊdɑːz/ | n. | 象轿，象鞍 | A seat, usually covered and canopied, placed on the back of an elephant for riding. |  The maharaja rode through the streets on an elephant, sitting in a magnificent howdah.  大君坐在华丽的象轿里，骑着大象穿过街道。

The howdahs were decorated with intricate carvings and colorful fabrics.  这些象轿装饰着复杂的雕刻和五彩缤纷的织物。 | 近义词: elephant saddle | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

词源: From Hindi हौदा (haudā), from Arabic هودج (hawdaj).
