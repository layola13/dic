divestiture:/daɪˈvɛstɪtʃər/ | n. |剥离，出售，放弃 |The act of selling off or disposing of assets, such as a subsidiary company or a product line.  |  The company announced the divestiture of its non-core businesses.  该公司宣布剥离其非核心业务。 | Synonym: disposal, sale, shedding | Antonym: acquisition, purchase, investment | noun

divest:/daɪˈvɛst/ | vt. |剥夺，使摆脱，脱去 | To deprive or dispossess, especially of property, authority, or title; to rid or free oneself of something. | The court divested him of his parental rights. 法院剥夺了他的父母权利。| Synonym: deprive, strip, rid | Antonym: invest, endow, grant | verb
