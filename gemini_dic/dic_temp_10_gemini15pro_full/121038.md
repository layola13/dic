misconception:/ˌmɪskənˈsɛpʃən/ | n. | 误解，错误观念 | A view or opinion that is incorrect because based on faulty thinking or understanding. |  There's a common misconception that all Scotsmen wear kilts.  人们普遍误认为所有苏格兰男人都穿苏格兰短裙。

There is a widespread misconception that eating fat makes you fat.  有一种普遍的误解，认为吃脂肪会使你发胖。

| 近义词：fallacy, delusion, misapprehension, misunderstanding | 反义词：correct understanding, accurate perception, truth | 词性: noun
