howled:/haʊld/| v.|嚎叫，怒吼；高声叫喊|emitted a long, loud, mournful cry, like a dog or wolf; cried out loudly and unrestrainedly|The dog howled at the moon. 狗对着月亮嚎叫。

The wind howled through the trees. 风在树林中呼啸。|近义词：cried, yelled, shrieked, wailed, roared|反义词:whispered, murmured|词性:动词
词源：From Middle English *houlen, from Old English *hūlian (“to howl”), from Proto-Germanic *hūlōną (“to howl, cry out”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to shout, make a noise”). Cognate with Dutch huilen (“to cry, weep”), German heulen (“to howl”), Swedish yla (“to howl”).
