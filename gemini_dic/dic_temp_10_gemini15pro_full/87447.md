hesitatively:/ˈhezɪtətɪvli/| adv.|犹豫地，迟疑地|In a way that shows hesitation; uncertainly or tentatively.|She hesitantly agreed to go with him. 她犹豫地同意和他一起去。|近义词：tentatively, uncertainly, cautiously, reluctantly|反义词：confidently, decisively, resolutely|词性:副词
词源: From the Latin word "haesitare" meaning "to stick fast, remain fixed, be undecided."
