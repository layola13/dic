hydriote:/ˈhaɪdriət/ | n. |居民，水生动物|A person or animal that lives in or near water, specifically a native or inhabitant of Hydra, a Greek island.|例句：The hydriote fishermen knew the waters well.  这些伊兹拉岛的渔民对水域非常熟悉。

例句：The small hydriote community maintained their traditions for centuries.  这个小小的伊兹拉岛社区保持了他们几个世纪的传统。|近义词：inhabitant, resident, native, local |反义词:foreigner, alien|词性:noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Hydriotes*, from Ancient Greek Ὕδρα (Hýdra, “Hydra”) + -ώτης (-ṓtēs, masculine agent noun suffix).
