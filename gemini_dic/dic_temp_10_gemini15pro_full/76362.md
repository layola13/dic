gadgeteers:/ˌɡædʒɪˈtɪərz/
n.
小玩意儿爱好者；精通小玩意儿的人
People who are enthusiastic about and skilled with gadgets, especially electronic or mechanical devices.
The gadgeteers were thrilled with the new virtual reality headset.  小玩意儿爱好者对新的虚拟现实耳机感到兴奋。
These gadgeteers are always the first to try out the latest tech. 这些精通小玩意儿的人总是最先尝试最新的科技产品。
近义词:technophiles, inventors, hobbyists, tinkerers
反义词: Luddites, technophobes
词性:noun
