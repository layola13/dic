crumenal: /ˈkruːmɪnəl/
adjective
钱包的；关于钱包的
Relating to a purse or pouch, especially a bird's crop.
The bird regurgitated food from its crumenal sac. 这只鸟从它的嗉囊里反刍食物。
近义词: pouch-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "gastric" if referring to digestion)
词性: adjective
