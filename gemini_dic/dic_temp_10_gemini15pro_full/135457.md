词源：
"Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not". "Pervasive" comes from the Latin "pervadere" (to pass through, permeate), from "per" (through) + "vadere" (to go). Therefore, "nonpervasive" literally means "not passing through" or "not spreading throughout".  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "nonpervasive" into the adverb "nonpervasively".
