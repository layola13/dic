displeaser:/dɪsˈpliːzər/
n.
令人不快的人或事物
Something or someone that causes displeasure or disapproval.
His constant complaining made him a displeaser to everyone around him. 他不断的抱怨使他成为周围每个人都不喜欢的人。
近义词：annoyance, irritant, bother, nuisance
反义词：pleaser, delight
