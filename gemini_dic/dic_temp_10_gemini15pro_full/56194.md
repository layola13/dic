dodgem: /ˈdɒdʒəm/ (UK) /ˈdɑːdʒəm/ (US) | n. | 碰碰车 | A small electric car driven at an amusement park, designed to be bumped into other similar cars. |  例句：The children loved riding the dodgems at the fair. 孩子们喜欢在游乐场玩碰碰车。

例句：We spent hours laughing and bumping into each other on the dodgems. 我们花了几个小时在碰碰车上互相碰撞，笑着玩耍。

近义词：bumper car | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
