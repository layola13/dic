non-vintage:/nɒnˈvɪntɪdʒ/ | adj. | 非年份的，非特定年份的 | Not of a particular vintage, especially of wine.  Referring to a wine that is not from a specific year, but rather a blend of grapes from different years. |  We opted for a non-vintage champagne for the toast. 我们选择了非年份香槟来祝酒。| Synonyms: blended, multi-vintage | Antonyms: vintage | adjective

non-vintage:/nɒnˈvɪntɪdʒ/ | adj. | 非年份的，非特定年份的 |  Referring to something (other than wine) that is not associated with a particular era or period and therefore considered timeless or classic.  | Her style is non-vintage; she always looks modern. 她的风格是非年份的；她看起来总是很现代。 | Synonyms: timeless, classic, ageless | Antonyms: dated, old-fashioned | adjective
