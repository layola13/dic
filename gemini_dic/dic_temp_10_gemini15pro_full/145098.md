overappraises:/ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪz/| v.|高估；过高评价|To estimate the value of something as greater than its true worth.|He overappraised the value of his old car. 他高估了他的旧车的价值。|The auction house overappraised the painting, and it failed to sell.  拍卖行对这幅画的估价过高，导致它没能售出。|近义词：overestimate, overvalue|反义词: underestimate, undervalue|动词
