fraternally:/frəˈtɜːrnəli/
adverb
兄弟般地；友爱地
In a brotherly manner; showing brotherly love.
He greeted his fellow lodge members fraternally. 他兄弟般地问候了他的同屋成员。
近义词: brotherly, amicably, cordially
反义词: coldly, hostilely
词性: 副词

fraternally:/frəˈtɜːrnəli/
adverb
友爱地
In a friendly and supportive way, like brothers.
They closed their letter fraternally, "Yours in brotherhood." 他们在信的结尾友爱地写道：“兄弟情谊永存”。
近义词: warmly, affectionately
反义词: unfriendlily, unkindly
词性: 副词


词源:
From Late Latin *fraternaliter, from Latin fraternus (“brotherly”), from frater (“brother”).
