nonoffers:/ˈnɒnˌɒfəz/ |n.| 非报价；未提出报价；未提供之物 |  Refers to situations where offers have not been made, or items or services that have not been offered.  |  The company received many inquiries but few nonoffers turned into actual orders.  这家公司收到了很多询价，但很少有未报价转化为实际订单。 | Near synonyms:  unoffered, unavailable, lacking | Antonyms: offers, proposals, bids | noun


nonoffers:/ˈnɒnˌɒfəz/ |n.| 未录用；未录取 | Refers to situations where candidates have not been offered a position or admission. | Many applicants received nonoffers after the interview process. 许多申请人在面试过程后没有收到录取通知。 | Near synonyms: rejections, declinations | Antonyms: acceptances, offers | noun


Etymology: "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "offers" (plural of "offer")
