curialistic: 教廷的；教皇法院的；罗马教廷的
curialistic: Relating to the papal court or the Curia Romana, the administrative apparatus of the Holy See.
curialistic: The archbishop's curialistic pronouncements alienated many of the more liberal members of the diocese.  大主教教廷式的声明疏远了许多教区中较为自由主义的成员。
curialistic: The new regulations reflected a more curialistic approach to church governance. 新的规章制度反映了教会管理中更加教廷式的方法。
curialistic: 近义词: papal, Vatican, Roman
curialistic: 反义词: secular, lay
curialistic: 词性: adjective
