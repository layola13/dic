congealableness:/kənˌdʒiːləbəlnəs/
n.
凝固性，易凝固性
The property of a substance that allows it to congeal; the readiness with which a substance congeals.
The congealableness of the gravy surprised me; it set much faster than I expected. 肉汁的凝固性让我吃惊；它凝固得比我预料的快得多。
近义词：coagulability, solidifiability
反义词：fluidity, liquidity
词性：noun

Etymology:
From congeal + -able + -ness.  Congeal comes from Old French congeler, from Latin congelāre "to freeze," from com- "together" + gelāre "to freeze."
