coursework: /ˈkɔːrswɜːrk/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrswɜːrk/ (US)
noun
课业；课程作业
written or practical work done by a student during a course of study, which is assessed to form part of the student's final grade.
Example: The coursework for this semester includes writing a research paper and completing a final exam.  这学期的课业包括写一篇研究论文和完成期末考试。

Example:  She was struggling to keep up with the heavy coursework load. 她很难跟上繁重的课业负担。

synonym:  assignments, homework, studies, schoolwork
antonym: leisure, free time, recreation
n.
