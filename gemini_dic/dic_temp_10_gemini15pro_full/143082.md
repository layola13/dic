outbrawls:/ˌaʊtˈbrɔːlz/
verb
胜过争吵；在争吵中胜过
To surpass or defeat someone in brawling; to outdo in noisy, quarrelsome behavior.
The seasoned politician outbrawled his younger opponent, silencing him with a barrage of witty insults.  这位经验丰富的政治家在争吵中胜过了他年轻的对手，用一连串机智的侮辱让他哑口无言。
He outbrawled the other patrons at the bar, eventually getting them kicked out. 他在酒吧里与其他顾客争吵，最终把他们赶了出去。
近义词: outfight, overpower, vanquish, subdue
反义词: lose to, surrender, yield, submit
词性: verb

outbrawl:/ˌaʊtˈbrɔːl/
noun
胜过争吵的行为
The act of outbrawling someone.
His outbrawl of the other negotiators secured him the best deal. 他在与其他谈判代表的争吵中胜出，为他赢得了最好的协议。
The witness described a noisy outbrawl outside the bar just before the shooting. 目击者描述了枪击发生前酒吧外发生的一场喧闹的争吵。
近义词: conquest, triumph, victory
反义词: defeat, loss, failure
词性: noun
