dull:/dʌl/ | adj. | 钝的，迟钝的，无聊的，阴暗的 | lacking sharpness or brightness; mentally slow; lacking interest or excitement; (of the weather) overcast; not bright | The knife was too dull to cut the rope. 这把刀太钝了，无法切断绳子。 | synonyms: blunt, boring, tedious, gloomy, overcast | antonyms: sharp, interesting, exciting, bright, sunny | adjective

dull:/dʌl/ | verb | 使钝，使迟钝，使无聊 | to make or become less sharp, bright, or sensitive |  The constant rain dulled the colors of the landscape.  连绵不断的雨水使景色的色彩变得暗淡。 | synonyms: blunt, dampen, deaden | antonyms: sharpen, brighten, enliven | verb
