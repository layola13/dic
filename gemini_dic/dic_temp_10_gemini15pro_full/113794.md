macruroid:/məˈkruːrɔɪd/| adj. n.|鼠尾状的，鼠尾类动物|Having a long, tapering tail like a rat; relating to or belonging to the Macruroidea, a group of deep-sea crustaceans.|The macruroid fish darted through the coral. 这条鼠尾状的鱼在珊瑚中飞快地游动。|近义词：long-tailed, rat-tailed|反义词:short-tailed|adjective, noun


macruroid:/məˈkruːrɔɪd/| n.|鼠尾类动物|A crustacean of the group Macruroidea, typically a deep-sea prawn or shrimp.|Macruroids are important components of deep-sea ecosystems. 鼠尾类是深海生态系统的重要组成部分。|近义词：deep-sea prawn, deep-sea shrimp|反义词: - |noun
