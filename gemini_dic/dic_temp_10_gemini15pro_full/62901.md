一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在看什么电视节目？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? 你最近学了什么？
What has she done today? 她今天做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你这周一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? 你生病前吃了什么？
What had she done before she left? 她离开前做了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she study in college? 她大学里要学什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference? 她将在会议上展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have finished reading by next month? 到下个月她将读完什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? 如果她知道真相，她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What time is the meeting? 会议是什么时候？
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
