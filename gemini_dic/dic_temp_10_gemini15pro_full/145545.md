overcommonness:/ˌoʊvərˈkɒmənəs/ | n. | 过于常见；过度普遍 | The overcommonness of the name "John" makes it less appealing to some parents.  约翰这个名字过于常见，使得它对一些父母来说不那么有吸引力了。|  The overcommonness of pigeons in the city park makes them almost invisible. 城市公园里鸽子的过度普遍使得它们几乎被人忽视。| 近义词：ubiquity, prevalence, commonality | 反义词：rarity, scarcity, uncommonness | 词性：名词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "common" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)  由表示“过度”的前缀 "over-" 加上 "common"（常见的）和后缀 "-ness"（由形容词构成名词）组成。
