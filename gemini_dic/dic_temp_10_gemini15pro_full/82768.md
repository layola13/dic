guilloche:/ɡɪˈloʊʃ/ | n. | ギヨシェ模様,扭索饰| An ornamental pattern of interlaced and waving lines, often engraved on metal or other materials, especially used in security printing to prevent counterfeiting. | The watch face features a delicate guilloche pattern.  这款手表的表面饰有精致的扭索饰。 | synonyms: engine turning, cross-hatching | antonyms: plain, unadorned | noun

guilloche:/ɡɪˈloʊʃ/ | v. |  ギヨシェ模様を施す,用扭索饰装饰| To decorate with a guilloche pattern. | The craftsman guilloched the silver plate with a complex design. 工匠在银盘上雕刻了复杂的扭索饰。| synonyms: engrave, etch | antonyms: erase, smooth | verb
