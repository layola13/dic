nonassimilations:/ˌnɒnəˌsɪmɪˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 非同化，非同化作用 | The process or fact of not being assimilated; lack of assimilation. Specifically (Phonetics): the absence of assimilation between sounds. | The nonassimilations of immigrant groups can lead to social divisions. 移民群体的非同化会导致社会分裂。|  The speaker's nonassimilations of certain sounds marked him as a foreigner.  说话者对某些声音的非同化表明他是一个外国人。 | synonyms: differences, distinctions, dissimilarities | antonyms: assimilations, integrations, homogenizations | noun
