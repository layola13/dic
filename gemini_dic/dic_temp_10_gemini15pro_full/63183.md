entangleable:/ɪnˈtæŋɡləbl/
|adj.|可缠绕的，易纠缠的|Capable of being entangled; easily tangled or interwoven.|The fisherman's net was entangleable and easily snagged on rocks. 渔夫的网很容易缠绕，容易被岩石钩住。|近义词：tangible, snarlable|反义词: smooth, disentangled|词性:形容词
词源: From entangle + -able.  Entangle comes from the Old French word "entangler," meaning "to ensnare or entrap," from "en-" (in) + "tangler" (to tangle).
