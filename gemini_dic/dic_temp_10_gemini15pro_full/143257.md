outdeviled:/ˌaʊtˈdɛvəld/
verb
胜过，比…更厉害
surpassed someone in devilish cleverness or wickedness; outdo in deviltry.
例句：She outdeviled him in the negotiation, securing a much better deal for herself.  她在谈判中比他更胜一筹，为自己争取到了更好的交易。

例句：He outdeviled his competitors with a cunning marketing strategy. 他以巧妙的营销策略战胜了竞争对手。

近义词：outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted
