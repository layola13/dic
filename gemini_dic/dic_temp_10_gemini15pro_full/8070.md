anthropomorphist: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪst/ | n. |拟人化者，赋予人性者|A person who attributes human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.|例句：The writer is an anthropomorphist who gives all her animal characters human emotions.  这位作家是一位拟人论者，她赋予了她所有的动物角色人类的情感。

例句：As an anthropomorphist, he believed that his dog experienced the world in much the same way he did. 作为一名拟人论者，他相信他的狗体验世界的方式与他大致相同。

近义词：humanizer, personifier |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

anthropomorphic: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. |拟人的，赋予人性的|Attributing human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.|例句：The children's book featured anthropomorphic animals who wore clothes and went to school. 这本儿童读物以拟人化的动物为主角，它们穿着衣服去上学。

例句：Many ancient cultures had anthropomorphic gods, depicted with human forms and emotions. 许多古代文化都有拟人化的神，被描绘成具有人类的形态和情感。

近义词：humanlike, personified |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective
