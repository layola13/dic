betr:/ˈbetər/ (US), /ˈbetə(r)/ (UK)| adj. adv. n. v.| 更好的；较好的；更；胜过；改善；改进|  From Old English *betera, comparative of *bōt "remedy, help, advantage," from Proto-Germanic *batiz "better," from PIE root *bha- "to appear, shine, gleam." Originally meant "of higher quality or value," later also "more desirable, preferable, more suitable."|

例句：
My new car is better than my old one.
我的新车比我的旧车好。

This coffee is better than the one we had yesterday.
这咖啡比我们昨天喝的好。

近义词：superior, finer, improved, enhanced
反义词：worse, inferior, poorer

词性: adjective
