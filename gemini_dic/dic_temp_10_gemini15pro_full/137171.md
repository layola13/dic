nonsuccessional:/ˌnɑːnsəkˈseʃənəl/
adjective
非演替的，非连续的
Not relating to or involved in ecological succession, the gradual process of change in an ecosystem.  It describes communities or species that do not typically follow predictable patterns of replacement over time.
The presence of invasive species can create nonsuccessional plant communities.  入侵物种的存在会造成非演替的植物群落。
These isolated ponds supported nonsuccessional communities of aquatic invertebrates. 这些孤立的池塘支持着非连续的水生无脊椎动物群落。
Near Antonyms: successional, seral
Antonyms: climax
词性: adjective
