loosened:/ˈluːsənd/| adj.|放松的，松开的|Made less tight or firm; also, less strict or severe.|The screws on the door handle had loosened over time, making it wobbly. 门把手上的螺丝随着时间的推移松动了，使它摇摇晃晃。|The government has loosened its restrictions on foreign travel. 政府放松了对外国旅行的限制。|Synonyms: relaxed, slack, unbound, unfastened|Antonyms: tightened, fastened, secured|verb (past participle)
