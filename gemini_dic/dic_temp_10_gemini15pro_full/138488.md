novena:/nəˈviːnə/| n.|九日敬礼，连续九天的祈祷|A novena is a series of prayers or services held over nine days.  九日敬礼是指连续九天举行的一系列祈祷或仪式。|My grandmother is saying a novena for my sick uncle. 我的祖母正在为我生病的叔叔做九日祈祷。|The family observed a novena for their deceased grandmother. 这家人为他们已故的祖母举行了九日祈祷。|prayer, devotion| - |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *novenarius* (“of nine”), from *noveni* (“nine each”), from *novem* (“nine”).  源自拉丁语 *novenarius*（“九的”），来自 *noveni*（“每个九个”），来自 *novem*（“九”）。
