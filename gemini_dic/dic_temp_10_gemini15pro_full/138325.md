/nəʊts/ | /noʊts/ | n. | 笔记，记录；注释；纸币；音符；调子 | A brief record of something written down to assist the memory or for future reference. |  He took notes during the lecture. 他在讲座期间做了笔记。 | notes, records, annotations, memos |  - | noun


/nəʊts/ | /noʊts/ | v. | 注意到；记录；指出 |  To observe or recognize something. | She noted his unusual behavior. 她注意到了他不寻常的行为。 | observe, perceive, remark, record | ignore, overlook | verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "note" (noun), and "noter" (verb), derived from Latin "nota" meaning "mark, character, sign".  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*gnō-" meaning "to know".
