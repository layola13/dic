corbel: /ˈkɔːrbl/ (US) /ˈkɔːbəl/ (UK)| n. | 肘托；托臂石；（墙上伸出的）梁托；|A projecting architectural member used as a support, often carved or decorated.  A corbel is a structural piece of stone, wood, or brick jutting from a wall to carry a superincumbent weight, a type of bracket.| The ornate corbels supporting the balcony were a striking feature.  装饰华丽的托臂石支撑着阳台，是一个引人注目的特点。| synonyms: bracket, support, console, cantilever | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps recess) | noun

corbels: /ˈkɔːrblz/ (US) /ˈkɔːbəlz/ (UK)| n. (plural)| 肘托；托臂石；（墙上伸出的）梁托；|Plural form of corbel.
