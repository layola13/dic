nonroyalists:/ˌnɒnˈrɔɪəlɪsts/
noun
非保皇党人；非王室成员
People who are not royalists; those who do not support a monarchy.
During the English Civil War, the nonroyalists, also known as Parliamentarians, fought against the King.  在英国内战期间，非保皇党人，也被称为议会派，与国王作战。
The nonroyalists gained control of the government. 非保皇党人控制了政府。
synonyms: republicans, parliamentarians, anti-monarchists
antonyms: royalists, monarchists
