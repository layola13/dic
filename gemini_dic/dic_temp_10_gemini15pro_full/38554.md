conclusive:/kənˈkluːsɪv/| adj.|结论性的，决定性的，最后的，确凿的|Serving as a conclusion; final.  Decisive; indisputable.|The evidence was conclusive and the jury reached a guilty verdict.  证据确凿，陪审团作出了有罪判决。|近义词：definitive, decisive, final, irrefutable|反义词：indecisive, inconclusive, ambiguous|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Late Middle English: from late Latin conclusivus, from concludere ‘to conclude’.
