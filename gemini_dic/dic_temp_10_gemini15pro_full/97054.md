inference:/ˈɪnfərəns/| n.|推论，推断，推理；结论|A conclusion reached on the basis of evidence and reasoning.|From the data collected, the scientists were able to make several inferences about the planet's atmosphere. 从收集到的数据中，科学家们能够对地球的大气层做出一些推论。|synonyms: deduction, conclusion, reasoning, assumption, presumption|antonyms: fact, proof, evidence|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French inference, from Latin inferentia, from inferre ‘bring in, allege’.
