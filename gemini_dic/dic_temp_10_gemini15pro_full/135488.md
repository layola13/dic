nonphilosophy:/ˌnɒn fɪˈlɒsəfi/ | n. |非哲学|A term coined by François Laruelle designating a practice of thought that claims to be prior to philosophy, challenging its foundational assumptions and methods. It aims to describe the Real without resorting to philosophical concepts or systems.| Laruelle's non-philosophy seeks to break free from the constraints of philosophical discourse. 拉鲁埃尔的非哲学试图摆脱哲学话语的限制。| near synonyms: non-standard philosophy, anti-philosophy | antonyms: philosophy, metaphysics | noun
