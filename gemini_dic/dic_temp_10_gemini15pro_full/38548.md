concludingly:/kənˈkluːdɪŋli/
adverb
最后地；总结性地
in a way that concludes something; lastly; finally
Concludingly, I would like to thank everyone for their participation. 最后，我要感谢大家的参与。
The lawyer concludingly summarized the evidence. 律师最后总结了证据。
近义词: finally, lastly, in conclusion, to conclude, in summary
反义词: initially, firstly, at first, in the beginning
