gutturally:/ˈɡʌtərəli/| adv.|喉音地，粗哑地|Produced in the throat; harsh-sounding.|He spoke gutturally, his voice thick with emotion. 他说话声音粗哑，充满了感情。| The singer gutturally screamed the lyrics. 歌手用喉音嘶吼着歌词。 |近义词：hoarsely, roughly, throatily|反义词：smoothly, melodically|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *gutturalis, from Latin guttur ("throat").
