nonabsorbencies:/ˌnɑːnəbˈzɔːrbənsiz/
noun
非吸收性；不吸收性
The nonabsorbencies of the material make it ideal for outdoor use. 这种材料的非吸收性使它非常适合户外使用。
The researchers tested the nonabsorbencies of various fabrics. 研究人员测试了各种织物的非吸收性。
non-absorbency (singular form)
absorbencies, absorption
noun

Etymology:
From non- + absorbency.  Absorbency from absorb + -ency. Absorb ultimately from Latin absorbēre, from ab- "from, away" + sorbēre "to suck up."
