campion:/ˈtʃæmpiən/ (US), /ˈtʃæmpɪən/ (UK)| n. adj.|冠军; 拥护者; 捍卫者|A person who has defeated or surpassed all rivals in a competition, especially in sports; a person who fights or argues in support of a cause or person.|例句：He is the reigning champion. 他是卫冕冠军。|例句：She was a champion for women's rights. 她是一位女权的捍卫者。|近义词：winner, victor, advocate, supporter|反义词：loser, opponent, detractor|词性:名词

campion:/ˈtʃæmpiən/ (US), /ˈtʃæmpɪən/ (UK)| adj.|优胜的;一流的|Especially of a plant or animal : of superior quality : first-rate|例句：A champion bull. 一头优胜的公牛。|例句：Champion roses. 一流的玫瑰。|近义词：excellent, first-rate, top-notch|反义词：inferior, poor|词性:形容词

词源：From Old French *champion, from Late Latin campionem (nominative campio) "gladiator, fighter in the campus," from Latin campus "field, level space."  The sporting sense is from 1590s.


注意事项:
* champion 既可以作名词也可以作形容词。
* 作为名词时，它可以指比赛的冠军，也可以指某项事业的拥护者或捍卫者。
* 作为形容词时，它通常用来描述动植物的品质优良，意为“优胜的”或“一流的”。
