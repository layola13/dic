obscurest:/əbˈskjʊərɪst/
adjective
最模糊的；最晦涩的
most obscure; most unclear or most difficult to understand.
The author's meaning was obscurest in the final chapter.  作者的意思在最后一章中最晦涩难懂。
The museum houses some of the obscurest artifacts known to man.  博物馆收藏了一些人类已知的最鲜为人知的文物。
近义词: vaguest, murkiest, most ambiguous, most enigmatic
反义词: clearest, most obvious, most transparent, most explicit
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *obscur, from Anglo-Norman obscur, from Latin obscūrus (“dark, dim, dusky; unclear, obscure; hidden, secret; inconspicuous, humble, unknown”), from ob (“over, against, before; towards, to; about, concerning; on account of, for; according to; as far as; down, downwards”) + scūrus (“covered”).
