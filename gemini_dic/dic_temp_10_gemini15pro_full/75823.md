fumigated:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪtɪd/
adjective
熏蒸过的
exposed to smoke or fumes, especially for disinfection.
The warehouse was fumigated to eliminate pests.  仓库经过熏蒸以消除害虫。
Near synonyms: disinfected, sterilized, sanitized
Opposite: infested, contaminated
词性: adjective
