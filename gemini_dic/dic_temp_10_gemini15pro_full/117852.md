mellowness:/ˈmɛloʊnɪs/| n. |成熟; 柔和; 圆润; 成熟度|A state of being ripe, soft, and sweet; pleasant and agreeable; lacking harshness. |The mellowness of the cheese made it a perfect dessert.  奶酪的醇厚使其成为完美的甜点。

The mellowness of the evening sun cast a golden glow on the landscape. 傍晚柔和的阳光给景色增添了金色的光彩。

|近义词：softness, ripeness, maturity, smoothness, gentleness, mildness|反义词：harshness, sharpness, acidity, immaturity, roughness|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mellow + -ness.  Mellow itself comes from Old English mearu, meaning "soft, tender."
