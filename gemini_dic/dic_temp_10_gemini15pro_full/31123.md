chancellor:/ˈtʃɑːnsələr/ /ˈtʃænsələr/| n.|大臣；总理；大学校长|A chancellor is a high-ranking official, often in a government or university. |The German chancellor visited the White House. 德国总理访问了白宫。

The university chancellor awarded the degrees. 大学校长授予了学位。|Synonyms: minister, premier, head, president, rector|Antonyms: subordinate, assistant|noun
