embowl:/ɪmˈbəʊl/ /ɪmˈboʊl/ | v. | 嵌入；包含；融入 | To include or incorporate something within something else, often in a close or intimate way. | The artist embowled the jewels within the sculpture. 艺术家将珠宝嵌入雕塑中。 | The story embowls several different cultural traditions.  这个故事融合了几种不同的文化传统。 | 近义词：embed, incorporate, include, integrate | 反义词:exclude, extract, remove | 动词
