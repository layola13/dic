malarian:/məˈleəriən/ | adj. n.| 疟疾的；患疟疾的；疟疾患者 | Relating to malaria; a person infected with malaria. |  The doctor diagnosed him as malarian. 医生诊断他患有疟疾。|  She suffered from malarian fevers. 她患有疟疾引起的发烧。 | 近义词: malarial | 反义词: healthy | 词性: 形容词, 名词

Etymology: From French *malarien*, from Italian *mal aria* "bad air," from *mala* "bad" and *aria* "air."  The disease was believed to be caused by bad air.
