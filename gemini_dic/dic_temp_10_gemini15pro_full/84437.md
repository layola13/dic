hallucinogenics:/həˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪks/
noun
致幻剂
Drugs that cause hallucinations, profound distortions in a person's perceptions of reality.
The shaman used hallucinogenics to enter a trance state.  萨满使用致幻剂进入恍惚状态。

The effects of hallucinogenics can be unpredictable and dangerous. 致幻剂的效果可能是不可预测的，而且是危险的。

synonym:psychedelics, hallucinogens
antonym:antipsychotics
n.

hallucinogenic:/həˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
致幻的
Causing hallucinations; relating to or resembling hallucinations.
The plant contains hallucinogenic compounds. 这种植物含有致幻化合物。

Some mushrooms have hallucinogenic properties.  一些蘑菇具有致幻特性。

synonym:psychedelic, psychotomimetic
antonym:sobering
adj.
