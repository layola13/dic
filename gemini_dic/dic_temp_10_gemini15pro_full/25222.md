budgeted:/ˈbʌdʒɪtɪd/
adjective
预算的；编入预算的
Planned or allocated within a budget.
The project is budgeted at $1 million.  这个项目预算为100万美元。
The company has budgeted $50,000 for marketing. 公司已将5万美元编入营销预算。
近义词:allocated, planned, earmarked
反义词:unbudgeted, unplanned

词性:adjective
词源:From budget (n.) + -ed
