inconvenient:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniənt/ | adj. | 不方便的，引起麻烦的 | Not convenient; causing trouble, difficulty, or discomfort. | Example: The meeting was scheduled at an inconvenient time. 会议安排在一个不方便的时间。

Example: It's inconvenient for me to get to the city center.  去市中心对我来说不方便。| 近义词: troublesome, awkward, cumbersome | 反义词: convenient, suitable, handy | 词性: 形容词
