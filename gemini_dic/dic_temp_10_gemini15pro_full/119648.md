middlebrowisms: /ˈmɪdəlˌbraʊɪzəmz/ | n. | 中庸之道；中流砥柱；庸俗文化；附庸风雅 |  Refers to cultural interests or tastes that are considered neither highbrow (intellectual or elitist) nor lowbrow (popular or mass-market), often implying a somewhat pretentious or superficial engagement with the arts.  |  Her book club discussions were full of middlebrowisms, focusing more on the author's biography than the literary merit of the work. 她读书俱乐部的讨论充满了附庸风雅，更多地关注作者的传记而不是作品的文学价值。 |  Near synonyms:  pretentiousness, superficiality, dilettantism | Antonyms: highbrow, lowbrow, intellectualism | noun
