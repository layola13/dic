animalculism:/ˌænɪˈmælkjʊlɪzəm/
n.
精源论；微生物说
The theory that the father alone provides the essential hereditary material to the offspring, the mother serving only as an incubator.  The male's semen was thought to contain tiny preformed animals or homunculi.
The old theory of animalculism was eventually replaced by modern genetics.  精源论的旧理论最终被现代遗传学所取代。

Etymology: From Late Latin *animalculum (diminutive of animal "living being") + -ism.  源自晚期拉丁语 *animalculum（animal“生物”的指小词）+ -ism。

Synonyms:  preformationism, spermism
Antonyms:  ovulism
