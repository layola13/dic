dissipable:/ˈdɪsɪpəbl/
adjective
可消散的；可驱散的；可挥霍的
able to be dissipated, or easily scattered or dispersed.  Can also refer to something that can be wasted or squandered.
The fog was dissipable and soon burned off under the morning sun.  晨雾消散，很快就在晨曦下消失了。
His inheritance was dissipable and he quickly spent it all on frivolous things. 他的遗产很容易挥霍，他很快就把它全部花在了轻浮的事情上。
近义词: dispersable, scatterable, squanderable, expendable
反义词: lasting, enduring, permanent, indestructible
词性: adjective
