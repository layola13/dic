archchemic:/ɑːrˈkɛmɪk/
adjective
炼金术的，炼丹术的
Of or relating to alchemy; alchemical.  Pertaining to the pre-scientific pursuit of transmutation of base metals into gold, and the search for the elixir of life.
The archchemic experiments of the Renaissance ultimately failed to produce gold. 文艺复兴时期的炼金术实验最终未能生产出黄金。
近义词:alchemic, alchemical
反义词:modern, scientific
词性:adjective
