词源:
From Old French conestable, from Latin comes stabuli (“count of the stable”), originally a high-ranking official in charge of the royal stables.  源自古法语 conestable，来自拉丁语 comes stabuli（“马厩伯爵”），最初是负责皇家马厩的高级官员。
