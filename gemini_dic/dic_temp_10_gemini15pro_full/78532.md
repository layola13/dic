ghostfish:/ˈɡoʊstˌfɪʃ/| n. |鬼鱼，幽灵鱼|A type of fish known for its pale, almost translucent appearance.  It refers specifically to the family Apteronotidae,  knifefishes native to South America.|I saw a ghostfish at the aquarium.  我在水族馆看到了一条鬼鱼。|Near synonyms: knifefish, glassfish (depending on the specific species) |Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific type of fish.  One could use "colorful fish" as a contrasting term)|noun
