PHRASE->
decide on: 决定…
decide against: 决定不做…
decide to do something: 决定做某事
decide between: 在…之间做出选择
decide for oneself:  自己决定


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French decider, from Latin decidere (“to cut off, determine, decide”), from de- (“off”) + caedere (“to cut”).  The notion is of resolving something by cutting off other possibilities.
