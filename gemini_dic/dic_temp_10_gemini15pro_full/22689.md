bordure:/ˈbɔːrdər/ (US) /ˈbɔːdjʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. |边饰，边框；（纹章学）盾牌边缘的装饰带 |A border or edge, especially an ornamental one in heraldry. |The shield has a bordure of gold.  这面盾牌有金色的边饰。|Synonyms: border, edge, rim, margin|Antonyms: center, interior, middle|noun

Etymology->
From Old French *bordure*, from *bord* ("edge, border"), from Frankish *bord*, from Proto-Germanic *burdiz* ("board, plank"). Cognate with Old English *bord* ("board, plank, shield").
