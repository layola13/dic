capitalling:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ |v.|资本化；利用；使资本化|The company is capitalizing on the growing demand for organic food.  该公司正在利用对有机食品日益增长的需求。|近义词: investing, financing|反义词: divesting, liquidating|词性: 动词

Etymology:  From "capital" + "-ize," ultimately from Latin "caput" (head).  The original sense related to treating something as capital or stock, then extending to taking advantage of something.
