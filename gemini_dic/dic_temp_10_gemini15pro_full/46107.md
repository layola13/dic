cylinders:/ˈsɪlɪndərz/
n.|圆柱，钢瓶|A solid or hollow three-dimensional geometrical shape that has two parallel circular bases joined by a curved surface.|例句：The gas is stored in cylinders. 这种气体储存在钢瓶中。

例句：The hydraulic cylinders lift the heavy load. 液压缸提起重物。


近义词：containers, tanks, vessels
反义词:|无明显的反义词|
词性:noun (plural)


词源: From Old French *cilindre*, from Latin cylindrus, from Ancient Greek κύλινδρος (kýlindros, “roller, cylinder”), from κυλίνδω (kylíndō, “I roll”).
