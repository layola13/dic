disrank:/dɪsˈræŋk/
verb
降低…的等级; 剥夺…的等级
To reduce in rank; to degrade.
He was disranked for his misconduct. 他因行为不端而被降级。
近义词:degrade, demote, reduce
反义词:promote, elevate, upgrade
词性:v.

disrank:/dɪsˈræŋk/
noun
降级
The act of reducing in rank; degradation.
His disrank was a public humiliation. 他的降级是一种公开的羞辱。
近义词:demotion, degradation, reduction
反义词:promotion, elevation
词性:n.
