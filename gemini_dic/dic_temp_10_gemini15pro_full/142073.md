orgies:/ˈɔːrdʒiz/ | n. | 狂欢；放荡；纵酒狂欢 |  Wild, unrestrained revelry, often involving excessive drinking and sexual activity. | The Roman emperor was known for his lavish orgies. 这位罗马皇帝以其奢华的狂欢而闻名。 |  revelry, debauchery, bacchanalia |  asceticism, abstinence | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *orgia, from Ancient Greek ὄργια (órgia), plural of ὄργιον (órgion) "secret rites, sacred rites," related to ἔργον (érgon) "work, deed."
