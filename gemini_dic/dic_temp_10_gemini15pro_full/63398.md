entozoologist:/ˌɛntoʊzoʊˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.
动物体内寄生虫学家
A scientist who studies entozoa, or internal animal parasites.
Entozoologists study the parasites that live inside animals, such as worms and protozoa.  These scientists play a crucial role in understanding and controlling diseases caused by these parasites.
An entomozoologist might research the life cycle of a particular tapeworm.  一位动物体内寄生虫学家可能会研究某种绦虫的生命周期。
The entomozoologist identified the parasite as a type of roundworm.  这位动物体内寄生虫学家将这种寄生虫鉴定为一种蛔虫。
近义词:parasitologist
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun
