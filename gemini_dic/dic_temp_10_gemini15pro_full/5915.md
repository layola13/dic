ambiparous:/æmˈbɪpərəs/
adjective
能双向生育的;左右手均能使用的
(of an animal) naturally giving birth to young of both sexes; (of a person) able to use both hands with equal skill.
例句：Some species of fish are known to be ambiparous, producing both male and female offspring.  一些鱼类是众所周知的双向生育，能产出雄性和雌性后代。

例句：An ambiparous surgeon is a valuable asset to any operating room.  一个左右手都能使用的外科医生对任何手术室来说都是宝贵的财富。
近义词：ambidextrous, versatile
反义词：unilateral, specialized
词性: adjective
