decoctible:/dɪˈkɒktɪbl/ | adj. | 可煎煮的，可熬出的 |  Capable of being decocted; that which may be boiled down to extract its essence or medicinal properties. | The herbalist explained that certain roots are decoctible and can be used to make medicinal teas.  草药师解释说某些根是可以煎煮的，可以用来制作药茶。|  Synonyms: extractable, infusible | Antonyms: non-extractable | adjective

decoctible:/dɪˈkɒktɪbl/ | n. | 煎煮液 | A liquid obtained by decoction. | The decoctible from the bark had a bitter taste but was known for its healing properties.  树皮煎煮液味道苦涩，但以其愈合特性而闻名。| Synonyms: decoction, extract | Antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *decoctibilis, from Latin decoctus (past participle of decoquere “to boil down”), from de- “down” + coquere “to cook, boil.”
