adores:/əˈdɔːrz/| v.|崇拜，爱慕，极喜欢|To love and respect someone deeply; to like something very much.|She adores her grandchildren. 她非常疼爱她的孙子孙女们。

He adores spicy food. 他非常喜欢吃辣的食物。|近义词：loves, cherishes, worships, is fond of, is keen on|反义词：hates, detests, loathes|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *adouren*, from Old French *adorer*, from Latin *adorare* (“to worship, adore, pray to”), from *ad-* (“to”) + *orare* (“to speak, pray”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He adores his wife. = 他很爱他的妻子。
She adores classical music. = 她很喜欢古典音乐。

一般过去时:
He adored his first car. = 他很喜爱他的第一辆车。
She adored her grandmother. = 她很爱她的祖母。

现在完成时:
He has always adored his children. = 他一直很爱他的孩子们。
She has adored that painting since she first saw it. = 自从第一次看到那幅画，她就一直很喜欢它。

过去完成时:
He had adored his teacher since he was a child. = 他从小就一直很尊敬他的老师。
She had adored that band before they became famous. = 在那个乐队成名前，她就一直很喜欢他们。

将来时:
He will adore his new puppy. = 他会很喜欢他的新小狗。
She will adore living in the countryside. = 她会喜欢住在乡下。

虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would adore traveling the world. = 如果我很有钱，我会很喜欢环游世界。
I wish I adored my job more. = 我希望我能更喜欢我的工作。

被动语态 (很少用):
The singer is adored by millions of fans. = 这位歌手被数百万粉丝喜爱。
The old car was adored by its owner. = 这辆旧车被它的主人珍爱。

疑问句:
Does he adore his new job? = 他喜欢他的新工作吗？
What does she adore about him? = 她喜欢他什么？

否定句:
He doesn't adore spicy food as much as she does. = 他不像她那样喜欢辛辣的食物。
She doesn't adore her job anymore. = 她不再喜欢她的工作了。

PHRASE->
absolutely adores = 绝对喜爱
simply adores =  就是很喜欢
secretly adores = 偷偷地喜欢
adores spending time with = 喜欢和...待在一起
adores going to = 喜欢去
