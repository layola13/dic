dictionary's:/ˈdɪkʃəˌneriz/ | n. |字典的；词典的| Belonging to a dictionary; of or relating to a dictionary. | This is my dictionary's cover. 这是我的字典的封面。|  The dictionary's definition is very clear. 这本字典的释义很清晰。 | 近义词: lexical, lexicographical | 反义词: None | 词性: Possessive noun (名词所有格)

词源: From "dictionary" + "'s" (possessive suffix).  "Dictionary" comes from Late Latin "dictionarium" (a collection of words), from "dictio" (word, saying), from "dicere" (to say, speak).
