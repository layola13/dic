nonhumuses:/ˌnɑːnˈhjuːməsəz/
n.
非腐殖质
the organic component of soil, formed by the decomposition of leaves and other plant material by soil microorganisms.  Nonhumuses would refer to components of soil that are not derived from this decomposed organic matter.
The soil sample contained a high proportion of nonhumuses, such as sand and clay particles. 土样中含有很高比例的非腐殖质，例如沙子和粘土颗粒。
The gardener added compost to increase the humus content and reduce the proportion of nonhumuses in the garden soil. 园丁添加堆肥以增加腐殖质含量并减少花园土壤中非腐殖质的比例。
