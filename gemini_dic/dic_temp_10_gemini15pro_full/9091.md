antimoderns:/ˌæntiˈmɑːdərnz/ /ˌæntiˈmɒdənz/| n.|反现代主义者；反现代派|People who oppose or reject modernism, often favoring traditional values and practices.  They are characterized by their skepticism or hostility towards modern cultural, intellectual, and social trends.|The conference attracted a diverse group of scholars, including several prominent antimoderns.|近义词：traditionalists, conservatives|反义词:modernists, progressives|noun

antimodern:/ˌæntiˈmɑːdərn/ /ˌæntiˈmɒdənz/| adj.|反现代的；反现代主义的|Opposed to or rejecting modernism, often favoring traditional values and practices.|His antimodern sentiments were evident in his preference for classical architecture.|近义词：traditional, conservative|反义词:modern, progressive|adjective
