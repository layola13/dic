overpassionately: /ˌoʊvərˈpæʃənɪtli/ | adv. | 过于热情地，过分激动地 |  In a manner that demonstrates excessive passion or emotion. It suggests an intensity of feeling that may be perceived as exaggerated or inappropriate. | She argued overpassionately, her voice rising with each point. 她争论得过于激动，每讲一点，声音就提高一些。| He embraced her overpassionately, causing her some discomfort. 他过于热情地拥抱她，让她感到有些不自在。| 近义词: excessively, immoderately, fervently, vehemently | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently, calmly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over" (Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across") + "passionately" (from "passion" (Latin "passio," meaning "suffering, endurance, feeling") + "-ate" (forming adjectives) + "-ly" (forming adverbs)).
