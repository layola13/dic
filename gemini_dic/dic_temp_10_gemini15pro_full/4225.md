airlifts:/ˈɛərlɪfts/| n. v.|空运，空投（物资或人员）|The act of transporting supplies or people by aircraft, especially in an emergency.|例句：The airlifts provided much-needed food and medicine to the isolated village. 空运为这个与世隔绝的村庄提供了急需的食品和药品。

例句：The military airlifted the refugees to safety. 军方将难民空运到安全地带。|近义词：air transport,  flying, aviation, transport|反义词:  ground transport|词性：名词，动词

airlift:/ˈɛərlɪfts/| v.|空运，空投（物资或人员）|To transport supplies or people by aircraft, especially in an emergency.|例句：They airlifted the injured climber to the hospital. 他们用飞机把受伤的登山者空运到医院。
