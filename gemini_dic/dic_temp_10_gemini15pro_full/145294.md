overbravado:/ˌoʊvərbrəˈvɑːdoʊ/| n.|过分虚张声势，过分逞能|An excessive display of bravado; exaggerated boasting or swaggering.  It's an instance where someone takes their confidence too far and it comes across as arrogant or insincere.|He displayed such overbravado that everyone could see through his facade.| 近义词：swagger, bluster, boastfulness, arrogance| 反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence|词性:名词

词源:
From over- + bravado.  "Over-" is a prefix indicating "too much" or "excessive." "Bravado" comes from Italian "bravata," meaning a boastful or defiant act.
