answering-back: /ˈɑːnsərɪŋ bæk/ | n. | 回嘴，顶嘴 | The act of replying rudely or disrespectfully to someone in authority. |  He got into trouble for answering back to the teacher. 他因为顶撞老师而惹上了麻烦。 | synonyms: backchat, retort, impudence | antonyms: obedience, respect | noun

answering-back: /ˈɑːnsərɪŋ bæk/ | adj. (less common) | 顶嘴的，爱回嘴的 |  Describing someone who habitually replies rudely or disrespectfully.  |  She has an answering-back attitude. 她有一种爱顶嘴的态度。 | synonyms: impertinent, impudent, disrespectful | antonyms: respectful, obedient, submissive | adjective
