ex-presser:/ɛksˈprɛsər/
n.
表达者; 榨汁机; 挤压器
A person or thing that expresses something, especially one that expresses juice or other liquid.
The lemon ex-presser made quick work of the citrus fruits.  这个榨汁机很快就榨好了柑橘类的水果。
The ex-presser of this opinion was quickly silenced.  表达这个观点的人很快就被噤声了。
Synonym: articulator, communicator, squeezer, juicer
Antonym: suppressor, repressor
