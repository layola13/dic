consoler's:/kənˈsoʊlərz/
n.
安慰者所有物; 安慰者拥有的东西
Something that belongs to or is associated with a consoler (a person who comforts someone).
The consoler's gentle words brought peace.  安慰者温柔的话语带来了平静。
The consoler's presence was a comfort. 安慰者的出现是一种安慰。
Synonym: comforter's
Antonym: tormentor's
