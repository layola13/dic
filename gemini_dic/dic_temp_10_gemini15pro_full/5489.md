aloofly:/əˈluːfli/ | adv. | 冷淡地，冷漠地 | In a manner that is distant, reserved, and uninterested. | She smiled aloofly and turned away. 她冷漠地笑了笑，转过身去。|  He aloofly declined their invitation to dinner.  他冷淡地拒绝了他们共进晚餐的邀请。 | 近义词：distantly, remotely, reservedly, coldly, indifferently | 反义词：warmly,  friendly,  intimately,  eagerly | 词性: 副词

aloof:/əˈluːf/ | adj. | 冷淡的，冷漠的 | Not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant. | He remained aloof from the other children. 他和其他的孩子们保持距离，显得冷漠。| She seemed aloof and unapproachable. 她看起来很冷淡，难以接近。| 近义词：distant, remote, reserved, detached, standoffish | 反义词：warm, friendly, approachable, sociable, involved | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From a- (meaning "on") + loof (meaning "windward"). Originally a nautical term referring to keeping a ship's head to the wind, away from danger.  The meaning later shifted to describe a person's emotional distance or detachment.
