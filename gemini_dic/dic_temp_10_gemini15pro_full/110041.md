licensed: /ˈlaɪ.sənst/ (US) /ˈlaɪ.sənst/ (UK)| adj.|已获许可的，得到授权的|Having been granted a license, especially to do something professionally.|The restaurant is licensed to sell alcohol. 这家餐厅获准出售酒类。|近义词：authorized, permitted, certified|反义词：unlicensed, unauthorized, forbidden|词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "license," which comes from Old French "licence" and ultimately from Latin "licentia," meaning "freedom, permission."
