hide-and-seek:/ˈhaɪd ən siːk/ |n.|捉迷藏|A children's game in which one player closes their eyes for a brief period while the others hide, and then the seeker tries to find them.|The children were playing hide-and-seek in the park. 孩子们正在公园里玩捉迷藏。|Synonyms: hide-and-go-seek|Antonyms: N/A|noun
