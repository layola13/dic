asymmetrical: /ˌeɪsɪˈmetrɪkəl/ /ˌæsɪˈmetrɪkəl/ | adj. | 不对称的，非对称的 | Lacking symmetry; having unequal or dissimilar parts on opposite sides of a dividing line or plane. | The artist deliberately used asymmetrical shapes to create a sense of imbalance.  这位艺术家特意使用不对称的形状来营造一种不平衡感。 | The building has an asymmetrical facade. 这座建筑有一个不对称的正面。 |  近义词: unbalanced, irregular, uneven, lopsided | 反义词: symmetrical, balanced, even, regular | 词性: 形容词

asymmetrous: /ˌeɪsɪˈmetrəs/ /ˌæsɪˈmetrəs/ | adj. | 不对称的，非对称的 |  An older and less common variant of "asymmetrical".  Has the same meaning as asymmetrical: lacking symmetry.  |  The asymmetrous design of the vase made it a unique piece.  这个花瓶的不对称设计使它成为一件独特的作品。 | The butterfly's wings were noticeably asymmetrous. 蝴蝶的翅膀明显不对称。 | 近义词: unbalanced, irregular, uneven, lopsided, asymmetrical | 反义词: symmetrical, balanced, even, regular, symmetrical | 词性: 形容词
