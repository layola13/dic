cobweb:/ˈkɒbwɛb/ (UK) /ˈkɑːbwɛb/ (US)| n.  v.|蜘蛛网；蛛网状物；陈腐过时的事物；使布满蛛网|The attic was full of cobwebs. 阁楼里满是蜘蛛网。| Spiders cobweb the corners of the room. 蜘蛛在房间的角落里结网。|近义词：spiderweb, gossamer|反义词:cleanliness|词性:名词, 动词

词源: From Middle English *coppeweb*, from Old English *coppeweb* (“spiderweb”), from *coppe* (“spider”) + *web*.  The "coppe" element is related to Low German *koppe* (“spider”), and further back to Proto-Germanic *kupǭ* (“spider, lump, swelling”).
