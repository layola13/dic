gold-plated:/ˈɡoʊld ˈpleɪtɪd/| adj.|镀金的，虚饰的，华而不实的|Covered with a thin layer of gold; superficially attractive or impressive but lacking real value.|例句：She received a gold-plated pen as a retirement gift. 她收到了一支镀金钢笔作为退休礼物。|例句：The company offered a gold-plated health insurance plan. 这家公司提供了一个表面上很诱人的医疗保险计划。|近义词：gilded, ornate, ostentatious|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词
