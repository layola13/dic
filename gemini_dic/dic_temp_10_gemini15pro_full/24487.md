brittlest:/ˈbrɪtlɪst/| adj.|最脆的，最易碎的|Superlative form of brittle: most brittle.  Meaning easily broken or cracked.|The brittlest bone in the human body is often considered to be the stapes in the ear. 人体最脆的骨头通常被认为是耳朵里的镫骨。|The old vase was so brittle, it felt like the brittlest thing I'd ever touched.  这古老的花瓶太脆了，感觉像是我摸过的最易碎的东西。|近义词：fragile, frail, delicate, breakable|反义词：tough, strong, resilient, flexible|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *britol (recorded only in the derived verb britlian 'to break, shatter'), of unknown origin.
