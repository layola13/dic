esker:/ˈɛskər/ | n. | 蛇形丘，冰河丘陵 | A long, winding ridge of stratified sand and gravel deposited by a meltwater stream flowing beneath a glacier. |  The hikers followed the esker for miles, enjoying the elevated view.  徒步旅行者沿着蛇形丘走了好几英里，欣赏着高处的景色。

| Example: Eskers are a common feature of landscapes that were once glaciated. 蛇形丘是曾经被冰川覆盖的地区的常见特征。 | Synonym: ridge, kame, moraine | Antonym: valley, depression | noun
