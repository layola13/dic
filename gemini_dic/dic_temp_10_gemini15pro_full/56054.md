docetae:/doʊˈsiːtiː/
n.
幻影说信徒，幻影论者
believers in Docetism, an early Christian heresy that maintained that Christ's body was not human but only appeared to be so.
The Docetae believed that Jesus only seemed to suffer on the cross.  幻影说信徒相信耶稣在十字架上只是看起来像受苦。
The writings of the early Church Fathers often refute the claims of the Docetae. 早期教会教父的著作经常驳斥幻影说信徒的主张。
近义词:Gnostics (某些方面)
反义词:orthodox Christians
词性:noun
