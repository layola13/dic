nonexemplary:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛmpləri/
adjective
非典范的，非示例性的
Not serving as a good example; not typical or representative.
This nonexemplary behavior led to his dismissal.  这个不典型的行为导致他被解雇。
The manual included a nonexemplary diagram that confused everyone.  手册中包含了一个非典型的图表，让每个人都很困惑。
近义词: atypical, unusual, irregular, aberrant
反义词: exemplary, typical, representative, standard
词性: adjective
