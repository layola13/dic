consented:/kənˈsɛntɪd/
verb
同意，准许
give permission for something to happen
例句：She consented to the medical treatment. 她同意接受治疗。

例句：He consented to marry her. 他同意和她结婚。

近义词：agreed, acquiesced, approved, complied
反义词：refused, objected, declined, denied
词性：verb (past tense and past participle of consent)
