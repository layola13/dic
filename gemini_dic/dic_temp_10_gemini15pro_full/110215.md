lifebelts:/ˈlaɪfˌbelts/ | n. | 救生圈；救生带 | A buoyant ring or belt designed to keep a person afloat in water. | The lifeguards threw lifebelts to the struggling swimmers. 救生员把救生圈扔给了挣扎的游泳者。| The ship was equipped with numerous lifebelts. 船上配备了许多救生圈。 | Synonyms: life preserver, life buoy, life jacket, life ring | Antonyms: N/A | noun
