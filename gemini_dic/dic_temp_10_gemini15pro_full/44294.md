crookedest: /ˈkrʊkɪdɪst/ | adj. | 最弯曲的，最不正当的 |  superlative form of crooked: most crooked.  Bent or twisted out of shape or out of a straight line. Dishonest or illegal. |  That's the crookedest tree I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最弯曲的树。 He was involved in some of the crookedest deals in the city. 他参与了该市一些最不正当的交易。 | 近义词:  bentest, most twisted, most corrupt, most dishonest | 反义词: straightest, most honest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old Norse *krokr, meaning "hook, bend."
