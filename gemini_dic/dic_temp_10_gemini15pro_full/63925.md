epiplexis:/ˌɛpɪˈplɛksɪs/| n. |反诘; 诘问; 责问|A rhetorical device in which the speaker reproaches or upbraids the audience, often in the form of a series of rhetorical questions.|Example 1: "Have you no shame?  Do you not realize the consequences of your actions?" 你不知羞耻吗？你没有意识到你行为的后果吗？

Example 2: "How could you be so careless? Didn't I warn you about this?" 你怎么能如此粗心？我不是警告过你吗？|近义词：reproach, upbraiding, rebuke, reprimand, admonition|反义词: praise, commendation, approval|词性:noun
