law-breaking: /ˈlɔːˌbreɪkɪŋ/
noun
违法；犯法
The act of breaking the law.
He was arrested for law-breaking. 他因违法行为被捕。
Law-breaking is not tolerated in this community. 在这个社区，违法行为是不能容忍的。
Synonyms: crime, offense, violation, transgression, infringement, delinquency
Antonyms: law-abiding, legal, lawful
