commencing:/kəˈmɛnsɪŋ/| v. adj.|开始，着手|Beginning; starting.  Derived from the Old French "commencier" and ultimately from the Latin "cominitiare," meaning "to begin."|The ceremony commencing at noon.  典礼将于中午开始。|近义词：beginning, starting, initiating, launching|反义词:ending, finishing, concluding, ceasing|v.
commencing:/kəˈmɛnsɪŋ/| adj.|开始的|Beginning; starting.|The commencing chapter of the book. 这本书的开篇章节。|近义词：initial, introductory, opening|反义词:closing, final, concluding|adj.
