ameers:/əˈmɪrz/ | n. |埃米尔（复数）;酋长（复数）|  A title of various Muslim (chiefly Arab) rulers.  | The ameers of the various provinces assembled. 各省的埃米尔们聚集在一起。 | synonyms: emirs, princes, chiefs, rulers, leaders | antonyms: followers, subjects | noun

ameers:/əˈmɪrz/ | n. | 指挥官;海军将领（复数）; | An officer of high rank in some navies.  | The ameers of the fleet met to discuss strategy. 舰队的指挥官们开会讨论战略。 | synonyms: admirals, commanders, captains, officers | antonyms: subordinates, crew | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic  ʾamīr  "commander," borrowing from  ʾamara "to command."
