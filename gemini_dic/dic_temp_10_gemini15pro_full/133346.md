nonextensive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛnsɪv/
adj.|非广延的|Not extensive; not covering a large area.  Specifically used in physics and thermodynamics to describe systems where the entropy is not an extensive quantity (i.e., it does not scale linearly with the system size).|The research focuses on nonextensive statistical mechanics. 这项研究的重点是非广延统计力学。|近义词:non-additive, non-linear|反义词:extensive, additive
词性:形容词
