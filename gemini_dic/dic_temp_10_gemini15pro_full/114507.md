maladministering:/ˌmælədˈmɪnɪstərɪŋ/
|v.|管理不善；治理不当|The report criticized the government for maladministering the economy. 报告批评政府对经济管理不善。|English Explanation:  Governing or managing poorly; administering (something) badly or wrongly.  It implies incompetence, neglect, or corruption in the handling of affairs.|Example 1: The maladministering of funds led to the charity's bankruptcy.  资金管理不善导致慈善机构破产。

Example 2:  The committee was accused of maladministering the election process. 委员会被指控选举过程管理不当。|近义词：mismanaging, mishandling, misgoverning|反义词：administering well, managing effectively|词性: Verb (present participle)
