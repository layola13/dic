muriated:/ˈmjʊərieɪtɪd/
adjective
氯化的; 盐酸处理的
treated with or containing a chloride, especially of a metal.
The muriated spring water is said to have healing properties.  这氯化矿泉水据说有疗效。
近义词:chlorinated, chloride-containing
反义词: (No common direct antonym)
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *muriaticus*, from *muria* (“brine”) + *-aticus* (adjectival suffix).
