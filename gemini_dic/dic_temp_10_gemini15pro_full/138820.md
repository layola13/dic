nuncio: /ˈnʌnsiˌoʊ/ (US), /ˈnʌnʃiˌəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 教廷大使，使节 | An ambassador of the pope to a foreign government. | The papal nuncio delivered a message from the Vatican. 教廷大使传达了梵蒂冈的讯息。 |  synonyms: ambassador, envoy, legate, emissary | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps *rebel*, *dissident* in a specific context) | noun

Etymology:
From Italian *nunzio*, from Latin *nuntius* "messenger, announcer," related to *novus* "new."
