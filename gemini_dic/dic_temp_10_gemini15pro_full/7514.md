anneal:/əˈniːl/| v. |使退火，使韧化；使变软|To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it. |The glass is annealed to make it stronger.  玻璃经过退火处理以增强强度。| The blacksmith annealed the steel before shaping it.  铁匠在塑形之前先对钢进行了退火处理。|synonyms: temper, toughen, harden|antonyms: weaken, soften|verb

annealed:/əˈniːld/| adj. | 退火的，韧化的|Having been treated by annealing.|Annealed glass is less likely to shatter. 退火玻璃不太可能破碎。|The annealed steel was more flexible.  退火后的钢材更具柔韧性。|synonyms: tempered, toughened, hardened|antonyms: weakened, softened|adjective
