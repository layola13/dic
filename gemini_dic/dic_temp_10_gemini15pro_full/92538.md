hypsometry:/hɪpˈsɒmɪtri/ | n. |测高学，测高法|The science of measuring heights, especially as related to determining elevations above sea level.|Hypsometry is crucial for creating accurate topographic maps. 测高学对于绘制精确的地形图至关重要。|Synonyms: altimetry, leveling |Antonyms: bathymetry |noun
