brachycephalic:/ˌbrækiːsəˈfælɪk/| adj.|短头型的，短颅的|Having a relatively broad, short skull, typically with the breadth at least 80% of the length. This term is often used in reference to dog breeds.|例句：Pugs and French Bulldogs are brachycephalic breeds. 八哥犬和法国斗牛犬是短头型犬种。

例句：Brachycephalic dogs are prone to breathing problems. 短头型犬容易出现呼吸问题。|近义词：short-headed, short-nosed|反义词：dolichocephalic (long-headed)|词性: adjective
