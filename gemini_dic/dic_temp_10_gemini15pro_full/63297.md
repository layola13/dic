enthusiastic: /ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 热情的，热心的 | Showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval. | She was enthusiastic about the new project. 她对这个新项目充满热情。|  He gave an enthusiastic welcome to the visitors. 他热情地欢迎来访者。 |  近义词: eager, ardent, passionate, zealous, fervent | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin enthusiasticus, from Greek enthousiastikos, from enthousiastes 'inspired by a god,' from en- 'in' + theos 'god.'
