aerobics: /ɛˈroʊbɪks/ /eəˈrəʊbɪks/
noun
有氧运动
Physical exercises that increase the amount of oxygen in the blood.
She does aerobics three times a week. 她每周做三次有氧运动。

I prefer aerobics to weightlifting. 我更喜欢有氧运动而不是举重。


近义词: exercise, workout, fitness training
反义词: inactivity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ā́er-, *āér (air) + *bios (life). Coined by Kenneth H. Cooper, an American physician, in the 1960s.
