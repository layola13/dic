dramatist:/ˈdræmətɪst/ | n. | 剧作家 | A person who writes plays.  A playwright. | Example 1: Shakespeare is considered one of the greatest dramatists of all time.  莎士比亚被认为是有史以来最伟大的剧作家之一。

Example 2: The dramatist received critical acclaim for her latest work.  这位剧作家最新的作品获得了评论界的好评。| 近义词: playwright, screenwriter, scriptwriter | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Middle English: from French dramatiste, via late Latin from Greek dramatourgos ‘playwright’, from drama (see drama) + -ourgos ‘worker’.
