microseismometer:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈsaɪzmɑːmɪtər/ | n. |微震仪，微震计 | An instrument that measures very small ground motions, often used to detect subtle vibrations caused by earthquakes, volcanic activity, or other geological phenomena. | The microseismometer detected a slight tremor that went unnoticed by humans. 微震仪检测到人类没有察觉到的轻微震动。

The geologists installed a sensitive microseismometer near the volcano to monitor its activity. 地质学家在火山附近安装了一个灵敏的微震仪来监测其活动。 | synonym: seismograph, seismometer | antonym: N/A | noun
