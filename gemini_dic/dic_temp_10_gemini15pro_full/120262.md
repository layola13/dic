mini-lp:/ˈmɪni ɛlˈpiː/ | n. | 迷你唱片,小型密纹唱片 | A smaller version of a long-playing (LP) record, typically containing fewer songs or a shorter duration of music.  It was a format briefly popular in the 1980s. |  They released a mini-LP with just four songs on it.  他们发行了一张只有四首歌的迷你唱片。| synonym: EP, extended play record | antonym: LP, full-length album | noun
