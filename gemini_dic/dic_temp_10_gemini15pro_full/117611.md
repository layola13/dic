megaphyll:/ˈmɛɡəfɪl/| n.| 巨型叶，大型叶 |A leaf with a highly branched vascular system, characteristic of ferns, gymnosperms, and angiosperms.  It is thought to have evolved from a flattened and webbed branch system.| Ferns have megaphylls.  蕨类植物有大叶。 |Synonyms: macrophyll |Antonyms: microphyll|noun
