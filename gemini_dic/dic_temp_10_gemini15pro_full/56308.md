doggy-paddle:/ˈdɒɡi pædl/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡi ˈpædl/ (US)| n. |狗刨式游泳|A swimming stroke resembling the paddling of a dog, characterized by short, quick movements of the arms and legs.|The child doggy-paddled across the shallow end of the pool. 孩子在泳池的浅水区狗刨式地游了过去。|Near synonyms: paddle, flutter kick|Antonym: crawl (swimming stroke)|Noun

doggy-paddles:/ˈdɒɡi pædlz/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡi ˈpædlz/ (US)| v. |狗刨式游泳|To swim using the doggy-paddle stroke.|He doggy-paddled to the edge of the pool. 他狗刨式地游到泳池边。|Near synonyms: paddles, swims (using doggy-paddle)|Antonym: crawls (swimming)|Verb
