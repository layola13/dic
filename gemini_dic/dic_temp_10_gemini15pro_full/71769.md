fleamy:/ˈfliːmi/| adj.|跳蚤多的；充满跳蚤的|Infested with fleas.|例句：The stray dog was fleamy and needed a bath. 那只流浪狗身上有很多跳蚤，需要洗澡。

例句：The old mattress was so fleamy we had to throw it away. 那张旧床垫跳蚤太多了，我们不得不把它扔掉。|近义词：lousy, infested|反义词:clean, flea-free|词性:形容词
词源: From flea + -y.  "Flea" comes from Old English *flēah, from Proto-Germanic *flauhaz.  The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
