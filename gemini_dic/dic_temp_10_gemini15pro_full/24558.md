broadwayite:/ˈbrɔːdweɪˌaɪt/| n.|百老汇石|A rare mineral, a hydrated sulfate of copper, magnesium, and potassium, occurring in blue acicular crystals at Bisbee, Arizona.  It's named after Broadway, the location where it was discovered. |Broadwayite is a beautiful but rare mineral specimen. 百老汇石是一种美丽而稀有的矿物标本。|Synonyms: None (due to its specific chemical composition)|Antonyms: None (due to its specific chemical composition)|noun
