flatlining:/ˈflætˌlaɪnɪŋ/
verb|变得毫无生气；停滞不前；垂危；(尤指心电图)呈平线|The patient's heart rate was flatlining. 病人的心率呈平线状态。|近义词：stagnating, declining, deteriorating, failing|反义词:improving, recovering, thriving|词性:v.
flatlining:/ˈflætˌlaɪnɪŋ/
noun|停滞；衰退；(尤指心电图)呈平线状态|The economy is showing signs of flatlining. 经济显示出停滞的迹象。|近义词：stagnation, decline, deterioration, failure|反义词:improvement, recovery, growth|词性:n.
