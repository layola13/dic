honeybees:/ˈhʌniˌbiz/ | n. |蜜蜂|A stinging winged insect that collects nectar and pollen, produces wax and honey, and lives in large communities. It is a social insect known for its organized colonies and crucial role in pollination. |Honeybees are essential for pollinating many crops. 蜜蜂对于许多农作物的授粉至关重要。| Bees, apis | Wasps, hornets | noun
