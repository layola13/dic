detailedness:/ˈdiːteɪldnəs/ /ˈdiːteɪldnɪs/ | n. | 详细；详尽 | The detailedness of the report impressed the client.  报告的详尽程度给客户留下了深刻的印象。|  The painting was remarkable for its detailedness and vibrant colors.  这幅画因其细腻和鲜艳的色彩而引人注目。 | 近义词: detail, thoroughness, comprehensiveness, elaborateness, meticulousness | 反义词: brevity, conciseness, vagueness, superficiality | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the adjective "detailed" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Detailed" comes from the verb "detail" meaning "to list or describe item by item," from French "détailler" (to cut into pieces, to itemize), from "dé-" (from, out of) + "tailler" (to cut).
