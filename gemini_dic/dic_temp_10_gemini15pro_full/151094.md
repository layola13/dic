parascender:/ˈpærəsɛndər/| n.|滑翔伞运动员，滑翔伞飞行员|A person who flies a paraglider.  Someone who practices the sport of paragliding. |He is a skilled parascender and enjoys soaring through the air. 他是一位熟练的滑翔伞运动员，喜欢在空中翱翔。|近义词：paraglider pilot|反义词:|n.

Etymology:
para- (from Greek παρά para, “beside, alongside, beyond”) + ascend (from Latin ascendere, "to climb up") + -er (agent suffix)
