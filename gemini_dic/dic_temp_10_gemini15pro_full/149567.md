pagodalike:/pəˈɡoʊdəˌlaɪk/| adj.|类似宝塔的，宝塔式的|Resembling a pagoda in appearance, typically having tiered roofs.  Having the characteristic tiered, upward-curving roofs of a pagoda.|The hotel was a pagodalike structure overlooking the bay.  这家酒店是一座俯瞰海湾的宝塔式建筑。|Synonyms: tiered, multi-storied, pagoda-shaped|Antonyms: flat-roofed, single-storied|adjective
