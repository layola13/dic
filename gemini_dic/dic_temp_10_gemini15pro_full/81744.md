greenwashing:/ˈɡriːnwɒʃɪŋ/ | n. |漂绿；绿色清洗|The practice of making an unsubstantiated or misleading claim about the environmental benefits of a product, service, technology or company practice in order to present a positive public image.|The company was accused of greenwashing after it was revealed that its "eco-friendly" products were actually made with harmful chemicals.| 近义词:eco-fraud, green sheen |反义词:environmental responsibility| noun
