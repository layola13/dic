auctioneer:/ˈɔːkʃəˈnɪər/ | n. |拍卖师|A person who conducts auctions by calling out bids and declaring goods sold.| The auctioneer banged the gavel and declared the painting sold. 拍卖师敲响木槌，宣布这幅画售出。|Synonyms: seller, broker, agent|Antonyms: bidder, buyer|Noun

auctioneers:/ˈɔːkʃəˈnɪərz/ | n. |拍卖师（复数）|Persons who conduct auctions.| The auctioneers were busy preparing for the upcoming antique sale.  拍卖师们正忙着为即将举行的古董拍卖会做准备。|Synonyms: sellers, brokers, agents|Antonyms: bidders, buyers|Noun
