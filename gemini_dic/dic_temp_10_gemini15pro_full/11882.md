arrogant: /ˈærəɡənt/ (US) /ˈærəɡənt/ (UK)| adj.|傲慢的，自大的|Having or revealing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities.|He was so arrogant, he thought he was better than everyone else. 他太傲慢了，认为自己比任何人都强。|Example:  She dismissed their concerns with an arrogant wave of her hand. 她傲慢地挥了挥手，对他们的担忧不屑一顾。|Synonyms: haughty, conceited, pompous, supercilious, presumptuous|Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming|Word type: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *arrogant-, present participle stem of arrogare "to claim for oneself," from Latin ad- "to" + rogare "to ask."
