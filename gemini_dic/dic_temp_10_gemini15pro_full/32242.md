chickenpox:/ˈtʃɪkɪnpɒks/ (UK), /ˈtʃɪkənˌpɑːks/ (US)| n. |水痘|An infectious disease causing a mild fever and an eruption of itchy blisters all over the body.|例句：My daughter caught chickenpox from her classmate. 我女儿从她同学那里感染了水痘。

例句：Chickenpox is common among children. 水痘在儿童中很常见。

近义词：varicella (医学术语)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，因为是一种具体的疾病)|词性:noun
