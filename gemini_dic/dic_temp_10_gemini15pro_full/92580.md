一般现在时 (Simple Present):
It's a beautiful day today. = 今天天气真好。
It's important to be on time. = 准时很重要。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It's raining heavily outside. = 外面正在下大雨。
The dog's barking at the mailman. = 狗正在对着邮递员叫。  (注意此处's是is的缩写，而非it's)

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It's been a long time since I've seen you. = 好久没见到你了。
It's happened before. = 之前发生过。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
It's been raining all day. = 下了一整天雨了。
She's been working on that project for months. = 她已经做了好几个月的项目了。(注意此处's是has的缩写，而非it's)

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
