overspaciousnesses:/ˈoʊvərˈspeɪʃəsnəsɪz/
n.
过度宽敞
The state of being excessively spacious.
The overspaciousnesses of the new apartment felt a bit impersonal.  这套新公寓过于宽敞，感觉有点没有人情味。
The sheer overspaciousnesses of the hangar made the small plane look even tinier.  飞机库的巨大空间让这架小飞机看起来更加渺小。
spaciousness, vastness, roominess
crampedness, confinement, tightness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + spacious + -ness. Over- intensifies the meaning of spacious. Spacious originates from the Latin spatium, meaning "space."  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
