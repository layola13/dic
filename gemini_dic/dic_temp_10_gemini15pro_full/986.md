A large group of bony fishes characterized by having spiny rays in their fins.  This group includes most of the familiar bony fishes.
The perch, bass, and tuna are all members of the Acanthopterygii.  鲈鱼、鲈鱼和金枪鱼都是棘鳍鱼类的成员。
