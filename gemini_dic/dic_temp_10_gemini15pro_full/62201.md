encounterers:/ɪnˈkaʊntərərz/
n.
相遇者；遭遇者
People who encounter something or someone.  Those who have an experience with something or someone.
例句：The encounterers of the strange phenomenon were baffled. 遭遇这种奇怪现象的人感到困惑。

例句：The encounterers of the Yeti described it as a large, hairy creature.  目击雪人的人都描述它为一种体型庞大、多毛的生物。

近义词：meeters, experiencers
反义词: avoiders

词性: noun
