curvilinear:/ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniər/| adj.|曲线形的，弯曲的|Consisting of or bounded by curved lines; represented by a curved line. |例句：The artist is known for her curvilinear designs.  这位艺术家以其曲线形的设计而闻名。

例句：The road followed a curvilinear path through the mountains. 这条路沿着一条弯曲的路径穿过群山。|近义词：curved, bending, sinuous, meandering|反义词：straight, linear, rectilinear|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *curvilinearis*, from Latin curvus (“curved”) + linea (“line”).
