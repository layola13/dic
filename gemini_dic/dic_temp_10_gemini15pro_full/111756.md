lodesman:/ˈloʊdzmən/ | n. |领港员，引水员 | A person, usually a local expert, who pilots ships through difficult or dangerous waters. | The lodesman guided the large tanker safely through the narrow channel. 领港员安全地引导大型油轮通过狭窄的航道。|Synonyms: pilot, guide, navigator | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "landlubber" as a humorous contrast) | Noun
