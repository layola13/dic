nonroyalties:/nɒnˈrɔɪəltiz/ /ˌnɑːnˈrɔɪəltiz/| n. |非特许权使用费，非版税| Payments received for something other than royalties, like a one-time fee for a piece of intellectual property.| The company received nonroyalties for the outright sale of the software.  这家公司因直接出售该软件而获得非版税收入。|  non-royalty payments, one-time fees| royalties | noun
nonroyalty:/nɒnˈrɔɪəlti/ /ˌnɑːnˈrɔɪəlti/ | adj. | 非版税的，非特许权使用费的 | Not subject to or involving the payment of royalties. |  They offered a nonroyalty payment for the design.  他们为该设计提供了一笔非版税付款。| royalty-free, non-licensed | royalty-based, licensed | adjective
