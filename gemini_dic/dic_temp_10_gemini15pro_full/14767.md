awkwardly:/ˈɔːkwərdli/ (US) /ˈɔːkwədli/ (UK)| adv.|笨拙地；尴尬地；难看地|In a way that is not graceful or elegant; clumsily.  Showing or causing a lack of social ease or grace.|She awkwardly stumbled over her words. 她笨拙地结结巴巴地说。|He smiled awkwardly. 他尴尬地笑了笑。|Synonyms: clumsily, gracelessly, ungainly, ineptly|Antonyms: gracefully, elegantly, smoothly|词性: 副词
