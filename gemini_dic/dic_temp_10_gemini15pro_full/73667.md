forgeries:/ˈfɔːrdʒəriz/
n. |伪造品；赝品|Something falsely made or copied in order to deceive; an act of forging.  |The museum discovered several forgeries in its collection.  博物馆在它的收藏中发现了几件赝品。|synonyms: counterfeits, fakes, imitations, reproductions|antonyms: originals, authentics|noun


forgery:/ˈfɔːrdʒəri/
n. |伪造；伪造罪|The action of forging or producing a copy of a document, signature, banknote, or work of art. |He was found guilty of forgery and sentenced to two years in prison. 他被判伪造罪，判处两年监禁。|synonyms: counterfeiting, faking, imitation, reproduction|antonyms: authentication, verification|noun
