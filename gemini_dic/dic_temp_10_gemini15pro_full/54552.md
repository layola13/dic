dislocate:/dɪsˈloʊkeɪt/ /dɪsˈləʊkeɪt/
|v. |使脱臼；使错位；扰乱|
|To put out of joint, as a limb; to displace.|
|He dislocated his shoulder playing rugby. 他在打橄榄球时肩膀脱臼了。|
|The earthquake dislocated the railway lines. 地震使铁轨错位。|
|近义词：displace, disturb, disrupt|
|反义词：locate, connect, order|
|词性：动词|


dislocated:/dɪsˈloʊkeɪtɪd/ /dɪsˈləʊkeɪtɪd/
|adj. |脱臼的；错位的；混乱的|
|Out of its proper place or order.|
|He had a dislocated hip. 他的臀部脱臼了。|
|The refugees are living in dislocated communities. 难民们生活在混乱的社区里。|
|近义词：displaced, disordered, chaotic|
|反义词：ordered, organized, connected|
|词性：形容词|
