disassociated:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 分离的，不相关的 |  No longer connected or associated; having severed ties or connections.  |  He felt disassociated from his former life. 他感觉与以前的生活脱节了。 |  detached, unconnected, separate, aloof | connected, associated, related | adjective

disassociate:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪt/ | v. |  使分离，使脱离关系 |  To disconnect or separate (something) from something else; to cease associating with. | She tried to disassociate herself from the scandal. 她试图把自己与丑闻撇清关系。| detach, separate, disconnect, disunite | connect, associate, unite | verb
