enlarger:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒər/ | n. |放大器；扩大器|A device used to make something bigger, especially a photographic enlarger used to make prints from negatives. |He used an enlarger to create a poster-sized print from the negative. 他用放大器把底片放大成海报大小的照片。| 近义词:magnifier, amplifier, expander | 反义词:reducer, diminisher | Noun

enlarge:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/ | v. |放大；扩大|To make something bigger. |She wants to enlarge the photograph. 她想放大这张照片。|近义词:expand, increase, magnify, amplify | 反义词:reduce, shrink, diminish, decrease | Verb
