libertarianism:/ˌlɪbərˈtɛəriənɪzəm/
noun
自由意志主义; 自由主义
A political philosophy that emphasizes individual liberty and minimizes the role of government.
Libertarianism is a political philosophy that holds individual liberty as the core principle.
自由意志主义是一种以个人自由为核心原则的政治哲学。
Synonyms: individualism, self-governance, autonomy
Antonyms: authoritarianism, totalitarianism, collectivism
