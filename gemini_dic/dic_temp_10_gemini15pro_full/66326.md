excretion:/ɪkˈskriːʃn/| n.|排泄，排泄物|The process of eliminating or expelling waste matter from the body.  The removal of metabolic waste products from an organism.|The kidneys play a vital role in excretion. 肾脏在排泄中起着至关重要的作用。|The excretion of toxins from the body is essential for health.  从体内排出毒素对健康至关重要。|elimination, discharge, expulsion|secretion, absorption|noun
