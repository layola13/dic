humidex:/ˈhjuːmɪdɛks/ | n. |体感温度；湿热指数 | A measure of how hot the weather feels to the average person, based on the combined effect of temperature and humidity. | The humidex reached 40 today, making it feel extremely hot and uncomfortable. 今天体感温度达到了40度，让人感觉非常闷热不舒服。| The humidex is high today, so be sure to drink plenty of water. 今天湿热指数很高，一定要多喝水。| synonyms: apparent temperature, heat index | antonyms: wind chill | noun
