feldspar: /ˈfeldˌspɑːr/ (US), /ˈfeldˌspɑː/ (UK)| n. |长石|A group of rock-forming minerals that make up as much as 60% of the Earth's crust. They are composed of aluminum silicates of potassium, sodium, and calcium.|Feldspar is a common ingredient in ceramics.|近义词: albite, anorthite, orthoclase|反义词: None (as it's a specific mineral group)|Noun
