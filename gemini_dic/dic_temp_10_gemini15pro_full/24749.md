broods:/bruːdz/ | n. | 孵蛋，一窝幼鸟，沉思 |A group of young birds hatched at the same time; a family of young animals; the act of thinking anxiously or sadly about something for a long time. | The hen fiercely protected her brood. 母鸡凶猛地保护着它的一窝小鸡。 | synonyms: offspring, clutch, litter, contemplation, rumination | antonyms:  | noun

broods:/bruːdz/ | v. | 孵蛋，冥想，(人)沉思 |(of a bird) sit on (eggs) to hatch them; think deeply about something that makes one unhappy. | She brooded over her failure for weeks. 她为自己的失败郁闷了好几个星期。 | synonyms: ponder, dwell, meditate, incubate | antonyms:  ignore, forget | verb
