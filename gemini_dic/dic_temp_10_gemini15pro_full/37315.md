commemorable:/kəˈmɛmərəbəl/| adj.|值得纪念的；难忘的|Deserving of or creating a fond or respectful memory.|Her performance on the final night was truly memorable. 她决赛之夜的表演令人难忘。|近义词：unforgettable, noteworthy, significant|反义词：forgettable, insignificant, unremarkable|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *commemorabilis, from Latin commemorare "to call to mind, recount, relate," from com- "together" + memorare "to bring to remembrance," from memor "mindful."
