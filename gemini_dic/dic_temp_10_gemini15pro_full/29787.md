catwalks: /ˈkætˌwɔːks/ (US), /ˈkætˌwɔːks/ (UK)| n. |T台，猫道|A narrow, elevated walkway or platform used by models to display clothes at a fashion show; a narrow walkway or platform high above the ground or floor, often used by workers.| The models strutted down the catwalk, showcasing the designer's latest collection. 模特们昂首阔步走在T台上，展示着设计师的最新系列。 The electrician carefully crossed the catwalk to reach the lighting fixtures. 电工小心翼翼地穿过猫道去够照明设备。|近义词：runway, ramp |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "aisle" in some contexts)| noun


catwalk: /ˈkætˌwɔːk/ (US), /ˈkætˌwɔːk/ (UK)| v. | 走猫步|To walk along a catwalk, especially as a fashion model. | She catwalked down the runway in a stunning red dress. 她穿着惊艳的红色连衣裙在T台上走猫步。 He nervously catwalked across the narrow beam high above the stage. 他紧张地在舞台上方狭窄的横梁上走猫步。 |近义词：strut, sashay, model |反义词:  (No direct antonym)| verb
