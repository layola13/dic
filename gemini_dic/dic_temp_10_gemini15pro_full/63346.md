entogenous:/ɛnˈtɒdʒɪnəs/| adj.|内生的，内源的|Growing or originating from within; produced or originating within an organism or part.|例句：Entogenous fungi live within the plant tissues. 内生真菌生活在植物组织内。

例句：The disease was caused by endogenous factors. 这种疾病是由内源性因素引起的。|近义词：internal, inherent, innate|反义词：exogenous, external|词性：adjective

词源: From Greek *endogenēs*  "growing within," from *endo-* "within" + *genēs* "produced, born."
