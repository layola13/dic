consolidatory:/kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtəri/ /kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtɔːri/
adjective
巩固的，加强的
serving or tending to consolidate; uniting.
例句：The government announced a series of consolidatory measures to strengthen the economy. 政府宣布了一系列巩固措施以加强经济。

例句：The company undertook a consolidatory merger to improve its market position. 公司进行了合并以巩固其市场地位。

近义词：strengthening, reinforcing, uniting, combining
反义词：weakening, dividing, separating
词性: adjective

词源：From Late Latin consolidatus, past participle of consolidare "to make solid," from com- "together" + solidus "solid."
