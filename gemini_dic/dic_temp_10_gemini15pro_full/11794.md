arraignment:/əˈreɪnmənt/| n.|传讯，提审，控告|The act of bringing someone before a court to answer a criminal charge.|The arraignment of the suspect was postponed due to lack of evidence.  嫌疑人的传讯由于缺乏证据而被推迟。|近义词：accusation, indictment, charge|反义词: acquittal, exoneration|noun
