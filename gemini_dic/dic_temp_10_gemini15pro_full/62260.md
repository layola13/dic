encurtain: /ɪnˈkɜːtən/ | vt. | 遮蔽，用窗帘遮住; 笼罩 | To cover or conceal with or as if with curtains. | The stage was encurtained as the scene changed.  舞台在场景变换时被幕布遮住了。|  The mist encurtained the valley.  薄雾笼罩着山谷。| 近义词: conceal, veil, shroud, cover | 反义词: reveal, uncover, expose | 词性: 动词

词源:  From en- + curtain.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into or onto," or "to cause to be." Thus, "encurtain" literally means "to put into curtains" or "to cause to be curtained."
