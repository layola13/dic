gorsiest:/ˈɡɔːrsiɪst/
adjective
最血腥的；最残忍的
superlative adjective of gory; involving much bloodshed and violence.
The movie contained the gorsiest scene I've ever witnessed. 这部电影包含了我所见过的最血腥的场景。
The gorsiest battle of the war was fought on this very ground. 这场战争中最血腥的战斗就在这片土地上进行。
近义词:bloodiest, ghastliest, gruesome
反义词:pleasant, beautiful, peaceful
词性:adjective

词源: From 'gory' + '-est' (superlative suffix).  'Gory' comes from Old English 'gōrig', meaning 'covered in gore (blood)'.
