burghs: /bɜːrz/ | n. | 自治市镇; 城镇（尤指苏格兰的）|  A borough is a town, or a division of a large town. The term is most often used in relation to towns in Scotland. | The ancient burghs of Scotland retain a unique charm. 苏格兰的古老自治市镇保留着独特的魅力。|  The burghs were granted certain trading privileges. 这些城镇被授予某些贸易特权。 | 近义词: towns, cities, municipalities | 反义词: countryside, rural areas | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Old English *burg, burh (“fortified place, town, city”), from Proto-Germanic *burgz (“fortress, castle, fortified town”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“high, elevated”). Cognate with German Burg (“castle”), Dutch burcht (“stronghold, citadel”), Swedish borg (“castle”).
