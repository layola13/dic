milkweed:/ˈmɪlkˌwid/| n. |乳草|A herbaceous plant that produces a milky sap and is known for being the primary food source for monarch butterfly larvae.  |例句：The monarch butterfly lays its eggs on milkweed.  帝王蝶把卵产在乳草上。


例句：Milkweed is essential for the survival of monarch butterflies. 乳草对帝王蝶的生存至关重要。|近义词：asclepias (scientific name)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *milkwede, from milk + weed.  Refers to the milky sap the plant exudes.
