backhand:/ˈbækˌhænd/| n. vt. adj.|反手击球，反手拍，反面|A stroke played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke (e.g., in tennis, badminton, squash).  Also, can refer to the hand used for such a stroke or something done in a roundabout or indirect way.|He won the point with a powerful backhand. 他以强有力的反手击球赢得了这一分.|近义词：backstroke|反义词:forehand|词性:名词，动词，形容词

backhands:/ˈbækˌhændz/| n. v.|反手击球（复数），反手拍（复数）|Plural form of "backhand". Refers to multiple backhand strokes or the hands used for them.|She practiced her backhands all afternoon. 她整个下午都在练习反手击球。|近义词：backstrokes|反义词:forehands|词性:名词，动词
