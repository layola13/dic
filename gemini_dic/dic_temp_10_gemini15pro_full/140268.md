offspring:/ˈɒfsprɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɔːfsprɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |子孙，后代，产物|A person's child or children, or the young of an animal.  A product or result of something.|
例句：The farm animals produce numerous offspring each year.  农场里的动物每年都会产下很多后代。
例句：This book is the offspring of many years of research. 这本书是多年研究的成果。
近义词：children, descendants, progeny, young, product, result|反义词: parent, ancestor |词性: noun
