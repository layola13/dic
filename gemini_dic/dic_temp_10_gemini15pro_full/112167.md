lookalike:/ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk/| n.|相似的人或物；替身；酷似某人的人|A person or thing that closely resembles another. |例句：He hired a lookalike to stand in for him at the press conference. 他雇了一个替身代替他出席新闻发布会。

例句：She bought a lookalike of her favorite handbag. 她买了一个和她最喜欢的包包很像的包。|近义词：double, twin, counterpart, replica|反义词: opposite, contrast|词性:noun


lookalike:/ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk/| adj.|相似的；酷似的|Resembling someone or something else closely. |例句：The two cars were so lookalike that it was hard to tell them apart. 这两辆车太像了，很难区分它们。

例句：They found a lookalike product at a much lower price. 他们找到了一个非常相似的产品，价格低得多。|近义词：similar, resembling, alike, identical|反义词: different, dissimilar, distinct|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "look alike" (meaning to resemble in appearance), which became compounded into a single word.
