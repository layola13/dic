ensures:/ɪnˈʃʊrz/ /ɛnˈʃʊrz/ | v. | 确保，保证 | To make certain that something will happen. | This policy ensures that all children have equal access to education. 这项政策确保所有儿童都能平等地接受教育。

The seat belt ensures your safety in case of an accident. 安全带能确保你在发生事故时安全。| 近义词: guarantees, assures, secures | 反义词: jeopardizes, endangers | verb
