infantine:/ˈɪnfənˌtaɪn/ /ˈɪnfənˌtin/ | adj. |幼稚的，孩子气的|Characteristic of or resembling an infant; childish.| Her infantine behavior embarrassed everyone at the dinner party. 她的幼稚行为让晚宴上的每个人都感到尴尬。|The boy, though ten years old, still had an infantine way of speaking.  虽然这个男孩已经十岁了，但他说话的方式仍然很孩子气。| 近义词：childish, immature, juvenile, babyish | 反义词：mature, adult, grown-up | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from French enfantin, from enfant ‘child’, from Latin infans (see infant).
