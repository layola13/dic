forlorn:/fərˈlɔːrn/ /fɔːrˈlɔːrn/| adj.|孤苦伶仃的；凄凉的；绝望的|appearing sad and lonely; marked by or showing hopelessness|例句：The forlorn figure sat on the park bench. 那个孤单的身影坐在公园的长椅上。|例句：The house stood forlorn and empty. 这房子孤零零地空在那里。|近义词：desolate, abandoned, lonely, miserable, wretched|反义词：happy, cheerful, hopeful, comforted|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Middle English *forloren, from Old English forloren (“lost, ruined, depraved, condemned, forfeited, abandoned”), past participle of forlēosan (“to lose, destroy, ruin, abandon”), from Proto-Germanic *farlēusaną (“to lose”), equivalent to for- + lose. Cognate with Dutch verloren (“lost”), German verloren (“lost”), Swedish förlora (“to lose”).
