intercommune:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmjuːn/ /ˌɪntərˈkɑːmjuːn/
| n. |互市镇;市镇联合体|An organization formed for cooperation between communes, especially in France.  A group of communes acting together. |The intercommune manages shared resources like water and waste disposal.| 近义词: municipality, district, association, federation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "individual commune")
|noun
