PHRASE->

词源:  From Latin *dīlātāre, frequentative of dīlātare (“to spread out, extend”), from lātus (“wide, broad”).

What's the dilation like? = 扩张程度如何？
Pupillary dilation = 瞳孔扩张
Cervical dilation = 宫颈扩张
The dilation of blood vessels = 血管扩张
Thermal dilation = 热膨胀


注意事项:

dilatation and dilation are both correct spellings and have the same meaning.  Dilatation is more common in British English, while dilation is more common in American English.
