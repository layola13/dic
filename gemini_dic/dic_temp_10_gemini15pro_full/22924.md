botryose:/ˈbɒtrɪˌəʊs/ (US) /ˈbɑːtriˌoʊs/| adj.|葡萄状的，似葡萄串的|Resembling a cluster of grapes; having a branched, grape-like structure.|The mineral formed botryose clusters.|Synonyms: racemose, clustered, grape-like|Antonyms: unbranched, singular|adjective

botryose:/ˈbɒtrɪˌəʊs/ (US) /ˈbɑːtriˌoʊs/| n.|葡萄状矿物|A mineral with a botryose structure.|The botryose was identified as chalcedony.|Synonyms: none (as a noun, it refers to a specific mineral formation)|Antonyms: none|noun
