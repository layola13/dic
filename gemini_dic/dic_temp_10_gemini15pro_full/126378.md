myrmecophyte:/mɜːrˈmɛkəˌfaɪt/ | n. |  蚁栖植物 | A plant that lives in a mutualistic relationship with ants, providing them with shelter (domatia) and sometimes food, while the ants protect the plant from herbivores and other threats. |  Example 1:  The acacia tree is a classic example of a myrmecophyte, offering ants hollow thorns to live in.  刺槐树是蚁栖植物的典型例子，它为蚂蚁提供空心的刺来居住。

Example 2:  Some myrmecophytes also produce food bodies rich in lipids and proteins for their ant inhabitants. 一些蚁栖植物还会为栖息其中的蚂蚁产生富含脂质和蛋白质的食物体。| 近义词: ant plant | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
