decently:/ˈdiːsntli/ | adv. |得体地，体面地，相当好地|In a proper, respectable, or socially acceptable manner; adequately or fairly well.|She was decently dressed for the interview. 她面试的着装很得体。| synonym: properly, respectably, suitably, adequately, fairly | antonym: improperly, indecently, poorly, inadequately | 词性: 副词

decently:/ˈdiːsntli/ | adv. |相当好地|Adequately or fairly well.|He plays the piano decently. 他钢琴弹得相当不错。| synonym: adequately, fairly, tolerably, quite well | antonym: poorly, badly, terribly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *descentli*, from Old French *descentment*, from *descendre* (“to descend”), from Latin *dēscendō* (“I descend”).  The adverbial suffix *-ly* is from Old English *-līce*.  The sense shift from "fittingly, suitably" to "morally well" happened in the 16th century.
