anti-capitalist:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪst/
adjective|反资本主义的|Opposed to capitalism. |Many anti-capitalist protesters gathered outside the World Bank meeting. 许多反资本主义抗议者聚集在世界银行会议场外。|
noun|反资本主义者|A person who is opposed to capitalism. |The anti-capitalists organized a demonstration against corporate greed. 反资本主义者组织了一场反对企业贪婪的示威活动。|
近义词:socialist, communist, Marxist, radical, left-wing|
反义词:capitalist, pro-capitalist, right-wing, conservative|


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite of, instead of, in place of"
capitalist: from capital + -ist
