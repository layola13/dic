palm-reading:/ˈpɑːmˌriːdɪŋ/ /ˈpɑːmˌriːdɪŋ/
n.|手相术，掌纹算命|The practice of supposedly interpreting a person's character or predicting their future by examining the lines and other features of the palms of their hands.|She went to a fortune teller for a palm-reading.|近义词：chiromancy, palmistry|反义词:|词性:noun
