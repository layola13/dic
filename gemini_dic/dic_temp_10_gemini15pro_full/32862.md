cholinergic:/ˌkoʊlɪˈnɜːrdʒɪk/| adj.|胆碱能的；胆碱的|Relating to or denoting nerve cells in which acetylcholine acts as a neurotransmitter.|例句：Cholinergic neurons are found in both the central and peripheral nervous systems. 胆碱能神经元在中枢神经系统和周围神经系统中都有发现。

例句：Cholinergic drugs enhance the effects of acetylcholine.  胆碱能药物增强乙酰胆碱的作用。|近义词:acetylcholine-mediated|反义词:anticholinergic|词性:adjective
