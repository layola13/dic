amplitudinous:/ˌæmplɪˈtjuːdɪnəs/
|adj.|
宽广的，广阔的，大量的|
Of great size or extent; spacious, vast, capacious.  Characterized by amplitude or fullness; extensive, copious.
|
The amplitudinous landscape stretched before them, seemingly endless.  广阔的风景展现在他们面前，似乎无边无际。

The museum boasted an amplitudinous collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆拥有大量的古代文物。
|
近义词：vast, immense, enormous, expansive, capacious, copious
|
反义词：small, tiny, limited, restricted, narrow
|
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *amplitudinosus, from Latin amplitūdō (“width, breadth, size, extent”) + -ōsus.  The Latin amplitūdō is derived from amplus (“large, spacious, ample”).
