compulsiveness:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvnəs/
n.|强迫性，强制性|The state of being compulsive; an irresistible urge to behave in a certain way, especially against one's conscious wishes.|His compulsiveness led him to check the locks repeatedly. 他的强迫症让他反复检查门锁。|近义词：obsession, compulsion|反义词:control, moderation|词性:名词
Etymology: From compulsive + -ness.  Compulsive from compel (from Latin compellere "to drive together, force") + -ive.
