interim:/ˈɪntərɪm/| adj. n.|暂时的，过渡的；中间时期，过渡时期|A temporary or provisional arrangement; the intervening time.|例句：An interim government was set up after the revolution.  革命后成立了一个临时政府。

例句：He took an interim job while he looked for a permanent position. 他在寻找固定职位期间，找了一份临时工作。|近义词：temporary, provisional, transitional|反义词：permanent, final|词性:形容词,名词

interim:/ˈɪntərɪm/| n.|中间时期，过渡时期|The intervening time.|例句：In the interim, we'll have to make do with what we have. 在这段过渡时期，我们只能将就着用现有的东西。

例句：The company appointed an interim CEO while searching for a permanent replacement.  公司在寻找永久替代人选期间，任命了一位临时首席执行官。|近义词：interval, meantime, meanwhile|反义词：permanence|词性:名词
