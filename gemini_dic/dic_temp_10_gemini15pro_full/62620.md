enforce:/ɪnˈfɔːrs/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːs/ (UK)| v. |执行，实施，强制|to make people obey a rule or law|The police enforce the law. 警察执行法律。|近义词：implement, apply, execute|反义词: neglect, ignore|动词

enforce:/ɪnˈfɔːrs/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːs/ (UK)| v. |强迫，迫使|to make something happen or force someone to do something|He enforced his will on the others. 他把自己的意志强加于人。|近义词：compel, coerce, oblige|反义词: allow, permit|动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enforcier "to strengthen, reinforce," from en- "in, into" + force "force, strength."
