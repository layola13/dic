fragrancy:/ˈfreɪɡrənsi/| n. |芬芳，香味，香气|A sweet or pleasant smell.|The air was filled with the fragrance of roses. 空气中弥漫着玫瑰花的香味。|近义词：aroma, scent, perfume, odor|反义词：stench, stink|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *fragrauncye*, from Middle French *fragrancie*, from Late Latin *fragrantia*, from Latin *fragrāns* (present participle of *fragrāre* (“to emit a sweet smell”), from *fragrō* (“I smell sweetly”), of uncertain origin.
