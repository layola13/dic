moby:/ˈmoʊbi/ | n. | 白色大鲸鱼；庞然大物 | A large, white whale, especially one like the fictional whale in Herman Melville's novel *Moby Dick*.  It can also refer to something huge and imposing. | Example: The old ship creaked like a moby as it sailed through the rough seas.  这艘老船在波涛汹涌的海面上航行时，像一头巨鲸一样嘎吱作响。

Example:  The new skyscraper loomed over the city like a moby. 这座新的摩天大楼像一头巨鲸一样耸立在城市上空。 | 近义词: whale, leviathan, behemoth | 反义词: minnow, dwarf | 词性: 名词
