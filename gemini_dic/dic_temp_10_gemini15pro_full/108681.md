leak:/liːk/| n. v.|泄漏，漏洞|An accidental hole or crack that lets something such as liquid or gas escape, or the escaping liquid or gas itself.  The act of allowing secret information to become generally known.|例句：The roof has a leak. 屋顶漏水。|近义词：drip, seep, escape, disclosure, divulgence|反义词:seal, closure, containment|词性:名词,动词

leak:/liːk/| n. v.|泄露，透露|The act of allowing secret information to become generally known.|例句：There have been several security leaks recently. 最近发生了几起安全泄密事件。|近义词：reveal, disclose, divulge, expose|反义词:conceal, withhold, suppress|词性:名词,动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *leka "to leak, drip," from Proto-Germanic *lekan "to drip, leak" (source also of Old English leccan "to moisten, water, irrigate").
