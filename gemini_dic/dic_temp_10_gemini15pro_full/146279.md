overfanciful: /ˌoʊvərˈfænsɪfəl/
adj.
过度想象的，过于花哨的
Too elaborate or ornate; excessively fanciful.
Her prose is sometimes criticized as being overfanciful and lacking substance. 她的散文有时被批评为过于华丽而缺乏实质内容。
The architect’s overfanciful design was ultimately rejected as impractical.  这位建筑师过于天马行空的设计最终因不切实际而被否决。
近义词: flamboyant, ornate, extravagant, elaborate
反义词: plain, simple, austere, understated
词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "fanciful" (from "fancy," ultimately from Latin "phantasia," meaning "appearance," "imagination")
