coagulation:/koʊˌæɡjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 凝结，凝固 | the process of changing from a liquid to a thickened or solid state |  The doctor was concerned about the slow coagulation of the patient's blood. 医生担心病人血液凝固缓慢。| clotting, thickening, solidification | liquefaction, dissolution | noun
