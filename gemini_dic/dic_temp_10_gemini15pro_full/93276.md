ilial:/ˈɪliæk/| adj.|髂骨的，回肠的|Relating to the ilium (the largest bone of the pelvis) or the ileum (the third portion of the small intestine).|The surgeon repaired the damage to the patient's ilial artery.  外科医生修复了病人髂动脉的损伤。|The ilial region of the small intestine is responsible for absorbing vitamin B12. 回肠负责吸收维生素B12。|近义词：pelvic, intestinal|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, as it denotes a specific anatomical location.)|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *iliacus, from Latin ilia (“flanks, loins”).
