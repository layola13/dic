ETYMOLOGY->
"discussion" comes from the Old French "discussion,"  derived from the Latin "discussió," meaning "examination, discussion," from "discuss-," past participle stem of "discutere" "to shake apart, investigate," from "dis-" "apart, asunder" + "quatere" "to shake."

"board" comes from Old English "bord" meaning "plank, table," of Germanic origin.
