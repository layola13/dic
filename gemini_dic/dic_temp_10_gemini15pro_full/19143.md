bevies:/ˈbɛviz/| n. |一群，一队（尤指女性或鸟类）|A large group, especially of women or birds. |例句：A bevy of beauties attended the ball. 一群美女参加了舞会。

|例句：A bevy of quail flew up from the field. 一群鹌鹑从田野里飞了起来。|近义词：group, flock, company, crowd, gathering|反义词:few, individual|noun
