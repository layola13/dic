herbaceously:/hɜːrˈbeɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 草本地；像草一样地 | In a way that resembles an herb, especially in having non-woody stems. |  The plants grew herbaceously, covering the ground in a soft green carpet. 这些植物长得像草一样，在地面上铺了一层柔软的绿色地毯。|  The garden was filled with herbaceously scented plants. 花园里充满了草本植物的香味。 | 近义词: grassily, weedily | 反义词:  woodily, shrubbily | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From herbaceous + -ly.  Herbaceous comes from Late Latin herbaceus "grassy," from Latin herba "grass, herb."
