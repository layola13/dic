midweek:/ˌmɪdˈwiːk/ | adj. n. | 周中的；周三 | Occurring in the middle of a week.  | We have a midweek meeting every Wednesday. 我们每周三都有一个周中会议。|  The restaurant offers a midweek special. 这家餐厅提供周中特价菜。 | 近义词: mid-week | 反义词: weekend | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *midweke, from Old English midwice (“middle of the week”), equivalent to mid +‎ week. Cognate with Middle Low German middeweke, Middle Dutch middeweke, Dutch midweek, German Mittwoch (“Wednesday”).
