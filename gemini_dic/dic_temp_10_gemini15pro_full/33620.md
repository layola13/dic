cincture:/ˈsɪŋktʃər/| n.|腰带，束带；环绕；包围|A belt, sash, or girdle worn around the waist. Something that encircles or confines.|The monk wore a simple cincture around his robe.  和尚的长袍上系着一条简单的束带。|Synonyms: belt, sash, girdle, band, ring, enclosure|Antonyms: looseness, opening, gap|noun

cincture:/ˈsɪŋktʃər/| v.|环绕，包围|To encircle or surround with a belt or band; to enclose.|Mountains cincture the valley. 群山环绕着山谷。|Synonyms: encircle, surround, encompass, gird, enclose|Antonyms: free, release, open|verb
