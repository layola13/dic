deceitful:/dɪˈsiːtfʊl/| adj.|欺骗的，不诚实的|Given to deceiving; misleading; dishonest.  Characterized by or tending to deceive or mislead.|He told a deceitful story to cover up his mistake. 他编造了一个欺骗性的故事来掩盖他的错误。|近义词：dishonest, misleading, untruthful, mendacious, fraudulent|反义词：honest, truthful, sincere, candid|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *deceitful*, from deceit + -ful.  Ultimately from Latin *dēcipere* (to deceive).
