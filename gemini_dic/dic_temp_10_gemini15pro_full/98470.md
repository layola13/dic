instabilities:/ˌɪnstəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |不稳定性，不稳定因素|The state of being unstable; lack of stability.  This can refer to physical instability, like a wobbly chair, or more abstract concepts like political or economic instability.|例句：The political instabilities in the region are a cause for concern. 该地区政治不稳定令人担忧。|近义词：unsteadiness, volatility, precariousness, fluctuation|反义词:stability, firmness, steadiness|noun
instability:/ˌɪnstəˈbɪləti/| n. |不稳定性，不稳定因素|The state of being unstable; lack of stability. This can refer to a lack of emotional or mental stability, or a tendency to change or fluctuate.|例句：The patient exhibited signs of emotional instability. 病人表现出情绪不稳定的迹象。|近义词：changeability, unpredictability, wavering|反义词:stability, constancy, equilibrium|noun
