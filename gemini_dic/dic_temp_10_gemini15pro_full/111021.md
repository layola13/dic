liroconite: /laɪˈrɒkənaɪt/
noun
铜铝砷酸盐矿物
A hydrated hydrous copper aluminum arsenate mineral, usually blue-green in color, occurring as vitreous or silky crystals.
Liroconite is a rare mineral found in oxidized copper deposits.  铜铝砷酸盐是一种罕见的矿物，发现于氧化的铜矿床中。
Liroconite specimens can be quite beautiful and are sought after by collectors.  铜铝砷酸盐标本非常漂亮，是收藏家追捧的对象。
