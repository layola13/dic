lookdown:/ˈlʊkdaʊn/| n.|轻蔑; 傲慢的态度; 俯视; (尤指从高处)向下看|An act of looking down, especially from a higher position.  A condescending or disdainful attitude. |He gave me a lookdown, as if I were beneath him. 他轻蔑地看了我一眼，好像我很卑微。|近义词：condescension, disdain, scorn, sneer|反义词：respect, admiration|词性:noun

look down:/lʊk daʊn/| v.|向下看；俯视；轻视；蔑视|To look in a downward direction. To regard with contempt or scorn.  |She looked down at the city from the top of the skyscraper. 她从摩天大楼的顶部俯瞰这座城市。 He looks down on anyone who isn't wealthy. 他瞧不起任何不富有的人。|近义词：despise, scorn, disdain|反义词：admire, respect|词性:verb
