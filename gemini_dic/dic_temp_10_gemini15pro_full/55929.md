dixielands: /ˈdɪksiˌlændz/
n.
南方乐曲；南方（尤指美国南部）
A style of jazz music that originated in New Orleans in the early 20th century, characterized by a strong two-beat rhythm and collective improvisation.  (一种爵士乐风格，起源于20世纪初的新奥尔良，其特点是强烈的二拍节奏和集体即兴演奏。)
The band played a lively set of dixielands.  乐队演奏了一组充满活力的南方爵士乐曲。
Synonym:  jazz, ragtime
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider genres like classical music or rock music)
词性: noun
