curviform:/ˈkɜːrvɪfɔːrm/
adjective
弯曲的，曲线形的
having a curved or sinuous form.
The artist specializes in creating curviform sculptures.  这位艺术家擅长创作曲线形的雕塑。
The bacteria exhibited a curviform shape under the microscope.  在显微镜下，这种细菌呈现出弯曲的形状。

近义词:curved, sinuous, bowed, arched
反义词:straight, linear, rectilinear
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *curvus* ("curved") + *-formis* ("-shaped," from *forma* "form, shape").
