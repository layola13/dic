contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃən/ | n. | 扭曲，扭歪；弯曲 |  a twisting or bending of something out of its normal shape. | The contortion of the metal was caused by the extreme heat. 金属的扭曲是由极热造成的。 | contortion of the face 面部扭曲 | synonyms: twisting, bending, warping, deformation, distortion | antonyms: straightening, aligning | noun

contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃən/ | n. | （尤指身体的）扭曲，扭歪；弯曲 |  (especially of the body) a twisted or bent condition.  |  The contortions of the acrobat were amazing. 杂技演员的扭曲令人惊叹。 |  He performed incredible contortions with his body. 他用身体做出了令人难以置信的扭曲动作。 | synonyms:  twisting, bending, writhing, squirming | antonyms: straightening, stillness | noun
