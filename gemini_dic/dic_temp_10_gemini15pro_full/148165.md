overserves:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvz/
verb
过度服务；服务过分
To provide more service than is necessary or desired.
The restaurant overserved us with bread, bringing three baskets before we even ordered.  这家餐厅给我们提供了过多的面包，在我们点餐之前就上了三篮。
The waiter overserved the wine, filling our glasses to the brim each time. 服务员倒酒倒得太多，每次都把我们的酒杯斟满。
近义词: overattend, overdo
反义词: underseve, neglect
词性:v.
