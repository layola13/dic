craggy: /ˈkræɡi/ (US) /ˈkræɡɪ/ (UK)| adj.|崎岖的，陡峭的|Having many crags; steep and rugged.|The craggy cliffs dropped sharply to the sea below. 崎岖的悬崖峭壁直插入海。|近义词: rugged, rough, uneven, jagged, precipitous|反义词: smooth, flat, even, level|词性: 形容词

Etymology: From crag + -y.  Crag (n.) comes from a Celtic source, perhaps related to Welsh *craig, "rock."
