dynamograph:/ˈdaɪnəmˌɡræf/ /ˈdaɪnəməɡræf/ | n. | 测力计，功率计 | An instrument for recording the force exerted, especially by the muscles during exercise. | Example 1: The athlete used a dynamograph to measure the strength of his grip.  运动员用测力计测量他的握力。

Example 2: The dynamograph showed a significant increase in power output after weeks of training.  测力计显示，经过几周的训练后，功率输出显著增加。 | Synonym: ergograph, dynamometer | Antonym: N/A | Noun
