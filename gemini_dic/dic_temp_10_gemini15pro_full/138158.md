northrop:/ˈnɔːrθrəp/| n.|诺斯罗普|An American aircraft manufacturer, especially known for its designs in the mid-20th century.|Northrop Grumman is a major defense contractor. 诺斯罗普·格鲁曼公司是一家主要的国防承包商。|近义词:Grumman (after the merger)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
Derived from the surname of John Northrop, one of the founders of the company.  John's surname itself is of English origin,  meaning "north village" from the Old English words "norþ" (north) and "þorp" (village).
