cajones:/kəˈhoʊneɪz/ | n. | cajones, 卡洪鼓 | A box-shaped percussion instrument played by slapping the front or sides with the hands.  Origin: Spanish, plural of cajón 'box, drawer'. |  He played a lively rhythm on the cajones. 他用卡洪鼓演奏了一段活泼的节奏。 | The band's percussionist used cajones instead of a traditional drum kit.  乐队的打击乐手用卡洪鼓代替了传统的架子鼓。 | synonyms: box drum, cajón | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument) | noun
