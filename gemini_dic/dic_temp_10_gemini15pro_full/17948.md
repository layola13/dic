befalls:/bɪˈfɔːlz/| v.|降临，发生(尤指不幸的事情)|Something unpleasant or unfortunate that happens to someone.  It typically takes a direct object and is often used in formal or literary contexts.|A great tragedy befell the family.  一个巨大的悲剧降临在这个家庭。|Synonyms: happens to, occurs to, comes upon, overtakes|Antonyms: avoids, averts|verb
