containment:/kənˈteɪnmənt/ | n. | 遏制；控制；容纳；牵制 |The act of containing something or someone, or preventing something harmful from spreading. | The government implemented a policy of containment to prevent the spread of the virus. 政府实施了遏制政策以防止病毒传播。 |  The containment of the fire prevented further damage. 控制火势防止了进一步的损害。 |Synonyms: restriction, confinement, suppression, control | Antonyms: release, spread, liberation | noun
