artsiness:/ˈɑːrtɪsinəs/
noun
做作，装腔作势，附庸风雅
Affected or pretentious artistic quality or style.
Her apartment was filled with artsiness: obscure paintings, sculptures made of driftwood, and poetry books scattered on every surface. 她的公寓里充满了附庸风雅的东西：晦涩的画作、用浮木做的雕塑，以及散落在各个表面的诗集。
The film's artsiness alienated some viewers who found it pretentious.  这部电影的做作让一些观众觉得它很装腔作势，从而疏远了他们。
近义词:pretentiousness, affectation, artificiality
反义词:genuineness, authenticity, naturalness
词性:n.
