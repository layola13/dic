non-retroactive:/ˌnɒn ˌretrəʊˈæktɪv/ /ˌnɑːn ˌretrəʊˈæktɪv/| adj.|不溯及既往的|Not applying to a period before it was implemented; not having retroactive effect.|The new tax law is non-retroactive, so it won't affect last year's taxes. 新的税法不溯及既往，所以它不会影响去年的税款。|The policy change is non-retroactive and will only apply to future cases.  这项政策变化不溯及既往，仅适用于未来的案例。|Synonyms: prospective, irretrospective|Antonyms: retroactive, retrospective|adjective
