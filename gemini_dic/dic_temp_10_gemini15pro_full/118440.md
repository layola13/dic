mesameboid:/ˌmɛsəˈmiːbɔɪd/
adjective
中变形虫的，间叶变形虫样的
Resembling or characteristic of a mesameboid; an amoeboid cell of the mesoderm.
The cells exhibited mesameboid movement. 这些细胞展现出中变形虫样的运动。
近义词: amoeboid, mesenchymal
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

词源:
"mes-" (from Greek mesos "middle") + "ameboid" (from amoeba + -oid "resembling")
