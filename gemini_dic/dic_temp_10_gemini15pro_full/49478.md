delusionist:/dɪˈluːʒənɪst/
n.
魔术师；骗子；制造错觉的人
A person who performs illusions to entertain, or one who creates or fosters false beliefs or impressions.
The delusionist amazed the audience with his incredible disappearing act.  魔术师以他令人难以置信的消失表演让观众惊叹。
He was a delusionist, promising wealth and happiness but delivering only disappointment. 他是个骗子，承诺财富和幸福，却只带来失望。
近义词：illusionist, magician, charlatan, trickster, fraud
反义词：realist
词性：名词
