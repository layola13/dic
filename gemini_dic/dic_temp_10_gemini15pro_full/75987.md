funnies:/ˈfʌniz/
n.
滑稽人物；連環漫畫；滑稽連環漫畫欄；（尤指報紙上的）幽默故事或漫畫
comic strips, especially in newspapers
I always read the funnies in the Sunday paper. 我总是阅读星期日报纸上的滑稽漫画。
synonyms: comics, comic strips
antonyms: tragedies, dramas
noun
