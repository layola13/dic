indefensibly:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsɪbli/
adjective
无法辩护地，不能原谅地
not justifiable; without any excuse or defense
His actions were indefensibly cruel. 他的行为残忍至极，不可原谅。
Synonyms: inexcusable, unjustifiable, unwarrantable, unforgivable
Antonyms: justifiable, excusable, defensible, pardonable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + defensible + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Defensible" comes from Old French "defensible," from Late Latin "defensibilis," from Latin "defendere" (to defend).
