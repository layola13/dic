orientalize: /ˌɔːriˈentəlaɪz/ /ˌɔːrˈiːɛntəˌlaɪz/
verb
使东方化;模仿东方风格;赋予东方色彩
To make (something) have characteristics or qualities associated with the cultures of East and Southeast Asia, often in a way that is inaccurate or stereotypical.
The architecture of the building was orientalized with the addition of curved roofs and intricate carvings.  这栋建筑的结构通过增加弧形屋顶和复杂雕刻而东方化了。

The writer orientalized the story by including stereotypical depictions of geishas and samurai.  这位作家通过加入对艺伎和武士的刻板描述，使故事东方化了。

近义词: exoticize, romanticize
反义词: westernize, occidentalize
词性: verb
