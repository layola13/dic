allodialism:/ˌæləˈdaɪəlɪzəm/ | n. |完全保有土地所有权;  自由保有土地权|The system of land ownership in which land is held free and clear of any rent or service due to a superior.  A system where land ownership is absolute and not subject to any feudal obligations.|  Allodialism is a rare concept in modern land ownership systems. 完全保有土地所有权在现代土地所有权体系中是一个罕见的概念。|  The settlers fought for allodialism, wanting land ownership free from feudal ties. 移民们为自由保有土地权而战，希望土地所有权摆脱封建束缚。|Synonyms: freehold, absolute ownership | Antonyms: feudal tenure, leasehold | noun
