nonconserving:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɜːrvɪŋ/ |adj.|非守恒的，不守恒的|Not conserving; specifically (Physics) not conforming to a conservation law, such as conservation of energy or momentum.  Failing to protect or preserve something, especially the natural environment.|例句：The nonconserving force caused the system's energy to decrease. 非守恒力导致系统的能量减少。

例句：Their nonconserving practices led to the depletion of natural resources. 他们不节约的做法导致了自然资源的枯竭。|近义词：dissipative, wasteful, destructive|反义词：conservative, preserving, protective|词性：形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + conserving (present participle of conserve, from Latin conservare "to preserve")
