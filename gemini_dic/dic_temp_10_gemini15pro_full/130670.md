nonadvocates:/ˌnɒnˈædvəkɪts/
noun
非倡导者；不提倡者
People who do not advocate or support a particular cause or policy.  Those who are not proponents.
The nonadvocates of the policy remained silent during the debate.  在辩论中，这项政策的非倡导者保持沉默。
The meeting included advocates and nonadvocates of the proposed changes. 会议包括提议修改的倡导者和非倡导者。

Synonyms: opponents, dissenters, objectors
Antonyms: advocates, supporters, proponents
