devotees:/ˌdɛvəˈtiːz/
n.
信徒，爱好者，热爱者
A person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something.
The guru had a small band of devoted devotees. 这位大师有一小群忠实的信徒。
synonyms:  followers, admirers, fans, enthusiasts, zealots,  aficionados
antonyms:  critics, detractors, skeptics
noun
