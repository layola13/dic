hermits:/ˈhɜːrmɪts/| n. |隐士，遁世者|A person who lives a solitary life and avoids other people, often for religious reasons. |例句：The hermits lived in caves in the mountains. 隐士们住在山里的洞穴里。

例句：He became a hermit after the death of his wife.  在他妻子去世后，他成了一个隐士。|近义词：recluse, anchorite, solitary|反义词：socialite, extrovert|Noun
