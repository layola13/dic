fiant:/ˈfaɪənt/ | n. | Warrant; authorization. | A fiant is an authorization or warrant, especially from a sovereign or high-ranking official.  | The king issued a fiant for the arrest of the traitor. 国王发布了逮捕叛徒的令状。 | Synonyms: warrant, authorization, decree, edict | Antonyms: prohibition, veto | noun

Etymology: From Anglo-French *fiant, present participle of fier "to trust," from Latin fidāre "to trust," from fidēs "faith, trust."
