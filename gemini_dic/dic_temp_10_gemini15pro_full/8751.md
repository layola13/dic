antidiscrimination:/ˌænti dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |反歧视，不歧视|The practice of opposing discrimination based on race, religion, gender, or other protected characteristics.  It refers to actions, policies, and laws aimed at preventing unfair or prejudicial treatment of individuals or groups.| The company has a strong antidiscrimination policy.  这家公司有强有力的反歧视政策。|Synonyms: equality, fairness, impartiality|Antonyms: discrimination, prejudice, bias|noun

antidiscrimination:/ˌænti dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/| adj. |反歧视的|Relating to or promoting actions, policies, and laws aimed at preventing discrimination.|  The antidiscrimination laws protect employees from unfair treatment. 反歧视法保护员工免受不公平待遇。|Synonyms: egalitarian, nondiscriminatory|Antonyms: discriminatory, prejudiced|adjective
