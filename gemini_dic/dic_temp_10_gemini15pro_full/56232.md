doff:/dɒf/ (US) /dɑːf/| vt.|脱（帽、衣等）；丢弃；摆脱|To remove (something, especially a hat or garment).|He doffed his cap to the lady. 他向那位女士脱帽致敬。|The knight doffed his helmet. 骑士摘下了他的头盔。|近义词:remove, take off, shed|反义词:don, put on|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *doffen, from *do of, equivalent to do + off.  Ultimately from Old English *dōn + of. Cognate with Dutch doffen (“to extinguish, put out, take off”), German duffen.
