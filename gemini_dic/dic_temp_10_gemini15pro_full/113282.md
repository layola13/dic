lutenists:/ˈluːtənɪsts/
n.
琵琶演奏家，鲁特琴演奏家
A person who plays the lute.
例句：The lutenists played a beautiful melody. 琵琶演奏家们演奏了一曲优美的旋律。

例句：The king employed several lutenists to entertain the court. 国王雇佣了几位琵琶演奏家来娱乐宫廷。
近义词：lute player
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-musician)
词性: noun
