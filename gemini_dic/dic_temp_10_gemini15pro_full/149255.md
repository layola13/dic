oyster:/ˈɔɪstər/ | n. |牡蛎；沉默寡言的人|A bivalve mollusk with a rough irregular shell, some kinds of which are highly valued as food.|例句：He slurped down the oyster. 他大口吞下牡蛎。

例句：She's been as close as an oyster about her plans. 她对自己的计划守口如瓶。|近义词：clam, shellfish |反义词: None |词性:noun
