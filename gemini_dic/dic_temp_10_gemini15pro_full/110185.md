lieutenancy:/luːˈtɛnənsi/ /ljuːˈtɛnənsi/
n.
中尉职位；副官职位；代理职位
the office or period of office of a lieutenant; the rank, authority, or position of a lieutenant or deputy.
He held the lieutenancy for five years. 他担任了五年的中尉职务。
synonym:  deputyship,  subordinate command,  second-in-command
antonym:  commandership, leadership
noun

lieutenancy:/luːˈtɛnənsi/ /ljuːˈtɛnənsi/
n.
中尉的管辖区域；中尉的辖区
the area over which a lieutenant has authority; a lieutenant's district.
The lieutenancy covered a vast area of the country.  这个中尉辖区覆盖了该国的大片区域。
synonym:  jurisdiction,  territory,  district
antonym:  N/A
noun
