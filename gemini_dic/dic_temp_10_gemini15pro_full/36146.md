coexistence:/ˌkəʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/ /ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/| n.|共存，共处|The peaceful coexistence of different cultures is a goal worth striving for. 不同文化之间的和平共处是一个值得努力的目标。|例句：

The two countries enjoyed a long period of peaceful coexistence.
这两个国家享有长期的和平共处。

It is possible for animals and humans to achieve coexistence.
动物和人类有可能实现共存。


|近义词：cohabitation, living together, existing together|反义词：conflict, antagonism, incompatibility|词性：名词

coexist:/ˌkəʊɪɡˈzɪst/ /ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/| v.|共存，共处|Different species of animals can coexist in the same habitat. 不同种类的动物可以在同一栖息地共存。|例句：

Can different religions coexist peacefully?
不同的宗教能和平共存吗？

The two groups coexisted without conflict.
这两个群体没有冲突地共存。

|近义词：cohabit, live together, exist together|反义词：conflict, clash, oppose|词性：动词
