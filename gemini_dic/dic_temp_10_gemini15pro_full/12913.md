assures:/əˈʃʊrz/ | v. | 保证；使确信；向…保证 | makes something certain, removes any doubt | He assured me that the project would be completed on time. 他向我保证项目会按时完成。 | synonyms: guarantees, promises, convinces | antonyms:  doubts, questions, denies | verb

assures:/əˈʃʊrz/ | v. | 使安心；使放心 | to make someone feel less worried or fearful | The doctor assured her that the operation was a routine procedure. 医生向她保证手术只是一个常规程序。 | synonyms: comforts, reassures, consoles | antonyms: worries, alarms, frightens | verb
