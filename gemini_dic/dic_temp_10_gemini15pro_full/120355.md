minimization:/ˌmɪnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |最小化，减到最小|The process of reducing something to the smallest possible amount or degree. |The company is focused on cost minimization. 公司致力于成本最小化。| synonyms: reduction, decrease, lessening | antonyms: maximization, increase, amplification | noun

minimise (British English) / minimize (American English): /ˈmɪnɪmaɪz/ | v. |最小化，使减到最小 |To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree. |We need to minimize the risk of infection. 我们需要将感染风险降到最低。| synonyms: reduce, decrease, lessen | antonyms: maximize, increase, amplify | verb
