hortative:/ˈhɔːrtətɪv/
adj.|劝告的，激励的|Encouraging; urging to some course of action; giving exhortation.|例句：The coach's hortative speech inspired the team to victory. 教练鼓舞人心的讲话激励了球队取得胜利。|近义词：encouraging, inspiring, exhortatory|反义词：discouraging, dissuasive|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *hortātīvus*, from hortātus, past participle of hortārī (“to urge, encourage”).
