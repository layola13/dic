decumbent:/dɪˈkʌmbənt/| adj.|卧姿的，躺卧的，伏卧的|Lying down; reclining; prostrate.  Characterized by a bending or drooping posture.|The decumbent tree branches reached towards the ground. 伏卧的树枝伸向地面。|近义词：recumbent, prostrate, procumbent|反义词:erect, upright|adjective
