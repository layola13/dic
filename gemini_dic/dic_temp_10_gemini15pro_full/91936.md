hypermetamorphosis:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɛtəmɔːrfəsɪs/| n. |超变态|A type of insect development characterized by more than one larval form, with marked differences between these forms and the pupa and adult.|The oil beetle undergoes hypermetamorphosis, with its first larval stage being a highly mobile triungulin.|Synonyms: complex metamorphosis|Antonyms: simple metamorphosis|noun
