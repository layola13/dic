egotistically: /ˌiːɡəˈtɪstɪkli/ /ˌeɡəˈtɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 自我中心地，自负地 | In a manner that shows excessive self-importance and preoccupation with oneself; conceitedly. | He egotistically boasted about his achievements, ignoring the contributions of his team. 他自负地吹嘘自己的成就，忽视了团队的贡献。|  He egotistically assumed everyone was interested in his stories. 他自以为是地认为每个人都对他讲的故事感兴趣。 | 近义词: conceitedly, arrogantly, selfishly, narcissistically | 反义词: humbly, modestly, selflessly | 词性: 副词
