materialistic:/məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
物质主义的；唯物论的
excessively concerned with material possessions; money-oriented.
例句：She's so materialistic; all she cares about are designer clothes and expensive cars. 她太物质了，她只在乎名牌衣服和豪车。

例句：His materialistic outlook on life prevented him from appreciating the simple joys. 他物质主义的人生观使他无法欣赏简单的快乐。

近义词：greedy, acquisitive, avaricious, mercenary
反义词：spiritual, altruistic, selfless
词性：adjective
