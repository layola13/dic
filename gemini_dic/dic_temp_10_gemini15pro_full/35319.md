cnicus: /ˈnaɪkəs/ | n. |蓟|A thistle; a genus of composite plants with prickly leaves and involucres, including the blessed thistle (Cnicus benedictus) and many species formerly included in this genus but now placed in Cirsium and other genera. | The field was covered in cnicus. 这片田野上长满了蓟。 | 近义词: thistle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant genus) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *cnicus*, from Ancient Greek κνῆκος (*knēkos*) "saffron thistle, bastard saffron".
