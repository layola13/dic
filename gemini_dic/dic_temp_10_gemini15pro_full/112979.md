lullaby: /ˈlʌləbaɪ/ (US) /ˈlʌləbʌɪ/ (UK) | n. | 摇篮曲；催眠曲 | A gentle song sung to soothe a baby or young child to sleep. |  She sang a lullaby to her baby. 她给她的宝宝唱了一首摇篮曲。

My grandmother used to sing me a lullaby every night. 我的祖母过去每晚都给我唱一首摇篮曲。


近义词: cradlesong,  berceuse | 反义词:  wake-up call, reveille | 词性: noun
