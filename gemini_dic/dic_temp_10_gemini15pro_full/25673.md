bungert:/ˈbʊŋɡərt/| n.|果园，果树园|A bungert is a type of orchard or fruit garden, especially one common in the Rhineland region of Germany, where fruit trees are interspersed with other plants and often used for communal gatherings and celebrations.|例句：The village bungert was the center of many summer festivities. 村里的果园是许多夏季节庆活动的中心。

例句：We spent a lovely afternoon picnicking in the bungert. 我们在一个美好的下午在果园里野餐。|近义词：orchard, fruit garden|反义词: - |词性:noun
