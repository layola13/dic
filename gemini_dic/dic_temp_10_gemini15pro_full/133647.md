nonformatively:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːrmətɪvli/ | adv. | 非形成地，不构成地 |  In a way that does not contribute to the formation or development of something; without formative influence.  It describes something that doesn't shape or mold something else in a fundamental way. | The feedback was given nonformatively, simply to gauge understanding, not to influence the final grade.  这项反馈是非形成性地给出的，仅仅是为了衡量理解程度，而不是影响最终成绩。|  non-constitutively, incidentally | formatively | adverb
