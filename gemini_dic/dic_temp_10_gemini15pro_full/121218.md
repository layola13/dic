misemphasizes:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪz/ | v. | 错误地强调；不恰当地强调 | To stress or emphasize something incorrectly or give it too little importance. | The report misemphasizes the role of technology in the company's success.  这份报告错误地强调了技术在公司成功中的作用。 |  underemphasizes, downplays | overemphasizes, exaggerates | verb

Etymology:
mis- (prefix): wrong, bad, wrongly
emphasize (verb): to give special importance or prominence to (something)
