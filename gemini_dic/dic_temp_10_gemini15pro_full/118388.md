merman:/ˈmɜːmæn/ (US) /ˈmɜːmən/ (UK)| n.|人鱼（男性）|A mythical male sea creature with the head and torso of a man and the tail of a fish.|例句：Sailors told tales of mermen luring ships to their doom. 水手们讲述了人鱼引诱船只走向毁灭的故事。

例句：The merman swam gracefully through the coral reef. 人鱼优雅地在珊瑚礁中游动。

近义词：sea creature, merfolk, merpeople|反义词:mermaid (specifically female merfolk), land dweller|词性:noun
