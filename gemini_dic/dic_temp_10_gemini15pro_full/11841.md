arrestingly:/əˈrɛstɪŋli/
adjective
引人注目地，醒目地
Strikingly; in a manner that attracts attention.
Her arrestingly beautiful features captivated everyone in the room. 她引人注目的美貌迷住了房间里的每一个人。
Synonyms: captivatingly, strikingly, compellingly, fascinatingly, mesmerizingly
Antonyms: dully, uninterestingly, plainly, simply
词性: 副词
