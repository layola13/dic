lurp:/lɜːrp/ | v. n. |发出啧啧的声音（吃东西或喝东西时）; 啧啧的声音; 浓稠的糊状物 |To eat or drink something noisily or with a sucking sound; a thick, sloppy mixture. |例句：He lurped his soup loudly, much to his mother's annoyance. 他大声地喝着汤，让他妈妈很恼火。

例句：The monster lurped out of the shadows.  怪物发出啧啧的声音从阴影中走了出来。|近义词：slurp, gulp, guzzle |反义词: sip, nibble |词性: 动词, 名词

词源:  Likely imitative of the sound made when eating or drinking something thick.  可能是模仿吃或喝浓稠东西时发出的声音。
