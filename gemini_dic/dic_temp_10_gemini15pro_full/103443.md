jk:/dʒeɪˈkeɪ/| abbreviation | 开玩笑，只是开玩笑 | Short for "just kidding". Used online and in text messages to indicate that something just said was not meant seriously. | Example: "I'm going to sell all my belongings and move to a desert island jk" 我要卖掉我所有的东西，搬到一个荒岛上去，开玩笑的 | 近义词:joking, kidding | 反义词:serious, for real | abbreviation
