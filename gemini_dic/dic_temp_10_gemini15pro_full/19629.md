bijoutry: /ˈbiːʒuːtri/ | n. | 珠宝; 珠宝首饰 | Small, delicate pieces of jewelry, often ornate and decorative.  It can refer to a single piece or a collection.  Generally considered more showy and less valuable than fine jewelry. | She wore a beautiful piece of bijoutry to the opera. 她戴着一件漂亮的首饰去歌剧院。 | Synonyms: trinkets, baubles, costume jewelry | Antonyms: fine jewelry | noun
