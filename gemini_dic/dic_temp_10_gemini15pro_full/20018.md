biomagnetic:/ˌbaɪoʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
生物磁性的
relating to the magnetic fields produced by living organisms or the study of such fields
The biomagnetic signals from the brain are incredibly faint.  大脑的生物磁信号极其微弱。

近义词:magnetobiological
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider non-magnetic)
词性:adjective
