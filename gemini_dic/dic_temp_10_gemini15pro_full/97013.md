infausting:/ɪnˈfɔːstɪŋ/
adjective
不吉利的，不幸的
Unlucky, ill-omened; unfavorable.
The infausting storm delayed their journey. 不祥的风暴耽搁了他们的旅程。
近义词: ominous, unfavorable, unfortunate, unlucky, ill-fated
反义词: auspicious, favorable, fortunate, lucky, propitious
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *infaustus*, from in- "not" + faustus "favorable, lucky".
