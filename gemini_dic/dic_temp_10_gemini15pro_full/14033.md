auspicate:/ˈɔːspɪkeɪt/| v. |预示；预兆；赞助；以吉兆开始|To inaugurate or begin, often with a ceremony or ritual suggesting good fortune; to predict or foreshadow.|The new CEO auspicated his tenure with a company-wide address outlining his vision. 新任首席执行官以一次公司全体大会致辞来开始他的任期，概述了他的愿景。|近义词：inaugurate, commence, initiate, foreshadow, portend|反义词: terminate, conclude, cease|词性:动词
词源: From Latin *auspicatus*, past participle of *auspicari* "to take the auspices," from *avis* "bird" + *specere* "to look at." Originally, it referred to observing the flight of birds to predict the future.
