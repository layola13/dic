nerite:/ˈnɛraɪt/ /ˈnɪraɪt/| n.|蝾螺; 笠螺;  |A small, usually brightly coloured marine snail of warm seas, with a thick, rounded shell.|Nerites are often found in tide pools.  蝾螺经常在潮池中被发现.|Synonyms: snail, periwinkle, whelk |Antonyms: None|noun

nerite:/ˈnɛraɪt/ /ˈnɪraɪt/| adj.|蝾螺的; 笠螺的;|Relating to or belonging to the family Neritidae.|The nerite shell was beautifully patterned.  这个蝾螺壳图案很漂亮。|Synonyms: None |Antonyms: None|adjective
