Example 2:  There was a strong sense of fellow-feelings among the refugees.  难民之间有一种强烈的同感。|Synonyms: empathy, compassion, sympathy, solidarity, camaraderie, rapport |Antonyms: apathy, indifference, hostility, animosity |noun
