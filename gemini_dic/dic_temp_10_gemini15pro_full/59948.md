edibilities:/ˌɛdɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|可食性；可食用性|The edibilities of certain wild mushrooms are still debated. 某些野生蘑菇的可食性仍然存在争议。|
|例句：The chef carefully assessed the edibilities of the various plants. 厨师仔细评估了各种植物的可食性。|
|例句：Research is ongoing into the edibilities of insects as a sustainable food source.  关于昆虫作为可持续食物来源的可食性的研究正在进行中。|
|近义词： palatability, comestibility|
|反义词：inedibility, toxicity|
|词性: 名词 (plural noun)|


edibility:/ˌɛdɪˈbɪlɪti/

|例句：He tested the edibility of the berries cautiously. 他小心翼翼地测试了浆果的可食性。 |
|近义词： palatability, comestibility|
|反义词：inedibility, toxicity|
|词性: 名词|


edible:/ˈɛdɪbl/
|adj.|可食用的|These berries are edible, but those are poisonous. 这些浆果可以食用，但那些有毒。|
|例句：The cake looked beautiful but wasn't very edible.  蛋糕看起来很漂亮，但不太好吃。|
|例句：Only the leaves of this plant are edible. 这种植物只有叶子可以食用。|
|近义词：eatable, comestible|
|反义词：inedible, poisonous, toxic|
|词性: 形容词|


词源:  From Late Latin edibilis "eatable," from Latin edere "to eat."
