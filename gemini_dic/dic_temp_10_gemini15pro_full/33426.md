churchwide:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃwaɪd/
adj.
遍及全教会的，全教会范围的
Extending throughout a church or denomination; involving the whole church.
The churchwide campaign for a new building was successful.  全教会范围内的新建筑募捐活动很成功。
The churchwide decision to implement the new program was met with mixed reactions.  全教会推行新计划的决定获得了好坏参半的反应。
近义词：church-wide, denominational, universal (within the church context)
反义词：local, congregational
词性：形容词
