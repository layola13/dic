dispositor:/dɪˈspɒzɪtər/| n.| 支配星|The planet that rules the sign in which another planet is placed, in astrology.  (占星学中)支配某个行星所在星座的行星|例句：Mars is the dispositor of Venus in Aries. 火星是白羊座中金星的支配星。

例句：The dispositor of the Sun shows how one expresses their core identity. 太阳的支配星显示了一个人如何表达他们的核心身份。|近义词：ruler, lord (in astrology)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dispositor, from Latin disponere "to arrange, distribute, dispose," from dis- "apart, asunder" + ponere "to put, place."
