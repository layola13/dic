disarray:/dɪsəˈreɪ/ | n. |混乱，紊乱|A state of disorder; confusion.|The room was in complete disarray after the party. 房间在派对后一片狼藉。|Near synonyms: disorder, chaos, confusion, mess|Near antonyms: order, tidiness, organization|noun


disarray:/dɪsəˈreɪ/ | vt. |使混乱，使紊乱|To throw into disorder; disarrange.|The sudden storm disarrayed the garden. 突如其来的暴风雨把花园弄得乱七八糟。|Near synonyms: disorganize, disorder, muddle, unsettle|Near antonyms: organize, arrange, order, tidy|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desarroi* (modern French désarroi), from des- (expressing reversal) + arroi ("array, order").
