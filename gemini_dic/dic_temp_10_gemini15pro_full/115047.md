mandragora:/ˈmændrəɡɔːrə/ (US), /ˈmændrəɡərə/ (UK)| n. |曼德拉草，曼陀罗|A plant of the nightshade family, with narcotic properties. Historically, its root was thought to resemble a human figure and to have magical powers.|例句：In folklore, the mandragora root was said to scream when pulled from the ground.  民间传说中，曼德拉草的根被拔出地面时会尖叫。

例句：The mandragora plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.  曼德拉草在传统医学中已经使用了几个世纪。|近义词：mandrake|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
