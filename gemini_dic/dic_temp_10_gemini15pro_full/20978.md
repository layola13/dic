blende:/blɛnd/ | n. | 混合物，闪锌矿 | A mineral that is the main ore of zinc. It is a sulfide of zinc and iron, and usually occurs as dark brown or black crystals. |  The miners discovered a rich vein of blende. 矿工们发现了一条丰富的闪锌矿脉。 | synonyms: sphalerite, zinc blende, mock lead, false galena | antonyms: N/A | noun

blende:/blend/ | v. | 混合，融合 | To mix or combine together. | The artist blended the colors skillfully. 艺术家巧妙地混合了颜色。| synonyms: mix, combine, merge, fuse | antonyms: separate, divide | verb
