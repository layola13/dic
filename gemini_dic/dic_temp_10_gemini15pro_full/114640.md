malevolent:/məˈlɛvələnt/| adj.|恶毒的；恶意的；有害的|Wishing evil or harm to another or others; showing ill will; ill-disposed; malicious.|He gave me a malevolent look. 他恶狠狠地瞪了我一眼。|Synonyms: malicious, spiteful, wicked, evil, vicious, malignant, hateful, baleful|Antonyms: benevolent, kind, benign, compassionate|adjective

malevolence:/məˈlɛvələns/| n.|恶意；狠毒|The state or condition of being malevolent; ill will; malice; hatred.|His eyes were filled with malevolence. 他的眼里充满了恶意。|Synonyms: malice, spite, spitefulness, venom, rancor, animosity, hostility, ill will, hatred|Antonyms: benevolence, kindness, goodwill, compassion|noun
