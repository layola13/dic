malfatti:/mælˈfɑːti/ | n. |  马尔法蒂（一种意大利面疙瘩） | A type of Italian dumpling, typically made with ricotta cheese and spinach, served in a sauce. |  We had malfatti with a sage butter sauce. 我们吃了鼠尾草黄油酱汁的马尔法蒂。 | 近义词: gnocchi, dumplings | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

malfatto:/mælˈfætəʊ/ /mɑlˈfatto/ | adj. |  做得不好的；拙劣的 (Italian origin, used in English primarily in art/design contexts) |  Badly made; poorly executed.  Referring to a work of art, especially a painting, poorly or clumsily done. | The painting was a malfatto imitation of a Renaissance masterpiece. 这幅画是对文艺复兴时期杰作拙劣的模仿。| 近义词:  poor, shoddy, clumsy, inept | 反义词: well-made, skillful, excellent | 词性: adjective
