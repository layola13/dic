fin:/fɪn/
n.|鱼鳍，鳍状物|A thin, flat part that sticks out from the body of a fish, used for swimming and balance. |The fish uses its fins to propel itself through the water. 鱼用它的鳍在水中推进。|近义词:flipper, blade|反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:名词

fin:/fɪn/
n.|（飞机、火箭、汽车等的）尾翼，鳍|A stabilizing or guiding part on an aircraft, rocket, or car, resembling a fin. |The airplane's tail fin helps to control its direction. 飞机的尾翼有助于控制方向。|近义词:stabilizer, airfoil|反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:名词
