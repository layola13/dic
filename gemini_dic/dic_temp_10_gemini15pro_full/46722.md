danda:/ˈdændə/| n.|棍棒; 杖; 惩罚; 警棍|A stick, rod, staff, or similar object, often used for punishment or as a symbol of authority.  It can also refer to a punishment itself.  Originates from Hindi/Sanskrit.|The guru carried a danda.  古鲁拿着一根棍棒。|synonyms: stick, rod, staff, cane, mace, baton|antonyms: reward, praise|noun
