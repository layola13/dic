flagless:/ˈflæɡləs/| adj.|无旗的，不挂旗的|Without a flag.  Derived from "flag" (meaning a piece of cloth bearing a distinctive design) + "-less" (a suffix indicating absence).|The flagless ship sailed into the harbor. 那艘没有挂旗的船驶入了港口。|The flagless car sped through the checkpoint. 那辆没有挂旗的汽车飞驰过了检查站。|近义词：unflagged|反义词: flagged|词性:形容词
