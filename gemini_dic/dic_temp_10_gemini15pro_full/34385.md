classifiers:/ˈklæsɪfaɪərz/
n.|分类器，分类者|A person or thing that classifies something.  A computer program that assigns items to categories based on shared characteristics.|The library uses a sophisticated system of classifiers to organize its books. 图书馆使用复杂的分类系统来整理书籍。|synonyms: categorizers, sorters|antonyms: aggregators, combiners|noun
