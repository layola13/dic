anticlimaxes: /ˌæntikˈlaɪmæksiːz/
noun
anticlimaxes (plural noun)
虎头蛇尾；扫兴的结局；突降法
a disappointing end to an exciting or impressive series of events
The ending of the movie was a real anticlimax after all the hype. 这部电影的结局真是虎头蛇尾，与之前的宣传大相径庭。
anticlimaxes (plural noun)
(修辞)突降法
a rhetorical device in which statements gradually descend in order of importance, sometimes for humorous effect
The comedian used anticlimax to great effect in his routine. 喜剧演员在他的表演中巧妙地运用了突降法。

近义词: disappointment, letdown, bathos
反义词: climax, culmination, peak
词性: noun (plural)
