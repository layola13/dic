oftenly: /ˈɒfənli/ (Non-standard) | adv. | 经常地 |  "Oftenly" is generally considered non-standard English. The correct and preferred form is "often". It's likely a redundant formation, combining "often" with the adverbial suffix "-ly". | *Oftenly* he walks to work (incorrect). Often he walks to work (correct). 他经常步行上班。| 近义词: often, frequently, regularly | 反义词: rarely, seldom, infrequently | 词性: Adverb (non-standard)
