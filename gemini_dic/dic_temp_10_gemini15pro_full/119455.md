micromanagers:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒərz/ | n. | 微观管理者；事无巨细的管理者 |  A micromanager is someone who observes and/or controls the work of their subordinates or employees excessively and unnecessarily.  |  The micromanagers constantly checked on their team's progress, stifling creativity and morale.  微观管理者不断检查团队的进度，扼杀了创造力和士气。

The new CEO wasn't a micromanager, giving his team autonomy to make decisions. 新任CEO不是一个事无巨细的管理者，他赋予他的团队自主决策权。| 近义词: control freaks, overseers | 反义词: delegators, macromanagers | 词性: 名词 (plural)
