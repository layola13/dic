identification:/aɪˌdentɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 辨认，鉴定；识别；认同；身份证明 | the act of recognizing and naming someone or something; the act of finding out who someone is or what something is |  The identification of the suspect took several weeks.  对嫌疑人的身份认定花了几个星期。| synonyms: recognition, pinpointing, determination, verification | antonyms: confusion, anonymity | noun

identification:/aɪˌdentɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 认同 | a strong feeling of sympathy, understanding, and emotional connection with another person or group of people |  Her identification with the feminist movement began in her college years. 她对女权运动的认同始于她的大学时代。| synonyms: empathy, sympathy, association | antonyms: disassociation, alienation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *identificatio, from Latin identificare “to identify,” from ident- (variant of idem “same”) + -ficare (from facere “to make”).
