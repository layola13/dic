nondoubter:/ˌnɑːnˈdaʊtər/ | n. | 无疑者；坚信者；不怀疑者 |  A person who does not doubt; someone with firm belief or certainty. | He is a nondoubter when it comes to the benefits of exercise. 他坚信锻炼的好处。 | 近义词: believer, adherent, devotee | 反义词: doubter, skeptic, cynic | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- + doubter. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "doubter" comes from the verb "doubt," which ultimately derives from the Old French "douter," meaning "to be uncertain."
