haplostemonous:/ˌhæpləˈstɛmənəs/
adjective
单轮雄蕊的
Having only one whorl or series of stamens, equal in number to the petals and alternating with them.
The haplostemonous flower structure is a key characteristic of this plant family.  这种植物科的主要特征是单轮雄蕊的花结构。
This species exhibits a haplostemonous arrangement of its stamens.  该物种的雄蕊呈单轮排列。
近义词: isostemonous (sometimes)
反义词: diplostemonous, polystemonous
词性: adjective
