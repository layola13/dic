词源: From Old French cisel, from Vulgar Latin *cisellum, from Latin caesellum, diminutive of caedere ("to cut").

注意事项:  chiseling 还有“骗取”的意思，例如 chiseling someone out of money (骗取某人的钱).  这层含义的近义词包括 defrauding 和 swindling.
