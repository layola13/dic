delusion:/dɪˈluːʒən/ |n.| 错觉，妄想|A fixed false belief that is not based in reality.|He suffers from delusions of grandeur. 他患有夸大妄想。|synonyms: hallucination, illusion, misconception, fallacy|antonyms: reality, truth|noun

delusional:/dɪˈluːʒənl/ |adj.| 妄想的，错觉的|Characterized by or holding delusions.|His delusional thinking led him to believe he was a king.  他妄想的思维让他相信自己是个国王。|synonyms: hallucinatory, illusionary, mistaken, false|antonyms: realistic, rational, sane|adjective
