featheriness:/ˈfeðərɪnɪs/
n.
轻盈；羽毛状；绒毛状
The quality of being light and airy like a feather.
The featheriness of the dandelion seeds allowed them to float on the breeze. 蒲公英种子的轻盈使它们能够随风飘浮。
lightness, fluffiness, airiness
heaviness, denseness
noun
