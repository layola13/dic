oilskin:/ˈɔɪlskɪn/ | n. | 油布；油布雨衣 | a cotton fabric made waterproof by treatment with oil or varnish. |  The fisherman wore an oilskin to protect himself from the rain. 渔夫穿了一件油布雨衣来防雨。 | synonyms: raincoat, waterproof jacket | antonyms:  | noun


oilskin:/ˈɔɪlskɪn/ | adj. | 油布制的；防水的 | made of oilskin. | He had an oilskin hat to keep his head dry. 他有一顶油布帽子用来保持头部干燥。 | synonyms: waterproof, water-repellent | antonyms: permeable, absorbent | adjective


Etymology:  From 'oil' + 'skin'.  The word refers to skin treated with oil to make it waterproof, later referring to the treated fabric itself.
