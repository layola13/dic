deflator:/dɪˈfleɪtər/ | n. | 通货紧缩因素；平减指数 | Something that deflates; specifically, a factor or index used to adjust data for changes in purchasing power, such as inflation. | The GDP deflator is a measure of the level of prices of all new, domestically produced, final goods and services in an economy. 国内生产总值平减指数衡量的是一个经济体中所有新的、国内生产的、最终商品和服务的价格水平。|  The consumer price index is a commonly used deflator. 消费者价格指数是一种常用的平减指数。 | synonyms: price index, price deflator | antonyms: inflator | noun


deflators:/dɪˈfleɪtərz/ | n. | 通货紧缩因素（复数）；平减指数（复数）| The plural form of "deflator."  |  Various economic deflators are used to adjust for inflation.  各种经济平减指数被用来调整通货膨胀。 |  Governments use deflators to compare economic output across different years. 政府使用平减指数来比较不同年份的经济产出。 | synonyms: price indices, price deflators | antonyms: inflators | noun
