drawler:/ˈdrɔːlər/| n. |慢吞吞说话的人;  拖长声音说话的人|Someone who speaks slowly and drawls their words. |He's such a drawler, it takes him forever to finish a sentence. 他说话慢吞吞的，要花很长时间才能说完一句话。|近义词：slow speaker, dawdler|反义词：fast talker, eloquent speaker|noun
