marsala:/mɑːrˈsɑːlɑː/ /mɑːrˈseɪlə/| n. |玛萨拉酒，玛萨拉色|A fortified wine produced around the town of Marsala in Sicily, Italy. Also, a brownish-red color resembling this wine. |例句：She ordered a glass of marsala after dinner. 她晚餐后点了一杯玛萨拉酒。

例句：The walls were painted a deep marsala, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. 墙壁被漆成了深玛萨拉色，营造出一种温暖宜人的氛围。|近义词：wine, color, hue|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
