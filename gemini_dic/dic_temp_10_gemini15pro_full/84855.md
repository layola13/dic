hangdog:/ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhæŋˌdɔːɡ/ (US)| adj.| 沮丧的，垂头丧气的，愧疚的 |Having a dejected or guilty appearance; shamefaced.  It suggests a drooping posture and a downcast expression, like a dog that has been scolded. | He had a hangdog expression after being caught cheating on the test.  他考试作弊被抓后，一脸垂头丧气的表情。|  The child walked in with a hangdog look, clearly having done something wrong. 孩子垂头丧气地走进来，显然是做错了什么事。 | Synonyms: ashamed, guilty, sheepish, dejected, downcast | Antonyms:  proud, cheerful,  confident, unashamed | adjective
