lobes:/loʊbz/| n. |叶；瓣；耳垂|A rounded projection or division, especially of an organ such as the lung, brain, or liver. |The frontal lobes of the brain are responsible for higher cognitive functions. 大脑的额叶负责高级认知功能。

The doctor examined the lobes of her lungs. 医生检查了她肺的叶。|segments, sections, divisions|whole, complete|noun


lobes:/loʊbz/| n. |(尤指外耳的) 耳垂 |The fleshy pendulous part of the external ear. |Her earrings dangled from her lobes. 她的耳环从耳垂上垂下来。|earlobes| |noun
