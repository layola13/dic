bitumed:/ˈbɪtʃuːmd/ /ˈbaɪtʃuːmd/
adjective
沥青的；铺上沥青的
Covered with bitumen.
例句：
The bitumed road shimmered in the heat.  沥青路面在高温下闪闪发光。

The driveway was newly bitumed.  车道新铺了沥青。


近义词：asphalted, tarred, paved
反义词:unpaved, dirt, gravel
词性:adjective
