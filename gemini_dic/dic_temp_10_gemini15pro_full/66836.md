expatiator:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪtər/ /ɛkˈspeɪʃieɪtər/
noun
详述者，细说者
One who expatiates; one who enlarges in discourse or writing.
The expatiator droned on for hours about the minute details of his trip.  这位详述者就他旅行的细枝末节喋喋不休地讲了几个小时。
近义词:commentator, elucidator, expounder
反义词:summarizer, abridger
词性:n.
