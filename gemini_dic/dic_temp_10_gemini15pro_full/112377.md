losers: /ˈluːzərz/ (US) /ˈluːzəz/ (UK)| n. |失败者，输家|A person or thing that loses or has lost something, especially a competition or game.  A person who is considered to be socially awkward or unsuccessful.|The losers of the competition went home disappointed. 比赛的输家失望地回家了。|Synonyms: defeated, unsuccessful, also-rans |Antonyms: winners, victors|Noun
