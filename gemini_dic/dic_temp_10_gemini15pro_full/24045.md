breeziest:/ˈbriːziɪst/| adj.|最轻快的，微风习习的| characterized by a gentle breeze; most cheerful and carefree|The breeziest day of the summer was perfect for a picnic. 夏天最凉爽的一天最适合野餐。|近义词：airiest, freshest, lightest|反义词：stuffiest, closest, sultriest|词性: 形容词

词源:
From breeze + -y + -est. Ultimately from the Old French *brese "northeast wind."
