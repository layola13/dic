nondesecrations:/ˌnɒndɛsɪˈkreɪʃənz/
|n.| 未亵渎，未玷污 |The preservation of the ancient burial grounds ensured the nondesecrations of the sacred sites.  保护古代墓地确保了圣地的未被亵渎。| Absence of desecration, respect, reverence | The nondesecration of the temple was paramount.  寺庙的未被亵渎至关重要。| sanctity, preservation | desecration, profanation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
desecration: from Late Latin *desecrationem,  from desecrare "to consecrate the opposite of, profane," from de- "reverse of, opposite of" + sacrare "to make sacred, consecrate," from sacer "sacred."
