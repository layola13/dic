cheer:/tʃɪr/| n. v.| 欢呼，喝彩；振奋，使高兴 | A shout of encouragement, praise, or joy.  To give a shout of encouragement, praise, or joy. | The crowd erupted in cheers as the team scored.  人群爆发出欢呼声，因为球队得分了。|  He cheered her up with a funny story. 他用一个有趣的故事让她高兴起来。 | 近义词: applaud, encourage, exhilarate | 反义词: boo, discourage, depress | 词性: 名词, 动词

词源: From Anglo-French *cherer "face, countenance, reception," from Late Latin cara "face." The original sense was "face, expression," later "frame of mind." The sense of "acclamation of applause or encouragement" is first recorded in the late 13c.
