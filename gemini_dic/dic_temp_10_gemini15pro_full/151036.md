parapet: /ˈpærəpɪt/ (US), /ˈpærəpɛt/ (UK) | n. | 栏杆，护墙，女儿墙 | A low protective wall along the edge of a roof, bridge, or balcony. |  The soldiers hid behind the parapet, firing at the enemy. 士兵们躲在护墙后面，向敌人开火。

The cat walked along the parapet of the roof. 猫沿着屋顶的女儿墙走。

近义词: railing, balustrade, barrier | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
