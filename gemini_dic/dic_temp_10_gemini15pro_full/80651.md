goramy:/ˈɡɔːrəmi/ /ɡəˈrɑːmi/ | n. |  丝足鱼，吻鲈，厚唇鱼 | A freshwater fish of the family Osphronemidae, native to Southeast Asia, popular in aquariums and cultivated for food.  These fish are known for their labyrinth organ, which allows them to breathe atmospheric air. |  Example: We have several colorful gouramis in our fish tank.  我们鱼缸里有好几条色彩鲜艳的丝足鱼。

Example:  Gouramis are relatively easy to care for, making them a popular choice for aquarium hobbyists.  丝足鱼相对容易照顾，使它们成为水族爱好者们的热门选择。| 近义词: fish, Osphronemid | 反义词:  None (no direct opposite for a type of fish) | 词性: noun
