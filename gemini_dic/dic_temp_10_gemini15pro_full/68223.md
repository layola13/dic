fairing:/ˈferɪŋ/| n.|整流罩，导流罩，外壳，(尤指飞机、摩托车或自行车的)流线型外壳;  装饰，装潢;  (集市或节日等的)庆祝活动，市集|A structure whose shape reduces drag from air resistance, especially on an aircraft, motorcycle, or bicycle. Also refers to decorative additions or festive celebrations. |The motorcycle's fairing protected the rider from the wind.  这辆摩托车的整流罩保护骑手免受风吹。|Synonyms: cowling, cladding, shell, casing, covering, housing, streamlining, decoration, adornment, festival, fair|Antonyms:  (For festival/fair) closure, cancellation|Noun
