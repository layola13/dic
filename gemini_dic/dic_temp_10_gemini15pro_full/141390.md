operettists:/ˌɑːpəˈrɛtɪsts/ | n. |轻歌剧作曲家；轻歌剧演员|A composer or performer of operettas.  A person who writes, performs, or produces operettas.| The operettists of the early 20th century created a unique blend of musical styles.  20世纪早期的轻歌剧作曲家创造了一种独特的音乐风格融合。|Synonyms: composers, performers, musicians |Antonyms: audience, listeners|noun
