bookended:/ˈbʊkˌɛndɪd/
adjective
以...开始和结束的;夹在中间的
having something at the beginning and end of a period of time, or with particular things or events at the beginning and end
The film is bookended by two dramatic scenes. 这部电影以两个戏剧性的场景开始和结束。
近义词: bracketed, enclosed, bordered
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "uninterrupted," "continuous")
词性: adjective

bookend:/ˈbʊkˌɛnd/
noun
书挡
a support placed at the end of a row of books to keep them upright
He bought a pair of brass bookends. 他买了一对黄铜书挡。
近义词: book support
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun

bookend:/ˈbʊkˌɛnd/
verb
以...开始和结束
to mark or form the beginning and end of (something)
The concert was bookended by two of their most popular songs. 这场音乐会以他们的两首最受欢迎的歌曲开始和结束。
近义词: bracket, enclose, border
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "interrupt," "disrupt")
词性: verb
