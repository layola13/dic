antiloquy:/ænˈtɪləkwi/
n.
反论; 对话; 对白
A response or contradiction; a conversation or dialogue, especially a formal or literary one.
Antiloquy between the two characters revealed their contrasting philosophies.  两个角色之间的反论揭示了他们截然不同的哲学。
The play featured a witty antiloquy between the hero and the villain.  这部戏的特点是英雄和反派之间机智的对白。
近义词:dialogue, conversation, discourse, exchange
反义词:monologue, soliloquy
词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *antiloquium, from anti- "against" + loquium "speech,"  from loqui "to speak."
