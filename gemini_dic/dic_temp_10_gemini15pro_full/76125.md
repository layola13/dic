furus:/ˈfʊərəs/| n.|皮毛，毛皮|Furus is a valuable commodity. 毛皮是一种珍贵的商品。|Example:  The furus of the arctic fox is highly prized. 北极狐的皮毛非常珍贵。

Example:  She wore a coat made of luxurious furus. 她穿着一件用奢华毛皮制成的大衣。|Synonyms: fur, pelt|Antonyms: hide, skin|noun


词源:

From Latin *fūrus, an adjective meaning "fiery, reddish yellow," also used as a noun for "thief, robber."  The sense shift to "fur" likely occurred because reddish-yellow furs were highly valued.  The word entered English via Old French forre.
