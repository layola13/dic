forebell:/ˈfɔːrˌbɛl/| n.|前钟，前导铃|A bell rung before a service or event, especially in a church, to signal its imminent start.|The forebell rang, summoning the parishioners to the church.|近义词：warning bell, preludial bell|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *forebelle*, equivalent to fore + bell.  Fore meaning "before" or "in front".
