crossings:/ˈkrɒsɪŋz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsɪŋz/ (US)| n. |十字路口，交叉口，渡口，穿越|A place where lines, roads, or tracks intersect; a place designated for crossing a street or river; the act of crossing something.|The train slowed down as it approached the railroad crossings. 火车在接近铁路交叉口时减速了。|近义词：intersection, junction, crossroads, transit, passage|反义词：divergence, separation|词性:名词
