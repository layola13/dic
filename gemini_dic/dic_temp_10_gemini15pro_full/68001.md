factitiously:/fækˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
人为地；不自然地；虚构地
in a factitious manner; artificially; not naturally or genuinely.
He coughed factitiously to attract attention. 他假咳嗽以引起注意。
factitiously created evidence 虚构的证据

近义词: artificially, synthetically, spuriously, falsely
反义词: genuinely, naturally, authentically
词性: adverb

factitious:/fækˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
人为的；不自然的；虚构的；假的
artificial rather than natural; not genuine.
a factitious enthusiasm  一种虚假的热情
The demand for the product was factitious, created by advertising. 对该产品的需求是人为的，是由广告创造出来的。
近义词: artificial, contrived, bogus, sham, spurious
反义词: genuine, natural, authentic
词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin facticius (“made by art”), from factus (“made, done”), perfect passive participle of faciō (“make, do”).
