coati:/koʊˈɑːti/ | n. | 南美浣熊，蜜熊 |  A coati (also known as coatimundi) is a raccoon-like mammal found in Central and South America, known for its long snout and banded tail. | We observed a coati searching for insects under a log. 我们观察到一只南美浣熊在木头下寻找昆虫。 |  The coati's nimble fingers helped it catch a lizard.  南美浣熊灵活的爪子帮它抓住了一只蜥蜴。 | 近义词: coati-mondi, coatimundi | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun


词源: From Tupi *kuati, perhaps reduplicated from *kuá “belt, girdle” + *ti “nose, beak” + Tupi Mondi.
