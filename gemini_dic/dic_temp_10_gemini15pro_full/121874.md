misrouting:/ˌmɪsˈruːtɪŋ/
n.
错误路由，路径错误
the act of sending something to the wrong destination.
The misrouting of the package caused a two-day delay. 包裹的错误路由导致了两天的延误。
misdirection, rerouting incorrectly
correct routing
noun
