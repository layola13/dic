mindbendingly:/ˈmaɪndˌbɛndɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人费解地，极其复杂地 | Extremely complex and difficult to comprehend; causing mental strain or confusion. | The plot of the movie was mindbendingly intricate. 这部电影的情节错综复杂，令人费解。|  The puzzle was mindbendingly difficult.  这个谜题难度极高，令人绞尽脑汁。 | 近义词：bafflingly, bewilderingly, confusingly, perplexingly | 反义词：simply, easily, clearly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "mind-bending" + "-ly". "Mind-bending" is a compound adjective formed from "mind" (referring to mental processes) and "bending" (suggesting a warping or distortion of perception or thought).  "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs.
