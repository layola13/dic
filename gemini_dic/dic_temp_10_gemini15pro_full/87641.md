hex:/hɛks/| n. vt.|十六进制，施魔法|A representation of a number in base 16, using the digits 0-9 and the letters A-F.  To cast a spell on; to bewitch.|例句：The color code is represented in hex. 颜色代码以十六进制表示。|例句：She believed she had been hexed. 她相信她被施了魔法。|近义词：curse, jinx|反义词:bless|词性:名词, 动词

hex:/hɛks/|词源|From hexadecimal (adjective), shortening and alteration of  sexadecimal, from Latin sex (six) + decimal (ten). The use as a verb developed from the noun, likely in the context of computer programming, where manipulating hexadecimal values is common.|词性:名词, 动词
