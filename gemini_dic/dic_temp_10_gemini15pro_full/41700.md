corrivalry:/kəˈraɪvəlri/
n.
竞争；对抗；相互竞争
Competition between rivals.
例句：There's a fierce corrivalry between the two companies for market share.  两家公司之间为争夺市场份额展开了激烈的竞争。

例句：The corrivalry between the two teams has been going on for years.  这两个队之间的竞争已经持续多年了。

近义词：rivalry, competition, contention
反义词：cooperation, collaboration, partnership
词性: 名词
