manifoldly:/ˈmænɪˌfəʊldli/ | adv. | 多方面地，多种多样地 | In many ways; variously. | The city's problems are manifoldly connected.  这座城市的问题是多方面关联的。 | The benefits of exercise are manifoldly apparent. 运动的好处是显而易见的。 | 近义词: variously, diversely, multifariously | 反义词: simply, singly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *manyfoldly*, from Old English *manigfealdlīce* (“manifoldly”), equivalent to *manifold* + *-ly*.
