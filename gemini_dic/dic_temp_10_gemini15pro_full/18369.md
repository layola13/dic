belly-aching:/ˈbeliˌeɪkɪŋ/
adjective
抱怨的，牢骚满腹的
Complaining, especially about something unimportant.
Example: He's always belly-aching about the food.  他总是抱怨食物。
Example:  The constant belly-aching about the workload needs to stop.  关于工作量的持续抱怨需要停止。
Synonym: complaining, whining, griping, moaning
Antonym: content, satisfied
词性: 形容词


belly-ache:/ˈbeliˌeɪk/
noun
肚子疼；抱怨
A pain in the belly; a complaint.
Example: I have a belly-ache. 我肚子疼。
Example:  He had a belly-ache about the long hours. 他抱怨工作时间太长。
Synonym: stomachache, complaint, gripe
Antonym: comfort, well-being
词性: 名词

belly-ache:/ˈbeliˌeɪk/
verb
抱怨，发牢骚
To complain, especially about something unimportant.
Example:  Stop belly-aching and get on with it.  别抱怨了，继续干吧。
Example: They belly-ached about the price of the tickets. 他们抱怨门票价格太贵。
Synonym: complain, whine, gripe, moan
Antonym: praise, commend
词性: 动词

词源:  "belly-ache" comes from the literal meaning of a pain in the belly.  Over time, it evolved figuratively to mean complaining, as excessive complaining can be likened to a nagging pain.
