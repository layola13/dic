emery:/ˈeməri/ | n. |金刚砂，刚玉粉|A very hard black or grayish-black crystalline form of corundum, consisting mainly of aluminum oxide and used as an abrasive. | Emery boards are commonly used for filing nails.  金刚砂板通常用于修指甲。|Synonyms: abrasive, corundum, grit | Antonyms:  smoothness, lubricant | noun

emeries:/ˈeməriz/ | n. |金刚砂（复数）|The plural form of emery. | Different grades of emeries are used for various polishing purposes. 不同等级的金刚砂用于各种抛光目的。|Synonyms: abrasives, corundums, grits | Antonyms:  smoothnesses, lubricants | noun
