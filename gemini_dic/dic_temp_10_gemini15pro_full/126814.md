napalming: /ˈneɪpɑːmɪŋ/ | n. |凝胶汽油弹轰炸，烧夷弹攻击|The act of attacking something or someone with napalm.  It refers to both the act of dropping the bombs and the resulting destruction and suffering.|The village was devastated by napalming. 这个村庄被凝固汽油弹摧毁了。|Synonyms: incineration, immolation, cremation|Antonyms: extinguishing, dousing|noun
