blueberries:/ˈbluːˌberiz/ | n. | 蓝莓 | Small, round, sweet, dark blue berries that grow on bushes. |  I love eating blueberries with my breakfast cereal. 我喜欢早餐麦片里放蓝莓。

I picked a basket of fresh blueberries at the farm. 我在农场摘了一篮新鲜的蓝莓。 | 近义词：huckleberries, bilberries | 反义词: N/A |  noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From blue + berry.  The word "blue" refers to the color of the fruit, and "berry" denotes its small, round, fleshy nature.
