claimless:/ˈkleɪmləs/| adj.|无索赔的；无人认领的；无主的|Having no claim; not claimed.  Not making or likely to make a claim. |The abandoned suitcase was claimless.  那个被遗弃的手提箱无人认领。|Synonyms: unclaimed, abandoned, ownerless|Antonyms: claimed, owned, possessed|词性:形容词

claimless:/ˈkleɪmləs/| adj.|无要求的；无权利的|Not entitled to make a claim.  Not having any demands or expectations.|He adopted a claimless attitude, surprising everyone. 他采取了一种无欲无求的态度，令所有人惊讶。|Synonyms: unassuming, modest, unpretentious|Antonyms: demanding, entitled, assertive|词性:形容词
