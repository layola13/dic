conscribe:/kənˈskraɪb/| vt.|征召，强制入伍|To compel into military service; to draft.|例句：The government conscribed young men into the army. 政府征召年轻人入伍。

例句：During wartime, many citizens were conscribed. 战时，许多公民被征召入伍。|近义词：draft, enlist, recruit, levy|反义词:discharge, release|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "conscribere" (to write together, enroll, enlist), from "com-" (together) + "scribere" (to write).
