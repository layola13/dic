emanant:/ˈɛmənənt/| adj.|流出的，发出的，放射的|Flowing out from a source or origin; issuing or proceeding from a source.|A strange energy seemed emanant from the ancient artifact. 古老的文物似乎散发着一种奇怪的能量。|近义词：issuing, emerging, radiating, emanating|反义词：absorbed, incoming, receptive|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin emanant- (stem of emanans), present participle of emanare ‘flow out’, from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out’ + manare ‘to flow’.
