nondispersion:/ˌnɒndɪˈspɜːʃən/ | n. | 非弥散，不分散 | The lack of dispersion; a state where components of a wave or substance do not separate or spread out. | The nondispersion of the laser beam allowed for precise targeting. 激光束的非弥散性使其能够精确定位。 | 近义词: non-scattering, coherence | 反义词: dispersion, scattering | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + dispersion (from Latin dispersio, meaning "a scattering")
