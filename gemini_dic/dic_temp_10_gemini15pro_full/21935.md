bogey:/ˈboʊɡi/ (US), /ˈbəʊɡi/ (UK)| n.| 柏忌; 鬼怪; 妖怪; 鼻屎|A score of one stroke over par on a hole.|He shot a bogey on the 18th hole. 他在第18洞打出了柏忌。|Synonym: bugbear, goblin, phantom, specter|Antonym: par, birdie|noun

bogey:/ˈboʊɡi/ (US), /ˈbəʊɡi/ (UK)| vt.|  使...担忧; 困扰|To haunt or trouble someone.|The thought of failure bogeyed him. 失败的想法困扰着他。|Synonym: haunt, plague, worry, trouble|Antonym: comfort, soothe|verb
