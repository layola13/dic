echelon:/ˈeʃəlɒn/ (US) /ˈeʃəlɒn/ (UK)| n.|梯队，等级，阶层|A level or rank in an organization, a profession, or society.|The upper echelons of the company are very secretive. 公司的高层非常神秘。|Synonyms: level, rank, tier, grade|Antonyms: none|noun

echelon:/ˈeʃəlɒn/ (US) /ˈeʃəlɒn/ (UK)| n.|梯形编队|A formation of troops, ships, or aircraft in which each unit is positioned to the rear and to one side of the unit ahead.|The planes flew in echelon formation. 飞机以梯形编队飞行。|Synonyms: formation, arrangement, configuration|Antonyms: none|noun
