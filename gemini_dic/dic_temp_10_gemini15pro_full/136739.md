nonsensitized:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 未致敏的，未敏感化的 | Not having been made sensitive or allergic to a particular substance.  This means the immune system hasn't been primed to react to it. | The patient received a nonsensitized blood transfusion.  病人接受了未致敏的输血。

The nonsensitized group showed no reaction to the allergen. 未致敏组对过敏原没有反应。 | 近义词: nonallergic, unexposed, naive | 反义词: sensitized, allergic | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + sensitized (past participle of sensitize, from sensitive + -ize)
