moths:/mɔːθs/ /mɔːðz/
n.
飞蛾
Nocturnal flying insects related to butterflies, typically drab-colored and drawn to light.
Moths are attracted to light.  飞蛾会被光吸引。
The moths were fluttering around the porch light. 飞蛾在门廊的灯周围飞舞。
近义词:insect, butterfly
反义词:None
词性:Noun
