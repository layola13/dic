actionable:/ˈækʃənəbl/| adj.|可操作的，可执行的，可起诉的|Capable of being acted on; affording grounds for legal action.|The report provided actionable advice on how to improve the sales process. 这份报告提供了关于如何改进销售流程的可行性建议。

The company is facing actionable claims of unfair dismissal. 该公司正面临着不公平解雇的可起诉索赔。|近义词：practical, feasible, viable, enforceable|反义词：unworkable, impractical, infeasible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *accionable*, from Anglo-Norman *accionable*, from Old French *actionable*, from *action* + *-able*.  Ultimately from Latin *actiō* (“action”) + *-ābilis*.
