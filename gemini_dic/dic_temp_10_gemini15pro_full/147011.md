overlapping:/ˌoʊvərˈlæpɪŋ/ | adj. | 重叠的，交叠的 | extending over and partly covering something else | The shingles on the roof are overlapping. 屋顶上的瓦片是重叠的。 |  synonyms: overlying, coincident, overlapping | antonyms: separate, distinct, non-overlapping | adjective

overlapping:/ˌoʊvərˈlæpɪŋ/ | n. | 重叠部分，重叠 | the amount by which two things or activities overlap | There is considerable overlapping between the two departments. 这两个部门之间有相当多的重叠。 | synonyms: intersection, convergence, concurrence | antonyms: separation, divergence, gap | noun
