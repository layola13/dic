nor:/nɔːr/ /nɔːr/
conjunction
也不；和…都不
Connects two negative alternatives.  Used after neither.
Neither John nor Mary went to the party.  约翰和玛丽都没去参加聚会。
近义词: and not, or not
反义词: and, or
词性: 连词

词源:  From Middle English *nor, *noor, from Old English ne (“not”) + oþþe, or (“or”).  Cognate with Dutch noch (“nor, neither”), German noch (“still, yet, nor”), Icelandic né (“nor”), Swedish nor (“nor”).
