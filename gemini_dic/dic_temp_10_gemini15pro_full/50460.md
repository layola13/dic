例句：The museum curator condemned the depredationist who vandalized the priceless artifacts.  博物馆馆长谴责了破坏无价文物的掠夺者。|近义词：predator, looter, plunderer, raider, vandal, despoiler|反义词:protector, guardian, conservator|词性:noun
