hyperstimulation:/ˌhaɪpərˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃn/
|n.|过度刺激|Excessive stimulation; overstimulation.|The constant noise and activity led to hyperstimulation of the child's senses.  持续不断的噪音和活动导致孩子感官过度刺激。|
|
hyperstimulation:|Overstimulation can cause anxiety and irritability. 过度刺激会导致焦虑和烦躁。|
|
hyperstimulation:|近义词: overstimulation, overexcitement|反义词: hypostimulation, understimulation|noun
