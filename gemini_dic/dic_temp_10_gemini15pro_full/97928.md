innocuousness:/ɪˈnɒkjuːəsnəs/
n.
无害;无毒
the state or quality of being innocuous.
Her comments seemed perfectly innocuous. 她的评论似乎完全无害。
近义词：harmlessness, safety, inoffensiveness
反义词：harmfulness, dangerousness, noxiousness
