ocellate: /ˈɒsɪleɪt/ (US) /ˈɒsɪlət/ (UK)| adj.|有小眼的；有眼状斑点的|Having small eyes or eye-like spots.|The butterfly's wings were ocellate, with beautiful blue and yellow rings.|近义词: eyed, spotted, ringed|反义词: plain, unspotted|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *ocellatus*, past participle of *ocellare*, "to furnish with little eyes," from *ocellus*, diminutive of *oculus*, "eye."
