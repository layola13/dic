in-the-clear:/ɪn ðə klɪr/
adjective
安全的；公开的；无罪的；解除嫌疑的
Free from suspicion or accusation; no longer in danger or trouble; transmitting or receiving a message unencrypted.
The suspect was in the clear after the police found the real culprit. 嫌疑人在警方找到真凶后被解除了嫌疑。
The radio transmission was in the clear, easily intercepted by anyone listening. 无线电传输是明码的，任何监听的人都可以轻易拦截。
近义词:safe, secure, cleared, exonerated, unencrypted
反义词:suspect, guilty, incriminated, encrypted
词性:adjective
