hebridean:/hɪˈbrɪdiən/ /hɛˈbrɪdiən/
adjective|赫布里底群岛的；赫布里底群岛居民的|Relating to or characteristic of the Hebrides, a group of islands off the west coast of Scotland.|The hebridean landscape is rugged and beautiful. 赫布里底群岛的风景崎岖而美丽。|例句：The strong winds are a typical hebridean weather feature.  强风是赫布里底群岛的典型气候特征。|近义词：Scottish, Gaelic, insular|反义词：mainland|词性：形容词

hebridean:/hɪˈbrɪdiən/ /hɛˈbrɪdiən/
noun|赫布里底群岛的居民；赫布里底群岛的动物|A native or inhabitant of the Hebrides, or an animal breed originating there.|The hebridean sheep are known for their hardiness.  赫布里底群岛的绵羊以其耐寒性而闻名。|例句：As a hebridean, she was used to the harsh weather conditions.  作为赫布里底群岛的居民，她习惯了恶劣的天气条件。|近义词：islander, crofter|反义词：mainlander|词性：名词
