compensation:/ˌkɒmpənˈseɪʃn/ (US) /ˌkɑːmpənˈseɪʃn/| n.|补偿，赔偿；报酬|Something, typically money, awarded to someone as a recompense for loss, injury, or suffering.|例句：He received compensation for the injuries he suffered in the accident. 他因事故受伤获得了赔偿。|近义词：remuneration, payment, recompense, restitution|反义词：penalty, forfeiture|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French compensacion, or from late Latin compensatio(n-), from compensare ‘weigh together’, from com- (expressing intensive force) + pensare ‘weigh’.
