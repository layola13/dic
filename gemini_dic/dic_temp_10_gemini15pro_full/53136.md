diplomacy: /dɪˈploʊməsi/ (US) /dɪˈpləʊməsi/ (UK)| n. |外交，外交手段| The art and practice of conducting negotiations between nations. Skill in handling affairs without raising hostility. |例句：Diplomacy is often the best way to resolve international conflicts. 外交通常是解决国际冲突的最佳途径。

例句：She showed great diplomacy in handling the sensitive situation.  她在处理敏感局势方面展现了高超的外交手腕。

近义词：negotiation, statesmanship, tact, finesse|反义词：hostility, aggression, belligerence|词性: noun
