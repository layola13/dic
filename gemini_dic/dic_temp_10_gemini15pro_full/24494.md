bro:/broʊ/| n.|兄弟，哥们|A slang term of address for a male friend or brother.|Bro, can you pass me the remote? 哥们，你能把遥控器递给我吗？|近义词：dude, man, buddy, pal|反义词:enemy, foe|noun

bro:/broʊ/| int.|哎，哥们|Used as an interjection expressing surprise, excitement, or disappointment.|Bro! That was awesome! 哎！哥们！太棒了！|近义词：wow, dude|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|interjection
