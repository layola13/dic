leveraging: /ˈliːvərɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 利用，运用 | using something to maximum advantage. | The company is leveraging its resources to expand into new markets.  公司正在利用其资源拓展新市场。| synonyms: utilizing, capitalizing on, exploiting | antonyms: squandering, wasting | verb

leveraging: /ˈliːvərɪdʒɪŋ/ | n. | 利用，运用（杠杆作用） | The act of using something to maximum advantage. | The leveraging of his connections helped him get the job.  利用他的人脉关系帮助他得到了这份工作。| synonyms: utilization, capitalization, exploitation | antonyms: waste, neglect | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "leverage," which comes from the Old French "levier" (to raise), ultimately from the Latin "levare" (to lift).  The financial sense of using borrowed capital for investment emerged in the late 19th century.
