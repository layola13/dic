denizenize:/ˈdɛnɪzəˌnaɪz/
verb
使成为居民；使本土化；接纳；移植
To make someone a denizen; to grant citizenship to.  To adapt to a new place or environment, as if becoming a naturalized citizen of it.  To adopt (a foreign word, custom, etc.) into the language or culture of a country or group.
The immigrants were quickly denizenized after arriving in the new country.  这些移民在新国家很快就融入了当地生活。
The once foreign plant has become denizenized in the local ecosystem. 这种曾经外来的植物已经融入了当地的生态系统。
Synonyms: naturalize, acclimatize, assimilate, domesticate
Antonyms: alienate, estrange, exile
