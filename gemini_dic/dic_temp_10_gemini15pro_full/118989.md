methanols:/ˈmɛθənɒlz/ | n. | 甲醇（复数） | Methyl alcohol, also known as wood alcohol, is a toxic, flammable liquid.  It is the simplest alcohol, and is used as a solvent, fuel, and antifreeze.  The plural form is less common than the singular "methanol". | Methanol is used as a solvent in many industrial processes. 甲醇在许多工业过程中用作溶剂。|  Methanol is a key component in the production of biodiesel. 甲醇是生产生物柴油的关键成分。 |Synonyms: methyl alcohol, wood alcohol | Antonyms: N/A | noun
