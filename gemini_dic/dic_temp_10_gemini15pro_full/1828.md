ETYMOLOGY->
From French "acrobatique," from Greek "akrobatikos" (walking on tiptoe, acrobatic), from "akron" (tip, extremity) + "bainein" (to go, walk). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*gʷem-" (to go, come).
