midfield:/ˈmɪdˌfiːld/ | n. adj.| 中场 |The area of a playing field between the two opposing teams' defense and forward lines.  It can also refer to the players who occupy this area. |  The midfielder controlled the midfield with his excellent passing.  这名中场球员以他出色的传球控制了中场。 | synonyms: center field, middle ground | antonyms:  forward line, back line | noun
midfield:/ˈmɪdˌfiːld/ | adj.| 中场的 |Relating to or located in the midfield. |  He made a midfield tackle to stop the attack. 他在中场铲球阻止了进攻。 | synonyms: central | antonyms: forward, back | adjective
