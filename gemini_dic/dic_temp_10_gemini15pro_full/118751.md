metalinguist:/ˌmetəˈlɪŋɡwɪst/ | n. |元语言学家；语言金属学家 |A metalinguist is a person who studies metalanguage, which is language or symbols used to describe or analyze another language.  |例句：The metalinguist explained the complex grammatical rules of the ancient language.  这位元语言学家解释了这门古代语言复杂的语法规则。

例句：Her research as a metalinguist focused on the evolution of semantic structures. 她作为元语言学家的研究重点是语义结构的演变。|近义词：linguist, language scholar|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
