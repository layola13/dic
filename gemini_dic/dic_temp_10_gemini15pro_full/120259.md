mini-condos:/ˈmɪni ˈkɑːndoʊz/ (US) /ˈmɪni ˈkɒndoʊz/ (UK)| n. |小型公寓|Small condominium units, typically less spacious than standard condos.  They often appeal to single people, young couples, or those looking for a more affordable or lower-maintenance housing option.|例句：Mini-condos are becoming increasingly popular in urban areas. 小型公寓在城市地区越来越受欢迎。

例句：She bought a mini-condo as her first investment property. 她买了一套小型公寓作为她的第一套投资房产。|近义词：studio apartments, small apartments, micro-apartments|反义词:large apartments, mansions, houses|词性:noun
