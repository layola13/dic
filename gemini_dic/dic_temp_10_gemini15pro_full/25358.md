built-up:/ˌbɪlt ˈʌp/| adj.|建成的；发达的；密集的|Covered with buildings; densely populated.|例句：The city center is a built-up area with tall buildings. 市中心是高楼林立的建成区。|例句：They live in a built-up suburb. 他们住在一个人口稠密的郊区。|近义词：developed, urbanized, densely populated|反义词：rural, undeveloped, sparsely populated|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb 'build' + 'up' (adverb indicating completion or intensification).  'Build' comes from Old English 'byldan'.
