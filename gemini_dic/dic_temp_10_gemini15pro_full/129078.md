nitromagnesite:/ˌnaɪtrəʊˈmæɡnɪsaɪt/ | n. | 硝酸镁石 | A mineral consisting of hydrous magnesium nitrate, typically found as white efflorescent crystals.  It's a naturally occurring form of magnesium nitrate. |  Nitromagnesite is often found in caves. 硝酸镁石经常在洞穴中被发现。 |  Nitromagnesite deposits were discovered in the cave. 在洞穴里发现了硝酸镁石矿床。 |Synonyms: magnesium nitrate hydrate, nitro-chalk | Antonyms: None (specific mineral, no direct opposite) | noun
