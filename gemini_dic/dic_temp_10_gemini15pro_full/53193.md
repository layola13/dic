diptera:/ˈdɪptərə/
n.
双翅目昆虫
An order of insects having a single pair of wings and a pair of halteres, club-shaped balancing organs.
Diptera are a large order of insects that includes flies, mosquitoes, gnats, and midges. 双翅目昆虫是一个很大的昆虫目，包括苍蝇、蚊子、蚊蚋和蠓。
Diptera such as mosquitoes and tsetse flies can transmit diseases. 像蚊子和采采蝇这样的双翅目昆虫可以传播疾病。
近义词:flies (泛指苍蝇，但双翅目包含更多种类)
反义词:无
词性:名词
