interlapse:/ˌɪntərˈlæps/| n.|间隔，间歇|A period of time that occurs between two events or points in time.|There was a long interlapse between the two world wars. 两次世界大战之间有一段很长的间隔。|近义词：interval, gap, pause|反义词:continuation, succession|名词
