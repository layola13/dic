inferno:/ɪnˈfɜːrnoʊ/ (US), /ɪnˈfɜːnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |地狱；烈火；大火灾|A large fire that is dangerously out of control.|The whole city was an inferno last night after the earthquake.|近义词: blaze, conflagration, firestorm|反义词: paradise, heaven|词性:名词
Etymology: From Italian *inferno* "hell," from Latin *infernus* "below, underground, of the lower world," from *inferus* "low," literally "beneath."
