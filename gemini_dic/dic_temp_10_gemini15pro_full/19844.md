bind-up:/ˈbaɪnd ʌp/| n.| 困境；僵局；难题|A complicated or difficult situation.|The project has become a real bind-up and we're not sure how to proceed.|近义词：predicament, dilemma, impasse|反义词:solution, resolution|名词

bind-up:/ˈbaɪnd ʌp/| v.| 包扎；装订；合订|To bandage or tie up (a wound or injury). To fasten sheets of paper together to form a book or magazine.|The doctor will bind up your injured ankle.|近义词：bandage, dress, wrap, tie, fasten|反义词:unbind, untie, loosen|动词
