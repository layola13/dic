haematolin:/ˌhiːməˈtəʊlɪn/| n. |苏木精|A dark red crystalline compound used as a biological stain. It is obtained from the heartwood of the logwood tree and stains cell nuclei blue-violet.|The pathologist used haematoxylin and eosin to stain the tissue sample.|Synonyms: hematoxylin|Antonyms: N/A|noun
