chippings:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋz/
n.
碎片，碎屑
Small pieces of something that have been broken off a larger object.
The workmen left a pile of chippings on the pavement. 工人们在人行道上留下了一堆碎屑。

近义词:fragments, pieces, shards
反义词:whole

词性:名词
