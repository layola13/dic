guards:/ɡɑːrdz/ | n. | 警卫，卫兵，守卫，防护装置 |  A person or group of people whose job is to protect a person, place, or thing. | The guards patrolled the perimeter of the building. 警卫在建筑物周围巡逻。| synonyms: protectors, sentinels, watchmen | antonyms: attackers, assailants | noun

guards:/ɡɑːrdz/ | v. | 保卫，守护，防止，提防 | To protect someone or something from attack or danger. |  She guarded her secrets carefully. 她小心翼翼地守护着自己的秘密。| synonyms: protect, defend, shield | antonyms: endanger, expose | verb
