belligerently:/bəˈlɪdʒərəntli/
adverb
好战地；挑衅地；充满敌意地
in a hostile and aggressive way

He stared belligerently at the other driver.
他充满敌意地盯着另一位司机。

She responded belligerently to the accusations.
她对这些指控做出了好战的回应。

Synonyms: aggressively, hostilely, pugnaciously, antagonistically, combatively
Antonyms: peacefully, amicably, cooperatively, passively

ETYMOLOGY->
From belligerent + -ly.  Belligerent comes from the Latin bellum "war" + gerere "to wage".
