cutesyness:/ˈkjutsiːnəs/| n.|过分的可爱;矫揉造作的可爱|An exaggerated or affected quality of being cute, often considered excessive or insincere.  It implies a deliberate attempt to appear charming, innocent, or childlike, which can sometimes be perceived as artificial or manipulative.|Her cutesyness was starting to grate on my nerves. 她过分的可爱开始让我心烦。|近义词：preciousness, affectation, artificiality|反义词：genuineness, sincerity|词性:noun
