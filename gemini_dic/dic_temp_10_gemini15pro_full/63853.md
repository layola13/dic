epigrammatist:/ˌɛpɪˈɡræmətɪst/
n.
警句家；善于写警句的人
A person who writes or is known for writing epigrams.
Oscar Wilde was a renowned epigrammatist, his witty and concise sayings are still quoted today. 奥斯卡·王尔德是一位著名的警句家，他机智而简洁的语录至今仍被引用。
He gained a reputation as a sharp epigrammatist, his words cutting through pretense and hypocrisy. 他以犀利的警句家而闻名，他的话语直指虚伪和矫饰。
Synonym:  wit, quipster, pundit
Antonym:  prolix writer, rambling speaker
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *épigrammatiste*, from Latin *epigramma* (epigram), from Greek *epigramma*  (inscription), from *epi-* (upon) + *graphein* (to write).
