词源:
"Fleek" emerged in African American Vernacular English (AAVE) around 2014, popularized by a Vine video by Kayla Newman (@Peaches Monroee) about her eyebrows. "Fleaking" is the adjectival form of "fleek," meaning "on fleek."  The exact etymology is uncertain, but it's speculated to be related to "slick" or "sleek."
