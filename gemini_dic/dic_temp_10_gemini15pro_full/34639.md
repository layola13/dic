clemently: /ˈklɛməntli/ | adv. | 仁慈地，温和地 | In a merciful or lenient manner; mildly. | The judge sentenced the young offender clemently, giving him probation instead of jail time. 法官仁慈地判处了这名年轻罪犯缓刑，而不是监禁。|  The king clemently pardoned the rebel. 国王仁慈地赦免了叛乱者。 | 近义词: mercifully, leniently, compassionately | 反义词: harshly, severely, cruelly | 词性: 副词

clement: /ˈklɛmənt/ | adj. | 仁慈的，温和的 | Showing mercy or leniency. |  It was a clement day in winter. 这是冬天里温和的一天。 |  The judge was known for his clement disposition.  这位法官以其仁慈的性格而闻名。 | 近义词: merciful, lenient, compassionate | 反义词: harsh, severe, cruel | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *clement* (Modern French clément), from Latin *clēmens*, present active participle of *clēre* "to lean, incline, be propitious."  The original sense was "inclined to be mild or merciful."
