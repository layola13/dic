palatopterygoid: /ˌpælətəʊtɛrɪˈɡɔɪd/ /ˌpælətəʊtɛrɪˈɡɔɪd/ | n. adj.|腭翼骨|A bone or cartilage forming part of the palate and extending to the pterygoid bone.|The palatopterygoid bone is crucial for jaw movement in many fish species. 腭翼骨对许多鱼类的下颌运动至关重要。|Synonym:  palatoquadrate (in some contexts)|Antonym: None|Noun, Adjective

Etymology:
palato- (from Latin *palātum, meaning "palate") + pterygoid (from Greek pteron, meaning "wing," and eidos, meaning "form")
