behowl: /biˈhaʊl/
verb
大声哀号，痛哭
to howl loudly and continuously, especially in grief or anguish
例句：The bereaved mother behowled her grief.  痛失爱子的母亲大声哀号。

例句：The wind behowled around the lonely cabin. 风绕着孤零零的小屋呼啸。
近义词：wail, lament, howl, cry
反义词：rejoice, celebrate
词性：v.
