monophysitical:/ˌmɒnəʊˈfɪzɪtɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmɑːnəˈfɪzɪkəl/| adj.|一性论的，单性论的|Of or relating to Monophysitism, a Christological doctrine asserting that Jesus Christ had only one nature, divine, as opposed to the orthodox view of two natures, divine and human.|The Coptic Church was historically considered monophysitical. 科普特教会在历史上被认为是一性论的。|The council condemned the monophysitical teachings.  会议谴责了一性论的教义。|近义词:miaphysite|反义词:dyophysite|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *monophysitus*, from Greek *μονοφυής* (monophysēs, “of one nature, single-natured”) + *-ίτης* (-itēs, adjectival suffix).  *μόνος* (monos, “single, alone”) + *φύσις* (physis, “nature”).
