foresightedly:/fɔːrˈsaɪtɪdli/
adverb
有远见地；深谋远虑地
In a manner that shows foresight; prudently with regard to the future.
She foresightedly invested her money in real estate. 她有远见地将钱投资于房地产。
He foresightedly planned for his retirement. 他有远见地规划了他的退休生活。
近义词: prudently, wisely, judiciously, sagaciously
反义词: shortsightedly, imprudently, unwisely
词性: adverb
词源: From foresighted + -ly. Foresighted from fore- + sighted. Fore- from Old English *fore-, from Proto-Germanic *furai, from Proto-Indo-European *per- (1). Sighted from sight + -ed. Sight from Middle English sight, from Old English siht, gesihþ, from Proto-Germanic *sihtīz, from Proto-Indo-European *sekʷ- (“to see”).
