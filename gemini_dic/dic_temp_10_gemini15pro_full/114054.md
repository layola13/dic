magnetise:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/| v.| 磁化，使磁化 |To make something magnetic.| He magnetised the needle with a magnet. 他用磁铁磁化了针。 | synonyms:  charge, polarize | antonyms: demagnetise, depolarize | verb
