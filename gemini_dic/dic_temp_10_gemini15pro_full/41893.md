cosignatory:/ˌkoʊˈsɪɡnəˌtɔːri/ /ˌkəʊˈsɪɡnətəri/
|n.| 共同签署人；连署人 |A person who signs a document along with another person or other people.| The president and the prime minister were cosignatories on the treaty. 总统和总理是该条约的共同签署人。|
|近义词：joint signatory, cosigner|反义词:|名词
