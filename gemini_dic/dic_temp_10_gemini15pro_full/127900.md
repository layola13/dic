neoliberalism:/ˌniːoʊˈlɪbərəlɪzəm/ /ˌnɪːəʊˈlɪbərəlɪzəm/| n. |新自由主义|A modified form of liberalism tending to favor free-market capitalism.|The rise of neoliberalism in the 1980s led to deregulation and privatization. 20世纪80年代新自由主义的兴起导致了放松管制和私有化。

Neoliberalism promotes free trade and minimal government intervention.  新自由主义提倡自由贸易和最小限度的政府干预。|近义词：laissez-faire capitalism, free-market economics|反义词：socialism, interventionism|词性: noun
