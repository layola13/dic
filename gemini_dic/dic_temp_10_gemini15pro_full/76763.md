gameness:/ˈɡeɪmnɪs/
noun
勇敢; 坚韧;  不屈不挠
The quality of being spirited, plucky, and determined.
He showed great gameness in the face of adversity.  他在逆境中表现出极大的坚韧。
近义词: pluck, spirit, grit, tenacity, fortitude, resilience, determination
反义词: cowardice, timidity, fearfulness
词性: 名词
