paracord:/ˈpærəkɔːrd/| n.|伞绳，降落伞绳|A lightweight nylon rope originally used in parachutes, now used for various purposes due to its strength and versatility.|例句：I used paracord to secure the tent. 我用伞绳固定帐篷。

例句：He always carries a bracelet made of paracord in case of emergencies. 他总是戴着一个由伞绳制成的腕带，以备不时之需。|近义词：cord, rope, twine|反义词: None|词性:noun
