outbrazens:/ˌaʊtˈbreɪzənz/| v. |厚颜无耻地胜过；在厚颜无耻方面超过|To surpass in boldness or impudence.  He outbrazened his rivals by openly flaunting his ill-gotten gains.|近义词：outfaces, outdares, surpasses, eclipses|反义词：cowers, shrinks, submits|词性：动词

Etymology:
From out- + brazen.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding, while "brazen" means bold and without shame.
