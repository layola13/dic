australopith:/ˌɔːstreɪloʊˈpɪθ/ | n. |南方古猿|An extinct hominid of the genus *Australopithecus*, closely related to and thought to be an ancestor of modern human beings. |例句：The australopith skull provided valuable clues about human evolution. 南方古猿的头骨提供了关于人类进化的宝贵线索。

例句：Scientists debate the exact position of australopiths in the human family tree. 科学家们争论南方古猿在人类族谱中的确切位置。|近义词：hominid, hominin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern human) |词性:noun
