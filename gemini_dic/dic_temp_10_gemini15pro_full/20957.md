bleak:/blik/
adjective
荒凉的，凄凉的；阴冷的；无望的；暗淡的
lacking vegetation and exposed to the elements; depressing; cold and miserable; offering little or no hope


Bleak landscapes often feature in dystopian novels.
荒凉的景色经常出现在反乌托邦小说中。

The economic outlook is bleak.
经济前景暗淡。


近义词:desolate, barren, grim, dreary, depressing
反义词:cheerful, bright, promising, hopeful, optimistic
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bleik, from Old Norse *bleikr (“pale, wan, whitish”).  Cognate with Icelandic bleikur (“pale”), Swedish blek (“pale”).  The semantic development from "pale" to "cold and cheerless" is similar to that of the word "chill."
