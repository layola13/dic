balky: /ˈbɔːlki/ (US) /ˈbɔːlki/ (UK)| adj. | 倔强的；不愿合作的；不肯动的 | Refusing to move or act as desired; uncooperative. | The old mule was balky and refused to cross the stream. 那头老骡子很倔，不肯过河。| The engine was balky this morning and took a few tries to start. 今早发动机启动不灵，试了几次才打着。|近义词: stubborn, obstinate, uncooperative, recalcitrant, refractory |反义词: compliant, cooperative, docile, obedient |词性: adjective
Etymology:  From the verb "balk," from Middle English balken "to hesitate, shy away from," probably of Scandinavian origin.
