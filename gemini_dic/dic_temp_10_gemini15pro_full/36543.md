collaged:/kəˈlɑːʒd/
adjective
拼贴的
Made by sticking various different materials such as photographs and pieces of paper or fabric onto a backing.
The artwork was collaged with newspaper clippings and old photographs. 这幅艺术品是用剪报和旧照片拼贴而成的。
Synonyms: assembled, combined, composite
Antonyms: separated, individual, single
词性: adjective


collaged:/kəˈlɑːʒd/
verb (past participle)
拼贴
To make a collage.
The artist collaged various materials onto the canvas.  艺术家将各种材料拼贴到画布上。
Synonyms: assembled, combined, created
Antonyms: disassembled, separated, deconstructed
词性: verb (past participle)
