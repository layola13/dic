crochet:/kroʊˈʃeɪ/ (US) /ˈkrəʊʃeɪ/ (UK)
n.
钩针编织；钩针织物
a method of creating fabric from yarn or thread using a crochet hook
例句：She made a beautiful crochet blanket for her granddaughter. 她为孙女钩了一条漂亮的毯子。

例句：The crochet stitches were tight and even. 钩针针脚紧密均匀。

v.
用钩针编织
to make (a piece of needlework) by looping thread with a hooked needle
例句：She crocheted a scarf for the winter. 她钩了一条围巾过冬。

例句：He learned to crochet from his grandmother. 他跟祖母学会了钩针编织。


近义词：knit, tat
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unravelling/拆开)
词性：名词, 动词
