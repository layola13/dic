besmoke:/bɪˈsmoʊk/
verb
熏制，用烟熏，弥漫着烟
to fill or cover with smoke
The chimney besmoked the entire house. 烟囱的烟熏满了整栋房子。

The campfire besmoked our clothes. 篝火的烟熏了我们的衣服。

近义词: smoke, fumigate
反义词: clear, ventilate
词性: verb
