biodegraded:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdɪd/| adj.|生物降解的|Broken down by microorganisms such as bacteria or fungi.|The packaging is completely biodegradable.  这种包装是完全可生物降解的。|Example:  Many plastics are not easily biodegradable and contribute to pollution. 许多塑料不容易生物降解，造成污染。|Synonyms: decomposable, compostable|Antonyms: non-biodegradable, persistent|adjective
