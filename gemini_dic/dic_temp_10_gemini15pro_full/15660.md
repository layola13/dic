bakeries:/ˈbeɪkəriz/| n.|面包店（复数）|A place where bread and other baked goods are made and sold.|There are many bakeries in this town.  这个镇上有很多面包店。|Synonyms: bakehouses, bread shops|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bakerie, from bakier (baker) + -ie (suffix denoting place or establishment). Ultimately from Latin *beccarius (baker).
