nonmarketables:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪtəb(ə)lz/ | n. | 非市场商品；滞销商品；非上市证券 |  Goods or assets that are difficult or impossible to sell or trade in a regular market.  They may lack demand, have legal restrictions, or be unique and illiquid. | The company's nonmarketables included specialized equipment and a collection of antique cars.  该公司的不良资产包括专用设备和一批老爷车。 |  The museum possesses several nonmarketable artifacts due to their historical significance.  由于其历史意义，博物馆拥有几件不可出售的文物。 |Synonyms: illiquid assets, unsalable goods | Antonyms: marketable assets, liquid assets | noun

nonmarketable:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪtəb(ə)l/ | adj. |  非市场的; 滞销的; 非上市的 | Not suitable for sale or trade in a regular market. | The handmade jewelry was beautiful but ultimately nonmarketable due to its high price point. 这款手工珠宝很漂亮，但最终因为价格太高而无法销售。 |  His unique business idea proved nonmarketable in the current economic climate.  他的独特商业理念在当前的经济环境下被证明是不可行的。 | Synonyms: unsalable, unsellable, untradeable | Antonyms: marketable, salable, tradeable | adjective
