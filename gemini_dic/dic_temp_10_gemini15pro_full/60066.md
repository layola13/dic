edwardian:/ɛdˈwɔːdiən/
adjective|爱德华时代的；爱德华七世时代的|Relating to the reign of King Edward VII of England (1901-1910), typically characterized by elegance and extravagance.|The house retained many of its original Edwardian features. 这栋房子保留了许多原有的爱德华时代特色。|近义词:Victorian, turn-of-the-century|反义词:modern, contemporary|词性:adjective

edwardian:/ɛdˈwɔːdiən/
noun|爱德华时代的人|A person who lived during the Edwardian era.|Many Edwardians were known for their lavish lifestyles. 许多爱德华时代的人以其奢华的生活方式而闻名。|近义词:Victorian|反义词:modern person, contemporary|词性:noun
