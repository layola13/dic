entice:/ɪnˈtaɪs/ /ɛnˈtaɪs/| vt.|诱惑，吸引|To attract someone to do something by offering them something pleasant or advantageous; tempt.|The smell of freshly baked bread enticed me into the bakery.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味把我吸引进了面包店。|Synonyms: tempt, lure, allure, attract, seduce|Antonyms: repel, deter, discourage, dissuade|Verb
