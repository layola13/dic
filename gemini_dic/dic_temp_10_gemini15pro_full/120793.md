misarticulates:/ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪts/
verb
发音不清晰，口齿不清
To pronounce incorrectly; to articulate badly.
He misarticulates several sounds, making him difficult to understand. 他几个音发不清楚，让人很难理解。
slurs, mumbles, garbles
enunciates, articulates
词性: 动词
