nightfall:/ˈnaɪtfɔːl/| n.|夜幕降临，黄昏|The animals emerged from their burrows at nightfall. 动物们在夜幕降临的时候从洞穴里出来。|Nightfall brings an end to the day's activities.  夜幕降临标志着一天活动的结束。|近义词：dusk, twilight, sunset|反义词：dawn, daybreak, sunrise|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *nīhtfeall, from nīht (night) + feall (fall).  来自古英语 *nīhtfeall，由 nīht（夜晚）+ feall（落下）组成.
