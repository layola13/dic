copiously:/ˈkəʊpiəsli/ (UK) /ˈkoʊpiəsli/ (US)| adv.|丰富地，大量地，充裕地|in large quantities; abundantly|The wound was bleeding copiously. 伤口大量出血。|abundantly, profusely, plentifully|sparsely, meagerly|adverb
Etymology:  From Late Latin *cōpiōsus, from Latin cōpia (“abundance”) + -ōsus.
