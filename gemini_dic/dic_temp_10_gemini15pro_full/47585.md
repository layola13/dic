debases:/dɪˈbeɪsɪz/
verb
降低…的价值；贬低；败坏
To reduce the quality or value of something; to lower in rank, dignity, or significance.
The politician debased himself by accepting bribes.  这个政客接受贿赂，自贬身份。
synonyms:degrade, cheapen, corrupt, demean, depreciate
antonyms:enhance, elevate, improve, upgrade, ennoble
词性:动词

debases:/dɪˈbeɪsɪz/
verb
降低…的品质；掺杂
To adulterate or make impure by adding inferior materials.
They debased the currency by adding lead to the gold coins.  他们在金币里掺了铅，降低了货币的价值。
synonyms:adulterate, contaminate, pollute
antonyms:purify, refine
词性:动词
