lithotint: /ˈlɪθəʊtɪnt/
noun
石版画着色剂, 彩色石版画
A colored lithographic print, or a coloring agent used in lithography.
The artist used a variety of lithotints to create a vibrant and detailed image. 艺术家使用各种石版画着色剂来创作一幅充满活力和细节的图像。
Lithotint allowed for subtle gradations of color in the print. 石版画着色剂使印刷品中的颜色能够微妙地渐变。
synonyms: chromolithograph, color lithograph
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "monochrome lithograph" for a print lacking color)
词性: noun
