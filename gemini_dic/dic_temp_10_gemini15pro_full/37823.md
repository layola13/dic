compensatory:/kəmˈpɛnsətɔːri/ (US) /kəmˈpɛnsətri/ (UK)
adjective
补偿的，赔偿的
Reducing or offsetting the unpleasant or unwelcome effects of something.
例句：
He received a compensatory payment for the accident.  他因事故得到了一笔赔偿金。

She was awarded compensatory damages for the emotional distress she suffered. 她因精神上的痛苦获得了赔偿金。

近义词：remedial, corrective, reparative, restorative
反义词：punitive, exacerbating, aggravating
词性: adjective
