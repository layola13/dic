congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (British) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (American)| v. vi. |聚集，集合|To come together in a group or crowd.|例句：People congregated in the town square. 人们聚集在城镇广场上。|例句：Birds congregated on the telephone wires. 鸟儿聚集在电话线上。|近义词：assemble, gather, cluster|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词

congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (British) /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ (American)| adj. |聚集的，集合的|(Rarely used) gathered together into a crowd or mass. |例句：The congregate audience awaited the start of the concert.  聚集在一起的观众等待着音乐会的开始。|例句：The congregate body of protesters marched towards the capital.  聚集在一起的抗议者队伍向首都进发。|近义词：assembled, gathered|反义词:dispersed, scattered|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin congregatus, past participle of congregare "to flock together," from com- "together" + gregare "to herd, flock," from grex "flock, herd."
