freshness:/ˈfrɛʃnɪs/| n.|新鲜，清新|The freshness of the morning air was invigorating.  早晨空气的清新令人振奋。|近义词：novelty, newness, bloom|反义词：staleness, mustiness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *fersknesse, equivalent to fresh +‎ -ness. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *friskaz.
