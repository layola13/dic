counter-disengage:/ˌkaʊntər-dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ | v. | 反脱离，对抗性脱离接触 | To disengage in response to an opponent's disengagement, often as a tactical maneuver. |  Example: The army counter-disengaged from the enemy, luring them into a trap.  军队反脱离敌人，引诱他们进入陷阱。 | Example:  After the initial skirmish, both sides counter-disengaged, reassessing their strategies.  在最初的小冲突之后，双方都进行了反脱离接触，重新评估他们的战略。 | Synonym: withdraw reciprocally, strategically disengage | Antonym: engage, pursue, press | verb
