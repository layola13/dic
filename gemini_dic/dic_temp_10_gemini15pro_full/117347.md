mediaevalism:/ˌmiːdiˈiːvəlɪzəm/
n.
中世纪主义，中古主义；中世纪的风格（或特征、习俗）
A characteristic of the Middle Ages; adherence to medieval customs or ways of thinking.
His house is full of mediaevalism, from the suits of armor to the huge fireplaces. 他的房子充满了中世纪的风格，从盔甲套装到巨大的壁炉。
The author's use of archaic language added a touch of mediaevalism to the novel. 作者使用古体语言为小说增添了一丝中世纪的色彩。

近义词：medievalism, archaism
反义词：modernism
词性：noun
