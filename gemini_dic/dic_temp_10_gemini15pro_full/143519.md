outgreened:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnd/
verb
胜过（在环保方面）; 比...更环保
surpass in environmental friendliness.
Tesla outgreened all other car companies with its solar roof tiles. 特斯拉凭借其太阳能屋顶瓦片在环保方面胜过了所有其他汽车公司。
outperform, eclipse, surpass
lag behind, fall behind
v.
