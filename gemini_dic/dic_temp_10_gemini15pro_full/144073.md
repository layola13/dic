outrowing:/ˌaʊtˈroʊɪŋ/
verb
胜过划船;在划船比赛中胜过
To row faster or better than.
例句：He outrowed his competitors in the final race. 他在决赛中划船胜过他的对手。

例句：She trained hard to outrow the defending champion. 她刻苦训练以在划船比赛中胜过卫冕冠军。

近义词：outrace, surpass, beat, defeat
反义词：lose to

词性：v.
