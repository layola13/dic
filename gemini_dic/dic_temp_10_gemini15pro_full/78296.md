germinative:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪnətɪv/| adj.|萌发的，发芽的|Relating to or capable of germination; tending to produce or develop.|例句：The seed contains germinative properties. 这颗种子具有发芽的特性。|例句：The warm spring rains provided the perfect germinative conditions for the wildflowers. 温暖的春雨为野花的萌发提供了完美的条件。|近义词：sprouting, budding, nascent, developing|反义词：dormant, inert, sterile|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *germinativus, from Latin germinare (“to sprout, bud”), from germen (“bud, sprout”).
