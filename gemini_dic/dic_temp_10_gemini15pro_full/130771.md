nonanalytical:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈlɪtɪkəl/
adjective
非分析的; 非解析的
Not analytical; not characterized by or given to analysis.
The artist took a nonanalytical approach to her work, relying on intuition rather than careful planning.  这位艺术家对她的作品采取了一种非分析性的方法，依靠直觉而不是仔细的计划。
His response to the problem was surprisingly nonanalytical, focusing on emotional reactions rather than logical solutions. 他对这个问题的反应出人意料地是非分析性的，关注的是情绪反应而不是逻辑解决方案。
synonyms: intuitive, instinctive, uncritical, subjective, impressionistic
antonyms: analytical, critical, logical, objective, rational
词性: 形容词
