meditation:/ˌmedɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 冥想，沉思，默想 | The act of focusing one's mind for a period of time, in silence or with the aid of chanting, for religious or spiritual purposes or as a method of relaxation. |  She practices daily meditation to find inner peace. 她每天练习冥想以获得内心的平静。| contemplation, reflection, pondering | distraction, agitation | noun
