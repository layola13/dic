homunculus:/hɒˈmʌŋkjʊləs/ /həˈmʌŋkjʊləs/ | n. |微型人，侏儒，小人，（炼金术）人造人|A very small human or humanoid creature. A representation of a small human being. In alchemy, a miniature fully formed human believed to be produced artificially.|Scientists sometimes use the term "homunculus" to describe the distorted representation of the human body in the brain, where areas with more sensory input are disproportionately larger.| 近义词:dwarf, midget, pygmy, manikin | 反义词:giant | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *homunculus*, diminutive of *homo* ("man, human being").
