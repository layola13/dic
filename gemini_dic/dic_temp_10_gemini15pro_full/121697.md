mispractised: /ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/
verb
处理不当；玩忽职守；滥用；错误地使用
to carry out (a profession, especially medicine or law) improperly or unethically or to use (something) incorrectly
The doctor was accused of having mispractised medicine.  这位医生被指控滥用医疗手段。
He mispractised his religion for personal gain. 他为了个人利益而滥用宗教。

近义词: abuse, misuse, mishandle
反义词: use correctly, use properly
词性: verb (past tense)
