archaean:/ɑːrˈkiːən/ | adj. n. |太古代的；太古代 |Relating to or denoting the earliest eon in the history of Earth, following the Hadean eon and preceding the Proterozoic eon, between about 4 and 2.5 billion years ago. Also a noun referring to this period or a rock formed during it.| The archaean rocks of Greenland are some of the oldest on Earth. 格陵兰岛的太古代岩石是地球上最古老的岩石之一。|  The Archaean eon saw the first emergence of life. 太古代时期出现了最早的生命。| synonyms: primordial, early, ancient | antonyms: modern, recent | adjective, noun
