friezeless:/ˈfriːzlɪs/
adjective
无霜的，不受霜冻的
Free from frost; not subject to frost.
The friezeless climate of Florida allows citrus fruits to grow year-round. 佛罗里达无霜的气候允许柑橘类水果全年生长。
Citrus trees thrive in the friezeless environment of Southern California. 柑橘树在南加州无霜的环境中茁壮成长。
