deferred:/dɪˈfɜːrd/ /dəˈfɜːrd/| adj.|延期的，推迟的|Postponed or delayed to a later time.|例句：The payment has been deferred until next month. 付款已延期至下个月。|近义词：postponed, delayed, adjourned|反义词：immediate, prompt|词性:形容词

deferred:/dɪˈfɜːrd/ /dəˈfɜːrd/| n.|延期付款，延期债务|Something, typically a payment, that is postponed or delayed.|例句：The company has a large number of deferreds on its balance sheet. 该公司的资产负债表上有大量的延期债务。|近义词：postponement, delay|反义词：advance payment|词性:名词
