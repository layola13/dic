blout: /blaʊt/
n.|喧闹；吵闹；争吵|A noisy disturbance or quarrel.|The blout in the marketplace made it difficult to hear. 市场上的喧闹声让人很难听到说话。|近义词：clamor, din, uproar, racket|反义词：quiet, silence|词性:名词

blout: /blaʊt/
v.|大声叫嚷；喧闹；争吵|To make a loud, confused noise; to quarrel noisily.|The crowd blouted their disapproval.  人群大声喧嚷表示反对。|近义词：clamor, yell, shout|反义词：whisper, murmur|词性:动词
