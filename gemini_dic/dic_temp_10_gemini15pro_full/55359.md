distances:/ˈdɪstənsɪz/ /ˈdɪstənsəz/| n.|距离；远方；疏远|The distance between two cities. 两座城市之间的距离。|例句：The distances between the stars are vast. 星星之间的距离很广阔。|近义词：remoteness, gap, interval|反义词:nearness, proximity|noun

distances:/ˈdɪstənsɪz/ /ˈdɪstənsəz/| v.|拉开距离；使疏远|He distanced himself from the scandal. 他 distancing himself from the scandal. 他使自己远离丑闻。|例句：She distanced herself from her family after the argument. 争吵后，她与家人疏远了。|近义词：detach, separate, alienate|反义词:approach, associate|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French distance (Modern French distance)  and directly from Latin distantia "a standing apart," from distans, present participle of distare "stand apart," from dis- "apart" + stare "to stand."
