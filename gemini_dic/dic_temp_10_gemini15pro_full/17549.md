beatifying:/biˈætɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
使人快乐的，祝福的
making someone very happy; conferring a blessing on someone.
Her smile was beatifying. 她的笑容令人快乐。
synonyms: blissful, exalting, joyous, rapturous
antonyms: saddening, depressing, distressing
词性:adjective

beatify:/biˈætɪfaɪ/
verb
（尤指天主教）为…行宣福礼
(especially in the Roman Catholic Church) declare (a dead person) to be blessed.
Mother Teresa was beatified in 2003. 特蕾莎修女于2003年被宣福。
synonyms: bless, exalt, sanctify
antonyms: condemn, curse
词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin beatificare, from Latin beatus (“blessed”) + -ficare, a suffix forming verbs from adjectives.
