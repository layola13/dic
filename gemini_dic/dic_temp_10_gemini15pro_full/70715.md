financist:/ˈfaɪnænsɪst/ /fɪˈnænsɪst/
noun
金融家；理财专家
A person skilled in or occupied with finance.
例句：The financist predicted a rise in interest rates.  这位金融家预测利率会上升。

例句：Several leading financists advised the government on the new economic policy.  几位主要的金融家就新的经济政策向政府提供建议。

近义词：financier, banker, investor
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider debtor, borrower depending on context)
词性：n.
