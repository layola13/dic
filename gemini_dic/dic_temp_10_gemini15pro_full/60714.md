electro-oculograph:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɒkjʊləɡrɑːf/
noun
眼电图仪
An instrument that measures the standing potential between the cornea and retina of the eye.
The electro-oculograph recorded rapid eye movements during REM sleep.  眼电图仪记录了快速眼动睡眠期间的眼球快速运动。
