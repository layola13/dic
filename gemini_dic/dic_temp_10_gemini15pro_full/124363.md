motivated:/ˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有动机的，有积极性的 | Driven by a desire or determination to achieve something. | She is a highly motivated student. 她是一个很有动机的学生。| The team was motivated by the desire to win. 这个团队被获胜的渴望所激励。 |  inspired, driven, ambitious, enthusiastic |  unmotivated, apathetic, lethargic, indifferent | adjective


Etymology->
From the verb "motivate," which comes from the Latin word "motivus," meaning "moving, serving to move," derived from "movere," meaning "to move."
