incivilization:/ɪnˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 不文明;野蛮;未开化 |  The state of being uncivilized or lacking in culture and refinement.  |  Their behavior was pure incivilization. 他们的行为纯粹是野蛮。 | 近义词: barbarity, savagery, rudeness | 反义词: civilization, refinement, civility | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "in-" (not) + "civilization" (the stage of human social development and organization that is considered most advanced.)  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "civilization."
