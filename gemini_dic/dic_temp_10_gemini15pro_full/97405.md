infrigidate:/ɪnˈfrɪdʒɪdeɪt/
verb
使寒冷；使冷淡；使沮丧
to make something very cold; to make someone feel less friendly or enthusiastic
The sudden drop in temperature infrigidated the hikers. 温度的骤降使徒步旅行者感到寒冷。
His aloof manner infrigidated the entire meeting. 他冷漠的态度使整个会议气氛冷淡下来。
Synonyms: chill, cool, dampen, discourage
Antonyms: warm, heat, encourage, excite
词性:动词
