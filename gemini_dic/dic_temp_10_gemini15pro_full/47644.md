debituminization:/diːbɪˌtjuːmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 脱沥青化 |The process of removing bitumen from a substance, such as oil sands or asphalt. |Example: The debituminization process is crucial for extracting valuable components from oil sands. 脱沥青化工艺对于从油砂中提取有价值的成分至关重要。

Example:  Debituminization technologies are constantly evolving to improve efficiency and reduce environmental impact. 脱沥青技术不断发展，以提高效率并减少对环境的影响。 |Synonym: deasphalting |Antonym: bituminization |Noun
