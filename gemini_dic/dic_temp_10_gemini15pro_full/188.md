ETYMOLOGY->
From the proper noun "ABBA," the name of the Swedish pop group. The 's' is the possessive marker in English, indicating ownership or association.  ABBA itself is an acronym formed from the first letters of the members' first names: Agnetha, Benny, Björn, and Anni-Frid.
