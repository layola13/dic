demerger:/diːˈmɜːr(d)ʒər/ /diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
| n. |公司分拆，业务拆分|The separation of a company into two or more independent companies.  A demerger often involves distributing shares of the new companies to existing shareholders of the original company.|The company underwent a demerger to focus on its core business. 这家公司进行了分拆，以便专注于其核心业务。|spin-off, divestiture, separation|merger, acquisition, consolidation|noun

demergers:/diːˈmɜːr(d)ʒər/ /diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
| n. (plural)|公司分拆（复数）|Multiple instances of companies separating into two or more independent companies.|Several demergers have taken place in the tech industry recently. 最近科技行业发生了数起公司分拆。|spin-offs, divestitures, separations|mergers, acquisitions, consolidations|noun
