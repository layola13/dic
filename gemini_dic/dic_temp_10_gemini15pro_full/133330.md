nonexpressively:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsprɛsɪvli/ | adv. | 无表情地，缺乏表情地 |  Without expression; in a way that does not convey feelings or thoughts. | She stared nonexpressively at the wall, her face a blank mask. 她面无表情地盯着墙，脸上毫无表情。| He listened nonexpressively, giving no indication of what he was thinking. 他毫无表情地听着，没有流露出任何想法。 | 近义词: blankly, impassively, emotionlessly, stolidly | 反义词: expressively, emotionally, feelingly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From non- + expressive + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "expressive" comes from the Latin "expressus," meaning "pressed out" or "clearly depicted," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
