effodient:/ɪˈfoʊdiənt/
adjective
挖掘的；掘地的
Adapted or specialized for digging.
The effodient forelimbs of the mole are perfectly designed for tunneling. 鼹鼠善于挖掘的前肢非常适合挖地道。
近义词:fossorial, burrowing
反义词:non-fossorial

词性:
adjective

词源:
From Latin *effodiēns*, present participle of effodere (“to dig out, unearth”), from ex (“out”) + fodere (“to dig”).
