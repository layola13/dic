outbake:/ˌaʊtˈbeɪk/
verb
比…烤得更好，在烘焙比赛中胜过
To bake for a longer time or with better results than someone else.
She outbaked her competitors at the county fair and won first prize. 她在县集市上烘焙比赛中胜过她的竞争对手，赢得了一等奖。
Synonyms: surpass, outdo, excel
Antonyms: lose to, be inferior to
词性: 动词
