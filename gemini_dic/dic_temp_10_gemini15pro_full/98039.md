inosculation: /ɪˌnɒskjʊˈleɪʃən/ /ɪˌnɑːskjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
吻合；接合；结合；（血管、神经等的）吻合；（植物枝条的）天然嫁接
The natural joining of parts, especially of veins, nerves, or branches of plants, so as to become or make continuous.
The inosculation of the branches created a natural archway. 树枝的吻合形成了一个天然的拱门。
近义词: anastomosis, union, junction, fusion, coalescence
反义词: separation, division, disjunction
词性: n.

inosculation: /ɪˌnɒskjʊˈleɪʃən/ /ɪˌnɑːskjʊˈleɪʃən/
verb
吻合；接合；结合；（血管、神经等的）吻合；（植物枝条的）天然嫁接
To unite or join by inosculation.
The surgeon inosculated the two blood vessels.  外科医生将两根血管吻合。
近义词: anastomose, unite, join, fuse, coalesce
反义词: separate, divide, disjoin
词性: v.
