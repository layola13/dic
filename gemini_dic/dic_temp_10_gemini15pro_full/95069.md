inadvertency:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtnsi/| n. |疏忽，无心之失|An oversight or unintentional act or omission. |  She apologized for the inadvertent omission of his name from the list. 她为无意中从名单上遗漏了他的名字而道歉。

I broke the vase by pure inadvertency. 我完全是无意中打碎了花瓶的。

|近义词：oversight, blunder, slip, lapse, error, mistake|反义词：carefulness, attention, deliberation|词性: noun
