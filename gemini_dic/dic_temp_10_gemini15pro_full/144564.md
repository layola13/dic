outwalloping: /ˌaʊtwɑːləpɪŋ/ | v. | 彻底击败，痛击 | To defeat decisively; to trounce. |  The home team outwalloped their rivals 10 to 0. 主队以10比0彻底击败了他们的对手。| The champion outwalloped the challenger in the first round. 冠军在第一轮就痛击了挑战者。 | 近义词: trounce, thrash, crush, rout | 反义词: lose to, be defeated by | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Out-" (超过) + "wallop" (猛击).  "Wallop" 源于14世纪的 "walop,"  可能源于古法语 "galoper" (飞奔).
