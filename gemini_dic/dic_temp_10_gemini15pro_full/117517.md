meep:/miːp/| int. n.| 哔; 嘟; (表示惊讶、兴奋、恐惧等的)叽; |A short, high-pitched sound, often used to express surprise, excitement, or fear.  It's often associated with electronic devices or cartoon characters.| Example: "Meep! I won the lottery!" 哔！我中彩票了！ | Example:  The robot let out a little "meep" before shutting down.  机器人关机前发出了一声小小的“哔”。 | 近义词：beep, peep, squeak| 反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a sound.  Could consider silence or a low growl depending on context) |词性: interjection, noun
