baits:/beɪts/| n. v.|诱饵，饵；引诱|Something used to attract or lure, especially to trap or catch.|The fisherman used worms as bait. 渔夫用虫子作诱饵。|近义词:lures, decoys, enticements|反义词:deterrents|词性:名词复数;动词第三人称单数

baits:/beɪts/| v.|引诱；激怒|To deliberately annoy or taunt someone.|He baited the dog by waving a stick in front of it. 他在狗面前挥舞棍子来激怒它。|近义词:provoke, tease, goad|反义词:soothe, placate|词性:动词第三人称单数
