outsprung:/ˌaʊtˈsprʌŋ/
adjective
向外伸出的，向外跳出的
Having sprung or leaped outward.  Projecting outward from a central point or support.
The outsprung branches of the tree provided ample shade.  这棵树向外伸出的枝条提供了充足的阴凉。
近义词: projecting, protruding, extending
反义词: retracted, withdrawn
词性: adjective

outsprung:/ˌaʊtˈsprʌŋ/
past participle of outspring
向外跳出的，涌出的
Having sprung or leaped outward. Originating or emanating from.
He was outsprung from a noble family. 他出身名门。
近义词: emanating, originating, stemming
