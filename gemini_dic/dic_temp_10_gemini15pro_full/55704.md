diurnalness:/daɪˈɜːrnlənəs/
noun
昼行性；白昼活动
The state or quality of being diurnal.
Bats exhibit a pronounced diurnalness, only venturing out at night.  蝙蝠表现出明显的昼行性，只在夜间外出。
The diurnalness of bees allows them to efficiently pollinate flowers during daylight hours. 蜜蜂的昼行性使它们能够在白天有效地授粉。
diurnality, activity during the day
nocturnality, inactivity during the day
n.
