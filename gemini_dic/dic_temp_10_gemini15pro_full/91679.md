hyperclimaxes:/ˌhaɪpərˈklaɪmæksiːz/
|n.| (rhetoric)  层进高潮,递进高潮|A series of climaxes, each one being more intense than the last, often used for comic effect.|The comedian used a series of hyperclimaxes to build up the tension before his punchline.|近义词:auxesis, crescendo|反义词:anticlimax, bathos|noun
