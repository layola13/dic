examination:/ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃn/ /ɪɡˌzæməˈneɪʃn/ | n. |考试；检查；审查|A detailed inspection or investigation.|The doctor gave him a thorough medical examination. 医生给他做了全面的体检。|近义词：test, inspection, scrutiny, analysis|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 lack of examination/inspection)|名词

exam:/ɪɡˈzæm/ /ɪɡˈzæm/ | n. |考试|A formal test of a person's knowledge or proficiency in a particular subject.|I have a history exam tomorrow. 我明天有历史考试。|近义词：test, quiz|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|名词


词源：
Both "examination" and "exam" come from the Latin word "examinare," meaning "to weigh, test, or examine."  This further derives from "examen," meaning "tongue of a balance," referring to the process of weighing and judging.
