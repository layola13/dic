hoax:/hoʊks/| n. vt.|骗局，恶作剧；欺骗，愚弄|A deliberate deception; a prank.|The radio station broadcast a hoax about a Martian invasion. 电台播出了一场关于火星人入侵的骗局。|synonyms:fraud, deception, trick, sham|antonyms:truth, reality|noun

hoax:/hoʊks/| vt.|欺骗，愚弄|To deceive or trick someone.|They hoaxed the public with a false report. 他们用一份虚假报告欺骗了公众。|synonyms:deceive, trick, fool, dupe|antonyms:enlighten, inform|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from *hocus,* a variant of *hocus-pocus* (17th century), a formula used in conjuring. 最初来源不确定，可能源于 *hocus*，是 *hocus-pocus*（17世纪）的变体，一个用于戏法的咒语。
