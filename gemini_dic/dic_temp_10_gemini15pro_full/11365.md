areola:/əˈriːələ/ /ɑːrˈiːələ/ | n. | 乳晕；晕圈；(某些植物或动物的)环状色斑 | A small circular area, in particular the dark area surrounding a nipple. | 例句1：The areola surrounding her nipple was darker than her skin. 她乳头周围的乳晕比她的皮肤颜色深。

例句2：The cactus flower emerged from a woolly areola. 仙人掌的花朵从一个毛茸茸的晕圈中冒出来。 | 近义词：ring, circle, halo | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

areolet:/ˈɛəriəˌlɛt/  | n. | (仙人掌)刺座；小孔 | A small cushion-like structure bearing spines or hairs, characteristic of cacti.  | 例句1：The spines on a cactus grow from the areolets. 仙人掌的刺从刺座上长出来。

例句2：Each areolet on the cactus contained several tiny spines. 仙人掌上的每个刺座都包含几个小刺。 | 近义词：spine cushion, tubercle | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
