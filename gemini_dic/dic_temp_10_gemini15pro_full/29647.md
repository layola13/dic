caterwauling:/ˈkætərˌwɔːlɪŋ/| n. vi.| 刺耳的尖叫声；嚎叫；|A long, loud, and unpleasant cry, like that of a cat in heat.  It can also refer to noisy, discordant singing or music. | The caterwauling of the cats kept me awake all night. 猫的嚎叫声让我整夜没睡。|The band's caterwauling was hardly music to my ears. 这个乐队的刺耳演奏对我来说简直算不上音乐。|近义词：shrieking, wailing, howling, screeching|反义词：purring, humming, crooning|noun & verb
