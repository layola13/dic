enfeloned:/ɪnˈfɛl.ənd/
verb
使成为重罪犯；宣布…为重罪
to make something a felony; declare something a felony
The new law enfeloned several previously minor offenses.  这项新法律将几项以前轻微的违法行为定为重罪。
The act of stealing the jewels was enfeloned by the new law.  偷窃珠宝的行为被新法律定为重罪。
近义词:criminalize, indict
反义词:decriminalize, pardon
词性:v.

Etymology:
From en- + felon + -ed.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be". "Felon" refers to a person who has committed a felony. "-ed" is a suffix used to form the past tense and past participle of verbs.
