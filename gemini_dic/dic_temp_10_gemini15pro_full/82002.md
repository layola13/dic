griot:/ˈɡriːoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡriːət/ (UK)| n.|吟游诗人，西非历史学家|A West African storyteller, singer, musician, and oral historian who perpetuates the oral traditions of a family or community.|The griot told stories of ancient kings and heroes. 这位吟游诗人讲述了古代国王和英雄的故事。|近义词：storyteller, bard, historian|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fabricator" in a negative sense)|Noun

词源:
From French *griot*, from Wolof *géwél* "praise-singer".
