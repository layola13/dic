crewmanship:/ˈkruːmənʃɪp/
noun
团队合作精神；团队协作能力
The skill and efficiency of a crew working together.  The quality of teamwork exhibited by a group, especially in a challenging or demanding situation.
例句：The success of the mission depended on the crewmanship of the astronauts.  这次任务的成功取决于宇航员们的团队协作能力。

例句：Their crewmanship was evident in how smoothly they handled the emergency. 他们在处理紧急情况时的团队合作精神显而易见。
近义词：teamwork, collaboration, coordination, synergy
反义词：discord, disunity, conflict
