dematerialization:/diːməˌtɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|非物质化，去物质化|The process of converting physical documents or assets into a digital format.|例句：Dematerialization of share certificates has made stock trading more efficient. 股票证书的非物质化使股票交易更加高效。|近义词：digitization, virtualization|反义词:materialization|词性:名词


dematerialize:/diːməˈtɪəriəlaɪz/
v.|非物质化，去物质化|To convert something from a physical form to a digital or electronic one; to disappear.|例句：The company decided to dematerialize its share certificates.  这家公司决定将其股票证书非物质化。
例句：The magician made the rabbit dematerialize. 魔术师使兔子消失了。|近义词：digitize, virtualize|反义词:materialize|词性:动词
