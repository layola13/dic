mafioso:/ˌmɑːfiˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌmæfiˈəʊsəʊ/| n.|黑手党成员|A member of the Mafia, especially a high-ranking one.|The mafioso was known for his ruthlessness and control over the city's underground operations.  这个黑手党成员以其残忍和对城市地下活动的控制而闻名。|Synonyms: gangster, mobster, racketeer|Antonyms: law-enforcer, police officer|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *mafioso*, from Sicilian *mafiusu* "swaggering, arrogant," perhaps related to Arabic *mahya* "boasting, bragging."  源自意大利语 *mafioso*，来自西西里语 *mafiusu* “招摇，傲慢”，可能与阿拉伯语 *mahya* “吹嘘，吹牛”有关。
