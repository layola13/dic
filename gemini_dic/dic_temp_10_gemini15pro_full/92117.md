hypersalivation:/ˌhaɪpərˌsælɪˈveɪʃən/
n.
流涎症，唾液分泌过多
Excessive production of saliva.
The patient experienced hypersalivation as a side effect of the medication.  这个病人由于药物的副作用而流口水过多。
Hypersalivation can be a symptom of certain medical conditions. 流涎症可能是某些疾病的症状。
近义词:sialorrhea, ptyalism
反义词:xerostomia (口干症)
词性:noun

Etymology:
From hyper- + salivation, from Late Latin salivationem (nominative salivatio), noun of action from past-participle stem of salivare "to salivate," from saliva "saliva."
