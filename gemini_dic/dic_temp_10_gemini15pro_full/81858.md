gridironed:/ˈɡrɪdˌaɪrnd/| adj.|佈滿格子的，像烤架的；(尤指体育比赛)严酷考验的|Marked with a grid; resembling a gridiron, especially in a way that suggests a severe ordeal or intense scrutiny.  The term originates from the resemblance to the pattern of a gridiron, historically used for cooking over an open fire.|The football field was gridironed with yard lines. 足球场被码线划分成格子状。|The team was gridironed by the media after their loss.  球队输球后受到了媒体的严厉批评。|Synonyms: gridded, checkered, crosshatched, grilled (figuratively)|Antonyms: smooth, unmarked, unblemished|adjective
