mistrials:/ˌmɪsˈtraɪəlz/
n.
无效审判，流审
A trial that is invalid because of some procedural error.
The judge declared a mistrial after a juror was found to be biased.  法官发现一名陪审员有偏见后宣布审判无效。
The case ended in a mistrial due to a hung jury.  由于陪审团意见不一致，案件以流审告终。
Synonyms: invalid trial, aborted trial
Antonyms: valid trial, successful trial
noun
