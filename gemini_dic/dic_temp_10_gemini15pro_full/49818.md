demonstrate:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪt/ (US) /ˈdɛmənstreɪt/ (UK)| v. |展示，演示；论证；证明；示威 |To clearly show something by giving proof or evidence; to participate in a public display of opinion.|The teacher demonstrated how to solve the equation. 老师演示了如何解方程。|synonyms: show, display, exhibit, illustrate, prove, establish |antonyms: conceal, hide, obscure|verb

demonstration:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən/ (US) /ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |示范，演示；论证；证明；示威|An act of showing something clearly by giving proof or evidence; a public display of group opinion.|The salesperson gave a demonstration of the new vacuum cleaner.  销售员演示了新的吸尘器。|synonyms: display, exhibition, presentation, proof |antonyms: concealment|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *demonstrare, from Latin demonstrare (“to point out, show, indicate, demonstrate”), from de- (“out, from”) + monstrare (“to show”).
