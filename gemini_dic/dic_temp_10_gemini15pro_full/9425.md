antirationality:/ˌæntiˌræʃəˈnæləti/| n. |反理性，非理性|The rejection of reason and logic, often in favor of intuition, emotion, or tradition.  The elevation of feeling and instinct over reason and evidence.|His antirationality led him to believe in conspiracy theories despite overwhelming evidence to the contrary. 他对理性的排斥使他相信阴谋论，尽管有压倒性的证据反驳。|近义词：irrationality, illogicality, unreasonableness|反义词: rationality, reason, logic|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek)
rationality (noun): the quality of being based on or in accordance with reason or logic (from Latin rationalis)
