lonesomeness:/ˈloʊnlinəsnəs/| n.|孤独，寂寞|A feeling of sadness because one has no friends or company.|例句：The lonesomeness of the long winter evenings was hard to bear. 漫长冬夜的寂寞难以忍受。

例句：She felt a deep lonesomeness after her children left home. 孩子们离家后，她感到深深的寂寞。|近义词：loneliness, solitude, isolation|反义词:togetherness, companionship|词性:noun
词源: From *lone* (alone) + *-some* (characterized by) + *-ness* (state of being).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *alainaz* (alone).
