euchite:/ˈjuːkaɪt/
n.
祝祷者; (尤指)早期基督教的祈祷者; (尤指)早期基督徒的祈祷者;
A member of a sect of early Christians who devoted themselves to prayer.  Specifically refers to a group of early Christians known for their ascetic practices and constant prayer.
The Euchites believed that through constant prayer they could achieve union with God.  祝祷者相信通过不断的祈祷，他们可以与上帝合一。
The practices of the Euchites were sometimes seen as heretical. 有时祝祷者的行为被视为异端。
Synonym:  ascetic, mystic
Antonym:  hedonist, materialist
词性: noun
