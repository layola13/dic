fending:/ˈfɛndɪŋ/| n. |防御; 抵挡; 照顾自己; 谋生 | the act of defending yourself from attack or criticism, or of looking after yourself without needing help from others |  He was left alone, fending for himself at the age of 14. 他14岁就被留下独自一人生活。 | synonyms: defending, protecting, guarding, looking after, taking care of, providing for | antonyms: surrendering, yielding, neglecting | noun

fend:/fɛnd/| v. |抵挡; 挡开; 避开; 照顾自己; 谋生 | to protect yourself from something dangerous or unpleasant, or to take care of yourself without needing help from other people |  She managed to fend off her attacker with a stick. 她用一根棍子成功地击退了袭击者。 | synonyms: defend, protect, guard, look after, take care of, provide for | antonyms: surrender, yield, neglect | verb
