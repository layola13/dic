destructively:/dɪˈstrʌktɪvli/
adverb
破坏性地; 毁灭性地
in a way that causes destruction or damage.
The hurricane destructively swept through the coastal towns. 飓风以毁灭性的方式席卷了沿海城镇。
Synonyms: ruinously, devastatingly, catastrophically
Antonyms: constructively, beneficially, helpfully
词性: 副词
