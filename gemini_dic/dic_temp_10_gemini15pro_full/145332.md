overbrutalization:/ˌoʊvərˌbruːtələˈzeɪʃən/
noun
过度残暴化; 过度野蛮化
The act of making something excessively brutal or savage.
The overbrutalization of warfare in the 21st century is a cause for concern.  21世纪战争的过度残暴化令人担忧。
The film depicted the overbrutalization of prisoners of war. 这部电影描绘了战俘被过度残暴对待的情况。
近义词: excessive brutality, extreme savagery, barbarization
反义词:  civilization, humanization, pacification
词性: noun
