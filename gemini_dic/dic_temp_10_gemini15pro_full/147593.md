overplanned:/ˌoʊvərˈplænd/ | adj. | 过度计划的，计划过多的 | Planned in excessive detail or to an excessive degree. | The trip was overplanned, leaving no room for spontaneity.  这次旅行计划过度，没有留下任何自发的空间。|  The conference schedule was so overplanned that attendees felt rushed and overwhelmed. 会议日程安排得过于紧凑，以至于与会者感到匆忙和不知所措。 | 近义词: over-organized, over-scheduled | 反义词: underplanned, flexible | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "planned" (past participle of the verb "plan").  "Over-" 源自古英语 "ofer," 意思是 "在...之上" 或 "过度"。 "Plan" 源自法语 "plan," 最终源自拉丁语 "planus," 意思是 "平坦的" 或 "水平的"。
