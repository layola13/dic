ditches:/ˈdɪtʃɪz/
n.
沟渠; 壕沟; 水沟
A long narrow channel dug in the ground, typically used for drainage or irrigation.
The car swerved to avoid a pothole and ended up in the ditches. 汽车为了避开坑洼而突然转向，结果掉进了沟里。
trenches, furrows, canals, gutters
ridges, mounds
