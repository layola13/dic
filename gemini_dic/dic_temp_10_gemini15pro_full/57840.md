dressiness:/ˈdrɛsɪnɪs/
n.
正式程度；衣着讲究程度
The degree to which clothing is formal or elegant.
The dressiness of the occasion called for a suit and tie.  这个场合的正式程度要求穿西装打领带。
近义词: formality, elegance, smartness
反义词: casualness, informality
词性: noun
