elide:/ɪˈlaɪd/ /iˈlaɪd/ | v. |省略，删除|To omit (a sound or syllable) when speaking.  To suppress or pass over (something, such as a point or fact). |She elided the last syllable of the word. 她省略了单词的最后一个音节。

The poet elided some lines from his original draft. 诗人从他的初稿中删除了几行。|近义词：omit, delete, suppress, drop, cut |反义词：include, add, insert |词性: 动词

词源：From Latin ēlīdō (“to strike out, crush out”), from ē- (“out”) + laedō (“to strike, hurt”).
