grieving:/ˈɡriːvɪŋ/| adj.| 悲伤的，悲痛的|Feeling or expressing great sadness, especially due to someone's death.|She is still grieving the loss of her husband. 她仍在为失去丈夫而悲伤。|近义词：mourning, sorrowful, heartbroken|反义词：joyful, happy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English greven, from Old English grēofan  "to grieve, lament," from Proto-Germanic *greufan, from Proto-Indo-European *ghreu- "to rub, grind; to be distressed."
