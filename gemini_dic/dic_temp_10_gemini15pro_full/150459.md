pantheism:/ˈpænθiːɪzəm/ | n. | 泛神论 | The belief that God is everything and everywhere, or that God is identical with the universe. |  Pantheism is a complex philosophical concept that has been interpreted in many ways. 泛神论是一个复杂的哲学概念，已被多种方式解读。

Pantheism suggests that the divine is immanent in all things. 泛神论认为神性存在于万物之中。 | 近义词: panentheism, cosmicism | 反义词: atheism, theism | 词性: noun
