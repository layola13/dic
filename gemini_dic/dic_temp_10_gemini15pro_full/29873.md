causeway:/ˈkɔːzweɪ/| n.|堤道，铺道|A raised road or path, typically built across water or marshland.|We drove along the causeway to the island. 我们沿着堤道开车到岛上。|近义词：road, path, track, highway, route|反义词:|noun

causeway:/ˈkɔːzweɪ/| n.|通道，通路|A path or roadway.|The ancient causeway led to the temple. 这条古老的通道通向寺庙。|近义词：path, track, lane, walkway|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *calceiée ("paved road"), from Vulgar Latin *calciata, from Latin calx ("heel, limestone, chalk"). Related to calcaneus and chalk. Doublet of chaussee.
