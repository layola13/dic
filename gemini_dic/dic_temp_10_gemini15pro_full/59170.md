dysfunction:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃən/| n.|功能障碍，机能不良|impaired or abnormal functioning|The family suffered from severe dysfunction after the loss of their father.  这个家庭在失去父亲后出现了严重的功能障碍。| The doctor diagnosed a dysfunction in his liver. 医生诊断出他的肝脏功能不良。|Synonyms: malfunction, impairment, disorder, abnormality|Antonyms: function, health, normality|noun

dysfunctional:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəl/| adj.|机能失调的，功能障碍的|not operating normally or properly|The dysfunctional family struggled to communicate.  这个不正常的家庭难以沟通。| The dysfunctional equipment posed a safety hazard.  功能失调的设备造成了安全隐患。|Synonyms: broken, impaired, defective, nonfunctional|Antonyms: functional, working, healthy, normal|adjective
