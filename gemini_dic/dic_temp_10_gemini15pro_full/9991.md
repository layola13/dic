aphrite:/ˈæfraɪt/| n.|海沫，泡沫，碳酸钙的一种|A pearly or chalky variety of calcium carbonate.  A frothy deposit of calcium carbonate.|例句：The aphrite coated the rocks near the hot spring. 温泉附近的岩石上覆盖着海沫。

例句：Aphrite can sometimes be mistaken for other minerals due to its varying appearance. 海沫有时会因为其多变的外观而被误认为是其他矿物。|近义词：foam, froth, calcite|反义词: (No direct antonym for a naturally occurring substance)|词性:noun
