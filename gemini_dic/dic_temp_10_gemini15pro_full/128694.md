nidian: /ˈnɪdiən/ | adj. | 巢的；nidicolous的 |  Relating to or characteristic of nidicolous birds, which are born helpless and require parental care in the nest. | The nidian young were completely dependent on their parents for food and warmth. 这些还在巢中的幼鸟完全依赖父母提供食物和温暖。 | 近义词: altricial, nestling | 反义词: precocial, fledgling | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *nidus* (nest) +‎ *-icolous* (dwelling in). Related to nidicolous.
