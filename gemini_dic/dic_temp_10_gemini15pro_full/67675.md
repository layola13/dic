extrusive:/ɪkˈstruːsɪv/
adjective
喷出的；外生的；火山喷出的
Formed by solidification of molten rock that has erupted onto the earth's surface.
例句：Extrusive rocks are formed from lava that cools quickly on the surface.  喷出岩是由地表快速冷却的熔岩形成的。

例句：Basalt is a common extrusive igneous rock. 玄武岩是一种常见的喷出火成岩。
近义词：volcanic, eruptive
反义词：intrusive
词性：adjective
