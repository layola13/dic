interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/ | v. | 干涉，干预，妨碍 | To become involved in a situation when your involvement is not wanted or is not helpful. |  Don't interfere in other people's business. 不要干涉别人的事。 | Synonyms: meddle, intervene, intrude | Antonyms: abstain, avoid, ignore | verb

interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/ | v. | 冲突，抵触 | To prevent something from working effectively or from developing successfully. | The drug interferes with the body's natural healing process. 这种药物干扰了身体的自然愈合过程。| Synonyms: clash, conflict, collide | Antonyms: aid, assist, cooperate | verb
