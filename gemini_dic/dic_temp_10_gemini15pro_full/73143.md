forar: /foˈɾaɾ/ (Brazilian Portuguese)| v.|钻孔，穿孔|To drill a hole, to pierce, to bore. |They foraram a parede para instalar o ar-condicionado.  They drilled a hole in the wall to install the air conditioner.|近义词： perfurar, furar|反义词: tapar, fechar|词性: verb

词源: From Vulgar Latin *fōrāre, from Latin forāmen (“hole”).
