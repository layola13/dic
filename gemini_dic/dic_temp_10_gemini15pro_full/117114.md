mealy-mouthed:/ˈmiːliˌmaʊðd/ | adj. | 说话婉转的，拐弯抹角的，不敢直言的 | Afraid to speak frankly or straightforwardly, especially about something unpleasant or controversial.  | He gave a mealy-mouthed apology, never actually admitting he was wrong. 他说了一些拐弯抹角的道歉话，却从未真正承认自己错了。 | Synonyms: evasive, indirect, reticent, noncommittal | Antonyms: forthright, blunt, candid, outspoken | adjective

mealy-mouthed:/ˈmiːliˌmaʊðd/ | adj. | 说话委婉的，含糊其辞的 |  Unwilling to express opinions or give information clearly and directly. | The politician gave a mealy-mouthed response to the journalist's probing questions.  这位政客对记者的尖锐提问给出了一个含糊其辞的回答。| Synonyms: vague, ambiguous, equivocal | Antonyms: clear, direct, unambiguous | adjective
