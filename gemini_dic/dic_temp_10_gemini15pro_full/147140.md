overlooker:/ˈoʊvərlʊkər/ | n. | 监督人；观察者；俯瞰者 |  A person who overlooks something or someone, often from a high position.  Someone who supervises or monitors. Someone who has a comprehensive view of something. | From a window in the skyscraper, the overlooker had a panoramic view of the city. 从摩天大楼的窗户里，这位观察者可以看到城市的全景。|  The construction site overlooker ensured all safety protocols were followed.  建筑工地的监督员确保所有安全协议都得到遵守。 | supervisor, observer, monitor, superintendent |  none | noun
