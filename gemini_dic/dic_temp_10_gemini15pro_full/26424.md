bylines:/ˈbaɪlaɪnz/ | n. | 署名；作者署名行 | A line at the beginning of a newspaper or magazine article that gives the writer's name. | The article appeared with no byline. 这篇文章没有署名发表。 | synonyms: credit, attribution | antonyms: anonymity, uncredited | noun

bylines:/ˈbaɪlaɪnz/ | n. | 副业；兼职 | A secondary occupation. | He makes a bit of extra money from his bylines as a sports journalist. 他作为一名体育记者的兼职工作让他赚了一些外快。| synonyms: sideline, second job | antonyms: main job, primary occupation | noun
