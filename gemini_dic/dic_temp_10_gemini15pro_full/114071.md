magnetograph:/ˈmæɡnɪtəɡræf/ | n. | 磁像仪，磁强记录仪 | An instrument that records variations in the strength of a magnetic field, especially the magnetic field of the sun.  It creates a visual representation called a magnetogram. | The magnetograph detected a significant increase in solar activity. 磁像仪探测到太阳活动显著增加。 |

magnetograph:/ˈmæɡnɪtəɡræf/ | n. | 磁像仪，磁强记录仪 |  An instrument used for measuring and recording the strength and direction of magnetic fields, particularly those associated with celestial bodies like the Sun. | Scientists use magnetographs to study the evolution of sunspots. 科学家使用磁强记录仪来研究太阳黑子的演化。 |  Synonyms: magnetometer (more general), magnetic recorder | Antonyms: N/A | noun
