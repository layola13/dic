orthosilicate:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/ | n. |原硅酸盐|A salt or ester of orthosilicic acid.  It contains the SiO₄⁴⁻ anion.|例句：Zircon is an orthosilicate mineral. 锆石是一种原硅酸盐矿物。

例句：Olivine is a group of orthosilicate minerals.  橄榄石是一组原硅酸盐矿物。|近义词：silicate|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ortho- + silicate.  "Ortho-" comes from Greek orthos meaning "straight," "right," or "true," indicating the highest degree of hydration or oxidation. "Silicate" refers to any of numerous salts derived from silica.
