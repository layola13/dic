overerupt:/ˌoʊvərɪˈrʌpt/| v. |过度萌出|To erupt beyond the normal occlusal plane. (指牙齿)萌出超过正常的咬合平面。|The lower incisors had overerupted due to the missing upper teeth. 由于上牙缺失，下切牙过度萌出。| The patient's upper canines overerupted after the loss of the lower premolars.  病人的下前磨牙缺失后，上尖牙过度萌出。 | 近义词:supraerupt| 反义词: infraerupt|词性:动词
