heterocera:/ˌhɛtərˈɒsərə/ | n. | 蛾类，异角亚目 | A suborder of Lepidoptera, commonly known as moths, distinguished from butterflies (Rhopalocera) by several characteristics, including antennae that are not clubbed. |  The heterocera are a fascinating group of insects, exhibiting a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and colors. 蛾类是一组迷人的昆虫，展现出各种各样的形状、大小和颜色。|  Many species of heterocera are nocturnal. 许多蛾类物种都是夜行性的。 |Synonyms: moths | Antonyms: butterflies (Rhopalocera) | noun
