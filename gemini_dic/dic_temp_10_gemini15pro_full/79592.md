glore:/ɡlɔːr/ | n. | 荣耀，光辉 | Brightness and magnificence; radiant beauty. | The knight returned home, covered in glore after his victory. 骑士凯旋而归，浑身散发着荣耀的光芒。 |  The setting sun cast a glore over the mountains. 落日给群山披上了一层光辉。 | 近义词: glory, splendor, brilliance | 反义词: obscurity, dullness | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *glorie, *gloure, from Anglo-Norman glorie, from Old French glorie, from Latin glōria (“glory, fame, renown”), from Proto-Italic *glōsā, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear”). Cognate with Italian gloria, Spanish gloria, Portuguese glória, Occitan glòria, Catalan glòria.
