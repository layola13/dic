decentralize:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/
decentralize|v.|分散；权力下放|To distribute the administrative powers or functions of (a central authority) over a less concentrated area or group.|The company decided to decentralize its operations and open smaller offices across the country. 公司决定分散业务，在全国各地开设小型办事处。|distribute, disperse, devolve|centralize|verb
decentralization:/diːˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃən/
decentralization|n.|分散化；权力下放|The act or process of decentralizing; the state or condition of being decentralized.|The decentralization of power led to greater regional autonomy. 权力下放导致更大的区域自治。|distribution, dispersal, devolution|centralization|noun
decentralized:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzd/
decentralized|adj.|分散的；权力下放的|Characterized by decentralization; having power or function dispersed from a central authority.|The company has a decentralized organizational structure, with each department having considerable autonomy.  这家公司采用分散的组织架构，每个部门都拥有相当大的自主权。|distributed, dispersed, devolved|centralized|adjective
