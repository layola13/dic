crescentic:/krɪˈsɛntɪk/| adj.|新月形的，月牙形的|Shaped like a crescent; resembling a new moon.|例句：The bacteria have a crescentic shape. 这些细菌呈新月形。

例句：The painting featured a crescentic moon hanging low in the sky. 这幅画描绘了一轮低垂在天空中新月。|近义词：lunar, moon-shaped, semicircular|反义词:circular, full, round|词性:adjective
crescent:/ˈkrɛsnt/| n. |新月，月牙|The curved sickle shape of the waxing or waning moon.|例句：A slender crescent moon hung in the sky.  一弯纤细的新月挂在天空。

例句：The symbol of Islam is a crescent moon and star. 伊斯兰教的象征是新月和星星。|近义词：meniscus, lunula|反义词:full moon|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *crescentis, present participle of crescere “to grow, increase,” from Proto-Italic *krez-o, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- “to grow.”
