capeline:/ˈkæpəˌliːn/ /ˈkæpəˌlaɪn/| n.|女式披肩；遮阳帽；头巾|A woman's hooded cloak or cape, often of lace or other light material.  A woman's hat that ties under the chin, typically with a wide, soft brim.|She wore a beautiful capeline to the garden party. 她戴着一条漂亮的披肩参加了花园派对。|She shielded her face from the sun with a wide-brimmed capeline. 她用一顶宽边遮阳帽遮挡阳光。|近义词：cape, cloak, shawl, bonnet, sunhat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "beanie" for a contrasting headwear)|noun
