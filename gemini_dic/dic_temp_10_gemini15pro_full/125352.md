multilist:/ˈmʌltiˌlɪst/| n.|多重列表，多维列表|A data structure that allows multiple elements to reside at each node, effectively creating multiple linked lists interwoven together.|The multilist structure allowed for efficient searching across different categories of data.|近义词：multidimensional array, nested list|反义词:single list, linear list|名词
