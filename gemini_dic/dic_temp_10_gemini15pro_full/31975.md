chemotype:/ˈkɛmɪtaɪp/ | n. |化学型|A chemically distinct entity in a plant or microorganism, characterized by a specific combination of secondary metabolites.  A plant may have different chemotypes that look the same but produce different chemical compounds.|The researchers identified several chemotypes of the plant, each with a unique essential oil profile. 研究人员鉴定出该植物的几种化学型，每一种都有独特的精油特征。|synonyms: chemoform, chemical race|antonyms: N/A|noun
