burred:/bɜːrd/ | adj. | 模糊的，(声音)低沉不清的 | Having rough or prickly edges; not clear or distinct. | The photo was blurred because the camera moved. | 照片模糊不清，因为相机动了。 | 近义词：fuzzy, indistinct, unclear, muffled | 反义词：sharp, clear, distinct | 词性:形容词
Etymology: From bur (n.) + -ed.  The original sense referred to a prickly seed vessel or plant.  The meaning extended to a rough or prickly edge, and then to a sound or image that is indistinct or fuzzy, as if obscured by something rough.
