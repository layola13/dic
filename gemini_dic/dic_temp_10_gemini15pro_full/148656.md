overthwartly:/oʊvərˈθwɔːrtli/ | adv. | 横斜地，反常地，乖张地 | In a manner that is contrary, perverse, or awkward; across or athwart something else. |  The ship sailed overthwartly across the bay, against the wind.  这艘船横着穿过海湾，逆风航行。|  The artist deliberately hung the painting overthwartly to create a sense of unease. 艺术家故意把这幅画挂歪，以营造一种不安的感觉。 |  awkwardly, perversely, contrarily, obliquely | directly, straightly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->

From "overthwart" + "-ly."  "Overthwart" itself comes from "over-" (across, beyond) and "thwart" (across, transverse).  "Thwart" ultimately derives from Old Norse "þvert" (across).
