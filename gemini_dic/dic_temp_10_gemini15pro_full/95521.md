included:/ɪnˈkluːdɪd/ | adj. | 包括的，包含的 |Contained as part of a whole or group. | The price includes breakfast.  价格包含早餐。 | Synonyms: contained, encompassed, incorporated | Antonyms: excluded, omitted | adjective

included:/ɪnˈkluːdɪd/ | v. | 包括，包含 |Past tense of "include".  To contain as part of a whole or group. | The tour included a visit to the museum.  旅行包括参观博物馆。 | Synonyms: comprised, contained, embraced | Antonyms: excluded, omitted | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *includere, from Latin includere ("to shut in, enclose, insert"), from in- ("in, into") + claudere ("to shut, close").
