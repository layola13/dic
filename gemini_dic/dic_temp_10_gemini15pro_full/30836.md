chabouk: /ʃəˈbuːk/ /tʃəˈbʊk/ | n. |  鞭子; 鞭打 | A short, heavy whip, usually made of rhinoceros or hippopotamus hide, used for flogging. |  The prisoner was beaten with a chabouk. 囚犯被鞭子抽打。 | Synonyms: whip, lash, scourge, flagellum | Antonyms: caress, soothe | noun

chabouk: /ʃəˈbuːk/ /tʃəˈbʊk/ | vt. |  鞭打 | To flog or whip someone. | The overseer chabouked the slaves for their disobedience. 监工因为奴隶不服从而鞭打他们。| Synonyms: whip, flog, lash, scourge | Antonyms: caress, soothe | verb
