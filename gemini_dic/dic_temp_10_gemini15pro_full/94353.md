impire:/ɪmˈpaɪər/ | v. | 损害，削弱 | to diminish in value, strength, or quality |  The scandal impired the company's reputation. 这桩丑闻损害了公司的声誉。 |  The constant rain impired the soil's fertility.  持续的降雨降低了土壤的肥力。 | 近义词: impair, undermine, weaken, damage | 反义词: enhance, improve, strengthen | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *impirare, from Latin in- + pejorare "to make worse," from pejor "worse."  Related to English "pejorative."
