overcoaching:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtʃɪŋ/
n.
过度指导；过分教导
Excessive instruction or guidance, often to the detriment of the learner's development or performance.
The team's performance suffered due to overcoaching.  这个团队的表现因为过度指导而受到影响。
The young pianist showed signs of overcoaching, playing mechanically without passion.  这位年轻的钢琴家表现出过度指导的迹象，演奏机械而缺乏激情。
近义词：micromanagement, overtraining
反义词：undercoaching, neglect
词性: 名词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "coaching" (from "coach," ultimately from the Hungarian town of Kocs, where a type of horse-drawn carriage originated)
