biconjugate:/baɪˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪt/ | adj. n. |双共轭的，双偶联的；双共轭物|Having or involving two conjugations or unions; a mathematical term related to matrices and vectors, specifically referring to a pair of vectors that are orthogonal with respect to two different inner products.  In biology, it can refer to a type of antibody that is produced in response to two different antigens. |The biconjugate gradient method is often used to solve large systems of linear equations. 双共轭梯度法常用于求解大型线性方程组。|Synonym: dual, paired|Antonym: single, unpaired|adjective, noun

biconjugate:/baɪˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪt/ | v. |双共轭，双偶联|To make or become biconjugate. This verb form is less common than the adjective or noun form. |  Researchers are exploring methods to biconjugate antibodies for improved therapeutic efficacy. 研究人员正在探索双偶联抗体以提高治疗效果的方法。|Synonym: combine, link, pair|Antonym: separate, unlink|verb
