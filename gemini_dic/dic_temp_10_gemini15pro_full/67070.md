explodent:/ɪkˈspləʊdənt/ /ɪkˈsploʊdnt/ | n. adj.|爆炸物，爆炸性的|A substance or device that explodes.  As an adjective, it means capable of exploding.|
The bomb squad carefully handled the explodent. 炸弹小组小心地处理爆炸物。

The explodent material was stored in a secure location. 爆炸性物质被存放在一个安全的地方。

|近义词：explosive, volatile, inflammable|反义词:inert, stable|词性:名词, 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin explodent- (stem of explodens), present participle of explodere "to drive out by clapping, hiss off the stage; explode," from ex- "out" + plaudere "to clap, applaud."
