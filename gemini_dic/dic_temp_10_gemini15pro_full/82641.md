guelph:/ɡwɛlf/| adj.|圭尔夫的|Relating to the city of Guelph, Ontario.|Guelph maple syrup is known for its distinct flavour. 圭尔夫枫糖浆以其独特的风味而闻名。|近义词：Guelphian|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
