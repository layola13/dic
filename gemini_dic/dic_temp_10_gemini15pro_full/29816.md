caul:/kɔːl/| n.|胎膜；头巾；网膜|A membrane enclosing a fetus, especially one that covers the head at birth.  Sometimes superstitiously regarded as a sign of good luck.|She was born with a caul, which her grandmother kept as a good luck charm.|近义词：membrane, veil|反义词: None|名词
