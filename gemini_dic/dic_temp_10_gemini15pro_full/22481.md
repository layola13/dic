booksellers:/ˈbʊkˌsɛlərz/| n.|书商；书店老板|A person or business that sells books.|例句：The booksellers on Charing Cross Road are famous for their rare books. 查令十字街的书商以其稀有书籍而闻名。

例句：Online booksellers are becoming increasingly popular. 在线书商越来越受欢迎。|近义词：bookdealer, retailer, vendor|反义词:book buyer, customer|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From book + seller.  "Seller" comes from the Middle English "sell(en)" meaning "to give up, deliver, yield," from Old English "sellan."  "Book" comes from Old English "bōc," related to "beech" because runes were originally carved on beechwood tablets.
