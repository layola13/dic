ammonites:/ˈæməˌnaɪts/| n. |菊石|An extinct group of cephalopod mollusks with a spiral, chambered shell, related to the modern nautilus and squid.  They lived in the seas from the Devonian period to the end of the Cretaceous period.|The museum had a fascinating display of ammonites.  博物馆里有一个很棒的菊石展览。|The paleontologist discovered a rare species of ammonite.  古生物学家发现了一种稀有的菊石种类。|Synonyms: cephalopods, fossils|Antonyms: None|Noun
