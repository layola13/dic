doglike:/ˈdɒɡlaɪk/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡlaɪk/ (US)| adj.|像狗的，狗似的|Resembling or characteristic of a dog, especially in behavior or appearance. |He gave me a doglike stare, full of devotion. 他给了我一个像狗一样充满深情的凝视。|Synonyms: canine, doggy, houndlike|Antonyms: feline, catlike|adjective
