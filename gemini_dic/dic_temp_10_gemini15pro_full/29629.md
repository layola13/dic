catenation:/ˌkætəˈneɪʃən/
noun
连接，连锁，串联
The act or process of linking things together in a series or chain.
Carbon atoms have the property of catenation, forming long chains and rings. 碳原子具有连锁的特性，能形成长链和环状结构。
concatenation, linking, connection, union
division, separation
