dotage:/ˈdoʊtɪdʒ/| n.|老朽；老年昏聩|The state of being old and weak, especially with some loss of mental ability.|He spent his last years in dotage, barely recognizing his own children. 他在老年痴呆中度过了他的最后几年，几乎认不出自己的孩子了。|Synonyms: senility, decrepitude, feebleness|Antonyms: youth, vigor, alertness|noun
