grandpa:/ˈɡrændpɑː/ (US), /ˈɡræn(d)pɑː/ (UK)| n.|爷爷；外公|A father of one's father or mother.|例句：My grandpa told me stories about his childhood. 我的爷爷给我讲了他童年时代的故事。

例句：I visited my grandpa last weekend. 我上周末去看望了我的爷爷。

近义词：grandfather|反义词:grandson, granddaughter|词性:名词
