inable:/ɪˈneɪbl/ | v. | 使能够，使成为可能 | To make able; to give the power, means, or opportunity to do something. |例句：The scholarship enabled her to attend college.  这笔奖学金使她能够上大学。

例句：The new software enables users to share files easily.  这款新软件使用户能够轻松共享文件。 |近义词：allow, permit, facilitate, empower |反义词:disable, prevent, hinder |词性:动词

词源: Late Middle English: from en-1 + able, probably influenced by Old French enabler.
