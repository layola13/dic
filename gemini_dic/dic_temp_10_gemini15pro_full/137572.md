nontransparentness:/nɒntrænˈspærəntnəs/ | n. |不透明性，不透明度 |The state or quality of being nontransparent; lack of transparency.  This refers to something that is not easily understood or seen through, either physically or metaphorically. | The nontransparentness of the government's decision-making process led to public distrust. 政府决策过程的不透明导致了公众的不信任。| The nontransparentness of the murky water made it impossible to see the bottom. 浑浊的水不透明，使得无法看到底部。| 近义词：opacity, opaqueness, obscurity | 反义词：transparency, clarity, translucence | 词性：名词
