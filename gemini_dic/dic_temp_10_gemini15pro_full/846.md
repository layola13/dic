abstracting: /æbˈstræktɪŋ/
v.
提取；抽象化；概括；摘要；使心不在焉
The process of considering something theoretically or separately from something else.  Removing something.

He was abstracting data from the research. = 他正在从研究中提取数据。

She is abstracting the key points from the report. = 她正在从报告中提取要点。

近义词: summarizing, extracting, synthesizing, conceptualizing
反义词: concretizing, specifying, detailing
词性: 动词 (present participle of abstract)


abstracting: /æbˈstræktɪŋ/
n.
抽象化；提取；概括
The act of abstracting something.

Abstracting key information is a crucial skill. = 提取关键信息是一项至关重要的技能。

The abstracting of the data took several hours. = 数据的提取花费了几个小时。


近义词: summarization, extraction, synthesis, conceptualization
反义词: concretization, specification, detailing
词性: 名词 (gerund of abstract)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abstractus, past participle of abstrahere "to draw away," from abs- "from, away from" + trahere "to draw."
