millstone:/ˈmɪlˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈmɪlˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n. | 磨石；重负；沉重的负担 | A large, heavy circular stone used for grinding grain. Figuratively, something that is a heavy burden or obstacle. | The debt was a millstone around his neck.  这笔债务是他沉重的负担。|  The old traditions were a millstone that prevented progress.  旧的传统是阻碍进步的沉重的负担。 | synonyms: burden, encumbrance, impediment, obstacle | antonyms: advantage, asset, benefit, boon | noun
