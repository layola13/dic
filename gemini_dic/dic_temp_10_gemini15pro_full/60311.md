eglomerate:/ɪˈɡlɒmərət/ | n. | 聚集体，混合物 | A mass formed by gathering or clustering together various elements or particles. | The rock is an eglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。| conglomerate, agglomeration, aggregate | - | noun

eglomerate:/ɪˈɡlɒmərət/ | v. |  聚集，使成团 | To gather into a mass or cluster. | The company eglomerated several smaller businesses into a large corporation.  该公司将几家小企业合并成一个大公司。|  amalgamate, combine, merge | separate, divide | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *eglōmerātus, past participle of eglōmerāre (“to wind into a ball, gather into a mass”), from ex- (“out”) + glōmus (“ball”).
