hyperrealize:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪz/ | v. |过度真实化；超现实化| To make something seem excessively real, often to the point of artificiality or distortion. |例句：The movie hyperrealized the violence, making it almost cartoonish.  这部电影过度渲染了暴力，使它近乎卡通化。

例句：Social media can hyperrealize people's lives, presenting only the most curated and polished versions of themselves. 社交媒体会过度美化人们的生活，只呈现出最精心策划和修饰过的版本。|近义词：exaggerate, amplify, intensify, distort |反义词：understate, downplay, minimize |词性:动词
