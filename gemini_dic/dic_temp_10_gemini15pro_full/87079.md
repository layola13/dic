heptastich: /ˈheptəstɪk/
noun
七行诗
A poem or stanza of seven lines.
Example: The poet concluded his work with a poignant heptastich.  这位诗人以一首凄美的七行诗结束了他的作品。

Example:  She challenged herself to write a heptastich every day for a month. 她挑战自己一个月内每天写一首七行诗。

synonym: septet
antonym: (No direct antonym exists, potentially poems with different numbers of lines, e.g., couplet, quatrain)
词性: 名词
