overwhelmingly:/ˌoʊvərˈwelmɪŋli/| adv.|压倒性地；不可抗拒地；极其|so strong as to be irresistible; very great in amount|The vote was overwhelmingly in favor of the proposal. 投票压倒性地支持该提案。| overwhelmingly positive reviews  获得压倒性的正面评价| 近义词: decisively, predominantly, vastly, immeasurably | 反义词: slightly, marginally, insignificantly | 词性: 副词
词源: From overwhelm + -ing + -ly. Overwhelm comes from Old English *oferwhelman, from ofer- (over) + *whelman (to turn over, submerge).
