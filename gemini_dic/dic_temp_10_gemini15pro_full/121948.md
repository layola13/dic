mission:/ˈmɪʃən/| n.|任务，使命，代表团|a special duty or function which a person or group is sent out to do|例句：Her mission was to help the refugees. 她的任务是帮助难民。|例句：The diplomatic mission was a success. 外交使团取得了成功。|近义词：task, assignment, delegation, objective, purpose, goal|反义词：leisure, vacation, idleness|noun

mission:/ˈmɪʃən/| vt.|派遣，给…交待任务|to send (someone) on a mission|例句：They missioned him to deliver a message. 他们派他去传递消息。|例句：The agency missioned its agents to infiltrate the criminal organization.  该机构派遣其特工渗透进犯罪组织。|近义词：dispatch, send, assign, commission|反义词：recall, withdraw|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
