addictively:/əˈdɪktɪvli/
adverb
上瘾地；容易使人上瘾地
in a way that causes or characterized by addiction.
This game is addictively fun.  这款游戏让人玩得停不下来。
Social media can be addictively engaging. 社交媒体很容易让人沉迷。
近义词:compulsively, obsessively
反义词:moderately, temperately
词性:adverb

USAGE->
She plays the game addictively. = 她玩游戏玩得非常上瘾。
He works addictively, often forgetting to eat or sleep. = 他工作起来非常投入，经常忘记吃饭睡觉。


PHRASE->
addictively fun = 非常有趣，容易上瘾

addictively delicious = 非常美味，让人欲罢不能
addictively playable = 非常容易上瘾的游戏
addictively readable = 非常容易上瘾的读物
