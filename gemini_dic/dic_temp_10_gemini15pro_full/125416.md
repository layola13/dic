multipaths:/ˈmʌltɪˌpæðz/ | n. | 多路径 | Multiple paths that a signal can take to reach a receiver, often resulting in interference or distortion. |The multipaths caused the radio signal to fade in and out. 多路径导致无线电信号断断续续。| synonyms: multiple routes, alternative paths | antonyms: single path, direct route | noun

multipath:/ˈmʌltɪˌpæθ/ | n. | 多路径 | A single instance of a signal traveling along multiple paths to a receiver. | The engineer identified a multipath that was causing the signal degradation. 工程师发现了一条导致信号衰减的多路径。| synonyms: indirect route, reflected path | antonyms: direct path, line of sight | noun
