nonpresentabilities:/ˌnɑːnprɪˌzɛntəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可展示性；无法呈现的状态；不宜公开的事情
The state or quality of being unsuitable or impossible to present, often due to being embarrassing, offensive, or confidential.
Due to the nonpresentabilities of the evidence, the case was dismissed. 由于证据无法出示，案件被驳回。
近义词: unsuitabilities, inadequacies, improprieties
反义词: presentabilities, suitabilities
