anguish:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃ/| n. |痛苦，苦恼|Extreme mental or physical suffering.|She cried out in anguish. 她痛苦地哭喊出来。|Synonyms: agony, torment, distress, pain|Antonyms: comfort, relief, peace, joy|noun
anguishes:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃɪz/| n. |(复数)痛苦，苦恼|Plural form of anguish, referring to multiple instances of extreme suffering.|The anguishes of war are felt for generations. 战争的痛苦延续数代。|Synonyms: agonies, torments, distresses|Antonyms: comforts, reliefs, joys, peaces|noun
