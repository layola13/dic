palmigrade:/ˌpælmɪˈɡreɪd/ /ˈpɑːlmɪɡreɪd/
adjective|跖行的；掌行的|Walking on the palms of the hands and soles of the feet, like bears and raccoons.|Bears and raccoons are examples of palmigrade animals. 熊和浣熊是跖行動物的例子。|plantigrade|digitigrade
noun|跖行动物|An animal that walks on the palms of the hands and soles of the feet.|The gorilla, a large palmigrade, lumbered through the forest.  大猩猩，一种大型的跖行动物，在森林中缓慢地走动。|plantigrade|digitigrade
