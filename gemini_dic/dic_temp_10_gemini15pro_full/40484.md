contributories:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɔːriz/ | n. | 捐助者；出资人；分摊者；（尤指破产公司）债权人 |  People or organizations that contribute something, especially money, or are liable to contribute in the event of a company's liquidation. | The contributories to the fund were mainly large corporations.  该基金的出资人主要是大型企业。| contributors, donors, investors, stakeholders | opponents, detractors | noun

contributory:/kənˈtrɪbjətɔːri/ | adj. | 贡献的；促成的；分摊的 | Playing a part in bringing something about. |  Smoking is a contributory factor to lung cancer. 吸烟是导致肺癌的一个因素。 | causative, influential, instrumental | irrelevant, unrelated | adjective
