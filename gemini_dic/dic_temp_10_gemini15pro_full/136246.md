nonrepatriation:/ˌnɒnriːˌpeɪtriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 不遣返，不汇回 | The nonrepatriation of profits earned overseas can be a complex tax issue.  在海外赚取的利润不汇回可能是一个复杂的税务问题。|  The policy of nonrepatriation of refugees has been criticized by human rights groups.  不遣返难民的政策受到了人权组织的批评。 | 近义词：non-return, retention | 反义词: repatriation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
repatriation: from re- "back, again" + patria "native land" (from Latin) + -ation (suffix forming nouns of action)
