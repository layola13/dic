insurrection:/ˌɪnsəˈrekʃən/ | n. |起义，暴动，叛乱 | An act of rising in active revolt against an established authority or government.  |  The insurrection was quickly quelled by the government. 政府迅速平息了叛乱。 | 近义词: rebellion, revolt, uprising | 反义词: obedience, submission | 词性: 名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin insurrectio(n-), from the verb insurgere ‘rise up’.
