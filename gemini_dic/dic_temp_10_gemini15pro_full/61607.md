emetine: /ˈɛmɪtiːn/ | n. | 吐根碱 |  A crystalline alkaloid, C₂₉H₄₀N₂O₄, obtained from ipecac root, used chiefly as an emetic and expectorant, and in the treatment of amebiasis. | Emetine is effective against certain parasites. 吐根碱对某些寄生虫有效。| Emetine can cause serious side effects. 吐根碱会引起严重的副作用。 | synonyms: ipecacuanha, cephaeline | antonyms: antiemetic | noun
