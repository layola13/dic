own-label:/ˌəʊn ˈleɪbl/ | adj. n. | 自有品牌，自产品牌 | A product that is sold by a retailer under its own name rather than the name of the manufacturer. |  Tesco's own-label products are often cheaper than branded goods.  特易购的自有品牌产品通常比品牌商品便宜。|synonyms: private label, store brand | antonyms: name brand, manufacturer's brand | adjective, noun

own-label:/ˌəʊn ˈleɪbl/ | n. | 自有品牌，自产品牌 | A product sold under a retailer's own brand. | The supermarket sells a range of own-label wines. 这家超市出售一系列自有品牌的葡萄酒。|synonyms: private label, store brand | antonyms: name brand, manufacturer's brand | noun
