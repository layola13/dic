noncuriously:/ˌnɒnˈkjʊəriəsli/
adverb
不感兴趣地，漠不关心地
without curiosity; in a way that shows no interest
He noncuriously glanced at the newspaper headlines. 他漠不关心地瞥了一眼报纸的标题。
Synonyms: indifferently, apathetically, listlessly
Antonyms: curiously, inquisitively, interestedly
词性: 副词
