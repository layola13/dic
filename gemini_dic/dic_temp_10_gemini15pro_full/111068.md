listens:/ˈlɪsənz/| v.|倾听，仔细听|He listens attentively to the music. 他专心致志地听音乐。|近义词：hears, attends, pays attention|反义词:ignores, disregards|verb (third person singular simple present)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *listnen, from Old English *hlysnan, from Proto-Germanic *hlusnōną (“to listen”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear”). Cognate with Scots listen (“to listen”), West Frisian lústerje (“to listen”), Dutch luisteren (“to listen”), German lauschen (“to listen, eavesdrop”), Danish lytte (“to listen”), Swedish lyssna (“to listen”), Icelandic hlusta (“to listen”).
