disembay:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbeɪ/
verb
使（船）驶出海湾; 出海湾
to sail out of a bay; to leave a bay
The ship disembayed at dawn. 这艘船在黎明时驶出了海湾。
近义词:emerge, exit, depart
反义词:embark, enter, dock
词性:v.
