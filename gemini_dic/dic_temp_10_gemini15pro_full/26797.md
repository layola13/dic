caffeine:/ˈkæfiːn/ /kæˈfiːn/| n.|咖啡因；咖啡碱|A crystalline compound that is found especially in tea and coffee plants and is a stimulant of the central nervous system.|I try to avoid caffeine after lunch. 我午饭后尽量避免摄入咖啡因。|Synonyms: stimulant, analeptic|Antonyms: depressant, sedative|noun
