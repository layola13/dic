beam:/biːm/| n. v.| 光束，梁，笑容; 发光，播送 |A ray of light; a long piece of strong wood, metal, etc. used in building; a wide smile. To send out rays of light; to smile broadly. |The sunlight beamed through the window. 阳光透过窗户照射进来。|近义词：ray, girder, smile, shine|反义词：frown, darkness|名词, 动词
