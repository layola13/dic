chronostratigraphical: /ˌkrɒnəʊˌstrætɪˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 年代地层学的 | Relating to chronostratigraphy, the branch of geology concerned with the relative ages of rock strata and their correlation in time. |  The chronostratigraphical classification of these rocks is complex due to the frequent changes in sea level. 这些岩石的年代地层分类很复杂，因为海平面经常变化。 | 近义词: geochronologic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "diachronic" which relates to change through time rather than relative age of strata) | 词性: adjective
