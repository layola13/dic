consumptions:/kənˈsʌmpʃənz/
n.
消费; 消耗; 消耗量; 消费量
the act of consuming something, or the amount of something that is consumed.
The consumption of alcohol is prohibited on these premises.  这些场所禁止饮酒。
consumption:/kənˈsʌmpʃən/
n.
肺痨; 痨病(尤指肺结核)
(old-fashioned) tuberculosis, especially of the lungs.
He died of consumption. 他死于肺结核。


近义词: expenditure, use, utilization, intake, ingestion
反义词: production, creation
