capital-intensive: /ˈkæpɪtlɪnˌtɛnsɪv/
adjective
资本密集型的
Requiring a large amount of capital investment relative to labor input.  An industry or business is capital intensive if it requires large amounts of money to produce goods or services.
Manufacturing semiconductors is a capital-intensive industry.  制造半导体是一个资本密集型产业。
The airline industry is notoriously capital-intensive. 航空业是出了名的资本密集型产业。
Synonyms: capital-heavy, high-capital
Antonyms: labor-intensive, low-capital
词性: adjective
