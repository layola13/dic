cherub:/ˈtʃɛrəb/ (US), /ˈtʃɛrʌb/ (UK)| n.|小天使，胖娃娃|A beautiful, innocent, and often winged child in art, traditionally one of the second order of angels.  A sweet, innocent child.|The painting depicted a cherub playing a harp. 这幅画描绘了一个小天使在弹奏竖琴。|Synonyms: angel, seraph, putto|Antonyms: devil, demon|Noun


cherubim:/ˈtʃɛrəbɪm/ (US), /ˈtʃɛrʊbɪm/ (UK)| n.|小天使（复数）|The plural form of cherub.  It refers to multiple cherubs.|The ceiling was adorned with cherubim. 天花板上装饰着许多小天使。|Synonyms: angels, seraphs|Antonyms: devils, demons|Noun
