avaricious:/ˌævəˈrɪʃəs/| adj.|贪婪的，贪财的|Excessively greedy; insatiable in the pursuit of wealth or gain.|He was an avaricious man, always wanting more money.|近义词：greedy, covetous, rapacious, grasping|反义词：generous, altruistic, philanthropic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *avarus* ("greedy"), from *avere* ("to crave, desire eagerly").
