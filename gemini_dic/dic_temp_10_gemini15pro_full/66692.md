obsolete:/ˌɒbsəˈliːt/ | adj. | 过时的，废弃的 | No longer in use; out of date. | Example:  This type of computer is now obsolete. 这种类型的电脑现在已经过时了。

Example: The use of cassette tapes has become obsolete. 使用盒式磁带已经过时了。 | 近义词: outdated, archaic, antiquated, outmoded, defunct | 反义词: modern, current, contemporary, state-of-the-art | 词性: adjective
