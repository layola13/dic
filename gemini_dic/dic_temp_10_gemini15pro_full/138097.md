normativism:/ˈnɔːrmətɪvɪzəm/
n.
规范主义
The doctrine that norms or standards are fundamental to the theory and practice of a particular subject.
Normativism in ethics emphasizes the importance of moral rules and principles. 规范主义在伦理学中强调道德规则和原则的重要性。
近义词: prescriptivism, regulism
反义词: descriptivism
词性: noun
