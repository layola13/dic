词源: From clod + -y.  From Middle English *clodde, from Old English *clod-, *clud-, from Proto-Germanic *kludō (“lump, clod”). Cognate with West Frisian klodd (“clod”), Dutch klod (“clod”), German Klotz (“block, log, lump”).
