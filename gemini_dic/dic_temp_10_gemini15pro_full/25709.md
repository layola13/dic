bunniest:/ˈbʌniɪst/| adj.|最像兔子的，最可爱的|Superlative adjective of bunny.  Means most resembling a bunny, most cute like a bunny, or having the most qualities associated with bunnies.|She brought the bunniest stuffed animal I’ve ever seen. 她带来了我见过的最像兔子的毛绒玩具。|近义词：cutest, sweetest, most adorable|反义词:ugliest, least appealing|词性:adjective
