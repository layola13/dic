oestrus:/ˈiːstrəs/ (US) /ˈiːstrəs/ (UK)| n. |发情期，动情期|A recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility in many female mammals; heat.|The lioness was in oestrus and attracting the attention of all the males.  母狮正处于发情期，吸引了所有雄狮的注意。|Synonyms: heat, rut, season|Antonyms: anoestrus|noun

oestrous:/ˈiːstrəs/ (US) /ˈiːstrəs/ (UK)| adj. |发情的|Relating to or characteristic of oestrus.|The oestrous cycle of a dog is typically six months. 狗的发情周期通常是六个月。|Synonyms:  in heat|Antonyms: non-oestrous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek οἶστρος (oîstros, “gadfly, frenzy, passion”).  The connection is due to the frenzied behavior of some animals during this period, likened to being stung by a gadfly.
