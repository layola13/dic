coachworks: /ˈkəʊtʃwɜːks/ (UK) /ˈkoʊtʃwɜːrks/ (US)
noun
车身制造厂；马车制造厂；（汽车）车身修理厂
The business of building or repairing coach or car bodies.
The coachworks is known for its high-quality craftsmanship. 这家车身制造厂以其高品质的工艺而闻名。
The vintage car was sent to the coachworks for restoration. 这辆老爷车被送到车身修理厂进行修复。
Synonyms: body shop, carriage works, car body repair shop
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
