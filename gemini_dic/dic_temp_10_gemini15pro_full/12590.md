assagai:/ˈæsəɡaɪ/| n.|轻矛，投枪|A slender, light spear, usually thrown, used by various peoples of southern and eastern Africa.|The warrior hurled the assagai with deadly accuracy.  战士以惊人的准确度投掷了轻矛。|近义词：spear, javelin|反义词:shield, armor|词性:noun
Etymology: From the Zulu word *isiAgayi*, from *isi-* (thing used for) + *-Agayi* (to stab, pierce).
