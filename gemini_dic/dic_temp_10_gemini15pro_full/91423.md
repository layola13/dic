hyetograph:/ˈhaɪɪtəɡræf/ | n. | 雨量图；雨量记录图 | A graphical representation of rainfall intensity over time.  It shows variations in rainfall rate during a storm or over a longer period. | The hyetograph clearly showed the peak rainfall intensity during the hurricane. 雨量图清楚地显示了飓风期间的峰值降雨强度。

The hyetograph helped the engineers design the drainage system. 雨量图帮助工程师设计排水系统。 | synonym: rainfall chart, pluviograph | antonym: N/A | noun
