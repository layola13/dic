overservileness:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvɪlnəs/| n. | 过分殷勤，奴颜婢膝 | Excessive eagerness to please or serve others, often to an excessive or fawning degree. | His overservileness made his colleagues uncomfortable. 他的过分殷勤让他的同事们感到不舒服。|  The waiter's overservileness bordered on obsequiousness. 服务员过分的殷勤近乎谄媚。| synonyms: obsequiousness, servility, fawning, sycophancy | antonyms: independence, assertiveness, self-respect, dignity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + servile + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Servile" comes from the Latin word "servilis," meaning "of a slave." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives.
