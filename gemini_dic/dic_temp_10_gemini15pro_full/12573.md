aspirin:/ˈæsprɪn/
n.
阿司匹林（单数）
A common pain reliever and fever reducer.  It is also used to prevent heart attacks and strokes.
The doctor recommended taking an aspirin daily. 医生建议每天服用一片阿司匹林。
近义词:painkiller, analgesic
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

词源: From the trade name Aspirin®, originally a trademark of Bayer, from acetyl + spir[saüre] (German, 'salicylic acid', from Latin spir[aea] 'meadowsweet' (the plant from which salicylic acid was originally derived)) + -in (chemical suffix).
