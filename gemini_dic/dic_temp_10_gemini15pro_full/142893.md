out-of-town:/ˌaʊt əv ˈtaʊn/
adjective
外地的；城外的
Located away from the city or town in which one lives or works.
We have some out-of-town guests staying with us this weekend.  我们这周末有一些外地客人来住。
They made an out-of-town trip to visit their family. 他们到外地探亲去了。
近义词:nonresident, non-local
反义词:local, in-town
词性:adjective
