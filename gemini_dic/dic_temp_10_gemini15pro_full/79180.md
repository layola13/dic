glamouring:/ˈɡlæmərɪŋ/ /ˈɡlæmərɪŋ/ | v. | 使着迷，使陶醉 | Filling someone or something with glamour; making something seem enchanting or fascinating. |  She was glamouring the audience with her beauty and talent. 她用她的美貌和才华使观众着迷。

The photographer was glamouring the model with soft lighting and flattering poses. 摄影师用柔和的光线和讨人喜欢的姿势为模特增添魅力。 | 近义词: enchanting, captivating, fascinating, mesmerizing, beguiling | 反义词: disenchanting, repelling, boring, dull | 词性: 动词 (present participle of glamour)
