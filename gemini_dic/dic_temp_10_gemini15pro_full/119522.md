microrecord:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌrɛkərd/ | n. | 微记录;缩微记录 | A photographic copy of printed material or a document reduced in size for storage or security purposes. | Libraries often keep microrecords of old newspapers. 图书馆经常保存旧报纸的缩微记录。| synonyms: microfilm, microfiche, microphotograph | antonyms: enlargement, macrorecord | noun

microrecord:/ˈmaɪkroʊrɪˈkɔːrd/ | v. |  缩微记录;微录 | To make a microrecord of something. |  The company microrecords all its important documents.  这家公司将其所有重要文件都进行了缩微记录。| synonyms: microfilm, microfiche | antonyms: enlarge | verb
