nonsculptured:/ˌnɒnˈskʌlptʃəd/ /ˌnɑːnˈskʌlptʃərd/
adjective
非雕刻的，未雕刻的
Not sculpted; not shaped by carving or molding.
The nonsculptured rock face contrasted sharply with the ornate carvings on the temple walls.  这块未经雕琢的岩石表面与寺庙墙壁上华丽的雕刻形成鲜明对比。
The artist preferred the nonsculptured beauty of natural driftwood. 这位艺术家更喜欢天然浮木未经雕琢的美感。
Synonyms: natural, uncarved, unshaped, plain
Antonyms: sculpted, carved, shaped, ornate
词性: 形容词
