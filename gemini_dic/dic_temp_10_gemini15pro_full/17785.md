bedphere: /ˈbedsfɪər/
noun
床圈; 床垫的支撑框架
A framework, typically made of wood or metal, that supports a mattress and forms the base of a bed.
The bedphere creaked under his weight.  床圈在他的重量下嘎吱作响。
The old bedphere collapsed.  旧床圈塌了。
synonyms: bed frame, bedstead
antonyms: N/A

ETYMOLOGY->
"Bed" comes from Old English "bedd," meaning "a place to sleep." "Sphere" comes from Old French "espere" and Latin "sphaera," meaning "ball" or "globe."  "Bedsphere" is a relatively recent coinage, likely combining the concepts of a bed and its encompassing space.
