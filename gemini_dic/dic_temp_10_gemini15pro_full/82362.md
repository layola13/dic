grown-up:/ˈɡroʊnˌʌp/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊnˌʌp/ (UK)| n. adj.|成年人，成年的|An adult; a person who is fully grown and mature.  A child can be described as grown-up if they act in a mature way. |例句：She's a grown-up now and can make her own decisions. 她现在是成年人了，可以自己做决定了。

例句：He acted very grown-up for his age.  就他的年龄而言，他表现得很成熟。

近义词：adult, mature, of age|反义词:child, childish, immature|词性:名词, 形容词
