parochial:/pəˈroʊkiəl/
adjective
狭隘的；地方性的；教区的
Relating to a church parish; having a limited or narrow outlook or scope.
The school was known for its parochial views, rarely venturing beyond local issues in its curriculum. 这所学校以其狭隘的视野而闻名，在其课程中很少涉及地方问题以外的内容。
His parochial attitude prevented him from appreciating other cultures. 他狭隘的态度使他无法欣赏其他文化。
synonyms: narrow-minded, provincial, insular, local, limited
antonyms: broad-minded, cosmopolitan, universal, global


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *parochialis, from Latin parochia "parish," from Greek paroikia "sojourning abroad," from paroikos "dwelling beside, neighbor," from para- "beside" + oikos "house."
