hoofbound:/ˈhuːfˌbaʊnd/ | adj. |蹄叶炎的，蹄窄的|A condition in horses and other hoofed animals in which the hoof becomes constricted, often causing lameness.|The farrier diagnosed the horse as hoofbound.|Synonyms: contracted heels, contracted hooves|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but a healthy hoof is the opposite condition)|Adjective
