ageists:/ˈeɪdʒɪsts/
noun
年龄歧视者
People who discriminate against others based on their age.
Many ageists believe that older people are less capable than younger people.
许多年龄歧视者认为老年人的能力不如年轻人。
These policies are ageists and discriminate against older workers.
这些政策带有年龄歧视，歧视老年工人。
近义词:discriminators
反义词:egalitarians
