breathabilities:/ˌbriːðəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |透气性，透气度|The breathability of a fabric is its ability to allow air to pass through it.  一种织物的透气性是指它允许空气通过的能力。|例句：The breathabilities of different fabrics vary greatly. 不同织物的透气性差异很大。

例句：High breathabilities are important for athletic wear.  高透气性对于运动服很重要。|近义词：porosity, permeability, ventilation|反义词：impermeability, airtightness|词性:名词
