hype:/haɪp/| n. vt.|炒作；大肆宣传；兴奋剂；骗局|Intense or exaggerated publicity, often used to promote a product or idea.  Can also refer to something that is overpromoted and doesn't live up to expectations, or a deception.|The hype surrounding the new phone was insane.  这款新手机的宣传铺天盖地。|synonyms: promotion, publicity, ballyhoo, exaggeration, buildup|antonyms: understatement, downplaying|noun

hype:/haɪp/| vt.|大肆宣传；使兴奋；骗|To promote or publicize excessively.|The company hyped the new product as revolutionary. 这家公司将新产品吹捧成革命性的。|synonyms: promote, publicize, boost, plug|antonyms: downplay, understate|verb
