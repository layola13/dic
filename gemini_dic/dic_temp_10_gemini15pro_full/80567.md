goody-two-shoes:/ˌɡʊdi ˈtuː ʃuːz/
noun|伪善者，假正经|A person who is affectedly good, virtuous, or religious.|She's such a goody-two-shoes, always telling everyone else what to do.|近义词:prig, sanctimonious person|反义词:sinner, reprobate|n.

goody-two-shoes:/ˌɡʊdi ˈtuː ʃuːz/
adjective|伪善的，假正经的|Affectedly good, virtuous, or religious.|He gave me a goody-two-shoes lecture about the dangers of drinking.|近义词:priggish, sanctimonious|反义词:wicked, sinful|adj.

词源: 源自18世纪的童话故事 *The History of Little Goody Two-Shoes*, 故事中的主人公是个贫穷但品德高尚的小女孩，总是只穿着一只鞋，后来得到了一双鞋，非常珍惜。这个词逐渐演变成贬义，用来形容那些过分强调自己美德的人。
