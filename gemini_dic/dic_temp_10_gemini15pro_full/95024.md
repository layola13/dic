inaccessible:/ˌɪnækˈsesəbl/ | adj. | 难以接近的，难以达到的；难以理解的 | Not able to be reached or easily obtained; difficult to understand or appreciate. |
The remote mountain peak was inaccessible to hikers.  那座偏远的山峰对于徒步旅行者来说是难以到达的。

The complex scientific theory was inaccessible to the average reader.  这个复杂的科学理论对于普通读者来说难以理解。

近义词: unreachable, unattainable, unapproachable, impassable | 反义词: accessible, reachable, attainable | 词性:形容词
