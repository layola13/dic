damsel:/ˈdæmzəl/ (US), /ˈdæmzəl/ (UK)| n.| 少女，闺女；(旧)未婚女子|A young unmarried woman, especially one of noble birth.  Often used in a literary or archaic context.|例句：The knight rescued the damsel from the dragon. 骑士从龙那里救出了少女。

例句：The damsel in distress waited for her hero.  遇难的少女等待着她的英雄。|近义词：maiden, girl, lass|反义词: matron, crone |词性:noun
