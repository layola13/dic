barajos:/baˈɾaxos/| n.|一副牌，纸牌（尤指西班牙纸牌）|A deck of cards, especially Spanish playing cards.|He shuffled the barajos expertly. 他熟练地洗牌。|近义词：deck of cards, cards|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology: From Spanish "barajas". Ultimately from Arabic  "waraqa" (ورقة), meaning "leaf," "paper," or "sheet."  This refers to the material cards are made from.
