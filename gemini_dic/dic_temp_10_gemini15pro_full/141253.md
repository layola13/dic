opah:/ˈoʊpə/ | n. | 月鱼，翻车鱼|A large, colorful, deep-sea fish found in tropical and temperate waters.  It is notable for its ability to maintain a warm body temperature. |  The fishermen were surprised to catch a massive opah. 渔民们惊讶地捕获了一条巨大的月鱼。

The opah is a unique fish because it can regulate its body temperature. 月鱼是一种独特的鱼，因为它可以调节体温。 | 近义词: moonfish, kingfish (in some regions) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hawaiian *ʻōpā*, of unknown ultimate origin.
