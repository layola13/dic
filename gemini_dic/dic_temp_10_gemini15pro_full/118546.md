mesomyodous:/ˌmezoʊˈmaɪədəs/| adj.|中肌型的|Having a moderate amount of muscle; between myodous (well-muscled) and amyodous (lacking muscle).|Example 1: The cheetah's mesomyodous physique contributes to its speed and agility. 猎豹中等的肌肉量有助于它的速度和敏捷性。

Example 2: Compared to the powerfully built gorilla, the chimpanzee has a more mesomyodous build. 与体格强壮的大猩猩相比，黑猩猩的肌肉量适中。|近义词:moderately muscled|反义词:amyodous, myodous|词性:形容词
