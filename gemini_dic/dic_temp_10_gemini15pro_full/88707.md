hobblingly:/ˈhɒblɪŋli/ (US) /ˈhɒblɪŋli/ (UK)| adv.|一瘸一拐地，蹒跚地|In a hobbling manner; with a limp.  It describes the way someone moves when they are injured or have difficulty walking.|He walked hobblingly towards the finish line after twisting his ankle. 他扭伤了脚踝，一瘸一拐地走向终点线。|Synonyms: haltingly, limpingly, lamely|Antonyms: smoothly, gracefully, easily|adverb
