folioed:/ˈfoʊlioʊd/
adjective
页码编号的，有页码的
Numbered with folios; having folios.
The manuscript was carefully folioed and bound.  这份手稿被仔细地标上页码并装订好了。
numbered, paginated
unnumbered, unpaginated
词性: 形容词
