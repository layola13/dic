connexive: /kəˈnɛksɪv/ | adj. | 连接的；关联的；连结的 |  Relating to or characterized by connection or logical implication.  A connexive logic is a system of logic that studies the principles of connection between propositions. | Connexive logic rejects certain classical inferences like "if not P, then (P implies Q)". 如果非P，则（P蕴含Q）| 近义词:  connected, related, associated, linked | 反义词: disconnected, unrelated, separate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *connexīvus, from connex-, past participle stem of connectere  "to connect,"  com- "together" + nectere "to bind."
