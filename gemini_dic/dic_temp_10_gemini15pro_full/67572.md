extrapolator:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtər/ | n. | 外推器；外推法使用者；外推者 |  Someone or something that extrapolates (estimates or concludes something by assuming that existing trends will continue or a current method will remain applicable). | The extrapolator predicted a significant increase in sales based on the current growth rate.  外推者根据目前的增长率预测销售额将大幅增长。| The demographer used complex software as an extrapolator to estimate future population growth. 人口统计学家使用复杂的软件作为外推器来估计未来的人口增长。 |  estimator, predictor, forecaster | interpolator | noun
