conniving:/kəˈnaɪvɪŋ/
adjective
诡计多端的，阴险的
Given to or involved in conspiring to do something immoral, illegal, or harmful.
She’s a conniving, manipulative woman. 她是一个诡计多端，爱操纵别人的女人。
近义词:scheming, devious, cunning, crafty, wily
反义词:honest, forthright, ingenuous
词性:adjective

connive:/kəˈnaɪv/
verb
密谋，纵容
Secretly allow (something considered immoral, illegal, or harmful) to occur.
The government connived with the security forces to permit the killings. 政府与安全部队串通一气，允许杀戮发生。
近义词:conspire, collude, intrigue
反义词:oppose, obstruct
词性:verb
