murrey:/ˈmɜːri/ | adj. | 紫红色的，桑葚色的 | Of a dark reddish-purple colour. | The queen wore a murrey gown, richly embroidered with gold thread. 女王穿着一件紫红色的长袍，上面绣着丰富的金线。 |  The curtains were a deep, rich murrey. 窗帘是深沉而浓郁的紫红色。 | 近义词: mulberry, maroon, purplish-red, wine-red, reddish-purple | 反义词: pale, light | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old French *moré, from Vulgar Latin *moraceus (“mulberry-coloured”), from Latin morus (“mulberry tree”).
