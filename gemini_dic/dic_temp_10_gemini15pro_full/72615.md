flyblown:/ˈflaɪˌbloʊn/
adjective
(肉)生蛆的；腐败的；污秽的
Covered with or infested with flyblows; tainted or spoiled.  Also figuratively, meaning corrupt or degraded.
The meat was flyblown and had to be thrown away. 肉生蛆了，不得不扔掉。
The old house had a flyblown, neglected air about it. 这栋老房子有一种腐败破落的感觉。
近义词: putrid, rotten, tainted, decayed, corrupt
反义词: fresh, clean, pure, pristine
词性: adjective

flyblown:/ˈflaɪˌbloʊn/
adjective
(比喻)堕落的；腐败的；败坏的
Figuratively, morally or socially corrupt or degraded.
The flyblown reputation of the politician ended his career.  这位政客声名狼藉，断送了他的职业生涯。
He described the organization as a flyblown institution riddled with corruption. 他将该组织描述为一个腐败堕落的机构，充满了腐败。
近义词:  degraded, corrupt, debauched, depraved, degenerate
反义词:  moral, upright, virtuous, honorable, respectable
词性: adjective


词源:  From "fly" + "blown,"  past participle of "blow" (in the sense of flies depositing eggs).  Essentially meaning "blown upon by flies," leading to the presence of maggots.
