goarish:/ˈɡɔːrɪʃ/| adj.|俗气的，粗俗的，低级的|Lacking in refinement or good taste; vulgar.  Derived from "goat," suggesting a coarse or animalistic nature.|Her goarish behavior embarrassed everyone at the party. 她的粗俗行为让派对上的每个人都感到尴尬。|近义词：vulgar, tasteless, crass, coarse, uncouth|反义词：refined, sophisticated, elegant, tasteful|词性:形容词

词源:  From "goat" + "-ish."  The association with goats likely stems from their perceived stubbornness, smelliness, and general lack of refinement.
