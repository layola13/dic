elater:/ˈɪleɪtər/| n.|弹器；叩头虫；|A device, typically a spring-loaded lever, for clicking or snapping something open or shut. Also, a type of beetle known for its ability to right itself by clicking its body.|The elater on the lighter broke, so I can't use it anymore. 打火机上的弹簧坏了，所以我不能用了。|近义词：click beetle, spring, snapper|反义词:|noun
