coloratura:/ˌkʌlərəˈtʊrə/ (US) /ˌkʌləʊrəˈtʊərə/ (UK)
noun
花腔；炫技的唱法；花腔女高音
Elaborate ornamentation of a vocal melody, especially in operatic singing, or a soprano specializing in such music.
The soprano delivered a dazzling coloratura performance. 女高音演唱了一段令人眼花缭乱的花腔。
She is a renowned coloratura soprano. 她是一位著名的花腔女高音。
synonyms: ornamentation, embellishment, flourish, trill, warble
antonyms: simplicity, plainness
