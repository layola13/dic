bisects:/baɪˈsekts/ /bɪˈsekts/
verb
平分；二等分
divides something into two equal parts.

The line bisects the angle.
这条线平分这个角。

The road bisects the town.
这条路把城镇一分为二。

近义词: divide, halve, split
反义词: join, unite
词性: verb

词源: From Latin bisect-, past participle stem of bisecare "to cut in two," from bi- "two" + secare "to cut"
