cotransports:/ˌkoʊˈtrænsˌpɔːrts/
|n.|协同转运；共转运|The process in which two or more different molecules or ions are transported across a cell membrane simultaneously by a common carrier protein.|Sodium-glucose cotransport is an example of secondary active transport. 钠-葡萄糖协同转运是继发性主动转运的一个例子。|cotransport, symport|antiport|noun


cotransport:/ˌkoʊˈtrænsˌpɔːrt/
|v.|协同转运；共转运|To transport two or more different molecules or ions across a cell membrane simultaneously by a common carrier protein.|The protein cotransports sodium and glucose into the cell.  这种蛋白质将钠和葡萄糖协同转运到细胞内。|transport together, symport|antiport|verb


Etymology: co- (together) + transport (to carry across)
