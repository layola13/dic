dysgenesis:/dɪsˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
发育不全，不良发育
Defective or abnormal development of an organ or part, especially of the gonads.
Gonadal dysgenesis can result in ambiguous genitalia. 性腺发育不全会导致生殖器不明确。
malformation, agenesis, hypoplasia
normal development, euplasia
noun
