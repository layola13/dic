changeover:/ˈtʃeɪndʒˌoʊvər/
n.|转换，交接，转变|The changeover to the new system was complex.  向新系统的转换很复杂。|Example:  The changeover from summer to winter brings colder temperatures. 从夏季到冬季的转变带来了更低的温度。|Synonyms: transition, switch, shift|Antonyms: continuation, stagnation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "change" + "over." "Change" comes from the Old French "changier" (to change), ultimately from the Latin "cambiare" (to barter, exchange). "Over" comes from Old English "ofer" (above, across).
