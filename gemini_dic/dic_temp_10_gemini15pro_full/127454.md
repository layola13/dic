nebulizer:/ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪzər/ | n. |喷雾器，雾化器 |A device that turns liquid medicine into a fine mist that can be inhaled. | The doctor prescribed a nebulizer for her asthma. 医生给她开了个喷雾器治疗哮喘。|Synonyms: atomizer, vaporizer |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun

nebulise:/ˈnɛbjʊˌlaɪz/ | v. |喷雾，雾化 |To convert (a liquid) into a fine spray or mist, especially for medicinal purposes. |  She nebulizes her medication twice a day. 她每天两次雾化她的药物。|Synonyms: atomize, vaporize |Antonyms: condense | verb
