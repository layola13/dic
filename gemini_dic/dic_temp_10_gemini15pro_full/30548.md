cephalopods:/ˈsefələpɒdz/ (US) /ˈsefələpɒds/ (UK)| n. |头足类动物|Marine mollusks characterized by bilateral body symmetry, a prominent head, and a set of arms or tentacles modified from the primitive molluscan foot.  They include octopuses, squid, cuttlefish, and nautiluses.|例句：Cephalopods are highly intelligent invertebrates. 头足类动物是高智商的无脊椎动物。

例句：Octopuses, squid, and cuttlefish are all cephalopods. 章鱼、鱿鱼和墨鱼都是头足类动物。|近义词：cephalopoda|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
