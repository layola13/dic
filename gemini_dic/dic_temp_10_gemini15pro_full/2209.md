addling:/ˈæd(ə)lɪŋ/
v.
使腐坏；使混乱；使糊涂
confusing or muddling.  Causing eggs to go bad, especially by incubation.
The extreme heat addled his brain. 酷热使他头脑昏沉。
The hot weather addled the eggs. 炎热的天气使鸡蛋变质了。

近义词:confusing, muddling, rotting, spoiling
反义词:clarifying, organizing, preserving
词性:动词
