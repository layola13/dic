indecency:/ɪnˈdiːsnsi/ | n. |猥亵；下流；不礼貌|The act or quality of being indecent; behavior or language that offends against accepted standards of morality or decency. |The comedian was fired for his act of indecency on stage. 这位喜剧演员因为在舞台上的不雅行为被解雇了。

The film was banned for its indecency. 这部电影因为内容下流而被禁播。| 近义词: impropriety, immodesty, obscenity | 反义词: decency, propriety, modesty | 词性: noun

Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French indecence, from Latin indecent- ‘unbecoming’, from the verb decere ‘be fitting’.
