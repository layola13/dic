donorships:/ˈdoʊnərʃɪps/
n.
捐赠; 捐赠者身份; 捐赠者地位
The act of donating something, or the status of being a donor.
Many universities rely on alumni donorships to fund scholarships. 许多大学依靠校友捐赠来资助奖学金。
The hospital gratefully accepted the donorships of new medical equipment. 医院感激地接受了新的医疗设备的捐赠。
近义词:donations, contributions, gifts
反义词:receipts
