credibilities: /ˌkrɛdəˈbɪlətiz/
| n. |可信度，可靠性|The quality of being believable or trustworthy.  The multiple sources of information enhanced the credibilities of the report.|例句：The witness's inconsistent statements damaged his credibility. 证人的前后矛盾的陈述损害了他的可信度。

例句：The multiple sources of information enhanced the credibilities of the report. 多个信息来源增强了报告的可信度。
|近义词：believability, plausibility, trustworthiness|反义词：unbelievability, implausibility, doubtfulness|词性:名词
