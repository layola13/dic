north-western:/ˌnɔːθˈwɛstən/ | adj.|西北的；来自西北的；朝向西北的|Situated in or directed toward the northwest; coming from the northwest.|例句：The north-western wind was cold and biting. 西北风又冷又刺骨。|近义词：northerly, boreal|反义词：south-eastern|词性:形容词

north-western:/ˌnɔːθˈwɛstən/ | n.|西北部地区；西北人|The northwestern part of a region; a person from the northwest.|例句：The north-western is known for its stunning scenery. 西北部以其迷人的风景而闻名。|近义词：northwesterner|反义词：south-easterner|词性:名词
