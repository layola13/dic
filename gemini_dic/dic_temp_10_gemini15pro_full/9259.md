antipatriot:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriət/ /ˌæntiˈpætʃriət/ | n. |反爱国者，不爱国的人| A person who does not love or support their own country; one who opposes patriotism. |  He was branded an antipatriot for speaking out against the war.  他因为公开反对战争而被打成不爱国者。

|Example 1:  Her antipatriot views were not well received by the veterans.  她的反爱国观点没有受到退伍军人的欢迎。

|Example 2: The antipatriot protested the national anthem.  这个不爱国的人抗议国歌。

|近义词：traitor, dissident, rebel, defector |反义词: patriot, nationalist |词性: noun
