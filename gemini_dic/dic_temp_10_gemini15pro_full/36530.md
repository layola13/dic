collaborates:/kəˈlæbəˌreɪts/
verb
合作，协作
works together with others on a joint project
The singer collaborates with several other musicians on her new album.  这位歌手与其他几位音乐家合作制作她的新专辑。
cooperate, partner, team up
compete, oppose
