arch-rival:/ˈɑːrtʃ ˈraɪvl/| n.| 主要竞争对手，劲敌 |A person or organization that is the main or strongest rival of another.|  Example: The two teams have been arch-rivals for years, their matches always fiercely contested. 这两支球队多年来一直是劲敌，他们的比赛总是竞争激烈。

Example:  Coca-Cola and Pepsi are arch-rivals in the soft drink industry. 可口可乐和百事可乐是软饮料行业的主要竞争对手。| 近义词: nemesis, main rival, chief competitor, principal opponent | 反义词: ally, friend, partner | 词性: noun | 词源: arch- (chief) + rival (competitor) - from Old French rival, from Latin rivalis "of a brook, using the same stream," hence, "a competitor"
