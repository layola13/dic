millerian:/mɪˈlɪəriən/
adjective
米勒管的，缪勒管的
Relating to the Müllerian ducts, embryonic structures that develop into the female reproductive tract in mammals.
The millerian ducts develop into the fallopian tubes, uterus, and upper part of the vagina. 米勒管会发育成为输卵管、子宫和阴道上部。
The physician explained the development of the millerian structures in the female embryo. 医生解释了女性胚胎中米勒结构的发育。
Synonyms: Müllerian
Antonyms: Wolffian
