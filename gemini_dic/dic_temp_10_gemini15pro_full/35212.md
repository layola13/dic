cluckiest:/ˈklʌkiɪst/| adj.|最爱抱窝的，最母鸡似的|Most inclined to cluck, like a broody hen.  It often implies excessive fussiness or overprotectiveness.|例句：She's the cluckiest mother I've ever met, constantly hovering over her children. 她是我见过的最爱抱窝的母亲，总是围着她的孩子们转。

例句：Of all the hens, Henrietta was the cluckiest, always eager to sit on her eggs. 在所有的母鸡中，亨丽埃塔是最爱抱窝的，总是急于坐在它的蛋上。|近义词：broodiest, fussiest, overprotective|反义词:inattentive, neglectful|词性:adjective
