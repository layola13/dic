centifidous: /ˌsɛntɪˈfaɪdəs/
adjective
多足的；百足的
Having many feet or legs; having a hundred feet.
Centifidous millipedes wriggled through the leaf litter. 多足的千足虫在落叶中蠕动。
Near Synonyms: multiped, polypod
Antonyms: apodal, footless
词性: adjective
