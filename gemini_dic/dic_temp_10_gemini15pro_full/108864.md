leches:/ˈletʃɪz/| interj.|哎呀；我的天；|Expressing mild surprise, annoyance, or disappointment.  It's a Spanish interjection often used in English, especially in the Southwest US.|例句：¡Ay, leches! I forgot my keys. 哎呀，我的天！我忘记带钥匙了。|近义词：darn, shoot, geez|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection expressing surprise/annoyance)|interjection
