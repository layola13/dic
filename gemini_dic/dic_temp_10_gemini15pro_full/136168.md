nonrehabilitation:/ˌnɒnˌriːəˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃn/
n.
non-rehabilitation, lack of rehabilitation
Absence or failure of rehabilitation; the state of not being rehabilitated.
The nonrehabilitation of the injured athlete led to the end of his career.  受伤运动员未能康复导致了他的职业生涯的结束。

The prison's focus on punishment rather than nonrehabilitation has led to high recidivism rates. 这所监狱注重惩罚而不是康复，导致了高累犯率。
lack of rehabilitation, failure to rehabilitate
rehabilitation, restoration
noun
