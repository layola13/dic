palmatilobed:/ˌpælməˈtɪloʊbd/| adj.|掌状浅裂的|Having lobes that resemble a hand with spread fingers; shaped like a hand with the fingers spread open.|The leaves of the fig tree are palmatilobed.  无花果树的叶子是掌状浅裂的。|The maple leaf is palmatilobed. 枫叶是掌状浅裂的。|近义词: palmate, lobed|反义词: entire, unlobed|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *palmatus* "shaped like a hand," from *palma* "palm of the hand" + *-lobed*, from *lobe* "a rounded projection or division."
