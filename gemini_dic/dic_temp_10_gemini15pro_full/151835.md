passible:/ˈpæsɪbl/| adj.| 易受影响的，可遭受的|Susceptible to something, capable of being affected or acted upon, especially by something unpleasant.| The fortress was passible to attack from the north.  这个堡垒容易从北面受到攻击。|例句：He was in a passible mood, easily swayed by others' opinions. 他情绪容易受影响，很容易被别人的意见左右。

例句：The city is passible to flooding during heavy rains.  这座城市在大雨期间容易发生洪水。|近义词：susceptible, vulnerable, liable, open, subject|反义词：impervious, resistant, immune|词性:形容词
