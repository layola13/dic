megapixel:/ˈmɛɡəˌpɪksəl/| n. | 百万像素 | A unit of graphic resolution equal to one million pixels.  A pixel is the smallest single component of a digital image. | This camera boasts a 24-megapixel sensor. 这款相机拥有一个2400万像素的传感器。| synonym: resolution | antonym: N/A | noun
