ctenophora:/tɪˈnɒfərə/
| n. |栉水母动物门|A phylum of marine invertebrates, commonly known as comb jellies, characterized by eight rows of cilia they use for locomotion.|Ctenophora are beautiful, bioluminescent creatures found in oceans worldwide. 栉水母是在世界海洋中发现的美丽的生物发光生物。|近义词:comb jellies|反义词:None|词性:noun

ctenophore:/ˈtɛnəˌfɔːr/
| n. |栉水母|An individual organism belonging to the phylum Ctenophora.|The ctenophore pulsed with light as it moved through the dark water. 这只栉水母在黑暗的水中移动时发出脉冲光。|近义词:comb jelly|反义词:None|词性:noun
