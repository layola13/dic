abases: /əˈbeɪsɪz/
verb
降低；贬低；使卑下
to lower in rank, prestige, or esteem; to humble; to degrade
He abased himself before the king. 他在国王面前自贬身份。
The company abased its employees by making them work long hours for low pay.  这家公司让员工长时间低工资工作，以此贬低他们。

近义词:humiliate, degrade, demean, lower, abase
反义词:exalt, elevate, praise, honor
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abasen, from Old French *abesser, from *a- "down" + *baissier "to lower," from Vulgar Latin *bassiare, from Late Latin bassus "low."
