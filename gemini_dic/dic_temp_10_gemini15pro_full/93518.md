imagenet:/ˈɪmɪdʒˌnɛt/ | n. |图像网络|A large visual database designed for use in visual object recognition software research.  It contains over 14 million images hand-annotated with labels identifying the objects present in the images. |  Example: Researchers trained their image recognition model on ImageNet. 研究人员在ImageNet上训练了他们的图像识别模型。| Synonym: dataset, database | Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "limited data") | noun
