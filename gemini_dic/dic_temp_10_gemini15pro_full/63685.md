epencephalic: /ˌɛpɛnˈsɛfəlɪk/ /ˌɛpɛnˈsɛfælɪk/
adjective
后脑的；有关后脑的
Relating to the hindbrain, the posterior division of the brain, composed of the metencephalon (cerebellum and pons) and myelencephalon (medulla oblongata).
The epencephalic structures play a crucial role in coordinating movement and regulating vital functions. 后脑结构在协调运动和调节重要功能方面起着至关重要的作用。
The doctor identified an epencephalic abnormality in the patient's MRI. 医生在病人的核磁共振成像中发现了一个后脑异常。
近义词: hindbrain
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider terms related to other brain regions like forebrain or midbrain)
词性: adjective
