metallochrome: /məˈtæləˌkroʊm/ | n. | 金属指示剂 | A compound that forms colored complexes with metal ions, used in analytical chemistry to indicate the presence or concentration of metals. | The metallochrome EDTA is commonly used in titrations to determine the concentration of metal ions. EDTA金属指示剂通常用于滴定以确定金属离子的浓度。 | synonyms: indicator, chelating agent | antonyms: None | noun

metallochromic: /məˌtæləˈkroʊmɪk/ | adj. | 金属指示剂的 | Relating to or characteristic of a metallochrome. |  The metallochromic properties of the dye allow for precise measurement of metal ion concentration.  这种染料的金属指示剂特性可以精确测量金属离子浓度。| synonyms: indicator, chelating | antonyms: None | adjective
