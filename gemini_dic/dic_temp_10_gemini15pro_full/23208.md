bowered:/ˈbaʊərd/| adj.|荫蔽的，被树荫遮蔽的；用凉亭装饰的|Covered with bowers; sheltered by trees or other plants.|The wedding ceremony took place in a bowered garden. 婚礼仪式在一个绿树成荫的花园里举行。|The couple exchanged vows under a bowered archway. 这对夫妇在一个树荫下的拱门下交换了誓言。|近义词：shaded, leafy, overgrown|反义词:exposed, bare|词性:形容词
词源: From bower + -ed.  "Bower" comes from the Old English "bur" meaning "dwelling, chamber, inner room".
