officinal:/əˈfɪsɪnəl/
adjective
药用的；药房的；出售的
Kept or sold in a pharmacy.
例句：Officinal herbs are used in traditional medicine. 药用草药用于传统医学。

例句：The officinal preparations are available at the chemist's shop.  这些药剂可以在药店买到。

近义词：medicinal, therapeutic, pharmaceutical
反义词：non-medicinal

词性：adjective
词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin officinalis, from Latin officina ‘workshop, store’.
