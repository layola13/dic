businessman:/ˈbɪznəsmæn/ | n. | 商人；实业家 | A man who is engaged in business, especially at an executive level. | He's a successful businessman with a thriving company. 他是一位成功的商人，拥有一家蓬勃发展的公司。|businessman:  entrepreneur, executive, industrialist, magnate, tycoon | businesswoman (female equivalent) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From business + man.  "Business" comes from the Old English "bisignes" meaning "care, anxiety, occupation."  "Man" comes from the Old English "mann" meaning "human being, person (male or female)."  The specific sense of "male human being" developed later.
