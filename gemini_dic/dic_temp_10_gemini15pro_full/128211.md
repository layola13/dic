neurobiotactic:/ˌnʊroʊˌbaɪoʊˈtæktɪk/
adjective
神经趋向性的
Relating to the movement of cells or organisms in response to neural tissue or neural signals.  This describes the tendency of certain cells or organisms to move towards or away from nervous tissue.
例句：Researchers are studying the neurobiotactic properties of certain stem cells to see if they can be used to repair damaged nerve tissue. 研究人员正在研究某些干细胞的神经趋向性，看看它们是否可以用来修复受损的神经组织。

例句：The neurobiotactic behavior of the parasite allows it to locate and infect the host's nervous system. 寄生虫的神经趋向性行为使其能够定位并感染宿主的  神经系统。
近义词: neurotropic
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "neurorepellent")
