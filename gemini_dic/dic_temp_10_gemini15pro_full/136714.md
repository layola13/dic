nonsensation:/ˌnɑːnsenˈseɪʃən/
n.
无感觉；麻木；不引起轰动的事情
Lack of sensation; numbness; something that does not cause a stir.
The new phone release was a nonsensation, with few people showing interest.  这款新手机的发布并没有引起轰动，很少有人表现出兴趣。
The drug caused a nonsensation in his fingers. 这种药使他的手指麻木了。
Synonyms: nonevent, anticlimax, disappointment
Antonyms: sensation, excitement, thrill
