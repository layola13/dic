discourages:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒɪz/
|v.|使气馁；劝阻；阻碍|Third person singular present tense form of discourage.  It means to cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm; dissuade (someone) from doing something.|He discourages his son from joining the army. 他劝阻儿子参军。|近义词：deter, dissuade, hinder|反义词：encourage, promote, inspire
词性:动词
