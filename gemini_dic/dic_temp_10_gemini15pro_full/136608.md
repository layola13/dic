nonsaponifiable:/ˌnɒnsəˌpɒnɪˈfaɪəbl/
adjective
不可皂化的
Not capable of being saponified; that is, not convertible into soap by reaction with alkali.
The nonsaponifiable fraction of shea butter is known for its skin-soothing properties.  乳木果油的不可皂化部分以其舒缓皮肤的特性而闻名。
Nonsaponifiable lipids include sterols and vitamins. 不可皂化的脂类包括甾醇和维生素。
synonym:non-hydrolyzable
antonym:saponifiable
词性:adjective
