consider:/kənˈsɪdər/ (US) /kənˈsɪdə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 考虑；认为；体谅；顾及 | To think carefully about something; to regard someone or something in a particular way. | I'm considering buying a new car. 我正在考虑买一辆新车。|  contemplate, ponder, deem, regard | disregard, ignore | verb

consider:/kənˈsɪdər/ (US) /kənˈsɪdə(r)/ (UK)| adj. |  (obsolete) Considerate; thoughtful | Thoughtful | He was a very consider man. 他是一个非常体贴的人 (古用法，现已少用). | thoughtful, kind | thoughtless, inconsiderate | adjective (obsolete)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French considerer, from Latin considerare "to look at closely, observe, contemplate," probably literally "to observe the stars," from com- "together" + sidus (genitive sideris) "star, constellation."
