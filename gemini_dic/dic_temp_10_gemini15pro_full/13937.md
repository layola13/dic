aunter:/ˈɔːntər/| n.|冒险; 冒险经历; 冒险活动|An exciting or dangerous experience or activity, typically one involving some travel.|He set off on an aunter to the Himalayas. 他开始了前往喜马拉雅山的冒险之旅。|近义词：adventure, escapade, expedition|反义词:stay, idleness|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *auntren, from Old French aventure, from Vulgar Latin *adventūra (“that which is about to happen”), from Latin adventurus, future active participle of advenīre (“to arrive”), from ad- (“to”) + venīre (“to come”).  The same root is found in the word "adventure".
