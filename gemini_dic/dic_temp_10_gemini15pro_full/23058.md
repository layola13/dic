bouncily:/ˈbaʊnsɪli/
adverb
有弹性地，活泼地
in a bouncy manner; springily.
She walked bouncily down the street. 她沿着街道轻快地走着。
The children jumped bouncily on the trampoline. 孩子们在蹦床上欢快地跳跃着。
近义词: springily, resiliently, elastically
反义词: stiffly, rigidly, sluggishly
词性: adverb

bounce:/baʊns/
verb & noun
弹跳，反弹；跳动，活力
(v.) To spring back after hitting something. (n.) The act of bouncing; resilience.
The ball bounced off the wall.  球从墙上弹了回来。
The children were full of bounce. 孩子们充满了活力。
近义词: (v.) rebound, spring, leap; (n.) resilience, elasticity, vigor
反义词: (v.) sink, drop, plop; (n.) stiffness, rigidity, inertia
词性: verb, noun

bounciness:/ˈbaʊnsɪnɪs/
noun
弹性，活力
The quality of being bouncy.
The bounciness of the trampoline made it fun to jump on. 蹦床的弹性使它跳起来很有趣。
The bounciness of her curls was admired by everyone.  每个人都羡慕她卷发的弹性。
近义词: resilience, elasticity, springiness
反义词: stiffness, rigidity, inflexibility
词性: noun

词源: From bounce + -ily (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Middle French bondir "to leap, jump."
