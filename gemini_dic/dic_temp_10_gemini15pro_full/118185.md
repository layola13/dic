mentions:/ˈmɛnʃənz/ | n. | 提及，说起；(社交媒体上的)@ | The article mentions the incident briefly. 这篇文章简短地提到了这件事。| Synonyms: references, cites, notes | Antonyms: ignores, omits | Noun


/ˈmɛnʃənz/ | v. | 提到，谈及 | He mentioned your name in the conversation. 他在谈话中提到了你的名字。| Synonyms: states, remarks, alludes to | Antonyms: conceals, hides | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mentioner, from Latin mentionem (nominative mentio) "a calling to mind, a reminding; a speaking of, mention," noun of action from past-participle stem of meminisse "to remember," from PIE root *men- (1) "to think."
