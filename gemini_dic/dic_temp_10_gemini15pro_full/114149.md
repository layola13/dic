mahayanist:/ˌmɑːhəˈjɑːnɪst/ | n. | 大乘佛教徒 | A follower of Mahayana Buddhism, one of the two major traditions of Buddhism. |  例句：The Mahayanist believes in the Bodhisattva ideal.  这位大乘佛教徒信仰菩萨理想。

例句：Many Mahayanist texts were translated into Chinese. 许多大乘佛教典籍被翻译成中文。 | 近义词: Buddhist (更广义) | 反义词: Hinayanist/Theravada Buddhist | 词性: noun
