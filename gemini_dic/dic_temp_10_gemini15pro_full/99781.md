intermembrane:/ˌɪntərˈmɛmbreɪn/
adjective
膜间的；膜与膜之间的
Located or occurring between membranes, especially between the inner and outer membranes of a mitochondrion or chloroplast.
The intermembrane space of the mitochondria plays a crucial role in energy production.  线粒体膜间隙在能量产生中起着至关重要的作用。
The intermembrane proteins are essential for cellular communication.  膜间蛋白对于细胞通讯至关重要。
Synonyms:  intermembranous
Antonyms:  N/A
词性: adjective
