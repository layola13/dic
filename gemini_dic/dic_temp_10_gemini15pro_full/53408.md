disambiguation:/dɪsˌæmbɪɡjuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 消歧；消除歧义 | The process of identifying the meaning of a word or phrase with multiple meanings based on its context. |  The use of the word "bank" in the sentence requires disambiguation - is it a financial institution or a river bank?  这句话中“bank”一词需要消歧——它是指金融机构还是河岸？ | clarification, elucidation | ambiguity | noun
