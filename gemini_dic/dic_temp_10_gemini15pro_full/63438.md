entreatingly:/ɪnˈtriːtɪŋli/
adv.
恳求地，哀求地
in a pleading or beseeching manner.
She looked at him entreatingly, hoping he would change his mind. 她恳求地看着他，希望他能改变主意。
近义词: pleadingly, beseechingly, imploringly, supplicatingly
反义词: demandingly, assertively, imperiously
