gemmule:/ˈdʒɛmjuːl/
n.
芽球；微芽；小宝石
A small gem.  Also, a theoretical particle or unit of inheritance, now discredited.  In some invertebrates, a cluster of cells encased in a resistant coat, capable of developing into a new individual.
Sponges release gemmules that can survive harsh conditions and later develop into new sponges. 海绵释放出能够在恶劣条件下生存并随后发育成新海绵的芽球。
synonyms: bud, propagule, germ
antonyms: N/A
