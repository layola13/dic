canned:/kænd/ | adj. | 罐装的；录音的；预先录制的；陈腐的 | Preserved in a sealed can or jar; pre-recorded or pre-arranged; lacking spontaneity or originality. |  We ate canned peaches for dessert. 我们吃了罐装桃子作为甜点。|  The laughter on the sitcom sounded canned.  情景喜剧里的笑声听起来像是预先录制的。 | synonyms: preserved, tinned, recorded, pre-packaged, formulaic | antonyms: fresh, live, spontaneous, original | adjective

canned:/kænd/ | v. |  解雇；装罐；录制 | Past tense and past participle of "can," meaning to dismiss from employment, to preserve food in a can, or to record. | He was canned for being late too often. 他因为经常迟到而被解雇了。 | They canned the peaches from their orchard. 他们把果园里的桃子装罐保存。 | synonyms: dismiss, fire, sack, preserve, tin, record | antonyms: hire, employ, open | verb
