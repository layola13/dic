chrysoprase:/ˈkrɪsəˌpreɪz/| n.|绿玉髓|A translucent apple-green variety of chalcedony, a cryptocrystalline form of quartz. |例句：The ring was set with a beautiful chrysoprase.  这枚戒指上镶嵌着一颗美丽的绿玉髓。

例句：She admired the chrysoprase pendant. 她很欣赏那块绿玉髓吊坠。


|近义词：chalcedony, gem, gemstone|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *crisopras< Latin chrysoprasus < Greek khrusoprasos, from khrusos "gold" + prason "leek" (referring to its color). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- "yellow, green, gold".
