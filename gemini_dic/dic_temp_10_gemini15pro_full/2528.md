admirablenesses: /ˌædmərəbəlˈnɛsɪz/
n.
令人钦佩的品质；优点
The numerous admirablenesses of her character. 她性格中诸多令人钦佩的品质。
Her admirablenesses include kindness, intelligence, and a great sense of humor. 她的优点包括善良、智慧和极强的幽默感。
近义词: merits, virtues, excellences
反义词: flaws, defects, shortcomings
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From admirable + -ness.  Admirable traces back through Middle French to Latin *admirābilis, from admirārī "to wonder at," from ad- "to" + mirārī "to wonder."

USAGE->
This word is rarely used in modern English.  It's more common to use "admirable qualities" or simply "virtues."  Here are some examples using synonyms in different tenses and grammatical structures:

一般现在时:
Her kindness is one of her admirable qualities. = 她的善良是她令人钦佩的品质之一。
His virtues are evident in his actions. = 他的美德在他的行为中显而易见。

一般过去时:
Her courage in the face of adversity was admirable. = 她面对逆境时的勇气令人钦佩。
His merits earned him the respect of his colleagues. = 他的优点为他赢得了同事们的尊重。

现在完成时:
She has always demonstrated admirable qualities. = 她一直展现出令人钦佩的品质。
He has consistently shown great virtue. = 他一直表现出伟大的美德。

过去完成时:
He had already shown admirable courage before joining the army. = 在参军之前，他已经表现出令人钦佩的勇气。
She had always possessed those admirable qualities. = 她一直拥有那些令人钦佩的品质。

将来时:
Her future actions will undoubtedly demonstrate her admirable qualities. = 她未来的行动无疑将展现出她令人钦佩的品质。
His virtues will continue to inspire others. = 他的美德将继续激励他人。

虚拟语气:
If she were less modest, she would boast about her admirable qualities. = 如果她不那么谦虚，她就会吹嘘自己令人钦佩的品质。
I wish I possessed his many virtues. = 我希望我拥有他的许多美德。

被动语态:
Her kindness was admired by everyone. = 她的善良受到大家的钦佩。
His virtues were praised by his teachers. = 他的美德受到老师们的称赞。

疑问句:
What are her most admirable qualities? = 她最令人钦佩的品质是什么？
What virtues does he possess? = 他拥有哪些美德？

否定句:
She is not without her flaws, but her admirable qualities outweigh them. = 她并非没有缺点，但她令人钦佩的品质超过了她的缺点。
He doesn't always show it, but he has many virtues. = 他并不总是表现出来，但他有很多美德。
