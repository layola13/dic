evulsion:/ɪˈvʌlʃən/ | n. | 撕脱，扯脱; 拔除 | The sudden and violent tearing away of a part or structure, such as skin or a tooth, from the body. | The dentist performed an evulsion of the impacted wisdom tooth.  牙医拔掉了那颗阻生的智齿。| synonyms: tearing, ripping, wrenching, extraction | antonyms: implantation, insertion, attachment | noun

evulsion:/ɪˈvʌlʃən/ | n. | (感情的)迸发 | A sudden outburst or expulsion. |  He felt an evulsion of anger. 他感到一阵怒火迸发。| synonyms: outburst, eruption, explosion | antonyms: suppression, repression | noun
