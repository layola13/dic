escalope:/ˈeskəlɒp/ (UK), /ˈeskəˌloʊp/ (US)|n.|薄肉片；炸肉排|A thin, boneless piece of meat, often veal or pork, that is coated in breadcrumbs and fried.|例句：
I ordered the veal escalope with a lemon sauce. 我点了一份柠檬酱小牛肉片。

The chef prepared a delicious chicken escalope. 厨师准备了一份美味的鸡肉片。


近义词：cutlet, fillet, scallop|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "thick steak" or "roast")|词性:noun
