mulch:/mʌltʃ/ | n. v. | 覆盖物，护根物；用覆盖物覆盖 | Material (such as decaying leaves, bark, or compost) spread around or over a plant to enrich or insulate the soil.  |  Example: I spread mulch around the roses to protect them from the frost. 我在玫瑰周围铺上覆盖物以防霜冻。 | Synonyms: covering, compost, straw, peat | Antonyms: uncover | Noun


mulch:/mʌltʃ/ | v. |  用覆盖物覆盖 | To cover (something, such as a plant) with mulch. | Example: We mulched the garden with straw. 我们用稻草覆盖了花园。 | Synonyms: cover, protect | Antonyms: uncover, expose | Verb
