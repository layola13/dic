hawkers:/ˈhɔːkəz/ | n. |小贩，叫卖者|A person who travels from place to place selling goods, typically by shouting or calling out to attract customers.|The hawkers in the market were selling fresh produce.| 近义词: vendors, peddlers, merchants, traders | 反义词: buyer, customer | noun

hawker:/ˈhɔːkər/ | n. |兜售者，推销员|Someone who tries to sell goods often by going from house to house or by approaching people on the street. |The software hawker was persistent, but I wasn't interested.|近义词:salesperson, canvasser, solicitor | 反义词: buyer, customer | noun
