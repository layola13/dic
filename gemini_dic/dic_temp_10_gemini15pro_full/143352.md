outfabled:/aʊtˈfeɪbəld/
|v.|胜过；在传说或虚构故事中超越|To surpass in fable or fictional storytelling; to be more legendary or mythical than.|Her exploits outfabled even the most imaginative of the ancient myths. 她的功绩甚至超过了最具想象力的古代神话。|The new superhero outfabled all those who came before.  这位新的超级英雄超越了所有前辈。|surpass, exceed, eclipse, outdo, transcend| - |verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + fabled.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding.  "Fabled" comes from "fable" meaning a short story, typically with animals as characters, conveying a moral.
