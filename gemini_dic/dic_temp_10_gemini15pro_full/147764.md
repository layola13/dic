overprolixity:/ˌoʊvərproʊˈlɪksɪti/
|n.|冗长，啰嗦|The overprolixity of the speaker made the audience lose interest. 演讲者的冗长乏味让听众失去了兴趣。|English Explanation:  Excessive wordiness; the use of too many words to express an idea.  It implies being tediously lengthy and often suggests a lack of focus or conciseness.|Example: His overprolixity in explaining the simple concept bored everyone. 他解释这个简单概念的冗长乏味让每个人都感到无聊。|Synonyms: verbosity, wordiness, prolixity, long-windedness|Antonyms: conciseness, brevity, terseness|Noun
