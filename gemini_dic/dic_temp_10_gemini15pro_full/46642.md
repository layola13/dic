damneds:/dæmdz/ | n. | 被诅咒的人；该死的家伙（复数） |  Plural form of "damned," referring to those who are condemned or cursed. |  The damneds cried out in torment. 被诅咒的人们痛苦地哭喊着。| 近义词: cursed, doomed, condemned | 反义词: blessed, saved | 词性: 名词

词源：From Old English *dēmd, past participle of dēman "to deem, judge," from Proto-Germanic *dōmijaną "to judge, set, establish."  The sense of "condemned to hell" developed from the religious context of judging souls.
