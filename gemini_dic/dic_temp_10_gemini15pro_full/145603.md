overconscientiously: /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsli/
adverb
过度认真地；过分谨慎地
in an excessively conscientious manner; with extreme care and attention to detail, often to an unnecessary degree.
She cleaned her apartment overconscientiously before her guests arrived, even dusting the individual books on the shelves.  她来客人之前，过分认真地打扫了公寓，甚至把书架上的每一本书都掸了灰尘。
He proofread the document overconscientiously, checking every comma and full stop multiple times. 他过分认真地校对了文件，每一个逗号和句号都检查了好几遍。
近义词: meticulously, scrupulously, painstakingly, excessively carefully
反义词: carelessly, negligently, perfunctorily
词性: adverb

词源:
over- (prefix) + conscientious + -ly (suffix)
"over-" denotes "excessive" or "too much"
"conscientious" comes from Latin "conscientia" (knowledge within oneself), from "com-" (with) + "scire" (to know).
"-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
