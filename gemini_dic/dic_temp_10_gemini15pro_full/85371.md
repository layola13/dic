harrumph:/hɑːˈrʌmpf/ |vi. n.|清嗓子; 哼哼声|To clear the throat noisily, often as an expression of disapproval or doubt.  A sound made in clearing the throat, especially to express disapproval or doubt.| He harrumphed and folded his arms. 他哼了一声，交叉双臂。|近义词: cough, clear one's throat, grunt,哼哼|反义词: whisper, murmur|词性: 动词, 名词
词源:  Imitative of the sound.  拟声词，模仿清嗓子的声音。
