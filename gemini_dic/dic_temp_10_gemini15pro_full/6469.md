ampullaceous:/ˌæmpjʊˈleɪʃəs/| adj.|瓶状的；烧瓶状的|Shaped like an ampulla, a small, rounded flask with two handles used by the Romans, typically for holding liquids; flask-shaped. |The ampullaceous base of the vase was intricately carved.  花瓶的瓶状底部雕刻精美。|The chemist carefully filled the ampullaceous container with the volatile liquid.  化学家小心翼翼地将挥发性液体装入烧瓶状的容器中。|近义词：flask-shaped, bottle-shaped, globular|反义词：linear, straight|词性:形容词
ampullaceous:/ˌæmpjʊˈleɪʃəs/|Etymology: From Latin *ampullaceus*, from ampulla (“flask, bottle”) + -aceus (“-aceous”).|词源：来自拉丁语 *ampullaceus*，源自 ampulla（“烧瓶、瓶子”）+ -aceus （“-aceous”）。
