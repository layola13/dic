glowy:/ˈɡloʊi/| adj.|发光的，容光焕发的|Having a soft warm light; appearing to shine.  A healthy, attractive appearance, especially of skin.|例句：Her skin looked healthy and glowy. 她的皮肤看起来健康且容光焕发。|例句：The moon cast a glowy light on the water. 月光在水面上投下柔和的光芒。|近义词：luminous, radiant, shining, bright, incandescent|反义词：dull, matte, lackluster|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From "glow" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'characterized by'). "Glow" originates from Middle English "glowen," from Old English "glōwan," related to Old High German "gluojan."
