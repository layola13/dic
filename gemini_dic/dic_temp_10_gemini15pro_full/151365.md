parkways:/ˈpɑːrkweɪz/ | n. | 公园道路，林荫大道 | A broad landscaped highway, often with a dividing strip or other features designed for scenic driving. | We drove along the parkway, enjoying the fall foliage. 我们沿着公园道路行驶，欣赏着秋天的落叶。|  The parkway offers stunning views of the coastline. 这条林荫大道可以欣赏到海岸线的壮丽景色。 | synonyms: boulevard, avenue, scenic route | antonyms: alley, backstreet | noun
