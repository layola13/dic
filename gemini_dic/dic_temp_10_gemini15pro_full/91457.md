hyke:/haɪk/
| n. |远足，徒步旅行；大幅提高（价格或费用）；|A long walk or journey, especially in the countryside; a sharp increase in price or cost.| We went on a five-mile hyke through the woods. 我们在树林里进行了五英里的徒步旅行。|近义词：hike, trek, tramp|反义词:rest, stay|名词

hyke:/haɪk/
| vt. |提高（价格或费用）；拉起，提起；|To increase (a price or cost) sharply; to pull or lift something upwards.|The landlord hyked the rent by 20%. 房东将租金提高了20%。|近义词：raise, increase, lift|反义词:lower, decrease|动词
