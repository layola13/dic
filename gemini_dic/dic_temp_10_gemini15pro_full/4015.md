ahistoricities:/ˌeɪhɪˌstɔːrɪˈsɪtiz/ | n. | 非历史性，脱离历史背景 |  Lack of historical perspective or context, often implying a disregard for the past.  The quality or state of being ahistorical. |  The movie's ahistoricities were jarring to those familiar with the period. 这部电影对熟悉那个时代的人来说很不协调。 |  anachronisms, inaccuracies, distortions | historical accuracy, contextualization | noun
