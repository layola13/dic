interchoke:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊk/
verb
相互扼制，互相干扰
to choke or obstruct each other; to interfere with one another.
The overgrown vines interchoked the rose bushes, hindering their growth.  过度生长的藤蔓互相干扰，阻碍了玫瑰丛的生长。
The signals from the two antennas interchoked, causing interference.  两个天线发出的信号互相干扰，导致干扰。
Synonyms: interfere, hinder, obstruct, impede
Antonyms: facilitate, aid, assist
