feudary:/ˈfjuːdəri/ | n. | 封臣；封地；采邑 | A person who holds land from a feudal lord in return for allegiance and service. |  The king granted land to his feudaries. 国王将土地授予他的封臣。 | Synonyms: vassal, liegeman, tenant | Antonyms: lord, overlord | Noun

feudary:/ˈfjuːdəri/ | adj. | 封建的；封臣的 | Relating to or characteristic of feudalism. | The feudary system was prevalent in medieval Europe. 封建制度在中世纪的欧洲很盛行。 | Synonyms: feudal, vassal | Antonyms: modern, democratic | Adjective
