groundhog:/ˈɡraʊndhɑːɡ/ (US) /ˈɡraʊndhɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|土拨鼠|A rodent of the squirrel family, Marmota monax, found in North America, noted for its hibernation and for the tradition that if it sees its shadow on February 2, there will be six more weeks of winter.|例句：The groundhog saw its shadow, predicting six more weeks of winter. 土拨鼠看到了它的影子，预示着还有六个星期的冬天。

例句：Groundhogs are known for their digging abilities. 土拨鼠以其挖掘能力而闻名。
