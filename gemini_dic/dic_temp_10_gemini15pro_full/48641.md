defends:/dɪˈfɛndz/
verb
保卫，防御
protects from attack or danger; supports or justifies.
The lawyer defends her client in court. 律师在法庭上为她的客户辩护。
近义词: protects, guards, supports, justifies
反义词: attacks, prosecutes, condemns
词性: verb

defends:/dɪˈfɛndz/
verb
辩护，维护
speaks or writes in support of (someone or something) that is being criticized.
He defended his decision to leave the company. 他为离开公司的决定辩护。
近义词: upholds, advocates for, champions
反义词: criticizes, attacks, denounces
词性: verb
