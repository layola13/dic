fti:/ɛf tiː ˈaɪ/ |abbr.| Federal Tax Identification |联邦税号| Federal Tax Identification is a unique nine-digit number assigned to businesses and other entities by the IRS for tax purposes. |Your FTI is required on various tax forms. 你的联邦税号在各种税务表格中是必需的。| The FTI is used by the IRS to track tax payments and filings. 国税局使用联邦税号来跟踪纳税和申报。 | synonyms: EIN, Tax ID | antonyms: N/A | noun
