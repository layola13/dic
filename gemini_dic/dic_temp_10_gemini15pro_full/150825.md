paragenic:/ˌpærəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
副生的，共生的
Formed or occurring as part of the same process of mineral formation.  Relating to the sequence in which minerals are deposited in an ore deposit.
例句：The paragenic sequence of the ore deposit shows that gold was deposited after quartz.  该矿床的共生顺序表明金是在石英之后沉积的。

例句：Paragenic studies are helpful in understanding the geological history of an area. 共生研究有助于了解一个地区的地质历史。
近义词：associated, related, cogenetic
反义词：unrelated
词性：adjective
