pandoras:/pænˈdɔːrəz/ | n. | 潘多拉的；潘多拉魔盒的 |  Relating to Pandora, especially Pandora's box, a mythical artifact containing all the evils of the world. | Pandora's box had been opened and there was no way to contain the evils within.  潘多拉的盒子已经打开，没有办法控制里面的邪恶。 |  Pandora's promise proved to be false. 潘多拉的承诺被证明是假的。 | synonymous: calamitous, disastrous, destructive | antonymous: beneficial, advantageous, fortunate | noun


pandoras:/pænˈdɔːrəz/ | n. | 潘多拉们 |  Plural form of "Pandora", referring to multiple individuals named Pandora or metaphorically to multiple sources of trouble or misfortune. | The pandoras of the modern world are numerous and complex. 现代世界的潘多拉魔盒数不胜足数，而且错综复杂。 | The pandoras of social media have unleashed a torrent of misinformation. 社交媒体的潘多拉魔盒释放出了大量的虚假信息。 | synonymous: scourges, plagues, banes | antonymous: blessings, boons, benefits | noun
