outstudied:/ˌaʊtˈstʌdid/
verb
学习上胜过，比...用功
surpass someone in studying; defeat someone by studying harder
She outstudied all her classmates and got the highest grade. 她比所有同学都用功，获得了最高分。
outwork, surpass
be outdone by, fall behind
