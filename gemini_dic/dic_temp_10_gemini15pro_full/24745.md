brooding:/ˈbruːdɪŋ/| adj.|沉思的，忧郁的；令人担忧的|Engrossed in or showing deep thought about something that makes one sad, angry, or worried.  Characterized by or expressive of gloom.|例句：He had a brooding expression on his face. 他脸上带着沉思的表情。

例句：The brooding storm clouds threatened rain. 乌云密布，预示着即将下雨。|近义词：pensive, contemplative, thoughtful, gloomy, melancholic, morose|反义词：cheerful, lighthearted, optimistic|词性:形容词
词源:From Old English *brōdian "sit on eggs," from Proto-Germanic *brodjan "to warm, cherish"
