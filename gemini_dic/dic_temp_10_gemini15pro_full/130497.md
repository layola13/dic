nonacceptances:/ˌnɒnəkˈsɛptənsɪz/
n.
不接受；拒收；未承兑
The act of not accepting something; a refusal to accept.
The company received a high number of nonacceptances for their new product. 这家公司的新产品收到了很多拒收。
nonacceptance, rejection, refusal
acceptance, approval
noun
