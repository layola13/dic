avidest:/ˈævɪdɪst/| adj.|最渴望的，最热衷的|Characterized by great eagerness or enthusiasm.|She is the avidest reader I know. 她是我认识的最爱读书的人。|近义词：keenest, most enthusiastic, most eager|反义词：apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词
词源：From avid + -est (superlative suffix).  Avid comes from the Latin "avidus," meaning "eager" or "greedy."
