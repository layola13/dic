charterages:/ˈtʃɑːrtərɪdʒɪz/
n.
包租费，租船费
The cost of hiring or renting a vessel or aircraft.
The charterages for the ship were exorbitant due to the increased demand. 由于需求增加，船舶的租船费用过高。
