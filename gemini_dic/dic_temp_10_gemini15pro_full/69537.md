词源：From Old English *fyllan, from Proto-Germanic *fulljaną (“to fill, fell”), from Proto-Indo-European *pl̥h₁- (“to fill”).  Cognate with Dutch vellen (“to fell”), German fällen (“to fell”), Danish fælde (“to fell”).  The sense of "to cut down (a tree)" developed from an earlier meaning "to cause to fall".
