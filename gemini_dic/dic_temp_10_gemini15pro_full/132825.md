nonecstatically:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstætɪkli/
adverb
并非欣喜若狂地; 不狂喜地
in a way that is not ecstatic; without ecstasy.
She accepted the news nonecstatically, a slight frown creasing her forehead. 她并非欣喜若狂地接受了这个消息，眉头微微皱了起来。
The team received the news of their draw nonecstatically, knowing they could have won. 球队得知平局的消息时并没有欣喜若狂，因为他们知道自己本来可以赢的。
Synonym: unenthusiastically, apathetically, indifferently
Antonym: ecstatically, joyfully, rapturously
词性: 副词
