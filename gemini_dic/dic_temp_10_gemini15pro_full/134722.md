nonmaturities:/ˌnɒnməˈtjʊərɪtiz/ | n. | 未到期债务，短期债务 | Liabilities or debts that are not yet due or mature.  These are typically short-term obligations. | Example: The company's nonmaturities include short-term loans and accounts payable.  这家公司的未到期债务包括短期贷款和应付账款。

Example:  Managing nonmaturities effectively is crucial for maintaining a healthy cash flow. 有效管理未到期债务对于维持健康的现金流至关重要。| 近义词: short-term liabilities, current liabilities | 反义词: long-term liabilities, maturities | 词性: 名词
