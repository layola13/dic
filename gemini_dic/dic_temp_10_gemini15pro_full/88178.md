hilly:/ˈhɪli/| adj.|丘陵的，多山的|Characterized by many hills; undulating.|例句：The area is known for its hilly terrain.  这个地区以其丘陵地形而闻名。|例句：They went for a hike in the hilly countryside. 他们在丘陵起伏的乡村远足。|近义词：mountainous, undulating, rolling|反义词:flat, level|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hill + -y.  From Middle English hil, from Old English hyll, from Proto-Germanic *hulniz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to rise, be elevated”).
