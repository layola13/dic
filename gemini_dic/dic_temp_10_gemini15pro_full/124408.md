Derived from "motorbike" (n.) + "-ed" (verb suffix indicating past participle, though here used as a simple past verb).| I motorbiked across Vietnam last summer. 我去年夏天骑摩托车穿越了越南。| 近义词: rode a motorcycle, rode a motorbike | 反义词: walked, drove a car |词性: 动词
