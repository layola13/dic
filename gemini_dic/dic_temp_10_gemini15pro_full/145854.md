overdepressing: /ˌōvərdiˈprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 极其压抑的，非常令人沮丧的 | Extremely depressing; causing a great deal of sadness or discouragement. | The news was overdepressing and left everyone feeling hopeless. 这消息极其令人沮丧，让每个人都感到绝望。|  The constant rain and grey skies were overdepressing.  连绵不断的雨和灰蒙蒙的天空令人非常沮丧。 | 近义词: very depressing, extremely depressing, overwhelmingly depressing | 反义词: uplifting, encouraging, cheering | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "depressing" (adjective derived from the verb "depress," ultimately from Latin "depressus," past participle of "deprimere," meaning "to press down").
