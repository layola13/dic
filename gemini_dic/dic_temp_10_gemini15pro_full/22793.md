boshbok:/ˈbɒʃbɒk/ /ˈbʊʃbɑːk/ | n. |羚羊（一种产于非洲南部的羚羊）| A large, spiral-horned antelope (Tragelaphus scriptus) native to southern Africa, with a reddish-brown coat marked with white stripes and patches. | The boshbok is known for its distinctive coat pattern.  这种羚羊以其独特的皮毛图案而闻名。

The boshbok prefers dense bushveld habitats.  羚羊喜欢茂密的灌木丛栖息地。|近义词：antelope, bushbuck |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


词源:
From Afrikaans _bosbok_, from _bos_ ("bush") + _bok_ ("buck," male antelope).
