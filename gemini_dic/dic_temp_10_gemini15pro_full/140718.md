omnigenous:/ɒmˈnɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
各种各样的；由所有种类组成的
composed of all kinds or of many different kinds
例句：The conference attracted an omnigenous crowd of scientists, artists, and business leaders.  这次会议吸引了由科学家、艺术家和商界领袖组成的形形色色的人群。

例句：The forest is an omnigenous ecosystem, teeming with a vast array of plant and animal life. 这片森林是一个包含各种物种的生态系统，充满了种类繁多的动植物。
近义词：diverse, varied, miscellaneous, heterogeneous
反义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *omnigenus ("of all kinds"), from omni- ("all") + genus ("kind, origin, race").
