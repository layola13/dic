antimechanist:/ˌæntiˈmɛkənɪst/ | n. |反机械论者；非机械论者| A person who opposes the philosophical theory of mechanism, which explains phenomena in terms of physical or biological mechanisms and denies the influence of purpose, design, or final causes. | The antimechanist argued that consciousness could not be reduced to mere physical processes. 反机械论者认为意识不能简化为单纯的物理过程。|  near synonyms: vitalist, teleologist, organicist | near antonyms: mechanist, materialist, reductionist | noun

antimechanistic:/ˌæntiˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. |反机械论的；非机械论的| Opposed to or rejecting mechanism as an explanation of phenomena. | His antimechanistic view of biology emphasized the importance of vital forces. 他反机械论的生物学观点强调了生命力的重要性。| near synonyms: vitalistic, teleological, organicistic | near antonyms: mechanistic, materialistic, reductionistic | adjective
