baptistery:/ˈbæptɪstri/ | n. | 洗礼堂，浸礼池 | A part of a church or other religious building used for baptism. |  The baptistery was octagonal in shape, symbolizing rebirth and new beginnings. 洗礼堂是八角形的，象征着重生和新的开始。

The ancient baptistery was adorned with beautiful mosaics.  古老的洗礼堂装饰着美丽的马赛克。| 近义词: font | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *baptisterium, from Latin baptisterium "a bathing place, baptistery," from Greek baptisterion "vessel for baptism, baptistery," from baptizein "to baptize," from baptein "to dip, immerse."
