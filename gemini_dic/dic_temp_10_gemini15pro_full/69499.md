felinely:/ˈfiːlaɪnli/| adv.|像猫一样地，猫科动物般地|In a manner characteristic of a cat; like a cat. |She moved feliney across the room. 她像猫一样优雅地走过房间。|近义词：gracefully, stealthily, nimbly, agilely|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly|词性：副词

词源: From feline + -ly.  "Feline" comes from the Latin word "felinus," meaning "of or belonging to a cat," ultimately derived from "feles," meaning "cat."
