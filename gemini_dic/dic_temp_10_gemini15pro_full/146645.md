overhardy:/ˌōvərˈhɑːrdi/
adjective
过于强壮的；鲁莽的；过分大胆的
Too hardy or robust; rash; excessively bold.
The overhardy seedlings were killed by the late frost.  过于强壮的幼苗被晚霜冻死了。
His overhardy attempt to climb the mountain in winter resulted in a serious injury. 他在冬天鲁莽地尝试攀登这座山，结果受了重伤。
近义词:reckless, rash, foolhardy, impetuous, audacious
反义词:cautious, prudent, timid
词性:adjective
词源:From over- + hardy.
