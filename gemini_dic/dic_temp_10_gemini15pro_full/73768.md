formalise:/ˈfɔːməlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrməlaɪz/ (US)| v. |使正式化；正式规定；使成为正式|To make something official or definite, often by expressing it in a written or structured way. |例句：They decided to formalise the agreement by signing a contract. 他们决定通过签署合同来使协议正式化。

例句：The company formalized its hiring procedures to ensure fairness. 公司将其招聘程序正式化，以确保公平。


近义词：codify, systematize, regularize, institutionalize|反义词: informalize|词性:动词
