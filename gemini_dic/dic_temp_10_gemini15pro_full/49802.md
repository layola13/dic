demonology:/ˌdiːmənˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌdiːmənˈɑːlədʒi/
n.
恶魔学
The study of demons, their nature, and their hierarchy.
He was fascinated by demonology and the occult. 他对恶魔学和神秘学很着迷。
synonyms: demonism
antonyms: angelology
noun
