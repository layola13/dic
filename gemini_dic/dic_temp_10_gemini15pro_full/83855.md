halcyonoid:/ˌhælsiˈɔɪnɔɪd/| adj. n.|翠鸟类的，宁静的，太平的|Resembling or related to a kingfisher; peaceful and tranquil, evocative of a golden age.|例句：The halcyonoid days of summer seemed to stretch on forever.  夏日宁静的日子似乎永远延续下去。|例句：The museum's exhibit featured a collection of halcyonoid birds from around the world.  博物馆的展览展出了一系列来自世界各地的翠鸟类。 |近义词：peaceful, tranquil, serene, calm, golden|反义词：turbulent, chaotic, tumultuous, restless|词性:形容词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *halkyōn* (kingfisher) + *-oid* (resembling).  The kingfisher was believed to nest at sea during the winter solstice, calming the waves and bringing a period of calm weather, known as the "halcyon days."
