lovelessness:/ˈlʌvləsnəs/
n.
lack of love; absence of affection
The lovelessness of his childhood left him emotionally scarred. 他童年时期缺乏爱，给他留下了情感上的伤疤。
His lovelessness towards his family was evident. 他对家人的冷漠显而易见。
近义词: coldness, indifference, apathy, heartlessness
反义词: love, affection, tenderness, warmth
词性: noun

词源: love + less + ness
