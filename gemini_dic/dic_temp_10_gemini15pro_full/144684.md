outyields:/ˌaʊtˈjiːldz/
verb
产量超过，胜过
Produces a greater yield than.
This new strain of wheat outyields the old one by 15%.  这种新的小麦品种比旧品种产量高15%。
Synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, betters
Antonyms: underperforms, lags behind
词性: 动词
