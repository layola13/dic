deadheaded:/ˌdɛdˈhɛdɪd/
v. |去除枯萎的花朵；空载返回；调动（人员）；取消（项目）| To remove dead flowers from a plant. To travel without passengers or cargo. To reassign personnel. To cancel a project. | I deadheaded the roses this morning. 我今天早上把玫瑰的枯萎花朵去掉了。|  synonyms: prune, clip, trim, reassign, cancel | antonyms: plant, grow, cultivate, initiate, start
词性: 动词

deadheaded:/ˌdɛdˈhɛdɪd/
adj. |(花)枯萎的；(运输工具)空载的；(人)被解雇的；(项目)被取消的|Describing a flower that has withered. Describing a vehicle traveling without passengers or cargo. Describing a person who has been dismissed from their job.
