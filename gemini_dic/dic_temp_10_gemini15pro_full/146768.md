overhyped: /ˌoʊvərˈhaɪpt/ | adj. | 过度炒作的，吹嘘过度的 | Promoted or publicized to an excessive degree, often creating unrealistic expectations. | The new phone was overhyped and didn't live up to the expectations. 这款新手机被过度炒作，并没有达到预期。| synonyms: overblown, exaggerated, inflated | antonyms: underrated, understated | adjective

overhyped: /ˌoʊvərˈhaɪpt/ | adj. | 过度宣传的 |  Given excessive publicity, resulting in inflated expectations. | The movie was overhyped; it wasn't nearly as good as the trailers made it seem. 这部电影被过度宣传了；它远没有预告片看起来那么好。 | synonyms: over-promoted, over-publicized | antonyms: under-promoted, understated | adjective
