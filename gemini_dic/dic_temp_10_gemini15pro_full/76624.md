galloping:/ˈɡæləpɪŋ/ | adj. v.| 奔驰的，飞驰的；疾驰；飞奔 | moving or progressing very quickly; to run at a gallop | The horse was galloping across the field.  马在田野里飞奔。|近义词：racing, dashing, sprinting, bolting |反义词：crawling, creeping, dawdling |词性: adjective, present participle of gallop

galloping:/ˈɡæləpɪŋ/ | n.| 奔驰；飞驰；飞奔 | an act of galloping | The galloping of the horses could be heard from miles away.  马匹的飞奔声几英里外都能听到。|近义词：race, dash, sprint |反义词：crawl, creep |词性: noun, gerund of gallop


Etymology: From Middle English *galopen, from Old French galoper, of Germanic origin.
