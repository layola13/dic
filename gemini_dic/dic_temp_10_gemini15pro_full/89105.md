homager:/ˈhɒməʒə(r)/ | n. |致敬者，效仿者| A person who pays homage or expresses admiration, especially by imitation. | Spielberg's film is a clear homager to the classic thrillers of the 1940s. 斯皮尔伯格的这部电影显然是对20世纪40年代经典惊悚片的致敬。|  The young painter was a homager of Monet.  这位年轻的画家是莫奈的崇拜者，并模仿他的风格。 | synonym: admirer, imitator, follower, devotee | antonym: critic, detractor | noun
