biosignatures:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsɪɡnətʃərz/ | n. | 生物特征，生命印记 | Any substance, element, or phenomenon that provides scientific evidence of past or present life. | Scientists are searching for biosignatures on Mars. 科学家正在火星上寻找生物特征。|  The rover is equipped with instruments to detect biosignatures. 探测车配备了探测生物特征的仪器。 | synonyms: biomarkers, indicators of life | antonyms: abiotic factors, non-living indicators | noun
