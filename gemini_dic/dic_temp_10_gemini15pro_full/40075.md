contagious:/kənˈteɪdʒəs/ | adj. | 传染的；有感染力的 | Spreading or tending to spread from one person or organism to another by contact. |  The flu is highly contagious. 流感是高度传染性的。

The laughter was contagious, and soon everyone was joining in.  笑声是会传染的，很快大家都加入了进来。

| 近义词：infectious, catching, communicable | 反义词：non-contagious | 词性: adjective
