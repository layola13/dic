compatibleness:/kəmˌpætəˈbɪlnəs/
n.|兼容性，相容性|The compatibleness of the two systems allows them to work together seamlessly. 这两个系统的兼容性使它们能够无缝地协同工作。|例句：The compatibleness of their personalities made them a great couple. 他们的性格相容性使他们成为一对很棒的夫妻。|近义词：compatibility, harmony, concord, agreement|反义词：incompatibility, discord, conflict|词性:名词
词源: Late Middle English: from compatible + -ness.
