insufferably:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbli/ | adv. |  难以忍受地，令人厌恶地 |  In a manner that is extremely annoying or unpleasant, difficult to bear. |  He talked insufferably about his new car. 他滔滔不绝地谈论他的新车，令人难以忍受。 |  The heat was insufferably intense.  酷热难耐。 |  unbearably, intolerably, excruciatingly |  tolerably, bearably | adverb

insufferable:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbl/ | adj. | 难以忍受的，令人厌恶的 |  Extremely annoying or unpleasant, difficult to bear. |  His arrogance is insufferable. 他的傲慢令人难以忍受。 | The pain in my leg was insufferable. 我的腿疼得难以忍受。 | unbearable, intolerable, excruciating | tolerable, bearable | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *insufferabilis, from in- "not" + sufferabilis "tolerable" (from Latin sufferre "to endure, undergo").
