nonsubstitution:/ˌnɒnsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | n. | 非替代，非置换 | The principle of nonsubstitution emphasizes the unique value of certain resources or elements, meaning they cannot be replaced by others. |  The nonsubstitution of certain cultural practices highlights their importance to the community.  非替代某些文化习俗突出了它们对社区的重要性。 | near synonyms: irreplaceability, non-interchangeability | antonyms: substitution, replacement | noun
