fictionalized:/ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 小说化的；虚构的 |  Made into or presented as fiction; given a fictional character.  Based on real events but with changes made to create a story. | The movie is a fictionalized account of his life. 这部电影是根据他的生平改编的虚构故事。| The author fictionalized the story of her grandparents' immigration.  作者将祖父母移民的故事写成了小说。 |  novelized, dramatized, invented, imagined | factual, real, historical | adjective
