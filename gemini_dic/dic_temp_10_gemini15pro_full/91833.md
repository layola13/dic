hypergeometrical:/ˌhaɪpərˌdʒiːəˈmetrɪkəl/
adjective
超几何的
Relating to or involving a hypergeometric distribution or function, a type of discrete probability distribution that describes the probability of k successes in n draws without replacement from a finite population of size N containing K successes.
The professor explained the hypergeometrical distribution in detail. 教授详细解释了超几何分布。
Researchers used a hypergeometrical model to analyze the data. 研究人员使用超几何模型来分析数据。
Near Synonyms: combinatorial, probabilistic
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps non-probabilistic, deterministic)
词性: adjective
