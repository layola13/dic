mugginess:/ˈmʌɡinəs/| n.|闷热，湿热|The mugginess of the tropical climate was oppressive.  热带气候的闷热令人窒息。|近义词：humidity, sultriness, closeness|反义词：freshness, coolness|词性:名词

Etymology->
From muggy + -ness. Muggy's origin is uncertain, possibly related to Norwegian *mugga ("drizzle, mizzle").
