dishouse:/ˈdɪʃˌhaʊs/| n.|洗碗间，碗碟存放处|A room or area in a house where dishes are washed and stored.  A less common and somewhat old-fashioned term, often used regionally.|The old farmhouse had a small dishouse off the kitchen.|Synonym: scullery, pantry (for storage aspect)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dining room" as a contrasting area)|noun
