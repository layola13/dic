maliciousness:/məˈlɪʃəsnəs/
n.
恶意；恶毒
The quality of being malicious; characterized by malice.
His maliciousness was evident in the way he spread rumors about his colleagues.  他的恶意体现在他散布关于同事的谣言的方式上。
The maliciousness of the attack shocked the community. 这次袭击的恶意震惊了整个社区。

近义词:malevolence, spite, venom, viciousness, malignity
反义词:benevolence, kindness, goodwill, compassion
