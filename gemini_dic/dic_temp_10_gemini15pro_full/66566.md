exhibitor:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtər/| n. |参展者，展出者|A person or company that displays their work or products at an exhibition. |例句：The exhibitor showcased their latest innovations at the tech expo.  参展商在科技博览会上展示了他们的最新创新。

例句：Many exhibitors at the trade show were offering free samples.  许多贸易展上的参展商都提供免费样品。|近义词：displayer, presenter, participant|反义词:visitor, attendee|词性:noun
Etymology->
From exhibit + -or. Exhibit ultimately derives from Latin exhibere "to hold forth, show, display."
