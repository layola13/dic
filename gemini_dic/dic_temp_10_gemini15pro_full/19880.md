binmen:/ˈbɪnmɛn/| n. |垃圾工，清洁工|A person whose job is to empty dustbins (= large containers for rubbish) into a dustcart (= a vehicle for collecting rubbish).|The binmen come every Tuesday. 垃圾工每周二来。|The binmen were on strike last week, so the rubbish piled up. 上周垃圾工罢工了，所以垃圾堆积如山。|近义词：garbage collectors, refuse collectors, dustmen|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|noun
