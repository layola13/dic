overattentively:/ˌoʊvərəˈtɛntɪvli/| adv.|过度专注地，过分关心地|Paying overattentively to details can sometimes hinder progress. 过度关注细节有时会阻碍进展。|例句：The teacher overattentively monitored the students during the exam. 老师在考试期间过度关注地监视着学生们。|近义词：excessively attentively, too attentively|反义词:inattentively, carelessly|词性:副词
Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "attentive" (from Latin attentus, past participle of attendere "to attend to, give heed to") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
