detorsion:/diˈtɔːrʃən/
n.|扭转复位，解扭|The act of untwisting or removing a torsion, especially of a bodily organ.|The surgeon performed a detorsion of the ovary.  外科医生进行了卵巢扭转复位。|近义词：untwisting, unwinding|反义词：torsion|词性：名词

detorsion:/diˈtɔːrʃən/
n.|解扭|The process of correcting a torsion.|The detorsion was successful, and blood flow was restored. 解扭手术成功，血流恢复。|近义词：correction, reversal|反义词：twisting|词性：名词
