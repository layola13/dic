nondigestiblenesses:/ˌnɒndaɪˈdʒɛstɪbəlnəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːndaɪˈdʒɛstɪbəlnəsɪz/
n.
不可消化性；难消化
The quality or state of being nondigestible.  The degree to which something is not capable of being digested.
Certain types of fiber contribute to the nondigestiblenesses of food.  某些类型的纤维导致了食物的不可消化性。
High levels of nondigestiblenesses can lead to digestive issues. 高度不可消化性会导致消化问题。
近义词:indigestibility, nonassimilability
反义词:digestibility, assimilability
词性:noun

词源:
From non- + digestible + -ness.  "Non-" prefixes a word to give its opposite meaning. "Digestible" comes from the Latin "digerere," meaning to separate, distribute, digest.  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
