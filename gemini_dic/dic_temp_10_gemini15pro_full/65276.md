euchlorine:/juːˈklɔːriːn/
noun
氯化物
A mixture of chlorine dioxide and chlorine.
Euchlorine was used as a disinfectant in the 19th century.  氯化物在19世纪被用作消毒剂。
Synonyms:  chlorine dioxide,  chlorine peroxide
Antonyms:  N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From eu- (good, well) + chlorine.  Coined by Sir Humphry Davy. 源自eu-（好的，良好的）+ chlorine（氯）。由汉弗莱·戴维爵士创造。
