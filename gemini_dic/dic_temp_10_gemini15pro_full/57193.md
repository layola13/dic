downloading:/ˈdaʊnˌloʊdɪŋ/
n.|下载; 下行传输|The downloading of the file took several minutes.  文件下载花了幾分鐘。|近义词:transfer, retrieval|反义词: uploading|词性:名词

downloading:/ˈdaʊnˌloʊdɪŋ/
adj.|下载的|The downloading speed is very slow. 下载速度很慢。|近义词: incoming|反义词: uploading|词性:形容词


Etymology:  From "download" + "-ing".  "Download" itself is a compound of "down" (indicating transfer from a larger system) and "load" (meaning to transfer data or programs into a computer).
