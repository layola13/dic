carbfix: /ˈkɑːrbfɪks/
noun
碳固定,碳封存
A method of permanently storing carbon dioxide emissions by reacting them with basaltic rock formations.  This process mineralizes the CO2, turning it into stone.
Carbfix is a promising technology for mitigating climate change. 碳固定是一项很有前景的减缓气候变化的技术。
Carbfix has successfully mineralized CO2 in Iceland. 碳固定已在冰岛成功地将二氧化碳矿化。
synonyms: carbon capture and storage, CCS, mineralization
antonyms: carbon emission, release
