brockman:/ˈbrɒkmən/ (British), /ˈbrɑːkmən/ (American)| n.|布罗克曼；(姓氏)|A surname.  It is derived from a place name meaning "badger's stream" in Old English.  It can also refer to John Brockman, a well-known literary agent and author.|John Brockman represents many prominent scientists and thinkers.|Synonym: agent (in the context of literary representation), representative|Antonym: N/A (surname doesn't typically have antonyms)|noun
