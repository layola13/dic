funneling:/ˈfʌnəlɪŋ/ /ˈfʌnəlɪŋ/
n.|漏斗作用; 汇集; 注入; |The process of narrowing down options or possibilities, or of concentrating resources or information into a single point.  |The sales team is funneling all their efforts into securing the new contract.|近义词：channeling, concentrating, focusing, directing|反义词：dispersing, scattering, spreading|词性:名词

funneling:/ˈfʌnəlɪŋ/ /ˈfʌnəlɪŋ/
v.|使成漏斗形; 汇集; (使)流入; |To move or cause to move through or as if through a funnel.  |The wind funneled through the canyon.|近义词：channel, direct, guide, convey|反义词：scatter, disperse, spread|词性:动词
