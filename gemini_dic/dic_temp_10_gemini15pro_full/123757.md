moores:/mʊrz/ | n. | 荒野，沼泽；摩尔人（复数）|  A tract of open uncultivated upland; a heath.  Also, the plural of "Moor," referring to a member of a Muslim people of mixed Arab and Berber descent, who inhabited northwestern Africa and conquered Spain in the 8th century. |  The hikers traversed the desolate moores of Scotland.  徒步旅行者穿越了荒凉的苏格兰沼泽。/ The Moores brought advancements in mathematics and astronomy to Europe. 摩尔人将数学和天文学的进步带到了欧洲。 | synonyms:  heathlands, bogs, fens; Moors (for the people). | antonyms:  meadows, valleys;  (no direct antonym for the people). | noun
