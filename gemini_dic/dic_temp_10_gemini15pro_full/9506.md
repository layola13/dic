antiseptic: /ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/ | n. adj. |消毒剂，防腐剂；抗菌的，防腐的 |A substance that inhibits the growth and reproduction of microorganisms, or an agent that prevents infection. |
例句：The doctor applied an antiseptic to the wound. 医生给伤口涂了消毒剂。

例句：Antiseptic wipes are essential for cleaning cuts and grazes. 消毒湿巾对于清洁割伤和擦伤至关重要。


近义词：disinfectant, germicide, antimicrobial |
反义词: septic, infected |
词性: noun, adjective


antiseptics: /ˌæntiˈsɛptɪks/ | n. |消毒剂，防腐剂 |Plural form of antiseptic, referring to multiple substances or agents that prevent infection.  |
例句：The nurse stocked the cabinet with various antiseptics.  护士在柜子里放了各种消毒剂。

例句：Different antiseptics are effective against different types of bacteria. 不同的消毒剂对不同类型的细菌有效。


近义词：disinfectants, germicides, antimicrobials |
反义词: none (plural form doesn't have a direct opposite) |
词性: noun


词源: From anti- (against) + septic (putrefying; relating to sepsis). Ultimately from Greek *sḗpein (to rot).
