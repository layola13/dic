acidimetry: /ˌæsɪˈdɪmətri/
noun
酸量滴定法
The determination of the amount of acid in a solution by titration with a standard base.
Acidimetry is used to determine the concentration of an unknown acid solution. 酸量滴定法用于测定未知酸溶液的浓度。

The lab technician performed acidimetry to analyze the sample. 实验室技术员用酸量滴定法分析样品。

近义词: acid-base titration, titration
反义词: alkalimetry
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French acidimétrie, from acide (“acid”) + -métrie (“-metry”).  Ultimately from Latin acidus (“sour”) + Greek -μετρία (-metria, “measurement”).
