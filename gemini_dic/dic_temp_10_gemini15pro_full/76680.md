galvanography:/ɡælˌvæˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.
电版印刷术，电流印刷术
A method of producing etched designs on a metal plate by electrolysis, especially for printing purposes.
The artist experimented with galvanography to create intricate textures on her copper plates.  这位艺术家尝试用电版印刷术在她的铜板上创造复杂的纹理。
Galvanography is a less common printmaking technique than etching or engraving. 电版印刷术是一种比蚀刻或雕刻更不常见的版画技术。
近义词:electrotyping, electrography
反义词: None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *galvanographie*, from *galvano-* (from Italian *Galvani*,  referring to Luigi Galvani, the discoverer of bioelectricity) + *-graphie* (from Greek *-graphia* "writing").
