ampullate:/æmˈpʌleɪt/ | adj. | 瓶状的；烧瓶状的；膨大的 | Shaped like a flask or bottle, swelling out like a bladder. | The plant has distinctive ampullate leaves. 这种植物有独特的瓶状叶。| The fungus produces ampullate spores. 这种真菌会产生瓶状孢子。| 近义词：flask-shaped, bottle-shaped, inflated, swollen | 反义词：constricted, narrow | 词性:形容词

ampulla:/æmˈpʌlə/ | n. | 壶腹；小瓶 | A small bottle or flask, especially one used for holding liquids. | The ampulla of Lorenzini in sharks detects electrical fields. 鲨鱼的洛伦齐尼壶腹可以探测电场。|  The Roman ampulla contained holy oil. 罗马小瓶里装着圣油。 | 近义词：flask, vial, bottle | 反义词：N/A | 词性:名词

词源: From Latin ampulla "small bottle, flask," diminutive of amphora "jar, jug."
