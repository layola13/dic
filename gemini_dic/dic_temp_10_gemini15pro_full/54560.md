dislocatory:/dɪsˈlɒkətəri/ /dɪsˈloʊkətɔːri/
adjective
脱臼的，导致混乱的，引起错位的
causing dislocation; disruptive
The dislocatory effects of the war on the economy were devastating. 战争对经济造成的混乱性影响是毁灭性的。
The earthquake had a dislocatory impact on the region's infrastructure. 地震对该地区的基础设施造成了破坏性的影响。
近义词: disruptive, disturbing, unsettling
反义词: stabilizing, constructive, ordering
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From dislocate + -ory.  Dislocate comes from the Latin dis- "apart" + locare "to place".  The suffix -ory means "relating to" or "serving to".
