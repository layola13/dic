mesothorax:/ˈmiːzəʊˌθɔːræks/ /ˈmiːsoʊˌθɔːræks/| n.|中胸|The middle of the three segments of the thorax in insects, to which the middle legs and the forewings are attached.  昆虫胸部三个部分的中间部分，中足和前翅附着于此。|The mesothorax bears the forewings and the second pair of legs. 中胸长有前翅和第二对足。|metathorax, prothorax||noun
