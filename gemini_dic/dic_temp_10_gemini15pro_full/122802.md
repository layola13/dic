moldiness:/ˈmoʊldɪnəs/| n. |发霉，霉味，霉烂|The moldiness in the basement was overwhelming. 地下室的霉味令人难以忍受。|例句：The old books had a distinct moldiness to them. 这些旧书有一股明显的霉味。

例句：The moldiness of the bread made it inedible.  面包发霉了，不能吃了。|近义词：mildew, mustiness, dampness|反义词:freshness, dryness|词性:名词

Etymology: From moldy + -ness. Mold ultimately comes from Old Norse *mygla.
