dumpiness:/ˈdʌmpɪnɪs/
noun
矮胖；臃肿
The state or quality of being short and stout.
Her dumpiness made her look adorable in the oversized sweater.  她矮胖的身材让她穿着超大号毛衣看起来很可爱。
近义词: stoutness, chubbiness, stockiness
反义词: slenderness, slimness, tallness
词性:n.


dumpiness:/ˈdʌmpɪnɪs/
noun
沉闷；压抑
The state or quality of being dull and depressing.
The dumpiness of the weather matched his mood. 阴沉的天气和他郁闷的心情很相配。
近义词: dreariness, gloominess
反义词: cheerfulness, brightness
词性:n.


词源: From "dumpy" + "-ness"
"Dumpy" originated from the Low German word "dump," meaning "short and thick."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns denoting a state or quality.
