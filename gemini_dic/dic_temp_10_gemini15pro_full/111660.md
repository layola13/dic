lochan:/ˈlɒxən/ (UK) /ˈlɑːxən/ (US)| n. |小湖，湖泊|A small lake, especially in Scotland or Ireland.  A pool or pond.|例句：We walked down to the lochan for a picnic. 我们走到小湖边野餐。

例句：The lochan was nestled amongst the hills. 小湖依偎在群山之中。|近义词：lake, pond, tarn, pool|反义词: (None -  Lochan describes a type of lake, not its opposite.)|词性:noun
