agha:/ˈɑːɣə/ /ˈɑːɡə/ | n. |老爷，先生，阿加|A title of respect, formerly used in Turkey, Persia, and other parts of the East, for a military commander or other person of high rank.  It can also be a term of address similar to "sir".|He bowed low before the agha. 他在老爷面前深深鞠了一躬。|Synonyms: sir, master, lord, chief|Antonyms: servant, slave|noun
