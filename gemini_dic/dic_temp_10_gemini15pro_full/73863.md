formulators:/ˈfɔrmjəleɪtərz/
n.
配方设计师；制定者；公式化者
People who formulate something, such as policies, plans, or products.  Those who devise or create something in a systematic or precise way.
The formulators of the new policy are experienced professionals. 新政策的制定者都是经验丰富的专业人士。
New product formulators are highly sought after in the cosmetics industry. 化妆品行业对新产品配方设计师的需求很高。
synonyms: creators, developers, designers, inventors, planners, authors
antonyms: destroyers, dismantlers, saboteurs
noun
