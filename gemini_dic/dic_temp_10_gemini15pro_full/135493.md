nonphonetically:/ˌnɑːnfoʊˈnɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 非语音地；不用语音表示地 | Not represented or expressed using phonetic symbols or sounds; in a way that does not relate to the sounds of speech. |  The word was written nonphonetically.  这个词是用非语音的方式写的。| synonym: non-phonetically | antonym: phonetically | adverb

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "phonetic" (relating to the sounds of speech) + "-ally" (adverbial suffix).  The word "phonetic" ultimately comes from the Greek "phōnē" meaning "sound" or "voice."
