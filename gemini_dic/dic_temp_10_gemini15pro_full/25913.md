burner:/ˈbɜːrnər/ (US) /ˈbɜːnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|燃烧器；炉头；灯头；烧录机|A device that produces a flame or heat, or that burns something.  A person who burns something.|The burner on the stove wasn't working.  炉子上的炉头坏了。|Synonyms: heater, igniter, incinerator, lamp, torch|Antonyms: extinguisher|noun

burner:/ˈbɜːrnər/ (US) /ˈbɜːnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|刻录机；烧录机|A device used to write data onto a CD, DVD, or Blu-ray disc.|He used a burner to create a copy of the DVD. 他用刻录机复制了这张DVD。|Synonyms: writer, recorder|Antonyms: player|noun

burner:/ˈbɜːrnər/ (US) /ˈbɜːnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|（一次性）手机；临时邮箱；匿名账户|A prepaid or disposable phone, email address, or online account used for temporary or anonymous communication.|He used a burner phone to avoid being traced. 他用一次性手机以免被追踪。|Synonyms: disposable phone, throwaway phone, temporary account|Antonyms: permanent account|noun
