disquisition:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən/
n.
正式的演讲；专题论文；学术讨论
A formal discourse or treatise in which a subject is examined and discussed; a long and elaborate essay or discussion on a particular subject.
His disquisition on the nature of good and evil was both lengthy and insightful. 他关于善恶本质的专题论文既长篇大论又富有洞察力。
treatise, discourse, essay, lecture, dissertation, exposition, study
chat, gossip
noun
