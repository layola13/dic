opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/| adj.|不透明的；晦涩难懂的|Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Also, difficult to understand; obscure.|例句：The windows were opaque with steam. 窗户上满是蒸汽，变得不透明了。|例句：The contract was written in opaque legal jargon. 这份合同是用晦涩难懂的法律术语写的。|近义词：nontransparent, cloudy, obscure, impenetrable|反义词：transparent, clear, lucid|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *opacus* "shaded, dark," related to *ops* "power, might, resources." The semantic shift may be due to the association of darkness with power or mystery.
