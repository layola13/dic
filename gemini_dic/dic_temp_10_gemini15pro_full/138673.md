nuffin:/ˈnʌfɪn/| pron.|什么也没有，没有东西 (非正式, 俚语)|A nonstandard pronunciation and spelling of "nothing".  It is often used for humorous or emphatic effect.|例句：I ain't got nuffin to do today. 我今天啥事也没有。|近义词：nothing, zip, zilch|反义词:something, anything|代词

nuffin:/ˈnʌfɪn/| n.|无关紧要的东西，琐碎的东西 (非正式, 俚语)|A nonstandard pronunciation and spelling of "nothing," also used to mean something trivial or worthless.|例句：That ain't nuffin but a scratch. 那只不过是划痕。|近义词：nothing, trifle, diddly-squat|反义词:something of value, something important|名词
