Cambodias:/kæmˈboʊdiəz/
n.
Cambodias (usually treated as singular)
柬埔寨
Cambodia is a country located in Southeast Asia. 柬埔寨是一个位于东南亚的国家。
The Cambodias of the past were very different from the Cambodia of today. 过去的柬埔寨和今天的柬埔寨非常不同。（虽然语法上不完全正确，但在某些语境下可以表示柬埔寨的不同历史时期）
近义词:Kampuchea
反义词:(无)
词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *Cambodge*, from Khmer *Kămpŭchéa*. Ultimately from Sanskrit *Kambujadeśa* (“land of the Kambujas”).
