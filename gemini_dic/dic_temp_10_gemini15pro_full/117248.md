mecha: /ˈmɛkə/ | n. | 机甲，机械战甲 | A large, armored robot or humanoid vehicle, typically piloted by a person inside.  Often featured in science fiction and anime. | Example: The mecha stomped across the battlefield, its weapons blazing.  机甲踏过战场，武器 blazing。

Example:  She piloted her mecha with skill, dodging enemy fire. 她熟练地驾驶着她的机甲，躲避敌人的炮火。 | Near synonyms: robot, giant robot, mech | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "infantry" in a military context) | noun
