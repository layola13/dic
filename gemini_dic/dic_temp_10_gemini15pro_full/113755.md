macrophage:/ˈmækrəˌfeɪdʒ/ | n. | 巨噬细胞 | A large phagocytic cell found in stationary form in the tissues or as a mobile white blood cell, especially at sites of infection. |  Macrophages play a crucial role in the immune system. 巨噬细胞在免疫系统中起着至关重要的作用。

Macrophages engulf and destroy bacteria. 巨噬细胞吞噬并摧毁细菌。| 近义词: phagocyte, histiocyte | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps consider suppressed immune cell or pathogen) | 词性: noun
