haunch:/hɔːntʃ/ | n. | 腰腿，臀部；(四足动物的)后腿及臀部 | The dog sat on its haunches. 狗蹲坐在它的后腿上。|  The cat crouched on its haunches, ready to spring. 猫蹲伏在它的后腿上，准备跳跃。 | synonyms: hindquarters, rump, rear | antonyms: forequarters | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hanche*, from Frankish *hanka* (compare Old High German *hanka* (“hip, haunch”)), from Proto-Germanic *hankō* (compare West Frisian *hinke*, Dutch *heup*, German *Hünke* (“hip, haunch”)), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱenk- (“to bend, curve”).
