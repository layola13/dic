nondecorousnesses:/nɒndɪˈkɔːrəsnəsɪz/
noun
不雅的行为；不得体的举止
lack of decorum; impropriety.  The plural form of nondecorousness.  It refers to multiple instances of behavior that are not considered proper or socially acceptable.
Her repeated nondecorousnesses at formal events eventually led to her being uninvited.  她在正式场合屡次不雅的行为最终导致她不再被邀请。
The book detailed the king's numerous nondecorousnesses, shocking the public. 这本书详细描述了国王的 numerous 不得体的行为，震惊了公众。
Synonyms: improprieties, indecencies, indecorums, unseemlinesses
Antonyms: proprieties, decencies, decorums
n.
