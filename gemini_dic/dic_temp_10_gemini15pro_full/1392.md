accusatively:/əˈkjuːzətɪvli/
adv.
控告地，指责地;作宾语地
in a way that relates to or is part of the accusative case
He looked at her accusatively. 他用责备的眼神看着她。
accusingly, reproachfully
absolvingly, approvingly
adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accusativus (from Latin accusativus), from accusare (“to accuse”) + -ivus.

USAGE->
He spoke accusatively, pointing his finger at the culprit.  他用指责的语气说话，手指着罪魁祸首。
She stared accusatively at him after he spilled the coffee. 她在他洒了咖啡后，用责备的眼神盯着他。


He treated her somewhat accusatively. 他对待她有点像在指责她。
The lawyer questioned the witness accusatively. 律师用指责的语气盘问证人。
