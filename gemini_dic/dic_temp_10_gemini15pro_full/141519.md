oppa:/ˈopə/| n.|哥哥 (用于女性称呼比自己年长的男性)|A Korean term of address used by females to refer to older males, typically close friends, older brothers, or romantic interests. It implies a level of familiarity and respect.|例句：Oppa, can you help me with this? 哥哥，你能帮我一下吗？

例句：My friend's oppa is really kind. 我朋友的哥哥人真的很好。|近义词：(Context-dependent) hyung (male to older male),  seonbae (junior to senior in school/work)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:Noun
