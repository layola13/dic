nonwithdrawable:/ˌnɒn.wɪðˈdrɔː.ə.bəl/ | adj. | 不可取出的，不可撤回的 | Not able to be withdrawn or revoked. |  例句：The deposit is nonwithdrawable for the first year.  这笔存款在第一年内不可取出。 | 例句：They invested in nonwithdrawable funds.  他们投资了不可撤回的基金。 | 近义词：nonrefundable, irrevocable, unrecallable | 反义词：withdrawable, revocable, refundable | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix) : not
withdraw (verb):  to remove or take away something
-able (suffix): capable of being
