noh:/noʊ/ (US), /nəʊ/ (UK)| interj. | 不，没有 | An expression of negation, refusal, or disagreement. | Example: Noh, I don't want any. 不，我不想要。| Example: "Noh, thank you," she replied politely. “不，谢谢你，”她礼貌地回答。| 近义词: no, nope, nay | 反义词: yes, yeah, aye | 词性: interjection


Etymology:
"Noh" is likely a variant or dialectal pronunciation of "no," which itself comes from Old English *nā, from Proto-Germanic *ne, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ne, meaning "not."
