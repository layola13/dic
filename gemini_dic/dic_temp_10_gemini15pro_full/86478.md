heifer:/ˈhɛfər/ (US), /ˈhɛfə/ (UK)| n.|小母牛|A young female cow that has not yet had a calf.|例句：The farmer bought two heifers to expand his herd. 农民买了两头小母牛来扩大他的牛群。|例句：The heifers grazed peacefully in the meadow. 小母牛们在草地上安静地吃草。|近义词：cow, bovine|反义词:bull, steer|noun
