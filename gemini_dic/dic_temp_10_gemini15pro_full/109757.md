levies:/ˈlɛviz/ | n. | 征税; 征兵; 征用 | An act of levying a tax, fee, or other amount.  A group of people levied or conscripted, especially for military service. | The government imposed new levies on imported goods. 政府对进口商品征收了新的税款。| The king ordered levies of men and supplies for the war. 国王下令征集兵员和物资以备战。| synonyms: taxes, duties, fees, charges, tolls, assessments, imposts, tariffs, exactions, collections, drafts, conscriptions | antonyms: refunds, reimbursements, repayments | noun

levies:/ˈlɛviz/ | v. | 征收(税款); 征集(兵员); 征用 | To impose (a tax, fee, or fine). To enlist or conscript (someone) for military service. | The city levies a tax on all sales. 该市对所有销售额征税。|  The army levied thousands of young men. 军队征召了数千名年轻男子。 | synonyms: impose, charge, assess, collect, exact, raise, gather, muster, conscript, draft | antonyms: refund, reimburse, repay | verb
