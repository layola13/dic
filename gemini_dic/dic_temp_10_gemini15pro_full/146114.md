overembroiders:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdərz/
|v.|过度绣花，绣花过多|To embroider something excessively or too ornately.|She overembroidered the cushion, making it look cluttered. 她把垫子绣得太多了，看起来很杂乱。|The tailor overembroidered the dress, making it gaudy.  裁缝把裙子绣得太多了，显得俗气。|近义词：overdecorate, overdo|反义词:understate, simplify|词性:动词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "embroider"
"over-" 表示“过度，过分”
"embroider" 源自古法语 "embroidier"，意为“用刺绣装饰”。
