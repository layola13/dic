nonchalkier:/ˌnɒnˈtʃɔːkiər/
adjective
更不白垩的;更不似白垩的
Less chalky; less like chalk.
The new paint made the wall nonchalkier than before.  新油漆使墙壁比以前更不白垩。
The clay is nonchalkier after being fired.  粘土烧制后更不似白垩。
Near Synonyms: less chalky, smoother, less powdery
Antonyms: chalkier, more chalky, more powdery
word type: adjective
