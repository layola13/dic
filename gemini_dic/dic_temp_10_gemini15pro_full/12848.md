associativity:/əˌsoʊʃiˈeɪtɪvɪti/ |n.|结合性，结合律|The associativity of an operator determines how operators of the same precedence are grouped in the absence of parentheses.  运算符的结合性决定了在没有括号的情况下，相同优先级的运算符如何分组。| In mathematics, addition has the property of associativity. 在数学中，加法具有结合性。| 近义词:combinability | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From associate + -ivity.  源自 associate（关联） + -ivity（名词后缀，表示性质或状态）。
