loathness:/ˈloʊθnɪs/ | n. | 不情愿，勉强 | Reluctance or unwillingness to do something. |  He showed great loathness to leave his home. 他很不愿意离开他的家。|She expressed her loathness to get involved in the argument. 她表示不愿意卷入这场争论。 | 近义词: reluctance, unwillingness, aversion, disinclination | 反义词: willingness, eagerness, readiness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *lothnes*, from Old English *lāþnes*, from *lāþ* (“hateful, hostile, loathsome”) + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns). Cognate with Scots *laithness* (“loathsomeness”), German *Leidenschaft* (“passion, suffering”).
