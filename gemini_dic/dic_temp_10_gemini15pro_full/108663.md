leaflets:/ˈliːflɪts/| n.|传单；小叶；散页印刷品|A printed sheet of paper, sometimes folded, containing information or advertising and usually distributed free.|They were handing out leaflets about the upcoming election. 他们正在分发有关即将举行的选举的传单。|synonyms: pamphlets, brochures, flyers, handbills|antonyms: books, volumes|noun

leaflets:/ˈliːflɪts/| n.|小叶|A small or young leaf, especially one forming part of a compound leaf.|The rose bush had delicate leaflets. 玫瑰丛长着精致的小叶。|synonyms:foliole, leaf|antonyms: branch, stem|noun
