borrow: /ˈbɒrəʊ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːroʊ/ (US) | v. | 借入，借用 | To take and use something belonging to someone else with the intention of returning it. |  Can I borrow your pen, please? 我可以借你的笔吗？

I borrowed a book from the library. 我从图书馆借了一本书。

近义词: loan, rent | 反义词: lend, loan | 词性: 动词
