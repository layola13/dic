goodwilled:/ˌɡʊdˈwɪld/ | adj. |  善意的，亲善的 | Having or showing good will; friendly. | She gave a goodwilled smile. 她露出了善意的微笑。|  Example: He extended a goodwilled offer of help to his neighbor. 他向邻居伸出了友好的援助之手。| 近义词: benevolent, kind, charitable | 反义词: malevolent, hostile, unkind | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "good" + "will" + "-ed".  "Good will" refers to a kindly feeling or attitude.  The "-ed" suffix transforms it into an adjective.
