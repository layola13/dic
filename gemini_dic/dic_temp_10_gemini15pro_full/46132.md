例句：The cymbium of this species is particularly large.  这个物种的海螺体特别大。|近义词：shell, aperture (in some contexts)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology->
From Latin *cymbium*, from Ancient Greek κύμβιον (kúmbion, “small cup, boat-shaped drinking vessel”), diminutive of κύμβη (kúmbē, “cup, bowl, boat”).
