diathetic:/ˌdaɪəˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
素质的，体质的，易患病的
Relating to or arising from a diathesis, a predisposition to a particular disease or condition.
Example:  His diathetic constitution made him susceptible to colds. 他易患病的体质使他容易感冒。

Example:  The doctor explained that her diathetic eczema was likely triggered by environmental factors. 医生解释说，她的体质性湿疹很可能是由环境因素引起的。

近义词:predisposed, susceptible, prone
反义词:resistant, immune
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek diathesis "arrangement, disposition," from diatithenai "to arrange," from dia- "through" + tithenai "to put, place."
