nonshrinking:/ˌnɒnˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ/
adjective
不缩水的，防缩的
Resistant to shrinking, especially after being washed.
This shirt is made of nonshrinking material, so you don't have to worry about it getting smaller after washing.  这件衬衫是用不缩水材料做的，所以你不用担心洗后会缩水。
The nonshrinking wool ensured the sweater maintained its shape.  不缩水的羊毛确保了毛衣保持其形状。
近义词:preshrunk, shrink-resistant, shrinkproof
反义词:shrinking
词性:adjective
