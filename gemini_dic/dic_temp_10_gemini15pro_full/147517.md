overpark: /ˈoʊvərˌpɑːrk/ | v. | 过度停车，停车占用过多空间 | To park a vehicle in a way that takes up more space than necessary, often encroaching on another parking space. | He overparked and took up two spaces. 他停车占用了两个车位。 |  The car was overparked, blocking part of the driveway. 这辆车停放过度，挡住了部分车道。 | 近义词: park badly, park incorrectly | 反义词: park properly, park correctly | 词性: 动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "park" (verb meaning "to leave a vehicle standing").  The prefix "over-" adds the sense of doing something to excess.
