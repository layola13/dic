eyre:/ɛər/ | n. | 荒野，旷野；继承人 | A large open area of moorland or heathland.  |  She wandered across the desolate eyre. 她漫步在荒凉的旷野上。 | synonym: moor, heath, wasteland | antonym:  cultivated land, city, town | noun

eyre:/ɛər/ | n. | (古代)巡回法庭 | A circuit court held periodically by a judge in a particular province or county of England. | The king's justices went on eyre throughout the kingdom. 国王的法官们在全国各地巡回审判。 | synonym: circuit court, assize | antonym: stationary court, local court | noun
