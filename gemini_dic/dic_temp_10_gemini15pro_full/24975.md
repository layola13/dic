n.

His brusqueness can be off-putting to some people. 他的粗鲁可能会让一些人反感。
近义词：rudeness, abruptness, curtness, sharpness, gruffness

词源:
From brusque + -ness.  Brusque comes from the Italian word *brusco*, meaning "sour, tart."
