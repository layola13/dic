hyponastic:/ˌhaɪpəˈnæstɪk/| adj.|下弯的，向下生长的|Relating to or characterized by hyponasty, the upward bending of a plant part caused by greater growth on the lower side.|The hyponastic response of the leaves helped the plant retain moisture. 叶子向下弯曲的反应帮助植物保持水分。|近义词:downward-curving, drooping|反义词:epinastic, upward-curving|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *hypo* "under" + *nastos* "pressed close".
