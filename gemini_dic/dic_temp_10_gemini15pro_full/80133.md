gobstopper:/ˈɡɒbˌstɒpə(r)/ /ˈɡɑːbˌstɑːpər/| n.|硬糖，（尤指）高波糖，彩虹糖|A hard, long-lasting candy, especially one that changes colors or flavors as it dissolves.|例句：The child loved the gobstopper because it lasted so long.  这孩子喜欢高波糖，因为它可以吃很久。

例句：She savored the changing flavors of the gobstopper. 她品味着高波糖不断变化的味道。|近义词：jawbreaker, everlasting gobstopper|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something that melts quickly like) soft candy, ice cream|词性:noun
