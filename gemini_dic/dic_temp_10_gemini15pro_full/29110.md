cascade:/kæˈskeɪd/ | n. v. | 小瀑布，瀑布状物；倾泻，大量落下 | a small waterfall, typically one of several that fall in stages down a steep rocky slope.  To fall or hang in copious quantities. | The water cascaded down the rocks. 水沿着岩石倾泻而下。|  She let her hair cascade down her back. 她让头发披散在背上。 | 近义词: waterfall, torrent, flood, avalanche, stream, pour, gush | 反义词: trickle, dribble | 词性: 名词, 动词

cascade:/kæsˈkeɪd/ | v. | 倾泻，大量落下 | To fall or hang in copious quantities; to pass something on to a succession of others. |  News of the merger cascaded through the office. 合并的消息迅速传遍了整个办公室。 | The benefits of exercise cascade throughout the body. 锻炼的好处遍及全身。 | 近义词: waterfall, torrent, flood, avalanche, stream, pour, gush | 反义词: trickle, dribble | 词性: 动词
