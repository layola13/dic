godspeed:/ˈɡɒdspiːd/ | n. |一路顺风，祝成功，|An expression of good wishes for someone starting a journey or undertaking a venture. |例句：I wished him godspeed as he embarked on his new career.  我祝愿他在新的职业生涯中一路顺风。

|例句：They sent her off with prayers and godspeeds. 他们为她送行，祈祷她一路平安，万事如意。

|近义词：good luck, success, bon voyage, best wishes|反义词: bad luck, failure |词性:名词
