例句：Her obsecratory tone made it difficult to refuse her request. 她恳求的语气使人难以拒绝她的请求。

近义词：supplicating, imploring, beseeching, pleading
反义词：demanding, commanding, assertive
词性: adjective


obsecration:/ˌɒbsɪˈkreɪʃn/ /ˌɑːbsɪˈkreɪʃn/
noun
恳求，哀求
An earnest entreaty or supplication.
例句：The prisoner's obsecrations for forgiveness were ignored. 囚犯的请求宽恕被忽视了。

例句：With obsecrations and tears, she begged him to reconsider. 她恳求并流着泪，求他重新考虑。

近义词：supplication, plea, entreaty, appeal
反义词：demand, command, order
词性: noun


词源:
From Latin *obsecratōrius*, from obsecrāre "to beseech, implore," from ob- "toward" + sacrāre "to dedicate, consecrate" (from sacer "sacred").
