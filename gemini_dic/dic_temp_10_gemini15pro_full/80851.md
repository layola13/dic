gourdlike:/ˈɡɔːrdlaɪk/| adj.|像葫芦的，葫芦形的|Resembling a gourd in shape; shaped like a gourd.  Having the rounded, bulging form characteristic of a gourd.|The plant produced a gourdlike fruit.  这种植物结出一种像葫芦一样的果实。|近义词：rounded, bulging, swollen, bulbous|反义词:narrow, slender, elongated|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From "gourd" + "-like."  "Gourd" comes from the Old French "gourde," from the Latin "cucurbita." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
