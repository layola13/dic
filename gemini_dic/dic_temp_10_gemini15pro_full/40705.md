converter:/kənˈvɜːrtər/ (US) /kənˈvɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |转换器，转化器|A device or machine that converts something from one form to another, such as an analog-to-digital converter or a catalytic converter.|This catalytic converter reduces harmful emissions from the car. 这个催化转换器减少了汽车的有害排放。|synonyms: transformer, adapter, transducer|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific type of converter)|noun
