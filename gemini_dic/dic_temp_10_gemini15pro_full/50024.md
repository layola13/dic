denominationally:/dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəli/
adv.
在教派方面；以面额计
From the point of view of different religious denominations; in terms of different denominations of currency.
Many churches work together denominationally on social issues. 许多教会在社会问题上以教派的方式合作。
Denominational differences should not divide us. 教派差异不应该分裂我们。
synonym:sectarian, factional
antonym:nondenominational, ecumenical
词性:副词
