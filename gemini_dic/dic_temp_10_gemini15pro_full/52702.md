digress:/daɪˈɡrɛs/ /dɪˈɡrɛs/
verb
离题，跑题
to deviate or wander away from the main topic or purpose in speaking or writing
The speaker digressed from his main point several times. 演讲者几次偏离了他的主题。

He digressed from the subject to tell a humorous anecdote. 他跑题讲了一个幽默的轶事。


synonym
deviate, stray, wander, ramble
antonym
focus, concentrate, stay on topic
word type
verb
