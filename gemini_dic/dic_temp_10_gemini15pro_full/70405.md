ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *figeten, frequentative of fiken (“to move restlessly, fidget”), from Old English ficol (“deceitful”), from Proto-Germanic *fīkuz (“eager, greedy”), from Proto-Indo-European *peygʷ- (“to try to persuade, beg”). More at fawning.
