distinctively:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪvli/
adjective
独特地，明显地
clearly different from others of the same kind
Her voice is distinctively deep. 她的声音明显低沉。
近义词: characteristically, uniquely, individually
反义词: typically, commonly, ordinarily
词性: adverb

distinctive:/dɪˈstɪŋktɪv/
adjective
独特的，有特色的
clearly different from others of the same kind
The zebra's distinctive stripes make it easily recognizable. 斑马独特的条纹使它很容易辨认。
近义词: characteristic, unique, individual
反义词: typical, common, ordinary
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *distinctīvus, from Latin distinct- (past participle stem of distinguere ‘distinguish’), from dis- ‘apart’ + stinguere ‘extinguish’.
