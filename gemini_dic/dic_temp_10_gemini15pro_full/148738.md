overtrims: /ˌoʊvərˈtrɪmz/| v. (overtrimmed, overtrimming)|过度修剪，修剪过短|To trim something excessively, cutting off more than is necessary or desirable.| The gardener overtrimmed the hedge, leaving it looking bare. 园丁把树篱修剪得太过头了，使它看起来光秃秃的。| 近义词: overcut, prune excessively| 反义词: undertrim|词性:动词

overtrims: /ˌoʊvərˈtrɪmz/| n. (plural overtrims)|过度修剪的部分|The excess amount that is trimmed off.| The overtrims from the hedge were collected and composted.  从树篱上修剪下来的多余部分被收集起来做成了堆肥。| 近义词: clippings, trimmings| 反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词
