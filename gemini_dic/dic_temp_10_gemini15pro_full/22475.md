bookmobiles:/ˈbʊkməˌbiːlz/| n. |流动图书馆；流动图书车|A vehicle designed as a traveling library, carrying books to areas where library facilities are limited.|The bookmobile visits our rural community once a week. 流动图书车每周来我们农村社区一次。|Synonyms: mobile library, traveling library|Antonyms: library (building), brick and mortar library|Noun
