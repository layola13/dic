mismanage:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒ/ | vt. | 管理不善，处置失当 | To manage badly or incompetently. | The company was mismanaged and eventually went bankrupt.  这家公司管理不善，最终破产了。| misgovern, mishandle | manage well, administer efficiently | verb

mismanagement:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 管理不善，处置失当 | The act or process of mismanaging. | The mismanagement of the project led to significant delays and cost overruns.  该项目的管理不善导致了严重的延误和成本超支。|  misgovernment, mishandling | good management, efficient administration | noun
