overcultivates:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌltɪveɪts/
verb
过度耕种, 过度培植
to cultivate (land) excessively, leading to depletion of soil nutrients.
The farmer overcultivated his fields, which resulted in poor yields the following year. 农民过度耕种了他的田地，导致来年产量很低。
overfarm, overuse
