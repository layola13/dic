non-apologetic:/ˌnɑːn əˌpɑːləˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
不道歉的，不辩解的，理直气壮的
Not feeling or expressing regret or remorse, especially for one's beliefs or actions.
She was unapologetic about her strong opinions. 她对自己的强烈观点毫不歉疚。
近义词：unrepentant, unapologetic, remorseless, unashamed
反义词：apologetic, remorseful, repentant, ashamed
词性: adjective

non-apologetic: /ˌnɒn əˌpɒləˈdʒetɪk/
adjective
不道歉的，不辩解的，理直气壮的
Not feeling or expressing regret or remorse, especially for one's beliefs or actions.
He remained non-apologetic about his controversial remarks. 他对自己的争议言论仍然没有表示歉意。
近义词：unrepentant, unapologetic, remorseless, unashamed
反义词：apologetic, remorseful, repentant, ashamed
词性: adjective

词源：
From non- + apologetic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Apologetic" comes from the Greek "apologia" meaning "a speech in defense."
