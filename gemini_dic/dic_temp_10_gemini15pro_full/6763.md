anamnesis:/ˌænæmˈniːsɪs/
n.
回忆, 记忆, (尤指) 病历, 既往病史
The process of recalling past events or experiences, especially in the context of medical history.
The doctor took a thorough anamnesis before making a diagnosis. 医生在做出诊断之前仔细询问了病史。
recollection, reminiscence, medical history
forgetting, amnesia
noun
