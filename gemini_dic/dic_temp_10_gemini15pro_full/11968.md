arterialization:/ɑːrˌtɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|动脉化|The process of making venous blood more like arterial blood, typically by increasing its oxygen content.  This can be achieved through various medical procedures.|The surgeon performed an arterialization procedure to improve blood flow to the limb.  外科医生进行了动脉化手术以改善肢体血流。|oxygenation, arterialization of venous blood|venous stasis|noun
