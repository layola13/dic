paradisaical:/ˌpærədɪˈsaɪɪkəl/| adj.|天堂般的，极乐的|Resembling paradise; heavenly.|The island boasts paradisaical beaches.  这个岛屿拥有天堂般的海滩。|近义词：heavenly, blissful, idyllic, utopian|反义词：hellish, infernal, nightmarish|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *paradisicalis, from Latin paradisus (“paradise”) + -alis (adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek παράδεισος (parádeisos, “park, paradise”).
