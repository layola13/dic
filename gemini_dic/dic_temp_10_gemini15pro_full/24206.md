bridesman:/ˈbraɪdzmən/| n. |男傧相|A male attendant of a bride at a wedding, typically a brother or close friend.|例句：My brother served as my bridesman at my wedding. 我的兄弟在我婚礼上担任了我的男傧相。

例句：He asked his best friend to be his bridesman. 他邀请他最好的朋友做他的男傧相。|近义词：groomsman, man of honor |反义词:bridesmaid|词性:noun
