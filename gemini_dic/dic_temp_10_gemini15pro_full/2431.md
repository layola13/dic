adjudicate:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/
verb
裁决，判定
To formally judge and settle a dispute or contest; to act as a judge in a competition.
The panel will adjudicate on the entries and select the winner.  评审小组将对参赛作品进行评判并选出获胜者。

The case was adjudicated in the High Court.  该案件在高等法院审理。

synonym:judge, decide, determine, arbitrate, referee, rule, resolve
antonym:defer, postpone, avoid, evade
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin adjudicat-, past participle stem of adjudicare "to award," from ad- "to" + judicare "to judge," from judex "judge."
