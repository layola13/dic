catheter: /ˈkæθɪtər/ (US) /ˈkæθɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |导管；插管|A thin tube inserted into a body cavity, duct, or vessel to permit injection or withdrawal of fluids or to keep a passage open.|The nurse inserted a catheter into the patient's bladder. 护士将导管插入病人的膀胱。|Synonyms: tube, cannula|Antonyms: None|Noun
