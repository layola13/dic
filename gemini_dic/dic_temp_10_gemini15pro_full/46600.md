damaskin:/ˈdæməskɪn/ | n. | 大马士革织物；大马士革钢刀 | A rich patterned fabric originally made in Damascus. A sword made of Damascus steel. |  例句：He wore a robe of fine damaskin. 他穿着一件精美的大马士革长袍。

例句：The damaskin sword gleamed in the sunlight.  大马士革钢刀在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词：brocade, damask | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


damaskin:/ˈdæməskɪn/ | adj. | 大马士革的；大马士革钢制的 | Made of or resembling damask or Damascus steel. | 例句：The collector proudly displayed his damaskin daggers. 收藏家自豪地展示了他的大马士革匕首。

例句：She admired the damaskin upholstery on the antique chair. 她欣赏古董椅子上的大马士革织物装饰。 | 近义词：damasked | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
