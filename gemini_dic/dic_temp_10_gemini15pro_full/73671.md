forget: /fərˈɡet/ (US), /fəˈɡet/ (UK) | v. | 忘记，遗忘 | To fail to remember; to lose the memory of something or someone. | I forgot to buy milk at the grocery store. 我忘记在杂货店买牛奶了。

I forgot her birthday. 我忘记了她的生日。

近义词：overlook, neglect, omit, disregard

反义词：remember, recall, recollect

词性：verb
