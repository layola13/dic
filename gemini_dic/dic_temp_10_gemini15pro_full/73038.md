词源： "foothot" is a somewhat archaic term, and its etymology is straightforward.  "Foot" refers to the feet, and "hot" refers to heat or quickness.  Together, they imply a quickness or rashness of the feet, hence acting impulsively.  It's likely related to the idea of "hot-headed."
