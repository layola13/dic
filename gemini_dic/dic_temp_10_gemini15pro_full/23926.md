breaks away:/breɪks əˈweɪ/
verb
脱离，逃脱，放弃，突然离开
To separate or detach oneself from something.  To escape from someone or something. To depart suddenly.

The cyclist broke away from the peloton and gained a significant lead.
这名自行车手从主车群中突围而出，取得了显著的领先优势。

The prisoner broke away from his captors during the transfer.
囚犯在转移过程中逃脱了抓捕。

He broke away from the family business to start his own company.
他脱离了家族企业，开始创办自己的公司。

近义词: escape, flee, depart, leave, secede
反义词: join, unite, stay, remain
词性: verb
