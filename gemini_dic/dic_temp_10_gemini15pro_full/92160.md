hypersocially:/ˌhaɪpərˈsoʊʃəli/
adjective
过度社交地
Excessively sociable; characterized by excessive social interaction.  Formed from "hyper-" (meaning "over, excessive") + "socially" (adverb form of "social").
She's become hypersocially active online since joining that new platform. 她自从加入那个新平台后，在网上变得过度活跃了。
He was hypersocially engaged at the party, talking to everyone in the room.  他在派对上过度社交，和房间里的每个人都交谈。
近义词:overly sociable, excessively gregarious
反义词:antisocial, withdrawn, introverted
词性:adjective
