PHRASE->
double up (as something):  兼作;  The sofa doubles up as a bed.  这个沙发也可以当床用。
double back:  往回走；折回； The fox doubled back to avoid the hunters.  狐狸为了躲避猎人而折回。
double down (on something):  加倍努力;  The company is doubling down on its efforts to attract new customers.  公司正在加倍努力吸引新客户。
double or nothing:  (赌博用语) 加倍或全输;  I'll flip this coin - double or nothing?  我来抛硬币——赌注加倍还是全输？
double take:  (因惊讶等) 再看一眼;  I did a double take when I saw her - she looked just like my sister.  我看到她时又看了一眼——她长得跟我姐姐一模一样。
