burnet:/ˈbɜːrnɪt/| n.| Burnet; Salad Burnet; Greater Burnet; Canadian Burnet|A Eurasian plant (Sanguisorba minor) of the rose family, having pinnate leaves with serrate leaflets and small, purplish flowers in dense heads. Its leaves are sometimes used in salads.  Also refers to other plants of the genus Sanguisorba.|The burnet added a cucumber-like flavor to the salad.|近义词：salad burnet, pimpernel|反义词:|noun
