outstriding:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪdɪŋ/
verb
大步超越，步幅超过
surpassing someone in length of stride when walking or running.
He outstriding his competitors on the track. 他在赛道上大步超越了他的竞争对手。
近义词：outrunning, outpacing, surpassing
反义词：lagging, trailing
词性：v.


outstriding:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪdɪŋ/
adjective
大步超越的，步幅超过的
Characterized by outstriding; having or taking a longer stride than another.
His outstriding gait gave him an advantage. 他大步流星的步态给了他优势。
近义词：long-striding
反义词：short-striding
词性：adj.


词源:  From out- + striding, present participle of stride.
