nonrehabilitative:/ˌnɒnˌriːəˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
非康复性的，非复原的
Not intended to rehabilitate; not promoting rehabilitation or recovery.
The prison focuses on punishment and offers few nonrehabilitative programs.  这所监狱注重惩罚，几乎不提供任何非康复性项目。
His injuries required nonrehabilitative surgery, simply to alleviate the pain. 他的伤势需要非康复性手术，只是为了减轻疼痛。
近义词:  non-restorative, punitive, correctional
反义词: rehabilitative, restorative, therapeutic
词性: adjective
