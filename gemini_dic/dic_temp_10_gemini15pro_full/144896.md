over-shortens:/ˈoʊvərˈʃɔːrtn/
verb
过度缩短
To shorten something excessively, to the point where it becomes too short or ineffective.
The tailor over-shortened the sleeves, making the jacket look ridiculous.  裁缝把袖子过度缩短了，让夹克看起来很滑稽。
He over-shortened the story, leaving out important details. 他把故事过度缩短了，遗漏了重要的细节。
近义词: truncate, curtail, abbreviate
反义词: lengthen, extend, prolong
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, excessively".
"-shorten" from "short" (Old English sceort) + "-en" (suffix forming verbs).  The combination signifies making something excessively short.
