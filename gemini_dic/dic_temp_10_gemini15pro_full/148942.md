overwrite:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪt/ | v. | 覆盖，重写 | To record new data over existing data, effectively replacing the original data. | I accidentally overwrote the file with an older version. 我不小心用旧版本覆盖了文件。

| The new software update overwrote my customized settings. 新的软件更新覆盖了我的自定义设置。 | 近义词: replace, supersede, erase | 反义词: restore, recover | verb

overwrite:/ˈoʊvərˌraɪt/ | n. | 覆盖，重写（名词形式较少用）|  The act of overwriting. | There was an overwrite of the original data.  原始数据被覆盖了。 | The overwrite caused a loss of information. 覆盖导致信息丢失。| 近义词: replacement | 反义词: restoration | noun
