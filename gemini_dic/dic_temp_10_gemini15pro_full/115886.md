marmalade:/ˈmɑːrməleɪd/| n. |橘子酱；类似橘子酱的果酱|A preserve made from citrus fruit, especially oranges, prepared with sugar and water.|例句：I love marmalade on toast. 我喜欢吐司面包涂橘子酱。

例句：She makes her own marmalade every year. 她每年都自己做橘子酱。|近义词：jam, preserve, conserve|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
