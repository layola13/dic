diacritical:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 变音符号的; 带变音符号的 |  Relating to or using a diacritical mark. | The diacritical marks on the vowels change their pronunciation. 元音上的变音符号会改变它们的发音. | accentual, orthographic | plain | adjective

diacritic:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɪtɪk/ | n. | 变音符号 | A mark added to a letter to indicate a special pronunciation. |  The acute accent is a common diacritic. 尖音符是一种常见的变音符号。| accent, cedilla, circumflex | letter | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *diacritique*, from Ancient Greek διακριτικός (diakritikós, “distinguishing”), from διακρίνω (diakrínō, “I distinguish”).
