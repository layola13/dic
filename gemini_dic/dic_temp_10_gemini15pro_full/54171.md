disentrammel:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtræməl/ | v. | 解放，使摆脱束缚 | To free from entanglement or restraint; to liberate. |  The company sought to disentrammel itself from burdensome regulations.  这家公司试图摆脱繁重的规章制度。

The artist disentrammeled her creativity from the constraints of traditional techniques.  这位艺术家将她的创造力从传统技术的束缚中解放出来。 | 近义词: liberate, free, extricate, unfetter, release | 反义词: restrict, confine, restrain, shackle, bind | 词性: 动词
