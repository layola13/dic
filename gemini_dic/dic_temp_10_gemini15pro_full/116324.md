masterhood:/ˈmæstərˌhʊd/| n.|大师地位，精通，掌握|The state of being a master or having mastery over something.  A high level of skill or knowledge in a particular subject or activity.|She finally achieved masterhood in her chosen craft. 她终于在她选择的工艺中达到了大师水平。|Synonym: expertise, proficiency, mastery, command, skill,  |Antonym: incompetence, inability, ineptitude|noun
