bicoastalism:/ˌbaɪˈkoʊstəlɪzəm/
n.
双海岸生活；两岸生活；横跨东西海岸的生活方式
The practice or state of having homes or offices on both the East Coast and West Coast of the United States.
Many Hollywood actors practice bicoastalism, maintaining residences in both Los Angeles and New York.  许多好莱坞演员过着双海岸生活，在洛杉矶和纽约都拥有住所。
近义词：bi-coastal life, dual residency
反义词:  localized living, single residency
