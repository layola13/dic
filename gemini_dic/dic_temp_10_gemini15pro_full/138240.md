nostalgia:/nɒˈstældʒə/ (US) /nɑːˈstældʒə/ (UK)| n. |怀旧，念旧|A sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations.|例句：A wave of nostalgia swept over her as she looked at old photographs. 当她看着老照片时，一股怀旧之情涌上心头。

例句：He felt a pang of nostalgia for his college days. 他对大学时光感到一丝怀念。|近义词：remembrance, reminiscence, sentimentality|反义词:apathy, indifference|词性:noun
