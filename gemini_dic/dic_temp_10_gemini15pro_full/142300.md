orthocone:/ˈɔːrθəˌkəʊn/| n. |直角石|A straight, conical fossil shell of an extinct cephalopod mollusk of the order Orthocerida, common in Paleozoic rocks.|The museum displayed a large orthocone fossil. 博物馆展出了一块大型直角石化石。|近义词: nautiloid (广义上的鹦鹉螺类)|反义词: None (由于是特定物种，没有直接的反义词)|noun
