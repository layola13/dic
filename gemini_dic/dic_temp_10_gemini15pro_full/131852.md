nonconstricting:/ˌnɑːnkənˈstrɪktɪŋ/
adjective
非紧缩的，不束缚的
Not tight; allowing freedom of movement.
例句：She prefers nonconstricting clothing for yoga. 她更喜欢穿不束缚的衣服练瑜伽。

例句：The doctor recommended nonconstricting bandages for the injury. 医生建议用不紧的绷带包扎伤口。

近义词：loose, comfortable, unrestrictive
反义词：tight, constricting, binding
词性：adjective
