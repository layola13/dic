albeit:/ɔːlˈbiːɪt/
conjunction
虽然，即使
though or although
He tried to remain cheerful, albeit with little success. 他试图保持愉快，尽管不太成功。
synonym:although, though, even though, notwithstanding, while
antonym:none (albeit introduces a concession, not a direct opposite)
词性:连词
