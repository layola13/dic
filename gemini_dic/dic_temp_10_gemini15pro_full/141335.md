opens:/ˈoʊpənz/ (American) /ˈəʊpənz/ (British)| v. |打开，开放，开始，展现|Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "open".  It describes the action of something becoming unclosed, accessible, or available.|The store opens at 9 am.  这家店早上九点开门。|The flower opens its petals to the sun. 这朵花向太阳张开花瓣。|近义词：unlocks, unfastens, commences, begins, reveals|反义词：closes, shuts, concludes, ends, conceals|词性:动词


词源:
From Middle English *openen*, from Old English *openian*, from Proto-Germanic *upanjaną*, from Proto-Indo-European *up-*.  Related to up.
