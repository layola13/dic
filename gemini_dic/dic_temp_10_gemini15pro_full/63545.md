envelope:/ˈenvələʊp/ (UK), /ˈɑːnvəloʊp/ (US)| n.|信封；封皮；包层|a flat paper container with a sealable flap, used to enclose a letter or document.|I sealed the letter in the envelope and put a stamp on it. 我把信装进信封，贴上邮票。|Synonyms: wrapper, covering, jacket|Antonyms: contents (内容物)|noun

envelope:/ɪnˈveləp/ (UK), /ɪnˈveləp/ (US)| vt.|包住；包围；笼罩|to wrap up, cover, or surround completely.|A thick fog enveloped the city. 浓雾笼罩着城市。|Synonyms: surround, encase, enfold|Antonyms: uncover, expose|verb
