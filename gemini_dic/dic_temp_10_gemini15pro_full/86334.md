hecki:/ˈhɛki/| interj.|见鬼，我去，我的天| An exclamation of surprise, frustration, or annoyance, similar to "heck."  It's considered a mild oath.|例句：Hecki, I forgot my keys!  见鬼，我忘了带钥匙！

例句：Hecki, this traffic is terrible!  我的天，这交通真糟糕！|近义词：heck, darn, gosh, shoot|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection of this type) |词性:感叹词
