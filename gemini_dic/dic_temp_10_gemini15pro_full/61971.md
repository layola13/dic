en-suite:/ˌɒn ˈswiːt/ /ˌɑːn ˈswiːt/| n.|套间；|A bathroom that is directly connected to a bedroom.|例句：We booked a room with an en-suite. 我们预订了一个带套间的房间。|近义词：attached bathroom, private bathroom|反义词:shared bathroom, communal bathroom|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *en suite* meaning "in sequence" or "following."  Originally used to describe things that followed one another, the term evolved to describe a bathroom attached to a bedroom, implying the bathroom follows directly from the bedroom.
