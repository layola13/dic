immitigable:/ɪˈmɪtɪɡəbl/
adjective
无法缓和的，不能减轻的
(of something bad) impossible to lessen the severity or remove the cause of.
例句：His grief was immitigable after the loss of his child. 他失去孩子后悲痛欲绝，无法缓解。

例句：The damage caused by the hurricane was immitigable. 飓风造成的损害无法弥补。

近义词：unappeasable, relentless, implacable, inexorable, irremediable
反义词：mitigable, appeasable, remediable
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *immitigabilis, from in- "not" + mitigabilis "capable of being mitigated," from the verb mitigare "to soften, alleviate, appease," from mitis "soft, mild" + -igare (frequentative suffix).
