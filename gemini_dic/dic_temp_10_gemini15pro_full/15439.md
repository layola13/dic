bad-assed:/ˌbædˈæst/ /ˌbædˈɑːst/ | adj. | 厉害的，强悍的，不好惹的 |  (Slang)  Tough, uncompromising, and intimidating; impressively skillful or powerful. Originating from "bad ass," referring to a stubborn or difficult donkey/mule, it evolved to describe a person with similar traits, then broadened to encompass general skill and coolness. |  He's a bad-assed guitarist. 他是一位非常厉害的吉他手。|  Synonyms: tough, awesome, hardcore, badass, kick-ass | Antonyms: weak, timid, cowardly | adjective

bad-ass:/ˈbædæs/ /ˈbædɑːs/ | n. |  坏蛋，捣蛋鬼；厉害的人  | (Slang)  A tough, uncompromising, or intimidating person. Originally a derogatory term for a stubborn donkey, it later became associated with rebellious or defiant individuals and eventually evolved to sometimes describe someone exceptionally skilled or cool. | That guy's a real bad-ass. 那家伙是个十足的坏蛋/狠角色。| Synonyms: tough guy, rebel, renegade, badass | Antonyms: wimp, coward, softy | noun
