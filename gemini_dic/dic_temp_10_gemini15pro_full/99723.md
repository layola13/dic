interluding:/ˌɪntərˈluːdɪŋ/| v. |插入，穿插；间奏；|Playing a short piece of music interluding the main performance.  在主要演出中穿插演奏一小段音乐。|Synonyms: inserting, intercalating, interposing, interrupting|Antonyms: continuing, extending, prolonging|verb

interlude:/ˈɪntərˌluːd/| n. |插曲，间奏；幕间休息|The comedian provided a humorous interlude between the two acts of the play.  喜剧演员在戏剧的两幕之间表演了一段幽默的插曲。|Synonyms: interval, intermission, break, pause|Antonyms: continuation, prolongation|noun
