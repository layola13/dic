orthotropic:/ˌɔːrθəˈtrɒpɪk/| adj.|正各向异性的|Having different physical properties in different directions, especially having three mutually perpendicular planes of symmetry.|例句：Wood is an orthotropic material, exhibiting different stiffness and strength along its grain, radial, and tangential directions. 木材是一种正交各向异性材料，沿其纹理、径向和切向表现出不同的刚度和强度。

例句：The orthotropic nature of composite materials allows engineers to tailor their properties for specific applications. 复合材料的正交各向异性使工程师能够根据特定应用定制其性能。|近义词：anisotropic|反义词：isotropic|词性:形容词
