low-key:/ˌloʊˈkiː/| adj. adv.|低调的，低调地|Not elaborate or showy; understated; quiet.  Also, relaxed and calm.|例句：They had a low-key wedding ceremony with only close family and friends. 他们举行了一个低调的婚礼，只有亲密的家人和朋友参加。

例句：She wanted to keep the party low-key, so she only invited a few people. 她想让派对保持低调，所以只邀请了几个人。|近义词：understated, subtle, quiet, relaxed, chill|反义词：ostentatious, flamboyant, showy, loud|词性:形容词，副词
