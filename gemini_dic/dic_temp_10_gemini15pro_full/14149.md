authorization:/ˌɔːθərəɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |授权，批准|The act of giving official permission or approval for something.|例句：You need authorization to access these files. 你需要授权才能访问这些文件。

例句：He received authorization to proceed with the project. 他获得了继续进行该项目的授权。|近义词：permission, sanction, approval, consent |反义词：prohibition, denial, refusal|词性:名词


词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin *authorizatio(n-), from the verb auctorizare (see authorize).
