automata:/ɔːˈtɒmətə/ /ɔːˈtɑːmətə/ | n. | 自动机；机器人 | A self-operating machine or control mechanism designed to follow automatically a predetermined sequence of operations or respond to predetermined instructions. |  The factory uses automata to perform repetitive tasks.  这家工厂使用自动机来执行重复性任务。| synonyms: robots, machines, androids | antonyms: humans, manual laborers | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *automatum*, from Greek *αὐτόματον* (automaton, “self-acting thing”), neuter of *αὐτόματος* (automatos, “acting of one’s own will”), from *αὐτός* (autos, “self”) + *μάω* (maō, “to strive after”).
