bluenose:/ˈbluːnoʊz/| n.|禁酒主义者；(尤指)假正经的人；严谨的人；新斯科舍省人|A person, especially one from Nova Scotia, or a ship from Nova Scotia. Also refers to someone who is excessively puritanical, or who advocates strict moral standards.|例句：He was known as a bit of a bluenose in the town, always complaining about the pubs staying open late.  他在这座小镇上有点假正经的名声，总是抱怨酒吧营业到很晚。

例句：The Bluenose II, a replica of the famous racing schooner, is a symbol of Nova Scotia.  布鲁诺斯二号，这艘著名竞速帆船的复制品，是新斯科舍省的象征。|近义词：puritan, moralist, prude, Nova Scotian|反义词：libertine, hedonist|词性:名词

bluenose:/ˈbluːnoʊz/| adj.|禁酒的；严谨的；新斯科舍省的|Relating to Nova Scotia, or characterized by strict moral standards.|例句：The bluenose laws prohibited the sale of alcohol on Sundays.  禁酒法禁止在星期日出售酒精。

例句：She gave him a bluenose stare when he mentioned going to the casino. 当他提到要去赌场时，她给了他一个严厉的眼神。|近义词：puritanical, strict, moralistic|反义词：permissive, lenient|词性:形容词
