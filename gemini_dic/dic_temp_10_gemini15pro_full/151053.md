paraphrased:/ˈpærəˌfreɪzd/| v. |释义的，改述的|Expressed in different words, especially to achieve greater clarity or concision.  Rewording something that has already been written or spoken. |She paraphrased the poem in modern English. 她用现代英语改述了这首诗。|Synonyms: reworded, restated, rephrased, summarized, epitomized|Antonyms: quoted, copied, plagiarized|Verb

paraphrase:/ˈpærəˌfreɪz/| n. v. |释义，改述|n. A rewording of something written or spoken by someone else. v. To express something using different words, especially to achieve greater clarity. |He gave a paraphrase of the speech. 他对演讲做了释义。/  Can you paraphrase what he just said? 你能解释一下他刚才说的话吗？|Synonyms: rewording, restatement, rendition, version, interpretation (n.); reword, restate, rephrase, summarize, epitomize (v.)|Antonyms: quote, copy, plagiarize (v.)|Noun, Verb
