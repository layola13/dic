lowland:/ˈləʊlənd/ (UK), /ˈloʊlənd/ (US)| n. adj.|低地，低洼地；低地的|An area of land that is lower than the surrounding country.|The Netherlands is known for its flat, lowland terrain. 荷兰以其平坦的低地地形而闻名。|The farmers grew crops in the fertile lowlands. 农民们在肥沃的低地种植庄稼。|近义词：valley, plain, bottomland|反义词：highland, upland|词性: 名词, 形容词
词源: From Middle English *loweland*, equivalent to low +‎ land.
