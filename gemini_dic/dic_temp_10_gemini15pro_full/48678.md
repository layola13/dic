defero:/dɪˈfɛrəʊ/ (UK), /dɪˈfɜːroʊ/ (US)| vt.|给予，授予；提出；把…归于；|He deferred the decision to his colleagues. 他把决定权交给了他的同事们。|Synonyms: bestow, confer, grant, entrust, ascribe, attribute|Antonyms: withhold, deny, refuse, retain|verb

defero:/dɪˈfɛrəʊ/ (UK), /dɪˈfɜːroʊ/ (US)| vt.|尊重，服从；|He deferred to his elders. 他尊重他的长辈。|Synonyms: respect, obey, comply with, yield to, submit to|Antonyms: disobey, defy, resist|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin defero ("bring down, carry away, transfer; offer, proffer; confer, bestow; report, accuse; submit, yield, defer"), from de- ("down, away") + fero ("bear, carry").
