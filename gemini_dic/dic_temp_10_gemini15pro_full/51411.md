deterability:/dɪˌtɜːrəˈbɪləti/
noun
可威慑性，遏制性
The quality of being able to be deterred.  The ease with which someone or something can be deterred.
The deterability of nuclear weapons has prevented large-scale wars. 核武器的威慑力阻止了大规模战争的爆发。
synonyms: dissuadability, preventability
antonyms:  inducement, encouragement
词性: 名词
