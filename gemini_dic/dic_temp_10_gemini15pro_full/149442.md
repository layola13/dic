pacu:/ˈpækuː/| n. |帕库鱼|A South American freshwater fish related to the piranha, but with flatter, human-like teeth.  It is herbivorous and primarily eats plants and nuts.|Pacu are often mistakenly sold as piranhas. 帕库鱼经常被误认为是食人鱼出售。|Pacu are native to the Amazon basin. 帕库鱼原产于亚马逊河流域。|近义词:tambaqui (a related species)|反义词:None|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Tupi *paku. 最终源自图皮语 *paku.
