coronate:/ˈkɒrəneɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrəneɪt/ (US)| v. adj.|加冕，冠以…|To crown; to invest with regal power and dignity; also, to honor, reward, distinguish, or complete. |例句：The king was coronated in a grand ceremony. 国王在一个盛大的仪式上加冕。

例句：Her career was coronated with a Nobel Prize.  她的职业生涯以诺贝尔奖加冕。|近义词：crown, enthrone, invest|反义词:dethrone, depose|词性:动词, 形容词

coronate:/ˈkɒrəneɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrəneɪt/ (US)| adj.|加冕的，冠状的|Crowned, as with a crown; having a corona.|例句：The coronate monarch addressed his subjects. 加冕的君主向他的臣民讲话。

例句：Some viruses have a coronate structure. 一些病毒具有冠状结构。|近义词：crowned, crested|反义词:uncrowned|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *coronatus*, past participle of *coronare* "to crown," from *corona* "crown, garland, wreath."
