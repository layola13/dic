gentianose:/ˌdʒɛnʃiˈənoʊs/
|n.|龙胆三糖|A trisaccharide composed of two glucose units and one fructose unit, found in the roots of gentian plants.|Gentianose is a reserve carbohydrate in gentians.|近义词:trisaccharide (泛指三糖)|反义词: None (没有确切的反义词，可以考虑单糖 monosaccharide)|词性:noun
