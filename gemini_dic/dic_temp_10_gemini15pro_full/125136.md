multi-platinum:/ˌmʌltiˈplætɪnəm/
adjective
多白金的
Certified as having sold a specified number of copies (usually more than two million).  Indicates a high level of commercial success in the music industry.
The singer's multi-platinum album cemented her status as a global superstar.  这位歌手的多白金专辑巩固了她作为全球巨星的地位。
Synonyms:  best-selling, blockbuster, chart-topping
Antonyms:  unsuccessful, unpopular, flop
