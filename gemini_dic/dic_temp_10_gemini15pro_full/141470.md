opinionable:/əˈpɪnjənəbl/| adj.|可评论的，有争议的|Open to discussion or debate; based on opinion rather than fact.  Allowing or susceptible to personal opinion. |Her interpretation of the poem is highly opinionable. 她对这首诗的解读很有争议。|近义词：debatable, disputable, arguable, contestable|反义词：undisputable, incontestable, factual, objective|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From opinion + -able.  Meaning "capable of being held as an opinion" is from the late 19th century.
