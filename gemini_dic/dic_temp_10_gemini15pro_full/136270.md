nonrepetitiously:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛpɪˈtɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 非重复地，不重复地 | In a way that avoids repetition; without repeating anything. | She explained the process nonrepetitiously, ensuring everyone understood each step clearly. 她非重复地解释了这个过程，确保每个人都清楚地理解每一步。 | Synonyms: uniquely, distinctly, originally | Antonyms: repetitively, redundantly | Adverb

Etymology:  From "non-" (not) + "repetitious" (characterized by repetition). "Repetitious" comes from the Latin "repetere" (to repeat).
