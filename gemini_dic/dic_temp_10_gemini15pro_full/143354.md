outfabling:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪblɪŋ/
verb
胜过；以讲故事的方式胜过
surpassing in the art of storytelling; telling a more fantastic or imaginative story than another.
The bard outfabled his rival, captivating the audience with tales of dragons and magic.  吟游诗人以讲述龙和魔法的故事吸引了观众，胜过了他的对手。
Synonyms: outdoing, excelling, surpassing, eclipsing
Antonyms: failing, losing, being surpassed
