neons:/ˈniːɒnz/ /ˈniːɑːnz/
n.|霓虹灯; 霓虹色彩|Bright lights, typically multicolored, produced by filling glass tubes with neon or other gases and passing an electric current through them.  Also, vibrant, fluorescent colors reminiscent of these lights.|The city was alive with the glow of neons. 城市里霓虹灯闪烁，充满活力。|近义词:fluorescent lights, bright lights|反义词:dull colors, pastel colors|词性:名词
