hypobole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ /haɪˈpɜːrboʊli/
n.
夸张法, 夸张的语句
an extravagant statement or figure of speech not intended to be taken literally, as “to wait an eternity.”
例句：He used a hyperbole to describe his fishing adventure, claiming he caught a fish as big as a boat. 他用夸张的手法描述他的钓鱼冒险，声称他钓到了一条像船一样大的鱼。

例句：Her claim that she could run faster than a cheetah was a clear hyperbole. 她声称自己跑得比猎豹还快，这显然是夸张的说法。

近义词：exaggeration, overstatement
反义词：understatement, litotes
