cashable:/ˈkæʃəbl/| adj.|可兑现的，可兑成现金的|Able to be exchanged for cash.|例句：Are these vouchers cashable at the bank? 这些代金券可以在银行兑现吗？|例句：The bonds are cashable after one year. 这些债券一年后可以兑现。|近义词：redeemable, negotiable|反义词:non-cashable, non-redeemable|词性:形容词
词源: From "cash" + "-able".  "Cash" ultimately derives from Old French "casse" (box, money box).
