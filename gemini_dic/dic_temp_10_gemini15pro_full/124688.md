moviegoer:/ˈmuːviˌɡoʊər/| n. |电影观众，常看电影的人|A person who goes to the cinema/movies frequently.|例句：The moviegoer was thrilled by the special effects.  这位电影观众对特效感到兴奋。

例句：Moviegoers lined up outside the theater for the premiere.  电影观众在剧院外排队等候首映。|近义词：filmgoer, cinemagoer, movie fan, cinephile|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "homebody" or someone who avoids movies) |词性:noun
