baths:/bɑːðz/
n.
浴室；浴缸；洗澡；浴疗
plural of bath
例句：The house has two baths. 这房子有两个浴室。

例句：She takes a bath every evening. 她每天晚上都洗澡。

近义词：restrooms, toilets, lavatories (for bathrooms), tubs (for bathtubs), soaks (for the act of bathing)
反义词：showers
词性:名词


bath:/bɑːθ/
n.
浴室；浴缸；洗澡；浴疗
An act of washing one's body in a bathtub filled with water. A container for water in which to wash one's body.
例句：I’m going to have/take a bath. 我要去洗澡。

例句：The hotel room had a private bath. 酒店房间里有私人浴室。

近义词：wash, shower, dip
反义词：shower
词性:名词

词源:
From Old English *bæþ (attested only as a prefix in bæþhūs "bathhouse"), from Proto-Germanic *baþą ("bath"), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂- ("to warm, heat"). Cognate with Old Frisian beth, Old Saxon bath, Old High German bad, Old Norse bað, Gothic 𐌱𐌰𐌸 (baþ).
