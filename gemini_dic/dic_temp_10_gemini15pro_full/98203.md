insectiferous:/ˌɪnsekˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
adj. 携带昆虫的；多昆虫的
Bearing or producing insects.
例句：The insectiferous plant trapped a fly.  这种食虫植物捕获了一只苍蝇。

例句：The jungle was dark and insectiferous.  丛林里阴暗且有很多昆虫。

近义词：insect-bearing, insect-ridden
反义词：insect-free, insectless
词性：adjective

词源: From Latin *insectífer, from insectum (“insect”) + -fer (“-bearing”).
