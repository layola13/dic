cornerstone:/ˈkɔːrnərˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈkɔːnəstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |基石，奠基石，基础|Something of great importance that everything else depends on.  A stone that forms part of a corner in a building, joining two walls. |例句：
Trust is the cornerstone of any good relationship.
信任是任何良好关系的基石。

The cornerstone of the new library was laid by the mayor.
新图书馆的基石由市长奠定。|近义词：foundation, base, basis, bedrock, underpinning|反义词: apex, peak|词性: noun
