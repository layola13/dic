obole:/ˈɒbəʊl (UK), ˈoʊboʊl (US)| n.| ὀβολός (古希腊货币单位), 小额捐款, 微不足道的量|A coin of small value, especially one used in ancient Greece.  A very small amount of money or a meager contribution. |Example 1:  The widow offered her last obol to the temple. 寡妇把最后一枚小钱捐给了寺庙。

Example 2:  He contributed his obol to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐献了一小笔钱。|Synonym: pittance, trifle, mite, modicum|Antonym: fortune, wealth|Noun
