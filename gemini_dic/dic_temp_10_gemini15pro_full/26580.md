cabotage:/ˈkæbətɑːʒ/ /ˈkæbətɑːdʒ/
n.
国内沿海贸易；沿海航行；国内空运
The transport of goods or passengers between two points in the same country by a vessel or aircraft registered in another country.
Cabotage laws restrict foreign airlines from operating domestic flights within a country.  国内空运法限制外国航空公司在国内运营国内航班。
The new regulations will liberalize cabotage in the shipping industry. 新规将放宽航运业的沿海运输限制。
近义词:coastal shipping, coastal trade, domestic shipping, domestic aviation
反义词:international trade, international shipping, international aviation
词性:noun

词源:
From French cabotage, from caboter 'to sail along the coast,' from cabe 'headland, cape.'
