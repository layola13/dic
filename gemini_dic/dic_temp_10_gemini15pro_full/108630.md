leadingly:/ˈliːdɪŋli/| adv.|居领导地位地，带头地，率先地|In a manner that suggests leadership or guidance; taking the initiative.  Indicates performing an action in a way that shows guidance or initiative.|He leadingly stepped forward to address the crowd. 他带头走上前向人群发表讲话。|近义词：dominantly, influentially, pioneeringly|反义词:submissively, passively|词性:副词
词源:From "leading" (present participle of "lead") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Lead" comes from Old English "lædan."
