hard-edged:/ˌhɑːrdˈɛdʒd/
adjective
轮廓鲜明的，线条硬朗的；尖锐的，不妥协的
clearly defined; sharp and uncompromising
The photographs had a hard-edged quality, capturing the harsh reality of life on the streets. 这些照片有一种鲜明的轮廓，捕捉到了街头生活的残酷现实。
His hard-edged style of negotiation alienated many potential allies. 他强硬的谈判风格疏远了许多潜在的盟友。
近义词:sharp, crisp, clear-cut, uncompromising, inflexible
反义词:soft, blurry, vague, compromising, flexible
词性:adjective
词源:Compound of "hard" and "edged."  "Hard" meaning firm and unyielding, and "edged" referring to having a sharp, distinct boundary.
