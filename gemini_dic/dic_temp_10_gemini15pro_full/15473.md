badgering:/ˈbædʒərɪŋ/ | n. v. | 獾的骚扰；纠缠不休 | The persistent questioning or harassment of someone, like a badger relentlessly digging at its prey. |  He was tired of the constant badgering from reporters. 他厌倦了记者们不断的纠缠。| 近义词：harassing, pestering, hounding | 反义词：leaving alone, ignoring | 词性: 名词/动词
Etymology: From badger (n.), referring to the animal's persistent digging and hunting habits. 最初来源于名词“badger”（獾），指的是獾持续挖掘和狩猎的习惯。
