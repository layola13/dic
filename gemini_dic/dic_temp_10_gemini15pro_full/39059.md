conformation's:/ˌkɑːnfɔːrˈmeɪʃənz/
noun
构造，形态，构象
The three-dimensional arrangement of atoms in a molecule, especially of a protein or nucleic acid.
The protein's conformation changes when it binds to its target.  这种蛋白质与其靶标结合时，构象会发生变化。
shape, structure, form, arrangement
deformation, distortion

词性:n.
