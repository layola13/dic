fluster:/ˈflʌstər/ | v. | 使慌乱，使不安，使窘迫 | To make someone nervous and confused, especially by giving them a lot to do or think about. |  The unexpected question flustered her.  突如其来的问题让她慌了神。| Synonyms: unsettle, agitate, disconcert | Antonyms: calm, compose, soothe | verb

flustered:/ˈflʌstərd/ | adj. | 慌乱的，不安的，窘迫的 |  Feeling nervous and confused, especially because you have been given a lot to do or think about. | She seemed a little flustered by all the attention.  她似乎被大家的关注弄得有点不知所措。| Synonyms: nervous, agitated, disconcerted | Antonyms: calm, composed, relaxed | adjective
