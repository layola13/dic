bourke:/bɜːrk/ | n. | Bourke鹦鹉; Bourke's parrot; 波克镇 | A type of small parrot native to Australia, known for its pinkish plumage.  It is named after Sir Richard Bourke, a former governor of New South Wales. | I saw a bourke at the aviary. 我在鸟舍里看到了一只波克鹦鹉。 | synonym:  pink-bellied parrot, crimson-winged parrot | antonym: N/A | noun
