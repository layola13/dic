beautification:/ˌbjuːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|美化；装饰；修饰|The beautification of the city involved planting trees and flowers.  这个城市的美化工作包括种植树木和花卉。|例句：The beautification project transformed the neglected park into a vibrant oasis.  美化项目将荒废的公园变成了充满活力的绿洲。

例句：The city council allocated funds for the beautification of the main street. 市议会拨款用于美化主要街道。

近义词：embellishment, ornamentation, enhancement, improvement|反义词：uglification, deterioration, neglect|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *beatification, from Late Latin beautificātiō (“a making beautiful”), from Latin beautificātus, past participle of beautificō (“I beautify”), from Latin bellus (“beautiful”) + -ficō (suffix forming verbs from adjectives).  Surface analysis is beautify + -fication.
