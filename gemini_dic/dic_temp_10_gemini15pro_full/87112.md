herb:/ɜːrb/ (US) /hɜːrb/ (UK)| n.|药草；香草；草本植物|A plant whose leaves, stems, or flowers are used for flavoring food, in medicine, or for their scent.|She grows herbs in her garden. 她在花园里种植香草。|Synonyms: spice, plant, botanical|Antonyms: artificial flavoring|noun


herb:/ɜːrb/ (US) /hɜːrb/ (UK)| n.|草本植物（尤指药用或调味植物）|Any seed-bearing plant that does not have a woody stem and dies down to the ground after flowering.|Many herbs have medicinal properties. 许多草本植物具有药用价值。|Synonyms: herbage, vegetation|Antonyms: tree, shrub|noun
