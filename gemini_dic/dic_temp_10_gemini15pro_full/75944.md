fungus:/ˈfʌŋɡəs/ (plural: fungi /ˈfʌŋɡaɪ/ or funguses)| n. |真菌；霉菌；菌类|Any of a group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter, including molds, yeast, mushrooms, and toadstools.|The damp wood was covered in fungus.|Synonyms: mold, mildew, yeast, mushroom, toadstool|Antonyms: None|Noun

fungi:/ˈfʌŋɡaɪ/| n. |真菌；霉菌；菌类 (fungus的复数形式)|Plural form of fungus.|Many fungi are beneficial to the environment.|Synonyms: molds, mildews, yeasts, mushrooms, toadstools|Antonyms: None|Noun

funguses:/ˈfʌŋɡəsəz/| n. |真菌；霉菌；菌类 (fungus的复数形式)|Plural form of fungus (less common than fungi).|The biologist studied various funguses in the lab.|Synonyms: molds, mildews, yeasts, mushrooms, toadstools|Antonyms: None|Noun
