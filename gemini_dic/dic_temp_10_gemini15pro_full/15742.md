bald-faced:/ˌbɔːldˈfeɪst/ | adj. | 公然的，明显的 |  Open and without shame or disguise. |  It was a bald-faced attempt to steal the election. 这是明目张胆地企图窃取选举结果。 | blatant, overt, unconcealed |  hidden, covert, disguised | adjective
