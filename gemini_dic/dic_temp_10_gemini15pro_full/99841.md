intermix:/ˌɪntərˈmɪks/ | v.  |混合，掺杂|To mix together; blend.|例句：The artist intermixed different colors to create a unique shade. 艺术家混合不同的颜色以创造独特的色调。|例句：He intermixed humor with serious advice in his speech.  他在演讲中穿插了幽默和严肃的建议。|近义词：mix, blend, mingle, combine |反义词:separate, divide, sort |词性:动词
intermix:/ˌɪntərˈmɪks/ | n.  |混合，混合物|A mixture of different things.|例句：The play was an intermix of comedy and tragedy.  这出戏是喜剧和悲剧的混合体。|例句：The crowd was an intermix of locals and tourists.  人群中既有当地人也有游客。|近义词：mixture, blend, combination, amalgamation |反义词:separation, division |词性:名词
