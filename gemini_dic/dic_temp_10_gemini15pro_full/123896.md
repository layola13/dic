mordant:/ˈmɔːrdənt/ (US) /ˈmɔːdənt/ (UK)| adj. n.| 尖刻的，辛辣的; 媒染剂 |
Having or showing a sharp or critical quality; a substance used to fix dyes on fabrics.|
Her mordant wit made her a popular, if sometimes feared, guest at parties. 她的尖刻的机智使她成为一个受欢迎的，即使有时令人害怕的派对客人。|
Synonyms:  caustic, biting, incisive, trenchant, acerbic, sardonic|
Antonyms:  bland, mild, gentle, kind, complimentary|
Etymology:  From Old French *mordant* (present participle of *mordre* "to bite"), from Latin *mordēre* "to bite."|
adjective

mordant:/ˈmɔːrdənt/ (US) /ˈmɔːdənt/ (UK)| n.| 媒染剂 |
A substance used to fix dyes on fabrics.|
The mordant helped the dye adhere to the wool. 这种媒染剂帮助染料附着在羊毛上。|
Synonyms:  fixative, dye fixative|
Antonyms: N/A|
Etymology:  From Old French *mordant* (present participle of *mordre* "to bite"), from Latin *mordēre* "to bite."|
