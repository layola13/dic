non-testable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛstəbl/ | adj. | 不可测试的 | Not able to be tested or verified through experimentation or assessment. |

例句：
The hypothesis about the existence of parallel universes is currently non-testable.
关于平行宇宙存在的假设目前是不可测试的。


This philosophical concept is non-testable through empirical observation.
这个哲学概念无法通过经验观察来检验。


近义词：unverifiable, unprovable, refutable
反义词：testable, verifiable, provable
词性: adjective
