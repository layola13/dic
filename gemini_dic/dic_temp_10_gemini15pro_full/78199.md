geothermometer:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈθɜːrmɒmɪtər/ | n. | 地温计 | An instrument or method used to estimate the temperature at depth within the Earth, typically based on the chemical or isotopic composition of minerals or fluids brought to the surface. |  Geologists used a geothermometer to estimate the temperature of the magma chamber.  地质学家使用地温计来估算岩浆房的温度。| Synonyms:  temperature indicator, thermal indicator | Antonyms: N/A | noun
