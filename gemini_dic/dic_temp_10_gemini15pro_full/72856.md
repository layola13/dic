folkloristics:/ˌfoʊklɔːrˈɪstɪks/ | n. |民俗学| The study of folklore, traditions, customs, and beliefs of a people.|  Folkloristics helps us understand the cultural heritage of different communities. 民俗学帮助我们理解不同社群的文化遗产。| Example: Her dissertation in folkloristics focused on the oral traditions of Appalachian communities. 她的民俗学论文重点研究了阿巴拉契亚社区的口头传统。| Synonyms: folklore studies | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
