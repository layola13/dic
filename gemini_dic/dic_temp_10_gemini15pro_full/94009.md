im·pale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/ /ɪmˈpæl/ | vt.| 刺穿；钉住；使陷入困境 | To pierce with a sharp stake or point; to fix on a point. | The defeated rebels were impaled on stakes as a warning to others.  叛军被打败后被钉在木桩上，以儆效尤。 |  pierce, spear, transfix | release, free | verb

im·pale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/ /ɪmˈpæl/ | vt.|  使陷入困境; 使深受折磨 | To put in a difficult situation. | He was impaled on the horns of a dilemma. 他左右为难。| trap, snare, embroil | extricate, liberate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *empailer, from en- "in" + pailer "to pale, stake," from Latin palus "stake".
