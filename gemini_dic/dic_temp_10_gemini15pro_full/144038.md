outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|支腿，舷外支架，突出物|A framework extending from the side of a boat to give it stability.|The canoe glided smoothly through the water, balanced by its outrigger. 独木舟在水面上平稳地滑行，由其支腿保持平衡。|Synonyms: stabilizer, support, prop|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hull" in the sense of the main body of the boat)|noun

outrigger:/ˈaʊtrɪɡər/| n.|划艇架|A canoe, boat, or raft stabilized by an outrigger.|The fishermen headed out to sea in their outriggers. 渔民们乘坐他们的划艇出海了。|Synonyms: canoe, boat, raft|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
