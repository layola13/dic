loveable:/ˈlʌvəbl/ (US), /ˈlʌvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可爱的，讨人喜欢的|Inspiring love or affection. Easy to love.|She has a lovable personality. 她性格可爱。|近义词：adorable, charming, sweet, endearing|反义词：unlovable, hateful, detestable|词性:形容词
词源: From love + -able,  suffix meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," "tending to," "able to."  The word "love" itself has roots in Old English "lufu."
