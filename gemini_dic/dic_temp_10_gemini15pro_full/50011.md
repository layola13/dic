denoiser:/diːˈnɔɪzər/| n. |去噪器，降噪器|A device or software program designed to reduce or eliminate noise from a signal, such as audio or image data.|例句：The photographer used a denoiser to remove the grain from the high-ISO image. 摄影师使用去噪器去除高 ISO 图像中的噪点。

例句：This new denoiser plugin significantly improves the quality of my audio recordings. 这个新的去噪器插件显著提高了我的录音质量。|近义词：noise reducer, noise suppressor, filter|反义词:noise amplifier, noise generator|词性:noun
