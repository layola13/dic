nonreproductiveness:/ˌnɒnriːprəˈdʌktɪvnəs/ | n. | 不可重复性，不可再现性 | The inability of a scientific experiment or study to be duplicated and yield the same results. | The nonreproductiveness of the initial findings led to further investigation.  最初发现的不可重复性导致了进一步的调查。 | synonyms: irreproducibility, nonrepeatability | antonyms: reproducibility, repeatability | noun
