arundiferous:/ˌærənˈdɪfərəs/
adjective
产芦苇的；多芦苇的
Bearing reeds; abounding in reeds.
The arundiferous banks of the river provided shelter for numerous birds. 这条河多芦苇的河岸为许多鸟类提供了庇护所。
Near synonyms: reedy, reed-grown
Antonyms: barren, treeless
词性: 形容词
