nonretractions:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈtræk.ʃənz/ | n. | 不撤回，不收回（通常指声明、指控等） |  The absence of retractions suggests the allegations are serious.  | 例句：The journalist stood by his story, issuing no nonretractions.  这位记者坚持他的报道，没有发布任何撤回声明。

例句：The continued nonretractions of the witnesses strengthened the prosecution's case.  证人持续不撤回证词加强了控方的论点。| 近义词：affirmations, confirmations | 反义词：retractions, withdrawals | 词性: 名词


词源: non- + retraction.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "retraction" comes from the Latin "retrahere," meaning "to draw back."
