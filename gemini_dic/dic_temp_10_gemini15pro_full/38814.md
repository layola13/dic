condyloma:/ˌkɒndɪˈləʊmə/ (US) /ˌkɑːndɪˈloʊmə/| n. |尖锐湿疣；湿疣|A small wart-like growth on the genitals or anus, caused by a virus.|Example 1: Condylomas are a common sexually transmitted infection. 尖锐湿疣是一种常见的性传播感染。

Example 2:  Treatment for condylomas can vary depending on their size and location.  治疗尖锐湿疣的方法取决于其大小和位置。|Synonym: wart, papilloma|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a medical condition)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *condyloma, from Greek kondylōma, diminutive of kondylos “knuckle.”  The resemblance of the growth to a knuckle likely led to the name.
