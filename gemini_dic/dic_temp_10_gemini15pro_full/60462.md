eisteddfod:/aɪˈstɛðvɒd/ (UK), /aɪˈstɛdfəd/ (US)| n. |音乐节，诗歌比赛会，(尤指威尔士的)吟诵会|A Welsh festival of poetry and music, typically including competitions.|The National Eisteddfod of Wales is a major cultural event. 威尔士全国吟诵会是一项重要的文化盛事。|festival, competition| |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Welsh *eistedd* (to sit) + *bod* (being).  Originally referred to a session where poets and musicians would sit and perform.
