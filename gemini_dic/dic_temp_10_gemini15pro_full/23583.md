break-out:/ˈbreɪkaʊt/| n. |越狱，爆发，突围|A sudden escape from prison or confinement; a sudden increase or occurrence of something undesirable, such as a disease or war.|例句：There was a break-out from the local prison last night. 昨晚当地监狱发生了一起越狱事件。|近义词：escape, eruption, outbreak|反义词:containment, suppression|noun
break-out:/ˈbreɪkaʊt/| adj. |突破性的，突出的|Characterized by a sudden increase or prominence.|例句：This new drug is a break-out treatment for cancer. 这种新药是一种突破性的癌症治疗方法。|近义词：groundbreaking, innovative, pioneering|反义词:conventional, traditional|adjective
