bemuddle: /bɪˈmʌd(ə)l/ | v. | 使糊涂，使迷惑 | to confuse or bewilder someone | The complicated instructions completely bemuddled me. 复杂难懂的说明彻底把我搞糊涂了。|  He was bemuddled by the contradictory reports. 互相矛盾的报告把他弄糊涂了。 | synonyms: confuse, bewilder, befuddle, addle | antonyms: clarify, enlighten | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + muddle, from Low German *muddelen "to make turbid," related to Middle Dutch moddelen "to dabble in mud."
