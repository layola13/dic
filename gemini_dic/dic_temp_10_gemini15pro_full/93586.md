imbalance:/ɪmˈbæləns/| n.|不平衡，失衡|lack of balance; a state in which things are not of equal weight, strength, or importance|The economic imbalance between the two countries led to trade tensions. 这两个国家之间的经济不平衡导致了贸易紧张。|近义词：disparity, inequality, asymmetry|反义词:balance, equilibrium, symmetry|noun

imbalance:/ɪmˈbæləns/| v.|使不平衡，使失衡|to cause something to be out of balance|Stress can imbalance the body's hormones. 压力会导致体内激素失衡。|近义词：destabilize, upset|反义词:balance, stabilize|verb
