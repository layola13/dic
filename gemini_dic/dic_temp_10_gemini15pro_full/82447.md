grumbliest:/ˈɡrʌmbliɪst/ | adj. | 最爱抱怨的，最爱发牢骚的 |  Superlative form of "grumbly," meaning most inclined to grumble or complain.  It describes someone who complains more than anyone else in a particular group. | He was the grumbliest person on the camping trip, complaining about the bugs, the weather, and the food. 他是露营旅行中最爱抱怨的人，抱怨虫子、天气和食物。|  Synonyms: most complaining, most cantankerous, most irritable | Antonyms: most cheerful, most agreeable, most content | adjective

grumble:/ˈɡrʌmb(ə)l/ | v. n. | 抱怨，发牢骚；怨言，牢骚 | To complain in a low, muttering voice; a low, muttered complaint. |  He grumbled about the long wait. 他抱怨等待时间太长。| Synonyms: complain, moan, gripe | Antonyms: praise, commend, applaud | verb, noun
