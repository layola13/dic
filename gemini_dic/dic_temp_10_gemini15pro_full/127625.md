needlepointed:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪntɪd/| adj.|针绣的；用针绣成的|Decorated or created using needlepoint.|例句：She gave me a needlepointed pillow as a gift. 她送给我一个针绣的枕头作为礼物。

例句：The needlepointed design on the chair was intricate and beautiful. 椅子上的针绣图案复杂而美丽。|近义词：embroidered, stitched|反义词:plain, unembellished|词性:adjective

词源:  From "needle" + "point" + "-ed". "Needle" refers to the tool used, "point" refers to the individual stitch, and "-ed" creates the adjective form.
