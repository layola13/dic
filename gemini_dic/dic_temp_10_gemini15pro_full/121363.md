misgrown:/ˌmɪsˈɡroʊn/
adjective
发育不良的，畸形的
Having grown in a defective or abnormal way.
The misgrown tree bore stunted fruit. 这棵发育不良的树结出了发育不良的果实。
近义词: deformed, stunted, malformed
反义词: well-developed, healthy
词性: adjective

词源:
From mis- + grown, past participle of grow.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "amiss."
