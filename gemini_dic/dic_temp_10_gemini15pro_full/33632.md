cindery: /ˈsɪndəri/ | adj. |  煤渣的；含煤渣的；像煤渣的 | Resembling or containing cinders; ash-like. | The ground was cindery and barren.  地面满是煤渣，一片荒芜。| Synonyms: ashen, ash-like, slaggy | Antonyms: fertile, lush | adjective


cindery: /ˈsɪndəri/ | adj. |  灰蒙蒙的；死气沉沉的 |  Having a grayish or dull appearance, like ashes. | The sky was a cindery gray. 天空是一片灰蒙蒙的灰色。| Synonyms: grayish, dull, lackluster | Antonyms: vibrant, bright | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From cinder + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *sindre, from Proto-Germanic *sindrō.
