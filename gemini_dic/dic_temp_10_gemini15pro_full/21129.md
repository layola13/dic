blithe:/blaɪð/ /blaɪθ/| adj.|快乐的，无忧无虑的，轻率的|Showing a casual and cheerful indifference considered to be inappropriate; carefree and lighthearted.|She walked with a blithe step, seemingly unconcerned about the world's problems. 她步伐轻快，似乎对世界上的问题漠不关心。|例句：He showed blithe disregard for the rules. 他对规则表现出满不在乎的态度。 他对规则表现出满不在乎的态度。|近义词：carefree, cheerful, lighthearted, merry, joyous|反义词：serious, somber, anxious, worried|词性：形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blīðe (happy, kind, friendly, merciful, gentle, gracious), from Proto-Germanic *bliþiz (gentle, mild, friendly). Cognate with Dutch blij (glad, happy), German blid (mild), Swedish blid (mild).
