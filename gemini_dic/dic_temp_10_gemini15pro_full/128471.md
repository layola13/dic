newslessnesses:/ˈnjuːzləsnəsɪz/
n.
缺乏新闻；无闻；寂静
The state of being without news or information.
The small town suffered from newslessnesses, with the local paper only published monthly.  这个小镇饱受缺乏新闻之苦，当地报纸每月只出版一次。
Near synonyms: information dearth, lack of news, silence
Antonyms: news, information, publicity
