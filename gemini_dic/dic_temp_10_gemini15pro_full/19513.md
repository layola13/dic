biennials:/baɪˈɛniəlz/| n.| 二年生植物；两年一次的事件|Plants that complete their life cycle in two years are called biennials.  完成生命周期需要两年的植物被称为二年生植物。|The festival is a biennial event, attracting visitors from all over the world.  这个节日是一个两年一度的活动，吸引了来自世界各地的游客。|近义词:two-year|反义词:annuals, perennials|noun

Etymology->
From Late Middle English *biennial, from Late Latin bienniālis (“of two years”), from Latin biennium (“a period of two years”), from bi- (“two”) + annus (“year”).
