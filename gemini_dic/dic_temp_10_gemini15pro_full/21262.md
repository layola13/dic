bloodbaths:/ˈblʌdbɑːθs/
n.
大屠杀，血腥屠戮
A massacre or slaughter characterized by the shedding of much blood.
The battle was a bloodbath, with casualties on both sides.  这场战斗是一场大屠杀，双方都有伤亡。
The film depicts the bloodbath that followed the revolution.  这部电影描绘了革命后的大屠杀。
massacre, slaughter, carnage
peace, truce
noun
