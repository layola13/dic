incendiarism:/ɪnˈsɛndiərɪzəm/
n.
纵火; 纵火罪
The deliberate and malicious burning of property.
The police suspect incendiarism in the recent fire at the warehouse. 警方怀疑最近仓库的火灾是纵火案。

Arson is suspected in the fire that destroyed the historic building last night.  警方怀疑昨晚烧毁这座历史建筑的大火是纵火案。
