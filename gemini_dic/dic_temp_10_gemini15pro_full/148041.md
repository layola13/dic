overrule:/ˌoʊvərˈruːl/ | v. | 驳回；否决；宣布无效；统治；支配 | To reject or disallow (a decision, argument, etc.) by virtue of higher authority. | The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了异议。|  The president overruled his advisors and decided to veto the bill. 总统否决了顾问们的意见，决定否决该法案。 | 近义词: override, reject, overturn, disallow | 反义词: uphold, sustain, allow, approve | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old French *surruler*, from sur- (“over”) + ruler (“to rule”).
