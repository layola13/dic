hawkster:/ˈhɔːkstər/| n.|鹰派人物；强硬派；主战派；鹰派政客|A person who favors aggressive policies, especially in foreign affairs and military matters.  A politically aggressive person.|The hawksters in the administration pushed for military intervention.|近义词：hawk, warmonger|反义词：dove, pacifist|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hawk (n.) + -ster (agent suffix).  The "-ster" suffix originally denoted feminine agents (like "spinster"), but later became generalized and sometimes pejorative.
