antiorganizations:/ˌæntiˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.|反组织; 反体制机构|Groups or movements that oppose traditional organizations or societal structures, often characterized by decentralized, informal, and sometimes radical approaches.|Antiorganizations emerged in the 1960s, challenging the established power structures.|近义词：counterculture, underground, nonconformist movements|反义词:establishment, mainstream organizations|词性:名词

antiorganization:/ˌæntiˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|反组织; 反体制机构|Singular form of antiorganizations.  Refers to a single group or movement with the characteristics described above.|The antiorganization protested against the war.|近义词：counterculture, underground, nonconformist movement|反义词:establishment, mainstream organization|词性:名词
