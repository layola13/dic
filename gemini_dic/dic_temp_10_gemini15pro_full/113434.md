lymphs:/lɪmfs/| n. |淋巴；淋巴液|A colorless fluid containing white blood cells, which bathes the tissues and drains through the lymphatic system into the bloodstream.|The doctor checked the patient's lymphs for signs of infection. 医生检查了病人的淋巴，寻找感染的迹象。|Synonyms: lymph fluid, lymphatic fluid|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a bodily fluid)|noun
