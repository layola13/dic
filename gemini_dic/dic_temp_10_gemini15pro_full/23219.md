bowhead:/ˈboʊˌhed/| n.|弓头鲸|A baleen whale (Balaena mysticetus) of Arctic seas, having a large head and a deeply arched mouth.|例句：The bowhead whale is the longest-living mammal. 弓头鲸是寿命最长的哺乳动物。

例句：Bowhead whales can break through thick Arctic ice. 弓头鲸可以冲破厚厚的北极冰层。|近义词：whale, right whale|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bow + head, referring to the highly arched shape of the whale's upper jaw.
