gurnet:/ˈɡɜːrnɪt/ | n. |  鲬鱼，红娘鱼 | A type of bottom-dwelling fish with a large head and spiny fins, often found in coastal waters. |  The fisherman caught a gurnet in his net. 渔夫在他的网里捕到了一条鲬鱼。|  Synonyms: gurnard, sea robin | Antonyms: None | noun

gurnet:/ˈɡɜːrnɪt/ | n. |  傻瓜，笨蛋 (old-fashioned) | A foolish or stupid person. | He's a right gurnet, he lost his keys again. 他真是个傻瓜，又把钥匙弄丢了。| Synonyms: fool, idiot, dunce | Antonyms: genius, intellectual | noun
