nonnaturalnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈnætʃrəlnəsəz/ | n. | 非自然性，不自然 |  The state or quality of being not natural; artificiality or unnaturalness in multiple instances.  This word highlights the plural form of unnaturalness, referring to many instances of things being not natural. | The nonnaturalnesses of the genetically modified organisms were a concern for many. 这些转基因生物的非自然性是许多人关心的问题。| The artist's exploration of nonnaturalnesses in his sculptures provoked much discussion. 这位艺术家在其雕塑中对非自然性的探索引发了很多讨论。 | 近义词: artificialities, unnaturalnesses | 反义词: naturalnesses, authenticities | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + natural + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality) + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *naturalis*.
