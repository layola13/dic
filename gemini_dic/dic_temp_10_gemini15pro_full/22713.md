borg:/bɔːrɡ/| n.|博格人，博格集合体|A fictional alien race in the Star Trek universe characterized by their collective consciousness and cybernetic enhancements. They assimilate other cultures and individuals into their collective, stripping them of individuality.|例句：The Borg are a formidable enemy in Star Trek. 博格人是星际迷航中一个强大的敌人。

例句：Resistance is futile. You will be assimilated. 抵抗是徒劳的。你将被同化。|近义词：collective, hive mind|反义词:individual|Noun
