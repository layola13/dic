hebridian:/hɪˈbrɪdiən/
adjective|赫布里底群岛的；赫布里底群岛居民的；赫布里底群岛文化的|Relating to the Hebrides, a group of islands off the west coast of Scotland. |The hebridian landscape is rugged and beautiful. 赫布里底群岛的风景崎岖而美丽。|
hebridian:/hɪˈbrɪdiən/
noun|赫布里底群岛居民|A person from the Hebrides. |The hebridian spoke Gaelic.  这位赫布里底群岛居民讲盖尔语。|
近义词:Islander, Gael (for the people)
反义词:Mainlander (Scottish mainland resident)
词性:adjective, noun
词源:From Hebrides, the name of the islands, ultimately from Old Norse *Havbreyjar “islands of the sea”.
