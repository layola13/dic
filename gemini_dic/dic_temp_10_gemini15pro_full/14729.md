awaynesses:/əˈweɪnəsɪz/
n.
疏离感；远离；脱离
states of being away or distant, either physically or emotionally.
He felt a profound sense of awayness from his family after years of traveling.  多年旅行后，他感到与家人之间深刻的疏离感。
She experienced a strange awayness from reality during the fever dream. 在发烧的梦中，她经历了一种与现实的奇怪的脱离感。
近义词：distance, remoteness, detachment, alienation
反义词：closeness, nearness, connection, intimacy
词性：名词
