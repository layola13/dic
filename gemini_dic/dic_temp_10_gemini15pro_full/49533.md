| n. |界限，分界线|A dividing line; a boundary or limit. The act of setting boundaries or limits.|例句：The treaty established a clear demarcation of the border. 该条约明确划定了边界。|例句：The demarcation between right and wrong is not always clear. 正确与错误之间的界限并不总是清晰的。|近义词：boundary, limit, border, division|反义词:connection, merge, union|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *demarcacoun*, from Middle French demarcation, from de- (expressing removal, separation) + marquer (to mark). Ultimately from Frankish *markōn* (“to mark, border”)
