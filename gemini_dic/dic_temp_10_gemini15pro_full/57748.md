dreamfulness:/ˈdriːmfʊlnəs/| n.|梦幻状态；沉思冥想；爱幻想|The tendency to be dreamy or lost in thought.|Her dreamfulness often led her to forget practical matters. 她爱幻想的性格经常让她忘记实际的事情。|Near synonyms: daydreaming, absentmindedness, fancifulness|Near antonyms: practicality, realism, focus|noun
