nereid:/ˈnɪəriɪd/ /ˈnɪəˌrɪd/ | n. |海仙女，涅瑞伊得斯|A sea nymph, one of the fifty daughters of Nereus.  Nereids are typically depicted as beautiful maidens attending Poseidon. |例句：The nereid combed her long, flowing hair.  海仙女梳理着她飘逸的长发。

例句：Legends say nereids would often help sailors in distress. 传说海仙女经常帮助遇险的水手。|近义词：sea nymph, mermaid, oceanid|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps land nymph or terrestrial spirit)|词性:noun
