gunboat:/ˈɡʌnboʊt/ (US) /ˈɡʌnbəʊt/ (UK)| n. |炮舰|A small warship equipped with guns, typically used in rivers or shallow coastal waters.  A gunboat is a relatively small ship, but it packs a powerful punch with its cannons. |The gunboat patrolled the river. 炮舰在河上巡逻。

The admiral dispatched a gunboat to protect the merchant ships.  海军上将派遣了一艘炮舰去保护商船。|近义词：warship, corvette|反义词: N/A|noun
