dissolvable:/dɪˈzɒlvəbl/ (US) /dɪˈsɒlvəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可溶解的；可分解的|Capable of being dissolved, liquefied, or broken down in a solvent, typically water.|The tablets are dissolvable in water. 这些药片可在水中溶解。|Synonyms: soluble, meltable, liquefiable|Antonyms: insoluble, indissoluble|adjective
