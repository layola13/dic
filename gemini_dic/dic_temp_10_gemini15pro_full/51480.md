detoxifier:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪər/ | n. | 解毒剂，净化剂 |  A substance or process that removes toxins from the body or a substance. |  She used a detoxifier to cleanse her body after a weekend of unhealthy eating. 她用了一种解毒剂来净化身体，因为她周末吃了很多不健康的食物。 |  purifier, cleanser, detox agent | contaminant, pollutant | noun
detoxify:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪ/ | v. | 解毒，净化 | To remove toxins or poisons from something, such as the body or a substance. |  The liver detoxifies harmful substances in the blood. 肝脏可以解毒血液中的有害物质。 |  cleanse, purify, purge | contaminate, pollute | verb
