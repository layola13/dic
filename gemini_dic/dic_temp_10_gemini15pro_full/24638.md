brokering:/ˈbroʊkərɪŋ/
n.|经纪；斡旋；调停|The act of arranging or negotiating something on behalf of others, especially in business or politics.|例句：The brokering of a peace deal between the two countries was a major diplomatic achievement.  两国之间的和平协议的斡旋是一项重大的外交成就。|例句：He made a fortune through brokering deals in the real estate market. 他通过在房地产市场中斡旋交易发了财。|近义词：negotiating, mediating, arranging, facilitating|反义词：obstructing, hindering|词性: noun (gerund or present participle)

Etymology:
From the verb "broke," from Middle English *broken, from Old English *brūcan "to use, enjoy, possess," of Germanic origin.  The sense of acting as an intermediary developed later, with "broker" emerging in the 16th century.
