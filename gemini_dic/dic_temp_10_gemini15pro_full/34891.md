clippingly:/ˈklɪpɪŋli/
adv.
简短地，快速地，干脆地
In a clipping manner; quickly and concisely.
She spoke clippingly, giving only the essential facts. 她说话简短，只说了要点。
近义词: curtly, abruptly, shortly, briskly
反义词: lengthily, verbosely, tediously
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From clipping (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix).  Clipping itself comes from clip (verb), meaning to cut short.  The ultimate origin is likely Proto-Germanic *klippan.
