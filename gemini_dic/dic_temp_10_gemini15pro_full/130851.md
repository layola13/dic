nonapproachableness:/ˌnɒnəˈprəʊtʃəbəlnəs/
n.
难以接近；冷漠；难以亲近
The state or quality of being unapproachable, unfriendly, or aloof.
Her nonapproachableness made it difficult to make friends with her. 她难以接近的态度使人难以和她交朋友。
近义词： aloofness, coldness, distance, remoteness, unfriendliness
反义词：approachability, friendliness, warmth, openness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- + approachable + -ness.  "Approachable" comes from the verb "approach," which ultimately derives from the Old French "aprochier," meaning "to draw near."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
