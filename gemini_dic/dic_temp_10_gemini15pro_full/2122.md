adaptative /əˈdæptɪv/
adjective
适应的，有适应力的
Having the ability to change or be changed to suit different conditions.
Desert animals have adaptative mechanisms for dealing with the harsh environment. 沙漠动物具有适应恶劣环境的机制。

The company demonstrated an adaptative strategy in response to changing market demands.  该公司针对不断变化的市场需求展示了一种适应性策略。

synonym
flexible, adaptable, adjustable, malleable, pliant
antonym
inflexible, rigid, unadaptable, fixed, unyielding
词性
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adaptativus, from adaptare “to adapt” + -ivus.

USAGE->
Attributive usage (before a noun):

Adaptative behaviors are essential for survival. 适应性行为对于生存至关重要。
He developed an adaptative learning style. 他发展了一种适应性的学习方式。

Predicative usage (after a verb):

The species proved highly adaptative. 该物种被证明具有很强的适应性。
Their business model is not very adaptative. 他们的商业模式适应性不强。


PHRASE->
adaptative behavior 适应性行为
adaptative capacity 适应能力
adaptative mechanism 适应机制
adaptative strategy 适应策略
adaptative response 适应性反应
