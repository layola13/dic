|The software was plagued with misbuilds that caused frequent crashes.|该软件深受构建错误的困扰，导致频繁崩溃。

|反义词：correct build, proper construction|

misbuild:/ˌmɪsˈbɪld/
|v.|错误地构建，错误地组装|To construct or assemble incorrectly, often resulting in defects or malfunctions.|
|The developers misbuilt the software, leading to numerous bugs.|开发人员错误地构建了软件，导致了大量的错误。
|The company misbuilt the engine, resulting in a recall.|该公司错误地组装了引擎，导致召回。
|近义词：misconstruct, misassemble|
|反义词：build correctly, construct properly|
|词性：动词|
