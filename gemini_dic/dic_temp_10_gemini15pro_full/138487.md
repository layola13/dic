novenary:/ˈnəʊvɪnəri/ /ˈnoʊvənɛri/| adj. n.|九的，九个一组的；九日祈祷|Consisting of nine; a group of nine; a nine-day devotion or period of prayer.|The novenary prayers were offered for the recovery of the sick child.  为生病孩子的康复进行了九日祈祷。|Near synonyms: ninefold, nonuple|Antonyms: singular, individual|Adjective, Noun

novenary:/ˈnəʊvɪnəri/ /ˈnoʊvənɛri/| n.|九日祈祷，九日经|A nine-day period of prayer, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.|She attended a novenary for her deceased grandmother. 她参加了为她已故祖母举行的九日祈祷。|Near synonyms: devotion, observance|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "celebration" in some contexts)|Noun
