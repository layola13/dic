inopportunity:/ˌɪnˌɑːpərˈtjuːnəti/ | n. | 不合时宜，时机不对 |  Lack of opportunity; an unfortunate or unsuitable time. |  His phone call was an inopportunity, interrupting an important meeting.  他的电话不合时宜，打断了一个重要的会议。 |  The heavy rain proved an inopportunity for our picnic.  这场大雨证明了我们的野餐时机不对。| 近义词: bad timing, inconvenience, unsuitability, inappropriateness | 反义词: opportunity, appropriateness, suitability, convenience | 词性: 名词

词源:  From in- "not" + opportunity.  由in-（不）+ opportunity（机会）组成。
