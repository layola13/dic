impasto:/ɪmˈpɑːstoʊ/ /ɪmˈpæstoʊ/
n.
厚涂颜料; 厚涂法
a technique used in painting, where paint is laid on an area of the surface very thickly, usually thick enough that the brush or painting-knife strokes are visible.  It is also any painting done with this technique.
The artist used impasto to create a textured effect on the canvas.  这位艺术家用厚涂法在画布上创造了纹理效果。
近义词: texture, thickness
反义词: wash, glaze
词性: 名词
