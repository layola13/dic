odalwoman:/ˈoʊdəlˌwʊmən/| n.|奥达尔妇女；拥有奥达尔土地的女人|A woman who owns odal land, which is land held under a system of allodial ownership.  Odal land is typically inherited and cannot be alienated without the consent of the family.  This system was prevalent in Scandinavian countries, particularly Norway.|The odalwoman fiercely defended her right to the land that had been in her family for generations.|Near Synonyms:  freeholder, landholder, proprietor|Antonyms: tenant, renter|noun
