misalignments: /ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnmənts/ | n. | 失准，错位，不一致 |  A failure of things to be positioned correctly in relation to each other, or a difference or disagreement between two or more things. | The misalignments in the engine caused significant vibration. 引擎的失准导致了严重的震动。| synonyms: discrepancies, disparities, inconsistencies, divergences | antonyms: alignments, agreements, harmonies, consistencies | noun
