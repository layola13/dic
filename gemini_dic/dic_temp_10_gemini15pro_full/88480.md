historians:/hɪˈstɔːriənz/ | n. |历史学家，史学工作者|  A person who studies or writes about history; an expert in history. | Historians have offered many interpretations of the Civil War. 历史学家对内战提出了许多不同的解释。| Historians are piecing together the events of the past. 历史学家正在拼凑过去的事件。|  Synonyms: chronicler, annalist, scholar | Antonyms: ahistorical, unhistorical | noun
