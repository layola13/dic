nutraceutical: /ˌnuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəl/ | n. |营养保健品；保健食品|A food or food product that reportedly provides health and medical benefits, including the prevention and treatment of disease.  It's a combination of "nutrition" and "pharmaceutical". |Example 1:  She takes several nutraceuticals daily, believing they boost her immune system. 她每天服用几种营养保健品，相信它们能增强她的免疫系统。

Example 2: The market for nutraceuticals is growing rapidly as people become more health-conscious. 随着人们越来越注重健康，营养保健品市场正在快速增长。|Synonym:  functional food, dietary supplement, health supplement|Antonym: junk food, unhealthy food|Part of Speech: noun
