abscissae:/æbˈsɪsiː/ /æbˈsɪsaɪ/ | n. |横坐标|The horizontal (x) coordinate of a point in a two-dimensional coordinate system.|The abscissae of these points are all positive. 这些点的横坐标都是正的。|近义词：x-coordinate, horizontal coordinate|反义词: ordinate|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abscissa*, from *abscindere* "to cut off, tear off," from *ab* "off, away from" + *scindere* "to cut, split."  Originally referred to the portion of a line segment cut off between a point and an ordinate.
