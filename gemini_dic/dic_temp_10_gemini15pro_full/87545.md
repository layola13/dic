heterologous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒləɡəs/| adj.|异源的，不同的|Derived from a different species or source; dissimilar in structure or origin.  Unlike in nature or kind.|例句：Heterologous expression of a gene involves producing the gene product in a different organism. 在不同生物体中表达基因涉及在不同生物体中产生基因产物。|例句：The surgeon performed a heterologous bone graft, using bone tissue from a donor.  外科医生进行了异种骨移植，使用了来自供体的骨组织。|近义词：foreign, different, dissimilar, unrelated|反义词：homologous, similar, related|词性:形容词
