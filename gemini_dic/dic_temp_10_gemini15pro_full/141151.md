oolitic:/ˌoʊəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 鮞状的；鱼卵状的 |  Composed of oolites or resembling oolites. Oolites are small, rounded grains of calcium carbonate formed in shallow marine waters.  | The geologist examined the oolitic limestone. 地质学家检查了鮞状灰岩。 | The beach is composed of fine, oolitic sand.  这片海滩由细小的鮞状沙子组成。 |  pisolitic, granular | amorphous, massive | adjective
