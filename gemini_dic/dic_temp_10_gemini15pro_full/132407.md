nondenseness:/ˌnɑːnˈdɛnsnɪs/ | n. | 非稠密性 | The state or quality of not being dense; sparseness.  This refers to a mathematical concept where a set is not dense in a particular space. | The nondenseness of the rational numbers in the real numbers is a fundamental concept in analysis.  有理数在实数中的非稠密性是分析学中的一个基本概念。 | sparseness, thinness | denseness, thickness | noun
