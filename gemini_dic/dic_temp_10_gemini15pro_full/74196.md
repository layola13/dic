fourierism:/ˈfʊrieɪˌɪzəm/| n. |傅立叶主义|A social system based on the principles of the French socialist Charles Fourier (1772-1837), especially the cooperative organization of communities.|The utopian community experimented with fourierism, hoping to create a more equitable society.|Synonyms: Fourieristic socialism, utopian socialism|Antonyms: Capitalism, individualism|noun
