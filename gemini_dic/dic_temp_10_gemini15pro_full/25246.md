buffaloed:/ˈbʌfələʊd/ (US) /ˈbʌfəloʊd/ (UK)| adj. | 感到困惑的，不知所措的，被吓倒的 | Confused, intimidated, or bewildered, especially by aggressive or confusing behavior. |  I was completely buffaloed by his aggressive sales tactics. 我被他咄咄逼人的销售策略完全吓倒了。

The complex legal jargon left her feeling buffaloed. 复杂的法律术语让她感到困惑。 | 近义词：intimidated, bewildered, confused, overwhelmed | 反义词：confident, clearheaded, unfazed | 词性：形容词


词源:
From the behavior of American bison (buffalo), which could be easily panicked and stampeded.  源于美洲野牛（buffalo）的行为，它们很容易受到惊吓而四处乱窜。
