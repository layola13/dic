distinguishedly:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪdli/| adv.|显著地，杰出地，出色地|In a way that sets someone or something apart from others, showing excellence or importance.|She dressed distinguishedly in a tailored suit. 她穿着一套剪裁得体的西装，显得格外优雅。|近义词：notably, remarkably, prominently, eminently|反义词：inconspicuously, ordinarily, commonly|词性:副词

词源: From distinguish + -ed + -ly.  "Distinguish" comes from Old French "distinguiss-" (stem of distinguer), from Latin "distinguere" meaning "to separate, divide, distinguish, mark off, prick apart," from "dis-" (apart, asunder) + "stinguere" (to prick, extinguish).
