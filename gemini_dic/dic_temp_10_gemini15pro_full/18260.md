believing:/bɪˈliːvɪŋ/
verb, adjective
相信; 信仰; 认为
Accepting something as true; holding something as an opinion.  As an adjective, it describes someone who is willing to accept or trust.

例句：
Believing in yourself is the first step to success.
相信自己是成功的第一步。

I am believing that he will come. (Less common usage - generally "believe" is preferred in the present continuous)
我相信他会来。（现在进行时用法较少见，通常用一般现在时believe）

近义词：trusting, accepting, credulous, faithful
反义词：doubting, skeptical, cynical, disbelieving
词性: Verb (present participle), Adjective
