anti-democratic:/ˌæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/| adj.| 反民主的；非民主的| Opposed to or hostile to the principles of democracy, which include individual rights, freedoms, and the rule of law.  Characterized by actions or policies that undermine or suppress democratic institutions or processes.|  The regime enacted several anti-democratic laws that restricted freedom of speech and assembly.  该政权颁布了几项反民主的法律，限制了言论和集会自由。

The leader's anti-democratic tendencies became increasingly apparent as he consolidated his power.  随着这位领导人巩固自己的权力，他的反民主倾向变得越来越明显。|近义词：authoritarian, totalitarian, despotic, tyrannical|反义词：democratic, liberal, egalitarian| 词性: adjective
