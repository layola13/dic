nonsilicate:/nɒnˈsɪlɪkət/ /nɑːnˈsɪlɪkeɪt/
adjective|非硅酸盐的|Not containing silica or silicates.|Garnet is a common nonsilicate mineral. 石榴石是一种常见的非硅酸盐矿物。|Nonsilicate minerals are less common than silicate minerals. 非硅酸盐矿物不如硅酸盐矿物常见。|non-siliceous|silicate|adjective
