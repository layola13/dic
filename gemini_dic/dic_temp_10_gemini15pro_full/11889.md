arrogatingly: /ˈærəɡeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 傲慢地，专横地 | In a way that shows a feeling of unwarranted importance or power; presumptuously. | He arrogatingly dismissed their concerns. 他傲慢地驳回了他们的担忧。 |  She arrogatingly claimed the credit for the entire project. 她傲慢地把整个项目的功劳都归功于自己。 | synonym: presumptuously, haughtily, imperiously, disdainfully | antonym: humbly, modestly, respectfully | word type: adverb

arrogate: /ˈærəɡeɪt/ | v. |  擅取；霸占；妄称 | Take or claim (something) without justification. |  He arrogated to himself the right to decide. 他擅自决定了。 | The dictator arrogated all power to himself. 独裁者将所有权力据为己有。 | synonym: usurp, seize, appropriate, commandeer | antonym: relinquish, renounce, abdicate | word type: verb
