imbed:/ɪmˈbed/ /ɪmˈbɛd/ | vt. | 嵌入，埋置 | To fix something firmly into a surrounding mass. |  The archaeologist carefully imbedded the fragile artifact in plaster. 考古学家小心翼翼地将易碎的文物嵌入石膏中。

The bullet imbedded itself in the wall. 子弹嵌进了墙里。

| 近义词：embed, insert, implant | 反义词：extricate, remove | 词性：动词
