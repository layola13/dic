nonascendance:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndəns/| n.| 非优势; 未取得优势地位|Lack of dominance or controlling influence; failure to achieve a position of power or authority. | The nonascendance of the reform movement led to continued social inequality. 改革运动的未取得优势导致社会不平等持续存在。| 近义词: subordination, subjection, obscurity| 反义词: dominance, ascendancy, supremacy| 词性: 名词


Etymology:

non- (prefix): from Latin, meaning "not"

ascendance (noun): from ascend (verb), from Latin *ascendere*, from ad- ("to, towards") + scandere ("to climb")
