fedary:/ˈfɛdəri/| n.|同谋，共犯，帮凶|A person who is a partner or accomplice in a crime or questionable activity. |例句：He was identified as a fedary in the robbery. 他被认定为抢劫案的同谋。

例句：The fedary denied any involvement in the conspiracy.  这个帮凶否认参与了阴谋。


近义词：accomplice, confederate, accessory, abettor|反义词:opponent, adversary|词性:noun
