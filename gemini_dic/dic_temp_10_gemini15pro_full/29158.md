caseworkers:/ˈkeɪswɜːrkərz/ (US) /ˈkeɪswɜːkəz/ (UK)| n.|个案工作者，社工|A person whose job is to provide support and advice to people with social problems, such as illness, unemployment, or family difficulties.|The caseworker helped the family find suitable housing.  这个个案工作者帮助这家人找到了合适的住房。|Synonyms: social worker, counselor, advisor|Antonyms: None|Noun
