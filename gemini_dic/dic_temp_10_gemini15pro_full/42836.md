courses:/ˈkɔːrsɪz/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrsɪz/ (US)| n.|课程，进程，路线，一道菜|A series of lessons or lectures on a particular subject. Also, the route or direction followed by something, or one of the separate parts of a meal.|She's taking a few online courses this semester. 她这学期选了几门在线课程。|The river follows a winding course through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。|The main course was roast beef. 主菜是烤牛肉。|近义词：classes, subjects, curriculum, path, route, dish|反义词: (For the academic meaning)  None specifically, perhaps digression. |noun
