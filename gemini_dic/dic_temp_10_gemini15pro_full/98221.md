insecurest:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊrɪst/ | adj. | 最不安全的，最没自信的 | Superlative form of insecure: lacking confidence; not certain or assured; unsafe.  Feeling very worried and lacking confidence. |  She felt insecurest when speaking in public. 她当众讲话时感觉最不安全。|  He was the insecurest member of the team, always doubting his abilities. 他是团队中最不自信的成员，总是怀疑自己的能力。 | 近义词: most vulnerable, most apprehensive, least confident | 反义词: most secure, most confident | 词性: 形容词

词源: From in- (not) + secure.  The "-est" ending indicates the superlative form.
