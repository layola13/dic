euphemistical:/ˌjuːfəˈmɪstɪkəl/
adjective
委婉的
Substituting a mild, indirect, or vague term for one considered harsh, blunt, or offensive.
She used a euphemism for death, referring to it as "passing away." 她用“去世”这个委婉的说法来指代死亡。
近义词:indirect, evasive, polite
反义词:blunt, direct, frank
词性:adjective
