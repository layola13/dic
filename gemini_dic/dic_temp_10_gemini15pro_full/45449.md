curate: /ˈkjʊəreɪt/ /ˈkjʊrət/ | n. v. | 策展人，管理；策划 | A person who selects, organizes, and looks after the items in a collection, such as a museum or art gallery. To select, organize, and present (online content, merchandise, information, etc.), typically using professional or expert knowledge. |

例句：
The museum curator is an expert in ancient Egyptian art.  博物馆策展人是古埃及艺术专家。

She curated a collection of essays on modern literature. 她策划了一套现代文学论文集。


近义词：administrator, manager, organizer, compiler, collector |
反义词: N/A |
词性: noun, verb |


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cūrātus*, past participle of cūrāre (“to take care of”), from cūra (“care”).
