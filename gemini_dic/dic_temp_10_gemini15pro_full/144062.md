outroars:/ˈaʊtrɔːrz/
n.
喧嚣声，吵闹声，强烈抗议
A loud outburst of noise, especially of protest or disapproval.
The outroars from the crowd drowned out the speaker.  人群的喧嚣声淹没了演讲者的声音。
The proposal was met with outroars of protest.  这项提议遭到了强烈抗议。
clamor, uproar, commotion
silence, quiet
noun
