leverets:/ˈlɛvərɪts/
n.
小野兔, 幼兔
Young hares, especially those in their first year.
The field was teeming with leverets, their long ears twitching nervously. 田野里到处都是小野兔，它们的长耳朵紧张地抽动着。
Synonyms: young hares, baby hares, leveret
Antonyms: adult hare, mature hare
