iconodule:/ˈaɪkənəˌduːl/
noun
破坏圣像者; 偶像崇拜者
A person who venerates or reveres religious images.  A person who destroys religious images.
He was accused of being an iconodule, worshipping statues in his private chapel.  他被指控为圣像崇拜者，在他私人的小教堂里膜拜雕像。

The iconodule kissed the icon reverently.  圣像崇拜者虔诚地亲吻了圣像。
近义词:idolater, image-worshipper
反义词:iconoclast
词性:n.
