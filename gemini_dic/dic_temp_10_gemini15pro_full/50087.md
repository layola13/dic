dentelli:/dɛnˈtɛli/ | n. | dentelle, lacework |A fine kind of lace.  It refers to a delicate, intricate lace, often used in high-end fashion or historical garments.| The antique wedding gown was adorned with exquisite dentelli. 这件古董婚纱装饰着精致的蕾丝。| 近义词: lace, trimming | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider plain cloth or fabric) | 词性: noun

dentelli:/dɛnˈtɛli/ | n. | 牙齿状装饰; 锯齿状装饰 | A tooth-like or serrated ornamentation, often found in architecture or furniture design. This usage is less common than the "lace" meaning. | The dentelli along the edge of the table added a touch of elegance. 桌子边缘的锯齿状装饰增添了一丝优雅。| 近义词: serration, indentation, notching | 反义词: smoothness | 词性: noun
