To remove the eyes of; to blind (literally or figuratively).

例句：The bright light excecated him momentarily.  强光使他暂时失明。

反义词: heal, cure
词性: verb

例句：The teacher excecated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师严厉斥责了考试作弊的学生。

反义词: praise, commend
