antrustion:/ænˈtrʌʃən/
n.
心腹；亲信；密友
A trusted follower or confidant, especially of a powerful person.
King Arthur's antrustions rallied to his defense.  亚瑟王的心腹们集合起来保卫他。

近义词: confidant, intimate, crony, associate
反义词: enemy, adversary, opponent
词性: noun
