equipensate: /ɪˌkwɪˈpɛnseɪt/
verb
补偿；赔偿
to give someone something, typically money, in recognition of loss, suffering, or injury incurred; recompense.
The company will equipensate employees for any travel expenses. 公司将补偿员工的任何差旅费用。
The victim was equipensated for the emotional distress caused by the accident. 受害者得到了事故造成的情绪困扰的赔偿。
synonyms: compensate, recompense, remunerate, indemnify
antonyms: penalize, fine, forfeit
词性: 动词
