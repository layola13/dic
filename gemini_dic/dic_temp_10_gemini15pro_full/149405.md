packages:/ˈpækɪdʒɪz/| n.|包裹，包装，软件包|A collection of related items or a set of proposals or measures presented as a unit.  Many software updates come in packages.|The postman delivered three packages this morning. 邮递员今天早上送来了三个包裹。|Synonyms: parcels, bundles, sets, kits|Antonyms: individual items, components|noun

packages:/ˈpækɪdʒɪz/| v.|打包，包装|The act of putting something into a container for transport or sale.|The company packages its products in eco-friendly materials. 这家公司用环保材料包装其产品。|Synonyms: wrap, box, bundle|Antonyms: unpack, unwrap|verb
