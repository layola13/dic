drib:/drɪb/| v. n.|运球，滴，少量|To move (a ball) by repeated slight taps or bounces, especially in basketball, soccer, and hockey.  Also, to fall or flow in small drops.|He dribbled the basketball down the court. 他运球过全场。 The faucet dripped steadily. 水龙头一直在滴水。|近义词：bounce, trickle, dribble|反义词:gush, pour|verb, noun

drib:/drɪb/| n.|运球，点滴，少量|A small quantity of something; a dribble.|She added a drib of honey to her tea. 她在茶里加了一点蜂蜜。|近义词：drop, bit, trickle|反义词:flood, deluge|noun
