disaffection:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛkʃən/ | n. | 不满，不忠，疏远 | A state of being dissatisfied with the people in authority and no longer willing to support them. |  There is growing disaffection with the government.  民众对政府越来越不满。 | synonyms: alienation, disloyalty, estrangement, resentment, hostility | antonyms: loyalty, affection, devotion | noun

disaffection:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛkʃən/ | n. |  不满，不忠，疏远 |  The feeling of no longer liking or supporting something or someone. |  The party is suffering from widespread disaffection among its supporters. 该党在其支持者中普遍存在不满情绪。 | synonyms: discontent, dissatisfaction, disillusionment | antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, loyalty | noun
