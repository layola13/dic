mispicturing:/ˌmɪsˈpɪktʃərɪŋ/
verb
错误地描绘; 误解
forming a wrong mental picture of something or someone; misunderstanding
She was mispicturing the situation, imagining it far worse than it actually was. 她错误地描绘了情况，想象得比实际情况糟糕得多。
misrepresenting, misinterpreting, distorting
correctly portraying, accurately depicting
v.
