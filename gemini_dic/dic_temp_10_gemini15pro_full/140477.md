old-school:/ˌoʊldˈskuːl/ | adj. | 老派的，传统的 |  Traditional or characteristic of an earlier or original style, manner, or form.  Referring to something that is from an older generation and is often seen as classic or nostalgic. | He has an old-school approach to management. 他有一种老派的管理方法。|  My dad has some old-school values. 我爸爸有一些传统的价值观。 |  classic, traditional, conventional, vintage | modern, contemporary, newfangled | adjective
