blackwork: /ˈblækˌwɜːrk/ (US), /ˈblækˌwɜːk/ (UK)| n.|黑色刺绣；黑色装饰；黑色蕾丝|A form of embroidery using black thread on white fabric, typically linen, featuring geometric patterns.  Also refers to dark or black decorative elements in art and architecture.  Sometimes used to describe black lace.|She learned blackwork embroidery from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里学会了黑色刺绣。|Synonyms: black embroidery, black stitching, black lace (sometimes)|Antonyms: whitework|noun
