nonretailer:/ˌnɑːnˈriːteɪlər/
n.
非零售商
A business or individual that does not sell goods directly to consumers.
Many nonretailers, like wholesalers, sell to other businesses. 许多非零售商，如批发商，将商品销售给其他企业。
Nonretailers often operate in the background of the supply chain. 非零售商通常在供应链的幕后运作。
wholesaler, supplier, distributor
retailer
noun
