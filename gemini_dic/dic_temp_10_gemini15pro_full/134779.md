nonmetamorphosis:/ˌnɒnˌmɛtəmɔːrfəsɪs/ | n. |无变态|Absence of metamorphosis; development without drastic change in form, as in certain insects and amphibians.|Silverfish exhibit nonmetamorphosis, meaning they hatch from eggs looking like miniature versions of the adults.|近义词:ametabolism|反义词:metamorphosis|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "metamorphosis" (from Greek "meta" (change) + "morphē" (form)).
