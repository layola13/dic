etypical:/iːˈtɪpɪkəl/
adj.
非典型的, 不典型的
not representative of a type, group, or class.
Her symptoms are atypical for this disease. 她的症状对于这种疾病来说是非典型的。

近义词:unusual, abnormal, irregular, deviant, anomalous
反义词:typical, normal, standard, regular, usual
词性:形容词
