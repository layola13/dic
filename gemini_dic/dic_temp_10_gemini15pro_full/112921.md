luffer:/ˈlʌfər/ | n. | 斜撑，转臂起重机，活动百叶窗，调节翼片 | A projecting structure on the roof of a building, usually having slats or louvers that can be adjusted to control ventilation or light.  A crane with a projecting arm or jib. A blade or vane that can be adjusted to control airflow or direction. |  The luffer on the barn roof helps keep the hay dry.  谷仓屋顶上的活动百叶窗有助于保持干草干燥。| synonyms: louver, vent, jib, vane, blade | antonyms: solid, fixed | noun

luffer:/ˈlʌfər/ | v. |  转动，调整（百叶窗，翼片等） | To turn or adjust a luffer, louver, or similar device. | He luffed the jib to catch the wind. 他调整了吊臂以捕捉风。 | synonyms: adjust, turn, rotate, swivel | antonyms: fix, secure | verb
