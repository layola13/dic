overcredulousness:/ˌoʊvərkrɪˈdʒuːləsnəs/ | n. | 过于轻信，过分相信 | The state of being too ready to believe things, especially things that are unlikely to be true. | His overcredulousness made him an easy target for scammers. 他的轻信使他很容易成为骗子的目标。 |  近义词：gullibility, naivety, credulity | 反义词：skepticism, cynicism, distrust | 词性: noun

overcredulous:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdʒələs/ | adj. | 过于轻信的 | Too ready to believe things, especially things that are unlikely to be true. |  He was overcredulous and easily deceived. 他太轻信，容易受骗。 | 近义词：gullible, naive, trusting | 反义词：skeptical, cynical, distrustful | 词性: adjective
