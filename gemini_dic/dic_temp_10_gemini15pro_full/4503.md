albolith:/ˈæl.bə.lɪθ/ | n. | 牙骨质; 牙根骨 | A bone-like concretion, especially one forming on the root of a tooth. |  Albolith is a hard deposit that can form on the root of a tooth, often due to poor oral hygiene. 牙骨质是一种在牙根上形成的坚硬沉积物，通常是由于口腔卫生不良造成的。| Synonym: calculus, tartar | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider healthy tooth structure) | noun
