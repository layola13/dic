malawians: /məˈlɑːwiənz/
noun
马拉维人
People from Malawi, a country in southeastern Africa.
Malawians are known for their warmth and hospitality.  马拉维人以热情好客而闻名。
Many Malawians rely on agriculture for their livelihood.  许多马拉维人的生计依赖于农业。
synonyms: citizens of Malawi, people of Malawi
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a nationality)
