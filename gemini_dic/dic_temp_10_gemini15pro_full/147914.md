overregiments:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛdʒɪmənts/ |n.|过度编制，超编；过多团| The army suffered from overregiments and lacked supplies.  军队因编制过多而缺乏补给。 | 近义词: overstaffing, surplus | 反义词: understaffing, shortage | 词性: 名词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "regiment" (from Old French "regiment," meaning "government, rule," ultimately from Latin "regimen," meaning "rule, guidance").  The sense of "regiment" as a military unit developed later.
