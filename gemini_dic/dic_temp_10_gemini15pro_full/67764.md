eyeglance:/ˈaɪɡlæns/| n.| 一瞥，匆匆一看 |A quick look or glance with the eyes. |She caught his eyeglance across the room. 她注意到他从房间的另一边瞥了她一眼。|近义词：glance, peek, glimpse|反义词：stare, gaze|词性：名词
Etymology:  From "eye" + "glance".  Both words have Old English origins. "Eye" comes from  ēage, and "glance" comes from glænc, meaning "bright, shining."  The combination suggests a quick, bright look.
