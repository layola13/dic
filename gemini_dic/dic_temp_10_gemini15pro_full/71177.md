fissure:/ˈfɪʃər/ (US) /ˈfɪʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. |裂缝；裂隙；分歧|A narrow opening or crack of considerable length and depth in rock or the earth.  A split or division between people or groups. |例句：A deep fissure appeared in the glacier. 冰川上出现了一道深深的裂缝。

例句：The fissure between the two political parties widened.  两个政党之间的分歧加剧了。|近义词：crack, crevice, split, rift, chasm, breach|反义词：closure, union, bridge|词性:noun

fissure:/ˈfɪʃər/ (US) /ˈfɪʃə(r)/ (UK)| v. | (使)裂开；(使)分裂|To crack or split open.|例句：The ground fissured during the earthquake. 地震时地面裂开了。

例句：The party fissured into several factions. 该党分裂成了几个派别。|近义词：crack, split, divide, break|反义词：unite, join, merge|词性:verb
