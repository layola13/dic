dorcahedron: /dɔːrkəˈhiːdrən/ | n. |十二面体（通常指菱形十二面体）| A polyhedron with twelve faces.  Specifically, it often refers to a rhombic dodecahedron, which has twelve rhombus-shaped faces. | 例句：The crystal structure of garnet is a dorcahedron. 石榴石的晶体结构是十二面体。

例句：He was fascinated by the geometry of the dorcahedron. 他对十二面体的几何形状很着迷。 | 近义词：dodecahedron, polyhedron | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
