chartless:/ˈtʃɑːrtlɪs/
adj.
无海图的；未知的；未绘制地图的
Without a chart; uncharted; unexplored.
例句：They sailed into chartless waters. 他们驶入了未绘制海图的水域。

例句：The expedition ventured into the chartless jungle. 探险队冒险进入了未知的丛林。

近义词：uncharted, unexplored, unknown, unmapped
反义词：charted, mapped, known, explored
词性：adjective
