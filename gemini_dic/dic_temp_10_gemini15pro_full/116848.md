maximised: /ˈmæksɪmaɪzd/ | v. | 最大化；充分利用 | To increase something to the greatest possible amount or degree. |  The company maximised its profits by cutting costs. 公司通过削减成本实现了利润最大化。

The athlete maximised her potential by training hard every day.  这位运动员通过每天刻苦训练最大限度地发挥了她的潜力。

| 近义词: increased, boosted, amplified, enhanced, optimized | 反义词: minimized, reduced, decreased | 词性: verb (past participle)

Etymology: From the verb "maximize," which is derived from "maximum" (greatest possible quantity), from Latin "maximus" (greatest).
