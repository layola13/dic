lymphoid:/ˈlɪmfɔɪd/| adj.| 淋巴的，淋巴样的| Relating to or denoting the tissue responsible for producing lymphocytes and antibodies.  It also refers to the lymphatic system.|  The lymphoid tissue plays a vital role in the immune system. 淋巴组织在免疫系统中起着至关重要的作用。| The doctor examined the patient's lymphoid organs. 医生检查了病人的淋巴器官。| synonyms: lymphatic | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms related to other tissue types, like "epithelial" or "connective") | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From lymph + -oid.  "Lymph" comes from Latin "lympha" meaning "water." "-oid" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "like."
