chokey:/ˈtʃəʊki/| n.|窒息, 阻塞; (英国俚语)监狱|A feeling of choking or being choked; a British slang term for prison.|The smoke made him feel a bit chokey. 烟让他感觉有点窒息。 He spent five years in the chokey for armed robbery. 他因为持械抢劫在监狱里待了五年。|近义词：jail, prison, suffocating, constricting|反义词: freeing, liberating|词性:名词

chokey:/ˈtʃəʊki/| adj.|令人窒息的|Causing a feeling of choking or being choked.|The air in the room was thick and chokey. 房间里的空气又浓又闷，令人窒息。|近义词：stuffy, stifling, suffocating|反义词: airy, fresh|词性:形容词
