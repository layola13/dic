berserks:/bərˈsɜːrks/ | n. | 狂暴战士；暴怒的人 |  A wild, ferocious warrior or one in a frenzied, violent rage.  It originates from Old Norse *berserkr, meaning "bear-shirt" or "bear-skin," referring to warriors who wore animal skins and fought with a furious, uncontrolled frenzy. | The Vikings were known for their berserks, who fought with unmatched ferocity. 维京人以其狂暴的战士而闻名，他们战斗起来异常凶猛。

The football hooligans turned into berserks after their team lost. 足球队输球后，足球流氓变成了暴徒。 | 近义词: maniacs, wildmen, fanatics | 反义词: pacifists, calm individuals | 词性: 名词
