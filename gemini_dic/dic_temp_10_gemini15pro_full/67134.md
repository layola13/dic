exporter:/ɪkˈspɔːrtər/ /ˈekspɔːrtər/| n. |出口商；出口国|A person, country, or company that sells goods to another country.|China is a major exporter of electronics. 中国是一个主要的电子产品出口国。|The company is a leading exporter of coffee beans. 这家公司是咖啡豆的主要出口商。|近义词：seller, vendor, trader, shipper|反义词：importer|词性：名词
