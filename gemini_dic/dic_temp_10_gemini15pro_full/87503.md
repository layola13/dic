heterochrony:/ˌhɛtərˈɒkrəni/ | n. | 异时性，异时发生 | Evolutionary change in the rate or timing of developmental events. |  The evolution of birds from dinosaurs involved significant heterochrony in the development of feathers and wings. 鸟类从恐龙进化而来，涉及羽毛和翅膀发育的显着异时性。

Heterochrony can lead to significant changes in the morphology of an organism.  异时性会导致生物体形态的重大变化。| 近义词: allochrony,  heterokairy | 反义词: isochrony | 词性: noun
