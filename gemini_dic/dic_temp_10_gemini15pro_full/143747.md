outmanipulate:/ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjuleɪt/
verb
以智取胜，玩弄手段胜过
to defeat or gain advantage over someone by being more skillful or clever at manipulating people or situations
The experienced politician easily outmanipulated his younger opponent.  这位经验丰富的政治家轻易地以智取胜了他的年轻对手。
She outmanipulated her rivals and secured the top position in the company. 她玩弄手段胜过了她的竞争对手， secured the company的最高职位。
近义词: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted, be outwitted
词性: verb

out- (prefix): beyond; exceeding; surpassing  超过；胜过
manipulate (verb): to control or influence (a person or situation) cleverly or unscrupulously  巧妙地或不择手段地控制或影响（一个人或情况）
-ate (suffix): forms verbs  构成动词
