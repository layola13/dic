briefings: /ˈbriːfɪŋz/
n.
简报；情况介绍会；指示；概要
A meeting for giving information or instructions.

例句：
The president received daily briefings from his national security advisor.
总统每天都会收到国家安全顾问的简报。

We attended several briefings on the new product.
我们参加了几个关于新产品的简报会。


近义词：
meetings, conferences, summaries, reports, instructions

反义词:
None (No direct antonym for briefings as a noun)

词性
Noun (Plural)

词源:
From the verb "brief" + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs)
"Brief" comes from Old French "bref" (short), ultimately from Latin "brevis" (short).
