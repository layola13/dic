letting: /ˈlɛtɪŋ/ | n. | 租賃; 允許; 出租 | The act of allowing something to happen or be done; the act of renting a property. |  The letting of the apartment was handled by a local agent. 这套公寓的出租是由当地一位代理人经手的。

The letting of the property included the use of the garden.  房产的租赁包括花园的使用权。 | synonyms: rental, lease, hiring | antonyms: withholding, refusal, prohibition | noun

letting: /ˈlɛtɪŋ/ | n. |  (感情的)流露，释放 | An expression of feelings. |  There was a letting go of all her pent-up emotions. 她所有的压抑情绪都释放了出来。

Her letting out a loud cry surprised everyone. 她放声大哭让每个人都吃了一惊。 | synonyms: expression, release, outburst | antonyms: suppression, repression, concealment | noun
