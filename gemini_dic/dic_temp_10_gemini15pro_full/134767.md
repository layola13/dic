nonmercantile:/ˌnɑːnmərˈkæntaɪl/ /ˌnɒnmərˈkæntaɪl/
adjective
非商业的；非营利的
Not intended for or engaged in commerce; not related to trade or buying and selling.
例句：The museum is a nonmercantile organization dedicated to preserving local history.  这家博物馆是一个致力于保护当地历史的非营利组织。

例句：She pursued nonmercantile interests, such as painting and poetry. 她追求非商业的兴趣，例如绘画和诗歌。

近义词：noncommercial, nonprofit, non-profit
反义词：commercial, mercantile, for-profit
词性：adjective
