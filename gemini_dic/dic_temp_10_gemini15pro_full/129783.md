non-fictional:/nɒnˈfɪkʃənəl/ (UK) /nɑːnˈfɪkʃənəl/ (US)| adj.|非虚构的，纪实的|Based on real events and facts, not imagined or invented.|例句：I prefer reading non-fictional books. 我更喜欢读非虚构类的书籍。|近义词：factual, true, real, authentic|反义词：fictional, imaginary, fabricated|词性:形容词
non-fiction:/nɒnˈfɪkʃən/ (UK) /nɑːnˈfɪkʃən/ (US)| n.|非虚构作品，纪实文学| Prose writing that is based on facts, real events, and real people, such as biography or history.|例句：She's a renowned author of non-fiction. 她是一位著名的非虚构类作家。|近义词：non-fiction writing, factual writing|反义词：fiction|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + fictional.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Fictional" comes from the Latin "fictiō"  meaning "a feigning, fashioning, a fiction."
