fecundation:/ˌfiːkənˈdeɪʃn/ /ˌfekənˈdeɪʃn/
n.
受精，授精
the act or process of fertilizing an egg or a female animal or plant, involving the fusion of male and female gametes to form a zygote.
例句：The fecundation of the egg by the sperm marks the beginning of a new life. 精子和卵子的受精标志着新生命的开始。

例句：In vitro fecundation has revolutionized the treatment of infertility. 体外受精彻底改变了不孕症的治疗。

近义词：fertilization, impregnation, conception
反义词：sterility, infertility
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin fecundātiōn- (stem of fecundātiō ), from Latin fecundātus (past participle of fecundāre  'to make fruitful'), from fecundus  'fruitful'.
