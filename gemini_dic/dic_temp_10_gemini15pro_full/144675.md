outyelled: /ˌaʊtˈjɛld/
verb
大声喊过，盖过…的喊声
To shout louder than someone else.
He outyelled the other fans at the concert. 他在音乐会上盖过了其他歌迷的喊声。

He outyelled his opponent during the debate.  他在辩论中用更大的声音盖过了对手。

Synonym: outshout, drown out
Antonym: be drowned out, be outshouted
词性: 动词
