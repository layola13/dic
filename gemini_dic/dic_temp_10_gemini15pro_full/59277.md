eagers:/ˈiːɡərz/| adj.|渴望的，热切的|Characterized by keen or ardent desire or interest; impatient.|The eagers fans waited for hours to see their favorite band. 渴望的歌迷等了几个小时才看到他们最喜欢的乐队。|近义词：enthusiastic, keen, avid, ardent|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, reluctant|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *eger, from Old French eigre, egre (“sharp, keen, sour, bitter”), from Latin acer (“sharp, keen, pungent, eager”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ḱrós (“sharp”). Cognate with Scots eager (“sour, sharp, keen”), Saterland Frisian äiger (“sour”), West Frisian eager (“sour, tart”), Dutch eager (“harrow”), German Eger (“harrow”), Danish eger (“harrow”). More at acre.
