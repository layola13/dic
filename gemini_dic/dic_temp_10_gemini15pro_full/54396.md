disimpale:/dɪsɪmˈpeɪl/
verb
从尖桩上取下;  从困境中解救
To remove from an impaling stake; to extricate from a predicament.
The knight disimpaled the dragon's head from the castle wall.  骑士把龙的头从城堡墙上取了下来。
The rescue workers carefully disimpaled the hiker from the jagged rocks.  救援人员小心翼翼地把徒步旅行者从锯齿状的岩石上救了下来。
extricate, remove, free, release, disentangle
impale, fix, stick, pierce, transfix
