exciton:/ˈeksɪtɒn/ (British), /ˈeksɪtɑːn/ (American)| n. |激子|A bound state of an electron and a hole which are attracted to each other by the Coulomb force.  It can transport energy without transporting net electric charge.|Excitons play a crucial role in the optical properties of semiconductors and insulators.|近义词:quasiparticle|反义词:None|词性:noun
