aculeolate: /əˈkjuːliəˌleɪt/
adjective
具小刺的，有细针的
Covered with small, needle-like prickles.
The aculeolate surface of the plant's stem made it difficult to handle.  这种植物茎上的小刺使它很难处理。
The insect's legs were aculeolate, allowing it to grip smooth surfaces.  这种昆虫的腿上有细针，可以让它抓住光滑的表面。
近义词: prickly, spiny, barbed
反义词: smooth, glabrous
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin aculeolatus, diminutive of aculeus (“prickle, thorn”).  源自拉丁语 aculeolatus，aculeus（“刺，荆棘”）的指小词。

USAGE->
The aculeolate texture of the fabric irritated my skin. 这块布料的细刺质地刺激了我的皮肤。

The biologist examined the aculeolate leaves under a microscope. 生物学家在显微镜下检查了这些带小刺的叶子。
