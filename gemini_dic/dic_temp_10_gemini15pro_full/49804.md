demonomania:/ˌdiːmənəˈmeɪniə/ | n. |邪魔狂；着魔；鬼迷心窍 |An excessive or abnormal belief that one is possessed by demons.  A form of madness characterized by the belief of being possessed by evil spirits. |He suffered from demonomania and believed he was constantly tormented by devils. 他患有邪魔狂，认为自己不断地受到魔鬼的折磨。|Synonyms: demonopathy, demoniacal possession |Antonyms: sanity, lucidity | Noun
