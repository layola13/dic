dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/ | v. |劝阻，阻止 | To persuade someone not to do something. |  I tried to dissuade her from marrying him, but she wouldn't listen. 我试图劝她不要嫁给他，但她不听。

She was dissuaded from going out by the heavy rain. 她因为下大雨而被劝阻不要外出。

| 近义词: deter, discourage, prevent | 反义词: persuade, encourage, urge |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dissuadere, from dis- (“apart, away”) + suadere (“to advise, urge”).  The prefix dis- indicates a reversal of the action of the base verb, thus meaning to advise *against* something.
