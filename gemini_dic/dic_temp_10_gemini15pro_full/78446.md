getaways:/ˈɡɛtəˌweɪz/
noun
假期, 短途旅行, 逃亡
A short holiday or vacation, often to a place that is relatively close and offers a change of scenery or pace.  Also, an escape or flight, especially from confinement or danger.
We're planning a few getaways this summer. 我们计划今年夏天去几个地方度假。
The thieves made their getaway in a stolen car. 小偷们乘坐偷来的汽车逃跑了。
synonyms: vacations, holidays, trips, escapes, flights
antonyms: imprisonments, confinements, captures
