hectolitre: /ˈhɛktəˌliːtər/ (US) /ˈhɛktəʊˌliːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |百升；公石|A metric unit of volume equal to 100 liters.|The farmer's silo can hold thousands of hectolitres of grain.  农民的筒仓可以容纳数千百升的谷物。|Synonyms:  100 liters, hectoliter (US)|Antonyms: millilitre, microlitre|noun
