parasynthetic:/ˌpærəsɪnˈθɛtɪk/
| adj. |  附加构词的; 并合构词的 | Formed by the simultaneous addition of a prefix and a suffix to a base, as in *enlighten* (from *light*) or *embolden* (from *bold*). | Many English verbs are parasynthetic formations. 许多英语动词是附加构词的。 |  synthetic, complex |  simple, primary | adjective
