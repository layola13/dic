copresidents:/ˌkoʊˈprɛzɪdənts/| n.|共同主席，联合主席|Two individuals sharing the role and responsibilities of president. |The company appointed two copresidents to lead the organization. 公司任命了两位共同主席来领导该组织。|The copresidents worked together effectively. 两位共同主席有效地合作。 |synonyms: co-chairmen, joint presidents| antonyms: subordinate, vice president |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"co-" (prefix) + "president"

"co-" comes from Latin "com-" meaning "with," "together," "jointly."

"president" comes from Latin "praesident," meaning "one who presides" or "one who sits before."
