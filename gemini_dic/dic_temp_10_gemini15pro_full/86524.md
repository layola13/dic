hectare:/ˈhɛkteər/ (US), /ˈhɛktɑːr/ (UK)| n. |公顷|A metric unit of square measure equal to 10,000 square meters (2.471 acres).|The farm covers 100 hectares.  这个农场占地100公顷。|Synonyms: acre (approx.), square hectometer|Antonyms: N/A (unit of measurement)|noun
