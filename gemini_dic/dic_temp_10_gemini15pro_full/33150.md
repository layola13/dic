chromatically:/krəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 色彩地，和声地 |  In a way that relates to color or musical chromatics (using notes outside of a particular key or scale). | The film is chromatically rich, with vibrant colors throughout. 这部电影色彩丰富，充满鲜艳的色彩。| 近义词: colorfully, harmonically | 反义词: monochromatically, atonally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:  From Greek "chrōma" (color) + "-atic" (relating to) + "-ally" (adverbial suffix).
