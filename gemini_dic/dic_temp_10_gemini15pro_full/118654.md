mestizo:/mɛˈstiːzoʊ/ (US), /mɛˈstiːzəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|混血儿，混血的|A person of mixed racial ancestry, especially of European and Indigenous American descent.  A plant or animal resulting from the crossbreeding of different varieties.|例句：Many Mexicans identify as mestizo, acknowledging their mixed indigenous and European heritage. 许多墨西哥人认为自己是混血儿，承认自己拥有土著和欧洲血统。

例句：The mestizo population is the largest demographic group in Mexico. 混血人口是墨西哥最大的族群。|近义词：hybrid, crossbreed|反义词:purebred|词性: noun, adjective
