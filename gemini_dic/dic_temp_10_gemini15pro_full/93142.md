igloos:/ˈɪɡluːz/
noun
冰屋，雪屋
A dome-shaped Inuit house, typically built of blocks of hard snow.
The Inuit people built igloos to shelter from the harsh Arctic weather. 因纽特人建造冰屋以躲避严酷的北极天气。
近义词: snow house, snow hut
反义词: house, building
词性: noun

Etymology: From Inuktitut ᐃᒡᓗ iglu ("house"), from ᐃᒡ ig ("to build").
