clinally:/ˈklaɪnəli/| adv.| 渐变地，呈梯度变化地|In a clinal manner; gradually changing over a geographic area.|The species varies clinally, with larger individuals found in the northern part of its range.  该物种呈梯度变化，体型较大的个体在其分布范围的北部被发现。|Synonym: gradually, progressively|Antonym: abruptly, suddenly|Part of Speech: adverb

Etymology: From "cline" + "-ally." "Cline" comes from Greek "klinein" (to slope).
