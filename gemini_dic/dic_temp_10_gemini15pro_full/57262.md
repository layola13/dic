downmost:/ˈdaʊnˌmoʊst/| adj.|最低的；最下面的|Situated at the lowest point; farthest down. |The downmost layer of rock contained fossils. 最底层的岩石包含化石。|The downmost drawer of the dresser was stuck.  衣柜最下面的抽屉卡住了。|Synonyms: lowest, bottommost, undermost|Antonyms: topmost, uppermost, highest|adjective
