caravansary: /ˌkærəˈvænsəri/ /ˌkærəˈvænsəri/
n.
商队旅馆，大旅舍
A large inn with a central courtyard for travelers with caravans in the Middle East and Asia.
The weary travelers found respite in the caravansary after a long day's journey.  疲惫的旅行者在长途跋涉后在商队旅馆找到了喘息的机会。

The caravansary provided shelter for both merchants and their animals. 这家商队旅馆为商人和他们的牲畜都提供了住所。

近义词: inn, hostel, lodge, hotel
反义词: None (no single antonym; perhaps "home" in a broad sense)
词性: noun
