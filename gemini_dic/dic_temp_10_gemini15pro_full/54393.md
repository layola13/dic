disimagine:/dɪsɪˈmædʒɪn/
verb
不再想象；停止想象；试图忘记
To cease imagining; to stop imagining; to try to forget.
She tried to disimagine the horrific scene she had witnessed. 她试图忘记她目睹的可怕场景。
近义词: forget, unimagine, dismiss from mind
反义词: imagine, visualize, conjure up
词性:v.
