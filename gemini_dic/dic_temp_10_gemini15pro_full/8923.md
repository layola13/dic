antigun:/ˈænˌtiˈɡʌn/ | adj. | 反枪的，反对枪支的 | Opposed to the ownership or use of guns, especially advocating for stricter gun control laws. |  The politician took an antigun stance after the school shooting.  这位政治家在校园枪击案后采取了反枪的立场。| 近义词: anti-firearm, pro-gun control | 反义词: pro-gun, pro-firearm | 词性: 形容词

antigun:/ˈænˌtiˈɡʌn/ | n. | 反枪者，反对枪支的人 | A person who opposes the ownership or use of guns. |  Many antiguns advocate for stricter background checks. 许多反枪者主张更严格的背景调查。 | 近义词: gun control advocate | 反义词: gun rights advocate | 词性: 名词
