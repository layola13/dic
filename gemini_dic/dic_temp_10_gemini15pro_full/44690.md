cruddiest:/ˈkrʌdiɪst/
adjective
最糟糕的，最差的
superlative adjective of cruddy: extremely bad or unpleasant
The food at that restaurant was the cruddiest I've ever had. 那家餐馆的食物是我吃过最难吃的。
Synonyms: worst, lousiest, rottenest, most unpleasant
Antonyms: best, finest, greatest, most pleasant
词性: 形容词

cruddy:/ˈkrʌdi/
adjective
糟糕的，差的
informal: very bad or unpleasant
I had a cruddy day at work. 我今天工作很不顺心。
Synonyms: lousy, rotten, awful, terrible
Antonyms: great, wonderful, excellent, fantastic
词性: 形容词
