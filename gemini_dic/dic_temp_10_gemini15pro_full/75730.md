fullback:/ˈfʊlbæk/ (US), /ˈfʊlbæk/ (UK)| n.| 后卫（足球、橄榄球等）|A player positioned behind the other backs, typically having a defensive role.|The fullback made a crucial tackle to prevent a goal.  后卫做出了关键的铲断阻止了进球。

The team's fullback is known for his powerful kicks.  这支球队的后卫以其强大的踢球能力而闻名。|近义词：defender, guard|反义词：forward, striker|词性:noun

fullback:/ˈfʊlbæk/ (US), /ˈfʊlbæk/ (UK)| adj.| 后卫的|Of or relating to the position of fullback.|He plays a fullback role in the team. 他在队里担任后卫的角色。

She has the build of a fullback. 她有着后卫的体格。|近义词：defensive|反义词：offensive|词性:adjective

词源:  "fullback" comes from "full" + "back," referring to the player's position furthest back in the traditional formations of certain sports.
