glitching:/ˈɡlɪtʃɪŋ/| n. |小故障，失灵|A malfunction or error in a system, typically electronic, often temporary and causing a brief interruption or unexpected result. |The computer kept glitching, making it difficult to work. 电脑一直出故障，很难工作。|近义词：bug, error, malfunction, fault, glitch |反义词:smooth operation, proper function| noun

glitching:/ˈɡlɪtʃɪŋ/| adj. |有故障的，失灵的|Experiencing glitches; malfunctioning.  |The glitching graphics made the game unplayable.  画面故障导致游戏无法进行。|近义词：malfunctioning, faulty, buggy |反义词:functioning, working| adjective

glitching:/ˈɡlɪtʃɪŋ/| v. |(it)发生小故障，失灵|(Of a system, especially electronic) suffer a temporary malfunction or fault. |My phone screen started glitching after I dropped it. 我的手机屏幕在我摔了之后开始出现故障。|近义词：malfunction, fail, act up, bug out |反义词:function, work, operate| verb
