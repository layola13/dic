hemoptysis:/ˌhiːmˈɒptɪsɪs/ (US) /ˌhiːˈmɒptɪsɪs/ (UK)| n. |咳血|The coughing up of blood or blood-stained mucus from the bronchi, larynx, trachea, or lungs.|例句：The patient presented with hemoptysis and shortness of breath. 病人出现咳血和呼吸急促的症状。

例句：Hemoptysis can be a sign of a serious underlying condition. 咳血可能是严重潜在疾病的征兆。|近义词：blood spitting, coughing up blood|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *haima* "blood" + *ptysis* "spitting," from *ptuein* "to spit."
