bumpiest:/ˈbʌmpiɪst/| adj.|最颠簸的|Superlative form of bumpy: having a rough or uneven surface.|The bumpiest road I've ever driven on was in the countryside. 我开过最颠簸的路是在乡下。|近义词：roughest, unevenest, most irregular|反义词:smoothest, flattest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From bump + -y + -est.  "Bump" comes from Middle English *bompen.
