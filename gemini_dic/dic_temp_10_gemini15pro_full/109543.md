lesser:/ˈlɛsər/| adj.|较小的，次要的|Of two things, the lesser is the one that is smaller in size, amount, importance, etc.  指两者之间较小的，较次要的。|He chose the lesser of two evils. 他选择了两种恶中较轻的一种。|近义词：smaller, minor, inferior|反义词:greater, major, superior|形容词

lesser:/ˈlɛsər/| n.|较小者，次要者|(archaic) A smaller or inferior person or thing.  较小或次等的人或物（古语）。|The lesser of the two warriors fell first.  两个战士中较弱的那个先倒下了。|近义词：inferior|反义词:superior|名词
