basketball:/ˈbæskɪtbɔːl/ (US) /ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl/ (UK)| n.|篮球|A game played between two teams of five players in which goals are scored by throwing a ball through a netted hoop fixed above each end of the court. Also, the ball itself.|He plays basketball every weekend. 他每个周末都打篮球。|近义词：roundball, hoops|反义词: None|noun
