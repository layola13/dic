nonprocessional:/ˌnɑːnprəˈsɛʃənəl/
adjective
非宗教仪式的，非游行的
Not pertaining to a procession; not done in a formal procession.
The bride opted for a nonprocessional entrance, simply walking into the venue with her father.  新娘选择了一种非游行的入场方式，只是和她的父亲一起走进了场地。
The conference had a nonprocessional opening, with attendees simply finding their seats. 会议开幕式没有游行，与会者只是各自找到座位。
synonyms: informal, casual, unceremonious
antonyms: processional, formal, ceremonial
词性: adjective
