home-free:/ˌhoʊm ˈfriː/
adjective
安全的；成功的；稳操胜券的
Safe from danger or failure; assured of success.
The team was home-free after scoring the winning touchdown in the final seconds.  球队在最后几秒钟得分后稳操胜券。
We're home-free! The project has been approved.  我们成功了！项目已获批准。
近义词:safe, secure, in the clear
反义词:in danger, at risk, vulnerable
词性:adjective

home-free (hyphenated) is derived from the baseball idiom "home free," which refers to a baserunner who is sure to score a run once they round third base and head for home plate.  Over time, the phrase has been broadened to apply to any situation where success is certain.
