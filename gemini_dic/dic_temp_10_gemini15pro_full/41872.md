cosecant: /ˌkoʊˈsiːkənt/ (US), /ˌkəʊˈsiːkənt/ (UK)| n. |余割|The cosecant of an angle in a right triangle is the ratio of the length of the hypotenuse to the length of the side opposite the angle.  直角三角形中一个角的余割是斜边长度与该角对边长度的比值。|The cosecant of 30 degrees is 2. 30度的余割是2。

The cosecant function is the reciprocal of the sine function. 余割函数是正弦函数的倒数。|例句：The cosecant of angle A is denoted as csc A.  A角的余割记作csc A。|近义词：1/sin|反义词: sine|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cosecans, present participle of cosecāre (“to cut off”), from co- (“together”) + secāre (“to cut”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *cosecans，cosecāre（“切断”）的现在分词，来自 co-（“一起”）+ secāre（“切断”）。
