gaultheria:/ɡɔːlˈθɪəriə/ | n. |冬青 |A genus of shrubs in the heath family (Ericaceae), native to North and South America, Asia, and Australasia, and having aromatic evergreen leaves and small, usually white or pink, flowers. |The gaultheria plants in my garden are thriving. 我花园里的冬青长得很好。| Gaultheria procumbens is commonly known as wintergreen. 平铺白珠树通常被称为冬青。| 近义词:wintergreen (especially for *Gaultheria procumbens*) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant genus) | 词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Jean-François Gaultier (1708-1756), a French-Canadian physician and botanist.
