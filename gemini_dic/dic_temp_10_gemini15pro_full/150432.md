pansy:/ˈpænzi/| n. |三色堇；（冒犯语）娘娘腔的男人|A garden flower with velvety petals, typically of three colours.  A derogatory term for an effeminate man, implying weakness or homosexuality.|例句：The pansies in her garden were a vibrant mix of purple, yellow, and white. 她花园里的三色堇是紫色、黄色和白色的鲜艳混合。

例句：Calling someone a "pansy" is offensive and homophobic.  称呼某人为“娘娘腔”是冒犯性的，并且带有恐同意味。|近义词：violet, viola (for the flower); sissy, wimp (derogatory term)|反义词: macho (derogatory term)|词性:noun
