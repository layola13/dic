dongas:/ˈdɒŋɡəz/ (UK), /ˈdɑːŋɡəz/ (US)| n. |工棚，临时住所|A temporary, often rudimentary shelter, usually made of corrugated iron or other readily available materials, commonly used in mining camps or construction sites.  Originates from South African English.|The miners lived in dongas near the mine shaft. 矿工们住在矿井附近的工棚里.|Synonyms: shack, hut, shanty|Antonyms: mansion, palace|noun

dongas:/ˈdɒŋɡəz/ (UK), /ˈdɑːŋɡəz/ (US)| n. |沟壑，峡谷 (less common)|A gully or ravine, particularly one formed by erosion. This usage is less common than the meaning of "temporary shelter."|Heavy rains carved deep dongas into the landscape.  暴雨在景观中刻出了深深的沟壑。|Synonyms: gully, ravine, ditch|Antonyms: ridge, hill|noun
