diffract:/dɪˈfrækt/ | v. |(使)衍射，(使)绕射 | To cause (a beam of light or other wave form) to spread out after passing through a narrow aperture or across an edge. | Light is diffracted when it passes through a narrow slit. 光线通过狭缝时会发生衍射。| The prism diffracted the light into a rainbow of colors. 棱镜将光衍射成彩虹的颜色。 | synonyms: disperse, scatter, spread | antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb
