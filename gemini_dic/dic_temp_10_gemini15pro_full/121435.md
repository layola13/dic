misinstructs:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌkts/
verb
错误地指导，误导
To instruct wrongly; to give incorrect or misleading instructions.
The manager misinstructed the employees on the new safety procedures, leading to several accidents.  经理错误地指导了员工新的安全程序，导致了几起事故。
misleads, misguides, misdirects
instructs correctly, guides correctly, directs correctly
