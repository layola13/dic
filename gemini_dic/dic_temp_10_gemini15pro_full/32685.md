chlamydia: /kləˈmɪdiə/ | n. | 衣原体感染; 衣原体 | A sexually transmitted infection caused by the bacterium Chlamydia trachomatis. |  Chlamydia is a common sexually transmitted infection. 衣原体是一种常见的性传播感染。| Chlamydia can cause infertility if left untreated. 如果不治疗，衣原体会导致不孕。 | 近义词: STI, STD | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可用 health 健康 来表示相对的概念) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Chlamydia, from Ancient Greek χλαμύς (khlamús, “cloak, mantle”) +‎ -id (suffix forming nouns referring to things shaped like the original word).  The bacteria are so named because of the appearance of their inclusion bodies within infected cells.
