half-lunged:/ˌhɑːfˈlʌndʒd/| adj.|半弓步的，半蹲的|Being in a position with one leg bent and the other extended backward, as in a lunge position, but only partially.  It describes a posture or stance.|The photographer captured the dancer in a half-lunged pose. 摄影师捕捉到了舞者半弓步的姿势。|The boxer stood in a half-lunged stance, ready to strike. 拳击手摆出半弓步的姿势，准备出击。|Near synonyms: semi-lunged, partially lunged|Antonym:  standing upright, fully extended|adjective
