greyness:/ˈɡreɪnəs/| n.|灰暗，灰色, 阴沉|The greyness of the sky mirrored the greyness of his mood. 天空的灰暗反映了他阴沉的心情。|例句：The greyness of the winter days made her feel depressed. 冬日灰暗的天色让她感到沮丧。

The pervasive greyness of the city landscape was oppressive. 城市景观无处不在的灰暗令人感到压抑。|近义词：dullness, drabness, gloominess|反义词:brightness, vividness|词性:名词

词源: From grey + -ness.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *grēwaz.
