frictionize:/ˈfrɪkʃənaɪz/
verb
使产生摩擦；增加摩擦力
To cause or increase friction.
The brakes were frictionized to improve stopping power. 刹车被摩擦化以提高制动力。
The surface of the road was frictionized to prevent skidding. 路面经过摩擦处理以防止打滑。
近义词:roughen, abrade
反义词:smooth, polish
词性:v.
