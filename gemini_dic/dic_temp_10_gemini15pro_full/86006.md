headwaters:/ˈhɛdˌwɔːtərz/ (US), /ˈhedˌwɔːtəz/ (UK)| n. |源头，上游|The source of a stream or river.|例句：The headwaters of the Amazon River are in the Andes Mountains. 亚马逊河的源头在安第斯山脉。

例句：Protecting the headwaters is crucial for the health of the entire river system. 保护河流源头对于整个河流系统的健康至关重要。

近义词：source, origin, fountainhead|反义词:mouth, delta|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From head + waters.  "Head" in this context refers to the beginning or top of something, and "waters" refers to a body of water.  Thus, "headwaters" literally means the beginning waters of a river or stream.
