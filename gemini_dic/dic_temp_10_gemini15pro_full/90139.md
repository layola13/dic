hotchpotch: n.
hotchpotch: 混合物，大杂烩
hotchpotch: A confused mixture of different things.
hotchpotch: The play was a hotchpotch of different styles and periods.  这出戏是不同风格和时期的杂烩。
hotchpotch:  The new housing development is a hotchpotch of different architectural styles. 新的住宅区是不同建筑风格的大杂烩。

hotchpotch:  order, uniformity
