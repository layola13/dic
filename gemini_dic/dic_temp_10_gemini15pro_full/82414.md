gruel:/ɡruːəl/ | n. |稀粥，麦片粥；乏味的东西|A thin, watery porridge, typically made of oatmeal or other grain.  Figuratively, something tedious or unpleasant.|例句：The prisoners were given a meager gruel for breakfast.  囚犯们早餐只得到稀薄的麦片粥。

例句：The film was a sentimental gruel. 这部电影是一部感伤的乏味之作。|近义词：porridge, slop, mush|反义词：feast, banquet|词性:noun
