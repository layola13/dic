clicking:/ˈklɪkɪŋ/| n. v. adj.|点击，咔嗒声，契合|A short, sharp sound as of a switch being operated or of two hard objects coming quickly into contact.  Also, the act of making such a sound, or the act of pressing a computer mouse button.|The clicking of the mouse echoed in the quiet room. 鼠标的咔嗒声在安静的房间里回荡。|近义词：tapping, ticking|反义词:dragging|词性: Noun, Verb, Adjective (present participle)

clicking:/ˈklɪkɪŋ/| n. v. adj.|点击，咔嗒声，契合|The act of pressing a button on a computer mouse.  Also describes things fitting together perfectly, or people getting along very well.|We clicked right away and became fast friends. 我们一拍即合，很快就成了好朋友。|近义词：connecting, harmonizing|反义词:clashing|词性: Noun, Verb, Adjective (present participle)
