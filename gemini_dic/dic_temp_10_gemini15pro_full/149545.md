pageant:/ˈpædʒənt/| n. | 盛会，庆典；选美比赛；虚饰，炫耀 | a public entertainment consisting of a procession of people in elaborate, colourful costumes, or an outdoor performance of a historical scene.  A beauty contest. A showy display. |  例句：The town held a pageant to celebrate its bicentennial.  小镇举行了一场盛会来庆祝其二百周年纪念。

例句：She won the Miss World pageant in 2000.  她在2000年赢得了世界小姐选美比赛。| 近义词：ceremony, festival, celebration, spectacle, show, competition | 反义词：simplicity, modesty | 词性: 名词
