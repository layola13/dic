hypocone:/ˈhaɪpəʊkəʊn/| n.| 次尖|A cusp on the distal lingual side of an upper molar tooth in mammals.|The hypocone is a key feature in distinguishing different mammalian species. 次尖是区分不同哺乳动物物种的关键特征。|The presence of a hypocone indicates a more advanced evolutionary stage. 存在次尖表明进化阶段更为高级。|近义词: cusp|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps absence of a hypocone)|词性:noun
