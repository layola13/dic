carbonises:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/
verb
使炭化，烧焦
to change something into carbon by burning or heating it
The intense heat carbonises the wood, turning it into charcoal.  高温使木材炭化，将其变成了木炭。
carbonize, char, scorch
mineralize

词性:v.
