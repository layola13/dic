aerospace:/ˈɛəroʊspeɪs/ | n. | 航空航天；航空航天技术 | The design and construction of aircraft and spacecraft, and the technology supporting these activities. | The aerospace industry is constantly innovating. 航空航天工业不断创新。|  The government is investing heavily in aerospace research. 政府正在大力投资航空航天研究。 | synonyms: aeronautics, astronautics | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology->
From aero- + space.  "Aero-" comes from Greek ἀήρ (aēr, “air”). "Space" comes from Old French espace (“space, area”), from Latin spatium (“room, area, distance”).
