noncomprehensible:/ˌnɒnkɒmprɪˈhensɪbl/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːmprɪˈhensəbl/| adj.| 不可理解的，费解的|not able to be understood; not intelligible| The ancient text was written in a noncomprehensible language. 这份古代文献是用一种无法理解的语言写成的。| The technical jargon was noncomprehensible to the average person.  这些技术术语对普通人来说是难以理解的。|近义词：incomprehensible, unintelligible, unfathomable, obscure|反义词：comprehensible, understandable, clear, lucid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + comprehensible.  "Comprehensible" comes from Late Middle English: from Old French comprehensible, from late Latin comprehensibilis, from comprehendere ‘to grasp mentally’.
