chaperonages:/ˈʃæpərəʊnɪdʒɪz/
n.
陪伴；监护；伴护
The act of chaperoning someone, or the state of being chaperoned.
例句：The school dance required chaperonages from parents. 学校舞会需要家长陪同。

例句：The chaperonages at the prom were strict.  毕业舞会的监护很严格。

近义词：supervision, guidance, escort
反义词：neglect, abandonment
词性：名词
