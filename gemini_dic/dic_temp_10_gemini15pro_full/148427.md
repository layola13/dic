overstiffen:/ˌoʊvərˈstɪfən/| v. |使…过硬，使…僵硬|To make something excessively stiff or rigid.|The starch overstiffened the collar of his shirt, making it uncomfortable. 浆洗使得他的衬衫领子过硬，穿起来很不舒服。|The artist overstiffened the clay, making it difficult to mold.  这位艺术家把黏土弄得太硬了，难以塑形。|近义词：overharden, make rigid|反义词:soften, loosen|词性:动词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "stiffen" (verb meaning "to make or become stiff")
