malapropists:/ˌmæləˈprɒpɪsts/ | n. |  误用词语者；用词错误的人 | A person who habitually misuses words, especially by confusing words that are similar in sound. |  The comedian's character was a classic malapropist, constantly mixing up words to hilarious effect.  这位喜剧演员的角色是一个典型的误用词语者，经常把单词混淆，造成滑稽的效果。 | synonyms: misuser of words, blunderer | antonyms:  linguist, lexicographer | noun

malapropism:/ˌmæləˈprɒpɪzəm/ | n. |  词语误用；用词错误 | The unintentional misuse of a word by confusion with one that sounds similar. |  "Dance a flamingo " (instead of flamenco) is a classic malapropism. “跳弗拉明戈舞” （而不是弗拉明戈舞）是一个典型的词语误用。 | synonyms: solecism, misusage, catachresis | antonyms: correct usage | noun
