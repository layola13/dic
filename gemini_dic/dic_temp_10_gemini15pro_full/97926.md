innocuous:/ɪˈnɒkjuəs/ /ɪˈnɑːkjuəs/| adj.|无害的；无恶意的|Not harmful or offensive.|例句：The spider appeared innocuous, but it was actually quite venomous. 这只蜘蛛看起来无害，但实际上毒性很强。

例句：He told a few innocuous jokes to lighten the mood. 他讲了几个无伤大雅的笑话来活跃气氛。

近义词：harmless, safe, inoffensive, benign|反义词：harmful, dangerous, noxious, pernicious|词性：形容词
