ilate:/ˈaɪleɪt/| v. | 积累，沉淀；隔离，分离(尤指蛋白质) | To accumulate or deposit; to isolate or separate (especially proteins). | The antibody was isolated from the blood sample.  抗体是从血液样本中分离出来的。

The minerals ilate from the solution. 矿物质从溶液中沉淀出来。 | 近义词：accumulate, deposit, isolate, separate, precipitate | 反义词: disperse, dissolve, distribute | 词性:动词

词源: From Latin *ilatus, past participle of *inferre ("to bring in, carry in"), from in- + ferre ("to carry, bear").  来源于拉丁语 *ilatus，*inferre（“带入，运入”）的过去分词，来自 in- + ferre（“携带，承受”）。
