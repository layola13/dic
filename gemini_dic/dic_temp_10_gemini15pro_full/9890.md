apathetic:/ˌæpəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 冷漠的，无动于衷的 | Having or showing little or no emotion; not interested or concerned; indifferent. | He was apathetic about the outcome of the election. 他对选举结果漠不关心。|  She remained apathetic despite the pleas for help. 尽管有人恳求帮助，她仍然无动于衷。 | 近义词: indifferent, uninterested, listless, passive | 反义词: enthusiastic, passionate, concerned, interested | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek "apathes" (without feeling), from "a-" (without) + "pathos" (feeling).
