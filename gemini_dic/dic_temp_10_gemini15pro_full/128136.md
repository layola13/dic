nethinim:/ˈnɛθɪnɪm/| n.|尼提宁人，圣殿仆役|Temple servants or assistants who performed menial tasks in the Jewish Temple.  They were likely descendants of the Gibeonites who were cursed by Joshua to be hewers of wood and drawers of water for the Israelites.|The Nethinim played a vital role in the upkeep of the Temple. 尼提宁人在维护圣殿方面发挥了至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: servants, assistants, laborers|Antonyms: masters, leaders|Noun
