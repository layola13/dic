grandparental:/ˌɡrændpəˈrɛntl/ | adj. |祖父母的，祖父般的|Of or relating to grandparents. |例句：She felt a surge of grandparental pride watching her grandson graduate. 她看着孙子毕业，心中涌起一股祖父母的自豪感。

例句：He offered some grandparental advice about saving money. 他提供了一些关于存钱的祖父般的建议。|近义词：ancestral |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: adjective

Etymology->
From grandparent + -al.  "Grandparent" itself comes from the Old French "grandparent," with "grand" meaning "great" and "parent" meaning "parent."
