brothers:/ˈbrʌðərz/| n. |兄弟; 同事; 同胞;|  Male siblings.  Members of a male religious order or fraternity. People who share a common cause or interest. |My brothers are my best friends. 我的兄弟们是我最好的朋友。| He joined the brotherhood of monks. 他加入了僧侣兄弟会。|synonyms: siblings, brethren, comrades, fellows |antonyms: sisters, opponents, enemies|noun
