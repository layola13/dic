harrower:/ˈhærəʊər/ (US) /ˈhærəʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|耙地机；折磨者；掠夺者|A person or thing that harrows (distresses or torments).  A farm implement used to break up and level plowed ground.|The farmer used a harrower after plowing the field. 农民在犁地后使用了耙地机。|近义词：tormentor, oppressor, raider|反义词:comforter, savior|noun

harrow:/ˈhæroʊ/ (US) /ˈhærəʊ/ (UK)| vt. |耙地；使痛苦；折磨；掠夺|To draw a harrow over (land). To cause distress to.|The experience harrowed him deeply. 这段经历使他深感痛苦。|近义词：torment, distress, pillage|反义词:comfort, soothe|verb
