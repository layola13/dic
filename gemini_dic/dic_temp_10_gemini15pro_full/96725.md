inefficiencies:/ɪnɪˈfɪʃənsiz/
|n.|低效；效率低下；无效率|The report highlighted the inefficiencies in the current system. 报告强调了当前系统存在的效率低下的问题。|
|近义词:|ineffectiveness, incompetence, inadequacy, wastefulness|
|反义词:|efficiency, effectiveness, competence, productivity|
|词性:|名词 (plural)|


inefficiency:/ɪnɪˈfɪʃənsi/
|n.|低效；效率低下；无效率|The inefficiency of the old machinery cost the company a lot of money. 旧机器的低效率使公司损失了很多钱。|
|近义词:|ineffectiveness, incompetence, inadequacy, wastefulness|

|词性:|名词 (singular)|


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + efficient + -cy (noun suffix).  "Efficient" comes from Latin efficiēns, present participle of efficiō (“I bring about, accomplish”), from ex- (“out”) + faciō (“I do, make”).
