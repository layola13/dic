omissive:/oʊˈmɪsɪv/ /əˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
疏忽的，遗漏的
failing to include or do something
The report was criticized for being omissive.  这份报告因有所遗漏而受到批评。
She was omissive in her duties. 她玩忽职守。
Synonyms: negligent, remiss, careless, neglectful, derelict
Antonyms: thorough, meticulous, careful, diligent, attentive
词性: 形容词
