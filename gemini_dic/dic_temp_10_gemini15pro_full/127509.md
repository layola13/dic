neckplate:/ˈnekˌpleɪt/| n. |颈板，护颈板|A metal plate that protects or reinforces the neck of a stringed instrument, such as a guitar or banjo, or that covers the heel of the neck where it joins the body.|The guitar's neckplate was engraved with a beautiful floral design. 这把吉他的颈板雕刻着美丽的图案。|Synonyms: neck reinforcement, heelplate|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider terms like "unreinforced neck")|Noun
