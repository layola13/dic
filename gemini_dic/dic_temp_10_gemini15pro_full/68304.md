falcons:/ˈfɔːlkənz/ (US) /ˈfɔːlkənz/ (UK)| n. |猎鹰（复数）|A falcon is a bird of prey with long pointed wings and a notched beak, known for its swift flight and keen eyesight.  They are often trained for falconry.|例句：The falcons soared high above the mountains. 猎鹰在群山上空翱翔。

例句：Trained falcons were used for hunting in medieval times.  在中世纪，训练有素的猎鹰被用于狩猎。|近义词：hawks, raptors|反义词:prey|词性:名词
