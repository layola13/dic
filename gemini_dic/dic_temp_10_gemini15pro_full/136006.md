nonrationalists:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəlɪsts/ | n. |非理性主义者| People who do not base their beliefs or actions on reason and logic.  Those who prioritize emotions, intuition, or other non-rational factors in their decision-making processes. |例句：The nonrationalists in the group were more likely to be swayed by emotional appeals.  小组中非理性主义者更容易被情绪化的诉求所左右。

例句：Some philosophers argue that nonrationalists can still arrive at truth through different pathways. 一些哲学家认为，非理性主义者仍然可以通过不同的途径获得真理。|近义词：irrationalists, mystics, sentimentalists|反义词: rationalists, logicians, empiricists|词性:noun
