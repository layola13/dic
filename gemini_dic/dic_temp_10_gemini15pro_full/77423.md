gavialoid:/ˈɡeɪviəlɔɪd/| adj. n.|  gavialoids (n. pl.)|  像长吻鳄的；长吻鳄类的；长吻鳄类动物 (n.) |Resembling or characteristic of a gavial; belonging to the Gavialoidea, a group of crocodilians that includes the gharial and its extinct relatives. |The gavialoid skull is long and slender, adapted for catching fish.  长吻鳄类的头骨又长又细，适合捕鱼。

The false gharial is a gavialoid crocodilian found in Southeast Asia.  马来长吻鳄是一种在东南亚发现的长吻鳄类鳄鱼。|近义词：gharial-like, long-snouted (when referring to the skull)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could use broad-snouted for the skull shape contrast)|词性: adjective, noun
