droned:/droʊnd/| v.|嗡嗡作响；单调地说；无人机飞行|to make a continuous low humming sound; to speak in a monotonous tone; (of a drone) to fly.|The bees droned lazily in the garden. 蜜蜂在花园里懒洋洋地嗡嗡叫。|近义词：hum, buzz, murmur|反义词：shout, yell|词性：动词

droned:/droʊnd/| adj.|单调的；无人机控制的|monotonous; controlled by a drone|His droned voice made me sleepy. 他单调的声音让我昏昏欲睡。|近义词：monotonous, tedious, dull|反义词：varied, exciting, lively|词性：形容词
