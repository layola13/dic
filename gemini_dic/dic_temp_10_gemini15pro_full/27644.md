candescence:/kænˈdɛsəns/
n.|白热；炽热；光辉|The candescence of the molten metal was blinding. 熔融金属的白热光令人目眩。|近义词：incandescence, brilliance, glow, luminosity|反义词：darkness, dimness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin "candēscere" (to become white-hot), from "candēre" (to glow white).
candescent:/kænˈdɛsənt/
adj.|白热的；炽热的；光辉的|The candescent filament in the light bulb glowed brightly. 灯泡里的白炽灯丝发出明亮的光芒。|近义词：incandescent, brilliant, glowing, luminous|反义词：dark, dim|词性:形容词
