cenobitical:/ˌsɛnəˈbɪtɪkəl/
adjective
修道院的；集体生活的
Relating to or characteristic of communal monastic life.
The monks lived a cenobitical existence, sharing meals and prayers.  修道士们过着集体生活，一起吃饭和祈祷。
The cenobitical rules were strict, requiring obedience and silence.  修道院的规矩很严格，要求服从和沉默。
