motley:/ˈmɒtli (UK), ˈmɑːtli (US)| adj. n.|杂色的，混杂的；杂色衣服，混杂的一群人|Made up of many different people or things.|The jester wore a motley tunic. 小丑穿着一件杂色的外衣.|The market attracted a motley crew of shoppers. 市场吸引了形形色色的购物者.|Synonyms: diverse, varied, heterogeneous, assorted, miscellaneous|Antonyms: uniform, homogeneous, consistent|adjective, noun

motleys:/ˈmɒtliz (UK), ˈmɑːtliz (US)| n.|杂色衣服，混杂的一群人 (motley 的复数形式，较少使用)|Plural form of motley, referring to various sets of motley clothing or diverse groups.|He donned one of his many motleys for the Renaissance fair. 他为文艺复兴节穿上他众多杂色服装中的一件。|Several motleys of performers gathered backstage. 几群不同的表演者聚集在后台。|Synonyms: assortments, collections, groups|Antonyms: uniformities, homogeneities|noun
