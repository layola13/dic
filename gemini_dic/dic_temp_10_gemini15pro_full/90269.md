householders:/ˈhaʊshəʊldəz/ (British) /ˈhaʊsholdərz/ (American)| n. |住户，户主|People who own or rent a house and live in it.  A householder is responsible for paying bills and maintaining the property.|例句：The survey was conducted among 1,000 householders. 这项调查是在1000名住户中进行的。|例句：Many householders are struggling with rising energy costs. 许多住户正努力应对不断上涨的能源成本。|近义词：residents, occupants, homeowners, tenants|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: landlord, property manager)|词性:noun
