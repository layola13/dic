papized:/ˈpeɪpaɪzd/| adj.| 教皇化的，罗马天主教化的 |Converted to Roman Catholicism; made to conform to Roman Catholic doctrine or practice.| The king tried to papize the country, but met with strong resistance. 国王试图将国家天主教化，但遭到了强烈抵抗。|Synonyms: Romanized, Catholicized|Antonyms: secularized, laicized|adjective
Etymology: From "pape" (Old French for "pope") + "-ize" (suffix meaning "to make or become like"). 源自“pape”（古法语，意为“教皇”）+“-ize”（后缀，意为“使成为……或变得像……”）。
