heathens:/ˈhiːðənz/ | n. |异教徒，不信教的人|People who do not belong to a widely held religion (especially one that is considered traditional) are often referred to as heathens. |The missionaries sought to convert the heathens. 传教士试图改变异教徒的信仰。

|They were considered heathens by the devout Christians.  虔诚的基督徒认为他们是异教徒。|Synonyms: pagans, infidels, unbelievers, nonbelievers |Antonyms: believers, the faithful|Noun
