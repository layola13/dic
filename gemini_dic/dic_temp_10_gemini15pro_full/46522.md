dakokuya: /dɑːkoʊkʊjɑː/ | n. | 打刻屋 (Time-clock station/office) | A place in a company or factory where employees clock in and clock out using a time clock.  This word is borrowed directly from Japanese. | Example: He hurried to the dakokuya to clock in before his shift started. 他急忙赶到打刻屋，以便在轮班开始前打卡。| Example: The dakokuya was crowded with employees at the end of the workday. 工作日结束时，打刻屋挤满了员工。 | Synonym: time clock station, timekeeping office | Antonym: N/A | Part of Speech: Noun
