antihumanism:/ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪzəm/ |n.|反人文主义|A philosophical stance that critiques or opposes the principles of humanism, often emphasizing the limitations or negative aspects of human reason, ethics, or culture. It can manifest in various forms, from questioning the centrality of human experience to emphasizing the importance of non-human factors in shaping the world.|Postmodernism is often seen as a form of antihumanism, rejecting the idea of a universal human nature. 后现代主义通常被视为一种反人文主义，它反对普遍人性的概念。

The rise of artificial intelligence has sparked new debates about antihumanism and the future of humanity. 人工智能的兴起引发了关于反人文主义和人类未来的新争论。|近义词:misanthropy, inhumanity|反义词:humanism|词性:名词
