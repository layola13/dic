leanest:/ˈliːnɪst/ | adj. | 最瘦的; 最精简的; 最贫乏的; |  Containing the least amount of fat;  most slender; also, containing the least amount of superfluity; most efficient. |  The leanest cut of meat is often the most expensive.  最瘦的肉通常是最贵的。| synonyms: slimmest, thinnest, skinniest, gauntest, sparest, most efficient, most streamlined | antonyms: fattest, thickest, heaviest, plumpest, fleshiest, least efficient | adjective
