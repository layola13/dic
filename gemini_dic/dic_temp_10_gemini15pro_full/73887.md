foro:/ˈfɔːroʊ/ (US), /ˈfɔːrəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 论坛，讨论区 | An online message board or discussion group where people can share their thoughts and ideas on a particular topic. | Example: The online foro was buzzing with activity after the announcement.  公告发布后，网上论坛一片热闹。

Example:  He posted a question on the photography foro. 他在摄影论坛上发布了一个问题。

|Synonym: forum, message board, discussion group|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "private conversation")| Noun
