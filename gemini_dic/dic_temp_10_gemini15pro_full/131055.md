nonballoting:/ˌnɒnˈbælətɪŋ/
adjective
非投票的; 无投票权的
Not having the right to vote.
例句：Nonballoting members of the committee were still allowed to participate in discussions.  委员会中无投票权的成员仍然被允许参与讨论。

例句：The nonballoting delegates observed the proceedings. 无投票权的代表旁听了会议进程。

近义词：voteless, disenfranchised
反义词：voting, enfranchised
词性：adjective

词源：
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "balloting" (present participle of "ballot," from Italian "ballotta," meaning "little ball used in voting")
