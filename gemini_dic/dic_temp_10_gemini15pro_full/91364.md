hydrosphere:/ˈhaɪdrəsfɪr/ | n. | 水圈 | All the waters on the earth's surface, such as lakes and seas, and sometimes including water over the earth's surface, such as clouds. | The hydrosphere covers over 70% of the Earth's surface.  地球表面70%以上都被水圈覆盖。| synonyms: water sphere | antonyms: lithosphere | noun
