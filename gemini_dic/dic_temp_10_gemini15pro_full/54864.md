displeased:/dɪsˈpliːzd/| adj.|不高兴的，不满的| feeling or showing annoyance and disapproval |例句：She was displeased with his performance. 她对他的表现不满意。|近义词：unhappy, dissatisfied, annoyed, irritated|反义词：pleased, happy, satisfied|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + pleased (from Old French plaisir, "to please").
