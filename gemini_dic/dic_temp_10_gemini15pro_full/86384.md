hedgebote:/ˈhɛdʒˌboʊt/| n.|修篱权；篱笆木料|The right of a tenant to take wood from the landlord's estate for the repair of fences.|The tenant exercised his right of hedgebote to repair the broken fence. 房客行使了他修篱的权利来修理破损的围栏。|近义词:fencebote|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *heggebot*, from Old English *heʒebōt*  "hedge-repair," from *heʒe* "hedge" + *bōt* "remedy, repair."
