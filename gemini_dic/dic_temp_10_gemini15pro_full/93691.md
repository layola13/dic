imitate:/ˈɪmɪteɪt/| v. |模仿，效仿|To copy someone's behavior, speech, appearance, etc.|She imitated her mother's voice perfectly. 她完美地模仿了她母亲的声音。|He tried to imitate the bird's song. 他试着模仿鸟的叫声。|近义词：copy, mimic, emulate|反义词：originate, invent|词性:动词
