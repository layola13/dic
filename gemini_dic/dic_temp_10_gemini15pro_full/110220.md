lifebuoy:/ˈlaɪfˌbɔɪ/| n.|救生圈，救生衣|A ring-shaped or horseshoe-shaped device, typically made of cork or other buoyant material, used to keep a person afloat in water.|The lifebuoy was thrown to the struggling swimmer. 救生圈被扔给了挣扎的游泳者。|Synonyms: life preserver, lifebelt, life ring, float|Antonyms: anchor, weight|noun


lifebuoy:/ˈlaɪfˌbɔɪ/| n.|（肥皂品牌名）舒肤佳|A brand of soap.|I use Lifebuoy soap for its antibacterial properties. 我用舒肤佳肥皂是因为它的抗菌特性。|Synonyms: soap, cleanser|Antonyms: N/A|noun
