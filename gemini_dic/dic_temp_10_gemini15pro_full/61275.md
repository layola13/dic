emasculator:/ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪtər/ | n. | 去势器；阉割器 | A surgical instrument used for castration. |  The veterinarian used an emasculator to castrate the bull. 兽医用去势器阉割了公牛。|  Synonyms: castrator, gelder | Antonyms: N/A | noun

emasculate:/ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪt/ | vt. | 阉割；使柔弱；削弱 | 1. To deprive of strength or vigor; weaken. 2. To castrate. | 1.  The new law emasculated the power of the governor. 新法律削弱了州长的权力。 2.  The farmer emasculated the young calves. 农夫阉割了小牛犊。 | Synonyms: weaken, debilitate, enfeeble, castrate, geld | Antonyms: strengthen, empower, invigorate | verb
