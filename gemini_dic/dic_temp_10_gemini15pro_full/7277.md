angostura:/ˌæŋɡəˈstʊərə/ | n. |安哥斯图拉树皮；安哥斯图拉苦酒|The bark of a South American tree (Galipea saroparensis syn. Cusparia febrifuga) used to make angostura bitters.  A type of bitters made from the angostura bark and other flavorings.| I added a dash of angostura bitters to my cocktail. 我在我的鸡尾酒里加了一点安格斯图拉苦酒。|Synonym: bitters|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific ingredient)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Angostura, the former name of Ciudad Bolívar, Venezuela, where it was originally manufactured.
