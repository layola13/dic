orangutans:/ɔːˈræŋuːtæŋz/ | n. | 猩猩 |  A large, arboreal ape with reddish-brown hair, long arms, and no tail, native to the forests of Borneo and Sumatra.  They are known for their intelligence and tool-using abilities. |  Orangutans are critically endangered due to habitat loss. 猩猩由于栖息地丧失而极度濒危。 | synonyms: ape, primate | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | noun
