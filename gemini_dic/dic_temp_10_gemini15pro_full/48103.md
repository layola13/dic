declinatory:/dɪˈklɪnətɔːri/
adjective
拒绝的，推却的，回避的
Relating to or expressing refusal or avoidance, especially of responsibility or jurisdiction.
The defendant filed a declinatory plea, arguing that the court lacked jurisdiction.  被告提出了拒绝受理的抗辩，认为法院缺乏管辖权。
The company issued a declinatory statement regarding the merger proposal. 公司就合并提案发表了拒绝声明。
近义词: refusing, rejecting, evasive, demurring
反义词: accepting, agreeable, receptive
词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *dēclīnātōrius, from Latin dēclīnātus, past participle of dēclīnāre (“to bend downwards, turn aside”), from dē- (“from, down from”) + clīnāre (“to bend, incline”).
