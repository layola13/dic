clergymen:/ˈklɜːrdʒɪmən/ /ˈklɝːdʒɪmən/| n. |神职人员，牧师（尤指基督教）|A clergyman is a male member of the clergy, especially a Christian priest, pastor, or minister.|例句：The clergyman delivered a moving sermon on Sunday.  牧师在星期天发表了一篇感人的布道。

例句：Several clergymen attended the funeral. 几位神职人员参加了葬礼。

近义词：priest, pastor, minister, parson, cleric |反义词:laity |词性:noun
