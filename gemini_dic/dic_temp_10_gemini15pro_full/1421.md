accustoms: /əˈkʌstəmz/
verb
使习惯于
To make someone or something familiar with something through repeated exposure or experience.
She accustomed herself to the cold weather. 她让自己习惯了寒冷的天气。

The soldiers quickly accustomed themselves to the harsh conditions. 士兵们很快适应了艰苦的条件。

近义词: habituates, familiarizes, adapts, trains
反义词: disaccustoms

词性: verb (动词)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He accustoms himself to getting up early. = 他习惯早起。
She accustoms her dog to walking on a leash. = 她让她的狗习惯用皮带牵着走。

一般过去时:
They accustomed the horses to the sound of gunfire. = 他们让马匹习惯了枪声。
She accustomed herself to the new environment quickly. = 她很快适应了新环境。

现在完成时:
I have accustomed myself to living in the city. = 我已经习惯了住在城市里。
He has accustomed his children to eating healthy food. = 他已经让他的孩子们习惯吃健康食品。

过去完成时:
She had accustomed herself to the noise before I moved in. = 在我搬进来之前，她已经习惯了噪音。
They had accustomed the animals to human contact before releasing them. = 在放生之前，他们已经让动物们习惯了与人类接触。

将来时:
We will accustom the new employees to the company's procedures. = 我们会让新员工熟悉公司的程序。
She will accustom herself to the new schedule soon. = 她很快就会适应新的时间表。

虚拟语气:
If I were to move to another country, I would accustom myself to the local customs. = 如果我要搬到另一个国家，我会让自己适应当地的习俗。
I wish I had accustomed myself to public speaking earlier. = 我希望我早点习惯公开演讲。

被动语态:
The children were accustomed to the routine. = 孩子们已经习惯了这种常规。
The new recruits are being accustomed to military life. = 新兵们正在适应军队生活。


PHRASE->
accustom oneself to = 使自己习惯于
accustom someone to something = 使某人习惯于某事
become accustomed to = 变得习惯于
get accustomed to = 习惯于
be well accustomed to = 非常习惯于


词源:
From Old French acostumer, from a- "to" + coustume "custom, habit."
