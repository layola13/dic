flinder:/ˈflɪndər/| n.|碎片，薄片；(澳大利亚)弗林德斯|A small, thin piece, especially one that has been broken or chipped off something larger.  (Often plural.) Also, Flinders is a place name in Australia, referring to Flinders Ranges, Flinders Island, or Flinders University.|The flint shattered, sending flinders flying. 燧石破碎了，碎片四处飞溅。|近义词：fragment, splinter, shard, chip|反义词: whole, complete|词性:noun


flinders:/ˈflɪndərz/| n.|弗林德斯山脉；弗林德斯岛|Flinders Ranges is a mountain range in South Australia. Flinders Island is the largest island in the Furneaux Group in Bass Strait,  also part of Tasmania, Australia.|We're planning a trip to the Flinders Ranges next year. 我们计划明年去弗林德斯山脉旅行。|近义词: (For Flinders Ranges) mountains, range|反义词:  (For Flinders Island) mainland|词性:noun
