overclimb:/ˌoʊvərˈklaɪm/ | v. |过度攀登；爬得过高|To climb beyond a safe or reasonable limit; to ascend too high. |The inexperienced climber overclimbed and found himself stranded on a precarious ledge.  那个没有经验的登山者爬得过高，发现自己被困在一个危险的岩架上。

The pilot overclimbed, stalling the aircraft and causing it to plummet. 飞行员爬升过高，导致飞机失速急速下降。| 近义词: overascend | 反义词: descend |词性:动词
