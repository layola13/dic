acescence:/ˌæsɪˈsɛns/| n.|酸败；发酸|The acescence of the wine ruined its flavor.  葡萄酒的酸败破坏了它的味道。|The unpleasant acescence of the milk indicated it had spoiled.  牛奶令人不快的酸味表明它已经变质了。|近义词：sourness, acidity, tartness|反义词: sweetness, alkalinity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acescere (“to turn sour”), from acēre (“to be sour”).

USAGE->
The acescence of the cider made it undrinkable.  苹果酒的酸味使它无法饮用。

The hot weather accelerated the acescence of the fruit. 炎热的天气加速了水果的酸败。
