over-drink:/ˌōvərˈdrɪŋk/
verb
过度饮酒
To consume an excessive amount of alcoholic beverages.
He over-drank at the party and had a terrible hangover the next day. 他在派对上喝多了，第二天宿醉得很厉害。
She tends to over-drink when she's stressed. 她压力大的时候往往会喝太多酒。
overindulge, overimbibe
drink moderately, abstain
v.
