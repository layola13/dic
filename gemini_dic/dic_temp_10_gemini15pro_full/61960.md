emulsionize: /ɪˈmʌlʃənaɪz/ | v. | 乳化 | To combine two liquids that do not usually mix, such as oil and water, into a smooth mixture. |  The chef emulsionized the oil and vinegar to create a vinaigrette.  厨师将油和醋乳化，制成香醋汁。| emulsify, blend, mix | separate | verb

emulsion: /ɪˈmʌlʃ(ə)n/ | n. | 乳状液 | A mixture of two liquids that do not usually mix, such as oil and water, where one liquid is dispersed as small droplets in the other. | Milk is an emulsion of fat and water. 牛奶是脂肪和水的乳状液。| mixture, suspension | solution, separation | noun
