nonhierarchically:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪərˈɑːrkɪkli/ | adv. | 非等级地，无等级地 | In a nonhierarchical system, all members have equal status and power. |  The team operates nonhierarchically, with everyone contributing ideas and sharing responsibility. | 近义词: egalitarian, democratic, horizontally | 反义词: hierarchically, vertically | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "hierarchical" (arranged in a hierarchy). "Hierarchical" ultimately comes from the Greek words "hieros" (sacred) and "arkhos" (ruler).
