词源:
From blackish + -ness.  Blackish comes from black + -ish. Black ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *blakaz, meaning "burned, scorched, blazing." The suffix -ish means "somewhat, rather." The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
