courtships:/ˈkɔːrtʃɪps/| n.|求爱，追求， ухаживание (Russian)|The period of time during which a couple develops a romantic relationship before getting married.  A man's courting of a woman; wooing.|例句：Their courtship was brief and passionate. 他们的恋爱期短暂而热烈。

例句：The peacock's elaborate courtship display is designed to attract a mate.  孔雀精心设计的求偶炫耀是为了吸引配偶。|近义词：wooing, romance, courting, pursuing|反义词: rejection, estrangement|名词
