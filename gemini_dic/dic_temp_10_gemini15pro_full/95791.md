inconvenience's: /ˌɪnkənˈviːniənsɪz/ | n. |不便之处，麻烦 |The possessive form of "inconvenience," referring to something belonging to or caused by an inconvenience.  It indicates the source or origin of the inconvenience. | The flight delay's inconvenience caused me to miss my connecting flight.  航班延误的不便让我错过了转机航班。|Synonyms: difficulty, trouble, hardship, bother, nuisance | Antonyms: convenience, ease, comfort | noun
