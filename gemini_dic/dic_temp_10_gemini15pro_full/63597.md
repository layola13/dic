envoy:/ˈɛnvɔɪ/ (US), /ˈɒnvɔɪ/ (UK)| n.|使节，特使，外交代表|A person delegated by a government or ruler to represent them in another country or to another government, especially in diplomatic affairs.|The president sent a special envoy to the peace talks. 总统派了一名特使参加和平谈判。|近义词: ambassador, delegate, representative, emissary|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"rebel","enemy"等表示敌对关系的词)|noun
