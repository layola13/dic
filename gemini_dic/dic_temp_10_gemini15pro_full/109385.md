lenticellate:/lɛnˈtɪsɪˌleɪt/ | adj. | 具皮孔的；有皮孔的 | Having lenticels; covered with lenticels. | The young branches are lenticellate. 幼枝有皮孔。 | Synonym: porous, aerated | Antonym: non-porous, impermeable | adjective

lenticel:/ˈlɛntɪˌsɛl/ | n. | 皮孔 | A small, raised pore in the bark of woody plants that allows gases to pass between the atmosphere and the internal tissues. |  The lenticels on the cherry tree are very prominent.  樱桃树上的皮孔很明显。 | Synonym: pore, stoma | Antonym: N/A | noun
