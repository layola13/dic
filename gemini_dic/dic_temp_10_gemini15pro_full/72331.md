flowabilities:/ˌfloʊəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 流动性 | The ease with which a substance can flow. This is often used in the plural to describe the different ways a substance can flow depending on conditions. | The flowabilities of different types of concrete are crucial for construction. 不同类型混凝土的流动性对于建筑至关重要。| The various flowabilities of the molten metal allowed it to be molded into intricate shapes.  熔融金属的各种流动性使其可以模制成复杂的形状。 | fluidity, viscosity | resistance | noun
