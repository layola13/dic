ETYMOLOGY->

From non- (prefix meaning "not") + hazardous + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Hazardous" ultimately derives from the Arabic word  "al-zahr" (meaning "the die," referring to the use of dice in games of chance), which evolved to mean "risk" or "danger."
