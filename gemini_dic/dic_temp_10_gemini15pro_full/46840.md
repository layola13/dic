dapperling: /ˈdæpərliŋ/
noun
衣着讲究的年轻人；帅小伙
A smartly dressed young man.
He was quite the dapperling in his new suit. 他穿着新西装，真是个帅小伙。
近义词: dandy, fop, swell
反义词: slob, ragamuffin
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From dapper + -ling (diminutive suffix).  Dapper itself comes from Middle Dutch dapper, meaning "brave, valiant."  The meaning shifted over time to "neat, active," and eventually to "smartly dressed."
