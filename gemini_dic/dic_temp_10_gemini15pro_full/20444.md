bitterest: /ˈbɪtərɪst/ | adj. | 最苦的，最痛苦的，最严酷的 |  Superlative form of bitter: most bitter.  Expressing the highest degree of bitterness, either in taste, experience, or feeling. | The coffee was the bitterest I had ever tasted. 这咖啡是我喝过最苦的。|  The bitterest pill to swallow was the realization that he had been betrayed by his closest friend. 最难接受的事实是他被最亲近的朋友背叛了。| 近义词: harshest, most acrimonious, most resentful | 反义词: sweetest, mildest, most pleasant | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "biter" (sharp, cutting, biting, cruel), from Proto-Germanic "*bitraz" (to bite), from Proto-Indo-European "*bheid-" (to split, bite, cut). The "-est" suffix is the superlative ending in English.
