materialists:/məˈtɪəriəlɪsts/
n.
唯物主义者；物质主义者
People who believe that only physical matter exists and that there is no spiritual world are called materialists.
相信只有物质存在而没有精神世界的人被称为唯物主义者。
Materialists often prioritize acquiring possessions.
唯物主义者往往优先考虑获取财产。
近义词:consumerists, material girls/boys
反义词:spiritualists, idealists
词性:noun
