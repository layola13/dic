indenize: /ɪnˈdɛnɪˌzaɪz/
verb
使入籍；给予公民权；归化
To grant citizenship to (someone); to naturalize.
The government is trying to indenize more refugees. 政府正试图给予更多难民公民权。
synonyms: naturalize, enfranchise
antonyms: expatriate, denaturalize
词性: 动词


indenization: /ɪnˌdɛnɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
入籍；公民权授予；归化
The act of granting citizenship; naturalization.
