molders:/ˈməʊldəz (British) /ˈmoʊldərz (American)| n.|塑造者；制模工；腐烂者|People who shape or influence something, or workers who make molds, or something that causes decay. |The molders in the foundry poured molten metal into the molds.  铸造厂的制模工将熔化的金属倒入模具中。|
The molders of public opinion have a great responsibility.  舆论的塑造者肩负着巨大的责任。| synonyms: shapers, influencers, casters, formers, decayers | antonyms: preservers, restorers, builders | noun


molder:/ˈməʊldə (British) /ˈmoʊldər (American)| v.|腐烂；腐朽；衰败|To decay or crumble slowly, usually due to dampness. |The fruit left on the counter began to molder.  留在柜台上的水果开始腐烂。| The old books in the attic moldered from neglect.  阁楼里那些旧书因为无人照管而腐朽了。| synonyms: decay, rot, decompose, crumble, perish | antonyms: flourish, thrive, grow, preserve | verb
