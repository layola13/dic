Showing or reflecting a bias or preference.|例句：The journalist's nonneutral reporting led to accusations of bias. 这位记者不中立的报道导致了偏见的指责。|例句：Her nonneutral stance on the issue made her a controversial figure. 她在这个问题上的不中立立场使她成为一个有争议的人物。|近义词：biased, partisan, prejudiced|反义词：neutral, impartial, objective|词性:形容词
