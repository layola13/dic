emulsifiability:/ɪˌmʌlsɪfaɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 乳化性，乳化能力 | The ability of a liquid to form an emulsion. | The emulsifiability of this oil is poor, it separates quickly.  这种油的乳化性很差，它很快就分离了。 | Synonyms: emulsifying power, emulsion capacity | Antonyms: immiscibility, non-emulsifiability | noun
