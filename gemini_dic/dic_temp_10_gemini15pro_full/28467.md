carburizers:/ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪzərz/
n.
渗碳剂; 增碳剂
Substances used to introduce carbon into the surface of steel or other metals in order to harden them.
The carburizers used in this process are a mixture of charcoal and barium carbonate.  这种工艺中使用的渗碳剂是木炭和碳酸钡的混合物。
Pack carburizing utilizes solid carburizers.  固体渗碳利用固体渗碳剂。
carburizing agents, hardening agents
softening agents
noun
