engagement:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 啮合 | The act of engaging or the state of being engaged, as gears. | The engagement of the gears caused a loud grinding noise. 齿轮啮合发出巨大的摩擦声。| synonyms: meshing, interlocking | antonyms: disengagement | noun
