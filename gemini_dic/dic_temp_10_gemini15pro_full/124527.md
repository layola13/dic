mountaineers:/ˌmaʊntɪˈnɪərz/| n.|登山者，爬山者|A person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport or profession.  A group of people who climb mountains.|例句：The mountaineers reached the summit after a grueling climb. 登山者们经过艰苦的攀登到达了顶峰。

例句：Experienced mountaineers know the importance of proper equipment. 有经验的登山者知道合适的装备的重要性。|近义词：climbers, alpinists|反义词:lowlanders, valley dwellers|词性:noun
