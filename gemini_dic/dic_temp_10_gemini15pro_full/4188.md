airest:/ˈɛərɪɪst/
adjective
最通风的，最轻快的
most airy; light and breezy
The airest room in the house was the sunroom with its large windows. 房子里最通风的房间是带有大窗户的日光浴室。
The bakery was known for its airest, most delicate pastries. 这家面包店以其最轻盈、最精致的糕点而闻名。
Synonyms: airiest, breeziest, lightest
Antonyms: stuffiest, closest, heaviest
词性: 形容词

词源:
From air + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *aerius* (airy), from *aer* (air).
