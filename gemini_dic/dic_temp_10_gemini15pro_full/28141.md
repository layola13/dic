capitulary:/kəˈpɪtʃʊləri/
n.|法令汇编；主教座堂议会章程；章节|A collection of ordinances, especially ecclesiastical ones, in chapters.  A code of laws, especially of the Frankish kings.|The capitulary issued by Charlemagne in 802 contained regulations about trade and currency.  查理曼大帝在802年颁布的法令汇编包含了关于贸易和货币的规定。|ordinances, statutes, decrees, canons, regulations| code, collection |noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *capitularium, neuter of capitularis (see capitular).
