liquidness:/ˈlɪkwɪdnəs/ | n. | 流动性；液态；流质 | The state of being liquid; readily convertible into cash. | The liquidness of the market allows for quick buying and selling of assets.  市场流动性允许快速买卖资产。 | synonyms: fluidity, liquidity, runniness | antonyms: solidity, thickness, viscosity | noun

liquidness:/ˈlɪkwɪdnəs/ | n. | 流动性；易变性；不稳定性 | The state of being unstable or easily changed.  | The liquidness of the political situation made it difficult to predict the outcome. 政治局势的不稳定性使得很难预测结果。| synonyms: instability, volatility, fluidity | antonyms: stability, firmness, fixedness | noun
