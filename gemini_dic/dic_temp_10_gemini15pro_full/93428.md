illuminances:/ɪˌluːmɪnənsɪz/
n.
光照度，照度
The illuminance on the desk was too low for reading.  桌子上的光照度太低，不适合阅读。
illuminance is the total luminous flux incident on a surface, per unit area. 光照度是指落在单位面积表面上的总光通量。
例句：The illuminances in the room varied depending on the position of the lamps. 房间里的光照度随着灯的位置而变化。
The photographer measured the illuminances to ensure proper exposure. 摄影师测量了光照度以确保曝光正确。

近义词：illumination, light level, brightness
反义词：darkness

词性：noun

词源：From Latin illuminare "to light up," from in- "in" + luminare "to light," from lumen "light."  源自拉丁语 illuminare “照亮”，由 in- “在” + luminare “照亮”，源自 lumen “光”。
