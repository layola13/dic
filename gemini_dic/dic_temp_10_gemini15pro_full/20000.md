biohazard:/ˈbaɪoʊˌhæzərd/ | n. | 生物危害 | A biological substance that poses a threat to the health of living organisms, especially humans. | The lab was sealed off due to a biohazard. 实验室因生物危害被封锁了。| The hospital disposed of the biohazard waste carefully. 医院仔细处理了生物危害废物。 | synonym: biological threat, biothreat, contagious risk | antonym: biosafety | noun
