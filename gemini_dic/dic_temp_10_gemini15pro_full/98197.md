insectary: /ˈɪnˌsɛktəri/
noun
昆虫饲养室；昆虫馆
A place or building where insects are bred, kept, and studied.
例句：The researchers studied the behavior of the butterflies in the insectary.  研究人员在昆虫饲养室里研究蝴蝶的行为。

例句：The insectary housed a variety of species for research purposes.  这个昆虫馆为了研究目的饲养了各种各样的昆虫。
近义词：insectarium, vivarium
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Latin *insectarium, from insectum ("insect") + -arium (a suffix forming nouns indicating a place for something).
