consulter:/kɒnˈsʌltər/ (UK), /kənˈsʌltər/ (US)| n.| 顾问，咨询师 |A person who provides expert advice professionally.|  She works as a financial consulter for a large firm. 她是一家大公司的财务顾问。| synonym: advisor, consultant | antonym: client, customer | noun

consulter:/kɔ̃.syl.te/ (French)| v. |咨询，商量|To seek information or advice from someone.| J'ai consulté mon médecin. (French) I consulted my doctor. 我咨询了我的医生。| synonym: discuss, confer | antonym: ignore, disregard | verb
