co-operatively:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪvli/
| adv. |合作地，协作地|In a way that involves mutual assistance in working towards a common goal. |The team worked co-operatively to complete the project.  团队合作完成了这个项目。| 近义词: jointly, collaboratively, together | 反义词: individually, separately, independently |词性:副词

co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/
| adj. |合作的，协作的|Involving mutual assistance in working towards a common goal. |They adopted a co-operative approach to problem-solving. 他们采取合作的方式解决问题。|近义词: collaborative, joint, shared | 反义词: uncooperative, individualistic, competitive|词性:形容词

co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/
| n. |合作社，合作组织|A business or organization owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits or benefits. |The local farmers formed a co-operative to market their produce. 当地的农民成立了一个合作社来销售他们的产品。| 近义词: collective, partnership, alliance | 反义词: corporation, conglomerate |词性:名词
