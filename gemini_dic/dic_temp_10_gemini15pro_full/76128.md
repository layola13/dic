furzechat:/ˈfɜːzˌtʃæt/ | n. | 金雀花丛聊天; 在金雀花丛中聊天 | A chat that takes place amidst furze bushes.  It evokes a sense of casual conversation in a natural, perhaps secluded, setting. |  We had a furzechat about our future plans. 我们在金雀花丛中聊了聊我们未来的计划。 |  They enjoyed a quiet furzechat in the warm afternoon sun. 他们在温暖的午后阳光下享受了一场宁静的金雀花丛聊天。 | synonyms: chat, conversation, talk | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "furze" (a spiny evergreen shrub with yellow flowers) + "chat" (a light informal conversation). "Furze" comes from Old English *fyrs, related to Old High German furz. "Chat" comes from the shortened form of "chatter," from the imitative verb "chatter," of uncertain origin.
