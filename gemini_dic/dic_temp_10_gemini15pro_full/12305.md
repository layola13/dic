ascomycetes:/ˌæskoʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz/
n.
子囊菌门，子囊菌纲
A large and diverse phylum of fungi (kingdom Fungi) characterized by the formation of an ascus (plural asci), a saclike structure that contains sexual spores (ascospores).
Morels, truffles, brewer's yeast, and baker's yeast are examples of ascomycetes.
羊肚菌、块菌、啤酒酵母和面包酵母都是子囊菌的例子。
近义词: sac fungi
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)
词性: noun
