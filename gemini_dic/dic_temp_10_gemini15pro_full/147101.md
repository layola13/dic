overlimiting:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过度限制的，超过限制的
Exceeding a limit or exceeding the normal limits; excessively limiting or restrictive.
例句：
The overlimiting regulations stifled innovation.  过度限制的法规扼杀了创新。

His parents' overlimiting parenting style made him rebellious. 他父母过度限制的教育方式让他变得叛逆。

近义词：restrictive, inhibiting, constraining, excessive
反义词：permissive, lenient, liberating

词性: adjective
