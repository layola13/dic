Igbo: /ˈiːboʊ/ (US), /ˈɪɡboʊ/ (UK) | n. adj.| 伊博族；伊博语 |A member of a people of southeastern Nigeria; the language of the Igbo people, belonging to the Kwa group of the Niger-Congo family.|  Many Igbo people live in southeastern Nigeria. 许多伊博人居住在尼日利亚东南部。| The Igbo language is tonal. 伊博语是有声调的。|  Synonym: Ibo | Antonym: N/A | Noun, Adjective
