depicting:/dɪˈpɪktɪŋ/
verb
描绘，描述
representing or showing something in a picture, story, movie, etc.; portraying
The painting depicts a scene from the artist's childhood. 这幅画描绘了艺术家童年时代的一个场景。
synonyms: portraying, representing, illustrating, picturing
antonyms: misrepresenting, distorting, concealing
