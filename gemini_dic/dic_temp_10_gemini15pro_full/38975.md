confineless:/kənˈfaɪnlɪs/
adjective
无边无际的，无限的
Not limited or restricted; without boundaries.
例句：Her spirit was confineless, always seeking new experiences. 她的精神是无边无际的，总是寻求新的体验。

例句：The confineless expanse of the ocean filled her with awe.  浩瀚无垠的大海让她充满了敬畏。

近义词：boundless, limitless, unlimited, unrestricted, infinite
反义词：confined, limited, restricted, bounded, finite
词性: adjective

词源:  "confine" (限制) + "-less" (无)
