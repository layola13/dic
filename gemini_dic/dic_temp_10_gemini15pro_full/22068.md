bolis:/ˈboʊlis/| n. |冰棍，冰棒|A frozen dessert on a stick, typically made of flavored ice or cream.|Example: The children enjoyed their bolis on a hot summer day. 孩子们在大热天里享用他们的冰棍。|Example:  I bought a mango bolis from the street vendor. 我从街头小贩那里买了一根芒果冰棍。|近义词：popsicle, ice pop, ice lolly|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hot drink")|词性:noun

词源:  From Mexican Spanish *bolis*, plural of *boli*, from *agua de boli*, literally "Boli water," named after its inventor, paleteiro Miguel Ángel Velázquez from Tocumbo, Michoacán, Mexico.
