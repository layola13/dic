impassively:/ɪmˈpæsɪvli/
adverb
不动声色地，冷漠地
Without showing any emotion; in an impassive manner.
He listened impassively to the news of the disaster. 他不动声色地听着灾难的消息。
Synonyms:  emotionlessly, stoically, indifferently, blankly, expressionlessly
Antonyms:  emotionally, expressively, passionately, excitedly
词性: 副词

impassive:/ɪmˈpæsɪv/
adjective
冷漠的，无动于衷的
Not feeling or showing emotion.
Her face remained impassive throughout the trial.  在整个审判过程中，她的表情始终保持冷漠。
Synonyms:  expressionless, emotionless, stoic, indifferent, unmoved
Antonyms:  emotional, expressive, passionate, responsive
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *impassivus, from in- (“not”) + passus, past participle of pati (“to suffer, endure, feel”).
