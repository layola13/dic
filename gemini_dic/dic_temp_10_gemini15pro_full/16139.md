banian: /ˈbænjən/ | n. | 印度榕树; (印度教徒或耆那教徒穿的) 背心 | A large East Indian tree that sends out roots which grow downwards to the ground forming additional trunks. Also, a man's sleeveless undershirt. |  The banian tree provided shade for the weary travelers. 印度榕树为疲倦的旅行者提供了阴凉。 He wore a white banian under his shirt. 他在衬衫里面穿了一件白色背心。 | 近义词: fig tree (for the tree), vest, undershirt | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for the tree. For the clothing, perhaps "overshirt") | 词性: noun

banian: /ˈbæniən/ | n. | (尤指旧时在印度的) 商人，经纪人| A Hindu trader or merchant, especially in former times in India. | The banian acted as an intermediary between the British traders and the local market.  这个经纪人在英国商人和当地市场之间充当中间人。  The banian negotiated a favorable deal for the spices.  这个商人谈成了香料的有利交易。 | 近义词: merchant, trader, broker, agent | 反义词: customer, buyer | 词性: noun
