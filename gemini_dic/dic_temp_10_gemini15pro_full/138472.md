novella:/nəˈvɛlə/ | n. | 中篇小说 | A short novel, typically between 17,500 and 40,000 words. |  Example:  "Heart of Darkness" by Joseph Conrad is considered a novella. 康拉德的《黑暗之心》被认为是一部中篇小说。

Example: She is writing a novella about a young woman's journey of self-discovery. 她正在写一部关于一位年轻女子自我发现之旅的中篇小说。

| 近义词: novelette, short novel | 反义词: novel (in terms of length) | 词性: noun
