ETYMOLOGY->
From complex + -ification.  "Complex" comes from the Latin word "complexus," meaning "embracing, encompassing," from the past participle of "complecti," meaning "to entwine, embrace."  "-ification" is a suffix that forms nouns meaning "the action or process of making or becoming."
