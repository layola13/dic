desertification: /dɪˌzɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 荒漠化 | The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture. |  Desertification is a serious environmental problem in many parts of the world.  世界许多地方的荒漠化是一个严重的环境问题。 | synonyms:  desertization, aridity, barrenness | antonyms:  afforestation, reforestation, greening | noun
