linearization:/ˌlɪniəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |线性化，线性化处理|The process of converting a nonlinear system or model into a linear one, often for simplification or easier analysis. |The linearization of the system allowed us to use simpler mathematical tools.  系统线性化后，我们就可以使用更简单的数学工具了。|linearisation (British spelling), linear approximation|nonlinearization|noun
