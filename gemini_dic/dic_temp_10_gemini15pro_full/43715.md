credo:/ˈkriːdoʊ/ /ˈkreɪdoʊ/
|n.|信条，教义|A statement of the beliefs or aims that guide someone's actions.|His credo is "Work hard, play hard." 他的信条是“努力工作，尽情玩乐”。|
|近义词：belief, principle, doctrine, tenet|
|反义词：disbelief, doubt, skepticism|
|词性：noun|

credos:/ˈkriːdoʊz/ /ˈkreɪdoʊz/
|n.|信条，教义（credo的复数形式）|Plural form of credo, referring to multiple statements of belief or aims.|Their credos varied widely, reflecting their diverse backgrounds. 他们的信条差异很大，反映了他们不同的背景。|
|近义词：beliefs, principles, doctrines, tenets|
|反义词：disbeliefs, doubts, skepticisms|
|词性：noun|
