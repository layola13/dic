haughtily:/ˈhɔːtɪli/| adv.|傲慢地，高傲地|In a haughty manner; arrogantly.  Displaying an attitude of superiority and disdain.|She tossed her head haughtily and walked away. 她傲慢地扬起头，走开了。|例句：He haughtily dismissed their concerns. 他傲慢地驳回了他们的担忧。|近义词：arrogantly, disdainfully, superciliously|反义词：humbly, modestly|词性:副词
