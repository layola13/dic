cosmically:/ˈkɒzmɪkli/ (UK) /ˈkɑːzmɪkli/ (US)| adv.|宇宙地；广大无边地|Relating to the cosmos or universe; inconceivably vast.|例句：The event was cosmically significant.  这个事件在宇宙层面上意义重大。

例句：The sheer scale of the universe is cosmically daunting. 宇宙的绝对规模令人望而生畏。

近义词：universally, globally, immensely, vastly|反义词：locally, narrowly, insignificantly|词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cosmic + -ly.  Cosmic comes from the French cosmique and ultimately from the Greek kosmikos, meaning "of the universe," from kosmos "order, the universe."
