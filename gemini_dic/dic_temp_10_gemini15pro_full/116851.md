maximist:/ˈmæksɪmɪst/| n. |最大化主义者；极端主义者|A person who holds extreme views or favors extreme measures.|He's a maximist in his political views, always advocating for radical change. 他政治观点极端，总是主张彻底变革。|近义词：extremist, radical, zealot|反义词：minimalist, moderate|词性: Noun

maximist:/ˈmæksɪmɪst/| adj. |最大化的；极端主义的|Characterized by holding extreme views or favoring extreme measures.|She has a maximist approach to life, always striving for the absolute best. 她的人生观是最大化的，总是力求做到最好。|近义词：extreme, radical, uncompromising|反义词：minimalist, moderate|词性: Adjective
