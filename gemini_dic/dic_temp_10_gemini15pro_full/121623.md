mispagination:/ˌmɪspeɪdʒɪˈneɪʃən/
n.

the action of paginating something incorrectly.
The mispagination in the book made it difficult to follow. 这本书的页码错误使得它很难读下去。
misnumbering, incorrect pagination
correct pagination
noun
