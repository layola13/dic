asphyxied:/əsˈfɪksiːd/| adj.|窒息的，闷死的|Deprived of oxygen, usually to the point of unconsciousness or death.|The fire victims were asphyxied by the smoke. 火灾受害者因烟雾窒息而死。|近义词：suffocated, choked, smothered|反义词:ventilated, oxygenated|词性:形容词
asphyxiate:/əsˈfɪksieɪt/| v.|使窒息，使闷死|To deprive of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death.|The fumes from the car exhaust asphyxiated the driver. 汽车排出的废气使司机窒息。|近义词：suffocate, choke, smother|反义词:resuscitate, ventilate|词性:动词
asphyxiation:/əsˌfɪksiˈeɪʃn/| n.|窒息|The state of being deprived of oxygen, leading to unconsciousness or death.|Asphyxiation can be caused by drowning, choking, or inhaling toxic fumes. 窒息可能是由溺水、窒息或吸入有毒烟雾引起的。|近义词：suffocation|反义词:ventilation, oxygenation|词性:名词


词源: From French asphyxie, from Late Latin asphyxia, from Greek asphyxia "stopping of the pulse," from a- "without" + sphyxis "pulse."
