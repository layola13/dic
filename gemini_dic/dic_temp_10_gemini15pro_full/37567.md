commotive:/kəˈməʊtɪv/ | adj. | 激动的，骚动的，煽动的 |  Causing commotion or tending to excite.  | The commotive speech roused the crowd to action. 这场煽动性的演讲激起了群众的行动。 | 近义词: inflammatory, provocative, agitating, exciting, stirring | 反义词: calming, soothing, appeasing, pacifying | 词性: adjective

commotion:/kəˈməʊʃ(ə)n/ | n. | 骚动，喧闹，混乱 | A state of confused and noisy disturbance. |  There was a great commotion when the fire alarm went off. 火警响起时，一片混乱。 | 近义词: disturbance, uproar, tumult, chaos, disorder | 反义词: calm, peace, quiet, order, tranquility | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commotivus, from Latin commovēre "to move violently, disturb, excite," from com- "together" + movēre "to move."
