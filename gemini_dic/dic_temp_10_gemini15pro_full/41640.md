corrective:/kəˈrektiv/ | adj. n. | 纠正的，矫正的；矫正物 | Intended to correct something, especially a fault or deficiency.  |

例句：
He was prescribed corrective lenses for his eyesight.  他被开了矫正视力的镜片。

例句：
The teacher provided corrective feedback on the student's essay. 老师对学生的作文进行了纠正性的反馈。


近义词：remedial, restorative, curative |
反义词：causing, worsening, exacerbating |
词性: adjective, noun |


corrective:/kəˈrektiv/ | n. | 矫正物，矫正剂 | Something that corrects, especially a cosmetic or medical preparation. |

例句：
She uses a corrective concealer to hide her blemishes. 她使用遮瑕膏来遮盖她的瑕疵。

例句：
The doctor recommended a corrective brace for his back. 医生建议他使用矫正背带。



近义词：remedy, cure, solution |
反义词：aggravant, irritant |
词性: noun |
