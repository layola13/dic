crash-in:/ˈkræʃ ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 突然闯入；不请自来；睡在别人家（尤指地板上）| To arrive uninvited and stay overnight, often sleeping on the floor. | Example:  Some friends crashed in on us after the concert last night. 昨晚音乐会结束后，一些朋友突然来我家过夜了。

Example:  Can I crash in at your place tonight? I missed the last train. 我今晚可以睡在你家吗？我错过了最后一班火车。 | 近义词: drop in, show up uninvited, gatecrash | 反义词: leave, depart | 词性: 动词短语
