nonbusiest:/nɒnˈbɪziɪst/
adjective
最不繁忙的
Not the busiest; less busy than others.  Formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the superlative form of "busy."
This is the nonbusiest time of year for the restaurant. 这是一年中这家餐厅最不繁忙的时候。
The library is nonbusiest on Sunday mornings.  这家图书馆在周日早上最不繁忙。
Near synonyms: quietest, slowest, calmest
Antonyms: busiest, most hectic, most active
词性: 形容词
