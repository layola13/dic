discographically:/ˌdɪskəˈɡræfɪkli/
adv.
以唱片目录的方式
in terms of a discography; with regard to recordings.
The band is discographically inactive.  这支乐队在唱片方面不活跃了。
近义词:  recording-wise, in terms of recordings
反义词:  None (This word doesn't have a direct antonym.)
词性: adverb

词源：
From discography + -ally.  Discography from disco- + -graphy.  Disco- from Greek diskos "disk".  -graphy from Greek -graphia "writing."
