insubmergible:/ˌɪnsəbˈmɜːrdʒəbl/ |adj.| 不能沉没的；不易沉没的；不屈不挠的|Not able to be submerged; incapable of sinking. Also, figuratively, not easily defeated or suppressed.|The Titanic was believed to be insubmergible, but tragically, it sank on its maiden voyage.  泰坦尼克号被认为是不会沉没的，但不幸的是，它在处女航中沉没了。

The human spirit is often described as insubmergible, capable of overcoming great adversity. 人类的精神常被描述为不屈不挠的，能够克服巨大的逆境。|近义词：unsinkable, buoyant, resilient|反义词：sinkable, submersible|词性: adjective
