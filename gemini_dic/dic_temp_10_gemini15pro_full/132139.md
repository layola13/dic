noncultivatable:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪˈveɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可培养的，无法栽培的 | Not capable of being cultivated; unable to grow or be grown (especially of microorganisms) under laboratory conditions. |  Many microorganisms found in natural environments are noncultivatable using standard laboratory techniques. 许多在自然环境中发现的微生物使用标准实验室技术无法培养。

The noncultivatable state of some bacteria makes them difficult to study. 一些细菌的不可培养状态使它们难以研究。 | 近义词: uncultivable, nonculturable | 反义词: cultivable, culturable | 词性: adjective
