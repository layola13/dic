gratitudes:/ˈɡrætɪtjuːdz/
n.
感激之情，感谢
The feeling of being grateful or thankful.
She expressed her gratitudes for their help. 她表达了对他们帮助的感激之情。
gratitude, thankfulness, appreciation
ingratitude, resentment
noun
