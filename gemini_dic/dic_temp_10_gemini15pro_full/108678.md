leaguing: /ˈliːɡɪŋ/
Verb
结盟，联合
The act of joining in a league or alliance.
Several community groups are leaguing together to fight the proposed development.  几个社区团体联合起来反对拟议的开发项目。
The workers leagued together to demand better pay. 工人们联合起来要求更高的工资。
Synonyms: uniting, allying, combining, joining forces, collaborating
Antonyms: separating, dividing, splitting, disbanding
词性: v.
