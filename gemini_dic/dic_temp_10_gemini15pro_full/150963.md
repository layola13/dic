paramedic:/ˌpærəˈmɛdɪk/ | n. | 护理人员；急救医士 | A person trained to give emergency medical treatment before or during transportation to a hospital. |  The paramedics arrived quickly and stabilized the injured man. 急救人员迅速赶到，使受伤男子情况稳定。| synonyms: Emergency medical technician (EMT), ambulance technician, first responder | antonyms: Doctor, physician, surgeon | noun

paramedic:/ˌpærəˈmɛdɪk/ | adj. |  护理的；急救的 | Relating to or provided by paramedics. | The patient received paramedic care at the scene of the accident. 病人在事故现场接受了护理。 | synonyms: Emergency, pre-hospital, ambulatory | antonyms: In-hospital, clinical, long-term | adjective
