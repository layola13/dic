mewing:/ˈmjuːɪŋ/| n.|Mewing|A facial restructuring technique that involves proper tongue posture, swallowing, and breathing habits purported to improve facial aesthetics and overall health.  It's named after Dr. John Mew, a British orthodontist.|John has been practicing mewing diligently for the past year.  约翰过去一年一直在努力练习Mewing。|Synonyms: orthotropics, myofunctional therapy|Antonyms: N/A|noun
