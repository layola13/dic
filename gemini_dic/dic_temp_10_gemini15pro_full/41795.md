Corsica: /ˈkɔːrsɪkə/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrsɪkə/ (US)| n. |科西嘉岛|A mountainous Mediterranean island, forming a région and département of France. It lies off the west coast of Italy, southeast of the French mainland, and north of the island of Sardinia. |例句：Corsica is known for its stunning beaches. 科西嘉岛以其迷人的海滩而闻名。

例句：Napoleon Bonaparte was born in Corsica. 拿破仑·波拿巴出生在科西嘉岛。|近义词：Isle, Island|反义词:Continent|词性:noun
