hamper:/ˈhæmpər/ (US), /ˈhæmpə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 阻碍，障碍；大篮子 | A large basket with a lid, often used for carrying food or laundry. |  The picnic hamper was overflowing with goodies.  野餐篮里装满了好吃的东西。| synonyms: basket, container, crate | antonyms: aid, assistance | noun

hamper:/ˈhæmpər/ (US), /ˈhæmpə(r)/ (UK)| vt. | 阻碍，妨碍 | To hinder or impede the movement or progress of someone or something. | The heavy rain hampered our progress.  大雨阻碍了我们的前进。| synonyms: hinder, impede, obstruct, restrict | antonyms: aid, assist, facilitate, help | verb
