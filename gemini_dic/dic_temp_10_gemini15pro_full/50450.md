depreciation:/dɪˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/ /dɪˌpriːsiˈeɪʃn/| n.|贬值，折旧|a reduction in the value of an asset over time, due in particular to wear and tear.|The depreciation of the car was quite rapid after the accident. 汽车在事故后贬值很快。|Synonyms: devaluation, decline, reduction|Antonyms: appreciation, increase, rise|noun

depreciation:/dɪˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/ /dɪˌpriːsiˈeɪʃn/| n.|（货币）贬值|a decrease in the value of a currency relative to other currencies.|The depreciation of the currency made imports more expensive. 货币贬值使得进口商品更加昂贵。|Synonyms: devaluation, decline|Antonyms: appreciation, revaluation|noun
