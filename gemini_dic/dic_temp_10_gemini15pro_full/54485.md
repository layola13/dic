disinterment's:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːrmənts/
noun
掘尸；发掘
The act of digging up something that has been buried, especially a body.
The disinterment's purpose was to gather further forensic evidence.  掘尸的目的是为了收集进一步的法医证据。
exhumation, unearthing
burial, interment
noun
