chillnesses:/ˈtʃɪlnɪsɪz/
n.
疾病，病痛
a state of being ill.
She suffered from various childhood illnesses. 她患有各种儿童疾病。

近义词:sicknesses, ailments, diseases, maladies
反义词:health, wellness

词性:noun (plural)
词源:From Middle English *chilnes (“chillness, coldness”), equivalent to chill +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Old English ciele (“chill, cold”).
