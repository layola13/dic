garcinia:/ɡɑːrˈsɪniə/ | n. | 藤黄果 | A tropical tree or shrub of the genus *Garcinia*, many species of which produce edible fruit and some of which are sources of spices, dyes, and resins. | The rind of the garcinia fruit is used in some Asian cuisines.  藤黄果的果皮在一些亚洲菜系中使用。 | Garcinia cambogia is a popular weight-loss supplement. 藤黄果柬埔寨是一种流行的减肥补充剂。 | 近义词: mangosteen (for some species) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a genus of plants) | noun
