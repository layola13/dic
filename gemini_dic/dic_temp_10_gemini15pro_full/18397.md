belonite:/ˈbɛləˌnaɪt/| n. |箭石|A slender, crystalline rod of calcium carbonate found in some belemnites (extinct cephalopods).|The museum displayed a collection of belonites from various belemnite fossils.|Near synonyms: guard, rostrum (referring to other parts of belemnites) |Antonyms: N/A|noun
