mortiser: /ˈmɔːrtaɪzər/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrtɪsər/ (US) | n. |榫眼机，凿榫机 |A machine tool used to cut mortises (rectangular holes or recesses) in wood or other materials, typically for joining pieces together using a tenon. |The carpenter used a mortiser to create precise mortises for the chair legs. 木匠用榫眼机为椅腿制作精确的榫眼。

The new mortiser made the job much faster.  新的榫眼机使工作速度更快了。


近义词: hollow chisel mortiser, chain mortiser, benchtop mortiser | 反义词: None (no direct opposite for a tool) | 词性: Noun
