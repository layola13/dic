anaemia:/əˈniːmiə/ (UK) /əˈniːmiə/ (US)| n.|贫血|A condition in which there is a deficiency of red cells or of haemoglobin in the blood, resulting in pallor and weariness.|She suffered from anaemia due to iron deficiency. 她因缺铁而患上贫血。|Synonyms: bloodlessness, deficiency of red blood cells|Antonyms: plethora, polycythemia|noun
