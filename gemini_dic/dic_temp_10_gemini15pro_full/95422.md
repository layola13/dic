incidentally:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/| adv.|顺便提一下，偶然地|Used to introduce a new topic or piece of information that is not strictly part of the main subject of conversation, or to add a remark unconnected to the main topic.|Incidentally, I saw John yesterday. 顺便说一下，我昨天看到约翰了。|Synonyms: by the way, in passing, parenthetically|Antonyms: intentionally, deliberately, purposely|词性:副词

incidentally:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntli/| adv.|附带地，伴随地|Happening as a result of something else, but not intended or planned.|The information was discovered incidentally during a routine check.  这些信息是在一次例行检查中偶然发现的。|Synonyms: accidentally, unintentionally, unexpectedly|Antonyms: deliberately, intentionally, purposely|词性:副词
