glitterings:/ˈɡlɪtərɪŋz/
noun
闪光; 闪烁; 闪耀; 灿烂
Small flashes of reflected light.
The Christmas tree was covered in glitterings. 圣诞树上覆盖着闪光的小装饰。
Near Synonyms: sparkles, shimmers, flashes
Near Antonyms: dullness, darkness
词性: 名词
