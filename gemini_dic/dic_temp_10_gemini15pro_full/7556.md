annihilative:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪtɪv/
adjective
毁灭性的，歼灭性的
Causing destruction or complete obliteration.
The annihilative power of the bomb was terrifying.  这颗炸弹的毁灭性力量令人恐惧。
The virus had an annihilative effect on the population. 这种病毒对人口具有毁灭性的影响。
近义词:destructive, devastating, ruinous
反义词:creative, constructive, beneficial
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin annihilativus, from Latin annihilatus (past participle of annihilare “to reduce to nothing”), from ad- “to” + nihil “nothing.”
