breakably:/ˈbreɪkəbli/ | adj. | 易碎地，脆弱地 |  In a way that is easy to break; fragilely. | The vase was breakably thin.  这个花瓶薄得易碎。| 近义词:fragilely, easily broken | 反义词:durably, strongly | 词性:形容词

词源: From "breakable" + "-ly."  "Breakable" comes from the verb "break" (Old English *brecan) + the suffix "-able" (from Old French -able, ultimately from Latin -abilis).
