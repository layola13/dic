dunbird:/ˈdʌnbɜːrd/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋 | A derogatory term for a stupid or foolish person.  It combines "dun" (meaning dull-witted) and "bird." | He acted like a real dunbird, locking his keys in the car. 他表现得像个十足的笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。 |  fool, idiot, nitwit, dolt | genius, mastermind | noun

Etymology:
From "dun" (meaning dull-witted, of obscure origin) + "bird" (a common suffix used in derogatory slang).
