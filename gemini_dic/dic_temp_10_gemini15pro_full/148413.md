oversteadfast:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛdfæst/| adj.| 过于坚定; 过于执着; 顽固 | Excessively steadfast or unwavering, often to a fault; stubbornly persistent. | He was oversteadfast in his beliefs, refusing to consider any other perspectives. 他过于执着于自己的信仰，拒绝考虑任何其他观点。

The captain, oversteadfast in his decision, sailed the ship straight into the storm. 船长固执己见，直接驾驶着船驶入了风暴。 | 近义词: obstinate, stubborn, inflexible, rigid, dogmatic | 反义词: flexible, adaptable, open-minded, wavering | 词性: 形容词
