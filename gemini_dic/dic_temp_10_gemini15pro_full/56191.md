dodgeable:/ˈdɒdʒəbl/ (US) /ˈdɒdʒɪbl/ (UK)| adj.|可躲避的，可回避的|Able to be dodged or avoided.|The missile was dodgeable, thanks to the pilot's quick reflexes.  导弹由于飞行员反应迅速而得以躲避。|Synonyms: avoidable, evasive, eludible|Antonyms: unavoidable, inescapable, inevitable|adjective
