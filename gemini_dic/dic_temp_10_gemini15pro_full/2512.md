administrationist: /ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃənɪst/
n.
行政主义者; 行政管理人员
Someone who believes in or practices administrationism.  An advocate or practitioner of administration as a primary means of achieving social or political goals.
The administrationist pushed for more bureaucratic control over the project.  这位行政主义者力求对该项目进行更多的官僚控制。
The administrationist believed that all problems could be solved through proper management. 这位行政主义者认为所有问题都可以通过适当的管理来解决。
近义词: bureaucrat, administrator, manager
反义词: anarchist
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From administration + -ist.  "Administration" comes from Late Latin *administratio, from Latin administrare "to manage, direct," from ad- "to" + ministrare "to serve, attend," from minister "servant." The suffix "-ist" forms agent nouns, denoting a person who performs a particular action, or adheres to a particular doctrine or system.
