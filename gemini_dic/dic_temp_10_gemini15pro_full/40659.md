converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ | v. | 汇聚，集中，趋于一致 | To come together from different directions; to meet at a common point; to become similar or identical | The two rivers converge near the city. 这两条河流在城市附近汇合。| synonyms: meet, join, unite, merge, coalesce | antonyms: diverge, separate, disperse | verb

converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ | v. | (意见、观点等)趋于一致 | To tend toward a common result or conclusion |  Our opinions on this matter have begun to converge. 我们在这个问题上的意见开始趋于一致了。 | synonyms: agree, coincide, concur | antonyms: differ, disagree, diverge | verb
