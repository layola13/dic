afflictively:/əˈflɪktɪvli/
adverb
痛苦地，折磨人地
in a way that causes suffering or distress.
She sighed afflictively, burdened by the news. 她痛苦地叹了口气，被这个消息压得喘不过气。
The loss of her child affected her afflictively.  失去孩子让她非常痛苦。
近义词: painfully, distressingly, agonizingly
反义词: soothingly, comfortingly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *afflictīvus, from Latin afflictus (past participle of affligere “to damage, to weaken, to knock down”), from ad- “to” + fligere “to strike”.
