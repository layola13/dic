dioeciously:/daɪˈiːʃəsli/| adv.| 雌雄异株地，雌雄异体地| Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.| Holly trees are dioecious, meaning you need both a male and a female plant to get berries. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着你需要雄性和雌性植物才能获得浆果。| Near synonyms: separately sexed| Near antonym: monoeciously| adverb
