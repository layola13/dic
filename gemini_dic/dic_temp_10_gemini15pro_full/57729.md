dreadlocks: /ˈdrɛdlɒks/ (UK) /ˈdrɛdlɑːks/ (US) | n. | 脏辫，雷鬼头 |  A hairstyle in which the hair is washed but not combed, and so forms into thick twisted masses.  |  He sported a thick set of dreadlocks. 他留着一头浓密的脏辫。 |  Synonyms: locs, braids, plaits | Antonyms:  straight hair, combed hair | noun

dreadlockses:  This is not a standard English word. The plural of "dreadlocks" is simply "dreadlocks".  There is no need to add "-es".
