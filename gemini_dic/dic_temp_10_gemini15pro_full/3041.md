aeroboat: /ˈɛəroʊboʊt/ | n. | 气垫船 | A boat that travels supported on a cushion of air | The aeroboat skimmed across the water. 气垫船掠过水面。| 近义词: hovercraft | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional boat/ship) | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + boat.  "Aero-" comes from Greek ἀήρ (aēr), meaning "air."
