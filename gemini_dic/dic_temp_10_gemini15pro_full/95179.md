inaudiblenesses:/ɪnˈɔːdəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
不可闻；听不见
the state or quality of being inaudible.
The inaudiblenesses of the whispers made it difficult to understand what was being said.  那些耳语听不见，难以理解他们说的是什么。
近义词：unhearability, indistinctness
反义词：audibility, clarity
词性: noun (plural)
