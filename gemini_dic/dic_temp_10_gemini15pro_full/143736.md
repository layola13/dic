outloves:/ˌaʊtˈlʌvz/
verb
胜过…的爱；比…更爱
To love someone or something more than someone or something else loves them/it.
She outloves him by a mile; his affection is tepid in comparison. 她对他的爱胜过他对她爱的一英里；相比之下，他的爱是冷淡的。
outdo, surpass, excel
