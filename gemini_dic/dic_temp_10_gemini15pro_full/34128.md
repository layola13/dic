cladocera:/kləˈdɒsərə/
n.
水蚤（总称）
small crustaceans (order Cladocera) that usually have a bivalve shell covering the body and a single median compound eye. They swim by means of branched antennae, and are found chiefly in fresh water.
Cladocera are an important food source for fish.  水蚤是鱼类的重要食物来源。
近义词:water fleas
反义词:None
词性:noun


cladoceran:/kləˈdɒsərən/
adj.
水蚤的；属于水蚤目的
of or relating to the Cladocera.
Cladoceran populations can fluctuate dramatically depending on environmental conditions. 水蚤的数量会根据环境条件发生剧烈波动。
n.
水蚤
a crustacean of the order Cladocera.
The cladoceran darted through the water. 这只水蚤在水中飞快地游动。
