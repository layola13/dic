layshaft:/ˈleɪʃæft/| n.|中间轴，惰轮轴|A shaft that does not transmit power directly but is used to support gears or other rotating parts.|The layshaft in the gearbox is responsible for transmitting power between different gear ratios.|近义词：countershaft, jackshaft|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "driveshaft" as a contrasting term)|词性: noun
