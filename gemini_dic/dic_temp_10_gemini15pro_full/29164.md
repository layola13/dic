cash-starved:/ˈkæʃ stɑːrvd/
adj.
资金匮乏的，缺乏现金的
Experiencing a severe shortage of cash or having very limited access to funds.
The cash-starved company was forced to lay off several employees. 这家资金匮乏的公司被迫解雇了几名员工。
Synonyms:  underfunded, impoverished, broke, strapped for cash, impecunious
Antonyms:  well-funded, affluent, prosperous, wealthy
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
"Cash" comes from Old French "casse" meaning "money box".  "Starved" comes from Old English "steorfan" meaning "to die". The compound "cash-starved" describes a situation similar to starvation, but related to a lack of money rather than food.
