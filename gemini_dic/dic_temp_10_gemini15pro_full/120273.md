miniature:/ˈmɪnɪtʃər/ /ˈmɪnɪəˌtʃʊr/ | adj. n. |微型的，缩影；微型复制品；微型画|A miniature version of something is much smaller than the usual size. |She bought a miniature of the Eiffel Tower as a souvenir. 她买了一个埃菲尔铁塔的微缩模型作为纪念品。|近义词:small, tiny, minute, diminutive, compact, reduced, scaled-down |反义词:large, huge, giant, enormous, massive, colossal|词性:adjective, noun

miniature:/ˈmɪnɪtʃər/ /ˈmɪnɪəˌtʃʊr/ | n. |微型复制品；微型画|A miniature is a very small version of something, such as a painting, a portrait, or a model.|He collects miniatures of vintage cars. 他收集老爷车的微缩模型。|近义词:model, replica, copy, reproduction|反义词:enlargement, magnification|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *miniature*, from Medieval Latin *miniatura*, from Latin *miniare* "to color with red lead," from *minium* "red lead." Originally referred to the small pictures in illuminated manuscripts.
