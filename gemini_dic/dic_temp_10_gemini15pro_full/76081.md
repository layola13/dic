furred:/fɜːrd/ | adj. | 毛皮的；覆有毛皮的 | Covered with fur. | The cat was small and furred. 这只猫很小，身上长满了毛。| The furred creature scurried under the bush. 这个毛茸茸的小动物 scurried 躲到灌木丛下面去了。| 近义词：hairy, furry, downy | 反义词：bald, hairless | 词性: 形容词

词源: From fur + -ed, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fuzą (“fur”).
