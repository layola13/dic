ligature:/ˈlɪɡətʃər/| n.|绷带；结扎线；连字|Something used to tie or bind, such as a thread or wire. A printed or written character combining two or more letters.|例句：The nurse applied a ligature to stop the bleeding. 护士用绷带止血。
