nonitemized:/ˌnɒnˈaɪtəmaɪzd/ | adj. | 未逐条列出的，总括的 | Not listed item by item; given as a total. | The bill was nonitemized, just showing the total amount due.  账单没有逐项列出，只显示了应付总额。|  The receipt was nonitemized, so I couldn't see how much each item cost.  收据没有逐项列出，所以我无法看到每件商品的价格。| generalized, summarized, aggregate | itemized, detailed | adjective
