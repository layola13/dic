improportionable:/ˌɪmprəˈpɔːrʃənəbəl/
adjective
不成比例的，不相称的
not in proper proportion; too large or too small in comparison with something else.
The punishment was improportionable to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。
The kitchen is improportionably small compared to the rest of the house.  与房子的其他部分相比，厨房小得不成比例。
近义词:disproportionate, inordinate, excessive, unreasonable
反义词:proportionate, commensurate, appropriate, reasonable
词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + proportionate + -able.
