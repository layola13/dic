disciplelike:/dɪˈsaɪpəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像门徒的，虔诚的|Resembling or characteristic of a disciple; showing devotion and obedience like a follower of a teacher or leader.|He had a disciplelike devotion to his mentor. 他对他的导师有着门徒般的虔诚。|近义词：devoted, devout, pious, reverent, faithful|反义词：irreverent, impious, faithless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From disciple + -like.  "Disciple" comes from Old French "disciple," from Latin "discipulus" meaning "pupil, learner," from "discere" meaning "to learn." "-like" is an Old English suffix meaning "having the characteristics of."
