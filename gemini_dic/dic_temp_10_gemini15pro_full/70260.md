fictitiously:/fɪkˈtɪʃəsli/
adverb
虚构地；杜撰地
in a fictitious manner; not real or true
He fictitiously described himself as a doctor. 他虚构地把自己描述成一个医生。
The events in the novel are entirely fictitious. 小说中的事件完全是虚构的。
Synonyms: imaginarily, falsely, untruly, spuriously, pretendedly
Antonyms: truly, genuinely, really, factually
词性: 副词
