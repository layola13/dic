ethnographical: /ˌeθnəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 人种志的；民族志学的 | Relating to ethnography, the scientific description of the customs of individual peoples and cultures. |  Example:  The researcher conducted an ethnographic study of the remote tribe. 研究人员对这个偏远部落进行了人种志研究。

Example:  Her ethnographic research focused on the role of women in the community. 她的人种志研究集中于女性在社区中的作用。

| Synonym: anthropological, ethnologic | Antonym:  N/A | word type: adjective
