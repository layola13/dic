nonconsumptively:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmptɪvli/
| adv. | 非消耗性地 | In a way that does not involve consuming or using up a resource. | Researchers observed the gorillas nonconsumptively, taking care not to disturb their natural habitat. 研究人员非消耗性地观察大猩猩，小心地不干扰它们的自然栖息地。 |  Researchers studied the butterflies nonconsumptively, simply observing their behavior. 研究人员对蝴蝶进行了非消耗性研究，只是观察它们的行为。|  sustainably, preservingly | destructively, consumptively | 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + consumptively (from consume + -tive + -ly).  Consume traces back to Latin *consumere, meaning "to use up completely."
