balneology:/ˌbælniˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌbælniˈɑːlədʒi/
n.
温泉疗法；沐浴学
The study of the therapeutic use of baths, especially mineral baths.
Balneology is often prescribed for patients with arthritis. 温泉疗法常用于关节炎患者。
