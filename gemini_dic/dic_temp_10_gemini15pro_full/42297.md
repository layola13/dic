counter-ion:/ˈkaʊntərˌaɪən/ /ˈkaʊntərˌaɪɑːn/ | n. |反离子，抗衡离子| An ion with a charge opposite to that of another ion.  It maintains electrical neutrality in a solution or compound.  Counter-ions are often associated with charged macromolecules or complexes. | The sodium ion acts as a counter-ion to the negatively charged chloride ion in sodium chloride.  氯化钠中，钠离子作为带负电的氯离子的反离子。

The chloride ion acts as a counter-ion to the positively charged sodium ion in sodium chloride. 氯化钠中，氯离子作为带正电的钠离子的反离子。 | synonym: gegenion | antonym:  (No direct antonym, the concept implies a paired relationship) | noun
