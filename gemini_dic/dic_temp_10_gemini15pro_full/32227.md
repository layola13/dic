chichin:/ˈtʃɪtʃɪn/| n.|小鸡；小孩；懦夫；(尤指女性的)乳房|A young chicken; a child (often used as a term of endearment); a coward; (slang, especially Caribbean) a woman's breast.|例句：The mother hen protected her chichins. 母鸡保护着它的小鸡。

例句：Don't be such a chichin! 别这么胆小！|近义词：chick, child, kid, coward, chicken (for coward), boob/breast (slang)|反义词：adult, brave, hero|词性:名词
