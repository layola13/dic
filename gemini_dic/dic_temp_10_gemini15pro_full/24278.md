brigadiership:/ˌbrɪɡəˈdɪrʃɪp/| n.|旅长职位；准将军衔|The position or rank of a brigadier.  It signifies leadership and command over a brigade. |He was promoted to the brigadiership after years of distinguished service. 他在多年的杰出服役后被提升为准将。|Near synonyms: command, leadership, captaincy (depending on context).| Antonyms: subordination,  (lack of specified rank).|noun


brigadier:/brɪɡəˈdɪr/| n.|准将；旅长|An army officer ranking immediately below major general and immediately above colonel, or the equivalent rank in other military services. |He served as a brigadier in the First World War. 他在第一次世界大战中担任准将。|Near synonyms: general, commander, officer| Antonyms: private, subordinate|(depending on context).|noun
