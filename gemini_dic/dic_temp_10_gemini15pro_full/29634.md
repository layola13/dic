catered:/ˈkeɪtərd/| adj. |提供饮食的；迎合的；满足的|designed or prepared to satisfy a particular need or demand|例句：The event was catered to the needs of young professionals.  这次活动是为年轻的专业人士的需求而设计的。|近义词：suited, tailored, adapted|反义词：unsuitable, inappropriate, unfitting|词性:形容词
