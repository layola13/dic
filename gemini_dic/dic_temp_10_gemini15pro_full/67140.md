exposability:/ɪkˌspoʊzəˈbɪləti/
n.
可暴露性；易暴露性；曝光度
The state or quality of being able to be exposed or made visible.
The exposability of the confidential information is a serious concern.  机密信息的曝光度是一个严重的问题。
The location of the house increases its exposability to strong winds. 房子的位置增加了它暴露在强风中的可能性。
近义词: vulnerability, susceptibility, openness
反义词:  impermeability, impenetrability, concealedness
词性: noun

expose: /ɪkˈspoʊz/
v.
暴露；揭露；使曝光
To make something visible or known, especially something that was previously hidden or secret.
The newspaper exposed the corruption within the government. 这家报纸揭露了政府内部的腐败。
He exposed his skin to the sun. 他把皮肤暴露在阳光下。
词性: verb
