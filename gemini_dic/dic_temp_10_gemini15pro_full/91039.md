husking:/ˈhʌskɪŋ/| n. |脱壳，去壳|The process of removing the outer covering (husk) from something, like corn or seeds. |The husking of the corn took place in the barn. 玉米脱壳在谷仓里进行。|近义词：shelling, shucking|反义词:covering, encasing|词性:名词
husking:/ˈhʌskɪŋ/| adj. |用于脱壳的|Used for or relating to the removal of husks. |A husking bee is a social gathering where people husk corn together. 脱壳聚会是一种人们一起剥玉米壳的社交聚会。|近义词：shelling, shucking|反义词:covering, encasing|词性:形容词
