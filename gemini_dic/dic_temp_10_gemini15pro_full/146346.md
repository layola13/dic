overfill:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪl/| v. n.|过度填充，过量填充|To fill something beyond its capacity or limit.  To become filled beyond capacity.|The attendant overfilled the gas tank, and some spilled out. 服务员把油箱加得太满了，有些溢出来了。|近义词：overflow, overpack, glut, surcharge|反义词: underfill|verb

overfill:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪl/| n.|过度填充，过量填充|The act of overfilling something or the state of being overfilled.|The overfill of the reservoir caused flooding downstream. 水库过度蓄水导致下游洪水泛滥。|近义词：excess, surplus, overflow|反义词: shortage, deficit|noun
