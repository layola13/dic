oligomyold:/ˌɒlɪɡəˈmaɪɔld/ | adj. |少肌的，肌肉发育不良的 | Having abnormally few or deficient muscles. | Example: The newborn exhibited signs of being oligomyold, with noticeably weak muscle development.  新生儿表现出少肌的迹象，肌肉发育明显虚弱。| Example:  Oligomyold lambs often struggle to survive. 少肌的羔羊通常难以存活。| Synonym: hypotonic, underdeveloped | Antonym: well-muscled, robust | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From  "oligo-" (Greek: ὀλίγος, oligos, "few, little") + "myo-" (Greek: μυός, myos,  "muscle") + "-old" (suffix indicating resemblance or likeness).
