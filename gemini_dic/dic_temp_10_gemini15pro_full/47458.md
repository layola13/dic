deal with:/diːl wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 处理，应付，涉及 | to take action to solve a problem, handle a difficult situation, or have as a subject |  I have to deal with a lot of paperwork. 我必须处理很多文书工作。| synonyms: handle, manage, address, cope with | antonyms: ignore, neglect, avoid | verb phrase

deal with:/diːl wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 与…打交道；与…做买卖 | to interact or do business with someone | I don't like dealing with pushy salespeople. 我不喜欢和咄咄逼人的销售员打交道。| synonyms: interact with, do business with, negotiate with | antonyms: avoid, shun | verb phrase
