de-objectification:/diːˌɒbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |去物化，去客体化|The process of ceasing to treat a person as an object, recognizing their individuality, agency, and inherent worth.  |例句：The feminist movement has focused on the de-objectification of women in media.  女权运动一直致力于女性在媒体中去客体化。|近义词：humanization, personification, empowerment|反义词：objectification, commodification|词性:名词

de-objectify:/diːˌɒbdʒɛktɪfaɪ/ | v. |去物化，去客体化|To cease treating someone as an object; to recognize a person's individuality and agency. |例句：The campaign aims to de-objectify women and portray them as strong individuals.  该活动旨在消除对女性的物化，并将她们描绘成坚强的个体。|近义词：humanize, personalize, empower|反义词：objectify, commodify|词性:动词
