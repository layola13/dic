linos:/ˈlaɪnəʊz/ /ˈlaɪnoʊz/
n.
亚麻布;油毡;油布
Linoleum (often shortened to lino) is a floor covering made from materials such as solidified linseed oil, pine resin, ground cork dust, wood flour, and pigments.
The kitchen floor was covered in worn linoleum. 厨房地板铺着磨损的油布。

近义词:linoleum, floor covering
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps carpet, rug for contrasting floor covering)
词性:noun
