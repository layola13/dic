heterogangliate:/ˌhɛtərəˈɡæŋɡliˌeɪt/
adjective
异神经节的
Having or relating to ganglia that are different in structure or function.
Heterogangliate mollusks have ganglia of different sizes and shapes throughout their nervous system. 异神经节软体动物在其神经系统中具有不同大小和形状的神经节。
The heterogangliate structure of the nervous system allows for specialized control of different body functions. 异神经节的神经系统结构允许对不同的身体功能进行专门控制。
Synonyms: varied ganglia (not a single word, but describes the concept)
Antonyms: homogangliate (though less common)
词性: adjective
