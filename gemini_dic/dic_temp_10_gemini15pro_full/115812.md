marked-down:/ˌmɑːrkt ˈdaʊn/ | adj. | 降价的，减价的 | Reduced in price. | The store had a sale on marked-down items.  这家商店对降价商品进行促销。| cheaper, discounted, on sale | inflated, full-price | adjective

marked-down:/ˌmɑːrkt ˈdaʊn/ | v. | 降价，减价 | To reduce the price of something. | The manager marked down the damaged goods. 经理降低了受损商品的价格。| reduce, lower, discount | increase, raise | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mark" (to make a visible impression) + "down" (indicating a lower position or amount). The combination literally refers to marking a lower price on an item.
