mudskipper:/ˈmʌdskɪpər/ | n. | 弹涂鱼 | A fish that lives in mudflats and can move on land using its pectoral fins. |  Mudskippers can breathe both in and out of water. 弹涂鱼可以在水里和水外呼吸。

Mudskippers are often seen skipping across the mud.  弹涂鱼经常被看到在泥滩上跳跃。 | 近义词: amphibious fish | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps deep-sea fish) | 词性: noun
