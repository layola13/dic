lunatically:/ˈluːnətɪkli/ /ˈljuːnətɪkli/| adv.|疯狂地，精神错乱地|In a manner characteristic of a lunatic; insanely.|He was laughing lunatically. 他疯狂地大笑。|近义词：insanely, madly, crazily, wildly|反义词：sanely, rationally, reasonably|词性:副词

Etymology:
From lunatic + -ally.  Lunatic itself comes from the Late Latin *lunaticus, from Latin luna (“moon”), based on the belief that changes of the moon caused intermittent insanity.
