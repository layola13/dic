gnathonical: /næθˈɒnɪkəl/
adjective
爱说教的，好争论的
Given to tedious moralizing; disputatious.
He launched into a gnathonical lecture about the importance of flossing. 他开始长篇大论地讲述用牙线清洁牙齿的重要性。
近义词: didactic, preachy, moralistic, sententious, pedantic
反义词: reticent, taciturn
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek gnathos (jaw) + -ical.  Relating to the jaw, hence, talkative, and then, excessively talkative and moralizing.
