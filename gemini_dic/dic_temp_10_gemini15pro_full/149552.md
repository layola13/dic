pager:/ˈpeɪdʒər/| n.|寻呼机；传呼机|A small electronic device that you carry so that people can leave messages for you. |例句：He received a message on his pager. 他在寻呼机上收到了一条消息。

例句：Doctors often carry pagers so they can be contacted in emergencies. 医生经常携带寻呼机以便在紧急情况下可以联系到他们。|近义词：beeper|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "landline" in a specific context) |词性:noun
