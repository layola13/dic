boundable:/ˈbaʊndəbl/
adjective
可绑定的；有限的；有界的
That can be bounded.  Something that has limits or boundaries.
The area of the park is boundable.  这个公园的面积是有限的。
The function's growth is boundable. 这个函数的增长是有界的。
Synonyms: limited, finite, restricted, definable
Antonyms: unlimited, infinite, boundless, unbounded
词性: 形容词
