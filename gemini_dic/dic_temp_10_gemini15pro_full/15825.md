ballade:/bəˈlɑːd/ /ˈbæləd/| n. |歌谣，叙事曲，民谣|A narrative poem, often of folk origin and intended to be sung, consisting of simple stanzas and usually having a refrain.|The singer performed a beautiful ballade. 歌手演唱了一首优美的叙事曲。|近义词：song, poem, balladry, chanson|反义词: story, prose|noun
