fat-lot:/ˈfæt lɒt/ (US) /ˈfæt lɒt/ (UK)| n. | 一点也没有；根本没有；丝毫没有 |  Used ironically to indicate a complete lack of something. It's a sarcastic way of saying "nothing at all."| "A fat lot of good that did!" = “那一点用也没有！” | 近义词:nothing, zip, zilch, diddly-squat | 反义词: a lot, much, plenty | 词性:名词短语 (noun phrase)
