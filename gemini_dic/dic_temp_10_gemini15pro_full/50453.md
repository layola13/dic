depreciator:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtər/
n.|贬低者；折旧者|A person who depreciates something, especially one who belittles or undervalues someone or something. |The constant depreciator brought down the morale of the entire team.  这位不断的贬低者降低了整个团队的士气。|Synonym: critic, detractor, disparager |Antonym: admirer, praiser, eulogist
词性: 名词

depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/
v.|贬值；折旧；轻视|1. Diminish in value over time. 2.  Belittle or undervalue. |My car has depreciated significantly since I bought it. 我的车自从我买来以后已经大大贬值了。|Synonym: devalue, decline, belittle|Antonym: appreciate, increase, value
词性: 动词
