gerbe:/ʒɛʁb/ (French pronunciation) /dʒɜːrb/ (anglicized pronunciation)| n.|花束，喷射，呕吐物|A sheaf or spray of flowers; a cluster of blossoms or firework bursts resembling a sheaf; an act of vomiting.|The fireworks finale featured a magnificent gerbe of golden sparks. 烟花表演的结尾是一个壮观的金色火花喷射。|近义词：bouquet, spray, cluster, eruption, vomit|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

gerbe:/ʒɛʁb/ (French pronunciation) /dʒɜːrb/ (anglicized pronunciation)| n.|呕吐，喷出|An act or instance of vomiting; forceful ejection.|The patient experienced a sudden gerbe of blood. 病人突然喷出一口血。|近义词：vomit, spew, puke|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gerbe*, meaning "sheaf," from Latin *gerba*, also meaning "sheaf."  The sense of "vomiting" is a more recent development, likely influenced by the image of a forceful ejection resembling a spray or sheaf.
