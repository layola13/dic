eruptivity:/ɪˌrʌpˈtɪvɪti/
n.
爆发性，喷发性
The explosivity or intensity of a volcanic eruption.  It considers factors like the volume of material erupted, the height of the eruption column, and the duration of the eruption.
The eruptivity of the volcano was surprising, sending ash clouds miles high. 这座火山的爆发性令人惊讶，火山灰云高达数英里。
近义词：explosivity, volatility
反义词：quiescence, dormancy
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From erupt + -ivity.  "Erupt" comes from the Latin ērumpere (to break out, burst forth), from ex- (out) + rumpere (to break).  "-ivity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state, from French -iveté or Latin -ivitas.
