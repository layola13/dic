circlets:/ˈsɜːklɪts/| n. |小环，圈，花冠|Small rings or circular ornaments, often worn on the head.|The princess wore circlets of gold in her hair. 公主头发上戴着金环。|近义词：rings, bands, crowns, wreaths|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French "cerclet," diminutive of "cercle" (circle), from Latin "circulus," diminutive of "circus" (circle).
