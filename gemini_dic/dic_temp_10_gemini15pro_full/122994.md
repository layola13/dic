monarchal:/məˈnɑːrkl/ | adj. | 君主的；君主政体的；帝王的 | Of or relating to a monarch or monarchy. |  The country retained its monarchal system despite calls for democracy.  这个国家尽管有人呼吁民主，但仍然保留了君主制。| Example: The United Kingdom has a monarchal system of government. 英国实行君主立宪制。| Synonyms: regal, royal, kingly, queenly | Antonyms: republican, democratic | adjective


monarchical:/məˈnɑːrkɪkl/ | adj. | 君主的；君主政体的；帝王的 | Of or relating to a monarch or monarchy. | The country's monarchical traditions are deeply ingrained in its culture.  这个国家的君主传统深深植根于其文化之中。| Example: He criticized the monarchical system and advocated for a republic. 他批评了君主制，并倡导建立共和国。| Synonyms: regal, royal, kingly, queenly | Antonyms: republican, democratic | adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French monarchique, or late Latin monarchicus, from Greek monarkhikos, from monarkhos ‘sole ruler’.
