outlandish:/aʊtˈlændɪʃ/| adj.|古怪的，奇异的，异国风情的|Looking very strange and unusual; strikingly foreign, unfamiliar, or bizarre.  Originating from outside one's own land.|She wore an outlandish outfit to the party. 她穿了一套古怪的服装去参加派对。|Synonyms: bizarre, eccentric, peculiar, strange, unusual, weird, exotic, foreign|Antonyms: conventional, normal, ordinary, typical, usual|adjective
