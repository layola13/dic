ostrogoth:/ˈɒstrəˌɡɒθ/ /ˈɑːstroʊˌɡɑːθ/ | n. | 东哥特人 | A member of the eastern branch of the Goths, who established a kingdom in Italy in the 5th century. |  The Ostrogoths, under Theodoric, conquered Italy.  东哥特人在狄奥多里克的领导下征服了意大利。

The Ostrogoths' reign in Italy was marked by a blend of Roman and Germanic cultures.  东哥特人在意大利的统治时期是罗马和日耳曼文化融合的时期。 | 近义词: Goth, barbarian (in a historical context) | 反义词: Roman (in a historical context) | 词性: Noun
