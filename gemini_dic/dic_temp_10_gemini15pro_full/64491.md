erodabilities:/ɪˌrəʊdəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可蚀性；侵蚀性 | The susceptibility of rock or soil to erosion by water, wind, or ice.  The degree to which earth materials are prone to being worn away by natural forces. | The erodabilities of different soil types vary significantly. 不同土壤类型的可蚀性差异很大。| The high erodabilities of the exposed hillsides contributed to the landslide.  暴露山坡的高侵蚀性导致了山体滑坡。 |  susceptibility to erosion, vulnerability to erosion | resistance to erosion | noun
