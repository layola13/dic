non-dramatic:/ˌnɒn.drəˈmæt.ɪk/ | adj. | 非戏剧性的；平静的；不夸张的 | Not dramatic; calm and ordinary; without exaggerated emotion or suspense. | The meeting was non-dramatic, just a routine update. 会议很平静，只是例行汇报。|  The changes were implemented in a non-dramatic fashion. 这些改变是以一种不夸张的方式实施的。 | 近义词: uneventful, undramatic, low-key, subdued, quiet | 反义词: dramatic, theatrical, sensational, exciting, intense | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + dramatic (from Greek "drama," meaning "action, play")
