lupi:/ˈluːpaɪ/ | n. |狼（复数，通常指母狼）|Plural form of lupa, meaning "she-wolf" in Latin.  It can refer to multiple female wolves or, less commonly, a mixed group of wolves. |The lupi howled at the moon.  那些母狼对着月亮嚎叫。| The lupi were fiercely protective of their cubs.  那些母狼极力保护着它们的幼崽。 | 近义词:wolves (更常见的狼的复数形式),  female wolves | 反义词: (无直接的反义词，可以考虑 prey 猎物) | 词性: 名词
