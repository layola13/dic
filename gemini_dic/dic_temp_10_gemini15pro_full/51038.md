desmogging:/diːˈsmɒɡɪŋ/
noun
除烟雾
The act of removing smog from the air.
The city invested heavily in desmogging efforts.  这个城市大力投资除烟雾工作。
Near synonyms: smog reduction, air purification
Antonyms: polluting, smog creation
词性: 名词
