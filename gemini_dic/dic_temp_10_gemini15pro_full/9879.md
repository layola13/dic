apartheid:/əˈpɑːrtheɪt/ /əˈpɑːrtaɪd/
n.
种族隔离
A policy or system of segregation and discrimination on grounds of race.
South Africa's apartheid regime was finally dismantled in the early 1990s. 南非的种族隔离政权最终在20世纪90年代初被废除。

近义词：segregation, discrimination, racism
反义词：integration, equality, multiracialism
词性：noun
