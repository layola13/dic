oso:/ˈosoʊ/ (US), /ˈəʊsəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.| daring, bold; bear (Spanish)|A Spanish word meaning "daring" or "bold" as an adjective, or "bear" as a noun.  The word is also sometimes used in English, particularly in the American Southwest, to refer to a bear.|Example: El oso es muy grande. (The bear is very big.)|Synonym: (adj.) audacious, brave, courageous (n.) bear|Antonym: (adj.) timid, cowardly|adjective, noun
