comprehend:/ˌkɑːmprɪˈhend/ /ˌkɒmprɪˈhend/| v. |理解，领悟，包含|To understand something fully; to grasp the meaning of something.  |She was too young to comprehend the meaning of death. 她太小了，无法理解死亡的意义。

|I couldn't comprehend what he was saying. 我无法理解他在说什么。|近义词：understand, grasp, perceive, fathom|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|词性:动词
