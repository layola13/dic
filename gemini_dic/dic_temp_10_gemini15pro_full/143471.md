outgains:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnz/
verb
胜过，超过（在收益或增长方面）
surpasses in gains or growth
例句：Company A outgains Company B in annual revenue.  A公司在年度收入方面超过了B公司。

例句：Her investments outgained the market average. 她的投资收益超过了市场平均水平。

近义词：outperforms, surpasses, exceeds
反义词：underperforms, lags behind
词性：v.
