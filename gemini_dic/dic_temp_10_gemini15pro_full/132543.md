nondevout:/ˌnɒndɪˈvaʊt/ | adj. | 不虔诚的，不信教的 | Not devout; not religious. |  She has a nondevout attitude towards religion. 她对宗教的态度不虔诚。 | The family is known for their nondevout lifestyle. 这个家庭以不虔诚的生活方式而闻名。 | 近义词: irreligious, secular, worldly, profane | 反义词: devout, pious, religious | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From non- + devout.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "devout" comes from Old French "devot," meaning "devoted."
