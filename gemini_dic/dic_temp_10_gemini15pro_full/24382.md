brininesses:/ˈbraɪninɪsɪz/
noun
咸味；咸度；海水的咸味
the quality or degree of being salty, like brine.
The brininesses of the different sea salts were compared by the chef.  厨师比较了不同海盐的咸度。
The oyster's brininesses reminded her of the ocean. 生蚝的咸味让她想起了大海。
saltiness, salinity
freshness, sweetness
n.
