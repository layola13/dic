conjunct:/kənˈdʒʌŋkt/ | adj. n. | 连接的，联合的；连接词 | Joined together; a word that connects other words, phrases, or clauses. | The two buildings are conjunct, sharing a common wall.  这两座建筑物相连，共用一堵墙。 |  近义词: joined, connected, united, combined | 反义词: disjunct, separate, divided | 词性: adjective, noun

conjunct:/ˈkɒndʒʌŋkt/ | n. | 连接词 | A word that connects other words, phrases, or clauses. | "And" and "but" are common conjuncts. “和”和“但”是常见的连接词。 | 近义词: conjunction, connective | 反义词:  N/A (as a noun, it doesn't have a direct opposite) | 词性: noun

注意事项:  Conjunct is less commonly used than its synonym "connected."  As a noun, it is primarily used in linguistics.
