manacle: /ˈmænəkl/ (US) /ˈmænəkl/ (UK)| n. v. | 手铐，束缚 | A metal band, chain, or shackle for fastening someone's hands or ankles. To restrain with manacles. | The prisoner's wrists were bound with manacles.  囚犯的手腕被手铐铐住了。|  The police manacled the suspect. 警察给嫌疑犯戴上了手铐。 |Synonyms: handcuff, shackle, fetter, chain | Antonyms: free, liberate, release | noun, verb
