cannula:/ˈkæn.jʊ.lə/ (US), /ˈkæn.jʊ.lə/ (UK)| n. |套管；插管|A thin tube inserted into a vein or body cavity to administer medicine, drain off fluid, or insert a surgical instrument.|The surgeon inserted a cannula into the patient's vein. 外科医生将套管插入病人的静脉。|近义词：catheter, tube|反义词: None|词性:名词
cannulas:/ˈkæn.jʊ.ləz/ (US), /ˈkæn.jʊ.ləz/ (UK)| n. |套管；插管（cannula的复数形式）|Plural form of cannula, referring to multiple thin tubes inserted into veins or body cavities.|The nurse prepared several cannulas for the IV drips. 护士准备了几个套管用于静脉滴注。|近义词：catheters, tubes|反义词: None|词性:名词
