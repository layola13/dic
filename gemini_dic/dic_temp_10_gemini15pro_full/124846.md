mucocele:/ˈmjuːkəˌsiːl/ | n. |黏液囊肿|A cyst containing mucus, typically occurring in the oral cavity or paranasal sinuses.  A mucocele forms when a salivary gland duct is damaged or blocked.| The doctor diagnosed a mucocele on the lower lip. 医生诊断下唇有一个黏液囊肿。|  Synonym: mucous cyst, ranula (when on the floor of the mouth) | Antonym: N/A | noun
