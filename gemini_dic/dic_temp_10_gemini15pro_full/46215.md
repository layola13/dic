cystic:/ˈsɪstɪk/| adj.|囊性的，囊肿的|Relating to, of the nature of, or containing cysts.|例句：Cystic fibrosis is a genetic disorder. 囊性纤维化是一种遗传性疾病。|例句：She had a cystic tumor removed from her ovary. 她切除了卵巢上的一个囊肿。|近义词：cystoid, bladdery|反义词: healthy, normal|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin *cȳsticus, from Ancient Greek κυστικός (kustikós, “of the bladder”), from κύστις (kústis, “bladder, pouch”).
