ignitibilities:/ɪɡˌnɪtɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可燃性，易燃性|The plural form of "ignitibility," referring to the capacity of multiple substances or materials to catch fire readily.|The ignitabilities of different types of wood vary greatly. 不同种类木材的可燃性差异很大。|Near synonyms: flammabilities, combustibilities|Near antonyms: fire resistances, non-flammabilities|noun
