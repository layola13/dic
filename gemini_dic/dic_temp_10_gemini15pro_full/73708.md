一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I sometimes forget names. 我有时会忘记名字。
He forgets his homework regularly. 他经常忘记他的作业。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm forgetting everything these days. 我这几天什么都忘。
She's forgetting her manners. 她正在忘记她的礼仪。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I've forgotten what I wanted to say. 我忘记了我想要说什么。
He's forgotten his password. 他忘记了他的密码。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been forgetting things lately. 我最近一直在忘记事情。
He's been forgetting to call his mother. 他一直忘记打电话给他母亲。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I forgot to buy milk. 我忘记买牛奶了。
She forgot her appointment. 她忘记了她的预约。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was forgetting everything she told me. 我正在忘记她告诉我的一切。
He was forgetting his lines in the play. 他正在忘记他在戏剧中的台词。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had forgotten her birthday. 我忘记了她的生日。
He had forgotten all about the incident. 他完全忘记了那件事。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been forgetting my wallet everywhere. 我一直到处忘记我的钱包。
She had been forgetting to water the plants. 她一直忘记给植物浇水。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will forget all about this by tomorrow. 明天我就把这件事忘得一干二净了。
He will forget to bring the snacks. 他会忘记带零食。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be forgetting all about this by next week.  下周我就把这件事忘得一干二净了。
He will be forgetting to pay the bills. 他将会忘记付账单。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have forgotten all about this. 到明年，我将完全忘记这件事。
By the time the movie is over, he will have forgotten the beginning. 等电影结束时，他将忘记开头。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been forgetting things for a decade. 到明年，我将已经忘记事情十年了。
By the time he retires, he will have been forgetting his glasses for years. 等到他退休时，他忘记眼镜已经好几年了。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't forgotten my umbrella, I wouldn't have gotten wet. 如果我没有忘记带伞，我就不会淋湿了。
I wish I hadn't forgotten her name. 我希望我没有忘记她的名字。


被动语态 (虽然forgot本身很少用于被动语态，但可以用于类似以下的例句):
The incident was forgotten by everyone. 这件事被大家遗忘了。
His name was forgotten over time. 他的名字随着时间的推移被遗忘了。


疑问句:
Did you forget your password? 你忘记你的密码了吗？
Have you forgotten about our meeting? 你忘记我们的会议了吗？


否定句:
I didn't forget your birthday. 我没有忘记你的生日。
I haven't forgotten what you said. 我没有忘记你说的话。
