myelomata:/ˌmaɪəˈləʊməˌtə/ | n. |骨髓瘤（复数）|Plural form of myeloma, referring to multiple tumors originating in the bone marrow's plasma cells.|Multiple myelomata were discovered during the routine blood test. 例句：在常规血液检查中发现了多发性骨髓瘤。|近义词:plasma cell tumors, multiple myeloma (when referring to the disease)|反义词:None (There isn't a direct opposite for a specific type of tumor.)|词性:Noun
