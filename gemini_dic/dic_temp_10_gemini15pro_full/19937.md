biodegradabilities:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdiːɡreɪdəˈbɪlɪtiz/ |n. |生物降解性；生物可降解性 |The ability of a substance to be broken down into simpler substances by the action of microorganisms such as bacteria or fungi. |  The biodegradabilities of different plastics vary widely. 不同塑料的生物降解性差异很大。| Synonyms: decomposability, biodegradability | Antonyms: non-biodegradability, persistence | noun
