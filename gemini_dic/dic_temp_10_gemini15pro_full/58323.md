drylander:/ˈdraɪˌlændər/| n.|陆地人；旱地居民|A person who lives on dry land, especially one who is not accustomed to the sea or boats.|The drylander struggled with the rocking of the boat.  这个旱鸭子在摇晃的船上挣扎着。|近义词：landsman, landlubber|反义词:seaman, mariner|词性:noun

drylander:/ˈdraɪˌlændər/| adj.|陆地的；旱地的|Of or relating to dry land.|Drylander crops require less water than aquatic ones. 陆地作物比水生作物需要的水更少。|近义词：terrestrial|反义词:aquatic, marine|词性:adjective
