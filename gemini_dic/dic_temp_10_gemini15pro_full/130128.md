non-realist:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪst/ /ˌnɑːnˈriːəlɪst/ | adj. | 非现实主义的；非写实主义的 | Not adhering to realism, especially in literature or art, often representing idealized versions of reality or exploring imaginative or fantastic themes. |  Her non-realist paintings featured vibrant colors and dreamlike landscapes. 她非现实主义的绘画以鲜艳的色彩和梦幻般的风景为特色。

| Example: The author's non-realist approach to storytelling involved magical creatures and talking animals.  这位作者非现实主义的讲故事方法涉及到神奇的生物和会说话的动物。 | Synonym:  anti-realist,  idealistic, romanticist, fantastical, imaginative | Antonym: realist, naturalistic, pragmatic,  verisimilitudinous | adjective
