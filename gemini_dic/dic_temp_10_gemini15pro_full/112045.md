longboard:/ˈlɒŋbɔːrd/ (UK), /ˈlɔːŋbɔːrd/ (US)| n.|长板|A type of skateboard that is longer and wider than a standard skateboard, often used for cruising, downhill racing, and freestyle riding.|He rode his longboard down the hill. 他骑着长板滑下山。|近义词：skateboard, cruiser|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps shortboard in surfing context)|noun

longboard:/ˈlɒŋbɔːrd/ (UK), /ˈlɔːŋbɔːrd/ (US)| v.|进行长板运动|To ride a longboard.|She longboards to work every day. 她每天都踩着长板去上班。|近义词：skateboard, cruise|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb
