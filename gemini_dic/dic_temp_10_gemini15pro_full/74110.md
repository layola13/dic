fougasse:/fuˈɡæs/ | n. | 佛卡夏面包；坑道爆破 | A type of bread, similar to focaccia, that is slashed or scored before baking to resemble an ear of wheat or leaves. Also refers to a type of mine used in warfare. |  Example:  I baked a fougasse with rosemary and olive oil. 我烤了一个迷迭香和橄榄油的佛卡夏面包。

Example: The engineers prepared the fougasse to demolish the bridge. 工程师们准备用坑道爆破来拆除桥梁。 | Near synonyms: focaccia, bread, mine (military) | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, depends on the meaning used - could be "fill" if referencing the mine meaning) | Noun
