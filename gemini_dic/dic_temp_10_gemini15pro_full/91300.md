hydromechanics:/ˌhaɪdrəməˈkænɪks/ | n. | 流体力学，水力学 | The study of the mechanical properties of liquids, particularly water, and the forces acting on them, including hydrostatics and hydrodynamics.  |  例句：Hydromechanics plays a vital role in designing dams.  水力学在大坝设计中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：He specialized in hydromechanics and its applications in naval architecture. 他专攻流体力学及其在造船学中的应用。 | 近义词：fluid mechanics | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: hydro- (water) + mechanics
