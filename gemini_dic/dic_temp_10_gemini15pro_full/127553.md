necropsied: /ˈnɛkrɑpsiːd/ /ˈniːkrɑpsiːd/
Verb
尸检过的；被解剖的
Performed a necropsy on (a dead body or plant).
例句：The bird was necropsied to determine the cause of death. 这只鸟被解剖以确定死因。

例句：All the deceased animals were necropsied. 所有死亡的动物都进行了尸检。

Synonym: autopsied, dissected, examined
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for the action of necropsy itself, perhaps "left unexamined" or "buried without examination")
词性: 动词(Past Participle)
