disimprovement:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpruːvmənt/
n.
恶化，退步，变坏
A worsening or decline in quality, condition, or performance.
The disimprovement in his health was alarming. 他的健康状况恶化令人担忧。
近义词: deterioration, decline, worsening, regression
反义词: improvement, betterment, enhancement, progress
词性: 名词

词源:  "dis-" (expressing negation) + "improvement" (from "improve," from Old French "emprover," meaning "to make better").
