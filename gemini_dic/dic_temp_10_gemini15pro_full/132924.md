nonelucidating:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不启发性的，不明确的
Not making something clear; failing to explain something.
The professor's nonelucidating lecture left the students more confused than before. 教授那不启发性的讲座让学生们比以前更困惑了。
His response was nonelucidating and unhelpful. 他的回答既不清晰又没有帮助。
Synonyms: unclear, confusing, obscure, opaque, ambiguous, vague
Antonyms: clear, clarifying, illuminating, enlightening, explicit, perspicuous
词性:形容词
