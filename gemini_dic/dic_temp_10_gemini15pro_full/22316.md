bonkers:/ˈbɒŋkəz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːŋkərz/ (US)| adj.|疯狂的，精神错乱的|Mad, crazy, or eccentric.|He went completely bonkers after losing his job. 他失业后彻底疯了。|例句：The traffic noise is driving me bonkers. 交通噪音快把我逼疯了。|近义词：crazy, mad, insane, nuts, loopy|反义词：sane, rational, sensible|词性:形容词
词源:  Likely from "bonk" (a blow to the head), implying being hit on the head and thus becoming crazy. This etymology is not universally accepted, but considered plausible.
