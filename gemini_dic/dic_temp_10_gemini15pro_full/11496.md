n.
aricine; 阿里新
A crystalline alkaloid, C23H26N2O4, obtained from cinchona bark and used as an antimalarial drug.  It is an isomer of quinine.
从金鸡纳树皮中提取的一种结晶生物碱，C23H26N2O4，用作抗疟疾药物。它是奎宁的异构体。
Aricine was isolated from the bark and shown to be effective against malaria.  阿里新是从树皮中分离出来的，并被证明对疟疾有效。
A new method for synthesizing aricine has been developed.  已经开发出一种合成阿里新的新方法。
