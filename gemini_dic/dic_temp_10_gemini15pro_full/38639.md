concupiscentious:/kənˌkjuːpɪˈsɛnʃəs/| adj.|好色的；贪婪的；肉欲的|Characterized by or showing strong sexual desire; lustful. Also, excessively desirous of something.|例句：The concupiscentious king was known for his many mistresses. 这位好色的国王以他的众多情妇而闻名。

例句：His concupiscentious gaze made her uncomfortable. 他贪婪的目光让她感到不舒服。|近义词：lustful, lecherous, lascivious, avaricious, covetous|反义词：chaste, pure, virtuous, ascetic|词性: adjective

词源: Late Latin *concupiscentiosus*, from Latin concupiscent- (stem of concupiscens ) 'desiring eagerly' + -osus '-ous'.
