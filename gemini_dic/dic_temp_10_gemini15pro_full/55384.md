distemperednesses:/dɪsˈtɛmpərdnɪsɪz/
|n.|
|坏脾气，易怒，精神错乱|
|A state of being distempered; ill humor, irritability, or mental derangement.|
|His distemperednesses made it difficult for him to maintain healthy relationships. 他的坏脾气使他难以维持健康的人际关系。|
|The king's distemperednesses led to rash and unwise decisions. 国王的易怒导致了轻率和不明智的决定。|
|近义词: irritability, irascibility, peevishness, grumpiness, crankiness, surliness, moodiness, testiness, choler, wrath, fury, rage, madness, insanity, lunacy, dementia|
|反义词: calmness, tranquility, serenity, placidity, composure, equanimity, patience, tolerance, sanity, lucidity|
|词性: 名词|


Etymology:
From distempered + -ness.  Distempered itself comes from dis- (meaning "not" or "opposite of") + tempered (meaning "moderate" or "balanced").  The ultimate root is the Latin word "temperare," meaning "to mix in due proportion."
