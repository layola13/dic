listen:/ˈlɪsən/ (US) /ˈlɪsn/ (UK)| vi. |听; 倾听|To pay attention to sounds; to hear something with thoughtful attention.|例句：Please listen to me. 请听我说。|近义词：hear, heed, attend|反义词:ignore, disregard|verb

listen:/ˈlɪsən/ (US) /ˈlɪsn/ (UK)| vi. |听从|To follow advice or instructions.|例句：He never listens to his parents. 他从来不听父母的话。|近义词：obey, comply, heed|反义词:disobey, ignore|verb
