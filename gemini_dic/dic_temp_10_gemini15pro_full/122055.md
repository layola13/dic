mistaking:/mɪˈsteɪkɪŋ/
|v.|弄错，误解|The act of understanding something incorrectly; making a mistake.|She apologized for mistaking me for someone else. 她为把我错认成别人而道歉。|Synonyms: misinterpreting, misconstruing, misunderstanding|Antonyms: understanding, comprehending|verb

mistaking:/mɪˈsteɪkɪŋ/
|adj.|错误的|Incorrect or based on a misunderstanding. |His mistaking assumption led to a series of problems. 他错误的假设导致了一系列问题。|Synonyms: erroneous, fallacious, incorrect|Antonyms: correct, accurate, right|adjective
