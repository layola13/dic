lionization:/ˌlaɪənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
名词
给予极大的关注和赞扬；捧成名人；崇拜
The act of treating someone as a famous or important person.
例句：The lionization of athletes often leads to inflated egos.  运动员被捧成名人常常导致他们自我膨胀。

例句：She enjoyed the lionization that came with winning the award. 她很享受获奖带来的追捧。

近义词：glorification, celebration, exaltation, deification
反义词：vilification, condemnation, criticism
