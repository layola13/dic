deluding:/dɪˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
欺骗的，误导的
making someone believe something that is not true
例句：He was deluding himself into thinking he would win the lottery. 他欺骗自己以为会中彩票。

例句：The advertisement was deluding customers with false promises.  这则广告用虚假的承诺欺骗顾客。

近义词：misleading, deceiving, tricking, fooling
反义词：enlightening, informing, undeceiving
词性：Verb (present participle of delude)


delude:/dɪˈluːd/
verb

例句：She deluded herself into thinking that he loved her. 她欺骗自己，以为他爱她。
