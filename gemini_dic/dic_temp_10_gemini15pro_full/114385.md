make a dent in:/meɪk ə dɛnt ɪn/ |v. phrasal| 取得进展；产生影响；减少; to make some progress with a task or to reduce the amount of something|例句：We've made a dent in the backlog of work. 我们在积压的工作上取得了一些进展。|近义词：make progress, have an impact, reduce, diminish|反义词:increase, worsen, aggravate|词性:动词短语

词源:  "Dent" originally refers to a hollow or depression made by a blow.  The phrase "make a dent in" metaphorically extends this meaning to represent making progress on something large or reducing something significantly, as if making a noticeable mark on it.
