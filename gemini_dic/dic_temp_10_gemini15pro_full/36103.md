coequal:/koʊˈiːkwəl/
adjective
同等的，平等的
Having the same rank or importance.
例句：The two countries are coequal partners in the trade agreement. 这两个国家是贸易协定的平等伙伴。

例句：The three branches of government are designed to be coequal. 政府的三个分支被设计成平等的。

近义词：equal, equivalent, peer
反义词：unequal, subordinate, inferior
词性: adjective

coequal:/koʊˈiːkwəl/
noun
同等的人；同僚
A person or thing that is equal to another.
例句：He treated his coequals with respect. 他尊重他的同僚。

例句：She is a coequal in the company hierarchy. 她在公司层级中是同等的。

近义词：peer, equal
反义词：subordinate, inferior
词性: noun


Etymology: From co- + equal.
