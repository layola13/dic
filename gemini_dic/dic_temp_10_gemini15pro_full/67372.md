extensor:/ɪkˈstɛnsər/ | n. | 伸肌 | A muscle serving to extend a bodily part, as an arm or a finger. | The extensor muscles in his forearm allowed him to straighten his wrist. 他的前臂伸肌让他能够伸直手腕。 |  近义词: straightener | 反义词: flexor | 词性: noun

extensor:/ɪkˈstɛnsər/ | adj. | 伸的，伸展的 | Serving to extend.  | The extensor tendon was damaged in the accident. 伸肌腱在事故中受损。 | 近义词: extending, stretching | 反义词: flexing, bending | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *extēnsōrius, from Latin extēnsus, past participle of extendere "to stretch out," from ex- "out" + tendere "to stretch."
