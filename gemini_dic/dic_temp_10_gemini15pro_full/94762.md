improvises:/ˈɪmprəvaɪzɪz/| v.| 即兴创作，临时凑合| creates and performs (music, drama, or verse) spontaneously or without preparation.| The musician improvised a solo.  这位音乐家即兴演奏了一段独奏。| synonyms: ad-lib, extemporize, wing it| antonyms: plan, rehearse, prepare| verb

Etymology->
From Late Latin improvisus, from in- "not" + provisus "foreseen," past participle of providēre "to foresee."
