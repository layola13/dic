filmiest:/ˈfɪlmiɪst/| adj.|最薄的；最像电影的|Having the nature of a film; resembling a film, especially in being unreal or romantic.  Thin and light, like a film.  | Her dress was of the filmiest silk. 她的连衣裙是用最薄的丝绸制成的。

The ending of the movie was so filmiest, completely unrealistic. 这部电影的结局太像电影了，完全不现实。|近义词：thinnest, gauziest, sheerest, flimsiest, most cinematic|反义词：thickest, densest, most opaque|词性:形容词

词源:  From "film" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "having the character of") + "-est" (superlative suffix).
