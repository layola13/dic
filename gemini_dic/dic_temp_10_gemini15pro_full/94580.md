impower:/ɪmˈpaʊər/| vt.|授权，使能够|To give someone the authority or power to do something; to enable.|例句：The new law impowers the police to search any vehicle. 新法律授权警察搜查任何车辆。

例句：Education impowers people to make better choices for their lives. 教育使人们能够为自己的生活做出更好的选择。|近义词：authorize, enable, empower|反义词：restrict, disable, disempower|词性:动词
