nonpalatableness:/ˌnɒnˌpælətəbəlnəs/ | n. | 不美味，难吃，令人不快的 |The nonpalatableness of the medicine made it difficult for the child to swallow.  这种药很难吃，孩子很难吞下去。|  The nonpalatableness of his proposal made it unlikely to be accepted.  他的提议令人不快，不太可能被接受。 | 近义词:unpleasantness, distastefulness, unacceptability | 反义词: palatableness, pleasantness, deliciousness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From non- + palatable + -ness.  "Palatable" comes from Old French "palatable"  (agreeable to the palate), from Late Latin "palatabilis", from Latin "palatum" (palate).
