coif:/kwɑːf/ (US), /kɔɪf/ (UK)| n. vt.|发型，头饰；梳理，做（头发）|A woman's hairstyle, typically elaborate.  To arrange (hair) elaborately.|She wore an elaborate coif for the wedding. 她为婚礼梳了一个精致的发型。 |She coifed her hair into an elegant updo. 她把头发梳成一个优雅的高髻。|近义词：hairstyle, hairdo, coiffure |反义词: mess (n., referring to messy hair)|noun, verb
