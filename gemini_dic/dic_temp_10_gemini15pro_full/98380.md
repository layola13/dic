insociable:/ɪnˈsoʊʃəbl/
adjective
不合群的，不爱交际的
Not sociable; averse to companionship or conversation.
例句：He was an insociable man who preferred to live alone. 他是一个不合群的人，喜欢独居。
例句：She found him rather insociable and difficult to talk to. 她觉得他相当不爱交际，很难与之交谈。
近义词：unsociable, antisocial, reserved, withdrawn
反义词：sociable, gregarious, outgoing, extroverted
词性：形容词

词源：
From in- (not) + sociable (from Late Latin sociabilis, from sociare "to associate," from socius "companion").  The "in-" prefix negates the meaning of "sociable."
