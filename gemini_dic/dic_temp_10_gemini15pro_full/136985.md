nonspinning:/ˌnɒnˈspɪnɪŋ/
| adj. | 非旋转的，无自旋的 | Not spinning; having no spin.  Relating to or denoting a particle, nucleus, or atom that has zero intrinsic angular momentum. |  Electrons in certain orbitals are nonspinning.  某些轨道上的电子是非旋转的。 | nonspinning electrons occupy the lowest energy levels. 非旋转电子占据最低能级。| nonrotating, motionless | rotating, spinning | adjective
