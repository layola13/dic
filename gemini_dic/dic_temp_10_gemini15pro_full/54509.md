disjointedly:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪdli/ | adv. | 不连贯地，支离破碎地 | In a disjointed or disconnected manner; lacking coherence or continuity. |  He spoke disjointedly, jumping from one topic to another. 他说话语无伦次，从一个话题跳到另一个话题。|  She presented her arguments disjointedly, making it difficult to follow her reasoning.  她断断续续地陈述了她的论点，让人很难理解她的推理。 | incoherently, disconnectedly, ramblingly |  coherently, fluidly, smoothly | adverb
