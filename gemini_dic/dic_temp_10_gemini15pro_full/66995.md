expiable:/ˈɛkspiəbəl/
adjective
可赎的；可补偿的
Capable of being expiated or atoned for; capable of being forgiven or excused.
His sins are expiable through prayer and good works. 他的罪孽可以通过祈祷和善行来赎清。
Synonyms: atonable, redeemable, forgivable, pardonable
Antonyms: inexpiable, unpardonable, unforgivable
词性: 形容词
