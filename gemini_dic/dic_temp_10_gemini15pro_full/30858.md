chador:/ˈtʃɑːdɔːr/ (US), /ˈtʃædɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. | 头巾，罩袍 | A large piece of cloth that some Muslim women wear to cover their head and body when they are in public. |  She wore a black chador that concealed her entire body except for her face. 她穿着一件黑色的罩袍，除了脸以外遮住了她的整个身体。

She adjusted her chador before entering the mosque.  她在进入清真寺前整理了一下她的头巾。 | 近义词：veil, hijab, niqab, burqa | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Persian چادر (čâdor), meaning "tent, pavilion, sheet".
