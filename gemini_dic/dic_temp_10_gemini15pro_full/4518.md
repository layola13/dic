albuminiparous: /ˌælbjʊˈmɪnɪpərəs/
adjective
产蛋白的；卵白生的
Producing albumin or albumen; specifically (Zool.) producing eggs in which the embryo is nourished by albumin.  Relating to animals whose eggs are rich in albumen, providing nourishment for the developing embryo.
The platypus is an albuminiparous mammal.  鸭嘴兽是一种产蛋白的哺乳动物。
The albuminiparous hen laid a large egg. 这只产蛋白的母鸡产下了一个大蛋。
近义词: oviparous
反义词: viviparous
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *albumini- (stem of *albumen "egg white") + -parous "bearing, producing" (from Latin -parus, from parere "to bring forth").
