antievolutionist:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst/ | n. | 反进化论者 |  A person who opposes the theory of evolution, especially biological evolution by natural selection. |  The antievolutionist argued against the fossil record.  反进化论者反对化石记录。| synonyms: creationist, anti-Darwinist | antonyms: evolutionist, Darwinist | noun

antievolutionist:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst/ | adj. | 反进化论的 | Opposing or denying the theory of evolution. | He holds antievolutionist views. 他持有反进化论的观点。| synonyms: creationist, anti-Darwinist | antonyms: evolutionary, Darwinian | adjective
