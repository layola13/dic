devote:/dɪˈvoʊt/ (US) /dɪˈvəʊt/ (UK)| vt. | 致力于，奉献 | to give all or a large part of one's time or resources to (a person, activity, or cause). | She devoted her life to helping others. 她一生致力于帮助他人。

She devotes most of her free time to playing the piano.  她大部分的空闲时间都用来弹钢琴。


近义词: dedicate, commit, allot, assign
反义词: neglect, forsake, ignore
词性: 动词 (verb)

词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin devovere, from de- ‘down, away’ + vovere ‘to vow’.
