overinstructions:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkʃənz/ | n. | 过度指导，过度指示 |  The act of giving too many instructions, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency. This can lead to decreased performance, especially in tasks requiring creativity or independent thought. | The employee was overwhelmed by the overinstructions and couldn't complete the project effectively.  员工被过多的指示弄得不知所措，无法有效地完成项目。 | synonyms: overguidance, micromanagement | antonyms: underinstruction, autonomy | noun

overinstructs:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkts/ | v. | 过度指导，过度指示 | To give too many instructions, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency. | The manager overinstructs his team, leading to frustration and decreased productivity. 经理过度指导他的团队，导致沮丧和生产力下降。 | synonyms: micromanages, overguides | antonyms: underinstructs, empowers | verb

overinstructed:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪd/ | adj. | 被过度指导的，被过度指示的 | Having received too many instructions, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency. |  The overinstructed intern felt paralyzed and unable to take initiative.  被过度指导的实习生感到不知所措，无法主动采取行动。 | synonyms: overguided, micromanaged | antonyms: underinstructed, empowered | adjective
