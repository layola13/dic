discretionarily:/dɪˈskreʃənɛrɪli/
adv.
酌情地；自由裁量地
In a way that involves making decisions based on one's own judgment.
The funds were distributed discretionarily by the committee. 委员会酌情分配了资金。
The CEO is authorized to discretionarily spend up to $1 million. 首席执行官有权酌情动用高达100万美元的资金。
Synonym: at one's discretion, optionally, at will
Antonym: obligatorily, mandatorily
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *discretionarius, from Latin discretio (“separation, distinction”), from discernere (“to distinguish”), from dis- (“apart”) + cernere (“to separate, sift, distinguish”).
