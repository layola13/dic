diacodium:/ˌdaɪəˈkoʊdiəm/
n.
止咳糖浆;罂粟糖浆
A syrup containing poppy heads and used to induce sleep or relieve pain.
The physician prescribed diacodium for the child's cough.  医生给孩子开了止咳糖浆。
近义词: syrup, opiate, anodyne, analgesic, paregoric
反义词: stimulant
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin diacodion, from Greek diakon 'poppy juice,' from dia- 'through' + konis 'powder.'
