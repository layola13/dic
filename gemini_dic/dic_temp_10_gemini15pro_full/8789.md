antiegoists:/ˈæntiˈiːɡoʊɪsts/
n.|反利己主义者|People who oppose egoism, a philosophical concept that emphasizes self-interest as the foundation of morality.  They prioritize the well-being of others, often advocating for altruism and cooperation.|Those antiegoists devoted their lives to helping the poor.|近义词: altruists, philanthropists|反义词: egoists, narcissists|词性: noun
