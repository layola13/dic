custom-built:/ˈkʌstəm bɪlt/ | adj. | 定制的，定做的 |  Designed and built to a particular customer's specifications. | Example:  They live in a custom-built house overlooking the ocean. 他们住在一栋可以俯瞰大海的定制房屋里。| Example:  She had a custom-built dress made for the wedding. 她为婚礼定制了一件礼服。 | Synonyms: bespoke, tailor-made, made-to-order | Antonyms: mass-produced, off-the-shelf | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "custom" (meaning made to order) + "built" (past participle of "build").  "Custom" comes from Old French "costume" and ultimately from Latin "consuetudo" meaning "habit, usage." "Built" comes from Old English "byldan."
