eyrie:/ˈɪəri/ /ˈɛəri/ | n. | 鹰巢; 高处危险的地方; |A large nest of a bird of prey, especially an eagle, built high in a tree or on a cliff.  A high and exposed place, often a dwelling. |The eagle returned to its eyrie on the cliff face.  老鹰回到了它在悬崖峭壁上的巢穴。|The hermit lived in an eyrie high in the mountains.  隐士住在高山上的一处高地。|近义词：nest, aerie, perch, roost, ledge|反义词:ground, valley|词性:名词
