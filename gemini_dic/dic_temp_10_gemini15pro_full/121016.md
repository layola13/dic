miscollocation: /ˌmɪskɒləˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |  错误搭配；词语误用 | The use of a word in a context where it does not belong, resulting in an awkward or illogical expression. |  His essay was full of miscollocations, making it difficult to understand. 他的文章充满了错误搭配，让人难以理解。 | 近义词: solecism, impropriety | 反义词: correct usage, proper collocation | 词性: noun
