long-sightedness: /ˌlɒŋˈsaɪtɪdnəs/ /ˌlɔːŋˈsaɪtɪdnəs/
noun
远视；目光远大
The condition of being able to see things clearly that are far away but not things that are close.  Being able to understand or plan for the future, rather than only thinking about the present.
She wears glasses to correct her long-sightedness. 她戴眼镜矫正远视。
His long-sightedness enabled him to anticipate the market trends. 他的远见使他能够预见市场趋势。
近义词: hyperopia, farsightedness, foresight, vision
反义词: short-sightedness, myopia, nearsightedness
