conspirative:/kənˈspɪrətɪv/
adjective
阴谋的；秘密策划的；会意的
Relating to or suggestive of a conspiracy; secretive.
They exchanged conspirative glances. 他们交换了心照不宣的眼神。
近义词:  clandestine, covert, furtive, secretive, sly
反义词: open, forthright, aboveboard
词性: adjective

conspiratorial:/kənˌspɪrəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
阴谋的；秘密策划的；会意的
Relating to or suggestive of a conspiracy; secretive.
He had a conspiratorial look on his face. 他脸上带着一副神秘兮兮的表情。
近义词: clandestine, covert, furtive, secretive, sly
反义词: open, forthright, aboveboard
词性: adjective


词源:  From Late Latin *conspirativus, from Latin conspiratus, past participle of conspirare (“to agree, unite, plot together”), from con- (“together”) + spirare (“to breathe”).
