huddler:/ˈhʌdlər/| n.| 挤在一起的人; 蜷缩者; 参加秘密会议的人|A person who huddles, especially to keep warm or to confer privately. |The huddlers around the fire tried to stay warm.  挤在火堆旁的人试图保持温暖。| The football team formed a huddler to discuss their next play. 足球队围成一团讨论他们的下一个战术。| synonym: croucher, squatter, conferrer | antonym: loner, individualist | noun
