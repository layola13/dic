chauvinism: /ˈʃoʊvɪˌnɪzəm/ | n. | 沙文主义；盲目爱国 | Excessive or prejudiced loyalty or support for one's own cause, group, or sex. |  National chauvinism is a dangerous force in the world. 国家沙文主义是世界上一股危险的力量。 | jingoism, bigotry, prejudice | tolerance, open-mindedness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chauvinisme*, derived from Nicolas Chauvin, a legendary excessively patriotic soldier in Napoleon's army.
