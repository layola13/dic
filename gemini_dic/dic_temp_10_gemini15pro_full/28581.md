careerist: /kəˈrɪərɪst/ | n. |野心家；一心追求名利的人 | A person whose primary concern is furthering their own career, often at the expense of others or ethical considerations. | He's such a careerist, he'd do anything to get ahead. 他真是个野心家，为了晋升什么事都做得出来。 |  She was accused of being a careerist when she accepted the promotion over her colleague. 当她接受晋升而同事没有时，她被指责为野心家。 | 近义词: opportunist, climber, go-getter | 反义词: altruist, philanthropist | 词性: noun
