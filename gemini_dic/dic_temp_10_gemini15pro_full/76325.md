gabled:/ˈɡeɪbəld/| adj.|有山墙的，人字屋顶的|Having a gable or gables. A gable is the triangular upper part of a wall at the end of a ridged roof.|The gabled roof of the cottage looked picturesque against the setting sun.  小屋的人字形屋顶在夕阳的映衬下显得格外美丽。|Synonyms: pitched, peaked|Antonyms: flat, hipped|adjective

gabled:/ˈɡeɪbəld/| n.|山墙, 人字形墙|A gable is the triangular upper part of a wall at the end of a ridged roof.|The gabled end of the barn faced the road. 谷仓的山墙面向道路。|Synonyms: gable wall, gable end|Antonyms: N/A|noun
