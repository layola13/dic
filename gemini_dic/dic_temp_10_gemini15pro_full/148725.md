overtrap:/ˈoʊvərˌtræp/| v. |过度捕猎，过度诱捕|To trap or hunt a species to the point where its population is severely reduced or depleted.|Excessive overtrapping has led to the near extinction of several species. 过度捕猎导致几个物种濒临灭绝。|Overtrapping decimated the beaver population in the area. 该地区的过度诱捕使海狸数量锐减。|近义词：overhunt, overfish|反义词:conserve, protect|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "trap" (verb meaning "to catch in a trap")
