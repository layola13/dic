overcommend: /ˌoʊvər.kəˈmend/ | vt. | 过度称赞，过分赞扬 | To praise excessively or unduly. |  He overcommended her performance, making it seem better than it actually was. 他过分赞扬了她的表演，使它看起来比实际情况要好得多。

|例句：The manager overcommended the new employee's initial efforts. 经理过度称赞了新员工最初的努力。
|例句：She felt he overcommended her presentation, making her uncomfortable. 她觉得他过分赞扬了她的演讲，让她感到不舒服。

|近义词：overpraise, laud excessively, extol excessively |反义词：underestimate, criticize, downplay |词性：动词
