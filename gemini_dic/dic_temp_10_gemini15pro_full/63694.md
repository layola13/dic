epexegesis:/ˌɛpɛkˈsiːdʒɪsɪs/ | n. | 解释，补充说明，诠释 | An explanation or clarification of a word or passage, especially in the Bible.  It adds further detail to something already mentioned. | The author provided an epexegesis of the difficult passage, making it easier to understand. 作者对这段难懂的文字进行了补充说明，使之更容易理解。 | 近义词: explanation, clarification, interpretation, exegesis, exposition | 反义词: ambiguity, obfuscation | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐπεξήγησις (epexēgēsis, “further explanation”), from ἐπι- (epi-, “upon, in addition”) + ἐξήγησις (exēgēsis, “explanation”).
