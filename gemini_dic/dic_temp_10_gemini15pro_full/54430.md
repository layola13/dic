disinfector:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktər/| n. |消毒员，消毒器|A person or device that disinfects something.|例句：The disinfector sprayed the entire room. 消毒员喷洒了整个房间。

例句：The hospital uses a UV disinfector for surgical instruments. 医院对手术器械使用紫外线消毒器。|近义词：sterilizer, sanitizer|反义词:contaminator|词性:noun
