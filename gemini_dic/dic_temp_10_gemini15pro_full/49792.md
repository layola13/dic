demonizes:/ˈdiːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
妖魔化；将…描绘成恶魔
portrays someone or something as wicked or threatening.
The media often demonizes politicians they disagree with. 媒体经常妖魔化他们不同意的政治家。
vilifies, criticizes harshly, condemns
humanizes, praises, glorifies
v.
