nationalisms:/ˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
民族主义（复数）
The belief that one's own nation is superior to others and should be independent.  It often involves strong feelings of patriotism and a desire to promote the interests of one's own nation, sometimes at the expense of other nations.
The rise of nationalisms in the 19th century led to many wars. 19世纪民族主义的兴起导致了许多战争。
The conference addressed the dangers of competing nationalisms. 会议讨论了相互竞争的民族主义的危险。
近义词: patriotism, chauvinism
反义词: internationalism, globalism
词性: noun
