go-for-broke:/ˌɡoʊ fər ˈbroʊk/ | idiom | 孤注一掷；全力以赴；破釜沉舟 | To risk everything on one single venture; to stake everything on one final effort. | He decided to go for broke and invest all his savings in the new business. 他决定孤注一掷，把所有积蓄都投资到新公司。| 近义词: risk it all, go all out, pull out all the stops, shoot the works | 反义词: play it safe, hold back, be cautious | idiom

go-for-broke:/ˌɡoʊ fər ˈbroʊk/ | adj. (informal) |  孤注一掷的；全力以赴的 | Characterized by risking everything on one venture. | It was a go-for-broke strategy, but it paid off in the end. 这是一个孤注一掷的策略，但最终得到了回报。 | 近义词: desperate, all-out | 反义词: cautious, conservative | adj.
