The word "effossion" doesn't exist in standard English dictionaries. It's likely a misspelling of "effusion."  Therefore, the following analysis is for "effusion":

effusion: /ɪˈfjuːʒən/ | n. | 流露；泻出；渗出物 | An instance of giving off something such as a liquid, light, or smell.  The expression of feelings in an unrestrained or heartfelt manner. |  The poet's work is characterized by effusions of romantic imagery. 这位诗人的作品的特点是充满了浪漫的意象。| synonyms: outpouring, discharge, emission, gushing, overflowing | antonyms: suppression, concealment, repression | noun
