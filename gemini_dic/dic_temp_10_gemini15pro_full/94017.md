impaneled:/ɪmˈpænəld/ | v. | 列入陪审团名单；组成(陪审团、委员会等) | to enroll and swear in (a jury); to select and constitute (a jury, committee, etc.) | The judge impaneled a jury of twelve members. 法官组成一个十二人的陪审团。 | The coroner impaneled a jury to investigate the death. 验尸官组成陪审团调查这起死亡事件。 |  Synonyms: enlist, enroll, swear in | Antonyms: discharge, dismiss | verb

impaneled:/ɪmˈpænəld/ | adj. | 被选入陪审团的 | enrolled as a member of a jury | The impaneled jury listened attentively to the evidence. 被选入陪审团的成员们认真听取证据。 | The impaneled jurors were sequestered during the trial. 被选入陪审团的陪审员在审判期间被隔离。 | Synonyms: sworn in, enrolled | Antonyms: dismissed, discharged | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *empaneller, from en- (“in, into”) + pannel (“panel, piece of parchment”).  The word originally referred to writing a jury list on a panel or parchment.
