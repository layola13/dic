adjudication: /əˌdʒuːdɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
裁决，判决，裁定
The formal act of judging a case, competition, or dispute, and making an official decision about it.
The adjudication of the competition took several days. 比赛的裁决花了几天时间。

The panel reached an adjudication after much deliberation.  评审团经过深思熟虑后做出了裁决。

近义词: judgment, decision, ruling, determination, verdict
反义词: indecision

词性: noun

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin adjudicatio(n-), from the verb adjudicare (see adjudicate).
