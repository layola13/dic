colloquialities: /kəˈloʊkwɪˈælɪtiz/ | n. | 口语；俗语；白话 |Informal expressions or phrases used in everyday conversation, often specific to a particular region or group. |  Example:  The book is full of colloquialities, making it sound authentic. 这本书充满了口语，听起来很地道。


Example:  Using colloquialities can make your writing more engaging.  使用口语可以让你的写作更引人入胜。


| 近义词: slang, idioms, jargon | 反义词: formal language, standard language | 词性: noun (plural) |


Etymology: Late 18th century. From colloquial + -ity + -es.  Colloquial comes from Latin colloquium "conversation"
