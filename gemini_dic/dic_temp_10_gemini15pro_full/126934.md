narnala:/nɑːrˈnɑːlə/ | n. | 纳尔纳拉, 澳洲原住民的撞木| A clapstick, a musical instrument of Aboriginal Australians, consisting of two pieces of wood struck together. | The elder played the narnala to accompany the ceremonial dance.  长老用撞木伴奏仪式舞蹈。| Synonym: clapstick, music stick | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific instrument) | noun

narnala:/nɑːrˈnɑːlə/ | n. | 撞击声，敲击声|The sound produced by striking two pieces of wood together, specifically referring to the sound of narnala clapsticks. | The rhythmic narnala echoed through the valley.  有节奏的撞击声回荡在山谷中。| Synonym: clapping, clacking, clicking | Antonym: silence, quiet | noun
