nonperpendicularity:/ˌnɒnpɜːrpɛndɪkjʊˈlærɪti/
n.|非垂直性；不垂直|The nonperpendicularity of the two lines caused a problem in the construction.  两条线的非垂直性给施工带来了问题。|
nonperpendicularity: lack of perpendicularity; not being at right angles.  非垂直性；不处于直角。|The architect noted the nonperpendicularity of the wall to the floor. 建筑师注意到墙壁与地板不垂直。|
近义词:non-orthogonality, obliqueness|反义词:perpendicularity, orthogonality|词性:noun
