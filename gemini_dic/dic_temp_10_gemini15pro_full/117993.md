memorialize: /məˈmɔːriəlaɪz/ /məˈmɔːriəlaɪz/
verb
纪念；将…永垂不朽
to preserve the memory of someone or something, as by a monument or ceremony
例句：
The town memorialized the fallen soldiers with a statue in the town square.
该镇在广场上竖立雕像以纪念阵亡的士兵。

They memorialized their beloved pet by planting a tree in the backyard.
他们通过在后院种一棵树来纪念他们心爱的宠物。

近义词：commemorate, celebrate, remember, honor
反义词：forget, ignore, neglect
词性：v.
