It produces feelings of euphoria, increased energy, and emotional warmth.  It is illegal in most countries.|MDMA is often associated with rave culture.  摇头丸通常与锐舞文化有关。|Synonyms: ecstasy, E, X, Molly|Antonyms: None (as it's a specific drug, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|noun
