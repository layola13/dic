inspectors:/ɪnˈspɛktərz/ | n. | 检查员，巡视员 |  People who are employed to examine something officially, often to ensure that regulations are being followed.  | The inspectors arrived unannounced to check the factory's safety procedures. 检查员们突击检查了工厂的安全程序。| synonyms: examiners, auditors, assessors, overseers | antonyms:  none (lack of inspection implies a lack of inspectors, but there isn't a direct opposite) | noun
