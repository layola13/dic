hematologist:/ˌhiːməˈtɒlədʒɪst (British), ˌheməˈtɑːlədʒɪst (American)/
n.
血液学家；血液病专家
A medical specialist who studies and treats diseases of the blood.
例句：The hematologist diagnosed her with leukemia. 血液学家诊断她患有白血病。

例句：My doctor referred me to a hematologist for further testing. 我的医生让我去看血液科医生做进一步检查。

近义词：blood specialist
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a medical specialist)
词性: noun
