misreporting:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrtɪŋ/
|n.| 误报，错误报道 |The misreporting of statistics can have serious consequences. 统计数据的误报可能会造成严重后果。|
| |The media's misreporting of the incident caused widespread panic.  媒体对这起事件的误报引发了广泛恐慌。|
| | 近义词: misrepresentation, falsification, distortion |
| | 反义词: accurate reporting, truthful reporting |


misreport:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrt/
|vt.| 误报，错误报道 |The journalist misreported the facts of the case.  这名记者错误地报道了案件的事实。|
| |He deliberately misreported his income to the tax authorities. 他故意向税务机关谎报收入。|
| | 近义词: misrepresent, falsify, distort |
| | 反义词: report accurately, report truthfully |


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + reporting (present participle of report, from Old French reporter "to carry back," from re- "back" + porter "to carry")
