freudianism: /ˈfrɔɪdiənɪzəm/ | n. | 弗洛伊德主义 |  The theories and doctrines of Sigmund Freud, especially those concerning the unconscious mind, the interpretation of dreams, and the role of sexuality in human behavior. |  His interest in dream analysis led him to a deep study of Freudianism. 他对梦的分析的兴趣使他对弗洛伊德主义进行了深入研究。|  synonyms: psychoanalysis, psychoanalytic theory | antonyms: behaviorism | noun
