holophytic:/ˌhɒləˈfɪtɪk/ | adj. | 全植物营养的，自养的 | Obtaining nourishment as green plants do, by synthesizing complex organic compounds from inorganic raw materials, using energy from light. | Example: Green plants are holophytic organisms, converting sunlight into energy. 绿色植物是自养生物，将阳光转化为能量。 | Example:  Holophytic nutrition is characteristic of algae and most higher plants. 全植物营养是藻类和大多数高等植物的特征。 | Synonym: autotrophic, photosynthetic | Antonym: heterotrophic, saprophytic | adjective

holozoic:/ˌhɒləˈzəʊɪk/ | adj. | 动物性营养的 | Obtaining nourishment by ingesting complex organic matter. | Example: Animals exhibit holozoic nutrition, consuming other organisms for sustenance. 动物表现出动物性营养，消耗其他生物来维持生命。 | Example: Holozoic organisms, unlike plants, cannot produce their own food. 与植物不同，动物性营养的生物不能自己生产食物。 | Synonym: heterotrophic | Antonym: autotrophic, holophytic | adjective
