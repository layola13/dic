in-nature-of:/ɪn ˈneɪtʃər əv/ |adj. phrase|具有...性质的；类似于...的|Having the characteristics of; similar to.  Relates to the essential qualities or characteristics of something, often implying a resemblance or approximation rather than a strict identity.|This payment is in the nature of a reward for his services. 这笔付款是对其服务的奖励。|Synonyms: akin to, analogous to, resembling, like, similar to |Antonyms: unlike, dissimilar to, different from|phrase
