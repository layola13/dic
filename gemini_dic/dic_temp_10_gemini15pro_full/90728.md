It was a humiliating experience to fall down the stairs in front of everyone. 在所有人面前从楼梯上摔下来是一次丢脸的经历。


| 近义词: embarrassing, degrading, mortifying, shameful | 反义词: uplifting, exalting, honoring | 词性: adjective
