brunches:/ˈbrʌntʃɪz/
n.
早午餐（brunch 的复数形式）
A meal that combines breakfast and lunch, typically eaten late in the morning.
We often have brunches on Sundays. 我们经常在星期天吃早午餐。
Brunch is served until 2 pm. 早午餐供应到下午2点。
近义词:late breakfast, midday meal
反义词:breakfast, lunch
词性:名词
