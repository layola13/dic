n.
自身免疫性疾病
Conditions or diseases caused by autoimmunity, where the body's immune system mistakenly attacks its own healthy tissues and organs.
Autoimmunities can range from mild to severe and affect various parts of the body.  自身免疫性疾病的程度从轻微到严重不等，并且会影响身体的各个部位。
Systemic lupus erythematosus and rheumatoid arthritis are examples of autoimmunities. 系统性红斑狼疮和类风湿性关节炎是自身免疫性疾病的例子。
近义词: autoimmune diseases, autoimmune disorders
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) immune tolerance, immune competence
词性: noun (plural)
