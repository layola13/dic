exterminable:/ɪkˈstɜːmɪnəbl/
adjective
可根除的，可消灭的
Capable of being exterminated; possible to destroy completely.
The exterminable pests were finally eliminated from the house.  这些可以根除的害虫最终从房子里被消灭了。
The government implemented a program to make the invasive species exterminable. 政府实施了一项计划，使入侵物种可以被根除。
近义词： eradicable, destroyable, killable
反义词： indestructible, ineradicable, immortal
词性：adjective

词源：From Late Latin *exterminabilis, from Latin exterminare "to drive beyond the boundaries, banish, expel, destroy," from ex- "out" + terminus "boundary, limit".
