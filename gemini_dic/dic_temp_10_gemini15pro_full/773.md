absolving: /əbˈzɒlvɪŋ/ /əbˈsɑːlvɪŋ/
verb
赦免，免除
Setting aside someone's guilt or responsibility.  Declaring someone free from blame or obligation.
The priest absolved him of his sins. = 神父赦免了他的罪过。

The court absolved her of any wrongdoing. = 法院免除了她的一切不法行为。

近义词: exonerating, acquitting, pardoning, forgiving
反义词: condemning, blaming, incriminating
词性: verb
