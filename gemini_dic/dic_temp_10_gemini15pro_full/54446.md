disinherit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/| vt.|剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|To prevent someone, usually a family member, from inheriting property.|He disinherited his son for marrying against his wishes. 他因为儿子违背他的意愿结婚而剥夺了他的继承权。|近义词：cut off, exclude|反义词: bequeath, endow|动词
