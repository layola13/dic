/ˌnɒnˈlæŋɡwɪdʒɪz/ |n.| 非语言; 非语言现象 | Systems of communication that do not involve spoken or written words, such as body language, gestures, and facial expressions. |  Nonverbal cues, like a firm handshake or direct eye contact, can be crucial in nonlanguages.  非语言暗示，例如坚定的握手或直接的眼神交流，在非语言交流中至关重要。 |  She's fluent in several nonlanguages, including sign language and body language. 她精通几种非语言，包括手语和肢体语言。 | synonyms: nonverbal communication, body language, kinesics | antonyms: language, verbal communication | noun
