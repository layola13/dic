pantalet:/ˈpæntəlɪt/ /ˈpæn.tə.let/| n. |女式衬裤，灯笼裤|Loose-fitting underpants or drawers worn by women and girls, typically gathered at the knee or calf.  Originally worn under crinolines and hoopskirts for modesty.|She wore pantalets under her skirt. 她裙子里面穿着衬裤。|近义词： drawers, bloomers, knickers |反义词: (No clear antonym)|noun
