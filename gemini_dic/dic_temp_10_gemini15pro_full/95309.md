incastellated:/ɪnˈkæsəleɪtɪd/
adjective
筑有城垛的；像城堡一样的
Fortified with or as if with a castle; resembling a castle.
The incastellated city walls provided a strong defense. 这座筑有城垛的城墙提供了坚固的防御。
The mansion was an incastellated structure, complete with towers and battlements. 这座大宅是一座像城堡一样的建筑，配有塔楼和城垛。
近义词: fortified, castellated, embattled
反义词: unfortified, undefended
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *incastellatus, past participle of *incastellare “to fortify with a castle,” from in- “in” + castellum “castle.”
