menacing:/ˈmɛnɪsɪŋ/| adj.|威胁的，险恶的|Suggesting the presence of danger; threatening.|The dog growled menacingly at the stranger. 狗对着陌生人发出威胁的低吼。|近义词：threatening, intimidating, ominous, forbidding|反义词：comforting, reassuring, welcoming|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *menacen*, from Anglo-Norman *menacer, manacer*,  from Late Latin *mināciāre* "to threaten," from Latin *minārī* "to threaten," from *minae* "threats."
