gentleship:/ˈdʒɛntlʃɪp/
n.
gentle quality; courtesy; nobility of character; kindness.
The quality of being gentle; refinement and courtesy of manners; nobility of character; kindness.
He treated everyone with gentleship and respect. 他待人温文尔雅，彬彬有礼。
近义词: gentility, courtesy, kindness, politeness, civility, refinement, grace
反义词: rudeness, coarseness, cruelty, harshness
词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *gentleschipe, *gentri, from Old French gentelise, from gentil 'noble, of gentle birth'. Ultimately from Latin gentilis 'of the same clan'.
