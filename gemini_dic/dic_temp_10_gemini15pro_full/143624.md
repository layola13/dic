outjinxing: /ˌaʊtˈdʒɪŋksiŋ/
|v.|智胜，在竞争中胜过|To surpass or defeat someone in a competition of wit or strategy, especially by anticipating and countering their moves.|例句：She outjinxed him by anticipating his every move and countering it effectively. 她预判了他的每一个动作并有效地反击，从而智胜了他。

|例句：In the complex world of international diplomacy, countries constantly try to outjinx each other. 在复杂的国际外交世界中，各国不断试图智胜彼此。|近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit|反义词：be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted, be outwitted|词性：动词
