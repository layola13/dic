lowborn:/ˈloʊbɔːrn/| adj.|出身低微的，卑贱的|Born into a family of low social status.  Of humble origins.|He was lowborn, but his talent propelled him to greatness. 他出身低微，但他的才能使他成就非凡。|近义词：baseborn, humble, plebeian|反义词：noble, highborn, aristocratic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English *lēaggeboren, from *lēage ("low") + geboren ("born").  The word has maintained its meaning throughout its history.
