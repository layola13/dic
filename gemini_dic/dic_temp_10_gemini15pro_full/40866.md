cookable:/ˈkʊkəbl/| adj.|可烹饪的，可煮的|Suitable for cooking; capable of being cooked.|例句：These potatoes are old but still cookable. 这些土豆老了，但仍然可以煮。|近义词：edible, culinary |反义词:inedible, uncookable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From cook + -able.  "cook" comes from Middle English *coken, from Old English *cocian "to cook," from Latin coquere. "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," from Old French -able and Latin -abilis.
