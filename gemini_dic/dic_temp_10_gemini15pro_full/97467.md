ingemination:/ɪnˌdʒɛmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |重复，反复；叠音；[植]芽接|The ingemination of consonants is a common feature of this language.  辅音重复是这门语言的一个共同特征。|例句：The poem uses ingemination to emphasize certain words. 这首诗使用叠音来强调某些词语。|例句：The ingemination of the initial consonant is a characteristic of this dialect. 首字母辅音的重复是这种方言的一个特点。|近义词：repetition, duplication, reduplication|反义词: simplification, abbreviation|词性:noun

词源: From Latin ingeminatio, from ingeminare "to double, redouble," from in- "in" + geminare "to twin, double," related to geminus "twin."
