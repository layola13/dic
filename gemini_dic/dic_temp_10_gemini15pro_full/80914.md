gowl:/ɡaʊl/
|vi. | 嚎叫；怒吼；（风）呼啸|To make a long, loud, mournful cry, like that of a dog or wolf, often expressing pain, anger, or loneliness. Also, to make a similar sound, as the wind. |The dog gowled at the intruder. 狗对着入侵者狂吠。

The wind gowled through the trees. 风在树林中呼啸。|近义词：howl, yell, shriek, roar|反义词:whisper, murmur|Verb
