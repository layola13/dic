outhowl:/ˌaʊtˈhaʊl/
verb
胜过…的叫喊声；比…叫得更响
To howl louder than.
The wolves outhowled the wind.  狼的嚎叫声盖过了风声。
Synonyms: outshout, outyell, drown out
Antonyms: be drowned out, be outshouted
词性: 动词
