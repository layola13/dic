honester:/ˈɒnɪstər/ (US) /ˈɒnɪstə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更诚实的|Comparative form of "honest" (though "more honest" is generally preferred).  It means more truthful or sincere. While grammatically correct, it sounds archaic or dialectical and "more honest" is typically used in modern English.|He claimed to be the honester of the two candidates. 他声称自己是两位候选人中更诚实的那个。|Synonyms: more honest, truthful, sincere, upright, honorable|Antonyms: more dishonest, deceitful, untruthful, corrupt, dishonorable|adjective
