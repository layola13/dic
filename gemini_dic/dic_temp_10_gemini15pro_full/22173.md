bombax:/ˈbɒmbæks/| n.|木棉树，木棉属|A genus of tropical trees of the family Bombacaceae (also now classified in the mallow family, Malvaceae), native to India, Southeast Asia, and northern Australia, and including the silk-cotton tree (Bombax ceiba).  It is cultivated in tropical and subtropical areas around the world.|The bombax tree is known for its beautiful red flowers. 木棉树以其美丽的红花而闻名。| The ancient bombax tree stood majestically in the center of the village. 古老的木棉树雄伟地矗立在村庄的中心。 |Synonyms: silk-cotton tree, kapok tree, red silk-cotton tree|Antonyms: N/A|noun
