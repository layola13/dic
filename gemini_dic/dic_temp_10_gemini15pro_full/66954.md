experientalist:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈmentəlɪst/
n.
经验主义者; 实验主义者
a person who emphasizes experience as the source of knowledge, especially in philosophy.  A person who uses or supports experimental methods.
John Dewey was a prominent American experientalist.  约翰·杜威是一位杰出的美国经验主义者。
The experientalist argued that knowledge comes from direct interaction with the world. 这位经验主义者认为，知识来自于与世界的直接互动。
Synonyms:empiricist, pragmatist, experimentalist
Antonyms:theorist, rationalist
