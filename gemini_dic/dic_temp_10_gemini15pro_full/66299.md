excrete:/ɪkˈskriːt/| v. | 排泄，分泌 | to pass out waste matter from the body, or to produce a liquid from a gland | The kidneys excrete urine. 肾脏排泄尿液。|  The skin excretes sweat. 皮肤分泌汗液。 | 近义词: expel, eliminate, discharge | 反义词: absorb, ingest | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin excrētus, past participle of excernere (“to sift out, separate”), from ex (“out”) + cernere (“to sift, separate”).
