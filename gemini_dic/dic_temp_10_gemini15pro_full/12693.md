assents:/əˈsɛnts/ | n. | 同意，赞成 |Agreement or consent.  The bill passed with the assents of both houses of Congress. |例句：The government assents to the use of the land for the project. 政府同意将这块土地用于该项目。

assents:/əˈsɛnts/ | v. | 同意，赞成|(third-person singular simple present assents, present participle assenting, simple past and past participle assented) To agree or express agreement. He assented to their request. |例句：She readily assented to his proposal. 她欣然同意了他的提议。

assent:/əˈsɛnt/ | n. | 同意，赞成 |Agreement or consent.  The bill passed with the assent of both houses of Congress. |例句：He gave his assent to the proposal. 他同意了这项提议。


近义词: agreement, consent, acceptance, approval | 反义词: dissent, disagreement, refusal, objection | 词性: noun (n.), verb (v.)
