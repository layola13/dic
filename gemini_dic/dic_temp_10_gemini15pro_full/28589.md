carefullest:/ˈkɛərfələst/ |adj.|最仔细的，最慎重的|This word is grammatically correct but rarely used.  It is the superlative form of "careful," although "most careful" is generally preferred.  It implies an extremely high degree of caution and attention to detail.| He was the carefullest driver I had ever seen, checking his mirrors every few seconds. 他是我见过最谨慎的司机，每隔几秒钟就检查一下后视镜。|She made the carefullest examination of the document before signing it.  她在签署文件之前对文件进行了最仔细的检查。|近义词: most careful, meticulous, scrupulous|反义词: careless, reckless, negligent|词性: adjective
