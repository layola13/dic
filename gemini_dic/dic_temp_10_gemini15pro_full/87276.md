hermaphroditically:/hɜːrmæfrəˈdɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 雌雄同体地，兼具两性特征地 |  Having or involving both male and female reproductive organs or characteristics. | Some species of fish reproduce hermaphroditically.  有些鱼类以雌雄同体的方式繁殖。 | Synonym:  bisexually, androgynously | Antonym: unisexually | Part of Speech: Adverb

hermaphroditic:/hɜːrmæfrəˈdɪtɪk/ | adj. | 雌雄同体的，两性的 | Possessing both male and female reproductive organs. | The earthworm is a hermaphroditic animal. 蚯蚓是一种雌雄同体的动物。| Synonym: bisexual, androgynous, monoecious | Antonym: unisexual, dioecious | Part of Speech: Adjective

hermaphrodite:/ˈhɜːrmæfrəˌdaɪt/ | n. | 雌雄同体，阴阳人 | A person, animal, or plant having both male and female reproductive organs. |  In Greek mythology, Hermaphroditus was a hermaphrodite. 在希腊神话中，赫马佛洛狄忒斯是一个雌雄同体。| Synonym: bisexual, androgynous | Antonym: unisexual | Part of Speech: Noun
