maases:/ˈmɑːsəs/| n. |施舍，善行（尤指犹太教的慈善捐款）| Acts of charity or donations, especially in a Jewish context.  This word is the plural of "ma'aseh," which is less commonly encountered in modern English. |Example: The maases of the community helped to support the less fortunate. 社区的施舍帮助了那些不幸的人。|Example:  His maases were known throughout the town. 他的善行全城皆知。 |Synonym: charities, donations, alms |Antonym: selfishness, greed |noun
