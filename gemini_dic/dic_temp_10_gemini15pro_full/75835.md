fumish:/ˈfjuːmɪʃ/ | adj. | 发霉的；有霉味的；闷浊的 | smelling or tasting stale or mouldy; musty | The air in the basement was damp and fumish. 地下室的空气潮湿而有霉味。| The old books had a fumish odor.  这些旧书有一股霉味。| 近义词:musty, moldy, stale, fusty | 反义词:fresh, airy, fragrant | 词性:形容词

词源:  From fume + -ish.  Ultimately from Latin *fūmus* (smoke).
