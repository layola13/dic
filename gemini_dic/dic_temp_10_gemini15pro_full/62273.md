encyclopedical:/ˌensaɪkləˈpiːdɪk(ə)l/ /ˌɛnsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 百科全书式的；知识广博的 | Covering a wide range of knowledge, like an encyclopedia.  Relating to or resembling an encyclopedia in style, scope, or comprehensiveness. |  His knowledge of history is encyclopedical.  他对历史的知识渊博。| comprehensive, exhaustive, wide-ranging | limited, narrow, specialized | adjective

Etymology:
From French *encyclopédique*, from *encyclopédie* (“encyclopedia”) + *-ique* (English *-ic*).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἐγκύκλιος* (*enkýklios*, “circular, recurring”) + *παιδεία* (*paideía*, “education, rearing of a child”).
