disinterestedness:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪdnəs/
n.
公正无私；不偏不倚
The state of being disinterested; impartiality; freedom from bias or self-interest.
The judge showed great disinterestedness in his handling of the case.  法官在处理此案时表现出极大的公正无私。
近义词: impartiality, neutrality, objectivity, fairness
反义词: bias, partiality, prejudice, selfishness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From dis- + interested + -ness.  Dis- is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  Interested comes from the Latin interesse, meaning "to be of importance or concern".  -ness is a suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality.
