infesters:/ɪnˈfɛstərz/
noun
侵扰者，感染者
Those who infest a place or thing, especially with pests or parasites.
The infesters were quickly eradicated from the building.  这些侵扰者很快就被从大楼里消灭了。
parasites, invaders, encroachers
protectors, guardians
