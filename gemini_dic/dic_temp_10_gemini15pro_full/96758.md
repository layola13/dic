inept:/ɪˈnɛpt/| adj.|笨拙的，无能的，不称职的|Having or showing no skill; clumsy.|He was completely inept at sports. 他完全不擅长运动。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, incompetent, unskilled, bungling|反义词：skillful, adept, competent, proficient|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *ineptus* "unfit, unsuitable," from *in-* "not" + *aptus* "fit, suitable."
