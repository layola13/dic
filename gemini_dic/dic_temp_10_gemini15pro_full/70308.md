fiducial:/fɪˈduːʃəl/ /fɪˈduːʃl/| adj. n.| 基准的，基准点；受托的，信托的；|Used as a fixed point of reference. fiducial:/fɪˈduːʃəl/ /fɪˈduːʃl/| n.| 基准点，参考点|A fixed point of reference.| The star served as a fiducial point for navigation. 这颗星是航行的参考点。|Synonyms: benchmark, datum, reference point|Antonyms: N/A|noun
