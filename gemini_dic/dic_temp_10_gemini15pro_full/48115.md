declivous:/dɪˈklaɪvəs/
adjective
倾斜的；下倾的
sloping downwards; descending
The declivous terrain made the hike challenging.  这倾斜的地形使得徒步旅行变得很有挑战性。
The road was declivous, leading down to the valley. 这条路是下坡的，通向山谷。
近义词:sloping, descending, inclined
反义词:acclivitous, ascending, rising
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *dēclīvis, from dē- (“down, away”) + clīvus (“slope”).
