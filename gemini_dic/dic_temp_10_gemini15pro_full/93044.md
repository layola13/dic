idling:/ˈaɪdlɪŋ/ | n. adj. v. | 空转，闲置，无所事事 | running an engine without engaging the gears; spending time doing nothing | The car was idling in the driveway. 汽车在车道上空转。| synonyms: loafing, dawdling, loitering | antonyms: working, active, busy | noun/adjective/verb

idling:/ˈaɪdlɪŋ/ | n. adj. v. | 空转，闲置，无所事事 | the act of running an engine while the vehicle is not moving; not working or active; wasting time |  He was idling away his time watching TV. 他闲着没事看电视。 | synonyms: inactive, inert, unoccupied | antonyms: working, productive, occupied | noun/adjective/verb
