lead-free:/ˌledˈfriː/| adj.|无铅的|Containing no lead.|This paint is lead-free and safe for children.  这种油漆是无铅的，对儿童安全。|leadless, unleaded|leaded|adjective
Etymology->
From *lead* (the metal) + *-free*.  The suffix *-free* means "without" or "not containing." The word became prominent with increasing awareness of the toxicity of lead, especially in paint and gasoline.
