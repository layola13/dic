entothorax:/ˈɛntoʊˌθɔːræks/| n. |内胸；腹内胸骨|The entothorax is a structure formed by the invagination of the ventral body wall in some arthropods, especially insects.  它是由一些节肢动物，特别是昆虫的腹壁内陷形成的结构。|The insect's entothorax plays a crucial role in supporting its internal organs.  昆虫的内胸对其支撑内脏器官起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:apodeme (sometimes)|反义词: (None)| noun
