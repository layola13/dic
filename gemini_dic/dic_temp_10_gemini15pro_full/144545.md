outvotes:/ˌaʊtˈvəʊts/
verb
以票数胜过，投票击败
defeats in a vote; gets more votes than
例句：The proposal outvoted the opposition by a large margin.  这项提案以很大的优势击败了反对意见。

例句：The mayor was outvoted by the city council on the new budget. 市长关于新预算的提议被市议会否决了。
近义词：defeats, overrules, overrides
反义词：is outvoted by, loses to
词性:v.
