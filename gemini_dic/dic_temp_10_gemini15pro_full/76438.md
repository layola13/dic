gainful:/ˈɡeɪnfʊl/| adj.|有利可图的；有报酬的|Providing a gain or profit; profitable.  Relating to or affording profitable employment.|He found gainful employment after graduation. 他毕业后找到了一份赚钱的工作。|近义词：profitable, lucrative, remunerative|反义词：unprofitable, unremunerative|词性:形容词
词源: From gain + -ful.  "Gain" comes from Old North French "gainer,"  meaning "to cultivate, till."
