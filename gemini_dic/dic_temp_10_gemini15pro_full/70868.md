finner:/ˈfɪnər/| n.|鱼鳍；发现者；（尤指鲸鱼的）鳍；|A person or thing that finds something, especially a whale's fin.  Someone who finds or discovers something, particularly something of value or interest.  A fin, especially of a whale.|例句：The finner spotted a pod of dolphins.  发现者发现了一群海豚。

例句：The whale's finner sliced through the water.  鲸鱼的鳍划破水面。|近义词：discoverer, finder, locator, spotter (for a person); fin (for a whale's fin)|反义词:loser (for a person - opposite of finder) |词性:noun
