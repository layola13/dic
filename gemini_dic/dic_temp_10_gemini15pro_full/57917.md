driller:/ˈdrɪlər/| n.|钻工，钻探机|A person or machine that drills.  A worker who operates a drill, especially in mining, oil extraction, or construction. |The driller carefully operated the machinery to extract the core sample. 钻工小心地操作机器提取岩心样本。|The driller hit a pocket of natural gas. 钻探机钻到了一处天然气囊。|近义词:operator, borer, miner (in specific contexts)|反义词:None (no direct opposite for a profession)|n.
