nosier:/ˈnɔɪziər/ (US), /ˈnɔɪzɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更爱管闲事的，更吵闹的|Comparative form of noisy: producing much noise or making much noise.|例句：The children are much nosier today than they were yesterday.  今天孩子们比昨天吵闹多了。

例句：My new neighbor is nosier than my old one. 我的新邻居比我以前的邻居更爱管闲事。|近义词：louder, more inquisitive, more curious|反义词:quieter, less inquisitive, less curious|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nosy, from nose + -y.  The comparative form is created by adding -er. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nasō.
