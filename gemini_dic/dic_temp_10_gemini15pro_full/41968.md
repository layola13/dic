cossacks:/ˈkɒsæks/ /ˈkɑːsæks/| n.|哥萨克人|A member of a people of southern Russia and Ukraine, noted for their horsemanship and military skill, traditionally forming an irregular cavalry.|The Cossacks were famed for their horsemanship. 哥萨克人以其骑术而闻名。|Cossack communities maintained a strong sense of independence. 哥萨克社群保持着强烈的独立意识。|Synonyms: horsemen, cavalrymen|Antonyms: civilians|Noun


cossack:/ˈkɒsæk/ /ˈkɑːsæk/| adj.|哥萨克的|Relating to or characteristic of the Cossacks.|A traditional Cossack dance. 一种传统的哥萨克舞蹈。|He wore a Cossack hat. 他戴着一顶哥萨克帽。|Synonyms:  Cossackian|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
