bourbonism:/ˈbɜːrbənɪzəm/ | n. | 波旁主义；波旁王朝的拥护者；保守主义 |  A conservative political philosophy associated with the Bourbon dynasties of France, emphasizing tradition, hierarchy, and centralized authority. It often implies a resistance to change and a preference for the established order. |  The resurgence of bourbonism after the revolution was short-lived. 革命后波旁主义的复兴是短暂的。|  Bourbonism is often seen as a reactionary force against progressive movements. 波旁主义通常被视为反对进步运动的反动力量。 | Synonyms: conservatism, traditionalism, reactionism | Antonyms: liberalism, progressivism, radicalism | noun
