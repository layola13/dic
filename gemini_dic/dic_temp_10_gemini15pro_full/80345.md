goldilocks economy:/ˈɡoʊldɪˌlɑːks ɪˈkɑːnəmi/ | n. |金发女孩经济|An economy that is not so hot that it causes inflation, and not so cold that it causes a recession, but is just right.|The US experienced a goldilocks economy in the late 1990s.  美国在20世纪90年代后期经历了金发女孩经济。|近义词:moderate growth economy, sustainable economy|反义词:recession, overheating economy|词性:名词

Etymology: From the children's story "Goldilocks and the Three Bears," in which Goldilocks finds a bowl of porridge that is "not too hot, not too cold, but just right."
