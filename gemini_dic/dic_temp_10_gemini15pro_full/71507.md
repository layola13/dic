flankerbacks:/ˈflæŋkərˌbæks/
n.
侧翼后卫（橄榄球）
Rugby players who play in the back row of the scrum, typically positioned behind the locks and acting as link players between the forwards and the backs.
The flankerbacks were instrumental in securing the win.  侧翼后卫在确保胜利方面发挥了重要作用。
The flankerbacks made a crucial tackle. 侧翼后卫完成了一次关键的擒抱。
Synonyms: flankers, back row
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider opposing positions like fly-half or centres)
