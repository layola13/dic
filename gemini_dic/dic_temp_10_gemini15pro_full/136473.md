nonrevoltingly:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvəʊl.tɪŋ.li/
adverb|不令人作呕地，不令人厌恶地|Not in a revolting manner; in a way that is not disgusting or offensive.|The food, while bland, was at least nonrevoltingly presented.  虽然食物清淡，但至少摆盘不令人反感。|She spoke of the gruesome details nonrevoltingly, focusing on the facts. 她平静地讲述了可怕的细节，专注于事实。|近义词:inoffensively, acceptably, palatably|反义词:revoltingly, disgustingly, nauseatingly|词性:adverb

Etymology:
non- + revoltingly
non-: prefix meaning "not"
revoltingly: adverb form of revolting, from Old French revolter "to rise in revolt," from Latin revolvere "to roll back." The sense of "disgusting" arose in the 19th century.
