This is the singular form, while "colobi" is technically the plural. However, "colobus" is often used as both singular and plural. | The colobus monkey swung effortlessly from branch to branch.  这只疣猴毫不费力地在树枝间荡来荡去。| 近义词:monkey, primate | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal name) | 词性:noun
