luncheonette:/ˌlʌnʃəˈnet/ | n. |小餐馆，快餐店|A small restaurant serving simple meals, especially lunch.  It often has a counter and stools.|例句：We grabbed a quick bite at the luncheonette before heading to the museum. 我们在去博物馆之前在小餐馆里匆匆吃了一点东西。

例句：The luncheonette was known for its delicious homemade pies. 这家小餐馆以其美味的自制馅饼而闻名。|近义词：cafe, diner, snack bar|反义词: restaurant (a more formal establishment)|词性: noun
