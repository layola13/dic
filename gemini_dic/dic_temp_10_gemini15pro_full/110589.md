limiting:/ˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/| adj.|限制的，限定的|Restricting the scope or extent of something.|例句：The limiting factor in the experiment was the amount of available sunlight.  实验中的限制因素是可用的阳光量。

例句：A limiting belief can prevent you from achieving your full potential.  一个限制性信念会阻碍你充分发挥你的潜力。|近义词：restrictive, constraining, confining|反义词：expansive, liberating, unlimited|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the verb "limit," which comes from the Old French "limite" and ultimately from the Latin "limitare," meaning "to bound, to limit."
