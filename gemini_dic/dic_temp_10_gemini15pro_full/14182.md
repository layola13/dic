auto-infective:/ˌɔːtəʊɪnˈfɛktɪv/
adjective
自身感染的；自体感染的
Capable of reinfecting oneself.  The infection spreads from one part of the body to another part of the same body.
The patient's wound became auto-infective, leading to a worsening of his condition. 病人的伤口发生了自身感染，导致病情恶化。

The doctor explained that the rash was auto-infective and could spread if not treated properly. 医生解释说皮疹是自身感染的，如果不及时治疗会扩散。

近义词: self-infecting
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "non-contagious" in certain contexts if referring to the initial source of infection.)
词性: adjective
