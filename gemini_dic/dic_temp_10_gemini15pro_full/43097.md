cowwheat:/ˈkaʊwiːt/| n.|牛小麦，一种小麦品种|A type of wheat traditionally used as fodder for cows, but now increasingly grown for human consumption due to its high protein content.  It is also known as "bread wheat". | Cowwheat is becoming a popular alternative to traditional wheat flour. 牛小麦正在成为传统小麦粉的流行替代品。| Cowwheat is now being used in some artisan breads.  牛小麦现在被用于一些手工面包中。 |近义词：wheat, bread wheat|反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially other grain types like rye or barley depending on context) |noun
