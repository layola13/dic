bilabiate:/baɪˈleɪbiət/| adj.|双唇形的，二唇形的|Having two lips, especially of a corolla or calyx, one upper and one lower.|The bilabiate flowers of the snapdragon are distinctive. 金鱼草的双唇形花朵很特别。|近义词:two-lipped|反义词:|adjective
