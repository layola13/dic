litigate:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪt/| v. |提起诉讼，打官司|to take a claim or dispute to a court of law|例句：The company decided to litigate the matter rather than settle out of court.  该公司决定提起诉讼，而不是庭外和解。

例句：They are litigating for compensation for injuries suffered at work. 他们正在提起诉讼，要求赔偿因工受伤的损失。|近义词：sue, contest, dispute|反义词:settle, arbitrate|词性:动词

litigated:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtɪd/| adj. |已提起诉讼的，已 litigate 的|having been the subject of a lawsuit|例句：The litigated case dragged on for years.  这个已提起诉讼的案件拖延了数年。

例句：This is a highly litigated area of law. 这是一个诉讼非常多的法律领域。|近义词：contested, disputed, in dispute|反义词:settled, resolved|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *litigatus*, past participle of litigare (“to dispute, quarrel”), from lis (“lawsuit”) + agere (“to do, drive, act”).
