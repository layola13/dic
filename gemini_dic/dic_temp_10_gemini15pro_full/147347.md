overmystification: /ˌoʊvərˌmɪstɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
| n. | 神秘化；故弄玄虚 | The act of making something unnecessarily mysterious or complicated. | The magician's overmystification of simple tricks made the audience lose interest. 魔术师对简单的戏法故弄玄虚，使观众失去了兴趣。 |  mystification, obfuscation | clarification, simplification | noun
