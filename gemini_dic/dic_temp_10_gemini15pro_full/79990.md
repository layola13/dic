go-getting:/ˈɡəʊˌɡetɪŋ/
adjective
积极进取的；有事业心的；努力争取成功的
Energetic and ambitious, especially in pursuing success; aggressively enterprising.
She's a real go-getting businesswoman. 她是一位真正积极进取的女商人。
近义词: ambitious, driven, enterprising, resourceful, aggressive, dynamic
反义词: lazy, passive, unmotivated, apathetic
词性: adjective

go-getting:/ˈɡəʊˌɡetɪŋ/
noun
积极进取；努力争取成功
The act or process of being energetic and ambitious, especially in pursuing success.
Her go-getting attitude helped her climb the corporate ladder quickly. 她积极进取的态度帮助她迅速晋升。
近义词: ambition, drive, enterprise, resourcefulness
反义词: laziness, passivity, apathy
词性: noun


词源:  "Go-getting" is a compound word formed from the verb "go" and the present participle of the verb "get."  The "go" emphasizes action and energy, while "getting" implies the pursuit and acquisition of something, in this context, success.  It likely emerged in American English in the late 19th or early 20th century, reflecting the growing emphasis on individual achievement and ambition during that period.
