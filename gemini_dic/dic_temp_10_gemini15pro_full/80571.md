gooeynesses:/ˈɡuːinɪsɪz/
n.
黏糊糊的东西；黏稠状态
The state or quality of being gooey; stickiness.
The marshmallow topping had a delightful gooeyness. 棉花糖的顶层有一种令人愉悦的黏糊糊的感觉。
近义词：stickiness, gumminess, tackiness
反义词：dryness, firmness
词性: 名词

词源:
