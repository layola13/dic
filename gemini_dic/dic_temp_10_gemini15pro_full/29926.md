cavalierly: /ˌkævəˈlɪəli/ | adv. | 傲慢地，轻蔑地 | Showing a lack of proper concern; offhand. | He cavalierly dismissed their objections. 他傲慢地驳回了他们的反对意见。 | Synonym: arrogantly, disdainfully, haughtily, superciliously | Antonym: respectfully, humbly, carefully, cautiously | 词性: 副词

cavalier: /ˌkævəˈlɪər/ | n. | 骑士，武士；(对女性)殷勤的男人 |  A supporter of King Charles I in the English Civil War.  A man who is gallant and attentive to women. |  He behaved like a true cavalier, opening doors and offering compliments. 他表现得像个真正的绅士，开门并说着赞美的话。 | Synonym: horseman, knight, gallant | Antonym: roundhead, churl, boor | 词性: 名词
