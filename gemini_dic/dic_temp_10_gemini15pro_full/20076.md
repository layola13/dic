biorhythmicities:/ˌbaɪoʊˈrɪðmɪsɪtiz/
|n.|生物节律|The cyclical variations in physiological processes over time, such as sleep-wake cycles, hormone levels, and body temperature.  These rhythms can influence a person's physical, emotional, and intellectual states.|例句：The study of biorhythmicities can help us understand how our bodies function.  生物节律的研究可以帮助我们理解我们的身体是如何运作的。
