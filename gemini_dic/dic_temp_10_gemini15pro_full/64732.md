eschewment:/ɪsˈtʃuːmənt/
n.
戒绝，避免
the act of eschewing something; deliberate avoidance
His eschewal of alcohol stemmed from a troubled past. 他对酒精的戒绝源于一段不堪回首的过去。
近义词：avoidance, abstaining, refraining, shunning
反义词：indulgence, acceptance, embrace
词性: 名词
