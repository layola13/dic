nonteachers:/ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃərz/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃəz/ (UK)
n.
非教师；非教学人员
people who are not teachers; those who do not work in the profession of teaching.
The nonteachers at the school, such as the administrative staff and custodians, also play a vital role.  学校的非教师人员，例如行政人员和管理员，也发挥着至关重要的作用。
The conference was attended by both teachers and nonteachers.  教师和非教师都参加了这次会议。
synonyms: non-educators, non-faculty, support staff
antonyms: teachers, educators, faculty
noun
