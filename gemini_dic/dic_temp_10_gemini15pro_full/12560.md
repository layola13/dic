aspire:/əˈspaɪər/ /æˈspaɪər/| v. |渴望，立志，追求|to have a strong desire to achieve something high or great|She aspired to a career in medicine. 她渴望从事医学事业。|He aspired to be a writer. 他立志成为一名作家。|近义词：desire, yearn, strive, aim, ambition|反义词:resign, give up|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *aspirare* "to breathe upon, blow upon," from *ad-* "to, toward" + *spirare* "to breathe."  The figurative sense of "to strive for something" arose from the notion of "breathing after" or "panting for" something.
