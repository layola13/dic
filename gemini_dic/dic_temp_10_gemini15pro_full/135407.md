nonperpendicular:/ˌnɒnˌpɜːrpənˈdɪkjʊlər/ | adj. | 不垂直的，非垂直的 | Not perpendicular; not at a right angle. | The two lines are nonperpendicular, intersecting at an acute angle.  这两条线不垂直，相交成一个锐角。

The wall and the floor are nonperpendicular, creating a slanted surface.  墙和地板不垂直，形成了一个倾斜的表面。| near synonyms: oblique, askew, slanted, inclined | antonyms: perpendicular, orthogonal, vertical, upright | adjective
