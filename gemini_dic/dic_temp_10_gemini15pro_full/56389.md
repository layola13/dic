dole:/doʊl/| n. vt.|施舍，发放救济；失业救济金；悲哀，忧伤|A portion or allotment of money, food, or other necessities given as charity or relief to the needy;  A share or portion, especially one given out sparingly;  Grief or sorrow.|例句：The government provided a dole to the unemployed. 政府向失业者提供救济金。|近义词：alms, charity, handout, allowance, grief, sorrow|反义词：abundance, fortune, joy, happiness|词性:名词,动词

dole:/doʊl/| vt.|发放，施舍|To distribute sparingly or as charity.|例句：She doled out the soup to the hungry children. 她把汤分发给饥饿的孩子们。|近义词：distribute, dispense, allot, apportion|反义词：hoard, withhold|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *dāl (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *dailą (“portion, share”), from Proto-Indo-European *dail- (“to divide, share”). Cognate with Old Saxon dēl (“part”), Old Frisian del (“part”), Dutch deel (“part”), Old High German teil (“part”), German Teil (“part”), Old Norse deild (“share, portion”), Gothic dails (“part”).
