miswires:/ˌmɪsˈwaɪərz/
verb
接错线；错误连接
to wire something incorrectly
The electrician miswired the house and caused a short circuit.  电工把房子接错了线，导致了短路。
The new stereo system was miswired, and the speakers wouldn't work. 新的音响系统接线错误，扬声器无法工作。
misconnect, misarrange
connect correctly, wire correctly
v.
