mellowspeak:/ˈmɛloʊspiːk/
adjective
轻声细语；温柔的说话方式；和缓的语气
Speaking in a soft, gentle, and relaxed tone; a way of speaking that is calm and soothing.
She used her mellowspeak to calm the frightened child. 她用轻柔的语气安慰受惊的孩子。
近义词:soft-spoken, gentle, soothing, calm, peaceful
反义词:harsh, loud, aggressive, strident, abrasive
词性:adjective


mellowspeak:/ˈmɛləʊspiːk/
adjective
轻声细语；温柔的说话方式；和缓的语气
Speaking in a soft, gentle, and relaxed tone; a way of speaking that is calm and soothing.
He delivered the bad news in a mellowspeak, trying not to upset anyone. 他用温和的语气 menyampaikan berita buruk itu, 尽量不让人心烦意乱。
近义词:soft-spoken, gentle, soothing, calm, peaceful
反义词:harsh, loud, aggressive, strident, abrasive
词性:adjective


词源:  A blend of "mellow" (meaning soft, gentle, relaxed) and "speak."  "Mellow" ultimately comes from Old English "mearu," meaning soft or tender.
