gallantly:/ˈɡæləntli/| adv.|勇敢地；英勇地；豪迈地|In a brave, spirited, or noble manner.  Showing courage and a chivalrous regard for women.|He gallantly offered her his coat. 他豪爽地把外套给了她。|近义词：bravely, courageously, heroically, chivalrously, nobly|反义词：cowardly, timidly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *galantment, from galant (“gallant”). Ultimately from Frankish *galant (“valiant”).
