divan:/daɪˈvæn/ | n. | 长沙发，沙发床；会议室，议事厅 | A long low sofa without a back or arms, typically placed against a wall. A council chamber or meeting room, especially in some Muslim countries. |例句：She stretched out on the divan, relaxing after a long day. 她伸展四肢躺在沙发上，在漫长的一天后放松身心。

例句：The divan met to discuss matters of state. 议事厅召开会议讨论国家大事。 | 近义词：sofa, couch, settee, bench | 反义词: chair (arguably, as divans are a type of seat, but generally offer a more reclined and less formal seating posture) | 词性: noun
