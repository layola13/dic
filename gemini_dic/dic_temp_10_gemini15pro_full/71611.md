flatboat:/ˈflætboʊt/| n.|平底船|A large, flat-bottomed boat used for transporting freight on rivers, especially in the 18th and 19th centuries in the United States.|例句：The flatboat slowly drifted down the Mississippi River. 平底船缓缓地沿着密西西比河漂流而下。

例句：Flatboats were a common sight on the Ohio River during westward expansion. 西进扩张时期，平底船在俄亥俄河上很常见。|近义词：barge, scow|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly a speedboat or a ship designed for ocean travel)|词性:noun
