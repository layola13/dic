bedecking:/bɪˈdɛkɪŋ/
verb
装饰，修饰
adorning or decorating something richly or elaborately
The streets were bedecked with flags for the holiday. 节日里街道上装饰着旗帜。
近义词: adorning, decorating, ornamenting, embellishing, garnishing
反义词: stripping, denuding
词性: verb (present participle of bedeck)


bedecked:/bɪˈdɛkt/
adjective
装饰的，修饰的
decorated or adorned, typically richly or elaborately
The bedecked Christmas tree sparkled with lights. 装饰华丽的圣诞树闪耀着灯光。
近义词: adorned, decorated, ornamented, embellished, garnished
反义词: bare, plain, unadorned
词性: adjective (past participle of bedeck)
