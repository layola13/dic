oppositifolious:/ˌɒpəzɪtɪˈfəʊliəs/
adjective

Having leaves arranged in pairs on opposite sides of the stem.
The plant's oppositifolious arrangement of leaves is a key identifying feature.  这种植物对生的叶片排列是其关键识别特征。

反义词: alternate, spiral, whorled
词性: adjective
