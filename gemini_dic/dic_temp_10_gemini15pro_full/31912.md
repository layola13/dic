cheater:/ˈtʃiːtər/| n.|骗子，作弊者|A person who acts dishonestly in order to gain an advantage, especially in a game, a competition, or an examination.|He was caught cheating on the test and expelled from school. 他考试作弊被抓，被学校开除了。|Synonyms: fraud, deceiver, trickster|Antonyms: honest person, fair player|noun
