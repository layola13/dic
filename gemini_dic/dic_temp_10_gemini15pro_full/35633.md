coatings:/ˈkoʊtɪŋz/
noun
涂层，覆盖层
A thin layer or covering spread over a surface.
The car has several coatings of paint to protect it from rust.  这辆车有几层油漆涂层以防止生锈。

The bridge has a special coating to prevent corrosion.  这座桥有一种特殊的涂层来防止腐蚀。

近义词:layers, films, finishes
反义词:substrate, base

词性:n. (noun)
