The museum displayed a beautiful lumachel containing various fossilized seashells.  博物馆展出了一块美丽的贝壳化石石灰岩，里面包含各种各样的贝壳化石。 | 近义词: shell marble, fire marble | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps granite or other non-shell-based stone could be considered contextually opposite) | 词性: noun
