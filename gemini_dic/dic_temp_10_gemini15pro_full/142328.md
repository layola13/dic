orthoepical:/ˌɔːrθəˈɛpɪkl/ | adj. | 正音法的，发音正确的 | Relating to the study of correct pronunciation. |  She gave an orthoepical explanation of the word's pronunciation. 她对这个单词的发音作了一个正音法的解释。|  The dictionary provides orthoepical guidance for learners. 这本词典为学习者提供了发音指导。 | 近义词: phonetic, pronunciational | 反义词: mispronounced, incorrect | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *orthoepeia*, from *orthos* "correct" + *epos* "word".
