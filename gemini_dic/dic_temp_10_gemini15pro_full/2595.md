adnascent:/ædˈnæsənt/| adj.| 生长的，萌芽的，初生的|Growing or budding; nascent.  Referring specifically to something growing on or near something else.|The adnascent buds on the stem promised a profusion of flowers. 茎上初生的芽苞预示着花朵将盛开。|近义词：nascent, budding, incipient, developing|反义词：mature, developed, full-grown|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ad- (“to, towards”) + nascens (“being born”), present participle of nasci (“to be born”).

USAGE->
Attributive usage:
The adnascent leaves unfurled slowly. = 初生的叶子慢慢展开。
Adnascent tumors can be difficult to detect. = 生长中的肿瘤可能难以检测。

Predicative usage (less common):
The growth appeared adnascent. = 生长看起来是初生的。
The new branch is adnascent to the main stem. = 新枝生长在主茎上。


PHRASE->
adnascent buds = 初生的芽
adnascent leaves = 初生的叶子
adnascent growth = 初生的生长
adnascent tissue = 初生组织
adnascent tumor = 生长中的肿瘤
