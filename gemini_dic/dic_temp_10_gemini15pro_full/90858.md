hungers:/ˈhʌŋɡərz/| n.|渴望，欲望，强烈愿望|A strong desire or craving, especially for food.|The refugees arrived with hungers gnawing at their bellies. 难民们到达时，饥饿感在啃噬着他们的肚子。|Synonyms: cravings, yearnings, longings, desires|Antonyms: satisfaction, satiety|noun


hungers:/ˈhʌŋɡərz/| v.|渴望，渴求|To have a strong desire or craving for something.|He hungers for recognition and success. 他渴望得到认可和成功。|Synonyms: craves, yearns, longs, desires|Antonyms:  is satisfied, is content|verb


Etymology: From Old English *hungrian, from Proto-Germanic *hungrijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *kenk- (“to long for”).
