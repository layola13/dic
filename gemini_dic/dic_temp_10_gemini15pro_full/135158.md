nonoperable:/nɒnˈɒpərəbl/ /nɑːnˈɑːpərəbl/
adjective
不能运作的；无法手术的
Not able to be operated or used; (of a medical condition) too advanced or serious to be improved by surgery.
The equipment is nonoperable due to lack of maintenance.  由于缺乏维护，该设备无法运行。
The tumor was declared nonoperable.  肿瘤被宣布为无法手术。
近义词: inoperable, unusable, dysfunctional
反义词: operable, functional, working
词性: adjective
