differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt/ | v. | 区分，区别，使不同 |  distinguishes between two or more things; makes different; causes a difference between |  He can differentiate between a robin and a sparrow. 他能区分知更鸟和麻雀。| synonyms: distinguishes, separates, contrasts, discriminates | antonyms: equates, assimilates, conflates, combines | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin differentiatus, past participle of differentiare "to make different," from Latin different-, differens, present participle of differre "to carry different ways, differ," from dis- "apart, in different directions" + ferre "to carry."
