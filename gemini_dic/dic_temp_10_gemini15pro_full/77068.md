garnishing:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪŋ/| n.| 装饰物，配菜 |Something used to decorate or embellish, especially food. | The chef added a garnishing of parsley to the dish.  厨师在菜肴上加了欧芹作为装饰。 | synonyms: decoration, embellishment, adornment, trimming | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

garnishing:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃɪŋ/| n.| 扣押(工资)|  The legal act of seizing a portion of someone's wages to satisfy a debt. |  His wages are being garnished to pay back taxes. 他的工资被扣押以偿还税款。| synonyms: seizure, attachment, sequestration | antonyms: release, repayment | noun
