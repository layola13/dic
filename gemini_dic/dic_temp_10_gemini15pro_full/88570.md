hitching:/ˈhɪtʃɪŋ/ | n. v. | 搭便车，拴住，套住; 搭便车旅行 | The act of hitching a ride or fastening something with a hitch. |  He got across the country by hitching. 他搭便车穿越了全国。| synonyms: fastening, securing, tethering, hooking, attaching | antonyms: detaching, unfastening, releasing | noun/verb

hitching:/ˈhɪtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 蹒跚而行，跛行| To walk with a limp or jerky movement. | The old man was hitching down the street. 老人一瘸一拐地走在街上。| synonyms: limping, hobbling, shuffling | antonyms: striding, walking smoothly | verb
