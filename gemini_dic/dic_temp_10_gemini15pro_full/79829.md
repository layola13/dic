glycyrrhizin:/ˌɡlɪsəˈraɪzɪn/ | n. |甘草甜素|A sweet-tasting crystalline glycoside C₄₂H₆₂O₁₆ obtained from licorice root and used as a flavoring and masking agent especially for pharmaceuticals and as an emulsifier and gel-forming agent.|Glycyrrhizin is used in some candies and medicines. 甘草甜素用于一些糖果和药物中。|Synonyms: licorice extract, liquorice extract|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
