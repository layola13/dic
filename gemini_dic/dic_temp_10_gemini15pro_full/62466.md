endoscopies:/ɛnˈdɒskəpiz/ |n.| 内窥镜检查 |The procedure involves inserting a thin, flexible tube with a camera attached to its end into the body to examine the internal organs.  这个过程包括将一根末端连接摄像头的细而灵活的管子插入体内以检查内部器官。| Endoscopies are often used to diagnose and treat digestive problems. 内窥镜检查常用于诊断和治疗消化问题。| 近义词: examinations, procedures | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Modern Latin endoscopia, from Ancient Greek ἔνδον (éndon, “within”) + σκοπέω (skopéō, “I observe, examine”).
