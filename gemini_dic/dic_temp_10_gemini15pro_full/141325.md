openest:/ˈoʊpənɪst/ | adj. | 最开放的 |  Superlative form of "open," meaning most willing to consider new ideas, most accessible, or most frank and honest. |  She has the openest mind of anyone I know. 她是我认识的人中最思想开放的。| Synonyms: most receptive, most broad-minded, most unguarded, most frank | Antonyms: most closed-minded, most narrow-minded, most secretive, most guarded | Word Type: Adjective
