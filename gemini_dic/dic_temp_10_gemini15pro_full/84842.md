hang-together:/hæŋ təˈɡɛðər/
|vi.|一致，连贯，符合逻辑|To be logical and consistent; to form a united whole.|The argument doesn't hang together.  这个论点不连贯。|
| |The different parts of the story don't hang together. 故事的不同部分不连贯。|近义词：cohere, connect, agree|反义词:conflict, disagree, contradict|词性:动词短语

hang-together:/hæŋ təˈɡɛðər/
|n.|连贯性，一致性|The state of being logical and consistent.|The hang-together of his explanation was questionable. 他的解释的连贯性值得怀疑。|
| |The essay lacked hang-together. 这篇文章缺乏连贯性。|近义词：coherence, consistency, unity|反义词:incoherence, inconsistency, disunity|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "hang" + "together," meaning literally "to hold together."  源自动词“hang”（悬挂）和“together”（一起），字面意思是“保持在一起”。
