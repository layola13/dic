dynamism:/ˈdaɪnəmɪzəm/| n.|活力，精力，劲头；动态，动向|The dynamism of the city is attracting young people. 这座城市的活力吸引着年轻人。|例句：The dynamism of the market is evident in the constantly fluctuating prices.  市场的活力体现在不断波动的价格中。

例句：She brought a new dynamism to the organization. 她为该组织注入了新的活力。|近义词：energy, vigor, vitality, drive, spirit|反义词：lethargy, inertia, stagnation|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
