barege: /bəˈrɑːʒ/ /bəˈrɑːʒ/ | n. | 薄纱; 巴里纱; (用薄纱做的)女服; |A thin, gauzy fabric, typically of silk or wool, used for women's clothing.| She wore a delicate barege dress to the summer party. 她穿着一件精致的薄纱连衣裙参加夏季派对。| Near synonyms: gauze, chiffon, gossamer | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) heavy fabric | Noun

Etymology: From French *barège*, named after Barèges, a town in the Pyrenees mountains where the fabric was originally made.
