gumlike:/ˈɡʌmlaɪk/| adj.|像口香糖的，胶状的|Resembling gum in texture or consistency; sticky.|The candy had a gumlike texture that stuck to my teeth.  这颗糖果有一种像口香糖一样的质地，粘在我的牙齿上。|近义词：sticky, gummy, adhesive, resinous|反义词：dry, brittle, smooth|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "gum" + "-like". "Gum" originates from the Old English "gōma," referring to the palate or gums, and later to gummy substances.  "-like" is a common suffix denoting resemblance.
