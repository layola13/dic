orthocarbonic:/ˌɔːrθəʊkɑːrˈbɒnɪk/ | adj. |原碳的; 正碳的| Relating to or containing carbon with four bonds.|  Hypothetical orthocarbonic acid is unstable and readily decomposes into carbonic acid and water. 假想的原碳酸不稳定，很容易分解成碳酸和水。|近义词: tetravalent carbon (describing carbon with four bonds) |反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

orthocarbonic:/ˌɔːrθəʊkɑːrˈbɒnɪk/ | n. |原碳酸| A hypothetical acid with the formula H₄CO₄. | Orthocarbonic acid, though theoretically possible, has never been synthesized. 原碳酸虽然理论上可能存在，但从未被合成过。|近义词: methanetetrol (another name for the same hypothetical compound)|反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ortho- + carbonic.  "Ortho-" is a Greek prefix meaning "straight," "true," or "correct," often used in chemistry to denote the most hydrated form of an acid. "Carbonic" refers to compounds containing carbon.
