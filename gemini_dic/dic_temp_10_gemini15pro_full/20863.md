blatherskite: /ˈblæðəskaɪt/ (US), /ˈblæðəskaɪt/ (UK) | n. |  饶舌之人，废话连篇的人 | A person who talks at length without making much sense; a foolish, talkative person. | He's a blatherskite who never seems to stop talking, but rarely says anything of substance. 他是个饶舌的人，似乎从不说话，但很少说有实质内容的话。

My uncle is such a blatherskite; he talked for an hour about his garden gnomes. 我叔叔真是个饶舌的人；他谈论他的花园侏儒谈了一个小时。

| 近义词: windbag, chatterbox, gossipmonger | 反义词: reticent person, quiet person | 词性: noun
