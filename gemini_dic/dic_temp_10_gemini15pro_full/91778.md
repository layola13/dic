hyperesthesia:/ˌhaɪpərɪsˈθiːʒə/ /ˌhaɪpərɪsˈθiːziə/ | n. |感觉过敏，感觉异常|Increased sensitivity to sensory stimuli such as touch, pain, or sound.| 例句：She experienced hyperesthesia after the surgery, finding even light touches painful. 她手术后出现感觉过敏，即使是轻微的触摸也会感到疼痛。

例句：The patient's hyperesthesia made it difficult for him to tolerate clothing. 病人的感觉过敏使他难以忍受穿衣服。|近义词：hypersensitivity, allodynia|反义词：hypoesthesia, anesthesia|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, above”) + αἴσθησις (aísthēsis, “sensation, feeling”).
