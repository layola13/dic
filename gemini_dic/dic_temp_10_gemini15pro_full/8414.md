anti-theist:/ˈæntiˌθiːɪst/ | n. | 反神论者；反对有神论者 | Someone who is actively opposed to theism; one who believes that theism is harmful. |  He's not just an atheist; he's an active anti-theist, campaigning against religious influence in government. 他不仅仅是一个无神论者；他是一个积极的反神论者，反对宗教在政府中的影响力。

| Example:  Her anti-theist views often led to heated debates with her religious family. 她的反神论观点经常导致与她信教的家人发生激烈的争论。 | Synonym:  militant atheist, strong atheist | Antonym:  theist, devout believer | Noun
