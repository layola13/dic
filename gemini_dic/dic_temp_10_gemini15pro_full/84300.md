half-volleyer:/ˌhɑːfˈvɒliər/ | n. | 半截截击；半高球截击 | A shot in tennis or other racket sports where the ball is hit just after it bounces, before it rises to its full height. |  Federer played a superb half-volley to win the point. 费德勒打出一个精彩的半截截击赢得了这一分。 | 近义词: drop shot, volley | 反义词: lob | 词性: 名词

half-volley:/ˌhɑːfˈvɒliər/ | vt. | 半截截击（动词） | To hit (a ball) as a half-volley. |  He half-volleyed the ball back over the net. 他半截截击把球打回了网。 | 近义词: volley | 反义词: lob | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "volley," with "volley" ultimately from the Old French "volée," meaning a flight.
