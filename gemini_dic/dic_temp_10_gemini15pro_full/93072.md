idolizes:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
崇拜；把…视为偶像
regards with blind adoration; worships
She idolizes her older brother. 她崇拜她的哥哥。
近义词:worships, adores, reveres, venerates
反义词:scorns, despises, loathes
词性:v.

Etymology: From idol + -ize.  Ultimately from Greek eidolon "image, phantom, idol" + -ize.
