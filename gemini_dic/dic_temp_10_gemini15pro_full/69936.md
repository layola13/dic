fescue:/ˈfɛskjuː/
n.
羊茅；羊茅属植物
a type of grass often used for lawns or pastures.
The golf course was planted with a new variety of fescue.  高尔夫球场种植了一种新品种的羊茅。

Etymology->
From Old French *festu "straw, stalk," from Latin festuca "straw, stalk."
