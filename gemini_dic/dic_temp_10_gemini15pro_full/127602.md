needfully:/ˈniːdfəli/| adv.|必要地；必须地|In a way that is necessary.  It implies that something is done only if required or essential.|例句：The matter will be dealt with needfully. 这件事将得到必要的处理。|近义词：necessarily, essentially, indispensably|反义词: unnecessarily, optionally|词性:副词
词源: From "needful" + "-ly." "Needful" comes from Old English "neodfull," meaning "necessary," from "neod" (need) + "-full" (full).
