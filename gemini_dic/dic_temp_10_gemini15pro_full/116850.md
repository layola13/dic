maximising:/ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzɪŋ/
verb
最大化；充分利用
the act of increasing something as much as possible
The company is focused on maximising profits. 公司致力于利润最大化。
近义词:optimizing, increasing, amplifying, boosting, enhancing
反义词:minimizing, reducing, decreasing, lessening
词性:present participle of maximize
