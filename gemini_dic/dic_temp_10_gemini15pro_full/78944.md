girdingly:/ˈɡɜːrdɪŋli/
adverb
准备地，警惕地
In a way that prepares or strengthens someone for something challenging.  Getting ready for action, often with a sense of determination or resolve.
He girdingly fastened his sword belt, preparing for the upcoming battle. 他警惕地系紧剑带，准备迎接即将到来的战斗。
The team girdingly reviewed their strategy, knowing the competition would be fierce.  团队认真地回顾了他们的策略，知道竞争会很激烈。
近义词: resolutely, determinedly, purposefully, decisively
反义词: hesitantly, reluctantly, halfheartedly
词性: adverb

词源: From the verb "gird," meaning to encircle or bind with a belt or band, often in preparation for action.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gurdijaną.
