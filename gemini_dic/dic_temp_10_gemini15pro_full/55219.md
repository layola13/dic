noun
分离，断绝，分裂
The disseverance of the two countries led to years of conflict.  这两个国家的分裂导致了多年的冲突。
The disseverance of church and state is a fundamental principle of modern democracies. 政教分离是现代民主的基本原则。
近义词：separation, division, rupture, split, disjunction
反义词：union, connection, junction, unification
词性：n.

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French disseverance, based on Latin disseverare, from dis- ‘apart’ + severare ‘to separate’.
