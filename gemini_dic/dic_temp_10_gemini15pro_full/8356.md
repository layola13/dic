anti-populisms:/ˌæntiˈpɒpjʊlɪzəmz/
n.
反民粹主义
opposition to populism; ideologies or movements that counter populist sentiment.
The conference explored the rise of anti-populisms across the globe.  这次会议探讨了全球反民粹主义的兴起。
The book analyzes the intellectual roots of various anti-populisms. 这本书分析了各种反民粹主义的知识根源。
近义词: anti-demagoguery, counter-populism
反义词: populism, demagoguery
词性: noun
