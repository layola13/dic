clashingly:/ˈklæʃɪŋli/
adverb
冲突地，不协调地
in a clashing manner; jarringly.  Describes something that clashes, often in terms of color, style, or personality.
Her clashingly bright outfit stood out in the crowd. 她那身冲突鲜艳的服装在人群中格外显眼。
The two styles clashed clashingly. 这两种风格极其不协调。
近义词: jarringly, discordantly, disharmoniously
反义词: harmoniously, congruously, matching
词性: adverb

clash:/klæʃ/
noun
冲突，撞击声
a loud, harsh, metallic sound. Also, a conflict or disagreement.
The clash of cymbals marked the end of the piece.  钹的撞击声标志着乐曲的结束。
There was a clash of personalities between the two leaders. 两位领导人之间发生了性格冲突。
近义词: conflict, collision, disagreement, clang, bang
反义词: agreement, harmony, concord
词性: noun

clash:/klæʃ/
verb
冲突，抵触
to come into conflict or disagreement; to make a loud, harsh, metallic sound.
Their political views clashed with ours. 他们的政治观点与我们的相冲突。
The swords clashed as the knights fought. 骑士们搏斗时，剑 clashing 在一起。
近义词: conflict, collide, disagree, clang, bang
反义词: agree, harmonize, concord
词性: verb
