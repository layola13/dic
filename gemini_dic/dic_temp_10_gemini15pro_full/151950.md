pastoralism:/ˈpɑːstərəlɪzəm/ |n.| 田园主义；畜牧业；牧歌体 |The practice of keeping livestock, especially sheep or cattle, or the lifestyle associated with this.  Also refers to literature or art that portrays an idealized view of rural life.|  Many nomadic cultures practice pastoralism, moving their herds with the seasons. 许多游牧文化都实行畜牧业，随着季节迁徙他们的牲畜群。|  nomadism, herding, shepherding | agriculture, farming | noun
