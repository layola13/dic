ahistoricisms: /ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəmz/
| n. | 非历史性，非史实性 |  Interpretations of the past that ignore or distort historical context, often to promote a particular agenda.  Lack of historical perspective or awareness.  |  The movie was full of ahistoricisms, portraying medieval knights with automatic weapons. 这部电影充满了非历史性，描绘了拿着自动武器的中世纪骑士。  The politician's speech was riddled with ahistoricisms that misrepresented the country's past.  这位政治家的演讲充斥着歪曲国家历史的非史实性。 | 近义词：inaccuracies, distortions, misrepresentations, anachronisms | 反义词：historical accuracy, historicity | 词性：名词

Etymology: a- (not) + historicism + -s (plural)  From "historicism,"  meaning "strong consciousness of and attention to the historical background of events or circumstances,"  with the prefix "a-" indicating negation.
