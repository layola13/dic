microcephalic: /ˌmaɪkrəʊsɪˈfælɪk/ (UK) /ˌmaɪkroʊsəˈfælɪk/ (US)| adj. | 小头的，头小畸形的 | Having an abnormally small head and underdeveloped brain. |  The child was diagnosed as microcephalic soon after birth.  这个孩子出生后不久就被诊断为小头畸形。 | Synonyms: small-headed | Antonyms: macrocephalic | adjective

microcephaly: /ˌmaɪkrəʊˈsɛfəli/ (UK) /ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɛfəli/ (US)| n. | 小头畸形 | A condition in which the head is abnormally small, usually because the brain has not developed properly. | Microcephaly can be caused by genetic factors or infections during pregnancy. 小头畸形可能是由遗传因素或怀孕期间的感染引起的。 | Synonyms: small-headedness | Antonyms: macrocephaly | noun
