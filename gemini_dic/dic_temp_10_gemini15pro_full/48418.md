deducer:/dɪˈduːsər/ |n.| 推断者；演绎者 |Someone who deduces, or reasons from the general to the particular.| Sherlock Holmes was a brilliant deducer. 夏洛克·福尔摩斯是一位杰出的推断者。|Synonyms: reasoner, inferrer, logician|Antonyms:  inducer,  guesser|noun

deduce:/dɪˈduːs/ |v.| 推断；演绎 |To reach a conclusion from something known; to infer.|  We can deduce from the evidence that he is guilty.  我们可以从证据推断出他有罪。|Synonyms: infer, conclude, gather|Antonyms:  speculate, guess|verb
