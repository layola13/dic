aberrancies: /ˌæbəˈreɪnsiz/
noun
异常，偏差，反常现象
Deviations or departures from the normal or expected course.
The report detailed the financial aberrancies that led to the company's downfall.  这份报告详细说明了导致公司倒闭的财务异常情况。

The study revealed some statistical aberrancies in the data. 这项研究揭示了数据中的一些统计异常。

synonym: anomalies, deviations, irregularities, abnormalities
antonym: normalities, regularities, standards
词性: 名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin aberrans, present participle of aberrare "to stray," from ab- "off, away from" + errare "to wander, err."
