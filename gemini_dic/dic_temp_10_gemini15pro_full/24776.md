broomstick:/ˈbruːmstɪk/ | n. |扫帚柄；飞天扫帚|The handle of a broom, traditionally made of wood.  Often associated with witches and magic. |She grabbed her broomstick and swept the floor. 她抓起扫帚柄扫地。| The witch flew off on her broomstick. 女巫骑着她的飞天扫帚飞走了。 |近义词: broom handle |反义词: None |词性: noun

PHRASE->
jump on the broomstick: 结婚 (informal, often humorous) They jumped on the broomstick last weekend. 他们上周末结婚了。
get off your broomstick: 少管闲事; 别生气 (informal) Get off your broomstick, it's none of your business! 少管闲事，这不关你的事！
ride one's broomstick: 骑着扫帚飞, 指的是女巫的活动 She claims to be able to ride her broomstick. 她声称自己会骑扫帚飞。
a witch on a broomstick: 一个骑着扫帚的女巫 Children dressed up as witches on broomsticks for Halloween. 孩子们在万圣节打扮成骑着扫帚的女巫。
broomstick wedding: 跳扫帚婚礼，一种非正式的婚礼形式 They had a broomstick wedding in their backyard. 他们在后院举行了跳扫帚婚礼。
