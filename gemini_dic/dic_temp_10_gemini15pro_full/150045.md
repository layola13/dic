palpiger:/ˈpælpɪdʒər/| n.|触须器，触肢|A sensory appendage, especially of an insect or arachnid, used for touch, taste, or smell.  It's often found near the mouthparts.|The palpiger of the spider helped it detect the vibrations of its prey. 蜘蛛的触肢帮助它探测猎物的震动。|Near synonyms: palpus, feeler, antenna|Antonym: N/A (lack of a specific antonym)|noun
