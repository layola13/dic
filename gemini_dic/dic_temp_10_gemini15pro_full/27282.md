calumnious: /kəˈlʌmniəs/
adjective
诽谤的；中伤的
making false and defamatory statements about someone
例句：The newspaper published a calumnious article about the politician. 这家报纸发表了一篇关于这位政治家的诽谤文章。

例句：He spread calumnious rumors about his rival. 他散布了关于他对手的诽谤谣言。


近义词：defamatory, libelous, slanderous, false, malicious, untrue, mendacious
反义词：laudatory, complimentary, praising
词性：adjective
