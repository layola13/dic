biodiversities:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈvɜːrsɪtiz/| n. |生物多样性|The variety of life in the world or in a particular habitat or ecosystem.|例句：Protecting biodiversities is crucial for the health of our planet. 保护生物多样性对我们星球的健康至关重要。

例句：The Amazon rainforest is known for its incredible biodiversities. 亚马逊雨林以其令人难以置信的生物多样性而闻名。|近义词：variety, richness, |反义词: uniformity, homogeneity | noun
