busyworks:/ˈbɪziwɜːrks/ | n. | 忙碌的工作，琐事 |  Refers to tasks that keep someone occupied, often implying they are trivial or unproductive.  It can also suggest a flurry of activity, possibly without a clear purpose or significant outcome. | He's always involved in a lot of busyworks, but I'm not sure how much he actually achieves. 他总是忙于各种琐事，但我不确定他实际上完成了多少。| synonyms: chores, errands, trivialities, fuss | antonyms: leisure, relaxation, downtime | noun
