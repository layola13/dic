nonreliance:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈlaɪ.əns/
n.
nonreliance, lack of reliance, independence
The state of not relying on something or someone.
The company's nonreliance on government funding is a point of pride. 公司不依赖政府资金是一件值得骄傲的事情。
independence, self-sufficiency, autonomy
dependence, reliance
noun
