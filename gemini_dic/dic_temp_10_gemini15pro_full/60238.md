effront:/ɪˈfrʌnt/| n. |厚颜无耻；放肆|An act of shameless audacity; impudence. |He had the effrontery to ask for a raise after only a week on the job. 他竟然只工作了一个星期就要求加薪，真是厚颜无耻。|近义词：audacity, impudence, nerve, cheek, boldness, temerity|反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence, shyness|词性:名词

effront:/ɪˈfrʌnt/| vt. |冒犯；轻蔑；公然蔑视|To insult openly; to treat with contempt. |She effronted the judge by rolling her eyes during his questioning.  她在法官提问时翻白眼，冒犯了法官。|近义词：insult, offend, affront, slight, disrespect|反义词：respect, honor, venerate|词性:动词
