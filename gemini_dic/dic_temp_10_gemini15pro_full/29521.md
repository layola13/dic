catatonic:/ˌkætəˈtɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 紧张症的；僵直的 | Relating to or characterized by catatonia, a state of apparent unresponsiveness to external stimuli in a person who is apparently awake.  |  The patient remained catatonic, unresponsive to any external stimuli.  病人一直处于紧张症状态，对外界刺激没有任何反应。 | Synonyms: stuporous, unresponsive, uncommunicative, immobile, rigid | Antonyms: responsive, alert, active, mobile | adjective
