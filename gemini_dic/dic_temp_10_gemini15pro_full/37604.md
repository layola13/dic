communicational:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənəl/
adjective

Relating to communication; of or pertaining to communication.
The class focused on developing students' communicational skills. 这门课专注于培养学生的沟通技巧。
communicative, interpersonal
uncommunicative
