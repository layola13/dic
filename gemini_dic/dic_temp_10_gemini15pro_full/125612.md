multivitamin:/ˌmʌltiˈvaɪtəmɪn/ | n. |多种维生素，复合维生素|A tablet or preparation containing a mixture of vitamins, typically those considered essential for health. |例句：She takes a multivitamin every day to supplement her diet. 她每天服用多种维生素来补充她的饮食。

例句：The doctor recommended a multivitamin for his overall health. 医生建议他服用复合维生素来改善整体健康。|近义词：vitamin supplement, vitamin pill |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) vitamin deficiency | 词性: noun
