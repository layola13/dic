nonmonists:/ˌnɒnˈmɒnɪsts/ | n. |非一元论者| People who do not subscribe to monism, a philosophical view that a variety of existing things can be explained in terms of a single reality or substance.| Nonmonists believe that the mind and body are distinct entities. 非一元论者认为身心是不同的实体。| pluralists, dualists | monists | noun
