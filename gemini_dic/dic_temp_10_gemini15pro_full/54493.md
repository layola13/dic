disinure:/ˌdɪsɪˈnjʊər/ /ˌdɪsɪˈnʊr/ | vt. | 使习惯于（不愉快或危险的事物）；使不再害怕；使麻木 | To accustom to something unpleasant or dangerous; to make no longer sensitive or afraid. |  The soldiers were disinured to violence. 这些士兵对暴力已经麻木了。|  Years of living in poverty had disinured her to hardship. 多年的贫困生活使她对艰苦习以为常。 | 近义词: harden, inure, accustom, habituate | 反义词: sensitize | 词性: 动词
