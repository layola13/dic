barenecked:/ˌbɛərˈnɛkt/| adj.|赤裸脖子的，露颈的|Having a bare neck; not covered by clothing or feathers.|The barenecked chickens huddled together for warmth. 赤裸脖子的鸡挤在一起取暖。|近义词：exposed, uncovered|反义词：covered, clothed|词性:形容词
Etymology: bare + necked.  "bare" from Old English "bær" (naked, open). "necked" from Old English "hnecca" (neck).
