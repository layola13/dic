demonstrable:/dɪˈmɒnstrəbl/ (US) /dɪˈmɑːnstrəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可证明的；可论证的；明显的|Clearly apparent or capable of being logically proved.|Her ability is demonstrable by her rapid progress. 她的能力从她快速的进步中可以得到证明。|The benefits of exercise are readily demonstrable. 运动的好处很容易证明。|近义词：provable, evident, apparent, obvious, clear|反义词：unprovable, questionable, dubious|词性:形容词

demonstrably:/dɪˈmɒnstrəbli/ (US) /dɪˈmɑːnstrəbli/ (UK)| adv.|可证明地；明显地|In a way that is clearly apparent or capable of being logically proved.|He is demonstrably innocent.  他显然是无辜的。|The new policy is demonstrably unfair. 新政策明显不公平。|近义词：clearly, evidently, obviously, apparently|反义词：questionably, dubiously|词性:副词
