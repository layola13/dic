enforcements:/ɪnˈfɔːrsmənts/
n.
执行,强制,实施
The act of enforcing; compelling observance of or compliance with a law, rule, or obligation.
The police are responsible for the enforcement of the law. 警察负责执法。

The government announced stricter enforcements of immigration laws. 政府宣布更严格地执行移民法。


近义词:implementation, application, execution
反义词:neglect, disregard, violation
词性:名词
