condemnors:/kənˈdemərz/
n.
谴责者，责难者
Those who express strong disapproval or pronounce judgment against something or someone.
The condemnors of the new policy were very vocal.  新政策的谴责者们表达了强烈的不满。
Critics, detractors, censurers
Supporters, advocates, approvers
noun
