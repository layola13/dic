dissyllabify:/dɪˈsɪləbɪfaɪ/ /daɪˈsɪləbɪfaɪ/| v. |将…分成两个音节|To divide (a word) into two syllables.|He dissyllabified the word "poem" as "po-em." 他把“poem”这个词分成两个音节“po”和“em”。|Example: The teacher asked the students to dissyllabify the word "banana." 老师让学生们把“banana”这个词分成音节。

Example:  She dissyllabified "window" into "win" and "dow". 她把“window”分成“win”和“dow”两个音节。|近义词: divide into syllables|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:动词
