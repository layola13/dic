ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek name Ἀλέξανδρος (Alexandros), composed of ἀλέξω (alexo) meaning "to defend, ward off" and ἀνήρ (aner), ἀνδρός (andros) meaning "man".  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *aleks- ("to ward off, protect").
