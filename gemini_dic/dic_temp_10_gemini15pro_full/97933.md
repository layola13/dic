innov:/ˈɪnəv/ | prefix |  创新，革新 |  A prefix meaning "new," "innovative," or "introducing something new."  Derived from the Latin word "novus" meaning "new." |  Example: Innovate (v.) - to introduce something new; make changes in anything established. | 近义词: neo- | 反义词: paleo- | 词性: prefix
