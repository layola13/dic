nonranging:/nɒnˈreɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 非漫游的，不四处觅食的 | Not ranging or roaming; staying within a limited area or territory. | The nonranging animals in the zoo seemed content in their enclosures. 动物园里不四处觅食的动物似乎对它们的圈舍很满意。| The nonranging sheep grazed peacefully in the pasture. 不四处觅食的羊群在牧场上平静地吃草。 | 近义词: sedentary, restricted, confined | 反义词: ranging, roaming, migratory | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "ranging" (present participle of "range," meaning "to roam or wander freely").  Ultimately from Old French "renger" (to set in a row).
