imports:/ˈɪmpɔːrts/ (US), /ˈɪmpɔːts/ (UK)| n.|进口; 进口商品; 进口额|Goods or services bought into one country from another.|The US imports a lot of oil from other countries. 美国从其他国家进口大量石油。|The company's imports have increased this year.  这家公司的进口额今年有所增加。|synonyms:  inward shipments, foreign goods|antonyms: exports|noun

imports:/ɪmˈpɔːrts/ (US), /ɪmˈpɔːts/ (UK)| v. (imports, importing, imported)|进口|To bring (goods or services) into a country from abroad for sale.|The US imports coffee from Colombia. 美国从哥伦比亚进口咖啡。|They import exotic fruits from all over the world.  他们从世界各地进口异国水果。|synonyms: bring in, ship in|antonyms: export, ship out|verb
