nonsedentary:/nɒnˈsɛd(ə)nt(ə)ri/ | adj. | 非久坐的，活跃的 | Not sedentary; characterized by or requiring physical activity. |  Example: A nonsedentary lifestyle is important for maintaining good health.  非久坐的生活方式对保持健康很重要。

Example:  She enjoys a nonsedentary job that keeps her moving all day. 她喜欢一份让她整天活动不停的非久坐的工作。| 近义词: active, mobile, energetic | 反义词: sedentary, inactive, immobile | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sedentary" (from Latin "sedentarius" meaning "sitting").
