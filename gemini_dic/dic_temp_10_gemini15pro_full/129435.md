nomothetic:/ˌnɒməˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 法则性的，普遍适用的 | Relating to or involving the study of general scientific laws or principles, rather than individual cases. |  Nomothetic research seeks to establish general laws that apply to all people. 法则性研究试图建立适用于所有人的普遍规律。

Nomothetic approaches are contrasted with idiographic approaches, which focus on the unique aspects of individuals. 法则性方法与个体方法形成对比，后者侧重于个体的独特方面。 | 近义词: general, universal, law-based | 反义词: idiographic, individual, particular | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *nomothetes*  "lawgiver," from *nomos* "law" + *thetes* "one who establishes."
