extemporizes:/ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
即兴创作；即兴表演
to perform or speak without preparation or notes
He extemporized a speech at the wedding. 他在婚礼上即兴发表了讲话。
improvises, ad-libs, wings it
prepares, plans, rehearses
词性: 动词
