afflictive:/əˈflɪktɪv/| adj.|痛苦的，折磨人的|Causing pain or suffering, both mentally or physically.  Relates to affliction, something that causes pain or suffering.|The afflictive memories of the war haunted him.  战争痛苦的记忆萦绕着他。|Synonyms: distressing, painful, tormenting, agonizing|Antonyms: soothing, comforting, pleasant, relieving|adjective
