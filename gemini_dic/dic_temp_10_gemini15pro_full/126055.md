mustangs: /ˈmʌstæŋz/ | n. | 野马（尤指北美西部平原上的）|  A small, strong horse of the American West that descended from horses brought to America by the Spanish. |  The mustangs roamed free on the open range.  野马在开阔的牧场上自由漫游。 | synonyms: wild horses, broncos | antonyms: domesticated horses | noun
