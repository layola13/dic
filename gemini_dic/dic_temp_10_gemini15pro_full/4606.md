alcyonaria:/ˌælsiəˈneəriə/
n.
软珊瑚目；八放珊瑚亚纲
An order of corals, subclass Octocorallia, that includes the sea fans, sea pens, and soft corals.
Alcyonaria are known for their vibrant colors and diverse shapes. 软珊瑚目以其鲜艳的色彩和多样的形状而闻名。
Many species of Alcyonaria are found in tropical reefs. 许多软珊瑚目物种都发现于热带珊瑚礁。
Synonym: Octocorallia
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with hard corals - Scleractinia)
