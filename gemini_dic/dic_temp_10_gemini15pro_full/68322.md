fall-away:/ˈfɔːl əˌweɪ/
adjective
逐渐减少的，逐渐消失的，倾斜的
sloping downward; decreasing gradually.
The graph shows a fall-away in sales during the summer months. 图表显示夏季月份销售额逐渐下降。
近义词:declining, diminishing, dwindling, receding
反义词:increasing, rising, growing, escalating
词性:adjective

fall-away:/ˈfɔːl əˌweɪ/
noun
背叛者，脱党者，变节者
a person who abandons a religion, cause, or party.
He was considered a fall-away by his former comrades.  他被以前的同志们视为背叛者。
近义词:defector, deserter, renegade, apostate
反义词:loyalist, adherent, supporter
词性:noun

词源:  A compound of "fall" and "away," suggesting a movement of falling or declining away from something.
