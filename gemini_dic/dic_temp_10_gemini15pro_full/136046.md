nonrebellious:/ˌnɑːnriˈbɛljəs/
adjective
非叛逆的；顺从的
Not rebellious; obedient and conforming to rules or authority.
Her nonrebellious nature made her a favorite with the teachers. 她顺从的性格使她成为老师们的宠儿。
synonyms: obedient, compliant, docile, submissive, tractable
antonyms: rebellious, defiant, disobedient, unruly, insubordinate
词性: 形容词
