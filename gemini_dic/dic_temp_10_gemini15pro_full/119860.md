milesian:/maɪˈliːʒən, -ʃən/
adjective
米利都的；米利都学派的；[哲]米利都学派的
Of or relating to Miletus, an ancient Greek city in Ionia (Asia Minor). Also, relating to the philosophical school founded there.
例句：
Thales was a Milesian philosopher.
泰勒斯是一位米利都哲学家。

The Milesian school of thought focused on finding the fundamental principle of reality.
米利都学派致力于寻找现实的基本原理。

近义词：Ionian, pre-Socratic
反义词：(No specific antonym)
词性：adjective
