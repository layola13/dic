inroad:/ˈɪnˌroʊd/| n. |入侵，侵蚀，进展|an advance or penetration, often at the expense of something else, especially into someone else's territory or sphere of activity|The company made significant inroads into the European market.  这家公司在欧洲市场取得了重大进展。

The new highway construction made inroads into the forest. 新公路的建设侵占了森林。|近义词：encroachment, intrusion, advance, penetration|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|词性:名词
