crayfishes:/ˈkreɪfɪʃɪz/
noun
小龙虾
Small freshwater crustaceans resembling lobsters, with pincers.
Crayfishes are a popular food in some parts of the world. 小龙虾在世界某些地方是一种受欢迎的食物。
Crayfishes were plentiful in the stream. 小溪里有很多小龙虾。
synonyms: crawfish, crawdads, freshwater lobsters
antonyms: (no direct antonym, could consider land animals)
