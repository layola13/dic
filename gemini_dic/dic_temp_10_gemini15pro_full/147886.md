overreadinesses:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛdinɪsɪz/
|n.|过度准备；过分 готовность|The athlete's overreadinesses for the competition led to anxiety and ultimately a poor performance.  这位运动员对比赛的过度准备导致了焦虑，最终表现不佳。|
|英文解析：The plural form of "overreadiness," referring to multiple instances of being excessively or prematurely prepared.  It implies a state of being too eager or anxious, often to the point of detriment.|
|例句：His overreadinesses manifested in packing three suitcases for a weekend trip. 他过度准备的表现是为周末旅行打包了三个行李箱。

|例句：Her overreadinesses for the presentation were evident in her constant rehearsing and fidgeting. 她对演讲的过度准备体现在她不断地排练和坐立不安。
|近义词：over-preparedness, excessive preparation, premature readiness|
|反义词：under-preparedness, lack of preparation, unreadiness|
|词性：Noun (Plural)|
