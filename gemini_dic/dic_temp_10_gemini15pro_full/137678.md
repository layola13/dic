nonuniversal:/ˌnɒnˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəl/ | adj. | 非普遍的，非通用的 | Not applicable to or existing in all cases or instances; not general. |  This phenomenon is nonuniversal and only occurs under specific conditions. 这种现象并非普遍存在，只在特定条件下发生。 | Examples: The theory has nonuniversal applicability.  这个理论的适用性并非普遍。 | Synonyms: particular, specific, restricted, limited, local, special | Antonyms: universal, general, global, common, widespread | adjective
