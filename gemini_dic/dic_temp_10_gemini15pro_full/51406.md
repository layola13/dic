determinist:/dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪst/| n.|决定论者|A person who believes in determinism, the philosophical doctrine that all events, including human actions, are ultimately determined by causes external to the will.|例句：The determinist argued that free will is an illusion.  决定论者认为自由意志是一种幻觉。

例句：She considered herself a determinist, believing all actions were predetermined. 她认为自己是一个决定论者，相信所有行为都是预先确定的。|近义词：fatalist, necessitarian|反义词：indeterminist, libertarian|noun
