The nontransportabilities of certain medical equipment pose challenges for remote areas. 某些医疗设备的不可运输性给偏远地区带来了挑战。 | 近义词: immobility, fixedness, unmovability | 反义词: portability, transportability | 词性: 名词 (Plural)

词源:
non- (prefix): not
transport (verb): to carry from one place to another
-able (suffix): capable of being
-ity (suffix): state or quality of being
