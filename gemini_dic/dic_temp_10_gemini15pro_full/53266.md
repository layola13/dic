dirgelike: /ˈdɜːrdʒlaɪk/ | adj. | 如丧歌般的，悲哀的 | Resembling a dirge; mournful and slow. | The procession moved at a dirgelike pace.  队伍以极其缓慢的速度前进。| synonyms: mournful, somber, doleful, funereal, lamenting | antonyms: joyful, cheerful, upbeat, lively, celebratory | adjective

Etymology: From "dirge" + "-like".  "Dirge" comes from the Latin "dirige, Domine, Deus meus" ("Direct, O Lord, my God"), part of the Office for the Dead.
