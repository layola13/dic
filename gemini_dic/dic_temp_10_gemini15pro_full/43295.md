crafty: /ˈkræfti/ (US), /ˈkrɑːfti/ (UK) | adj. | 狡猾的，灵巧的 | Skilled in deception or trickery; cleverly resourceful. |  He's a crafty old fox, always getting what he wants. 他是个狡猾的老狐狸，总是能得到他想要的东西。

She made a crafty excuse to avoid going to the meeting. 她编了个巧妙的借口不去参加会议。

近义词: cunning, sly, wily, artful, ingenious, clever, shrewd | 反义词: honest, straightforward, naive, artless, clumsy | 词性: adjective

craftily: /ˈkræftɪli/ (US), /ˈkrɑːftɪli/ (UK) | adv. | 狡猾地，灵巧地 | In a crafty manner; with skill and cunning. | The thief craftily picked the lock. 小偷巧妙地打开了锁。

He craftily avoided answering the question. 他巧妙地避开了这个问题。

近义词: cunningly, slyly, artfully | 反义词: honestly, straightforwardly, artlessly | 词性: adverb

craftiness: /ˈkræftinɪs/ (US), /ˈkrɑːftinɪs/ (UK) | n. | 狡猾，灵巧 | The quality of being crafty; skill in deception or trickery. | His craftiness allowed him to win the negotiation. 他的狡猾使他赢得了谈判。

The craftiness of the plan was impressive. 这个计划的精巧令人印象深刻。

近义词: cunning, slyness, wiliness, artfulness | 反义词: honesty, straightforwardness, naiveté, artlessness | 词性: noun


词源: From Middle English *crafti, from craft + -y. Ultimately from Old English cræft (“strength, skill, power”).
