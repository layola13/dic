circumbendibus:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈbɛndɪbəs/ | n. | 迂回曲折的说法; 绕圈子 | An indirect and lengthy way of expressing something. |  He told us about his journey in a long and weary circumbendibus. 他用冗长而乏味的迂回说法讲述了他的旅程。

The lawyer avoided a direct answer and spoke in circumbendibus. 律师避免直接回答，而是拐弯抹角地说。

| 近义词： circumlocution, periphrasis, roundabout way, indirectness | 反义词: directness, straightforwardness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *circumbendibus*, a form of *circumbens*, from *circum* (around) + *bend* from PIE *bhendh* (to bind, tie).  Originally a humorous pseudo-Latin plural.
