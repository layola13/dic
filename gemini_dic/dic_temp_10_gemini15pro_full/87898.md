hierarchism:/ˈhaɪərɑːrkɪzəm/
n.|等级主义，等级制度|A belief in or support for hierarchical principles and structures; a system characterized by hierarchy.|The company's strict hierarchism stifled creativity and innovation.  这家公司严格的等级制度扼杀了创造力和创新。|hierarchy, stratification|egalitarianism|noun
