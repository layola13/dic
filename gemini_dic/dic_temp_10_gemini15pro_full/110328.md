lightering:/ˈlaɪtərɪŋ/
n.
减载，驳运
The act of transferring part of a cargo from a larger vessel to a smaller one, typically a barge or lighter, to reduce the vessel's draft.
The ship underwent lightering operations before entering the shallow harbor.  这艘船在进入浅港前进行了减载作业。
transshipment, offloading
loading
