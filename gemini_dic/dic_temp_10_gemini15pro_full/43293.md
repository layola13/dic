craftworkers:/ˈkrɑːftˌwɜːrkərz/ (US), /ˈkrɑːftˌwɜːkəz/ (UK)| n.|手工艺者，工艺工人|People who make things skillfully with their hands, especially in a traditional way.  Skilled workers in a particular craft.|例句：The craftworkers of Murano are famous for their glassblowing skills.  穆拉诺的手工艺者以其吹制玻璃的技艺而闻名。

例句：The market showcased a variety of goods made by local craftworkers.  市场展示了当地手工艺者制作的各种商品。|近义词：artisans, craftspeople, tradespeople|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider unskilled laborers or factory workers)|词性:noun
