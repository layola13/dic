mollycoddle:/ˈmɒlɪkɒdl/ (UK), /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdl/ (US)| v. n.|娇惯；溺爱；娇生惯养的人|To treat someone very indulgently; pamper.  A pampered person.|He mollycoddled his only child. 他溺爱他唯一的孩子。|近义词：pamper, coddle, spoil, indulge|反义词:neglect, discipline|词性：verb, noun

mollycoddle:/ˈmɒlɪkɒdl/ (UK), /ˈmɑːliˌkɑːdl/ (US)| n.|娇生惯养的人|A pampered person.|He's a mollycoddle who can't handle any hardship. 他是个娇生惯养的人，一点苦都吃不了。|近义词：softy, weakling|反义词:tough guy, stalwart|词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Molly (a girl's name, used generically) + coddle (to treat indulgently).  Implies treating someone like a pampered child.
