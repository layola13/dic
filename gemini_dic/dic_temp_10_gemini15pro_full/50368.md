depopulation:/diːˌpɒpjuˈleɪʃn/ | n. |人口减少，人口稀疏|The depopulation of rural areas is a serious problem. 农村地区人口减少是一个严重问题。|例句：The war caused widespread depopulation. 这场战争造成了大范围的人口减少。

The depopulation of the island has left it vulnerable to invaders.  这个岛屿的人口减少使得它很容易受到侵略者的攻击。|近义词：decline, decrease, emptying, desertion |反义词:population growth, increase, overpopulation |词性:noun

depopulate:/diːˈpɒpjuleɪt/ | vt. |使人口减少，减少…的人口|The Black Death depopulated vast swathes of Europe. 黑死病导致欧洲大片地区人口减少。|例句：The disease depopulated the entire village.  这种疾病使整个村庄人口减少。

Forced migration has depopulated many towns. 强制移民导致许多城镇人口减少。 |近义词：reduce, diminish, deplete |反义词:populate, inhabit |词性:verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *depopulatio, from de- (“away, from”) + populus (“people”).
