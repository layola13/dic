misappraise:/ˌmɪsəˈpreɪz/
verb
错误地评估；估价过低或过高
To assess or estimate the value or quality of something incorrectly.
He misappraised the value of the antique clock. 他错误地估计了这台古董钟的价值。

The painting was misappraised and sold for far less than its true worth. 这幅画被错误估价，以远低于其真实价值的价格出售。

近义词: undervalue, underestimate, overestimate, misjudge, miscalculate
反义词: appraise correctly, assess accurately, evaluate properly
词性: verb
