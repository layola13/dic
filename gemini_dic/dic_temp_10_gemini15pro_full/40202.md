contestably:/kənˈtɛstəbli/ | adv. | 可争辩地，有争议地 |  In a way that can be contested or disputed; arguably. |  His claims are contestably inaccurate.  他的说法有争议地不准确。|  arguably, disputably, debatably |  indisputably, undoubtedly, irrefutably | adverb

contestable:/kənˈtɛstəbəl/ | adj. | 可争辩的，有争议的 |  Open to contest or dispute; debatable. | The election results were contestable. 选举结果是有争议的。| debatable, disputable, arguable |  indisputable, undeniable, irrefutable | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *contestābilis, from Latin contestārī (“to call to witness”), from con- (“together”) + testārī (“to bear witness”), from testis (“witness”).
