acetol:/ˈæsɪtɒl/ (US) /ˈæsɪtɔːl/ (UK)| n. |丙酮醇; 羟基丙酮|A colorless liquid ketone alcohol, CH3COCH2OH, used as a solvent and reducing agent. It is an intermediate in the metabolism of acetone.|Acetol is an intermediate in the conversion of propane-1,2-diol to pyruvate.|近义词: hydroxyacetone, 1-hydroxy-2-propanone|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acétol*, from *acétone* (acetone) + *-ol* (indicating an alcohol). Ultimately from Latin *acetum* (vinegar).
