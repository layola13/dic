detriment:/ˈdetrɪmənt/| n.|损害，伤害，不利|Something that causes damage, harm, or loss. |例句：Smoking is a detriment to your health. 吸烟有害健康。|例句：The policy change was to the detriment of small businesses. 这项政策的改变对小型企业不利。|近义词：harm, damage, injury, disadvantage|反义词：benefit, advantage|词性:名词

detriments:/ˈdetrɪmənts/| n.|损害，伤害，不利（复数）|The plural form of "detriment," referring to multiple factors causing harm or disadvantage. | 例句：He ignored the detriments of his lifestyle and continued his unhealthy habits. 他无视自己生活方式的种种害处，继续着不健康的生活习惯。|例句：The factory closing posed several detriments to the local economy. 工厂关闭给当地经济带来了若干损害。|近义词：harms, damages, injuries, disadvantages|反义词：benefits, advantages|词性:名词
