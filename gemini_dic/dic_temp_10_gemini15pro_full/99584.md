interinserted:/ˌɪntərɪnˈsɜːtɪd/
adjective
插入的，相互插入的
inserted between or among other things.
The story is interspersed with flashbacks to the character's childhood.  这个故事穿插着对主人公童年的闪回。
Near Synonyms: interleaved, intercalated, interwoven
Antonyms: separate, detached
