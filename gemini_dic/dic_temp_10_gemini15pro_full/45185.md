cuisine:/kwiˈziːn/ | n. |菜肴，烹饪，菜系 |A style or method of cooking, especially as characteristic of a particular country, region, or establishment. |例句：The restaurant offers a wide variety of French cuisine. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的法国菜。

例句：Italian cuisine is known for its use of fresh ingredients. 意大利菜以使用新鲜食材而闻名。| 近义词：cooking, gastronomy, food | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cuisine*, from Old French *cuisigne*, from Latin *coquina* (“kitchen”), from *coquere* (“to cook”).
