exonerate: /ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪt/ /ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪt/
verb
免除；宣布…无罪
To clear from blame or accusation; to prove innocent.
The court exonerated him of all charges. 法庭宣布他所有罪名均不成立。

exonerated: /ɪɡˈzɑːnəreɪtɪd/ /ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪtɪd/
adjective
免罪的；被证明无罪的
Cleared from blame or accusation.
He felt exonerated after the jury delivered the not guilty verdict. 陪审团宣判无罪后，他感到如释重负。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exoneratus, past participle of exonerare "to free from a burden," from Latin ex- "out of" + onus "burden."

SYNONYMS->
absolve, acquit, clear, vindicate, discharge, exculpate

ANTONYMS->
incriminate, convict, accuse, blame, condemn
