clamp:/klæmp/| n. vt.|夹具，夹钳；钳住，夹紧|A device used to hold or fasten objects tightly together.|He used a clamp to hold the wood in place. 他用夹具固定木头。|近义词：vise, grip, fastener|反义词：release, loosen|词性:名词,动词
clamps:/klæmps/| n.|夹具，夹钳（clamp的复数形式）|Plural form of "clamp," referring to multiple clamping devices.|The mechanic used clamps to secure the car parts. 机械师用夹具固定汽车零件。|近义词：vises, grips, fasteners|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch or Middle Low German *klampe, related to Dutch klamp and German Klampe.  Ultimately from a Proto-Germanic root meaning "lump, mass."
