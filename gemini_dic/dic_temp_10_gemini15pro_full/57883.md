dried:/draɪd/| adj.|干的，干燥的|Having had all moisture removed; no longer wet.|The dried leaves crumbled in my hand. 干枯的叶子在我手中碎裂了。|近义词：dehydrated, parched, desiccated|反义词：wet, moist, damp|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drien, from Old English drīgan (“to dry”), from Proto-Germanic *drūgiz (“dry”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewg- (“dry”).  Cognate with Dutch drogen (“to dry”), German trocknen (“to dry”), Icelandic þurr (“dry”).
