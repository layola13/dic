extinguishment:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃmənt/
n.
熄灭；消灭；偿清
the act of extinguishing something, especially a fire or a debt.
The fire department achieved rapid extinguishment of the blaze. 消防部门迅速扑灭了大火。
synonyms: putting out, quenching, smothering, suppressing, stamping out, elimination, eradication, abolition, annihilation
antonyms: ignition, kindling, lighting, inflammation
