generics: /dʒəˈnɛrɪks/ | n. | 泛型，通用的东西 |  Relating to or denoting a product that is not branded, especially a drug or other commodity sold under its chemical name rather than under a brand name.  |  The doctor prescribed me generics to save money. 医生给我开了非专利药以节省开支。 | synonyms: unbranded, nonproprietary | antonyms: branded, proprietary | noun

generics: /dʒəˈnɛrɪks/ | adj. | 泛型的，通用的 |  Characterized by or relating to a genus or group; general. (In computing) of or relating to code that can work with different types of data. | This is a generic term for a wide range of products. 这是一个广泛产品的通用术语。 | synonyms: general, universal, common | antonyms: specific, particular, unique | adjective
