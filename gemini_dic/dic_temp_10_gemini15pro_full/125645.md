mummichog:/ˈmʌmɪˌtʃɒɡ/ (US) /ˈmʌmɪtʃɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|鳉鱼|A small killifish (Fundulus heteroclitus) found in salt marshes and brackish waters along the Atlantic coast of North America.|Mummichogs are remarkably tolerant of changes in salinity.|近义词：killifish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Narragansett *mamúchequock*, meaning "they go in crowds".
