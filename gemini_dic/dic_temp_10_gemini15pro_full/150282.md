panegyric:/ˌpænɪˈdʒɪrɪk/| n.|颂词，赞词， панегирик|A formal speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly.|The CEO delivered a panegyric on the company's founder at the annual meeting. 首席执行官在年会上发表了对公司创始人的颂词。

The book is a panegyric to the natural beauty of the countryside. 这本书是对乡村自然美景的赞美。|近义词：eulogy, encomium, tribute, accolade|反义词：diatribe, criticism, denunciation|词性:noun
