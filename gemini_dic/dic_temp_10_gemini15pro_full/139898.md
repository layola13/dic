oddment:/ˈɒdmənt/| n. | 零碎物品；残余物；零头；奇数|a small article or amount of something left over from a larger piece or quantity; something miscellaneous|例句：She sold the oddments of material at a discount. 她把剩余的零碎布料打折出售了。

例句：The box contained various oddments of jewelry and trinkets. 盒子里装着各种各样的零碎珠宝和小饰品。|近义词：remnant, fragment, scrap, leftover, residue|反义词: whole, entirety |词性:noun
