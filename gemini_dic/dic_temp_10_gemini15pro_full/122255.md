miters:/ˈmaɪtərz/ (US), /ˈmaɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|斜接；斜接角；主教冠；斜接处（miter的复数）|The plural form of miter, referring to multiple joints made at an angle.|The miters on the window frame were perfectly aligned. 窗框上的斜接角完美对齐。|
近义词:bevels, angles|
反义词:butt joints|
词性:noun


词源: From Old French *mitre* (modern French *mitre*), from Latin *mitra*, from Ancient Greek *μίτρα* (*mítra*) meaning "headband, turban."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\Hsmih₁-tréh₂-* "band, belt."
