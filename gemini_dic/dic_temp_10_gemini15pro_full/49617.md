demibastion:/ˈdɛmiˌbæstʃən/
n.|半棱堡，半堡|A projecting part of a fortification, smaller than a bastion.|The demibastion offered limited protection from enemy fire. 半棱堡对敌人的炮火只能提供有限的保护。|
Example 1: The soldiers retreated to the demibastion for cover. 士兵们撤退到半棱堡寻求掩护。

Example 2: The demibastion was strategically placed to overlook the valley. 半棱堡被战略性地放置在可以俯瞰山谷的地方。
|近义词:ravelin, outwork|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inner keep")|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *demi-bastion*, from *demi-* "half" + *bastion*. Ultimately from Italian *bastione*.
