beheader:/bɪˈhɛdər/| n.|斩首者，刽子手|A person who beheads someone. |The beheader was a masked figure. 刽子手是一个蒙面人。|The king ordered the beheader to execute the traitor. 国王命令刽子手处死叛徒。|executioner, headsman|  |noun
Etymology->
From behead + -er.  Behead comes from the Old English "beheafdian," meaning "to deprive of one's head."
