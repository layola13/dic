ethnic cleansing:/ˈɛθnɪk ˈklɛnzɪŋ/
n.
种族清洗
The systematic forced removal of ethnic or religious groups from a given territory by a more powerful ethnic group, with the intent of making it ethnically homogeneous.
The Bosnian War witnessed horrific acts of ethnic cleansing. 波斯尼亚战争见证了可怕的种族清洗行为。
genocide, forced displacement, mass expulsion
integration, coexistence
noun
