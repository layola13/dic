macaroons:/ˌmækəˈruːnz/
n.
杏仁小圆饼, 马卡龙
small, sweet, brightly colored almond-flavored cookies made with egg whites, sugar, and sometimes coconut.
例句：The macaroons were delicate and colorful. 这些马卡龙精致且色彩鲜艳。

例句：She brought a box of assorted macaroons to the party. 她带了一盒什锦马卡龙来参加聚会。

近义词：cookies, biscuits
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of food)
词性: Noun
