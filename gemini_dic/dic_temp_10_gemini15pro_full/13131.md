asura:/ˈɑːsurə/| n. |阿修罗；非天；恶魔|A powerful demigod or demon in Hindu and Buddhist mythology, often depicted as having multiple heads and arms and being engaged in perpetual conflict with the devas (gods).  They are beings of great power, but are driven by envy, pride, and insatiable desires. |Example 1: The asura Ravana kidnapped Sita in the Ramayana.  罗摩衍那中，阿修罗罗波那绑架了悉达。

Example 2:  In Buddhist cosmology, asuras inhabit a realm below the heavens, constantly battling the gods. 在佛教宇宙论中，阿修罗居住在低于天堂的境界，不断与神战斗。|近义词：demon, titan, giant|反义词:deva, god, angel|词性:noun
