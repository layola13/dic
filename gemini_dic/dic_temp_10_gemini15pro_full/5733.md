alveoliform:/ælˌviːˈəʊlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
蜂窝状的；小窝状的
Resembling alveoli; having a pitted or honeycomb-like surface.
The fossilized coral had an alveoliform structure. 这块珊瑚化石有着蜂窝状的结构。
The underside of the leaf was alveoliform.  叶子的背面呈蜂窝状。
alveolate, honeycombed, pitted
smooth, flat
