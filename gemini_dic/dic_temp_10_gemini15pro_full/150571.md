papaverine:/pəˈpævərɪn/| n.|罂粟碱|A smooth muscle relaxant derived from opium poppy, used to treat spasms and improve blood flow.|Papaverine is sometimes used to treat erectile dysfunction. 罂粟碱有时用于治疗勃起功能障碍。|Synonyms: vasodilator, antispasmodic|Antonyms: vasoconstrictor|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *papaver* (poppy) + *-ine* (alkaloid suffix).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *pap* (to swell), likely referring to the seed pod of the poppy.
