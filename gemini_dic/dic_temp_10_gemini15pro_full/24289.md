brigands:/ˈbrɪɡəndz/
n.
土匪，强盗
A member of a gang of robbers, especially one operating in mountainous or forested regions.
The brigands held the travelers hostage for ransom. 土匪劫持了旅客作为人质以勒索赎金。

The stagecoach was attacked by brigands. 驿马车遭到土匪袭击。

近义词:bandits, robbers, outlaws, highwaymen, thieves
反义词:law enforcement, police, soldiers
