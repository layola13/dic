bible-bashing:/ˈbaɪblˌbæʃɪŋ/
noun
圣经抨击；以宗教名义进行的批评
The act of aggressively or vehemently quoting or citing the Bible to support one's views or to condemn others, often perceived as hypocritical or self-righteous.
He’s always bible-bashing, but I’ve never seen him in church. 他总是搬出圣经教训人，但我从来没在教堂见过他。
近义词: moralizing, sermonizing, preaching, proselytizing
反义词: tolerance, open-mindedness, acceptance
