doctrinaire:/ˌdɒktrɪˈnɛːr/ /ˌdɑːktrɪˈnɛːr/
adjective
教条的；空谈理论的；迂腐的
dogmatically adhering to a theory or doctrine regardless of its practicality or suitability.
例句：He has a doctrinaire approach to education. 他对教育采取了一种教条主义的态度。

例句：The party's doctrinaire policies alienated many voters.  该党教条式的政策疏远了许多选民。

近义词：dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, unyielding, uncompromising
反义词：pragmatic, flexible, adaptable, open-minded
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From French *doctrinaire*, from *doctrine* ("doctrine") + *-aire*, equivalent to English *-ary*.  Ultimately from Latin *doctrina* ("teaching, instruction").
