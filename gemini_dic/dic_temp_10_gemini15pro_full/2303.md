adepts:/ˈædepts/| n.|专家，能手|A person who is skilled or proficient at something.|The company employs several adepts in computer programming.  这家公司雇佣了几位计算机编程专家。

Adepts at origami can create intricate designs. 摺纸高手可以创造出复杂的图案。|近义词：experts, masters, professionals, proficient, skilled|反义词：novices, beginners, amateurs|词性：名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adeptus* "having attained, achieved," past participle of *adipisci* "to attain to," from *ad* "to" + *apisci* "to obtain."

USAGE->
The word "adepts" is typically used in the plural form.

Example with preposition "at":
They are adepts at playing musical instruments. = 他们是演奏乐器的行家。
He became an adept at woodworking after years of practice. = 经过多年的练习，他成为了木工方面的专家。

Example following "become":
She became an adept at public speaking. = 她成为了一个演讲高手。
With practice, anyone can become an adept at coding. = 通过练习，任何人都可以成为编程高手。


PHRASE->
adepts in (a field)
adepts at (a skill)
