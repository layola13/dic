methylate:/ˈmɛθɪleɪt/ | v. | 甲基化 | To introduce a methyl group (CH3) into a molecule or compound. |  Scientists methylated the DNA to study gene expression. 科学家将DNA甲基化来研究基因表达。| methylate DNA with enzymes 用酶甲基化DNA | synonyms: alkylate | antonyms: demethylate | verb

methylation:/ˌmɛθɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 甲基化 | The process of introducing a methyl group into a molecule. | DNA methylation plays a crucial role in gene regulation. DNA甲基化在基因调控中起着至关重要的作用。|  Protein methylation can alter protein function. 蛋白质甲基化可以改变蛋白质功能。 | synonyms: alkylation | antonyms: demethylation | noun
