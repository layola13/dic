bulkheaded:/ˈbʌlkˌhɛdɪd/
adjective
用隔板隔开的，用舱壁分隔的
divided into compartments by bulkheads; having bulkheads
The ship's engine room was bulkheaded to prevent the spread of fire. 船的机舱用舱壁隔开，以防止火势蔓延。
The submarine is bulkheaded for safety. 潜水艇用舱壁隔开是为了安全。
compartmentalized, partitioned
open-plan
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bulkhead + -ed.  Bulkhead itself comes from bulk (meaning large body or mass) + head (in this context likely referring to a crossbeam or partition).
