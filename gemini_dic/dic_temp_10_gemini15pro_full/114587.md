malcontent:/ˌmælkənˈtent/ | adj. n. | 不满的；不满的人 | Dissatisfied and complaining; a person who is dissatisfied and rebellious. | The malcontent workers went on strike. 不满的工人举行罢工。 | 近义词: dissatisfied, discontented, disgruntled, rebellious | 反义词: content, satisfied, happy | 词性: 形容词, 名词

malcontent:/ˌmælkənˈtent/ | n. | 不满分子；反抗者 | A person who is dissatisfied and rebellious. | The government was worried about the growing number of malcontents. 政府对日益增多的不满分子感到担忧。 | 近义词: rebel, dissident, insurgent | 反义词: loyalist, conformist | 词性: 名词
