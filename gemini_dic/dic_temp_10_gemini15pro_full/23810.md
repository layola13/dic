brazer:/ˈbreɪzər/| n.|钎焊工，铜焊工，焊接器|A person who brazes or a tool used for brazing.|The brazer joined the two metal pieces together skillfully.  钎焊工熟练地将两块金属连接在一起。|The brazer heated the metal until it glowed red.  焊接器把金属加热到通红。|近义词：welder, solderer|反义词: N/A|noun


braze:/breɪz/| v.|钎焊，铜焊|To join (two pieces of metal) by soldering with an alloy (as brass) that melts at a lower temperature than that of the pieces being joined.|He brazed the pipes together. 他把管道钎焊在一起。|She used a torch to braze the wires. 她用焊炬把电线钎焊起来。|近义词：solder, weld|反义词:separate, detach|verb


Etymology:
From Old French *braser (compare Old Occitan brazar), from braise "embers," from Frankish *brasa, from Proto-Germanic *brasōn.
