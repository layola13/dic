inapplicably:/ˌɪnəˈplɪkəbli/
adjective
不适用的，无法应用的
Not applicable; impossible to apply.
The rules of formal grammar are inapplicably to casual conversation.  这些正式语法规则不适用于日常对话。
This theory is inapplicably to the current situation.  这个理论不适用于当前的情况。
近义词: irrelevantly, inappropriately, unsuitably
反义词: applicably, appropriately, suitably
词性:adjective
词源:  From in- (not) + applicable (capable of being applied), from Latin applicabilis, from applicare (to apply).
