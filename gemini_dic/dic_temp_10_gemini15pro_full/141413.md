ophiophagous:/ˌɒfiˈɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食蛇的；以蛇为食的 | Feeding on snakes. |  Example: The secretary bird is an ophiophagous bird, known for its ability to kill and eat venomous snakes.  秘书鸟是一种食蛇鸟，以其杀死和吃掉毒蛇的能力而闻名。

Example:  The king cobra is an ophiophagous snake, meaning it preys on other snakes.  眼镜王蛇是一种食蛇蛇，这意味着它捕食其他蛇。| 近义词: snake-eating | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ophio-* (snake) + *-phagos* (eater).  来自希腊语 *ophio-*（蛇）+ *-phagos*（吃者）。
