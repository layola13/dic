commissary: /ˈkɒmɪsɛri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪseri/ (US)| n.| 軍需官；代表；雜貨店；餐飲服務|A commissary is an officer in charge of supplying provisions for an army or other body of persons, or a store that sells food and supplies to military personnel. It can also refer to a deputy or delegate, or a restaurant or catering service in a film studio, theater, etc.|The soldiers lined up at the commissary to collect their rations. 士兵们在军需处排队领取口粮。|Synonyms: store, canteen, shop, delegate, representative|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


commissary: /ˈkɒmɪsɛri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɪseri/ (US)| adj.| 委任的；代理的 |Appointed as a deputy or delegate.|He held a commissary position in the local government. 他在当地政府担任代理职务。|Synonyms: delegated, deputized, representative|Antonyms: primary, principal|Adjective
