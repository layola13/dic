draft:/dræft/ /drɑːft/| n. vt. adj.|草稿，汇票；通风；征兵|A preliminary version of a piece of writing. |She's sending me a first draft of the contract. 她会把合同的初稿发给我。|synonym: sketch, outline, plan|antonym: final version|noun

draft:/dræft/ /drɑːft/| vt.|起草，征召；选秀|To select (someone) for a particular purpose, especially military service.|He was drafted into the army. 他应征入伍了。|synonym: conscript, recruit, enlist|antonym: discharge, release|verb

draft:/dræft/ /drɑːft/| adj.|初步的，非正式的|Denoting a preliminary version of something, such as a document.|This is just a draft proposal. 这只是一个初步的建议。|synonym: preliminary, provisional, tentative|antonym: final, definitive|adjective
