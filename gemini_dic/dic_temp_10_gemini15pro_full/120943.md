miscensure: /ˌmɪsˈsɛnʃər/ | n. |  错误的责备，误判 | An act of miscensuring someone; blaming or condemning someone unjustly. | He expressed deep regret for his miscensure of his colleague. 他对他同事的误判表示深深的歉意。 | 近义词：misjudgment, misinterpretation, condemnation | 反义词：praise, commendation | 词性: noun

词源:  From mis- + censure.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly," and "censure" means "express severe disapproval of (someone or something), typically in a formal statement."
