hobnob:/ˈhɒbnɒb/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbnɑːb/ (US)| v. | 交往，亲密交谈；(与要人)过从甚密 | To socialize with someone, especially someone who is rich or famous. | He was seen hobnobbing with the city's elite. 他被看到与这座城市的精英们交谈甚欢。|  She hobnobbed with royalty at the garden party.  她在花园派对上与皇室成员亲切交谈。 | synonyms: socialize, fraternize, mingle, consort | antonyms: isolate, shun, avoid | verb

hobnobbing:/ˈhɒbnɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbnɑːbɪŋ/ (US) | n. | 交往，亲密交谈 | The act of socializing, especially with someone who is rich or famous. | There was a lot of hobnobbing going on at the gala.  晚会上有很多社交活动。 | The hobnobbing of politicians and businessmen can sometimes lead to corruption. 政客和商人的交往有时会导致腐败。 | synonyms: socializing, fraternization, mingling | antonyms: isolation, avoidance | noun
