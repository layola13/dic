avenaceous:/ˌævɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
燕麦色的；禾本科植物的
Resembling oats or other plants of the grass family, especially in color or texture.  Relating to, or characteristic of the grass family (Poaceae), which includes oats.
The field was covered in an avenaceous haze.  这片田野笼罩在一片燕麦色的薄雾中。
synonyms: oat-colored, grassy, gramineous
antonyms: nongramineous
