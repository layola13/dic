brisker:/ˈbrɪskər/ | adj. | 更活泼的，更轻快的，更 brisk 的 |Comparative form of brisk: more brisk.  Meaning more active, lively, or fast-paced. | The wind grew brisker as the day went on.  风随着时间的推移越来越大了。| The brisker pace of life in the city excited her. 城市里更快的生活节奏让她兴奋。 | synonyms: quicker, faster, livelier, more energetic | antonyms: slower, calmer, gentler, more sluggish | adjective
