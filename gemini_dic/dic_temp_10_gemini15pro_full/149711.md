palaeographically:/ˌpeɪliəˈɡræfɪkli/
adverb|古文字学地|in a way that relates to the study of ancient writing|The text was analyzed palaeographically to determine its age. 这篇文章从古文字学的角度进行了分析，以确定其年代。|
近义词:scribally
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "modernly" in certain contexts)
词性:adverb

palaeography:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfi/
noun|古文字学|the study of ancient writing systems and the deciphering and dating of historical manuscripts.|Palaeography can help us understand the evolution of language. 古文字学可以帮助我们理解语言的演变。|
近义词:scribal studies
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun
