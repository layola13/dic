outtaken:/ˌaʊtˈteɪkən/ | adj. |  被删除的，未采用的 | Removed or excluded from a final version, especially of a film or recording. | The outtaken scenes from the movie were later released as bonus features. 这部电影被删除的场景后来作为花絮发布了。| 近义词: deleted, omitted, excised, unused | 反义词: included, used, incorporated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "outtaken" is formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning "out of" or "away from") and the past participle of the verb "take."  It literally means "taken out."
