fastnesses:/ˈfɑːstnɪsɪz/
n.
要塞，堡垒；(尤指山)险要之地；安全地带
strongholds; fortified places, especially in mountainous regions; places of safety and security

The rebels retreated to their mountain fastnesses.
叛军撤退到他们的山区要塞。

The ancient city's fastnesses protected it from invaders.
这座古城的要塞保护它免受侵略者的侵扰。

近义词:strongholds, fortresses, refuges, sanctuaries
反义词:vulnerabilities, weaknesses

词性:名词(plural)
