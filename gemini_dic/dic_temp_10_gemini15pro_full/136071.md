nonrecessive:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈsɛsɪv/
adjective
非隐性的
Not recessive; dominant or codominant.  A nonrecessive gene will express itself even if only one copy is present.
The nonrecessive gene for brown eyes determined the child's eye color.  这个非隐性的棕色眼睛基因决定了孩子的眼睛颜色。
Near synonyms: dominant, codominant
Antonym: recessive
词性: adjective
