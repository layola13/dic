词源：enormous + -ness
enormous (adj.) "huge, vast, very large," 1590s, from Latin enormis "out of rule, irregular, exceeding proper measure," from e- "out of" (see ex-) + norma "rule, pattern" (see norm). The notion is of something that deviates from a normal standard in size or character. Related: Enormously; enormousness.
