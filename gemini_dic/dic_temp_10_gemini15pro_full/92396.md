hypodermic:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. n. |皮下的；皮下注射的；皮下注射器 | administered or used under the skin.  |
例句：The doctor gave him a hypodermic injection. 医生给他打了一针皮下注射。
例句：Hypodermic needles are used to administer medication. 皮下注射针头用于给药。
近义词：subcutaneous, injected |反义词：oral, topical |词性：adjective, noun

hypodermic:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | n. |皮下注射器 | a syringe used to inject a drug or other substance under the skin.  |
例句：She filled the hypodermic with morphine. 她给注射器装满了吗啡。
例句：The nurse prepared the hypodermic for the injection. 护士准备好了注射器进行注射。
近义词：syringe, needle |反义词：N/A |词性：noun
