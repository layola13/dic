corrosible:/kəˈroʊzəbl/ |adj.|可腐蚀的，易腐蚀的|Susceptible to corrosion; easily corroded by chemical action.|Acid rain is corrosive to metal. 酸雨对金属有腐蚀性。|The battery acid is highly corrosive and can burn skin. 电池酸具有高度腐蚀性，可以灼伤皮肤。|近义词:corrodible, erodible|反义词:corrosion-resistant, inert|词性:形容词
