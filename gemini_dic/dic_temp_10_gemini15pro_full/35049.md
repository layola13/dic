closefisted:/ˌkloʊsˈfɪstɪd/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Unwilling to spend money; stingy.| He was known for being closefisted and never offering to buy a round of drinks. 他以吝啬而闻名，从不主动请客喝酒。|  He's so closefisted he squawks if he has to buy his own tea bags. 他非常吝啬，如果必须自己买茶包，他就会抱怨。|近义词：stingy, miserly, tightfisted, parsimonious|反义词：generous, openhanded, liberal|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "close" (meaning reluctant to give or share) + "fist" (referring to a tightly closed hand, symbolic of holding onto wealth).  Dates back to the late 16th century.
