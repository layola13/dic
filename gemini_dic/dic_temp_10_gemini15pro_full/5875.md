ambassadorships:/æmˈbæsədərʃɪps/
n.
大使的职位；大使的任期
The position or term of service of an ambassador.
She held several ambassadorships during her career in the foreign service.  她在外交部门的职业生涯中担任过多个大使职位。
His ambassadorship to France lasted five years. 他驻法国大使的任期为五年。
近义词: embassy, diplomatic mission, legation
反义词: None
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ambassador + -ship.  Ambassador comes from Old French ambassadeur, ultimately from Late Latin ambactiare "to go on a mission."  The suffix -ship denotes "state, condition, or character of being."
