counterinstitutions:/ˈkaʊntərˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənz/
|n.|反体制; 对抗性机构|Organizations that challenge or oppose established institutions or norms.|The activists formed counterinstitutions to provide alternative services and resist the dominant power structure.  激进分子组建了对抗性机构，以提供替代服务并抵抗主流权力结构。|
|n.|对抗性机构|Groups established to challenge existing institutions.|These counterinstitutions offered an alternative space for political organizing. 这些对抗性机构为政治组织提供了一个替代空间。|
|近义词：alternative institutions, opposition groups, parallel structures|
|反义词：established institutions, mainstream organizations|
|词性：noun|
