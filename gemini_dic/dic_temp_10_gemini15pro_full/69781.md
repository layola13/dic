fermentative:/fərˈmɛntətɪv/
adjective
发酵的; 促使发酵的
Causing or relating to fermentation, the chemical breakdown of a substance by bacteria, yeasts, or other microorganisms, typically involving effervescence and the giving off of heat.
The process produces a distinctive odor due to the fermentative activity of the bacteria.  这个过程由于细菌的发酵活动而产生一种特殊的气味。
The winemaker carefully monitored the fermentative process.  酿酒师仔细地监测发酵过程。
Synonyms: zymogenic, fermenting
Antonyms: non-fermentative, sterile
词性: adjective
