amorist:/ˈæmərɪst/| n. |好色之徒，沉溺于爱情的人|A person who is in love or who writes about love, especially in a highly idealized or sentimental way.  A person who is devoted to or who practices the art of love.|He fancied himself an amorist, writing flowery poems to every woman he met. 他自认为是一个多情种子，给他遇到的每个女人都写华丽的诗。|Synonyms: lover, romantic, paramour, sweetheart, admirer|Antonyms: hater, enemy, ascetic|Noun
