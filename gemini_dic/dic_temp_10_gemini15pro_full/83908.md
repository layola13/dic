half-begging:/ˌhɑːfˈbeɡɪŋ/
adj.
半乞求的，近乎乞求的
Engaging in begging in a hesitant or indirect way; almost begging.
The child gave me a half-begging look, hoping I would buy him an ice cream. 孩子半乞求地看着我，希望我给他买个冰淇淋。
Near synonyms: pleading, imploring, supplicating
Antonyms: demanding, assertive, commanding
Word Origin:  A compound formed from "half" and "begging," both from Old English.
part of speech: adjective
