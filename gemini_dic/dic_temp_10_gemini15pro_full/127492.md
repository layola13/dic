neckerchief:/ˈnɛkərˌtʃiːf/| n.|围巾，领巾|A piece of cloth worn around the neck, often decorative or part of a uniform.|She wore a brightly colored neckerchief. 她戴着一条色彩鲜艳的围巾。|Synonym: scarf, bandana|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neck + kerchief.  Kerchief comes from Middle English *coverchef* meaning a cloth to cover the head, from Old French *couvrechief*, from *couvrir* (to cover) and *chief* (head).
