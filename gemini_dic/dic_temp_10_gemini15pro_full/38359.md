concentrativeness:/ˌkɒnsənˈtreɪtɪvnəs/ /ˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪtɪvnəs/
n.
专注力，专心度
The state or quality of being concentrative; the ability to focus one's attention.
Her concentrativeness during the exam was remarkable.  她在考试中的专注力非凡。
近义词：concentration, focus, attention
反义词：distraction, inattention
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From concentrate + -ive + -ness.  Concentrate comes from Late Latin concentrātus, past participle of concentrāre "to join in a center," from com- "together" + centrum "center."
