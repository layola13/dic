antismokers:/ˌæntiˈsməʊkəz/ (US) /ˌæntiˈsmoʊkərz/| n.| 反吸烟者；反对吸烟的人|People who are opposed to smoking, often advocating for stricter tobacco control measures. |The antismokers protested outside the tobacco company's headquarters. 反吸烟者在烟草公司总部外抗议。|Synonyms: nonsmokers, anti-tobacco activists|Antonyms: smokers, pro-smoking advocates|Noun

antismoking:/ˌæntiˈsməʊkɪŋ/ (US) /ˌæntiˈsmoʊkɪŋ/| adj.| 反对吸烟的；禁烟的|Opposed to or designed to discourage smoking. |The restaurant has an antismoking policy. 这家餐厅有禁烟政策。|Synonyms: anti-tobacco|Antonyms: pro-smoking|Adjective
