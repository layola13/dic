macedoine:/ˌmæsɪˈdɔɪn/ | n. | 马其顿沙拉，水果什锦，蔬菜丁 | A mixture of fruits or vegetables, often diced, typically served as a salad or dessert.  Derived from "Macedonia," a historically diverse region. |  例句：She prepared a refreshing macedoine of summer fruits. 她准备了一份清爽的夏季水果沙拉。

例句：The chef's macedoine of roasted vegetables was a colorful addition to the meal.  厨师做的烤蔬菜丁为这顿饭增添了色彩。 | 近义词：medley, mixture, assortment, salad | 反义词: (No clear single antonym, perhaps "single ingredient dish") | 词性: noun
