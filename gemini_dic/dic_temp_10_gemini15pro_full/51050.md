desolater:/ˈdesəleɪtər/| n.| 荒芜者，使荒凉的人或物 |Someone or something that desolates a place. |The war was a desolater of cities and lives. 战争是城市和生命的毁灭者。|近义词: devastator, destroyer, ruiner |反义词: builder, creator, restorer| noun

desolate:/ˈdesələt/ | adj. | 荒凉的，无人烟的；孤独的，凄凉的 |(of a place) deserted of people and in a state of bleak and dismal emptiness. Feeling or showing misery, unhappiness, or loneliness. |The landscape was desolate and barren.  这片土地荒凉贫瘠。 She felt desolate and abandoned. 她感到孤独无助。| 近义词: barren, bleak, deserted, lonely | 反义词: populated, cheerful, happy | adjective
