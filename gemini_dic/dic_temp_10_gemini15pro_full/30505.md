centuriator:/ˌsɛntʃʊəˈreɪtər/| n. |百夫长，百人队长|A commander of a century in the Roman army, typically consisting of 80 to 100 men.  A leader or commander of a group of approximately one hundred people.|The centuriator led his men bravely into battle. 百夫长勇敢地带领他的士兵投入战斗。|Synonyms: commander, captain, leader, officer|Antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun
