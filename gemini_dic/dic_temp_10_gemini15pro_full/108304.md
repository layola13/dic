laurels: /ˈlɔːrəlz/ | n. | 荣誉，桂冠；成就 |  A crown of laurel leaves, awarded as a prize in ancient Greece and Rome, and hence a symbol of victory or distinction. |  He rested on his laurels after winning the championship. 他在赢得冠军后就躺在功劳簿上睡大觉了。| synonyms: honors, awards, accolades, trophies, achievements | antonyms: failures, defeats, setbacks | noun


laurel: /ˈlɔːrəl/ | n. | 月桂树；荣誉 |  A small evergreen tree with dark green, shiny leaves.  Also, honor or fame gained. | The poet received critical laurels for his latest work. 这位诗人的新作获得了评论界的赞誉。| synonyms: bay tree, bay laurel, honor, acclaim, distinction | antonyms: disgrace, dishonor, obscurity | noun
