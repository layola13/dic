crucialities:/kruːʃiˈælɪtiz/ | n. |关键因素；至关重要的事情 | The crucialities of the situation demand immediate action.  局势的关键性需要立即采取行动。 |  crucial aspects, key elements, vital points | insignificances, trivialities | noun

Etymology:
From crucial (from Late Latin crucialis "of the form of a cross," from Latin crux "cross") + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives) + -es (plural suffix).
