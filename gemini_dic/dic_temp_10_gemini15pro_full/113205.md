lurks:/lɜːrks/ /lɝːks/
| v. | 潜伏，埋伏；（危险、疑虑等）暗藏，隐藏 |  To exist unseen or unnoticed, typically with the intent to do something harmful or wrong. |  A crocodile lurks beneath the surface of the water.  一条鳄鱼潜伏在水面之下。|  hide, skulk, prowl | emerge, appear | verb


lurking:/ˈlɜːrkɪŋ/ /ˈlɝːkɪŋ/
| adj. | 潜伏的，埋伏的 | Existing unseen or unnoticed, typically with the intent to do something harmful or wrong. |  There's a lurking suspicion that he's not telling the truth.  人们心中暗藏着一种怀疑，觉得他没有说实话。 | hidden, concealed, dormant |  obvious, apparent | adjective
