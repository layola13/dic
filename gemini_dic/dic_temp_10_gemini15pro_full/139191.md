objection:/əbˈdʒekʃən/| n.|反对，异议，缺点，反对的理由|An objection is an expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition; a reason for disagreeing.|例句：I have no objection to your proposal. 我对你的提议没有异议。

例句：Her objection to the plan was based on its cost. 她对这个计划的反对是基于它的成本。

近义词：protest, challenge, opposition, disapproval, dissent|反义词:agreement, approval, acceptance|词性:noun
