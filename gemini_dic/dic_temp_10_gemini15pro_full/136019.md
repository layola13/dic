nonrealisms:/ˌnɑːnˈriːəlɪzəmz/
n.|非现实主义|Theories that reject realism, encompassing various approaches that emphasize subjective experience, idealism, fantasy, or the constructed nature of reality.|His art explores themes of nonrealisms, blurring the lines between dreams and reality.|近义词:idealisms, fantasies, unrealities|反义词:realisms, actualities|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
realism (noun): from Late Latin realis "relating to things," from Latin res "thing, matter, affair, circumstance, fact," of unknown origin + -ism (suffix) indicating a doctrine, belief, or practice.
