motherers:/ˈmʌðərərz/
n.
过度呵护者，溺爱孩子的人
People who mother excessively; overprotective parents.
She felt smothered by the motherers in her family, always telling her what to do. 她觉得家里的那些溺爱她的人都让她窒息，总是告诉她该做什么。
近义词: overprotective parents, helicopter parents, cosseters
反义词: neglectful parents
词性: 名词
