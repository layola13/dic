lumbars:/ˈlʌmbɑːrz/ (US), /ˈlʌmbɑːz/ (UK)| n.|腰椎，腰部|The part of the back between the ribs and the pelvis. |He injured his lumbars in the accident. 他在事故中伤了腰椎。|Synonyms: lower back, loin|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a body part. Could consider cervical (neck) as a contrasting body part.)|noun

lumbars:/ˈlʌmbɑːrz/| n.|腰肌|The muscles of the lumbar region. |Strong lumbars are essential for good posture.  强壮的腰肌对良好的姿势至关重要。|Synonyms: back muscles, lower back muscles|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a body part.  Could consider abdominal muscles as a contrasting muscle group.)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
