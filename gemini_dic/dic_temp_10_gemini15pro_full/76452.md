gainsaying:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪɪŋ/
n.|否认，反驳|The evidence is overwhelming, there's no gainsaying it.  证据确凿，无可辩驳。|近义词：denial, contradiction, disputation, refutation|反义词：agreement, acceptance, acknowledgment, confirmation|词性:名词

gainsay:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/
v.|否认，反驳|Nobody could gainsay her dedication to the project. 没有人能否认她对这个项目的奉献精神。|近义词：deny, contradict, dispute, refute|反义词：confirm, acknowledge, accept, admit|词性:动词
