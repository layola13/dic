overtightened: /ˌōvərˈtītn̩d/
adjective
过度拧紧的；拧得太紧的
tightened excessively; fastened too securely.
The bolts were overtightened, causing the metal to crack. 螺栓拧得太紧，导致金属破裂。
Synonyms: over-torqued, excessively tightened
Antonyms: loose, slack
词性: adjective
