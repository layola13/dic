hippodrome:/ˈhɪpəˌdroʊm/ | n. |跑马场；竞技场|A large, oval stadium for horse racing and chariot races, especially in ancient Greece and Rome.| The emperor watched the chariot races from his box at the hippodrome.  皇帝在他的包厢里观看了跑马场的战车比赛。|Synonyms: racetrack, stadium, arena |Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct antonym, perhaps "theater" or "concert hall" as contrasting venues) | noun
