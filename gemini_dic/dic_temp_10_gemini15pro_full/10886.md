arachnophobe: /əˈræknəˌfoʊb/ | n. | 蜘蛛恐惧症患者 | A person with an extreme or irrational fear of spiders. |  My sister is an arachnophobe; she screams whenever she sees a spider. 我妹妹有蜘蛛恐惧症；她一看到蜘蛛就尖叫。

| Example:  He's such an arachnophobe that he can't even look at pictures of spiders. 他有严重的蜘蛛恐惧症，甚至连蜘蛛的照片都看不下去。| Synonym:  arachnophobic | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider someone who loves spiders) | Word type: Noun
