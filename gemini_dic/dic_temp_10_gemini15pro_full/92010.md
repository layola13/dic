hyperorthodoxy:/ˌhaɪpərˈɔːrθədɑːksi/
noun
极端正统；超级正统
Extreme or excessive adherence to orthodox religious beliefs or practices.
The community's hyperorthodoxy discouraged any deviation from traditional customs. 这个社群的极端正统使得任何偏离传统习俗的行为都受到压制。
近义词:ultraorthodoxy, fundamentalism
反义词:heterodoxy, liberalism, secularism
词性:n.

Etymology:
From hyper- + orthodoxy.  "Hyper-" is a Greek prefix meaning "over, above, beyond." "Orthodoxy" comes from the Greek "orthos" (right, true) + "doxa" (opinion, belief).
