orangemen:/ˈɔːrɪndʒmən/ | n. |奥兰治人（通常指新教徒，尤其指北爱尔兰的新教徒）| A member of a Protestant political organization, especially in Northern Ireland, founded to maintain the Protestant Ascendancy.  The Orange Order is known for its parades and marches, often seen as provocative by Catholics. |例句：The Orangemen marched through the streets on the Twelfth of July. 奥兰治人在七月十二日那天走过街道。

例句：Tensions often rise during the marching season, when Orangemen hold their parades.  在游行季节，当奥兰治人举行游行时，紧张局势经常加剧。|近义词：Protestant, Unionist |反义词: Catholic, Nationalist |词性: 名词
