disservicing:/dɪsˈsɜːvɪsɪŋ/
verb
损害，伤害
to do something that harms someone or something
例句：By focusing only on short-term profits, the company is disservicing its customers. 只关注短期利润，这家公司损害了顾客的利益。

例句：The media disserved the public by failing to report the story accurately. 媒体未能准确报道该事件，从而损害了公众的利益。

近义词：harming, damaging, injuring, undermining
反义词：helping, aiding, assisting, supporting
词性：v.
