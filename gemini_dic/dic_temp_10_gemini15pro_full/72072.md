flittingly:/ˈflɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 轻快地，一闪而过地 | Moving lightly and swiftly; briefly appearing or disappearing. | The butterflies flittingly danced among the flowers. 蝴蝶在花丛中轻盈地飞舞。| The thought flittingly crossed her mind. 这个念头在她脑海中一闪而过。|  Synonyms: briefly, fleetingly, momentarily, transiently | Antonyms: permanently, enduringly, constantly | 词性:副词

flit:/flɪt/ | v. | 掠过，轻快地飞，迁居 | To move swiftly and lightly; to flutter; to migrate. | The birds flitted from branch to branch. 鸟儿在枝头间飞来飞去。 | They flitted from city to city. 他们从一个城市迁到另一个城市。 | Synonyms: flutter, dart, skim, whisk, migrate | Antonyms: stay, remain, settle | 词性:动词
