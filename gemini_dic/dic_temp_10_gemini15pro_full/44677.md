|近义词：execution, torment, agony|反义词: resurrection, salvation|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *crucifixio, from Latin crucifixus, past participle of crucifigere “to crucify,” from crux “cross” + figere “to fix, fasten.”
