loadstar:/ˈloʊdstɑːr/ |n.| 指导原则；指路明灯；北极星 |A guiding principle or inspiration.  Something that serves as a guide or gives direction.  Also refers to Polaris, the North Star. |例句：Her faith was her loadstar in difficult times.  在困难时期，她的信仰是她的指路明灯。

例句：The company's loadstar is innovation and customer satisfaction.  公司的指导原则是创新和客户满意。|近义词：guide, inspiration, polestar, North Star, lodestar|反义词： (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用表示迷惑或误导的词) confusion, misdirection|词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English lode sterre, from Old English lād-steorra, from lād (“way, journey”) + steorra (“star”).  The word "lode" eventually became "load".
