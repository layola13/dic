orcs:/ɔːrks/ | n. |兽人，半兽人|A fictional humanoid creature resembling a monstrous and brutal goblin.  They are often portrayed as savage, warmongering, and of low intelligence. | The orcs attacked the village at dawn. 兽人们在黎明时袭击了村庄。| The orcs breached the castle walls. 兽人攻破了城堡的城墙。 |Synonyms: goblin, ogre, troll | Antonyms: elf, human, dwarf | noun
