backdrops:/ˈbækdrɒps/ (UK), /ˈbækdrɑːps/ (US)| n.|背景，背景幕布|A painted cloth hung at the back of a theater stage as part of the scenery.  A setting or background against which something happens.|The mountains formed a dramatic backdrop to the scene. 群山构成了这幅景象的壮丽背景。|synonyms: background, setting, scenery, context|antonyms: foreground|noun
