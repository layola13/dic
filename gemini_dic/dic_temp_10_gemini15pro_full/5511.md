alpestrine:/ˈælˌpaɪstraɪn/ | adj. | 高山的，高山生长的 | Growing or thriving at high altitudes; characteristic of high mountains. |  Edelweiss is an alpestrine flower. 雪绒花是一种高山植物。 | The alpestrine meadows were covered in wildflowers. 高山草甸上开满了野花。 | 近义词：alpine, mountainous, highland | 反义词：lowland | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *alpēstris, from Latin Alpēs (“the Alps”) + -estris (adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *albʰós (“white”).
