deified:/ˈdiːɪfaɪd/
verb |神化的，奉若神明的|Having been treated as a god; exalted or revered as a deity.|例句：The ancient Romans deified their emperors. 古罗马人神化他们的皇帝。|例句：The fans deified the rock star. 歌迷们把这位摇滚明星奉若神明。|近义词：worshipped, idolized, exalted, venerated|反义词：vilified, condemned, denigrated|词性:v.

词源:
From Latin deus (“god”) + -ify.  Related to deity.
