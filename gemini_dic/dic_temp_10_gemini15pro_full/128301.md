neurosis:/njʊˈroʊsɪs/ /nʊˈrəʊsɪs/| n.|神经症；神经官能症|A relatively mild mental illness that is not caused by organic disease, involving symptoms of stress (depression, anxiety, obsessive behaviour, hypochondria) but not a radical loss of touch with reality.|She suffers from acute neurosis.|近义词:mental disorder, mental illness, emotional disorder|反义词:mental health, stability|词性:noun
