exhortative:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtətɪv/
adj.
劝告的，激励的
Serving or tending to exhort.
The coach gave an exhortative speech to his team before the final game. 教练在决赛前对他的团队发表了激励人心的讲话。
近义词：encouraging, urging, inspiring, motivational
反义词：discouraging, dissuading, deterrent
词性：adjective

exhort:/ɪɡˈzɔːrt/
v.
劝告，激励
Strongly encourage or urge (someone) to do something.
The teacher exhorted her students to study hard for the upcoming exam. 老师劝诫她的学生们为了即将到来的考试努力学习。
近义词：encourage, urge, advise, counsel
反义词：discourage, dissuade, deter
词性：verb
