iatrochemist:/aɪˌætroʊˈkɛmɪst/ | n. | 医药化学家；炼金术士 |  A person who practices iatrochemistry, a historical branch of medicine and chemistry that sought to understand and treat disease in chemical terms. It combined alchemy and medicine.  | Paracelsus is considered one of the most important iatrochemists.  帕拉塞尔苏斯被认为是最重要的医药化学家之一。 |  Iatrochemists believed that chemical imbalances in the body caused diseases. 医药化学家认为体内化学失衡会导致疾病。 |  alchemist, chemist, physician | - | noun
