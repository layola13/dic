disporous:/dɪˈspɔːrəs/ /dɪˈspɔrəs/
adjective
分散的; 具有两种孢子的
Having two types of spores, as in some ferns.  Relating to or exhibiting two distinct forms or types of spores.
The fern is disporous, producing both megaspores and microspores. 这种蕨类植物是双孢子的，能产生大孢子和微孢子。
The disporous nature of the plant allows for greater genetic diversity.  这种植物的双孢子特性使其具有更大的遗传多样性。
heterosporous
isosporous
