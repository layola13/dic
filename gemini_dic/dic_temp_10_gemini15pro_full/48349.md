decrypted:/diːˈkrɪptɪd/ | adj. | 解密的，解密的 | Converted from a coded or encrypted form back into its original form. | Example: The decrypted message revealed the spy's true location. 解密后的消息揭示了间谍的真实位置。| Synonyms: deciphered, decoded, unveiled | Antonyms: encrypted, encoded, obscured | adjective

decrypted:/diːˈkrɪptɪd/ | v. | 解密 | To convert (a coded or encrypted message) back into its original form. | Example: The team worked tirelessly to decrypt the enemy's communications.  团队不知疲倦地工作，以解密敌人的通讯。| Synonyms: decipher, decode, unveil | Antonyms: encrypt, encode, obscure | verb
