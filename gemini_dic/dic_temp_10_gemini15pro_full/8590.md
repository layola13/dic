anticlerical:/ˌæntiˈklɛrɪkəl/
| adj. |反教权主义的；反对神职人员的| Opposed to the power or influence of the clergy in political or public affairs. | The French Revolution was a period of intense anticlerical sentiment.  法国大革命时期反教权情绪高涨。| The new government implemented several anticlerical policies. 新政府实施了几项反教权政策。 |近义词：secular, irreligious |反义词: clerical, devout | 词性: adjective

anticlericalism:/ˌæntiˈklɛrɪkəlɪzəm/
| n. |反教权主义| Opposition to the power or influence of the clergy in political or public affairs. |  Anticlericalism was a powerful force in 19th-century Europe.  反教权主义是19世纪欧洲一股强大的力量。| The rise of anticlericalism led to the confiscation of Church property. 反教权主义的兴起导致了教会财产的没收。|近义词：secularism |反义词: clericalism | 词性: noun
