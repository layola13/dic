drippily:/ˈdrɪpɪli/ | adv. |滴着地，湿淋淋地 | in a manner that drips; with liquid falling in drops | The faucet was leaking, and water dripped drippily onto the floor. 水龙头漏水了，水滴滴答答地滴到地板上。| Synonym: drippingly | Antonym: dryly | Part of Speech: Adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From drip + -ily.  "Drip" comes from Old English *dryppan, from Proto-Germanic *druppjan, of imitative origin.
