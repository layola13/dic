homegirl:/ˈhoʊmˌɡɜːrl/ (US), /ˈhəʊmˌɡɜːl/ (UK)| n.|闺蜜，女性好友，老乡|A close female friend, often from one's hometown or neighborhood.  A term of endearment and solidarity among women.|例句：My homegirl always has my back. 我的闺蜜总是支持我。

例句：She went out with her homegirls last night. 她昨晚和她的闺蜜们出去了。


近义词：best friend, close friend, pal, mate (more common in British English), bestie|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
