binding:/ˈbaɪndɪŋ/ /ˈbaɪndɪŋ/| adj. n.|有约束力的，捆绑；装订|Something that is binding forces people to obey or follow it.  A binding is something that ties things together, like the cover of a book.|The contract is legally binding.  这合同具有法律约束力。|Synonyms: obligatory, compulsory, mandatory|Antonyms: non-binding, optional|adjective

binding:/ˈbaɪndɪŋ/ /ˈbaɪndɪŋ/| n.|捆绑，装订|The act of tying something or the material used to tie something.  Also, the cover and fastening that hold the pages of a book together.|The binding on the old book was falling apart.  这本旧书的装订脱落了。|Synonyms: fastening, tie|Antonyms: loosening, untying|noun
