humblings:/ˈhʌmblɪŋz/
n.
羞辱；谦卑；低声下气
an act of humbling oneself; a humbling experience
例句：The experience was a humbling for him.  这段经历让他感到羞辱。

例句：He spoke of the humblings he had endured. 他讲述了他所忍受的屈辱。

近义词：humiliation, degradation, abasement
反义词：exaltation, elevation
词性：名词

humbling:/ˈhʌmblɪŋ/
adj.
令人羞辱的；使人谦卑的
making you feel humble
例句：It was a humbling experience to realize how little I knew. 意识到自己知道得如此之少，真是一次令人谦卑的经历。

例句：Losing the election was a humbling experience for the candidate.  败选对候选人来说是一次令人羞辱的经历。

近义词：humiliating, mortifying, chastening
反义词：exalting, elevating
词性：形容词
