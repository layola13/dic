mojo:/ˈmoʊdʒoʊ/ (US) /ˈməʊdʒəʊ/ (UK)| n.|魔力; 运气; 魅力; 吸引力|A magic charm, talisman, or spell; also, a quality that attracts people to you and makes you successful and full of energy.|He's lost his mojo. 他已经失去了他的魔力。|Synonyms: charisma, magic, charm, appeal, magnetism|Antonyms: apathy, dullness|noun
