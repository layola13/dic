gaggle:/ˈɡæɡəl/| n.|一群（鹅），咯咯叫声|A flock of geese, especially when on the move.|A gaggle of geese waddled across the road. 一群鹅摇摇摆摆地穿过马路。|The gaggle of tourists followed the guide closely.  一群游客紧跟着导游。|synonyms: flock, group, cluster, crowd|antonyms: individual, single|noun


gaggle:/ˈɡæɡəl/| v.|（鹅）发出咯咯的叫声|To make the characteristic crying sound of a goose.|The geese gaggled loudly as they flew overhead.  鹅群飞过头顶时大声地咯咯叫。|The children gaggled with laughter. 孩子们咯咯地笑着。|synonyms: cackle, honk, squawk|antonyms: whisper, murmur|verb
