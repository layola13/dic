overcull: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌl/
verb
过度捕杀，过量剔除
To cull or remove more than is necessary or desirable.
The deer population was overculled last year, leading to a shortage this year. 去年鹿群被过度捕杀，导致今年鹿群数量短缺。
Overfishing has led to the overculling of several species of fish. 过度捕捞导致几种鱼类被过度捕杀。
Synonyms: overharvest, overcrop
Antonyms: undercull
