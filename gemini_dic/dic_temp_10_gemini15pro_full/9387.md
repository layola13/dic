antique:/ænˈtiːk/ | adj. n. | 古董的；古老的；古董 | Belonging to or typical of an earlier period.  |  例句：She collects antique furniture. 她收集古董家具。 | 近义词：ancient, vintage, old, archaic | 反义词：modern, contemporary, new | 词性：adjective, noun

antique:/ænˈtiːk/ | n. | 古董 | A collectible object such as a piece of furniture or work of art that has a high value because of its considerable age. | 例句：This clock is a genuine antique.  这个钟是真古董。 | 近义词：relic, artifact | 反义词：modernity | 词性：noun
