extramural:/ˌɛkstrəˈmjʊrəl/
adjective
校外的；墙外的；城外的
Situated or done outside the walls or boundaries of a building or institution, especially a college or university.
例句：
She is involved in many extramural activities. 她参与许多校外活动。

He took an extramural course in philosophy. 他参加了一个校外哲学课程。

近义词：outside, external, outer
反义词：intramural, internal, inner
词性：adjective

extramural:/ˌɛkstrəˈmjʊrəl/
noun
校外课程；校外活动
An activity or course of study that is not part of the regular curriculum of a school or college.
例句：
The university offers a wide range of extramurals. 这所大学提供各种各样的校外课程。

He participates in extramurals to broaden his horizons. 他参加校外活动来拓宽视野。

近义词：outside activity, external program
反义词：intramural activity, internal program
词性：noun
