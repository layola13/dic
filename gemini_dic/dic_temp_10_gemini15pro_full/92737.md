ichthulin:/ɪkˈθjuːlɪn/
n.
鱼精蛋白
a phosphoprotein found in the eggs of bony fishes, especially the salmon.
Salmon eggs are a good source of ichthulin.  鲑鱼卵是鱼精蛋白的良好来源。
Synonyms: vitellin  
Antonyms: None
noun
