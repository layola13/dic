nonenviable:/ˌnɒnˈɛnviəbl/| adj.|不值得羡慕的，令人不快的|Not desirable; unpleasant or difficult.|例句：He was in the nonenviable position of having to choose between two equally bad options. 他处于不得不从两个同样糟糕的选择中做出选择的令人不快的境地。

例句：She had the nonenviable task of cleaning up after the party. 她承担了派对后清理的苦差事。|近义词：undesirable, unpleasant, unfortunate, difficult|反义词：enviable, desirable, fortunate, pleasant|词性:形容词
词源:non- (not) + enviable (worthy of envy)
