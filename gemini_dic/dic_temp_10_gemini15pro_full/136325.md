nonrescission:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈsɪʒən/
n.
不撤销，不废除
The nonrescission of the contract means it remains in effect.  合同的不撤销意味着它仍然有效。
The nonrescission of the agreement caused further complications.  协议的不废除导致了进一步的复杂化。
Synonyms: continuation, upholding, maintenance
Antonyms: rescission, cancellation, revocation
