ants:/ænts/ | n. |蚂蚁|Small, social insects that live in colonies.|例句：The ants are marching one by one. 蚂蚁一只接一只地行进。|例句：Ants work together to build their nests. 蚂蚁一起工作建造它们的巢穴。|近义词：emmets|反义词:|名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *amtes, *antes, from Old English ǣmette (“ant”), from Proto-West Germanic *āmaittī, from Proto-Germanic *amaitjō (“ant”), diminutive of *amaiō (“ant”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂em- (“to take, distribute”). Cognate with West Frisian eam, Dutch emt, German Ame (“ant”).
