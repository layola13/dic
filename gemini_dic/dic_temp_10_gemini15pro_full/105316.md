kiblah:/ˈkɪblə/ (US), /ˈqɪblə/ (UK)| n.|克拜, 朝向|The direction that should be faced when a Muslim prays (towards the Kaaba in Mecca).|Muslims pray facing the kiblah.  穆斯林祈祷时面向克拜。|The imam indicated the kiblah with his hand.  伊玛目用手指出克拜的方向。|Synonyms: qibla, qiblah|Antonyms: N/A|noun
