melodie:/ˈmɛlədi/ | n. | 旋律; 曲调; 歌曲 |A sequence of single notes that is musically satisfying.  A tune.| She hummed a sweet melodie. 她哼唱了一首甜美的曲子。 | synonym: tune, air, theme, song | antonym: dissonance, cacophony | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *melodie*, from Late Latin *melodia*, from Greek *melodía* "singing, chanting, a song, choral song," from *melos* "song, strain, limb" + *oide* "song, ode."
