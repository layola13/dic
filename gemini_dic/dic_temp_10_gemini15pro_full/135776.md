nonprogram:/nɒnˈprəʊɡræm/ | adj. | 非节目的，非编排的 | Not included in a regular broadcast schedule; not pre-arranged or planned.  | The nonprogram material included commercials and station identifications. 非节目内容包括广告和电台标识。|  The meeting was a nonprogram event, organized spontaneously by the employees.  这次会议是非正式的，是由员工自发组织的。| 近义词：unscheduled, unplanned, impromptu | 反义词：programmed, scheduled, planned | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "program" (from Greek "programma" meaning "a public notice")
