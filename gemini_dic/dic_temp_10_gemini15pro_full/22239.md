bonders:/ˈbɒndəz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndərz/ (US)| n.| 保税人；担保人；建立联系的人|People who create bonds, either financial or emotional.  Those who work in a bonded warehouse or engage in bonding activities. |The bonders ensured the goods were stored securely until duties were paid. 保税人员确保货物安全存放，直到缴纳关税。| The group quickly became bonders over their shared love of hiking. 这群人因为共同热爱徒步旅行而迅速建立了联系。 | synonyms: guarantors, sureties, connectors, linkers | antonyms: dividers, separators | noun
