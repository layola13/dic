curviest:/ˈkɜːrviɪst/| adj.|最弯曲的，曲线最美的| having the most curves; most shapely. |例句：She was voted the curviest actress in Hollywood. 她被选为好莱坞身材最火辣的女演员。|近义词：shapely, voluptuous, curvy|反义词:straight, linear|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From curve + -y + -est.  "Curve" ultimately comes from Latin "curvus" meaning "bent, curved." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives indicating having the quality of, and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
