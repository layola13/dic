nonprovisional:/ˌnɒnprəˈvɪʒənəl/ | adj. | 非临时性的，最终的，不可撤销的 | Not provisional; final or absolute; not subject to change or revocation. |  The patent office issued a nonprovisional patent after a thorough review. 专利局在彻底审查后颁发了最终专利。

The nonprovisional application must contain a full and clear description of the invention. 非临时申请必须包含对本发明的完整清晰的描述。


近义词：final, absolute, conclusive, definitive | 反义词: provisional, temporary, conditional | 词性：adjective
