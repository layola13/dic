passbook:/ˈpɑːsbʊk/ /ˈpæsbʊk/| n. |存折|A small book issued by a bank or other financial institution to a depositor, recording deposits, withdrawals, and interest earned.  A document showing a person's identity and allowing them to pass through a checkpoint or barrier (dated).|例句：She kept all her passbooks in a locked drawer. 她把所有的存折都锁在抽屉里。

例句：He presented his passbook at the teller window. 他在柜台窗口出示了他的存折。


近义词：bank book, savings book|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "debit card," "credit card," or "online banking" as functionally contrasting terms) |词性:noun
