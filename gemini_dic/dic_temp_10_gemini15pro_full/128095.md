nesslerize:/ˈnɛsləˌraɪz/
verb
奈斯勒化，用奈斯勒试剂检验
to treat with Nessler's reagent, especially to detect the presence of ammonia.
The water sample was nesslerized to determine the ammonia concentration.  将水样进行奈斯勒化以测定氨浓度。
Nessler's reagent was added to nesslerize the solution. 奈斯勒试剂被添加到溶液中进行奈斯勒化。
Synonyms: test for ammonia
Antonyms: N/A
词性: v.
