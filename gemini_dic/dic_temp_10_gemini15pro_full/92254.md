hypervariability:/ˌhaɪpərˌvɛəriəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 高变异性，高度变异性 |  The quality or state of being hypervariable; extreme or excessive variability.  Specifically used in genetics to describe regions of DNA that are highly polymorphic. | The hypervariability of this gene makes it a useful marker for population studies.  这种基因的高度变异性使其成为种群研究的有用标记。 | Near synonyms:  polymorphism, variability, mutability | Antonym:  stability | noun

hypervariable:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛəriəbl/ | adj. | 高变的，高度变异的 | Exhibiting hypervariability; highly or excessively variable.  In genetics, refers to a gene or DNA sequence that exhibits a high degree of polymorphism. | The hypervariable regions of antibodies allow them to bind to a wide range of antigens. 抗体的高变区使它们能够与各种抗原结合。 | Near synonyms: polymorphic, variable, mutable | Antonym: stable | adjective
