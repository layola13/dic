chlorometry:/ˌklɔːˈrɒmɪtri/ | n. | 氯量法；氯定量法 | The determination of the amount of chlorine in a compound.  It is a chemical analysis method used to determine the concentration of chlorine or chlorine-containing substances in a sample. |  Chlorometry was used to determine the chlorine content of the swimming pool water.  用氯量法测定了游泳池水的氯含量。|Synonyms: chlorimetric analysis, chlorine titration | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
