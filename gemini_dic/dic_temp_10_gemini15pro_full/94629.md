imprescriptible:/ˌɪmprɪˈskrɪptəbl/
adjective
不可剥夺的；不可废除的；不因时效而消灭的
Not subject to prescription; incapable of being lost or extinguished by the passage of time or by failure to enforce a right.
The Declaration of Independence speaks of  imprescriptible rights to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness.  《独立宣言》阐述了生命权、自由权和追求幸福的不可剥夺的权利。
The constitution guarantees the imprescriptible rights of its citizens. 宪法保障其公民的不可剥夺的权利。

近义词:inalienable, unassailable, inviolable
反义词:forfeitable, revocable, prescriptible
词性:adjective
