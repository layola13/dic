myxomatized:/ˌmɪksəˈmətaɪzd/ | adj. |感染粘液瘤的，患粘液瘤病的|Affected with myxomatosis, a highly infectious and usually fatal viral disease of rabbits.|The farmer found several myxomatized rabbits in his field. 农民在他的田里发现了幾只感染粘液瘤的兔子。|Synonyms: infected (with myxomatosis) | Antonyms: healthy, uninfected | Word Type: Adjective

myxoma:/mɪkˈsəʊmə/ | n. |粘液瘤|A benign tumor of connective tissue containing gelatinous or mucoid material.|The doctor diagnosed the growth as a myxoma. 医生诊断该增生为粘液瘤。|Synonyms:  mucoid tumor, mesenchymal tumor | Antonyms: N/A | Word Type: Noun

-oma:/ˈəʊmə/ | suffix |瘤，肿块|A suffix used in medicine to form the names of tumors and other abnormal growths.|Examples: carcinoma, sarcoma, melanoma | Synonyms: N/A | Antonyms: N/A | Word Type: Suffix


Etymology:

From myxoma + -atized.  "Myxoma" comes from the Greek "muxa" meaning "mucus" and "-oma" meaning "tumor" or "mass".  "-atized" is a suffix indicating the state of being affected by something.
