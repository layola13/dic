goldilocks-planet:/ˈɡoʊldɪˌlɑːks ˈplænɪt/| n. |宜居行星|A planet located within a star's habitable zone, where the temperature is just right – not too hot nor too cold – for liquid water to exist on the surface, making it potentially suitable for life.|Scientists are searching for goldilocks planets in other solar systems. 科学家们正在其他太阳系中寻找宜居行星。|近义词：habitable planet|反义词：uninhabitable planet|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From the children's story "Goldilocks and the Three Bears," in which Goldilocks finds a bowl of porridge that is "just right" – not too hot or too cold.
