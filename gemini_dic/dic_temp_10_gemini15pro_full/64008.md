epistrophe:/ɪˈpɪstrəfi/ /ɪˈpɪstroʊfi/
n.
首语重复法；末语重复
Repetition of a word or phrase at the end of successive clauses, sentences, or verses.
"Government of the people, by the people, for the people shall not perish from the earth." – Abraham Lincoln.  (民有、民治、民享的政府永世长存。– 亚伯拉罕·林肯。)
repetition, anaphora, epiphora, conduplicatio
anadiplosis
noun
