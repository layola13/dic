deferrers:/dɪˈfɜːrərz/
n.
延期者; 缓办者; 拖延者
People who defer or postpone something.  Those who put off doing something.
The deferrers of student loan payments will eventually have to pay.  那些推迟偿还学生贷款的人最终还是要还款的。
Congress criticized the deferrers of crucial legislation. 国会批评了那些拖延关键立法的人。
Synonyms: procrastinators, postponers, delayers
Antonyms: expediters, agilists
