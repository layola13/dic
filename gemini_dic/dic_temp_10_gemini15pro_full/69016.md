fattens:/ˈfætənz/
verb
使…长肥；使…变胖
makes something become fatter.
The farmer fattens his pigs for market.  农民养肥他的猪以便出售。
近义词:plumps, expands
反义词:thins, shrinks
词性:v

词源: From Old English *fætian "to make fat," from *fæt "fat"
