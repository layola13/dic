curateship:/ˈkjʊərətʃɪp/ |n.| 馆长[策展人]的职位；策展工作|The curatorship of the museum's modern art collection is a prestigious position.  博物馆现代艺术收藏的策展工作是一个享有盛誉的职位。|Synonym: curation, management, directorship | Antonym: neglect, abandonment | noun
etymology: From curate +‎ -ship. Ultimately from Latin cūrātus (“cared for”), past participle of cūrō (“I care for”).
