convertive:/kənˈvɜːtɪv/ | adj. | 有转换能力的; 皈依的 | Having the power or tendency to convert or change something; relating to religious conversion. |

例句：
The convertive power of solar panels turns sunlight into electricity.  太阳能电池板的转换能力将阳光转化为电能。

His convertive experience led him to a life of religious devotion. 他的皈依经历使他过上了虔诚的宗教生活。


近义词：transformative, changing, converting, proselytizing |
反义词：unchanging, static, resistant |
词性: adjective


convertive (n.): /kənˈvɜːtɪv/ | n. | 皈依者 | A person who has been converted to a different religion or belief.  |

例句：
The new convertive embraced his newfound faith with enthusiasm.  这位新皈依者热情地拥抱了他新发现的信仰。

The group of convertives met regularly to study their new religion. 这群皈依者定期聚会学习他们的新宗教。

近义词：convert, proselyte |
反义词：apostate |
词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *convertīvus, from Latin convertere "to turn around, change."
