anticodonic:/ˌæntikəˈdɒnɪk/ /ˌæntikaˈdɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 反密码子的 | Relating to or involving an anticodon.  An anticodon is a sequence of three nucleotides forming a unit of genetic code in a transfer RNA molecule, corresponding to a complementary codon in messenger RNA. | The anticodonic loop is crucial for the accurate translation of genetic information. 反密码子环对于遗传信息的准确翻译至关重要。|  The anticodonic sequence pairs with the codon on the mRNA. 反密码子序列与mRNA上的密码子配对。 |  complementary, corresponding | antonymous (not a standardly used term) | adjective

anticodon:/ˌæntikəˈdɒn/ /ˌæntikaˈdɑːn/| n. | 反密码子 | A sequence of three nucleotides forming a unit of genetic code in a transfer RNA molecule, corresponding to a complementary codon in messenger RNA. | The anticodon recognizes and binds to the codon. 反密码子识别并结合密码子。 | The anticodon ensures the correct amino acid is added to the growing polypeptide chain. 反密码子确保将正确的氨基酸添加到不断增长的多肽链中。| codon complement, complementary sequence |  (no direct antonym, conceptually the absence of an anticodon would prevent translation) | noun
