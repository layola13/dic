The critic's mordicant review of the play left the actors devastated.  评论家对这部戏的尖刻评论让演员们崩溃了。| 近义词：caustic, biting, acrimonious, trenchant, cutting, sharp, sarcastic, sardonic | 反义词:  gentle, kind, mild, complimentary, bland | 词性: 形容词
