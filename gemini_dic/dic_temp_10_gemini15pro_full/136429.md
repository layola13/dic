nonretroactivity:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊækˈtɪvɪti/ | /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊækˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 不溯及既往；非追溯性 | The principle of nonretroactivity prevents laws from being applied to past actions.  不溯及既往原则防止法律应用于过去的行为。 |  例句：The new tax law has nonretroactivity, so it won't affect your previous tax returns. 新的税法不溯及既往，所以它不会影响你以前的纳税申报单。 | 近义词：irretroactivity, prospectivity | 反义词: retroactivity, retrospectivity | noun

nonretroactive:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊˈæktɪv/ | /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 不溯及既往的；非追溯的 |  A nonretroactive law only applies to actions taken after it comes into effect.  不溯及既往的法律只适用于其生效后采取的行动。 | 例句：The change to the policy is nonretroactive.  这项政策的改变是不溯及既往的。 | 近义词：irretroactive, prospective | 反义词: retroactive, retrospective | adjective
