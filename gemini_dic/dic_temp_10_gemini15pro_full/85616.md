haul-up:/ˈhɔːl ʌp/ | n. |停顿；检查；大修；拖上岸|A haul-up is a stop or check, often for repairs or maintenance, or the act of pulling something ashore. |The fishermen had a haul-up on the beach to mend their nets. 渔民们在沙滩上停下来修补渔网。|The ship was due for a haul-up in dry dock. 这艘船预定要进干船坞大修。|近义词：check, inspection, overhaul, pulling ashore|反义词:continuation, launch|词性:名词

haul-up:/ˈhɔːl ʌp/ | v. |拖起；拉起；使停下；逮捕|To haul up something means to pull it upwards or to force it to stop.  It can also mean to arrest someone.|They hauled up the anchor. 他们拉起了锚。|The police hauled him up for questioning. 警察把他拘捕审问。|近义词：pull up, raise, stop, arrest|反义词:lower, release|词性:动词
