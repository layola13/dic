einsteinian:/aɪnˈstaɪniən/
adjective
爱因斯坦的；爱因斯坦学说的；(理论等)深奥的
Of, relating to, or being the theories of relativity of Albert Einstein.  Also, exceptionally intelligent or innovative; brilliant.
例句：His theories were so complex they seemed almost Einsteinian. 他的理论如此复杂，几乎像是爱因斯坦的学说。

例句：She possessed an Einsteinian intellect. 她拥有爱因斯坦般非凡的才智。

近义词：brilliant, genius, revolutionary, groundbreaking, insightful
反义词：unoriginal, mundane, conventional, simple
词性: adjective

词源: From the proper noun "Einstein" (referring to Albert Einstein) + the suffix "-ian" (used to form adjectives meaning "related to" or "characteristic of").
