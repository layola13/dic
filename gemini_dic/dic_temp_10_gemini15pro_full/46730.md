dandies: /ˈdændiz/ (US), /ˈdændiːz/ (UK)| n. | 花花公子，纨绔子弟 | A man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance. |  The dandies of the 19th century were known for their elaborate outfits. 19世纪的花花公子以其精致的服装而闻名。

The club was full of dandies in their finest suits.  俱乐部里挤满了穿着最漂亮西装的花花公子。 | 近义词: fop, coxcomb, swell, dude | 反义词: slob, ragamuffin | 词性: noun (plural)
