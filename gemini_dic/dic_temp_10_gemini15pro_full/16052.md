bandh:/bʌnd/  /bɑːnd/ | n. | 罢工，停工，关闭 | A general strike, especially in India, observed as a protest. |  India observed a nationwide bandh to protest the government's policies. 印度举行了全国性的罢工，抗议政府的政策。|  strike, shutdown, protest, hartal | reopening, work  | noun
Etymology: From Hindi बन्ध (bandh), from Sanskrit बन्ध (bandha, "binding, tying").
