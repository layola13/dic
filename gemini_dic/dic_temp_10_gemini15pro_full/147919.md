overregulation:/ˌoʊvərˌreɡjuˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 过度管制，过度调节 | The act of regulating something too much, often with negative consequences.  It often refers to the application of grammatical rules in situations where they don't apply, especially by children. |  The government's overregulation of the market stifled innovation. 政府对市场的过度管制扼杀了创新。|  Overregulation of children's behavior can hinder their development. 对儿童行为的过度监管会阻碍他们的发展。 | overcontrol, excessive regulation | deregulation, underregulation | noun
