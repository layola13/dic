expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/
verb
阐述，详细解释
To explain or interpret something in detail; to clarify or elaborate on a subject.
The professor expounded on the theory of relativity. 教授详细阐述了相对论。
近义词: explain, elaborate, clarify, interpret, illustrate
反义词: summarize, condense, abbreviate
词性: verb
词源: From Latin *exponere* "to put forth, set forth, explain," from *ex* "out" + *ponere* "to put, place."
