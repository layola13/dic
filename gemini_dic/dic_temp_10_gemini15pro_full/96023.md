indagate:/ɪnˈdæɡeɪt/ | v. |调查，查究|To inquire or investigate thoroughly; to examine systematically.|The detective will indagate the crime scene. 侦探将调查犯罪现场。|例句：The journalist indagated the corruption scandal. 这位记者调查了腐败丑闻。|例句：Researchers indagated the causes of the disease. 研究人员调查了这种疾病的起因。|近义词：investigate, examine, probe, explore, scrutinize|反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook|词性：动词

词源: From Latin indagare "to track, trace out, investigate," from in- "in" + dagare "to drive, hunt," related to sagire "to perceive keenly or acutely."
