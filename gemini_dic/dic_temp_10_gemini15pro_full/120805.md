misassertion:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrʃən/
n.
错误的断言；虚假的声明
An incorrect or untrue statement; a false assertion.
The lawyer pointed out the misassertion in the witness's testimony.  律师指出了证人证词中的错误断言。
Synonyms:  falsehood, untruth, lie, fabrication, misstatement, inaccuracy
Antonyms: truth, fact, accuracy, correctness
noun
