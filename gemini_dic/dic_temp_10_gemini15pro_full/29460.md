catalytic:/ˌkætəlɪtɪk/| adj.|催化的；促进的|Relating to or involving catalysis.  A catalytic agent is a substance that increases the rate of a chemical reaction without itself undergoing any permanent chemical change.|The converter in a car's exhaust system plays a catalytic role in reducing harmful emissions. 汽车排气系统中的转换器在减少有害排放方面起催化作用。|The president's speech had a catalytic effect on the peace talks. 总统的讲话对和平谈判起了促进作用。|Synonyms: accelerative,  facilitative,  stimulating|Antonyms: inhibitive, hindering, retarding|adjective
