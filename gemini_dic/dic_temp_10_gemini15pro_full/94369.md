implantation:/ɪmplɑːnˈteɪʃn/ /ɪmplænˈteɪʃn/
n.
植入；移植；着床；灌输
the process of implanting something, or the state of being implanted.
The implantation of the embryo in the uterus is a crucial stage of pregnancy.  胚胎在子宫内的着床是怀孕的关键阶段。

The implantation of the new system went smoothly. 新系统的实施进展顺利。


近义词:insertion, embedding, grafting
反义词:removal, extraction
词性:noun
