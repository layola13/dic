fluid:/ˈfluːɪd/ /ˈfluːɪd/| adj. n.|流动的，流畅的；液体，流体|Something that is fluid flows easily and is not solid.  A fluid is a liquid or gas.|The artist's style is fluid and expressive. 这位艺术家的风格流畅且富有表现力。|Synonyms: liquid, flowing, smooth, adaptable|Antonyms: solid, rigid, inflexible|adjective

fluid:/ˈfluːɪd/ /ˈfluːɪd/| n.|液体，流体|A substance that flows freely, such as a liquid or gas.|The mechanic checked the car's fluids. 机械师检查了汽车的液体。|Synonyms: liquid, gas, solution|Antonyms: solid|noun
