dumpily:/ˈdʌmpɪli/| adv.|矮胖地；笨拙地|In a short, thick, and clumsy manner.|He walked dumpily across the room. 他笨拙地走过房间。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, heavily|反义词：gracefully, elegantly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dump + -ily.  "Dump" originally referred to a dull thud or a shapeless mass. The adverbial suffix "-ily" transforms the adjective "dumpy" into an adverb indicating the manner of action.
