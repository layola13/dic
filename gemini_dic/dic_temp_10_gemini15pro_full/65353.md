eulogistical: /ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪkəl/
adj.
赞美的，颂扬的
Praising highly; laudatory.
He delivered a eulogistical speech about his mentor. 他发表了歌颂他导师的演讲。
近义词: laudatory, complimentary, praising, panegyrical
反义词: critical, derogatory, defamatory
词性: 形容词

eulogy: /ˈjuːlədʒi/
n.
悼词，颂词，赞美
A speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died.
The priest delivered a moving eulogy at the funeral. 牧师在葬礼上致了感人的悼词。
近义词: tribute, panegyric, encomium, accolade
反义词: criticism, condemnation, denunciation
词性: 名词

词源:
From French *élogistique*  and directly from Late Latin *eulogius*, from Greek *eulogia* "praise", from *eu-* "well" + *-logia* "speaking".
