overspender:/ˈoʊvərˌspɛndər/
n.
挥霍者；过度消费的人
Someone who spends more money than they should.
He's an overspender and always in debt. 他是个挥霍无度的人，总是负债累累。
近义词：spendthrift, prodigal, squanderer
反义词：saver, economizer
词性：名词

overspend:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛnd/
v.
过度消费；超支
To spend more money than one can afford or than is necessary.
They overspent their budget on the vacation. 他们在度假上超支了。
近义词：splurge, squander
反义词：save, economize
词性：动词
