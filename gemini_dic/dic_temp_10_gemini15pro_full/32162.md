chews:/tʃuːz/
|n.|咀嚼的动作或声音 (罕用)|The chews of the horse were loud. 马咀嚼的声音很大。(较不自然)|
|例句：The repetitive chews of the gum annoyed me.  口香糖反复咀嚼的声音让我很烦。(较不自然)|
|例句：We could hear the chews of the cattle in the barn. 我们可以听到牛棚里牛咀嚼的声音。(较不自然)|
|近义词：bites, gnawing, mastication|
|反义词：swallowing, gulping|
|词性：noun (名词)|


词源: From Middle English *chewen, from Old English cēowan (“to chew”), from Proto-Germanic *kewwaną (“to chew”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵyeuh₂- (“to chew”). Cognate with Scots cheu (“to chew”), West Frisian kôgje (“to chew, ruminate”), Dutch kauwen (“to chew”), German kauen (“to chew”), Danish tygge (“to chew”), Swedish tugga (“to chew”), Icelandic tyggja (“to chew”).
