optometry: /ɒpˈtɒmɪtri/ (UK) /ɑːpˈtɑːmətri/ (US) | n. | 验光；验光学 | The science and practice of examining the eyes, refracting, and prescribing corrective lenses or other visual aids. |  I'm studying optometry because I want to help people see better. 我正在学习验光，因为我想帮助人们看得更清楚。

She went to the school of optometry for a check-up. 她去验光学校做检查。 | 近义词: ophthalmology, eye care | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *optométrie*, from Ancient Greek ὄψις (ópsis, “sight, view”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
