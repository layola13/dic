dimensioning:/daɪˈmɛnʃənɪŋ/ /dɪˈmɛnʃənɪŋ/
n.|尺寸标注，标注尺寸|The process of adding dimensions to a drawing or model.|The engineer spent hours dimensioning the blueprint. 工程师花了几个小时标注蓝图的尺寸。|measurement, sizing|reduction|noun
dimension:/daɪˈmɛnʃən/ /dɪˈmɛnʃən/
n.|维度，尺寸，方面|A measurable extent of some kind, such as length, breadth, depth, or height.|The dimensions of the room are 10 feet by 12 feet. 房间的尺寸是10英尺乘12英尺。|size, magnitude, aspect|insignificance|noun
