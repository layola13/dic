castaway:/ˈkæstəweɪ/| n. adj.| 被抛弃的人，漂流者；被抛弃的，遭难的|A person who has been shipwrecked and stranded on a deserted island or coast.  A person or thing that has been rejected or discarded.|例句：The castaway survived for months on coconuts and fish.  那个漂流者靠椰子鱼活了好几个月。

例句：The old toys were castaway in the attic.  那些旧玩具被丢弃在阁楼里。|近义词：shipwrecked, marooned, stranded, derelict, abandoned|反义词:rescued, saved|词性: noun, adjective
cast away:/ˌkæst əˈweɪ/| v.| 抛弃，丢弃|To throw or drop something from a ship or aircraft. To discard or reject someone or something.|例句：The sailors cast away the damaged cargo.  水手们把损坏的货物扔掉了。

例句：He cast away his old habits and started anew.  他抛弃了旧习惯，重新开始。|近义词：discard, jettison, abandon, reject|反义词:keep, retain|词性: verb
