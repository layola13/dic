crispiness:/ˈkrɪspɪnəs/| n.|酥脆，脆度|The crispiness of the bacon was perfect. 培根的酥脆程度恰到好处。|例句：I love the crispiness of freshly baked cookies. 我喜欢刚烤好的饼干的酥脆口感。|近义词：crunchiness, brittleness|反义词：softness, chewiness|词性:名词
Etymology: From crispy + -ness.  "Crispy" comes from the Middle English "crysp," meaning "curled," likely of imitative origin.
