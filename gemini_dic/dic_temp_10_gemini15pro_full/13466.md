atrociousness:/əˈtroʊʃəsnəs/
noun
残暴；凶恶；骇人听闻
The quality of being atrocious; extreme wickedness or cruelty.
The atrociousness of the crime shocked the nation.  这起罪行的残暴震惊了全国。
The witness testified to the atrociousness of the dictator's regime.  证人证明了独裁者政权的残暴。
atrocity, barbarity, heinousness, savagery, wickedness
kindness, goodness, benevolence, compassion, humanity
