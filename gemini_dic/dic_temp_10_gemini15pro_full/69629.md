feministic:/fɛmɪˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
女权主义的；拥护女权主义的
Relating to or supporting feminism.
She made some feministic remarks about the portrayal of women in the media. 她对媒体中女性的形象发表了一些女权主义的评论。
Her views are quite feministic. 她的观点很女权主义。
Near Synonyms: feminist
Antonyms: anti-feminist
词性: 形容词
