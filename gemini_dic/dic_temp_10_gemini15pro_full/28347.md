carapaces:/ˈkærəpeɪsɪz/| n. |甲壳，背甲|The hard upper shell of a turtle, crustacean, or arachnid.|例句：The turtle retreated into its carapace.  乌龟缩进了它的壳里。

例句：The crab's carapace was a vibrant blue.  螃蟹的背甲是鲜艳的蓝色。|近义词：shell, covering, exoskeleton |反义词:underbelly|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French *carapace, alteration of escavace "husk, shell, pod" (perhaps influenced by Spanish carapacho "tortoise shell"), from Late Latin scarafaius "kind of crab."
