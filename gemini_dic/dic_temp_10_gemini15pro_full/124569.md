mourner:/ˈmɔːrnər/ (US), /ˈmɔːnə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 哀悼者；送葬者 | A person who grieves or shows sorrow, especially at a funeral. |  She was a mourner at his funeral.  她参加了他的葬礼，是一位哀悼者。

| Example: Many mourners attended the funeral to pay their respects. 许多哀悼者参加了葬礼以示敬意。

| Example: The mourners wore black and wept openly.  哀悼者们穿着黑衣，公开哭泣。

| Synonyms: griever, bereaved, lamenter | Antonyms: celebrant, reveler | Noun
