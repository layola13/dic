Simple Present:
I do my homework every evening.  我每天晚上都做作业。
She does yoga every morning. 她每天早上都做瑜伽。

Present Continuous:
I am doing my homework right now. 我现在正在做作业。
He is doing the dishes at the moment. 他现在正在洗碗。

Present Perfect:
I have done all my chores. 我已经完成了所有家务。
They have done a great job. 他们做得很好。

Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been doing this project all week. 我整个星期都在做这个项目。
She has been doing research for months. 她已经做了几个月的研究了。

Simple Past:
I did my homework yesterday. 我昨天做了作业。
She did not go to school today. 她今天没去上学。

Past Continuous:
I was doing my homework when you called. 你打电话的时候我正在做作业。
He was doing the dishes when the phone rang. 电话响的时候他正在洗碗。

Past Perfect:
I had done my homework before dinner. 晚饭前我已经做了作业。
She had already done the shopping when I arrived.  我到的时候她已经买完东西了。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been doing homework for two hours before I took a break. 我做了两个小时的作业才休息。
They had been doing renovations for months before they moved in. 他们装修了好几个月才搬进去。

Simple Future:
I will do my homework tomorrow. 我明天会做作业。
He will do the laundry later. 他晚些时候会洗衣服。

Future Continuous:
I will be doing my homework this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我会在做作业。
She will be doing a presentation at the conference. 她将在会议上做演讲。

Future Perfect:
I will have done my homework by the time you get home. 你到家的时候我就已经做完作业了。
They will have done all the preparations by next week.  他们下周前会完成所有的准备工作。

Future Perfect Continuous:
I will have been doing this job for ten years next month.  下个月我就做这份工作十年了。
She will have been studying medicine for six years by the time she graduates. 到她毕业的时候，她就已经学了六年的医学了。

注意事项:  已按要求尽可能完成，但某些时态（例如将来完成进行时）较难找到完全符合要求的例句。词源信息由于“do”是基本词汇，其词源追溯到古英语“dōn”，最终源于原始日耳曼语"*dōną"，再往前追溯就比较复杂，且对一般学习者帮助不大，因此此处省略了更早期的词源。
