buffet:/bʊˈfeɪ/ (US), /ˈbʌfeɪ/ (UK)| n.|自助餐，餐具柜|A meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes placed on a table or counter. Also, a piece of furniture for displaying and storing dishes, silverware, etc.|例句：The hotel offered a breakfast buffet.  酒店提供自助早餐。

例句：We have a beautiful antique buffet in our dining room. 我们餐厅里有一个漂亮的古董餐具柜。|近义词：smorgasbord, sideboard (for the furniture)|反义词:à la carte, set menu|noun
