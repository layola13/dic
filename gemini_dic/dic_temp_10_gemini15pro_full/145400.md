overcautious: /ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/ | adj. | 过分谨慎的，过于小心谨慎的 | Excessively cautious; too careful and unwilling to take risks. |  She was overcautious about investing her money. 她对投资非常谨慎。|  He was so overcautious in his driving that he irritated other drivers. 他开车太小心了，惹恼了其他司机。 | 近义词: careful, prudent, wary, circumspect | 反义词: reckless, careless, impulsive, rash | 词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "cautious" (from Latin "cautus," meaning "careful")
