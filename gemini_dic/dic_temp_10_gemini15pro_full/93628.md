imblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/
verb
中文翻译：纹章；炫耀；使华丽；刻印
英文解析：To decorate or adorn richly; to display prominently or vividly.
例句：The knight's shield was imblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金色的狮子。

例句：Her name was imblazoned across the headlines. 她的名字赫然印在报纸头条上。

近义词：adorn, decorate, embellish, display, exhibit
反义词：obscure, conceal, hide
词性：v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emblaisoner, from en- "in" + blason "coat of arms, shield".
