hyperparoxysm:/ˌhaɪpərˈpærəkˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 剧烈发作，阵发加剧 | A sudden, violent, and uncontrollable outburst or intensification of a paroxysm (e.g., of laughter, rage, or pain). It represents an extreme degree of a sudden attack or spasm. |  He experienced a hyperparoxysm of laughter upon hearing the joke. 他听到笑话后一阵狂笑。| Near synonyms: convulsion, fit, seizure, spasm | Antonyms: calmness, tranquility | noun
