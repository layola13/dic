hermitship:/ˈhɜːmɪtʃɪp/ |n.|隐居生活；遁世；隐居之处|The life of a hermit, characterized by seclusion and solitude.  A place where a hermit lives.|He sought hermitism in the remote mountains. 他在偏远的山区寻求隐居生活。|Synonyms: seclusion, solitude, isolation, retirement, retreat|Antonyms: sociability, gregariousness, companionship, community|noun
