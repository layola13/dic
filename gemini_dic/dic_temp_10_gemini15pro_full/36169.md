cofferdam:/ˈkɒfərdæm/ (UK), /ˈkɔːfərdæm/ (US)| n.|围堰，沉箱|A watertight enclosure pumped dry to permit construction work below the waterline, such as building bridge piers or repairing a ship's hull.|例句：The engineers built a cofferdam to allow them to work on the foundations of the bridge. 工程师们建造了一个围堰，以便他们能够在桥梁的地基上工作。

例句：The cofferdam protected the workers from the strong currents of the river. 围堰保护了工人们免受河流强水流的侵袭。|近义词：caisson, dry dock|反义词: None|词性: noun
