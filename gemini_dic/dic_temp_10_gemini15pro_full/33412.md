churchliness:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃlɪnɪs/
n.
虔诚；宗教气息；教堂风格
The quality of being churchly; adherence to church practices and traditions.
The churchliness of the service was impressive.  这场仪式的虔诚令人印象深刻。
近义词：piety, devoutness, religiosity
反义词：irreverence, impiety, worldliness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From churchly + -ness.  Churchly from church + -ly.  Church from Old English *cirice, ultimately from Greek kyriakon "the Lord's (house)."
