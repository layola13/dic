parasphenoid: /ˌpærəˈsfɛnɔɪd/ | n. | 副蝶骨；翼蝶骨 | A bone forming part of the base of the cranium in many vertebrates, including fishes and amphibians. It is situated ventral to the braincase and often extends forward between the orbits. |  The parasphenoid in this fossil fish is remarkably well-preserved.  这条化石鱼的副蝶骨保存得非常好。|  synonyms:  pterygoid (in some contexts) | antonyms: (No direct antonyms.  Anatomical terms rarely have true opposites.) | noun
