canonises:/ˈkænənaɪzɪz/
verb
正式宣布（死者）为圣徒；将…奉为圣徒；高度赞扬
officially declare (a dead person) a saint; regard as being above reproach
The Catholic Church canonises saints. 天主教会册封圣徒。
Near Synonyms: exalt, glorify, deify, enshrine
Antonyms: condemn, denounce, vilify
词性: 动词
