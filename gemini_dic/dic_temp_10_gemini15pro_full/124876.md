例句：Mudbox is a powerful tool for 3D artists.  Mudbox对于3D艺术家来说是一个强大的工具。


近义词：ZBrush, Sculptris | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From "mud" (referring to the clay-like manipulation of digital models) + "box" (suggesting a contained environment for working).  Essentially, it evokes the idea of a digital equivalent of a sculptor's workbench.
