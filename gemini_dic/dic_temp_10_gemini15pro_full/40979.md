coordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ /kəʊˈɔːdɪnət/| adj. n. v.|同等的，并列的；坐标；协调，配合|Of equal rank or importance; a number representing a position; to bring into common action, movement, or condition.|例句：The two departments need to coordinate their efforts. 这两个部门需要协调他们的工作。|近义词：harmonize, synchronize, integrate, match, align|反义词:clash, conflict|词性:adjective, noun, verb

coordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnət/ /kəʊˈɔːdɪnət/| adj. n. v.|同等的，并列的；坐标；协调，配合|Of equal rank or importance; a number representing a position; to bring into common action, movement, or condition.|例句：We use coordinates to locate points on a map. 我们使用坐标来在地图上定位。|近义词：harmonize, synchronize, integrate, match, align|反义词:clash, conflict|词性:adjective, noun, verb

Etymology: From Late Latin  *coōrdinātus*, past participle of *coōrdināre* "to arrange in order," from *com-* "together" + *ōrdināre* "to arrange."
