glossist:/ˈɡlɒsɪst/ | n. |注释者；词汇表编纂者|A person who writes glosses or glossaries; a commentator. |The glossist provided helpful explanations of the difficult terms in the text. 注释者对文本中的难词提供了有益的解释。|近义词: commentator, annotator, lexicographer |反义词:  (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *glossista, from glossa (“gloss”) + -ista. Ultimately from Ancient Greek γλῶσσα (glôssa, “tongue, language, word”).
