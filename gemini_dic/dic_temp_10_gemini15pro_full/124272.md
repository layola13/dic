motels:/moʊˈtɛlz/ | n. |汽车旅馆|A roadside hotel designed primarily for motorists, typically having the rooms arranged in low buildings with parking directly outside.|We stayed in a motel on our road trip. 我们公路旅行时住在汽车旅馆。|Example: We pulled into a motel for the night. 我们晚上把车开进了一家汽车旅馆。|Synonyms: motor hotel, motor inn, motor lodge|Antonyms: hotel, resort|Noun
