alpines:/ˈælpaɪnz/ | adj. n. | 高山的，高山植物；高山居民 | Relating to or characteristic of high mountains; a plant or animal native to high mountains. |  The alpines are blooming beautifully this year. 今年高山植物开得很漂亮。| 近义词：mountain, highland | 反义词: lowland | 词性: adjective, noun
alpines:/ˈælpaɪnz/ | n. | 高山植物；高山居民 | Plants or people native to high mountains. | The alpines have adapted to the harsh conditions. 高山植物已经适应了恶劣的环境。 | 近义词：mountain dwellers, highlanders | 反义词: lowlanders | 词性: noun

词源: From French *alpin*, from Latin *alpīnus*, from *Alpēs* ("the Alps").
