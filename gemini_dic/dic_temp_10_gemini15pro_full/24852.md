brownist:/ˈbraʊnɪst/| n.|布朗派教徒；清教徒|A follower of the doctrines of Robert Browne (1550-1633), an early separatist from the Church of England.  Browne advocated for independent congregations, free from state control.|John was considered a Brownist because he believed in the separation of church and state.|近义词：Separatist, Dissenter, Nonconformist|反义词:Conformist|词性:noun
