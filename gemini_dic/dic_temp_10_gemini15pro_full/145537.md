overcommercialization:/ˌoʊvərˌkəˈmɜːrʃələˌzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度商业化 | The excessive focus on commerce and profit, often at the expense of other values. | The overcommercialization of Christmas has led to a focus on buying gifts rather than celebrating the true meaning of the holiday. 圣诞节的过度商业化导致人们关注购买礼物而不是庆祝节日的真正意义。| overcommercialisation (British spelling), commercial excess, crass commercialism | underselling, restraint | noun
