enragedly:/ɪnˈreɪdʒɪdli/
adverb
愤怒地，暴怒地
in a manner showing great anger
He slammed the door enragedly. 他愤怒地摔门。
Synonyms: furiously, angrily, wrathfully, fiercely
Antonyms: calmly, peacefully, gently, quietly
词性: 副词
