misfit:/ˈmɪsfɪt/| n. adj.|不适应环境的人，不称职的人；不合适的|A person who is not suited to their social environment, or something that is not suited to its intended purpose.|例句：He was a social misfit in high school. 他在高中时是一个不合群的人。

例句：The shoes were a misfit and pinched her toes. 这双鞋不合脚，夹住了她的脚趾。|近义词：nonconformist, maverick, outcast, eccentric, anomaly|反义词：conformist, insider|词性:名词, 形容词
