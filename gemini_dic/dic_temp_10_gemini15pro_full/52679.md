indignation: /ˌɪndɪɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 愤怒; 愤慨; 义愤 | a feeling of anger and surprise caused by something that you think is unfair or unreasonable |  She expressed her indignation at the unfair treatment. 她对不公平的待遇表示愤慨。

The news of the corruption scandal sparked public indignation. 腐败丑闻的消息引发了公众的愤怒。

近义词: anger, resentment, fury, outrage | 反义词: calmness, composure, pleasure, satisfaction | 词性: 名词
