benedictive:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪv/
adjective
祝福的，祈福的
Expressing or conveying a blessing.
The priest offered a benedictive prayer for the newlyweds.  神父为新婚夫妇做了祝福祷告。
Near Synonyms: blessing, benedictory, consecratory
Antonyms: cursing, maledictory
word type: adjective


benedictive:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪv/
noun
祝福语，祝祷词
A word or phrase expressing a blessing.
The ancient text contained many benedictives for various occasions. 这部古老的文献包含许多适用于不同场合的祝福语。
Near Synonyms: blessing, benediction
Antonyms: curse, malediction
word type: noun
