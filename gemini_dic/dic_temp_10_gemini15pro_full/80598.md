googol:/ˈɡuːɡɒl/ (US) /ˈɡuːɡɔːl/ (UK)| n. |古戈尔；10的100次方|A googol is the large number 10 to the power of 100 (which is 1 followed by one hundred zeros).  It's a number larger than the estimated number of atoms in the observable universe.|There are more ways to arrange a deck of cards than a googol.  一副牌的排列方式比古戈尔还多。|近义词: (no direct synonym, but related terms include: large number, vast number, immense number)|反义词: (no direct antonym, but related terms might be: small number, tiny number, infinitesimal)|词性:noun


googols:/ˈɡuːɡɒlz/ (US) /ˈɡuːɡɔːlz/ (UK) | n. |古戈尔(复数)；10的100次方的复数形式|The plural form of googol, referring to more than one instance of the number 10 to the 100th power.|He calculated with googols and googols of googols. 他用古戈尔和古戈尔的古戈尔进行计算。|近义词: (no direct synonym, but related terms include: large numbers, vast numbers, immense numbers)|反义词: (no direct antonym, but related terms might be: small numbers, tiny numbers, infinitesimals)|词性:noun
