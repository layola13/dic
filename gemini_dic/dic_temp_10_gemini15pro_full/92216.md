hypertensions:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛnʃənz/| n. |高血压（复数形式，通常用单数）|A condition in which the blood pressure in the arteries is persistently elevated.|High blood pressure, or hypertension, can lead to serious health problems. 高血压会导致严重的健康问题。|Synonyms: high blood pressure, elevated blood pressure|Antonyms: hypotension, low blood pressure|noun
