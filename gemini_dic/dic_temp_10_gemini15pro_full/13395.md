atomism:/ˈætəmɪzəm/ | n. |原子论；原子主义| The theory that all of reality and all the objects in the universe are composed of very small, indivisible and indestructible building blocks known as atoms.  It also refers to a social theory that views society as a collection of self-interested individuals. |  Ancient Greek philosophers like Democritus and Leucippus developed the concept of atomism. 古希腊哲学家，如德谟克利特和留基伯，发展了原子论的概念。

Atomism influenced the development of modern physics. 原子论影响了现代物理学的发展。 | synonyms:  atomic theory, reductionism, individualism | antonyms: holism, collectivism | noun
