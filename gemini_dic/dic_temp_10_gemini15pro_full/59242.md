e-cash:/ˈiːkæʃ/| n.|电子现金|A form of electronic money that allows users to make payments anonymously over a network, often using digital tokens.  It's designed to offer the same privacy and convenience as physical cash in online transactions.|I used e-cash to purchase the software because I didn't want to use my credit card. 我用电子现金购买了软件，因为我不想使用我的信用卡。|Synonyms: digital money, cybercash, electronic currency|Antonyms: physical cash, hard currency|noun
