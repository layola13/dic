hypocleidium:/ˌhaɪpəˈkliːdiəm/| n. |下锁骨|The interclavicle of a bird, or a corresponding part in other vertebrates.|The hypocleidium is a small bone situated below the junction of the two clavicles in birds. 下锁骨是鸟类中位于两块锁骨连接处下方的一块小骨。|The hypocleidium is an important skeletal element for flight in birds. 下锁骨是鸟类飞行重要的骨骼元素。|Synonym: interclavicle|Antonym: N/A|noun
