interdebates:/ˌɪntərdiˈbeɪts/ | n. |相互辩论，互相辩论| Debates or discussions between or among different groups or individuals. | Example: The interdebates between the political parties were quite heated. 各政党之间的辩论相当激烈。| Example:  The interdebates among the students helped them to understand the different perspectives on the issue. 学生之间的辩论帮助他们理解了这个问题的不同观点。|Synonym: discussions, arguments, exchanges | Antonym: agreements, harmony, consensus | noun
