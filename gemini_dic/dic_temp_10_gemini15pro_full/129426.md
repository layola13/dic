nominee:/ˌnɑːmɪˈniː/ /ˌnɒmɪˈniː/
n.
被提名者；候选人
A person who is nominated for an award, position, or honor.
She is a nominee for the Best Actress award. 她获得了最佳女演员奖提名。
The nominees for the presidency were announced yesterday. 总统候选人名单昨天公布了。
近义词：candidate, contender
反义词: elector, voter
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Latin nominatus, past participle of nominare "to name," from nomen "name."
