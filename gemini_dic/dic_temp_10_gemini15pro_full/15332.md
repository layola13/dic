backspaced:/ˈbækˌspeɪst/| v. |退格，删除|To move the cursor backward one space at a time, usually deleting the preceding character.|She backspaced to correct the typo. 她退格删除了拼写错误。|synonyms: delete, erase|antonyms: insert, add|verb

backspaced:/ˈbækˌspeɪst/| adj. |被退格的，被删除的|Having been moved backward and deleted with the backspace key.|The backspaced words were no longer visible. 被退格的文字不再可见了。|synonyms: deleted, erased|antonyms: inserted, added|adjective
