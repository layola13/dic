glowstick:/ˈɡloʊstɪk/| n. |荧光棒，发光棒|A self-contained, short-term light source. It consists of a translucent plastic tube containing isolated substances that, when combined, make light through chemiluminescence.|The children waved their glowsticks during the concert. 孩子们在音乐会上挥舞着荧光棒。|近义词：lightstick, chemiluminescent stick|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "flashlight" as a contrasting light source)|noun
