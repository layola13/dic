dictature:/dɪkˈteɪtʃər/ | n. | 独裁; 专政; 独裁统治; |A government by a dictator.  |例句：The country suffered under a brutal dictatorship. 这个国家遭受残酷的独裁统治。

例句：He seized power and established a dictatorship. 他夺取了政权，建立了独裁统治。|近义词：tyranny, despotism, autocracy |反义词：democracy, republic | noun
