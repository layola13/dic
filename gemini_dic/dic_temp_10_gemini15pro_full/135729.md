nonprobabilities:/ˌnɑːnˌprɑːbəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. |非概率; 不可能性|The absence of probabilities or the state of events not being governed by probability.  It refers to situations where the likelihood of something happening cannot be quantified using standard probability methods.| Due to the complexity of the system, we had to rely on nonprobabilities to make our prediction. 由于系统的复杂性，我们不得不依靠非概率来进行预测。| synonyms: uncertainties, imponderables | antonyms: probabilities, certainties | noun
