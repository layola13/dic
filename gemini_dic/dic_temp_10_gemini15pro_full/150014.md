palo:/ˈpɑːloʊ/ (US), /ˈpɑːləʊ/ (UK)| n.|棍，棒，木棍；(西班牙语)杆，棒；(菲律宾塔加禄语)敲，打|A stick or pole, especially a heavy one. In Spanish, it refers generally to a stick, pole, or rod. In Tagalog (Filipino), it means to hit or strike.|
He used a palo to stir the fire. 他用一根棍子拨火。

The old man leaned on his palo as he walked. 老人走路时拄着拐杖。

She used a palo to beat the rug. 她用棍子敲打地毯。(Tagalog usage)


近义词:stick, rod, pole, staff, club
