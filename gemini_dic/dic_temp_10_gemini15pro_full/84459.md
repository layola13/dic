halon:/ˈheɪlɒn/ (US), /ˈheɪlən/ (UK)| n.|哈龙，卤代烷|A type of fire extinguisher that contains hydrocarbons, halogens, and sometimes other chemicals. Halons are very effective at extinguishing fires, but they are also ozone-depleting substances and are now largely banned.|The museum's fire suppression system uses halon.|近义词:fire suppressant, fire retardant|反义词:fuel, accelerant|词性:noun
