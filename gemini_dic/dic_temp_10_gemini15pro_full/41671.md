correligionist:/ˌkɔːrɪˈlɪdʒənɪst/ /ˌkɑːrɪˈlɪdʒənɪst/
n.
教友；同教者
A person who shares the same religion as another.
例句：He greeted his correligionists warmly. 他热情地问候了他的教友。

例句：She found comfort and support among her correligionists during difficult times.  在困难时期，她在教友中找到了安慰和支持。

近义词：fellow believer, co-religionist
反义词：nonbeliever, infidel
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "cor-" (together) + "religio" (religion) + "-ist" (one who practices or believes in).
