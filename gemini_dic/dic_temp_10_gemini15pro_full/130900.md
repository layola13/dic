nonartistic:/ˌnɒnɑːrˈtɪstɪk/
adjective
非艺术的；无艺术性的
Lacking artistic qualities or talent.
He had a nonartistic temperament. 他没有艺术气质。
synonyms: unartistic,inartistic, noncreative
antonyms: artistic, creative
词性: 形容词
