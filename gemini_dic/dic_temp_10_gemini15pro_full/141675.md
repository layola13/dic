optocoele: /ˈɒptəʊˌsiːl/ (UK), /ˈɑːptəˌsiːl/ (US)
n.
optic vesicle
视泡；视杯；视囊
An optocoele is an abnormal sac-like protrusion of the eye, specifically the optic vesicle, through a gap in the surrounding tissues during embryonic development. This can lead to various eye malformations.
Optocoeles are often associated with other craniofacial abnormalities. 视泡常常伴有其他颅面畸形。
The fetus was diagnosed with an optocoele during the ultrasound. 超声检查发现胎儿患有视泡。
近义词: optic vesicle cyst
反义词: None (This is a developmental abnormality; it doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性: noun
