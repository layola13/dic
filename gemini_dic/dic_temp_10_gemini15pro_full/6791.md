anaphylactic:/ˌænəfɪˈlæktɪk/| adj.|过敏性的，过敏反应的|Relating to or exhibiting anaphylaxis; a severe, potentially life-threatening allergic reaction.|例句：He had an anaphylactic reaction to peanuts. 他对花生有过敏反应。|例句：Anaphylactic shock can be fatal if not treated immediately. 过敏性休克如果不立即治疗可能是致命的。|近义词：allergic, hypersensitivity|反义词: non-allergic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ana- (up, back, again) + phylaxis (protection).  Originally referred to the opposite of prophylaxis (protection against disease), hence a heightened sensitivity or reaction.
