destainer:/diːˈsteɪnər/ | n. |去污剂；脱色剂|A chemical or substance used to remove stains, especially from microscopic slides.|The lab technician used a destainer to remove excess dye from the tissue sample.  实验室技术员使用脱色剂去除组织样本中多余的染料。|Synonyms: stain remover, bleach, cleaner|Antonyms: stainer, dye|noun
