arthropoda:/ɑːrˈθrɒpədə/
| n. |节肢动物门|A phylum of invertebrate animals that includes insects, arachnids, crustaceans, and myriapods, characterized by jointed appendages and an exoskeleton.|Insects, spiders, and crabs belong to the phylum Arthropoda. 昆虫、蜘蛛和螃蟹都属于节肢动物门。|近义词:invertebrate|反义词:vertebrate|词性:名词

arthropodan:/ɑːrˈθrɒpədən/
| adj. |节肢动物的|Relating to or characteristic of arthropods.|The arthropodan exoskeleton provides protection and support. 节肢动物的外骨骼提供保护和支撑。|近义词:invertebrate|反义词:vertebrate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek ἄρθρον (arthron, “joint”) + πούς (pous, “foot”)
