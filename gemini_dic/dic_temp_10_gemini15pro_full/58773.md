dunnage:/ˈdʌnɪdʒ/| n.|垫舱物，衬垫材料，行李|Loose materials used to protect cargo from damage during transport. Also, personal belongings. |The ship's hold was filled with dunnage to keep the cargo from shifting. 船舱里装满了垫舱物，以防止货物移动。

The explorers used their extra clothing as dunnage to sleep more comfortably on the hard ground. 探险家们用多余的衣服作垫子，以便在坚硬的地面上睡得更舒服。|近义词：packing, padding, stuffing|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

dunnage:/ˈdʌnɪdʒ/| n.|行李（较旧用法）|Personal belongings, baggage. (Older usage)|He packed his dunnage and headed for the docks.  他收拾好行李，前往码头。

She rummaged through her dunnage for a clean shirt.  她在行李中翻找一件干净的衬衫。|近义词：luggage, baggage|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
