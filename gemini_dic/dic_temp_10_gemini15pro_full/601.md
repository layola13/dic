ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *abra*, itself from Hebrew *haver*, meaning “companion” or “associate”. The word’s association with magic likely comes from its use in Kabbalistic traditions.  The word evolved through various forms and languages, including Greek and Latin, before becoming the "abracadabra" we know today, which is a related term.
