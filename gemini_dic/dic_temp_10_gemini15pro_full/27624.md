cancerous:/ˈkænsərəs/| adj.|癌的；恶性的；像癌一样蔓延的|Relating to or affected with cancer; malignant.  Harmful and destructive in a way that resembles cancer.|例句：The doctor discovered a cancerous growth. 医生发现了一个恶性肿瘤。|例句：The cancerous cells spread rapidly. 癌细胞迅速扩散。|近义词：malignant, carcinogenic, deadly, destructive|反义词：benign, healthy, harmless|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French canceros, from Latin cancer ‘crab, cancer’ + -ous.
