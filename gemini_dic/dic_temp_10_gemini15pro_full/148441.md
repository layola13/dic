overstocked: /ˌoʊvərˈstɑːkt/ /ˌəʊvəˈstɒkt/ | adj. | 过度库存的；库存过剩的 | Having more goods in stock than is necessary or desirable. |  The warehouse was overstocked with last season's merchandise. 仓库里堆满了上个季度的商品。

The bookstore was overstocked with unsold copies of the novel.  这家书店积压了大量未售出的小说。| 近义词: oversupplied, glutted | 反义词: understocked, depleted | 词性: 形容词
