fancy-free:/ˈfænsiˌfriː/
adjective
无拘无束的；自由自在的；无牵无挂的
Free from the constraints of love or other emotional ties.  Not involved in a romantic relationship and therefore free to do as one pleases.
She's fancy-free and enjoying her single life. 她无拘无束，享受着单身生活。
近义词：carefree, unattached, unburdened, untroubled
反义词：attached, committed, burdened, troubled
词性: adjective
词源: From fancy (n.) in the sense of "love, inclination, liking" + free (adj.).  The original sense was "free from love," specifically from being in love or romantically attached.
