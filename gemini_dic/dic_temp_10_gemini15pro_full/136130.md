nonreflections:/ˌnɒnrɪˈflɛkʃənz/
n.
无反思;缺乏反思
Lack of reflections; absence of thoughtful consideration.
The artist's work was criticized for its nonreflections on the social issues of the time.  这位艺术家的作品因其对当时社会问题的缺乏反思而受到批评。
Near synonyms: thoughtlessness, inconsideration, superficiality
Antonyms: reflection, contemplation, introspection
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + reflection.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without." "Reflection" comes from the Latin "reflectere," meaning "to bend back."
