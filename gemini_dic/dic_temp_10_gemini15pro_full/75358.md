frothily:/ˈfrɒθɪli/ (US) /ˈfrɔːθɪli/ (UK)| adv.|轻浮地，起泡地|In a manner resembling froth; lightly, airily, frivolously.|She frothily recounted her adventures in Paris. 她轻浮地讲述了她在巴黎的冒险经历。|例句：The champagne frothily filled the glass. 香槟酒起着泡沫注满了杯子。|近义词：frivolously, airily, giddily|反义词：seriously, soberly|词性:副词
Etymology: From frothy + -ly.  Frothy itself comes from froth, from Old English *froþ, from Proto-Germanic *frōþą, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)preu- “to scatter, spray.”
