depopulators:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪtəz/
n.
减少人口的人或事物
Those who or that which depopulates; things that cause a decrease in population.
The Black Death was one of history’s most devastating depopulators. 黑死病是历史上最具破坏性的人口减少因素之一。
Agents of disease can be major depopulators. 疾病可以是主要的减少人口的因素。
Synonym: decimators, reducers
Antonym:  repopulators,  increasers
词性: 名词(plural noun)


depopulator:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊleɪtə/
n.
减少人口的人或事物
Something that reduces population.
War can be a terrible depopulator. 战争可能导致人口锐减。
Famine acted as a depopulator in the region.  饥荒导致了该地区的人口减少。
Synonym: decimator, reducer
Antonym: repopulator, increaser
词性: 名词(singular noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From depopulate + -or/-er.  Depopulate comes from the Latin prefix *de-* (meaning "down from, away, off") + *populāre* (meaning "to furnish with inhabitants, people").
