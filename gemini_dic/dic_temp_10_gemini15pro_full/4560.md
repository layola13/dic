alchemists:/ˈælkɪmɪsts/
noun
炼金术士
A person who practices alchemy.  Alchemy was a philosophical tradition practiced throughout history and in many parts of the world.  It aimed to purify, mature, and perfect certain objects.
The alchemists of old sought to turn base metals into gold.  古代的炼金术士试图将贱金属变成黄金。
近义词:  chemists, sorcerers, magicians
反义词: scientist
词性: noun
