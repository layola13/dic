haggardnesses:/ˈhæɡərdnɪsɪz/
n.
憔悴；枯槁；疲惫不堪
The state of being haggard, showing signs of tiredness, worry, or illness.
Her face showed the haggardnesses of a sleepless night. 她的脸上显露出彻夜未眠的疲惫。
Near synonyms: exhaustion, fatigue, weariness
Antonyms: freshness, vibrancy, vitality
noun

haggardness:/ˈhæɡərdnəs/
n.
憔悴；枯槁；疲惫不堪
The state of being haggard, showing signs of tiredness, worry, or illness.
His haggardness betrayed the stress he was under. 他的憔悴暴露了他承受的压力。
Near synonyms: exhaustion, fatigue, weariness
Antonyms: freshness, vibrancy, vitality
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From haggard + -ness.  Haggard itself likely comes from a variant of  "hawk," referring to a hawk trained for hunting, which would appear thin and gaunt after a period of training.
