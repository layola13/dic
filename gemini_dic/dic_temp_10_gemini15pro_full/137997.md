noodly:/ˈnuːdli/| adj.|面条状的；软弱无力的；糊状的|Resembling noodles in shape, texture, or consistency; limp and drooping; lacking firmness.|The overcooked pasta was noodly and unappetizing.  煮过头的意大利面软塌塌的，毫无食欲。|近义词：limp, flabby, soft, stringy|反义词:firm, stiff, rigid|词性:形容词
词源:From noodle + -y.  由noodle(面条)加形容词后缀-y构成。
