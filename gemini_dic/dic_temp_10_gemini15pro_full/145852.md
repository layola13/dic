overdepressed:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛst/ | adj. |过度沮丧的，极度抑郁的 | Excessively depressed; extremely low in spirits.  It combines "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) with "depressed" (meaning sad, gloomy, or suffering from depression).|  She felt overdepressed after losing her job and her apartment in the same week. 她在一周内失去了工作和公寓后感到极度沮丧。 |  He was so overdepressed he couldn't even get out of bed. 他非常沮丧，甚至无法下床。 | Synonyms: despondent, dejected, miserable, downcast | Antonyms: elated, joyful, cheerful, happy | adjective
