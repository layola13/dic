came-around:/keɪm əˈraʊnd/
verb
苏醒；恢复知觉；回心转意；造访
regain consciousness; change one's mind; visit
He came around a few minutes after the accident.  事故发生几分钟后，他苏醒了过来。
She eventually came around to our way of thinking. 她最终同意了我们的想法。
I'll come around and see you next week.  我下周会过来看你。
近义词:recover, revive, change one's mind, visit
反义词:pass out, faint, remain opposed
