overslow:/ˌoʊvərˈsloʊ/
verb
过慢；速度太慢
to reduce the speed of something, or to go too slowly
The driver overslowed and caused a traffic jam. 司机开得太慢，造成了交通堵塞。
近义词: decelerate, retard
反义词: accelerate, speed up
词性:v.

overslow:/ˌoʊvərˈsloʊ/
adjective
过慢的
too slow
The overslow train made me late for work. 过慢的火车让我上班迟到了。
近义词: slow, sluggish
反义词: fast, rapid
词性:adj.
