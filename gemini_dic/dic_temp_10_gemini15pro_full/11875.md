arriviste:/ˌærɪˈviːst/ | n. | 暴发户，新贵，野心家 | An ambitious person who has recently acquired wealth or social status, typically regarded as being uncultured or pushy. |  He's seen as an arriviste by the old money families. 他被那些老牌富豪家族视为暴发户。

The young arriviste flaunted his new wealth with expensive cars and clothes.  这个年轻的暴发户用昂贵的汽车和衣服炫耀他的新财富。

| 近义词：social climber, parvenu, upstart, nouveau riche | 反义词：aristocrat, patrician, established member | 词性: noun
