geophagist:/ˈdʒiːəˌfeɪdʒɪst/| n. |食土癖者；食土动物|A person or animal that eats earth or clay, especially as a dietary supplement or for medicinal purposes.  A geophagist might consume clay to obtain essential minerals or to soothe an upset stomach. |The documentary showed a geophagist consuming clay from a riverbank.  这部纪录片展示了一个食土癖者从河岸上吃土。|Synonyms: clay-eater, earth-eater|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but one could consider someone with a normal diet)|Noun
