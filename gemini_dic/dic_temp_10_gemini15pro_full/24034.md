breeze:/briːz/| n. |微风，轻风|A gentle wind.|A cool breeze swept across the beach. 一阵凉爽的微风吹过海滩。|近义词：wind, zephyr, draft|反义词:gale, storm|noun

breeze:/briːz/| v. |轻快地走，轻松地做|To move or accomplish something quickly and easily.|He breezed through the exam. 他轻轻松松地完成了考试。|近义词：sail, glide, coast|反义词:struggle, toil|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *brese "north-east wind," probably of Celtic origin.
