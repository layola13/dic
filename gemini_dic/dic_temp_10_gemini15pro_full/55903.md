divorcing: /dɪˈvɔːsɪŋ/ | v. | 离婚; 脱离; 分离; 断绝关系 | The process of legally ending a marriage.  The act of separating or disuniting. |  They are divorcing after ten years of marriage. 他们结婚十年后正在离婚。 | separating, splitting, parting | reconciling, uniting | verb

Etymology:
From the Old French *divorcier, from Latin divortium "divorce, separation," from divertere "to turn aside, separate," from dis- "apart" + vertere "to turn."
