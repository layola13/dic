over-again:/ˌoʊvər əˈɡɛn/ | adv. | 再一次，重新 | Doing something from the beginning to the end one more time. |  He had to start the project over again because of a computer crash. 他因为电脑崩溃不得不重新开始这个项目。 | Example: She sang the song over again. 她把歌又唱了一遍。 | Synonyms: anew, afresh, once more | Antonyms: never again |  adverb
