palpitation:/ˌpælpɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 心悸，悸动 | A noticeably rapid, strong, or irregular heartbeat due to agitation, exertion, or illness. |  She experienced heart palpitations after drinking too much coffee. 她喝了太多咖啡后感到心悸。 | synonyms: flutter, throbbing, pounding, racing | antonyms: calmness, stillness | noun

palpitation:/ˌpælpɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | （不安、恐惧等的）强烈感受 | A noticeably rapid, strong, or irregular heartbeat due to agitation, exertion, or illness.
