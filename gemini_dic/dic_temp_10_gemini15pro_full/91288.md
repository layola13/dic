hydrologically:/ˌhaɪdrəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
水文地
in a way that relates to the study of water on the earth, especially its movement and distribution
例句：The area is hydrologically connected to the river.  这个地区在水文上与河流相连。

例句：The dam has hydrologically altered the downstream ecosystem.  大坝改变了下游生态系统的水文状况。

近义词：water-wise, concerning water resources
反义词：N/A
词性：adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hydrologic" + "-ally".
"Hydrologic" comes from "hydro-" (water) + "-logic" (study of).  "Hydro-" ultimately from Greek "hydōr" (water). "-logic" from Greek "logos" (word, reason, study). "-ally" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
