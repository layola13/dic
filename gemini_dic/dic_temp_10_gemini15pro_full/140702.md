omni-antenna:/ˈɒmni ænˈtɛnə/ | n. | 全向天线 | An antenna that radiates radio frequency power uniformly in all directions in a given plane, typically the horizontal plane.  It has a 360-degree radiation pattern. | The omni-antenna on the roof provides Wi-Fi coverage to the entire area. 屋顶上的全向天线为整个区域提供Wi-Fi覆盖。 |  Synonyms: isotropic antenna, omnidirectional antenna | Antonyms: directional antenna,  Yagi antenna | noun

omni:/ˈɒmni/ | prefix | 全，所有，遍及 | From Latin omnis "all," related to Old English eall "all." Used to form compound words meaning "all," "every," "of all things," "in all ways or places." |  Example: omnipresent (无所不在的), omnipotent (无所不能的) | N/A | N/A | prefix

antenna:/ænˈtɛnə/ | n. | 天线 | A device for sending or receiving radio waves. | The television antenna on the roof receives signals from broadcasting stations. 屋顶上的电视天线接收来自广播电台的信号。 | Synonyms: aerial, receiver, transmitter | Antonyms: N/A | noun
