demisabilities:/ˌdɛmiˌdɪsəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |轻度残疾，部分残疾|A state of having reduced abilities or functionalities, less severe than a full disability.  It acknowledges a spectrum of ability and recognizes that individuals may experience limitations in some areas but not others.  It emphasizes the remaining abilities rather than focusing solely on the limitations.|例句：The organization provides support for people with demisabilities to access employment.  该组织为轻度残疾人士提供就业支持。

例句：Demisabilities can range from mild learning differences to physical impairments that don't significantly restrict daily activities. 轻度残疾的范围可以从轻微的学习差异到不严重限制日常活动的生理缺陷。|近义词：partial disabilities, mild disabilities, limited abilities|反义词:full disability, able-bodiedness|词性:noun
