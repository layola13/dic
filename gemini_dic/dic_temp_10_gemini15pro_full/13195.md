at-loss:/æt lɔːs/ | adj. | 亏损的；不知所措的 | Being in a state of uncertainty or confusion; experiencing a financial deficit. | The company was at a loss after the stock market crash. 公司在股市崩盘后遭受了损失。 |  He was at a loss for words when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息时不知所措。 | synonyms: perplexed, bewildered, confused, lost, unprofitable | antonyms: profitable, certain, decisive, clear, gaining | adjective
