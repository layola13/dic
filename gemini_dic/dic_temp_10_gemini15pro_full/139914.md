odeon:/ˈəʊdiən/ (UK), /ˈoʊdiˌɑːn/ (US)| n.|音乐厅，剧院，电影院|A building designed for musical or theatrical performances, or for showing films.|We went to see a play at the odeon. 我们去音乐厅看了一场戏。|The Odeon was packed for the premiere.  首映式上音乐厅座无虚席。|synonyms: theater, cinema, playhouse, auditorium|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open-air venue")|noun
