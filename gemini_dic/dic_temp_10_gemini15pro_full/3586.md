agatiferous:/ˌædʒəˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
产玛瑙的；多玛瑙的
Containing or producing agate.
The explorer discovered an agatiferous cave, filled with stunning formations of the colorful mineral. 探险家发现了一个盛产玛瑙的洞穴，里面充满了令人惊叹的彩色矿物构造。
The region is known for its agatiferous rocks. 该地区以其含玛瑙的岩石而闻名。
近义词: agate-bearing, agate-rich
反义词: barren (缺乏的), devoid (空的)
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *agat- (stem of *agates, from Greek akhatēs "agate") + -ferous (from Latin -fer "bearing," from ferre "to carry, bear").
