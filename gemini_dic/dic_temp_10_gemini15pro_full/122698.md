moggan:/ˈmɒɡən/ (UK) /ˈmɑːɡən/ (US)| n.|无舵雪橇，平底雪橇|A sled without runners, usually made of a single piece of wood or other material, used for sliding downhill on snow or ice.|We used a moggan to go down the snowy hill. 我们用无舵雪橇滑下雪山。|The children were thrilled to ride the moggan down the slope. 孩子们兴奋地坐着平底雪橇滑下山坡。|近义词：sled, toboggan|反义词:|noun
