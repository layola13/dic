evolution:/ˌevəˈluːʃn/ | n. |进化，演变，发展|The process of gradual development in something, especially from a simple to a more complex form. | Darwin's theory of evolution by natural selection revolutionized biology. 达尔文的自然选择进化论彻底改变了生物学。 |The evolution of the internet has transformed communication. 互联网的进化改变了人们的沟通方式。 | 近义词: development, progression, growth, advancement | 反义词: devolution, regression, deterioration | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēvolūtiō (“an unrolling, opening”), from ēvolvere (“to unroll”), from ex- (“out of”) + volvere (“to roll”).
