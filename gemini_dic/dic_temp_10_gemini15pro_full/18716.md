bereave: /bɪˈriːv/ | vt. | 使丧失（尤指亲属）; 使失去| to deprive and make desolate, especially by death; to take away | The accident bereaved her of her husband and left her with three young children.  这场事故使她失去了丈夫，留下三个年幼的孩子。| synonyms: deprive, strip, rob, dispossess | antonyms: provide, give, endow | verb

bereaved: /bɪˈriːvd/ | adj. | 丧失亲人的；失去亲人的 | deprived of something valuable or beloved; especially : deprived by death | The bereaved family received many condolences.  这个失去亲人的家庭收到了许多慰问。 | synonyms: grieving, mourning, sorrowful | antonyms: joyful, happy, celebratory | adjective
