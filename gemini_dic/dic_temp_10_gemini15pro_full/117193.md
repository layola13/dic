measuredness:/ˈmeʒərdnɪs/| n.|慎重，稳重；适度；有分寸|The measuredness of her response impressed everyone. 她的回应谨慎周到，给大家留下了深刻印象。|English Explanation: The quality of being measured, deliberate, and controlled in one's actions or words. It implies a sense of careful consideration and restraint, avoiding extremes or excesses.|例句：He spoke with measuredness, choosing his words carefully. 他说话很有分寸，字斟句酌。

例句：The measuredness of his movements suggested great self-control. 他举止稳重，显示出极强的自制力。|近义词：deliberateness, composure, calmness, restraint, caution, discretion|反义词：recklessness, impulsiveness, hastiness, carelessness|词性:noun
