coranto:/kəˈræntoʊ/ /kɒˈræntoʊ/
| n. |库兰托舞曲；快步舞；时事通讯|A lively, fast dance and a piece of music for this dance, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries. Also, an early form of newspaper, providing news from abroad.| The musicians played a lively coranto.  乐师们演奏了一曲轻快的库兰托舞曲。| 近义词：courante, gazette, newsletter | 反义词:  (For the dance) slow dance, waltz | 词性: noun
