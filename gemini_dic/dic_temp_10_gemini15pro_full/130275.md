non-subscribing:/ˌnɒn səbˈskraɪbɪŋ/ | adj. | 非订阅的，未订阅的 | Not subscribing; not having subscribed.  Referring to individuals or entities that have not signed up for or agreed to a service, belief, or publication. | Example: The non-subscribing members will not receive the newsletter.  非订阅会员将不会收到简报。| Example:  The non-subscribing population to the streaming service is still quite large.  未订阅该流媒体服务的人口仍然相当多。| 近义词: unsubscribed, non-member | 反义词: subscribing, member | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
subscribing (present participle): from Latin "subscribere" meaning "to write underneath", later evolving to mean "to agree to" or "to sign up for"
