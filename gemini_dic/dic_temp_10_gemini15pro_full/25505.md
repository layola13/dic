bullies:/ˈbʊliz/| n.| 恃强凌弱者，恶霸 |A person who uses strength or power to frighten or hurt weaker people. |The school bullies made his life miserable. 学校里的恶霸让他生活得很痛苦。|近义词：tormentors, oppressors, intimidators, persecutors |反义词: protector, defender, advocate | noun

bullies:/ˈbʊliz/| v.| 欺凌，恐吓 |To use superior strength or influence to intimidate (someone), typically to force him or her to do what one wants.|He was bullied into giving them his lunch money. 他被恐吓交出了午饭钱。|近义词：harass, intimidate, torment, persecute |反义词: protect, defend, support | verb
