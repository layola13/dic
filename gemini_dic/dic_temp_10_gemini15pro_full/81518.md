gravigrade:/ˈɡrævɪɡreɪd/| adj. n.| 行走于地面的；陆行性动物 | Walking on the ground; a terrestrial animal. |  Example: Kangaroos are gravigrade animals, moving by hopping on their hind legs. 袋鼠是陆行性动物，通过后腿跳跃移动。

Example:  The study focused on the gravigrade locomotion of several mammalian species.  这项研究的重点是几种哺乳动物的陆地运动方式。 | 近义词: terrestrial, cursorial | 反义词: arboreal, aerial | 词性: adjective, noun
