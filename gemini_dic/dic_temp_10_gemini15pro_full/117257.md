mechanicalness:/məˈkænɪkəlnəs/| n.|机械性；呆板性|The mechanicalness of his response suggested he wasn't really listening. 他的回答很机械，表明他并没有真正在听。|例句：The pianist's performance lacked emotion, characterized by a certain mechanicalness.  这位钢琴家的演奏缺乏情感，带有一种机械性。

The mechanicalness of the assembly line made the work tedious.  流水线的机械性使工作变得单调乏味。|近义词：automatism, rigidity, inflexibility|反义词：fluidity, spontaneity, naturalness|词性:noun
