bois:/bwɑː/| n.|guys, fellas; (offensive slang) a derogatory term for a gay man|A group of men or boys, often used informally.  Can be used offensively as a derogatory term for a gay man.|例句：Hey bois, what are you up to tonight? 嘿，伙计们，今晚你们打算做什么？

例句：I saw the bois hanging out at the mall. 我看到那些家伙在商场闲逛。|近义词：guys, dudes, fellas, blokes|反义词:girls, women|Noun
