basaltiform:/bəˈsɔːltɪfɔːrm/| adj.|玄武岩状的，类似玄武岩的|Resembling basalt in form or structure; having the columnar structure characteristic of some basalt flows.|The cliff face exhibited a striking basaltiform structure.|Near synonyms: columnar, basaltic|Antonyms: amorphous, shapeless|Adjective
