monoculturist:/ˈmɒnəʊkʌltʃərɪst/ /ˈmɑːnəʊkʌltʃərɪst/ | n. |单一作物种植者；单一文化主义者 |A person who practices or advocates monoculture, the cultivation of a single crop in a given area. |The monoculturist focused solely on growing corn, neglecting the health of the soil and the surrounding ecosystem.  单一作物种植者只专注于种植玉米，而忽视了土壤和周围生态系统的健康。| Synonyms: specialist, single-crop farmer | Antonyms: polyculturist, diversified farmer | noun
