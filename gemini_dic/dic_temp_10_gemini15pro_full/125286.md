multiflorous:/ˌmʌltiˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
多花的；多花瓣的
Having many flowers or blossoms.
The multiflorous rose bush filled the garden with fragrance.  这棵多花的玫瑰丛让花园里充满了芬芳。
The multiflorous orchid produced a stunning display. 这株多花的兰花展现出惊人的美景。
synonyms: multifloral, many-flowered, floriferous, prolific
antonyms: pauciflorous, few-flowered
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *multiflōrus*, from multi- (“many”) + flōs (“flower”).
