imparisyllabic:/ˌɪmpərɪsɪˈlæbɪk/
adjective
不等音节的；音节数不等的
Having unequal numbers of syllables.  Specifically referring to two or more words or lines of verse having different numbers of syllables.
The poet intentionally used imparisyllabic lines to create a sense of imbalance and unease. 诗人特意使用不等音节的诗行来营造一种不平衡和不安的感觉。
The two words "cat" and "elephant" are imparisyllabic. “cat”和“elephant”这两个词音节数不等。
Near Synonyms:  uneven, asymmetrical (in terms of syllables)
Antonyms:  parisyllabic, even (in terms of syllables)
词性: adjective
