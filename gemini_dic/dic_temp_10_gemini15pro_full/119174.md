mezcal:/mɛˈskæl/ | n. |梅斯卡尔酒|An alcoholic spirit made from the agave plant, primarily in Oaxaca, Mexico.  It is similar to tequila, but often smokier in flavor.|  I tried a new mezcal last night; it had a lovely smoky flavor. 昨晚我尝了一种新的梅斯卡尔酒，它有一种可爱的烟熏味。|  The bartender recommended a mezcal cocktail with a spicy rim.  调酒师推荐了一种加了辣边的梅斯卡尔鸡尾酒。 | 近义词: tequila, agave spirits | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-alcoholic beverages) | 词性: noun
