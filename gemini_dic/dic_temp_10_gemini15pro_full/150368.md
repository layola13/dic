panned:/pænd/
verb
严厉批评；抨击；（淘金时）用淘金盘淘洗
criticize (someone or something) severely.
The movie was panned by the critics. 这部电影受到了评论家的严厉批评。

The critics panned the play. 评论家们批评了这部戏剧。

verb
（淘金时）用淘金盘淘洗
wash (gravel, sand, etc.) in a pan to separate out gold.
They panned for gold in the river. 他们在河里淘金。

He panned for gold, but found nothing of value. 他淘金，但什么值钱的东西也没找到。


近义词:criticize, slam, blast, condemn, censure
反义词:praise, laud, acclaim, commend
词性:verb
