overgratifying: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于满足的，过度令人愉悦的 | Providing excessive gratification; overly satisfying. |  The dessert was so rich and decadent, it was almost overgratifying. 这甜点太浓郁太腐朽了，几乎让人觉得过于满足了。

The constant praise felt overgratifying and insincere.  不断的赞扬让人感觉过于满足，而且不真诚。


| 近义词: cloying, excessive, overindulgent, oversatisfying | 反义词: unsatisfying, underwhelming | 词性: adjective
