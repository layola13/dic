griper:/ˈɡraɪpər/| n.|抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人|A person who complains chronically; a complainer.|He's such a griper, always finding something to whine about. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到一些事情来抱怨。|Example: The constant griper was never happy, no matter what. 这个不停抱怨的人无论如何都不会开心。|Synonyms: complainer, whiner, grumbler|Antonyms: optimist, enthusiast|noun

gripe:/ɡraɪp/| n. vi. vt.|抱怨，牢骚；肠绞痛；紧握|A complaint; to complain; a sharp pain in the bowels; to grasp firmly.|My only gripe about the hotel was the slow Wi-Fi. 我对这家酒店唯一的抱怨就是无线网络太慢。|Example: He griped about the food, the service, and the weather. 他抱怨食物、服务和天气。|Synonyms: complaint, grievance, moan, grumble (v.)|Antonyms: praise, compliment (v.)|noun, verb, intransitive verb, transitive verb
