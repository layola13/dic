moot:/muːt/| adj. n.|未决定的，可争辩的；辩论会|Subject to debate; open to discussion; A mock court at which law students argue hypothetical cases.|例句：The question of whether to build a new stadium is still moot.  是否要建一个新体育场的问题仍然悬而未决。

例句：The law students participated in a moot court competition.  法学院学生参加了模拟法庭比赛。|近义词：debatable, arguable, disputable, unresolved|反义词：undisputed, resolved, settled|词性:形容词,名词

mooters:/ˈmuːtərz/| n.|参加辩论者，讨论者|Participants in a moot court or debate; those who moot a point.|例句：The mooters prepared their arguments carefully.  辩论者仔细准备了他们的论点。

例句：The mooters presented their cases to the judges. 辩论者向法官陈述了他们的案件。|近义词：debaters, discussants, arguers|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
