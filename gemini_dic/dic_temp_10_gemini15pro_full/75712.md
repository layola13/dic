full-fat:/ˌfʊl ˈfæt/ | adj. | 全脂的 | Containing all the fat originally present; not low-fat or reduced-fat. |  I always buy full-fat milk because I prefer the taste. 我总是买全脂牛奶，因为我更喜欢它的味道。 |  I prefer full-fat yogurt to low-fat.  比起低脂酸奶，我更喜欢全脂酸奶。 | Synonyms: whole-fat, regular | Antonyms: low-fat, reduced-fat, skimmed, nonfat | adjective
