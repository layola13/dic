goshawk:/ˈɡɒs.hɔːk/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːs.hɑːk/ (US)| n. |苍鹰|A medium-to-large bird of prey, known for its powerful flight and hunting prowess. It often preys on birds and small mammals.|The goshawk swooped down and caught a rabbit. 苍鹰俯冲下来，抓住了一只兔子。|近义词：raptor, hawk, falcon|反义词:prey|词性:noun
