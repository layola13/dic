liquable:/ˈlaɪkwəbl/ | adj. |容易交朋友的；讨人喜欢的|Easy to get along with; agreeable; pleasant.|例句：He's a very liquable fellow, always ready with a joke. 他是个非常容易相处的人，总是准备着讲笑话。|近义词：affable, amiable, congenial, genial, sociable|反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, unsociable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *liquabilis, from Latin liquere "to be clear, liquid," figuratively "to be manifest, evident, plain."  The sense evolution likely went from "clear" to "transparent" (in character) to "easy to understand and get along with."
