antigraph:/ˈæntiɡræf/
noun
对照; 反题; 对偶句
A word, phrase, or sentence that is the opposite in meaning to another.  A figure of speech in which contrasting or opposite ideas are balanced against each other.
The antigraph of "hot" is "cold."  "Hot"的对照是"cold"。

The poet used antigraph to highlight the conflicting emotions of love and loss.  诗人用对偶句来突出爱与失去的矛盾情感。

Synonym: opposite, antithesis, contrast, foil
Antonym: synonym, equivalent, likeness
