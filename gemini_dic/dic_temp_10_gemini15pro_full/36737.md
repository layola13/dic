collophore:/ˈkɒləfɔːr/ (US) /ˈkɒləfɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |弹器；腹管|A tube-like structure on the ventral side of the first abdominal segment of springtails (Collembola), used for water uptake, adhesion, and sometimes for jumping. |Springtails use their collophore to adhere to surfaces. 弹尾目昆虫用它们的弹器附着在物体表面。|近义词: ventral tube 腹管 |反义词: None|noun

collophore:/ˈkɒləfɔːr/ (US) /ˈkɒləfɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |粘管|A tube-like structure on the underside of a springtail, used for adhering to surfaces. |The collophore helps the springtail stick to smooth surfaces. 粘管帮助弹尾虫粘在光滑的表面上。|近义词: ventral tube, adhesive tube |反义词: None|noun
