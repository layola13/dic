afro's: /ˈæfroʊz/ | n. | 非洲式发型（复数） | A large, bushy hairstyle, typically worn by people of African descent.  It is characterized by its round shape and tightly curled hair. |  She admired the singer's magnificent afro's. 她很欣赏这位歌手蓬松的非洲式发型。 |  The dancers all wore dazzling afro's. 舞蹈演员都梳着耀眼的非洲式发型。 |  Synonyms:  Afros, natural hair | Antonyms: straightened hair, permed hair | noun
