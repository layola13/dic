hootingly:/ˈhuːtɪŋli/ | adv. | 嘲讽地，轻蔑地，喧闹地 |  In a way that expresses scornful amusement or disapproval, often with loud laughter or derision.  It can also simply mean "loudly," especially in the context of owls. |  They laughed hootingly at his clumsy attempt to dance. 他们嘲讽地大笑他笨拙的舞姿. | synonyms: derisively, mockingly, scornfully, loudly | antonyms: respectfully, politely, quietly, approvingly
词性: 副词
