mousk: /mʌsk/ | n. |麝香; 麝香鹿|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance secreted by a gland in the abdomen of the male musk deer, used in perfumery.|The perfume had a strong scent of mousk.| 近义词: musk, civet |反义词: None| 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *musk*, from Old French *musc*, from Late Latin *muscus*, from Greek *μόσχος (moskhos)*, from Persian *مشک (mušk)*, ultimately from Sanskrit *मुष्क (muṣka)* meaning "testicle," because of the shape of the gland.
