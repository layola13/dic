assyriology:/əˌsɪriˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |亚述学| The study of the language, history, and culture of ancient Assyria. | Example 1: He is a renowned scholar of assyriology. 他是一位著名的亚述学学者。

Example 2:  Assyriology has illuminated many aspects of Mesopotamian civilization. 亚述学阐明了美索不达米亚文明的许多方面。 | 近义词: Near Eastern studies,  Mesopotamian studies | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a field of study) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Assyrian + -ology (study of).  Assyrian comes from Assyria, the ancient Mesopotamian kingdom.  The "-ology" suffix derives from the Greek -λογία (-logia), meaning "study of," "theory," "science," or "collection."
