exhortatively:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtətɪvli/
adv.
劝告地，激励地
in a manner intended to urge or encourage someone to do something.
The coach spoke exhortatively to his team, urging them to give their all in the final quarter. 教练激励地对他的队员讲话，敦促他们在最后一节全力以赴。
The teacher exhortatively reminded the students to study for the upcoming exam. 老师劝勉学生们为即将到来的考试做好准备。
近义词: encouragingly, urgingly, persuasively
反义词: discouragingly, dissuasively
词性: adverb
词源: From exhortative + -ly.  Exhortative from Late Middle English: from late Latin exhortativus, from exhortat- ‘urged,’ from the verb exhortari.
