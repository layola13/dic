aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/
verb
加重，恶化
To make a problem, situation, or illness worse
例句：Stress can aggravate the symptoms of many illnesses. 压力会加重许多疾病的症状。

例句：His rude remarks aggravated the already tense situation. 他的粗鲁言论加剧了原本已经很紧张的局面。

近义词：worsen, exacerbate, intensify
反义词：alleviate, improve, mitigate
词性：verb
