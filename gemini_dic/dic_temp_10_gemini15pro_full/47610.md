debauchery:/dɪˈbɔːtʃəri/
n.
放荡，沉湎酒色，荒淫
Excessive indulgence in pleasure or sensuality.
He lived a life of debauchery. 他过着放荡的生活。
synonyms: dissipation, profligacy, licentiousness, corruption, depravity
antonyms: abstinence, moderation, virtue, purity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *debaucherie*, from *debaucher* "to corrupt, mar, seduce," from *des-* "away, from" + *bauche* "workshop," perhaps metaphorically implying "to lead astray from work."
