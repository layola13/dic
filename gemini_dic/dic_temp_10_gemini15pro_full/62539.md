enduros:/ɛnˈdʊəroʊz/
noun
耐力赛，耐力越野赛
A long-distance race, especially for motor vehicles, typically over rough terrain.
He competed in several enduros throughout the year. 他一年中参加了几场耐力赛。
近义词: endurance races, rallies
反义词: sprints
