musclier:/ˈmʌskliər/ (US), /ˈmʌskjʊlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更强壮的，肌肉更发达的|Comparative form of muscular: having well-developed muscles.|He looked much musclier after months of weight training.  他经过几个月的重量训练后看起来强壮多了。|  His arms were musclier than before. 他的胳膊比以前更强壮了。 |近义词：stronger, more robust, brawnier, buffer, fitter|反义词：weaker, less muscular, flabbier|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *musculier*, from Late Latin *musculāris*, from Latin *musculus* ("muscle") + *-āris*.
