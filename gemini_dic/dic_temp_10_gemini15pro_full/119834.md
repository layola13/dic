mild:/maɪld/ | adj. | 温和的；轻微的；淡的；文雅的 | gentle and not severe; not strong or harsh; not extreme |  The symptoms were mild. 症状轻微。| synonyms: gentle, moderate, soft, lenient, temperate | antonyms: harsh, severe, strong, extreme, acute | adjective

mild:/maɪld/ | adj. | 温和的；轻微的；淡的；文雅的 | not having a strong taste or smell |  a mild cheese 味道淡的奶酪 | synonyms: bland, light | antonyms: strong, pungent, sharp | adjective

mild:/maɪld/ | adj. | 温和的；轻微的；淡的；文雅的 | (of weather) pleasantly warm and not cold |  It's been a mild winter this year. 今年冬天很暖和。| synonyms: warm, balmy, temperate | antonyms: cold, harsh, frigid | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mildu (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *mildijaz (compare West Frisian myld, Dutch mild, German mild), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“soft, lenient”).
