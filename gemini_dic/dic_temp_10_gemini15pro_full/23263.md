bowstrings:/ˈboʊstrɪŋz/
n.
弓弦
The string of a bow, made of various materials like sinew, hemp, or silk, that propels the arrow.
The archer carefully waxed his bowstrings before the competition.  弓箭手在比赛前仔细地给他的弓弦上了蜡。
