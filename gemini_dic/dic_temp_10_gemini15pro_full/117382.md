mediatress:/ˈmiːdiətərɪs/
n.
女调解人，女仲裁人
a woman who mediates between two parties in a dispute
例句：The mediatress worked tirelessly to find a peaceful resolution. 女调解员不知疲倦地工作，以寻求和平解决办法。

例句：She acted as a mediatress between the warring factions. 她在交战派别之间充当调解人。

近义词：mediator, arbitrator, negotiator, peacemaker
反义词：agitator, instigator
