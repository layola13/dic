afar:/əˈfɑːr/
adverb
远处地；遥远地
at or to a great distance.
The lighthouse could be seen afar.  灯塔在远处可见。
近义词: distantly, remotely
反义词: near, close
词性: adverb

词源:
From Old English *on feorran (at a distance), from feor (far).
