anthracometer:/ˌænθrəˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. |煤尘计，测煤器|An instrument for measuring the amount of carbon dioxide in the air, especially as an indicator of the presence of coal dust. |例句：The anthracometer revealed dangerous levels of coal dust in the mine. 煤尘计显示矿井中煤尘含量危险。

例句：The use of an anthracometer is crucial for monitoring air quality in coal mines. 使用煤尘计对于监测煤矿中的空气质量至关重要。|近义词：coal dust meter |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ánthrax* (coal, carbuncle) + *-meter* (measurer).
