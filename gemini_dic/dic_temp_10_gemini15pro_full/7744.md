anorexia: /ˌænəˈrɛksiə/ (US) /ˌænəˈrɛksiə/ (UK)| n.|厌食症；食欲减退|Anorexia is an eating disorder characterized by an abnormally low body weight, an intense fear of gaining weight and a distorted perception of weight.|She was diagnosed with anorexia nervosa. 她被诊断患有神经性厌食症。|Synonym: loss of appetite, inappetence|Antonym: hyperphagia, bulimia|noun

anorexic: /ˌænəˈrɛksɪk/ (US) /ˌænəˈrɛksɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.| 厌食的；患厌食症的；厌食症患者|Anorexic individuals often have a distorted body image. 厌食症患者通常对自己的身体形象认知扭曲。|He looked gaunt and anorexic. 他看起来憔悴消瘦，像是得了厌食症。|Synonym: emaciated, undernourished|Antonym: obese, overweight|adjective, noun
