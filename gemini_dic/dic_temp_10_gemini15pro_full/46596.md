damask:/ˈdæməsk/ (US), /ˈdæməsk/ (UK)| n. adj.|锦缎，大棉布；锦缎似的|A rich, patterned fabric made from silk, linen, wool, cotton, or synthetic fibers, often with a reversible pattern.|The tablecloth was made of fine damask.|近义词：brocade, satin, tapestry|反义词:muslin, calico|noun
damask:/ˈdæməsk/ (US), /ˈdæməsk/ (UK)| adj.|锦缎似的|Made of or resembling damask.|She wore a damask gown to the ball.|近义词：silken, satiny, rich|反义词:plain, simple|adjective
