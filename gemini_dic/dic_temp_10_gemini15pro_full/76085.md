furries:/ˈfɜːriz/| n.|毛茸茸的动物爱好者；兽迷|People who are interested in anthropomorphic animal characters, often creating and wearing costumes of these characters.  These characters are also referred to as "furries." |Many furries attended the annual convention. 许多毛茸茸的动物爱好者参加了年度大会。|The furry fandom has grown significantly in recent years.  兽迷群体近年来发展壮大。|近义词:anthropomorphic animal enthusiasts|反义词:None|词性:Noun (plural)

furries:/ˈfɜːriz/| adj.(informal, less common)|毛茸茸的|Covered with fur.
