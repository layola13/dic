nonaccommodation:/ˌnɑːnəkɑːməˈdeɪʃən/
n.
不妥协，不迁就，不调节
Lack of adjustment or compromise; refusal to adapt or make concessions.
The nonaccommodation of the two parties led to a stalemate in the negotiations. 双方的不妥协导致谈判陷入僵局。
intransigence, inflexibility, rigidity
compromise, accommodation, flexibility
noun
