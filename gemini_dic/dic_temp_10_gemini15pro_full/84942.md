hanuman:/ˌhʌnʊˈmɑːn/ | n. |哈努曼|A Hindu deity, the monkey god, son of the wind god, and a devotee of Rama. He is known for his strength, devotion, and courage. |Hanuman is a central figure in the Ramayana. 哈努曼是《罗摩衍那》中的中心人物。

Hanuman helped Rama rescue Sita. 哈努曼帮助罗摩营救悉多。|近义词: monkey god |反义词: demon,  Ravana |词性: noun
