granter:/ˈɡræn.tər/ (US), /ˈɡrɑːn.tər/ (UK)| n.| 授权人；批准人；让渡人|A person or entity that grants something, such as a right, privilege, or property.|例句：The granter of the license retains ownership of the software.  许可证的授权人保留软件的所有权。

例句：The granter agreed to the terms of the contract. 授权人同意合同条款。

近义词：donor, giver, grantor|反义词：recipient, receiver|词性：noun
