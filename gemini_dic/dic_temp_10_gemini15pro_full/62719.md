engiscope:/ˈɛŋɡɪskoʊp/
noun
内窥镜; (尤指)检耳镜
a medical instrument for visually examining the interior of a bodily canal or a hollow organ such as the colon, bladder, or stomach.
The doctor used an engiscope to examine the patient's ear canal. 医生用检耳镜检查了病人的耳道。
近义词:endoscope, otoscope
反义词:None
词性:n.
