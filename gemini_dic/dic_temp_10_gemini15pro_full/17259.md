baying:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/ | n. | 吠叫，咆哮；强烈抗议 | The baying of the hounds echoed through the forest. 猎犬的吠叫声在森林中回荡。|  The baying of the crowd grew louder as the referee made the controversial call.  裁判做出争议判罚时，人群的抗议声越来越大。 | 近义词：howling, barking, clamor | 反义词：whimpering, silence | 词性:名词

bay:/beɪ/ | n. | 海湾；月桂树；隔间 |  We sailed across the bay. 我们航行穿过海湾。 |  She was crowned with a wreath of bay. 她被戴上了月桂花环。| 近义词：gulf, cove, inlet (for body of water); laurel (for tree) | 反义词：promontory, headland (for body of water) | 词性:名词


bay:/beɪ/ | vi. | (猎犬)吠叫；(人群)大声叫喊 | The hounds bayed at the fox. 猎犬对着狐狸吠叫。 | The crowd bayed for blood. 群众叫嚣着要杀人。 | 近义词：howl, bark, yell | 反义词：whimper | 词性:动词


bay:/beɪ/ | vt. | 向…吠叫；大声呼喊 |  The hounds bayed the moon. 猎犬对着月亮吠叫。 | The crowd bayed their disapproval. 群众大声表达他们的不满。| 近义词：bark at, howl at, yell at | 反义词：whisper | 词性:动词



词源:  From Old French *baiier, from Vulgar Latin *batare (frequentative of *batere "to gape, open wide"), of imitative origin.  (源自古法语 *baiier，来自通俗拉丁语 *batare（*batere “张口，张大”的重复形式），是拟声词。)

PHRASE->
at bay:  The dogs held the stag at bay.  猎犬把雄鹿逼得走投无路。
bring to bay: The police finally brought the escaped convict to bay. 警方最终将逃犯逼入绝境。
hold/keep at bay: She managed to hold her attackers at bay with a stick. 她用一根棍子成功地阻止了袭击者靠近。
bay for blood: The crowd bayed for blood after the horrific crime.  可怕的罪行发生后，人群叫嚣着要杀人。
bay the moon:  The lonely dog bayed the moon.  孤独的狗对着月亮吠叫。

注意事项:
baying 作为名词，常用于描述猎犬或人群的叫喊声。
bay 作为动词，既可以是不及物动词也可以是及物动词。
bay 作为名词，还可以指海湾、月桂树或隔间，需要根据上下文区分含义。
