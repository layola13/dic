absinthian: /ˌæbsɪnˈθiən/
adjective|苦艾酒的；苦艾酒爱好者的；像苦艾酒的|Of or relating to absinthe; suggestive of the effects of absinthe; a devotee of absinthe.|The bar had a distinctly absinthian atmosphere. 酒吧里有一种明显的苦艾酒氛围。|
近义词:alcoholic, intoxicating|反义词:sober, temperate|词性:adjective

absinthian: /ˌæbsɪnˈθiən/
noun|苦艾酒爱好者|A devotee of absinthe.|He was a well-known absinthian in the Parisian art scene. 他是巴黎艺术圈著名的苦艾酒爱好者。|
近义词:drinker, imbiber|反义词:teetotaler, abstainer|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *absinthin*, from Latin *absinthium*, from Greek *apsinthion*, from *apsinthein* "to be undrinkable," perhaps from *a-* "not" + *psinthos* "enjoyment."  The connection with wormwood is due to its bitter taste.
