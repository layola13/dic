arhizous:/eɪˈraɪzəs/ | adj. | 无根的；没有真正根的 | Lacking true roots.  | Example: Some parasitic plants are arhizous, deriving nutrients directly from their host.  一些寄生植物是无根的，直接从宿主那里获取营养。

Example:  Arhizous fungi obtain nutrients by absorbing them from their surroundings. 无根真菌通过从周围环境吸收养分来获取营养。 | Near synonyms: rootless, non-rhizomatous | Antonyms: rhizomatous, rooted | Word type: adjective
