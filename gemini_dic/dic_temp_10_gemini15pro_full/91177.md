hydrargyrate:/haɪˈdrɑːrdʒɪˌreɪt/ | n. |汞齐化物，水银化合物|A compound formed with mercury. |Mercury can hydrargyrate other metals like gold and silver. 水银可以与金、银等其他金属形成汞齐。|Example 2:  The hydrargyrate was found to be toxic.  这种汞化合物被发现有毒。|近义词:mercurial compound |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + -argyr- (silver) + -ate (suffix forming verbs and nouns from Latin).  Ultimately from Greek hydor (water) and arguros (silver), referring to mercury's liquid, silvery appearance.
