homeboy:/ˈhoʊmˌbɔɪ/| n.|老乡，哥们，发小，(尤指来自同一街区或社区的)同乡|A close friend or associate, typically a male, often used to refer to someone from the same neighborhood or social group. |例句：My homeboy from back in the day just got married. 我以前的发小结婚了。

例句：He's my homeboy, we grew up together. 他是我的哥们，我们一起长大。|近义词：friend, buddy, pal, mate, dude|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
