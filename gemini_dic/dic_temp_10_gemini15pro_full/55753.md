divergingly:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 分叉地，发散地 | In a way that separates or goes in different directions. | The two paths diverged, leading divergingly into the forest.  两条小路分叉，朝着不同的方向伸向森林。| Synonyms: differently, separately, disparately | Antonyms: convergently, similarly, uniformly | 词性: 副词

divergingly:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 越来越不同地，差异越来越大地 | In a way that becomes increasingly different or distinct. | Their opinions on the matter diverged divergingly over time. 他们在这个问题上的观点随着时间的推移差异越来越大。| Synonyms: increasingly differently, progressively dissimilarly | Antonyms: increasingly similarly, progressively alike | 词性: 副词
