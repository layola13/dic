discumber:/dɪsˈkʌmbər/ | v. |  解除负担，使摆脱累赘; 解救；使脱离困境 | To free from something that encumbers; relieve of a burden or impediment. |  He discumbered himself of his heavy coat. 他脱掉了沉重的大衣。 | 近义词: disencumber, relieve, unburden | 反义词: encumber, burden, load | 词性: 动词

词源:  From dis- + encumber, from Old French encombrer "to hinder," from en- "in" + combre "obstacle," from Latin cumulus "heap."
