excitations:/ˌɛksɪˈteɪʃənz/
n.
兴奋，激动；刺激；激发
The excitations of city life.  城市的刺激生活。
The excitations caused by the news. 这条消息引起的兴奋。
Excitations are disturbances of a physical system from a state of equilibrium. 激发是指物理系统偏离平衡状态的扰动。
The laser produces excitations in the atoms of the material. 激光在材料的原子中产生激发。
Synonym:stimulations, arousals, thrills
Antonym:inhibitions, suppressions, calmness
noun
