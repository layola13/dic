genres:/ˈʒɑːnrəz/ (US), /ˈʒɒnrəz/ (UK)| n.|类型，种类|A category of artistic composition, as in music or literature, characterized by similarities in form, style, or subject matter.|This bookstore has a wide variety of genres, from sci-fi to romance.  这家书店有各种各样的类型，从科幻到浪漫。|synonyms: categories, types, classes, sorts, kinds|antonyms:  (genres doesn't have a direct antonym, but one might consider "heterogeneity" or "miscellany" as conceptually opposite).|noun
