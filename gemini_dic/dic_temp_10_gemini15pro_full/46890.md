darius:/ˈdɛəriəs/| n. |大流士（男子名）|A male given name, of Persian origin, meaning "possessing goodness" or "rich and kingly." It's derived from the Old Persian name *Dārayavauš*.|Darius the Great was a powerful Persian king. 大流士大帝是一位强大的波斯国王。|Darius led his army to victory. 大流士率领他的军队取得了胜利。| 近义词: (无直接近义词，可考虑其他男性名字，如: Cyrus, Xerxes)| 反义词: (人名无反义词) |词性:noun
