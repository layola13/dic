muttina:/muˈtiːnə/ | n. |叛乱，兵变；暴动|A rebellion against authority, especially by sailors or soldiers against their officers.| The muttina on the ship was quickly suppressed by the captain. 船上的叛乱很快就被船长镇压了。| 近义词:rebellion, revolt, uprising, insurrection | 反义词: obedience, submission | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Italian *mutina*, from Vulgar Latin *movĭtīna*, from Latin *mōtus* "movement," past participle of *movēre* "to move."
