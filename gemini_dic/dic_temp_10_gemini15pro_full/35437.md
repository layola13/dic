co-referential:/ˌkoʊˌrɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|共同指代的；指称相同的|Referring to the same thing or person.|例句：The pronouns "he" and "John" are co-referential in this sentence. 在这个句子中，代词“他”和“约翰”是指称相同的。|例句：The expressions "the morning star" and "the evening star" are co-referential, both referring to Venus.  “晨星”和“昏星”这两个表达是指称相同的，都指的是金星。|近义词：coreferent, synonymous, equivalent|反义词:non-coreferential, unrelated|词性:adjective
