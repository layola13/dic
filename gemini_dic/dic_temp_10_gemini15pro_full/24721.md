bronzen:/ˈbrɒnzən/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːnzn/ (US)| adj.|青铜制的，青铜色的|Made of or resembling bronze; having a bronze-like color.|The statue was bronzen and gleamed in the sun. 这座雕像是青铜制的，在阳光下闪闪发光。|She had a bronzen tan from her vacation. 她度假回来，晒成了青铜色。|近义词：bronze, copper-colored, brassy|反义词:silver, golden|词性:形容词

词源: From *bronze* + *-en*, a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, meaning "made of" or "having the nature of". *Bronze* comes from the Italian *bronzo*, likely from the Persian *berenj*, meaning "brass".
