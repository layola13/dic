blindish:/ˈblaɪndɪʃ/| adj.|有点瞎的，视力不太好的|Somewhat blind; having imperfect vision.|例句：His eyesight is a bit blindish these days.  他最近视力不太好。

例句：The old man was becoming blindish.  这位老人视力越来越差了。|近义词：nearsighted, shortsighted, visually impaired|反义词:sighted, keen-sighted|词性:形容词

词源:blind + -ish
