missamples:/mɪsˈsæmpəlz/ | n. | 错误样本；误采样 | Incorrect or improperly collected samples. These samples can skew research results and lead to inaccurate conclusions. | The study was compromised by missamples taken from the contaminated area. 这项研究因从污染区域采集的错误样本而受到损害。| The missamples led to a recall of the product. 错误的样本导致了产品的召回。 | 近义词: faulty samples, flawed samples, bad samples | 反义词: accurate samples, valid samples, representative samples | 词性: 名词


词源:  "missamples" is a combination of the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "bad") and the noun "samples."  "Sample" comes from the Old French "essample," ultimately derived from the Latin "exemplum," meaning "example."
