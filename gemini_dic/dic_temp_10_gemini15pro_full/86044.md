healthless:/ˈhelθləs/| adj.|不健康的，有害健康的|Lacking health; unhealthy.  Characterized by a lack of health or a tendency to cause poor health.|例句：A healthless diet can lead to many problems.  不健康的饮食会导致很多问题。|例句：Living in a polluted city is a healthless lifestyle.  生活在一个污染的城市是一种不健康的生活方式。|近义词：unhealthy, insalubrious, noxious, detrimental|反义词:healthy, wholesome, salubrious|词性:adjective

词源:
From health + -less.  "Health" comes from Old English *hǣlþ, meaning "wholeness, a being whole, sound or well," related to "whole." The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking."
