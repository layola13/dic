nonshatters:/ˌnɒnˈʃætəz/ /ˌnɑːnˈʃætərz/ | adj. | 不破碎的，不易碎的 | Not shattering; resistant to shattering.  A material that doesn't break into pieces easily. | This new type of glass is nonshatters, making it safer for use in car windshields. 这种新型玻璃不易碎，用在汽车挡风玻璃上更安全。 | 近义词：shatterproof, unbreakable, toughened | 反义词: brittle, fragile | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "shatter" (verb meaning "to break suddenly into many pieces").  This word is formed by adding the negative prefix "non-" to the verb "shatter," creating an adjective that describes something that does not shatter.
