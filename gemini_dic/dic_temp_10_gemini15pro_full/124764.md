mrt:/ɛmɑːrˈtiː/ /ɛmɑːrˈtiː/ | n. | Mass Rapid Transit (地铁) | An electric railway system in urban areas, usually underground, providing fast and frequent public transport. |  例句：I take the MRT to work every day. 我每天坐地铁上班。

例句：The MRT is a convenient way to get around the city. 地铁是在城市里出行的一种便捷方式。| 近义词：subway, metro, underground | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly bus, taxi, car) | 词性: noun
