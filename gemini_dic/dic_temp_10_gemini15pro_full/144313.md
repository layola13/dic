outspreads:/ˌaʊtˈsprɛdz/ | v. (outspreads, outspreading, outspread) | 展开，铺开 | extends over a large area; spreads out | The eagle outspreads its wings.  老鹰展开翅膀。| 近义词：extend, expand, unfurl | 反义词：contract, fold, shrink | 词性：动词

outspread:/ˈaʊtˌsprɛd/ | adj. | 展开的，铺开的 | extended, spread out | The bird soared with outspread wings. 这只鸟展开翅膀翱翔。| 近义词：extended, expanded, unfurled | 反义词：contracted, folded, shrunk | 词性：形容词


词源: From Old English *ūt + sprǣdan (to spread).
