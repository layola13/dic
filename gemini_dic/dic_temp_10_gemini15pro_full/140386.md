oilman:/ˈɔɪlmən/|n.|石油工人；石油商|A man who works in the oil industry, especially one who drills for or refines oil.|The oilman made a fortune during the oil boom.  这位石油商在石油繁荣时期发了大财。|Synonyms: oil worker, petroleum worker, roustabout (for a laborer)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
