divisionor: /dɪˈvɪʒənər/ | n. |除数，分隔物| A number by which another number is divided.  Something that divides or separates. | Example 1: In the equation 10 ÷ 2 = 5, 2 is the divisionor. 在等式 10 ÷ 2 = 5 中，2 是除数。

Example 2: The Great Wall acted as a divisionor between ancient China and the nomadic tribes.  长城是古代中国和游牧部落之间的分隔物。| 近义词：divisor, separator | 反义词: multiplicand, uniter | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *dividour, *divysour, from Anglo-Norman divisour, from Old French deviseor, from deviser (“to divide”), from Latin dīvīdere (“to divide”).
