centrosphere:/ˈsɛntrəsfɪr/
noun
地心圈;中心圈
The dense, central part of the Earth's core.  The centrosphere is primarily composed of iron and nickel and is believed to be solid.
The intense pressure at the centrosphere makes it incredibly dense. 地心圈的巨大压力使其密度极高。

The study of the centrosphere helps scientists understand the Earth's magnetic field. 对地心圈的研究帮助科学家了解地球磁场。
Synonym: core, inner core
Antonym:  crust, mantle
