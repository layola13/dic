chemotherapeutic: /ˌkiːmoʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/ | adj. n. | 化疗的；化疗药物 | Relating to or used in chemotherapy; a chemotherapeutic agent. |  The doctor prescribed a chemotherapeutic drug to treat the cancer. 医生开了化疗药物来治疗癌症。 | The patient underwent chemotherapeutic treatment. 病人接受了化疗。 | Synonyms: antineoplastic, cytotoxic | Antonyms:  (As a noun - there is no direct opposite for a treatment.  One could potentially use "palliative" as an antonym if speaking of treatment approaches, but this is context-dependent) | adjective, noun

chemotherapy: /ˌkiːmoʊˈθɛrəpi/ | n. | 化疗 | The treatment of disease by the use of chemical substances, especially for cancer. | The patient started chemotherapy last week. 病人上周开始化疗。| Chemotherapy can have debilitating side effects. 化疗会有使人衰弱的副作用。 | Synonyms: chemo | Antonyms: (No direct antonym; one might consider alternative treatments like surgery, radiation, immunotherapy etc. depending on the context) | noun
