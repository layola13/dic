haversack: /ˈhævərsæk/ | n. | 背包，干粮袋 | A bag, typically made of canvas or other strong material, carried on the back or over the shoulder and used for carrying supplies, especially by soldiers or hikers. |  例句：The soldier carried his rations in his haversack. 士兵把他的口粮装在他的背包里。

例句：The hiker filled her haversack with food and water for the day.  徒步旅行者在她的背包里装满了当天所需的食物和水。| 近义词：backpack, rucksack, knapsack | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 suitcase (行李箱) if context refers to luggage) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *havresac*, from Middle Dutch *haversack* (modern Dutch *haverszak*), literally "oat sack".  Originally a bag for carrying oats for horses, later generalized to any bag for provisions.
