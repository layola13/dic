nehiloth:/nɛhɪˈloʊθ/ /nəˈhiːlɒθ/
|n.|
|上行之诗; 登阶之诗|
|A term found in the titles of five Psalms (84, 88, 120, 121, 122) in the Hebrew Bible.  Its meaning is uncertain, but it is traditionally interpreted as referring to songs sung by pilgrims ascending to Jerusalem for festivals.  Some scholars suggest it may relate to a musical instrument or a melodic style. |
|Psalm 84 is one of the nehiloth.|诗篇84篇是上行之诗之一。|
|Psalms of Ascents, Songs of Degrees|
