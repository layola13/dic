consequently:/ˈkɒnsɪkwəntli/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwentli/
adverb
因此，所以
as a result; therefore
例句：He lost his job and consequently had to move. 他失去了工作，因此不得不搬家。

例句：She was ill and consequently couldn't come to the party. 她病了，所以不能来参加聚会。

近义词：therefore, thus, hence, accordingly, as a result
反义词：nevertheless, nonetheless, however
词性: adverb

词源: From Late Latin *consequenter, from Latin consequens, present participle of consequi "to follow".
