foresting:/ˈfɔːrɪstɪŋ/ /ˈfɑːrɪstɪŋ/
verb
造林; 植林; 绿化
The process of establishing a forest on land that has been without trees for a long period of time, or which has never been forested.
The government has invested heavily in foresting the barren hillsides. 政府已大力投资绿化荒山。
afforestation, reforestation, planting
deforestation, logging, clearing
v.
