democrat-wagons:/ˈdɛməˌkræt ˌwæɡənz/| n.|民主驴车，民主党竞选车|A derogatory term referring to vehicles, often SUVs or minivans, perceived as being driven by affluent, liberal, or Democratic voters, especially in suburban areas.  It implies a disconnect from the working class and a perceived hypocrisy in espousing progressive values while enjoying a comfortable lifestyle.|例句：They mocked the democrat-wagons lined up outside the organic food store. 他们嘲笑停在有机食品店外面的那些民主驴车。|例句：The cartoon depicted a democrat-wagon with a "Coexist" bumper sticker. 这幅漫画描绘了一辆贴着“共存”保险杠贴纸的民主党竞选车。|近义词：Volvo-driving liberals, limousine liberals|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
