defrayment:/dɪˈfreɪmənt/| n.|支付，付款，支付的款项|The defrayment of the travel expenses was a relief.  旅行费用的支付令人宽慰。|Etymology: From Old French defraier "to break up, destroy, annul, pay," from de- "down, away" + fraier "to rub, break," from Latinfricare "to rub."  The sense of "pay expenses" arose from the notion of "doing away with a debt."|例句：The company will provide defrayment for all business-related travel expenses. 公司将支付所有与商务相关的旅行费用。

The grant provided defrayment for tuition and living costs.  这笔赠款支付了学费和生活费。|近义词：payment, settlement, reimbursement|反义词:charge, expense, debt|词性:noun
