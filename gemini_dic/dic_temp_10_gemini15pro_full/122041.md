missyish:/ˈmɪsiɪʃ/| adj.|小姐气的，娇气的，女孩子气的|Resembling or characteristic of a young girl, especially in a way considered affected or frivolous.|She gave him a missyish look. 她给了他一个娇气的眼神。|近义词：girlish, childish, coy, demure|反义词：boyish, masculine, mature|词性:形容词
Etymology: missy + -ish.  Missy is a diminutive of miss, meaning a young unmarried woman or girl. The suffix -ish transforms a noun into an adjective.
