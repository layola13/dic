moccasins: /ˈmɑːkəsɪnz/ (US) /ˈmɒkəsɪnz/ (UK)| n. | 鹿皮鞋；软皮鞋 |A soft leather shoe, originally worn by Native Americans, made of deerskin or other soft leather and typically having no heel. |例句：She wore a pair of beaded moccasins. 她穿着一双串珠的鹿皮鞋。

例句：He slipped his feet into his comfortable moccasins. 他把脚伸进舒适的软皮鞋里。

近义词：slippers, loafers |反义词: boots, shoes |词性: noun
