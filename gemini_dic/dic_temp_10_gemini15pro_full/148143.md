oversensitivenesses: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvnəsɪz/
| n. | 过敏，过度敏感 | The state of being excessively or unduly sensitive; excessive susceptibility to emotional or physical stimuli. |  Her oversensitivenesses made it difficult for her to handle criticism. 她过度敏感，很难接受批评。 | hypersensitivity, touchiness | insensitivity, indifference | noun

oversensitive: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/
| adj. | 过度敏感的 |  Excessively or unduly sensitive; easily offended or upset. | He's oversensitive about his height. 他对自己的身高过于敏感。 | touchy, thin-skinned | insensitive, thick-skinned | adjective

**词源:**

From over- + sensitive + -ness.  "Over-"  indicates "excessive" or "too much." "Sensitive" comes from Late Latin *sensitivus*, from Latin *sensus* "feeling, sense."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
