decamping:/dɪˈkæmpɪŋ/
|n.| 潜逃，撤离，突然离开 |The decamping of the CEO raised suspicions. 首席执行官的潜逃引起了怀疑。|
|v. (decamp)|潜逃，撤离，突然离开|The circus decamped in the middle of the night. 马戏团半夜撤走了。|
| | |They decamped from the city to the countryside. 他们从城市搬到了乡下。|
|近义词：fleeing, escaping, absconding, departing, leaving|反义词：arriving, staying, remaining|
|词性：名词，动词|


词源：
From French *décamper*, from *dé-* (prefix indicating reversal or removal) + *camper* (to encamp).  Ultimately from Latin *campus* (field).
