mis-parsed:/ˌmɪsˈpɑːrst/ | adj. | 错误解析的 | Incorrectly analyzed or interpreted, especially by a computer program.  | The sentence was mis-parsed by the software, leading to an incorrect translation.  这个句子被软件错误地解析了，导致翻译不正确。 |  The data was mis-parsed due to a bug in the code.  由于代码中的一个错误，数据被错误地解析了。 | 近义词: misinterpreted, misread, incorrectly analyzed | 反义词: correctly parsed, accurately interpreted | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "parsed" (past participle of "parse," from Latin "pars" meaning "part").
