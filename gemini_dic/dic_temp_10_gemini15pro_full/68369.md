fallibly:/ˈfælɪbli/| adv.|易错地，可能犯错地|In a way that is capable of making mistakes or being wrong. |Humans, fallibly, interpreted the data. 人类以容易出错的方式解释了这些数据。|Even the most experienced doctors can fallibly diagnose a patient's illness. 即使是最有经验的医生也可能误诊病人的病情。|近义词：erroneously, imperfectly, inaccurately|反义词：infallibly, perfectly, accurately|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *fallibilis, from Latin fallere "to deceive, disappoint, fail, err" + -ibilis "-ible."
