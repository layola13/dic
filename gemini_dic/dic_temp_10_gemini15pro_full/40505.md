contrivers:/kənˈtraɪvərz/
noun
发明者，设计者，策划者
People who invent, design, or plan something, especially something clever or complicated.
The contrivers of this plan deserve an award.  这个计划的设计者应该得到奖励。
synonyms: inventors, designers, planners, schemers, architects
antonyms: destroyers, dismantlers, saboteurs
