burro:/ˈbɜːroʊ/ (US), /ˈbʌrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|驴; 毛驴|A small donkey, especially one used as a pack animal.|例句：The burro carried a heavy load up the mountain. 毛驴驮着沉重的货物上山。

例句：We used burros to carry our supplies on the camping trip. 我们用驴子来搬运我们露营旅行的物资。


近义词：donkey, ass|反义词: None|词性:noun
