neurapophysis:/ˌnjʊərəˈpɒfɪsɪs/ /ˌnʊrəˈpɑːfɪsɪs/
n.
神经弓
A bony process projecting dorsally from the vertebral centrum, that with the vertebral body forms the vertebral arch, enclosing the spinal cord.
The neurapophysis of the cervical vertebra is fractured.  颈椎的神经弓骨折了。
近义词:neural arch
反义词: (None - anatomical structures generally lack direct antonyms)
词性:noun
