noncustomers:/ˈnɒnˈkʌstəmərz/ (British) /ˈnɑːnˈkʌstəmərz/ (American)| n.|非客户，非顾客|People who are not customers of a particular business or service.  Those who do not purchase goods or services from a specific company.|例句：The company is trying to understand the needs of its noncustomers.  这家公司正试图了解其非客户的需求。

例句：Marketing to noncustomers can be a challenging but rewarding strategy.  向非客户推销可能是一项具有挑战性但回报丰厚的策略。|近义词：non-clients, prospects, potential customers|反义词:customers, clients|词性:Noun
