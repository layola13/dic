meaningly:/ˈmiːnɪŋfʊli/| adv.|有意义地，意味深长地|In a way that has meaning or significance; expressively.|She smiled meaningfully at him. 她意味深长地对他笑了笑。|近义词：significantly, expressively, pointedly|反义词：meaninglessly, vacantly|词性:副词
Etymology: From meaning + -ly.  Meaning comes from Old English *mēnung (unattested), from *mēnan "to mean, intend, signify," from Proto-Germanic *mainijan "to think, have in mind."
