nabbers:/ˈnæbəz/| n.|邻居；朋友；伙伴|A colloquial and often friendly term for "neighbors" or "friends."|例句：I'm going to the pub with my nabbers tonight. 今晚我要和我的朋友们去酒吧。

例句：The nabbers are having a barbecue this weekend.  邻居们这周末要举行烧烤。|近义词：friends, pals, mates, buddies, neighbors|反义词:enemies, foes|词性:noun

词源:Shortened form of "neighbors," influenced by similar colloquialisms like "gabbers."
