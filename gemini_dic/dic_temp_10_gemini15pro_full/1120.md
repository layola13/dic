accessorinesses: /əkˈsɛsərɪnɪsɪz/ | n. | 附属品；配件；次要性 | The state or quality of being accessory or subordinate; secondary or nonessential details or features. |  The accessorinesses of the costume, such as the hat and gloves, completed the look.  这套服装的配件，例如帽子和手套，使整体造型更加完整。| synonyms: accessories, appendages, additions, supplement, trappings | antonyms: essentials, necessities | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From accessory + -ness + -es (plural).  Ultimately from Latin *accessorius, from accedere "to approach".

USAGE->
There are few examples of usage because "accessorinesses" is a rarely used and awkward plural form.  It's much more common to use "accessories."  However, here are some examples using "accessories" in different tenses and contexts:
