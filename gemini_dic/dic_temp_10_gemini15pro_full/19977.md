biogas:/ˈbaɪ.oʊɡæs/| n.|沼气|A mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide, produced by the anaerobic decomposition of organic matter, such as manure or agricultural waste.|Biogas can be used as a renewable energy source. 沼气可以作为一种可再生能源。|Biogas is produced in a biogas digester. 沼气在沼气池中产生。|synonyms: methane, natural gas|antonyms: fossil fuels|noun
