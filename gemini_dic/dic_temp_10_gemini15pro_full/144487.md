outthrows:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊz/
verb
投掷得比…更远；比…投掷得更远
Throws (something) further or with more force than (someone).
The athlete outthrew his competitors by a significant margin.  这位运动员以明显的优势超过了他的竞争对手。
He consistently outthrows other pitchers in the league. 他一直比联盟中的其他投手投得更远。
近义词:surpasses, exceeds, outdistances
反义词:falls short of, underperforms
词性:v.
