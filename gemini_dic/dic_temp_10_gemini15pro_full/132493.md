nondeserts:/ˌnɒnˈdɛzəts/ | n. | 非沙漠地区; 非荒漠 |Areas that are not deserts; inhabited and cultivated regions. | Many animals thrive in nondeserts due to the availability of water and vegetation. 许多动物在非沙漠地区茁壮成长，因为那里有充足的水和植被。| 近义词: inhabited areas, cultivated lands | 反义词: deserts, wastelands | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "desert" (from Old French "desert," ultimately from Latin "desertum," meaning "abandoned place").
