biarticulate:/baɪ.ɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
adjective
双关节的
Having two joints; articulated twice.
The biarticulate muscle spans two joints. 这块双关节肌肉跨越两个关节。
近义词:double-jointed
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

biarticulate:/baɪ.ɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
adjective
(语音学)双重清晰发音的
(phonetics)  pronounced with two distinct articulations.
The "k" sound in "pack" can be considered biarticulate. “pack”中的“k”音可以被认为是双重清晰发音的。
