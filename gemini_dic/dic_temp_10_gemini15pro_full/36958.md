colourless:/ˈkʌlərləs/ /ˈkʌlər.ləs/| adj.|无色的，苍白的，平淡的|Lacking colour; pale.|The water is colourless.  水是无色的。|近义词：pale, bland, uncolored, achromatic|反义词：colourful, bright, vibrant|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From color + -less.  Ultimately from Latin color.
