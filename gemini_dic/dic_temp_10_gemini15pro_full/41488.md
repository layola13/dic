cornstalk: /ˈkɔːrnstɔːk/ (US), /ˈkɔːnstɔːk/ (UK)| n. |玉米杆；玉米秆|The dried cornstalks were used to build a scarecrow. 干枯的玉米杆被用来做了一个稻草人。|例句：The farmer piled the cornstalks high after the harvest.  农民在收割后把玉米杆堆得很高。

例句：Children played hide-and-seek amongst the tall cornstalks. 孩子们在高高的玉米杆丛中玩捉迷藏。

近义词：stalk, stem|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "grain/kernel" as the edible part opposite the stalk)|词性: noun
