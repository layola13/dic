eluxate: /ɪˈlʌkseɪt/
verb
使奢华；使豪华
to make luxurious or elegant.
The hotel was eluxated with imported marble and crystal chandeliers.  这家酒店用进口大理石和水晶吊灯装饰得富丽堂皇。
The designer eluxated the simple dress with intricate beading.  设计师用复杂的串珠装饰这件简单的连衣裙。
Synonyms: enrich, enhance, embellish
Antonyms: simplify, impoverish
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "luxus" (luxury) + "-ate" (verb-forming suffix).
