hyperbolae:/haɪˈpɜːrbəliː/
n.
双曲线（复数）
A hyperbola is a type of open curve with two branches, formed by the intersection of a plane with a cone.
The graph of the equation xy = 1 is a hyperbola.  这个方程 xy = 1 的图形是一条双曲线。
Hyperbolae are often studied in analytic geometry.  双曲线经常在解析几何中被研究。
近义词:conic sections
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider ellipse, parabola as contrasting conic sections)
词性:noun


hyperbola:/haɪˈpɜːrbələ/
n.
双曲线（单数）
A hyperbola is a type of open curve with two branches, formed by the intersection of a plane with a cone.
The path of a comet can sometimes be a hyperbola.  彗星的轨迹有时可以是双曲线。
The shape of the cooling tower resembles a hyperbola. 冷却塔的形状类似于双曲线。
近义词:conic section
反义词:(No direct antonym, but could consider ellipse, parabola as contrasting conic sections)
词性:noun
