gentleman's:/ˈdʒɛntlmənz/| adj. n.|绅士的；男士的；男厕|A possessive form of gentleman, used to indicate something belonging to or associated with a gentleman or gentlemen.  It can refer to items, places, or qualities typically associated with a gentleman.| This is the gentleman's agreement we made. 这是我们达成的君子协定。|近义词：men's, masculine|反义词：ladies', women's|词性:形容词/名词

Etymology:
From *gentleman* + *-s*.  *Gentleman* comes from *gentle* (meaning "noble, of good family") + *man*. The suffix *-s* indicates possession.
