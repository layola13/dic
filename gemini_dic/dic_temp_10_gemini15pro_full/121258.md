misestimations:/ˌmɪsˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃənz/
n.
错误估计；估错
An act of misestimating something.
The project was delayed due to misestimations of the cost.  这个项目由于成本估算错误而延期。
Synonyms: miscalculations, misjudgments, underestimations, overestimations
Antonyms: accurate estimations, correct assessments
Noun

misestimate: /ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪt/
v.
错误估计；估错
To estimate something incorrectly.
We misevaluated the potential risks. 我们错误地估计了潜在的风险。
Synonyms: miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate
Antonyms: accurately estimate, correctly assess
