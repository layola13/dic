bewitcheries:/bɪˈwɪtʃəriːz/
|n.| 魅力，施魔法，迷惑力
|The bewitcheries of the tropical island made it hard to leave.  热带岛屿的魅力使人难以离开。|
|例句：
The actress used all her bewitcheries to charm the audience. 女演员用尽浑身解数来迷住观众。

Her bewitcheries were legendary.  她的魅力是传奇的。|
|近义词：charms, enchantments, allure, magic, spells|
|反义词：repulsiveness, ugliness|
|词性: 名词 (plural of bewitchery)|
