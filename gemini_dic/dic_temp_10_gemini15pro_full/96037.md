indebtedness:/ɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs/ | n. | 负债，受恩，亏欠 | The state of owing something (usually money) to someone or something.  A feeling of gratitude for a favor or service. |  例句：The company's indebtedness increased significantly after the acquisition. 该公司在收购后负债显著增加。

例句：She felt a deep sense of indebtedness to her mentor. 她对导师深感亏欠。

| 近义词：debt, obligation, liability, gratitude | 反义词：credit, asset | 词性：名词
