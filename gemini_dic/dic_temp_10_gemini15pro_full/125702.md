munged:/ˈmʌndʒd/ | adj. | 混乱的，糟糕的，被篡改的 |  Damaged or spoiled, especially in a clumsy or careless way; altered or manipulated, often in a way that makes the original meaning or data unclear or inaccurate. From the verb "to munge," likely a variant of "mangle." |  The data was completely munged after the system crash.  数据在系统崩溃后完全混乱了。| Synonyms: garbled, corrupted, botched, mangled | Antonyms: pristine, intact, uncorrupted | adjective

/ˈmʌndʒd/ | v. | 弄乱，损坏，篡改 | To damage or spoil something, especially in a clumsy or careless way; to alter or manipulate data, often in a way that makes the original meaning unclear or inaccurate.  Likely a variant of "mangle." | He munged the recipe by adding too much salt. 他把食谱弄糟了，加了太多的盐。| Synonyms: garble, corrupt, botch, mangle | Antonyms: preserve, protect, maintain | verb
