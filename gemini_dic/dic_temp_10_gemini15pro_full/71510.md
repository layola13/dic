flanks:/flæŋks/ | n. | 侧翼，侧面，胁腹 |The sides of something, especially the sides of a body. |The soldiers attacked the enemy's left flank. 士兵们攻击了敌人的左翼。

The building's flanks are covered in ivy.  建筑物的侧面爬满了常春藤。| synonyms: sides, wings, flanks | antonyms: front, center, rear | noun

flank:/flæŋk/ | v. | 位于…的侧面，守卫…的侧翼 |To be situated on each side of something or somebody. |The house is flanked by two large trees. 这栋房子的两侧各有一棵大树。

Guards flanked the president as he walked through the crowd.  总统穿过人群时，警卫守卫在他的两侧。 | synonyms: border, edge, line | antonyms: face, confront | verb
