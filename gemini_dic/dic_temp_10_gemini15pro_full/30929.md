chairing:/ˈtʃeərɪŋ/| v. n.|主持，担任主席|The act of presiding over a meeting or committee.  It can also refer to the position of chairperson.|例句：She is chairing the meeting today. 她今天主持会议。|例句：Chairing the committee is a demanding role. 担任委员会主席是一个要求很高的角色。|近义词：presiding, leading, moderating|反义词:attending, participating|词性:动名词

词源: From the noun "chair" (meaning the seat of a presiding officer) + "-ing" (present participle suffix). Ultimately from Old English "cearr" and Latin "cathedra".
