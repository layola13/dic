commitment:/kəˈmɪtmənt/| n.|承诺，保证；投入，奉献；承担的义务|A commitment is a promise or pledge to do something. It also refers to the state of being dedicated to a cause, activity, or relationship. |She made a commitment to help the homeless. 她承诺要帮助无家可归的人。|近义词：promise, pledge, dedication, engagement|反义词：disengagement, apathy, indifference|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *committement*, from commit + -ment.  The verb *commit* comes from Latin *committere*, meaning "to unite, connect, entrust, perpetrate".
