antiprofiteering:/ˌæntiprɒfɪˈtɪərɪŋ/
antiprofiteering: |n.| 反暴利，禁止牟取暴利|The act of preventing or opposing profiteering, especially during times of shortages or emergencies.|The government introduced antiprofiteering measures after the hurricane. 政府在飓风过后出台了反暴利措施。|Synonyms: price control, price regulation|Antonyms: profiteering, exploitation|noun


antiprofiteering: |adj.| 反暴利的|Designed to prevent or oppose profiteering.|Antiprofiteering laws were enacted to protect consumers.  制定了反暴利法以保护消费者。|Synonyms: price-controlling, regulatory|Antonyms: exploitative, profiteering|adjective


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
profiteering (noun): making excessive profits, especially unfairly or illegally
