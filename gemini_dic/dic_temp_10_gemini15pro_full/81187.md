grandiloquence:/ˌɡrændɪˈləkwəns/| n.|夸夸其谈；浮夸的言辞；|A lofty, often pompous style of speaking or writing, characterized by exaggerated claims or bombastic language. | His grandiloquence impressed no one; everyone saw through his pretentious facade. 他的夸夸其谈并没有打动任何人；每个人都看穿了他虚伪的表面。

The politician's speech was filled with grandiloquence, but lacked substance.  这位政客的演讲充满了浮夸的言辞，但缺乏实质内容。|近义词：bombast, pomposity, rhetoric, verbosity|反义词:simplicity, conciseness, modesty, understatement|noun
