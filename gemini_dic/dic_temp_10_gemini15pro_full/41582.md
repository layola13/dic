corporealist:/kɔːˈpɔːriːəlɪst/
n.
唯物主义者；物质主义者；肉体至上者
A person who believes that only physical matter exists, or who emphasizes the importance of the physical body.
The corporealist dismissed the notion of an afterlife. 唯物主义者驳斥了来世的概念。

He's such a corporealist, always focused on physical pleasures. 他真是个物质主义者，总是专注于肉体上的享乐。

materialist, physicalist, sensualist
idealist, spiritualist
noun
