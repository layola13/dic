benchmark: /ˈbentʃ.mɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbentʃ.mɑːk/ (UK)| n. |基准；标准；参照|A standard or point of reference against which things may be compared or assessed.|The company's profits this year exceeded the benchmark set last year.  这家公司今年的利润超过了去年设定的基准。|synonyms: standard, criterion, gauge, yardstick, touchstone|antonyms:  deviation, anomaly|noun

benchmark: /ˈbentʃ.mɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbentʃ.mɑːk/ (UK)| vt. |以…为基准；评估|Evaluate or check (something) by comparison with a standard.|The department’s performance was benchmarked against best practices in the industry.  该部门的绩效是根据行业最佳实践进行评估的。|synonyms: assess, evaluate, measure, gauge, compare|antonyms: N/A|verb
