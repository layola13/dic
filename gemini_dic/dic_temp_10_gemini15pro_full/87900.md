hierarchization:/ˌhaɪərɑːrkaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 等级化；层级化；阶级化 | The process of organizing or structuring something into a hierarchy, creating levels of power, importance, or status. | The hierarchization of society creates inequalities. 社会的等级化造成了不平等。|  The company underwent a hierarchization of its management structure. 公司对其管理结构进行了等级化。 |  stratification, ranking, ordering, grading |  equality, egalitarianism | noun
