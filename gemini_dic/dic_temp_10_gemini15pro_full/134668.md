nonmanufactured:/ˌnɑːnˌmænjuˈfæktʃərd/
adjective
非制造的；天然的
not manufactured; not produced by industrial processes.
The island's economy relies heavily on nonmanufactured goods, such as agricultural products and fish.  这个岛屿的经济严重依赖非制产品，例如农产品和鱼类。
The artist prefers using nonmanufactured materials like stones and wood in her sculptures. 这位艺术家喜欢在她的雕塑中使用石头和木头等非制材料。
synonyms: natural, raw, unprocessed
antonyms: manufactured, processed, synthetic
词性: 形容词
