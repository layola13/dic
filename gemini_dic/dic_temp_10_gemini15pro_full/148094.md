overscrupulous: /ˌoʊvərˈskruːpjələs/ | adj. | 过分谨慎的，过于拘泥的 | Excessively careful and conscientious to a fault; excessively concerned with minor details and rules. |  She was so overscrupulous in her work that she often missed deadlines. 她工作过于谨慎，经常错过截止日期。 | He is overscrupulous about cleanliness. 他对清洁过于一丝不苟。 | 近义词: meticulous, punctilious, conscientious, fastidious | 反义词: careless, negligent, remiss, unscrupulous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From over- + scrupulous.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" and "scrupulous" comes from the Latin "scrupulus" meaning "a small sharp stone," hence "a cause of uneasiness or anxiety."
