overpresumptuous: /ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmptʃuəs/
adjective
过于自信的；专横的；放肆的
Excessively forward or bold; taking liberties.  Characterized by presumption; arrogant.
He made an overpresumptuous remark about the boss's wife. 他对老板妻子的评论过于放肆。
It would be overpresumptuous of me to tell you how to run your business. 我要是告诉你该如何经营你的生意就太放肆了。
Synonyms: arrogant, presumptuous, audacious, impertinent, insolent, forward
Antonyms: humble, modest, respectful, reserved, diffident
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + presumptuous.  "Presumptuous" comes from Late Latin praesumptuosus, from Latin praesumptus, past participle of praesumere "to anticipate, take for granted," from prae- "before" + sumere "to take."
