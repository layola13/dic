furtherest:/ˈfɜːrðɪst/| adj.|最远的；进一步的| furthest is the superlative form of far, indicating the greatest distance or extent. It is often used interchangeably with farthest, though some style guides prefer farthest for physical distance and furthest for figurative distance. |He lives in the furtherest house down the road. 他住在这条路最远的那栋房子里。

The furtherest point we reached was the summit of the mountain. 我们到达的最远点是山顶。|近义词：farthest, remotest, utmost|反义词：nearest, closest|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *ferrest, furtherest, from Old English fyrrest (“furthest, most distant”), superlative of feor (“far”). Cognate with West Frisian fierst (“furthest”), Dutch verst (“furthest”), German fürst (“first, foremost, chief, prince”). Double superlative of far.
