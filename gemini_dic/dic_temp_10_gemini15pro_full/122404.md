mnemonic's:/nɪˈmɒnɪks/ | n. | 助记符；记忆法；记忆技巧 |  A system such as a pattern of letters, ideas, or associations which assists in remembering something. |  He uses mnemonics to remember people's names. 他用记忆法来记住人名. | synonyms: memory aid, reminder, cue | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "obstacle to memory") | noun

mnemonic:/nɪˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. | 记忆的；助记的；用于记忆的 | Aiding or designed to aid the memory. |  Mnemonics can be very helpful when trying to learn a new language. 当试图学习一门新语言时，记忆法可能非常有用。 | synonyms: memory-aiding,  remembrance | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | adjective
