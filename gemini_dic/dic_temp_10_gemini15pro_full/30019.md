cayenne's:/keɪˈɛnz/| n.|辣椒粉的；辣椒的|Cayenne's is a possessive form, meaning "belonging to or associated with cayenne." Cayenne itself refers to a type of hot chili pepper, or the powder made from it.|The cayenne's heat was intense. 这辣椒粉很辣。|近义词:chili's, pepper's|反义词:(No direct antonym for a possessive form.  Antonyms for "cayenne" itself would relate to mildness, e.g., "bell pepper's")|词性:Possessive noun (functions as an adjective)
