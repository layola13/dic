indiscussed:/ˌɪndɪˈskʌst/
adj.
未讨论的，未商议的
not discussed or debated.
The indiscussed topic caused confusion later.  未讨论的话题后来引起了混乱。
The financial implications remained indiscussed.  财务影响仍然没有讨论。
近义词:unaddressed, unexamined, unexplored
反义词:discussed, debated, addressed
词性:形容词

词源: "in-" (not) + "discussed" (past participle of discuss).  "in-" 字首表示否定，"discuss" 源于拉丁语 "discutere"，意为 "shake apart,"  引申为仔细检查或辩论。
