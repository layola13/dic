insatiate:/ɪnˈseɪʃiət/ /ɪnˈseɪʃɪət/| adj.|贪得无厌的，不知足的|Never satisfied; always wanting more.  Unable to be satisfied.|Her insatiable appetite for knowledge led her to pursue multiple degrees. 她对知识永不满足的渴望使她攻读了多个学位。|The dictator's insatiable thirst for power led to countless atrocities.  这位独裁者对权力贪得无厌的渴望导致了无数暴行。|近义词:greedy, ravenous, voracious, unquenchable|反义词:satisfied, content, satiated|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Latin insatiatus, from in- ‘not’ + satiatus (past participle of satiare ‘satisfy’).
