carolingian:/ˌkærəˈlɪndʒən/ | adj. n.| 加洛林王朝的；加洛林王朝的人 | Relating to the Frankish dynasty founded by Charlemagne. | The Carolingian Renaissance was a period of intellectual and cultural revival. 加洛林文艺复兴时期是一个知识和文化复兴的时期。|  The Carolingian minuscule is a script developed during this era. 加洛林小写体是在这个时代发展起来的一种文字。 | 近义词: Frankish | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Carolus (Latin for Charles, specifically Charlemagne) + -ing + -ian.
