infuscate: /ɪnˈfʌskeɪt/ /ɪnˈfʌsˌkeɪt/ | vt. | 使模糊；使昏暗；使困惑 | To make obscure or unclear; to darken or muddy.  |  The author infuscated the issue with jargon.  作者用术语把问题搞得模糊不清。 | 近义词: obscure, confuse, bewilder, obfuscate | 反义词: clarify, elucidate, illuminate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *infuscatus, past participle of infuscare "to make dark," from in- "in" + fuscare "to make dark," from fuscus "dark."
