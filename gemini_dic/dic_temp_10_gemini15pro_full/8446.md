antialiasing:/ˌæntiˈeɪliəsɪŋ/ | n. | 抗锯齿，反锯齿 |  The technique of smoothing jagged edges in digital images by averaging the colors of pixels at a boundary. |  The game looks much better with antialiasing enabled.  游戏启用抗锯齿后看起来好多了。 | synonyms: smoothing, de-jagging | antonyms: aliasing | noun
