anti-british:/ˌæntiˈbrɪtɪʃ/
adj. | 反英的；仇视英国的 | Opposed to or hostile towards Britain, its people, or its culture. |  His anti-British sentiment stemmed from his experience during the colonial era.  他反英的情绪源于他在殖民时代的经历。 | anti-England, anti-UK | pro-British, Anglophile | adjective

anti-british:/ˌæntiˈbrɪtɪʃ/
n. | 反英分子 | A person who is opposed to or hostile towards Britain. | The anti-British protested outside the embassy. 反英分子在使馆外抗议。 |  opponent of Britain | supporter of Britain | noun
