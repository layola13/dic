nonlocalizable:/ˌnɒnləʊkəˈlaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 不可定位的，无法局部的 | Not able to be localized or limited to a particular area or part. | The pain was nonlocalizable, spreading throughout his entire body. 这种疼痛无法定位，遍布他全身。 |  The doctor said the infection was nonlocalizable and could spread quickly. 医生说感染无法定位，可能迅速扩散。 | 近义词: diffuse, widespread, general, global | 反义词: localized, focal, specific, confined | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + localizable.  "Localizable" comes from "localize," which ultimately derives from the Latin "locus" meaning "place."  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning.
