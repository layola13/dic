misassessment:/ˌmɪsəˈsɛsmənt/
n.
错误的评估；误判
an incorrect assessment of something or someone.
The misassessment of the market led to significant financial losses. 对市场的错误评估导致了重大的经济损失。
misjudgment, miscalculation, misestimation, wrong assessment
accurate assessment, correct evaluation, proper appraisal
noun
