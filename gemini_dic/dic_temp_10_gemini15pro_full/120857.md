misbehaves:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvz/
verb
行为不端；举止不当
To act in a way that is not acceptable or that breaks rules.
The child misbehaves in class and disrupts other students.  这个孩子在课堂上行为不端，扰乱其他学生。
My dog misbehaves whenever we have visitors.  每当我们有客人时，我的狗就举止不当。
synonyms: act up, misconduct oneself, be naughty
antonyms: behave, obey, comply
词性: 动词
