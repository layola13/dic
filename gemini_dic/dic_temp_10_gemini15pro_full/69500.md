felineness:/ˈfiːlaɪnˌnɛs/ /ˈfiːlaɪnˌnɪs/
n.
猫的特性；猫科动物性
The quality of being feline; resembling or suggestive of a cat.
Her movements had a certain felineness, a graceful, predatory quality. 她的动作带有一种猫科动物的特性，优雅而具有掠夺性。
Synonyms:  catlikeness, feline quality, stealth, grace, agility, sleekness
Antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness
词性: noun
