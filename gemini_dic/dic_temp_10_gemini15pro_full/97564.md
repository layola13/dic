ingrieve:/ɪnˈɡriːv/ |v. |使悲伤，使苦恼 |To cause to feel grief or sorrow. |例句：The news of her friend's death ingrieved her deeply. 她朋友去世的消息使她深感悲痛。

例句：It ingrieved him to see his daughter so unhappy. 看到女儿如此不快乐，他感到很难过。|近义词：sadden, distress, upset, grieve |反义词：gladden, delight, please |词性: 动词

Etymology: From in- + grieve.  "Grieve" comes from Old French "grever" meaning "to burden, oppress, injure," ultimately from Latin "gravare" meaning "to burden."
