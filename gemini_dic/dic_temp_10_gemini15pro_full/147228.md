overmeddled:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdəld/
verb
过度干预，管得太多
having interfered too much
He overmeddled in his children's lives.  他过度干预孩子的生活。
The government was accused of overmeddling in the economy. 政府被指责过度干预经济。
近义词: interfere, meddle, intrude
反义词: neglect, ignore
词性: verb (past participle)
