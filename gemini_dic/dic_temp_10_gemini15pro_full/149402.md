packaged:/ˈpækɪdʒd/| adj.|包装好的，打包的|Contained or supplied in a package or packages; prepackaged. |例句：The packaged food is convenient for busy people. 包装食品方便忙碌的人。|例句：We bought pre-packaged meals for the camping trip. 我们为露营旅行买了预先包装好的餐点。|近义词：wrapped, boxed, contained|反义词:unpacked, loose, bulk|词性:形容词

词源: From package + -ed.  Ultimately from Middle French *pacquet.
