leadening:/ˈlɛdənɪŋ/| adj.|沉重的，令人沮丧的，使迟钝的|Making something heavy, dull, or depressing.  Depriving of force or briskness; oppressive.|The leadening atmosphere of the room made it difficult to concentrate.  房间里沉闷的气氛让人难以集中注意力。|Synonyms: oppressive, stifling, heavy, dulling, numbing|Antonyms: invigorating, stimulating, exciting, enlivening|adjective

leadening:/ˈlɛdənɪŋ/| n.|铅板，铅覆盖物（罕见）|A covering of lead.|The roof had leadening to prevent leaks. 屋顶覆盖了铅以防止漏水。|Synonyms: lead covering, lead sheet|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could be something like "exposed surface")|noun
