antisocially:/ˌæntiˈsəʊʃəli/
adverb
反社会地；危害社会地
In a manner that is harmful or disruptive to society.
He behaved antisocially by vandalizing the park.  他通过破坏公园的行为表现得很反社会。
He was punished for driving antisocially. 他因反社会驾驶而受到惩罚。
近义词: disruptively, harmfully, destructively, malevolently
反义词: sociably, prosocially, cooperatively
词性: adverb (副词)

词源:
From anti- + socially.  由anti-（反）+ socially（社会地）构成。
