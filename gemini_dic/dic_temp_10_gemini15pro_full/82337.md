grovers:/ˈɡroʊvərz/
n.
树林居民;在小树林里工作或生活的人;
People who live or work in a grove (a small wooded area).
The grovers harvested the berries in the late summer.  树林居民在夏末收获浆果。
The grovers tended the young saplings carefully.  树林居民细心照料着幼树。
近义词:woodsmen, foresters
反义词:city dwellers
