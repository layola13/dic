gallivanter:/ˌɡælɪˈvæntər/ | n. | 浪荡者；游荡的人 | A person who travels or goes out frequently for pleasure, especially in an idle or irresponsible way. |  He's a gallivanter, always off to some exotic locale. 他是个浪荡子，总是去一些异国情调的地方。

She's known as a gallivanter, rarely staying in one place for long.  她以游荡闻名，很少在一个地方待很长时间。 | 近义词: gadabout, wanderer, rover, globe-trotter | 反义词: homebody, stay-at-home | 词性: 名词

gallivanting:/ˌɡælɪˈvæntɪŋ/ | n. |  游荡；闲逛 | The act of gallivanting; going around from place to place in search of amusement. | They were gallivanting around Europe all summer. 他们整个夏天都在欧洲游荡。

 I spent my youth gallivanting around the world. 我年轻时周游世界。 | 近义词: roaming, wandering, rambling, traversing | 反义词: staying put, settling down | 词性: 名词，动名词
