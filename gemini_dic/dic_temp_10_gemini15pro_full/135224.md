nonpacific:/nɒnpəˈsɪfɪk/
adjective
非太平洋的; 非和平的
Not relating to or characteristic of the Pacific Ocean; not peaceful.
例句：The nonpacific nature of the negotiations led to a stalemate.  谈判的非和平性质导致了僵局。

例句：Nonpacific winds battered the Atlantic coast. 非太平洋的风袭击了大西洋海岸。
近义词：atlantic, unpeaceful, turbulent, agitated
反义词：pacific, peaceful, calm, tranquil
词性: adjective
