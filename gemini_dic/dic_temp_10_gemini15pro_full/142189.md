ornamenting: /ˈɔːrnəˌmentɪŋ/ | v. | 装饰，修饰 | The act of adding decoration or embellishment to something. | She was ornamenting the Christmas tree with colorful baubles. 她正在用彩色的饰品装饰圣诞树。 |  He was ornamenting his speech with literary allusions. 他用文学典故修饰他的演讲。 | decorating, embellishing, adorning | stripping, simplifying | Verb

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: ornamenten, from Old French ornementer, from Latin ornamentum ‘equipment, adornment’.
