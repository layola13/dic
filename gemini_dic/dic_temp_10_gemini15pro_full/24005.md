breathlessnesses:/ˈbreθləsnɪsɪz/
n.
气喘吁吁，呼吸困难（复数）
The state of being breathless; shortness of breath.  This word is rarely used in the plural form.
The marathon runner experienced several breathlessnesses during the race. 马拉松运动员在比赛中经历了几次呼吸困难。
The patient reported multiple breathlessnesses after climbing a flight of stairs. 病人报告说爬了一段楼梯后多次呼吸困难。
近义词: shortness of breath, dyspnea, panting
反义词: normal breathing, eupnea
词性: noun


breath:/breθ/
n.
呼吸,气息
The air inhaled or exhaled.
He took a deep breath before diving into the pool. 他深吸一口气，然后跳入泳池。
She could smell the fresh breath of the ocean. 她能闻到海洋的新鲜气息。
近义词: respiration, exhalation, inhalation
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider suffocation or asphyxiation)
词性: noun


breathless:/ˈbreθləs/
adj.
气喘吁吁的，上气不接下气的；屏息的
Out of breath; gasping for air.
After running up the stairs, she was breathless. 跑上楼梯后，她气喘吁吁。
They waited breathlessly for the announcement of the winner. 他们屏息以待宣布获胜者。
近义词: winded, gasping, panting
反义词: calm, relaxed
词性: adjective


breathlessly:/ˈbreθləsli/
adv.
气喘吁吁地；屏息地
In a breathless manner.
She whispered breathlessly, "I love you." 她气喘吁吁地低声说：“我爱你。”
The audience watched breathlessly as the acrobat performed. 观众屏息凝神地看着杂技演员表演。
近义词: gaspingly, pantingly
反义词: calmly, relaxedly
词性: adverb


breathlessness:/ˈbreθləsnəs/
n.
气喘，呼吸困难
The state of being breathless.
He suffered from breathlessness after minimal exertion. 他稍一用力就呼吸困难。
The doctor diagnosed her breathlessness as a symptom of asthma. 医生诊断她的呼吸困难是哮喘的症状。
近义词: shortness of breath, dyspnea, panting
反义词: normal breathing, eupnea
词性: noun


词源:
From Old English *bræþles, from bræþ (“breath”) + -leas (“-less”).
