bell-ringer:/ˈbelˌrɪŋər/ | n. | 敲钟人；引起轰动的人（或事物）；精彩的开场；（尤指拳击比赛的）第一回合 | a person who rings a bell, especially one that signals the start of work or school; something that creates a sensation or excitement; a strong opening, especially in a boxing match | The bell-ringer called the students to class. 敲钟人叫学生们去上课。| Synonyms: announcer, herald, initiator, instigator, chime-master | Antonyms: follower, latecomer | noun

bell-ringer:/ˈbelˌrɪŋər/ | n. | 好的开始；精彩的开场 | something that starts well or makes a strong opening impression, especially in sports | His goal in the first minute was a real bell-ringer. 他在第一分钟的进球真是一个精彩的开场。 | Synonyms: attention-grabber, showstopper, eye-opener  | Antonyms: anticlimax, disappointment | noun
