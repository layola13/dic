hated-on:/ˈheɪtɪd ɒn/ | adj. | 被憎恨的，被讨厌的 |  Subjected to hate or intense dislike, often expressed through criticism or negativity, especially online or within a specific community.  It's formed by combining the verb "hate" with the preposition "on," creating a phrasal adjective. | Example 1: The celebrity was constantly hated-on by the tabloids.  这位名人一直被小报憎恨。

Example 2: After expressing a controversial opinion online, she found herself hated-on by many.  在网上表达了有争议的观点后，她发现自己被很多人讨厌。 | 近义词：vilified, criticized, attacked, denigrated | 反义词: loved, adored, praised, celebrated | 词性:形容词
