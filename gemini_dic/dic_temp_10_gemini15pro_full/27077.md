caliber:/ˈkælɪbər/ (US), /ˈkælɪbə/ (UK)| n.|口径，才干，水准|The diameter of a bullet or shell, or the diameter of the bore of a gun. Also refers to the quality of someone's character or level of ability.|The rifle was of large caliber. (这支步枪口径很大。) He's a writer of considerable caliber. (他是一位很有才华的作家。)|synonyms: gauge, diameter, quality, ability, competence|antonyms: incompetence, inability, inadequacy|noun
