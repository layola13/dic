constrainedly:/kənˈstreɪnɪdli/
adverb
拘谨地，勉强地
In a manner that is restricted or limited, often unnaturally or unwillingly.
She smiled constrainedly, clearly uncomfortable with the attention. 她拘谨地笑了笑，显然对这种关注感到不舒服。
He spoke constrainedly about his feelings, revealing very little. 他拘谨地谈论着自己的感受，几乎没有透露什么。
近义词:stiffly, awkwardly, formally, reservedly, inhibitedly
反义词:freely, openly, naturally, uninhibitedly
词性:adverb

词源:
From constrain (v.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  The verb constrain comes from Old French constraindre, from Latin constringere "to bind together," from com- "together" + stringere "to draw tight."
