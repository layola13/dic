on-boards:/ˈɒn.bɔːdz/ /ˈɑːn.bɔːrdz/ | v. (on-board, on-boarded, on-boarding) | 使…熟悉情况；让…加入| To familiarize someone with the procedures of a new job or situation; to integrate someone into an organization or team. | The company on-boards new employees with a week-long training program. 公司通过为期一周的培训项目让新员工熟悉情况。| synonym: integrate, assimilate, induct, orient | antonym: dismiss, discharge, offboard | verb

on-boards:/ˈɒn.bɔːdz/ /ˈɑːn.bɔːrdz/ | n. |  (通常指飞机、火车、轮船的)乘客; (尤指飞机、火车、轮船的)所有乘客; | The on-boards were asked to remain seated during the turbulence. 乘客被要求在飞机颠簸时保持 seated。 | synonym: passengers, travelers | antonym: crew | noun
