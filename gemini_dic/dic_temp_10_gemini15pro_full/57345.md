dowress:/ˈdaʊərɪs/| n.|寡妇; 遗孀（拥有亡夫部分财产者）|A widow who holds property from her deceased husband.|例句：The dowress lived in the manor house until her death.  这位遗孀在庄园里住到去世。

例句：The young dowress found herself in control of a vast estate.  年轻的遗孀发现自己掌管着巨大的财产。|近义词：widow, relict|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly husband if referring to the marital status)|词性:noun
Etymology-> From Old French *doueresce*, from *douaire* ("dower"), from Latin *dōtārium* ("dower"). Ultimately from Latin *dōs* ("dowry").
