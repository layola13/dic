bogsucker:/ˈbɑːɡˌsʌkər/ | n. | 沼泽居民;乡巴佬;没见识的人 | A person who lives in a bog or swampy area. Often used derogatorily to imply someone is uneducated, unsophisticated, or backward. |  He called them a bunch of bogsuckers, showing his disdain for rural people. 他称他们是一群乡巴佬，表现出他对农村人的蔑视。|  swamp rat, hillbilly, yokel | cosmopolitan, sophisticate | noun
