loach:/loʊtʃ/| n. |泥鳅，胡子鱼|A small freshwater fish of the family Cobitidae, with a long, slender body and barbels around the mouth.  It is often used as bait or food.|例句：The loach burrowed into the mud at the bottom of the pond. 泥鳅钻进了池塘底部的泥里。

例句：He caught a loach while fishing in the creek. 他在小溪里钓鱼时钓到了一条泥鳅。|近义词：groundling, mudfish|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps large fish like trout or salmon as a contrasting example)|词性:noun
