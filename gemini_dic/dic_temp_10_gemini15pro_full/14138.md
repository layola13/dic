authoritarianism:/əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːrɪənɪzəm/
n.|专制主义，独裁主义|A form of government characterized by strong central power and limited political freedoms.|Individual rights are often suppressed under authoritarianism. 在专制主义统治下，个人权利常常受到压制。|The country has slid into authoritarianism. 这个国家已经滑向了专制主义。|近义词：despotism, dictatorship, tyranny|反义词：democracy, liberalism|词性:名词

authoritarian:/əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːrɪən/
adj.|专制的，独裁的|Favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority, especially that of the government, at the expense of personal freedom.|The government implemented authoritarian policies. 政府实施了专制政策。|His parenting style is quite authoritarian. 他的育儿方式非常专制。|近义词：despotic, dictatorial, tyrannical|反义词：democratic, liberal|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *autoritarisme*, from *autoritaire* (authoritarian) + *-isme* (-ism).  *Autoritaire* is from Latin *auctoritas* (authority).
