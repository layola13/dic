compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənət/ | adj. | 同情的，有同情心的 | Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others. | She was a compassionate nurse, always going the extra mile for her patients. 她是一位富有同情心的护士，总是为病人付出额外的努力。 |  He felt compassionate towards the homeless man. 他对那个无家可归的人感到同情。 | 近义词: sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring | 反义词: uncaring, callous, indifferent, apathetic | 词性: adjective | 词源: Late Middle English: from Old French *compassionat, from compassion  'sympathy'.
