例句：Nontactile communication, such as body language and tone of voice, can be just as important as verbal communication. 非触觉交流，例如肢体语言和语气，与口头交流同样重要。|近义词：intangible, incorporeal, impalpable|反义词: tactile, tangible, palpable |词性:形容词
