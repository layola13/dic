fray:/freɪ/
noun|磨损，争吵，冲突|the process of wearing something down by rubbing it; a fight or argument|The rope began to fray at the edges.  绳子的边缘开始磨损了。|近义词: unravel, wear, fight, quarrel|反义词: mend, repair, reconcile|n.

fray:/freɪ/
verb|磨损，使烦躁，使紧张|to become worn down by rubbing; to make someone annoyed or nervous|The constant arguments were fraying her nerves.  不断的争吵使她神经紧张。|近义词: unravel, wear, irritate, aggravate|反义词: mend, repair, soothe, calm|v.
