overartificially: /ˌoʊvərˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəli/ | adv. | 过于人为地，矫揉造作地 |  Formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning excessively) to "artificial" (meaning made by humans, not natural), and then the suffix "-ly" to create an adverb. It describes something done in a way that is too obviously contrived or unnatural. | Her acting was overartificially dramatic, making the performance seem insincere.  她表演得过于做作，显得不真诚。| The dialogue was overartificially witty, sounding forced and unnatural.  对话过于矫揉造作，听起来很做作，不自然。| 近义词: affectedly, pretentiously, artificially | 反义词: naturally, genuinely, authentically | 词性: 副词
