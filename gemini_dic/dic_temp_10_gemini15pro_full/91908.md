hyperlogicalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈlɒdʒɪkəlnəs/
n.
极端逻辑性，过度理性
The state of being excessively logical or rational, often to the point of neglecting emotional or practical considerations.
His hyperlogicalness made it difficult for him to connect with others on an emotional level. 他极端的逻辑性使他难以与他人建立情感联系。
over-rationality, excessive logicality
sentimentality, irrationality
noun
