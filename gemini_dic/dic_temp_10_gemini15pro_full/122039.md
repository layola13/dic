missworn:/ˌmɪsˈswɔːrn/
adjective
发誓不实的；作伪证的
falsely sworn; having sworn falsely
The judge dismissed the case due to the missworn testimony of a key witness.  法官因一位关键证人的伪证而驳回了此案。
近义词:perjured, false
反义词:truthful, accurate
词性:adjective

词源:
From "mis-" (wrongly) + "sworn" (past participle of "swear").  由"mis-"（错误地）+ "sworn"（"swear"的过去分词）组成。
