breadcrumb:/ˈbredkrʌm/ (US), /ˈbredkrʌm/ (UK)| n. |面包屑，线索，导航路径|A small piece of bread, or a trail of breadcrumbs, especially one left to mark a path; a clue or piece of information that helps to lead one to a desired outcome or conclusion, especially on a website or in a software application.| Hansel and Gretel left a trail of breadcrumbs so they could find their way home.  汉赛尔和格莱特留下了一条面包屑的踪迹，以便他们能找到回家的路。| synonyms: clue, hint, trace, sign, indicator, guide | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider)  dead end, obstacle, misdirection | noun
