medicative:/ˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
药用的；治疗的；有疗效的
Having the property of healing or relieving pain.
Example: The herb has medicative properties and is used in traditional medicine. 这种草药具有药用价值，用于传统医学。

Example:  The warm bath had a medicative effect on her sore muscles.  热水澡对她酸痛的肌肉有疗效。
synonym: medicinal, therapeutic, curative, healing
antonym: harmful, injurious, toxic
词性:adjective
