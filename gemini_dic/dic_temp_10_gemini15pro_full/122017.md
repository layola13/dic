misstep:/ˌmɪsˈstɛp/| n. |失误，错误，过失|A wrong or awkward step; a blunder or mistake.|She made a misstep in her career and lost her job. 她在职业生涯中犯了一个错误，失去了工作。|例句：His latest misstep cost him the election. 他最近的失误让他失去了选举。

例句：Taking that job was a major misstep. 接那份工作是一个重大失误。

近义词：blunder, error, mistake, gaffe, slip-up|反义词:success, triumph|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + step (a single movement of the foot in walking).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss."  "Step" comes from Old English "steppan" meaning "to step, walk, go."
