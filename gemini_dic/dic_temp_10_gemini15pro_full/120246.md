ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *minglen*, from Old English *gemengan* (“to mingle, mix, combine”), from Proto-Germanic *mgangijaną* (“to go among, mix”), from *mang* (“among”). Cognate with German *mengen* (“to mix”), West Frisian *mingje* (“to mix”), Dutch *mengen* (“to mix”), Swedish *mänga* (“to mix”).
