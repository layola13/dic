drizzlingly:/ˈdrɪzəlɪŋli/
adv.
毛毛细雨地，下着毛毛雨地
in a drizzling manner; lightly and steadily.
The rain was drizzlingly falling, creating a misty atmosphere. 雨丝丝缕缕地飘落，营造出朦胧的氛围。
近义词: lightly, gently, softly
反义词: heavily, torrentially
词性: 副词

drizzle:/ˈdrɪzl/
n.
毛毛雨
light rain falling in very fine drops.
The drizzle made the streets slick. 毛毛雨使街道湿滑。
近义词: mist, sprinkle
反义词: downpour, deluge
词性: 名词

drizzle:/ˈdrɪzl/
v.
下毛毛雨
to rain lightly in fine drops.
It began to drizzle as we left the house. 我们离开房子时开始下毛毛雨。
近义词: sprinkle, spit
反义词: pour, downpour
词性: 动词


词源:  From the noun "drizzle," which is of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old English *drisol "drizzle," or Low German *drieseln, frequentative of *driesen "to fall as dew."  The "-ingly" suffix transforms the adjective "drizzling" into an adverb.
