codeine:/ˈkoʊdiːn/ (US), /ˈkəʊdiːn/ (UK)| n.|可待因|An opioid pain medication and cough suppressant. It is derived from opium and is similar to morphine, but less potent.|She was prescribed codeine for her persistent cough. 她因为持续咳嗽开了可待因。|Synonyms: methylmorphine, opioid analgesic|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific drug, could consider pain stimulant or expectorant as a functional opposite)|noun
