ingenious:/ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/| adj.|巧妙的；有独创性的；心灵手巧的| (of a person) clever, original, and inventive.|(of a machine or idea) cleverly and originally devised and well suited to its purpose.|例句：He was ingenious enough to overcome the limited resources. 他足智多谋，能够克服资源有限的困难。

例句：It was an ingenious solution to a seemingly impossible problem. 这是一个巧妙地解决了一个看似不可能的问题的方案。|近义词：clever, inventive, innovative, creative, original, resourceful, bright, brilliant, inspired, talented, gifted, adept, skillful, adroit, deft, handy, expert, masterful, masterly|反义词：unimaginative, unoriginal, uninventive, clumsy, stupid, foolish|词性:形容词
