monospore:/ˈmɒnəspɔːr/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəspɔːr/ (US)| n. |单孢子|A spore produced singly or not grouped with others. It germinates to produce a single individual.|The fungus reproduces by monospores.|近义词:ascospore, basidiospore|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "multicellular spore")|词性:noun
