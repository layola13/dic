frontalized:/ˈfrʌntəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 前置的，额化的 | Moved to the front (especially of a linguistic constituent).  A sound change where the place of articulation of a sound moves further forward in the mouth. | The word order in this sentence is frontalized, placing the object before the verb.  这个句子的语序是前置的，宾语放在动词之前。

The frontalized vowel in this dialect sounds very distinct.  这种方言中额化的元音听起来很独特。 | 近义词: preposed, advanced | 反义词: postposed | 词性: adjective
