inflective:/ɪnˈflɛktɪv/| adj.|屈折的，词形变化的|Relating to or involving inflection; capable of inflection.|The English language is inflective, meaning words change form to indicate tense, number, or gender.|近义词：variable, changeable, modifying|反义词：invariant, unchangeable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *inflectivus*, from Latin inflectere "to bend, curve, change," from in- "in, into" + flectere "to bend."

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The verb "walk" inflects to "walks" in the third person singular. =  动词“walk”在第三人称单数时变成“walks”。
She sings beautifully. = 她唱歌优美。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The verb "be" is inflecting to show continuous action. =  动词“be”正在变化以表示持续的动作。
He is writing a novel. = 他正在写小说。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The verb "have" has inflected to form the present perfect tense. =  动词“have”已经变化以构成现在完成时。
They have eaten all the cake. = 他们已经吃完了所有的蛋糕。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The verb "be" and the present participle are being used to show an ongoing action that has duration. =  动词“be”和现在分词被用来表示一个持续了一段时间的正在进行的动作。
She has been studying English for five years. = 她学习英语已经五年了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The verb "walk" inflected to "walked" to indicate past tense. = 动词“walk”变成“walked”以表示过去时。
He visited his grandmother yesterday. = 他昨天去看望了他的祖母。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The verb "be" was inflecting to show continuous action in the past. = 动词“be”在过去时态中变化以表示持续的动作。
They were playing football when it started to rain. = 开始下雨时，他们正在踢足球。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The verb "have" had inflected to form the past perfect tense. = 动词“have”已经变化以构成过去完成时。
She had finished her homework before dinner. = 她在晚饭前完成了作业。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The past perfect continuous shows an action that was ongoing before another action in the past. = 过去完成进行时表示在过去另一个动作之前正在进行的动作。
He had been working on the project for months before it was cancelled. = 在项目被取消之前，他已经为这个项目工作了好几个月。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The modal verb "will" is used to indicate future tense. = 情态动词“will”用于表示将来时。
We will travel to Europe next summer. = 明年夏天我们将去欧洲旅行。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
The future continuous tense indicates an action that will be in progress at a specific time in the future. = 将来进行时表示在将来某个特定时间正在进行的动作。
This time next week, I will be lying on a beach. = 下周这个时候，我将躺在沙滩上。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
The future perfect tense indicates an action that will be completed before a specific time in the future. = 将来完成时表示在将来某个特定时间之前完成的动作。
By next year, I will have graduated from university. = 到明年，我将大学毕业。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
The future perfect continuous tense indicates an ongoing action that will have been happening for a period of time before a specific point in the future. = 将来完成进行时表示在将来某个特定时间点之前已经持续了一段时间的动作。
By the time you arrive, I will have been cooking for hours. = 当你到达时，我已经做了几个小时的饭了。
