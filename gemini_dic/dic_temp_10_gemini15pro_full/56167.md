dodecagynous: /ˌdoʊdɛkəˈɡaɪnəs/ /ˌdɒdɪkəˈɡaɪnəs/
adjective
十二雌蕊的
Having twelve pistils (the female reproductive part of a flower).
The botanist identified a rare dodecagynous flower in the rainforest.  这位植物学家在雨林中发现了一种罕见的十二雌蕊的花。
The newly discovered species was characterized as dodecagynous due to its twelve distinct pistils.  这个新发现的物种由于其十二个不同的雌蕊而被鉴定为十二雌蕊的。
Near Synonyms:  polygynous (多雌蕊的)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like monogynous - 单雌蕊的)
词性: adjective
