outspying:/ˌaʊtˈspaɪɪŋ/
verb
从事间谍活动超越，胜过…的间谍活动
The act of spying more effectively or extensively than another entity.
例句：Their outspying of the enemy provided crucial intelligence. 他们胜过敌人的间谍活动提供了关键情报。

例句：The agency was accused of outspying its allies.  该机构被指控对盟友进行间谍活动。
近义词：surpassing in espionage, outmaneuvering in intelligence gathering
反义词：being outspied
词性：动词 (gerund or present participle)
