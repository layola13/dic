gladliest:/ˈɡlædlɪɪst/ | adv. | 最高兴地，最乐意地 | Most gladly; with the greatest pleasure or willingness. | She would gladliest have stayed home. 她最想待在家里。| 近义词: most willingly, most happily | 反义词: reluctantly, unwillingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Old English *glædlicost, superlative of glædlice ("gladly"), derived from glæd ("glad").
