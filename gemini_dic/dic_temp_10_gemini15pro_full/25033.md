bryologist:/braɪˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |苔藓学家|A scientist who studies bryophytes (mosses, liverworts, and hornworts).|Example 1: The bryologist spent years cataloging the different species of moss found in the cloud forest.  这位苔藓学家花了数年时间对云雾林中发现的不同种类的苔藓进行编目。

Example 2:  A renowned bryologist discovered a new species of liverwort during his expedition. 一位著名的苔藓学家在他的探险过程中发现了一种新的地钱。|Synonym: moss expert, bryophyte specialist|Antonym: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bryo- (Greek: moss) + -logist (one who studies).
