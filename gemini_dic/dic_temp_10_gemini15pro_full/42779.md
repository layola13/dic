coup:/kuː/ /kuːp/
noun
政变；突然成功的行动；出乎意料的举动
a sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government
例句：
He was overthrown in a military coup.
他被军事政变推翻了。

The coup attempt failed.
政变企图失败了。

近义词：overthrow, seizure, takeover
反义词：surrender, submission
