implicate:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 牵连，暗示，意味着 | to show that someone is involved in a crime or partly responsible for something bad that has happened | The evidence implicates the mayor in the corruption scandal. 证据表明市长与腐败丑闻有关。| involve, incriminate, entangle | exonerate, absolve | verb

implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 含义，暗示，牵连 | something that is suggested, or happens, indirectly | The implication of his statement was that he was resigning. 他声明的含义是他要辞职。| connotation, suggestion, inference | explicit statement, direct statement | noun
