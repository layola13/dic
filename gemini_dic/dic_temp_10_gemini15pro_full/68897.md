fastidiousnesses:/fæˈstɪdiəsnɪsɪz/| n.|一丝不苟；挑剔；苛求|The state or quality of being fastidious; excessive attention to detail and correctness, often to the point of being overly critical or difficult to please.|Her fastidiousnesses about cleanliness were well-known. 她对清洁的挑剔是众所周知的。|Synonyms: meticulousness, punctiliousness, scrupulousness, exactingness|Antonyms: carelessness, negligence, sloppiness|Noun
