clingers:/ˈklɪŋərz/
n.| 依赖者; 紧抓不放的人（或物）; 难以摆脱的人（或物）|People who are emotionally dependent or have difficulty letting go of relationships or situations are often referred to as clingers.  Clingers can also refer to things that stick tightly to a surface.|She became a real clinger after the breakup, constantly calling and texting him. 分手后，她变得非常依赖他，不断地打电话和发短信给他。|synonyms: adherent, devotee, follower, leech, parasite|antonyms: independent,  autonomous|noun

clingers:/ˈklɪŋərz/
n.|（衣服上的）粘附物|Burrs are examples of clingers that stick to clothing. 刺果是粘在衣服上的粘附物的一个例子。|synonyms: burrs, stickers|antonyms: N/A|noun
