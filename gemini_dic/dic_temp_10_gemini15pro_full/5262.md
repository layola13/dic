allograph:/ˈæləˌɡræf/ | n. |异体字，同形异义字 |A different way of writing the same letter or other grapheme.  A handwritten or printed representation of a grapheme. | Her handwriting contains several distinct allographs of the letter 'g'. 她的笔迹中包含了字母“g”的几种不同的异体字。| synonym: variant, form | antonym: original, prototype | noun
