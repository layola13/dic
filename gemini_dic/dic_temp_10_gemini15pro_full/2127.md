adapter: /əˈdæptər/ | n. | 适配器; 转接器 | A device used to connect two incompatible pieces of equipment.  | I needed an adapter to plug my American laptop into the European outlet. 我需要一个适配器来将我的美式笔记本电脑插入欧式插座。 |  I bought a new adapter for my phone charger. 我买了一个新的手机充电器适配器。 | synonym: connector, converter, coupler | antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "adapt," which comes from the Latin "adaptare," meaning "to fit."

USAGE->
Countable Noun:
This adapter allows you to use your headphones with your new phone. = 这个适配器可以让你在新手机上使用耳机。
I lost my travel adapter, so I couldn't charge my phone. = 我弄丢了旅行适配器，所以无法给手机充电。


PHRASE->
power adapter = 电源适配器

network adapter = 网络适配器
