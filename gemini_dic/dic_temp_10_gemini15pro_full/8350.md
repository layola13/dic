anti-polish:/ˌæntiˈpɒlɪʃ/ | adj. | 反抛光的，防抛光的，去光泽的 | Opposing or preventing polish; designed to remove polish or prevent its application. |  The car wax has anti-polish properties, leaving a matte finish. 这款车蜡具有反抛光特性，留下哑光效果。

The anti-polish coating prevents fingerprints from showing.  这种防抛光涂层可以防止指纹显现。 | 近义词: matte, non-glossy, dulling | 反义词: polishing, glossy, shiny | 词性: adjective
