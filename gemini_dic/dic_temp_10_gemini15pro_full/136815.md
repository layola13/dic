nonshrinkable:/ˌnɒnˈʃrɪŋkəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈʃrɪŋkəbl/| adj.|不可缩的，防缩的|Not able to shrink; resistant to shrinking.  Made of a material that will not shrink when washed or dried.|This shirt is made of nonshrinkable cotton. 这件衬衫是用防缩棉做的。|The nonshrinkable wool is perfect for sweaters.  这种不可缩水的羊毛非常适合做毛衣。|近义词:preshrunk,  non-shrink|反义词:shrinkable|词性:形容词
