homologate:/həˈmɒləɡeɪt/ /həˈmɑːləɡeɪt/| vt.| 批准，认可，使合法化; 使(尤指赛车)符合技术标准，使合格|To officially approve or confirm something. To officially approve a car or part of a car as being of a good enough standard|The new car design was homologated for use on public roads. 这款新车设计已获准在公共道路上使用。|synonyms: approve, certify, ratify, validate, sanction|antonyms: reject, disapprove, invalidate|verb

homologation:/həˌmɒləˈɡeɪʃən/ /həˌmɑːləˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|批准，认可，合法化|The official act of approving or confirming something.|The homologation of the new car model took several months. 新车型的审批花了几个月的时间。|synonyms: approval, certification, ratification, validation, sanction|antonyms: rejection, disapproval, invalidation|noun
