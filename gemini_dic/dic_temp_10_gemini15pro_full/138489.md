The king's novennial reign was marked by prosperity and peace. 国王的九年统治以繁荣与和平为标志。|近义词：nine-year, recurring every nine years|反义词：annual, biennial|词性: adjective

词源: From Latin "novennis" (nine years old), from "novem" (nine) + "annus" (year).
