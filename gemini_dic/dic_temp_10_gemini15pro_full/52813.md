dilucidate:/dɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/
verb
阐明；说明
to make something clear and easy to understand
The report elucidated the reasons for the company's failure. 该报告阐明了公司失败的原因。

例句：
The professor elucidated the complex theory with clear examples. 教授用清晰的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。

The spokesperson elucidated the company's position on the recent controversy. 发言人阐明了公司对最近争议的立场。


近义词：clarify, explain, illuminate, expound
反义词：obfuscate, confuse, obscure
词性：verb
