antiterrorisms:/ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪzəmz/
n.
反恐措施，反恐行动
Systematic actions to prevent or combat terrorism.
The government implemented new antiterrorisms after the attack. 政府在袭击后实施了新的反恐措施。
近义词: counterterrorism, antiterrorism measures
反义词: terrorism, terrorist acts
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
terrorism (noun): the unlawful use of violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in the pursuit of political aims.
