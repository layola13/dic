almond:/ˈɑːmənd/ (US), /ˈɑːmənd/ (UK)| n.|杏仁|A pale oval nut with a slightly bitter taste, or the tree that it grows on.  The almond is technically not a nut, but a drupe, consisting of a hull, shell, and seed.|I like to snack on almonds. 我喜欢吃杏仁。|She added sliced almonds to the cake batter. 她在蛋糕糊里加了杏仁片。|Synonyms: nut, kernel|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific nut)|noun
