flow:/floʊ/ (US) /fləʊ/ (UK)| vi. vt.|流动，涌流，飘垂|To move or run smoothly, as a liquid. Also, to proceed smoothly and readily.|Traffic flowed smoothly through the city.  车辆在城市里畅通无阻。|近义词：glide, drift, run, circulate|反义词:stop, halt, stagnate|verb
