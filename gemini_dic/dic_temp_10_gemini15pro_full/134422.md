nonirate:/ˌnɒnˈaɪˌreɪt/
adjective
无愤怒的；平静的
Not showing or feeling anger; calm.
例句：His nonirate response to the provocation surprised everyone. 他对挑衅的平静反应让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：She maintained a nonirate demeanor throughout the heated argument. 在激烈的争论中，她始终保持着平静的态度。
近义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene, placid
反义词：irate, angry, furious, enraged, wrathful
词性:adjective

词源：The word "nonirate" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "irate" (meaning "angry").  "Irate" comes from the Latin word "iratus," the past participle of "irasci," meaning "to grow angry."
