industries: /ˈɪndəstriːz/ | n. | 产业，工业，行业 | branches of economic activity concerned with the processing of raw materials and manufacture of goods in factories |
The steel industry is facing a crisis. 钢铁行业正面临危机。

The tourism industry is booming in this region.  旅游业在这个地区蓬勃发展。


近义词: businesses, trades, sectors | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用inactivity, unemployment等) | 词性: 名词 (plural of industry)

Etymology:  From Old French *industrie "diligence, activity", from Latin industria "diligence, activity; ingenuity", from industrius "diligent, active", from indo "in" + struere "to build, pile up".
