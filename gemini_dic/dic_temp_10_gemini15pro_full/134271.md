noninformation:/ˌnɒnɪnfəˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 无信息；无意义的信息；无效信息 | Lack of information; meaningless or useless data.  It can also refer to data that actively obscures real information. | The report was filled with noninformation, offering no real insights. 这份报告充满了无信息，没有提供任何真正的见解。 | The press release was mostly noninformation, designed to deflect attention from the real issue.  新闻稿大部分都是无意义的信息，旨在转移人们对真正问题的注意力。 | 近义词: meaningless data, useless information, noise | 反义词: information, data, intelligence | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "information" (from Old French "informacion," meaning "formation of the mind, instruction, communication of news").
