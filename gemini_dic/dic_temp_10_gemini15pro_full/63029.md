enraging:/ɪnˈreɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
使人愤怒的，令人暴怒的
Causing intense anger; infuriating.
His enraging behavior made everyone in the room furious. 他令人愤怒的行为让房间里的每个人都怒火中烧。
The constant noise from the construction site was enraging. 建筑工地持续不断的噪音令人恼火。
近义词: infuriating, maddening, exasperating, provoking
反义词: calming, soothing, pleasing, delightful
词性: adjective
