deaf:/dɛf/ | adj. | 聋的 | Unable to hear, either completely or partly. |  She was born deaf. 她天生失聪。|  The explosion left him partially deaf. 爆炸让他部分失聪。 | hard of hearing | hearing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dēaf, from Proto-Germanic *daubaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbh- (“dull, insensible”). Cognates include German taub, Dutch doof, and Gothic 𐌳𐌰𐌿𐍆𐍃 (daufs).
