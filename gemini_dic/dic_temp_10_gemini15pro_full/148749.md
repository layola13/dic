overtruthful: /ˌoʊvərˈtruːθfəl/ | adj. | 过于诚实的，过分坦率的 | Excessively truthful; candid to a fault.  Characterized by excessive honesty, often to the point of being tactless or blunt. |  He was perhaps a little overtruthful when he told her she looked terrible in that dress. 他告诉她她穿那条裙子很难看，也许有点过于直白了。| Synonyms: excessively honest, candid, blunt, outspoken, frank, undiplomatic | Antonyms: dishonest, deceitful, evasive, tactful, diplomatic | adjective
