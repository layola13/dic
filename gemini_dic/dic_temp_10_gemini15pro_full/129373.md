nomadism: /ˈnəʊmædɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈnoʊmædɪzəm/ (US)| n. |游牧；流浪|The way of life of nomads, involving moving from place to place. |The documentary explored the history and culture of Bedouin nomadism in the Sahara Desert. 这部纪录片探讨了撒哈拉沙漠中贝都因人游牧的历史和文化。| 近义词: wandering, migration, roaming | 反义词: sedentism, settlement | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *nomadisme*, from *nomade* (“nomad”), from Late Latin *nomas* (plural *nomades*), from Ancient Greek νομάς (nomás, “roaming, wandering, pasturing”), from νέμω (némō, “I distribute, allot, assign, pasture, graze”).
