buzzworthy:/ˈbʌzwɜːrði/ (US) /ˈbʌzwɜːði/ (UK)
adjective
引起热议的；值得关注的
Generating a lot of discussion or excitement; worthy of attention.
The new phone release was incredibly buzzworthy, trending on social media for days.  这款新手机的发布引起了极大的关注，在社交媒体上流行了好几天。
Her latest single is a buzzworthy track that blends pop and R&B influences. 她的最新单曲是一首融合了流行和节奏布鲁斯元素的热门歌曲。
synonyms:  talked-about,  hyped, trending, popular, noteworthy
antonyms:  unremarkable,  forgettable,  insignificant,  dull,  uninteresting
Etymology:  A combination of "buzz" (meaning excited talk or activity) and "worthy" (deserving of).
