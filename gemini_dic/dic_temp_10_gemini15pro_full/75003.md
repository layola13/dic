friend: /frɛnd/ (US), /frend/ (UK)| n.| 朋友 |A person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection, typically exclusive of sexual or family relations. | She's my best friend. 她是我的最好的朋友。|  My friends and I went to the movies. 我和我的朋友们去看电影了。| 近义词: companion, pal, buddy, mate | 反义词: enemy, foe | noun
