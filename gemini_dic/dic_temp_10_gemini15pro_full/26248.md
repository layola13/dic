buttes:/bjuːts/
n.
孤峰，孤山，平顶山
An isolated hill with steep sides and a flat top, similar to but smaller than a mesa.
We drove past several impressive buttes on our road trip through Arizona. 我们在穿越亚利桑那州的公路旅行中经过了几座令人印象深刻的孤峰。

近义词: mesas, hills, bluffs
反义词: valleys, canyons


词性: noun
