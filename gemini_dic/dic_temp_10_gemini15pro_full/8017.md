anthracites: /ˈænθrəsaɪts/ | n. | 无烟煤 | A hard, compact variety of coal that has a high luster. It contains a high percentage of carbon and burns with little flame and smoke. | Anthracite coal burns longer and hotter than bituminous coal. 无烟煤比烟煤燃烧时间更长，温度更高。 | The blacksmith used anthracite in his forge. 铁匠在他的锻炉里使用无烟煤。 | synonyms: hard coal, culm | antonyms: bituminous coal, lignite | noun

anthracite: /ˈænθrəsaɪt/ | adj. | 无烟煤的 | Relating to, resembling, or consisting of anthracite. | Anthracite coal is known for its clean burning properties. 无烟煤以其清洁的燃烧特性而闻名。 | The region is rich in anthracite deposits. 该地区富含无烟煤矿藏。 | synonyms: hard coal, culm | antonyms: bituminous coal, lignite | adjective
