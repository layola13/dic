crackless:/ˈkræklɪs/| adj.|无裂纹的，完好无损的|Free from cracks or flaws; unbroken.  Formed by adding the suffix "-less" (meaning "without") to the noun "crack".|The antique vase was miraculously crackless despite its age.|近义词：unbroken, flawless, undamaged, intact, perfect|反义词:cracked, broken, flawed, damaged|词性:形容词
