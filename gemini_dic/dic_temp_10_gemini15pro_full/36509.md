coli:/ˈkoʊlaɪ/| n.|大肠杆菌|A type of bacteria that is commonly found in the intestines of humans and animals.  It can sometimes cause food poisoning.|Example: The outbreak was traced back to contaminated coli bacteria.  这次疫情可以追溯到受污染的大肠杆菌。|Example:  E. coli can be harmful if ingested in large quantities.  如果大量摄入大肠杆菌，可能会有害。|近义词:bacteria, microorganism|反义词:None|词性:noun
