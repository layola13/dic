biryani:/bɪrˈjɑːni/ /bɪrˈiɑːni/
noun
印度香饭，抓饭
A mixed rice dish with meat, vegetables, and spices. It is popular in South Asia, especially in India and Pakistan.
We ordered chicken biryani for dinner. 我们晚餐点了鸡肉香饭。
