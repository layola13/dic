inaffected:/ˌɪnəˈfɛktɪd/ | adj. | 未受影响的；自然的；不矫揉造作的 | Not influenced or changed by something; natural and sincere. |例句：He seemed inaffected by the news. 他似乎没被这消息影响。|例句：She has a very inaffected manner. 她举止非常自然。| 近义词：unmoved, untouched, unchanged, natural, genuine, artless, sincere | 反义词：affected, pretentious, artificial, insincere | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + affected.
