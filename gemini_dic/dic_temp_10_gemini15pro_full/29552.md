catchier:/ˈkætʃɪər/| adj.|更吸引人的，更易记住的|More likely to attract attention or be remembered.  It's the comparative form of "catchy."| This song is catchier than the last one. 这首歌比上一首更朗朗上口。|synonyms:  more memorable, more appealing, more infectious|antonyms: less memorable, less appealing, dull, boring|adjective


Etymology->
From "catchy" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Catchy" originates from the verb "catch," meaning to seize or attract.
