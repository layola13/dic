bodychecking:/ˈbɒdiˌtʃɛkɪŋ/ | n. | 身体冲撞，身体对抗 |  The act of using one's body to obstruct or impede an opponent, typically in ice hockey.  It involves delivering a hit or using physical force to separate the opponent from the puck. |  Bodychecking is a key part of hockey strategy. 身体对抗是曲棍球策略的关键部分。 |  Bodychecking can be dangerous if done incorrectly. 如果做得不正确，身体对抗可能会很危险。 |  hitting, checking |  avoiding, dodging | noun
