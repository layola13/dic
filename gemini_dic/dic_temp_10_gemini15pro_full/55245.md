dissimilitude:/ˌdɪsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/
n.
lack of resemblance or similarity; difference
The dissimilitude between the twins was striking, despite their identical genes. 这对双胞胎尽管基因相同，但差异却很惊人。
Synonyms: difference, dissimilarity, unlikeness, disparity, divergence, distinction
Antonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness, similitude, sameness
词性: 名词
