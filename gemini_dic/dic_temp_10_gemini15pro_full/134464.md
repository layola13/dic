nonjurant:/ˌnɒnˈdʒʊərənt/
n.|拒誓者；不愿宣誓者|A person who refuses to take an oath, especially the oath of allegiance.
|Example 1: The nonjurors were persecuted for their beliefs. 拒誓者因其信仰而遭到迫害。

Example 2:  Many nonjurors lost their positions after refusing to swear allegiance to the new monarch. 许多拒誓者在拒绝向新君主宣誓效忠后失去了职位。
|Synonym: recusant, dissenter|Antonym: conformist|noun
