immeability:/ɪˌmiːəˈbɪləti/
n.
不能移动; 不动性; 固定性
The state or quality of being immobile or not movable.
The immeability of the ancient statue made it difficult to transport.  古代雕像的固定性使其难以运输。
The patient's immeability after the stroke was a major concern. 中风后病人的不能移动是一个主要问题。
immobility, fixity, immovability
mobility, movability
noun
