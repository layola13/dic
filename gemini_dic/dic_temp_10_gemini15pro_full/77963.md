genteelish:/ˌdʒɛnˈtiːlɪʃ/| adj.|有点上流社会的；略显高雅的；假斯文的| Appearing or trying to appear genteel; affectedly refined.  Attempting to appear refined or upper-class, often in a pretentious or slightly exaggerated way.| She spoke in a genteelish accent, carefully enunciating each word. 她说话带着一种略显高雅的口音，每个字都发音清晰。|  He gave a genteelish cough before addressing the room.  他在向房间里的人讲话前，装模作样地咳了一声。| 近义词：refined, polished, cultured, pretentious, affected | 反义词：crude, vulgar, unrefined, down-to-earth | 词性: adjective
