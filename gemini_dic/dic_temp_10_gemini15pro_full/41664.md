correlatively:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 相互关联地，相关地 | in a correlatively manner; in a way that shows correlation or interconnectedness. | The rise in crime rates correlates relatively with unemployment levels. 犯罪率的上升与失业率相对相关。|  The price of gold and the value of the dollar often move correlatively. 黄金价格和美元价值通常相互关联地变动。 | 近义词: correspondingly, accordingly,  interdependently | 反义词: independently, separately | 词性:副词

词源: From "correlative" + "-ly." "Correlative" comes from Late Latin "correlativus," from "correlari," meaning "to bring into mutual relation."
