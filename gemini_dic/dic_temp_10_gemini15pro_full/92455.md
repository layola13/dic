hyporhachis:/ˌhaɪpəˈreɪkɪs/| n. |花序轴下部|The lower part of the main axis of an inflorescence, below the other branches.  The part of the rachis below a spikelet in grasses.|The hyporhachis of the plant was thick and sturdy.  这种植物的花序轴下部粗壮而结实。|Synonyms: lower rachis|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, potentially upper rachis or terminal rachis depending on context)|noun
