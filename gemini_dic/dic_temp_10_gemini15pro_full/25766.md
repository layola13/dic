burdened:/ˈbɜːrdənd/| adj.|负担沉重的，不堪重负的|Loaded, oppressed, or troubled with something difficult or unpleasant.|He was burdened with heavy debts. 他背负着沉重的债务。|近义词：laden, loaded, weighed down, oppressed|反义词：unburdened, free, light|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "burden," from Old English *byrðen "load, weight," from Proto-Germanic *burthinjo- "that which is carried," related to "bear."
