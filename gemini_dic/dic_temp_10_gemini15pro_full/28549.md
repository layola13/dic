cardiology:/ˌkɑːrdiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |心脏病学| The study of the heart and its functions in health and disease. | He's a specialist in cardiology. 他是心脏病学专家。| Cardiology is crucial for understanding and treating heart conditions. 心脏病学对于理解和治疗心脏疾病至关重要。 | 近义词:  none (as a specific field of medicine) | 反义词: none | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *kardía* (heart) + *-logia* (study of).
