freewares:/ˈfriːwɛəz/| n.|免费软件（通常指非商业用途的软件）|Software available for use at no monetary cost, often but not always for non-commercial purposes.  It is often distributed without the source code.|I downloaded some freewares to edit my photos. 我下载了一些免费软件来编辑我的照片。|近义词：free software, shareware|反义词：commercial software|词性:名词

词源:  A blend of "free" and "software."  The term emerged in the 1980s, particularly with the rise of personal computers and the distribution of software outside traditional commercial channels.
