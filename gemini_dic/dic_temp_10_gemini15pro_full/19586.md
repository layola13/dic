biggen:/ˈbɪɡən/| v. |使变大，增大|To make or become bigger.|例句：They biggened the house by adding a new wing. 他们在房子上加盖了一座新侧楼，使房子变大了。

例句：The company's profits have biggened over the past year. 公司的利润在过去一年里增加了。|近义词：enlarge, expand, increase, augment|反义词:shrink, reduce, decrease, diminish|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From big + -en (suffix forming verbs of making or becoming).  First recorded in the 14th century.
