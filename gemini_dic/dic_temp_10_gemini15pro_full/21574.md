bluethroat:/ˈbluːθroʊt/| n.|蓝喉歌鸲|A small Old World thrush (Luscinia svecica) breeding in northern Europe and Asia and having a brilliant blue throat patch bordered with black and chestnut.|I saw a bluethroat flitting through the reeds by the river. 我看到一只蓝喉歌鸲在河边的芦苇丛中飞来飞去。|近义词：bird, thrush|反义词:|noun


bluethroat:/ˈbluːθroʊt/| adj.|蓝喉的|Having a blue throat.|The male bluethroat is easily recognizable by its vibrant blue throat patch. 雄性蓝喉歌鸲很容易通过其鲜艳的蓝色喉咙斑块辨认出来。|近义词:|反义词:|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From blue + throat.  The name literally describes the bird's most distinctive feature.
