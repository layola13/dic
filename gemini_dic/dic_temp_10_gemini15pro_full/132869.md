nonejecting:/ˌnɒnɪˈdʒɛktɪŋ/
adjective
非弹出式的，不弹出的
Not ejecting something, especially of a device that typically ejects something like a CD or DVD drive.
The laptop has a nonejecting DVD drive, which means you have to manually remove the disc. 这台笔记本电脑有一个非弹出式DVD光驱，这意味着你必须手动取出光盘。
Near Synonyms: nonremovable, fixed
Antonyms: ejecting, removable
词性: 形容词
