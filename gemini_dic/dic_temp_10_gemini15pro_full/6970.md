andis: /ˈændɪs/
conjunction
和，与
And is a conjunction that connects words, phrases, or clauses.
例句：He and I are going to the movies. 他和我将去看电影。

例句：She likes apples and bananas. 她喜欢苹果和香蕉。

近义词：plus, also, as well as
反义词: nor, neither
