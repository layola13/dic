antinepotism:/ˌæntiˈnɛpətɪzəm/
|n.|反裙带关系主义，任人唯贤|The practice of opposing nepotism, especially in appointments to jobs.|The company has a strict policy of antinepotism to ensure fair hiring practices.  这家公司有严格的反裙带关系政策，以确保公平的招聘流程。|近义词:impartiality, fairness, meritocracy|反义词:nepotism, favoritism, cronyism|词性:名词

Etymology:
From anti- + nepotism.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Nepotism" comes from the Latin "nepos" meaning "nephew" or "grandson," referring to the practice of favoring relatives.
