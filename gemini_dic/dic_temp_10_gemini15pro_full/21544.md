bluefish:/ˈbluːfɪʃ/| n.|蓝鱼|A marine game fish (Pomatomus saltatrix) that is bluish or greenish above and silvery white below, found in temperate and tropical seas worldwide. It is known for its aggressive feeding habits.|We caught several bluefish while fishing off the coast. 我们在海岸边钓鱼时钓到了几条蓝鱼。|Synonyms: tailor, snapper, skipjack|Antonyms: None (No direct opposite for a specific type of fish)|noun
