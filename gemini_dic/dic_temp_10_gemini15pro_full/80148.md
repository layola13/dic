godamn:/ˌɡɑːdˈdæm/ | /ˌɡɒdˈdæm/ | adj. interj. | 該死的；討厭的；可惡的 | Used as a curse word to express anger, frustration, or annoyance. It can also be used as an intensifier before a noun or adjective. | Goddamn it, I locked my keys in the car!  該死的，我把鑰匙鎖在車裡了！ | Synonyms: damned, cursed, blasted | Antonyms: blessed, holy | adjective, interjection

goddamn:/ˌɡɑːdˈdæm/ | /ˌɡɒdˈdæm/ |  vt. | 咒罵 | To curse or condemn someone or something. | He goddamned the day he was born.  他咒罵他出生的那一天。 | Synonyms: curse, damn | Antonyms: bless | verb
