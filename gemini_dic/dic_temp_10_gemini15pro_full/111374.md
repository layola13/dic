livery's:/ˈlɪvəriz/| n.|制服；（尤指旧时仆人或官员的）特殊制服；（公司、行业等的）专用颜色；（出租车、公共汽车等的）公司标记；（飞机、船只等的）外观涂装|A company's or organization's livery is the special uniform or style of dress worn by its employees, or the special design and colors of its vehicles.  Specifically, it refers to the distinctive clothing, colors, or insignia worn by servants or officials, especially in the past. It can also refer to the characteristic design and colors of a company's vehicles or products. Finally, it can refer to the external appearance or coating of an aircraft, ship, etc.|The chauffeur wore the family's livery. 司机穿着家族的制服。

The airline's new livery features a striking blue and white design.  这家航空公司的新涂装以醒目的蓝白设计为特色。|近义词：uniform, attire, colors, markings|反义词: plain clothes|词性:名词
