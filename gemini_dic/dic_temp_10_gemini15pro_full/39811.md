consound:/kənˈsaʊnd/ | vt. | 使惊愕；使困惑；咒骂 | to confuse and surprise someone very much; to curse someone or something |  The sudden change in policy confounded us.  政策的突然改变使我们感到困惑。| 近义词: bewilder, confuse, perplex, astound, damn | 反义词: clarify, enlighten | 词性: 动词

consound:/kənˈsaʊnd/ | interj. | （表示愤怒或惊讶）该死，真见鬼 | used to express anger or surprise |  Consound it! I've lost my keys again!  该死！我又把钥匙弄丢了！ | 近义词: damn, blast | 反义词: (无) | 词性: 感叹词
