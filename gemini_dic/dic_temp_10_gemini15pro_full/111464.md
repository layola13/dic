lo-tech:/ˌloʊˈtɛk/
adjective
低技术的；技术含量低的
Using simple or readily available technology; not technologically sophisticated.
例句：
The school used a lo-tech solution to the problem of internet access.
学校使用低技术含量的方案解决了互联网接入问题。

例句：
They prefer lo-tech solutions, valuing simplicity and reliability.
他们更喜欢低技术解决方案，重视简单性和可靠性。

近义词：simple, basic, rudimentary, unsophisticated, low-end
反义词：high-tech, advanced, sophisticated, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art
词性: adjective
