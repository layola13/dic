mesosphere:/ˈmɛzəʊsfiə(r)/ /ˈmɛsoʊsfɪr/ | n. | 中间层 | The region of the earth's atmosphere above the stratosphere and below the thermosphere, between about 50 and 85 kilometers in altitude. | The meteors burn up in the mesosphere. 流星在中间层燃烧殆尽。 | synonyms: middle atmosphere | antonyms: N/A | noun

mesospheres:/ˈmɛzəʊsfiəz/ /ˈmɛsoʊsfɪrz/ | n. | 中间层（复数）|  The plural form of mesosphere, referring to multiple regions with the characteristics of a mesosphere. |  He studied the mesospheres of different planets. 他研究了不同行星的中间层。| synonyms: middle atmospheres | antonyms: N/A | noun
