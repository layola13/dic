nonrequisites:/nɒnˈrɛkwɪzɪts/ |n.|非必修课程；非先修课程|Courses that are not required to be taken before or alongside another course.|Students may choose from a range of nonrequisites in their final year.|Synonyms: electives, optional courses|Antonyms: prerequisites, required courses|noun
nonrequisite:/nɒnˈrɛkwɪzɪt/ |adj.|非必需的；非必备的|Not required or necessary as a prior condition.|A nonrequisite course can be taken at any time during the degree.|Synonyms: optional, elective, non-essential|Antonyms: prerequisite, required, mandatory|adjective
