mastic:/ˈmæstɪk/ | n. adj. |乳香，胶泥，油灰；乳香的|A resin obtained from the mastic tree, used in varnishes, chewing gum, and other products; used as an adjective to describe something made of or containing mastic. |The painter used mastic to seal the gaps in the woodwork. 画家用乳香来填补木制品中的缝隙。| 近义词:resin, gum, sealant, putty | 反义词: (As a sealant) solvent | 词性:noun, adjective
