hardwood:/ˈhɑːrdwʊd/| n.|硬木|The wood from a broad-leaved tree (such as oak, ash, or beech) as distinguished from that of conifers.  A tree that yields hardwood.|  The floors are made of beautiful hardwood.  地板是用漂亮的硬木做的。|synonyms: timber, lumber|antonyms: softwood|noun

hardwood:/ˈhɑːrdwʊd/| adj.|硬木的，落叶的|Made of hardwood; (of a tree) broad-leaved and deciduous.|They chose a hardwood table for its durability. 他们选择了一张硬木桌子，因为它很耐用。|synonyms: deciduous, broad-leaved|antonyms: coniferous, softwood|adjective
