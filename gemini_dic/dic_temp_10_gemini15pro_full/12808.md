assisted:/əˈsɪstɪd/| v. adj.|辅助的，协助的|To give support or help to someone in doing something.  Meaning something was done with help.|例句：The nurse assisted the doctor during the surgery.  护士在手术期间协助医生。|近义词：aided, helped, supported|反义词：hindered, obstructed|词性:动词(过去分词/过去式), 形容词


Etymology:
From Latin *assistere*, from *ad-* (“to, towards”) + *sistere* (“to stand”).
