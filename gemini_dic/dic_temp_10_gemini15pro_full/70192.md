fibrinoplastic:/ˌfaɪbrɪnoʊˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 促纤维素形成的；纤维蛋白形成的 | Relating to or promoting the formation of fibrin, a protein involved in blood clotting. |  Fibrinoplastic substances play a crucial role in hemostasis. 促纤维蛋白形成物质在止血过程中起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: coagulant, hemostatic | antonyms: anticoagulant, thrombolytic | adjective
