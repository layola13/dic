glomming:/ˈɡlɑːmɪŋ/
noun
依附；黏附；缠着；迷恋
The act of glomming onto something or someone.  This often implies an excessive or overly enthusiastic attachment.
She's been glomming onto me all night. 她整晚都缠着我。
synonyms: clinging, latching onto, attaching to, fixating on, obsessing over
antonyms: ignoring, avoiding, detaching from
n.

glomming:/ˈɡlɑːmɪŋ/
verb (gerund or present participle)
依附；黏附；缠着；迷恋
The act of becoming attached to something or someone, often excessively or overly enthusiastically.
He's glomming onto the latest fad. 他迷上了最新的潮流。
synonyms: clinging, latching onto, attaching to, fixating on, obsessing over
antonyms: ignoring, avoiding, detaching from
v.
