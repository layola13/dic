outwalloped: /ˌaʊtwɑːˈlɒpt/ | v. | 彻底击败，重创 | To defeat decisively or soundly; to surpass greatly. |  The underdog team outwalloped the reigning champions.  这支弱队彻底击败了卫冕冠军。|Synonyms: trounce, thrash, crush, rout, clobber | Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by | verb

outwalloped: /ˌaʊtwɑːˈlɒpt/ | v. | 远远超过，大大胜过 | To exceed or surpass by a large margin; to greatly outperform. | Our sales figures this quarter outwalloped all projections.  我们本季度的销售额远远超过了所有预测。| Synonyms: outstrip, surpass, exceed, eclipse | Antonyms: fall short of, lag behind | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + wallop (meaning "to beat soundly").  The prefix "out-" intensifies the meaning of "wallop," suggesting a victory or surpassing that is not just significant but overwhelming.
