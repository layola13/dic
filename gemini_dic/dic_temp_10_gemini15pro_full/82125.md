groining:/ˈɡrɔɪnɪŋ/
n.|腹腹股沟|The act of building or reinforcing a groin.  修建或加固腹股沟的行为。|例句：The groining of the riverbank prevented further erosion.  对河岸的加固防止了进一步的侵蚀。|近义词: reinforcement, buttressing|反义词: weakening, undermining|词性:名词
groining:/ˈɡrɔɪnɪŋ/
n.|交叉拱|An architectural feature formed by intersecting vaults.  由相交的拱顶形成的建筑特征。|例句：The cathedral's ceiling featured intricate groining.  大教堂的天花板以复杂的交叉拱为特色。|近义词: vaulting, ribbing|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词
