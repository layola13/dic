home:/hoʊm/ (US) /həʊm/ (UK)| adj.|家庭的；家乡的；国内的；本地的|Relating to or connected with one's home or family. |例句：I enjoy home cooking. 我喜欢家常菜。|近义词：domestic, household|反义词：foreign, away|adj.
