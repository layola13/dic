nonabrogable:/nɒnˈæbrəɡəbl/
adjective
不可废除的，不可取消的
Not able to be repealed or abolished.
The constitution guarantees certain nonabrogable rights. 宪法保障某些不可废除的权利。

The treaty established several nonabrogable principles of international law.  该条约确立了几项不可废除的国际法原则。

Synonym:inalienable, inviolable, unassailable
Antonym:revocable, repealable, abrogable
词性:adjective
