detaching:/dɪˈtætʃɪŋ/
verb, adjective|分离，拆卸；超然的，不带感情的|removing or separating something; emotionally removed or uninvolved|The workers are detaching the engine from the aircraft.  工人们正在将引擎从飞机上拆卸下来。|
synonyms: separating, disconnecting, uncoupling, aloof, indifferent
antonyms: attaching, connecting, coupling, involved, emotional
verb

detaching:/dɪˈtætʃɪŋ/
adjective|分离的，拆卸的；超然的，不带感情的|removed or separated; emotionally removed or uninvolved|She spoke in a detached tone, as if the news didn't affect her. 她用一种超然的语气说话，好像这个消息对她没有影响。|
synonyms: separated, disconnected, uncoupled, aloof, indifferent
antonyms: attached, connected, coupled, involved, emotional
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "destachier"  meaning "to untie, unfasten," from "de-" (expressing separation) + "estachier" (to attach).
