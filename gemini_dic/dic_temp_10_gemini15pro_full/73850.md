formulaic:/ˌfɔːrmjəˈleɪɪk/ | adj. | 公式化的，刻板的 | characterized by or consisting of fixed patterns; unoriginal and predictable. | The plot of the movie was formulaic and predictable. 这部电影的情节公式化，可以预见。 |  The speech was so formulaic it put everyone to sleep.  这篇演讲太公式化了，让所有人都睡着了。 | 近义词: clichéd, stereotyped, conventional, unoriginal, predictable, stock, trite, hackneyed | 反义词: original, creative, innovative, imaginative, fresh | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From formula + -ic.  "Formula" comes from Late Latin, meaning a "prescribed form" or "rule."
