fungite:/ˈfʌŋˌdʒaɪt/
n.
真菌化石;菌类化石
A fossil of a fungus.
Example: The researchers discovered a rare fungite embedded in the ancient rock. 研究人员在古老的岩石中发现了一种罕见的真菌化石。

Example: The fungite provided valuable insights into the evolution of fungi. 这块真菌化石为了解真菌的进化提供了宝贵的见解。
近义词:fossil fungus
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "living fungus")
词性:noun
