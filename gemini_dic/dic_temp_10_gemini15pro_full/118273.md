merchantmen: /ˈmɜːrtʃəntmən/ | n. | 商船；商船船员 | A ship used for commercial purposes; a sailor on a merchant ship | The old merchantmen sailed the seven seas. 那些老商船航行七大洋。 | 近义词: trading vessel, freighter | 反义词: warship | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Middle English *marchantman, equivalent to merchant + man.
