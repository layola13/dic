belatednesses:/bɪˈleɪtɪdnəsɪz/
n.
迟到；延误；姗姗来迟
the state of being late or delayed
His belatednesses caused him to miss the beginning of the meeting. 他的迟到让他错过了会议的开始。

例句：
The belatednesses of the train caused much inconvenience. 火车的延误造成了很多不便。
Her belatednesses in responding to the email was unprofessional. 她回复电子邮件的迟缓很不专业。

近义词：lateness, tardiness, delay
反义词：promptness, punctuality
