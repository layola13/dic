homeostatic:/ˌhoʊmioʊˈstætɪk/| adj.|体内平衡的，恒定的|Relating to the tendency of a system, especially the physiological system of higher animals, to maintain internal stability, owing to the coordinated response of its parts to any situation or stimulus tending to disturb its normal condition or balance.|The human body has various homeostatic mechanisms to regulate temperature. 人体具有多种体内平衡机制来调节体温。|近义词：regulatory, stabilizing, self-regulating|反义词：unstable, fluctuating|词性:形容词
homeostasis:/ˌhoʊmioʊˈsteɪsɪs/| n.|体内平衡，恒定性|The process by which an organism maintains a stable internal environment, such as a constant temperature or pH level, despite external changes.|Homeostasis is essential for the survival of all living organisms. 体内平衡对于所有生物的生存至关重要。|近义词：equilibrium, stability, balance|反义词：imbalance, instability, disequilibrium|词性:名词
