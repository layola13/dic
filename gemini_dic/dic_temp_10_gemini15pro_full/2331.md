adherent:/ədˈhɪərənt/ /ədˈhɪrənt/| n. |信徒，追随者，拥护者|A person who supports a particular party, person, or set of ideas.|She is a strong adherent of the vegan lifestyle. 她是一位严格的纯素生活方式的追随者。

adherent:/ədˈhɪərənt/ /ədˈhɪrənt/| adj. |附着的，粘着的|Sticking fast to a surface or substance.|An adherent stamp.  一张粘着的邮票。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adherentem*, present participle of *adhaerēre* "to stick to," from *ad-* "to" + *haerēre* "to stick."

USAGE->
(Noun)

The party's adherents campaigned tirelessly. = 该党的拥护者不知疲倦地进行竞选。

The cult leader had many adherents who believed his every word. =  这个邪教头目有很多信徒，他们相信他说的每一句话。


(Adjective)

The adherent dirt was difficult to remove. = 粘附的污垢很难清除。

The label was adherent to the bottle. = 标签粘在瓶子上。


SYNONYMS->
(Noun)
follower, supporter, devotee, advocate, believer, disciple

(Adjective)
sticky, clinging, adhering, attached, tenacious


ANTONYMS->
(Noun)
opponent, antagonist, critic, detractor

(Adjective)
non-adherent, loose, detached


PHRASE->
an adherent of (a philosophy/a religion/a political party) = （某种哲学/宗教/政党）的拥护者
ardent adherent =  热烈的拥护者
loyal adherent = 忠实的追随者
staunch adherent = 坚定的支持者
new adherent =  新信徒
