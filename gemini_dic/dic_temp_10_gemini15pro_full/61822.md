empirically:/ɪmˈpɪrɪkli/
adverb
经验地；以经验为依据地
Based on observation or experience rather than theory or logic.
The theory has been empirically verified.  这个理论已经通过经验验证。
Empirically, the method has been proven effective.  从经验上看，这种方法已被证明是有效的。
Synonym: experimentally, practically, observationally
Antonym: theoretically, hypothetically
词性: 副词
