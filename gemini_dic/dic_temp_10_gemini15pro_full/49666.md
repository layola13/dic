demisemiquavers:/ˌdɛmiˌsɛmiˈkweɪvəz/
n.
三十二分音符
A note played for one thirty-second of the duration of a whole note.  It is represented by three flags on the stem or a beam connecting to other demisemiquavers.
The piece is full of fast demisemiquavers, making it a challenge for even experienced musicians. 这首曲子充满了快速的三十二分音符，即使对经验丰富的音乐家来说也是一个挑战。

近义词:thirty-second notes
反义词:breve (二全音符)
