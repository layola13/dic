dopaminergic:/ˌdoʊpəməˈnɜːrdʒɪk/| adj.|多巴胺能的|Relating to or involving dopamine or its action in the nervous system.  Influenced by or releasing dopamine.|
例句：Parkinson's disease is caused by the degeneration of dopaminergic neurons. 帕金森氏病是由多巴胺能神经元的退化引起的。

例句：Dopaminergic pathways in the brain are involved in reward and motivation. 大脑中的多巴胺能通路参与奖励和动机。
|近义词：dopamine-related, dopamine-mediated|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps consider "antidopaminergic")|词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dopamine + -ergic.  Dopamine itself comes from dihydr**o**xy**p**henyl**a**lanine (its precursor molecule) + **amine**.  The "-ergic" suffix denotes "activated by" or "releasing."
