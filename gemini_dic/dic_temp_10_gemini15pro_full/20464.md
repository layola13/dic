bitterwort:/ˈbɪtərwɜːrt/| n. |龙胆，一种苦味的药草|A European gentian (Gentiana lutea) with a thick, branching rootstock, used as a tonic and in flavoring alcoholic beverages. It is known for its extremely bitter taste.|The apothecary used bitterwort in his tonic. 药剂师在他的补品中使用了龙胆。|近义词：gentian, felwort|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant, but perhaps something sweet like) licorice, sugar|noun
