concessionist:/kənˈseʃənɪst/
n.
妥协者；让步者
Someone who is willing to make concessions or compromises, especially in political negotiations.
The concessionist agreed to drop the demand for higher wages in exchange for better health benefits.  妥协者同意放弃提高工资的要求，以换取更好的健康福利。
The president was criticized for being too much of a concessionist in the negotiations. 总统因在谈判中过于妥协而受到批评。
近义词: compromiser, appeaser
反义词: hardliner, intransigent
词性: noun

concessionary:/kənˈseʃənəri/
adj.
让步的；特许的
Relating to or involving concessions.  Often used to describe prices or goods offered at a reduced rate.
The government offered concessionary fares for senior citizens on public transport. 政府为老年人提供公共交通的优惠票价。
The company offered concessionary loans to its employees. 公司向其员工提供优惠贷款。
近义词: discounted, reduced
反义词: full-price, standard
词性: adjective
