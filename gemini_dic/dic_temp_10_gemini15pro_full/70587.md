fillipeen:/ˈfɪlɪˌpiːn/
n.
小费; (尤指)餐馆服务生的小费;
A small sum of money given as a tip, especially to a waiter or waitress.
Example: He left a generous fillipeen for the waiter. 他给服务员留了一笔丰厚的小费。
Example:  She forgot to leave a fillipeen after her meal. 她吃完饭后忘记付小费了。
Synonyms: tip, gratuity
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "stinginess" in context)
noun
