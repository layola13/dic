hinged:/hɪndʒd/| adj.|铰接的，装有铰链的；依…而定的,取决于…的|Attached with a hinge or hinges; depending on something else|The door was hinged on the left side. 门左边装有铰链。|The success of the project is hinged on securing funding.  这个项目的成功取决于能否获得资金。|近义词：jointed, pivoted, dependent, contingent|反义词：fixed, rigid, independent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *hingen*, from Old English *hīnan* ("to hang, incline, bow down"), from Proto-Germanic *hēnganą* (“to hang”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱenk- (“to hang”).  The "-ed" suffix transforms the verb into a past participle, often used adjectivally.
