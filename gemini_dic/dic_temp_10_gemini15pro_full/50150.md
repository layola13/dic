denunciations: /dɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃənz/
n.
公开谴责，告发
Public accusations or condemnations of something as wrong or evil.
The government issued strong denunciations of the terrorist attacks. 政府强烈谴责了恐怖袭击。
synonyms: condemnations, criticisms, accusations, indictments, censures
antonyms: praises, commendations, endorsements, approvals
noun
