copyedited:/ˈkɒpiˌɛdɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːpiˌɛdɪtɪd/ | adj. | 已编辑的，已校订的 | Having been edited and corrected for publication. | The copyedited manuscript was sent to the printer. 已编辑好的稿件被送到了印刷厂。| The copyedited version was much clearer and more concise.  编辑后的版本更加清晰简洁。 | 近义词: edited, revised, proofread, corrected | 反义词: unedited, raw, draft | 词性: adjective

词源:  From "copy" (referring to written material) + "edit" (to prepare for publication).  The "-ed" suffix indicates the passive voice, meaning the copy *has been* edited.
