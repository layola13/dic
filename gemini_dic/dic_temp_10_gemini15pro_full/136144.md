nonrefractivenesses:/ˌnɒnriːˈfræktɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
非折射性
The quality or state of being nonrefractive.  The absence of refraction.
The material exhibited several nonrefractivenesses, leading to the distorted image.  这种材料表现出几种非折射性，导致图像失真。
Near synonyms: non-refractivity, lack of refraction
Opposite: refractiveness, refractivity
