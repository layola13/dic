mats:/mæts/| n. |垫子; 衬垫|Pieces of coarse material used to cover floors, protect surfaces, or absorb spills.  They can be made of various materials such as rubber, fabric, or straw. |The mechanic wiped his hands on some greasy mats. 技工在一些油腻的垫子上擦了擦手。|Synonyms: rugs, carpets, pads, cushions|Antonyms: bare floor, uncovered surface|Noun
