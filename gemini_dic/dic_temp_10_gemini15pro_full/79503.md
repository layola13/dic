globe:/ɡloʊb/ (US) /ɡləʊb/ (UK)| n.|地球，地球仪，球体|A spherical or globe-shaped object; especially a representation of the earth.|例句：The globe shows all the countries of the world. 地球仪展示了世界各国。|例句：She spun the globe and pointed to a small island. 她转动地球仪，指着一个小岛。|近义词：sphere, earth, world, ball|反义词: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *globe*, from Old French *globe*, from Latin *globus* (“sphere, ball, round mass”), of uncertain origin.
