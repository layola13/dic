immunodeficiency:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊdɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. | 免疫缺陷 | A condition in which the body's immune system is not able to function properly, leaving the person vulnerable to infections. |  People with immunodeficiency are more susceptible to infections. 免疫缺陷的人更容易受到感染。 |  The patient's immunodeficiency made him vulnerable to opportunistic infections. 病人的免疫缺陷使他容易受到机会性感染。 |  immunosuppression, immune deficiency | immunocompetence | noun
