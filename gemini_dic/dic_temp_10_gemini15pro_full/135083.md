nonobedience:/ˌnɒnəˈbiːdiəns/ | n. |不服从，违抗|The act of not obeying; disobedience. |His nonobedience to the rules resulted in his expulsion. 他不遵守规则导致他被开除。| The soldier's nonobedience of the direct order led to his court-martial.  士兵不服从直接命令导致他被军事法庭审判。 | 近义词：disobedience, defiance, insubordination, recalcitrance| 反义词：obedience, compliance, submission| 词性：名词
