litchi:/ˈliːtʃiː/ (US), /ˈlaɪtʃiː/ (UK)| n. |荔枝|A small rounded fruit with sweet white scented flesh, a brittle shell, and a large central stone.  Native to southeastern Asia.|例句：I love the sweet taste of fresh litchis. 我喜欢新鲜荔枝的甜味。

例句：Litchis are a popular fruit in many Asian countries. 荔枝在许多亚洲国家是一种很受欢迎的水果。|近义词：lychee|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
