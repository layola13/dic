nonprolificacies:/ˌnɒnˌprɒlɪˈfɪkəsiz/
n.
缺乏多产性；不丰富
Lack of prolificacy; the state of being unproductive or not yielding much.
The nonprolificacies of the artist's later years were attributed to his failing health.  这位艺术家晚年作品匮乏，这归因于他日渐衰弱的健康。
Near synonyms: barrenness, sterility, unproductiveness
Antonyms:  abundance, fertility, prolificacy
