mocks:/mɑːks/ (US), /mɒks/ (UK)| n. v.|嘲弄，模仿；仿制品|To tease or laugh at someone in a disrespectful or scornful manner.  To make something that looks like something else but is not real or genuine.|He mocks her attempts at cooking. 他嘲笑她做饭的尝试。|近义词：ridicules, teases, imitates, parodies|反义词:praises, respects|noun, verb
