herbalist:/ˈɜːrbəlɪst/ | n. | 草药师；草药医生 | A person who is expert in the use of herbs, especially medicinal herbs. |  She visited a local herbalist for a natural remedy. 她去看了一位当地的草药师，寻求一种自然疗法。| synonym: herbal practitioner, herborist | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional doctor") | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *erbalist*, from Anglo-Norman and Middle French *herboliste*, from Medieval Latin *herbalista*, from Latin *herba* ("herb") + *-ista* (agent suffix).
