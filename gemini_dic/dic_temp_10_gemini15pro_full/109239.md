leisurelinesses:/ˈliːʒərlɪnɪsɪz/
noun
悠闲，从容，缓慢
The state of being leisurely; unhurriedness.
The leisurelinesses of his movements suggested a relaxed attitude. 他动作的悠闲表明他很放松。
leisureliness, calmness, slowness
hastiness, hurriedness, rapidity
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From leisurely + -ness.  Leisurely itself comes from leisure (from Old French leisir, ultimately from Latin licēre "to be permitted") + -ly.
