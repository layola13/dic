dentiroster:/ˈdɛntiˌrɒstər/| n. |齿嘴龙|A type of extinct toothed bird from the Cretaceous period.|The dentiroster fossils provided valuable insights into the evolution of birds. 齿嘴龙化石为了解鸟类的进化提供了宝贵的见解。|Synonyms: toothed bird,  Cretaceous bird|Antonyms: modern bird|noun
