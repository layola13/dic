omg:/oʊ ɛm ˈdʒiː/ | interjection | 我的天哪；哦，我的上帝 | Oh my God (or gosh). Used to express surprise, excitement, or disbelief. | OMG, I won the lottery! 我的天哪，我中彩票了！ | Oh my god, oh my gosh, oh my goodness |  (No direct antonym for an interjection like this) | interjection

Example: OMG, that's amazing! 我的天啊，太棒了！

Example:  OMG, I can't believe it! 我的天啊，我简直不敢相信！

Etymology:  Initialism of "Oh my God".  First recorded use in 1917.
