adenology: /ˌædɪˈnɒlədʒi/
noun
腺体学
The study of glands.
Adenology is a branch of medicine concerned with the structure and function of glands. 腺体学是医学的一个分支，研究腺体的结构和功能。
His research in adenology led to a breakthrough in understanding hormonal imbalances.  他的腺体学研究在理解激素失衡方面取得了突破。
近义词:  glandular science
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider areas outside of gland study, e.g., cardiology 心脏病学)
词性: noun
