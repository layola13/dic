breaming:/ˈbriːmɪŋ/| adj.|喜气洋洋的，容光焕发的，盈满的|Full of cheerfulness; radiant; overflowing.|She was beaming with pride after her daughter's graduation. 她女儿毕业后，她脸上洋溢着自豪。|近义词：radiant, cheerful, glowing, smiling, happy|反义词：glum, gloomy, sad, unhappy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *bremen, from Old English brēman (“to make famous, celebrate, extol, shine, gleam, burn”), from Proto-Germanic *brōmijaną (“to be famous”). Cognate with Old High German brāman (“to resound, roar, rage”), Old Norse brá (“eyelash, eyelid”), Icelandic brá (“eyelash, eyelid”).  The sense development is from "shining" to "radiant with joy".
