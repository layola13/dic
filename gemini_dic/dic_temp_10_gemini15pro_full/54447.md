disinheritable:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtəbl/
adj. |可剥夺继承权的|Able to be deprived of the right of inheritance. |The prodigal son's actions made him disinheritable in his father's eyes.  这个败家子的行为使他在父亲眼中失去了继承权。|Synonyms: excludable,  deprivable |Antonyms: inheritable, entitled| adjective

disinherit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/
vt. |剥夺继承权|To deprive (someone) of the right to inherit property. |He disinherited his son for marrying against his wishes. 他剥夺了违背他意愿结婚的儿子的继承权。|Synonyms: exclude, deprive |Antonyms: bequeath, endow| verb
