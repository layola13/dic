cadaverine:/kəˈdævəˌriːn/
noun
尸胺
A foul-smelling compound produced by the putrefaction of animal tissue.
例句：Cadaverine is one of the amines responsible for the distinctive odor of decaying flesh. 尸胺是造成腐肉特有气味的一种胺。

例句：The presence of cadaverine can be used to estimate the time of death. 尸胺的存在可以用来估计死亡时间。

近义词:putrescine
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a chemical compound)
