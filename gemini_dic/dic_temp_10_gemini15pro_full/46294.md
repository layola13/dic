/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) |pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which|反义词: (No direct antonym)|pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie started? 电影开始前他们吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? 到明年夏天他们会建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years? 到时候你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on by the time she retires? 到她退休的时候，她将一直在做什么？

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt (Northumbrian, Mercian), hwat (West Saxon), from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷod. Cognates include Old Frisian hwet, Old Saxon hwat, Old High German hwaz, Old Norse hvað, Gothic ƕa, Latin quod.
