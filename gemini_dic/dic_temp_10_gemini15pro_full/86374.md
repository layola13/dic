heddle:/ˈhɛdəl/| n.|综框，综片，导综|A heddle is a wire, string, or thin flat piece of metal with a hole or eyelet in the middle through which a warp yarn is threaded in a loom.|The weaver carefully threaded each yarn through its designated heddle.|Synonyms: heald, leash|Antonyms: N/A|noun
