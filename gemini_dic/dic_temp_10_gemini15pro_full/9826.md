anything: /ˈɛniθɪŋ/ | adv. | 稍微；丝毫 |To any extent or degree; at all.  (Usually used in negative, interrogative, and conditional contexts.) |例句：He isn’t anything like his brother. 他一点也不像他哥哥。| 近义词: somewhat, slightly | 反义词: not at all | 词性:副词
