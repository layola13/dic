heartfelt:/ˈhɑːrtfelt/ (US), /ˈhɑːtˌfelt/ (UK)| adj.|衷心的，真诚的|Deeply and sincerely felt; earnest.|She expressed her heartfelt gratitude. 她表达了衷心的感谢。|例句：He gave a heartfelt apology for his mistake. 他诚挚地为自己的错误道歉。|近义词：sincere, genuine, earnest, warm|反义词：insincere, fake, artificial|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From heart + felt (past participle of feel).  Meaning "felt deeply; sincere" is from the late 18th century.
