full's:/fʊlz/ | n. | 傻瓜; 蠢人 (Informal, often offensive) |  A foolish or stupid person. This word is the possessive form of "fool" and is rarely used on its own.  It's more common to see "a fool's errand" or "a fool's paradise." |  It's a fool's errand to try and reason with him.  试图和他讲道理是徒劳的。| 近义词: idiot, moron, imbecile | 反义词: genius, sage | 词性: 名词 (possessive)
