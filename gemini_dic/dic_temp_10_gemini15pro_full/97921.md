innocently:/ˈɪnəsəntli/| adv.|无辜地，天真地，单纯地|In a way that shows a lack of guilt or wrongdoing; in a naive or unsuspecting manner.  Derived from "innocent" + "-ly".  The Latin root is "innocens" (not harming).|She smiled innocently at him. 她天真地对他笑了笑。|The child innocently asked why the sky was blue.  孩子天真地问为什么天空是蓝色的。|近义词：naively, guilelessly, artlessly|反义词：guiltily, knowingly, deliberately|词性:副词
