mollify:/ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 平息，抚慰，缓和 | to make someone less angry or upset; to make something less severe or less extreme |  The manager tried to mollify the angry customer by offering a refund. 经理试图通过提供退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。

The warm bath helped to mollify her aching muscles.  热水澡帮助她缓解了肌肉酸痛。 | 近义词: appease, soothe, pacify, placate | 反义词: aggravate, exacerbate, provoke, incite | 词性: verb (动词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mollificāre, from Latin mollis (“soft”) + -ficāre (from facere (“to make”)).
