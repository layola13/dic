fufu:/ˈfuːfuː/
n.
fufu; foo-foo
A starchy dish, especially in West Africa, made from boiled and mashed starchy vegetables such as cassava, yams, or plantains.
Fufu is often served with stews and soups.  富富是西非的一种淀粉类食物，由煮熟并捣碎的淀粉类蔬菜（如木薯、山药或大蕉）制成。
She made fufu for dinner. 她晚饭做了富富。
The fufu was served with a spicy peanut stew.  富富配上辣花生炖菜一起食用。
近义词: pounded yam, akpu, sadza
