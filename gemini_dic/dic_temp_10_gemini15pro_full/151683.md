partitionism:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
分裂主义，分治主义
The advocacy or practice of partitioning a geographical area, typically by separating differing ethnic or religious groups.
India and Pakistan gained independence after the partition of British India.  印度和巴基斯坦在英属印度分裂后获得独立。
The partitionism movement gained momentum in the 1990s.  分裂主义运动在20世纪90年代势头强劲。
近义词：separatism, division, sectarianism
反义词：unity, integration, unification
词性: noun


Etymology:
From partition + -ism.  源自 partition（分隔） + -ism（主义）
