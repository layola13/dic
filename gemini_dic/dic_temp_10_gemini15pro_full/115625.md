marchers:/ˈmɑːrtʃərz/| n.|游行者，行进者|People who march, especially in a protest or demonstration. |Thousands of marchers gathered in the city square to protest against the new law. 数千名游行者聚集在城市广场抗议新法律。|
marchers:/ˈmɑːrtʃərz/| n.|行军者|Soldiers or other people marching in formation. |The marchers were exhausted after days of walking through the desert. 行军者在沙漠中走了几天后筋疲力尽。|
marchers:/ˈmɑːrtʃərz/| n.|参加游行者|Participants in a march, such as a parade. |The marchers in the annual St. Patrick's Day parade were dressed in green.  一年一度的圣帕特里克节游行中的游行者都穿着绿色衣服。 |近义词：protesters, demonstrators, participants, walkers|反义词:onlookers, spectators|词性:Noun
