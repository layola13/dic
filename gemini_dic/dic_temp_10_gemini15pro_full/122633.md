modificate:/ˈmɒdɪfɪkeɪt/| v. |修改，更改|To make partial or minor changes to something, typically so as to improve it or to make it less extreme.|例句：The software needs to be modificated to accommodate the new hardware.  这个软件需要修改以适应新的硬件。

例句：We modificated our plans because of the bad weather. 由于天气不好，我们修改了计划。|近义词：alter, change, adjust, amend, revise, modify|反义词:maintain, preserve, keep|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *modificātus, past participle of modificāre (“to measure, moderate, restrain, limit”), from Latin modus (“measure, limit”) + facere (“to make, do”). Equivalent to modify + -ate. Related to mode, modify.
