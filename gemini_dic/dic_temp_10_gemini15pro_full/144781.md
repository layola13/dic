over-expressed:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsprɛst/ | adj. | 过度表达的 |  Expressed at a higher level than normal.  This term is commonly used in biology to describe genes or proteins that are produced in larger quantities than usual. | The over-expressed protein caused significant changes in the cell.  过度表达的蛋白质导致细胞发生了显著变化。

The over-expressed gene led to an increased risk of cancer.  过度表达的基因导致癌症风险增加。 | overproduced, upregulated | underexpressed, downregulated | adjective
