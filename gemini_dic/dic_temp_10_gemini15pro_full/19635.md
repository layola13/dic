biked:/baɪkt/
verb
骑自行车（过去式）
past tense of "bike," meaning to travel by bicycle.
I biked to work yesterday. 我昨天骑自行车去上班。
He biked ten miles this morning. 他今天早上骑了十英里自行车。
近义词: cycled
反义词: walked, drove
词性: verb
