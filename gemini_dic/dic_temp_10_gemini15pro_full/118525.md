mesodermal:/ˌmezəˈdɜːrməl/ /ˌmiːzəˈdɜːrməl/
|adj.|中胚层的，中胚叶的|Relating to or derived from the mesoderm.|The mesodermal layer gives rise to muscles and connective tissues. 中胚层会产生肌肉和结缔组织。|Synonyms: mesoblastic|Antonyms: ectodermal, endodermal|adjective

mesodermal:/ˌmezəˈdɜːrməl/ /ˌmiːzəˈdɜːrməl/
|n.|中胚层组织|Tissue derived from the mesoderm.|The scientist studied the mesodermal development in the embryo. 科学家研究了胚胎中中胚层的生长发育。|Synonyms: mesoblast|Antonyms: ectoderm, endoderm|noun
