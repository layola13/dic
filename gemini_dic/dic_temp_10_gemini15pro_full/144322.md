outsource:/ˈaʊtsɔːrs/| v. |外包，外判|To obtain (goods or a service) from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source.|例句：The company outsources its IT support.  这家公司将其IT支持外包。|例句：Many companies outsource their manufacturing to developing countries. 许多公司将他们的制造业务外包给发展中国家。|近义词：subcontract, contract out|反义词：insource|词性：动词

outspurn:/ˈaʊtspɜːrn/| v. |超越，胜过|To surpass or outdo someone or something, especially in speed or activity.|例句：He easily outspurned his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中轻松超越了他的对手。|例句：The new sports car outspurns all others in its class. 这款新的跑车在同级别车型中胜过所有其他车型。|近义词：outstrip, surpass, exceed, outdo|反义词：lag behind, fall behind|词性：动词
