donned:/dɑnd/ | v. | 穿上，戴上 | put on (an item of clothing) | He donned his coat and hat and went out. 他穿上外套，戴上帽子，出去了。| She donned a beautiful gown for the ball. 她穿上了一件漂亮的礼服去参加舞会。 | synonyms: put on, wear, assume, dress in | antonyms: doff, remove, take off | verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *donnen*, from Old French *doner*, *doigner*, from Latin *donāre* (“to give, present, bestow”), from *donum* (“gift”).  The original sense was "to give or present (something to someone)", hence "to bestow (a garment) upon oneself," i.e., "to put on."
