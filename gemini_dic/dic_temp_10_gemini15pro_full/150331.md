panhellenium:/ˌpænheˈliːniəm/ | n. |泛希腊主义；泛希腊运动 | A movement advocating the political unification of all Greeks. | Greece aspired to panhellenium during the classical period. 希腊在古典时期渴望泛希腊主义。|  The Olympic Games were an expression of panhellenium. 奥运会是泛希腊主义的一种表达。 | Synonyms:  Hellenism, Greek unity | Antonyms:  Disunity, division | noun

panhellenium:/ˌpænheˈliːniəm/ | adj. |泛希腊的 | Relating to or encompassing all of ancient Greece. | The panhellenic games were attended by athletes from all Greek city-states. 泛希腊运动会吸引了来自所有希腊城邦的运动员。 | The panhellenic spirit fostered a sense of shared identity. 泛希腊精神培养了一种共同认同感。 | Synonyms:  All-Greek, pan-Hellenic | Antonyms:  Local, regional | adjective
