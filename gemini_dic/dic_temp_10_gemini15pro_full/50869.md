desensitise: /diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/ | v. | 使脱敏；使不再敏感 | To make someone less sensitive to something.  | Repeated exposure to violence in movies can desensitise viewers to real-life violence.  反复接触电影中的暴力会使观众对现实生活中的暴力麻木。 |  numb, dull, inure | sensitise | verb

desensitizes: /diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使脱敏；使不再敏感 (第三人称单数形式) |  Third person singular present tense form of "desensitise". | Playing violent video games desensitizes him to the suffering of others. 玩暴力电子游戏让他对别人的痛苦麻木不仁。 | numbs, dulls, inures | sensitises | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (meaning "remove" or "reduce") + sensitise (meaning "to make sensitive").  The word "sensitise" ultimately comes from the Latin "sensus," meaning "sense" or "feeling."
