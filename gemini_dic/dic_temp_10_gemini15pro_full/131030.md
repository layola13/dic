nonauthoritativenesses:/ˌnɒnɔːˌθɒrɪˈteɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏权威性；非权威性；非官方性
The nonauthoritativenesses of the various sources made it difficult to determine the truth.  各种来源的非权威性使得难以确定真相。
The report highlighted the nonauthoritativenesses of the online information. 报告强调了在线信息的非权威性。
Synonyms:  unofficialness, invalidity, informality
Antonyms: authority, validity, officialness


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not
authoritative (adjective): having due authority; having the sanction or weight of authority
-ness (suffix): forming nouns expressing state or quality
