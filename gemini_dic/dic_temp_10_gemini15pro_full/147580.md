overpessimistic: /ˌoʊvərˌpɛsɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过于悲观的 | Excessively pessimistic; expecting the worst possible outcome. |  He had an overpessimistic view of the future, convinced everything would go wrong. 他对未来过于悲观，确信一切都会出错。|  The overpessimistic forecast deterred investors from the project. 过于悲观的预测吓退了项目的投资者。| 近义词: excessively pessimistic, unduly gloomy, overly negative | 反义词: optimistic, hopeful, positive | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "pessimistic" (from Latin "pessimus," meaning "worst").
