corporatism:/ˈkɔːrpərətɪzəm/| n.|社团主义，统制主义|A political system where interest groups, like businesses, labor unions, and agricultural groups, become integrated into the government's decision-making process.|Italy under Mussolini was an example of corporatism.|Synonyms: syndicalism, statism|Antonyms: individualism, liberalism|noun
corporatisms:/ˈkɔːrpərətɪzəmz/| n.|社团主义（复数）|Plural form of corporatism, referring to multiple instances or types of corporatist systems.|The corporatisms of the 20th century varied greatly in their implementation and impact.|Synonyms:  syndicalisms, statisms|Antonyms: individualisms, liberalisms|noun
