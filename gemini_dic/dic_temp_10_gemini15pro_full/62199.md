encountered: /ɪnˈkaʊntərd/ | v. | 遇到，遭遇 | past tense of "encounter" meaning to experience something, especially something unexpected or difficult.  | I encountered several problems while trying to complete the project.  我试图完成这个项目时遇到了一些问题。 | synonyms: met, faced, experienced, confronted | antonyms: avoided, missed, bypassed | verb

encountered: /ɪnˈkaʊntərd/ | adj. | 遇到的，遭遇的 |  (less common usage) describes something that has been met or experienced. | The encountered difficulties delayed the project.  遇到的困难推迟了项目。 | synonyms: experienced, faced | antonyms: avoided, unexperienced | adjective
