commented:/ˈkɒmɛntɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɛntɪd/ (US)| adj.|已评论的，已加注释的|Having had comments or notes added.  This refers to something, usually a piece of writing or code, that has been explained or reviewed through the addition of comments.|The commented code was much easier to understand. 已加注释的代码更容易理解。|近义词：annotated, explained, reviewed|反义词：uncommented, unexplained|词性:形容词

commented:/ˈkɒmɛntɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmɛntɪd/ (US)| v.|评论，注释 (过去分词形式)|Past participle of the verb "comment," meaning to express an opinion or reaction.|He commented on the current political situation. 他对当前的政治局势发表了评论.  (This is the verb form, not the adjective.)|近义词：remarked, noted, observed|反义词：(No direct antonym for the verb form)|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "comment," which comes from the Old French "comenter," meaning "to devise, invent, compose," ultimately from the Latin "comminisci," meaning "to devise, contrive."  The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle, indicating a completed action.
