outsped:/ˌaʊtˈspɛd/
verb
超过…的速度; 比…跑(或游、飞等)得快
to move faster than someone or something else
例句：He easily outsped the other runners. 他轻松地超过了其他赛跑者。
例句：The cheetah outsped its prey. 猎豹比它的猎物跑得快。
近义词：outrun, outpace, overtake
反义词：lag behind, trail
词性：v.
