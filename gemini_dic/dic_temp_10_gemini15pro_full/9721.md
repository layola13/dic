antiunion:/ˌæntiˈjuːnjən/ | adj. | 反工会的，反对工会的 | Opposed to labor unions; against the formation or existence of labor unions. | The company's antiunion stance led to several protests. 这家公司的反工会立场引发了几次抗议。|  The senator proposed antiunion legislation.  这位参议员提出了反工会的立法。 | 近义词: anti-labor | 反义词: pro-union | 词性: adjective

词源: anti- (against) + union (labor union)
