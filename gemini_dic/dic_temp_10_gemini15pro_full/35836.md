cockney:/ˈkɒkni/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkni/ (US)| n. adj.|伦敦东区人，伦敦东区方言|A native of the East End of London, traditionally one born within earshot of the Bow Bells; the dialect or accent traditionally spoken by such people, characterized by dropping the 'h' at the beginning of words and by rhyming slang.|例句：He spoke with a broad cockney accent. 他说话带着浓重的伦敦东区口音。|近义词：East Ender, Londoner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone from a different region like a "West Ender")|词性: noun, adjective

cockney:/ˈkɒkni/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkni/ (US)| adj.|伦敦东区的，伦敦东区方言的|Relating to or characteristic of the East End of London or its inhabitants.|例句：She grew up in a cockney family. 她在一个伦敦东区家庭长大。|近义词：East End|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: adjective
