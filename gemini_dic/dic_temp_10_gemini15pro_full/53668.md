disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/ /dəˈsaɪpəl/
n.
弟子，信徒，追随者
A follower or student of a teacher, leader, or philosopher.
Jesus had twelve disciples. 耶稣有十二个门徒。
The renowned scientist had many disciples who carried on his work.  这位著名的科学家有很多继承他工作的弟子。
follower, adherent, pupil, student
opponent, critic
noun
