cherriest:/ˈtʃɛriɪst/| adj.|最愉快的，最兴高采烈的|superlative adjective of cheery; most cheery|Her smile was the cherriest I'd seen all day. 她今天一整天都笑容满面，是我见过的最开心的笑容。|The cherriest welcome awaited us. 我们受到了最热烈的欢迎。|近义词：happiest, most cheerful, jolliest|反义词：gloomiest, saddest, most miserable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From cheery + -est.  Cheery itself comes from Middle English *cheri*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old North French *chiere* "face, countenance."
