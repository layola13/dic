nonreproduction:/ˌnɑːnˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən/
|n.| 非繁殖，不生殖|The nonreproduction of certain species is a concern for conservationists.  某些物种的无法繁殖是环保主义者关心的问题。|
|英文解析: The inability or failure to reproduce; the state or condition of not reproducing.  It can refer to biological reproduction (like animals and plants) or the copying of other things (like documents).|
|例句: The nonreproduction of the endangered panda is a serious problem. 濒危熊猫的无法繁殖是一个严重的问题。|

|例句:  The museum ensures the nonreproduction of its valuable artifacts. 博物馆确保其珍贵文物不被复制。|
|近义词: sterility, barrenness, infertility|
|反义词: reproduction, procreation, propagation|
|词性: noun|
