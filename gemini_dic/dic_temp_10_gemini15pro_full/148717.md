overtrace:/ˈoʊvərˌtreɪs/
verb
过度描摹，过分追溯
To trace or copy (something) excessively or with too much attention to detail.
He overtraced the lines, making the drawing look heavy. 他过度描摹线条，使绘画看起来很沉重。
The historian overtraced the origins of the conflict, losing sight of the bigger picture.  历史学家过分追溯冲突的起源，忽略了全局。
近义词: overdo, overemphasize, belabor
反义词: skim, summarize, overlook
词性: v.
