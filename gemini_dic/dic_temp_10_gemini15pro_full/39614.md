consentaneity:/ˌkɒnsɛntəˈneɪɪti/
n.
一致，同意，和谐
Agreement, accord, harmony, especially in thoughts, feelings, or actions.
The consentaneity of the group's decision was remarkable, given their diverse backgrounds.  小组成员的决定如此一致，考虑到他们不同的背景，真是了不起。
近义词：unanimity, concord, agreement, harmony
反义词：discord, disagreement, dissonance
词性: 名词
