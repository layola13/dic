brash: /bræʃ/| adj.|傲慢的，鲁莽的；性急的；华而不实的|Self-assertive in a rude, noisy, or overbearing way.|He's a brash young man who isn't afraid to speak his mind. 他是一个傲慢的年轻人，不怕说出自己的想法。|例句：The brash young executive alienated his colleagues with his arrogant behavior.  这位傲慢的年轻主管以其傲慢的行为疏远了他的同事。

例句：Her brash manner made her unpopular with her classmates.  她鲁莽的态度让她在同学中不受欢迎。

近义词：impudent, insolent, arrogant, cocky, audacious, brazen, bold, forward, pushy|反义词：modest, humble, reserved, timid, diffident|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From earlier brash "attack impetuously, rush," probably of imitative origin. Related to crash, dash.  Meaning shifted to "impetuous" and then to "impudent."
