drowsily:/ˈdraʊzɪli/| adv.|昏昏欲睡地，睡眼惺忪地|In a sleepy or drowsy manner.  Characterized by drowsiness.|She smiled drowsily and closed her eyes. 她睡眼惺忪地笑了笑，闭上了眼睛。|近义词：sleepily, lethargically|反义词: alertly, energetically|词性: 副词
Etymology: From drowsy + -ly.  Drowsy comes from Old English *drūsian "become sluggish, sink," related to dreosan "fall, sink."
