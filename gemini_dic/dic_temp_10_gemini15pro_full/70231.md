fickleness:/ˈfɪk(ə)lnəs/
n.
易变；反复无常；薄情
the quality of being likely to change your opinion or your feelings suddenly and without a good reason
Her fickleness is notorious; she changes boyfriends every month. 她以反复无常而闻名，每个月都换男朋友。

近义词:capriciousness, volatility, instability, inconstancy
反义词:constancy, steadfastness, stability
词性:noun
