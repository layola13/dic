fame:/feɪm/
n.|名词|声誉，名望|The state of being known or talked about by many people, especially on account of notable achievements.|His fame spread throughout the country. 他的名声传遍了全国。|
synonym: renown, reputation, celebrity, notoriety, eminence|
antonym: obscurity, anonymity|
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fame*, from Old French *fame*, from Latin *fāma* ("rumor, report, public opinion, reputation, fame").
