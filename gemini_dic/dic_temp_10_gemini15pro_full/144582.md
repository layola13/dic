outwatching:/ˌaʊtˈwɒtʃɪŋ/ | present participle | 在外面观看；外出观看 |  The act of being outside and watching something. It's a combination of "out" (outside) and the present participle of "watch" (watching). |  I was outwatching the meteor shower last night. 我昨晚在外面观看流星雨。| 近义词: observing, viewing, watching outdoors | 反义词: indoors, inside | verb
