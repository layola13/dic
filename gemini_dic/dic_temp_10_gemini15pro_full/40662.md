convergency:/kənˈvɜːrdʒənsi/
n.
趋同，收敛
The convergency of the two lines created a focal point. 两条线的趋同创造了一个焦点。
The convergency of their opinions led to a unified strategy. 他们的意见趋同，形成了统一的战略。
convergence
divergence
noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *convergentia, from Latin convergō (“incline together”) + -ia.  Equivalent to converge +‎ -ency.
