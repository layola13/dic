fluids:/ˈfluːɪdz/| n.|流体|Substances that can flow and take the shape of their container, such as liquids and gases.|Hydraulic fluids are essential for the proper functioning of machinery. 液压油对于机器的正常运转至关重要。|近义词：liquids, gases|反义词:solids|词性:名词

fluids:/ˈfluːɪdz/| adj.|流动的，流畅的|Having the characteristics of a fluid; able to flow easily.|Her movements were fluid and graceful. 她的动作流畅而优雅。|近义词：flowing, smooth|反义词:rigid, stiff|词性:形容词
