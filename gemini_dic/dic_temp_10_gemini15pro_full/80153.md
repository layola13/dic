godchild:/ˈɡɒdˌtʃaɪld/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdˌtʃaɪld/ (US)| n.|教子，教女|A child who has a godparent.|例句：My godchild is starting school next year. 我的教子明年就要上学了。

例句：She bought a lovely christening gift for her godchild. 她为她的教女买了一份可爱的洗礼礼物。

近义词: ward (被监护人)  (Note: meaning is not exactly the same, a ward is under legal guardianship, while a godchild is under spiritual guidance)

反义词: None (There is no direct antonym for godchild)
词性: noun
