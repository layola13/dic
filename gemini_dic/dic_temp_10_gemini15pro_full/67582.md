extrasensory:/ˌekstrəˈsɛnsəri/| adj.|超感官的，超感觉的|Beyond the normal senses; perceived by or seeming to involve a sense other than the usual five. |She claimed to have extrasensory perception and could predict the future. 她声称拥有超感官知觉，可以预测未来。|例句：He believed he had extrasensory powers and could communicate with the dead. 他相信自己拥有超感官能力，可以与死者交流。|近义词：psychic, supernatural, clairvoyant|反义词：normal, ordinary|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From extra- (meaning "outside, beyond") + sensory (relating to the senses).  "Extra-" comes from Latin, and "sensory" ultimately derives from Latin "sensus" (meaning "sense, feeling").
