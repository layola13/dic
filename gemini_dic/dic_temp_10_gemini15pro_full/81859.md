gridironing:/ˈɡrɪdˌaɪərnɪŋ/
n.|网格化；严密控制；在烤架上烤|The act of marking something with a grid pattern, subjecting something to strict control or scrutiny, or cooking on a gridiron.|例句：The city was subjected to intense gridironing by the police after the riots. 骚乱发生后，警察对这座城市进行了严密的控制。

例句：The steak was perfectly gridironed. 牛排烤得非常完美。
|近义词：scrutinizing, examining, grilling|反义词: neglecting, ignoring|词性:名词

gridiron:/ˈɡrɪdˌaɪərn/
n.|烤架；橄榄球场|A metal frame with parallel bars for cooking food over a fire; a football field.|例句：The burgers sizzled on the gridiron. 汉堡在烤架上发出咝咝声。

例句：The gridiron was packed with cheering fans. 橄榄球场挤满了欢呼的球迷。
|近义词：grill, barbecue, football field|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
