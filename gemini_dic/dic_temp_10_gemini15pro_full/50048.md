denounce:/dɪˈnaʊns/ /dɪˈnaʊns/| vt.|谴责，告发|To publicly declare to be wrong or evil; to condemn openly.|The newspaper denounced the government's new policy. 这家报纸谴责了政府的新政策。|近义词：condemn, criticize, censure, reprehend|反义词: praise, commend, laud, extol|verb

denounce:/dɪˈnaʊns/ /dɪˈnaʊns/| n.|谴责，告发（名词形式较少使用）|An act of denouncing; a public condemnation.|His denouncement of the corruption was met with applause. 他对腐败的谴责赢得了掌声。|近义词：condemnation, criticism, censure|反义词: praise, commendation, laudation|noun
