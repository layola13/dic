词源:  "Inbetween" comes from the combination of "in" and "between".  "Inbetweeners" is simply the plural form of this with the -er suffix, indicating a person in that state. "In" 和 "between" 组合而成 "inbetween"。“inbetweeners”只是这个词的复数形式，加上 -er 后缀，表示处于这种状态的人。
