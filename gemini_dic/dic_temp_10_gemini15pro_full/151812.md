passed-along:/ˌpæst əˈlɒŋ/ | adj. | 传递的，转述的 |  Something that is passed along is given to someone who then gives it to someone else.  This often refers to information, stories, or objects. | My grandmother passed along her secret recipe for apple pie. 我的祖母把她的苹果派秘方传给了我。 | Synonyms: handed-down, inherited, transmitted | Antonyms: kept, withheld | Adjective

passed-along:/ˌpæst əˈlɒŋ/ | n. | 传递，转述 | The act of passing something along.  Rarely used as a noun. | The passed-along of the family heirloom was a significant event.  家族传家宝的传递是一件意义重大的事件。| Synonyms: transmission, transfer | Antonyms: retention, withholding | Noun
