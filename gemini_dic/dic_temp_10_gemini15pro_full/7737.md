anoplotherium:/əˌnɒpləˈθɪəriəm/ | n. | 无角兽，中兽 | An extinct, hoofed mammal of the Eocene and Oligocene epochs, resembling a small rhinoceros with a long tail and two toes on each foot. It is considered an early relative of both pigs and hippos. |  The anoplotherium, despite its name, wasn't related to modern rhinoceroses. 尽管名字如此，无角兽与现代犀牛并没有亲缘关系。 | The anoplotherium fossils were found in Europe. 无角兽化石在欧洲被发现。 | synonyms: X (No close modern equivalent) | antonyms: X (Not applicable to extinct animals) | noun
