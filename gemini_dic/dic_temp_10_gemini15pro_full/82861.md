gulleted:/ˈɡʌlɪtɪd/
adjective
有食道的；大嘴的；贪吃的
having a gullet (esophagus); having a large mouth or appetite.
The gulleted bird swallowed the fish whole. 这只大嘴鸟把鱼整个吞了下去。
近义词：greedy, ravenous, voracious
反义词：abstemious, temperate
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "gullet" + "-ed." "Gullet" comes from Old French "goulet," diminutive of "goule," meaning "throat," ultimately from Latin "gula," also meaning "throat."
