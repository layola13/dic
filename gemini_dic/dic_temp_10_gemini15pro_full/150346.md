paniculate: /ˈpænɪkjʊlət/ /ˈpænɪkjʊˌleɪt/ | adj. | 圆锥花序的；惊慌的 | Arranged in a panicle; resembling a panicle. paniculate: /ˈpænɪkjʊlət/ /ˈpænɪkjʊˌleɪt/ | v. | 使…惊慌 | To cause to panic.  | The loud noise paniculated the horses. 巨大的噪音使马匹惊慌失措。 | The news reports paniculated the investors. 新闻报道使投资者感到恐慌。| 近义词: frighten, scare, alarm, terrify | 反义词: calm, soothe, reassure | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *paniculatus*, from panicula (“tuft, panicle”), diminutive of panus (“ear of millet”).
