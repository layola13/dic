discradle: /dɪsˈkreɪdəl/
verb
剥夺…的舒适；使…不再受保护；使…离开舒适的环境
to remove someone or something from a place of comfort or security
The war discradled many children, forcing them to flee their homes and families.  战争剥夺了许多儿童的舒适，迫使他们逃离家园和家人。
The economic downturn discradled many families from their middle-class lifestyles. 经济衰退使许多家庭失去了中产阶级的生活方式。
近义词: displace, uproot, dislodge
反义词: shelter, protect, nurture
词性: verb
