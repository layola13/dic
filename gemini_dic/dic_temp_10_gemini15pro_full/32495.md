chinked:/tʃɪŋkt/ |adj. | 有裂缝的，有缝隙的 | having chinks or cracks | The chinked armour offered little protection. 有裂缝的盔甲几乎无法提供保护。 | synonyms: cracked, fissured, split | antonyms: sealed, intact, whole | adjective
