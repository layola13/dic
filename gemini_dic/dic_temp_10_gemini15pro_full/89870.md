ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *horologium, from Ancient Greek ὡρολόγιον (hōrológion, “instrument for telling the hour”), from ὥρα (hōra, “hour, time”) + -λόγιον (-lógion, “instrument for”, from λέγω (légō, “to speak, tell”)).
