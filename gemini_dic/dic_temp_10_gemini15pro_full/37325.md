commemorativeness:/kəˌmɛməˈreɪtɪvnəs/ | n. | 纪念性，值得纪念的性质 | The commemorativeness of the event was underscored by the presence of the president.  这个活动的纪念意义因总统的出席而显得格外重要。 | The monument has a certain commemorativeness about it. 这座纪念碑具有一定的纪念意义。 | 近义词：memorability, significance | 反义词：insignificance, unimportance | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From commemorate + -ive + -ness.  Commemorate comes from the Latin commemorare "call to remembrance," from com- "together" + memorare "to remind, mention," related to memor "mindful."
