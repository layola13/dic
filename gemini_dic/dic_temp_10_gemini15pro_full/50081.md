dentately:/ˈdɛn.tɪt.li/
adverb|齿状地；锯齿状地|in a dentate manner; having tooth-like projections.|The leaf margins were dentately lobed. 叶子边缘呈齿状分裂。|
近义词:toothed, serrated, jagged|
反义词:smooth, even|
词性:adverb


dentate:/ˈdɛn.teɪt/
adjective|齿状的；锯齿状的|having a toothed or serrated edge, like the teeth of a saw.|The plant had dentate leaves. 这种植物有锯齿状的叶子。|
近义词:toothed, serrated, jagged|
反义词:smooth, even|
词性:adjective


词源: From Latin *dentatus*, from dens (tooth).
