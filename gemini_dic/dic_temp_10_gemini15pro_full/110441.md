likening:/ˈlaɪkənɪŋ/
n.
比拟; 比较; 
the act of comparing one thing to another.
例句：He drew a likening between the two situations. 他对这两种情况进行了比较。
例句：The likening of teaching to gardening is quite apt. 将教学比作园艺非常贴切。

近义词：comparison, analogy, simile, parallel
反义词：contrast, differentiation

词性: noun
