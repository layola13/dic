dittoes:/ˈdɪtoʊz/| n.|同上，同前，副本|A mark (") used to indicate that something written above should be repeated.|The budget proposed for next year is largely dittoes of last year's, with only minor adjustments.|近义词：copies, duplicates, repeats|反义词: originals, differences|词性:名词

ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/| n. adv.  v.|同上，同前；(用于避免重复前文)；重复，复制|Used to indicate that something already said or written should be repeated.|He echoed the views of his predecessor, saying, "Ditto everything he said about the need for reform."|近义词：same, likewise, identically|反义词: differently, oppositely|词性:名词，副词，动词
