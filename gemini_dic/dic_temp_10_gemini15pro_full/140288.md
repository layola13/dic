oftentimes:/ˈɔːfənˌtaɪmz/ /ˈɑːfənˌtaɪmz/
| adv.|经常，常常|Oftentimes is an adverb that means frequently or many times. It's a more formal and slightly old-fashioned way of saying "often."|Oftentimes, I find myself thinking about the future. 我常常发现自己在思考未来。|近义词：often, frequently, regularly, usually|反义词:rarely, seldom, infrequently
