condo:/ˈkɑːndoʊ/ (US) /ˈkɒndəʊ/ (UK)| n.|公寓；共管公寓|An individually owned apartment unit in a multi-unit building or complex.|例句：They bought a condo in Miami. 他们在迈阿密买了一套公寓。|例句：The condo has a beautiful view of the ocean. 这套公寓可以欣赏到美丽的海景。|近义词：apartment, flat|反义词:house|noun
