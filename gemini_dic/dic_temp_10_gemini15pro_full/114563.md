malax:/məˈlæks/| v. |揉捏；软化；按摩 |To soften or knead (something) by rubbing or working with the hands.|The baker malaxed the dough for several minutes. 面包师揉捏面团几分钟。|近义词:knead, massage, work, manipulate|反义词:harden, solidify|词性:动词
Etymology: From French *malaxer*, from Latin *malaxare* "to soften by kneading," from Greek *malakizein* from *malakos* "soft."
