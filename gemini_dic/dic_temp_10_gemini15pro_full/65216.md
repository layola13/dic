etiolin: /ˈiːtiəʊlɪn/ | n. |叶绿素醇; 乙醇素|A colorless, crystalline alcohol, C20H40O, obtained by hydrolysis of chlorophyll.  It is a constituent of chlorophyll. | Chlorophyll molecules contain etiolin. 叶绿素分子含有叶绿素醇。| The etiolin was extracted from the plant. 从植物中提取了叶绿素醇。| synonyms: phytol | antonyms: N/A | noun
