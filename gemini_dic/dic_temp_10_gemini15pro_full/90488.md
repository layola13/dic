huchen:/ˈhuːkən/| n. |胡钦鱼，多瑙河鲑鱼|A large salmonid fish (Hucho hucho) native to the Danube River basin.  It is the largest permanent freshwater salmonid in the world.|The huchen is a prized game fish. 胡钦鱼是一种珍贵的游钓鱼。|The huchen can grow to over five feet long. 胡钦鱼可以长到五英尺多长。|Synonyms: Danube salmon, hucho|Antonyms: N/A|noun
