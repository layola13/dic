gangsterisms:/ˈɡæŋstərɪzəmz/
|n.|黑帮行径，黑帮用语|The gangsterisms in the movie were exaggerated for dramatic effect.  这部电影中的黑帮行径为了戏剧效果而被夸大了。|
|English explanation:  The characteristic practices and language of gangsters, often involving violence, intimidation, and illegal activities.  It can refer to the specific slang or jargon used by gangsters, or to their behavior and methods in general.|
|Example: The use of gangsterisms in his speech made him sound tough. 他讲话中使用黑帮用语让他听起来很强硬。|
|Synonyms: thuggery, racketeering, criminal activity, underworld slang|
|Antonyms: lawfulness, lawful behavior, legal activities|
|Word type: noun|
