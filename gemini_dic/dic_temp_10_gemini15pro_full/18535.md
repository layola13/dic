beneficent:/bɪˈnɛfɪs(ə)nt/
adjective
仁慈的；慈善的；有益的
Doing or producing good, especially performing acts of kindness or charity.
The beneficent ruler donated a large sum of money to the hospital.  仁慈的统治者向医院捐赠了大笔款项。
Her beneficent influence was felt by everyone in the community.  她慈祥的影响力被社区里的每个人都感受到了。
近义词: kind, benevolent, charitable, generous, philanthropic
反义词: malevolent, malicious, harmful, detrimental
词性: adjective

beneficence:/bɪˈnɛfɪs(ə)ns/
noun
仁慈；善行；恩惠
The quality or state of being beneficent; kindness, generosity.
His beneficence extended to all creatures, great and small.  他的仁慈惠及所有生物，无论大小。
The scholarship fund was a testament to her beneficence.  奖学金基金证明了她的仁慈。
近义词: kindness, generosity, charity, benevolence, philanthropy
反义词: malevolence, malice, cruelty
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *beneficens*, present participle of *benefacere* "to do good," from *bene* "well" + *facere* "to do."
