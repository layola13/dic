modals:/ˈmoʊdəlz/ (US), /ˈməʊdəlz/ (UK)| n.|情态动词|Auxiliary verbs that express modality, such as possibility, necessity, permission, or ability.|Modals like "can," "could," "may," "might," "must," "shall," "should," "will," and "would" are used to express different levels of certainty, obligation, or permission. |She can speak French fluently. 她能流利地说法语。|Synonyms: auxiliary verbs, helping verbs| Antonyms: main verbs|noun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He can play the piano. 他会弹钢琴。
She must leave now. 她现在必须离开。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
They should be studying now. 他们现在应该在学习。
He may be arriving soon. 他可能很快就会到。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
She must have finished the report. 她一定已经完成了报告。
He might have left already. 他可能已经离开了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They should have been working on this project. 他们应该一直在做这个项目。
She may have been sleeping. 她可能一直在睡觉。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He could swim when he was young. 他小时候会游泳。
She had to leave early. 她不得不早点离开。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
They should have been studying at that time. 他们当时应该在学习。
He might have been arriving then. 他当时可能正在到达。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
She must have finished the report before I arrived. 在我到达之前，她一定已经完成了报告。
He might have left already by then. 到那时他可能已经离开了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They should have been working on this project for weeks. 他们应该已经在这个项目上工作了好几个星期了。
She may have been sleeping for hours. 她可能已经睡了好几个小时了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will be able to drive next year. 明年他就能开车了。
She will have to work late tomorrow. 她明天不得不工作到很晚。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
They should be studying at this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候他们应该在学习。
He may be arriving at that time. 他可能在那个时候到达。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
She will have finished the report by next week. 到下周她将完成报告。
He might have left by then. 到那时他可能已经离开了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
They should have been working on this project for a year by then. 到那时，他们将已经在这个项目上工作一年了。
She may have been sleeping for 10 hours by then. 到那时她可能已经睡了10个小时了。



虚拟语气:
If I could fly, I would travel the world. 如果我会飞，我会环游世界。
I wish I could speak Spanish. 我希望我会说西班牙语。


被动语态:
The rules must be followed. 必须遵守规则。
The work should be completed by tomorrow. 这项工作应该在明天之前完成。


疑问句:
Can you help me? 你能帮我吗？
Should we leave now? 我们现在应该离开吗？


否定句:
He cannot swim. 他不会游泳。
She must not go. 她不能去。
