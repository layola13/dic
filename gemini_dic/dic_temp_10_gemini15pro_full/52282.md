dichotomous:/daɪˈkɑːtəməs/| adj.|二分的，二叉的|Divided or dividing into two sharply distinguished parts or classifications.|The survey presented a dichotomous choice: agree or disagree.  这项调查提出了一个二分法的选择：同意或不同意。|例句：The philosopher explored the dichotomous nature of good and evil. 这位哲学家探讨了善与恶的二分性质。|近义词：binary, dual, twofold|反义词：multiple, multifaceted|词性:形容词

dichotomous:/daɪˈkɑːtəməs/| adj.|对立的|Mutually exclusive or completely opposite.|The politician presented a dichotomous view of the issue, ignoring the nuances. 这位政治家提出了对这个问题的二分观点，忽略了细微差别。|例句：The two approaches to the problem seemed strangely dichotomous.  解决这个问题的两种方法看起来奇怪地对立。|近义词：opposite, contrasting, conflicting|反义词：similar, complementary|词性:形容词
