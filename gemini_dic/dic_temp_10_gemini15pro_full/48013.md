decipherments:/dɪˈsaɪfərmənts/| n. |解密，破译|The act or process of deciphering something, or the product of this process.  The decipherments of the ancient scrolls revealed a lost civilization.  这些古代卷轴的破译揭示了一个失落的文明。|近义词：interpretations, translations, solutions, decodings|反义词: encryptions, codifications| noun
