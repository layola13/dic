omnivorousness:/ˌɒmˈnɪvərəsnəs/ | n. |杂食性；无所不读 |The state or quality of being omnivorous; eating or absorbing everything. |His omnivorousness extends beyond food; he devours books, movies, and information of all kinds. 他不仅什么都吃，而且什么都学；他如饥似渴地阅读书籍、观看电影，吸收各种信息。

|近义词：versatility, all-inclusiveness, eclecticism |反义词: pickiness, selectivity, fastidiousness |词性: noun

omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. |杂食的；无所不读的 |Eating both plants and animals; taking in or using everything. |Pigs are omnivorous animals. 猪是杂食动物。 He is an omnivorous reader, consuming books of all genres. 他是一个什么书都读的读者，各种类型的书都看。| 近义词：all-devouring, indiscriminate |反义词: herbivorous, carnivorous |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *omnivorus* (all-devouring), from *omnis* (all) + *vorare* (to devour). The suffix *-ness* transforms the adjective into a noun denoting the state or quality.
