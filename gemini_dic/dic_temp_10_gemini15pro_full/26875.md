cajuputene:/ˌkædʒəˈpjuːtiːn/ | n. |白千层烯|A bicyclic sesquiterpene hydrocarbon, C15H24, found in the oil of cajuput. It is a component of the essential oil derived from certain species of Melaleuca trees, notably *Melaleuca cajuputi*. |Cajuputene is one of the main components responsible for the characteristic aroma of cajuput oil. 白千层烯是造成白千层油特有香味的主要成分之一。| The researchers isolated cajuputene from the essential oil. 研究人员从精油中分离出了白千层烯。 |Synonyms:  No exact synonyms, as it is a specific chemical compound. However, related terms include sesquiterpene, terpene, hydrocarbon. | Antonyms: N/A | noun
