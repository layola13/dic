monogenic:/ˌmɒnəˈdʒɛnɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj. |单基因的|Controlled by or involving a single gene.|Cystic fibrosis is a monogenic disorder. 囊性纤维化是一种单基因疾病。|Huntington's disease is a well-known example of a monogenic condition. 亨廷顿舞蹈症是单基因疾病的一个著名例子。|Synonyms: single-gene, Mendelian|Antonyms: polygenic|adjective
