misarticulate: /ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ /ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjəleɪt/
verb
错误地发音；口齿不清
to pronounce incorrectly; to articulate poorly
He misarticulated the word "February," saying "Febuary" instead. 他把“February”这个词发错了音，说成了“Febuary”。
The speaker occasionally misarticulated, but the audience could still understand the message. 演讲者偶尔会发错音，但观众仍然能理解他的意思。
近义词: slur, mumble, mispronounce
反义词: articulate, enunciate
词性: verb
