coverup:/ˈkʌvərʌp/| n.|掩盖；隐瞒；遮掩物|An attempt to prevent the public from discovering something dishonest or embarrassing.|The government tried to cover up the scandal. 政府试图掩盖这桩丑闻。|近义词：concealment, whitewash, suppression|反义词: revelation, exposure|词性:名词

cover-up:/ˈkʌvər ʌp/| adj.|掩盖的；隐瞒的|Designed to hide a mistake or wrongdoing.|The report was a cover-up for the company's illegal activities. 这份报告是为了掩盖公司的非法活动。|近义词：concealing, deceptive|反义词: revealing, honest|词性:形容词
