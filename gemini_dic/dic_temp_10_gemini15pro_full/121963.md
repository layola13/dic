Mississippi:/ˌmɪsɪˈsɪpi/ | n. | 密西西比河；密西西比州 |  A state in the southern United States and a major river flowing through it.  It comes from the Ojibwe word "misi-ziibi" meaning "Great River". |  Example 1:  We took a steamboat down the Mississippi.  我们乘汽船顺密西西比河而下。

Example 2:  Jackson is the capital of Mississippi.  杰克逊是密西西比州的首府。 | Synonym: (for the river) Big Muddy,  (for the state) Magnolia State | Antonym:  N/A | proper noun
