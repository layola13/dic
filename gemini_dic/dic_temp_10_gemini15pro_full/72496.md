fluorescein:/ˌflʊərəˈsiːɪn/ /ˌflɔːrəˈsiːɪn/ | n. | 荧光素 | A bright yellow, crystalline, water-soluble dye that becomes intensely fluorescent in alkaline solution. It is used as a diagnostic aid and in microscopy. |  Example 1: Fluorescein is used in angiography to visualize blood vessels.  荧光素用于血管造影术以观察血管。

Example 2: The ophthalmologist used fluorescein drops to examine the patient's cornea. 眼科医生使用荧光素滴剂检查患者的角膜。 | 近义词: dye, tracer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

fluoresce:/flʊəˈrɛs/ /flɔːˈrɛs/ | v. | 发荧光 | To emit light by fluorescence. | Example 1: The mineral fluoresced under ultraviolet light.  这种矿物在紫外线下发出荧光。

Example 2: Certain bacteria fluoresce green under specific conditions.  某些细菌在特定条件下发出绿色荧光。 | 近义词: glow, shimmer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb
