annihilate:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪt/ | v. |歼灭，毁灭，彻底摧毁 |To destroy utterly; obliterate. To defeat utterly; vanquish.| The enemy army was annihilated in the battle. 敌军在战斗中被歼灭。| 近义词:destroy, obliterate, exterminate, demolish | 反义词:create, build, establish | 词性:verb
