cyathophylloid:/ˌsaɪəθoʊˈfɪlɔɪd/
adjective
杯状珊瑚的；似杯状珊瑚的
Resembling or characteristic of the extinct order of corals known as Cyathophyllida, typically having a horn-shaped or cylindrical skeleton with radial internal plates.  Characterized by a horn or cone shape with prominent radial septa.
The fossil exhibited a distinct cyathophylloid structure, indicating its coral origins. 这块化石展现出独特的杯状珊瑚结构，表明了它的珊瑚起源。
The researchers discovered a new species of cyathophylloid coral in the ancient reef deposits. 研究人员在古代礁石沉积物中发现了一种新的杯状珊瑚物种。
Synonym:  cup coral-like (less formal)  coralliform
Antonym:  None (highly specific scientific term, lacks a direct opposite)
word type: adjective
