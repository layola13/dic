mightily: /ˈmaɪtɪli/ | adv. | 强烈地，非常地，大大地 | With great strength or force; extremely. | He struggled mightily against the current. 他奋力与水流搏斗。|  The wind blew mightily all night. 整夜狂风大作。 | 近义词: powerfully, strongly, extremely, vigorously, intensely | 反义词: weakly, feebly, slightly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *myȝtily, from Old English *mihtiglīċe (“mightily”), equivalent to mighty +‎ -ly.
