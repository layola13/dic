importancy: /ɪmˈpɔːtənsi/ | n. | 重要性，重要，重大 |The state or quality of being important; significance. |The importancy of regular exercise cannot be overstated.  规律运动的重要性怎么强调都不为过。| 近义词: importance, significance, relevance, consequence | 反义词: insignificance, unimportance, triviality | 词性: noun

词源:  Late 16th century: from important + -cy.  源自 important（重要的） + -cy（名词后缀）。
