airplay:/ˈɛərpleɪ/| n.|无线播放，隔空播放|The ability to wirelessly stream audio or video content from a device (like an iPhone, iPad, or Mac) to another device (like an Apple TV or AirPlay 2-compatible speaker).  It allows you to share music, photos, videos, and more from your Apple devices to your TV or speakers.|例句：I used AirPlay to stream music from my phone to the speakers. 我用AirPlay把手机里的音乐传到扬声器上。

例句：The presentation was easily shared on the big screen via AirPlay.  通过 AirPlay 很容易在大屏幕上共享演示文稿。|近义词：streaming, screen mirroring, casting|反义词:download, local playback|词性:noun
