magnesia:/mæɡˈniːʒə/ /mæɡˈniːziə/
n.
氧化镁; 轻质氧化镁; 菱镁矿
A white, tasteless powder used as an antacid and laxative.  It is also used in industry as a refractory material.
Milk of magnesia is often used to treat indigestion.  牛奶氧化镁常用于治疗消化不良。

近义词: magnesium oxide, periclase (for the mineral form)
反义词: None (as it's a specific chemical compound)
词性: noun


magnesia:/mæɡˈniːʒə/ /mæɡˈniːziə/
n.
镁土; 轻质氧化镁; 菱镁矿
A white, tasteless powder used as an antacid and laxative.  It is also used in industry as a refractory material.
Magnesia bricks are used in high-temperature furnaces. 镁砖用于高温炉。

近义词: magnesium oxide, periclase (for the mineral form)
反义词: None (as it's a specific chemical compound)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *magnesia (terra), meaning "magnesian earth," from Magnesia, a region of ancient Thessaly in Greece, where the mineral was found.  Ultimately from Greek Μαγνησία (Magnēsía).
