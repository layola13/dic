novelty:/ˈnɒvəlti/ (UK) /ˈnɑːvəlti/ (US)| n.|新奇事物；新奇感；新颖小巧而廉价的物品|Something new and unusual, or the quality of being new and unusual.  A small, cheap, unusual object that is often given as a present or bought as a souvenir.|
The novelty of the experience soon wore off.  这种体验的新鲜感很快就消失了。

The shop sells all sorts of novelties - masks, funny hats, things like that.  这家商店出售各种新奇的小玩意儿——面具、滑稽的帽子等等。

近义词:innovation, originality, freshness, trinket, knick-knack|
反义词:familiarity, staleness, banality|
词性:noun
