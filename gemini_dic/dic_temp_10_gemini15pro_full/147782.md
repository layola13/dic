overpromptness:/ˈoʊvərˈprɑːmptnəs/| n.| 过于迅速；过分及时|The overpromptness of his reply suggested he had been expecting the question. 他回答得过于迅速，表明他一直在期待这个问题。|例句：Her overpromptness in offering help was sometimes interpreted as being intrusive. 她提供帮助过于迅速，有时被理解为多管闲事。

例句：His overpromptness to agree made her suspicious. 他答应得太快了，让她起了疑心。


近义词：prematureness, hastiness, alacrity|反义词: tardiness, delay, procrastination|词性:noun
