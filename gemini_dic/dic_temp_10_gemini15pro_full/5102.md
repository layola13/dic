allective:/əˈlɛktɪv/
adjective
吸引的，有吸引力的
Having the power to attract or allure.
Her allective smile drew everyone's attention. 她那迷人的微笑吸引了所有人的注意。

近义词:attractive, alluring, captivating, magnetic
反义词:repulsive, repellent, off-putting
词性:adjective


词源:
From Latin *allectivus, from allectus, past participle of allicere "to allure," from ad- "to" + lacere "to entice."
