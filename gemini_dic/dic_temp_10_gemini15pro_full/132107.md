noncrisis:/ˌnɒnˈkraɪsɪs/ |n.| 非危机状态，正常状态 |A situation that is not a crisis; a state of normalcy. |例句：The company assured investors that it was a noncrisis and operations were continuing as normal. 公司向投资者保证，这并非危机，运营一切正常。

例句：Despite the initial panic, the situation turned out to be a noncrisis. 尽管最初出现了恐慌，但情况最终证明并非危机。|近义词：normality, stability, calm, peace |反义词：crisis, emergency, catastrophe| noun
