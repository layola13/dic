decreer:/dɪˈkriː/
verb
颁布；裁定；规定
To officially order or decide something.
The judge decreed that the property should be sold. 法官裁定该房产应予出售。

The government decreed a state of emergency. 政府颁布了紧急状态。

synonym: order, rule, command, dictate, pronounce, ordain
antonym: repeal, revoke, countermand, rescind
词性: 动词
