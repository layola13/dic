consubstantialism:/ˌkɒnsəbˈstænʃəlɪzəm/
n.
同质说，同体说
The doctrine that the Son is of the same substance as the Father.
The Nicene Creed affirms the consubstantialism of the Father and the Son. 尼西亚信经确认了圣父和圣子同体。
近义词：coessentiality, homoousianism
反义词：heterousianism
词性：noun

consubstantial:/ˌkɒnsəbˈstænʃəl/
adj.
同体的，同质的
Having the same substance or essence.
The council declared that Christ was consubstantial with the Father.  会议宣布基督与圣父同体。
近义词：coessential, homoousian
反义词：heterousian
词性：adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *consubstantialis, from con- "together" + substantialis "of substance".  Ultimately from Latin substantia "substance, essence".
