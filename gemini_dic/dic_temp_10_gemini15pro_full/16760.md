barycentric:/ˌbɛəriˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 重心的；质心的 | Relating to or using a system of coordinates whose origin is at the center of mass of a system of particles, weighted according to their masses. | The barycentric coordinates of a point inside a triangle are proportional to the areas of the three triangles formed by the point and the vertices of the original triangle. 三角形内一点的重心坐标与该点和原始三角形顶点形成的三个三角形的面积成正比。| The spacecraft's trajectory was calculated using barycentric coordinates.  航天器的轨迹是用重心坐标计算的。| 近义词: center-of-mass, centroidal | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

barycenter:/ˈbɛəriˌsɛntər/ | n. | 重心；质心 | The center of mass of two or more bodies that orbit one another and are considered as one system. | The barycenter of the Earth-Moon system lies within the Earth, about 4,671 km from the Earth's center. 地月系统的质心位于地球内部，距离地球中心约4671公里。|  The two stars orbit around their common barycenter. 这两颗恒星围绕它们的共同质心运行。| 近义词: center of mass, centroid | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *barys* (heavy) + *kentron* (center).
