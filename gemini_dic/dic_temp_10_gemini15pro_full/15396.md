backwashing:/ˈbækˌwɒʃɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbækˌwɒʃɪŋ/ (UK)| n. | 反冲洗，逆洗 | The process of reversing the flow of water through a filter medium to remove trapped solids. |  The pool filter requires regular backwashing to maintain its efficiency.  游泳池过滤器需要定期反冲洗以保持其效率。 |  backflushing, rinsing | filtering | noun
