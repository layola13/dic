harnessless:/ˈhɑːrnɪslɪs/| adj.|无安全带的，无挽具的|Without a harness. A harness is a set of straps and fastenings used to control or secure something, especially a horse or other animal pulling a vehicle, or a person climbing or working at a height.  Harnessless, therefore, means lacking this equipment.|The harnessless climber scaled the cliff face.  那位没有系安全带的攀岩者攀登了悬崖峭壁。|近义词：unharnessed, unrestrained|反义词：harnessed, restrained|词性:形容词
