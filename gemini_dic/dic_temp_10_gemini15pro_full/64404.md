ergaster:/ərˈɡæstər/ | n. |匠人| An extinct hominin species (Homo ergaster) that lived in eastern and southern Africa during the early Pleistocene.  It is considered by some to be an early form of Homo erectus. |  Homo ergaster used tools and may have been the first hominin to control fire. 匠人使用工具，可能是第一个控制火的原始人。| synonym: Homo erectus (sometimes), early human, hominin | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a species, could consider "modern human" as a contrasting term) | noun
