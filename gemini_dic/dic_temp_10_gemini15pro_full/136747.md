nonsensuousness:/ˌnɒnsenˈsjuːəsnəs/ /ˌnɑːnsenˈsuːəsnəs/
n.
无感觉；非感官性；超感官性
The state of being nonsensuous; lack of appeal to the senses.
His art explored a realm of pure nonsensuousness, devoid of any tactile or visual elements.  他的艺术探索了一个纯粹的非感官领域，没有任何触觉或视觉元素。
The philosopher discussed the nonsensuousness of pure thought. 这位哲学家讨论了纯粹思想的非感官性。
近义词: abstractness, immateriality, incorporeality
反义词: sensuousness, sensuality
词性: noun


Etymology:
From non- + sensuous + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," sensuous means "relating to or affecting the senses," and -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
