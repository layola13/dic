healthfully:/ˈhɛlθfəli/| adv.|健康地，有益健康地|In a way that promotes good health.  It describes actions or habits that contribute to physical or mental well-being.|She eats healthfully and exercises regularly. 她健康饮食并且规律锻炼。|近义词：healthily, wholesomely|反义词: unhealthily, poorly|词性:副词
Etymology:  From "health" + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Health" ultimately derives from Old English "hǣlth," meaning "wholeness, a state of being whole, sound."
