overpleased: /ˌoʊvərˈpliːzd/ | adj. | 过于高兴的，过分满意的 | Excessively pleased; too delighted.  It implies a level of satisfaction that may be excessive or even inappropriate given the circumstances.  It can sometimes carry a slightly negative connotation, suggesting that the person's pleasure is exaggerated or insincere. | She was overpleased with her new car, bragging about it to everyone. 她对她的新车过于满意，逢人就夸。 |  He seemed overpleased with himself after winning the competition, which annoyed some of the other contestants.  他在赢得比赛后似乎对自己过分满意，这让其他一些参赛者很恼火。 | 近义词: delighted, overjoyed, thrilled, ecstatic | 反义词: displeased, dissatisfied, unhappy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "pleased" (adjective from the verb "please").
