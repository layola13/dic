encourager:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒər/ | n. | 鼓励者，鼓舞人心的人 |  Someone who inspires others with courage, spirit, or confidence. |  Her coach was her biggest encourager, always believing in her potential.  她的教练是她最大的鼓励者，总是相信她的潜力。 | supporter, motivator, inspirer |  discourager, critic, detractor | noun

encourage:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/ | vt. | 鼓励，激励；促进，助长 | To inspire with courage, spirit, or confidence; to stimulate. |  He encouraged her to apply for the job. 他鼓励她申请这份工作。| support, motivate, inspire, promote, foster | discourage, hinder, obstruct | verb
