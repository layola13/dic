overambition:/ˌoʊvəræmˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 过度野心，过高的抱负 | Excessive ambition; the condition of being excessively ambitious. | His overambition ultimately led to his downfall. 他的过度野心最终导致了他的垮台。 |  Overreaching, Presumption, Pretension |  Modesty, Humility | noun

Etymology:
From over- + ambition, from Middle French ambition, from Latin ambitiō (“a going around, a canvassing for office”), from ambīre (“to go around”).
