coacher:/ˈkoʊtʃər/| n.|教练; 指导者; 长途公共汽车; 四轮大马车 |A person who trains or instructs people or teams in sports or other activities; a long-distance bus; a horse-drawn carriage.|例句：The coacher encouraged his team to never give up. 教练鼓励他的团队永不放弃。

例句：She hired a coacher to help her improve her presentation skills. 她聘请了一位教练来帮助她提高演讲技巧。|近义词：instructor, trainer, tutor, mentor, teacher|反义词：student, pupil, trainee|词性:noun
coach:/koʊtʃ/|n. vt. vi. |教练;  旅客车厢;  辅导;  训练;  指导|A person who trains athletes or other performers; a passenger vehicle; to train or instruct someone|词源: From the Hungarian town of Kocs, where fast stagecoaches originated.|
