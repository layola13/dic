fistulae:/ˈfɪstjʊliː/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊleɪ/ (UK)| n. |瘘管；瘘|An abnormal passage or tube-like opening between two organs or between an organ and the surface of the body.|The patient developed fistulae between the bowel and the bladder. 患者的肠和膀胱之间形成了瘘管。|Synonyms: fistula, tract, channel|Antonyms: closure|Noun
