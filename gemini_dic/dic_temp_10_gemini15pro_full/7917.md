antepaschal:/ˌæntiˈpæskəl/| adj.|复活节前的，逾越节前的|Occurring or done before Easter or Passover.|例句：The antepaschal fast is a period of preparation for Easter. 复活节前的斋戒是为复活节做准备的时期。

例句：Antepaschal liturgies often focus on themes of repentance and renewal. 复活节前的礼拜仪式通常侧重于忏悔和更新的主题。|近义词：pre-Easter, pre-Paschal|反义词：post-Paschal, post-Easter|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *antepaschalis*, from ante- "before" + paschalis "relating to Easter," from Late Latin *pascha* "Easter," from Greek *pascha* "Passover," from Aramaic *pasḥā*, from Hebrew *pesaḥ*.
