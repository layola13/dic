parriers:/ˈpæriərz/| n.|格挡者; 挡开者|People skilled at deflecting or warding off attacks, especially in sword fighting or other combat.  Those who parry blows.|The parriers defended themselves bravely against the onslaught.  这些格挡者勇敢地抵御猛烈的攻击。|近义词：defenders, blockers, protectors|反义词:attackers, aggressors|noun

Etymology:
From Middle French *parrieur*, from Old French *parier* "to ward off, protect," from *parer* "to prepare, adorn, protect," from Latin *parāre* "to prepare."
