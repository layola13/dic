benign:/bɪˈnaɪn/
adjective
良性的; 温和的; 和蔼的;
(of a disease) not serious or dangerous.  Kind and gentle.
例句：He had a benign tumour. 他患有良性肿瘤。

例句：a benign smile  和蔼的微笑
近义词：kind, gentle, harmless, mild, favorable
反义词：malignant, harmful, hostile, severe, unfavorable
词性: adjective

benign:/bɪˈnaɪn/
adjective
(气候或环境)宜人的，良好的
gentle and favorable; mild.
例句：a benign climate  宜人的气候

例句：The industry has benefited from benign economic conditions. 该行业受益于良好的经济环境。
近义词：favorable, mild, gentle, pleasant
反义词：harsh, unfavorable, severe, unpleasant
词性: adjective
