outgrosses:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊsɪz/
verb
超过…的总收入
surpasses in gross revenue or earnings.
This year's box office outgrosses last year's by a significant margin. 今年的票房收入大大超过了去年。
The new movie outgrosses all previous releases in the franchise.  这部新电影的票房收入超过了该系列之前的所有发行影片。
synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, outstrips, overtakes
antonyms: underperforms, lags behind
词性:动词
