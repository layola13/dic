orthognathic:/ˌɔːrθəˈɡnæθɪk/
adjective
正頜的，正顎的
Relating to or characterized by the condition of having a normal jaw relationship.  Having a normal occlusion.
例句：She underwent orthognathic surgery to correct her overbite. 她接受了正颌手术来矫正她的覆咬合。

例句：Orthognathic treatment can improve both function and aesthetics. 正颌治疗可以改善功能和美观。

近义词：normal occlusion
反义词：dysgnathic, malocclusion
词性:adjective

词源:
ortho- (Greek: straight, correct) + gnathic (Greek: relating to the jaw)
