hurdy-gurdy:/ˈhɜːrdi ˈɡɜːrdi/ /ˈhɝːdi ˈɡɝːdi/| n. |手摇风琴，轮擦提琴|A stringed musical instrument played by turning a crank that rubs against the strings, producing a droning sound. It often has keys that change the pitch of some of the strings.|The hurdy-gurdy player entertained the crowd with lively folk tunes. 手摇风琴演奏者用活泼的民歌娱乐了人群。|Synonyms: wheel fiddle, organistrum|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific instrument) |noun
