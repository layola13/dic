hogpen:/ˈhɒɡpɛn/ (US), /ˈhɒɡpɛn/ (UK)| n. |猪圈；肮脏的地方|A pen or enclosure for hogs.  Also used figuratively to describe a dirty or messy place.|The farmer kept his pigs in a hogpen behind the barn.|农场主把他的猪养在谷仓后面的猪圈里。|Synonyms: pigpen, sty, pigsty|Antonyms: palace, mansion|noun
