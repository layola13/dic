outcries:/ˈaʊtˌkraɪz/
noun
强烈抗议; 呐喊; 喊叫声
A strong expression of public disapproval or protest.
例句：The new tax prompted outcries from small businesses. 这项新税引发了小企业的强烈抗议。

例句：There were outcries of dismay when the news was announced.  消息宣布后，一片惊愕的喊叫声。

近义词：protests, objections, complaints, clamor, uproar
反义词：praise, approval, acceptance
词性:n.
