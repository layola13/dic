marxists:/ˈmɑːksɪsts/| n.|马克思主义者|Followers or advocates of Marxism, a socio-political theory developed by Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels.|Many marxists believe in the abolition of private property. 许多马克思主义者相信废除私有财产。|近义词：communists, socialists|反义词:capitalists|Noun

marxist:/ˈmɑːksɪst/| adj.|马克思主义的|Relating to or based on the theories of Karl Marx.|The party adopted a marxist ideology. 该党采取了马克思主义的意识形态。|近义词：communist, socialist|反义词:capitalist|Adjective
