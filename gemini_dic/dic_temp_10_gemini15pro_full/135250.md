nonpantheistical:/ˌnɒnpænθiˈɪstɪkəl/
adjective
非泛神论的
Not pantheistic; characterized by the belief that God is distinct from the universe.
The philosopher argued for a nonpantheistical view of the divine, asserting God's transcendence over creation.  哲学家论证了一种非泛神论的神圣观，声称上帝超越了创造物。
Her nonpantheistical beliefs led her to view nature as a beautiful creation, separate from the divine. 她的非泛神论信仰使她将自然视为一种美丽的创造物，与神圣分离。
近义词:non-pantheistic,  transcendentalist (in some contexts)
反义词:pantheistic
词性:adjective
