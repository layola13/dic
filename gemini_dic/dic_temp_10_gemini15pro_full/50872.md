desensitization:/diːˌsɛnsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
脱敏;脱敏作用;减感作用
The process of making someone less sensitive to something, especially by repeated exposure.
Repeated exposure to violence in movies can lead to desensitization.  反复接触电影中的暴力会导致脱敏。
近义词:habituation, acclimation, adaptation
反义词:sensitization
词性:名词

desensitizations:/diːˌsɛnsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
脱敏;脱敏作用;减感作用(desensitization的复数形式)
Multiple instances of the process of making someone less sensitive to something.
The study examined various desensitizations to different allergens.  这项研究检测了对不同过敏原的各种脱敏作用。
近义词:habituations, acclimations, adaptations
反义词:sensitizations
词性:名词


词源:  From de- + sensitize + -ation.  "De-" prefix meaning to reverse or remove, "sensitize" meaning to make sensitive, and "-ation" a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
