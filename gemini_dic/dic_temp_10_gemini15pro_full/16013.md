banalized:/bəˈnəlaɪzd/
|adj.|平庸的，陈腐的，老套的|Deprived of originality or freshness; made commonplace; trite.  Lacking originality, freshness, or novelty; hackneyed.|The plot of the movie was banalized by clichés and predictable twists. 这部电影的情节因为陈词滥调和可预测的转折而变得平庸。|Synonyms: trite, hackneyed, clichéd, commonplace, stale, stereotyped|Antonyms: original, fresh, novel, innovative, unique|Word type: Adjective
