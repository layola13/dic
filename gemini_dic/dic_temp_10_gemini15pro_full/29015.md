cartographers:/kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfərz/
n.
制图师，制图员
A person who makes maps.
例句：Early cartographers often relied on celestial navigation to determine locations. 早期的制图师经常依靠天文导航来确定位置。

例句：Modern cartographers use sophisticated software and satellite imagery to create highly accurate maps.  现代制图师使用先进的软件和卫星图像来创建高度精确的地图。

近义词：mapmakers, chartmakers
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (plural)
