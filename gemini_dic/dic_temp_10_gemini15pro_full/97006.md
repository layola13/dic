infare:/ˈɪnˌfeə(r)/| n.|优惠票价；新航线开通时的特价机票；庆祝新成员加入家庭或社区的聚会|A reduced fare, especially one offered by an airline to celebrate the inauguration of a new route, or a party to celebrate the arrival of a new member of a family or community.|The airline offered a special infare to promote its new route to Hawaii. 航空公司提供了一种特殊的优惠票价来推广其新的夏威夷航线。|
The family held an infare to welcome the new bride.  这个家庭举行了一个聚会来欢迎新媳妇。|synonyms: discount, special offer, celebration, welcome party|antonyms: surcharge, premium, farewell party|noun
