detest:/dɪˈtest/ | vt. | 憎恶，厌恶 | To feel intense dislike or hatred for something or someone. | She detests hypocrisy. 她厌恶虚伪。|  I detest the smell of cigarettes. 我讨厌香烟的味道。 | 近义词：loathe, abhor, despise, hate | 反义词：adore, love, cherish | verb

词源: From Latin detestari "to curse while calling a god to witness; to execrate, denounce, abominate," from de- "down, completely" (see de-) + testari "to bear witness, attest" (see testament).
