autotypy:/ɔːˈtɒtɪpi/ | n. | 自动书写；自动打字 | Automatic writing; the supposed production of written matter without conscious direction, often attributed to spirits or subconscious forces. |  She claimed the poem was a result of autotypy, not her own conscious creation. 她声称这首诗是自动书写的结果，而不是她自己有意识的创作。

| Example: The medium claimed to be able to communicate with the deceased through autotypy.  灵媒声称能够通过自动书写与死者沟通。 | Synonym: automatic writing | Antonym: conscious writing | noun
