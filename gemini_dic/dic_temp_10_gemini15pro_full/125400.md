multinucleate:/ˌmʌltiˈnjuːkliɪt/ /ˌmʌltiˈnuːkliɪt/| adj.|多核的|Having multiple nuclei within a single cell.|例句：Multinucleate cells are common in fungi.  多核细胞在真菌中很常见。

例句：Osteoclasts are multinucleate cells that break down bone tissue.  破骨细胞是分解骨组织的多核细胞。|近义词：polynuclear, polynucleated|反义词：mononuclear, uninucleate|词性:形容词

multinucleated:/ˌmʌltiˈnjuːkliˌeɪtɪd/ /ˌmʌltiˈnuːkliˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|多核的|Having multiple nuclei within a single cell.|例句：Multinucleated muscle fibers are formed by the fusion of multiple cells. 多核肌纤维是由多个细胞融合形成的。

例句：Some protists are multinucleated. 一些原生生物是多核的。|近义词：polynuclear, polynucleated|反义词：mononuclear, uninucleate|词性:形容词
