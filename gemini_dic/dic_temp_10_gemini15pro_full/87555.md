heteronomous: /ˌhɛtərˈɑːnəməs/ | adj. | 受外部法则支配的；由外因决定的 | Subject to external laws or principles; governed by an outside force or authority. |  Children's morality is initially heteronomous, dictated by rules set by adults.  孩子们最初的道德是异律的，由成人制定的规则支配。

A heteronomous economy is dependent on external markets. 一个异律经济依赖于外部市场。 | 近义词: subject, dependent, subordinate | 反义词: autonomous, independent, self-governing | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *heteros* ("other") + *nomos* ("law").
