hymn:/hɪm/
n.
赞美诗，圣歌
A religious song or poem of praise, typically to God.
We sang a hymn at the beginning of the service. 我们在礼拜开始时唱了一首赞美诗。
The congregation joined in singing the hymn.  会众一起唱起了赞美诗。
synonyms: psalm, anthem, carol, chant
antonyms: dirge, elegy
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ymne*, from Late Latin *hymnus*, from Greek *hymnos* "song of praise," originally "festive song, ode," related to *hymen* "wedding song, bridal song"
