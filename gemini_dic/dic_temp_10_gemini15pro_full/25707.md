bunnier:/ˈbʌniər/ | adj. | 更像兔子的；更幸运的 | More resembling a rabbit; more fortunate (especially in gambling or sport) |  He looked bunnier with his two front teeth missing. 他掉了两颗门牙，看起来更像兔子了。| Synonym: luckier, more rabbit-like | Antonym: less lucky, less rabbit-like | adjective

bunnier:/ˈbʌniər/ | n. | 比兔子更像兔子的东西；比别人更幸运的人 | Something that is more like a rabbit than a rabbit; someone who is luckier than others |  He's a real bunnier at the poker table. 他在扑克牌桌上真是个幸运儿。| Synonym: lucky charm, fortunate person | Antonym: unlucky person, jinx | noun
