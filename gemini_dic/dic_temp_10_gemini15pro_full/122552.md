modelling:/ˈmɒdlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdəlɪŋ/ (US)| n.|建模，造型，模特工作|The process of creating a representation of something, often using mathematical or computer-based methods.  It can also refer to the work of a fashion model.|例句：The modelling of the new aircraft was done using advanced software. 新型飞机的建模是使用先进的软件完成的。

例句：She enjoyed her career in modelling. 她很享受她的模特生涯。|近义词：simulation, representation, imitation, posing|反义词: - |noun

model:/ˈmɒdl/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdl/ (US)| n. v.|模型，模范，模特；模仿，塑造|A three-dimensional representation of a person or thing or of a proposed structure, typically on a smaller scale than the original. It can also refer to a person employed to pose for artists, photographers, or advertisers, or a person or thing regarded as an excellent example of its kind. As a verb, it means to create a model of something or to work as a model.|例句：He built a model of the Eiffel Tower. 他建造了一个埃菲尔铁塔的模型。

例句：She models for a living. 她以当模特为生。|近义词：pattern, example, prototype, mannequin, imitate, shape|反义词: - |noun, verb
