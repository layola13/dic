indissolublist:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊblɪst/ |n.|不可分割主义者；坚信婚姻不可解除的人|A person who believes that marriage cannot be dissolved.| He was a staunch indissolublist, arguing against divorce on any grounds.| 近义词: opponent of divorce | 反义词: divorce advocate | 词性: noun

indissoluble:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ |adj.|不能分解的；不能溶解的；牢不可破的|Not able to be dissolved; permanent.|They believed in the indissoluble bond of marriage. |例句：Their friendship appeared indissoluble. 他们的友谊似乎牢不可破。| 近义词: permanent, lasting, unbreakable | 反义词: dissoluble, soluble, breakable | 词性: adjective
