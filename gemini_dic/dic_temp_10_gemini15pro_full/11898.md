arrow:/ˈæroʊ/ (US) /ˈærəʊ/ (UK)| v.|（使）像箭一样移动|To move swiftly and directly like an arrow.|The car arrowed down the highway. 汽车像箭一样飞驰在高速公路上。|近义词：shoot, dart, zoom|反义词: meander|verb
