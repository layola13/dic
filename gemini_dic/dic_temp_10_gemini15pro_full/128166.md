nettling:/ˈnɛtlɪŋ/| adj.  v. | 刺激的，恼人的；荨麻的；用荨麻刺 | irritating; annoying; of or pertaining to nettles; to sting with or as if with nettles |  His nettling remarks angered her. 他的刺激性言论激怒了她。| synonyms: irritating, annoying, vexing, provoking, galling | antonyms: soothing, calming, pleasing, agreeable | adjective
