greener:/ˈɡriːnər/| adj.|更绿的，更环保的|Comparative form of "green."  Indicates a greater degree of greenness in color, or a greater degree of environmental friendliness.|The grass is greener on the other side.  这山望着那山高。| The company is trying to adopt greener practices.  这家公司正在尝试采用更环保的做法。|近义词：more environmentally friendly, more sustainable|反义词：less green, browner, less sustainable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *grener*, comparative of *grene*, from Old English *grēne*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grōniz* (green).
