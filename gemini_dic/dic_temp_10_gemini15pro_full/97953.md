innuendo:/ˌɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ (US) /ˌɪnjuˈɛndəʊ/ (UK)| n.|影射，暗讽|An indirect remark, gesture, or expression, usually suggesting something disparaging or improper, often of a sexual nature. |例句：His reputation was damaged by innuendos about his personal life. 他的名誉因对他私生活的影射而受损。

例句：The comedian's act was full of sexual innuendos.  这位喜剧演员的表演充满了性暗示。

近义词：insinuation, implication, suggestion, hint|反义词:direct statement|词性:noun
