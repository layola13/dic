adjusting:/əˈdʒʌstɪŋ/
verb
调整，调节；适应
The process of changing something slightly to make it work better, or to make it more suitable for a particular purpose or situation.
She is adjusting to her new job. 她正在适应她的新工作。

He was adjusting the telescope to get a clearer image. 他正在调整望远镜以获得更清晰的图像。

近义词:adapting, modifying, regulating, altering
反义词:worsening, damaging, disrupting
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ajuster "to arrange, put in order," from Vulgar Latin *ad-iuxtare "to place near," from Latin iuxta "near, next."
