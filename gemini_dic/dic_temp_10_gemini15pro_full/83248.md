gymnic: /ˈdʒɪmnɪk/
adjective |体操的；体育的；健身的| relating to gymnastics or physical exercise. |例句：Gymnic activities are good for health. 体操运动有益健康。|例句：She enjoys gymnic exercises. 她喜欢体操运动。|近义词：athletic, calisthenic|反义词：sedentary|词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *gymnikos* “pertaining to gymnastics,” from *gymnos* “naked” (athletes originally competed naked).
