heaviest:/ˈheviɪst/| adj.|最重的|Superlative form of heavy; of greatest weight.|The elephant is the heaviest animal in the zoo.  这头大象是动物园里最重的动物。|The heaviest box was at the bottom of the pile. 最重的箱子在箱子堆的底部。|近义词：largest, biggest, weightiest|反义词：lightest, smallest|词性:形容词
heavy:/ˈhevi/| adj.|重的|Having great weight; difficult to lift or move.|This box is too heavy for me to carry. 这个箱子太重了，我搬不动。|The heavy rain caused flooding.  这场大雨导致了洪水。|近义词： weighty, ponderous|反义词：light, weightless|词性:形容词
