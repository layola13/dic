outroaring: /ˌaʊtˈrɔːrɪŋ/
verb
胜过咆哮，盖过…的吼叫声
exceeding in loudness, surpassing or drowning out another roar.
The thunder was outroaring the lion's roar. 雷声盖过了狮子的吼叫。
近义词: outbellowing, overpowering, drowning out
反义词: muffled, subdued, quiet
词性: verb (动词)

Etymology:
"outroar" combines "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) with "roar" (a loud, deep cry or howl).  "Roar" comes from the Old English word "rārian,"  and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*rēraną."
