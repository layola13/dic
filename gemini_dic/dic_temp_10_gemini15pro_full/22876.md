ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French botanique, via Latin from Greek botanikē (epistēmē ) ‘(knowledge) of plants’, from botanē ‘plant’, ‘herb’, ‘pasture’.
来自中世纪晚期英语：来自古法语 botanique，经由拉丁语来自希腊语 botanikē (epistēmē )“（关于）植物的（知识）”，来自 botanē “植物”、“草本植物”、“牧场”。
