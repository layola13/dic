longhand:/ˈlɔːŋhænd/ | n. |  普通书写；手写 | Writing done by hand, not typed or printed. |  I wrote the letter in longhand because my printer was broken. 我因为打印机坏了，所以用手写了这封信。 | synonyms: handwriting, script, penmanship | antonyms: typing, printing, shorthand | noun
