doria:/ˈdɔːriə/ | n. | 多利亚（意大利地名，一种烹饪方式）| A style of cooking, named after the Italian region of Liguria, featuring seafood and vegetables often baked with cheese and breadcrumbs. It also refers to the region of Liguria itself. | Example: We had a delicious chicken doria for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了美味的鸡肉多利亚。 | Example:  My family is from Doria, Italy. 我的家人来自意大利的多利亚。| Synonym: Ligurian (when referring to the cooking style), casserole (similar dish) | Antonym: N/A | noun
