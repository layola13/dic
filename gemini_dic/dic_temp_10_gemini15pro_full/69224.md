fearmonger:/ˈfɪəmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 恐惧贩子；散布恐慌的人 | A person who deliberately spreads frightening rumors or information to create fear and anxiety. | The politician was accused of being a fearmonger, using scare tactics to gain votes.  这个政客被指控散布恐慌，利用恐吓策略来赢得选票。| 近义词: alarmist, scaremonger | 反义词: reassurer, comforter | Noun

fearmonger:/ˈfɪəmʌŋɡər/ | v. | 散布恐慌；制造恐慌 | To deliberately spread frightening rumors or information. | The media was criticized for fearmongering about the virus. 媒体因散布关于病毒的恐慌而受到批评。 | 近义词: alarm, scare, terrify | 反义词: reassure, comfort | Verb
