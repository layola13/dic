disturbative:/dɪˈstɜːrbətɪv/| adj.|扰动的，干扰的|Causing disturbance or interruption; tending to disturb.  Relating to or causing perturbation, especially in a physical system.|The construction noise was highly disturbative to the residents.|近义词：disruptive, unsettling, troubling, perturbing|反义词：calming, soothing, peaceful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "disturb" (from Old French *destorber, from des- "out, away" + torber "to disturb," from Latin turbare "to disturb, confuse") + "-ative" (suffix forming adjectives indicating a tendency or capacity).
