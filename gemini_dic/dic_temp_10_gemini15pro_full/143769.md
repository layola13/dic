outmeasured:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərd/
verb
超过，胜过，在…方面优于
surpassed in measurement; exceeded in size, extent, degree, or quantity.
He easily outmeasured his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中轻松胜过对手。
surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, better
fall short of, be inferior to
