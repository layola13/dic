misregard:/ˌmɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd/ | n. vt. |忽视，漠视；不理会|To ignore or treat as unimportant.|She completely disregarded my advice. 她完全无视我的建议.|近义词：ignore, neglect, overlook, dismiss|反义词：heed, regard, consider, respect|词性:名词,动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + regard (to consider).  Ultimately from the Old French regarder (to look at).
