embossment:/ɪmˈbɒsmənt/ /ɪmˈbɑːsmənt/
n.
浮雕;凸饰;凸纹;（图案、文字等的）浮雕效果
an ornament or design that is raised from a surface
The embossment on the leather cover made it look very elegant.  皮革封面的浮雕使其看起来非常优雅。

近义词:relief, raising, projection
反义词:indentation, depression
词性:名词
