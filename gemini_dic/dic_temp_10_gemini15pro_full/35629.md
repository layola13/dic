coati-mondis:/koʊˌɑːtiˈmʌndiz/| n. |南美浣熊，蜜熊|A small, arboreal mammal native to Central and South America, known for its long, ringed tail and pointed snout.|Coati-mondis are highly social animals, often found in groups of up to 30 individuals. 南美浣熊是高度群居的动物，经常以多达 30 只的群体出现。| The coati-mondi used its long tail for balance as it moved through the trees. 南美浣熊在树上移动时用它长长的尾巴保持平衡。 |Synonyms:  coati, ring-tailed coati, South American coati, Brazilian aardvark| Antonyms: N/A| Noun
