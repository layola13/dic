moralising:/ˈmɒrəlaɪzɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 说教的，道德化的 | The act of expressing moral judgments, especially in a tedious or self-righteous way.  | He gave a moralising lecture on the dangers of drinking. 他进行了一场关于饮酒危害的说教。 | synonyms:preaching, sermonizing, pontifical, sanctimonious | antonyms: encouraging, supportive, understanding | adjective

moralising:/ˈmɒrəlaɪzɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 说教 | The practice of moralizing. | His constant moralising was tiresome. 他不停的说教让人厌烦。 | synonyms:preaching, sermonizing | antonyms: neutrality, open-mindedness | noun
