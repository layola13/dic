illegitimating:/ɪˌlɪˈdʒɪtɪmeɪtɪŋ/
v.|使私生;剥夺合法性;宣布…非法|Rendering something illegitimate; depriving of legal status; declaring unlawful.|The new law is illegitimating previously accepted practices.  新法律使先前被接受的做法非法化。|The regime is illegitimating any opposition. 该政权正在宣布任何反对派为非法。|Synonyms: invalidating, nullifying, delegalizing|Antonyms: legitimizing, validating, authorizing|Verb
