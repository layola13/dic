faddy:/ˈfædi/| adj.|一时流行的；爱赶时髦的；喜新厌旧的|Intensely fashionable for a short time.|She's very faddy about her food. 她对食物很挑剔。|近义词：trendy, fashionable, stylish, chic, voguish|反义词：classic, timeless, enduring|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From fad + -y. Fad is likely a shortening of fiddle-faddle.
