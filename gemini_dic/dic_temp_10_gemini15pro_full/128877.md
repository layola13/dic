niller:/ˈnɪlər/ | n. | 零分;一无所获;毫无价值的人或物 |  A person or thing of no value or use; a zero score.  This word is informal and often used in a humorous or derogatory way. | He got a niller on the test. 他考试得了零分。| 近义词: loser, failure, zero | 反义词: winner, success | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely derived from "nil," meaning zero, with the "-er" suffix added to create a noun. "Nil" itself comes from Latin "nihil," meaning nothing.
