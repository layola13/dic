ampyx:/ˈæmpɪks/| n.|三叶虫的一种，弓形虫|A genus of trilobite with a broad head shield, a small body, and long spines extending from the head and tail.  It lived in the Ordovician period.|The Ampyx is a fascinating example of early marine life. 弓形虫是早期海洋生物的一个迷人例子。|The museum displayed a well-preserved fossil of an Ampyx.  博物馆展出了一块保存完好的弓形虫化石。|近义词:trilobite|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific genus of trilobite)|词性:noun
