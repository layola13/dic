debarb:/diːˈbɑːrb/
verb
去除倒钩；去除鱼钩的倒钩
To remove a barb or barbs from something, especially a fishhook or wire.
The angler carefully debarbed the hook before releasing the fish back into the water.  钓鱼者在把鱼放回水中之前小心翼翼地去掉了鱼钩的倒刺。
Synonyms: remove barbs, unbarb
Antonyms: barb
词性: 动词


debarb:/diːˈbɑːrb/
noun
去除倒钩的工具或行为
The act of removing barbs or a tool used for this purpose. (Rare usage)
The debarb of the hook was quick and efficient.  去除鱼钩倒刺的动作迅速而有效。
Synonyms: barb removal (as a noun phrase)
Antonyms: barbing
词性: 名词 (罕用)
