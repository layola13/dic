eikon:/ˈaɪkɒn/ (UK) /ˈaɪkɑːn/ (US)| n.|图像，肖像，圣像|An image, picture, or representation of a person, especially a painting of Jesus Christ or another holy figure; a likeness. |例句：The eikon of the Virgin Mary was displayed prominently in the church. 圣母玛利亚的圣像在教堂里显著地陈列着。

例句：The emperor's eikon was stamped on every coin.  皇帝的肖像被印在每一枚硬币上。|近义词：image, icon, likeness, portrait, representation|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
