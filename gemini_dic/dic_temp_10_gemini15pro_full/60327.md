egoism:/ˈiːɡoʊɪzəm/ /ˈeɡəʊɪzəm/
n.|利己主义；自我主义；自私自利|The doctrine that morality ultimately rests on self-interest.|He is motivated by pure egoism.|近义词：selfishness, self-centeredness, narcissism|反义词：altruism, selflessness|词性: noun
Etymology: From French *égoïsme*, from Latin *ego* ("I") + *-isme* (from Greek *-ismos*).
