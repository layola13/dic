misanthropists:/ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪsts/ /ˌmɪsənˈθroʊpɪsts/| n. |厌世者；遁世者；愤世嫉俗者|A person who hates or distrusts humankind.|The old hermit was considered a misanthropist by the villagers because he avoided all contact with them.  这位老隐士被村民们认为是一个厌世者，因为他避免与所有人接触。|Example:  Modern misanthropists often express their disdain for society online. 现代的厌世者经常在网上表达他们对社会的蔑视。|Synonyms: cynic, pessimist, recluse|Antonyms: philanthropist, altruist|Noun
