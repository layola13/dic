notarize:/ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz/ | vt. | 公证，证明 | To certify (a document, such as a deed or contract) officially. | Example 1: I need to have this document notarized before I submit it.  我需要在提交这份文件之前将其公证。

Example 2: The lawyer notarized the will. 律师对遗嘱进行了公证。 | Synonyms: certify, authenticate, validate | Antonyms: invalidate,  deny | verb
