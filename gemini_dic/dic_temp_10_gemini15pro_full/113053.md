lumped:/lʌmpt/| adj.|成块的，总括的，集中在一起的|Treated as a single entity or group, without distinguishing individual components.  Combined or classified together. |The expenses were all lumped together in one bill.  所有的费用都集中在一张账单里。|The different types of income were lumped together for tax purposes. 为了税收目的，不同类型的收入被汇总在一起。|近义词：combined, aggregated, collective, grouped, bundled|反义词：separated, individual, distinct, itemized|词性:形容词
lump:/lʌmp/| n. vt. vi.|块，肿块；笨人；(使)成块，(使)成团|A compact mass of something, especially one without a definite shape.  To put or treat things together in a single group.|There was a lump of coal in the stocking.  袜子里有一块煤。|She lumped all the clothes together in a pile. 她把所有的衣服都堆成一堆。|近义词：chunk, mass, clump,  combine, group|反义词：separate, divide|词性:名词,动词
