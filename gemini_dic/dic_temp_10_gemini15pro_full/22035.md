bol:/bɒl/ (UK) /boʊl/ (US)| n.|碗；钵；(尤指盛液体的)大杯；大碗|A large bowl, typically used for holding liquids. |例句：She poured the soup into a large bol. 她把汤倒进一个大碗里。

例句：He drank a bol of water after his workout. 他锻炼后喝了一大碗水。

近义词：bowl, basin, container|反义词:|词性:名词

词源: From Middle Dutch *bolle, from Old French bole, ultimately from Late Latin bolla (“round vessel”).
