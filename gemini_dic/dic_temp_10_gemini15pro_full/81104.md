ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gramaire (Modern French grammaire), from Latin grammatica "grammar, learning, scholarship," from Greek grammatikē tekhnē "art of letters, of reading and writing," from gramma (genitive grammatos) "letter of the alphabet," from graphein "to write," related to graphikos "of or for writing."
