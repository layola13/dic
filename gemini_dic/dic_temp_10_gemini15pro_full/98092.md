inquisitor:/ɪnˈkwɪzɪtər/| n. |审问者，调查者|A person who asks many questions, often in a harsh or aggressive way, especially one who conducts an official inquiry.|The inquisitor demanded to know the whereabouts of the rebel leader.|近义词:interrogator, examiner, questioner, investigator|反义词:respondent, witness|noun
