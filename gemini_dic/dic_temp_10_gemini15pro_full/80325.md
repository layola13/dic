goldenly:/ˈɡoʊldənli/| adv. |金灿灿地，金黄地|In a golden manner; splendidly.  It describes something happening or appearing in a way reminiscent of gold, often implying beauty, richness, or success.|The sun set goldenly over the ocean. 夕阳金灿灿地落入海平面。|近义词：brightly, radiantly, gloriously|反义词:dully, dimly|词性:副词

词源: From "golden" + "-ly". "Golden" comes from Old English "gylden," from Proto-Germanic "gulþinaz."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
