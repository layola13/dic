homeroom:/ˈhəʊmrʊm/ (UK), /ˈhoʊmrʊm/ (US)| n.|教室；班会；导师时间|A classroom in which a group of students assembles daily for attendance, announcements, etc., typically at the beginning of the school day.|例句：Students go to their homeroom first thing in the morning. 学生们早上第一件事就是去他们的教室。|例句：Important announcements were made during homeroom. 重要的通知是在班会上宣布的。|近义词：classroom, form room|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
