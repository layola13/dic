fattest:/ˈfætɪst/ | adj. | 最胖的 | Superlative form of "fat," meaning having the most body fat. |  He was the fattest cat I'd ever seen. 他是我见过的最胖的猫。| synonyms: plumpest, heaviest, largest, obese, stoutest | antonyms: thinnest, slimmest, leanest, skinniest | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fætt* (well-fed, fattened), from Proto-Germanic *faitaz (fat).  Cognates include Dutch *vet*, German *fett*, and Icelandic *feitr*.  The "-est" ending is a superlative suffix.
