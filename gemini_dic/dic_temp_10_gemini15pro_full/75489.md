frutage:/ˈfruːtɪdʒ/
n.
水果拼盘; 水果甜点; (尤指用作装饰的)水果图案
A dish or sweet made with fruit, especially one used as a decoration.
The chef prepared a beautiful frutage for the wedding dessert.  主厨为婚礼甜点准备了一个漂亮的水果拼盘。
The frutage on the cake was made of fresh berries.  蛋糕上的水果装饰是用新鲜浆果做的。
近义词:fruit salad, fruit dessert, fruit platter
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider savoury dish)
