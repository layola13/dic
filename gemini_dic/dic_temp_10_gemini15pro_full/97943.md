innovatively:/ˈɪnəveɪtɪvli/
adv.
创新地，有创意地
in a new and original way
The company is constantly looking for ways to market its products innovatively.  这家公司一直在寻找创新地推广其产品的方法。
They approached the problem innovatively. 他们用创新的方法处理这个问题。
近义词: creatively, originally, imaginatively
反义词: traditionally, conventionally, routinely
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From innovate + -ive + -ly.  Innovate comes from Latin innovare "to renew, alter," from in- "into" + novus "new."
