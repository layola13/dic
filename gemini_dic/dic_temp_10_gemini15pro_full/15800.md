balkanize: /ˈbɔːlkənaɪz/ /ˈbɑːlkənaɪz/
| vt. | 使巴尔干化，分裂成小国 | To divide (a region or body) into smaller mutually hostile states or groups. |  The Austro-Hungarian empire was balkanized after World War I.  奥匈帝国在第一次世界大战后被巴尔干化了。 | divide, fragment, partition, split | unite, integrate | verb

balkanization: /ˌbɔːlkənɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌbɑːlkənɪˈzeɪʃən/
