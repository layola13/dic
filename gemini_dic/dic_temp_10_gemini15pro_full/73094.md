footworn:/ˈfʊtwɔːrn/| adj.| 磨损的，因行走过多而疲惫的| Worn or weary from much walking.|  His footworn boots testified to the miles he had traveled.  他磨损的靴子证明了他走了很多路。|  例句：The traveler's footworn shoes showed the extent of his journey. 旅行者磨损的鞋子显示了他旅程的长度。

例句：Her footworn sandals were a testament to her adventurous spirit. 她磨损的凉鞋证明了她的冒险精神。| 近义词：worn, weary, tired, exhausted, haggard| 反义词：fresh, new, unworn, rested, energetic| 词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From foot + worn (past participle of wear).  Meaning "worn by the feet" is from the late 16th century.
