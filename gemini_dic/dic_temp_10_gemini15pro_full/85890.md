header:/ˈhɛdər/ | n. | 标题，页眉，头球 |A title or heading at the top of a page or section of a document or display. |  Example: The header of the email contained the sender's information.  电子邮件的标题包含了发件人的信息。| Synonyms: title, heading, caption | Antonyms: footer, bottom | noun

header:/ˈhɛdər/ | vt. | 用头顶（球），加标题 |To hit (a ball) with one's head. To provide with a header.|  Example: He headed the ball into the goal. 他用头顶球进了球。 | Example: I need to header this document before printing it. 我需要在打印前给这份文件加上标题。 | Synonyms: head, top | Antonyms: foot, base | verb
