hypergenetically:/ˌhaɪpərjəˈnɛtɪkli/
adjective
超遗传地
Relating to or arising from hypergenesis, which refers to evolutionary development or structural modification occurring above or beyond the genes; influenced by factors external to the genome.
The observed changes in the species were thought to be hypergenetically induced, possibly due to environmental pressures. 该物种中观察到的变化被认为是超遗传诱导的，可能是由于环境压力。
Near Synonyms: epigenetically, environmentally induced
Antonyms: genetically determined, innate
词性: adjective
