expectative:/ɪkˈspektətɪv/ | adj. n. |期待的，期望的；期待者，候选人| Characterized by expectation;  indicating or expressing expectation.  A person who is expecting something, especially a position or office. | She had an expectant look on her face. 她脸上带着期盼的神情。| 近义词: hopeful, anticipatory, prospective | 反义词: pessimistic, despairing | 词性: adjective, noun
