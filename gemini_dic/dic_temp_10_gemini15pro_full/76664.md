galvanising:/ˈɡælvənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj. n.| 镀锌的，激励的；刺激的 |The process of coating iron or steel with a protective layer of zinc.  Also, stimulating someone to take action.| The galvanising effect of the news spurred them into action.  新闻的激励作用促使他们采取行动。 | 近义词: stimulating, exciting, inspiring, electrifying, energizing | 反义词: demotivating, depressing, discouraging | 词性: adjective, noun

galvanise:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪz/ (US)| vt.| 镀锌，激励；刺激 | To coat (iron or steel) with a protective layer of zinc. Also, to shock or excite someone into taking action.| The news galvanised them into action. 这消息激励他们行动起来。 | 近义词: stimulate, excite, inspire, electrify, energize | 反义词: demotivate, depress, discourage | 词性: verb


Etymology: From French *galvaniser*, from the name of Italian scientist Luigi Galvani (1737-1798).
