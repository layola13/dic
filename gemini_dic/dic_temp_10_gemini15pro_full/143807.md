outpacing:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪsɪŋ/
verb
超越，胜过
growing faster than something else
China's economy is outpacing many developed nations. 中国的经济增长速度超过了许多发达国家。
outstripping, exceeding, surpassing
lagging behind, falling behind
v.
