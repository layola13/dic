outpass:/ˈaʊtpɑːs/
n.|外出证；通行证|A document authorizing a person to leave a place, especially a military base or prison.|He needed an outpass to leave the base. 他需要一张通行证才能离开基地。
近义词:permit, pass
反义词:(No direct antonym - perhaps "incarceration" or "confinement" in the context of prisons)
词性:名词
