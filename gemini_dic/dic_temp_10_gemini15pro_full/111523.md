loather:/ˈloʊðər/ | n. |憎恶者，厌恶者|A person who intensely dislikes someone or something. | He is a loather of all things modern. 他厌恶所有现代的东西。| 近义词: hater, detester, abhorrer | 反义词: lover, admirer | 词性: noun
loathe:/loʊð/ | v. | 憎恶，厌恶 | To feel intense dislike or disgust for. | I loathe spiders. 我讨厌蜘蛛。 | 近义词: hate, detest, abhor, despise | 反义词: love, adore, cherish | 词性: verb
