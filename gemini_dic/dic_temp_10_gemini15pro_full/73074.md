footsie: /ˈfʊtsi/ (UK) /ˈfʊtsi/ (US)| n. |调情，玩暧昧；碰脚游戏|A playful touching of feet under a table, especially between people who are attracted to each other.  It suggests a flirtatious or romantic interest.|例句：They were playing footsie under the table throughout dinner. 他们在晚餐期间一直桌子底下玩碰脚游戏。

例句：I noticed them playing footsie, so I knew they were interested in each other. 我注意到他们在玩碰脚游戏，所以我知道他们互相有意思。|近义词：flirt, dally|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of disinterest or avoidance) |词性:noun
