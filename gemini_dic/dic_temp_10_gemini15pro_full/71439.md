flambe:/flɑːmˈbeɪ/ /flɑːmˈbeɪ/
v.
(of food) served flaming, after being ignited in brandy or other liquor
(指食物)以白兰地或其他烈酒点燃后燃烧着上桌
The chef flambéed the cherries jubilee.  厨师将樱桃甜点 flambé 了。
v.
to serve (food) flaming, after igniting it in brandy or other liquor.
以白兰地或其他烈酒点燃后燃烧着上桌（食物）。
He flambéed the steak with cognac. 他用干邑白兰地 flambé 了牛排。
近义词: ignite, blaze, flare
反义词: extinguish, douse
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French flambé, past participle of flamber "to flame," from Old French flamber, from flambe "flame." Ultimately from Latin flamma.
