butterlike:/ˈbʌtərlaɪk/| adj.|像黄油的，像黄油一样滑的|Resembling butter in consistency, smoothness, or color.  Having a smooth, rich texture or appearance similar to butter.|The sauce had a butterlike consistency.  酱汁的稠度像黄油一样。|近义词：buttery, creamy, smooth, rich|反义词:gritty, coarse, rough|词性:形容词

Etymology: butter + like.  "Butter" comes from Old English "butere," from Latin "butyrum," from Greek "boutyron."  "Like" comes from Old English "gelic," meaning "similar, resembling."
