outthreatening:/ˌaʊtˈθrɛtnɪŋ/ | adj. | 更具威胁性的；威胁更大的 | Posing a greater threat than another; surpassing in the ability to threaten. |  The new virus strain is outthreatening all previous variants.  这种新的病毒株比所有以前的变种都更具威胁性。| Example: The rising floodwaters were outthreatening the levees, prompting an immediate evacuation.  不断上涨的洪水对堤坝的威胁越来越大，促使立即疏散。|Synonyms: more menacing, more intimidating, more dangerous| Antonyms: less threatening, benign, harmless | Word type: Adjective
