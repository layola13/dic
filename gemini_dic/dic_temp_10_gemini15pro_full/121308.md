misfocussing:/ˌmɪsfəʊkəsɪŋ/ | v. | 失焦，注意力不集中 |Failing to focus correctly, either physically (as with a lens) or mentally. | The photographer misfocussed the shot, resulting in a blurry image. 摄影师没有对准焦距，导致照片模糊不清。|近义词：blurring, losing focus, distracting|反义词：focussing, concentrating, sharpening|词性:动词

misfocussing:/ˌmɪsfəʊkəsɪŋ/ | n. | 失焦，注意力不集中（名词化用法较少）| The act of failing to focus correctly. | His misfocussing during the lecture meant he missed key information. 他在讲座期间注意力不集中，意味着他错过了关键信息。|近义词：lack of focus, distraction|反义词：focus, concentration|词性:名词


词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning wrong or bad) + "focus" (from Latin "focus" meaning hearth, fireplace; later used metaphorically for a center of activity or attention) + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
