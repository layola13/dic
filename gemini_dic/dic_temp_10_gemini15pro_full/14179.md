auto-focuses:/ˌɔːtəˈfoʊkəsɪz/| v.|自动对焦|The camera auto-focuses quickly in low light. 相机在弱光下快速自动对焦。|例句：My new phone camera auto-focuses on faces. 我的新手机摄像头可以自动对焦人脸。

例句：The lens auto-focuses silently and smoothly. 镜头自动对焦，安静而流畅。

近义词：adjusts automatically, self-focuses|反义词:manual focus|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "auto-" (self) + "focus" (to adjust a lens to make a clear image). "Focus" comes from the Latin "focus" (hearth, fireplace), later used in optics for the point where rays of light converge.
