contract:/ˈkɒntrækt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrækt/ (US)| n. vt. vi.|合同；契约；收缩；感染|A legally binding agreement between two or more parties.|例句：We signed a contract for the purchase of the house. 我们签署了房屋购买合同。

例句：The company won a lucrative contract to build the new bridge.  这家公司赢得了一份利润丰厚的建造新桥的合同。|近义词：agreement, pact, deal|反义词：expand, enlarge|n.

contract:/ˈkɒntrækt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrækt/ (US)| vt. vi.|收缩；感染；订契约|To become smaller or shorter; to catch or develop a disease.|例句：The muscles in his arm contracted. 他手臂上的肌肉收缩了。

例句：She contracted malaria while traveling in Africa. 她在非洲旅行时感染了疟疾。|近义词：shrink, compress, catch|反义词：expand, enlarge|v.
