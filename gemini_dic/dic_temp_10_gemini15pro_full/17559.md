beatto:/biˈɑːtoʊ/ /biˈætəʊ/
| n. |  人迹罕至的地方；偏僻小路 | A little-used or secluded place or path. |  They found a quiet beatto along the river. 他们在河边找到了一个僻静的地方。 |
| 近义词: hideaway, retreat, secluded spot | 反义词: busy place, city center | 词性: 名词
