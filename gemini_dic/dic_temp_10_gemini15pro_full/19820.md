bimetallic:/ˌbaɪməˈtælɪk/
adjective
双金属的
Made of two different metals, especially fused together.
The bimetallic strip in the thermostat bends when heated.  恒温器中的双金属片受热时会弯曲。

bimetallic:/ˌbaɪməˈtælɪk/
noun
双金属
An object made of two different metals.
The bimetallic responded quickly to changes in temperature.  这种双金属对温度变化反应迅速。


SYNONYMS->
two-metal, dual-metal

ANTONYMS->
monometallic (although less common)

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + metallic (relating to metal).  "Bi-" comes from Latin, and "metallic" ultimately derives from Greek "metallon" (metal).
