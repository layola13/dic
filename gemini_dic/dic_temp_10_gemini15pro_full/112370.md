lose-it:/ˈluːz ɪt/|v.|失去控制；发脾气；精神崩溃|To lose control of one's temper or composure; to become emotionally unstable.|He lost it when he found out his car had been stolen. 他发现他的车被偷后，情绪失控了。|近义词：freak out, go crazy, snap|反义词：calm down, keep one's cool|verb

lose-it:/ˈluːz ɪt/|informal|失去控制；发脾气；精神崩溃|To become angry, upset, or irrational.|She lost it with the kids when they wouldn't stop screaming. 孩子们不停地尖叫时，她对他们发脾气了。|近义词：go ballistic, have a meltdown|反义词：stay calm, maintain composure|idiom
