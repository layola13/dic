impugnation:/ˌɪmpjʊɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 质疑，非难，抨击 | The act of challenging something as false or questionable.  |  His impugnation of her motives was unfair.  他对她动机的质疑是不公平的。 | Synonyms: challenge, questioning, attack, criticism, discrediting,  | Antonyms: defense, support, vindication, corroboration | noun

impugnation:/ˌɪmpjʊɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 质疑，非难，抨击 | The act of challenging the integrity or honesty of someone or something. | The politician's integrity was subject to impugnation.  这位政治家的正直受到了质疑。 | Synonyms:  disparagement, denigration, aspersion, slander | Antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, endorsement | noun
