hyperabsorption:/ˌhaɪpərəbˈzɔːrpʃən/ /ˌhaɪpərəbˈsɔːrpʃən/| n.|过度吸收|The hyperabsorption of nutrients can lead to toxicity. 过度吸收营养会导致中毒。|例句：The doctor warned about the dangers of hyperabsorption of certain vitamins. 医生警告了某些维生素过度吸收的危险。

例句：Hyperabsorption of water can cause hyponatremia. 水的过度吸收会导致低钠血症。|近义词：overabsorption, excessive absorption|反义词:malabsorption, hypoabsorption|词性:名词

hyperabsorption:| n.|过度吸收|An abnormally high rate of absorption, often with negative consequences.  指吸收率异常高，通常会带来负面后果。|例句：The study examined the phenomenon of hyperabsorption of iron in certain individuals. 这项研究调查了某些个体对铁的过度吸收现象。


例句：Hyperabsorption of fat-soluble vitamins can be harmful.  脂溶性维生素的过度吸收可能是有害的。|近义词：overabsorption, excessive uptake|反义词:malabsorption, hypoabsorption|词性:名词
