automobilists:/ˈɔːtəməʊbɪlɪsts/ | n. |汽车驾驶者；开汽车的人|A person who drives an automobile. |例句：The automobilists were stuck in traffic for hours. 汽车驾驶者们在交通堵塞中被困了几个小时。

例句：The association of automobilists is lobbying for lower gas prices.  汽车驾驶者协会正在游说降低汽油价格。

近义词：drivers, motorists |反义词:pedestrians, cyclists |词性:noun

Etymology: From automobile + -ist.  "Automobile" comes from French, ultimately from Ancient Greek "autos" (self) + Latin "mobilis" (movable). "-ist" is a suffix indicating a person who practices or is involved with something.
