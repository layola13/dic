antiparalytic:/ˌæntiˌpærəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. n. |抗麻痹的；抗麻痹药|Preventing or counteracting paralysis.  Relating to a drug or other agent that relieves paralysis.|
The doctor prescribed an antiparalytic drug. 医生开了抗麻痹药。
This new medication has shown promising antiparalytic properties. 这种新药显示出有希望的抗麻痹特性。|
近义词：antispastic, neuromuscular blocking agent antagonist |反义词: paralytic |词性: adjective, noun
