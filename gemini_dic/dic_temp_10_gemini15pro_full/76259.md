fuzzbox:/ˈfʌzbɒks/ (UK) /ˈfʌzbɑːks/ (US)| n. |电子失真效果器；模糊的思维或情况|A device used to create a distorted or fuzzy sound, especially in electric guitars; a state of mental confusion or a situation that is unclear or poorly defined.|例句：He used a fuzzbox to give his guitar a psychedelic sound. 他用电子失真效果器让他的吉他发出迷幻的声音。

例句：My head's a complete fuzzbox after that meeting. 那次会议后我的脑子一片混乱。|近义词：distortion pedal, overdrive pedal, confusion, chaos|反义词：clarity, order|词性:名词
