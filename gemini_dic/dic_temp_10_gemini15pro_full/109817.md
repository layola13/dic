what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask for information about people or things.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which|反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? = 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been studying for the past few months? = 过去几个月他一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the power went out? = 停电前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the price be next year? = 明年的价格会是多少？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时你将完成什么？
What will she have achieved by the time she retires? = 到她退休时她将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will he have been studying by the time he gets his PhD? = 到他获得博士学位时他将已经学习什么多久了？
