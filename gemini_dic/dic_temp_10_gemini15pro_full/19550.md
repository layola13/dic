bifurcated:/ˈbaɪfərkeɪtɪd/| adj.|分叉的，分歧的|Divided into two branches or parts; forked.|The river bifurcated into two smaller streams. 这条河流分叉成两条小溪。|近义词：forked, branched, divided|反义词：unified, combined, joined|adjective


bifurcate:/ˈbaɪfərkeɪt/| v.|分叉，分歧|To divide into two branches or parts.|The road bifurcates a few miles ahead. 这条路在几英里外分叉。|近义词：fork, branch, divide|反义词：merge, combine, join|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin bifurcatus, past participle of bifurcare (“to divide into two forks or branches”), from bi- (“two”) + furca (“fork”).
