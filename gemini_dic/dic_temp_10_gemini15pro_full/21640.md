blurry:/ˈblɜːri/ (US) /ˈbləːri/ (UK)| adj.|模糊的，看不清的|Lacking in clarity or sharpness of visual definition.|The photograph was blurry because my hand was shaking. |照片很模糊，因为我的手在抖。|近义词:fuzzy, hazy, indistinct, out of focus|反义词:clear, sharp, distinct, focused|词性:形容词

blur:/blɜːr/ (US) /bləː(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|模糊，模糊不清的事物；使模糊|Something that is indistinct or hazy; to make something indistinct or hazy.|The city lights were a blur through the rain-streaked window. |透过沾满雨水的窗户，城市灯光一片模糊。|近义词:haze, smudge|反义词:clarity, sharpness|词性:名词，动词

词源:  From late Middle English *blurre, *blurren, probably of imitative origin.
