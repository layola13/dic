defoliant:/diːˈfoʊliənt/ | n. | 落叶剂；脱叶剂 | A chemical that causes the leaves of plants to fall off. |  The use of defoliants in the Vietnam War caused widespread environmental damage. 在越南战争中使用落叶剂造成了广泛的环境破坏。| synonyms: herbicide, leaf remover | antonyms: fertilizer, growth stimulant | noun


defoliate:/diːˈfoʊlieɪt/ | v. | 使落叶；使脱叶 | To cause the leaves of a plant to fall off, usually by using chemicals. | The chemical defoliated the trees, leaving them bare in the middle of summer. 这种化学物质使树木落叶，在仲夏时节使它们光秃秃的。 | synonyms: strip of leaves, denude | antonyms:  grow foliage, flourish | verb
