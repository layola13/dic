ignorance:/ˈɪɡnərəns/| n.|无知，愚昧，不知|lack of knowledge or information.|Her ignorance of the law is no excuse. 她不懂法不能成为借口。|近义词：unawareness, inexperience, nescience|反义词：knowledge, awareness, wisdom|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ignorance* (unattested), from Latin *ignōrantia* "ignorance, want of knowledge," abstract noun from *ignōrāns* (genitive *ignōrantis*), present participle of *ignōrō* "not know, be unacquainted with, misunderstand, ignore," from *in-* "not" (see in- (1)) + *gnōrō* (see know).
