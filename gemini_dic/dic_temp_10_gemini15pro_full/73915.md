forswearer:/fɔːrˈswɛərər/
noun
发誓放弃者；背弃者；伪证者
One who forswears; one who renounces or abjures something under oath.
He was labeled a forswearer after changing his testimony in court.  他在法庭上改变证词后被贴上了伪证者的标签。
Near synonyms: perjurer, liar, renegade, apostate
Antonyms: loyalist, adherent, devotee
词性: 名词
