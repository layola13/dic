legislatures:/ˈledʒɪsleɪtʃərz/
n.
立法机关
The body of people who have the power to make and change laws.
State legislatures across the country are considering new voting laws.  全国各州立法机关正在考虑新的投票法。
The national legislature is responsible for enacting laws for the entire country. 国家立法机关负责为整个国家制定法律。

synonym: parliament, congress, assembly
antonym: executive, judiciary
