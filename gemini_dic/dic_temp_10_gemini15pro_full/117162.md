means-tested:/ˈmiːnzˌtestɪd/ | adj. | 经过经济状况调查的；以经济情况调查结果为依据的 |  Relating to a system in which financial support from the government is given only to those who are officially considered to be poor enough to need it. |  Medicaid is a means-tested program for low-income individuals.  医疗补助是针对低收入人群的一项经济状况调查项目。| synonyms: income-based, needs-based | antonyms: universal, unconditional | adjective

means-tested:/ˈmiːnzˌtestɪd/ | adj. | 经过经济状况调查的；以经济情况调查结果为依据的 |  Based on an assessment of someone's income and assets to determine eligibility for assistance. |  Means-tested benefits are available to those who fall below a certain income threshold. 只有收入低于一定门槛的人才能获得经过经济状况调查的福利。| synonyms: income-qualified, needs-based | antonyms: universal, non-means-tested | adjective
