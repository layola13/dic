outrun:/ˌaʊtˈrʌn/
verb
超越，超过
to run faster or further than someone or something else
The thief outran the police. 小偷跑得比警察快。
近义词：outstrip, surpass, exceed, overtake
反义词：lag behind, trail
词性：v.

outrun:/ˌaʊtˈrʌn/
verb
超出...的范围；发展得比...快
to develop more quickly than something else
The demand for new housing has outrun supply. 对新住房的需求已经超过了供应。
近义词：exceed, surpass
反义词：fall behind
词性：v.
