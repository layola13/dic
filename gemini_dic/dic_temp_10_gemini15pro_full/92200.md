Etymology:
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + suggestible (adjective meaning "easily influenced by suggestion") + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  The root word "suggest" comes from Latin suggerere, meaning "to carry or bring under."
