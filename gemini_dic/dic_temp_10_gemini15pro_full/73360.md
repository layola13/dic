foregathers:/fɔːrˈɡæðərz/
| v. |聚集；集合；偶遇|To come together, assemble, or meet, especially by chance.|例句：The family foregathers every year for Christmas. 全家每年都聚在一起过圣诞节。|例句：We foregathered with some old friends at the pub. 我们在酒吧里和一些老朋友相遇了。|近义词：assemble, gather, congregate, meet|反义词：disperse, scatter|词性：动词

Etymology:
From fore- + gather.  "Fore-" acts as a prefix meaning "before" or "beforehand," but in this context, it intensifies the meaning of "gather," implying a complete or thorough gathering. "Gather" comes from Old English "gaderian," meaning "to collect."
