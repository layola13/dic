overwetly: /ˌoʊvərˈwɛtli/ | adv. | 过于潮湿地，过分湿润地 |  In a way that is excessively wet.  It describes an action done with excessive wetness. | The plants were overwetly watered, leading to root rot.  植物浇水过多，导致根部腐烂。| The child overwetly painted the paper, causing it to tear. 孩子把纸涂得过湿，导致纸张撕裂。 | 近义词: excessively wetly, soppingly, drippingly | 反义词: dryly, aridly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "wet" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
