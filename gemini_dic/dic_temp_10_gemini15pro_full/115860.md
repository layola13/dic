markupping: /ˈmɑːrkʌpɪŋ/ | n. | 标记；加价；涨价 | The process of adding markup to the cost price of an item to arrive at its selling price.  Adding codes or symbols to text to indicate formatting or structure. |  The markupping of the product added 20% to its cost. 产品的加价使其成本增加了 20%。 The markupping of the document made it easier to read.  对文档进行标记使其更易于阅读。 | synonyms:  pricing, increasing, tagging, coding | antonyms: discounting, reducing | noun
