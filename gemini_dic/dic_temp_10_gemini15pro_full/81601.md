greatcoats: /ˈɡreɪtˌkəʊts/
n.
厚大衣；长外衣
A long, heavy overcoat.
The soldiers wore their greatcoats to protect them from the bitter cold.  士兵们穿着厚大衣御寒。

近义词: overcoats, topcoats
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑轻薄的衣物) shirts, shorts


词性: noun (名词)
词源: From 'great' (large) + 'coat' (outer garment).  由“great”（大的）和“coat”（外套）组成。
