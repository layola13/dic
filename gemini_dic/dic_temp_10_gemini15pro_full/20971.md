blencher's:/ˈblɛntʃərz/
n.
漂白工的; 漂白剂的
belonging to or relating to a blencher (someone who bleaches something).
The blencher's gloves were stained with bleach. 漂白工的手套沾满了漂白剂。
The blencher's vat was full of fabric.  漂白工的缸里装满了布料。
synonyms: bleacher's, whitener's
antonyms: dyer's, colorist's
noun
