overaggressive: /ˌoʊvərəˈɡrɛsɪv/

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "aggressive" (from Latin aggress-, past participle stem of aggredi "to go towards, approach," from ad- "to" + gradi "to step, walk") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
