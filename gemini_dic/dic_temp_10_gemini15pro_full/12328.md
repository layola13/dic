aseptically:/eɪˈsɛptɪkli/| adv.|无菌地，无菌操作地|In a manner free from contamination caused by bacteria, viruses, or other microorganisms. Done using sterile methods to prevent infection.|The surgeon aseptically prepared the instruments for the operation. 外科医生无菌地准备了手术器械。|Synonyms: sterily, hygienically|Antonyms: unsterile, contaminated|Adverb
