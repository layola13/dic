mechanicals: /məˈkænɪkəlz/
noun
机械部件; 机械装置; 机械学
The parts of a machine or device that move or cause movement.  Relating to the principles of mechanics.
The car's mechanicals are in good condition. 这辆车的机械部件状况良好。
machinery, apparatus, components
electronics, software
