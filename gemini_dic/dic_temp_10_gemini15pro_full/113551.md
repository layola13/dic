macadamize: /məˈkædəˌmaɪz/ | v. | 用碎石铺路 | To cover (a road or path) with a layer of small broken stones. |  The city council voted to macadamize the main street. 市议会投票决定用碎石铺设主要街道。

The road was macadamized to improve its surface. 这条路用碎石铺设以改善路面。| 近义词: pave, surface | 反义词: excavate | 词性: 动词
