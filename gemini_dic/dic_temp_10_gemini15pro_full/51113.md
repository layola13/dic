despisableness:/dɪˈspaɪzəblənəs/
n.
卑鄙；可鄙；令人厌恶
The state or quality of being despicable; contemptibility.
His despisableness was evident in his cruel treatment of animals. 他的卑鄙在他虐待动物的行为中显而易见。
Near Synonyms:  contemptibility, vileness, baseness, worthlessness, odiousness
Antonyms:  admirability, worthiness, respectability
Noun


despise:/dɪˈspaɪz/
v.
鄙视；蔑视；看不起
Feel contempt or a deep repugnance for.
He despised liars and cheats. 他鄙视说谎者和骗子。
Near Synonyms:  scorn, loathe, detest, abhor, disdain
Antonyms:  admire, respect, esteem, cherish
Verb
