misconnect:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkt/
verb
错误连接，连接错误
to connect incorrectly or improperly
The wires were misconnected, causing the device to malfunction. 电线连接错误，导致设备故障。
The phone operator misconnected me and I ended up talking to a stranger. 电话接线员把我接错了，结果我和一个陌生人聊了起来。
