hipless:/ˈhɪpləs/| adj.|无臀的，臀部不明显的|Lacking hips or having poorly developed hips.  Describes someone with a straight figure, lacking curves around the hips. |Her hipless figure made it difficult to find well-fitting jeans. 她没有曲线的身材让她很难找到合适的牛仔裤。|Near Synonyms: straight-figured, slender, boyish, slim|Antonyms: curvy, voluptuous, shapely|Adjective
