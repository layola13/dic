mishappen:/mɪsˈhæpən/| v. |发生意外；不幸发生；|To happen badly or unfortunately; to go wrong. |例句：Something has mishappened to the engine. 发动机出了故障。

例句：I hope nothing mishappens to him on his journey. 我希望他在旅途中一切顺利，不要发生意外。|近义词：go wrong, fail, backfire, come to grief|反义词:succeed, go right, prosper|词性:动词
