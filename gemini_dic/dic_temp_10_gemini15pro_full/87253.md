heriotable: /ˌhɛriˈəʊtəbəl/
adjective
可继承的;  可世袭的
Relating to inheritance, especially of land or titles; capable of being inherited.
The estate was heriotable, passing down through generations of the family.  这片地产是可继承的，一代代传下来。
Synonyms: inheritable, hereditary, transmissible
Antonyms: non-heritable, uninheritable
词性: adjective
