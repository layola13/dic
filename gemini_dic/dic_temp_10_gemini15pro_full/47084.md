datura: /dəˈtʃʊərə/ /dəˈtʊrə/ | n. |曼陀罗，洋金花 | Any of several poisonous plants of the genus *Datura*, such as jimsonweed, having large, often fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers and spiny fruit. | The datura's intoxicating scent filled the night air. 曼陀罗的醉人香味弥漫在夜空中。
