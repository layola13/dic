geomantical:/ˌdʒiːəˈmæntɪkəl/
adjective
地占的；风水术的
Relating to geomancy; pertaining to divination by means of figures or lines or geographic features.
例句：
The geomantical traditions of ancient China are complex and fascinating.  古代中国的地占传统复杂而迷人。

He consulted a geomancer for advice on the best location for his new house. 他咨询了一位风水师，就新房的最佳位置征求意见。


近义词：divinatory, augural
反义词：scientific, empirical
词性：adjective
