bravery:/ˈbreɪvəri/| n.|勇敢，勇气|The firefighter showed incredible bravery in rescuing the child from the burning building. 消防员在从燃烧的建筑物中救出孩子时表现出了令人难以置信的勇气。|例句：He received a medal for his bravery in battle. 他因在战斗中的英勇而获得一枚奖章。

例句：Her bravery in the face of danger was inspiring.  她在面对危险时的勇气令人鼓舞。

近义词：courage, valor, fearlessness, boldness, daring|反义词:cowardice, fear, timidity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *braverie, borrowed from Old French braverie (“finery, magnificence, bravery”), from brave (“brave, worthy, excellent”), of uncertain origin, probably from Italian bravo (“brave, fierce, wild”).
