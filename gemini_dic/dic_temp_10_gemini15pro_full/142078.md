orgyia:/ɔːrˈdʒɪə/ | n. | 狂欢；纵欲；放荡 | A wild, unrestrained revelry; an excessive indulgence in a particular activity.  | The ancient Romans were known for their orgies. 古罗马人以他们的狂欢而闻名。 |  The weekend turned into an orgy of eating and drinking.  周末变成了吃喝的狂欢。| 近义词：revelry, bacchanal, debauchery, spree | 反义词：abstinence, moderation | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *orgia, from Ancient Greek ὄργια (órgia, “secret rites, orgies”), plural of ὄργιον (órgion).
