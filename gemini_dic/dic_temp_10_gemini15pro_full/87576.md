heteropod:/ˈhetərəpɒd/ (US) /ˈhetərəpɑːd/ (UK)| n. |异足类动物，异足纲软体动物|A free-swimming pelagic sea snail, typically large and predatory, with a modified foot acting as a fin.|The heteropod *Atlanta peronii* is a common species found in tropical waters.  异足类动物*亚特兰大圆盘螺*是热带水域中常见的物种。|近义词：pelagic snail, sea snail|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Greek *héteros* "other" + *poús* "foot".
