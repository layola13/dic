humbuckers:/ˈhʌmbʌkərz/
n.
双线圈拾音器
A type of electric guitar pickup that uses two coils of wire to reduce noise and produce a thicker, more powerful sound.
My Les Paul has a pair of powerful humbuckers. 我的Les Paul吉他有一对强劲的双线圈拾音器。
humbucking pickups, double-coil pickups
single-coil pickups
noun
