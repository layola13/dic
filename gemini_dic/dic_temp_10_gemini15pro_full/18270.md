belittler:/bɪˈlɪtlər/| n.|轻视者，贬低者|A person who belittles or disparages others; someone who makes others feel small or unimportant.|He's such a belittler, constantly putting down everyone around him. 他真是个爱贬低别人的人，总是不断地奚落周围的人。|Synonyms: detractor, disparager, critic, denigrator|Antonyms: admirer, praiser, flatterer|noun

belittle:/bɪˈlɪtl/| vt.|轻视，贬低，小看|To make someone or something seem unimportant or of less value.|She belittled his efforts, making him feel incompetent. 她贬低了他的努力，让他觉得自己很无能。|Synonyms: disparage, denigrate, minimize, underestimate, depreciate|Antonyms: praise, extol, magnify, overestimate, appreciate|verb
