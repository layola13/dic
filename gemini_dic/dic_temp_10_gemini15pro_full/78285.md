germicidal:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
杀菌的
destructive to germs or other microorganisms; preventing infection.
This hand sanitizer is germicidal and kills 99.9% of bacteria.  这种洗手液具有杀菌作用，可杀死 99.9% 的细菌。
Germicidal UV lamps are used to sterilize equipment.  杀菌紫外线灯用于对设备进行消毒。
synonyms:  antibacterial, antimicrobial, antiseptic, disinfectant
antonyms:  non-germicidal, septic

Etymology:
From germ + -cidal (from Latin -cida, "killer," from caedere, "to kill").
