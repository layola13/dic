hydraemia: /haɪˈdriːmiə/ | n. | 水血症，血浆过多 | An excess of water in the blood plasma, resulting in a decreased concentration of red blood cells and other blood components.  | The patient presented with hydraemia after excessive fluid intake.  病人因摄入过多的液体而出现水血症。 |  Hydraemia can be a complication of certain medical conditions. 水血症可能是某些疾病的并发症。| synonyms: hypervolemia, hemodilution | antonyms: hypovolemia, hemoconcentration | noun
