calculate: /ˈkælkjuleɪt/ (US), /ˈkælkjʊleɪt/ (UK)
verb
计算
determine (the amount or number of something) mathematically.
例句：He carefully calculated the cost of the project. 他仔细计算了项目的成本。

例句：We need to calculate the risks involved. 我们需要计算涉及的风险。

近义词：compute, determine, figure, estimate
反义词：guess, approximate
词性：v.
