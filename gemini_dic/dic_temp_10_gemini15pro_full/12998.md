astonishingness:/əˈstɒnɪʃɪŋnɪs/ | n. | 令人惊讶的程度；惊人 | The astonishingness of the discovery left the scientists speechless. |  The sheer scale of the project is breathtaking in its astonishingness. | 近义词：amazement, wonder, surprisingness | 反义词：ordinariness, commonness, mundanity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From astonishing + -ness.  Astonishing itself comes from the verb astonish, which is derived from Old French estonner "to stun, daze," from Vulgar Latin *extonare "to thunder at," from Latin ex- "out" + tonare "to thunder."
