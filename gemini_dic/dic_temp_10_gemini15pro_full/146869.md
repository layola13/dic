overinflation:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃən/| n.|过度膨胀；通货膨胀过度|A sustained increase in the general price level of goods and services in an economy at a rate that exceeds normal levels.  It typically occurs when the money supply is growing faster than the economy's capacity to produce goods and services.|The Weimar Republic in Germany experienced hyperinflation, a severe form of overinflation, in the early 1920s. 德国魏玛共和国在20世纪20年代初经历了恶性通货膨胀，这是一种严重的过度膨胀。

Overinflation can lead to a decline in the purchasing power of money. 过度膨胀会导致货币购买力下降。|近义词：hyperinflation, excessive inflation, runaway inflation|反义词: deflation, disinflation|词性:noun
