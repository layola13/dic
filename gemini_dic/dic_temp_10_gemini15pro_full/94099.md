impeach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/| vt.|弹劾，控告，怀疑|To charge (a public official) with misconduct in office.|The House of Representatives voted to impeach the president. 众议院投票弹劾总统。|Synonyms: accuse, indict, charge, censure|Antonyms: exonerate, acquit|verb

impeach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/| n.|弹劾，控告|The act of impeaching.|The impeachment trial lasted for weeks. 弹劾审判持续了数周。|Synonyms: accusation, indictment, charge|Antonyms: exoneration, acquittal|noun
