ethopoetic:/ˌeθoʊpoʊˈetɪk/
adjective
人种诗学的；民族诗学的；描绘民族性格的
Relating to or characterized by the representation of a particular ethnic group or culture in literature or art, often focusing on their customs, beliefs, and values.  It emphasizes the imaginative recreation of a culture's ethos or character.
Ethopoetic literature vividly portrays the spirit of a people. 人种诗学的文学作品生动地描绘了一个民族的精神。
The anthropologist's study used an ethopoetic approach to understand the tribe's worldview.  这位人类学家运用人种诗学的方法来理解该部落的世界观。
近义词: cultural, ethnographic, anthropological
反义词: ahistorical, universal
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *ethos* (character, custom) + *poietikos* (poetic, creative).
