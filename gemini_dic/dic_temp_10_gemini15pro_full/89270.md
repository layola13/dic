homocategoric:/ˌhɒmoʊˌkætəˈɡɒrɪk/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˌkætəˈɡɔːrɪk/ | adj. |同类的，同范畴的 | Belonging to the same category or class. | Example: The study focused on homocategoric stimuli, specifically comparing different shades of blue.  这项研究集中在同类刺激物上，特别是比较不同深浅的蓝色。

| Example: Homocategoric responses were analyzed to identify patterns in participant behavior. 对同类反应进行了分析，以确定参与者行为的模式。 | Near synonyms: similar, analogous, comparable, like, uniform, homogeneous | Antonyms: different, dissimilar, heterogeneous, diverse, varied | Word type: Adjective
