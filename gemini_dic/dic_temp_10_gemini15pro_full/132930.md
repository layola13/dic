nonelusiveness:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪvnəs/
n.
不可回避性; 明确性; 显而易见
The quality of being easy to find, understand, or define; not elusive.
The nonelusiveness of the evidence made the case easy to solve.  证据的明确性使得案件很容易解决。
Synonyms: clarity, obviousness, directness, explicitness,  unambiguity, perspicuity, intelligibility, transparency
Antonyms: elusiveness, obscurity, vagueness, ambiguity,  indirectness, impenetrability, incomprehensibility
