franc: /fræŋk/ | n. | 法郎 |A former monetary unit of France and several other countries, replaced by the euro in 2002. Also, the name of the currency used in Switzerland and several African countries. |  I exchanged my dollars for francs. 我把美元兑换成了法郎。

She paid for the souvenir in Swiss francs. 她用法郎支付了纪念品的费用。| synonym: currency, money | antonym: (No direct antonym for a currency unit) | noun
