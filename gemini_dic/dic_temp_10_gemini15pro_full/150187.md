注意事项:

词源：From French pancarte, from Italian pancarta ‘notice fixed to canvas’, from panno ‘cloth’ (from Latin pannus) + carta ‘paper, card’.

pancarte主要用于英式英语，美式英语更常用placard, banner, sign等词。
