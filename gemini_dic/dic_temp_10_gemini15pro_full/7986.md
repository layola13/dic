anthologising:/ænˈθɒlədʒaɪzɪŋ/
verb
编选；选集；收入选集
The process of compiling an anthology, which is a collection of selected literary works, such as poems, short stories, or plays.
She is anthologising poems for a new collection. 她正在为一部新的诗集选编诗歌.
compiling, collecting, curating
excluding, omitting
v.
