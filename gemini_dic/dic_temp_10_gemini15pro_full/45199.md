culiciform:/ˌkjuːlɪˈsɪfɔːrm/ | adj. |蚊形的，像蚊子的|Resembling or related to mosquitoes.  Having a slender body and long legs, like a mosquito.|The culiciform larvae were found in the stagnant water.  这些蚊形幼虫是在死水中发现的。|Near synonyms: mosquito-like, gnat-like|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could use antonyms of mosquito traits like "robust," "thick-bodied")|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *culiciformis*, from *culex* (gnat, mosquito) + *-formis* (having the form of). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱu̯el-* (to sting, prick).
