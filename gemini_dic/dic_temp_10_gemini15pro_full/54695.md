disorganised:/dɪsˈɔːɡənaɪzd/ (UK), /dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|杂乱无章的，组织混乱的|Lacking organization or order; messy and inefficient.|例句：Her desk was always disorganised, covered in papers and pens. 她的桌子总是乱七八糟的，上面堆满了纸和笔。|近义词：messy, cluttered, chaotic, disordered|反义词：organised, orderly, tidy, neat|词性:形容词

disorganized:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|杂乱无章的，组织混乱的|Lacking organization or order; messy and inefficient.|例句：The disorganised event was a complete disaster. 这场组织混乱的活动完全是一场灾难。|近义词：messy, cluttered, chaotic, disordered|反义词：organized, orderly, tidy, neat|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + organised/organized (meaning "arranged in a systematic way").  The root word "organize" ultimately comes from the Greek word "organon" meaning "tool" or "instrument."
