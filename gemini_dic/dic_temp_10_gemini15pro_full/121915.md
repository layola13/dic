misserve:/ˌmɪsˈsɜːrv/
verb
服务不周；亏待；损害
to serve badly or inadequately; to fail to serve the needs of
The government has miserved the public by failing to invest in education. 政府因未能投资教育而亏待了公众。
The company miserved its customers by selling them faulty products.  这家公司通过销售有缺陷的产品来损害客户的利益。
近义词: harm, damage, hurt, prejudice, disadvantage, fail, neglect
反义词: serve, aid, assist, help, support, benefit

词性: v.
