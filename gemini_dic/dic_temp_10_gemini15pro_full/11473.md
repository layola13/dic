argy:/ˈɑːrɡi/| n.|争吵，争论|A quarrel or disagreement; a heated discussion.|例句：They had a big argy about money. 他们就钱的问题大吵了一架。|例句：Don't start an argy with me, I'm in no mood. 别跟我吵，我没心情。|近义词：argument, quarrel, dispute, squabble|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord|词性：名词

argy:/ˈɑːrɡi/| v.|争吵，争论|To quarrel or disagree; to have a heated discussion.|例句：They were argying about who should do the dishes. 他们在争论谁应该洗碗。|例句：Stop argying with me! 别跟我争论了！|近义词：argue, quarrel, dispute, squabble|反义词：agree, harmonize, concur|词性：动词
