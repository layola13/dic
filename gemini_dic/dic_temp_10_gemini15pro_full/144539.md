outvoice:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪs/
verb
压制；盖过…的声音；比…声音更大；使…失声
to speak louder than someone else so that they cannot be heard; to make someone unable to speak.
The singer was outvoiced by the cheering crowd. 歌手的歌声被欢呼的人群盖过了。
The protesters were outvoiced by the police. 抗议者的声音被警察盖过了。
近义词: drown out, overpower, muffle
反义词: amplify, enhance
词性: verb

outvoice:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪs/
noun
(比喻)更响亮的声音；更大的影响力
a louder voice; a greater influence.
The outvoice of the people demanded change. 人民更响亮的声音要求改变。
The small business owners need an outvoice in government. 小企业主需要在政府中有更大的影响力。
近义词: dominance, power, sway
反义词: weakness, impotence
词性: noun
