affranchise:/əˈfræntʃaɪz/
verb
解放，释放；给予选举权
To free from bondage or slavery; to grant the rights of citizenship, especially the right to vote.
The government affranchised the serfs. 政府解放了农奴。
The Nineteenth Amendment affranchised women in the United States.  第十九修正案赋予了美国妇女选举权。
近义词: liberate, emancipate, enfranchise
反义词: enslave, subjugate
词性: verb

affranchise:/əˈfræntʃaɪz/
noun
解放；释放
The act of freeing someone from bondage or slavery.  (This noun form is less common than the verb.)
The affranchisement of the serfs was a major social change.  农奴的解放是一项重大的社会变革。
近义词: liberation, emancipation
反义词: enslavement, subjugation
词性: noun

词源:
