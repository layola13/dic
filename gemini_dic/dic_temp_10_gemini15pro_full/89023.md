hollys:/ˈhɑːliz/ (US) /ˈhɒliz/ (UK)| n.|冬青树，冬青属植物；冬青树枝(尤指用作圣诞节装饰)|Holly refers to a genus of shrubs and trees, often with spiny leaves and red berries, particularly associated with Christmas decorations. It can also refer to the branches of these trees used decoratively.|The carolers were singing under the hollys.  唱诗班的人在冬青树下唱歌。|Holly branches are a popular Christmas decoration. 冬青树枝是受欢迎的圣诞装饰。|Synonyms: holly tree, Ilex|Antonyms: None|Noun
