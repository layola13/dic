endorphins:/ɛnˈdɔːrfɪnz/ /ɛnˈdɔrˌfɪnz/
n.
内啡肽
Any of a group of hormones secreted within the brain and nervous system and having a number of physiological functions. They are peptides which activate the body's opiate receptors, causing an analgesic effect.
Endorphins are released during exercise, excitement, pain, and sexual activity, and they produce a feeling of well-being or even euphoria.
内啡肽是在大脑和神经系统内分泌的一组激素，具有多种生理功能。它们是激活身体阿片受体的肽，从而产生镇痛作用。 内啡肽在运动、兴奋、疼痛和性活动期间释放，它们会产生幸福感甚至欣快感。

Endorphins are released by the body during exercise.
运动时，身体会释放内啡肽。

The runner's high is caused by a release of endorphins.
跑步者的兴奋感是由内啡肽的释放引起的。

近义词: enkephalins, opioid peptides
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) pain-inducing substances
