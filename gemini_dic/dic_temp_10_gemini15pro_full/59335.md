earlier:/ˈɜːrliər/ /ˈɝːliər/| adj. adv.|更早的，较早的；早些时候|Referring to a time or period before the present or before a specified time.|I arrived earlier than expected. 我比预期更早到达。|近义词：sooner, previously, formerly|反义词：later, afterward, subsequently|词性：形容词/副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *erlier*, comparative of *erli* "early," from Old English *ærlice* "early," from *ær* "before, ere" (from Proto-Germanic *air* "before," from Proto-Indo-European *ayer-) + *-lice* adverbial suffix.
