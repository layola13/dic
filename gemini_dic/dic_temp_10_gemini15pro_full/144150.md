outshaped:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪpt/
verb
胜过…的形状; 比…更有型
surpass in shape; be superior to in shape
The new sports car outshaped all its competitors. 这款新型跑车比所有竞争对手的造型都更胜一筹。
近义词: outclass, surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词: be inferior to, be worse than
词性: verb (动词)

词源:  "out-" (超越) + "shaped" (成形的).  The prefix "out-" often indicates surpassing or exceeding something.
