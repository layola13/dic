anchylotic:/ˌæŋkɪˈlɒtɪk/ | adj. |强直的，僵硬的 |Relating to or suffering from ankylosis; stiff or fixed.  | Example: The anchylotic joints made movement difficult. 强直的关节使活动变得困难。 | Example:  His fingers were anchylotic due to arthritis. 他的手指因为关节炎而僵硬。 | Near synonyms: stiff, rigid, immobile, fixed | Antonyms: flexible, mobile, supple | Adjective

anchylosis:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊsɪs/ | n. |强直，僵硬 |Stiffness or fixation of a joint. | Example: Ankylosis of the knee made it impossible for him to bend his leg. 膝盖强直使他无法弯曲腿。| Example:  She suffered from ankylosis in her spine after the accident.  事故发生后，她的脊椎僵硬了。 | Near synonyms: stiffness, rigidity, immobility | Antonyms: flexibility, mobility | Noun
