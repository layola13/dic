counterstream:/ˈkaʊntərˌstriːm/ | n. |逆流，反向潮流|A current flowing in a direction opposite to that of another current, especially a main current.|Salmon swim upstream against the counterstream.  鲑鱼逆流而上，对抗着反向的水流。|The counterstream of public opinion made it difficult to implement the new policy.  公众舆论的逆流使得新政策难以实施。|近义词：backflow, reverse current, upstream |反义词:downstream, current |词性:名词
