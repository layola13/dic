bumptiousness:/ˌbʌmpʃəsnəs/| n. |傲慢；自大；自以为是 |The quality of being bumptious; offensively self-assertive. |His bumptiousness alienated many of his colleagues. 他的傲慢疏远了他的许多同事。|Synonyms: arrogance, conceit, pomposity, swagger, presumptuousness |Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence, unassumingness| noun


Etymology->
From bumptious + -ness.  Bumptious itself comes from bump (meaning "to strike or collide with")  with the suffix -ious, suggesting an overbearing and intrusive manner.
