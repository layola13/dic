alow:/əˈlaʊ/ | v. | 允许，准许 | To permit; to grant; to allow someone to do something. |  例句：My parents allowed me to go to the party. 我的父母允许我去参加派对。|  例句：The rules allow for some flexibility. 规则允许一些灵活性。| 近义词：permit, authorize, grant | 反义词：forbid, prohibit, deny | 词性: 动词


词源:
From Middle English *alowien, from Old French aloer, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + louer (from Latin laudare ‘to praise’).  Originally meant "to praise, commend, approve," the sense shifted to "permit, allow" in Middle English.
