mourners:/ˈmɔːrnərz/| n.| 哀悼者；送葬者 |People who mourn the death of someone.  |  Many mourners attended the funeral. 许多哀悼者参加了葬礼。 |  grievers, sympathizers |  celebrators | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mourneres*, from Anglo-Norman *murnur*, from Old French *murneor*, agent noun from *murnir* (“to mourn”), from Vulgar Latin *mūrināre*, from Latin *mūriāre*, present active infinitive of *mūriō* (“I die”).
