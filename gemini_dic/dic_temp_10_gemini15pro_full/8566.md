anticipations:/ænˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃənz/
n.
预期，期望；预料；盼望
The act of anticipating or the state of being anticipated.  The feeling of looking forward to something.
例句：
His anticipations of the trip were high. 他对这次旅行的期望很高。
The children's anticipations for Christmas were palpable. 孩子们对圣诞节的期待 palpable.

近义词：expectations, prospects, hopes, forecasts
反义词：dreads, fears, apprehensions

词性: 名词 (plural)

词源: From Late Latin anticipationem, from Latin anticipatus, past participle of anticipare “to take beforehand, anticipate," from ante “before” + capere “to take.”
