hermeneutist:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪst/| n.|释经家，解释者|A specialist in interpretation, especially of the Bible or literary texts.  A hermeneutist analyzes the meaning and significance of texts, considering their historical, cultural, and linguistic context.|The renowned hermeneutist offered a new interpretation of the ancient scripture.  这位著名的释经家对古代经文提出了新的解释。|Synonyms: interpreter, exegete, analyst, expounder|Antonyms: misinterpreter, distorter|noun
