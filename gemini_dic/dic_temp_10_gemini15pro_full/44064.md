crinitory:/ˈkrɪnɪˌtɔːri/ /ˈkrɪnɪˌtɔːri/

Covered with hair; hairy.
例句：The crinitory mammoth roamed the icy plains. 多毛猛犸象漫游在冰封的平原上。

例句：The old man had a surprisingly crinitory back.  这位老人有着令人惊讶的多毛的后背。

近义词：hairy, pilose, hirsute
反义词：bald, hairless, glabrous
词性：adjective


crinitory:/ˈkrɪnɪˌtɔːri/ /ˈkrɪnɪˌtɔːri/
noun
毛发，鬃毛
A mass of hair; mane.  (This usage is less common than the adjectival form.)
例句：The lion shook its magnificent crinitory. 狮子摇动着它壮丽的鬃毛。

例句：The horse's crinitory flowed in the wind. 马的鬃毛在风中飘扬。


近义词：hair, mane, fur
反义词: baldness
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *crinitorius, from crinis ("hair").
