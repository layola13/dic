noncreditors:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɛdɪtərz/ | n. | 非债权人 |  People or entities who are not owed money by a particular individual or organization. They do not have a claim against the assets of a debtor. |  Example: The noncreditors were not involved in the bankruptcy proceedings. 非债权人没有参与破产程序。 | Synonym: non-claimants | Antonym: creditors | noun
