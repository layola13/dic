overimitative:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
过度模仿的
Given to excessive imitation; tending to copy or mimic beyond what is necessary or appropriate.
Young children are often overimitative, copying even irrelevant actions.  幼儿经常过度模仿，甚至会模仿不相关的动作。
Toddlers can be overimitative, mimicking behaviors they see without understanding their purpose.  幼儿可能过度模仿，模仿他们看到的行为而不理解其目的。
近义词:  mimicking, copying, emulative,  apelike
反义词:  original, independent, innovative
词性: adjective

overimitation: /ˌoʊvərˌɪmɪˈteɪʃən/
noun
过度模仿
The act of imitating excessively; copying behavior beyond what is necessary or appropriate.
Overimitation in children can be a valuable learning strategy, but it can also lead to inefficient behavior.  儿童的过度模仿可能是一种有价值的学习策略，但也可能导致低效的行为。
Researchers have studied overimitation to understand how children learn social conventions.  研究人员研究了过度模仿，以了解儿童如何学习社会习俗。
近义词: mimicry, copying, emulation
反义词: originality, independence, innovation
词性: noun

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "imitative" (adjective from "imitate," derived from Latin "imitari")
