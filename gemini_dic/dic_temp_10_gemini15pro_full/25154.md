buckram:/ˈbʌkrəm/| n. adj.|粗硬棉布，粗硬的|A coarse, stiff cloth made of cotton or linen, sized with glue.  Figuratively, something inflexible or unyielding.|The book was bound in buckram. 这本书是用粗硬棉布装订的。|近义词：canvas, linen, stiff, rigid|反义词:flexible, pliable|noun
buckram:/ˈbʌkrəm/| adj.|粗硬的，僵硬的|Stiff and unyielding, like buckram cloth. Often used figuratively to describe a person's demeanor or attitude.|His buckram personality made him difficult to approach. 他僵硬的个性让他难以接近。|近义词：stiff, rigid, inflexible|反义词:flexible, pliable, relaxed|adjective
