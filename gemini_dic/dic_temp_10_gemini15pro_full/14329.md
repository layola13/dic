automatous:/ɔːˈtɒmətəs/ (US) /ɔːˈtɑːmətəs/| adj.|自动的；自主的；自发的|Acting or operating independently of external control; self-governing.|例句：The factory is now fully automatous, requiring minimal human intervention.  这家工厂现在完全自动化，只需要很少的人工干预。

例句：Many biological processes are automatous, occurring without conscious thought. 许多生物过程是自主的，无需有意识的思考。|近义词：automatic, self-acting, self-regulating, independent, autonomous|反义词：manual, controlled, dependent|词性: 形容词
