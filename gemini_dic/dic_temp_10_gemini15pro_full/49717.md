democratic: /ˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 民主的，民主主义的；大众的 | Relating to or supporting democracy or its principles.  Characterized by or advocating equal rights and opportunities for all citizens.  | Example:  The United States is a democratic republic. 美国是一个民主共和国。

Example:  They held a democratic election. 他们举行了一次民主选举。 | Synonym: egalitarian, representative, popular | Antonym: authoritarian, totalitarian, dictatorial | adjective
