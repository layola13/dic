exceptant:/ɪkˈspektənt/
adjective
期待的，怀孕的
expecting something, especially something good, or pregnant
例句：The expectant crowd waited for the concert to begin.  期待的人群等待着音乐会的开始。

例句：The expectant mother felt the baby kick. 准妈妈感到婴儿踢了一下。

近义词：expecting, pregnant, hopeful, anticipating
反义词：uncertain, doubtful, pessimistic
