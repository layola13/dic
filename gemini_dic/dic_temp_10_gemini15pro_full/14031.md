ausi: /ˈɔːsi/ | n. | 澳大利亚人；澳洲人 | An informal term for an Australian person. |  Example:  He's an Aussie through and through. 他是个地地道道的澳大利亚人。

Example:  Many Aussies enjoy surfing. 许多澳大利亚人喜欢冲浪。

| 近义词: Australian | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a nationality) | 词性: 名词


Etymology: Shortened from "Australian," influenced by the tendency in Australian English to shorten words and add the "-ie" or "-y" suffix.
