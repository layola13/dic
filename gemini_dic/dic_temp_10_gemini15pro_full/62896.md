enlighteningly:/ɪnˈlaɪtnɪŋli/
adverb
启发地；使人领悟地
in a way that provides insight or understanding.
The speaker enlighteningly explained the complex scientific theory. 演讲者以启迪人心的方式解释了复杂的科学理论。
Synonyms: informatively, instructively, revealingly
Antonyms: confusingly, obscurely, misleadingly
词性: 副词
