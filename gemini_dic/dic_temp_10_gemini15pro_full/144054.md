outrivalling:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过
surpassing or exceeding in rivalry
China's tech industry is outrivalling many Western companies in innovation.  中国科技行业在创新方面正在超过许多西方公司。
The new company is outrivalling its competitors in sales. 这家新公司在销售方面胜过其竞争对手。

outrival:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəl/
