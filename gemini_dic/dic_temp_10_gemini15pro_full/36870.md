colorant:/ˈkʌlərənt/| n. |着色剂，染色剂|A substance that is used to give something a particular color. |例句：The food contains natural colorants.  这种食物含有天然色素。

例句：This colorant is safe for use in cosmetics.  这种着色剂可安全用于化妆品。|近义词：dye, pigment, tint, stain|反义词:bleach|词性:noun
