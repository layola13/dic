osteopathy: /ˌɒstiˈɒpəθi/ (UK), /ˌɑːstiˈɑːpəθi/ (US)| n.|整骨疗法|A system of medicine based on the belief that the body is a unified whole and that the musculoskeletal system plays a crucial role in health and disease. It emphasizes manual techniques to diagnose and treat structural problems.|She's undergoing osteopathy for her back pain. 她正在接受整骨疗法治疗背痛。|近义词: chiropractic, manipulative therapy|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps conventional medicine)|词性: noun
