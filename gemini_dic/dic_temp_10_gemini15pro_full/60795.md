electronegativity:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌnɛɡəˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 电负性 | A measure of the tendency of an atom to attract a bonding pair of electrons. | Fluorine has the highest electronegativity of all the elements. 氟是所有元素中电负性最高的。| synonyms: electron affinity | antonyms: electropositivity | noun
