diamagnetically:/ˌdaɪəˌmæɡˈnɛtɪkli/| adv.| 反磁性地 | In a diamagnetic manner; exhibiting diamagnetism.  指一种物质在磁场中被磁化的方式，即产生与外加磁场方向相反的磁化，从而被外加磁场排斥。 |  Bismuth is known to behave diamagnetically. 铋的磁性是反磁性的。|  The material responded diamagnetically to the applied magnetic field. 这种材料对外加磁场表现出反磁性。 | 近义词: nonmagnetically (并非完全等同，但可以表示类似的弱磁性) | 反义词: paramagnetically, ferromagnetically | 词性: 副词


Etymology:

dia- (Greek) through, across + magnetic + -ally (adverbial suffix)
