figure-hugging:/ˈfɪɡjər ˌhʌɡɪŋ/| adj.|紧身的，贴身的|Close-fitting, following the contours of the body.|She wore a figure-hugging dress to the party. 她穿着一件紧身连衣裙去参加派对.|近义词：tight-fitting, close-fitting, bodycon, skintight|反义词:loose-fitting, baggy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"figure" (from Old French "figure" and Latin "figura") meaning "shape, form, figure" + "hugging" (present participle of "hug") meaning "embracing closely".  The term emphasizes the garment's close adherence to the body's shape.
