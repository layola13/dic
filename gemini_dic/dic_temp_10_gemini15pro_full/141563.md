oppositeness:/ˈɒpəzɪtnəs/ | n. | 对立性，相反性 | The state or quality of being opposite or contrary. | The oppositeness of their views caused a lot of conflict. 他们的观点的截然相反导致了很多冲突。 | synonyms: contrariety, antithesis, contradiction, inverse, reverse | antonyms: similarity, sameness, likeness, resemblance | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From opposite + -ness.  "Opposite" comes from the Old French "oposite" and ultimately from the Latin "oppositus," the past participle of "opponere" (to place against, oppose). The suffix "-ness" is a native English suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
