fusteric: /ˈfʌstərɪk/
adjective
狂乱的，慌乱的
Being in a state of agitated excitement or confusion.
例句：He became fusteric when he realized he had lost his passport. 他意识到护照丢了之后变得慌乱起来。

例句：The sudden noise made the animals fusteric. 突如其来的噪音使动物们狂乱不安。

近义词：frantic, agitated, distraught, frenetic
反义词：calm, composed, tranquil
词性：adjective
