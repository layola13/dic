hero-worship:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˌwɜːrʃɪp/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊ ˌwɜːʃɪp/ (UK)| n. | 英雄崇拜，偶像崇拜 |The excessive admiration of someone, often a public figure, treating them as a hero.| He has a strong hero-worship for his favorite athlete. 他对他最喜欢的运动员非常崇拜。| 近义词: idolatry, adulation | 反义词: disdain, contempt | 词性: noun


Etymology:  "hero" + "worship". "Hero" comes from the Greek "hērōs," meaning "demi-god" or "warrior." "Worship" comes from Old English "weorþscipe," meaning "worthiness" or "honor."
