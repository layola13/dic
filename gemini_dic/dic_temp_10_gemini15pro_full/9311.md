antipodal:/ænˈtɪpədəl/ | adj. | 正相反的，对跖的 | Situated on opposite sides of the earth; diametrically opposed. |  Australia is antipodal to England. 澳大利亚与英国在地球的两端。 | synonyms: opposite, contrary, diametrically opposed | antonyms: similar, identical, same | adjective

antipodal:/ænˈtɪpədəl/ | n. | 对跖点 | A thing or person that is the direct opposite of another. (Less common usage) | The North Pole and the South Pole are antipodals. 北极和南极是对跖点。| synonyms: opposite, counterpart | antonyms: same, identical | noun
