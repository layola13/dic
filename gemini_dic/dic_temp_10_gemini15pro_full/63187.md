Etymology:
From entangle + -ness.  "Entangle" comes from the Old French word "entanglier," meaning "to ensnare or trap," from "en-" (in) + "tangler" (to tangle).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
