afforesting: /əˈfɔːrɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 造林，植树造林 | The act of establishing a forest on land that has not been forested for a long time, or which has never been forested. |  The government is afforesting large areas of the country. 政府正在给这个国家的大片土地造林。|  They are afforesting the bare hills to prevent soil erosion.  他们正在光秃秃的山上植树造林，以防止水土流失。 |  reforesting, planting | deforestation, clearing | verb
