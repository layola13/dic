die-hard:/ˈdaɪhɑːrd/ | n. |顽固分子；死硬派 | A person who strongly opposes change or who is extremely loyal to a particular belief or group. | The die-hards refused to accept the new rules. 顽固分子拒绝接受新规则。 | Near Synonyms:  fanatic, zealot, extremist | Antonyms:  moderate, reformer | noun
