nonfindings:/ˌnɒnˈfaɪndɪŋz/
noun
未发现; 负面结果; 无发现
The absence of something expected, or the failure to discover something sought.  Specifically, in research or investigation, the result that indicates the absence of a particular effect, phenomenon, or substance.
The study's nonfindings regarding the link between coffee and heart disease were surprising. 这项关于咖啡与心脏病之间联系的研究未发现任何关联，结果令人惊讶。
synonyms: null results, negative results, lack of findings, absence of evidence
antonyms: findings, discoveries, positive results
n.
