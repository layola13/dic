ottrelite:/ˈɒtrɪˌlaɪt/ | n. |奥特莱石|A manganese-rich chlorite group mineral, typically occurring in metamorphic rocks. It is a silicate mineral with the chemical formula (Mn,Mg,Fe)3Al2(Si3O10)(OH)8.|The geologist identified the green mineral as ottrelite. 地质学家将这种绿色矿物鉴定为奥特莱石。|Synonyms: None (specific mineral name)|Antonyms: None|Noun
