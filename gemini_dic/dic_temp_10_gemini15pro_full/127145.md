nattery:/ˈnætəri/
adjective
唠叨的，爱闲聊的
Given to excessive or frivolous chatter; talkative, especially about unimportant matters.
She's always so nattery on the phone, I can never get a word in edgewise. 她总是在电话里唠叨个不停，我一句话都插不上。
近义词:chatty, talkative, gossipy, loquacious
反义词:quiet, reserved, taciturn
词性:adjective

nattery:/ˈnætəri/
noun
闲聊，唠叨
Trivial or frivolous conversation; chatter.
I couldn't concentrate with all the nattery going on in the background.  我无法集中注意力，因为背景里一直有人闲聊。
近义词:chatter, gossip, prattle
反义词:silence, quiet
词性:noun
