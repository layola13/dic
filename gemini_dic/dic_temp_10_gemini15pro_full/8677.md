anticonventionalists:/ˌæntiˌkənˈvɛnʃənəlɪsts/
| n. |反传统主义者|People who oppose or reject established conventions, norms, or traditions.  Those who challenge the status quo and advocate for alternative ways of thinking or behaving.|The anticonventionalists in the art world often faced criticism from the established galleries.|近义词：nonconformists, rebels, mavericks, iconoclasts, radicals|反义词:conformists, traditionalists|noun
