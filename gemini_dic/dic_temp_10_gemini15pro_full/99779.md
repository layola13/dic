intermezzo:/ˌɪntərˈmetsoʊ/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈmetsəʊ/ (UK)| n.|间奏曲，幕间表演；加餐|A short piece of entertainment performed between the acts of a play or opera, or a short course served between the main courses of a meal.  It can also refer to a short period of time between two events.|The orchestra played a delightful intermezzo between the first and second acts.  管弦乐队在第一幕和第二幕之间演奏了一段令人愉悦的间奏曲。

The chef surprised us with a refreshing sorbet intermezzo.  厨师用一份清爽的冰糕加餐给我们带来了惊喜。|近义词：interlude, interval, break|反义词:main course, main act|词性:noun
