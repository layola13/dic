overfertile: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrtəl/ | adj. | 过于肥沃的；过度肥沃的 | Excessively fertile; too rich in nutrients for healthy plant growth.  | The overfertile soil caused the plants to grow too quickly and become weak. 过于肥沃的土壤导致植物生长过快，变得虚弱。| The overfertile garden produced an abundance of weeds. 过于肥沃的花园里长满了杂草。 | synonyms:  rich, lush, productive, fecund | antonyms: infertile, barren, sterile | adjective
