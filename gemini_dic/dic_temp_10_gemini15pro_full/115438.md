mantra:/ˈmæntrə/ (US), /ˈmɑːntrə/ (UK)| n. |咒语，真言；准则；口号|A mantra is a word or sound repeated to aid concentration in meditation. It can also refer to a statement or slogan repeated frequently.|She repeated her mantra, "I am strong, I am capable." 她重复着她的咒语：“我很强大，我很能干。”|Synonyms: chant, incantation, slogan, motto|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'silence' in a meditative context)|noun

mantrams:/ˈmæntrəmz/ (US), /ˈmɑːntrəmz/ (UK)| n. |咒语，真言；准则；口号（mantra的复数形式）|The plural form of mantra, referring to multiple words or sounds repeated to aid concentration in meditation, or multiple frequently repeated statements or slogans.|He had several mantrams he used for different situations. 他有几个用于不同情况的咒语。|Synonyms: chants, incantations, slogans, mottoes|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
