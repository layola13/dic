miscategorize:/ˌmɪskætəɡəˈraɪz/ | v. | 错误分类 | To categorize something incorrectly. |  例句：The librarian miscategorized the new biography as fiction. 图书管理员把这本新传记错误地归类为小说。

例句：He miscategorized the expense as a business cost when it was personal. 他把这笔支出错误地归类为商业成本，而实际上是个人支出。

近义词：misclassify, mislabel, misfile | 反义词： categorize, classify, label | 词性：动词
