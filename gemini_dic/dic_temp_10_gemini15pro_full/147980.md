overreward:/ˌoʊvərɪˈwɔːrd/| vt.|过度奖励；过分报酬|To give someone a reward that is excessive or disproportionate to their efforts or achievements.|The company overrewarded its executives with exorbitant bonuses.  这家公司过度奖励其高管，给予了过高的奖金。

The teacher overrewarded the student for a simple task. 老师因为一个简单的任务就过度奖励了这个学生。


|近义词：overcompensate, overpay|反义词: underreward, underpay|词性:动词
