dozes:/ˈdoʊzɪz/| n.|瞌睡，打盹 (复数)|A short light sleep, especially during the day. |The old man often takes dozes in his armchair after lunch. 老人在午饭后经常在他的扶手椅上打瞌睡。|近义词：naps, siestas|反义词:wakefulness|noun

doze:/doʊz/| v.|打瞌睡，打盹|To sleep lightly and briefly, especially during the day. |I dozed off during the boring lecture.  我在无聊的讲座中打瞌睡了。|近义词：nap, slumber|反义词:awaken|verb
