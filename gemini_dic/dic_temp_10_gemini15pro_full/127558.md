necroscopical: /ˌnɛkrəˈskɒpɪkəl/ /ˌnɛkroʊˈskɑːpɪkəl/
adjective
验尸的；尸体解剖的
Relating to or involving a post-mortem examination.
A necroscopical examination revealed the cause of death. 验尸检查揭示了死因。

The necroscopical findings were consistent with drowning. 验尸结果与溺水一致。

synonym:  autopsy, post-mortem
antonym:  N/A (No direct antonym for a descriptive term like this)
