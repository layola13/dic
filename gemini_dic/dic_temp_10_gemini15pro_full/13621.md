atticism:/ˈætɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 雅典风格；雅典式的措辞 |  A concise and elegant expression characteristic of Athenian writers and orators, often marked by wit, irony, or understatement. |  Her writing was praised for its atticism and clarity. 她的写作因其雅典风格和清晰度而受到称赞。 |  The author's atticism made his prose a delight to read.  这位作家的雅典式措辞使他的散文读起来令人愉悦。 |Synonyms: elegance, refinement, wit, conciseness | Antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, verbosity | noun
