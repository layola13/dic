assidean:/ˌæsɪˈdiːən/
n.|哈西典人；虔诚的犹太教徒|A member of a Jewish sect, distinguished by strict observance of traditional and written law, and commonly held to have originated in Maccabean times. |例句：The Assideans joined the Maccabees in their revolt against the Seleucid Empire. 哈西典人加入了马加比起义反抗塞琉古帝国。

例句：Some scholars believe the Pharisees evolved from the Assidean movement. 一些学者认为法利赛人是从哈西典运动演变而来的。|近义词：Hasidim, pious Jew|反义词：Hellenist|词性:名词
