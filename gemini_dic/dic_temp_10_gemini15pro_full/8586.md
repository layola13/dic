anticlassicist:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪst/| n. adj.|反古典主义者，反古典主义的|Someone who opposes or rejects classicism, especially in art, literature, or architecture.  A style that opposes or rejects classicism.|The architect, a staunch anticlassicist, favored brutalist designs.  这位建筑师是一位坚定的反古典主义者，喜欢粗犷主义的设计。|Near synonyms:  iconoclast, avant-gardist, modernist|Near antonyms: classicist, traditionalist|Noun, Adjective
