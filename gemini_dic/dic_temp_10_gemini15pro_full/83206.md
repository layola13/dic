guzzle:/ˈɡʌzl/| v.| 大口地喝，狂饮；狼吞虎咽；(机器或车辆)耗油|To drink or eat something quickly and greedily.|He guzzled down the whole bottle of water after the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛后一口气喝完了一整瓶水。|Synonyms: gulp, chug, devour, consume rapidly|Antonyms: sip, nibble|verb

guzzle:/ˈɡʌzl/| n.| 狂饮；豪饮|An act of guzzling.|He took a long guzzle of beer. 他猛灌了一大口啤酒。|Synonyms: draft, swig|Antonyms: sip|noun
