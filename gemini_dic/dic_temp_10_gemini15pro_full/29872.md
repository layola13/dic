causewaying:/ˈkɔːzweɪɪŋ/
n.|筑堤,铺路,铺设堤道|The causewaying of the swamp allowed for easier access.  沼泽的筑堤使人们更容易进入。|近义词:paving, road building, construction|反义词:excavation, demolition|词性:名词

Etymology: From "causeway" + "-ing" (present participle suffix). "Causeway" comes from the Anglo-French "cauce," meaning "causeway," from the Latin "calciata (via)," meaning "paved (road)."
