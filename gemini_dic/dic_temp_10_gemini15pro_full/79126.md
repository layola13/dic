gladiators:/ˈɡlædiˌeɪtərz/| n. |角斗士（复数）|A Roman armed combatant who entertained the public by fighting other gladiators or wild animals in an arena.|The gladiators fought bravely in the arena. 角斗士们在竞技场上英勇作战。|Synonyms: fighters, combatants, warriors|Antonyms: pacifists, peacemakers|noun
