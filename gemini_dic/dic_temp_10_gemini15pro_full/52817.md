dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/| vt. adj.|稀释，冲淡；削弱，降低|To make a liquid thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent. To reduce the strength, intensity, or value of something.|例句：The artist diluted the paint with water. 艺术家用清水稀释了油漆。|近义词：weaken, thin, reduce, diminish, lessen|反义词:concentrate, strengthen, thicken|词性:verb, adjective

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/| adj.|稀释的，冲淡的|Made thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent.  Lacking strength or force.|例句：The juice was too concentrated, so I added water to make it more dilute. 果汁太浓了，所以我加了水把它稀释一下。|近义词：weak, thin, watered-down|反义词:concentrated, strong, thick|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diluere*, from *di-* (apart, asunder) + *luere* (to wash).
