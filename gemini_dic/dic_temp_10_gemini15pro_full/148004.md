overrigidity:/ˌoʊvərɪˈdʒɪdɪti/| n. |过度僵硬，过度刻板|The state of being excessively rigid, inflexible, or unyielding, often referring to mental or behavioral patterns.|His overrigidity made it difficult for him to adapt to new situations. 他的过度僵硬使他难以适应新环境。|近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness|反义词: flexibility, adaptability|词性:名词
