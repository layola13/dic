clavate:/ˈkleɪveɪt/| adj.|棒状的，棍棒形的|Shaped like a club, thickened towards the distal end.|The fungus had a distinctive clavate fruiting body.  这种真菌有一个独特的棒状子实体。|The stem of the plant is clavate.  这种植物的茎是棒状的。|近义词：club-shaped, thickened, swollen|反义词：tapered, pointed|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *clāvātus, from clāva (“club”).
