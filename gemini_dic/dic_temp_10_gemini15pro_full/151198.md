parchmenty: /ˈpɑːrtʃmənti/ | adj. | 像羊皮纸的；干燥的 | Resembling parchment in texture, color, or dryness. |  The old woman's skin was parchmenty and thin. 老妇人的皮肤像羊皮纸一样又干又薄。 |  The desert wind left his lips feeling parchmenty. 沙漠的风使他的嘴唇感觉干燥如羊皮纸。 | 近义词: dry, leathery, papery | 反义词: soft, moist, supple | 词性: 形容词

词源: From parchment + -y.  "Parchment" itself comes from the Old French "parchemin," ultimately derived from the name of the ancient Greek city Pergamon, where a method of preparing animal skins as writing material was perfected.
