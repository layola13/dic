literariness:/ˈlɪtərərɪnɪs/| n.|文学性；文学性特征|The literariness of the text is evident in its use of metaphor and imagery. 这篇文本的文学性在其使用隐喻和意象方面表现得很明显。|Examples:
1. The professor analyzed the literariness of Shakespeare's sonnets. 教授分析了莎士比亚十四行诗的文学性。
2.  The high degree of literariness makes this novel challenging for young readers.  高度的文学性使这本小说对年轻读者来说具有挑战性。

|Synonyms: artistry, poetic quality, literary merit, refinement |Antonyms: artlessness, simplicity, plainness |noun
