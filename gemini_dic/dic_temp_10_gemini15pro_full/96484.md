indivinity:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪnɪti/| n.|非神性，世俗性|The indivinity of human nature is often contrasted with the divine.|人类的非神性经常与神性形成对比。|例句：

The artist explored the indivinity of everyday life in his paintings.
这位艺术家在他的画作中探索了日常生活的世俗性。

Her focus on the indivinity of the material world led her to a career in science.
她对物质世界非神性的关注使她走上了科学的职业道路。

|近义词：mortality, worldliness, secularity|反义词：divinity, godliness, sacredness|词性:名词


词源:  From in- "not" + divinity.  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "divinity," which refers to the state or quality of being divine.
