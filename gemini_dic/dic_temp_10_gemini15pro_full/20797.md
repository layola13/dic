blarneyer:/ˈblɑːrniər/
n.
奉承者；花言巧语的人
Someone who uses flattering or coaxing talk.
He's such a blarneyer, always buttering up the boss. 他真是个马屁精，总是拍老板的马屁。
flatterer, charmer, smooth talker, sweet talker
critic, skeptic
