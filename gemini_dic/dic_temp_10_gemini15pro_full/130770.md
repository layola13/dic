nonanalytic:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
非解析的
Not able to be analyzed into simpler elements.  Relating to a function that does not have a derivative at some point or in some region. In some contexts, it can also mean not susceptible to or resolvable by analysis.
The function proved to be nonanalytic at the origin.  该函数在原点被证明是非解析的。
