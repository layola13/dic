duluthian: /duːˈluːθiən/
noun
德卢斯人；德卢斯居民
A person who lives in or is from Duluth, Minnesota.
She's a Duluthian, born and raised. 她是一位土生土长的德卢斯人。
近义词: Duluth resident, inhabitant of Duluth
反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps someone not from Duluth)
词性:n.
