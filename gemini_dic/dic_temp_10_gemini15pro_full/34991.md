cloisteral:/ˈklɔɪstərəl/ | adj. | 修道院的；隐居的；与世隔绝的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a cloister; secluded from the world. | The library had a cloisteral atmosphere, quiet and conducive to study. 图书馆有一种修道院的氛围，安静且有利于学习。| The scholar lived a cloisteral life, dedicating his time to research and writing.  这位学者过着隐居的生活，把时间都花在研究和写作上。 | 近义词: monastic, secluded, reclusive, solitary | 反义词: worldly, gregarious, social | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *cloistral, from Old French cloistral, from Medieval Latin claustrālis, from Latin claustrum (“bolt, bar, lock, enclosed place”).
