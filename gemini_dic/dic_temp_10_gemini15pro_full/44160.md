criticising: /ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 批评，批判 |  The act of expressing disapproval of someone or something, pointing out faults or shortcomings. |  He is always criticising my work. 他总是批评我的工作。|  censuring, condemning, denouncing |  praising, commending | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From the French critiquer (to criticize), from Greek kritikos (able to judge), from krinein (to separate, decide, judge).
