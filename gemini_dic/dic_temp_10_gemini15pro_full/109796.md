levying: /ˈlɛviɪŋ/
verb
征收; 徵收
The act of imposing or collecting a tax, fee, or other charge.
The government is levying a new tax on cigarettes. 政府正在对香烟征收新的税。
Synonyms: imposing, collecting, charging
Antonyms: remitting, refunding
