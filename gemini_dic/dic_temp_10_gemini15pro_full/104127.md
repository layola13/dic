jumpy:/ˈdʒʌmpi/ /ˈdʒʌmpi/| adj.|神经质的，易受惊的，跳跃的|Nervous and easily startled; tending to make sudden, jerky movements.|The dog was jumpy after the fireworks.  这只狗在放完烟花后变得很紧张。|The market has been jumpy all week.  整个星期市场都很不稳定。|近义词：nervous, skittish, edgy, restless, apprehensive|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, steady|词性:形容词
词源:From jump + -y.
