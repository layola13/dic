outgross:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊs/
verb
胜过，超过（在收入或利润方面）
to surpass in gross earnings or profits
Example: The new movie outgrossed all previous box office records.  这部新电影的票房收入超过了以往所有的票房纪录。

Example:  His company outgrossed its competitors by a significant margin. 他的公司在总收入上大幅超过了竞争对手。

近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed, outperform
反义词: underperform, lag behind
词性: verb
