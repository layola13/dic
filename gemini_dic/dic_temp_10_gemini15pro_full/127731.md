negligibly:/ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbli/| adv.|微不足道地，可以忽略地|in a way that is so small or unimportant as to be not worth considering; insignificantly|The difference in price was negligibly small. 价格差异微不足道。|
The risk is negligibly small.  这种风险小到可以忽略不计。|
Synonyms: insignificantly, unimportantly, trivially, slightly|Antonyms: significantly, substantially, considerably|adverb
