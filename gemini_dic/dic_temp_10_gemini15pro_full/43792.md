cremia:/ˈkriːmiə/ | n. | 奶油，乳脂，冰淇淋 |A rich, smooth, creamy texture, often used in reference to ice cream or gelato.  It evokes a sense of indulgence and high quality. | This cremia is incredibly smooth and flavorful.  这种奶油冰淇淋口感无比顺滑，味道浓郁。| The gelato had a delightful cremia.  这意式冰淇淋有着令人愉悦的乳脂状口感。 | synonyms: cream, richness, smoothness | antonyms: thinness, wateriness | noun
