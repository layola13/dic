anbury:/ˈænˌbɛri/ | n. | 根瘤病，马铃薯癌肿病 | A plant disease, especially of brassicas such as turnips and cabbages, characterized by swellings on the roots. Also called clubroot.  | The farmer lost a significant portion of his turnip crop due to anbury. 农民因根瘤病损失了很大一部分芜菁作物。 |  The anbury devastated the cabbage patch. 根瘤病摧毁了卷心菜地。 | synonyms: clubroot, finger-and-toe disease | antonyms: plant health | noun
