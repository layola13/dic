candle:/ˈkændl/ (US) /ˈkændl/ (UK)| n.|蜡烛|A cylinder or block of wax or tallow with a central wick that is lit to produce light as it burns.|She lit a candle to create a romantic atmosphere. 她点了一根蜡烛来营造浪漫的气氛。|近义词：taper, light|反义词: darkness|noun
