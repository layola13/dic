awareness:/əˈwɛrnəs/| n.|意识，认识；了解|knowledge or perception of a situation or fact|例句：Public awareness of the problem is growing. 公众对这个问题的意识正在增强。|例句：She had a growing awareness of her own abilities. 她越来越清楚自己的能力。|近义词：consciousness, understanding, perception, knowledge|反义词：ignorance, unawareness|词性:noun
