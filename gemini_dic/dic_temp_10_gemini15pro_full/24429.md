bristled:/ˈbrɪsəld/| v. adj.|竖起（毛发），发怒，|The cat bristled its fur when it saw the dog.  猫看到狗时竖起了毛。|近义词：rise, stand up, bristle up, stiffen|反义词:flatten, smooth, calm, soothe|词性:动词，形容词
bristled:/ˈbrɪsəld/| adj.|有刚毛的，毛发竖立的|The bristled toothbrush is good for cleaning.  硬毛牙刷很适合清洁。|近义词：hairy, prickly, spiky|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bristelden, frequentative of *bristen, from Old English *byrst, meaning "bristle." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *burstiz. Related to Dutch borstel and German Bürste.
