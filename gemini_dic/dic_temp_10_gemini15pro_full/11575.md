aristulate:/əˈrɪstjʊleɪt/ | adj. |  具小芒的；具短尖头的 | Having short, stiff points; minutely barbed. | The leaf margins are aristulate, giving them a slightly rough texture. 叶子边缘具小芒，使它们的质地略显粗糙。 | Near synonyms: barbed, pointed, acuminate, mucronate | Near antonyms: smooth, blunt | Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aristulatus, diminutive of *aristatus, from arista "awn" (of grain), probably from a PIE root meaning "point, ear of corn."
