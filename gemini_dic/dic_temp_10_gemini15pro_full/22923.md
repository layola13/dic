botryolite:/boʊˈtraɪ.oʊˌlaɪt/| n. |葡萄石|A mineral, a hydrous calcium borosilicate, typically occurring in grape-like clusters of small, translucent crystals.|The botryolite specimens from this mine are highly prized by collectors.  这个矿的葡萄石标本受到收藏家的高度重视。|Synonyms: datolite, prehnite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
