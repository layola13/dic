astrobiology:/ˌæstroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 天体生物学 |The study of life in the universe, including its origin, evolution, distribution, and future.  | Astrobiology is a relatively new field of science. 天体生物学是一个相对较新的科学领域。 |  Astrobiology research seeks to answer fundamental questions about the nature of life. 天体生物学研究试图回答关于生命本质的基本问题。|  近义词:exobiology | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
astro- (from Greek astron, meaning "star") + biology (from Greek bios, meaning "life" + -logia, meaning "study of")
