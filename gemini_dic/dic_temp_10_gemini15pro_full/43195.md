crabbily:/ˈkræbɪli/| adv.|暴躁地，乖戾地|In a bad-tempered or irritable way.  Derived from "crabby," meaning irritable, which likely comes from the perceived sideways, grumpy movement of crabs.|She answered crabbily, clearly annoyed by the interruption. 她暴躁地回答，显然是被打扰恼火了。|近义词：grumpily, peevishly, sullenly|反义词：cheerfully, pleasantly|词性:副词
