crupper:/ˈkrʌpər/| n.| (马的) 臀部，尾根；(防止马鞍向前的) 尾带 |A leather strap fastened to the back of a saddle and looped under a horse's tail to prevent the saddle from slipping forward.|The rider tightened the crupper to secure the saddle.|近义词:breeching|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *croupiere, from croupe (“croup”), from a Germanic source. Cognate with Dutch kruiper.
