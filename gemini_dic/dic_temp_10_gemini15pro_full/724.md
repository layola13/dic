absentminded:/ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|心不在焉的，健忘的|Not paying attention to what is happening around you because you are thinking about something else.|He's so absentminded, he's always forgetting where he put his keys. 他太心不在焉了，总是忘记把钥匙放在哪里。

He was so absentminded that he left his umbrella on the bus. 他心不在焉到把伞落在公交车上了。

|近义词：forgetful, preoccupied, inattentive, distracted|反义词：attentive, mindful, alert, focused|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From absent + minded.  "Absent" traces back to the Latin "absens," meaning "being away."  "Minded" comes from the Old English "gemyndig," meaning "mindful."  The combination creates the meaning of having one's mind elsewhere, or not present in the current moment.
