co-sovereignty:/ˌkoʊˈsɑːvrɪnti/
n.
共同主权，共治
Joint sovereignty; shared authority and control over a territory or political entity.
The agreement established co-sovereignty over the island. 该协议确立了该岛的共同主权。
Co-sovereignty allows both nations to exercise control over the region. 共同主权允许两国对该地区行使控制权。

近义词:joint sovereignty, shared rule, dual control
反义词:sole sovereignty, absolute rule, unilateral control
词性:noun
