glucogenesis:/ˌɡluːkoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
糖异生，糖原异生
The formation of glucose within the animal body from precursors other than carbohydrates.
The liver plays a crucial role in glucogenesis.  肝脏在糖异生中起着至关重要的作用。
Gluconeogenesis helps maintain blood glucose levels during fasting. 糖异生有助于在禁食期间维持血糖水平。
近义词:glucose production
反义词:glycolysis
词性:noun
