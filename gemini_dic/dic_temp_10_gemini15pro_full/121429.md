misinforms: /ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmz/ | v. | 误报；提供错误信息 | To give someone wrong information about something. | The article misinforms the public about the true cost of the project. 这篇文章向公众误报了该项目的真实成本。|  The advertisement misinforms consumers about the product's effectiveness.  这则广告误导了消费者对该产品有效性的认知。 | synonyms: misleads, deceives, misguides | antonyms: informs, educates, enlightens | verb
