derailed:/dɪˈreɪld/| adj. |脱轨的，出轨的；偏离正轨的|  Forced off the rails; also, figuratively, diverted from a course or purpose.  | The train was derailed after hitting a cow. 火车撞到一头牛后脱轨了。| The project was derailed by unforeseen circumstances.  这个项目由于不可预见的情况而偏离了正轨。| 近义词: diverted, sidetracked, obstructed, frustrated | 反义词: on track, progressing, focused| 词性:形容词

derailed:/dɪˈreɪld/| v. |(使)脱轨；(使)偏离正轨|(past tense and past participle of derail) To cause to run off the rails; also, figuratively, to obstruct the progress of. |  The investigation revealed that a broken rail derailed the train.  调查显示，一根断裂的铁轨导致火车脱轨。|  The scandal derailed his political career.  这桩丑闻使他的政治生涯脱轨。| 近义词: divert, sidetrack, obstruct, frustrate | 反义词: support, advance, further| 词性:动词
