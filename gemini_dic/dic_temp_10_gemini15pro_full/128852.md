nigua:/niːˈɡwɑː/ | n. |沙蚤，沙蚤病|A tropical flea (Tunga penetrans) that burrows into the skin, especially the feet, causing intense itching and inflammation.| The doctor diagnosed her with nigua after finding the burrowed flea in her foot. 医生在她脚上发现了钻入皮肤的跳蚤后，诊断她患有沙蚤病。|Synonym: chigoe, jigger, sand flea|Antonym: N/A|Noun

nigua:/niːˈɡwɑː/ | n. |沙蚤病|Infestation with or disease caused by the nigua flea.| Nigua is common in tropical and subtropical regions. 沙蚤病在热带和亚热带地区很常见。|Synonym: tungiasis, chigoe infestation, jigger infestation|Antonym: N/A|Noun
