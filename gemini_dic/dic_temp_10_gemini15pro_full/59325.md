earlap:/ˈɪrˌlæp/ (US), /ˈɪəˌlæp/ (UK)| n.|耳垂；帽子的护耳|The part of the ear that hangs down below the earlobe. It is often covered by earmuffs or the flaps of a hat in cold weather.|He pulled his earlap to make sure he heard correctly. 他拉了拉耳垂，以确保自己听得正确。|近义词：earlobe|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
