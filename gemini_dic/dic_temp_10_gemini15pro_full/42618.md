counterreformer:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrmər/ |ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrmər/
n.|反改革者，反对改革的人|A person who opposes reform, especially one who opposed the Protestant Reformation.| The Council of Trent was a key event for the Counter-Reformers. 特伦托会议是反改革者的一个关键事件。|opponent of reform, reactionary|reformer, progressive|noun
counterreformer:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrmər/ |ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrmər/
n.|反宗教改革者|Someone who participated in the Counter-Reformation, a movement within the Catholic Church that responded to the Protestant Reformation.|Many Counter-Reformers sought to revitalize Catholic practices and doctrines. 许多反宗教改革者试图复兴天主教的习俗和教义。|opponent of the Reformation, Catholic reformer|Protestant reformer|noun
