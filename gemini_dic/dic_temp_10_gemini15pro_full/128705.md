nidulite: /ˈnɪdjʊlaɪt/
n.
镍黄铁矿，红砷镍矿
A rare nickel sulfide mineral, Ni₃S₂.
Nidulite is a rare mineral found in sulfide deposits. 红砷镍矿是一种罕见的矿物，发现于硫化物矿床中。
Nidulite was discovered in Bellver de Cerdanya, Spain. 红砷镍矿是在西班牙的贝尔韦德塞尔达尼亚发现的。
近义词:  millerite (similar composition), polydymite (similar composition)
反义词:  N/A
词性: noun
