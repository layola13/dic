photography:/fəˈtɒɡrəfi/ /fəˈtɑːɡrəfi/
n.
摄影；摄影术
The art or practice of taking and processing photographs.
Photography is my passion.  摄影是我的爱好。
He studied photography in college. 他在大学学习摄影。
近义词:  snapshot, picture-taking
反义词:  None (Photography doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性: noun
