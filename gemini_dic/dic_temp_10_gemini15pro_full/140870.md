on-the-run:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈrʌn/ | adj. | 逃亡的，奔波的 | Fleeing from the law or other pursuers; constantly traveling or busy. | The police are looking for a dangerous criminal who is on the run. 警察正在追捕一名在逃的危险罪犯。 | The salesman is always on the run, traveling from city to city.  这个推销员总是在奔波，从一个城市到另一个城市。|近义词：fugitive, fleeing, absconding, at large, in flight, nomadic |反义词: captured, apprehended, settled, stationary | 词性:形容词

on-the-run:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈrʌn/ | adv. | 匆忙地，奔波地 | In a hurried or busy manner, while fleeing.  | She grabbed a quick breakfast on the run. 她匆忙地吃了一顿早餐。 | He's been living on the run ever since he escaped from prison. 自从他越狱以来，他一直过着逃亡的生活。|近义词：hastily, hurriedly, quickly |反义词: leisurely, slowly, calmly | 词性:副词
