hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪn/ /ˈhaɪdroʊˌpleɪn/
n.
水上飞机；水翼船
A seaplane; an airplane designed to take off from and land on water.  A fast motorboat designed to skim over the surface of the water.
The hydroplane skipped across the lake. 水上飞机掠过湖面。
The hydroplane sped across the bay. 水翼船快速驶过海湾。
近义词:seaplane, floatplane
反义词:(None -  There isn't a direct opposite for a vehicle)
词性:noun


hydroplane:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪn/ /ˈhaɪdroʊˌpleɪn/
vi.
(指车辆)在水膜上滑行；打水漂
(of a vehicle) slide uncontrollably on a wet surface.
The car hydroplaned on the wet road. 汽车在湿滑的路面上打滑了。
The tires hydroplaned and the car spun out of control. 轮胎打滑，汽车失控旋转。
近义词:aquaplane, skid
反义词:(None - describes a loss of traction)
词性:verb
