basihyoid:/ˌbeɪsiˈhaɪɔɪd/| n. | 基舌骨 |A small, U-shaped bone located at the base of the tongue in some vertebrates, including humans. It is part of the hyoid apparatus and serves as an attachment point for muscles involved in swallowing and tongue movement. | The basihyoid provides support for the tongue. 基舌骨为舌头提供支撑。| The basihyoid is crucial for proper swallowing function. 基舌骨对于正常的吞咽功能至关重要。 | hyoid bone, lingual bone | N/A | noun
