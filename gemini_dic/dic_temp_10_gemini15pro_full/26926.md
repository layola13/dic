calamitousness:/kəˌlæmɪtəsnəs/
noun
灾难性，不幸
The state or quality of being calamitous; disastrousness.
The calamitousness of the earthquake left the city in ruins. 地震的灾难性使这座城市沦为废墟。
Near synonyms: disastrousness, catastrophicity, tragedy, devastation
Near antonyms: fortunate, lucky, auspicious, blessedness
词性: noun
