fluting:/ˈfluːtɪŋ/ | n. | 笛声；凹槽；竖笛演奏 | The act of playing a flute, or a decorative groove or channel in a surface. |  The fluting on the columns added a touch of elegance to the building.  柱子上的凹槽为建筑增添了一丝优雅。 | synonyms: grooving, channeling, piping | antonyms:  protrusion, bulge | noun

fluting:/ˈfluːtɪŋ/ | n. | 褶裥；褶饰 | A strip of gathered or pleated material used for ornamentation. | The dress featured delicate fluting around the neckline. 这件连衣裙在领口周围有精致的褶饰。 | synonyms:  ruffling, gathering, pleating | antonyms: smoothing, flattening | noun
