nonagreements:/ˌnɒnəˈɡriːmənts/
n.
中文翻译：不一致，分歧，未达成协议
英文解析：lack of agreement; failure to agree; instances of disagreement.  Plural form of nonagreement.
例句：The nonagreements between the two parties prevented the deal from closing.  双方之间的分歧阻止了交易的完成。

例句：Numerous nonagreements in the contract need to be resolved before signing.  合同中的许多不一致之处需要在签署前解决。
近义词：disagreements, discrepancies, conflicts, disputes
反义词：agreements, accords, consensus, harmony
词性：名词(plural noun)

nonagreement:/ˌnɒnəˈɡriːmənt/
n.
中文翻译：不一致，分歧，未达成协议
英文解析：lack of agreement; failure to reach an agreement.
例句：The nonagreement on the budget led to a government shutdown. 预算上的分歧导致了政府停摆。

例句：There was a nonagreement about the terms of the contract.  对于合同条款存在分歧。
近义词：disagreement, discord, difference, variance
反义词：agreement, accord, concord, harmony
词性：名词 (noun)
