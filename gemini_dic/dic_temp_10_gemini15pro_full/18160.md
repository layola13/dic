bejade:/bɪˈdʒeɪd/ | v. | 使疲惫；使厌烦；使失去光泽 | To tire or weary, especially by making someone do something repeatedly; to make someone lose interest or enthusiasm. Also, to make something appear dull or less attractive. |  The endless paperwork bejaded him.  没完没了的文书工作使他厌倦了。| synonyms: weary, tire, exhaust, bore, jade, disillusion | antonyms: energize, excite, refresh, invigorate | verb
