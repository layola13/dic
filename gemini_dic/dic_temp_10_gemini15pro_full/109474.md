lepra:/ˈlɛprə/| n.|麻风病|A chronic infectious disease caused by Mycobacterium leprae, affecting the skin, nerves, and mucous membranes and characterized by the formation of nodules.|Lepra is a debilitating disease that can cause permanent disfigurement. 麻风病是一种使人衰弱的疾病，可造成永久性毁容。|Synonyms: leprosy, Hansen's disease|Antonyms: health|noun
