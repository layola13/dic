parfocal:/pɑːrˈfoʊkəl/| adj.| 等焦的，齐焦的|  Remaining in focus when the magnification is changed.  When one objective lens of a microscope is in focus, the other objectives will also be in focus. |Example 1: The parfocal lenses allowed the pathologist to quickly switch between magnifications without readjusting the focus. 病理学家可以使用等焦镜头快速切换放大倍率而无需重新调整焦距。

Example 2:  A good quality microscope will have parfocal objectives, making observations much more efficient.  高质量的显微镜将具有等焦物镜，使观察更加有效。| 近义词:  isofocal| 反义词:  non-parfocal|词性:形容词
