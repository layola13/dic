generality:/ˌdʒɛnəˈrælɪti/ | n. | 普遍性，概括性，通则；笼统，泛泛之谈 |  The quality or state of being general; a general principle, statement, or concept. |  He spoke in generalities, avoiding specific details. 他泛泛而谈，避免提及具体细节。 | synonyms: abstraction, generality, vague statement, sweeping statement | antonyms: specificity, particularity, detail | noun

generality:/ˌdʒɛnəˈrælɪti/ | n. | 大多数，大部分 |  The greater part or number; the majority. | The generality of people support the new policy. 大多数人支持这项新政策。  | synonyms: majority, bulk, mass | antonyms: minority, few | noun
