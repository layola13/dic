cramming:/ˈkræmɪŋ/| n.|填鸭式学习，突击学习|The act of trying to learn a lot of information in a short amount of time, especially just before an exam.|He was cramming for his history exam all night. 他整晚都在突击准备历史考试。|近义词：swotting, revising, studying intensively|反义词: relaxing, procrastinating|Noun
