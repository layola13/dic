commensuratenesses:/kəˈmɛnʃərɪtnɪsɪz/
|n.|相称性；相当性；相等性|The state of being commensurate; proportionality or correspondence in measure or degree.|The commensuratenesses of the salaries and the workload convinced the employees that the company valued fairness.|近义词：proportionality, correspondence, equivalence, parity|反义词：disproportion, disparity, inequality|词性:名词

commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərɪt/
|adj.|相称的；相当的；相等的|Corresponding in size or degree; proportionate.|Her salary is commensurate with her skills and experience. 她的薪水与她的技能和经验相称。|近义词：proportionate, corresponding, equivalent, comparable|反义词：disproportionate, unequal, mismatched|词性:形容词
