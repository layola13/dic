academician: /əˌkædɪˈmɪʃən/
noun
院士；学会会员
A member of an academy, especially a society of distinguished scholars, artists, or scientists.
He was elected an academician of the Royal Academy of Arts. 他被选为皇家艺术学会的院士。

She is a respected academician in the field of linguistics. 她是语言学领域受人尊敬的院士。

近义词: scholar, intellectual, learned person, savant, pundit
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) amateur, dilettante
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *académicien*, from *académie* ("academy") + *-ien* (suffix forming agent nouns).  Ultimately from Greek *Ἀκαδήμεια (Akadḗmeia)*, Plato's academy, named after the hero Akademos.
