noneffervescently:/ˌnɒnˌɛfəˈvɛsəntli/
Adverb
平淡地，沉闷地，不兴奋地
In a manner that lacks liveliness, enthusiasm, or excitement.
She delivered the speech noneffervescently, failing to capture the audience's attention. 她演讲得平淡无奇，没能抓住观众的注意力。
The party ended noneffervescently, with everyone feeling rather tired and bored.  派对结束得沉闷无趣，每个人都感到相当疲倦和无聊。
Synonym: flatly, dully, vapidly, listlessly
Antonym: excitedly, enthusiastically, vibrantly, animatedly
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "non-" (not) + "effervescent" (giving off bubbles; lively) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Effervescent" ultimately comes from the Latin "ex-" (out) + "fervescere" (to begin to boil).
