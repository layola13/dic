fascial:/ˈfæʃəl/ | adj. | 筋膜的 | Relating to or affecting fascia (a thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ). | The fascial layers provide support and allow muscles to glide smoothly. 筋膜层提供支撑并允许肌肉平稳滑动。 |  The surgeon carefully repaired the damaged fascial tissue.  外科医生仔细地修复了受损的筋膜组织。 |  Synonyms:  aponeurotic, tendinous | Antonyms:  muscular, bone  | 词性: adjective

fascia:/ˈfæʃə/ | n. | 筋膜 | A thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ. |  The fascia helps to support and protect the muscles. 筋膜有助于支撑和保护肌肉。 |  The athlete suffered a tear in his fascia.  这位运动员的筋膜撕裂了。 | Synonyms:  aponeurosis | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "organ" or "muscle" as the thing the fascia encloses) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "fascia" (band, bandage, ribbon), diminutive of "fascis" (bundle).
