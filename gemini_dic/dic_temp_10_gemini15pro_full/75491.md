frutex:/ˈfruːtɛks/| n. |灌木，灌木丛|A shrub; a woody plant smaller than a tree, usually with several stems arising from the base.|The hillside was covered in a dense frutex of juniper and gorse. 山坡上覆盖着茂密的杜松和金荆棘灌木丛。|近义词：shrub, bush|反义词:tree|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frutex*, meaning "shrub" or "bush".
