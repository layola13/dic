incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/| n.|累赘，妨碍，负担，债务|Something that impedes or is burdensome; a hindrance or obstacle.  A claim or lien on property. |The mortgage was an incumbrance on the property. 这笔抵押贷款是这处房产的负担。|Synonyms: burden, hindrance, obstacle, impediment, liability, encumbrance |Antonyms: advantage, asset, benefit, help |noun

incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/| n.|阻碍物，妨碍|Something that makes movement or activity difficult. |The heavy backpack was an incumbrance during the hike.  这个沉重的背包在徒步旅行中是个累赘。|Synonyms: impediment, obstruction, barrier |Antonyms: aid, assistance |noun

incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/| n.|（法律）债务，留置权|A legal claim or liability attached to a property. |There were several incumbrances on the title to the land.  这块土地的所有权上有几个留置权。|Synonyms: lien, charge, mortgage |Antonyms: clear title |noun
