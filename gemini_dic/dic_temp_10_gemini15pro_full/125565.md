multitasks:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːsks/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæsks/| v.| 多任务处理，同时处理多项任务 |She multitasks effortlessly, handling phone calls, emails, and client meetings simultaneously. 她毫不费力地处理多项任务，同时处理电话、电子邮件和客户会议。| He multitasks by listening to podcasts while exercising. 他一边锻炼一边听播客，以此来进行多任务处理。 |synonyms: juggles, handles multiple tasks, performs several duties concurrently | antonyms: focuses, concentrates, single-tasks | verb

multitasking:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskɪŋ/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæskɪŋ/| n.| 多任务处理，同时处理多项任务的行为 |Multitasking is a common requirement in today's fast-paced work environment. 在当今快节奏的工作环境中，多任务处理是一项普遍的要求。|  The study showed that multitasking can reduce productivity. 研究表明，多任务处理会降低生产力。| synonyms: concurrency, parallelism | antonyms: focus, concentration | noun
