counterforce:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɔːrs/| n.|反作用力，对抗力量|A force that opposes or counteracts another force.|The government deployed a counterforce to quell the rebellion. 政府部署了一支反作用力来镇压叛乱。|近义词：opposition, resistance, counteraction|反义词:support, assistance|noun

counterforce:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɔːrs/| v.|对抗，抵消|To oppose or counteract with equal force.|The company's new marketing campaign is designed to counterforce the competitor's efforts. 该公司的新营销活动旨在对抗竞争对手的努力。|近义词：oppose, counteract, resist|反义词:support, aid|verb
