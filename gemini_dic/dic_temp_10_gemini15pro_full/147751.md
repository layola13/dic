overprogrammed:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræmd/
adjective
过度编程的；程序过多的
having too many activities or things to do scheduled, often resulting in stress or inefficiency.  Being burdened with excessive structure and direction, often leaving little room for spontaneity or individual initiative.
例句：The children were overprogrammed with extracurricular activities and had no time to just play. 孩子们的课外活动安排得太多了，没有时间玩耍。

例句：Feeling overprogrammed and stressed, she decided to cancel some of her commitments. 感到行程安排过满、压力过大，她决定取消一些承诺。

近义词：overscheduled, overburdened, overwhelmed
反义词：under-scheduled, free, relaxed
词性：adjective
