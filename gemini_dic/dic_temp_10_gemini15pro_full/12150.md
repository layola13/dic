aruspex:/əˈrʌspɛks/ | n. | 占卜师；预言家 | A Roman priest who practiced divination by inspecting the entrails of sacrificial animals. |  The aruspex examined the liver of the sacrificed ox for signs from the gods. 占卜师检查了献祭的牛的肝脏，以寻找来自神的迹象。

The aruspex foretold a great victory based on the condition of the animal's heart.  根据动物心脏的情况，占卜师预言了一场伟大的胜利。 |  Synonym: soothsayer, diviner, prophet, oracle | Antonym: skeptic | Noun
