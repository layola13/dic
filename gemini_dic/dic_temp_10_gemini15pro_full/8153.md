anti-consumer:/ˌænti kənˈsuːmər/
anti-consumer| adj.|反消费主义的|Opposed to consumerism, often advocating for reduced consumption, simpler lifestyles, and awareness of the environmental and social impacts of consumer culture.|Many anti-consumer activists advocate for buying less and repairing more. 许多反消费主义活动家提倡少买多修。|
anti-consumer| adj.|反消费主义的|Characterized by a rejection of consumerism. |The anti-consumer movement is gaining traction as people become more aware of the environmental impact of their purchases.  随着人们越来越意识到他们购买行为对环境的影响，反消费主义运动正在获得越来越多的支持。|
anti-consumer| n.|反消费主义者|A person who opposes consumerism.|He's an anti-consumer who believes in living a simple life. 他是一个反消费主义者，信奉简单生活。|
anti-consumer| n.|反消费主义者|Someone who actively avoids participating in consumer culture.|As an anti-consumer, she refuses to buy anything she doesn't absolutely need.  作为一个反消费主义者，她拒绝购买任何她并非绝对需要的东西。|
anti-consumerism| n.|反消费主义|A sociopolitical ideology that opposes consumerism, the continual buying and consuming of material possessions.|Anti-consumerism is a growing movement. 反消费主义是一项日益壮大的运动。|
anti-consumerism| n.|反消费主义|The belief that excessive consumption is harmful to individuals, society, and the environment.|She practices anti-consumerism by buying secondhand clothes and avoiding fast fashion. 她通过购买二手衣服和避免购买快时尚来实践反消费主义。|

近义词:anticonsumption, non-consumerist
反义词:pro-consumer, consumerist
词性:adjective, noun (depending on usage)
词源: "anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "consumer" (from Latin *consumere*, meaning to consume)
