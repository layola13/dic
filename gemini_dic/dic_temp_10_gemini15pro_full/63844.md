epiglottis: /ˌɛpɪˈɡlɑːtɪs/ (US), /ˌɛpɪˈɡlɒtɪs/ (UK) | n. | 会厌 | A flap of cartilage at the root of the tongue, which is depressed during swallowing to cover the opening of the windpipe. | The epiglottis prevents food from entering the trachea.  会厌防止食物进入气管。|  The epiglottis closed over his windpipe, causing him to choke. 会厌盖住了他的气管，导致他窒息。 | synonym:  none (It's a unique anatomical structure) | antonym: none | noun
