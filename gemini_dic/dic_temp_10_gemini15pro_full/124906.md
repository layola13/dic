muddy:/ˈmʌdi/ (US), /ˈmʌdi/ (UK)| adj.|泥泞的；浑浊的；模糊不清的；（颜色）暗淡的|Covered or filled with mud; not clear or bright; vague or confused.|例句：The roads are muddy after the rain. 雨后的路很泥泞。|例句：The river was brown and muddy. 河水是棕色的，很浑浊。|近义词：dirty, miry, cloudy, unclear|反义词：clean, clear, bright|词性:形容词

muddy:/ˈmʌdi/ (US), /ˈmʌdi/ (UK)| v.|使泥泞；使浑浊；弄脏|To make something muddy.|例句：The children muddied their boots playing in the garden. 孩子们在花园里玩耍，弄脏了靴子。|例句：Don't muddy the water by adding more complications. 不要通过增加更多复杂性来把水搅浑。|近义词：soil, dirty, stain|反义词：clean, clear|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old English *muddig, from mud + -ig.
