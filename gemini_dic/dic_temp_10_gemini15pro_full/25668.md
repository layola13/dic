bunged:/bʌndʒd/
|adj.|堵塞的，塞住的；（非正式）坏掉的，破损的|Blocked or closed with something; (informal) broken or unusable.|The sink is bunged up with hair. 水槽被头发堵住了。|近义词：blocked, clogged, broken, unusable|反义词：clear, open, working, functional
|v. (bung, bunged, bunging)|（用塞子）塞住，堵住；（非正式）扔，投|To close or seal (an opening) with a bung or stopper; (informal) to throw or put something somewhere carelessly.|He bunged the bottle with a cork. 他用软木塞塞住了瓶子。|近义词：block, clog, plug, throw, toss|反义词：unblock, unclog, open
