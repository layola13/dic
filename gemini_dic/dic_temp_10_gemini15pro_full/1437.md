ace-up-my-sleeve:/eɪs ʌp maɪ sliːv/| idiom | 底牌；秘密武器；锦囊妙计 |  A secret advantage or resource that can be used if needed. It refers to a card player hiding an ace up their sleeve to cheat, but now it's commonly used figuratively. | I knew I had an ace up my sleeve during the negotiation. 我知道在谈判中我有一个秘密武器。

He still has an ace up his sleeve. 他还有锦囊妙计。| 近义词: secret weapon, hidden advantage, trump card | 反义词: disadvantage, weakness | 词性: idiom

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He always has an ace up his sleeve. = 他总是有锦囊妙计。
She keeps an ace up her sleeve for emergencies. = 她留着锦囊妙计以备不时之需。

一般过去时:
I had an ace up my sleeve during the interview. = 面试时我有一个秘密武器。
She used her ace up her sleeve to win the competition. = 她用她的秘密武器赢得了比赛。

现在完成时:
He has always had an ace up his sleeve in business dealings. = 他在商业交易中总是有锦囊妙计。
She has never revealed her ace up her sleeve. = 她从未透露过她的秘密武器。

过去完成时:
I had already played my ace up my sleeve before the final round. = 在最后一轮之前，我已经使出了我的秘密武器。
She had kept her ace up her sleeve until the last minute. = 她一直把她的秘密武器保留到最后一刻。

将来时:
I will have an ace up my sleeve for the next meeting. = 下次会议我将有一个秘密武器。
She will use her ace up her sleeve if necessary. = 如果有必要，她会使用她的秘密武器。

虚拟语气:
If I had an ace up my sleeve, I would use it. = 如果我有一个秘密武器，我就会使用它。
I wish I had an ace up my sleeve right now. = 我希望我现在有一个秘密武器。

被动语态:(不适用，ace-up-my-sleeve 是习惯用语，通常不用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Do you have an ace up your sleeve? = 你有锦囊妙计吗？
Did she have an ace up her sleeve during the debate? = 辩论时她有秘密武器吗？

否定句:
I don't have an ace up my sleeve. = 我没有锦囊妙计。
She didn't have an ace up her sleeve for that situation. = 对于那种情况，她没有秘密武器。


PHRASE->
have an ace up one's sleeve =  有一个锦囊妙计
play one's ace up one's sleeve =  使出自己的秘密武器
keep an ace up one's sleeve =  留一手；藏有锦囊妙计
with an ace up one's sleeve =  胸有成竹；胜券在握
reveal one's ace up one's sleeve =  亮出底牌；摊牌


注意事项: ace-up-my-sleeve 虽然字面意思是“袖子里的王牌”，但通常不用于字面意义，而是比喻意义。
