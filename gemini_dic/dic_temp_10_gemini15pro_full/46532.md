dalal:/dɑːˈlɑːl/| n.| 中间人，经纪人，掮客|A broker or intermediary, especially one who buys and sells on commission.|The dalal negotiated a good price for the farmer's produce.  中间人为农民的农产品谈了个好价钱。|Synonyms: broker, agent, middleman, intermediary|Antonyms: buyer, seller|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi/Urdu दलाल/دلال (dalāl), from Arabic دَلَّال (dallāl), meaning "broker," "agent," or "pimp."  Ultimately from the Arabic root د ل ل (d-l-l) relating to showing or guiding.
