cornu:/ˈkɔːrnjuː/ (US), /ˈkɔːnjuː/ (UK)| n. |角; 角状物; 突出部分|A horn-shaped projection or protuberance.  It comes directly from Latin "cornu," meaning "horn."|The greater cornu of the hyoid bone.  舌骨大角。|Synonyms: horn, projection, protuberance, appendage|Antonyms: hollow, indentation|noun
