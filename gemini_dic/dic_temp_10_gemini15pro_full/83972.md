half-cotton:/ˌhɑːf ˈkɒtn/ (UK) /ˌhæf ˈkɑːtn/ (US)| adj.|半棉的|Made of a blend of cotton and another fiber, typically polyester.  A fabric or garment composed of 50% cotton and 50% another fiber.|例句：I prefer half-cotton shirts because they are more wrinkle-resistant. 我更喜欢半棉衬衫，因为它们更抗皱。|例句：The half-cotton blend is soft and breathable. 这种半棉混纺面料柔软透气。|近义词：cotton blend, polycotton|反义词:pure cotton, all-cotton|词性:adjective
