obmutescence:/ˌɒbmjuˈtɛsəns/
n.
沉默，缄默，无声
The state of being silent or speechless.
After the shocking news, a heavy obmutescence fell over the room.  震惊的消息过后，整个房间陷入沉重的沉默。
The witness's obmutescence during the trial was unsettling.  证人在审判过程中的沉默令人不安。
synonym: silence, muteness, speechlessness
antonym: loquacity, talkativeness, garrulity
