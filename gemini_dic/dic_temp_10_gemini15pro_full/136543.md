nonrustically:/ˌnɒnˈrʌstɪkli/ | adv. | 非乡村式地，不粗俗地 | In a manner that is not rustic; elegantly, refinedly. 词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin.
rustic (adjective/noun):  Referring to the countryside; rural; simple or unsophisticated. From Latin *rusticus*, from *rus* "country."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
