PHRASE->
fummel about/around:  乱摸；瞎摸  He was fumbling around in the dark, looking for his glasses. 他在黑暗中摸索着，寻找他的眼镜。
fummel for: 摸索着寻找  She fumbled for her keys in her bag. 她在包里摸索着找钥匙。
fummel with: 摆弄；笨拙地处理  He was fumbling with the controls of the new machine. 他笨手笨脚地摆弄着新机器的控制器。
have a fummel: (非正式) 摸索; 尝试  Let me have a fummel at fixing it. 让我试试修好它。
no amount of fumbling: 无论怎么摸索也...  No amount of fumbling with the lock would open it. 无论怎么摆弄锁都打不开。
