noninterruptive:/ˌnɒnɪntəˈrʌptɪv/
adjective
非中断的，不干扰的
Not interrupting or causing a disruption.  Allowing something else to continue without interference.
The software update was noninterruptive, allowing me to continue working.  这个软件更新是非中断的，允许我继续工作。
The noninterruptive nature of the background music made it ideal for studying.  背景音乐不干扰的特性使它成为学习的理想选择。
non-disruptive, seamless, continuous
