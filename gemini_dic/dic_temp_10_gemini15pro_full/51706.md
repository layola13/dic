devise:/dɪˈvaɪz/ | v. | 设计; 发明; 策划; 想出 | To invent or plan something, especially something new or complicated. |  He devised a new method for teaching English. 他设计了一种新的英语教学方法。

The engineers devised a clever solution to the problem. 工程师们想出了一个巧妙的解决问题的办法。 | 近义词：invent, create, conceive, formulate, design, contrive | 反义词：copy, imitate | 词性: 动词

devise:/dɪˈvaɪs/ | n. |  遗赠条款；遗赠 | A clause in a will leaving real estate to someone. |  The devise of the property to his nephew was contested. 他将财产遗赠给侄子的条款受到了质疑。

He made a devise of his estate to his daughter. 他立下遗嘱将他的财产遗赠给他的女儿。 | 近义词：bequest, legacy | 反义词：N/A | 词性: 名词
