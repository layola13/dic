check-off:/ˈtʃɛk ɔːf/
|n.| 核对; 勾选; 检查表; 对号|An act of checking or verifying something, often by marking it on a list.  The process of verifying each item on a list.|例句：The manager did a check-off of inventory. 经理核对了库存。|近义词：verification, confirmation, check, tick-off|反义词: omission|noun
check-off:/ˈtʃɛk ɔːf/
|v.| 核对; 勾选; 检查|To mark or check an item on a list as completed or verified.|例句：I checked off all the items on my grocery list. 我把我购物清单上的所有东西都勾选了。|近义词：verify, confirm, check, tick off|反义词: omit, miss|verb
