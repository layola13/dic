overflowing:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊɪŋ/| adj.| 溢出的，充满的，洋溢的|Filled to excess; brimming over. Also, abundant in feeling or expression. |The river was overflowing its banks after the heavy rain.  河水在大雨过后溢出了河岸。

Her heart was overflowing with joy.  她的内心充满了喜悦。|近义词：brimming, teeming, overflowing, awash, inundated|反义词：empty, lacking, deficient|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *oferflowan, from ofer- (over) + flowan (to flow).  源自古英语 *oferflowan，由 ofer-（超过）+ flowan（流动）组成。
