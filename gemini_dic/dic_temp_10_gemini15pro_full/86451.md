heftiest:/ˈhɛftiɪst/| adj.|最重的，最大的，最强壮的|Superlative adjective of hefty: largest, strongest, or heaviest.|He carried the heftiest suitcase I'd ever seen. 他提着我见过的最重的行李箱。|近义词：bulkiest, heaviest, weightiest, most substantial|反义词:lightest, smallest, weakest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From hefty + -est. Hefty is from heft + -y.  Heft is from Middle English *heften, from Old Norse *hepta “to seize,” related to Old English hebban “to heave, lift.”
