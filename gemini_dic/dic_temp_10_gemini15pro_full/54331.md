disheartenment:/dɪsˈhɑːrtn.mənt/| n. |沮丧，灰心，气馁|The team's loss led to a feeling of disheartenment among the players.  该队的失利导致球员们感到沮丧。|例句：The constant setbacks filled her with disheartenment.  不断的挫折使她充满了沮丧。|近义词：dejection, despondency, discouragement |反义词: encouragement, hope, optimism |词性:名词

dishearten:/dɪsˈhɑːrtn/| v. |使沮丧，使灰心，使气馁|The news disheartened him. 这消息使他灰心。|例句：Don't let the difficulty of the task dishearten you. 不要让任务的难度使你气馁。|近义词：demoralize, discourage |反义词: encourage, hearten |词性:动词
