bletting:/ˈblɛtɪŋ/| n.| (果实的)软熟, (果实的)后熟|The bletting process softens the fruit and enhances its flavor.  软熟过程使水果变软并增强其风味。|例句：The pears need bletting before they can be eaten.  梨子需要软熟后才能食用。

例句：Bletting is essential for some varieties of medlar. 对某些品种的欧楂来说，软熟是必不可少的。|近义词：ripening, maturing, softening|反义词: hardening, firming|词性: noun

Etymology->
From Middle English *bletten, from Old English *blettian, from Proto-Germanic *blatjaną (“to make or become soft”). Cognate with Middle High German blatzen (“to make soft”).
