Asperger's: /ˈæspɜːrɡərz/ | n. | 阿斯伯格综合症 |  A developmental disorder, considered to be part of the autism spectrum, characterized by difficulties in social interaction and nonverbal communication, along with restricted and repetitive patterns of behavior and interests. It is often considered a high-functioning form of autism.  No longer an official diagnosis in DSM-5 (replaced by Autism Spectrum Disorder). | 例句：He was diagnosed with Asperger's syndrome as a child. 他小时候被诊断出患有阿斯伯格综合症。

例句：Many people with Asperger's have exceptional skills in specific areas. 许多患有阿斯伯格综合症的人在特定领域拥有非凡的技能。

近义词：Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), high-functioning autism | 反义词:  (No direct antonym as it's a condition, not a characteristic) | 词性: noun
