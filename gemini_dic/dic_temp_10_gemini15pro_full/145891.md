overdevotedly: /ˌoʊvərdiˈvoʊtɪdli/
adverb
过度虔诚地，过度投入地
Excessively devoted; with extreme dedication and loyalty.  Characterized by excessive or exaggerated devotion.
She overdevotedly cared for her sick mother, neglecting her own health. 她过度投入地照顾生病的母亲，忽略了自己的健康。
He overdevotedly followed his mentor's teachings, even when they seemed questionable. 他过度虔诚地追随导师的教诲，即使这些教诲看起来值得怀疑。
近义词: excessively, fanatically, zealously
反义词: halfheartedly, apathetically, indifferently
词性: adverb
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + devotedly (adverb form of devoted, from the French "devot," past participle of "devover" meaning "to dedicate")
