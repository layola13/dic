bejewelled:/bɪˈdʒuːəld/ /biˈdʒuːəld/
adjective
镶满宝石的；饰以珠宝的
Adorned or decorated with jewels.
The bejewelled crown sparkled under the lights.  镶满宝石的王冠在灯光下闪闪发光。
Her fingers were bejewelled with rings.  她的手指上戴满了宝石戒指。
Synonyms: jeweled, gem-studded, ornamented, decorated
Antonyms: plain, unadorned, undecorated
词性: adjective
