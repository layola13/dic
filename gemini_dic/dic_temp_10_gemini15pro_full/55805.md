divertive:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪv/
adjective
消遣的，娱乐的，转移注意力的
Tending to divert or amuse; entertaining.
The game provided a divertive break from studying.  这个游戏提供了一个从学习中消遣的休息时间。
synonyms: entertaining, amusing, diverting, distracting
antonyms: boring, tedious, monotonous
词性: 形容词
