doughlike:/ˈdoʊlaɪk/
adjective
像面团的；面团状的
Resembling dough in texture; soft, thick, and malleable.
The artist molded the clay into a doughlike consistency.  艺术家把粘土塑造成面团状的稠度。
The cake batter was too doughlike and needed more liquid.  蛋糕糊太像面团了，需要加更多液体。
近义词:pasty, doughy
反义词:dry, crumbly
词性:adjective
