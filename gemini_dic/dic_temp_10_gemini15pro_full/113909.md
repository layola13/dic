madrina: /mɑːˈdriːnə/ (UK), /mɑːˈdrɪːnə/ (US) | n. | 教母；女证婚人；（尤指拉丁美洲国家的）保护人；赞助人 | A woman who sponsors a person at baptism or confirmation. Also, a female mentor or protector, especially in Latin American cultures. |  My aunt was my madrina at my confirmation. 我阿姨是我坚信礼的教母。

|  The madrina helped the young artist find a gallery to showcase her work.  这位女赞助人帮助这位年轻的艺术家找到了一个画廊来展示她的作品。| 近义词: godmother, sponsor, patron, mentor | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Spanish *madrina*, derived from *madre* (mother). Ultimately from Latin *mater*.
