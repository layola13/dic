clipping:/ˈklɪpɪŋ/| n.|剪报; 剪下的东西; 修剪; |A clipping is a piece of writing or a picture that has been cut from a newspaper, magazine, etc.  剪报是指从报纸、杂志等上剪下来的文章或图片。|Example: He kept a clipping of the article about his daughter's graduation. 他保留了一份关于他女儿毕业典礼的文章的剪报。

Example:  The gardener swept up the grass clippings. 园丁扫走了剪下来的草。|Synonyms: cutting, snippet, excerpt, trim|Antonyms: extension, lengthening|noun

clipping:/ˈklɪpɪŋ/| v.|修剪; 剪; 夹住; |To clip something means to cut it, especially with scissors or shears.  修剪某物意味着剪掉它，尤指用剪刀或大剪刀。|Example: I clipped the hedge this weekend. 我这周末修剪了树篱。

Example:  He clipped the microphone to his shirt. 他把麦克风夹在衬衫上。|Synonyms: trim, snip, shear, prune|Antonyms: extend, lengthen|verb
