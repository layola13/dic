overrapid:/ˈoʊvərˈræpɪd/| adj.|过快的，过速的|Excessively rapid; too fast.|The overrapid growth of the city led to infrastructure problems. 城市过快的发展导致了基础设施问题。|近义词：excessive, hasty, precipitate, accelerated, too fast, unduly rapid, overhasty|反义词:slow, gradual, measured|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "rapid" (fast).  "Rapid" comes from the Latin "rapidus," meaning "swift" or "hurrying."
