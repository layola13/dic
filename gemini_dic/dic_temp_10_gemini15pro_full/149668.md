paisas:/ˈpaɪsəs/ (US), /ˈpaɪsɑːs/ (UK)| n.| 比索（哥伦比亚货币单位）; (informal) 哥伦比亚人|Colombian pesos; (informal) A person from the Paisa region of Colombia (Antioquia, Caldas, Risaralda, Quindío) or someone considered to have the cultural traits associated with that region. |例句：He paid 10,000 paisas for the coffee. 他花了10,000比索买了咖啡。

例句：Many paisas are known for their entrepreneurial spirit. 许多哥伦比亚人以其创业精神而闻名。|近义词：Colombians (when referring to people), pesos (when referring to currency)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *paisano* "compatriot," ultimately from Latin *pagensis* "villager," from *pagus* "village, district."  The term originally referred to people from the Paisa region of Colombia.  It later became associated with the Colombian currency.
