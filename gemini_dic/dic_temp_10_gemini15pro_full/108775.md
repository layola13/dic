leaseless:/ˈliːslɪs/| adj.|无租赁的，无人承租的|Without a lease; not leased or rented out.  It describes a property or item that is currently not being rented or leased by anyone.|The apartment building remained leaseless for months. 这栋公寓楼几个月都没人租。|Synonyms: vacant, unoccupied, empty, unrented, available|Antonyms: leased, rented, occupied|adjective
