bobance:/bɒˈbɑːns/ /boʊˈbɑːns/
n.
炫耀；夸耀；虚张声势
An ostentatious or swaggering display; boastful or pompous behavior.
He strutted around with an air of bobance, clearly enjoying the attention. 他趾高气扬地走来走去，显然很享受这种关注。
swagger, boastfulness, ostentation, pomposity, braggadocio
humility, modesty, unassumingness
noun

bobance:/bɒˈbɑːns/ /boʊˈbɑːns/
vi.
昂首阔步；趾高气扬地走
To strut or move with a swaggering or boastful air.
He bobanced into the room, full of self-importance. 他趾高气扬地走进房间，充满了自以为是。
strut, swagger, prance, flounce
cringe, cower, shrink
verb
