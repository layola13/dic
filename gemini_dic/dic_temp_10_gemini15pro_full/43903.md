cretism: /ˈkriːtɪzəm/ | n. |融合主义，混合主义| The fusion of different, often contradictory beliefs, practices, or styles, especially in religion or art. |  The statue exhibited cretism, blending elements of Buddhist and Hindu iconography. 这座雕像展现了融合主义，混合了佛教和印度教的图像元素。

The architecture of the cathedral displayed cretism, incorporating Gothic and Romanesque features.  这座大教堂的建筑展现了融合主义，融合了哥特式和罗马式风格。 | 近义词：syncretism, fusion, hybridity | 反义词：purity, orthodoxy | 词性: noun
