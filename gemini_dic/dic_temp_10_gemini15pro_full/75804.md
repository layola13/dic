fumeless:/ˈfjuːmlɪs/
adjective
无烟的
producing no fumes; smokeless.
The factory installed a fumeless incinerator.  这家工厂安装了一个无烟焚烧炉。
Near synonyms: smokeless, odorless, scentless
Antonyms: smoky, fuming
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fume + -less.  Fume comes from Old French fumier "to smoke," ultimately from Latin fumus "smoke."  The suffix -less means "without."
