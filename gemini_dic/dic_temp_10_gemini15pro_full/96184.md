indestructibility:/ˌɪndɪˌstrʌktɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可摧毁性；坚不可摧
The quality or state of being impossible to destroy.
The indestructibility of diamond makes it a valuable material. 钻石的不可摧毁性使其成为一种珍贵的材料。
近义词:invincibility, impenetrability, durability
反义词:fragility, vulnerability, destructibility
词性:noun

词源:
From in- (not) + destructible + -ity.  "destructible" comes from Latin *dēstruct-, past participle stem of dēstruere "to pull down, demolish," from de- "down" + struere "to pile, build."
