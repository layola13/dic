circumventing:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈventɪŋ/
verb
规避，绕过
Avoiding or bypassing something, especially in a clever or indirect way.
He circumvented the problem by using a different approach. 他通过使用不同的方法规避了这个问题。
The company circumvented the trade restrictions by shipping goods through a third country.  这家公司通过经由第三国运输货物来规避贸易限制。
近义词:avoiding, bypassing, dodging, evading, sidestepping
反义词:confronting, facing, meeting
词性:Verb (present participle of circumvent)

circumvent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvent/
verb
规避，绕过
To find a way around (an obstacle).
The lawyer circumvented the problem by finding a loophole in the law.  律师通过发现法律漏洞规避了这个问题。
They circumvented the security system by climbing over the wall. 他们通过翻墙规避了安全系统。
近义词:avoid, bypass, dodge, evade, sidestep
反义词:confront, face, meet
词性:Verb
