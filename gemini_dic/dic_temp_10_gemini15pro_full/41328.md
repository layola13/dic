coresidential:/ˌkɔːrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl/
adjective
共同居住的；合住的
Living in the same residence or building.
Example:  The coresidential arrangement allowed the siblings to share expenses and support each other.  共同居住的安排使得兄弟姐妹们可以分摊费用并互相支持。

Example: The coresidential housing project provides affordable living for seniors. 这个合住的住房项目为老年人提供了经济实惠的居住环境。
近义词: cohabiting, shared
反义词: separate, individual
词性: adjective
