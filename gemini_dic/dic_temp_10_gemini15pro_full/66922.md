expendable:/ɪkˈspendəbl/| adj. n.| 可牺牲的，可消耗的；消耗品 |Something that is expendable is not considered important and can be sacrificed or lost if necessary.  A consumable item that is used up and replaced.|例句：During wartime, soldiers were often considered expendable.  战争时期，士兵常被认为是可牺牲的。

例句：Office supplies like pens and paper are expendable. 办公用品如笔和纸是消耗品。|近义词：dispensable, replaceable, nonessential |反义词：essential, indispensable, crucial|词性:形容词,名词

expendable:/ɪkˈspendəbl/| n.| 消耗品 | Consumable items that are used up and replaced. |例句：The troops carried extra food and other expendables. 部队携带了额外的食物和其他消耗品。

例句：Ammunition and medical supplies are typical military expendables. 弹药和医疗用品是典型的军事消耗品。|近义词：supplies, consumables|反义词：essentials, necessities|词性:名词
