mini-industries:/ˈmɪni ˈɪndəstriːz/
noun
小型产业，微型产业
Small-scale industries, often involving a limited number of people and resources.
The area is known for its mini-industries, like handcrafted jewelry and artisanal foods.  这个地区以其小型产业而闻名，例如手工珠宝和手工食品。
The government is providing support for the development of mini-industries in rural areas. 政府正在为农村地区小型产业的发展提供支持。
small businesses, cottage industries
large industries, major industries
