missioned:/ˈmɪʃənd/
|v.|被派遣的，被赋予使命的|Past participle of "mission," meaning having been given a specific task or purpose.|The newly missioned diplomats were sent to establish relations with the emerging nation. 新任命的几位外交官被派去与新兴国家建立关系。|例句：The missioned officer carried out his orders without question.  被派遣的军官毫不犹豫地执行了命令。

例句：They were missioned to explore the uncharted territory. 他们被派去探索未知的领域。|近义词：assigned, appointed, commissioned, dispatched|反义词: unassigned, unappointed|词性:动词(过去分词)
