coniferization:/ˌkɒnɪfərɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 针叶林化；人工造林（尤指种植针叶树）|The coniferization of the area has led to a decrease in biodiversity. 该地区的针叶林化导致生物多样性下降。|Example: The government's policy of coniferization has been criticized by environmentalists. 政府的针叶林化政策受到了环保人士的批评。

Example:  Coniferization can lead to soil acidification. 针叶林化会导致土壤酸化。| 近义词: afforestation, reforestation | 反义词: deforestation | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From conifer + -ization.  "Conifer" comes from the Latin "conifer," meaning "cone-bearing," from "conus" (cone) + "ferre" (to bear).  "-ization" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
