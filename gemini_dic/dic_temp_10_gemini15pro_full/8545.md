antichlor:/ˈæntiˌklɔːr/
noun
去氯剂
A substance used to remove excess chlorine after bleaching.
The photographer used an antichlor to remove residual chlorine from the prints.  摄影师用去氯剂去除照片上残留的氯。
synonyms: dechlorinator, chlorine neutralizer
antonyms: chlorinating agent
n.
