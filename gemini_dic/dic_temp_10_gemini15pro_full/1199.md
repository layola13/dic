accoladed: /ˈækəˌleɪdɪd/
adjective
受到赞扬的
Having received praise or an award.
The accoladed scientist received a Nobel Prize.  这位备受赞誉的科学家获得了诺贝尔奖。

The accoladed author's new book was an instant bestseller.  这位获奖无数的作家的新书一上市就成了畅销书。

近义词: praised, lauded, honored, acclaimed, celebrated
反义词: criticized, condemned, denigrated
词性: adjective
词源: From the noun "accolade," which comes from the Old French "acolade" meaning "an embrace," later referring to the ceremony of conferring knighthood.  Ultimately derived from the Latin "ad" (to) + "collum" (neck), referring to the embrace around the neck that was part of the ceremony.
