didelphic:/daɪˈdɛlfɪk/
adj.
双输卵管的，有两个子宫的
Having two uteri and two vaginas, as in marsupials.  Relating to or characteristic of didelphous mammals.
Didelphic mammals, such as kangaroos, have two separate uteri.  双输卵管的哺乳动物，例如袋鼠，有两个独立的子宫。
The didelphic reproductive system is a defining characteristic of marsupials.  双输卵管生殖系统是袋鼠的显著特征。
近义词:bitubal
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑monodelphic - 单输卵管的，用于描述大多数哺乳动物)
