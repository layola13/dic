museum:/mjuˈziːəm/ | n. |博物馆；展览馆|A building in which objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are stored and exhibited. |例句：We visited the natural history museum. 我们参观了自然历史博物馆。

例句：The museum houses a vast collection of ancient artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的古代文物。

近义词：gallery, exhibition |反义词:None |词性:noun
