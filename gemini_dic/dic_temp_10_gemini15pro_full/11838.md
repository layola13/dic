arrester:/əˈrestər/| n.|避雷器，阻止器，逮捕者|A device used to protect electrical equipment from overvoltage, or something that stops or impedes motion.|The lightning arrester protected the power station from damage. 避雷器保护了电站免受损害。|近义词：protector, inhibitor, stopper|反义词:accelerator|noun
