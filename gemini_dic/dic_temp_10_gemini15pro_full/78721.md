gigged:/ɡɪɡd/
verb
演出过；做过零工
past participle of gig: performed a short-term job or engagement, especially as a musician.
The band gigged at several local clubs before hitting the big time.  这支乐队在成名之前在几家当地俱乐部演出过。
近义词: performed, played, worked
反义词: rested, relaxed
词性: v.


gigged:/ɡɪɡd/
adjective
做过零工的；演出过的
Having performed a short-term job or engagement, especially as a musician.
He felt gigged out after playing three shows in one day. 他一天演了三场演出后感觉筋疲力尽。
近义词: experienced, seasoned
反义词: inexperienced, green
词性: adj.
