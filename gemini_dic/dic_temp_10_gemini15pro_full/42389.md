counterbore: /ˈkɑʊntərˌbɔːr/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəbɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 沉孔；锪孔 |A cylindrical recess around a bolt hole, larger in diameter than the hole itself, to allow the bolt head to sit flush with the surface.  To create such a recess.| The carpenter used a counterbore to recess the screws in the tabletop. 木匠用沉孔器将螺丝钉嵌入桌面。 |Synonyms: recess, countersink |Antonyms: protrusion, projection| noun
