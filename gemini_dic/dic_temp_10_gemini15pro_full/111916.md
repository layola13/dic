loiteringly:/ˈlɔɪtərɪŋli/
adverb
徘徊地，闲逛地
in a slow, lingering manner; idly
He walked loiteringly through the park, enjoying the sunshine. 他在公园里悠闲地散步，享受着阳光。
Synonyms: slowly, leisurely, idly, lazily
Antonyms: quickly, briskly, rapidly, hurriedly
词性: 副词
