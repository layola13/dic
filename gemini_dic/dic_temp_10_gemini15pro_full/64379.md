erectly:/ɪˈrɛktli/| adv.|竖直地，笔直地，正直地|In a straight upright position; in a vertical position.  Also, in a morally upright manner.|The flagpole stood erectly in the wind. 旗杆在风中屹立.|近义词：uprightly, vertically, straightly|反义词：crookedly, askew, horizontally|词性:副词

Etymology: From Middle English *erectly*, from Latin *ērectus* (past participle of *ērigō* (“I raise up”)) + *-ly*. *ērigō* is from *ē-* (variant of *ex-*, “out”) + *regō* (“I rule, guide, keep straight”).
