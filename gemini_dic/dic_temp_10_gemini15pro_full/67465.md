extolls:/ɪkˈstəʊlz/ | v. | 赞美；颂扬；称赞 |  praises enthusiastically. | The poet extolls the beauty of nature in his work. 诗人在他的作品中歌颂了大自然的美。| 近义词：praise, laud, commend, celebrate | 反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin extollere "to raise up, lift up, elevate, exalt," from ex "up, out" (see ex-) + tollere "to lift, raise" (see extol).
