feast-days:/ˈfiːst deɪz/| n.|节日，节庆日|Special days dedicated to religious or other celebrations, often marked by feasting and festivities.|例句：Many cultures celebrate feast-days with special meals and traditions. 许多文化以特殊的餐食和传统庆祝节日。

例句：Feast-days are an important part of many religious calendars. 节日是许多宗教日历的重要组成部分。|近义词：holidays, festivals, celebrations|反义词:ordinary days, weekdays|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old English *fæstdagas (unattested), from fæst "fast, firm, fixed" (related to fast (n.)) + dagas "days" (plural of dæg "day"). The sense evolved from days on which religious fasts were broken to days of feasting and celebration.
