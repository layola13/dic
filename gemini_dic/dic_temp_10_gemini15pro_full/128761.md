niggles:/ˈnɪɡəlz/| n. v.|小麻烦，琐事；困扰；吹毛求疵|A niggle is a slight doubt or worry about something.|例句：I had a niggle that I had forgotten something. 我隐约觉得我忘了什么东西。|近义词：worry, bother, irritate, annoy|反义词:please, soothe|词性:名词(通常复数) 动词

niggle:/ˈnɪɡəl/| v.|使烦恼；吹毛求疵；不断地烦扰|To niggle means to cause slight but persistent annoyance, discomfort, or anxiety.|例句：The constant dripping of the tap niggled at her.  水龙头不停地滴水让她很烦。|近义词：worry, bother, irritate, annoy|反义词:please, soothe|词性:动词
