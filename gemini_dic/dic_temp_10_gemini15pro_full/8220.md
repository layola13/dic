anti-gallicanism:/ˌæntiˈɡælɪkənɪzəm/
n.
反高卢主义，反对法国或法国文化的影响
Opposition to French influence or French culture, especially in political or ecclesiastical matters.
During the 18th century, anti-Gallicanism was a prominent force in British foreign policy.  18世纪，反高卢主义是英国外交政策中的一股重要力量。
The rise of anti-Gallicanism in England led to several wars with France.  反高卢主义在英国的兴起导致了与法国的多次战争。
近义词: Francophobia
反义词: Gallomania
词性: noun
