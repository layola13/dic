eclectic:/ɪˈklektɪk/
adjective
折衷的，兼收并蓄的
selecting or choosing from various sources.
例句：She has an eclectic taste in music, enjoying everything from classical to hip-hop. 她对音乐的品味很折衷，从古典到嘻哈都喜欢。

例句：The museum's eclectic collection includes artifacts from all over the world.  这家博物馆的藏品兼收并蓄，包含了来自世界各地的文物。

近义词：diverse, varied, broad, comprehensive
反义词：narrow, limited, specific, homogeneous
词性：adjective
