melancholic: n. | 忧郁的人 | A person who is habitually melancholic. | He was a melancholic, prone to long periods of brooding. 他是个忧郁的人，容易长时间沉思。 | synonyms: brooder, pessimist | antonyms: optimist, extrovert | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *melancholicus, from Greek melankholikós, from mélas (black) + kholé (bile).  The ancient Greeks believed that an excess of black bile caused melancholy.
