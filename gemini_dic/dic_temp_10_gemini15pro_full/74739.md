freestyles:/ˈfriːstaɪlz/| n. v.|自由式，即兴创作，自由泳|A freestyle is a performance, especially in music, art, or sport, done without following a set pattern or form.  It can also refer to a swimming stroke. |He did a freestyle rap at the party. 他在派对上即兴表演了一段说唱。|近义词：improvisation, ad-lib|反义词：rehearsed, planned|词性:名词,动词
