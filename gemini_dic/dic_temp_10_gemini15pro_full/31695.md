chauvinist: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪst/ (US) /ˈʃəʊvɪnɪst/ (UK)| n. adj.|沙文主义者，盲目爱国者；沙文主义的|A chauvinist is someone who believes that their own group (e.g., country, race, gender) is superior to others.|He's such a chauvinist – he thinks men are naturally better leaders than women. 他真是个沙文主义者——他认为男性天生就比女性更适合当领导。|近义词：bigot, sexist, racist, jingoist|反义词：cosmopolitan, egalitarian|词性: noun, adjective
