glitch:/ɡlɪtʃ/ | n. |小故障；失灵；差错|A sudden, usually temporary malfunction or irregularity in equipment or a system.|The computer program had a glitch that caused it to crash. 电脑程序出现了一个小故障，导致它崩溃了。|近义词：bug, error, malfunction, fault, defect|反义词：perfection, flawlessness| noun

glitch:/ɡlɪtʃ/ | v. |出现小故障；失灵|To experience a sudden, usually temporary malfunction or irregularity.|The system glitched and lost all the data. 系统出现故障，所有数据都丢失了。|近义词：malfunction, fail, crash|反义词：function, work| verb
