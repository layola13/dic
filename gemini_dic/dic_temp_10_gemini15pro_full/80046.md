goadlike:/ˈɡoʊdˌlaɪk/| adj.|像刺棒的，刺激性的，煽动性的|Resembling or characteristic of a goad; serving to urge on.|His goadlike questions forced her to confront the truth. 他像刺棒一样的质问迫使她面对真相。|近义词：provocative, stimulating, urging, inciting, irritating|反义词：soothing, calming, pacifying|词性:形容词

词源: From "goad" (a pointed stick used to urge animals on) + "-like" (suffix meaning resembling).  "Goad" comes from Old English "gād," related to Old Norse "gaðr."
