lindon:/ˈlɪndən/| n. |椴树；菩提树|A linden tree, also known as a lime tree in Europe, is a deciduous tree with heart-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers.|The linden tree in the park provides welcome shade in the summer. 公园里的菩提树在夏天提供了受欢迎的阴凉。|近义词：lime tree, basswood|反义词:|noun
