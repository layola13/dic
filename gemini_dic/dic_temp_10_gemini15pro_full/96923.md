inexpressible:/ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsəbl/ | adj. | 不可言喻的，难以表达的 | Not capable of being expressed; too great or too sacred to be expressed in words. |  The beauty of the sunset was inexpressible.  日落的美丽难以言喻。

The pain he felt was inexpressible. 他感受到的痛苦无法表达。 | 近义词：unutterable, indescribable, ineffable | 反义词：expressible, describable, utterable | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + expressible from late Latin express- ‘pressed out’ + -ible.
