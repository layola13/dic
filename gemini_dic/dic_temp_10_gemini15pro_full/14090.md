autarch:/ˈɔːtɑːrk/ | n. | 独裁者，专制君主 | A ruler who has absolute power. |  The autarch ruled his small island nation with an iron fist.  这个独裁者以铁腕统治着他小小的岛国。| Synonyms: dictator, despot, tyrant, autocrat | Antonyms: democrat | noun

autarchy:/ˈɔːtɑːrki/ | n. |  独裁统治; 自给自足 |  1. Absolute sovereignty. 2. Self-sufficiency; independence. | The country's autarchy made it difficult for international trade.  这个国家的自给自足使其难以进行国际贸易.  | Synonyms: despotism, absolutism, self-sufficiency, independence | Antonyms: democracy, interdependence | noun

autarchical: /ɔːˈtɑːrkɪkəl/ | adj. | 独裁的; 自给自足的 | Of or relating to autarchy; absolute; self-sufficient. | The autarchical regime controlled all aspects of the citizens' lives. 独裁政权控制着公民生活的方方面面。  The small village aimed for an autarchical lifestyle, growing their own food and producing their own goods.  这个小村庄的目标是自给自足的生活方式，自己种植粮食，自己生产商品。 | Synonyms: dictatorial, despotic, self-sufficient, independent | Antonyms: democratic, interdependent | adjective
