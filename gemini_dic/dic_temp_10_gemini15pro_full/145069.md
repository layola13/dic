overanalytical:/ˌoʊvərˌænəˈlɪtɪkl/
adjective
过度分析的
Inclined to analyze something excessively or in too much detail.
She's so overanalytical that she misses the big picture. 她过度分析以至于忽略了整体情况。

He tends to be overanalytical, which can be paralyzing when making decisions. 他往往过度分析，这在做决定时会让他陷入瘫痪。

近义词: overthinking, obsessive, meticulous, nitpicking
反义词: intuitive, instinctive, impulsive, superficial
词性: adjective
