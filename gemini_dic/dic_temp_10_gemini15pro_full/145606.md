overconscious:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnʃəs/| adj.|过度意识到的，过分在意的|Excessively aware of oneself and one's actions or appearance, often to the point of anxiety or awkwardness.|She was overconscious of her appearance at the party, constantly adjusting her dress.  她在聚会上过分在意自己的外表，不停地整理衣服。|例句：He's overconscious of his accent when speaking English. 他说英语时过分在意自己的口音。|近义词：self-conscious, insecure, uneasy|反义词：confident, relaxed, self-assured|词性:形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + conscious (adjective meaning "aware")  "over-"（前缀，意为“过度”）+ conscious（形容词，意为“意识到的”）
