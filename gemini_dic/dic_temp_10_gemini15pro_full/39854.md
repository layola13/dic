The state of being constant; immutability.|例句：Her Constance in the face of adversity was admirable.  她在逆境中的坚定令人钦佩。|近义词：firmness, steadfastness, resolution, perseverance, persistence, tenacity|反义词:inconstancy, fickleness, variability|noun
