else:/ɛls/| adv. adj. pron.|其他，另外；别的，不同的|Used after words such as `someone`, `something`, `somewhere`, etc. to refer to a different person, thing, or place.|If you don't like this one, I can show you something else. 如果你不喜欢这个，我可以给你看其他的。|近义词：other, different, additional|反义词:same|词性:副词，形容词，代词
