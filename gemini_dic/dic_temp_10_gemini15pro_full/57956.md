drippers:/ˈdrɪpərz/| n.|滴水器；点滴器；滴注器；(非正式)输液者|A device that allows a liquid to drip slowly and steadily onto something.  People receiving intravenous fluids (informal).|The plants are watered by drippers. 这些植物由滴水器浇水。|The hospital ward was full of drippers. 病房里满是输液的病人。|近义词：irrigators, tricklers (for devices);  patients, invalids (for people)|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

dripper:/ˈdrɪpər/| n.|滴水的人/物；滴落的东西；(非正式)输液者；滴滤式咖啡壶|Something or someone that drips; a person receiving intravenous fluids (informal); a coffee maker that brews coffee by dripping hot water over ground coffee beans contained in a filter.|The leaky faucet was a constant dripper. 漏水的水龙头一直在滴水。|He's a dripper now, hooked up to an IV. 他现在在输液，连着静脉注射。|近义词：leaker (for objects); patient, invalid (for people); filter coffee maker|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
