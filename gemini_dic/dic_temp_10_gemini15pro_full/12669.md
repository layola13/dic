assembly's:/əˈsɛmbliz/| n.|集会；议会；装配；（尤指工厂里的）配件|The assembly's decision was final. 议会的决定是最终的。|近义词：meeting, gathering, congress, legislature, construction|反义词:disassembly, dispersion|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French assemblee (“assembly, meeting”), from assembler (“to assemble”), from Vulgar Latin *assimulare, from Latin ad (“to”) + simul (“together”).
