electorally:/ɪˈlɛktərəli/ | adv. | 选举地，与选举有关地 |  Relating to elections or the process of electing someone. | The issue was electorally damaging to the party.  这个问题对该党在选举方面是具有破坏性的。|  The candidate won electorally, but not by a large margin. 这位候选人赢得了选举，但优势不大。 | 近义词: by election, by vote | 反义词: None | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "electoral" + "-ly."  "Electoral" comes from the Late Latin "electoralis,"  meaning "pertaining to an elector,"  from "elector" (one who elects).  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
