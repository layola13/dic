objectivity:/ˌɒbdʒekˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌɑːbdʒekˈtɪvɪti/| n.|客观性；客观现实|The state or quality of being objective; lack of bias or prejudice.|  The judge must consider all the evidence with objectivity.  法官必须客观地考虑所有证据。|synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, detachment, fairness|antonyms: subjectivity, bias, prejudice|noun

objectiveness:/əbˈdʒɛktɪvnəs/ /əbˈdʒɛktɪvnɪs/| n.|客观性；客观现实|The state or quality of being objective; lack of bias or prejudice.| Journalistic objectiveness is crucial for credible reporting.  新闻报道的客观性对于可靠的报道至关重要。|synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, detachment, fairness|antonyms: subjectivity, bias, prejudice|noun
