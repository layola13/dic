gemma:/ˈdʒɛmə/| n. |芽，胞芽；宝石|A small bud or cell that separates from a parent and develops into a new individual; a precious stone.|例句：The fern reproduces by gemmae.  这种蕨类植物通过胞芽繁殖。

例句：Her necklace was adorned with sparkling gemmae. 她的项链上装饰着闪闪发光的宝石。|近义词：bud, sprout, jewel, gem|反义词: - |n.
