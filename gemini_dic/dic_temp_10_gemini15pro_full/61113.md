elogium:/ɪˈloʊdʒiəm/ /jʊˈloʊdʒiəm/
n.
颂词，赞美词，悼词
A formal expression of praise; a eulogy.
The professor delivered a moving elogium at the memorial service for his esteemed colleague.  教授在他尊敬的同事的追悼会上发表了感人的颂词。
synonym: eulogy, panegyric, tribute, accolade, commendation
antonym: criticism, censure, denunciation, condemnation
noun
