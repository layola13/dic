leucoethiops:/ˌluːkoʊˈiːθiɒps/ | n. |白化病黑人；白皮肤的黑人|A person of Black African descent with albinism. |  In some historical texts, the term "leucoethiops" was used to describe individuals of African descent with albinism. 在一些历史文献中，“leucoethiops”一词被用来描述患有白化病的非洲人后裔。

Leucoethiops were considered a rarity and often featured in curiosity cabinets. 白化黑人被认为是稀有品种，经常出现在珍奇柜中。| 近义词:albino (person of African descent) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek λευκός (leukós, “white”) + Αἰθίοψ (Aithíops, “Ethiopian”).
