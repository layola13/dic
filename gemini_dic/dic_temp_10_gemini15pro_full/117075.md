mead:/miːd/
noun
蜂蜜酒
An alcoholic drink made from fermented honey and water.
例句：He enjoyed a glass of mead at the Renaissance fair.  他在文艺复兴集市上享用了一杯蜂蜜酒。

例句：The mead was sweet and potent.  蜂蜜酒香甜而浓烈。
