overpublicize: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌblɪˌsaɪz/ | vt. | 过度宣传；炒作 | To publicize something excessively.  To give something too much publicity, often to the point of diminishing its impact. | The studio overpublicized the movie, and as a result, audiences had inflated expectations.  这家电影制片厂过度宣传了这部电影，结果观众的期望值过高。| Synonyms: overhype, overpromote, saturate | Antonyms: underpublicize, downplay | verb
