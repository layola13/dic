lilted:/ˈlɪltɪd/ | v. |轻快地抑扬顿挫地说；用轻快的抑扬顿挫的语调说|spoke or sang with a lilt; having a pleasant, rising and falling intonation|Her voice lilted with excitement as she described her trip. 她描述旅行时，声音因兴奋而抑扬顿挫。|近义词：sing-song, melodious, rhythmic|反义词：monotone, flat|词性：动词

词源：From lilt (n.) + -ed, from Scottish Gaelic *liùgh*
