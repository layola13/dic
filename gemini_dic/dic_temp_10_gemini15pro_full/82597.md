guardianships:/ˈɡɑːrdiənʃɪps/ | n. |监护权，监护(权) |The legal right and responsibility to care for someone who cannot care for themselves, such as a child or an incapacitated adult. | The court awarded her guardianship of her younger siblings after their parents died.  法院在她父母去世后授予她对其弟弟妹妹的监护权。| synonyms: custodianship, wardship, care, charge, protection | antonyms: neglect, abandonment | noun
