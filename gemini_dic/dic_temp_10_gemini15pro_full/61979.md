enabling:/ɪˈneɪblɪŋ/
adjective
使能够的，使成为可能的
making something possible, practical, or easy to do.
The software provides an enabling technology for developers.  这个软件为开发者提供了一项使能技术。
This new technology is enabling online education to reach more students. 这项新技术使在线教育能够惠及更多的学生。
近义词:empowering, facilitating, allowing
反义词:hindering, restricting, disabling
词性:adjective


词源:
From late Middle English *enablen,  from en- + able + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin habilis "able, fit."
