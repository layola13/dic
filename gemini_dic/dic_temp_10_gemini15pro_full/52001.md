diagnostics:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪks/ /ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑːstɪks/| n.|诊断; 诊断法; 诊断学|The doctor ran some diagnostics to determine the cause of her illness. 医生进行了一些诊断以确定她患病的原因。|Synonyms: analysis, assessment, examination|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider perhaps:  misdiagnosis, oversight)|noun


diagnostics:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk/ /ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑːstɪk/| adj.|诊断的; 用于诊断的|Diagnostic tests are used to identify diseases. 诊断测试用于识别疾病。|Synonyms: analytical, assessing|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
