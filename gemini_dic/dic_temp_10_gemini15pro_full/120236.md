minging:/ˈmaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 糟糕的，极差的; 令人作呕的；令人不快的 | Extremely unpleasant, bad, or objectionable. |  例句：That movie was absolutely minging. 那部电影糟透了。

例句：The smell coming from the bins was minging. 垃圾桶里散发出的气味令人作呕。 | 近义词：awful, terrible, disgusting, revolting, horrible, dreadful | 反义词：wonderful, excellent, fantastic, great, pleasant, delightful | 词性：形容词

Etymology: British slang, possibly originating from the unpleasant conditions associated with mining.
