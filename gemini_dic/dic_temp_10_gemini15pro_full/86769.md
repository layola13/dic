helps:/hɛlps/ | v. | 帮助；有助于；促进；改善 | Third-person singular simple present indicative form of help | He helps his mother with the housework. 他帮他妈妈做家务。| assists, aids, supports | hinders, obstructs | verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *helpen*, from Old English *helpan* (to help, support, assist, avail, benefit, cure), from Proto-Germanic *helpaną* (to help), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel-p- (to help, support).
