mistrustless:/mɪsˈtrʌstləs/
adjective
无猜忌的；信任的
characterized by lack of mistrust; trusting.
例句：In a mistrustless society, people would be more open and collaborative. 在一个没有猜忌的社会里，人们会更加开放和合作。

例句：Children are naturally mistrustless until they learn otherwise. 孩子们天生是毫无猜忌的，直到他们学会了猜忌。

近义词：trusting, confiding, unsuspicious, naive, innocent
反义词：mistrustful, suspicious, wary, cautious, cynical
词性: adjective
