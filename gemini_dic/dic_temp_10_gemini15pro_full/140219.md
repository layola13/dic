officiant:/əˈfɪʃiənt/| n.| 主持人；司仪；官员|A person who performs a ceremony, especially a wedding.|The officiant at their wedding was a close friend of the family.  他们的婚礼司仪是家里的一位密友。|近义词：celebrant, minister, priest, judge, moderator|反义词: None|noun


officiate:/əˈfɪʃieɪt/| v.| 主持；执行；担任公职|To act as an official in charge of something, especially a ceremony or a sporting event.|He officiated at their wedding. 他主持了他们的婚礼。|近义词：preside, administer, conduct, manage|反义词: None|verb
