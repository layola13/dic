insolvency's:/ɪnˈsɒlvənsiːz/
noun
无力偿还;破产;资不抵债
the state of not having enough money to pay what you owe
The company's insolvency led to its eventual liquidation. 该公司的无力偿还最终导致其清算。
insolvency's impact on the market was significant.  无力偿债对市场的影响是巨大的。
Near-synonyms: bankruptcy, failure, ruin
Antonyms: solvency, affluence, prosperity
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insolvens, from in- (not) + solvens (paying), present participle of solvere (to loosen, dissolve, pay).
