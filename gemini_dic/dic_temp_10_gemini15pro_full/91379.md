hydrosulphurous:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈsʌlfərəs/| adj.|连二亚硫酸的，低亚硫酸的|Containing or derived from hydrosulfurous acid (H2S2O4).  This acid is unstable and is not typically isolated in pure form.  The term "hydrosulphurous" generally refers to salts containing the dithionite anion (S2O42-), such as sodium dithionite.|Sodium hydrosulphurous is a powerful reducing agent. 连二亚硫酸钠是一种强还原剂。|Synonyms: dithionous (more common in modern chemistry)|Antonyms: N/A (lacks a direct chemical opposite)|adjective
