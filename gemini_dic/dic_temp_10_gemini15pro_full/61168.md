elucidating:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪŋ/
verb
阐明，说明
making something clear; explaining
The professor spent an hour elucidating the complex theory to his students. 教授花了一个小时向学生们阐明这个复杂的理论。
Synonyms: clarifying, explaining, illuminating, expounding
Antonyms: obscuring, confusing, concealing
verb
