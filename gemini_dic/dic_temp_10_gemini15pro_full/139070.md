oath:/oʊθ/| n.|誓言，誓约；诅咒|A solemn promise, often invoking a divine witness, regarding one's future action or behavior; a curse.|She took an oath to tell the truth. 她发誓说实话。|近义词：pledge, vow, promise, swear|反义词：falsehood, lie, perjury|noun
