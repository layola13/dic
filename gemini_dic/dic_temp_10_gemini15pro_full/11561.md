aristocratize:/əˌrɪstəˈkrəˌtaɪz/
verb
使贵族化；使带有贵族气息；变得高贵
To give someone or something aristocratic qualities or characteristics; to make something more refined or elegant.
The wealthy merchant attempted to aristocratize his family by sending his sons to prestigious boarding schools.  这位富有的商人试图通过把他的儿子送到著名的寄宿学校来使他的家庭贵族化。

He tried to aristocratize his speech, avoiding slang and colloquialisms. 他试图使他的讲话贵族化，避免使用俚语和口语。

近义词:refine, elevate, ennoble
反义词:vulgarize, coarsen, demean
词性:verb
