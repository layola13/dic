eutectic:/juːˈtɛktɪk/
adjective|共熔的；易熔的|Relating to or denoting a mixture of substances (in fixed proportions) that melts and solidifies at a single temperature that is lower than the melting points of the separate constituents or of any other mixture of them.|The eutectic point of this alloy is reached at 70 degrees Celsius.|Near-eutectic|N/A|adjective
eutectic:/juːˈtɛktɪk/
noun|共晶；共熔混合物|A mixture of substances (in fixed proportions) that melts and solidifies at a single temperature that is lower than the melting points of the separate constituents or of any other mixture of them.|The solder used is a lead-tin eutectic.|Eutectoid|N/A|noun
