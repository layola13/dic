broodiest:/ˈbruːdiɪst/| adj.|最爱沉思的，最闷闷不乐的|Superlative adjective of broody: most broody.  It describes someone who is inclined to dwell moodily on their thoughts and feelings, often in a sullen or gloomy way.|例句：She was the broodiest of the three sisters, always lost in her own world. 她三姐妹中最爱沉思的，总是沉浸在自己的世界里。

例句：He gave me his broodiest stare, clearly unhappy with the situation. 他阴沉地盯着我，显然对情况很不满意。|近义词：sullen, gloomy, morose, contemplative, pensive|反义词：cheerful, jovial, happy, lighthearted|词性：形容词
