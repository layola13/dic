flange:/flændʒ/
n.|凸缘，法兰|A projecting rim, edge, rib, or collar used to strengthen an object, hold it in place, or attach it to another object. |The pipe was secured with a flange.  管道用法兰固定。|rim, edge, lip|center, core
词性: 名词

flanges:/flændʒɪz/
n.|凸缘，法兰（复数）|Plural form of "flange."  |The flanges on the wheels prevent the train from derailing.  车轮上的凸缘防止火车脱轨。|rims, edges, lips|centers, cores
词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *flange (“broad flat piece of metal”), from flanchir (“to bend sideways”), from Frankish *flankōn (“to bend”), from Proto-Germanic *flankō (“flank”).

近义词: rim, edge, lip, collar

反义词:  没有明确的反义词，视语境而定。例如，如果指“突出边缘”，反义词可以是“recess”（凹陷）。
