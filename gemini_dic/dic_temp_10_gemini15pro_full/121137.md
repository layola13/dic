misdelivering: /ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/
verb
投递错误；送错
failing to deliver something to the intended recipient, or delivering it to the wrong place or person
The package was misdelivered to the wrong address. 包裹被误送到错误的地址。
misdirecting, misrouting
delivering correctly, routing correctly
verb
