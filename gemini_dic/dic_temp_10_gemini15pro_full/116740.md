maunder:/ˈmɔːndər/ (UK) /ˈmɑːndər/ (US)| v. n.| 喃喃自语；漫步；闲逛|To talk in a low, unclear way; to wander or move about slowly and aimlessly. |例句：He maundered on about his childhood. 他絮絮叨叨地讲起了他的童年。|近义词：mutter, mumble, ramble, wander, roam|反义词：articulate, be direct, stay put|词性：动词, 名词

maunder:/ˈmɔːndər/ (UK) /ˈmɑːndər/ (US)| n.| 唠叨；漫步|A rambling or incoherent speech; an act of wandering aimlessly. |例句：The old man's maunder about the good old days bored everyone.  老人关于过去美好时光的唠叨让每个人都感到厌烦。|近义词：ramble, digression|反义词：conciseness, brevity|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain; perhaps related to obsolete *mander 'to beg'.
