parapleura:/ˈpærəˈplʊərə/| n. |侧膜，侧胸壁|The parapleura is the connective tissue layer lining the inner surface of the rib cage.|The surgeon carefully dissected the parapleura to access the lung. 手术医生仔细地解剖侧膜以接近肺部。|Synonyms: costal pleura, parietal pleura|Antonyms: visceral pleura|noun

Etymology:
From Greek "para-" (beside, alongside) + "pleura" (rib, side).
