fizzy:/ˈfɪzi/| adj.|嘶嘶作响的，起泡的|Containing bubbles of gas; effervescent.  Characterized by a light, airy, and bubbly quality.|The soda was fizzy and refreshing. 这苏打水嘶嘶作响，令人清爽。|Synonyms: bubbly, sparkling, effervescent, carbonated|Antonyms: flat, still|adjective
