encapsulating:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 封装的；概括的 | enclosing something in or as if in a capsule; summarizing or condensing | The new design encapsulates all the best features of the previous model.  新设计囊括了之前模型的所有最佳特性。 | Synonyms: summarizing, epitomizing, compressing, condensing | Antonyms: expanding, elaborating, detailing | adjective

encapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/
| v. |  封装；概括 | express the essential features of (something) succinctly. |  This paragraph encapsulates the main points of the article. 这段概括了文章的要点。| Synonyms: summarize, epitomize, sum up, condense | Antonyms: expand, elaborate, detail | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin "incapsulatus," past participle of "incapsulare" ("to enclose in a capsule"), from "in-" ("in") + "capsula" ("small box"), diminutive of "capsa" ("box").
