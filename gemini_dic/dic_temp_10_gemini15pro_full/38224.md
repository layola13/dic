computationally:/ˌkɑːmpjuˈteɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 计算上地；通过计算地 | In terms of or by means of computation. |  This problem is computationally intensive.  这个问题计算量很大。 |  Example:  Analyzing large datasets can be computationally expensive. 分析大型数据集在计算上可能很昂贵。

Example: The simulation was computationally demanding, requiring a powerful supercomputer.  这个模拟对计算要求很高，需要一台强大的超级计算机。 |  Synonym:  algorithmically, numerically | Antonym:  analytically, conceptually | 词性: 副词
