mesole:/ˈmesəʊl/| n.|间位, 中间体|A substance formed as an intermediate product in a chemical reaction.|The reaction produced a mesole which was then further processed.|近义词：intermediate, intermediary|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *mesos* "middle" + *-ol*  indicating an alcohol or phenol derivative.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *medhyo- "middle".
