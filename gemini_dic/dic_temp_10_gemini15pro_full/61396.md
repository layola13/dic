emblematist: /ˈɛmbləmətɪst/
n.
纹章学家，纹章设计者
A person who designs or studies emblems.
The king employed a skilled emblematist to design a new coat of arms. 国王聘请了一位技艺精湛的纹章学家来设计新的盾形徽章。

近义词: heraldist, armorist
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider someone who defaces or destroys emblems)
