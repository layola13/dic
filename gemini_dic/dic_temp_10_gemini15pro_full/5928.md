ambitiousnesses:/æmˈbɪʃəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|野心，雄心壮志（复数）|The ambitiousnesses of the young executives were evident in their aggressive business strategies. 年轻管理人员的雄心壮志在他们积极的商业策略中显而易见。|例句：Their ambitiousnesses knew no bounds. 他们的野心勃勃无止境。|近义词：aspirations, ambitions, drives|反义词：apathy, indifference, lack of motivation|词性:名词

Etymology: From ambitious + -ness.  "Ambitious" comes from the French "ambitieux," ultimately derived from the Latin "ambitio,"  meaning "a going around" (especially to solicit votes), from "ambire" "to go around." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
