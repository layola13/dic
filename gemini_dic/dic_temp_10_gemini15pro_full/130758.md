nonamphibian:/ˌnɒnæmˈfɪbiən/
| adj. n.|非两栖类的，非两栖动物|Not pertaining to or characteristic of an amphibian; not living both on land and in water.  A creature that is not an amphibian.|The zoo has a diverse collection of nonamphibian reptiles.|近义词：terrestrial, land-dwelling|反义词:amphibian|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + amphibian.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of."  "Amphibian" comes from the Greek "amphíbios," meaning "living a double life,"  from "amphí" (on both sides) + "bios" (life).
