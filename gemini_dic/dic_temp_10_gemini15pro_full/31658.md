chatterers:/ˈtʃætərərz/
n.
饶舌者，喋喋不休的人
People who talk rapidly and continuously, especially about unimportant things.
The cafe was full of chatterers, making it difficult to concentrate. 咖啡馆里满是喋喋不休的人，很难集中注意力。
The group of chatterers gossiped about the latest celebrity news. 这群饶舌者八卦着最新的名人新闻。
近义词：gossips, talkers, jabberers
反义词：quiet people, introverts
词性: noun (plural)

chatterer:/ˈtʃætərər/
n.
饶舌者，喋喋不休的人
A person who talks rapidly and continuously, especially about unimportant things.
He's such a chatterer, he never seems to stop talking. 他真是个饶舌的人，似乎永远不会停止说话。
She's known as a chatterer, always sharing the latest gossip. 她以饶舌而闻名，总是分享最新的八卦。
近义词：gossip, talker, jabberer
反义词：quiet person, introvert
词性: noun (singular)
