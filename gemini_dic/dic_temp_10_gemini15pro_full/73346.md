forefighter:/ˈfɔːrˌfaɪtər/ (US), /ˈfɔːˌfaɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消防员|A person whose job is to extinguish fires and rescue people.|The firefighters bravely rushed into the burning building. 消防队员勇敢地冲进燃烧的建筑物。|Example:  Firefighters work long hours and face dangerous situations. 消防员工作时间长，面临危险的情况。|Synonyms: fireman, firewoman, smoke eater|Antonyms: arsonist|Noun
