out-fencing: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪŋ/
|v.| 击败；胜过；以击剑胜过|To defeat or surpass someone in fencing.  More broadly, to outmaneuver or surpass someone in a competitive situation, often through superior skill or strategy.|She out-fenced her opponent in the final round and won the gold medal. 她最后一轮击败了对手，赢得了金牌。|He out-fenced his rivals in the business deal and secured the contract. 他在商业交易中胜过对手，并获得了合同。|Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, outperform, outwit, defeat|Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by|verb
