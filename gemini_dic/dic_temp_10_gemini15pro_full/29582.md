catechetics:/ˌkætəˈkɛtɪks/ | n. | 教理问答；教理教育学 |  The study and practice of religious instruction, especially in the Christian faith. It encompasses the methods and materials used to teach the doctrines, beliefs, and practices of the faith. |  The parish priest dedicated much time to catechetics, ensuring the children understood the tenets of their faith.  教区神父花了很多时间在教理问答上，确保孩子们理解他们信仰的原则。| synonyms: religious education, religious instruction | antonyms: secular education, irreligion | noun
