melligo:/məˈlaɪɡoʊ/
noun
白癜风
A skin condition characterized by the loss of pigment, resulting in patches of lighter skin.
Melanin is lost from areas of skin, causing whitish patches.
Melligo is a common skin condition. 白癜风是一种常见的皮肤病。
The patient presented with patches of melligo on his hands and face. 病人的手和脸上出现白癜风斑块。
