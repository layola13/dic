denigration:/ˌdɛnɪˈɡreɪʃən/
n.|诋毁，诽谤，贬低|The denigration of her character was completely unfair. 对她性格的诋毁完全是不公平的。|例句：The constant denigration of his work by his boss made him quit.  他老板不断贬低他的工作，使他辞职了。

例句：The politician's denigration of his opponent was seen as unprofessional.  这位政客对对手的诋毁被认为是不专业的。
|近义词：defamation, slander, libel, disparagement, belittling|反义词：praise, commendation, acclaim, exaltation|词性: 名词
