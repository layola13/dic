despondently:/dɪˈspɒndəntli/ | adv. | 沮丧地，失望地 | In a despondent manner; with low spirits and discouragement. | She sighed despondently and turned away. 她沮丧地叹了口气，转过身去。|  He walked despondently back to his apartment after being rejected. 他被拒绝后，沮丧地走回公寓。 | 近义词: dejectedly, despairingly, glumly, hopelessly, miserably | 反义词: cheerfully, hopefully, optimistically | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the adjective "despondent," which comes from the Latin word "despondere" meaning "to lose heart, give up hope."  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective into an adverb.
