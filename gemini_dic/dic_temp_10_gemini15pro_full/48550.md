defalcation:/ˌdiːfɔːlˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 亏空；挪用；盗用 | The act of embezzling or misappropriating funds. | The defalcation of the company's funds led to its bankruptcy. 公司资金的亏空导致其破产。| Synonyms: embezzlement, misappropriation, peculation | Antonyms: restitution, repayment | noun
