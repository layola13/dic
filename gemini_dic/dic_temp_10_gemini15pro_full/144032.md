outrhymes:/ˌaʊtˈraɪmz/
verb
胜过…的韵律; 压过…的韵律; 比…押韵押得更好
to rhyme (something) with a word having more syllables or with a phrase
Example:  "moon" outrhymes "June". “moon”比“June”的音节更多。

Example: He outrhymed all the other poets at the competition. 他在比赛中比所有其他诗人的韵律都更好。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "be outdone by" or "lose to" in a rhyming context)
词性: verb
