obliterate:/əˈblɪtəreɪt/| vt.|清除，摧毁，抹去|To remove or destroy all traces of; do away with completely.|The bombing raid obliterated the small town.  轰炸袭击彻底摧毁了小镇。|synonyms: destroy, erase, eradicate, wipe out|antonyms: create, build, preserve|verb
