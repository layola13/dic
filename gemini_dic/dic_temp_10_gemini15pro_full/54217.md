disfigurer:/dɪsˈfɪɡjərər/| n. |毁容者，损毁者|Someone or something that disfigures.  A person or thing that spoils the appearance of something or someone.|The acid attack left her with permanent scars, making the attacker a disfigurer of her beauty.|近义词：deformer, mutilator, vandal|反义词：beautifier, decorator|noun
