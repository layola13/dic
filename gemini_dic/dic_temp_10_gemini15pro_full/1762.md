acquisitions:/ˌækwɪˈzɪʃənz/| n.|收购，获得，获得物|The company made several acquisitions last year to expand its market share.  这家公司去年进行了几次收购，以扩大其市场份额。|The museum's latest acquisition is a rare 17th-century painting. 该博物馆的最新藏品是一幅罕见的17世纪画作。|近义词：purchases, mergers, takeovers|反义词:divestments, disposals|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *acquysicioun*, from Old French *aquisition* and directly from Latin *acquisitiō* (“a getting, acquiring, obtaining”), from *acquīsītus*, perfect passive participle of *acquīrere* (“to acquire”).  *Acquīrere* is from *ad* (“to”) + *quaerere* (“to seek, gain”).

USAGE->
Plural Form:
The company's recent acquisitions have strengthened its position in the industry. = 该公司近期的收购增强了其在行业中的地位。
Several acquisitions were made to diversify the company's portfolio. =  进行了几项收购以使公司的投资组合多样化。

Prepositional Phrases:
The acquisition of new skills is essential for career advancement. = 获取新技能对于职业发展至关重要。
The museum is proud of its acquisition of a rare artifact. =  博物馆为获得一件稀有文物而感到自豪。

As part of a larger sentence:
The acquisitions contributed significantly to the company's growth. = 收购对公司的发展做出了重大贡献。
The acquisitions have been met with mixed reactions from investors. = 投资者对这些收购的反应褒贬不一。


PHRASE->
