noncompetitively:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/
adverb
非竞争性地，非竞争地
in a way that does not involve competition
The enzyme binds noncompetitively to the substrate.  这种酶以非竞争性的方式与底物结合。
The drug interacts noncompetitively with the receptor.  这种药物与受体非竞争性地相互作用。
Near synonyms: non-competitively
Antonyms: competitively
词性: 副词
