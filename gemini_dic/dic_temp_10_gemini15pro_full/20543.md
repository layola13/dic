blabber:/ˈblæbər/ | v. n. | 喋喋不休；胡言乱语；泄密 | to talk foolishly or carelessly; to reveal secrets | He blabbered about the surprise party. 他把惊喜派对的事泄露了. | synonyms: chatter, prattle, babble | antonyms: be quiet, keep silent | verb

blabber:/ˈblæbər/ | n. | 喋喋不休的人；胡言乱语 | foolish or careless talk |  His constant blabber annoyed everyone. 他不停的胡言乱语惹恼了所有人. | synonyms: chatterbox, prattler | antonyms: reticent person | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *blabberen, frequentative of a verb meaning "to blab," probably of imitative origin.
