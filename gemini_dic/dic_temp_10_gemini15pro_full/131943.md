noncontributable:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjətəbəl/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtrɪbjətəbəl/
adjective
不可贡献的；不能计入的
Not able to be contributed or counted towards something.
Employees' commuting expenses are generally noncontributable to a retirement plan.  员工的通勤费用通常不能计入退休计划。
The judge ruled that the evidence was noncontributable to the case. 法官裁定该证据与案件无关，不能作为证据。
near synonyms: non-deductible, ineligible, non-qualifying
antonyms: contributable, deductible, eligible
词性: adjective
