concussed:/kənˈkʌst/ | adj. | 脑震荡的；受震荡的 | Affected by concussion; stunned. | He was concussed after the fall and couldn't remember anything. 他摔倒后脑震荡，什么也记不起来了。 |  The boxer was clearly concussed after the knockout blow. 拳击手被击倒后明显脑震荡了。 | 近义词: dazed, stunned, bewildered | 反义词: alert, lucid | 词性: adjective
