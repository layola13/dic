melange:/meɪˈlɑːnʒ/ /məˈlɑːnʒ/| n.|混合物，大杂烩|A mixture of different things, often a pleasing or interesting one. |The market offered a melange of exotic spices and colorful fabrics.  市场上供应着各种异国香料和色彩鲜艳的布料。|synonyms: mixture, blend, medley, assortment, potpourri, hodgepodge|antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity|noun

melange:/meɪˈlɑːnʒ/ /məˈlɑːnʒ/| n.|混杂的人群|A varied mixture of people or things. |The city is a vibrant melange of cultures and ethnicities.  这座城市是一个充满活力的文化和种族的大熔炉。|synonyms: mix, blend, assortment|antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity|noun
