luckless:/ˈlʌklɪs/| adj.|不幸的；倒霉的|Unlucky; unfortunate.  Characterized by a persistent absence of good fortune.|例句：The luckless gambler lost all his money.  这个倒霉的赌徒输光了所有的钱。|近义词：unlucky, unfortunate, hapless, ill-fated, jinxed|反义词：lucky, fortunate, blessed|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *lukles*, from Old English *lūclēas* (“lacking luck”), equivalent to luck +‎ -less. Cognate with Scots luckless (“unlucky”), Dutch lukloos (“luckless”), German *lücklos*.
