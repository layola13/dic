misspenders:/ˌmɪsˈspɛndərz/
|n.|挥霍者，浪费者|People who spend money unwisely or wastefully are misspenders.|那些不明智地或浪费地花钱的人是挥霍者。|
例句：The misspenders quickly ran through their inheritance. 挥霍者很快就花光了他们的遗产。

例句：The government was accused of being misspenders of public funds. 政府被指控浪费公共资金。
|近义词：squanderers, wasters, profligates|反义词：savers, economizers|词性：名词|
