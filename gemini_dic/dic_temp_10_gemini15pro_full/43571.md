creaght: /kreɪxt/ | n. | 托儿所，保育院 (尤指爱尔兰旧时的一种集体保育方式)| A creaght was a form of fosterage or communal child-rearing practiced in Ireland and Scotland, particularly in the more remote, pastoral areas.|  The children of the village were cared for in the creaght during the day while their parents worked. 村里的孩子们白天在保育院里被照顾，而他们的父母则去工作。|  The creaght system provided an essential service to families in rural communities. 保育院系统为农村社区的家庭提供了必要的服务。| synonyms: nursery, daycare, crèche | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "home" in the context of childcare) | noun
