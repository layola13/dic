ammerman:/ˈæmərmən/ | n. | 铁匠，盔甲工 | A person who makes armor.  A skilled metalworker who creates protective armor, especially in medieval times. |  例句：The ammerman skillfully hammered the steel into shape. 铁匠熟练地把钢材锤打成形。

例句：The ammerman crafted a beautiful suit of armor for the king. 铁匠为国王打造了一套漂亮的盔甲。 | 近义词：armorer, blacksmith, metalsmith | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
