ownerless:/ˈoʊnərlɪs/
adjective
无主的，无所有者的
Without an owner; not belonging to anyone.
The ownerless dog wandered the streets.  这只无主的狗在街上游荡。
The ownerless boat drifted out to sea.  那艘无主的船漂向了大海。
synonyms:abandoned, stray, unclaimed, derelict
antonyms:owned, possessed, claimed
词性:形容词
