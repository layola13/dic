overexpressiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspresivnəs/ | n. | 过度表达，表情过于丰富 | The state or quality of expressing oneself excessively, often emotionally.  It refers to a tendency to communicate feelings and thoughts with exaggerated intensity or frequency. | Her overexpressiveness can be overwhelming at times. 她的过度表达有时会让人难以招架。 |  exuberance, effusiveness, demonstrativeness |  reserve, restraint, inhibition | noun
