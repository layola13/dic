mobilize:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |动员; 调动; |To organize and make something ready for use, especially for war or other emergencies; to make people or resources available for action.|The government mobilized the army for war. 政府动员军队准备战争。| 近义词: activate, deploy, marshal, rally | 反义词: demobilize, disband | verb

mobilize:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |(使)能活动; (使)能行走; |To make someone able to move after an illness or injury.|Doctors hope to mobilize him next week. 医生们希望下周能让他下床活动。|近义词: activate | 反义词: immobilize | verb
