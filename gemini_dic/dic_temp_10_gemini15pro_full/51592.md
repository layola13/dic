devaluing: /ˌdiːˈvæljuɪŋ/ | v. | 贬值；降低…的价值；贬低；轻视 | reducing or underestimating the worth or importance of something | The government's policies are devaluing the currency. 政府的政策正在使货币贬值。| synonyms: depreciating, cheapening, undermining, belittling | antonyms: appreciating, enhancing, valuing, praising | verb

devalue: /ˌdiːˈvæljuː/ | v. | 贬值；降低…的价值；贬低；轻视 | reduce or underestimate the worth or importance of something |  They devalued the currency by 10%. 他们将货币贬值了10%。| synonyms: depreciate, cheapen, undermine, belittle | antonyms: appreciate, enhance, value, praise | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (expressing reversal or negation) + value + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin valere "to be strong, be worth".
