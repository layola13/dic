distills:/dɪˈstɪlz/
verb
提取；蒸馏；吸取；渗出
extracts the essential meaning or elements of something; purifies a liquid by heating it so that it vaporizes, then cooling and condensing the vapor back into a liquid form; gives off or exudes (a liquid) slowly
The writer distills the essence of the story into a single paragraph.  这位作家将故事的精髓提炼成一段。
The distillery distills whiskey from fermented grain.  这家酿酒厂用发酵的谷物蒸馏威士忌。
The flowers distilled a sweet fragrance.  花朵散发出甜美的香味。
synonyms: extract, refine, concentrate, purify, condense
antonyms: combine, mix, dilute, adulterate
