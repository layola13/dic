nonphenomenal:/ˌnɑːn fəˈnɑːmɪnl/
adjective
非现象的；非经验的；超越现象的
Not pertaining to or perceptible by the senses; not apparent or observable.
His work explores the nonphenomenal realm of pure thought. 他的作品探索了纯粹思想的非现象领域。

His interest lies in the nonphenomenal world of abstract ideas. 他感兴趣的是抽象观念的非现象世界。

近义词:noumenal, transcendental, metaphysical
反义词:phenomenal, empirical, observable
词性:adjective
