drownings:/ˈdraʊnɪŋz/
noun
溺水事件（复数）
instances of people dying from suffocation in liquid, especially water.
There have been several drownings at the beach this summer. 今年夏天这片海滩发生了几起溺水事件。
近义词: fatalities, deaths (by drowning)
反义词: rescues, resuscitations
词性: Noun (plural)

drowning:/ˈdraʊnɪŋ/
noun
溺水
The act of dying from suffocation in liquid, especially water.
The lifeguard saved the child from drowning. 救生员把孩子从溺水中救了出来。
近义词: suffocation (in liquid)
反义词: survival, rescue
词性: Noun (singular)


词源: From Middle English *drownen, from Old English *druncnian, from Proto-Germanic *drunkjaną (“to drown”), from Proto-Indo-European *dhrug- (“to swallow”).
