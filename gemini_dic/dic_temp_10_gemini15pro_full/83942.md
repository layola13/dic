half-coaxingly:/ˌhɑːf kəʊˈæksɪŋli/
half-coaxingly| adv.|半哄半劝地|In a manner that is partly coaxing and partly something else, often implying a gentle persuasion mixed with another element like firmness or slight manipulation.|She half-coaxingly, half-firmly led the toddler away from the busy street. 她半哄半劝，半强硬地把小孩从繁忙的街道上带走。|近义词：persuasively, gently, encouragingly|反义词：forcefully, aggressively|词性:副词
half-coaxingly|词源： "half-" + "coaxingly". "Coax" comes from the Middle English "cokesen," of uncertain origin. | | | | |
