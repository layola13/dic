forgiving:/fərˈɡɪvɪŋ/
adj.
宽容的，宽恕的
readily disposed to forgive or excuse; merciful
She has a very forgiving nature. 她性情非常宽容。
近义词: merciful, lenient, tolerant, compassionate
反义词: unforgiving, resentful, vindictive
词性: 形容词

词源: From forgive + -ing.  Forgive comes from Old English forgiefan "to give, grant, allow; give up, remit (a debt); pardon," from for- "completely" + giefan "to give".
