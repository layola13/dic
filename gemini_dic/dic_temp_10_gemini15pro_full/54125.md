disenchant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃænt/ /ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃɑːnt/ | vt. | 使清醒，使不再抱幻想 | To free from illusion or false belief; disillusion. |  He became disenchanted with politics after seeing how corrupt the system was. 他在目睹了政治体制的腐败后对政治不再抱有幻想。

The experience disenchanted her with the idea of working abroad.  这次经历让她对出国工作的想法不再抱有幻想。


近义词: disillusion, undeceive, disappoint | 反义词: enchant, charm, fascinate | 词性: verb
