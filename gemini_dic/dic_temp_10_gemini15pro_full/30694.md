certificated:/sərˈtɪfɪkeɪtɪd/
|adj.|
|持有证书的，被证明的|
|Officially recognized as meeting certain standards, usually by having passed an examination or completed a course of study.|
|She is a certificated teacher. 她是一位持有教师资格证书的老师。|

|He is a certificated pilot. 他是一位持有飞行员执照的飞行员。|


|certified, licensed, qualified|
|uncertified, unlicensed, unqualified|
|adjective|

Etymology->
From certificate + -ed, ultimately from Latin certus "sure"
