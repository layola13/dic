nonplacental:/ˌnɑːnpləˈsɛntəl/
adjective
非胎盘的
Not having or developing a placenta.  Relating to or characteristic of animals whose embryos develop without a placenta.
The platypus is a nonplacental mammal.  鸭嘴兽是一种非胎盘哺乳动物。
Marsupials are nonplacental mammals. 有袋动物是非胎盘哺乳动物。
近义词:aplacental
反义词:placental
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + placental.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "placental" refers to the placenta, the organ that connects the developing fetus to the uterine wall to allow nutrient uptake, waste elimination, and gas exchange via the mother's blood supply.
