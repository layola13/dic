noncomplacent:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpleɪsnt/ | adj. | 不自满的，不满足的 | Not complacent; not satisfied with the current situation and desiring improvement. |  The coach stressed the need for the team to remain noncomplacent despite their recent victories. 教练强调，尽管最近取得了胜利，球队仍需保持不满足现状。

The noncomplacent CEO constantly sought ways to innovate and expand the company.  这位不自满的首席执行官不断寻求创新和扩展公司的方法。

| 近义词:  dissatisfied, restless, driven, ambitious, uneasy | 反义词: complacent, satisfied, content, smug | 词性: 形容词
