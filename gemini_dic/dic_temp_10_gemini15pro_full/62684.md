engenderer:/ɪnˈdʒɛndərər/
n.
引起者；产生者；创造者
One who engenders; a begetter, producer, or creator.
The engenderer of the conflict remains unknown.  这场冲突的始作俑者仍然未知。
近义词:creator, producer, author, originator, father, parent
反义词:destroyer, ruiner
词性:名词
