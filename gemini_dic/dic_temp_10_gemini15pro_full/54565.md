dislodges:/dɪˈslɒdʒɪz/
verb
驱逐，取出，移动
knocks or forces something out of its position
The earthquake dislodged several boulders from the cliff face. 地震使几块巨石从悬崖面上掉落。
synonyms: remove, displace, shift, extricate, eject
antonyms: lodge, embed, fix, secure
词性: 动词
