partan:/ˈpɑːrtən/| n.|欧洲绿蟹; 螃蟹 (尤指苏格兰)|A large edible crab (Cancer pagurus) found in the North Atlantic and especially common around the coasts of Scotland and Ireland. |例句：The fishermen brought in a boatload of partans. 渔民们带回了一船的欧洲绿蟹。

例句：Partans are a popular delicacy in Scotland.  欧洲绿蟹在苏格兰是一道受欢迎的美味佳肴。|近义词：crab, edible crab|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could use "land animal" as a broad contrast) |词性:noun
