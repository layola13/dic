depressives:/dɪˈprɛsɪvz/
| n. |抑郁症患者|People suffering from clinical depression. | Many depressives find solace in support groups. 许多抑郁症患者在互助小组中找到了慰藉。| 近义词: sufferers of depression | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider) healthy individuals, mentally well people | 词性: Noun


Etymology:
From *depressive* (adjective), derived from *depress* (verb), ultimately from Latin *deprimere* ("to press down").  The suffix *-ive* forms adjectives, and in this case, the adjective is used substantively (as a noun) to refer to people who are depressive.
