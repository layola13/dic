blockaded:/blɒˈkeɪdɪd/ | adj. |被封锁的|Sealed off or isolated by a blockade; prevented from entering or leaving a place.|The blockaded city was running out of supplies. 被封锁的城市物资即将耗尽。|近义词：besieged, surrounded, closed off|反义词：open, accessible|词性:形容词

blockade: /blɒˈkeɪd/ | n. vt. |封锁，阻塞|An act or means of sealing off a place to prevent goods or people from entering or leaving.|The navy imposed a blockade on the enemy port. 海军对敌港实施了封锁。|近义词：siege, obstruction|反义词：opening, access|词性:名词,动词

词源: From French *blocus*, from Dutch *blokhuis* "blockhouse," from *blok* "block" + *huis* "house."  The verb is a back-formation from the noun.
