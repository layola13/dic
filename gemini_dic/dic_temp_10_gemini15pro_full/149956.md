pallors:/ˈpæləz/ | n. |苍白，脸色发白|An unhealthy paleness of the skin, typically from illness, shock, or fear.  A noticeable paleness of complexion.| Her face was drained of color, a mask of pallors. 她的脸色苍白，像一张没有血色的面具。|Synonyms: paleness, wanness, whiteness, lividness, ashenness|Antonyms: flush, ruddiness, rosiness, bloom, tan|noun
