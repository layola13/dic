forthcomingness:/ˌfɔːrθˈkʌmɪŋnəs/
|n.|坦率，直爽；乐于助人|The quality of being forthcoming; willingness to divulge information or to offer help.|Her forthcomingness about her past surprised everyone. 她对过去的坦诚让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: openness, candor, frankness, directness, honesty, sincerity,  cooperativeness|Antonyms: reticence, evasiveness, secretiveness, reluctance, unwillingness|noun

forthcoming:/ˌfɔːrθˈkʌmɪŋ/
|adj.|即将到来的；现成的；乐于助人的|1. About to appear or happen; 2. Readily available or produced; 3. Willing to divulge information or to offer help.|1. The forthcoming election is generating a lot of excitement. 即将到来的选举激起了很多人的兴奋。 2. Information about the new product was not forthcoming. 关于新产品的信息还没有公布。 3. He was very forthcoming about his plans. 他对自己的计划非常坦诚。|Synonyms: (1) approaching, imminent, impending (2) available, ready, accessible (3) open, frank, candid|Antonyms: (1) past, bygone (2) unavailable, scarce (3) reticent, evasive|adjective

词源:  From "forthcoming" (adjective), from "forth" + "coming."  "Forth"  means "forward" or "out," and "coming" refers to the act of approaching or appearing.  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective into a noun denoting the state or quality of being forthcoming.
