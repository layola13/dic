bioplasmic:/ˌbaɪoʊˈplæzmɪk/
adjective
生物原生质的
Relating to or consisting of bioplasm.
The bioplasmic membrane surrounds the cell. 生物原生质膜包围着细胞。
近义词: protoplasmic
反义词: None
词性: adjective

bioplasmic:/ˌbaɪoʊˈplæzmɪk/
noun
生物原生质
The living substance of a cell (excluding the nucleus).  This usage is considered outdated.
Early biologists studied the bioplasmic contents of cells. 早期生物学家研究细胞的原生质内容物。 (This usage is less common now.)
近义词: protoplasm
反义词: None
词性: noun
