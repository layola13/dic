coups:/kuːz/ /kuːps/| n. |政变；突然成功的行动|A sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government.|例句：The military coups in the country led to widespread unrest.  这个国家的军事政变导致了广泛的动乱。

例句：Several coups have taken place in the region in recent years. 近年来该地区发生了几次政变。|近义词：overthrow, takeover, rebellion, revolt |反义词:  compliance, submission|词性:名词(plural)
