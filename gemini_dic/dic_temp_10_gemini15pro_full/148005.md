overrigidly:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdʒɪdli/
| adv. | 过于严格地，过于死板地 | in an excessively rigid manner; too inflexibly. |  He applied the rules overrigidly, causing resentment among his staff. 他过于严格地执行规则，引起了员工的不满。 |例句：The child was raised overrigidly and lacked social skills.  这个孩子被过于严格地抚养长大，缺乏社交技能。 | 近义词：inflexibly, unyieldingly, strictly | 反义词：flexibly, leniently | 词性: 副词

overrigid:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdʒɪd/
| adj. | 过于严格的，过于死板的 | excessively rigid; too inflexible. |  His overrigid parenting style stifled his children's creativity.  他过于严格的教养方式扼杀了孩子们的创造力。 |例句：The company's overrigid hierarchy hindered communication. 公司过于僵化的等级制度阻碍了沟通。 | 近义词：inflexible, unyielding, strict | 反义词：flexible, lenient | 词性: 形容词

rigid:/ˈrɪdʒɪd/
| adj. | 严格的，死板的，僵硬的 | unable to bend or be forced out of shape; not flexible. |  The teacher had a rigid classroom structure. 这位老师的课堂结构很死板。 |例句：The metal beam was rigid and unyielding.  金属梁很坚硬，不会弯曲。 | 近义词：stiff, inflexible, firm | 反义词：flexible, pliable, soft | 词性: 形容词


USAGE->词源
From over- + rigid + -ly.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Rigid" comes from the Latin "rigidus" meaning stiff or hard. "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
