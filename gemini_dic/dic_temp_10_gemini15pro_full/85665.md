hautgout:/ˈoʊɡuː/ (US), /ˈəʊɡuː/ (UK)| n.|高级风味；（尤指略带腐败味的）浓郁香味；（引申义）过分的矫揉造作|A strong, slightly tainted flavor, often associated with game or cheese that is starting to decompose; (figuratively) excessive refinement or sophistication.|The cheese had a definite hautgout that wasn't unpleasant.  这奶酪有一种明显的浓郁香味，但并不令人讨厌。|The décor was a bit much, bordering on hautgout.  这装饰有点过了，近乎矫揉造作。|Synonyms: tang, savor, zest, piquancy (in the sense of strong flavor), affectation, pretension|Antonyms: blandness, insipidity, simplicity, naturalness|noun
