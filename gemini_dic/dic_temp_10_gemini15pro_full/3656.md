aggie: /ˈæɡi/ | n. | Aggie; 德州农工大学的学生; |A student or graduate of Texas A&M University.  It's a shortened, informal form of "Agricultural and Mechanical." |例句：
My brother is an Aggie, and he's very proud of it.
我的兄弟是德州农工大学的学生，他为此感到非常自豪。

例句：
The Aggies won the football game last weekend.
德州农工大学的球队上周末赢得了橄榄球比赛。


|近义词：Texas A&M student, |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a student from a rival university like Longhorn for a University of Texas student) |词性: noun
