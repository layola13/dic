formalistically:/ˌfɔːməˈlɪstɪkli/

词源:  From "formalist" + "-ically".  "Formalist" comes from "formal" (from Old French "formal" and ultimately Latin "formalis"), meaning "pertaining to form," with the suffix "-ist" denoting adherence to a doctrine or practice.  "-Ically" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
