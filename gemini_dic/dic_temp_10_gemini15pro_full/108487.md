layaway: /ˈleɪəˌweɪ/ (US) /ˈleɪəweɪ/ (UK)| n.|分期付款，预留付款|A system of paying for goods in installments, where the goods are kept by the store until the final payment is made.|I put the new TV on layaway. 我把新电视机分期付款了。|The store offered a layaway plan for Christmas gifts. 这家商店为圣诞礼物提供分期付款计划。|Synonyms: installment plan, payment plan|Antonyms: full payment|Noun
