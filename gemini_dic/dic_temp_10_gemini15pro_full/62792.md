engrosses:/ɪnˈɡroʊsɪz/
|v.|全神贯注于，使沉浸于|absorbs all the attention or interest of.|The intricate plot of the novel completely engrossed her.|近义词: absorbs, captivates, monopolizes, preoccupies|反义词: bores, distracts, disinterests|词性:动词
词源: From Old French engrossir "to make thick, enlarge, increase," from en- "in" + grossir "to make big," from gros "big."  The meaning shifted from "making something larger" to "taking up all of someone's attention."
