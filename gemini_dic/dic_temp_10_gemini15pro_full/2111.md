adapt:/əˈdæpt/ (US) /əˈdɑːpt/ (UK)| v. |适应；改编|To adjust to different conditions or uses.|例句：The animals quickly adapted to their new environment. 这些动物很快适应了它们的新环境。

The play was adapted from a novel.  这个剧本是由一部小说改编的。

|近义词：adjust, modify, accommodate, acclimatize, conform|反义词: remain, stay, resist|词性:动词

adapt:/əˈdæpt/ (US) /əˈdɑːpt/ (UK)| adj. |适合的；有适应能力的|Suitable or able to adjust to different conditions.|例句：She is an adapt learner and picks up new skills quickly. 她是一个适应能力强的学习者，能很快掌握新技能。

This type of plant is highly adapt to dry climates. 这种植物非常适应干燥的气候。


|近义词：suitable, appropriate, adaptable, flexible|反义词: unsuitable, inappropriate, inflexible|词性:形容词 (较少使用, adaptable 更为常见)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adaptare, from Latin ad- + aptare "to fit," from aptus "fit, suitable."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He adapts well to new situations. = 他能很好地适应新环境。
She adapts her teaching methods to suit different students. = 她调整教学方法以适应不同的学生。

一般过去时:
They adapted quickly to the colder climate. = 他们很快适应了较冷的气候。
The company adapted its marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. = 公司调整了营销策略，以覆盖更广泛的受众。

现在完成时:
The school has adapted its curriculum to include more technology. = 学校调整了课程，增加了更多技术内容。
We have adapted to the new working conditions. = 我们已经适应了新的工作环境。

过去完成时:
They had adapted the building for use as a school before the term started. =  开学前，他们已经将这栋建筑改建成学校。
He had adapted the novel into a screenplay before he died. = 他去世前把小说改编成了剧本。

将来时:
We will adapt our plans if necessary. = 如果有必要，我们将调整我们的计划。
The animals will adapt to the changing climate over time. = 随着时间的推移，动物们将适应不断变化的气候。

被动语态:
The book was adapted for the screen. = 这本书被改编成了电影。
The building has been adapted for disabled access. =  该建筑已改建为无障碍通道。


PHRASE->
adapt to = 适应...
adapt from = 从...改编
be adapted for = 被改编为...
be adapted to = 适应于...
well adapted = 非常适应
