hovers:/ˈhʌvərz/ (US), /ˈhɒvəz/ (UK)| v.|盘旋，徘徊，犹豫不决|To remain in one place in the air. To remain in or near one place or state.|The hummingbird hovers over the flower.  蜂鸟在花朵上盘旋。|The helicopter hovered over the landing pad. 直升机在降落坪上方盘旋。|synonyms: float, drift, linger, waver|antonyms: plunge, dive, descend|verb

hovers:/ˈhʌvərz/ (US), /ˈhɒvəz/ (UK)| n.|盘旋，徘徊 (较少用作名词)|The act of hovering.|The hawk's hover allowed it to spot its prey.  鹰的盘旋让它能够发现猎物。|The prolonged hover of the drone irritated the neighbors. 无人机长时间的盘旋激怒了邻居。|synonyms: suspension, floating|antonyms: descent, landing|noun
