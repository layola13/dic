overtalk: /ˌoʊvərˈtɔːk/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈtɔːk/ (UK) | v. | 打断别人说话；滔滔不绝地说；盖过…的声音 | To speak over someone else, interrupting them or making it difficult to hear them. | "He tends to overtalk in meetings, making it hard for others to contribute." 他在会议上总是打断别人说话，让其他人很难发表意见。 | "The loud music overtalked the conversation." 喧闹的音乐盖过了谈话声。 | 近义词: interrupt, dominate, drown out | 反义词: listen, be quiet | 词性: 动词
