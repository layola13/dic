nondeleteriously:/ˌnɒndɛlɪˈtɪəriəsli/
adverb
无害地，非破坏性地
in a way that does not harm or damage something.
The mutation nondeleteriously affects the protein's function.  这个突变不会对蛋白质的功能造成有害影响。
The chemical was found to interact nondeleteriously with the environment. 经发现，这种化学物质与环境的相互作用是无害的。

Synonym: harmlessly, innocuously, safely, benignly
Antonym: deleteriously, harmfully, destructively
词性: 副词
