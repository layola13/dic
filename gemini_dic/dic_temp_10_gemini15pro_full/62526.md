endure:/ɪnˈdjʊər/ (US), /ɪnˈdʒʊə(r)/ (UK)| v.|忍受，忍耐；持续，持久|To suffer (something painful or difficult) patiently over a long period; To remain in existence; last.|例句：She endured years of hardship. 她忍受了多年的苦难。|近义词：tolerate, bear, withstand, last, persist, continue|反义词: succumb, yield, surrender|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *enduren*, borrowed from Old French *endurer*, from Latin *indūrāre* (“to harden, make hard, inure”), from *in-* + *dūrus* (“hard”).
