geocaching: /ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃɪŋ/ /ˈdʒiːəʊˌkæʃɪŋ/
noun
寻宝游戏；地理寻宝
The outdoor recreational activity of searching for and finding a hidden object by means of GPS coordinates posted on a website.
例句：We went geocaching in the woods last weekend.  我们上周末去树林里玩寻宝游戏了。

例句：Geocaching is a fun way to explore new places. 地理寻宝是探索新地方的一种有趣方式。

近义词：treasure hunting, letterboxing
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "staying indoors")
词性: noun
