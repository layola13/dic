endochondral:/ˌɛndoʊˈkɒndrəl/| adj.|软骨内成的，软骨内的|Formed or occurring within cartilage; relating to the development of bone within cartilage.  The process of endochondral ossification, where cartilage is replaced by bone during development.|Endochondral bone formation is essential for the growth of long bones. 软骨内成骨对于长骨的生长至关重要。

The fracture healed by endochondral ossification.  骨折通过软骨内骨化愈合。|近义词: cartilaginous|反义词: intramembranous|词性:adjective
