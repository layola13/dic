impel:/ɪmˈpel/| v. |驱使，迫使|to drive, force, or urge (someone) to do something|His conscience impelled him to confess. 他的良心迫使他坦白。|例句：Fear impelled her to run. 恐惧驱使她逃跑。

例句：The desire for peace impelled him to negotiate. 对和平的渴望促使他进行谈判。

近义词：compel, force, drive, urge, motivate|反义词：restrain, deter, discourage|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin impellere, from in- 'towards' + pellere 'to push, drive'.
