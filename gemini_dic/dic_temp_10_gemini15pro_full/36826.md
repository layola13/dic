colonics:/kəˈlɑːnɪks/
n.
结肠灌洗法
The process of cleansing the colon by flushing it with water or other fluids.
She underwent a series of colonics to detoxify her body. 她进行了一系列结肠灌洗来排毒。
近义词:colonic irrigation, colonic hydrotherapy
反义词:(None specific - perhaps 'laxative' as a less invasive alternative)
词性:noun
