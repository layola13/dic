antiracisms:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪzəmz/
n.
反种族主义,  反种族主义行为/实践
The various practices and ideologies aimed at opposing racism and promoting racial equality.
Colleges are now implementing new antiracisms trainings for faculty and staff.  大学现在正在为教职员工实施新的反种族主义培训。
Antiracisms initiatives are gaining momentum across the country. 全国各地的反种族主义倡议正在蓬勃发展。
近义词: anti-discrimination, anti-bigotry
反义词: racism, bigotry
词性: 名词
