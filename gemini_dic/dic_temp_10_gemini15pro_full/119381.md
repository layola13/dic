microcyst: /ˈmaɪkrəʊsɪst/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊsɪst/ (US) | n. | 微囊肿 | A small cyst, often microscopic. A cyst is a closed sac, having a distinct membrane and developing abnormally in a body cavity or structure. |  Microscopic examination revealed numerous microcysts in the tissue sample. 显微镜检查显示组织样本中存在大量微囊肿。 |  The doctor discovered a microcyst during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中发现了一个微囊肿。 | Synonyms: microcystic lesion, tiny cyst | Antonyms:  macroscopic cyst, large cyst | noun
