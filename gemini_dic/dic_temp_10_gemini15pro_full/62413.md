endognath:/ˈɛndəɡnæθ/| n.|内颚类动物|An arthropod of the class Entognatha, such as a springtail, having the mouthparts recessed within the head.|Springtails are endognaths that live in soil and leaf litter.  弹尾虫是生活在土壤和落叶层中的内颚类动物。|近义词:entognathous insect|反义词:ectognath|词性:noun

endognathous:/ˌɛndəɡˈneɪθəs/| adj.|内颚的|Having the mouthparts enclosed within the head, as in springtails.|Collembola are endognathous arthropods.  跳虫是内颚的节肢动物。|近义词:entognathic|反义词:ectognathous|词性:adjective
