cartographies: /kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfiz/ | n. | 制图学; 地图集; 地图绘制法 |  The study and practice of making maps. It encompasses all aspects of map creation, from surveying and data collection to design and reproduction. |  Her research focuses on the cartographies of power. 她的研究集中于权力制图。 The library has a vast collection of historical cartographies.  图书馆收藏了大量历史地图。 | 近义词: mapping, mapmaking, chartmaking | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "misrepresentation" if referring to deliberately inaccurate maps) | 词性: 名词 (plural)

Etymology: From French *cartographie*, from *carte* (map) + *-graphie* (-graphy). Ultimately from Greek *khartēs* (papyrus leaf, map) + *-graphia* (writing).
