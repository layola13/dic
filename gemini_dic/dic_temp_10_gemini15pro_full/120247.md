minglingly:/ˈmɪŋɡlɪŋli/
adverb
混合地，交融地
in a mingling manner; in a way that involves mixing or blending together.
The scents of flowers and freshly cut grass mingled minglingly in the air. 花香和新割的草的气味在空气中混合在一起。
Synonyms:  intermixedly, blendedly, combinedly, fusedly
Antonyms:  separately, distinctly, individually
词性: 副词
