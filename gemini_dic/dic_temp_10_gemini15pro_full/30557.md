cephalotrocha:/ˌsefələˈtroʊkə/ | n. | 头盘幼虫; 头盘担轮幼虫 | A free-swimming larval stage of certain polychaete worms, characterized by a ciliated band around the head. |  Cephalotrochae are commonly found in marine environments. 头盘幼虫常见于海洋环境。|  The cephalotrocha larva eventually develops into the adult worm. 头盘幼虫最终发育成成虫。 | 近义词: trochophore larva (更广义的担轮幼虫) | 反义词: adult worm | 词性: noun

Etymology:
cephalo- (Greek kephalē, "head") + -trocha (Greek trokhos, "wheel," referring to the ciliated band)
