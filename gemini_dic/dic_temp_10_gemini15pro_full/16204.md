bannered:/ˈbænərd/| adj.|用横幅装饰的，张挂着横幅的|Decorated or furnished with banners.  The streets were bannered for the festival. 节日的街道上挂满了横幅。|近义词：decorated, adorned, festooned|反义词: bare, undecorated|词性:形容词
词源: From the noun "banner" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Frankish *bandwo "sign, standard."
