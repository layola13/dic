clownery:/ˈklaʊnəri/| n.|小丑行为，滑稽可笑的行为; 愚蠢的行为|Foolish or playful behavior, often involving silly or exaggerated actions.  It can also refer to something that is poorly done or organized, suggesting incompetence or a lack of seriousness.|例句：The politician's speech was pure clownery, full of empty promises and ridiculous gestures. 这位政客的演讲完全是小丑行为，充满了空洞的承诺和荒谬的姿态。

例句：Their attempt to fix the car themselves resulted in a bit of clownery, with tools scattered everywhere and the engine still not working. 他们试图自己修理汽车，结果弄得一团糟，工具散落一地，发动机还是不能工作。|近义词：antics, buffoonery, tomfoolery, shenanigans|反义词：seriousness, solemnity, dignity|词性:noun
