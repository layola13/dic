limic:/ˈlɪmɪk/
adjective
泥的；淤泥的；与淤泥有关的
of or relating to mud or slime; living in mud
The limic organisms thrived in the swamp. 这些泥生生物在沼泽中 thrive。
近义词：muddy, slimy
反义词：clean, dry
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *limus (“mud, slime”), from Proto-Indo-European *ley- (“to smear, stick”).
