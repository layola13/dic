molding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|  造型，塑造，模塑；装饰线条 |The process of shaping a material, such as plastic or metal, by pouring it into a mold; a decorative strip of wood, plastic, etc., used for ornamentation. |  The molding around the windows was intricately carved. 窗户周围的装饰线条雕刻精美。| synonyms: shaping, forming, casting, trim, cornice | antonyms:  destroying, deforming | noun

molding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈməʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|  塑造的，成型的 | Participating in giving form or shape to something. | The molding process is crucial to the final product.  成型过程对最终产品至关重要。| synonyms: formative, shaping | antonyms: destructive, deformative | adjective
