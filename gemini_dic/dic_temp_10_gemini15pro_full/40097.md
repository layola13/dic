contaminated:/kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. |被污染的；被感染的 | Made impure or unclean by contact or mixture with something harmful or noxious. | The water supply was contaminated with bacteria.  供水被细菌污染了。| polluted, tainted, infected, defiled | pure, clean, sterile, unblemished | adjective

Etymology->
From Late Latin contaminatus, past participle of contaminare "to pollute," from Latin con- "together" + taminare "to defile," from tagmen, accusative of tag-, root of tangere "to touch."
