grungers:/ˈɡrʌndʒərz/
n.
邋遢摇滚乐迷；垃圾摇滚乐迷
People who are fans of grunge music, a subgenre of alternative rock characterized by distorted electric guitars, often angsty lyrics, and a generally disaffected or apathetic attitude.
The grungers gathered at the music festival.  垃圾摇滚乐迷聚集在音乐节上。

近义词:fans, enthusiasts, followers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) mainstream music lovers

词性:noun (plural)
