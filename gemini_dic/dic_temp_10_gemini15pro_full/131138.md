nonblindingly:/ˌnɒnˈblaɪndɪŋli/
adv. |非盲地，不设盲地|In a way that does not involve blinding (concealing information about the treatment from participants or researchers in a study). |The researchers nonblindingly administered the drug to the control group. 研究人员非盲地给对照组服用了药物。|openly, transparently|secretly, blindly|adverb
