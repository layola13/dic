fumify:/ˈfjuːmɪfaɪ/
verb
熏蒸；以烟熏消毒
to treat or disinfect with fumes, especially to kill insects or other pests.
The greenhouse was fumigated to eliminate the whiteflies.  温室经过熏蒸以消灭白粉虱。
The furniture was fumigated to get rid of the woodworm.  家具被熏蒸以清除蛀虫。
synonyms: fumigate, disinfest, exterminate
antonyms: infest, contaminate
词性: 动词
