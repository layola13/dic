banshee:/ˈbænʃiː/| n. |报丧女妖|A female spirit in Irish folklore whose wailing warns of impending death in a family.|Legend says that if you hear a banshee wail, someone in your family will soon die.| 近义词:harbinger, omen, wraith |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From Irish *bean sídhe*  "woman of the fairy mound," from *ben* "woman" + *síd* (genitive *síde*) "fairy mound."
