ha'penny:/ˈheɪpni/ (UK) /ˈheɪpəni/ (US)| n. | 半便士（英国旧货币，等于一便士的二分之一） |A former British coin equal to half a penny. |例句：It used to cost a ha'penny to ride on the bus. 过去乘坐公共汽车只需半便士。

例句：She gave the boy a ha'penny for sweets. 她给了那个男孩半便士买糖果。

近义词：halfpenny (more common modern spelling)|反义词: (No direct opposite, but perhaps "pound" or "shilling" as larger denominations) |词性: noun
