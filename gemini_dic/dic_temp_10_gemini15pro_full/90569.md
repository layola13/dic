hui:/ˈhuːiː/  /ˈhui/
n.| n. |(New Zealand) a Māori or Pacific Islander social gathering | A meeting or assembly, especially of Māori people. |例句：There's a big hui on at the marae this weekend.  这周末在毛利人会堂有一个大型聚会。|近义词：meeting, gathering, assembly|反义词: N/A
词性: 名词


词源: The word "hui" originates from the Māori language of New Zealand.  In Māori, "hui" refers to a gathering, assembly, or meeting.  Its use as a pronoun is a more recent development in New Zealand slang, particularly among younger generations.
