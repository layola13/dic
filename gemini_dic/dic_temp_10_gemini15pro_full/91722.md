hypercyanotic:/ˌhaɪpərˌsaɪəˈnɒtɪk/ | adj. | 深紫绀的，极度紫绀的 |  Marked by or causing extreme cyanosis (a bluish discoloration of the skin resulting from poor circulation or inadequate oxygenation of the blood).  It's a medical term used to describe a severe degree of cyanosis. | The baby was hypercyanotic shortly after birth.  这个婴儿出生后不久就出现了严重的紫绀。

The patient became hypercyanotic during the asthma attack. 病人在哮喘发作期间变得严重紫绀。 | Near synonyms: deeply cyanotic, extremely cyanotic | Antonyms:  pink, ruddy (indicating good oxygenation) | adjective
