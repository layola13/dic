pamphleting:/ˈpæmflɪtɪŋ/
n.
散发传单；发放小册子
the activity of distributing pamphlets
Example: Pamphleting is a common way to spread political messages.  散发传单是传播政治信息的一种常见方式。

Example:  The students engaged in pamphleting to raise awareness about environmental issues. 学生们散发传单以提高人们对环境问题的认识。

近义词: leafleting, distributing, circulating
反义词: suppressing, censoring
词性: noun (gerund)


pamphlet:/ˈpæmflɪt/
n.
小册子；传单
a small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments about a single subject.
Example:  They picked up a pamphlet about local attractions. 他们拿了一本关于当地景点的宣传册。

Example: The pamphlet outlined the benefits of the new healthcare plan.  这本小册子概述了新医疗保健计划的好处。


近义词: brochure, leaflet, booklet, flyer
反义词: book, tome
词性: noun
