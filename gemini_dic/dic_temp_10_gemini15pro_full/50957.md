designedly:/dɪˈzaɪnɪdli/| adv.|故意地，蓄意地|In a deliberate or intentional manner; purposely. |She designedly left the door unlocked. 她故意没锁门。|He designedly insulted her. 他故意侮辱她。|近义词：intentionally, deliberately, purposely, on purpose|反义词：accidentally, unintentionally, inadvertently|词性: 副词

词源:
From design + -ed + -ly.  "Design" comes from the Latin "designare," meaning "to mark out."
