cajun:/ˈkeɪdʒən/| adj. n.|卡津人（的），卡津文化的|A descendant of French-speaking Acadian exiles from Canada.  Relating to the Cajun people or their culture, especially with reference to their folk music.|例句：Cajun music is known for its lively rhythms. 卡津音乐以其活泼的节奏而闻名。

例句：He makes authentic Cajun cuisine. 他做正宗的卡津菜。|近义词：Acadian, Creole|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
