impunctual: /ˌɪmpʌŋkˈtʃuːəl/ | adj. | 不守时的，不准时的 | Not arriving or doing something at the expected or correct time; late. | He was consistently impunctual, always arriving late for meetings. 他总是迟到，开会总是迟到。|  She was impunctual for the dinner party, arriving a full hour late.  她参加晚宴迟到了，迟到了整整一个小时。| 近义词: late, tardy, unpunctual, overdue | 反义词: punctual, prompt, timely | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + punctual.
