effectiveness:/ɪˈfɛktɪvnɪs/| n.|有效性，有效率，效力|The effectiveness of the new drug is still being studied.  这种新药的有效性仍在研究中。|例句：The effectiveness of the medication is impressive.  这种药物的疗效令人印象深刻。

The program's effectiveness in reducing crime is debatable.  该项目在减少犯罪方面的有效性是有争议的。|近义词：efficiency, efficacy, potency, power|反义词：ineffectiveness, inefficiency, impotence|词性:名词

Etymology: From effective + -ness.  Effective comes from Late Middle English: from medieval Latin effectivus, from Latin effect- ‘brought about’ (past participle of efficere; see effect) + -ive.
