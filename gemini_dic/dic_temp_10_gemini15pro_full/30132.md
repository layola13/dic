cedule:/ˈsɛdjuːl/ /ˈʃɛdjuːl/
noun
清单; 进度表; (尤指官方的)通知, 布告
A cedule is a schedule or a list, especially an official notice or announcement.
The cedule of events for the festival was posted online.  节日活动安排表已在网上公布。
The court issued a cedule outlining the terms of the settlement. 法院发布了一份列出和解条款的通知。
synonym: schedule, list, notice, announcement
antonym: disorder, chaos
