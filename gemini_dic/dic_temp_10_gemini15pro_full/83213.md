gweilo:/ˈɡwaɪloʊ/ (US) /ˈɡweɪloʊ/ (UK)| n.|鬼佬 (贬义)|A Cantonese slang term for a white person, often considered offensive due to its historical association with colonialism and racism.  It literally translates to "ghost man."|Example 1:  He heard someone call him "gweilo" as he walked down the street. 他走在街上时听到有人叫他“鬼佬”。

Example 2:  The use of the word "gweilo" can be considered highly offensive.  使用“鬼佬”这个词会被认为是非常冒犯的。|Synonym:  foreigner (more neutral), expat (more neutral)|Antonym:  local (although not a direct opposite, it represents the contrasting group)|Noun
