coalfish:/ˈkoʊlfɪʃ/| n. |黑线鳕，绿青鳕|A dark North Atlantic fish (Pollachius virens) related to and resembling the cod, valued as a food fish.|例句：Coalfish are abundant in the North Atlantic. 黑线鳕在北西洋很丰富。

例句：We caught several coalfish while fishing off the coast of Norway. 我们在挪威海岸捕到了几条黑线鳕。|近义词：pollock, saithe|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |词性:noun
