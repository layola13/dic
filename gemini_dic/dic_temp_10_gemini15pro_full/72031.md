flippantly:/ˈflɪpəntli/
adverb
轻率地，轻浮地
in a way that shows you do not take something as seriously as you should
He flippantly dismissed their concerns. 他轻率地驳回了他们的担忧。
近义词：casually, frivolously, lightly, impertinently
反义词：seriously, respectfully, thoughtfully
词性：adverb

flippant:/ˈflɪpənt/
adjective
轻率的，轻浮的
not showing a serious or respectful attitude
a flippant remark 轻率的言论
近义词：cheeky, irreverent, disrespectful
反义词：serious, respectful, earnest
词性：adjective

词源:  From the verb "flip," meaning "to flick" or "move quickly," suggesting a quick, thoughtless response.  The "-ant" suffix transforms it into an adjective, while "-ly" makes it an adverb.
