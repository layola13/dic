chargehouse: /ˈtʃɑːrdʒhaʊs/ | n. | 充電站，充電房 | A building or facility equipped with charging stations for electric vehicles. |  Example:  The new chargehouse has multiple charging stations for different types of EVs.  新的充电站有多个不同类型电动汽车的充电桩。

Example: We stopped at a chargehouse on our road trip to recharge the car's battery. 我们在公路旅行中停在一个充电站给汽车电池充电。 | 近义词: charging station, EV charging station | 反义词: gas station | 词性: 名词


词源:  Compound of "charge" (from Old French "charger" - to load, burden) and "house" (from Old English "hūs").
