blastier:/ˈblɑːstiər/ | adj. | 更猛烈的，更爆炸性的 |  Comparative form of "blasty," meaning more prone to explosions or having a greater explosive force. | The new fireworks were blastier than last year's. 新的烟花比去年的更猛烈。 |  The demolition crew used a blastier explosive to bring down the building.  爆破小组使用了一种更猛烈的炸药来拆除建筑物。 | 近义词：more explosive, more powerful, stronger | 反义词：weaker, less explosive |词性: 形容词
