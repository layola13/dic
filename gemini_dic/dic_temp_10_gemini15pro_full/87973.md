high-hat:/ˈhaɪˌhæt/| n. v.|踩镲（一种打击乐器）; 瞧不起；冷落|A pair of cymbals operated by a foot pedal.  To treat someone with disdain or aloofness.|例句：The drummer's high-hat kept a steady beat. 鼓手的踩镲保持着稳定的节奏。|近义词：cymbal, sneer, snub|反义词: respect, admire|词性:名词, 动词

high-hat:/ˈhaɪˌhæt/| adj.|傲慢的，自命不凡的|Snobbish, arrogant.|例句：He gave her a high-hat look. 他傲慢地看了她一眼。|近义词：snobbish, arrogant, supercilious|反义词: humble, modest|词性:形容词
