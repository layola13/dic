boustrophedon:/ˌbuːstrəˈfiːdɒn/ /ˌbuːstroʊˈfiːdɑːn/
noun
回旋书写法，牛耕式书写
A way of writing in which alternate lines are read in opposite directions, as in ancient Greek inscriptions.
The ancient inscription was written in boustrophedon. 这块古代铭文是用回旋书写法书写的。
近义词:alternate writing
反义词:normal writing
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek βουστροφηδόν (boustrophēdón), from βοῦς (boûs, “ox”) + στροφή (strophḗ, “turn”) + -δόν (-dón, adverbial suffix).  Literally "ox-turning-wise".  Imagine an ox plowing a field, reaching the end of a row, and turning around to plow the next row in the opposite direction.
