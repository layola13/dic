airbrushing:/ˈɛərbrʌʃɪŋ/
n.
喷绘；喷枪技术
the process of spraying paint onto a surface using an airbrush to create a smooth, even finish.
The magazine cover had clearly been airbrushed. 这本杂志的封面显然是喷绘过的。

airbrush:/ˈɛərbrʌʃ/
vt.
用喷枪喷绘
to spray paint onto a surface using an airbrush.
They airbrushed the model's blemishes for the photoshoot. 他们在拍摄前用喷枪修饰了模特的瑕疵。

词源: air + brush,  "air" referring to the compressed air used in the tool and "brush" referring to the spraying action.

近义词: spraying, retouching
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hand-painting" for contrast)
词性: noun (airbrushing), verb (airbrush)
