cancelers:/ˈkænsələrz/| n. |取消者，抵制者|People who cancel or withdraw support from a person, organization, or product, often due to perceived offensive behavior or opinions.  These individuals may boycott, unsubscribe, or otherwise publicly denounce their former affiliation. |Many companies are wary of cancelers and the potential impact they can have on their brand. 许多公司都对抵制者及其对品牌的潜在影响感到担忧。| synonyms: boycotters, critics, detractors | antonyms: supporters, patrons, fans | noun


cancelers:/ˈkænsələrz/| n. |取消预定的人|Those who revoke or annul bookings or reservations.|The hotel had a few cancelers, freeing up some rooms.  酒店有一些取消预订的人，空出了一些房间。| synonyms: those who revoke bookings, those who annul reservations | antonyms: those who book, those who reserve | noun
