commemorates:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪts/| v. |纪念，庆祝|serves to remind (someone or something) of a person or event.|例句：The monument commemorates the soldiers who died in the war. 这座纪念碑纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。|近义词：celebrate, honor, memorialize, mark|反义词:forget, ignore|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Latin commemoratus, past participle of commemorare "to call to mind, remember," from com- "together" + memorare "to bring to mind," from memor "mindful."
