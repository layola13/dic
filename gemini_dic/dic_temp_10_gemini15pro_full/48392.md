dedesignate:/ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/
verb
取消指定；撤销指定
To remove the official designation from someone or something.
The area was dedesignated as a national park.  该区域被取消了国家公园的称号。
The building was dedesignated a historical landmark. 这座建筑被取消了历史地标的称号。
近义词: revoke, rescind, cancel, withdraw
反义词: designate, appoint, assign
词性: verb

词源:
From de- + designate.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse," and "designate" means to officially assign a name or status.
