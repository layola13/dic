narcotize:/ˈnɑːrkətaɪz/ /ˈnɑːrkəˌtaɪz/| vt.|麻醉；使麻木；使昏迷|To drug someone so that they become unconscious or unable to feel pain; to make someone feel as if they are drugged, or to make an activity seem less unpleasant than it is.|The doctor narcotized the patient before the surgery. 医生在手术前麻醉了病人。

The repetitive music seemed to narcotize the audience.  重复的音乐似乎使观众麻木了。|近义词：drug, anesthetize, numb, stupefy|反义词：stimulate, energize, awaken|词性: verb
