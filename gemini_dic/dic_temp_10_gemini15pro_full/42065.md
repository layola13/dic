cotenant:/ˈkəʊˈtenənt/ (UK) /ˈkoʊˈtenənt/ (US)| n.|共同租户，共同承租人|A person who rents and occupies property jointly with another or others.|例句：The cotenants shared the rent and utilities equally. 共同租户平均分摊租金和水电费。|例句：The cotenants had a disagreement about the use of the common areas. 共同租户在公共区域的使用上产生了分歧。|近义词：joint tenant, co-lessee|反义词:landlord, lessor|词性:noun

cotenant:/ˈkəʊˈtenənt/ (UK) /ˈkoʊˈtenənt/ (US)| adj.|共同租赁的，共同占有的|Holding or possessing property jointly with another or others.|例句：They were cotenant owners of the vacation home. 他们是度假屋的共同业主。|例句：The cotenant farmers shared the profits from the harvest.  共同租赁的农民分享了收获的利润。|近义词：joint, shared|反义词:sole, individual|词性:adjective
