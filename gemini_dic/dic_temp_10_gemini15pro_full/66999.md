expiating:/ˈekspiˌeɪtɪŋ/
verb
赎罪; 补偿
Making amends or atonement for a sin or wrongdoing.
He was expiating his sins by doing good deeds. 他通过做好事来赎罪。
近义词:atoning, redeeming, compensating
反义词:sinning, offending
