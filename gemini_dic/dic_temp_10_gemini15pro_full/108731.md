leaper:/ˈliːpər/ (US), /ˈliːpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|跳跃者；跳跃能力强的人或动物|Someone or something that leaps or is capable of leaping.|The deer are graceful leapers, easily clearing the high fence. 鹿是优雅的跳跃者，可以轻松越过高高的栅栏。|Synonyms: jumper, vaulter, springer|Antonyms: crawler, slitherer|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *leper, from Old English *hlēapere, from Proto-Germanic *hlaupaz, meaning "runner" or "leaper."  Related to the verb "leap."
