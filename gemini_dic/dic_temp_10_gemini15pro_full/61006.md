eliminate:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt/
verb
消除; 清除; 淘汰
completely remove or get rid of (something)
例句：The company is eliminating jobs to cut costs.  公司正在裁员以削减成本。
近义词：remove, eradicate, abolish, exterminate, get rid of
反义词：create, introduce, add, include, establish


词源:
From Late Latin eliminatus, past participle of eliminare "to drive out of doors, expel," from ex- "out" + limen (genitive liminis) "threshold, lintel, doorway, entrance."
