emoluments:/ɪˈmɒljʊmənts/ | n. | 酬金，薪水，津贴 |  A salary or fee for work done, especially in a professional capacity; compensation received by virtue of holding an office or employment. |  The emoluments of the CEO included a generous stock option plan. 首席执行官的酬金包括一项优厚的股票期权计划。| synonyms: compensation, remuneration, salary, wages, stipend, earnings, perquisites, perks | antonyms: losses, deductions | noun
