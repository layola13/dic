enumerating:/ɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 列举，枚举 |  mentioning a number of things one by one. |  She was enumerating the benefits of the new system. 她正在列举新系统的好处。| list, count, detail, specify | omit, skip | verb

enumerating:/ɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 列举的 |  relating to or involving enumeration. |  The enumerating process took several hours. 列举的过程花了几个小时。| listing, counting, detailing | omitting, skipping | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin enumeratus, past participle of enumerare "to count out, reckon up," from ex- "out" + numerare "to count," from numerus "number."
