globigerina:/ˌɡloʊbɪdʒəˈraɪnə/| n. |球房虫|A single-celled planktonic organism (specifically a foraminiferan) with a calcareous shell, found in the ocean and contributing to the formation of calcareous ooze on the seabed. |例句：Globigerina are important indicators of past climates. 球房虫是过去气候的重要指标。

例句：The sediment contained numerous globigerina fossils.  沉积物中包含大量的球房虫化石。|近义词：foraminifer, foraminiferan|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *globus* (globe) + -iger (bearing) + -ina (suffix forming feminine nouns).  Essentially meaning "globe-bearing".
