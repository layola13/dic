carpologist:/kɑːrˈpɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 果实学家；果实研究者 | A scientist who studies fruits and seeds. |  Example 1: The carpologist examined the apple's seeds under a microscope. 果实学家在显微镜下检查了苹果的种子。

Example 2:  The museum hired a carpologist to catalog their collection of rare fruit specimens.  博物馆聘请了一位果实学家来为他们收藏的稀有水果标本进行分类。| Synonym: pomologist (specifically for fruits), botanist (more general) | Antonym: N/A | Noun
