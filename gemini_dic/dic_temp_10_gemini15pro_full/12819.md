assize:/əˈsaɪz/| n.|巡回审判；(旧时)法庭；会议；(面包等的)法定尺寸、重量|A session of a court held periodically in each county of England and Wales by judges of the High Court, primarily for the trial of criminal cases.  (Formerly) A fixed or official rate, standard, or measure, especially of the price or weight of bread.|The assizes were held in the town hall. 巡回审判在市政厅举行。|近义词：court, trial, session|反义词: N/A|名词

assize:/əˈsaɪz/| v.|规定(面包等的)尺寸、重量|To fix the size, weight, or price of (something, especially bread or ale).|The government assized the price of bread. 政府规定了面包的价格。|近义词：regulate, fix, standardize|反义词: N/A|动词
