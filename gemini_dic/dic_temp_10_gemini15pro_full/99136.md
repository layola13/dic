interceptive:/ˌɪntərˈsɛptɪv/
adjective
拦截的，截取的
designed to intercept something, especially a missile or aircraft.
The interceptive missiles successfully neutralized the threat. 拦截导弹成功地消除了威胁。
Synonyms:  intercepting, obstructive, preventative
Antonyms:  allowing, permissive, facilitating
词性: 形容词

interception:/ˌɪntərˈsɛpʃən/
noun
拦截，截取
the action or fact of intercepting or being intercepted.
The interception of the enemy's communication gave us a tactical advantage.  对敌人通讯的拦截给了我们战术上的优势。
Synonyms:  interception, blockage, obstruction
Antonyms:  allowance, permission, facilitation
词性: 名词

intercept:/ˌɪntərˈsɛpt/
verb
拦截，截取
obstruct (someone or something) so as to prevent them from continuing to a destination.
Police intercepted a shipment of drugs. 警方截获了一批毒品。
Synonyms:  catch, seize, stop
Antonyms:  miss, release, allow
词性: 动词
