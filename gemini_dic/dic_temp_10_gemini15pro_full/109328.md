lengthily: /ˈlɛŋθɪli/ | adv. |冗长地，啰嗦地|In a long and drawn-out manner; tediously. |He spoke lengthily about the history of the company. 他冗长地讲述了公司的历史。| The meeting dragged on lengthily. 会议拖沓冗长。 | 近义词: tediously, verbosely, prolixly | 反义词: concisely, briefly, succinctly | 词性: 副词

词源: From length + -ly.  "length" 源自古英语 "lengþu,"  意思是长度，延伸。"-ly" 是一个常见的副词后缀。
