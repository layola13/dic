affixes:/ˈæfɪksɪz/| n.|词缀|A morpheme or a meaningful part of a word that is attached to a word root to form a new word or to modify its meaning.  An affix can be a prefix (added to the beginning), a suffix (added to the end), or an infix (inserted within the word root).|The word "unbreakable" is formed by adding the prefixes "un-" and the suffix "-able" to the root word "break".  “不可破坏”这个词是由词根“break”加上前缀“un-”和后缀“-able”构成的。|synonyms: morphemes, additions, attachments|antonyms: roots, bases, stems|noun
