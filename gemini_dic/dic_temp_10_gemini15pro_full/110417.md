like-mindedly:/laɪkˈmaɪndɪdli/
| adv. | 同样地，志趣相投地 | In a way that shows shared thoughts, feelings, or interests. |  They worked like-mindedly on the project, sharing a common vision. 他们志趣相投地在这个项目上工作，拥有共同的愿景。 | synonym: harmoniously, concordantly, agreeably | antonym: discordantly, differently, incompatibly | 词性: 副词

like-minded:/ˌlaɪkˈmaɪndɪd/
| adj. | 想法相同的，志趣相投的 | Having similar tastes, opinions, or purposes. |  She enjoys spending time with like-minded individuals who share her passion for hiking. 她喜欢和和她一样热爱徒步旅行的人在一起。| synonym: similar, compatible, kindred | antonym: different, incompatible, dissimilar | 词性: 形容词

mind:/maɪnd/
| n. | 头脑，心智；想法，意见 | The element of a person that enables them to be aware of the world and their experiences, to think, and to feel; the faculty of consciousness and thought. | What's on your mind? 你在想什么？ | synonym: intellect, brain, psyche | antonym: -- | 词性: 名词
| v. | 介意，注意；照看 | Be bothered or upset by (something); object to. | Do you mind if I open the window? 你介意我打开窗户吗？| synonym: object, care, heed | antonym: disregard, ignore, neglect | 词性: 动词


词源:
"like-minded" comes from "like" (meaning similar) and "minded" (referring to one's mental disposition or opinion).  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "like-minded" into the adverb "like-mindedly."
