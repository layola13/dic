interagreement: /ˌɪntərəˈɡriːmənt/
n.
相互协议，互相一致
Agreement between or among multiple parties.
The interagreement between the three companies ensured a smooth collaboration.  三家公司之间的相互协议确保了合作的顺利进行。
The interagreement on the project's goals was crucial for its success.  就项目目标达成一致对项目的成功至关重要。
Synonyms: accord, consensus, harmony, unanimity
Antonyms: disagreement, discord, conflict, dispute
词性: 名词
