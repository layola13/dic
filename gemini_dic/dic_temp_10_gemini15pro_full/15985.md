balustrade:/ˌbæləˈstreɪd/ | n. |栏杆，扶手|A railing supported by balusters, especially an ornamental parapet on a balcony, bridge, or terrace. | The balustrade of the bridge was intricately carved. 桥的栏杆雕刻精美。 | synonym: railing, banister, parapet | antonym: none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *balustrade*, from Italian *balaustrata*, from *balaustro* "baluster," of uncertain origin, perhaps from Late Latin *balaustium* "flower of the wild pomegranate," from Greek *balaústion* "wild pomegranate flower," from its resemblance in shape.
