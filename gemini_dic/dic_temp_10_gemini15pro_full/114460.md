maki-zushi:/ˈmɑːki ˈzuːʃi/ | n. | 巻き寿司 (Makizushi), rolled sushi | A type of sushi made by wrapping vinegared rice and fillings in nori (dried seaweed). | Example: Maki-zushi is my favorite type of sushi.  巻き寿司是我最喜欢的寿司种类。

Example:  We ordered several different kinds of maki-zushi for lunch. 我们午餐点了几种不同的巻き寿司。| 近义词: norimaki, sushi roll | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps nigiri-zushi as a contrasting type) | 词性: noun
