mothlike:/ˈmɔθˌlaɪk/| adj.|像蛾的，蛾一般的|Resembling a moth, especially in being drab-colored and attracted to light.|The mothlike insects fluttered around the porch light.  像蛾一样的昆虫在门廊灯周围飞舞。|近义词：moth-winged, nocturnal, flitting|反义词：bright, diurnal, still|词性:形容词

Etymology: moth + like.  "Moth" comes from Old English "moththe," related to other Germanic words for moth. "Like" comes from Old English "gelic," meaning similar.
