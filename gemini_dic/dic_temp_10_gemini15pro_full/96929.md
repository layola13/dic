inexpugnable:/ˌɪnɪkˈspʌɡnəbl/
adjective
攻不可破的；无法征服的
Unable to be captured or broken into by force; unconquerable.
The castle, situated on a high cliff, proved inexpugnable.  这座城堡坐落在高高的悬崖上，被证明是攻不可破的。
Their courage and determination made them inexpugnable. 他们的勇气和决心使他们不可战胜。
近义词: invincible, impregnable, unassailable, unconquerable
反义词: vulnerable, weak, defenseless
词性: adjective
