词源:  From "modify" + "-able" + "-ity".  "Modify" comes from Old French "modifier," ultimately from Latin "modificare"  (to measure, limit, regulate). "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being." "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
