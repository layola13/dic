non-quality:/nɒnˈkwɒləti/ | n. | 非质量，不合格，次品 |  Something that lacks the desired standard or level of excellence.  It often refers to products or services that are defective or do not meet specifications. | The company recalled the products due to a non-quality issue.  这家公司因为质量问题召回了产品。 | The factory produced a batch of non-quality goods.  这家工厂生产了一批不合格产品。 | synonym: defect, flaw, imperfection, fault | antonym: quality, excellence, perfection | noun
