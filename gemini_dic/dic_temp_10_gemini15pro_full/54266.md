disgrade:/dɪsˈɡreɪd/| vt. n.|使丢脸；降级；耻辱|To bring shame or dishonor upon; to reduce in rank or status.  A loss of respect or honor; a state of being dishonored.|He was disgraded from his position for misconduct. 他因行为不当被降职。| 近义词:degrade, shame, dishonor, demote|反义词:honor, promote, elevate|词性:动词, 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *disgradare, from dis- "away, down" + gradus "step, rank."
