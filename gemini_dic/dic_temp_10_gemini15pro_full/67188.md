expressly:/ɪkˈsprɛsli/| adv. |明确地，特意地，专门地|Clearly and unmistakably; for a particular purpose.|例句：I expressly forbade you to go. 我明确禁止你去。|近义词：explicitly, definitely, specifically, pointedly|反义词:implicitly, vaguely|词性:副词
词源: From Latin expressus, past participle of exprimere (“to press out, portray, represent”), from ex (“out”) + premere (“to press”).
