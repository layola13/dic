bellicus:/ˈbɛlɪkəs/
adjective
好战的，战争的
Of or relating to war; warlike.
The bellicus nation was always prepared for conflict.  好战的国家总是为冲突做好准备。
Julius Caesar described the bellicose nature of the Gauls in his Commentaries.  尤利乌斯·凯撒在他的《高卢战记》中描述了高卢人好战的本性。
近义词:warlike, martial, pugnacious, belligerent
反义词:peaceful, pacifistic, dovish
词性:adjective
词源:Latin bellum "war"
