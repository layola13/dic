on-guard:/ˌɑːnˈɡɑːrd/ | adj. | 警惕的，提防的 | Watchful and prepared for danger or trouble. | The soldiers were on-guard all night, expecting an attack. 士兵们整夜都保持警惕，提防着袭击。|  The security guard was on-guard at the front desk. 保安在前台保持警惕。 | 近义词: watchful, vigilant, alert, cautious | 反义词: careless, unguarded, unwary | 词性: adjective

词源: From French "en garde," literally "on guard," a fencing term.
