didactic:/daɪˈdæktɪk/
adjective
教诲的；说教的
Intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive.
Example: The novel is overly didactic and lacks subtlety. 这部小说过于教诲，缺乏细致入微之处。
Example:  His didactic approach to teaching turned off many students. 他那套说教式的教学方法让很多学生感到厌烦。
Synonym: instructive, pedagogical, preachy, moralizing
Antonym: nondescript, uninformative, entertaining


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *didacticus*, from Greek didaktikos "apt at teaching," from didaskein "to teach."
