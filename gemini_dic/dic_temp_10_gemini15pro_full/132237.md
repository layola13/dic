nondeciduate:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪdʒuət/
adjective
非落叶的，常绿的
Not shedding leaves seasonally; evergreen.
The live oak is a nondeciduate tree, remaining green throughout the year.  活橡树是一种常绿乔木，一年四季都是绿色的。
The nondeciduate foliage provided year-round shelter for the animals.  常绿的叶子为动物们提供了全年的庇护所。
synonyms: evergreen, persistent
antonyms: deciduous, caducous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + deciduate (from Latin deciduus, "falling off," from decidere, "to fall off").
