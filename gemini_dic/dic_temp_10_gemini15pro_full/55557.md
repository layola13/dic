distruster:/dɪsˈtrʌstər/
noun
不信任者；怀疑者
A person who distrusts or is suspicious of others.
He's a distruster of all politicians. 他不信任所有的政客。
synonyms: skeptic, cynic, doubter
antonyms: believer, truster, optimist
