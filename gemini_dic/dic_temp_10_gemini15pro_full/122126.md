mistranscriptions:/ˌmɪstrænˈskrɪpʃənz/ | n. | 抄写错误，誊写错误 | An act of transcribing something, especially speech sounds, inaccurately.  Mistranscriptions can lead to miscommunication and errors in data analysis. |  There were several mistranscriptions in the court reporter's notes. 法庭记录员的笔记中有几处抄写错误。

The student's mistranscriptions of the ancient text led to a misunderstanding of its meaning. 学生对古代文本的抄写错误导致对其含义的误解。| Near synonyms: errors, mistakes, typos, misspellings | Near antonyms: accuracy, correctness | Noun
