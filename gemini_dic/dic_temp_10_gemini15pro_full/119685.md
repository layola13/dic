midheaven: /ˈmɪdˌhɛvn/ | n. | 中天；天顶 |The highest point in the sky that a celestial body reaches in its daily apparent path.|  My astrologer says my Midheaven is in Leo, which suggests a career in the arts. 我的占星家说我的中天在狮子座，这表明我会从事艺术事业。

midheaven: /ˈmɪdˌhɛvn/ | n. | 最高点；顶峰 | The highest or culminating point.| Reaching the midheaven of his career, the CEO decided to retire.  这位首席执行官在达到事业的顶峰后决定退休。

midheaven: /ˈmɪdˌhɛvn/ | n. | (占星术)第十宫 | The tenth house in astrology, associated with career, public image, and reputation.|  Her Midheaven in Capricorn indicates a strong drive for success and recognition. 她的中天位于摩羯座，表明她对成功和认可有着强烈的渴望。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mydheven, from Old English *middeheofon, from midde ("middle") + heofon ("heaven").  Equivalent to mid +‎ heaven.

SYNONYMS->
zenith, apex, culmination, acme, peak

ANTONYMS->
nadir, bottom, low point
