elasmobranch: /ɪˈlæzməˌbræŋk/ | n. |  软骨鱼纲 | A cartilaginous fish of the class Chondrichthyes, such as a shark, ray, or skate.  These fish have skeletons made of cartilage rather than bone. | Sharks and rays are elasmobranchs.  鲨鱼和鳐鱼都是软骨鱼。 | 近义词: chondrichthyan | 反义词:  (No direct antonym; could consider bony fish/osteichthyan) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *elasmos* (plate) + *branchia* (gill)
