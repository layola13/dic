dalmania:/dælˈmeɪniə/ | n. |达尔曼虫，达尔曼尼亚虫（三叶虫的一种）| An extinct genus of trilobites, marine arthropods that lived during the Ordovician and Silurian periods, particularly known for their distinctive head shields. | Example: The Dalmania trilobite is a popular fossil among collectors. 达尔曼虫化石在收藏家中很受欢迎。

Example:  Researchers studied the Dalmania fossils to understand the Ordovician ecosystem. 研究人员研究了达尔曼虫化石以了解奥陶纪生态系统。 | 近义词: trilobite (更广义) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after the Swedish paleontologist Johan Wilhelm Dalman (1787-1828).  Derived from "Dalman" + "-ia" (a common taxonomic suffix used to form genus names).
