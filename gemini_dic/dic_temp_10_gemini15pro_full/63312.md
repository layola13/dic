entices:/ɪnˈtaɪsɪz/| v. |诱惑，引诱|To attract someone to do something by offering something pleasant or advantageous.|The smell of freshly baked bread enticed me into the bakery.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味引诱我走进了面包店。|近义词：tempt, lure, attract, seduce|反义词：repel, deter, discourage|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enticier, from Vulgar Latin *intitiāre, frequentative of Latin *initiāre, "to begin, initiate," from initium "beginning."  The sense shifted from "initiate into evil" to "tempt, lure."
