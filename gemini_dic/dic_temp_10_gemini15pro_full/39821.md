conspicuously:/kənˈspɪkjuəsli/| adv.|显著地，明显地|In a manner that attracts notice; noticeably; obviously.|Her brightly colored dress made her conspicuously out of place in the subdued elegance of the restaurant. 她颜色鲜艳的裙子让她在餐厅低调优雅的氛围中显得格外引人注目。|Synonyms: noticeably, obviously, prominently, strikingly, visibly|Antonyms: inconspicuously, subtly, unobtrusively|Word Type: Adverb
