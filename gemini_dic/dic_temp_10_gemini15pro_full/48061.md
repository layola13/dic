declamatoriness:/dɪˌklæməˈtɔːrɪnəs/
n.
雄辩；慷慨激昂；演说腔
The quality of being declamatory; a pompous or rhetorical way of speaking or writing.
His speech was marked by a certain declamatoriness that put some people off. 他的演讲带有某种演说腔，让一些人感到反感。
近义词：bombast, grandiloquence, rhetoric
反义词：simplicity, understatement, conciseness
词性：noun
