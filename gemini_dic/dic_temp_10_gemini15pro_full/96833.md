inestimably:/ɪnˈɛstɪməbli/
adverb
无法估量地，极大地
in a way that is too great to calculate or measure
Her contributions to the project were inestimably valuable. 她对这个项目的贡献是极其宝贵的。
Synonyms: immeasurably, incalculably, infinitely, boundlessly
Antonyms: measurably, calculably, finitely
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From inestimable + -ly.  Inestimable from in- + estimable. Estimable from Late Middle English: from Old French estimable, from estimer ‘to estimate’, from Latin aestimare.
