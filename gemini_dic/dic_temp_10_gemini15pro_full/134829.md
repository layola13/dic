ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin, meaning "not"
miraculous (adjective): from Late Latin miraculosus, from Latin miraculum "wonder, marvel," from mirari "to wonder at"
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition
