looking-out:/ˈlʊkɪŋ aʊt/| adj. n.|注意的，警戒的；瞭望；前景，展望|A looking-out place offers a good view, especially of the sea. 一个瞭望台可以提供良好的视野，特别是海景。|The castle has a looking-out over the sea. 这座城堡可以眺望大海。|近义词：watchful, vigilant, outlook, prospect|反义词：inattentive, careless|词性:形容词, 名词

词源:  From the verb phrase "look out," meaning to be watchful or vigilant, and "look out (over/onto something)," meaning to have a view of something.  "Look" comes from Old English "locian," and "out" comes from Old English "ūt."
