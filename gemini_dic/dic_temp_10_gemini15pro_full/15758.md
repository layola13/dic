baldrib:/ˈbɔːldrɪb/| n. |光秃的肋骨；瘦骨嶙峋的人（或动物）|A rib that is bare of flesh; a thin, emaciated person or animal. |例句：The starving dog's bald ribs were showing through its thin coat.  这只饿狗瘦骨嶙峋的肋骨透过薄薄的皮毛显露出来。

例句：He was just skin and bald ribs after months at sea with limited rations.  在海上漂泊数月，食物匮乏，他瘦得只剩皮包骨头了。|近义词：emaciated, gaunt, skeletal, bony |反义词:plump, fleshy, well-fed|词性:名词

词源->
From *bald* (meaning bare, naked) + *rib*. The word dates back to the late 14th century.
