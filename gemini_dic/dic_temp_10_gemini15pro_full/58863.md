durableness:/ˈdjʊərəbəlnəs/ /ˈdʊrəbəlnəs/
|n.|耐久性，耐用性|The quality of being durable; ability to withstand wear, decay, or damage.|The durableness of the tires is impressive. 这些轮胎的耐用性令人印象深刻。|近义词：durability, toughness, strength, sturdiness, longevity|反义词：fragility, weakness,  flimsiness|词性:名词
Etymology: From durable + -ness.  Durable comes from Late Middle English, from Old French durable, from Late Latin durabilis, from Latin durare "to harden, last," from durus "hard."
