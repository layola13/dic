disproportionatenesses:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətnəsɪz/
|n.|不相称；不成比例；不均衡|The state or quality of being disproportionate; lack of proportion.|The disproportionatenesses of their reactions to the minor incident was surprising. 他们对这件小事的反应如此不相称，真是令人惊讶。|Synonym: imbalance, disparity, inequality|Antonym: balance, proportionality, equality|noun


disproportionateness:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətnəs/
|n.|不相称；不成比例；不均衡|The state or quality of being disproportionate; lack of proportion.|The disproportionateness of the punishment to the crime was evident.  对罪行的惩罚明显不相称。|Synonym: imbalance, disparity, inequality|Antonym: balance, proportionality, equality|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From disproportionate + -ness.  Disproportionate comes from dis- (not) + proportionate (proportional). Proportionate comes from Late Latin proportionatus, past participle of proportionare (to make proportionate), from Latin proportio (proportion).
