nonundulate:/ˌnɒnˈʌndjʊleɪt/ | adj. | 不波动的，不起伏的 | Not undulating; not moving in waves. | The surface of the still pond was nonundulate, reflecting the clear sky. 平静的池塘水面波澜不惊，倒映着晴朗的天空。| The table was nonundulate, providing a stable surface for writing.  桌子平稳不晃动，提供了一个稳定的书写表面。 | 近义词: flat, smooth, level, still | 反义词: undulating, wavy, rippling | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "undulate" (from Latin "undulatus," past participle of "undulare" meaning "to wave," from "unda" meaning "wave").
