bicarbureted: /ˌbaɪˈkɑːrbjəˌreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 双重碳化的; 饱和的 | Combined with two parts of carbon.  Specifically, used of a gas, such as illuminating gas, enriched with a volatile hydrocarbon, such as benzene. | The bicarbureted hydrogen gas provided a brighter flame. 双重碳化的氢气提供了更明亮的火焰。 | 近义词: saturated, enriched | 反义词: depleted | 词性: 形容词

词源: bi- (two) + carburet (to combine with carbon) + -ed (past participle suffix)
