aggrandizement:/əˈɡrændɪzmənt/
|n.|扩张；夸大；强化；提高|the act of increasing the power or importance of something or someone|His insatiable desire for aggrandizement led him to conquer neighboring territories. 他对扩张的无尽渴望导致他征服了邻近的领土。|synonyms: enhancement, elevation, augmentation, enlargement|antonyms: diminution, reduction, decrease|noun

aggrandize:/əˈɡrændaɪz/
|v.|增大，扩张；夸大，吹捧|to increase the power, status, or wealth of; to enhance or exaggerate the importance, power, or reputation of|He sought to aggrandize himself by claiming credit for other people's work. 他试图通过把别人的功劳归功于自己来抬高自己。|synonyms: exalt, glorify, magnify, amplify|antonyms: diminish, belittle, minimize|verb
