butterwort:/ˈbʌtərˌwɜːrt/ (US), /ˈbʌtəwɜːt/ (UK)| n. |捕虫堇|A carnivorous plant with sticky leaves that trap insects.|Butterworts are fascinating carnivorous plants. 捕虫堇是一种迷人的食肉植物。

Butterworts thrive in boggy environments. 捕虫堇在沼泽环境中茁壮成长。|近义词：sundew, pitcher plant, Venus flytrap|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
