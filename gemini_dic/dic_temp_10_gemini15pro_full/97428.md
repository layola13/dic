infundibulum:/ˌɪnfənˈdɪbjʊləm/| n. |漏斗；漏斗状结构|A funnel-shaped cavity or passage, particularly: 1. the hollow stalk connecting the hypothalamus and the posterior pituitary gland. 2. any of various funnel-shaped parts of the body.|

例句：The infundibulum of the uterine tube leads to the ovary. 输卵管漏斗部通向卵巢。

例句：The hypothalamus communicates with the posterior pituitary gland through the infundibulum. 下丘脑通过漏斗与垂体后叶沟通。

近义词：funnel, cone|反义词:|词性:noun
