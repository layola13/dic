emoter:/ɪˈmoʊtər/ | n. | 表情符号发送者；感情外露者 | A person who expresses emotions openly, especially online. | She's a prolific emoter on social media, always using emojis and expressive language. 她在社交媒体上是一个非常爱用表情符号的人，总是使用表情符号和表达丰富的语言。| 近义词: expresser, communicator | 反义词: stoic, reserved | 词性: noun

emote:/ɪˈmoʊt/ | v. | 表露感情；（尤指在线）使用表情符号 | To express emotions, especially online using emoticons or emojis. | He emoted his excitement through a series of celebratory emojis. 他用一系列庆祝的表情符号表达了他的兴奋。 | 近义词: express, react, feel | 反义词: suppress, conceal | 词性: verb
