morphing:/ˈmɔːrfɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɔːfɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |变形，变体，渐变|A smooth transformation of one image into another by computer animation techniques.|The video showed a morphing of a tiger into a lion. 视频展示了老虎变成狮子的变形过程。|近义词：transforming, transmuting, changing, metamorphosis|反义词：fixing, stagnating|词性:noun

Etymology: From morph (v.) + -ing.  "Morph" itself comes from Greek *morphē* meaning "form, shape."
