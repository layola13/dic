bumblings:/ˈbʌmblɪŋz/
n.
笨拙的行为，踉跄的行走，语无伦次的讲话
awkward actions or movements, often involving stumbling or confused speech
His bumblings on stage were embarrassing to watch. 他在舞台上的笨拙表演令人尴尬。
近义词：blunders, stumbles, fumbles
反义词：grace, dexterity, eloquence
词性: 名词

bumble:/ˈbʌmbəl/
v.
踉跄而行，笨手笨脚地做，语无伦次地说
to move or act clumsily and awkwardly; to speak in a confused or hesitant way
He bumbled through his speech, forgetting his lines. 他结结巴巴地做完了演讲，忘了台词。
近义词：stumble, blunder, fumble
反义词：glide, sail, articulate
词性: 动词


词源:  From Middle English *bomben, likely of imitative origin.  Related to boom.
