became:/bɪˈkeɪm/| v.|成为，变成|A linking verb indicating a change of state or condition.|She became a doctor after years of study. 她经过多年的学习成了一名医生. |synonyms: turned into, grew into, developed into|antonyms: remained, stayed|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *becamen*, from Old English *becōm* (past tense of *becuman* "to come to be, become, happen, arrive"), from Proto-Germanic *bikwemaną* (source also of Old Frisian *bikuma*, Old Saxon *bikoman*, Old High German *biqueman*, German *bekommen* "to get, receive"), from *bi-* "by" + *kwemaną* "to come".

PHRASE->
become of =  发生（通常指不好的事情） What became of him after he left the company? 他离开公司后怎么样了？
become clear = 变得清晰 It became clear that he was lying. 很明显他在撒谎。
become involved = 参与 She became involved in a political campaign. 她参与了一场政治运动。
become accustomed to = 习惯于  He became accustomed to the noise. 他习惯了噪音。
become aware of = 意识到  She became aware of the problem. 她意识到了这个问题。
