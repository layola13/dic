non-acceptance:/ˌnɒn əkˈsɛptəns/ | /ˌnɑːn əkˈsɛptəns/ |n.|不接受，不承认|The non-acceptance of the proposal was a setback for the team.  这个提案不被接受对团队来说是一个挫折。|The artist struggled with the non-acceptance of his unconventional style. 这位艺术家很难接受他的非传统风格不被认可。| rejection, refusal, denial | acceptance, approval | noun
Etymology->
From non- + acceptance.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not". Acceptance comes from the Old French "accepter"  meaning "to take willingly".
