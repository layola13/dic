disseizor:/dɪˈsiːzər/ | n. |非法剥夺者，强占者|A person who unlawfully dispossesses another of real property.|例句：The disseizor was ordered by the court to return the land to its rightful owner. 法院命令强占者将土地归还原主。

例句：The disseizor claimed the land was rightfully his, despite lacking any legal documentation.  强占者声称这块土地理应属于他，尽管没有任何法律文件。|近义词：usurper, intruder, encroacher |反义词: owner, possessor | Noun
