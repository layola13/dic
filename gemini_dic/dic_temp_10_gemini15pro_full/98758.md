intactness:/ɪnˈtæktnəs/| n.|完整性，完好无损|The archaeologist was amazed by the intactness of the ancient pottery. 考古学家对古代陶器的完整性感到惊讶。|例句：The intactness of the ecosystem is vital for the survival of many species.  生态系统的完整性对于许多物种的生存至关重要。

例句：The painting survived the fire with its intactness remarkably preserved. 这幅画在火灾中幸存下来，其完整性得到了显著的保存。


|近义词：completeness, wholeness, integrity|反义词:damage, brokenness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From intact + -ness.  Intact from Latin intactus "untouched," from in- "not" + tactus "touched," past participle of tangere "to touch."
