monarchists:/ˈmɒnəkɪsts/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnərkɪsts/ (US)| n. |君主主义者|People who support the principle of monarchy, a system of government in which a monarch (such as a king or queen) is the head of state.|例句：The monarchists rallied in support of the king. 君主主义者集会支持国王。

例句：Many monarchists believe in the divine right of kings. 许多君主主义者相信君权神授。|近义词：royalists, conservatives (in some contexts)|反义词:republicans, anti-monarchists|Noun
