nondetachment:/ˌnɒndɪˈtætʃmənt/
n.|非执着，不执着|The state of not being emotionally attached to something or someone.  A Buddhist concept emphasizing freedom from craving and clinging.|He practiced nondetachment to material possessions. 他对物质财富不执着。|近义词：neutrality, impartiality, objectivity, detachment |反义词：attachment, clinging, possessiveness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + detachment.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Detachment" comes from the Old French "destachier" meaning "to detach" or "to separate."
