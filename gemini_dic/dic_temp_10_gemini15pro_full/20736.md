blameworthy:/ˈbleɪmwɜːrði/
adjective
应受谴责的；该受责备的
Deserving of blame; culpable.
He was found blameworthy for the accident. 他被认为应对这起事故负有责任。

近义词: culpable, guilty, at fault, responsible, liable
反义词: blameless, innocent, faultless, irreproachable
词性: adjective
