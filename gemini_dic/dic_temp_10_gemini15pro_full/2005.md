actualization: /ˌæktʃuələˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌæktʃwələˈzeɪʃən/
n.
实现，实行; 现实化; 潜能发挥;
The process of making something real or actual. The achievement of one's full potential.
She finally achieved self-actualization through years of hard work and dedication. 她通过多年的努力和奉献最终实现了自我实现。
He focused on the actualization of his dreams. 他专注于实现他的梦想。

近义词: realization, fulfillment, achievement, attainment
反义词:  failure, frustration, suppression
词性: noun
