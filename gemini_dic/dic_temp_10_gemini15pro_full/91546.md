hyper-elegantly:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛlɪɡəntli/
adverb
极其优雅地，非常优美地
in a way that is extremely elegant and refined.
She was dressed hyper-elegantly for the gala. 她为了晚会盛装打扮，极其优雅。
The dining room was decorated hyper-elegantly. 餐厅装饰得非常精美。
Synonyms: exquisitely, supremely elegantly, exceptionally elegantly
Antonyms: inelegantly, clumsily, crudely
词性: 副词
