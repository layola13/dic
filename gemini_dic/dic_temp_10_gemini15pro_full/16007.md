banal:/bəˈnɑːl/, /bəˈnæl/ | adj. | 平庸的，陈腐的，老套的 | So lacking in originality as to be obvious and boring. | The plot of the movie was banal and predictable.  这部电影的情节平庸且可预测。|  The dialogue was so banal I almost fell asleep.  对话如此陈腐，我差点睡着了。 | 近义词: trite, clichéd, commonplace, hackneyed, stale, stereotyped, unoriginal, vapid, insipid | 反义词: original, fresh, innovative, creative, novel, imaginative, inspiring | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *banal*, "pertaining to compulsory feudal service," from Old French *ban* "proclamation, jurisdiction, territory subject to a lord's ban," from Frankish *ban* "command, order."  The meaning shifted from "common, shared by all tenants" to "commonplace, trivial" by the late 18th century.
