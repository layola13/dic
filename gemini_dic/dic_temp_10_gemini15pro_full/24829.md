brown-bagger:/ˈbraʊnˌbæɡər/
n.
自带午餐者；自带酒水者
A person who brings their own lunch, typically in a brown bag, to work or school, often to save money or eat healthier.
He's a brown-bagger, always bringing his lunch from home. 他是个自带午餐的人，总是从家里带午饭。
lunch-packer, BYOLer (Bring Your Own Luncher)
(No real antonym, perhaps "restaurant-goer" for lunch)
noun


brown-bag:/ˈbraʊnˈbæɡ/
v.
自带午餐；自带酒水
To bring one's own lunch or alcoholic beverages, typically in a brown bag, to an event or establishment.
They brown-bagged it for the picnic. 他们自带了野餐的午餐。
pack a lunch, BYOB (Bring Your Own Bottle/Booze)
eat out, dine out
verb
