muscularis: /ˌmʌskjʊˈlɛərɪs/
noun
肌层
The muscular layer of a hollow organ or tubular structure, such as the gut or a blood vessel.  This layer is responsible for contractions that move substances through the organ.
The muscularis of the stomach is composed of three layers of smooth muscle. 胃的肌层由三层平滑肌组成。

The muscularis externa of the small intestine propels chyme towards the large intestine. 小肠的外肌层将食糜推向大肠。


Synonym: muscular coat, muscular layer
