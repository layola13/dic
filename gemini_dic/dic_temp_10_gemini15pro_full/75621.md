fuggily:/ˈfʌɡɪli/
adjective
闷热地；潮湿地；不舒服地
in a stuffy, warm, and unpleasant way, because the air is not fresh
The room was fuggily warm. 房间闷热难耐。
近义词:stuffily, stiflingly, close
反义词:airily, freshly
词性:adjective
