infliction:/ɪnˈflɪkʃən/| n. |施加，给予（通常指痛苦或惩罚）;  痛苦，折磨 |The infliction of pain is considered inhumane. 施加痛苦被认为是不人道的。|例句：The constant noise was an infliction upon the neighborhood.  持续不断的噪音对邻居来说是一种折磨。

例句：The judge considered the infliction of a heavy fine as an appropriate punishment. 法官认为处以重罚是适当的惩罚。|近义词：imposition, enforcement|反义词:relief, alleviation|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin inflictio, from Latin inflictus, past participle of infligere "to strike against, dash upon; inflict," from in- "in, on, upon" + fligere "to strike."
