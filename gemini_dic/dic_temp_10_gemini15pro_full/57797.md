dredging:/ˈdrɛdʒɪŋ/
n.
疏浚；挖掘
the process of removing mud and sand from the bottom of a river, lake, etc.
例句：Dredging the river helped to improve navigation. 疏浚河流有助于改善航行。

例句：The dredging operation unearthed several ancient artifacts. 疏浚作业挖掘出几件古代文物。

近义词：excavation, deepening
反义词：filling, silting
词性：名词


dredge:/drɛdʒ/
v.
疏浚；挖掘；捞取
to remove mud and sand from the bottom of a river, lake, etc.
例句：They dredged the harbor to make it deeper.  他们疏浚了港口以使其更深。

例句：He dredged the bottom of the lake for lost treasure. 他在湖底打捞丢失的宝藏。

近义词：excavate, scoop
反义词：fill, bury
词性：动词


词源:
From Middle English *draggen, from Old English *dragan, from Proto-Germanic *dragōną (“to draw, pull, drag”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰregʰ- (“to draw, pull”). Cognate with Dutch dragen (“to carry, wear”), German tragen (“to carry, wear”), Icelandic draga (“to draw, pull, drag”).
