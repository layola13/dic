bufferhead:/ˈbʌfərˌhɛd/| n.|小潜鸭，凤头潜鸭|A small diving duck (Bucephala albeola) of North America, having a large head and a short bill.|例句：The male bufferhead is a striking little duck with a puffy head. 雄性小潜鸭是一种引人注目的小鸭子，有着蓬松的脑袋。

例句：We saw a flock of bufferheads diving for food in the lake. 我们看到一群小潜鸭在湖里潜水觅食。|近义词：bufflehead, butterball|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
