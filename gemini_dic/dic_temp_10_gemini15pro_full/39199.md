congenital:/kənˈdʒɛnɪtəl/| adj.|先天的，天生的|Existing from birth; present at birth, but not necessarily hereditary. |例句：The baby was born with a congenital heart defect.  这个婴儿出生时患有先天性心脏缺陷。

例句：Congenital blindness can be caused by a variety of factors. 先天性失明可能是由多种因素引起的。|近义词：innate, inborn, inherent|反义词：acquired, developed|词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from Latin *congenitalis, from congenitus ‘born together’, from con- ‘together with’ + gignere, genitus ‘beget’.
