arthropleura:/ˌɑːrθrəˈplʊərə/ | n. |节胸属, 一种已灭绝的巨型千足虫| An extinct genus of giant millipede arthropods that lived from the late Carboniferous to the early Permian periods, approximately 345 to 295 million years ago.  They are believed to be the largest known land invertebrates of all time. |例句：Arthropleura was a massive invertebrate.  节胸属是一种巨大的无脊椎动物。

例句：Fossils of Arthropleura have been found in Europe and North America.  在欧洲和北美都发现了节胸属的化石。| 近义词：millipede（泛指千足虫） | 反义词: None（无，因为它是特指一个属） | 词性:noun
