openhearted:/ˌoʊpənˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|慷慨的，宽宏大量的，坦率的|Having or showing a warm and generous heart; frank and candid.  Derived from "open" + "hearted."  "Open" meaning receptive and "hearted" referring to the emotions and feelings.  The word suggests a willingness to share feelings and connect with others authentically.|He wrote her an openhearted letter expressing his remorse. 他给她写了一封坦诚的信表达他的悔恨。|近义词：charitable, compassionate, sympathetic|反义词：unsympathetic, uncharitable, tight-fisted|词性:形容词
