coalfields: /ˈkəʊlfiːldz/
noun
煤田
Areas where coal is found and mined.
The coalfields of Pennsylvania were once a major source of employment. 宾夕法尼亚州的煤田曾经是主要的就业来源。
synonyms: coal mines, collieries
antonyms: oil fields, gas fields
n.
