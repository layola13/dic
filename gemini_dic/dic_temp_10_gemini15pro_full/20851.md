blasty:/ˈblɑːsti/| adj.|有爆炸性的；狂风大作的；受风吹的；|exposed to, affected by, or resembling blasts of wind or explosions. |The weather was blasty and cold. 天气刮着大风，很冷。|近义词：windy, stormy, gusty|反义词：calm, still|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From blast + -y.  "Blast" comes from Old English *blǣst, from Proto-Germanic *blǣstaną (“to blow, blast”).
