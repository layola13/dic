contrastable:/kənˈtræstəbl/ | adj. | 可对比的，可形成对比的 |  Able to be contrasted or compared, showing noticeable differences.  | The two paintings are highly contrastable, one bright and vibrant, the other dark and somber.  这两幅画形成了鲜明的对比，一幅明亮鲜艳，另一幅阴暗忧郁。 | synonyms: comparable, distinguishable, differentiable | antonyms: similar, indistinguishable, homogenous | adjective

Etymology:  From "contrast" + "-able".  "Contrast" comes from the Old French "contraster"  (to withstand, resist, oppose), and ultimately from the Latin "contrastare" (to stand against). The suffix "-able"  indicates capability or worthiness.
