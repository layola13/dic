bruts:/bruːts/
n.
Brut (plural bruts)
香槟酒；干型香槟酒；天然香槟酒
A very dry champagne or sparkling wine.
例句：
He ordered a bottle of bruts for the celebration. 他点了一瓶香槟来庆祝。

She preferred bruts to sweeter sparkling wines.  比起甜味起泡酒，她更喜欢干型香槟。

近义词：champagne, sparkling wine
反义词：sweet wine, dessert wine
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brut*, literally "raw, unrefined, coarse," from Late Latin *brutus* "heavy, dull, stupid," itself probably of Gaulish origin.  The sense of "dry (wine)" arose from its being less sweet than other wines.
