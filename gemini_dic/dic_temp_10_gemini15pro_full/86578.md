heliocentric:/ˌhiːliːoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ /ˌhiːlɪəʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|日心的，以太阳为中心的|Relating to the heliocentric theory, which places the sun at the center of the solar system.|例句：The heliocentric model of the solar system eventually replaced the geocentric model.  日心说最终取代了地心说。|近义词：solar-centric|反义词:geocentric|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *hēliocentricus*, from Greek hēlio- (from hḗlios ‘sun’) + Latin -centricus ‘-centric’.
