drunkenly: /ˈdrʌŋkənli/ | adv. | 醉醺醺地；酩酊大醉地 | In a drunken manner; while drunk. | He stumbled drunkenly out of the bar. 他醉醺醺地从酒吧里踉跄而出。 |  She laughed drunkenly and slurred her words. 她醉醺醺地笑着，说话含糊不清。 | 近义词: tipsily, intoxicatedly | 反义词: soberly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *dronkenliche, equivalent to drunken +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drankjaną (“to drink”).
