muskish:/ˈmʌskɪʃ/| adj.|麝香的；似麝香的；有麝香味的|Having a smell or taste resembling musk.|The room had a muskish odor. 房间里有一股麝香味。|近义词:musky|反义词:odorless, scentless|词性:形容词
词源: From musk + -ish. Ultimately from Sanskrit मुष्क muṣka.
