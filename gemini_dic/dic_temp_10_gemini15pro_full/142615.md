osteophone:/ˈɒstiəfəʊn/ | n. | 骨传导电话，骨传导助听器 | A device that transmits sound through the bones of the skull to the inner ear.  It is used primarily by individuals with conductive hearing loss. |  The doctor recommended an osteophone to help her hear better. 医生建议她使用骨传导助听器来更好地听到声音。 |  He found the osteophone more comfortable than traditional hearing aids. 他觉得骨传导助听器比传统的助听器更舒服。 | synonym: bone conduction hearing aid, bone vibrator | antonym: air conduction hearing aid | noun
