blow-drying:/ˌbloʊˈdraɪɪŋ/ | n. | 吹干 |The act of drying something, especially hair, with a blow-dryer. |She spent an hour blow-drying her hair. 她花了一个小时吹干头发。|近义词:drying |反义词:wetting |词性:noun (名词)
blow-dry:/ˌbloʊˈdraɪ/ | v. | 吹干 |To dry something, especially hair, using a blow-dryer. |I always blow-dry my hair after showering. 我淋浴后总是吹干头发。|近义词:dry |反义词:wet |词性:verb (动词)

词源: blow (v.) + dry (v.)  "blow" 指的是吹气，"dry" 指的是干燥。两个词组合在一起，表示用吹风机吹干。
