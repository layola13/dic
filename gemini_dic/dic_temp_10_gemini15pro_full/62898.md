enlightenments:/ɪnˈlaɪtnmənts/ | n. | 启迪，启蒙；教化，开导 |  The state of being enlightened or the act of enlightening.  A period of intellectual and philosophical awakening. |  The 18th century was a time of great enlightenments. 18世纪是一个伟大的启蒙时代。  His travels brought him many spiritual enlightenments. 他的旅行给他带来了许多精神上的启迪。 | 近义词：insights, revelations, awakenings, understandings | 反义词：ignorance, delusion, confusion | 词性: 名词

词源:  From enlighten + -ment.  Enlighten comes from en- (meaning "to cause to be") + light + -en (verb-forming suffix). Ultimately from the Old French enluminer, meaning "to illuminate."
