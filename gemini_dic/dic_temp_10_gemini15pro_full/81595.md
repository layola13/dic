greasy:/ˈɡriːsi/ /ˈɡriːzi/| adj.|油腻的；滑溜溜的；谄媚的|Covered with or resembling grease; oily or fatty. Also, unpleasantly or insincerely smooth or ingratiating.|The mechanic's hands were greasy after working on the car. 技工修完车后，手上油腻腻的。|The salesman had a greasy smile that made me uncomfortable.  那个售货员的笑容油腻腻的，让我很不舒服。|Synonyms: oily, fatty, slick, slippery, unctuous, ingratiating|Antonyms: clean, dry, nonfat, sincere|adjective

greasiness:/ˈɡriːsinəs/ /ˈɡriːzinis/| n.|油腻；油污|The quality or state of being greasy.|The greasiness of the fast food made me feel sick. 快餐的油腻让我感到恶心。|I couldn't stand the greasiness of his hair. 我受不了他头发的油腻。|Synonyms: oiliness, fattiness, slickness|Antonyms: cleanliness, dryness|noun
