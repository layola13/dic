beeping: /ˈbiːpɪŋ/ | adj. | 发出哔哔声的 |The beeping sound was coming from the smoke detector. | Making short, high-pitched electronic tones. | A beeping noise alerted us to the low battery. | 近义词：chirping, peeping, bleeping | 反义词: silent | adjective
