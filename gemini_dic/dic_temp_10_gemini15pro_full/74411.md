franchisability:/ˌfræntʃaɪzəˈbɪləti/
n.|可特许经营性|The quality or degree to which something can be franchised.  The ease with which a business model can be replicated and operated by franchisees.|The franchisability of the business model is key to its rapid expansion.  这个商业模式的可特许经营性是其快速扩张的关键。|franchise potential, replicability|non-franchisability
