demining:/diːˈmaɪnɪŋ/
n.|扫雷，排雷|The act of removing landmines from an area. |例句：Demining is a dangerous but necessary task in post-conflict zones.  在冲突后地区，扫雷是一项危险但必要的任务。|例句：The demining operation was successful, clearing the area for resettlement. 扫雷行动很成功，清理了该地区以便重新安置。|近义词：minesweeping, mine clearance|反义词:mining|noun
