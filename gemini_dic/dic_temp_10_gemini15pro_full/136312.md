nonrepudiative:/ˌnɒnriˌpjuːdiˈeɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnriˌpjuːdiˈeɪtɪv/
adjective
不可否认的
Unable to deny participation in a transaction.  This term is often used in a digital security context, referring to the ability to prove that someone took a specific action, such as sending a message or signing a document.
Digital signatures provide nonrepudiation, ensuring the sender cannot deny their involvement. 数字签名提供了不可否认性，确保发送者无法否认他们的参与。
Nonrepudiative evidence is crucial in online transactions to prevent fraud.  不可否认的证据在在线交易中对于防止欺诈至关重要。

近义词: irrefutable, undeniable, incontestable
反义词: deniable, repudiable
词性: adjective


词源:
From non- + repudiate (from Latin repudiatus, past participle of repudiare "to reject, cast off") + -ive.
