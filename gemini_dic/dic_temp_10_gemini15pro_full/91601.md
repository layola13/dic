hyperaggressivenesses:/ˌhaɪpərəˈɡrɛsɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|过度侵略性；极度攻击性|The state of being excessively aggressive.  It refers to a level of aggression that goes beyond normal assertiveness or competitiveness, often involving hostility, destructive behavior, and a lack of control.|His hyperaggressivenesses led to numerous physical altercations. 他的极度攻击性导致了多次肢体冲突。|Synonyms: belligerence, pugnacity, truculence|Antonyms: pacifism, gentleness, docility|noun
