genuineness:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪnnəs/| n.| 真实性；真诚；真挚|The genuineness of his remorse was evident. 他的悔恨是真诚的。|近义词：authenticity, sincerity, honesty|反义词：falseness, artificiality, insincerity|词性:名词
词源: From genuine + -ness.  From Latin *genuinus "innate, native, natural," from genus "race, stock, kind," from PIE root *gene- "give birth, beget," with derivatives referring to procreation and familial and tribal groups.
