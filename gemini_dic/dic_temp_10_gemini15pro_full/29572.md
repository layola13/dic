catchweed:/ˈkætʃˌwiːd/ | n. | 粘草，猪殃菜 | A common name for plants of the genus *Galium*, which adhere to clothing. These plants are also known as cleavers, goosegrass, or bedstraw.  | Example: The catchweed clung to my socks as I walked through the field.  走在田野里，粘草粘在了我的袜子上。| Synonym: cleavers, goosegrass, bedstraw | Antonym: N/A | noun
