impetuses: /ˈɪmpɪtəsiz/ | n. | 推动力；刺激 | The main impetus for change came from consumers. 变革的主要推动力来自消费者。|
impetus: /ˈɪmpɪtəs/ | n. | 推动力；刺激 |  The discovery gave fresh impetus to the research.  这一发现为研究注入了新的动力。| 近义词: stimulus, motivation, drive, impulse, incentive | 反义词: deterrent, hindrance, obstacle | 词性: noun

词源:  From Latin *impetus* "attack, assault, onset, impulse," literally "a setting in motion," from *in-* "in, on, upon" + *petere* "to go to, seek, strive after, aim at, attack, demand."
