humblebrag:/ˈhʌmbəlbræɡ/| n. v.| 谦虚式炫耀；假装谦虚地吹嘘|A statement that appears to be modest but is actually intended to draw attention to something the speaker is proud of.  It's a combination of "humble" and "brag."|例句：She humblebragged about her "terrible" problem of having too many job offers. 她“谦虚地”炫耀着她工作机会太多这个“糟糕的”问题。

例句：He made a humblebrag about his expensive new car by complaining about the high cost of insurance. 他通过抱怨高昂的保险费，“谦虚地”炫耀了他那辆昂贵的新车。|近义词：boast, brag, show off|反义词：self-deprecate, downplay|词性：名词；动词
