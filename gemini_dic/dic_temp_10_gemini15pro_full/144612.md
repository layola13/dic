outwhirling:/ˌaʊtˈhwɜːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 外旋，向外旋转 | The act of whirling or spinning outward.  A rapid outward movement. | The outwhirling of the helicopter blades created a strong downdraft. 直升机叶片的外旋产生了强烈的下沉气流。 | 近义词: rotating outward, spinning outward | 反义词: inwhirling, inward rotation | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from "out-" (meaning outward) + "whirl" (meaning to turn rapidly). "Whirl" comes from Old Norse "hvirfla" meaning to whirl or spin.
