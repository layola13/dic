gullies:/ˈɡʌliz/| n.|沟壑，溪谷，雨水冲刷成的沟|A deep ditch or channel cut in the earth by running water, especially after a prolonged downpour. |Heavy rains carved deep gullies in the hillside. 大雨在山坡上冲刷出深深的沟壑。|近义词：ravines, ditches, channels, gorges|反义词:ridges, mounds|名词

gullies:/ˈɡʌliz/| n.|集水槽，排水沟|A channel or gutter at the side of a road or other paved area for carrying away surface water. |The gullies were overflowing with rainwater. 排水沟里充满了雨水。|近义词：gutters, drains, channels|反义词: - |名词
