misbecome:/ˌmɪsbɪˈkʌm/
verb
不适合；不相称；使显得难看
to look bad on (someone); to be unsuitable for (someone)
That dress misbecomes her. 那件衣服不适合她。
Synonyms: unsuit, unbecoming, ill-becoming, ill-suited
Antonyms: suit, become, befit
词性: 动词

词源:
From mis- + become.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating something wrong, bad, or amiss.  Therefore, "misbecome" literally means "to not become," or to be unsuitable for.
