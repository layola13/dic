holdership:/ˈhoʊldərʃɪp/| n.|持有权，所有权|The state of being a holder; the act, fact, or right of holding or possessing something, especially property or an office.  The period during which something is held.|He retained holdership of the property for many years.  他多年来一直持有该房产的所有权。|Synonyms: ownership, possession, tenure, title|Antonyms: relinquishment, surrender|noun
