orienting:/ˈɔːriˌɛntɪŋ/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛntɪŋ/
verb|使适应；使熟悉；使确定方向|The program is designed to orient students to college life.  这个项目旨在帮助学生适应大学生活。|例句：The compass helps in orienting yourself in unfamiliar territory. 指南针能帮助你在不熟悉的地方确定方向。|近义词：familiarize, accustom, adapt, adjust|反义词：disorient, confuse|v.

orienting:/ˈɔːriˌɛntɪŋ/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛntɪŋ/
adjective|定向的；适应的|Orienting responses are crucial for survival. 定向反应对生存至关重要。|例句：The company is holding an orienting session for new employees. 公司正在为新员工举办一个入职培训会。|近义词：directive, guiding|反义词：disorienting, confusing|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *oriēntāre, present active infinitive of *oriēntō, from Latin oriēns (present participle of orīrī (“to rise”)), used as a noun (“the rising sun, the east”).
