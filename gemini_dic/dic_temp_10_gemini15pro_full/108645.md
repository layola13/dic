leafage:/ˈliːfɪdʒ/
n.
叶子；簇叶；树叶（总称）
The mass of leaves of a plant or tree; foliage.
The tree has thick leafage. 这棵树叶子茂密。

近义词:foliage, leaves
反义词:bareness, defoliation
词性:名词
