congregativenesses:/kənˌɡrɛɡəˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
群居性，聚集性
The tendency of individuals or animals to gather together in groups.
The scientist studied the congregativenesses of different ant species.  科学家研究了不同蚂蚁种类的群居性。
The congregativenesses of the starlings was remarkable, forming massive swirling flocks.  椋鸟的聚集性非常显著，形成了巨大的盘旋鸟群。
近义词：gregariousness, sociability
反义词：solitariness, unsociability
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From congregative + -ness.  Congregative from Latin congregativus, from congregare "to flock together," from com- "together" + gregare "to flock," from grex "flock."
