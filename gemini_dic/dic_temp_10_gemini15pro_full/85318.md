harped-on:/ˈhɑːrpt ɒn/ | adj. | 反复提及的，唠叨的 |  Constantly mentioned or discussed to an excessive degree, often to the point of annoyance.  Derived from the verb "harp on," meaning to dwell tediously on a subject. | He's always harped-on about his childhood. 他总是唠叨他的童年。| She's constantly harped-on about the unfairness of it all. 她不断抱怨这一切的不公平。 |  overemphasized, dwelled upon, belabored | ignored, overlooked | adjective
