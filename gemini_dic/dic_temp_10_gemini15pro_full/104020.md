juice:/dʒuːs/| n.|果汁，精力，电流|The juice from fresh oranges is delicious.  新鲜橙汁很好喝。|Example: I like apple juice. 我喜欢苹果汁。|Synonyms: beverage, drink, nectar|Antonyms: solid, pulp|noun

juice:/dʒuːs/| vt.|榨取…的汁液，给…通电|He juiced the oranges for breakfast. 他榨了橙汁做早餐。|Example:  She juiced the lemons to make lemonade. 她榨了柠檬做柠檬水。|Synonyms: extract, squeeze|Antonyms: solidify, dry|verb
