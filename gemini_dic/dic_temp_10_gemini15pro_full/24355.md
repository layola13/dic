brine:/braɪn/| n. vt.|盐水，卤水；海水|Water saturated or strongly impregnated with salt.|The olives had been preserved in brine. 这些橄榄一直保存在盐水里。|近义词：saltwater, seawater|反义词:freshwater|noun
brine:/braɪn/| vt.|用盐水浸泡|To soak or preserve in salty water.|Brining the turkey before roasting makes it more flavorful. 在烤之前用盐水浸泡火鸡，味道会更好。|近义词：pickle, soak|反义词:desalinate|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brýne (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *brunjo, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (to boil, bubble, effervesce). Cognate with Dutch brijn, German Brine.  The verb sense is a back-formation from the noun.
