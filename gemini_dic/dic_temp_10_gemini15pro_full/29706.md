catholicism: /kəˈθɒlɪsɪzəm/ (UK) /kəˈθɑːləˌsɪzəm/ (US)| n. |天主教, 天主教教义|The faith, practice, and system of the Roman Catholic Church. |例句：He converted to Catholicism in his twenties. 他在二十多岁时皈依了天主教。|例句：Catholicism is the dominant religion in many countries. 天主教是许多国家的主要宗教。|近义词：Roman Catholicism|反义词: Protestantism, atheism |词性:noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin catholicismus, from catholicus ‘universal’ + -ismus (see -ism).
