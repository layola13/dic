centicipitous:/ˌsɛntɪˈsɪpɪtəs/| adj.|陡峭的，险峻的；仓促的，轻率的|Characterized by a steep slope or rapid descent; hasty, rash, or impulsive.|例句：The centicipitous drop in stock prices alarmed investors. 股票价格的暴跌令投资者感到恐慌。|例句：His centicipitous decision to quit his job surprised everyone. 他突然决定辞职让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：precipitous, steep, abrupt, hasty, rash, impetuous|反义词：gradual, slow, deliberate, cautious|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *centiceps*, from *centum* "hundred" + *caput* "head".  Originally referring to a mythical creature with a hundred heads, later used to describe something very steep as if falling headlong.
