misproud:/ˌmɪsˈpraʊd/ | adj. | 傲慢的，虚荣的，不值得骄傲的 | Having or showing improper pride; arrogant.  Characterized by a misplaced sense of pride, often based on something unworthy or trivial. | He was misproud of his ill-gotten gains. 他对他不正当的所得感到沾沾自喜。 | 近义词: arrogant, haughty, conceited, vain | 反义词: humble, modest | 词性: adjective
词源: mis- (Old English, meaning "wrongly, badly") + proud (Old French prud, from Latin prodesse "to be useful")
