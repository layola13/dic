bacule:/ˈbækjʊl/| n. | 小棒; 杆状体; 杆菌 |A small rod, staff, or rod-shaped bacterium.|  Bacules were observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到了杆菌。| 近义词: rod, stick, bacterium | 反义词: coccus (球菌) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French bacule, diminutive of Latin baculus "staff, walking stick," from baculum.
