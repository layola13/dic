overcoloring: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌlərɪŋ/
noun
过度着色
The act of applying too much color to something, resulting in a loss of detail or an unnatural appearance.
The artist's overcoloring made the portrait look garish.  这位艺术家的过度着色使肖像看起来很俗气。
over-saturation, excessive coloring
