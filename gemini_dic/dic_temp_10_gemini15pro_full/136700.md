nonsegregable:/ˌnɒnˌsɛɡrɪˈɡeɪb(ə)l/
adjective
不可隔离的，不可分割的
Not able to be segregated or separated.
The company's financial statements showed that its nonsegregable assets were substantial. 该公司的财务报表显示，其不可分割资产非常可观。
Government regulations stipulate that certain hazardous materials are nonsegregable and must be handled accordingly. 政府法规规定某些危险材料是不可分割的，必须相应地处理。
近义词: inseparable, indivisible, unsegregated
反义词: segregable, separable, divisible
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + segregable (capable of being segregated).  "Segregable" comes from the Latin "segregare" (to separate), from "se-" (apart) + "greg-" (flock, herd).
