by-nature:/baɪ ˈneɪtʃər/
| adj. | 天生的，生性，本质上 | Inherently; as a result of inborn or inherent qualities. | He is by nature a shy person. 他生性害羞。 | 近义词:innately, naturally, inherently | 反义词:unnaturally, artificially | 词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *bi nature*, from Old French *par nature*, from Latin *per nātūram* ("by birth, naturally"). Equivalent to *by* + *nature*.
