overspent:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛnt/ | /ˌəʊvəˈspɛnt/ | adj. | 超支的，过度消费的 | Having spent more money than was intended or allowed. |  I'm completely overspent this month after that vacation. 这个月假期过后，我完全超支了。| We were overspent on our marketing budget. 我们的营销预算超支了。| 近义词: broke, bankrupt, in the red | 反义词: under budget, flush | 词性: 形容词

overspend:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛnd/ | /ˌəʊvəˈspɛnt/ | v. | 超支 | To spend more than one can afford or more than is budgeted. | I tend to overspend when I'm stressed. 我在压力大的时候往往会超支。 |  They overspent on their credit cards and now have a lot of debt. 他们在信用卡上超支了，现在负债累累。 | 近义词: splurge, squander | 反义词: save, budget | 词性: 动词
