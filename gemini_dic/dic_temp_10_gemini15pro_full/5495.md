alopecist:/ˌæləˈpiːsɪst/ | n. | 脱发症专家；治疗脱发症的医生 | A doctor who specializes in the treatment of hair loss. |  She consulted an alopecist after noticing significant hair thinning. 她注意到头发明显变薄后咨询了一位脱发症专家。

| Example: He visited an alopecist to discuss treatment options for his male pattern baldness. 他去看了一位脱发症专家，讨论治疗男性型秃发的方案。 | Synonym: hair loss specialist, trichologist | Antonym: N/A | noun
