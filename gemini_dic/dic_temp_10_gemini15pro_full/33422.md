churchwardenize: /ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdənaɪz/ | v. | 干涉教区事务；以教会委员的姿态行事 | To manage or interfere in the affairs of a church, especially in a fussy or officious way, like a churchwarden. |  The vicar was accused of churchwardenizing when he insisted on choosing the hymns himself.  那位牧师被指责干涉教区事务，因为他坚持要亲自挑选赞美诗。 |  The new committee member has a tendency to churchwardenize, constantly offering unsolicited advice on how the church should be run.  新来的委员有干涉教区事务的倾向，经常主动提供教会该如何运作的建议。 | Synonyms: meddle, interfere, intrude | Antonyms: abstain, refrain, ignore | Verb
