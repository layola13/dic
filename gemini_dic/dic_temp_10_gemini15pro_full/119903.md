militaryism: /ˈmɪlɪtərɪzəm/
n.
军国主义
The belief or desire of a government or people that a country should maintain a strong military capability and be prepared to use it aggressively to defend or promote national interests.
例句：
The rise of militaryism in the early 20th century contributed to the outbreak of World War I.
20世纪初军国主义的兴起导致了第一次世界大战的爆发。

Militaryism is a dangerous ideology that can lead to conflict and war.
军国主义是一种危险的意识形态，可能导致冲突和战争。


近义词：
bellicosity, warmongering, jingoism
反义词：
pacifism, peacefulness

词性:
noun
