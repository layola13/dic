milden:/ˈmaɪldən/ | v. | 使缓和；使温和；使变淡 | To make something less severe, harsh, or strong. | The government has mildened its stance on immigration. 政府已经缓和了在移民问题上的立场。 | The warm weather mildened the harsh winter.  温暖的天气使得严冬变得温和了一些。 | 近义词: alleviate, soften, moderate, temper | 反义词: aggravate, worsen, intensify, exacerbate | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *milden, from Old English mildian (“to make mild”), from mild (“mild”) + -ian (suffix forming verbs).  The Old English mild is from Proto-Germanic *mildijaz, from Proto-Indo-European *meldʰ- (“soft, mild”).
