epicurish:/ˌɛpɪˈkjʊərɪʃ/ | adj. | 享乐主义的，贪图口腹之乐的 | Given to or adapted to luxury; devoted to pleasure; pertaining to Epicurus or his philosophy. | He lived an epicurish lifestyle, indulging in fine wines and gourmet food. 他过着享乐主义的生活方式，沉迷于美酒和美食。| 近义词: hedonistic, luxurious, sybaritic, voluptuous | 反义词: ascetic, austere, spartan | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *Epicūrēus, from Ancient Greek Ἐπίκουρος (Epíkouros, “Epicurus”), the name of the Greek philosopher Epicurus + -ish.
