daftly:/ˈdæftli/
adverb
愚蠢地，傻气地
In a foolish or silly manner.
He daftly left his keys in the car. 他傻乎乎地把钥匙忘在车里了。
She daftly believed everything he said. 她愚蠢地相信了他所说的一切。
近义词: foolishly, stupidly, idiotically, absurdly, senselessly
反义词: wisely, sensibly, intelligently, prudently
词性: 副词

daft:/dæft/
adjective
愚蠢的，傻的
Silly, foolish.
That's a daft idea! 那真是个愚蠢的主意！
He made a daft mistake. 他犯了一个愚蠢的错误。
近义词: silly, foolish, stupid, idiotic, absurd
反义词: wise, sensible, intelligent, prudent
词性: 形容词

词源: From Scots *daft*, from earlier *daffed*, past participle of *daff* (“to befool, make foolish”), from Old Norse *dafa* (“to become numb”).
