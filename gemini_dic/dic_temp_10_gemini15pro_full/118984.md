methane:/ˈmeθeɪn/ | n. | 甲烷 | A colorless, odorless, flammable gas, CH₄, the main constituent of natural gas. It is the simplest member of the alkane series of hydrocarbons. |  Methane is a potent greenhouse gas. 甲烷是一种强效的温室气体。|  Natural gas is mostly methane. 天然气主要成分是甲烷。| synonyms: marsh gas, firedamp | antonyms:  N/A | noun
