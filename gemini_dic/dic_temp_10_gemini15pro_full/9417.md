n.
反激进化主义；去激进化主义
Doctrines or measures intended to counter or prevent radicalization, especially the adoption of extremist political or religious views.
The government implemented new antiradicalisms programs aimed at vulnerable youth. 政府实施了针对弱势青年的新的反激进化项目。
Several European countries are sharing antiradicalisms strategies to combat terrorism.  几个欧洲国家正在分享反激进化战略以打击恐怖主义。
