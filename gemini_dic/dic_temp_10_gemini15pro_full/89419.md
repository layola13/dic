homotypic:/ˌhɒməˈtɪpɪk/ /ˌhoʊməˈtɪpɪk/| adj. |同型的，同质的| Of the same type or structure.  Relating to or being a homotype.|例句：Homotypic cell adhesion is the binding of cells of the same type. 同型细胞粘附是指相同类型细胞的结合。|例句：The researchers observed homotypic interactions between the neurons. 研究人员观察到神经元之间的同型相互作用。|近义词：isotypic, homologous|反义词：heterotypic|词性:adjective
