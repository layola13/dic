gauzelike: /ˈɡɔːzlaɪk/ | adj. | 薄纱似的，像纱布的 | Resembling gauze in being thin, light, and translucent. | The dress was made of a gauzelike material that flowed in the breeze. 这件连衣裙是用一种薄纱似的材料制成的，在微风中飘动。 | The spider spun a gauzelike web.  蜘蛛织了一个薄纱般的网。 |  近义词: sheer, transparent, filmy, diaphanous | 反义词: opaque, thick, dense | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From gauze +‎ -like. Gauze itself comes from Old French gaze, from Gaza, a city in Palestine where it was originally made.
