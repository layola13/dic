infant:/ˈɪnfənt/ (US) /ˈɪnfənt/ (UK)| n. |婴儿，幼儿|A very young child, especially one that has not yet learned to walk or talk.|The infant slept soundly in her crib. 婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。|近义词:baby, newborn, toddler|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:名词

infant:/ˈɪnfənt/ (US) /ˈɪnfənt/ (UK)| adj. |婴儿的，幼稚的|Of or relating to infants; in an early stage of development.|The infant mortality rate is a key indicator of public health. 婴儿死亡率是公共卫生的一个关键指标。|近义词:babyish, immature|反义词:mature, developed|词性:形容词
