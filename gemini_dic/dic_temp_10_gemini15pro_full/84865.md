hangers:/ˈhæŋərz/
n.
衣架；挂钩
A device, typically a shaped piece of wood, plastic, or metal, with a hook at the top, from which clothes may be hung in order to keep them in shape and free of creases.
The hangers in my closet are all full. 我的衣橱里的衣架都满了。
近义词:racks, hooks
反义词: N/A
词性:名词

hangers:/ˈhæŋərz/
n.
(飞机的)吊架，悬挂装置
A structure from which something hangs or is hung, especially an aircraft from the undercarriage of an aircraft carrier.
The mechanics inspected the aircraft hangers carefully. 机修工仔细检查了飞机的吊架。
近义词:supports, mounts
反义词:N/A
词性:名词
