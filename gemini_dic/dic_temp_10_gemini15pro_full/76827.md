gandered:/ˈɡændərd/ | v. | 瞥了一眼，匆匆一看 |  Looked or glanced at something quickly, especially in a casual or curious way.  | He gandered at the newspaper headlines while waiting for his coffee. 他一边等咖啡，一边瞥了一眼报纸的标题。| 近义词: glanced, peeked, glimpsed | 反义词: stared, scrutinized | 词性: 动词

词源: From the noun "gander," meaning a male goose, which is known for its quick, inquisitive looks.
