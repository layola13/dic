parthenogenetic:/ˌpɑːrθənəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
单性生殖的，孤雌生殖的
Relating to or characterized by parthenogenesis.
Parthenogenetic reproduction allows female aphids to rapidly increase their population.  蚜虫的单性生殖使得雌性蚜虫能够快速增加种群数量。
Some lizard species reproduce parthenogenetically. 一些蜥蜴物种进行单性生殖。
近义词: asexual, agamic
反义词: sexual
词性: adjective


parthenogenesis:/ˌpɑːrθənəʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
单性生殖，孤雌生殖
Reproduction from an ovum without fertilization, especially as a normal process in some invertebrates and lower plants.
Parthenogenesis is a common reproductive strategy in some insect species. 单性生殖是一些昆虫物种中常见的繁殖策略。
The Komodo dragon is capable of parthenogenesis. 科莫多巨蜥能够进行单性生殖。
近义词: asexual reproduction, agamogenesis
反义词: sexual reproduction
词性: noun
