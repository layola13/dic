deallocate:/diˈæləkeɪt/
verb
释放，解除分配
To release (something, especially computer storage space) from allocation.
The program deallocates memory when it's finished.  程序在完成后会释放内存。
The system will deallocate the resources after the task is completed.  任务完成后，系统将释放资源。
synonyms: release, free, liberate
antonyms: allocate, assign, reserve
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (expressing reversal) + allocate (from Latin allocāre, "to place, assign").
