objectified:/əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪd/
adj.
被物化的；被客体化的
treated as an object, especially a sexual object; deprived of human qualities or individuality.
She felt objectified by the way the men stared at her. 她觉得男人们盯着她的方式让她感到被物化了。
近义词:dehumanized, depersonalized
反义词:humanized, personalized
词性:形容词

objectify:/əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ/
vt.
物化；使客观化
to treat someone as an object rather than a person.  To express something in an objective way.
The advertisement objectifies women by portraying them as sexual objects. 这则广告将女性描绘成性对象，物化了女性。
近义词:dehumanize, degrade
反义词:humanize, personalize
词性:动词

词源:
From object + -ify.  "Object" comes from the Latin "objectum," meaning "something thrown before" or "presented."  "-ify" is a suffix that creates verbs meaning "to make or become."
