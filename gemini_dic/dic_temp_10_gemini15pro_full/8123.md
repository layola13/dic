anti-Austria:/ˌæntiˈɔːstriə/
adj.
反奥地利的
Opposed to Austria, its policies, or its people.
The anti-Austria sentiment was strong in Italy after the war.  战后，意大利的反奥情绪高涨。
Synonyms: anti-Austrian
Antonyms: pro-Austria, pro-Austrian
adjective

anti-Austria:/ˌæntiˈɔːstriə/
n.
反奥地利主义者；反奥地利运动
A person or movement opposed to Austria, its policies, or its people.
The anti-Austria movement gained traction in the early 20th century. 20世纪初，反奥地利运动获得了发展。
Synonyms: anti-Austrian movement
Antonyms: pro-Austria movement
noun
