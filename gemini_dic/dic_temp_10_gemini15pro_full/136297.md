nonrepressible:/ˌnɒnrɪˈprɛsəbəl/ |adj.|不可抑制的，无法压制的|Not able to be repressed or restrained; irrepressible.|Her nonrepressible enthusiasm was infectious. 她那抑制不住的热情很有感染力。|Synonyms: irrepressible, uncontrollable, unquenchable|Antonyms: repressible, controllable, suppressible|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + repressible, from repress + -ible. Repress comes from Latin repressus, past participle of reprimere "to press back, check, curb," from re- "back" + premere "to press."
