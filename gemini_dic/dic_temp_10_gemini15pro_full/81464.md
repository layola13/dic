gratuitousness:/ˌɡrætʃuˈɪtəsnəs/
n.
无偿；无故；免费
the state of being gratuitous; being without apparent reason, cause, or justification.
His speech was filled with gratuitous violence, shocking the audience. 他的演讲充满了无端的暴力，震惊了观众。
gratuity, freeness, uncalled-for-ness
necessity, justification, requirement
noun
