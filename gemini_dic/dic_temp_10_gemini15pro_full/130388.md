non-verbalized:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 非言语化的；未用言语表达的 | Not expressed in words; communicated through gestures, facial expressions, or other non-verbal means. |  He gave a non-verbalized agreement by nodding his head. 他点头表示了默许。| Synonyms: unspoken, unsaid, implicit, tacit | Antonyms: verbalized, spoken, expressed, explicit | adjective

non-verbalized:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːrbəlaɪzd/ | v. |  使…非言语化 | To make something non-verbal; to express something without using words. | The actor non-verbalized the character's emotions through his facial expressions. 演员通过面部表情将角色的情感非言语化地表达出来。 | Synonyms: unexpressed, unspoken | Antonyms: verbalized, spoken | verb
