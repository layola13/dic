noneclectic:/ˌnɒnɪˈklɛktɪk/
adjective
非折衷的，非兼容并蓄的
Not eclectic; not drawing on or made up of a variety of sources.
Her noneclectic taste in music was limited to classical composers. 她对音乐的品味非兼容并蓄，仅限于古典作曲家。
His noneclectic art collection focused solely on 18th-century French paintings.  他的艺术收藏非折衷主义，只专注于18世纪的法国绘画。
近义词: narrow, limited, restricted, specific
反义词: eclectic, diverse, varied, broad
