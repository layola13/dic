disincentive's:/dɪsɪnˈsɛntɪvz/
noun
抑制因素，遏制因素
Something that discourages a particular action or behaviour.
High taxes are a disincentive to investment. 高税收抑制了投资。
The threat of jail is a disincentive to crime. 监禁的威胁是对犯罪的威慑。
Synonym:deterrent, obstacle, impediment
Antonym:incentive, encouragement, motivation
词性: 名词 (复数形式)
