nonfelicitousnesses:/ˌnɒnfɪˈlɪsɪtəsnəsɪz/
n.
不幸；不恰当；不合适
The state or quality of being nonfelicitous; inappropriateness or unsuitability.
His nonfelicitousnesses in his speech caused much embarrassment.  他演讲中的不恰当之处引起了很多尴尬。
The series of nonfelicitousnesses led to the project's ultimate failure. 一系列的不恰当导致了该项目的最终失败。
近义词: inappropriateness, unsuitability, infelicity, awkwardness
反义词: appropriateness, suitability, felicity, aptness
词性: noun (plural)
