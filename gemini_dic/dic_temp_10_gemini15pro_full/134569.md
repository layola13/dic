nonlocally:/nɒnˈləʊkəli/ /nɑːnˈloʊkəli/| adv.|非局部地|In a way that is not confined to a particular place or region; over a wide area.|The effects of climate change are felt nonlocally.|近义词：globally, widely, extensively|反义词: locally, regionally|词性:副词
Etymology:  "non-" (not) + "local" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Local" comes from Old French "local," ultimately from Latin "localis" (of a place).
