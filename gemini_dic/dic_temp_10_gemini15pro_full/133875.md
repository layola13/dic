nongreasier:/nɒnˈɡriːsiər/
adjective
更不油腻的; 不那么油腻的
Less greasy than something else.
The new formula is nongreasier and absorbs quickly.  新配方更不油腻，吸收迅速。
The lotion is nongreasier than the cream I used before.  这款乳液比我之前用的面霜更不油腻。
近义词:less greasy, oil-free
反义词:greasier, oily
词性:adjective
