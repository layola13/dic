lingerings:/ˈlɪŋɡərɪŋz/
noun
徘徊；逗留；残留；萦绕
The act of lingering; remaining or staying longer than usual or expected.
The lingering scent of perfume filled the room. 香水残留的香味弥漫了整个房间。
近义词: remnants, traces, remains
反义词: departures, disappearances
词性:n.

Etymology: From the verb "linger," which comes from Middle English *lingren*, from Old Norse *lengja* "to lengthen," related to *langr* "long."
