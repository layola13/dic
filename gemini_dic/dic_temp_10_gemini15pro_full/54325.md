disheartened:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
Having lost confidence, hope, and energy.
He was disheartened by the news that his project had been rejected. 他得知他的项目被拒绝后感到很沮丧。
discouraged, demoralized, dejected
encouraged, heartened
adjective
