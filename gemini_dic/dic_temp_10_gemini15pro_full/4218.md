airings:/ˈeərɪŋz/
noun
播出; 播放; 公开发表
the act of broadcasting something on radio or television, or the act of making opinions or complaints public
The show's first airing was last night.  这个节目的首播是昨晚。
broadcast, showing, presentation
suppression, concealment
n.
