discouragements: /dɪˈskʌrɪdʒmənts/ | n. | 沮丧，气馁；令人沮丧的事物 | Something that discourages or disheartens.  The state of being discouraged. |  The team faced many discouragements, including injuries and funding cuts. 该团队面临许多挫折，包括伤病和资金削减。 | synonyms: setbacks, obstacles, hindrances, impediments, frustrations | antonyms: encouragements, incentives, motivations | noun

discouragement: /dɪˈskʌrɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 沮丧，气馁 | The act of discouraging or the state of being discouraged.  A loss of confidence or enthusiasm. | His constant criticism led to her discouragement. 他不断的批评使她灰心丧气。 | synonyms: despair, hopelessness, despondency, dejection | antonyms: encouragement, hope, optimism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *descoragement, from descoragier "to discourage," from des- "away" + corage "courage, heart."
