outstay:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪ/
verb
停留过久，逗留过长
to stay somewhere longer than you are welcome or longer than you should
例句：He outstayed his welcome by a couple of hours. 他多待了几个小时，让人觉得他待得太久了。

例句：She had outstayed her visa and was forced to leave the country. 她的签证过期了，被迫离开了这个国家。
近义词：overstay
反义词: leave, depart
词性: verb
