outspeeds:/ˌaʊtˈspiːdz/
verb
超过…的速度, 比…更快
to move faster than someone or something else
例句：The cheetah easily outspeeds the gazelle. 猎豹很容易就能跑得比瞪羚快。

例句：The new car outspeeds the old one by a significant margin. 新车的速度明显比旧车快。

近义词：outrun, surpass, exceed
反义词：lag behind, trail

词性：v.


词源：out- + speed.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "beyond" or "exceeding," and "speed" refers to velocity or swiftness.
