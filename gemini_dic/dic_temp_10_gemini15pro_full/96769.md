inequality's:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɒlətiz/ | n. | 不平等；不均等；差异 |  The possessive form of "inequality," referring to something belonging to or associated with a state of being unequal. | The inequality's impact on society is significant.  社会不平等的影响是巨大的。 | synonyms: disparity's, imbalance's, unevenness's, asymmetry's | antonyms: equality's, parity's, fairness's, uniformity's | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *inequalité (modern French inégalité), from Latin inaequalitas ("unevenness, inequality"), from inaequalis ("unequal"), from in- ("not") + aequalis ("equal").
