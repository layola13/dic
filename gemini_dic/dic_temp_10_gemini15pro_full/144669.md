outwrites:/ˌaʊtˈraɪts/| v.|胜过，超过（在写作方面）；产量高于|To surpass or exceed in writing; to produce a greater literary output than. |Shakespeare outwrites most other playwrights.  莎士比亚的写作超过了大多数其他剧作家。|Synonyms: outproduce, surpass, excel, exceed |Antonyms:  be inferior to, fall short of|verb
outwrites:/ˌaʊtˈraɪts/| n.|胜过，超越（在写作方面）|The act of surpassing or exceeding in writing. |His outwrites of his contemporaries astonished critics. 他在写作上超越同时代人的成就让评论家们感到震惊。|Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, excelling |Antonyms: inferiority, deficiency|noun
