multanimous:/ˌmʌlˈtænɪməs/ | adj. | 大气的，宽宏大量的，慷慨的 | Having a great soul or spirit; magnanimous. Showing or suggesting a lofty and courageous spirit.  Characterized by magnanimity or generosity of mind; great-hearted; noble. | Example: His multanimous gesture of donating his entire fortune to charity was applauded by everyone.  他将全部财产捐给慈善机构的慷慨举动赢得了所有人的赞扬。

Example:  The multanimous king pardoned the rebel leader. 宽宏大量的国王赦免了叛军领袖。 | 近义词: magnanimous, generous, liberal, bountiful, charitable | 反义词: petty, mean, stingy, selfish, parsimonious | 词性: 形容词
