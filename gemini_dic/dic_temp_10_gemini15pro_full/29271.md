cassowaries:/ˈkæsəˌwɛriz/| n.|食火鸡|Large, flightless birds native to the tropical forests of New Guinea and northeastern Australia, known for their powerful legs and sharp claws.|Cassowaries are dangerous birds and should not be approached. 食火鸡是危险的鸟类，不应该靠近它们。|Cassowaries have a distinctive casque on their heads. 食火鸡的头上有一个独特的盔状突起。|近义词：ratites (鸵鸟目鸟类)|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可考虑会飞的鸟类,例如 eagle)|词性:noun
