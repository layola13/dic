interprismatic:/ˌɪntərprɪzˈmætɪk/
adjective
棱柱间的；位于棱柱之间的
Situated between prisms.
The interprismatic spaces in the rock formation were filled with calcite crystals. 岩层中棱柱间的空间充满了方解石晶体。
