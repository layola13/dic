hydroponically:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 水培地；用水栽法 | In a manner relating to hydroponics, the process of growing plants without soil, using mineral nutrient solutions in a water solvent. |  The tomatoes were grown hydroponically in a greenhouse. 这些西红柿是在温室里用水培法种植的。| Synonyms: soillessly, water-cultured | Antonyms: traditionally (in soil) | adverb
