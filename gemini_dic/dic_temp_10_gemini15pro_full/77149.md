gasher:/ˈɡæʃər/
n.
割伤者；砍伤者；大伤口；深切口
One who gashes or inflicts deep cuts.  A large wound or deep cut.
The gasher left a deep wound on the soldier's arm. 割伤者在士兵的胳膊上留下了一道很深的伤口。
The surgeon quickly stitched the gasher.  外科医生迅速缝合了大伤口。
近义词:cutter, slasher, wounder
反义词:healer, mender
词性:名词
