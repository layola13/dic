doubter:/ˈdaʊtər/| n.|怀疑者；持怀疑态度的人|A person who doubts or questions something.|例句：He was a doubter of the new policy. 他对新政策持怀疑态度。

例句：The doubters were silenced by the evidence.  证据让怀疑者们哑口无言。

近义词：skeptic, cynic, questioner|反义词:believer, supporter, adherent|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *douter*, from Anglo-Norman *douter*, from Old French *douter* (“to doubt”), from Latin *dubitāre* (“to hesitate, waver, doubt”).
