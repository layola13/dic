immediately: /ɪˈmiːdiətli/ | adv. | 立即，马上 |  Without delay; at once.  Derived from the adjective "immediate" which comes from the Latin "immediatus" meaning "without a medium." | I need you to come here immediately. 我需要你马上过来。

She responded immediately to his email. 她立即回复了他的电子邮件。


near synonym: instantly, directly, promptly, forthwith
antonym: later, eventually, subsequently
word class: adverb
