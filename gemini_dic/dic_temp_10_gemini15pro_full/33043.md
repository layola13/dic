chouan:/ʃuˈɑːn/| n.|舒昂党人；保皇党游击队员|A member of a group of counterrevolutionary royalist guerrillas in western France during the French Revolution, especially in the region formerly called Brittany.|The chouans harassed the republican troops throughout the region. 舒昂党人在整个地区骚扰共和军。|Synonym: royalist, insurgent, guerrilla|Antonym: republican, revolutionary|noun

chouan:/ʃuˈɑːn/| adj.|舒昂党人的；保皇党游击队的|Relating to or characteristic of the Chouans.|The chouan uprising posed a serious threat to the new government. 舒昂党人的起义对新政府构成了严重威胁。|Synonym: royalist, counterrevolutionary|Antonym: republican, revolutionary|adjective
