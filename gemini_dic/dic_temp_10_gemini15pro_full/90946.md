hurls:/hɜːrlz/ /hɝlz/| v.|投掷，猛扔，用力投出|The pitcher hurled the baseball towards home plate. 投手用力将棒球投向本垒。|He hurled insults at his opponent. 他对他的对手大肆辱骂。|近义词：throw, fling, cast, pitch, toss|反义词:catch, receive|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *hurlin, *hirlen, from Anglo-Norman hurler, from Old North French hurler (“to howl, yell, hurl, fling”), of uncertain origin, possibly from Frankish *hurlian (“to knock, push, strike”).
