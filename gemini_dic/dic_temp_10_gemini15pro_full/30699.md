certificator:/ˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪtər/ |n.|认证员，鉴定员|A person who certifies or guarantees something, especially the quality or standard of something. |The certificator confirmed the authenticity of the document. 认证员确认了文件的真实性。|Synonym: certifier, authenticator, validator|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider  "challenger," "disputer," or "invalidator")|noun

certificator:/ˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪtər/ |n.|证书颁发者|A person or organization that issues certificates.|The certificator issued a certificate of authenticity for the artwork. 证书颁发者为这件艺术品颁发了真品证书。|Synonym: issuer, authority|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
