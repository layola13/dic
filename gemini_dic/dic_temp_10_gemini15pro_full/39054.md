conformal:/kənˈfɔːrm(ə)l/| adj.|保形的，共形的|Preserving angles between curves; having the same shape as something else but perhaps a different size. |例句：A conformal map projects a sphere onto a plane.  一个保角映射将球面投影到平面上。

例句：Conformal geometry is the study of angle-preserving transformations. 共形几何学是研究保角变换的学科。|近义词：isometric, similar|反义词：non-conformal, distorted|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conformalis, from Latin conformis (“similar in form”), from com- (“together”) + forma (“form, shape”).
