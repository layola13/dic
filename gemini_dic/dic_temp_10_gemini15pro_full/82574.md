guardage:/ˈɡɑːrdɪdʒ/| n.|监护；守护；监护权；监护期|The state of being under guard or guardianship; the period of time during which someone is under guard or guardianship.  The concept of guardianship, especially of a minor. |The child was placed in the guardage of his aunt.  这个孩子被置于他姑妈的监护之下。|Synonyms: guardianship, custody, wardship, protection, care, charge|Antonyms: neglect, abandonment|noun
