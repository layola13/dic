detox:/ˈdiːtɒks/ (UK) /ˈdiːtɑːks/ (US)| n. v.| 解毒；戒毒期；戒瘾期|A process or period of time in which one abstains from or rids the body of toxic or unhealthy substances; a period of withdrawal.|She went on a detox diet to cleanse her body. 她进行了戒毒饮食来净化身体。|近义词：cleanse, purify, abstinence|反义词:intoxication, contamination|词性:名词, 动词

detoxify:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪ/ (UK) /diːˈtɑːksɪfaɪ/ (US)| v. |解毒；使排除毒素|To remove toxic substances or qualities.|The liver detoxifies the blood. 肝脏能解毒血液。|近义词：cleanse, purify|反义词:contaminate, poison|词性:动词

词源:  "detox" is a shortened form of "detoxification," which comes from "de-" (meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "toxic" (meaning "poisonous").  The word "toxic" ultimately derives from the Greek word "toxikon," meaning "poison for arrows."
