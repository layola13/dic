confounded:/kənˈfaʊndɪd/ | adj. | 困惑的，糊涂的；恼火的，讨厌的 | confused, bewildered, perplexed; annoyed, irritated |  The confounded instructions were impossible to follow.  这该死的说明书根本看不懂。 | synonyms: bewildered, perplexed, baffled, puzzled, confused, frustrated, irritated | antonyms: clear, lucid, understandable, calm, pleased | adjective

confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ | vt. | 使困惑，使糊涂；证明…是错的；挫败，使失败 | to confuse or perplex; to prove wrong; to defeat or frustrate | His sudden change of heart confounded us all. 他突然改变主意，把我们都搞糊涂了。 | synonyms: bewilder, perplex, baffle, puzzle, confuse, frustrate, thwart | antonyms: clarify, explain, enlighten, aid, assist | verb
