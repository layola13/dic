outsallying:/ˌaʊtˈsæliɪŋ/
n.|出击，突围|A sudden attack made from a defensive position.  The act of rushing or bursting forth.|The troops made a daring outsallying against the enemy siege.  部队对敌人的围攻进行了大胆的突击。|近义词:sortie, attack, assault, rush, charge|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|词性:名词(gerund or present participle of outsally)

outsally:/ˌaʊtˈsæli/
v.|出击，突围|To rush out suddenly, especially from a besieged place to attack the besiegers. To burst forth suddenly.|The defenders outsallyed from the castle and surprised the enemy.  守军从城堡中冲出，奇袭了敌人。|近义词:sortie, attack, assault, rush, charge|反义词: retreat, withdraw|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *outsalien, from Old French *exsaillir (Modern French exsaillir), from Latin exsilire (“to leap forth”), from ex- (“out”) + salire (“to leap”).
