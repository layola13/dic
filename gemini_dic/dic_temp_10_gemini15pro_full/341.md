abhorrent-to:/əbˈhɔːrənt tuː/| adj.|令人憎恶的；可恶的；|Inspiring disgust and loathing; repugnant.  Something abhorrent *to* someone is something they strongly dislike or find repulsive.|Racism is abhorrent to any decent person. 种族主义为任何正派的人所憎恶。

The idea of betraying his friend was abhorrent to him. 背叛朋友的想法让他感到憎恶。|近义词：repugnant, detestable, loathsome, odious, abominable|反义词：appealing, attractive, pleasing, agreeable|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abhorrens, present participle of abhorrere (“to shrink back from, shudder at, detest”), from ab- (“away from”) + horrere (“to bristle, shudder”).

USAGE->
Subject + be + abhorrent to + object
Racism is abhorrent to me.  种族主义让我憎恶。

Something + be + abhorrent to + someone
The thought of eating insects is abhorrent to many Westerners.  吃昆虫的想法让很多西方人感到厌恶。

It + be + abhorrent to + someone + that clause
It is abhorrent to me that such cruelty exists in the world.  世界上存在这样的残酷让我感到憎恶。


PHRASE->
abhorrent to the senses:  令人感官厌恶的
abhorrent to reason:  违背常理的
abhorrent to nature:  违反自然的
behavior abhorrent to society: 为社会所不容的行为
practices abhorrent to human rights:  违反人权的做法
