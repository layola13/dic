decussation:/ˌdiːkʌˈseɪʃn/
n.
交叉，十字形交叉，交叉点
An intersection or crossing of two or more things, especially nerves or bands of fibers, resembling an X.
The optic nerve fibers undergo decussation in the brain.  视神经纤维在大脑中进行交叉。
The decussation of the pyramids is a prominent feature of the medulla oblongata. 延髓锥体的交叉是一个显著的特征。
