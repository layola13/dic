nebuly:/ˈnɛbjʊli/| adj.|云状的，星云状的|Representing clouds, or nebulous forms, as in heraldry.  In heraldry, it refers to a depiction of clouds or cloud-like forms.  It can also be used more generally to describe something that resembles a nebula.|The shield was nebuly argent and azure. 盾牌是银色和天蓝色的云状图案。|近义词：cloudy, hazy, misty, nebulous|反义词:clear, distinct|词性:adjective
词源: From Old French *nebule, from Latin nebula (“mist, fog, cloud, vapor”), from Proto-Italic *nebula, from Proto-Indo-European *nébʰōl. Cognate with Sanskrit nabhas (“cloud, mist, air, sky”)
