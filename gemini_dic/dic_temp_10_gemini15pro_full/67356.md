extensibleness:/ɪkˌstɛnsɪbəlnəs/| n.|可扩展性，延展性|The extensibleness of the software allows for easy customization.  这个软件的可扩展性使得它易于定制。|例句：The extensibleness of the system allows it to adapt to future needs.  该系统的可扩展性使其能够适应未来的需求。

The extensibleness of the fabric makes it suitable for various applications. 这种织物的延展性使其适用于各种用途。|近义词：expandability, flexibility, scalability|反义词：inflexibility, rigidity|词性:名词

词源: From extensible + -ness.  Extensible from Late Latin extendere, meaning "to stretch out."
