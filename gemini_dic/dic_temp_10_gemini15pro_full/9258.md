antipatriarchy:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriˌɑːrki/
n.
反父权制
opposition to patriarchy; the system or belief that men should hold the power in society and women should be excluded.
The antipatriarchy movement seeks to dismantle systems of male dominance.  反父权运动旨在瓦解男性主导的体系。
Her book is a powerful critique of antipatriarchy. 她的书是对反父权的有力批判。
近义词:feminism, gender equality
反义词:patriarchy, sexism
词性:noun
