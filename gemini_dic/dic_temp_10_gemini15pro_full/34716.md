cliché:/ˈkliːʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkliːʃeɪ/ (UK)| n.| 陈词滥调；老生常谈|a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought.|The plot of the movie was full of clichés.|synonyms: platitude, banality, truism, commonplace|antonyms: originality, novelty, freshness|noun


clichéd:/ˈkliːʃeɪd/ (US), /ˈkliːʃeɪd/ (UK)| adj.| 陈腐的；老套的|based on clichés.|The dialogue was clichéd and predictable.|synonyms: hackneyed, stale, trite, overused|antonyms: original, fresh, innovative|adjective
