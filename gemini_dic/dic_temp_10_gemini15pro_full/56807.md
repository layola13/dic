doric: /ˈdɔːrɪk/ (UK), /ˈdɔːrɪk/ (US) | adj. n. | 多立克式的，多立克方言；多立克柱式 | Relating to or denoting a classical order of architecture characterized by plain, sturdy columns with simple capitals. Also, a dialect of Ancient Greek spoken in Doris and other regions, or a low-lying plain in Scotland.  |  The Doric columns of the Parthenon are iconic.  雅典卫城帕特农神庙的多立克式圆柱是标志性的。| The Doric dialect of Ancient Greek is considered to be archaic. 古希腊的多立克方言被认为是古老的。| synonyms: spartan, simple, austere | antonyms: ornate, elaborate, Corinthian | adjective, noun
