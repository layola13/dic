add-ons:/ˈæd.ɒnz/ (US) /ˈæd.ɒnz/ (UK)| n.|附加组件；附加装置；额外费用|Something extra that is added to something else, often for an additional cost.  They can refer to software, hardware, or services.|The car comes with a number of attractive add-ons, including a sunroof and heated seats. 这款车配有许多吸引人的附加装置，包括天窗和加热座椅。

The price includes accommodation, but meals are an add-on. 价格包含住宿费，但餐费需额外支付。|近义词：accessories, extras, supplements, attachments|反义词:base, core|词性:noun
