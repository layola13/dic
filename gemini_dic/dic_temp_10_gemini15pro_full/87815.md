hickest:/ˈhɪkɪst/| adj.|最土的，最乡巴佬的|Superlative form of "hick," meaning most unsophisticated and rural.|He had the hickest accent I'd ever heard. 他有着我听过的最土的口音。|Synonyms: most rustic, most countrified, most provincial|Antonyms: most sophisticated, most cosmopolitan, most urbane|adjective

hick:/ˈhɪk/| n. adj.|乡巴佬，土包子；乡下的，土气的|A person who lives in the country, regarded as unsophisticated or unintelligent; characteristic of country people.|He's a hick from the sticks. 他是个来自乡下的土包子。|Synonyms: yokel, rustic, country bumpkin|Antonyms: sophisticate, cosmopolitan|noun, adjective
