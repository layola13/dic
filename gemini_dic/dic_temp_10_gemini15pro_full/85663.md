hauteur: /oʊˈtɜːr/ (US), /əʊˈtɜː/ (UK)| n. |高度，身高；傲慢，自大|A French word adopted into English, meaning literally "height," often used to describe social standing or arrogance. |例句：He spoke with an air of hauteur. 他带着傲慢的口气说话。|近义词：arrogance, haughtiness, loftiness, pride, superciliousness|反义词:humility, modesty|noun
