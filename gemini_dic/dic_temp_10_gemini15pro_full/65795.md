evictor:/ɪˈvɪktər/| n.|驱逐者，逐出者|A person who evicts someone, especially a landlord or their agent.|The evictor gave the family only three days to leave the apartment. 驱逐者只给了这家人三天时间搬离公寓。|Synonyms: expeller, ejector|Antonyms: protector, defender|noun

evict:/ɪˈvɪkt/| vt.|驱逐，逐出|To force someone to leave a place, especially a building, by legal process.|The landlord evicted the tenants for not paying rent. 房东因租户未付房租而将其驱逐。|Synonyms: expel, oust, eject|Antonyms: admit, welcome|verb
