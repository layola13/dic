herring-like:/ˈherɪŋˌlaɪk/
adjective
像鲱鱼的；鲱鱼似的
Resembling a herring in appearance or characteristics.
The fish had a silvery, herring-like body. 这条鱼有着银色的，像鲱鱼一样的身体。
The small, silvery fish were herring-like in appearance. 这些小的，银色的鱼外表看起来像鲱鱼。
近义词: herring-shaped, clupeid-like
反义词: (No specific antonym, could use descriptions like "unlike a herring", "dissimilar to a herring")
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'herring' + '-like'. 'Herring' comes from Old English 'hæring', of Germanic origin.  '-like' is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "similar to," "characteristic of," or "resembling."
