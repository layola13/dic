duratives:/ˈdjʊərətɪvz/ | n. |持续体，持续性事件|A durative is a grammatical aspect that describes a situation viewed as continuing over a period of time.|The lecture was a durative, lasting over two hours. 这场讲座持续了两个多小时，是一个持续性事件。|近义词：continuants, extended events|反义词：punctuals, instantaneous events|词性: noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The dog sleeps on the bed.  狗睡在床上。
She eats breakfast every morning. 她每天早上吃早餐。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The dog is sleeping on the bed. 狗正在床上睡觉。
She is eating breakfast right now. 她现在正在吃早餐。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The dog has slept on the bed all day. 狗整天都睡在床上。
She has eaten breakfast already. 她已经吃过早餐了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The dog has been sleeping on the bed for hours. 狗已经在床上睡了好几个小时了。
She has been eating breakfast for the past hour. 她吃了一个小时的早餐了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The dog slept on the bed last night. 狗昨晚睡在床上。
She ate breakfast yesterday morning. 她昨天早上吃了早餐。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The dog was sleeping on the bed when I arrived. 我到的时候，狗正在床上睡觉。
She was eating breakfast when the phone rang. 电话响的时候，她正在吃早餐。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The dog had slept on the bed before I woke up. 我醒来之前，狗已经在床上睡过了。
She had eaten breakfast before she went to work. 她上班前已经吃过早餐了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The dog had been sleeping on the bed for hours before I woke him. 在我叫醒它之前，狗已经在床上睡了好几个小时了。
She had been eating breakfast for an hour before she left. 她离开前已经吃了一个小时的早餐了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The dog will sleep on the bed tonight. 狗今晚会睡在床上。
She will eat breakfast tomorrow morning. 她明天早上会吃早餐。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
The dog will be sleeping on the bed when you arrive. 你到的时候，狗会在床上睡觉。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

By the time you get here, she will have eaten breakfast. 你到这儿的时候，她将会已经吃过早餐了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

By the time she finishes work, she will have been eating breakfast for two hours. 等她完成工作的时候，她将会已经吃了两个小时的早餐了。
