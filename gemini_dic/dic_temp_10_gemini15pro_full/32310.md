chignon:/ˈʃɪnjɒn/ (US) /ʃiːˈnjɔːŋ/ (UK)| n. |发髻，盘在脑后的低垂发髻|A chignon is a hairstyle achieved by pinning the hair into a knot at the nape of the neck or at the back of the head.|She wore a simple chignon for the wedding. 她婚礼上梳了一个简单的发髻。|近义词：bun, knot, updo|反义词: loose hair|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chignon*, from Old French *caignon* meaning "nape of the neck." Ultimately from Latin *canna* "reed, cane."
