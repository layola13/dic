neighbourly:/ˈneɪbərli/| adj.|邻近的；友好的；睦邻的|Friendly and helpful towards one's neighbours; sociable.|例句：She was always very neighbourly, offering help whenever she could. 她总是很友善，随时提供帮助。|近义词：friendly, amiable, amicable, sociable, congenial|反义词：unfriendly, hostile, unsociable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *neȝborlich, equivalent to neighbour +‎ -ly.
