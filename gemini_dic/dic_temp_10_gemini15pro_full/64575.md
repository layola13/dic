ersatzes:/ˈɛərzɑːtsəz/ /ˈɜːrzɑːtsəz/| n.|替代品；代用品|An inferior substitute or imitation.|Due to the wartime shortages, they had to use ersatz coffee made from roasted grain. 由于战时物资短缺，他们不得不使用由烘焙谷物制成的咖啡替代品。|Synonyms: substitute, replacement, imitation, alternative, equivalent, makeshift, synthetic, artificial, mock, pseudo|Antonyms: genuine, real, authentic, original|noun

ersatz:/ˈɛərzɑːts/ /ˈɜːrzɑːts/| adj.|替代的；代用的|Made or used as a substitute, typically an inferior one.|The ersatz leather didn't last very long.  人造革并不耐用。|Synonyms: substitute, replacement, imitation, artificial, synthetic, mock, pseudo, spurious|Antonyms: genuine, real, authentic, original|adjective
