inter-chains:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪnz/
adjective
链间的，跨链的
Connecting or involving different blockchain networks.  This term is frequently used in the context of blockchain technology and cryptocurrency.
Inter-chain communication protocols allow different blockchains to interact and exchange value.  跨链通信协议允许不同的区块链进行交互和交换价值。
The development of inter-chain solutions is crucial for the future of decentralized finance.  跨链解决方案的开发对于去中心化金融的未来至关重要。
Synonyms: cross-chain
Antonyms: intra-chain (within a single blockchain)
词性: adjective
