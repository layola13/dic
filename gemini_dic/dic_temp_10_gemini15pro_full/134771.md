nonmetal:/ˈnɑːnˌmetl/ /ˈnɒnˌmetl/| n. |非金属|An element that doesn't have the characteristics of a metal (such as hardness, luster, usually high melting point and electrical conductivity).  A nonmetal is typically brittle and a poor conductor of heat and electricity. |Oxygen is a nonmetal essential for life. 氧气是一种对生命至关重要的非金属。|Synonyms: non-metal, metalloid|Antonyms: metal|noun
