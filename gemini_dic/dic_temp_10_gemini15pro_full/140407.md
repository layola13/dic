ojibwa:/oʊˈdʒɪbwə/ /əˈdʒɪbweɪ/ | n. |奥吉布瓦人；奥吉布瓦语|A member of a Native American people of the Great Lakes region. Also, the Algonquian language of the Ojibwa. |例句：The Ojibwa have a rich oral tradition. 奥吉布瓦人有丰富的口头传统。|例句：She is learning to speak Ojibwa. 她正在学习说奥吉布瓦语。|近义词：Chippewa, Anishinaabe |反义词: (无明确的反义词，因为是指特定族群和语言) |词性:名词
