packman:/ˈpækmæn/| n. |吃豆人; 贪吃的人|A character in a video game who eats dots and avoids ghosts.  Also used informally to refer to someone who eats a lot.|例句：The packman gobbled up all the power pellets. 吃豆人吃掉了所有能量球。

例句：He's a real packman; he ate the entire pizza himself! 他真是个吃货，他一个人吃掉了整个披萨！|近义词：glutton, gourmand|反义词: anorexic|noun
