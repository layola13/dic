annuler: /a.ny.le/ (French)| v. |取消，撤销|To make void or of no effect; to cancel; to abolish.|例句：The meeting was annulled due to bad weather. 会议因天气恶劣被取消了。

例句：They annulled the contract. 他们取消了合同。

近义词：cancel, revoke, abolish, nullify, invalidate|反义词: ratify, confirm, validate|词性: verb
