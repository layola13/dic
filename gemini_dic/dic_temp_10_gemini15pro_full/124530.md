mountainous:/ˈmaʊntɪnəs/| adj.|多山的；巨大的；如山的|Characterized by the presence of many mountains; huge, immense.|例句：The region is known for its mountainous terrain. 这个地区以其多山的地形而闻名。|例句：They faced mountainous obstacles in their quest for justice.  他们在寻求正义的过程中面临着巨大的障碍。|近义词：hilly, rugged, alpine|反义词:flat, level, plain|词性:adjective
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French montaigneus, from montaigne ‘mountain’.
