counterproductively:/ˌkaʊntərprəˈdʌktɪvli/
adverb
适得其反地，反效果地
in a way that has the opposite of the desired effect
Working late into the night can be counterproductive if you're too tired the next day.  熬夜到很晚工作，如果第二天太累的话，可能会适得其反。
His aggressive tactics proved counterproductively. 他咄咄逼人的策略适得其反。
Synonyms: adversely, detrimentally, harmfully
Antonyms: productively, beneficially, helpfully
词性: adverb
