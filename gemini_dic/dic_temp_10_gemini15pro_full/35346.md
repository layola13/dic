co-effect:/ˌkoʊɪˈfɛkt/ | n. | 共同效应，协同效应 | A result or consequence that is produced by two or more factors acting together. | The co-effect of stress and lack of sleep can lead to serious health problems. 压力和睡眠不足的共同作用会导致严重的健康问题。| Synonyms: synergy, interaction, combined effect | Antonyms: individual effect, isolated effect | noun


co-effect:/ˌkoʊɪˈfɛkt/ | v. | 共同作用，协同作用 | To act together to produce a particular result. |  The two medications co-effect to lower blood pressure. 这两种药物共同作用以降低血压。 | Synonyms: synergize, interact, combine | Antonyms: act independently, act alone | verb
