nonconservative:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɜːvətɪv/ | adj. n.| 非保守的，非保守主义者 | Not adhering to or supporting conservative principles or practices;  a person who is not conservative. |  例句：Her nonconservative views on education surprised many. 她的非保守教育观点让很多人感到惊讶。

例句：He's a nonconservative in his political leanings. 他的政治倾向是非保守的。 | 近义词：liberal, progressive, radical | 反义词：conservative, traditional, orthodox | 词性：形容词，名词

Etymology: From non- (prefix meaning "not") + conservative.  "Conservative" traces back to Latin "conservare" meaning "to preserve."
