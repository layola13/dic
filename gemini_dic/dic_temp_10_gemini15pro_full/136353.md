nonresisting:/ˌnɒnrɪˈzɪstɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈzɪstɪŋ/
adjective
不抵抗的，不反抗的
Not resisting; offering no resistance.
The nonresisting crowd was easily dispersed by the police.  不抵抗的群众很容易就被警察驱散了。
The soldiers quickly subdued the nonresisting villagers. 士兵们迅速制服了不抵抗的村民。

近义词:passive, submissive, yielding, compliant
反义词:resisting, defiant, opposing, combative
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + resisting, present participle of resist (v.).  来自non-（不）+ resisting（resist的现在分词），resist (v.) 表示抵抗。
