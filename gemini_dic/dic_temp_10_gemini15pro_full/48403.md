dedicating:/ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
v.
奉献，致力于
devoting time, effort, or oneself to a particular task or purpose
She is dedicating her life to helping others. 她正致力于帮助他人。

dedicating:/ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
v.
题献，献给
addressing or offering a creative work to someone as a mark of respect or affection
He dedicated his first novel to his mother. 他将他的第一部小说献给了他的母亲。


SYNONYMS->
devoting, committing, consecrating, offering, addressing

ANTONYMS->
abandoning, neglecting, forsaking, ignoring, withholding

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dedicatus, past participle of dedicare "to dedicate, consecrate," from de- "down, completely" + dicare "to proclaim, dedicate."

WORD TYPE->
Verb (present participle)
