cockshead:/ˈkɒkshed/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkshed/ (US)| n. |鸡冠花，傻瓜，(罕用，冒犯)阴茎头|A plant with bright red, pink, or yellow flowers that look like a rooster's comb. Can also be used as a derogatory term for a foolish person or (rarely and offensively) for the head of a penis. |例句：The cockshead in the garden was a vibrant shade of red. 花园里的鸡冠花呈现鲜艳的红色。

The child called the other boy a cockshead after he tripped him.  那个孩子在他绊倒另一个男孩后，叫他傻瓜。|(罕用，冒犯) He drunkenly referred to his genitals as his cockshead. 他醉醺醺地指着自己的生殖器说是鸡冠头。|近义词：(for plant) celosia, woolflower; (for fool) idiot, moron, imbecile |反义词: (for fool) genius, wise person |词性:noun
