interflowing:/ˌɪntərˈfloʊɪŋ/
adjective
相互流动的，交汇的
flowing into and out of one another; blending or merging together.
The interflowing rivers created a fertile delta. 交汇的河流形成了肥沃的三角洲。
The interflowing streams eventually joined to form the great river. 这些交汇的溪流最终汇聚成大河。
intermingling, merging, blending, converging
diverging, separating, parting
adjective
