centric:/ˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|中心的，以…为中心的|placed in or at the center; arranged around or with respect to a center|例句：a Eurocentric view of the world  以欧洲为中心的世界观

例句：This research project is very student-centric.  这个研究项目非常以学生为中心。|近义词：central, focal, core|反义词:peripheral, marginal|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centricus, from centrum (“center”) + -icus.
