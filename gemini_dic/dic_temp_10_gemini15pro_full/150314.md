pangeometry:/ˌpænʤiˈɒmɪtri/ | n. | 泛几何 | The study of various geometries, including Euclidean geometry and non-Euclidean geometries, in a unified and comparative framework. |  Pangeometry encompasses the study of both Euclidean and non-Euclidean spaces. 泛几何包含对欧几里得空间和非欧几里得空间的研究。

Pangeometry allows mathematicians to compare and contrast different geometric systems. 泛几何允许数学家比较和对比不同的几何系统。 | 近义词: meta-geometry | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "pan-" (Greek: πᾶν) meaning "all" + "geometry"
