mosaicked:/moʊˈzeɪkt/ | adj. | 拼花的，镶嵌的 | Decorated with a mosaic; resembling a mosaic. |  The floor was mosaicked with tiny, colorful tiles. 地板是用五颜六色的小瓷砖拼成的。

The artist mosaicked the table with broken pieces of pottery.  艺术家用破碎的陶器碎片拼成了桌子。 | 近义词: inlaid, tessellated, patterned | 反义词: plain, simple | 词性: 形容词

词源: From mosaic + -ed.  Ultimately from Greek *mūseion* "belonging to the Muses," hence "artistic."
