ganed:/ɡeɪnd/ |v. (past tense of gain)| 获得，赢得 | This archaic form is rarely used in modern English. It is the past tense and past participle of "gain". Its meaning is "acquired or obtained something". | He ganed a great victory in the battle. 他在战斗中取得了伟大的胜利。 | Near synonyms: obtained, acquired, won, earned | Antonyms: lost, forfeited | verb

gain:/ɡeɪn/ |v. n. |获得，赢得；收益，增益| To obtain or acquire something desirable, to increase in wealth, size, or amount. |She gained a lot of experience from her internship. 她从实习中获得了许多经验。 | I gained five pounds over the holidays. 假期里我体重增加了五磅。| Near synonyms: obtain, acquire, win, earn, profit, increase | Antonyms: lose, forfeit, decrease, decline | verb, noun
