cormorant:/ˈkɔːrmərənt/ | adj. (less common) | 贪婪的，狼吞虎咽的 | Excessively greedy or rapacious. | His cormorant appetite for power led to his downfall. 他对权力的贪婪导致了他的垮台。| synonyms: ravenous, voracious, greedy | antonyms: abstemious, moderate | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corp marenc* (“sea raven”), from *corp* (“raven”) + *marenc* (“marine”).  The "p" was later dropped.  Ultimately from Latin *corvus marinus*.
