outpainting:/ˈaʊtˌpeɪntɪŋ/
n.
外绘, 扩展绘画
The process of extending an existing image by generating new visual content beyond its original boundaries.  This is often used in art and image editing to create larger canvases or add context to a scene.
The artist used outpainting to make the small portrait into a grand, full-length depiction.  这位艺术家使用外绘技术将这幅小肖像画变成了一幅宏伟的全身像。
extrapolation, extension, expansion
cropping, reduction, trimming
noun
