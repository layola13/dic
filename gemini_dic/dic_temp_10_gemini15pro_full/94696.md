improbative:/ɪmˈprɒbətɪv/ | adj. | 无证据的，不证明的 | Not tending to prove or disprove; having no probative value. | The defense lawyer argued that the evidence was improbative and should be dismissed. 辩护律师辩称，该证据无证据价值，应予驳回。 | The witness's testimony was deemed improbative and irrelevant to the case.  证人的证词被认为与案件无关，没有证据价值。| 近义词: irrelevant, immaterial, inconclusive | 反义词: probative, conclusive, relevant | 词性: adjective
