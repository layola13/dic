over-stored:/ˌoʊvərˈstɔːrd/ | adj. | 过度存储的，库存过剩的 | Having more goods stored than is necessary or desirable.  This often leads to increased storage costs and the risk of obsolescence. |  The warehouse was over-stored with unsold merchandise.  仓库里堆满了未售出的商品。

The company found itself over-stored with winter coats after an unexpectedly warm season.  在一个异常温暖的季节过后，这家公司发现自己积压了大量的冬衣。 | 近义词: overstocked, glutted | 反义词: understocked, depleted | 词性: 形容词
