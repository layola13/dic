access: /ˈækses/ (US) /ˈækses/ (UK)| n. v.|通道，入口；使用权，机会；读取（计算机信息）|The only access to the island is by boat. 到达该岛的唯一途径是乘船。|例句：Students have easy access to the library. 学生可以很方便地使用图书馆。

Students need access to computers and the internet for their studies. 学生学习需要使用电脑和互联网。


近义词：approach, entry, admittance, retrieval|反义词: denial, restriction, blockage|词性: 名词

access: /ˈækses/ (US) /ˈækses/ (UK)| v.|访问，进入；使用，获取|He accessed the files on the computer. 他访问了电脑上的文件。|例句：I cannot access my email account. 我无法访问我的电子邮件帐户。

She accessed the information she needed through the library's online database. 她通过图书馆的在线数据库获取了她需要的信息。


近义词：obtain, retrieve, reach, enter|反义词: block, prevent|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "acces" (noun), "acceder" (verb), from Latin "accessus" meaning "arrival, approach," past participle of "accedere"  "to approach, go to," from "ad-" ("to, toward") + "cedere" ("to go, move").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She accesses the internet daily for work. = 她每天上网工作。
He has access to confidential information. = 他可以接触到机密信息。

一般过去时:
I accessed the building using my security card. = 我用我的安全卡进入了大楼。
They accessed the database to find the missing information. = 他们访问了数据库以查找丢失的信息。


现在完成时:
We have accessed this file several times this week. =  这周我们已经多次访问过这个文件。
The hackers have accessed the company's servers. = 黑客已经访问了公司的服务器。


过去完成时:
After he had accessed the account, he changed the password. = 在他访问了账户之后，他更改了密码。
She realized someone had accessed her files without her permission. = 她意识到有人未经她的许可访问了她的文件。

将来时:
I will access the report as soon as it's available. = 报告一出来我就去看。
They will access the funds next week. = 他们下周将使用这些资金。

虚拟语气:
If I could access the system, I would fix the problem. = 如果我能访问系统，我会解决这个问题。
I wish I could access the files remotely. = 我希望我可以远程访问这些文件。


被动语态:
The website was accessed by thousands of users. = 该网站被成千上万的用户访问。
The data is being accessed by unauthorized personnel. = 数据正被未经授权的人员访问。


疑问句:
Can you access the internet from your phone? = 你可以用手机上网吗？
How do I access my online banking account? = 我如何访问我的网上银行账户？

否定句:
I cannot access the file because it's password-protected. = 我无法访问该文件，因为它受密码保护。
They did not access the restricted area. = 他们没有进入禁区。




PHRASE->
have access to = 可以使用/接触...
gain access to = 获得...的访问权
deny access to = 拒绝访问...
easy access to = 容易获得/使用...
access to information = 获取信息
