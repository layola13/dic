out-thunder:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndər/
verb
以更大的雷声盖过; 比…雷声更大
to surpass in loudness of thunder; to thunder louder than
The storm out-thundered the previous one. 这场暴风雨的雷声比上次的更大。
近义词: drown out, overpower
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "be quieter than" in the context of thunder)
词性: verb
