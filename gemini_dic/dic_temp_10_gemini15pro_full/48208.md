deconglomerate:/ˌdiːkənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ /ˌdiːkənˈɡlɑːməreɪt/
verb
分解，拆分（大型企业集团）
To separate a large business organization into smaller, independent companies.
The company decided to deconglomerate and focus on its core business.  这家公司决定拆分，专注于其核心业务。
The government encouraged the deconglomeration of large monopolies. 政府鼓励大型垄断企业拆分。
Synonyms: break up, divest, dismantle, separate
Antonyms: merge, consolidate, combine
词性: 动词


deconglomerate:/ˌdiːkənˈɡlɒməreɪt/ /ˌdiːkənˈɡlɑːməreɪt/
noun
分解，拆分（大型企业集团）
The act of separating a large business organization into smaller, independent companies.
The deconglomerate of the company led to increased competition.  公司的拆分导致竞争加剧。
The 1990s saw a wave of deconglomerates in the US. 20世纪90年代，美国出现了一波企业拆分浪潮。
Synonyms: breakup, divestiture, dismantling, separation
Antonyms: merger, consolidation, combination
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
