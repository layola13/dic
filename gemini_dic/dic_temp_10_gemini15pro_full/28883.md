carpoolers:/ˈkɑːrˌpuːlərz/| n. |拼车者；合伙用车的人|People who travel together in a car, usually to work or school, in order to save money and reduce traffic. |例句：The carpoolers met at the designated spot every morning. 拼车的人每天早上在指定地点集合。

例句：The company offered incentives to encourage employees to become carpoolers. 公司提供奖励措施鼓励员工拼车。|近义词：ride-sharers, commuters|反义词:solo drivers|词性:Noun (plural)
