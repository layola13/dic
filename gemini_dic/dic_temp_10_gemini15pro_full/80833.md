gougers:/ˈɡaʊdʒərz/
n.| 敲竹杠者；奸商；暴利者|A person who charges exorbitant prices for goods or services, especially during a time of shortage or emergency.|The gougers were selling bottled water for $10 after the hurricane. 飓风过后，奸商们以10美元的价格出售瓶装水。| profiteers, extortionists | philanthropists, altruists
n. | 凿子；圆凿|A chisel with a curved blade used for scooping or gouging.|He used a gouger to carve the intricate details of the wooden statue. 他用圆凿雕刻木雕的复杂细节。 | chisel, carving tool | -
