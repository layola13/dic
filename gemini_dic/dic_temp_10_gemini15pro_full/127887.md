neocyanine:/ˌniːoʊˈsaɪəˌnaɪn/| n.|新花青|A synthetic dye belonging to the cyanine group, typically used as a near-infrared fluorescent probe in biological and medical imaging.|Neocyanine is used to label cells for in vivo imaging. 新花青用于标记细胞进行体内成像。|Near-infrared dyes, fluorophores|N/A|noun
