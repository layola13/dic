enclosures:/ɪnˈkləʊʒəz/ (UK), /ɪnˈkloʊʒərz/ (US)| n.| 附件，围栏，围场 |Something that is enclosed, such as a document or photograph sent with a letter, or a piece of land surrounded by a fence or wall. |Please find the enclosures with this email.  请查看此邮件的附件。|The sheep were safe in their enclosure. 羊群在围栏里很安全。| synonyms: attachments, supplements, compounds, fences, barriers | antonyms:  exclusions, detachments, openings | noun
