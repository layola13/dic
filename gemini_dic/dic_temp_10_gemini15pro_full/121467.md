misk:/mɪsk/| n.|麝香; 麝;|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance secreted by a gland in the abdomen of the male musk deer, used in perfumery.|The perfume had a base note of musk. 这款香水有麝香的基调。|近义词:perfume, scent, fragrance|反义词: stench, odor|名词
