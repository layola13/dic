ghazel:/ˈɡɑːzəl/ | n. | غزل (ghazal) | A lyric poem with a fixed number of verses and a repeated rhyme, often expressing the pain of loss or separation and commonly set to music. |  She recited a beautiful ghazel about the pain of unrequited love. 她吟诵了一首优美的表达单恋之痛的抒情诗。| synonym: lyric, ode, poem | antonym: prose, essay | noun
