hyperconservatively:/ˌhaɪpər kənˈsɜːrvətɪvli/ | adv. | 极端保守地 | In a way that is extremely averse to change or innovation, adhering rigidly to traditional values and practices. | The budget was drafted hyperconservatively, avoiding any new spending initiatives.  预算起草得极端保守，避免任何新的支出计划。|  The new CEO approached the company's challenges hyperconservatively, reluctant to deviate from established procedures. 新任首席执行官以极端保守的态度应对公司挑战，不愿偏离既定程序。 | Synonyms: ultraconservatively, rigidly, reactionarily | Antonyms: progressively, liberally, radically | Adverb
