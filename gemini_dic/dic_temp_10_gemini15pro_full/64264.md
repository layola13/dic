equipotential:/ˌiːkwɪpəˈtɛnʃəl/
adjective|等势的，等位的|Having the same potential (voltage).|The equipotential lines on a weather map connect points of equal barometric pressure.|Synonyms: isopotential, level|Antonyms: none|adjective


equipotential:/ˌiːkwɪpəˈtɛnʃəl/
noun|等势面，等位面|A surface or line on which the potential is constant.|The electric field is always perpendicular to the equipotential surfaces.|Synonyms: isopotential surface, level surface|Antonyms: none|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From equi- (equal) + potential.  "Equi-" comes from Latin "aequus" meaning "equal, level, fair". "Potential" comes from Late Latin "potentialis" meaning "possible, potential".
