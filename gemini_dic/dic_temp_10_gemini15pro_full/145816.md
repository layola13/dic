overdeepening:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpənɪŋ/
n.
过度深化；过深挖掘；过度下切
The process by which a glacier erodes a valley floor below sea level, resulting in a basin that can become a lake after the glacier retreats.
Glacial overdeepening created the deep fjord. 冰川的过度深化创造了深邃的峡湾。

近义词: overexcavation
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shallower formation)
词性: noun

overdeepen:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːpən/
v.
过度深化；过深挖掘；过度下切
To erode (a valley floor, for example) to an excessive depth.
The glacier overdeepened the valley, creating a lake. 冰川过度下切山谷，形成了一个湖泊。

近义词: overexcavate
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: verb
