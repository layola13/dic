limits:/ˈlɪmɪts/ | n. | 限制；界限；范围 |The point, edge, or boundary beyond which something does not or cannot extend. |  He pushed his body to its limits. 他把自己的身体推向了极限。| synonyms: boundaries, restrictions, constraints | antonyms: boundlessness, infinity, vastness | noun

limits:/ˈlɪmɪts/ | v. | 限制；限定 | To restrict or confine within certain bounds. |  Budget cuts have limited our spending. 预算削减限制了我们的开支。| synonyms: restrict, confine, restrain | antonyms: expand, broaden, extend | verb
