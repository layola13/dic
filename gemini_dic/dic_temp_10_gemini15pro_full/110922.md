lipoprotein:/ˌlɪpoʊˈproʊtiːn/, /ˌlɪpəˈprəʊtiːn/ | n. | 脂蛋白 |  Any of a group of soluble proteins that combine with and transport fat or other lipids in the blood plasma. |  High levels of low-density lipoprotein (LDL) can increase your risk of heart disease.  高密度脂蛋白 (LDL) 偏高会增加患心脏病的风险。| synonyms:  lipid-protein complex | antonyms: None | noun

lipoprotein:/ˌlɪpoʊˈproʊtiːn/, /ˌlɪpəˈprəʊtiːn/ | n. | 脂蛋白 |  A biochemical assembly that contains both proteins and lipids, bound to the proteins, which allow fats to move through the water inside and outside cells. The proteins serve to emulsify the lipid molecules. | Regular exercise can increase your levels of high-density lipoprotein (HDL).  规律的运动可以提高你的高密度脂蛋白 (HDL) 水平。| synonyms:  lipid-protein conjugate | antonyms: None | noun
