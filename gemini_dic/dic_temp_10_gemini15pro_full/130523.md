nonaccredited:/ˌnɒnəˈkrɛdɪtɪd/
adjective
未经认证的，未经认可的
Not officially recognized or authorized by a relevant authority, especially in education.
The online course I took was nonaccredited, so it didn't count towards my degree. 我上的在线课程未经认证，所以它不算我的学位。
The diploma mill issued nonaccredited degrees.  文凭工厂颁发未经认证的学位。
Synonym: uncertified, unauthorized, unapproved
Antonym: accredited, certified, approved
词性: 形容词
