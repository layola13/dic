expiscatory:/ˌɛkspɪˈskeɪtəri/ /ˌɛkspɪˈskətɔːri/
adjective
解释的；辩解的；赎罪的
Serving or intended to expiate or atone for; making amends or reparation.
例句：He offered an expiscatory sacrifice to appease the gods. 他献上赎罪祭品来安抚众神。

例句：She wrote an expiscatory letter to her friend after their argument.  她和朋友争吵后，写了一封道歉信。
近义词：apologetic, atoning, propitiatory, conciliatory
反义词：accusatory, incriminating
词性: adjective
