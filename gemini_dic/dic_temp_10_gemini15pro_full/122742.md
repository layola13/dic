moistening:/ˈmɔɪsənɪŋ/ | v. | 润湿，使湿润 | The gentle rain was moistening the dry earth. 温柔的雨水滋润着干燥的土地. |  Making the cake involved moistening the dry ingredients with milk. 制作蛋糕需要用牛奶润湿干燥的配料。|近义词：dampening, wetting, humidifying |反义词：drying, dehydrating |词性:动词(现在分词)

moistening:/ˈmɔɪsənɪŋ/ | adj. | 润湿的，使湿润的 |  a moistening rain 一场润湿的雨 | She applied a moistening lotion to her dry skin. 她把润肤露涂在干燥的皮肤上。 |近义词：dampening, wetting, humidifying |反义词：drying, dehydrating |词性:形容词

词源: From moisten + -ing.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *maikstaz (“moist”).
