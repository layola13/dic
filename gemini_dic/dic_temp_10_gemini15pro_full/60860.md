elegance:/ˈɛlɪɡəns/| n.|优雅，雅致，精致|The elegance of her movements captivated the audience. 她的优雅举止吸引了观众。|例句：She furnished her apartment with simple elegance. 她用简洁雅致的风格布置了她的公寓。

例句：He admired the elegance of her writing style. 他欣赏她优雅的写作风格。

近义词：grace, refinement, sophistication, poise, style|反义词：inelegance, awkwardness, clumsiness, gaudiness, vulgarity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French elegance (“choice, refinement”), from Latin ēlēgantia (“taste, refinement”), from ēlēgans (“choice, tasteful, refined, elegant”), present participle of ēlēgō (“I choose, select”), from ex (“out”) + legō (“I gather, choose”).
