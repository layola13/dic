goldfinches:/ˈɡoʊldˌfɪntʃɪz/| n. |金翅雀（复数）|A small, brightly colored finch, typically with yellow markings on the wings and tail.  The plural form of "goldfinch".|例句：The goldfinches flitted among the branches. 金翅雀在树枝间飞来飞去。

ETYMOLOGY->
From "gold" + "finch".  "Finch" comes from Old English "finc",  referring to the characteristic twittering sound of the bird.
