accusable:/əˈkjuːzəbl/| adj.|可指责的，可控告的|Subject to or deserving of accusation; blameworthy.  Able to be accused.|He was accusable of negligence in his duties. 他因玩忽职守而受到指责。|近义词：blameworthy, culpable, guilty, indictable|反义词: innocent, blameless|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *accusable*, from Old French *acusable*, from Latin *accusabilis*, from *accusare* ("to accuse"), from *ad* ("to") + *causa* ("cause, reason").
USAGE->
He is accusable of theft. = 他被指控犯有盗窃罪。
The company is accusable of polluting the river. = 这家公司被指控污染了河流。

He was found to be accusable in the matter. = 他被发现在此事中应负责任。
She was accusable of breaking her promise. = 她被指责违背了诺言。

PHRASE->
accusable of =  因...可被指控
is accusable =  应负责任
was accusable = 曾应负责任
hold someone accusable = 认为某人应负责任
find someone accusable = 发现某人应负责任
