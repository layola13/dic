overtwists:/ˌōvərˈtwɪsts/ | v. n. |过度扭转，过度缠绕|To twist or coil something too much.  As a noun, it refers to the state of being overtwisted.|
The rope overtwisted and snapped. 绳子过度扭转而断裂了。
She overtwisted her ankle while dancing. 她跳舞时扭伤了脚踝。
The plot of the movie was overtwisted and confusing. 这部电影的情节过于曲折，令人费解。
He gave the knob an overtwist, and it broke off. 他过度扭转了旋钮，把它弄断了。
The yarn overtwists easily, making it difficult to knit with. 这种纱线很容易过度缠绕，很难用来编织。|
近义词: overwind, overtighten |
反义词: untwist, loosen |
词性: verb, noun
