disassemblable:/dɪsəˈsɛmbləbəl/
adj.
可拆卸的
Capable of being disassembled; able to be taken apart.
The prefabricated house was easily disassemblable and transportable.  这种预制房屋易于拆卸和运输。
dismountable, detachable, separable
non-disassemblable, fixed, permanent
词性:形容词


disassemble:/dɪsəˈsɛmbəl/
v.
拆卸
To take apart; to separate something into its component parts.
He disassembled the clock to clean it. 他拆卸了钟表以便清洁。
dismantle, take apart, break down
assemble, put together, construct
词性:动词
