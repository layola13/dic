hems:/hɛmz/
noun|边缘，摺边|The edge or border of a garment or piece of cloth, especially when turned up and sewn.|She stitched the hems of the curtains. 她缝纫了窗帘的边缘。|edges, borders, margins|centers, middles|n.
hems:/hɛmz/
verb|(hems, hemming, hemmed)|缝边，包围，限制|To turn up and sew the edge of a piece of cloth or garment; to surround and confine.|She hemmed the skirt to make it shorter. 她给裙子缝边以使其变短。|sew, stitch, border|unravel, free|v.
