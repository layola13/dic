adlai:/ˈædˌlaɪ/
n.|阿德莱，阿德莱·史蒂文森|Adlai refers to Adlai Stevenson, a prominent American politician and diplomat who served as the 31st Governor of Illinois and was the Democratic Party's nominee for president in 1952 and 1956.|Adlai Stevenson was known for his eloquence and wit. 阿德莱·史蒂文森以其雄辩和机智而闻名。
Adlai Stevenson delivered a powerful speech at the United Nations. 阿德莱·史蒂文森在联合国发表了强有力的演讲。|近义词:statesman, politician|反义词: N/A
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the given name Adlai, ultimately of Hebrew origin.
