instant:/ˈɪnstənt/ (US), /ˈɪnstənt/ (UK)| n.|瞬间，片刻|a very short period of time|例句：The pain was only for an instant. 疼痛只是一瞬间的事。|近义词：moment, second, jiffy|反义词:eternity, lifetime|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *instāns*, present participle of *instāre* (“to stand near, urge, press on”), from *in-* + *stāre* (“to stand”).
