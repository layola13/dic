bipartient:/baɪˈpɑːrʃənt/ | adj. |二分的，由两部分组成的 | Divided into two parts; shared by two. |  The bipartient agreement ensured both companies benefited. 这份二分协议确保两家公司都受益。|  Example: The bipartient commission was made up of members from both political parties.  这个两党委员会由来自两个政党的成员组成。 | Synonyms:  dichotomous, dual, two-fold | Antonyms: unified, whole, singular | adjective

bipartition:/baɪpɑːrˈtɪʃən/ | n. |二分，一分成二 | The act of dividing something into two parts. | The bipartition of Germany after World War II resulted in two separate states. 二战后德国被一分为二，形成了两个独立的国家。| Example: The bipartition of Cyprus has created lasting political tension. 塞浦路斯的二分造成了持久的政治紧张局势。 | Synonyms: division, dichotomy, severance | Antonyms: union, unification, merger | noun
