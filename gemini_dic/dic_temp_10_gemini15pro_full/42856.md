courteousness:/ˈkɜːrtiəsnəs/ | n. | 礼貌；客气；殷勤 | The quality of being polite, respectful, and considerate. |  She treated everyone with the utmost courteousness. 她以最大的礼貌对待每个人。| synonyms: politeness, civility, respect, courtesy, grace, good manners | antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect, discourtesy | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corteisie* "courtesy, courtliness," from *corteis* "courteous," from *cort* "court" (from Latin *cohors* "enclosed yard, company of soldiers").  The suffix *-ness* is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
