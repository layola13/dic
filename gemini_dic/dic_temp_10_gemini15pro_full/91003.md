husbandage:/ˈhʌzbəndɪdʒ/
n.|丈夫的职责；家政；精打细算；(土地、资源等的)管理|The act or practice of managing resources economically; thrift.  The responsibilities of a husband. The management of a household or farm.|例句：He showed good husbandage in his financial affairs. 他在财务方面表现出良好的精打细算。|例句：Good husbandage of our natural resources is essential for future generations.  妥善管理我们的自然资源对子孙后代至关重要。|近义词：management, thrift, economy, stewardship|反义词：wastefulness, extravagance|词性:名词
