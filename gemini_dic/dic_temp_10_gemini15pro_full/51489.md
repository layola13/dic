detracting:/dɪˈtræktɪŋ/
adjective
减损的，贬低的
reducing the value, importance, or quality of something
The scandal is detracting from his reputation as a trustworthy politician. 这桩丑闻损害了他作为值得信赖的政治家的声誉。
近义词: diminishing, lessening, belittling
反义词: enhancing, improving, augmenting
词性: adjective

detract:/dɪˈtrækt/
verb
贬低，减损
to reduce the value, importance, or quality of something
The constant interruptions detracted from the enjoyment of the film.  不断的打扰降低了电影的观赏性。
近义词: diminish, lessen, belittle
反义词: enhance, improve, augment
词性: verb

词源:  From Latin *dētractāre*, frequentative of *dētrahere*  "to draw away," from *dē* "away" + *trahere* "to draw."
