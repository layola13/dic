oxaldehyde:/ˌɒksælˈdɪhaɪd/ | n. | 醛糖;氧化醛 | An aldehyde group attached to a sugar.  A carbohydrate with an aldehyde functional group. |  Example: Glucose is an oxaldehyde. 葡萄糖是一种醛糖。

Example:  The oxaldehyde reacted with the Tollen's reagent to form a silver mirror. 氧化醛与托伦试剂反应形成银镜。| 近义词: aldose | 反义词: ketose | 词性: noun
