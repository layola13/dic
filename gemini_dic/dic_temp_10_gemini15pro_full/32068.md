cherubimical: /ˌtʃɛrʊˈbɪmɪkəl/
adjective
天使般的，似小天使的
Resembling or characteristic of a cherub, especially in being innocent-looking, sweet, and beautiful.
例句：The child had a cherubimical face with rosy cheeks and bright eyes.  这孩子有着天使般的面容，有着红润的脸颊和明亮的眼睛。

例句：The cherubimical choirboys sang with angelic voices.  天使般的小唱诗班成员用天籁之音歌唱。

近义词：angelic, seraphic, cherubic
反义词：diabolic, demonic, fiendish
词性：adjective

词源：From cherubim + -ical.  Ultimately from the Hebrew word *kərūb*.
