niggler:/ˈnɪɡlər/| n.|吹毛求疵的人；爱挑剔的人；琐碎的抱怨者|A person who constantly finds fault, especially with trivial matters; a petty complainer.|He's such a niggler, always complaining about the smallest things. 他真是个爱挑剔的人，总是抱怨一些鸡毛蒜皮的小事。|近义词：nitpicker, quibbler, faultfinder, caviler|反义词:easygoing person, tolerant person|词性:noun

niggle:/ˈnɪɡl(ə)l/| v.|1. 为小事烦恼；不断地烦扰；使苦恼  2. 挑剔；吹毛求疵|1. To worry or irritate continuously; to fret about minor details. 2. To find fault with; to criticize pettily.|1.  The doubt niggled at the back of her mind.  一丝疑虑在她脑海里挥之不去。|2. He keeps niggling about the cost. 他一直对费用吹毛求疵。|近义词：fuss, fret, worry, carp, cavil|反义词:praise, commend|词性:verb
