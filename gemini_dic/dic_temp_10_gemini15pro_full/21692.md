boa:/boʊə/ (US) /ˈbəʊə/ (UK)| n.|蟒蛇; 女用长围巾|A large snake that kills animals by wrapping itself around them and crushing them; A long, thin piece of fur or feathers worn by women, usually around the shoulders.|例句：The boa constrictor is a powerful snake.  蟒蛇是一种强壮的蛇。

例句：She wore a fluffy boa to the opera. 她戴着一条蓬松的围巾去歌剧院。|近义词：python (for the snake), scarf, stole (for the garment)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
