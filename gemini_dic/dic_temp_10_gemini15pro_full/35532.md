coagulator:/koʊˈæɡjəleɪtər/| n.| 凝结剂，凝固剂 |A substance that causes a liquid to change to a solid or semisolid state.|The doctor applied a coagulator to stop the bleeding. 医生使用凝结剂来止血。|Synonyms: clotting agent, thickener |Antonyms: thinner, anticoagulant|noun


coagulate:/koʊˈæɡjəleɪt/| v.| 凝结，凝固 |To change from a liquid to a thick mass; to clot.|The blood began to coagulate around the wound. 血液开始在伤口周围凝结。|Synonyms: clot, thicken, congeal |Antonyms: thin, liquefy|verb
