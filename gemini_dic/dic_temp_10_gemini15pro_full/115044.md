mandor:/mænˈdɔːr/ | n. | 工头，领班 | A person who is in charge of a group of workers, especially in agriculture or construction. |  The mandor instructed the workers to start harvesting the crops. 工头指示工人开始收割庄稼。| synonyms: foreman, overseer, supervisor | antonyms: worker, laborer | noun

mandor:/mænˈdɔːr/ | n. | (chiefly Malaysia and Indonesia)  a headman or village chief.  | The mandor called a village meeting to discuss the upcoming festival. 村长召集村民开会讨论即将到来的节日。 | synonyms: headman, chief, village leader | antonyms: villager, resident | noun
