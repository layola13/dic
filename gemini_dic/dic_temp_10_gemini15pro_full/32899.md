chong:/tʃɒŋ/ /tʃɑːŋ/ | n. | 虫; 昆虫; 蠕虫 | A general term for insects, worms, and other small creeping creatures. | Example:  The bird ate a chong from the ground.  鸟从地上吃了一条虫子。

Example:  Many different chongs live in the rainforest.  雨林里生活着许多不同的昆虫。 | Synonym: insect, bug, worm, creepy-crawly | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a general term like "chong") | noun
