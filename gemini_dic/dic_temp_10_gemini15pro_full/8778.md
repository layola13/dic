antiecclesiastical:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkəl/
adjective
反对教会的；反教权的
Opposed to the church or its power and influence.
The writer's antiecclesiastical views were evident in his satirical portrayal of the clergy.  这位作家对教会的反对观点在他对神职人员的讽刺描写中显而易见。
