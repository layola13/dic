individualism: /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəm/ (US) /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒʊəlɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |个人主义|The doctrine that the interests of the individual are or ought to be ethically paramount; also: a policy of individualism.|  Western culture often emphasizes individualism. 西方文化通常强调个人主义。

Individualism can be a strength, but also a weakness. 个人主义可以是一种优势，也可以是一种劣势。

近义词: independence, self-reliance, autonomy | 反义词: collectivism, socialism, communalism | 词性: noun
