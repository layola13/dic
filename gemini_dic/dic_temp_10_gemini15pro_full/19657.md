bilamellated:/ˌbaɪləˈmɛleɪtɪd/
adjective
薄片状的，由薄片组成的
composed of thin layers or lamellae
The bilamellated structure of the bone allows for flexibility and strength.  这块骨头的薄片状结构使其兼具柔韧性和强度。
Electron microscopy revealed the bilamellated structure of the cell membrane. 电子显微镜揭示了细胞膜的薄片状结构。
Synonyms: layered, laminated, stratified
Antonyms: homogeneous, uniform
词性: 形容词
