ascertainers:/ˌæsərˈteɪnərz/
n.
确定者，查明者
People who ascertain something; investigators.
The ascertainers of the facts presented their findings to the court. 事实查明者向法庭提交了他们的调查结果。

The ascertainers worked tirelessly to determine the cause of the accident. 查明者不知疲倦地工作，以确定事故的原因。
近义词: investigators, examiners, researchers, inquirers
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps those who obfuscate or conceal)  没有直接的反义词，也许是那些混淆或隐瞒的人
