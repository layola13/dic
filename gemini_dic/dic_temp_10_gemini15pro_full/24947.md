brush-like:/ˈbrʌʃˌlaɪk/
adjective
像刷子一样的；刷子状的
Resembling a brush, especially in shape or texture.
The plant had brush-like flowers.  这种植物的花像刷子一样。
The artist used a brush-like tool to create the texture.  艺术家用一种像刷子一样的工具来创造纹理。
近义词: bristly, brushy
反义词: smooth, sleek
词性: adjective
