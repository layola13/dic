hearten:/ˈhɑːrtn/ (US), /ˈhɑːtn/ (UK)| v. |鼓励，鼓舞，振奋人心|To give someone courage or confidence; to cheer someone up.|The coach's words heartened the team after their initial defeat. 教练的话在球队初次失利后鼓舞了他们。|
The news of the successful surgery heartened her family.  手术成功的消息让她的家人振奋不已。|
Synonyms: encourage, cheer, uplift, inspire, invigorate|
Antonyms: discourage, dispirit, deject, depress, dampen|
