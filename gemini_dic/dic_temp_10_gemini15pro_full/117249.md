mechanic: /məˈkænɪk/ | n. | 技工，机修工 | A person skilled in the operation or repair of machinery, especially motor vehicles. |  例句：The mechanic quickly diagnosed the problem with the car. 技工很快诊断出了汽车的问题。

例句：She took her car to the mechanic for a tune-up. 她把车送到修理工那里去调校。

近义词：technician, repairman, engineer | 反义词: amateur, novice | 词性: noun

mechanic: /məˈkænɪkəl/ | adj. | 机械的；力学的；呆板的 | Relating to machines or mechanics; operated or produced by a machine or machinery; done without thought; automatic. | 例句：The car had a mechanical failure. 汽车发生了机械故障。

例句：He gave a mechanical response, showing no real emotion. 他机械地回应，没有表现出任何真实的情感。

近义词：automated, robotic, involuntary | 反义词: manual, intentional, spontaneous | 词性: adjective
