figged:/fɪɡd/ | adj. |打扮好的，盛装的; (尤指)穿着奇特服装的 | Dressed up, especially in fancy or unusual clothes.  |例句：She was all figged out for the party. 她为派对盛装打扮。

例句：The children were figged out in their Halloween costumes. 孩子们穿着万圣节服装打扮得花枝招展。|近义词：dressed up, dolled up, decked out |反义词：dressed down, casual |词性:形容词

词源:  The origin is uncertain.  One suggestion connects it to "fig," referring to a small piece of decorative material or a particular style of dress. Another links it to "fike," meaning to be restless or fidgety, perhaps implying the extra energy one puts into dressing up.
