mispropose:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊz/
verb
错误地求婚；提出不当的建议
to propose something inappropriate or unsuitable; to make a bad proposal.
He misproposed to her, thinking she was wealthy. 他错误地向她求婚，以为她很富有。
近义词: misjudge, blunder
反义词: propose correctly
