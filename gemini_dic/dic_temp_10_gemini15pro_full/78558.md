ghostwritten:/ˈɡoʊstˌrɪtn/ | adj. | 代笔的，由他人代写的 | Written by someone else on behalf of the credited author. | The celebrity's autobiography was ghostwritten by a journalist.  名人的自传是由一位记者代笔的。| Example:  Many politicians' speeches are ghostwritten. 许多政治家的演讲稿都是由人代笔的。| Synonyms:  authored by another, penned by a ghost, ghost-authored | Antonyms: self-written, personally authored | word property: adjective

ghostwritten:/ˈɡoʊstˌrɪtn/ | v. | 代笔 | To write for and in the name of another person. | He ghostwrote the autobiography for the aging film star. 他为这位年迈的电影明星代笔写自传。| Example: She makes a living ghostwriting novels for busy celebrities. 她靠为忙碌的名人代笔写小说为生。| Synonyms: ghost-author, ghost | Antonyms: author, write oneself | word property: verb (past participle)
