fictionary:/ˈfɪkʃəˌnɛri/
n.
虚构词典，虚构词汇集
A dictionary of fictional words or a collection of fictional vocabulary.
She compiled a fictionary of terms used in her fantasy novel. 她为自己的奇幻小说编写了一本虚构词汇集。
