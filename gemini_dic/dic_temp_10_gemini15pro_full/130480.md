nonabusively:/ˌnɑːnəˈbjuːsɪvli/
adverb
非滥用地；不带侮辱性地
in a way that does not involve abuse or insult.
例句：He spoke to her nonabusively, even though he was angry. 即使他很生气，他也心平气和地和她说话。

例句：The software is designed to be used nonabusively and for educational purposes.  这款软件设计用于非滥用和教育目的。

近义词：respectfully, kindly, politely, decently
反义词：abusively, offensively, insultingly


词性: adverb
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "abusive" (adjective meaning "using harsh and insulting language") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
