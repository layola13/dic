corroval: /kəˈroʊvəl/
n.
approval; confirmation
An act of approving or confirming something.
The president gave his corroval to the new policy. 总统批准了这项新政策。
The project needs the corroval of the board. 这个项目需要董事会的批准。
近义词: approval, confirmation, ratification, sanction, endorsement
反义词: disapproval, rejection, veto
词性: noun

词源:  Likely a back-formation from "corroborate" (to confirm or support), influenced by words like "approval."  While not a standard English word, it appears occasionally, especially in legal or technical contexts, intending to convey a stronger sense of official or formal approval.
