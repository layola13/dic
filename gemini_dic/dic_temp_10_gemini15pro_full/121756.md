mispurchases:/ˌmɪsˈpɜːrtʃəsɪz/
noun
错误购买；买错的东西
The act of purchasing something incorrectly, either by buying the wrong item, quantity, or at an unfavorable price.
Due to a computer glitch, the customer made several mispurchases and had to return the items. 由于电脑故障，顾客买错了好几样东西，不得不退货。
近义词:  wrong purchases, incorrect purchases, bad buys
反义词:  good buys, successful purchases
词性: noun
