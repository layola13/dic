developmentalist: /dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəlɪst/ | n. | 发展主义者；研习发展论者 | A specialist in the study of development, particularly child development.  |  Example: The developmentalist observed the child's interaction with its mother.  这位发展专家观察了孩子与母亲的互动。

Example:  Many developmentalists believe that early childhood experiences are crucial. 许多发展专家认为，幼儿时期的经历至关重要。 | 近义词: development expert, development specialist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "developmental" + "-ist". "Developmental" comes from "develop" (from Old French "desveloper," meaning "to unfold, unwrap") + "-mental" (suffix forming adjectives). "-ist" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something, or holds certain principles or beliefs.
