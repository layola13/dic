alloo:/əˈluː/
interjection
喂; (打电话时用语)
Used to attract attention, especially when answering the telephone.
Alloo, is that Jean? 喂，是琼吗？
近义词:hello, hi
反义词:goodbye
词性:interjection

词源:
From French *allô*, from *halloa*, probably from Germanic *halō* "hello".
