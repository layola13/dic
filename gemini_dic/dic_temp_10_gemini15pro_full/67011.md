expiration:/ˌɛkspəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |到期，期满，呼气|The expiration date on the milk is tomorrow.  牛奶的有效期是明天。| The expiration of his term as president is next year. 他的总统任期明年到期。|expiry, termination| commencement, beginning | noun

etymology->
From Latin *exspiratio*, from exspirare (“to breathe out, expire”).

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The contract expires next month.  合同下个月到期。
My passport expires in two years. 我的护照两年后到期。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
My membership is expiring soon. 我的会员资格即将到期。
The coupon is expiring at the end of the month.  优惠券在这个月底到期。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The warranty has expired. 保修期已过。
His driver's license has expired. 他的驾照已经过期了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The food has been expiring slowly in the fridge. 食物在冰箱里慢慢过期。
The medication has been expiring gradually.  药物逐渐失效。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The lease expired last week. 租约上周到期。
The offer expired yesterday.  优惠昨天到期。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The contract was expiring when we signed the new one. 我们签新合同时，旧合同即将到期。
The medicine was expiring, so I had to get a new prescription.  药快过期了，所以我不得不重新开处方。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The milk had expired before we drank it. 我们喝牛奶之前它就已经过期了。
The license had expired before he realized it.  在他意识到之前，执照已经过期了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The yogurt had been expiring in the back of the fridge for weeks.  酸奶在冰箱后面已经过期好几个星期了。
The cheese had been expiring slowly in the pantry.  奶酪在食品储藏室里慢慢地过期了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The agreement will expire next year. 协议将于明年到期。
The visa will expire soon. 签证很快就会到期。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
The contract will be expiring soon, so we need to negotiate a new one.  合同很快就要到期了，所以我们需要协商一个新的合同。
The parking permit will be expiring next month. 停车许可证下个月到期。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By the time we get there, the offer will have expired.  等我们到那里的时候，优惠就已经过期了。
By next week, the coupon will have expired. 到下周，优惠券就过期了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, my driver’s license will have been expiring for six months. 到明年，我的驾照将已经过期六个月了。(不太自然)
By the end of the month, this promotion will have been expiring for two weeks. 到月底，这个促销活动将已经过期两周了。(不太自然)


虚拟语气:
If the milk hadn't expired, I would have used it. 如果牛奶没有过期，我就会用它了。
I wish the contract hadn't expired. 我希望合同 hadn't expired.


被动语态:
The expiration date is printed on the package.  有效期印在包装上。
The contract was expired by the company.  合同被公司终止了。(注意：expiration 通常不以被动语态使用，这里用 terminate 更自然)


疑问句:
When is the expiration date?  有效期是什么时候？
What is the expiration date of this product?  这个产品的有效期是什么时候？

否定句:
The product does not have an expiration date.  该产品没有有效期。
The contract hasn't expired yet.  合同还没有到期。
