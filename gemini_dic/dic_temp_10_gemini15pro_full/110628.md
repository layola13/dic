limpidnesses:/lɪmˈpɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
透明度；清澈；明净
The state or quality of being limpid; clarity.
The limpidnesses of the Caribbean waters are breathtaking. 加勒比海水的清澈度令人叹为观止。
clarity, transparency, pellucidity
murkiness, opacity, cloudiness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From limpid + -ness.  Limpid from Latin limpidus, equivalent to limp- (variant stem of līquēre to be liquid, clear) + -idus -id.
