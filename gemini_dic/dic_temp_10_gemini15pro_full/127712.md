neglect:/nɪˈɡlɛkt/ | n. vt. | 忽视，忽略；疏忽；玩忽职守 |  The state of being neglected. / To fail to care for properly. | The garden had fallen into a state of neglect. 花园疏于管理了。/  She neglected to mention that she was married. 她忘了提及她已婚。| synonyms: negligence, carelessness, inattention, omission | antonyms: attention, care, diligence, heed | noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *neglegere*, from *nec legere* "not to gather, not to choose," from *nec* "not" + *legere* "to gather, choose, read."
