gelation:/dʒəˈleɪʃən/
n.
凝胶化，胶凝
The process of forming a gel.
The gelation of the mixture occurred after it cooled.  这种混合物冷却后发生了凝胶化。
solidification, coagulation
melting, liquefaction
noun
