melodramatic:/ˌmelədrəˈmætɪk/| adj.|夸张的，感情过分渲染的|Exaggeratedly emotional or sentimental; characteristic of melodrama, often involving sensationalized plots and stereotypical characters.|例句：Her reaction was so melodramatic, bursting into tears over a spilled cup of coffee. 她的反应太夸张了，只因洒了一杯咖啡就泪流满面。

例句：The soap opera was full of melodramatic plot twists and over-the-top acting.  这部肥皂剧充满了戏剧性的情节转折和夸张的表演。|近义词：histrionic, theatrical, overwrought, exaggerated, sensationalized|反义词：understated, restrained, subtle, realistic|词性：形容词
