nonreliable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈlaɪəbl/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈlaɪəbl/| adj.|不可靠的|Not able to be relied or depended upon; not trustworthy.|The nonreliable witness's testimony was disregarded by the jury.  陪审团无视了不可靠证人的证词。|The nonreliable equipment caused delays in the project.  不可靠的设备导致项目延误。|近义词：untrustworthy, undependable, unreliable|反义词：reliable, trustworthy, dependable|词性:形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reliable" (from rely + -able).  "Rely" ultimately comes from the Old French "relier" meaning "to rally, bring together."
