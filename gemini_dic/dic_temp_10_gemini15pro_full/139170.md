obituaries:/əˈbɪtʃuəriːz/
noun
讣告；讣闻
a notice of a death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person.
The newspaper published obituaries of the prominent citizens who had passed away that year. 那家报纸刊登了当年去世的杰出市民的讣告。
synonyms:death notice, obituary notice, funeral notice, announcement of death
antonyms:birth announcement
