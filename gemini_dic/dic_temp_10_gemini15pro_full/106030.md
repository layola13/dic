knebelite:/ˈnɛbəˌlaɪt, ˈniːb-/| n. |铁橄榄石|A mineral, manganese iron silicate, (Mn, Fe)2SiO4, belonging to the olivine group.|Knebelite is typically found in metamorphic rocks. 铁橄榄石通常发现于变质岩中。|Synonyms: fayalite, olivine|Antonyms: N/A|noun
