nonascendently:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndəntli/
| adv. | 非升序地 |  Not in ascending order; in a way that does not increase in value or importance.  From "non-" (not) + "ascendent" (rising) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). | The numbers were arranged nonascendently, from 10 to 1.  这些数字按非升序排列，从 10 到 1。| synonym: descendingly | antonym: ascendently | 词性: 副词
