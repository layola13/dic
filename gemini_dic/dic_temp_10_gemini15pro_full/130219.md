non-separable:/ˌnɒnˈsɛp(ə)rəb(ə)l/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛp(ə)rəb(ə)l/| adj.|不可分的，不可分割的|Not able to be separated or divided.|例句：This is a non-separable phrasal verb. 这是一个不可分的短语动词。|近义词：inseparable, indivisible|反义词: separable, divisible|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + separable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "separable" comes from the Late Latin word "separabilis," meaning "that can be separated."
