meiotic:/maɪˈɒtɪk/ /miˈɑːtɪk/ |adj.|减数分裂的|Relating to meiosis, a type of cell division that results in four daughter cells each with half the number of chromosomes of the parent cell.|Meiotic division is essential for sexual reproduction.|Synonyms: reductional (referring to chromosome number)|Antonyms: mitotic|词性:形容词

meiosis:/maɪˈoʊsɪs/ /miˈoʊsɪs/|n.|减数分裂|A type of cell division that results in four daughter cells each with half the number of chromosomes of the parent cell, as in the production of gametes and plant spores.|Meiosis is crucial for genetic diversity.|Synonyms: reduction division|Antonyms: mitosis|词性:名词
