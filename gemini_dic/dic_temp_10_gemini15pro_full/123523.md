monotonous:/məˈnɑːtənəs/ | adj. | 单调的，单一的，枯燥的 | Lacking in variety and interest; tediously uniform. | The speaker's monotonous voice made it difficult to stay awake. 演讲者单调的声音让人难以保持清醒。| The long drive across the desert was monotonous.  开车穿越沙漠的长途旅行很枯燥。 | 近义词: tedious, boring, dull, repetitive, unvaried | 反义词: interesting, exciting, varied, stimulating | 词性: adjective
词源: From French monotone, from Greek monotonos, from mono- (one) + tonos (tone).
