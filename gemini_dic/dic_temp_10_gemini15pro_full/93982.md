impactor:/ɪmˈpæktər/| n.|撞击器，冲击器|An object which strikes another, causing an impact.  It often refers to a celestial body that collides with a planet or moon.|例句：The impactor left a large crater on the surface of the moon.  撞击器在月球表面留下了一个大陨石坑。

例句：Scientists study impactors to understand the formation of the solar system. 科学家研究撞击器以了解太阳系的形成。|近义词：collider, striker|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology->
From impact + -or. Ultimately from the Latin word impingere, meaning "to push against."
