cannot:/ˈkænɒt/, /ˈkæˌnɑːt/ | modal verb | 不能，不可能 | Expresses inability or impossibility.  It is the negative form of "can". | I cannot fly. 我不能飞。| 近义词: unable to, may not, must not | 反义词: can, able to | verb
