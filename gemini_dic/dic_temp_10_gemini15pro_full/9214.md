antioxygenation:/ˌæntiˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ |n.| 抗氧化，去氧 |The process of removing oxygen or preventing the addition of oxygen. This term is less commonly used than "antioxidation".|  The researcher studied the antioxygenation of the compound in a nitrogen atmosphere. 研究人员在氮气环境下研究了该化合物的抗氧化作用。|  Antioxygenation is crucial in preserving certain food products. 抗氧化对于保存某些食品至关重要。 |Synonyms: deoxygenation, antioxidation| Antonyms: oxidation, oxygenation| noun
