hierogrammatic:/ˌhaɪərəɡrəˈmætɪk/
| adj. |圣书体的，象形文字的| Relating to or resembling hieroglyphics; expressed by hieroglyphs. |The ancient Egyptian civilization left behind a wealth of hierogrammatic inscriptions. 古埃及文明留下了大量的圣书体铭文。|  The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher the hierogrammatic language of the ancient Egyptians. 罗塞塔石碑帮助学者破译了古埃及人的圣书体文字。 |近义词：hieroglyphic, pictographic|反义词：alphabetic|词性: 形容词

hierogram:/ˈhaɪərəɡræm/
| n. |圣像，圣书体文字|A sacred symbol or hieroglyph. |The archaeologists discovered a new hierogram on the wall of the tomb. 考古学家在墓墙上发现了一个新的圣像。|  Each hierogram represented a different concept or sound in the ancient Egyptian language. 在古埃及语中，每个圣像代表不同的概念或声音。|近义词：hieroglyph, pictogram|反义词：letter, alphabet character|词性: 名词
