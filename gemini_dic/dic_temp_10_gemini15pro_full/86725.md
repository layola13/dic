helminthologist:/ˌhɛlmɪnˈθɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 寄生虫学家 | A scientist who specializes in the study of parasitic worms (helminths). |  A helminthologist studies the life cycle, distribution, and physiology of parasitic worms.  寄生虫学家研究寄生虫的生命周期、分布和生理学。

| Synonyms: parasitologist | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
