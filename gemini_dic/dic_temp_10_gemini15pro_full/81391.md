grass:/ɡræs/ /ɡrɑːs/| n. |草，草地，草坪|Green grass covered the field.  绿色的草覆盖着田野。|例句：The cows are grazing in the grass. 奶牛正在草地上吃草。|近义词：lawn, turf, herbage|反义词: concrete, pavement |noun

grass:/ɡræs/ /ɡrɑːs/| vt. |用草覆盖，使长满草|They grassed the front yard. 他们在前院铺了草坪。|例句：  He grassed the bare patch in the garden. 他在花园里光秃秃的地方种上了草。|近义词：sod, turf|反义词: unearth, expose |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *græs* "grass, herb, plant," from Proto-Germanic *grasan* (source also of Old Frisian *gres*, Old Saxon *gras*, Old High German *gras*, German *Gras*, Old Norse *gras*, Gothic *gras*), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrō-/*ghrē- "to grow, become green."
