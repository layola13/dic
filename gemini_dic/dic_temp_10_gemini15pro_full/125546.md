multistemmed:/ˌmʌltiˈstemd/
| adj. |多茎的；多杆的|Having multiple stems or trunks.  A plant that grows with several stems rather than a single trunk.|The multistemmed tree provided ample shade.|近义词：many-stemmed, multi-trunked|反义词: single-stemmed, single-trunked|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many") + "stemmed" (from "stem," referring to the main stalk of a plant).
