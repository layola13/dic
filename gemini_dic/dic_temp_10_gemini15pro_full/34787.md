climatologies: /ˌklaɪməˈtɑːlədʒiz/ | n. |气候学；气候志| The study of climates, including the causes, variations, and distributions of long-term weather patterns. It encompasses the statistical analysis of weather data over extended periods, typically 30 years or more, to identify trends, averages, and extremes.  It also considers the influence of factors like latitude, altitude, ocean currents, and landmasses on climate. |  Researchers are using climatologies to predict future climate change. 研究人员正在使用气候学来预测未来的气候变化。 |  Climatic records, climate data, climate studies | meteorological records (气象记录), weather data (天气数据)| noun
