indefectible:/ˌɪndɪˈfɛktɪbl/
adjective
完美的；无瑕的；不会犯错的
Not liable to fail; unfailing.
例句：His indefectible memory for detail. 他对细节的记忆力完美无缺。

例句：An indefectible argument. 一个无懈可击的论点。

近义词：perfect, flawless, impeccable, unerring, faultless
反义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect, defective
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indefectibilis, from in- ‘not’ + defectibilis ‘liable to fail’ (from deficere ‘to fail, desert’).
