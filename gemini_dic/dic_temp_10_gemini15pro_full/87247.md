hereunto:/ˌhɪərʌnˈtuː/
adv.
至此，于此
to this document or matter; appended to this
例句：We have set our hands hereunto. 我们已在此签名。

例句：The parties hereunto agree to the following terms.  双方在此同意以下条款。
近义词：hereto, to this
反义词： (No direct antonym exists for this formal, legalistic term)
词性：adverb
