haggle:/ˈhæɡl/| v. |讨价还价；争论|To dispute or bargain persistently, especially over the price of something.|例句：They spent hours haggling over the price of the car. 他们在汽车价格上争论了好几个小时。

例句：I managed to haggle the price down by 10%. 我设法把价格砍掉了10%。|近义词：bargain, negotiate, barter, dicker|反义词：agree, concede|词性:动词
