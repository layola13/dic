nonimmanent:/nɒnˈɪmənənt/
adjective
非内在的，外在的
Not indwelling, inherent, or intrinsic; external.
God, in most religions, is considered nonimmanent, existing outside of the physical world. 上帝在大多数宗教中被认为是非内在的，存在于物质世界之外。
The artist's inspiration was nonimmanent, coming from external sources rather than from within.  这位艺术家的灵感是非内在的，来自外部资源而不是来自内心。
近义词: external, extrinsic, transcendent
反义词: immanent, inherent, intrinsic
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + immanent.  "Immanent" comes from Late Latin "immanentem" (nominative immanens), present participle of "immanēre" "to remain in," from "in-" "in" + "manēre" "to stay."
