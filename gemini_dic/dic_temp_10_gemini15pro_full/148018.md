overripening:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpənɪŋ/ | n. | 过熟，过度成熟 |The process of a fruit or vegetable becoming too ripe, past its peak of flavor and texture, often resulting in spoilage.| The overripening of the bananas made them mushy and unappetizing. 香蕉过熟了，变得软烂不好吃。 | 近义词: overmaturity | 反义词: underripeness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "ripe" (Old English rīpe "ripe, mature," from Proto-Germanic *ripaz "ripe," from Proto-Indo-European *rei- "to scratch, tear, cut") + "-en" (suffix forming verbs) + "-ing" (suffix forming present participles and gerunds, used here to create a noun of action).
