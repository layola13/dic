paralleling:/ˈpærəˌlɛlɪŋ/
verb|平行；与…并行；比得上；相比|Connecting or operating components in a parallel configuration; also, being similar or analogous to something else.|The electrician was paralleling the two generators to increase power output.  电工正在将两台发电机并联以提高功率输出。|Synonyms: matching, equaling, comparing, juxtaposing|Antonyms: diverging, contrasting|v.

paralleling:/ˈpærəˌlɛlɪŋ/
noun|并联；平行；对比|The act of connecting or operating components in a parallel configuration; a comparison or similarity.|The paralleling of the two circuits caused a surge in power.  两个电路的并联导致了功率的激增。|Synonyms: juxtaposition, comparison, matching|Antonyms: divergence, contrast|n.
