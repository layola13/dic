negotiatrix: /nɪˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪtrɪks/
noun
女谈判者；女交涉者
A woman who negotiates, especially in business or political matters.
例句：The skilled negotiatrix secured a favorable deal for her company.  这位老练的女谈判代表为她的公司争取到了一笔有利的交易。

例句：The negotiatrix worked tirelessly to resolve the conflict between the two parties. 女谈判代表不知疲倦地工作，以解决双方之间的冲突。

近义词：negotiator, mediator, intermediary
反义词：opponent, adversary
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *negotiatrix, feminine of negotiator (see negotiator).
