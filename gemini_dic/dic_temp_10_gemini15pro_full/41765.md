corruptible:/kəˈrʌptɪbl/| adj.|可腐败的，易腐败的，易堕落的|Susceptible to corruption, bribery, or decay.  Able to be morally corrupted.|The official was corruptible and accepted bribes.  这位官员容易腐败，接受贿赂。|Synonyms: dishonest, venal, unscrupulous, bribable, susceptible|Antonyms: honest, incorruptible, upright, principled|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corruptibilis*, from Latin corrumpere "to corrupt," from com- "together" + rumpere "to break."
