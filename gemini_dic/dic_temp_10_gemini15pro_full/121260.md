misevaluated:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljueɪtɪd/
|adj.|被错误评估的，被低估的|Incorrectly judged or assessed the value or worth of something.|The company's potential was misevaluated by investors, leading to a lower than expected IPO. 该公司的潜力被投资者错误评估，导致IPO低于预期。|The painting was misevaluated and sold for a fraction of its true worth. 这幅画被低估了，以其真正价值的一小部分售出。|近义词：underestimated, undervalued, misjudged|反义词：overestimated, overvalued|词性:形容词

词源:
mis- (prefix): wrongly, badly  错误地，糟糕地
evaluate (verb): form an idea of the amount, number, or value of something  评估
-ed (suffix):  (forming the past participle of verbs)  过去分词后缀
