empathized: /ˈɛmpəθaɪzd/ | v. |  同情，共情，感同身受 | To understand and share the feelings of another. |  She empathized with the refugees, having experienced a similar situation herself. 她对难民感同身受，因为她自己也经历过类似的情况。 | sympathize, understand, relate to |  be indifferent to, disregard | verb

empathize: /ˈɛmpəθaɪz/ | v. | 同情，共情，感同身受 | To understand and share the feelings of another. | I can empathize with your frustration. 我能理解你的沮丧。 | sympathize, understand, relate to |  be indifferent to, disregard | verb

词源: From empathy + -ize, from Greek empatheia, from em- 'in' + pathos 'feeling.'
