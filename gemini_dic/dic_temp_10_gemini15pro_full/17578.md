beausoleil:/ˌboʊsoˈleɪ/ (US), /ˌbəʊsɔːˈleɪ/ (UK)| n.|阳光明媚的地方，美好的地方|A place of sunshine; a beautiful place. Literally "beautiful sun" in French.|We spent our honeymoon in Beausoleil, a charming village on the French Riviera. 我们在法国里维埃拉一个迷人的村庄——阳光明媚的地方度过了蜜月。|近义词：paradise, haven, retreat|反义词：slum, wasteland|词性：名词

Etymology: From French *beau soleil*, literally "beautiful sun". *Beau* from Latin *bellus* "beautiful". *Soleil* from Latin *soliculus*, diminutive of *sol* "sun".
