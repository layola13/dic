pall:/pɔːl/ (UK) /pɔl/ (US)| n. v.| 棺罩；阴郁的气氛；失去兴趣；|A cloth spread over a coffin, hearse, or tomb. /  To lose its stimulating or interesting quality; to become dull. |The coffin was draped with a purple pall. 棺材上覆盖着紫色的棺罩。/  A pall of gloom settled over the town. 一股忧郁的气氛笼罩着整个城镇。/ The excitement of the trip began to pall. 旅行的兴奋感开始消退。|近义词： shroud, covering (n.);  bore, tire (v.) |反义词:brighten, enliven (v.)|词性:名词,动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *pal*, from Old English *pæll* "rich cloth or robe," from Latin *pallium* "cloak, coverlet."
