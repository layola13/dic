decoupled:/diːˈkʌpəld/
adjective
分离的，解耦的
separated, disconnected, or independent from something else.
The economy has become increasingly decoupled from manufacturing. 经济越来越与制造业脱钩。
The two systems are now completely decoupled. 这两个系统现在完全分离了。
Synonyms: separate, disconnected, independent, unlinked, autonomous
Antonyms: coupled, connected, linked, dependent, integrated
词性:形容词


decouple:/diːˈkʌpəl/
verb
使分离，使解耦
to separate or disconnect something from something else.
The government is trying to decouple the currency from the dollar. 政府正试图将货币与美元脱钩。
The new policy decouples pay from performance. 新政策将薪酬与绩效脱钩。
Synonyms: separate, disconnect, disengage, unlink, detach
Antonyms: couple, connect, link, join, integrate
词性:动词
