measure:/ˈmeʒər/ (US) /ˈmɛʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 测量，措施，程度，尺寸|An action or system for dealing with a situation or problem; the size, amount, or degree of something.|例句：The government has promised to take measures to tackle crime. 政府已承诺采取措施打击犯罪。|近义词：action, step, degree, size, amount|反义词:inaction, neglect|noun
measure:/ˈmeʒər/ (US) /ˈmɛʒə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 测量，估量，衡量|Ascertain the size, amount, or degree of something by using an instrument or device marked in standard units.|例句：They measured the length of the room. 他们测量了房间的长度。|近义词：calculate, assess, gauge, evaluate|反义词:guess, estimate|verb
