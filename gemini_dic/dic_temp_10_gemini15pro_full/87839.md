hiddenly:/ˈhɪdnli/| adv.|隐秘地，秘密地|In a concealed or secret manner; not openly.  It describes an action or state of being that is kept out of sight or done in a way that avoids detection.|He smiled hiddenly, revealing a secret only he knew. 他偷偷地笑了，流露出只有他自己知道的秘密。|She hiddenly slipped the note into his hand. 她偷偷地把纸条塞进他手里。|近义词：secretly, covertly, privately, stealthily|反义词：openly, publicly, visibly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hidden" + "-ly". "Hidden" comes from the Old English "hydan," meaning "to hide," of Germanic origin.  "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs from adjectives.
