administrant:/ədˈmɪnɪstrənt/
n.
管理者，行政人员
A person who administers something, especially business or governmental affairs.
The administrant of the program ensured everything ran smoothly. 该项目的管理者确保一切顺利进行。

The school administrant announced new policies. 学校的行政人员宣布了新的政策。
近义词:administrator, manager, director, executive, superintendent
反义词:subordinate, employee, staff


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *administrantem, present participle of administrare ("to manage, direct"), from ad- ("to, towards") + ministrare ("to serve, attend, supply"), from minister ("servant, attendant").

USAGE->
The administrant oversees the daily operations. 管理员负责日常运营。

The new administrant implemented several changes. 新的管理者实施了一些改变。


PHRASE->
the administrant of the estate 遗产管理人
the administrant of the fund 基金管理人
the administrant of the program 项目管理人
the administrant of the school 学校管理人员
the administrant of the hospital 医院管理人员
