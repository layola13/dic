chemosis:/kɪˈmoʊsɪs/
noun
结膜水肿
Swelling of the conjunctiva.
The patient presented with chemosis and eye pain after the allergic reaction.  病人过敏反应后出现结膜水肿和眼痛。
Synonyms: conjunctival edema, swelling
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a medical condition like this.  One might consider "normal conjunctiva" or "absence of swelling" as opposites in a descriptive sense.)
n.


chemosis:/kɪˈmoʊsɪs/
noun
化学性眼炎
(Rarely used in this sense) Inflammation of the conjunctiva caused by a chemical irritant.
The chemical spill resulted in chemosis. 化学品泄漏导致了化学性眼炎。(This usage is less common than the meaning of conjunctival edema.)
Synonyms: chemical conjunctivitis
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, similar to the primary definition)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "khēmē" meaning "gaping" + "-osis"  a suffix indicating a condition, process, or state.
