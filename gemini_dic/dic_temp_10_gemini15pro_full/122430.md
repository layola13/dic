moat:/moʊt/| n. |护城河，壕沟 |A deep, wide ditch surrounding a castle, fort, or town, typically filled with water and intended as a defense against attack. |例句：The castle was protected by a deep moat. 这座城堡受到一条深护城河的保护。

例句：The moat was once filled with crocodiles to deter intruders.  护城河里曾经满是鳄鱼，用来吓退入侵者。

|近义词：ditch, trench, canal, channel |反义词: bridge, pathway| noun
