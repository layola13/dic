magisterialness:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəlnəs/| n. |权威性，威严，专横 |The quality of being magisterial; authoritative and commanding presence or manner. |He spoke with a magisterialness that silenced the room. 他说话的权威性让整个房间鸦雀无声。|Synonyms: authority, authoritativeness, command, dominance, imperiousness, loftiness, pomp, pontificality|Antonyms: humility, modesty, meekness, subservience|noun
