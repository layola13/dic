overmoral:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəl/ | adj. | 过于道德的，道德观念过强的 | Excessively or rigidly moral; concerned with morals to an excessive degree.  Characterized by excessive adherence to moral principles, often to the point of being impractical or self-righteous. | He has an overmoral attitude towards sex. 他对性的态度过于道德化。 |  例句：Her overmoral stance on the issue alienated many potential supporters. 她在这个问题上过于道德的立场疏远了许多潜在的支持者。| 近义词：priggish, puritanical, moralistic | 反义词：amoral, immoral, unethical | 词性: 形容词
