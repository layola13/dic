calorification:/ˌkælərɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |热化；生热作用；卡路里含量 | The process of producing heat or increasing the amount of heat in something, or the measurement of the amount of heat produced.  |  The calorification of the food was carefully measured in the lab.  实验室里仔细测量了食物的热值。 | synonyms: heating, warming, thermogenesis | antonyms: cooling, refrigeration | noun
