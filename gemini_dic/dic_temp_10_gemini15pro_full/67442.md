extirpable:/ɪkˈstɜːrpəbl/ /ekˈstɜːrpəbl/
adjective
可根除的，可消灭的
Capable of being extirpated; capable of being completely destroyed or removed.
The doctor assured him that the tumor was extirpable. 医生向他保证肿瘤是可以根除的。

The weeds in my garden are proving surprisingly extirpable. 我花园里的杂草竟然很容易根除。

近义词:removable, eradicable, exterminable
反义词:ineradicable, incurable, permanent
词性:adjective
