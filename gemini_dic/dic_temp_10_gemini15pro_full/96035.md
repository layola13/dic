indebt:/ɪnˈdɛt/ | adj. | 负债的，受恩惠的 | Owing gratitude or recognition to another, especially for help or kindness; obliged.  | Example 1: I am deeply indebted to my parents for their support. 我深深地感激父母的支持。

Example 2:  He felt indebted to his mentor for guiding him through his career. 他对导师在他职业生涯中的指导心存感激。 | Synonym: obliged, beholden, grateful | Antonym: ungrateful, unappreciative | word property: adjective
