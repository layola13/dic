delegation: /ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 代表团，授权，委托 |  The act of entrusting a task or responsibility to another person or group.  A group of people appointed or elected to represent others. | The delegation from China arrived yesterday. 中国代表团昨天抵达。|  He delegated the task to his assistant. 他把任务委托给了他的助手。 | synonyms: commission, deputation, assignment, entrustment | antonyms: retention, micro-management | noun

delegation: /ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 委派；授权 | The act of giving control, authority, a job, a duty, etc. to someone else so that they can deal with it for you. | The manager gave her the delegation to hire new staff. 经理授权她招聘新员工。 | Effective delegation is a key management skill. 有效的委派是一项重要的管理技能。| synonyms: assignment, entrustment, empowerment | antonyms: micromanagement, retention | noun
