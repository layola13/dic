incompetent:/ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənt/ (UK) /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtənt/ (US)| adj.|无能的；不称职的|not having the ability to do something successfully; not meeting the required standards|He was fired for being incompetent. 他因为不称职而被解雇了。|inept, unskilled, incapable|competent, capable, skilled|adjective
