alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层，冲积土 | Clay, silt, sand, gravel, or similar detrital material deposited by running water.| The fertile alluvium of the Nile delta has supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河三角洲肥沃的冲积土已经支持农业数千年了。| Example: The river deposited a thick layer of alluvium on the floodplain. 这条河在泛滥平原上沉积了一层厚厚的冲积土。| Synonyms: sediment, deposit, silt | Antonyms: bedrock | Noun
