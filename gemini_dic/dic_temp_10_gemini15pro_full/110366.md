lightwood:/ˈlaɪtwʊd/| n.|轻木，松脂木，富含树脂的木材|Resin-rich wood, often from pine trees, that is easily ignited and burns brightly. It is often used as kindling.|例句：The camper used lightwood to start the fire. 露营者用松脂木生火。

例句：Lightwood is highly valued for its flammability. 松脂木因其易燃性而备受重视。|近义词：kindling, tinder, fatwood|反义词:greenwood|词性:noun
