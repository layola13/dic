backstop:/ˈbækstɒp/ (US) /ˈbækˌstɒp/ (UK)| n. v.|最后保障；防护网；支持；阻止；担任后捕手|A backstop is a measure taken to prevent something undesirable from happening.|The government introduced regulations as a backstop against financial instability. 政府引入了法规作为防止金融不稳定的最后保障。|Synonyms: safeguard, safety net, precaution, support, prevent|Antonyms:  risk, danger|noun

backstop:/ˈbækstɒp/ (US) /ˈbækˌstɒp/ (UK)|v.|支持；担任后捕手；阻止|To backstop is to support or reinforce something, or to act as a backstop.|The central bank backstopped the failing financial institutions. 中央银行支持了濒临倒闭的金融机构。|Synonyms: support, reinforce, underpin, protect|Antonyms: undermine, weaken|verb
