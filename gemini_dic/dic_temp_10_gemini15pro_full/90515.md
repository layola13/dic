huds:/hʌdz/| n.| 平视显示器（Heads-Up Displays 的缩写）|A transparent display that presents data without requiring users to look away from their usual viewpoints.|The pilot checked the huds for altitude and airspeed. 飞行员查看了平视显示器上的高度和空速。|Synonyms: HUD, heads-up display|Antonyms: N/A|noun
