emancipative:/ɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪv/
adjective
解放的，使人获得自由的
Relating to or promoting emancipation; setting free from legal, social, or political restrictions.
The emancipative power of education can transform lives and communities. 教育的解放力量可以改变生活和社区。
The 13th Amendment had an emancipative effect on enslaved people in the United States.  第十三修正案对美国被奴役的人民具有解放作用。
近义词:liberating, freeing, releasing, redemptive
反义词:oppressive, restrictive, enslaving, confining
词性:adjective

词源:
From emancipate + -ive.  Emancipate comes from Latin emancipare "to set free," from e- "out" + mancipare "to transfer ownership," from manus "hand" + capere "to take."
