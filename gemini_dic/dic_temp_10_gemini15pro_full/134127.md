nonimperatively:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɛrətɪvli/
adv.
非命令式地，非强制性地
In a manner that does not express a command or requirement.
The system suggests options nonimperatively, allowing users to choose their preferred course of action.  系统以非强制方式建议选项，允许用户选择他们喜欢的行动方案。
He phrased the request nonimperatively, making it sound more like a suggestion. 他用非命令式的语气表达了请求，使其听起来更像一个建议。
近义词: optionally, non-compulsorily
反义词: imperatively, mandatorily
词性: 副词
