overgrievous:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvəs/| adj.|过度悲伤的，过分沉痛的|Excessively grievous; causing or characterized by excessive grief or sorrow.|His overgrievous reaction to the news surprised everyone. 他对这个消息的过度悲伤反应让每个人都感到惊讶。| The loss of her pet was overgrievous for the young child. 对这个小孩来说，失去她的宠物让她过度悲伤。|近义词:excessively sorrowful, overly mournful, too distressing|反义词:joyful, happy, cheerful|词性:形容词
