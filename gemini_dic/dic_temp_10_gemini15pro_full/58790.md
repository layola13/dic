dunted:/ˈdʌntɪd/| adj.|畏惧的，胆怯的，气馁的|Made to feel less confident : intimidated or discouraged|例句：The sheer size of the task left her feeling somewhat daunted.  这项任务的艰巨性让她感到有些畏惧。

例句：He was undaunted by the challenge. 他毫不畏惧挑战。|近义词：intimidated, discouraged, disheartened, dismayed|反义词：undaunted, emboldened, encouraged|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dunten, from Old French *donter (Modern French dompter "to tame, subdue"), from Latin domitare "to tame," frequentative of domāre "to tame, subdue, domesticate."
