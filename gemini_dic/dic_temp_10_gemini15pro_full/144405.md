outstriving: /ˌaʊtˈstraɪvɪŋ/
verb
努力超越，胜过
Trying hard to do better than someone or something else.
He was always outstriving his classmates, determined to be the best. 他总是努力超越他的同学，决心做到最好。
The company is outstriving its competitors in the race for market share.  这家公司在争夺市场份额的竞争中力图胜过其竞争对手。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, excelling, exceeding
反义词: failing, lagging, underperforming
词性: Verb (present participle)

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + "strive" (verb meaning "to make great efforts to achieve or obtain something").

PHRASE->
Currently not commonly used in set phrases.  This word is typically used on its own.  However,  it can be combined with prepositions like "in" or "by":

outstriving in their efforts (在他们的努力中超越)
outstriving by a significant margin (以显著的优势超越)

It's more common to see phrases built around the synonyms of "outstrive", such as "outdo oneself in...", "surpass expectations", "excel in...", or "exceed limitations".
