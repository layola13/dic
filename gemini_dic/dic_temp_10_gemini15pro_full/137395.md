nontensile:/nɒnˈtɛnsaɪl/
|adj.|非张力的，无拉力的|Not capable of being stretched or drawn out; lacking tensile strength.|The rope was nontensile and snapped under pressure.  这根绳子没有拉力，在压力下断裂了。|Example: The material is nontensile and therefore unsuitable for use in a suspension bridge.  这种材料没有拉力，因此不适合用于吊桥。|Synonyms: inelastic, unyielding, inflexible|Antonyms: tensile, elastic, flexible|adjective
