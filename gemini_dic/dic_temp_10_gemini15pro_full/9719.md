antitypy:/ænˈtɪtɪpi/ | n. | 对照，对应，预表 |A figure or event in the New Testament seen as fulfilling one in the Old Testament.|  The Passover lamb is seen as an antitype of Christ. 逾越节的羔羊被视为基督的预表。 | synonym:  prefiguration, type, symbol | antonym: archetype, original | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antitypia, from Ancient Greek ἀντίτυπος (antítupos, “a copy, impression, figure, type”), from ἀντί (antí, “against, opposite, corresponding to”) + τύπος (túpos, “a blow, impression, figure, type”).
