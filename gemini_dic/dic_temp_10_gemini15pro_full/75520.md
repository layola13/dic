ftoc:/ˈɛfˌtɒk/ /ˈɛfˌtɑːk/| adj. n.|华氏到摄氏度的，华氏到摄氏度的转换|A ftoc conversion is used to change a temperature reading from Fahrenheit to Celsius.|Example: Use a ftoc calculator to convert 80°F to Celsius.  使用华氏到摄氏度的计算器将 80°F 转换为摄氏度。|近义词: Fahrenheit to Celsius conversion|反义词: ctof (Celsius to Fahrenheit)|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:  "ftoc" is a modern abbreviation formed by combining the first letters of "Fahrenheit" and "Celsius" (or "Centigrade," an older term for Celsius).  "Fahrenheit" is named after Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit, a German physicist who developed the Fahrenheit temperature scale. "Celsius" is named after Anders Celsius, a Swedish astronomer who developed the Celsius temperature scale.
