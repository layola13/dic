deblai:/dəˈbleɪ/ (US), /ˈdeɪbleɪ/ (UK)| n.| 废墟，瓦砾，清除的障碍物|The deblai from the demolition blocked the street.  拆除产生的瓦砾堵塞了街道。|Removal of earth or other material, especially from a site being excavated. The deblai was transported to a landfill. 这些废墟被运往垃圾填埋场。|近义词：rubble, debris, waste|反义词: construction, building materials|词性:noun
