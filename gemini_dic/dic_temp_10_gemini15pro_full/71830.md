fleetingly:/ˈfliːtɪŋli/ | adv. | 短暂地，飞快地 | Briefly; for a short time. | The thought crossed her mind fleetingly.  这个念头在她脑海中一闪而过。 | synonym: briefly, shortly, momentarily, transiently | antonym: enduringly, permanently, lastingly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From *fleet* (adj.) + *-ly*.  *Fleet* comes from Old English *flēot* "ship, vessel," later "act of flowing, stream, flood," then "swift, shallow," finally "quick, nimble."  The sense of "passing quickly" developed from the notion of something moving swiftly like a ship or a stream.
