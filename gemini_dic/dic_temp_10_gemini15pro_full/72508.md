fluorous:/ˈflʊərəs/| adj.|含氟的，氟化的|Relating to or rich in fluorine; resembling fluorine in its chemical properties.  Characterized by the presence of many C-F bonds.  This term is frequently used in the context of fluorous chemistry, which exploits the unique properties of fluorinated compounds for separation and catalysis.|Fluorous solvents are immiscible with both organic solvents and water. 含氟溶剂与有机溶剂和水都不互溶。

Fluorous tags can be used to simplify the purification of organic molecules. 含氟标签可用于简化有机分子的纯化。|近义词:fluorinated, perfluorinated|反义词:non-fluorinated, hydrocarbon|词性:形容词
