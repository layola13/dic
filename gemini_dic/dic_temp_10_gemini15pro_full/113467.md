lyra:/ˈlaɪrə/| n. |天琴座; 七弦琴|A small constellation in the northern hemisphere near Cygnus and Draco, containing the bright star Vega.  A lyre, a stringed instrument of the harp family.|例句：The constellation Lyra contains the star Vega. 天琴座包含织女星。

例句：He played a beautiful melody on his lyra. 他用他的七弦琴演奏了一曲优美的旋律。|近义词：lyre, harp|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
