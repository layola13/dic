foralite:/ˈfɔːrəlaɪt/ | n. |镁橄榄石|A magnesium-rich olivine mineral, (Mg,Fe)₂SiO₄, common in mafic and ultramafic igneous rocks and some metamorphic rocks.  |例句：Foralite is a common mineral in the Earth's mantle. 镁橄榄石是地幔中常见的矿物。

例句：The rock sample contained a significant amount of foralite.  该岩石样本中含有大量的镁橄榄石。|近义词：olivine|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
