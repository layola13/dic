altocumulus:/ˌæltoʊˈkjuːmjələs/ | n. | 高积云 | A type of middle-altitude cloud characterized by white or gray patches or layered sheets, often arranged in groups or lines.  These clouds are composed of water droplets and sometimes ice crystals. | The sky was filled with altocumulus clouds, hinting at the possibility of rain later.  天空布满了高积云，暗示着稍后可能会下雨。| Synonym:  mid-level clouds | Antonym:  clear sky | noun


altocumuli:/ˌæltoʊˈkjuːmjʊlaɪ/ | n. | 高积云 | The plural form of altocumulus.  Refers to multiple instances of these clouds. | We observed several formations of altocumuli during our flight.  我们在飞行中观察到了几处高积云。| Synonym:  mid-level cloud formations | Antonym: clear skies | noun
