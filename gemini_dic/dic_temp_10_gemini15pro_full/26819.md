cageynesses:/ˈkeɪdʒinəsɪz/
noun
谨慎；小心；警惕
The quality of being cagey, or reluctant to give information.
His cageyness about his past made her suspicious. 他对他过去的谨慎让她起了疑心。
Synonyms: reticence, evasiveness, secretiveness
Antonyms: openness, frankness, candor
n.


Etymology:
From cagey + -ness.  Cagey itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to the idea of being wary or "caged" in, unwilling to reveal oneself.
