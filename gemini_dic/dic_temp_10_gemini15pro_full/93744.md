immature:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊr/ /ˌɪməˈtʊr/| adj.|不成熟的，幼稚的|Not fully developed or grown; behaving in a way that is not appropriate for someone of their age.|例句：He's rather immature for his age.  他年纪不小了，但举止仍相当幼稚。

例句：The immature fruit is sour.  未成熟的果子是酸的。

近义词：childish, juvenile, undeveloped, green|反义词：mature, ripe, developed, grown-up|词性:形容词

immature:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊr/ /ˌɪməˈtʊr/| n.|未成熟的人或事物|A person or thing that is not fully developed.|例句：The market is flooded with immatures. 市场充斥着不成熟的产品。

例句：He acted like an immature. 他表现得像个小孩子。

近义词：child, adolescent, juvenile |反义词：adult, grown-up|词性:名词
