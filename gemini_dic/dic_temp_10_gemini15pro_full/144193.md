outsider:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdər/ | n. | 局外人；外行；非本团体成员 | Someone who is not accepted as a member of a society, group, or team. |  She felt like an outsider looking in on their close-knit group. 她感觉自己像个局外人，只能旁观他们关系紧密的群体。

The consultants were outsiders brought in to offer a fresh perspective.  这些顾问是从外部聘请的，目的是提供新的视角。 | 近义词: stranger, alien, foreigner, nonmember | 反义词: insider, member, native | 词性: 名词

outsiders:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪd ərz/ | n. | 局外人（outsider 的复数形式） | The plural form of outsider.  |  The outsiders were not privy to the group's inside jokes. 这些局外人不知道这个团体内部的笑话。

The club was known for excluding outsiders.  这个俱乐部以排斥外人而闻名。 | 近义词: strangers, aliens, foreigners, nonmembers | 反义词: insiders, members, natives | 词性: 名词
