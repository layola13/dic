blackberry:/ˈblækˌberi/ (US), /ˈblækbri/ (UK)| n.|黑莓；黑莓手机|A small, dark purple fruit that grows on a thorny bush. Also, a brand of smartphone.|例句：I picked some fresh blackberries from the garden. 我从花园里摘了一些新鲜的黑莓。

例句：My old Blackberry phone still works. 我的旧黑莓手机还能用。|近义词：bramble, (for the phone) smartphone|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
