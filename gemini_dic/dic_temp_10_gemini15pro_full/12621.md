assassinated:/əˈsæsɪneɪtɪd/
verb
被暗杀的
Killed by surprise attack for political or religious reasons.
John F. Kennedy was assassinated in 1963. 约翰·F·肯尼迪于1963年被暗杀。
President Lincoln was assassinated in Ford’s Theater. 林肯总统在福特剧院遇刺身亡。
synonyms: murdered, killed, slaughtered, executed
antonyms: saved, rescued, protected
v.
