decants:/dɪˈkænts/ | v.  n. | 倾析，滗；醒酒；倾析出的液体 | To carefully pour a liquid from one container to another, especially to separate it from sediment. Also, to expose wine to air to improve its flavor. |  He decanted the wine into a carafe. 他把酒倒进玻璃水瓶里。 | synonyms: pour off, siphon, rack | antonyms: mix, combine | verb

decants:/dɪˈkænts/ | n. | 倾析出的液体 | The liquid that has been decanted. | The decants from the wine were discarded. 醒酒后剩下的酒渣被丢弃了。 | synonyms:  dregs, sediment (referring to what's left behind) | antonyms:  mixture, solution (referring to the original combined liquid) | noun
