fakers:/ˈfeɪkərz/| n.|骗子，假货，冒牌货|A person who pretends to be someone or something they are not, or who creates a false impression.  A faker is essentially a deceiver who fabricates a persona or situation for personal gain or other motives.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying dishonesty and manipulation.|Those fakers online pretended to be experts in finance.  那些网上的骗子假装是金融专家。|imposters, charlatans, fraudsters, pretenders|genuine people, authentic individuals|noun
