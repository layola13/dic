half-shut:/ˈhɑːf ʃʌt/ | adj. | 半闭的，半开的 | Partially closed, not fully open or shut. |  The cat sat with its eyes half-shut, enjoying the sun. 猫半闭着眼睛坐着，享受着阳光。

The shop was half-shut, with only a few lights on.  这家商店半开着，只亮着几盏灯。 | near synonyms:  ajar, partly open, partially closed | antonyms: wide open, fully open, completely closed | adjective
