equip:/ɪˈkwɪp/ (US) /ɪˈkwɪp/ (UK)| vt.|装备，配备|to provide someone or something with the necessary tools, skills, or knowledge|例句：The soldiers were equipped with the latest weapons. 士兵们配备了最新的武器。

例句：We equip our students with the skills they need to succeed. 我们为学生配备了他们成功所需的技能。|近义词：furnish, provide, supply, outfit|反义词:strip, deprive|verb
