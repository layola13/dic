charms:/tʃɑːrmz/| n.|魅力，魔力；护身符；小饰物|A quality that attracts or fascinates by pleasing, delighting, or arousing interest; a small ornament worn on a bracelet or necklace.|Her smile is one of her greatest charms. 她的笑容是她最大的魅力之一。|Synonyms: appeal, attraction, allure, magic, amulet, trinket|Antonyms: repulsion, distaste, ugliness|Noun

charm:/tʃɑːrm/| v.|使着迷，吸引；施魔法|To attract or fascinate by pleasing, delighting, or arousing interest; to protect by or as if by magic.|He was charmed by her beauty and wit. 他被她的美貌和智慧所吸引。|Synonyms: enchant, captivate, bewitch, fascinate|Antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant|Verb
