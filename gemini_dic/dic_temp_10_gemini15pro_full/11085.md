archaism:/ˈɑːrkeɪɪzəm/ | n. | 古语；古体；过时的东西 |  A word, expression, or other element that is considered old-fashioned and no longer in common use.  | Example 1: "Thou" is an archaism rarely used in modern English.  汝在现代英语中很少使用。

Example 2:  The author's use of archaisms gave the novel a historical feel. 作者使用古语使小说具有历史感。 | Synonym:  obsolete word, antiquated word, anachronism | Antonym: neologism, modernism | noun
