catelectrode:/ˈkætɪlɛkˌtroʊd/| n.|阴极|The negative electrode of an electrolytic cell, to which cations are attracted.|The catelectrode in this battery is made of zinc.  这个电池的阴极是由锌制成的。|Synonyms: cathode, negative electrode|Antonyms: anode, positive electrode|noun
