dice:/daɪs/ (plural dice) /daɪs/| n. |骰子；掷骰子游戏|A small cube with each side marked with a different number of spots (one to six), used in games of chance.  The act of throwing dice.|She rolled the dice and got a six. 她掷骰子掷到了六点。|近义词：cube, die (singular)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


dice:/daɪs/ (plural dice) /daɪs/| v. |切成小方块|To cut food into small cubes.|He diced the onions for the stew. 他把洋葱切成丁，准备炖菜。|近义词：chop, cube, mince|反义词: N/A|词性:verb


Etymology: From Old French *dé, from Latin datum "something given," past participle of dare "to give."  Originally referring to the act of throwing dice, later to the dice themselves.
