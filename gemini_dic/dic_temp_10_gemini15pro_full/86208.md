heathenize:/ˈhiːðənaɪz/
verb
使异教化；使不信教；使世俗化
To make heathen; to convert to heathenism; to cause to abandon one's religion.
The missionaries feared the natives would heathenize the new converts.  传教士担心当地人会使新皈依者异教化。
The increasing secularization of society is seen by some as heathenizing the culture. 一些人认为社会日益世俗化正在使文化异教化。
近义词: paganize, secularize
反义词: Christianize, evangelize
词性: v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From heathen + -ize.  源自 heathen（异教徒） + -ize（使...化）。
