overbrutalizing: /ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|过度残暴化，过度野蛮化|The act of brutalizing something or someone excessively.|The dictator was accused of overbrutalizing the protesting citizens.  独裁者被指控过度残暴地对待抗议的公民。|
|近义词：overpowering, tyrannizing, oppressing|反义词：pacifying, appeasing, moderating|Verb|



overbrutalizing: /ˌoʊvərˈbruːtəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|adj.|过度残暴的，过度野蛮的|Characterized by excessive brutality.|The overbrutalizing regime was eventually overthrown.  过度残暴的政权最终被推翻。|
|近义词：excessively brutal, overly harsh, tyrannical|反义词：peaceful, gentle, moderate|Adjective|
