counter-secure:/ˌkaʊntər-səˈkjʊr/
adjective
反安全的，对抗安全的
Designed or intended to counteract security measures.  This term describes actions or systems that attempt to bypass or neutralize security measures.
The hackers developed a counter-secure system to bypass the firewall. 黑客开发了一个反安全系统来绕过防火墙。
Near Synonyms: insecure, vulnerable, exposed
Antonyms: secure, protected, safe
词性: adjective
