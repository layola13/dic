advisership: /ədˈvaɪzərʃɪp/
n.
顾问的职位；顾问身份
The position or role of being an advisor.
She accepted an advisership with the World Bank. 她接受了世界银行的顾问职位。

He held an advisership to the government on economic policy. 他担任政府经济政策顾问。

近义词: advisory position, consultant role, mentorship
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider)  client, advisee
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From advisor + -ship.  "Advisor" comes from the Old French "aviser" meaning "to consider," ultimately from Latin "ad" (to) + "visum" (seen).  "-ship" is a suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
