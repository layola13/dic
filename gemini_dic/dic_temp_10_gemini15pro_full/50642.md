derisive:/dɪˈraɪsɪv/
adjective
嘲笑的，嘲弄的
Expressing or causing contemptuous ridicule or mockery.
She gave a derisive laugh. 她发出一声嘲笑。
synonyms: mocking, scoffing, scornful, contemptuous, sneering
antonyms: respectful, admiring, complimentary
词性: adjective
