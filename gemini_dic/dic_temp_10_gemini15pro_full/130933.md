nonaspirating:/ˌnɒnˈæspəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |不送气的|Not aspirated; pronounced without an audible puff of breath.|The sound /p/ in "spin" is nonaspirating.|近义词:unaspirated|反义词:aspirated|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From non- + aspirating, from Latin aspiratus, past participle of aspirare "to breathe on," from ad- "to" + spirare "to breathe."
