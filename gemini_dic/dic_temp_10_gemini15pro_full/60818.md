electroplater:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪtər/ | n. | 电镀工，电镀器 | A person or machine that electroplates objects.  A person or device that coats a metal object with another metal using electrolysis. | The electroplater carefully dipped the piece of jewelry into the solution. 电镀工小心地将这件珠宝浸入溶液中。|  The factory uses an automated electroplater for efficiency.  这家工厂使用自动电镀机以提高效率。 | synonyms: plater | antonyms: N/A | noun

electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌpleɪt/ | v. | 电镀 | To coat (a metal object) with another metal by electrolysis. |  They electroplated the spoons with silver.  他们给勺子镀上了银。| The company electroplates car parts to prevent rust. 这家公司对汽车零件进行电镀以防止生锈。 | synonyms: plate, coat | antonyms: strip, remove | verb
