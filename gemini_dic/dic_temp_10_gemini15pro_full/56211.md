dodo:/ˈdoʊdoʊ/ (US), /ˈdəʊdəʊ/ (UK)| n.|渡渡鸟;  (喻) 灭绝的事物;  愚蠢的人|An extinct flightless bird that was endemic to the island of Mauritius. It was last seen in the 17th century.  一种已经灭绝的不会飞的鸟，是毛里求斯岛的特有物种。它最后一次被发现是在17世纪。|The dodo is a symbol of extinction. 渡渡鸟是灭绝的象征。|近义词:extinct creature, none (in its literal meaning)|反义词:none (as it is extinct)|词性:noun

dodo:/ˈdoʊdoʊ/ (US), /ˈdəʊdəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|愚蠢的; 过时的|Stupid or obsolete. 愚蠢的或过时的|His ideas about marketing are dodo. 他关于市场营销的想法已经过时了。|近义词:stupid, foolish, obsolete, outdated|反义词:intelligent, clever, modern, current|词性:adjective
