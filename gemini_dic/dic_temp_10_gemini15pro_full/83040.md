guppy:/ˈɡʌpi/| n. |孔雀鱼，百万鱼|A small, brightly colored freshwater fish, popular in aquariums.|例句：My guppies have had babies! 我的孔雀鱼生小鱼了！

例句：He keeps a tank full of guppies. 他养了一缸孔雀鱼。|近义词：fish, tropical fish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Robert John Lechmere Guppy (1836–1916), a British naturalist who discovered the species in Trinidad.
