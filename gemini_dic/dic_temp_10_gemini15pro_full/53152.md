diplopoda:/dɪˈplɒpədə/| n. |倍足纲;马陆|A class of arthropods characterized by having two pairs of legs on most body segments, commonly known as millipedes.|Millipedes, members of the class Diplopoda, play a vital role in decomposition within forest ecosystems. 马陆，作为倍足纲的成员，在森林生态系统的分解过程中起着至关重要的作用。

Diplopoda are often mistaken for centipedes, but they have two pairs of legs per body segment, unlike centipedes which have one.  马陆经常被误认为是蜈蚣，但它们每个体节有两对腿，而蜈蚣只有一对。|近义词:millipedes|反义词:None|词性:noun
