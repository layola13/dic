anglicize:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪz/ | v. | 使英国化；使英语化 | To make something more English in form or character. | The spelling of many foreign words has been anglicized. 许多外来词的拼写已经被英语化了。 |  Many immigrants anglicize their names to fit in better. 许多移民将他们的名字英语化以便更好地融入。 | 近义词: anglify, adapt, assimilate | 反义词: nativize, localize | verb
