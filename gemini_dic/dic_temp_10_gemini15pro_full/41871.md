coscripts:/ˈkoʊˌskrɪpts/| n.|共同撰稿人；合著者|People who write something together, such as a play, a book, or a screenplay, are coscripts.|Shakespeare and Fletcher were coscripts on the play "Two Noble Kinsmen". 莎士比亚和弗莱彻是戏剧《两位贵族亲戚》的共同编剧。|The two journalists were coscripts on the exposé. 这两位记者是这篇揭露性文章的合著者。|co-authors, collaborators, joint authors|antonyms: (This word doesn't have a direct opposite.  One might consider "sole author" or "ghostwriter" as contrasting concepts, but they are not true antonyms.)|noun
