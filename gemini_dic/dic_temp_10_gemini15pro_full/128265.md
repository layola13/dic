neuronet:/ˈnjʊərəʊnɛt/ /ˈnʊroʊnɛt/| n. |神经网络|A computational model inspired by the structure and function of the biological brain, consisting of interconnected nodes (neurons) organized in layers.  It processes information by passing signals through these connections and adjusting the weights of these connections to learn from data. |Example: The researcher trained a neuronet to recognize handwritten digits. 研究人员训练了一个神经网络来识别手写数字。

Example:  The neuronet was able to accurately predict stock market trends.  这个神经网络能够准确预测股票市场趋势。|近义词：neural network, artificial neural network|反义词: None|词性:noun

"-net" is a shortened form of "network."  The term signifies a network of interconnected neurons.
