asthmatical:/æsˈmætɪkəl/ | adj. | 喘息的；哮喘的 | Relating to or characteristic of asthma; affected with asthma. | He had an asthmatical cough. 他咳嗽得像哮喘一样。 | Synonyms: wheezy, short-winded, breathless | Antonyms: healthy, well | adjective

asthmatic: /æsˈmætɪk/ | adj. n. | 哮喘的；哮喘患者 | Relating to, or suffering from asthma.  A person who suffers from asthma. |  She's asthmatic and has to use an inhaler. 她患有哮喘，必须使用吸入器。 The clinic specializes in treating asthmatics. 这家诊所专门治疗哮喘患者。 | Synonyms: wheezy, short-winded, breathless | Antonyms: healthy, well | adjective/noun
