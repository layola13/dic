mantis:/ˈmæntɪs/ (US), /ˈmæntɪs/ (UK)| n. |螳螂|A predatory insect with a long body, large eyes, and front legs adapted for grasping prey.  It is known for its characteristic praying posture.|例句：The mantis patiently waited for its prey. 螳螂耐心地等待着它的猎物。

例句：A praying mantis is a fascinating insect. 螳螂是一种迷人的昆虫。


近义词：mantid|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
