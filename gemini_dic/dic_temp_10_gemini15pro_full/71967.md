flimflammed:/flɪmˈflæmd/
verb
欺骗，诈骗
to deceive or trick someone, especially in order to get money from them
He flimflammed me out of $20. 他骗了我20美元。
synonyms: swindle, cheat, defraud, trick, con
antonyms: be honest, be truthful
