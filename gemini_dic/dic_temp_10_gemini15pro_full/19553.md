bifurcating:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |分叉的，分歧的|Dividing into two branches or parts.|The bifurcating road led to two different towns. 分叉的道路通向两个不同的城镇。|近义词：forking, branching, dividing|反义词：merging, joining, uniting|词性:形容词
bifurcate:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪt/| v. |分叉，分歧|To divide into two branches or parts.|The river bifurcates a few miles downstream. 这条河在下游几英里处分叉了。|近义词：fork, branch, divide|反义词：merge, join, unite|词性:动词
bifurcation:/ˌbaɪfərˈkeɪʃən/| n. |分叉，分歧|The point or action of dividing into two branches or parts.|The bifurcation of the company into two separate entities was a strategic move. 公司分成两个独立实体的举动是一项战略举措。|近义词：fork, branch, division|反义词：merger, union, junction|词性:名词
