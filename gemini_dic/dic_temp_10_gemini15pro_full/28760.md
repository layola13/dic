carnival: /ˈkɑːrnɪvl/ (US) /ˈkɑːnɪvl/ (UK)| n. |嘉年华，狂欢节|A period of revelry, typically associated with Shrove Tuesday, usually involving processions, music, dancing, and colourful costumes. It can also refer to a travelling amusement show or fair. |例句：The carnival was a riot of color and sound. 嘉年华充满了色彩和声音。

例句：We went to the carnival and rode the Ferris wheel. 我们去了嘉年华，坐了摩天轮。

近义词：festival, celebration, fete, fair|反义词:wake, funeral|词性:noun
