intercausative:/ˌɪntərˈkɔːzətɪv/
adjective
相互致使的，相互引起的
Relating to or involving mutual causation; characterized by reciprocal or interactive cause and effect.
The intercausative relationship between poverty and lack of education is a complex issue. 贫困和缺乏教育之间的相互因果关系是一个复杂的问题。
The researchers explored the intercausative link between stress and illness. 研究人员探讨了压力和疾病之间的相互因果关系。
近义词: reciprocal, interactive, interdependent
反义词: independent, unrelated, uncorrelated
词性: adjective
