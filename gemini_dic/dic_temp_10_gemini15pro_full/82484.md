grunginess:/ˈɡrʌndʒɪnəs/| n.|肮脏，邋遢， grunge风格|The grunginess of the apartment repelled potential tenants. 公寓的脏乱吓跑了潜在的租户。|近义词：dirtiness, grime, filth, squalor|反义词:cleanliness, tidiness, neatness|词性:名词
Etymology: From grunge + -iness.  Grunge originated in the late 1980s as a term for a style of rock music and the associated fashion.  It's believed to be a shortening and alteration of "grimy" and "grubby."
