grease-heel:/ˈɡriːsˌhiːl/ /ˈɡrisˌhil/
| n.|溜须拍马的人，马屁精|A person who acts in a servile or overly flattering manner, especially to gain an advantage.  A fawning sycophant.|He's such a grease-heel, always trying to get on the boss's good side. 他真是个马屁精，总是想拍老板的马屁。|Synonyms: sycophant, flatterer, yes-man, brown-noser, toady |Antonyms: rebel, nonconformist|noun
