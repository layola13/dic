overarch:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrtʃ/| v. n.| 形成拱状覆盖；支配；包含 | to form an arch over something; to dominate or embrace something comprehensively |  A large tree overarched the path. 一棵大树覆盖在小路上。| synonyms: span, cover, dominate, encompass | antonyms: be subordinate to, be subject to | verb

overarch:/ˈoʊvərˌɑːrtʃ/| n. | 拱状结构；总体结构；支配地位| an overarching structure or influence | The overarching theme of the conference was sustainability. 会议的首要主题是可持续性。 | synonyms: framework, structure, dominance | antonyms: subordination, subjection | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + arch (n. or v.).  "Over-" signifies "above" or "across." "Arch" refers to a curved structure.  The combined meaning relates to forming an arch above something or encompassing it.
