opiniator: /əˈpɪnɪˌeɪtər/
noun
评论员；意见发表者
Someone who expresses an opinion, often publicly.
The political opiniator wrote a scathing column about the new policy.  这位政治评论员就这项新政策写了一篇尖刻的专栏文章。
pundit, commentator, analyst
critic, detractor
n.
