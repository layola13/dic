horsewhips:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪps/ (US), /ˈhɔːsˌwɪps/ (UK)| n. vt.|马鞭；鞭打|A whip designed for horses; to whip with a horsewhip.|He cracked the horsewhips to urge the horses on. 他挥舞马鞭，催促马匹前进。|The stable owner horsewhipped the unruly stallion. 马厩主人用马鞭抽打那匹不听话的种马。|近义词：whip, flog, lash|反义词: caress, soothe|词性:名词, 动词
