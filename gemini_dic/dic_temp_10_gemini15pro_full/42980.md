coveters: /ˈkʌvɪtərz/
| n. | 贪婪者；觊觎者 | People who strongly desire something that belongs to someone else. | The coveters eyed the expensive sports car with envy. 那些贪婪者羡慕地看着那辆昂贵的跑车。 |  近义词: desirers, wanters, enviers, yearners | 反义词: philanthropists, altruists | 词性: 名词(复数)
