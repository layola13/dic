copyrighter:/ˈkɑːpiraɪtər/ | n. |版权所有人；版权登记人| A person or company that holds the copyright to something. | Example 1: The copyrighter of the song refused to allow it to be used in the film.  这首歌的版权所有人拒绝允许它在电影中使用。

Example 2: The photographer is the copyrighter of all the images on her website.  这位摄影师是她网站上所有图片的版权所有人。 | Synonym: copyright holder, copyright owner | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps infringer - someone who violates copyright) | noun
