arcograph:/ˈɑːrkəɡræf/ | n. | 弧线规，弧形尺 | An instrument used for drawing arcs of large radius, especially in architectural drawing. |  The architect used an arcograph to draw the sweeping curve of the roof. 建筑师用弧线规画出了屋顶的弧线。| synonyms: curve ruler, circle template | antonyms: ruler (arguably, as a ruler primarily draws straight lines) | noun
