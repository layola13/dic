nonheritable:/ˌnɒnˈhɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可遗传的 |Not able to be inherited genetically. |  Example: Eye color is heritable, but learned behaviors are nonheritable.  眼睛颜色是可遗传的，但习得行为是不可遗传的。| Example:  A suntan is a nonheritable trait. 晒黑是一种不可遗传的特征。 | Synonyms: non-inheritable, acquired | Antonyms: heritable, genetic, inherited | adjective
