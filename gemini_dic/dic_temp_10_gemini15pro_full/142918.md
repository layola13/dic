out-shown:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊn/
verb
胜过，比…更出色
surpassed in showiness or ostentation; eclipsed.
Her elaborate costume out-shone everyone else's at the party. 她的精心制作的服装在派对上胜过了所有人。
The new model's performance out-shone the older version. 新模型的性能胜过旧版本。
Synonyms: outshone, eclipsed, surpassed, outdazzled, outstripped
Antonyms:  overshadowed, outdone
