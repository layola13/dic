emotioned:/ɪˈmoʊʃənd/ | adj. | 感动的，情绪激动的 | Affected by or expressing emotion, especially strong emotion. | She was deeply emotioned by the film. 她被这部电影深深地感动了。 |  I felt very emotioned when I heard the news. 当我听到这个消息时，我感到非常激动。 | 近义词: moved, touched, affected, stirred, sentimental | 反义词: unemotional, apathetic, indifferent | 词性: adjective

词源:  From the verb "emotion" + "-ed" (past participle suffix). The verb "emotion" comes from the French "émouvoir," from the Latin "ēmovēre," meaning "to move out, remove, stir up, agitate."
