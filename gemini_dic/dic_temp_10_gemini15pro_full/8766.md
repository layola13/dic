antidote:/ˈæntidəʊt/ (UK) /ˈæntidˌoʊt/ (US)| n.|解毒剂；矫正方法；对抗手段|A medicine taken or given to counteract a particular poison.|The doctor quickly administered an antidote to the snake venom. 医生迅速给予蛇毒的解毒剂。|Synonyms: remedy, cure, countermeasure, corrective|Antonyms: poison, toxin, venom|noun

antidotes:/ˈæntidəʊts/ (UK) /ˈæntidˌoʊts/ (US)| n.|解毒剂（antidote 的复数形式）|Plural form of antidote, referring to more than one substance that counteracts a poison or unwanted condition. |The hospital stocked various antidotes for different types of poisons.  这家医院储备了各种不同类型的毒药的解毒剂。|Synonyms: remedies, cures, countermeasures, correctives|Antonyms: poisons, toxins, venoms|noun
