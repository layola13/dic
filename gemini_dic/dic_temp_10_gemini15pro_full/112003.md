lonesomely:/ˈloʊnsəmli/ | adv. | 孤独地，寂寞地 | In a lonely manner; all alone.  Characterized by a feeling of sadness because one is alone. |  She walked lonesomely down the empty street. 她孤零零地走在空荡荡的街道上。|  The dog howled lonesomely in the night.  那只狗在夜里孤独地嚎叫。 | synonym: solitarily, forlornly, desolately | antonym: sociably, cheerfully, companionably | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From lonesome + -ly.  Lonesome itself comes from alone + some.
