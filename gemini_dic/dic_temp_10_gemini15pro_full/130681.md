nonaffective:/ˌnɒn əˈfɛktɪv/
adjective
非情感的；不涉及情感的
Not relating to, caused by, or expressing emotion or feeling.
Her response was nonaffective, devoid of any warmth or empathy. 她回应很冷淡，没有任何热情或同理心。
The study focused on the nonaffective components of decision-making.  这项研究专注于决策的非情感因素。
Synonyms: unemotional, impassive, apathetic, indifferent, detached
Antonyms: emotional, affective, passionate, sentimental
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + affective (relating to, caused by, or expressing emotion or feeling).  The word "affective" ultimately comes from the Latin "affectus," meaning "influence, passion, or disposition."
