bitten:/ˈbɪtn/| adj.|被咬的；被叮的|Past participle of "bite."  Having been bitten.  Used to describe something that has received bites. |The bitten apple lay discarded on the ground. 被咬过的苹果丢弃在地上。|近义词：chewed, gnawed|反义词：whole, untouched|词性:形容词
