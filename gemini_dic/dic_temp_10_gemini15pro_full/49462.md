deludedly:/dɪˈluːdɪdli/
adverb
中文翻译：受骗地；妄想地
英文解析：In a deluded manner; under the influence of a delusion.  Being deceived or misled by false beliefs or impressions.
例句：He deludedly believed he could fly. 他妄想地认为自己会飞。
例句：She deludedly invested all her money in a pyramid scheme. 她受骗地把所有钱都投资到一个金字塔骗局里。
近义词：erroneously, mistakenly, foolishly, wrongly
反义词：rationally, realistically, sensibly, wisely
词性：adverb

词源：From the adjective "deluded" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Deluded" comes from the verb "delude," which originates from the Latin word "deludere" meaning "to mock, deceive, or play false."
