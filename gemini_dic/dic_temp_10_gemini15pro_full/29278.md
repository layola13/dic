cast-aside:/kæst əˈsaɪd/ | adj. |被抛弃的，被丢弃的|Something that is cast aside has been discarded or rejected as unwanted. | The old toys were cast aside in favor of new ones.  旧玩具被丢弃，换成了新的。| 近义词: discarded, abandoned, rejected, dumped, forsaken | 反义词: cherished, treasured, valued, kept | 词性: 形容词


cast-aside:/ˈkæst əˌsaɪd/ | n. |被抛弃的人/物|A person or thing that has been discarded or rejected. |  The library sale was full of cast-asides from wealthier homes. 图书馆的义卖活动上到处都是富裕家庭丢弃的物品。| 近义词: outcast, reject, discard | 反义词: treasure, keeper | 词性: 名词


词源:  From the verb phrase "cast aside," meaning to throw something to the side or discard it.  "Cast" comes from Old Norse *kasta "to throw," and "aside" from Old English on sīdan "on the side."
