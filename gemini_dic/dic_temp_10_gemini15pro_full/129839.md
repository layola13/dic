non-homologous:/ˌnɑːn həˈmɑːləɡəs/
adjective
非同源的
Not having the same evolutionary origin but serving a similar function.  Derived from different ancestral sources.
The wings of a bat and the wings of a bird are non-homologous structures.
蝙蝠的翅膀和鸟的翅膀是非同源结构。
The bacterial flagellum is a non-homologous structure to the eukaryotic flagellum.
细菌鞭毛与真核生物鞭毛是非同源结构。
analogous, similar
