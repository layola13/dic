outsells:/ˌaʊtˈsɛlz/
verb
(比...更)畅销，(销量上)超过
sells more than
例句：Product A outsells product B by a large margin. 产品A的销量远远超过产品B。

例句：The new model outsells all its competitors.  这款新型号比所有竞争对手都更畅销。

近义词：surpasses, exceeds, tops
反义词：is outsold by,  lags behind
词性：v.
