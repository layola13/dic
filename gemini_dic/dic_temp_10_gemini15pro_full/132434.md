nondependents:/ˌnɑːndɪˈpɛndənts/ | n. | 非受抚养人；非扶养人 | People who are not financially dependent on someone else, such as children or elderly parents.  | The tax form asked for information about dependents and nondependents.  该税表要求提供有关受抚养人和非受抚养人的信息。|  Example: The company's health insurance plan covers employees and their nondependents. 公司的健康保险计划涵盖员工及其非受抚养人。 | Synonyms: independent adults, self-supporting individuals | Antonyms: dependents, children, elderly parents | noun
