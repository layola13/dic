losange: /lɒˈzɑːndʒ/ | n. | 菱形，斜方形 | A rhombus, especially one with two acute and two obtuse angles. | The tiles in the bathroom were arranged in a losange pattern. 浴室的瓷砖排列成菱形图案。|  The diamond on the ring was a perfect losange. 戒指上的钻石是完美的菱形。 | 近义词: rhombus, diamond | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From French losange, from Old French lauzange, probably from Vulgar Latin *lausa "flat stone", from Latin lausia.
