authorised:/ˈɔːθəraɪzd/ | adj. |授权的，批准的|Having official permission or approval.|The authorised biography of the president was finally published. 总统的授权传记终于出版了。| 近义词:approved, sanctioned, licensed, permitted | 反义词: unauthorised, forbidden, prohibited | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "authorise," which comes from Old French "autoriser" and ultimately from Latin "auctorizare," meaning "to give authority to."
