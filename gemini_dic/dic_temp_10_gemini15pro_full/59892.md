eddic:/ˈɛdɪk/
adjective
古代诗歌的; 古体诗的; 古斯堪的纳维亚诗歌的
Relating to or characteristic of Eddic poetry, a body of Old Norse poems from medieval Iceland.
The eddic poem tells of the creation of the world. 这首古斯堪的纳维亚诗歌讲述了世界的创造。
近义词:Norse, Icelandic, Skaldic
反义词:Modern
词性:adjective
