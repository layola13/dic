muckrakers:/ˈmʌkreɪkərz/
noun
揭丑记者；专门揭发丑闻的记者
Journalists who expose corruption and wrongdoing in business, government, or other institutions.
Example 1: The muckrakers of the early 20th century played a key role in bringing about social and political reform.  20世纪初的揭丑记者在推动社会和政治改革方面发挥了关键作用。
Example 2: Upton Sinclair's "The Jungle" is a classic example of muckraking journalism.  厄普顿·辛克莱的《屠场》是揭丑新闻的经典例子。
Synonym: investigative journalists, exposers, whistleblowers
Antonym: propagandists, apologists
