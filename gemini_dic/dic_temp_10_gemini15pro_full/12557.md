aspirator:/ˈæspəreɪtər/ (US), /əˈspaɪreɪtər/ (UK)| n.|抽吸器；吸气器；真空吸尘器|A device that uses suction to remove fluids or gases.  It can be a medical device, a laboratory tool, or a cleaning appliance.|

例句：The doctor used an aspirator to remove the fluid from the patient's lungs. 医生用抽吸器清除了病人肺部的积液。

例句：The chemist used an aspirator to create a vacuum in the flask. 化学家用抽吸器在烧瓶中制造真空。


近义词：suction device, vacuum pump, extractor|
反义词:injector|
词性:noun|

aspirator:/ˈæspəreɪtər/ (US), /əˈspaɪreɪtər/ (UK)| n.|喷雾器|A device that sprays a liquid, often used for applying pesticides or perfume.|

例句：She used an aspirator to spray insecticide on the plants. 她用喷雾器在植物上喷洒杀虫剂。

例句：He filled the aspirator with perfume and sprayed it into the air. 他把香水装进喷雾器，然后喷洒到空气中。


近义词：sprayer, atomizer|
反义词:N/A|
词性:noun|



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aspiratus*, past participle of *aspirare* "to breathe on, breathe to," from *ad-* "to" + *spirare* "to breathe."
