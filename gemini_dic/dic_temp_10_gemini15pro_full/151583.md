partheniad:/ˌpɑːrθəˈnɪæd/ | n. |处女诗；以处女生育为主题的诗；|A poem celebrating a virgin birth, especially that of Athena or Christ. |例句：Milton's "On the Morning of Christ's Nativity" is considered a partheniad.  弥尔顿的《论基督诞生之晨》被认为是一首处女诗。

例句：The poet composed a beautiful partheniad in honor of the goddess.  诗人创作了一首美丽的处女诗来歌颂女神。|近义词：virgin-birth poem, nativity poem |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
