affrightedly:/əˈfraɪtɪdli/
adverb|惊恐地，害怕地|In a manner expressing or indicating fright; fearfully.|She looked at him affrightedly, her eyes wide with terror. 她惊恐地看着他，眼里充满了恐惧。|He backed away affrightedly from the snarling dog.  他惊恐地从咆哮的狗身边退开。|近义词:fearfully, terrifiedly, scaredly|反义词:bravely, courageously|词性:副词
Etymology: From affrighted + -ly.  Affrighted comes from the verb affright, from Middle English *affrighten, from Old French *esfreer, from Frankish *frihtjan "to frighten," from Proto-Germanic *frihtjaną "to frighten."
