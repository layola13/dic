akub:/ˈɑːkʊb/| n.|阿库布长袍|A loose, embroidered, robe-like garment, traditionally worn by Igbo men in Nigeria. It is often white or cream-colored and features intricate embroidery, especially around the neckline and sleeves.|He wore a beautifully embroidered akub to the wedding. 他穿着一件绣花精美的阿库布长袍参加婚礼。|Near synonyms: agbada, gown|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps casual wear)|noun
