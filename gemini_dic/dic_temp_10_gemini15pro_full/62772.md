engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ /ɛnˈɡreɪv/| v. |雕刻，铭刻|To cut or carve (a design, inscription, etc.) into a hard surface, such as wood, stone, or metal.|The jeweler engraved our initials inside the rings. 珠宝商将我们的姓名首字母刻在戒指内侧。|
engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ /ɛnˈɡreɪv/| v. |铭记，牢记|To impress deeply on the memory or mind.|The experience was engraved on his memory. 那次经历深深地铭刻在他的记忆中。|近义词：carve, etch, inscribe, imprint|反义词: erase, efface|词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *engraver*, from en- "in" + graver "to carve". Ultimately from Latin *gravare* "to burden, make heavy".
