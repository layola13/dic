loofah:/ˈluːfə/ (US), /ˈluːfɑː/ (UK)| n.|丝瓜络，丝瓜，（也指用丝瓜络或其他材料制成的）沐浴球，洗澡巾|A rough sponge made from the dried fruit of a tropical plant, used for washing.|例句：I use a loofah every day in the shower. 我每天淋浴都用丝瓜络。

例句：She bought a new loofah at the market. 她在市场上买了一个新的丝瓜络。


|近义词：sponge, scrubber, washcloth|反义词:|词性:noun
