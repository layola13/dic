nonrelenting:/ˌnɒnrɪˈlɛntɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈlɛntɪŋ/| adj.|不屈不挠的，不松懈的，持续的|Not diminishing in intensity, strength, or effort; unyielding.|The unrelenting pressure of the media eventually forced the politician to resign.  媒体持续不断的压力最终迫使这位政治家辞职。|近义词: persistent, relentless, incessant, continuous,  unyielding|反义词: yielding, intermittent, lenient, merciful|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From non- + relenting. Relenting comes from the Old French *relentir, from re- + lent (“slow”).
