cerebellar:/ˌserəˈbelər/| adj.|小脑的|Relating to the cerebellum, a part of the brain responsible for coordinating voluntary movements, balance, and posture.|Example: Cerebellar ataxia is a condition affecting movement and coordination. 小脑性共济失调是一种影响运动和协调的疾病。|Example:  Damage to the cerebellar region can result in difficulties with balance. 对小脑区域的损伤会导致平衡困难。|近义词:None (This is a specific anatomical term)|反义词:None|词性:adjective

cerebellum:/ˌserəˈbeləm/| n.|小脑|The part of the brain at the back of the skull in vertebrates. Its function is to coordinate and regulate muscular activity.|Example: The cerebellum plays a vital role in motor control. 小脑在运动控制中起着至关重要的作用。|Example:  Alcohol can impair the function of the cerebellum. 酒精会损害小脑的功能。|近义词:None (This is a specific anatomical term)|反义词:None|词性:noun
