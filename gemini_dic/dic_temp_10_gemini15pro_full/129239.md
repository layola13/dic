noctograph:/ˈnɑːktəˌɡræf/ | n. | 夜写器；夜视仪 | An instrument for writing in the dark. A device that allows one to see or record images in low-light or dark conditions. |  He used a noctograph to jot down his thoughts in the middle of the night. 他用夜写器在半夜记下他的想法。

The spy used a noctograph to navigate the darkened building.  间谍使用夜视仪在黑暗的建筑物中导航。 | Synonym: night-writing instrument, night vision device | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "daylight camera" for the night vision device sense) | noun
