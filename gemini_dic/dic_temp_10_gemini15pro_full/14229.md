autocompleted:/ˌɔːtəʊkəmˈpliːtɪd/ | adj. |自动完成的|Having been completed automatically, typically by software.  The search bar autocompleted my query before I finished typing it.|例句：The form was autocompleted with my saved information.  表格用我保存的信息自动完成了。

例句：My email address was autocompleted when I started typing it into the login field. 当我开始在登录字段中输入我的电子邮件地址时，它自动完成了。|近义词：pre-filled, auto-filled, automatically completed|反义词: manually completed, incomplete |词性:形容词
