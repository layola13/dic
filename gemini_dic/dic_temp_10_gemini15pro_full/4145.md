air-tight:/ˈɛərtʌɪt/
adjective
密封的，不透气的
impermeable to air or nearly so; sealed so that no air can enter or escape
The container is air-tight to keep the food fresh.  这个容器是密封的，以保持食物新鲜。
The evidence against him is air-tight.  针对他的证据确凿无疑。
synonym:sealed, hermetic, impermeable, airtight
antonym:leaky, permeable, porous
词性:adjective
