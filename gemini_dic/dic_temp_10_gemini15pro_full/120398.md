ministerialist:/ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəlɪst/
n.
部长主义者；墨守成规的人；官僚主义者
a person who adheres rigidly to the official line or policies of a ministry or government department, often without regard for practical considerations or the needs of the people.
The ministerialist refused to consider any alternative solutions, sticking to the official policy.  这个部长主义者拒绝考虑任何替代方案，坚持官方政策。
The citizens were frustrated with the ministerialist's inflexible approach. 公民们对这位部长主义者的僵化态度感到沮丧。
Synonyms: bureaucrat, functionary, official
Antonyms: reformer, innovator, maverick
Etymology: From "ministerial" + "-ist"
