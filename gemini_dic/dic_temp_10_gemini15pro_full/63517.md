enunciability:/ɪˌnʌnsiəˈbɪləti/ |n.|可表达性；可陈述性|The enunciability of a concept depends on its clarity and precision.  一个概念的可表达性取决于它的清晰度和精确度。|例句：The enunciability of complex scientific ideas can be challenging for the general public.  对于普通大众来说，表达复杂的科学思想可能具有挑战性。

例句：The lawyer questioned the enunciability of the witness's testimony due to its vagueness.  由于证词含糊不清，律师质疑其可表达性。|近义词：articulability, expressiveness, utterability|反义词：ineffability, inarticulacy|词性:noun
