gothmog:/ˈɡɒθmɒɡ/ | n. | 哥斯莫格 | A fictional character, the name of several Orc commanders in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, most notably in *The Lord of the Rings*. | Gothmog, Lord of the Balrogs, appeared in The Silmarillion. 哥斯莫格，炎魔之王，出现在《精灵宝钻》中。 | Gothmog led the attack on Minas Tirith. 哥斯莫格率领了对米那斯提力斯的进攻。 | 近义词: Orc commander, lieutenant | 反义词: None (character-specific) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  Invented by J.R.R. Tolkien.  The elements of the name are of unclear meaning, possibly derived from Sindarin elements meaning "dread" and "oppression."  Tolkien himself offered no definitive explanation.
