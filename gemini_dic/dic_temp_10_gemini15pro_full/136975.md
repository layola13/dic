nonspheric:/nɒnˈsfɪərɪk/ /nɑːnˈsfɪrɪk/| adj.|非球形的|Not spherical; not having the shape of a sphere.|例句：The asteroid was nonspheric, more like a potato. 这颗小行星不是球形的，更像一个土豆。|例句：Nonspheric lenses are used to correct astigmatism. 非球面透镜用于矫正散光。|近义词：aspherical, non-spheroidal|反义词：spherical, globular|词性:形容词
Etymology->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".
spheric (adjective combining form):  From Late Latin sphericus, from Greek sphairikos "spherical," from sphaira "sphere, ball."
