oversanguine:/ˌoʊvərˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.| 过于乐观的，过于自信的| Excessively optimistic; too confident or hopeful.| He was oversanguine about the company's prospects. 他对公司的前景过于乐观。|  She was perhaps a little oversanguine about her chances of winning the lottery. 也许她对赢得彩票的机会有点过于乐观了。 | 近义词: optimistic, hopeful, confident, bullish | 反义词: pessimistic, realistic, cautious, dubious | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin oversanguineus "having too much blood," from Latin over- "over" + sanguineus "bloody, cheerful."  Originally related to the medieval theory of the four humors, where an excess of blood was believed to cause excessive optimism.
