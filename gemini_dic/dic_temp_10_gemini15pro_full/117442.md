meditant:/ˈmɛdɪtənt/
|adj.| 冥想的，沉思的 |Given to meditation; contemplative. |She sat in a meditant pose. 她以冥想的姿势坐着。|He has a meditant nature. 他有一种沉思的性格。|近义词: contemplative, reflective, pensive, meditative|反义词: unreflective, thoughtless, inattentive|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin meditans, present participle of meditarī (“to meditate”), from medius (“middle”) (whence also mean) + *hait- (“to consider”).
