elliptical:/ɪˈlɪptɪkəl/ (US), /iˈlɪptɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|椭圆的; 省略的|Having the shape of an ellipse or an oval.  Omitting a word or words that are easily understood from the context.|The planet follows an elliptical orbit. 这颗行星沿着椭圆轨道运行。|近义词：oval, oblong, elliptic|反义词:circular, round|形容词

elliptical:/ɪˈlɪptɪkəl/ (US), /iˈlɪptɪkl/ (UK)| n.|椭圆机|An exercise machine that simulates walking, running, or climbing stairs with a low-impact elliptical motion.|She uses the elliptical at the gym for cardio. 她在健身房用椭圆机做有氧运动。|近义词：cross-trainer|反义词:None|名词
