bipeltate:/baɪˈpɛlteɪt/
adj.
双盾形的
Having two shield-shaped parts, typically referring to a leaf attached to the petiole at a point within the blade margins.
The water lily has a bipeltate leaf.  睡莲的叶子是双盾形的。
Bipeltate leaves are an adaptation to aquatic life. 双盾形叶是对水生生活的适应。
近义词: peltate
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bi- (two) + peltatus (shield-shaped), past participle of peltare (to push, drive, protect with a shield), from pelta (small light shield).
