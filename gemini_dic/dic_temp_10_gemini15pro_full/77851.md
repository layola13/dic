genesee:/ˌdʒɛnəˈsiː/
n.
Genesee River/ Genesee County/ Genesee beer
杰纳西河/ 杰纳西县/ 杰纳西啤酒
The Genesee River flows north through western New York. 杰纳西河流经纽约州西部，向北流淌。
The Genesee Brewery is located in Rochester, New York. 杰纳西啤酒厂位于纽约州罗切斯特。
Genesee County is known for its agricultural production. 杰纳西县以其农业生产而闻名。
近义词: (For the river) waterway, stream  (For the county) region, district (For the beer) lager, ale
反义词:  None (Proper noun)
词性: noun
