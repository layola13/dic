PHRASE->
boss around:  颐指气使，指挥
boss someone into doing something:  强迫某人做某事
boss the show:  掌控全局，发号施令
like a boss:  像老板一样，很有气势，很成功
boss up:  振作起来，拿出气势

词源：From boss, ultimately from Middle Dutch baas "master."
