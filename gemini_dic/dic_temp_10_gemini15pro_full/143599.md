outinfluenced:/ˌaʊtˈɪnfluənst/
verb
超过…的影响力; 比...更有影响力
To influence someone or something more strongly than another influence.
The charismatic leader outinfluenced his rivals and secured the nomination.  这位充满魅力的领导人比他的对手更有影响力，从而获得了提名。
The marketing campaign outinfluenced negative press and boosted sales.  营销活动的影响力超过了负面新闻，并促进了销售。
近义词: surpass, eclipse, overshadow, dominate
反义词: be overshadowed by, be outweighed by
词性: verb
