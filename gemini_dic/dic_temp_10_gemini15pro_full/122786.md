mold:/məʊld/ (US), /məʊld/ (UK)| n. v. |霉菌; 模具; 类型, 塑造|A furry growth of minute fungal hyphae occurring typically in moist warm conditions, especially on food or other organic matter.  A hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot liquid material when it cools and hardens.  A distinctive and typical style, form, or character. |The bread had a thick layer of mold on it. 面包上有一层厚厚的霉菌。| 近义词: mildew, fungus, form, shape, cast, pattern | 反义词:  (For mold as fungus) sterility, cleanliness | 词性: 名词, 动词
