nixing:/ˈnɪksɪŋ/| v. |取消，否决，拒绝|To reject or stop something; to put an end to something. |例句：The boss nixed the proposal. 老板否决了这个提议。

例句：They nixed the idea of going to the beach because of the rain. 因为下雨，他们取消了去海滩的计划。

近义词：canceling, rejecting, vetoing, scrapping, dismissing|反义词:approving, accepting, permitting, allowing|词性:Verb
