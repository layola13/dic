bullying:/ˈbʊliɪŋ/ | n. |欺凌，霸行|The act of intimidating or harming someone weaker, often repeatedly.  It can be physical, verbal, or psychological. | He was a victim of bullying at school.  他在学校里是欺凌的受害者。| 近义词：harassment, intimidation, persecution, victimization | 反义词：support, encouragement, kindness, protection | 词性: 名词

词源: From bully + -ing.  From Middle Dutch *boele "lover, brother," probably ultimately from Middle Low German bule, bole "kinsman, brother." The sense development is from "lover" to "pimp, souteneur," then to "blusterer, braggart" (1560s), finally to "one habitually cruel to others" (1706).
