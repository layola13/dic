educability:/ˌɛdʒʊkəˈbɪləti/
n.
可教育性，可教性
The capacity or potential for being educated; the extent to which someone is capable of learning and developing intellectually.
Her educability was evident from a young age, quickly grasping complex concepts. 她很小的时候就表现出很强的可教育性，能很快掌握复杂的概念。

反义词: ineducability, unteachability, intractability
词性: noun
