contemn:/kənˈtɛm/| v. |蔑视，鄙视|To treat or regard with contempt; scorn.|He contemned their hypocrisy. 他鄙视他们的虚伪。|近义词：despise, scorn, disdain, spurn|反义词：admire, respect, esteem, venerate|动词

Etymology:
From Latin *contemnere*, from *com-* (intensive) + *temnere* "to slight, scorn".
