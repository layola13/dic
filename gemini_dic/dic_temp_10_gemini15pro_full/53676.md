disciplinableness:/ˈdɪsɪplɪnəbəlnəs/
n.
可训导性；可管教性；纪律性
The quality of being able to be disciplined or trained.
Her disciplinableness made her an ideal student for the strict music academy.  她的可训导性使她成为这所严格的音乐学院的理想学生。
近义词: trainability, docility, manageability
反义词: unruliness, indiscipline, waywardness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From discipline + -able + -ness.  Discipline from Latin *disciplīna learning, instruction, knowledge, from discō learn.
