gallic:/ˈɡælɪk/| adj.|高卢的；法国的；没食子酸的|Relating to Gaul or France, or derived from gallnuts.|The wine had a distinctly gallic flavor. 这葡萄酒有一种明显的法国风味。|Near synonyms: French, Gaulish|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider Germanic, Roman depending on context)|adjective

gallic:/ˈɡælɪk/| n.|没食子酸|Gallic acid is an organic acid found in gallnuts, sumac, witch hazel, tea leaves, oak bark, and other plants.|Gallic acid is used in the pharmaceutical industry. 没食子酸用于制药工业。|Near synonyms: 3,4,5-trihydroxybenzoic acid|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound)|noun
