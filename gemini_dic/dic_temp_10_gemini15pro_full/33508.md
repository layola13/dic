cicatricial:/ˌsɪkəˈtrɪʃəl/| adj.|瘢痕的；由瘢痕引起的|Relating to, or characterized by a scar or cicatrix.  Marked by or arising from the formation of scar tissue.|The cicatricial tissue was thick and inflexible. 瘢痕组织又厚又硬。|Synonyms: scarred, scar-forming|Antonyms: unscarred, healthy|adjective

cicatrix:/ˈsɪkətrɪks/| n.|瘢痕|A scar of a healed wound.|The surgeon pointed out the cicatrix from his previous surgery.  外科医生指出了他之前手术留下的疤痕。|Synonyms: scar, mark|Antonyms: healthy skin, unblemished skin|noun
