concentrator:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪtər/ (US)| n.|浓缩器；集中器；精选机；专注的人|A device or substance that concentrates something, especially minerals or liquids.  A person who is able to concentrate effectively.|The copper concentrator processed thousands of tons of ore daily. 这台铜精矿浓缩机每天处理数千吨矿石。|近义词：condenser, thickener, intensifier|反义词: diffuser, disperser|noun
