foody:/ˈfuːdi/| n.|美食家；吃货|A person who enjoys and cares about food, especially good food; a gourmet.|例句：He's a real foody and loves trying new restaurants. 他是个真正的美食家，喜欢尝试新的餐馆。

例句：She's such a foody that she plans her vacations around trying different cuisines. 她是个十足的吃货，她会围绕着尝试不同的菜肴来计划她的假期。|近义词：gourmet, gourmand, epicure|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) picky eater, indifferent to food|词性:noun
