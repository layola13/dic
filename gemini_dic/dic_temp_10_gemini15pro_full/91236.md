hydrochlorofluorocarbon:/ˌhaɪdrəˌklɔːrəˈflʊərəʊˌkɑːrbən/ /ˌhaɪdroʊˌklɔːrəˈflʊrəˌkɑːrbən/ | n. | 氢氯氟烃 | A compound containing hydrogen, chlorine, fluorine, and carbon atoms, used as refrigerants, solvents, and propellants. Many hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFCs) are known to deplete the ozone layer. |  Refrigerators used to contain hydrochlorofluorocarbons, but these are now being phased out.  冰箱曾经含有氢氯氟烃，但现在正逐步淘汰。| synonym: HCFC, freon | antonym:  (no direct antonym, but consider) ozone-friendly refrigerant | noun
