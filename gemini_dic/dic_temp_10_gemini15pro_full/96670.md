industriousnesses:/ɪnˌdʌstriəsnəsɪz/
n.
勤奋，勤勉
The state of being industrious; diligence.
Her industriousness and dedication led to her success. 她的勤奋和奉献精神使她走向成功。
近义词:diligence, assiduity, industry
反义词:laziness, idleness, indolence
词性:名词

词源:  From industrious + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *industria "diligence," from industrius "diligent, active," from indo "in, within" + struere "to build."
