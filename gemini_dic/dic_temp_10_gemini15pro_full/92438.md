hypophalangy:/ˌhaɪpəʊfəˈlændʒi/
n.
指骨发育不全;指骨短小症
A congenital condition characterized by abnormally short phalanges (finger or toe bones).
Hypophalangy can affect one or more digits and vary in severity. 指骨短小症可影响一个或多个手指或脚趾，严重程度各不相同。
Hypophalangy is a rare skeletal anomaly. 指骨短小症是一种罕见的骨骼异常。
brachydactyly, short fingers
arachnodactyly, long fingers
noun
