寒冷；冷淡；冷漠
The frostiness in her voice made it clear she was still angry. 她声音里的冷淡表明她还在生气。
There was a distinct frostiness in the air this morning. 今早空气中明显透着寒意。

The sudden frostiness in his demeanor surprised her. 他态度的突然冷淡让她吃惊。

近义词: coldness, iciness, frigidity, chilliness
反义词: warmth, cordiality, friendliness
词性: 名词

词源: From frosty + -ness.  "frosty" comes from Old English "forstiġ" meaning "frozen."
