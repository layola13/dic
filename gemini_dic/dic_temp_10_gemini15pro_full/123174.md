monks:/mʌŋks/
n.
僧侣；修道士
A member of a religious community of men typically living under vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience.
The monks chanted in the monastery. 僧侣们在修道院里诵经。
近义词：friars, brothers
反义词: laymen
词性: 名词
