amended:/əˈmɛndɪd/
adjective
修改的，修正的
changed or modified, especially for the better
The amended contract includes a clause about liability.  修改后的合同包含了关于责任的条款。
Near Synonyms: revised, altered, corrected, improved, modified
Antonyms: original, unchanged, uncorrected
词性: adjective
