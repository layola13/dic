belayer:/bɪˈleɪər/ | n. | 确保者；保护者 | A person who secures a climber by controlling the tension of the rope. |  The belayer kept a close watch on the climber.  确保者密切注视着攀登者。 |  spotter, securer | antagonist (in a broader sense, not a direct antonym) | noun

belay:/bɪˈleɪ/ | v. |  确保；保护（攀登者）；停止；固定 | To secure a climber by controlling the tension of the rope; to stop or make fast. |  He belayed his partner from the top of the cliff. 他在悬崖顶上保护他的同伴。 | secure, fasten, anchor | release, loosen | verb
