inattentively:/ˌɪnəˈtɛntɪvli/ | adv. |不专心地，心不在焉地 | in a manner that lacks attention; without paying attention | He listened inattentively to the lecture, his mind wandering elsewhere. 他心不在焉地听着讲座，思绪飘到了别处。 | The child played inattentively with his toys, more interested in watching the birds outside. 孩子心不在焉地玩着玩具，对窗外的小鸟更感兴趣。 | 近义词: absentmindedly, carelessly, distractedly, negligently | 反义词: attentively, carefully, diligently, thoughtfully | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + attentive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Attentive" comes from the Latin "attendere" (to attend to).
