horseshod:/ˈhɔːrsʃuːd/ /ˈhɔːrʃuːd/| adj.|马蹄形的；装有马蹄铁的|Shaped like a horseshoe; fitted with a horseshoe. |The horseshod archway added a touch of rustic charm. 马蹄形的拱门增添了一丝乡村的魅力。| The farrier horseshod the stallion.  蹄铁匠给种马钉了马蹄铁。 |近义词：U-shaped, semicircular |反义词:straight, linear |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hors-scōd, from hors "horse" + scōd "shod," past participle of scēon "to shoe." Literally, "horse-shoed."
