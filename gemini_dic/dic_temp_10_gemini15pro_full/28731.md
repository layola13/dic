carnality:/kɑːrˈnælɪti/
n.
肉慾，肉体性，淫荡
The state of being concerned with bodily pleasures and appetites, especially sexual ones.
The preacher warned against the dangers of carnality.  牧师警告人们肉慾的危险。
近义词：sensuality, lust, lewdness, lasciviousness
反义词：chastity, purity, abstinence, spirituality
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin carnalitas, from Latin carnalis (“of the flesh”), from caro, carn- (“flesh”) + -alis (adjective suffix).
