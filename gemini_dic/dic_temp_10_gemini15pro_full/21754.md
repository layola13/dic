boathouses:/ˈboʊthaʊzɪz/
n.
船库；停船棚
A shed or building for housing boats.
The boathouses along the river were painted in bright colors. 河边的船库被漆成了鲜艳的颜色。
近义词：docks, wharves, piers, marinas
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps open water)
词性: 名词 (Noun)
