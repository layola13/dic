eponyme:/ˈɛpənɪm/ | n. | 名词 | 命名来源，同名者，以某人命名的事物 | A person after whom a discovery, invention, place, etc., is named or thought to be named.  A thing named after a person or thing. | Example: Achilles is the eponym of the Achilles tendon.  阿喀琉斯是跟腱的命名来源。

Example: The sandwich is an eponym of the Earl of Sandwich. 三明治是以三明治伯爵的名字命名的。 | 近义词: namesake | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
