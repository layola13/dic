damasken:/ˈdæməskən/| v. |镶嵌，装饰，使…带有大马士革钢的纹理|To decorate (something, especially steel) with a wavy pattern resembling that of Damascus steel; to inlay metal into other metal.|The artisan damaskened the sword with gold.|近义词：inlay, ornament, decorate, embellish|反义词:strip, bare|词性:动词

Etymology: From Damascus, Syria, famed for its patterned steel.  The word ultimately comes from the Arabic word دمشق (Dimashq), the name of the city.
