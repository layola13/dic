co-operation:/kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃn/ (UK) /koʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃn/ (US)| n. |合作，协作|The act of working together towards a common goal or purpose.|例句：International co-operation is essential to solving global problems. 国际合作对于解决全球问题至关重要。

例句：The project requires co-operation between several departments.  这个项目需要几个部门之间的合作。

近义词：collaboration, teamwork, partnership|反义词：opposition, antagonism, conflict|词性:名词

co-operations:/kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃnz/ (UK) /koʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃnz/ (US)| n. | 多次合作，多种合作 |Plural form of co-operation, referring to multiple instances of working together.|例句：The successful completion of the project depended on several co-operations between different teams.  这个项目的成功完成取决于不同团队之间的多次合作。

例句：The report highlighted the various co-operations between the government and private sector.  报告强调了政府和私营部门之间的各种合作。


近义词：collaborations, partnerships|反义词：conflicts, antagonisms|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin cooperatio(n-), from the verb cooperari, from co- ‘together’ + operari ‘work’.
