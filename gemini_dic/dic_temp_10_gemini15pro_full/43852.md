crescendi: /krəˈʃendi/ /krəˈʃɛndi/ | n. | 渐强，（尤指音乐）音量逐渐增大 |  The plural of crescendo, indicating a gradual increase in loudness in a piece of music.  It comes from the Italian word "crescendo," which is the present participle of the verb "crescere," meaning "to grow." | The symphony built to a series of dramatic crescendi.  这交响曲发展到一系列戏剧性的渐强。| Synonyms:  increases, swells, rises | Antonyms: diminuendi, decrescendi | noun
