inseparabilities:/ɪnˌsɛpərəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |不可分割性；不可分离性|The quality or state of being inseparable.  This refers to things or concepts that cannot be separated from each other.|The inseparabilities of mind and body are a key concept in some philosophies.  身心不可分割性是某些哲学中的一个关键概念。|near synonyms: indivisibility, unity, coherence|near antonyms: separability, divisibility, disunity|noun
