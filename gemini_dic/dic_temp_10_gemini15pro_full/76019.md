furfuran:/ˈfɜːrfjʊræn/ | n. | 糠醛 |  A colorless, oily, aldehyde liquid, C₄H₃OCHO, having an aromatic odor, obtained from oat hulls, corncobs, and other plant materials and used in the manufacture of nylon, resins, and other products. | Furfural is used in the production of nylon. 糠醛用于尼龙的生产。| synonyms: furaldehyde | antonyms: None | noun

furfuran:/ˈfɜːrfjʊræn/ | n. | 呋喃 | A heterocyclic organic compound, C₄H₄O, used in organic synthesis. This definition is less common than the aldehyde definition. | Furfuran is a building block in certain pharmaceuticals. 呋喃是某些药物的组成部分。| synonyms:  tetraphene, tetrol, divinylene oxide | antonyms: None | noun
