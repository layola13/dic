outdelivering:/ˌaʊtdiˈlɪvərɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
超越交付；在交付方面胜过
performing better than others in terms of delivery, often exceeding expectations in terms of speed, quality, or quantity.
Our company is outdelivering the competition by offering faster shipping times. 我们的公司通过提供更快的运输时间在交付方面胜过竞争对手。
Synonyms: outperforming, surpassing, exceeding
Antonyms: underperforming, lagging behind
词性: 动词(现在分词)
