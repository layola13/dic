bogeyman:/ˈboʊɡimæn/ (US), /ˈbʌɡimæn/ (UK)| n. |妖怪，吓唬小孩的怪物|An imaginary evil being used to frighten children.|例句：The children were scared of the bogeyman hiding under their beds. 孩子们害怕藏在床底下的妖怪。

例句：Don't worry, the bogeyman is just a story. 别担心，妖怪只是故事而已。

近义词：monster, goblin, ghoul|反义词:protector, guardian|词性:noun
