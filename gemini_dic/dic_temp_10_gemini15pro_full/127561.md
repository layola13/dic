necrosis:/nɪˈkroʊsɪs/| n. |坏死，组织坏死|The localized death of living cells.  It may be caused by injury, infection, or lack of blood supply.|Example 1: The doctor diagnosed tissue necrosis in the patient's leg due to poor circulation. 医生诊断病人的腿部由于血液循环不良而出现组织坏死。

Example 2:  Severe frostbite can lead to necrosis of the fingers and toes. 严重的冻伤会导致手指和脚趾坏死。|近义词：gangrene, mortification|反义词:growth, regeneration|词性:noun
