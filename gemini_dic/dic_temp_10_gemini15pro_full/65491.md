europeanizes:/jʊrəˈpiːənaɪzɪz/
verb
使欧洲化
To cause to become European in character or outlook.
The influx of foreign investment has europeanized the local economy. 外国投资的涌入使当地经济欧洲化了。
The new regulations are attempting to europeanize the industry's standards. 新法规试图使该行业的标准欧洲化。
europeanise, westernize
localize, nationalize
词性:动词
