guilt:/ɡɪlt/| n.|内疚，罪行，犯罪|A feeling of having done wrong or failed in an obligation.|She felt a pang of guilt for not visiting her grandmother. 她因没有去看望祖母而感到一阵内疚。|近义词：shame, remorse, regret|反义词：innocence, blamelessness|noun

guilt:/ɡɪlt/| vt.|使内疚，定罪|To cause (someone) to feel guilty; to find guilty of an offense.|He guilted her into going to the party. 他让她感到内疚，让她去了聚会。|近义词：blame, condemn|反义词：exonerate, absolve|verb
