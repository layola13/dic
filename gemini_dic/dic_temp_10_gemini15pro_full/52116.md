diamorphines:/daɪəˈmɔːrfiːnz/
noun
二乙酰吗啡；海洛因
A highly addictive analgesic drug derived from morphine, often used illicitly.  It is a narcotic and considered a controlled substance in most countries.
Diamorphines are highly addictive and illegal in many countries. 二乙酰吗啡（海洛因）具有很高的成瘾性，在许多国家是非法的。
Diamorphines were once used medicinally but are now rarely prescribed. 二乙酰吗啡曾经用于医疗，但现在很少开处方。
Synonym: heroin, morphine diacetate
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific drug, but could use) antidote, naloxone
