connivancy:/kəˈnaɪvənsi/
noun
共谋；纵容；默许
Secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing.
The crime was committed with the connivancy of the security guard. 这起犯罪是在保安的纵容下进行的。
connivance, collusion, complicity
opposition, resistance
n.
