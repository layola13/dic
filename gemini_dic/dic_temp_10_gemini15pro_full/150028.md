palpate:/ˈpælpeɪt/ (US), /pælˈpeɪt/ (UK)| v. |触诊，触摸|To examine (a part of the body) by touch, especially for medical purposes.|The doctor palpated the patient's abdomen to check for any abnormalities. 医生触诊了病人的腹部，以检查是否有异常。| The nurse gently palpated the lump to determine its size. 护士轻轻触摸肿块以确定其大小。 |examine, feel, touch|observe, watch|verb
