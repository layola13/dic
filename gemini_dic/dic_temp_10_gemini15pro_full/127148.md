nattily:/ˈnætɪli/| adv.| 衣着整洁地；漂亮地|in a natty way; smartly.  Stylishly or smartly dressed.| He was nattily dressed in a blue suit. 他穿着一套蓝色的西装，打扮得十分整洁漂亮。| 近义词：smartly, stylishly, dapperly, sprucely | 反义词：sloppily, messily, shabbily | 词性: 副词
