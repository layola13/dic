awfully:/ˈɔːfəli/ (US), /ˈɔːfʊli/ (UK)| adv.|非常地，极其地，可怕地|An adverb used to emphasize the degree of something, often something unpleasant or negative, but also sometimes something positive. It can mean 'very' or 'extremely'.|例句：The traffic was awfully heavy this morning. 今早交通拥堵非常严重。

例句：She's awfully good at playing the piano. 她钢琴弹得非常好。

近义词：very, extremely, terribly, dreadfully|反义词：slightly, moderately, a little|词性：副词

awfully:/ˈɔːfəli/ (US), /ˈɔːfʊli/ (UK)| adj.(old-fashioned) |令人敬畏的；可怕的|Inspiring awe; terrible.|例句：An awfully impressive sight.  一个令人敬畏的景象。

例句：He had an awfully nasty cut on his leg. 他的腿上有一个非常严重的伤口。

近义词：awesome, dreadful, terrible|反义词：unimpressive, pleasant|词性：形容词


词源：From "awe" (meaning fear or reverence) + "-ful" (meaning full of) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
