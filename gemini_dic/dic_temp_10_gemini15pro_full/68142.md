fail-safed:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪft/ | adj. |自动防故障的，有安全装置的 | Designed to return to a safe state in the event of a failure.  |  The airplane has a fail-safed system that will allow it to land even if the engine fails. 这架飞机有一个自动防故障系统，即使发动机出现故障也能让它安全着陆。 |  fault-tolerant, reliable, secure | unreliable, risky | adjective

fail-safe:/ˈfeɪlˌseɪf/ | n. |安全装置，故障保险装置| A device or system that is designed to return to a safe state in the event of a failure. | The fail-safe prevented a catastrophic meltdown. 这个安全装置防止了灾难性的熔毁。 | safeguard, precaution, backup | hazard, risk | noun


词源:  Compound of "fail" and "safe."  Dates back to the mid-20th century, arising from engineering and aerospace contexts.
