misphrasing:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪzɪŋ/ | n. |  措辞错误，表达失当 | The misphrasing in the contract led to a legal dispute.  合同中的措辞错误导致了法律纠纷。| Example:  He realized his misphrasing could be misinterpreted. 他意识到他的措辞错误可能会被误解。 | Synonyms: miswording, faulty wording, inaccurate phrasing,  malapropism (when a similar-sounding word is used incorrectly) | Antonyms: accurate phrasing, correct wording, proper expression | noun

misphrase:/ˌmɪsˈfreɪz/ | vt. |  措辞错误，表达失当 |  I misphrased my request and accidentally offended her. 我措辞不当，无意中冒犯了她。 | Example: Be careful not to misphrase the question. 小心不要把问题措辞错误。 | Synonyms: misword, misstate, express poorly | Antonyms: phrase correctly, word accurately, express clearly | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + phrasing (present participle of phrase, from French phrase, ultimately from Greek phrasis).
