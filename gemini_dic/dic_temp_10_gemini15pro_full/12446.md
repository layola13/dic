asleep:/əˈsliːp/| adj.|睡着的, 入睡的|In a state of sleep; sleeping.|例句：The baby is asleep. 婴儿睡着了。|近义词：sleeping, slumbering, resting|反义词：awake, conscious, alert|词性:形容词
词源:Old English on slæpe "in sleep," from slæp (see sleep (n.)). The adverbial sense "into sleep" is obsolete.
