overfrustrations:/ˌoʊvərfrʌˈstreɪʃənz/
|n.| 过度沮丧，过度的挫败感 |A state of extreme frustration, often caused by repeated or prolonged difficulties or setbacks.|  He suffered from overfrustrations after failing the exam multiple times. 他多次考试不及格后感到非常沮丧。|Synonyms: extreme frustration, exasperation, despondency|Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction|noun
