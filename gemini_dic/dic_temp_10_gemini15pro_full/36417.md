cokes:/koʊks/| n.|可乐（尤指可口可乐）;焦炭|Coca-Cola or other similar carbonated drinks; a solid fuel made by heating coal in the absence of air.|例句：I'd like two cokes, please.  我想要两杯可乐。

例句：The factory uses cokes in its furnaces. 这家工厂在熔炉中使用焦炭。|近义词：soda, pop (for the drink); coal, charcoal (for the fuel)|反义词: (No direct antonym for either sense) |词性: Noun

cokes:/koʊks/| v. (cokes, coking, coked)|焦化;炼焦|To heat coal in the absence of air to produce coke.|例句：The coal is coked in large ovens. 煤在大型烤炉中焦化。

例句：They coked the coal to remove impurities.  他们炼焦以去除杂质。|近义词：carbonize, char|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: Verb
