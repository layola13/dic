The insurgence was quickly suppressed by the military.  叛乱很快被军队镇压了。| 近义词：rebellion, revolt, uprising, mutiny | 反义词：obedience, submission, compliance | 词性: noun

insurgent:/ɪnˈsɜːrdʒənt/ /ɪnˈsɝːdʒənt/ | n. adj. | 叛乱者，起义者；起义的，叛乱的 | A person who rises in active revolt against an established government or authority; rising in revolt. | The insurgents were captured and imprisoned. 叛乱分子被俘虏并监禁。

The insurgent group claimed responsibility for the bombing.  叛乱组织声称对爆炸事件负责。| 近义词：rebel, revolutionary, mutineer | 反义词：loyalist, conformist | 词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin insurgens, insurgent- ‘rising up’, from the verb insurgere, from in- ‘into, upon’ + surgere ‘to rise’.
