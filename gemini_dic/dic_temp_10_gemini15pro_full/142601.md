osteolysis: /ˌɒstiˈɒlɪsɪs/ (British) /ˌɑːstiˈɑːlɪsɪs/ (American)| n. |骨溶解；骨质溶解|The gradual destruction or thinning of bone tissue.  This can be caused by various factors, including inflammation, infection, or pressure.|例句：The doctor diagnosed osteolysis in her hip joint. 医生诊断她髋关节骨溶解。

例句：Osteolysis around the implant was observed in the X-ray.  X光片观察到植入物周围骨溶解。

|近义词：bone resorption, bone destruction|反义词: bone formation, bone growth|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ὀστέον (ostéon, “bone”) + λύσις (lúsis, “loosening, dissolution”).
