nonspiritually:/nɒnˈspɪrɪtʃuəli/
adv.
非精神上地，世俗地
in a way that does not relate to spiritual matters; in a worldly way.
She approached the problem nonspiritually, focusing on practical solutions. 她以非精神的方式处理这个问题，专注于实际的解决方案。
