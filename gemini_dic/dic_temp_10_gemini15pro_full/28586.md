词源:
"carefree" comes from "care" (Old English *caru "sorrow, anxiety, grief") + "free" (Old English freo "free, exempt, noble").  The suffix "-ness" (Old English -nes(s)) creates the noun form "carefreeness," indicating the state or quality of being carefree.  Adding "-es" makes it plural, "carefreenesses."
