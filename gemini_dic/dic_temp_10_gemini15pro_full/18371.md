bellyache: /ˈbɛliˌeɪk/ /ˈbɛliˌæk/| n.| 抱怨，牢骚 | a complaint |  His constant bellyaches were getting on everyone's nerves. 他不断的抱怨让每个人都很烦躁。| complaint, grievance, grumble | praise, compliment | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From belly + ache. Originally referred to literal stomach pain, later extended to mean complaining in a whining manner.
