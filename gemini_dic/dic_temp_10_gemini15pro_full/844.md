abstracters: /æbˈstræktərz/
n.
摘要员，编写摘要的人
People who make abstracts of academic papers or legal documents.
The abstracters prepared concise summaries of the lengthy research articles. 摘要员们为冗长的研究文章准备了简要的摘要。

The team of abstracters worked diligently to condense the complex legal jargon into understandable summaries.  摘要员团队努力工作，将复杂的法律术语浓缩成易于理解的摘要。

近义词: summarizers, epitomizers
反义词: elaborators
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From abstract (v.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns).  The verb "abstract" comes from Latin "abstractus," the past participle of "abstrahere," meaning "to draw away" or "to separate."  It's formed from "abs-" (from, away from) + "trahere" (to draw).
