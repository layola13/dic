entoplastic:/ˌɛntoʊˈplæstɪk/ | adj. | 内塑的，由内部产生的 | Relating to the internalization of psychic energy or impulses.  Originating or operating within the body or mind. |  Entoplastic phenomena, such as dreams and hallucinations, can offer insights into the unconscious. 内塑现象，如梦和幻觉，可以提供对无意识的洞察。|近义词: endogenous, intrapsychic |反义词: exogenous |词性: adjective
