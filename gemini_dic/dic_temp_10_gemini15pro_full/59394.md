earsplitting:/ˈɪərˌsplɪtɪŋ/| adj.|震耳欲聋的，刺耳的|Extremely loud and harsh, as if capable of splitting the ears.|The earsplitting sound of the explosion made everyone jump. 爆炸的震耳欲聋的声音让每个人都跳了起来。|The band played at an earsplitting volume. 乐队演奏的声音震耳欲聋。|近义词：deafening, piercing, loud, shrill|反义词：quiet, soft, faint|词性:形容词
词源:  "ear" (耳朵) + "splitting" (撕裂的，来自split).  A compound word literally meaning loud enough to split one's ears.
