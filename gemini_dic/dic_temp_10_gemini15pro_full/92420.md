hypolemniscus:/ˌhaɪpoʊlɛmˈnɪskəs/
noun
下丘系
A tract of nerve fibers, especially one of the sensory tracts in the medulla oblongata, that forms a loop beneath each medial lemniscus.
The hypolemniscus plays a crucial role in auditory processing. 下丘系在听觉处理中起着至关重要的作用。
The researchers studied the effects of lesions on the hypolemniscus. 研究人员研究了病变对下丘系的影响。
Synonyms: lemniscus, auditory pathway
