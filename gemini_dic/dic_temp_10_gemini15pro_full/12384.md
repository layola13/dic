ashweed:/ˈæʃwiːd/| n. |毒参，毒芹，臭参|A poisonous Eurasian plant (Aegopodium podagraria) in the carrot family, having small white flowers and finely divided leaves. It is considered a noxious weed in some areas.|例句：The ashweed has spread rapidly throughout the garden. 毒参在整个花园里迅速蔓延。

例句：Ashweed is difficult to eradicate once established. 毒参一旦生长就很难根除。|近义词：ground elder, goutweed, bishop's weed, herb gerard|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æsc-wyrt, from æsc "ash tree" + wyrt "plant, herb".  The name likely refers to the similarity of its leaves to those of the ash tree.
