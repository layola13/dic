(modifies the adjective "good") He runs extremely fast. (modifies the adverb "fast")  Frankly, I don't care. (modifies the whole sentence)|近义词:(Often depends on the specific adverb) quickly, slowly, loudly, softly, very, really, quite, extremely|反义词:(Often depends on the specific adverb) fast/slow, loudly/quietly, high/low|词性:副词

adverb:/ˈædˌvɜːrb/ (US), /ˈædˌvɜːb/ (UK)| adv.|副词|A word or phrase that modifies or qualifies an adjective, verb, or other adverb or a word group, expressing a relation of place, time, circumstance, manner, cause, degree, etc. (e.g., gently, quite, then, there).|He spoke softly. 她轻声说话。 The car drove slowly. 车开得很慢。|近义词:modifier|反义词:N/A|词性:副词


USAGE->
Manner:
She sings beautifully. = 她唱得优美。
He walked slowly. = 他走得很慢。

Time:
They arrived yesterday. = 他们昨天到达。
We'll meet again tomorrow. = 我们明天再见。

Place:
The book is here. = 书在这里。
She went upstairs. = 她上楼去了。

Frequency:
I always brush my teeth. = 我总是刷牙。
She often goes to the gym. = 她经常去健身房。

Degree:
He is very tall. = 他很高。
She is quite intelligent. = 她很聪明。

Sentence Adverbs:
Fortunately, nobody was hurt. = 幸好没有人受伤。
Honestly, I don't know. = 老实说，我不知道。
