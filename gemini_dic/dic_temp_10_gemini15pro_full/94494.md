importer:/ɪmˈpɔːrtər/ |/ɪmˈpɔːrtər/ | n. |进口商；输入者|A person or company that imports goods from another country. | The importer is responsible for paying any import duties.  这家进口商负责支付所有进口税。|  The company is a major importer of Italian wines. 这家公司是意大利葡萄酒的主要进口商。 |Synonyms:  buyer, trader, dealer, merchant | Antonyms: exporter, shipper| noun
