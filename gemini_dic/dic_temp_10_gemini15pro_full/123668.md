mood: /muːd/ | n. | 心情，情绪；气氛；语气 | A temporary state of mind or feeling. |  She's in a good mood today. 她今天心情很好。| synonyms: temper, disposition, humor, feeling, frame of mind | antonyms:  None (Mood is a state, not a direction, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.  One could argue for "lack of mood" or "apathy," but these are not perfect antonyms.) | noun


mood: /muːd/ | n. | (语法)语气 | A set of verb forms that indicate the speaker's attitude toward what is being said (e.g., indicative, subjunctive, imperative). | The subjunctive mood is used to express hypothetical situations.  虚拟语气用于表达假设情况。| synonyms: mode | antonyms: None | noun
