oilskins:/ˈɔɪlˌskɪnz/
noun
油布雨衣；防水服
protective clothing made of oiled cloth, worn especially by sailors
The fisherman donned his oilskins before heading out in the storm. 渔夫在暴风雨来临前穿上了油布雨衣。

waterproof clothing, foul-weather gear
rainwear, rain gear
fair-weather clothes
