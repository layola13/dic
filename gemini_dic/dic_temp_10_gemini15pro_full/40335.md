contraception:/ˌkɑːntrəˈsepʃn/ /ˌkɒntrəˈsepʃn/| n. |避孕（法）|The prevention of pregnancy, especially by artificial methods.|例句：Access to contraception is a fundamental right.  获得避孕措施是一项基本权利。

例句：Many couples use contraception to plan their families. 许多夫妇使用避孕措施来计划他们的家庭。|近义词：birth control, family planning|反义词:conception, procreation|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *contraceptio*, from contracipere (“to conceive beforehand, intercept”), from contra- (“against”) + concipere (“to conceive”).
