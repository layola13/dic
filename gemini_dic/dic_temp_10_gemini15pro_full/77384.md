gauntness:/ˈɡɔːntnəs/| n.|消瘦；憔悴；荒凉|The gauntness of his face was striking. 他的脸消瘦得惊人。|近义词：thinness, emaciation, leanness, boniness, haggardness|反义词：plumpness, stoutness, robustness|词性:noun

Etymology: From gaunt + -ness.  Gaunt itself comes from Middle English *gaunt, probably from Old Norse *gauntr "lean, haggard," of uncertain origin.
