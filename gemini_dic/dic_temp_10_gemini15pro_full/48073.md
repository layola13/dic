declaratory:/dɪˈklærətɔːri/ (US), /dɪˈklærətri/ (UK)| adj.|声明的；宣告的；陈述的|Serving to declare; constituting or containing a declaration.  Relating to a statement that asserts something without proof.|The court issued a declaratory judgment. 法院发布了宣告性判决。|近义词： assertive, affirmative, expressive|反义词：interrogative, questioning|词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin declaratorius, from Latin declarare (see declare).
