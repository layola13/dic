asteriscus: /ˌæstəˈrɪskəs/ | n. | 星号 | A small star-like symbol (*) used in printing or writing to indicate a footnote, omission, etc. |  Example 1: The asteriscus next to the word indicated a footnote at the bottom of the page.  页面底部的脚注用单词旁边的星号表示。

Example 2:  He used an asteriscus to mark the sections he needed to edit later.  他用星号标记了他需要稍后编辑的部分。 | synonyms: star, asterisk | antonyms: N/A | noun
