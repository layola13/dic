decreeable:/dɪˈkriːəbl/
adjective
可判定的；可决定的；可下令的
Capable of being decreed or ordered.
The judge ruled that the contract was decreeable and binding. 法官裁定该合同是可判定的且具有约束力。
The king issued a decreeable order. 国王发布了一项可下令执行的命令.
近义词:ordainable, prescribable, dictatable
反义词:non-decreeable, unordainable
