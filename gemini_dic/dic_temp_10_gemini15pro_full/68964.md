fathers-in-law:/ˈfɑːðərz ɪn lɔː/ /ˈfæðərz ɪn lɔː/
noun
岳父；公公（复数）
The plural form of "father-in-law," referring to more than one father-in-law.  This could be multiple fathers of one's spouses, or the fathers of multiple spouses.
Both my fathers-in-law are retired teachers. 我的两位岳父都是退休教师。
My wife and her sister are close, so I see both my fathers-in-law often. 我妻子和她姐姐关系很好，所以我经常见到我的两位岳父。
