denigrator:/ˈdɛnɪɡreɪtər/| n. |诽谤者，诋毁者|A person who denigrates someone or something; a critic or attacker of the reputation of someone or something. |The politician's denigrator spread false rumors about his personal life.  这个政客的诋毁者散布关于他私生活的谣言。|Synonyms: critic, detractor, slanderer, backbiter, defamer|Antonyms: admirer, supporter, advocate|noun
