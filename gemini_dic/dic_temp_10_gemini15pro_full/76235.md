futures:/ˈfjuːtʃəz/| n.|期货，未来|Contracts to buy or sell an asset at a specified price on a future date.|Investors are increasingly turning to futures markets. 投资者越来越多地转向期货市场。|近义词：options, prospects, forwards|反义词：past, present|词性:名词

Etymology: From the Old French futur, from Latin futurus, future participle of esse "to be."
