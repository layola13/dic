finger-pointer:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌpɔɪntər/| n.|指责者，告密者|Someone who accuses or blames others, often without taking responsibility themselves.  A person who identifies others as responsible for something, often to avoid blame themselves. |He's such a finger-pointer; he never admits his own mistakes. 他真是个爱指责别人的人；他从来不承认自己的错误。|近义词：accuser, blamer, critic, faultfinder|反义词：self-critic, scapegoat|词性:noun


finger-pointer:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌpɔɪntər/| adj.|指责的，告密的|Involving or characterized by blaming others.  Describing something that involves accusing or blaming others. |The meeting turned into a finger-pointing session, with everyone blaming everyone else. 会议变成了互相指责的场合，每个人都在责怪别人。|近义词：accusatory, blaming|反义词：self-critical|词性:adjective
