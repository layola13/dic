coachloads:/ˈkəʊtʃləʊdz/
noun
满载游客的游览车；许多，大量
A large number of people, as would fill several coaches.
Coachloads of tourists arrive every day. 每天都有满载游客的游览车到达。
synonyms: busloads, crowds, hordes, masses
antonyms: handful, few
n.
