effecter:/ɪˈfɛktər/
n.
执行者; 效应器;  
One that effects or brings about a result.  A nerve ending that carries impulses to a muscle, gland, or organ and activates muscle contraction or glandular secretion.
The president is the primary effecter of foreign policy. 总统是外交政策的主要执行者。
Specialized cells, called effector cells, eliminate the antigen. 特殊的细胞，叫做效应细胞，清除抗原。
Synonyms: agent, executor, operator
Antonyms: inhibitor, preventer
noun
