gemmiflorate: /dʒɛmɪˈflɔːreɪt/
adjective
双生花的；孪生花的
Bearing twin flowers.  Characterized by paired flowers arising from the same point.
The gemmiflorate stem produced a beautiful pair of blossoms.  这根双生花的茎开出了一对美丽的花朵。
近义词: twin-flowered, paired-flowered
反义词: solitary-flowered (单花的)
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin "gemmi-" (bud, sprout) + "-florate" (flowered).
