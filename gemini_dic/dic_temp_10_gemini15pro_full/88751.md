hockey:/ˈhɒki/ (UK) /ˈhɑːki/ (US)| n.|曲棍球|A game played on an ice rink or a field, in which two teams try to score goals by hitting a small hard ball with a curved stick.|例句：I play hockey every Saturday. 我每个星期六都打曲棍球。
