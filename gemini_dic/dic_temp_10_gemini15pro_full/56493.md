domes:/doʊmz/
|n.|穹顶，圆顶，圆屋顶；住宅，家|A large rounded roof or ceiling, typically of a church or cathedral. Also, figuratively, a home or dwelling.|The magnificent domes of St. Basil's Cathedral dominate the Moscow skyline.  圣瓦西里大教堂宏伟的圆顶俯瞰着莫斯科的天际线。|Synonyms: cupola, dome roof|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun

domes:/dəʊmz/
|n.|家，住宅|(Informal, often plural) A person's home or residence.|He returned to his domes after a long journey.  长途旅行后，他回到了家。|Synonyms: residence, abode, dwelling|Antonyms:  exile, homelessness|noun
