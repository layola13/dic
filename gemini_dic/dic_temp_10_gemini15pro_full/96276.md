indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə/ (UK)| n.|指示器，指标，指示剂|Something that shows or points to the state or level of something. |The economic indicators suggest a recession is coming. 经济指标表明经济衰退即将到来。|Synonyms: sign, signal, measure, gauge|Antonyms:  concealer, hider|noun

indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə/ (UK)| adj.|指示的|Serving as a sign or indication of something. |The indicator light turned red, signaling a problem. 指示灯变红了，表示有问题。|Synonyms: indicative, signifying, suggestive|Antonyms: concealing, hiding|adjective
