arrangement:/əˈreɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 安排，布置；约定；整理；改编 |  The act of putting things in a particular order or position, or the result of this.  Also, an agreement or plan.  Can also refer to a musical adaptation. | We made arrangements to meet at the restaurant. 我们约好在餐馆见面。 | synonyms: plan, agreement, organization, preparation, adaptation, version | antonyms: disarray, disorder, mess, chaos | noun
