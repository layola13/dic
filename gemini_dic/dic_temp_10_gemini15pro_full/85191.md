harbourless:/ˈhɑːrbələs/| adj.|无港的，没有港口的|Lacking a harbor or harbors; without a place of shelter or refuge. |The harbourless coast offered little protection from the storm.  这无港的海岸几乎无法抵御风暴。|The small boat was tossed about in the harbourless bay. 小船在无港的海湾里颠簸。|近义词：unsheltered, exposed, unprotected|反义词：sheltered, protected|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From harbor + -less.  Harbor (meaning a place of shelter) comes from the Old English *herebeorg*, meaning "army shelter."  The suffix "-less" indicates a lack or absence of something.
