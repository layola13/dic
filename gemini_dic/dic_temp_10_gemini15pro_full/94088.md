impatiency:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənsi/| n. |不耐烦，急躁|The state of being impatient; lack of patience.|His impatiency was evident in his constant fidgeting. 他不停地坐立不安，显得很不耐烦。|Synonyms: restlessness, eagerness, impatience|Antonyms: patience, forbearance, tolerance|noun

impatient:/ɪmˈpeɪʃənt/| adj. |不耐烦的，急躁的|Not patient; restless or short of temper especially under irritation, delay, or opposition.|He was impatient to get started. 他急于开始。|Synonyms: restless, eager, hasty|Antonyms: patient, calm, tolerant|adjective
