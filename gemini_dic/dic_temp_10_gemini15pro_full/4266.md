airstream:/ˈɛərˌstriːm/ /ˈeərˌstrim/| n.|气流，空气流|A continuous flow of air.  The plane flew into a strong airstream.  飞机飞进了一股强气流。|Synonyms: current of air, air current, airflow, wind, breeze|Antonyms: still air, stagnant air|noun
Etymology-> From air + stream.  First recorded in the late 19th century.
