curlingly:/ˈkɜːrlɪŋli/ | adv. | 卷曲地，弯曲地 | In a curling or wavy manner; with curls or waves.  | Her hair fell curlingly around her shoulders. 她的头发卷曲地披在肩上。 |  The smoke rose curlingly from the chimney. 烟雾从烟囱里袅袅升起。| 近义词: wavily, sinuously, spirally | 反义词: straightly, directly | 词性: 副词

词源: From curl + -ing + -ly.  "Curl" comes from Middle Dutch or Middle Low German "krul," meaning "curly," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*kruzlō."
