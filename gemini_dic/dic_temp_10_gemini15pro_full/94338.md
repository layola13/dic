impinge:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ/| v. | 撞击；冲击；侵犯； impinge on/upon |
To have an effect, especially a negative one; to encroach; to hit or dash against. |
The noise of the traffic impinged on my concentration. 交通噪音影响了我的注意力。|
The falling rock impinged on the hiker's head. 落石击中了徒步旅行者的头部。|
近义词: encroach, infringe, intrude, affect, influence, impact |
反义词: avoid, eschew, abstain |
词性: verb
