indistinctly:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋktli/ | adv. | 模糊地，不清楚地 | Not clearly or sharply defined; vaguely. |  She mumbled indistinctly, and I couldn't understand what she was saying. 她含糊不清地咕哝着，我听不懂她在说什么。| 近义词: unclearly, faintly, vaguely, mutedly | 反义词: clearly, distinctly, sharply | 词性: 副词
