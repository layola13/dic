mucronate:/ˈmjuːkrəneɪt/
adjective
尖的，具短尖头的
Ending abruptly in a short, sharp point; tipped with a mucro.
The holly leaves are mucronate, ending in a sharp point. 冬青树的叶子是尖的，末端有一个尖头。
The leaf tip is mucronate.  叶尖是尖的。
近义词:acute, sharp, pointed, cuspidate
反义词:obtuse, rounded
词性:adjective
