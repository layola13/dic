gens:/ˈɡɛnz/ /dʒɛnz/ | n. | 家族，氏族；民族 |A clan, house, or gens is a group of families who are all descended from a common ancestor.  It is often used in discussions of ancient Roman society.| The gens Julia was one of the most prominent families in ancient Rome.  尤利娅家族是古罗马最显赫的家族之一。| synonyms: clan, tribe, house, family | antonyms: individual, outsider | noun
