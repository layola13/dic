intercountry:/ˌɪntərˈkʌntri/ | adj. | 国家间的，跨国的 | Existing or carried on between two or more countries. |  Intercountry adoption is a complex process. 国家间的收养是一个复杂的过程。|  Intercountry trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，国家间贸易显著增加。| 近义词: international, transnational, cross-border | 反义词: domestic, intranational | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From inter- + country.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," derived from Latin.
