ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Epicurēus, from Ancient Greek Ἐπίκουρος (Epíkouros, “Epicurus”), the philosopher who advocated for a life of pleasure and absence of pain.  The word evolved to encompass not just his philosophy, but a lifestyle focused on sensual pleasures, especially food and drink.
