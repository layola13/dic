fingerless:/ˈfɪŋɡərlɪs/| adj.|无指的，露指的|Without fingers, or designed to leave the fingers uncovered.|She wore a pair of fingerless gloves to keep her hands warm while still allowing her to use her phone. 她戴着一副露指手套，既保暖又不妨碍她使用手机。|Synonyms: open-fingered, mittened|Antonyms: full-fingered, gloved|adjective
