outpraised:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪzd/
verb
胜过...的赞美; 比...更受赞扬
surpassed in praising; praised more than
He outpraised all the other poets of his time. 他比同时代所有其他诗人都更受赞扬。
synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
antonyms: understate, downplay
词性: 动词 (past participle)
