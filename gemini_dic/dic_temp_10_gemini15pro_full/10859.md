arabism:/ˈærəbɪzəm/| n.|阿拉伯风格；阿拉伯语词汇；阿拉伯习俗|An arabism is a word, custom, or other characteristic that shows the influence of Arab culture.  It refers to a word or expression borrowed from Arabic or a custom or belief derived from Arab culture.|The use of "algebra" is an arabism in English.  “代数”这个词在英语中是源自阿拉伯语的。| The architecture of the Alhambra displays many arabisms. 阿尔罕布拉宫的建筑展现了许多阿拉伯风格。|synonyms: Arabic loanword, Arabic expression, Arabic influence|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider words like "occidentalism")|noun
