eruptively:/ɪˈrʌptɪvli/
adverb
爆发地，喷发地
in a manner characterized by eruption or outburst; explosively
例句：The volcano erupted eruptively, sending ash and lava high into the sky. 火山爆发性地喷发，将火山灰和熔岩喷向高空。

例句：His anger erupted eruptively, surprising everyone in the room. 他的愤怒爆发出来，让房间里的每个人都感到惊讶。

近义词：explosively, violently, suddenly
反义词：gradually, slowly, calmly
词性: adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From erupt + -ive + -ly.  "Erupt" comes from the Latin *ērumpere*, meaning "to break out," from *ē-*, "out" + *rumpere*, "to break."
