feast:/fiːst/| n. vi.|盛宴，节日，筵席；尽情地吃|A large meal, especially one held to celebrate a special occasion.  A thing that gives great pleasure.|例句：We prepared a feast for the guests. 我们为客人准备了盛宴。|例句：The art exhibition was a feast for the eyes.  这场艺术展览是一场视觉盛宴。|近义词：banquet, celebration, festival|反义词：fast, famine|noun
feast:/fiːst/| vi.|尽情地享受；饱餐|To eat and drink sumptuously.|例句：They feasted on roast turkey and all the trimmings. 他们尽情享用了烤火鸡和各种配菜。|例句：We feasted our eyes on the beautiful scenery. 我们饱览了美丽的景色。|近义词：indulge, revel|反义词：starve|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feste* "religious festival, holiday," from Vulgar Latin *festa* (plural), from Latin *festum* "festival, holiday; feast day," related to *feriae* "holidays."
