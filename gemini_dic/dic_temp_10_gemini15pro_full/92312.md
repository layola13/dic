hypnotherapists:/ˌhɪpnəˈθerəpɪsts/
noun
催眠治疗师
A person who practices hypnotherapy.
例句：Many hypnotherapists use relaxation techniques to help their clients. 许多催眠治疗师使用放松技巧来帮助他们的客户。

例句：The hypnotherapists helped me quit smoking.  催眠治疗师帮助我戒烟了。


近义词：hypnotists
反义词：(None -  no direct opposite for a profession)
词性: Noun (plural)



Etymology:
From hypno- + therapist.  "Hypno-" comes from the Greek "hypnos" meaning sleep. "Therapist" comes from the Greek "therapeutēs" meaning attendant, healer.
