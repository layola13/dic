grieve:/ɡriːv/| v. |悲伤，悲痛，哀悼|To feel or express deep sadness, especially because of someone's death.|She grieved for her lost child. 她为失去的孩子感到悲伤。|例句：He grieved over the loss of his friend. 他为失去朋友而悲伤。|近义词：mourn, sorrow, lament|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|verb

grieve:/ɡriːv/| v. |使…悲伤，使…伤心|To cause someone to feel sad or distressed.|It grieved him to see his friend so unhappy. 看到朋友如此不快乐，他感到很伤心。|例句：The news of her death grieved us all. 她去世的消息使我们所有人都感到悲伤。|近义词：sadden, upset, distress|反义词：gladden, please|verb
