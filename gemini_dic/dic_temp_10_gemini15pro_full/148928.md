overwit:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪt/| vt.| 智胜，瞒骗，以智取胜 |To outsmart or defeat by cunning or cleverness. |She overwitted her opponents with a brilliant strategy. 她以出色的策略智胜了对手。

The con man overwitted the elderly woman and stole her life savings. 骗子骗过了老妇人，偷走了她一生的积蓄。

|近义词：outsmart, outfox, outmaneuver, deceive, trick|反义词：be outwitted, be fooled, be deceived|词性: 动词(verb)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overwitten*, from Old English *oferwitan* (“to know more than, outwit”), from *ofer* (“over”) + *witan* (“to know”).  The word essentially means to "out-know" someone.
