cooperation:/koʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃn/| n.|合作，协作|The project requires cooperation between several departments. 这个项目需要几个部门之间的合作。|例句：International cooperation is essential to solving global problems.  国际合作对于解决全球问题至关重要。

例句：The success of the event depended on the cooperation of all the volunteers.  活动的成功取决于所有志愿者的合作。|近义词：collaboration, teamwork, partnership|反义词：opposition, conflict, resistance|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin cooperatio(n-), from the verb cooperari, from co- ‘together’ + operari ‘work’.
