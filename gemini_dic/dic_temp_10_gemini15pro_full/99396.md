interdifferentiating:/ˌɪntərˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪeɪtɪŋ/
verb
相互区分，互相分化
The process by which cells become specialized, developing into different types of cells within a multicellular organism.
例句：The cells are interdifferentiating to form various tissues. 细胞正在相互分化形成各种组织。

例句：The researchers studied the mechanisms of interdifferentiating in embryonic development. 研究人员研究了胚胎发育中相互分化的机制。
近义词：differentiating, specializing, diversifying
反义词：dedifferentiating, homogenizing
词性：v.
