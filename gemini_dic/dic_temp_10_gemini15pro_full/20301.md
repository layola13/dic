bisector:/ˈbaɪˌsektər/ |n.|二等分线；平分线|A line or plane that divides something into two equal parts.|The bisector of the angle created a perfect right triangle. 这条角平分线创造了一个完美的直角三角形。| synonyms: divider, splitter | antonyms: joiner, uniter | noun

bisect:/baɪˈsekt/ |vt.| 平分；二等分 |To divide something into two equal parts.|The road bisects the town into two distinct neighborhoods. 这条路把小镇分成两个截然不同的街区。| synonyms: divide, halve, split | antonyms: join, unite, combine | verb
