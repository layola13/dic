disadventurous:/ˌdɪsədˈvɛntʃərəs/ | adj. | 不爱冒险的；缺乏冒险精神的 | Not adventurous; unwilling to take risks or try new things. |  He led a rather disadventurous life, preferring routine to excitement. 他过着相当不爱冒险的生活，喜欢墨守成规而不是刺激。 | Example: She was too disadventurous to try the new rollercoaster. 她太不爱冒险了，不敢尝试新的过山车。| Synonyms: unadventurous, cautious, timid, unenterprising | Antonyms: adventurous, daring, bold, enterprising | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (prefix meaning "not") + adventurous.  The prefix "dis-" negates the meaning of "adventurous".
