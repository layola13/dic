gnathonic: /nəˈθɑːnɪk/ /næˈθɑːnɪk/
| adj. |  阿谀奉承的，谄媚的 |  Excessively flattering; sycophantic.  Characterized by flattery for personal gain.  |  His gnathonic praise made me uncomfortable. 他的谄媚的赞扬让我感到不舒服。 |  例句：The gnathonic courtier showered the king with empty compliments.  阿谀奉承的朝臣对国王大肆奉承。|  近义词：flattering, sycophantic, fawning, obsequious, adulatory | 反义词：candid, frank, sincere, forthright | 词性: adjective

gnathonic: /nəˈθɑːnɪk/ /næˈθɑːnɪk/
| n. | 奉承者，谄媚者 | A person who flatters for personal gain.  |  He is a gnathonic who will say anything to get ahead. 他是一个为了上位不择手段的奉承者。 | 例句：The king was surrounded by gnathonics vying for his favor. 国王身边围绕着一群争宠的谄媚者。 | 近义词：flatterer, sycophant, yes-man | 反义词：critic, detractor | 词性: noun (rarely used)

词源：From Greek gnathos (jaw, cheek).  Originally referring to parasites who hung around the tables of the wealthy, flattering them for scraps of food.  The meaning later broadened to encompass any kind of insincere flattery for personal gain.
