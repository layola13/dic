interavailable:/ˌɪntərəˈveɪləbl/
adjective
相互可用的；可互换的
able to be used by or in exchange with each other.
The two systems are interavailable, allowing data to be shared seamlessly. 这两个系统可以互换使用，允许数据无缝共享。
The library's resources are interavailable with other libraries in the city.  该图书馆的资源可与市内其他图书馆互换使用。
近义词: interchangeable, compatible, exchangeable
反义词: incompatible, non-interchangeable
