hatchet-faced:/ˈhætʃɪtˌfeɪst/| adj.|脸瘦削的，尖刻的|Having a thin, sharp, and sometimes severe-looking face, often suggesting disapproval or ill temper.  The term evokes the image of a hatchet's sharp blade.  It implies a face with prominent bones and a lack of fullness.|例句：The hatchet-faced librarian glared at the noisy children.  脸瘦削的图书管理员怒视着吵闹的孩子们。

例句：He had a hatchet-faced expression that made him seem unapproachable. 他一脸刻薄的表情，让人觉得难以接近。|近义词：gaunt, sharp-featured, thin-faced, austere, severe|反义词：round-faced, cherubic, plump-faced|词性:adjective
