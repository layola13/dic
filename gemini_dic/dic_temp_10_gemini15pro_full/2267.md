adept:/əˈdept/| adj.|熟练的，内行的|Highly skilled or proficient in a particular area.|She is adept at playing the piano. 她擅长弹钢琴。|近义词：skilled, proficient, expert, masterful|反义词：inept, unskilled, clumsy|词性:形容词
adept:/əˈdept/| n.|专家，内行|A person who is highly skilled or proficient in a particular area.|He is an adept in the art of negotiation. 他是谈判艺术的行家。|近义词：expert, specialist, professional, master|反义词：novice, beginner, amateur|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adeptus*, past participle of *adipisci* "to attain, reach, achieve," from *ad* "to" + *apisci* "to reach, obtain."  Originally referred to someone who had attained mastery in alchemy or occult arts.

USAGE->
形容词用法:

He is adept at solving complex problems. = 他擅长解决复杂的问题。

She is an adept musician, capable of playing multiple instruments. = 她是一位技艺精湛的音乐家，能够演奏多种乐器。


名词用法:

The adepts of this ancient martial art are few and far between. = 这种古老武术的专家现在已经很少了。

He is considered an adept in the field of computer programming. = 他被认为是计算机编程领域的专家。


PHRASE->
adept at =  擅长于
adept in =  精通于
an adept of = ...的专家
become adept at =  变得擅长
highly adept =  非常熟练
