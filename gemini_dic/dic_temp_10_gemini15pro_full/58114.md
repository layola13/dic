dropper:/ˈdrɒpər (UK), ˈdrɑːpər (US)| n.|滴管；滴瓶；投递者|A small tube with a rubber bulb at one end, used to release drops of liquid.  A device or person that drops something.|She used a dropper to administer the medicine to the baby. 她用滴管给婴儿喂药。|Synonyms: pipette, eyedropper|Antonyms: N/A|noun

dropper:/ˈdrɒpər (UK), ˈdrɑːpər (US)| n.|投递者；空投者|Someone or something that drops items, often from an aircraft.|The plane acted as a dropper, releasing supplies to the stranded hikers. 这架飞机充当了空投者，向被困的徒步旅行者投放物资。|Synonyms: dispenser, releaser|Antonyms: collector, gatherer|noun
