glimmery:/ˈɡlɪməri/| adj.|微光的，闪烁的|Faintly shining or shimmering, often in a magical or mysterious way. Characterized by a soft, wavering light.|The glimmery light of the fireflies danced in the night. 萤火虫闪烁的光芒在夜空中舞动。|The lake had a glimmery surface in the moonlight. 月光下，湖面波光粼粼。|近义词：shimmering, sparkling, twinkling, glistening|反义词：dull, dark, opaque|词性:形容词
词源: From glimmer + -y.  "glimmer" comes from Middle English *glimeren, frequentative of glimen "to shine," of uncertain origin.
