overtimorous: /ˌoʊvərˈtɪmərəs/ | adj. | 过分胆怯的，过分谨慎的 | Excessively timid or fearful; lacking in confidence and boldness. Characterized by excessive fear and caution. |  He was too overtimorous to ask her out on a date. 他太胆小了，不敢约她出去。| synonyms: timid, fearful, apprehensive, cowardly, faint-hearted, pusillanimous | antonyms: bold, brave, courageous, daring, audacious | adjective

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "timorous" (fearful, timid). "Timorous" comes from Latin "timor" (fear) + "-ous" (suffix forming adjectives).
