bantered:/ˈbæntərd/ (US) /ˈbæntəd/ (UK)| v.| 开玩笑，打趣，善意地嘲弄|To exchange playful remarks; to tease or mock lightly and good-naturedly.|They bantered back and forth about their favorite sports teams. 他们就各自喜欢的球队互相打趣。|Synonyms: joked, teased, kidded, joshing|Antonyms:  insulted, offended|verb

banter:/ˈbæntər/ (US) /ˈbæntə/ (UK)| n.| 玩笑，打趣，善意的嘲弄|Playful and teasing conversation.|The friendly banter between the coworkers made the workday more enjoyable.  同事之间友好的玩笑让工作日更加愉快。|Synonyms: teasing, raillery, badinage|Antonyms:  seriousness, solemnity|noun
