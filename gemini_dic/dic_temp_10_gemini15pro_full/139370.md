obsecrate:/ˈɒbsiˌkreɪt/ (British) /ɑˈbseˌkreɪt/ (American)| v. |恳求；哀求；央求|To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.|例句：He obsecrated her to forgive him. 他恳求她原谅他。

例句：She obsecrated the judge for mercy. 她恳求法官开恩。

近义词：beg, implore, beseech, entreat, supplicate|反义词：demand, command, order|词性：动词

词源：From Latin *obsecratus*, past participle of *obsecrare* (“to beseech, entreat”), from *ob-* (“before, towards”) + *sacrare* (“to consecrate, dedicate”).  Essentially, it means to appeal to something sacred in the other person, to implore them by all that is holy.
