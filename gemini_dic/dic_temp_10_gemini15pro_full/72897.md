followon:/ˈfɒləʊɒn/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊɑːn/ (US)| n. adj.|后续行动，后续的|Something that follows something else, especially as a continuation or consequence.|The conference included a followon session for new members.|近义词：sequel, continuation, follow-up|反义词：prelude, precursor|词性:名词/形容词

Etymology:  A compound formed from "follow" and "on." "Follow" comes from Old English *folgian "to follow," from Proto-Germanic *fulgijaną, from PIE root *pel- (2) "to fold, bend, wrap." "On" comes from Old English "on," from Proto-Germanic *ana "on," from PIE *h₂en "on."
