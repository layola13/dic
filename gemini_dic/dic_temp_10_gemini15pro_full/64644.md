erythrogranulose:/ɪˌrɪθroʊˈɡrænjuːloʊs/
adjective
嗜红颗粒的，嗜酸性颗粒的
Relating to or characterized by erythrogranules, which are acidophilic granules found in certain cells.
Erythrogranulose material was observed in the cytoplasm of the eosinophils. 在嗜酸性粒细胞的细胞质中观察到嗜红颗粒物质。
Near Synonyms: acidophilic, eosinophilic
Antonyms: basophilic
