clinician:/klɪˈnɪʃən/| n.|临床医生；临床医师|A healthcare professional who works directly with patients, providing medical care and treatment.|The clinician diagnosed the patient with pneumonia. 这位临床医生诊断病人患有肺炎。|近义词：doctor, physician, practitioner, therapist|反义词: researcher, administrator|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From clinic + -ian.  "Clinic" ultimately comes from the Greek word *kline* meaning "bed," referring to a place where people were treated. The suffix "-ian" denotes a person associated with something.
