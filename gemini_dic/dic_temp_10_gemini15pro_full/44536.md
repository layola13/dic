crossway:/ˈkrɒsweɪ/ (UK), /ˈkrɔːsweɪ/ (US)| n.|十字路口，交叉路，人行横道|A place where two or more roads meet and intersect.|We met at the crossway near the church. 我们在教堂附近的十字路口相遇了。|The deer cautiously crossed the crossway. 鹿小心翼翼地穿过了人行横道。|近义词：intersection, crossroads, junction|反义词:dead end, cul-de-sac|词性:noun
