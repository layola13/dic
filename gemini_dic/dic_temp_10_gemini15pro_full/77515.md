gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/ | v. | 哄抬价格；抬高要价(尤指在买方已同意原价后)；毁约加价 | To raise the price of something (especially a house) that has already been agreed on with another buyer, often at the last minute. |  The seller gazumped us by accepting a higher offer from someone else. 卖家接受了别人更高的报价，哄抬了价格，使我们无法购房。| synonyms: outbid, overbid | antonyms: underbid, undercut | verb
