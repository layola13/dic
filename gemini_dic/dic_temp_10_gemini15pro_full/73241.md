fording:/ˈfɔːrdɪŋ/| n.|涉水，徒涉|The act of crossing a body of water, such as a river or stream, by wading through it.|Example: The fording of the river was difficult due to the strong current.  徒涉这条河很困难，因为水流湍急。|Example:  The explorers faced many challenges, including the fording of several icy rivers. 探险家们面临着许多挑战，包括穿越几条冰冷的河流。|近义词：wading, crossing|反义词: bridging|词性:noun
