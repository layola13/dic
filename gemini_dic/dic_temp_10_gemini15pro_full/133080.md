nonequitable:/ˌnɒnˈɛkwɪtəbl/
adjective
不公平的，不公正的
Not fair or equitable; unjust.
The nonequitable distribution of resources led to widespread discontent. 资源分配不公导致普遍不满。
The judge deemed the contract nonequitable and voided it. 法官认为该合同不公平，并宣布其无效。
近义词: unfair, unjust, inequitable, biased, discriminatory
反义词: fair, just, equitable, impartial, unbiased
词性: adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "equitable" (fair and impartial).  "Equitable" comes from Old French "equitable," from Late Latin "aequitabilis," from Latin "aequitas" (equality, fairness).
