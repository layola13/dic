mistrusting:/mɪsˈtrʌstɪŋ/
adjective
不信任的，怀疑的
inclined to mistrust; suspicious.  Lacking trust or confidence.
She gave him a mistrusting look. 她用怀疑的目光看着他。
近义词:suspicious, wary, skeptical, distrustful, cynical
反义词:trusting, confident, believing, credulous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From mistrust + -ing. Mistrust itself comes from the prefix mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") and the word trust.
