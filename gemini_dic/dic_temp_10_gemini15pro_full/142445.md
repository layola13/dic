oscillator:/ˈɒsɪleɪtə(r) (UK), ˈɑːsɪleɪtər (US)| n. |振荡器，振动器|A device that produces oscillations, especially electrical or mechanical ones.  An electronic circuit that produces a repetitive electronic signal, typically a sine wave or a square wave.  A mechanical device that periodically vibrates or swings back and forth.|The oscillator in the radio creates the carrier wave.  收音机里的振荡器产生载波。

The pendulum of a clock acts as a mechanical oscillator. 时钟的钟摆充当一个机械振荡器。|近义词：vibrator, alternator|反义词: stabilizer, damper|词性:noun
