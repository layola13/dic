envying: /ˈɛnviɪŋ/
Verb
羡慕; 嫉妒
Feeling or showing envy.
She couldn't help envying her sister's success. 她不禁羡慕她姐姐的成功。

近义词: jealous, covetous, begrudging
反义词: admiring, appreciating, approving
词性:  Present Participle of "envy"
