miscalculates:/ˌmɪskælkjʊˈleɪts/| v.|错误地计算，算错|To calculate something incorrectly; to make a mistake in a calculation.|She miscalculated the cost of the project and ended up going over budget. 她错误地计算了项目的成本，结果超出了预算。|He miscalculated the distance and ran out of gas before reaching his destination. 他错误估计了距离，在到达目的地之前就没油了。|近义词：misjudge, underestimate, overestimate, err, blunder|反义词: calculate correctly, estimate accurately|动词
