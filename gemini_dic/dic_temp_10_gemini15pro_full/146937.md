overintensity:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪti/| n. |过度强度；过度紧张|Excessive intensity; extreme tension or vehemence.|The overintensity of her emotions was overwhelming. 她过度紧张的情绪令人难以承受。|Near Synonyms:  overexertion, overstrain, hyperintensity|Antonyms: calmness, relaxation, tranquility|noun


overintensity:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪti/| adj. (rarely used) |过度强烈的；过度紧张的|Excessively intense; characterized by extreme tension or vehemence.  (This adjectival usage is less common than the noun form.)|His overintensity approach to the game led to burnout.  他过度紧张的比赛方式导致了倦怠。|Near Synonyms: overwrought, overexcited, hyperintense|Antonyms: calm, relaxed, tranquil|adjective
