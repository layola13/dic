nutritionary:/njuːˈtrɪʃənəri/| adj.|营养的，滋养的|Relating to or concerned with nutrition.  Pertaining to the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth.|A nutritionary supplement can help address vitamin deficiencies. 营养补充剂可以帮助解决维生素缺乏症。|Synonyms: nutritive, nourishing, trophic|Antonyms:  non-nutritive, unhealthy|adjective
