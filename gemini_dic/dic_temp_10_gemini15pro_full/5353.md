allure:/əˈlʊr/ | v. | 引诱，诱惑 | To attract someone strongly, often in a mysterious or exciting way. | The traveler was allured by the beauty of the tropical island.  旅行者被热带岛屿的美丽所吸引。| synonyms: tempt, entice, attract, charm | antonyms: repel, deter, discourage | verb

allure:/əˈlʊr/ | n. | 魅力，诱惑力 | The quality of being powerfully attractive or fascinating. | The allure of the big city drew many people from rural areas. 大城市的魅力吸引了许多来自农村地区的人。| synonyms: attraction, appeal, charm, magnetism | antonyms: repulsion, distaste | noun
