ballpoint:/ˈbɔːlˌpɔɪnt/| n. adj.|圆珠笔；圆珠笔的|A pen with a tiny ball bearing as its writing point.  The ball rotates as you write, transferring ink from a cartridge to the paper.|I prefer writing with a ballpoint. 我更喜欢用圆珠笔写字。|Synonyms:  pen, biro (British)|Antonyms:  fountain pen, quill|noun

ballpoint:/ˈbɔːlˌpɔɪnt/| adj.|圆珠笔的|Relating to or characteristic of a ballpoint pen.|This is a ballpoint pen. 这是一支圆珠笔。|Synonyms: none|Antonyms: none|adjective
