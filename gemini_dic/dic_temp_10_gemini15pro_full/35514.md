coadventurer:/ˌkoʊədˈvɛntʃərər/| n. |共同冒险者；合作者；伙伴|A person who shares in an adventure or undertaking with another.|例句：My coadventurer and I climbed Mount Everest together. 我的伙伴和我一起攀登了珠穆朗玛峰。

例句：She proved to be a loyal and resourceful coadventurer. 她证明了自己是一个忠诚而足智多谋的伙伴。

近义词：partner, collaborator, associate, companion, ally|反义词: opponent, adversary, enemy |词性:noun
