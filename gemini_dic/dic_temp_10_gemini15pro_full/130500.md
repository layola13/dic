nonaccessions:/ˌnɒnækˈseʃənz/
|n.|未入藏品；未登录藏品|Items, typically books or museum artifacts, that have not been formally added to a library or museum's collection.|The library had a backlog of nonaccessions that needed to be processed. 图书馆积压了一批需要处理的未入藏书籍。|Synonyms: uncataloged items, unregistered items|Antonyms: accessions, holdings|noun
