opalotype:/ˈoʊpəlˌtaɪp/| n.| 蛋白石照片，蛋白石印相|A photographic print on milky white glass, giving an opal-like effect.|An opalotype of his grandmother sat on the mantelpiece. 他祖母的一张蛋白石照片摆放在壁炉台上。|Near synonyms:  milk glass photograph, opal print|Antonyms: None|noun
