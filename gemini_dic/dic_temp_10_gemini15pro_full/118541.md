mesometrium:/ˌmɛzəˈmiːtriəm/ | n. | 子宫系膜 | The part of the broad ligament specifically associated with the uterus, excluding the parts related to the uterine tubes and ovaries. It is the largest portion of the broad ligament and serves as a pathway for blood vessels and nerves to the uterus. | The surgeon carefully dissected the mesometrium to avoid damaging the uterine arteries.  外科医生小心地解剖子宫系膜，以免损伤子宫动脉。 | The mesometrium supports the uterus and provides a route for its blood supply. 子宫系膜支撑子宫并为其提供血液供应的途径。 | Synonyms: uterine broad ligament | Antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From meso- (middle) + metra (uterus) + -ium (noun suffix denoting a structure or tissue).  来自 meso-（中间）+ metra（子宫）+ -ium（表示结构或组织的名词后缀）。
