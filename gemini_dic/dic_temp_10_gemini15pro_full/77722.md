gemmipares:/dʒɛmˈɪpəriːz/
| n. |芽生动物|Animals that reproduce by budding, a form of asexual reproduction where a new organism develops from an outgrowth or bud due to cell division at a particular site.|Gemmipares, like hydra, reproduce asexually.|近义词:budding animals|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin "gemma" (bud) + "parere" (to bring forth).
