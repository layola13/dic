minifics:/ˈmɪnɪfɪks/
noun
微型小说，极短小说
Very short stories, typically under 1,000 words.  These are sometimes called "flash fiction" or "short short stories."
She enjoys writing minifics in her spare time because she can complete a whole story in one sitting. 她喜欢在空闲时间写微型小说，因为她可以一次性完成一个完整的故事。
Minifics are perfect for sharing on social media platforms. 微型小说非常适合在社交媒体平台上分享。
synonyms: flash fiction, short short stories, microfiction, drabbles
antonyms: novel, novella, short story (though these are relative)
