mandarinism:/ˈmændərɪnɪzəm/| n.| 官话；官僚作风；繁文缛节 |A style of speech considered characteristic of officialdom; high-flown or pompous language.  The ways of a mandarin; excessive adherence to official routine or procedure.|  His speech was filled with mandarinism, making it difficult for ordinary people to understand. 他的演讲充满了官腔，让普通人难以理解。|近义词：bureaucracy, officialese, jargon|反义词: plain speaking, simplicity|noun
