cowan:/ˈkaʊən/ | n. |石匠，干石匠，筑墙工|A person, especially in Scotland, who builds walls of dry stone, without mortar. |例句：The cowan carefully placed each stone to create a sturdy wall.  这位石匠小心翼翼地放置每一块石头，筑起了一堵坚固的墙。

例句：Cowans were highly skilled in the art of drystone dyking.  干石墙的建造技艺需要石匠们具备高超的技能。|近义词：mason, stone mason, waller, drystone dyker |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who demolishes walls)|词性:noun
