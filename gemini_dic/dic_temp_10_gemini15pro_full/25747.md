bur's:/bɜːrz/ |n.|刺果，毛刺，牛蒡|A rough, prickly seed case or flower head that clings to clothing or fur.  It can also refer to the plant that produces these burs, especially the burdock.| The dog came back from the field covered in burs.  狗从田野回来，身上沾满了刺果。|Synonyms: burr, prickle, thistle, sticker | Antonyms: smoothness, sleekness | noun

bur's: /bɜːrz/ |v.| (Third-person singular simple present of bur, also spelled burr)|去除毛刺，装上毛刺 (在金属加工中)| To remove burs from or add burs to (a machined surface).|  The machinist burs the edges of the metal. 机械师去除金属边缘的毛刺。 |Synonyms: deburr, trim, finish | Antonyms: roughen, snag | verb
