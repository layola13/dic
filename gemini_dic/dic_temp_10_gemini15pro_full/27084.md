calibration:/ˌkælɪˈbreɪʃn/ | n. | 校准；标定；刻度 | the process of checking a measuring instrument to see if it is accurate or the process of making slight changes to an instrument so that it measures accurately | The laboratory carries out regular calibrations of its equipment.  实验室定期对其设备进行校准。| synonyms: standardization, adjustment, tuning | antonyms: miscalibration, distortion | noun

calibration:/ˌkælɪˈbreɪʃn/ | n. | 校准；标定；刻度 |  the act of checking or adjusting (by comparison with a standard) the accuracy of a measuring instrument. | The telescope needed calibration before it could be used. 这架望远镜在使用前需要校准。 | synonyms: standardization, adjustment | antonyms: miscalibration | noun

calibrations:/ˌkælɪˈbreɪʃnz/ | n. | 校准；标定；刻度（复数） | multiple instances of the process of checking or adjusting the accuracy of measuring instruments.  | Regular calibrations are essential for maintaining accuracy in scientific experiments.  定期校准对于维持科学实验的准确性至关重要。| synonyms: standardizations, adjustments | antonyms: distortions, miscalibrations | noun
