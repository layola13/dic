noncredulous:/ˌnɒnˈkrɛdjʊləs/ | adj. | 不轻信的，怀疑的 | Not easily convinced; skeptical. |  She cast a noncredulous glance at the salesman's extravagant claims. 她对推销员夸大的说法投以怀疑的目光。

| Example: He listened with a noncredulous ear to the politician's promises.  他带着怀疑的态度听着政客的承诺。 | Synonym: skeptical, doubtful, distrustful, incredulous | Antonym: credulous, gullible, trusting, believing | Word type: Adjective
