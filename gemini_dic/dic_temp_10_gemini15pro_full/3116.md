aerothermodynamic:/ˌɛroʊˌθɜːrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/
adjective
空气热力学的
Relating to the study of aerodynamic heating and other thermodynamic effects.  It deals with the interaction of aerodynamic forces and heating effects on objects moving at high speeds through the atmosphere.
例句：Aerothermodynamic analysis is crucial for spacecraft re-entry design.  空气热力学分析对于航天器再入设计至关重要。

例句：The aerothermodynamic properties of the material were tested in a wind tunnel.  该材料的空气热力学特性在风洞中进行了测试。

近义词：aerodynamic, thermodynamic
反义词: N/A
词性：adjective


Etymology:
aero- (Greek): relating to air
thermo- (Greek): relating to heat
dynamic (Greek): relating to force or power
