decentralization:/ˌdiːsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 分散化，去中心化 |The transfer of control of an activity or organization to several local offices or authorities rather than one single one. |例句：The decentralization of power is crucial for a healthy democracy. 权力下放对于一个健康的民主至关重要。

例句：The company is undergoing a period of decentralization, giving more autonomy to regional branches.  这家公司正在经历一个权力下放的时期，给予区域分支机构更多的自主权。|近义词：distribution, dispersal, diffusion |反义词：centralization, consolidation |词性：名词
