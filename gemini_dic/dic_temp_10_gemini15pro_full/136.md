abandons:/əˈbændənz/
verb
放弃，抛弃
cease to support or look after (someone or something); desert.
He abandoned his wife and children. = 他抛弃了他的妻子和孩子。
The company abandoned the project due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该公司放弃了该项目。

近义词:desert, forsake, relinquish, renounce
反义词:maintain, support, cherish, retain
词性:v


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abandoner, from abandon "surrender, power to dispose of," from a bandon "at one's disposal, in one's power," from a "at, to" + bandon "power, jurisdiction," from Late Latin bandum "proclamation, ban."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abandons his post when faced with danger. = 面对危险时，他放弃了自己的岗位。
She abandons her diet every weekend. = 她每个周末都放弃节食。

一般过去时:
The captain abandoned ship after the hull was breached. = 船体破裂后，船长弃船了。
They abandoned the car on the side of the road. = 他们把车丢弃在路边。

现在完成时:
The search party has abandoned all hope of finding the missing hiker. = 搜救队已经放弃了找到失踪徒步旅行者的所有希望。
He has abandoned his dream of becoming a doctor. = 他放弃了成为医生的梦想。

过去完成时:
The family had abandoned their home before the hurricane hit. = 在飓风来袭之前，这家人已经放弃了他们的家。
She had abandoned her studies before moving abroad. = 在移居国外之前，她已经放弃了学业。

将来时:
If the storm worsens, they will abandon the climb. = 如果暴风雨加剧，他们将放弃攀登。
He will abandon his old habits for the new year. = 他将在新年放弃他的旧习惯。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his position, I wouldn't abandon the project. = 如果我在他的位置上，我就不会放弃这个项目。
I wish he wouldn't abandon his responsibilities so easily. = 我希望他不要这么轻易地放弃他的责任。

被动语态:
