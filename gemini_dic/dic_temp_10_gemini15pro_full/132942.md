nonemendation:/ˌnɒnɪmenˈdeɪʃən/
n.
lack of emendation; absence of correction
The nonemendation of the text led to further confusion.  文本未经修订导致了进一步的混乱。
Near Antonyms: correction, emendation, amendment, revision
Antonyms: emendation, correction

Etymology:
From non- + emendation.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without".  "Emendation" comes from the Latin "emendatio," meaning "a correction."
