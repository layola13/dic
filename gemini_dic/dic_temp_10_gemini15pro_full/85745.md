hawkishness:/ˈhɔːkɪʃnəs/
noun
强硬态度，鹰派作风
The quality of being hawkish, advocating aggressive or warlike policies, especially in foreign affairs.
His hawkishness on defense spending worried some voters.  他对国防开支的强硬态度令一些选民担忧。
近义词: aggressiveness, belligerence, militancy
反义词: dovishness, pacifism
词性: noun

hawkish:/ˈhɔːkɪʃ/
adjective
强硬的，鹰派的
Favoring aggressive or warlike policies, especially in foreign affairs.
He's known for his hawkish stance on foreign policy. 他以在外交政策上的强硬立场而闻名。
近义词: aggressive, belligerent, militant
反义词: dovish, pacifist
词性: adjective

词源: From hawk + -ish + -ness.  源自 hawk（鹰）+ -ish（形容词后缀）+ -ness（名词后缀）。  鹰被视为猛禽，因此“鹰派”比喻主张强硬或好战政策的人。
