nonresurrectional:/ˌnɒnˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃənəl/
adjective
非复活的，不涉及复活的
Not pertaining to resurrection; not involving or suggesting resurrection.
The philosopher presented a nonresurrectional view of the afterlife. 这位哲学家提出了一个不涉及复活的来世观。

The theory offers a nonresurrectional explanation for the origins of life.  这个理论对生命的起源提供了一个不涉及复活的解释。

Synonym: non-resurrectionary
Antonym: resurrectional, resurrectionary
词性: adjective
