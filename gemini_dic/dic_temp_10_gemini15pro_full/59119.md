dynamical:/daɪˈnæmɪkl/ | adj. |动态的，动力的，有活力的| characterized by continuous change, activity, or progress | The dynamical system is complex and constantly evolving. 这个动力系统很复杂，并且在不断发展。|  The artist's dynamical brushstrokes conveyed a sense of movement and energy.  这位艺术家充满活力的笔触传达了运动感和能量。| 近义词: active, energetic, vigorous, forceful | 反义词: static, inert, passive | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dynamicus, from Ancient Greek δυναμικός (dunamikós, “powerful”), from δύναμις (dúnamis, “power”).
