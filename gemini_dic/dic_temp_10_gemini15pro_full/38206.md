compunct:/kəmˈpʌŋkt/ | adj. | 懊悔的，内疚的 | Feeling or expressing remorse or regret for wrongdoing. |  He felt compunct about his harsh words to his friend. 他为自己对朋友的苛刻言辞感到懊悔。| remorseful, penitent, contrite | unrepentant, remorseless | adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *compunctus*, past participle of compungere (“to prick sharply, sting, vex”), from com- (“together”) + pungere (“to prick”).
