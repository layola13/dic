dairyman:/ˈdɛəri mən/ | n. | 奶农；乳品商 | A person who works in or owns a dairy farm or business, especially one who milks cows or makes and sells milk products. | The dairyman delivers fresh milk every morning.  奶农每天早上都送新鲜牛奶。 |  The dairyman woke early to milk the cows. 奶农早起挤牛奶。 | Synonyms: milkman, farmer, cowherd | Antonyms: consumer | noun
