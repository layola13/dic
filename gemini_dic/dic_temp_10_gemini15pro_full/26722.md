cadge:/kædʒ/
verb
乞讨，索取
to ask for or obtain something from someone, often by taking advantage of their generosity or weakness
He's always cadging cigarettes from his friends. 他总是向他的朋友们索要香烟。
近义词：beg, scrounge, sponge
反义词：give, provide, donate
词性: verb

cadge:/kædʒ/
noun
乞讨，索取（行为）
the act of cadging
He makes a living by cadge. 他靠乞讨为生。（这种用法比较少见）
近义词：begging, scrounging, sponging
反义词：giving, provision, donation
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly related to Scottish cadger, a carrier or peddler, or to obsolete cadge, to bind or tie up.
