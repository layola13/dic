beadleship:/ˈbiːdəlʃɪp/| n. |法警的职位；法警的管辖区域|The office or jurisdiction of a beadle.  A beadle was a minor parish official who had various duties, such as keeping order during services.|The beadleship became vacant when old Thomas passed away.  托马斯老先生去世后，法警的职位空缺了。|Synonyms:  office, position, post, jurisdiction|Antonyms: unemployment|noun
