constructors:/kənˈstrʌktərz/ | n. | 构造器，构造函数 | A special method within a class used to create and initialize objects of that class.  It's called automatically when a new object of the class is created. |  The `Dog` class has a constructor that takes the dog's name and age as arguments. | synonyms: initializer, builder | antonyms: destructor | noun
