interleaved:/ˌɪntərˈliːvd/
interleaved| adj.|交错的，插层的|arranged in alternate layers or interspersed with other elements|The chapters are interleaved with photographs. 章节之间穿插着照片。|interlaced, interspersed, alternating|separate, distinct|adjective
interleave:/ˌɪntərˈliːv/
interleave| vt.|插入，交错|to insert between or among others; to arrange in alternate layers|He interleaved the pages with blank sheets of paper. 他在书页之间插入了空白纸。|insert, intersperse, interlace|remove, separate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + leaf (page).  The original meaning referred to inserting a leaf or leaves between the pages of a book.
