dullish:/ˈdʌlɪʃ/| adj.|略显迟钝的，有点无聊的|Somewhat dull; slightly lacking in brightness, sharpness, or interest.|The lecture was rather dullish.|近义词：boring, tedious, uninteresting, bland|反义词：interesting, exciting, stimulating, vibrant|词性:形容词
词源: From dull + -ish.  The suffix "-ish"  means "somewhat" or "slightly".  So, "dullish" literally means "somewhat dull."
