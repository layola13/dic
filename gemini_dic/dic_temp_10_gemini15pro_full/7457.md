anisophyllous:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈfɪləs/ | adj. | 异形叶的；不等叶的 | Having leaves of two or more sizes and shapes on the same plant.  | Example 1: The anisophyllous nature of the plant made it difficult to identify.  这种植物异形叶的特性使其难以识别。

Example 2:  Some aquatic plants exhibit anisophyllous foliage, with submerged leaves differing from surface leaves. 一些水生植物表现出异形叶的叶子，其水下叶子与水面叶子不同。 | Synonym: heterophyllous | Antonym: isophyllous | word type: adjective
