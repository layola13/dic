headteacher:/ˈhedˌtiːtʃər/ (UK) /ˈhɛdˌtitʃɚ/ (US)| n. |校长|The principal teacher in charge of a school. |Example: The headteacher addressed the students at assembly. 校长在集会上向学生们讲话。
Example:  The headteacher made the final decision about the school trip. 校长对学校旅行做了最终决定。

|Synonym: principal, headmaster, headmistress |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps student, teacher) |Noun|
