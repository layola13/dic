curtail:/kərˈteɪl/| vt.|缩减，削减，限制|To reduce in extent or quantity; to shorten.|例句：The government decided to curtail spending on defense. 政府决定削减国防开支。|例句：She had to curtail her vacation due to the unexpected emergency. 由于突发的紧急情况，她不得不缩短假期。|近义词：reduce, shorten, decrease, diminish, restrict, limit|反义词:expand, increase, lengthen, extend|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *curteillier, *cortailler "to dock, cut short," from Vulgar Latin *curtaliare, from Latin curtus "shortened, cut short."
