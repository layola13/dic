inarable:/ɪˈnærəbl/ | adj. | 不可耕种的，贫瘠的 | Not capable of being cultivated for crops. | The land is too dry and rocky, making it inarable. 这片土地过于干燥和多岩石，使其无法耕种。|  The farmers abandoned the inarable land and moved to more fertile regions.  农民们放弃了贫瘠的土地，搬到了更肥沃的地区。 | 近义词：infertile, barren, unproductive | 反义词：fertile, arable, productive | 词性：形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from in- (expressing negation) + arable.
