falcate:/ˈfɔːlkeɪt/ /ˈfælkət/ | adj. | 镰刀形的，弯曲的 | Curved like a sickle; hooked. | The bird has a falcate beak. 这只鸟有一个镰刀状的喙。 | The moon appeared as a thin, falcate sliver in the sky.  月亮在天空中呈现出一弯细细的镰刀形。 | 近义词: curved, hooked, sickle-shaped | 反义词: straight | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *falcātus, from falx (“sickle”).
