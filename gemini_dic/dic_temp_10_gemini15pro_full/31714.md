chawi:/ˈtʃɑːwiː/| n.| 咀嚼物; 嚼烟; |Chewing tobacco is a type of smokeless tobacco product consumed by placing a portion of the tobacco between the cheek and gum or upper lip teeth and chewing.  嚼烟是一种无烟烟草制品，通过将一部分烟草放在脸颊和牙龈或上唇牙齿之间咀嚼来食用。|He took a chaw of tobacco. 他嚼了一口烟草。|近义词：quid, chew|反义词:|n.
