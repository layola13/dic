browbeat:/ˈbraʊˌbiːt/| vt.| 恐吓；威逼；吓唬|To intimidate or dishearten by stern, overbearing, or domineering manners; to bully.|The boss browbeat his employees into working overtime. 老板威逼员工加班。|近义词：intimidate, bully, coerce, threaten|反义词：cajole, coax, persuade|verb

browbeat:/ˈbraʊˌbiːt/| adj.|专横跋扈的；盛气凌人的 (较少用)|Overbearing and intimidating.|He had a browbeating manner that made everyone uncomfortable.  他专横跋扈的态度让每个人都不舒服。|近义词：overbearing, domineering, intimidating|反义词：gentle, kind, meek|adjective
