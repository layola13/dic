oligoclase: /ˈɒlɪɡəˌkleɪz/ /ˈɑːlɪɡoʊˌkleɪz/ | n. |  奥长石 | A plagioclase feldspar mineral with a composition between albite and andesine. It is a common constituent of igneous rocks such as granite and diorite. | Oligoclase is often used in the production of ceramics. 奥长石常用于陶瓷生产。| synonym: plagioclase, feldspar | antonym: N/A | noun
