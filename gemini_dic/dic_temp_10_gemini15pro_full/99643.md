interlay:/ˌɪntərˈleɪ/ | v. n. |交错；插入；隔层放置|To place between or among other things; insert. Also, a layer placed between other layers.|例句：They interlaid sheets of parchment between the pages of the book. 他们在书页之间夹了羊皮纸。|近义词：intersperse, interpose, insert, intercalate|反义词: remove, extract|词性:动词, 名词


interlay:/ˌɪntərˈleɪ/ | n. |夹层；中间层|Something laid between other things; a layer placed between other layers.|例句：The interlay of fat and lean gave the meat a marbled appearance. 肥瘦相间的夹层使肉呈现出大理石花纹。|近义词：layer, stratum, lamination|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
