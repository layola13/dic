bolden:/ˈboʊldən/ (US) /ˈbəʊldən/ (UK)| v.|使加粗；使大胆；使更显著|To make something bolder, more noticeable, or more courageous.|The new CEO **boldened** the company's marketing strategy. 新任首席执行官使公司营销策略更加大胆。|近义词：strengthen, empower, embolden|反义词: weaken, subdue, diminish|词性:动词

Etymology:
From "bold" + "-en" (suffix meaning "to make or become").  "Bold" comes from Middle English *bald*, meaning "brave, confident," from Old English *beald*.
