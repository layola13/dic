besmirchers:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃərz/
noun
诽谤者，玷污者
People who besmirch someone's reputation or character.
The besmirchers were eventually exposed and their lies discredited. 这些诽谤者最终被揭露，他们的谎言也被推翻。
synonyms: slanderers, defamers, vilifiers, calumniators
antonyms: defenders, supporters, champions
n.


Etymology:
From besmirch + -er.  Besmirch comes from Middle English *besmerchen, from be- + smerchen "to soil, stain," probably from Old English *smercian, frequentative of smear.
