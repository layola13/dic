aristocratic:/ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. |贵族的；贵族式的；有贵族气派的| Of or relating to the aristocracy; having the qualities associated with the aristocracy, such as elegance, refinement, and a sense of superiority. |  She had an aristocratic bearing and a disdain for commoners. 她举止优雅，对平民不屑一顾。| The aristocratic family traced their lineage back centuries.  这个贵族家庭的世系可以追溯到几个世纪以前。 | 近义词：noble, patrician, highborn, elite, upper-class | 反义词：plebeian, common, lower-class, working-class | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aristocratique*, from Greek *aristokratikos* "of the aristocracy," from *aristokratia* "rule of the best," from *aristos* "best" + *-kratia*, from *kratos* "power, strength, rule."
