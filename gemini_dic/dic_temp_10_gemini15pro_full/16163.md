bankable:/ˈbæŋkəbl/| adj.|可靠的，有银行信誉的，有票房保证的|Likely to make a profit; financially sound and reliable. Also, (of an actor, etc.) sure to attract a large audience and guarantee financial success.|例句：This project is bankable, and we expect a good return on investment. 这个项目可靠，我们预计会有很好的投资回报。

例句：He's a bankable star, guaranteeing a box office hit. 他是一位有票房保证的明星，确保票房大卖。|近义词：reliable, dependable, credible, trustworthy, marketable, lucrative|反义词：risky, unreliable, untrustworthy|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bank + -able.  The original sense referred to something acceptable as security for a loan.
