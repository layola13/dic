obstinateness:/ˈɒbstɪnətnəs/ (US) /ˈɒbstɪnɪtnəs/ (UK)| n.|固执，顽固|The quality of being unreasonably determined to act in a particular way despite argument or persuasion; stubbornness.|Her obstinateness made it impossible to reach a compromise. 她的固执使得达成妥协成为不可能。|近义词：stubbornness, tenacity, persistence, intransigence|反义词：compliance, flexibility, acquiescence|词性:名词

obstinate:/ˈɒbstɪnət/ (US) /ˈɒbstɪnɪt/ (UK)| adj.|固执的，顽固的|Unreasonably determined to act in a particular way despite argument or persuasion; stubborn.|He was obstinate and refused to listen to any advice. 他很固执，拒绝听取任何建议。|近义词：stubborn, headstrong, willful, unyielding|反义词：compliant, amenable, docile|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *obstinatus*, past participle of *obstinare* "to persist, stand firm," from *ob-* "against" + *stinare* (from *stare* "to stand").
