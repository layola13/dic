menthene:/ˈmenθiːn/| n.|薄荷烯|A cyclic monoterpene hydrocarbon, C₁₀H₁₈, found in several essential oils, especially peppermint oil. It exists in several isomeric forms.|Menthene is a component of peppermint oil. 薄荷烯是薄荷油的一种成分。| Menthene is used in some fragrances. 薄荷烯用在一些香水中。|Synonyms:  p-Menthene, 1-methyl-4-(1-methylethyl)cyclohexene|Antonyms: N/A|noun
