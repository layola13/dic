anteater:/ˈæntˌiːtər/| n.|食蚁兽|A mammal that feeds on ants and termites, typically having a long snout and tongue and strong claws.|The anteater used its long tongue to lap up ants from the anthill. 食蚁兽用它长长的舌头从蚁丘里舔食蚂蚁。|Near synonyms:  pangolin, aardvark (though these are not closely related)|Antonyms:  N/A (There's no direct opposite of an animal)|noun
