hangs:/hæŋz/ | v. | 悬挂，垂下；吊着；(被)绞死；逗留；坚持；悬而未决 |The picture hangs on the wall. 这幅画挂在墙上。|近义词：suspend, dangle, linger, persist |反义词:stand, rise |词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *hangen*, from Old English *hangian* (intransitive, "to hang"), *hōn* (transitive, "to hang, suspend"), from Proto-Germanic *hangijaną* (intransitive), *hanhaną* (transitive), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱongʰ-*. Cognate with Scots *hing* (“to hang”), West Frisian *hingje* (“to hang”), Dutch *hangen* (“to hang”), German *hängen* (“to hang”), Danish *hænge* (“to hang”), Swedish *hänga* (“to hang”), Icelandic *hengja* (“to hang”).  The transitive and intransitive forms merged in Middle English.
