negates:/nɪˈɡeɪts/| v.|否定，取消，使无效|The new evidence negates his previous testimony. 新证据否定了之前的证词。|contradicts, denies, invalidates|affirms, confirms, supports|verb
Etymology: From Latin "negatus," past participle of "negare" (to deny), from "nec" (not) + "aio" (I say).
