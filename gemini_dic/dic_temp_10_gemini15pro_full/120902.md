miscall:/ˌmɪsˈkɔːl/ | v.  n. |叫错名字，误称；错误的称呼|To call by a wrong name; to name incorrectly.| He miscalled her "Mary" instead of "Marie". 他把她叫成了“玛丽”，而不是“玛丽”。|Synonyms: misname, mislabel, mistitle|Antonyms: name correctly, identify correctly|verb

miscall:/ˌmɪsˈkɔːl/ | n. |错误的称呼; 叫错名字|A wrong name or appellation.|His miscall of her name embarrassed everyone. 他叫错她的名字让每个人都很尴尬。|Synonyms: misnomer, misnaming|Antonyms: correct name, correct appellation|noun
