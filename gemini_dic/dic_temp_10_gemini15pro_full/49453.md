delthyris:/dɛlˈθaɪrɪs/
noun
无脊椎腕足动物门的一属（石燕贝属）
A genus of brachiopods (lamp shells).
Delthyris are common fossils found in Devonian rocks. 石燕贝属化石常见于泥盆纪岩石中。

近义词:brachiopod, spirifer
反义词:None (Specific genus; no direct opposite)
词性:n.


delthyrium:/dɛlˈθɪriəm/
noun
腕足动物壳瓣上三角形的凹陷
A triangular opening or notch in the dorsal valve of a brachiopod shell.
The delthyrium in some brachiopods is partially closed by a deltidium.  一些腕足动物的三角孔被一个三角板部分地封闭。

近义词:notch, opening
反义词:None (Specific anatomical feature; no direct opposite)
词性:n.
