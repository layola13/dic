eye-popping:/ˈaɪˌpɑːpɪŋ/| adj.|令人瞠目结舌的，令人惊叹的|Something that is eye-popping is extremely impressive, surprising, or shocking, especially because of being very large or brightly coloured.|The special effects in the movie were eye-popping.  这部电影的特效令人叹为观止。|The price of the car was eye-popping. 这辆车的价格令人咋舌。|Synonyms: astonishing, amazing, stunning, breathtaking, spectacular|Antonyms: dull, boring, mundane, unimpressive|adjective
