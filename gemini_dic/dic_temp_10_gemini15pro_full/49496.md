demagnetize:/diːˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/
verb
消磁，去磁
To eliminate or reduce the magnetic properties of (a material).
The watchmaker demagnetized the watch after it was exposed to a strong magnetic field.  钟表匠在手表暴露于强磁场后对其进行了消磁。
degauss, neutralize
