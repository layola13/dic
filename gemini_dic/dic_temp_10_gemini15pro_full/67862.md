fabler:/ˈfeɪblər/| n.|寓言家；讲故事的人|A person who writes or tells fables. |Aesop was a famous fabler of ancient Greece.  伊索是古希腊著名的寓言家。|Synonym: storyteller, mythmaker, writer of fables|Antonym:  (No direct antonym; consider liar, deceiver if the fables are presented as truth)|Noun
