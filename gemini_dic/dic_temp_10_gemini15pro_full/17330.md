bewitchingly: /bɪˈwɪtʃɪŋli/ | adv. | 迷人地，令人着迷地 | In a manner that is enchanting and captivating, as if by magic. | She smiled bewitchingly at him across the room. 她妩媚地笑着，眼神越过房间，看着他。|  The music played bewitchingly, transporting us to another world.  音乐演奏得如痴如醉，把我们带到了另一个世界。 | 近义词: charmingly, enchantingly, captivatingly, alluringly | 反义词: repulsively, unattractively, off-puttingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From bewitching + -ly.  Bewitching comes from bewitch, which in turn comes from be- + witch.  Be- is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely," and witch refers to a person who practices witchcraft.  Therefore, the original meaning was to affect as if by witchcraft.
