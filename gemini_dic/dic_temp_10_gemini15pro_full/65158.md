ethnicist:/ˈɛθnɪsɪst/ | n. | 民族学家；种族研究专家 | A scholar who specializes in the study of ethnicity and ethnic relations. |  Ethnicists study the formation, maintenance, and transformation of ethnic identities. 民族学家研究民族认同的形成、维持和转变。 |  The ethnicist's research focused on the cultural practices of various immigrant groups. 这位民族学家的研究重点是各移民群体的文化习俗。| Synonyms: ethnologist, anthropologist | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun
