oviparous:/ōˈvɪpərəs/
adjective
卵生的
Producing eggs that hatch outside the body.
Most reptiles are oviparous, laying eggs that develop and hatch externally. 大多数爬行动物都是卵生的，产下的卵在体外发育和孵化。
Birds are oviparous animals. 鸟类是卵生动物。
synonyms: egg-laying
antonyms: viviparous, ovoviviparous
adj.
