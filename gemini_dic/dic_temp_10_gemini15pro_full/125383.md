multimodality:/ˌmʌltiˈmoʊˈdælɪti/ | n. |多模态，多模式 |The integration of different modes of communication or representation, such as text, audio, images, animation, video, and gestures. |例句：Multimodality is becoming increasingly important in education. 多模态在教育中变得越来越重要。

|例句：The museum exhibit used multimodality to engage visitors.  博物馆展览使用多模态来吸引游客。

|近义词：multimedia, multimodal |反义词: unimodal, single-mode |词性: noun
