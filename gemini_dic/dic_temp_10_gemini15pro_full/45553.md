curl:/kɜːrl (British)/ /kɝl (American)/| n. v.|卷曲，卷发；使…卷曲|A curl is a small section of hair that has been curved or twisted.|Her hair was a mass of dark curls. 她的头发是一团乌黑的卷发。|近义词：twist, wave, coil, spiral|反义词:straighten, uncurl|词性:名词,动词

curl:/kɜːrl (British)/ /kɝl (American)/| v. |卷曲，使…卷曲；蜷缩|To curl means to form or cause something to form into a curved or spiral shape.|The smoke curled upwards. 烟雾袅袅上升。|近义词：twist, wave, coil, spiral|反义词:straighten, uncurl|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *curle, from Old Norse *kurla (“to be curled”), frequentative of *kūra (“to bend, bow, stoop”), from Proto-Germanic *hūrą (“to bend, bow”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“to turn, bend”).  Cognate with Icelandic krulla (“to curl”), Swedish krulla (“to curl”).
