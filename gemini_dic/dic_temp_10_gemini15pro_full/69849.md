ferrite:/ˈfɛraɪt/ | n. | 铁氧体 | A ceramic-like material containing iron oxide, usually combined with other metallic oxides, and having magnetic properties.  A ferrite core is used in inductors and transformers. | Ferrite magnets are commonly used in speakers. 铁氧体磁铁常用于扬声器中。| synonyms: ceramic magnet, magnetic oxide | antonyms: paramagnet, diamagnet | noun

ferrite:/ˈfɛraɪt/ | adj. | 铁素体的 |  Made of or containing ferrite. |  The ferrite core improves the inductor's performance. 铁氧体磁芯提高了电感器的性能。| synonyms: ferromagnetic | antonyms: non-magnetic | adjective
