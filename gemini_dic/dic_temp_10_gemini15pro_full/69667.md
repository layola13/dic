fencing:/ˈfɛnsɪŋ/| n.|击剑, 围栏, 剑术|The sport or art of attack and defense with a sword, especially a foil, épée, or saber.  Also, the fences enclosing a piece of land. |He took up fencing in college. 他在大学时开始学习击剑。|The property was surrounded by a high fencing. 这处房产被高高的围栏包围。|近义词：swordsmanship, barrier, enclosure|反义词: openness|noun


fencing:/ˈfɛnsɪŋ/| v.|围起来, 击剑|The act of enclosing an area with a fence.  Also, participating in the sport of fencing.|They are fencing the garden to keep the rabbits out. 他们正在把花园围起来，防止兔子进入。|She was fencing with her opponent.  她正在和对手击剑。|近义词：enclosing, barricading|反义词: opening|verb
