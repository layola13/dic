lively:/ˈlaɪvli/ /ˈlaɪvli/| adj.|活泼的，生动的，充满活力的|Full of life and energy; active and enthusiastic.|The band played a lively tune that got everyone dancing. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的曲子，让每个人都跳起舞来。|近义词：active, energetic, vibrant, animated|反义词：dull, boring, lifeless, inactive|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *lively, *livly, from Old English līflīc (“living, vital”), equivalent to life +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots lifely (“lively”), West Frisian liiflik (“pleasant”), Dutch levendig (“lively”), German lebhaft (“lively”).
