cartridge:/ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ/ /ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ/ | n. |墨盒；弹药筒；软片盒；卡带|A container holding ink, film, magnetic tape, or other material for insertion into a mechanism, such as a pen, printer, camera, or tape recorder.|The printer ran out of ink, so I replaced the cartridge. 打印机墨水用完了，所以我换了墨盒。|Synonyms: container, capsule, cassette, magazine|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cartridge:/ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ/ /ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ/ | n. |子弹|A metal casing containing the explosive charge and projectile for a firearm.|He loaded the cartridge into the rifle. 他把子弹装进了步枪。|Synonyms: bullet, round, shell|Antonyms: N/A|noun
