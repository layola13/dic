consumerism:/kənˈsjuːmərɪzəm/| n. |消费主义|The preoccupation of society with the acquisition of consumer goods.|  Example 1: Consumerism drives the economy in many developed countries. 消费主义推动了许多发达国家/地区的经济发展。

Example 2:  He criticized the rampant consumerism of modern society. 他批评了现代社会猖獗的消费主义。|近义词：materialism, commercialism|反义词:frugality, asceticism|词性:noun
