miseration:/ˌmɪzəˈreɪʃən/
n.
怜悯，同情；悲惨的境遇
a feeling of sympathy and sorrow for the misfortunes of others; the state of being in a very unhappy or unfortunate state.
She looked at him with miseration. 她同情地看着他。
He was living in abject miseration. 他生活极其悲惨。
近义词:pity, compassion, sympathy, commiseration
反义词:indifference, apathy, cruelty
词性:名词
