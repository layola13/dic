gourd:/ɡɔːrd/ /ɡʊərd/| n. |葫芦，瓜|A fruit with a hard rind and many seeds, whose dried shell is used as a container.|例句：She used a gourd as a water bottle. 她用葫芦当水壶。

例句：The gourds hanging on the porch added a rustic touch. 挂在门廊上的葫芦增添了乡村气息。|近义词：calabash, squash|反义词:|名词

gourdy:/ˈɡɔːrdi/ /ˈɡʊərdi/| adj. |像葫芦的，圆鼓鼓的|Resembling or characteristic of a gourd, especially in shape.|例句：The plant had a gourdy shape.  这种植物呈葫芦状。

例句：His stomach was rather gourdy after the feast.  盛宴过后，他的肚子圆鼓鼓的。|近义词：rounded, bulbous|反义词: slender, thin|形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gourde, from Latin cucurbita, of uncertain origin.
