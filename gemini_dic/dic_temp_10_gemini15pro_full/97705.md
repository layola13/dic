inimically:/ɪˈnɪmɪkli/
adverb
敌对地，怀敌意地
in a hostile or unfriendly manner
例句：He stared inimically at his rival. 他敌意地盯着他的对手。

例句：She spoke inimically about her former employer. 她以敌对的态度谈论她的前任雇主。

近义词：hostilely, antagonistically, unfriendly, unfavorably
反义词：amicably, friendly, favorably
词性：副词

词源：
From Late Latin inimīcus (“enemy”) + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin in- (“not”) + amīcus (“friend”).
