foremost:/ˈfɔːrmoʊst/ (US), /ˈfɔːməʊst/ (UK)| adj. |最重要的；最著名的；首先的| Situated at the front; first in place, order, rank, or importance.| Einstein is regarded as the foremost physicist of the 20th century. 爱因斯坦被认为是20世纪最重要的物理学家。|  She's one of the foremost experts on child psychology. 她是儿童心理学领域最著名的专家之一。 | synonyms: leading, principal, primary, chief, paramount, preeminent, outstanding, supreme | antonyms: last, hindmost, least important, insignificant | adjective
