groan:/ɡroʊn/ (US) /ɡrəʊn/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|呻吟，抱怨，叹息；发出吱嘎声|A deep inarticulate sound conveying pain, grief, or disapproval.  A creaking sound.|The injured man groaned in pain. 受伤的男子痛苦地呻吟。|近义词：moan, sigh, complain, creak|反义词：cheer, laugh, rejoice|noun, verb
