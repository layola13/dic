decadence:/ˈdɛkədəns/ | n. | 衰落，颓废，堕落 | Moral or cultural decline as characterized by excessive indulgence in pleasure or luxury. |  The Roman Empire's decadence ultimately led to its downfall. 罗马帝国的颓废最终导致了它的灭亡。

The artist's later work reflected a sense of decadence and despair.  这位艺术家后期的作品反映了一种颓废和绝望的感觉。

| 近义词：decline, decay, degeneration, corruption, depravity | 反义词： morality, virtue, integrity, uprightness,  ascendancy | 词性: 名词
