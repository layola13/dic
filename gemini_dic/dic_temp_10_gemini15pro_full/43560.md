crazily: /ˈkreɪzɪli/ /ˈkreɪzəli/ | adv. |疯狂地，狂热地，精神失常地 | In a crazy or insane manner; wildly; enthusiastically. | She laughed crazily, throwing her head back. 她疯狂地大笑，把头向后仰。| The fans cheered crazily when their team scored. 当他们的球队得分时，球迷们疯狂地欢呼。| 近义词: insanely, madly, wildly, frantically | 反义词: sanely, rationally, calmly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From crazy + -ly.  Crazy comes from Middle English *crased, from Old Norse *kreisk "bent, crooked," related to Old English *cræc "crack."
