nautilite:/ˈnɔːtɪˌlaɪt/| n. |鹦鹉螺; 鹦鹉螺壳; |A pearly, iridescent substance forming the inner layer of certain mollusk shells, especially those of the nautilus. It is used for making buttons, ornaments, etc.|The buttons were made of nautilite.|近义词：nacre, mother-of-pearl|反义词:None|noun
