outdespatched:/ˌaʊtdɪˈspætʃt/
verb
超越派遣；在派遣上胜过
to despatch something or someone more quickly or effectively than another.
Napoleon outdespatched his enemies, enabling him to achieve a series of stunning victories. 拿破仑的派遣速度超过了他的敌人，这使他取得了一系列惊人的胜利。
The company outdespatched its competitors by streamlining its logistics processes.  该公司通过简化物流流程，在发货速度上胜过了竞争对手。
Synonyms: outstrip, surpass, outperform
Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind
词性: 动词
