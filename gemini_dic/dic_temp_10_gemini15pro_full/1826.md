acrobatically: /ˌækrəˈbætɪkli/
adverb
杂技般地; 技巧高超地
In a way that involves great skill and agility, like an acrobat.
The gymnast acrobatically flipped across the floor.  体操运动员以杂技般的动作翻滚过地板。

The monkey acrobatically swung from branch to branch. 猴子灵巧地在树枝间荡来荡去。

近义词: skillfully, deftly, nimbly, adroitly
反义词: clumsily, awkwardly
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From acrobat + -ic + -ally.  Acrobat comes from the Greek akrobatēs, meaning "walking on tiptoe, rope-dancer," from akros "highest, topmost" + bainein "to go, walk, step."

USAGE->
She acrobatically maneuvered the car through the narrow streets. 她熟练地驾驶着汽车穿过狭窄的街道。

He acrobatically leaped over the fence. 他灵巧地跳过了栅栏。
