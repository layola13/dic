miswiring:/ˌmɪsˈwaɪərɪŋ/
noun
错误布线，接线错误
incorrect wiring; faulty connections of wires.
The miswiring caused a short circuit and started a fire.  错误的线路导致了短路并引发了火灾。
The robot's malfunction was due to a miswiring in its control panel.  机器人的故障是由于其控制面板中的接线错误造成的。
近义词: faulty wiring, incorrect wiring, bad wiring
反义词: correct wiring, proper wiring
词性: n.


miswire:/ˌmɪsˈwaɪər/
verb
错误地连接线路，接错线
to wire something incorrectly.
He miswired the circuit and blew a fuse. 他把电路接错了，烧断了保险丝。
I think I miswired the stereo because it's not working. 我想我把音响接错了，因为它不工作。
近义词: wire incorrectly
反义词: wire correctly
词性: v.


ETYMOLOGY->
mis- + wire + -ing
mis-: prefix meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly"
wire: from Old English *wīer, of Germanic origin, related to Old Norse vír "wire," from Proto-Germanic *wīrą, meaning "metal thread."
-ing: suffix forming present participles and gerunds of verbs, also used to form nouns from verbs.
