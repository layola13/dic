outstrides:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪdz/
verb
超越，胜过
to surpass someone in walking or running, or to exceed something in scope or importance.
She easily outstrides her competitors in every race. 她轻松地在每场比赛中超越她的对手。
The company's growth outstrides all expectations. 该公司的发展超出了所有预期。
surpass, outstrip, exceed, outdo
lag behind, fall behind
v.
