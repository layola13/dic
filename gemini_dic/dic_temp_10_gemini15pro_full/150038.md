palpebral:/pælˈpiːbrəl/
adjective
眼睑的；眼皮的
Relating to the eyelids.
The palpebral reflex is a blink in response to a light tap on the forehead.  眼睑反射是指轻敲前额引起的眨眼。

The doctor examined the patient's palpebral fissure.  医生检查了病人的睑裂。

synonym:  eyelid
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps consider terms related to other parts of the eye like "corneal" or "retinal")
词性: adjective
