embody:/ɪmˈbɑːdi/ /ɛmˈbɒdi/| v.| 体现，使具体化，包含|to represent an idea or quality in a clear and obvious way; to include or contain something| The dancer embodies grace and elegance.  这位舞者展现了优雅和高贵。| synonyms: personify, represent, exemplify, incorporate | antonyms: exclude, omit | verb

embodiment:/ɪmˈbɑːdɪmənt/ /ɛmˈbɒdimənt/| n.| 体现，化身，具体化|a person or thing that represents an idea or quality in a clear and obvious way; the act of embodying something|  She is the embodiment of kindness. 她就是善良的化身。 | synonyms: personification, incarnation, epitome, representation | antonyms: abstraction | noun
