miniconglomerate: /ˌmɪni kənˈɡlɒmərət/ (UK) /ˌmɪni kənˈɡlɑːmərət/ (US) | n. | 小型企业集团，小型混合体 | A small conglomerate, a company that is formed by merging several smaller companies, often in unrelated industries. | The entrepreneur built a miniconglomerate by acquiring several small businesses.  这位企业家通过收购几家小企业建立了一个小型企业集团。|  conglomeration, combine | independent businesses, separate entities | noun
