ETYMOLOGY->
"Go-over" originates from the phrasal verb "to go over," which combines the verb "go" with the adverb "over." "Go" in this context means "to proceed" or "to move," while "over" indicates traversing or covering something completely. The combined meaning implies a thorough examination or review.
