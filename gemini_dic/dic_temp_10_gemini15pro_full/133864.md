in a way that does not give pleasure or satisfaction.
The experiment ended nongratifyingly, with no clear results.  这个实验结果不尽人意，没有得出明确的结论。
The play ended nongratifyingly, leaving the audience confused and disappointed. 这部戏的结局令人不满意，让观众感到困惑和失望。
近义词:unsatisfactorily, disappointingly, displeasingly
反义词:satisfactorily, pleasingly, gratifyingly
词性:adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
gratify (verb): from Latin gratificari "to give pleasure to," from gratus "pleasing, thankful, agreeable" + -ficare, combining form of facere "to make, do."
-ing (suffix): forms present participles and adjectives.
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs.
