non-u-ness:/ˌnɒn ˈjuːnɪs/ | n. |非上流社会特质；粗俗 |The state or quality of not being upper-class or posh, often in terms of behavior, language, or taste.  It reflects a perceived lack of refinement or sophistication according to traditional British class standards.| He exhibited a certain non-u-ness in his choice of attire. 他在着装上的选择展现出某种粗俗的特质。|Synonyms: vulgarity, commonness, inelegance |Antonyms: refinement, elegance, sophistication |noun
