overclinical:/ˌoʊvərˈklɪnɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于临床的；过分注重医学理论的；缺乏人情味的 | Excessively focused on medical details or procedures, often at the expense of empathy or holistic care.  Characterized by excessive detachment and an overly scientific or technical approach. | The doctor's approach was overclinical, making the patient feel like just another case study.  医生过于临床化的方式让病人觉得自己只是另一个案例研究。 |  The hospital's sterile and overclinical atmosphere made her feel uneasy. 医院里无菌和过于临床化的氛围让她感到不安。| 近义词： impersonal, detached, cold, sterile | 反义词： empathetic, compassionate, warm, humane | 词性: 形容词
