cozies:/ˈkoʊziz/| n.|舒适温暖的东西；保暖服；保暖套|Cozy things that make a place or person warm and comfortable, such as blankets, slippers, or warm clothing.|She snuggled up in her coziest blanket and read a book. 她裹着她最舒适的毯子看书。|近义词: comforts, snuggies|反义词: discomforts|词性:名词

cozies:/ˈkoʊziz/| v. (cozy 的第三人称单数)|使舒适；使温暖|To make something or someone cozy and comfortable.|She cozied up to the fire. 她偎依在火炉旁取暖。|近义词: snuggle, cuddle|反义词: expose|词性:动词
