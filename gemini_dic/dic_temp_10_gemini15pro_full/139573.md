obverse:/ˈɒbvɜːs/ (US) /ˈɑːbvɜːrs/ (UK)| n. adj.|正面，相反面|The obverse of the coin depicts the head of the monarch.  硬币的正面描绘了君主的头像。|The obverse side of the leaf is smooth and dark green. 叶子的正面光滑，呈深绿色。|近义词: front, face, head|反义词: reverse, inverse|词性:名词，形容词
obverse:/ˈɒbvɜːs/ (US) /ˈɑːbvɜːrs/ (UK)| adj.|正面的，显而易见的|His obverse reaction was to laugh, but inside he was worried. 他表面上的反应是笑，但内心却很担心。|The obverse effect of the policy was an increase in unemployment. 这项政策带来的显而易见的结果是失业率上升。|近义词: apparent, obvious, evident|反义词: hidden, concealed, obscure|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obversus*, past participle of *obvertere* "to turn towards," from *ob-* "towards" + *vertere* "to turn."
