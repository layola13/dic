designate:/ˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/ | v. adj.| 指定；指派；标明；命名 | To officially choose someone or something for a particular purpose, or to describe someone or something using a particular word or phrase. | The area has been designated as a national park.  这个地区已被指定为国家公园。| synonyms: appoint, nominate, select, choose, assign, name, label, identify, earmark | antonyms: dismiss, reject, disqualify | verb

designate:/ˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/ | adj.| 指定的；选定的 | Officially chosen for a particular purpose. | The designated driver is responsible for getting everyone home safely. 指定的司机负责把每个人安全送回家。| synonyms: appointed, nominated, selected, chosen, assigned, named, official | antonyms: unofficial, unselected | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *designatus, past participle of designare ("to mark out, describe, choose"), from de- ("out, from") + signare ("to mark"), from signum ("mark, sign").
