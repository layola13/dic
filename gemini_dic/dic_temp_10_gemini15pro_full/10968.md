arbitrarious: /ˌɑːrbɪˈtreəriəs/ | adj. | 任意的；武断的；专横的 | Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system. |  The choice of restaurant was completely arbitrary.  餐厅的选择完全是任意的。 | The CEO made an arbitrary decision to fire the marketing director. 首席执行官武断地决定解雇市场总监。 | 近义词: random, capricious, whimsical, despotic | 反义词: reasoned, logical, rational, objective | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *arbitrarius, from Latin arbiter (“judge, witness, umpire”), from ad- (“to”) + baetere (“to come, go”).
