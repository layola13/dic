nontobacco:/ˌnɑːn.təˈbæk.oʊ/ /ˌnɒn.təˈbæk.əʊ/
adjective
非烟草的；无烟的
Relating to or denoting products that do not contain tobacco.
例句：
The store sells a variety of nontobacco nicotine products.
这家商店出售各种非烟草尼古丁产品。

The rise of nontobacco vaping products is causing concern among health officials.
非烟草电子烟产品的兴起引起了卫生官员的担忧。

近义词：tobacco-free
反义词：tobacco, nicotine-containing
