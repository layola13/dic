ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *Mexicalli*, meaning "place where mexcalli grows".  Mexcalli is the Nahuatl (Aztec language) word for a type of agave plant used to make pulque, an alcoholic beverage.  Over time, the term became associated with the region and eventually the country of Mexico.
