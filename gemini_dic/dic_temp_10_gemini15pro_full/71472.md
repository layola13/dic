flamineous:/fləˈmɪniəs/
adjective
火焰般的；火红色的
Like fire; flaming; fiery.
The flamineous sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues of orange and red. 火红色的夕阳将天空染成了鲜艳的橙色和红色。
Near synonyms: fiery, flaming, incandescent, ablaze
Near antonyms: dark, dull, ashen, extinguished
Etymology: From Latin *flaminius*, from *flamen* ("flame, blaze").
