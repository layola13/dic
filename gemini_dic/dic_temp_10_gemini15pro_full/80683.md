gorgeously:/ˈɡɔːrdʒəsli/ | adv. |华丽地，灿烂地，极好地 | In a very beautiful or impressive way; extremely well | The sunset was gorgeously painted across the sky. 夕阳在天空华丽地描绘。| 近义词：beautifully, stunningly, splendidly, magnificently | 反义词：plainly, simply, uglily | 词性: 副词

词源:
From gorgeous + -ly.  "Gorgeous" ultimately derives from Old French *gorgias "elegant, fashionable,"  likely influenced by gorge "throat," suggesting a sense of richness or fullness.
