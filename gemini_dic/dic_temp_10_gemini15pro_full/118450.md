mescal:/mɛˈskæl/ | n. | 龙舌兰酒; 梅斯卡尔酒 | An alcoholic drink distilled from the agave plant, especially one made in Mexico.  Specifically, a distilled alcoholic beverage made from any of various species of agave, especially in Oaxaca, Mexico. Differs from tequila in that it is often made from varieties of agave other than blue agave, and it may be smoked during processing.|  I tried a smoky mescal at the bar last night. 我昨晚在酒吧里尝了一杯烟熏味的梅斯卡尔酒。 |  Synonyms: agave, tequila (sometimes), liquor | Antonyms:  N/A | noun


mescals:/mɛˈskælz/ | n. | 龙舌兰酒; 梅斯卡尔酒（复数） | Plural form of mescal, referring to multiple types or servings of the alcoholic beverage. | We sampled several different mescals during our trip to Oaxaca.  我们在瓦哈卡之旅中品尝了几种不同的梅斯卡尔酒。 | Synonyms: agaves, tequilas (sometimes, but less precise), liquors | Antonyms: N/A | noun
