beer-bellied:/ˈbɪrˌbɛlid/| adj.|啤酒肚的，大腹便便的|Having a large belly, typically as a result of drinking a lot of beer.|He's a beer-bellied man in his fifties. 他是一个五十多岁的大腹便便的男人。|Example: The beer-bellied man laughed heartily.  那个啤酒肚的男人开怀大笑。|Synonyms: pot-bellied, paunchy, rotund|Antonyms: slim, slender, thin|Adjective
