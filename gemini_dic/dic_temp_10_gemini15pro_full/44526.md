crossroads:/ˈkrɒsrəʊdz/ (British) /ˈkrɔːsroʊdz/ (American)| n. |十字路口；转折点|A place where two or more roads meet; a point of decision or change.|例句：We're at a crossroads in our lives. 我们正处于人生的十字路口。

例句：The town is located at a crossroads of major highways.  这个城镇位于几条主要公路的交汇处。|近义词：intersection, junction, turning point|反义词:dead end,  continuation|名词
