The nonperceptiblenesses of some changes make them difficult to study.  有些变化的难以察觉性使得它们难以研究。

n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + perceptible (capable of being perceived) + -ness (state or quality of being).  Perceptible comes from Latin perceptus, past participle of percipere (to perceive), from per- (thoroughly) + capere (to take).
