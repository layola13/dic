extendibilities:/ɪkˌstɛndɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可扩展性，可延展性|The quality of being able to be extended or expanded; the capacity for extension or enlargement.  This refers to the ability of something (like software, a system, or a concept) to be expanded upon or to have new features added to it without requiring major changes to its core structure.|This software platform is known for its extendibilities, allowing developers to easily integrate new functionalities.  这个软件平台以其可扩展性而闻名，允许开发者轻松集成新功能。|Near synonyms: scalability, flexibility, adaptability|Antonym: inflexibility|noun
