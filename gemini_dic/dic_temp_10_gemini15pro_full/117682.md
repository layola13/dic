meistersinger:/ˈmaɪstərˌzɪŋər/ | n. | 歌唱大师；名歌手 | A master singer, especially a member of one of the German guilds of singers flourishing in the 14th, 15th, and 16th centuries. |  Wagner's opera "Die Meistersinger von Nürnberg" is about a Meistersinger. 瓦格纳的歌剧《纽伦堡的名歌手》讲述的是一位歌唱大师的故事。 | synonyms: minstrel, bard, troubadour | antonyms: amateur, novice | noun
