一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they watching on TV? = 他们在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned in this class? = 你在这门课上学到了什么？
What has he accomplished this year? = 他今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been studying at university? = 她在大学里一直在学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was he thinking about when I interrupted him? = 我打断他时他在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will they eat for lunch tomorrow? = 他们明天午餐吃什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 她下学期会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have eaten by the time we arrive? = 等我们到达时他们会吃些什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
