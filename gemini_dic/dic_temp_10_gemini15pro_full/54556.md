dislocate:/ˌdɪsləˈkeɪt/ /ˌdɪsloʊˈkeɪt/| vt.|使脱臼，使错位；扰乱，打乱|He dislocated his shoulder in the accident. 他在事故中肩膀脱臼了。|近义词：displace, disrupt, disturb|反义词：order, organize|verb
