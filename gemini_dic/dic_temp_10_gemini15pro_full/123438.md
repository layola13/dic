monopolizations: /məˌnɒpəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ /məˌnɑːpəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
垄断；独占；专卖
The act of monopolizing something, gaining exclusive control over a commodity or service.
Microsoft was accused of monopolizations of the operating system market. 微软被指控垄断了操作系统市场。

The government is investigating the company's monopolizations of the pharmaceutical industry. 政府正在调查该公司对制药行业的垄断。

近义词: cornering, dominating, controlling
反义词: sharing, distributing, opening

词性: noun (plural)
