in-effect:/ɪn ɪˈfɛkt/
adjective
实际上；生效
actually in operation; effectively in reality
The new regulations are in effect from today. 新规章从今天开始生效。
synonyms: effectively, essentially, practically, virtually
antonyms: ineffective, nominally, theoretically
词性: 形容词


in-effect:/ɪn ɪˈfɛkt/
adverb
实际上；事实上
in reality, although perhaps not formally stated
He is, in effect, the manager of the company. 他实际上是这家公司的经理。
synonyms: practically, virtually, essentially, effectively
antonyms: nominally, theoretically
词性: 副词
