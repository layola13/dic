cull:/kʌl/
| vt.|挑选；剔除；宰杀|To select from a large quantity; obtain from a variety of sources.|例句：The farmer culled the weakest lambs from the flock. 农民从羊群中剔除了最弱的小羊。|近义词：select, choose, pick, glean, gather|反义词:reject, discard|verb

cull:/kʌl/
| n.|剔除；挑选|The act of selecting or gathering.|例句：The annual deer cull helps to control the population.  每年的鹿群剔除有助于控制鹿群数量。|近义词：selection, choice|反义词:rejection|noun
