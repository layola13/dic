bricking:/ˈbrɪkɪŋ/
v.|使（电子设备）变砖|Rendering a device unusable, like a brick.|My phone is bricking after the latest update. 我的手机在最新的更新后变砖了。|近义词：malfunctioning, crashing, failing|反义词：working, functioning
n.|变砖|The act of a device becoming unusable.|The bricking of my console was a disaster. 我的游戏机变砖了，真是个灾难。|近义词：failure, malfunction|反义词：operation, function
