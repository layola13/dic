fell-down:/fɛl daʊn/
adjective
倒下的，落下的，破败的
fallen into disrepair or dilapidation
The old fell-down shack was barely habitable. 那座破败的小屋几乎不能住人。
Near Antonyms: upright, standing, erect
Antonyms:  well-maintained, renovated, restored
adjective

fell-down:/fɛl daʊn/
noun
(informal) something that has fallen down, especially a dilapidated building.
a dilapidated or neglected building.
The property was a fell-down, but she saw its potential. 这处房产很破败，但她看到了它的潜力。
Near Synonyms: ruin, wreck, shambles
Antonyms:  mansion, palace, estate
noun
