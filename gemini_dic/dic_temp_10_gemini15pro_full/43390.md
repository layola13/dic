cranked-up|adj.|兴奋的，加强的，高亢的|Increased in intensity, volume, or energy. Often used to describe music, emotions, or activities.|The party was cranked-up and everyone was dancing. 派对气氛高涨，每个人都在跳舞。|
cranked-up|近义词|amplified, boosted, intensified, enhanced, increased, hyped-up|
cranked-up|反义词|toned-down, subdued, decreased, lowered|
cranked-up|词性|形容词|
cranked-up|词源|From "crank," originally referring to turning a crank to increase something (like volume on an old radio).  "Up" intensifies the meaning.|
