antimedical:/ˌæntiˈmedɪkəl/
adj.| 反医疗的; 反对医学的 | Opposed or hostile to medical treatment or the medical profession. | Some people hold antimedical views, preferring alternative therapies.  有些人持有反医疗的观点，更喜欢替代疗法。|  Example:  His antimedical stance prevented him from seeking conventional treatment for his illness. 他的反医疗立场使他无法寻求传统的疾病治疗方法。  |  Example:  She expressed antimedical sentiments, believing that natural remedies were superior. 她表达了反医疗的情绪，认为自然疗法更优越。 | synonyms: antiscientific, alternative, holistic  | antonyms: pro-medical, medical, scientific | word type: adjective


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to. From Greek anti-.
medical: relating to medicine. From Latin medicus, meaning "physician."
