depreciation:/dɪˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃn/ /dɪˌpriːsiˈeɪʃn/
n.
折旧；贬值；轻视
a reduction in the value of an asset over time, due in particular to wear and tear.
例句：The depreciation of the currency has made imports more expensive. 货币贬值导致进口商品更加昂贵。

例句：Annual depreciation is calculated at 20% of the original cost.  每年的折旧按原价的20%计算。

近义词：devaluation, amortization, decline
反义词：appreciation, increase, gain
