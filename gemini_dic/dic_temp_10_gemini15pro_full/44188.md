croakier:/ˈkroʊkiər/| adj.|更嘶哑的|Comparative form of croaky: more croaky.  Indicates a voice that is rougher, harsher, and lower than usual, often due to illness or overuse.|例句：Her voice was even croakier this morning than it was yesterday.  她今天早上的声音比昨天更沙哑了。

例句：After yelling at the football game, his voice became much croakier. 在足球比赛中大喊大叫之后，他的声音变得更加沙哑了。|近义词：hoarser, raspier, rougher|反义词:clearer, smoother, stronger|词性:adjective
词源:From croak + -y + -er. Ultimately from Old Norse *kraka.
