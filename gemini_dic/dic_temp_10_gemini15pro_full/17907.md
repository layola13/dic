beerhouse:/ˈbɪəhaʊs/| n.|啤酒屋；小酒店|A place where beer is sold and drunk; a pub or tavern.|例句：The village beerhouse was the center of social life.  村里的啤酒屋是社交生活的中心。

例句：We stopped at a beerhouse for a refreshing pint. 我们在一家啤酒屋停下来喝了一品脱清爽的啤酒。|近义词：pub, tavern, inn, bar|反义词: None (no single antonym; perhaps "temperance hall" in a historical context)|noun
