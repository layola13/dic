holier-than-thou:/ˈhoʊliər ðæn ˈðaʊ/
adjective
道貌岸然的，伪善的
Characterized by an attitude of moral superiority; self-righteous and sanctimonious.
例句：He had a holier-than-thou attitude that made everyone dislike him. 他有一种道貌岸然的态度，让每个人都讨厌他。

例句：She gave me a holier-than-thou lecture about the importance of recycling, even though I saw her throw a plastic bottle in the trash earlier. 她对我进行了一场关于回收重要性的伪善的教训，即使我之前看到她把一个塑料瓶扔进了垃圾桶。

近义词：sanctimonious, self-righteous, pious, moralistic
反义词：humble, modest, unpretentious
词性：adjective
