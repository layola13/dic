gorillas:/ɡəˈrɪləz/ | n. |大猩猩|A large ape native to forested regions of central Africa.  It is the largest living primate and is closely related to humans. |例句：The gorillas in the zoo were fascinating to watch. 动物园里的大猩猩非常引人注目。

例句：Mountain gorillas are critically endangered. 山地大猩猩极度濒危。|近义词：apes, primates|反义词:|noun
