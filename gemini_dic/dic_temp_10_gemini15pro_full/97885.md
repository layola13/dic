innate:/ɪˈneɪt/| adj.|天生的，固有的，先天的|Existing from birth; natural.|例句：He has an innate talent for music. 他有天生的音乐才能。|例句：Her innate sense of justice compelled her to speak out. 她天生的正义感迫使她直言不讳。|近义词：inborn, inherent, natural, intrinsic|反义词：acquired, learned, extrinsic|词性：形容词

词源：From Latin *innatus*, past participle of *innasci* (“to be born in”), from *in-* (“in, on”) + *nasci* (“to be born”).
