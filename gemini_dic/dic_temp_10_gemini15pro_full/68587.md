fanon:/ˈfænɒn/ (UK), /ˈfænɑːn/ (US)| n.|同人作品；同人设定|A body of fictional stories and other creative works based on an original work, created by fans rather than the original creators. It often expands upon, interprets, or reimagines elements of the original work.|The Star Wars fanon is vast and includes countless stories and characters. 星球大战的同人作品浩如烟海，包含了无数的故事和角色。

The fanon surrounding the character often depicts him as a misunderstood hero.  围绕这个角色的同人设定经常把他描绘成一个被误解的英雄。|近义词：headcanon, fanfiction|反义词：canon|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Derived from "fan" (short for "fanatic") + "-on" (a suffix used to form nouns denoting a body of works or ideas, as in "canon").  源自“fan”（“fanatic”的缩写）+“-on”（用于构成表示作品或思想主体的名词的后缀，如“canon”）。
