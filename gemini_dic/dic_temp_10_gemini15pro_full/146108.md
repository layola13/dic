overembellished: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈbɛlɪʃt/
| adj. | 过分装饰的；过分修饰的 | Excessively decorated or adorned; excessively elaborate in style or language.  Characterized by an overabundance of embellishments. | The wedding cake was overembellished with sugary flowers and figurines. 婚礼蛋糕上装饰了过多的糖花和小雕像。 | The writer's prose was overembellished and difficult to follow.  这位作家的散文过于华丽，难以理解。 | ornate, flamboyant, fussy, overdone, extravagant | simple, plain, understated, minimalist | adjective
