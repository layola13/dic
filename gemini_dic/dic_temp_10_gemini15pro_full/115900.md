marmorosis:/ˌmɑːrməˈroʊsɪs/| n. |大理石病，石末沉着病|A lung disease caused by inhaling marble dust, characterized by the deposition of marble particles in the lung tissue, leading to inflammation and fibrosis.|He was diagnosed with marmorosis after years of working in the marble quarry. 他在采石场工作多年后被诊断出患有大理石病。|近义词:pneumoconiosis, chalicosis|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific disease)|词性:noun
