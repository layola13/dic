impel:/ɪmˈpel/| v. |驱使，迫使|to force, drive, or urge forward|例句：He felt impelled to help the poor man. 他觉得有义务帮助那个可怜的人。|例句：She was impelled by a sense of duty to report the crime.  一种责任感迫使她去报告这起犯罪。|近义词：compel, force, urge, drive, motivate|反义词：restrain, deter, discourage|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impellere, from Latin impellō ("strike against, push, drive forward"), from in- ("in, into, on, upon, against") + pellō ("drive, push, beat").
