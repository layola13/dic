monanthous:/məˈnænθəs/
adjective
单花的
Having only one flower.
The monanthous orchid produces a single, exquisite bloom.  这种单花的兰花开出一朵精致的花。
Monanthous plants are relatively rare. 单花植物相对稀少。
Near synonyms: single-flowered, uniflorous
Antonyms: polyanthous, multiflorous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek monos "single, alone" + anthos "flower"
