anaesthetic:/ˌænəsˈθetɪk/ (UK), /ˌænɪsˈθetɪk/ (US)| n. adj.|麻醉剂，麻醉的|A substance that produces insensitivity to pain, either locally or generally.|The patient was given a general anaesthetic before the surgery. 病人在手术前进行了全身麻醉。|近义词：narcotic, anesthetic (US spelling)|反义词: stimulant|词性:noun, adjective

anaesthetic:/ˌænəsˈθetɪk/ (UK), /ˌænɪsˈθetɪk/ (US)| adj.|麻醉的|Relating to or producing anaesthesia.|The anaesthetic effect of the drug wore off after a few hours.  药物的麻醉效果几个小时后就消失了。|近义词：numbing, soporific|反义词: stimulating|词性:adjective

词源: From Greek ἀναισθητικός (anaisthetikos, “without sensation”), from ἀν- (an-, “without”) + αἴσθησις (aisthēsis, “sensation”).
