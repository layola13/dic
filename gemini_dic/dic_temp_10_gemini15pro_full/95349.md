incentivized:/ɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪzd/
|v.| 受激励的; 被鼓励的 | motivated or encouraged by the offer of something (such as money or a reward)|The company incentivized its employees to meet their sales targets by offering bonuses.  这家公司通过提供奖金来激励员工达到销售目标。| The sales team was incentivized to exceed their quota with the promise of a trip to Hawaii. 销售团队受到激励，如果超过配额，就能获得夏威夷之旅。| 近义词: motivated, encouraged, stimulated, spurred | 反义词: discouraged, demotivated |词性: 动词的过去分词形式，用作形容词
