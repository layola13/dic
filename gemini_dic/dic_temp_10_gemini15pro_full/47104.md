daughterless:/ˈdɔːtərləs/
adj.
无女儿的，没有女儿的
Lacking female offspring; having no daughters.
She was a daughterless woman, her only child a son. 她是一个没有女儿的女人，唯一的孩子是个儿子。
The daughterless queen worried about the succession. 没有女儿的女王担心继承问题。
近义词: childless, sonless (in the specific context of lacking daughters)
反义词: (No single direct antonym, but consider) having daughters, having female offspring
词性: adjective
