broil:/brɔɪl/| v. n.| 烤，炙烤；争吵，激怒|To cook something by exposing it to direct, intense heat, or to become very hot.  Also, to be in a state of great emotional agitation.|例句：I broiled the steak for five minutes on each side. 我把牛排两面各烤了五分钟。|近义词：grill, roast, scorch, burn, sear|反义词:freeze, chill|词性:动词

broil:/brɔɪl/| n.| 烤肉，炙烤；争吵|The act of broiling or the state of being broiled, or a heated argument or commotion.|例句：The salmon was perfectly cooked under the broil.  鲑鱼在烤箱里烤得恰到好处。|近义词：grilling, roasting|反义词:freezing|词性:名词
