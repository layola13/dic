awkwardest:/ˈɔːkwərdɪst/| adj.|最笨拙的，最尴尬的|Superlative form of awkward: most awkward.  Meaning extremely uncomfortable, clumsy, or embarrassing.|例句：That was the awkwardest silence I've ever experienced. 那是我经历过的最尴尬的沉默。

例句：He made the awkwardest attempt at a compliment. 他笨拙地尝试赞美，结果非常尴尬。

近义词：clumsiest, most uncomfortable, most embarrassing|反义词: graceful, elegant, comfortable|词性:形容词
