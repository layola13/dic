panhypopituitarism:/ˌpænˌhaɪpoʊpɪˈtuːɪtərɪzəm/| n.|垂体功能减退症|A condition in which the pituitary gland does not produce enough of most of its hormones.|The patient's fatigue and weight loss were attributed to panhypopituitarism.  病人的疲劳和体重减轻被认为是垂体功能减退症引起的。|Synonym: hypopituitarism|Antonym: hyperpituitarism|noun
