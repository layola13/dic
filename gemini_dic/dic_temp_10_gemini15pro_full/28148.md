PHRASE->
(Capitulate doesn't typically form strong set phrases like "what's up".  Its usage is more contextual. Here are some examples illustrating typical collocations.)

1. capitulate to demands: 屈服于要求
2. capitulate unconditionally: 无条件投降
3. capitulate to pressure: 屈服于压力
4. forced to capitulate: 被迫投降
5. capitulate to the enemy: 向敌人投降

词源：From Latin *capitulātus*, past participle of capitulāre (“to draw up in chapters, treat upon terms”), from capitulum (“chapter heading, small head”).
