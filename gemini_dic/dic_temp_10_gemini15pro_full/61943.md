emulsic:/ɪˈmʌlsɪk/ | adj. | 乳化的，乳状的 | Having the nature of, or resembling, an emulsion. | The substance had an emulsic appearance, like milk mixed with oil.  这种物质呈现乳状外观，就像牛奶和油混合在一起。 | Example 2: The emulsic mixture was difficult to separate.  这种乳状混合物难以分离。| 近义词: milky, creamy | 反义词: clear, transparent | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēmulsus*, past participle of *ēmulgēre* "to milk out, drain out," from *ex- "out" + *mulgēre* "to milk."  The word "emulsion" came first, and "emulsic" is derived from it as an adjective.
