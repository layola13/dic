dissenterism:/dɪˈsɛntərɪzəm/
n.
异议，持不同政见者
The act of dissenting; disagreement with the established or dominant opinion, especially in religious matters.
The government cracked down on all forms of political dissenterism. 政府镇压一切形式的政治异议。
nonconformity, heresy, unorthodoxy
conformity, orthodoxy
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dissenter + -ism.  Dissenter from dissent, from Old French dissentir, from Latin dissentire, from dis- "apart" + sentire "to feel".
