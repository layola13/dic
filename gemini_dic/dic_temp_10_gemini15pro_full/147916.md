overregulated:/ˌoʊvərˈreɡjuleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度监管的
Subject to excessive regulation; regulated too much.
The banking industry is overregulated, stifling innovation.  银行业监管过度，扼杀了创新。
Overregulated markets can hinder economic growth.  监管过度的市场会阻碍经济增长。
synonyms: over-regulated, excessively regulated
antonyms: unregulated, deregulated
词性: adjective
