mascot:/ˈmæskɑːt/ (US), /ˈmæskɒt/ (UK)| n.|吉祥物|A person, animal, or object adopted by a group as a symbolic figure, especially to bring them good luck.|The team's mascot is a fierce-looking eagle.|Synonym: emblem, symbol, icon|Antonym: jinx, curse|noun
