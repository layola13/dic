frowy:/ˈfraʊi/ | adj. | 霉臭的；闷热的；有霉味的 | Having a stale, musty smell, often from being closed up; stuffy and stale. | The frowy attic smelled of dust and mothballs.  阁楼霉臭，满是灰尘和樟脑丸的味道。| synonyms: musty, stale, stuffy, close, airless | antonyms: fresh, airy, ventilated | adjective

frowy:/ˈfraʊi/ | adj. |  邋遢的；不修边幅的 |  Untidy and slovenly in appearance. |  He looked frowy and unkempt after his long journey.  长途旅行后，他看起来邋遢不修边幅。 | synonyms: unkempt, slovenly, disheveled, scruffy | antonyms: neat, tidy, well-groomed | adjective
