epilepsies:/ˈɛpɪˌlɛpsiːz/
n.
癫痫症（复数）
A disorder in which nerve cell activity in the brain is disturbed, causing seizures.
My dog suffers from epilepsies. 我的狗患有癫痫。
近义词：seizure disorders, convulsions
反义词：(None -  Absence of a disease doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性: noun
