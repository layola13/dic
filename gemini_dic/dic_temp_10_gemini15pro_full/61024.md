elision:/ɪˈlɪʒən/
n.
省略；删节；省略的部分
The act of omitting a sound or a syllable when speaking.
He made several elisions when reading the poem aloud. 他在朗读这首诗时省略了几个音节。
omission, deletion, suppression
inclusion, addition, insertion
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin elisio, from Latin elidere (to strike out), from e- (out) + laedere (to strike).
