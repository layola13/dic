overoperates:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːpəˌreɪts/ | v. |过度运作，过度操作|To perform surgery more frequently than is medically necessary or beneficial.  The doctor was accused of overoperating on his patients, performing procedures that weren't clinically justified.| 例句：The surgeon overoperates, putting patients at unnecessary risk.  这个外科医生过度手术，使病人处于不必要的风险之中。

例句：Some doctors overoperate to increase their income. 一些医生为了增加收入而过度手术。| 近义词：overtreat, overdo | 反义词: undertreat | 词性：动词
