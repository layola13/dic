heedless:/ˈhiːdləs/| adj.|不注意的，粗心的，漠视的|Showing a reckless lack of attention or care. |Heedless of the danger, he rushed into the burning building.  他不顾危险，冲进了燃烧的建筑物。|Synonyms: careless, reckless, inattentive, unmindful, negligent |Antonyms: careful, cautious, attentive, mindful, heedful|adjective
