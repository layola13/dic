moating:/ˈmoʊtɪŋ/| n.|护城河，壕沟，隔离|The act of surrounding something with a moat, or the state of being surrounded by a moat.  The castle was protected by a deep moating. |例句：The moating around the castle provided a strong defense. 城堡周围的护城河提供了坚固的防御。

例句：The city's moating has long since been filled in. 这座城市的护城河早已被填平了。|近义词：ditch, trench, canal, moat|反义词:bridge, connection|词性:noun
