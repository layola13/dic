nestler:/ˈnestlər/ (US), /ˈneslə(r)/ (UK)| n.|筑巢者；恋巢的人；喜欢待在家里的人|A bird that is building a nest or someone who enjoys staying home and avoids going out.|例句：The robin is a diligent nestler, constantly adding twigs to its nest. 知更鸟是一个勤劳的筑巢者，不断地向它的巢里添加细枝。

例句：My brother is a real nestler; he prefers staying in and reading to going out to parties. 我弟弟是一个真正的恋家者；他喜欢待在家里看书，而不是出去参加聚会。|近义词：homebody, stay-at-home|反义词:gadabout, social butterfly|词性:noun
