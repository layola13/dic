malet: /ˈmælɪt/ | n. | 木槌；狼牙棒 | A heavy, short-handled hammer, often made of wood, used for driving wedges or chisels. |  He used a malet to drive the tent pegs into the ground. 他用木槌把帐篷钉钉在地上。 |  The sculptor shaped the stone with a mallet and chisel. 雕塑家用木槌和凿子塑造石头。 | 近义词: hammer, maul | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源:
From Old French *malet (Modern French maillet), diminutive of mail “hammer, mallet,” from Latin malleus “hammer.”
