back-to-the-drawing-board:/ˌbæk tə ðə ˈdrɔːɪŋ bɔːrd/
idiom
回到绘图板；重新开始；从头再来
The project failed, so it's back to the drawing board.  这个项目失败了，所以我们得重新开始。
The design was rejected, so it was back to the drawing board for the team.  设计被否决了，所以团队只好回到绘图板重新设计。
近义词: start over, begin again, from scratch
反义词: succeed, complete, finish
词性: idiom (习语)
