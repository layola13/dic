mayo:/ˈmeɪoʊ/ (US), /ˈmeɪəʊ/ (UK)| n.|蛋黄酱; mayonnaise的简称|A creamy condiment made from oil, egg yolks, and vinegar or lemon juice.|例句：Do you want mayo on your sandwich? 你想在你的三明治里加蛋黄酱吗？

例句：I always put mayo in my tuna salad. 我总是在我的金枪鱼沙拉里放蛋黄酱。|近义词：mayonnaise|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps alternatives like mustard, ketchup)|词性:noun
