misguide:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪd/ | v. | 误导，使误入歧途 | to lead someone in the wrong direction or into a mistaken belief or action. |  The advertisement misguides consumers about the product's true capabilities. 这则广告误导了消费者对该产品真正功能的理解。

The tourists were misguided by the faulty map.  游客被错误的地图误导了。

近义词: mislead, deceive, delude | 反义词: guide, direct, inform | 词性: 动词
