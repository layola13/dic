coadministration:/koʊədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/| n.|共同给药，合并用药|The coadministration of these two drugs can lead to adverse effects. 这两种药物的共同给药会导致不良反应。|例句：The study investigated the coadministration of vitamins C and D.  这项研究调查了维生素C和D的共同给药。

例句：Coadministration of certain medications can enhance their effectiveness.  某些药物的合并使用可以增强它们的疗效。

近义词：concomitant administration, combined administration|反义词:separate administration|词性:名词

词源: co- (together) + administration (the process of giving something)
