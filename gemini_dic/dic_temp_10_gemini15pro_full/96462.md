individualistic:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|个人主义的；个人主义者的|Characterized by individualism; independent in thought and action; stressing individual liberty and self-reliance.|例句：She has a very individualistic style of dress. 她有着非常个性化的穿衣风格。|近义词：independent, self-reliant, nonconformist, egoistic|反义词：collectivist, conformist|词性:形容词

individualistic:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|个人主义的；个人主义者的|He took an individualistic approach to the problem. 他用一种个人主义的方式来处理这个问题。|近义词：independent, self-reliant, nonconformist, egoistic|反义词：collectivist, conformist|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From individual + -istic.  "Individual" comes from Late Middle English: from medieval Latin individualis, from Latin individuus ‘indivisible’ (from in- ‘not’ + dividuus ‘divisible’).
