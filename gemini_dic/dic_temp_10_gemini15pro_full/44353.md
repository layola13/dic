cross-bencher:/ˈkrɒsˌbɛntʃər/ /ˈkrɔːsˌbɛntʃər/| n.|无党派议员；中立议员|A member of the House of Lords (UK upper house of Parliament) or other legislative body who is not formally aligned with any political party and sits on the crossbenches.|The cross-bencher argued for a more balanced approach to the legislation.|近义词：independent, neutral|反义词:partisan|词性:noun

cross-bencher:/ˈkrɒsˌbɛntʃər/ /ˈkrɔːsˌbɛntʃər/| adj.|无党派议员的；中立议员的|Relating to a cross-bencher.|The cross-bencher peer raised concerns about the bill.|近义词：independent, neutral|反义词:partisan|词性:adjective
