nonperverse:/ˌnɒnpərˈvɜːrs/
adjective
正常的，不反常的
Not perverse; normal, conventional, or conforming to accepted standards.
Her reaction to the news was entirely nonperverse, a mixture of relief and joy. 她对这个消息的反应完全正常，既感到宽慰又感到高兴。

The psychologist assured him that his desires were entirely nonperverse. 心理学家向他保证，他的愿望完全正常。

Synonyms: normal, conventional, standard, typical, regular, orthodox, natural, usual, common, ordinary
Antonyms: perverse, abnormal, deviant, atypical, irregular, unconventional, unnatural, unusual, uncommon, extraordinary
词性: 形容词
