inside:/ɪnˈsaɪd/ (US), /ɪnˈsaɪd/ (UK)| adj. n. adv. prep.|内部的；里面；内部；在…里面|The inside of the box was lined with velvet. 盒子内部衬着天鹅绒。|近义词：interior, inner, within|反义词：outside, exterior|词性: adjective

inside:/ɪnˈsaɪd/ (US), /ɪnˈsaɪd/ (UK)| n.|内部；里面|The inside of the house was surprisingly spacious. 房子的内部出奇地宽敞。|近义词：interior, inner part|反义词：outside, exterior|词性: noun

inside:/ɪnˈsaɪd/ (US), /ɪnˈsaɪd/ (UK)| adv.|在里面；在内部|The children were playing inside. 孩子们在里面玩耍。|近义词：within, indoors|反义词：outside, outdoors|词性: adverb

inside:/ɪnˈsaɪd/ (US), /ɪnˈsaɪd/ (UK)| prep.|在…里面；在…内部|The cat was hiding inside the cupboard. 猫藏在橱柜里。|近义词：within, in|反义词：outside, out of|词性: preposition

词源: From Old English *innesīde, from inne (“within, inside”) + sīde (“side”). Cognate with Dutch inside, German Innenseite.
