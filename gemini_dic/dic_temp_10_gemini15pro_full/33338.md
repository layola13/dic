chuk:/tʃʌk/| n. |扔, 抛, 咯咯声|A sharp, explosive sound, often made by a chicken. Also, the act of throwing something casually. | The hen gave a loud chuk and then laid an egg. 母鸡咯咯叫了一声，然后下了一个蛋。 | He chukked the ball to his friend. 他把球扔给了他的朋友。| synonyms: toss, fling, pitch, cluck | antonyms: catch, hold, receive | noun
chuk:/tʃʌk/| v. |扔, 抛, 发出咯咯声|To throw something casually or to make a sharp, explosive sound like a chicken. | She chukked the empty can in the bin. 她把空罐扔进了垃圾桶。|The chickens were chukking contentedly in the yard.  鸡在院子里咯咯地叫着，显得很满足。| synonyms: toss, fling, pitch, cluck | antonyms: catch, hold, receive | verb
