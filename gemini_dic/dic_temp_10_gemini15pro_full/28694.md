carling:/ˈkɑːlɪŋ/ | n. | 卡林啤酒；船的龙骨，支架 | A type of strong lager originally brewed in Canada. Also, a nautical term for a fore-and-aft member of a ship's framing. | 例句：He ordered a carling at the pub. 他在酒吧点了一杯卡林啤酒。

例句：The carling of the ship was damaged in the storm. 船的龙骨在暴风雨中受损了。| 近义词：lager, keel, brace | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
