antispiritualists:/ˌæntiˈspɪrɪtʃuəlɪsts/
| n. | 反唯灵论者 | People who oppose spiritualism or belief in spirits.  Those who believe that phenomena attributed to spirits can be explained by natural causes, fraud, or delusion. | The antispiritualists argued that the supposed communication with the dead was simply trickery.  反唯灵论者认为所谓的与死者沟通只是骗术。 | skeptics, materialists, rationalists | spiritualists, believers | noun
