beachhead:/ˈbiːtʃhɛd/| n.|滩头阵地，立足点|A position gained by an army that has landed on an enemy shore, which it can use as a base for further advance into enemy territory.  A secure initial position that can be used for further advancement.|The troops established a beachhead and began to move inland.  部队建立了滩头阵地，并开始向内陆推进。|The company's new product gave them a beachhead in the competitive market.  该公司的新产品使他们在竞争激烈的市场中获得了一席之地。|foothold, bridgehead, base, starting point|hindrance, obstacle|noun
