alumnuses: /əˈlʌmnaɪ/ /əˈlʌmniː/ | n. | 校友（尤指男校友，也指男女合校的校友）|  A graduate or former student of a particular school, college, or university, especially a male graduate.  Formerly used to refer to male graduates, but now increasingly used to refer to graduates of either sex.  The plural of alumnus when referring to a group of men and women. |  The university invited all its alumnuses to the reunion. 大学邀请了所有的校友参加聚会。| synonyms: graduates, alumni, former students | antonyms:  current students, undergraduates, freshmen | noun
