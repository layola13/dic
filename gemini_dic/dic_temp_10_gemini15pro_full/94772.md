imprudently:/ɪmˈpruːdntlɪ/| adv.|轻率地，不谨慎地|Not wisely or carefully; without good judgment.|He imprudently invested all his money in a single stock. 他轻率地把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|She spoke imprudently and revealed confidential information. 她说话轻率，泄露了机密信息。|近义词: unwisely, rashly, foolishly, recklessly|反义词: wisely, cautiously, prudently|词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *imprudent-ia, from Latin imprudent-, imprudēns imprudent (see imprudent).
