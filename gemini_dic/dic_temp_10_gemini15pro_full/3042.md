aerobus:/ˈɛərəˌbʌs/| n.|机场巴士|A bus that carries passengers to and from an airport.|The aerobus runs every 30 minutes. 机场巴士每30分钟一班。
The aerobus was packed with tourists. 机场巴士上挤满了游客。|近义词：airport shuttle, airport bus|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to aircraft) + bus.  "Aero" comes from Greek ἀήρ (aēr) meaning "air".

USAGE->
The aerobus is a convenient way to get to the city center.
乘坐机场巴士是到达市中心的一种便捷方式。

The aerobus departs from the terminal every hour.
机场巴士每小时从航站楼出发。


PHRASE->
take the aerobus = 乘机场巴士
catch the aerobus = 赶机场巴士
the aerobus schedule = 机场巴士时刻表
aerobus to the airport = 前往机场的机场巴士
aerobus from the airport = 从机场出发的机场巴士
