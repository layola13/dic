incriminator:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪtər/ | n. |控告者；告发者；检举者|A person who incriminates another, especially someone who presents evidence or accusations that implicate another in a crime or wrongdoing.  | The incriminator testified against the mob boss.  控告者出庭作证指控黑帮老大。|  The police were looking for the incriminator who had tipped them off. 警方正在寻找向他们告密的人。 |Synonyms: accuser, informant, witness, betrayer | Antonyms: defender, alibi, exonerator| noun
