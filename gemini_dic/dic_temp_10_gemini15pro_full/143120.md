outburst:/ˈaʊtbɜːrst/ (US), /ˈaʊtbɜːsts/ (UK)| n.|爆发，迸发；突发|A sudden release or occurrence of something.|There was an outburst of cheering when the team scored. 当球队得分时，爆发出一阵欢呼声。|eruptions, explosions, fits|suppressions, calmness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
