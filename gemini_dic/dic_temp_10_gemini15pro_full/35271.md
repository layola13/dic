clustery:/ˈklʌstəri/
adjective
成群的，簇状的
growing or appearing in clusters
The grapes were clustery and ripe. 葡萄成串地成熟了。
Synonyms: clustered, grouped, bunched
Antonyms: scattered, dispersed
Part of Speech: adjective

clustery:/ˈklʌstəri/
adjective
群集的, 聚集在一起的
gathered closely together in a group or cluster.
The stars appeared clustery in the night sky.  星星在夜空中成群出现。
Synonyms:  concentrated, dense, thick
Antonyms: sparse, scattered, spread out
Part of Speech: adjective
