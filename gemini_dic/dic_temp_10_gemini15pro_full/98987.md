inter-university:/ˌɪntərˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəti/
adjective
大学间的；校际的
Existing or occurring between universities.
The inter-university competition attracts students from all over the country. 校际比赛吸引了来自全国各地的学生。
The inter-university debate was a stimulating intellectual event. 校际辩论是一场激动人心的智力盛宴。
Synonyms: intercollegiate
Antonyms: intra-university (although less common)
词性: adjective
