hypermasculine:/ˌhaɪpərˈmæskjʊlɪn/| adj.|极度男性化的，超级阳刚的|Exaggeratedly masculine; exhibiting extreme masculine traits often considered stereotypically male.|例句：He had a hypermasculine attitude, always bragging about his physical strength. 他有着极度男性化的态度，总是吹嘘自己的体力。|近义词：machismo, overly masculine|反义词：feminine, effeminate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hyper- + masculine.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "beyond," derived from the Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ). "Masculine" comes from the Old French "masculin," ultimately from the Latin "masculinus," meaning "male," from "masculus" ("male").
