gymnospermous:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈspɜːməs/| adj.|裸子植物的|Having seeds that are not enclosed in an ovary, as in conifers and cycads.|Conifers are gymnospermous plants.  针叶树是裸子植物。|The pine tree is a typical gymnospermous species. 松树是典型的裸子植物物种。|Synonyms:  naked-seeded, achlamydeous|Antonyms: angiospermous|adjective
