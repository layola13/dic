enlightenedly:/ɪnˈlaɪtndli/
adverb
开明地；启发地；有见识地
In a manner that demonstrates understanding and awareness, often with a progressive or tolerant perspective.
She spoke enlightenedly about the need for social reform. 她开明地谈到了社会改革的必要性。
The company is enlightenedly embracing new technologies. 这家公司开明地接受新技术。
Synonyms: wisely, judiciously, sagaciously, perceptively, intelligently, knowingly
Antonyms: ignorantly, foolishly, blindly, narrow-mindedly, unintelligently
词性: 副词

词源:
From enlighten + -ed + -ly.  Enlighten comes from en- "in" + light, meaning to bring light to, to illuminate.
