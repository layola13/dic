mimeograph:/ˈmɪmiəɡræf/ | n. vt.|油印机，复印机；用油印机复印|A duplicating machine that uses a stencil through which ink is pressed.  Also, the copy produced by such a machine. |例句：The teacher mimeographed the exam papers for the class. 老师用油印机复印了考试卷子给全班同学。

例句：The mimeograph was a common sight in offices before the advent of photocopying. 在影印技术出现之前，油印机在办公室里很常见。|近义词：duplicate, copy, reproduce, photocopy|反义词:original|词性:名词, 动词
