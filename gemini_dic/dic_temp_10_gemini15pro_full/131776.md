nonconnectively:/ˌnɒnkəˈnɛktɪvli/ | adv. | 非连接地，不连贯地 |  In a way that is not connected or continuous; disjointedly. | The ideas were presented nonconnectively, making it difficult to follow the argument.  这些想法的呈现方式不连贯，让人难以理解论证。 | disjointedly, incoherently, separately | connectively, coherently, continuously | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + connective + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Connective" refers to something that connects or joins. "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
