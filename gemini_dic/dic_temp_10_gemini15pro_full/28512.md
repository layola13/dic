cardgraphic:/ˈkɑːrdˌɡræfɪk/ | n. | 卡片图形; 图形卡 | A visual representation, often on a card, displaying information graphically.  This can refer to the design or image itself, or a physical card containing such a graphic.  It could also, less commonly, refer to a graphics card in a computer. | The cardgraphic on the front of the birthday card was a cute puppy. 生日卡片上的图形是一只可爱的小狗。| The museum used cardgraphics to illustrate the timeline of ancient Egypt.  博物馆用卡片图形来说明古埃及的年代顺序。| synonyms: infographic, visual aid, graphic, illustration, image | antonyms: text, words, writing | noun
