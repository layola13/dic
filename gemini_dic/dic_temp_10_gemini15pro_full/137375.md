nontemperateness:/ˌnɑːnˈtɛmpərɪtnəs/ | n. | 不节制，无节制 | Lack of moderation or self-restraint, especially in satisfying desires or appetites. |  His nontemperateness with food led to health problems. 他饮食无度导致了健康问题。| synonyms: intemperance, immoderation, excess, unrestraint | antonyms: temperance, moderation, restraint, self-control | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + temperateness (the state of being temperate).  "Temperateness" itself comes from the Latin "temperantia," meaning moderation or self-control.
