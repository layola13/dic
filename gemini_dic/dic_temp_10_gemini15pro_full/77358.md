gauffer:/ˈɡɔːfər/ (US) /ˈɡɒfər/ (UK)| v. n.| 压花，用(热)模子压印；(用热)模子压印的图案或花样|To crimp, pleat, or emboss a material, especially cloth or paper, using a heated iron or die.  A pattern or design created by gauffering.| The dressmaker gauffered the silk to create a textured effect.  裁缝师用压花的方法使丝绸产生纹理效果。| emboss, crimp, pleat | smooth, flatten | verb, noun
