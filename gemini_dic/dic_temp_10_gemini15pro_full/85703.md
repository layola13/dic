haversian:/həˈvɜːrʒən/
adjective
哈弗氏的；哈弗斯氏的
Relating to or denoting the canals in bone tissue that contain blood vessels and nerves.
The haversian canals are a key feature of compact bone. 哈弗斯管是密质骨的一个关键特征。
Osteons are composed of concentric lamellae surrounding a central haversian canal. 骨单位由围绕中心哈弗斯管的同心薄片组成。
近义词: bone, osseous
反义词: None
词性: adjective

Etymology: Named after Clopton Havers (1657–1702), English physician and anatomist.
