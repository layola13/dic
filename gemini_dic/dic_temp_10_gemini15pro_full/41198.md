coquet:/koʊˈkɛt/ /ˈkɒkɛt/| v. |调情，卖弄风情|To behave in such a way as to suggest a playful sexual attraction; flirt.|She coquetted with the bartender to get free drinks. 她跟酒保调情以获得免费饮料。|近义词：flirt, tease, charm|反义词：ignore, repel|词性:动词
coquette:/koʊˈkɛt/ /ˈkɒkɛt/| n. |卖弄风情的女人，风骚女子|A woman who flirts lightheartedly.|She's a real coquette, always batting her eyelashes at men. 她真是个卖弄风情的女人，总是对男人抛媚眼。|近义词：flirt, vamp|反义词：prude|词性:名词
coquettish:/koʊˈkɛtɪʃ/ /kɒˈkɛtɪʃ/| adj. |卖弄风情的，娇媚的|Behaving in a flirtatious manner.|She gave him a coquettish smile. 她给了他一个娇媚的微笑。|近义词：flirtatious, playful, teasing|反义词：serious, demure|词性:形容词

词源:
From French coquette, from coq 'cock, rooster'.  The original sense was 'vain or conceited woman'.
