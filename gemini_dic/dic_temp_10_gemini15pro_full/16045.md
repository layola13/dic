bandelet:/ˈbændəlɪt/ |n.| 小旗; 细带;  |A small flag or streamer, especially one attached to a lance.  Also, a narrow band or strip of material.| Knights often carried bandelets on their lances during tournaments. 骑士在比赛中经常在长矛上挂着小旗。 The dress was decorated with delicate bandelets of silk. 这件衣服装饰着精致的丝绸细带。|近义词: pennant, streamer, ribbon, band|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old French *bandelette, diminutive of bande ("band, strip"), from Frankish *binda, from Proto-Germanic *bindaną ("to bind"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰendʰ- ("to bind").
