dearest:/ˈdɪərɪst/| adj. n.|最亲爱的，最珍贵的；最亲爱的人|Most loved; most cherished.  Can also be used as a noun to refer to a person who is most loved.|例句：My dearest friend, I miss you so much. 我最亲爱的朋友，我非常想念你。

|例句：You are my dearest. 你是我最亲爱的人。|近义词：beloved, darling, precious, closest|反义词：despised, hated|词性:形容词,名词
词源->From Middle English dereworthe, equivalent to dere (adjective and adverb) + -worthe “worthy, of value.”  Influenced by dear (adjective).
