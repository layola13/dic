pacification:/ˌpæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 平定；安抚；绥靖 |The act of pacifying or the state of being pacified.  The process of bringing peace or calm to a situation or place, often through force or political negotiation.| The pacification of the rebels took several months.  平息叛乱花了几个月的时间。 | synonyms: appeasement, conciliation, propitiation, subjugation | antonyms: agitation, incitement, provocation | noun

pacify:/ˈpæsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 平息；安抚；绥靖 |To bring or restore to a state of peace or tranquility; quiet; calm. To appease or soothe someone who is angry or upset.| The government tried to pacify the protesters with promises of reform. 政府试图用改革的承诺来安抚抗议者。| synonyms: appease, placate, soothe, mollify | antonyms: agitate, incite, provoke | verb
