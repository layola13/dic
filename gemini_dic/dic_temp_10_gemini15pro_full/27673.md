candiru:/kænˈdɪruː/
n.
牙签鱼，寄生鲇
a small, slender, parasitic catfish of the Amazon basin, reputed to enter the human urethra.
The candiru is a parasitic catfish feared in the Amazon. 牙签鱼是一种在亚马逊河令人恐惧的寄生鲶鱼。
Candiru are said to be attracted to urine. 据说牙签鱼会被尿液吸引。
Synonyms: toothpick fish, vampire fish
Antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)
