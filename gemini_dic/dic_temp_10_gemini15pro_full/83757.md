hair-raisingly:/ˈhɛərˌreɪzɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人毛骨悚然的，极其惊险的 |  In a way that causes excitement and fear.  It is derived from the sensation of one's hair standing on end due to fright. | The car chase scene was hair-raisingly exciting. 汽车追逐的场面惊险刺激，令人毛骨悚然。 | Synonyms: terrifyingly, chillingly, frightfully | Antonyms: calmly, peacefully | adverb
