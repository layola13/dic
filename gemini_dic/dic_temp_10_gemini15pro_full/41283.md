cordial:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl/ (US) /ˈkɔːdiəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|热情友好的；兴奋的；烈性甜酒；|Warm and friendly.|例句：We received a cordial welcome. 我们受到了热情的欢迎。|例句：She gave us a cordial smile and invited us in. 她热情地微笑着邀请我们进去。|近义词：friendly, warm, affable, amiable, genial, hearty|反义词：unfriendly, cold, hostile|词性:形容词

cordial:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl/ (US) /ˈkɔːdiəl/ (UK)| n.|烈性甜酒；|A sweet, strong, alcoholic drink.|例句：After dinner, they served a delicious cordial.  晚餐后，他们端上了一杯美味的甜酒。|例句：She offered him a glass of homemade cordial. 她给他倒了一杯自制的甜酒。|近义词：liqueur|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "soft drink")|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cordial* and Late Latin *cordiālis* "of or for the heart, heartfelt," from Latin *cord-* "heart."  The sense of a stimulating medicine or drink came from the belief that it invigorated the heart.
