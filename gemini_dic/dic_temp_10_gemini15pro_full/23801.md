brazen:/ˈbreɪzn/| adj.| 厚颜无耻的，肆无忌惮的；黄铜制的|  bold and shameless. Made of brass.| He told a brazen lie. 他说了一个弥天大谎。|近义词：bold, shameless, impudent, audacious|反义词：shy, timid, modest, humble|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *brasen, from Old English bræsen (“made of brass”), from bræs (“brass”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brasą (“brass”).
