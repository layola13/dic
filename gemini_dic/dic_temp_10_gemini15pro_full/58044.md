drollest:/ˈdroʊləst/| adj.|最滑稽的，最古怪有趣的|Most amusing or comical in an odd or whimsical way.  The drollest thing happened on the way to work today – a squirrel wearing a tiny hat ran across the road.|例句：His drollest stories always involve his eccentric uncle. 他最滑稽的故事总是和他古怪的叔叔有关。

例句：She told the drollest joke I’ve ever heard. 她讲了我听过的最滑稽的笑话。|近义词：funniest, most amusing, wittiest, most comical|反义词：seriousest, dullest, most boring|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle French *drol, from Old French drue “sweetheart,” “lover,” of Germanic origin.  The sense development went from “jester,” “buffoon” to “amusing,” “funny.”
