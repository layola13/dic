bollocking:/ˈbɒləkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːləkɪŋ/ (US)| n.|斥责，痛骂|A severe scolding or reprimand.  A strong verbal attack.  It often implies anger and frustration on the part of the person doing the bollocking.|例句：He got a right bollocking from his boss for being late. 他因为迟到被老板狠狠地骂了一顿。

例句：She gave him a bollocking for forgetting their anniversary. 她因为他忘记了他们的结婚纪念日而痛骂了他一顿。|近义词：scolding, reprimand, telling-off, dressing-down, chewing-out|反义词:praise, commendation|词性:noun
