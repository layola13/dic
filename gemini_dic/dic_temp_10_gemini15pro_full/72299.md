flounderingly:/ˈflaʊndərɪŋli/
adverb
笨拙地；挣扎地；踉跄地
in a floundering manner; clumsily and awkwardly.
The company is flounderingly trying to adapt to the new market.  这家公司正笨拙地试图适应新的市场。
He flounderingly explained his tardiness. 他结结巴巴地解释他迟到的原因。

近义词:awkwardly, clumsily, stumblingly, hesitantly
反义词:gracefully, smoothly, deftly, adroitly
词性:adverb
