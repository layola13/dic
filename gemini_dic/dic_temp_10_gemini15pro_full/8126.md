anti-bible:/ˌæntiˈbaɪbl/ | n. |反圣经；反对圣经的言论或著作|Something that is against the Bible, or opposes its teachings.  It can refer to a specific text or a general attitude or philosophy. |He considered Nietzsche's philosophy to be an anti-bible. 他认为尼采的哲学是反圣经的。| The politician's rhetoric was seen as anti-bible by many religious groups.  这位政治家的言论被许多宗教团体视为反圣经。 |Near Synonyms: blasphemous, sacrilegious, heretical | Antonyms: scriptural, biblical |noun
