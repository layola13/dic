一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? 你最近学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? 他们在看电影前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? 他们已经讨论了几个小时什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? 他们在会议上将要展示什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? 到明天他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习了什么多久？
