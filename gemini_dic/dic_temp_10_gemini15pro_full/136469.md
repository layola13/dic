nonrevivals:/ˌnɒnriˈvaɪvəlz/
noun
非复兴；未复兴的事物
Things that are not revivals; instances of something not being revived or brought back.
The museum showcased several exhibits of nonrevivals, focusing on forgotten crafts.  博物馆展出了几个非复兴的展览，重点展出被遗忘的工艺。
The theater company's season of nonrevivals featured all-new works.  这家剧团的非复兴季以全新作品为特色。
synonyms: new works, original productions, debuts
antonyms: revivals, restorations, comebacks
