n.
平等；均等；相同；类似
the quality or state of being equal, similar, or identical.
例句：The principle of equinities underpins democratic systems. 平等原则是民主制度的基础。

例句：There's a surprising equinities between the two seemingly disparate cultures. 这两种看似迥异的文化之间存在着惊人的相似性。

近义词：equality, similarity, likeness, sameness, uniformity, parity
反义词：inequality, disparity, difference, dissimilarity

词性: noun


词源:
From Late Latin *aequinitas, from Latin aequus (“equal, level, fair, just, calm”).
