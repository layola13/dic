merits:/ˈmerɪts/| n. |优点，价值，功绩；是非曲直|Something that deserves praise or reward. The intrinsic value of something.|The merits of the case are clear.  这案子的是非曲直很清楚。|synonyms: advantages, strengths, virtues, benefits, values|antonyms:  demerits, flaws, shortcomings, weaknesses, disadvantages|noun

merits:/ˈmerɪts/| v. |值得，应得|Deserve or be worthy of (something, especially reward, punishment, or attention).| This idea merits further consideration. 这个想法值得进一步考虑。|synonyms: deserves, warrants, justifies, earns|antonyms: forfeits, loses|verb
