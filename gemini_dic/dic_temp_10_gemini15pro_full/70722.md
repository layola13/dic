finchbacked:/ˈfɪntʃbækt/| adj.|雀背的；有雀斑的|Having a back the color of a finch, often streaked or speckled.|The finchbacked thrush is a beautiful bird.  雀背画眉是一种美丽的鸟。|The old woman had finchbacked hands. 老妇人的手背上长满了雀斑。|近义词：speckled, freckled|反义词:clear, unblemished|词性:形容词

词源:
finch (n.) + backed (adj.)

finch: from Old English finc, of imitative origin.
backed: from back (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).
