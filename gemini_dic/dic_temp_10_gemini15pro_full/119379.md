microcrystalline:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | adj. |微晶的；由微小晶体组成的 |Consisting of minute crystals, too small to be seen with the naked eye but detectable by other means. |The rock is composed of microcrystalline quartz. 这块岩石由微晶石英组成。|The sample exhibited a microcrystalline structure under the microscope.  在显微镜下，样品呈现出微晶结构。|近义词:fine-grained, cryptocrystalline |反义词:macrocrystalline, coarsely crystalline|词性:形容词

Etymology:
micro- (Greek μικρός mikrós) meaning "small" + crystalline (from crystal + -ine, meaning "of or pertaining to crystals")
