muscoid:/ˈmʌskɔɪd/| adj. n.| 苔藓状的，苔藓动物；| resembling moss; relating to or resembling flies.  | The decaying log was covered in a muscoid growth.  腐烂的木头上覆盖着一层苔藓状的东西。| The muscoid fly landed on the rotting fruit. 苔藓蝇落在腐烂的水果上。 |近义词：mossy, bryophytic (for the adjective); fly-like (for the noun)|反义词: (No clear antonym for mossy appearance);  (For the noun, perhaps "butterfly," offering a contrasting insect) |词性: adjective, noun

muscoid:/ˈmʌskɔɪd/| n.| 苔藓虫，苔藓动物|any of various invertebrates, typically small and aquatic, that resemble moss in appearance.|  These muscoids form colonies on submerged rocks. 这些苔藓虫在水下岩石上形成群落。|  The researcher studied the intricate structure of the muscoid's exoskeleton. 研究人员研究了苔藓虫外骨骼的复杂结构。| 近义词：bryozoan|反义词:  (No clear antonym) |词性: noun
