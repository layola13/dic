natality:/neɪˈtælɪti/
n.
出生率
The ratio of live births to total population.
The natality rate has been declining in recent years.  近年来出生率一直在下降。
近义词：birth rate, fertility rate
反义词：mortality rate
词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *natalitas, from Latin natalis (“of or relating to birth”), from natus (“born”), past participle of nasci (“to be born”).
