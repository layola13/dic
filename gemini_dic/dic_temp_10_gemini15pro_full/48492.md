deeper:/ˈdiːpər/| adj.|更深的，更深层次的|Comparative form of "deep."  Indicates a greater degree of depth, either literally or figuratively. |The well is deeper than I thought. 这口井比我想象的要深。

The deeper meaning of the poem eluded me.  这首诗的深层含义我无法理解。|近义词：more profound, further, lower|反义词：shallower, higher|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *depper, from Old English dēopre (“deeper, more deeply”), comparative of dēop (“deep”). Cognate with Dutch dieper (“deeper”), German tiefer (“deeper”), Swedish djupare (“deeper”).
