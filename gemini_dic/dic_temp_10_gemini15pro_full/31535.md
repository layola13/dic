charterer:/ˈtʃɑːrtərər/ | n. | 租船人；包租人 | A person or company that hires or leases a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle for their own exclusive use. | The charterer planned a trip to the Caribbean.  租船人计划去加勒比海旅行。 |  Synonyms: lessee, hirer | Antonyms: lessor, owner | noun

charterer:/ˈtʃɑːrtərər/ | n. | 特许设立人；特许状持有者 | A person or organization to whom a charter is granted.  | The charterer of the new school had extensive experience in education.  这所新学校的特许设立人在教育方面经验丰富。 | Synonyms: grantee, licensee | Antonyms: grantor, licensor | noun
