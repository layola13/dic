alignment: /əˈlaɪnmənt/ | n. | 校准，对准；队列，排列；结盟，联合 |  The alignment of the wheels is crucial for the car's performance. 车轮的校准对汽车的性能至关重要。 |  例句：The text alignment should be centered.  文本对齐方式应为居中。 | 近义词：arrangement, positioning, alliance, affiliation | 反义词：misalignment, discord, disunity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From align + -ment.  Align comes from Old French *aligner, from à ligne "in line," from Latin linea "line."
