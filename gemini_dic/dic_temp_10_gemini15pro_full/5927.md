ambitiousness:/æmˈbɪʃəsnəs/
n.
雄心；野心；有雄心（壮志）的特性
The quality of being ambitious; the possession of a strong desire for achievement, power, or distinction.
Her ambitiousness led her to pursue a challenging career in law. 她的雄心壮志使她追求充满挑战的法律事业。
近义词: ambition, drive, aspiration, determination
反义词: apathy, indifference, lack of motivation, contentment
词性: 名词

ambitiousness:/æmˈbɪʃəsnəs/
n.
雄心；野心
The state of being ambitious; having a strong desire and determination to succeed.
His ambitiousness sometimes made him seem ruthless. 他的雄心勃勃有时让他显得冷酷无情。
近义词: aspiration, drive, zeal
反义词: apathy, indifference, complacency
词性: 名词


词源:
From ambitious + -ness.  Ambitious comes from the Latin ambitiōsus, meaning "going around canvassing for votes," from ambītiō "a going around," from ambīre "to go around."
