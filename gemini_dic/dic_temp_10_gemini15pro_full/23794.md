bray:/breɪ/
verb|发出驴叫声；大声而刺耳地叫喊|to make the loud, harsh cry of a donkey; to speak or laugh loudly and harshly|The donkey brayed loudly. 驴子大声地叫着。|近义词：roar, bellow, shriek|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:v.
bray:/breɪ/
noun|驴叫声；刺耳的声音|the loud, harsh cry of a donkey; a loud, harsh sound|The bray of the donkey echoed through the valley. 驴叫声在山谷中回荡。|近义词：roar, bellow, shriek|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:n.
