coproprietors:/ˌkoʊprəˈpraɪətərz/| n. |共同所有人，共有者|A coproprietor is a person who owns something jointly with one or more other people.|Example: The coproprietors of the business shared the profits equally. 这家企业的共同所有者平分了利润。

Example:  The coproprietors of the building were responsible for its maintenance.  这栋楼的共同业主负责其维护。|近义词：joint owners, co-owners|反义词:|noun
