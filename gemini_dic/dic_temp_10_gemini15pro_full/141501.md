opisthopulmonate:/ˌɒpɪsθɒˈpʌlməneɪt/ | adj. n. | 后肺亚纲的，后肺亚纲动物 |  Relating to or denoting a subclass (Opisthobranchia) of gastropods in which the gill, if present, is posterior to the heart.  This group includes sea slugs, sea hares, and bubble snails.  They are characterized by having undergone detorsion, a twisting of the body plan during development. |  Example: The opisthopulmonate sea slug is a fascinating creature.  后肺亚纲的海蛞蝓是一种迷人的生物。

Example:  Many opisthopulmonates are brightly colored, warning predators of their toxicity. 许多后肺亚纲动物颜色鲜艳，警告捕食者它们的毒性。 | 近义词:  gastropods (更广泛的类别) | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词，因为它是一个分类学术语) | 词性: 形容词，名词


Etymology:
From Greek *opisthen* (behind, at the back) +  Latin *pulmo*, *pulmonis* (lung) + *-ate* (suffix forming adjectives and nouns).
