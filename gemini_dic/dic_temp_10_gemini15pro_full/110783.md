linear:/ˈlɪniər/| adj.|线的，线性的|Relating to a line; involving measurement in one dimension only.  Progressing from one stage to another in a single series of steps; sequential.|例句：The graph shows a linear relationship between the two variables.  图表显示了两个变量之间的线性关系。|例句：The company followed a linear development strategy.  这家公司遵循线性发展战略。|近义词：straight, direct|反义词：nonlinear, curved|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *linearis, from Latin linea (“line”).
