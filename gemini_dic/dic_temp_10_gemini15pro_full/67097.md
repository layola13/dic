explorative:/ɪkˈsplɔːrətɪv/ /ɪkˈsplɔːrəˌtɪv/| adj.|探索的，探究的，考察的|Relating to or involving exploration or investigation.  Characterized by a desire to discover or learn new things.|The team went on an explorative mission to the Amazon rainforest.  该团队前往亚马逊雨林进行了一次探索任务。

The study took an explorative approach to understanding consumer behavior.  这项研究采用探索性方法来理解消费者行为。|近义词：investigative, inquiring, searching, exploratory|反义词：definitive, conclusive|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From explore + -ative.  Explore ultimately comes from the Latin explorare, meaning "to search out, investigate."
