literalness:/ˈlɪtərəlnəs/ | n. | 字面性；拘泥字面；逐字翻译 | Adherence to the exact letter or the primary meaning of a word or text, often without regard to context or figurative language. |  He interpreted the poem with a surprising literalness, missing the underlying metaphor. 他对这首诗的解读惊人的字面化，忽略了其潜在的隐喻。| literalism, exactness, precision |figurativeness, abstractness | noun
