eruption:/ɪˈrʌpʃən/| n. |爆发，喷发|A sudden outburst or discharge, as of a volcano, geyser, or disease.|The volcanic eruption covered the town in ash. 火山爆发使整个城镇覆盖在火山灰下。|近义词：outburst, explosion, discharge|反义词：suppression, quiescence|词性：名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēruptiōn- (stem of *ēruptiō), from Latin ērumpere (to break out, burst forth), from ex- (out) + rumpere (to break).
