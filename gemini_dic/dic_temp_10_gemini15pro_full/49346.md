delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/
n.
描绘；勾画；轮廓
a precise description of a theory, situation, or proposal
The artist’s skill lies in the delicate delineation of the human form.  这位艺术家的技巧在于对人体形态的精细描绘。
description, portrayal, representation, outline, sketch
obscurity, vagueness
noun


delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/
n.
界线
the action of indicating the exact position of a border or boundary.
The delineation between the two properties was marked by a fence.  两处房产之间的界线用栅栏标出。
boundary, border, demarcation, limit
inclusion, integration
noun
