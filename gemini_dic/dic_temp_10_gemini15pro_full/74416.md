franchisee:/ˌfræn.tʃaɪˈziː/
n.
特许经营人，加盟商
A person or company that is granted a license to operate a business under the franchisor's trademark and business model.
The franchisee agreed to pay a percentage of their profits to the franchisor.  特许经营人同意将一部分利润支付给特许经营商。
The franchisee opened their third store in the city.  该加盟商在该市开设了第三家门店。
synonyms:  franchise holder, licensee
antonyms:  franchisor
noun
