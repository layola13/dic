近义词：singing, serenading | 反义词: None | 词性: verb (gerund or present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *caroler "to dance in a ring," from Late Latin *choraulare, from Greek *choreuein "to dance in chorus."  The word evolved to refer to the songs sung during these dances and eventually became associated primarily with Christmas.
