outdwelled:/ˌaʊtˈdwɛld/
verb
居住得比…久; 比...活得长
To live longer than; to outlive.  Specifically, to continue to dwell or reside in a place after another has departed.
He outdwelled all his siblings, living to be over a hundred. 他比他所有的兄弟姐妹都活得长，活到了一百多岁。
Synonym: outlive, survive
Antonym: predecease
词性: 动词
