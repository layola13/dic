aerogramme: /ˈɛərəɡræm/ | n. | 航空信笺; 航空邮件 | A sheet of lightweight paper that folds into an envelope and is designed for sending by airmail. | I sent her an aerogramme from Paris. 我从巴黎给她寄了一封航空信。

She received an aerogramme from her pen pal in Australia. 她收到了来自澳大利亚笔友的航空信。| 近义词: air letter, airmail | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "surface mail") | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- + -gram.  "Aero-" comes from Greek "aēr," meaning "air," and "-gram" comes from Greek "-gramma," meaning "something written" or "letter."
