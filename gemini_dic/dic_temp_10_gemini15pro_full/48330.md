decries:/dɪˈkraɪz/
verb
公开谴责，强烈批评
to criticize something or someone strongly and publicly
The report decries the lack of investment in education.  这份报告强烈谴责了对教育缺乏投资。
denounces, condemns, criticizes
praises, commends, lauds
