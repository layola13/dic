maladjusted: /ˌmæləˈdʒʌstɪd/ | adj. | 失调的；不适应环境的；适应不良的 | Failing to cope with the demands of a normal social environment.  | Example: The maladjusted child struggled to make friends.  这个不适应环境的孩子难以交朋友。

Example:  He seemed maladjusted and withdrawn after the trauma he experienced.  在他经历了创伤之后，他似乎变得格格不入，性格孤僻。| 近义词: disturbed, troubled, unstable, antisocial, unadapted | 反义词: well-adjusted, stable, adapted | 词性: adjective

词源:  From mal- (badly) + adjusted (adapted).
