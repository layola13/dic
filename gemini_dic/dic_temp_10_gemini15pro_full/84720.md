handily:/ˈhændɪli/| adv.|容易地，方便地，灵巧地|easily and skillfully; conveniently; near at hand|She handily defeated her opponent in the election. 她轻而易举地赢得了选举。|Synonyms: easily, effortlessly, conveniently, skillfully, deftly|Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, with difficulty|adverb

handily:/ˈhændɪli/| adv.|容易地，方便地，灵巧地|in a way that is easy and skillful; conveniently; near at hand|The tools were handily placed near the workbench. 工具方便地放在工作台附近。|Synonyms: easily, effortlessly, conveniently, skillfully, deftly|Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, with difficulty|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From hand + -ly.  The original meaning was "near at hand," later evolving to "easily managed" and then "skillfully."
