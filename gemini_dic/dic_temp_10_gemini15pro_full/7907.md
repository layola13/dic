antennae:/ænˈteniː/
n.
触角；天线
A pair of long, thin organs on the head of an insect, crab, etc., used for feeling and touching.  (昆虫、螃蟹等头上一对细长的器官，用于感觉和触碰)
Butterflies use their antennae to smell. 蝴蝶用它们的触角来闻气味。

近义词:feelers, horns
反义词:(无明显的反义词)
词性:名词(plural noun)

antenna:/ænˈtɛnə/
n.
触角；天线
A metal rod or wire used to send and receive radio and television signals. (用于发送和接收无线电和电视信号的金属棒或线)
The car's radio antenna was broken. 汽车的无线电天线坏了。

近义词:aerial
反义词:(无明显的反义词)
词性:名词(singular noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *antenna*, "sail yard," from *ante*, "before." The plural form "antennae" retains the original Latin plural. The singular form "antenna" is more common in American English, especially when referring to radio or television antennas.
