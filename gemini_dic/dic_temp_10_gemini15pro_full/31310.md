chapoy:/ˈtʃæpɔɪ/ |n.|墨西哥薄饼;玉米粉圆饼;|A thin, crisp, unleavened Mexican flatbread made from masa harina.|We had some delicious chicken and chapoys for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了一些美味的鸡肉和墨西哥薄饼。|Synonyms: tortilla, flatbread|Antonyms: None|Noun

chapoy:/tʃəˈpɔɪ/ |n.|轻便折叠床;行军床;|A light folding bed, especially one used by soldiers.|The soldiers slept on chapoys in their tents. 士兵们在帐篷里睡在行军床上。|Synonyms: camp bed, cot|Antonyms: None|Noun
