observances:/əbˈzɜːrvənsɪz/
n.
仪式，惯例；遵守
The act of observing a custom, rule, or law.
Jewish people celebrate many religious observances. 犹太人庆祝许多宗教仪式。
ceremonies, rituals, practices
violations, breaches
