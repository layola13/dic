diluted:/daɪˈluːtɪd/ /dɪˈluːtɪd/
adjective
稀释的；冲淡的
weakened in force, intensity, or value by admixture with something less essential.
The company's profits were diluted by the recent acquisition. 该公司的利润因最近的收购而被稀释了。
The artist's message was diluted by the commercialization of his work. 这位艺术家的信息因其作品的商业化而被淡化了。

近义词: weakened, thinned, watered-down
反义词: concentrated, strengthened, pure
词性: adjective
