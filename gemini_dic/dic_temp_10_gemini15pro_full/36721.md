collocation's:/ˌkɒləˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|搭配，词语搭配|The meaning of a word often depends on its collocations. 一个词的含义通常取决于它的搭配。| This dictionary provides many useful collocations. 这本词典提供了许多有用的搭配。| synonyms: combination, association, pairing| antonyms: separation, disassociation| noun

Etymology: From the late 19th century, from the verb collocate + -ion.  "Collocate" comes from the Latin "collocatus," past participle of "collocare," meaning "to place together," from "com-" (together) + "locare" (to place).
