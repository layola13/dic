light-at-the-end-of-the-tunnel:/laɪt æt ðiː ɛnd əv ðə ˈtʌnəl/| n.|曙光，希望|A sign of hope or relief after a difficult period.  A glimpse of a better future after a time of difficulty.|After months of unemployment, finally getting a job offer felt like seeing the light at the end of the tunnel.|近义词：hope, glimmer of hope, promise, silver lining|反义词: despair, hopelessness|名词

词源：This idiom originated from the experience of traveling through a long, dark tunnel.  The appearance of light signifies the approaching end of the confined and often frightening journey, bringing a sense of relief and hope.
