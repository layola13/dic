palpitated: /ˈpælpɪteɪtɪd/
verb
悸动，快速跳动
beat rapidly, strongly, or irregularly
Her heart palpitated with fear. 她的心因恐惧而怦怦直跳。
近义词: throbbed, pounded, pulsed
反义词: calmed, steadied


词性: v.


词源:  From Latin palpitatus, past participle of palpitare (“to throb, flutter, pant”), frequentative of palpare (“to stroke, touch gently”).
