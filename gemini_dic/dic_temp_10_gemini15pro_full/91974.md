hypermystical:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɪstɪkəl/
adjective
极其神秘的，超级神秘的
Relating to something beyond the ordinary mystical; extremely or excessively mystical.
The ancient ruins radiated a hypermystical aura, hinting at forgotten rituals and cosmic powers.  古代遗迹散发着极其神秘的光环，暗示着被遗忘的仪式和宇宙力量。
The story was filled with hypermystical elements, including talking animals and shape-shifting deities. 故事充满了极其神秘的元素，包括会说话的动物和变形的神。
近义词:  arcane, esoteric, occult, otherworldly
反义词:  mundane, ordinary, common, prosaic
词性: adjective
词源: hyper- (prefix meaning "over, beyond") + mystical (from mystic, ultimately from Greek mystikos "of mysteries")
