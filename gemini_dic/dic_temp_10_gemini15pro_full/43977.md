cries: /kraɪz/
noun
哭泣，叫喊
An act of weeping or shedding tears, typically as an expression of distress, pain, or sorrow.
The baby's cries woke me up. / 婴儿的哭声把我吵醒了。
近义词: sobs, wails, weeping, tears
反义词: laughter, cheers

cries: /kraɪz/
verb (third-person singular simple present cries, present participle crying, simple past cried, past participle cried)
哭，喊叫
To shed tears, typically as an expression of distress, pain, or sorrow; to shout or call out loudly.
He cried out in pain. / 他痛得大叫。
近义词: weeps, shouts, yells, screams
反义词: laughs, rejoices
