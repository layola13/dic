lymphadenoma:/lɪmfˌædɪˈnoʊmə/ | n. |淋巴腺瘤|A benign tumor of lymphoid tissue.|  He was diagnosed with a lymphadenoma after a biopsy.  他活检后被诊断出患有淋巴腺瘤。|Synonym: lymphoma, adenopathy|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of tumor exists.  Perhaps consider "healthy lymphoid tissue")|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  *lymph-* + *-adeno-* + *-oma*.  *Lymph-* refers to the lymphatic system. *-adeno-* refers to a gland. *-oma* is a suffix denoting a tumor or swelling.
