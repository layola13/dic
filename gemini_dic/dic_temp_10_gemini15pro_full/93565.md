imaginings:/ɪˈmædʒɪnɪŋz/
n.
想象，幻想；空想
products of the imagination; things imagined.
She shared her childhood imaginings with her friend. 她和朋友分享了她童年的幻想。
近义词：fantasies, daydreams, visions, conceptions
反义词：realities, facts
词性：名词

PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if it rains tomorrow? 如果明天下雨怎么办？

what for = 为了什么
What did you buy that for? 你买那个是为了什么？

no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what happens, I'll be there for you. 无论发生什么，我都会在你身边。

what's more = 而且；更重要的是
He's intelligent, and what's more, he's kind. 他很聪明，而且很善良。

what about = ...怎么样
What about going to the cinema tonight? 今晚去看电影怎么样？


词源： From Late Middle English *imaginynge, from Anglo-Norman imaginer, from Latin imaginārī (“to imagine, conceive, form an image or likeness”), present active infinitive of imaginor, from imāgō (“image, likeness”).
