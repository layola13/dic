hypervariabilities:/ˌhaɪpərˌvɛəriəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |高度变异性，高变区|Regions of high sequence variability within a molecule, such as an antibody or protein.|The hypervariabilities in the antibody's structure allow it to bind to a wide range of antigens.|近义词：hypervariable regions, variable regions|反义词：conserved regions|词性:noun

hypervariability:/ˌhaɪpərˌvɛəriəˈbɪlɪti/| n. |高度变异性，高变区|The quality or state of being hypervariable; the presence of hypervariable regions.|The hypervariability of this gene makes it difficult to study.|近义词：high variability, extreme variability|反义词：low variability, conservation|词性:noun
