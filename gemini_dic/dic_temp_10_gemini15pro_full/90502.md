huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/| n.|叫卖小贩，推销员，骗子|a person who sells goods, especially by shouting about them in a loud and annoying way, often using dishonest methods|The huckster promised amazing results, but his product was worthless.  这个叫卖小贩承诺了惊人的效果，但他的产品一文不值。|synonyms: peddler, hawker, salesman, charlatan, con artist|antonyms: philanthropist, benefactor|noun

huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/| v.|兜售，推销（尤指用夸大或不诚实的手段）|to promote or sell something, often using aggressive or dishonest methods|He was huckstering his wares on the street corner. 他在街角兜售他的商品。|synonyms: peddle, hawk, tout, promote|antonyms: donate, give away|verb
