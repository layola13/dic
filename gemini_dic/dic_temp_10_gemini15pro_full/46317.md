dabbers:/ˈdæbəz/
n.
涂抹器；轻拍者；赌徒
People who dab or apply something lightly. This can refer to artists using dabbing tools, people applying makeup or medication, or even gamblers.
The artist used dabbers to apply the paint to the canvas. 艺术家用涂抹器将颜料涂到画布上。
The makeup artist used small dabbers to blend the foundation. 化妆师用小涂抹器来混合粉底。
近义词: applicators, dabbing tools, gamblers (in some contexts)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


dabber:/ˈdæbə(r)/
n.
涂抹器；轻拍者；赌徒
A tool or person that dabs.
He used a dabber to apply the glue. 他用涂抹器涂胶水。
She's a dabber at bingo. 她是宾果游戏的常客。
近义词: applicator, tool, gambler (in some contexts)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
