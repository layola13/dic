nicolaitan:/ˌnɪkəˈleɪɪtən/ | n. | 尼哥拉党人；尼哥拉派的信徒 | A follower of the doctrines of the Nicolaitans, a heretical sect mentioned in the Book of Revelation.  They are condemned for their practices of idolatry and sexual immorality. |  Early church fathers identified the Nicolaitans as a Gnostic sect. 早期教父认为尼哥拉党是一个诺斯底教派。|  The Nicolaitans were condemned in the Book of Revelation. 尼哥拉党人在启示录中被谴责。 | synonyms: heretic, sectarian | antonyms: orthodox, believer | noun
