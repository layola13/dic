auteurists:/ˌɔːtəˈrɪsts/
n.
auteur理论拥护者，作者论者
Film critics who are auteurists look for recurring themes and stylistic choices in a director's films.
那些作者论的影评家会在导演的电影中寻找重复出现的主题和风格选择。
English explanation: A proponent of the auteur theory, which emphasizes the director as the primary creative force in filmmaking, analogous to an author of a novel.  Auteurists focus on how a director's personal vision and style are reflected in their films, often looking for recurring themes, motifs, and stylistic choices across their body of work.
例句：Many auteurists consider Quentin Tarantino a master of dialogue and stylized violence. 许多作者论者认为昆汀·塔伦蒂诺是对话和程式化暴力的大师。

例句：The auteurists championed directors like Hitchcock and Ford, elevating them to the status of artists. 作者论者拥护像希区柯克和福特这样的导演，将他们提升到艺术家的地位。

近义词：cinephiles, film buffs
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider) traditional critics, formalists
词性: noun (plural)
