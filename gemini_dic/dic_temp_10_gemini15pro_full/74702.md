freelancer:/ˈfriːlænsər/| n.|自由职业者|A person who works independently for various companies or clients, rather than being employed by one organization.|例句：She works as a freelance writer. 她是一位自由撰稿人。

例句：He decided to become a freelancer after years of working in a corporation.  在一家公司工作多年后，他决定成为一名自由职业者。|近义词：independent contractor, self-employed person, consultant|反义词：employee, full-time worker|词性:noun

freelance:/ˈfriːlæns/| adj. vi.|自由职业的；从事自由职业|Working independently for various companies or clients.|例句：She does freelance work for several magazines. 她为几家杂志做自由撰稿工作。

例句：He freelanced for a few years before finding a permanent job.  他做了几年自由职业之后才找到一份固定工作。|近义词：independent, self-employed|反义词：employed, full-time|词性: adjective, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From free lance, originally referring to medieval mercenary soldiers who were not sworn to any particular lord.  The term later evolved to describe writers and artists who worked for different publications or patrons.  "Free" means independent, and "lance" refers to the weapon carried by these soldiers.
