moblogs:/ˈmoʊblɒɡz/ (US), /ˈməʊblɒɡz/ (UK)| n.|手机博客，移动博客|A blog that is updated using a mobile phone or other handheld device, typically with short posts, photos, or videos.  A contraction of "mobile" and "weblog."|Example: Moblogs were popular in the early 2000s before smartphones became widespread. 手机博客在智能手机普及之前的2000年代初期很流行。

|Example: He updated his moblog with pictures from his trip. 他用旅行照片更新了他的手机博客。|近义词：microblog, mobile blog|反义词:blog (in the broader sense, since a moblog is a type of blog)|词性:noun
