atonalities:/ˌeɪtɒnˈælɪtiz/ /ˌeɪˌtoʊnˈælɪtiz/
noun
无调性
the absence of key or tonality in a musical composition; the use of the twelve notes of the chromatic scale in a way that avoids emphasizing any one note as a tonic
The composer experimented with atonalities in his later works.  这位作曲家在后期的作品中尝试了无调性。
near synonyms: atonality, dissonance, discord
opposite: tonality, consonance, harmony
n.

atonality:/ˌeɪtɒnˈælɪti/ /ˌeɪˌtoʊnˈælɪti/
noun
无调性
music that lacks a tonal center or key
The piece was characterized by its stark atonality. 这首曲子的特点是明显的无调性。
near synonyms: atonalities, dissonance, discord
opposite: tonality, consonance, harmony
