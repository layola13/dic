oversilences: /ˌoʊvərˈsaɪlənsɪz/
verb
使过度沉默；压制；用沉默压服
To silence or suppress excessively, especially by imposing silence or making someone afraid to speak.
The dictator oversilenced any opposition, creating a climate of fear.  独裁者压制了任何反对的声音，制造了恐怖气氛。
The company tried to oversilence the whistleblowers, but the truth eventually came out. 公司试图压制告密者，但真相最终还是大白了。
Synonym: suppress, stifle, muzzle, silence
Antonym: encourage, empower, promote, amplify
词性: 动词
