dewired:/diːˈwaɪərd/
adj.
断开的；拆除线路的；去除了线路的
Removed from a wired system or connection; disconnected from electronic wiring.  Figuratively, it can also mean freed from constraints or limitations.
The device is now completely dewired and safe to handle.  该设备现在已完全断线，可以安全处理。
The company decided to dewire the entire building and switch to a wireless network.  公司决定拆除整栋楼的线路，改用无线网络。
disconnected, unwired, unplugged
wired, connected
adjective
