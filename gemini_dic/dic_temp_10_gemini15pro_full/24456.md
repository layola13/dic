britannicize:/brɪˈtænɪsaɪz/
verb
使英国化
To make or become British in character, customs, or institutions.
The government tried to Britannicize the colonies by imposing British laws and customs. 政府试图通过强制推行英国法律和习俗来使殖民地英国化。
The school system was Britannicized in the 19th century.  学校系统在19世纪被英国化了。
近义词: anglicize
反义词: None
词性: verb
