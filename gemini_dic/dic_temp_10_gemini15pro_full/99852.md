intermobility:/ˌɪntərmoʊˈbɪləti/ | n. | 交互移动性，跨系统移动性 | The intermobility of transportation systems allows for seamless travel between different modes.  交通系统的交互移动性允许在不同模式之间无缝旅行。 |  Intermobility is crucial for efficient urban planning. 交互移动性对于有效的城市规划至关重要。 | 近义词: interoperability, connectivity | 反义词: immobility, isolation | 词性: noun

词源: "inter-" (between) + "mobility" (the ability to move)
