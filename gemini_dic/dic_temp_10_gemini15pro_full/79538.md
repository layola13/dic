glocalization:/ˌɡloʊkələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |全球本土化|The adaptation of international products or services to local markets.  It combines the words "globalization" and "localization."|McDonald's offers McRice in Asian countries as an example of glocalization. 麦当劳在亚洲国家提供麦香饭，这是全球本土化的一个例子。

Glocalization requires companies to be mindful of cultural nuances.  全球本土化要求公司注意文化差异。|近义词：localization, adaptation, customization|反义词：globalization, standardization|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From *globalization* + *localization*, a blend of the two words.  The term was reportedly coined in the late 1980s in Japanese business journals.
