accomplishment: /əˈkʌmplɪʃmənt/
n.
成就；完成；实现
Something that has been achieved successfully; an achievement.
Her greatest accomplishment was climbing Mount Everest. 她最大的成就是攀登珠穆朗玛峰。

His scientific accomplishments are well known. 他的科学成就在业内很有名气。

近义词: achievement, feat, attainment, fulfillment
反义词: failure, defeat, lack

词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French acomplissement, from acomplir "to complete, fulfill," from a- "to" + complir "to fulfill," from Latin complere "to fill up, complete," from com- "together" + plere "to fill."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His accomplishment is impressive. = 他的成就是令人印象深刻的。
Her main accomplishment is her dedication to charity work. = 她的主要成就是她对慈善事业的奉献。

一般过去时:
The accomplishment of the project took several years. = 该项目的完成花费了数年时间。
They celebrated the accomplishment of their goals. = 他们庆祝目标的达成。

现在完成时:
She has already accomplished a great deal in her field. = 她在她的领域已经取得了很大的成就。
He has accomplished more than most people his age. = 他比大多数同龄人取得了更大的成就。

过去完成时:
By the time he retired, he had accomplished everything he set out to do. = 到他退休时，他已经完成了所有他计划要做的事情。
They had accomplished the first phase of the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了项目的第一阶段。

将来时:
I hope to accomplish my goals within the next five years. = 我希望在未来五年内实现我的目标。
She will accomplish great things in her career. = 她将在她的职业生涯中取得伟大的成就。

虚拟语气:
If I were to accomplish one thing in my life, it would be to help others. = 如果我要在我的生命中完成一件事，那就是帮助别人。
I wish I could accomplish everything I want to do. = 我希望我能完成我想做的所有事情。

被动语态:
The task was accomplished with great effort. = 这项任务是经过巨大努力才完成的。
Much has been accomplished in recent years. = 近年来取得了许多成就。

疑问句:
What is your greatest accomplishment? = 你最大的成就是什么？
How was this accomplishment achieved? = 这个成就是如何取得的？

否定句:
He didn't accomplish much in his first year at the company. = 他在公司第一年没有取得多大成就。
The project was not accomplished on time. = 该项目没有按时完成。
