criterional:/kraɪˌtɪəriənəl/
adjective|标准的，规范的|Relating to or serving as a criterion or standard. |The criterional features of a good essay include clarity, coherence, and strong evidence.|近义词:standard, normative, defining|反义词:nonstandard, atypical|词性:adjective

Etymology: From "criterion" + "-al."  "Criterion" comes from the Greek "kritērion," meaning "means for judging."
