litmus:/ˈlɪtməs/| n. |石蕊；试金石；检验标准|A dye obtained from certain lichens that changes color depending on acidity or alkalinity, used as an indicator.|The government's reaction to this issue will be a litmus test of its commitment to reform. 政府对此问题的反应将是检验其改革承诺的试金石。|Synonym: indicator, touchstone, barometer|Antonym: N/A|noun
