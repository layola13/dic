asiphonea:/ˌeɪsaɪˈfoʊniə/ | n. |无管亚纲; 无管目|A subclass or order of mollusks, especially bivalves, characterized by the absence of siphons.|Asiphonea are a group of bivalve mollusks that lack siphons.| 近义词: Bivalvia (in a broader sense)| 反义词: Siphonida |词性:名词

Etymology:
From a- (without) + siphon + -ea (a taxonomic suffix).  The term refers to the lack of siphons, tube-like structures used by some bivalves for water intake and expulsion.
