bodices:/ˈbɒdɪsɪz/
n.
紧身胸衣，女装上衣

The shop specializes in bespoke bodices and corsets. 这家商店专门定制紧身胸衣和束腹。

She wore a beautiful gown with a beaded bodice. 她穿着一件带有串珠胸衣的漂亮礼服。

近义词:corsets, stays, basque

词性:noun (plural)
