gratulate:/ˈɡrætʃʊleɪt/ /ˈɡræʒʊleɪt/ | v. | 祝贺，恭喜 | To express pleasure to someone about their success or good fortune. |  I gratulate you on your recent promotion. 我祝贺你最近升职了。

I gratulate her on her graduation. 我祝贺她毕业。

近义词: congratulate, felicitate | 反义词: commiserate, condole | 词性: verb (及物动词)
