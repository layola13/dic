all-and-sundry:/ɔːl ænd ˈsʌndri/ | n. | 所有的人；各式各样的人；全体 | everyone, without exception |  The party was open to all and sundry.  这个派对对所有人开放。| synonyms: everyone, each and every one, one and all | antonyms: none, nobody | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *al and sondry*, from *al* (all) + *sondry* (sundry, separate, various).  The original sense was “each and every one individually.”
