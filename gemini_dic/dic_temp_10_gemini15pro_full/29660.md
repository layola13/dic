catgut:/ˈkætɡʌt/| n. |羊肠线|A strong cord made from the dried intestines of sheep or other animals, formerly used for surgical sutures and strings of musical instruments.|The surgeon used catgut to stitch the wound.  外科医生用羊肠线缝合伤口。|Catgut strings give a unique tone to the instrument. 羊肠线琴弦赋予乐器独特的音色。| 近义词：suture, string|反义词: None|名词
