decivilizations: /ˌdiːsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
去文明化，野蛮化
The process of losing the qualities and characteristics of a civilization; a decline into barbarism.
The decivilizations of war often lead to widespread suffering and destruction. 战争的野蛮化通常会导致广泛的苦难和破坏。

The collapse of the Roman Empire saw decivilizations in many parts of Europe. 罗马帝国的崩溃见证了欧洲许多地区的野蛮化。

近义词: barbarization, savagery
反义词: civilization, enlightenment
词性: 名词
