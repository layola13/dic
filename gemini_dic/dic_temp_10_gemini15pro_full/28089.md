capilliform:/kəˈpɪlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 毛状的，毛发的 | Resembling a hair; hair-shaped. |  The plant has capilliform leaves. 这种植物有毛状的叶子。|  The fungus produced capilliform structures. 这种真菌产生了毛发状的结构。| 近义词: hairlike, filamentous, threadlike | 反义词: thick, broad | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *capillus* (hair) + *-formis* (having the form of).
