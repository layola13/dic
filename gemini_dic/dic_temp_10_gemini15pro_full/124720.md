mozambiques:/ˌmoʊzæmˈbiːks/ | n. |莫桑比克人; 莫桑比克舞蹈 |People from Mozambique, or a type of lively southern African dance. |  Mozambique is known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant mozambiques. 莫桑比克以其美丽的海滩和充满活力的莫桑比克舞蹈而闻名。|  The dancers performed energetic mozambiques all night long. 舞者们整夜表演充满活力的莫桑比克舞蹈。| 近义词: (for the dance)  Southern African dances,  Marrabenta | 反义词:  None (specific dance forms could be considered opposites in style, e.g., slow waltz vs. fast mozambique) | 词性: 名词 (Noun)
