deco:/ˈdɛkoʊ/| n. adj.|装饰艺术；装饰的|Short for Art Deco, a style of decorative art, design, and architecture that flourished in the 1920s and 1930s, characterized by geometric shapes, bold colors, and lavish ornamentation.  It can also be used as an adjective meaning "decorated in the Art Deco style".|The hotel lobby featured stunning deco chandeliers and geometric patterns on the walls.  酒店大堂以令人惊叹的装饰艺术吊灯和墙壁上的几何图案为特色。|近义词：Art Deco, ornamental, decorative|反义词：plain, simple|词性:noun, adjective
