distantness:/ˈdɪstəntnəs/| n. |疏远；冷淡；遥远|The distantness in her voice made me feel unwelcome. 她语气中的冷淡让我觉得不受欢迎。|例句：There was a certain distantness in his manner. 他的举止略显冷淡。

例句：The vast distantness of the stars makes them seem unreal. 浩瀚遥远的星空让它们看起来很不真实。|近义词：remoteness, aloofness, coldness, reserve, detachment|反义词：closeness, intimacy, warmth, friendliness|词性:名词

词源: From distant + -ness.  "distant"  itself comes from Old French "distant,"  from Latin "distantem" (nominative distans), present participle of "distare" "to stand apart," from "dis-" "apart" + "stare" "to stand."
