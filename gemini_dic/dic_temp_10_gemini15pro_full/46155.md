cynarctomachy:/ˌsɪnɑːrkˈtɒməki/ | n. |  熊和狗之间的争斗；熊和狗的搏斗 | A fight between a bear and a dog, or bear-baiting. |  The ancient Romans enjoyed the spectacle of cynarctomachy in the Colosseum. 古罗马人喜欢在斗兽场观看熊狗搏斗。 | 近义词: bear-baiting, animal fighting | 反义词:  peace, harmony | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *kunarktomakhia*, from *kun* (dog) + *arktos* (bear) + *makhe* (fight).
