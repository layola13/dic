materialist:/məˈtɪəriəlɪst/| n. adj.|唯物主义者; 物质主义者|A person who considers material possessions and physical comfort as more important than spiritual values.  A person who believes that only matter exists and that everything can be explained in terms of physical processes.|He's such a materialist, always focused on the latest gadgets. 他是个十足的物质主义者，总是关注最新的电子产品。|Synonyms: consumerist, hedonist, epicurean|Antonyms: spiritualist, idealist|noun

materialist:/məˈtɪəriəlɪstɪk/| adj.|物质主义的; 唯物论的|Excessively concerned with material possessions; relating to materialism.|She has a very materialistic outlook on life. 她的人生观非常物质化。|Synonyms: mercenary, acquisitive, grasping|Antonyms: spiritual, idealistic|adjective
