concision:/kənˈsɪʒn/| n.|简洁，简明|The concision of her writing style made it easy to understand. 她的写作风格简洁明了，易于理解。|例句：The concision of the summary was impressive.  摘要的简洁令人印象深刻。|近义词：brevity, terseness, succinctness|反义词:verbosity, prolixity|noun
concise:/kənˈsaɪs/| adj.|简洁的，简明的|His concise explanation clarified the complex issue. 他简洁的解释阐明了这个复杂的问题。|例句：The report was concise and to the point. 报告简洁扼要。|近义词：brief, succinct, terse|反义词:verbose, prolix|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin concīsio (“a cutting short, abridgment”), from concīsus, past participle of concīdō (“cut short, cut down, cut off, abridge, diminish, curtail”), from con- (“together, completely”) + caedō (“cut, strike, fell, kill”).
