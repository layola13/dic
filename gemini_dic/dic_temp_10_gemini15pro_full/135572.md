The nonponderosity of the helium-filled balloons made them rise quickly. 充满氦气的气球的轻盈使它们迅速上升。|近义词：lightness, weightlessness, buoyancy, airiness|反义词：heaviness, weight, ponderosity|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + ponderosity (heaviness).  Ponderosity comes from Latin *ponderositas*, from ponderosus (heavy), from pondus (weight).
