barleybrake:/ˈbɑːrlib.reɪk/
n.|大麦游戏；一种老式乡村游戏|A rustic game played especially in the 16th and 17th centuries, involving three couples and a designated space.  The middle couple tried to catch the others as they ran between two bases.|They played barleybrake on the village green. 他们在村里的草地上玩大麦游戏。|近义词:tag, prisoner's base|反义词:None|词性:名词
