hypnoses:/hɪpˈnoʊsiːz/
noun
催眠状态；催眠术
The plural of hypnosis.  A state of consciousness characterized by heightened suggestibility and focused attention.
He underwent several hypnoses to try to quit smoking. 他接受了几次催眠治疗以尝试戒烟。
The therapist used hypnoses to help the patient recall repressed memories.  治疗师用催眠帮助病人回忆起被压抑的记忆。

近义词: trances, mesmerisms
反义词: wakefulness, alertness
词性: noun (plural)
