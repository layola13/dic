beggarlinesses:/ˈbɛɡərlɪnəsɪz/
n.
贫穷；乞丐状态；极度缺乏
the state of being beggarly; extreme poverty or scarcity.
The beggarlinesses of the region were shocking; many families lacked basic necessities.  该地区的贫困状况令人震惊；许多家庭缺乏基本生活必需品。
近义词：poverty, destitution, indigence, penury
反义词：wealth, affluence, opulence, abundance
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From beggarliness + -es (plural suffix).
Beggarliness from beggarly + -ness (suffix forming abstract nouns).
Beggarly from beggar + -ly (suffix forming adjectives).
Beggar (from Old French begart, of uncertain origin).
