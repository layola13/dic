excretin:/ɪkˈskriːtɪn/
n.
排泄物，分泌物
A waste product eliminated from the body, especially from the kidneys.
The kidneys filter the blood and remove excretins. 肾脏过滤血液并清除排泄物。
近义词:waste, discharge, secretion
反义词:intake
