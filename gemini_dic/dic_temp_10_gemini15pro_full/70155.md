fiatist:/ˈfaɪətɪst/ | n. | 法币主义者；名义主义者 | A person who believes that something has value only because a government says it does, especially in the context of money. |  The fiatist argued that gold was no longer relevant in the modern economy.  法币主义者认为黄金在现代经济中已不再重要。 | synonyms:  inflationist, monetarist | antonyms:  goldbug, hard money advocate | noun
