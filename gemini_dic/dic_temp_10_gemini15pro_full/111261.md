litigious:/lɪˈtɪdʒəs/
adjective
好诉讼的；爱打官司的
Prone to engage in lawsuits; inclined to sue.
She works in a very litigious society. 她在一个非常喜欢诉讼的社会工作。
The company has a reputation for being litigious. 这家公司以好诉讼而闻名。
近义词:argumentative, quarrelsome, contentious
反义词:conciliatory, peaceable, amicable
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *litigiosus, from litigium (“lawsuit”), from lis (“lawsuit”) + agere (“to do, drive, act, lead”).
