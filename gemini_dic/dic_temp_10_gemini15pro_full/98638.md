insubordinate:/ˌɪnsəˈbɔːrdɪnət/| adj.|不服从的，反抗的|Defying authority; refusing to obey orders. Not submitting to authority.|例句：His insubordinate behavior led to his dismissal. 他的不服从行为导致他被解雇。

例句：The insubordinate soldier was court-martialed.  这名不服从命令的士兵受到了军事法庭的审判。

近义词：disobedient, rebellious, defiant, unruly, mutinous|反义词: obedient, compliant, submissive, docile|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Late Latin *insubordinatus*, from in- "not" + subordinatus "subordinate".
