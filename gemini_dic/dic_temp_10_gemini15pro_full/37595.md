communicably:/kəˈmjuːnɪkəbli/
adjective
可传达地，能沟通地
In a manner that can be communicated or transmitted, especially of diseases.  能够沟通或传播的方式，尤指疾病。
The disease is communicably transmitted through coughing and sneezing. 这种疾病可以通过咳嗽和打喷嚏传播。
近义词:contagiously, infectiously, transmittably
反义词:noncommunicable, noncontagious
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From communicate + -able + -ly.  来自 communicate + -able + -ly。
