enteric's:/enˈtɛrɪks/
adjective
肠的；肠内的
Relating to or occurring in the intestines.
The enteric nervous system controls the function of the gastrointestinal tract.  肠神经系统控制胃肠道的功能。
Enteric bacteria are found in the intestines of animals. 肠道细菌存在于动物的肠道中。
Synonyms: intestinal, bowel
Antonyms: extra-intestinal
