brontotherium:/ˌbrɒntəˈθɪəriəm/| n.|雷兽|An extinct genus of large mammals related to horses and rhinoceroses, prevalent in North America during the Eocene epoch.  They were characterized by large, bony horns on their noses.|例句：The brontotherium, with its massive horns, roamed the prehistoric plains.  雷兽，以其巨大的角，漫游在史前的平原上。

例句：Fossils of the brontotherium have been found in various parts of North America. 在北美各地都发现了雷兽的化石。|近义词：titanothere|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
