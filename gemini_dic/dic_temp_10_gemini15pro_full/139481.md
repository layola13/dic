obstreperous: /əbˈstrɛpərəs/ /ɑːbˈstrɛpərəs/
adjective
喧闹的，吵闹的；不服管束的；难驾驭的
noisy and difficult to control
Example: The obstreperous children were finally quiet after their teacher threatened to take away recess. 喧闹的孩子们在老师威胁要取消课间休息后终于安静下来了。

Example:  The obstreperous crowd booed the politician off the stage. 吵闹的观众把政客嘘下了台。

Synonym: unruly, boisterous, clamorous, vociferous
Antonym: quiet, docile, well-behaved, obedient
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin obstrepere ("to make a noise against, clamor against"), from ob- ("against, toward") + strepere ("to make a noise").
