glutinative:/ˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/| adj.|粘着的，胶着的；(尤指语言)粘缀的|A glutinative language is one in which words are formed by stringing together morphemes, each of which carries only one meaning.|Turkish is a glutinative language. 土耳其语是一种粘着语。|agglutinative, agglutinating|isolating|adjective
