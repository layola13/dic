hyphenation:/ˌhaɪfəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 断字；用连字符连接 | The hyphenation of this word is incorrect. 这个单词的断字是错误的。| The hyphenation rules can be complex. 断字规则可能很复杂。 |  近义词：syllabication, division | 反义词: combination, joining | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *hyphenātiō, from Latin hyphenātus, past participle of hyphenāre (“to mark with a hyphen”), from Ancient Greek ὑφενόω (huphenoō, “to mark with a hyphen”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + ἕν (hén, “one”).
