oversqueamish:/ˌoʊvərˈskwiːmɪʃ/ | adj. | 过分拘谨的，过分易受惊的，过分洁癖的 | Excessively fastidious or scrupulous; easily shocked or disgusted.  | She's so oversqueamish she can't even watch a nature documentary. 她太敏感了，甚至连自然纪录片都看不下去。 |  He was oversqueamish about eating street food. 他对吃街边小吃过于谨慎。 | synonyms: prudish, fastidious, squeamish, delicate, fussy  | antonyms:  coarse, insensitive,  unfastidious, tolerant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From over- + squeamish.  "Squeamish" comes from the Old English *squeamish, of uncertain origin, possibly related to the Old Norse sveimr ("dizzy"). The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning.
