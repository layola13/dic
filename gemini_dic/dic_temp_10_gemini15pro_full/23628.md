branchiopod: /ˈbræŋkiəpɒd/ (UK) /ˈbræŋkiəpɑːd/ (US)| n. |鳃足类动物|A crustacean of the class Branchiopoda, which comprises small freshwater animals, such as fairy shrimps and water fleas, having branched, leaflike appendages used for respiration and locomotion.|Fairy shrimps are branchiopods that inhabit ephemeral pools. 仙女虾是栖息在临时水池中的鳃足类动物。|Synonyms:  water flea, brine shrimp, clam shrimp, tadpole shrimp |Antonyms: None| Noun


branchiopod: /ˈbræŋkiəpɒd/ (UK) /ˈbræŋkiəpɑːd/ (US)| adj. |鳃足类的|Relating to or belonging to the class Branchiopoda. |The branchiopod crustaceans are an important food source for many fish. 鳃足类甲壳动物是许多鱼类的重要食物来源。|Synonyms: None |Antonyms: None| Adjective
