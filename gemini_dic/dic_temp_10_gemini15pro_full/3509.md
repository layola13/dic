aftershocks: /ˈɑːftərʃɒks/ (UK) /ˈæftərʃɑːks/ (US)
| n. | 余震 | A smaller earthquake that follows a larger earthquake, in the same area. | The initial earthquake was followed by a series of powerful aftershocks. 初震之后发生了一系列强烈的余震。 |  例句：The aftershocks continued for days, causing further damage. 余震持续了数天，造成了进一步的破坏。| 近义词: tremors, secondary earthquakes | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 main shock/initial earthquake 表示主震) | 词性: 名词
