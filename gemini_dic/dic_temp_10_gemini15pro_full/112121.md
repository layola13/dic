longtime:/ˈlɔːŋtaɪm/| adj.| 长时间的，长期的|A longtime friend, employee, resident, etc. is one that has existed or been in a particular state for a long time. | He's a longtime friend of the family. 他是这个家庭的老朋友。|She is a longtime supporter of the arts. 她是一位长期支持艺术的人。|近义词：long-standing, long-term|反义词：short-term, recent|词性:形容词
Etymology:  A compound of "long" and "time," both words with Germanic roots dating back to Old English.  "Long" traces back to "lang," and "time" to "tīma."
