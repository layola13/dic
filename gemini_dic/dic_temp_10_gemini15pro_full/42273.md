counter-compony:/ˈkaʊntər ˌkʌmpəni/ | n. | 对顶图案; 相对纹章; (纹章学中)相对而立的动物图案 | A heraldic bearing depicting two animals, such as lions, facing each other. | The Scottish Royal Banner features a lion rampant within a double tressure flory counter-flory, which is a form of counter-compony. 苏格兰皇家旗帜的特点是在双重的花卉图案中有一头跃立的狮子，这是一种对顶图案的形式。 |  The shield displays a counter-compony of gold and azure lions. 盾牌上显示着金色和天蓝色狮子组成的对顶图案。 | synonyms: opposing charges, confronted figures | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider single charges or non-symmetrical arrangements) | noun
