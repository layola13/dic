chirology:/kɪˈrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 手相术，手相学 | The study of the hand, especially its lines and markings, as a means of character analysis or fortune-telling.  |  She learned chirology from her grandmother. 她从她祖母那里学了手相术。 | Synonym: palmistry, hand reading | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider scientific character analysis) | noun

chiromancy:/ˈkaɪrəˌmænsi/ | n. | 手相术，手相 |  Fortune-telling through the study of the palm. | He doesn't believe in chiromancy, but he found it entertaining. 他不相信手相术，但他觉得它很有趣。| Synonym: palmistry, chirology | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | noun
