exasperatingly:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪtɪŋli/
adverb|令人恼火地，使人恼怒地|in a way that causes irritation or annoyance.
The website was exasperatingly slow.  这个网站慢得令人恼火。
He exasperatingly missed the last train. 他错过了最后一班火车，真让人恼火。
近义词:annoyingly, irritatingly, infuriatingly
反义词:pleasingly, soothingly
词性:adverb
词源:From "exasperate" (from Latin exasperatus, past participle of exasperare "to roughen, irritate," from ex- "out" + asper "rough") + "-ing" + "-ly."
