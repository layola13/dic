nondefined:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪnd/ | adj. | 未定义的，未界定的 | Not formally or precisely defined; lacking a definition. |  Example: The scope of the project is nondefined, leading to confusion.  项目范围未界定，导致了混乱。| Example:  Many terms in the contract remained nondefined, making it difficult to enforce. 合同中的许多条款仍未定义，使其难以执行。 | 近义词: undefined, unspecified, indeterminate | 反义词: defined, specified, determined | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + defined (past participle of the verb "define," from Latin "definire," meaning "to limit, determine").
