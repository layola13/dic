omniformity:/ˌɒmnɪˈfɔːmɪti/ | n. | 全形性，各种形式，无所不包 |The state or quality of having every possible form or shape.  The concept of omniformity suggests a universe of infinite possibilities and endless variation. | The artist explored the concept of omniformity in his latest exhibition, presenting works that defied categorization. 艺术家在他最新的展览中探索了全形性的概念，展示了难以归类的作品。 | Synonyms: universality, all-encompassingness, comprehensiveness | Antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity, sameness | noun
