capitalize:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪz/ |v. |使资本化；利用；把…的首字母大写|To capitalize means to take advantage of something; to make the most of something; to convert into capital; to write or print with an initial capital or capitals.|He capitalized on her mistake and won the game. 他利用她的错误赢得了比赛。  She capitalized "China" in the title. 她在标题中将“中国”的首字母大写。|Synonyms: exploit, utilize, leverage|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|verb
