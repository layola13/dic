lysis:/ˈlaɪsɪs/
n.
分解；溶解
the disintegration of a cell by rupture of the cell wall or membrane.
The lysis of the red blood cells was caused by the venom. 红细胞的溶解是由毒液引起的。
decomposition, breakdown, disintegration
synthesis, formation
noun
