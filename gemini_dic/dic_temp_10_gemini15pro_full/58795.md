duodecahedral:/ˌduːəʊdekəˈhiːdrəl/| adj.|十二面体的|Having twelve faces.  Relating to a dodecahedron.|例句：The mineral formed duodecahedral crystals. 这种矿物形成了十二面体的晶体。|例句：He was fascinated by the duodecahedral dice. 他被十二面体的骰子迷住了。|近义词：twelve-sided, dodecahedron-shaped|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider shapes with different numbers of faces, e.g., tetrahedral, octahedral)|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Late Latin *duodecahedrus, from Ancient Greek δωδεκάεδρον (dōdekáedron), from δώδεκα (dṓdeka, “twelve”) + ἕδρα (hédra, “seat, base, face”).
