overproducers:/ˈoʊvərprəˌdusərz/ /ˈəʊvəprəˌdjuːsəz/
noun
过度生产者；产量过剩者
Those who produce more than is needed or demanded.  This can refer to individuals, companies, or even countries.
The government is trying to find ways to help overproducers of agricultural products. 政府正在设法帮助农产品生产过剩的生产者。
synonyms: oversuppliers
antonyms: undersuppliers
n.
