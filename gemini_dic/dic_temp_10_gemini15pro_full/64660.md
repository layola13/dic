escalate:/ˈɛskəleɪt/ (US), /ˈɛskəleɪt/ (UK)| v. | 升级；扩大；加剧；逐步上升 | To increase or intensify, especially in a rapid or uncontrolled manner.  To become or cause to become more intense or serious.| The conflict escalated quickly into a full-blown war. 冲突迅速升级为全面战争。|  The price of oil has escalated dramatically in recent months.  近几个月来，石油价格急剧上涨。|intensify, increase, heighten, magnify, amplify| de-escalate, decrease, reduce, lessen | verb
