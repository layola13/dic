draper:/ˈdreɪpər/| n.| 布商，窗帘商；呢绒商|A person who sells cloth and sometimes other household items such as curtains.|例句：The draper measured the fabric for the new curtains.  布商为新窗帘量了布料。

例句：He inherited his father's business and became a successful draper. 他继承了父亲的生意，成了一名成功的布商。|近义词：cloth merchant, textile dealer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps customer)|词性:noun
