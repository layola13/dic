calando:/kəˈlændoʊ/ /kaˈlando/ | adv. adj. | 渐弱 | Gradually softer (in music). | The piece ends with a long, calando phrase. 这首曲子以一段长而渐弱的乐句结束。| 近义词: diminuendo, decrescendo | 反义词: crescendo | 词性: 副词，形容词

Etymology:  From Italian *calando*, present participle of *calare* "to lower," from Latin *chalare*, variant of *calare* "to proclaim, call, announce."  Originally meaning "letting down," hence "lowering the voice."
