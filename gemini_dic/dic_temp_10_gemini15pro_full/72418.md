fluffily:/ˈflʌfɪli/| adv.|蓬松地，柔软地|In a fluffy manner; like fluff.  Characterized by a light and airy texture.|The cat slept fluffily curled up on the bed.  这只猫蜷缩在床上，睡得很蓬松。|近义词：softly, lightly, airily|反义词:firmly, compactly|词性:副词
词源: From fluff + -ily.  Fluff comes from Middle English *fluffen, of imitative origin.
