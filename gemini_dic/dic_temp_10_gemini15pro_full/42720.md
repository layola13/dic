counterviolence:/ˌkaʊntərˈvaɪələns/| n. |反暴力，对抗暴力，非暴力抵抗|Actions taken to oppose or prevent violence, often through peaceful means. It can also refer to retaliatory violence in some contexts, though this usage is less common and often considered problematic.|

例句：
The community organized a counterviolence campaign to address the rising gang activity.
社区组织了一场反暴力运动，以应对日益猖獗的帮派活动。

Many activists believe that counterviolence should primarily focus on peaceful resistance and community building.
许多活动家认为，反暴力应该主要集中在和平抵抗和社区建设上。


近义词：non-violent resistance, peaceful resistance, civil disobedience, conflict resolution|
反义词：violence, aggression, retaliation|
词性：noun|

词源: "counter-" (prefix meaning "against" or "opposite") + "violence" (noun meaning "physical force intended to hurt, damage, or kill someone or something").
