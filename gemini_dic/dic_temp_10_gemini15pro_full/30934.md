chairmaned:/ˈtʃɛəmən/ /ˈtʃɛəˌmænd/ | v. |担任主席；主持|acted as chairman of a meeting or organization.  |例句：She chairmaned the meeting effectively. 她有效地主持了会议。|例句：He has chairmaned several important committees. 他担任过几个重要委员会的主席。|近义词：presided over, led, headed, moderated|反义词:participated, attended|词性:动词

词源:
From "chairman" + "-ed". "Chairman" itself comes from "chair" (meaning a seat of authority) + "man".  While "chairman" has become less common due to its gendered nature, the verb form "chairmaned" persists, although "chaired" is generally preferred.
