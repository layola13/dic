houseflies:/ˈhaʊsflaɪz/
n.
家蝇
A common type of fly often found in and around human habitations.
Houseflies can spread diseases. 家蝇会传播疾病。
近义词：flies
反义词：（无直接反义词，可以考虑 pest 害虫 或 beneficial insect 益虫）
词性：名词

Etymology:
From house + fly.  The word "fly" comes from Old English *flēoge, from Proto-Germanic *fleugō, from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to flow, swim, fly”).  "House" comes from Old English hūs, from Proto-Germanic *hūsan, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱews- (“to cover, conceal”).
