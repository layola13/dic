goody-goody:/ˈɡʊdi ɡʊdi/| adj. n.|伪善的，装作一本正经的人|A person who is affectedly good or proper, often to an irritating degree.  Someone who behaves in a way intended to appear morally superior.|例句：She's such a goody-goody, always telling the teacher what everyone else is doing. 她真是个伪君子，总是告诉老师其他人在做什么。

例句：He was a goody-goody as a child, always following the rules. 他小时候是个循规蹈矩的孩子，总是遵守规则。|近义词：prig, sanctimonious, self-righteous|反义词：rebellious, mischievous, wicked|词性:形容词/名词
