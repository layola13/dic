inchoative:/ɪnˈkoʊətɪv/| adj. n.|开始的，初始的；未完成的；开始阶段| An inchoative verb indicates the beginning of an action or process.  An inchoative form is a word, especially a verb, that expresses the beginning of an action.  As a noun, it refers to a verb or verb form expressing inception or beginning.|The verb "to darken" is the inchoative form of "dark." The darkening sky signaled an impending storm. |近义词：inceptive, beginning, commencing|反义词: conclusive, ending, finished|词性: adjective, noun
