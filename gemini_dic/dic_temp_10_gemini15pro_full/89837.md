horning:/ˈhɔːrnɪŋ/| n.|戴角；角状物|The deer's horning was impressive. 鹿的角很壮观。|例句：The horning of the young stag signified its maturity. 小雄鹿长角标志着它的成熟。|近义词：antler, horn|反义词: None|n.
