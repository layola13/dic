overbrightness:/ˌoʊvərˈbraɪtnəs/ | n. | 过亮；过度亮度 | Excessive brightness; a condition where something is brighter than it should be or is comfortable to view. | The overbrightness of the screen gave me a headache. 屏幕过亮让我头疼。|  The overbrightness of the image made it difficult to see the details. 图像过亮使得难以看清细节。 | 近义词: excessive brightness, glare | 反义词: dimness, darkness | 词性: 名词


词源:

"over-" (prefix) + "brightness" (noun)

"over-" 表示“过度”、“超过”

"brightness" 表示“亮度”
