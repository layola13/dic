brainstorming:/ˈbreɪnstɔːrmɪŋ/
noun
头脑风暴,脑力激荡
A group creativity technique by which efforts are made to find a conclusion for a specific problem by gathering a list of ideas spontaneously contributed by its members.
We need a brainstorming session to come up with new marketing ideas. 我们需要一个头脑风暴会议来提出新的营销理念。
近义词:idea generation, thought showering
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

词源: "Brain" + "storming" -  A metaphorical combination suggesting a sudden burst of ideas from the brain.
