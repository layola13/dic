hyoglossus:/ˌhaɪoʊˈɡlɒsəs/ | n. |舌骨舌肌|A thin, quadrilateral muscle extending from the hyoid bone to the side of the tongue. It depresses the sides of the tongue and helps retract it.|The hyoglossus muscle plays a crucial role in swallowing and speech. 舌骨舌肌在吞咽和说话中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: None (highly specific anatomical term)|Antonyms: None (highly specific anatomical term)|noun
