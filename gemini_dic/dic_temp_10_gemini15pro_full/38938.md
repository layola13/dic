confidency:/ˈkɒnfɪdənsi/| n.|自信；把握|The state of feeling certain about the truth of something.|He spoke with confidency about the future of the company. 他自信地谈论了公司的未来。|近义词：certainty, assurance, self-assurance, confidence|反义词：uncertainty, doubt, diffidence|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French confidence, from Latin confidentia, from confident- ‘having full trust’, from the verb confidere, from con- ‘with’ + fidere ‘to trust’.
