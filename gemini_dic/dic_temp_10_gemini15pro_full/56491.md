domed:/doʊmd/| adj.|圆顶的，拱形的|Having a dome, or resembling a dome. |例句：The domed stadium hosted the championship game. 圆顶体育场举办了冠军赛。

例句：The mosque has a beautiful domed roof.  这座清真寺有一个美丽的圆顶屋顶。


近义词：arched, vaulted, rounded|反义词:flat|词性:形容词


词源：From dome + -ed.  "Dome" ultimately comes from Latin "domus" (house).
