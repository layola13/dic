howard:/ˈhaʊərd/ |n.|霍华德 (男子名)|A masculine given name of Germanic origin.  It is derived from the Old High German name "Hugihard," composed of the elements "hug," meaning "heart, mind, spirit," and "hard," meaning "brave, hardy."|My friend Howard is a talented musician. 我的朋友霍华德是一位才华横溢的音乐家。|Synonyms:  (As a name, no direct synonyms, but other common male names could be considered)  John, David, Michael|Antonyms:  (Names do not have antonyms)|noun
