dunce:/dʌns/
noun
笨学生；傻瓜；蠢人
a stupid person, especially a student who is slow to learn
例句：He was treated like a dunce in school.  他在学校里被当成笨蛋对待。

例句：She felt like a dunce when she couldn't answer the easy question.  当她回答不出这个简单的问题时，她觉得自己像个傻瓜。

近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, simpleton, blockhead
反义词：genius, scholar, intellectual
词性：noun
