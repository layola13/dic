catadromous: /kəˈtædrəməs/
adjective
降河产卵的；降河洄游的
Migrating down rivers to the sea to spawn.  (Of fish, such as the eel.)
Eels are catadromous fish, spending their adult lives in freshwater but migrating to the ocean to breed.  鳗鱼是降河产卵的鱼类，它们的成年生活在淡水中，但会迁移到海洋繁殖。
The European eel is a classic example of a catadromous fish. 欧洲鳗鱼是降河洄游鱼类的典型例子。
近义词: ocean-spawning
反义词: anadromous (溯河产卵的)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek κατά (katá, “down”) + δρόμος (drómos, “running, course”).
