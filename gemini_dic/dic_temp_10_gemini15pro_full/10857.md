arabin:/ˈærəbɪn/| n.|阿拉伯糖|A white crystalline sugar, C5H10O5, obtained by hydrolysis of certain gums, such as gum arabic and beet pulp.|Arabin is a component of many plant cell walls. 阿拉伯糖是许多植物细胞壁的组成部分。|The arabin was extracted from the gum arabic. 从阿拉伯树胶中提取了阿拉伯糖。|Synonyms: pectinose, arabose|Antonyms: None|Noun
