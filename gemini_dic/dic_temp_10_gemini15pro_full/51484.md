detoxing:/diːˈtɒksɪŋ/
verb
排毒，戒除毒瘾
the process of removing toxic substances or qualities.
She's detoxing from alcohol. 她正在戒酒。
近义词: cleansing, purifying
反义词: contaminating, polluting
词性: verb (present participle)

detox:/ˈdiːtɒks/
noun
解毒，戒毒期，戒毒中心
a period or process of abstaining from or ridding the body of toxic or unhealthy substances; a clinic or facility that provides such treatment.
She went to a detox center to get off drugs. 她去了一家戒毒中心戒毒。
近义词: cleanse, purification
反义词: contamination, pollution
词性: noun

Etymology:  "detox" and "detoxing" are derived from "detoxification". "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse", and "toxic" refers to poison.  The "-ification" suffix turns the word into a noun representing the process. "-ing" creates the present participle form of the verb "detoxify".
