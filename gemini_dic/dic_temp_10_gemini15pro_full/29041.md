cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/| n. |涡卷形装饰;  铭牌; (古埃及象形文字的)椭圆框 |A carved tablet or drawing, typically oval or oblong in shape, used ornamentally or bearing an inscription. In ancient Egypt, a cartouche enclosed hieroglyphs representing a royal name.| The pharaoh's name was inscribed within a cartouche.  法老的名字刻在一个椭圆框内。| synonyms: medallion, ornament, emblem, insignia | antonyms: N/A | noun
