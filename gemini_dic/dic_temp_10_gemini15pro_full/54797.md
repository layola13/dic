dispense:/dɪˈspɛns/ | v. | 分配；分发；提供；免除；配（药）；执行 | to distribute or provide (something, especially a service or information) to a number of people. To prepare and give out medicine. To exempt or release from a duty or obligation. | The government dispensed emergency food to the refugees. 政府向难民分发了紧急食品。| synonyms: distribute, allocate, administer, provide, supply | antonyms: withhold, retain, collect, gather | verb

dispense:/dɪˈspɛns/ | n. | 分配；分发 | the act of dispensing something.  (Less common as a noun) | The dispense of justice is crucial.  正义的执行至关重要。| synonyms: distribution, allocation, administration | antonyms: collection, gathering | noun
