disarticulator:/dɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtər/
n.
脱节器，断骨器
A tool or instrument used to separate bones at their joints.
The anthropologist used a disarticulator to prepare the skeleton for study.  人类学家使用脱节器准备骨骼以供研究。

The disarticulator made it easier to clean the individual bones.  断骨器使得更容易清洁单个骨骼。
近义词:separator, dissector
反义词:articulator
词性:noun
