erodable:/ɪˈroʊdəbl/ | adj. | 可侵蚀的，易受腐蚀的 | Susceptible to erosion; easily worn away by natural forces (wind, water, ice). | The soft sandstone cliffs are highly erodable.  柔软的砂岩悬崖很容易被侵蚀。 | The coastline is erodable due to the constant wave action.  由于持续的波浪作用，海岸线很容易被侵蚀。 | synonyms: corrodible, vulnerable, degradable | antonyms:  impervious, resistant, durable | adjective
