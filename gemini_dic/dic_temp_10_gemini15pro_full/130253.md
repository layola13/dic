non-spiritual:/ˌnɒnˈspɪrɪtʃuəl/ | adj. | 非精神的，世俗的 | Not concerned with religious or spiritual matters; worldly. |  She's a very non-spiritual person, more interested in practical matters. 她是一个非常不注重精神生活的人，对实际的事情更感兴趣。 | He led a rather non-spiritual life, focused on business and pleasure. 他过着一种相当世俗的生活，专注于生意和享乐。 | 近义词: secular, worldly, materialistic, earthly | 反义词: spiritual, religious, devout, pious | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".
spiritual (adjective): from Late Latin spiritualis "of breathing, of the spirit," from Latin spiritus "breath, spirit".
