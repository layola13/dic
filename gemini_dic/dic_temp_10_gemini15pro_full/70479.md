filaceous:/fɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
丝状的；纤维状的
Composed of or resembling filaments; threadlike.
The fungus had a filaceous structure, spreading across the surface like fine hairs.  这种真菌呈丝状结构，像细毛一样在表面蔓延。
The spider's filaceous silk shimmered in the sunlight. 蜘蛛的丝状蛛丝在阳光下闪闪发光。
近义词:  threadlike, filamentous, fibrous
反义词:  thick, solid, robust
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *filace- (stem of filacium "spinning basket"), from filum "thread".
