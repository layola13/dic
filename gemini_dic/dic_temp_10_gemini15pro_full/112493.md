lour:/laʊər/ (US), /ˈlaʊə(r)/ (UK)| vi. | 皱眉；阴沉；(天空)变黑暗 | to look angry or sullen, typically with a frowning expression; (of the sky) to become dark or threatening | The sky loured, and soon it began to rain. 天空阴沉下来，很快就要下雨了。|  He loured at me from across the table. 他隔着桌子怒视着我。 | synonyms: frown, scowl, glare, darken, gloom | antonyms: brighten, clear, smile, beam | verb
