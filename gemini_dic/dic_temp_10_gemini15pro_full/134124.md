nonimpedimental:/ˌnɒnɪmˌpɛdɪˈmɛntəl/
adjective
无障碍的，不妨碍的
Presenting no impediment; not hindering or obstructing.
The flat terrain proved nonimpedimental to the army's advance.  这片平坦的地形对军队的推进没有构成障碍。
The new regulations are nonimpedimental to trade. 新的规章制度不会妨碍贸易。
近义词: unobstructed, unhindered, facilitating
反义词: obstructive, hindering, impeding
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From non- + impedimental, from impediment (n.) + -al (suffix forming adjectives). Impediment comes from Latin impedimentum "hindrance, obstacle," from impedire "to shackle the feet of, entangle, hinder," from in- "in, on" + pes (genitive pedis) "foot."
