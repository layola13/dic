monarchies: /ˈmɒnəkiːz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnərkiːz/ (US)
|n.|君主制；君主政体；君主国（monarchy的复数）|A system of government in which one person reigns, usually a king or queen, whose position is hereditary and often for life.|Many European countries were once monarchies but are now republics. 许多欧洲国家曾经是君主制国家，但现在是共和国。|The conference brought together representatives from several monarchies.  这次会议汇集了来自几个君主国的代表。|近义词：kingdoms, empires|反义词：republics|词性: 名词

词源:
From Old French *monarchie*, from Latin *monarchia*, from Ancient Greek μοναρχία (monarkhía, “rule by one”), from μόνος (mónos, “alone, single”) + ἄρχω (árkhō, “to rule”).
