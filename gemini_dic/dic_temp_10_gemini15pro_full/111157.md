lithiasis:/lɪˈθaɪəsɪs/
n.
结石病
The formation of stony concretions (calculi) in the body, such as kidney stones or gallstones.
He suffers from renal lithiasis. 他患有肾结石。
近义词:calculus formation, stone formation
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "stone dissolution")
