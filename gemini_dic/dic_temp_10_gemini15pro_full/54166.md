disenthrallment:/ˌdɪsɪnˈθrɔːlmənt/
n.
解放，释放
The act of freeing someone from bondage or oppression; liberation.
The disenthrallment of the slaves was a long and difficult process. 奴隶的解放是一个漫长而艰难的过程。
liberation, freedom, release, emancipation
captivity, enslavement, subjugation
noun

disenthrall:/ˌdɪsɪnˈθrɔːl/
v.
解放，释放
To free from bondage or oppression; liberate.
Education can disenthrall people from ignorance and superstition. 教育可以使人们摆脱无知和迷信。
liberate, free, release, emancipate
enslave, imprison, confine
verb
