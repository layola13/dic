asphyxy: /æsˈfɪksi/ | n. | 窒息，窒息状态 |A condition arising when the body is deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocation.| The victim died of asphyxy. 受害者死于窒息。 | 近义词: suffocation, choking, strangulation | 反义词: respiration, breathing | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *asphyxie*, from Late Latin *asphyxia*, from Greek *asphuxia*, from *a-* (without) + *sphuxis* (pulse).
