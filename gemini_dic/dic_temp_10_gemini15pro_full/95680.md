in conclusion:/ɪn kənˈkluːʒən/| adverbial phrase|总之，最后|Used to introduce the concluding section of a piece of writing or a speech. It signals that the speaker or writer is summarizing the main points and offering a final thought or judgment.|In conclusion, regular exercise and a balanced diet are essential for good health. 总之，规律的运动和均衡的饮食对健康至关重要。|Synonyms: finally, lastly, to sum up, in summary, to conclude|Antonyms: initially, at first, to begin with|phrase
