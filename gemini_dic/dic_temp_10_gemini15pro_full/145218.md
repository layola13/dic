overbearingly:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋli/
adverb
专横地，傲慢地
in a bossy and domineering manner; showing arrogant superiority to and disdain of those one views as unworthy.
He spoke overbearingly to the waiter. 他对服务员说话很傲慢。
Synonyms: domineeringly, imperiously, haughtily, arrogantly, dictatorially, tyrannically, autocratically
Antonyms: humbly, modestly, submissively, meekly
词性: 副词
