outbelches:/ˌaʊtˈbɛltʃɪz/
|v.|大声喷出; 咆哮而出|To erupt or expel (something, such as smoke, fire, or lava) forcefully, or to emit a loud, deep belch.|The volcano outbelched a cloud of ash and smoke. 火山喷出一团火山灰和烟雾。|近义词：erupt, spew, belch, expel|反义词: inhale, absorb|词性:动词

outbelches:/ˌaʊtˈbɛltʃɪz/
|n.|喷出物|The material that is erupted or expelled forcefully.|The outbelches from the factory polluted the air. 工厂排出的废气污染了空气。|近义词：emissions, discharge, output|反义词: intake, input|词性:名词

词源： out- + belch.  Out- 表示“向外”，belch 表示“打嗝，喷出”。


注意事项：这个词比较生动形象，多用于描述火山、引擎、工厂等喷出烟雾、火焰、熔岩等物质的场景。 在日常生活中较少使用。
