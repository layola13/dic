hobnobbing:/ˈhɒbnɒbɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbnɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 交往，交际；(尤指)与地位高的人交往 | The act of mixing socially, especially with those of higher social status. | He was seen hobnobbing with several celebrities at the party. 他被看到在派对上与几位名人交往。| synonyms: socializing, mingling, fraternizing, associating | antonyms: isolating, avoiding, shunning | noun
