parnassian:/pɑːrˈnæsiən/ /pɑːrˈnæsiən/
| adj. n.|帕尔纳斯的，帕纳斯派诗人|Relating to Parnassus, a mountain in Greece associated with poetry and the Muses; also refers to a 19th-century French school of poetry that emphasized formal perfection and restraint of emotion.|Her poetry is characterized by a Parnassian attention to form. 她的诗歌以帕尔纳斯式的对形式的关注为特征。|Synonyms: classical, formal, restrained, academic, polished|Antonyms: romantic, emotional, expressive, passionate, unrestrained|adjective
