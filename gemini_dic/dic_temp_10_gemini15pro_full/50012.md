denoisers:/diːˈnɔɪzərz/| n.|降噪器，去噪器|A denoiser is a tool or algorithm designed to reduce or eliminate noise from a signal, such as an image, audio recording, or other data.|The photographer used a denoiser to remove the grain from the high-ISO image. 摄影师使用降噪器去除高 ISO 图像中的噪点。|Synonyms: noise reducers, filters|Antonyms: noise generators, amplifiers|Noun

denoise:/diːˈnɔɪz/| v.|降噪，去噪|To remove noise from a signal, such as an image, audio recording, or other data.|The software can denoise the audio recording to make it clearer. 该软件可以对录音进行降噪，使其更清晰。|Synonyms: noise reduction, filtering|Antonyms: noise generation, amplification|Verb

denoising:/diːˈnɔɪzɪŋ/| n.|降噪，去噪|The process of removing noise from a signal.|Denoising is an important step in image processing. 降噪是图像处理中的一个重要步骤。|Synonyms: noise reduction, filtering|Antonyms: noise generation, amplification|Noun
