leprousness:/ˈlɛprəsnəs/| n. |麻风病, 麻风病的症状; (比喻)道德败坏|The state of being leprous; having leprosy.  A condition resembling leprosy, especially in being morally corrupting or destructive. |The leprousness of the abandoned building was evident in its crumbling facade. 这栋废弃建筑的破败从它摇摇欲坠的外墙上可见一斑。|The novel explored the leprousness of political corruption. 这部小说探讨了政治腐败的危害。|近义词：leprosy, decay, corruption|反义词:health, purity|词性:noun

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French lepros, from late Latin leprosus (see leprous).
