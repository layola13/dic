heritance: /ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/
n.
继承；遗产；遗传
The act or process of inheriting something, or the fact of being inherited. Something that is inherited.
She received a large inheritance from her grandfather. 她从她祖父那里继承了一大笔遗产。
His musical talent is an inheritance from his mother. 他的音乐天赋是从他母亲那里遗传来的。
legacy, heritage, patrimony
bequest

词性: 名词 (noun)
