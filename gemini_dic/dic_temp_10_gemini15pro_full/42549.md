countermoves:/ˈkaʊntərmuːvz/ /ˈkaʊntɚˌmuvz/| n.|反制措施，应对策略|An action taken in opposition to another action.|例句：The company made a series of countermoves to protect its market share.  公司采取了一系列应对措施来保护其市场份额。


例句：His countermoves were swift and effective. 他的反击迅速而有效。

近义词：countermeasures, responses, reactions, retaliations|反义词:inactions, submissions|词性:noun
