contrariantly:/ˌkɒntrəˈrɛəriənli/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈrɛəriənli/| adv. |相反地，反驳地|In a contrary manner; in opposition to something. |He smiled contrariantly, clearly disagreeing with her statement. 他反驳地笑了笑，显然不同意她的说法。|例句：She acted contrariantly to her usual behavior. 她一反常态。|近义词：perversely, oppositely, contradictorily|反义词：agreeably, compliantly|词性:副词

contrary:/ˈkɒntrəri/ /ˈkɑːntrɛri/| adj. n.|相反的，对立的；相反，对立面|Opposite in nature, direction, or meaning. |The wind was blowing in a contrary direction. 风向相反。|例句：Contrary to popular belief, cats can be very affectionate. 与普遍的看法相反，猫其实很 affectionate。|近义词：opposite, opposing, conflicting|反义词：similar, alike, same|词性:形容词/名词

词源:
From Middle English *contrarie*, from Old French *contraire*, from Latin *contrarius* (“opposite, contrary”), from *contra* (“against”).
