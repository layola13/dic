antiexpansionism:/ˌæntiɪkˈspænʃənɪzəm/
n.
反扩张主义
opposition to expansion, especially territorial expansion.
The senator's antiexpansionism made him unpopular with the more hawkish members of the party.  参议员的反扩张主义立场让他在党内更强硬的成员中不受欢迎。
近义词：noninterventionism, isolationism
反义词：expansionism, imperialism
词性：名词
