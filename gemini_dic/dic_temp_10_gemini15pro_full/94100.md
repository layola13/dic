impeachabilities:/ɪmˌpiːtʃəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可弹劾性；无过失；无可指责
The quality or state of being unimpeachable.  The fact that someone cannot be impeached or accused of a serious crime or misdemeanor.
The judge's impeccabilities were never questioned.  法官的无可指责从未受到质疑。
Synonym:  irreproachability, blamelessness, faultlessness
Antonym: culpability, guilt, impeachability
词性: 名词
