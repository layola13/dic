brain-child:/ˈbreɪnˌtʃaɪld/| n.|智力产物，脑力劳动的成果，主意，发明|An idea or invention that is the product of someone's creative imagination. |例句：This project is the brainchild of our CEO. 这个项目是我们CEO的创意。

例句：The new marketing campaign was her brainchild.  新的营销活动是她的创意。

近义词：invention, creation, innovation, idea, concept|反义词: imitation, copy|词性:noun
