meditativeness:/ˌmɛdɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/| n.|冥想; 沉思;|The quality of being meditative; given to or characterized by meditation.  A state of deep thought or contemplation.|She achieved a state of deep meditativeness through yoga. 她通过瑜伽达到了深度冥想的状态。|近义词：contemplativeness, thoughtfulness, pensiveness|反义词：distraction, restlessness|词性:noun
Etymology: From meditate + -ive + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin meditari "to ponder".
