nontragical:/nɒnˈtrædʒɪkəl/
adjective
非悲剧的; 不悲惨的
Not tragic; not involving tragedy or sorrow.
The ending of the movie was surprisingly nontragical, leaving the audience feeling uplifted. 这部电影的结局出人意料地并非悲剧，让观众感到振奋。
Synonyms: untragic, happy, joyful, cheerful, lighthearted
Antonyms: tragic, sorrowful, sad, depressing, mournful
词性: 形容词
