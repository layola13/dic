disservices:/dɪsˈsɜːrvɪsɪz/
|n.|损害，伤害，帮倒忙|A disservice is an action that harms or damages someone or something, often unintentionally.|His comments were a disservice to the community, furthering harmful stereotypes. 他的言论对社区造成了损害，进一步加深了有害的刻板印象。|The media did her a disservice by publishing misleading information. 媒体发布了误导性信息，对她造成了损害。|detriment, harm, injury|service, help, benefit|noun
