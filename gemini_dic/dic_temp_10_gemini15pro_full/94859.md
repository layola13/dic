in-a-manner-of-speaking:/ɪn ə ˈmænər əv ˈspiːkɪŋ/
|adv.|可以这么说；在某种意义上|In a manner of speaking, he is my brother.  可以这么说，他算是我兄弟。|English explanation: Used to indicate that something is not entirely true or accurate, but is close enough to be considered true in a general sense.|例句：In a manner of speaking, the project was a success. 可以这么说，这个项目是成功的。|近义词：so to speak, in a way, sort of, kind of|反义词:literally, precisely|词性:副词
