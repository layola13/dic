limelighted: /ˈlaɪmˌlaɪtɪd/
verb
聚光灯下的；引人注目的
Having been made the focus of public attention.
The actress was limelighted after her Oscar win.  这位女演员在获得奥斯卡奖后备受瞩目。

adjective
聚光灯下的；引人注目的
Being the focus of public attention.
She enjoyed the limelighted existence of a celebrity. 她享受着名人聚光灯下的生活。


近义词: prominent, celebrated, publicized, featured
反义词: obscure, unknown, anonymous, ignored
词性: verb, adjective
词源: From "limelight," a type of intense stage lighting used in the 19th century, from the chemical compound calcium oxide (lime) which, when heated, produces a brilliant white light.
