mesmerizer:/ˈmezməˌraɪzər/ | n. |催眠师，迷住人的人|A person who mesmerizes or hypnotizes.  | He's a real mesmerizer with his smooth talk and intense gaze. 他说话流畅，目光炯炯，真是个迷人的人。| synonyms: hypnotist, charmer, enchanter | antonyms: bore, dullard | noun

mesmerize:/ˈmezməˌraɪz/ | vt. | 催眠，迷住|To hypnotize or fascinate someone completely. | The magician's performance mesmerized the audience. 魔术师的表演迷住了观众。| synonyms: captivate, enthrall, spellbind | antonyms: bore, repel | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Franz Mesmer (1734-1815), an Austrian physician who developed a theory of animal magnetism, later called mesmerism.
