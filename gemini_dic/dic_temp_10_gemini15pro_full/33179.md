chromoblast:/ˈkrəʊməblæst/| n. |色素细胞，成色素细胞|An immature pigment cell, especially one that develops into a pigment cell containing a coloured plastid.  A cell from which a pigment cell develops. |例句：Chromoblasts are responsible for the vibrant colors in many flowers and fruits. 成色素细胞是许多花卉和水果鲜艳颜色的原因。

例句：The development of chromoblasts is crucial for the coloration of certain animal tissues. 成色素细胞的发育对于某些动物组织的着色至关重要。|近义词：pigmentocyte|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
