filthify:/ˈfɪlθɪfaɪ/
verb
使污秽，弄脏
to make filthy; defile.
The scandal filthified his reputation. 这桩丑闻玷污了他的名声。
The mud filthified my new shoes. 泥巴弄脏了我的新鞋。
近义词:soil, dirty, pollute, contaminate, taint
反义词:clean, cleanse, purify
词性:v.
