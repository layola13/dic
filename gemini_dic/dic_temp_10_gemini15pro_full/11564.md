aristophanes:/ˌærɪˈstɒfəniːz/ /ˌærɪˈstɑːfəniːz/
n.
阿里斯托芬
Ancient Greek comedic playwright, considered the greatest representative of Old Attic Comedy.
Aristophanes satirized the political and social life of Athens in plays like "The Clouds" and "Lysistrata."
阿里斯托芬在他的戏剧《云》和《吕西斯特拉特》中讽刺了雅典的政治和社会生活。
近义词: playwright, dramatist, comedian
反义词: tragedian
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Aristophanes, from Ancient Greek Ἀριστοφάνης (Aristophánēs).  The name is possibly derived from ἀριστεύς (aristeús, “best, noblest”) + φαίνω (phaínō, “to show, to appear”).
