howker:/ˈhɔːkər/| n.|叫卖小贩；沿街叫卖者|A person who sells goods, typically food or small items, by shouting in the street or going from door to door.|The howker advertised his wares with a loud voice. 叫卖小贩大声吆喝着他的商品。|近义词：vendor, peddler, street vendor|反义词:buyer, customer|词性:noun
Etymology: From the Middle English *howken*, "to carry on one's back and offer for sale," from the Old Norse *hauka*, "to hawk."
