impute:/ɪmˈpjuːt/ | v. | 归咎于；归罪于；认为…是…的起因 | to attribute or ascribe something undesirable, such as a fault or crime, to a person or group |  The police impute the accident to the driver's negligence. 警方将事故归咎于司机的疏忽。| synonyms: attribute, ascribe, assign, credit | antonyms: exculpate, absolve, exonerate | verb

impute:/ɪmˈpjuːt/ | v. | 估算；推算 | to calculate or estimate something when you do not have exact figures | The missing data were imputed based on the existing data.  缺失的数据是根据现有数据估算的。 | synonyms: estimate, calculate, reckon, gauge | antonyms: N/A | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin imputare "to reckon, count, charge," from Latin in- "into" + putare "to think, consider, reckon."
