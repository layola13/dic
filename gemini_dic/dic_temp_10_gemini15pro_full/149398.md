packability:/ˌpækəˈbɪləti/| n.|可打包性；易装性|The quality of being easy to pack and transport.  How compactly and easily something can be packed for storage or travel. |This tent gets high marks for its packability. 这款帐篷因其易于打包而获得高分。|portability, compactness, storability|bulkiness, awkwardness|noun
