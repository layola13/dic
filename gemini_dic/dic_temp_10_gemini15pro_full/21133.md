blitheness:/ˈblaɪðnəs/ /ˈblaɪθnɪs/| n. |愉快，高兴，无忧无虑|The state of being blithe; cheerful lightheartedness. |She accepted the news with surprising blitheness. 她以惊人的愉快心情接受了这个消息。|He showed a blitheness that was unusual after such a tragedy.  在如此悲剧之后，他表现出一种不同寻常的快乐。|近义词：cheerfulness, gaiety, lightheartedness, merriment, joviality|反义词：sadness, gloom, melancholy, dejection, unhappiness|词性:名词

词源:
From blithe + -ness.  Blithe comes from Old English *blīðe, from Proto-Germanic *blīþiz.  Related to Old High German blīdi "kind, friendly, cheerful."
