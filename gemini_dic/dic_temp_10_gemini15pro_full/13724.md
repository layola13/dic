atypicality:/ˌeɪtɪpɪˈkæləti/| n. |非典型性，异常性|The state or quality of being atypical; deviation from the norm.  |Her shyness was an atypicality in her otherwise extroverted family. 她的害羞在她外向的家庭中显得格格不入。|Synonyms: abnormality, anomaly, irregularity, peculiarity, eccentricity, unconventionality|Antonyms: typicality, normality, regularity, conformity|noun
