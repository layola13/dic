byword:/ˈbaɪwɜːrd/| n.|箴言，格言；代名词，绰号|A byword is a word or phrase that is associated with a particular person, place, or thing, often used as a nickname or synonym.  It can also refer to a proverb or saying. |Shakespeare is a byword for literary genius. 莎士比亚是文学天才的代名词。| synonym: proverb, saying, motto, catchphrase, nickname, epithet | antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) obscurity, anonymity | noun
