chromophotograph:/ˈkrəʊməˌfoʊtəɡræf/| n.|彩色照片|A photograph in color, typically produced by an early color photography process.|例句：The museum displayed a collection of chromophotographs from the late 19th century.  博物馆展出了一批19世纪后期的彩色照片。

例句：He found a chromophotograph of his great-grandparents in the attic. 他在阁楼里找到了一张曾祖父母的彩色照片。|近义词：color photograph, colour photograph|反义词:monochrome photograph|词性:noun
