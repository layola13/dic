molybdenite:/məˈlɪbdəˌnaɪt/| n. |辉钼矿|A soft black mineral, MoS₂, the chief ore of molybdenum.  It has a layered structure similar to graphite.|Molybdenite is an important source of molybdenum. 辉钼矿是钼的重要来源。|Molybdenite is often found in hydrothermal veins. 辉钼矿常发现于热液矿脉中。|Synonyms: molybdenum glance, molybdic sulfide|Antonyms: None|Noun
