basquish:/ˈbæskwɪʃ/| v. |征服，制服|To subdue or conquer, often by force.|例句：The army basquished the rebels and restored order. 军队征服了叛军，恢复了秩序。

例句：The king basquished his enemies and expanded his kingdom. 国王征服了他的敌人，扩张了他的王国。|近义词：conquer, subdue, vanquish, defeat, overcome|反义词：surrender, yield, submit|词性：动词

词源: From Middle French *basquir* (unattested), probably from Basque *ba-etz-ki* "under him/it," from *ba* "under" + *etz* (variant of *ez*) "he/it" + *ki* "also, too, and". This etymology is uncertain, however, and the word may be of Romance origin.
