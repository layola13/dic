governor:/ˈɡʌvərnər (US), ˈɡʌvənə(r) (UK)| n.|州长；总督；统治者；管理者|A governor is the political head of a state, province, or region.  A governor can also be a member of a governing board of an institution.|The governor of California gave a speech yesterday. 加州州长昨天发表了演讲。|synonyms: administrator, director, executive, leader, manager, official, overseer, ruler, superintendent|antonyms: subject, subordinate|noun

governor:/ˈɡʌvərnər (US), ˈɡʌvənə(r) (UK)| n.|调速器；调节器|(in a machine) a device that automatically controls speed.|The governor on the engine prevented it from over-revving.  发动机上的调速器防止它超速运转。|synonyms: regulator, controller, control|antonyms: accelerator|noun
