omit: /əˈmɪt/ (əˈmɪt)| v. |省略，遗漏，删除；疏忽，忘记；不做，不做应该做的事| To leave out or exclude (someone or something); fail to do or mention. |例句：She omitted to mention that she was leaving. 她没有提到她要离开。|近义词：exclude, leave out, neglect, miss, drop|反义词: include, add, insert|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *omittere*, from *omiss-*, past participle stem of *omittere*  "to let go, send away, lay aside, leave undone," from *o(b)-* "away, down" + *mittere* "to send, let go."
