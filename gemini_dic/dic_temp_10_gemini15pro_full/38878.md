conferencing:/ˈkɒnfərənsɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfərənsɪŋ/ (US)| n.|会议；电话会议；视频会议|The act of holding or participating in conferences, especially by telephone or video.|例句：Video conferencing has become essential for businesses. 视频会议对企业至关重要。|例句：They invested heavily in new conferencing technology. 他们在新的会议技术上投入了巨资。|近义词：meeting, discussion, consultation|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

conferencing:/ˈkɒnfərənsɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfərənsɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|会议的|Relating to or used for conferences.|例句：Conferencing facilities are available in the hotel. 酒店提供会议设施。|例句：This is a state-of-the-art conferencing system. 这是一个最先进的会议系统。|近义词：meeting, discussion|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "conference" + "-ing". "Conference" comes from Old French "conference," from Latin "conferentia"  "a bringing together," from "conferre" "to bring together," from "com-" (together) + "ferre" (to bring).
