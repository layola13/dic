co-organizer:/koʊˈɔːrɡənaɪzər/| n.|共同组织者，联合举办者|A person or group who organizes something together with another person or group.|例句：She was the co-organizer of the conference. 她曾是这次会议的共同组织者。

例句：The local community group acted as co-organizers for the annual music festival. 当地的社区团体担任了年度音乐节的联合举办者。

|近义词：co-planner, joint organizer, collaborator|反义词:|noun
