outprayed:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪd/
verb
超越祈祷，祈祷胜过
To have prayed longer or more fervently than someone else.
He outprayed his rival in the hopes of securing divine favor. 他祈祷的时间比他的对手长，希望能获得神的眷顾。
近义词: outdo, surpass, defeat
反义词: lose to, be surpassed by
词性: verb
