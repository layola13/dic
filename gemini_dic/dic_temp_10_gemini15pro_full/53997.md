discriminateness:/dɪˌskrɪmɪnɪtnəs/| n. | 辨别力，识别力 | The ability to distinguish accurately between things, especially those that are subtle or closely related. | Her discriminateness in choosing friends is admirable. 她择友的辨别力令人钦佩。| synonyms: discernment, judgment, perceptiveness, insight, acuteness, sagacity | antonyms: indiscrimination, naivety, gullibility | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From discriminate + -ness.  Discriminate comes from Late Latin discriminat-, past participle stem of discriminare "to divide, separate, distinguish," from Latin discrimen "interval, difference,"  from discernere "to separate, distinguish."
