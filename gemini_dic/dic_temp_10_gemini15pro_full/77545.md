GDDR:/ˌdʒiː diː diː ˈɑːr/ | n. | 图形双倍数据速率 | Graphics Double Data Rate, a type of synchronous graphics random access memory (SGRAM). It is specifically designed for use in graphics cards and other graphics processing units (GPUs). | Example: My new graphics card has GDDR6 memory. 我的新显卡有 GDDR6 内存。 | Synonym: video RAM, graphics memory | Antonym:  N/A | noun
