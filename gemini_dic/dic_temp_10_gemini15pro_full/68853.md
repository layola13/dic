fashioning:/ˈfæʃənɪŋ/
v.
塑造；制作；设计
The artist was fashioning a sculpture out of clay. 艺术家正在用黏土塑造一个雕塑。
He fashioned a makeshift shelter from branches and leaves. 他用树枝和树叶做了一个临时住所。
creating, shaping, forming, molding
destroying, ruining, dismantling
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *faschonner, from faschon “make, fashion,” from Latin factionem (nominative factio) "a making, doing, performing, a deed, act, execution," from past-participle stem of facere "to make, do."
