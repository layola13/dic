omnipresent:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.ənt/ | adj. | 无所不在的，普遍存在的 | Present everywhere at the same time. | God is considered omnipresent by many religions. 上帝被许多宗教认为是无所不在的。

The influence of social media is omnipresent in modern life. 社交媒体的影响在现代生活中无处不在。

| 近义词：ubiquitous, pervasive, universal | 反义词：localized, limited, restricted | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *omnipraesent-*, present participle of *omnipraeesse* "to be present everywhere," from *omni-* "all" + *praeesse* "to be present," from *prae-* "before" + *esse* "to be."
