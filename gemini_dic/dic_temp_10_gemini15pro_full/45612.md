curry:/ˈkɜːri/ (UK), /ˈkʌri/ (US)| n. vt.|咖喱，咖喱粉；用咖喱烹调|A dish originating in South Asia, consisting of meat or vegetables cooked in a spicy sauce.|例句：I love chicken curry. 我喜欢鸡肉咖喱。|近义词：spice,seasoning|反义词:blandness|noun

curry:/ˈkɜːri/ (UK), /ˈkʌri/ (US)| vt.|用咖喱烹调|To flavor or cook with curry.|例句：She curried the lamb with a variety of spices. 她用各种香料给羊肉做了咖喱。|近义词：season, spice|反义词:N/A|verb
