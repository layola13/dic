dumpster:/ˈdʌmpstər/ (US) /ˈdʌmpstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |垃圾箱，垃圾桶|A large metal container into which people put garbage or trash, usually found outside buildings. |The dumpster was overflowing with trash. 垃圾桶里堆满了垃圾。|Synonyms: bin, skip (UK), trash can |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object, but conceptually: recycling bin) |noun

dumpster:/ˈdʌmpstər/ (US) /ˈdʌmpstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |(informal) 任何混乱或不愉快的情况|A messy or unpleasant situation. |His life was a dumpster fire after he lost his job. 他失业后，生活一团糟。|Synonyms: mess, disaster, shambles|Antonyms: order, perfection |noun
