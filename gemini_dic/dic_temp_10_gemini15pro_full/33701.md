cipherable:/ˈsaɪfərəbl/ |adj.|可加密的，可译码的|Something that is cipherable can be written in or converted into cipher (a secret or disguised way of writing).|The message was cipherable, ensuring its secrecy during transmission. 这条消息是可加密的，确保了它在传输过程中的保密性。| The general issued cipherable instructions to his troops. 将军向他的部队发出了可加密的指示。|Synonyms: encodable, decryptable|Antonyms: indecipherable, uncodable|adjective
