marinated:/ˈmærɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|腌制的，浸泡过的|Soaked in a marinade.  The meat was marinated overnight.|例句：The marinated chicken was delicious. 腌制的鸡肉很美味。

例句：We had marinated tofu for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了腌制的豆腐。|近义词：soaked, steeped, pickled|反义词:unmarinated, dry|词性:形容词

marinate:/ˈmærɪneɪt/| v.|腌制，浸泡|To soak food in a marinade.|例句：I'm going to marinate the steak for a few hours before grilling it. 我打算在烤牛排前把它腌制几个小时。

例句：She marinated the chicken in a mixture of herbs and spices. 她用香草和香料的混合物腌制了鸡肉。|近义词：soak, steep, pickle|反义词:dry|词性:动词

词源: From French *mariner*, from Italian *marinare* "to pickle in brine," from *marina* "brine," from Late Latin *marina* (aqua) "(water of the) sea," from Latin *marinus* "of the sea," from *mare* "sea."
