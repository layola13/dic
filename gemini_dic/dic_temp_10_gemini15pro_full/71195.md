fistula:/ˈfɪstʃələ/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊlə/ (UK)| n.|瘘管；瘘|An abnormal connection or passageway between two organs or vessels that do not normally connect.  A fistula can occur in various parts of the body.|The patient developed a fistula between the bowel and the bladder.|Synonym: channel, passage|Antonym: closure|noun
