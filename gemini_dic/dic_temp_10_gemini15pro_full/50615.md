deregulators:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪtərz/
n.
解除管制者；撤销管制规定者
People who remove or reduce government regulations on a particular industry or activity.
The deregulators argued that less government intervention would lead to greater economic efficiency. 解除管制者认为，更少的政府干预将带来更高的经济效率。

The airline industry benefited greatly from the changes introduced by the deregulators. 航空业从解除管制者引入的变革中受益匪浅。

近义词:liberalizers
反义词:regulators

词性:名词(plural)
