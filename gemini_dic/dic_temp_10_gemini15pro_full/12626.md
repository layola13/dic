assassinative:/əˈsæsɪˌneɪtɪv/
adjective
暗杀的；行刺的；刺客似的
Characterized by or resembling assassination; suggestive of an assassin.
His assassinative gaze made her shiver. 他那刺客般的眼神让她不寒而栗。
近义词：murderous, homicidal, deadly
反义词：peaceful, harmless
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From assassinate + -ive.  Ultimately from the Arabic word "hashishiyya" (حشيشية), referring to a group of Nizari Ismailis who were reputed to be assassins.
