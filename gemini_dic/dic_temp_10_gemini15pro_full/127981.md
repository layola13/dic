nep:/nep/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or naive person.|例句：He's such a nep, he believed everything she said. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信她说的每一句话。

例句：Don't be a nep, that's obviously a scam. 别傻了，那显然是骗局。|近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, simpleton|反义词：genius, sage|词性:名词

词源: Possibly a shortened form of "nephew," used condescendingly. 也可能是"nephew"的缩写，用于带有优越感的称呼。
