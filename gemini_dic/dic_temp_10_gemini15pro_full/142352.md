orthokinetic:/ˌɔːrθəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 正动力学的，直线运动的 | Relating to movement in a straight line, especially of particles in a fluid due to differences in their velocity caused by forces such as gravity or an electric field. |  The orthokinetic aggregation of particles is important in various industrial processes. 正动力学的粒子聚集在各种工业过程中都很重要。

The orthokinetic flocculation process is used in water treatment. 正动力学絮凝工艺用于水处理。 | 近义词: rectilinear | 反义词: Brownian (related to random motion) | 词性: adjective

词源: From ortho- (Greek 'straight, right') + kinetic (Greek 'moving').
