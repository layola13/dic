adjudged:/əˈdʒʌdʒd/
verb
判决；裁定；宣布
Considered or declared to be (legally or officially)
He was adjudged the winner of the competition. = 他被宣布为比赛的获胜者。
The court adjudged him guilty. = 法院判决他有罪。

近义词: decreed, ruled, decided, judged, deemed
反义词: acquitted, pardoned, reversed

词性: verb (过去分词)
词源: From Old French *ajujer, from Latin adjudicō (“to award”), from ad (“to”) + jūdicō (“to judge”).
