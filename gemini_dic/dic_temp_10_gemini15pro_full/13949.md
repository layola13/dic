aunty:/ˈɑːnti/ (US), /ˈɑːnti/ (UK)| n.|阿姨；姑妈；伯母；舅妈；姨妈 (a term of affection for a woman, often an older relative, or a close family friend)|Aunty is often used as a term of respect or endearment, similar to "Auntie" in American English. It is commonly used in British English, Australian English, Indian English, and other varieties of English.|例句：My aunty made me a delicious cake. 我的阿姨给我做了一个美味的蛋糕。

例句：I'm going to visit my aunty in London next week. 我下周要去伦敦看望我的阿姨。|近义词：aunt, auntie|反义词: (No direct antonym exists)|Noun
