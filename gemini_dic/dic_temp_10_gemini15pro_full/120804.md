misasserting:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrtɪŋ/
verb
错误地断言；虚报；不实陈述
Making a statement that is not true or accurate.
He was misasserting the facts of the case. 他歪曲了案件的事实。
misrepresenting, falsifying
affirming, verifying
