malmsey: /ˈmɑːmzi/ | n. | 马姆齐酒（一种甜葡萄酒） | A strong, sweet white wine from Greece or Madeira. Originally from Monemvasia in Greece. |  This malmsey is rich and decadent.  这马姆齐酒浓郁而醇厚。

She savored the sweet notes of the malmsey. 她品味着马姆齐酒的甜美气息。 | 近义词: Madeira, sweet wine | 反义词: dry wine | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *malvesie, from Old French malvoisie, from Medieval Latin malvasia, from the name of the city Monemvasia (Μονεμβάσια) in Greece, from whence the wine was exported.
