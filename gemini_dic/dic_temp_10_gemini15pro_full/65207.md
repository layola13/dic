ethynes:/ˈɛθaɪnz/ /ˈiːθaɪnz/
n.
乙炔类
A class of organic compounds containing a carbon-carbon triple bond, also called alkynes.
Acetylene is the simplest example of ethynes.  乙炔是最简单的乙炔类化合物。
Ethyne is highly reactive due to its triple bond. 由于其三键，乙炔具有很高的反应活性。
