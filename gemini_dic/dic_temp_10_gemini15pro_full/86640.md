hellbent:/ˈhelˌbent/| adj.|一心想做...的；决意要...的；拼命三郎的|Determined to achieve something at all costs; stubbornly or recklessly pursuing a goal.  It implies a single-minded focus, often with disregard for consequences or other people's feelings.|He was hellbent on revenge. 他一心想复仇。|近义词:determined, resolute, adamant, insistent, fixed, set, single-minded, obsessed, fanatical|反义词:hesitant, reluctant, wavering, ambivalent|词性:形容词

词源:
From "hell" + "bent."  "Bent" in this context means "determined" or "inclined."  The image is of someone so determined to go somewhere or do something that they are leaning or angled strongly in that direction, as if being pulled towards hell itself.
