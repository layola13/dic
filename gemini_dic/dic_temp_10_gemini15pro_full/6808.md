anarchical:/əˈnɑːkɪkəl/
adjective
无政府的；无政府主义的
Characterized by a lack of government or control; chaotic.
The country descended into an anarchical state after the revolution.  这个国家在革命后陷入了无政府状态。
The classroom was anarchical without a teacher present.  没有老师在场，教室里一片混乱。
近义词: lawless, chaotic, disorderly, ungoverned
反义词: orderly, organized, governed, controlled
词性: adjective

anarchic:/əˈnɑːkɪk/
adjective
无政府的；无政府主义的
Relating to or supporting anarchy.
He holds anarchic views about society. 他对社会持有无政府主义的观点。
The protest turned into an anarchic riot. 抗议变成了无政府主义的暴乱。
近义词: lawless, chaotic, disorderly, ungoverned
反义词: orderly, organized, governed, controlled
词性: adjective


词源: From Late Latin *anarchicus, from Greek *anarchikos, from anarkhos "without a chief, rulerless," from an- "without" + arkhos "leader, ruler."
