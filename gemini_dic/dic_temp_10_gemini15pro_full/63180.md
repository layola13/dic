ental:/ˈɛntəl/
adjective
of or relating to the mind
Relating to or involving the mind.  This word is not commonly used on its own but is usually part of a compound word.
Mental health is important. 心理健康很重要。
synonyms: psychological, intellectual, cognitive
antonyms: physical, bodily
词性: adjective
