litigiousness:/lɪˈtɪdʒəsnəs/
n.
诉讼倾向，好讼性
A tendency toward suing people or initiating lawsuits; inclination to engage in lawsuits.
The company’s litigiousness has earned it a bad reputation.  这家公司的好讼性使其名声不好。
The litigiousness of the family is well known in the town.  这个家庭的好讼性在镇上是出了名的。
近义词:disputatiousness, quarrelsomeness, contentiousness
反义词:agreeableness, peacefulness
词性:noun
