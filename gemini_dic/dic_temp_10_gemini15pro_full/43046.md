cowiest:/ˈkaʊɪɪst/
adjective
最胆小的;最懦弱的
most timid or cowardly.
例句：He was the cowiest boy in the class, always afraid to speak up. 他是班上最胆小的男孩，总是害怕大声说话。

例句：She gave him a cowiest look and ran away. 她胆怯地看了他一眼就跑了。

近义词：timid, cowardly, fearful, apprehensive, faint-hearted
反义词：brave, courageous, bold, daring, fearless
词性：adjective
