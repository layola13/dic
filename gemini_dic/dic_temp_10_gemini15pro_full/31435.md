charismatics:/ˌkærɪzˈmætɪks/
n.|魅力型人物；有魅力的人（charismatic 的复数）|People are drawn to charismatics. 人们会被有魅力的人所吸引。|例句：The charismatics in the group naturally took on leadership roles.  组里那些有魅力的人自然而然地担任了领导角色。|近义词：leaders, influencers, magnetic personalities|反义词：unremarkable people, wallflowers|词性:名词
