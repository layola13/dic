dedesignation:/diːˌdɛzɪɡˈneɪʃən/| n. |取消指定，撤销名称|The dedesignation of the area as a national park caused controversy.  该地区被取消国家公园的称号引起了争议。|例句：The dedesignation of the species as endangered allowed for increased hunting.  该物种被取消濒危物种的称号，允许增加狩猎活动。|近义词：removal, revocation, cancellation|反义词：designation, appointment, nomination|词性:名词
Etymology:  de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + designation (from Latin designare, meaning "to mark out")
