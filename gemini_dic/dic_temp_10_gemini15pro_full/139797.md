octennial: /ɒkˈtɛniəl/ /ɑːkˈtɛniəl/
adjective
八年的；每八年的；延续八年的
happening every eight years; lasting for eight years
The octennial election of the city council is scheduled for next month. 市议会的八年一次的选举定于下个月举行。
The society holds octennial conferences.  该协会每八年举行一次会议。
近义词: eight-year, octoennial
反义词: annual, biennial
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *octennis*  (eight years old, occurring every eight years), from *octo* (eight) + *annus* (year).
