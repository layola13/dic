nonnebulousness:/nɒnˌnɛbjələsnəs/
noun
清晰度；明确性；非含糊性
The quality of not being nebulous; clarity, definiteness.
The nonnebulousness of the contract left no room for misinterpretation.  合同的清晰性使人无法误解。
Near Synonyms: clarity, precision, definiteness, explicitness, lucidity, perspicuity
Antonyms: nebulousness, vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity, fuzziness
