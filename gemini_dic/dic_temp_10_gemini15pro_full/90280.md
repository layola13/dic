houseleek:/ˈhaʊslɪːk/| n. |长生草，瓦松|A succulent plant of the genus *Sempervivum*, typically having rosettes of fleshy leaves and cultivated as a ground cover or rock-garden plant. It is also known as hen and chickens due to its growth habit.|例句：The houseleek thrived in the dry, rocky soil. 长生草在干燥的岩石土壤中茁壮成长。

例句：My grandmother always kept a pot of houseleeks by the kitchen window. 我祖母总是在厨房窗台上放一盆长生草。|近义词：sempervivum, hen and chickens, liveforever|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
