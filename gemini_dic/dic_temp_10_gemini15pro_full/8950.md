antihumanistic:/ˌæntiˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/
adj.
反人道主义的，反人文主义的
Opposed to or denying the principles of humanism; devaluing human life or individuality.
His antihumanistic philosophy led him to believe that individuals were expendable for the greater good. 他反人道主义的哲学使他相信，为了更大的利益，个人是可以牺牲的。
近义词:inhuman, brutal, cruel, barbaric
反义词:humanistic, humane, compassionate
词性:形容词
