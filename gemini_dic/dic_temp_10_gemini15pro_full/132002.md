noncorporations:/ˌnɒnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 非公司组织，非法人团体 |  Organizations that are not incorporated, meaning they haven't gone through the formal legal process of becoming a corporation.  They can include partnerships, sole proprietorships, and other unincorporated associations. | Example: Many small businesses operate as noncorporations to avoid the complexities of corporate law. 许多小型企业以非公司形式运营，以避免公司法的复杂性。

Example: Noncorporations often face different tax regulations compared to corporations. 与公司相比，非公司通常面临不同的税收规定。


| Synonym: unincorporated businesses, unincorporated associations | Antonym: corporations, incorporated entities | Noun |
