disinfectants:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfektənts/
noun
消毒剂
Chemical agents that destroy or inhibit the growth of microorganisms on inanimate objects.
The hospital uses powerful disinfectants to prevent the spread of infection.  医院使用强力消毒剂来防止感染传播。
synonyms: antiseptics, germicides, sanitizers
antonyms: contaminants, pollutants
