nonconversions:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɜːrʒənz/ | n. | 未转化，未转变；（尤指）未皈依者 |  Lack of conversions or a failure to convert something. This often refers to a situation where attempts to change something from one form or state to another are unsuccessful, particularly in marketing, advertising, or religious contexts. | The marketing campaign resulted in a high number of nonconversions, indicating that the target audience was not persuaded to purchase the product.  营销活动导致了大量的未转化，表明目标受众没有被说服购买产品。|Near synonyms: failures, non-responders, holdouts | Antonyms: conversions, successes | noun

nonconversion:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɜːrʒən/ | n. | 未转化，未转变；（尤指）未皈依者 | The state of not being converted. |  His nonconversion to Christianity surprised his family. 他没有皈依基督教，令他的家人感到惊讶。 | Near synonyms: resistance, refusal | Antonyms: conversion, acceptance | noun
