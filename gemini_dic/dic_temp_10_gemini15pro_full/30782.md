cesura:/siˈzjʊərə/ /siˈʒʊrə/ | n. | 休止；停顿；诗行中间的停顿 | A pause near the middle of a line of verse, typically dictated by sense or natural speech rhythm rather than by metrics. |  Example 1: The cesura in the line added a dramatic effect to the poem.  这行诗中的休止为诗歌增添了戏剧性的效果。

Example 2:  The poet used the cesura to emphasize a particular word. 诗人用休止来强调一个特定的词。 | Near synonyms: pause, break, interruption | Antonyms: continuation, flow | noun
