auditability:/ˌɔːdɪtəˈbɪləti/
|n.|可审计性|The quality of being auditable; capable of being audited.  The extent to which financial records and other information can be verified by an independent auditor.|The company improved the auditability of its financial records. 该公司提高了其财务记录的可审计性。|Synonyms: verifiability, traceability, accountability|Antonyms: non-verifiability, obscurity|Noun

auditabilities:/ˌɔːdɪtəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|可审计性（复数）|Plural form of auditability, referring to multiple instances or aspects of being auditable.|The auditabilities of different departments varied significantly. 各个部门的可审计性差异很大。|Synonyms: verifiabilities, traceabilities, accountabilities|Antonyms: non-verifiabilities, obscurities|Noun
