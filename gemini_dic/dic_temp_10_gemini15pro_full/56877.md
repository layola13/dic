dosed:/dəʊst/ |v. |给…服药；(使)服用(药物)；(给食物或饮料)掺(药) | administered a dose of medicine to. |例句：He dosed himself with cough medicine. 他服用了咳嗽药。|近义词：medicated, treated |反义词: untreated |词性：动词

dosed:/dəʊst/ |adj. |(非正式) (尤指服用了过量的药物)兴奋的，飘飘然的；(食物或饮料)掺了药的 | under the influence of drugs, especially to an excessive degree. |例句：The cookies were dosed with sleeping pills.  饼干里掺了安眠药。|近义词：drugged, intoxicated|反义词: sober |词性：形容词
