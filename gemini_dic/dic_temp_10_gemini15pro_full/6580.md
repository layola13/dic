anabranch:/ˈænəˌbrɑːntʃ/ /ˈænəˌbræntʃ/| n. |支流；汊河；分流|A branch of a river that flows away from the main stream and rejoins it further down.|The Amazon River has numerous anabranches that create a complex network of waterways. 亚马逊河有许多支流，形成了一个复杂的水道网络。|例句：

The anabranch rejoined the main river several miles downstream.  这条支流在几英里下游重新汇入干流。

Many species of fish thrive in the slower-moving waters of the anabranch. 许多种类的鱼在支流较慢的水流中茁壮成长。


|近义词：tributary, branch, offshoot, fork |反义词: mainstream|词性: 名词
