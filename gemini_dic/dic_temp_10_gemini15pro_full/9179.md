antinomianism:/ˌæntiˈnoʊmiənɪzəm/| n. |反律主义，反律法主义|The belief that Christians are released by grace from the obligation of observing the moral law.| Some sects were accused of antinomianism, claiming that faith alone was necessary for salvation.  一些教派被指责为反律主义，声称只有信仰才能得救。|Near synonyms: lawlessness, immorality, libertinism|Near antonyms: legalism, moralism|noun
