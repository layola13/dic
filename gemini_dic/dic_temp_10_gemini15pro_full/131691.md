noncondensation:/ˌnɒnkɒndenˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 非凝结，不凝结 | Absence of condensation; the process of a substance not changing from a gas to a liquid. | The noncondensation of the steam caused a build-up of pressure.  蒸汽的非凝结导致压力积聚。| The noncondensation of the water vapor in the air led to high humidity. 空气中水蒸气的非凝结导致了高湿度。 | 近义词: nonliquefaction | 反义词: condensation | 词性: 名词
