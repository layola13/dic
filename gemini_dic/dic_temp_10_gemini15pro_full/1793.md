acreages: /ˈeɪkərɪdʒɪz/
noun
土地面积; 英亩数
A large area of land, especially one used for farming or other specific purposes, measured in acres.
The family owns vast acreages in the west.  这个家族在西部拥有大片土地。
They farm several acreages in the valley. 他们在山谷里耕种几块土地。
Synonyms: lands, estates, properties, tracts, expanses
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical area.  Consider using terms like "small plot," "limited area," or specifying a smaller unit of measurement)
词性: 名词
