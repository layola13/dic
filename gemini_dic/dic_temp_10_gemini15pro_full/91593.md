hyperaesthete:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛsθiːt/ | n. |过度敏感的人；感觉过敏者|A person of highly sensitive taste or refinement, especially in art or literature. |  He was known as a hyperaesthete, appreciating the finest nuances of music and art. 他以感觉过敏者而闻名，欣赏音乐和艺术中最微妙的细微差别。|Synonyms: aesthete, connoisseur, epicure | Antonyms: philistine, boor | noun

hyperaesthesia:/ˌhaɪpərɛsˈθiːʒə/ | n. |感觉过敏；感觉过度|Excessive physical sensitivity, especially of the skin.  | The patient experienced hyperaesthesia after the surgery, finding even light touch painful. 病人在手术后出现了感觉过敏，即使是轻微的触碰也会感到疼痛。|Synonyms: hypersensitivity, oversensitivity | Antonyms: hypaesthesia, numbness | noun
