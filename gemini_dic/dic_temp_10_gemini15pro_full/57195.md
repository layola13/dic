down-payment:/ˌdaʊnˈpeɪmənt/| n. |预付款，首付，定金|A portion of the total price of an item paid at the time of purchase, with the remainder to be paid later. |例句：We made a down payment of $10,000 on the house.  我们支付了1万美元的房屋首付款。

例句：A large down payment can reduce your monthly mortgage payments.  大量的首付款可以减少你的每月抵押贷款支付。|近义词：deposit, initial payment, installment|反义词: balance due, remaining payment|词性:noun
