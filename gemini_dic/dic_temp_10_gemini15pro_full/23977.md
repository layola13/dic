breathability:/ˌbriːðəˈbɪləti/
n.|透气性，透气度|The quality of being breathable; the extent to which a fabric or material allows air to pass through it.|This fabric has excellent breathability, making it ideal for sportswear.  这种面料透气性极佳，非常适合运动服。|porosity, permeability, ventilation|impermeability, airtightness|noun
