overloyally: /ˌoʊvərˈlɔɪəli/
adverb| 过于忠诚地，过分忠诚地 | Showing excessive loyalty; too loyal. | He served his company overloyally, neglecting his family and friends. 他过于忠诚地服务于他的公司，忽略了他的家人和朋友。 |例句：She defended her friend overloyally, even when he was clearly in the wrong. 她过分忠诚地维护她的朋友，即使他明显是错的。| 近义词: excessively loyally, too faithfully, unduly devotedly | 反义词: disloyally, treacherously, faithlessly | 词性: adverb

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "loyally" (adverb form of "loyal")
