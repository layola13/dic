abandoned: /əˈbændənd/
adjective
被遗弃的，被抛弃的
Deserted or left alone, especially by someone who is responsible for it.
The abandoned car was towed away.  那辆被遗弃的汽车被拖走了。

The abandoned kitten was rescued by a kind family. 被遗弃的小猫被一个好心家庭救助了。

近义词: deserted, forsaken, derelict
反义词: inhabited, occupied, cherished
词性: adjective

abandoned: /əˈbændənd/
verb
放弃，抛弃
To leave completely and finally; to give up.
He abandoned his family and ran off with another woman. 他抛弃了家人，和另一个女人私奔了。

The sailors abandoned the sinking ship.  水手们弃船逃生。

近义词: desert, forsake, relinquish
反义词: keep, maintain, continue
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French abandoner, from abandon "control, authority, surrender," from à bandon "at disposal, at will," from a "to, at" + bandon "power, jurisdiction," from Late Latin bandum "ban, proclamation."
