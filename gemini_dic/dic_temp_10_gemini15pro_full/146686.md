overhearer:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪrər/ | n. | 偷听者；无意中听到的人 | Someone who overhears a conversation. |  I didn't mean to eavesdrop, but I became an accidental overhearer of their argument. 我不是故意偷听的，但我无意中听到了他们的争论。 | Synonyms: eavesdropper, listener | Antonyms: speaker, talker | noun

overhear:/ˌoʊvərˈhɪr/ | v. |  无意中听到；偷听到 | To hear (something) without the speaker's intention or knowledge. | I overheard them talking about the surprise party. 我无意中听到他们在谈论惊喜派对。 | Synonyms: eavesdrop on, listen in on | Antonyms: announce, broadcast | verb
