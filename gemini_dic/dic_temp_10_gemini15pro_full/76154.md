fusible:/ˈfjuːzəbl/ | adj. | 可熔的；易熔的 | Capable of being melted or liquefied by heat. |  The metal is fusible at a low temperature.  这种金属在低温下是可熔的。| synonyms: meltable, liquefiable | antonyms: infusible, non-fusible | adjective

fusible:/ˈfjuːzəbl/ | n. | 保险丝 | A safety device that melts and interrupts an electric circuit if the current exceeds a safe level. | The fusible blew and the lights went out. 保险丝烧断了，灯灭了。| synonyms: fuse | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fusible*, from Late Latin *fusibilis*, from Latin *fusus* (past participle of *fundere* "to pour, melt") + *-bilis* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").
