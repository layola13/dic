concert:/ˈkɒnsət/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsərt/ (US)| n.|音乐会，演奏会；一致，和谐|A public performance of music.|例句：We went to a rock concert last night. 我们昨晚去听了一场摇滚音乐会。|例句：The orchestra gave a magnificent concert. 管弦乐队举行了一场盛大的音乐会。|近义词：performance, recital, show|反义词:discord, disharmony|noun

concert:/kənˈsɜːt/ (UK) /kənˈsɝːt/ (US)| v.|协调，协同；一致行动|To plan or arrange something together.|例句：They concerted their efforts to finish the project. 他们齐心协力完成了这个项目。|例句：The various groups concerted their plans to overthrow the government.  各个团体协调他们的计划以推翻政府。|近义词：coordinate, cooperate, collaborate|反义词:differ, disagree|verb
