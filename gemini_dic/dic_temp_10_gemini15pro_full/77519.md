gazumping:/ɡəˈzʌmpɪŋ/
| n.|抬价; 临时加价(尤指在交易即将完成时);  哄抬物价|The practice of gazumping is particularly prevalent in a seller's market. 哄抬物价的做法在卖方市场尤其盛行。|
|The seller gazumped us at the last minute by accepting a higher offer from someone else. 卖方在最后一刻接受了别人的更高报价，对我们抬价了。|
|近义词: price gouging, profiteering, outbidding|
|反义词: underselling, discounting|
|词性: noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From *gazzump*, a colloquial British term meaning "to swindle, cheat, or deceive," likely of unknown origin. First recorded in the mid-20th century.  The -ing suffix turns the verb into a noun representing the action or process of gazumping.
