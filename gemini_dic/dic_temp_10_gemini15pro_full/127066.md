natant:/ˈneɪtənt/| adj.|漂浮的，游泳的|Swimming or floating on the surface of a liquid.  Existing or moving in a fluid medium, especially water.|例句：Natant organisms are commonly found in ponds.  池塘里常见漂浮的生物。

例句：The boat was seen natant on the lake.  那条船漂浮在湖面上。|近义词：floating, swimming, buoyant|反义词：submerged, sunken|词性: adjective
