avatar: /ˈævətɑːr/ (US) /ˈævətɑː/ (UK)| n. |化身，头像，替身|An icon or figure representing a particular person in a video game, internet forum, etc. |例句：She chose a cartoon cat as her avatar in the online game. 她选择了一只卡通猫作为她在网络游戏中的头像。

例句：He attended the meeting via his digital avatar. 他通过他的数字替身参加了会议。

近义词：icon, profile picture, persona, embodiment|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "real person/self")|词性:noun

avatar: /ˈævətɑːr/ (US) /ˈævətɑː/ (UK)| n. |(印度教)神的化身|The descent of a deity to the earth in an incarnate form, especially in Hinduism. |例句：Vishnu is said to have ten avatars.  据说毗湿奴有十个化身。

例句：The avatar of Krishna is a popular subject in Indian art. 克里希纳的化身是印度艺术中的一个流行主题。

近义词：incarnation, manifestation|反义词: (No direct opposite)|词性:noun
