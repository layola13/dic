outwording:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrdɪŋ/
|v.|以言语胜过；措辞比…好| Expressing oneself better than someone else; surpassing in eloquence or effectiveness of expression.|He outworded his opponent in the debate, leaving him speechless. 他在辩论中以言语胜过了对手，让他哑口无言。|近义词：outspeak, outtalk, outargue|反义词: be outdone, be worsted|词性:动词

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding) + "word" (noun) + "-ing" (suffix forming present participle and gerund)
