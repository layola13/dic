carrier:/ˈkæriər/ (US), /ˈkæriə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 运输工具；运营商；带菌者；搬运工；航母|A person or thing that carries something.  A company that transports goods or people.  A person or animal that transmits a disease without showing symptoms. A structure for carrying something.|The airline is a major carrier of passengers and cargo.  这家航空公司是主要的客货运输公司。|synonyms: transporter, conveyor, bearer, vector, aircraft carrier |antonyms: receiver, recipient|noun


carrier:/ˈkæriər/ (US), /ˈkæriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|运输的；航母的|Used for carrying something; related to an aircraft carrier.|He works for a carrier company. 他在一家运输公司工作。|synonyms: transport, conveying |antonyms: receiving|adjective
