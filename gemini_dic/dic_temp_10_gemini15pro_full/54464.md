disintegration:/dɪsˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. | 瓦解，解体，衰变 | The disintegration of the Roman Empire was a long and complex process.  罗马帝国的瓦解是一个漫长而复杂的过程。|  The disintegration of the radioactive material released harmful particles. 放射性物质的衰变释放了有害粒子。 |  decomposition, decay, breakdown, fragmentation |  integration, unification, consolidation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disintegrationem,  from dis- "apart, not" + integrationem "making whole".  The English word was formed in the late 16th century.
