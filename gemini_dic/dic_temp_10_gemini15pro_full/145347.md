overbulky:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌlki/| adj.|过大的，过于笨重的|Excessively large and awkward to handle or store; too bulky.|The package was overbulky and difficult to carry.  这个包裹太大太笨重，难以携带。|The furniture was overbulky for the small apartment. 这家具对于小公寓来说太笨重了。|近义词：cumbersome, unwieldy, bulky, massive|反义词：compact, small, manageable|词性:形容词
