ovation:/oʊˈveɪʃən/| n.|热烈欢迎; 欢呼; 鼓掌|an enthusiastic public reception of a person, marked especially by loud and prolonged applause.|The returning astronauts were given a tumultuous ovation.  返回的宇航员受到了热烈的欢迎。|Synonyms: applause, cheering, acclaim, plaudits|Antonyms: booing, hissing, disapproval|noun
