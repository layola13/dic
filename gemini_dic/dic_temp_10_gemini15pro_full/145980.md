overdocumenting:/ˌōvərˈdɒkjʊmentɪŋ/ | v. | 过度记录，过度文档化 | Providing excessive documentation, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency. | The project was so overdocumented that it became difficult to find the essential information. 这个项目被过度记录，以至于很难找到重要的信息。| overdocumenting is a gerund or present participle form of the verb "overdocument."  It combines the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") with the verb "document" (meaning "to record or provide evidence of something"). | synonyms: overreporting, overdescribing | antonyms: underdocumenting, underspecifying | verb (gerund/present participle)

overdocument:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjəment/ | vt. | 过度记录，过度文档化 | To record or document something excessively. | They overdocumented the process, creating unnecessary paperwork. 他们过度记录了这个过程，制造了不必要的文书工作。| "Overdocument" is formed by adding the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") to the verb "document." | synonyms: overreport, overdescribe | antonyms: underdocument, underspecify | verb
