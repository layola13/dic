galop:/ˈɡæləp/ (US), /ˈɡælɒp/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.| 疾驰，飞奔；(音乐)加洛普舞曲；急速进行|To ride a horse at a galop; to proceed at great speed.|He galopped the horse across the field. 他骑着马飞奔过田野。|近义词：canter, sprint, dash, run|反义词：walk, stroll, crawl|词性:名词, 动词

Etymology:  From French *galoper*, from Old French *waloper* "to gallop," of Germanic origin.
