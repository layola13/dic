overcompress:/ˌoʊvər.kəmˈprɛs/| v.|过度压缩|To compress something too much, resulting in a loss of quality or detail. |The image was overcompressed, making it look pixelated. 这张图片被过度压缩了，看起来像是像素化的。|Synonyms: over-compress, squeeze, crush|Antonyms: expand, decompress|verb

overcompress:/ˌoʊvər.kəmˈprɛs/| adj.|过度压缩的|Compressed too much. |The overcompressed audio file sounded distorted. 过度压缩的音频文件听起来失真了。|Synonyms: over-compressed, squeezed, crushed|Antonyms: expanded, decompressed|adjective
