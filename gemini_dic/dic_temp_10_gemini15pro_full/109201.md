leguminous:/ləˈɡjuːmɪnəs/| adj.|豆科的；豆类的|Relating to or denoting plants of the pea family (Leguminosae).  These plants bear pods containing seeds and typically have root nodules containing nitrogen-fixing bacteria.|例句：Leguminous plants, like beans and peas, are a valuable source of protein. 豆科植物，如豆类和豌豆，是宝贵的蛋白质来源。

例句：The farmer planted a field of leguminous crops to improve the soil.  这位农民种植了一块豆科作物来改良土壤。|近义词：papilionaceous, pod-bearing|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *leguminosus, from Latin legumen "pulse, legume," of uncertain origin.
