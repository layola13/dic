forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 预防，阻止，避免 | To avert, prevent, or ward off (something evil or unpleasant). |  Charms and amulets were worn to forefend evil spirits.  佩戴护身符和护符是为了抵御邪灵。| Synonyms: prevent, avert, obviate, preclude | Antonyms: cause, facilitate, encourage | verb

forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 保护，守护 | To protect or guard (someone or something). (Less common usage) |  May God forefend us from such a fate.  愿上帝保佑我们免遭这样的命运。| Synonyms: protect, guard, defend, shield | Antonyms: endanger, expose, harm | verb
