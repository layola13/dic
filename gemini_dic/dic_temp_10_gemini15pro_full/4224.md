airlifting: /ˈɛərlɪftɪŋ/ | n. |空运，空投|The act of transporting goods or people by aircraft, especially in an emergency. |例句：The airlifting of supplies to the disaster zone is underway.  向灾区空运物资正在进行中。

例句：The airlifting of the refugees to safety was a complex operation.  将难民空运到安全地带是一项复杂的操作。|近义词：air transport, flying, aviation|反义词：land transport, sea transport|noun


airlift: /ˈɛərlɪft/ | vt. |空运，空投|To transport goods or people by aircraft, especially in an emergency. |例句：They airlifted the injured climbers to the hospital.  他们用飞机把受伤的登山者送到医院。

例句：The UN is airlifting food and medical supplies to the war-torn region. 联合国正在向饱受战争蹂躏的地区空运食品和医疗用品。|近义词：fly, transport by air|反义词：transport by land, transport by sea|verb

词源：air + lift.  "lift" 本身有举起的意思，"air" 指的是空中，组合起来表示通过空中举起/运输。
