dumb:/dʌm/
adj.
愚蠢的，哑的，无言的
Lacking the power of speech; mute. Also: temporarily speechless; stunned or silenced.
例句：He was born dumb. 他天生哑巴。

例句：She was struck dumb with fear. 她吓得说不出话来。

近义词：mute, silent, speechless, inarticulate
反义词：articulate, eloquent, talkative, intelligent
词性：形容词


dumb:/dʌm/
adj.
( informal ) 愚蠢的，笨的
Stupid; foolish.
例句：That was a dumb thing to do.  那是一件愚蠢的事。

例句：He made a dumb mistake. 他犯了一个愚蠢的错误。

近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, idiotic
反义词：smart, intelligent, clever, wise
词性：形容词

PHRASE->
dumb down (v.): 简化；降低…的水平
例句：The textbook was dumbed down to make it easier for students.  这本教科书被简化了，以便学生更容易理解。

例句：Don't dumb down your language when you talk to me.  你跟我说话的时候不要降低你的语言水平。

play dumb: 装傻
例句：He played dumb, pretending he didn't know anything. 他装傻，假装什么都不知道。


dumb luck:  纯粹的运气；侥幸
例句：It was just dumb luck that I won the lottery. 我中彩票纯粹是运气好。


dumb as a post:  非常愚蠢
例句：He's as dumb as a post. 他蠢得像根木头。


strike dumb: 使…说不出话来；使…惊呆
例句：The news struck her dumb.  这个消息让她惊呆了。
