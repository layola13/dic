gasps:/ɡæsps/| n.|喘气，倒抽气|Sudden short intakes of breath, especially because of surprise, shock, or pain.|She let out a series of gasps when she saw the accident. 她看到事故后倒抽了几口气。|近义词：breaths, pants, puffs|反义词:exhales|noun

gasps:/ɡæsps/| v.|喘气，倒抽气|(third-person singular simple present gasps, present participle gasping, simple past and past participle gasped) To inhale suddenly and sharply, as in surprise or shock.|He gasped in pain as he fell to the ground.  他摔倒在地时痛苦地喘着气。|近义词：pants, breathes heavily|反义词:exhales|verb
