baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in some Asian and Middle Eastern countries. It can also refer to a bribe.|例句：He gave the porter a small baksheesh. 他给了搬运工一小笔小费。

例句：The official hinted that a little baksheesh would expedite the process.  这位官员暗示，一点小费可以加快办理进程。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe|反义词: None|词性:noun

baksheesh:/bɑːkˈʃiːʃ/| v.|给小费，行贿|To give a tip or bribe.|例句：He baksheeshed the waiter generously. 他慷慨地给了服务员小费。

例句：They had to baksheesh the guards to get through the gate. 他们不得不贿赂警卫才能通过大门。|近义词：tip, bribe|反义词: None|词性:verb
