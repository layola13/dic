gangplank: /ˈɡæŋplæŋk/ (US), /ˈɡæŋplæŋk/ (UK)
n.|跳板，舷梯|A movable bridge-like structure used for boarding or leaving a ship or boat.|The pirates ran down the gangplank and onto the shore. 海盗们沿着跳板跑到岸上。|Synonyms: ramp, walkway, bridge, gangway|Antonyms: N/A
