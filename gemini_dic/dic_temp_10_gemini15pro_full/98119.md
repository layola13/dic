insalubriously:/ˌɪnsəˈlubriəsli/
adverb
有害健康地；不利健康地
in a way that is harmful to health
The miners worked insalubriously in the dark, damp tunnels. 矿工们在黑暗潮湿的隧道里有害健康地工作。
The family lived insalubriously in a cramped, poorly ventilated apartment. 这家人在一个狭窄、通风不良的公寓里生活，对健康有害。

近义词:unhealthily, harmfully
反义词:healthily, healthfully
词性:adverb
