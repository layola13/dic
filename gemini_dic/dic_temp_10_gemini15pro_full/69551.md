following:/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊɪŋ/ (US)| adj. n.|接下来的，随后的；下列的；追随者，拥护者|Something that is following comes after something else in time or order. It can also refer to people who support a particular person, belief, or cause. |The following week, I went to Paris.  接下来的那个星期，我去了巴黎。| synonyms: subsequent, ensuing, next, succeeding | antonyms: preceding, previous, prior| adjective

following:/ˈfɒləʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊɪŋ/ (US)| n.|追随者，拥护者|Refers to a group of supporters or followers. |The artist has a large following on social media.  这位艺术家在社交媒体上有很多追随者。| synonyms: supporters, fans, adherents | antonyms: opponents, detractors| noun
