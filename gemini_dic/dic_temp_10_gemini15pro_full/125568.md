multititular:/ˌmʌltiˈtɪtjʊlər/
adjective
多头衔的；拥有多个头衔的
Having multiple titles or holding several offices or positions of authority.
The multititular emperor ruled over vast territories. 这位多头衔的皇帝统治着广阔的领土。
multi-titled, polyonymous
single-titled

Etymology:
From multi- (many) + titular (holding a title).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "Titular" comes from Latin "titulus" meaning "title, inscription."
