implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|含义; 暗示; 可能的影响; 牵连|a suggestion that is not made directly; the effect that an action or decision will have on something else in the future|His implication was that I was lying. 他的言外之意是我在撒谎。|synonyms: suggestion, inference, hint, innuendo, connotation, undertone, overtone|antonyms: explicit statement, direct statement|noun

implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|牵涉; 卷入|the fact of being involved, or of involving someone, in something, especially a crime|The implication of the mayor in the scandal shocked the city. 市长卷入丑闻震惊了全市。|synonyms: involvement, entanglement, incrimination|antonyms: exoneration, acquittal|noun
