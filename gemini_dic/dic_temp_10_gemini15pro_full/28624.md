caretakings:/ˈkeərˌteɪkɪŋz/
n.
照料；照顾；护理
The process or act of taking care of someone or something.
The caretakings of the elderly require patience and compassion.  照顾老人需要耐心和同情心。
My neighbor provided caretakings for my cat while I was away. 我不在的时候，我的邻居帮我照顾我的猫。
近义词: care, tending, nursing, looking after, supervision
反义词: neglect, abandonment
