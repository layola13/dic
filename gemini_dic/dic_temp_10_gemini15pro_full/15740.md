balcony: /ˈbælkəni/ (US) /ˈbælkəni/ (UK)| n.|阳台，露台|A platform projecting from the wall of a building, enclosed by a railing or balustrade, and typically accessible from an upper-floor window or door.|We had breakfast on the balcony overlooking the sea. 我们在阳台上吃早餐，俯瞰大海。|近义词：veranda, terrace, porch|反义词: (no direct antonym)|noun
