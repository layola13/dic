abscissa: /æbˈsɪsə/ (US) /æbˈsɪs.ə/ (UK)| n. |横坐标|The horizontal (x) coordinate in a two-dimensional Cartesian coordinate system.|The abscissa of the point (3,2) is 3.  该点(3,2)的横坐标是3。|近义词：x-coordinate, horizontal coordinate|反义词: ordinate|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscissa (linea), "a line cut off," from abscindere, "to cut off," from ab-, "off, away from" + scindere, "to cut, split."

USAGE->
The abscissa represents the horizontal position of a point in a two-dimensional coordinate system.

The value of the abscissa is the distance of the point from the y-axis.

The abscissa and ordinate together define the location of a point in the plane.


Plotting the data points with time on the abscissa and temperature on the ordinate reveals a clear warming trend.  以时间为横坐标，温度为纵坐标绘制数据点，可以看出明显的变暖趋势。

The scientist marked the abscissa of each data point on the graph. 科学家在图表上标记了每个数据点的横坐标。
