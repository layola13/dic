nonmenial:/ˌnɒnˈmiːniəl/
adjective
非体力劳动的，非卑贱的
Not requiring or involving menial labor; not servile or degrading.
She finally landed a nonmenial job in an office. 她终于找到了一份非体力劳动的工作，在办公室里。
近义词：skilled, professional, white-collar
反义词：menial, unskilled, blue-collar
词性：adjective
