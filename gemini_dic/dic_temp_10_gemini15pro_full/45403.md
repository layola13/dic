cupidity: /kjuːˈpɪdɪti/ /kjuːˈpɪdəti/
n.
贪婪，贪心
Intense desire, especially for wealth; avarice.
The investor's cupidity led him to make risky investments.  这位投资者的贪婪导致他进行了高风险投资。
His eyes glittered with cupidity as he gazed at the jewels. 他盯着珠宝，眼中闪烁着贪婪的光芒。

近义词: avarice, greed, rapacity, covetousness
反义词: generosity, altruism, contentment
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin cupiditas ("desire, eagerness, passion"), from cupidus ("eager, passionate, desirous"), from cupere ("to desire, long for, covet"). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *keup- ("to tremble; to desire").
