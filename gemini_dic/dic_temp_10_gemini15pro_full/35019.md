clonking:/ˈklɒŋkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈklɑːŋkɪŋ/ (US)| adj.  n.|发出沉闷的金属撞击声的；沉闷的金属撞击声|A heavy, dull sound, like that of metal hitting metal.  It can also describe something that makes this sound.  It often suggests something is old, worn out, or malfunctioning.|例句：The old car drove off with a clonking sound. 那辆旧车发出哐啷哐啷的声音开走了。

例句：The clonking of the pipes kept me awake all night. 水管的哐当声让我整夜没睡。|近义词：clanging, banging, rattling, thumping|反义词: silent, quiet|词性: adjective, noun

clonk:/klɒŋk/ (UK) /klɑːŋk/ (US)| n.  v. |沉闷的金属撞击声；发出沉闷的金属撞击声|A heavy, dull sound, as of metal striking metal; to make such a sound.|例句：I heard a loud clonk from under the car. 我听到车底下传来一声巨响。

例句：The metal lid clonked shut. 金属盖子砰地一声关上了。|近义词：clang, bang, rattle, thump|反义词: - |词性: noun, verb
