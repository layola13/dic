intergrapples:/ˌɪntərˈɡræpəlz/
verb
互相扭打；互相格斗
engages in a close fight or struggle, especially one that involves grappling or wrestling.
The two wrestlers intergrappled fiercely in the ring.  两个摔跤手在擂台上激烈地扭打。
He intergrappled with his opponent until he was exhausted. 他和对手扭打在一起，直到筋疲力尽。
近义词: wrestle, grapple, struggle, fight
反义词: disengage, separate, yield
词性: verb
