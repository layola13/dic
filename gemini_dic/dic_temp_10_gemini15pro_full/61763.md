empathising:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzɪŋ/| v.|同情，共情，感同身受|The act of understanding and sharing the feelings of another.|She was empathising with the character's struggles in the novel. 她对小说中角色的挣扎感同身受。|Synonyms: sympathizing, commiserating, relating|Antonyms:  disregarding, ignoring|Verb (present participle)

empathy:/ˈɛmpəθi/| n.|同情，共鸣，同感|The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|He felt a deep empathy for the victims of the earthquake. 他对地震灾民深表同情。|Synonyms: compassion, understanding, sympathy|Antonyms: apathy, indifference|Noun
