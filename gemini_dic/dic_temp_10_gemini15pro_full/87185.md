herds:/hɜːrdz/ | n. |兽群，牧群|A large group of animals, especially hoofed mammals, that live together or are kept together as livestock. | The shepherd guided his herds to the pasture. 牧羊人把他的羊群赶到牧场。|  The herds of wildebeest migrated across the plains. 角马群迁徙穿过平原。| synonyms: flocks, droves, packs | antonyms: individual, single | noun
