impugnment:/ɪmˈpʌɡnmənt/| n.|质疑，抨击，非难|The impugnment of the witness's testimony cast doubt on the entire case.  对证人证词的质疑使整个案件蒙上阴影。|The politician faced constant impugnment of his motives.  这位政治家不断面临对其动机的抨击。|近义词：challenge, questioning, criticism, attack,  dispute, contradiction, gainsaying|反义词：support, defense, endorsement,  acceptance, corroboration|noun
