harangueful:/həˈræŋɡfʊl/
adjective
爱长篇大论的；喜欢训斥的
Given to delivering harangues; scolding.
The harangueful professor lectured the students for an hour about the importance of punctuality.  这位爱长篇大论的教授就守时的重要性给学生们讲了一个小时。
近义词:preachy, sermonizing, scolding, lecturing
反义词:concise, brief, taciturn, reticent
词性:adjective
