letterman: /ˈlɛtərmən/ (US), /ˈlɛtəmən/ (UK)| n. | 校队运动员（尤指穿有学校首字母标志运动衫的）; 给总统写信的人| A high school or college athlete, especially one awarded a letter for distinction in a sport.  A person who writes letters to the president. | He was a proud letterman in high school. 他高中时是一位自豪的校队运动员。| 近义词: athlete, sportsman, sportswoman | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly non-athlete) | noun
