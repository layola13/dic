enlisting:/ɪnˈlɪstɪŋ/
|n.|征募，入伍|The act of enrolling or engaging someone in the armed forces or in a cause.|The army is stepping up its advertising campaign for enlisting new recruits. 军队正在加强招募新兵的广告宣传。|recruitment, enrollment, conscription|discharge, release|noun

enlisting:/ɪnˈlɪstɪŋ/
|v.|争取，谋取（帮助或支持）|To secure the support or aid of.|They are enlisting the help of volunteers. 他们正在争取志愿者的帮助。|engage, recruit, obtain|reject, refuse|verb
