indignancy:/ɪnˈdɪɡnənsi/ | n. | 愤慨，义愤 | A feeling of anger or annoyance provoked by what is perceived as unfair treatment. |  She expressed her indignance at being unfairly dismissed. 她对被不公平地解雇表示愤慨。

| Example: The manager's decision sparked widespread indignance among the employees.  经理的决定激起了员工们的普遍愤慨。| Synonym: resentment, anger, fury, outrage | Antonym: calmness, composure, acceptance | Noun
