co-head:/ˌkoʊˈhed/| n.|共同领导人，共同负责人|A person who shares the leadership of an organization or department with another person.|Example: The company appointed two co-heads to oversee the merger.  这家公司任命了两位共同负责人来监督合并。|Example:  The co-heads presented a united front at the press conference.  两位共同负责人在新闻发布会上展现了团结一致的姿态。|Synonyms: co-leader, joint head, shared leader|Antonyms: subordinate, assistant|noun

co-head:/ˌkoʊˈhed/| v.|共同领导，共同负责|To share the leadership of an organization or department with another person.|Example: They will co-head the new department. 他们将共同领导新部门。|Example:  The two executives co-headed the project successfully. 两位高管成功地共同领导了这个项目。|Synonyms: co-lead, jointly head, share leadership|Antonyms: follow, support|verb
