cantered:/ˈkæntərd/ (US), /ˈkæntəd/ (UK)| v.|慢跑，缓驰|Moved at a moderate gallop;  a three-beat gait intermediate between a trot and a gallop. |The horse cantered gracefully around the track. 这匹马在跑道上优雅地慢跑。|近义词：loped, trotted|反义词:galloped, raced|verb
