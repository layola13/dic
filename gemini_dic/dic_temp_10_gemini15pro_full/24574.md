broccolis:/ˈbrɒkəliz/ (British) /ˈbrɑːkəliz/ (American)| n. |西兰花（复数）|The plural form of broccoli, referring to more than one head or plant of broccoli.  While "broccoli" is already considered a plural noun, "broccolis" is sometimes used informally to emphasize multiple individual broccoli heads or plants.| I bought three broccolis at the market today. 我今天在市场买了三棵西兰花。|近义词：broccoli heads, broccoli plants|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially other vegetables) | Noun
