oxyphenic:/ˌɒksɪˈfiːnɪk/ | adj. | 氧苯的，羟基苯的 | Relating to or containing oxyphenol (catechol). | Oxyphenic acid is another name for catechol. 氧苯酸是儿茶酚的另一个名称。|  Oxyphenic compounds are found in certain plants. 氧苯化合物存在于某些植物中。 | 近义词: catecholic | 反义词:  (No common direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  oxy- (combining form meaning "containing oxygen") + phenic (relating to phenol)
