homophobia:/ˌhoʊməˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 同性恋恐惧症，恐同症 | An irrational fear of, aversion to, or discrimination against homosexuality or homosexuals. |  Homophobia is a serious issue that needs to be addressed. 同性恋恐惧症是一个需要认真对待的严重问题。

His homophobic comments were met with outrage. 他的恐同言论遭到了强烈谴责。| 近义词: prejudice, bigotry, discrimination | 反义词: acceptance, tolerance | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From homo- (combining form meaning "same," used in scientific terms like homosexual) + -phobia (suffix meaning "fear of," from Greek phobos "fear, panic fear, terror, outward show of fear").
