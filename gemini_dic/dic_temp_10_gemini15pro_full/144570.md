outward:/ˈaʊtwərd/ (US) /ˈaʊtwəd/ (UK)| adj. adv.|向外的，外表的；表面上的|Moving or directed towards the outside; relating to the external appearance.|例句：The outward appearance of the building was impressive. 这栋建筑的外观令人印象深刻。|近义词：external, outer, superficial|反义词：inward, internal, inner|词性:形容词/副词

outward:/ˈaʊtwərd/ (US) /ˈaʊtwəd/ (UK)| adv.|向外地，在外表上|In an outward direction; towards the outside.|例句：The door opens outward. 这扇门向外开。|近义词：externally, outwardly|反义词：inwardly, internally|词性:副词

词源: From Old English *ūtweard (unattested), from *ūt (out) + -weard. Cognate with Old Frisian ūtwird, Old Saxon ūtward, Middle Dutch ūtewaerts, Old High German ūzwart.
