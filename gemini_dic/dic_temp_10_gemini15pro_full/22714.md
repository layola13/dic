borgard:/ˈbɔːrɡɑːrd/| n.|堡垒，城堡，围墙|A fortified place, a castle, or an enclosure.|例句：The borgard stood strong against the siege. 城堡在围攻中屹立不倒。

例句：The borgard protected the city from invaders. 堡垒保护城市免受入侵者的侵害。

近义词：fortress, castle, citadel, stronghold|反义词：village, town|词性：名词


词源：

From Middle Old Norse *borgarðr, from *borg “castle” + *garðr “yard, enclosure”. Cognate with Swedish borgård, Danish borggaard.
