embellish: /ɪmˈbɛlɪʃ/ | v. | 装饰，修饰，润色 | To make something more attractive or interesting by adding details or decorations. | The dress was embellished with lace and pearls. 这件衣服用蕾丝和珍珠装饰。|  He embellished his stories with details from his imagination. 他用自己想象的细节润色了他的故事。 | synonyms: adorn, decorate, ornament, enhance, enrich | antonyms: simplify, strip, bare | verb
