nuking:/ˈnjuːkɪŋ/
v.
用核武器攻击; 摧毁; 用微波炉烹调
attacking or destroying with nuclear weapons; cooking in a microwave oven
The city was nuked during the war.  这座城市在战争期间遭到核武器攻击。
I'm nuking some leftovers for lunch. 我正在用微波炉加热一些剩菜做午餐。
近义词:bombarding, obliterating, microwaving
反义词:protecting, preserving

词性:动词 (verb)
