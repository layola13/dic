overdiscreetnesses: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈskriːtnəsɪz/
noun
过分谨慎；过分拘谨
Excessive cautiousness or reserve in speech, behavior, or appearance.
Her overdiscreetnesses made her seem aloof and unapproachable. 她的过分谨慎让她显得冷漠，难以接近。
Synonyms: reserve, reticence, cautiousness, prudence, circumspection
Antonyms: indiscretion, imprudence, recklessness, boldness, audacity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "discreet" (careful and prudent in one's speech or actions, especially in order to keep something confidential or to avoid embarrassment) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Discreet" comes from the Old French "discret," from the Latin "discretus" (separated, distinct), past participle of "discernere" (to separate, distinguish).
