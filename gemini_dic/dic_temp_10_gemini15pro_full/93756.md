immeasurable:/ɪˈmeʒərəbl/| adj.|不可估量的，无限的|Too great to be measured; limitless.|The damage caused by the earthquake was immeasurable. 地震造成的损失不可估量。|近义词：immense, vast, boundless, infinite|反义词：measurable, finite, limited|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + measurable.
