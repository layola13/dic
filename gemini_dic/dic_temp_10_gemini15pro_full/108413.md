lawnd:/lɔːnd/ | n. |草地，草坪 | An area of short, mown grass in a yard, park, or other setting. |  The children were playing on the lawnd. 孩子们在草坪上玩耍。 | Synonyms: lawn, grass, sward | Antonyms: wasteland, desert | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *launde*, from Old French *lande*, from Frankish *landa*. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *landą* (meaning "land"). Related to English "land."
