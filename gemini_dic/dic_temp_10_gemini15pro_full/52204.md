diastema:/daɪˈæstiːmə/ |n.|间隙，齿隙|A diastema is a space or gap between two teeth. |She has a noticeable diastema between her two front teeth. 她两颗门牙之间有一个明显的齿隙。|Synonym: gap, space|Antonym: closure|noun

diastema:/daɪˈæstiːmə/ |n.|空隙，裂隙 (general, less common)|A diastema can also refer to a gap in other contexts, such as a break in a bone or a geological formation.|The earthquake caused a diastema in the rock face. 地震在岩壁上造成了一条裂缝。|Synonym: gap, fissure, cleft|Antonym: closure, bridge|noun
