floatiest:/ˈfloʊtiɪst/| adj.|最轻飘飘的，最漂浮的|Superlative form of floaty: most floaty.  Describes something that is extremely light and airy, tending to drift or wave gently in the air or on water.|Her dress was the floatiest I'd ever seen, made of layers of delicate silk chiffon.|近义词：airiest, gauziest, ethereal, diaphanous|反义词:heavy, weighty|词性:adjective
