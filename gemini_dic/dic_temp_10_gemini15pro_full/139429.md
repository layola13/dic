obsessiveness:/əbˈsɛsɪvnəs/| n. |痴迷，着魔|The state of being obsessive; excessive preoccupation with something.|Her obsessiveness with cleanliness was starting to worry her family. 她对清洁的痴迷开始让她的家人担心。|例句：His obsessiveness about his work affected his health. 他对工作的痴迷影响了他的健康。|近义词：fixation, preoccupation, compulsion|反义词:indifference, apathy|词性:noun
词源: From obsessive + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *obsessus*, past participle of *obsidere* "to besiege, occupy, block, beset" (from *ob* "against" + *sedere* "to sit").
