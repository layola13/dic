always:/ˈɔːlweɪz/ /ˈɑːlweɪz/
adverb|总是，一直，永远|Always do your best. 始终尽力而为。|
例句：She always arrives on time. 她总是准时到达。
例句：I always try to be positive. 我总是努力保持积极。
近义词：constantly, invariably, perpetually
反义词：never, rarely, seldom
词性：副词

词源：From Old English *ealne weg "all the way," composed of ealne (genitive masculine singular of eall "all") + weg "way."
