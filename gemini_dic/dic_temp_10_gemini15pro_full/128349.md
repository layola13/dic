neutralizer:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzər/ | n. | 中和剂；抵消因素 | A substance that neutralizes something, especially a chemical that neutralizes an acid or alkali. | The company acted as a neutralizer in the conflict.  这家公司在冲突中充当了调解人。| synonyms: counteragent, antidote,  remedy | antonyms: intensifier, catalyst | noun

neutralize:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/ | v. | 中和；抵消；使无效 | To make something neutral; to counteract or cancel the effect of something. |  The base neutralized the acid. 碱中和了酸。 | synonyms: counteract, offset, nullify | antonyms: intensify, enhance | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From neutral + -ize.  Neutral comes from Late Latin neutralis "neither,"  from Latin neuter "neither," literally "not either," from ne- "not" + uter "which of two." -ize is a suffix forming verbs, from French -iser, from Greek -izein.
