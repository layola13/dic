horsetails:/ˈhɔːrsteɪlz/ | n. | 马尾；木贼（植物） |A plant of the genus Equisetum, with a hollow, jointed stem and scalelike leaves. Horsetails are among the oldest living plants on Earth. |  Ancient horsetails grew much larger than their modern descendants. 古代的木贼比它们的现代后代长得大得多。

Horsetails can be found in damp areas around the world. 木贼可以在世界各地的潮湿地区找到。 | synonyms: Equisetum, scouring rush | antonyms: N/A | noun

horsetail:/ˈhɔːrsteɪlz/ | adj. | 马尾状的 | Resembling the tail of a horse. | The plant had a horsetail-like appearance. 这种植物的外观像马尾。 | synonyms:  equisetoid | antonyms: N/A | adjective
