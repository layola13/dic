fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/| n. vt.|油炸馅饼， fritter away 浪费|A fritter is a small piece of fried batter often containing fruit or meat.  To fritter something away is to waste it gradually.|She made apple fritters for dessert. 她做了苹果油炸饼作为甜点。 He frittered away his inheritance on gambling. 他把遗产都浪费在赌博上了。|近义词：(n.) doughnut, pancake, beignet (v.) squander, waste, dissipate|反义词：(v.) save, conserve, hoard|词性:名词, 动词

fritter away:/ˈfrɪtər əˈweɪ/| v.|浪费，挥霍|To fritter something away means to waste it, especially time or money, on unimportant things.|She frittered away the whole afternoon watching TV. 她浪费了整个下午看电视。 He frittered away his fortune on frivolous purchases. 他把他的财产浪费在轻浮的购物上。|近义词：squander, waste, dissipate|反义词：save, conserve, hoard|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "friture" (fried food), from "frire" (to fry), from Latin "frigere" (to roast, fry).
