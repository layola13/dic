cogently:/ˈkoʊdʒəntli/
adverb
令人信服地
in a way that is clear, logical, and convincing
She argued cogently for a more flexible approach. 她令人信服地论证了采用更灵活的方法。
近义词: persuasively, convincingly, forcefully
反义词: unconvincingly, weakly, vaguely
词性: adverb

词源:
From Latin cogent- ‘compelling’, from the verb cogere (see coerce).
