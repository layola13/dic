ETYMOLOGY->
From French *inexactitude*, from in- + exactitude.  Exactitude comes from Late Latin *exactitūdō*, from exactus (past participle of exigō, "to demand, weigh, measure"). Ultimately from ex- ("out") + agō ("to drive, lead, do").
