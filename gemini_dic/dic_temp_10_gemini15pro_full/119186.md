mezzotint: /ˈmɛtsəʊtɪnt/ | n. adj. | 铜版雕刻(法); 中间色调, 半色调 | A method of engraving copper or steel to produce prints in which the design is composed of dots of varying sizes and densities, creating tonal gradations. As an adjective, it describes something having the soft, graded tones characteristic of a mezzotint. | The artist created a beautiful mezzotint of the countryside.  这位艺术家创作了一幅美丽的乡村铜版画。| synonym: engraving, etching, aquatint | antonym: lithograph, woodcut | noun

mezzotinted: /ˌmɛtsəʊˈtɪntɪd/ | adj. |  用铜版雕刻(法)制作的; 中间色调的 | Engraved using the mezzotint technique; having the soft, subtly graded tones characteristic of a mezzotint. | The mezzotinted portrait had a dreamlike quality. 这幅用铜版雕刻法制作的肖像画有一种梦幻般的感觉。 | synonym: shaded, half-toned | antonym: bright, stark | adjective
