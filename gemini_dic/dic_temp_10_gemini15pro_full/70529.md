filiation:/ˌfɪliˈeɪʃn/
n.
父子关系;血缘关系;起源;隶属关系
the fact of being the child of a particular parent or parents, or the fact of belonging to a particular group, type, or organization.
The painting reveals the artist's artistic filiation to the Impressionists. 这幅画展现了这位艺术家与印象派之间的艺术渊源。
synonyms: descent, lineage, parentage, ancestry, origin, derivation
antonyms: disaffiliation, disownment
noun

n.
隶属关系
The report explored the political filiation of the various groups involved. 这份报告探讨了各相关组织的政治背景。
synonyms: affiliation, association, connection
antonyms: disaffiliation, disconnection
noun
