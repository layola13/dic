ETYMOLOGY-> From Old French *arismetique* (modern French *arithmétique*), from Latin *arithmetica*, from Ancient Greek *ἀριθμητική (τέχνη)* (arithmētikḗ (tékhnē), “art of counting”), from *ἀριθμός* (arithmós, “number”).
