omnisciently: /ɑːmˈnɪʃəntli/ | adv. | 全知地，无所不知地 | In a manner characterized by infinite awareness, understanding, and insight.  | The narrator, speaking omnisciently, revealed the character's innermost thoughts. 叙述者以全知视角揭示了人物内心深处的想法。 |  The author omnisciently described the events unfolding in the different countries. 作者以全知视角描述了不同国家发生的事件。 | 近义词: all-knowingly | 反义词: ignorantly, unknowingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *omniscient-, from Latin omnis ("all") + scient- ("knowing"), present participle of scire ("to know").
