formulary:/ˈfɔːrmjəleri/ (US) /ˈfɔːmjʊləri/ (UK)| n.| 配方；处方集；公式集；礼典| A book containing a list of formulas, especially for the preparation of medicines.| The doctor consulted the formulary to check the correct dosage. 医生查阅了处方集以核对正确的剂量。|  synonyms: pharmacopeia, pharmacopoeia, recipe book,  prescription list| antonyms:  None (lack of a direct opposite)| noun

formulary:/ˈfɔːrmjəleri/ (US) /ˈfɔːmjʊləri/ (UK)| adj.| 公式的；惯例的| Relating to or based on a formula or set of formulas.| The formulary procedures must be followed precisely.  必须严格遵守规定的程序。| synonyms:  formulaic, prescribed, conventional| antonyms:  unconventional, original, innovative| adjective
