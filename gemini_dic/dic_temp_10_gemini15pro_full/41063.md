coportion:/kəʊˈpɔːʃən/ |n.|共同部分，共有，分担|A coportion is a joint ownership or sharing of something, especially property or rights.  It implies a division or distribution among multiple parties.|The coportion of the land was divided equally among the three siblings. 这块土地由三兄弟姐妹平分。|近义词: joint ownership, shared possession, sharing, division|反义词: sole ownership, exclusive possession|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *comportionem, accusative of *comportio ("sharing, portioning together"), from com- ("together") + portio ("portion, share").
