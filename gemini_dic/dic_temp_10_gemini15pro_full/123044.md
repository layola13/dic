moneran:/ˈmɒnərən/ (US), /ˈmɒnərən/ (UK)| n.|原核生物|A single-celled organism belonging to the kingdom Monera (or Prokaryotae); a prokaryote. Monerans lack a membrane-bound nucleus and other organelles. They include bacteria and cyanobacteria (formerly called blue-green algae).|例句：Monerans are the simplest form of life. 原核生物是最简单的生命形式。

例句：Bacteria are a type of moneran. 细菌是一种原核生物。|近义词：prokaryote|反义词: eukaryote|词性: noun
