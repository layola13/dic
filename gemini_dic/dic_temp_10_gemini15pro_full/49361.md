deliquesce:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛs/| vi.| 潮解，溶解|To absorb moisture from the air and dissolve.|The salt deliquesced in the humid air.  盐在潮湿的空气中潮解了。|近义词：dissolve, melt, liquefy|反义词：dry, solidify, harden|verb

deliquescence:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛsəns/| n.| 潮解，溶解|The process of absorbing moisture from the air and dissolving.|The deliquescence of the salt caused a mess. 盐的潮解造成了一片狼藉。|近义词：dissolution, melting, liquefaction|反义词：drying, solidification, hardening|noun

deliquescent:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛsənt/| adj.| 潮解的，易潮解的|Having the property of absorbing moisture from the air and dissolving.|Calcium chloride is a deliquescent substance. 氯化钙是一种易潮解的物质。|近义词：hygroscopic, soluble|反义词：insoluble, non-hygroscopic|adjective
