angelot:/ˈeɪndʒələʊt/ | n. |小天使；（古）小羊羔；小傻瓜| A young angel; (archaic) a little lamb; a simpleton. |  She looked like a little angelot in her white dress. 她穿着白裙子，看起来像个小天使。 |  The shepherd cared for the newborn angelot. 牧羊照顾着新生的羊羔。 | Synonyms: cherub, putto (for young angel); lambkin (for little lamb); innocent, naif (for simpleton)| Antonyms: devil, fiend (for young angel);  wolf (for little lamb);  sophisticate, cynic (for simpleton) | noun
