give-backs:/ˈɡɪvˌbæks/
noun
让步，妥协；(尤指工人在工资或福利方面的)退让
concessions made by workers, usually involving a reduction in pay or benefits
The union agreed to give-backs in order to save jobs. 工会同意做出让步以保住工作。
synonyms: concessions, compromises, sacrifices
antonyms: gains, increases, benefits
n.
