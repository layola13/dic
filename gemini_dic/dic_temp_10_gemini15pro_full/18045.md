begrave: /bɪˈɡreɪv/ | vt. |埋葬; 使沉浸于;  | To bury (a corpse) in the ground. To cause (someone) to be preoccupied. |  He was begraved in the family plot. 他被安葬在家族墓地里。/ Grief begraved her. 她沉浸在悲痛之中。| 近义词: bury, inter, entomb, immerse | 反义词: exhume, unearth | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *begraver, from be- + graver "to dig, engrave," from Latin gravare "to burden."
