domite:/ˈdoʊmaɪt/| n.|白云石；苦灰岩|A white or colorless mineral consisting of calcium magnesium carbonate, used as an ornamental building stone and as a source of magnesium.|The cathedral was built of domite.  这座大教堂是用白云石建造的。|Synonyms: dolomite, dolostone|Antonyms: N/A|noun

domite:/ˈdoʊmaɪt/| n.|【地质】粗面岩|A light-colored, volcanic rock.|The volcanic plug is composed primarily of domite. 这个火山栓主要由粗面岩构成。|Synonyms: trachyte|Antonyms: N/A|noun
