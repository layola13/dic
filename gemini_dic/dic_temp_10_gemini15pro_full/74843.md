frequencies:/ˈfriːkwənsiz/ /ˈfriːkwənsis/ | n. |频率; 出现次数; 发生率|The plural form of frequency, referring to how often something happens or occurs.|The frequencies of earthquakes in this area have increased over the last decade.  这个地区的地震频率在过去十年中有所增加。|近义词：occurrences, rates, repetitions|反义词: rarity, infrequency |noun
