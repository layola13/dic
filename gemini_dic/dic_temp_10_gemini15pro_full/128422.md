newfie:/ˈn(j)uːfi/| n.|纽芬兰犬；纽芬兰人|A large dog of a breed originating in Newfoundland, known for its size, strength, and webbed feet, often used as a working dog, particularly for water rescue. Also, informally, a person from Newfoundland.|例句：My newfie loves swimming in the lake. 我的纽芬兰犬喜欢在湖里游泳。

例句：He's a newfie through and through. 他是个地地道道的纽芬兰人。|近义词：Newfoundlander (for a person), Newfoundland dog (for the dog breed)|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a breed or person)|词性:noun
