enchanter:/ɪnˈtʃæntər/ | n. | 魔法师；施魔法的人；使人陶醉的人或物 | Someone who enchants; a sorcerer or magician.  A person or thing that delights or captivates. | The enchanter cast a spell on the princess. 魔法师对公主施了咒语。 The beautiful music was an enchanter, holding the audience spellbound.  优美的音乐令人陶醉，使观众听得如痴如醉。 | 近义词: magician, sorcerer, wizard, charmer | 反义词: bore | 词性: 名词
