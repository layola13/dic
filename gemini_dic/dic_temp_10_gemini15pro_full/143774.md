outmigration:/ˌaʊtmaɪˈɡreɪʃən/
n.|迁出;人口外移|The outmigration from rural areas to cities is a common phenomenon.  农村地区向城市的迁出是一个普遍现象。|
Etymology: out- (Old English ūt, "out") + migration (from Latin migratio, "a removal, migration")|
Example: The outmigration from the small town has led to a decline in its population.  这个小镇的人口外移导致了人口下降。
Example:  High unemployment rates contributed to the outmigration of young people seeking better opportunities. 高失业率促使年轻人迁出以寻求更好的机会。

|近义词：emigration, exodus|
|反义词：immigration, in-migration|
|词性：noun|
