detectabilities:/dɪˌtɛktəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可探测性；可检测性|The quality or state of being detectable; the extent to which something can be detected.  It often refers to the likelihood of something being discovered or noticed, especially in scientific contexts.|The detectabilities of the different viruses varied depending on the test used. 不同病毒的可检测性根据所用测试的不同而有所不同。|Synonyms: discernibility, perceptibility, observability|Antonyms: undetectability, imperceptibility, invisibility|noun
