gwiniad:/ˈɡwɪniæd/| n. |白鲑鱼(一种主要产于英国湖泊的鱼)|A type of freshwater whitefish, mainly found in British lakes, of the salmon family.|例句：The gwiniad is a prized fish among anglers.  白鲑鱼是垂钓者珍视的鱼。

例句：Gwiniad populations have declined in recent years.  近年来，白鲑鱼的数量有所下降。|近义词：whitefish, schelly, powan|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology->
From Welsh *gweniad, from gwyn ("white").
