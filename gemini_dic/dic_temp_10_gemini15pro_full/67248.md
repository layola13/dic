expunged:/ɪkˈspʌndʒd/
verb
删除，抹去，消除（记录）
removed (something) from a record or other written material.
His criminal record was expunged after he completed his community service. 他的犯罪记录在他完成社区服务后被删除了。
expunge:/ɪkˈspʌndʒ/ | v. | 删除，抹去，消除（记录） |  The judge ordered the conviction expunged from his record. 法官下令将他的定罪记录删除。 | synonyms: erase, delete, obliterate, remove, efface | antonyms:  record, preserve, retain, maintain | verb
