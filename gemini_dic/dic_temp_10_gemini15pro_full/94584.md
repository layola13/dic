impracticable:/ɪmˈpræktɪkəbl/ | adj. | 不可行的，不切实际的 | Not able to be done or put into practice because of difficulties or impossibilities. |  The plan to build a bridge across the ocean was deemed impracticable.  建造一座跨越大洋的桥梁的计划被认为是不可行的。|  infeasible, unworkable, impossible | feasible, practical, possible | adjective

impractical:/ɪmˈpræktɪkl/ | adj. | 不切实际的，不实用的 | Not sensible or realistic; not likely to succeed. |  Wearing high heels to hike a mountain is impractical. 穿着高跟鞋去爬山是不切实际的。| unrealistic, unwise, unsuitable | practical, sensible, realistic | adjective
