clearwing:/ˈklɪərwɪŋ/| n. |透翅蛾，玻璃翅蝴蝶|A moth or butterfly with mostly transparent wings.|例句：The clearwing hummingbird moth hovered near the flowers. 透翅蜂鸟蛾在花朵附近盘旋。

例句：We saw a beautiful clearwing butterfly in the rainforest. 我们在雨林中看到一只美丽的透翅蝶。|近义词：glasswing, transparent-winged|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider moths/butterflies with opaque wings)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'clear' + 'wing', referring to the transparent nature of the wings.  'Clear' comes from Old French 'cler' meaning "bright, shining". 'Wing' comes from Old Norse 'vængr'.
