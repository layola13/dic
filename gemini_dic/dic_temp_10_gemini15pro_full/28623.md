caretaking:/ˈkɛərˌteɪkɪŋ/
n.|照顾；照料；看管|The act of taking care of someone or something, especially a house or property while the owner is away.|She offered her services for the caretaking of the elderly woman's cat while she was on vacation. 她主动提出在她度假期间照顾那位老妇人的猫。|近义词：babysitting, tending, minding, supervision, guardianship|反义词：neglect, abandonment|词性:名词

caretaking:/ˈkɛərˌteɪkɪŋ/
adj.|照看的；护理的|Relating to or involved in the act of taking care of someone or something.|He found the caretaking responsibilities to be quite demanding. 他发现照料的责任相当繁重。|近义词：caring, nurturing, protective, fostering|反义词：neglectful, uncaring|词性:形容词
