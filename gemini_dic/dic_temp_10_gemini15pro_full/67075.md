exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ (BrE) /ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ (AmE)| n. |功绩；勋绩；英勇事迹| a notable or heroic deed|例句：His wartime exploits made him a national hero. 他的战时功绩使他成为民族英雄。|近义词：feat, accomplishment, achievement|反义词:failure, defeat|词性:名词

exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ (BrE) /ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ (AmE)| vt. |利用；剥削；开采| to make full use of a resource; to use a person or situation in a way that is considered unfair or selfish|例句：The company exploited its workers by paying them low wages. 这家公司剥削工人，支付低工资。|近义词：utilize, capitalize on, take advantage of|反义词:preserve, protect|词性:动词
