lorry:/ˈlɒri/ (UK) /ˈlɔːri/ (US)| n.|卡车，货车|A large, heavy motor vehicle for transporting goods; a truck.|例句：The lorry was loaded with goods. 卡车装满了货物。

例句：The lorry driver was tired after a long journey. 卡车司机长途旅行后很累。|近义词：truck, van, HGV (heavy goods vehicle)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "lurry," meaning "to pull or drag," which is probably of dialectal origin, possibly related to Dutch "lorren" (to shake, to rumble). The meaning shifted from the act of pulling to the vehicle being pulled, and then to a self-propelled motor vehicle for carrying goods.
