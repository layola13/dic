antemetic:/ˌæntiɪˈmɛtɪk/
n. adj.|止吐药；止吐的|A medication that prevents or reduces nausea and vomiting.|The doctor prescribed an antemetic to help with the patient's nausea. 医生给病人开了止吐药以缓解恶心。|近义词：antiemetic drug, antiemetic agent, antinauseant|反义词:emetic|词性:名词, 形容词

antemetic:/ˌæntiɪˈmɛtɪk/
adj.|止吐的|Preventing or reducing nausea and vomiting.|The antemetic properties of ginger are well-known.  生姜的止吐特性是众所周知的。|近义词：antinauseant|反义词:emetic|词性:形容词
