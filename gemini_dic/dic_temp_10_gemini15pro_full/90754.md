hummus: /ˈhʊməs/ (US), /ˈhʌməs/ (UK) | n. | 鹰嘴豆泥 | A Middle Eastern dip or spread made from cooked, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil. | I love to eat hummus with pita bread. 我喜欢用皮塔饼蘸鹰嘴豆泥吃。|  Near synonyms: chickpea spread, dip | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "dry snack") | noun
