needn't:/ˈniːdnt/| modal verb|不需要|Contraction of "need not." It indicates a lack of obligation or necessity.|You needn't worry about that. 你不需要担心那个。|You needn't bring anything to the party. 你不需要带任何东西来参加聚会。|近义词：don't have to, don't need to|反义词:must, have to|词性:情态动词

被动语态:

疑问句:
Needn't you go to work today? = 你今天不用上班吗？
Needn't she attend the meeting? = 她不需要参加会议吗？

否定句:
You needn't not go to work today. (Double negative, implies you should go) = 你今天应该去上班。(双重否定，意味着你应该去)
He needn't not worry about it. (Double negative, implies he should worry) = 他应该担心它。(双重否定，意味着他应该担心)




PHRASE->
(Needn't is generally not used in phrases. It stands alone as a modal verb.)


注意事项:
Needn't is more common in British English than American English.  American English more often uses "don't need to" or "don't have to."
