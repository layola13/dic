lithogenous:/lɪˈθɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
岩成的，来源于岩石的
Derived from the breakdown, or weathering, of pre-existing rocks.
Lithogenous sediments are derived from the weathering of continental rocks. 岩成沉积物来源于大陆岩石的风化作用。
The lithogenous component of marine sediments is primarily terrigenous. 海洋沉积物中的岩成成分主要来源于陆地。
近义词: terrigenous, rock-derived
反义词: biogenous, hydrogenous, cosmogenous
词性: adjective
