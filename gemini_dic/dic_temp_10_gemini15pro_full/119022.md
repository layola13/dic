methodological:/ˌmɛθədəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmeθədəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (UK)| adj.|方法论的；方法的|Relating to the system of methods used in a particular area of study or activity.|The study employed a rigorous methodological approach.|近义词：systematic, methodical, procedural|反义词：unsystematic, haphazard|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From French *méthodologique*, from *méthode* (“method”) + *-ique* (“-ic”) + *-al* (“-al”), ultimately from Ancient Greek *μέθοδος* (méthodos, “way of inquiry or teaching; pursuit”) + *-λογία* (-logía, “study of”).
