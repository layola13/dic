abracadabra:/ˌæbrəkəˈdæbrə/| interj. n.|咒语，胡言乱语|A word said by magicians when performing a magic trick.  It is believed to have mystical powers.|The magician waved his wand and said, "Abracadabra!"  魔术师挥舞着魔杖，说道：“阿布拉卡达布拉！”|近义词：hocus pocus, magic word, incantation|反义词: (none -  it's a unique word)|interjection, noun

abracadabra:/ˌæbrəkəˈdæbrə/| n.|咒语，胡言乱语|Meaningless words or gibberish.|He was talking abracadabra, I couldn't understand a thing. 他胡言乱语，我一句也听不懂。|近义词：gibberish, nonsense, mumbo jumbo|反义词: (none - refers to lack of meaning)|noun
