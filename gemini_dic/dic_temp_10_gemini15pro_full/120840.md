misawarding:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrdɪŋ/
verb

The committee was accused of misawarding the prize to the wrong person. 委员会被指控将奖项错误地颁发给了错误的人。
The scholarship was misawarded due to an administrative error.  由于管理错误，奖学金被错误地授予。
misallocate, misassign, wrongly award
correctly awarding, justly awarding
