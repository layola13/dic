overneutralizes:/ˌoʊvərˈnjuːtrəˌlaɪz/
verb
过度中和
To neutralize to an excessive degree; to add too much of a neutralizing agent, resulting in a shift to the opposite extreme.
The chemist overneutralized the acid, making the solution slightly basic. 化学家过度中和了酸，使溶液略呈碱性。
overcompensate, overdo
underneutralize
v.
