coleslaw:/ˈkoʊlslɔː/
n.
凉拌卷心菜丝，菜丝沙拉
A salad made of finely shredded raw cabbage with a dressing, typically mayonnaise based.
例句：The coleslaw was creamy and tangy.  凉拌卷心菜丝又滑又酸。

例句：We had burgers and coleslaw for lunch. 我们午餐吃了汉堡和凉拌卷心菜丝。

近义词：cabbage salad
反义词:  (Coleslaw doesn't have a direct antonym.)
词性: noun
