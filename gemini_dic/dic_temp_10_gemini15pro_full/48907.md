defray:/dɪˈfreɪ/
verb
支付，承担费用
to provide money to pay (the cost of something)
The company defrays all expenses for employees attending the conference.  公司承担员工参加会议的所有费用。
The grant defrays the cost of tuition for low-income students. 这笔助学金支付了低收入学生的学费。
