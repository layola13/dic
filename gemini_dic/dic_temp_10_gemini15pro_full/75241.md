frolicsomely:/ˈfrɑːlɪksəmli/ | adv. |嬉戏地，欢乐地 | In a playful and lively manner; merrily. | The children frolicsomely chased each other around the park. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏地互相追逐。| The lambs frolicsomely leaped in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上欢快地跳跃着。 | 近义词：playfully, merrily, cheerfully, joyfully | 反义词：somberly, seriously, gloomily | 词性:副词 | 词源: From frolicsome + -ly. Frolicsome is from frolic (v.) + -some. Frolic (v.) is from Middle Dutch vrolyc "joyful, merry," related to Middle Low German vrolik, Dutch vroolijk.
