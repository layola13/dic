overmanaging:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/| v. |过度管理，管得太多|The act of managing something or someone too closely or excessively, often to the detriment of efficiency and morale. |He was accused of overmanaging his team, stifling their creativity. 他被指责过度管理他的团队，扼杀了他们的创造力。| Micromanaging, overcontrolling|Undermanaging, delegating| Verb
