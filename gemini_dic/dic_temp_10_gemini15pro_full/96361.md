indiretin: /ɪndɪˈrɛtɪn/
noun
间接视紫红质, 一种光敏色素
A photosensitive pigment found in some bacteria, similar to rhodopsin, that plays a role in light-sensing and energy production.
Indiretin is a light-sensitive pigment found in certain bacterial species, enabling them to respond to light stimuli.
间接视紫红质是一种存在于某些细菌物种中的光敏色素，使其能够对光刺激做出反应。

Synonyms: bacteriorhodopsin, microbial rhodopsin
Antonyms: None (No direct opposite for a specific pigment)
