boardlike:/ˈbɔːrdlaɪk/
adj.
像木板的；僵硬的；呆板的
Resembling a board in stiffness, flatness, or thinness.  Having a stiff, flat, and unyielding quality.
The old man's face was boardlike and expressionless.  老人的脸像木板一样僵硬，毫无表情。
His handshake was surprisingly boardlike. 他的握手出奇地僵硬。
近义词:stiff, rigid, inflexible, wooden
反义词:flexible, supple, pliable
词性:adjective
