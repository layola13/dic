oversolidifications:/ˌoʊvərˌsəlɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度凝固 | The process of solidifying beyond the normal or desired extent.  Excessive solidification, often leading to undesirable material properties. | The oversolidifications in the casting process resulted in brittleness.  铸造过程中过度凝固导致了脆性。| Near synonyms: overcooling, excessive solidification | Near antonyms:  undercooling, incomplete solidification | noun
