dispiriting:/dɪˈspɪrɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的，使人灰心的
Causing someone to lose enthusiasm and hope; disheartening.
The news from the front lines was dispiriting.  前线传来的消息令人沮丧。
近义词: disheartening, discouraging, demoralizing, depressing
反义词: encouraging, heartening, uplifting, inspiring
词性: adjective

词源: From dis- + spirit + -ing.  Dis- 表示否定或相反，spirit 指精神，-ing 构成形容词。
