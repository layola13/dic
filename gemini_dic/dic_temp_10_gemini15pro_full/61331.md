embattail:/ɪmˈbætəl/ | v. | 投入战斗，部署战斗; 设防 | To arrange troops for battle; to fortify. | The general embattled his troops on the hilltop. 将军把他的部队部署在山顶上。 |  The city was quickly embattled in preparation for the siege.  这座城市迅速设防，准备迎接围攻。 | 近义词: deploy, array, fortify, prepare for battle | 反义词: withdraw, retreat | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Old French *em bataillier (“to arrange in battle order”), from en- (“in, into”) + bataille (“battle”). Ultimately from Late Latin battualia, plural of battuālia (“fencing, fighting”).
