counterrolment:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈroʊlmənt/
n.
反注册，对抗性注册
An act of enrolling in opposition to another enrolment, especially in a rival military force.
Example: The counterrolment of young men in the rebel army increased rapidly. 年轻人在叛军中的反注册人数迅速增加。

Example:  Historians documented the counterrolment of citizens in the occupied territory, demonstrating resistance to the imposed regime.  历史学家记录了被占领土上公民的反注册，这表明了他们对强加政权的抵抗。

Near Synonyms: counter-inscription, counter-registration, opposing enrollment
Antonyms: enrolment, registration, conscription
