mediates:/ˈmiːdieɪts/| v. |调解，斡旋；传达，传递|acts as an intermediary between two parties to effect an agreement or reconciliation; transmits or conveys|例句：The lawyer mediates between the two companies. 律师在两家公司之间进行调解。

例句：The teacher mediates the discussion between the students. 老师在学生之间调解讨论。

近义词：arbitrates, negotiates, intercedes, conveys, transmits|反义词:aggravates, exacerbates|词性:动词
