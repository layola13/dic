aftershock:/ˈɑːftərʃɒk/ (UK) /ˈæftərʃɑːk/ (US)| n.|余震；后续冲击|A smaller earthquake following the main shock of a large earthquake. Figuratively, it can also refer to the continuing effects of a major event.|例句：The aftershocks of the earthquake continued for days. 地震的余震持续了好几天。

例句：The company is still dealing with the aftershocks of the scandal.  公司仍在处理丑闻的后续冲击。|近义词：tremor, repercussion, consequence|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "precursor" in a figurative sense) |词性:noun
