demonstratively:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪtɪvli/
adverb
公开地；明显地；凭示范
in a way that clearly shows a particular feeling or quality
She demonstratively kissed her husband in front of the waiting photographers.  她在等候的摄影师面前公开地吻了她的丈夫。


demonstratively:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪtɪvli/
adverb
指示性地
pointing or referring to something
He waved demonstratively at the empty space where the building used to stand. 他指着那栋建筑物曾经矗立的空地，做着手势。


demonstratively:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪtɪvli/
adverb
论证性地
in a way that proves something clearly
The study demonstratively links smoking with lung cancer. 这项研究明确地将吸烟与肺癌联系起来。



近义词:clearly, evidently, obviously, openly, plainly
反义词:subtly, implicitly, covertly
词性:adverb

词源: From Late Latin *dēmonstrātīvus + -ly.  From Latin dēmōnstrātīvus (“serving to point out”).  From dēmōnstrō (“I point out, demonstrate”) + -īvus.
