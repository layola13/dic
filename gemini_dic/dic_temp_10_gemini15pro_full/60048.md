educators:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtərz/
n.
教育工作者；教育家
People who provide instruction or guidance in educational settings, such as teachers, professors, trainers, and tutors.
The educators at this school are highly qualified. 这所学校的教育工作者素质很高。
Synonyms: teachers, instructors, trainers, tutors, mentors
Antonyms: students, pupils, learners
noun
