coden:/ˈkoʊdən/| n. |密码子|A sequence of three nucleotides that together form a unit of genetic code in a DNA or RNA molecule.|Example:  The codon AUG codes for the amino acid methionine. 密码子 AUG 编码氨基酸甲硫氨酸。|Example:  A change in a single codon can lead to a different protein being produced.  单个密码子的改变会导致产生不同的蛋白质。|Synonym: triplet |Antonym: N/A|Noun
