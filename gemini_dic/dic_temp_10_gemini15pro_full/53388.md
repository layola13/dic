disagreement's:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːmənts/ | n. |分歧，不一致 |The disagreement's root lies in their differing interpretations of the contract. 这个分歧的根源在于他们对合同的不同解释。|  The lawyer pointed out the flaws in the disagreement's core arguments. 律师指出了分歧核心论点中的缺陷。 | synonyms: discord, contention, difference, dispute, variance | antonyms: agreement, accord, harmony, concord, consensus | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *disagrement, from Anglo-Norman desagreement, from des- (prefix meaning "not, opposite of") + agrement ("agreement").  The suffix -'s is a possessive marker, indicating that the disagreement "belongs to" something or someone, or is associated with a particular thing.
