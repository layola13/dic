overplayed: /ˌōvərˈplād/ | adj. | 过度的；播放过多的；表演过火 | Played or performed too much; exaggerated. | The song was so overplayed on the radio that I got sick of it. 这首歌在电台上播放得太多了，我都听腻了。| 近义词: overdone, exaggerated, hackneyed | 反义词: understated, subtle | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "played" (past participle of "play," from Old English plegan "to exercise oneself, move briskly, contend, frolic").
