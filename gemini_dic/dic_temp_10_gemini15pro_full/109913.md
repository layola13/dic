libatory:/ˈlaɪbətɔːri/
adjective
奠酒的；祭酒的；用于奠酒的
relating to or suitable for the pouring out of a drink as an offering to a god
The libatory rituals were performed at dawn.  黎明时分举行了奠酒仪式。

近义词:ceremonial, ritualistic
反义词:None

词性:adjective
词源:From Latin lībātōrius (“of libation”), from lībātiō (“libation”), from lībō (“pour out, make a libation”), possibly from Proto-Italic *leibō, from Proto-Indo-European *лейbʰ- (“pour, libate”).
