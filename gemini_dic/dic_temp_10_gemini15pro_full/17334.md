beachcomber:/ˈbiːtʃˌkəʊmə(r)/ (US) /ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊmər/| n. |海滩拾荒者；海滩流浪汉|A person who walks along beaches looking for things that have been washed ashore, especially valuable or useful things. |例句：The beachcomber found a beautiful seashell amongst the debris.  海滩拾荒者在残骸中发现了一个美丽的贝壳。

例句：He lived a simple life as a beachcomber, surviving on what the sea provided. 他过着简朴的海滩拾荒者的生活，靠大海的馈赠生存。|近义词：scavenger, forager, beach walker|反义词: None (没有明确的反义词)|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From beach + comb (v.), meaning "to search thoroughly."  The term originated in the 18th century, referring to people who searched beaches for valuable goods from shipwrecks.
