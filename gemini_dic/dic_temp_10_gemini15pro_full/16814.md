basemap:/ˈbeɪsˌmæp/| n. |底图；基础地图|A basemap is a map that provides a base layer of geographic information, such as landforms, roads, and political boundaries, on which other data can be overlaid.|The cartographer used a basemap showing elevation and major rivers.|近义词：foundation map, background map|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps thematic map which focuses on specific data)|noun
