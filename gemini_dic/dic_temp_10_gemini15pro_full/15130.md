bacillar:/bəˈsɪlər/ | adj. |杆状的；棒状的|Relating to, resembling, or containing bacilli (rod-shaped bacteria).|Bacillar dysentery is a type of dysentery caused by bacilli. 杆菌性痢疾是一种由杆菌引起的痢疾。|The infection was confirmed to be bacillar. 感染被确认为是杆菌引起的。|rod-shaped, bacilliform |coccal, spherical|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bacillus + -ar.  "Bacillus" comes from Latin bacillum, meaning "little staff" or "rod".
