grisaille:/ɡrɪˈzaɪl/ | n. | 灰色画; 灰画法; 单色画 | A method of painting in gray monochrome, typically to imitate sculpture.  A painting executed in this style. |  例句：The artist used grisaille to create a sense of depth and form in the painting.  这位艺术家用灰色画法在画作中创造了深度和形式感。

例句：The grisaille underpainting provided a solid foundation for the subsequent layers of color. 灰色底色为随后的彩色层提供了坚实的基础。 | 近义词：monochrome, camaieu | 反义词: polychrome | 词性: noun
