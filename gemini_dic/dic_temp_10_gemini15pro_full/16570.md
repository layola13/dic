barnets:/ˈbɑːrnɪts/| n.|孩子（古英语，通常指私生子）|An archaic term, primarily used in dialects of English, referring to children, often with the implication of illegitimacy.|例句：The barnets were playing in the street. 孩子们在街上玩耍。|例句：She had several barnets, none of whom were acknowledged by their father. 她有几个孩子，没有一个被他们的父亲承认。|近义词：children, kids, offspring|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly adults)|词性:名词
