disordination:/dɪsˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/

|近义词：disorder, chaos, confusion, mess|
|反义词：order, organization, harmony|
|词性：名词|

disordination:/dɪsˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/
|n.|（生理）协调障碍|lack of coordination, especially of muscular movements.|The patient exhibited disordination in their gait after the stroke. 病人在中风后步态出现协调障碍。|
|近义词：incoordination, ataxia|
|反义词：coordination|
|词性：名词|
