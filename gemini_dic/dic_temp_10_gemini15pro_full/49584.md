demerges:/diːˈmɜːrdʒɪz/
| v. |合并解除，拆分|To separate (a previously merged company) into its constituent companies.|The company demerged its telecommunications division.  这家公司拆分了其电信部门。|Synonyms: separate, split, divide, divest|Antonyms: merge, combine, consolidate|verb

demerger:/diːˈmɜːrdʒər/
| n. |合并解除，拆分|The act of separating a previously merged company into its constituent companies.|The demerger of the company resulted in two separate entities.  公司的拆分导致了两个独立实体的产生。|Synonyms: separation, split, division, divestiture|Antonyms: merger, combination, consolidation|noun
