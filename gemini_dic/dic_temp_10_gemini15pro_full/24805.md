brothiest:/ˈbrɔːθiɪst/
adjective
最像肉汤的；最浓郁的
Having the qualities of broth; rich and flavorful.
The brothiest soup I've ever tasted was made with roasted chicken and vegetables.  我品尝过的最浓郁的汤是用烤鸡和蔬菜做的。

近义词:  rich, flavorful, savory, hearty
反义词:  thin, watery, bland, tasteless
词性: adjective
