discourteous:/dɪsˈkɜːrtiəs/| adj.|不礼貌的，失礼的|Lacking courtesy; rude and impolite.|He was discourteous to the waiter, snapping his fingers and speaking rudely.|近义词：rude, impolite, disrespectful, insolent, uncivil|反义词: courteous, polite, respectful, civil|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From dis- "not" + courteous (from Old French curteis "courtly," from court). Ultimately from Latin cohors "enclosure, retinue."
