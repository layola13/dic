co-dependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.| 共同依赖; 互相依赖 |A psychological condition in which someone is in a relationship with another person who is addicted to something or has some kind of psychological or emotional problem, and the co-dependent person is controlled or manipulated by the other person.|She was in a co-dependent relationship with her alcoholic husband.| 近义词:interdependence, mutual dependence| 反义词:independence, autonomy| 词性:noun

co-dependent:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənt/| adj.| 共同依赖的; 互相依赖的| Relating to or characterized by codependency.|He recognized his co-dependent tendencies and sought therapy.|近义词:mutually dependent, interconnected|反义词:independent, self-sufficient| 词性:adjective
