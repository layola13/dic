exungulate:/eksʌŋɡjʊleɪt/
adjective
无蹄的；失去蹄的
Having lost hooves; lacking hooves.
Exungulate mammals like whales and dolphins evolved from hoofed ancestors. 像鲸鱼和海豚这样的无蹄哺乳动物是从有蹄的祖先进化而来的。
近义词: hoofless
反义词: ungulate
词性: adjective
