insuavity:/ɪnˈswɑːvɪti/
n.
粗鲁，不文雅，不客气
lack of suavity; the quality of being unpleasant in manner.
His insuavity made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的粗鲁让他很难交朋友。
近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy, incivility, ungraciousness
反义词：suavity, politeness, courtesy, civility, graciousness
词性：名词

词源：Late 16th century: from French *insuavité or late Latin insuavitas, from insuavis ‘unpleasant’ (see insuave).
