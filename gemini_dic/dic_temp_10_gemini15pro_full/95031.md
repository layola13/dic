inaccurate:/ɪnˈækjurət/| adj.|不准确的，不精确的|Not correct or exact; containing errors or mistakes.|The report was inaccurate and misleading. 这份报告不准确，而且具有误导性。|近义词：incorrect, erroneous, imprecise, inexact|反义词:accurate, precise, correct, exact|词性:形容词
Etymology: From in- "not" + accurate (from Latin accuratus "done with care," past participle of accurare "take care of," from ad- "to" + curare "take care of").
