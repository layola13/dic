bromism:/ˈbrəʊmɪzəm/ (US) /ˈbroʊmɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |溴中毒|A condition caused by excessive intake or absorption of bromine or bromine compounds, characterized by various symptoms such as skin eruptions, headache, and drowsiness.|He suffered from bromism after taking too much bromide medication. 他服用了过量的溴化物药物后患上了溴中毒。|近义词:bromine poisoning|反义词:None|noun
