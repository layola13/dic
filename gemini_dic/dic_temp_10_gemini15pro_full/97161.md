infinituple:/ˈɪnfɪnɪtjuːpəl/| n.|无限元组|An ordered list of infinitely many elements.  It's a generalization of the concept of a tuple, which has a finite number of elements.|Example:  An infinituple of real numbers could be represented as (x1, x2, x3, ...). 一个实数的无限元组可以表示为 (x1, x2, x3, ...).|Example: The concept of an infinituple is used in certain branches of mathematics and computer science. 无限元组的概念用于数学和计算机科学的某些分支。|近义词: infinite sequence, infinite list|反义词: tuple, finite sequence|词性: noun
