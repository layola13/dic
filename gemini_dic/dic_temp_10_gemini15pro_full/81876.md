grievance:/ˈɡriːvəns/ | n. |委屈；抱怨；不平之事|A real or imagined wrong or other cause for complaint or protest.|例句：He filed a grievance against his employer. 他对雇主提出了申诉。

例句：She had a long list of grievances. 她有一长串的委屈。

近义词：complaint, injustice, wrong, beef, gripe|反义词：satisfaction, contentment|词性：名词

grievancer:/ˈɡriːvənsər/ | n. |申诉人；抱怨者|A person who complains or protests about something.|例句：The grievancer was unhappy with the outcome. 申诉人对结果不满意。

例句：The company met with the grievancers to discuss their concerns. 公司与申诉人会面讨论他们的担忧。

近义词：complainant, protester, objector|反义词：supporter, approver|词性：名词
