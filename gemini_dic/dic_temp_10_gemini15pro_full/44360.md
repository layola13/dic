cross-checked:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃekt/ (British), /ˌkrɔːsˈtʃekt/ (American)| adj.|交叉核对的，反复核实的|Verified by comparing with other sources or information.  The process of verifying information from multiple sources to ensure accuracy and consistency.|例句：The data was cross-checked with several independent sources. 数据与几个独立来源进行了交叉核对。|近义词：verified, validated, corroborated|反义词：unchecked, unverified|词性:形容词

cross-check:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃek/ (British), /ˌkrɔːsˈtʃek/ (American)| v.|交叉核对，反复核实|To verify information by comparing it with other sources. |例句：We need to cross-check these figures against last year's results. 我们需要将这些数字与去年的结果进行交叉核对。|近义词：verify, validate, corroborate|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性:动词
