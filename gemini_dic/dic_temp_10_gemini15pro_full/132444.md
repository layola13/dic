nondeposition:/ˌnɒndɪpəˈzɪʃən/
n.
非沉积; 未沉积; 不沉积
The absence of deposition, or the process of not depositing something.  The nondeposition of sediment in that area resulted in a gap in the geological record.  该地区沉积物的未沉积导致地质记录的缺失。
The nondeposition of the funds caused a delay in the project. 这笔资金的未到位导致了项目的延误。
non-accumulation, non-laying down
deposition, sedimentation
noun
