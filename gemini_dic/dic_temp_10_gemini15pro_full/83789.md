hairless:/ˈheələs/| adj.|无毛的，光秃的|Without hair; bald.|The hairless cat sphinx is known for its unusual appearance. 无毛猫斯芬克斯以其不寻常的外表而闻名。|The baby's skin was soft and hairless. 婴儿的皮肤柔软且无毛。|近义词：bald, glabrous|反义词: hairy, furry|词性:形容词
Etymology: Old English *hǣrlēas, from hǣr ‘hair’ + -lēas ‘-less’.
