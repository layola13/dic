outpopulate:/ˌaʊtˈpɑːpjuleɪt/
|v.|人口超过，人口数量胜过|To exceed in population; to have a larger population than another group or area.|The rabbits quickly outpopulated the foxes on the island. 兔子在这个岛上的人口数量迅速超过了狐狸。|近义词：exceed, surpass, outnumber|反义词：be outnumbered by|词性:动词
