cd-rw:/ˌsiːdiː ɑːrˈdʌbəljuː/ |n.| 光盘刻录机；可擦写光盘 |A CD-RW (Compact Disc-ReWritable) is a type of compact disc that can be written to, erased, and rewritten multiple times. | I bought a new CD-RW to back up my data. 我买了一个新的可擦写光盘来备份我的数据。|  synonyms: rewritable CD, erasable CD | antonyms: CD-R (Compact Disc-Recordable) |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
CD-RW is an abbreviation of Compact Disc-ReWritable.  "Compact Disc" refers to the optical disc format. "ReWritable" signifies the disc's ability to be written to multiple times.
