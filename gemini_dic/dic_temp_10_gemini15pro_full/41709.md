corroboration:/kəˌrɑːbəˈreɪʃn/
n.
证实，确证
Evidence that confirms or supports a statement, theory, or finding.
The witness's statement provided corroboration for the victim's account of the robbery.  证人的陈述证实了受害者对抢劫案的描述。

The detective sought corroboration of the suspect's alibi.  侦探试图证实嫌疑人的不在场证明。


confirmation, verification, validation, substantiation
contradiction, refutation, denial, disproof
noun
