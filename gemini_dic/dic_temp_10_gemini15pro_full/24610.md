broilingly:/ˈbrɔɪlɪŋli/ | adv. | 酷热地，灼热地 | Extremely hot, like under a broiler.  | The sun beat down broilingly on the desert sand. 太阳酷热地炙烤着沙漠的沙子。|  The steak was broilingly hot, straight off the grill. 牛排刚从烤架上下来，热得烫人。 | 近义词: scorchingly, intensely hot, swelteringly | 反义词: coldly, freezingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "broil" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Broil" comes from the Old French word "broillier," meaning "to roast on a gridiron."
