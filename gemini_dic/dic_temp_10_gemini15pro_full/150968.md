paramere:/ˈpærəmɪər/| n. |交配器，阳茎侧突|A sclerotized, paired appendage of the aedeagus (penis) in male insects, used for holding the female during copulation.|The shape of the parameres is often species-specific and useful in insect identification. 交配器的形状通常是物种特异性的，可用于昆虫识别。|Synonyms: clasper, gonopod|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific anatomical structure)|noun
