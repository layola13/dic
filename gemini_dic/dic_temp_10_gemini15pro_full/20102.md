biosystem:/ˈbaɪoʊˌsɪstəm/ | n. | 生物系统 | A complex network of living organisms and their physical environment, interacting as a unit. |  The Amazon rainforest is a complex biosystem. 亚马逊雨林是一个复杂的生物系统。|  The health of a biosystem depends on the balance of its components.  一个生物系统的健康取决于其组成部分的平衡。 | ecosystem, biota, ecocomplex |  None | noun
