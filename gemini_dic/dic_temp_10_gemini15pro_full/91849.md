hyperhypocrisy:/ˌhaɪpərhɪˈpɑːkrəsi/
n.
超级伪善，极端虚伪
The act of condemning others for behavior that one engages in oneself, often to an even greater extent.  It's a heightened form of hypocrisy.
He accused the company of polluting the environment, while his own factory had a terrible environmental record, a clear case of hyperhypocrisy. 他指责该公司污染环境，而他自己的工厂却有着糟糕的环境记录，这是一个明显的超级伪善的例子。
近义词：duplicity, sanctimony, cant, sham
反义词：sincerity, honesty, genuineness
词性：noun

词源:
"Hyper-" (Greek) meaning "over, above, excessive" + "hypocrisy" (from Greek "hypokrisis" meaning "acting of a stage player, pretense").  Essentially, "excessive hypocrisy."
