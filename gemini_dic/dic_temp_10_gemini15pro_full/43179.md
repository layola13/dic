cqd:/siːkjuːˈdiː/ | abbreviation |  CQD (无线电求救信号，现已淘汰) | CQD is an obsolete distress signal, superseded by SOS.  It was used in wireless telegraphy and is a combination of two Morse code prosigns: CQ ("seek you" - calling all stations) and D ("distress"). |  "The operator tapped out 'CQD' repeatedly, hoping someone would hear."  操作员反复敲出“CQD”，希望有人能听到。 | 近义词: SOS | 反义词: None | abbreviation
