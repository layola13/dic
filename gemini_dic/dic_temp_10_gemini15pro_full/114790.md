maltreating:/ˌmælˈtriːtɪŋ/
verb
虐待，苛待
subjecting to cruel or inhumane treatment
The child was maltreating the family pet.  这个孩子在虐待家里的宠物。

近义词:abusing, mistreating, ill-treating, harming
反义词:caring for, cherishing, nurturing, protecting
词性: Verb (present participle)

词源:
From mal- (badly) + treat (to act or behave toward).  The "mal-" prefix is from Latin, meaning "bad" or "evil."  "Treat" comes from Old French "traitier" and ultimately from Latin "tractare."
