fall behind:/fɔːl bɪˈhaɪnd/
verb phrase
落后，拖欠
to fail to keep up with a schedule, payments, etc.; to make less progress than others
例句：He fell behind with his mortgage payments. 他拖欠了抵押贷款。
例句：She fell behind in her studies after missing several classes.  她缺了几节课后，学习落后了。
近义词：lag behind, fall back, lose ground
反义词：catch up, keep up, get ahead
词性：动词短语

fall behind:/fɔːl bɪˈhaɪnd/
noun phrase
落后，拖欠（状态）
the state of being behind schedule, payments, etc. (less common usage as a noun phrase)
例句：His fall behind in his studies worried his parents. 他学习上的落后让他的父母很担心。(不常用)
例句：The company's fall behind in technology led to its decline.  公司在技术上的落后导致了它的衰败。(不常用)
近义词：arrears, backlog, deficit
反义词：lead, advantage, surplus
词性：名词短语

词源:  "Fall" comes from Old English *feallan "to fall, tumble, fall down, fall into ruin, die,"  "Behind" comes from Old English behindan, from bi "by" + hindan "from behind."  The combination of these words creates the phrasal verb meaning to make less progress than others or a plan.
