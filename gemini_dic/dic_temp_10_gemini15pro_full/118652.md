mestiza:/mɛˈstiːsə/ /mɛˈstiːθə/
noun
混血女子（尤指西班牙人和美洲印第安人的后裔）; a woman of mixed race, especially one having Spanish and Indigenous American ancestry.
Maria is a mestiza, her father is Spanish and her mother is Native American. 玛丽亚是一个混血女子，她父亲是西班牙人，她母亲是美洲原住民。
Synonyms: hybrid, mixed-race
Antonyms: purebred (for animals), full-blooded
词性: noun
