haram:/həˈrɑːm/ | adj. n. | 禁止的，非法；禁忌 | Forbidden by Islamic law; religiously unlawful.  Something that is haram is prohibited. |  Eating pork is haram in Islam.  在伊斯兰教中，吃猪肉是禁忌。| Synonyms: forbidden, prohibited, unlawful, illicit | Antonyms: halal, permissible, lawful | adjective, noun

haram:/həˈrɑːm/ |  n. |禁忌，非法事物 | Something that is forbidden by Islamic law. | The sale of alcohol is haram. 酒精的销售是非法的。 | Synonyms: prohibition, taboo | Antonyms: halal | noun
