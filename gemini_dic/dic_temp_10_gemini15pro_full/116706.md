matweed:/ˈmætˌwiːd/| n. |野麦草; 旱雀麦|A type of wild grass, specifically *Elymus repens*, also known as couch grass. It is a common weed in many parts of the world.|The matweed was so thick, it was difficult to walk through. 野麦草太密了，很难穿过。|The farmer struggled to eradicate the matweed from his field. 农民努力从他的田地里根除野麦草。|Synonyms: couch grass, quackgrass, twitch grass|Antonyms: cultivated grass|noun
