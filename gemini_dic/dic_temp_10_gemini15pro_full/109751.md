leviathan:/ləˈvaɪəθən/ /lɪˈvaɪəθən/| n.|巨兽，庞然大物，强大的机构或组织|A sea monster mentioned in the Bible; something huge and powerful.|例句：The company was a leviathan in the industry.  这家公司是行业巨头。

例句：The government is often described as a leviathan. 政府常被描述为一个庞然大物。|近义词：monster, behemoth, giant, colossus|反义词:dwarf, midget|词性:名词
