parquette:/pɑːrˈkeɪ/ | n. |  （剧院的）正厅后座；正厅后座观众| the part of the main floor of a theater behind the orchestra section |  They had cheap seats in the parquette. 他们的座位在正厅后座，很便宜。 | synonyms: orchestra stalls, rear stalls | antonyms: balcony, mezzanine | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *parquet*, diminutive of *parc*, from Old French, from Frankish *parruk*, enclosure, of Germanic origin; akin to Old English *pearroc* park — more at park
