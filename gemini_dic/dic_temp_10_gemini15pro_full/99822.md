intermingle:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡl/ | v. |混合，交织|To mix or blend together.|例句：The two families intermingled at the wedding reception.  两个家庭在婚宴上互相交融。

例句：The flavors intermingled to create a unique taste. 各种口味混合在一起，创造出独特的风味。|近义词：mix, blend, merge, combine, fuse|反义词：separate, divide, segregate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + mingle, from Old English mengan "to mix."
