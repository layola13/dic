netizen:/ˈnetɪzən/| n.|网民|A person who uses the internet, especially a habitual or avid user.|例句：Netizens quickly shared the news on social media. 网民们很快在社交媒体上分享了这个消息。

例句：Many netizens expressed their opinions on the new policy. 许多网民对新政策表达了他们的意见。|近义词：internet user, cybercitizen, online user|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Combination of "net" (from "internet") and "citizen."  Coined in the mid-1990s.
