overrational:/ˌoʊvərˈræʃənəl/ | adj. | 过度理性 | Excessively rational; basing decisions or actions on logic and reason to an extreme or inappropriate degree, often neglecting emotional or social factors. |  He made an overrational decision to sell his beloved family home, purely based on market value, ignoring the sentimental value. 他过度理性地决定卖掉他深爱的家庭住宅，纯粹基于市场价值，忽略了情感价值。

The overrational approach to child-rearing sometimes overlooks the importance of play and emotional development. 过于理性的育儿方法有时会忽略游戏和情感发展的重要性。 | 近义词: hyperrational, excessively logical | 反义词: irrational, emotional, impulsive | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "rational" (adjective from Latin "rationalis," meaning "reasonable").
