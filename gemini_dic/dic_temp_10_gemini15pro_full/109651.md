leukaemia:/luːˈkiːmiə/ (UK), /luːˈkimiə/ (US)| n. |白血病|A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leucocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anaemia and other symptoms.|My uncle was diagnosed with leukaemia last year. 我叔叔去年被诊断出患有白血病。| Leukemia is a type of blood cancer that affects the bone marrow. 白血病是一种影响骨髓的血癌。|Synonym: leukemia (US spelling), cancer of the blood|Antonym: health, wellness|noun
