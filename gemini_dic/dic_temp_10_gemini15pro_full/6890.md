anchoress:/ˈæŋkərɪs/| n. |女隐士，女锚修者|A woman who withdraws from the world for religious reasons, often living in seclusion and dedicating herself to prayer and contemplation. |例句：The anchoress lived a solitary life in her small cell, devoted to prayer.  这位女隐士在她的小房间里过着孤独的生活，致力于祈祷。

例句：Stories of anchorites and anchoresses abound in the medieval period.  中世纪时期，关于隐士和女隐士的故事比比皆是。|近义词：hermit, recluse, nun|反义词:|n.
