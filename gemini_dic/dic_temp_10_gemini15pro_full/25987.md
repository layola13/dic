bursiculate: /bɜːˈsɪkjʊleɪt/ | adj. |  囊状的；小囊的 | Having or resembling small pouches or burs. | The plant's bursiculate leaves held tiny droplets of water. 这种植物囊状的叶子上挂着细小的水滴。 |  pouch-like, saccate | - | adjective
Etymology: From Latin *bursicula*, diminutive of bursa (“purse, bag”), + -ate.
