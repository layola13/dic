allopathist:/əˈlɒpəθɪst/
noun
对抗疗法医师；对症疗法医师
A practitioner of allopathy, a system of medical practice that aims to combat disease by using remedies that produce effects different from those produced by the disease itself.
例句：The allopathist prescribed antibiotics for the infection. 对抗疗法医师开了抗生素来治疗感染。

例句：She consulted an allopathist for her chronic pain. 她咨询了一位对抗疗法医师来治疗她的慢性疼痛。

近义词：conventional doctor, traditional physician, Western doctor
反义词：homeopath, naturopath, alternative medicine practitioner
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From allo- (different) + -pathy (suffering, disease).  The term was coined in the mid-19th century by Samuel Hahnemann, the founder of homeopathy, to contrast his system of medicine with the then-dominant medical practices.
