melada:/meˈlɑːdə/ /məˈlɑːdə/ | n. |果酱，蜜饯|A sweet spread or preserve made from fruit and sugar, often used on bread or toast. |例句：She spread some strawberry melada on her toast. 她把一些草莓酱涂在吐司上。

例句：He enjoys apricot melada with his breakfast. 他喜欢早餐吃杏酱。

近义词：jam, preserve, conserve, marmalade |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


Etymology->
From Portuguese *melada* (attested from the 16th century),  from Late Latin *mellāta*, from Latin *mel* (“honey”).  The original meaning was "honeyed," but the term broadened to include fruit preserves.
