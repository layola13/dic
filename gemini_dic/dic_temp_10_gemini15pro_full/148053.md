overs:/ˈəʊvəz/ (British), /ˈoʊvərz/ (American)| n.| 局; 结束; 额外的东西; |Overs refers to a set of six legal deliveries bowled in cricket. It can also mean something that is finished or extra/surplus.|Example: The match ended after 50 overs.  比赛在50个回合后结束。

Example:  We have a few overs of fabric left from the project. 我们项目结束后还剩下一些多余的布料。|近义词: remainders, surplus, innings (in cricket)|反义词: shortage, deficit|词性:名词
