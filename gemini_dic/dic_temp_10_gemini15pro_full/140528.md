oleiferous:/ˌoʊlɪˈfɛrəs/
adjective
产油的；油质的
producing oil; oily
例句：Oleiferous seeds, such as sunflower seeds, are used to produce oil.  产油种子，例如葵花籽，被用来生产油。

例句：The oleiferous shale deposits are a valuable resource. 含油页岩矿床是一项宝贵的资源。

近义词：oily, unctuous
反义词：non-oily, dry
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oleifer, from oleum (“oil”) + -fer (“bearing”).
