breathtaking:/ˈbreθteɪkɪŋ/| adj.| 令人叹为观止的；惊险的；激动人心的| Inspiring awe; extremely exciting; thrilling. | The view from the mountaintop was breathtaking.  从山顶望下去的景色令人叹为观止。| synonyms: stunning, awesome, spectacular, amazing, astounding | antonyms: dull, boring, unimpressive, ordinary | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From breath + taking (present participle of take).  The literal sense is "taking one's breath away".
