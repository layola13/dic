foreplane:/ˈfɔːrpleɪn/| n. |前翼;水平安定面|A stabilizing plane located forward of the main wing of an aircraft. |The foreplane generated lift, helping the aircraft maintain stable flight.  前翼产生升力，帮助飞机保持稳定飞行。|Synonym: canard, forward wing|Antonym: tailplane, horizontal stabilizer|noun

foreplane:/ˈfɔːrpleɪn/| n. |(木工用的)长刨|A long plane used for smoothing wood. |The carpenter used a foreplane to smooth the surface of the board. 木匠用长刨来打磨木板的表面。|Synonym:  jointer plane, trying plane|Antonym: smoothing plane, block plane|noun
