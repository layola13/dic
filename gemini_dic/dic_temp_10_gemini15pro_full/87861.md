hideout: /ˈhaɪdaʊt/ (US), /ˈhaɪdaʊt/ (UK) | n. | 隐藏处，藏身处 | A place where someone hides, especially from the police or from other people who are looking for them. | The police found the robber's hideout in an abandoned warehouse. 警察在一个废弃的仓库里找到了劫匪的藏身处。|  The rebels used the cave as a hideout from government forces.  叛军用这个洞穴作为躲避政府军的藏身处。| synonyms: refuge, shelter, sanctuary | antonyms: exposure, open area | noun
