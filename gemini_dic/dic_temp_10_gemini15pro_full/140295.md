ogam:/ˈoʊɡəm/| n.|欧甘字母|An ancient alphabet used primarily by the Celts in Ireland and Britain, consisting of a series of strokes and notches cut on the edges of standing stones.|The inscription on the stone was written in ogam.|Synonyms:  Celtic script, old Irish script|Antonyms:  modern alphabet|noun
