crueler:/ˈkruːələr/ (US), /ˈkrʊələr/ (UK)| adj.|更残酷的，更无情的|Comparative form of cruel: more cruel.  Used to describe someone or something that is more disposed to inflict pain or suffering than another.|He was a crueler master than his predecessor. 他比他的前任是一个更残酷的主人。|Synonyms: harsher, meaner, more ruthless, more severe, more unkind|Antonyms: kinder, gentler, more compassionate, more merciful|adjective

cruelest:/ˈkruːəlɪst/ (US), /ˈkrʊəlɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最残酷的，最无情的|Superlative form of cruel: most cruel. Used to describe someone or something that is the most disposed to inflict pain or suffering.|That was the cruelest thing she could have said. 那是她能说的最残酷的话了。|Synonyms: harshest, meanest, most ruthless, most severe, most unkind|Antonyms: kindest, gentlest, most compassionate, most merciful|adjective
