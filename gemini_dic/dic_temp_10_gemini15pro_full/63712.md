ephemeris: /ɪˈfɛmərɪs/ /əˈfɛmərɪs/ | n. | 星历表；日历 | A table or data file giving the calculated positions of a celestial object at regular intervals throughout a period. |  The astronomer consulted the ephemeris to determine the planet's position. 天文学家查阅星历表来确定行星的位置。

The ephemeris provided the precise location of the comet. 星历表提供了彗星的精确位置。

近义词: almanac, calendar | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *ephemeris, from Greek *ephémeros "daily," from *epí "on, upon" + *hēméra "day."
