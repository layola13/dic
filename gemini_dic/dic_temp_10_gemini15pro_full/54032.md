discusses:/dɪˈskʌsɪz/
verb
讨论，谈论
Third-person singular present tense form of "discuss".  It means to talk about something with someone or a group of people, usually to exchange ideas or opinions.
He discusses the matter with his colleagues. 他和同事讨论这件事。
The committee discusses the budget proposal. 委员会讨论了预算提案。

discuss:/dɪˈskʌs/
verb
讨论，谈论
To talk about something with someone or a group of people, usually to exchange ideas or opinions.
We need to discuss the next steps. 我们需要讨论接下来的步骤。
They discussed the problem at length. 他们详细地讨论了这个问题。

近义词: talk over, debate, deliberate, consider, examine
反义词: ignore, avoid, disregard

词性: verb
