copublisher:/ˈkoʊˌpʌblɪʃər/| n.|共同出版商；联合出版者|A copublisher is a publisher who works with another publisher to produce and distribute a book or other publication.|Example 1: The small independent press acted as copublisher with the larger, more established company. 这家小型独立出版社与规模更大、更成熟的公司合作出版。

Example 2:  The university press and the museum were copublishers of the exhibition catalog. 大学出版社和博物馆是展览目录的联合出版商。|Synonym: joint publisher|Antonym: sole publisher|noun
