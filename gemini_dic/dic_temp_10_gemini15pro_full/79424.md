glisten:/ˈɡlɪsən/ | vi. | 闪耀，闪光 | To shine with a soft reflected light, often due to a wet or smooth surface. |  The rain made the streets glisten. 雨水使街道闪闪发光。| 近义词：sparkle, shimmer, glitter | 反义词：dull | 词性:动词

glisten:/ˈɡlɪsən/ | n. | 闪光，光泽 | A sparkling or shimmering light reflected from a wet or smooth surface. | The glisten of the dew on the grass was beautiful. 草地上露珠的闪光很美。| 近义词：sparkle, shimmer, glitter | 反义词：dullness | 词性:名词
