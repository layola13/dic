contentments:/kənˈtɛntmənts/
n.
满足; 满意
The state of being contented; satisfaction.
She found great contentment in her garden.  她在花园里找到了极大的满足。
近义词:satisfactions, gratifications, pleasures
反义词:discontentments, dissatisfactions, displeasures
词性:名词

contentment:/kənˈtɛntmənt/
n.
满足，满意
A state of happiness and satisfaction.
He felt a deep sense of contentment. 他感到了一种深深的满足感。
近义词:satisfaction, happiness, pleasure
反义词:discontent, unhappiness, displeasure
词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *contement, from Anglo-Norman contentement, Old French contentement, from contenter (“to content”), from Latin contentus (“contained, content”), past participle of continēre (“to hold together, contain”), from con- (“together”) + tenēre (“to hold”).
