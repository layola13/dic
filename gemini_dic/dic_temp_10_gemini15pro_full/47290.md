de-evolutions:/diːˌɛvəˈluːʃənz/
|n.|退化，逆进化|The concept of de-evolutions suggests a reverse process of evolution, where organisms become less complex or adapted over time.|Some argue that the loss of certain traits in some species could be seen as de-evolutions.|Synonyms: regression, degeneration, devolution|Antonyms: evolution, progress, advancement|noun
