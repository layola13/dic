immobilised:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzd/ (US) /ɪˈməʊbəlaɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|固定的，不能动的|Made unable to move or function.|例句：The accident victim was immobilised by neck brace.  事故受害者的脖子被颈托固定住了。|例句：His arm was immobilised in a cast. 他的手臂被石膏固定住了。|近义词：fixed, stationary, stuck, incapacitated|反义词：mobile, movable, free|词性:形容词

immobilise:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ (US) /ɪˈməʊbəlaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|使固定，使不能动|To prevent something or someone from moving or operating as normal.|例句：The police immobilised the stolen vehicle with a wheel clamp. 警察用车轮夹锁锁住了被盗车辆。|例句：Fear immobilised her.  恐惧让她动弹不得。|近义词：restrict, restrain, disable|反义词：mobilise, activate, free|词性:动词

词源: im- (not) + mobilis (movable) + -ised/-ize (suffix forming verbs and adjectives)  来自拉丁语，im- 表示“不”，mobilis 表示“可移动的”，-ised/-ize 是构成动词和形容词的后缀。
