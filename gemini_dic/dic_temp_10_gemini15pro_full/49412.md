delivers:/dɪˈlɪvərz/
verb
递送；发表；履行；接生
carries out or provides something promised or expected.
The postman delivers the mail every day. 邮递员每天都送信。
Synonyms: distributes, provides, executes, fulfills
Antonyms: withholds, retains, neglects, fails
词性: 动词 (verb)

delivers:/dɪˈlɪvərz/
verb
第三人称单数形式的动词 deliver
Third-person singular simple present form of the verb "deliver."
He delivers newspapers every morning. 他每天早上送报纸。
Synonyms: distributes, provides, executes, fulfills
Antonyms: withholds, retains, neglects, fails
词性: 动词 (verb)
