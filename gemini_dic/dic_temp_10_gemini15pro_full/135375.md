nonperforating:/ˌnɒnpɜːrfəˈreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非穿孔的，不穿透的 | Not penetrating through a surface or substance; not making a hole. | The doctor diagnosed a nonperforating ulcer. 医生诊断出非穿孔性溃疡。 |  A nonperforating wound didn't require stitches.  一个非穿孔的伤口不需要缝合。 | 近义词: nonpenetrating, intact | 反义词: perforating, penetrating | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From non- (not) + perforating (from Latin perforare, from per- 'through' + forare 'to bore').
