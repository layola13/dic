danube:/ˈdænjuːb/| n.|多瑙河|A major river in Central and Eastern Europe, flowing from Germany through Austria, Hungary, Slovakia, Serbia, Bulgaria, Romania, and Ukraine to the Black Sea.|The Danube flows through many European capitals.|近义词:river (generic)|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Danubius, from Celtic *Dānuvius, from Proto-Celtic *Dānowios.  Related to Old Irish dánae ("bold, strong, fierce"), Welsh don ("wave, billow").  The name possibly refers to the strong current of the river.
