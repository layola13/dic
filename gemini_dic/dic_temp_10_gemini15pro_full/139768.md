octahedral:/ˌɒktəˈhiːdrəl/ /ˌɑːktəˈhiːdrəl/| adj.|八面体的；八面晶系的|Having the shape of an octahedron; relating to an octahedron.  A solid figure with eight plane faces.|Diamonds are often found as octahedral crystals. 钻石常以八面体结晶的形式被发现。|Synonym: eight-sided, hexahedral|Antonym: non-octahedral|adjective
