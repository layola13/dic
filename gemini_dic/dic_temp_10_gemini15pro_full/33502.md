cicada:/sɪˈkeɪdə/, /sɪˈkɑːdə/ | n. |蝉|A large homopterous insect with long transparent wings, occurring chiefly in warm countries. The male produces a loud, high-pitched drone.| The cicadas were buzzing loudly in the trees.  树上的蝉鸣声很大。|Synonyms:  locust| Antonyms: None | Noun
