direct-mail:/dɪˈrɛkt meɪl/, /daɪˈrɛkt meɪl/ | n., adj. | 直邮广告，直邮的 | A marketing technique that involves sending promotional materials directly to potential customers through the postal service. | The company invested heavily in direct-mail advertising.  这家公司在直邮广告上投入了巨资。| synonyms: junk mail, advertising mail, direct advertising | antonyms: email marketing, online advertising | noun

direct-mail:/dɪˈrɛkt meɪl/, /daɪˈrɛkt meɪl/ | adj. | 直邮的 | Relating to or characteristic of direct mail. |  They launched a direct-mail campaign to target new customers. 他们发起了一项直邮活动以瞄准新客户。| synonyms: postal, mailed | antonyms: online, digital | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"direct" comes from the Latin word "directus," meaning "straight."
"mail" comes from the Middle French word "male," meaning "bag," referring to the bag used to carry letters.
