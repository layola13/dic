escharine:/ˈeskərɪn/
adjective
焦痂的; 结痂的
Relating to or resembling an eschar; of the nature of an eschar.  Formed from or covered with eschar.
The escharine tissue was removed to promote healing. 焦痂组织被移除以促进愈合。
The burn left an escharine wound. 烧伤留下了一个结痂的伤口。
Synonyms:  scabbed, crusted, escharotic
Antonyms:  healthy, unscarred
词性: adjective

eschar:/ˈɛskɑːr/
noun
焦痂; 痂
A dry, dark scab or falling away of dead skin, typically caused by a burn, or by the bite of a mite or spider.  A slough or piece of dead tissue that is cast off from the surface of the skin, particularly after a burn or cauterization.
The doctor removed the eschar from the burn. 医生清除了烧伤的焦痂。
The spider bite left a small eschar. 蜘蛛咬伤留下了一个小焦痂。
Synonyms:  scab, crust
Antonyms:  healthy skin
词性: noun
