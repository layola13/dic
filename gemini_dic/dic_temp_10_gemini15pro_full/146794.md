overillustrations:/ˌoʊvərˌɪləˈstreɪʃənz/| n. |过度插图，过多的插图|The use of excessive or unnecessarily detailed illustrations, often to a degree that detracts from the text or overwhelms the reader.|The children's book suffered from overillustrations; there was a picture on every page, making it hard to focus on the story.|近义词：excessive illustrations, superfluous illustrations|反义词:underillustration, lack of illustrations|词性:名词
