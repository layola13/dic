derange:/dɪˈreɪndʒ/ | vt. | 使精神错乱，扰乱 | To disturb the order or arrangement of; to make insane. |  His mind was deranged by grief.  他的精神被悲痛折磨得错乱了。

The violent storm completely deranged the garden.  暴风雨把花园彻底摧毁了。 | 近义词: unbalance, unsettle, disarrange, disorder, confuse, muddle | 反义词: organize, order, arrange, calm, soothe | 词性: 动词

deranged:/dɪˈreɪndʒd/ | adj. | 精神错乱的 | Insane; disturbed. | The deranged man shouted at passersby. 那个精神错乱的男人对着路人 shouted大喊大叫。

The furniture was in a deranged state after the party.  派对结束后，家具乱成一团。 | 近义词: insane, mad, lunatic, demented, unbalanced | 反义词: sane, rational, balanced, composed | 词性: 形容词

derangement:/dɪˈreɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 精神错乱，混乱 | The act of deranging or the state of being deranged; disorder; chaos. | He suffered from a mental derangement. 他患有精神错乱。

The derangement of his affairs caused him much stress.  他事务的混乱给他带来了很大的压力。 | 近义词: disorder, chaos, confusion, disarray, muddle | 反义词: order, arrangement, organization, harmony | 词性: 名词


词源:
From French *dérengier*, from *des-* (expressing reversal) + *rengier* "to arrange," from Frankish *hring* "circle, ring."
