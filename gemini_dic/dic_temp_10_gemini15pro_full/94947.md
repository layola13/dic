in-real-time:/ɪn ˈriːəl taɪm/| adj. adv.|实时地，实时|Happening or done instantly and without delay.|The data is processed in real time. 数据实时处理。|近义词：live, instantly, immediately|反义词：delayed, offline|词性:形容词/副词
Etymology: "in" (preposition) + "real" (adjective) + "time" (noun). The term emerged with the development of computing and telecommunications, referring to processes that occur with no perceptible delay.
