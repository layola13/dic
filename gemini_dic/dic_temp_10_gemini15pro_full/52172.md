diapnoic:/daɪˈæpnoɪk/| adj.|透明的，透光的|Allowing light to pass through; translucent.  Relating to or characterized by diapnosis.|The artist used diapnoic fabrics to create a sense of ethereal beauty. 艺术家用透光织物营造出一种空灵的美感。|Synonyms: translucent, transparent, pellucid|Antonyms: opaque, impermeable|adjective
