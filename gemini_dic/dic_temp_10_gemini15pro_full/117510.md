meekly: /ˈmiːkli/ | adv. | 温顺地；谦恭地；逆来顺受地 | In a quiet, gentle, and submissive manner.  Showing little strength or spirit. | She meekly accepted the criticism. 她温顺地接受了批评。|  quietly, gently, submissively, humbly, passively | boldly, assertively, defiantly | adverb

Etymology:
From Middle English *mekeli, from Old English *mēclīce (“meekly”), equivalent to meek +‎ -ly.
