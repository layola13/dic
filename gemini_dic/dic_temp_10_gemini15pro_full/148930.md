overwithheld:/ˌoʊvərwɪθˈheld/ | adj. |  预扣过多的；多扣的 | Having had too much tax withheld from one's earnings. | Example:  I received a large tax refund because my income tax was overwithheld. 我的所得税预扣过多，所以收到了大笔退税。

Example:  She realized she had overwithheld taxes and adjusted her W-4. 她意识到自己多扣了税，于是调整了她的W-4表格。 | 近义词: overdeducted, excessively withheld | 反义词: underwithheld | 词性: 形容词

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "withheld" (past participle of "withhold," meaning "to retain or keep back").  "Withhold" itself comes from Old English "wiðhaldan," from "wið" (against, back) + "haldan" (to hold).
