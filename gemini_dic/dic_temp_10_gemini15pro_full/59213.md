dysprosium:/dɪˈsproʊziəm/ | n. |镝|A soft silvery-white metallic element of the lanthanide series, used in lasers and nuclear reactors. It is difficult to separate from other rare earth elements.|Dysprosium is used in control rods in nuclear reactors. 镝用于核反应堆的控制棒。|Synonyms:  (As a lanthanide) erbium, holmium, thulium |Antonyms: None (Elements don't have antonyms)|Noun
