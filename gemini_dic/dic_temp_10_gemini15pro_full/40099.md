contaminate:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/
verb|污染，弄脏|To make something impure by adding a harmful substance.|The water supply was contaminated with bacteria. 供水被细菌污染了。|pollute, taint, adulterate|purify, clean, cleanse|v.
contamination:/kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃn/
noun|污染，污染物|The process of making something impure by adding a harmful substance, or the substance that causes this.|The contamination of the water supply caused a widespread outbreak of illness.  供水污染导致疾病的广泛爆发。|pollution, taint, adulteration|purification, cleaning, cleansing|n.
