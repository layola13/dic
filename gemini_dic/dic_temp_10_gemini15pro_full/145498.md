过于密切地
Examining something overclosely can sometimes obscure the bigger picture.  过度密切地检视事物有时会模糊全局。
He overclosely monitored his employees, creating a tense work environment. 他过于密切地监视他的员工，制造了紧张的工作氛围。
closely, excessively, meticulously, intently
distantly, casually, superficially
