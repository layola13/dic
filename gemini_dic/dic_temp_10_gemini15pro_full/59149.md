dynasty: /ˈdaɪnəsti/ (US) /ˈdɪnəsti/ (UK) | n. | 王朝；朝代 |A sequence of rulers from the same family, stock, or clan.  A powerful group or family that maintains its position for a considerable time.| The Ming dynasty ruled China for centuries. 明朝统治中国数百年。|  The Kennedy dynasty has had a profound impact on American politics. 肯尼迪家族对美国政治产生了深远的影响。 |近义词：house, lineage, reign |反义词: anarchy, disorder | 词性: noun
