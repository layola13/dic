gainfulness:/ˈɡeɪnfʊlnəs/
n.
获利性；有利可图；有益性
The quality of being profitable or beneficial.
The gainfulness of the new business venture was immediately apparent. 这项新商业投资的盈利能力立即显现出来。
profitability, advantageousness, usefulness
unprofitability, disadvantageousness

ETYMOLOGY->
From gainful + -ness.  Gainful from gain + -ful. Gain from Old North French gain, gaain (“profit, advantage”). Ultimately from Frankish *waidanjan (“to hunt, forage, pasture; gain by foraging”).
