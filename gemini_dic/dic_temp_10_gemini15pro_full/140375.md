oilest:/ˈɔɪliɪst/
adjective
油腻的；油污的
most oily; covered or saturated with oil.
The oilest fish I've ever eaten. 我吃过最油腻的鱼。
The mechanic's hands were the oilest I'd ever seen.  我见过的技工的手是最油腻的。
近义词:greasiest, fattiest
反义词:cleanest, driest
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From oily + -est (superlative suffix).  Oily itself comes from oil + -y (adjective suffix). Ultimately from Old French *oile (modern French huile), from Latin oleum.
