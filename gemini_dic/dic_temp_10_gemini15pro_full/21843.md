bodhisattva:/ˌboʊdiˈsʌtvə/ /ˌbɒdiˈsʌtvə/
n.
菩萨
a person who is able to reach nirvana but delays doing so out of compassion in order to save suffering beings.  A being who has produced bodhicitta.
例句：The bodhisattva vowed to help all beings achieve enlightenment.  菩萨发愿帮助一切众生觉悟。

例句：Statues of bodhisattvas are common in Buddhist temples.  菩萨像在佛教寺庙中很常见。

近义词：enlightened being, compassionate being
反义词：arhat (in some interpretations)
词性：noun
