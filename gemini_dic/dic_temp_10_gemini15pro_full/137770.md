nonvascular:/ˌnɑːnˈvæskjələr/
adjective
无维管束的，非维管的
Lacking vascular tissue, which in plants is specialized tissue for conducting water and nutrients.
Mosses are nonvascular plants.  苔藓是无维管束植物。
Nonvascular plants lack the xylem and phloem found in vascular plants.  无维管束植物缺乏维管束植物中存在的木质部和韧皮部。
synonym: avascular
antonym: vascular
词性: adjective
