huddlers:/ˈhʌdlərz/| n.| 挤在一起的人；拥抱者；蜷缩者|People who huddle together, often for warmth or comfort.  Those who huddle. |The huddlers around the fire tried to stay warm. 围在火堆旁挤在一起的人试图保持温暖。|
The penguins were huddlers against the biting wind.  企鹅挤在一起抵御刺骨的寒风。|近义词：crowd, group, throng, cluster|反义词: individuals, loners|词性:名词(plural)

Etymology:
From huddle (v.) + -er (agent suffix).  "Huddle" comes from the Middle Low German "huddeln," meaning "to crouch, to hurry."
