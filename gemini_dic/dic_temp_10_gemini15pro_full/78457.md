getter:/ˈɡɛtər/| n.|获取器，读取器|A method that retrieves the value of a private instance variable.|Example: The `getName()` method is a getter for the `name` variable. `getName()` 方法是 `name` 变量的获取器。|Synonym: accessor, reader|Antonym: setter|noun

getter:/ˈɡɛtər/| n.|获得者，得到者 (少用)|Someone who gets or obtains something.|Example: He was the getter of the prize. 他是奖品的获得者。|Synonym: obtainer, acquirer|Antonym: loser, giver|noun
