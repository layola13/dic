municipality:/mjuːˌnɪsɪˈpæləti/ | n. | 市，自治市；市政当局 | A city or town that has corporate status and local government.  A municipality is a political subdivision of a state within which a municipal corporation has been established to provide local government.  | The municipality provides services such as garbage collection and street cleaning.  该市政当局提供垃圾收集和街道清洁等服务。|  The municipality is responsible for maintaining the parks. 该市负责维护公园。 | 近义词: city, town, borough, district | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps unincorporated area) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *municipalite, from Middle French municipalité, from Medieval Latin municipālitās (“citizenship, municipality”), from Latin municipālis (“pertaining to a citizen”), from mūniceps (“citizen, townsman”), from mūnus (“duty, service, office, gift”) + -ceps (“taker, receiver”), from capere (“to take, seize”).
