exchangers:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒərz/| n.| 交换者；交换器|People who exchange something, or things that facilitate exchange.  Those who participate in an exchange of goods, services, or information. |The currency exchangers offered competitive rates. 货币兑换商提供了有竞争力的汇率。|Synonyms: traders, brokers, dealers, negotiators|Antonyms: hoarders, keepers|noun

exchangers:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒərz/| n.| 交换机；交换设备|Devices or systems that facilitate the exchange of heat, fluids, or data.  A device or system that transfers something from one place, system, or medium to another.|The heat exchangers in the power plant are crucial for its operation. 发电厂的热交换器对其运行至关重要。|Synonyms: converters, transformers, transfer devices|Antonyms: retainers, insulators|noun
