advanced: /ədˈvænst/ | adj. | 先进的，高级的，晚期的，年老的 |  Being beyond the elementary or introductory level; far or further along in development or progress. |  This is an advanced course in physics. 这是物理学的高级课程。

He has advanced ideas about education. 他对教育有先进的理念。 | 近义词：progressive, sophisticated, developed, higher, senior, latter | 反义词：basic, elementary, rudimentary, backward, junior, early | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avancer (to move forward, promote), from Late Latin *abante (from ab- (from) + ante (before)).  The meaning shifted from "placed ahead" to "improved, further developed" over time.
