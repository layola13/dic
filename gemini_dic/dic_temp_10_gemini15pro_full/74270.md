foy:/fɔɪ/ | n. | 忠诚，忠实，诚实 |  A pledge of faith or loyalty; faithfulness. | He swore his foy to the king. 他向国王宣誓效忠。 | 近义词: loyalty, fidelity, allegiance | 反义词: treachery, disloyalty, infidelity | 词性: 名词

词源: Middle English *fei, *fay, from Anglo-French fei, fei, from Latin fides "faith."
