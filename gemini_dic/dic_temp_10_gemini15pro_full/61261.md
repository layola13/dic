emancipist:/ɪˈmænsɪpɪst/ | n. |  被解放的人；（尤指）获得赦免的囚犯；解放论者 | A person, especially a convict, who has been emancipated.  In Australian history, it refers specifically to a convict who had been granted freedom. |  The emancipists formed a powerful political force in the early colony.  这些被解放的囚犯在早期殖民地构成了强大的政治力量。 | synonyms: freedman, liberated person, parolee (in some contexts) | antonyms: convict, prisoner, slave | noun

emancipist:/ɪˈmænsɪpɪst/ | adj. |  解放的；解放论者的 | Relating to emancipists or their cause. | The emancipist movement played a significant role in shaping Australian society. 解放运动在塑造澳大利亚社会方面发挥了重要作用。| synonyms: liberationist, freedom-seeking | antonyms: oppressive, enslaving | adjective
