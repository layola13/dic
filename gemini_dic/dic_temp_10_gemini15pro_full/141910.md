orders:/ˈɔːrdərz/ (US) /ˈɔːdəz/ (UK)| n. |命令，指示；订单；顺序；等级|A command given by a person in authority; requests to supply goods; a sequence or arrangement; a hierarchical classification.


例句：
The general gave orders to attack. 将军下令进攻。

He placed an order for a new computer. 他订购了一台新电脑。


近义词：commands, instructions, directives (for commands); requests, purchases (for orders); sequence, arrangement, series (for sequence); rank, hierarchy, class (for hierarchy)


反义词：disorder, chaos (for order); countermands (for commands); cancellations (for orders)


词性: noun


词源: From Old French "ordre" and ultimately from Latin "ordo" (meaning rank, row, series, arrangement).
