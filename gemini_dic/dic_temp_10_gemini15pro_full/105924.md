USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this award? = 他做了什么值得这个奖？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before making a decision? = 他们在做决定之前讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for by the time you graduate? = 到你毕业的时候，你将已经学习什么多久了？
What will they have been working on for the past five years? = 在过去的五年里，他们将一直在做什么？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

Interrogative Sentences:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She didn't say what she wanted. = 她没说她想要什么。
