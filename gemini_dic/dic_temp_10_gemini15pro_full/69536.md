fellifluous:/fɛˈlɪfluːəs/| adj.|流畅的，流利的|Characterized by smooth and fluent speech or writing, flowing effortlessly.|Her fellifluous prose captivated the audience. 她流畅的散文深深吸引了观众。|Example 2: The fellifluous speaker held the crowd's attention for hours.  这位口若悬河的演说家让观众聚精会神地听了几个小时。|近义词：eloquent, fluent, articulate, silver-tongued|反义词:halting, hesitant, inarticulate|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *fellifluus (“flowing with gall (bile)”), from fēl (“gall”) + fluō (“flow”). Originally, fellifluous had a negative connotation, referring to a bitter outpouring of words. However, over time, the word lost its negative connotation and came to mean simply "flowing."
