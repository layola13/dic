broil:/brɔɪl/| v. |烧烤，烤炙|To cook by exposing to direct radiant heat, as over a fire or under a heating element.|例句：I broiled the steak for five minutes on each side. 我把牛排两面各烤了五分钟。|例句：The chicken was broiled to perfection. 鸡肉烤得恰到好处。|近义词：grill, barbecue, roast|反义词：boil, simmer, stew|词性：动词
broiled:/brɔɪld/| adj. |烤的，炙烤的|Cooked by broiling.|例句：We enjoyed a delicious broiled fish for dinner. 我们晚餐享用了美味的烤鱼。|例句：The broiled vegetables were perfectly crisp. 烤蔬菜非常脆。|近义词：grilled, roasted|反义词：boiled, simmered, stewed|词性：形容词
