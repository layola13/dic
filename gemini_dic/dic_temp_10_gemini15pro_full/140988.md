onely:/ˈoʊnli/| adj.|孤独的，寂寞的|Feeling sad and alone because you are not with other people.|例句：She felt onely after her husband died. 她丈夫去世后感到很孤独。|近义词：lonely, solitary, alone|反义词：accompanied, together|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *onelī, *ōnelī, from Old English *ānlīċ, equivalent to one +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots anely (“only”). More at one, -ly.
