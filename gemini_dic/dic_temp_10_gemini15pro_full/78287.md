germicides: /ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈsaɪdz/ | n. | 消毒剂，杀菌剂 | Substances that kill germs (bacteria, viruses, and fungi). These substances are used to prevent the spread of infection and disease.  | Hospitals use strong germicides to sterilize surgical instruments. 医院使用强力杀菌剂对外科器械进行消毒。| synonyms: disinfectants, antiseptics, bactericides, fungicides, virucides | antonyms:  contaminants, pollutants | noun
