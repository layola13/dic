habitableness:/ˈhæbɪtəblnəs/
n.
适居性；可居住性
The habitableness of a planet depends on several factors, including the presence of liquid water.
一个行星的适居性取决于几个因素，包括液态水的存在。
habitability, livability
uninhabitableness, inhospitability
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From habitable + -ness.  Habitable from Late Middle English: from Old French habitable, from habiter ‘inhabit’, from Latin habitare, frequentative of habere ‘to have’.
