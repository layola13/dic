chuckling:/ˈtʃʌklɪŋ/| n. vi.|轻声笑，咯咯笑|The act of laughing quietly or softly.|He was chuckling to himself as he read the comic strip. 他读漫画时自己轻声笑着。|近义词：giggling, snickering, laughing softly|反义词：crying, sobbing|词性: noun (as a gerund), verb (present participle)
Etymology: From the verb "chuckle," which likely originated as an imitative word, echoing the sound of soft laughter.
