aggregately:/ˈæɡrɪɡɪtli/
adverb|合计地，总共地|In a manner that combines all parts or elements into a single whole.|例句：The company's profits, aggregately, exceeded expectations. 公司的利润总计超过了预期。|例句：Aggregately, the small donations added up to a significant sum.  零星的捐款合计起来成了一笔可观的金额。|近义词：collectively, totally, overall, combined|反义词：individually, separately, singly|词性: adverb
词源: From Latin *aggregatus*, past participle of *aggregare* "to add to, attach," from *ad-* "to" + *gregare* "to herd, flock," from *grex* "flock, herd."
