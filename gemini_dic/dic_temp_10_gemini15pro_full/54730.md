disparagement:/dɪˈspærɪdʒmənt/
|n.|贬低，轻蔑，诋毁|The act of speaking about someone or something in a way that shows you do not respect or value them. |His disparagement of his opponent was seen as unprofessional. 他对对手的贬低被认为是不专业的。|Synonyms: belittlement, denigration, depreciation, derogation, detraction |Antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, applause|noun

disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/
|v.|贬低，轻蔑，诋毁|To regard or represent as being of little worth.|He never missed an opportunity to disparage his competitors. 他从不错过任何一个贬低竞争对手的机会。|Synonyms: belittle, denigrate, depreciate, derogate, detract from|Antonyms: praise, commend, acclaim, applaud|verb
