frostless:/ˈfrɔːstləs/| adj.|无霜的，耐霜的|Not affected by frost; protecting against frost.  Free from frost; not likely to experience frost. |例句：Frostless refrigerators are very convenient. 无霜冰箱非常方便。

例句：They live in a frostless climate. 他们生活在一个无霜的气候中。|近义词：frost-free, non-freezing|反义词: frosty, freezing|词性:形容词

词源: frost (霜) + -less (无，没有)
