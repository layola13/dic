biophotophone:/ˌbaɪoʊˈfoʊtəfoʊn/
noun
生物光电话
An instrument for transmitting speech by means of a beam of light, the intensity of which is modulated by the voice acting upon a diaphragm reflector.  It converts sound into light signals for transmission.
The biophotophone allowed for communication over long distances without wires. 生物光电话使得远距离无线通信成为可能。
The team experimented with a biophotophone to send messages across the valley.  团队试验用生物光电话跨越山谷发送信息。
synonyms:photophone, light telephone
antonyms: N/A
词性:n.
