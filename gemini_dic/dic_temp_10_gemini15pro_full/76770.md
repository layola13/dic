gamesmanship:/ˈɡeɪmz.mən.ʃɪp/
noun
策略，手腕，比赛技巧
The use of dubious methods, that are not strictly illegal, to gain an advantage.
He won the election more through gamesmanship than genuine popularity. 他赢得选举更多的是靠策略而不是真正的声望。

近义词:  trickery, cunning,  craftiness,  deviousness, strategy
反义词: sportsmanship, fairness
词性: noun
