nonsustainabilities:/ˌnɒnsəˌsteɪnəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可持续性（复数） |  The lack of qualities that allow something to continue at the same rate or level without causing damage to the environment or depleting resources.  It refers to practices, systems, or processes that cannot be maintained indefinitely due to their negative impact on environmental, social, or economic factors. | The report highlighted the nonsustainabilities of current farming practices.  这份报告强调了当前农业实践的不可持续性。| The conference focused on the economic nonsustainabilities of relying solely on fossil fuels.  这次会议的重点是仅仅依靠化石燃料的经济不可持续性。 |  unsustainabilities | sustainability,  viability | noun
