displace:/dɪsˈpleɪs/| v.|排出（液体，气体等）|To take the place of (a liquid, gas, etc.).|The ship displaces 10,000 tons of water. 这艘船排水量为1万吨。|近义词：replace, supplant|反义词:N/A|词性:动词
