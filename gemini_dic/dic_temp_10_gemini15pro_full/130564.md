nonactionable:/ˌnɒnˈækʃənəbl/
adjective
不可控告的，无法起诉的
Not giving sufficient reason to take legal action.
The lawyer determined that the complaint was nonactionable.  律师认定该投诉不具备可诉性。
His comments, while offensive, were nonactionable. 他的评论虽然令人不快，但不足以构成诉讼理由。
近义词:unactionable, non-suable
反义词:actionable, suable
词性:adjective
