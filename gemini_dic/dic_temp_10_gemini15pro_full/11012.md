arborization:/ˌɑːrbəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
树枝状结构，分枝，树状化
The branching of nerves or blood vessels resembles the arborization of a tree.  神经或血管的分支类似于树的树枝状结构。
The arborization of the dendrites allows the neuron to receive signals from many other neurons.  树突的树枝状结构使神经元能够接收来自许多其他神经元的信号。
ramification, branching, dendritic
n/a
noun
