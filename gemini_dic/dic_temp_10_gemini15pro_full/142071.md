orgiastically:/ˌɔːrdʒiˈæstɪkli/ | adv. | 狂欢地；放纵地 | In a wild, unrestrained, and intensely emotional manner, often suggesting excessive indulgence or frenzy, like a wild celebration or orgy. | They danced orgiastically through the night. 他们整夜狂欢地跳舞。 | The crowd cheered orgiastically as the band played their hit song. 当乐队演奏他们的热门歌曲时，人群狂热地欢呼。 | 近义词：wildly, frenziedly, ecstatically, exuberantly | 反义词：calmly, restrainedly, soberly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Ancient Greek *ὄργια (órgia, “secret rites, secret worship”), plural of *ὄργιον (órgion, “secret rite”).
