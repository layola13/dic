nonimperiousnesses:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəsnɪsɪz/
noun
非专横; 不傲慢; 温和
lack of imperiousness; the quality of not being domineering or arrogant.
His nonimperiousness made him a beloved leader.  他的不专横使他成为一位受人爱戴的领导者。
Synonyms: humility, modesty, unassumingness, meekness
Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, imperiousness, domineeringness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + imperious (adjective meaning "domineering or overbearing") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality)
