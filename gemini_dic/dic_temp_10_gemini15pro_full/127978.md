neothalamus: /ˌniːoʊˈθæləmʌs/ | n. | 新丘脑 |  The neothalamus is a part of the thalamus, a structure in the brain that relays sensory and motor signals to the cerebral cortex. It is involved in higher-order cognitive functions. |  The neothalamus plays a crucial role in processing information from the senses. 新丘脑在处理感官信息方面起着至关重要的作用。

The neothalamus is interconnected with the cerebral cortex. 新丘脑与大脑皮层相互连接。 | 近义词: dorsal thalamus | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider paleothalamus as a contrasting structure) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neo- + thalamus.  "Neo-" is a combining form meaning "new" or "recent," derived from Greek *néos*.  "Thalamus" comes from the Greek *thálamos*, meaning "inner chamber" or "bridal bed."
