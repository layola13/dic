geocentric:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|地心的，以地球为中心的|Relating to or measured from the earth's center; having or representing the earth as the center, as of the solar system.|例句：The ancient Greeks believed in a geocentric model of the universe. 古希腊人相信宇宙是以地球为中心的模型。

例句：Geocentric coordinates are used to specify locations on Earth. 地心坐标用于指定地球上的位置。|近义词：earth-centered|反义词:heliocentric|词性:形容词
