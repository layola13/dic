murmurs:/ˈmɜːrmərz/ (US) /ˈmɜːməz/ (UK)| n.|低语声，喃喃声；抱怨声|a low, continuous, indistinct sound; a softly spoken complaint|The murmurs of the crowd grew louder as the speaker continued. 观众的低语声随着演讲者的继续而越来越大。|近义词：whispers, mutterings, grumbles, complaints|反义词：shouts, yells, screams|noun

murmur:/ˈmɜːrmər/ (US) /ˈmɜːmə(r)/ (UK)| v.|低语，喃喃自语；抱怨|to speak in a low, continuous, indistinct voice; to complain softly|He murmured an apology. 他低声 murmured一声道歉。|近义词：whisper, mutter, grumble, complain|反义词：shout, yell, scream|verb
