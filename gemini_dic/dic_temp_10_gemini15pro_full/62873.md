enlarged:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒd/
adjective
放大的，扩大的
Made larger or wider.
The doctor examined the enlarged lymph nodes. 医生检查了肿大的淋巴结。
近义词:expanded, increased, magnified, amplified
反义词:reduced, shrunk, diminished, decreased
词性:adjective

enlarge:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒ/
verb
扩大，放大
Make or become larger or wider.
They are planning to enlarge their house. 他们正计划扩建他们的房子。
近义词:expand, increase, magnify, amplify
反义词:reduce, shrink, diminish, decrease
词性:verb
