allot:/əˈlɒt/ (US) /əˈlɑːt/ (UK)| v. |分配，拨给|To give something to someone as a share or task.|例句：The teacher allotted each student a different task. 老师给每个学生分配了不同的任务。|例句：They allotted a small portion of the budget to marketing. 他们把预算的一小部分分配给了市场营销。|近义词：allocate, assign, distribute, apportion|反义词：withhold, keep, retain|词性：动词
