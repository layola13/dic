hyperaggregate:/ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡeɪt/ | v.  n. |过度聚集，超聚集|To aggregate excessively; to gather into an abnormally large mass or clump.  As a noun, refers to the resulting mass or clump.|
例句：Platelets can hyperaggregate in certain disease states. 血小板在某些疾病状态下会过度聚集。

例句：The hyperaggregate of cells formed a blockage in the artery.  细胞的超聚集在动脉中形成了阻塞。|
近义词：clump, agglomerate, aggregate, cluster|反义词：disperse, separate, disaggregate|词性: verb, noun
