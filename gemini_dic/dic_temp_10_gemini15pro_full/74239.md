fox:/fɑːks/ (US) /fɒks/ (UK)| n.|狐狸|A carnivorous mammal of the dog family with a pointed muzzle and a long bushy tail, proverbial for its cunning.|The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.  这只敏捷的棕色狐狸跳过了懒惰的狗。|Synonyms: vixen (female fox), reynard, |Antonyms: None (animals don't typically have true antonyms)|Noun

fox:/fɑːks/ (US) /fɒks/ (UK)| v.|使迷惑，欺骗|To deceive or trick someone.|He foxed his opponents with a clever move. 他用一个聪明的举动迷惑了他的对手。|Synonyms: trick, deceive, outsmart, outwit|Antonyms: be honest, be truthful|Verb
