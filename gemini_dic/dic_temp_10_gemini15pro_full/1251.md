accomptant: /əˈkaʊntənt/ | n. |会计；会计师| A person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts. |  The accomptant prepared the company's annual report.  会计准备了公司的年度报告。

The accomptant helped me file my taxes. 会计师帮我报了税。| 近义词: accountant, bookkeeper | 反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a profession) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French acomptant, present participle of acompter “to count, compute,” from a- “to” + compter “to count,” from Latin computare "to reckon, count."
