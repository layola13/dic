nonvenousness:/nɒnˈviːnəsnəs/
n.
无毒性；非静脉注射
absence of venomousness; not administered intravenously.
The nonvenousness of the snake bite was a relief.  这条蛇咬伤没有毒性，真是让人松了一口气。
The doctor emphasized the nonvenousness of the medication, assuring the patient it wouldn't be administered through an IV. 医生强调了这种药物的非静脉注射性，向病人保证不会通过静脉注射。
近义词:harmlessness, innocuousness
反义词:venomousness, toxicity
词性:noun

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + venous (relating to veins) + -ness (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality)
