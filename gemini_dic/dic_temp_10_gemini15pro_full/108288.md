laurate:/ˈlɔːreɪt/ /ˈlɒrˌeɪt/| n. adj.|月桂酸盐；月桂酸酯；获奖者|A salt or ester of lauric acid.  Also, a person who has been honored with an award, especially a prestigious one.|The soap contains sodium laurate. 这块肥皂含有月桂酸钠。|近义词: dodecanoate (for the chemical sense), winner, recipient (for the award sense)|反义词: (No direct antonym.  For the award sense, perhaps "loser" but this is highly context-dependent.)|noun, adjective
