incestuousness:/ɪnˈsɛstʃuəsnəs/| n. |乱伦，血亲相奸|The state or quality of being incestuous; sexual relations between people classed as being too closely related to marry each other. |The incestuousness of their relationship shocked the community. 他们的乱伦关系震惊了整个社区。|近义词：incest, inbreeding|反义词: chastity, purity|词性: noun

incestuous:/ɪnˈsɛstʃuəs/| adj. |乱伦的，近亲相奸的|Involving or guilty of incest; sexual relations between people classed as being too closely related to marry each other.  |Their incestuous affair was a closely guarded secret. 他们的乱伦关系是一个严守的秘密。|近义词：forbidden, taboo|反义词: proper, acceptable|词性: adjective
