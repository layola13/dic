exogenism: /ɛkˈsɒdʒənɪzəm/ | n. |外成论，外生论|The theory that something originates from outside a system, especially a linguistic form or cultural practice borrowed from another group.|The presence of exogenisms in the language reflects the culture's history of contact with other groups. 语言中外来语的存在反映了该文化与其他群体接触的历史。|近义词: borrowing, loanword |反义词: endogenism |词性: noun
