parcelled:/ˈpɑːsəld/ (UK), /ˈpɑːrsəld/ (US)| v. |打包，包裹；分配，分派|Divided into or consisting of parcels or parts.  The land was parcelled out among the settlers. 这块土地被分给了定居者们。|例句：The inheritance was parcelled out among the relatives. 遗产被分配给了亲戚们。

例句：She carefully parcelled up the gifts. 她仔细地把礼物包好。|近义词：packaged, wrapped, distributed, apportioned|反义词：combined, amalgamated|词性：动词

詞源->

From Middle English *parcellen, from Old French *parceler, from Late Latin *particellare, frequentative of Latin particula (“particle”).
