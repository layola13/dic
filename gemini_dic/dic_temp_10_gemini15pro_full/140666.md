omelette:/ˈɑːmlɪt/ (US), /ˈɒmlət/ (UK)| n. |煎蛋卷，蛋饼|A dish made from beaten eggs fried with butter or oil in a frying pan, often with other ingredients such as cheese, vegetables, or meat added.|例句：I had a delicious cheese omelette for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个美味的奶酪煎蛋卷。

例句：She made an omelette with onions, peppers, and mushrooms. 她做了一个洋葱、辣椒和蘑菇煎蛋卷。


近义词：fritatta|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
