breeze-in:/briːz ˈɪn/
verb phrase
轻松获胜，毫不费力地赢得
to win easily or succeed without effort
He breezed in to the finals without dropping a set. 他毫不费力地进入了决赛，一盘未输。
breeze in to the meeting fifteen minutes late.  他轻松地迟到了十五分钟才到会。
近义词:walk over, coast, sail through
反义词:struggle, toil
词性:动词短语
