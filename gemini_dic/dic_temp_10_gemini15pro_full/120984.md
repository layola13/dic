miscibility:/ˌmɪsɪˈbɪləti/| n. |混溶性，互溶性|The miscibility of two liquids depends on their molecular structures. 两种液体的混溶性取决于它们的分子结构。|例句：The miscibility of water and oil is very low. 水和油的混溶性很低。

The miscibility of ethanol and water is very high.  乙醇和水的混溶性很高。|近义词：compatibility, blendability|反义词：immiscibility|noun

词源: From Latin miscēre (to mix) + -ibility (suffix forming abstract nouns denoting quality or state).
