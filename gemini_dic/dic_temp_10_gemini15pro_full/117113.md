mealy:/ˈmiːli/| adj.|粉状的；粗糙的；（果肉）干面状的|Covered with meal; powdery; dry and crumbly.|The apples were dry and mealy. 这些苹果干巴巴的，像面粉一样。|近义词：powdery, floury, dry, crumbly|反义词:moist, juicy|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *melie, from Old English *mealu, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *melwą (“meal, flour”), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, mill”). Cognate with Scots melie (“mealy”), West Frisian melich (“mealy”), Dutch melig (“mealy”), German mehlig (“mealy”), Danish melet (“floury”), Swedish mjölig (“mealy”).
