motiv:/ˈmoʊtɪv/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪv/ (UK)| n. |主题;动机;主旨;动因|A recurrent thematic element in an artistic or literary work.  A dominant or recurring idea in an artistic work.  Something (such as a need or desire) that causes a person to act.|The main motiv of the novel is the search for identity. 这部小说的主题是寻找身份认同。|Synonym: theme, subject, topic, motif, leitmotif |Antonym: effect, result, outcome|noun
