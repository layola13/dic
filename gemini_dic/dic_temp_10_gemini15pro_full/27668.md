candids:/ˈkændɪdz/| n.|抓拍照，偷拍照；坦率的话|Photographs of people taken informally, especially without the subject's knowledge.  Also, frank and honest statements or opinions.|例句：The photographer captured some wonderful candids of the wedding guests. 摄影师捕捉到了一些婚礼宾客精彩的抓拍照。

例句：He shared some surprising candids about his struggles with depression. 他坦率地分享了一些他与抑郁症斗争的经历。|近义词：snapshots, informal photos, frank remarks|反义词:posed photos, formal portraits, deceptive statements|词性:名词
