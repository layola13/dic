animative:/ˈænɪmətɪv/
adjective
赋予生命的，有生气的
Having the power to give life; capable of animating.
The animative power of nature is evident in the spring. 大自然的生命力在春天显而易见。
近义词:  life-giving, vitalizing, invigorating, quickening
反义词: inanimate, lifeless, dead
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *animativus, from Latin animatus (past participle of animare “to endow with breath, give life to”).
