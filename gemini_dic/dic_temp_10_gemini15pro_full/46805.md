词源:
From Middle English *dank, from Old Norse døkkr (“dark”), from Proto-Germanic *dunkwaz (“dark”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰengʷ- (“dark, hidden”).  The slang meaning evolved from its association with high-quality marijuana, which is often kept in damp, dark places to preserve its potency.
