eutexia:/juːˈtɛksiə/| n.|共熔|The lowest melting point of an alloy or solution of two or more substances.|The eutexia of this alloy is reached at 700 degrees Celsius.  这种合金的共熔点达到700摄氏度。|近义词：eutectic point|反义词:|名词

eutectic:/juːˈtɛktɪk/| adj.|共熔的|Relating to or denoting a mixture of substances (usually metals) that melts and solidifies at a single temperature that is lower than the melting points of the separate constituents or of any other mixture of them.|This alloy has a eutectic composition. 这种合金具有共熔成分。|近义词:|反义词:|形容词
