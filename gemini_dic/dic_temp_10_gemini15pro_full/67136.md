exporting:/ɪkˈspɔːtɪŋ/ | n. | 出口; 输出 | The act of sending goods or services to another country for sale. |  The country's economy relies heavily on exporting agricultural products.  这个国家的经济严重依赖农产品出口。 | synonyms: shipping, selling abroad | antonyms: importing, buying from abroad | noun

exporting:/ɪkˈspɔːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 出口的 | Relating to the act of sending goods or services to another country for sale. | The exporting company faced new tariffs. 这家出口公司面临新的关税。 | synonyms: outward-bound, outbound | antonyms: importing, inbound | adjective
