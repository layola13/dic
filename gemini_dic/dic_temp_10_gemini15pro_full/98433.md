inspector:/ɪnˈspektər/ (US) /ɪnˈspɛktə(r)/ (UK)| n.|检查员，巡视员|A person who examines something officially, especially to make sure that regulations are being obeyed. |The inspector checked the factory for safety violations. 检查员检查了工厂的安全违规情况。|Synonyms: examiner, auditor, surveyor, overseer |Antonyms:  None (lack of inspection doesn't have a single word antonym) |noun


inspector:/ɪnˈspektər/ (US) /ɪnˈspɛktə(r)/ (UK)| n.|督察，警官|A police officer of middle rank. |The inspector questioned the suspect. 督察讯问了嫌疑人。|Synonyms: detective, officer |Antonyms: None |noun
