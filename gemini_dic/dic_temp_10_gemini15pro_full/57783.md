dreary:/ˈdrɪəri/ (US), /ˈdrɪəri/ (UK)| adj.| 沉闷的，阴沉的，凄凉的|Causing a feeling of sadness or gloom; depressing and bleak.  Characterized by dullness and gloom.|例句：The dreary weather made me feel melancholic. 阴沉的天气让我感到忧郁。

例句：The long, dreary winter seemed to stretch on forever. 漫长而沉闷的冬天似乎永远持续下去。|近义词：gloomy, bleak, dismal, dull, somber, depressing|反义词：bright, cheerful, sunny, lively, vibrant|词性:adjective

词源:From Middle English *dreri, from Old English *drēorig (“sad, mournful, bloody, gory”), from Proto-Germanic *drauziją (“mournful, troubled”). Cognate with Old Saxon drōrag (“sad, miserable”), Old High German trūrag (“sad, miserable”), Old Norse dreyrigr (“bloody”). Related to Old English drēor (“blood, gore”).
