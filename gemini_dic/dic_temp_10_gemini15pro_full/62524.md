endurance:/ɪnˈdjʊərəns/
n.
耐力，忍耐力，持久力
The ability to withstand hardship, adversity, or stress for a prolonged period.
He showed remarkable endurance throughout the race. 他在整个比赛中表现出了非凡的耐力。
近义词: stamina, resilience, fortitude, perseverance
反义词: weakness, fragility
词性: 名词(单数)


词源:  From Old French *endurance (Modern French endurance), from endurer “to endure,” from Latin indurare “to harden, make hard, toughen,” from in- “in” + durare “to harden, last, continue,” from durus “hard.”
