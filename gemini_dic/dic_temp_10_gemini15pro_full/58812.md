duong:/ˈdʒʊəŋ/ | n. |  路, 街道 (Vietnamese) | A Vietnamese word meaning "road" or "street". It's often used in place names. | Example:  Nguyen Hue Duong is a famous street in Ho Chi Minh City.  阮惠街是胡志明市一条著名的街道。| Synonym: street, road, avenue, way (in English) | Antonym:  (No direct English antonym; perhaps "path" or "trail" if contrasting a major thoroughfare with a smaller route) | Noun
