ashlar: /ˈæʃlər/ (US), /ˈæʃlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|琢石；方石|A finely dressed (squared, smooth) building stone, typically rectangular cuboid.|The walls were built of solid ashlar.|Synonyms: facing stone, dressed stone, block, hewn stone|Antonyms: rubble, rough stone|noun

ashlar: /ˈæʃlər/ (US), /ˈæʃlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|方石的；琢石的|Made of or resembling ashlar.|The ashlar façade of the building was impressive.|Synonyms: squared, dressed, hewn|Antonyms: rough, unhewn, rubble|adjective
