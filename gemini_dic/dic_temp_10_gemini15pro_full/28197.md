词源:
From French capricieux, from Italian capriccioso, from capriccio "sudden start, whim," of uncertain origin. Perhaps from capo "head" + riccio "hedgehog," hence "head like a hedgehog" (i.e., bristling up suddenly), or perhaps from capra "goat" + riccio "frisking, quivering."
