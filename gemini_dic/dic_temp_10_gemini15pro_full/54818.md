dispersement:/dɪˈspɜːsmənt/ | n. | 分散，散布，支付 | The dispersement of seeds by the wind is essential for plant reproduction.  风力传播种子对植物繁殖至关重要。 | Example: The dispersement of the crowd after the concert was orderly.  音乐会后人群的散开是有序的。 | Example: The government oversaw the dispersement of funds to the affected areas. 政府监督向受灾地区拨款。 | Synonyms: dispersal, distribution, scattering, dissemination, payment | Antonyms: collection, gathering, concentration, accumulation | noun

disbursement:/dɪsˈbɜːsmənt/ | n. | 支付，支出 | The disbursement of funds was delayed due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，资金支付被推迟了。 | Example: The company's annual disbursement for research and development has increased. 公司每年的研发支出有所增加。 | Example:  Accurate record-keeping is essential for tracking disbursements.  准确的记录对于跟踪支出至关重要。| Synonyms: expenditure, outlay, payment, expense | Antonyms: income, receipt, revenue | noun
