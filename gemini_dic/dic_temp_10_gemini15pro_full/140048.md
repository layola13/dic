oenophile:/ˈiːnəˌfaɪl/| n.|葡萄酒爱好者；品酒行家|A connoisseur of wines.|He's a true oenophile, with a cellar full of vintage bottles. 他是一位真正的葡萄酒爱好者，酒窖里满是年份酒。|近义词：wine lover, wine connoisseur, vinophile|反义词：teetotaler, abstainer|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *oinos* (wine) + *-philos* (loving).
