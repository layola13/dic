melaninlike:/ˈmɛlənɪnˌlaɪk/
adjective
类似黑色素的，像黑色素的
Resembling melanin in appearance, properties, or function.  A substance that is melaninlike might have a similar dark color, absorb UV light, or play a role in pigmentation.
The researchers discovered a melaninlike pigment in the feathers of the bird.  研究人员在鸟的羽毛中发现了一种类似黑色素的色素。
The compound exhibited melaninlike properties in its absorption of ultraviolet light.  该化合物在吸收紫外线方面表现出类似黑色素的特性。
