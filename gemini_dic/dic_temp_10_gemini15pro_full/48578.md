defeatable:/dɪˈfiːtəbl/| adj.|可战胜的，可击败的|Capable of being defeated or overcome.|例句：The champion seemed unbeatable, but he proved defeatable in the final match.  这位冠军看起来不可战胜，但他在决赛中被证明是可以击败的。

例句：With the right strategy, any army is defeatable.  有了正确的策略，任何军队都是可以战胜的。|近义词：vulnerable, conquerable, beatable|反义词：invincible, unbeatable, unconquerable|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From defeat (v.) + -able.  "defeat" 源自古法语 "defete"，意为 "undoing, destruction"，最终源自拉丁语 "disfacere"，意为 "undo, destroy".  "-able" 是一个后缀，表示 "capable of being".
