chap:/tʃæp/| n. |小伙子，家伙; (尤指)男人; 皲裂，裂口|a man or boy|例句：He's a friendly chap. 他是个友好的家伙。

例句：The cold weather chapped my lips.  寒冷的天气让我的嘴唇皲裂了。|近义词：fellow, guy, bloke (for a man); crack (for skin)|反义词:woman, girl (for chap as a person); heal (for chap as a crack)|词性:名词

chap:/tʃæp/| v. |(使)皲裂，(使)裂开|to become or make rough and sore, especially because of cold weather|例句：My skin chaps easily in the winter. 我的皮肤在冬天很容易皲裂。

例句：The wind chapped his face. 风吹裂了他的脸。|近义词：crack, split, roughen|反义词:heal, soothe|词性:动词
