galeas:/ˈɡæliəs/ | n. | 大型桨帆船；盖利斯帆船 | A large galley or low-decked warship, common in the Mediterranean from the 16th to the 18th century, propelled by both oars and sails. | The galeas, with its mixed propulsion of oars and sails, was a formidable warship in its time.  盖利斯帆船，依靠桨和帆的混合推进力，在当时是一艘强大的战舰。

The Venetian galeas was particularly renowned for its size and firepower. 威尼斯盖利斯帆船因其尺寸和火力而闻名。 | 近义词: galley, warship | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps merchant ship or transport ship could be considered loosely antonymous.) | 词性: noun
