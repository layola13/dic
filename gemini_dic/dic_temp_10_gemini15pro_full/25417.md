bulimia: /buˈlɪmiə/ (US), /bjuːˈlɪmiə/ (UK)| n. |贪食症，暴食症|An eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating, followed by compensatory behaviors such as self-induced vomiting, excessive exercise, or the use of laxatives to prevent weight gain.|She struggled with bulimia for years before seeking help. 她与贪食症抗争多年才寻求帮助。|Synonyms: binge eating disorder, hyperphagia|Antonyms: anorexia|Noun

bulimia nervosa: /buˈlɪmiə nɜːrˈvəʊsə/ (UK) /buˈlɪmiə nɜːrˈvoʊsə/ (US) |n.|神经性贪食症|A specific type of bulimia characterized by recurrent episodes of binge eating followed by inappropriate compensatory behaviors to prevent weight gain.|Bulimia nervosa can have serious health consequences. 神经性贪食症会对健康造成严重后果。|Synonyms: bulimia|Antonyms: anorexia nervosa|Noun
