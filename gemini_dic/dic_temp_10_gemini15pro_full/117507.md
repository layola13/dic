meeker:/ˈmiːkər/| adj.|更温顺的，更谦恭的|More meek; more humble and quiet. |例句：He was meeker after his failure.  他失败后变得更谦逊了。

例句：She has become meeker with age.  随着年龄的增长，她变得更加温和了。|近义词：humbler, gentler, milder, quieter|反义词：bolder, more assertive, more aggressive|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *meker, comparative of meek, from Old Norse *mīkr, from Proto-Germanic *mīkaz (“small, slender, weak”). Cognate with Danish meek (“meek”), Swedish mjuk (“soft”). Doublet of micro-.
