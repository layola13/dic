ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inflammabilitas, from Latin inflammabilis (inflammable) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Inflammable comes from Latin inflammare (to set on fire), from in- (in, into) + flamma (flame).
