cysticule: /sɪˈstɪkjuːl/
noun
囊泡；小囊；（尤指水生动物的）皮囊
A small cyst or cyst-like sac, especially in aquatic animals.
The cysticule of the parasite was examined under a microscope.  寄生虫的囊泡在显微镜下被检查。
The fish's skin was covered in tiny cysticules.  鱼的皮肤上覆盖着微小的囊泡。
Synonyms: vesicle, saccule
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cysticula, diminutive of cystis (“bladder, bag”), from Ancient Greek κύστις (kústis, “bladder, pouch”).
