grownness:/ˈɡroʊnnɪs/
n.
成年；成熟；长大的状态
The state of being grown or adult.
His grownness surprised me; he seemed so much younger just last year. 他的成熟让我惊讶，去年他还显得那么年轻。
maturity, adulthood, development
childishness, immaturity
noun
