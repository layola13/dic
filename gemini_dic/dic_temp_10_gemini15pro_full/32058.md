cherry-picks:/ˈtʃɛri pɪks/ | v. | 精心挑选；择优挑选 | selects the best or most desirable items from a larger quantity, often ignoring the less desirable ones. | He cherry-picked the best strawberries from the basket. 他从篮子里精心挑选了最好的草莓。| The company cherry-picked the most qualified candidates from a large pool of applicants.  公司从大量的申请者中挑选出最合格的候选人。 |  selects, culls, handpicks | rejects, overlooks, ignores | verb

cherry-picks:/ˈtʃɛri pɪks/ | n. | 精心挑选出的东西；择优挑选的行为 | the act of selecting the best or most desirable items. | These examples are cherry-picks and don't represent the whole picture. 这些例子是精心挑选的，并不能代表全部情况。|  The report was accused of being a cherry-pick of positive data.  该报告被指责为只挑选了有利的数据。 | selection, choice, best | whole, entirety, all | noun
