afore:/əˈfɔːr/| prep. adv. conj.|在…之前；以前；|Before.  It happened afore I knew it. 它发生在我意识到之前。|近义词：before, previously, earlier|反义词:after, afterward, later|词性:介词, 副词, 连词

Etymology->
From Middle English *aforen, from Old English *onforan (attested as on foran, with the preposition on sometimes treated as a prefix to foran), from on (“on, onto, in, at”) + foran (“before, in front”). Cognate with Dutch van voren (“from the front”), German von vorn (“from the front”).
