colloquy: /ˈkɒləkwi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləkwi/ (US)
n.
对话，谈话；会议
a formal discussion or conversation
例句：The professors engaged in a colloquy about the new curriculum.  教授们就新课程进行了讨论。

例句：The peace colloquy lasted for several days. 和平会谈持续了数天。

近义词：conversation, discussion, dialogue, conference
反义词：silence, monologue, argument
词性：名词
