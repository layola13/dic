chrysaline:/ˈkrɪsəlaɪn/| adj.|似蛹的，金黄色的，透明的|Resembling a chrysalis; having a pale yellow or golden color; translucent.|The butterfly emerged from its chrysaline shell. 蝴蝶从它的蛹壳中出现。|近义词：aureate, golden, translucent|反义词:opaque, dark|adjective


chrysaline:/ˈkrɪsəlaɪn/| n.|蛹|The pupa stage of certain insects, especially butterflies.|The chrysaline hung from a branch, shimmering in the sunlight.  蛹挂在树枝上，在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：pupa, cocoon|反义词:larva, imago|noun
