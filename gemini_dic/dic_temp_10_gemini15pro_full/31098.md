championing:/ˈtʃæmpiənɪŋ/
verb
支持；拥护；捍卫
actively supporting or defending a cause, person, or principle.
例句：She is championing the rights of the disabled. 她正在捍卫残疾人的权利。

例句：He has been championing educational reform for many years. 他多年来一直倡导教育改革。

近义词：supporting, advocating, defending, promoting, espousing
反义词：opposing, attacking, hindering, undermining
词性: v.


championing:/ˈtʃæmpiənɪŋ/
gerund or present participle
支持；拥护；捍卫

例句：Her championing of environmental issues has made her a popular figure. 她对环境问题的拥护使她成为一个受欢迎的人物。

近义词：support, advocacy, defense, promotion
反义词：opposition, attack
词性: n.
