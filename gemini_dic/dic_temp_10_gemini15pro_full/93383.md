illiberalness:/ɪˈlɪbərəlnəs/ | n. |缺乏开明; 狭隘; 不宽容|The state of being illiberal; narrow-mindedness or intolerance. |His illiberalness prevented him from accepting new ideas. 他的狭隘使他无法接受新思想。|The illiberalness of the regime was evident in its suppression of dissent. 该政权的不宽容体现在其压制异议上。|近义词：intolerance, narrow-mindedness, bigotry, prejudice|反义词：tolerance, open-mindedness, liberality|词性: 名词

Etymology: From illiberal + -ness.  "illiberal" comes from  "in-" (not) + "liberal" (from Latin "liberalis" meaning "of freedom, pertaining to a freeman"). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or condition.
