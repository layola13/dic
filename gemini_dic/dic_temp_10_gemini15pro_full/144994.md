overactively:/ˌoʊvərˈæktɪvli/
adverb|过度活跃地|in a way that is too active|例句：The child was overactively bouncing off the walls.  这孩子过度活跃地在墙上弹来弹去。|例句：Her imagination was working overactively, conjuring up all sorts of scenarios. 她的想象力过度活跃，编造出各种各样的场景。|近义词：excessively, hyperactively, frenetically|反义词：inactively, passively, sluggishly|词性：副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "active" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
