orthology:/ɔːrˈθɒlədʒi/ /ɔːrˈθɑːlədʒi/
n.
直系同源性
the study of homologous genes that have evolved by direct vertical descent from a single ancestral gene in a last common ancestor
The researchers used orthology analysis to determine the evolutionary relationships between the genes. 研究人员使用直系同源分析来确定基因之间的进化关系。
homologous genes, synteny
paralogy
noun
