archus: /ˈɑːrkəs/ | n. | 统治者，酋长 | A ruler or chief, especially in ancient Greece.  Derived from the Greek *arkhos*, meaning "leader" or "ruler".|  The archus ruled the small city-state with an iron fist.  这位统治者用铁腕统治着这个小城邦。 | synonyms: ruler, leader, chief, sovereign, governor | antonyms: subject, follower, subordinate | noun
