diagram:/ˈdaɪəɡræm/ | n. | 图表，示意图 | A simplified drawing showing the components of something or the stages of a process. |  The teacher drew a diagram of the human heart on the board. 老师在黑板上画了一幅人体心脏的示意图。

The diagram illustrates the flow of information within the organization. 这张图表说明了组织内部的信息流动。 | synonyms: chart, graph, illustration, schematic, sketch | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but could consider "photograph," "reality," "physical object" as representing the opposite of a simplified representation) | noun
