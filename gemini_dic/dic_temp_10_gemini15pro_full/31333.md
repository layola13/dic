characterful: /ˈkærɪktərfʊl/ (US), /ˈkærɪktəfʊl/ (UK)| adj.| 充满个性的；有特色的；生动的|Having a strong or interesting character; full of personality.|例句：The old house had a characterful charm.  这栋老房子有一种独特的魅力。|例句：She gave a characterful performance as Lady Macbeth. 她饰演麦克白夫人，表演得淋漓尽致。|近义词：distinctive, individual, unique, quirky, eccentric|反义词：bland, dull, nondescript|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From character + -ful.  "Character" comes from the Late Latin character, from Greek kharaktēr "engraved mark," from kharassein "to scratch, engrave." "-ful" is an Old English suffix meaning "full of."
