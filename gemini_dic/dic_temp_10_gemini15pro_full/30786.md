cetacean:/sɪˈteɪʃən/ /sɪˈteɪʃn/| n. |鲸类动物|A marine mammal of the order Cetacea ; a whale, dolphin, or porpoise.|例句：Whales and dolphins are cetaceans. 鲸鱼和海豚都是鲸类动物。

例句：Many cetaceans use echolocation to navigate and find prey. 许多鲸类动物使用回声定位来导航和寻找猎物。|近义词：whale, dolphin, porpoise|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑用terrestrial mammal 陆生哺乳动物)|词性:noun
