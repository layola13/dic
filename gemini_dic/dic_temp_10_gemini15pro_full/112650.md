lowbrow:/ˈloʊbraʊ/| adj.|低俗的，庸俗的，缺乏文化修养的|Not intellectually demanding or stimulating; intended for or appealing to the masses.|例句：I found the movie to be rather lowbrow. 我觉得这部电影很低俗。|例句：Lowbrow humor doesn't appeal to me. 低俗的幽默对我没有吸引力。|近义词：vulgar, tasteless, unrefined, crude, philistine|反义词：highbrow, sophisticated, cultured, refined, intellectual|词性:形容词

词源:  The term "lowbrow" originated in the late 19th century based on the pseudoscientific theory of phrenology, which claimed that the shape of one's forehead (brow) indicated intelligence. A low forehead was believed to signify low intelligence.
