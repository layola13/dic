nonsanctimoniously:/ˌnɒnsæŋktɪˈməʊniəsli/
| adv. |不虚伪地，不矫揉造作地|Without feigning piety or religious virtue; in a straightforward, sincere manner. |He nonsanctimoniously admitted his mistakes, earning the respect of his colleagues. 他坦诚地承认了自己的错误，赢得了同事们的尊重。|近义词：candidly, frankly, honestly, genuinely, sincerely, unpretentiously|反义词：sanctimoniously, hypocritically, piously, self-righteously|词性: 副词

nonsanctimonious:/ˌnɒnsæŋktɪˈməʊniəs/
| adj. |不虚伪的，不矫揉造作的|Not displaying or characterized by sanctimony; not pretending to be morally better than others. |Her nonsanctimonious approach to charity work made her a beloved figure in the community.  她不矫揉造作地从事慈善工作，使她成为社区中受爱戴的人物。|近义词：candid, frank, honest, genuine, sincere, unpretentious|反义词：sanctimonious, hypocritical, pious, self-righteous|词性: 形容词


词源:
From non- + sanctimonious.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "sanctimonious" comes from "sanctimony," meaning "the quality or state of being sanctimonious; pretense of holiness."  Ultimately derived from Latin "sanctus" meaning "holy."
