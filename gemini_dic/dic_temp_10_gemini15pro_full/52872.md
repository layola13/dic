diminishingly: /dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋli/
adverb
逐渐减少地，越来越少地
in a way that becomes gradually smaller, weaker, or less important.
The pain diminished gradually. 疼痛逐渐减轻。
Her enthusiasm diminished gradually. 她的热情逐渐减弱。
His influence is diminishingly small. 他的影响力越来越小。
The returns are diminishingly small. 收益越来越少。
近义词: decreasingly, progressively less
反义词: increasingly, progressively more
词性: adverb

词源: From diminish + -ing + -ly.  Diminish comes from Old French *diminuer, from Latin diminuere "to lessen," from dis- "apart, away" + minuere "to lessen."
