anecdotic:/ˌænɪkˈdɑːtɪk/ | adj. |轶事的，趣闻的，传闻的|Based on personal accounts rather than facts or research; informal.|例句：Anecdotal evidence suggests that the medicine is effective.  传闻证据表明该药有效。

例句：The book is full of anecdotal stories about famous scientists. 这本书里充满了关于著名科学家的轶事。|近义词：informal, casual, unscientific, hearsay|反义词：scientific, empirical, statistical, factual|词性:adjective
