conk's:/kɒŋks/ /kɑːŋks/ | n. | （俚语）头，脑袋；鼻子 |  A slang term, primarily used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE), referring to someone's head or nose, sometimes specifically a large nose.  It can also refer to the hairstyle known as a "conk."  The possessive form "conk's" is less common but would refer to something belonging to or associated with someone's conk (head/nose). |  I got a bump on my conk's. 我的头上起了一个包。 (This usage is less common and might sound unnatural.) | 近义词: head, noggin, nose, beak (slang) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
