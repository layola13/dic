owenite:/ˈoʊəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 欧文主义者；欧文信徒 | A follower of the socialist theories of Robert Owen. | Robert Owen's followers, the Owenites, established a number of experimental communities. 罗伯特·欧文的追随者，欧文主义者，建立了一些实验社区。 |  synonyms: socialist, utopian, communist | antonyms: capitalist, individualist | noun

owenite:/ˈoʊəˌnaɪt/ | adj. | 欧文主义的；欧文信徒的 | Relating to the socialist theories of Robert Owen. | The Owenite communities were based on principles of cooperation and shared ownership. 欧文主义社区建立在合作和共有所有权的原则之上。 | synonyms: socialist, utopian, communal | antonyms: capitalist, individualist | adjective
