gumptionless:/ˈɡʌmpʃənləs/| adj.|没有进取心的，缺乏魄力的，没有决断力的|Lacking initiative, resourcefulness, or courage; spineless.|He was too gumptionless to stand up for himself. 他太缺乏魄力，不敢为自己辩护。|Synonyms: spiritless, timid, unenterprising, passive, indecisive|Antonyms: spirited, bold, enterprising, resourceful, decisive|adjective

Etymology->
From gumption + -less.  Gumption itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from Scots dialect.
