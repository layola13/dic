nonconducivenesses:/ˌnɑːnkənduːsɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
不传导性，不利条件
The state or quality of not being conducive; unfavorableness.
The nonconducivenesses of the environment hampered the growth of the plants.  这种环境的不利条件阻碍了植物的生长。
