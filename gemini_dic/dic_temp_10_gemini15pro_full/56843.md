dorsad:/ˈdɔːrsæd/| adj. adv.|背侧的，向背侧地|Toward or on the back, especially of an animal.  Relating to the dorsal side or surface.|The butterfly's wings displayed intricate patterns dorsad.|近义词：dorsal, posterior|反义词:ventral, anterior|词性:形容词，副词

Etymology: From Latin *dorsum* (back) + *-ad* (toward).
