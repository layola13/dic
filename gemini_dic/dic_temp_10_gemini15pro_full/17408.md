beamed:/biːmd/
| v. | 微笑，眉开眼笑；发射，播送 | To smile broadly and radiantly, or to transmit (a signal, program, etc.) by a beam. | She beamed at him when he gave her the flowers. 当他给她花的时候，她眉开眼笑地望着他。| synonyms: smiled, grinned, radiated, transmitted | antonyms: frowned, scowled, absorbed | verb

beamed:/biːmd/
| adj. |  (指脸)喜气洋洋的，面带笑容的 | Having a broad, radiant smile. |  He had a beamed face after winning the lottery.  他中了彩票后喜气洋洋的。| synonyms: smiling, radiant, cheerful | antonyms: frowning, glum, somber | adjective
