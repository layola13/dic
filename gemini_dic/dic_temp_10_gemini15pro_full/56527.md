domicile: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK), /ˈdɑːməsaɪl/ (US) | n. |住所，居所|A person's fixed, permanent, and principal home for legal purposes.| He changed his domicile from London to Paris. 他把住所从伦敦迁到了巴黎。 | synonyms: residence, abode, dwelling | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "temporary lodging") | noun


domicile: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK), /ˈdɑːməsaɪl/ (US) | vt. |使定居，安置|To establish in a domicile.| The refugees were domiciled in temporary housing. 难民被安置在临时住房里。 | synonyms:  settle, establish, house | antonyms:  evict, displace | verb
