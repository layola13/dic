inhospitably:/ˌɪnˈhɑːspɪtəbli/ /ˌɪnˈhɒspɪtəbli/
adjective
不友好地，冷淡地，荒凉地
in an unfriendly or unwelcoming manner; in a barren and desolate way
The travelers were treated inhospitably by the villagers.  旅行者们受到了村民们不友好的对待。
The barren landscape stretched before them inhospitably.  荒凉的景色在他们面前不友好地延伸。
近义词：unwelcomingly, unkindly, coldly, hostilely, forbiddingly
反义词：hospitably, welcomingly, warmly, kindly
词性: adverb (inhospitable 的副词形式)

词源:
From in- (not) + hospitable (welcoming to guests) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin hospes (guest).
