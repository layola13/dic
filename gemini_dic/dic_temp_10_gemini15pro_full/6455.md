amplify:/ˈæmplɪfaɪ/ | v. |放大，增强，详述|To increase the strength or intensity of something, especially sound; to expand or elaborate on something (e.g., a story).|例句：The microphone amplified his voice so everyone could hear.  麦克风放大了他的声音，让每个人都能听到。

例句：She amplified on her earlier remarks, providing more details about the incident. 她详细阐述了她之前的言论，提供了更多关于事件的细节。|近义词：increase, boost, expand, elaborate, magnify|反义词：reduce, diminish, decrease, condense|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin amplificāre, from Latin amplus (“large”) + -ficāre,  a combining form meaning "to make" or "to do."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂emH- (“raw, crude”).
