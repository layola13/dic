evection:/ɪˈvɛkʃən/| n. |月球的二均差；(尤指不公正的)逐出，驱逐|A periodic perturbation in the motion of the moon, caused by the attraction of the sun.|The evection of the moon is a significant perturbation that affects its orbit.|近义词：perturbation, displacement, expulsion, ejection|反义词: attraction, retention|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Latin ēvectiō (“a carrying out”), from ēvehō (“I carry out”), from ex (“out of”) + vehō (“I carry”).
