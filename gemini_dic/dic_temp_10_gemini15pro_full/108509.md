layoff:/ˈleɪˌɔːf/ (American) /ˈleɪɒf/ (British)| n.|裁员；临时解雇；停止活动期|a period of being laid off from work, especially temporarily.|He's facing a layoff from his job. 他面临着被解雇的危险。|synonyms: unemployment spell, redundancy period|antonyms: employment period, work period|noun
