overlearnedness:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrnɪdnəs/ | n. | 过度学习 | The quality or state of having learned something so thoroughly that it becomes automatic and difficult to change.  It often refers to skills or knowledge that have been practiced far beyond the point of mastery. |  His overlearnedness of the piano piece allowed him to play it flawlessly even when distracted. 他对这首钢琴曲的过度学习使他即使分心也能完美地演奏。 | 近义词：automaticity, mastery, proficiency | 反义词：unfamiliarity, inexperience | 词性: noun

overlearn:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrn/ | v. | 过度学习 | To learn something so thoroughly that it becomes automatic and difficult to change. | She overlearned the multiplication tables as a child. 她小时候过度学习了乘法表。| 近义词：master, perfect | 反义词：unlearn | 词性: verb
