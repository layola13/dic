condense:/kənˈdens/ | v. | 浓缩，凝结，精简 | to reduce something, such as a speech or piece of writing, in length; to change or cause to change from a gas or vapor to a liquid | The author condensed his three-volume novel into a single book. 作者将他的三卷本小说浓缩成了一本书。 |  synonyms: compress, shorten, abridge | antonyms: expand, lengthen, dilute | verb

condense:/kənˈdens/ | v. |  凝结，冷凝 | to change or cause to change from a gas or vapor to a liquid | Water vapor condenses to form clouds. 水蒸气凝结形成云。| synonyms: liquefy, solidify | antonyms: evaporate, vaporize | verb
