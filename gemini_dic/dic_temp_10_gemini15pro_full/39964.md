construers:/kənˈstruːərz/ | n. | 解释者，理解者 | People who interpret or understand something in a particular way.  Those who assign meaning. |  Legal scholars are often the construers of complex statutes. 法律学者通常是复杂法规的解释者。

The construers of the ancient text disagreed on its meaning.  古代文献的解释者对其含义意见不一。 | interpreters, analysts, decoders | misinterpreters, distorters | noun
