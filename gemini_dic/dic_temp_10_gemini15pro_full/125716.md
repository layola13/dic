municipalizer:/mjuːˌnɪsɪpəlaɪzər/ | n. | 市政化者，市政化支持者 |  A person who advocates for or supports the municipalization of a service or industry, meaning bringing it under the control of a local government or municipality. | The municipalizers argued that public ownership of the water system would lead to lower rates and better service.  市政化支持者认为，公共拥有供水系统将导致更低的费率和更好的服务。 |  proponent, advocate, supporter | opponent, privatizer | noun

municipalize:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəlaɪz/ | v. | 市政化 | To bring (a service, industry, etc.) under municipal ownership or control. | The city council voted to municipalize the public transportation system. 市议会投票决定将公共交通系统市政化。 | nationalize, socialize | privatize | verb
