noncombative:/ˌnɒnkəmˈbætɪv/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈbætɪv/| adj.|非战斗性的，不具攻击性的|Not inclined to fight or argue; peaceful.|例句：He adopted a noncombative stance during the negotiations.  他在谈判中采取了非战斗性的姿态。|近义词：peaceful, conciliatory, cooperative, passive, nonaggressive|反义词：aggressive, combative, belligerent, hostile|词性:形容词

noncombative:/ˌnɒnkəmˈbætɪv/ /ˌnɑːnkəmˈbætɪv/| n.|非战斗人员|A person who is not engaged in combat.|例句：Noncombatives were evacuated from the war zone. 非战斗人员已从战区撤离。|近义词：civilian, non-combatant|反义词：soldier, combatant|词性:名词
