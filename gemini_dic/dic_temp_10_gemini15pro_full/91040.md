husklike:/ˈhʌskˌlaɪk/| adj.|像壳的；似外壳的|Resembling a husk; having the dry, thin, and often papery texture of a husk.  It describes something that looks or feels like the outer covering of a seed or fruit.|The old book had husklike pages, brittle and yellowed with age. 这本旧书的书页像壳一样，又脆又黄，饱经风霜。|Near synonyms:  papery, dry, brittle, scaly, shell-like|Near antonyms:  fleshy, moist, supple, soft|Etymology: From "husk" (Old English *husc, related to Dutch hulsche) + "-like" (Old English -līc)|Word Type: Adjective
