livingness:/ˈlɪvɪŋnɪs/
n.
活力; 生机; 活力四射
The state or quality of being alive and vital; vitality.
The room was filled with a sense of livingness.  房间里充满了生机。
liveliness, vitality, animation, vibrancy
inertness, lifelessness, dullness
noun
