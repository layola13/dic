frighting:/ˈfraɪtənɪŋ/| adj.|令人恐惧的；骇人的|Causing fear or alarm; terrifying.|The movie was so frighting that I couldn't sleep. 这部电影太吓人了，我睡不着。|近义词：scary, terrifying, horrifying|反义词：comforting, reassuring|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *frightenen, from Old English *fryhtnian (“to frighten”), from Proto-Germanic *furhtinōną (“to make afraid”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“high; to rise”). Cognate with Dutch frightenen (“to frighten”), German *früchten (“to frighten, to fear”), dialectal German verfrichten (“to frighten”). Related to fright.
