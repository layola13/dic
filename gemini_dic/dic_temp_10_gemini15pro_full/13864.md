augmented:/ɔːɡˈmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 增强的，扩增的 | Made greater in size or value or extent or scope; increased or intensified |  The augmented reality game overlays digital images onto the real world.  增强现实游戏将数字图像叠加到现实世界中。 | synonyms: increased, enhanced, expanded, amplified, supplemented, boosted | antonyms: decreased, diminished, reduced, lessened, weakened | adjective

augmented:/ɔːɡˈmɛntɪd/ | v. | 增加，增大（augment的过去式和过去分词）| Made greater in size or value or extent or scope; increased or intensified |  The team augmented their income by selling merchandise.  团队通过销售商品增加了收入。 | synonyms: increased, enhanced, expanded, amplified, supplemented, boosted | antonyms: decreased, diminished, reduced, lessened, weakened | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *augmentatus, past participle of augmentare (“to increase”), from Latin augmentum (“increase”), from augēre (“to increase”).
