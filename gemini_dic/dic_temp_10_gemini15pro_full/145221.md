overbearingnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛrɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
傲慢自大；专横跋扈；盛气凌人
The state or quality of being overbearing; arrogance, domineering behavior.
His overbearingnesses alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢态度疏远了他的同事。
Synonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, imperiousness, domineeringness, high-handedness, despotism
Antonyms: humility, modesty, meekness, submissiveness
noun


overbearing:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛrɪŋ/
adj.
专横的；跋扈的；盛气凌人的
Having or showing a tendency to force one's will on others; domineering.
He has an overbearing personality. 他性格专横。
Synonyms: domineering, autocratic, dictatorial, tyrannical, imperious, bossy
Antonyms: humble, meek, submissive, unassuming
adjective
