duals:/ˈduːəlz/ | n. | 对偶，双数，双重 |  Something that consists of two parts or aspects; a pair.  Often used in mathematics and physics to refer to related but distinct concepts or structures. |  The relationship between the primal and dual problems in linear programming is well-studied.  线性规划中原始问题和对偶问题之间的关系得到了很好的研究。


The physicist explored the wave-particle duality of light.  这位物理学家探索了光的波粒二象性。| synonyms: counterparts, opposites, complements, pairs | antonyms: singles, singulars | noun
