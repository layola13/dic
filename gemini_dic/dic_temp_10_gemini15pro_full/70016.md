feting:/ˈfɛtɪŋ/| n. |庆祝，盛宴|A lavish celebration or entertainment in honor of someone or something. |The city held a week-long feting of the victorious team.  这座城市为获胜的队伍举行了为期一周的庆祝活动。|
|近义词：celebrating, honoring, commemorating, partying|反义词:mourning, grieving|词性:noun (derived from the verb "fete")|
feting:|/ˈfɛtɪŋ/| v. |庆祝，宴请|To honor or entertain (someone) lavishly. |They were feting the return of their hero. 他们正在庆祝英雄的归来。|
|近义词：celebrating, honoring, commemorating|反义词:ignoring, neglecting|词性:verb (present participle of "fete")|

词源: From the Old French word "feste" meaning "festival, feast," ultimately derived from the Latin "festa" (plural of "festum").
