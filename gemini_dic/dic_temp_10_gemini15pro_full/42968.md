covert:/ˈkʌvərt/ (US), /ˈkʌvət/ (UK)| adj. n.|秘密的，隐蔽的；掩蔽处，藏身处|Concealed; secret; disguised.  Also, a hiding place.|She was involved in covert operations for the CIA. 她参与了中央情报局的秘密行动。|The fox disappeared into its covert in the woods. 狐狸消失在树林里的藏身处。|近义词：clandestine, surreptitious, hidden, secret|反义词：overt, open, public|词性:形容词,名词

covert:/ˈkʌvərt/ (US), /ˈkʌvət/ (UK)| n.|掩蔽处，藏身处|A hiding place; shelter.|The deer sought covert in the dense undergrowth. 鹿在茂密的灌木丛中寻找藏身之处。|近义词：shelter, refuge, hiding place|反义词：exposure|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *`covert` (modern French `couvert`), past participle of `covrir` "to cover," from Latin `cooperire` "to cover completely," from `com-` "completely" + `operire` "to cover."
