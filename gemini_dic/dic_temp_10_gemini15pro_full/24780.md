bros's:/brəʊzɪz/| n.|兄弟们的 (属于多个兄弟)|The bros's car is in the driveway. 兄弟们的车停在车道上。|英文解析: This is a nonstandard and generally considered incorrect possessive form of "bros," short for "brothers."  The correct possessive would be "bros'."  "Bros's" adds an extra "s" which is unnecessary and grammatically incorrect. While occasionally seen in informal writing, it's best avoided.|例句：The bros' car is parked outside. 兄弟们的车停在外面。

例句：We went to the bros' house for the party. 我们去兄弟们的家参加派对。
