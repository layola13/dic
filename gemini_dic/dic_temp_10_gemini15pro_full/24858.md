brownout:/ˈbraʊnaʊt/| n.|电压骤降；部分停电；灯光变暗|A reduction in voltage in an electrical power supply system.  This can cause lights to dim and appliances to malfunction.|The frequent brownouts have been damaging our appliances.  频繁的电压骤降损坏了我们的电器。|近义词：power dip, voltage drop, dimming|反义词:power surge, blackout|名词

brownout:/ˈbraʊnaʊt/| n.|（尤指因炎热引起的）体力衰竭；意识模糊|A temporary loss of consciousness or mental acuity, often caused by excessive heat.|He suffered a brownout during the marathon due to the extreme heat.  由于酷热，他在马拉松比赛中体力衰竭，意识模糊。|近义词：fainting spell, dizziness, lightheadedness|反义词: alertness, consciousness|名词
