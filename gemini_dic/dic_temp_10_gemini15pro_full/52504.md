differencing:/ˈdɪfərənsɪŋ/
n.|差分，求差|The process of subtracting one value from another, often used in time series analysis to remove trends or seasonality.|Time series differencing helped to stabilize the data. 时间序列差分有助于稳定数据。|subtraction, differentiation|integration|noun

differencing:/ˈdɪfərənsɪŋ/
adj.|作差分的，差分的|Relating to or involving the process of differencing.|Differencing methods are commonly used in econometrics. 差分方法常用于计量经济学。|subtractive, differential|integrative|adjective
