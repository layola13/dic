bisexual:/baɪˈsɛkʃuəl/ | n.|双性恋者|A person who is sexually attracted to both men and women.|例句：There are many bisexual people in the LGBTQ+ community.  LGBTQ+群体中有许多双性恋者。|例句：He is a bisexual man. 他是一位双性恋男性。|近义词：无|反义词：无|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (prefix meaning "two") + sexual.  The prefix "bi-" comes from Latin, meaning "two, twice, double." "Sexual" comes from Late Latin "sexualis," meaning "of or pertaining to sex."
