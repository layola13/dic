nonconcur:/ˌnɒnkənˈkɜːr/ (US) /ˌnɒnkənˈkɜː/ (UK)| v. |不同意，不一致，持异议|To disagree or refuse to consent; to withhold assent; to dissent.|The judge nonconcurred with the majority opinion. 法官不同意多数人的意见。|Synonyms: disagree, dissent, object, oppose|Antonyms: agree, concur, assent, approve|verb

nonconcurrence:/ˌnɒnkənˈkʌrəns/ (US) /ˌnɒnkənˈkʌrəns/ (UK)| n. |不一致，不同意，异议|Disagreement; lack of concurrence; failure to agree.|The nonconcurrence of the two reports led to further investigation. 两份报告的不一致导致了进一步的调查。|Synonyms: disagreement, dissent, discord, variance|Antonyms: agreement, concurrence, accord, harmony|noun
