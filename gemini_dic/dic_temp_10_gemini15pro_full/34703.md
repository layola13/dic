cleverer:/ˈklɛvərər (UK), ˈklɛvərər (US)| adj.|更聪明的|Comparative form of "clever," meaning more intelligent, skillful, or ingenious.|She's cleverer than her brother at math. 她比她哥哥更擅长数学。|He's a cleverer negotiator than I am.  他比我更会谈判。|近义词：brighter, smarter, sharper, more intelligent|反义词：dumber, stupider, less intelligent|词性:形容词
