elect's: /ɪˈlɛkts/
noun
被选者；当选者
Those elected will serve a four-year term.  那些当选者将任期四年。
The elect's responsibilities are clearly defined. 当选者的职责有明确的规定。
Synonyms: chosen, selected, voted in
Antonyms: rejected, defeated, unelected
词性: 名词
