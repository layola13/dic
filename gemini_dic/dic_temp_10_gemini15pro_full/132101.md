noncriminal:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪmɪnl/
| adj.|非犯罪的；不构成犯罪的|Not involving or relating to crime.  |例句：Parking violations are considered noncriminal offenses. 违规停车被认为是非刑事犯罪。|近义词：lawful, legal, legitimate|反义词：criminal, illegal, unlawful|词性:形容词
noncriminal:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪmɪnl/
| n.|非罪犯|A person who is not a criminal. |例句：The majority of the population are noncriminals. 大多数人都是非罪犯。|近义词：civilian, law-abiding citizen|反义词：criminal, offender, felon|词性:名词
