flacks:/flæks/ | n. | 公关人员，新闻发言人；吹捧者 |  A person, especially one employed by a political party or organization, who disseminates information to the media and answers questions from journalists; someone who promotes or praises something or someone, often excessively. | The company hired a team of flacks to generate positive publicity.  这家公司雇佣了一批公关人员来制造正面宣传。| synonyms: publicist, press agent, spokesman, spinner | antonyms: critic, detractor | noun

flacks:/flæks/ | v. | (非正式) 担任公关；吹捧 | To act as a publicist; to promote or praise something or someone, often excessively. |  He flacked for the new movie, telling everyone how great it was. 他为这部新电影大肆宣传，逢人便说它有多棒。 | synonyms: publicize, promote, hype | antonyms: criticize, denounce | verb
