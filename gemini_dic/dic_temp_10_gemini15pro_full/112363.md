los:/lɔs/ (Spanish)| determiner, pronoun| 这些，那些（阳性复数）|  The plural definite article in Spanish used before masculine nouns.  It corresponds to "the" in English, but indicates multiple masculine items.| Ejemplo: Los libros están en la mesa. The books are on the table.| 近义词: estos (these - close to the speaker), aquellos (those - far from both speaker and listener) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: determiner, pronoun
