fiddlers:/ˈfɪdlərz/| n.|提琴手；拉小提琴的人；骗子；<口>瞎搞的人|A fiddler is someone who plays the fiddle, a type of violin. It can also refer to someone who trifles or wastes time, or even a dishonest person. |例句：The fiddlers tuned their instruments before the concert.  音乐会前，小提琴手们调好了他们的乐器。

例句：He's been fiddling with the computer all morning. 他一早上都在瞎摆弄电脑。|近义词：violinists, musicians, swindlers, cheaters|反义词: (For musician meaning) non-musicians, amateurs (For dishonest meaning) honest people|词性:noun
