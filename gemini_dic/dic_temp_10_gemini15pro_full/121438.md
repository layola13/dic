misintended:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndɪd/
adjective
误解的，非计划中的
Having or showing an unintended meaning or purpose.
The misintended comment caused a lot of confusion. 这句并非故意的话造成了很多混乱。
近义词:unintentional, accidental, unintended
反义词:intentional, deliberate, purposeful
词性:adjective

词源:
From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "badly") + intended (past participle of intend, meaning "to have in mind as a purpose").
