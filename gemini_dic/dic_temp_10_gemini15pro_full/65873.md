evolutility:/ˌiːvəluːˈtɪləti/ |n.|进化性；可进化性|The evolutility of software is crucial in today's rapidly changing technological landscape.  软件的可进化性在当今快速变化的技术领域至关重要。| Evolutility allows organisms to adapt to changing environments. 进化性使生物体能够适应变化的环境。 |near: adaptability, flexibility, plasticity | antonym: rigidity, inflexibility | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "evolute" (meaning to develop or unfold) + "-ility" (a suffix forming abstract nouns denoting a quality or state).  "Evolute" itself comes from the Latin "evolvere," meaning "to unroll" or "to develop."
