confessedly:/kənˈfɛsɪdli/
adv.
坦白地，公然地
admittedly, openly acknowledging something
He confessedly had no idea how to solve the problem. 他坦白地承认自己不知道如何解决这个问题。
Confessedly, I was nervous about the upcoming exam.  坦白说，我对即将到来的考试感到紧张。
近义词:admittedly, avowedly, openly
反义词:secretly, covertly
词性:副词

词源: From confess + -ed + -ly.  From Latin *confessus, past participle of confitere "to confess," from com- "together" + fateri "to acknowledge."
