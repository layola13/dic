become:/bɪˈkʌm/ (US), /bɪˈkʌm/ (UK)| v.| 变成，成为 |She became a doctor after years of study. 她经过多年的学习成了一名医生。|The sky became dark as the storm approached.  随着暴风雨的临近，天空变得昏暗。|近义词: grow, turn, get, develop|反义词: remain, stay|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old English *becuman "arrive, happen, suit, befall," from be- "by" + cuman "to come."
