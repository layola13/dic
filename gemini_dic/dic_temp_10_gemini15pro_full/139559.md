obtusely:/əbˈtjuːsli/ /ɑːbˈtuːsli/
adverb
迟钝地；愚笨地；不敏感地
In a manner that lacks sharpness or quickness of intellect; dully or stupidly.  Characterized by a lack of sensitivity or awareness.
He obtusely missed the obvious clues in the mystery novel. 他迟钝地错过了推理小说中明显的线索。
She obtusely stared at the painting, failing to grasp its meaning. 她茫然地盯着那幅画，无法理解它的含义。
Synonyms: dully, stupidly, insensitively, unperceptively, bluntly
Antonyms: sharply, keenly, intelligently, sensitively, perceptively
词性: 副词
