packed:/pækt/ | adj. | 挤满的，塞满的；忙碌的 | Filled to capacity; very busy. | The train was packed with commuters. 火车上挤满了通勤者。| The restaurant was packed on Saturday night.  这家餐馆星期六晚上很拥挤。| crowded, full, crammed | empty, sparse | adjective

packed:/pækt/ | v. |  包装，打包；塞满 | To put things into a container; to fill a space tightly.  |  She packed her suitcase for the trip. 她为旅行打包行李。 | He packed the box with books. 他把箱子装满了书。| fill, cram, stuff | unpack, empty | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *pakken*, from Old Norse *pakka* (“to pack, wrap, bind”), from Proto-Germanic *pakkōną* (“to pack, wrap”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂ǵ-* (“to fasten, fix”).  Related to pack (n.).
