hiragana:/ˌhɪrəˈɡɑːnə/ |n.|平假名|A Japanese syllabary used in writing Japanese, one of two kana systems, the other being katakana. Hiragana is primarily used for native Japanese words and grammatical elements.|例句：Children in Japan learn hiragana before katakana. 日本的小孩先學平假名再學片假名。|例句：Many Japanese books for young children are written entirely in hiragana. 許多日本兒童讀物完全是用平假名寫成的。|近义词：kana|反义词: None|词性:noun
