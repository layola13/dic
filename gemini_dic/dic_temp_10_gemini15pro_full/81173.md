grandest:/ˈɡrændɪst/| adj.|最宏伟的，最壮丽的|superlative form of grand: most impressive in size, scope, or effect.|例句：The grandest building in the city is the opera house. 城市中最宏伟的建筑是歌剧院。|近义词：most magnificent, most splendid, most impressive|反义词：humblest, simplest, smallest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *grandest, superlative of grand, from Old French grand “large, big, great, tall, long; important; powerful; senior; old, ancient; pregnant” (12c., Modern French grand), from Latin grandis “grown up, full-grown, great, large, grand, lofty, tall; powerful, important, chief, principal; advanced (in age), old.”
