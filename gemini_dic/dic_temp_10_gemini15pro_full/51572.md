deuthydroguret: /djuːˌtaɪdrəˈɡjʊərɪt/ | n. |氘化物| A compound formed by the replacement of one or more hydrogen atoms in a molecule with deuterium atoms.  It refers specifically to a compound where hydrogen has been replaced by deuterium (heavy hydrogen).|  Example:  Deuterated chloroform (CDCl3) is a common deuteride used in NMR spectroscopy. 氘代氯仿 (CDCl3) 是一种常用于核磁共振波谱的氘化物。 | Example: The synthesis of the deuteride involved several complex steps.  这种氘化物的合成包括几个复杂的步骤。 | Synonym: deuterated compound | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could be considered the non-deuterated equivalent of the compound) | noun
