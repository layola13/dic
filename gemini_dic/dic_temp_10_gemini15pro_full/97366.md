infract:/ɪnˈfrækt/
verb
违反，违背
to break a rule, law, or agreement
The company infracted several environmental regulations.  这家公司违反了几项环境法规。
synonyms: violate, breach, disobey, transgress
antonyms: obey, comply, observe
词性: 动词


infract:/ɪnˈfrækt/
noun
违规，违反行为
an act of breaking a rule or law
Parking infractions can result in hefty fines. 停车违规会导致巨额罚款。
synonyms: violation, breach, offense, transgression
antonyms: obedience, compliance, observance
词性: 名词
