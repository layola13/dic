accommodator: /əˈkɒmədeɪtə(r)/ (US) /əˈkɑːmədeɪtər/
n.|容纳者；提供住宿者；适应者|A person who provides lodgings or makes adjustments to suit others' needs.|The hotel accommodator found us a larger room.  这家旅馆的住宿提供者为我们找到了一个更大的房间。|synonyms: host, provider, facilitator|antonyms: obstructor, hinderer|noun

accommodator: /əˈkɒmədeɪtə(r)/ (US) /əˈkɑːmədeɪtər/
n.|调节者|A person who adjusts or adapts.|He was a skilled accommodator, always finding ways to make things work. 他是一个熟练的调节者，总是能找到方法让事情顺利进行。|synonyms: adjuster, adapter|antonyms: inflexible person, rigid person|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accommodator, from accommodare "to fit, adapt," from Latin ad- "to" + commodare "to make fit," from commodus "suitable, convenient."

USAGE->
The word "accommodator" is most commonly used as a noun referring to someone who provides lodging. It can also describe someone who adapts or adjusts to situations or the needs of others.  Here are some examples in different tenses and sentence structures:

一般现在时:
The accommodator offers comfortable rooms. =  这位住宿提供者提供舒适的房间。
The accommodator always tries to meet our needs. =  这位住宿提供者总是尽力满足我们的需求。

一般过去时:
The accommodator welcomed us warmly. =  这位住宿提供者热情地欢迎我们。
The accommodator prepared a delicious breakfast. =  这位住宿提供者准备了美味的早餐。

现在完成时:
The accommodator has upgraded our room. =  这位住宿提供者已经升级了我们的房间。
The accommodator has always been helpful. =  这位住宿提供者一直都很乐于助人。

过去完成时:
The accommodator had already prepared the room before we arrived. =  在我们到达之前，这位住宿提供者就已经准备好了房间。
The accommodator had anticipated our needs. =  这位住宿提供者已经预料到了我们的需求。

将来时:
The accommodator will arrange transportation for us. =  这位住宿提供者将为我们安排交通工具。
The accommodator will ensure our comfort during our stay. =  这位住宿提供者将确保我们入住期间的舒适。


被动语态:
We were accommodated in a spacious room. = 我们被安排在一个宽敞的房间里。
Our needs were accommodated by the hotel staff. = 酒店工作人员满足了我们的需求。

疑问句:

Is there an accommodator available? = 有住宿提供者吗？

否定句:

She didn't act as an accommodator in this situation. =  在这种情况下，她没有充当调节者的角色。
