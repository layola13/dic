anosmia:/ænˈɒzmiə/ /ænˈɑːzmiə/
n.
失嗅症;嗅觉缺失
loss of the sense of smell, either total or partial.
She suffered from anosmia after a severe head injury. 她头部受重伤后患上了失嗅症。
