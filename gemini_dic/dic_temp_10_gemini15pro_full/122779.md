molar-tooth:/ˈmoʊlər tuːθ/| adj.|臼齿状的，臼齿形的|Resembling a molar tooth, especially in shape or structure.  Characterized by a broad, flat crown with multiple cusps used for grinding. |The fossil had molar-tooth like structures. 这块化石有臼齿状的结构。|Synonyms: molariform, multicuspidate|Antonyms: incisiform, caniniform|adjective

molar-tooth:/ˈmoʊlər tuːθ/| n.|臼齿|A grinding tooth at the back of a mammal's mouth.|The dentist examined my molars-tooth. 牙医检查了我的臼齿。|Synonyms: molar|Antonyms: incisor, canine|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "molar" (from Late Latin "molaris," meaning "grinding") + "tooth" (Old English "tōþ").
