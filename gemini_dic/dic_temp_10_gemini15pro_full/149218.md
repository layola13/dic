oxymoron:/ˌɒksɪˈmɔːrɒn/ (UK) /ˌɑːksɪˈmɔːrɑːn/ (US)| n.|矛盾修饰法，逆喻|A figure of speech in which apparently contradictory terms appear in conjunction.  A combination of words that have opposite or very different meanings.|例句： "Jumbo shrimp" is a classic oxymoron.  “巨型虾”是一个经典的矛盾修饰法。|例句：The phrase "cruel kindness" is an oxymoron. “残酷的仁慈”是一个矛盾修饰法。|近义词：paradox, contradiction, antithesis|反义词:tautology, pleonasm|noun
