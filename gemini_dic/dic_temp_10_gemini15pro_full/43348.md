crampy:/ˈkræmpi/| adj.|痉挛的，抽筋的|Affected with or characterized by cramps; causing cramps.|例句：I have a crampy stomach. 我的胃痉挛。|例句：I get crampy legs after long runs. 长跑后我的腿会抽筋。|近义词：spasmodic, tight, stiff|反义词:relaxed, loose|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *cramp* + *-y*.  *Cramp* comes from Middle English *cramp*, from Old English *cramp*, *crumb*, from Proto-Germanic *krampuz*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)krembh-*, "to draw together, to bend, to curve".
