finalist:/ˈfaɪnəlɪst/| n.|决赛选手；参加决赛者|A person who reaches the final round of a competition.|例句：She was a finalist in the spelling bee. 她曾是拼字比赛的决赛选手。

例句：The finalists will compete for the grand prize. 决赛选手将角逐大奖。|近义词：contender, candidate|反义词：non-finalist, loser|词性：名词

Etymology:
From final + -ist.  "Final" traces back to the Latin word "finalis," meaning "of or pertaining to an end." The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who performs a particular action or holds a particular belief.
