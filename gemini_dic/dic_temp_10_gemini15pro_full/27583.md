canaro:/kəˈnɑːroʊ/
noun
卡纳罗犬; 一种大型猛犬
A large, powerful breed of dog, often used as a guard dog.
The canaro charged at the intruder, barking ferociously. 卡纳罗犬猛扑向入侵者，狂吠不止。
Synonyms:  guard dog, mastiff, watchdog
Antonyms:  lapdog, toy dog
n.

canaro:/kəˈnɑːroʊ/
noun
卡纳罗(乌拉圭足球俱乐部);  指乌拉圭的佩纳罗尔足球俱乐部(Club Atlético Peñarol)
A nickname for the Uruguayan football club Club Atlético Peñarol.
The Canaro won the championship last year. 卡纳罗去年赢得了冠军。
Synonyms:  Peñarol, CA Peñarol,  Aurinegros (another nickname)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could list rival teams like Nacional)
n.

词源:  Canaro is a shortened form of "Canario," the Spanish word for "Canary Islander."  The breed originated in the Canary Islands.  It's also a nickname for Club Atlético Peñarol, derived from a former club president, nicknamed "el Canario."
