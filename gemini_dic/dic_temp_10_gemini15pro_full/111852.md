logician:/ləˈdʒɪʃən/| n.|逻辑学家；逻辑家|A person skilled in logic; a person who studies or teaches logic. |He is a renowned logician and philosopher. 他是一位著名的逻辑学家和哲学家。|Example: Her arguments were so well-constructed, she seemed like a true logician. 她的论证如此严密，看起来像个真正的逻辑学家。|Synonyms:  reasoner, thinker, philosopher, analyst|Antonyms: illogical person, irrational person|noun
