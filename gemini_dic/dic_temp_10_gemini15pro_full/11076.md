archaeostomatous:/ˌɑːrkiːoʊˈstɒmətəs/ | adj. | 古口的，原始口的 | Having a mouth formed as in primitive mollusks, with the blastopore becoming the mouth.  |  Example:  Archaeostomatous gastropods represent an older evolutionary lineage.  古口腹足纲动物代表了一个更古老的进化谱系。 | Example:  The study focused on the development of the archaeostomatous mouth in certain marine snails.  该研究的重点是某些海螺的古口发育。 |  Near Synonyms:  primitive, ancestral | Antonyms:  advanced, derived | Word type: Adjective
