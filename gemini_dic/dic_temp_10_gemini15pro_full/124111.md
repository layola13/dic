immortalize:/ɪˈmɔːrtəlaɪz/ | v. | 使不朽，使永垂不朽 | To make something or someone famous or remembered for a long time, usually by creating a work of art such as a book, film, or painting. |  Shakespeare immortalized the name of Romeo and Juliet.  莎士比亚让罗密欧与朱丽叶的名字永垂不朽。

|  The artist attempted to immortalize the beauty of the sunset.  艺术家试图使日落的美丽永存。|  commemorate, eternalize, memorialize |  forget, ignore | verb
