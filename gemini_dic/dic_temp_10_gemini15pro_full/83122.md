gustless:/ˈɡʌstləs/| adj.|平淡的，无味的，缺乏激情的|Lacking flavor, zest, or spirit; bland.  Derived from "gust" (meaning a burst of flavor or sensation) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).|The soup was disappointingly gustless.|近义词：bland, insipid, tasteless, vapid, uninspired|反义词：flavorful, tasty, zesty, spirited, exciting|词性:形容词
