neuropteran: /nʊˈrɒptərən/ | n. adj. | 脉翅目昆虫；脉翅目的 | An insect of the order Neuroptera, such as a lacewing or antlion.  These insects are characterized by their delicate, net-veined wings. |  Lacewings are beneficial neuropterans that prey on aphids.  草蛉是有益的脉翅目昆虫，它们捕食蚜虫。 | 近义词: lacewing, antlion (specific examples), net-winged insect | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, adjective

neuropterous: /nʊˈrɒptərəs/ | adj. | 脉翅目的 | Belonging to or characteristic of the insect order Neuroptera. | The delicate, neuropterous wings of the insect shimmered in the sunlight.  这种昆虫纤细的脉翅在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective
