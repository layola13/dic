idolater:/ˈaɪdəˌleɪtər/ (US), /aɪˈdɒlətər/ (UK)| n. |偶像崇拜者；盲目崇拜者|A person who worships idols or someone or something other than God.|例句：The ancient Romans were idolaters, worshipping a pantheon of gods. 古罗马人是偶像崇拜者，崇拜众神。

例句：He accused them of being idolaters, worshipping money instead of God. 他指责他们是偶像崇拜者，崇拜金钱而不是上帝。|近义词：worshipper, devotee, fan, admirer|反义词：iconoclast|词性:noun
