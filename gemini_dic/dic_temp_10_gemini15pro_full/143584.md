outhumors:/ˈaʊtˌhjuːmərz/
n.
过时的体液; 古体液学说中的体液
Bodily fluids, specifically the four humors of ancient and medieval physiology (blood, phlegm, yellow bile, and black bile), believed to determine a person's health and temperament.
The doctor explained that an imbalance of the outhumors was believed to cause illness. 医生解释说，人们认为体液失衡会导致疾病。
近义词:humors, four humors
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑现代医学理论) modern medical theories
词性: 名词
