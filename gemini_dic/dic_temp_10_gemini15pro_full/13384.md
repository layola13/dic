atole:/ɑːˈtoʊleɪ/ (US), /əˈtəʊleɪ/ (UK)| n. |阿托利，玉米粥|A hot corn-based beverage originating in Mesoamerica, typically made with masa (corn dough), water, and spices like cinnamon or vanilla. It can also be flavored with chocolate, fruit, or other ingredients.|Example:  I enjoyed a warm atole for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上享用了一碗热腾腾的阿托利。

Example:  Street vendors often sell atole in the early mornings.  街头小贩经常在清晨售卖阿托利。|近义词：gruel, porridge|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a cold beverage like "iced tea" or "juice")|词性:noun
