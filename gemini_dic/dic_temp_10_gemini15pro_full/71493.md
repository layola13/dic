flanerie:/flɑːn(ə)ˈriː/ /flæn(ə)ˈriː/
n.
闲逛，漫步，徜徉
The aimless strolling of a flaneur.
例句：She enjoyed a morning of flanerie through the charming streets of Paris. 她享受了一个上午在巴黎迷人的街道上闲逛。

例句：Flanerie was a popular pastime for the Parisian intellectuals of the 19th century.  对于19世纪的巴黎知识分子来说，闲逛是一种流行的消遣方式。

近义词：strolling, wandering, sauntering, rambling
反义词：rushing, hurrying, scurrying
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French flânerie, from flâner "to stroll," of uncertain origin.
