dragon:/ˈdræɡən/| n.|龙; 凶暴的人; 严厉的女人|A mythical monster, typically depicted as a large reptile with wings, scales, and the ability to breathe fire.  A fierce or intractable person, especially a woman.|例句：The dragon guarded its treasure hoard.  这条龙守护着它的宝藏。

例句：She was a real dragon of a woman.  她真是个母夜叉。|近义词：monster, beast, wyvern, hydra (mythical creatures), tyrant, shrew (difficult people)|反义词:hero, angel|词性:noun
