innkeepers:/ˈɪnˌkiːpərz/
n.
旅店老板，客栈老板
People who own or manage an inn.
The innkeepers welcomed the weary travelers with open arms.  旅店老板张开双臂欢迎疲惫的旅行者。
The innkeepers provided a hearty meal and comfortable beds. 旅店老板提供了一顿丰盛的饭菜和舒适的床铺。
synonyms: hoteliers, landlords, landladies, publicans
antonyms: guests, patrons, lodgers
