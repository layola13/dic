nonproprietor:/ˌnɒnprəˈpraɪətər/ |n.|非业主，非所有者|A person who does not own a particular business or property. | Example:  The nonproprietor was allowed to use the facilities, but not to make decisions about their management. 非业主可以使用这些设施，但不能对它们的管理做出决定。

Example:  Nonproprietors are welcome to attend the conference, but voting rights are reserved for members. 非业主欢迎参加会议，但投票权保留给会员。|Synonyms: nonowner, renter, tenant|Antonyms: proprietor, owner, landlord|Noun
