nutritive:/ˈnjuːtrətɪv/| adj.|营养的，滋养的|Relating to nutrition; providing nourishment.|例句：A nutritive diet is essential for good health. 营养丰富的饮食对健康至关重要。|近义词：nourishing, nutritious, wholesome|反义词：unhealthy, non-nutritive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *nūtrītīvus*, from Latin *nūtrīre* (to nourish).
