huaca: /ˈwaka/ | n. | 圣地，神物，古迹 | A pre-Columbian sacred object or place, especially in the Andean region of South America. |  Example 1: The archaeologists discovered a huaca containing ancient pottery. 考古学家发现了一处包含古代陶器的圣地。

Example 2: The Inca considered many natural formations to be huacas. 印加人认为许多自然构造物都是圣地。 | Synonym: shrine, sanctuary, relic | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "profane place/object") | noun
