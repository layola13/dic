mesosternum:/ˌmiːzəʊˈstɜːnəm/, /ˌmiːsoʊˈstɜːrnəm/ | n. | 中胸骨 | The middle part of the sternum, located between the manubrium and the xiphoid process.  It is the longest part of the breastbone in most mammals. | The mesosternum provides attachment points for several ribs. 中胸骨为几根肋骨提供了附着点。

The surgeon carefully examined the patient's mesosternum for any fractures.  外科医生仔细检查了病人的中胸骨是否有骨折。 | 近义词: gladiolus (in anatomy) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
