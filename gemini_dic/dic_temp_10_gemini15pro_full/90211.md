houndish:/ˈhaʊndɪʃ/| adj.|像猎犬的；卑鄙的，追逐的|Resembling or characteristic of a hound;  meanly inquisitive or prying;  given to pursuing relentlessly.|He had a houndish look, sniffing around for gossip. 他一脸猎犬似的表情，四处打探八卦。|Synonyms:dogged, relentless, pursuing, inquisitive, prying|Antonyms:apathetic, indifferent, uninterested|adjective
