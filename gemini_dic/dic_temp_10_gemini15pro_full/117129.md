PHRASE->
meander through: 漫步穿过 / 蜿蜒穿过  Meander through the streets of the old town. 漫步在老城的街道上。
meander along: 沿着…蜿蜒  The path meandered along the riverbank. 小路沿着河岸蜿蜒前行。
meander into:  漫步进入 / 蜿蜒进入  The conversation meandered into a discussion about politics.  谈话不知不觉地变成了对政治的讨论。
meander around:  漫步于 / 蜿蜒于  We meandered around the park for an hour. 我们在公园里漫步了一个小时。
meander off topic/subject:  离题  The speaker meandered off topic several times. 演讲者几次离题。


词源: From Greek *maiandros*, name of a winding river in Phrygia (now the Menderes in Turkey). 来自希腊语 *maiandros*，是弗里吉亚一条蜿蜒河流的名称（现在是土耳其的曼德雷斯河）。
