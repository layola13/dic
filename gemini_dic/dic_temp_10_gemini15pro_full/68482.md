family:/ˈfæmɪli/ (US) /ˈfæməli/ (UK)| n.|家庭，家人，家族|A group of people related by blood, marriage, or adoption, typically living together or not.|例句：My family is very important to me. 我的家庭对我来说非常重要。

例句：The whole family gathered for Christmas. 全家都聚在一起过圣诞节。|近义词：household, kin, relatives|反义词:stranger, foe|noun
