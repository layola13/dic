letterers: /ˈlɛtərərz/
noun
题写者；写字的人；印字员
People who draw, paint, or inscribe letters, especially in a decorative or artistic way.
The letterers carefully hand-painted the store's name onto the window.  题字员小心翼翼地在橱窗上手工绘制了商店的名字。
The letterers worked meticulously to ensure the calligraphy was flawless.  印字员一丝不苟地工作，以确保书法完美无瑕。
