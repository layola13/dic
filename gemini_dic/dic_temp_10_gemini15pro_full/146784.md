overidentifying:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 过度认同 | Identifying with someone or something to an excessive degree, often unconsciously. |  He was overidentifying with the fictional character, blurring the lines between fantasy and reality. 他过度认同了那个虚构的角色，模糊了幻想和现实之间的界限。 | Example: The young boy, lacking a father figure, started overidentifying with his favorite superhero.  这个缺少父爱的男孩开始过度认同他最喜欢的超级英雄。 | Synonyms: over-relating, excessively empathizing | Antonyms: detaching, disassociating | verb

overidentified:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度认同的 | Having identified with someone or something to an excessive degree. |  She felt overidentified with the struggles of the protagonist in the novel. 她对小说主人公的挣扎感同身受。 | Example: He became overidentified with his job, neglecting his family and personal life. 他变得过度认同他的工作，忽略了他的家庭和个人生活。 | Synonyms: overly empathetic, excessively involved | Antonyms: detached, uninvolved | adjective
