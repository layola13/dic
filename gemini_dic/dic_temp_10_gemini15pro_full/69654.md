fence-mending:/ˈfɛnsˌmɛndɪŋ/
| n. | 修补栅栏; 改善关系 |  The act of repairing a fence, often used metaphorically to describe the act of repairing a damaged relationship. |  After their argument, they engaged in some fence-mending.  争吵过后，他们努力和好。 | reconciliation, rapprochement, making amends |  estrangement, alienation | noun

fence-mending:/ˈfɛnsˌmɛndɪŋ/
| adj. |  修补关系的; 旨在和解的 |  Used to describe actions or efforts aimed at improving a damaged relationship. |  He made a fence-mending visit to his estranged brother. 他去拜访了关系疏远的兄弟，以求和好。 | conciliatory, placatory, peacemaking |  divisive, antagonistic | adjective
