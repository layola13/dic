pasteurizing:/ˈpæstʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/ /ˈpɑːstʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 巴斯德消毒，巴氏灭菌 | The process of heating a liquid, such as milk or juice, to a specific temperature for a short period of time to kill harmful microorganisms while preserving its quality. | The milk is being pasteurized to kill any bacteria.  牛奶正在进行巴氏灭菌以杀死任何细菌。| synonyms: sterilizing, disinfecting | antonyms: contaminating | verb


pasteurization:/ˌpæstʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌpɑːstʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 巴斯德消毒法，巴氏灭菌法 | The process of pasteurizing. | Pasteurization helps prevent foodborne illnesses. 巴氏灭菌有助于预防食源性疾病。| synonyms: sterilization, disinfection | antonyms: contamination | noun
