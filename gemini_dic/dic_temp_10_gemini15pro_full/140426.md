okaz:/ˈoʊkɑːz/| n.|オカズ (Japanese, meaning "side dish")|A Japanese term borrowed into English, referring to a side dish, typically served with rice. It can be anything from vegetables, fish, meat, or tofu, and is meant to complement the plainness of the rice.|例句：She prepared several different okaz to go with the rice. 她准备了几种不同的配菜来配米饭。

例句：The okaz at that restaurant are always delicious and varied. 那家餐厅的配菜总是美味又多样。|近义词：side dish, accompaniment, garnish|反义词:main dish, main course|词性:noun
