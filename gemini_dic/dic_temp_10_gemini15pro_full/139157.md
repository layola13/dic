obfuscating:/ˈɒbfʌskeɪtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɒbfʌskeɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|使模糊的，使混淆的，使晦涩的|Making something unclear, confusing, or difficult to understand.|The company's financial reports were obfuscating, making it difficult for investors to understand its true performance. 该公司的财务报告令人费解，使投资者难以了解其真实业绩。|Synonyms: confusing, bewildering, obscuring, concealing, veiling|Antonyms: clarifying, elucidating, illuminating, simplifying|adjective

obfuscate:/ˈɒbfʌskeɪt/ (US) /ˈɒbfʌskeɪt/ (UK)| v.|使模糊，使混淆，使晦涩|To make something unclear, confusing, or difficult to understand.|The lawyer deliberately obfuscated the issue with complex legal jargon. 律师故意用复杂的法律术语混淆问题。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, obscure, conceal, veil|Antonyms: clarify, elucidate, illuminate, simplify|verb
