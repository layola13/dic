legalities:/liːˈɡælɪtiz/
n.
合法性，法律程序，法律手续
the rules and regulations related to the law
例句：
The legalities of the merger are still being reviewed.
合并的法律程序仍在审查中。

例句：
We need to ensure all the legalities are covered before we proceed.
我们需要确保在进行之前涵盖所有法律手续。

近义词：legal aspects, legal requirements, legal formalities
反义词：illegalities

词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源: From "legality" + "-ies" (plural suffix). "Legality" comes from "legal" (from Old French "legal," ultimately from Latin "lex," meaning "law") + "-ity" (suffix forming abstract nouns).
