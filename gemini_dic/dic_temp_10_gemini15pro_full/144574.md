outwards:/ˈaʊtwədz/ (UK) /ˈaʊtwərdz/ (US)| adv., adj.|向外地，外面的|Moving or directed away from a center or inner point; relating to the outside or exterior.|例句：The door opens outwards. 这扇门向外开。|近义词：outward, externally, outside|反义词:inwards, internally, inside|词性:副词，形容词

outward:/ˈaʊtwəd/ (UK) /ˈaʊtwərd/ (US)| adj., adv., n.|外面的，向外的，外表|Relating to the outside or exterior; moving or directed away from a center or inner point; external appearance.|例句：The outward appearance of the building was impressive. 这栋建筑的外观很 impressive。|近义词：external, outer, outside|反义词: inward, inner, inside|词性:形容词，副词，名词


词源：From Old English *ūtweard, from ūt (out) + -weard (toward).
