hachure:/ˈhæʃər/ | n. | 阴影线，剖面线 |  A series of short parallel lines used in drawing and engraving to indicate shading or texture. | The architect used hachures to represent the different elevations on the map.  建筑师用阴影线来表示地图上的不同海拔。| synonym: shading, cross-hatching | antonym: blank, unshaded | noun
hachure:/ˈhæʃər/ | v. | 用阴影线表示 | To shade or mark with hachures. | He hachured the slopes of the mountain on the drawing. 他在图纸上用阴影线标注了山坡。| synonym: shade, cross-hatch | antonym: erase, clear | verb
