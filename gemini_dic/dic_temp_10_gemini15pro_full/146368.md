overfloat:/ˈoʊvərˌfloʊt/
n.|过剩，溢出; 浮动超额股本|An excess amount of something, especially shares or money. |The overfloat of shares on the market depressed prices.  市场上股票的过剩压低了价格。|
近义词：excess, surplus, overflow|反义词: shortage, deficit, lack|词性:名词

overfloat:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊt/
v.|溢出，泛滥; 超额发行(股票等)|To flood or overflow; to issue an excessive amount of (shares, etc.).|The river overfloated its banks after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水溢出了河岸。
The company overfloated its shares, leading to a drop in price. 该公司超额发行股票，导致股价下跌。|近义词：overflow, inundate, glut|反义词: undersell, undersupply|词性:动词
