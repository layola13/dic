fragrantness:/ˈfreɪɡrəntnəs/| n.|芬芳，香气|The fragrantness of the flowers filled the air.  花的芬芳弥漫在空气中。|例句：The fragrantness of the roses was intoxicating. 玫瑰花的香气令人陶醉。  The fragrantness of the pine forest was refreshing. 松树林的芬芳令人神清气爽。|近义词：aroma, scent, perfume, fragrance|反义词:odor, stench, stink|词性:名词
Etymology: From fragrant + -ness.  Fragrant comes from the Old French word "fragrant" meaning "sweet smelling," ultimately derived from the Latin "fragrare" meaning "to emit a sweet smell."
