buffy:/ˈbʌfi/| adj.|健壮的，强壮的；浅黄色的|Strong and healthy; having a pale yellowish color. |She has a buffy complexion. 她肤色浅黄。|近义词：strong, sturdy, robust, muscular, tawny, beige|反义词:weak, frail, delicate, pale|词性:形容词

词源:
From buff (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  "Buff" originally referred to a type of strong, flexible leather, often yellowish in color, used for military uniforms and other purposes.  The meaning extended to describe someone with a physique reminiscent of the toughness of the leather, and later also to the color itself.
