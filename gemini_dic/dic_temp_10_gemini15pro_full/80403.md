goloshe:/ɡəˈlɒʃ/ (UK), /ɡəˈlɑːʃ/ (US)| n.|套鞋，橡胶雨鞋|A waterproof overshoe, typically made of rubber.|例句：She wore her goloshes to keep her feet dry in the rain. 她穿着套鞋以防雨淋湿了脚。

例句：The children splashed in puddles wearing their bright yellow goloshes. 孩子们穿着亮黄色的套鞋在水坑里嬉戏。|近义词：overshoe, rubber, Wellington boot|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps sandals or flip-flops for contrasting footwear)|词性:noun
