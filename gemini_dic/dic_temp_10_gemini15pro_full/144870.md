over-read:/ˈoʊvərˌriːd/ | v. | 过度解读，读得过多，过度诠释 | To interpret a text or situation in a more complex or profound way than is warranted; to read too much into something. | He over-read her smile, thinking she was interested when she was just being polite. 他过度解读了她的微笑，认为她对他有意思，而实际上她只是出于礼貌。|  She over-read the instructions and ended up making the cake wrong. 她过度解读了说明书，结果把蛋糕做错了。| 近义词: overinterpret, overanalyze | 反义词: under-read, skim, glance | 词性: 动词
