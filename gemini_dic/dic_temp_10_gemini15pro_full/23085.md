boundness:/ˈbaʊndnɪs/
n.|有限性；界限；约束|The boundness of the resources limited their expansion. 资源的有限性限制了他们的扩张。|例句：The boundness of human knowledge is a humbling concept. 人类知识的有限性是一个令人谦卑的概念。|近义词：limitation, restriction, finiteness|反义词：boundlessness, infinity|词性：名词
Etymology: From "bound" (meaning limit) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bundaną "to bind."
