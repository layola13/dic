expression's:/ɪkˈspreʃənz/ | n. | 表达; 表情; 说法; 词语 |  The plural form of "expression," referring to the ways in which thoughts, feelings, or ideas are shown or communicated.  It encompasses spoken or written words, facial expressions, gestures, and artistic representations. |  Her facial expressions revealed her true feelings. 她的面部表情揭示了她的真实感受。 | synonyms: manifestations, utterances, pronouncements, articulations | antonyms: suppressions, concealments | noun
