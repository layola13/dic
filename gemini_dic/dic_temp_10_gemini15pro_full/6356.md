amphibiological:/ˌæmfɪbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ | adj. | 两栖的，水陆两用的 | Relating to or adapted for both land and water; amphibious. | The frog is an amphibiological animal.  青蛙是一种两栖动物。| The research team developed an amphibiological vehicle. 研究团队开发了一种水陆两用车辆。 | 近义词：amphibious, aquatic-terrestrial | 反义词：terrestrial, aquatic | 词性: 形容词

amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. | 两栖动物，两栖的 |  An animal that lives both on land and in water; relating to or characteristic of an amphibian. | Frogs and salamanders are amphibians. 青蛙和蝾螈是两栖动物。 |  The amphibian nature of the platypus makes it a unique creature.  鸭嘴兽的两栖特性使它成为一种独特的生物。 | 近义词：amphibiological, aquatic-terrestrial | 反义词：terrestrial, aquatic | 词性: 名词, 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek amphi- "both" + bios "life"  + -logy "study"  + -ical  "relating to".  Ultimately meaning "relating to the study of life in both land and water".
