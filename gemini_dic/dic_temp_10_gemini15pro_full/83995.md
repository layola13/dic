half-deified:/ˌhæf ˈdiːɪfaɪd/
adjective
半神化的，被奉为半神的
partly treated or worshipped as a god; raised to a status approaching that of a god.
The ancient Romans half-deified their emperors. 古罗马人将他们的皇帝半神化。
The rock star was half-deified by his fans.  这位摇滚明星被他的歌迷半神化了。
Near synonyms: semi-divine, partly deified, exalted, venerated
Opposite: mortal, human, mundane
word type: adjective
