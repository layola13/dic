forethoughts:/ˈfɔːrθɔːts/| n. |预先考虑；深谋远虑；先见之明|Careful planning or thought about the future; prudent foresight.|例句：Her forethoughts ensured the success of the trip. 她的深谋远虑确保了旅行的成功。

例句：With a little forethought, you could have avoided this problem.  只要事先稍微考虑一下，你就能避免这个问题。|近义词：premeditation, planning, foresight, anticipation, provision|反义词：imprudence, recklessness, afterthought|词性:名词
