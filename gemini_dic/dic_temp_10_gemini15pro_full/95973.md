incumbency:/ɪnˈkʌmbənsi/ | n. |  在职; 现任; 责任; 义务 | The state of holding an office or position; the period during which one holds an office.  It can also refer to something that is incumbent upon someone, i.e., a duty or responsibility. |  His incumbency as mayor ended last year.  他作为市长的任期去年结束了。| synonyms: tenure, term, office, duty, responsibility | antonyms: vacancy, unemployment | noun

incumbencies:/ɪnˈkʌmbənsiz/ | n. | 在职期间; 现任职位(复数) | Plural form of incumbency, referring to multiple periods of holding office or multiple duties/responsibilities. |  He juggled multiple incumbencies, serving as both chairman and CEO. 他同时担任董事长和首席执行官，身兼数职。 | synonyms: tenures, terms, offices, duties, responsibilities | antonyms: vacancies | noun
