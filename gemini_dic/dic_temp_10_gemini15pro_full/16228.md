banqueters:/ˈbæŋkwɪtərz/
n.
宴会宾客，参加宴会的人
People attending a banquet; guests at a feast.
The banqueters were all dressed in their finery. 宴会宾客都穿着华丽的服装。
The banqueters enjoyed the delicious food and lively music.  宴会宾客们享受着美味的食物和欢快的音乐。
guests, attendees, celebrants, diners, partakers
non-attendees, absentees
noun
