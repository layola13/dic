chapfallen:/ˈtʃæpfɔːlən/ | adj. | 沮丧的，垂头丧气的 | Dejected and dispirited; having the lower jaw hanging loosely, as from exhaustion or dejection. |  He looked chapfallen after his team lost the championship. 他在球队输掉冠军后看起来很沮丧。| synonyms: dejected, downcast, crestfallen, disheartened | antonyms: cheerful, elated, jubilant | adjective

Etymology:
From "chap" (jaw) + "fallen."  Originally referred to the slackened jaw of someone exhausted or dejected, later extended to describe the overall appearance and emotional state.
