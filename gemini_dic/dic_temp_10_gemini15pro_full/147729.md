overprivileged:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪvəlɪdʒd/
adjective
过度优越的，享有特权的
Having more privileges than are considered reasonable or necessary.
例句：Overprivileged children often don't appreciate the value of hard work. 过度优越的孩子往往不懂得珍惜努力工作的价值。

例句：He comes from an overprivileged background and has never had to worry about money. 他出身于一个过度优越的家庭，从来不需要为钱发愁。

近义词：privileged, advantaged, affluent, wealthy
反义词：underprivileged, disadvantaged, poor, impoverished
词性: adjective
