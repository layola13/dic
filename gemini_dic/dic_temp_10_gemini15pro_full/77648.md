gelatinousness:/dʒəˌlætɪnəsnəs/
n.|胶状；胶质；粘性|The substance had a strange gelatinousness.|这种物质有一种奇怪的胶状.|近义词：jellylike, viscous, gummy, slimy|反义词:firm, solid, rigid
词性:名词

gelatinous:/dʒəˈlætɪnəs/
adj.|胶状的；凝胶状的|The jellyfish had a gelatinous body.|水母有一个胶状的身体。|近义词：jellylike, viscous, gummy, slimy|反义词:firm, solid, rigid
词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gelatinosus, from Latin gelatus (“frozen, congealed”), past participle of gelare (“to freeze”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“cold”).
