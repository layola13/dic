che:/tʃeɪ/| interj.|切！|An exclamation used to express annoyance, impatience, or disappointment, especially in British English.|Che! I missed the bus again! 切！我又错过公车了！|近义词:damn, blast, bother|反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation of annoyance)|interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you been up to lately? = 你最近在忙什么？
What has she learned from this experience? = 她从这次经历中学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been studying for the past few months? = 过去几个月他一直在学习什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她婚礼上穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? = 他们在看电影前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the time you're 30? = 到你30岁时你会取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the end of the year? = 到年底会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做这件事五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？

注意事项:  (Note: "che" is not commonly used in American English.  Similar exclamations like "damn" or "shoot" are more prevalent.)
