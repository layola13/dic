paise:/pɔɪˈseɪ/ | n. | 派萨（印度、巴基斯坦和尼泊尔的货币单位，等于百分之一卢比）| A monetary unit of India, Pakistan, and Nepal, equal to one hundredth of a rupee. |  He gave the beggar a few paise. 他给了乞丐几派萨。| synonym: cent, penny | antonym: rupee (arguably) | noun
