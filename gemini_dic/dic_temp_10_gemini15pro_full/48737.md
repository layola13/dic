definable:/dɪˈfaɪnəbl/
adj.|可定义的；可下定义的；可界定的|Able to be defined or described clearly. |The boundaries of the property are clearly definable.  这处房产的边界清晰可界定。|The concept of beauty is not easily definable. 美的概念不容易定义。|近义词：describable, explicable, specifiable|反义词：indefinable, vague, ambiguous|词性:形容词
词源: From define + -able.  来自动词define加上形容词后缀-able.
