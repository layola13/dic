malobservation:/ˌmælˌɒbzərˈveɪʃn/
n.
错误观察；观察不准确
An inaccurate observation; faulty observation.
The doctor's malobservation led to a misdiagnosis. 医生观察不准确导致误诊。
近义词：misobservation, misperception, inaccuracy
反义词：accurate observation, correct perception
词性：名词

词源：mal- (bad, wrong) + observation
