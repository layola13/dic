antireduction:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃən/
| n. | 反还原论 | The philosophical position that a complex system cannot be fully explained by reducing it to its simpler constituent parts. |  Antireductionism argues against explaining complex phenomena solely in terms of simpler ones. 反还原论反对仅仅用更简单的现象来解释复杂现象。|  The study embraced an antireductionist approach to understanding consciousness. 这项研究采用了一种反还原论的方法来理解意识。 | holism, organicism | reductionism | noun
