macronutrients:/ˌmækroʊˈnuːtriənts/ | n. |  宏量营养素 |  Nutrients required in large amounts that provide energy (calories). These include carbohydrates, fats, and proteins. |  A balanced diet should include all three macronutrients.  均衡的饮食应该包含所有三种宏量营养素。 | synonyms:  macronutrient, energy-yielding nutrients | antonyms: micronutrients | noun

macronutrient:/ˌmækroʊˈnuːtriənt/ | n. | 宏量营养素 | Any of the nutritional components of the diet that are required in relatively large amounts: protein, carbohydrate, and fat. |  Protein is an important macronutrient for building muscle. 蛋白质是增肌的重要宏量营养素。 | synonyms: macronutrients, energy-yielding nutrients | antonyms: micronutrient | noun
