manducus: /mænˈduːkəs/ | n. | 贪吃的人；暴食者；饕餮 | A glutton; someone who eats greedily and excessively. |  He was a manducus, always piling his plate high with food. 他是个饕餮之徒，总是把盘子堆得满满的。

| Example: The manducus devoured the entire feast in record time.  这个贪吃的人以创纪录的速度吃光了整个宴会。| Synonyms: glutton, gourmand, gorger | Antonyms: ascetic, abstainer | Noun


manducus: /mænˈduːkəs/ | n. | 鬼脸；丑陋的面孔 |  A grimace; an ugly or distorted facial expression. | He made a manducus at the bitter medicine. 他对着苦药做了个鬼脸。

| Example:  The child's manducus showed his displeasure at having to eat his vegetables. 孩子做了个鬼脸，表示他不喜欢吃蔬菜。 | Synonyms: grimace, scowl | Antonyms: smile, grin | Noun
