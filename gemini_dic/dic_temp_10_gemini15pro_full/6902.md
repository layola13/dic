anchormen: /ˈæŋkərmɛn/
n.
新闻主播（尤指男性）;主持人
A man who presents and coordinates a live television or radio program, especially a news program.
The anchorman calmly reported the breaking news.  这位新闻主播平静地报道了突发新闻。
Synonyms: newsreader, presenter, broadcaster, host, emcee
Antonyms: guest, interviewee
noun


anchormen: /ˈæŋkərmɛn/
n.
(尤指电视新闻节目的)男主持人
A man who presents a live television program involving discussions or interviews with guests.
The anchorman introduced the panel of experts. 男主持人介绍了专家组。
Synonyms:  host, moderator, compere, emcee
Antonyms: guest, panelist
noun
