commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | v. | 犯罪，承诺，托付 | to perform an action, typically one that is bad or illegal; to pledge or bind (a person or oneself) to a certain course or policy; to entrust |  He committed a serious crime. 他犯了重罪。 | synonyms: perpetrate, perform, pledge, entrust | antonyms: abstain, refrain, renounce, withdraw | verb

commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | n. | 承诺，保证 | a pledge or undertaking | He made a firm commit to the project. 他对这个项目做出了坚定的承诺。(这个用法较少见，通常用commitment) | synonyms: pledge, undertaking, promise | antonyms:  renunciation, withdrawal | noun
