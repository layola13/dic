morology:/məˈrɒlədʒi/ /məˈrɑːlədʒi/
n.
愚蠢，愚蠢行为，蠢话
The study of foolishness or foolish behavior.  It can also refer to the foolish behavior itself.
His latest investment was an act of sheer morology. 他最近的投资纯粹是愚蠢的行为。
近义词：foolishness, stupidity, absurdity, silliness
反义词：wisdom, prudence, sagacity
词性：noun
