centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfˌyudʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)| n. v.| 离心机；离心分离|A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.|The lab uses a centrifuge to separate blood components. 实验室使用离心机分离血液成分.|近义词：separator, extractor|反义词: None|词性: noun, verb

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfˌyudʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)| v.| 离心分离|To rotate something at high speed in a centrifuge to separate its components.|The technician centrifuged the blood sample.  技术人员对血液样本进行了离心分离。|近义词：spin, separate|反义词: None|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centrum* (center) + *fugere* (to flee).  Meaning "fleeing from the center."
