adolescence: /ˌædəˈlesəns/
n.
青春期
The period of life when a child develops into an adult.
She struggled with the typical anxieties of adolescence. 她与青春期特有的焦虑作斗争。

His adolescence was marked by a rebellious phase. 他的青春期以叛逆阶段为标志。

近义词:puberty, youth, teens
反义词:childhood, adulthood, infancy
词性:名词


adolescences: /ˌædəˈlesənsiz/
n.
青春期（复数，较少用）
Multiple periods of adolescence, though uncommon, can occur in certain social contexts.
Though rare, the anthropologist studied the varying adolescences across different cultures.


近义词:puberties, youths
反义词:childhoods, adulthoods
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin adolescentia, from Latin adolescens, present participle of adolescere "to grow up," from ad- "to" + alescere "to grow," from PIE root *al- (2) "to grow, nourish."
