brocket:/ˈbrɒkɪt/ (US) /ˈbrɑːkɪt/ (UK)| n.|幼鹿（尤指一岁以内的雄鹿）;支架;括号|A brocket is a young male deer, typically less than a year old.  It can also refer to a support bracket or parentheses in punctuation. |The hunter spotted a young brocket in the woods.|Synonyms: fawn, yearling (for the animal), support, brace (for the object), parenthesis (for punctuation)|Antonyms: adult, mature (for the animal)|noun
