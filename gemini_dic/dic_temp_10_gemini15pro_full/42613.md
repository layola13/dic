counterraids:/ˈkaʊntərˌreɪdz/
|n.|反突袭，反击|A military attack undertaken in retaliation for an enemy raid.|The army launched counterraids against the enemy positions. 军队对敌方阵地发动了反突袭。|近义词: counterattacks, reprisals|反义词: raids, attacks|词性: 名词

Etymology:
"counter-" + "raids"

"counter-"  a prefix meaning "against," "opposing," "in return," or "corresponding to," from Old French *contre-, from Latin contra "against, opposite."

"raid" from a Scots word rade or raid "a riding, a hostile or predatory incursion," of uncertain origin.
