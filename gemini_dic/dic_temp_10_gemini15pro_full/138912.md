The correct spelling is "nutrition".  There is no word "nutrication".

nutrition: /njuːˈtrɪʃn/ | n. | 营养; 滋养; 营养学 |The process by which living things receive the food necessary for them to grow and be healthy. | Example: Good nutrition is essential for growing children.  良好的营养对正在成长的儿童至关重要。
| Example: He studied nutrition in college. 他在大学学习营养学。|Synonyms: nourishment, sustenance, diet | Antonyms: malnutrition, starvation | noun
