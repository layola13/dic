graunt:/ɡrɔːnt/ | v. | (尤指正式地)授予，准予; 同意，允许 | Agree to give or allow (something requested) to someone. | The council graunted him permission to build an extension. 委员会批准他扩建房屋。| synonyms: grant, concede, bestow | antonyms: refuse, deny | verb
