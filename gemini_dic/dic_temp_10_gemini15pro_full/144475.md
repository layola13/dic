outthreaten:/ˌaʊtˈθrɛtən/
verb
恐吓，威胁胜过，发出更严重的威胁
To threaten to a greater degree than someone else.
The dictator outthreatened his rivals, promising even more severe consequences for disobedience.  独裁者比他的对手发出更严重的威胁，承诺对不服从行为将施以更严厉的惩罚。
He outthreatened his opponents, vowing to destroy them. 他对对手发出更严重的威胁，发誓要消灭他们。
近义词: intimidate, menace, cow
反义词: appease, conciliate, placate
词性: verb
