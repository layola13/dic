dryly:/ˈdraɪli/ | adv. | 干巴巴地；冷淡地；枯燥无味地 |  In a dry, matter-of-fact, or unemotional manner.  | "That's interesting," she said dryly, clearly not interested at all. “这很有趣，”她干巴巴地说，显然一点也不感兴趣。 | 近义词:  sharply, ironically, sarcastically, curtly | 反义词: warmly, enthusiastically, emotionally, humorously | 词性: 副词

dryly:/ˈdraɪli/ | adv. | 干燥地 |  Literally, free from moisture. (Less common usage) |  The paint dried dryly in the sun. 油漆在阳光下干燥地干了。 | 近义词: aridly | 反义词: damply, wetly | 词性: 副词
