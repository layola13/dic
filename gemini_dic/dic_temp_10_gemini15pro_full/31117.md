chanceably:/ˈtʃænsəbli/
adv.
偶然地；意外地；可能地
By chance; accidentally.  It is possible that something might happen.
It was chanceably discovered during a routine inspection.  它是在一次例行检查中偶然发现的。
The error was, chanceably, caught before it caused any damage.  幸运的是，这个错误在造成任何损害之前就被发现了。
近义词: accidentally, possibly, perhaps, maybe, perchance
反义词: deliberately, intentionally, purposely
词性: 副词
