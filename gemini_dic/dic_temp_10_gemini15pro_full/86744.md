help oneself to: /ˌhɛlp wʌnˈsɛlf tuː/
verb phrase
自助；自取
To serve oneself with something, especially food or drink.
Example: Please help yourself to the buffet.  请自助餐。

Example:  Feel free to help yourself to more coffee.  请随意再喝点咖啡。

Synonym: serve oneself, take, grab, partake
Antonym: wait to be served, abstain

词性: verb phrase
