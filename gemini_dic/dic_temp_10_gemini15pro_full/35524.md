coagulant:/koʊˈæɡjələnt/| n.| 凝结剂，凝固剂 |A substance that causes a liquid to change to a solid or semisolid state.|  The water treatment plant uses a coagulant to remove impurities from the water.  水处理厂使用凝结剂去除水中的杂质。| synonyms: clotting agent, thickener | antonyms: anticoagulant, thinner | noun

coagulate:/koʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/| v.| 凝结，凝固 |(of a fluid, especially blood) change to a solid or semisolid state.| The blood began to coagulate around the wound. 血液开始在伤口周围凝结。| synonyms: clot, thicken, congeal | antonyms: liquefy, thin | verb
