deporters:/dɪˈpɔːrtərz/
n.
驱逐出境者，遣返者
People who deport others; officials who expel foreigners from a country.
The deporters escorted the illegal immigrants to the plane. 驱逐人员将非法移民押送上了飞机。

The government hired more deporters to enforce the new immigration laws. 政府雇佣了更多的驱逐人员来执行新的移民法。
synonyms: expellers, removers, evictors
antonyms: admitters, welcomers, protectors
noun
