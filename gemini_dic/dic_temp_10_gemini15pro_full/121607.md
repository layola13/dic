misorganizations:/ˌmɪsˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 组织混乱，组织不善 | The company suffered from numerous misorganizations, leading to inefficiency and lost profits.  这家公司组织混乱，导致效率低下，利润损失。| The misorganizations within the government led to a delayed response to the crisis. 政府内部的组织混乱导致对危机的反应迟缓。 | 近义词: disorganization, mismanagement, chaos | 反义词: organization, order, efficiency | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "organization" (from Old French "organizacion" and ultimately Latin "organizare," meaning "to arrange, organize").
