detentes:/deɪˈtɑːnt, deɪˈtɑ̃t/
n.
缓和，缓和关系
a period of improved relations between two countries which have been enemies
例句：The period of détente between the United States and the Soviet Union began in the early 1970s.  美国和苏联之间的缓和时期始于20世纪70年代初。

例句：The recent détente between the two countries has led to increased trade.  两国最近的缓和关系导致了贸易的增长。

近义词：rapprochement, reconciliation, easing of tensions
反义词：hostility, animosity, conflict
词性: 名词

词源：From French détente, from détendre "to relax," from Latin destendere "to stretch out, loosen."
