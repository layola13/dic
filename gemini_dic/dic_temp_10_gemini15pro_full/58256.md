drumly:/ˈdrʌmli/ | adv. | 沉闷地；单调地；像鼓声一样 | In a dull, monotonous, or drum-like manner.  | The rain drummed drumly against the windowpanes. 雨沉闷地敲打着窗玻璃。 | Near Antonyms: melodically, rhythmically | Far Antonyms: silently, quietly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From drum + -ly.  The suffix "-ly" is commonly used to form adverbs from adjectives.  "Drum" itself comes from Middle Dutch *dromme or Middle Low German *trumme, ultimately of imitative origin.
