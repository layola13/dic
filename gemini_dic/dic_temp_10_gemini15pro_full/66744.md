exorcize:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛksərˌsaɪz/| vt.|驱魔；除去（坏念头等）|To expel (an evil spirit) from a person or place by means of a ritual; to free from evil spirits. To remove or get rid of something troublesome or undesirable.|The priest exorcized the house.  神父为房子驱魔。

The therapist helped her exorcize the demons of her past.  治疗师帮助她驱除了过去的阴影。|近义词：expel, cast out, banish, purge|反义词: invite, welcome|词性: verb
