industrialising:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
工业化
The process of developing industries in a country or region on a wide scale.
Many developing countries are industrialising rapidly. 许多发展中国家正在快速工业化。
The government is implementing policies to industrialise the rural areas. 政府正在实施政策，使农村地区工业化。
近义词: developing, modernizing
反义词: deindustrialising, ruralising
词性: verb (present participle)

industrialise:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪz/
