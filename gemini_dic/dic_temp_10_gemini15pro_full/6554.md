amylopsin: /ˌæmɪˈlɒpsɪn/ | n. | 胰淀粉酶 | An enzyme in pancreatic juice that catalyzes the hydrolysis of starch into maltose and dextrin. | Amylopsin plays a crucial role in carbohydrate digestion. 胰淀粉酶在碳水化合物消化中起着至关重要的作用。| Example: Amylopsin breaks down starches in the small intestine. 胰淀粉酶在小肠中分解淀粉。|Synonym: pancreatic amylase, diastase | Antonym: None | Noun
