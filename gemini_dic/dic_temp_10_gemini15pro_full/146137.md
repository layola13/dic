ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix): Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across, over."  Indicates excess or surpassing a limit.
"-empty" (adjective/verb): Old English "ǣmtig," meaning "empty, unoccupied, vacant."  From Proto-Germanic "*amtigas."
