haemocytometer:/ˌhiːməsaɪˈtɒmɪtər (British), /ˌhiməsaɪˈtɑːmɪtər (American)| n. |血细胞计数器|An instrument used for counting blood cells. It consists of a thick glass microscope slide with a rectangular indentation that creates a chamber of precise volume. This chamber is etched with a grid of perpendicular lines.|The lab technician used a haemocytometer to count the white blood cells in the patient's blood sample. 实验室技术员使用血细胞计数器来计算病人血液样本中的白细胞。|Synonyms: hemocytometer, cell counter, blood cell counter|Antonyms: None|Noun
