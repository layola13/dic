claritude: /klærɪtjuːd/ /klɛrɪtud/
n.
清晰，明晰，清楚
the quality of being clear and easy to understand
例句：She spoke with remarkable claritude. 她说话非常清晰。
例句：The claritude of his explanation made the complex topic easy to grasp. 他清晰的解释使得这个复杂的话题易于理解。
近义词：clarity, lucidity, perspicuity, transparency
反义词：obscurity, ambiguity, vagueness, confusion
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French *claritude*, from Latin *claritudo*, from *clarus* ("clear, bright").
