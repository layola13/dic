incorruptible:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptəbl/
adj.
不可腐蚀的；廉洁的；不腐败的
Not susceptible to corruption, especially bribery; upright.
例句：He was known as an incorruptible judge. 他以廉洁的法官而闻名。

例句：She has an incorruptible belief in justice. 她对正义有着坚定不移的信念。

近义词：honest, upright, honorable, principled, righteous
反义词：corrupt, dishonest, venal, unscrupulous
词性：形容词


incorruptibility:/ˌɪnkəˌrʌptəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可腐蚀性；廉洁；不腐败性
The quality of being incorruptible.
例句：The incorruptibility of the judge was praised by all.  法官的廉洁受到所有人的称赞。

例句：The organization is known for its incorruptibility and transparency. 该组织以其廉洁和透明度而闻名。

近义词：honesty, integrity, probity
反义词：corruption, dishonesty, venality
词性：名词


词源:
From Late Latin *incorruptibilis, from Latin in- ‘not’ + corruptibilis ‘corruptible’.
