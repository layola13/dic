heusler:/ˈhɔɪslər/| adj. n.| 豪斯勒合金（的）|A Heusler compound is a type of intermetallic compound containing a ferromagnetic element, a group IIIA or IVA element, and a group VA or VIA element. Heusler alloys are known for their diverse magnetic properties, making them useful in a variety of applications. |This material is a Heusler alloy. 这种材料是一种豪斯勒合金。|Synonyms:  intermetallic, ferromagnetic (when exhibiting ferromagnetism)|Antonyms: non-magnetic, paramagnetic (when exhibiting paramagnetism)|adjective, noun
