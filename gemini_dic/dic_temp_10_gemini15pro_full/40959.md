cooperatively:/koʊˈɑːpərətɪvli/
adverb
合作地；协作地
in a cooperative manner; working together toward a common goal.
The team worked cooperatively to finish the project.  团队合作完成了这个项目。
The children played cooperatively, sharing their toys. 孩子们一起玩，分享他们的玩具。
近义词:jointly, collaboratively, together, harmoniously
反义词:independently, separately, alone, competitively
词性:adverb

词源: From cooperate + -ive + -ly.  Cooperate comes from co- (together) + operari (to work).
