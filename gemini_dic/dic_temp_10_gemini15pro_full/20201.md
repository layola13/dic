birdcall:/ˈbɜːrdˌkɔːl/ (US), /ˈbɜːdˌkɔːl/ (UK)| n. |鸟叫声|The distinctive sound made by a bird, often used for communication or mating. |  I woke up to the sound of a beautiful birdcall. 我被一阵优美的鸟叫声吵醒了。|近义词：birdsong, chirp, tweet, warble |反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑silence, quiet) |名词
