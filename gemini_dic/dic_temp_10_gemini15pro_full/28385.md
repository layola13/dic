carbinol:/ˈkɑːrbɪnɒl/ /ˈkɑːrbɪnɔːl/| n. |甲醇; 甲醇基|A carbinol is a term in organic chemistry referring to methanol or a substituted methanol. Specifically, it designates the grouping >C-OH where the carbon atom is attached to three other atoms or groups.|Methanol is the simplest carbinol. 甲醇是最简单的甲醇。| Isopropyl alcohol can be described as dimethyl carbinol. 异丙醇可以被描述为二甲基甲醇。|近义词：methanol, methyl alcohol|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
