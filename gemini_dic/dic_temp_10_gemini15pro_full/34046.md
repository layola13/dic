citylike: /ˈsɪtiˌlaɪk/
adjective
像城市的; 도시 같은 (Korean)
Resembling a city, especially in terms of its characteristics, appearance, or atmosphere.  Having the characteristics or appearance of a city.
The small town had a surprisingly citylike atmosphere.  这个小镇有着令人惊讶的城市氛围。
The sprawling suburb had taken on a citylike density.  不断扩张的郊区呈现出像城市一样的密度。
近义词: urban, metropolitan, citified
反义词: rural, rustic, countrified
词性: adjective
