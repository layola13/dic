downcast:/ˈdaʊnkæst/| adj.|沮丧的，垂头丧气的|Feeling or looking unhappy and discouraged.|He walked with a downcast gaze after hearing the bad news. 他听到坏消息后垂头丧气地走着。|近义词：dejected, depressed, gloomy, sad|反义词：cheerful, happy, optimistic|adjective

downcast:/ˈdaʊnkæst/| v.|低垂（眼睛），使沮丧|To lower (one's eyes or gaze), typically in shame or dejection; to make somebody feel discouraged or depressed.|She downcast her eyes when she was scolded. 她被责骂时低下了眼睛。|近义词：lower, droop|反义词：raise, lift|verb
