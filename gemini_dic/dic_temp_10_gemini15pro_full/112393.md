lost: /lɒst/ (British) /lɔːst/ (American) | adj. | 丢失的，迷路的，丧失的 |  Unable to find one's way; no longer possessed or held. |  I lost my keys. 我丢了钥匙。

lost: /lɒst/ (British) /lɔːst/ (American) | v. | 丢失，迷路，输掉 | Past tense and past participle of "lose".  | He lost the game. 他输了比赛。


lost: /lɒst/ (British) /lɔːst/ (American) | n. | 损失，遗失物 | Something that has been lost. | The lost city was finally found. 失落的城市终于被找到了。 (This usage is less common)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lostian, from Proto-Germanic *lausa-, meaning "loose, free, released."


SYNONYMS->
(adj.) missing, misplaced, vanished, gone, forfeited, irrecoverable
(v.) mislaid, squandered, forfeited


ANTONYMS->
(adj.) found, located, discovered, retained, kept, won
(v.) find, locate, discover, win, gain


USAGE->

Simple Present:
I often lose my phone. 我经常丢手机。
She loses her temper easily. 她很容易发脾气。

Present Continuous:
I am losing my patience. 我正在失去耐心。
He is losing weight. 他正在减肥。

Present Perfect:
I have lost my appetite. 我已经没有胃口了。
They have lost their way. 他们迷路了。

Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been losing sleep lately. 我最近一直失眠。
She has been losing money on the stock market. 她一直在股票市场上赔钱。

Simple Past:
I lost my wallet yesterday. 我昨天丢了钱包。
He lost his job last month. 他上个月丢了工作。

Past Continuous:
I was losing hope when you called. 你打电话的时候我正在失去希望。
She was losing her mind with worry. 她担心得快要疯了。

Past Perfect:
I had lost my keys before I left the house. 我离开家之前就已经丢了钥匙。
They had lost the game by halftime.  他们在半场结束前就输了比赛。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been losing weight for months before the holiday. 假期前我已经减肥好几个月了。
He had been losing money on gambling for years. 他多年来一直在赌博中赔钱。

Simple Future:
I will lose my mind if this continues. 如果继续这样下去，我会疯掉的。
She will lose the election if she doesn't campaign harder. 如果她不更加努力地竞选，她会输掉选举。

Future Continuous:
I will be losing sleep over this. 我会为此失眠的。
They will be losing money if they don't change their strategy. 如果他们不改变策略，他们会赔钱的。

Future Perfect:
I will have lost 10 pounds by next month. 到下个月我将减掉10磅。
He will have lost all his savings by then. 到那时他将失去所有的积蓄。

Future Perfect Continuous:
By next year, I will have been losing weight for a full year. 到明年，我将已经减肥整整一年了。
By the time she graduates, she will have been losing sleep over her studies for four years. 到她毕业的时候，她将因为学业而失眠四年了。
