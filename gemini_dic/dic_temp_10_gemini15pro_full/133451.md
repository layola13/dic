nonfattening:/ˌnɒnˈfæt(ə)nɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈfæt(ə)nɪŋ/
adjective
不增肥的；低脂的
Containing little or no fat, and therefore unlikely to cause weight gain.
This yogurt is nonfattening, so you can enjoy it without worrying about your weight.  这种酸奶不增肥，所以你可以尽情享用，不用担心体重。
These nonfattening snacks are perfect for those watching their calorie intake. 这些低脂零食非常适合那些注意卡路里摄入量的人。
近义词:low-fat, diet, slimming
反义词:fattening, high-calorie
