hymnic:/ˈhɪmnɪk/ | adj. | 赞美诗的；赞歌的 | Relating to or characteristic of a hymn; used in or resembling a hymn. | The congregation joined in singing the hymnic praise. 教众一起唱起了赞美诗。 | The organ played a hymnic introduction.  风琴演奏了一段赞美诗式的序曲。 | 近义词: devotional, liturgical, sacred, praiseful | 反义词: secular, profane | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *hymnos* "hymn" + *-ikos* "-ic."
