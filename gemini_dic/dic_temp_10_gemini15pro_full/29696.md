cathodes:/ˈkæθəʊdz/ (UK) /ˈkæθoʊdz/ (US)| n.|阴极|The negative electrode of an electrolytic cell, battery, or electron tube, toward which positively charged particles (cations) move.|Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode. 电子从阳极流向阴极。|
The cathode ray tube is a vacuum tube containing one or more electron guns, and a fluorescent screen used to view images.  阴极射线管是一个包含一个或多个电子枪和一个用于查看图像的荧光屏的真空管。|
Synonyms: negative electrode, negative pole|
Antonyms: anode, positive electrode|
