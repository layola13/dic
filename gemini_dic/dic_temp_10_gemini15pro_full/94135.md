impedible:/ɪmˈpedɪbəl/
adjective
可阻碍的，可阻止的
able to be impeded or hindered.
The impedible rise of technology is transforming our lives. 科技的不可阻挡的兴起正在改变我们的生活。
近义词:preventable, hinderable, obstruct
反义词:unstoppable, inexorable, irresistible
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *impedibilis, from impedire "to shackle the feet of," from in- "in, into" + pes "foot."
