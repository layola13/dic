conciliatingly: /kənˈsɪliˌeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 调解地，安抚地 | In a manner intended to placate or pacify someone. |  He spoke conciliatingly, hoping to avoid further argument. 他语气和缓地说着，希望避免进一步的争论。 |  The manager addressed the angry customer conciliatingly. 经理安抚了愤怒的顾客。| 近义词: appeasingly, soothingly, placatingly | 反义词: antagonistically, aggressively, provocatively | 词性: 副词

词源: From conciliate + -ing + -ly.  Conciliate comes from Latin conciliare "to bring together, unite in feelings, make friendly," from concilium "council, meeting," from com- "together" (see com-) + calare "to call, summon" (see calendar).
