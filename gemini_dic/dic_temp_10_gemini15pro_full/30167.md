celebrative: /ˈsɛləbreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 庆祝的；欢庆的 | Relating to or expressing celebration.  Marking a happy occasion. | The town held a celebrative parade after their team won the championship.  小镇在他们的球队赢得冠军后举行了庆祝游行。 | 近义词: celebratory, festive, joyous, commemorating | 反义词: mournful, somber, gloomy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "celebrate," which ultimately derives from Latin "celebrare," meaning "to frequent, honor, praise."
