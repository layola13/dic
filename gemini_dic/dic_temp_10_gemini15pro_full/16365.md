barbellulate: /bɑːrˈbɛljʊleɪt/ | adj. | 具小倒钩的；具倒刺毛的 | Having minute barbs or barbules. |  The barbellulate antennae of the moth helped it detect subtle changes in air currents.  飞蛾具倒刺毛的触角帮助它察觉到气流的细微变化。|  barbed, denticulate | smooth | adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *barbellulatus, diminutive of barbellatus ("bearded, furnished with barbs"), from barbula ("little beard"), diminutive of barba ("beard").
