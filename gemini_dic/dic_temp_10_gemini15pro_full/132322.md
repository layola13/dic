nondefinitively:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɪnɪtɪvli/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɪnɪtɪvli/
adverb
非确定地；不确定地；没有定论地
Not definitively; in a way that is not conclusive or decisive.
The results of the study nondefinitively suggest a link between the two variables. 这项研究的结果并非最终确定了两变量之间的联系。
Near Synonyms: inconclusively, tentatively, provisionally, indefinitely
Antonyms: definitively, conclusively, decisively
词性: 副词
