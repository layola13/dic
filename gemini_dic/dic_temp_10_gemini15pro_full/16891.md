basing:/ˈbeɪsɪŋ/
verb
以…为基础；基于…
present participle of base
例句：Basing their arguments on solid evidence, the lawyers presented a compelling case.  律师们以确凿的证据为基础，提出了令人信服的案例。

例句：Basing her decisions on intuition, she often made successful investments. 她凭直觉做决定，经常成功投资。

近义词：founding, establishing, grounding, predicating
反义词：N/A
词性：verb (present participle)

base:/beɪs/
noun
基础；底部；基地
The base of the statue was made of marble.  雕像的底座是大理石做的。

近义词：foundation, bottom, basis, support
反义词：top, apex, summit
词性：noun

base:/beɪs/
verb
以…为基础

近义词：found, establish, ground, predicate
反义词：N/A

base:/beɪs/
adjective
卑鄙的；低劣的；不纯的
He showed base motives in his dealings. 他在交易中表现出卑鄙的动机。

近义词：mean, low, vile, contemptible
反义词：noble, honorable, virtuous
词性：adjective
