effectuate:/ɪˈfɛktʃueɪt/
verb
实施，实现，使生效
To put into effect; to bring about; to cause to happen; to achieve.
The company president effectuated the new policy immediately. 公司总裁立即实施了新政策。
The lawyer effectuated the transfer of ownership. 律师使所有权转让生效。
synonym: execute, implement, achieve, accomplish, realize, complete, fulfill
antonym: hinder, obstruct, prevent, impede
