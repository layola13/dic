arbitrariness:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtrerɪnəs/
n.
任意性；武断；专横
The quality of being based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system.
The arbitrariness of the decision angered many employees. 这个决定的武断性激怒了许多员工。

近义词:
capriciousness, randomness, whimsicality, despotism, tyranny

反义词:
