defensor:/dɪˈfɛnsər/| n.|防御者，拥护者，辩护人|A person who defends someone or something from attack or criticism. |例句：He was a staunch defensor of human rights. 他是人权的坚定捍卫者。

例句：She acted as her brother's defensor in the family dispute.  在家庭纠纷中，她充当了哥哥的辩护人。|近义词：protector, advocate, champion, supporter|反义词：attacker, opponent, critic, aggressor|词性:noun
