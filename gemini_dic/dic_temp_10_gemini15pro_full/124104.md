morsure:/mɔːrˈsʊər/ (US), /mɔːˈsjʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|咬; 咬伤; 咬痕;|The act of biting, or a wound inflicted by biting.  A bite mark can be referred to as a morsure.|The dog's morsure left a deep wound on the man's leg. 狗咬伤在那人的腿上留下了一个很深的伤口。|Synonyms: bite, nip, snap, gnaw|Antonyms: lick, kiss|Noun
