dacoities: /dəˈkɔɪtiz/ | n. | 盗匪行为，抢劫（尤指成群结队的）| Acts of robbery, especially by gangs. | Several dacoities have been reported in the area recently. 最近该地区发生了几起抢劫案。 |  The gang committed a series of dacoities. 这伙匪徒进行了一系列抢劫。 | Synonyms: robberies, heists, burglaries | Antonyms: donations, gifts | Noun

Etymology: From Hindi "ḍakaitī," from "ḍakait" (robber), ultimately from Sanskrit "dakṣiṇāpatha" (southern road), referring to robbers who infested the southern roads.
