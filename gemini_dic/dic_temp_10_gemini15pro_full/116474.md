materialises:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzɪz/ /məˈtɪriəˌlaɪzəz/
verb
实现，出现，实体化
to become real or actual; to appear or become visible
The opportunity she had been hoping for finally materialised. 她一直盼望的机会终于实现了。
Suddenly, a figure materialised out of the fog. 突然，一个身影从雾中出现。
近义词: appear, emerge, manifest, take shape
反义词: disappear, vanish, dematerialize
词性: verb
