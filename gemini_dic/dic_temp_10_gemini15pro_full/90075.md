hostelry: /ˈhɒstəlri/ (UK), /ˈhɑːstəlri/ (US) | n. | 旅馆，客栈 | An establishment that provides lodging, food, and drink, especially for travelers.  A somewhat old-fashioned or literary term for an inn or hotel. |  We stayed at a quaint hostelry in the Cotswolds. 我们在科茨沃尔德住了一家古雅的客栈。|  The weary travelers sought refuge in the nearby hostelry.  疲惫的旅行者在附近的客栈寻求庇护。 | synonyms: inn, hotel, lodge, tavern, public house | antonyms: home, residence, private dwelling | noun
