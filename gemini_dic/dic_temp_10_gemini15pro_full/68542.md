fandom:/ˈfændəm/ (US), /ˈfændəm/ (UK)| n.|粉丝圈，粉丝群体|A group of fans of a particular person, team, fictional series, etc., especially considered as a community or subculture.|例句：The Star Wars fandom is one of the largest in the world. 星球大战的粉丝圈是世界上最大的粉丝圈之一。

例句：She's actively involved in the K-pop fandom. 她积极参与K-pop粉丝圈。|近义词：fanbase, following, supporters|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "detractors" or "critics")|词性:noun
