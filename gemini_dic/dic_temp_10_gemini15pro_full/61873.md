empowerments:/ɪmˈpaʊərmənts/| n. |授权，赋权，增强权能|The process of becoming stronger and more confident, especially in controlling one's life and claiming one's rights.|The government is committed to local empowerments. 政府致力于地方赋权。|Synonyms: authorizations, enablements, commissions|Antonyms: restrictions, limitations|noun

empowerments:/ɪmˈpaʊərmənts/| n. |授权，赋权|The act of giving someone the authority or power to do something.|These new empowerments will enable them to make decisions independently. 这些新的授权将使他们能够独立做出决定。|Synonyms: delegations, warrants, sanctions|Antonyms: prohibitions, denials|noun
