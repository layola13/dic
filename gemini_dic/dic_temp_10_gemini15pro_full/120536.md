miquelet: /ˌmɪkəˈlɛt/ | n. |米克莱特步枪；米克莱特士兵 | A kind of flintlock musket used in Spain and North Africa, especially by irregular troops.  Also refers to a soldier armed with such a musket. | The miquelet fired from the hip, rather than the shoulder.  米克莱特步枪是从臀部而不是肩膀发射的。|  The miquelets were known for their guerilla tactics. 米克莱特士兵以其游击战术而闻名。 |  Synonyms: musket, firearm | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps peace treaty, diplomacy) | noun
