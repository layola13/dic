micro-evolutionary:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri/
|adj.|微观的演化的，微进化的|Relating to small-scale evolutionary changes within a species or population, typically over a short period.|The study documented micro-evolutionary changes in the beak size of finches over a few generations. 这项研究记录了几代雀鸟喙大小的微进化变化。|
micro-evolutionary:|adj.|微观的演化的，微进化的|Characterized by or involving microevolution.|The researchers observed micro-evolutionary adaptations in the bacteria's resistance to antibiotics.  研究人员观察到细菌对抗生素耐药性的微观进化适应。|
micro-evolutionary:|adj.|微观的演化的，微进化的|Of or pertaining to microevolution, the change in allele frequencies that occurs over time within a population.|Micro-evolutionary processes can lead to the development of new traits. 微进化过程可以导致新性状的发展。|
micro-evolutionary:|adj.|微观的演化的，微进化的|Dealing with or exhibiting microevolution.|The researchers observed a micro-evolutionary shift in the population's genetic makeup. 研究人员观察到种群基因组成的微观进化转变。|
micro-evolutionary:|adj.|微观的演化的，微进化的|Concerning the small-scale changes in the genetic structure of a population over a few generations.|The micro-evolutionary changes observed in the insect population were attributed to environmental pressures.  在昆虫种群中观察到的微进化变化归因于环境压力。|
近义词:small-scale evolutionary, intraspecific evolutionary
反义词:macro-evolutionary


词源:
"micro-" (small) + "evolutionary" (relating to evolution)
