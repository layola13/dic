bothies:/ˈbɒθiz/ (UK), /ˈboʊθiz/ (US)| n.|小木屋，避难小屋|Small, simple shelters, usually left unlocked and available for anyone to use, typically found in remote mountainous areas of Scotland, Northern England, Wales, and Ireland.|We stayed in a bothy during our hiking trip through the Scottish Highlands. 我们在穿越苏格兰高地的徒步旅行中住在了一个小木屋里。|Synonyms: huts, shelters, cabins|Antonyms: mansions, hotels|noun
