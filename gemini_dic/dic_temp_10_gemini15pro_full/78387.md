get a handle on:/ɡet ə ˈhændl ɑːn/
idiomatic phrase
控制住，掌握，理解
To understand or control a situation or problem.
例句：I'm finally starting to get a handle on my finances. 我终于开始控制我的财务状况了。

例句：It took me a while to get a handle on the new software. 我花了一段时间才掌握了这个新软件。
近义词：understand, control, grasp, manage, comprehend
反义词：lose control of, misunderstand, be baffled by
词性：短语动词
