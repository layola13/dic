exsanguine:/ɪkˈsæŋɡwɪn/
adjective
血色全无的；脸色苍白的；毫无生气的
Drained of blood; pale; lifeless.
The soldiers found their comrade exsanguine and near death on the battlefield. 士兵们在战场上发现他们的战友脸色苍白，奄奄一息。

近义词: pale, pallid, ashen, bloodless, anemic
反义词: ruddy, flushed, sanguine

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin exsanguis (“bloodless”), from ex (“out of, from”) + sanguis (“blood”).
