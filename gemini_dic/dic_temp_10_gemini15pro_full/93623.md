imbitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/ |v. |使怨恨；使苦恼|To make bitter; to arouse bitter feelings in. |The loss of his job imbittered him toward his former employer. 失业让他对以前的雇主怀恨在心。|Synonyms:  resentful, sour, aggrieved,  disenchanted |Antonyms:  sweeten,  cheer,  encourage, console|Verb

imbitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/ |adj. |充满怨恨的; 苦恼的 (This is a less common usage, derived from the verb form) |He was an imbittered old man, full of resentment towards the world. 他是一个充满怨恨的老人，对世界充满了怨恨。 |Synonyms:  resentful, bitter,  disgruntled |Antonyms:  content, happy,  satisfied|Adjective
