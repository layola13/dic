funiculate:/fjuːˈnɪkjʊleɪt/| adj.|索状的，绳状的|Resembling a cord or rope; consisting of small fibers or filaments twisted together.|The plant has funiculate roots that anchor it to the rocky terrain.  这种植物的根呈索状，使其能够固定在岩石地形上。|例句：The climber used a funiculate rope to ascend the cliff face.  攀登者用一条绳索攀登悬崖峭壁。

例句：The fungus had funiculate strands growing across the log.  真菌的绳状细丝在原木上生长。|近义词：cordlike, ropelike, stringy, fibrous|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *funiculus* (diminutive of *funis* "rope, cord") + -ate (forming adjectives).
