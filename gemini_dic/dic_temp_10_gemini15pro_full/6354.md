amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ /æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. |两栖动物，水陆两栖的|A cold-blooded vertebrate animal of a class that comprises the frogs, toads, newts, salamanders, and caecilians. They are distinguished by having an aquatic gill-breathing larval stage followed (typically) by a terrestrial lung-breathing adult stage. |例句：The frog is an amphibian. 青蛙是两栖动物。

|例句：Amphibian populations are declining worldwide. 全世界的两栖动物数量都在下降。|近义词：frog, toad, newt, salamander|反义词:|n. adj.
