briny: /ˈbraɪni/ (US), /ˈbraɪni/ (UK) | adj. | 咸的，海水的 | Resembling brine; salty.  Characteristic of or suggestive of the sea or ocean. | The briny air invigorated her. 海边的咸空气让她精神焕发。 | The briny depths of the ocean. 海洋的咸水深处。| Synonyms: salty, saline, brackish | Antonyms: fresh, sweet | Adjective
