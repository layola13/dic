conglutinative:/kənˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/
adjective
粘着的；胶着的
Characterized by conglutination; joined together.  A conglutinative language is one in which words are formed by stringing together morphemes without significant changes in the morphemes themselves.
Agglutinative languages, like Turkish and Finnish, tend to have long words formed by joining multiple morphemes. 土耳其语和芬兰语等粘着语倾向于通过连接多个词素来构成长词。
The glue created a conglutinative bond between the two pieces of wood.  胶水在两块木头之间形成了粘着的结合。
Synonyms: agglutinative, adhesive, cohesive
Antonyms: separating, dividing
词性: adjective
