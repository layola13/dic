advertency: /ədˈvɜːrtənsi/
noun
注意, 留心, 专心
The state of being advertent; attentiveness; heedfulness.
His advertency to detail saved the project. 他对细节的注意挽救了这个项目。

He demonstrated advertency to the needs of his students. 他对学生的需要表现出了关切。

Synonyms: attention, heed, mindfulness, awareness, vigilance
Antonyms: inattention, negligence, carelessness, disregard
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *advertentia, from Latin advertens, present participle of advertere (“to turn to”), from ad- (“to”) + vertere (“to turn”).
