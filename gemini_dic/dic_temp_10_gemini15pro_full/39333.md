conjecturer:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərər/ | n. | 推测者；猜想者 |  A person who forms conjectures or makes guesses. |  The scientist, a renowned conjecturer, proposed a new theory about the origin of the universe.  这位科学家，一位著名的推测者，提出了一个关于宇宙起源的新理论。 | synonyms: guesser, speculator, hypothesizer, theorizer | antonyms: knower, expert, authority | noun

conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | v. | 推测；猜想 | To form an opinion or supposition about something on the basis of incomplete information. |  He conjectured that the painting was a forgery. 他推测这幅画是赝品。 | synonyms: guess, surmise, speculate, hypothesize, theorize | antonyms: know, ascertain, confirm | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conjecturator, from Latin conjectus past participle of conicere "to throw together," from com- "together" + jacere "to throw."
