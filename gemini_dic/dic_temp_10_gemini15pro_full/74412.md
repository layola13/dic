franchisable:/ˈfræntʃaɪzəbl/
adjective
可特许经营的
Suitable for being sold as a franchise.
例句：The business model is highly franchisable, with potential for rapid expansion.  这种商业模式非常适合特许经营，具有快速扩张的潜力。

例句：The company is looking for franchisable concepts that can be easily replicated.  该公司正在寻找易于复制的可特许经营理念。

近义词：franchiseable, marketable
反义词：unmarketable

词性: adjective
