oswego:/ɒsˈwiːɡoʊ/ (US), /ɒsˈwiːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|奥斯威戈|A city in north-central New York, USA, situated on Lake Ontario.  It is the county seat of Oswego County.|例句：Oswego is known for its harbor on Lake Ontario. 奥斯威戈以其在安大略湖上的港口而闻名。

例句：We visited Oswego during our trip to New York. 我们在纽约之旅期间参观了奥斯威戈。|近义词：city, port, town|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Onʌda’géga’ (Onondaga language), meaning "pouring-out place," referring to the mouth of the Oswego River.
