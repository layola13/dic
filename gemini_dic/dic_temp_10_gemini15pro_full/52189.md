diarrhoea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (UK), /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |腹泻|Frequent and watery bowel movements.  A symptom of various digestive disorders.|  She had diarrhoea after eating contaminated food. 她吃了被污染的食物后腹泻了。|近义词: the runs, loose stools|反义词: constipation|词性: noun
diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US), /ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (UK)| n. |腹泻|Frequent and watery bowel movements. A symptom of various digestive disorders.| He experienced diarrhea during his trip abroad. 他在国外旅行期间腹泻了。|近义词: the runs, loose stools|反义词: constipation|词性: noun

词源: From Greek διάρροια (diarrhoia), from δια- (dia-) "through" + ῥέω (rheō) "to flow."
