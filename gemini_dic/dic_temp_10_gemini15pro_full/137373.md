nontemperate:/nɒnˈtɛmpərɪt/ | adj. | 非温带的，不温和的 | Not temperate; not moderate or self-restrained.  |  The nontemperate climate of the arctic is not suitable for most plant life.  北极的非温带气候不适合大多数植物生长。 | Synonyms:  intropical, extratropical, immoderate, extreme, excessive | Antonyms: temperate, moderate, mild, restrained | adjective
