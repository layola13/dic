entombing:/ɪnˈtuːmɪŋ/ |v. |埋葬，安葬；禁锢|The process of placing a body in a tomb, or enclosing something as if in a tomb.  |The eruption entombed the entire city in ash. 火山爆发将整座城市埋葬在火山灰下。| 近义词: burying, interring, immuring | 反义词: unearthing, excavating |词性: 动词
