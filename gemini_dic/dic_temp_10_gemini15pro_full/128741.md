niggardliness:/ˈnɪɡərdlinəs/| n. |吝啬，小气|Extreme unwillingness to spend money; stinginess.|His niggardliness was evident in his refusal to leave a tip. 他的吝啬表现在他拒绝给小费上。|近义词：stinginess, parsimony, miserliness, meanness|反义词：generosity, liberality, munificence|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *nigardlinesse, from nigard +‎ -linesse, equivalent to niggardly +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Old Norse *hnǫggr (“stingy”). Cognate with Swedish njugg (“stingy”).
