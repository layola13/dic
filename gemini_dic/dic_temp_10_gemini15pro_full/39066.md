conforming:/kənˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| adj.|一致的，顺从的，符合的|Complying with rules, standards, or laws; behaving according to socially accepted conventions.|例句：Her dress was conforming to the latest fashion. 她的穿着符合最新的时尚。|例句：He felt pressured into conforming to the group's expectations. 他感到压力，不得不顺从团队的期望。|近义词：compliant, obedient, acquiescent, submissive|反义词：rebellious, nonconformist, defiant, individualistic|词性:形容词
conforms:/kənˈfɔːrmz/| v.(third-person singular simple present)| 符合，遵照，顺应 |  Agrees or behaves according to something, such as a rule, standard, or expectation. | 例句：The building conforms to all safety regulations. 这座建筑符合所有安全规定。|例句: His behavior conforms to the expected norms of society. 他的行为符合社会预期的规范。|近义词: complies, obeys, follows|反义词: defies, disobeys, violates|词性:动词
