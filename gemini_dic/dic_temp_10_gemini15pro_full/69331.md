fecund:/ˈfiːkənd/ /ˈfekənd/| adj.|多产的，肥沃的，富饶的|Producing or capable of producing offspring or vegetation in abundance; fertile.|例句：The fecund soil produced a bountiful harvest. 肥沃的土壤带来了丰收。|例句：She is a fecund writer, publishing several novels a year. 她是一位多产的作家，每年出版好几本小说。|近义词：fertile, productive, prolific|反义词：barren, infertile, sterile|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *fecundus*, from *fe*-, related to *ferre* "to bear, carry" + *-cundus*  forming adjectives meaning "inclined to, tending to."  The same root gives us "fertile."
