demi-circle:/ˈdɛmiˌsɜːrkl/ (US), /ˈdɛmiˌsɜːkl/ (UK)| n.|半圆，半圆形|A half of a circle; a shape consisting of half a circle and a diameter.|A rainbow formed a perfect demi-circle in the sky. 彩虹在天空中形成了一个完美的半圆。|
The stage was in the shape of a demi-circle. 舞台是半圆形的。|synonym: half-circle, semicircle|antonym: circle, full circle|noun

demi-:/ˈdɛmi/ (US), /ˈdɛmi/ (UK)| prefix, adj.|半，部分|Indicating half, partial, or lesser in size, degree, or status. (As a prefix)|A demi-god in mythology is half human and half god.  神话中的半神是半人半神。|
She ordered a demi-tasse of coffee. 她点了一小杯咖啡。|synonym: half, semi-|antonym: full, whole|prefix, adjective
