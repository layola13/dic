bitchy:/ˈbɪtʃi/ (US), /ˈbɪtʃi/ (UK)| adj.|刻薄的，恶毒的；暴躁的|Maliciously or spitefully critical; inclined to complain.  A bitchy remark or manner.  Can also refer to a difficult or unpleasant situation.|例句：She made a bitchy comment about her friend's new dress. 她对朋友的新裙子说了一些刻薄的话。

例句：The weather has been really bitchy lately. 最近的天气真是糟糕透了。|近义词：malicious, spiteful, catty, nasty, mean, cruel, vicious,  ill-tempered|反义词：kind, pleasant, agreeable, nice, friendly|词性:形容词

bitchy:/ˈbɪtʃi/ (US), /ˈbɪtʃi/ (UK)| n.|坏女人，泼妇 (Informal and derogatory)|A spiteful or unpleasant woman.|例句：She's such a bitchy to everyone. 她对每个人都那么刻薄。

例句：Don't be such a bitchy!  别这么泼妇！|近义词：shrew, vixen, harpy|反义词：sweetheart, darling|词性:名词 (Informal and derogatory)
