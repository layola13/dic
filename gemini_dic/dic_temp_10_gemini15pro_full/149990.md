palmin:/ˈpɑːlmɪn/| n. |棕榈油；人造黄油|A semi-solid white fat obtained from palm oil, used in cooking and in making margarine.|She used palmin to make the pastry. 她用棕榈油做糕点。|Synonyms:  vegetable shortening, vegetable fat, palm oil shortening|Antonyms: butter, lard|noun
palmin:/ˈpɑːlmɪn/| n. |棕榈人造奶油|A brand of vegetable shortening made from palm oil.|He prefers palmin to butter for baking. 他烘焙时更喜欢用棕榈人造奶油而不是黄油。|Synonyms:  Crisco, vegetable shortening|Antonyms: butter, lard|noun
