glacially:/ˈɡleɪʃəli/| adv.|冰川地；极其缓慢地|Moving or occurring so slowly as to resemble the movement of a glacier.  Resembling a glacier; icy or very cold.  |例句：The project proceeded glacially.  这个项目进展极其缓慢。

例句：He spoke glacially, each word carefully chosen. 他说话极其缓慢，每个字都经过仔细斟酌。|近义词：slowly, gradually, frigidly, icily|反义词：quickly, rapidly, warmly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *glacial* + *-ly*. *Glacial* comes from Latin *glacialis* (“icy, frozen”), from *glacies* (“ice”).
