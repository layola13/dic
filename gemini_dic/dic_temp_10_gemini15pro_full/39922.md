constrained:/kənˈstreɪnd/ | adj. | 受约束的，拘束的，被迫的 | Limited or restricted; forced to act in a particular way. |例句：He felt constrained to accept the offer. 他觉得不得不接受这个提议。|近义词：restricted, limited, compelled, forced |反义词：free, unconstrained, liberated |词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin "constringere" (to bind tightly), from "com-" (together) + "stringere" (to draw tight).
