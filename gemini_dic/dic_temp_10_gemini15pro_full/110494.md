limaçon:/ˈlɪməsɒn/ (US) /ˈlɪməkɒn/ (UK)| n. |蚶线;蜗牛线|A plane curve formed by the path of a point fixed to a circle as it rolls around the outside of a circle of equal radius.  It is a special case of an epitrochoid.|The equation of the limaçon is usually given in polar coordinates.  |Synonym: limaçon of Pascal|Antonym: N/A|noun

limaçon:/ˈlɪməsɒn/ (US) /ˈlɪməkɒn/ (UK)| n. |利马松曲线|一种平面曲线，由一个固定在圆上的点在另一个半径相同的圆外滚动时形成的轨迹。它是外旋轮线的一种特殊情况。|利马松曲线的方程通常用极坐标表示。|近义词: Pascal's limaçon (帕斯卡的蜗牛线)|反义词: 无|名词
