chiasmus: /kaɪˈæzməs/ | n. | 交叉配列法，交错配列 | A rhetorical or literary figure in which words, grammatical constructions, or concepts are repeated in reverse order, in the same or a modified form. |  Example 1: "Ask not what your country can do for you; ask what you can do for your country."  肯尼迪的就职演说：“不要问你的国家能为你做什么，而要问你能为你的国家做什么。”

Example 2: "Poetry is the record of the best and happiest moments of the happiest and best minds."  诗歌是最佳头脑中最快乐时刻的记录，也是最快乐头脑中最美好时刻的记录。 | 近义词: antimetabole, inversion | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

chiasmi: /kaɪˈæzˌmaɪ/ | n. | 交叉 (复数) |  The plural form of chiasma, referring to points where paired chromosomes remain in contact during the first metaphase of meiosis, and at which crossing over and exchange of genetic material occur between the strands.  | Example 1: Chiasmi are essential for the proper segregation of chromosomes during meiosis. 交叉对于减数分裂过程中染色体的正确分离至关重要。

Example 2:  The researchers studied the formation of chiasmi in various species. 研究人员研究了不同物种中交叉的形成。 | 近义词: crossovers | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
