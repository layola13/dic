crooner:/ˈkruːnər/| n.|低吟歌手，柔情歌手|A singer, especially a male singer, who sings slow, sentimental songs in a soft, low voice.|例句：He was a popular crooner in the 1950s. 他是20世纪50年代一位受欢迎的低吟歌手。

例句：The crooner's smooth voice filled the room.  这位低吟歌手柔和的嗓音充满了整个房间。

|近义词：singer, vocalist|反义词:shouter, screamer|词性:noun
