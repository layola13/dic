libretto:/lɪˈbrɛtoʊ/ (US), /lɪˈbretoʊ/ (UK)
n.
剧本；歌词；歌剧脚本
The text of an opera or other dramatic musical work.
The composer wrote the music, but another artist wrote the libretto. 作曲家创作了音乐，但另一位艺术家创作了剧本。
synonyms: text, book, script, words, score
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object. Perhaps "improvisation" if contrasting a written libretto with a spontaneous performance)
noun
