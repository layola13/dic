what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)| pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which, how, who|反义词: (No single antonym)|pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading all afternoon? = 她整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? = 你对他说了什么？
What happened at the party last night? = 昨晚的派对发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed prior to the announcement? = 宣布之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on for the past decade? = 过去十年她一直在做什么？

虚拟语气:
If I knew what he wanted, I would buy it for him. = 如果我知道他想要什么，我会买给他的。
I wish I knew what to do in this situation. = 我希望我知道在这种情况下该怎么办。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the conference? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to think. = 我不知道该怎么想。
She didn't say what time she would arrive. = 她没有说她什么时候到。
