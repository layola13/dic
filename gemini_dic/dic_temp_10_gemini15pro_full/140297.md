ogdoad:/ˈɒɡdəæd/| n.|八人组，八元体，八首诗|A group or set of eight.  Specifically, in ancient Greece, a group of eight deities.  Also, a stanza of eight lines.|The ogdoad of Hermopolis consisted of four pairs of primordial deities.  赫尔墨波利斯的八元神由四对原始神灵组成。|The poet composed an ogdoad in iambic pentameter. 诗人用抑扬格五音步写了一首八行诗。|octet, group of eight|N/A|noun
