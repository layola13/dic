mammals:/ˈmæməlz/ | n. | 哺乳动物 | Animals that are warm-blooded, have hair or fur, and feed their young with milk produced by mammary glands. |例句：Whales and dolphins are marine mammals.  鲸鱼和海豚是海洋哺乳动物。

|例句： Mammals are found in a wide range of habitats.  哺乳动物生活在各种各样的栖息地。| 近义词： beasts, creatures | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑非哺乳动物 non-mammals) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mammālis*, from *mamma* ("breast, udder, teat"), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *méh₂m̥* ("mother's milk").
