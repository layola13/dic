draftee:/ˌdræfˈtiː/
n.
被征召入伍者；应征入伍者
A person who has been drafted into military service.
The young draftee was nervous about leaving home for basic training.  这位年轻的应征入伍者对离开家去接受基础训练感到紧张。
conscript, inductee
volunteer

词性: 名词
