esthetics:/ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ /iːsˈθɛtɪks/ | n. |美学；审美观|The study of beauty and taste, particularly in art.  It explores the nature of art, beauty, and taste, and the creation and appreciation of beauty.|The museum's new exhibit focuses on the esthetics of ancient Greek pottery.  这家博物馆的新展览侧重于古希腊陶器的美学。|Synonyms: aesthetics, beauty, taste, artistry|Antonyms: ugliness, vulgarity|Noun
