hawala:/həˈwɑːlə/ | n. | 哈瓦拉，一种非正式的资金转移系统 | An informal system of transferring money, typically used in the Middle East and South Asia, based on trust and often operating outside of traditional banking systems. It involves a network of brokers who facilitate the transfer of funds through a system of promises and IOUs, rather than physical cash or bank transfers. |  Example:  The businessman used hawala to send money to his family overseas.  这位商人使用哈瓦拉系统向他海外的家人汇款。

Example: Hawala is often used to circumvent banking regulations. 哈瓦拉经常被用来规避银行法规。 | Synonym:  underground banking, informal value transfer system | Antonym:  formal banking system | Noun
