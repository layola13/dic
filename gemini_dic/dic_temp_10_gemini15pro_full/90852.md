hunger:/ˈhʌŋɡər/ (US), /ˈhʌŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|饥饿；渴望；|A feeling of discomfort or weakness caused by lack of food, coupled with the desire to eat.|She fainted from hunger. 她饿晕了。|Synonyms: starvation, craving, appetite, yearning|Antonyms: satiety, fullness|noun

hunger:/ˈhʌŋɡər/ (US), /ˈhʌŋɡə(r)/ (UK)| vi. vt.|感到饥饿；渴望|To feel a strong desire or need for something.|He hungered for her touch. 他渴望她的触摸。|Synonyms: crave, yearn, long for, desire|Antonyms: be satisfied, be content|verb
