chieftainess:/ˈtʃiːftənɪs/ | n. | 女酋长；女首领 | A female chieftain, a woman who is the leader of a clan or tribe. |  The chieftainess led her people through the harsh winter. 女酋长带领她的人民度过了严酷的冬天。|  The chieftainess wisely ruled her clan for many years.  女酋长英明地统治了她的家族很多年。| 近义词：chief, leader, matriarch | 反义词: follower, subordinate | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "chieftain" + "-ess" (a suffix denoting feminine gender). "Chieftain" comes from Old French "chevetain"  (head, captain), ultimately from Late Latin "capitanus" (chief, captain).
