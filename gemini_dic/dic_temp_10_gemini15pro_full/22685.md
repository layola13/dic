bordlode: /ˈbɔːrdloʊd/
n.
喧闹的谈话; 吵闹的聚会
A loud, boisterous conversation or social gathering.
The party devolved into a bordlode of drunken shouting and laughter.  派对变成了醉酒的喊叫和大笑的喧闹场面。
Their regular Friday night get-togethers were always a delightful bordlode. 他们每周五晚上的聚会总是热闹愉快。
近义词: babel, clamor, din, hubbub, racket, uproar
反义词: quiet, silence
词性: 名词

词源:
Likely a blend of "board" (suggesting a table or gathering place) and "lode" (a rich vein or supply, in this case of noise).  Possibly influenced by words like "broadside" (a volley of criticism). The word is considered archaic or dialectal and is rarely used in modern English.
