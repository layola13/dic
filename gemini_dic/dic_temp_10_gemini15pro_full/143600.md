outinfluences:/ˌaʊtˈɪnflʊənsɪz/
|v.| 比...更有影响力; 胜过 |
|The company outinfluenced its competitors in the advertising campaign.|该公司在广告宣传活动中胜过其竞争对手。|
|Synonym: surpass, outweigh, outdo, eclipse|
|Antonym:  be overshadowed by, be outdone by|
|verb|


outinfluences:/ˌaʊtˈɪnflʊənsɪz/
|n.|  更大的影响力（较少用作名词）|
|His outinfluences in the region are undeniable.| 他在该地区的影响力是不可否认的。（这种用法比较罕见）|
|Synonym: dominance, ascendancy|
|Antonym:  subservience, weakness|
|noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
"out-" (prefix): from Old English "ūt," meaning "out, outside, outward"
"influence" (verb/noun): from Middle English "influence," from Old French "influence," from Late Latin "influentia," from Latin "influere" meaning "to flow into," from "in-" (in) + "fluere" (to flow).
