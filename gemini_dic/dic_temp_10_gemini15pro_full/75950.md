funicular:/fəˈnɪkjʊlər/ /fjuːˈnɪkjʊlər/
n.|缆索铁路；缆车|A cable railway, especially one on a mountainside, in which ascending and descending cars are counterbalanced by each other.|We took the funicular up the steep hillside. 我们乘坐缆车上了陡峭的山坡。|Synonyms: cable car, incline railway, mountain railway|Antonyms: None|noun
funicular:/fəˈnɪkjʊlər/ /fjuːˈnɪkjʊlər/
adj.|索状的；绳状的|Of or relating to a funiculus.|The funicular structure of the plant's stem provided support.  这种植物茎的绳状结构提供了支撑。|Synonyms: rope-like, cord-like|Antonyms: None|adjective
