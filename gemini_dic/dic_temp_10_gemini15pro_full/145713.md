overcovetousness:/ˈoʊvərˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/
noun
过度贪婪，贪得无厌
Excessive or inordinate desire for wealth or possessions.
Example: His overcovetousness led him to cheat his business partners. 他的过度贪婪导致他欺骗了他的生意伙伴。
Example: Overcovetousness is a dangerous sin.  贪得无厌是一种危险的罪恶。
Synonyms: greed, avarice, cupidity, rapacity
Antonyms: generosity, contentment, altruism
词性: 名词
