ego-tripping:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 自我陶醉；自我膨胀；自吹自擂 | An act of indulging in self-admiration or fantasies of power and importance. | He was on an ego trip, bragging about his latest conquest. 他在自我陶醉，吹嘘他最近的征服。|  例句：She accused him of being on an ego trip. 她指责他自我膨胀。| 近义词：narcissism, self-aggrandizement, vanity | 反义词：humility, modesty | 词性：名词

ego-tripping:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpɪŋ/ | adj. |  自我陶醉的；自我膨胀的 | Characterized by excessive self-admiration or fantasies of power and importance. |  He had an ego-tripping personality, always seeking attention. 他有一个自我陶醉的性格，总是寻求关注。| 例句：That was an ego-tripping speech he gave. 他的演讲真是自吹自擂。 | 近义词：narcissistic, self-absorbed, vain | 反义词：humble, modest | 词性：形容词
