coactors:/koʊˈæktərz/
noun
共同演员；合作者
Those who act together, especially in a play, film, or television program.  People who participate jointly in an action or process.
The coactors in the new play developed a strong bond during rehearsals. 这部新剧的演员们在排练期间建立了牢固的联系。
The president and his coactors in the peace negotiations worked tirelessly to reach an agreement. 总统和他在和平谈判中的合作者们不知疲倦地工作以达成协议。
synonyms: collaborators, colleagues, partners, associates
antonyms: opponents, adversaries, competitors
