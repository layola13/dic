antiloquist:/ænˈtɪləkwɪst/
n.
腹语表演者
a person who can speak or appear to speak without moving their lips, making it seem as if the voice is coming from somewhere else, such as a puppet
例句：The antiloquist made the dummy seem to come alive with his skillful voice manipulations. 腹语表演者用他熟练的声音技巧使木偶看起来栩栩如生。

例句：The children were amazed by the antiloquist's ability to throw his voice. 孩子们对腹语表演者能够发出不同声音的能力感到惊讶。

近义词：ventriloquist
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists.  One could consider "speaker" or "orator" as a broad contrast, though these aren't true opposites.)
词性: noun
