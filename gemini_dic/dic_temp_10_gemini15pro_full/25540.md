bullweed:/ˈbʊlˌwiːd/| n. |牛舌草; 紫草科植物的一种|A coarse, weedy plant, typically with rough, prickly leaves and small, blue flowers. It is often found in fields and waste places.  It's considered invasive in some areas.|The bullweed has taken over the entire field. 牛舌草已经占领了整片田地。|Synonyms:  anchusa, bugloss|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


bullweed:/ˈbʊlˌwiːd/| n. |霸凌者，恃强凌弱者|A person who bullies others; someone who is aggressive and intimidating.  This meaning is less common than the plant definition.|He acted like a bullweed, pushing smaller kids around. 他像个恶霸一样，把比他小的孩子推来推去。|Synonyms: bully, intimidator, aggressor|Antonyms: protector, defender|Noun
