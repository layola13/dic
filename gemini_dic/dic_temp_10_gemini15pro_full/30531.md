cephalization:/ˌsefələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 头部化 | The concentration of sense organs, nervous control, etc., at the anterior end of the body, forming a head and brain, both during evolution and in the course of an embryo's development. |  Cephalization is a key feature of vertebrate evolution. 头部化是脊椎动物进化的一个关键特征。

Cephalization allows animals to respond quickly to their environment.  头部化使动物能够快速响应环境。 | 近义词: concentration (of nervous tissue) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps decentralization of nervous system) | 词性: noun
