erythrophyllin:/ɪˌrɪθrəˈfɪlɪn/| n. |赤藓红素，紫球藻红素|A red protein pigment found in certain red algae (Rhodophyta), particularly those belonging to the order Porphyridiales. It is involved in photosynthesis. |Example 1: Erythrophyllin is a type of phycobilin, which helps red algae absorb light in deeper water. 赤藓红素是一种藻胆蛋白，可以帮助红藻在更深的水中吸收光线。

Example 2:  Researchers are studying erythrophyllin to understand its role in light harvesting and energy transfer. 研究人员正在研究赤藓红素，以了解它在光收集和能量转移中的作用。 |近义词：phycoerythrin (藻红蛋白 - a broader category)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
