carriage-house:/ˈkærɪdʒ haʊs/
n.
马车房；（改装的）马车房公寓
A building originally used to house horse-drawn carriages, often converted into a dwelling or other use.
The carriage-house has been converted into a beautiful apartment.  马车房已经被改建成一套漂亮的公寓。
