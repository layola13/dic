bludgeon:/ˈblʌdʒən/| v. n.|棒打，用棍棒打；（比喻）威逼，胁迫|To hit someone repeatedly with a heavy object; to force someone to do something by repeatedly making unreasonable demands or by threatening them.|The protesters were bludgeoned by the police. 抗议者遭到警察的殴打。|He bludgeoned her into agreeing to his demands. 他胁迫她同意了他的要求。|Synonyms: club, beat, batter, coerce, bully|Antonyms: caress, soothe, persuade|verb
bludgeon:/ˈblʌdʒən/| n.|棍棒，大头棒|A short, heavy club.|The burglar used a bludgeon to break into the house. 窃贼用一根棍棒闯入房子。|Synonyms: club, cudgel, truncheon|Antonyms: feather, caress|noun
