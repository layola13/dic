grunts:/ɡrʌnts/ | n. | 咕噜声，哼哼声 |  A low, guttural sound, typically made by a pig or a person expressing effort, pain, or displeasure. | The pig emitted a series of grunts as it ate.  猪吃东西时发出了一连串的哼哼声。| synonyms: groans, oinks, growls | antonyms: whispers, murmurs | noun

grunts:/ɡrʌnts/ | v. | 咕哝，哼哼 |  To make a low, guttural sound, typically expressing effort, pain, or displeasure. | He grunted as he lifted the heavy box. 他搬起沉重的箱子时哼了一声。| synonyms: groans, oinks, growls | antonyms: whispers, murmurs | verb
