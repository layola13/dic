divisively:/dɪˈvaɪsɪvli/| adv.|分裂地，引起分歧地|In a way that causes disagreement or division.|The issue of immigration has been debated divisively in recent years. 近年来，移民问题一直备受争议。|近义词：controversially, contentiously, disruptively|反义词：unifyingly, harmoniously|词性:副词

Etymology: From "divisive" + "-ly." "Divisive" comes from the Late Latin "divisivus," meaning "tending to divide," from the Latin "dividere," meaning "to divide."
