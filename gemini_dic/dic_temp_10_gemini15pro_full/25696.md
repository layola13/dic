bunker:/ˈbʌŋkər/| n.|掩体，沙坑，地堡|A reinforced underground shelter, typically for use in wartime. Also, a sand trap on a golf course.|The soldiers took cover in the bunker. 士兵们躲进了掩体。|Synonyms: shelter, dugout, foxhole, sand trap|Antonyms: open field, exposed area|noun

bunker:/ˈbʌŋkər/| v.|躲藏，储藏|To store or hide something safely.|They bunkered down for the storm. 他们躲起来躲避风暴。|Synonyms: store, hide, shelter|Antonyms: expose, reveal|verb
