nurturers:/ˈnɜːrtʃərərz/ | n. |养育者，培育者|People who encourage and support the development of someone or something, often providing care, guidance, and resources.|例句：The teachers are the nurturers of young minds. 老师是年轻心灵的培育者。|近义词：carers, guardians, mentors, supporters|反义词：neglecters, abusers|词性:名词

nurture:/ˈnɜːrtʃər/ | vt. n.|养育，培育；营养物|To care for and encourage the growth or development of.|例句：She nurtured her plants with great care. 她悉心照料她的植物。|近义词：cultivate, foster, raise|反义词：neglect, stifle|词性:动词/名词
