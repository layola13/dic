cartoons: /kɑːrˈtuːnz/ | n. |卡通片，动画片|A film or television programme, especially one for children, that is made by photographing a series of drawings so that they appear to move. |例句：My kids love watching cartoons on Saturday mornings. 我的孩子们喜欢在星期六早上看动画片。

例句：The cartoons in this newspaper are very funny. 这份报纸上的漫画非常有趣。|近义词：animation, comics, animated films|反义词: documentaries, live-action films |词性: noun
