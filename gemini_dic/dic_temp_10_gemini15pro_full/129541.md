non-Bolsheviki:/ˌnɒnˌbɒlʃɪˈviːkiː/ | n. |非布尔什维克派；非布尔什维主义者|  Those who were not members of the Bolshevik party, often including other socialist and revolutionary groups.  Refers to individuals or groups who were not aligned with the Bolshevik faction of the Russian Social Democratic Labour Party, which seized power in the October Revolution of 1917. | The non-Bolsheviki parties were quickly suppressed after the revolution.  革命之后，非布尔什维克党派很快就被镇压了。

The provisional government included many non-Bolsheviki socialists.  临时政府包括许多非布尔什维克的社会主义者。 | 近义词: Mensheviks (sometimes), anti-Bolsheviks | 反义词: Bolsheviks | 词性: Noun
