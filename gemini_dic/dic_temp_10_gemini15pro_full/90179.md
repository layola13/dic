hotpress:/ˈhɒtprɛs/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtprɛs/ (US)| n. |热压机；热压; 热压唱片|A machine used for pressing materials, such as paper, textiles, or wood, under heat and pressure to smooth or glaze them; a vinyl record pressed on high-quality vinyl using superior cutting equipment, often signifying a limited edition or special release.|

例句：
The vinyl was a limited edition hotpress. 这张黑胶唱片是限量版热压唱片。

The hotpress gave the paper a smooth, glossy finish.  热压机使纸张表面光滑有光泽。


近义词：press, mangle (for the machine); limited edition, special release (for the record)|反义词:  (No direct antonym for the machine or record itself)|词性:noun
