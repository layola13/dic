anthropocentricity:/ˌænθrəpəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪti/ | n. | 人类中心主义 | The belief that human beings are the most important entity in the universe.  Considering human beings as the central or most important element of existence, especially as opposed to God or animals. |  Many environmental problems stem from our anthropocentricity. 许多环境问题源于我们的人类中心主义。  |  The film critiques the anthropocentricity of modern society. 这部电影批判了现代社会的人类中心主义。 |  human-centeredness, egotism, humanocentrism |  biocentrism, ecocentrism | noun
