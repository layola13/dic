chabasite:/ˈkæbəˌsaɪt/| n.|菱沸石|A common rock-forming mineral belonging to the zeolite group.  It has a characteristic rhombohedral crystal habit and is known for its ability to act as a molecular sieve.|The chabasite crystals were beautifully formed. 这些菱沸石晶体形态优美。|近义词：heulandite, stilbite|反义词: None|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chabasie*, from Ancient Greek *χᾰβᾰ́σιος* (*chabásios*), a name used by Pliny the Elder for a type of stone.
