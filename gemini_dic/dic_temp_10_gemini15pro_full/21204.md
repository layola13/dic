blockheadedness:/ˌblɒkˈhɛdɪdnəs/ /ˌblɑːkˈhɛdɪdnəs/| n.|愚蠢，迟钝|The sheer blockheadedness of the decision astonished everyone.  这个决定的愚蠢程度震惊了所有人。|例句：His blockheadedness prevented him from seeing the obvious solution. 他的愚蠢使他看不到显而易见的解决方案。

例句：Her blockheadedness led her to make the same mistake repeatedly. 她的愚蠢导致她反复犯同样的错误。

|近义词：stupidity, obtuseness, denseness, doltishness|反义词: intelligence, cleverness, brightness, acumen|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From blockhead + -ness.  Blockhead (meaning a stupid person) comes from block (meaning a solid piece of wood) suggesting a head as thick and unyielding as a block of wood.
