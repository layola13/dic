bandal:/ˈbændəl/ | n. | 帮派，匪帮；(尤指儿童或年轻人的)一伙，一群 | A group of criminals, especially one that is organized.  A group of people, especially children or young people. |  例句：The vandal was caught by the police. 故意破坏者被警察抓住了。| The bandal of youths terrorized the neighborhood.  这群年轻人恐吓了整个社区。 | 近义词：gang, crew, troop, pack | 反义词: individual | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Hindi  बंदाल (bandāl) or Urdu بندال (bandāl), meaning "a gang of robbers," ultimately from Sanskrit बन्ध (bandha, "bond, tie").
