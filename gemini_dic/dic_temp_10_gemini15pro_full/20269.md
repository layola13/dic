birthrate:/ˈbɜːrθreɪt/| n.|出生率|The number of births in a population over a given period, typically expressed as a ratio of live births per 1,000 people per year.|The birthrate in the country has been declining steadily.  这个国家的出生率一直在稳步下降。|Synonyms:  fertility rate, natality, birth rate|Antonyms: mortality rate, death rate|noun
