accesses:/ˈæksesɪz/
noun|访问; 使用; 通道; (情感的) 流露|The system provides quick access to the required information.  该系统提供对所需信息的快速访问。|近义词: entries, approaches|反义词: exits, departures|词性:n.

accesses:/ˈæksesɪz/
verb|访问; 使用; 获取; 到达|She accessed the files on the computer. 她访问了电脑上的文件。|近义词: obtain, retrieve, reach|反义词: block, restrict|词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aces, from Latin accessus ("approach, arrival, entrance"), past participle of accedere ("to approach, arrive"), from ad- ("to, towards") + cedere ("to go, yield").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He accesses the internet daily for news. = 他每天上网看新闻。
She accesses the company database remotely. = 她远程访问公司数据库。

一般过去时:
I accessed my bank account online yesterday. = 我昨天在网上访问了我的银行账户。
The police accessed the suspect's phone records. = 警方访问了嫌疑人的电话记录。

现在完成时:
We have accessed all the necessary documents. = 我们已经访问了所有必要的文件。
They have accessed the server multiple times today. = 他们今天多次访问了服务器。

过去完成时:
After he had accessed the confidential information, he resigned. = 在他访问了机密信息之后，他辞职了。
She had already accessed the files before the system crashed. = 在系统崩溃之前，她已经访问了文件。

将来时:
I will access the report as soon as it's available. = 我会在报告可用后立即访问它。
They will access the building using their key cards. = 他们将使用他们的钥匙卡进入大楼。

虚拟语气:
If I could access the network, I would finish the project. = 如果我可以访问网络，我就能完成这个项目。
I wish I could access the restricted area. = 我希望我能进入限制区域。

被动语态:
The database was accessed by unauthorized personnel. = 数据库被未经授权的人员访问了。
The website is being accessed by thousands of users right now. = 目前有数千名用户正在访问该网站。

疑问句:
How do I access my email account? = 我如何访问我的电子邮件帐户？
When did you last access this file? = 你上次访问这个文件是什么时候？

否定句:
I cannot access the internet without a password. = 没有密码我无法访问互联网。
She did not access the system during the weekend. = 她周末没有访问系统。
