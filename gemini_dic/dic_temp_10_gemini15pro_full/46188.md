cyprine: /ˈsaɪpriːn/
noun
赛普林
Cyprine is a brand name for amino-cyclopentane carboxylic acid, a synthetic non-steroidal anti-androgen used to treat hirsutism (excessive hair growth) in women.
Cyprine can cause side effects such as nausea and fatigue. 赛普林会导致恶心和疲劳等副作用。
Cyprine helped reduce her facial hair growth. 赛普林帮助减少了她面部毛发生长。
synonyms: anti-androgen, androgen receptor blocker
antonyms: androgen
词性: 名词
