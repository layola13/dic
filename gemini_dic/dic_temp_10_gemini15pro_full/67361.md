extensionalisms:/ɪkˈstɛnʃənəlɪzəmz/
|n.|外延主义|The philosophical doctrine that the meaning of a term is determined by the set of things to which it applies.|Extensionalisms in logic focus on the objects a term refers to, rather than its inherent meaning.|近义词: extensionalism|反义词: intensionalism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From extensional + -ism.  Extensional itself derives from extension (from Latin extensio, "a stretching out") + -al.
