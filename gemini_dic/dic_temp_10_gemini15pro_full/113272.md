lutanists:/ˈluːtənɪsts/
n.
鲁特琴演奏家；琵琶演奏者
A person who plays the lute.
The lutanists performed a beautiful Renaissance piece.  鲁特琴演奏家们演奏了一首优美的文艺复兴时期乐曲。

The king employed several lutanists to entertain the court. 国王雇佣了几位鲁特琴演奏家来娱乐宫廷。

Near Synonyms: lute players, musicians
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider non-musicians, or players of other instruments)
