crutching:/ˈkrʌtʃɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
拄拐行走; 支撑; 依赖
The act of walking with crutches or supporting oneself with something.  It can also refer to relying heavily on something for support, either physically or emotionally.
He was crutching around after his knee surgery.  他膝盖手术后拄着拐杖走动。
She's been crutching on that old excuse for years. 她多年来一直依赖那个老借口。
Synonyms:  limping, hobbling, leaning, depending, relying
Antonyms: walking unaided, independent, self-sufficient
