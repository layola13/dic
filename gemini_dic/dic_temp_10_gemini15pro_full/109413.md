leonced: /liˈɒnst/ /liˈɑːnst/ | adj. | 像狮子的；勇猛的 | Resembling a lion, especially in courage or fierceness. | Example: He fought with leonced bravery. 他以狮一般的勇猛作战。 Example:  She had a leonced spirit, unafraid of any challenge. 她有一颗像狮子一样勇敢的心，不惧怕任何挑战。 | 近义词: lionhearted, brave, courageous, valiant, bold | 反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful | 词性: 形容词

词源：From French *leoncé, from Latin *leonītus, from leō (“lion”) + -ītus (suffix forming adjectives).
