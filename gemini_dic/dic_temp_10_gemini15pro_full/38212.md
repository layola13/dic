compunctive:/kəmˈpʌŋktɪv/| adj.|内疚的，悔恨的，良心的不安的| Feeling or expressing remorse or regret; prompted by conscience. |  He felt compunctive about his harsh words. 他对自己的刻薄言辞感到懊悔。|近义词：remorseful, contrite, penitent, repentant|反义词：unrepentant, remorseless|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *compunctus*, past participle of *compungere* "to prick sharply, sting, pierce," from *com-* "together" + *pungere* "to prick."
