half-forgiven:/ˌhæf fərˈɡɪvən/
adj.
部分原谅的，未完全原谅的
partially forgiven; not completely absolved of guilt or blame.
He felt half-forgiven, the tension still lingering in the air. 他感觉自己只被部分原谅了，空气中仍然弥漫着紧张的气氛。
The half-forgiven trespasser was allowed back on the property.  被部分原谅的侵犯者被允许回到该房产。
近义词:partly forgiven, partially absolved
反义词:fully forgiven, completely absolved
词性:形容词

词源:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "forgiven" (past participle of "forgive" - Old English forgiefan -  for- + giefan "to give")
