dandyish:/ˈdændiɪʃ/
adj.
衣着讲究的，花哨的；纨绔子弟的
Resembling or characteristic of a dandy; excessively concerned with or elaborate in dress or appearance.
He wore a dandyish purple suit. 他穿着一套花里胡哨的紫色西装。
近义词：foppish, stylish, dapper
反义词：slovenly, unkempt, scruffy
词性: adjective

dandy:/ˈdændi/
n.
花花公子，纨绔子弟
A man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance.
He was a dandy, always impeccably dressed. 他是个花花公子，总是衣着无可挑剔。
近义词：fop, coxcomb, popinjay
反义词：slob
词性: noun
