overmorally:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəli/
adjective
过于道德的；过分拘泥于道德的
Excessively or unduly concerned with morality; excessively moralistic.
He judged her actions overmorally, failing to consider the difficult circumstances. 他对她的行为进行了过于道德的评判，没有考虑到她所处的困境。
She has an overmorally critical view of modern society. 她对现代社会持有一种过分道德批判的观点。
近义词: moralistic, puritanical, self-righteous, priggish
反义词: amoral, immoral, unethical
词性: adjective

Etymology: "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "morally" (adverb form of "moral")  词源：“over-”（表示过度的词缀）+ “morally”（“moral”的副词形式）
