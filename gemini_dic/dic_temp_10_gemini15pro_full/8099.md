anthroposcopy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒskəpi/
n.
人体观察；人体测量学
The visual examination of the human body for diagnostic purposes.  It involves observing physical features, posture, and movement.
Doctors used anthroposcopy to assess the patient's overall health. 医生运用人体观察来评估病人的整体健康状况。
