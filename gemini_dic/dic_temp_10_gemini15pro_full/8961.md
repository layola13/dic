antihydropic:/ˌæntihaɪˈdrɒpɪk/ | adj. |抗水肿的; 利尿的| Preventing or relieving dropsy (an old term for edema, which is swelling caused by excess fluid in the body tissues). |  This medicine has antihydropic properties. 这种药具有抗水肿的特性。| The doctor prescribed an antihydropic medication to reduce the swelling in her legs. 医生开了抗水肿药来减轻她腿部的肿胀。 | 近义词: diuretic, antiedemic | 反义词: hydropic | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
anti- (Greek) : against, opposing
hydropic (from Greek hydrōpikos) : relating to dropsy (edema)
