claudication: /ˌklɔːdɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |跛行，间歇性跛行|A condition in which cramping pain in the leg is induced by exercise, typically caused by obstruction of the arteries.  由运动引起的腿部痉挛性疼痛，通常由动脉阻塞引起。|例句：He experienced claudication in his left leg after walking for a short distance. 他走了很短一段路后左腿就出现了跛行。|例句：Claudication is a common symptom of peripheral artery disease. 间歇性跛行是外周动脉疾病的常见症状。|近义词：limping, lameness|反义词:mobility, free movement|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *claudicatio, from claudicare "to limp," from claudus "lame."
