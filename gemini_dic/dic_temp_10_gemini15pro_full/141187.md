oological:/ˌoʊəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 动物学的 | Relating to the study of eggs, especially bird eggs. |  He has a large oological collection, containing hundreds of different bird eggs. 他收藏了大量的鸟蛋，包含数百种不同的鸟类。 | 近义词: ornithological (鸟类学的) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "oology" + "-ical."  "Oology" comes from Greek "ōion" (egg) + "-logy" (study of).
