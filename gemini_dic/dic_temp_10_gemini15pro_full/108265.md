launderability:/ˌlɔːndərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可洗性，耐洗性 | The quality of a fabric or garment that allows it to be washed without shrinking, fading, or otherwise being damaged.  | This shirt has excellent launderability and can be machine-washed frequently. 这件衬衫耐洗性极佳，可以经常机洗。 | Synonyms: washability, cleanability | Antonyms: non-washable, delicate | noun


launderability:/ˌlɔːndərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可洗性，耐洗性 | The ease with which a fabric or garment can be washed and retain its appearance and quality. |  The launderability of this silk blouse is questionable; it might be best to dry clean it. 这件丝绸衬衫的可洗性值得怀疑；最好干洗。 | Synonyms: washability, durability (in washing) | Antonyms: non-washable, fragility (in washing) | noun
