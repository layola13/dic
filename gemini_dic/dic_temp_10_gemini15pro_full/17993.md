beget:/bɪˈɡet/| vt.|使产生；引起；生育|To cause something to happen or exist; to procreate, to father a child.|Stress can beget various health problems. 压力会导致各种健康问题。|He begot three sons and two daughters. 他生了三个儿子和两个女儿。| engender, generate, procreate, sire, father | extinguish, prevent | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *begietan (“to get, obtain, acquire, seize, beget”), from be- + gietan (“to get”).  Cognate with Old Frisian bigita (“to obtain”), Old Saxon bigetan (“to receive, acquire”), Old High German bigezzan (“to get, obtain”), Gothic bigitan (“to find”).
