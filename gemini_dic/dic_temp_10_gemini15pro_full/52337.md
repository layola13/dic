dictagraph:/ˈdɪktəɡræf/ /ˈdɪktəɡrɑːf/| n.|录音机，侦听器|A Dictagraph is a device used for recording speech, often secretly. dictaphone:/ˈdɪktəfoʊn/| n.|录音电话，口述录音机|A Dictaphone is a brand name that has become a generic term for a dictation machine, used for recording speech for later transcription.|She used a Dictaphone to record her notes for the book. 她用口述录音机记录了她的书的笔记。|Synonyms: dictation machine, voice recorder|Antonyms: None (brand name/generic term, lacks a direct antonym)|noun
