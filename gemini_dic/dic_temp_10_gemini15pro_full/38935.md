confide:/kənˈfaɪd/| v.|吐露；委托；信赖|To tell something secret or personal to someone whom you trust not to tell anyone else.|She confided her fears to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的恐惧。|Synonyms: reveal, disclose, divulge, share, admit|Antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress|Verb

confide:/kənˈfaɪd/| v.|委托；交托|To place trust (in)|He confided the task to his most competent subordinate. 他把这项任务交给了他最有能力的下属。|Synonyms: entrust, commit, assign, delegate|Antonyms: withhold, retain|Verb
