dimorphic:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪk/
adjective
二形的，二态的
Existing in or occurring in two distinct forms.
The butterfly is dimorphic, the male being yellow and the female white.  这种蝴蝶是二态的，雄性是黄色的，雌性是白色的。

The fungus exists in both yeast and mold dimorphic forms.  这种真菌以酵母和霉菌两种二形形式存在。

synonym:bimodal,  dimorphous
antonym:monomorphic
词性:形容词
