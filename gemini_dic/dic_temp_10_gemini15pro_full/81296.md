granularity:/ˌɡrænjuˈlærəti/ | n. | 颗粒度，粒度 |The level of detail in a set of data; the size of the smallest distinguishable element.  |The database allows for a high degree of granularity in data analysis.  这个数据库允许在数据分析中实现高度的粒度。 |synonyms: detail, precision, fineness, resolution | antonyms: coarseness, generality, vagueness | noun

granular:/ˈɡrænjələr/ | adj. | 颗粒状的，粒状的；详细的 |Resembling or consisting of granules; having a high level of detail. |We need a more granular understanding of the market. 我们需要对市场有更细致的了解。 | synonyms: detailed, precise, fine-grained | antonyms: coarse, general, vague | adjective
