monarchism:/ˈmɒnəkɪzəm/ (US) /ˈmɒnɑːkɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |君主主义；君主政体|A political system in which a state is ruled by a monarch (king, queen, emperor, etc.).|例句：Monarchism is still prevalent in some countries.  君主制在一些国家仍然盛行。

例句：There is a growing movement supporting monarchism in that region.  在那个地区，支持君主主义的运动正在兴起。|近义词：monarchy, royalism|反义词：republicanism, democracy|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *monarchisme*, from *monarque* ("monarch") + *-isme* ("-ism").  *Monarque* from Latin *monarcha*, from Ancient Greek *μονάρχης* (monárkhēs, “sole ruler”), from *μόνος* (mónos, “alone, single”) + *ἄρχω* (árkhō, “to rule”).
