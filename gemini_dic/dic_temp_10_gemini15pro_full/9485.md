antirock:/ˈæntiˌrɒk/ (US) /ˈæntiˌrɒk/ (UK)| n.|防倾杆|A device fitted to a vehicle's suspension to reduce body roll during cornering. |The antirock bar helped to keep the car stable through the tight turns. 防倾杆帮助汽车在急转弯时保持稳定。|The antiroll bar significantly improved the car's handling. 防倾杆显著提高了汽车的操控性。|Synonyms: anti-roll bar, stabilizer bar, sway bar|Antonyms: N/A|noun

antirock:/ˈæntiˌrɒk/ (US) /ˈæntiˌrɒk/ (UK)| adj.|防倾杆的|Relating to or characteristic of an antirock bar. |The antirock system kept the car level during hard cornering.  防倾杆系统使汽车在急转弯时保持水平。|He installed an antirock kit on his car. 他在他的车上安装了一套防倾杆套件。|Synonyms: anti-roll, stabilizing|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
