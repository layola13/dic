innuendoes:/ˌɪnjuˈɛndoʊz/
n.
含沙射影，影射，暗讽
An indirect or subtle, usually derogatory implication in expression; an insinuation.
The politician's speech was filled with innuendoes about his opponent's personal life. 这位政客的演讲充满了对他对手私生活的影射。

近义词:insinuation, implication, suggestion, hint, allusion
反义词:direct statement, explicit statement
