craniota:/kreɪˈniːətə/ | n. | 颅骨动物；有颅动物 | An animal possessing a cranium (skull).  A member of the subphylum Craniata, characterized by a skull enclosing the brain. |  例句：Craniota are a diverse group including fish, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals. 颅骨动物是一个多样化的群体，包括鱼类、两栖动物、爬行动物、鸟类和哺乳动物。

例句：The study of craniota helps us understand the evolution of the nervous system. 对颅骨动物的研究有助于我们了解神经系统的进化。 | 近义词：vertebrate, chordate | 反义词: invertebrate | 词性: noun
