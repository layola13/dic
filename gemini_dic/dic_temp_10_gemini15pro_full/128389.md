new-build: /ˈnjuː bɪld/ (UK) /ˈnuː bɪld/ (US)| n. adj.|新建房屋；新建的|A newly built house or other building.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that has been recently constructed.|

例句：
They've just moved into a new-build on the edge of town.
他们刚搬进了城郊的一栋新建房屋。

例句：
The new-build estate lacks character.
新建的住宅区缺乏特色。


近义词：newly constructed, recently built|反义词:old, existing|词性:noun, adjective
