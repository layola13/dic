forgiveness:/fərˈɡɪvnəs/ /fɔːrˈɡɪvnɪs/| n. |宽恕，原谅|The act of forgiving someone for something they have done wrong. | She asked for his forgiveness. 她请求他的原谅。|
synonyms: pardon, absolution, remission, reprieve|
antonyms: condemnation, punishment, revenge, retribution|
noun
