dulia:/ˈduːliə/ | n. | 敬礼，崇敬 (尤指对圣徒的) | Veneration or reverence given to saints and other holy persons, distinguished from the worship due to God alone. It acknowledges their holiness and seeks their intercession. |  Catholics practice dulia to saints. 天主教徒向圣人行敬礼。|  veneration, reverence, homage | worship, latria | noun
