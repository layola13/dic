obituary:/əˈbɪtʃʊəri/ /oʊˈbɪtʃuəri/
n.
讣告，讣闻
a notice of death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person.
The obituary described her as a "beloved mother and grandmother."  讣告称她是一位“受人爱戴的母亲和祖母”。
The local newspaper published his obituary. 当地报纸刊登了他的讣告。
synonyms: death notice, announcement of death, necrology,  eulogy
antonyms: birth announcement, celebration of life
