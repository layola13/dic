meanderings:/miˈændərɪŋz/
n.
漫游，曲折，迂回
The act of wandering aimlessly or indirectly; a winding path or course.
His memoirs are full of amusing meanderings through his childhood. 他的回忆录充满了童年时期漫游的趣事。
近义词：wanderings, ramblings, digressions
反义词：directness, straightforwardness
词性: 名词

meandering:/miˈændərɪŋ/
adj.
蜿蜒的，曲折的，漫无目的的
Winding or rambling; proceeding in an indirect way.
The river follows a meandering course through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。
近义词：winding, rambling, circuitous
反义词：straight, direct
词性: 形容词
