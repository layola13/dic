maladies:/ˈmælədiz/ | n. | 疾病，弊病 | Diseases or ailments, especially those that are chronic or serious. | The country suffers from a host of social and economic maladies.  这个国家遭受着一系列社会和经济弊病。| 近义词: diseases, ailments, illnesses, sicknesses, disorders | 反义词: health, wellness | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *maladie "illness, sickness," from malade "sick, ill" (12c.), from Latin male habitus "badly held, badly constituted," from male "badly, ill" (see mal-) + habitus "condition," past participle of habere "to have, hold" (see habit (n.)).
