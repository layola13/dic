artiodactyla:/ɑːrtiəˈdæktɪlə/
| n. |偶蹄目动物|An order of hoofed mammals in which the weight of the body is borne equally by the third and fourth toes, such as pigs, hippos, deer, cattle, camels, and giraffes. |Cows and sheep are artiodactyla. 牛和羊都是偶蹄目动物。|Synonyms: even-toed ungulates |Antonyms: perissodactyla (奇蹄目动物)|noun
