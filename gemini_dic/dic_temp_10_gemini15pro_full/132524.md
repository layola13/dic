nondetrimental:/ˌnɒndɛtrɪˈmɛnt(ə)l/
adjective
无害的；不损害的
Not causing damage or harm.
例句：
The development will be nondetrimental to the environment.  这个发展不会对环境有害。

It's crucial to ensure that any changes made are nondetrimental to the project's overall success. 确保任何修改都不会损害项目的整体成功至关重要。

近义词：harmless, innocuous, safe, benign
反义词：detrimental, harmful, damaging
