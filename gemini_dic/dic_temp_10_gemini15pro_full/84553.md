hammercloth:/ˈhæmərklɒθ/ (US) /ˈhæmərklɔːθ/ (UK)| n. |马车的御者座位前挡住车辕的布帘；车座罩布|A cloth covering for the driver's seat of a horse-drawn carriage, especially one used for ceremonial occasions, often highly ornamented.|The antique carriage featured a crimson velvet hammercloth.|Synonyms: coach cloth, carriage cloth|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "exposed seat" in a descriptive sense)|Noun
