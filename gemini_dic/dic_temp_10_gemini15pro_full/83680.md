hagdon:/ˈhæɡdən/ | n. | 海雀，海鸠 | A seabird, specifically a guillemot or auk. |  The cliffs were teeming with hagdons. 悬崖上到处都是海雀。| Synonym: guillemot, auk | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific bird) | noun

词源:
From Middle English *hagdon, likely from Old Norse *höfði (“head”) + *dunna (“down feather”), referring to the bird's distinctive head plumage.
