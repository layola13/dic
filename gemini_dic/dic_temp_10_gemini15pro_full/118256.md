merchandising:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 商品推销；商品销售；促销；销售规划 | The process of promoting and selling goods, especially by their presentation in retail outlets. | Example 1:  The company's merchandising strategy is focused on online sales.  这家公司的销售策略侧重于在线销售。

Example 2: Effective merchandising can significantly increase sales. 有效的商品推销可以显著提高销售额。 | 近义词: marketing, promotion, sales | 反义词: N/A | noun
