decoder:/diːˈkoʊdər/ (US), /diːˈkəʊdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|解码器，译码器|A device or program that converts a coded message into intelligible language.  A person or thing that decodes.|The decoder translated the encrypted message. 解码器翻译了加密的信息。|synonyms: interpreter, translator, decipherer|antonyms: encoder, coder|noun
