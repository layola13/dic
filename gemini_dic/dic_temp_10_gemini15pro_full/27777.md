cannibalization:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 同类相食，自相残杀；（新产品或新服务）蚕食（原有产品或服务）的市场 | The act of reducing sales of one product by introducing a new product that competes with it. |  The introduction of the new model led to the cannibalization of sales of the existing car.  推出新款导致现有车型的销量下降。 | synonyms:  undermining, erosion, encroachment | antonyms:  synergy, cooperation, support | noun
