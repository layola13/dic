frap:/fræp/| v. n.|缠绕，捆扎；冰沙咖啡|To wrap or bind tightly; A blended iced coffee drink.|The sailors frapped the mast to secure it during the storm. 水手们把桅杆捆紧，以便在暴风雨中固定住它。  She ordered a caramel frap. 她点了一杯焦糖冰沙咖啡。|近义词：bind, lash, secure, blended iced coffee, frappuccino|反义词:unbind, loosen|词性:动词,名词
词源:  Likely a shortened form of "frappuccino," a trademarked name for a blended iced coffee drink sold by Starbucks. The verb form likely developed from the image of the ingredients being blended or "frapped" together.
