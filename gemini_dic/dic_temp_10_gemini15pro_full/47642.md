debitor:/ˈdɛbɪtər/ (US), /ˈdɛbɪtə/ (UK)| n.|债务人，借方|A person or entity that owes a debt to another.  Someone who owes money or goods to another.|例句：The debitor is required to repay the loan by the end of the month.  债务人必须在月底前偿还贷款。

例句：The company listed all its debitors and creditors in its annual report.  该公司在其年度报告中列出了所有债务人和债权人。|近义词：borrower, debtor|反义词:creditor, lender|Noun
