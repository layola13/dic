non-extensive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstensɪv/ | adj. | 非广延的，非外延的 | Not extensive; not covering a large area.  Relating to a property that does not depend on the size or extent of the system.  In thermodynamics, it refers to a property that does not scale with the size of the system. |  Entropy is an extensive property, while temperature is non-extensive. 熵是广延性质，而温度是非广延性质。 |  The melting point of a substance is a non-extensive property.  一种物质的熔点是非广延性质。 | 近义词: intensive, local | 反义词: extensive, global | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extensive" (from Latin "extendere," meaning "to stretch out")
