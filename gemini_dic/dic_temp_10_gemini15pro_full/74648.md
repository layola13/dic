free-for-all:/ˌfriː fər ˈɔːl/ | n. |混战，多人乱斗；自由放任|A disorganized or unrestricted situation in which everyone acts according to their own wishes. |The Black Friday sale was a complete free-for-all, with people grabbing items off the shelves. 黑色星期五的促销完全是一场混战，人们从货架上抢东西。|The meeting descended into a free-for-all, with everyone shouting at once. 会议演变成了一场混战，每个人都在同时喊叫。|近义词：brawl, melee, fracas, scrum|反义词：order, organization|词性: 名词

free-for-all:/ˌfriː fər ˈɔːl/ | adj. |自由放任的，无限制的|Open to everyone; unrestricted. |It was a free-for-all buffet, so everyone could eat as much as they wanted.  这是一个自由放任的自助餐，所以每个人都可以想吃多少就吃多少。|The discussion became a free-for-all debate, with no rules or moderation. 讨论变成了一场自由放任的辩论，没有任何规则或主持。|近义词：unrestricted, open, unregulated|反义词：restricted, limited, controlled|词性: 形容词
