livered:/ˈlɪvərd/| adj.|面色苍白的，脸色发灰的；胆怯的，怯懦的|Having a complexion of a grayish-yellow color, like the color of a liver; cowardly or craven.|He looked livered and unwell after his long illness. 他久病之后面色苍白，看起来很不舒服。|近义词：pale, pallid, ashen, cowardly, craven|反义词：ruddy, healthy, brave, courageous|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *liuered*, from *liuer* (“liver”) + *-ed*.  The sense of "cowardly" comes from the ancient belief that the liver was the seat of courage.
