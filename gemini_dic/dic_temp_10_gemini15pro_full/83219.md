gwynned:/ˈɡwɪnɪd/ | adj. | 幸福的，快乐的，幸运的 |  Of Welsh origin, meaning "blessed, fortunate, fair, white, holy."  Derived from the Welsh elements *gwen* ("white, fair, blessed") and possibly *-ydd* (a suffix forming adjectives). It's often used as a given name. |  She led a gwynned life, full of love and laughter. 她过着幸福的生活，充满了爱和欢笑。 | blessed, fortunate, lucky, happy | unfortunate, unhappy, unlucky | adjective
