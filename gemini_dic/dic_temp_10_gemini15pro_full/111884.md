logogriph:/ˈlɒɡəɡrɪf/ | n. | 字谜游戏，文字游戏 | A word puzzle in which a starting word has letters removed to create new words.  A logogriph can also be a puzzle where, by adding, subtracting, or rearranging letters, new words are made from an original word. |  The teacher gave the students a logogriph to solve during free time. 老师在自由活动时间给学生们出了一道字谜游戏。| synonyms: word puzzle, anagram, riddle | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a game type) | noun
