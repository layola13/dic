funeral:/ˈfjuːnərəl/ (US), /ˈfjuːnərəl/ (UK)| n. |葬礼|A ceremony or service held shortly after a person's death, typically including the burial or cremation of the body.|We attended his grandfather's funeral last week. 我们上周参加了他祖父的葬礼。|Synonyms: burial, interment, obsequies|Antonyms: celebration, birth|noun
