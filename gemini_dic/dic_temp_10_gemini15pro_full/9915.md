apepsy: /əˈpɛpsi/
n.
lack of digestive power; indigestion
Lack of digestive power, especially in the stomach; loss of appetite and indigestion.
The patient complained of apepsy and abdominal discomfort. 病人抱怨消化不良和腹部不适。

近义词: dyspepsia, indigestion
反义词: eupepsia (good digestion)
词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *a* (without) + *pepsis* (digestion)
