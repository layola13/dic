PHRASE->
keep off of  =  远离
get off of = 从...下来/离开
fall off of = 从...掉下来
take off of = 从...取下
jump off of = 从...跳下


注意事项:  "off of" is generally considered informal and redundant.  Using just "off" is almost always preferable in formal writing and speech.  While "off of" is common in some dialects, it's generally best to avoid it to maintain clarity and conciseness.
