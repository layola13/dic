It can also be used as a general term for people, often with a slightly negative or dismissive connotation.|例句：Those buggers have parked right across my driveway! 那些讨厌鬼把车停在我的车道上了！

例句：Come on, you buggers, let's get this finished! 快点，伙计们，让我们把这个完成！
