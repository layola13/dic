airport:/ˈɛərˌpɔːrt/ (US), /ˈeəpɔːt/ (UK)| n. |机场|A place where aircraft regularly take off and land, with buildings for passengers to wait in.|
I'll meet you at the airport. 我会在机场和你见面。

The airport was crowded with travelers. 机场挤满了旅客。


近义词：airfield, airstrip|
