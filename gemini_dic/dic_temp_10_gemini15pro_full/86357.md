hectoliter:/ˈhɛktəˌliːtər/ |n.|百升；公石|A hectoliter is a unit of volume in the metric system equal to 100 liters.  It is primarily used for measuring liquids, especially in the context of agriculture and the beverage industry.|The winery produced 500 hectoliters of wine this year. 今年这家酒庄生产了500百升葡萄酒。|Synonyms:  100 liters |Antonyms: N/A|noun
