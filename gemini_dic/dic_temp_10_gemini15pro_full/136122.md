nonreferences:/ˌnɒnˈrɛfərənsɪz/ | n. | 非引用; 不涉及 | Absence of references or citations.  This usually implies that something is not backed up by external sources or evidence. | The report was criticized for its nonreferences, making its claims dubious.  这份报告因没有参考文献而受到批评，使其论断令人怀疑。| Example: The student's paper lacked nonreferences and was therefore considered poorly researched.  这个学生的论文缺乏参考文献，因此被认为研究不足。 |  Synonyms: uncited, unsubstantiated, unsupported | Antonyms: referenced, cited, documented | noun
