a-bomb:/ˈeɪ bɒm/ (UK) /ˈeɪ bɑːm/ (US)| n.|原子弹|An atomic bomb; a nuclear weapon.|The a-bomb was dropped on Hiroshima in 1945.  1945年原子弹投在了广岛。

The US developed the first a-bomb during World War II. 美国在二战期间研制出了第一颗原子弹。|近义词：atom bomb, nuclear bomb, nuclear weapon|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "peace treaty" in a metaphorical sense)|词性:noun
