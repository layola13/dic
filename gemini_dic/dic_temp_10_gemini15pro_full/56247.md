dog-legging:/ˈdɒɡˌlɛɡɪŋ/ | n. |  欺骗；作弊；不正当竞争；（管道等的）弯曲部分 | The practice of using unfair or deceptive methods to gain an advantage, especially in business or competition; a sharp bend or angle in something, like a pipe or a road. |  The company was accused of dog-legging to win the contract. 这家公司被指控为了赢得合同而作弊。 | Synonyms: cheating, swindling, defrauding, sharp bend, kink | Antonyms: fair play, honesty, straight | noun

dog-legging:/ˈdɒɡˌlɛɡɪŋ/ | v. |  欺骗；作弊；不正当竞争；沿弯曲路线行进 | To use unfair or deceptive methods to gain an advantage; to travel along a sharply bending path. | The runner was accused of dog-legging the course to shorten the distance.  这名跑步者被指控为了缩短距离而抄近路作弊。 | Synonyms: cheat, swindle, defraud, meander | Antonyms: play fair, be honest, go straight | verb
