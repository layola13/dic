overcompensatory:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːmpənˈseɪtɔːri/
adjective
过度补偿的，过分补偿的
Going too far in correcting a deficiency or perceived deficiency.  Making more than the necessary adjustment to compensate for something.
His overcompensatory behavior after the divorce led him to buy a flashy sports car.  他离婚后过度补偿的行为让他买了一辆炫耀的跑车。
The government's overcompensatory response to the economic downturn resulted in excessive spending. 政府对经济衰退的过度补偿反应导致了过度支出。
近义词: excessive, extreme, exaggerated
反义词: inadequate, insufficient, undercompensatory
词性: adjective

词源:
From over- + compensatory.  "Over-" indicates "excessive" or "too much." "Compensatory" comes from the Late Latin "compensatus," past participle of "compensare," meaning "to weigh together, counterbalance."
