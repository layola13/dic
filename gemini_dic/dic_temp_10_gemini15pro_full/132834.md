nonedified:/ˌnɒnˈɛdɪfaɪd/
adjective
未被启发的，未受教化的
Not enlightened, instructed, or improved morally or intellectually.
After the lecture, I felt nonedified; it was a complete waste of time.  演讲结束后，我感觉没有受到任何启发；这完全是浪费时间。
The students were left nonedified by the confusing presentation.  学生们对令人困惑的演示文稿感到茫然，没有学到任何东西。
Synonym:unenlightened, unimproved, uninstructed
Antonym:edified, enlightened, improved, instructed
词性:adjective
