alka:/ˈælkə/
noun
碱
A soluble base.
Example: The alka in the solution neutralized the acid. 溶液中的碱中和了酸。

Example:  She used an alka to make soap. 她用碱来制作肥皂。
Synonyms: base, alkali
Antonyms: acid
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Arabic *al-qalī*  "the ashes of saltwort," from which potash, an alkali, was extracted.
