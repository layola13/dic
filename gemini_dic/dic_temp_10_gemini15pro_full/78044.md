genuinely:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪnli/ | adv. | 真诚地，真正地 | In a truthful and sincere way; truly. | She genuinely cared about her students. 她真心关心她的学生。| He was genuinely surprised by the news. 他听到这个消息是真的感到惊讶。| 近义词: sincerely, truly, honestly, authentically | 反义词: falsely, insincerely, dishonestly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From genuine + -ly.  "Genuine" comes from the Latin "genuinus" meaning "innate, native, natural," ultimately from "gignere" meaning "to beget."
