by-law: /ˈbaɪˌlɔː/ (US), /ˈbaɪˌlɔː/ (UK)| n. |地方法规，规章制度|A rule or law made by a local council or other authority to regulate matters within its own area.| The city council passed a by-law prohibiting smoking in public parks.  市议会通过了一项禁止在公园吸烟的地方法规。

Example 1: The housing association has strict by-laws about keeping pets.  该住宅协会对饲养宠物有严格的规定。

Example 2:  The by-law prohibits parking on this street between 7am and 7pm.  该规章制度禁止早上7点到晚上7点之间在这条街上停车。


|近义词：ordinance, regulation, rule, statute|反义词: N/A |词性: noun|
