clerking:/ˈklɜːrkɪŋ/ /ˈklɜːrkiɪŋ/
noun
办事员工作；职员工作；店员工作
The act of working as a clerk.
He started his career with a year of clerking in a small law firm. 他在一家小型律师事务所做了一年的办事员，开始了他的职业生涯。
synonyms: administrative work, office work, paperwork
antonyms: management, executive work
n.

clerking:/ˈklɜːrkɪŋ/ /ˈklɜːrkiɪŋ/
verb (gerund)
担任职员；做店员
Working as a clerk.
She's clerking at the local supermarket to earn some extra money. 她在当地超市做店员赚些外快。
synonyms: working as a clerk, serving customers, performing clerical duties
antonyms: managing, directing
