oligarchize: /ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkaɪz/ | v. | 寡头政治化；使成为寡头政治 | To make into or cause to become an oligarchy.  To concentrate power in the hands of a small group. | The country's political system was gradually oligarchized by a wealthy elite.  The media landscape has been oligarchized by a handful of powerful corporations. | 近义词: monopolize, centralize, consolidate | 反义词: democratize, decentralize | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From oligarchy + -ize.  Oligarchy comes from the Greek *oligarkhía*, from *olígos* (few) + *arkhé* (rule).
