grecianized:/ˌɡriːʃənaɪzd/
adjective
希腊化的
Made Greek in character or style; Hellenized.
The architecture of this building is grecianized, with its iconic columns and pediments. 这座建筑的建筑风格是希腊化的，带有标志性的柱子和山形墙。
The Roman Empire adopted many grecianized customs and traditions. 罗马帝国采用了许多希腊化的习俗和传统。
Near synonyms: Hellenized, Hellenic, Greek, classical
Antonyms: Romanized, Latinized
词性: adjective
