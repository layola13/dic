cop-out:/ˈkɑːpˌaʊt/ (US) /ˈkɒpˌaʊt/ (UK)
cop-out: n.
cop-out: 逃避；推诿；回避
cop-out: An act of avoiding a commitment, responsibility, or confrontation.  A way of escaping something difficult or unpleasant.
cop-out: He promised to help but then copped out at the last minute. 他答应帮忙，但在最后一刻却退缩了。

cop-out: Claiming illness was just a cop-out to avoid taking the test.  声称生病只是为了逃避考试的借口。
cop-out: 近义词: evasion, excuse, dodge, weaseling out
cop-out: 反义词:  commitment, facing (something), tackling (something)
cop-out: 词性: 名词
