bipinnate:/baɪˈpɪneɪt/| adj.|二回羽状的|Twice pinnate; having the primary divisions of a pinnate leaf themselves pinnate.|例句：The fern has bipinnate fronds. 这种蕨类植物有二回羽状的叶子。|例句：Bipinnate leaves are common in many fern species. 二回羽状的叶子在很多蕨类物种中很常见。|近义词：bipinnatifid|反义词：(no common antonym)|词性:形容词
