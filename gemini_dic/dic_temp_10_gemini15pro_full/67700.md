exultancy: /ɪɡˈzʌltənsi/ | n. | 狂喜；欢欣；兴高采烈 | A feeling of triumphant elation or jubilation. |  She felt a surge of exultancy as she crossed the finish line. 她冲过终点线时感到一阵狂喜。

The team's exultancy was evident in their cheers and celebrations.  球队的欢欣表现在他们的欢呼和庆祝中。

| 近义词：elation, jubilation, triumph, rejoicing | 反义词：despair, gloom, despondency | 词性: noun


exultant: /ɪɡˈzʌltənt/ | adj. |  狂喜的；欢欣的；兴高采烈的 | Feeling or expressing great joy or triumph. |  He gave an exultant cry of victory. 他发出一声胜利的欢呼。

Her exultant smile lit up the room. 她欣喜的笑容照亮了整个房间。

| 近义词：joyful, jubilant, triumphant, elated | 反义词：despondent, dejected, gloomy | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exultantia, from exultant-, exultantem, present participle of exultare "to leap up, rejoice," from ex- "out" + saltare "to leap, dance"
