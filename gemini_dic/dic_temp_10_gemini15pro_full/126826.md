naphthalate:/ˈnæfθəˌleɪt/| n. |萘酸盐；萘二甲酸酯|A salt or ester of naphthalic acid.  It is often used in the context of phthalate plasticizers, where the naphthalene ring replaces the benzene ring.|The most common naphthalate used in industry is dimethyl naphthalate.  工业上最常用的萘酸盐是萘二甲酸二甲酯。|Synonyms: naphthalene-dicarboxylate|Antonyms: None|Noun
