glider:/ˈɡlaɪdər/| n.|滑翔机；滑翔者；滑翔物|A glider is a non-powered aircraft that is designed to glide through the air. |The glider soared gracefully through the sky. 滑翔机优雅地在空中翱翔。|The pilot skillfully maneuvered the glider. 飞行员熟练地操纵滑翔机。|近义词：sailplane|反义词：airplane, powered aircraft|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From glide + -er.  "Glide" comes from Old English *glīdan, from Proto-Germanic *glīdaną (“to glide, slide”).
