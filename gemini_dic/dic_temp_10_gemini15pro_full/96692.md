inedibilities:/ˌɪnɛdɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
不可食性；不能吃的东西
the quality or state of being inedible.
Example: The inedibilities of certain wild mushrooms are well-documented.  某些野生蘑菇的不可食性是有据可查的。

Example:  He was surprised by the inedibilities of the fruit, despite its appealing appearance.  尽管这种水果外表诱人，但他对它的不可食性感到惊讶。


近义词:uneatableness, nonedibility
反义词:edibility, palatability
词性:noun (plural)
