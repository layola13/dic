nonheritabilities:/ˌnɒnˌherɪtəˈbɪlɪtiz/ |n.| 非遗传性 |The study focuses on the nonheritabilities of certain traits.  这项研究关注某些性状的非遗传性。|  The nonheritabilities of personality traits are complex. 人格特征的非遗传性很复杂。|Synonyms: non-inheritable traits, acquired characteristics| Antonyms: heritabilities, inherited traits | noun

Etymology:
* non-:  Prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*.
* heritability:  From *heritable* + *-ity*. *Heritable* from *inherit* + *-able*. *Inherit* from Old French *enheriter*, from Latin *inhaereditare* "to appoint as heir," from *in-* "in" + *haereditare* "to inherit," from *haeres* "heir."
* -ity: Suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or condition, from French *-ité* or directly from Latin *-itatem* (accusative singular of *-itas*).
* -s: Plural marker.
