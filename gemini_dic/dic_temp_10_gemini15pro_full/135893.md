nonproximities:/ˌnɒnprɒkˈsɪmətiz/
n.| 非接近性，非邻近性，距离，疏远|The algorithm measures the nonproximities between data points.| 该算法测量数据点之间的非接近性。
|The study focused on the social nonproximities within the community.| 这项研究的重点是社区内的社会距离。
|近义词：distances, remoteness, separations|反义词：proximities, closeness, nearness|词性：名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) : not, absence of (Latin)
proximity (noun): nearness in space, time, or relationship (Latin proximitas, from proximus 'nearest')
-ity (suffix): forming nouns of quality or condition
