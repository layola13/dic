disliked:/dɪsˈlaɪkt/ | v. | 不喜欢，讨厌 | To have a feeling of aversion or disapproval for. |  She disliked the taste of the medicine. 她不喜欢这药的味道。

I disliked his arrogant attitude. 我讨厌他傲慢的态度。

| 近义词: hated, detested, loathed, abhorred | 反义词: liked, loved, adored, cherished | 词性: 动词
