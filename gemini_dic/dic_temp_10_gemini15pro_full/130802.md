nonanthropologists:/ˌnɒnˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒɪsts/
noun
非人类学家
people who are not anthropologists; those outside the field of anthropology.
Nonanthropologists might struggle to understand the nuances of kinship systems in different cultures.  非人类学家可能难以理解不同文化中亲属关系的细微差别。
The article was written for a general audience, including nonanthropologists. 这篇文章是为普通读者写的，包括非人类学家。
synonyms: laypersons, non-specialists, outsiders
antonyms: anthropologists
