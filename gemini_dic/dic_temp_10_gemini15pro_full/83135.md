gutlessly:/ˈɡʌtlɪsli/| adv.|怯懦地，胆小地|Without courage or determination; in a cowardly manner.  Lacking guts; spinelessly.|He gutlessly backed down from the challenge. 他怯懦地退出了挑战。|近义词：cowardly, timidly, cravenly|反义词：bravely, courageously, boldly|词性:副词

词源:

From gutless + -ly.  Gutless meaning lacking courage, from gut (referring to bowels, believed to be the seat of courage) + -less (suffix indicating absence).
