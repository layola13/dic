calamiferous:/ˌkæləˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
带来灾难的；多灾多难的
Producing or involving calamity; disastrous.
The calamiferous storm destroyed homes and businesses. 这场灾难性的风暴摧毁了房屋和企业。
The region experienced a calamiferous earthquake. 该地区经历了一场灾难性的地震。
近义词: disastrous, catastrophic, ruinous
反义词: fortunate, beneficial, auspicious
词性: adjective

calamity:/kəˈlæməti/
noun
灾难；灾祸
An event causing great and often sudden damage or distress; a disaster.
The earthquake was a calamity for the entire region. 地震对整个地区来说是一场灾难。
The flood was a major calamity. 洪水是一场重大灾难。
近义词: disaster, catastrophe, tragedy
反义词: fortune, blessing, boon
词性: noun

-ferous:/fərəs/
suffix
带有…的；产生…的
Bearing or producing something specified.
"coniferous" means cone-bearing. “coniferous”的意思是带有球果的。
"odoriferous" means emitting a smell. “odoriferous”的意思是散发气味的。
近义词: -bearing, -producing
反义词: None
词性: suffix

词源：
From Latin *calamifer, from calamitas "disaster, damage, loss" (see calamity) + -fer "bearing, producing," from ferre "to bear, carry" (from PIE root *bher- (1) "to carry," also "to bear children").
