dislink:/dɪsˈlɪŋk/
verb
断开连接，拆开
to detach or separate something that was previously linked or connected
The company decided to dislink its website from its social media accounts due to privacy concerns. 由于隐私问题，该公司决定将其网站与其社交媒体账户断开连接。
The mechanic dislinked the fuel line before starting the repair. 机械师在开始修理之前断开了燃油管路。
disconnect, detach, unlink, sever
link, connect, join, attach
v.
