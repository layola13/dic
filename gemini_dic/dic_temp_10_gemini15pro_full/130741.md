nonambulatory:/ˌnɒnæmˈbjʊlət(ə)ri/
adjective
不能步行的；非走动的
Unable to walk about; not ambulatory.
Example: The nonambulatory patients require assistance with mobility.  不能步行的病人需要帮助才能活动。
Example:  The hospital wing was designed specifically for nonambulatory patients.  医院的侧楼是专为不能步行的病人设计的。
Near Synonyms: immobile, bedridden, incapacitated
Antonyms: ambulatory, mobile, walking
词性: adjective
