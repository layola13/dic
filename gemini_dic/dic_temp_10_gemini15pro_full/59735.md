eco-geographic:/ˌiːkoʊˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
生态地理的
Relating to both ecological and geographical factors; considering the interrelationships between organisms and their environment, including the distribution of organisms across geographical space.
The eco-geographic distribution of the species is influenced by climate and altitude.  该物种的生态地理分布受气候和海拔的影响。
The study focused on the eco-geographic factors affecting the spread of the disease. 这项研究的重点是影响疾病传播的生态地理因素。
Near synonyms: biogeographic, ecological, geographical
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider conceptually opposite ideas like "ahistorical," "unnatural")
词性: adjective
