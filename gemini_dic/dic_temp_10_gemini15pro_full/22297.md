bongrace:/ˈbɒŋɡreɪs/ /ˈbɑːŋɡreɪs/| n.|意外的收获, 好运|An unexpected piece of good fortune.|Finding that rare coin was a real bongrace. 发现那枚稀有硬币真是意外的收获。|Synonyms: windfall, boon, godsend|Antonyms: misfortune, hardship|noun
