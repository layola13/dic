dementedly:/dɪˈmɛntɪdli/
adverb
疯狂地；精神错乱地
In a way that suggests someone is demented or insane.  Showing signs of madness; insanely.
He laughed dementedly and ran into the street. 他疯狂地大笑，跑进了大街。
Synonyms: madly, insanely, crazily, wildly, frantically
Antonyms: sanely, rationally, calmly, lucidly
词性: 副词
