hypoplastron:/ˈhaɪpəʊˌplæstrən/| n. |腹甲（尤指龟的腹甲）|The hypoplastron is the part of a turtle's shell that covers the belly.  腹甲是龟壳覆盖腹部的部分。|例句：The hypoplastron of this ancient turtle fossil is remarkably well-preserved.  这块古代龟化石的腹甲保存得非常好。

|例句：Researchers studied the hypoplastron to determine the turtle's age. 研究人员研究了腹甲以确定这只龟的年龄。|近义词：plastron (更通用)|反义词: carapace (背甲)|词性:noun
