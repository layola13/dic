clarified:/ˈklærɪfaɪd/| adj.| 已澄清的，已阐明的|Made clear or easier to understand.|The company clarified its position on the merger. 公司澄清了其在合并问题上的立场。|近义词：explained, elucidated, simplified|反义词：confused, obscured, complicated|词性:形容词

clarify:/ˈklærɪfaɪ/| vt.| 澄清，阐明|To make (a statement or situation) less confused and more clearly comprehensible.|Could you clarify your last remark? 你能解释一下你最后一句话吗？|近义词：explain, elucidate, simplify|反义词：confuse, obscure, complicate|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *clarificare, from Latin clarus (“clear”) + -ficare (from facere, “to make”).
