derelict:/ˈderəlɪkt/ (US), /ˈdɛrəlɪkt/ (UK)| adj. n.|被遗弃的，玩忽职守的；流浪者|in a very poor condition because it has been abandoned:  |例句：The derelict building was a haven for stray cats. 这栋废弃的建筑成了流浪猫的避难所。|近义词：abandoned, forsaken, neglected, ramshackle|反义词：maintained, cared-for|词性:形容词

derelict:/ˈderəlɪkt/ (US), /ˈdɛrəlɪkt/ (UK)| n.|流浪汉，被遗弃的人或物|a person without a home, job, or property: |例句：The city shelters are full of derelicts. 城市收容所里挤满了流浪汉。|近义词：vagrant, bum, homeless person|反义词：resident, homeowner|词性:名词
