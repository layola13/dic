figging:/ˈfɪɡɪŋ/
|v.|
塞满无花果; (粗俗) 欺骗; 戏弄|
Filling with figs; (vulgar, chiefly Britain) cheating, fiddling, or trifling with.  Also (vulgar, chiefly Britain) an intensifier used with present participles.|

例句：Stop figging about and get to work! 别再胡闹了，开始工作吧！


近义词：cheating, fiddling, tampering, manipulating, tricking
|
反义词：being honest, being truthful
