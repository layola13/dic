delicater:/ˈdelɪkətər/ | adj. | 更精致的，更微妙的 | More delicate; finer; more subtle.  A comparative form of "delicate". | Her touch was delicater than a butterfly's wing. 她的触摸比蝴蝶的翅膀更轻柔。| The delicater flavors of the wine were lost on him.  这种葡萄酒更微妙的味道他没能品尝出来。| 近义词: finer, subtler, more refined | 反义词: coarser, rougher, cruder | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *delicatere, comparative of delicat, from Old French delicat, from Latin delicatus (“delightful, charming, dainty”), past participle of delicari (“to allure, entice”), from deliciae (“allurements, delights”), from delicio (“to allure”).
