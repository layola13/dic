commiserating:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
表示同情的，怜悯的
expressing or feeling sympathy
She gave him a commiserating pat on the shoulder. 她同情地拍了拍他的肩膀。
近义词：sympathetic, compassionate, empathetic
反义词：unsympathetic, indifferent, callous
词性: adjective

commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/
verb
同情，怜悯
to express or feel sympathy or pity for someone
I commiserated with her over the loss of her job. 我对她的失业表示同情。
近义词：sympathize, console, empathize
反义词：ignore, disregard, gloat
词性: verb


词源：From Latin *commiserārī*, present active infinitive of *commiseror*, from *com-* (together) + *miseror* (I lament, bewail), from *miser* (wretched, unfortunate).
