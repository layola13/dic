insusceptible:/ˌɪnsəˈsɛptəbl/
adjective
不受影响的；不敏感的；无感觉的
Not susceptible; not capable of being affected, influenced, or harmed by something.
He seems insusceptible to colds, he never gets them. 他似乎对感冒不敏感，从来不感冒。
Synonyms: immune, resistant, impervious
Antonyms: susceptible, vulnerable, sensitive
词性: 形容词
