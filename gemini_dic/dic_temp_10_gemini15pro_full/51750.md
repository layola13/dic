devolving:/dɪˈvɒlvɪŋ/ /diˈvɑːlvɪŋ/
verb
下放，移交；退化，衰落
passing on (power, duties, etc.) to a lower level; deteriorating, regressing.
The federal government is devolving more power to the states.  联邦政府正在将更多权力下放给各州。
The situation in the country is devolving into chaos.  该国的局势正在恶化为混乱状态。
近义词:transferring, delegating, deteriorating, worsening
反义词:centralizing, improving, progressing
词性:v.
