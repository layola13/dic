immerse:/ɪˈmɜːrs/ (US) /ɪˈmɜːs/ (UK)| vt. |沉浸，使专心于|To involve oneself deeply in an activity or interest; to submerge something completely in a liquid.|He immersed himself in his studies. 他沉浸在他的学习中。|近义词：submerge, engross, absorb, involve|反义词：distract, remove, superficial|词性:动词

immerse:/ɪˈmɜːrs/ (US) /ɪˈmɜːs/ (UK)| vt. |浸泡|To dip or plunge something into a liquid.|She immersed the fabric in the dye. 她把布料浸泡在染料里。|近义词：submerge, dip, soak|反义词：remove, withdraw|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ymersen, *immersen, from Latin immersus, past participle of immergere (“to dip, plunge, sink, immerse”), from in- (“in, into”) + mergere (“to dip, plunge, merge”).
