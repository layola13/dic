liberators:/ˈlɪbəreɪtərz/
n.
解放者，释放者
People who free others from captivity, oppression, or difficulty.
The liberators were welcomed with open arms by the grateful citizens.  解放者受到感激的市民的热烈欢迎。
The monument honors the liberators of the city.  这座纪念碑是为了纪念城市的解放者。
近义词: rescuers, emancipators, saviors, deliverers
反义词: oppressors, captors, enslavers
词性: noun


liberate:/ˈlɪbəreɪt/
v.
解放，释放
To free someone from captivity, oppression, or difficulty.
The army liberated the prisoners of war.  军队解放了战俘。
She felt liberated after quitting her stressful job.  辞掉压力大的工作后，她感到解脱了。
近义词: free, release, emancipate, rescue
反义词: imprison, enslave, confine, oppress
词性: verb
