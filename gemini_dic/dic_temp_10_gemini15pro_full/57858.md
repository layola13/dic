dreul:/druːl/ | n. |口水, 流口水 |Saliva that flows from the mouth, typically as a result of hunger, desire, or illness.| The dog was dreuling over the bone. 狗对着骨头流口水。 | 近义词: slobber, dribble, drool | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“吞咽” swallow) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *drevelen, frequentative of dreven ‘to drip,’ from Old Norse drafa ‘dregs.’
