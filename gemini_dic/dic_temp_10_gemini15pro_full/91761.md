hyperemotively:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊtɪvli/
adverb
过度情绪化地; 过于激动地
in a hyperemotional manner; with excessive emotion.
She reacted hyperemotively to the news, bursting into tears. 她对这个消息反应过度情绪化，突然哭了起来。
hyperemotionally
unemotionally
