ordinals:/ˈɔːrdɪnəl/ | adj. | 序数的 | Relating to or denoting the order of something in a series. |  The **ordinal** number of the house is 10. 这栋房子的门牌号是10号。| synonyms: sequenced, ranked | antonyms: cardinal | adjective
