bloviator:/ˈbloʊviˌeɪtər/| n. |空谈家，吹牛大王|A person who talks at length, especially in an inflated or empty way.  A pompous or verbose windbag.|例句：The bloviator held the floor for hours, boring everyone with his tedious pronouncements.  这个空谈家滔滔不绝地讲了几个小时，用他冗长乏味的声明让每个人都感到厌烦。

例句：He's known as a bloviator who loves to hear himself talk. 他以喜欢自吹自擂而闻名。|近义词：windbag, gasbag, blowhard, braggart|反义词:  reticent person, quiet person|词性:noun
