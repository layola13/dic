dandelion: /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (US), /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (UK) | n. | 蒲公英 | A common wild plant with bright yellow flowers and notched leaves. The flowers mature into fluffy white seed heads. |  The field was covered with dandelions.  田野里长满了蒲公英。 | synonyms: blowball, lion's tooth | antonyms: N/A | noun


dandelion: /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (US), /ˈdændɪlaɪən/ (UK) | adj.
