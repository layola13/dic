moonraker:/ˈmuːnˌreɪkər/ | n. | 月球探测器；捕月者；（旧时）在月光下工作的农夫 | A person or thing that rakes the moon, or a high-flying sail. Originally, a long spar or pole used to scrape the yards of a sailing ship. Later popularized as a term for a spy satellite, through the James Bond novel and film of the same name. |例句：In the James Bond film, the Moonraker was a space shuttle. 在詹姆斯·邦德电影中，“捕月者”号是一架航天飞机。

例句：He was a moonraker, harvesting the last of the summer crop under the light of the full moon. 他是一个在月光下工作的农夫，在满月的照耀下收割着最后一批夏季作物。 | 近义词：spy satellite, space enthusiast | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
