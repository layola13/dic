lunched:/lʌntʃt/
|v.|吃过午饭|Past tense and past participle of "lunch," meaning to have eaten lunch.|We lunched at a small bistro near the park. 我们在公园附近的一个小酒馆吃了午饭。|近义词：ate lunch, dined (midday)|反义词：(none - the opposite would be "didn't have lunch")|verb
