overmitigates:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪtɪɡeɪts/| v. |过度缓解，过度减轻|Reduces the negative impact of something to an excessive degree, often resulting in unintended consequences.| The government's policy overmitigated the economic downturn, leading to inflation. 政府的政策过度缓解了经济衰退，导致了通货膨胀。|  The company overmitigated the risk of data breaches, hindering innovation and productivity.  公司过度减轻了数据泄露的风险，阻碍了创新和生产力。 |近义词：overcompensates, overcorrects|反义词: undermitigates, exacerbates |词性:动词
