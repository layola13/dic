coachabilities:/ˌkoʊtʃəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可教练性；可指导性|The quality of being open to and responsive to coaching or guidance.  This refers to a person's willingness and ability to learn, adapt, and improve with the help of a coach or mentor.|Her coachabilities were evident in her eagerness to learn and implement feedback. 她渴望学习并落实反馈，这表明她具有很强的可指导性。|近义词: trainability, malleability, receptiveness, docility|反义词: intractability, unresponsiveness, resistance|noun
