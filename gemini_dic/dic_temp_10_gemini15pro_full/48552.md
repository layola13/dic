defamations:/ˌdefəˈmeɪʃənz/
n.
诽谤，中伤
The act of damaging someone's reputation by making false and malicious statements about them.
He sued the newspaper for defamations. 他起诉这家报纸诽谤。
slanders, libels, aspersions
praise, commendation
