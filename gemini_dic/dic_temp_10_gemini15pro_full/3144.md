aestivate:/ˈɛstɪveɪt/ /ˈiːstɪveɪt/| v. |夏眠；过夏季|To spend the summer in a state of dormancy or inactivity, especially in order to avoid heat and drought.|Many desert animals aestivate during the hottest months. 很多沙漠动物在最热的月份夏眠。

The snails aestivated in the shade of the rocks.  蜗牛在岩石的阴影下夏眠。|近义词：hibernate, sleep, rest|反义词:be active, awaken|词性:动词
