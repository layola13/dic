nonglacial:/ˌnɒnˈɡleɪʃəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈɡleɪʃəl/| adj.|非冰期的，非冰川的|Not pertaining to, characteristic of, or derived from a glacier or ice age.  Relating to periods of warmer climate between ice ages.|例句：Nonglacial sediments were deposited in the valley.  山谷中沉积了非冰川沉积物。

例句：The nonglacial period saw a rise in sea levels. 非冰期海平面上升。|近义词：interglacial, postglacial|反义词：glacial|词性: adjective
