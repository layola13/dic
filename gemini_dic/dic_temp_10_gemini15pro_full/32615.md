chirping:/ˈtʃɜːrpɪŋ/| n. vi. adj.|啁啾声，唧唧喳喳声|the short, sharp, high-pitched sounds made by small birds or insects, especially crickets.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a cheerful or lively sound.|例句：The chirping of the crickets filled the night air. 蟋蟀的鸣叫声充满了夜空。|近义词：twittering, peeping, chirruping|反义词:silent, quiet|词性:noun, verb, adjective
chirp:/tʃɜːrp/| v. n.|啁啾，唧唧喳喳|to make a short, sharp, high-pitched sound, like a small bird or insect, especially a cricket. It can also be used figuratively to describe speaking in a cheerful or lively way. |例句：The birds were chirping merrily in the trees. 鸟儿们在树上欢快地啁啾着。|近义词：twitter, peep, chirrup|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:verb, noun
