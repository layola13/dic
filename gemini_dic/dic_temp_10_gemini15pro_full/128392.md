new-wave:/ˈnjuː weɪv/ /ˈnuː weɪv/| n. adj.|新浪潮|A genre of rock music popular in the late 1970s and early 1980s, characterized by electronic sounds and often unconventional lyrics.  As an adjective, it describes something characteristic of this genre. |例句：The band's new album is a throwback to the new-wave sound of the 80s.  这支乐队的新专辑是对80年代新浪潮音乐的回归。

例句：She wore a new-wave outfit to the concert. 她穿着一套新浪潮风格的服装去参加音乐会。|近义词：post-punk, synth-pop|反义词:classic rock, mainstream pop|词性:名词,形容词
