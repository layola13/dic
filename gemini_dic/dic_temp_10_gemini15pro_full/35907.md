cocounselling:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/
n.
共同咨询;合作咨询;同辈咨询
A form of therapy in which two people alternate between the roles of counsellor and client, providing each other with mutual support and understanding.
Co-counselling sessions can be very beneficial for emotional release and personal growth. 共同咨询课程对于情绪释放和个人成长非常有益。
peer counselling, mutual support, self-help therapy
(No clear antonym)
