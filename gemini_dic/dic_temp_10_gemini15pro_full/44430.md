cross-promotion:/ˌkrɒs prəˈməʊʃən/ (US) /ˌkrɑːs prəˈmoʊʃən/| n.|交叉推广，互相推广|The act of promoting two or more products or services together, often from different companies, to reach a wider audience.|The movie studio and the fast-food chain engaged in cross-promotion, with toys from the movie appearing in kids' meals.|Synonyms: joint promotion, cooperative marketing, synergy marketing|Antonyms: independent promotion|noun

cross-promote:/ˌkrɒs prəˈməʊt/ (US) /ˌkrɑːs prəˈmoʊt/| v.|交叉推广，互相推广|To promote two or more products or services together.|The companies agreed to cross-promote each other's products on their websites.|Synonyms: jointly promote, cooperatively market|Antonyms: independently promote|verb
