cicisbeo:/ˌtʃiːtʃɪzˈbeɪoʊ/ /ˌtʃiːtʃɪzˈbeɪəʊ/
n.
男伴；情夫；(已婚妇女的)陪伴；护花使者
A man who attends a married woman in public, often with the knowledge and consent of her husband.
例句：In 18th-century Italy, it was fashionable for married women to have a cicisbeo.  在18世纪的意大利，已婚妇女拥有男伴是一种时尚。

例句：The cicisbeo escorted the lady to the opera, engaging in witty banter and offering his arm for support. 男伴护送这位女士去歌剧院，机智地开玩笑，并伸出手臂让她搀扶。

近义词：gallant, escort, admirer, lover
反义词：husband
词性：名词
