interdefinable:/ˌɪntərdiˈfaɪnəbl/
|adj.|可互相定义的，可互相解释的|able to be defined in terms of each other; mutually definable|The terms "knowledge" and "justified true belief" are often considered interdefinable. “知识”和“合理的真实信念”这两个术语通常被认为是可以互相定义的。|Synonyms: mutually definable, reciprocally definable|Antonyms: independent, unrelated|adjective
