copromoter:/kəʊprəˈməʊtər/ | n. | 共同发起人，共同赞助者 | A person or organization that promotes something jointly with another.  | Example 1: The two companies were copromoters of the music festival. 这两家公司是音乐节的共同赞助商。

Example 2:  She acted as a copromoter for the charity event, alongside the local council. 她和地方议会一起担任了慈善活动的共同发起人。 | 近义词: cosponsor, joint promoter | 反义词: opponent, competitor | 词性: noun

Etymology: co- (prefix meaning "jointly," "together") + promoter (from promote, from Latin promotus, past participle of promovere "to move forward")
