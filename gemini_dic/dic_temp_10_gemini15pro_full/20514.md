bivouac: /ˈbɪvuæk/ (US), /ˈbɪvuæk/ (UK)| n. v.|露营；临时营地；露宿|A temporary camp without tents or cover, used especially by soldiers or mountaineers.|The soldiers bivouacked in the forest. 士兵们在森林里露营。|They made a bivouac at the foot of the mountain. 他们在山脚下露营。|近义词：camp, encampment, shelter|反义词: house, home|词性:名词, 动词

bivouac: /ˈbɪvuæk/ (US), /ˈbɪvuæk/ (UK)| v.|露营；宿营|To stay in a temporary camp without tents or cover.|We bivouacked for the night on the mountainside. 我们在山坡上露营过夜。|The climbers were forced to bivouac when the storm hit.  暴风雨来袭时，登山者被迫露宿。|近义词：camp, encamp|反义词: lodge, reside |词性:动词

词源: French bivouac, from Swiss German Beiwacht, from bei by + Wacht watch.
