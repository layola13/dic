adulates: /ˈædjʊleɪts/
verb
过分称赞；谄媚；奉承
To praise or admire someone excessively or obsequiously.
He adulates her constantly, telling her how brilliant and beautiful she is.  他不断地奉承她，告诉她她多么聪明漂亮。

She was adululated by the press for her performance. 她因其表演而受到媒体的吹捧。

近义词: flatter, praise excessively, fawn over, worship, idolize
反义词: criticize, condemn, disparage
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin adulāri (“to fawn upon, flatter”), frequentative of adūlāre.
