morselling:/ˈmɔːrsəlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmɔːsəlɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |分割; 切碎; 零碎|The act of dividing something into small pieces, often food.  It can also refer to the small pieces themselves, or figuratively to fragments of something larger, like information or time.|She spent the afternoon morselling the bread for the stuffing. 她下午都在把面包切碎做馅。|近义词：dividing, fragmenting, chopping, dicing, mincing|反义词:combining, unifying, joining|noun
