metastasis: /məˈtæs.tə.sɪs/ (US) /məˈtæs.tə.sɪs/ (UK)| n.|转移，扩散 (尤指癌细胞)|The spread of a disease-producing agent (such as cancer cells) from the initial or primary site of disease to another part of the body.|The cancer had already metastasized to his lungs.|Synonyms: spread, dissemination, diffusion|Antonyms: localization, confinement|noun

metastatic: /ˌmet̬.əˈstæt̬.ɪk/ (US) /ˌmet.əˈstæt.ɪk/ (UK) | adj.|转移的，扩散的|Relating to or characterized by metastasis.|Doctors discovered a metastatic tumor.|Synonyms: spreading, diffused, disseminated|Antonyms: localized, confined|adjective
