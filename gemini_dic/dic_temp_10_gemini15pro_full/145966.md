overdiverse:/ˈoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrs/
adjective
过于多样化的；过度多样化的
Containing too much diversity; excessively diverse.
The ecosystem was considered overdiverse, making it difficult to manage effectively.  这个生态系统被认为过于多样化，难以有效管理。
The committee was overdiverse in its opinions, leading to gridlock. 委员会的意见过于多样化，导致陷入僵局。
近义词: excessively diverse, hyperdiverse
反义词: homogeneous, uniform, simple
词性: adjective
