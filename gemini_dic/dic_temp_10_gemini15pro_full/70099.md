feverishness:/ˈfiːvərɪʃnəs/| n. |发烧，狂热，兴奋|A state of having or showing the symptoms of a fever; a state of heightened activity or emotion.|She felt a growing feverishness and knew she was getting sick. 她感到越来越发烧，知道自己生病了。|The feverishness of the stock market is worrying investors. 股市的狂热令投资者担忧。|近义词：fever, heat, excitement, delirium|反义词：calmness, coolness, tranquility|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fever + -ishness.  Fever comes from the Old French *fievre* and ultimately from the Latin *febris*. The suffix *-ishness* forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
