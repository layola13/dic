goitred:/ˈɡɔɪtərd/| adj.|甲状腺肿大的；患甲状腺肿的|Having or affected with a goiter (a swelling of the thyroid gland in the neck).|例句：The doctor examined the patient's goitred neck. 医生检查了病人肿大的脖子。|例句：The iodine deficiency resulted in a noticeable goitred condition in the population. 碘缺乏导致该地区人口出现明显的甲状腺肿大。|近义词：goiterous, thyroid-swollen|反义词: healthy (regarding thyroid)
词性: 形容词

词源:
From *goitre* (noun) + *-ed* (adjective suffix). *Goitre* comes from Old French *goitre*, from Vulgar Latin *gutturia* "throat," from Latin *guttur* "throat."
