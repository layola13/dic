idiolects:/ˈɪdiəlekts/| n. |个人语言; 个人方言|The way a particular person speaks, including their unique vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation.  It's like a linguistic fingerprint. |Her idiolect was peppered with Southern slang and expressions from her childhood. 她个人的语言习惯中夹杂着南方的俚语和她童年时期的一些表达方式。

Every person has their own unique idiolect, shaped by their background and experiences. 每个人都有自己独特的个人语言，由他们的背景和经历塑造。|近义词：speech pattern, linguistic fingerprint|反义词:N/A|Noun
