anxiousness:/æŋˈzaɪəsnəs/| n. |焦虑，不安|A feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease about something with an uncertain outcome.|She felt a growing anxiousness about the upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试感到越来越焦虑。|近义词: worry, anxiety, concern, apprehension, solicitude|反义词: calmness, tranquility, serenity, composure, peace of mind|词性:名词

Etymology:
From anxious + -ness.  "Anxious" comes from the Late Latin "anxius," meaning "solicitous, troubled," ultimately derived from the Latin "angere" meaning "to choke, strangle, torment." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
