groupie: /ˈɡruːpi/ (US), /ˈɡruːpi/ (UK)| n. |狂热追星族，乐队迷（尤指女性）|A person, especially a young woman, who regularly follows a pop group or other celebrity, often trying to get to know them personally.|She was a dedicated groupie, following the band on every tour. 她是一个忠实的追星族，每次巡演都跟着乐队。|The groupies screamed and waved banners as the band arrived. 当乐队到达时，追星族们尖叫着挥舞着旗帜。|近义词：fan, follower|反义词: critic, detractor|词性: noun

词源:  Originated in the late 1960s, from *group* + *-ie*, a diminutive suffix.
