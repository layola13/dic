nonordination:/ˌnɒnɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/
n.|非按立，未授圣职|The nonordination of women is still a contentious issue in some religious denominations.  在某些宗教派别中，不授予妇女圣职仍然是一个有争议的问题。|The church's stance on nonordination has alienated many progressive members. 教会关于不授予圣职的立场疏远了许多进步成员。|近义词:non-appointment, exclusion from clergy|反义词:ordination, appointment to clergy|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
ordination (noun): from Late Latin ordinationem (nominative ordinatio), "a setting in order, arrangement," from ordinare "to set in order, arrange," from Latin ordo (genitive ordinis) "row, rank, order."
