missioning:/ˈmɪʃənɪŋ/
verb
派遣；委任；给…交待任务
the act of giving someone a mission or task
The missioning of the new recruits took place at headquarters.  新兵的派遣仪式在总部举行。
近义词: assigning, commissioning, deploying, dispatching
反义词: recalling, withdrawing
词性: verb (动名词形式)

派遣；委任
the act of giving someone a mission or task
