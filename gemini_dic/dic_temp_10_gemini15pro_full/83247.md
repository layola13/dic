gymnasts:/ˈdʒɪmnæsts/| n. |体操运动员|A person who performs gymnastics, especially as a sport.| The gymnasts performed incredible feats of strength and flexibility. 体操运动员展现了惊人的力量和柔韧性。| Synonyms: athlete, acrobat | Antonyms: couch potato, sluggard | noun

Etymology:  From Ancient Greek γυμναστής (gumnastḗs, “trainer, teacher of gymnastics”), from γυμνός (gumnós, “naked, unclothed”).  Gymnastics was originally performed naked in ancient Greece.
