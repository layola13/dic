ferrumination:/ˌfɛruːmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 反刍，沉思，反复思考 | The act of chewing the cud or of meditating or thinking obsessively about something. |  He was lost in ferrumination over his recent breakup. 他沉浸在对最近分手的反复思考中。|  Ferrumination on past failures can be detrimental to mental health.  对过去失败的反复思考可能对心理健康有害。 |近义词：rumination, brooding, mulling, pondering, contemplation |反义词: disregard, neglect, indifference |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *ferruminatio, derived from Latin ferruminare "to solder, join," from ferrumen "cement, solder," from ferrum "iron."  The sense development likely arose from the idea of repeatedly working or turning something over, like metal being soldered.
