anemia:/əˈniːmiə/ | n. | 贫血 |A condition in which the blood doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells.|She suffers from anemia. 她患有贫血。|Synonyms: bloodlessness, deficiency|Antonyms: plethora, excess|noun

anemic:/əˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 贫血的 |Suffering from anemia; lacking vigor or vitality.|He looked anemic after his illness. 他病后看起来很虚弱。|Synonyms: pale, wan, feeble|Antonyms: robust, healthy|adjective
