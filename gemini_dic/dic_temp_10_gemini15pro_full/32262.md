chicory: /ˈtʃɪkəri/ | n. | 菊苣，苦苣 | A blue-flowered plant whose leaves are eaten in salads, and whose root is roasted and ground to be used as a substitute for or additive to coffee. |  例句：I added chicory to my coffee this morning. 我今天早上在我的咖啡里加了菊苣。

例句：Chicory is a common ingredient in New Orleans coffee. 菊苣是新奥尔良咖啡的常见成分。| 近义词：endive | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *cichorée*, from Latin *cichorium*, from Ancient Greek *κίχορα* (*kíchora*).
