ante:/ˈænti/| prefix |在...之前，先于|A prefix meaning "before," "prior to," or "in front of."|例句：antenatal care 产前护理|近义词：pre-, fore-|反义词：post-|词性：前缀

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ante* ("before," "in front of"). Cognate with Ancient Greek *ἀντί* (*antí*).
