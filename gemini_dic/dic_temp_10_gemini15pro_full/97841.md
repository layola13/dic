inlaid:/ˈɪnleɪd/ /ɪnˈleɪd/| adj.|镶嵌的；嵌入的|Decorated or made with a design that is set into a surface.|The antique table was inlaid with mother-of-pearl. 这张古董桌子镶嵌着珍珠母。|近义词：embedded, inset, encrusted|反义词: plain, unembellished|词性:形容词
inlay:/ˈɪnleɪ/ /ɪnˈleɪ/| n. vt.|镶嵌；镶嵌物；镶嵌工艺|The act of setting a design into a surface, or the material used for this.|The inlay on the box was made of ivory. 盒子上的镶嵌物是用象牙做的。|近义词：inset, embedding|反义词: removal, extraction|词性:名词，动词

词源: From Middle English *inleyen, from in + lay.  Essentially, "laid in."
