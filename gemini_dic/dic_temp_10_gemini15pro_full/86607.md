heliopora:/ˌhiːliˈɒpərə/ | n. |蓝珊瑚|A genus of corals, the only genus in the family Helioporidae, commonly known as blue coral. It is unique among reef-building corals as its skeleton is made of aragonite and contains iron, giving it a distinctive blue colour.|例句：The divers marveled at the vibrant blue heliopora colonies on the reef. 潜水员惊叹于珊瑚礁上充满活力的蓝色珊瑚群。

例句：Heliopora is an important reef-building coral in the Indo-Pacific region. 蓝珊瑚是印度洋-太平洋地区重要的造礁珊瑚。|近义词：coral |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
