flunkydom:/ˈflʌŋ.ki.dəm/
n.
奴才地位；奴性；卑躬屈膝
The state or condition of being a flunky; servile behavior.
He escaped the flunkydom of his father's household and started his own business. 他摆脱了父亲家里的奴才地位，开始了自己的事业。
近义词:servility, subservience, obsequiousness
反义词:independence, autonomy, self-reliance
词性:名词


flunkydom:/ˈflʌŋ.ki.dəm/
n.
一群奴才
A group of flunkies.
The CEO surrounded himself with a flunkydom of yes-men. 首席执行官身边围绕着一群唯唯诺诺的奴才。
近义词:retinue, entourage, staff
反义词:leaders, superiors, bosses
词性:名词


词源:  From flunky + -dom.  Flunky itself likely comes from the now obsolete flunk (to fail an exam), implying someone incompetent who performs menial tasks.
