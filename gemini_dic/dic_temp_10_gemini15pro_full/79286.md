glaucosis:/ɡlɔːˈkoʊsɪs/ |n.|青光眼，房水混浊|A disease of the eye marked by increased intraocular pressure, causing gradual loss of sight.|Glaucosis can lead to blindness if left untreated. 青光眼如果不治疗会导致失明。|Synonyms: glaucoma|Antonyms: None (disease states don't typically have antonyms)|noun
