mechanicality:/ˌmɛkəˈnɪkəlɪti/
n.|机械性；机械呆板；机械动作|the quality of being mechanical; automatic and unthinking behavior.|Her performance lacked spontaneity; it was pure mechanicality.|近义词：automatism, rigidity, stiffness|反义词：spontaneity, naturalness|词性:noun
Etymology: Late 16th century: from French *méchanicité* or late Latin *mechanicalitas*, from *mechanicus* (see mechanical).
