deadhead:/ˈdedhed/| n. v.|枯萎的花头；免费乘客；空车返回；清除枯萎的花朵|A person who attends a concert or other performance without paying for a ticket, especially one who gets in by hitchhiking or by asking for a free ride from a band's vehicle.  As a verb, it means to remove dead flowers from (a plant).|The band picked up a few deadheads outside the stadium. 乐队在体育场外搭载了几个免费乘客。|Synonyms: freeloader, non-paying passenger (n.); prune, snip (v.)|Antonyms: paying customer (n.)|noun, verb
