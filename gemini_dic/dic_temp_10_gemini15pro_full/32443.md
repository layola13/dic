chiminage:/ˈtʃɪmɪnɪdʒ/
n.
壁炉使用费，烟囱税
A fee paid for the use of a chimney; hearth money.
In medieval times, families would pay a chiminage for the right to use the lord's oven.  在中世纪，家庭需要支付壁炉使用费才能使用领主的烤炉。
The chiminage was a significant source of income for the manor. 壁炉使用费是庄园重要的收入来源。
近义词: hearth tax, chimney tax
反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a specific tax)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old French *cheminage "toll for passage," from Late Latin caminagium "tax on chimneys, hearth money," from Latin caminus "furnace, forge, fireplace."
