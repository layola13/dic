desiccated:/ˈdesɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
干燥的，脱水的
thoroughly dried; deprived of moisture
The desiccated landscape offered little in the way of food or water.  干燥的土地几乎无法提供食物或水。
The desiccated fruit was chewy and sweet. 干燥的水果很有嚼劲，而且很甜。
synonyms: dried, dehydrated, parched, withered
antonyms: moist, hydrated, damp, soggy
word origin: from Latin desiccatus, past participle of desiccare "to dry up," from de- "thoroughly" + siccare "to dry," from siccus "dry"
