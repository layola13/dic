inexpressibly:/ˌɪnɪkˈspresəbli/
adverb
难以形容地；无法表达地
so extraordinary as to be impossible to express in words.
The beauty of the sunset was inexpressibly lovely.  日落的景色美得难以形容。
Synonyms: indescribably, unutterably, ineffably
Antonyms: expressibly, describably
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From in- (not) + expressible (capable of being expressed) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin exprimere (to press out, express).
