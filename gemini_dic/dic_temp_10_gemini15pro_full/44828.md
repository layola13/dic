crust:/krʌst/| n.|外壳，硬皮，面包皮|A hard outer covering of something.|The pie had a golden brown crust.|近义词：shell, covering, rind, exterior|反义词:interior, center, core|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *crouste*, from Latin crusta (“shell, rind, bark”).
