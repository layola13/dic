lefted:/ˈlɛftɪd/ | adj. | 左侧的，左边的 (Non-standard, considered incorrect) |  This word is a non-standard formation. The correct form is "left". It attempts to create a past participle form of "left" where none is needed.  Use "left" instead. | *Incorrect:*  The lefted box contained the treasure.  *Correct:* The left box contained the treasure. | 近义词: left | 反义词: right | 词性: adjective (non-standard)
