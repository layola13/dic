herringbone: |n. adj.| 人字形，人字形图案|A pattern of columns of short slanted lines, each column slanting in the opposite direction to the next, resembling the bones of a herring. |The floor was laid in a herringbone pattern.|近义词:chevron, zigzag|反义词:|noun, adjective
