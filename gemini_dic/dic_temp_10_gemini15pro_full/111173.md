lithofracteur: /ˌlɪθoʊˈfræktər/ | n. | 碎石炸药；矿石炸药 | A type of high explosive used in mining and quarrying.  It was a precursor to dynamite. |  Lithofracteur was widely used in the 19th century for blasting rock. 碎石炸药在19世纪被广泛用于爆破岩石。| Synonyms: dynamite, explosive, blasting agent | Antonyms:  None (no direct antonym for a specific type of explosive) | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lithofracteur*, from *litho-* (stone) + *fracteur* (breaker), from Latin *lapis* (stone) + *frangere* (to break).
