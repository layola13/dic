compasser:/ˈkʌmpəsər/ | n. | 制图员，测量员; 包含者; 构思者;  | One who, or that which, encompasses, encircles, or includes.  |  The compasser of the land surveyed the property.  这块土地的测量员测量了这块地产。 | synonym: surveyor, measurer, designer, planner | antonym: excluder | noun

compasses:/ˈkʌmpəsɪz/ | n. | 圆规；指南针；罗盘 | An instrument for drawing circles, or measuring distances, consisting of two pointed legs movable on a pivot. | He used compasses to draw a perfect circle. 他用圆规画了一个完美的圆。 | synonym: dividers, calipers | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a physical tool) | noun

compasses:/ˈkʌmpəsɪz/ | v. | 理解；包围；完成；达到；绕行 | To go around; to make a circuit of; to encircle; to surround or enclose; to include; to comprehend; to attain; to achieve | The sailors compassed the globe. 水手们环游了地球。 | synonym: encircle, surround, achieve, attain, comprehend | antonym: exclude, fail, miss | verb
