marcescible:/mɑːrˈsɛsəbl/
adj.
凋落的；枯而不落的；宿存的
Withering but not falling off; remaining attached to the stem after dying.
The oak tree is marcescible, its brown leaves clinging to the branches throughout winter. 橡树的叶子是枯而不落的，棕色的叶子整个冬天都 clinging在树枝上。
The beech tree also exhibits marcescent leaves, providing winter interest in the landscape.  山毛榉树也展现出宿存的叶子，为冬天的景观增添了趣味。
近义词: persistent,  withered,  dead,  dry
反义词: deciduous,  abscising, falling,  fresh
