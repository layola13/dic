noticeability:/ˌnoʊtɪsəˈbɪləti/| n.|显著性；明显度；引人注目|The noticeability of the changes surprised everyone. 这些变化的显著性让每个人都感到惊讶。|The dress's bright color increased its noticeability. 这条裙子鲜艳的颜色增加了它的醒目程度。|近义词：conspicuousness, prominence, visibility|反义词：inconspicuousness, obscurity, unnoticeableness|词性:noun
词源:From noticeable + -ity. Noticeable is from notice + -able. Ultimately from Latin *notitia.
