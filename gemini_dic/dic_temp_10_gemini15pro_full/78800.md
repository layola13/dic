gilthead:/ˈɡɪlthed/ | n. | 金头鲷，金头海鲷 | A marine fish with a golden band across its forehead. | 例句：The gilthead bream is a popular fish in Mediterranean cuisine. 金头鲷是地中海菜肴中很受欢迎的鱼。

例句：We caught a large gilthead while fishing off the coast. 我们在近海捕鱼时捕获了一条很大的金头鲷。| 近义词：sea bream, dorado | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "gill" (referring to the fish's respiratory organ) + "head" (referring to the golden band on the fish's head).
