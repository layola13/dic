barenesses: /ˈbɛrnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
赤裸；空旷；贫瘠
The state of being bare or uncovered; nakedness.  A lack of covering or adornment.  The state of being empty or unfurnished.
The barenesses of the landscape stretched before them, a testament to the harsh climate.  他们面前展现着荒凉的景象，证明了这里气候的恶劣。
The barenesses of the room made it feel cold and uninviting.  房间的空旷使它感觉寒冷而不受欢迎。

近义词: nakedness, emptiness, barrenness, destitution, starkness
反义词: fullness, abundance, richness, lushness, opulence
词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From bareness + -es (plural suffix). Bareness comes from bare + -ness (noun-forming suffix).  Bare originates from Old English "bær," meaning "naked, uncovered."
