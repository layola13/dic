nonresiliences:/ˌnɒnrɪˈzɪliənsɪz/
|n.| 非弹性，非韧性；缺乏适应力|The nonresiliences of the system were exposed during the crisis.|该系统的非弹性在危机期间暴露无遗。
|例句：The coastal communities' nonresiliences to rising sea levels are a major concern. 沿海社区对海平面上升的适应力不足是一个主要问题。

|例句：The study highlighted the nonresiliences of the economy to external shocks.  这项研究突出了经济对外部冲击的缺乏适应力。
|近义词：fragility, vulnerability, weakness, susceptibility|
|反义词：resilience, strength, robustness, adaptability|
|词性：名词|
