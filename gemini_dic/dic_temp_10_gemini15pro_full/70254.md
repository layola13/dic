fictionally:/ˈfɪkʃənəli/| adv.|虚构地，小说式地|In a fictional way; as if in a story.  Describing something presented as if it were part of a fictional narrative, even if it's based on real events.|He fictionally recounted the events of the day, adding embellishments and drama.|近义词：imaginatively, inventively, hypothetically|反义词：factually, truly, realistically|词性:副词
