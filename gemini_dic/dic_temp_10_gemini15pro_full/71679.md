flaunt:/flɔːnt/ | v. |炫耀，夸示|To display something ostentatiously, especially in order to provoke envy or admiration or to show defiance.|She flaunted her new diamond ring. 她炫耀着她新买的钻戒。|Synonyms: show off, parade, display, boast, brandish|Antonyms: hide, conceal, downplay|verb

flaunt:/flɔːnt/ | n. |炫耀，夸示（的行为）|An ostentatious display; a flaunting action.|Her expensive car was a flaunt of her wealth. 她的豪车是她财富的炫耀。|Synonyms: show, display, ostentation|Antonyms: modesty, humility|noun
