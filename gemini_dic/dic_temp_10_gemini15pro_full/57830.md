dressage:/drɛˈsɑːʒ/ /drəˈsɑːʒ/
noun
马术；盛装舞步
The art of training a horse in obedience and precision of movement.
例句：The rider demonstrated impressive skill in dressage.  骑手展示了令人印象深刻的马术技巧。

例句：Dressage requires years of dedicated training for both horse and rider. 盛装舞步需要马和骑手多年的专门训练。

近义词：horsemanship, equitation
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "lack of training" or "undisciplined riding")
词性：n.
