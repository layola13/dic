metalloidal:/ˌmɛtəlˈɔɪdəl/| adj.|类金属的；准金属的|Having properties intermediate between those of a metal and a nonmetal; semimetallic.|Boron is a metalloidal element. 硼是一种类金属元素。|Synonyms: semimetallic, nonmetallic|Antonyms: metallic|adjective
metalloid:/ˈmɛtəlɔɪd/| n.|类金属；准金属|An element whose properties are intermediate between those of metals and solid nonmetals. They are electrical semiconductors.|Silicon is a common metalloid used in computer chips. 硅是一种常用的类金属，用于计算机芯片。|Synonyms: semimetal, nonmetal|Antonyms: metal|noun
