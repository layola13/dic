aircraft:/ˈɛərkræft/ (US) /ˈeəkrɑːft/ (UK)| n. |飞机; 航空器|A vehicle that is able to fly in the air, such as an airplane or helicopter.|Example: The aircraft landed safely despite the bad weather. 尽管天气恶劣，飞机还是安全着陆了。|Example:  Military aircraft patrolled the skies. 军用飞机在空中巡逻。|近义词：airplane, plane, aeroplane|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From air + craft. "Craft" in this context means "a vehicle designed for navigation".  The word "craft" itself comes from Old English cræft, meaning "strength, skill, power".
