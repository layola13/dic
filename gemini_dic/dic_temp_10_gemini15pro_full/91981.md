hypernatural:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəl/ | adj. | 超自然的，超越自然的 | Existing beyond or outside of nature; supernatural.  A concept often used in nonstandard analysis in mathematics. |  His theory posited a hypernatural realm beyond human comprehension. 他的理论假设了一个超越人类理解的超自然领域。 |  The mathematician explored the concept of hypernatural numbers.  这位数学家探索了超自然数的概念。 | 近义词: supernatural, transcendental, metaphysical | 反义词: natural, mundane, earthly | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From hyper- "beyond, over" + natural.  The term gained prominence through its use in nonstandard analysis, a branch of mathematics.
