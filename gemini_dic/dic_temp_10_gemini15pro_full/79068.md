gizmo:/ˈɡɪzməʊ/ (UK) /ˈɡɪzməʊ/ (US)| n. |小玩意儿；小装置|A gadget, especially a mechanical or electronic one whose name is unknown or forgotten.|

例句：
He showed me a gizmo that opened cans automatically.
他给我展示了一个自动开罐器的小装置。

例句：
I can't figure out how to use this gizmo.
我不知道怎么用这个小玩意儿。

近义词：gadget, contraption, device, widget|
反义词: -(gizmo 通常指小而不知名的小装置，没有明确的反义词)|
词性: noun

词源：
The origin of "gizmo" is uncertain, but it likely emerged in the early 20th century, possibly as slang within the US Navy or among engineers.  Some speculate it might be a playful alteration of "gadget."
