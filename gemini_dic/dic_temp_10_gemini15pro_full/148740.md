overtrouble:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌbəl/
verb
过度烦扰，过分担忧
To cause excessive worry or distress; to burden excessively.
He overtroubled himself with the details of the project.  他过分担忧项目的细节。
She overtroubled her friends with her constant complaining. 她不停的抱怨让她的朋友们不堪其扰。
近义词:overwhelm, overburden, distress
反义词:comfort, soothe, reassure
词性: verb

Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "trouble" (Old French trubler, from Vulgar Latin *turbulare, from Latin turba "turmoil, crowd").
