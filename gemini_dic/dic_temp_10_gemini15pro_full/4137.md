air-headed: /ˈɛə(r)ˌhɛdɪd/ | adj. |  轻浮的，愚蠢的，傻乎乎的 | Lacking seriousness, common sense, or good judgment; scatterbrained.  | She made an air-headed remark about the situation, which made everyone uncomfortable. 她对情况做出了轻率的评论，让每个人都不舒服。 |  He's so air-headed he forgot his own birthday. 他傻乎乎的，竟然连自己的生日都忘了。 |  flighty, dizzy, scatterbrained, ditzy | sensible, intelligent, thoughtful | adjective
