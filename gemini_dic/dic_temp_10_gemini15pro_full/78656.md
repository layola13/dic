giddy-up:/ˈɡɪdiˌʌp/ | int. | 加速；驾；快走（用于催促马匹前进） | An exclamation used to urge a horse to move faster. | Giddy-up, horsey!  驾，小马！ | 近义词: go, mush, get up | 反义词: whoa, halt | 词性:感叹词

giddy-up:/ˈɡɪdiˌʌp/ | n. | 加速；驾；快走（用于催促马匹前进） | The act of urging a horse forward. |  The horse responded to the giddy-up with a burst of speed. 这匹马对加速的命令做出了快速反应。| 近义词: urging, encouragement | 反义词: restraint, halting | 词性:名词
