brawns:/brɔːnz/
n.|肌肉；体力；膂力|Muscular strength; physical power.|"He relied on his brawns rather than his brains." 他靠的是体力而不是脑力。|
近义词:muscle, strength, power
反义词:weakness, frailty
词性:名词(plural)

词源:
From Old French *braon, from Frankish *brā̆n (compare Old High German brāto (“piece of meat, fleshy part of the body”), Dutch braad (“roasted meat”)), from Proto-Germanic *brādiz (“piece of meat”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreh₁- (“to swell, grow”).
