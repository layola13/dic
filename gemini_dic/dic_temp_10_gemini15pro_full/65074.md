etg: /iːtiːˈdʒiː/
abbreviation| ETG |乙基葡糖苷|Ethyl glucuronide (ETG) is a direct metabolite of ethanol (alcohol). It can be detected in urine, blood, and hair. It's often used as a marker of recent alcohol consumption.|The patient's urine ETG test was positive.|近义词: Ethyl glucuronide (no commonly used synonym)|反义词: N/A (it's a biomarker, not a concept with an opposite)|noun
