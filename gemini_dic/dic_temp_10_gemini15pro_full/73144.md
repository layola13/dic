PHRASE->
(Forasmuch is primarily used on its own and doesn't typically form set phrases.  It's more of a formal, literary conjunction.)  Therefore, examples are focused on demonstrating synonyms in context:

1. Because of the rain, we stayed inside.
2. Since it was late, we went home.
3. Considering the circumstances, we decided to postpone the meeting.
4. Seeing that he was tired, we let him rest.
5. Inasmuch as it is necessary, we will provide assistance.
