germain:/ʒɜːrˈmeɪn/ | adj. |密切相关的；有重要关系的；本质的|Relevant, pertinent, closely or significantly related.|例句：The evidence was germane to the case.  证据与案件密切相关。|例句：Her remarks were not germane to the discussion. 她的言论与讨论无关。|近义词：relevant, pertinent, applicable, apposite, appropriate|反义词：irrelevant, extraneous, immaterial|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French germain "having the same parents, full, own," from Latin germanus "of the same stock, genuine, true," originally "having the same parents," from germen (genitive germinis) "bud, sprout; offspring."
