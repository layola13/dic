debouchement:/ˌdeɪbuːʃˈmɒ̃/ |/ˌdeɪbuːʃˈmɑːnt/| n. |出口，河口；流出；（军队）出动；销路|An outlet or opening; the mouth of a river; the act of emerging or flowing out; (military) the act of deploying troops from a confined space; a market for goods.|The debouchement of the river into the sea created a fertile delta. 这条河流入大海，形成了肥沃的三角洲。|Synonyms: outlet, exit, egress, vent, mouth, discharge, deployment|Antonyms: entrance, inlet, intake|Noun

debouche:/ˌdeɪˈbuːʃ/ |/dɪˈbuːʃ/| v. |（军队）出动；流出；涌出|To emerge or flow out from a confined space; (military) to deploy troops from a confined space.|The troops debouched from the forest onto the open plain. 部队从森林中涌出，来到开阔的平原。|Synonyms: emerge, issue, flow out, deploy|Antonyms: enter, retreat, withdraw|Verb
