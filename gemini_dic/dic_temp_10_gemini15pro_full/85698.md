havenward:/ˈheɪvənwərd/ | adv. | 朝向避难所；走向天堂；| Towards a haven; towards a place of safety or refuge, often with spiritual connotations of heaven. |例句：The ship sailed havenward through the storm. 这艘船在暴风雨中驶向避风港。|例句：Her soul yearned havenward after a long and difficult life.  在漫长而艰难的一生之后，她的灵魂渴望天堂。|近义词：heavenward, homeward, refugeward |反义词: seaward, outward, away |词性:副词

词源: haven + -ward.  "Haven" comes from Old English *hæfen "haven, port," from Proto-Germanic *hafno "sea." "-ward" is an Old English suffix denoting direction, from Proto-Germanic *-werdaz.
