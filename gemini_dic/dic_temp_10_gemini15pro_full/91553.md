hyper-intelligence:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/ | n. | 超级智能 | Extreme intelligence surpassing human cognitive abilities. The capacity to understand or solve problems far beyond the capabilities of a typical human. |  Scientists are exploring the potential and risks of hyper-intelligence. 科学家们正在探索超级智能的潜力和风险。|  The AI demonstrated hyper-intelligence in solving complex mathematical equations.  人工智能在解决复杂的数学方程式方面展现出了超级智能。 | synonyms: superintelligence, ultra-intelligence | antonyms: unintelligence, stupidity | noun
