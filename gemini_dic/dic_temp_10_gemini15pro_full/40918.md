coolibah:/ˈkuːlɪbɑː/
noun
桉树的一种，尤指生长在澳大利亚的树种
A type of eucalyptus tree, especially one growing in Australia.
The coolibah tree is a common sight in the Australian outback.  澳洲内陆地区常见到桉树。
Coolibah trees provide shade for animals in the hot Australian sun.  桉树为动物们在炎热的澳大利亚阳光下提供了阴凉。
synonyms: eucalyptus, gum tree
antonyms: N/A
