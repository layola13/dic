daintrel: /ˈdeɪntrəl/ | n. | 美味佳肴; 珍馐; |  A choice morsel or delicacy; something delicious and rare. |  The chef prepared a daintrel of wild mushrooms and truffles.  厨师准备了一道由野生蘑菇和松露做成的美味佳肴。| 近义词: delicacy, tidbit, treat | 反义词: slop, swill | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle English *dayntrel, diminutive of deynte ("delicacy"). Ultimately from Old French daintie.
