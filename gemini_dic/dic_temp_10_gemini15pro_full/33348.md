chummiest:/ˈtʃʌmiɪst/| adj.|最亲密的|Superlative form of chummy: most chummy.  Meaning having a close and friendly relationship.|They've become the chummiest of friends since starting university.  他们自从上大学以来就成了最亲密的朋友。|近义词：closest, friendliest, thickest|反义词：distant, aloof, unfriendly|词性:形容词
词源: From chum + -y + -est. Chum itself likely originates from chamber-fellow, later shortened to chum.
