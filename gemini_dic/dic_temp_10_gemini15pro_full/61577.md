emerge:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/ (US) /iˈmɜːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |出现，浮现，暴露|to come out of something or out from behind something|The sun emerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云层后面露了出来。|appear, arise, surface|disappear, vanish|verb

emerge:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/ (US) /iˈmɜːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |(从困境中)摆脱，(从水中)出来|to come out of a difficult experience|She emerged from the scandal relatively unscathed. 她从丑闻中摆脱出来，相对而言毫发无损。|survive, overcome, recover|succumb, fail|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *emergere, present active infinitive of emergō (“I emerge, rise up, come forth”), from ex (“out of, from”) + mergō (“I dip, plunge, immerse”).
