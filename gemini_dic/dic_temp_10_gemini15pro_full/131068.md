nonbarbiturates:/ˌnɒnbɑːrˈbɪtʃʊrɪts/ | n. | 非巴比妥类药物 |  Drugs that are not barbiturates, a class of sedative and hypnotic drugs. These drugs have different chemical structures and mechanisms of action compared to barbiturates. | The doctor prescribed nonbarbiturates for her insomnia because she had a bad reaction to barbiturates in the past. 医生给她开了非巴比妥类药物治疗失眠，因为她过去对巴比妥类药物有过不良反应。 |  sedative-hypnotics, non-narcotic analgesics | barbiturates | noun
