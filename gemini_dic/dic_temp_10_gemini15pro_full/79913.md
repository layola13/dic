gneissoid:/ˈnaɪsɔɪd/| adj.|片麻状的，类似片麻岩的|Resembling gneiss; having the banded or foliated structure characteristic of gneiss.  The rock displays a gneissoid texture. |例句：The metamorphic rock exhibited a distinctly gneissoid appearance.  这块变质岩呈现出明显的片麻状外观。

例句：The gneissoid texture suggests that the rock has undergone intense pressure and temperature changes.  片麻状结构表明这块岩石经历了强烈的压力和温度变化。|近义词：gneissic, foliated, banded|反义词：massive, non-foliated|词性:形容词
词源: From gneiss + -oid.
