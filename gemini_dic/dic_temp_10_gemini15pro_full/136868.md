nonskilled:/ˌnɒnˈskɪld/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈskɪld/ (UK)| adj.|非熟练的，无需技能的|Not requiring or involving special skills or training.|Many nonskilled workers lost their jobs due to automation. 许多非熟练工人由于自动化而失业。|The company hired a number of nonskilled laborers for the construction project.  这家公司为该建筑项目雇佣了一些非熟练工人。|近义词：unskilled, untrained, unqualified|反义词：skilled, trained, qualified|词性:形容词
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + skilled
