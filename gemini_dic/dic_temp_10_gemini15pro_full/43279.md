craftsmanly:/ˈkræftsmənli/| adj.|技艺精湛的；巧夺天工的|Showing the skill and care of a craftsman; expertly made.|The furniture was craftsmanly made, with intricate carvings and dovetail joints. 这件家具制作技艺精湛，有着复杂的雕刻和燕尾榫接。|近义词：skillful, expert, meticulous, precise, adroit|反义词：clumsy, unskilled, amateurish|词性:形容词
词源: craftsman (n.) + -ly (adv./adj. suffix)  From craft + man.  Craft from Old English cræft "strength, skill, art," and man.  The suffix -ly transforms nouns and adjectives into adverbs and adjectives, respectively. In this case, it transforms the noun "craftsman" into the adjective "craftsmanly."
