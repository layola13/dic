dccs:/ˈdiː siː siː ɛs/ |abbr.| 数据中心交换系统 (Data Center Switching System), 分布式控制系统 (Distributed Control System) | Data Center Switching System refers to a network infrastructure designed for high-performance and high-availability data center environments. Distributed Control System refers to a computerized control system for a process or plant usually with many control loops, in which autonomous controllers are distributed throughout the system, but there is no central operator supervisory control.  |  The new DCCSs improved the efficiency of the data center.  新的数据中心交换系统提高了数据中心的效率。 | synonym: network infrastructure, control system | antonym: (No direct antonym for technical terms like these) | abbreviation
