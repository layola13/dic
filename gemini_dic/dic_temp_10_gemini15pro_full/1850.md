Acronis: /əˈkrɒnɪs/
noun|爱克罗尼斯（公司名）|A company that develops backup and disaster recovery software.|"I use Acronis True Image to back up my computer." 我使用爱克罗尼斯真实映像备份我的电脑。|近义词: Backup software, data protection software|反义词: None|n.

ETYMOLOGY->
Acronis is a portmanteau of the Greek words "akros" (meaning "high" or "top") and "nekros" (meaning "dead"). The company's founders intended the name to reflect their goal of bringing high-level data protection to even "dead" or failed systems.
