half-bleached:/ˌhæfˈbliːtʃt/
adj.
半漂白的
Partially bleached; bleached to a lighter shade but not completely white.
The half-bleached hair gave her a two-toned look.  半漂白的头发让她看起来像染了两种颜色。
The denim jacket was half-bleached for a trendy, faded look. 这件牛仔夹克经过半漂白处理，营造出一种时尚的褪色效果。
近义词:partially bleached, lightened
反义词:fully bleached, unbleached
词性:adjective
