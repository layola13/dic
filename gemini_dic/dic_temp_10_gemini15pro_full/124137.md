morticing:/ˈmɔːrtɪsɪŋ/ | n. |榫眼加工；开榫眼|The process of creating a mortise, a hole or recess cut into a piece of wood or other material, usually to receive a tenon or other fitting part. |  He spent the afternoon morticing the door frame. 他一下午都在给门框开榫眼。| Synonyms: grooving, slotting, routing | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps filling or joining) | noun

mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/ | n. |榫眼；槽孔|A hole or recess cut into a piece of wood or other material, usually to receive a tenon or other fitting part. | The mortise and tenon joint is a strong and traditional woodworking technique. 榫卯接合是一种坚固的传统木工技术。 | Synonyms:  slot, groove, recess | Antonyms: projection, tenon | noun

mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/ | vt. |开榫眼；凿榫眼|To create a mortise in a piece of wood or other material.  | He mortised the beam to accept the tenon. 他在梁上凿了榫眼以便插入榫头。| Synonyms: groove, slot, rout | Antonyms: fill, join | verb
