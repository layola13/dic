outargue:/ˌaʊtˈɑːrɡjuː/
verb
胜过…的辩论；以辩论压倒
to defeat someone in a debate or argument
He outargued his opponents and won the debate. 他在辩论中胜过对手，赢得了辩论。
Synonyms: outreason, defeat, conquer
Antonyms: lose to, concede
词性: 动词
