eco-efficient:/ˌiːkoʊɪˈfɪʃənt/| adj.|生态效率高的；环保高效的|Relating to or promoting products, services, or processes that reduce waste and pollution while minimizing the use of resources, thereby maximizing resource productivity and minimizing environmental impact.|This company is striving to become more eco-efficient by reducing its carbon footprint. 这家公司正在努力通过减少碳足迹来提高生态效率。|Eco-friendly, sustainable, green|Wasteful, inefficient|adjective


Etymology->
eco- (prefix): from Greek oikos, meaning "house, household, dwelling place."  This prefix denotes "ecology," "environment," or "ecological."
-efficient (suffix): from Latin efficiens, present participle of efficere "to work out, accomplish," from ex- "out" + facere "to do, make."  This suffix indicates "capable of producing a desired effect."
