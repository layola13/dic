meloniere: /ˈmɛləˌnɪər/ | n. |甜瓜温室，甜瓜种植园|A place where melons are grown, especially under glass.  A melon house.| Melons are grown in the meloniere during the summer months. 夏天，甜瓜在温室里种植。| The family built a meloniere to extend the growing season for their prized melons. 这家人建造了一个甜瓜温室来延长他们珍贵甜瓜的生长季节。| synonym: melon house, melon patch, hothouse | antonym: N/A | noun
