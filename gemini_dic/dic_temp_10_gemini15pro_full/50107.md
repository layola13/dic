dentiloquy:/ˌdɛntɪˈlɒkwi/
n.
腹语术
ventriloquism; the practice of speaking in such a way that the voice appears to come from a source other than the speaker, typically a dummy.
The magician's dentiloquy act, featuring a sassy rabbit puppet, was the highlight of the show.  魔术师的腹语表演，以一只活泼的小兔子木偶为主角，是演出的亮点。
近义词:ventriloquism
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "clear speech" or "articulate speech")
