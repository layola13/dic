/ˈmænər/ (US) /ˈmænə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 方式，举止，礼貌，风俗 |A way of acting or behaving.  |She has a charming manner. 她举止优雅。|Synonyms: way, style, approach, method, fashion, custom|Antonyms: impropriety, rudeness| Noun

词源 (For "Manner") ->

From Old French *maniere* "way, fashion, kind, sort," from Latin *manuarius* "of or belonging to the hand," from *manus* "hand."  The sense evolution is from "handling" to "way of handling" to "way of acting or behaving."
