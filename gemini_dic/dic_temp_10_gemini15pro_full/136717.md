nonsensations:/ˌnɒnsenˈseɪʃənz/ | n. | 无感觉；缺乏感觉；无意义的事情 |  The lack of sensations; a state of numbness or apathy, or things that are trivial and meaningless. |  He experienced nonsensations in his fingers after the accident.  事故发生后，他的手指失去了知觉。| synonym: numbness, apathy, trivialities | antonym: sensations, feelings, excitement | noun

nonsensation:/ˌnɒnsenˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 无感觉；缺乏感觉；无意义的事情 |  Something that does not cause a sensation or stir up excitement; a non-event. | The new product launch was a nonsensation, failing to generate any buzz.  新产品的发布毫无轰动效应，未能引起任何关注。| synonym: non-event, flop, dud | antonym: sensation, hit, success | noun
