lichenic:/laɪˈkɛnɪk/
adjective
地衣的；似地衣的
Relating to, resembling, or consisting of lichens.
Lichens are a complex life form that is a symbiotic partnership of two separate organisms, a fungus and an alga.
The lichenic growth on the rocks provided a splash of color.  岩石上的地衣生长增添了一抹色彩。
The biologist specialized in lichenic species of the high arctic.  这位生物学家专门研究高纬度北极地区的地衣物种。
Near synonyms:  lichenous, mossy
Antonyms:  algal (referring solely to algae), fungal (referring solely to fungi)
