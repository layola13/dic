mendole:/ˈmɛndəleɪ/| n.|曼陀林琴 (一种拨弦乐器)|A small, stringed musical instrument, similar to a lute, with a pear-shaped body.|He played a beautiful melody on his mendole. 他用他的曼陀林琴演奏了一段优美的旋律。|Synonyms: mandolin, mandora|Antonyms: N/A|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *mandolino*, diminutive of *mandola* "almond," referring to the shape of the instrument's body. Ultimately from Greek *amygdala* "almond."
