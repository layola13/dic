nonrepressed:/ˌnɒnrɪˈprɛst/ | adj. |  未被压抑的，未被抑制的 | Not repressed; allowed to be expressed or developed freely. |  Her nonrepressed emotions were evident in her passionate speech.  她毫无掩饰的情感在她充满激情的演讲中表露无遗。 | synonyms: uninhibited, unsuppressed, free, open, unrestrained | antonyms: repressed, suppressed, inhibited, restrained | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + repressed.  Repressed comes from the Latin repressus, past participle of reprimere "to press back, keep back, check, restrain."
