goggin:/ˈɡɒɡɪn/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːɡɪn/ (US)| n. |戈金（姓氏）|A surname of Irish origin, an Anglicized form of the Irish Ó Gogáin, meaning "descendant of Gogán".
