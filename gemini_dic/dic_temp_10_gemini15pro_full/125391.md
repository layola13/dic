multinationally:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəli/
adv.|跨国地，多国地|In a way that involves several different countries.|例句：The company operates multinationally. 这家公司跨国经营。|例句：The product is marketed multinationally. 该产品在多国销售。|近义词：internationally, globally|反义词：locally, domestically|词性:副词
nationally:/ˈnæʃənəli/
adv.|国家地，全国地|Throughout a nation; on a national scale.|例句：The company operates nationally. 这家公司在全国范围内运营。|例句：The product is sold nationally. 该产品在全国销售。|近义词：countrywide, nationwide|反义词：locally, regionally|词性:副词
national:/ˈnæʃənəl/
adj.|国家的，民族的|Relating to or characteristic of a nation; common to a whole nation.|例句：This is a national holiday. 这是一个全国性的节日。|例句：National unity is important. 国家团结很重要。|近义词：domestic, countrywide|反义词：international, foreign|词性:形容词
nation:/ˈneɪʃən/
n.|国家，民族|A large body of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular country or territory.|例句：The nation is proud of its athletes. 这个国家为它的运动员感到自豪。|例句：He belongs to a small nation. 他属于一个小国。|近义词：country, state|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
multi-: /ˈmʌlti-/
