antilogarithm: /ˌæntiˈlɔːɡərɪðəm/ | n. | 反对数 |  A number that corresponds to a given logarithm.  It is the inverse function of the logarithm. | The antilogarithm of 2 (base 10) is 100. 反对数2（以10为底）是100。| synonym: antilog | antonym: logarithm | noun
