indigenously:/ɪnˈdɪdʒənəsli/
adverb
本土地；土著地；原生地
in a way that is native to a particular place
The plant grows indigenously in the Amazon rainforest.  这种植物原生于亚马逊雨林。
Synonyms: natively, locally, naturally
Antonyms: foreignly, exotically, externally
词性: 副词
