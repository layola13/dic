inexpressive:/ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsɪv/| adj.|无表情的，缺乏表情的，不善表达的|Not conveying or revealing feelings or thoughts readily; lacking in expression.|Her face was inexpressive, giving no clue to her thoughts. 她的脸上毫无表情，让人猜不透她的想法。|Synonyms: blank, deadpan, emotionless, impassive, stoic|Antonyms: expressive, animated, demonstrative, eloquent|词性:形容词

inexpressive:/ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsɪv/| adj.|不鲜明的，不引人注目的|Not striking or noticeable.|The landscape was flat and inexpressive.  这片风景平坦而单调。|Synonyms: dull, uninteresting, monotonous, bland|Antonyms: striking, vivid, dramatic, interesting|词性:形容词
