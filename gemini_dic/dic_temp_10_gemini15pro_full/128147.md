nettable:/ˈnɛtəbl/
adjective
可净化的，可净得的
Able to be netted or yielded as net profit.  Relating to the amount of profit remaining after all deductions or expenses have been taken out.
The nettable income from the investment was higher than expected.  这笔投资的可净收入高于预期。
近义词: realizable, clear
反义词: gross, total
词性: adjective
