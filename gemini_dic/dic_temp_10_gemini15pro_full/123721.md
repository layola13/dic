moonling:/ˈmuːnlɪŋ/| n. |月饼|A rich, round pastry traditionally eaten during the Mid-Autumn Festival, especially in Chinese culture.  It typically contains a sweet filling, such as lotus seed paste or red bean paste, and may also include a salted duck egg yolk.|例句：Moonlings are a symbol of reunion and togetherness during the Mid-Autumn Festival. 月饼是中秋节团圆的象征。

例句：My grandmother makes delicious homemade moonlings every year. 我的祖母每年都做美味的自制月饼。|近义词：mooncake|反义词: (No direct antonym exists)|词性:noun
