pandermite:/ˈpændərmaɪt/| n. |硼酸钙石|A white, earthy mineral consisting of a hydrated calcium borate, Ca₂B₆O₁₁·3H₂O.  It is used as a source of borax.|Pandermite is mined in Turkey. 硼酸钙石在土耳其开采。|Synonyms: priceite, colemanite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
