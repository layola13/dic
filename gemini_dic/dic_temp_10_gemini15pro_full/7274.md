angor:/ˈæŋɡɔːr/ (US), /ˈæŋɡɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.| 痛苦，苦恼，焦虑 |A feeling of extreme distress, suffering, or pain, especially of the mind or body; anguish.|He suffered terrible angor following the death of his wife.| 近义词: anguish, agony, distress, torment, suffering | 反义词: comfort, relief, solace | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Latin *angor (anxiety, anguish, tightening of the throat), from angō (“I squeeze, strangle, vex, torment”), related to angustus (“narrow”).
