flaggy:/ˈflæɡi/ | adj. | 湿软的；下垂的；多菖蒲的 | resembling or containing a flag or flags; weak, limp, or insipid | The soil in the marsh was flaggy and difficult to walk on.  沼泽地的土壤湿软，难以行走。|  The flaggy leaves drooped in the heat. 这些下垂的叶子在高温下枯萎了。 | 近义词：limp, drooping, weak, soggy | 反义词：firm, stiff, strong, dry | 词性:形容词

词源: From flag (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  源自名词 flag（旗帜，菖蒲） + 形容词后缀 -y。
