expectedly:/ɪkˈspektɪdli/
adverb|意料之中地，不出所料地|As expected, the traffic was terrible. 正如预期的那样，交通状况很糟糕。|例句：Expectedly, she won the competition.  不出所料，她赢得了比赛。|近义词：predictably, unsurprisingly, naturally|反义词:unexpectedly, surprisingly|词性:副词
词源: From "expected" + "-ly." "Expected" comes from the Latin "expectare" (to look out for, await, anticipate).
