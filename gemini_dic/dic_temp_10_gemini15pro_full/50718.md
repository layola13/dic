dernier:/dɛʁ.nje/ (French), /ˈdɜːr.njər/ (anglicized)| n.|最后的人或事物|The last one; the last remaining thing or person.|例句：He was the dernier of his family. 他是他家族的最后一人。|近义词：last, final one, survivor, remnant|反义词：first, pioneer|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *derainier, later *dernnier, from Vulgar Latin *deretaneus, from Latin de- + retro “backward.”  Related to English "rear."  The modern French spelling with -ier comes from analogy with other adjectives.
