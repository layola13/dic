fauxer:/ˈfoʊər/| n.|伪造者，赝品制造者|A person who creates a faux, or imitation, typically of a work of art, piece of furniture, or other decorative object.  A person who makes or sells fakes.|The painting was discovered to be the work of a skilled fauxer.|近义词：forger, counterfeiter, imitator, copyist|反义词：authenticator|词性:noun
