arenot:/ɑːr nɒt/ /ɑːr nɑːt/ | v. | are not, 不是 (be 动词 are 的否定形式) |  A contraction of "are not."  Used to indicate the negative form of the present tense plural and second person singular of the verb "to be". | They arenot coming to the party. 他们不会来参加派对。| We arenot happy with the results. 我们对结果不满意。 | Near synonyms: aren't, ain't (informal) | Antonym: are | verb

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

PHRASE->
(arenot 本身就是一个词组，它不和其他词构成固定搭配。以下列出包含are的常用搭配。)
You are what you eat. 人如其食
They are on their way. 他们正在路上。
Things are not what they seem.  事情并非表面那样。
We are in trouble. 我们有麻烦了。
They are out of town. 他们出城了。



注意事项: 题目要求分析"arenot"，而arenot主要用于一般现在时，其他时态用其他be动词形式的否定。在完成时态中，are not been的用法是错误的。正确用法是 haven't been.  未来时态用will not be.
