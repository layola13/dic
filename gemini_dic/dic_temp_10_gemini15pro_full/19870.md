bingers:/ˈbɪndʒərz/| n.| 狂欢作乐的人;  放纵者 (尤指饮食);  煲剧的人|People who engage in excessive consumption of something, especially food, alcohol, or entertainment, over a short period of time.  It often implies a loss of control.|例句：The weekend bingers emptied the fridge.  周末狂欢的人把冰箱清空了。

例句：Netflix bingers often find themselves sleep-deprived.  Netflix煲剧的人经常发现自己睡眠不足。|近义词：overindulgers, addicts, devotees (when referring to hobbies)|反义词:moderates, abstainers|词性:noun (plural)
