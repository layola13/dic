antheridium:/ænθəˈrɪdiəm/| n. | 雄器；精子器 |The male reproductive organ of algae, fungi, mosses, ferns, and other nonflowering plants, usually a single cell or a multicellular structure containing male gametes.|  The fern's antheridium releases sperm into the water.  蕨类的雄器将精子释放到水中。 | Synonyms:  spermatangium | Antonyms: archegonium, oogonium | noun

antheridia:/ænθəˈrɪdiə/| n. | 雄器；精子器 (复数) | The plural form of antheridium, referring to multiple male reproductive organs.| The antheridia are located on the underside of the prothallus. 雄器位于原叶体的下侧。| Synonyms: spermatangia | Antonyms: archegonia, oogonia | noun
