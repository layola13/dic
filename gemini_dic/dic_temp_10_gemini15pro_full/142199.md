orneriness:/ˈɔːrnərɪnɪs/
noun
固执；乖戾；倔强
The state of being ornery; cantankerousness, irritability, stubbornness.
His orneriness made it difficult to work with him. 他的固执使得很难和他共事。
Synonyms:  cantankerousness,  cussedness,  grumpiness,  irascibility,  irritability,  perverseness,  spitefulness,  stubbornness,  contrariness,  crossness
Antonyms:  agreeableness,  amenability,  compliance,  docility,  tractability
