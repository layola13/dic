ozone-friendly:/ˈoʊzoʊn ˈfrɛndli/| adj.|臭氧友好的|Not harmful to the ozone layer.  Designed or manufactured in a way that minimizes damage to the ozone layer.|This new air conditioner is ozone-friendly.|Synonyms: environmentally friendly, eco-friendly|Antonyms: ozone-depleting|adjective

ozone:/ˈoʊzoʊn/| n.|臭氧|A pale blue gas (O3) that is an allotrope of oxygen.  It is present in the upper atmosphere and absorbs harmful ultraviolet radiation from the sun.|The ozone layer protects us from harmful radiation.|Synonyms: O3|Antonyms: N/A|noun
