flitter:/ˈflɪtər/ /ˈflɪdər/| v. n.|轻快地飞舞，飘动；（金钱、时间）迅速消逝；碎片，小片|To move lightly and swiftly; to pass quickly (especially of time or money); a small piece or fragment|Butterflies flittered among the flowers. 蝴蝶在花丛中飞舞。|近义词：flutter, dart, flit, flash, flicker|反义词：plod, trudge|verb

flitter:/ˈflɪtər/ /ˈflɪdər/| n.|碎片，小片|A small piece or fragment|Glittering flitters of foil decorated the Christmas tree.  闪闪发光的金属箔碎片装饰着圣诞树。|近义词：fragment, shard, sliver, speck|反义词：whole, entirety|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fliteren, frequentative of flitten (“to fly or move quickly”), from Old Norse flytja (“to transport, remove”), from Proto-Germanic *flutjaną (“to float, swim, fly”), from Proto-Indo-European *plewd- (“to flow, float, swim, fly”).
