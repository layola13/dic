notodontian:/ˌnoʊtəˈdɒntiən/| adj. n.| 舟蛾科的；舟蛾科昆虫|Relating to or characteristic of moths of the family Notodontidae, typically having hairy larvae and often holding a distinctive posture at rest.|The notodontian caterpillar camouflaged itself perfectly against the bark of the tree.  舟蛾科毛虫完美地伪装在树皮上。| The entomologist specialized in notodontian moths. 这位昆虫学家专门研究舟蛾科的飞蛾。|近义词: none (more specific term)|反义词: none|词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *νοτος* (notos, “back”) + *ὀδούς* (odous, “tooth”), referring to the tooth-like projections on the wings of some species.
