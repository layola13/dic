gainly:/ˈɡeɪnli/| adj.|优美的，优雅的，灵巧的|Moving with effortless grace and elegance; graceful.|She was a tall, gainly woman with a dancer's poise. 她是一位高挑、优雅的女人，举手投足间都带着舞者的气质。|近义词：graceful, elegant, agile, nimble|反义词：awkward, clumsy, ungainly|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *gegnligr "straight, direct," from gegn "straight, short."  The original sense was "direct," "ready," then "suitable, convenient," and finally "graceful."
