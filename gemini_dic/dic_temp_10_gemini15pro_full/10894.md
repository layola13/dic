araeostyle:/ˌærɪˈɒstaɪl/| adj.|稀柱式的；柱间距宽的|Characterized by wide spacing between columns, typically four or more times the diameter of the columns.  This style creates a sense of openness and spaciousness. | The temple was built in the araeostyle manner, with its columns widely spaced. 这座寺庙以稀柱式建造，柱子间距很宽。

The architect chose an araeostyle design for the colonnade, emphasizing the vastness of the space.  这位建筑师为柱廊选择了稀柱式设计，强调了空间的广阔。|近义词：wide-spaced, widely-spaced|反义词：pycnostyle|词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *ἀραιόστυλος* (araióstulos), from *ἀραιός* (araiós, “loose, sparse”) + *στῦλος* (stûlos, “column”).
