glebe-lands:/ˈɡliːbˌlændz/
noun
耕地，教会土地
Land belonging to a parish church or ecclesiastical benefice.
The glebe-lands provided income for the vicar. 教会的土地为牧师提供了收入。
Synonyms: church lands, parish lands, ecclesiastical lands
Antonyms: secular lands, private lands
词性: noun
