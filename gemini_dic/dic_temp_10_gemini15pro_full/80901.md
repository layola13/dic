governors:/ˈɡʌvərnərz/
n.
州长；总督；统治者；管理者
A governor is the elected head of a state in the US.  州长是美国一个州的民选首脑。
The governors of several states met to discuss the issue.  几个州的州长开会讨论这个问题。
近义词: administrators, directors, managers, officials, rulers, supervisors
反义词: governed, subjects
词性: noun (plural)

governors:/ˈɡʌvərnərz/
n.
(机构、组织等的)理事，董事
The governors of the school are meeting tonight. 学校的理事们今晚要开会。
The board of governors is responsible for the overall management of the organization. 理事会负责该组织的全面管理。
近义词: board members, trustees, directors
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Old French *governeur*, from Latin *gubernator* "pilot, steersman, governor," from *gubernare* "to steer, guide, govern,"  related to Greek *kubernan* "to steer, pilot."
