exigible:/ɪˈksɪdʒɪbl/
adjective
可要求的；应立即支付的；可强制执行的
due; demandable; requiring immediate payment or performance.
The loan is exigible on December 31st. 这笔贷款在12月31日到期应偿还。
Synonyms: due, payable, demandable, claimable, collectible
Antonyms: non-exigible, deferrable, postponable
词性: adjective
