gee-gee:/ˈdʒiːdʒiː/
noun
儿童用语，马
A children's word for a horse.
例句：The little girl rode her gee-gee around the park. 小女孩骑着她的马在公园里转了一圈。

例句："Giddy up, gee-gee!" shouted the child. “驾，马儿！”孩子喊道。

近义词：horse, pony
反义词:None
词性:n.
