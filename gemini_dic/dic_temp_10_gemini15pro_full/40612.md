convener:/kənˈviːnər/| n.|召集人，召集者|A person who convenes or arranges a meeting or activity. |The convener of the committee announced the next meeting. 委员会的召集人宣布了下次会议的时间。|Synonyms: organizer, chairperson, facilitator|Antonyms: attendee, participant|noun

convene:/kənˈviːn/| v.|召集，集合|To come or bring together for a meeting or activity; to assemble formally. |The CEO convened a meeting of the board of directors. 首席执行官召集了董事会会议。|Synonyms: assemble, gather, summon|Antonyms: disperse, scatter, adjourn|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *convenen*, from Anglo-Norman *convenir*, from Old French *convenir*, from Latin *convenīre* (“to come together, assemble, meet, agree”), from *com-* (“together”) + *venīre* (“to come”).
