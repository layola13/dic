hood-like:/hʊd-laɪk/
adjective
像兜帽的；罩状的
Resembling a hood; shaped like a hood.
The cobra raised its hood-like head.  眼镜蛇抬起它像兜帽一样的头。
The flower had hood-like petals.  这朵花有像兜帽一样的花瓣。
Synonyms: hooded, cowled, capped
Antonyms: uncovered, open, exposed
词性: 形容词
