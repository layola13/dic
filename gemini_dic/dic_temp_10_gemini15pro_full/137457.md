nonthreaded:/ˌnɒnˈθrɛdɪd/ | adj. | 非线程的，单线程的 | Not using or designed for multiple threads of execution, typically referring to software or processes. |  This application is nonthreaded and can only handle one task at a time.  这个应用程序是非线程的，一次只能处理一个任务。 | Example: The older version of the software was nonthreaded, leading to performance bottlenecks.  旧版本的软件是非线程的，导致性能瓶颈。| Synonyms: single-threaded, serial | Antonyms: multithreaded, threaded | adjective
