belles-lettres:/ˌbelˈlɛtrə/ /ˌbɛlˈlɛtər/| n. |纯文学；优美文学；文学研究|Literature regarded as a fine art, especially as having a purely aesthetic function.  Refers to literary studies, especially of poetry, drama, fiction, and essays.|  He devoted his life to belles-lettres. 他一生致力于纯文学。|Synonyms: literature,  literary works,  writings,  creative writing,  fiction,  poetry,  prose|Antonyms: non-fiction, technical writing|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French belles lettres, literally "beautiful letters."
