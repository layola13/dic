eponymist:/ɪˈpɒnɪmɪst/
n.
命名者；名字被用来命名的人
A person for whom something is or is thought to be named.
The sandwich is named after the fourth Earl of Sandwich - he is its eponymist.  三明治是以第四代三明治伯爵的名字命名的——他是它的命名者。
近义词:namesake
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun


eponymous:/ɪˈpɒnɪməs/
adj.
同名的；以此命名的
Giving one's name to a tribe, place, etc.
The eponymous hero of the novel is a young woman. 这部小说的同名主人公是一位年轻女子。
近义词:namesake
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:adjective


词源: From Greek *epōnymos* “giving a name,” from *epi* "upon, at, after" + *onoma* "name".
