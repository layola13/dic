nimblest:/ˈnɪmbləst/| adj.|最敏捷的，最灵活的|Superlative form of nimble: most nimble.  It means quick and light in movement or action; agile.|The nimblest of the acrobats performed a breathtaking routine.  最敏捷的杂技演员表演了一套惊险的动作。|The gymnast was the nimblest of all the competitors and easily won the gold medal. 体操运动员是所有参赛者中最灵活的，轻松地赢得了金牌。|近义词：quickest, fastest, most agile, most adept|反义词：slowest, clumsiest|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nemel*, from Old English *nǣmel* (“quick to grasp, capable of catching”), from Proto-Germanic *namiz* (“able to seize”), from Proto-Indo-European *nem- (“to take, distribute”).  Cognate with German *nehmen* (“to take”).  The suffix *-est* marks the superlative form.
