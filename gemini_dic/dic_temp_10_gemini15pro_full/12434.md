askewnesses:/əˈskjuːnɪsɪz/
|n.|偏度，歪斜度|A measure of the asymmetry of the probability distribution of a real-valued random variable about its mean. It describes the degree to which a distribution is skewed to the left or right.|The data showed significant askewnesses, indicating a non-normal distribution. 数据显示出显著的偏度，表明其非正态分布。|synonyms: skewness, asymmetry|antonyms: symmetry|noun
