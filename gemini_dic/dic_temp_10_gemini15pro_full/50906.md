deserve: /dɪˈzɜːrv/ (British), /dɪˈzɜːrv/ (American)
verb
应得；值得
If someone deserves something, it is right that they should have it, because of the way they have behaved or the qualities they have.
She deserves a promotion after all her hard work. 她工作如此努力，应该升职。
He deserves to be punished for his crimes. 他罪有应得。
