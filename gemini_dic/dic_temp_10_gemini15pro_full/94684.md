imprisoning:/ɪmˈprɪzənɪŋ/
verb
监禁；禁锢
confining someone in prison or another place they cannot escape from.
The government is imprisoning political dissidents. 政府正在监禁政治异议人士。
He was imprisoned for ten years for his crimes.  他因犯罪被监禁了十年。
Synonyms: confining, incarcerating, jailing, detaining
Antonyms: releasing, freeing, liberating
