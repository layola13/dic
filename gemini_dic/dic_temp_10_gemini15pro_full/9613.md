antispiritualistic:/ˌæntiˌspɪrɪtʃuəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反唯灵论的
opposed to spiritualism; not spiritualistic
He held an antispiritualistic view of the universe. 他对宇宙持有反唯灵论的观点。

Her father, a staunch materialist, held strongly antispiritualistic beliefs. 她父亲，一个坚定的唯物主义者，持有强烈的反唯灵论信仰。

近义词:materialistic, rationalistic, skeptical
反义词:spiritualistic, mystical, supernatural
