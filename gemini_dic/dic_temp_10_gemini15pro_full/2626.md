adopt: /əˈdɒpt/ (UK) /əˈdɑːpt/ (US)| vt.| 收养，领养；采用，采纳；正式接受|To legally take another's child and raise it as one's own. To start to use a particular method or to show a particular attitude. To formally accept a suggestion or proposal.|They adopted a child from China. 他们从中国收养了一个孩子。

The company decided to adopt a new marketing strategy. 这家公司决定采用新的营销策略。|近义词：foster, embrace, accept, choose|反义词：reject, abandon, disown|词性：动词

adopt: /əˈdɒpt/ (UK) /əˈdɑːpt/ (US)| n.| (过继的)子女|A child who has been adopted.|She is their adopted daughter. 她是他们的养女。|近义词：foster child|反义词：biological child|词性：名词 (罕见)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Many couples adopt children when they can't have their own. = 许多夫妇在无法生育的情况下会选择领养孩子。
He adopts a cautious approach to investing. = 他采取谨慎的投资方式。

一般过去时:
They adopted their son ten years ago. = 他们十年前收养了他们的儿子。
The school adopted a new curriculum last year. = 学校去年采用了新的课程。


现在完成时:
She has adopted two children from overseas. = 她从海外收养了两个孩子。
The government has adopted a new policy on immigration. = 政府已经采取了新的移民政策。


过去完成时:
They had already adopted a dog before they got a cat. = 在他们养猫之前，他们已经收养了一条狗。
The committee had adopted the proposal before the public hearing. = 委员会在公开听证会之前已经通过了这项提案。


将来时:
They will adopt a baby girl next month. = 他们下个月将收养一个女婴。
The company will adopt new technologies to improve efficiency. = 公司将采用新技术来提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If I were to adopt a child, I would adopt a girl. = 如果我要收养一个孩子，我会收养一个女孩。
I wish they would adopt a more flexible approach. = 我希望他们能采取更灵活的方法。


被动语态:

The proposal was adopted unanimously by the board. = 董事会一致通过了该提案。


疑问句:
Why did they adopt a child? = 他们为什么收养孩子？
Have you considered adopting a pet? = 你考虑过养宠物吗？


否定句:

The company didn't adopt the new technology. = 这家公司没有采用新技术。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English adopten, from Old French adopter, from Latin adoptāre (“to choose for oneself, select, assume”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + optāre (“to choose, wish, desire”).
