headily:/ˈhedɪli/| adv.|轻率地，鲁莽地；令人兴奋地；强烈地|Rashly or carelessly; in an exciting or intoxicating way; intensely.|He drank the champagne headily. 他狂饮香槟。|近义词：recklessly, rashly, impetuously, wildly, excitedly, intensely|反义词：cautiously, carefully, prudently|词性:副词

Etymology: From heady + -ly.  "Heady" comes from Middle English *hedi, from Old English *hēdig, from hēafod "head."  The original sense referred to affecting the head, as with strong drink.
