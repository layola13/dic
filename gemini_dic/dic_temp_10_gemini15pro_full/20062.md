biopoeic:/ˌbaɪəʊˈpɔɪɪk/ | adj. | 生命产生的，生命起源的 | Relating to biopoiesis, the origin and evolution of life or living organisms from inorganic or inanimate substances. |  The scientist dedicated his life to understanding the biopoeic processes that led to life on Earth.  这位科学家毕生致力于了解导致地球生命出现的生命起源过程。

The conference focused on recent advancements in biopoeic research.  这次会议的重点是生命起源研究的最新进展。 | 近义词：biogenetic, abiogenetic (in some contexts, referring to the origin of life itself) | 反义词：None | 词性: adjective

词源:
bio- (life) + -poeic (making, producing) from Greek 'bios' (life) and 'poiein' (to make).
