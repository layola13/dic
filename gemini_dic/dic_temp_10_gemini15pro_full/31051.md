chameleon:/kəˈmiːliən/ | n. |变色龙，善变的人|A lizard that can change the color of its skin to match its surroundings. Also, a person who changes their behavior or opinions to fit in with different people or situations. |例句：The chameleon blended in perfectly with the leaves. 变色龙完美地融入了树叶中。

例句：He's a bit of a chameleon; he changes his personality depending on who he's with. 他有点像变色龙，他会根据与他在一起的人改变自己的个性。|近义词：adapter, conformist |反义词：nonconformist, individualist| noun
