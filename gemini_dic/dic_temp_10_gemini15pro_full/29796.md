caucusing:/ˈkɔːkəsɪŋ/
n.|核心小组会议；预选会议；党团会议|The act of holding or participating in a caucus.  A meeting of supporters or members of a specific political party or movement.|The Democrats are caucusing to choose their nominee. 民主党人正在召开预选会议，以选出他们的候选人。|近义词：meeting, conference, assembly|反义词: N/A
词性:名词 (gerund)

caucus:/ˈkɔːkəs/
n.|核心小组；预选会议；党团会议|A meeting of supporters or members of a specific political party or movement.|The Republican caucus will be held next week. 共和党核心小组会议将于下周举行。|近义词：meeting, conference, assembly|反义词: N/A
词性:名词

caucus:/ˈkɔːkəs/
v.|召开核心小组会议；参加预选会议|To hold or participate in a caucus.|The senators caucused to discuss the new bill. 参议员们召开核心小组会议讨论新法案。|近义词：meet, confer, assemble|反义词: N/A
词性:动词


词源: From Algonquian (probably Natick) *kaukauasu 'counselor'.
