heeding:/ˈhiːdɪŋ/| n.|注意，留心|The act of paying attention to something; taking notice of.|例句：Heeding the warnings, they turned back before the storm hit. 注意到警告后，他们在暴风雨来临前返回了。|例句：She wasn't heeding my advice and went ahead with her plans. 她没有理会我的建议，继续执行她的计划。|近义词：attention, notice, observance, mindfulness|反义词:ignorance, neglect, disregard|词性:noun (gerund or verbal noun)
heed:/hiːd/| v.|注意，留心|To pay attention to; take notice of.|例句：Heed my warning, there's danger ahead. 注意我的警告，前面有危险。|例句：She didn't heed the doctor's advice and her condition worsened. 她没有听从医生的建议，病情恶化了。|近义词：listen to, obey, follow, mind|反义词:ignore, disobey, disregard|词性:verb
