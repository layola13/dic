idealic: /aɪˈdiːlɪk/ /aɪˈdilɪk/
adjective
理想的；田园诗般的
Extremely pleasant, beautiful, or peaceful.  Suggesting a perfect natural scene.
例句：
We spent an idyllic week in the countryside.
我们在乡下度过了一个田园诗般的星期。

例句：
The island is an idyllic vacation spot.
这个岛屿是一个理想的度假胜地。

近义词：perfect, utopian, picturesque, peaceful, tranquil, serene
反义词：unpleasant, flawed, imperfect, dystopian, chaotic, stressful
词性: adjective
