cubing:/ˈkjuːbɪŋ/| n.|立方；求立方；三次幂；魔方运动|The process of raising a number to the third power; the activity of solving a Rubik's Cube.|

例句：
Cubing two gives you eight. 
两的三次方是八。

He enjoys cubing and can solve a Rubik's Cube in under a minute.
他喜欢玩魔方，能在不到一分钟内复原一个魔方。

近义词：raising to the third power|反义词:|词性:noun
