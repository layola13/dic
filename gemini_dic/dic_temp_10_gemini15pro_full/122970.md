monadelphia: /ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfiə/ | n. | 单体雄蕊 | The condition of having all the stamens united into one bundle by their filaments, with the anthers remaining free. | Example: Monadelphia is a characteristic feature of some plant families. 单体雄蕊是一些植物科的特征。 | Example: The flowers exhibit monadelphia, with their stamens fused into a single structure. 这些花展现出单体雄蕊，雄蕊融合成一个单一的结构。 | Synonym: monadelphy | Antonym: polyandria, diadelphia | Noun
