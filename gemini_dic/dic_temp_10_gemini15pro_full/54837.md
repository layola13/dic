dispiteous:/dɪˈspɪtiəs/
adjective
无情的，冷酷的，残忍的
Lacking pity or compassion; merciless; cruel.
He gave a dispiteous laugh as he watched his enemy fall. 他看着敌人倒下，发出无情的笑声。
近义词: merciless, ruthless, cruel, pitiless, inhumane
反义词: merciful, compassionate, kind, humane
词性: adjective


dispiteous:/dɪˈspɪtiəs/
词源: From Middle English *dispitous*, from Old French *despitous*, from *despit* (“contempt, scorn”) + *-ous*.  Ultimately from Latin *dēspicere* (“to look down on”).
