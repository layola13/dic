nonbeneficently:/nɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəntli/
adverb
无益地，没有好处地
in a way that does not produce any benefits or good effects.
The policy was implemented nonbeneficently, leading to widespread hardship.  这项政策的实施没有带来任何好处，反而导致了普遍的困难。
He acted nonbeneficently, prioritizing personal gain over the welfare of others. 他行事无益，将个人利益置于他人福祉之上。
Synonyms: harmfully, detrimentally, disadvantageously
Antonyms: beneficially, advantageously, helpfully
词性: 副词
