obtuse:/əbˈtʃuːs/ /ɑːbˈtuːs/
adjective
钝的；迟钝的；愚笨的
(of an angle) more than 90° and less than 180°.  Not quick or alert in perception, feeling, or intellect; not sensitive or observant; dull.

He made an obtuse remark about her appearance.
他说了句关于她外貌的愚蠢话。


The second corner is obtuse, almost flat.
第二个角是钝角，几乎是平的。


近义词: blunt, dull, stupid, insensitive
反义词: acute, sharp, intelligent, perceptive
词性: adjective
