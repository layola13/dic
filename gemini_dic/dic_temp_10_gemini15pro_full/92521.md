hypotrich/ˈhaɪpətrɪk/ |n.| 下毛类纤毛虫 |A ciliate protozoan of the subclass Hypotrichia, characterized by cilia grouped into cirri on the ventral surface.|Hypotricha are common in freshwater habitats. 下毛类纤毛虫常见于淡水栖息地。|近义词: ciliate |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

hypotrichs/ˈhaɪpətrɪks/ |n.| 下毛类纤毛虫（复数）|Plural form of hypotrich, referring to ciliate protozoans of the subclass Hypotrichia.|Many hypotrichs are found in soil. 许多下毛类纤毛虫存在于土壤中。|近义词: ciliates |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
