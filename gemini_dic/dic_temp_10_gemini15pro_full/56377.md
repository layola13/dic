dolabriform:/ˌdoʊləˈbrɪfɔːrm/| adj.|斧形的，似斧状的|Shaped like a hatchet or axe; axe-shaped.|The dolabriform leaves of the plant were its most distinctive feature.  这种植物斧形的叶子是它最显著的特征。|Example 2:  The ancient tool was dolabriform, suggesting it was used for chopping. 这件古代工具呈斧形，表明它是用来砍东西的。|Synonyms: axe-shaped, hatchet-shaped, securiform|Antonyms: N/A|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dolabriformis*, from dolabra (“axe, mattock”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
