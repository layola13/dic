muschelkalk:/ˈmʊʃəlkælk/ | n. |  壳灰岩，贝壳灰岩 | A type of limestone formed largely from fossilized shells, particularly common in the Triassic period of Europe.  It literally translates from German as "mussel chalk". | The vineyards in Franconia are known for their Muschelkalk soils, which contribute to the wines' minerality.  弗兰肯地区的葡萄园以其壳灰岩土壤而闻名，这有助于葡萄酒的矿物质含量。| The castle was built on a foundation of Muschelkalk. 这座城堡建在壳灰岩地基上。 |synonyms: shell limestone,  Triassic limestone | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps other rock types like granite or sandstone could be considered contextually opposite.) | noun
