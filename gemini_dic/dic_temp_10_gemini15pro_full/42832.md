course:/kɔːrs/ (British) /kɔːrs/ (American)| n. v.|课程; 路线; 行程;一道菜|A series of lessons or lectures on a particular subject. Also, the route or direction followed by a ship, aircraft, road, or river. Can also refer to a particular dish in a meal.|

例句：
The university offers a wide range of courses.
这所大学提供各种各样的课程。

The ship altered its course to avoid the storm.
这艘船改变了航线以避开暴风雨。


近义词：class, program, route, path, direction, dish|
反义词：deviation, digression|
词性：noun, verb


course:/kɔːrs/ (British) /kɔːrs/ (American)| v.|进行;发生;快速流动|To move swiftly or freely, especially of liquids.|

例句：
Tears coursed down her cheeks.
泪水顺着她的脸颊流了下来。

The river courses through the valley.
这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。

近义词：flow, run, stream, proceed|
反义词：stagnate, halt|
词性：verb
