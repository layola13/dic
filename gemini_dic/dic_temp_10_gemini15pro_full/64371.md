erector:/ɪˈrɛktər/| n.|安装者，竖立者，建造者|A person or thing that erects something, especially a structure or building. |The erector of the crane was highly skilled.  起重机安装者技术娴熟。|Synonyms: builder, constructor, assembler|Antonyms: demolisher, destroyer|noun

erect:/ɪˈrɛkt/| vt.|竖立，建造，安装|To construct (something) by putting parts or material together. |They erected a statue in his honor. 他们为他竖立了一座雕像。|Synonyms: build, construct, assemble|Antonyms: demolish, destroy|verb
