non-stock:/ˌnɑːnˈstɑːk/ | adj. | 非股份的，非股票的 | Not organized as a corporation with shares of stock that can be bought and sold. |  A non-stock corporation is usually formed for purposes other than making a profit.  一个非股份公司通常是为了盈利以外的目的而成立的。 | Example:  Many non-profit organizations are structured as non-stock corporations. 许多非营利组织的结构是非股份公司。 | Synonym:  not-for-profit, non-profit | Antonym: for-profit, stock, public | Adjective
