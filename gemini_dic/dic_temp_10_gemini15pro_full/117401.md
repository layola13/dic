medicating:/ˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
用药；治疗
administering medicine to (someone or something) or, in informal use, drugs.
The doctor is medicating the patient for pain. 医生正在给病人用药止痛。
treating (someone or something) with a medicine or drug.
He medicated his headache with aspirin. 他用阿司匹林治疗头痛。
synonyms: treating, dosing, drugging, administering medicine to
antonyms: abstaining
