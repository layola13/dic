abscind: /æbˈsɪnd/
verb
切除，剪除
To cut off; to separate or remove, typically by cutting.
The surgeon abscinded the diseased tissue.  外科医生切除了病变组织。

The gardener abscinded the dead branches from the tree. 园丁把树上的枯枝剪掉了。

近义词: sever, detach, excise, cut off
反义词: attach, join, connect
词性：v.
