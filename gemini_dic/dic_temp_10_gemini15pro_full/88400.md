hiron: /ˈhaɪrən/ (假设发音，因为这不是一个标准英语单词) | n. | 燕子（古英语或方言用法）| An archaic or dialectal word for a swallow. |  The hiron dipped and soared through the sky. 燕子在天空中俯冲翱翔。| 近义词：swallow, martin | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English *hyrne, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *swinjon.  Related to Old High German swalawa, German Schwalbe.  The modern English "swallow" largely replaced "hiron" in common usage.
