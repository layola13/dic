alkalization:/ˌælkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |碱化；碱性化|The process of making something alkaline, or increasing its alkalinity. |Soil alkalization is a common problem in arid regions. 土壤碱化是干旱地区的常见问题。| The alkalization of the water made it safe to drink. 水的碱化使其可以安全饮用。 | 近义词:neutralization, basification | 反义词: acidification | 词性:名词

词源: From alkali + -ization. Alkali comes from Arabic *al-qalīy*, meaning "ashes of the saltwort".
