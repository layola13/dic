crowers:/ˈkroʊərz/| n.| 啼叫者；（尤指）公鸡 |Those crowers woke me up every morning. 那些啼叫者每天早晨都把我吵醒。|近义词：roosters, cocks|反义词:hens|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "crow" (verb) + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns). "Crow" comes from Old English "crāwan," imitative of the sound.
