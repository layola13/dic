fasciculated:/fəˈsɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. |簇生的，束状的| Arranged in or forming bundles or fascicles; clustered together. | The fasciculated roots of the old tree spread out like a network. 这棵老树簇生的根像网络一样散开。|  The plant has fasciculated stems. 这种植物有簇生的茎。| 近义词：bundled, clustered, fascicular | 反义词：scattered, dispersed | 词性: adjective

fasciculate:/ˈfæsɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. |使成束，使簇生 | To form into bundles or fascicles; to cluster together. |  The nerves fasciculate to form a larger nerve trunk. 神经纤维束在一起形成更大的神经干。 | The artist fasciculated the dried flowers into a beautiful arrangement. 艺术家把干花扎成美丽的造型。 | 近义词：bundle, cluster | 反义词：separate, disperse | 词性: verb

词源: From Latin fasciculus (small bundle, diminutive of fascis, bundle), from Proto-Italic *faski-, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhasko- (band, tie).
