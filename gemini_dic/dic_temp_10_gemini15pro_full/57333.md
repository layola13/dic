downwardly:/ˈdaʊnwərdli/
adverb|向下地，下降地|Moving or extending downwards.  Tending to go down or decrease.|The graph shows the stock price moving downwardly.|Synonyms: downwards, descendingly|Antonyms: upwardly, ascendingly|词性: 副词

downward:/ˈdaʊnwərd/
adjective|向下的|Moving or extending downwards.|The downward spiral of the economy is concerning. 经济的螺旋式下降令人担忧。|Synonyms: descending, falling|Antonyms: upward, rising|词性: 形容词
downward:/ˈdaʊnwərd/
adverb|向下地|Towards a lower place or level.|She looked downward and saw the valley below. 她向下看去，看到了下面的山谷。|Synonyms: downwards|Antonyms: upwards|词性: 副词
