go-for-it:/ˌɡoʊ ˈfɔːr ɪt/| idiom | 加油；努力争取；放手一搏 | An expression of encouragement to pursue something or take a risk. | Example: "I'm thinking of applying for that promotion." "Go for it!"  “我正在考虑申请那个晋升职位。” “加油！” | 近义词: give it a try, take a chance, go all out | 反义词: hold back, hesitate, give up | 词性: idiom

go-for-it:/ˌɡoʊ ˈfɔːr ɪt/| idiom | 尽力争取；不遗余力 | To try one's best to achieve something. | Example: He decided to go for it and try to break the world record. 他决定全力以赴，争取打破世界纪录。 | 近义词: strive for, make every effort, do one's best | 反义词: hold back, give up easily | 词性: idiom
