antievolutionist:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪst/
n.
反进化论者
Someone who opposes the theory of evolution.
The antievolutionist argued against the fossil record.  反进化论者反对化石记录。
The speaker, a known antievolutionist, dismissed the evidence presented.  演讲者，一个著名的反进化论者，驳斥了提出的证据。
近义词:creationist
反义词:evolutionist
词性:名词
