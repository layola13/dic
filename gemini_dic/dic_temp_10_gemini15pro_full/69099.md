fauvism:/ˈfɔːvɪzəm/| n.|野兽派|A style of painting with vivid expressionistic and non-naturalistic use of color that was characteristic of a group of early 20th-century French artists led by Henri Matisse.|The museum's exhibit showcased several key works of Fauvism.  博物馆的展览展示了几幅重要的野兽派作品。|Synonym:  Expressionism, non-naturalism|Antonym: Realism, Naturalism|noun
