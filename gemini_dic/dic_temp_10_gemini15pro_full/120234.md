mingier:/ˈmɪndʒiər/| adj.|更吝啬的，更小气的|Comparative form of "mingy," meaning more unwilling to spend money or give things to people.|He's become even mingier since he lost his job.  他失业后变得更加吝啬了。|Synonyms: stingier, miserly, parsimonious|Antonyms: generous, liberal, magnanimous|adjective

Etymology:
From Scottish and Northern English dialect "mingy," of uncertain origin, possibly related to "mingle" in the sense of withholding something from a mixture.
