distributionist:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənɪst/
n.| 分配主义者；分配论者 | A person who advocates or supports distributionism, a socio-economic theory that advocates widespread ownership of the means of production.| He's a strong distributionist, believing that economic power should be more evenly spread.| 近义词: decentralist, localist | 反义词: centralist, collectivist | 词性: 名词
