benzoinated:/ˈbɛnzoʊˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|苯甲酸酯化的，用苯甲酸处理过的|Treated with benzoic acid or a benzoate, especially as a preservative.|The food contains benzoinated oil.  这种食物含有苯甲酸酯化的油。|Example: Benzoinated lard is commonly used in baking.  苯甲酸酯化的猪油常用于烘焙。|Synonyms: preserved, treated|Antonyms: untreated, natural|adjective
