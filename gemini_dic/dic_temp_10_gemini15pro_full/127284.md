navigability:/ˌnævɪɡəˈbɪləti/| n.|适航性，可航性；可通行性|The navigability of the river is affected by the tides. 这条河的通航能力受潮汐影响。|例句：The navigability of the river was improved after dredging.  疏浚后，这条河的通航能力得到了改善。

例句：The government is investing in improving the navigability of inland waterways. 政府正在投资改善内陆水道的通航能力。|近义词：passability, accessibility|反义词: impassability, inaccessibility|词性:noun
