hyperscepticalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈskeptɪkəlnəs/| n. |极度怀疑主义，极度怀疑的态度|An extreme level of skepticism, characterized by a tendency to doubt or disbelieve everything.|His hyperscepticalness prevented him from accepting even the most well-supported evidence. 他的极度怀疑使他甚至不接受最有力的证据。|近义词：extreme skepticism, cynicism, distrust, incredulity|反义词：credulity, gullibility, trust, faith|词性:名词
Etymology: From "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, excessive") + "skeptical" (from Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquirer, thoughtful") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
