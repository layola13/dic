parring:/ˈpɑːrɪŋ/
n.
削减，减少; 平价，同等; 修剪，修整; 高尔夫球标准杆数
The act of reducing something, especially expenses; the act of trimming something, like nails or branches;  in golf, achieving a score of par on a hole or round.
The company is parring down its workforce to cut costs. 公司正在裁员以削减成本。
He was parring his fingernails with a small knife. 他用小刀修剪指甲。
She finished the round of golf with a par. 她以标准杆的成绩完成了这轮高尔夫球。
近义词: reducing, trimming, cutting, clipping
反义词: increasing, expanding, lengthening
词性: 名词

词源: From the verb "par," originally meaning to make equal or level, from Old French "par" and Latin "par."
