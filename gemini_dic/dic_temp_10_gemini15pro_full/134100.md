nonimmanencies:/ˌnɒnɪˈmænənsiz/
|n.|非内在性；非固有性；非内在状态|The concept of nonimmanencies challenges the idea that everything is interconnected and part of a single, unified whole.  非内在性概念挑战了万物互联、构成单一统一整体的观点。| Nonimmanencies suggest the existence of elements or forces that are external to or independent of a given system or structure. 非内在性表明存在着外部于或独立于特定系统或结构的元素或力量。|近义词:transcendence, externality, independence|反义词:immanence, inherence, internality|词性:名词
