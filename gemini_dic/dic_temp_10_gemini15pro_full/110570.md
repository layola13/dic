limitarian:/lɪmɪˈtɛəriən/
n.|限制性饮食者；立足于减少肉类和动物产品摄入但并非完全素食的人|A person who reduces their consumption of meat and animal products, but does not completely abstain from them. |Example:  As a limitarian, she still enjoys the occasional piece of fish. 作为一名限制性饮食者，她偶尔还是会吃鱼。

Example:  Limitarianism is gaining popularity as a sustainable dietary approach. 限制性饮食作为一种可持续的饮食方式正越来越受欢迎。
|近义词:reducetarian, flexitarian|反义词:carnivore|词性:名词
