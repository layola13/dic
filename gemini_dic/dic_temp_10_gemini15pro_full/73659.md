forgeability:/ˌfɔːrdʒəˈbɪləti/
n.
可伪造性
The quality of being able to be forged or copied fraudulently.
The forgeability of the document led to its rejection by the authorities.  该文件的可伪造性导致当局拒绝接受它。

The forgeability of the signature was a major concern for the bank.  签名的可伪造性是银行的主要担忧。
malleability, duplicability, counterfeitability
authenticity, genuineness
noun
