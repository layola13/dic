buzz-off:/ˌbʌz ˈɒf/ (US) /ˌbʌz ˈɔːf/ (UK)| v. phr.|走开；滚开；别烦我|An informal, often rude way of telling someone to go away and leave you alone.|Example 1: "Just buzz off and leave me alone!"  “走开，别烦我！”

Example 2:  He told the annoying salesman to buzz off. 他叫那个烦人的推销员滚开。|近义词：go away, get lost, clear off, beat it|反义词: stay, come here|词性:动词短语
