balker:/ˈbɔːkər/ (US) /ˈbɔːlkə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 阻碍者，妨碍者，拒绝者；（尤指马）不肯前进的动物|Someone who refuses to do something or to allow something to happen. |The project was stalled by balkers who refused to compromise.  这个项目被那些拒绝妥协的人阻挠了。|  He is a balker at hard work. 他不肯出力干活。|synonyms: objector, resister, nonconformist, refusenik, dissenter, opponent| antonyms: supporter, cooperator, conformist| noun
