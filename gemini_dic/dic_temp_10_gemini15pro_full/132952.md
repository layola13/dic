nonemotional:/ˌnɑːnɪˈmoʊʃənəl/ /ˌnɒnɪˈməʊʃənəl/
adjective
无感情的；不动感情的；不感情用事的
Not showing, expressing, or relating to emotion.
She delivered the news in a calm, nonemotional voice. 她以平静、不动声色的语气宣布了这个消息。

He has a very nonemotional personality. 他性格非常不感情用事。


近义词: unemotional, impassive, stoic, apathetic, indifferent, detached, cool, calm, reserved
反义词: emotional, passionate, sensitive, sentimental


词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + emotional (adjective)
