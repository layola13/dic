boggled:/ˈbɒɡəld/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡəld/ (US)| v. adj.| 困惑的，吃惊的 |To be astonished or overwhelmed; to be confused or bewildered. |I was completely boggled by the complex instructions. 我完全被复杂的指令弄糊涂了。|synonyms: bewildered, confused, perplexed, astonished, overwhelmed |antonyms: clear-headed, unfazed, composed| verb, adjective
