factioneer:/ˈfækʃəˌnɪər/| n.|派系斗争者，党派人士|A person who promotes the interests of a faction or engages in factionalism.|The factioneer stirred up trouble within the group by spreading rumors.  派系斗争者通过散布谣言在团体内部制造麻烦。|Example: The factioneer was known for his divisive tactics.  这个派系斗争者以其分裂策略而闻名。|Synonyms: partisan, sectary,  agitator|Antonyms: conciliator, mediator, peacemaker|noun
