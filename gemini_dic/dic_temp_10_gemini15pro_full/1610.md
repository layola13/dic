acidifications:/əˌsɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|酸化；酸化作用|The process of becoming or making something more acidic.  It refers to the decrease in pH of a solution or environment, often caused by the introduction of acidic substances. |Ocean acidification is a serious threat to marine life. 海洋酸化是对海洋生物的严重威胁。

The acidifications of soils in the region have been linked to industrial pollution. 该地区土壤的酸化与工业污染有关。|近义词：acidifying, souring|反义词: alkalization, neutralization|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From acidify + -ation.  Acidify comes from acid + -ify.  Acid ultimately derives from the Latin word "acidus" meaning "sour" or "sharp".  The suffix "-ation" forms nouns of action or process.

Scientists are studying the effects of soil acidifications on plant growth. = 科学家们正在研究土壤酸化对植物生长的影响。
