euthanize:/ˈjuːθəˌnaɪz/ | vt. | 安乐死，对…实施安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness. | The vet euthanized the dog to end its suffering.  兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死，以结束它的痛苦。| synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitate, revive | verb

euthanize:/ˈjuːθəˌnaɪz/ | vt. | 彻底根除，彻底消除 | To completely eradicate or eliminate something.  (Less common usage) |  The company decided to euthanize the failing product line. 公司决定彻底淘汰这条亏损的产品线。 | synonyms: eradicate, eliminate, exterminate | antonyms: create, establish | verb
