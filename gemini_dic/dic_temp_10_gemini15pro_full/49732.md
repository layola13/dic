democratizing:/dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使民主化；使大众化 | making something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions based on class, wealth, etc. | The internet has democratized access to information. 互联网使获取信息大众化了。|  The company is democratizing its decision-making process by involving employees at all levels. 公司通过让各级员工参与进来，使其决策过程民主化。| synonyms: popularizing, making accessible, spreading, opening up | antonyms: restricting, limiting, monopolizing, controlling | verb

democratization:/dɪˌmɒkrətɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 民主化；大众化 | the process of making something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions based on class, wealth, etc. | The democratization of education is essential for a just society. 教育的民主化对于一个公正的社会至关重要。| The democratization of technology has empowered individuals in unprecedented ways. 科技的民主化以前所未有的方式赋予了个人权力。| synonyms: popularization, accessibility, diffusion | antonyms: restriction, limitation, monopolization, control | noun
