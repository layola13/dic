cratonic:/krəˈtɒnɪk/
adjective
克拉通的; 稳定大陆块的
Relating to a craton, a large, stable block of the earth's crust forming the nucleus of a continent.
The cratonic core of the continent has remained relatively unchanged for billions of years.  这块大陆的克拉通核心数十亿年来保持相对不变。
The researchers studied the cratonic rocks to learn about the early history of the Earth. 研究人员研究了克拉通岩石以了解地球的早期历史。
Synonyms: continental, stable
Antonyms: unstable, oceanic
词性: adjective
