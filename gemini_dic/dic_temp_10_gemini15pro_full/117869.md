melodious:/məˈləʊdiəs/ | adj. | 悦耳的，优美的 | Producing or having a pleasant tune; musical. | Her voice was melodious and soothing. 她的声音悦耳动听，令人舒缓。| The melodious sound of the piano filled the room. 悦耳的钢琴声充满了整个房间。 | 近义词:tuneful, harmonious, musical, sweet | 反义词:discordant, cacophonous, unmusical | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *melodiōsus, from Latin melodia (“melody”) + -ōsus.
