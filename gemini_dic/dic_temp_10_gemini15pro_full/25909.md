burn the candle at both ends:/bɜːrn ðə ˈkændl ət boʊθ ɛndz/
idiom
过分消耗精力；拼命工作；夜以继日地工作
To overwork oneself, typically by staying up late and getting up early.
He's been burning the candle at both ends trying to finish this project. 他为了完成这个项目一直夜以继日地工作。
近义词: overwork, exhaust oneself, work too hard
反义词: relax, rest, take it easy
词性: idiom (习语)
