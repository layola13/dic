insurances:/ɪnˈʃʊrənsɪz/
n.
保险(复数)
The act, business, or system of insuring property, life, etc., against loss or harm; the state of being insured; a contract whereby one party undertakes to indemnify or guarantee another against loss by a specified contingency or peril; the sum for which something is insured.
He has several insurances, including health, life, and car insurance. 他有几种保险，包括健康、人寿和汽车保险。
synonyms: indemnity, guarantee, protection, security, coverage, assurance
antonyms: risk, vulnerability, exposure
