mis-adapted:/ˌmɪsəˈdæptɪd/
adjective
不适应的，适应不良的
not properly adapted; poorly suited to a particular purpose or situation.
The mis-adapted polar bear struggled in the warm climate.  这头不适应环境的北极熊在温暖的气候中挣扎求生。
The factory's machinery was mis-adapted for producing the new model.  这家工厂的机器不适合生产新型号的产品。
近义词:maladapted, ill-adapted, unsuited, unfit
反义词:adapted, suited, fit, well-adapted
词性:adjective
