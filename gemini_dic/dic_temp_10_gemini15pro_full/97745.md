initializes:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪz/
|v.|初始化，设定初值|Sets a program or system to its starting configuration or values.|The program initializes the variables to zero.  程序将变量初始化为零。|Synonyms: set up, prepare, boot, reset|Antonyms:  terminate, conclude, finish|Verb

initializes:/ɪˈnɪʃəlaɪzɪz/
|n.|初始化，初始化程序|The act of setting a program or system to its starting configuration or values, or a program that does this.  (This noun form is less common)|The initializes completed successfully. 初始化成功完成。|Synonyms: setup, preparation, boot, reset|Antonyms: termination, conclusion, finish|Noun (less common)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "initialize," which combines "initial" (from Latin "initialis," meaning "of the beginning") and "-ize," a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become."
