crustlike:/ˈkrʌstlaɪk/
adjective
像外壳的;硬皮状的
Resembling a crust; having a hard or brittle surface.
The bread had a crustlike exterior.  这面包的外面像外壳一样。
The dried mud formed a crustlike layer on the ground. 干泥在地上形成了硬皮状的一层。
近义词:crusty, shell-like, hardened
反义词:soft, pliable, tender
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From "crust" + "-like".  "Crust" comes from the Old French "crouste", ultimately from the Latin "crusta" meaning "shell, rind, bark". "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of".
