foundling:/ˈfaʊnd.lɪŋ/
n.
弃婴，被遗弃的婴儿
a baby or very young child who is found abandoned and whose parents are unknown
The nuns took care of the foundling left on the doorstep of the convent. 修女们照顾了被遗弃在修道院门口的弃婴。

近义词:orphan, waif, stray
反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider phrases like "child with known parentage" or "child raised by parents" for contrasting concepts.)
词性:noun
