ignores:/ɪɡˈnɔːrz/| v.| 忽略，忽视，不理睬 |  To refuse to take notice of or acknowledge; disregard intentionally. | She ignores all my advice. 她无视我所有的建议。| He ignored the warning signs and continued driving. 他无视警告标志，继续开车。|近义词：disregards, overlooks, neglects |反义词：acknowledges, notices, heeds |词性:动词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *ignoren*, from Old French *ignorer*, from Latin *ignorare* "not to know, to be unacquainted with," from *in-* "not" + *gnoscere*, later form of *noscere* "to come to know."
