massoret:/ˈmæsəˌrɛt/| n.|马索拉学者，马索拉文本的编纂者|A Jewish scholar of the Middle Ages who copied and preserved Hebrew scriptures, adding vowel points and accents.|The Massoretes meticulously copied the Hebrew Bible, ensuring its accuracy. 马索拉学者一丝不苟地抄写希伯来圣经，确保其准确性。|The work of the Massoretes was crucial in preserving the Old Testament text.  马索拉学者的工作对于保存旧约文本至关重要。|近义词：scribe, copyist|反义词:|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Medieval Latin *Massoretes*, from Hebrew  *masōrâ*  "tradition". Ultimately from the Semitic root *MSR* "to hand down".
