defaming:/dɪˈfeɪmɪŋ/
verb
诽谤，中伤
the act of damaging someone's reputation by saying or writing bad things about them that are not true
He accused the newspaper of defaming him. 他指责这家报纸诽谤他。
slandering, libeling, disparaging, maligning
praising, commending, extolling
v.
