dramaturgy:/ˈdræmətɜːrdʒi/ /drɑːmətɜːrdʒi/
n.
戏剧学，剧本结构；剧作法
The art or technique of dramatic composition and theatrical representation.
Her lectures on dramaturgy were fascinating. 她关于戏剧学的讲座引人入胜。

近义词:dramatic art, playwriting, theater, dramatic literature
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider concepts like "improvisation" or "unstructured performance")
词性:noun
