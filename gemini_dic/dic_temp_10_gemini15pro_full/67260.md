expurgator:/ˈekspərɡeɪtər/ | n. | 净化者; 删改者 |  A person who expurgates (removes objectionable material) from a book, film, etc.  |  The expurgator removed all references to violence from the children's book.  净化者从童书中删除了所有关于暴力的内容。 | 近义词: censor, editor, reviser | 反义词:  -- | 词性: noun

expurgate:/ˈekspərɡeɪt/ | v. | 净化; 删改 |  To remove objectionable material from a book, film, etc. | The publisher expurgated the novel before releasing it in schools.  出版商在学校发行这本小说之前，先净化了其中的内容。 | 近义词: censor, bowdlerize, sanitize | 反义词: include, insert | 词性: verb
