lawlessnesses:/ˈlɔːləsnəsəz/
| n. |违法行为；无法无天；混乱|The state of being without law; a lack of respect for and obedience to the law.  Absence of government or control; anarchy.|The city descended into lawlessness after the earthquake.  地震过后，这座城市陷入了混乱。|Synonyms: anarchy, disorder, chaos, turmoil, mayhem|Antonyms: order, lawfulness, peace, control, stability
