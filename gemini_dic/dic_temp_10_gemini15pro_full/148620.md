overtheorize: /ˌoʊvərˈθiːəˌraɪz/ /ˌəʊvəˈθɪəˌraɪz/ | v. | 过度理论化；想得过多；过度分析 | To develop or explain theories about something in excessive detail or complexity, often to the point of impracticality or irrelevance. |  He tends to overtheorize simple problems, making them seem more complicated than they are. 他倾向于过度理论化简单的问题，使它们看起来比实际更复杂。

|  Don't overtheorize it; just try it and see what happens. 别想太多了；试试看会发生什么。 |  overanalyze, overthink, intellectualize | underthink, simplify | verb
