choicely:/ˈtʃɔɪsli/| adv. | 精选地，优良地，讲究地 | In a choice or carefully selected manner; with excellent taste or discrimination.  |  The food was choicely prepared and beautifully presented.  食物经过精心准备，摆盘精美。| 近义词: carefully, meticulously, discriminatingly, selectively, elegantly | 反义词: carelessly, haphazardly, randomly, indiscriminately | 词性: 副词

choicely:/ˈtʃɔɪsli/| adv. | 优良地，上等地 | Of superior quality; excellent. | The wines were all choicely selected from the best vineyards. 这些葡萄酒都是从最好的葡萄园精心挑选的。 | 近义词: excellently, superbly, finely | 反义词: poorly, badly, inferiorly | 词性: 副词
