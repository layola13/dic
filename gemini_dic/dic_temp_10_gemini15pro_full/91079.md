hutter:/ˈhʌtər/ | n. |棚屋；茅舍；小屋|A small, simple dwelling, usually made of wood or other basic materials. |例句：The shepherd lived in a small hutter on the hillside. 牧羊人住在山坡上的一间小棚屋里。

例句：The hikers sought shelter from the storm in a deserted hutter.  徒步旅行者在一间废弃的棚屋里躲避暴风雨。|近义词：hut, cabin, shack, shed |反义词: mansion, palace |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hoter, from Old English *hyter, from Proto-Germanic *hudrą (“hut”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewd- (“to cover, conceal, hide”). Cognate with Dutch hoed (“hood”), German Hütte (“hut, cabin”), Icelandic hyrta (“hovel”).
