indecisive:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪv/| adj.|优柔寡断的，犹豫不决的|not able to make decisions quickly and effectively|例句：He is so indecisive that he can't even choose what to eat for dinner. 他太优柔寡断了，甚至连晚餐吃什么都选不出来。|例句：She was indecisive about whether to accept the job offer or not. 她对于是否接受这份工作犹豫不决。|近义词：hesitant, wavering, irresolute, ambivalent|反义词：decisive, resolute, determined|词性:形容词

词源:
From in- (expressing negation) + decisive (from Latin decidere "to decide," from de- "off" + caedere "to cut").  The notion is of "not cutting off" or not coming to a conclusion.
