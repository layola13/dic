dextrogyrate:/ˈdɛkstroʊˌdʒaɪˌreɪt/| adj.|右旋的，向右旋转的|Rotating to the right, clockwise.  Specifically, refers to the rotation of the plane of polarized light to the right.|The solution was dextrogyrate, indicating the presence of the D-isomer.|近义词：clockwise, dextrorotatory|反义词:levogyrate, levorotatory|词性:adjective
dextrogyrate:/ˈdɛkstroʊˌdʒaɪˌreɪt/| n.|右旋物质|A substance that rotates plane-polarized light to the right.|Dextrogyrate is less common than its adjectival form.|近义词：dextrorotatory substance|反义词:levorotatory substance, levogyrate|词性:noun
