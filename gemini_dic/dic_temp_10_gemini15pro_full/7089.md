anesthetically:/ˌænɪsˈθɛtɪkəli/ | adv. |麻醉地；失去知觉地|In a way that produces or relates to anesthesia; numbly.  The procedure was performed anesthetically. |The surgery was performed anesthetically.  手术是在麻醉状态下进行的。

The dentist anesthetically froze the gum before extracting the tooth. 牙医在拔牙前麻醉了牙龈。|Synonym: numbly, insensibly| Antonym: sensitively, consciously|词性:副词

词源: From anesthetic + -ally.  Anesthetic comes from the Greek roots "an-" (without) and "aisthesis" (sensation).
