aerocyst: /ˈɛəroʊsɪst/
n.
气囊，气泡
An air sac or bladder in certain algae and other aquatic organisms, providing buoyancy.
Certain types of seaweed use aerocysts to float closer to the surface and receive more sunlight for photosynthesis.  某些类型的海藻使用气囊漂浮到更接近水面的地方，以获得更多的阳光进行光合作用。

The Portuguese man-of-war uses an aerocyst filled with carbon monoxide to stay afloat.  葡萄牙僧帽水母使用充满一氧化碳的气囊来保持漂浮。

近义词: air bladder, float, gas vesicle
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

词源:  From aero- (relating to air) + cyst (bladder, sac). Ultimately from Greek *āēr (air) and kystis (bladder).
