automatisms:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪzəmz/ | n. | 无意识动作；自动症 | Involuntary actions or behaviors performed without conscious thought or intention.  These actions can be simple, like lip smacking, or more complex, like driving a car while daydreaming. | He exhibited several automatisms during the seizure, such as lip smacking and picking at his clothes. 他在癫痫发作期间表现出几种无意识动作，例如咂嘴和拉扯衣服。

The sleepwalking patient displayed a series of complex automatisms. 夢遊症患者表現出一系列複雜的無意識行為。 |  Synonyms: involuntary movements, reflex actions, unconscious actions | Antonyms: conscious actions, deliberate actions, voluntary movements | noun
