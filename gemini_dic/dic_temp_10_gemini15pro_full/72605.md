fly-tipping:/ˈflaɪˌtɪpɪŋ/ | n. |  非法倾倒垃圾；乱丢垃圾 | The illegal dumping of waste, especially large items like furniture. | Fly-tipping is a serious environmental problem. 乱丢垃圾是一个严重的环境问题。 | 近义词：illegal dumping | 反义词：waste disposal | 词性：名词


词源: "fly-" (meaning quick or hasty) + "tip" (meaning to dump or discard).  The term originated in the late 19th or early 20th century, likely referring to the quick and surreptitious nature of the act.
