benevolently:/bəˈnevələntli/
adverb
仁慈地；善意地
in a kind and well-meaning way

The king smiled benevolently upon his subjects.
国王仁慈地对他的臣民微笑。

He benevolently offered his assistance to the stranded travelers.
他善意地向滞留的旅客提供帮助。

Synonyms: kindly, charitably, compassionately, generously, humanely
Antonyms: cruelly, harshly, malevolently, unkindly, maliciously
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin benevolens, from bene ("well") + volens ("wishing").
