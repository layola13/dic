bleakness:/ˈbliːknəs/| n. |荒凉；凄凉；黯淡；阴郁|A state of emptiness or despair; a lack of hope or cheerfulness.  A landscape that is bare, desolate, and exposed to the elements.|The bleakness of the winter landscape made her feel depressed. 冬日景色的荒凉让她感到沮丧。|synonyms: desolation, dreariness, barrenness, gloom, starkness|antonyms: warmth, brightness, cheerfulness, hope, lushness|noun
