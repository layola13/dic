accepter:/əkˈsɛptər/| n.|接受者；承认者；容纳者|Someone who accepts something that is offered.  A person who takes something that is offered. |The bank was the accepter of the bill.  这家银行是这张汇票的承兑人。| synonym: receiver, recipient,  taker | antonym: refuser, rejector | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "accept" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs an action).  "Accept" comes from the Old French "accepter" (modern French "accepter"), which in turn derives from the Latin "accipere," meaning "to receive," formed from "ad-" (to) + "capere" (to take).
