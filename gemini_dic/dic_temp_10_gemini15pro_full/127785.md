neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|邻居，邻国，邻近的人或物|A person who lives next to or near another.|My neighbors are very friendly. 我的邻居们很友好。|synonyms: resident, local, dweller|antonyms: stranger, foreigner|noun

neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə(r)/ (UK)| v.|邻接，毗邻，与…为邻|To live or be situated next to or near another.|Our countries neighbor each other. 我们的国家彼此相邻。|synonyms: adjoin, abut, border|antonyms: separate, divide|verb
