orbitar:/ˈɔːrbɪtɑːr/ (Spanish)| v. | 绕轨道运行，盘旋 | To revolve around a celestial body; to move in an orbit around something. |例句：El satélite orbita la Tierra. 卫星绕地球运行。|例句：The planets orbit the sun. 行星绕太阳运行。 |近义词：girar, rotar, rodear |反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps "desviar" - to deviate) |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *orbitāre, frequentative of Latin orbītāre "to go in a circle, make round," from orbis "circle, ring, orbit."
