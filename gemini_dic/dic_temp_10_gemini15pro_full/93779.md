She was overwhelmed by the immensenesses of the Sahara Desert. 她被撒哈拉沙漠的广阔无垠所震撼。|近义词：vastness, expanse, immensity, magnitude|反义词：smallness, tininess|词性：名词

Etymology: From immense + -ness.  "Immense" comes from French "immense," from Latin "immensus" meaning "immeasurable," from "in-" (not) + "mensus" (measured), past participle of "metiri" (to measure).
