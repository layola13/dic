nonharmonies:/nɒnˈhɑːməniz/ | n. |不和谐，不协调|Lack of harmony; discord.  A combination of sounds that are unpleasant or jarring.|The nonharmonies in the music made it difficult to listen to.  这音乐的不和谐音让人难以入耳。|Synonyms: discord, dissonance, cacophony, clash, conflict|Antonyms: harmony, consonance, concord, agreement, unity|Noun
