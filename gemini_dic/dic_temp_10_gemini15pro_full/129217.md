nobunagas:/ˌnoʊbuˈnɑːɡəz/ | n. | 织田信长（们） |  Plural form referring to multiple people named Nobunaga, or sometimes used hyperbolically to refer to a single person with qualities reminiscent of Oda Nobunaga (a powerful Japanese daimyo during the Sengoku period).  This word is not standard English and is primarily used by those familiar with Japanese history. | Oda Nobunagas shaped the course of Japanese history. 织田信长们塑造了日本历史的进程。
