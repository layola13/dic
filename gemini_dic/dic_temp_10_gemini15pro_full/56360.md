doilies:/ˈdɔɪliz/ (US) /ˈdɔɪliːz/ (UK)| n. |小垫子，装饰垫，蕾丝垫|A small ornamental mat, often made of lace or paper, typically placed under a plate or other object on a table or other surface.|She carefully placed the doilies under each teacup. 她小心翼翼地把小垫子放在每个茶杯下面。|Synonyms: placemat, mat, coaster|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps tablecloth as a larger covering)|Noun
