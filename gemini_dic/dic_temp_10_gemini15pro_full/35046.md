closed-captioned:/ˌkloʊzdˈkæpʃənd/| adj.|隐藏式字幕的，配有隐藏式字幕的|A closed-captioned program allows viewers to see the dialogue displayed as text on the screen, especially helpful for the hearing impaired.|例句：This movie is closed-captioned, so you can watch it without the sound. 这部电影有隐藏式字幕，所以你可以不关掉声音观看。

例句：I always turn on closed-captioning when watching foreign films. 我在看外国电影时总是打开隐藏式字幕。|近义词：subtitled|反义词：uncaptioned|词性:形容词
