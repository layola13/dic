anconoid:/ˈæŋkənɔɪd/| adj.|肘形的，尺骨鹰嘴状的|Relating to or resembling the ancon process, a bony prominence on the distal end of the ulna (elbow bone).  It describes a shape or structure similar to the elbow's point.|The anconoid process in some fossil reptiles is particularly prominent.  一些爬行动物化石中的肘突尤为突出。|近义词:cubital, olecranal|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., smooth, rounded)|词性:adjective

anconoid:/ˈæŋkənɔɪd/| n.|肘突|The ancon process itself, a bony prominence on the distal end of the ulna.|The anconoid is well-developed in this species.  该物种的肘突非常发达。|近义词:olecranon|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a concavity or fossa)|词性:noun
