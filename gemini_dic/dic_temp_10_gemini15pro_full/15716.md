balancereef:/ˈbæləns riːf/| n. |平衡礁|A type of coral reef that develops in areas with high wave energy and sediment transport. It is characterized by a balance between reef growth and erosion.|The balancereef protects the coastline from wave action. 平衡礁保护海岸线免受波浪作用。|近义词：fringing reef, barrier reef|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps deep-sea ecosystems)|noun
