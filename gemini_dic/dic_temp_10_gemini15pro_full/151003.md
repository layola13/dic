paranoiac:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪæk/ | adj. n. |偏执的，患妄想狂的；妄想狂患者|Excessively or unreasonably suspicious or distrustful.  Characterized by or suffering from paranoia.|例句：He had a paranoiac fear of being followed. 他偏执地害怕被人跟踪。|近义词：paranoid, suspicious, distrustful|反义词：trusting, confiding|词性：形容词，名词

paranoiac:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪæk/ | n. |妄想狂患者|A person who is paranoid or suffers from paranoia.|例句：The paranoiac saw enemies everywhere.  这个妄想狂患者到处都看到敌人。|近义词：paranoid|反义词：None|词性：名词
