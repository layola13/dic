afterclap:/ˈæftərˌklæp/| n.|后续掌声，余音，后效|A burst of applause following a performance or speech, often after a brief pause; a subsequent effect or consequence.|例句：The afterclap of the explosion shook the entire building. 爆炸的余音震动了整栋楼。

例句：The economic afterclap of the pandemic was felt worldwide.  这场疫情带来的经济后效波及全球。|近义词：repercussion, consequence, aftermath|反义词:prelude, antecedent|词性:名词
