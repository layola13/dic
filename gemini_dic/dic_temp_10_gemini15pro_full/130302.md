non-syndicated:/ˌnɒnˈsɪndɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 非辛迪加的，未联合的 | Not syndicated; not sold or distributed by a syndicate.  This refers to content, particularly media like television programs or newspaper columns, that are produced and distributed independently, without the involvement of a syndicate that sells the rights to multiple outlets. |  The local news program is non-syndicated, airing only on this channel.  这个地方新闻节目是非辛迪加的，只在这个频道播出。

The documentary was non-syndicated and only shown at a few film festivals. 这部纪录片是非辛迪加的，只在几个电影节上放映。| 近义词: independent, exclusive | 反义词: syndicated | 词性: 形容词
