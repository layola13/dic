nouniness:/ˈnaʊninəs/| n. |名词性，名词化|The degree to which a word or phrase functions as a noun.  The nouniness of the word "running" is apparent in the sentence "Running is good exercise."|例句：The nouniness of the gerund "reading" is clear.  阅读的“名词性”很明显。

例句：The high nouniness of the phrase "the quick brown fox" makes it a suitable subject for a sentence.  “快速的棕色狐狸”这个短语很强的名词性使其成为句子的合适主语。|近义词：nominality, substantiveness|反义词: verbality, adjectivality|词性:noun
