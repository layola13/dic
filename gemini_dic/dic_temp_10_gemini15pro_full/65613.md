evangelical: /ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/ | adj. n. | 福音派的；福音主义的；福音传道者 | Of or relating to the Christian gospel, especially the four Gospels of the New Testament.  Characterized by a belief in the literal truth of the Bible and the importance of personal conversion and evangelism. |  The evangelical preacher delivered a fiery sermon. 福音派传教士发表了热情洋溢的布道。| synonyms: gospel, fundamentalist, revivalist | antonyms: liberal, secular, nonreligious | adjective, noun

evangelical: /ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/ | n. | 福音传道者 | A person who seeks to convert others to the Christian faith, especially by public preaching. | He is a passionate evangelical. 他是一位充满激情的福音传道者。 | synonyms: missionary, evangelist, preacher | antonyms: nonbeliever, atheist, skeptic | noun
