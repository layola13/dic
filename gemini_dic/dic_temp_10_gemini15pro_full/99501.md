interfluous:/ˌɪntərˈfluːəs/| adj.|河流间的，两河之间的|Situated between rivers; pertaining to the region between rivers.  Specifically, the area of land between two rivers that join to form a confluence.|The interfluous region was rich in fertile soil, perfect for agriculture.|近义词：interfluvial|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


Etymology->
From Latin *interfluus (“flowing between”), from inter- (“between”) + fluere (“to flow”).
