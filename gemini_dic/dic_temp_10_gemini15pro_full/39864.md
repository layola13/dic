constants:/ˈkɒnstənts/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstənts/ (US)| n.|常数，常量|A constant is a value that does not change.|The speed of light is a constant. 光速是一个常量。|synonyms: fixed value, invariable, unchanging quantity|antonyms: variable, parameter|noun

constants:/ˈkɒnstənt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnstənt/ (US)| adj.|持续的，不变的，恒定的|Something that is constant happens all the time or never changes.|He was in constant pain. 他一直很痛苦。|synonyms: continuous, perpetual, incessant, unchanging, invariable|antonyms: intermittent, occasional, variable|adjective
