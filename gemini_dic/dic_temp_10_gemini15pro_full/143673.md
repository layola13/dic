outlasts:/ˌaʊtˈlæsts/ | v. | 比…活得长；比…持久；经受住；胜过 | to live or last longer than; to survive or endure longer than something else |  The tortoise outlasts the hare. 乌龟比兔子活得长。 | synonyms: survives, endures, outlives | antonyms: succumbs to, perishes, gives in to | verb
