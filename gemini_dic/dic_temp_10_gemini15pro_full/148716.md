overtower:/ˌōvərˈtaʊər/ | v. |  高耸于…之上；超过；凌驾；比…高 | to be much taller than something else; to dominate something | The new skyscraper overtowers all the other buildings in the city.  这座新的摩天大楼比城市里所有其他建筑物都高。| The cliffs overtowered the beach. 悬崖高耸于海滩之上。 | synonyms: dominate, dwarf, overlook, surpass | antonyms: be dwarfed by, be beneath | verb
