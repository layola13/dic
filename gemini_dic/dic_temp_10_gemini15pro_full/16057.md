bandier:/ˈbændɪər/ | n. | 旗手，掌旗官 | A person who carries a banner, especially in a procession or ceremony. | The bandier led the parade, waving the school's flag proudly. 旗手带领着游行队伍，骄傲地挥舞着学校的旗帜。| synonym: flag-bearer, standard-bearer | antonym: follower | noun

bandier:/ˈbændɪər/ | adj. |  更鲜艳的，颜色更丰富的 (Rarely Used)  |  More brightly colored, exhibiting a greater variety or intensity of colors. |  The bandier version of the painting truly popped. 这幅画颜色更鲜艳的版本真的很抢眼。| synonym:  brighter, more colorful, vivid | antonym: duller, drab | adjective
