paragraphism: /ˈpærəɡræfɪzəm/ | n. | 段落主义，过度使用段落 | The excessive use of paragraphs, often short and unconnected, in writing.  This can disrupt the flow of writing and make it difficult to follow. |  Her essay suffered from paragraphism; every sentence seemed to start a new paragraph. 她的文章充满了段落主义；每个句子似乎都开始了新的一段。 |  verbosity, prolixity | conciseness, brevity | noun
