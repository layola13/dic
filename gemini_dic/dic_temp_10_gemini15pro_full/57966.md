drippy:/ˈdrɪpi/| adj.|滴水的，潮湿的；俗气的，矫情的|Something that is drippy is wet and has drops of liquid falling from it.  In slang, it can also mean overly sentimental or excessively stylish in a way that is considered uncool or inauthentic.|The faucet was drippy and needed to be fixed. 水龙头漏水了，需要修理。|近义词：wet, soggy, sappy, cheesy|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词
