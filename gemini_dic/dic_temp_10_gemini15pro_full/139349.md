obscurantism:/əbˈskjʊərənˌtɪzəm/ | n. | 蒙昧主义，愚民政策 | The practice of deliberately preventing the facts or full details of something from becoming known. |  The government's policy of obscurantism is hindering scientific progress. 政府的愚民政策阻碍了科学进步。

The historian criticized the obscurantism of the church during the Middle Ages.  这位历史学家批评了中世纪教会的蒙昧主义。| 近义词：anti-intellectualism, dogmatism, illiberalism, reactionism | 反义词：enlightenment, liberalism, rationalism | 词性: 名词
