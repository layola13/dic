overeager:/ˌoʊvərˈiːɡər/| adj.| 过于热心的，过分渴望的| Excessively eager; too enthusiastic or impatient.|She was overeager to please her new boss. 她过分渴望取悦她的新老板。| The overeager fans mobbed the singer. 过于热情的粉丝围住了歌手。|近义词：impatient, enthusiastic, keen, avid|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|adjective
