huguenotism:/ˈhjuːɡənətɪzəm/
|n.|
|胡格诺主义，胡格诺教派|
|The doctrines and practices of the Huguenots, French Protestants of the 16th and 17th centuries.  It refers to the historical movement of French Protestantism based on Calvinism.|
|The Edict of Nantes in 1598 granted religious toleration to Huguenots, marking a significant moment in the history of Huguenotism.|胡格诺敕令于1598年给予胡格诺派宗教宽容，标志着胡格诺主义历史上的一个重要时刻。

|The persecution of Huguenots in France led many to flee to other countries.|对法国胡格诺派的迫害导致许多人逃往其他国家。
|近义词：French Protestantism, Calvinism (in France)|
|反义词：Catholicism (in the French context)|
|词性：noun|
