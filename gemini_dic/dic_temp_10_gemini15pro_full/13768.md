audiblenesses:/ˌɔːdəˈbɪlɪtɪz/ /ˌɑːdəˈbɪlɪtɪz/| n. |可闻度；可听度|The quality or state of being audible; the degree to which something can be heard. |The audibleness of the whisper was surprising given the distance.  鉴于距离如此之远，耳语的可闻度令人惊讶。|Synonym: audibility, distinctness, clarity |Antonym: inaudibility, faintness|noun
