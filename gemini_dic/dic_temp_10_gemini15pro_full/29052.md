cartwheel:/ˈkɑːrtwiːl/ | v. n. | 侧手翻，翻筋斗 | to perform a cartwheel; a sideways handspring with arms and legs extended | He cartwheeled across the room. 他翻着筋斗穿过房间。| 近义词：tumble, roll | 反义词: stand still | 词性: 动词/名词

词源:  From "cart" + "wheel," referring to the circular motion of the body resembling a turning cartwheel.
