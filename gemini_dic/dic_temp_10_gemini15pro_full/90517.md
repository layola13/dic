hudsonian:/hʌdˈsoʊniən/
adjective
哈德逊湾的；与哈德逊湾有关的；哈德逊湾风格的
Of or relating to the Hudson Bay or the Hudson River.
The Hudsonian godwit is a long-legged shorebird.  哈德逊湾塍鹬是一种长腿滨鸟。
The museum has a large collection of Hudsonian artifacts.  博物馆收藏了大量哈德逊湾地区的文物。

近义词:arctic, subarctic (depending on context)
反义词:(No direct antonym; consider tropical, temperate depending on specific usage)
词性:adjective
