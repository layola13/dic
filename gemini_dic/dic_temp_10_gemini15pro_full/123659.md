moo:/muː/
noun|哞哞叫;牛叫声|The cow made a loud moo. 牛大声地哞哞叫。
verb|（牛）哞哞叫|The cows were mooing in the field. 奶牛在地里哞哞叫。
interjection|哞（拟声词）|"Moo," said the cow. “哞，”牛说。


Etymology->
From Middle English *mouen, *mowen, from Old English *mūgian (“to low”), of imitative origin. Cognate with Dutch moeën (“to moo”), German muhen (“to moo”).
