ceasefire: /ˈsiːsfaɪər/ (US) /ˈsiːsfaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. |停火;停战;休战|A temporary suspension of fighting, typically by agreement between opposing sides in a war or conflict.|例句：The two sides agreed to a ceasefire. 双方同意停火。

例句：A ceasefire was declared at midnight. 午夜宣布停火。

近义词：truce, armistice, peace, suspension of hostilities|反义词：war, conflict, fighting, hostilities|词性：名词

ceasefire: /ˈsiːsfaɪər/ (US) /ˈsiːsfaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. |停火;停战|To stop fighting, typically by agreement between opposing sides in a war or conflict.|例句：The rebels have agreed to ceasefire. 叛军同意停火。

例句：Both nations ceased fire after the treaty was signed. 条约签署后两国停火。


近义词：halt, stop, end, discontinue|反义词：begin, start, continue, resume|词性：动词
