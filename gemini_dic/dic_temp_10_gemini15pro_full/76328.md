词源:
From Middle English *gable, from Old North French gable, from Old Norse gafl (“gable”), from Proto-Germanic *gabulą, *gibulą (“fork; gable”). Cognate with Dutch gevel (“gable”), German Giebel (“gable”), Swedish gavel (“gable”).
来自中古英语 *gable，来自古北法语 gable，来自古诺尔斯语 gafl（“山墙”），来自原始日耳曼语 *gabulą, *gibulą（“叉子；山墙”）。 与荷兰语 gevel（“山墙”）、德语 Giebel（“山墙”）、瑞典语 gavel（“山墙”）同源。
