impanelling:/ɪmˈpænəlɪŋ/
n.
组成陪审团；选任陪审团
The act of selecting a jury for a trial.
The impanelling of the jury took several hours.  组成陪审团花了几个小时。
近义词: jury selection, empaneling
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) discharge of jury
词性: noun


impanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/
vt.
选任（陪审团）
To enroll a list of jurors; to constitute a jury.
The judge impaneled a jury of twelve people.  法官选任了一个由十二人组成的陪审团。
近义词: empanel, enlist, enroll
反义词: (No direct antonym) discharge
词性: verb
