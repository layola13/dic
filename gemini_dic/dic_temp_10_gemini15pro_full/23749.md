braved:/breɪvd/| v.|勇敢面对，冒（风险）；经受（困难）|Faced or endured with courage.|She braved the storm to get home. 她冒着暴风雨回家。|近义词：faced, endured, defied, withstood|反义词:avoided, shirked, feared|词性:动词

词源: From the verb "brave," which comes from the Italian "bravo" meaning "wild" or "fierce."
