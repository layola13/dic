heartbrokenly:/ˈhɑːrtˌbroʊkənli/| adv.|心碎地，悲痛欲绝地|In a way that shows that someone is extremely sad because their love for someone else has ended.|She wept heartbrokenly when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后悲痛欲绝地哭了起来。|Synonyms: sadly, sorrowfully, miserably, despairingly|Antonyms: joyfully, happily, cheerfully|adverb
