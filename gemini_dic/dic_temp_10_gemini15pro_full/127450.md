nebulation:/ˌnɛbjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|雾化，喷雾|The process of reducing a liquid to a fine spray, as by a nebulizer. 将液体变成细雾的过程，如通过雾化器。|The doctor prescribed nebulation therapy for his asthma. 医生给他开了雾化疗法治疗哮喘。|Synonyms: atomization, vaporization|Antonyms: condensation, solidification|noun
