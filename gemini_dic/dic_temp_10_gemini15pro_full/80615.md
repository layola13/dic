goosander:/ˈɡuːsændər/| n. |秋沙鸭|A large fish-eating duck with a long saw-toothed bill, also called a merganser.|例句：The goosander dived expertly to catch a fish. 秋沙鸭熟练地潜入水中捕鱼。

例句：We observed a goosander preening its feathers by the riverbank. 我们观察到一只秋沙鸭在河岸边梳理羽毛。|近义词：merganser, sawbill|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From gooose + ander, from Old English ened "duck"
