armistice: /ˈɑːrmɪstɪs/ (US) /ˈɑːmɪstɪs/ (UK)
n.|停战，休战；停战协定|An agreement made by opposing sides in a war to stop fighting for a certain time.|The armistice was signed at 11:00 on November 11, 1918. 1918年11月11日11时签署了停战协定。|Synonyms: truce, ceasefire, peace|Antonyms: war, conflict, hostilities|Noun
