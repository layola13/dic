hyposternum:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈstɜːrnəm/ | n. | 腹板; 下腹片 |The hyposternum is a plate on the ventral side of the thorax in some insects.  在某些昆虫中，腹板是胸部腹侧的一块板。| The hyposternum of this beetle is unusually large.  这只甲虫的腹板异常大。 | 近义词: metasternum, ventral plate | 反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps tergum for dorsal counterpart) | noun
