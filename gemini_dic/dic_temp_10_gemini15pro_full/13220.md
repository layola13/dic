atafter:/ˈɑːftər/ | prep. adv. conj.| 在...之后，后来，以后 | After the rain, the sun came out. 雨后，太阳出来了。| 近义词: following, subsequently, afterward, later | 反义词: before, prior | 词性: 介词, 副词, 连词

Etymology: From Middle English *after, from Old English æfter (“after, behind, according to, along, next”), from Proto-Germanic *after (“after, behind”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ep- (“near, at”). Cognate with Dutch achter (“behind”), German after (“anus”), Old Norse eptir (“after”).
