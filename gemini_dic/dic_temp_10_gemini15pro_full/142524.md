ossiferous:/ˈɒsɪfərəs/
adjective
含骨的；生骨的
Containing or composed of bone; bone-forming.
The paleontologist unearthed an ossiferous layer of rock, rich with the remains of ancient creatures.  古生物学家发掘出一层富含古代生物遗骸的含骨岩石层。
The dinosaur skeleton was embedded in an ossiferous matrix. 恐龙骨骼被嵌在含骨的基质中。
synonyms: bony, osseous
antonyms: boneless, cartilaginous
word type: adjective
