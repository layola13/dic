opaque:/əʊˈpeɪk/ (UK) /oʊˈpeɪk/ (US)| adj.|不透明的；浑浊的；难懂的|Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Also, difficult to understand; obscure.|The windows were opaque with steam. 窗户上蒙了一层水汽，变得不透明了。|

The meaning of the poem remains opaque to me. 这首诗的意思我仍然无法理解。|近义词：cloudy, murky, obscure, nontransparent|反义词：transparent, clear, lucid|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *opacus* "shaded, dark," related to *ops* "power, riches, resources, abundance, aid, help," from PIE root *op- "to work, produce in abundance." The connection of senses might be because abundant resources can be "shading" in the sense of "overwhelming."
