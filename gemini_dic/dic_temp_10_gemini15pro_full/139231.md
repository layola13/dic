oblation:/əʊˈbleɪʃən/ /ɑːˈbleɪʃən/| n.|祭品；供奉；奉献|an offering or presentation made to a deity or sacred personage|The monks brought an oblation of fruit to the temple. 僧侣们向寺庙献上了水果祭品。|Synonyms: offering, sacrifice, tribute, donation|Antonyms: desecration, withholding|noun

oblation:| n.|祭品；供奉；奉献|something offered or presented as a religious sacrifice|They made an oblation of their time and talents to the cause. 他们为了这项事业奉献了时间和才能。|Synonyms: offering, sacrifice, tribute, donation|Antonyms: desecration, withholding|noun
