biopsychic:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsaɪkɪk/| adj.|生物心理的|Relating to or involving both biological and psychological factors.|例句：Biopsychic factors can influence a person's susceptibility to certain diseases. 生物心理因素会影响一个人对某些疾病的易感性。

例句：Her approach to therapy is biopsychic, considering both physical and mental well-being. 她采用的疗法是生物心理的，兼顾身心健康。|近义词：mind-body, psychosomatic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

biopsychic:/ˌbaɪoʊˈsaɪkɪk/| n.|生物心理学家|A person who studies the interaction between biological and psychological factors.|例句：The biopsychic researched the link between gut bacteria and mental health.  这位生物心理学家研究了肠道细菌与心理健康之间的联系。

例句：As a biopsychic, she believes that mental illness has both physical and psychological roots. 作为一名生物心理学家，她认为精神疾病既有生理根源，也有心理根源。|近义词：biological psychologist, psychobiologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
