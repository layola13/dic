arashiyama:/ɑːrɑːʃiːˈjɑːmə/ | n. | 嵐山 (Arashiyama) |A district on the western outskirts of Kyoto, Japan, known for its natural scenery, particularly the bamboo grove and the Hozugawa River.  It's a popular tourist destination.|  We visited Arashiyama during our trip to Kyoto and were captivated by the bamboo forest. 我们在京都之旅期间参观了岚山，并被竹林所吸引。

|近义词: Sagano (another name for the area), scenic spot|反义词: urban area|词性: noun


arashi:/ɑːˈrɑːʃiː/ | n. | 嵐 (storm; tempest) | A Japanese word meaning "storm" or "tempest". This is the first part of the word Arashiyama. | The arashi raged all night. 暴风雨肆虐了整夜。

|近义词: storm, tempest, gale|反义词: calm, stillness|词性: noun


yama:/ˈjɑːmə/ | n. | 山 (mountain) | A Japanese word meaning "mountain".  This is the second part of the word Arashiyama. |  Mount Fuji is a famous yama in Japan. 富士山是日本一座著名的山。

|近义词: mountain, peak, mount|反义词: valley, plain|词性: noun
