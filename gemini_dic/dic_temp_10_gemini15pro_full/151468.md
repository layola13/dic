paroxysmal:/pærəkˈsɪzməl/| adj.|阵发的，突发的|Sudden and violent but intermittent; coming in spasms.|She experienced paroxysmal coughing fits. 她经历了阵发性的咳嗽。|近义词:spasmodic, fitful, convulsive|反义词:continuous, persistent, constant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *paroxysmal*, from Greek *paroxusmos* "irritation, exasperation," from *paroxunein* "to sharpen, irritate, provoke," from *para-* "beside" + *oxunein* "to sharpen."
