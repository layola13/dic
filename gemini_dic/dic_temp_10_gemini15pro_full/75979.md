funnelform:/ˈfʌn(ə)lˌfɔːrm/ | adj. | 漏斗形的 | Shaped like a funnel; tapering towards one end. | The flower had a funnelform corolla.  这朵花的花冠呈漏斗形。| The tornado formed a funnelform cloud. 龙卷风形成了一个漏斗状的云。 | 近义词：funnel-shaped, infundibuliform, conical | 反义词：cylindrical, flat | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "funnel" + "-form." "Funnel" comes from Middle English *fonel*, from Old French *funel*, diminutive of *fune*, from Latin *infundibulum* "funnel." "-form" comes from Latin *-formis*, from *forma* "form, shape."
