aggrandizable:/əˈɡrændɪzəbl/
adjective
可增大的；可夸大的；可提高的
Capable of being aggrandized; that can be made greater, larger, or more powerful.
His reputation as a scholar is easily aggrandizable.  他的学者声誉很容易被夸大。
The company's profits are aggrandizable through strategic investments. 通过战略投资，公司的利润可以增加。
Synonyms:  enlargeable, expandable, increasable, amplifiable
Antonyms:  diminishable, reducible, decreasable
词性: adjective
