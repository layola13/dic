exhilarator:/ɪɡˈzɪləˌreɪtər/
noun
兴奋剂；使人高兴的事物
Something that exhilarates; a stimulant.
The roller coaster was an exhilarating exhilarator.  过山车是一种令人兴奋的刺激物。
The news of her promotion was a true exhilarator. 她升职的消息真是令人振奋。
Synonyms: stimulant, tonic, boost, pick-me-up
Antonyms: depressant, downer
n.
