NTSC:/ˈɛn tiː ɛs siː/
n.
National Television System Committee (NTSC)  美国国家电视系统委员会制式；NTSC制式
The NTSC standard is used in North America and parts of South America and Asia.  NTSC制式在北美和南美洲及亚洲的部分地区使用。
近义词: PAL, SECAM
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as these are different standards, not opposites.)
词性: noun (缩写)
