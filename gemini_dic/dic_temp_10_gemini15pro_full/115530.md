map:/mæp/ | n. vt. |地图；绘制地图；映射；计划；|A visual representation of an area, typically drawn to scale.  To represent something visually, often in a diagrammatic form. To plan or outline something in detail.|

例句：
We used a map to find our way through the city. 我们用地图找到了穿过城市的路。

The human genome has been mapped. 人类基因组已被绘制出来。


近义词：chart, diagram, plan, scheme, plot |
反义词: (n.) none; (v.) distort, misrepresent |
词性: noun, verb


map:/mæp/ | v. |绘制地图；映射；计划；|To represent something visually, often in a diagrammatic form. To plan or outline something in detail.|

例句：
They mapped the unexplored territory. 他们绘制了未开发的领土的地图。

The marketing team mapped out a new strategy. 营销团队制定了一个新的战略。


近义词：chart, diagram, plan, scheme, plot |
反义词: distort, misrepresent |
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "mappa" (napkin, cloth), which also came to mean "map" in Late Latin.  The original sense likely referred to a cloth on which geographical features were depicted.
