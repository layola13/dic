farcer:/ˈfɑːrsər/| n.|小丑；滑稽剧演员；闹剧作家|A person who writes or acts in farces (light humorous plays based on improbable situations).|例句：The farcer delighted the audience with his witty jokes and slapstick humor.  滑稽剧演员用他机智的笑话和滑稽的动作逗乐了观众。

例句：He was a renowned farcer in the early 20th century. 他是20世纪早期一位著名的闹剧作家。|近义词：clown, comedian, buffoon, jester|反义词: tragedian|词性:noun
