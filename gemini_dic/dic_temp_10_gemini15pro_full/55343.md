dissymmetrical:/ˌdɪsɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/ | adj. | 不对称的，不对称的 | Lacking symmetry; not symmetrical.  Having two halves that are not mirror images of each other. | The crystal structure is dissymmetrical.  晶体结构是不对称的。| The artist intentionally made the sculpture dissymmetrical to create a sense of unease. 艺术家特意使雕塑不对称，以营造一种不安感。 | asymmetrical, irregular, uneven | symmetrical, regular, even | adjective
