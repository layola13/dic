nonperpetuations:/ˌnɒnpərˌpetʃuˈeɪʃənz/
|n.| 非延续，不永存 |The nonperpetuation of outdated traditions is essential for societal progress.  过时的传统不被延续对于社会进步至关重要。|  The museum's mission includes the nonperpetuation of harmful stereotypes.  博物馆的使命包括不延续有害的刻板印象。| 近义词: cessation, discontinuation | 反义词: perpetuation, continuation | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- + perpetuation.  Perpetuation comes from Late Latin perpetuationem,  from Latin perpetuatus, past participle of perpetuare "to make perpetual,"  from perpetuus "continuous."
