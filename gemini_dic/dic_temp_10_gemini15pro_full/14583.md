avid:/ˈævɪd/| adj.|热衷的；渴望的；贪婪的|Characterized by enthusiasm and vigorous pursuit.|He is an avid reader of science fiction.|近义词：keen, enthusiastic, eager, passionate|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
