nominalizing:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈnɑːmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | n. |名词化；名词化过程|The process of turning a verb or adjective into a noun.  |Example: "Nominalizing 'run' creates the noun 'runner'."  “将动词‘run’名词化后就成了名词‘runner’”。|近义词:noun formation, nominalisation|反义词:verbalizing|词性:noun


nominalize:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪz/ /ˈnɑːmɪnəlaɪz/ | v. |名词化；变成名词|To convert (a word, especially a verb or adjective) into a noun. |Example:  "The adjective 'happy' can be nominalized to form the noun 'happiness'." “形容词‘happy’可以名词化，构成名词‘happiness’”。 |近义词:nounify|反义词:verbalize|词性:verb
