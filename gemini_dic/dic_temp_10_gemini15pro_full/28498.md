cardamom:/ˈkɑːrdəməm/ (US), /ˈkɑːdəmɒm/ (UK)| n.|小豆蔻，豆蔻籽，豆蔻粉|A spice from the seeds of various plants in the ginger family, used in South Asian and Middle Eastern cooking.|例句：I added a pinch of cardamom to the cake batter. 我在蛋糕面糊里加了一撮小豆蔻。

例句：Cardamom is a common ingredient in Indian curries. 小豆蔻是印度咖喱的常见成分。|近义词：spice, flavoring|反义词:|noun
