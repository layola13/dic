anti-platonic:/ˌæntipləˈtɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 反柏拉图式的，非柏拉图式的 |  Opposed to or rejecting Platonic ideals, especially in emphasizing physical or sexual aspects of a relationship rather than intellectual or spiritual ones. |  They have an anti-platonic relationship, focused more on physical intimacy than shared interests. 他们之间是一种反柏拉图式的关系，更注重身体上的亲密而不是共同的兴趣。 | Example: Their friendship quickly evolved into something anti-platonic. 他们的友谊迅速发展成反柏拉图式。| Synonyms: unplatonic, non-platonic, physical | Antonyms: platonic, chaste, spiritual | adjective


anti-platonic:/ˌæntipləˈtɑːnɪk/ | n. | 反柏拉图主义 | The philosophy or state of being anti-platonic. | He embraced anti-platonicism in his youth, prioritizing physical relationships. 他年轻时信奉反柏拉图主义，优先考虑肉体关系。 | Example: Her anti-platonic stance surprised her friends. 她的反柏拉图立场令她的朋友们感到惊讶。| Synonyms: non-platonism | Antonyms: platonism | noun
