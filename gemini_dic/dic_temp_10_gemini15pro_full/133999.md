nonhomogeneous:/ˌnɑːnhoʊmoʊˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌnɒnhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
非齐次的，不均匀的
Not having a uniform composition or structure throughout; not homogeneous.
The mixture was nonhomogeneous, with clumps of undissolved sugar at the bottom.  这种混合物是非均质的，底部有不溶解的糖块。
The soil sample was nonhomogeneous, containing a variety of different particles. 土壤样本是非均质的，包含各种不同的颗粒。
heterogeneous, dissimilar, varied, diverse
homogeneous, uniform, similar, consistent
词性: 形容词
