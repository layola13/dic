interagreed:/ɪntərəˈɡriːd/ | adj. |  互相同意的，共同约定的 | Agreed upon mutually; having reached a mutual agreement. |  The two parties interagreed on the terms of the contract. 双方就合同条款达成了一致。| 近义词: mutually agreed, concerted, agreed upon | 反义词: disagreed, disputed, contested | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "inter-" (between, among) + "agreed" (past participle of "agree").  The prefix "inter-" indicates a reciprocal action or relationship between two or more parties.
