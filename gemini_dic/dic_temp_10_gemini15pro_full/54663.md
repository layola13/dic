disobligation:/ˌdɪsˌɑːblɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
解除义务；免除义务
The contract's clause provided for disobligation in certain circumstances.  该合同的条款规定了在某些情况下可以免除义务。
His bankruptcy resulted in the disobligation of his debts. 他的破产导致他的债务被免除。
近义词: release, exemption, absolution
反义词: obligation, duty, responsibility
词性: noun

Etymology:
From dis- + obligation.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Obligation" comes from the Old French "obligacion" and ultimately from the Latin "obligare," meaning "to bind."
