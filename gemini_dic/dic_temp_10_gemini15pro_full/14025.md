auscultate:/ˈɔːskəlˌteɪt/ (US), /ˈɔːskʌlˌteɪt/ (UK)| v. |听诊|To listen to sounds within the body, typically using a stethoscope, for diagnostic purposes.|The doctor auscultated the patient's chest. 医生听诊了病人的胸部。|Synonyms: examine, listen to, monitor|Antonyms: ignore, disregard|verb
