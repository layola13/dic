neogene:/ˈniːədʒɛn/ | adj. | 新生代的 | Relating to or denoting the Neogene period or system. |  Neogene fossils are valuable for understanding recent evolutionary history. 新生代化石对于理解近代进化史很有价值。 | 近义词: Cenozoic (部分重叠) | 反义词: Paleogene | 词性: 形容词
