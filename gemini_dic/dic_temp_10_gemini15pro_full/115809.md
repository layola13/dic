markdown:/ˈmɑːrkdaʊn/ | n. | 标记语言，一种轻量级标记语言 | A lightweight markup language with plain text formatting syntax. It is designed so that it can be converted to HTML and many other formats using a tool by the same name. |  I wrote the document in markdown. 我用markdown写了这份文档。 |  近义词:  plaintext, reStructuredText | 反义词: HTML, XML | noun
