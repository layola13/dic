hornest:/ˈhɔːrnɪt/ (US), /ˈhɔːnɪt/ (UK)| n. |大黄蜂，马蜂|A large stinging insect of the wasp family, having a predominantly black and yellow or brown body.|例句：The hornet's sting is very painful. 马蜂的刺非常疼。

例句：A hornet flew into the car and caused panic. 一只大黄蜂飞进了车里，引起了恐慌。|近义词：wasp, yellow jacket|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
