overexplaining:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle) | 过度解释；解释得过于详细 | explaining something in too much detail, often to the point of being tedious or condescending |  "She tends to overexplain things, making simple concepts seem complicated." 她往往会过度解释，使简单的概念显得复杂。| synonyms: overelaborating, belaboring,  | antonyms:  unders explaining, summarizing, being concise | verb
