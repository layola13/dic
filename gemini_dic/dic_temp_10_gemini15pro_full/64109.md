epoxy:/ɪˈpɒksi/ (UK) /ɪˈpɑːksi/ (US)| n. adj.| 环氧树脂；环氧的|A synthetic resin in which the repeating unit contains an epoxide group, used in adhesives, coatings, and other applications.|The table was coated with a layer of epoxy.  桌子涂了一层环氧树脂。|Synonyms: resin, adhesive, sealant|Antonyms: solvent|noun

epoxy:/ɪˈpɒksi/ (UK) /ɪˈpɑːksi/ (US)| adj.| 环氧的；含环氧基的|Containing or using epoxy resin.|An epoxy coating protects the metal from corrosion.  环氧涂层保护金属免受腐蚀。|Synonyms: resinous|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
