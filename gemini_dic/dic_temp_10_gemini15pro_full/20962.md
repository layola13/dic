blemishers:/ˈblemɪʃərz/
n.
瑕疵，缺点，污点
Imperfections or flaws that mar the appearance of something.
The blemishes on her skin made her feel self-conscious.  她皮肤上的瑕疵让她感到很不自在。
synonyms: flaws, imperfections, defects, marks, spots, stains
antonyms: perfections, advantages, merits
noun

blemish:/ˈblemɪʃ/
n.
瑕疵，缺点，污点
A small mark or flaw which spoils the appearance of something.
The painting had a small blemish in the corner.  这幅画的角落里有一个小瑕疵。
vt.
损害，玷污
To spoil the appearance of something.
The scandal blemished his reputation.  这桩丑闻玷污了他的名声。
synonyms: flaw, imperfection, defect, mark, spot, stain, tarnish, mar
antonyms: perfection, enhance, improve
noun, verb
