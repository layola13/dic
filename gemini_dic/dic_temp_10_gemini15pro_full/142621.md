osteoporosis:/ˌɒstiəʊpəˈrəʊsɪs/ /ˌɑːstioʊpəˈroʊsɪs/| n. |骨质疏松症|A medical condition in which the bones become brittle and fragile from loss of tissue, typically as a result of hormonal changes, or deficiency of calcium or vitamin D.|My grandmother suffers from osteoporosis and has to be very careful to avoid falls. 我的祖母患有骨质疏松症，必须非常小心避免摔倒。| Osteoporosis can increase the risk of fractures. 骨质疏松症会增加骨折的风险。|近义词：bone loss, osteopenia|反义词: bone density|词性:noun
