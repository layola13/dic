famed:/feɪmd/
adjective
著名的，闻名的
well known and talked about
例句：The famed musician gave a memorable performance.  这位著名的音乐家进行了一场令人难忘的表演。

例句：She is famed for her generosity. 她因慷慨大方而闻名。

近义词：renowned, celebrated, well-known, distinguished, illustrious
反义词：unknown, obscure, anonymous
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *famen, from Old French famer (“to make famous”), from Latin fāma (“fame, reputation”).
