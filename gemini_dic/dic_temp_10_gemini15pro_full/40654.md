conventions:/kənˈvɛnʃənz/
| n. |习俗，惯例；会议；公约|a way in which something is usually done, especially within a particular area or activity.  A large formal meeting of people with a shared interest, typically one that takes place annually.|
例句：Social conventions change over time. 社会习俗随着时间的推移而改变。

例句：The annual convention of stamp collectors will be held in July. 集邮爱好者的年度大会将于7月举行。

近义词：customs, traditions, practices, norms, gatherings, conferences, agreements, treaties
反义词：unorthodoxies, deviations, irregularities
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *convencioun*, from Old French *convencion* (modern French *convention*),  from Latin *conventiō* ("a meeting, assembly, agreement"), from the verb *conveniō* ("come together, assemble, agree"), from *con-* ("together") + *veniō* ("come").
