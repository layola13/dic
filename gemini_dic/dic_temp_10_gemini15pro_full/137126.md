nonsubsidiaries:/nɒnˌsəbˈsɪdiəriːz/
n.
非子公司；非附属公司
Companies that are not controlled by a parent company.  A company is a nonsubsidiary if it operates independently and is not owned or controlled by another company.
Our report covers the financial performance of both subsidiaries and nonsubsidiaries. 我们的报告涵盖了子公司和非子公司两者的财务业绩。
近义词: independent companies, unaffiliated companies
反义词: subsidiaries, affiliates
词性: 名词
