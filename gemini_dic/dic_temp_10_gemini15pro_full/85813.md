hazel:/ˈheɪzəl/ | n. adj.|榛子，榛树；淡褐色|A small tree or shrub bearing edible nuts.  A light brown or yellowish brown color, like that of hazelnuts.|例句：The squirrels were busy burying hazelnuts for the winter. 松鼠们正忙着埋藏榛子过冬。|近义词：brown, tan, chestnut (for color); filbert (for nut) |反义词: (No direct antonym for the tree/nut. For the color, perhaps) black, white |词性: noun, adjective

hazel:/ˈheɪzəl/ | adj.|淡褐色的|Having a light brown or yellowish brown color, like that of hazelnuts.|例句：She has beautiful hazel eyes. 她有一双美丽的淡褐色眼睛。|近义词：brown, tan, light brown |反义词: dark, black |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hæsel, from Proto-Germanic *hasalaz, from Proto-Indo-European *koselo-. Cognate with German Hasel, Dutch hazel.
