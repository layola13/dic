emus:/ˈiːmjuːz/
noun
鸸鹋（复数）
Large, flightless Australian birds similar to ostriches.
We saw a group of emus at the wildlife park. 我们在野生动物园看到了一群鸸鹋。
emus are native to Australia. 鸸鹋原产于澳大利亚。

近义词:ostriches, rheas
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑会飞的鸟类) birds


词性:n.


词源:From Portuguese ema, from Old Provençal ema 'large bird', of unknown origin.
