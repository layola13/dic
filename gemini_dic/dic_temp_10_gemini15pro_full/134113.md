nonimpacted:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpæktɪd/ | adj. | 未受影响的，未嵌入的 | Not impacted; not wedged tightly into a place, such as a tooth that has not erupted or a bone fracture where the broken ends are not forced together. | The dentist said my wisdom teeth are nonimpacted and shouldn't cause any problems. 我的牙医说我的智齿没有嵌入，应该不会引起任何问题。| The bullet wound was nonimpacted, making it easier to remove. 子弹的伤口没有嵌入，更容易取出。| Synonyms: unaffected, uninvolved, normal | Antonyms: impacted, wedged, embedded | adjective
