beefless:/ˈbiːflɪs/
adjective
无牛肉的;不含牛肉的
Containing no beef.
She prepared a beefless stew with hearty vegetables and lentils. 她准备了一份不含牛肉的炖菜，里面有丰盛的蔬菜和扁豆。
近义词:vegetarian, meatless
反义词:beefy
