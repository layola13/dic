overprocrastinations: /ˌoʊvərproʊˌkræstɪˈneɪʃənz/
noun
过度拖延，拖延症发作
The act of procrastinating excessively or repeatedly.  Putting off tasks or decisions beyond what is reasonable or necessary.
His overprocrastinations led to him failing the course.  他过度拖延导致他挂科了。
Near Synonyms: procrastination, delaying, stalling, deferring
Antonyms: diligence, promptness, action, decisiveness
n.
