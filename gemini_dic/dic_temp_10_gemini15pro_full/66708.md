exophthalmia:/ˌɛksɒfˈθælmiə/ /ˌɛɡzɒfˈθælmiə/
noun
眼球突出；突眼症
Abnormal protrusion of the eyeball.
例句：Graves' disease is a common cause of exophthalmia.  格雷夫斯病是突眼症的常见病因。

例句：The doctor noticed the patient's exophthalmia and suspected a thyroid problem. 医生注意到病人的眼球突出，怀疑是甲状腺问题。

近义词：proptosis, exophthalmos
反义词: enophthalmos
词性: noun
