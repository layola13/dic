lorencia:/ləˈrɛnsɪə/| n.|洛伦兹|A brand name for abatacept, a medication used to treat rheumatoid arthritis and other autoimmune diseases. It works by blocking the activation of T cells, a type of white blood cell that plays a key role in the immune response.|My doctor prescribed me Lorencia for my rheumatoid arthritis.|近义词:abatacept, Orencia (brand name)|反义词:N/A|noun
