extrudability:/ɪkˌstruːdəˈbɪləti/
n.|可挤出性，挤出性|The property of a material that determines how easily it can be extruded.  指材料容易被挤出的特性。|The extrudability of the plastic was tested before mass production.  这种塑料的可挤出性在量产前进行了测试。|Synonyms:  malleability, plasticity, ductility|Antonyms: rigidity, stiffness|noun
