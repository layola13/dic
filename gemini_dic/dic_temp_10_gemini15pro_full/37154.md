come to one's senses:/kʌm tə wʌnz ˈsɛnsɪz/
verb phrase
清醒过来；恢复理智；
To become reasonable or rational again after a period of confusion or irrationality.
He finally came to his senses and realized he was making a huge mistake. 他终于清醒过来，意识到自己犯了一个巨大的错误。
After her initial panic, she came to her senses and called for help. 她最初的恐慌过后，恢复了理智并打电话求救。


近义词: regain consciousness, become aware, see reason, think clearly, sober up
反义词: lose one's mind, go crazy, become irrational
