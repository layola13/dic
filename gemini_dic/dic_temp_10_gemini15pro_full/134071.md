nonilluminating:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
无启发性的，无益的
Not providing insight or clarity; unhelpful.
The expert's testimony was surprisingly nonilluminating.  专家的证词令人惊讶地没有启发性。
The instructions were nonilluminating and only added to our confusion.  说明书毫无启发性，反而让我们更加困惑。
Synonyms: uninformative, unhelpful, unenlightening, useless, pointless
Antonyms: informative, helpful, enlightening, useful, insightful
词性: 形容词
