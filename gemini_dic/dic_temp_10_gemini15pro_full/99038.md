interamerican:/ˌɪntərəˈmɛrɪkən/
adjective
美洲间的，泛美的
Relating to or involving countries in North, Central, and South America.
The Organization of American States (OAS) is an interamerican organization.  美洲国家组织（OAS）是一个泛美组织。
The interamerican highway connects many countries. 泛美公路连接了许多国家。
近义词:Pan-American
反义词:None
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + American.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among."  "American" refers to the Americas.
