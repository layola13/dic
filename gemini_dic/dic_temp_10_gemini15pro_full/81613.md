greave: /ɡriːv/
n.
护胫，腿甲
A piece of armor that protects the leg from the ankle to the knee.
The knight polished his greaves until they gleamed. 骑士把他的护胫擦得锃亮。
synonyms: leg guard, shin guard, jambeau
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps vulnerability, exposure)
noun
