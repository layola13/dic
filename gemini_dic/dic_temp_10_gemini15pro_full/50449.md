depreciatingly:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋli/
adverb
轻蔑地，贬低地
in a way that shows that you think something is of little value or importance.
He spoke depreciatingly of his colleagues. 他轻蔑地谈论他的同事。
Synonyms:disparagingly, contemptuously, scornfully
Antonyms: admiringly, respectfully, approvingly
词性: 副词
