diabolicalness:/ˌdaɪəˈbɒlɪkəlnəs/
n.
极端邪恶; 恶魔般的性质; 恶毒
The quality of being extremely wicked or cruel.
His diabolicalness was evident in his cruel treatment of animals. 他的恶毒在他对动物的残忍虐待中显露无遗。
近义词：wickedness, evil, depravity, cruelty, malice
反义词：goodness, benevolence, kindness, virtue
词性：名词

diabolical:/ˌdaɪəˈbɒlɪkəl/
adj.
恶魔般的；极恶的；糟糕透顶的
Extremely wicked or cruel; very bad or unpleasant.
The weather was diabolical - it rained non-stop for days. 天气糟糕透了——连续几天不停地下雨。
近义词：wicked, evil, fiendish, atrocious, terrible, awful
反义词：wonderful, excellent, good
词性：形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *diabolicālis, from Latin diabolus (“devil”) (from Ancient Greek διάβολος (diábolos, “slanderer”)).
