一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does he usually do on weekends? = 他周末通常做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she doing right now? = 她现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? = 他整个下午一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议之前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们一直在讨论什么好几个小时了？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你会完成什么？
What will they have finished by the end of the month? = 到月底他们会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
