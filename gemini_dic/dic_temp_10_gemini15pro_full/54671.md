disoccupation: /ˌdɪsˌɒkjuˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 失业，闲散，空闲 | The disoccupation rate has risen sharply in recent months. 近几个月的失业率急剧上升。|  A long period of disoccupation can lead to feelings of hopelessness. 长期失业会导致绝望感。 | 近义词: unemployment, idleness, inactivity | 反义词: employment, occupation, activity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From French *désoccupation*, from dés- (prefix denoting reversal) + occupation.  "Dés-" comes from Latin "dis-" meaning "apart, asunder, in different directions." "Occupation" comes from Latin "occupatio,"  from "occupare" meaning "to seize, take possession of, occupy."
