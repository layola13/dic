leadwort:/ˈlɛdwɜːt/ | n. | 蓝雪花,  白花丹 | A plant of the genus *Plumbago*, especially *Plumbago auriculata*, cultivated for its clusters of small blue, white, or purplish flowers. |  Example: The leadwort cascaded down the wall, a profusion of delicate blue blossoms. 蓝雪花像瀑布般倾泻而下，开满了精致的蓝色花朵。

Example:  She planted leadwort in her garden to attract butterflies.  她在花园里种了蓝雪花来吸引蝴蝶。 | Synonyms:  Cape leadwort, plumbago | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
