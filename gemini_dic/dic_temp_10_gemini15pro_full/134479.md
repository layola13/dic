nonlabelling:/ˌnɒnˈleɪbəlɪŋ/
|adj.|非标记的，未贴标签的|The process involves nonlabelling techniques to study protein interactions.  这个过程涉及使用非标记技术来研究蛋白质相互作用。|Nonlabelling methods are becoming increasingly popular in biological research. 非标记方法在生物学研究中越来越受欢迎。|近义词:unlabeled, untagged|反义词:labeled, tagged|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- + labelling

"non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without." It comes from Latin "non".

"labelling" comes from the verb "to label," which means to attach a label or tag to something.  It ultimately derives from Old French "label," meaning "ribbon, strip."
