armrest: /ˈɑːmˌrest/ (US), /ˈɑːmˌrest/ (UK)| n. |扶手，靠手|A padded or upholstered part of a chair or other seat on which a person can rest their arm.|I leaned back in my seat and rested my arm on the armrest. 我向后靠在座位上，把胳膊放在扶手上。|The armrest of the chair was broken. 椅子的扶手坏了。|Synonyms: arm support, elbow rest|Antonyms: None|Noun
