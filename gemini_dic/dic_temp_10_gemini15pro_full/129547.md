non-brahminical:/nɒn-brɑːˈmɪnɪkəl/
adjective
非婆罗门的
Not belonging to or associated with the Brahmin caste (the highest Hindu caste, traditionally of priests and scholars).
The non-brahminical movement sought to challenge the social hierarchy.  非婆罗门运动试图挑战社会等级制度。
The temple was open to non-brahminical worshippers.  这座寺庙对非婆罗门信徒开放。
近义词: non-Brahman, Shudra, Dalit (depending on context)
反义词: Brahminical
词性: adjective
