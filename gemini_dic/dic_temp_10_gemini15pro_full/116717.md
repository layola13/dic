maudeline:/ˈmɔːdlɪn/| n. |玛德琳(蛋糕);忏悔的玛德琳(指抹大拉的玛利亚);悔过的妓女|A small rich shell-shaped sponge cake. Also, a repentant prostitute, in allusion to Mary Magdalene.|例句：She baked a batch of delicious madeleines. 她烤了一批美味的玛德琳蛋糕。

例句：The character in the novel was portrayed as a modern-day madeleine. 小说中的人物被描绘成现代版的忏悔的玛德琳。|近义词：cake, pastry (for the food); penitent (for the figurative meaning)|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the context) |词性:noun
