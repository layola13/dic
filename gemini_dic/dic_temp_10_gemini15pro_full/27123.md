caliph:/ˈkeɪlɪf/ /ˈkælɪf/| n. |哈里发；伊斯兰教国家元首|A caliph is the civil and religious leader of a Muslim state considered to be a successor of Muhammad. |The caliph ruled over a vast empire. 哈里发统治着一个庞大的帝国。

The current caliph declared a holy war. 现任哈里发宣布了一场圣战。|近义词：ruler, sovereign, imam |反义词: subject, follower |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cali(p)he, from Old French calife, from Medieval Latin calipha, from Arabic خَلِيفَة‎ (ḵalīfa, “successor, representative”), verbal noun of خَلَفَ (ḵalafa, “to succeed, to follow”).
