contentiousness:/kənˈtɛnʃəsnəs/
|n.|争论性，争议性|The contentiousness of the issue made it difficult to reach a consensus.  这个问题的争议性使得很难达成共识。| English explanation:  A state of being contentious; characterized by disagreement or controversy.  |例句：The contentiousness of the topic led to a heated debate.  这个话题的争议性引发了激烈的辩论。

|例句：The political climate was marked by its contentiousness.  政治气氛的特点是充满争议。

|近义词：disputatiousness, argumentativeness, quarrelsomeness|反义词：agreeableness, amiability, harmony|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From contentious + -ness.  Contentious derives from the Latin contendere, meaning "to strive, contend."
