exhibitionists:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪsts/
noun
暴露狂；爱出风头的人
A person who behaves in an extravagant way in order to attract attention.
例句：He’s a bit of an exhibitionist – he loves being the center of attention. 他有点爱出风头——他喜欢成为关注的中心。

例句：The exhibitionist flashed the crowd from the stage.  那个暴露狂在舞台上向人群暴露自己。


synonyms: show-off, attention-seeker, flasher (for indecent exposure)
antonyms: introvert, recluse, wallflower
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Late Latin *exhibitionista, from Latin exhibere "to show forth".
