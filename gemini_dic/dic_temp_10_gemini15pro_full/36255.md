cognoscibility:/ˌkɒɡnɒsɪˈbɪləti/ /ˌkɑːɡnɑːsəˈbɪlədi/
n.
可知性；可认识性
The quality or state of being cognoscible; knowability.  The capability of being known or understood.
The cognoscibility of the universe is a question that has puzzled philosophers for centuries. 宇宙的可知性是几个世纪以来一直困扰着哲学家的一个问题。
Synonyms: knowability, understandability, comprehensibility, intelligibility
Antonyms: unknowability, incomprehensibility, unintelligibility
