foreclose:/fɔːrˈkloʊz/ /fɔrˈkloʊz/
verb
取消赎回权；排除；阻止
to take possession of a property from a mortgagor because of defaults on payments
例句：The bank foreclosed on their mortgage after they missed several payments.  银行在他们拖欠了几次付款后取消了他们的抵押品赎回权。

例句：The economic downturn has led to a rise in foreclosures. 经济衰退导致丧失抵押品赎回权的案例增加。

近义词：repossess, seize, confiscate
反义词：redeem, reinstate
词性：verb
