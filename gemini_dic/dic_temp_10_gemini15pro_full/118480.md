mesitylol:/ˈmɛsɪtɪlɒl/| n.|均三甲苯酚，2,4,6-三甲基苯酚|A colorless crystalline phenol that is used as an antiseptic and disinfectant.  It is a derivative of phenol with three methyl groups substituted at the 2, 4, and 6 positions on the aromatic ring.|Mesitylol is used in some disinfectants. 均三甲苯酚用于某些消毒剂中。|Mesitylol is an effective antiseptic against a broad range of bacteria. 均三甲苯酚是一种对多种细菌有效的防腐剂。|Synonyms: 2,4,6-trimethylphenol, mesitol|Antonyms: None|noun
