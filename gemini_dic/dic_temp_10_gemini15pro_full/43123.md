coyest:/ˈkɔɪɪst/ (US), /ˈkɔɪɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最害羞的，最腼腆的|Superlative form of coy (shy or reserved, especially in a playful or flirtatious way).  It describes someone who exhibits shyness to the highest degree.|She gave him the coyest look, batting her eyelashes. 她害羞地看了他一眼，眨了眨睫毛。|Synonyms: bashful, demure, timid, shy, reserved|Antonyms: bold, brazen, forward, immodest|adjective
