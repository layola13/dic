agast:/əˈɡæst/| adj.|惊骇的，吓呆的|Filled with sudden and overwhelming shock or amazement; terrified.|例句：She stood aghast at the sight of the bloody scene. 她看到血腥的场面吓得目瞪口呆。|例句：He was aghast at the news of his friend's sudden death. 他听到朋友突然去世的消息惊呆了。|近义词：shocked, horrified, stunned, appalled, dismayed|反义词：calm, composed, unfazed|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *agast, from Old English āgǣstan (“to frighten, terrify”), from ā- (“on, at, in”) + gǣstan (“to terrify, torment”). Related to ghost.
