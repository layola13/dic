insuperableness:/ˌɪnsuːpərəblnəs/
n.
不可战胜, 不可克服
the quality or state of being insuperable; invincibility.
His insuperableness in the face of adversity inspired everyone. 他面对逆境的不可战胜精神鼓舞了所有人。
Synonyms: invincibility, unconquerability, indomitability, impenetrability
Antonyms: vulnerability, weakness, conquerability, surmountability
noun
