hangout:/ˈhæŋaʊt/| n.|闲逛; 常去的地方|A place where someone spends a lot of time; an informal social gathering.|例句：The mall is a popular hangout for teenagers.  这个购物中心是青少年常去的地方。

例句：We had a hangout at the beach last weekend. 上周末我们在海滩聚会了一下。|近义词：gathering, get-together, haunt, joint|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
