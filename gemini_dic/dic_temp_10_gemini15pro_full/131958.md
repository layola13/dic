noncontrollably:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊləbli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈtroʊləbli/| adv.|不可控制地|In a way that cannot be controlled.  This word describes actions or processes that happen outside of someone's ability to manage or regulate them.|
Her hands trembled noncontrollably. 她的双手不由自主地颤抖。

The patient wept noncontrollably after receiving the bad news. 病人接到坏消息后，无法控制地哭泣。


近义词: uncontrollably, involuntarily, irrepressibly|
反义词: controllably, voluntarily|
词性: adverb|

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non.
controllable (adjective): From control (verb) + -able (suffix).  Control ultimately comes from Latin *contra ("against") + *rotulus ("roll," "list").  The suffix -able forms adjectives meaning "capable of being."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
