antiestablishmentarianism:/ˌæntiɪˈstæblɪʃmənˌtɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
反建制主义，反体制主义
Opposition to the establishment.  A political philosophy that opposes the conventional power structures and institutions of a society.
The student protests reflected a growing antiestablishmentarianism on campus.  校园里的学生抗议反映了日益增长的反建制主义情绪。
Synonyms: iconoclasm, dissidence, nonconformity, rebellion, radicalism
Antonyms: conformism, conservatism, traditionalism
noun
