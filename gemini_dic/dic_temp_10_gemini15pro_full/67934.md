facetious:/fəˈsiːʃəs/ | adj. | 爱开玩笑的；滑稽的；轻浮的 | Treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant. |  He made a facetious remark about the boss, which didn't go over well. 他对老板开了个不合时宜的玩笑，结果不太好。

She was being facetious when she said she wanted to run for president.  她说想竞选总统，只是在开玩笑。

| 近义词：flippant, frivolous, jocular, playful, waggish | 反义词：serious, earnest, sincere, solemn | 词性: adjective
