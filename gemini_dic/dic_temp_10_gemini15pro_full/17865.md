beefing:/ˈbiːfɪŋ/
v.
抱怨，发牢骚
Complaining, expressing dissatisfaction.
They were beefing about the long lines at the store. 他们在抱怨商店里排的长队。

beefing:/ˈbiːfɪŋ/
n.
抱怨，牢骚
A complaint, a grievance.
The constant beefing about the food got on my nerves.  对食物不断的抱怨让我很烦。
