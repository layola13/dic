dyers:/ˈdaɪərz/| n.|染工；染色工人|People who dye fabrics or materials.  Those who work with dyes to color textiles or other items.|The dyers carefully mixed the indigo to achieve the perfect shade of blue. 染工们小心地混合靛蓝，以达到完美的蓝色阴影。|The dyers' guild was a powerful force in the medieval city. 染工行会在中世纪城市中是一股强大的力量。|近义词：tinter, colorist|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a profession)|noun
