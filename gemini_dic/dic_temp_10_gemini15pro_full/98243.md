insensitivenesses:/ɪnˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
麻木不仁；感觉迟钝；不敏感
lack of sensitivity or responsiveness.
Her insensitivenesses to his feelings hurt him deeply. 她对他的感受麻木不仁，深深地伤害了他。
近义词: apathy, indifference, unresponsiveness, coldness, callousness
反义词: sensitivity, responsiveness, empathy, compassion, awareness
