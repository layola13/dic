biomaterials:/ˌbaɪoʊməˈtɪriəlz/| n.|生物材料|Materials designed to interface with biological systems to evaluate, treat, augment, or replace any tissue, organ, or function of the body.|Biomaterials are used in joint replacements and dental implants. 生物材料用于关节置换和牙科植入物。|synonyms: biocompatible materials, implants, prostheses|antonyms: natural tissues, organs|noun
