baguette:/bæˈɡɛt/ | n. | 法式长棍面包 | A long, thin loaf of French bread with a crisp crust. |  I bought a fresh baguette from the bakery. 我从面包店买了一条新鲜的法式长棍面包。

|例句：
This baguette is perfectly crusty. 这根法棍面包的硬皮烤得恰到好处。
Would you like some butter with your baguette?  你的法棍面包要涂黄油吗？


|近义词：French bread, loaf |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of bread) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *baguette*, diminutive of *bague* ("ring, stick"), from Old Provençal *baga*, from Late Latin *baca* ("stick, staff"). Ultimately from Gaulish *baccos* ("hook").
