loud-mouthed:/ˌlaʊdˈmaʊðd/ | adj. | 嗓门大的，大声喧哗的；口无遮拦的 |  Someone who is loud-mouthed talks too much, often saying things that are offensive or boastful.  It combines "loud" (high volume) and "mouthed" (having a particular kind of mouth, in this case, one that speaks loudly and often inappropriately). |  He's a loud-mouthed bully who picks on smaller kids. 他是一个嗓门大、爱欺负小孩的恃强凌弱者。 | The loud-mouthed commentator annoyed many viewers.  这个口无遮拦的评论员惹恼了许多观众。 |  boisterous, clamorous, vociferous | quiet, reserved, soft-spoken | adjective
