ectopic: /ɛkˈtɒpɪk/ (British) /ɛkˈtɑːpɪk/ (American)| n.|异位器官，异位组织|An organ or tissue occurring in an abnormal position.|Ectopic heartbeats can be a sign of a serious heart condition.  异位心跳可能是严重心脏疾病的征兆。|Synonyms: displacement, malposition|Antonyms: normal position|noun
