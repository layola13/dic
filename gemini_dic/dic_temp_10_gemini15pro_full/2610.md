adolescently: /ˌædəˈlɛsəntli/
adverb
青春期地，幼稚地
In a manner characteristic of adolescence; immaturely.
He giggled adolescently at the joke. 他幼稚地咯咯笑着听笑话。
She adolescently slammed the door in her parents' faces. 她像个青春期的孩子一样，当着父母的面摔门。

近义词: immaturely, childishly, youthfully
反义词: maturely, responsibly, adultly
词性: adverb

词源: From adolescent + -ly.  Adolescent comes from the Latin word "adolescens," meaning "growing up" or "young."
