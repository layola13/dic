compartmentalises:/kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentəlaɪzɪz/
verb
划分；分隔；把…分成各自独立的单位
To separate something into sections or categories, often to isolate or deal with it individually.
He compartmentalises his life, keeping work and personal matters strictly separate.  他将自己的生活划分开来，工作和私事严格分开。
The brain compartmentalises information to make it easier to process.  大脑将信息分隔开来，以便更容易处理。
categorizes, divides, separates, isolates
integrates, combines, unites
v.
