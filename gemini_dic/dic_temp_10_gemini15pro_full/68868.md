fast-tracker:/ˈfæstˌtrækər/ | n. | 快速晋升者，迅速成功人士 | Someone who is identified as having the potential to progress rapidly in their career and is given special training or opportunities. |  She's a real fast-tracker and has already been promoted twice in the last year. 她是一个真正的快速晋升者，在过去一年里已经升职两次了。


|  The company has a fast-tracker program for talented graduates.  这家公司为有才华的毕业生设立了快速晋升计划。 | 近义词：high-flyer, go-getter, rising star | 反义词：underachiever, laggard | 词性: 名词
