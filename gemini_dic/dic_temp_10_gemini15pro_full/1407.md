accustomable: /əˈkʌstəməbl/
adjective
可习惯的；易适应的
Able to become accustomed to something.
He found the climate accustomable. 他发现这个气候很容易适应。
The food was strange at first, but quite accustomable.  起初这食物很奇怪，但很容易习惯。
近义词: adaptable, used to, habituated
反义词: unaccustomed, inadaptable
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *accustomābilis, from accustomāre (“to accustom”) + -bilis. Equivalent to accustom +‎ -able.
