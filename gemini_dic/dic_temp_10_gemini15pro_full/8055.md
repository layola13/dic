anthropologically:/ˌænθrəpəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
人类学地
in a way that relates to the study of humankind
例句：The problem was examined anthropologically.  这个问题是从人类学的角度来检验的。

例句：She analyzed the social structure anthropologically. 她从人类学的角度分析了社会结构。


近义词：ethnologically, culturally
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, could consider using terms like "non-anthropologically" or focusing on specific contrasting approaches like "biologically," "psychologically," etc.)
词性: adverb
