noninstinctive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈstɪŋktɪv/
adjective
非本能的；非直觉的
Not based on instinct; learned rather than innate.
例句：Her noninstinctive reaction to the crisis showed her training. 她对危机的非本能反应表明了她受过的训练。

例句：The cat's hunting skills are instinctive, while the dog's obedience is largely noninstinctive. 猫的狩猎技能是本能的，而狗的服从很大程度上是非本能的。

近义词：learned, acquired, conditioned, deliberate, conscious
反义词：instinctive, innate, inborn, natural, intuitive
词性：adjective
