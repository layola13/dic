ok:/oʊˈkeɪ/ (US), /əʊˈkeɪ/ (UK)| adj. adv. interj. n.|可以，没问题，还可以|Used to express agreement, acceptance, or approval.|例句：Is everything ok? 一切都好吗？|近义词：alright, fine, acceptable|反义词:unacceptable, bad, awful|adjective, adverb, interjection, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Possibly from "oll korrect," a humorous misspelling of "all correct," popularized during the 1839 US presidential election.
