一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think about this movie? = 你觉得这部电影怎么样？
What does she usually do on weekends? = 她周末通常做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? = 她做了什么值得这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我希望我知道该做什么。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？


否定句:

There's nothing what I can do. 我什么也做不了。
