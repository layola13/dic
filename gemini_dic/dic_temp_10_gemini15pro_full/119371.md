microcosmical:/ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒzmɪkəl/| adj.|微观的，小世界的，缩影的|Relating to or characteristic of a microcosm; a miniature world or universe; a community or situation regarded as encapsulating in miniature the characteristics of something much larger.| This bustling city is a microcosmical representation of the nation as a whole.  这个繁忙的城市是整个国家的一个缩影。| The ant colony served as a microcosmical model for studying social behavior.  这个蚁群作为一个微观模型来研究社会行为。| synonyms: miniature, representative, epitomizing| antonyms: macrocosmical, universal, global| adjective
