fasd:/fæzd/ | n. |  胎儿酒精谱系障碍 (Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorders) |  A group of conditions that can occur in a person whose mother drank alcohol during pregnancy. These effects can include physical problems and problems with behavior and learning. |  FASD is a preventable condition.  胎儿酒精谱系障碍是一种可预防的疾病。|  Early intervention can help children with FASD reach their full potential. 早期干预可以帮助患有胎儿酒精谱系障碍的儿童充分发挥其潜力。| 近义词: None (as it's a specific medical term) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
