lukar:/ˈluːkɑːr/| n.| 卢卡尔（苏美尔城邦的统治者）|A lukar was the ruler of a Sumerian city-state, often holding both religious and political power.  卢卡尔是苏美尔城邦的统治者，通常同时拥有宗教和政治权力。|The lukar of Uruk led the city to prosperity. 乌鲁克的卢卡尔带领这座城市走向繁荣。|Synonym:ruler, king, governor|Antonym:subject, citizen|noun


lukar:/ˈluːkɑːr/| n.| (古罗马)角斗士学校|A lukar was sometimes used to refer to a gladiatorial school in ancient Rome, though this usage is less common.  卢卡尔有时也指古罗马的角斗士学校，尽管这种用法不太常见。|Gladiators trained in the lukar for years before entering the arena.  角斗士在进入竞技场之前在卢卡尔训练多年。|Synonym:gladiator school, training ground|Antonym:arena, colosseum|noun
