chiselled:/ˈtʃɪzəld/ (US) /ˈtʃɪzəld/ (UK)| adj.|轮廓分明的，雕刻般的|Clearly defined, as if sculpted; angular.|He had a chiselled jawline and high cheekbones. 他有着轮廓分明的下颌和高颧骨。|近义词：sculpted, sharp, defined, angular|反义词：round, soft, undefined|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "chisel" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Chisel" comes from Old French "cisel", diminutive of "cis" meaning "cutting tool".
