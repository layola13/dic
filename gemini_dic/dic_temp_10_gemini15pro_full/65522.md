eurypterus:/jʊˈrɪptərəs/ | n. | 广翅鲎，板足鲎 | An extinct arthropod of the class Eurypterida, related to horseshoe crabs and scorpions, that lived in the Paleozoic Era.  They are also known as sea scorpions. | Example:  The Eurypterus was a fearsome predator in the ancient seas. 广翅鲎是古代海洋中可怕的掠食者。

Example:  Fossils of Eurypterus have been found in various parts of the world. 在世界各地都发现了广翅鲎的化石。 | 近义词: sea scorpion | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *eury-* (wide) + *-pterus* (wing/fin).
