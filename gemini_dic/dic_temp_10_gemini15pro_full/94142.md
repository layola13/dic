impedingly:/ɪmˈpiːdɪŋli/
adv.
阻碍地，妨碍地
in a way that impedes or hinders progress or movement.
The heavy traffic was moving impedingly slowly.  交通拥堵，缓慢行驶。
Synonyms: obstructively, hinderingly, restrictively
Antonyms: helpfully, supportively, facilitatively
词性: 副词
