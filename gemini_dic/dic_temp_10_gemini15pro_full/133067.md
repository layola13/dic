词源:  "nonequalization" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the word "equalization." "Equalization" comes from the verb "equalize," which in turn derives from "equal" with the suffix "-ize."  The root "equal" ultimately comes from the Latin word "aequalis."
