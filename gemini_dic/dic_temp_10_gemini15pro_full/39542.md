consanguineous:/ˌkɒnˌsæŋˈɡwɪniəs/ /ˌkɑːnˌsæŋˈɡwɪniəs/| adj.|同血缘的；血亲的；近亲的|Descended from the same ancestor; related by blood.|例句：They were forbidden to marry because they were consanguineous. 他们被禁止结婚，因为他们是近亲。

例句：Consanguineous marriages are more common in some cultures. 在某些文化中，近亲结婚更为普遍。|近义词：related, kindred, cognate|反义词：unrelated, non-consanguineous|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin consanguineus (“of the same blood”), from con- (“with”) + sanguis (“blood”).
