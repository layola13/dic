mickey-mousing:/ˈmɪki ˌmaʊsɪŋ/ | n. |米老鼠式配乐，亦称“照搬动作” | Synchronizing the music closely to the action on screen, often in an exaggerated or cartoonish way, so that the music mimics every movement or gesture. | The cartoon used mickey-mousing to emphasize the character's comical slips and falls. 这部动画片用米老鼠式配乐来强调角色滑稽的滑倒和摔倒。|  Mickey-mousing effectively highlighted the slapstick comedy in the scene.  米老鼠式配乐有效地突出了场景中的闹剧喜剧。 | 近义词: synchronous scoring, illustrative music | 反义词: contrapuntal music, asynchronous sound |词性:名词
