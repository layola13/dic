biostatistics:/ˌbaɪoʊstəˈtɪstɪks/ | n. | 生物统计学 | The application of statistical methods to the solution of biological problems. |  Biostatistics plays a crucial role in designing and analyzing clinical trials. 生物统计学在设计和分析临床试验中起着至关重要的作用。

Biostatistics helped researchers understand the spread of the disease. 生物统计学帮助研究人员了解疾病的传播。| 近义词: biometry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + statistics.  "Bio-" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life." "Statistics" ultimately derives from the Latin "statisticus" meaning "of state affairs."
