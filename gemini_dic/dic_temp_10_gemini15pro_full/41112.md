coproduced:/ˌkoʊprəˈduːst/
| v. | 共同制作，联合制作 | Produced together by two or more people or organizations. | The film was coproduced by American and French companies. 这部电影是由美国和法国公司联合制作的。 | The play was coproduced by two rival theatre groups. 这部戏是由两个相互竞争的剧团联合制作的。 |  collaborated, jointly produced | independently produced | verb
