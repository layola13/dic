gapeseed:/ˈɡæpˌsiːd/| n. |葡萄籽|Grape seed; the small seed found inside a grape.|The gapeseeds can be pressed to make grapeseed oil. 葡萄籽可以被压榨用来制作葡萄籽油。| They spit out the gapeseeds after eating the grapes. 他们吃完葡萄后吐出了葡萄籽。|近义词：grape pip, grape stone|反义词: N/A|词性：名词

词源:
Compound word from "grape" and "seed". "Grape" comes from Old French "grape," and "seed" comes from Old English "sæd."
