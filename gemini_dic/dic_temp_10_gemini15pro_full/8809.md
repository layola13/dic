antientropic:/ˌæntiɛnˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 反熵的，抗熵的 | Relating to or characterized by antientropy; counteracting or reversing entropy, which is a measure of disorder or randomness.  | Example:  The antientropic force of life maintains complex biological structures. 生命的反熵力量维持着复杂的生物结构。 | Example:  His theory proposed an antientropic mechanism for the evolution of consciousness. 他的理论提出了一种意识进化的反熵机制。 | Near Synonyms:  negentropic, counter-entropic, order-increasing | Antonyms:  entropic, disorder-increasing | Word Type: adjective
