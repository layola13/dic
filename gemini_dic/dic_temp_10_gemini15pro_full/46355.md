dactylist:/ˈdæktɪlɪst/
n.
指法训练者，打字员
A person skilled in using a keyboard, especially a typewriter.
Her years as a dactylist made her fingers fly across the keyboard. 她多年的打字员经验使她的手指在键盘上飞舞。
近义词:typist, keyboarder
反义词:None
词性:noun

dactylist:/ˈdæktɪlɪst/
n.
韵律诗专家，精通长短短格律的诗人
A person skilled in or specializing in dactylic verse.
The dactylist composed a beautiful poem in dactylic hexameter. 这位韵律诗专家用长短短六步格写了一首美丽的诗。
近义词:prosodist, metricist
反义词:None
词性:noun
