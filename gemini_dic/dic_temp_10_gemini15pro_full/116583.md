matriarchy: /ˈmeɪtriˌɑːrki/ (US), /ˈmeɪtriˌɑːki/ (UK)| n. |母系社会，母权制|A social system in which women hold primary power, especially in families and government.|The anthropologist studied the Minangkabau people of Indonesia, a well-known example of a matriarchy.|Synonyms: matriarchate, gynarchy|Antonyms: patriarchy, patriarchy|noun
