metazoan: /ˌmɛtəˈzoʊən/ | n. | 后生动物 | Any animal of the Metazoa subkingdom; a multicellular animal having cells differentiated into tissues and organs and usually a digestive cavity and nervous system.  |  Metazoans are a diverse group, ranging from simple sponges to complex mammals. 后生动物是一个多样化的群体，从简单的海绵到复杂的哺乳动物。 | synonyms: animal, multicellular animal | antonyms: protozoan, single-celled organism | noun

metazoans: /ˌmɛtəˈzoʊənz/ | n. | 后生动物（复数） | Plural form of metazoan. Refers to multiple multicellular animals.  | Many metazoans exhibit complex behaviors. 许多后生动物表现出复杂的行为。 | synonyms: animals, multicellular animals | antonyms: protozoa, single-celled organisms | noun
