overhorse:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːrs/ | v. | 过度骑乘；使过度劳累 | To ride a horse too much or too hard, causing it to become exhausted or injured. It can also be used figuratively to mean overworking or exploiting someone or something. | He overhorsed his prize stallion and the animal went lame. 他过度骑乘了他的良种公马，导致这匹马瘸了。|  Overworked, overused, exploit | Underuse, rest | Verb
