disinter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːr/ /ˌdɪsɪnˈtər/| vt.|掘出；挖出；使重现|To dig up (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth; unearth; to bring back from obscurity; revive.|They disinterred the body.|近义词：exhume, unearth, uncover, resurrect|反义词: bury, inter, conceal|词性:动词
词源：dis- (not, opposite of) + inter (to bury)
