carves:/kɑːrvz/| v. |雕刻，切割，开创|cuts or shapes something, especially wood or stone, using a sharp tool; achieves something by hard work and effort|He carved a beautiful statue out of wood. 他用木头雕刻了一尊美丽的雕像。

The artist skillfully carved the intricate details of the dragon.  这位艺术家巧妙地雕刻了龙的复杂细节。

|近义词：sculpts, engraves, cuts, fashions, shapes, hews, whittles, molds, forms, creates, establishes, builds|反义词:constructs (sometimes, depending on context), erases, destroys|词性:动词
