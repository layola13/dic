nonprohibitively:/ˌnɑːnproʊˈhɪbɪtɪvli/ | adv. |并非禁止地；价格适中的；可承受的|Not prohibitively; at a manageable or reasonable cost.  It describes something that is not so expensive or difficult as to prevent it from being done or obtained.|例句：The software is nonprohibitively priced, making it accessible to small businesses.  这个软件定价适中，小企业也能负担得起。

例句：The trip was nonprohibitively expensive, so we decided to go.  这次旅行的费用并非高不可攀，所以我们决定去。|近义词：affordable, reasonable, moderate, manageable|反义词：prohibitive, exorbitant, expensive, unaffordable|词性:副词
