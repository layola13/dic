/ætˈmaɪətri/ | n. | 大气疗法; 空气疗法 | The therapeutic use of air, especially of different climates or altitudes, in the treatment of pulmonary tuberculosis and other diseases. | He went to the mountains for atmiatry. 他去山上进行空气疗法。 | 近义词: aerotherapy, climatotherapy | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *ἀτμός (atmós, “vapor, breath”) + -iatry.
