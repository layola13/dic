ferulaceous:/ˌfɛruˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
似茴香的；有茴香味的；含树脂的
Resembling a ferula; having the properties of a ferula; containing resin.
The ferulaceous scent filled the air as we walked through the fennel field.  我们走过茴香地时，空气中弥漫着茴香的气味。
The plant's sap had a ferulaceous quality. 这种植物的汁液有一种类似茴香的特性。
synonyms: resinous, gummy, fennel-like
antonyms: non-resinous
