overamplifying:/ˌoʊvərˈæmplɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
过度放大；过分夸大
To amplify something excessively, beyond what is necessary or accurate.
The media is overamplifying the severity of the storm, causing unnecessary panic. 媒体过分夸大了风暴的严重程度，造成了不必要的恐慌。
He tends to overamplifying his achievements, making him seem arrogant. 他倾向于过分夸大自己的成就，这让他看起来很傲慢。
overstating, exaggerating, magnifying
understating, downplaying, minimizing
v.
