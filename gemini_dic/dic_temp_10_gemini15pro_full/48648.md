defenselessly:/dɪˈfɛnslɪsli/ | adv. | 无助地，毫无防备地 | In a manner that lacks defense or protection; vulnerably. | The small animal crouched defenselessly as the hawk swooped down. 小动物在老鹰俯冲下来时无助地蜷缩着。|  The child stood defenselessly in the face of the bully. 孩子面对恶霸无助地站着。 |  Synonyms: helplessly, vulnerably, unprotectedly | Antonyms: protectedly, securely, guardedly | adverb
