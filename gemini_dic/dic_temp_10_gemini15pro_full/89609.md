hoodwinked:/hʊdˈwɪŋkt/
verb
欺骗，蒙蔽
deceived or tricked
The con man hoodwinked them into giving him their money. 骗子骗走了他们的钱。
deceived, tricked, duped, fooled, swindled
informed, enlightened, aware, discerning
