hypospadias:/ˌhaɪpəˈspeɪdiəs/
n.
尿道下裂
A congenital condition in which the opening of the urethra is on the underside of the penis, instead of at the tip.
Hypospadias is a birth defect that affects the urethra. 尿道下裂是一种影响尿道的出生缺陷。
Surgery is usually required to correct hypospadias. 通常需要手术来矫正尿道下裂。
近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include) congenital anomaly, birth defect
反义词: None
