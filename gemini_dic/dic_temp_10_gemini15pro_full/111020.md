liripoop:/ˈlɪrɪpuːp/| n.| 学位帽上的垂布；长带；垂饰；（旧时）从学位帽垂下的黑色丝带|A long band or tail of a hood, especially the black silk band formerly worn hanging from an academic hood. |He adjusted the liripoop of his doctoral hood. 他调整了一下博士学位帽上的垂布。|近义词: tassel, pendant, band, streamer |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "cap" as the opposite of a hanging part of a hat) |词性: noun
