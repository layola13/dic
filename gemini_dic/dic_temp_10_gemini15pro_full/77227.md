gastroenteritis:/ˌɡæstroʊˌɛntəˈraɪtɪs/
n.
肠胃炎
Inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically resulting from bacterial toxins or viral infection and causing vomiting and diarrhea.
Gastroenteritis can be caused by a number of different viruses. 肠胃炎可能由多种不同的病毒引起。
Many children suffer from gastroenteritis during the summer months. 许多孩子在夏季会患上肠胃炎。
近义词：stomach flu, belly flu
反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease, could consider "health" or "wellness" as a contrasting state)
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
gastro- (Greek gastēr, gastrós = stomach) + entero- (Greek enteron = intestine) + -itis (Greek -itis = inflammation)
