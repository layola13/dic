overexplained:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪnd/ | adj. | 过度解释的，解释过多的 | Explained something in excessive detail, often to the point of being confusing or condescending. |  She overexplained the joke, and it wasn't funny anymore. 她把笑话解释得太多了，一点也不好笑了。 | Overexplained the process, making it seem more complicated than it was. 把过程解释得过于详细，使它看起来比实际更复杂。| 近义词: overelaborated, belabored | 反义词: unexplained, concise, succinct | 词性: 形容词
