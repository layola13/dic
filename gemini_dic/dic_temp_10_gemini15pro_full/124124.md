mortgagee:/ˌmɔːrɡəˈdʒiː/
noun
贷款人，抵押权人
The person or entity to whom property is mortgaged.  The lender in a mortgage transaction.
The mortgagee has the right to foreclose on the property if the mortgagor defaults on the loan. 贷款人有权在抵押人拖欠贷款时取消抵押品赎回权。
lender, creditor
mortgagor, debtor
n.
