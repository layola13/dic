clannish: /ˈklænɪʃ/ (US), /ˈklænɪʃ/ (UK)
adjective
排外的；小集团的；宗派的
inclined to stick together as a group and to distrust or dislike outsiders.
Example: The company was very clannish, and it was difficult for newcomers to fit in.  这家公司非常排外，新来的人很难融入。

Example:  They were a clannish bunch, rarely socializing with anyone outside their own circle.  他们是一个小团体，很少与圈子以外的人交往。

近义词: cliquish, exclusive, insular, partisan
反义词: inclusive, open, welcoming
词性: adjective
