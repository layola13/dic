hypervascular:/ˌhaɪpərˈvæskjələr/
adjective
血管丰富的，血管增生的
Characterized by excessive formation of blood vessels.
Example: Hypervascular tumors can be difficult to treat. 血管丰富的肿瘤可能难以治疗。
Example: The scan revealed a hypervascular lesion in the liver. 扫描显示肝脏内有一个血管增生的病灶。

近义词: angiogenic, vascularized
反义词: avascular, hypovascular
词性: adjective
