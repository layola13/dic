nonconductor:/ˌnɑːnkənˈdʌktər/ | n. | 非导体；绝缘体 | A substance that does not readily transmit heat, sound, or electricity. |  Rubber is a good nonconductor of electricity. 橡胶是良好的绝缘体。| synonyms: insulator, dielectric | antonyms: conductor | noun

non-conducting:/ˌnɑːnkənˈdʌktɪŋ/ | adj. | 不导电的；非传导性的 | Not transmitting heat, sound, or electricity. |  A non-conducting material is used to insulate the wires.  一种非导电材料被用来隔离电线。| synonyms: insulating | antonyms: conducting | adjective
