one-stop:/ˌwʌnˈstɒp/ /ˌwʌnˈstɑːp/ | adj. | 一站式的；全方位的 | Providing or offering a comprehensive range of goods or services in one location. |  This supermarket is a one-stop shop for all your grocery needs. 这家超市可以满足你所有的食品杂货需求。

One-stop shopping is very convenient for busy people.  一站式购物对于忙碌的人来说非常方便。 | 近义词: comprehensive, all-in-one | 反义词: multi-stage, piecemeal | 词性: 形容词
