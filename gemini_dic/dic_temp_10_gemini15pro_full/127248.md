nautiform: /ˈnɔːtɪfɔːrm/
adjective
船形的；似船的
Shaped like a ship or boat.
The nautiform sculpture in the park was a popular attraction. 公园里船形的雕塑是一个受欢迎的景点。
The architect designed a nautiform building that resembled a sleek yacht.  建筑师设计了一座类似于豪华游艇的船形建筑。
近义词: boat-shaped, ship-shaped, navicular
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider amorphous or shapeless)
词性: adjective
