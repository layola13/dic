hell-around:/ˌhɛl əˈraʊnd/

胡闹，狂欢作乐，到处乱跑
To behave in a wild and reckless manner, often involving excessive drinking, partying, and causing mischief.
He spent his youth hell-arounding with his friends, going to parties and getting into trouble. 他年轻时和朋友们一起胡闹，参加聚会，惹是生非。
They were hell-arounding in their sports car, speeding and playing loud music. 他们开着跑车四处乱窜，超速行驶，大声播放音乐。
近义词: carouse, revel, raise hell
反义词: behave, obey
