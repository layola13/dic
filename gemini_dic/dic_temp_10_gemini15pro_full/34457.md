clavicular:/kləˈvɪkjʊlər/
adjective
锁骨的
Relating to the clavicle (collarbone).
The clavicular fracture required surgery. 锁骨骨折需要手术。
The doctor examined the patient's clavicular area. 医生检查了病人的锁骨区域。

近义词: supraclavicular, infraclavicular
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific bone)


词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *clavicularis, from clavicula (“small key, collarbone”) + -aris.
