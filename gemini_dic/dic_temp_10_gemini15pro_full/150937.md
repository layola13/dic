paralympian:/ˌpærəˈlɪmpiən/ | n. | 残奥运动员 | An athlete who participates in the Paralympic Games, an international multi-sport event involving athletes with a range of disabilities, including impaired muscle power, impaired passive range of movement, limb deficiency, leg length difference, short stature, hypertonia, ataxia, athetosis, vision impairment and intellectual impairment. |  The Chinese Paralympian broke the world record in the 100-meter sprint.  中国残奥运动员打破了100米短跑世界纪录。

 The Paralympian showed incredible strength and determination.  这位残奥运动员展现了惊人的力量和决心。 | 近义词: disabled athlete, para-athlete | 反义词: able-bodied athlete | 词性: 名词

Etymology: para- (prefix meaning beside, alongside, or beyond) + Olympian (referring to the Olympic Games)
