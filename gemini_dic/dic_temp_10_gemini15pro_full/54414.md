disincline:/ˌdɪsɪnˈklaɪn/
|v.|使不情愿，使不愿|To make someone unwilling or reluctant to do something.|His poor health disinclined him to travel. 他的健康状况不佳，使他不愿旅行。|deter, discourage|incline, encourage
|verb|


disinclination:/ˌdɪsɪŋklɪˈneɪʃən/
|n.|不情愿，不愿|A lack of willingness or desire to do something.|She felt a strong disinclination to go out in the cold. 她非常不愿意在寒冷的天气里出门。|reluctance, aversion|inclination, willingness
|noun|
