misacted:/ˌmɪsˈæktɪd/ | v. | 演得不好，行为不当 | to act badly or inappropriately, especially in a play or movie; to behave badly or improperly. |  The lead actor misacted the crucial scene, ruining the emotional impact. 男主角演砸了关键的一场戏，破坏了情感冲击力。

The CEO misacted by using company funds for personal expenses.  首席执行官行为不当，将公司资金用于个人开支。 | Synonyms: mishandled, misbehaved, misconducted | Antonyms: performed well, behaved well, acted properly | verb
