iced:/aɪst/ | adj. | 冰镇的，覆有冰的 | Covered with ice; chilled or cooled with ice. |  I enjoy a nice iced coffee on a hot day. 我喜欢在大热天喝一杯冰咖啡。 | iced tea 冰茶 |例句：

The road was iced over and dangerous to drive on. 道路结冰了，开车很危险。

We sipped iced tea on the porch. 我们在门廊上啜饮冰茶。

| 近义词: chilled, frosty, frozen, refrigerated | 反义词: warm, hot, melted | 词性:形容词
