bowler-hatted:/ˈboʊlər ˌhætɪd/| adj.|戴圆顶硬礼帽的；戴着圆顶礼帽的|Wearing a bowler hat; characterized by wearing a bowler hat.  The word combines "bowler" (referring to the hat style) and "hatted" (meaning wearing a hat).  The "bowler" part likely comes from the hat's resemblance to a wooden bowl used in the game of bowls.|例句：A bowler-hatted gentleman stepped out of the carriage. 一位戴着圆顶礼帽的绅士从马车里走了出来。

例句：The bowler-hatted figure disappeared into the fog. 戴着圆顶礼帽的身影消失在雾中。|近义词：formal, old-fashioned|反义词：casual, modern|词性:形容词
