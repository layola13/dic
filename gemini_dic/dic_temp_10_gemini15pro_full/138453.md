novatian:/nəˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 诺瓦天派教徒；诺瓦天主义|A follower of Novatian, a priest in the 3rd century who held rigorous views on the treatment of those who had lapsed from the faith during persecution. |  Novatian believed that the Church should not readmit those who had denied Christ.  诺瓦天认为教会不应该重新接纳那些否认基督的人。| Synonyms: rigorist, puritan | Antonyms: lenient, lax | Noun

novatianism:/nəˈveɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 诺瓦天主义 |The doctrines of Novatian, especially his belief that the Church should not readmit those who had lapsed from the faith during persecution. | Novatianism was condemned as a heresy. 诺瓦天主义被谴责为异端邪说。| Synonyms: rigorism, puritanism | Antonyms: leniency, laxity | Noun
