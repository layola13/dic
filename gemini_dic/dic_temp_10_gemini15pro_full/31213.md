channel-hop:/ˈtʃænl hɒp/ (UK) /ˈtʃænl hɑːp/ (US)| v. | 跳台，快速转换电视频道 | To quickly change between television channels, typically using a remote control, often in search of something more interesting. | I was channel-hopping and stumbled across a really good documentary. 我在跳台时偶然发现了一部非常好的纪录片。| synonyms: channel-surf, flick through, zap | antonyms: watch attentively, focus on one channel | verb

channel-hop:/ˈtʃænl hɒpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈtʃænl hɑːpɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 跳台，转换频道 | The act of quickly changing between television channels. | Channel-hopping is a common habit during commercial breaks. 在广告时段跳台是一个常见的习惯。| synonyms: channel-surfing, flicking through, zapping | antonyms: attentive viewing, focused watching | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"Channel" refers to a television channel or frequency. "Hop" suggests a quick, short movement or change. The combined term "channel-hop" literally means to quickly jump from one channel to another.
