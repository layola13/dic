parsimony:/ˈpɑːrsɪməni/ |n.|节俭，吝啬；简约性|Extreme parsimony is sometimes mistaken for poverty.  极度的节俭有时会被误认为是贫穷。|Synonyms: thrift, frugality, economy, austerity|Antonyms: extravagance, prodigality, lavishness|noun
parsimonies:/ˈpɑːrsɪməniz/ |n.|节俭，吝啬；简约性（parsimony的复数形式）|The parsimonies of the government have caused widespread discontent. 政府的节俭措施引起了广泛的不满。|Synonyms: thriftinesses, frugalities, economies, austerities|Antonyms: extravagances, prodigalities, lavishnesses|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *parsimonia, from Latin parsus ("spared"), past participle of parcere ("to spare").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *perk- ("to spare").
