eminency:/ˈɛmɪnənsi/ | n. | 显赫，卓越，高位 |  A high rank or station, especially in a hierarchy such as the Church. |  His Eminency the Cardinal presided over the mass.  主教大人主持了弥撒。 | synonyms:  prominence, distinction, renown, eminence | antonyms: obscurity, insignificance, lowliness | noun

eminency:/ˈɛmɪnənsi/ | n. | 紧急，危急 |  A state of being imminent or urgent. (archaic) |  The eminency of the danger made them act quickly. 危险的紧迫性使他们迅速采取行动。(古语) | synonyms: urgency, imminence, exigency | antonyms:  delay, postponement | noun
