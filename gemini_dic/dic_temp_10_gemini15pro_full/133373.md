nonextraneously:/ˌnɒnɛkˈstreɪniəsli/
adjective
非外在地；内在地；固有地
inherently; intrinsically; not extraneously
The value of a work of art is often judged nonextraneously, based on its inherent qualities rather than external factors like market trends.  一件艺术作品的价值通常是非外在地判断的，基于其固有品质而不是像市场趋势这样的外部因素。
The properties of the material are nonextraneously linked to its molecular structure.  这种材料的特性与其分子结构内在地联系在一起。
Synonym: intrinsically, inherently, essentially
Antonym: extraneously, externally
词性: adjective
