The nonintermittence of her efforts finally paid off. 她坚持不懈的努力最终得到了回报。|近义词：continuity, constancy, persistence|反义词：intermittence, interruption, discontinuity|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From non- (not) + intermittent (occurring at irregular intervals) + -ence (forming nouns of state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *intermittere* (to discontinue, interrupt).
