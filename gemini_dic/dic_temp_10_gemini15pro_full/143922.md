outproducing:/ˌaʊtprəˈdjuːsɪŋ/
verb
生产过剩，产量超过
Producing more than someone or something else.
China is outproducing the US in steel. 中国的钢铁产量超过了美国。
synonyms: exceeding, surpassing, outstripping
antonyms: underproducing, lagging behind
