hdri:/ˌeɪtʃ diː ɑːr ˈaɪ/| n. |高动态范围图像 (High Dynamic Range Image)|A high dynamic range image (HDRI or HDR, sometimes called high dynamic range rendering), is a computer graphics technique used to represent more realistic lighting than traditional methods.  It allows for a greater range of luminance between the lightest and darkest areas of an image.|The artist used an HDRI to create a realistic sky in the 3D scene.|近义词:HDR|反义词:LDR (Low Dynamic Range)|abbr.
