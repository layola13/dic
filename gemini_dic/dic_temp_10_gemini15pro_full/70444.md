figurated:/ˈfɪɡjʊreɪtɪd/
adjective
形象的；比喻的；修饰的；（数字）由图形构成的
representing by a figure or figures; adorned with figures; (of a number) such that it can be represented by a geometrical figure
The carpet was figurated with flowers and leaves.  地毯上饰有花卉和树叶的图案。
The mathematician studied figurated numbers, such as triangular and square numbers.  这位数学家研究图形数，例如三角形数和正方形数。
近义词:pictorial, symbolic, metaphorical, representative
反义词:abstract, unadorned, plain
词性:adjective
