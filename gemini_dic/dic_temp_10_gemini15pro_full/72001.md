flintier:/ˈflɪntiər/ | adj. | 更坚硬的，更冷酷的，更无情的 | (comparative of `flinty`)  Made of or resembling flint; very hard; unyielding; showing no sympathy or pity. | Her gaze was flintier than usual after the argument. 争吵过后，她的目光比平时更加冷酷。| The soil here is flintier than in the valley. 这里的土壤比山谷里的更坚硬。 | 近义词: harder, sterner, colder, more ruthless, more unfeeling | 反义词: softer, gentler, kinder, more compassionate | 词性: adjective

flintiest:/ˈflɪntist/ | adj. | 最坚硬的，最冷酷的，最无情的 | (superlative of `flinty`)  Made of or resembling flint; very hard; unyielding; showing no sympathy or pity. | He had the flintiest stare of anyone she had ever met. 他有着她所见过的任何人中最冷酷的目光。| This is the flintiest soil I've ever encountered. 这是我遇到过的最坚硬的土壤。| 近义词: hardest, sternest, coldest, most ruthless, most unfeeling | 反义词: softest, gentlest, kindest, most compassionate | 词性: adjective

词源: From flint + -y + -er/-est.  Flint is an extremely hard sedimentary rock. The "-y" suffix turns it into an adjective, and "-er" and "-est" create the comparative and superlative forms, respectively.
