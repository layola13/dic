gaveled:/ˈɡævəld/ | v. | 用小木槌敲击；宣布（裁决或决定）|  The judge gavelled the meeting to order. 法官敲响小木槌宣布会议开始。 | Synonyms: adjourned, concluded, finalized | Antonyms: commenced, initiated, opened | verb

gaveled:/ˈɡævəld/ | adj. | 用小木槌敲击的；宣布的 |  The gavelled decision was met with applause.  用小木槌敲定的决定赢得了掌声。 | Synonyms: declared, announced, proclaimed | Antonyms:  undeclared, unannounced, withheld | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From gavel (n.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).  The noun "gavel" comes from Old Norse "gafl" meaning "gable."  Originally, gavels were likely house-shaped, resembling the gable of a roof.
