canister:/ˈkænɪstər/| n. |小罐，筒，容器|A metal container, typically cylindrical and airtight, used for storing dry goods, especially tea, coffee, or flour. |例句：The tea is kept in a canister. 茶叶保存在罐子里。

例句：He opened the canister of coffee beans. 他打开了咖啡豆罐。|近义词：container, tin, box, jar|反义词: None|词性:noun


canister:/ˈkænɪstər/| n. |（防毒面具的）滤毒罐|A sealed metal container holding a filter for removing poisonous gases from inhaled air. |例句：The soldiers wore gas masks with canisters. 士兵们戴着带有滤毒罐的防毒面具。

例句：The canister protected him from the fumes. 滤毒罐保护他免受烟雾的侵害。|近义词：filter|反义词: None|词性:noun
