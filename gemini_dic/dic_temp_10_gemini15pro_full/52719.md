dihedral:/daɪˈhiːdrəl/ /daɪˈhiːdɹəl/| adj. n.|二面的，二面角的；二面角|Relating to two intersecting planes or surfaces, especially two wings of an aircraft.|The dihedral angle of the wings helps to stabilize the aircraft in flight. 机翼的二面角有助于飞机在飞行中保持稳定。|近义词:bilateral, two-sided|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective, noun

dihedral:/daɪˈhiːdrəl/ /daɪˈhiːdɹəl/| n.|二面角|The angle between two intersecting planes or surfaces.|The dihedral angle between the wings is crucial for stability.  机翼之间的二面角对于稳定性至关重要。|近义词:angle of inclination|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
