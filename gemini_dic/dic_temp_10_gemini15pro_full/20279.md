biryanis:/bɪrˈjɑːniz/
noun
印度香饭；抓饭
A mixed rice dish, especially one containing meat, vegetables, and spices, originating from the Indian subcontinent.
We ordered chicken biryanis for dinner. 我们晚餐点了鸡肉抓饭。

The restaurant is famous for its delicious biryanis. 这家餐厅以其美味的印度香饭而闻名。

synonym: pilaf, pulao
antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific dish)
