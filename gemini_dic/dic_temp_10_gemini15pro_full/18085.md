behaving:/bɪˈheɪvɪŋ/
|v.|表现；行为；举止；（机器等）运转；（事物）作出反应；|acting or conducting oneself in a specified way, especially toward others.  Also, functioning or reacting in a certain manner.|The children were behaving well at the party.  孩子们在派对上表现良好。|Synonyms: acting, conducting, comporting, demeaning |Antonyms: misbehaving, acting up|verb (present participle)
behaving:/bɪˈheɪvɪŋ/
|adj.|表现好的；乖的|Used to describe someone who is acting in a good or proper manner.|The children are being very behaving today. 孩子们今天表现得很好。|Synonyms: well-behaved, good, obedient|Antonyms: misbehaving, naughty, disobedient|adjective
