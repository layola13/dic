extension:/ɪkˈstenʃən/| n.|延伸；扩展；延期；电话分机|An extension is a part that is added to something to make it longer or bigger, or the act of adding such a part.  It can also refer to an extra period of time allowed for something or a telephone line connected to a main line in a building.|The farmer built an extension to his barn. 农民扩建了他的谷仓。|synonyms: expansion, enlargement, prolongation, add-on|antonyms: reduction, shortening, contraction|noun

extension:/ɪkˈstenʃən/| n.|扩大；推广；应用；普及|The act of making something available to more people or spreading it to a wider area.|The extension of credit to small businesses is essential for economic growth.  向小企业提供信贷对经济增长至关重要。|synonyms: expansion, spread, proliferation|antonyms: restriction, limitation, reduction|noun

extension:/ɪkˈstenʃən/| n.|（大学的）推广部；推广课程|A department of a university that provides educational opportunities to people who are not full-time students.|He took an accounting course through the university's extension program. 他通过大学的推广课程学习了会计。|synonyms: outreach, continuing education|antonyms: N/A|noun
