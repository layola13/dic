make-work:/ˈmeɪkˌwɜːrk/| adj. n.|虚假的；忙碌而无用的（工作）；为了给人工作而安排的工作|Work that is created to keep people busy but has little real value or purpose.|The government was accused of creating make-work schemes to reduce unemployment. 政府被指责为了减少失业而制造一些虚假的岗位。|近义词：busywork, unnecessary work, pointless work|反义词：productive work, meaningful work|adjective, noun
