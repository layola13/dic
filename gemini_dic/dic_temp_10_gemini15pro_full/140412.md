ojibwe:/oʊˈdʒɪbweɪ/ |n.|奥吉布瓦人; 奥吉布瓦语|A member of a Native American people of the Great Lakes region. Also, the Algonquian language of the Ojibwe. |The Ojibwe traditionally lived by hunting and fishing.|近义词:Anishinaabe, Chippewa|反义词: N/A|名词

ojibwes:/oʊˈdʒɪbweɪz/ |n.|奥吉布瓦人（复数）|Plural form of Ojibwe, referring to multiple members of the Ojibwe people.|The Ojibwes have a rich cultural heritage.|近义词:Anishinaabeg, Chippewas|反义词: N/A|名词

词源: From Ojibwe self-designation.  Related to other Algonquian languages.
