hybrids:/ˈhaɪbrɪdz/ | n. | 混合物，杂交品种 |Something that is a mixture of two very different things.|Electric cars are hybrids between regular cars and electric vehicles. 电动汽车是普通汽车和电动汽车的混合体。| The mule is a hybrid of a horse and a donkey. 骡子是马和驴的杂交品种。 | synonyms: mixtures, combinations, blends, crosses | antonyms: purebreds, thoroughbreds | noun
