non-ionizing:/ˌnɒnˈaɪənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 非电离的 | Not having enough energy to ionize atoms or molecules; unable to cause ionization. |  Exposure to non-ionizing radiation, such as from radio waves, is considered generally safe. 暴露于非电离辐射（例如来自无线电波）通常被认为是安全的。| near synonyms: non-ionising, low-energy | antonyms: ionizing, high-energy| adjective
