monoclinous: /ˌmɒnəˈklaɪnəs/ /ˌmɑːnəˈklaɪnəs/
| adj. |雌雄同株的，雌雄同花的| Having both stamens and pistils in the same flower; hermaphrodite.  A monoclinous flower contains both male and female reproductive structures. |  The hibiscus flower is monoclinous, containing both male and female parts within a single bloom.  木槿花是雌雄同株的，单朵花中同时含有雄性和雌性部分。

Many common garden plants are monoclinous, simplifying pollination. 许多常见的园林植物都是雌雄同花的，简化了授粉过程。 | 近义词: hermaphrodite, bisexual, perfect (flower) | 反义词: diclinous, unisexual | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *monos* "single, alone" + *kline* "bed"  源自希腊语 *monos* “单个，单独” + *kline* “床”
