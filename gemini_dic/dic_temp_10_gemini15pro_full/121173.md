misdiet:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪət/
|n.|不良饮食;不当饮食|An unhealthy diet.  A diet lacking proper nutrients or containing excessive amounts of unhealthy substances.|Her misdiet of junk food and sugary drinks led to health problems.|近义词:poor diet, unhealthy diet, unbalanced diet|反义词:healthy diet, balanced diet|词性:名词
Etymology: mis- (badly, wrongly) + diet (food and drink regularly consumed)
