non-fortuitous:/ˌnɒn fɔːrˈtjuːɪtəs/
adjective
非偶然的；非意外的；故意的
Not occurring by chance; deliberate or planned.
例句：The non-fortuitous meeting between the two rivals sparked rumors of a secret deal.  两个竞争对手之间并非偶然的会面引发了秘密交易的传闻。

例句：Her choice of words was clearly non-fortuitous and designed to provoke a reaction. 她措辞的选择显然是故意的，旨在激起反应。

近义词：deliberate, intentional, planned, purposeful
反义词：fortuitous, accidental, chance, random
词性: adjective
