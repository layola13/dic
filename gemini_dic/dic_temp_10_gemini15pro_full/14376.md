autopsychography:/ˌɔːtəʊsaɪˈkɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 自动心理书写; 灵应书写 |  A form of writing that purports to be produced automatically by the unconscious or through the agency of spirits. It is often associated with Surrealism and automatic writing. |  The artist claimed the piece was an example of autopsychography, dictated by his subconscious. 这位艺术家声称这幅作品是自动心理书写的一个例子，由他的潜意识支配。| Synonym: automatic writing, psychography | Antonym:  (No direct antonym; perhaps "conscious writing") | noun
