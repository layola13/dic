energy:/ˈɛnərdʒi/ (US), /ˈɛnədʒi/ (UK)| n.|能量，精力，活力|The capacity to do work or produce heat.  It can exist in various forms, such as potential, kinetic, thermal, electrical, chemical, nuclear, or other various forms.|The sun's energy is essential for life on Earth. 太阳的能量对地球上的生命至关重要。

|近义词：power, force, vigor, strength, dynamism|反义词：lethargy, weakness, inactivity, lassitude|词性：noun
