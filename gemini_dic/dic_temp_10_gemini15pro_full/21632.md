blurrednesses:/ˈblɜːrdnɪsɪz/ /ˈblɜːrdnəsɪz/
noun
模糊不清，模糊状态（复数）
The state or quality of being blurred; lack of clarity or distinctness.
The photo suffered from several blurrednesses, making it difficult to identify the subjects.  照片中有多处模糊，难以辨认拍摄对象。
The blurrednesses of the memories made it hard to recall the details. 记忆的模糊使得难以回忆起细节。
近义词: blurriness, indistinctness, haziness, fuzziness
反义词: clarity, sharpness, distinctness
词性: noun
