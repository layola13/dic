overslowness:/ˈoʊvərˌsloʊnɪs/| n.|过度缓慢，迟缓|A state of being excessively slow; extreme slowness.  This can refer to physical movement, mental processes, or the progress of something.|The overslowness of the internet connection made it impossible to stream the video.  网络连接过度缓慢，导致无法播放视频。|Synonyms: sluggishness, tardiness, dilatoriness|Antonyms: speed, rapidity, swiftness|noun
