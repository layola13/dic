discontentedness:/dɪskənˈtɛntɪdnəs/
n.
不满, 不满足
The state of being discontented; lack of contentment; dissatisfaction.
Her discontentedness with her job led her to resign. 她对工作的`不满`导致她辞职。
近义词: dissatisfaction, unhappiness, discontent, malcontentment
反义词: contentment, satisfaction, happiness
词性: 名词

discontent:/dɪskənˈtɛnt/
n.
不满
Lack of contentment; dissatisfaction.
There is widespread discontent among the workers. 工人们普遍`不满`。
adj.
不满的
Not content; dissatisfied.
He seemed discontent with the arrangements. 他似乎对安排`不满`。
近义词: dissatisfaction, unhappiness, discontentedness, malcontentment (n.); dissatisfied, unhappy, malcontent (adj.)
反义词: contentment, satisfaction, happiness (n.); content, satisfied, happy (adj.)
词性: 名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + content (satisfied) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  The root "content" ultimately comes from Latin "contentus," past participle of "continere" (to contain, hold together).
