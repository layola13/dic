goose-stepping:/ˈɡuːsˌstɛpɪŋ/
noun
正步走，鹅步
A stiff-legged marching style, with the legs raised high and straight with each step.  Originates from Prussian military drills.
The soldiers were goose-stepping across the parade ground. 士兵们在阅兵场上正步走。
近义词:marching, parading
反义词:strolling, ambling
n.


goose-step:/ˈɡuːsˌstɛp/
verb
正步走
To march in a stiff-legged style, with the legs raised high and straight.
The troops goose-stepped past the reviewing stand.  部队正步走过检阅台。
近义词:march, parade
反义词:stroll, amble
v.
