microservices:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɜːrvɪsɪz/| n. |微服务|An architectural style that structures an application as a collection of loosely coupled services, which implement business capabilities.|Netflix uses microservices to handle millions of subscribers and streaming requests.  Netflix使用微服务来处理数百万的订阅者和流媒体请求。|近义词：SOA (Service-Oriented Architecture)|反义词：Monolithic architecture|名词
