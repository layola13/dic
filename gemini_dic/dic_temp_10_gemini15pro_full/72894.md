ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *folewend, from Old English *folgend, present participle of folgian (“to follow”), from Proto-Germanic *fulgijaną (“to follow”), from Proto-Indo-European *pḷek- (“to plait, twist”). Cognate with West Frisian folgjend (“following”), Dutch volgend (“following”), German folgend (“following”).
