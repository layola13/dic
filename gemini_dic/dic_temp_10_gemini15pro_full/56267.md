dogfight: /ˈdɒɡfaɪt/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡfaɪt/ (US) | n. |  空战，混战，激烈的争斗 | A close-range aerial combat between fighter aircraft.  It can also refer to any fierce struggle or competition. |  The two planes engaged in a fierce dogfight high above the clouds. 两架飞机在云层上方进行了一场激烈的空战。|  The election turned into a real dogfight between the two candidates.  选举变成了两位候选人之间的一场真正的混战。 | synonyms: air battle, aerial combat, brawl, scuffle, struggle | antonyms: truce, peace, agreement | noun
