edfu:/ˈɛdfuː/ | n. |埃德富 (埃及城市)| Edfu is a city in Upper Egypt, located on the west bank of the Nile River. It is known for its well-preserved Ptolemaic temple dedicated to the god Horus. |  We visited Edfu during our trip to Egypt. 我们在埃及之旅期间参观了埃德富。|  The Temple of Edfu is one of the best-preserved ancient temples in Egypt. 埃德富神庙是埃及保存最完好的古代神庙之一。 | synonyms: none (it's a proper noun) | antonyms: none (it's a proper noun) | noun
