braggest:/ˈbræɡɪst/ | adj. (superlative of 'bragging') | 最爱吹嘘的；最自夸的 |  Superlative adjective form of "brag," meaning to boast or speak with excessive pride about oneself or one's achievements. It describes someone who brags more than anyone else in a particular group. | He's the braggest person I've ever met; he can't stop talking about himself. 他是我见过最爱吹嘘的人；他不停地谈论自己。|  She's the braggest kid in the class, always boasting about her test scores. 她是班上最爱吹牛的孩子，总是吹嘘她的考试成绩。 | Near synonyms: most boastful, most arrogant, most conceited | Antonyms: most humble, most modest | Adjective
