lullabied:/ˈlʌləbaɪd/ | v. | 哄睡；用催眠曲使入睡 |  soothed or calmed as if by a lullaby.  The baby was lullabied to sleep by his mother's gentle singing. 婴儿被妈妈温柔的歌声哄睡了。 | 近义词：soothed, calmed, quieted | 反义词：agitated, disturbed, roused | 词性: 动词

词源:  From *lullaby* + *-ed*.  *Lullaby* itself is likely of imitative origin, echoing the sounds used to soothe a baby.
