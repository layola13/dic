guidebook:/ˈɡaɪdˌbʊk/| n.|指南；手册；旅行指南|A book that gives information about a place, especially for tourists.|例句：I bought a guidebook before my trip to Italy. 我在去意大利旅行之前买了一本旅行指南。

例句：The guidebook was very helpful in finding good restaurants. 这本指南在寻找好的餐馆方面非常有用。|近义词：handbook, manual, directory|反义词: None|词性: noun
