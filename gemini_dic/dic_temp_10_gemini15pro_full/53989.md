discretized:/dɪˈskriːtaɪzd/| adj.|离散化的， дискретизированный|Converted from a continuous variable or data set to a discrete one, often involving the division into intervals or categories.  The process involves transforming continuous data into a discrete format.|例句：The continuous signal was discretized for digital processing. 连续信号被离散化以便进行数字处理。

例句：The researchers discretized the age variable into five-year intervals for their analysis. 研究人员将年龄变量离散化为五年间隔进行分析。|近义词：quantized, digitized, sampled|反义词：continuous, analog|词性：形容词
