martians:/ˈmɑːrʃənz/| n.|火星人|Inhabitants of Mars, either fictional or hypothetical.  A being from Mars. |例句：The martians in the movie were depicted as friendly and intelligent.  电影中的火星人被描绘成友好而聪明的。

例句：Scientists are searching for signs of life, including martians. 科学家们正在寻找生命的迹象，包括火星人。|近义词：extraterrestrials, aliens|反义词:earthlings, humans|词性:noun

词源: From Mars + -ian (suffix forming adjectives and nouns relating to places). Ultimately from Latin Mars (the Roman god of war, whose name was given to the planet).
