bittily:/ˈbɪtɪli/
| adv.|尖刻地，讽刺地，恶意地|In a bitter, resentful, or sarcastic manner.  Characterized by sharpness, acrimony, or sarcasm.|She spoke bittily about her ex-husband. 她尖刻地谈论她的前夫。|近义词：acrimoniously, caustically, sarcastically, spitefully|反义词：kindly, sweetly, gently|词性:副词
词源:From "bitter" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "biter."
