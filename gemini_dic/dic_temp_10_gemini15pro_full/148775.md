overurging: /ˌoʊvərˈɜːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective|过度催促的，过分敦促的|Characterized by excessive urging or pressure; overly insistent.|He apologized for his overurging manner.|近义词：insistent, persistent, pressing, importunate|反义词：patient, lenient, laid-back|词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "urge" (verb meaning "to try earnestly or persistently to persuade someone to do something")
