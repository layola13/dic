exoatmospheric:/ˌɛksoʊˌætməsˈfɪrɪk/
adjective
大气层外的；在大气层外的
Existing or occurring outside the Earth's atmosphere.
例句：
The exoatmospheric missile interception test was successful.
大气层外导弹拦截试验成功。

The satellite carries an exoatmospheric kill vehicle.
这颗卫星携带一个大气层外杀伤拦截器。

近义词：extraterrestrial, in space
反义词：atmospheric, subatmospheric
词性：adjective
