nasalization:/ˌneɪzələˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌneɪzəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |鼻音化；鼻音化作用|The process of making a sound nasal, typically by lowering the soft palate so that air escapes through the nose.|The nasalization of vowels is a common feature in French. 元音鼻音化是法语中的一个常见特征。|近义词:nasality|反义词:oralization|词性:noun
