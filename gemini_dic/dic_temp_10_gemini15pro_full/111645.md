located:/loʊˈkeɪtɪd/ /ˈləʊkeɪtɪd/
adjective
位于;坐落在
situated in a particular place.
The office is located in the city center.  办公室位于市中心。
The school is located near the park. 学校位于公园附近。
近义词:situated, positioned, based
反义词:unlocated, displaced
词性:adjective
