dizzy:/ˈdɪzi/| adj.|头晕的，眩晕的；使人头晕的；昏乱的；愚蠢的| feeling as if everything is turning around, and that you are going to fall|例句：The height made me feel dizzy. 高度让我感到头晕。|例句：I felt dizzy after spinning around too many times.  我转圈转太多次后感到头晕。|近义词：giddy, lightheaded, faint|反义词:steady, clearheaded|词性:形容词

dizzy:/ˈdɪzi/| v.|使头晕眼花；使混乱；使茫然|to make someone feel dizzy|例句：The fast pace of city life can dizzy you. 城市生活的快节奏会让你晕头转向。|例句：The sheer complexity of the issue dizzied me.  这个问题的复杂性让我头晕目眩。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, disorient|反义词:orient, clarify|词性:动词
