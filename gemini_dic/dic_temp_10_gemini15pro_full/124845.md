mucky:/ˈmʌki/ | adj. | 肮脏的，污秽的 | Covered with or containing muck; dirty. |  The children came in from the garden, all mucky from playing in the mud. 孩子们从花园里进来，身上都沾满了泥巴，脏兮兮的。| 近义词:dirty, filthy, muddy, grimy | 反义词:clean, spotless | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From muck + -y.  Muck itself comes from Old Norse "myki," meaning manure or dung.
