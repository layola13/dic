etiology's:/ˌiːtiˈɑːlədʒiz/
noun
病因学（的）
The study of the causes or origins of diseases or abnormal conditions.  It investigates all the factors that may be involved in the development of a disease, including genetic predisposition, environmental influences, and lifestyle choices.
The etiology of the disease is still unknown.  这种疾病的病因仍然未知。
Synonyms: cause, origin, reason, root, source
Antonyms: effect, result, outcome, consequence
