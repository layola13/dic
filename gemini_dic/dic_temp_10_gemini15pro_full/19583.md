bigfoots:/ˈbɪɡˌfʊts/ |n.|大脚怪；野人|A large hairy humanoid creature believed by some to inhabit forested regions of the United States and Canada.  It is often described as a type of ape or hominid.|Example 1:  Many people claim to have seen bigfoots in the Pacific Northwest. 许多人声称在太平洋西北地区见过大脚怪。

Example 2: The search for bigfoots continues to fascinate cryptozoologists. 对大脚怪的搜寻继续吸引着神秘动物学家。|近义词：Sasquatch, Yeti, Abominable Snowman|反义词:  N/A (Mythical creature)|词性:名词
