favorer:/ˈfeɪvərər/ | n. | 支持者，赞助者 | Someone who favors or supports someone or something. | He is a great favorer of the arts. 他是一位艺术的热心支持者。 | The king was a favorer of the new policy. 国王是这项新政策的支持者。 |  Synonyms: supporter, advocate, patron, backer | Antonyms: opponent, critic, detractor | noun

favorer:/ˈfeɪvərər/ | n. | 偏袒者，偏爱者 | Someone who shows favoritism or unfair preference to someone or something. |  She's a favorer of her youngest child. 她偏爱她最小的孩子。|  The teacher was accused of being a favorer. 这位老师被指责偏袒学生。 | Synonyms: partisan | Antonyms: impartial person | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *favoreor, from Latin *favorātor, from favor ("favor") + -or ("-er, one who"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʰewh₂- ("to enjoy").
