guru-ships:/ˈɡʊruːʃɪps/
n.
大师地位；导师身份；专家地位
The state or position of being a guru; expertise in a particular field.
Many young professionals seek guru-ships in their respective industries. 许多年轻的专业人士都在各自的行业中寻求成为专家。
Tech companies often cultivate guru-ships within their ranks to drive innovation. 科技公司经常在内部培养专家，以推动创新。
近义词: mentorships, leadership, expertise, mastery
反义词: apprenticeship, novice status, beginner status
词性: 名词
