outbitting:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtɪŋ/
verb
胜过，输出比特数超过
The process of exceeding or surpassing someone or something in the output of bits.  It often refers to a situation in cryptocurrency mining where one miner produces a higher hash rate or solves more complex cryptographic problems than another, leading to a greater reward.
Example 1:  The new mining rig was outbitting all the others in the farm, generating significantly more Bitcoin. 新的矿机胜过了矿场里所有其他的矿机，产生了更多的比特币。
Example 2:  His superior hardware allowed him to outbit the competition and earn a larger share of the block reward.  他优越的硬件使他能够胜过竞争对手，并获得更大的区块奖励份额。
Near Synonyms:  outperforming, surpassing, exceeding
Antonyms: underperforming, lagging, trailing
word type: verb
