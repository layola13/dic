misnomer:/ˌmɪsˈnoʊmər/ | n. | 误称；用词不当 | An incorrect or inappropriate name or designation. |  Calling a whale a "fish" is a misnomer. 把鲸鱼叫做“鱼”是用词不当。|  misnaming, inaccurate designation | correct name, appropriate term | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mesnomer "to misname," from mes- "wrongly" (from Latin minus "less") + nomer "to name" (from Latin nominare "to name").
