parole:/pəˈroʊl/ | n. | 假释；誓言；语言；（尤指官方的）声明；（战俘的）释放宣言 |  the release of a prisoner temporarily (for a special purpose) or permanently before the completion of a sentence, on the promise of good behavior. |  He was released on parole after serving two years of his sentence. 他服刑两年后获得假释。 | synonyms: probation, release, freedom | antonyms: imprisonment, incarceration, detention | noun

parole:/pəˈroʊl/ | v. |  (正式)准许（某人）假释；(古)用言语表达 | to grant parole to (someone)./ to express in words. |  The board decided to parole the prisoner. 委员会决定假释这名囚犯。| synonyms: liberate, free, release | antonyms: imprison, confine, detain | verb
