hyperintellectualness:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlnəs/
n.
过度理智；过分理性
Excessive intellectualism or focus on intellectual pursuits.
His hyperintellectualness made it difficult for him to connect with people on an emotional level. 他过度的理性使他难以与人们在情感层面上产生联系。

近义词: intellectualism, cerebrality, highbrowism
反义词: anti-intellectualism, philistinism
