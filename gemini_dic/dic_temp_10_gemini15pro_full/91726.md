hyperdeifying:/ˌhaɪpərˈdiːɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
过分神化；极度神化
The act of treating someone or something as more divine than is warranted, elevating them to an excessive degree of worship or reverence.
He was hyperdeifying his mentor, treating his every word as gospel truth. 他过分神化了他的导师，把他的每一句话都奉为真理。
The cult leader encouraged his followers to hyperdeify him, claiming he was a direct messenger of God.  邪教头目鼓励他的追随者过分神化他，声称他是上帝的直接使者。

近义词: idolizing, worshipping, exalting, glorifying
反义词: denigrating, belittling, disparaging, vilifying
词性: verb (present participle)


词源:
"hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér) meaning "over, above, beyond" + "deify" (from Latin deus "god" + -fy)
