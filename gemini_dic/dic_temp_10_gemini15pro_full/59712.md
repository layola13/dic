eclectically:/ɪˈklɛktɪkli/
adverb
兼收并蓄地，博采众长地
in a way that derives ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources.
She furnished her apartment eclectically, with pieces from various eras and cultures. 她用不同时代和文化的物品兼收并蓄地布置了她的公寓。
Synonym: diversely, broadly, comprehensively
Antonym: narrowly, homogeneously, restrictively
词性: 副词
