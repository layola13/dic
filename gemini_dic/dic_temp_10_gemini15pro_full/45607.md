curried:/ˈkɜːrid/ /ˈkʌrid/| adj.|咖喱味的，加了咖喱的；(函数式编程)柯里化的|A dish flavored with curry.  A function that has been curried.|例句：We had a delicious curried chicken for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了美味的咖喱鸡。

例句：The curried function takes one argument at a time.  这个柯里化的函数一次接受一个参数。|近义词：spiced, seasoned (for food); partial (for functions)|反义词:bland, unseasoned (for food)|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the past participle of *curry*, from Tamil *kari* meaning "sauce, relish for rice". The functional programming sense comes from Haskell Brooks Curry, a logician.  来源于动词curry的过去分词形式，curry源于泰米尔语 *kari*，意思是“酱汁，米饭的佐料”。函数式编程的含义来源于逻辑学家哈斯凯尔·布鲁克斯·柯里。
