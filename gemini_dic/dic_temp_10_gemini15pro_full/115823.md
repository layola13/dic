marketability:/ˌmɑːrkɪtəˈbɪləti/ /ˌmɑːrkɪdəˈbɪlədi/| n.|适销性；市场性|The ease with which something can be sold.  The ability of a product or service to be sold in a market.|例句：The marketability of the product was enhanced by its new packaging.  该产品的适销性因其新包装而增强。|近义词：salability, merchantability, vendability|反义词：unmarketability|词性:noun
