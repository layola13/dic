factious:/ˈfækʃəs/ | adj. | 搞派系的，好捣乱的 | Given to faction; dissentious. Tending to promote internal dissension or cause disunity. |  The factious group within the political party threatened to split off and form their own.  这个政党内部搞派系的团体威胁要分裂出去，另立门户。

The factious nature of the committee made it difficult to reach a consensus. 委员会好搞内讧的性质使得很难达成共识。 | 近义词: divisive, dissentious, seditious, rebellious, mutinous | 反义词: unifying, harmonious, cooperative, collaborative | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *factiōsus* "active, busy," from *factiō* "a doing, making, performing; a party, faction," from *factus*, past participle of *facere* "to do, make."
