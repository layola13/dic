deaden:/ˈdɛdn/ /ˈdiːdn/| v.|使减弱，使降低，使失去活力|To make something less strong, less loud, or less painful.|The thick curtains deadened the sound from the street. 厚厚的窗帘减弱了街上的声音。|The doctor gave him some medicine to deaden the pain. 医生给他开了一些药来减轻疼痛。|近义词：dull, numb, mute, soften, weaken|反义词：sharpen, intensify, heighten, amplify|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *dēadian, from dēad "dead."
