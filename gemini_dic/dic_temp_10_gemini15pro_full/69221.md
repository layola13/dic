fearlessly:/ˈfɪərləsli/| adv.|无畏地，勇敢地|Without fear; bravely.|She fearlessly confronted the bully. 她勇敢地面对了恶霸。|例句：He fearlessly jumped into the river to save the drowning child. 他毫不畏惧地跳进河里救溺水的孩子。|近义词：bravely, courageously, boldly, daringly|反义词：fearfully, timidly, cowardly|词性：副词
Etymology: From "fearless" + "-ly."  "Fearless" comes from Old English "fǣrlēas," meaning "without fear," from "fǣr" (fear) + "-lēas" (without).
