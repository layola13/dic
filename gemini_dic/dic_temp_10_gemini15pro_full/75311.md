frontline:/ˈfrʌntlaɪn/ | n. adj. | 前线，第一线 |The area or position closest to the fighting in a war; the most advanced or active position in a field or sphere of activity. |  The soldiers on the frontline are bravely defending their country.  前线的士兵们正在英勇地保卫他们的国家。| synonyms: forefront, vanguard, cutting edge | antonyms: rear, back, support | noun

frontline:/ˈfrʌntlaɪn/ | adj. |  前线的，第一线的 | Involved in direct provision of services or care, especially in a medical setting. | Frontline workers, like doctors and nurses, are essential during a pandemic.  像医生和护士这样的前线工作人员在大流行期间至关重要。 | synonyms: leading, primary, principal | antonyms: secondary, supporting, auxiliary | adjective
