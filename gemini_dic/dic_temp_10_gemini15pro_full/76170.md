fusionist:/ˈfjuːʒənɪst/| n.|融合主义者；融合论者|A person who advocates or practices fusion, especially in politics, religion, or the arts.|The fusionist sought to combine elements of classical and modern music in his compositions.  这位融合主义者试图在他的作品中融合古典和现代音乐的元素。|Synonyms: synthesist, integrator, combiner|Antonyms: purist, separatist|noun

fusionist:/ˈfjuːʒənɪst/| adj.|融合的；融合主义的|Relating to or characterized by fusion, especially in politics, religion, or the arts.|The fusionist movement aimed to bridge the gap between different artistic styles.  融合主义运动旨在弥合不同艺术风格之间的差距。|Synonyms: synthetic, integrated, combined|Antonyms: purist, separate|adjective
