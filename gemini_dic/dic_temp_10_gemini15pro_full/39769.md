consolidators:/kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtərz/| n.|合并者，整合者|A person or company that consolidates something, especially businesses or debts.|The airline consolidators offer cheaper fares.|Synonyms: integrator, unifier, merger, combiner|Antonyms: divider, separator|Noun

consolidate:/kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪt/| v.|巩固，加强，合并|To combine or unite into one; to make stronger or more secure.|The two companies consolidated their resources to form a stronger entity.  两家公司合并资源，组成了一个更强大的实体。|Synonyms: strengthen, reinforce, combine, merge|Antonyms: weaken, divide, separate|Verb
