hirundine:/ˈhaɪrənˌdaɪn/| adj. n.|燕子的，燕子|Relating to or characteristic of swallows. A swallow.|例句：The hirundine wings flitted across the sky. 燕子般的翅膀掠过天空。|例句：The barn was full of hirundines. 谷仓里满是燕子。|近义词：swallow-like|反义词:|词性：adjective, noun
词源：From Latin *hirundininus, from hirundo "swallow."
