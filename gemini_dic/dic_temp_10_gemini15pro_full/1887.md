acrotomous: /əˈkrɒtəməs/
adj.
顶生的，顶端的；截顶的
Growing from the apex or top; having the apex cut off.
The acrotomous branching pattern of the plant was unique.  该植物顶生的分枝模式非常独特。
The gardener made an acrotomous cut on the tree. 园丁在树上做了一个截顶的切口。
近义词: apical, terminal
反义词: basal
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek akros (topmost, outermost) + -tomous (cutting).

USAGE->
The acrotomous growth of the plant allowed it to reach sunlight more effectively. =  这种植物的顶端生长使其能够更有效地获取阳光。

The fungus exhibited an acrotomous fruiting body. =  这种真菌呈现出一个顶生的子实体。


PHRASE->
acrotomous branching = 顶端分枝
acrotomous growth = 顶端生长
acrotomous inflorescence = 顶生花序
acrotomous leaf = 顶生叶
acrotomous stem = 顶生茎
