covet:/ˈkʌvɪt/| v.|渴望，贪求|To yearn for or desire something, especially something belonging to another.|She coveted her neighbor's beautiful garden. 她渴望拥有邻居的美丽花园。|Synonyms: desire, crave, yearn for, envy|Antonyms: disdain, despise|verb

covet:/ˈkʌvɪt/| n.|渴望，贪求（较少用作名词）|A strong desire to possess something, typically something that belongs to someone else.|His covet for power led him down a dark path. 他对权力的贪婪让他走上了一条黑暗的道路。|Synonyms: yearning, craving, desire, envy|Antonyms: indifference, apathy|noun
