manliness:/ˈmænlɪnəs/| n. |男子气概，阳刚之气|The quality of being manly; masculine behaviour or appearance.  Possessing qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength, courage, and independence.|He displayed great manliness in the face of danger. 他在危险面前展现了极大的男子气概。|synonyms: masculinity, virility, bravado, courage, boldness|antonyms: effeminacy, weakness, timidity|noun
