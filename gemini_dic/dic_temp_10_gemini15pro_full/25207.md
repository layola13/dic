buddying-up:/ˈbʌdiɪŋ ʌp/| phrasal verb |结伴，成为伙伴，建立友好关系 |The act of becoming friends with someone, often for a specific purpose such as support or guidance. |Example 1:  The new students were encouraged to buddy up with older students to help them adjust to college life. 新生被鼓励与老生结伴，以帮助他们适应大学生活。

Example 2:  They buddied up during the training program and have remained close friends ever since. 他们在培训项目中结成了伙伴，从此以后一直是亲密的朋友。|Synonyms: pairing up, teaming up, partnering up, joining forces |Antonyms: separating, parting ways, going solo |verb phrase
