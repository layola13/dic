nonnutritive:/ˌnɒnˈnjuːtrətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈnuːtrətɪv/| adj.|非营养的，不提供营养的|Not providing nourishment or nutrients.|Artificial sweeteners are nonnutritive. 人造甜味剂是非营养的。|Many processed foods contain nonnutritive additives. 许多加工食品含有非营养添加剂。|近义词：non-nourishing, innutritious|反义词：nutritive, nourishing|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + nutritive (adjective meaning "providing nourishment") from Latin nutritivus, from nutrīre "to nourish."
