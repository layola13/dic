microtubular:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈtjuːbjʊlər/ |adj.|微管的；微管状的|Relating to or consisting of microtubules, which are microscopic hollow tubes made of protein that are part of a cell's cytoskeleton and involved in various cellular processes. |Microtubular proteins play a crucial role in cell division. 微管蛋白在细胞分裂中起着至关重要的作用。|The microtubular structure of cilia and flagella enables their movement.  纤毛和鞭毛的微管结构使其能够运动。|近义词:tubular|反义词: None|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + tubule + -ar. Micro- means "small," tubule refers to a small tube, and -ar is an adjective-forming suffix.
