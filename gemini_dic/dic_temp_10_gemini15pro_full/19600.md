bight:/baɪt/
noun
弯曲; 海湾;绳圈
a curve or bend in a shoreline, river, or other geographical feature.  A loop of rope.
The ship anchored in the bight of the bay. 这艘船停泊在海湾的弯曲处。
The sailor made a bight in the rope. 水手在绳子上打了一个绳圈。
Synonyms: bend, curve, loop, bay, cove, inlet
Antonyms: straight, line

bights:/baɪts/
noun
弯曲; 海湾;绳圈 (bight的复数形式)
curves or bends in a shoreline, river, or other geographical features. Loops of rope.
The coastline is characterized by numerous bights and inlets. 这条海岸线的特点是有许多海湾和水湾。
The sailor tied several bights in the rope. 水手在绳子上打了几个绳圈。
Synonyms: bends, curves, loops, bays, coves, inlets
Antonyms: straights, lines

Etymology: From Old English *bȳht, from Proto-Germanic *buhtą (“bend, corner, angle”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewǵ- (“to bend, curve”). Cognate with Old High German buht (“bend, bay, corner”), Old Norse búð (“booth, shop”).
