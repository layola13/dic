compromise:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/ (US)| n. v. |妥协，折衷；妥协方案；危害，损害|An agreement or a settlement of a dispute that is reached by each side making concessions.|例句：They reached a compromise over the terms of the contract. 他们在合同条款上达成了妥协。|近义词：agreement, settlement, concession, bargain|反义词：intransigence, disagreement, refusal|noun
