closedai:/kloʊzd eɪˈaɪ/ | n. | 封闭式人工智能 |  A hypothetical type of artificial intelligence system that operates within a restricted environment and with limited access to external information. Its knowledge and capabilities are predetermined and confined to the specific domain it was designed for.  | Example: A closedai chatbot trained on a specific product manual might be able to answer customer questions about that product, but it wouldn't be able to discuss current events or other unrelated topics.  封闭式人工智能聊天机器人经过特定产品手册的训练后，或许能够回答客户关于该产品的问题，但无法讨论时事或其他无关主题。 | Example:  Unlike open AI models, a closedai system cannot learn from new data or adapt to changing circumstances.  与开放式人工智能模型不同，封闭式人工智能系统无法从新数据中学习或适应变化的环境。 | 近义词: restricted AI, domain-specific AI | 反义词: open AI, general AI | 词性: noun
