chlamydiae:/kləˈmɪdiˌiː/ | n. | 衣原体 |  A group of gram-negative bacteria that are obligate intracellular parasites and cause various diseases in humans and animals, including chlamydia, a sexually transmitted infection. | Chlamydiae are responsible for a wide range of infections. 衣原体是多种感染的病原体。| The doctor tested her for chlamydiae. 医生对她进行了衣原体检测。| 近义词: N/A (Specific bacterial genus) | 反义词: N/A | noun
