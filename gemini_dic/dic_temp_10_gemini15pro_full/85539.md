hatcher:/ˈhætʃər/| n.|孵化器; 孵化者; 策划者|A device or place for hatching eggs; a person who hatches a plot or scheme. |The salmon hatcher released thousands of fry into the river.  鲑鱼孵化场向河里放生了数千条鱼苗。|近义词:incubator, breeder, plotter, schemer|反义词: N/A|noun

hatches:/ˈhætʃɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present hatches, present participle hatching, simple past and past participle hatched) |孵化; 策划; 舱口| (of an egg) open and produce a young animal; devise (a plot or scheme).|The eggs hatched after two weeks.  两周后，卵孵化了。|近义词:incubate, breed, devise, contrive|反义词: N/A|verb
