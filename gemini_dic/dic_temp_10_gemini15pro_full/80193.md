godlessness:/ˈɡɒdləsnəs/ /ˈɡɑːdləsnəs/| n. |无神论；不信神；不敬神|The state of being godless; lack of religious belief or reverence.|His godlessness shocked his devout parents. 他的无神论使他虔诚的父母感到震惊。|近义词：atheism, impiety, irreverence, ungodliness|反义词：piety, devoutness, religiousness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From godless + -ness.  Godless from Old English *godlēas (attested as its derivative godlēasnes "godlessness"), from god "god" + -lēas "-less".
