dualistically:/ˌduːəlˈɪstɪkli/
adv.|二元地，二元论地|In a way that emphasizes a contrast between two opposing elements or concepts.|He viewed the world dualistically, seeing everything in terms of good and evil. 他以二元论的视角看待世界，将一切事物都归结为善与恶。|
Synonym:dichotomously|
Antonym:holistically|
Part of Speech:adverb


dualism:/ˈduːəlɪzəm/
n.|二元论|The division of something conceptually into two opposed or contrasted aspects, or the state of being so divided.|Cartesian dualism separates mind and matter. 笛卡尔二元论将精神和物质分开。|
Synonym:dichotomy|
Antonym:monism|
Part of Speech:noun
