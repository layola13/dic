negate:/nɪˈɡeɪt/ | v. | 取消，使无效 |To cause (something) to have no effect. |例句：Alcohol negates the effects of the medicine. 酒精会抵消药物的作用。|近义词：cancel, invalidate, nullify, counteract|反义词：activate, enhance, reinforce, validate|词性:动词
