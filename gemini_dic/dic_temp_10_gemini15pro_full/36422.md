coky:/ˈkəʊki/ (UK) /ˈkoʊki/ (US)| adj.|自大的，骄傲的，过于自信的|Conceited or arrogant, especially in a bold or cheeky way.  Showing excessive self-confidence and pride, often in a playful or provocative manner.  Suggests a slightly mischievous or playful arrogance.|He strutted around with a cocky grin after winning the game.  他赢了比赛后趾高气扬地走来走去，脸上带着得意的笑容。|Synonyms: arrogant, conceited, swaggering, boastful, pompous, presumptuous|Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming, diffident, timid|adjective
