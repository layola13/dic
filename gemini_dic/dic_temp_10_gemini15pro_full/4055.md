aidite:/ˈeɪdaɪt/| n. |艾迪特（一种稀有矿物）|A rare mineral, a tetragonal dimorph of thorite.  It is a silicate of thorium. |Example 1: The geologist discovered a small deposit of aidite in the volcanic rock.  地质学家在火山岩中发现了一小块艾迪特矿床。

Example 2: Aidite is often found in association with other rare earth minerals. 艾迪特通常与其他稀土矿物伴生。|Synonym: thorite (dimorph)|Antonym: N/A|Noun
