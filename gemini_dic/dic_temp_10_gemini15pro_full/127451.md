nebule:/ˈnebjʊl/ (US) /ˈnɛbjʊl/ (UK)| n.|星云；云状物|A cloud of interstellar gas and dust.|例句：The Hubble telescope captured stunning images of the nebula. 哈勃望远镜捕捉到了令人惊叹的星云图像。

例句：A nebula is often a birthplace of stars. 星云通常是恒星的诞生地。

近义词：cloud, haze, mist|反义词: clarity, clearness|词性: noun
