copublishing:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ/
n.
联合出版
the act of publishing something together with another publisher
Example: The two companies agreed on copublishing the textbook.  这两家公司同意联合出版这本教科书。

Example: Copublishing allowed the smaller publisher to reach a wider audience. 联合出版使得这家小型出版社能够接触到更广泛的读者。
近义词:joint publication, collaborative publishing
反义词:sole publishing, independent publishing
词性:noun
