connected:/kəˈnɛktɪd/ | adj. | 连接的，关联的，有联系的 | joined, linked, or related | The two events are connected. 这两件事是相关的。 | related, linked, associated | unconnected, separate | adjective

connected:/kəˈnɛktɪd/ | adj. | (人)有权势的，有影响力的 | having power or influence | He's a well-connected businessman. 他是一位很有影响力的商人。 | influential, powerful | unconnected, powerless | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *connectere, from con- "together" + nectere "to bind."
