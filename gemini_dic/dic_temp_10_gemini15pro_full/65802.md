evidencing:/ˈɛvɪdənsɪŋ/| v.|证明；表明|Showing clearly; providing proof.|The data is evidencing a clear trend towards online shopping.  这些数据表明网上购物的趋势越来越明显。|Synonyms: demonstrating, showing, proving, indicating, manifesting|Antonyms: concealing, hiding, obscuring|Verb

evidencing:/ˈɛvɪdənsɪŋ/| adj.|表明的；证明的 |Serving as evidence; indicative.|The evidencing documents were submitted to the court.  证明文件已提交法院。|Synonyms: demonstrative, illustrative, probative|Antonyms: inconclusive, unproven|Adjective
