bowerbird: /ˈbaʊəˌbɜːrd/ | n. | 园丁鸟 | A bird of the family Ptilonorhynchidae, the males of which build elaborate bowers decorated with brightly coloured objects to attract females. | The bowerbird is known for its unique mating ritual.  园丁鸟以其独特的交配仪式而闻名。| The male bowerbird decorates its bower with flowers, berries, and even shiny pieces of plastic. 雄性园丁鸟用花朵、浆果，甚至闪亮的塑料碎片来装饰它的凉亭。 | synonyms: decorator bird | antonyms: N/A | noun
