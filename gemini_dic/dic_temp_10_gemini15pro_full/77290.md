gatecrashes:/ˈɡeɪtkræʃɪz/
| v. |擅自闯入，不请自来|To enter (a party or other gathering) without an invitation or ticket.|He gatecrashed the wedding reception. 他不请自来参加了婚礼招待会。|近义词：intrude, barge in, crash|反义词：be invited, be welcome|词性:动词

gatecrash:/ˈɡeɪtkræʃ/
| n. |擅自闯入，不速之客|The act of entering a party or other gathering without an invitation or ticket.|His gatecrash caused a scene. 他的擅自闯入引起了一场骚动。|近义词：intrusion|反义词：invitation|词性:名词


词源: gate + crash.  "Gate" refers to a physical gate or entrance, while "crash" implies entering forcefully or without permission.  The term originated in the early 20th century.
