overgeneralise:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪz/ /ˌəʊvəˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlʌɪz/| v. | 过度概括，过分概括 | To draw a conclusion or make a statement about a group or category based on too few examples or insufficient evidence. | He overgeneralized from his limited experience and assumed all women were bad drivers. 他根据自己有限的经验过度概括，认为所有女性都不擅长驾驶。

Children tend to overgeneralize grammatical rules when they are learning a language. 孩子们在学习语言时往往会过度概括语法规则。

| 近义词：stereotypize, generalize, simplify | 反义词: specify, particularize | 词性: 动词
