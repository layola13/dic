nonvicarious:/ˌnɒnvaɪˈkɛəriəs/
adjective
非替代性的，非代理的，亲身经历的
Not experienced or felt through the actions or feelings of another person; personally experienced.
She felt a nonvicarious joy at seeing her daughter graduate.  她亲眼看到女儿毕业，感到无比喜悦。
Synonyms: firsthand, direct, personal, immediate
Antonyms: vicarious, indirect, secondhand
词性: 形容词
