minimality:/ˌmɪnɪˈmælɪti/ | n. | 最小性，最低限度 |The principle or practice of using the smallest or fewest things possible; the state or quality of being minimal. |  The artist achieved a sense of minimality through the use of just three colors.  这位艺术家仅使用三种颜色就达到了极简主义的效果。|  minimalism, simplicity, conciseness |  maximality, excess, complexity | noun


minimal:/ˈmɪnɪməl/ | adj. | 最小的，最低限度的 |Smallest or least possible; negligible. | The damage to the car was minimal. 车辆的损坏很小。 | slightest, least, negligible | maximal, maximum, extensive | adjective
