cornishes:/ˈkɔːrnɪʃɪz/
n.
檐口，飞檐，（尤指糕点）烤成金黄色的顶部
The top edges of a pie crust, baked to a golden brown.  Also, a decorative molding along the top of a wall or building.
The cornishes of the pie were perfectly browned. 这个派的檐口烤得金黄完美。
The building's elaborate cornishes added to its grandeur.  这座建筑精美的飞檐增添了它的宏伟气势。
近义词:edges, crusts, moldings
反义词:base, bottom
