compensator:/ˈkɒmpənseɪtə(r) (UK), /ˈkɑːmpənseɪtər (US)| n. |补偿器；平衡器；校正装置|A device that compensates for something, typically an automatic device for correcting variations in a mechanical or electrical system.|The voltage compensator kept the output stable despite fluctuations in the input. 电压补偿器在输入波动的情况下保持输出稳定。|Synonyms: regulator, equalizer, counterbalance, corrector|Antonyms: amplifier, intensifier|noun

compensator:/ˈkɒmpənseɪtə(r) (UK), /ˈkɑːmpənseɪtər (US)| n. |补偿者；赔偿者|A person who compensates for something.|The company acted as a compensator for the victims of the faulty product.  该公司充当了缺陷产品受害者的赔偿者。|Synonyms: payer, indemnifier|Antonyms: offender, wrongdoer|noun
