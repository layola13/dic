carbonization:/ˌkɑːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |碳化，炭化|The process of converting organic matter into carbon or a carbon-containing residue through heating in the absence of air. |The carbonization of wood produces charcoal. 木材的碳化产生木炭。|The slow carbonization of plant matter over millions of years created coal.  数百万年来植物的缓慢碳化形成了煤。|synonyms: charring, coking|antonyms: decarbonization|noun
