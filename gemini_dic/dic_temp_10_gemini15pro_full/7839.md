antapoplectic:/ˌæntɪˌæpəˈplɛktɪk/ | adj. | 预防中风的，缓解中风的 | Good for preventing or relieving apoplexy (stroke).  Relating to a medicine or remedy for apoplexy. | Example:  The doctor prescribed an antapoplectic medication for the patient at risk of stroke. 医生给有中风风险的病人开了预防中风的药物。

Example:  Leech therapy, once considered an antapoplectic treatment, is rarely used today.  曾经被认为是中风治疗方法的蚂蟥疗法，现在已经很少使用了。 | 近义词: anti-apoplectic, stroke-preventing | 反义词:  N/A (no direct opposite for a preventative) | 词性: adjective
