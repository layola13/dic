nombril: /nɔ̃.bʁil/ (French pronunciation)| n. |肚脐，脐|The central point or middle of something; also the navel.  Specifically in French, it refers to the navel or belly button. |例句：She has a piercing in her nombril. 她肚脐上有一个穿孔。

例句：Le nombril du monde (figuratively) The center of the world (比喻) 世界中心|近义词：navel, belly button, umbilicus|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
