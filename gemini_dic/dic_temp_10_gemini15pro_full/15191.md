backbreaking:/ˈbækˌbreɪkɪŋ/| adj.| 令人筋疲力尽的；非常辛苦的|  (of manual labor) so arduous as to be liable to cause injury to the back.  |  Example: Harvesting sugarcane is backbreaking work.  收割甘蔗是一项非常辛苦的工作。

Example:  She had a backbreaking day at the office, dealing with a mountain of paperwork. 她办公室的一天非常辛苦，处理了一大堆文件。 |  Near Synonyms:  grueling, exhausting, strenuous, arduous, laborious  |  Antonyms:  easy, effortless, simple, light  |词性: adjective
