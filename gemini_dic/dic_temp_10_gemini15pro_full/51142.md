despoil:/dɪˈspɔɪl/
verb
掠夺，剥夺，破坏
to steal something valuable from a place or person; to make a place less attractive by damaging or destroying it
The invaders despoiled the city of its treasures. 侵略者掠夺了这座城市的宝藏。
The countryside has been despoiled by pollution. 乡村已经被污染破坏了。
synonyms: plunder, pillage, rob, loot, ransack, strip, ravage, devastate
antonyms: protect, preserve, conserve
v.
