chagrining:/tʃəˈɡraɪnɪŋ/| adj.|令人懊恼的，令人失望的|Causing annoyance or disappointment.|It was chagrining to discover that all her hard work had been for nothing. 发现她所有的努力都白费了，这真令人懊恼。|近义词：disappointing, frustrating, vexing, annoying|反义词：pleasing, satisfying, gratifying|词性:形容词
词源: From the French chagrin, meaning sadness, grief, or vexation. Ultimately from the Old North French chagreiner, meaning to grieve or sadden.
