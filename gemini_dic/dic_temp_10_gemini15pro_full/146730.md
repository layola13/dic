overhonors: /ˌoʊvərˈɑːnərz/ |v. | 过分地给予荣誉；过誉| To give someone too much honor or praise; to honor excessively. | He felt they overhonored him with the award, as he didn't think he deserved it.  他觉得他们授予他的奖项过誉了，因为他认为自己不配得到。| 近义词: overpraise, overestimate | 反义词: underestimate, underappreciate |词性:动词

overhonors: /ˌoʊvərˈɑːnərz/ |n. | 过分的荣誉；过多的赞扬 | Excessive honor or praise. | The overhonors heaped upon the young athlete made him uncomfortable.  给予这位年轻运动员过多的赞扬让他感到不适。| 近义词: excessive praise, undue accolades | 反义词: insufficient praise, deserved recognition |词性:名词
