augury:/ˈɔːɡjəri/ | n. | 预兆，征兆；占卜 | An augury is a sign or omen of what will happen in the future.  It can also refer to the practice of interpreting such signs. | The flight of birds was considered an augury of good fortune. 鸟的飞翔被认为是好运的预兆。| synonyms: omen, portent, presage, sign, prognostication | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider)  coincidence, chance, accident | noun
