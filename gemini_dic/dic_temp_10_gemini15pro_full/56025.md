doab:/ˈduːɑːb/
n.
冲积平原, 两河之间的土地
The land between two converging rivers.
The fertile doab between the Ganges and the Yamuna rivers has been cultivated for centuries.  恒河和亚穆纳河之间肥沃的冲积平原已经被耕种了几个世纪。
近义词:interfluve, floodplain
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: 名词


Etymology: From Hindi-Urdu  *doāb*  (دوآب), from Persian  *do*  (دو, “two”) +  *āb*  (آب, “water, river”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European  *dwóh₁*  (“two”) and  *h₂eḱwós/*h₂ékʷeh₂*  (“horse”, by extension “swift stream”).
