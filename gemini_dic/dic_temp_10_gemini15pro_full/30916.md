chain-smokes:/ˈtʃeɪn sməʊks/ | v. | 连抽（香烟）;不停地抽烟 | to smoke cigarettes one after another, almost without stopping | He chain-smoked all through the meeting. 他整个会议期间都在不停地抽烟。 | 近义词: smoke heavily, smoke continuously | 反义词: abstain from smoking | 词性: 动词

chain-smoker:/ˈtʃeɪn ˌsməʊkər/ | n. | 连续抽烟的人；烟鬼 | someone who smokes cigarettes one after another, almost without stopping | My uncle is a chain-smoker and he coughs all the time. 我叔叔是个烟鬼，总是咳嗽。 | 近义词: heavy smoker, inveterate smoker | 反义词: non-smoker | 词性: 名词
