glossological:/ˌɡlɒsəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ | adj. | 词汇学的，语义学的 | Relating to glossology; concerned with the study of language, especially vocabulary. | Glossological studies can reveal the evolution of words over time. 词汇学研究可以揭示词语随时间的演变。 |  | Near synonyms: linguistic, lexical, semantic | antonym: non-linguistic | adjective
