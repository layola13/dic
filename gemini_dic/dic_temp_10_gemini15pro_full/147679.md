ETYMOLOGY->
From "overpower" + "-ful" + "-ness". "Overpower" comes from "over-" (excessive) + "power" (strength, ability). "-ful" forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by". "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives.
