nourishments:/ˈnɜːrɪʃmənts/
n.|营养品；滋养物|Something that nourishes; food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition.|The soil provides the necessary nourishments for healthy plant growth. 土壤为植物的健康生长提供必要的营养。|近义词:nutrients, sustenance, provisions|反义词:deprivations|词性:名词

nourishment:/ˈnɜːrɪʃmənt/
n.|营养;滋养;养料|The food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition.|Proper nourishment is essential for a child's development. 适当的营养对儿童的发育至关重要。|近义词:nutrition, sustenance, food|反义词:starvation|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *nōrīsʃment*,  from Anglo-Norman *nūrissement, nūrissment*, and Old French *norrissement*, from *norrir* (“to nourish”), from Latin *nutrire*.
