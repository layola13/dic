fumbler:/ˈfʌmblər/| n. |笨手笨脚的人；常犯错误的人|A person who fumbles; someone who is clumsy or inept, especially with their hands.|He was such a fumbler that he dropped the ball every time he tried to catch it. 他真是个笨手笨脚的人，每次想接球都会掉球。|近义词：clumsy person, butterfingers, klutz|反义词：dexterous person, skillful person|词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "fumble" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Fumble" comes from Middle English *fomblen*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Low German *fummeln* "to grope."
