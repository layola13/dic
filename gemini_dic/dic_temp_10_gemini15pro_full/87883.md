hie:/haɪ/
verb
急忙前往；匆匆而去
go quickly; hasten
例句：He hied himself to the station. 他急忙赶往车站。
例句：She hied away to her room. 她匆匆回到房间。
近义词：hurry, hasten, rush, dash
反义词：dawdle, linger
词性：verb
