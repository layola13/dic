equiponderant:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndərənt/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndərənt/
adjective
均重的；相等的；同等重要的
equal in weight, force, or importance.
例句：The two opposing arguments were equiponderant, making it difficult to reach a decision.  正反两方的论点势均力敌，难以做出决定。

例句：The two sides in the conflict maintained equiponderant military forces. 冲突双方保持着均等的军事力量。

近义词：equivalent, equal, balanced, commensurate
反义词：unequal, disparate, imbalanced
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *aequiponderant- (stem of *aequiponderans), present participle of *aequiponderare ‘to be of equal weight,’ from aequi- ‘equally’ + ponderare ‘to weigh,’ from pondus, ponder- ‘weight.’
