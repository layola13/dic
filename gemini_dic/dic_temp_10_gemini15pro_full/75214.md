froglet:/ˈfrɒɡlɪt/ | n. | 小青蛙，幼蛙 | A young frog, especially one that has recently undergone metamorphosis from a tadpole. |  The pond was teeming with froglets. 池塘里到处都是小青蛙。| synonyms: tadpole, polliwog, young frog | antonyms: adult frog | noun
