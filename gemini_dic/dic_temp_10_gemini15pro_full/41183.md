copyists:/ˈkɒpiɪsts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiɪsts/ (US)| n. | 抄写员；复制者 |A person who copies documents, manuscripts, or other texts, especially before the invention of printing. | The medieval copyists preserved many ancient texts. 中世纪的抄写员保存了许多古代文献。|  The museum employed copyists to reproduce the ancient scrolls.  博物馆雇佣抄写员复制古代卷轴。| synonyms: scribe, transcriber, duplicator | antonyms: author, originator, creator | noun
