howler-monkeys:/ˈhaʊlər ˌmʌŋkiz/| n.|吼猴|A type of monkey known for its loud, howling vocalizations.|例句：The howler-monkeys' calls echoed through the jungle. 吼猴的叫声在丛林中回荡。

例句：We saw a troop of howler-monkeys high in the canopy. 我们看到一群吼猴在树冠高处。|近义词：monkeys, primates|反义词:|词性:noun (plural)

howler:/ˈhaʊlər/| n.|吼叫者；发出大声吼叫的东西|Someone or something that howls.|例句：The wind was a real howler last night. 昨晚的风真是呼啸不停。

例句：He's a real howler when he's had too much to drink. 他喝多了就大声吼叫。|近义词：shouter, screamer|反义词:whisperer|词性:noun

monkey:/ˈmʌŋki/| n.|猴子|A primate mammal with a long tail and hands and feet adapted for grasping.|例句：The monkey swung from branch to branch. 猴子在树枝间荡来荡去。

例句：The children loved watching the monkeys at the zoo. 孩子们喜欢在动物园里看猴子。|近义词：primate, ape|反义词:|词性:noun
