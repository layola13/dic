auralities:/ɔːrˈælɪtiz/
n.
听觉特性；声音特质；可听性
The qualities of sound or sounds; things that are heard.  This word emphasizes the perceived qualities of sounds, rather than simply the sounds themselves.
The auralities of the rainforest at dawn were breathtaking, a symphony of bird calls and rustling leaves.  黎明的雨林里的声音令人叹为观止，鸟鸣声和树叶沙沙作响交织成一首交响曲。
Near synonyms: soundscape, sounds, acoustics
Antonyms: silence, quiet
noun
