obsidional:/əbˈsɪdiənəl/
adjective
围城的，攻城的
Pertaining to a siege; used in besieging.
The obsidional artillery bombarded the city walls for days.  攻城炮连续几天轰炸城墙。
The obsidional forces surrounded the castle. 围城部队包围了城堡。
近义词: besieging, siege
反义词: None
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *obsidionālis*, from obsidio ("siege").
