ouch:/aʊtʃ/ | int. | 哎哟; 痛;| An exclamation expressing sudden pain. | Example: Ouch! I stubbed my toe. 哎哟！我的脚趾撞到了。| Example:  Ouch! That bee sting hurts. 哎哟！蜜蜂蜇得好痛。 | 近义词: yelp, ow | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation of pain) | 词性: interjection
