nonreinvestment:/ˌnɑnˌriːɪnˈvɛstmənt/ | n. | 非再投资 | The practice of not reinvesting earnings or dividends, but instead taking them as cash. |  The investor opted for nonreinvestment of dividends, preferring to receive the cash payouts. 投资者选择不将股息再投资，而是宁愿获得现金支付。

He chose nonreinvestment because he needed the money for other expenses. 他选择不进行再投资，因为他需要这笔钱用于其他开支。 | 近义词: non-reinvestment, cashing out | 反义词: reinvestment, compounding | 词性: noun
