discerningly:/dɪˈsɜːrnɪŋli/
adverb
敏锐地，有识别力地
In a way that shows good judgment, especially about style and quality.
She dressed discerningly and elegantly. 她穿着打扮既敏锐又有品位。
Synonyms: astutely, perceptively, shrewdly, wisely, judiciously
Antonyms: indiscriminately, uncritically, blindly, foolishly
词性: 副词


discern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/
verb
辨别，识别；看出，察觉
Recognize or find out.
It is difficult to discern any pattern in these figures.  很难从这些数字中看出任何规律。
Synonyms: perceive, detect, recognize, distinguish, differentiate
Antonyms: overlook, miss, ignore, confuse
词性: 动词
