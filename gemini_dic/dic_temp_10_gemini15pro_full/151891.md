passports:/ˈpɑːspɔːrts/
n.
护照
An official document issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship and entitling them to travel under its protection to and from other countries.
Please show your passports at the check-in counter.  请在值机柜台出示您的护照。

近义词:travel documents, identification papers
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


passports:/ˈpɑːspɔːrts/
词源: From Old French passeport "safe-conduct," from passer "to pass" + port "port." Originally referred to a document needed to pass through a port or gate.
用法说明: Passports are essential for international travel. They are official documents that prove your identity and citizenship.

例句：
All passengers must present their passports to immigration officials.
所有乘客必须向移民官员出示护照。

Foreign visitors need valid passports and visas to enter the country.
外国游客需要有效的护照和签证才能入境。
