neuropteris: /ˌnjʊːrɒpˈtɛrɪs/
n.
一种已灭绝的蕨类植物；脉羊齿属植物
An extinct seed fern of the Carboniferous and Permian periods, having large, compound, fernlike leaves.
Neuropteris fossils are commonly found in coal deposits.  脉羊齿属植物化石常见于煤矿床中。

Neuropteris was a dominant plant during the Carboniferous period. 脉羊齿属植物是石炭纪时期的优势植物。
近义词: seed fern (种子蕨), Alethopteris (翼羊齿属),  Pecopteris (栉羊齿属) (These are related extinct genera)
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for an extinct genus)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *neuron* ("nerve") + *pteris* ("fern"), referring to the nerve-like venation of the leaves.  源自希腊语 *neuron*（“神经”）+ *pteris*（“蕨类植物”），指其叶片类似神经的脉络。
