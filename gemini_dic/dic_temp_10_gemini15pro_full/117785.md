melezitose:/ˌmɛləˈzaɪtoʊs/| n. |蜜三糖|A trisaccharide sugar found in honeydew and manna. It is composed of two glucose units and one fructose unit.|Melezitose is a common ingredient in insect diets used in research. 蜜三糖是研究中常用的昆虫食物成分。|The melezitose pathway allows certain bacteria to thrive in specific environments. 蜜三糖途径使某些细菌能够在特定环境中茁壮成长。|Synonyms: trisaccharide|Antonyms: monosaccharide|noun
