logistics:/ləˈdʒɪstɪks/ | n. |物流；后勤；逻辑学|The organization of moving, supplying, and accommodating troops and equipment. The detailed coordination of a complex operation involving many people, facilities, or supplies.| The logistics of the relief effort were complicated by the flooding. 救援工作的后勤保障由于洪水而变得复杂。|synonyms: organization, planning, management, coordination, administration, arrangement, systematization, handling, maneuvering, deployment, mobilization | antonyms: disorganization, chaos | noun


logistics:/ləˈdʒɪstɪks/ | adj. | 逻辑的 | Relating to logistics. |  The logistics team faced a logistical nightmare.  后勤团队面临着一个逻辑上的噩梦。 | synonyms: organizational, operational, strategic, tactical | antonyms: illogical | adjective
