flawier:/ˈflɔːiər/ | adj. | 更有缺陷的，瑕疵更多的 | More flawed; having more imperfections or defects. | The second attempt at the sculpture was even flawier than the first.  第二次尝试的雕塑甚至比第一次更有缺陷。|  The diamond was flawier than expected and therefore less valuable. 这颗钻石比预期的瑕疵更多，因此价值更低。 | synonyms: more imperfect, more defective, blemished, damaged, faulty | antonyms: more perfect, flawless, impeccable, pristine | adjective
