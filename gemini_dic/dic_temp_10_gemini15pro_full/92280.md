hypes:/haɪps/| n. |炒作；宣传；兴奋剂|Intense publicity and promotion, often exaggerated, to create excitement and interest around a product, person, or event. Also, can refer to stimulants or drugs.|The company used a lot of hype to promote their new phone. 这家公司用大量的炒作来宣传他们的新手机。|synonyms: promotion, publicity, ballyhoo, buzz, exaggeration,  stimulants, drugs|antonyms: understatement, reality, downplaying|noun
