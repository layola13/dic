colorize:/ˈkʌləraɪz/ | v. | 使彩色化，给…着色 | To add color to something, especially a black and white film or photograph. |  例句：They colorized the old black and white movie. 他们把那部黑白老电影彩色化了。

例句：The software can colorize old photos. 这个软件可以给老照片上色。

近义词：tint, color, paint, dye | 反义词：bleach, decolorize, monochrome | 词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From color + -ize.  "Color" comes from the Old French "couleur," ultimately from Latin "color."  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or become" something.
