ossianic:/ˌɒsiˈænɪk/ /ˌɑːsiˈænɪk/
adjective
奥西恩式的；像奥西恩诗歌的；浪漫的；感伤的
Relating to or characteristic of the poems attributed to Ossian, a legendary Gaelic warrior and bard, noted for their melancholy and romantic tone.
Ossianic themes of heroism and loss resonate throughout the novel.  这本小说充满了奥西恩式的英雄主义和失落主题。
The landscape was bleak and ossianic. 这片风景荒凉而带有奥西恩诗歌般的伤感。
近义词:romantic, sentimental, melancholic, elegiac
反义词:realistic, pragmatic, cheerful
词性:adjective
