non-trivial:/nɒnˈtrɪviəl/ /nɑːnˈtrɪviəl/| adj.|重要的，非平凡的，不寻常的|Not simple or obvious; significant.  Something that requires careful consideration or is not easily solved.|Finding a cure for cancer is a non-trivial task. 找到治愈癌症的方法是一项艰巨的任务。|Example:  Designing a bridge that can withstand earthquakes is a non-trivial engineering problem. 设计一座能承受地震的桥梁是一个不简单的工程问题。|Synonyms:  significant, important, complex, challenging, substantial, weighty|Antonyms:  trivial, simple, easy, unimportant, insignificant|adjective
