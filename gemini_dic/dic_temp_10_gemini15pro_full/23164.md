bovine:/ˈboʊvaɪn/ (US) /ˈbəʊvaɪn/ (UK)| adj. n.|牛的；迟钝的；似牛的；牛科动物|Relating to cows or cattle; sluggish and unresponsive.|例句：The farmer moved the bovine herd to a new pasture. 农民将牛群转移到新的牧场。|近义词：cowlike, oxlike|反义词：alert, quick-witted|词性:形容词

bovine:/ˈboʊvaɪn/ (US) /ˈbəʊvaɪn/ (UK)| n.|牛科动物|A bovine animal, such as a cow, bull, or ox.|例句：The veterinarian specialized in bovine medicine. 这位兽医专攻牛科动物医学。|近义词：cattle, ox, cow|反义词:None|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *bovin, from Latin bovīnus (“of or belonging to oxen or cows”), from bōs (“ox, cow”).
