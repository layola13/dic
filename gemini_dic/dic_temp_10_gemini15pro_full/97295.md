infobox:/ˈɪnfoʊbɒks/ |n.|信息框|A panel or box, typically displayed alongside an article or entry in a digital encyclopedia or other online resource, containing key facts and data summarized from the main text.|The infobox on the Wikipedia page for "Paris" provides quick facts about the city's population and history.|近义词:fact box, sidebar, data box|反义词:main text|词性:名词
Etymology: "info" (short for "information") + "box"
