firefighter:/ˈfaɪərfʌɪtər/ | n. | 消防员 | A person whose job is to extinguish fires and rescue people from burning buildings or other emergencies. |  The firefighters bravely entered the burning building. 消防队员勇敢地进入了燃烧的建筑物。

 The firefighters worked tirelessly to contain the wildfire. 消防队员不知疲倦地工作以控制野火。 |  fireman, smoke eater |  arsonist (纵火犯 -  not a direct opposite, but related to the negative aspect of fire) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fire + fighter. "fighter" comes from the verb "fight" which has roots in Old English "feohtan" and ultimately Proto-Germanic "*fehtaną".  "Fire" comes from Old English "fyr", from Proto-Germanic "*fūr-" and ultimately Proto-Indo-European "*péh₂wr̥".
