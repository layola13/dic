/ˈkɔːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɔːlɪŋ/ (US) | adj. | 呼唤的，召唤的 | Used to describe something that summons or attracts. |The calling of the wild.  荒野的呼唤。 |近义词：summoning, beckoning, inviting|反义词:repelling, deterring|词性:adjective
