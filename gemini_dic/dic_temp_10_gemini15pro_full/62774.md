engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/ (US), /ɛnˈɡreɪv/ (UK)| vt.|雕刻，铭刻|To cut or carve (a design, inscription, etc.) into a hard surface, such as wood, stone, or metal.|The jeweler engraved the ring with their initials. 珠宝商在戒指上刻上了他们的姓名首字母。

They engraved their names on the tree. 他们在树上刻了他们的名字。

|近义词：carve, etch, inscribe|反义词:erase, efface|词性:动词
