furuncular:/fʊˈrʌŋkjʊlər/| adj.|疖的，似疖的|Relating to, or resembling a furuncle (boil).|He had a furuncular infection on his neck. 他脖子上有一个疖子感染。|近义词：boil-like, abscess-like|反义词: healthy, clear (skin)|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *furuncularis, from Latin furunculus (“boil”), diminutive of fur (“thief”).  The connection may be due to the idea of a boil "stealing" health or vitality.
