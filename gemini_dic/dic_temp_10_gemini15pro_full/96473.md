individualizing:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
个性化；个别化
The act of making something specific to an individual.  Making something suitable for a particular person's needs or interests.
例句：
The teacher is individualizing the learning plan for each student.  老师正在为每个学生制定个性化的学习计划。

They are individualizing the training program to meet the needs of each athlete. 他们正在根据每个运动员的需求调整训练计划。

近义词：personalizing, customizing, tailoring
反义词：standardizing, generalizing
词性：verb (动词)
