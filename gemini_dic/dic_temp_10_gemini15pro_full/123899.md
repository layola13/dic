mordantly:/ˈmɔːrdəntli/ | adv. | 尖刻地，辛辣地，讽刺地 | In a biting, sarcastic, or sharply critical manner. |  He mordantly criticized the government's new policy. 他尖刻地批评了政府的新政策。

|  She delivered a mordantly witty speech. 她发表了一场辛辣而机智的演讲。

| Synonyms: caustically, bitingly, acrimoniously, sarcastically, cuttingly | Antonyms: gently, kindly, sweetly, mildly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *mordant* (biting, pungent), present participle of *mordre* (to bite), from Latin *mordēre* (to bite).  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "mordant" into an adverb.
