mucker:/ˈmʌkər/| n.|伙伴，朋友；勤杂工，马夫|A person who works with another, especially in a difficult or unpleasant job. Also, a close friend or companion.|例句：He's been my mucker for years. 他是我多年的好朋友。

例句：The muckers shoveled coal into the furnace. 工人们把煤铲进炉子里。|近义词：friend, pal, mate, partner, comrade, colleague, workmate|反义词：enemy, foe|词性：noun

mucker:/ˈmʌkər/| v.|清理马厩|To clean (a stable) of dung.|例句：He mucked out the stables every morning. 他每天早上清理马厩。

例句：She was mucking the stalls when the vet arrived.  兽医来的时候她正在清理马厩。|近义词：clean, clear|反义词：dirty, soil|词性：verb

词源：From the Dutch *moker* (one who cuts peat), from *moken* (to cut peat), related to *muck*.  源于荷兰语 *moker*（切割泥炭的人），来自 *moken*（切割泥炭），与 *muck*（粪便，污物）相关。 这表明这个词最初可能指在脏乱环境中一起工作的人，后来演变成指亲密的朋友或伙伴。
