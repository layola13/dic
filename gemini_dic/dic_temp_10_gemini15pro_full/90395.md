howl-down:/ˈhaʊl daʊn/
verb
压制；以吼叫声盖过…的声音；大声喊叫阻止…讲话
to prevent someone from speaking by shouting loudly to drown out their voice
The speaker was howled down by the angry crowd. 演讲者被愤怒的人群的喊叫声所淹没。
shout down, drown out
allow to speak, listen to
v.
