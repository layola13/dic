commodiousness:/kəˈmoʊdiəsnəs/
n.
宽敞，舒适
The state of being spacious and convenient; roominess.
The commodiousness of the new apartment was a welcome change.  新公寓的宽敞令人欣喜。
近义词: spaciousness, roominess, amplitude, vastness
反义词: crampedness, confinement, smallness
词性: 名词

commodious:/kəˈmoʊdiəs/
adj.
宽敞的，舒适的
Spacious and convenient; roomy.
They have a commodious house with a large garden. 他们有一栋宽敞的房子，带有一个大花园。
近义词: spacious, roomy, ample, capacious
反义词: cramped, confined, small, restricted
词性: 形容词
