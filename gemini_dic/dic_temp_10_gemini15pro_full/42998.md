cowalker:/ˈkoʊwɔːkər/
noun
同事; 同行者
A person who walks with another person, especially for exercise or companionship.
例句：I met my cowalker at the park entrance every morning for our walk.  我每天早上都在公园入口处与我的散步同伴见面一起散步。

例句：She enjoyed having a cowalker to chat with during her daily exercise. 她喜欢在日常锻炼时有位同伴可以聊天。

近义词：walking companion, walking buddy, fellow walker
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "lone walker")
词性: noun
