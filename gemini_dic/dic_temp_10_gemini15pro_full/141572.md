oppositions:/ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənz/
n.
反对；反抗；敌对；对立
The government faced strong opposition to its new policies. 政府面临对其新政策的强烈反对。
There is considerable opposition to the proposed changes. 对提议的修改有很多反对意见。
Synonyms: resistance, defiance, antagonism, hostility, objection, dissent
Antonyms: support, agreement, approval, consent
noun
