overfrugally: /ˌoʊvərˈfruːɡəli/
| adv. | 过于节俭地 | excessively frugally; in a way that is too economical |  She lives overfrugally, even denying herself basic comforts. 她生活过于节俭，甚至连基本 comforts 都舍不得。|  He ate overfrugally, always choosing the cheapest options. 他吃得过于节俭，总是选择最便宜的。 | 近义词: too economically, excessively thriftily, parsimoniously | 反义词: extravagantly, lavishly | 词性: 副词
