calcifications: /ˌkælsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
| n. |钙化（复数）|The process of hardening of tissue due to the deposition of calcium salts.|Mammograms can detect calcifications in breast tissue. 乳房X光照片可以检测乳房组织中的钙化。|
| n. |钙化物（复数）|A hardened deposit of calcium salts in body tissue.|The doctor found calcifications in the patient's arteries. 医生在病人的动脉中发现了钙化物。|
|近义词：mineralization, hardening, deposits|反义词: softening|词性:名词

词源:
From Latin *calx* (lime) + *-ficātiō* (making) + *-iōn* (act, state, result) > Old French *calificacion* > Middle English *calcificacion*
