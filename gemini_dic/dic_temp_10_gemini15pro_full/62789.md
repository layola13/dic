engrossedly:/ɪnˈɡroʊsɪdli/ /ɪnˈɡrɒsɪdli/| adv. |全神贯注地，专注地|  In a manner that absorbs all one's attention or interest. | She listened engrossedly to his story. 她全神贯注地听着他的故事。|  He worked engrossedly on the project, oblivious to the passing time. 他专注地投入到项目中，忘记了时间的流逝。| 近义词: attentively, intently, absorbedly | 反义词: distractedly, inattentively | 词性: 副词

词源: From engrossed + -ly.  Engrossed itself comes from the Old French word "engrossir," meaning "to thicken" or "to enlarge,"  figuratively extending to mean "to occupy fully."
