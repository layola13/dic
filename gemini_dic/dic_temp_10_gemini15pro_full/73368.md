foregone:/fɔːrˈɡɒn/ /fɔːrˈɡɔːn/
adjective
预先决定的，必然发生的，不可避免的
involving something that is certain to happen
例句：
The outcome of the election was a foregone conclusion.
选举的结果已成定局。

It seemed a foregone conclusion that he would win the race.
他似乎肯定会赢得比赛。

近义词：inevitable, certain, predetermined
反义词：uncertain, unpredictable, doubtful
词性：adjective
