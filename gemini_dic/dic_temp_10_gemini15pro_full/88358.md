hippopotamus:/ˌhɪpəˈpɒtəməs/ (UK) /ˌhɪpəˈpɑːtəməs/ (US)| n. |河马|A large, thick-skinned, semiaquatic African mammal with short legs, a massive head, and a very large mouth.|例句：The hippopotamus wallowed in the mud. 河马在泥里打滚。
