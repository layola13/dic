crowner:/ˈkraʊnər/| n.|加冕者，加冕的人；验尸官|A person who crowns someone, especially a monarch. Also, an official who investigates sudden, violent, or suspicious deaths.|The king's crowner placed the crown upon his head.|近义词:coroner (especially for the official who investigates deaths)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

crowner:/ˈkraʊnər/| n.|完成者，成就者 (较少用)|Someone who completes or perfects something.|He was the crowner of the project, bringing it to its successful conclusion.|近义词:finisher, completer|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

注意事项:
