canon: /ˈkænən/ | n. | 准则，规范；教规；正典；真作 | a general law, rule, principle, or criterion by which something is judged | The canons of good taste dictate that you should not wear white after Labor Day.  良好的品味准则规定，劳动节后不应该穿白色衣服。| synonyms: principle, rule, law, standard, precept | antonyms: heresy, unorthodoxy | noun

canon: /ˈkænən/ | n. | (教堂的)教士 | a member of the clergy who is on the staff of a cathedral | The canons met to discuss the upcoming service.  教士们开会讨论即将举行的仪式。| synonyms: clergyman, ecclesiastic, prelate | antonyms: layman | noun

canon: /ˈkænən/ | n. | (尤指艺术家的)真作，真迹 | a list of works considered to be definitely written by a particular author | The play is not included in the Shakespearean canon. 这部戏剧不包括在莎士比亚的真作中。 | synonyms:  oeuvre, works | antonyms: apocrypha | noun

canon: /ˈkænən/ | n. | 卡农(一种复调音乐作品) | a musical composition in which two or more voices or instruments sing or play the same melody, one after another |  Pachelbel's Canon in D is a popular wedding song.  帕赫贝尔的D大调卡农是一首很受欢迎的婚礼歌曲。| synonyms: round, fugue | antonyms: N/A | noun
