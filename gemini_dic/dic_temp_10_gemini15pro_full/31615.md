chastities: /ˈtʃæstɪtiz/
noun
贞洁，纯洁
The state or practice of refraining from extramarital, or especially from all, sexual intercourse.
She valued her chastity above all else. 她视贞洁为至宝。
近义词: purity, virtue, innocence
反义词: lewdness, promiscuity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chastite, from Old French chasteté, from Latin castitās (“purity, chastity”), from castus (“pure, chaste”).
