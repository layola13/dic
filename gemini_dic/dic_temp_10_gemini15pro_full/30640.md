ceresin:/ˈsɛrəsɪn/
n.
地蜡，矿脂
A wax derived from ozokerite, used in candles, polishes, etc.
Ceresin wax is often used in cosmetics.  地蜡常用于化妆品中。

Ceresin is added to candles to harden them. 地蜡被添加到蜡烛中以使其变硬。

Synonym:
paraffin wax, mineral wax, ozokerite

Antonym:
(No direct antonym, but consider contrasting materials like beeswax or soy wax)

词性: noun
