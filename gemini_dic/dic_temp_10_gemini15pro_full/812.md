abstains:/əbˈsteɪnz/ | v. |  戒除，弃权 |  To restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something.  | He abstains from alcohol.  他戒酒了。

She abstained from voting in the election. 她弃权了，没有在选举中投票。 | 近义词：refrain, forgo, avoid | 反义词：indulge, partake | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abstenen, from Old French astenir, from Latin abstinēre, from abs- "from" + tenēre "to hold".  The prefix abs- becomes ab- before t.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abstains from smoking. = 他戒烟。
She abstains from eating meat. = 她不吃肉。

一般过去时:
He abstained from alcohol during his recovery. = 他在康复期间戒酒了。
She abstained from voting on that particular issue. = 她在那个特定问题上弃权了。

现在完成时:
He has abstained from sugar for a year. = 他已经戒糖一年了。
She has abstained from commenting on the case. = 她一直没有对这个案件发表评论。

过去完成时:
He had abstained from caffeine before the competition. = 他在比赛前戒掉了咖啡因。
She had abstained from voting in the past. = 她过去弃权过。

将来时:
He will abstain from alcohol at the party. = 他在聚会上不会喝酒。
She will abstain from making any decisions until she has all the facts. = 她会在掌握所有事实之前避免做出任何决定。

虚拟语气:
If he abstained from gambling, he would have more money. = 如果他不赌博，他就会有更多钱。
I wish she would abstain from gossiping. = 我希望她不要八卦。

被动语态: (Abstain is rarely used in the passive voice.)

疑问句:
Does he abstain from alcohol? = 他戒酒吗？
Why did she abstain from voting? = 她为什么弃权？

否定句:
He doesn't abstain from anything. = 他什么都不戒。
She didn't abstain from expressing her opinion. = 她没有放弃表达自己的意见。


PHRASE->
abstain from voting = 弃权
abstain from alcohol/drugs = 戒酒/戒毒
abstain from commenting = 不予置评
abstain from eating meat =  不吃肉
abstain from sex = 禁欲
