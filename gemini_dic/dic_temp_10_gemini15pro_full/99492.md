interfiltration:/ˌɪntərˌfɪlˈtreɪʃən/ | n. | 渗透，互相渗透 | The interfiltration of groundwater and surface water is a complex process. 地下水和地表水的互相渗透是一个复杂的过程。 | The interfiltration of political ideas between countries can lead to social change.  各国之间政治思想的互相渗透会导致社会变革。 | 近义词：infiltration, permeation, osmosis | 反义词: separation, isolation | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From inter- + filtration, from filter. "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," and "filtration" refers to the process of passing through a filter.  Thus, "interfiltration" implies a mutual or reciprocal passing through or into each other.
