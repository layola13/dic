linguadental:/ˌlɪŋɡwəˈdɛntl/ | adj. |舌齿音的；舌齿音|A sound produced by placing the tip of the tongue against the upper teeth, such as the sounds represented by the letters 'th' in 'thin' and 'this'.|The sounds /θ/ and /ð/ are linguadental fricatives. /θ/和/ð/是舌齿摩擦音。|The "th" sound in "think" is a linguadental sound. “think”中的“th”音是舌齿音。|近义词:dental|反义词:(No common antonym) |词性:adjective
