guardrail:/ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪl/ /ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪl/ | n. | 护栏，防护栏 |A barrier erected along the edge of a road, bridge, or other elevated structure to prevent people or vehicles from falling off. | The guardrail prevented the car from plunging down the ravine.  护栏阻止了汽车坠入峡谷。| synonym: barrier, railing, fence | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "opening") | noun

guardrail:/ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪl/ /ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪl/ | n. | (比喻) 保障措施 |A protective measure or safeguard against danger or error. |  Strict regulations act as a guardrail against financial mismanagement. 严格的法规是防止财务管理不善的保障措施。| synonym: safeguard, protection, precaution | antonym: risk, danger | noun
