impetuousnesses:/ɪmˈpɛtʃʊəsnɪsɪz/| n. |冲动，鲁莽|The quality or state of being impetuous; rash impulsiveness.|His impetuousnesses led him to make several poor decisions. 他的冲动导致他做了几个糟糕的决定。|Near synonyms: rashness, impulsivity, recklessness|Near antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From impetuousness + -es (suffix forming plural nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *impetus ("attack, assault") + -ous (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns).
