croqueted:/kroʊˈkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 用钩针编织的；指食物裹上面包屑油炸的 | Formed into small balls or cakes, coated with breadcrumbs, and deep-fried. |  The croqueted potatoes were crispy and golden brown. 炸土豆丸酥脆金黄。| Synonym: breaded, fried | Antonym: uncooked, raw | Adjective


croqueted:/kroʊˈkeɪtɪd/ | v. | 用钩针编织；把…做成炸丸子 | To form (food) into small balls or cakes, coat with breadcrumbs, and deep-fry. | She croqueted the leftover chicken into bite-sized appetizers. 她把剩下的鸡肉做成了小块的开胃菜。 | Synonym: bread, fry | Antonym:  bake, roast | Verb
