amphipodous:/ˌæmfɪˈpɒdəs/| adj.|片脚类的，端足类的|Having feet or legs of two kinds, or adapted for different purposes, like those of an amphipod.  Relating to or characteristic of amphipods.|Amphipodous crustaceans are common in marine environments. 片脚类甲壳动物在海洋环境中很常见。|近义词:crustacean|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "non-amphipodous")|词性:adjective
