creditable:/ˈkrɛdɪtəbl/| adj.|值得称赞的，可信的|Deserving praise, commendation, or esteem; bringing credit or honor.|Her performance in the play was creditable. 她在剧中的表演值得称赞。|近义词：praiseworthy, commendable, laudable, meritorious|反义词：discreditable, shameful, disgraceful|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from credit + -able.
