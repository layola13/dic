enhancement:/ɪnˈhænsmənt/ | n. | 增强，改进，提高 | An increase or improvement in quality, value, or extent. |  The new software includes several enhancements designed to improve user experience.  这款新软件包含多项旨在提升用户体验的改进。 | synonyms: improvement, upgrade, betterment, augmentation | antonyms: reduction, decrease, diminishment | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "enhauncer" (to raise, elevate), derived from "en-" (in, into) + "haucer" (to raise).  The word entered English in the late Middle English period.
