chattinesses:/ˈtʃætɪnɪsɪz/| n. |健谈，饶舌，话多|The quality of being chatty; talkativeness.  The tendency to engage in casual conversation. |Her chattinesses made it difficult to focus on the task at hand. 她太健谈了，让人很难集中精力完成手头的任务。|例句：His chattinesses often led to him revealing too much information. 他太爱说话了，经常导致他泄露太多信息。|近义词：loquacity, garrulity, talkativeness|反义词: taciturnity, reticence|noun
