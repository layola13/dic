basify:/ˈbeɪsɪfaɪ/| vt.|碱化，使成碱性|To make a solution or substance more basic (alkaline) by increasing its pH.|Sodium hydroxide is commonly used to basify solutions. 氢氧化钠通常用于碱化溶液。|The chemist basified the solution by adding ammonia. 化学家通过添加氨来碱化溶液。|alkalize, neutralize (with a base)|acidify|verb
