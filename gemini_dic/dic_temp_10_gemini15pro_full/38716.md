condiment:/ˈkɑːndɪmənt/ | n. | 调味品；佐料 |A substance such as salt, pepper, or mustard that is added to food to improve its flavour.|  I always keep a variety of condiments on the table. 我总是在餐桌上放各种各样的调味品。 | synonyms: spice, seasoning, relish, flavoring | antonyms: blandness | noun

condimentary:/ˌkɑːndɪˈmentəri/ | adj. | 用作调味品的；辛辣的 | Relating to or used as a condiment. | Condimentary sauces can enhance the flavour of a dish. 调味酱可以增强菜肴的味道。 | synonyms: spicy, pungent, flavorful, aromatic | antonyms: bland, tasteless | adjective
