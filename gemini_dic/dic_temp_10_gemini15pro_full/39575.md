conscriptable:/kənˈskrɪptəbl/
adjective
可征召的，可应征入伍的
Liable to be conscripted.
Eligible to be drafted into military service.

例句：
In times of war, all able-bodied men are conscriptable.
战争时期，所有体格健全的男子都是可以被征召的。

During the war, even teenagers were deemed conscriptable.
战争期间，甚至连青少年都被认为是可以征召入伍的。

近义词：
draftable, eligible (for conscription)
反义词：
exempt, ineligible (for conscription)
词性
adjective
