graphene: /ˈɡræfiːn/ | n. |石墨烯|A single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal lattice.  It is the thinnest compound known to man at one atom thick, the lightest material known (with 1 square meter coming in at around 0.77 milligrams), the strongest compound discovered (between 100-300 times stronger than steel), the best conductor of heat at room temperature and also the best conductor of electricity known. |Graphene is a revolutionary material with numerous potential applications. 石墨烯是一种具有众多潜在应用的革命性材料。|  Graphene's unique properties make it ideal for use in electronics. 石墨烯的独特性质使其成为电子产品中理想的材料。 | synonyms:  (no direct synonyms, but related materials include) graphite, carbon nanotubes | antonyms:  N/A | noun
