crystal:/ˈkrɪstl/ (US), /ˈkrɪstəl/ (UK)| n. adj.| 水晶; 晶体; 清澈的; 透明的 |A clear, transparent mineral or glass resembling ice.  Can also refer to something clear and transparent or highly refined.| The chandelier was adorned with hundreds of sparkling crystals. 枝形吊灯上装饰着数百颗闪闪发光的水晶。|  synonyms: glass, quartz, diamond, transparent, clear, lucid, crystalline | antonyms: opaque, cloudy, murky | noun, adjective
