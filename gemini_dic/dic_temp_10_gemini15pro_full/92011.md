hyperosmolar:/ˌhaɪpərɒzˈməʊlə(r)/ /ˌhaɪpərɑːzˈmoʊlər/ | adj. | 高渗的 | Having an abnormally high osmolality (concentration of solute particles) compared to a reference solution. |  Diabetic ketoacidosis can lead to a hyperosmolar state. 糖尿病酮症酸中毒会导致高渗状态。| hypertonic, concentrated | hypotonic, dilute | adjective

hyperosmolality:/ˌhaɪpərɒzməˈlælɪti/  /ˌhaɪpərɑːzməˈlælɪti/ | n. | 高渗透压 | The property of having a higher osmolality (concentration of solute particles) than another solution, usually compared to normal body fluids. |  Hyperosmolality can cause dehydration. 高渗透压会导致脱水。 | hypertonicity | hypotonicity | noun
