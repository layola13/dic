creamiest:/ˈkriːmiɪst/ | adj. | 最乳脂状的，最柔滑的 |  Superlative form of creamy: having a smooth, rich texture like cream.  | This is the creamiest ice cream I've ever had. 这是我吃过最柔滑的冰淇淋。 | The creamiest cheesecake was the highlight of the dessert buffet.  最柔滑的芝士蛋糕是甜点自助餐的亮点。 | 近义词: richest, smoothest, silkiest | 反义词: thin, watery, runny | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cream + -y + -est.  "Cream" comes from Old French "cresme," ultimately from Latin "crassus" (thick).  "-y" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "having the quality of." "-est" is a suffix used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
