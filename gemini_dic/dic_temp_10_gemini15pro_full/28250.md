captaining:/ˈkæptɪnɪŋ/
Verb
领导，指挥
The act of being a captain; leading or directing a team or group.
He is captaining the team this year.  他今年担任队长，领导团队。
She is captaining the ship through rough seas. 她正带领船只穿越汹涌的大海。
Synonyms: leading, commanding, heading, directing
Antonyms: following, obeying, submitting
