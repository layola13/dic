misplanned:/ˌmɪsˈplænd/
adjective
计划不周的，筹划不当的
badly planned; planned in a way that is not suitable or effective
The misplanned invasion resulted in heavy casualties. 计划不周的入侵导致了重大伤亡。
近义词: ill-conceived, poorly planned, ill-advised
反义词: well-planned, carefully planned
词性: adjective
词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + "planned" (past participle of the verb "plan")
