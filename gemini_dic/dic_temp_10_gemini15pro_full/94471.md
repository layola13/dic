imponderables:/ɪmˈpɒndərəblz/ | n. | 不可估量的事物；无法衡量因素；未知数 | factors that are difficult or impossible to assess or measure; unknowns |  The success of the project depends on a number of imponderables.  这个项目的成功取决于许多不可估量的因素。| synonyms: uncertainties, unknowns, variables | antonyms: certainties, knowns, constants | noun

imponderable:/ɪmˈpɒndərəbl/ | adj. | 不可估量的；难以衡量的 | difficult or impossible to estimate, assess, or measure | The impact of the new policy is imponderable at this stage. 新政策的影响在现阶段是难以估量的。| synonyms: immeasurable, incalculable, unpredictable | antonyms: measurable, calculable, predictable | adjective
