mythopoetic:/ˌmɪθəʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk/ | adj. | 神话诗意的；神话创造的 | Relating to the creation of myths or having the qualities of a myth.  It describes something that evokes the power and symbolism of myths, often focusing on archetypal themes and characters. |  His paintings have a distinct mythopoetic quality, drawing on ancient symbols and narratives. 他的绘画有一种独特的神话诗意，借鉴了古代的符号和叙事。

The author's mythopoetic vision helped to shape a new understanding of the hero's journey. 作者的神话诗学视野帮助人们对英雄之旅有了新的理解。 | 近义词:mythical, legendary, archetypal | 反义词:realistic, mundane, factual | 词性:adjective


Etymology:
From "myth" + "poetic"
"myth" from Greek "mythos" (story, tale)
"poetic" from Greek "poietikos" (pertaining to poetry)
