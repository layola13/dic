gentianaceous:/ˌdʒɛnʃiəˈneɪʃəs/
adj.
龙胆科的；像龙胆的
Belonging to or resembling the Gentianaceae family of plants, which includes gentians.
The meadow was dotted with gentianaceous flowers of vibrant blue.  这片草地上点缀着鲜艳的蓝色龙胆科花朵。
The botanist specialized in gentianaceous plants. 这位植物学家专门研究龙胆科植物。

词性: adjective
