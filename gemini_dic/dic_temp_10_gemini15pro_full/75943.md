fungous:/ˈfʌŋɡəs/| adj.|真菌的，似真菌的|Of or relating to fungi; fungal.  Resembling a fungus in texture or appearance.|The fungous growth spread quickly across the damp wall.  真菌生长迅速蔓延过潮湿的墙壁。|近义词：fungal, spongy, mushroomy|反义词: non-fungal|词性: 形容词

fungus:/ˈfʌŋɡəs/| n.|真菌，菌类|Any of a group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter, including molds, yeast, mushrooms, and toadstools.|The tree was covered in a strange green fungus.  这棵树上覆盖着一种奇怪的绿色真菌。|近义词：mold, mildew, yeast|反义词:|词性: 名词


词源:
From Late Middle English *fungous*, from Latin *fungōsus* (“spongy, fungous”), from *fungus* (“mushroom, fungus”).
