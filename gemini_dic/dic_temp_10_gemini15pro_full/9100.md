antimonarchy:/ˌæntiˈmɒnəki/ /ˌæntiˈmɑːnərki/| n. |反君主制，反对君主制|Opposition to monarchy; the principles or movement of those opposed to monarchy. |例句：Antimonarchy sentiment is growing in some countries. 一些国家的反君主制情绪正在增长。

例句：The group published pamphlets promoting antimonarchy.  该组织出版了宣传反君主制的小册子。|近义词：republicanism, antiroyalism|反义词：monarchism, royalism|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) + monarchy (noun)

"anti-" comes from Greek, meaning "against" or "opposite".
"monarchy" comes from the Greek "monarkhia", meaning "rule by one".
