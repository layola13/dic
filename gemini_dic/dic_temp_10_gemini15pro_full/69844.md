ferriferous:/fɛˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
含铁的
Containing or yielding iron.
The soil in this region is ferriferous, making it ideal for iron mining.  这个地区的土壤含铁，使其成为铁矿开采的理想之地。
The rocks are ferriferous. 这些岩石含铁。
synonym: iron-bearing, iron-rich
antonym: non-ferriferous


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ferrifer* (iron-bearing), from *ferrum* (iron) + *-fer* (bearing, carrying).  来自拉丁语 *ferrifer*（含铁的），由 *ferrum*（铁）+ *-fer*（带有，携带）组成。
