capite:/ˈkæpɪteɪ/
n.
head, chapter
Latin word meaning "head" or "chapter."  It is often used in legal or academic contexts.
The capite of the organization made the final decision. 该组织的首脑作出了最终决定。
Synonyms: head, leader, chief, chapter, section
Antonyms: subordinate, follower
