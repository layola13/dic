loellingite:/ˈlɜːlɪŋˌaɪt/ | n. | 斜方砷铁矿 | An iron arsenide mineral, FeAs₂, that crystallizes in the orthorhombic system and is typically silver-white to steel-gray in color. It is a relatively common arsenic ore. | Loellingite is often found in hydrothermal veins associated with other sulfide minerals. 斜方砷铁矿通常与其他硫化物矿物一起出现在热液矿脉中。| Synonyms: leucopyrite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
