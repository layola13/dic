dynamometer:/ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |测力计；功率计|An instrument for measuring force or power, such as the output of an engine.|例句：The engineer used a dynamometer to measure the engine's horsepower. 工程师用测力计测量发动机的马力。

例句：A dynamometer is essential for testing the performance of electric motors. 测力计对于测试电动机的性能至关重要。


近义词：gauge, meter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
