colonialized:/ˈkɒlənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ (US)| v. |殖民化的；被殖民的|to establish political control over (a territory or people) and settle it with colonists; to send settlers to a new territory and establish political control over it.|The British colonized many countries around the world.  英国在世界各地殖民了许多国家。|Synonyms: conquered, settled, occupied, subjugated |Antonyms: liberated, freed, independent|verb
