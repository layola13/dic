farcement:/ˈfɑːrsmənt/ | n. | 闹剧；胡闹；滑稽剧 | An exaggerated, often boisterous comedy; a farce. | The play was a complete farcement, full of slapstick humor and ridiculous situations. 这部戏完全是一场闹剧，充满了滑稽的幽默和荒谬的情境。|  近义词: farce, buffoonery, slapstick | 反义词: tragedy, drama | 词性: noun

词源:  From French "farce," meaning "stuffing," (referring to interpolations in liturgical texts), which later came to mean "comic interlude" and then "broad comedy."
