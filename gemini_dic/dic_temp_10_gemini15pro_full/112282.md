lopseed:/ˈlɑːpsiːd/| n. |歪嘴，歪斜，歪种植物|A condition in which one side of something droops or hangs lower than the other, especially of a flower or face.  Also, any of various plants of the family Phrymaceae, especially of the genus *Phryma*, having asymmetrical flowers.|The lopseed flowers hung delicately from the stem. 歪嘴花娇弱地垂在茎上。|近义词：asymmetrical, uneven, crooked|反义词:symmetrical, even, straight|词性:noun

词源:
From *lop* (meaning "to hang down") + *seed*, referring to the drooping or hanging appearance of the flower or fruit.
