nonindustrious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌstriəs/ | adj. | 不勤劳的，懒惰的 | Not industrious; lazy or idle.  Characterized by a lack of diligence and a tendency to avoid work. | He was a nonindustrious student, preferring to socialize than study. 他是一个不勤奋的学生，宁愿社交也不愿学习。

| The nonindustrious farmer let his fields lie fallow.  这个懒惰的农民让他的田地休耕。| 近义词: lazy, idle, indolent, slothful | 反义词: industrious, diligent, hardworking, assiduous | 词性: 形容词
