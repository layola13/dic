nonubiquitously:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəsli/
adv.
并非普遍地，并非无所不在地
Not ubiquitously; not present everywhere.
Specialized retailers are nonubiquitously distributed.  专业零售商的分布并非无所不在。
Near synonyms: locally, regionally, sparsely
Antonyms: ubiquitously, everywhere, universally
词性: 副词
