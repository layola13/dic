nonfreemen:/nɒnˈfriːmən/ | n. | 非自由人；奴隶 |  A person who is not free, especially a slave or serf.  Historically, this term referred to individuals who lacked the rights and privileges of freemen, often due to their social status or legal condition. | In ancient Rome, nonfreemen were often prisoners of war or indebted individuals.  在古罗马，非自由人通常是战俘或负债的个人。|  The laws governing nonfreemen were often harsh and restrictive.  管理非自由人的法律通常是严苛和限制性的。 |Synonyms: slave, serf, bondman, captive | Antonyms: freeman, citizen, liberator | noun
