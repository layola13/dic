nonreceipt:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈsiːt/
n.
未收到; 收不到
lack of receipt; failure to receive something.
The company apologized for the nonreceipt of my payment.  该公司为我没有收到付款而道歉。
近义词:non-arrival, absence, lack
反义词:receipt, receiving, arrival
词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + receipt.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "receipt" comes from the Old French "receite," meaning "a received payment."
