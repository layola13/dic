mannishnesses:/ˈmænɪʃnɪsɪz/
noun
男子气概；男性化特征
The quality of being mannish, especially in a woman; masculine characteristics.
She cultivated a certain mannishness in her dress and demeanor. 她刻意在穿着和举止上表现出某种男子气概。
masculinity, maleness
femininity, womanliness
