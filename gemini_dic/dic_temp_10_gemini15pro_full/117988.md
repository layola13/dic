memorialises:/məˈmɔːriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
纪念，缅怀
To preserve the memory of someone or something, especially with a monument or ceremony.
The statue memorialises the soldiers who died in the war. 这座雕像纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。
commemorates, remembers, celebrates
forgets, ignores
