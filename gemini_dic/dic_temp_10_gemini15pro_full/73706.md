forgoing:/fɔːrˈɡoʊɪŋ/
verb
放弃，摒弃
going without (something desirable).
She is forgoing sugar in her diet. 她正在节食期间戒糖。
近义词: abstaining from, refraining from, giving up, relinquishing, sacrificing
反义词: indulging in, partaking of, embracing, accepting
词性: verb (present participle of forgo)

词源:
From Middle English *forgon, from Old English forgān (“to forgo, relinquish, abandon”), from for- + gān (“to go”).  The modern spelling is influenced by the present participle.
