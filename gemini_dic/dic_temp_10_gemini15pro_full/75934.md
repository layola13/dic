fungilliform:/fʌŋˈdʒɪlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 菌状的，蘑菇形的 | Resembling a mushroom in shape. Having a stalk with a rounded cap. |  The cloud was fungilliform, billowing outwards at the top.  这朵云呈菌状，顶部向外膨胀。| The strange, fungilliform growth on the tree stump puzzled the botanist.  树桩上奇怪的菌状生长物让植物学家感到困惑。 |  mushroom-shaped, umbonate, pileate | - | adjective
