calcified:/ˈkæl.sɪ.faɪd/
calcified|adj.|钙化的；僵化的|Hardened by deposits of calcium salts.|例句：The calcified arteries restricted blood flow. 钙化的动脉限制了血液流动。|例句：His political views had become calcified over the years. 他的政治观点多年来变得僵化了。|近义词：hardened, rigid, inflexible, ossified|反义词：flexible, pliable, supple|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *calcificāre*, from *calc-, calx* "lime, limestone" + *-ficāre* "-fy". Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*ḱelh₂- “chalk, lime”.
