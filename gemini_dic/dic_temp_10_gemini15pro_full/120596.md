mirrorlike:/ˈmɪrərlaɪk/
adjective
像镜子的；光滑的
Resembling a mirror; smooth and reflective.
The lake was mirrorlike, reflecting the surrounding mountains.  湖面像镜子一样，倒映着周围的山峦。
The car had a mirrorlike finish.  这辆车有着像镜子一样的光洁度。
近义词:reflective, glassy, polished, smooth
反义词:dull, rough, matte, opaque
词性:adjective

词源:
From mirror +‎ -like.
