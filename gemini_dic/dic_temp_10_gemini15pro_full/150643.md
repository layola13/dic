papistly:/ˈpeɪpɪstli/ | adv. | 教皇式地；像天主教徒一样地；偏袒天主教地 | In a manner resembling or characteristic of a papist; with a bias towards Catholicism.  | He spoke papistly about the importance of the Pope. 他像天主教徒一样谈论教皇的重要性。 |  He papistly condemned the Reformation. 他以教皇信徒的口吻谴责宗教改革。 | 近义词: Catholically | 反义词: Protestantly | 词性: 副词

词源: From "papist" (a derogatory term for a Roman Catholic) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Papist" ultimately derives from "papa," meaning "pope."
