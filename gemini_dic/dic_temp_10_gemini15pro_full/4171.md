aircraftmen:/ˈɛəkrɑːftmən/| n. |飞机驾驶员；空勤人员|A member of an air force, especially one who is not a commissioned officer.  A skilled worker who builds or repairs aircraft. |例句：The aircraftmen were responsible for maintaining the fighter jets.  空勤人员负责维护战斗机。

例句：Several skilled aircraftmen worked tirelessly to repair the damaged wing.  几个熟练的飞机制造工人不知疲倦地修理受损的机翼。|近义词：airman, aviator, pilot (in some contexts)|反义词:civilian|Noun
