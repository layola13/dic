frames:/freɪmz/| n.|框架，边框，眼镜框；(电影的)画面，帧；(保龄球)局|A rigid structure that surrounds or encloses something.  The individual pictures on a strip of film, or the images displayed on a screen.  A complete game of bowling.|The picture was in a beautiful gilded frame. 这幅画装在一个漂亮的镀金画框里。

The film frames were blurred. 电影画面模糊不清。|synonyms: structures, skeletons, chassis, scaffolding, framework, casing, shell, housing,  pictures, images, shots, stills|antonyms:  content, substance|noun


frames:/freɪmz/| vt.|给…装框；陷害；表达；制定|To put a frame around something. To incriminate someone falsely. To express something in a particular way. To formulate or develop something.|She framed the photograph and hung it on the wall. 她给照片装上框，挂在墙上。

He was framed for a crime he didn't commit. 他被诬陷犯了他没有犯的罪。|synonyms: enclose, surround, border,  incriminate, set up,  express, articulate, formulate, devise, construct|antonyms:  exonerate, acquit,  distort, misrepresent, dismantle, deconstruct|verb
