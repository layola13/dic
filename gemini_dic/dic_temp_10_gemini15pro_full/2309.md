adequatenesses: /ˈædɪkwətnəsɪz/
noun
足够；充分性；适当性
The plural form of "adequateness," referring to the state or quality of being enough or suitable for a particular purpose.
The adequatenesses of the resources were questioned.  资源的充足性受到了质疑。
The report assessed the adequatenesses of the safety measures. 报告评估了安全措施的充分性。
近义词: sufficiencies, suitabilities, fitnesses
反义词: inadequacies, insufficiencies, unsuitabilities
词性: 名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From "adequate" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).
"Adequate" comes from the Latin "adaequatus," past participle of "adaequare" (to make equal), from "ad-" (to) + "aequus" (equal).
