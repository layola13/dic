decapitating:/dɪˈkæpɪteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
斩首的；砍头的
The act of cutting off someone's head.
The terrorist group released a video showing the decapitating of a hostage. 恐怖组织发布了一段斩首人质的视频。
近义词:beheading, guillotining
反义词:sparing, preserving
词性:adjective

decapitating:/dɪˈkæpɪteɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
斩首；砍头
The act of cutting off someone's head.
The executioner is decapitating the prisoner.  刽子手正在斩首囚犯。
近义词:beheading, guillotining
反义词:sparing, preserving
词性:verb

词源: From Late Latin decapitatus, past participle of decapitare "to behead," from Latin de- "off" + caput "head."
