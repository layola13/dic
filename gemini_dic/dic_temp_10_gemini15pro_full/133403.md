nonfaculty:/nɒnˈfækəlti/ | adj. n. |非教师的；非学院的；非学术人员；非教师职工| Not related to or consisting of the faculty of a college or university.  Referring to staff, employees, or personnel who are not part of the teaching or academic research staff. | The nonfaculty staff at the university plays a vital role in its smooth operation. 大学的非教师职工在其顺利运作中起着至关重要的作用。|  The university has a large nonfaculty workforce, including administrators and maintenance personnel. 这所大学有大量的非教师职工，包括行政人员和维修人员。 |synonyms: nonacademic, non-teaching, administrative, support staff | antonyms: faculty, academic, teaching | adjective, noun
