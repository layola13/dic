misintentions: /ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. | 恶意；不良意图 | Bad or malicious intentions. |  He apologized for his misintentions, claiming he had only wanted to help. 他为自己的恶意道歉，声称他只是想帮忙。

She suspected his misintentions from the start. 她从一开始就怀疑他不怀好意。| 近义词: malice, ill will, bad faith | 反义词: good intentions, goodwill, benevolence | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (bad, wrong) + "intentions" (purposes, aims). The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss." "Intention" comes from Old French "entencion" and ultimately from Latin "intentio" meaning "a stretching out, exertion, effort, attention."
