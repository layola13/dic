facer:/ˈfeɪsər/| n.| 难题，困境，令人尴尬的局面，(尤指拳击)迎面痛击|Something that is difficult or embarrassing to deal with, especially a hard blow to the face (in boxing).|He was given a real facer when his girlfriend dumped him. 他被女朋友甩了，这对他来说是一个沉重的打击。|近义词：problem, difficulty, challenge, setback, blow|反义词:advantage, benefit, boon|词性:noun

Etymology:
From the verb "face" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns from verbs).  Ultimately from Latin "facies" (face, appearance).
