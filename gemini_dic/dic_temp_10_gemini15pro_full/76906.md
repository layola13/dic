ganoid:/ˈɡeɪnɔɪd/| adj. n.|硬鳞鱼纲的；硬鳞鱼|Relating to or denoting a group of primitive ray-finned fishes characterized by thick scales with a shiny, enamel-like surface.|The gar is a ganoid fish. 雀鳝是一种硬鳞鱼。|近义词：bony, scaled|反义词: soft-scaled|词性:形容词, 名词
ganoid:/ˈɡeɪnɔɪd/| n.|硬鳞鱼|A ganoid fish.|Gars and sturgeons are ganoids. 雀鳝和鲟鱼都是硬鳞鱼。|近义词：gar, sturgeon|反义词: (No specific antonym for the noun form)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ganoïde*, from Greek *ganos* "brightness, sheen" + *-eidos* "-oid".
