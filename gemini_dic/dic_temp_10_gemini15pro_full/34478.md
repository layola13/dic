clayed:/kleɪd/
v. |用黏土处理|To cover or treat with clay.
The potter clayed the wheel before starting to work.|陶工在开始工作之前先用黏土处理了转盘。
近义词: coated, covered, daubed
反义词: cleaned, uncovered
词性:动词

clayed:/kleɪd/
adj. |黏土的，涂有黏土的|Covered or treated with clay.
The clayed surface of the sculpture felt smooth and cool.|雕塑的黏土表面摸起来光滑凉爽。
近义词: earthen, clayey
反义词: clean, bare
词性:形容词


词源: From the noun "clay" + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klaijo-, meaning "sticky earth."
