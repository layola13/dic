foreteller:/ˈfɔːrˌtelər/ (US), /fɔːˈtelə(r)/ (UK)| n. |预言者，预卜者|A person who foretells or predicts future events.|The ancient foreteller warned of a great catastrophe.|Synonym: prophet, soothsayer, diviner, oracle, seer|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps skeptic, doubter)|noun

foretell:/fɔːrˈtel/ (US), /fɔːˈtel/ (UK)| v. |预言，预示|To predict or indicate a future event.|The dark clouds foretell a storm.|Synonym: predict, forecast, prognosticate, augur, presage|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|verb
