depravedly:/dɪˈpreɪvɪdli/| adv.|堕落地；邪恶地；腐败地|In a morally corrupt or wicked manner.  Characterized by a deviation from what is considered right, proper, or good.|He laughed depravedly as he watched the chaos unfold. 他看着混乱的场面堕落地大笑。|Synonyms: wickedly, perversely, corruptly, sinfully, immorally|Antonyms: virtuously, righteously, morally, ethically|adverb
