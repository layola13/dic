myriagramme:/ˌmɪriəˈɡræm/ | n. | 万克，一万克 | A unit of mass equal to 10,000 grams (10 kilograms). |  A myriagramme is equivalent to about 22 pounds. 一万克相当于大约22磅。| synonym: myriagram, dekagram  | antonym:  milligram, microgram | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
