fretsaw:/ˈfrɛtsɔː/
n.
镂花锯，细锯
a fine-toothed saw used for cutting intricate curves
He used a fretsaw to create the delicate wooden ornament. 他用镂花锯制作了精致的木制装饰品。
synonyms: scroll saw, jigsaw, coping saw
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "hacksaw" for a coarser saw)
noun

fretsaw:/ˈfrɛtsɔː/
v.
用镂花锯切割
to cut with a fretsaw
She fretsawed a beautiful design into the wood.  她用镂花锯在木头上切割出一个美丽的图案。
synonyms: saw, cut
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "join," "assemble")
verb
