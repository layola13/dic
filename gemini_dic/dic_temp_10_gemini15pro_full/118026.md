Memphises: /ˈmɛmfɪsiːz/
n.
孟菲斯（地名，通常指美国田纳西州的孟菲斯市，也可指埃及古都孟菲斯）
Memphis, a city in southwestern Tennessee, is known for its rich musical history, especially blues and rock 'n' roll. It is also the location of Graceland, Elvis Presley's former home.  The ancient city of Memphis in Egypt was the capital of the Old Kingdom.
例句：I visited Memphis last summer and had a great time exploring Beale Street.  去年夏天我去了孟菲斯，在比尔街玩得很开心。

例句：Ancient Memphis was a significant center of power and culture. 古代孟菲斯是一个重要的权力和文化中心。
近义词：(For the US city) Nashville, New Orleans (similar musical hubs). (For the ancient city) Thebes, Heliopolis (other ancient Egyptian cities).
反义词: None (Place names typically lack antonyms).
词性: Noun

Etymology:
(US city) Named after Memphis, Egypt.
(Egypt) From Egyptian *mn-nfr, meaning "established and beautiful" or "enduring and beautiful".
