impecuniousness:/ˌɪmpɪˈkjuːniəsnəs/

|近义词：poverty, penury, indigence, destitution|
|反义词：wealth, affluence, prosperity, riches|
|词性：名词|


impecunious:/ˌɪmpɪˈkjuːniəs/
|adj.|贫穷的，没钱的|Having little or no money.|He was an impecunious student, relying on scholarships and part-time jobs. 他是一个贫穷的学生，依靠奖学金和兼职工作。|
|近义词：poor, penniless, needy, destitute|
|反义词：wealthy, affluent, prosperous, rich|
