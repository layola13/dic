gargantuanly:/ɡɑːrˈɡæn.tʃu.ən.li/

巨大地，庞大地
in a gargantuan manner; to a gargantuan degree.
He gargantuanly devoured the entire roast chicken. 他狼吞虎咽地吃掉了整只烤鸡。
近义词: enormously, immensely, hugely, massively
反义词: minutely, slightly, insignificantly
词性: 副词

gargantuan:/ɡɑːrˈɡæn.tʃu.ən/
adj.
巨大的，庞大的
of immense size or scale.
They had a gargantuan task ahead of them. 他们面前有一项艰巨的任务。
近义词: enormous, immense, huge, massive
反义词: tiny, small, minute
词性: 形容词
