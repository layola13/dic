concerted:/kənˈsɜːrtɪd/ /kɑːnˈsɜːrtɪd/| adj.|协调一致的，共同筹划的|Planned or performed in cooperation; done together.|The team made a concerted effort to win the game. 团队齐心协力赢得了比赛。|近义词：joint, collaborative, cooperative, combined, united, collective|反义词：individual, separate, uncoordinated, independent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the Late Latin *concertāre*, from *con-* (together) + *certāre* (to contend, strive).  Originally meaning "to settle by dispute," the meaning shifted to "to arrange by mutual agreement" and then to "perform together."
