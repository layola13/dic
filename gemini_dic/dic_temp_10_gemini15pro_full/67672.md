extrusile:/ɪkˈstruːsaɪl/ /ɪkˈstruːsɪl/
adjective
可伸出的;能突出的
capable of being extruded; capable of being thrust out or pushed forward.
The snail's extrusile antennae waved back and forth.  蜗牛的可伸出触角来回摆动。
近义词:protrusible, extensible
反义词:retractile, non-protrusible
词性:adjective

extrusile:/ɪkˈstruːsaɪl/ /ɪkˈstruːsɪl/
noun
可伸出器官;突出物
An organ or part that can be extruded.
The chameleon's tongue is a remarkable extrusile. 变色龙的舌头是一个非凡的可伸出器官。
近义词:protrusion, extension
反义词:retraction
词性:noun
