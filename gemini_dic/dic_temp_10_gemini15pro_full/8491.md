antibillous:/ˌæntibaɪliəs/
adjective
抗胆汁的；预防胆汁过多的；治疗胆汁病的
Preventing or relieving biliousness; counteracting excessive secretion of bile.
She took an antibillous medicine after feeling nauseous. 她感到恶心后服用了抗胆汁药。
The old recipe called for several antibillous herbs.  这古老的药方需要几种抗胆汁的草药。
近义词:choleretic, cholagogue (these relate to bile production/flow but not necessarily in a negative context)
反义词:N/A (There isn't a direct opposite for reducing bile.  Words like "choleretic" might be considered contextually opposite if they *increase* bile production)
词性:adjective
