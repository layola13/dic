codified:/ˈkoʊdɪfaɪd/| adj.|编纂的，成文的|arranged systematically into a code; especially : reduced to a code|The codified laws of the land are available for all to see.  这片土地上编纂的法律人人可见。|The rules of the game were codified in 1863.  这项运动的规则在1863年被编纂成册。|近义词：systematized, organized, formulated|反义词:unwritten, informal|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin codificare, from Latin codex (book, code) + -ficare (to make).
