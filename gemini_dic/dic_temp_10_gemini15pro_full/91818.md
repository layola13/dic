hyperfunctioning:/ˌhaɪpərˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/
adjective
机能亢进的，功能过强的
Characterized by excessive or abnormally increased function.
The patient's hyperfunctioning thyroid was causing a rapid heart rate.  病人甲状腺功能亢进导致心率加快。
近义词: overactive, hyperactive
反义词: hypofunctioning, underactive
词性: adjective

词源:
From hyper- + functioning, from function + -ing.  "Hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" meaning "over, above, beyond." "Function" comes from Latin "functio(n-)" meaning "performance, execution."
