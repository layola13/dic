词源: From Middle French *muser "to ponder," of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old French muse "muzzle, snout," from Late Latin *musa.  The meaning shifted from "loiter, dream" to "ponder."  The connection to the Muses (from Greek Mousa) may be a folk etymology.
