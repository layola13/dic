dubiously:/ˈdjuːbiəsli/ /ˈduːbiəsli/| adv.|怀疑地，可疑地|in a way that shows you are not certain about something and suspect that something may be wrong.|He looked at her dubiously. 他怀疑地看着她。|近义词：doubtfully, skeptically, uncertainly|反义词：certainly, confidently, definitely|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *dubiosus*, from Latin dubius (“doubtful”) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
