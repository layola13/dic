outtravel: /ˌaʊtˈtrævl/
verb
超越旅行；比…旅行更多更远
to travel further or more extensively than someone or something else.
He outtravelled all his companions. 他比所有同伴都走得更远。
近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip
反义词: lag behind
词性: verb
