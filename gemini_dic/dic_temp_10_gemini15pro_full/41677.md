correspondences:/ˌkɔːrɪˈspɒndənsɪz/ (UK), /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənsɪz/ (US)
|n.|通信，信件，一致，符合|Communication by exchanging letters with someone; similarity or analogy between things.|例句：She maintained a regular correspondence with her family while she was abroad.  她在国外时与家人保持定期通信。|近义词：communication, letters, emails, agreement, conformity, similarity|反义词：disagreement, difference, disparity|词性:名词
