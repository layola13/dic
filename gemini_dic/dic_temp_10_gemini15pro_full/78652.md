giddily:/ˈɡɪdɪli/| adv.|轻率地，头晕地|In a lightheaded or dizzy way, often from excitement or happiness.|She danced giddily around the room after hearing the good news. 她听到好消息后，在房间里晕头转向地跳着舞。|近义词：lightheadedly, dizzyingly, frivolously|反义词：seriously, solemnly, soberly|词性:副词
Etymology: From giddy + -ly.  Giddy itself comes from Old English *gydig, meaning "possessed by a god, insane," related to god.
