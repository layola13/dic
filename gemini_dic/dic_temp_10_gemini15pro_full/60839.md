electrosurgical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈsɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
电外科的
Relating to or used in electrosurgery.  Utilizing high-frequency electric current to cut or coagulate tissue.
The surgeon used an electrosurgical unit to remove the mole.  外科医生使用电外科装置切除了痣。
electrosurgical pencil: The surgeon carefully used the electrosurgical pencil to cauterize the blood vessel. 外科医生小心地使用电外科笔烧灼血管。
Synonyms:electrocautery
Antonyms:None
词性:adjective
