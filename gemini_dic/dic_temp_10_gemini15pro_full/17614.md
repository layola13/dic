beaverlike:/ˈbiːvərlaɪk/| adj.|像河狸的，勤勉的|Resembling a beaver in appearance, habits, or industriousness.  This word often refers to someone who works tirelessly, much like a beaver building its dam.|The children worked beaverlike, building a magnificent sandcastle.|近义词：industrious, diligent, assiduous, hardworking|反义词：lazy, indolent, slothful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From beaver +‎ -like.
