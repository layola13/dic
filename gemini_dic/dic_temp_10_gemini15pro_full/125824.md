murnival:/ˈmɜːrnɪvəl/| n.|一组四个人; 四人一组|A company or set of four persons.  It originally referred to a group of four knights chosen by lot to decide a trial by combat. |The king chose a murnival of knights to judge the dispute. 国王选择了一组四名骑士来裁决这场争端。| synonym: quartet, foursome | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "individual" or "singleton" in contrast)| noun
