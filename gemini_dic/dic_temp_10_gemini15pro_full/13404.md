atomist:/ˈætəmɪst/| n.|原子论者; 原子学家|A person who believes in or specializes in atomism (the theory that all matter is made up of tiny indivisible particles called atoms).|例句：The atomist argued that the universe was composed entirely of atoms and void. 原子论者认为宇宙完全由原子和虚空组成。

例句：Democritus was a prominent atomist in ancient Greece. 德谟克利特是古希腊著名的原子论者。|近义词：physicist, scientist, materialist|反义词：holist|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Modern Latin *atomismus*, from Greek *atomos* "indivisible" + *-ismos*.
