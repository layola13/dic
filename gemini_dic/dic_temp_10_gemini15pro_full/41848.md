corymbiferous:/ˌkɔːrɪmˈbɪfərəs/
adjective
伞房花序的，开伞房花序的
Bearing corymbs; producing flowers in corymbs.
The corymbiferous plant produced a beautiful display of white flowers.  这种伞房花序植物开出了一片美丽的白色花朵。
The gardener cultivated several corymbiferous shrubs in the border. 园丁在花坛里种植了几种伞房花序的灌木。
Synonyms: corymbose
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider plants with different inflorescence types like racemose or cymose)
词性: adjective
