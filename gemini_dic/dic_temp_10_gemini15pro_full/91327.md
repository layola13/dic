hydrophobic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪdrəˈfɒbɪk/ (UK)| adj.|疏水的，憎水的|Tending to repel or fail to mix with water.|Oil is hydrophobic and does not mix with water. 油是疏水的，不会与水混合。|The lotus leaf's hydrophobic surface causes water to bead up and roll off. 荷叶的疏水表面使水珠聚集并滚落。|近义词:water-repellent, nonpolar|反义词:hydrophilic, polar|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hydro- (water) + -phobic (fearing), from Greek *hudrophobos (water-fearing), from hudōr (water) + phobos (fear).
