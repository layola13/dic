crewless:/ˈkruːləs/
adjective
无人驾驶的；无船员的
Without a crew; operating without human personnel.
例句：The company is developing a crewless cargo ship.  这家公司正在开发无人驾驶货船。

例句：Crewless aircraft are becoming increasingly common. 无人驾驶飞机正变得越来越普遍。

近义词：unmanned, autonomous, robotic
反义词：crewed, manned
词性: adjective
