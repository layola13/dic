hushed:/hʌʃt/ | adj. |安静的，寂静的|Characterized by quiet and stillness.|The hushed tones of the librarian created a peaceful atmosphere. 图书管理员安静的语气营造了一种宁静的氛围。|例句：The room fell hushed as the speaker began. 演讲者开始讲话时，房间里一片寂静。|近义词：quiet, silent, still, muted|反义词：loud, noisy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "hush," which comes from Middle English *huschen*, probably of imitative origin.
