pained:/peɪnd/| adj.|痛苦的，感到疼痛的；（表情）显露出痛苦的|experiencing or expressing physical or emotional pain|例句：He had a pained expression on his face. 他脸上露出痛苦的表情。|例句：She gave a pained smile. 她勉强地笑了笑。|近义词：hurting, aching, distressed, anguished|反义词：comfortable, happy, relieved|词性:形容词
词源：From Old French *peiné*, past participle of *peiner* (“to trouble, afflict, punish”), from Latin *poena* (“punishment, penalty”).
