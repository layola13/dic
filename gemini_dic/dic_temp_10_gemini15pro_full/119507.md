microphthalmia:/ˌmaɪkroʊfˈθælmiə/| n. |小眼症|A developmental disorder characterized by abnormally small eyes.|Microphthalmia can be caused by genetic mutations. 小眼症可能是由基因突变引起的。|Synonyms: micropsia|Antonyms: macrophthalmia|noun
