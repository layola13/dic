craic:/kræk/
noun
乐趣; 欢乐; 美好时光;  爱尔兰俚语，尤用于爱尔兰共和国
fun and entertainment, especially good conversation and company:
例句：We had great craic at the party last night. 昨晚的派对我们玩得很开心。
例句：The craic was mighty that night. 那晚我们玩得非常尽兴。
近义词：fun, enjoyment, banter, amusement
反义词：boredom, dullness
词性:n.


craic:/kræk/
noun
新闻; 消息; 八卦
news, gossip, or information of any kind:
例句：What's the craic? 有什么新鲜事吗？
例句：Any craic on the match?  比赛有什么消息吗？
近义词：news, gossip, information, lowdown
反义词：secrecy
词性:n.


词源:
From Irish *craic* (“fun, enjoyment, amusement, news, gossip, chat”), of uncertain origin. Possibly from Middle English *crake* (“loud noise, boast”), perhaps related to crack.
