necessities:/nəˈsesɪtiz/| n.|必需品|Things that are essential or indispensable for life or a particular purpose.|例句：Food, water, and shelter are basic necessities.  食物、水和住所是基本必需品。

例句：Education is one of the necessities of modern life. 教育是现代生活的必需品之一。|近义词：essentials, requisites, requirements|反义词：luxuries, nonessentials|Noun
