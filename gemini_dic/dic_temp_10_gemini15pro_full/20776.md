blanketers:/ˈblæŋkɪtərz/
n.
覆盖者；用毯子盖的人
People who cover something or someone with blankets.
The blanketers carefully covered the sleeping child.  覆盖者小心翼翼地盖住了熟睡的孩子。
The volunteers acted as blanketers for the homeless people sheltering in the park. 志愿者们充当了公园里无家可归者的覆盖者。
近义词:coverers
反义词:exposers
词性:名词


Etymology:
From the verb "blanket" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting agents). "Blanket" comes from the Old French "blankete," a diminutive of "blanc" meaning "white."  Originally referring to white woolen cloth, the term broadened to include any type of bed covering.
