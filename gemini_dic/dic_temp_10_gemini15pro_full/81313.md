grapeless:/ˈɡreɪplɪs/| adj.|无葡萄的；没有葡萄的|Lacking grapes; without grapes.  Derived from "grape" + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).|The grapeless vine yielded no fruit this year. 今年这棵没有葡萄的藤蔓没有结出果实。|Synonyms: fruitless, barren, unproductive|Antonyms: fruitful, grape-bearing, productive|adjective
