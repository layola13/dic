beeped:/biːpt/ | v. | 发出哔哔声；已发出哔哔声 | to make a short, high-pitched sound, typically of an electronic device | The microwave beeped to signal that the food was ready. 微波炉发出哔哔声，表示食物已经准备好了。|  The alarm beeped loudly. 警报器大声地响了起来。 |近义词：chirped, peeped, bleeped | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 silenced, muted) | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the sound itself, imitative.  It became common in the mid-20th century with the rise of electronic devices.
