bearing-down:/ˈbɛərɪŋ daʊn/
adjective
下压的，施加压力的
Exerting downward pressure; approaching with force or intensity.
The bearing-down pain during labor was intense. 分娩时的下坠疼痛非常剧烈。
The bearing-down train was a terrifying sight.  快速驶近的火车景象令人恐惧。
近义词:pressing, descending, overwhelming
反义词:lifting, ascending, receding
词性:adjective

bearing-down:/ˈbɛərɪŋ daʊn/
noun
下压力，下坠
Downward pressure; the act of exerting downward pressure.
The bearing-down of the weight caused the structure to collapse. 重量的下压导致结构倒塌。
(This usage as a noun is less common than the adjectival form.)
近义词:pressure, weight, force
反义词:lift, buoyancy
词性:noun


词源: From the verb "bear down," which combines "bear" (carry, support, endure) and "down" (toward a lower place).  The verb "bear" has its roots in Old English "beran," while "down" comes from Old English "dūne."
