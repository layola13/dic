nonexclusion:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈskluːʒən/ | n. |非排他性，非排斥性|The principle of nonexclusion means that one person's use of a public good does not prevent another person from using it. |Public parks operate on a principle of nonexclusion, meaning anyone can enter. 公共公园的运营原则是 nonexclusion，意味着任何人都可以进入。|近义词：inclusion, admittance|反义词：exclusion, restriction|词性:名词

Etymology->
From non- + exclusion.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Exclusion" comes from the Latin "exclusio," meaning "a shutting out."
