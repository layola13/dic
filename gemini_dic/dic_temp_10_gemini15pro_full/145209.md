overbark:/ˌoʊvərˈbɑːrk/| v. |过度吠叫；吠叫盖过（其他声音）|To bark excessively or to bark louder than another sound.|The small dog overbarked the larger one, trying to assert dominance.  这只小狗对着大狗狂吠，试图维护自己的统治地位。

The hounds overbarked when they caught the scent of the fox. 当猎犬闻到狐狸的气味时，它们开始狂吠不止。|近义词：outbark|反义词: whimper, whine|词性:动词

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "bark" (Old English beorcan, of imitative origin).  The prefix "over-" indicates excess or surpassing, hence "to bark excessively."
