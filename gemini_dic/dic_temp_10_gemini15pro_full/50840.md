desecraters:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtərz/
n.
亵渎者，污损圣物者
Those who treat sacred things with disrespect; people who violate or profane something holy or highly valued.
The desecraters of the ancient temple were condemned by all.  那些亵渎古代寺庙的人受到了所有人的谴责。
The vandals were desecraters, spraying graffiti on the war memorial.  这些破坏者是亵渎者，在战争纪念碑上喷涂涂鸦。
synonyms: vandals, profanes, violators, defilers
antonyms: protectors, guardians, reverers, venerators
