concave:/ˈkɒnkeɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnkeɪv/ (US)| adj.|凹的，凹面的|Curving inwards, like the inner surface of a bowl.|The telescope uses a concave mirror to gather light.|近义词：hollow, indented, recessed|反义词:convex|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *concavus, from Latin con- (“together, with”) + cavus (“hollow”).
