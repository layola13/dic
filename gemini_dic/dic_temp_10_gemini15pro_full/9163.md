antinephritic:/ˌæntiːnɪˈfrɪtɪk/
adjective
抗肾炎的;治疗肾炎的
Relieving or preventing nephritis.
The doctor prescribed antinephritic medication for the patient's kidney inflammation. 医生给病人开了抗肾炎药来治疗肾脏炎症。
近义词:  renal-protective, nephroprotective
反义词:  nephrotoxic
词性: adjective
词源:  anti- (against) + nephr- (kidney) + -itic (relating to inflammation)

antinephritic:/ˌæntiːnɪˈfrɪtɪk/
noun
抗肾炎药;治疗肾炎的药物
A medicine that relieves or prevents nephritis.
The antinephritic helped reduce the patient's proteinuria.  这种抗肾炎药帮助减少了病人的蛋白尿。
近义词:  renal-protective agent, nephroprotective drug
反义词:  nephrotoxin
词性: noun
词源:  anti- (against) + nephr- (kidney) + -itic (relating to inflammation)
