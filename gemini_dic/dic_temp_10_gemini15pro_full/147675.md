overpower:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/| vt.|制服，压倒，使无法抵抗|To defeat or subdue by superior force; to overwhelm.|The army overpowered the rebels.  军队镇压了叛乱分子。|The smell of the lilies overpowered me. 百合花的香味让我无法忍受。|近义词：overwhelm, conquer, subdue, vanquish, defeat|反义词：yield, surrender, submit|动词

overpower:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/| adj.|压倒性的，不可抗拒的|Excessively strong or intense; overwhelming.|The heat was overpowering.  酷热难耐。|His grief was overpowering. 他的悲伤难以抑制。|近义词：overwhelming, intense, potent|反义词：weak, mild|形容词 (较少使用)
