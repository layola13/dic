healthily:/ˈhɛlθɪli/| adv.|健康地，有益健康地|In a healthy manner; in a way that promotes good health.  It describes actions or behaviors that contribute to good health.|She eats healthily and exercises regularly. 她健康饮食并规律锻炼。|Synonyms: wholesomely, nutritiously, hygienically|Antonyms: unhealthily, poorly, harmfully|词性:副词
