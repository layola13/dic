/kənˈdɪʃən/ |/kənˈdɪʃn/| n. |条件，状况，状态|A state of being; circumstances affecting the way in which people live or work.|The car is in good condition. 这辆车的车况很好。|Synonyms: state, situation, circumstance, status|Antonyms: disorder, disrepair|noun

/kənˈdɪʃən/ |/kənˈdɪʃn/| vt. |使适应；训练；使习惯于；决定，制约|To accustom to or train in some action or response; to have a determining influence on.|Pavlov conditioned his dogs to salivate at the sound of a bell. 巴甫洛夫训练他的狗一听到铃声就流口水。|Synonyms: train, accustom, adapt, habituate|Antonyms: decondition, unlearn|verb
