pantomimically:/ˌpæntəˈmɪmɪkli/| adv.|以哑剧的方式，以动作表演|Using only gestures and facial expressions, without speaking, like in a pantomime. |She explained the situation pantomimically, as the music was too loud to talk. 她用手势和表情解释了情况，因为音乐太吵了，无法说话。|Example: He pantomimically conveyed his need for water. 他以哑剧的方式表达了他对水的需求。|Synonyms: silently, wordlessly, gesturally|Antonyms: verbally, vocally|Part of Speech: Adverb


Etymology->
From "pantomime" + "-ically."  "Pantomime" comes from Greek "pantomimos" (all-imitating), from "pan-" (all) + "mimos" (imitator, actor).
