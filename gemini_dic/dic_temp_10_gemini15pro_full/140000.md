odysseys:/ˈɒdəsiz/ (UK) /ˈɑːdəsiz/ (US)| n. |奥德赛; 长途冒险旅程; 精神探索; |A long and eventful or adventurous journey or experience.  A spiritual quest. Derived from Homer's epic poem, "The Odyssey," about the long journey home of Odysseus after the Trojan War. |例句：His career has been a real odyssey. 他的职业生涯是一次真正的漫长冒险。

例句：The refugee's journey across the border was a harrowing odyssey.  难民跨越边境的旅程是一次痛苦的艰辛历程。|近义词：journey, voyage, trek, expedition, quest|反义词:stay, idleness, inaction|词性:noun
