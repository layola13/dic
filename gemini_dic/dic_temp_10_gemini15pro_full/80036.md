go-up-in-smoke:/ɡoʊ ʌp ɪn smoʊk/|idiomatic expression|化为乌有，付之一炬，泡汤|To be destroyed or ruined completely, often suddenly and unexpectedly, as if consumed by fire.  It often refers to plans, hopes, or investments.|例句：His dreams of owning a business went up in smoke after the economic downturn.  他拥有自己企业的梦想在经济衰退后化为乌有。

例句：All her hard work went up in smoke when the computer crashed. 当电脑崩溃时，她所有的努力都付诸东流了。|近义词：fall through, fail, collapse, be ruined, come to nothing|反义词：succeed, thrive, flourish|phrasal verb
