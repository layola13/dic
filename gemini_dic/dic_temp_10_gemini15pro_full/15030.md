baboonish:/bəˈbuːnɪʃ/ | adj. | 狒狒似的；粗野的；愚蠢的 | Resembling or characteristic of a baboon, especially in being coarse, rough, or bad-tempered. |  He had a baboonish grin on his face after winning the prank war.  他赢了恶作剧大战后，脸上露出了狒狒似的笑容。|  The baboonish behavior of the soccer hooligans was appalling. 足球迷的粗野行为令人震惊。 | 近义词: apelike, brutish, coarse, vulgar, uncivilized | 反义词: civilized, refined, polite, courteous | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From baboon + -ish.  Baboon comes from Old French babouin.
