grammars:/ˈɡræmərz/ | n. | 语法 | The set of rules that govern the structure of a language, including syntax and morphology. |  She is studying English grammars. 她正在学习英语语法。|  syntax, morphology | vocabulary | noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French gramaire, via Latin from Greek grammatikē (tekhnē) ‘(art) of letters’, from gramma, grammat- ‘letter of the alphabet’.
