newsworthiness:/ˈnjuːzwɜːrðinəs/ /ˈnuːzwɜːrðinəs/| n. |新闻价值；报道价值|The newsworthiness of an event is determined by several factors.  一个事件的新闻价值是由几个因素决定的。|Examples: The newsworthiness of the story was debated by the editors.  这个故事的新闻价值受到了编辑们的争论。  The local paper questioned the newsworthiness of the mayor's cat getting stuck in a tree. 地方报纸质疑了市长的猫被困在树上的新闻价值。|近义词：news value, reportability|反义词: insignificance, triviality|词性:noun
