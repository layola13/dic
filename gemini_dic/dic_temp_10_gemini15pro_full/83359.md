gyrfalcon:/ˈdʒɜːrfɔːlkən/, /ˈdʒaɪrfɔːlkən/| n. |矛隼，猎鹰|A large, powerful falcon (Falco rusticolus) found in Arctic regions, the largest of all falcons.|例句：The gyrfalcon is a prized bird in falconry. 矛隼是猎鹰中珍贵的品种。|例句：The gyrfalcon plummeted from the sky, snatching its prey. 矛隼从天而降，抓住了它的猎物。|近义词：falcon, hawk|反义词:  (没有确切的反义词，可以考虑prey猎物)|词性:noun
