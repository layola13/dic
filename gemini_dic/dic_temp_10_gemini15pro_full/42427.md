counterculturalist:/ˌkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | n. | 反文化者 | A person who opposes the dominant values and norms of society. |  He was a counterculturalist in the 1960s, rejecting mainstream values and embracing alternative lifestyles.  他是一位60年代的反文化者，拒绝主流价值观，拥抱另类生活方式。| Synonyms: nonconformist, rebel, dissident | Antonyms: conformist, traditionalist | noun

counterculturalist:/ˌkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ | adj. | 反文化的 | Relating to or characteristic of a counterculture. |  His counterculturalist views often put him at odds with his conservative family. 他的反文化观点经常使他与保守的家庭格格不入。 | Synonyms: unconventional, alternative, bohemian | Antonyms: conventional, mainstream, traditional | adjective
