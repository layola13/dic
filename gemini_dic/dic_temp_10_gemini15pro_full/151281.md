parfleche:/pɑːrˈflɛʃ/ |n.| 干肉；肉干 |Dried meat, especially buffalo meat. |They packed parfleche and pemmican for their long journey. 他们为长途旅行打包了干肉和肉干粉。| synonyms: jerky, dried meat | antonyms: fresh meat | noun
