epicondylar: /ˌɛpɪˈkɒndɪlə(r)/ /ˌɛpɪˈkɑːndɪlər/ | adj. | 肱骨外上髁的; 肱骨内上髁的 | Located on or above a condyle (a rounded protuberance at the end of a bone, especially the femur or humerus).  Specifically, it usually refers to the area around the medial or lateral epicondyle of the humerus. | The tennis player suffered from epicondylar pain.  这位网球运动员患有肱骨外上髁疼痛。

epicondylar: /ˌɛpɪˈkɒndɪlə(r)/ /ˌɛpɪˈkɑːndɪlər/ | n. | 肱骨外上髁; 肱骨内上髁 |  An epicondyle (the bony prominence) itself.  |  He fractured his lateral epicondylar. 他肱骨外上髁骨折了。
