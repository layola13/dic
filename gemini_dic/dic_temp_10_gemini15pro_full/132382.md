nondemise:/ˌnɒnˈdɪˈmaɪz/ | adj. | 非租赁终止的，非转租的 |  Relating to a lease that does not terminate upon the death of the lessee or lessor.  It indicates that the lease continues despite the death of either party involved. |  The nondemise clause in the contract ensures the business can continue operating even if the owner passes away.  该合同中的非租赁终止条款确保即使业主去世，企业也能继续运营。 |  nonterminable, continuing | terminable | adjective
