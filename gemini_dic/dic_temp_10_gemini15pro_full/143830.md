outpeoples:/ˌaʊtˈpiːpəlz/
|v. |超越，胜过（某人群）; 在人数上超过; | to surpass a people or group of people in number or achievement. |The newcomers outpeopled the indigenous population within a few generations.  新来的人在几代之内就超过了土著人口的数量。|
|v. |迫使…迁出; | to force (a people or group of people) to leave a place. |The invading army outpeopled the villages, forcing the inhabitants to flee. 入侵的军队迫使村民们逃离，使村庄变得人烟稀少。|
|近义词：outnumber, surpass, exceed, displace|
|反义词：be outnumbered by|
|词性：动词|
