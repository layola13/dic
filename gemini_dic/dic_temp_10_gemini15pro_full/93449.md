illumines:/ɪˈluːmɪnz/
verb
照亮；启发
lights up; makes clear
The full moon illumines the night sky.  满月照亮了夜空。
Her smile illumined her face. 她的笑容照亮了她的脸庞。
Synonyms: brightens, illuminates, enlightens
Antonyms: darkens, obscures
词性: 动词
