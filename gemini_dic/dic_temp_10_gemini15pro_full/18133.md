behoovable:/bɪˈhuːvəbl/
adjective
适当的，合适的，有益的
Suitable, proper, or beneficial; fitting or appropriate.
It is behoovable for a student to study hard.  对于学生来说，努力学习是应该的。
It is behoovable for the government to address the concerns of its citizens. 政府有必要处理公民的担忧。
近义词: fitting, appropriate, proper, suitable, seemly, decent, decorous
反义词: inappropriate, unsuitable, improper, unbecoming, indecent, indecorous
词性: adjective
词源: From behoove + -able. Behoove comes from Old English *behōfian "to have need of, require," from Proto-Germanic *bihōfjaną "to be useful, have use for," from *bihōfaz "use, advantage."
