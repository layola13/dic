broomy:/ˈbruːmi/| adj.|多金雀花的，似金雀花的|Covered with or resembling broom; abounding in broom. |The hillside was broomy and bright yellow in spring. 春天，山坡上长满了金雀花，一片金黄。|近义词：gorse-covered, furze-covered|反义词: barren, bare|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From broom + -y.  "Broom" itself comes from Old English *brōm, referring to various shrubs.
