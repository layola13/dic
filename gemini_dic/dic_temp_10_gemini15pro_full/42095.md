cotransfers:/ˈkoʊˌtrænsˈfɜːrz/| n.|共同转移，协同转移|The cotransfer of genes is a crucial aspect of bacterial evolution. 基因的共同转移是细菌进化的一个重要方面。|This study investigates the cotransfer of plasmids between bacterial species.  这项研究调查了细菌种类之间质粒的共同转移。|近义词：joint transfer, simultaneous transfer|反义词: independent transfer|词性:noun


Etymology: co- (prefix meaning "together") + transfer (from Latin "transferre" - to carry across)
