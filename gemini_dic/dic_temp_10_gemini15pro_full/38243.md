computerized:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzd/
adj. |计算机化的，电脑化的| Operated or controlled by computer. |例句：The factory is now fully computerized.  这家工厂现在完全计算机化了。|例句：Many tasks in offices are now computerized. 办公室里的许多工作现在都已计算机化。|近义词：automated, digital, electronic|反义词：manual, analog|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From computer + -ize + -ed.  "Computer" itself originates from the Latin "computare" meaning "to calculate, sum up, compute." "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or cause to become" and "-ed" is a suffix used to form the past participle of verbs and also adjectives derived from verbs.
