menially:/ˈmiːniəli/ | adv. | 卑贱地；仆人般地；乏味地 |  In a servile or subservient manner; performing uninteresting, routine tasks. | He worked menially in the kitchen, chopping vegetables for hours. 他在厨房里做着卑贱的工作，连续几个小时切菜。| Example: She felt she was being treated menially, constantly being asked to fetch coffee and run errands. 她觉得她被当作仆人对待，不断地被要求去取咖啡和跑腿。 | Synonyms: servilely, abjectly, slavishly, humbly | Antonyms: nobly, grandly, importantly | 词性: 副词
