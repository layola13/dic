agues:/ˈeɪɡjuːz/ | n. |疟疾；寒战|A recurring bout of chills and fever, often associated with malaria. | He suffered from recurring agues after his trip to the tropics. 他去热带地区旅行后，反复发作疟疾。| 近义词: chills, fever, malaria, rigors | 反义词: health, wellness | 词性: 名词

agues: /ˈeɪɡjuːz/ | n. | (古语)极度痛苦;焦虑 | Extreme suffering; anguish. |  He endured the agues of despair after losing his fortune.  他失去财富后忍受着绝望的煎熬。 | 近义词: anguish, torment, distress, suffering | 反义词: comfort, solace, relief | 词性: 名词


词源: From Middle English *agues*, plural of *ague*, from Old French *aguë*, feminine of *agu*, from Latin *acūta (febris)*, "acute (fever)".
