cataclysmic:/ˌkætəˈklɪzmɪk/
adjective
灾难性的，大变动的
Relating to or denoting a violent natural event.
例句：The earthquake was a cataclysmic event that destroyed the entire city. 地震是一场摧毁了整个城市的灾难性事件。

例句：The stock market crash had a cataclysmic effect on the global economy. 股市崩盘对全球经济造成了灾难性的影响。

近义词：disastrous, catastrophic, devastating, ruinous
反义词：beneficial, advantageous, fortunate, auspicious
词性：adjective
