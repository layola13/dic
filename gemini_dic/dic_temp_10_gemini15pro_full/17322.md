be at someone:/bi æt ˈsʌmwʌn/ | phrasal verb | 纠缠某人，烦扰某人；不断批评或指责某人 | To persistently bother, nag, or criticize someone. | He's always been at me about my weight. 他总是对我念叨我的体重。| 近义词：nag, harass, pester, bother | 反义词: praise, commend | 词性: 动词短语

例句：
She's been at him all day to fix the leaky faucet.
她整天缠着他让他修理漏水的水龙头。

He's been at his kids about their messy rooms.
他一直在批评他的孩子们房间很乱。
