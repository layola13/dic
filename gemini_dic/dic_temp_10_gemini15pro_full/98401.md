insolvency:/ɪnˈsɒlvənsi/ /ɪnˈsɑːlvənsi/| n.|无力偿还; 破产; 资不抵债|The state of being unable to pay one's debts.|The company filed for insolvency after several years of losses.  这家公司在连年亏损后申请破产。|近义词：bankruptcy, failure, ruin|反义词：solvency, affluence|词性:名词

insolvent:/ɪnˈsɒlvənt/ /ɪnˈsɑːlvənt/| adj.|无力偿还的; 破产的|Unable to pay debts.|The insolvent company was forced to liquidate its assets. 这家无力偿还债务的公司被迫清算资产。|近义词：bankrupt, broke, ruined|反义词：solvent, wealthy|词性:形容词
