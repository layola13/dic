broaden:/ˈbrɔːdn/ (UK) /ˈbrɑːdn/ (US)| v. |拓宽，扩大，扩展|To make something wider or more extensive.|Travel can broaden your horizons. 旅行可以拓宽你的视野。|近义词：expand, widen, enlarge, extend|反义词：narrow, restrict, limit|词性:动词
Etymology: From broad (wide) + -en (suffix forming verbs of making or becoming).  Originating from Old English "brād" (wide).
