huts:/hʌts/| n. |小屋，棚屋|A small, simple dwelling, often made of wood or other natural materials.|The shepherds lived in huts in the mountains. 牧羊人住在山里的小屋里。|synonyms: cabins, sheds, shacks|antonyms: mansions, palaces|noun

huts:/hʌts/| n. |临时住所|A temporary shelter.|They built huts to protect themselves from the storm. 他们建造小屋来保护自己免受暴风雨的侵袭。|synonyms: shelters, lean-tos|antonyms: permanent homes|noun
