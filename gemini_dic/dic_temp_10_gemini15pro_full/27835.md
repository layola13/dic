ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *canon (rule, standard), from Ancient Greek κανών (kanṓn, “straight rod, ruler, standard, model”), from Proto-Hellenic *kanā, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱneh₂- (to scratch).
