cradlelike:/ˈkreɪdəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像摇篮一样的，摇篮似的|Resembling a cradle, especially in shape or function; gently supporting.|The valley nestled between the mountains was cradlelike in its gentle curves. 山谷依偎在群山之间，曲线柔和，像摇篮一样。|近义词：protective, sheltering, comforting, snug, cozy|反义词：exposed, harsh, uncomfortable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "cradle" + "-like". "Cradle" comes from Old English "cradel", meaning "basket, crib". The suffix "-like" forms adjectives indicating resemblance.
