dribblet:/ˈdrɪblət/ | n. |少量，点滴，细流 | A small quantity of something, typically falling or flowing in drops. |  The tap was dripping, releasing a dribblet of water into the sink. 水龙头在滴水，一点点水滴入水槽里。

The information reached us in dribblets. 信息断断续续地传到我们这里。| 近义词：trickle, drip, drop, bit, speck | 反义词：flood, torrent, deluge | 词性: 名词

dribble:/ˈdrɪbl/ | v. |滴下，运球 | (Of a liquid) fall slowly in drops or a thin stream. / (in sports such as basketball and soccer) move (a ball) with repeated slight touches or kicks. |  The baby's chin was dribbling with milk.  婴儿的下巴滴着牛奶。

He dribbled the basketball down the court. 他运球过半场。 | 近义词：drip, trickle, leak (for liquid); bounce, control (for ball) | 反义词：gush, pour (for liquid) | 词性: 动词
