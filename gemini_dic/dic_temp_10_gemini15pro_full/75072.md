frigidarium:/frɪdʒɪˈdɛəriəm/
n.
冷水浴室，冷浴池
A cold bathing room in ancient Roman baths.
The frigidarium was a welcome respite from the heat of the caldarium.  冷水浴室是高温浴室caldarium的令人愉快的休憩场所。
The museum's reconstruction of a Roman bath complex included a tepidarium, a caldarium, and a frigidarium.  博物馆重建的罗马浴场包括温水浴室、热水浴室和冷水浴室。
cold bath, cold pool, plunge pool
hot tub, sauna, steam room
noun
